Vacuum cleaners "Karcher": models, specifications, reviews

It is unlikely that at least one person imagines his life without a vacuum cleaner. Thanks to it, you can significantly reduce the cleaning time without sacrificing quality. Progress does not stand still - new devices have appeared. It's about steam cleaners. The article will describe some of the most famous models of the manufacturer Karcher.



Karcher SV 1802

Studying Karcher steam cleaners, it is necessary to talk about the SV 1802. Although it has some negative aspects, all the positive ones justify the cost. Customers praise the device for its ability to steam clean, as well as the fact that with just one nozzle you can carry out large-scale cleaning. The garbage container will have to be washed often, since its volume is only 0.6 liters.

Filter installed in the device, type HEPA. Like many other Karcher steam cleaners, this one weighs impressive - almost 9 kg. Prices for the device are high. They vary between $ 650- $ 800. Noise during operation is not strong. That is what it attracts many potential buyers.



Karcher SV 7

Describing the Karcher steam cleaners, I would like to say a few words about the SV 7 model. Its main advantage is versatility. You can use this model as a steam cleaner or as a normal technique for dry garbage collection.

The water filter holds dust and dirt. It should be noted that thanks to the special technology, you do not have to constantly replace the liquid, making a long cleaning. The disadvantages include the following nuances:

  • installed HEPA filter;
  • high price (over $ 700).

Often, this model is chosen by those who need to clean fairly large areas. But it is worth considering - although you do not have to spend money on the purchase of household chemicals, you will need to constantly purchase new HEPA filters, since they quickly become clogged.

Electricity consumption is low, so saving money with the SV 7 will be easy. This is exactly what Karcher emphasized when designing the device. A steam cleaner, the instructions for which are provided with the most important information, consumes only 2.2 kW. In order to heat the water, the device needs to spend about 5 minutes. At the same time, the steam generator has a very small power - only 1.1 kW. This is a definite plus. However, this model weighs a little more than 9 kg, which is a negative criterion for some consumers.

Review of Karcher SV 1902

This device has several useful functions. In particular, steam cleaning, suction of garbage are available. In fact, acquiring the described model, a person receives several devices at once:

  • A vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter that is able to carry out dry cleaning. It also has a special antifoam.
  • Vacuum cleaner for cleaning surfaces.
  • A steam cleaner that can fight dirt, grease, soot, etc. All substances are immediately absorbed by a steam vacuum cleaner. Such a device will be quite useful in the kitchen or in the bathroom.

Speaking about the benefits of Karcher SV 1902, you need to clarify that it will be useful in eliminating bad odors. This model is safe, environmentally friendly, economical, efficient and quite convenient. It is inexpensive, unpretentious in maintenance, and also able to be stored anywhere.



Reviews on "Karcher SV 1902"

Often, customers note the maximum convenience that accompanies the hostess every time you use a vacuum cleaner. Also often in consumer reviews we are talking about reliability and versatility. However, this makes it possible to believe that all the characteristics declared by the manufacturer are true.

Like other Karcher steam cleaners, this model carries out high-quality cleaning without leaving garbage. It is capable of washing both surfaces and windows, and helps in cleaning mirrors and furniture.

It is best to buy the SV 1902 model for cleaning inside the car. Every driver advises this.

Buyers also note the presence of a water filter. It allows you to purify the air. This function will be especially useful for allergy sufferers.



The cost of the device impresses everyone. And the main thing is that this Karcher steam cleaner, the reviews of which are very positive, perfectly combines quality and low price category. On average, you will have to pay $ 600- $ 700 for such a model.

You also need to consider that when using this steam cleaner there is no need to buy household chemicals.

Among the shortcomings, consumers note a hose that is too heavy and noisy. Some also do not like the fact that there is no function to automatically rewind the wire.
