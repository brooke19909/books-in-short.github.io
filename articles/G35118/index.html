Marirena Hotel 3 * (Greece / Crete) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Each year, the largest Greek island of Crete, tens of thousands of tourists come from all over the world, including from the Russian Federation. They are attracted not only by the magnificent nature and clear azure sea, but also by developed infrastructure, as well as a wide hotel base. Entertainment and accommodation here can easily be found both by family people with children and bustling youth. If you decide to join the ranks of fans of Crete and want to spend an unforgettable vacation here, while choosing a comfortable economy class hotel, we suggest you consider the Marirena Hotel 3 *. Next we will get to know this hotel more closely, and also find out whether our compatriots liked to relax here.





Where is

The three-star Marirena Hotel is located in a tourist area called Amoudara. The distance to the major resort city of Crete - Heraklion - is only four kilometers. In the vicinity of the hotel there are shopping centers, a cinema, shops, souvenir shops, numerous bars, restaurants, taverns, as well as nightclubs and discos. Therefore, everyone here will find entertainment to their taste. In addition, Marirena is located just a hundred meters from the beautifully equipped sandy beach.

How to get there

Most travelers who plan to stay at the Marirena Hotel 3 * (Crete) and other hotels in the vicinity of Heraklion arrive on the island through the airport, located only 10 kilometers from this city. In this case, after landing the plane on the way to the hotel, it will take you no more than half an hour. If, during your trip to Greece, you plan to visit the capital of this state, then you can get from Athens to Crete by local airlines. Such a ticket will cost about 75 euros. Heraklion can also be reached from Athens and by sea. Comfortable liners run along this route, the trip on which will be an unforgettable experience.





Crete, Greece, Marirena Hotel 3 *: photos and description

Marirena is a modern, stylish, three-star hotel of small size, located on the very shore of the sea. It includes 54 comfortable standard rooms and apartments. On a small but cozy and green area of ​​the hotel there is a restaurant, two bars, an adult and children's outdoor pools, a children's playground and a sun terrace. Hotel guests can count on convenient accommodation, as well as excellent service. Attentive and friendly hotel staff will create a homely atmosphere in which families with children, older people and cheerful and noisy youth will feel comfortable.

Hotel Policies

Check-in for those staying at the Marirena Hotel 3 * ( Crete, Heraklion, Ammoudara) starts after two in the afternoon. If you arrive earlier, then if you have free rooms you will be settled immediately. On the day of departure from the hotel, vacate your apartment before noon. You can pay for accommodation and use of additional hotels here both with plastic cards and cash.





Special conditions

When placing children under three years old in your room on existing beds, their accommodation will be free. You can also ask the hotel staff to provide a playpen for babies up to two years old. Such a service will cost 3 euros per night. Extra beds are not available for Mariren rooms. As for tourists traveling with their pets, their accommodation is not allowed.

Number of rooms

As already mentioned, Marirena Hotel 3 * has 54 comfortable rooms. All are decorated in a pleasant style and equipped with everything necessary for living. So, in each of the rooms there is a private bathroom with a shower, a TV, a telephone, air conditioning, a safe, as well as an electric kettle and tea / coffee making facilities, as well as a balcony. Rooms are cleaned every day, and maids change bed linen and towels twice a week.





Nutrition

When booking a room at the hotel, you can choose one of the following catering schemes: breakfast only, half board or all inclusive. For meals three times a day, a buffet is served in the hotel restaurant. In addition, the hotel has two bars with a large selection of drinks for every taste.

Beach vacation

Just 100 meters from Marirena Hotel 3 * there is an excellent sandy beach with a gentle entry into the water. For the rental of sun loungers and parasols here have to pay. However, you can always stay completely free on the beach space not occupied by deck chairs, bringing a towel with you.





Cost of living

The price for accommodation in the Marirena hotel is consistent with its three-star category. So, a seven-day stay here at the height of the holiday season will cost you from 38 thousand rubles for a standard double room, from 41 thousand rubles for a family double room, from 47 thousand rubles for a triple standard room, from 52 thousand rubles for a family room for three and from 62 thousand rubles for a family room for four.

Marirena Hotel 3 *: reviews of tourists from Russia

In order for your idea of ​​the three-star Marirena hotel, located on the Greek island of Crete, to be more complete and closer to reality, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the impressions of our compatriots who stayed here on vacation. Looking ahead a bit, we say that the vast majority of Russian tourists were quite satisfied with their choice. According to them, here you will find more than the best value for money. But we offer to learn about everything in detail.





First of all, we offer to understand the impressions of travelers about hotel rooms. So, most tourists found them quite spacious, comfortable and equipped with everything necessary. Equipment and furniture are not new, but everything is in good and working condition. If suddenly something fails, then contact the reception, and all breakdowns will be quickly eliminated. Also pleased the guests of the Marirena Hotel 3 * the presence in the rooms of balconies, where you can conveniently place wet things for drying. Hotel guests note that the rooms were cleaned every day, the quality of which they were satisfied with. Linen and towels, they said, also changed regularly. In addition, all these items were of good quality (no torn sheets and dirty pillowcases, as in some other hotels).

As for the territory of the hotel, its travelers found a small, very green and comfortable. Guests also enjoyed the outdoor pool with sun terrace and bar. Family tourists also note the presence of a pool for children and a playground with a slide and a swing. Our compatriots also call its location a plus of the hotel. So, according to them, in the vicinity of the hotel there are many restaurants, taverns, bars and shops. In addition, there is a bus stop near the hotel, from where you can take a bus to almost any village in Crete. Guests also liked the fact that Marirena Hotel 3 * (Amoudara) is located close to the sea. You can walk to the beach in just a few minutes. The beaches here, according to travelers, are sandy and very clean. They belong to local bars and a restaurant, so that, having bought any drink, you can occupy sun loungers and an umbrella at least all day. Experienced travelers claim that the beaches in this place are the best in the vicinity. If you go a little to the side, instead of sand there appears large pebbles both on the shore and when entering the water.





As for food at the Marirena Hotel, here the opinions of the guests were divided. To some, the menu offered in the restaurant seemed monotonous. Another part of the guests is surprised at such comments and advises real gourmets not to skimp on five-star hotels, and not to test their taste buds in a three-star economy class hotel. In their opinion, a sufficient selection of dishes has always been here. So, for breakfast, cheese, ham, granola, cereal, yogurt and much more were always served. For lunch and dinner, guests were offered to taste a variety of meat, fish and seafood dishes, as well as several side dishes for every taste. And every day the menu was different. In general, experienced travelers recommend that people going on vacation to Crete do not choose the all-inclusive option. It will be enough to take only breakfast or half board. In this case, you will not be attached to the hotel all day, but you will be able to taste delicious local dishes in various restaurants and taverns. Moreover, the prices in them are very attractive.

One of the main advantages of Marirena Hotel Ammoudara 3 * most tourists from Russia consider the hotel staff, headed by an administrator named George. So, all the hotel staff are very friendly, welcoming, always happy to help, and also do their job well. The local staff does everything possible to make the stay of tourists in the hotel as comfortable and convenient as possible, and also helps to quickly solve problems. The administrator is always polite and always ready to help the hotel guests. In general, travelers are sure that such an attentive and pleasant service can not be found not only in many three-star, but even in five-star hotels.

Summing up, we note that, according to Russian tourists, Marirena Hotel 3 * (Crete) is a comfortable and convenient place to stay with excellent service near the sea and tourist infrastructure. Moreover, the cost of hotel accommodation is very affordable for most travelers. Many of our compatriots who have been here have been very pleased with their choice of a hotel in Crete and are ready to recommend it to their relatives and friends, and also do not exclude the possibility of returning here again.
