Several ways to get from Tyumen to Chelyabinsk

Each person at least once in his life paves a certain road. Why is he doing this? There may be several options! To enjoy the beauty of unknown places, visit your distant relative or friend and for many other, very different reasons. The Tyumen-Chelyabinsk route is one of the most popular among travelers. There are five surefire ways to overcome this distance.





Features of these amazing cities

Tyumen is considered a true Orthodox city. Only here you can carefully familiarize yourself with all Russian traditions. He was especially fond of foreign tourists. But for the residents of Russia there are plenty of ways to brighten up leisure. In the central part of this region is the Tura River. The most romantic bridge for people in love is built over it. Newlyweds come here every week to hang a lock. The view from the bridge is incredibly beautiful; it can take your breath away from the surrounding landscapes.

On Pervomaiskaya Street there is a square of cats. In it you can admire 12 unusual sculptures of these animals. Each tourist seeks to replenish his home gallery with new pictures. The cathedral, the museum in honor of Gregory Rasputin, the local philharmonic society and many other places - all this encourages tourists to come from Chelyabinsk to Tyumen.





In Chelyabinsk, the main natural attraction is Lake Smolino. It is hard to believe that he is already more than three million years old. Despite such a great age, it still remains crystal clear. The Scarlet Field, the Revolution Square and the Victory Garden are attractive in its scenery. There are many theaters in this city that are definitely worth a visit; outstanding actors of the country perform in them.

Driving in a personal vehicle

The distance from Tyumen to Chelyabinsk is only 416 kilometers. If you look at the map, you can see that it represents one straight line, which is quite simple to overcome by personal transport (car, motorcycle or moped).



There is the fastest way to cope with such a distance, for this you will need to drive through the city of Shadrinsk. It is worth noting that pits, potholes and other irregularities are often found on the track. If the driver wants to drive on a flat road, then he better choose the path through the mound, the canvas was repaired several years ago. However, the path itself will be 50 kilometers more.





The approximate travel time is 5 hours 30 minutes. On the way you can meet a large number of gas stations, cafes and hotels. There are several places where the trucks stop. There you can relax, have a bite to eat and enjoy the picturesque local nature.

If there is no personal transport, then you can take a taxi, but the cost of the road in this way will exceed 2500 rubles.

Train ride




The train has always been considered the most reliable and comfortable transport. It does not cradle on it, there is no need to sit in one position for a long time and you can use the bathroom. On such a vehicle, you can transfer from one specified point to another in approximately 8 hours 12 minutes. Unfortunately, there is no direct train flight from Chelyabinsk to Tyumen and vice versa, but there are more than 10 passing routes. The earliest departure is at 1:46, and the last at 18:54. Most locals prefer the train coming from Nizhnevartovsk to Samara, it is the fastest way to get to the right place (departure at 06:20, arrival at 15:52). The minimum ticket price for one person is only 900 rubles, you can purchase it at the box office of any station.

Bus Transfer

There is a daily direct bus service from Tyumen to Chelyabinsk. The path promises to be painful, long and exhausting, so many people abandon this venture, preferring more reliable transport.

The total travel time will be more than 9 hours, provided that there are no traffic jams on the road. Several routes per day are also carried out from Khanty-Mansiysk (at 9:50), Surgut (at 10:50) and Tobolsk (at 11:00).



The approximate cost of a ticket for one person is 1000 rubles.

Flight by plane

If you need to get from Tyumen to Chelyabinsk in just an hour and a half, then you should give preference to the plane. Flight 7R-64 is operated only twice a week - on Monday and Friday. The approximate ticket price is about 6,500 rubles per person. In the summer, it is recommended to book them at the airport ticket offices in advance, as there is a lot of tourists rush.





There are several flight options with a change, but you will have to spend more than 7 hours on the whole trip. In financial terms, this is also not profitable, more than 15,000 rubles will have to be spent on tickets.

The disadvantage of this method is that the point of arrival and departure is located a few kilometers from the city limits, you will additionally have to use public transport.

There are several ways to get from Tyumen to Chelyabinsk, it is worth choosing the most suitable option for yourself.
