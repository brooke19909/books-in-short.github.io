Already in 2010, their offspring made such a significant contribution to the development of the Russian Internet in the field of "Economics and Business", which became the winner of the prestigious Runet Prize. Main users

Today, according to the current legislation, a company can choose for itself one of the many legal forms and tax systems. However, from an extensive list of novice businessmen, as a rule, choose:

- IP - individual entrepreneurship;
- LLC - a limited liability company;
- NPO - non-profit type of organization;
- MUP - the form of a municipal unitary enterprise.

For which organizations does online accounting “My business” work? Service services are intended only for LLC and IP. Anyone who is interested in conducting their business through this program should take this information into account at the stage of choosing the legal form.

There are differences in companies and their taxation system. There are only two of them. This is a common system (OSNO) and simplified - (USN). At the first of them, the organization must keep accounting in its classical form. Such a regime is disadvantageous for small companies, but large organizations cannot refuse it. With a simplified tax system, an enterprise has a lower tax burden. Such a regime is legally provided for small and medium-sized businesses and has four varieties: USN 6%, 15%, as well as UTII and Unified Social Tax. Who is the new Internet accounting intended for? For SP USN of the first three types. These are the systems:

- "STS income" when the tax is paid in the amount of 6% of the amount of revenue for the reporting period;
- “income minus expenses” with the application of taxation in the amount of 15% of the difference between the amount of revenue and the amount of expenses incurred;
- a single tax on imputed income, the rates of which are regulated by municipal laws.



There is also a single agricultural tax (UTN). It is used by organizations of the agricultural sector. However, the My Business program does not work for this business.

Acquaintance with your personal account

How does “My business” online accounting work? The description of the first page of your personal account, where the user first of all gets, is quite accessible and understandable. Tabs with the following names appear on the screen: “Home” and “Money”, “Documents” and “Stocks”, “Contracts” and “Cashier”, “Counterparties” and “Salary”, “Employees” and “Banks”, “Analytics "And" Webinars. "



In addition, the first page of your personal account contains services such as:
- the balance in the account;
- expert advice;
- selected documents;
- instructions for the operation of the service, technical support contacts, password creation;
- company business card;
- details of the organization - the owner of the personal account.

Let's consider the functionality of all these tabs in more detail.

"Home"

This tab includes services such as:
1. Activities. Using this tab, the user creates primary documents and enters information about their counterparties.
2. Tax calendar. It is needed for reporting and making payments on them.
3. Analytics. It duplicates the tab of the same name.
4. Electronic reporting. Using this tab, statistics are compiled for those reports that were sent via the Internet.

"Money"

This tab is a tool for the implementation and accounting of financial transactions of the organization. It contains:
1. KUDIR and cash book layout. These documents can be downloaded and printed at any time. The cash book records the amount of cash receipts and disbursements made by the organization. KUDIR (a book on accounting for income and expenses) is maintained by all individual entrepreneurs and organizations with STS. This document in chronological order reflects the business operations that took place in this reporting period.
2. Information on available income and expenses. It is entered either manually or using a bank statement.
3. Service sending payment orders.

"Documents"

Using this tab, the user is given the opportunity to generate accounts and acts, invoices, etc. To create documents, you need to select the one you need from the list, and then select the action algorithm. Subsequently, the form may be:
- downloaded, printed and transmitted;
- sent by email to the client;
- exposed with reference to payment either by Yandex Money or by credit card.

"Stocks"

This tab provides an opportunity to issue the bill necessary for payment, accept or ship materials or goods, transfer products from warehouse to warehouse.





At the same time, the user owns information on income and expenditure, as well as inventory balances at a given time.

"Contracts"

This tab provides many features. On it you can download a template, create a contract and view statistics on transactions.

To create a new document, a client is selected. In this case, from the drop-down list, you must select the desired contract template. It will be filled automatically.

Nineteen templates of various contracts that are created by service experts are available to the client. A custom version of the document can also be loaded into the program.

"Cashbox"

This tab works as a draft. Information from the Money service flows here. Here, draft receipts and settlement cash orders can be created.

"Counterparties"

This tab is intended for entering information on customers and partners. Here, the counterparties are checked using the state register extract, and statistics are also generated for all organizations with which contracts for the supply or sale of products were concluded.

"Salary"

On this tab you can see all the information on the payment of cash compensation to company employees. Namely:
- calculations in general;
- calculations for each employee;
- settlement statements;
- timesheet;
- statements of taxes and contributions;
- payment of cash compensation.

"Staff"

Using this tab, you can calculate sick leave and vacation pay. To do this, you only need to mark the dates of absence of the employee. The calculation formulas and the total amounts payable will appear on the screen.

"Forms"

This tab, which contains the service "My business" (Internet accounting), greatly simplifies the lives of entrepreneurs. Using the program, they fill out the forms currently in force, without thinking about their relevance. At the disposal of users is more than 2000 different samples, from which it is easy to choose the right one.

"Analytics"

Using this tab, the user can view the statistics of his income, expenses, as well as profit. Moreover, this can be done for different periods in the context of months.

Webinars

This tab contains videos introducing legislative changes. There are also video instructions that provide invaluable assistance in working with your personal account. The user can also view various interviews with experts and successful businessmen.

These tabs contain the service "My business" - Internet accounting. However, not all of them are available to the client. The number of tabs to open depends on the selected tariff.
