Yes, Pattaya is not the resort where you should bring your wife, mother-in-law and children. The city beach cannot be called clean, and besides, it is always very crowded there. Tourists go swimming and sunbathing on the white sand to nearby islands. Therefore, if you wanting to relax with your family at sea, due to a misunderstanding, came to this city of sins, hotels in the center of Pattaya will not suit you. It is better to choose a good hotel in the elite Jomtien area (south of the down town) or a cheap guest house directly at the Bali Hai pier, from which boats depart for the islands.

But if you come for entertainment (including carnal), hotels in the center of Pattaya are exactly what you need. In this regard, before you open the widest selection of hotels of different price categories. Let's try to group them by cost factor, because the range of services provided by the hotel directly depends on this.



Guest houses and two-star hotels in the center of Pattaya are the most affordable and affordable housing. Usually these are several rooms in a house managed by the same family. The disadvantage of such mini-hotels is the Spartan atmosphere (not all rooms have their own shower and toilet, as well as air conditioning). But a definite plus is the ridiculous price of 500 baht per room and the ability to freely bring girls. One or two star hotels, unlike guest houses, can be pre-booked. They sometimes have small rooftop pools. From food, such guesthouses offer breakfast only.



Three-star hotels in the center of Pattaya are characterized by an optimal ratio of price and quality. There is always a swimming pool available, which is important in the equatorial heat and not very convenient beach for swimming. The rooms have air conditioning, WIFI, refrigerator. On the territory there is a restaurant, sometimes a massage room. You can recommend the "Mercure Hotel Chonburi", "Central Wong Amat Beach Resort", "Tai Garden". The price of accommodation in them ranges from 700 to a thousand butts per room.

For VIP-clients there are also good hotels in the center of Pattaya - 4 stars. They are also located directly on or off the odious Walking Street. The service is supplemented by a hairdryer, slippers, interchangeable hygiene items in the bathroom, a minibar and so on. On the territory there are entertainment, an extensive pool and SPA-salon. Tourists respond well to Amari Orchid Resort, Asia Pattaya Hotel, Siam Bayview and so on .. Room prices start at 2,000 baht.
