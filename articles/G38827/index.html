America's Top Landmark: List of Popular Places

The United States attracts hundreds of thousands of tourists every year. It seems that in this country you can see everything: pristine beaches and high mountains, stunning skyscrapers and stunning bridges. That is why, dreaming of visiting the Western Hemisphere, the majority recalls not the ancient sights of Latin America and not even a bright carnival in Rio, but the lights of New York or the architecture of San Francisco. So what do you need to see first?





National parks

Listing the places to visit, you can start with a marvelous corner in Utah. Here is the Bryce Canyon Park, a natural attraction of America, interesting for its landscapes and rock formations called Hoodoo. They arose naturally, as a result of erosion, which destroyed the breed and created arches and pillars of amazing shades, from light yellow to red. It is best to go to see this attraction of America in the winter, when it is not too hot, and visibility reaches a maximum. Another park that can be seen is Yosemite. It is located in downtown California and delights in its scale. A distinctive feature of the park is the incredible number of waterfalls, each of which is not like all the others. The most interesting is the so-called fiery waterfall Yosemite. He conquers with beauty and romance, so to see this attraction of America will be completely out of place.





Mountain peaks

In America there are amazing peaks. For example, Mount McKinley, located in Alaska, deserves special attention. The north of the United States generally represents one of the best destinations for winter travel - countless mountains, transparent rivers, picturesque towns and the dense forest surrounding them allow you to find yourself in a new reality. McKinley, located in Denali National Park, is the highest point in the country and is surrounded by stunning lakes, so a trip to it is guaranteed to be remembered for a lifetime. No less attractive for tourists is the Grand Canyon. It is located in Arizona, at an altitude of more than a thousand meters above sea level. The Grand Canyon is one of the first on the list to include America‚Äôs finest natural attractions. Photos of the local landscapes conquer even people indifferent to the mountains. Once at the Colorado River, you will definitely fall in love with this place.





Cities

Known to the whole world, this city is perhaps the most important attraction of America. New York is full of places connected with world history, the most famous architects created here. The metropolis constantly appears on large and small screens and inspires musicians, writers and artists. In addition, this city is the most densely populated in the country. It is also one of the largest cities in the world. New York is especially good at night, when Manhattan flashes with multi-colored lights reflected in the Hudson. This spectacle cannot be conveyed in photographs - it is much better to see it at least once in a lifetime. New York is America embodied. Sights, photos and descriptions of which are known to every tourist, necessarily include such a city as Las Vegas. This is a cult place filled with luxurious hotels and casinos, known throughout the world. Like the business capital, Las Vegas is best viewed at night when entertainment venues come alive with thousands of lights. To see this city is for everyone who travels in the United States.





Beaches and coasts

America is washed by several oceans. It is not surprising that the country is popular for its beaches. Hawaii deserves special attention, namely the island of Kauai, located in the west of the archipelago. It is known for its impressive biological diversity, and the local beach is a natural wonder. Cliffs are located off the coast, rising almost a thousand meters up and perfectly emphasizing the majestic beauty of the ocean with its transparent blue waves.

Another unique place is in California. Big Sur coast has a unique landscape. Vertical coastal cliffs and surging ocean waves make a lasting impression. The best solution for tourists will be a boat trip, allowing you to view the coast from an unusual angle.
