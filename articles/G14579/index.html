"Side Sural Sarai" - the pearl of Turkish resorts

Turkey is the most popular country for holidays, it attracts with its amazing beaches, resort towns, wonderful Mediterranean climate and many interesting sights. Its settlements are distinguished by good hotels, spa centers and beach areas. The quality of the services provided is always at the highest level. One of the most popular holiday destinations is the Side Sural Saray Hotel, which is located near the seashore.





Hotel Location

This hotel attracts with its convenient location - its distance from the sea is only two hundred meters. The hotel complex "Side Sural Saray" is located near the village of Cholakly, nine kilometers from it lies the wonderful resort town of Side. The nearest airport is Antalya Airport, 55 km from the hotel.

Room Feature

The complex is a four-story building, which housed 264 rooms with charming sea or garden views. The apartments of the Side Sural Saray Hotel are decorated in a modern style, have all the necessary furniture, a minibar, a TV, air conditioning, a telephone with an intercity line. If necessary, a cot will be provided in the room, and facilities for people with disabilities are also provided.



Hotel facilities

The hotel has a library, a mini club, an amphitheater, a lounge for watching and watching TV shows. Qualified staff speaks many foreign languages, including Russian. On the territory of the hotel there are outdoor and indoor swimming pools with fresh water, a separate pool for young tourists is located separately. A wonderful place of entertainment will be a water park equipped with two slides. Vacationers are provided with free use of sun loungers, umbrellas, towels, mattresses. The hotel restaurant provides an excellent menu (Turkish and international cuisine), a cozy and comfortable atmosphere.

Entertainment and sports





The hotel has a huge number of sports grounds for playing volleyball, basketball, tennis. You can also play billiards and visit the fitness center. Own sandy beach, Internet cafe, hairdresser, massage room are some of the many advantages of this resort complex. If you want to have a wonderful rest in peace and harmony with nature, choose a vacation in a country like Turkey. "Side Sural Saray" is a hotel that will give a wonderful vacation for the whole family.

Turkey Resort - Side

In addition to a beach holiday, tourists are given the opportunity to visit nearby villages with an excursion. The city of Side, which is located near the Hotel Side Sural Saray, is one of the three most popular resorts in Turkey. He is famous for his wonderful sandy beaches, picturesque landscapes, a combination of modern buildings and architectural structures. The city has well preserved ancient cultural monuments and attractions of the ancient world, which are so rich in Turkish land. If you are going on vacation, it makes sense to choose a direction such as Turkey (Side). Hotel "Sural Saray" is exactly the place where you can relax and explore various sights.
