"Tavush", recreation center: description, photo

In our country, recreation centers are very popular among tourists. Most of them are built in the lap of picturesque nature, near rivers, waterfalls and lakes, where there is the opportunity to fish and hunt, collect mushrooms and berries. Such a pastime distracts from the daily routine of life and makes it possible to recharge your batteries. There are many such recreation centers in the Krasnodar Territory.

The nature of this region is truly amazing. Krasnodar Territory has always been considered a special region. It is called a place of pilgrimage for a huge army of tourists from all over our boundless country. The sea and mild climate, mineral springs, mountains and forests, crystal clear air saturated with ozone, vineyards and much more attract people here. Here, in this truly amazing region, there are many places where you can relax.



The recreation center "Tavush"

Labinsky district, in whose territory it is located, is famous not only for ecologically clean nature, but also for its thermal spring. It hits from a depth of two and a half thousand meters. Water from it has amazing healing properties: it heals and restores the body. It is next to this unique natural spring that the Tavush recreation center was built. Staying here is a great opportunity to combine thermal therapy and enjoy your time in an ecologically clean natural corner.

The address where you can find this complex is Labinsky district, st. Persistent. The recreation center "Tavush" is located near the station in the forest.

Description

Favorable ecology, developed infrastructure, affordable prices, the ability to restore their vitality - all this makes tourists come back here more than once. Tavush is a recreation center that offers its guests comfortable living conditions, but the most important thing here is a complex of mineral thermal water pools. This tourist complex consists of a main building and detached houses.

For those who come here for the weekend without spending the night, there are wooden arbors with barbecue grills, which can be rented for a very reasonable fee. Tavush is a recreation center that has free parking for vacationers. There is also a cafe. Nearby are shops where you can buy everything you need for a comfortable stay.



Housing

The recreation center "Tavush" (Resistant station) offers for living both double and triple rooms in the main building, as well as detached cottages, which can comfortably accommodate both families and fairly large groups of tourists. Bed linen, pillows, blankets are provided.

The standard rooms have one double or two single beds with comfortable mattresses, a sofa, which is considered an extra bed, a table, chairs, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a refrigerator, a TV with satellite channels. Bathrooms combined. They have a big mirror. The shower room is made with a drain to the floor, there is also a toilet and sink.

Houses

Many come to this recreation center in large companies, staying for several days. In such cases, it is most convenient to stay in houses. They can accommodate up to fifteen people at a time. The conditions in the houses are no different from those in the hotel rooms of the main building.

At the same time, such accommodation has several advantages. Firstly, it is beneficial, and secondly, in front of each house there is a barbecue area with barbecue and gazebo. Therefore, tourists can independently solve the problem of nutrition. Since Tavush is a recreation center that operates all year round, its rooms are heated.



Nutrition

The complex has a cafe. The price does not include meals. The service "complex lunch" can be obtained for a fee. In the menu offered in the cafe, dishes from the Caucasian and European cuisine, here you can taste incredibly delicious mountain honey, drink tea with herbs.

Many tourists solve the problem of food very simply: they cook kebabs. Food can be bought at nearby stores.

Pools

"Tavush" is a recreation center built near the thermal spring. The water here at the outlet reaches a temperature of 90 degrees. The source is classified as "very hot." The chemical composition of water is unique: the minerals that are dissolved in it contain such active chemical elements that restore skin balance, accelerate cell metabolism and increase resistance.

Judging by the reviews of those who rested at the Tavush recreation center, bathing in pools located at the source improves well-being and contributes to the treatment of diseases such as musculoskeletal disorders, circulatory disorders, neuralgia, bronchitis, insomnia and weak immunity.

There are several pools with thermal water: one and four individual. They say that “Tavush” is a recreation center where people seem to be transformed: their skin becomes silky, soft, lightness appears in the body and the mood just rises. Around the pools are loungers and umbrellas.



Additional Information

Excellent conditions are created for children here. Younger guests can play on the playground, ride on a swing, swim in the pool. In the cafe, which operates on the territory of the recreation center, you can organize various holidays. For crowded events, there is a banquet hall for up to forty to fifty people. Pets are not allowed.

Reviews

"Tavush" is a recreation center where residents of not only the Labinsky district, but also the entire Krasnodar Territory come. The fame of the amazing properties of the thermal spring has reached many corners of our country.



Many liked the vacation, which, in their opinion, was a success. Pools are always clean, the area is cleaned. Some reviews expressed dissatisfaction with the quality of housekeeping, but there are few such people. But those who were satisfied with the service that is provided at the recreation center "Tavush" are much more. Resting, judging by the numerous reviews, was a success, so those who have not been here are recommended to come and take a dip in the thermal spring, and later taste delicious barbecue in nature.
