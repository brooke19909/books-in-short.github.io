Park-Hotel "Kartmazovo-Vnukovo": address, phone, description of rooms, services

Park Hotel "Kartmazovo-Vnukovo" will allow you to relax in a luxurious setting, in a distance from the city bustle. A friendly atmosphere is created here, which is permeated with warmth and comfort. This is a great option for a family vacation, business trip, as well as for celebrations in the lap of nature.





Brief description of the hotel

Having passed only 20 minutes from the noisy capital, you will find yourself in a beautiful institution that will give you an unforgettable vacation. Park Hotel "Kartmazovo-Vnukovo" is a luxurious option for celebrations, business negotiations, as well as a family weekend in the lap of nature. A pleasant moment is the opportunity to relax on an all-inclusive system, which will completely relieve you of any worries. Also here will be presented to your attention a lot of entertainment, including quad biking, sauna, hiking and much more.

As for the number of rooms, the park-hotel "Kartmazovo-Vnukovo" made sure that the guests feel as comfortable as possible. All apartments are decorated in a luxurious classic style. The color scheme is designed in calm warm colors, which gives your room the atmosphere of home comfort. A full range of modern amenities provides you the comfort you are used to.

Special attention deserves the work of the restaurant, which has the Park-Hotel Kartmazovo-Vnukovo. Due to the fact that it operates around the clock, you can satisfy your hunger at any time convenient for you. Thanks to the open terrace, you can taste the best dishes of Caucasian, Russian and European cuisines, admiring the beautiful nature. If necessary, your order will be delivered directly to your room.





Hotel Location

In order to get to the hotel, you need to go to the Kiev highway and drive about 4 kilometers. The road takes you no more than 20 minutes. The hotel address is Kartmazovo village, Kievskaya street, 4. In the immediate vicinity are metro stations such as Salaryevo, Rumyantsevo, as well as Troparevo. If we talk about the distance from Vnukovo International Airport, then, having left on the Kiev highway, you will have to overcome about 10 kilometers.

Hotel Rooms

If you have chosen such an option as the village of Kartmazovo for your vacation in the countryside, then by all means stay in a modern park-hotel. The following accommodation options will be available here:

  • Economy category apartments are suitable for those tourists who do not want to spend a lot of money on accommodation and will be satisfied with a minimum set of necessary amenities. This room is designed for two people, and therefore there is a large or a pair of single beds. The bathroom has a modern shower.
  • Superior room has a larger area than the previous category, as well as an expanded set of amenities. The apartment consists of one room, in which there is not only a bed, but also soft armchairs, a small work area and a dressing table. You can choose the option of accommodation with a bath or shower.
  • Studio apartments are divided into a bedroom and a work area by decorative zoning. Due to the fact that the room is decorated in bright colors, visually it seems even larger. There is also upholstered furniture for relaxing or for additional guests.
  • The suites are luxuriously designed with a spacious area. You can choose a two-room or one-room option. Special attention should be paid to the bathroom, the decoration of which is made of light marble. There is also a jacuzzi.
  • Luxury apartments are distinguished by luxurious decoration and a huge area. The layout of the room is thought out to the smallest detail so that guests feel as comfortable as possible. Here you will find a cozy bedroom, as well as a spacious living room, decorated in bright colors.




The amenities provided in the apartment

If you want to relax from the bustle of the city and enjoy the beautiful nature, then pay attention to the hotel "Vnukovo-Kartmazovo". Here you will be provided with the following amenities for a comfortable stay in the apartment:

  • thanks to the presence of huge panoramic windows, your room will always have a lot of light, and you can also enjoy the beautiful surrounding landscapes;
  • double or one and a half beds with modern orthopedic mattresses, which will provide you with a full and healthy sleep;
  • Plasma panel with excellent color rendering quality, which will allow you to get a real aesthetic pleasure from watching satellite channels;
  • in all rooms you can use the wireless Internet absolutely free;
  • the minibar serves not only for cooling drinks, because it is quite possible to store a small amount of products in it;
  • depending on the category of the room, your bathroom will have a shower or bathtub with a jacuzzi;
  • thanks to the presence of individual climate control, you can create a comfortable temperature in your room;
  • The bathroom has bath and toiletries, as well as a built-in electric hairdryer.




Hotel Infrastructure

If you want to have a good rest outside the city, then Kartmazovo-Vnukovo (park hotel) is just perfect for you . Moscow has a frantic pace of life, and therefore sometimes I want to stay in silence for at least a couple of days. The following infrastructure is provided for you in this hotel:

  • several banquet rooms in which your holidays and special occasions will be held at the highest level;
  • a large garden with a huge number of flowers, as well as benches and gazebos;
  • a large assortment of drinks and light snacks awaits you at the bar;
  • in the hotel restaurant you can taste delicious dishes, as well as make an order directly to your room;
  • in public places, as well as in apartments, it is possible to use wireless Internet (the service is provided for free);
  • in the courtyard of the hotel there is a private parking, the use of which is included in the price;
  • Friendly staff who works at the reception desk, at any time of the day is ready to come to your aid and answer all your questions;
  • in the laundry you can tidy up dirty or wrinkled things;
  • for business negotiations and seminars a modern conference room is provided;
  • If necessary, you will be provided with a shuttle to the airport (paid service).




Entertainment and recreation opportunities

If you want to relax your soul and body, then the Vnukovo-Kartmazovo country hotel will be an ideal option for you. The Park Hotel offers you the following entertainment options:

  • a visit to the spa to undergo anti-aging, wellness or relaxing treatments;
  • outdoor barbecue;
  • organization of excursion programs in order to get acquainted with the sights of Moscow and its environs;
  • You can relieve stress in a traditional Russian bath;
  • You will have the opportunity to have a good time riding ATVs in the company of friends.

What the restaurant offers

Park Hotel has a luxurious restaurant that offers the following features:

  • try traditional Russian dishes that belong to different regions of the country;
  • extensive European menu, which is based on Mediterranean recipes;
  • You can feel all the charm of the cuisine of the peoples of the Caucasus;

Useful information for guests

It will be useful for tourists to familiarize themselves with a number of rules in accordance with which the "Kartmazovo" (hotel) operates. Telephone for information: +7 (495) 782-74-47. So, it is worth paying attention to the following points:

  • Check-in at the hotel is from 14:00 to midnight, and check-out must be in time before noon;
  • Children under 6 years old stay at the hotel for free if they can stay in existing beds;
  • the cost of an extra bed per day - 1000 rubles;
  • the hotel can pay for the services received through the terminal for cashless payments;
  • a shuttle service to Vnukovo Airport is provided free of charge, and to Sheremetyevo and Domodedovo - at an additional cost;
  • upon check-in, guests are required to pay a security deposit in the amount of the daily room rate.




Park-hotel "Kartmazovo": reviews, advantages

Tourists find a lot of positive moments of relaxation in this hotel, namely:

  • the transfer service is very high quality - good cars, polite drivers;
  • reasonable prices for accommodation;
  • proximity to Vnukovo International Airport;
  • large free parking with 24-hour security;
  • the room rate includes delicious and hearty breakfasts;
  • beautiful green area;
  • Friendly and polite staff.

Negative reviews

Of the negative points, it is worth highlighting the following:

  • quite often, in this hotel, guests are provided with rooms that do not match the parameters booked;
  • in many rooms there is such a design "feature" as a transparent wall between the bathroom and the bedroom, which is actually not very convenient and not at all aesthetically pleasing;
  • the rooms have very poor sound insulation;
  • some rooms do not have air conditioning, and staff do not respond to requests for measures;
  • very high prices in the restaurant;
  • the rooms are poorly cleaned;
  • blockers are on the windows, and therefore it is simply impossible to open them for ventilation;
  • the doors in the shower cabin do not fit snugly, and therefore the floor is always flooded with water;
  • old crushed mattresses in standard rooms, as well as pillows too hard;
  • the hotel is in a state of constant construction and repair, which greatly spoils the appearance;
  • rooms and hallways have a pungent smell of tobacco smoke.

conclusions

The frantic pace of life in the metropolitan metropolis sooner or later bothers, but because many residents of Moscow tend to leave at least for a couple of days to rest in a quiet place. A good option is Park Hotel Kartmazovo. The address of this institution: st. Kiev, 4. An indisputable advantage of this institution can be considered the proximity to the Vnukovo international airport, as well as its location in the forest zone. Many people choose this place for celebrations or just a family weekend.

Of the negative aspects, it is worth noting the dirt, continuous construction and poor sound insulation in the rooms. If a restaurant is celebrating a wedding or other celebration, then you will not be able to fall asleep. Also, many guests are confused by the transparent wall in the bathroom. It is impossible not to say about the overpriced food prices in the restaurant. Given that there are no other establishments nearby, the food is quite expensive. Although the food itself is at a high level. Thus, the park-hotel "Kartmazovo-Vnukovo" has not yet justified its high status.
