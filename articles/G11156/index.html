Crocheting a swimsuit is quite simple, it is enough to have a crochet hook, multi-colored threads, a little imagination and patience. Even if you do not know how to knit, be sure to try to do such an original thing. It is enough to understand the simple process in order to subsequently enjoy the enthusiastic looks of others on the beach.

How to crochet a swimsuit - the most necessary

First, you should choose the threads for the product. At this stage, special attention must be paid specifically to the quality of the yarn. In particular, it is better to purchase threads made from natural fibers for a swimsuit. Cotton with an admixture of elastane is perfect. These components complement each other perfectly. In particular, cotton will provide the necessary comfort, and elastane will not allow the product to deform.

In the event that you cannot use synthetic fabrics as a material, you can crochet a swimsuit of 100% natural threads. At the same time, the edges of the product must be sheathed with silicone thread (spandex), especially a bra.

Do not forget that a crocheted swimsuit will certainly be visible. Even if a dense pattern is used for knitting, when wet, all covered charms will be visible. This is also fixable. To do this, it is worth taking care of the lining, which will be sewn from the wrong side and made of fabric. In addition, she additionally maintains the shape of a bra.

You can start knitting from the bottom of the swimsuit. The first step is to perform a belt. In this case, do not forget to knit a gum in the first row. Next, we knit according to the scheme:





In the process, do not forget about fitting and periodically apply the product to the body or swimming trunks, otherwise you can make a mistake with the size. After knitting reaches the middle of the gusset, you can start adding loops. The edges should be tied with an elastic band after the panties are ready. This is necessary so that in the process of wearing a swimsuit the product does not deform, but fits to the body and looks great.

Bra can be made in different forms. As a rule, it is a bra in the form of two triangles. However, it is not suitable for those who have a large breast size. In this case, a crochet swimsuit, the scheme of which is standard, will not suit you. But you can choose an individual form and use supporting bones. After the end of the basic knitting, we begin to create a unique swimsuit design. In particular, rhinestones or sequins, various bows, etc. can be used as decorations. Some use multi-colored threads that can simply be knitted into the product. In principle, here you can use your own imagination or look at various options for decoration.
