Emergency call 112. Emergency call: number

Caught in an emergency and do not know whom to call in the first place? Now there is no need to waste time deciding which service to call. It is enough to know how to make an emergency call. 112 can be dialed absolutely from any phone and talk about the reason for the appeal. The operator will determine exactly which rescue unit should come to you.

From mobile or city?

It was possible to call the rescue service from a cell phone by dialing 112 for a long time. But this number became publicly available from August 12, 2013. Prior to this date, the indicated number worked in pilot mode in certain subjects of the Federation. It was available in the Astrakhan and Kursk regions, in Tatarstan and other regions.

It is worth noting that the emergency call until 2017 is also available at the usual numbers: 01 for the fire department, 02 for the police, 03 for the ambulance arrival, 04 for calling the gas workers. Job prospects



According to the developers, the emergency call to 112 allows you to quickly connect the victims with the service they need. You can call him when you need the police, ambulance, fire or gas services, Antiterror, emergency.
The specified line operates around the clock, without any interruptions, and even more so weekends.

To simplify the work, a bill was developed according to which telecom operators can find out and transmit to the selected service the coordinates of the caller and other information that is necessary for the most accurate and correct call processing. The provision of such data is intended to expedite the work of emergency services.

The cost of creating a single service

Of course, the introduction of a single contact phone cost the state a lot. In order for the emergency call number to work throughout the entire Federation, almost a billion rubles was spent. These funds were distributed between the regions of the country.



They were spent on the development and start of operation of special centers involved in processing calls, building premises, equipping offices, and training personnel. For example, the pilot Kursk region received 39.3 million numbers for implementation, and 137.9 million were allocated to Tatarstan.

Service fee

Any calls to the emergency number are free of charge. You can dial 112 both from a regular landline phone and from a mobile. It does not matter where you are located - in the center of a metropolis or in a distant village. You can call the specified number from anywhere. Separately, it is worth noting that an emergency call is made absolutely free. You can dial 112 from your mobile, even if you do not have money in your account. Also, calls will be available if your mobile device does not have a SIM card or if it is blocked for some reason. The only obstacle to a call from a mobile phone will be the complete discharge of the battery.

Separately, it is worth noting that it does not matter what kind of carrier you use. Of course, each of them provides its own numbers for calling emergency services, but sometimes not everyone can remember them. It is much easier to learn one emergency number, which can be used in any emergency.

Information Processing and Response Speed



During the work of the program in the pilot regions, specialists could evaluate its effectiveness. Considering that considerable funds were spent on introducing a single number, it was important to understand how well this system works, whether it has advantages over standard, familiar to all numbers.

As practice has shown, the response time when using the number 112 was reduced by almost 20%. That is, each specific person receives help faster. And if we are talking about emergency situations, when a person cannot decide where to call first, then the efficiency increases even more. For example, after witnessing a crime in which the victim was seriously injured, the person must decide what is more important - an emergency call of the police or ambulance? On the one hand, the victim needs to provide medical care as soon as possible, but on the other, during this time the offender will have time to escape. It is much easier to dial one number - 112.

Benefits for citizens



Speaking about improving the response speed, one can not help but pay attention to other positive aspects from the introduction of a single contact phone. First of all, it is worth noting that in a critical situation, many could confuse the numbers of the police, ambulance or fire, it is much easier to remember a single number, by which an emergency call is made in any critical situation. The phone is one for the whole country - 112.

The advantages of introducing one number also include fixing all calls in one system, so no call will be left unattended. Experts who can provide psychological support or tell how to behave in the situation that has arisen can also be connected to the conversation.

State benefit

By creating a single number by which an emergency call can be made at any time, the state, of course, cares about its citizens. But do not forget that the country itself receives certain benefits from this. A person does not call several numbers, thereby increasing the load on specialized dispatch networks, he contacts all emergency services with one number. In addition, the introduction of number 112 made it possible to automate a number of processes. For example, when receiving a call, the operator automatically fills in a card that immediately displays the phone number (and information about its owner). This significantly reduces the time of making a call, which means it speeds up the response.



All created cards must be filled out, not one of them can be skipped. The actions that were carried out should be recorded in it. This helps to control the entire process of helping those in need.

Also, an analysis of all calls received to a single number allows you to assess the security situation and the level of health of the population in the region. The use of a single dispatching base also favors the normal interaction of all related emergency services.

Do not forget that in order to create the number it was required to develop a new technical base, make non-standard decisions to maximize the process of its implementation. And this, in turn, affects the innovative development of the state.

Western counterparts

It is worth noting that Russia did not become an innovator by introducing a single number by which emergency calls are made. Such a system is practiced in many countries. For the first time, one emergency number was introduced in the UK back in 1937. From that moment, it was possible to get through to the police, fire service or doctors by dialing 999.



In Australia since 1961, all emergency calls are accepted by the number 000. At first, the service worked only in large cities, but in the 80s it already covered the entire territory of the country. In New Zealand, a single contact telephone number was introduced back in 1958. The government decided that it would be most convenient to dial 111 in an emergency.

The most famous emergency service in the United States began to function only in 1968. Even many Russians know that in order to call rescuers in the United States you need to dial 911. It is worth noting that they began to use this number throughout the country only by the end of the 80s.
