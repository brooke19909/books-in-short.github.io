Becici Beach, Montenegro: description, features and reviews of tourists

Travelers are well aware that the sea is different, and they choose not a certain place for relaxation, but the impressions that they would like to get while being there.

So, lovers of lying all day on the beach are expected on exotic islands. Here you can even not go to the bar for food or drinks - they will bring everything you need. For those who like to combine the useful with the beautiful, it is better to visit Becici beach, since few people offer such a rich vacation, as in Montenegro. It has everything: water sports, and the beautiful coast, and mountains, and ancient history, and unusually picturesque landscapes, and delicious food.

Beach description

As you know, at the dawn of the twentieth century, people just started to get involved in water tourism, but already in 1935 Becici beach (Montenegro) received a grand prix in Paris as one of the best in Europe. He is considered such today.

Throughout 1950 meters it changes - slightly dark sand at the northern end of the coast turns into golden sand at the southern end. A strip of very small pebbles runs along the surf, in which children are especially fond of messing around.





The beach in Becici (reviews of vacationers confirm this) has several advantages over other sea coasts in the same Montenegro and Europe:

  • firstly, it is municipal, and therefore free even in those parts that belong to hotels;
  • secondly, a gentle descent into the sea guarantees safety and a gradual increase in depth, which is especially important for travelers with children;
  • thirdly, it is equipped with everything necessary - showers, changing rooms, cafes, bars and toilets;
  • fourthly, it is constantly cleaned, so that ecology and cleanliness are at the highest level here;
  • fifthly, it is wide enough for everyone to decide how to relax - rent a deck chair and umbrella or lie on a towel near the water itself.

Among the minuses of this beach, you can point out only that there are a lot of people in season, but this is understandable, still the best in Europe. Comfort prices on the beach are quite reasonable - a chaise lounge under an umbrella will cost 8 ‚Ç¨, and a wide comfortable bed - 20 ‚Ç¨.

Since there are no shops on the coast, you should stock up on water and food in advance or buy everything you need in a cafe, but at different prices.

June is considered the best time for a relaxing holiday on Becici beach, since the main influx of tourists begins in July and decreases only by the end of September.

Apartments Dukley

On the north side, Becici beach ends with a cape that separates the village from the city of Budva. It is fully developed by the Dukley Gardens 4 * hotel. If you look at it from the sea, it looks very much like a rock with swallow's nests, but in reality it is full of light and very comfortable apartments. Each of them has a kitchen with a dining room, a living room, a bedroom and a comfortable terrace with sea views.





The apartments are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable life: air conditioning, cable TV, LCD TVs, Wi-Fi. There are bathrobes, slippers, bath accessories, dishes and household appliances.

For those who prefer not to waste time cooking, the hotel has a restaurant with a wide selection of international and Mediterranean dishes.

Hotel Dukley Gardens 4 *, as noted by those who rested here, allows not only their guests to feel comfortable here, but also their pets, whose accommodation in the apartments is absolutely free.

Villas on the coast

For those who prefer privacy, renting a house in the immediate vicinity of the coast will be the best choice. Becici beach can offer a number of villas, accommodation in which will be a real pleasure, but they should be booked in advance.

For example, an old five-bedroom stone mansion, well known locally as the "house with olive", is suitable for a large family or group of up to 10 people.





In the courtyard of the house, a bicentennial olive grows and bears fruit, but besides it, there is something to pay attention to:

  • two-level courtyard with dense greenery and a swimming pool;
  • 4 terraces for evening or afternoon relaxation, one of which is open (under the olive), and three covered with comfortable garden furniture;
  • well-kept garden, flower beds and lawns;
  • 2 equipped kitchens, 3 bathrooms;
  • sun loungers, barbecue area.

To the sea from the house with olive, only about 300 m on the road past expensive hotels where you can eat in restaurants if you do not want to cook. As noted by those who rested here, this villa is the best option for a secluded holiday with the whole family, when there are no people nearby except relatives, the sea and nature.

Island of st. Stefan

For customers who choose a luxury vacation, the best option would be a hotel located on the island of St. Stefan. Once there was a fishing village here, but today it is a hotel complex, fully occupying the island.

It is connected to the coast by a sandy isthmus and until the middle of the twentieth century, few people knew about it, since the island was inhabited by fishing families. After the government of Yugoslavia bought it and turned it into an elite vacation spot, celebrities such as Sophia Loren and Kirk Douglas, Princess Margaret and Indira Gandhi, Claudia Schiffer and Sylvester Stallone and many others rested here.





The island is only 6 km from Budva, and hotel guests can relax both on the coast near it and visit the Becici beach (Montenegro). For active people, the western part will be the best place to prove themselves.

Entertainment

Like all the best beaches, Becici offers its guests numerous attractions, including:

  • water skiing;
  • paragliding;
  • boat trips;
  • Beach football;
  • fishing, which will leave the best memories about itself;
  • riding a catamaran;
  • walks.

For lovers of thrills, bungee jumping from the highest bridge in Europe and descent along mountain rivers are suitable.
