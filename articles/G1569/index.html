Hotel Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): description and reviews

If you want to relax on the sea and get acquainted with the culture of Turkey, go to Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Here you can enjoy incredibly beautiful nature and high standards of service.

Briefly about the hotel

In 2006, the Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 * resort complex opened its doors to tourists. Thanks to the overhaul carried out in 2012, the furnishings and equipment of the hotel fully comply with modern standards and trends. The hotel area is quite small - 6000 square meters. m. There is a 5-storey building, designed for 109 guest apartments. The hotel is 55 km from Antalya International Airport.





In the city of Kemer, on Ataturk Boulevard, 38, there is a resort complex Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Reception staff will tell you how to get there if you contact them upon arrival. The easiest option is to order a taxi or hotel transfer. You can also get to Kemer by regular bus.

Accommodations

It is worth noting the comfortable living conditions that are provided to guests of the Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Kemer is a hospitable resort where you can enjoy a good rest. All rooms of this institution correspond to the standard category. There is one room equipped for the comfortable stay of people with disabilities. It is also worth noting the elegant interior of the rooms of Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Photo shows a modern concise design.





All apartments are equipped with the following amenities:

  • satellite TV with a package of entertainment and music channels, among which there are also Russian-speaking;
  • split system that allows you to cool or warm the air temperature in the room;
  • mini-bar, which is filled with alcohol and soft drinks for an additional fee;
  • a bathroom additionally equipped with an electric hairdryer;
  • a safe for storing valuables, which can be used for an additional fee;
  • landline telephone with direct access to the reception and with the possibility of making international calls;
  • free use of wireless internet;
  • Balcony with garden furniture and clothes dryer.

Infrastructure

Everything that a tourist may need in the process of living and relaxing can be found on the territory of the Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Here, the following infrastructure facilities are provided for guests:

  • a restaurant where you can enjoy traditional Turkish and international dishes;
  • 3 bars in which a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks is available to guests;
  • conference room for 75 people for business negotiations;
  • a medical office where guests can go in case of illness or injury;
  • Private parking, which guests can use for free;
  • hairdresser with male and female rooms;
  • outdoor and indoor swimming pools;
  • elevators in a residential building;
  • garden with walking paths, gazebos and barbecue grills;
  • night club on site;
  • private beach area with all necessary amenities;
  • a library with a large collection of literature (including in Russian);
  • a left-luggage office where guests can leave bulky and valuable luggage;
  • a business center where guests can access office equipment;
  • mini-market with a round-the-clock work schedule;
  • specially equipped smoking areas;
  • Internet cafe in the lobby area.




Opportunities for children

A great family vacation will give travelers Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Kemer , thanks to its mild climate and warm sea, has become one of the best places for families with children. For the smallest tourists, such opportunities are provided here:

  • pool with water slides and inflatable toys;
  • a mini club where kids can take part in game programs or creative activities;
  • the restaurant has a children's corner with comfortable furniture;
  • outdoor play area;
  • during your absence at the hotel, a qualified nanny can look after the children;
  • evening disco and theatrical show.

Guest Services

The quality service will delight its guests the resort complex Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. The overview of services is as follows:

  • delivery of orders from the restaurant to the rooms, as well as the formation of an individual diet menu;
  • organization and holding events of a solemn and business nature;
  • the possibility of renting vehicles (car or motorcycle);
  • safe box rental at the reception;
  • use of laundry services;
  • round-the-clock currency exchange;
  • providing transfer from the airport and back;
  • packed lunches for guests who, for whatever reason, missed a meal;
  • the ability to pay for received services using payment cards.




Dining at the hotel

The resort complex Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 * operates in accordance with the "All Inclusive" system, which means a carefree and good rest. The food in this hotel is as follows:

  • from 07:00 to 10:00 in the main restaurant of the hotel serves a traditional Turkish breakfast with lots of fresh fruits, vegetables and nutritious dishes;
  • for those who like to sleep longer or have already managed to get hungry, there is the opportunity to visit the brunch, which is held from 10:00 to 10:30;
  • lunch is served in the main restaurant from 12:30 to 14:00;
  • from 14:00 to 17:00, guests can have a snack at a mid-morning snack, which usually includes fruits, pastries and sour-milk products;
  • from 17:00 to 17:30 in the lobby bar you can enjoy pastries with hot tea and coffee;
  • from 19:00 to 21:00 you can enjoy a themed dinner, which includes dishes from around the world;
  • From midnight to 00:30 a night soup is organized.




Free entertainment

A lot of opportunities for organizing leisure guests provides Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Description of free entertainment is as follows:

  • a modern fitness center where you can conduct an independent training session or use the services of an instructor;
  • a steamy aromatic sauna or a traditional Turkish hammam will help you relax and improve your well-being;
  • playing table tennis outdoors;
  • darts in the game room;
  • daytime animation and evening shows;
  • chess, backgammon, checkers and other board games.




Paid entertainment

Some leisure facilities are available to guests at an additional cost. The list of these services is as follows:

  • a visit to the massage parlor, where qualified masters will spend relaxing, health and beauty treatments;
  • game of billiards subject to hourly pay;
  • water skiing;
  • windsurfing for guests with an appropriate license;
  • water parachute;
  • canoeing and catamarans;

Positive reviews

A lot of positive moments will give guests a resort hotel Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews of tourists note the following advantages of this institution:

  • convenient location relative to the sea;
  • a large enough pool with fun water slides;
  • comfortable atmosphere in the rooms; comfortable orthopedic beds;
  • spacious balconies with sea views;
  • the hotel is located near the central part of the city;
  • children's pool is equipped separately from an adult;
  • the restaurant menu has a lot of meat and fish dishes;
  • the buffet includes a huge assortment of confectionery products, among which a special place is occupied by traditional oriental sweets;
  • air conditioners work well (cool the air well and practically do not make noise);
  • Despite the schedule for changing bed linen, maids can remodel the bed according to your first requirement;
  • the staff is polite and helpful;
  • photos of the hotel presented on the official website are fully true;
  • clean and comfortable beach;
  • comfortable entry into the sea, strewn with sand mixed with small pebbles (depth increases gradually);




  • good quality room cleaning;
  • upon arrival, guests are immediately put on "All Inclusive" bracelets, even if they have not yet passed the registration procedure and have not managed to check into the room;
  • quality local beer;
  • beautifully decorated reception, as well as a cozy atmosphere in the lobby;
  • polite and sociable bartenders.




Negative reviews

Unfortunately, there were some negative points at the Lims Bona Dea Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews contain such negative comments:

  • unlike most Turkish hotels, visiting the hammam is possible only at an additional cost;
  • the repair in the room is quite old and shabby;
  • the shower is uncomfortably screwed to the wall, and the ability to adjust the direction of the water is not provided;
  • paid use of the safe;
  • maids do not clean the balcony (do not even wipe the furniture);
  • dirty dishes in a restaurant;
  • even in cold weather, hotel staff do not include pool heating;
  • despite the ban on smoking in the territory, in fact this rule is not respected, and therefore everywhere smells strongly of tobacco smoke;
  • the coastline is quite narrow, which is why the beach is constantly crowded;
  • dusty filter in the air conditioner;
  • in the evening it is impossible to open the windows in the room due to the loud music that plays near the pool, as well as in neighboring hotels;
  • a very small area in which there are practically no places free from development;
  • sun loungers both by the pool and on the beach are too close to each other;
  • rather passive and boring animators;
  • Russian-speaking administrators do not help solve problems, but, on the contrary, create them;
  • the lunch and dinner menus are almost identical;
  • the refrigerator emits an unpleasant odor;
  • employees can forget about a pre-booked transfer;
  • queues are often gathered in the dining room at the distribution;
  • animators sometimes behave too intrusively, forcing guests to dance and participate in sports activities;
  • water slides operate only a few hours a day;
  • both at the pool and on the beach there is an acute problem of the lack of sun loungers, which is why they have to be occupied even before breakfast;
  • obsessive sellers and travel agents walk along the beach to present their goods and paid services;
  • The TV works with strong interference.

General impression

For lovers of sea holidays this accommodation option is just perfect. The hotel is located just a few meters from the beach. In addition, in the immediate vicinity is the central part of the city. But for passive tourists who like to spend time in a hotel, this is not the best option: the territory is practically absent, and the atmosphere is quite old. As for nutrition, it is at a fairly high level, but without any frills.
