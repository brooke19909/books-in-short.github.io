Fortaleza, Brazil: description, history, attractions and reviews

Brazil is an exotic distant country that attracts the attention of tourists. One of the resort cities of the country is the famous Fortaleza (Brazil). In just a few decades, the city has turned from a simple fishing village into a modern recreation and tourism area. The main changes affected, of course, the coastal zone. Entertainment centers and hotels were built here. However, Fortaleza in Brazil is not only chic beaches, but also exciting excursions, attractions and all kinds of entertainment.

A bit about Fortaleza ...

Fortaleza (Brazil) is a fairly large city in the north-east of the country, spread out on the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. The population of the sinter is about 2.5 million people. Translated from Portuguese, the name of the city means nothing but a fortress.

The story of Fortaleza (Brazil) began in 1500, when the Spanish traveler Vicente Pinson with the team was the first among all Europeans to go to Brazil. A little later, at the beginning of the seventeenth century, the Portuguese built their fort here. Then for a short while the land passed into the hands of the Dutch.

The city of Fortaleza in Brazil was founded in 1726, it appeared on the site of a Dutch fort. And since 1799, it became the center of the whole province of Ceara. Throughout the nineteenth century, the region has been very actively developing, new educational institutions, enterprises and cultural objects have appeared here.



It is currently the fifth largest Brazilian city. In addition, Fortaleza is the capital of the state of Ceara. In recent decades, the city has become a popular tourist destination, and just a bright place in Brazil.

Resort Climate

The description of the city of Fortaleza (Brazil) should start with a mention of its climate, because it is he and the beautiful beaches that attract many tourists here. The city is located in a typical tropical climate. High temperatures and equally high humidity are observed here throughout the year. Sometimes the heat is tempered by oceanic winds. The hottest are January and December. The rainy season lasts from January to May. Fortaleza is surrounded by rainforests.

Description of Fortaleza (Brazil)

The cultural part of the city is the famous Drago do Mar district. There are a number of major museums. Among them, it is worth recalling the Museum of Art and the Memorial of Culture. Also nearby is a planetarium and a theater. Fans of outdoor activities will enjoy it, as there are entertainment complexes, playgrounds and amusement parks in the region.

In the city center, tourists can admire the old buildings that were recently restored and many of them currently have bars, shops and restaurants. Also in the center is the Cathedral of the Catedral Metropolitan, built in 1978, which rightfully received the status of the most beautiful in the whole city. The towers of the cathedral reach a height of 75 meters.

One of the most visited places in the city is the Jose de Alencar Theater, located in a park. It is no less interesting to visit the Ceara Museum, whose exposition consists of more than seven thousand copies. The ten halls of the museum feature unusual collections of clothing, jewelry, furniture and other antique items.





Another historical place can be called the house-museum of Jose de Alencar, which once belonged to the writer. It completely preserved the interiors and furnishings of those times. There is a large library and ancient manuscripts.

Boardwalk

One of the main attractions of Fortaleza (Brazil) are beautiful beaches. No wonder the city is recognized as a resort center throughout the country. The beaches of the region stretch along the coast for thirty kilometers. They are all interconnected by the incredibly beautiful promenade of Avenida-Beira-Mar. The embankment has a great length, it is interesting to walk along it not only during the day. She is especially good in the evening in the evening light of lights. In one of the establishments located on the boardwalk, you can not only have a meal in the afternoon, but also have a great time in the evening. The length of the famous promenade is about six kilometers. Its pedestrian part offers many cafes, clubs, restaurants, galleries and museums. By the way, there is a ski area on the promenade, so if you like active entertainment, then you are welcome to the promenade. Here you can ride on skates and other modern vehicles.

Beach vacation

According to reviews of Fortaleza (Brazil), Meireles Beach is considered the best in the city. A cobbled promenade was built along the coast. And against the backdrop of a beautiful beach, palm trees, modern buildings rise. Cafes, restaurants and other infrastructure facilities. Meyreles is a favorite place for surfers and fishermen. There is always a wind here, although it is not strong, but it is enough for riding on boards.

Mukuripi beach is a very lively tourist place with developed infrastructure. Mukuripi is connected to other sections of the coast by a promenade.

At 36 kilometers from the city is Kumbuko Beach. It is located in a fishing village. Bright greens, clear water and white sand attract crowds of tourists.



Iracema is a popular beach in the city, located in a very prestigious area. This section of the coast was named after the heroine of one of the works of the famous local novelist. The Indian girl Iracema became the symbol of all Fortaleza.

It is worth saying that the city has fifteen city beaches, the length of which is more than thirty kilometers. But it is Iracema that most attracts tourists.

Akirazh Riviera

The list of attractions of Fortaleza (Brazil) includes the Akiraj Riviera. This is a secluded and wild beach, which is perfect for a romantic and relaxing holiday. There is no intrusive infrastructure and hotels. Even fans of surfing and kitesurfing have not yet managed to take a fancy to the wild coast. Here, local residents always fish in small boats, setting off to sea in any weather.

Modern Fortaleza is a real city of contrasts. There are chic hotel complexes and slums nearby. Not all areas of the city are as attractive as the resort area. Where to stay in Fortaleza (Brazil)? The choice of hotels and hotel complexes in the city is incredibly rich. At one time, significant investments were made in Fortaleza for the construction of hotels. The color of the city

According to reviews of Fortaleza (Brazil), we can say that the locals are incredibly hospitable to tourists. In general, the atmosphere of fun reigns in the city. Residents of the city are always happy to welcome guests and with open arms welcome newcomers to their company. If you want to learn something about the city and its attractions, you should ask any Brazilian, you will certainly explain everything and recommend those places that are definitely worth a visit. Local residents are always interested in the life of foreigners, as soon as possible they try to ask guests about this, though they do it very delicately and unobtrusively.



The main feature of the city is its multinational composition. Residents are very responsive and do not tolerate any manifestations of racism. Brazilians are incredibly fun people. Fortaleza Attractions

Sights of the city of Fortaleza in Brazil will help diversify your vacation, diluting beach holidays with cultural trips.

Guests of the city should visit the old lighthouse, built back in 1827. It is one of the oldest buildings in Fortaleza. The lighthouse was opened in 1846, but twenty years later it became unusable. And in 1869 the building was spoiled by a fire that destroyed the lantern. After a while, everything was restored, but this was not enough for full work. In general, the lighthouse lasted another 85 years, after which it was replaced with a new one. Now the building houses the Museum of Sailors. In it you can see unique drawings and marine documents.

Recommendations from experienced travelers

Going to Fortaleza, you should find out what items you need to take with you. Tourists recommend stocking up with high-quality sunscreens. They will certainly come in handy here. You can sunbathe not only on the beach, but also during walks.

The water in the city from the water supply is completely unsuitable for consumption. Therefore, always buy drinking water in supermarkets. Vegetables and fruits bought in the markets must be washed thoroughly.



Fortaleza is a beautiful city, but do not forget about the high crime rate. Here every day not only numerous thefts, but also murders take place. Indeed, along with luxury tourist hotels in the city there are neighborhoods that are real slums. In such places, tourists should not appear at all. In addition, you should not walk around the city yourself after sunset. Locals do not recommend carrying significant amounts of money or jewelry. Going to the beach, try to leave all the most valuable in your room. Pocket thieves - this is a very big problem Fortaleza.
