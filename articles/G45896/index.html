Vacations in Vitino: features, attractions and reviews of tourists

Vitino is a cute resort town that is the dream of every vacationer. This is a picturesque corner of the Crimea with a warm sun and a magnificent sea breeze. Administratively, the city belongs to a resort called Evpatoria. From a geographical point of view, we can say that the village of Vitino is located in a unique place, because the city is protected on both sides by the Crimean Range and the Black Sea. Due to this picturesque location, a huge number of tourists come here every year.

From the center of Evpatoria Vitino is located 17 kilometers. Holidays in Vitino will bring a lot of pleasure even to the most experienced traveler.

What is famous for Vitino





The resort was renowned for its magnificent sandy beaches, where you can bask under the gentle summer sun. As you know, on most Crimean coasts, the beaches are covered with pebbles. Therefore, compared to other cities, Vitino is significantly different. Some come here solely for the sake of the beach, where you can enjoy basking in the warm sand, as well as take wellness treatments. The village gained particular popularity among couples and people who do not like too noisy entertainment. Therefore, in most cases, those who prefer a quiet and relaxing holiday gather here. In Vitino there are always a lot of vacationers with children.

Climate of the village

The climate of this region is relatively mild, subtropical. Mostly warm and sunny weather, with low humidity. People with breathing problems, cardiovascular system, and also nervous disorders come here very often to improve their health. Pleased with the winters, which are very warm in this resort. The temperature does not fall below 3 degrees. In summer, the temperature reaches 28 degrees.

Natural conditions

Natural conditions are very comfortable for a stay. Thanks to its proximity to the coastal strip, you can always enjoy the fresh sea air. The city has a large number of juniper landings. This combination has a positive effect on the respiratory tract, especially with colds. In addition to juniper, the city has many green spaces and beautiful flower beds. Due to the abundance of greenery and the absence of harmful industries, the microclimate of the city is very useful for human health. Holidays in Vitino in September are especially favorable. The air temperature drops, there is no such heat. This period is great for relaxing people of advanced age and families with small children. Therefore, this resort has received the status of a family.

Beach Condition





The magnificent sandy beach is probably one of the main reasons why you should visit this resort. Sand of golden color, a little pebbles and shells - a great combination for relaxation. And you can take away from here the most beautiful shell. The beach is very convenient for families with children. The thing is that the shallow water is 30 meters long, the child can safely swim and learn to swim. The water in the sea is always warmed up, the temperature is as pleasant as possible, the child will definitely not catch a cold. Since the beginning of May, the water is warm and you can absolutely comfortably swim without fear of catching a cold. The holiday season begins in April. All beaches are free, clean, equipped with showers, changing rooms and toilets. Always quiet and very cozy on the beaches of Vitino. Recreation facilities are located off the coast.

Historical information about the city





The village dates back to the 2nd century BC. It was such a date that was determined by archaeologists who, in the process of excavation, found tools and ancient writings here.

As a recreation area Vitino is only developing. There is a construction of hotels, cafes and other places of entertainment. The population of the village is only 1,000 people.

The city's attractions





If we talk about entertainment in Vitino, then there are practically none here, so young people can be bored. But some people find in this a number of positive aspects. For example, you can fully connect with nature, enjoy the horizons and silence. The village will allow you to take a break from city dust and fuss. In the evening, a particularly beautiful sky, a huge number of stars that seem so close. Some kind of entertainment is hunting, as well as underwater swimming. Spearfishing is also popular. Taking a mask and fins, you can catch a lot of mussels and crayfish. Near the village, the sea is quite shallow, so you can study the bottom at a depth of about 100 meters.

In addition, you can take a walk in the surroundings. One of the attractions is the Center for Long-Range Space Communications. The building looks like an object from a science fiction film. Also from the city you can go on a tour and explore the entire west coast of Crimea. Excursions are really interesting, especially for those who love history and beautiful nature. Therefore, those who want to find entertainment, still have something to do. Not so monotonous rest in Vitino. Guest reviews of the resort suggest that you can have fun here, especially in the circle of a large company.
