Picturesque place to relax

Bida is a forest lake. It is surrounded on all sides by birch and pine groves. Many residents of the Chelyabinsk and Sverdlovsk regions come here to relax. Here you can spend the weekend by setting up a tent. But those who prefer comfort over wild vacations may well be accommodated in cottages offered by the Quiet Dawns recreation center. Chelyabinsk and this quiet lake are seventy kilometers distant from each other, so many citizens come here for the weekend.

In the grove surrounding this forest lake, you can collect mushrooms, flowers, berries. For swimming and fishing on the lake there is a pontoon and a pier for boats.

In about. Bids are inhabited by crucian carp. Judging by the reviews, it is quite possible to catch a six hundred gram fish. It is in this idyllic corner that the recreation center "Quiet Dawns" (Chelyabinsk) is located.

Description

It was built in a small village. The recreation center "Quiet Dawns" (Chelyabinsk) appeared here relatively recently - in 2014. It is a comfortable cottage complex, open year-round. In summer, vacationers can simply walk through the forest, picking berries, fishing and, of course, swim, and in winter ride snowmobiles and steam in a Russian bath.

From Chelyabinsk to the recreation center you need to go north along the Yekaterinburg highway towards Lake Uvildy, turning from it at a bend to the village of Bidinsky.



Residence

The recreation center "Quiet Dawns" (Chelyabinsk) is two two-, three three- and one one-story cottages, designed respectively for eight to ten people. Several houses have their own infrared sauna and a separate four-meter pool. One-storey cottage is divided into four rooms with separate entrances, each of which can accommodate up to four vacationers.

In two- and three-story houses there is a spacious living room combined with a kitchen, all the necessary utensils, as well as a microwave, kettle, refrigerator. The dining and relaxation areas are visually separated from each other. There is a TV, comfortable furniture.

On the second floor there are two or three bedrooms. Bathrooms and showers are perfectly renovated. In front of each cottage there is a barbecue, a convenient area with benches and a table.

Services

For younger guests, which are often hosted by the recreation center "Quiet Dawns" (Chelyabinsk), a playground is arranged. Here, kids can ride a slide, play in a large sandbox, swing on a swing and have fun on an unusual ladder.

It offers guests an excellent Russian bath, which is designed for six to eight people. Her rent is not included in the price. On the territory there is a cafe, which in the winter season works as a bar. There is an outdoor pool that can be used in the summer, a volleyball court, a banquet hall for thirty to forty people. If desired, vacationers can rent an Xbox game console. For leisure, billiards and table tennis are also provided. In the winter season, vacationers ride in tubing - from a hill or behind a snowmobile on the lake.

Nutrition

This service is not included in the price. Living at the recreation center "Quiet Dawns" themselves cook their own food. For this, the cottages have a kitchen area with all the necessary utensils. Barbecue lovers can treat themselves to grilling meat or fish on the grill. Drinks can be bought at a cafe located on the shores of Lake Bida.



Cost

Accommodation in cottages per day depends on the day of arrival. On weekdays, the cost of a double room is from one thousand five hundred rubles. For a cottage for six to eight people, the price per day is from five and a half to seven thousand. Children under five years of age are free of charge, while for the provision of additional space for a child under twelve years of age, a fee of fifty percent is charged.

You can use the Russian bath on weekdays for eight hundred, on weekends for a thousand rubles. For renting ATVs, snowmobiles and a billiard table, you need to pay separately.

The price includes accommodation, parking, use of the barbecue and relaxation area in front of the cottage, children's and volleyball courts.

Many residents of the region come here for the weekend with large companies. And not only the residents of Chelyabinsk, but also Yekaterinburg can be found here. The remoteness of one hundred and seventy kilometers is not an obstacle for the latter. You can get to the cottage complex from Yekaterinburg along the Chelyabinsk highway in a southerly direction to Tyubuk. Then you need to go west towards Lake Uvildy. Two and a half kilometers from the children's sanatorium "Sun" and this recreation center is located.
