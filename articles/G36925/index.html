Hotel "Barcelona", Sudak: photos and reviews of vacationers

An unforgettable sea vacation will give you the Barcelona Hotel. Sudak is one of the most popular resorts of the southern coast of Crimea. Thanks to the stylish design solution, you can feel the real Spanish atmosphere.

Hotel Barcelona (Sudak) - description

Everyone dreams of a luxurious sea vacation. Comfortable living conditions and quality service will be provided by the Barcelona Hotel. Sudak is a popular Black Sea resort that attracts tourists with its natural and architectural beauties, which you can enjoy from the windows of your rooms. The hotel offers guests a choice of 27 apartments equipped with the latest technology.

The hotel has a spacious patio with a clean pool. This is just the perfect place for relaxing, as well as for weddings and other celebrations. It is also worth noting the presence of a spacious sun terrace, located directly on the roof of the hotel.





Where is the hotel located

A rather favorable location is characterized by the resort hotel "Barcelona". Sudak, street South-West, 89 - the address of this hotel. The road to the beach will take you about a quarter of an hour. But the main tourist attractions are in close proximity.

Many tourists prefer the hotel "Barcelona" (Sudak). How to get there If you arrived in the city by your own car, the reference will be the Genoese fortress, which is the main attraction of the city. Next you need to turn onto the Border Guards. If you arrived from Simferopol by bus, the hotel staff will meet you absolutely free and take you to the hotel.





Hotel Barcelona (Sudak) - guest apartments

At the Barcelona Hotel, guests have the opportunity to stay in one of the following rooms:

  • The standard offers double occupancy on large or twin beds. There is a private balcony with dryer for bathing suits and towels.
  • Junior Suite is furnished in a studio format. The large room combines a bedroom and a living room, as well as a small work area. There is a separate entrance hall with a wardrobe and a mirror.
  • Luxury apartments consist of two rooms. In the cozy bedroom you will be provided with a good rest, and the spacious living room is equipped with upholstered furniture, a coffee table and a TV. This option is great for families up to 4 people.

Room Amenities

The guest apartment features the following amenities:

  • small TV with cable and satellite channels;
  • free use of wireless Internet;
  • compact fridge with freezer;
  • air conditioning that will save you from the Crimean heat;
  • a safe to ensure the safety of your valuables and documents;

Fans of outdoor activities

Many tourists are attracted by such a destination as Sudak, Crimea. Hotel "Barcelona" takes into account all the wishes of guests. In particular, it provides all the necessary conditions for fans of outdoor activities.

There is a large swimming pool in the courtyard, as well as a sports field with natural grass lawn. Here you can do a morning run or just do exercises. It also periodically hosts football and volleyball matches between guests of this and neighboring hotels.

Since the hotel is located in close proximity to the Genoese fortress and other attractions, you will certainly be pleased with the opportunity to rent a bicycle for an exploratory walk.

Guest Services

Guests of the Barcelona Hotel are provided with the following list of services:

  • delivery of dishes from the restaurant to the rooms;
  • organization of meetings and seminars in a modern conference room equipped with the latest technology;
  • use of a free parking zone;
  • organization and holding of weddings and other special occasions;
  • if necessary, you can use the service of early registration or late check-out;
  • Express delivery;
  • the formation of lunch boxes.




Additional Information

Guests of this hotel will find it helpful to read the following information:

  • by prior arrangement, tourists can check into a room with pets;
  • for long stays in a hotel for the 21st day, no fee will be charged;
  • for corporate orders of ten rooms a 10% discount;
  • The newlyweds are provided with romantic-style rooms, as well as a pleasant surprise in the form of fruits and a bottle of champagne.




Positive hotel reviews

Quite a popular accommodation option in Crimea is the resort hotel Barcelona (Sudak). Reviews in 2016 indicate the following positive aspects:

  • warm and friendly atmosphere;
  • a large clean pool, which practically does not emit the smell of bleach;
  • the painstaking work of the designer on the design of rooms in a Spanish manner is visible;
  • very helpful staff;
  • excellent quality of food in the restaurant;
  • spacious and bright rooms with huge orthopedic beds;
  • from the windows of the apartments a bewitching view of the Genoese fortress;
  • impeccable quality of cleaning in the rooms;
  • Hotel staff can take you to the New World and back for free; great sports ground;
  • the restaurant can prepare dietary and vegetarian dishes for you, although they are not provided for on the menu;
  • through large and clean windows a lot of light penetrates into the rooms;
  • on the roof there is a large terrace where you can sunbathe and enjoy the beautiful views;
  • if you arrive late at night, you will certainly be fed dinner;
  • pleasant music is played in the courtyard all day;
  • the hotel has a small number of residents, and therefore there is no crowding and noise;
  • new and clean bedding;
  • rooms are cleaned only at the request of the guests, and therefore you can not worry that someone will enter the room in your absence;
  • Hotel staff can provide you with comprehensive information on local attractions;
  • the corridors of the hotel are decorated with beautiful bright paintings;
  • upon eviction from the hotel, a gift in the form of a bottle of local wine was a pleasant surprise.




Negative hotel reviews

Some flaws and negative aspects are characterized by the hotel "Barcelona" (Sudak). Guest reviews contain the following negative comments:

  • there is no canopy above the car parking, because of this, in the heat of the car they overheat very much, and after the rain they become very dirty;
  • go far enough from the hotel to the beach (the pool does not save the situation at all, because they go to the Crimea just overseas);
  • with the impressive cost of a breakfast room you have to pay extra (by the way, they are quite scarce);
  • the rooms are very old TVs with a small screen and disgusting image quality;
  • Wireless Internet signal is rather weak (practically does not catch in rooms);
  • the room has very cold tiled floors and slippers are not provided;
  • mold and disgusting smell of dampness in the bathroom due to poor ventilation;
  • there is no hot water in the mornings and evenings;
  • the hotel periodically stinks of sewage;
  • replacement of towels is very rare;
  • the football field is not lit, and therefore in the evening you will not play football;
  • the sockets are hidden behind the beds, and therefore they have to be moved away, for example, to charge the phone;
  • very cold water in the pool, and heating is not provided;
  • due to the remoteness from the beach there is a lack of a free shuttle;
  • Drinking water is brought to the room only on the day of arrival, after which you have to buy it yourself;
  • there are no signs on the way to the hotel, so you can easily get lost if you walk or drive your own car;
  • uncomfortable sunbeds by the pool;
  • the road to the sea goes uphill, and therefore it is quite problematic for the elderly, as well as vacationing with small children;
  • if for some reason you want to cancel the reservation, then the prepayment will not be returned to you, even if you cancel the room more than a week before the date of the expected arrival;
  • the restaurant opens around 10 in the morning, and therefore, if you leave early, you will most likely be left without breakfast.

General impression of the hotel

One of the best accommodation options in Sudak can be considered a resort hotel "Barcelona". Crimea gives tourists a warm sea and beautiful nature, but the service, unfortunately, has not yet reached the proper level. As for this institution, the guests call it a real oasis. Despite the remoteness from the beach and the city center, Barcelona is very popular among travelers. This is due to the luxurious design in the Spanish style, the conscientious attitude of the staff to their work and good conditions for entertainment and relaxation. Workers treat each guest as an old friend, and therefore they want to come back here again and again.
