Nutrition

Base "Kila Bay" (Pavlovka) offers several food options. Breakfast is included in the price, lunch and dinner need to be negotiated additionally. They are held at the local restaurant "Lighthouse". You can eat, have a bite and just have a drink in good company at the base in the night bar-club, in the summer - in the beach "Fish-bar" and in the sports bar "Attic".





For guests who prefer homemade food, all apartments are equipped with kitchen areas with the necessary appliances.
