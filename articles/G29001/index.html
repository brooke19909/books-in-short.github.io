What are the properties of camel hair?

In the old days, the power of all eastern states depended almost entirely on camels and their stamina. The territory of these countries is cut off from the outside world by miles of deserts that only such hardy animals can overcome. After all, a camel can replace a horse, a ram, and a cow. From him, a person could receive such valuable products as felt, milk and meat.

Camel's wool. Reviews The properties

Firstly, this material is very warm. First of all, this is a great insulator, which has the ability to constantly maintain body temperature. Many, probably, more than once thought about whether it was hot for a camel with such hair. In the heat, this animal feels more than comfortable. His coat in the sun can warm up to eighty degrees, while his body temperature will not exceed forty. In the storm and cold, the body of a camel will always remain dry, since the wool will provide not only the quickest absorption of moisture, but also its rapid evaporation.





Camel hair is an excellent temperature controller. It retains heat well and protects against moisture. Not without reason in our country in winter, clothes made of this material are popular. For example, a blanket can be a good New Year present . Camel hair will warm its owner even in the coldest winters.

Up to about one year, a camel has white coat. It is such fluff that is valued most of all, since it is the thinnest, soft and gentle. The camel's color changes as the outer hair grows. By the way, camel wool is not dyed, so the color palette of products made from it is not too wide. Judging by customer reviews, it is white camel hair that best shows its positive properties.

Camel Wool Pricing

Fabrics made from premium camel hair will cost about ten times more than similar but sheep. And all due to the fact that camel hair is very difficult and time-consuming to process. All products made from this material are highly appreciated because of their lightness, softness, hygroscopicity and durability. That is why you cannot buy a cheap blanket made from wool.





Camel wool is a valuable material for making clothes

The first thing that catches your eye when looking at a camel is two large humps covered with thick thick hair. It performs several functions at once. Firstly, camel hair protects its owner from sudden changes in temperature. The structure of the camel's hair is very heterogeneous. The outer hair is coarse and thicker, while the inner hair is fluff. The length of camel hair depends on the age of the animal. The older it is, the bigger it is. By world standards, the Mongolian camel hair is considered the highest quality. If you calculate, then on average from one beast you can get from 400 to 900 grams of fluff. This represents about eighty-three percent of all animal hair.





Camel wool products. Yarn

Today you can see on sale a variety of things made from this material. These are stoles, blankets, pillows and linens, mastresses and much more. In addition, this wool makes excellent clothes and the best yarn for knitting, from which then delightful items of women's, men's and children's knitwear are created. Camel yarn is popular with needlewomen. In addition, it is used in the textile industry.

A little bit about healing properties

Since ancient times, camel hair has been used by humans to treat diseases such as neuritis, arthritis, rheumatic pains and osteochondrosis. By warming the skin, camel hair improves blood circulation and dilates blood vessels. Thus, tissue repair is faster. In addition, for many centuries, people have used dressings for bruises and sprains.
