Belovskoe reservoir: description, ecological situation, rest

In the Kemerovo region in 1964, the Belovskoye reservoir was built, which is the second most important reservoir in this area. It was built in conjunction with the construction of the Belovskaya State District Power Plant in order to cool the worked water and drain sewage with it. And of course, for process water supply according to the reverse scheme.



Briefly about the reservoir

Belovsky reservoir is located on the river Inya. Its length is approximately about 10 kilometers, and its depth is almost five meters. Which is significantly larger than the rest of the reservoirs located in this region. The water level is regulated depending on the amount of precipitation and meltwater.

Belovsky Reservoir - an artificial reservoir. The local population calls it the sea or the Pearl of Kuzbass. This name is not accidental, because the impressive size of this object is simply amazing.

How to get there

In order to get to the reservoir, you need to get to the Belovsky district. It belongs to the Kemerovo region. This area captures the central part of this region. You must continue your way along the highway. It is necessary to get to the village of Insky, which is located very close. The road is good enough, so there should be no problems along the way.

Fishing

The Belovskoye Reservoir is simply an amazing place for fishing lovers who will not be left without a notable catch. Very often you can see people with fishing rods standing on the shore of a pond. However, if there is a boat, then you can swim deeper in order to catch larger fish.

Aquatic life and fauna are significantly diverse. Carp, trout, grass carp, silver carp, and catfish live here. And this is not all of the listed varieties of representatives living here. Water is in a fairly clean state, which is why the aquatic animal world will amaze even a professional angler.



Rest on the Belovsky reservoir

For tourists, all conditions are created here. This is a huge number of health camps, which attracts children and students, and various cottages, boarding houses and recreation centers. Not only locals come to these places to enjoy nature, but also tourists from all over the Kemerovo region. Here, every vacationer has the opportunity to go water-skiing or embark on a boat trip, taking a walk along the water surface of the reservoir. For those who wish to learn to surf in the territory opened a specialized school. Residents of Siberia are simply delighted with this place and consider it a truly paradise.

Ecological situation

But, despite all this, the environmental situation in these places leaves much to be desired. The Belovskoye Reservoir (reviews of local residents indicate an awaiting danger) is oversaturated with toxic waste, pollutants and bacteria. Every year, copper, lead, manganese, zinc, nickel, iron, chromium, nitrogen, and many other harmful substances fall into the reservoir. At the moment, tourists leave garbage and broken glass ashore, which makes the surrounding landscape quite unpresentable.
