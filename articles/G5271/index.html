From the 90s to the present

For many residents of the Ural region, the name "VUZ-Bank" is known firsthand. Back in 1991, several universities in Yekaterinburg, including SINH (Sverdlovsk Institute of National Economy), credit organizations and enterprises participated in the organization of "VUZ-Bank". Initially, the bank, in addition to the standard service of enterprises, was engaged in lending to special programs of universities of the Ural Federal District. After 12 years, the controlling stake in the bank became wholly owned by the Life financial group. "VUZ-Bank" (Yekaterinburg, Tyumen and some other cities of the Urals) began to provide various services to both individuals and corporate clients. A separate niche was occupied by small and medium-sized businesses.





The modern VUZ-Bank operates under the management of the Ural Bank for Reconstruction and Development.

License

Recently, many of the banks are faced with the issue of termination of activities in connection with bankruptcy. In this regard, such rumors often began to circulate about banks, which in fact have nothing to do with this situation. This problem was not bypassed by VUZ-Bank. His license (1557), issued by the Central Bank of Russia, is valid. In 2015, when the license was revoked from the parent bank (Probusinessbank) of the Life financial group, many customers were worried that VUZ-Bank was waiting for a similar fate. Tyumen was worried no less than other cities. But the credit organization was saved by reorganization. The insurance and deposits agency selected Ural Bank for Reconstruction and Development as a sanatorium from several applications of financial institutions.





Basic services for individuals

VUZ-Bank provides a wide range of standard banking services for private clients. These are loans and deposits, credit and debit cards, various payments (utilities, tuition, fines, etc.) and the possibility of transfers without opening an account, a mobile bank and a call center.

But besides all this list of services there are also individual additional options. So, the photo payment service allows you to quickly pay a paper bill without making any details. And Life Wallet is an opportunity to make payments directly from home, by analogy with the Yandex.Money service.

"VUZ-Bank" (Tyumen, Surgut, Nizhnevartovsk and all other cities where the bank operates) offers insurance services: life, health (including abroad), at home, etc.

Basic services for legal entities provided by VUZ-Bank

Tyumen is not the city with the largest number of offices, but this does not lose its significance and offers various organizations a wide selection of banking services. In addition to settlement and cash services, loans and overdrafts, deposits and bank guarantees, small and medium-sized businesses in the Urals region can use a salary project and a corporate card, and connect retail or mobile acquiring.





In addition, if desired, a legal entity can join the Club Business and receive additional service options. The club will help to acquire new business contacts and get advice from specialists in the field of HR, jurisprudence and taxation.

Depending on the field of activity, organizations may offer an individual work scheme. For startups, this is one set of services, for trading companies - another, for manufacturers - the third.

Credits of VUZ-Bank

The most popular type for a long time remains the consumer loan "People's". The amount can be issued from 5 thousand to 1 million in rubles for a period of up to 7 years with the possibility of early repayment. The age of the borrower must be at least 20 and not more than 80 years. Up to 200 thousand rubles, you can get a loan in just two documents. Such conditions are offered to its private customers by VUZ-Bank. You can apply for a loan by phone, online or in person at a bank branch.

For business, "VUZ-Bank" offers express loans for up to 5 years. The maximum amount is calculated individually, but cannot exceed 5 million rubles.
