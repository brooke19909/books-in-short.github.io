The best hotels of Pitsunda by the sea: a list, description and reviews of tourists

In Pitsunda there are several large pensions, while mini-hotels and private hotels of Pitsunda on the seafront prevail, they are mainly located in the village of Ldzaa (a suburb of Pitsunda), as well as on Rybzavodskaya, Nozadze, River. The cost of living per day is in the range of 1000-3500 rubles. depending on the time of year.



Large pensions may include meals in the city of Pitsunda. Hotels on the beach, prices for accommodation in which are usually slightly lower, provide the opportunity to eat here for a fee. The advantages of private establishments include lower prices, as well as the presence of small cozy courtyards with places for barbecue and relaxation, fruit trees.

1. Hotel "Iren"

Considering the best hotels of Pitsunda by the sea, it is worth highlighting this comfortable and modern hotel, which opened 4 years ago, and today it offers its guests a wonderful vacation and a high level of service. The hotel is 400 meters from the central part of the city, while the sea coast and the local nature reserve are 150 meters away.



Judging by the reviews, nearby there is all the tourist infrastructure: bars, shops, restaurants, cafes, discos, promenade, etc.

"Irene" is a complex that consists of two six-story buildings, in which there are rooms of the categories "lux" and "standard". Hot and cold water supply is constant, which is very convenient for vacationers.

Cost: from 1000 rubles per day.

6. Hotel Pshada

Considering the hotels of Pitsunda by the sea, it should be noted the new hotel "Pshada", which is located in the village of Ldzaa, a hundred meters from the spacious and clean beach. The institution is a cottage complex, which is located in the spa part of the pine reserve. From here it is only 30 minutes walk to the city center and its main attractions. Judging by the reviews, the area surrounding the institution is an amazing corner of nature with healing air, a peaceful environment and the conditions necessary for people who prefer a measured rest. The hotel is open from May to November.



The complex consists of 2 two-story cottages. The total number of rooms is 40. The rooms have a refrigerator, TV, coffee table, kettle, beds, bedside tables, air conditioning, a bathroom. It offers guests a restaurant-dining room, parking, pebbly and sandy beach, table tennis and a summer cafe.

Cost: from 1400 rubles per day.

7. Park Hotel Ostrov

In the park-hotel Ostrov, vacationers are accommodated in small cottages located on the coast of a clean lake. Other Pitsunda hotels cannot boast of this - there are a great many of them on the seashore, but in this wonderful place there is only this establishment. The complex is located one and a half kilometers from the sea, in a closed and protected area. From here, the center of Pitsunda can be reached by private car in 5 minutes, therefore, all the infrastructure of this Abkhaz resort is available to guests.

Reading reviews, you can find out that cottages are surrounded by a lake with swans and a green park, as well as natural landscapes of incredible beauty. This place offers a secluded and relaxing holiday. Park Hotel Ostrov does not have its own beach, while guests can use the small pebble city, which is located nearby.

Cost: from 1000 rubles per day.
