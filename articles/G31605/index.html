The best hotels in Rostov Veliky: description, traveler reviews and photos

Rostov the Great is one of the oldest Russian cities. It is located on the shores of Lake Nero.

The first annalistic mentions of this settlement date back to 862. A huge number of architectural monuments and Orthodox shrines are concentrated here. This makes the city attractive to travelers. The best hotels in Rostov the Great will provide guests with a warm welcome.

Hotel "Moscow Tract"

Among the hotels of Rostov the Great in the city center, one of the most popular is the Moscow highway. It is located 600 meters from the railway station at 29A Okruzhnaya Street. This hotel provides guests with such benefits:

  • Good location near a large park and a picturesque pond.

  • 51 rooms of different categories of comfort, equipped with all necessary amenities. The cost of living is from 2000 rubles per day.

  • Free parking under video surveillance. Designed for 20 cars and 2 buses.

  • Meals in a luxurious hotel restaurant with a dining room for 100 people and a summer terrace for 50 people. Breakfast - 250 rubles, business lunch - 200 rubles.

  • Organization of banquets in the hotel restaurant, as well as catering.

  • Two modern conference rooms for 70 and 100 people.

Reviews

About this hotel in Rostov the Great, guests leave such approving reviews:

  • The hotel has a relaxed, homely atmosphere;
  • beautifully decorated rooms and public areas;
  • the staff works very unobtrusively, but at the right time is always ready to help;
  • good location;
  • beautiful architecture;
  • good quality food in the restaurant;
  • delicious and hearty breakfasts.

And such comments:

  • terminals for paying for services with plastic cards often fail;
  • the rooms located next to the restaurant are usually very noisy;
  • instead of double beds, most rooms have shifted twin beds;
  • in the vicinity of the hotel it is unsafe in the dark;
  • old bath towels.

Hotel "Selivanov"

If you are looking for the best hotel in Rostov Veliky, pay attention to the Selivanov Hotel. The institution is located at: Okruzhnaya street, 5, on the shores of Lake Nero. The institution is located in an old building, which is an architectural monument and has a rich history. The following benefits are available to guests:

  • 25 cozy rooms of different categories of comfort. The cost of living is from 1300 rubles per day (on weekdays) and from 1700 rubles per day (on weekends).

  • Access to free wireless internet throughout.

  • Meals at the restaurant "Paris", designed for 55 seats (plus a summer terrace for 60 seats), and the coffee shop "Comme il faut", designed for 20 seats.

  • Sauna with a pool.

  • Private car parking with video surveillance.

  • Ability to stay with pets.

  • Blacksmithing workshops (forging horseshoes). Cost - from 250 rubles per person for 1-2 hours.

Hotel "Russian Compound"

Among the hotels in Rostov the Great, such an institution as the Russian Compound, located at 9 Marshal Alekseev Street, is quite popular. The hotel is located in a historic building. In addition, it has the following advantages:

  • 40 rooms for 84 guests. The rooms have an exclusive design and modern facilities. The cost of living is from 2600 rubles per day.
  • Own museum, thanks to which you can learn about peasant life.
  • Bath complex with a Russian and Finnish steam room, lounge, swimming pool, extreme shower and other recreational opportunities. The cost is from 1200 rubles per hour (on weekdays) and from 2000 rubles per hour (on weekends and holidays).
  • Food in the restaurant of Russian cuisine.
  • Organization of banquets and holidays on a turn-key basis.

Reviews

After examining the reviews about this hotel in Rostov Veliky, we can conclude about such positive aspects of this institution:

  • sane rates for accommodation and related services;

And such negative ones:

Hotel "Yaroslavna"

In search of a hotel with a swimming pool in Rostov Veliky, it is worth paying attention to the cozy Yaroslavna complex. This is a country hotel located at the village of Lions, 1B. To get here, you need to set the coordinates 57.1474152 and 39.3423843 in the navigator. The institution has the following advantages:

  • 12 cottages that can accommodate up to 110 guests. The cost of living - from 3500 rubles per day on weekdays and from 4300 rubles per day on weekends.

  • Gift shop with exclusive ceramic figures and designer stuffed animals.

  • A mini-farm with a contact zoo, a garden, a vegetable garden and a museum of peasant life.

  • Meals in the restaurant of Russian cuisine "Traktir", designed for 60 seats.

Reviews about "Yaroslavna"

Hotel "Manor Pleshanova"

  • Meals in a restaurant with two dining rooms (for 70 and 80 people). In summer, there is an outdoor terrace for 20 people and gazebos.
  • Conference room with 80 seats.
  • Sauna with a pool.
  • Organization of weddings and celebrations.
  • Organization of historical, artistic and educational programs.
  • Massage room.
  • Own museum.
