Tablet electronics failed

Each gadget, including Lenovo tablets, is a small box inside which there is an electronic filling of the device. It can fail due to many non-mechanical reasons: a power surge, high humidity, or even factory defects. Most of these failures are covered under warranty.

But sometimes even problems with electronics can be solved at home. Many people wonder: if the Lenovo tablet does not turn on, what should I do? For example, when did a gadget fall into the water? If you do not react quickly, the electronic "filling" of the tablet will not be restored.





When the device has entered the water, you must:

  • If possible, remove the battery.
  • Dry the gadget with a hairdryer.
