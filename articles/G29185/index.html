"Alara Park" 5 * (Turkey, Alanya): hotel infrastructure, description of rooms, prices

How do people imagine a holiday in the southern resorts? This is necessarily a beautiful sandy or sandy-pebble beach, which is pushed by the warm waves of the Mediterranean Sea. A light breeze that brings life-giving coolness to the shining shore. The high clear sky, the sun and the greens surrounding home-like cozy hotel buildings. All this is found by tourists coming to Alara Park Residence Hotel.

Location

The hotel buildings are surrounded by emerald green palm trees. Everything is drunk with the aroma of a pine forest and citrus trees. Nothing violates the silence and does not interfere with the rest of the guests. The nearest airport is about 100 km away. Alanya is the beautiful heart of Antalya, a 20-minute drive away, and the Avsallar settlement is a half-hour leisurely walk. Due to its remoteness from large industrial cities and large highways, Alara Park is an environmentally friendly place for recreation.

It was built at the end of the twentieth century. next to the famous Incekum beach. The name of this beach, with a length of more than 7 km, is fully consistent with its name. Finer sand is nowhere to be found. The beach is just perfect for a family holiday, not only by the coastal territory, but also by a convenient entrance to the water. The sea is so clear that every grain of sand is visible at the bottom.



Guests can sit at a table in the restaurant and enjoy a meal with sun glare on the surface of the sea waves. And small sweet tooth tired of swimming, and their parents on the shore are waiting for numerous cafes "Alara Park 5 *". Turkey is famous for its affordable prices, and cafes with restaurants are no exception.

Tourist walks

Avsallar is a place with a rich history. For tourists who come not only to soak up the sun and swim in the Mediterranean Sea, the Alara Park residence hotel organizes a variety of excursions and entertainment. All places of visit were selected near the boarding house itself and are designed for short trips.

1. These are day and evening boat trips in the Mediterranean:

  • During day trips, the guide tells the mysterious stories of the remaining overboard surroundings. The yacht enters the most picturesque places. In quiet safe bays, travelers, heated by the southern sun, are allowed to walk and cool under the canopy of trees, as well as swim. Returning on board, guests will enjoy a buffet lunch and the continuation of a fascinating walk. In addition to the guide, there is always an animator on board the yacht that does not let you get bored.
  • During evening shows, where the children's contingent is already excluded, a variety of drinks are offered and discos are held. These walks end no earlier than three nights.

2. The mountains around the resort abound in mountain rivers, in rafting along one of them Alara Park invites its vacationers to take part. The route is very well thought out. There are difficulty levels designed for adults and strong people, and there are those that children of five years old can handle.

3. In addition to the entertainment that Turkey suggests, the 5-star Alara Park Hotel and its employees recommend tickling their nerves with diving. The first dive takes place to a depth of not more than five meters. If it is tolerated normally, then after lunch and rest, a deeper one takes place.

4. Visiting historical monuments in Alanya is also one of the most chosen holiday trips. And everyone must go on an excursion to the archaeological museum.



The territory of the complex

Between the six buildings that make up the Alara Park 5 * hotel (Turkey), there are three outdoor pools for adults and two for children. There is also an indoor pool. If visitors do not want to go to the sea, then they can lie down and sunbathe on the sun loungers by the pools. Since the resort is always sunny, in order to avoid overheating, there is always an umbrella near each sun lounger. Children and adults can ride a water slide, play basketball, soccer, volleyball and even compete in accuracy, playing darts.

Beach area

If guests decide to take a walk to the beach, then they will find sun loungers with umbrellas and mattresses. Necessarily fresh and soft towels. And a free bar with refreshments.

Dining at Alara Park

Excellent restaurants and cafes, bars and snack bars are located at Alara Park 5 * (Turkey). It is impossible to go on vacation at this hotel and not try the delicious dishes prepared by local chefs. Turks are great cooks, and cooking is treated like art.



Breakfast is an introduction to a new day. Do not be surprised if you are offered a large number of high-calorie dishes and do not give your beloved coffee. A traditional breakfast consists of crispy freshly baked bread or flat cakes with butter and several varieties of cheese, vegetable salad, scrambled eggs and freshly brewed aromatic tea, which you can choose from honey and a variety of jams, and of course, a huge amount of fruit for every taste. Coffee in Turkey for breakfast is not accepted.

You can, of course, go to the cafe in the morning and cheer up with the usual mug of drink with puff pastry with minced meat or with freshly baked buns stuffed with olives.

For lunch, it is worth ordering soup. It does not even matter which one you choose, the main thing is that it be a soup. They are prepared as mashed soup, and can be served not only for lunch, but also as a separate dish for dinner. Among other dishes, grilled lamb ribs should be ordered. This is not fatty, but surprisingly tasty and aromatic meat, having tasted it once, you will never forget its taste. And most importantly, when ordering meat, you can not pay for the side dish, it is always attached as an addition.

Dishes served in restaurants are striking in variety and can satisfy the most sophisticated tastes. And all the more it is pleasant to accept the offer of the owners of Alara Park 5 * (Turkey) and visit each restaurant for free once during a vacation, after tasting various dishes there.

Small sweet tooth (and not only small ones) must visit a candy store. Turks are a nation that adores various sweets. Many desserts that are so popular all over the world come from Turkey. From one of their names is breathtaking. This is baklava with halva, Turkish delight and pastille, twitter and hundreds of other names, the taste of which is familiar from distant childhood. In addition to the usual names of dishes, there are many unknown names. They also need to try, because they have no analogues in the world.

In "Alara Park" 5 * (Turkey) prices are very affordable. On holidays, rooms must be reserved in advance, as otherwise places may not remain. It is also difficult to buy tickets for summer holidays.
