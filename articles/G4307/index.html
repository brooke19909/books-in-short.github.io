User's manual

After reading the instructions for the Carrier air conditioner, you will understand how to operate it in order to avoid damage. For example, if you encounter a problem that is expressed in equipment failure to work, you need to check the main switch and make sure that the power cord is not damaged. Sometimes a clogged filter also indicates such a problem.

If the air conditioner does not respond to commands, you must unplug the plug and then insert it back. Carrier air conditioning has a display with which you can monitor the operation of the device. If you see the ES designation, this may indicate an unstable power supply. The plug from the outlet in this case is removed. You must wait 10 minutes, and then restart the unit again.

By studying the errors of the Carrier air conditioner, you will find out that the letters HB may appear on the display. They indicate that the condensate can is full. The plug is removed from the outlet, the canister is empty, and after three minutes the equipment can be restarted.





Air filters are recommended to be cleaned every 30 days. They are located at the back of the device. Before this, the unit must be disconnected from the power supply. To remove the filters, you need to press on the brackets of the grill and remove the board, only after that you can start cleaning. For this, running water or a vacuum cleaner is usually used. After drying, the filters are installed in their place. Carrier air conditioners can also be washed outside. To do this, use soapy water and soft rags. Do not use flammable liquids and spray.

Condensate must be drained using a tube located at the rear of the unit. After completing this operation, the plug closes and the handset returns to its original position. If you do not plan to use the unit for a long time, then it must be disconnected from the power supply. Water drains, and then closes the plug.
