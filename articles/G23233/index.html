How to make a ball of flowers? Our master class will help you

Author's decor is always exclusive and unique. Having done a handmade thing, you will not be disappointed. After all, any ideas and ideas can be realized. How to make a ball of flowers? Nothing is impossible! Flower balls are a universal decoration for any celebration. They can also serve as a worthy alternative to the classic bouquet.

You will need such materials:

- flowers (from paper, fabric);

- foam ball;

- glue gun;

- tape.

How to make a ball of flowers? First, put a drop of glue on any point of the ball and glue the tip of the tape. Then wrap the tape around the ball and glue it to the other end.



Apply hot glue to the base of the flower and press firmly to the base. Arrange the products in a checkerboard pattern and make sure that no gaps are visible.







If desired, you can decorate the decoration with beads, small flowers, leaves. How to make a ball of flowers? You have the answer to this question and all the possibilities for creating this flower miracle.

Probably, each of us was in a situation where you need to choose an original gift, but there are no ideas. Flower arrangements are a win-win. Indeed, today there are many offers and options for gifts in this category. But how to make a toy of flowers yourself? You just need to purchase the necessary materials and be patient.

Here are the basic steps for creating a flower toy:

  1. Draw a model of the toy on paper, carefully calculate the amount of detail.
  2. Transfer the drawing to an oasis (floral sponge) using a marker.
  3. Carefully cut the parts from the sponge (a sharp clerical knife is best). Then fasten them with floristic wire.
  4. Prepare the flowers for work: make the tip of the stem (it should be at least 3 cm) sharp. Soak the blank from the oasis with water.
  5. Carefully start fixing flowers. Do this so that no gaps are visible. Combine flowers of different sizes and colors to make the toy look realistic. Eyes, nose is best done using buttons.


If the flower toy is properly looked after, it will be able to please its owners for at least three weeks. Avoid direct sunlight and dust. You need to water the composition every day, starting from the top.

How to make paper flowers with your own hands? There are many options for creating them. They will serve as a wonderful decoration both at home and any celebration. Here is one of the options for creating a fantasy flower of needlewoman Maria Stuart.

Step 1. Print a sheet of mail paper with the pattern you like (it is very thin, so attach it to a standard sheet).



Step 2. Cut 8 small rectangles. Fold each of them along in half, smoothly cut the edge, forming a semicircle. Form the petals.







Step 3. Wrap the stamens with a strip of corrugated paper.



Step 4. Form a flower from the petals and stamens, wrap the stem with paper or tape.



The flower is ready! You can make the right amount and form a bouquet or use it as a decorative element.

Now you know how to make a ball, a toy or a flower out of flowers, and you can please yourself and loved ones with handmade gifts.
