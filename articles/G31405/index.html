About VEKO

The VECO brand is Turkish and has existed since 1997. However, the lion's share of production is made in Russia. How is this possible? Very simple. In 2006, the first plant was launched on the territory of the Russian Federation. The company has always been engaged in the production of household appliances. These were refrigerators, microwaves, washing machines and much more. However, over time, the brand began to produce smaller items: hair dryers, toasters and more. It is worth noting that the company is doing well. Their products are actively sold on the Russian market. Refrigerators and washers are in very steady demand. Sometimes in production they do not even have time to produce new batches. The percentage of factory defects is by no means low. But this does not affect popularity. Apparently, the whole point is that products are one of the most affordable. All is sold at very reasonable prices. No overpayment for the brand. An example of such a product is a BEKO WKB 51001 M. automatic washing machine . Reviews and photos will be presented a little later. In the meantime, consider the appearance of the technique.

The top cover of the washing machine is removable. This is done specifically in the expectation that the machine will be built into the kitchen. Also, the machine body has partial leakage protection. Energy and water consumption. Noise level

We continue to disassemble this interesting washing machine. What is it with power consumption? How economical is it? It should be noted that this device belongs to the energy class A +. This means that with a fairly high specific power, the machine is quite economical. In numbers, it will be 0.17 kW \ h. Now about the consumption of water. For one wash it takes only 41 liters. This is much less than the competition. For many, such efficiency will be a decisive factor when choosing a washing machine. Now about the noise level. In standard washing mode, the machine does not make loud noises. The noise level is only 59 dB. It is not enough. But when the spin turns on, then the noise increases sharply. Key Specifications
