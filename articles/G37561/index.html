Resort Hotel "Barut Lara" (Turkey, Antalya): description, service, reviews. Barut Lara 5 *

Antalya is a huge resort center in Turkey. The most luxurious hotels are located here, in which vacationers can feel like real kings. One of them is Barut Lara, which has the status of "five stars" and, according to most travelers, is fully consistent with it.

general information

The hotel was built recently - in 2005. The authors of his project in the process of creating an architectural idea thought out, it would seem, all the possibilities that can contribute to a comfortable stay of tourists in his rooms. That is why all the equipment and furniture that is used here is modern, from the point of view of recreation there are many improved ideas. On the territory, work is regularly carried out to improve the quality of accommodation, one of the last was carried out in 2009, when a modern climate system was installed throughout the territory of Barut Lara (Turkey).

Location

"Barut Lara" (Turkey) is located in the heart of outdoor activities and fun - in Antalya. It is located almost on the coast of the Mediterranean Sea, which is warm for most of the year. In the midst of the spa season, the water temperature here is about 25 degrees, which is very comfortable for tourists.

If we talk about transportation routes, the largest airport is literally 10 km from the hotel, which is very convenient for tourists who arrive from other countries.

Rooms

Barut Lara Hotel offers tourists almost 500 apartments of various categories: standard, deluxe, suite, as well as superior rooms, which are defined in the Junior, Presidential and Penthouse groups. Despite the categories, each room is comfortable in its own way.

Each of them has at least one bathroom, in which, in addition to high quality sanitary ware, bath accessories are also provided. There is also a hair dryer for drying hair.

"Standard"

Apartments "standard" are conditionally divided into several categories, depending on what can be seen from their windows. Of these, as a rule, an overview opens on the sea coast, on the mountains, as well as on the gardens. These rooms can accommodate up to 3 guests - this is the number of beds provided here. In addition to modern beds, standard rooms also have cabinets in which you can not only store things, but also use their surfaces as stands for a tea substation. Separately, there is a coffee table and a couple of chairs - here you can drink tea.

As for the technical equipment of the rooms, they have one large TV connected to satellite channels, as well as a DVD player. If necessary, guests can use safes as well as telephones. A minibar is also installed in the rooms, which, if used, is filled with soft drinks.



Deluxe

The windows of the deluxe apartments of the Barut Lara Hotel (Turkey) offer wonderful views of the sea coast, which you can also enjoy while staying on the balcony. Here, on an area of ‚Äã‚Äã50 square meters. m can accommodate up to 3 people. As a sleeping place there is a large French bed, as well as a folding sofa. The peculiarity of such apartments is that there are three sun loungers on the balcony, where guests can relax and sunbathe.

"Suite"

The suites offer a very comfortable environment for four guests. They are located not relatively spacious territory of 50 square meters. m - this area is divided into two rooms, each of which represents a sleeping area.

In total, the room has two double beds, as well as an equivalent number of cabinets for things, calculated on the number of guests. Each bedroom has a plasma TV and DVD player. All apartments of this group have a private balcony, where there is furniture for relaxing in the form of chairs and a coffee table, in some of them the balcony is replaced by an open sun terrace, where everything is equipped for sunbathing.



Superior rooms

Barut Lara 5 * offers several options for apartments of a higher level of comfort. They are designed for 2-3 people but, despite this, have a fairly large area - from 50 to 70 square meters. m, which is divided into several zones, usually a living room and one that is designed for relaxation. Without fail, apartments of this class have an outdoor terrace with furniture for relaxing.

In the sleeping area there is a huge French bed designed for two. There is also a large folding sofa, which, if necessary, can serve as an extra bed. The bedroom has a wardrobe.

In apartments of this kind in the living area you can conduct business meetings and negotiations, there are tables and armchairs designed for several people. The windows of the room are covered with blackout curtains, which not only can protect guests from the blinding sunlight, but also give the interior a more luxurious, rich look.

Electronics in these rooms are the most modern and high quality. Here in each room there is an individual air conditioning, as well as a TV with the ability to view satellite channels. On a free basis, guests staying here have the opportunity to use the safe in order to store valuables, a minibar, and also a telephone connected to the international line. On an ongoing basis, uninterrupted access to the Internet.

Conditions for children

In many official descriptions, the Barut Lara hotel is positioned as a place that is ideal for families. The thing is that here there is not only everything necessary for a comfortable stay for adults, but also safe conditions for children, as well as places for their pastime.

So, in Barut Lara 5 * there is a well-equipped children's club where kids can have fun in the company of their peers, here they are entertained by a team of funny animators, many of whom are Russian-speaking. Parents can also order babysitting services, which are paid hourly.

In the open area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel there are two children's pools, the niches of which are made in accordance with all international standards. In particular, their coating prevents slipping, and the depth is shallow, which ensures the safety of young guests. Also not far from it there is a small playground for active pastime of children.

In the rooms, if necessary, a cot can be installed - this is done completely free of charge, on request.



Entertainment

In "Barut Lara" (Turkey) regularly hosts show programs with the participation of animators and guest artists of different genres. As a rule, events of this kind can be visited in the evening - at this time discos are held on the territory of the aquazone and DJs, dancers, as well as circus performers perform.

Throughout the day, the guests are entertained by a team of animators, whose members can find an approach to a guest of any age category. During the daytime, they organize competitions in the aquatic zone or on sports fields in which hotel guests take a direct part.

If desired, vacationers can visit separate halls of interest: a billiard room, a table tennis club or a chess room.



Nutrition

The hotel offers all-inclusive catering facilities. A buffet is organized every day in the main restaurant Palma, which serves dishes prepared according to the best culinary traditions of Turkey and, in particular, Antalya. In addition to the main one, there are five more restaurants where it is possible to taste Italian, Far Eastern, Mediterranean and international cuisines.

If desired, guests of the hotel can also visit bars, which are five in Barut Lara (Antalya). They are located in the aquazone area, on the beach, as well as in the hotel building. In such places you can order delicious snacks and various drinks, among which there are both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. If you want to taste branded Turkish desserts, you need to go to the confectionery, which is located in the hotel building - here you can offer cakes, pastries, cookies and real oriental sweets, which will surely appeal to the majority.

Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle will not remain indifferent to the opportunity to do their body and health at the Barut Lara Hotel (Turkey). Everyone here can visit the gym, where it is offered the possibility of training on different units that affect different parts of the body. Vacationers can also visit sports fields designed for playing volleyball or basketball, there is a beautifully arranged football field and even a golf course. Tourists pay special attention to the game of tennis, for which there are three fields in Barut Lara (Antalya).

SPA

SPA center occupies a special place in the leisure organization system for tourists who relax in this place. It is quite large in size and is divided into the thermal part, pool, massage room, as well as an office for cosmetic procedures.

The thermal zone includes a sauna and hammam. There is also a pool and jacuzzi. If desired, tourists can take advantage of the services of parmeisters, whose team works around the clock.

As for the massage room, then all the services provided in it are paid, but can please guests with their variety. A number of cosmetic procedures can also be performed here in the form of peeling, body wraps, etc.



Tourists reviews

All guests who spent their holidays at the Barut Lara hotel often respond very positively about their time in their reviews. Travelers say that the apartments provided for living are very comfortable, the interior is expensive, which is often reflected in photos taken by guests.

The service at Barut Lara, according to the guests, is very high. Only professional administrators work here, who are attentive to each guest, listening to all the wishes and fulfilling them. Special attention deserves the cuisine presented in restaurants and bars. According to travelers, these establishments serve nutritious and beautiful dishes. Vacationers recommend paying special attention to seafood dishes that have a special taste.

Barut Lara in Turkey is a hotel that attracts a large number of vacationers not only with a high level of conditions and service, but also with relatively inexpensive prices.
