Hotel Eden Spa Resort 5 * (Sri Lanka): description and photos

If you want to discover exotic Sri Lanka and decide to stay in a comfortable hotel with comfortable spacious rooms and a high level of service, then perhaps the Eden Resort Spa 5 * (Beruwela) will be a suitable option for you. We invite you to find out together about what awaits tourists here.





Location

The five-star hotel in question is located in Beruwela (Sri Lanka) on the Indian Ocean. The distance from Eden Spa Resort 5 * to Colombo is 56 kilometers, and to the nearest international airport - 87 kilometers. According to our compatriots having a rest here, the way to the hotel from the air harbor takes about two and a half hours. The place where the Eden Spa Resort is located is rightfully considered one of the most picturesque on the entire southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. So, here the Bentota River flows into the Indian Ocean. Experienced travelers are advised to go here from December to April. When monsoons arrive, the water in the river and ocean near the hotel becomes unclear.





General information, photo

Eden Resort Spa 5 * (Sri Lanka) was built in 1995. In 2013, a large-scale reconstruction was made here. So today, guests have the opportunity to stay in comfortable, updated rooms, equipped with modern furniture and appliances.

The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 158 apartments, located in a four-story building, equipped with elevators for the convenience of guests.

According to travelers, this Eden Spa Resort is perfect for people who want to relax away from city noise in a luxury hotel with a magnificent sandy beach.

Our compatriots, who left reviews about the Eden Resort Spa 5 *, claim that the hotel is well worth the money. This is also evidenced by its rating, based on guest ratings. So, it is 4.4 points out of five possible.





Types of apartments

As already mentioned, Eden Spa Resort 5 * has at its disposal 158 rooms. They are represented by the following categories:

  • Superior (one room, 38 sq. Meters, maximum occupancy 2 + 2 guests). Depending on the floor, rooms have a balcony or terrace. Apartments in this category have king, queen or twin beds. Some rooms can be interconnected, which will be very convenient for families with children.
  • Deluxe (one room, an area of ‚Äã‚Äã49 sq. Meters, 2 + 2 people can stay maximum). These rooms are located on the ground floor and have a terrace.
  • Super deluxe (one room, area 38 sq. M, 2 + 2 guests). This apartment is located on the second floor. They feature a balcony with wooden floors.
  • Paradise (one room, 43 sq. M, a maximum of two guests can live). The rooms are located on the third floor and are equipped with king size beds, a bathroom with bathtub and shower, and a balcony. Please note that this unit cannot accommodate a baby cot.
  • Paradise Suite (53 sq. M, two rooms). These rooms are designed for two guests and are equipped with a king-size bed. The bedroom is separated from the living room by a door. The apartments have a bathroom with shower and jacuzzi, and a spacious furnished balcony.
  • Penthouse (49 sq. M, maximum occupancy - 2 people). These rooms have two rooms, separated by a door, a bathroom with bathtub and shower, an outdoor shower and a large balcony with panoramic sea views.




Room features

Regardless of the category, all apartments of the Eden Spa Resort 5 * hotel (Sri Lanka) are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of even the most demanding travelers. So, in the rooms there is a modern TV with a large number of cable and satellite channels, air conditioning, safe, telephone, electric kettle and tea set, minibar, bathroom with bathtub and shower, hairdryer, balcony or terrace. Some categories of apartments also have a jacuzzi. All rooms have Wi-Fi access. If necessary, you will also be provided with an iron and ironing board. Laundry and dry cleaning services are also available. 24-hour room service is also available.
