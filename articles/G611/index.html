Guest houses, Gurzuf: reviews and photos

Many travelers traveling to the Crimean warm regions are interested in guest houses in Gurzuf. 2016 stands out among other seasons in that it was this year that such proposals were in great demand. Firstly, living in a private villa is much cheaper than in a luxury hotel. Secondly, a vacation spent here will delight tired guests with silence, calm, cordiality of the hosts, a homely warm atmosphere.





Therefore, this article will describe the popular guest houses of Gurzuf. Reviews of tourists, prices for accommodation and provided services will be presented to your attention.

"Cedar"

Guest houses of Gurzuf are small and neat. Often they are presented in the form of beautiful houses twined with greenery and flowers. That is exactly what Cedar is, a two-story building with eight rooms. Some rooms have their own kitchen, while other guests can cook in a shared room.

Judging by the reviews of visitors, the advantages of this cottage are the presence of free parking, proximity to the beach and promenade (only 7 minutes). There is also a bus stop, a pharmacy, a grocery store and a market within walking distance of the guest house.





This small hotel has three types of rooms: standard with and without kitchen, luxury. The only difference between the apartments is that the rooms of the last category have a balcony and a dining area. In addition, in those rooms where there is a kitchen, there is a corresponding set.

Guest reviews indicate that the hosts are very welcoming and hospitable people. Many tourists are pleased with the presence of all sorts of useful household trifles: dishwashing detergents, rags, plates and cups. Among the minuses, tourists note the lack of the opportunity to come here with children who are not yet 3 years old. By the way, the prices for accommodation here are low: 2,000 rubles per day per person will have to be paid for the standard, 3,000 - for the suite.

"Gurzuf Dawns"

Gurzufe guest houses offer good and quality rest. Among the many boarding houses, the villa "Gurzuf Dawns" stands out. This is a whole complex of small buildings that can accommodate visitors. In total there are 29 rooms: 13 one-room double (with the ability to deliver a crib for a child), 14 two-room, 2 three-room.

There is a pebble beach nearby - you can walk to it in 10 minutes. Reviews of guests say that they really like the kitchen in the cafe located on the territory. She is delicious, homemade. An absolute plus is the inclusion of breakfast in the room rate.





Many tourists traveling with pets are happy to be able to agree with the owner about placing their pet. And families with kids like the presence of a swimming pool, playground, green area, gazebo. Young people note the possibility of using a sauna and barbecue, where you can cook a delicious barbecue. By the way, the price range is quite wide: the cost ranges from 2.5 to 6.5 thousand rubles per day per person, depending on the season and room category.

"Over the Bay"

In Crimea, there are numerous guest houses. Gurzuf offers tourists some of the best guest houses. For example, "Over the Bay." This guest house is small but very cozy. There are only 6 rooms with windows overlooking the sea. Tourists in their reviews admire the local beauties: a beautiful panorama of the pedestrian part of old Gurzuf and the mirror surface of the bay.

In addition, they note that you can come here with a big company or with your whole family, including grandparents, since the rooms are very diverse and can accommodate from two to seven people. Each of them has a TV, cable TV, air conditioning, a bathroom. The cost of the apartments is 2000-2500 rubles per day for a simple one-room apartment (without terrace and kitchen), 5000 - for a two-room apartment with a sea view, 5000-8000 - for a three-room room with a small balcony overlooking the bay.





Among the advantages, guests distinguish the following points: the proximity of various shops (from souvenir shops to food markets), pharmacies, bars and canteens. In addition, nearby is a stunning fountain with color music. If you go even further, you can go to the church, public transport and taxi rank.

Villa silver

This is another guest house (Gurzuf). It is presented in the form of a four-story building, which has 12 rooms. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the positive aspects of the complex are the cleanliness and hard work of the staff. Here every day they clean the rooms, towels and linen are changed according to any requirement. The downside is nutrition. Guests have to cook in the shared kitchen. Although for an additional fee you can arrange meals to order.





Only a five to six minute walk are a pebble beach and promenade. The same amount of time will be required to get to the water park and dolphinarium. This moment is especially liked by families with children. According to them, kids always have something to entertain, since at hand there are various children's amusements. And most surprisingly, the guest house also works in winter. In this case, prices are significantly reduced. If a double standard room in July and August costs 4000 rubles per day, then in January or February you will pay only 3 thousand.

Ecohouse

Gurzuf guest houses are often located near the sea. "Ecohouse" is a wonderful place near the coast. There are only five rooms: four of them are standard double rooms (2-3 thousand rubles per day), one is a two-room suite (3.5-6 thousand).

Judging by the reviews of tourists, the advantage is the presence of a safe in the apartment, minus the lack of balconies. Only those people who live on the second floor can go to a small terrace, but it is shared. But families with young children greatly praise the location of the hotel, as it takes just half an hour to get to places of entertainment by public transport: these are two water parks (in Yalta and Alushta) and a dolphinarium (in Partenit).





Guest reviews indicate that food is not provided here. Each guest cooks independently in a shared kitchen or eats in cafes located nearby. Dissatisfaction of guests is caused by an infrequent change of linen (in five days). At the same time, they admire the fact that this house is made of environmentally friendly materials.

"Rocky"

Talking about the guest houses of Gurzuf, you can not forget about this quiet boarding house. It offers its guests six rooms located on three floors. It has many advantages: diverse rooms that will satisfy the needs of any travelers, individual kitchen in each apartment, the ability to rent a gazebo and barbecue. By cons, tourists in their reviews include the lack of parking for cars and a change of linen only once a week.





"Rock" is located near the pebble beach - just five minutes. You will spend the same amount of time to get to the promenade or the nearest pharmacy, even less - to the store, market, entertainment center. Standard room prices - 3000 rubles per day per person. Although three-room two-story apartments will cost from 5 to 9 thousand (depending on the season).
