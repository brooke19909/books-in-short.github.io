Cretan Pearl Resort & Spa 5 * (Greece / Crete): photos and reviews of tourists

Choosing a place for a summer vacation, people consider Greece as an option. This is a wonderful country that can offer European service, low prices, sincere hospitality and, of course, a huge scope for sightseeing.

According to surveys, the Greek islands are popular with vacationers, each of which boasts a unique culture and traditions. Crete is considered to be a true paradise for a vacation. Tourists have a lot to choose from, because there are a lot of hotels on the coast. One of the best and most comfortable places to stop is considered the resort complex Cretan Pearl Resort Spa 5 *.

Of course, before booking you should familiarize yourself with the available information. What is the hotel Cretan Pearl Resort Spa 5 *? Description and reviews, the availability of additional services, the degree of comfort, nearby attractions - all this interests travelers who are going to spend several unforgettable days on the largest and most famous island of Greece. All information is presented below.

Hotel Cretan Pearl Resort Spa 5 *: how to get? Location Information





When choosing a hotel for a vacation experienced wanderers pay attention to many characteristics. Considered the location of the hotel. Where to look for the hotel Cretan Pearl Resort Spa 5 *? Greece, about. Crete, Chania - it is here, almost on the beach, and has a large modern resort.

A walk to the picturesque seashore takes only a few minutes. By the way, the distance to Chania Airport (this is where many tourists fly here) is only 10 km. You can get to the hotel in a few minutes using public buses. Some travelers prefer to take a taxi, because the prices here are not too high. The package tour price already includes a comfortable transfer.

Chania city center is about 15 km away. The main airport of the island (Heraklion) is 98 km away.

Room categories offered by the hotel

This is a medium-sized hotel complex consisting of 142 rooms. It offers travelers rooms of different categories:

  • Superior rooms are distinguished by their exquisite design and are ideal for 2-4 people. The rooms, whose area is 30-35 square meters, are divided into a bedroom (there is a king size bed) and a living room with a folding sofa. It offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.
  • Deluxe rooms are also designed for 2-4 tourists. There is a bedroom and a living area with folding furniture. It offers views of the well-kept tropical garden.
  • Premium rooms are 70-80 square meters in size. There is a spacious bedroom with a king size bed, as well as a large living room with two folding sofas. It can fit from 3 to 6 people. The room is suitable for the whole family. From the windows you can enjoy the beach and romantic sunsets. The rooms are decorated with white marble, decorated with interesting decorative elements - the design here is really interesting.

Description of room amenities





What living conditions can tourists expect at the Cretan Pearl Resort Spa 5 *? The photos show that the hotel rooms are spacious, clean and well equipped. There is a seating area with upholstered furniture and a TV. It also offers travelers the necessary appliances, including air conditioning and a telephone.

Guests enjoy a roomy safe with a combination lock. There is also a minibar, however, maids bring only drinking water in plastic bottles for free (the rest of the drinks bring an extra charge). All rooms have internet access.

Guests have access to a bathroom with modern facilities. You can take a shower at any time of the day. By the way, guests are provided with high-quality natural cosmetics. Towels are delivered to the room during cleaning, which, by the way, is carried out here daily.

Food scheme for tourists





Booking a room at the Cretan Pearl Resort Spa 5 * hotel, guests can choose a convenient meal plan themselves. You can order only breakfast or pay three full meals a day. In any case, if you wish, you can eat in the hotel restaurant, paying the bill.

Meals are held in the form of a buffet already familiar to tourists. Reviews indicate that the menu here is not bad. Guests are offered meat and fish dishes, vegetable salads, side dishes, a variety of desserts, seasonal fruits and, of course, drinks.

Restaurants and bars on site

At the hotel grounds you can have a meal at any convenient time. Tourists are always welcome in the luxurious Rafale Restaurant. Here you can enjoy traditional Cretan and Mediterranean dishes.

Relax and have a good evening by visiting the Tamarind Restaurant. In addition to stylish surroundings and a large selection of cocktails, Indian and Chinese cuisines await guests here. Travelers praise the Eros Bar & Restaurant. Here, sitting on the open terrace overlooking the sea, you can enjoy delicious desserts and exotic sushi.

At the very top of the hill is a bar where tourists are offered a large selection of strong drinks, as well as light snacks. There is another bar on the terrace next to the pool, Onyx, where guests can enjoy a variety of teas and views of the Mediterranean Sea.

In a five-star hotel, tourists can count on additional services. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available for guests, as well as clothes for ironing.

Conditions for living with a child





Many tourists prefer to spend holidays with children, and the hotel complex Cretan Pearl Resort Spa 5 * is ideal for this. Your child will be able to have fun on the beach, and the hotel itself has something to do.
