Picturesque places of the Novosibirsk region: rest. Novosibirsk region recreation center - prices and reviews

Planning your vacation? The Novosibirsk region recreation centers attract more and more tourists; holidays here are pleasant and useful at any time of the year. This is evidenced by the numerous reviews of vacationers, and many of them quite consciously refused to rest abroad, preferring the local regions.

This article will focus on what constitutes such a vacation. The Novosibirsk region recreation centers, motels, camp sites will be considered in detail. The reader will receive valuable information about the weather and climate, local attractions and pastimes.

Section 1. General Description





The territory of this region is located in the central part of Russia, in the southeast of the West Siberian Plain.

The flora of the region is diverse. Many species from Central Europe and North Asia are found here. More than 20% of the region is forest and 17% is swamps. The swampy taiga, consisting of coniferous forests, is located in the north of the region. Mosses, lichens and ferns, many berries grow here. In the forests south, birch, aspen, and pine prevail.

Rest in the Novosibirsk region involves spending time in the bosom of amazing natural landscapes. The region is characterized by a forest-steppe relief with pegs, where small birch-aspen forests are found in open areas. In Siberia, you can find many medicinal plants.

Most of the marshes and swampy meadows are in the Baraba Lowland. In the south-west of the region steppe drought tolerant plants prevail. Here, at the border with Kazakhstan, the Kulundinskaya plain begins.

But especially in the landscape of the region, the black taiga of the Salair ridge, consisting of mixed forests, stands out. Priobsky pine forests are rich in berries, mushrooms and have lush grass cover. Many plots are occupied by agricultural land.

Vacation in these places is recommended to be planned in advance. Especially popular with tourists, for example, with. Borovoe (Novosibirsk region). Recreation facilities here almost never experience a shortage of visitors. Among other things, it is impossible not to mention that in these places there is also a dendrological park with introducers introduced from different parts of the world.

Section 2. Barsukovskaya cave





A visit to the recreation center (Novosibirsk region), the prices for a daily stay on which very vary from high to the economy option, is impossible without acquaintance with local attractions. And there is really something to look at here.

On the banks of the Urop River, near the village of Barsukovo, there is a cave of the same name, where hundreds of bats of various species, many of which are listed in the local Red Book, found refuge from the cold. This is one of the largest winters in the region. The main bat colonies hide in places inaccessible to humans. Marmots live in burrows near the cave.

The cave belongs to the corridor-labyrinth type and has karst origin. The depth of the dungeon is about 20 m, the total length is 195 m. The entrance to the cave is located on a slope. The passage inside is quite narrow, and you can only move along it crawling. At a depth of 15 m, the manhole abuts against a fork. One branch goes into a small cavity, and the other enters a large grotto 5 m high and about 8 m long. There are several circular passages leading to other parts of the cave and to the exit. There are almost no streak forms in the form of stalactites and stalagmites. Small cracks can be found in cracks.

In order to protect the wintering of bats and the local ecosystem, the cave is a natural monument of regional significance. You cannot visit it on your own, so organized groups of tourists are often brought here. For example, in sec. Zavyalovo (Novosibirsk region), the recreation center arranges such excursions in the summer on average once a week.

Section 3. Belovsky Falls





In the Iskitim district, near the village of Belova, there is Belovsky waterfall. It differs in that it is located on the plain. The source of the waterfall is a lake, which was a quarry for coal mining. Over time, it was flooded with groundwater, and workers built a dam. Gradually, water broke a stone, and a waterfall appeared. Now, according to travelers, this is one of the best places to relax. The water in the summer is very warm and great for natural hydromassage. Who will refuse such pleasure? True, as many have noted, it is inconvenient to walk barefoot here, since the coast is dotted with small sharp stones. Recreation facilities (Novosibirsk region) located nearby are proud of such a neighborhood.

Section 4. Salair Ridge





The Salair ridge is a low-mountain hill in southern Siberia, similar in height to a green island. Its main ridge and slopes located in the center are especially distinguished. The ridge itself stretches from south to north for almost 300 km and originates in the upper Neni, and ends with the Bugotak hills. Its average height reaches about 400 m above sea level. The highest are the mountains of Kivda (618 m), Husek (590 m) and Fir (585 m).

There are no snowfields and mountain lakes on the ridge. But despite this, the riverbeds flowing east and west originate here. The bowels of the Salair Ridge are rich in minerals. There are marble deposits in the vicinity of the village of Peteni. Ridge is famous for its gold-bearing rivers. The local population in these places has long been engaged in gold mining.

In some areas of the ridge, still dense forests, called black taiga, have been preserved. To preserve this unique nature, nature reserves have been created, which everyone is recommended to visit. Section 5. Lake Karachi





What should be an ideal and complete rest? Recreation facilities in the Novosibirsk region are very popular, but vacationing is not possible without visiting this reservoir. So say everyone who has been here at least once.

The pond is located in the Chanovsky district. Its length is 2.5 km and its width is 1.5 km. This is a continental type closed lake. Legend has it that it was named after the first adviser to Khan Kuchum - Karachi Kadyr-Alibek, who tricked Yermak and destroyed his squad. Initially, the lake was called Achu-Tabis, which translates as "bitter-salt water." The southern shore of the reservoir is decorated with birch trees, and a fruit and berry garden has been planted on its northern side.

Mineral mud of Karachi have healing properties and are used for treatment and recovery. Water and brine of a salt lake also have a healing effect.

Section 6. Ulantova Mountain





The natural monument "Ulantova Gora" is located in a picturesque place of Toguchinsky district near the village of Lebedevo.

The mountain, consisting of pink granite, belongs to the northern peak of the Salair Ridge. Altitude is more than 400 m. At the foot of the mountain begins forest-steppe. And in the northwest towers Bugotak hills. Most of the slopes are covered with birch and pine forests.

Ulantova Gora is part of the watershed between the Ini and Birdie rivers, flowing in the north and southwest. Unique species of flora and fauna live here, many of which are rare. It is in search of valuable exhibits that those who are interested in the nature of their native country should go here.

Section 7. Gorky Lake





A small lake is located in the forest-steppe zone, 500 km from Novosibirsk, near the village of Novoklyuchi. Locals most often call it Novoklusky.

The reservoir is famous for its high content of healing salt, used since 1898 to treat many diseases. The salt concentration is so great that a person in the water keeps afloat. The lake is declared a natural monument. There is a health complex "Gorky Lake", reviews of which are distributed the most enthusiastic.
