How to ask for help if you run out of money on the phone?

If the funds in the account have run out, then you can ask for help from relatives or friends in two ways. The first one is to use the Call Me service. To do this, dial the command on your phone: * 135 *, the number of the subscriber to whom the request is sent to call back, in ten-digit format, # (pound) and press the call button. The second way: use the service "Top up my account". The command is as follows: * 136 *, the number of the subscriber to whom the request is sent to call back, in ten-digit format, #. Both services are provided free of charge.

Borrow money

If you do not ask anyone to replenish the balance, you can borrow money. For this, the NSS provided the Mobile Credit service. To use it, you need to dial the command: * 301 # on your cell phone and press the call button. The subscriber will be informed about the results of the request via SMS message after some time. How much is the loan money provided?

The amount that the NSS is ready to lend to its subscriber depends on the size of its costs for communication services over the past three months. If it is 200 rubles, then the loan amount will be 25 rubles, if 450 rubles, then 50 rubles. In the first case, the commission charged will be 5 rubles, in the second - 10 rubles.
