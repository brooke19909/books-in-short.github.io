Pattern of summer tops: dozens of self-made images

In the summer, no other clothes can save you from the destructive heat and create a variety of images for every day, like a top.



For strict trousers, an option from an elegant flowing fabric like batista or silk, to a flirty skirt - chiffon and lace, and for shorts - linen, cotton and other natural fabrics. If a woman has enough free time, then a pattern of summer tops and their independent tailoring can easily take a couple of hours. After all, she has at her disposal shops of fabrics and sewing accessories, handicraft magazines and, of course, her own imagination. So, in just a few hours, she will be able to create a top that is ideally suited to her and her wardrobe, and besides it is exclusive of its kind. There are a lot of variations of this element of clothing: these are sleeveless shirt blouses, and elongated with trimming under the chest, and crocheted with delicate patterns, and a top with an open back.





The pattern of the simplest products will take no more than half an hour, and sewing - less than that. Therefore, a woman should arm herself with trimmings of fabric, decorative elements at will (beads, beads, sequins, lace, ribbons, etc.). The easiest way is to pattern the top from chiffon, because for such instances you will need to take no more than three or four measurements and a minimum of fabric, and the result will amaze everyone. Such clothes will become indispensable in a summer wardrobe, because such a fabric gives the image femininity, elegance and romanticism.

Sewing

The pattern of summer tops should begin primarily with taking measurements. For the option with drop-down sleeves, somewhat reminiscent of Roman sources, it will be necessary to measure the length of the future product, the volume of the chest and the distance between future shoulder straps on the shoulders. You can use the simpler method, using your favorite T-shirt (it is advisable to choose not fitted, but fitted, which is perfectly matched to the parameters of the figure). So, the pattern of summer tops consists of two identical parts sewn together on the sides, and on top (on the shoulders) connected by short straps. To do this, fold a piece of fabric in half, attach a T-shirt and circle the resulting pattern with a dry remnant. This creates an incomplete outline of the future product.

Now, on the side on which the neck is located, the distance between the seams of the sleeves must be connected by one smooth line, so that a neat semicircle is obtained on the top between the contours of the shoulders. It was the turn of cutting and processing all the edges so that the fabric did not crumble. Next, two symmetrical seams should be made from the hem to the armpit of the former T-shirt, and connect the edges of the front and back semicircles of the top and back of the top a little earlier with short straps made of braid or ribbon.

Decor

That's all. Such a simple pattern of summer tops is suitable for any elements of clothing, and flowing sleeves will add romance and flirty to the image. To improve this product, the back can be made lace, and the straps can be replaced with ribbons tied in bows. You can also decorate the neck with beads or sequins, which will reflect the bright rays of the summer sun.
