Mastering knitting: a hat for women (a classic model made with knitting needles)

Hand-knitted hats are distinguished by exquisite beauty and originality. Each self-respecting craftswoman must have several do-it-yourself accessories in her wardrobe. And no matter what the general patterns of knitting hats for women are used in the work, each product for each needlewoman turns out to be exclusive and unique. And all because she not only adheres to the technology of making a headgear, but also puts all her soul, effort, and, of course, imagination into its creation. These components are the basis of such a craft as hand knitting.



Knitted hat for women - simple, fast, beautiful

In this article, a master class is presented to the attention of readers, which describes the process of performing the classic model of a hat. This accessory is very fast, the patterns used in the work are available even for beginners in this type of needlework. Thanks to the lush mohair yarn and the embossed pattern, the hat turns out to be very warm, cozy and elegant.

For work, we need knitting needles No. 6 and a 100 gram skein of yarn containing mohair (70%) and acrylic (30%). The footage of this thread is 200 m / 100 g. Do not be surprised that it is so thin and the number of needles is large. You need to complete the product with a thread in four additions.

We start preparing for the creative process

Knitting winter hats for women, however, as for men and children, begins with taking measurements. How is this procedure performed? Using a measuring tape, measure the circumference of the head through the forehead, ears and back of the head. Write down the result. Next, determine the height of the future accessory by measuring the head from the forehead up to the crown.



The next step is to knit a pattern. Type 10 stitches on the knitting needles and knit 10 rows with a pattern, the description of the technology for which is given below. When the sample is completed, measure how many centimeters are 10 stitches of the pattern. From these data, calculate, in accordance with the result of measuring the circumference of the head, how many loops you need to dial initially to start knitting.

The hat for women is made with the English gum pattern (simplified). All the front rows fit like this: hem (remove), * 1 eyelet to be loosened, 1 wrong *. Then repeat the pattern from the * - * icons to the end of the row. The wrong rows are performed as follows: 1 front eyelet, 1 wrong eyelet. Repeat this sequence of loops until the end of the row.

The lower edge of this model of the cap is made by the front stitch: the front loops in the front rows and the wrong ones in the wrong rows.

Headgear Stage

We start knitting directly. The hat for women will turn out this way: we collect 67 loops and knit 3 centimeters up (4 rows) with the front surface. Next, complete the product with the main pattern. Knit until the height of the fabric reaches the size you want (focus on the result of the second measurement). Now we begin to carry out the reduction, forming the top of the cap. Knit all loops 2 together. Draw the following rows with the front stitch, while in the 3rd row again reduce the number of loops, knitting them 2 together. Complete another row without cuts and finish knitting. The hat for women is ready. It remains only to pick up the upper edge on the thread and sew together the sides of the product.



This classic hat model is universal. She will be to face almost every woman, starting from a baby girl and ending with an older woman.
