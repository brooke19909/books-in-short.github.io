Bellevue (Montenegro, Ulcinj): hotel description, reviews

Montenegrin resort Ulcinj is not very well known to Russian beach lovers. However, some domestic tour operators with local hotels still sometimes work. For example, Russian tourists can relax in Ulcinj by buying a ticket with accommodation at the Bellevue Hotel (Montenegro) .

Where is the complex located and how to get to it

Look for this comfortable two - star hotel on the 2nd kilometer of the Big Beach. It is located in the suburbs of Ulcinj, about 4 km from its center. Near the hotel are a picturesque park and pine forest.





The proximity to the sea is what the Bellevue Hotel (Montenegro, Ulcinj) first of all deserved good reviews for guests . The 1 line on which this hotel is located is convenient, of course, because tourists do not need to spend too much time hiking to the beach. Residents of the complex can get to the sea in just a couple of minutes.

Of course, Russian tourists most often fly to Montenegro by plane. The nearest airport to Ulcinj is Podgorica. This air transport hub is located about 70 km from the hotel. However, there are no direct flights from Russia to this airport. You have to get to it by plane with transfers. Therefore, more often hotel guests still arrive in Montenegro at Tivat airport. It is located 84 km from Ulcinj. Tourists usually get from it to the hotel by comfortable transfer bus provided by the operator. But you can, if necessary, get to Ulcinj on your own. The bus to this city does not enter Tivat. Therefore, those tourists who decide to get to Ulcinj from the airport on their own will probably have to rent a taxi.

What kind of housing is for rent in the complex

The size of the recreation center Bellevue (Montenegro) is quite large. In total, 329 comfortable rooms are equipped in its buildings for guests of Ulcinj. Tourists have the opportunity to rent class rooms here:

  • SNGL;

  • Apartment;

  • DBL

The most common rooms in this hotel are DBL . So they call the usual standard, designed for 2 adults plus one extra bed. SNGL rooms are economy class and are available only to guests of one- and two-star hotels. The apartments are designed for accommodation of tourists with high incomes and resemble no longer hotel rooms, but small apartments.





In rooms rented at the Bellevue Hotel (Montenegro), tourists can use:

  • air conditioning;

  • TV

  • a refrigerator.

Each room includes a balcony. In the shower rooms for guests, a free set of household chemicals intended for bathing is provided. In the apartment, residents can also use the minibar. There are two bathrooms in these rooms. The television in this hotel is satellite.





What is the opinion of tourists about hotel rooms

Most vacationers believe that the hotel Bellevue (Montenegro) is somewhat reminiscent of the Soviet boarding house in appearance and in terms of service . Indeed, this complex was built in Bulgaria once as a children's pioneer camp. However, according to many tourists, the rooms in this hotel are still equipped slightly better than in the old sanatoriums of the USSR.

Tourists attribute to the pluses of rooms rented at the Bellevue 2 * hotel (Montenegro) , first of all, their rather large sizes. Bed linens at this hotel are usually changed daily. Maids and housekeeping are often carried out quite often. So what about the cleanliness of too bad reviews about this hotel on the Internet there. Many of his former guests consider the fact that linen and towels are offered a little gray and washed out as some drawback of the hotel rooms.

Also, the minuses of the hotel rooms, its guests often attribute the presence of a large number of mosquitoes. Going to this complex, you should definitely take the Raptor with you from home. What service is provided to hotel guests

The infrastructure of the Bellevue Hotel (Montenegro) , taking into account the fact that it is only two-exploration, is developed, according to many tourists, very good. On the hotel territory there are:

  • gym;

  • sun terrace;

Near the complex there are several sports fields. On the beach, tourists can rent windsurfing equipment.

What reviews are available about the hotel infrastructure

The staff of the Bellevue hotel (Montenegro, Ulcinj) leave different reviews. Some tourists find the hotel staff quite polite. To other guests, they seemed too indifferent to the guests. Especially unfriendly, according to many vacationers, the girls working at the reception at this hotel. They can not answer questions of guests for a long time, while doing their own gadgets.

The fact that the Bellevue Hotel ( line 1 is a definite advantage) has no pool, of course, can be attributed to its shortcomings. However, the absence of inconvenience to guests can only be delivered, for example, during a storm. After all, the sea is located almost on the territory of the hotel.

Unfortunately, no entertainment is provided for guests at this hotel. However, if desired, residents of the complex can visit the nearby Otrant Hotel in the evening . Here dances are very often held and live music is played. Tourists can enter the territory of this complex quite freely. There is no need to pay to attend such parties.
