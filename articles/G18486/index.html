Hotel Grand Bayar Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya): reviews and photos

Turkish resorts annually attract many tourists. In our article we want to talk about one of the hotels in Alanya - Grand Bayar Beach Hotel 4 *.

A bit about the hotel ...

Grand Bayar Beach Hotel 4 * is a good four-star hotel, built on the Mediterranean coast, in the town of Alanya, in the Obagel region. It is located just a hundred meters from its own sandy beach. The hotel complex consists of one five-story building, built in 1996. The last major overhaul was carried out in 2003.



The hotel has a small territory and a total of 3000 square meters. The nearest airport in Antalya is 110 kilometers away. There is a bus stop just fifty meters away, which allows you to travel independently.

Number of rooms

Grand Bayar Beach Hotel 4 * has at its disposal 50 standard rooms with sea views and 35 standard apartments with garden views, as well as five family rooms. All rooms are equipped with a balcony, TV, minibar, shower, air conditioning. Cots are available upon request. Towels and bed linen are changed according to the schedule, three times a week. The rooms are new and clean, as renovations were made not so long ago.

Food in the complex

Grand Bayar Beach Hotel 4 * offers the beloved All Inclusive system, like many hotels in Turkey. The complex has an open restaurant for 250 visitors and one indoor restaurant for 150 guests. There are also two bars on site offering tourists liquor and cocktails, ice cream and snacks.

Hotel infrastructure

Grand Bayar Beach Hotel (ex. Turkmen) 4 * has a beautiful pool and a comfortable relaxation area. The hotel has a laundry service, there is a paid safe, a conference room, and a doctor is on duty. The territory of the complex is not very large, it can rather be called compact.



Guests have access to a hammam, shops and a beauty salon. The service is organized at a decent level and unobtrusive. The staff speaks German, English and Russian. Animation entertainments are held several times a week in two languages ‚Äã‚Äã(German and Russian).

A little bit about the resort ...

Grand Bayar Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey), as we have already mentioned, is located in Alanya, which is a typical town of Antalya province. The city has a strategically important location on the peninsula of the Mediterranean Sea, and therefore, since ancient times, was a fortified point of many empires (Ottoman, Byzantine, Roman). Alanya acquired serious political significance in the Middle Ages. It was at that time that the main attractions were built here: the Alanya fortress, Tersane, the Kyzyl-Kule tower.

The wonderful Mediterranean climate, historical sights and natural beauties make the city very attractive for those vacationers who love the sea, but also do not want to be bored. Since 1958, tourism has been the main source of income.



The coastal area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe resort is very open, with a minimum amount of greenery, not counting palm plantings. It includes Konakli, Mahmutlar, Avsallar, Inzekum. In general, the city is focused on poor tourists who prefer an economical vacation option. It is for this reason that local hotels close in the winter, the territories of hotels are very small, and in the summer a lot of young people relax.

How to get to the hotel?

The nearest international airport is in Antalya. The road from it to Alanya is about 1.5-2 hours. You can get there by bus. In Alanya, the bus station is three kilometers from the center, and minibuses run around the city.

Tours and activities

Alanya can be safely called one of the most pleasant cities in Turkey. Of course, this resort can not be compared with the bustling Istanbul, but here there are undeniable advantages. It is not in vain that the city has gained such popularity among tourists. In the vicinity of the resort you can admire the ancient ruins of the port of Aytap, Alara Khan, the town of Siedra, the village of Leartis. The city‚Äôs business card can be called the famous Cleopatra beach and the Kyzyl-Kule tower. The history of the beach is interesting. Once, Marc-Anthony presented Cleopatra with a part of modern Turkey. So, sand was transported to the Queen‚Äôs favorite beach from the north of Africa, since it does not contain dust at all. Therefore, the water on the beach is never muddy. The length of this piece of coast is about thirty kilometers. This beach is definitely worth seeing.

The list of objects required to be seen includes the Alanya fortress, which is considered the main historical attraction. It is perfectly preserved and appears before vacationers in all its glory. No less interesting can be considered the Kyzyl-Kule tower, located in the port of the city.



No less interesting is the Alanya Museum (archaeological). Since excavations are ongoing in Turkey, it is always replenished with new artifacts. Among tourists, caves are very popular. Another widely visited place in the city is the Tersane shipyard, located near the Kyzyl-Kul tower. In the evening, Tersane is blessed with numerous lights, which makes it look even more beautiful.

The complex has its own beach, located a five-minute walk. The road leads to it through an underpass under a busy road. The hotel is built on the beach, only the highway separates it from the beach.
