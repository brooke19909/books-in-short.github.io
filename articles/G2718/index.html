Volume children's sleeveless jacket for the girl (knitting)





For a three-year-old child, you need to buy 300 g of acrylic wick yarn and 6 mm knitting needles. The sleeveless jacket consists of two patterns: a 1 x 1 elastic band and short braids. The braid report consists of 8 loops with the addition of 5 loops at the edges (3 IP and 2 edge):

  • 5LP, 3IP;
  • according to the drawing;
  • * 1LP, 2 braids (on the auxiliary knitting needle 1 loop at work, on the working knit 1LP and go to the auxiliary), 3IP;
  • according to the drawing;
  • 2 braids, 1LP, 3IP;
  • according to the drawing *.

Continue the pattern from the 3rd to the 6th row. All purl even rows are knitted according to the pattern. Before work, compare your knitting density with a sample consisting of 13 loops and 17 rows of 10 cm. For the back, dial 45 loops and knit 4 centimeters with an elastic band. Then go to the braid pattern and continue working for another 24 centimeters.

Then, on both sides, close 3 loops for the armhole and then loop three times in each even row. There should be 33 loops on the spoke. Next, continue to work on the drawing. At a height of 43 cm, simultaneously close the shoulders (8 loops) and the neck (17 p.).

Knit front and sleeveless assembly

Now you collect 45 loops for the front and knit 4 cm with an elastic band. Next, go to the main drawing. Now at a height of 24 cm from the elastic at the same time close the armholes and form a V-shaped neckline. To do this, find the center loop and remove it with a pin.



Next, knit each side separately. From the side of the armhole, first close 3 loops, then three times in each wrong row for 1 p. And from the neck in each wrong row, reduce the loop 8 times. Once you have reached 43 cm, close all the loops.

Now connect the shoulder and side seams. On the neck you collect loops, taking into account the central one, which is located on the pin and knit with a 4 cm elastic band. Moreover, from the middle of the cutout in each front row, close 2 back loops, and in the back rows, reduce 2 front loops. Thus emphasize the sharp corner of the cut.

The armholes are also knitted with a 4 cm elastic band. It turned out a festive vest, which is perfect for a snow-white blouse. Even a novice will be able to knit a sleeveless jacket for a girl with knitting needles according to the above scheme, since the yarn is thick and the product knits very quickly.

Fancy Lightweight Tank Top

For this model, take yarn of one or two colors from wool and acrylic (50 g 146 meters) and 4 mm knitting needles. Tie the sample and calculate the number of loops based on the fact that the 10-centimeter sample should have 14 loops and 18 rows. The sleeveless jacket for the girl (knitted with knitting needles) consists of two patterns: a 1 x 1 elastic band and a lace.



The openwork pattern is given without taking into account the edge:

  • 1LP, * 1 yarn (H), broach (remove the loop as the wrong one, knit 1LP and pull it through the first shot) () *;
  • all wrong loops;
  • * 2LP, knitted together, 1H *, 1LP;
  • all wrong seams.

Next, the series are repeated from the first to the last desired number.

You start knitting with the back with dark yarn. Collect 56 loops and knit 4 rows with an elastic band. Next, go to the main openwork pattern. At a height of 25 cm, change the thread to a light skein. After 11 cm, form the neck, close the central 16 loops. Tie each shoulder separately, while on the side of the cut, reduce 2 loops three times in each second row. At a height of 44 cm, close the shoulder loops.

Knit front and sleeveless assembly

We continue to consider how a sleeveless jacket for a girl is knitted with knitting needles from two-color yarn. For the front, you collect 56 loops of dark yarn, knit 4 rows with an elastic band 1 x 1 and 25 cm - the main pattern. Next, change the thread to a light one, knit a 3 cm openwork pattern and begin to form the neck.



To do this, find the center 4 loops and close them. Next, on both sides of the cutout, in each third row, reduce 2 loops 6 times. We continue to knit the shoulders to 44 cm and close the remaining loops. Now stitch the details, leaving cutouts for the armholes.

Crochet the armholes in two rows and the neck with 8 rows of dark yarn according to the pattern:

  • 1st row: all single crochet;
  • 2nd and subsequent rows: * 1 double crochet, 1 air loop *.

The resulting collar is folded out and sew the corners. So the tank top for the girl is ready. Needles in this model do not form armholes, so it is easier for beginners to start working with this product. This sleeveless jacket can be knitted from plain yarn and without a collar.
