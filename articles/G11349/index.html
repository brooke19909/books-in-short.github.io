Telefunken TV: customer reviews, manufacturer and prices

The article will be devoted to the Telefunken TV lineup. Reviews of their owners, technical specifications of this group of devices, as well as a universal algorithm for their settings will be discussed in detail below. We also provide brief information about the manufacturing company.

About the manufacturer

Telefunken Industrial Group was founded in Germany in 1903. This company specializes in the production of television and radio equipment and communications. Moreover, in its catalog there are both exclusive devices and ordinary, everyday solutions.

It is to the second group, for the most part, that the modern Telefunken TVs belong. The manufacturer most often transfers the rights to this brand to third-party companies that manufacture high-quality equipment under the supervision of German specialists. Therefore, the German control over the manufacture of such multimedia centers is carried out in full.

Positioning

Any modern Telefunken TV is deprived at the software level of such a function as Smart TV. This fact alone relates the model range of this manufacturer to the segment of entry-level devices. It is also important to note the fact that the type of matrix used in such multimedia devices is currently outdated TN + Film. In this case, the screen resolution can be either 1366X768 (HD format) or 1920X1080 (the picture meets the specifications of the FullHD standard).

But such technical characteristics significantly reduce the cost of all Telefunken TVs without exception. Owner reviews confirm the availability of such entertainment multimedia centers. Younger models of this manufacturer can be purchased for 7500 rubles, and the cost of the most advanced solutions reaches 14500 rubles.

First level

The most affordable, at the moment, in the lineup is a device such as Telefunken TV with the LED19S64T2 marking. Its screen size is 19.5 ‚Äù, and the maximum picture format is 1366X768. The type of matrix installed in this entry-level multimedia device is Direct LED. The highest brightness value of the latter may be 200 cd / m2 and this is a rather low value by current standards. Similar competing solutions are capable of delivering up to 300 cd / m2. The dynamic contrast of the device is declared at 3000: 1, and the response time is 16 ms.

Many questions raise the capabilities of the built-in tuner. It can only work with analogue PAL / SECAM broadcast and digital DVB-T2. In this case, there can be no talk of supporting cable and satellite television. To receive such a signal, you must install an additional external set-top box.

The speaker system consists of 2 speakers. Each of them can give out 5 watts. Well, and in total this allows you to get 10 watts in peak mode.

The functionality of the built-in software shell is minimal and there can be no talk of SmartTV support in this case.

Most advanced model

Basic setup

The standard procedure must be followed during the setup process of any Telefunken TV . Reviews at the same time indicate that anyone can handle it. This algorithm includes the following main steps:

  1. Unpacking the multimedia device.
  2. Removing all delivery components.
  3. Assembly of the TV.
  4. Connection to the power supply network and signal cable from the antenna.
  5. Installing batteries in the control panel.
  6. Turn on the TV.
  7. Enter the menu and start the search for all available television programs. Moreover, both analog and digital.
  8. Saving the channel list.
