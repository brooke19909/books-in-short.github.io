Hotel Iberostar Hermes 4 (Greece / Crete)

Description: The ten-story complex Iberostar Hermes 4, founded in 1971 in the vicinity of Aghios Nikolaos on the first line of the beach, is the best option for an interesting, informative and relaxing vacation. Near the hotel there is not only the coast, but also the picturesque Vulismeni lake with the promenade, where you can walk and sunbathe on the sandy beaches, famous for their cleanliness.



Just a couple of minutes walk - the city center with a variety of entertainment venues, taverns, souvenir shops and luxury nightclubs. Guests can enjoy the amazing mountain scenery right from the windows of their rooms and watch the sun go down.

For business travelers, the conditions for productive work are created. Fans of excursion vacations will be able to visit the cultural and historical places of the city and get acquainted with the traditions of local residents. Iberostar Hermes 4 Hotel is a tourist destination where everyone, regardless of age, social status and status, will be able to truly relax and forget about current problems.

You can get here from the airport of Heraklion, located 65 km. A bus stop is also nearby.



Rooms: All 217 rooms at Iberostar Hermes Hotel are decorated in calm, warm colors. All rooms (except for single rooms) have balconies with mountain or sea views. Seasonally air-conditioned rooms . The total price includes a DVD player, satellite TV (Russian and music channels), Internet, hypoallergenic linen, hairdryer and direct dial telephone. If you want to use the bar, fridge and safe, you will have to pay. Daily indoor linen change and wet cleaning.

Meals: Iberostar Hermes 4 offers several food systems: all-inclusive, full board and half board, depending on the length of stay. Meals are served in the main restaurant on schedule. At lunch and dinner, alcoholic and non-alcoholic products are served. Cold snacks, cold drinks, draft wine and draft beer are available throughout the day.



In the afternoon, visit the Bay View snack bar, located in the building. It offers delicious coffee, gourmet desserts and quality service.

In the cozy cafe "Del Mar", the design resembling a yacht, you can spend time with a glass of delicious cocktail.

For connoisseurs of fine alcoholic drinks, the Hermes bar is open until 24.00. The menu has a rich assortment of liquors, aperitifs, beer, wine, whiskey.

Beach: A clean pebbly and sandy beach extends 50 meters from the Iberostar Hermes 4 hotel. Tourist reviews of the coast are positive. Beach amenities are paid. For guests there is a water entertainment center and a diving center.

Additional information: The building has three different-sized conference rooms (for 380 people), equipped with modern equipment and furniture. A rich library, reading room and wireless Internet are available.



For leisure: an outdoor pool filled with sea water, a fitness club, a travel agency. For children, a playground and a swimming pool are built. In addition, jewelry and souvenir shops, a sauna, an exchange office and a laundry are open.



Digest: The main advantages of Iberostar Hermes 4: proximity to the sea and the city center, an abundance of entertainment services and developed external infrastructure. For a reasonable price you can relax here on an all-inclusive basis.

Here guests are provided with comfort, tranquility and warm attitude of the staff. Noisy tourists were not noticed at the hotel; the main contingent was business foreigners and elderly people who came here for new emotions and impressions. As can be seen from the reviews, about 70% of vacationers will gladly return to this place next year.
