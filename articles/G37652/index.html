Tsanotel 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol) - photos, prices and reviews

Every year, more and more Russians who want to spend their long-awaited vacation in Cyprus, choose a vacation in Limassol. This city is located almost in the center of Cyprus, which makes it very convenient to travel to all corners of the island. In addition, there are a lot of hotels, restaurants, bars, shops and entertainment for every taste. This city, by the way, boasts the only zoo on the whole island. So, if you decide to choose the island of Cyprus as your vacation destination , the Tsanotel 3 * hotel (Limassol) will be a great economical option for living. We offer you to get to know this hotel better by finding out what tourists are expecting here and at what price.

Tsanotel 3 *: photo, description

This hotel complex cannot be called very large. So, he has 96 rooms of various categories. Please note that this hotel is not designed to accommodate people with disabilities in its walls, so it will be very inconvenient for people with disabilities, because there are no ramps, no additional handrails and other equipment. As for history, the Limassol Tsanotel 3 * was opened in 1982. Since then, there have been several reconstructions. The last one was held in 2013.

Thus, today Tsanotel is a modern three-star hotel, offering everyone who wants to stay in cozy, comfortable and equipped with all necessary rooms. This hotel is designed both for people who come to rest with their families with children, as well as for cheerful youth groups. Due to the developed infrastructure of the hotel, as well as the presence in its immediate vicinity of a mass of restaurants, bars, shops and various entertainment, no one will be bored here.





Internal rules

In accordance with the rules adopted at the Tsanotel 3 * hotel (Limassol, Cyprus), check-in of newly arrived guests is done after two in the afternoon. If you arrived earlier than this date and your room is ready, you can enter it immediately. As a rule, such an opportunity is absent in the midst of the tourist season, when the hotel is filled, as they say, ‚Äúto the eyeballs‚Äù. In this case, you will be prompted to leave things in the luggage storage room and go for lunch, to the pool, for a walk or immediately straight to the beach.

On the day of departure, in accordance with the internal rules , it is necessary to free your room, hand over the keys and pay for the period of stay before noon. Payment can be made both in cash and with plastic cards of such systems as "Visa", "Mastercard", "American Express" and "Diners Club". If you have already pre-paid with a travel agency for accommodation, in the hotel you will only have to pay for additional services, if you have used them.

Accommodation with children

Since the Tsanotel 3 * hotel is positioning itself, among other things, as a place for family holidays, guests who come to rest with children of all ages are only welcome here. In this case, if you place a baby up to two years old in a crib or a child up to twelve years old in an extra bed, then you do not have to pay extra for this. Placing adults and older children on an extra bed will cost 20 euros per night per person. Each room can accommodate no more than one extra child or adult bed. The need to provide such a service should be notified in advance to the administration and wait for confirmation from it.





Pets welcome

If you are going on vacation in the company of your little four-legged friend, then this item will be very important for you. So, unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the Tsanotel 3 * hotel. Therefore, if you do not want to leave your pet for a vacation at home, you will have to find another hotel in the Pets Friend category.

Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the Tsanotel 3 * hotel (Limassol, Cyprus) has 96 comfortable rooms located in multi-storey buildings equipped with elevators. They are represented by the following categories: standard (60 rooms with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã21 square meters with a maximum occupancy of 2 (+1) guests), studios (12 rooms with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã30 square meters with a maximum occupancy of 2 (+1) people) and suites (24 rooms with an area of 45 square meters, having a bedroom and a living room and designed to accommodate 2 (+2) people). As already mentioned, rooms and infrastructure for the disabled are not available in the hotel.

Each room, regardless of category, has a private bathroom equipped with a shower and hairdryer, as well as air conditioning, a TV (flat screen, satellite TV), telephone, safe and a balcony. Throughout the hotel you can use wireless internet access for an additional fee of 10 euros per day. At any time, guests can order room service. As for cleaning, it is carried out in the rooms every day. Twice a week, bed linen and bath towels are changed here.





Nutrition

On the territory of the Tsanotel 3 * hotel there is a restaurant and two bars. Guests are provided with half-board meals. So, the price includes breakfast and dinner. For an additional fee during the day, you can also have lunch in the hotel restaurant.

Sea and beach

Literally a couple of hundred meters from the hotel is a large and clean sandy municipal beach. On the way to it, you will need to cross the road and cross the promenade, stretching along the seashore. It has everything you need for an excellent holiday: sunbeds, sun umbrellas, equipment for water sports and entertainment. All beach and sports equipment is provided for a fee. In addition, the promenade has plenty of restaurants, cafes and bars, where you can both have a bite to eat and refresh yourself with your favorite drink.





Cyprus, Limassol, Tsanotel 3 *: entertainment

The hotel has an outdoor pool with a sun terrace, equipped with sun loungers and awnings, and a bar with a wide selection of various soft and alcoholic drinks.

Lovers of outdoor activities can spend time in the gym, play table tennis or billiards. In addition, hotel guests have the opportunity to go to the spa, where you can relax in the jacuzzi, sauna or a professional massage. On the nearest beach you will find everything you need for a relaxing holiday on the water and water sports.

The Tsanotel 3 * hotel has an animation team. During the day, they entertain children and adults, and in the evenings organize shows and performances. Every day the hotel hosts a fun disco.

As for the smallest guests of the hotel, for them there is a playground and a children's pool. In addition, if necessary, tourists can use the services of a nanny for an additional fee.





Infrastructure

The Cypriot Tsanotel hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay for tourists. So, the reception is open 24 hours a day. Therefore, with a late check-in or an urgent issue, you can always contact the administrator at the reception. Also at the hotel you can exchange currency, leave valuables for storage in a safe at the reception, rent a car, order a transfer service from the airport or back, book a tour of interest, and also call a doctor if necessary.

The hotel also has a conference room equipped with everything necessary for holding various business events: meetings, symposia, seminars, etc.

Cost of living

Since the Tsanotel hotel (Limassol, Cyprus) is a three-star hotel, it can be classified as a budget accommodation. So, living in it will cost you in the amount of four thousand rubles per room per day.





Reviews about Tsanotel Hotel (Limassol, Cyprus)

Since today more and more travelers in the process of selecting a hotel in a particular country pay much attention to the opinion of compatriots who have already been there, we summarized several comments by Russians who spent their summer vacation at Tsanotel. It should be noted that, like the whole island of Cyprus, Tsanotel 3 * for the most part left a pleasant impression on tourists. But we offer to understand everything in more detail.

So, with regard to the rooms and the entire decoration of the hotel, thanks to the reconstruction carried out last year, everything here looks very decent and modern. The decor in the rooms is quite simple, without frills, but comfortable and cozy. Bathrooms have good and trouble-free plumbing. Everything is very clean, cleaning is done six days a week with the exception of Sunday. The only thing that slightly disturbed some vacationers was the continuous operation of the central air conditioner, regardless of the air temperature in the room. Therefore, sometimes tourists were a little cool in the room. Otherwise, there are no complaints about the numbers.

The real "bone of contention" in the comments of travelers is often the issue of nutrition. I must say that the Tsanotel hotel is an exception to the rule here. So, most tourists note a good level of food quality. Of course, there wasn‚Äôt much variety of dishes here, but this is a normal situation for a three-star hotel. Nobody remained hungry.

As for the pool at the hotel, some of our compatriots found it quite good. However, for some tourists, the water in it seemed too chlorinated.

Since the vast majority of travelers come to Cyprus to relax on the sea coast, the issue of the beach is one of the most important. So, the nearest municipal beach is really just a couple of hundred meters from the Tsanotel hotel. You can reach it in a leisurely step in just a few minutes. It is quite clean, with pleasant fine volcanic sand and a gentle entry into the water.
