Ayia Napa: Attractions and Pilgrimage

Ayia Napa is one of the most popular resorts in Cyprus. It is located in one of the bays of Cape Greco, and tourists love it for its unique natural and climatic conditions, beautiful beaches, quality of service and a huge amount of entertainment. Ayia Napa,

the sights of which require special attention, in translation means "sacred forest", and this is due to the acquisition of the Virgin icon in the vicinity of the city. Today this place is quite popular among pilgrims.

Ayia Napa is popular among both young people and couples. Here you can enjoy the crystal clear water, the summer sun, equipped and well-groomed beaches, as well as enjoy a cultural program by visiting the best places in Ayia Napa - attractions that all Cypriots are proud of.

The main architectural feature of the city is the Ayia Napa monastery, the construction of which began in the middle of the sixteenth century. It is located in the park. The locals consider the main feature of the monastery a fountain decorated with relief figures. The building itself belongs to the Orthodox Church, although it was originally built as a Catholic Cathedral.

To date, only the monastery church operates on the territory.

Among the architectural sights, the Tallass Folk Museum is highlighted, which contains all the information about sunken ships in the Mediterranean Sea, as well as the whole history of the city with its features, and the Museum of Marine Life, which tells about all the inhabitants of the water.

The cultural and entertainment feature of Ayia Napa is a water park. Here are collected all the most popular and modern water attractions. The water park is striking in its thematic focus: these are ancient Greek columns, statues, and various decorations. For a variety of attractions, scale and practicality, the water park is recognized as the most popular and large-scale throughout Europe.



Cape Cape Greco Cape, where steep cliffs, ruins of castles, amaze with its beauty, does not go unnoticed. Here you can enjoy the sunset, and diving enthusiasts enjoy the sea beauties.

Among tourists popular modern restaurants, clubs, discos, which are generous in entertainment, outdoor activities, excellent cuisine and dance music. Night walks, fiery rhythms, bars, as well as quiet family restaurants in Ayia Napa are the number one tourist attractions. Each tourist will find a rest to his taste at the resort and will be charged with positive emotions for a long time.

Cyprus, Ayia Napa, attractions, vacation at sea, club life - what else is needed for a modern person who wants to spend his vacation in a picturesque place and without worries? This vacation will give you a huge amount of truly unforgettable impressions and delight. The city of Ayia Napa, the sights of which you see, will sink into your soul for a long time. You will tell everyone about it and, most likely, more than once decide to come here. After all, everything that you see here is indescribable, and it is simply impossible to store it in memory.
