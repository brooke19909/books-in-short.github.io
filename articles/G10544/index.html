Try to sew a bag





Some of the ideas offered in fashion magazines are simply striking in their simplicity. To implement most of them, you need to know at least a little about what sewing is. Alterations from old things very often look much better than the original taken as a basis.

For example, a bag. Most often, its pattern looks like a rectangle, the sides of which are equal to the expected result, to which we must not forget to add allowances for the seams. There is another win-win option for those who like to sew from old things - slippers. Decorated to the taste and desire of the creator, they will warm on cold evenings much better than those bought in the store, and you can use suede clothes, old sheepskin coats, faux fur coats, hats, knitted sweaters to make them.

Sewing and remaking from old things for girls is a whole epic. The little fashionista surely wants to look like her mother, so any skirt or blouse remade from her things will become a favorite new thing in the wardrobe, and you should not even talk about bracelets, hairpins, brooches and handbags. Very comfortable for girls sundress. To create it you will need old flared jeans. Given the length of the future product, you need to cut them off at the knees and flutter the internal seams. You will get two identical parts in the form of a trapezoid. They need to be steamed and swept away among themselves - the bottom is almost ready. Mat? Why not





Sewing from old things is not only creating a children's wardrobe. Our grandmothers also remade clothes into rugs. Today there are two of the most popular methods for creating these products, which will find their place in any home, especially in the corridor and bathroom.

In the first case, clothes of any quality are cut in a spiral into strips of approximately the same width. Just sit down to watch your favorite movie in the evening, take an old skirt or T-shirt, scissors, and by the end of the series at least one ball will be ready for you. Then take the hook thicker and single crochet, moving in a circle, begin to create. A special plus is the ability to knit parts. Day after day you will see what shape the mat takes and adjust the necessary sizes and colors. The finished product does not open and holds its shape well. Looking after him is also very simple. After all, washing it in a typewriter or even with your hands in a basin is much easier than doing dry cleaning of a carpet purchased for a lot of money.

If you do not know what a hook is, and even do not know the most basic knitting technique, there is a second option. Similarly, the product is cut into ribbons. After you get three balls of approximately equal size, start weaving a pigtail out of ribbons. The ribbons of the same color or different will be decided by the needlewoman. We wrap the resulting pigtail so that it turns out something similar to the shell of a snail, and we sew it with a regular needle at the joints. Such a rug, if it is even slightly steamed at the end of work, will look simply amazing.
