Gelendzhik. Thin cape. Travel Reviews

Probably almost everyone heard about such a resort as Gelendzhik. However, not everyone knows that his bay was formed due to two capes: Tonkoy and Tolstoy. But more on that later. After all, few people know where this place is. The thin cape in Gelendzhik is considered the most northern point of the bay. It is located between the Blue Bay and the Cape Thick. Those who wish to reach the central part of the city will have to cover a distance of 6 kilometers.



The first thing that attracts tourists here is fishing. After all, Gelendzhik, the Thin Cape, is considered the meeting place for fishermen. Responses of vacationers indicate that here you can carry out both classic fishing with a fishing rod or spinning from the pier, or achieve a big catch by going out on a private boat to the sea. In addition, you can get skills in catching shrimp and mussels.

In spring, various competitions are held here, the main of which is swimming. The distance starts from Cape Thin and ends at Cape Thick. If someone thinks that this is nothing, then it should be noted that the distance is 1800 meters, which is not so small.



But this is not all that Gelendzhik, the Subtle Cape is famous for. Vacationers also leave reviews about the new beach, which is equipped for a comfortable stay in accordance with modern standards. In the immediate vicinity is a cottage village, which has several cafes, a swimming pool. In addition, the descent to the sea, which is famous for high waves, is equipped. That is why Thin Cape is a paradise for surfers.

To get to this place of rest, it is recommended to use a car or public transport. Those who prefer more deserted beaches will have to walk some distance on foot or use an all-terrain vehicle.



As for housing, no one will remain on the street, having come to rest in Gelendzhik. Thin cape (reviews about accommodation are only positive) offers its guests to stay in one of the private hotels that rise on the sea coast. You can stay at the Thin Cape boarding house or use housing in the private sector.

Trips to Gelendzhik can be purchased at almost any travel company. Their advantage is affordable prices and excellent leisure facilities. For those who want to merge with nature, there are many places for camping. In seasonal periods, you can observe the whole campgrounds. It should be noted that it is impossible to reach such places on ordinary cars, it is recommended to rent an all-terrain vehicle that will easily take you and your luggage to the selected place. You need to know that those beaches that are far away do not have an equipped descent. In most cases, they are rocky, and a trodden path leads to the sea.

In addition to the beaches, you can walk through the many shops and souvenir shops, markets. And in the evening, guests will enjoy restaurants where you can relax and have a good time. In addition, you can visit the disco, which is held almost daily in the boarding house "Thin Cape". Gelendzhik will leave only pleasant memories in the memory of his guests.
