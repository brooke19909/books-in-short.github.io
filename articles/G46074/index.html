Hotel Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *, Turkey: description and reviews of tourists

The Turkish Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * (do not confuse it with the same name Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * in Cyprus) is the newest multi-story hotel complex built on the Mediterranean coast in 2016. It is located in the city of Alanya, in the Obagol area. This picturesque place, surrounded by the Toros Mountains, is famous for its mild climate and is popular among tourists.



Hotel infrastructure

The five-star complex is located 5 km from the center of Alanya, 35 km from the Gazipasha-Alania airport, 130 km from Antalya airport. It offers car rental, free public parking without prior reservation. For hotel payments, an airport shuttle service is provided.



The front desk is open 24 hours a day, here vacationers can use luggage storage (free of charge). Check-in is from 14:00, and departure is until noon. The hospitable staff speaks four languages: Turkish, Dutch, English, German. Pets are not allowed. The hotel is smoke free. It is possible to use paid services: ironing, dry cleaning, laundry, fax, photocopying.



In the building of the complex are located:

  • common lounge with TV;
  • hall for banquets and festive events;
  • minimarket;
  • Souvenir shop;
  • beauty saloon.

Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * in Alanya is suitable not only for family or romantic holidays, but also for business meetings. There are special rooms for business conferences equipped with modern audio / visual equipment.

The hotel has all the conditions for families with children. A mini-club (4-12 years old) and a playroom allow you to entertain the child, helping parents to fully relax. It is possible to take advantage of babysitting services or rent a stroller. The main restaurant has children's chairs, as well as a menu designed specifically for children.



Room types and amenities

All rooms have shared air conditioning. Wireless internet is connected. Sound insulation allows you to get rid of extraneous noise and relax in a relaxed atmosphere. For greater comfort and safety, the hotel has an electronic DND system. This is a new alternative to obsolete Do Not Disturb signs. Cleaning is done daily.

In total, the hotel has 320 modern rooms, each of which has a stylish design solution, its own individual features:

  • 255 standard (Standard Rooms);
  • 30 family rooms;
  • 28 family suites (Family Suite Rooms);
  • 6 for honeymoon rooms;
  • 1 royal suite (King Suite Room).

The rooms have laminate flooring. From windows or from a balcony the wonderful view of the sea and mountains opens. To create coziness and homely atmosphere, the walls of the rooms are decorated with paintings. The minibar is replenished daily. In the shower room, combined with the toilet, there are cosmetics, hairdryer, slippers, bathrobe, towels. Each room has an LCD TV with satellite channels. A direct telephone is connected everywhere, which allows you to contact personnel at any time. A safe is provided for storing valuables. A tea / coffee set makes it easy to have a meal in the room.

Beach holidays at the hotel

The hotel is located on the 1st coastline and has its own beach (sand, pebbles), to which an underpass. Near the coast there is a long wooden pier for sunbathing. The beach area is equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas, changing cabins. The safety of vacationers is monitored by a rescue team. Guests can enjoy water sports.



The hotel has four pools: two open (large for adults and children) and two indoor. Closed work at any time of the year. Also near the outdoor pool there is a bar and a mini water park consisting of several slides.

Power Ultra All inclusive

Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * in Turkey operates on an Ultra All Inclusive system. Guests are offered three meals a day in the form of a buffet. It offers guests a buffet, several bars and three restaurants:

1. The main Alasya Main Restaurant, which is free to eat. Local, dietary and vegetarian options are available. A late breakfast until 11 am, snacks, sweets, Turkish cakes, fresh juices, ice cream, night soup, alcohol, non-alcoholic cocktails are also provided.

2. Asitane A'la Carte. Offers Turkish cuisine. Open for dinner. One free visit is possible. The next visit is paid and upon reservation.

3. La Toscana A'la Carte, which can be visited once for free. The menu includes Italian dishes. Open for dinner.



Spa services

The Tilia Spa at the hotel allows guests to spend time also with health benefits. Beautiful atmosphere, the latest cosmetic procedures - all this helps to relax and restore the body. There is a massage room, a jacuzzi. A gymnasium and a fitness center are open to keep the body in good shape. It is possible to relax in the sauna or Turkish bath (free).



Entertainment events, games, outdoor activities

Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * offers visitors a wide selection of activities for active and interesting leisure. The opportunity to play for free:

  • squash;
  • darts;
  • table tennis;
  • bocce.

Paid options include bowling and billiards. You can spend time with pleasure in a small cinema. And for lovers of intellectual leisure there is a library. Also, hotel guests can play beach volleyball, water polo; rent a bike; do yoga, aerobics. For children there is an outdoor playground.



Every evening - an entertaining show program. Animation staff will delight children. There is a night club with a DJ. Once a week, a Turkish night is held, which will allow foreign guests to get acquainted with the culture of Turkey. This is a real celebration with live music, oriental and national dances, where they are treated to local dishes and sweets.

Positive and negative moments of relaxation in Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *

Rest in this hotel complex leaves a lot of pleasant emotions and impressions. According to reviews, Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * has a positive rating among 80% of respondents (based on Booking and Agoda data). Most tourists note a high level of service and excellent location relative to the city center. All the essentials are concentrated in one place: shops, restaurants, entertainment, currency exchange, car rental, health and sports centers. Friendly staff will always respond to any questions, help in solving existing problems. The hotel has an administrator who is fluent in Russian.



The cuisine in the restaurant was praised. The food in the hotel is of the best quality. Vacationers like a varied menu: a lot of meat dishes, salads, vegetables, fruits, desserts, pastries. Grilled dishes are prepared on the terrace: chicken, beef, trout.

Among the advantages of a beach holiday are:

  • the proximity of the hotel to the beach (2 minutes walk);
  • clear sea and pools;
  • stunning wooden pier;
  • a beautiful promenade near the hotel, along which you can take a walk in the evening;
  • a large number of sun loungers and umbrellas, which is enough for all vacationers.

Many recommend a massage at the spa. Visitors love the beautiful rooms in a modern style. The staff carefully monitors cleanliness. The furniture is new, all electrical appliances are working properly. Satellite TV shows Russian channels. The pluses include the high speed of the Internet. In the bathroom there is a large set of cosmetic accessories: shampoo, hair conditioner, shower gel, body lotion.

It is worth noting very good reviews about prices at Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *. Visitors believe that for this money they get much more services and bonuses than in other five-star hotels in Turkey.



Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel has several disadvantages. Among the minuses called an uncomfortable entry into the sea. It is preferable to descend into the water from the pier. There are not enough places for the evening animation, so it is better to occupy them in advance. The night club, located on the ground floor, is open only until 2 a.m. Some guests complain of problems with check-in and lack of available rooms. Sometimes you have to wait several hours before you get the keys to the number. Many expected a wider variety of seafood on the menu. Among the seafood - only fish.

One side of the windows faces the neighboring hotel. Also note the lack of greenery in the surrounding area around the hotel. Tourists arriving at Antalya Airport have a long drive to the hotel. Small slides in a mini water park are more suitable for children. In general, Asia Beach Resort & Spa Hotel is a hotel more for families than for youth, if this can be considered a disadvantage.
