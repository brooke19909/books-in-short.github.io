So, here are their main options:

  • Options for athletes - analysis of class time, counting calories burned, steps taken, etc.
  • Sleep analysis - fitness trackers can track the phases of sleep and its quality, can wake a person during the fast phase (most favorable for awakening)
  • Synchronization with smartphone and PC
  • Notification of calls, SMS and other events that occurred with the smartphone
  • Time demonstration

However, it is important to note that the last two options are not available in all trackers.

In general, as you see, there are not so many opportunities. In addition, even with the available options, in fact, not everything is smooth. One has only to go to some portal of reviews and start reading about the impressions of the owners of fitness trackers, it immediately becomes clear. There are a lot of complaints, but we highlighted only their main ones.

The main problem is the inability to configure the tracker for different types of activity. Of course, there is a primitive set, for example, walking, running, jumping. But, for example, if you want to calculate how many calories you spend doing in the gym on various simulators, your tracker can no longer help you in this matter.

The second important drawback is inaccurate data. The software algorithms of most trackers are very doubtful, many "happy" owners write that sometimes calories for walking are counted and when you sit, but, for example, strongly gesticulate.

Another not very pleasant moment - trackers interact, as a rule, only with special companion applications, which are often not only not very functional, but also work with constant lags.

And finally, about sleep analysis - a feature that attracts so many users. What are its advantages? Firstly, when calculating physical activity, he can use not only built-in sensors, but also access to GLONASS and GPS . This fact provides more accurate data and allows you to evaluate more activity parameters, for example, acceleration and pace. Secondly, today there is a huge amount of software for smartphones, these programs allow you to evaluate the productivity of a lesson in almost any sport - from table tennis to dancing.
