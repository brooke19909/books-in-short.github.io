Barkhatovo, lake (Krasnoyarsk Territory): description, reviews of tourists

The nature in the Krasnoyarsk Territory is very beautiful. There are many convenient places to relax. For example, many residents of the region like to visit the Barkhatovo reservoir. The lake (Krasnoyarsk Territory, Berezovsky District - its location) is popular with both fishermen and beach lovers. He rents a picturesque pond of LLC Velvet. Unfortunately, there are no tourist bases on the lake. However, there is a rather comfortable, well-equipped beach.

Location Features

In the north, the nearest settlement to this reservoir is Esaulovo. A little further from the lake in the same direction is the town of Sosnovoborsk. The distance from the center of this village to the reservoir is only about 7 km.

Not only the residents of Sosnovoborsk enjoy the popularity of Barkhatovo, the lake. Krasnoyarsk is also very close to it. The distance to the city center from this place is approximately 23 km in a straight line. Krasnoyarsk is located south of the reservoir.





Lake Barkhatovo (Krasnoyarsk): how to get there by car

To get to this popular holiday destination from the regional center, you must first go along Semafornaya Street. Near the Tire Plant should turn towards the bus station "Vostok" and go to the street. Glinka. At the exit from Novaya Bazarikha, near the school, you need to turn right onto the Krasnoyarsk-Zheleznogorsk highway and move to the village of Berezovka. In the village and further along the highway, you should just go in a straight line. The lake will be visible on the right side of the road.

general description

It consists of Barkhatovo (lake, Krasnoyarsk Territory), in fact, of two separate large reservoirs connected by a wide channel. Its banks are indented and densely covered with reeds. Most of the lake is an artificial reservoir formed as a result of flooding of the opencast mine (with a "wet" development).

Features of rest

The equipped beach is located on the eastern shore of the lake near the canal. Resting here is quite convenient. If desired, tourists can have lunch, dinner or breakfast in the cafe directly on the beach.

To get to Velvetny beach, you first need to get to the village of Esaulovka. Then you should turn in the direction of Kidyakovka.



Actually the beach works, of course, only in the summer. In winter, in the immediate vicinity of the lake, a small hotel complex operates in this place, in which corporate parties are often held.

Tourists who have chosen Lake Barkhatovo (Krasnoyarsk) for relaxation, can not only sunbathe on its shore or swim. On the beach near the village of Esaulovo, among other things, there is a rental center for sports equipment. There is also a private bath in this recreation center.

Of course, vacationers can use this locker room on this beach. Toilets are also available here. If desired, barbecue can also be fried on the beach. For this, several braziers are installed near the cafe.

On the shore of the lake, among other things, rescuers and a doctor are on duty. Many tourists believe that the Barkhatovo pond is very suitable for staying in a tent on a weekend. Unfortunately, the lake (the Krasnoyarsk Territory has too many suitable ponds for recreation) is popular with many tourists because you can swim here any day of the week. Entrance to the Velvet beach is free.

Fishing

Of course, all kinds of fish are found in this lake. Most often, small perches are caught in Barkhatovo's quarry. Also, sometimes fishermen fish out the plots. There are also pike in this lake. Many go to this reservoir not only for fish, but also for crayfish. There are just a huge number of them in the lake.





Barkhatovo, lake: tourist reviews

Opinions about this pond are different. Some praise the lake, others it seems dirty. But in any case, in the summer, hundreds of tourists visit the pond. Many come here with their children.

The fishermen, unfortunately, did not deserve especially rave reviews from Barkhatovo. The lake (the Krasnoyarsk Territory is large, but mostly residents of the regional center and Sosnovoborsk come here) are really popular among fans of sitting on the shore with a fishing rod. However, experienced fishermen in this pond are not advised to rely on too rich a catch.

Ecological problems

A pretty clean coastline is what characterizes Barkhatovo Lake (Krasnoyarsk Territory). However, the same cannot be said about the water itself in the quarry. It doesn‚Äôt look too dirty visually. The only thing is that a lot of algae always floats on its surface. However, unfortunately, with regard to the safety of swimming on the lake, everything is far from being as smooth as we would like. For example, as a result of an inspection of the SES carried out in the summer of 2016, it became known that the water here does not comply with hygiene standards. Moreover, the pollution indicators exceed the permissible 14 times.





The result of the audit, among other things, was that the Krasnoyarsk administration recommended that the townspeople refrain from visiting the lake. The Velvet company was fined 10 thousand rubles. The company's management was ordered to immediately eliminate all identified violations.
