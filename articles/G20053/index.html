Radisson Hotel, Bukovel: photos, reviews, how to get

If you dream of a fabulous vacation in the Carpathians, then you can recover in Bukovel. One of the most famous hotels in the resort called "Radisson" (Bukovel) will help to realize all your wishes for a wonderful vacation in the mountains.

A bit about the hotel ...

The Radisson Hotel (Bukovel) is located in the center of the Ukrainian Carpathians and belongs to the famous Radisson Blu chain. The complex will be an ideal holiday destination not only for active guests of the resort, but also for those who come here in search of romance. The hotel is located in the resort of Bukovel near ski lift number 7.



The Radisson complex (Bukovel), owned by a well-known chain, offers its guests accommodation in comfortable rooms, excellent infrastructure and first-class service. The high level of service at the hotel provides a five-star level of relaxation in the mountains in summer and winter.

Beautiful mountain design in the interiors is amazingly combined with glass elements of decor. A view of the picturesque Carpathian mountains opens from all the windows of the hotel. All apartments have dressing rooms shared with glass partitions with a bathroom.

Hotel Restaurants

The Radisson Hotel (Bukovel) offers its customers not only an extensive selection of apartments for every taste, but also a variety of restaurants with gastronomic delights. Without leaving the hotel, you can order any food yourself, starting with breakfast and ending with dinner.

Hotel Services

The Radisson Bukovel Hotel offers its guests a wide range of additional services that will help make their stay more comfortable.

The hotel has a ski room where you can store your equipment. It is equipped with special lockers (252 pcs.), Their number corresponds to the number of apartments in the hotel. Each of them is equipped with dryers for helmets and boots. The key to such a locker is received by each hotel guest at check-in. Such a convenient service allows you not to worry about where and how to dry things. Putting them in a closet, in the morning you will get them dry. If you do not have your own ski equipment, then you can rent it at the hotel.



The administration of the institution takes care of its guests, therefore, provides a transfer from the airport of Lviv or Ivano-Frankivsk and from the station. Ordering services is carried out in the reservation department.

In addition, the additional services of the Radisson Blue Bukovel Hotel include:

  1. Free 24-hour internet access throughout the complex.
  2. Super breakfast. This service allows guests of the establishment to appreciate the buffet during the morning meal.
  3. Late check out. Such a service provides guests with a unique opportunity to stay at the hotel until 18:00 at no extra charge, but only subject to availability.
  4. All rooms are equipped with special buttons. By pressing this button on your phone, you can solve any issue of room service that interests you and call the concierge.
  5. Grab & Run - this service is relevant for always rushing guests who do not have enough time for a full breakfast. A light grab and run meal will save time. Coffee, tea in disposable tableware, sports bars, fresh fruits - all this is laid out on the table in the lobby, so every tourist can eat.
  6. Express check out from the hotel will save valuable time, and all bills will be prepared in advance or sent to you by e-mail.
  7. As a rule, on vacation there is a need to wash things, especially if you come with children. The hotel has an express laundry service, which returns clean, dry laundry in three hours.

The main goal of the hotel is 100% satisfaction of the wishes of its customers, so all their comments and suggestions are taken into account.

Radisson Bukovel: how to get there?

Getting to the complex is not difficult. This can be done by bus, car or train from such settlements as Ivano-Frankivsk or Yaremche. As we mentioned, the hotel provides a shuttle service.

The road from Ivano-Frankivsk (bus station, railway station or airport) takes one and a half hours (distance - 100 km), from Lviv - 3.5 hours (230 km), from Yaremche - half an hour (35 kilometers).

If you plan to travel by your own transport, you need to get to the city of Ivano-Frankivsk, and then follow (110 km) to Bukovel via Nadvirna and Yaremche.
