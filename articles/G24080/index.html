Goodbarber

This is one of the most popular designers from French developers. The product is primarily striking with an abundance of beautiful and vibrant patterns. The platform easily synergizes with both the Android operating system and iOS.

The designer offers a lot of useful and relevant functionality now: integration with social services, chats, iBeacon beacons and geozoning. Using this platform, you can quickly create an application from scratch. Just select the template you like and start filling it.

The output is products that are very difficult to distinguish from similar applications made by professionals. It is also worth noting that for working with databases, the GoodBarber Android application builder is ideal, so here you can create complex and multi-level software. The platform is flexible, understandable, convenient and has great features.

As for the entry threshold, a beginner in this matter will quite cope with the designer. Detailed instructions are in the reference section, where step by step all the stages of creating applications are explained, from the choice of a template to the final layout. The only thing worth mentioning is that, alas, there is no Russian-language localization, and the developer offers only English, French and German interface languages.

Estimated cost of the product is about 2,000 rubles / month.

Shoutem

The platform has been successfully operating since 2011 and during this time it was, as they say, polished to the brilliance by the developers. The product offers a wide and quite powerful functionality. One of these and the most popular is the geolocalized catalog of places. This allows you to use the designer as an advertising software for restaurants, museums, entertainment centers and other points associated with the map.

In addition, the platform received sensible integration tools with third-party monetization applications. Advertising is fully supported, and not easy in the format of pop-ups, but with a competent synergy in the interface.

Product Features

In the library of templates you can spend more than one hour looking for the right option, but you will surely find it, because the developer offers a lot of solutions for every taste and color. The entry threshold for the platform is low and those who have at least once encountered such products will quickly master the software. If you encounter any difficulties, then the platform help system is at your service. Of the minuses, we can note the lack of Russian-language localization, but the problem is solved by installing third-party add-ons. The latter have a minimal effect on usability, but you will have an application designer for Android in Russian, especially if you have very tight English.

The approximate cost of the platform is about 1,500 rubles / month.

Swiftic

Another powerful application builder for Android, launched in 2010 by Israeli developers. The platform offers users a chic set of features for almost any project. Many advanced users call this constructor the best of its kind.

The local library pleases with the widest choice of building blocks: a lot of templates, the integration of organizers, calendars, directories, loyalty cards, e-commerce and much more. Developers position their product as an ideal tool for typesetting applications in the field of restaurant services, music groups, cinemas and other entertainment.

Platform Features

The service allows you to publish ready-made applications right away in Google Play, and with the so-called guarantee of success. That is, if you do not achieve any significant results in your activities, then the developers will provide free six months of using the platform. And for beginners in this matter - this is a serious argument in favor of this product.

As for the entry threshold, it is minimal here. In addition, the designer is literally teeming with all kinds of hints and instructions. There is also a competent master assistant who will tell you what in general terms and guide you through all the hot spots of the platform: getting started, choosing a template, designing columns, titles, as well as a selection of optimal tools for monetization and geo targeting.
