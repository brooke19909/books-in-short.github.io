Interesting village Vityazevo: entertainment for children and adults

Vityazevo is a very small village in the Krasnodar Territory, located on the Black Sea. Great place for families with children, couples in love or a group of friends. Men can go to the billiards club or bowling. There is also a great modern sports complex. Children can have fun in the amusement park or dolphinarium. There is a good water park.
