About the complex

There are very different reviews about LCD Tridevyatkino Tsarstvo: some interest holders are satisfied with the cooperation with this company, others complain about the poor quality of work and the deadlines.

Currently, a residential complex is being built in the Vsevolzhsky district near the village of Murino. Nearby there is a metro station "Devyatkino". This is an economy-class housing, brick-monolithic buildings - from 9 to 16 floors. The exterior of the house is bricked. In total, about 4,000 apartments are planned. Housing is being sold finished, there is the possibility of acquiring real estate in a mortgage.

The work is carried out by the Unisto Petrostal group of companies, and for quite some time. The first houses began to be commissioned in mid-2014. Currently, 8 out of 10 lines have been built. The last two have encountered difficulties, work has not yet been completed.
