Olymp Plaza





Hotel Olympus (Kemerovo) has 66 rooms of four categories. All are equipped with comfortable and new furniture, air conditioning, minibars, safes and televisions. Kuzbass





Hotel "Kuzbass" (Kemerovo) is located near the city center. 190 standard, comfort, deluxe, junior suites and family rooms are offered for living. They have the necessary furniture, a TV and a refrigerator. The bathroom includes a set of towels and cosmetics. The suites are equipped with a jacuzzi, an electric kettle and kitchenware. The cost of such a room will cost from 5,000 to 8,000 rubles per day, depending on the class. Rooms are cleaned daily and the front desk is open 24 hours a day. Guests can eat in a restaurant, cafe, use a coffee machine, breakfast is included in the price. Internet is free, car rental is offered, there are dry cleaning, shoe shine and laundry services. The hotel organizes excursions. Smoking is prohibited throughout the entire hotel, except for specially designated places. The highest rated visitors noted the convenient location, cleanliness of rooms, courtesy of staff. According to guest reviews, on a 10-point scale, this hotel is rated at 7.6. Among the negative sides, the following can be distinguished: noisy street, outdated interior, standard rooms have poor plumbing.
