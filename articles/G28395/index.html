Smartphone Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim: review, features and reviews

What can I say, Microsoft, like many other electronics manufacturers, actively supports these trends. Why did the smartphone get such a nickname? Why praise and scold Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim customer reviews? Read on and we will answer all these questions.

The target audience of the Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim is, of course, young people.

Well, the case for the Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim and, moreover, the USB cable and headset will have to be purchased separately. "Stuffing" Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim

The characteristics of the smartphone are quite impressive - it has a quad-core Snapdragon 400 SoC processor with a frequency of 1.2 GHz to quickly solve most of the average user issues. And 1 GB of RAM and even the Adreno 350 video processor provide him with proper support and distinguish this model from its predecessor at number 720, which has only 512 MB of memory.

Overview of appearance and body

The smartphone has a classic "Nokiev" strict and minimalist design.

This model is not large, it can be said to have standard sizes of 13.5 x 6.8 cm. Thickness is 0.9 cm and the total weight is only 130 g. With these parameters, the Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim smartphone fits comfortably in your hand, despite pointy angles and it is convenient to carry.





The case is made of plastic, there is a choice of 4 colors:

  • classic: dark gray, white;
  • bright - glossy orange and matte lime.

The smartphone is equipped with Corning Gorilla Glass version 3.

The screen protrudes slightly above the body. Above it are the camera and speaker for conversations.





Of the ports Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim has only two - for mini-USB and standard for headphones. They are located in the middle of the bottom and top of the smartphone, respectively.

On the back of the case you will find a camera, as well as a speaker located at the bottom right. This somewhat degrades the sound quality of the latter, since when the phone lies on the surface, the sound is muffled. In addition, virtually no bass. So the speaker is loud, but it is of little use for listening to music.

The volume ‚Äúrocker‚Äù button and the power and unlock keys are located on the right and conveniently protrude above the level of the case, so they can be easily found by touch.





Please note that there are no familiar buttons at the bottom of the screen.

This model uses two micro SIM cards, the slots for which you will find by pulling out the battery. There is also a slot for microSD-cards. Unlike one of the predecessors of this model - Lumia 1520, here the ordinary user can remove the battery without special tools. To do this, put the smartphone in an upright position, press the thumb under the camera lens and gently bend the panel with the index finger. Do not use sharp objects, the whole procedure should be performed only with nails.

It gives the user all the advantages of tiled design with the elimination of some flaws in the first version. Necessary applications, a calendar, the Cortana voice assistant, a gallery of your images, missed calls, SMS - all this is available on the start screen. Of course, it is customizable. An interesting feature that users liked was the ability of some applications to ‚Äúdisguise themselves‚Äù as a background image, only the inscriptions on the highlighted squares speak of their presence. The brightness of the screen is adjusted manually and with the help of an automatic sensor, and you can also change its sensitivity - for example, set it to maximum for working with a smartphone even in winter gloves.

Conveniently, there is a separate tile for each SIM card, which makes it easy to track calls and messages for each of them.

But it soon became clear that the Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim was included in the official list of smartphones suitable for installing Windows 10. This means that Microsoft guarantees its stable operation on this model. Therefore, fans of new products can safely update their device.

Cameras

These are individual prides of the Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim. In front there is a 5 megapixel camera with a wide-angle lens. She is on guard that you and all your friends "got into the frame." At the back you will find a 6.7 megapixel camera, with a flash and a microphone for recording audio for video, which, incidentally, can be shot in HD quality.

Both cameras focus well, it is possible to use auto or manual focus, the position of which is easy to change on the touch screen. The quality of the pictures is pleasantly surprising, although there are some difficulties with the white balance, and when shooting with insufficient lighting, a lot of noise appears.





Camera software

In addition to the standard application that is part of Windows Phone 8.1, the Nokia 730 Dual Sim has applications designed specifically for Lumiy:

  • Lumia Camera - has 3 modes: photo, video and Smart Mode. The latter allows you to make changes to the frame right in the process of shooting - to remove objects, change faces, etc.
  • Lumia Cinemagraph - allows you to create GIF-images.
  • Lumia Creative Studio - a program for simple photo editing, for example for applying filters, cropping, gluing panoramas, etc.
  • Lumia Selfie is the highlight of the model. The application works with both the front and the main cameras. It has a timer for delayed shooting, a face recognition system (though glasses often become an insurmountable obstacle for it), as well as features for retouching the face, for example, to increase a smile, whiten teeth, increase eyes and change their color, as well as change the brightness of the picture. In addition, it has functions for working with a monopod.
  • Lumia Storyteller - a program for creating virtual photo albums. In Windows 10, it is absent, since its functions were absorbed by a special OS application.

Customer reviews

According to statistics from large stores, the Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim reviews in 90% of cases positive. Many note its compact size, thanks to which it can be used even with one hand, and a bright design. Older people are happy to use the laconic dark gray smartphone Nokia Lumia 730 Dual Sim gray.

Most applications run fast, but their launch often takes more than a second (including those that are designed to work with the camera). But even resource-demanding games do not freeze in the process.

A single glance of the screen is enough to assess the current situation: calls, weather and much more.

Free preinstalled Microsoft Office also came in handy for many users.

Assistant ‚ÄúCortana‚Äù, although inferior to ‚ÄúSiri‚Äù from Apple, but still knows how to solve simple problems: call numbers from contacts, find answers to questions on the Internet. The cameras allow you to make very good shots, the quality of which is comparable to the results of smartphones with a 12 megapixel camera from other manufacturers.

The battery holds a charge of about 1.5 days during normal operation, which is good news for buyers.





Model flaws

Criticism is caused by a non-standard way of removing the back cover: not all users find the courage to do it themselves and the procedure is not very convenient. Nevertheless, buyers claim that plastic does not leave a feeling of fragility and certainly does not break in the process. But changing SIM-cards on the fly will be inconvenient.

Many were surprised by the lack of the familiar camera shutter button on the right, now this function is completely transferred to applications.

But most often, users were disappointed that still not all applications available on Android or iOS can be found on models with Windows Phone. Many companies do not want to increase budgets for application development for the release of their versions for so far not so many fans of Windows phones.

Rarely do users complain about system crashes for no apparent reason and heating of the smartphone while working with Wi-Fi.





To summarize
