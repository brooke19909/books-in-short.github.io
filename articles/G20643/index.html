The best Chinese tablets: a review of models

Some models are of good quality, they are in many ways superior to the options offered by Europe and America. Great examples are Xiaomi, Huawei, Asus. The device has a processor that runs on 8 cores, each of which accelerates to 2.3 GHz. How to distinguish a fake from the original?

When buying a device, you must always pay attention to the warranty card, the book in which the instructions for the device are written, the battery level, and you should also try the device in operation.

  • In the original model, the camera will be in the center, and in the Chinese version it will be in the corner.
  • You need to pay attention to screen resolution. Samsung's Chinese tablet has a score of 960 by 600. The first thing to watch out for is the application shortcuts and app icons.
  • All fake tablet models do not have GPS.
  • All Samsung devices will have a variety of applications downloaded from the manufacturer. In the Chinese tablet Galaxy N800, this will not happen, since the functionality is greatly reduced.
  • At the moment, the IMEI code is no longer the main indicator of fake, but it is registered on all new devices. The fake has instead written unknown, which means ‚Äúunknown‚Äù.

When buying a device, it is best to pay attention to all the documents that are provided with the device.
