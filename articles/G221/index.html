How to call to Germany? Options for every taste

Germany is a country located in the Center of Europe. A country with a rich history and culture is traditionally one of the ten most active economic partners of Russian business. It is very attractive for tourism, leisure and shopping. Many Russians have economic, friendly, and even family ties with this country . Therefore, the question often arises of how to call to Germany?



Twenty years ago, the answer to this question was one. You need to call from the call center. And calls to Germany were more like a lottery. Now the situation has changed dramatically. The proliferation of digital technology and satellite communications has made communication simple and affordable. At the moment, calling Germany is easy and simple, you only need to choose the type of communication convenient for you.

So how to call to Germany? You can call from a landline or mobile phone, it is possible to use communication via the Internet, there are also special communication cards for long-distance and international calls.

Using a landline telephone is the most reliable, but also the most expensive form of communication.



Mobile international calls will cost you a little less. But one must keep in mind that this connection becomes truly accessible, it is absolutely necessary to activate the Intercity service. When you connect this service, a subscription fee will be taken from you, each operator has its own, but ranges from 1.5 to 2 rubles per day. By connecting this service, you can call Germany on average 5-10 rubles, depending on the region. And if this service is not connected, an international call may become gold. This type of communication is suitable for those who often call abroad, then it is beneficial. For the sake of one call, such manipulations are not worth it.

When deciding how to call to Germany, the most profitable option is communication over the Internet. Especially if you use Skype. This program provides great opportunities for communication. But there is one big thing: it is desirable that the Internet be wired, as the mobile Internet does not work at a sufficiently high speed. This is especially true for not very large settlements, where there is no wired Internet, and mobile is malfunctioning.



The option with international calling cards is good for those who do not have the opportunity to use other forms of communication. He for a fee makes it possible to get a fairly large number of minutes for a conversation.

Now about how to call Germany correctly. To call Germany from Russia, you need to dial the code 8 1049 (landline) and +7 1049 (mobile). Next comes the code of German land and city, for example, for Berlin it will be 30, for Hanover 511 and so on. Attention! In Germany, numbers start from 0, you do not need to dial it during calls, the land and city code and the subscriber number are dialed right away.
