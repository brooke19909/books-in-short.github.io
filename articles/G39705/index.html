Land Rover a9: description, specifications

Recently, not just smart phones have come into fashion, but devices designed for those who lead an active restless lifestyle, who consider extreme and constant attacks on nature to be the norm. For such people, the Land Rover a9 smartphone was released, preserving the best technical and design traditions of its predecessor, the eighth model, and using a more modern filling than the eight. This is a new, improved model that we will consider today.

Design and performance of the new model

We will provide information about the Land Rover a9 in comparison with its predecessor. The first thing worth noting is the desire to keep up with the trends of modern fashion. For this reason, the display diagonal has been increased to 4.3 inches. Although this value is far from the most outstanding, but not far behind. Looking at the declared characteristics, we can state a very important fact: by doubling the number of cores (from two to four) and the amount of RAM up to 1 GB, the phone‚Äôs performance was doubled.

Developing a secure Land Rover a9 smartphone, the designers slightly changed the design of the front panel. The mechanical buttons used to navigate the menu were removed from it. On the one hand, it turned out to be a more pleasant design for visual perception, on the other - an annoying omission. But the appearance has become more attractive from this decision.

Battery, Camera, NFC and GPS

The power reserve of the previous model was quite enough for the new product, so manufacturers did not change anything. So the capacity of 3000 mAh remained. There is a specific, albeit so far not very relevant change: Land Rover a9 smartphones have NFC, a modern short-range communication module that allows you to open doors and pay for travel by public transport using your phone. It is clear that for the module to function fully, it is necessary to have a mate - terminals that would understand NFC, as well as doors with electronic locks and built-in NFC tags.





Nothing special can be said about the camera. The quality of the images is consistent with current market conditions and satisfies most users. The resolution of the module is 8 megapixels. The GPS function, which has become standard for all modern devices, is fully implemented and can work independently even in those places where high-quality Internet and communication cannot be provided.

Phone Protection Level

The protection level of the Land Rover a9 we are considering is high (IP-68) and helps to cope well with exposure to water and dust. This device behaves quite worthy even in water, calmly withstands immersion in it to a depth of one meter and lasting up to 30 minutes. But these clear and precise designations of time and depth must be strictly adhered to, which is caused by very fragile materials that protect microphones and speakers from moisture.

The thinnest membranes stretch under prolonged exposure to water overpressure and can break, while flooding and spoiling the device‚Äôs electronic circuits. In all other places where moisture can enter, double special gaskets are installed. It turns out that the plug for charging sockets and headphones, a battery cover, half of the case are provided for reliability with two gaskets at once. In principle, the consumer receives an updated smartphone, which is a hit in the market of phones with protection.

Some device specifications

We present for consideration some of its characteristics, not all, but the main ones. Phone Land rover a9 supports all communication standards, except LTE, two SIM cards (3G + GSM), withstands falling onto concrete from a height of more than 2-2.5 meters. The temperature regime is from -20 degrees to +50. Has an IPS display with a resolution of 960x540. The camera, as already mentioned, is 8 megapixels, HD 720p-video recording, with autofocus, which allows you to shoot underwater, the front - 0.3 megapixels.

There is a flashlight, support for microSD cards up to 32 GB. A battery with a capacity of no less than 3000 mAh allows you to talk for six hours, and eight days is the standby time. Installed OS Android 4.2.2, the new Quad Core processor modification MTK 6589. The smartphone has a built-in 4 GB memory, as well as the following wireless interfaces: GPS, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, NFC. 138x69x21 mm - its dimensions. According to customer reviews, this model has two main disadvantages: a decrease in the level of dust and moisture resistance and the absence of mechanical buttons. The cost of a smartphone is about 15,000 rubles.

Conclusion

This Chinese device, Land Rover a9, has passed very stringent tests. And he showed that in any extreme conditions he will remain your stable, reliable and productive friend. Manufacturers, which does not always happen, have turned out an unkillable, full-fledged gadget. For the lowest price for this class, you get a device with maximum functionality. If previously only military rescuers could have had such phones, now everyone can afford it. Although it is made in the same style as for military operations.

In addition to the above advantages, the smartphone has a MIL-STD-810G military certificate, and is also covered with Gorilla Glass of the Corning II degree, which meets all standards of protection of the American army. Our smartphone is also suitable for an ordinary user who wants to have a reliable device so as not to be afraid of any troubles that the gadget might fall into: no accidental or forced bathing, no fall, no dust or ash, and so on.
