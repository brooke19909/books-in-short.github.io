The Palms Hotel Apts 3 * (Limassol, Cyprus): hotel description, reviews

Many people prefer to spend summer time on the seashore, resting from work and improving their health with clean sea air. If we talk about Europe, then one of the most popular resort places is Cyprus. In this country, tourists can be offered a truly high-quality vacation, modern comfort and high-quality service.

One of the most famous resorts in Cyprus is the city of Limassol. Naturally, here, on the seashore, there are a huge number of hotel complexes, one of which is The Palms Hotel Apts.

When choosing a hotel for your vacation, every detail is important. For example, you should pay attention to the location of the hotel, as well as the living and dining conditions that are offered here. Where to look for a hotel? Location Information





Of course, when searching for a place to temporarily stop, the traveler first of all pays attention to the location. So where to look for the hotel The Palms Hotel Apts 3 *? Limassol, a cozy and calm area near the coast - this is where this small hotel is located.

As you know, Limassol is a large resort city. Its center is about 4.5 km away. By the way, you can get there without too much difficulty, since a public transport stop is located not far from the hotel, using the services of which you can freely travel around the area. The international airport is just over 60 km away. The hotel, and travel agencies, provide guests with a comfortable transfer, and the road is unlikely to take much time.

The Palms Hotel Apts (Limassol, Cyprus): what does the area look like?

This is a relatively small but nonetheless comfortable hotel. The Palms Hotel Apts 3 * is a four-story modern building equipped with an elevator system. On the ground floor there is a registration desk, as well as a spacious lounge for relaxation and waiting, where, by the way, you can connect to the Internet.

More recently, a complete reconstruction was carried out here. Reviews say that spending time here is really comfortable. There is also a small, but very beautiful and green area, where guests can also relax.

Description and photos of rooms for tourists





This is a relatively small hotel, which offers guests check-in in one of the 20 rooms. The hotel exclusively equipped apartments, which consist of a bedroom, a living area and a kitchen corner. The furnishings in the rooms are modern, there is a private balcony with a view of the city. The rooms are kept clean as staff clean it here daily.

Of course, you can count on the availability of household appliances. The air conditioning system helps to create a pleasant coolness in the room. In your free time you can relax watching satellite channels on a large-screen TV. There is also a telephone and a small safe.

Many guests are pleased with the availability of kitchen appliances, including a microwave, electric stove, kettle, toaster, in short, all that is needed to prepare a delicious dinner. Of course, at your disposal will be a set of dishes.





The bathroom is spacious with modern working fixtures. Here you can take a bath or shower. In addition, there is a toilet, a mirror, a washbasin, branded cosmetics, as well as clean towels, which are constantly replenished.

Do guests offer food?





As you know, food is an integral and important part of a truly enjoyable vacation. What can the guests at The Palms Hotel Apts count on? Cyprus is a hospitable country, which is famous for its good cuisine and quality service.

Paying for the room, tourists themselves can choose the appropriate meal plan - it can be either breakfast, dinner, or even full three meals a day. If you wish, you can completely refuse to visit a local restaurant. All events are held in the form of a buffet where guests can choose dishes according to their own taste. Reviews indicate that the food here is quite good - they offer light snacks, vegetable and meat dishes, fresh fruits, side dishes, fish and, of course, fresh pastries and desserts.

Eating in any case is not a problem for travelers, as several restaurants and cafes are located nearby, where you can eat decently. In addition, the apartments have a fully equipped kitchenette with all the necessary equipment - guests can cook themselves by buying products in the nearest supermarkets.

Relax on the beach: entertainment and amenities on the beach





The Palms Hotel Apts 3 * hotel complex is located not so far from the beach - the distance to the seashore is about 500 m. A convenient road leads to it, but if you wish, you can use the shuttle service that runs regularly to the beach.

Traveler reviews indicate that the seashore is beautiful, the water is warm, and the waves are very small. The entrance to the sea is cleared of stones, and the beach is cleaned every day. This is a public territory, although a large influx of tourists is not observed so often. For a small fee, you can rent a sunbed with an umbrella. And next to the shore there are several bars and cafes where you can eat and relax. Of course, tourists can enjoy water sports and other more active leisure activities, including water-skiing, windsurfing, parasailing, and scuba diving.

The list of additional services





While staying at The Palms Hotel Apts, guests can rely on some additional services. For example, the hotel complex has Internet access. Here you can exchange currency (convenient rate). The hotel offers laundry and ironing services, as well as a laundry service.

If necessary, guests can use the luggage storage room, as well as rent a safe at the reception. There is a small but well-equipped business center where you can use computer and office equipment. The hotel offers to rent a car or any other form of transport, and the rental price is not too high.

How do tourists have fun on site?





Most holiday travelers prefer having fun on the beach and exploring the surroundings. Nevertheless, you can have some good fun at The Palms Hotel Apts. For example, many guests relax by the small pool in the courtyard. Here you can freshen up, sunbathe and relax while lying on a comfortable sun lounger, and just chat with other travelers.

Tennis courts are located nearby, which, of course, will appeal to fans of this sport. Rental of rackets, balls, as well as evening lighting is paid separately. Beginners can take a few lessons from professionals for a small fee. You can have a good time playing billiards. By the way, in the evenings, guests are often entertained by animators, and after the performance everyone is invited to have fun at the disco.

You can have a good time outside The Palms Hotel Apts 3 *. Limassol is a place that is perfect for excursions.

Rest with a child: can one count on additional conditions?

When going to relax with a child, tourists are primarily interested in the issue of comfort. So what can parents expect to decide to spend their holidays at The Palms Hotel Apts? Reviews suggest that this place is well suited for families, although there are not so many additional conditions.

The spacious apartments are perfect for family living, and in the kitchen you can prepare tasty and wholesome food. For a small fee, you can use the services of an experienced nanny. There is a shallow pool with clean, warm water in the courtyard for the kids, where the kids love to splash around.

Hotel complex The Palms Hotel Apts 3 * (Limassol): traveler reviews and recommendations

A little communication with people who have already managed to spend several days at the hotel, sometimes helps to make the most clear picture of what awaits you during your vacation. The Palms Hotel Apts is a suitable place for a relaxing beach holiday. The territory here is small, but well-groomed, and the interior decoration creates a cozy atmosphere.

The food offered to guests is not bad - the menu is quite varied, and the dishes are always delicious. The road to the beach does not take too much time, and the coast here is quite beautiful.
