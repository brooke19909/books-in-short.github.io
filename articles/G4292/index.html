Anomalous zones of Moscow and Moscow region

There is an opinion that the capital of Russia is literally teeming with such zones in which a person cannot fully live, work, and just be. Such areas are called abnormal zones. It is believed that they are formed as a result of the fracture of several tectonic plates on which cities stand and in the hollows of which wide and mighty rivers flow.

Why are abnormal zones dangerous?

Living near such places or directly at their epicenter, each person feels their influence on himself. Often people complain of dizziness, apathy, weakness and nausea, a sharp decrease in performance. It is dangerous to live in such places, because as a result of this, a person can sharply fall ill with serious ailments, sometimes even fatal.

Geopathogenic areas in Moscow

Some areas of Moscow in anomalous zones are considered the most dangerous for living. One of them is the central part of the metropolis, located at the junction of river flows. Inside this zone, as many as 5 underground rivers flow, originating in one place. According to scientists, an extremely high concentration of negative energy was found in the Okhotny Ryad district, the Yugo-Zapadnaya metro station, not far from the Akademicheskaya metro station, near the State Duma department, at the Rechnoy Vokzal station, and also in Belyaevo and Khoroshevsky district . As for the Moscow Zoological Park, it is known that this place is considered a real energy funnel, and the behavior of animals and visitors here causes mixed feelings. Below will be listed the most dangerous anomalous zones of Moscow and black spots that are not worth visiting even out of great curiosity.

Elk Island - a funnel into the unknown

A good example of the presence of an anomaly is a park called Elk Island. Here, like the Bermuda Triangle, people disappear, and after returning to a state of complete amnesia. Despite the most beautiful landscapes and landscape, in these places you can not hear the singing of birds, and people always go around this territory per kilometer.

Very remarkable is the case of the disappearance of not one person in this park, but a whole group of 15 people and equipped with all means of communication with the outside world and excellent navigation. On this day, the company was busy completing the orienteering quest, and unfortunately, it was not possible to establish the reason for the disappearance of all members of the group. It is known that before the departure the group underwent a thorough briefing.

Many scientists put forward the assumption that in this place even time travel is possible , but, anyway, it is not possible to confirm this with documentation and facts. Today, scientists can only wait for new facts.

Patriarch's Ponds

Extremely strange events take place on Malaya Bronnaya Street and Patriarch's Ponds. According to some reports, these are the most terrible and terrible anomalous zones of Moscow. Rumor has it that at night you can see various ghosts and entities, and especially sensitive people often experience panic attacks and fainting. Failures here literally follow on the heels and motorists. For example, according to one driver, in the same territory, his car crashed twice, and ominous black smoke poured from the engine. These were not just puffs of smoke that had no shape; on the contrary, smoke took on a human shape. Although this motorist considered himself a staunch atheist, he still dared to turn to a scientific institute, after which the professors began to examine in detail this area.

As a result of the experiments, it turned out that the fears were not groundless, and this territory can safely be included in the list of anomalous zones of the capital. As the locals said, in the past there were cemeteries, which over time were simply flooded. The mystics and doctors are echoing, saying that people walking in this place, for no reason, had heart attacks, sometimes even leading to sudden death. Perhaps such sudden bouts of heart failure appeared only because people saw ghosts that they were not mentally prepared to meet with.

Kutuzovsky Prospekt - the place of death of thousands of people

Geopathogenic and anomalous zones of Moscow are represented by another unusual place. Kutuzov burial, according to experts, is another rather strong pathogenic zone. According to statistics, the largest number of traffic accidents occur here. This territory, without any doubt, can be recognized as dangerous. Every year, a huge number of perfectly serviceable cars are crashed on the road, while drivers never drive while intoxicated, and it is not possible to find another significant reason for all the accidents that occur. It is noteworthy that the type of accidents that occur here are mainly frontal collisions. According to many historians, this anomalous zone was formed due to the demolition of an ancient cemetery and the construction of a busy highway on this site. Presumably, car owners see supposedly living people in the middle of the road and, trying to dodge the collision, fall into the oncoming lane, from where they no longer get out alive. Of course, this information is very difficult to believe, but the fact, unfortunately, remains a fact.
