Kotelnikovo hotels: the most famous places in the city

Why Kotelnikovo began to be so called, now no one will say for sure. According to some assumptions, because of the location: the area where the settlement is located is a lowland, which is surrounded by hills on all sides. All this together resembles a boiler. Others claim that the village was founded by the famous writer Kotelnikov, having installed a chicken there. Both versions have the right to life.

But this fact does not attract tourists here at all, but local attractions, beautiful nature and a favorable ecological situation. That is why Kotelnikovo hotels are never empty. And since there are always plenty of people who want to relax here, going on a trip, it is best to book a seat in advance. Where to stay, everyone chooses to his liking, and the information below will help to make a choice.

Hotel Crystal





If we compare the hotels of Kotelnikov in the Volgograd region, then one of the first places is occupied by the Crystal Hotel. The institution has an impeccable interior. Fresh air and beautiful views complete the picture. In addition, there are bus and train stations nearby, which is very important for many people - you will not need to hire a taxi or carry heavy bags in public transport.

Before checking into one of the 30 rooms, each hotel guest enters a large hall. The columns and walls here are decorated with sections of minerals, causing delight and admiration for its beauty.

On the second floor there is a comfortable relaxation area, and further - premises for living in various categories, from VIP-apartments to economy. Regardless of the level of comfort, each room has everything that a person needs for quality rest and sleep.

Electronic locks reliably protect things left by guests. A bar, restaurant, secure parking - this and much more is available to everyone who stops here.

Seven Kings





Few hotels in Kotelnikovo boast the presence of a video surveillance system, but this institution has one. The hotel has its own cafe "Boulevard", as well as a guarded parking lot, which is provided completely free of charge to those who live here. If you want, food can be ordered in the room.

The number of rooms is quite diverse: from luxury to standard. The main motto of the institution: "High service at affordable prices." Judging by the reviews, this is so.

"Terrace"

For those traveling by car, the best hotels of Kotelnikovo are those located on or near the highway. This number includes the Terrace Hotel. It is located on the Volgograd-Salsk motorway.

The complex, namely the institution, is distinguished by a high level of comfort and fairly low prices. The cuisine is well known throughout the district: not every restaurant can eat so tasty and at the same time inexpensive. That is why the banquet hall often hosts local celebrations. After a hearty lunch or dinner, to warm up and stay awake on a full stomach, you can play billiards or take a walk in the surroundings.

Hotel Trio





Like other Kotelnikovo hotels, the complex offers rooms of different categories. Those who come by train or bus most often stop here - both stations are located nearby. But most customers book rooms here for the reason that the interior of the establishment is very close to home, because it is always cozy and comfortable here.

Among the wide range of services, the most popular are the Podkova cafe, a sauna, a service station, where both imported and domestic cars can be repaired.
