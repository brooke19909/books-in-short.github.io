Do I need a license for construction work?

In our country today there are a huge number of organizations that carry out construction activities. The main purpose of the state bodies regulating the work of these companies is to create optimal conditions for the development of this industry. This can be done only if the legislation will not hamper the work of developers. For this purpose, major changes were made to it, as a result of which the building license lost its original strength.

Therefore, the question of whether a license for construction work is needed is one of the most relevant today. It is not difficult to issue it, the most important thing is to collect the necessary package of documents, and then write an application for receipt.

general information



The activities of construction companies are fully regulated by the state, which in case of poor quality work may deprive the developer of a license to carry out construction activities. However, this approach has many disadvantages, as it creates a large number of difficulties for the business.

Therefore, the legislation was revised, and the issuance of licenses and quality control of work was transferred to the SRO. Thus, any construction company operating in Russia must buy a construction license from the SRO and obtain permits for all types of work that it plans to carry out.

License Definition

A license for construction work is a document granting the right to perform construction and other types of work related to design. It spelled out the rules of work, which are mandatory. If the developer makes any violations, then a disciplinary sanction is applied against him, and with regular violations he may lose his license altogether.

Do I need to get a license?



According to the current legislation of the Russian Federation, a license for construction work is obligatory for all developers and industrial companies whose activities are related to construction. In addition to the license, it is also necessary to obtain permits for the types of work that the company will perform.

However, there is a small exception. Organizations that are engaged in low-rise construction can work without any authorization. Similar measures were taken in order to reduce the burden on small and medium-sized businesses and create more comfortable working conditions for them .

However, this does not mean at all that such companies should not follow the rules and monitor the quality of their work. Their activities are also regulated by SROs, which may impose disciplinary sanctions.

What types of activities are covered by a building license?



Many people are interested in the question of what kind of construction work needs a license. It is mandatory for those companies whose activities are associated with the development of construction projects and technological documentation, the construction of buildings for various purposes, as well as engineering.

Today, SRO is engaged in issuing licenses and regulating the activities of developers. However, in order to obtain permits, the developer must first become a member. It is important to understand that, in addition to the main activity, insurance companies also perform various types of work, which also requires permission.

If any company whose activities are not commercial, you need to obtain permission for construction or other works, then it is issued a limited license for construction and installation works.

Varieties of building licenses



To date, licenses providing the right to engage in construction activities are divided into the following varieties:

  1. Design - allows you to develop construction projects.
  2. Construction - provides the right to engage in the construction of houses and structures.
  3. Engineering - involves carrying out engineering research and developing new solutions.

Each type of permit document involves the conduct of a certain type of activity, therefore, when registering a license, the direction of the company should be taken into account.

Which companies are licensed?

Any company whose activity is in one way or another connected with the construction of residential and commercial buildings, as well as any other architectural structures, must have a license to carry out construction work.

The legislation defines quite a few types of construction activities, therefore, before contacting the SRO for documentation, it is recommended that you familiarize yourself with the information provided in the Classifier. However, it is worth noting that after amendments to the legislation, the types of activities that require a license have become much less than before 2010.

The process of obtaining a building license



The license for construction work is issued in the following order:

  1. Preparation of a package of necessary documents.
  2. Submission of application to the regulatory authority.
  3. Inspection by an expert commission of a construction company for compliance with the technical base, the level of qualification of working personnel, as well as the level of quality control with established rules and requirements.
  4. If everything is in order, the developer will be issued a license.

I would like to say that the decision of the commission when issuing a license is also affected by the feedback from customers of the company that applied for documents. Therefore, regardless of whether you have a license or not, you must always work efficiently.

During the evaluation of the developer, third-party companies may be involved. This is necessary in order for the assessment to be carried out independently and to be objective. However, it is important to understand that the recommendations of third-party organizations are extremely informative, therefore they do not have a significant impact on the decision of the commission.

According to the legislation, the procedure for issuing a license for construction work should not exceed 60 days from the moment the developer submits an application.

What documents will be required?

To obtain a license granting the right to carry out construction activities, you will need to prepare the following documents:

  • statement of established form;
  • photocopy of identity document;
  • photocopies of the Charter of the company, certificate of state registration and TIN;
  • license fee receipt;
  • a list of the types of work that the company plans to do;
  • documents confirming the qualifications of employees.


It is worth noting that the list of documents may differ depending on the type of activity that the company plans to engage in. In addition, all regulatory documents that are required by law are required.

Conclusion

A license for construction work is a permitting document, without which the activity of a construction company is impossible. However, getting it is only half the battle, because SRO can always deprive her of an unscrupulous company for violations and poor quality work. Therefore, after obtaining a license, always adhere to high standards and comply with the requirements put forward by the SRO.
