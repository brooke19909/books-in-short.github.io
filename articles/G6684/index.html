We disassemble the knitting pattern: the first four rows

The knitting report is only 8 loops and 2 hem:

  • The first row, which will be the front, is knitted with purl loops.
  • The second row, which will be the wrong one, must be knitted with facial loops, and after each knitted loop, make a crochet.
  • The third row - the crochets must be dropped, the loops knit face: pull them from the crochets and knit.




  • The pattern with knitting needles ‚ÄúScales‚Äù (the diagram will explain in detail how to knit it) in the next, fourth row, should be knitted as follows: it should be borne in mind that it is in this row that volume elements should be formed due to the knitting of seven long loops instead of one wrong loop. In order not to cause any difficulties, you can use a hook besides knitting needles. Insert it through the half-loop of the seven loops located on the left knitting needle, grab the working thread and stretch the loop, which then will be thrown onto the right knitting needle, and seven knitted loops should be dropped from the left.

So you need to do this until the end of the row, except for the hem: seven loops together front and from the same loop to knit seven new ones. Thanks to this, you can get a number of scales with a beautiful wavy edge from lush neat elements.

We disassemble the knitting pattern: the second four rows

The pattern "Scales" with knitting will then be knitted like this. It is from the beginning of knitting the fifth row that work begins on the second part of performing rapport in height. You need to knit again from the first row, but when you reach the eighth row, slightly change the order of the stitches:

  • The fifth row will be front, it must be tied with purl.
  • Sixth knit facial with crochets.




  • In the seventh row, knit the front loops, dropping the crochets.
  • In the eighth row, it's time to form the scales, only you need to change their order. First, the first hem, then from one knit seven new stitches, and then sew the next seven stitches together.

Now that the two rows of scales are connected, from the first to eighth rows, you can continue to work on the pattern in height, repeating the work from the first row to the eighth.
