Hotel "Olympic", Khanty-Mansiysk: address, phone, room reservation

A fairly popular option is the hotel "Olympic". Khanty-Mansiysk will give you a warm welcome, despite the cold weather, if you prefer this hotel.





general characteristics

One of the largest in the city is the Olympic Hotel. Khanty-Mansiysk can be proud of this institution, because thanks to the high level of service, it maintains a positive image of the city. There are many services available to guests, including a free parking area, wireless Internet, room service, a gym, banquets and conferences, and much more.

Room reservation for this hotel is made both through the official website and through a specialized resource or by phone. At the same time, you can choose different categories of apartments depending on personal preferences and financial income. Without exception, all rooms are equipped with modern orthopedic furniture, as well as a wide range of amenities that will provide you with home comfort and a high level of comfort.

If you have chosen such an accommodation option as the Olimpiyskaya Hotel (Khanty-Mansiysk), then you can be sure that you will always have full and tasty food in the restaurant. Breakfast included in the price is served in buffet style. As for the √† la carte service, here you will be available both traditionally Russian and foreign dishes. Hotel "Olympic" (Khanty-Mansiysk). Address

For both tourists and business people, the convenient location of the hotel is extremely important. So, almost in the very center of the city is the hotel "Olympic". Khanty-Mansiysk, Engels street, 45 - this is the address of this institution. Within walking distance is a huge number of shops, restaurants and shopping centers. In addition, the hotel is located near the train station.

Guest apartments

Several categories of apartments are provided by the Olimpiyskaya Hotel (Khanty-Mansiysk) to choose from for its guests. Accommodation prices range from 3000 rubles for a single standard room to 6500 rubles for accommodation in a suite. So, you can choose for yourself one of the following accommodation options:

  • Standard apartments are designed to accommodate 1, 2 and 3 guests. It provides orthopedic single beds, a desk with chairs, as well as other necessary furniture and appliances. It is worth noting that for an additional fee, folding beds are available for additional guests. Several double apartments are equipped according to the needs of people with disabilities.
  • The studio is a large comfortable room, which by decorative zoning is divided into a bedroom and a living room. There is also a small entrance hall with a wardrobe and shelves for suitcases.
  • Luxury apartments are the best in this hotel. Despite the fact that they consist of only one room, thanks to a successful layout, the feeling of having a full bedroom and a living room is created. In addition, the interior and quality of decoration are much better than in all other categories of apartments. Indoor plants make the home-like atmosphere warm. The price includes a comprehensive three meals a day.

Please note that booking a room means booking a room of a certain category, rather than booking specific apartments.





Advantages

In all rooms of this hotel, guests are provided with the use of:

  • a set of necessary comfortable furniture;
  • a modern flat-screen TV, thanks to which you can view about 100 satellite channels and video from external media;
  • floor lamp for reading in the evening;
  • landline phone, which allows you to call the reception, restaurant or contact your friends and relatives who are in another city;
  • reliable code safe ensures absolute safety of your valuables;
  • a minibar, which is often used as a small refrigerator for storing food;
  • the bathroom has an electric hairdryer, a set of towels, and a cosmetic set.




Hotel Services

If you are looking for an opportunity for a complete and high-quality vacation, then give preference to this hotel. The following hotel services are provided for you here:

  • in the restaurant "Olympus" you can get a comprehensive complete meal or make an order from the international menu (it is possible to deliver dishes directly to your room, as well as packing lunch boxes);
  • there is the possibility of paying for accommodation and other services received in non-cash form;
  • in the lobby bar not only alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are available, but also desserts and a large selection of salads;
  • The price of the apartment includes a visit to the modern gym, where you can also get the advice of a professional instructor;
  • if you have very bulky suitcases that take up a lot of space in the room, then they can be taken to the storage room (this also applies to valuable luggage);
  • fans of billiards will be delighted by the presence in the hotel of the corresponding club;
  • throughout the hotel operates a wireless Internet signal, access to which is provided absolutely free of charge;
  • next to the hotel building there is a private parking zone with round-the-clock security, equipped with video cameras;
  • a conference room is provided for business negotiations, seminars and other working events;
  • if you urgently need to put your clothes in order, then the laundry will help with this (washing, dry cleaning and ironing are possible);
  • For an additional fee, a transfer to the airport, railway station or any other place you need can be provided;
  • if you have a festive event, then the hotel will not only provide a banquet hall, but will also assist in organizing;
  • On the ground floor there are several ATMs for withdrawing cash from plastic cards.

It is worth noting that some services provided by the Olympic Hotel (Khanty-Mansiysk) require prior reservation. Phone for recording +7 (3467) 312-009.





Positive reviews

Reviews of hotels help to make an objective impression and choose the right accommodation. As for the Olympic Hotel, the tourists distinguish its following advantages:

  • Responsive and friendly staff who immediately respond to comments and requests;
  • the price includes a large number of additional services;
  • From the windows of the rooms, which are located on the upper floors, a beautiful view of the city opens.




Negative reviews

Reviews of hotels make it possible to find out not only the pros, but also the cons of the proposed place of residence. The opinions of guests about the "Olympic" are characterized not only by positive, but also by negative comments. So, in this institution you can distinguish the following negative points:

  • the rooms are not thoroughly cleaned (in some places there is dust, and the carpet is stained);
  • too high food prices in the restaurant;
  • towels and cosmetic sets in the rooms for one person only (although it was indicated at the time of booking that two guests would be accommodated).




Conclusion

We can say that the hotel "Olympic" in Khanty-Mansiysk makes a very positive impression. Booking a room, you get not only a room for living, but also a wide range of additional services (comprehensive meals, visits to the gym, use of private parking). Thus, we can say that this hotel is for practical people. Fans of the beautiful will enjoy beautiful city panoramas that open from the windows of the upper floors.
