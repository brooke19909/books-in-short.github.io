How to sew a dress from packages

Creative ideas and the limitless imagination of our contemporaries surprise and sometimes shock. The fashion world also participates in these creative marathons. Many designers of famous fashion houses like to create clothes from unusual shocking materials and fabrics: wooden clothespins, rubber gloves, paper, white and black chocolate, or even trash bags.


A dress from packages is a popular and well-known topic, especially since ‚Äútailoring‚Äù such a non-standard outfit does not require large financial expenses and a lot of time.

To make this crazy thing, we need the following items:

- Garbage bags or food bags (e.g. chips). If you decide to use bags that previously contained food, they must be thoroughly washed, dried, and trimmed. Also, do not forget to check such material for integrity and the absence of holes.
- Threads of different colors.
- Pattern of the future dress.
- Scissors on the fabric.
- An old dress that will be the frame for the new outfit.
- Cellophane bags for breakfast.
- Sewing machine.
- A piece of cotton fabric.

How to make a dress from packages?

  1. The main thing in creative work is a great idea and its good execution.
    Independently invent an unusual and unique style of the future dress. Bags, especially large dark ones for garbage, hide a lot of interesting ideas and pans. If nothing comes out, look in the magazines for photos of dresses made from such material, and, combining several ideas, create your own. The main thing is to start! After all, bags can be inflated, weave from them many small or large braids. In addition, they stretch perfectly, do not require complex processing of seams, easily melt and solder with each other.
  2. Dress from packages (option No. 1) - simple and minimalist. You need a sturdy, dark 120-liter garbage bag with ties. It is necessary to make 3 holes in it - two are located on the sides, for the hands, and the third - on the head. After slightly tighten the special ties. The outfit is ready!
  3. Dress from packages (option No. 2). It will take an old long T-shirt or T-shirt. News decor will be attached to this frame. You need to inflate the small bags used for breakfast and tie them so that the air does not come out. After sew all the resulting details to our base. The result is a funny outfit.
  4. Dress from packages (option No. 3). Create a dress from bags of chips. The main thing is to create a beautiful patterned ornament on the product. If you sew a skirt, then it is advisable to use a fabric base and sew it to the belt. Bags for skirts do not need to be cut, it is better to sew them on the frame row by row. It will turn out a rustling fluffy skirt.
  5. Dress from packages (option No. 4). This is the most complex product that is sewn by all the rules. First you need to make a pattern and cut out the future details of the dress from the prepared material. For such an outfit, a very strong "fabric" is suitable - thick cellophane, which is not amenable to stretching and tearing. Add imagination, perhaps you will use bright color bags and create a real masterpiece of design art!
