Buttons - the main element of a decorative panel

From this type of furniture you can make beautiful paintings that will become a wonderful decoration of the overall interior of your home or a gift for a loved one.





What is the technology for making such a picture from improvised materials with your own hands? First, decide on the plot that you would like to see in the panel. The next stage is the preparation of the necessary materials: cardboard, wrapping paper or fabric, buttons, the necessary colors and sizes, glue. Now you can proceed directly to the production of paintings.

From the materials at hand we make the background. To do this, a thick sheet of cardboard needs to be wrapped in cloth or glued with wrapping paper of your desired color. Next, make a sketch of the drawing with a simple pencil. Now lay out all the buttons as they will be attached.

If the result that you see in front of you satisfies you, start gluing accessories. This can be done using a heat gun or any glue that is designed to work with plastic. When all parts are attached, leave the product to dry completely. So that such paintings from improvised materials, made with their own hands, have a finished and aesthetic appearance, they must be placed in a frame. It can be made of wooden planks, stripes of cardboard. Framing made of the same buttons will look original and harmonious with the general background of the panel.

Pasta. How to turn this product into exclusive paintings?

From improvised materials of this type to carry out a decorative panel is very interesting. Then transfer the sketch to the base and fasten the pasta. This can be done with PVA glue or a hot gun.





During this procedure, remove excess glue immediately with a napkin, otherwise the pasta will become limp and deformed. When the product dries, you can paint it with gouache or acrylic paints. The picture will become brilliant and more durable if it is coated with varnish for decorative work. As a frame for this craft, you can use wooden slats, plastic baguettes. A picture of pasta, the edges of which are decorated with spaghetti, will look beautiful.
