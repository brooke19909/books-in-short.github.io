How to trade options - features, instructions, recommendations and reviews

Options trading provides traders with good opportunities to earn money on exchanges. To receive income, it is necessary to study the fundamentals of the laws of the financial market and the rules of trading. Every newbie should know how to trade options, what factors and conditions must be taken into account, as well as how to analyze market movements.

Concept of option

An option is a contract under which a transaction is made with a selected trading asset with pre-agreed conditions and with a fixed and fixed expiration time. Before starting work, each trader analyzes the market movement in order to know which way to open a deal, and selects a suitable instrument for trading.

Options trading is a financial speculative operation performed by a trader with a specific trading asset. Depending on the specifics of the contract, the speculator can choose the most suitable trading conditions for the transaction and independently determine the duration of the option, that is, the period and date of expiration.

Since the tools for trading are different, and they all differ in their characteristics, a trader needs to know the features of the selected tool and how to trade options in certain market conditions. Only subject to the general laws of the financial market and trading rules, you can earn in this area.

Types of options and features

All options are divided into two types: for the purchase and sale of the underlying asset. Types of options:

  • call option;
  • put option.

The first option of trading instruments relates to purchases, that is, a trader has the opportunity to buy the underlying asset he needs for a certain fixed price. The second type involves the sale of an underlying asset. In addition, options are distinguished by several characteristics:

  1. Interest options (interest rate futures contracts or rates).
  2. Currency options (currency contracts or futures).
  3. Stock options (stocks, futures indices).
  4. Commodity options (physical commodities and commodity futures).

Additionally, options can be divided into:

  • binary
  • exotic.

And also all contracts are divided into option styles:

  1. American options.
  2. European options.
  3. Asian options.

All trading instruments have their own characteristics and characteristics. For example, American options can be closed any day at the request of its copyright holder until the contract expires. And the European version can be closed only on a strictly specified date, that is, at the end of the expiration time. Therefore, it is very important to know all the conditions of option contracts and the rules on when and how to trade options.

Benefits of Options Trading

Options as underlying assets are a very convenient tool for trading. The advantages or the main advantages of options trading include the following characteristics:

  1. They do not need to be physically bought, since all speculative operations occur on the trader‚Äôs trading account. When it comes to buying or selling an option, in fact, the speculator only predicts a change in market quotes, but does not physically buy anything. If his analytical data is correct, then he will earn a fixed amount. If the forecast is unfavorable, the trader will know the amount of loss in advance.
  2. Minimum financial risks. In option contracts, the amount of loss or profit is specified in advance. It is fixed and cannot be changed under any circumstances. And most importantly, the trader will know this amount before buying or selling an option.
  3. Wide trading opportunities. A large number of the most diverse strategies and tools for making money are available to traders. Many use the hedging method, automated programs, methods of copying transactions and other innovative solutions for trading.

Financial risk

It is important for every novice trader to remember that any transactions in financial markets are fraught with risks. Options are no exception. Therefore, it is important to be able to minimize risks in trading options.

There is a special section in trading - Money Management, which allows you to trade in a more secure mode. Proper management of capital or deposit funds helps traders minimize financial risks, and in some cases completely avoid them. Beginners should not only know how to trade options, but also how to avoid losses.

Most often, for these purposes, traders use the hedging method, which allows you to simultaneously open positions in different directions and thereby reduce losses, and under favorable circumstances, to bring transactions into profit.

For example, consider binary options. Suppose a trader, when buying an option, made a mistake in the calculations and after a while it becomes completely clear to him that the transaction will close at a loss. In this case, he can use the hedging method and open a position in the opposite direction with an increased bet volume. Such actions will help him block losses and make a profit.

Binary options trading

Unlike exchange options, binary contracts are the simplest and most understandable tool for beginners. The task of the trader will be the correct forecasting of changes in market quotes. The profit or loss on the transaction depends on how accurately the analytical forecast will be made.

For example, the speculator, after an appropriate analysis of the market movement for the asset he needs, decided to buy the Higher option, since, in his opinion, the price will increase in the future. He chose a bet size and expiration time and opened a deal. As soon as the binary contract expires, the position will be closed and the trader will immediately receive the result.

If the outcome is positive, the amount spent on the bet will be returned to his trading account, plus the winning profit, which was agreed in advance on the terms of the contract purchase. Suppose the amount of his transaction was $ 20, and the return on a trading asset was 80%, therefore, these $ 20 will be returned to his account, plus a plus of $ 16 gain. As a result, his deposit will be replenished by $ 36, since when opening a position, the amount of the bet was written off.

In those cases when the transaction closes with a loss, that is, the trader incorrectly predicted a change in market movement, only the bid amount will be debited from his trading account. As a result, in trading binary options all financial risks are limited, and the profit in case of successful trading is known in advance.

Acceleration of deposit funds

Overclocking a deposit is a very important point for many beginners. Not every novice trader has a sufficiently large capital, and, moreover, any trade in the financial market is always an inherent risk. To increase trading opportunities and your capital, you need to know how to disperse a deposit on options using safe methods. The most promising method in trading for binary options is hedging transactions. You can use this method as follows:

  1. Conduct an analytical forecast of market movement and find out in which direction the quotes will move.
  2. Open a deal with pre-selected parameters and expiration time.
  3. Wait until the option expires.
  4. If the deal closes with a negative result, then open two positions at once in different directions.
  5. Analyze the position before the end of the expiration time and open an additional deal in the right direction.

As a result, one transaction closes with a negative result, and two others with a positive result. They will block losses and take the position to a plus.

Hedging can be used without waiting for the closure of the very first loss transaction. For example, when there is little time left before the end of the suction period and it becomes completely clear that the position will be closed at a loss, you need to open a position with an increased bid volume in the opposite direction for a shorter time range.

There are a lot of various options trading strategies in trading and how to disperse a deposit on options in a short time. The main thing is to be able to minimize financial risks and correctly predict changes in market quotes.
