Paphos Gardens Apts (Cat. A) 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Paphos is deservedly considered one of the best resorts in Cyprus. Both wealthy travelers and tourists with an average income level come here to relax. Therefore, you can be sure that the infrastructure and entertainment are available for all tastes and financial possibilities. If you want a budget, but comfortably relax in Paphos, then as an option for accommodation, we recommend the three- star Paphos Gardens Apts Hotel. We will talk further about what tourists expect here, as well as about the impression left by our compatriots staying at this hotel.





Where is

The hotel in question is located in Paphos itself, just 800 meters from its center. Thus, all the main attractions and attractions of this city are within walking distance for guests of Paphos Gardens Apts (Cat. A) 3 *. Near the hotel there are also shops, shops, taverns, bars and other institutions. As for the beach, it is 500 meters from the hotel. The beach here is municipal, so you have to pay extra for using sun loungers and parasols.

How to get there

Typically, tourists arrive in Cyprus at two airports located near the cities of Paphos and Larnaca. If you plan to stay at Paphos Gardens Apts, then the best option is to pick a flight to your local airport. In this case, after the plane lands to the hotel, you will have to travel only 20 kilometers. Moreover, you can get both by taxi and by bus. In addition, you can book a transfer at the hotel. If you choose Larnaca Air Harbor as your point of arrival in Cyprus, then the road to Paphos will take about one and a half to two hours, because the distance between these cities is 150 kilometers.





Paphos Gardens Apts (Cat. A) 3 *: photo and description

This hotel is over thirty years old. So, it was built in 1982. However, in the past, 2013, renovation was carried out here, and today Paphos Gardens is a completely modern and comfortable hotel, which will be convenient for both young people arriving in Cyprus in search of vivid impressions and fun entertainments, and family tourists, Those who wish to spend a great vacation with their children. In total, the hotel has 222 rooms, some of which are specially equipped to accommodate people with disabilities. The hotel has swimming pools, a conference room, restaurants, bars, a sun terrace, sports grounds, a spa, infrastructure for children and much more.





Posting Rules

In accordance with the internal rules of the Paphos Gardens Apts Hotel (Cyprus), check-in of arriving tourists in their booked rooms starts after two in the afternoon. But this does not mean at all that if you arrive at the hotel much earlier than the indicated time, you will have to wait a few hours to check in. So, if the hotel has available rooms, you will be settled immediately upon arrival. With this, problems can arise only in the midst of the holiday season, when Paphos Gardens is completely filled with guests. In this case, you will be asked to leave things in the luggage compartment and relax by the pool, have a snack, take a walk around the hotel or the surrounding area. As for the date of departure, it is supposed to release the occupied room before 12 noon. At the same time, you will be asked to hand over the room key and pay for the entire stay in the hotel. You can pay here either in cash or with cards of payment systems such as Mastercard, Visa, American Express, Maestro and Diners Club.

Accommodation with children

Due to the fact that the Paphos Gardens Apts (Cat. A) 3 * hotel (Paphos) is mainly oriented towards family tourists, travelers who come to rest with children of any age category are always welcome here. At the same time, you can place them both on existing sleeping places, and on extra beds. How best to arrange the accommodation of children with you, as well as its cost should be clarified in advance with the hotel.

Accommodation with pets

If you belong to the category of travelers who prefer to take their four-legged pets with them on trips abroad, then you should pay increased attention to this item. So, in accordance with the rules of the Paphos Gardens hotel, guests are not allowed to stay on its territory with their pets. Therefore, you just have to leave your pet for a vacation, or look for a hotel of the Pets Friend category for a vacation.

Nutrition

When booking a room at the hotel, you can choose one of the following catering systems: breakfast, breakfast and dinner (half board) or all-inclusive. Be that as it may, be sure that in Paphos Gardens Apts (Cat. A) 3 *, reviews of tourists confirm this, you will find a very varied menu with delicious and excellently prepared dishes.

Sea and beach

Since Paphos Gardens does not have its own beach, you will have to pay extra for the use of umbrellas and sunbeds. The beach closest to the hotel is sand and gravel. It has all the necessary equipment for water entertainment, as well as bars and taverns, where you can refresh yourself with your favorite drink and enjoy delicious dishes and snacks.





Entertainment and infrastructure

We can say with confidence that in Paphos Gardens Apts 3 * there is everything you need for a comfortable stay, and for an excellent pastime. So, the reception is open around the clock. The hotel has sports grounds, a picturesque garden, a spa, a mini market, laundry and dry cleaning, free parking, as well as a large pool. Children can have fun and use their time in the mini club.

Cost of living

As for the prices for accommodation at the Paphos Gardens Apts hotel, they are quite consistent with its star rating. So, accommodation during the week in the midst of the season (July and August) will cost you from 37 thousand rubles for a standard double room, from 46 thousand rubles for a studio, from 57 thousand rubles for a one-bedroom apartment and from 74 thousand rubles for a two-bedroom apartment.
