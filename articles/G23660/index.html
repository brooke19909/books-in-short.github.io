Washing vacuum cleaner Bissell: reviews, specifications, instructions for use

An irreplaceable thing for every modern housewife is a washing vacuum cleaner. Having such a technological design in home use, purity connoisseurs will be able to reduce the time spent on cleaning and for a longer period to maintain the attractiveness of soft coatings. In this article, we will look at reviews on the Bissell washing vacuum cleaner, which helps to maintain perfect order in the house.

general characteristics

Actually, this is not about a single vacuum cleaner, but about some models from the lineup manufactured by Bissel. A washing vacuum cleaner is designed to perform several functions. It can be used for typical dry cleaning of floors and other hard and soft surfaces. Using the washing functions, you can wash and dry the carpets and upholstery of upholstered furniture.

With the ability to destroy stereotypes, a washing vacuum cleaner can be used to clean clogged sinks and bathtubs.

The different types of nozzles that the Bissell models are equipped with help to clean different types.

The case and some details of a modern design are made of plastic. The model range includes vacuum cleaners, quite massive and heavy, which are designed for professional cleaning. They are more suitable for cleaning in offices, child care facilities and banquet rooms.

The American manufacturer offers more compact models. These are vertical, manual appliances of household appliances, which significantly save the time of the hostesses.

Each individual design has its own characteristics and principle of operation. But they are all equipped with an innovative dust collection mechanism.

Further we provide the description of the Bissell washing vacuum cleaners and the characteristics of several models.





Bissell 81 N7-L

The multi-purpose device is designed for deep cleaning. There is a designated place for washing shampoo with a heating system. Thanks to this device, you can clean the room from bacteria.

The complete set of the unit includes a lot of nozzles, which are laid in a container fixed to the vacuum cleaner itself. In their reviews of the Bissell 81N7-L Vacuum Cleaner, people praised the turbo brush. She is able to efficiently collect pet hair and hair.

HEPA filters, water and dust, perfectly refresh the air in the apartment, collecting the smallest dust particles.

The design has the ability to easily maneuver around the house and do not scratch the floors. Due to the rubber wheels of this vacuum cleaner, your surface will look like new for a long time.

Like other models from this series, the vacuum cleaner is endowed with replaceable water containers.





Bissell 7700-j

The universal design successfully copes with any kind of cleaning. Different types of nozzles allow you to perfectly clean the laminate, parquet, linoleum and carpet.

The unit is equipped with a triple cleaning system. There is a water filter that absorbs the first dirt, as well as a filter to delay small particles of dust. And one more - to protect against allergens.

Some owners of the Bissell washing vacuum cleaner in reviews indicate that this model is good for people who have a lot of furniture with textile upholstery and carpets. Very well, it cleans a soft surface from dirt and stains. Since the case of this model is made of fragile material, it is better to purchase low-cost household appliances for current cleaning.

This unit is quite bulky and noisy. It has powerful wheels, removable transparent water tanks and large buttons for switching. Suitable for cleaning in public places and large cottages.

When buying a vacuum cleaner, it comes with 3 types of washing solution for different types of cleaning.





Bissell 88d6-j

A feature of this model is an automatic device that can independently calculate the percentage of water and shampoo as a percentage. In order for the device to reproduce such actions, it is only necessary to establish a certain function.

The vacuum cleaner has a unique oval-like configuration. Ergonomic housing allows you to store nozzles in it. The brush for a manual mode is included in the package. The spiral nozzle removes stains and complex dirt.

In addition to soft coatings, the vacuum cleaner copes well with dirt on tiles and linoleum.

The disadvantages of the owners include a small water tank, weak retraction force and scrupulous care. In addition, floors and noise are too long to dry.





Bissell 57F4-J Steam & Sweep

The Bissell vertical washer is equipped with a steam generator and a long cord. The hard surface cleaner does a good job cleaning in spacious rooms. The replaceable container and the steam supply button free the housewives from additional funnels for pouring water. A wave of hot steam allows for high-quality cleaning without chemicals. The nozzle rotates easily and smoothly, thanks to which it is possible to disinfect floors in hard-to-reach places.





Bissell 17132

A vertical hand-held vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter that allows you to clean quickly and efficiently. Unlike the above models, the case is much lighter. A long cord (7.5 m) allows you to make free movements around the room.

There is a rotary mechanism on the electric brush . To make it convenient to clean in poorly lit corners, the manufacturer took care of the backlight.

The vacuum cleaner is very easy to care for. After cleaning, it is not difficult to disassemble and clean the brush.

Hard surfaces, such as laminate or parquet, are easily washed with this device. However, it does not leave stains and collects water well.

The technical design works with a power consumption of 560 watts, while it has no adjustment.

The downside is that the brush cannot be advanced under the bed or bedside table due to the small angle of inclination. Also, the vacuum cleaner is noisy and carries out high-quality cleaning only on flat surfaces.





Vacuum cleaner recommendations

Since the instructions of the Bissell washing vacuum cleaner are attached to each model individually, we will use some user tips.

After the next wet cleaning, all parts of the vacuum cleaner intended for regular care must be washed and laid out on a dry cloth.

It is no coincidence that the Bissel washing vacuum cleaner is endowed with various functions. It turns out that before carrying out wet cleaning, it is necessary to collect dust in a classical way. Two-stage cleaning allows you to better wash away dirt and maintain perfect cleanliness in the house.

Carpet products should be washed by gently moving the brush. Otherwise, the dirt will erode on the surface and it will be difficult to collect.

Before dry cleaning, pour water about 2.5-3 cm.

For wet cleaning, use a special shampoo for a Bissell washing vacuum cleaner. Other detergents are best avoided.

It is important to carefully study the instructions for a particular model and follow it. This approach will ensure that the cleaning equipment in your home remains a reliable and durable assistant.





Reviews

For those who have managed to adapt to universal technology, Bissell vacuum cleaners provide useful services. They are indispensable household items when washing upholstered furniture.

In negative reviews there is information that using a vertical vacuum cleaner, it is impossible to wash the skirting board. Some do not like the fragile and overall designs of large devices and their cost. The price of a Bissell washing vacuum cleaner varies from 29 990 to 40 938 rubles. It is annoying to people that after each cleaning you need to clean filters and tanks. The suction power of structures equipped with filters is not very strong.

Conclusion

After reviewing the reviews for Bissell washing vacuums, we can conclude that they are too different. And this is not surprising, because we must admit that the models are very different from each other. When choosing a vacuum cleaner, it is important to consider your living conditions. Since many models are designed for rooms with a large quadrature, it will be inconvenient to use them in small rooms. For small apartments without carpet coverings, models with a vertical configuration are more suitable. With the right household appliances for cleaning, cleaning your home will be a joy.
