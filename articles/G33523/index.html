Iset Tower - the most expensive residential apartments in Yekaterinburg

Very soon, the most expensive residential building in the city will be commissioned in Yekaterinburg. This is the Iset tower, which is part of the modern business district of Yekaterinburg City. Iset today is the tallest building among the northern skyscrapers in the world. What else is remarkable for the new high-rise, what is the cost of its apartments?





Iset Tower: photos and interesting facts

The official start date for the construction of a residential tower of the Yekaterinburg City complex is considered to be 2008. Several times the construction of a skyscraper was suspended. By the end of 2014, the main construction processes were completed. The final stage of work is interior decoration, as well as installation and testing of facade lighting. The Iset tower should be fully operational at the end of 2016. The most expensive skyscraper in Yekaterinburg has 52 aboveground floors and 4 underground. Despite the record height, there will be no viewing platform on the roof. Do not forget that this skyscraper is residential, which means that the peace and tranquility of residents is always in the first place.





Safety combined with top-level comfort

The Iset Tower (Yekaterinburg) is a true masterpiece of modern architecture. During the construction of the building, advanced engineering solutions were used. In the central part of the skyscraper, a special fire protection zone is provided. The tower is equipped with two staircases with smokeless cells and an evacuation elevator. There are four technical floors in the building, there is a private boiler room. According to builders, the Iset tower is an ideal place to live. All the necessary infrastructure will be located on the lower tiers of the building: shops, restaurants, a fitness center with a swimming pool, a kindergarten, a mini-clinic, a beauty salon and other institutions necessary for a comfortable life. The skyscraper has its own underground parking, as well as a closed house area. And this is another innovative solution: the esplanade platform will help create a sense of unity and hide from prying eyes. The courtyard of the Iset tower is decorated in a European style, for children there is a modern playground.





Premium Housing Features

In addition to residential apartments, in the highest northern skyscraper is a modern hotel. There are only 225 apartments for permanent residence in the tower. And all this is premium housing. The apartments are sold with fully finished designer interiors, made in three styles, such as modern, classic and high-tech. All apartments have a separate water supply for drinking water and a modern filtration system for household. The apartments are connected to central air conditioning and heating. There is also a "warm floor" in all rooms and a modern security system. The Iset Tower will be serviced by the concierge service. Happy homeowners in the most modern house in Yekaterinburg will be able to manage all the options of their own apartments using the Smart Home multimedia system.





Prices for apartments in the Iset tower

Sale of luxury apartments was started back in 2013 (at the stage of glazing of the facade). The first buyers were found almost immediately, today the last few apartments are available for purchase. The Iset tower has various apartments; their area is from 80 to 450 m 2 . All apartments have high ceilings: 3, 4 and 8 meters. The last residential floors are occupied by unique two-level apartments. The cost of an apartment in the Iset skyscraper starts at 21.26 million rubles. Today it is the most expensive housing in Yekaterinburg. However, do not forget that the tower is located in the heart of the city, on B. Yeltsin Street. The panoramic windows of the apartments offer a unique view of Yekaterinburg and its environs, and from some apartments you can admire the Iset River.

Buying housing in this fantastic skyscraper today is possible directly with the developer. Buyers are offered a variety of payment options, including installment and mortgage. Today, one of the most recognizable buildings in the city is the Iset tower. Yekaterinburg is developing rapidly, but it is unlikely that in the near future a more luxurious residential complex will be built in it.
