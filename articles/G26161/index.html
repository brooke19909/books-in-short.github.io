How to make a wallet out of paper?

In order for you to succeed, just follow the simple instructions outlined below. To create an original decoration, you only need scissors, paper, rhinestones or beads for decoration. Take a rectangular sheet of paper. The size of its sides should be correlated 2: 3 to get an accurate proportional model. To outline the transverse and longitudinal lines, fold the sheet first across and then along. Expand the paper and bend all four corners of the rectangle so that the paired edges are joined along a longitudinal line. Bend the upper corners of the resulting figure to the base of each of the triangles, and then bend again inward so that the sides of the triangles join. Now bend the sides of the resulting structure outward. You should get a square. Fold it over the central fold. Then remove one small triangle from the middle. They will close your accessory. That's all the secrets of how to make a wallet out of paper with two convenient compartments.

Also try creating a designer model. Decorate your wallet with beautiful rhinestones or bright beads.
