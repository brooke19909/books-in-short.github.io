Newspaper Roses: A Great Gift

Going on holiday to a female representative is customary with flowers. This rule applies to close relatives. However, one can show imagination and replace the bouquet of natural flowers that is familiar to everyone and replace them with roses made by oneself. There are a number of advantages that this gift will have. Do-it-yourself craft for grandmother‚Äôs birthday in the form of roses from the newspaper, firstly, will be stored longer than usual flowers, and secondly, the hero of the occasion will like it more, because she will understand that her grandson or granddaughter did it specifically for her.

So, to make such an unusual bouquet you will need glue, pages of old books or newspapers, scissors and ribbon for decoration. You can also draw on cardboard and cut 5 petals of various sizes. Thanks to these templates, DIY crafts for grandma‚Äôs birthday will be created faster and easier. If a small child will make roses, then the petals should be drawn by an adult.





Production of parts for roses

It is necessary to start making a bouquet from cutting long strips from a newspaper. Their width should correspond to the width of the available templates. Next, each strip should be folded with an accordion, put a petal on top, circle the outline and cut. In total, 5 petals of each size need to be prepared. The width of the smallest part should be approximately 3 cm, and each subsequent 1-1.5 cm more. The upper part of the resulting petals should be rounded.

Further, in order to add volume, each petal should be bent vertically in the center so that the curved edges are on the opposite side of the part. Now you need to make the stem - for this you need to twist the sheet into a tube and fix its end, preventing unwinding.

Rose assembly

It remains to collect the flower. A petal should be glued to the paper tube, which will be only 2-3 mm above the stem. Consistently, you need to attach all the parts - first smaller, then large. When all the petals are glued to the stem, the rose will only have to dry and can be given. You can make a lot of such flowers and form a whole bouquet of them.

Now that you know about the possibility of creating roses from newspapers, you will never again have the question of how to make a gift for mom, grandmother and sister with your own hands - such flowers can please any lady.





DIY postcard

Another way to show attention is to grandmother's birthday card. With your own hands you can make this little thing in many ways - here a lot depends on the age of the child or adult who will manufacture it. So, a baby can create a card for her grandmother in the following way: fold a sheet of colored cardboard in half and decorate its front.

To do this, cut out 9 circles from paper of different colors, they do not have to be very even, most importantly, their sizes should be different. You will also need 3 bright buttons. In the upper part of the future postcard at different heights, stick 3 circles of the largest diameter. From above, alternately glue two more similar parts of a smaller size, and then a button in their center, this will turn out flowers. However, this is not a complete birthday card for grandmother. With your own hands, using felt pens, pencils or paints, you need to finish stems and leaves. And inside write a congratulation.

Coffee Bean Hot Stand: A Great Gift for Grandma

A hot stand is another useful birthday craft for your grandmother, which you can do with your own hands. For work, you need square boards of the right size, burlap, glue gun and coffee beans themselves . First, the base must be pasted over on both sides with burlap, and from above, the expected external side must be covered with grains. This should be done from the edge in a circle, gradually moving to the center. It is important to glue each grain firmly and place it carefully to get a beautiful pattern.

You can make a whole set of such coasters, the number of which in the set will correspond to the number of letters that make up the name of the grandmother. If you choose this method of creating a stand, then you will need to start gluing with grains by creating a letter, and then fill the remaining space. Similarly, you can lay out any inscription. When the coasters are ready, for greater expressiveness of the laid out letter, it can be covered with paint, for example, white.





Grandma's box

A multi-colored box is another interesting crafts for a grandmother for her birthday. With your own hands you can do this thing in literally 20 minutes. And the grandmother will be where to store a variety of little things, including buttons, needles and much more.

For the manufacture of crafts will need wooden flat sticks, glue gun and paint. First, all the sticks must be painted in various colors and dried. Then lay them on the surface next to one another, reaching the required width. After that, glue two more sticks and place them across the existing ones from two different ends. Next, you should increase the height of the walls, laying wooden blanks on top of each other so that they cross at the corners.





When the required casket height is reached, it remains to make a lid. The basis for it is made similarly to the bottom of the box, however, from the outside, you will still need to attach the handle, gluing together 4 sticks together in height. The finished product can become either an independent gift for grandmother, or the original packaging for a more expensive gift.
