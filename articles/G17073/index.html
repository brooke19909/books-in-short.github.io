A receiver for a TV in the country: overview, types, features of choice

Most of the summer cottages are located at a considerable distance from the settlements, many of them are cut off from some of the benefits of civilization, mainly from digital television. Many of us, going out of town for a whole summer, are not ready to refuse to watch our favorite TV shows, so we take a digital receiver for a TV to the country with us.

However, arriving at the place, they find out that its power and technical characteristics are not enough to receive a signal and watch channels in high resolution. To prevent a similar situation from happening with you, competently approach the choice of a TV set-top box.

Subtleties of choice

Before buying a digital TV receiver, you need to determine the type of receiver. It can be a satellite dish, home or outdoor television antenna. The choice of this or that equipment depends on a number of factors. In order not to make a mistake with the choice, it is worth answering for yourself several of the most important questions:

  1. How many TV channels do you want to watch? If their number exceeds 10, or government programs are not included in their list, then you are better off choosing a receiver for a TV for your summer cottage along with a satellite dish.
  2. How far is the suburban area from the city? The farther your hacienda is from the village, the lower the likelihood that a conventional antenna will be able to receive a television signal.
  3. Are there television towers in the summer cottage? Most modern TV towers broadcast in digital format. To receive the signal in this case, a combination of a conventional set-top box and a home antenna will suffice.

The choice of the device will be significantly affected by the home budget. If you are ready to spend a certain amount on watching TV channels, then you can safely choose a set of satellite television. When this option is impossible, you will have to make a bit of effort and spend some free time.



Satellite dish receiver

After weighing all the pros and cons and deciding that the receiver for the TV at the cottage for the plate is the most acceptable option for you, you need to answer another question: which one should I choose, whether it is independent or attached to a particular operator? In the first case, you can configure your ‚Äúplate‚Äù on different satellites and watch any free channels supplied from any provider. But image quality and signal stability can significantly suffer. If you choose a satellite dish of a certain company, you will have access to the channels of only this provider. However, image quality and signal stability will be much better. In addition, you do not have to configure the equipment yourself.

In addition to this, you need to decide whether you are ready to leave the receiver for the TV for the winter cottage? If not, then give preference to independent devices - they are more mobile.

Traditional antenna and receiver

Today, TV towers broadcast a digital signal in the decimeter range - in the same way as analog television. Therefore, to watch TV channels, you can select any antenna. It remains only to decide which one: passive or active?

In the first case, the amplifier block will be located separately from the receiving structure itself, and in the second, the amplifier block will be combined with the "horns" receiving the signal. Passive antennas are more durable, but interference interferes with their operation. Active devices are not weatherproof, but are more compact and better able to receive signals.

Internet TV

Few people know about the possibility of watching TV channels via the Internet due to the non-proliferation of this broadcasting technology. Unfortunately, even today the quality of cellular communications leaves much to be desired in many regions of Russia. However, if there was never a problem with mobile communications at the summer cottage, then this option promises to be the most optimal.

To watch TV channels, you need to purchase a special receiver from a mobile operator - MTS, Beeline, TELE2 or from other providers, connect a subscription to channels and buy an access card - an analogue of a SIM card in your phone. How not to miscalculate with a purchase?

In order for the set-top box you purchased to properly ‚Äúcatch‚Äù digital TV channels, before choosing a receiver for your TV in the country, you must carefully study the local conditions. Find out which mobile operator works best in the area, how far the TV tower is from your home, and whether your neighbors have satellite dishes.

When the budget does not allow you to sort through the various options, start with the cheapest and easiest - connect a bundle of wire to your TV. If you can find the gear, then you can get by with the indoor antenna. If there is no signal at all (there is not even interference), then even a street receiver will not cope here. Experiment, and you will surely find something suitable for you.
