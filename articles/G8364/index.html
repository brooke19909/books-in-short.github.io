In order to understand what are the main advantages and disadvantages of a market economy, we will try to understand the definition of its concept and basic functions. What problems does the economic system solve?

Before answering the question what advantages and disadvantages of a market economy are decisive, it is important to know why it is needed at all, and what basic problem it solves. Actually, any economy is called upon to solve the global contradiction between the unlimited material needs of society and limited, or even rare, economic resources.

Development trends

The system of market relations never stands still - this is its main advantage, and the shortcomings of a market economy are offset by trends to correct them. A large role here is played by state regulation and the changing forms of all types of entrepreneurial activity. New forms of labor relations arise when hired workers take part in the management of an enterprise or firm. The state has the ability to influence the narrowing or expansion of competition. The share of small businesses that are more mobile to changing conditions is increasing. Although profit still remains in the first place, enterprises are interested in maintaining their competitiveness and are taking targeted actions for this. And, most importantly, a market economy is focused on satisfying consumer demand.
