The hotel is located on the island of Phuket (Thailand) in the bay of Kata Noi. Patong is 18 kilometers away. You can get here by tuk-tuk or taxi. The nearest airport is 67 kilometers from the hotel. Travel time is about one and a half hours.

Check in, departure

According to our compatriots, acquaintance with Thai hospitality is waiting for you from the first minutes of arrival at the Katathani Phuket Beach Resort 5 * hotel. So, you will immediately be offered soft drinks, wet towels and bracelets made from fresh orchids. Regardless of the time of your arrival, the reception administrator will do everything possible so that you can check into the room as soon as possible, and you do not have to spend time waiting. Please note that new arrivals are asked to leave a deposit (approximately two hundred dollars). You can deposit it both in cash and with a plastic card. The amount of the deposit will be returned to you upon discharge from the hotel.

As for the day of departure, then, in accordance with the rules of the hotel, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. If your departure from the hotel is planned at a later time, then you can extend your stay at an additional cost. It is advisable to notify the administrator at the reception in advance.

Nutrition

According to travelers who stayed at Katathani Phuket Beach Resort 4 * (Phuket Island, Thailand) in their reviews, only breakfast is included in the price. Beach vacation

According to many travelers, one of the main advantages of this hotel is its location a couple of hundred meters from the popular Kata Beach. However, most tourists prefer to sit directly on a fine clean sand, laying towels or a mat. As for the sea, as our compatriots note, waves are not uncommon here.
