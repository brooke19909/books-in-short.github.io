Guests of the recreation center may be offered accommodation in comfortable and cozy deluxe rooms or in the standard and economy rooms that are most fully equipped for family vacations. "Demidov Manor" (Nizhny Tagil) is a recreation center with the atmosphere of old noble houses: wooden, spacious, filled with light and fresh air.
