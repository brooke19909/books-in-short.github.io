Principle of operation

A tracker is installed on the vehicle, capable of receiving signals from the navigation system. Moreover, satellite signals and a global wireless network can be used. For this, a GLONASS system, GPS or LBS module must be installed. This tracker saves in its memory all the events occurring with the car. At certain intervals, this information is sent to the main server or to the owner‚Äôs phone in the form of an SMS message.

Functions

The main function of the system is to determine the exact location of the vehicle with an accuracy of 2.5 meters. In addition, it allows you to view the history of vehicles. More modern systems also provide voice communication with the driver, allow you to record audio of the surroundings near the car, and also report the amount of gas mileage and any deviations of the car from a given route.

Differences

Monitoring systems for moving objects are very similar to each other, basically their trackers are identical, but there are still important differences that distinguish different objects in the market. Naturally, the main difference is state affiliation.



There are also various methods of operating radio frequencies. So, for example, in GPS control systems, weak protection and stable coded CDMA access, while they are more economical and low cost. But the GLONASS system has a secure, reliable dedicated FDMA line, but at the same time it requires more resources.



In addition, there are differences in the locations of the satellites receiving the signal. So, Russian satellites are unchanged during the rotation of the Earth. It uses eight satellites and three planes. But in the case of American satellites, constant synchronization and adjustment of data is needed. There is the operation of four satellites and six planes. There are also differences in different latitudes and in the signal error. For example, GPS monitoring systems can distort data from two to four meters, and the GLONASS system from three to six. According to forecasts, such a gap will be reduced to ten centimeters by 2020.

Recommendations

The best option for transport companies is to use systems that support both navigation methods. They make it possible to make the percentage of detection accuracy of vehicles better and work even in difficult conditions of large cities with a high density of buildings affecting signals from satellites.

Scope of application

Satellite monitoring of transport is relevant for courier and service services that need to receive operational data regarding the location of couriers and cargo. Thus, it is possible to quickly track which of the employees is closest to the office of the client of the company. They are also needed by companies that have their own fleet, including taxi services, logistics companies, and public transport regulators.



With the help of monitoring systems for moving objects, you can actually track the path, calculate the most convenient and inexpensive routes and reduce the cost of shipping goods. In addition, this will protect the company from unscrupulous employees who specifically wind up extra kilometers or drain gasoline for their needs. It is worth considering that modern systems also allow you to control the driver. For example, if a car drives outside a previously defined route, you can remotely shut off the engine. There is also an option to control the ignition by tuning the car so that it will work only after sending an SMS message. GPS monitoring systems allow you to limit the speed of the machine, thereby affecting the reduction of fuel consumption. Many trackers are equipped with an alarm button that allows the driver to call for help in a timely manner, protecting his life and the safety of the cargo. And the presence of voice communications significantly reduces the consumption of mobile devices.
