Rooms

The place for rest belongs to the category of medium in size, as its number of rooms provides for a small number of apartments - 75. Each of them is made in the best traditions, there is a high-quality repair and modern furniture that is very comfortable.

As for their placement, a small part of the rooms is located in the main block - there are only 9 apartments of the Standard class. There are also 9 chalets, each of which is a two-story building - they contain 36 rooms of the standard group, which are more likely intended for a family vacation. In a separate part of the territory, 10 villas are built, in which 30 rooms are located, among them there are 2 luxury rooms.



"Standard"

In the standard rooms of the Resort Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort, everything is simple, the overall picture of the interior is represented by a harmonious combination of white and brown colors. A huge amount of detail is made of dark wood.

As for the furniture, there is a double bed for guests, on which there is an orthopedic mattress. If desired, guests can use the cabinets and cabinets, which are designed to store things. There is also a desk and chair - such furniture is designed to work. The interior has a lot of textiles and lighting fixtures.

Each room has air conditioning, with which you can adjust the air temperature in the room to the optimum. Also, tourists who relax here can use the bathroom, which has all the necessary plumbing and bath accessories, as well as a set of bathrobes and towels.

"Family"

In Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort 4 * there are 36 "Family" apartments. Everything is arranged in them in such a way that the accommodation is comfortable for both adults and children.

As for the furniture equipment, it is designed for four adult guests, for whom there is the right number of beds. In addition, if necessary, in the apartment you can request a cot, which will be provided free of charge. Guests can put their things in specially designated cabinets and cabinets.

This room type also has a private bathroom and air conditioning, which operates from a central control panel. If you wish, you can rent an electronic safe for the duration of your stay. Also, if necessary, guests can use the phone, which is also paid.

"Suite"

Throughout the Resort Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort, there are only two suites. They offer superior conditions, designed for a couple of guests. Often they are chosen either by couples in love who decide to spend time together, or business people who need a comfortable environment for successful business.

This type of room is divided into two zones, one of which is a bedroom, and the second is provided as a living room.

This apartment category has a large French bed, which is designed for two, it has an orthopedic mattress, and it is covered with expensive bedding. Tourists living here put things in separate wardrobes. The interior of the room has a huge amount of textiles and lamps that make the interior more luxurious.

In the living room of the Suite there is a beautiful furniture set consisting of a large sofa and a pair of soft armchairs. there is also a coffee table made of glass and a pair of side tables. Vacationers can do work in the room, for which a desk and chair are provided. If you wish, being here, you can watch satellite channels on a plasma TV that hangs on the wall.

Conditions for children

Little guests who are on vacation at Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort 4 * (Fukuok) with their parents can also feel completely comfortable. All this is because for them in this place for recreation provides a wide range of conditions. First of all, the hotel administration pays special attention to the fact that a cot can be installed in the family-type apartments for free, which is designed as a sleeping place for small guests (up to 12 years old).

The hotel also has certain conditions for this category of tourists. If desired, children can spend time in the well-equipped playground, which is located outdoors. There are several simple children's simulators, as well as various obstacles. In addition, several swings were installed on its territory. Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort and Spa has a shallow paddling pool. which is made in accordance with all safety requirements.

Pool

Throughout the resort town, the hotel is widely known for its luxurious pool, which is available on its territory. It is quite large in size, water is regularly cleaned, so that it is always crystal clear. It has an excellent water filtration system and there is a function of automatic water heating, which can make bathing guests more comfortable at any time.

Around the pool there is a beautiful sun terrace where tourists can relax and enjoy their vacation. Here, for greater comfort, guests are provided with large sun loungers, beach umbrellas and, if necessary, you can take towels. Near the pool there is a bar where it is always possible to drink drinks and taste good snacks.

The pool area is open around the clock, but you can swim in it only at certain times - from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.



Nutrition

For all tourists staying at the Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort (Vietnam / Phu Quoc) tourists who are on an all-inclusive basis, can eat at the local restaurant as part of the buffet, which is served daily during breakfast, lunch and dinner.



The hotel has three restaurants - Laguna Seafood Restaurant, which serves traditional Vietnamese cuisine, as well as the Neptune Seafood Restaurant and La Terrace BBQ, where guests can enjoy excellent barbecue food. The menu of each of these establishments also has a considerable list of international dishes.

Guests can also visit the bar, which offers a wide range of snacks, desserts and, of course, drinks, among which are both alcoholic and non-alcoholic.



Sport

Fans of a sports lifestyle regularly visit the gym in the Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort 4 *, where there are a considerable number of simulators designed to work on different parts of the body. There is also a fitness room, where mainly the fair sex loves to attend aerobics classes.

If desired, hotel guests have the opportunity to play team sports on specially equipped playgrounds for football, volleyball and basketball. There is also a tennis court with special lighting.

Equipment for sports and team games can be taken at special points, and completely free of charge.

SPA

Fans of relaxation and beauty treatments regularly visit the SPA center, located in the main building of the Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort (Vietnam). On its territory there is an excellent thermal zone, represented by a sauna - a team of good parmeisters works in it. There are also several massage rooms where guests can relax under the arms of professional massage therapists. Here you can also make a number of cosmetic procedures that will be useful for appearance and internal health.

For business

Reviews of the Sasco Blue Lagoon Resort (4 stars) often state that this hotel is perfect for people whose life is somehow connected with doing business.

In order to be able to organize business events in the form of presentations or business meetings, there is a large conference room in which there is comfortable furniture and the necessary equipment for this. The territory of the hall is fully equipped with modern sound and lighting equipment, office equipment is presented here, as well as a small number of stationery. Guests invited to meetings can sit at the massive conference table, made of lacquered dark wood, in soft armchairs.

Beach
