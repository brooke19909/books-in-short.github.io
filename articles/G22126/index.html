Alushta. "Golden Ear" - everything for health and relaxation

On the Black Sea coast there is a beautiful resort city of Alushta. This is a place where tourists come from all over Russia and Ukraine for outdoor activities, sports and tourism. The conditions here are equally good both in summer and in other seasons. There are many health centers, recreation centers, health camps for schoolchildren and students, private hotels. Among them, the Golden Spike recreation complex compares favorably with others.

Mountain ranges and the sea



What is remarkable about the “Golden Ear” located in Alushta - a sanatorium for rest? Vacationers have a wonderful view of the mountains, overgrown with forests, and the Black Sea coast. The sanatorium is located in a beautiful place among tall slender cypress trees.

Programs for all



Ideally chosen place to stay - the city of Alushta. The Golden Ear is open all year, and guests can enjoy wellness treatments not only in the summer. In addition, the resort has all the necessary conditions for a family vacation. For the guests of the sanatorium, special programs have been developed to satisfy any of their requirements. These are discounts for children over 7 years old, and procedures for restoring health. Fans of spa treatments can choose for themselves spa programs that you don’t need to pay for, as they are included in the total cost of your stay.

Go to neighboring cities

Those who want to relax on the Black Sea, breathe in the fresh sea and mountain air, swim in the sea or the pool, must definitely go to the Golden Ear sanatorium. Alushta is located in the Crimea, just a few kilometers from it are famous resorts such as Yalta, Simferopol, Feodosia. You can visit these cities using a trolley bus or bus.

Food

Since we are talking about Crimea, it should be noted that the restaurant “Golden Ear” the restaurant pays special attention to the preparation of a wide variety of dishes. The system "buffet", which ensures the use of Crimean products in the restaurant menu.



What can you do there?

You can find rest for every taste in the Crimean place Alushta. The Golden Ear, for example, can be proud of its park with remarkable architectural forms. In the park area you can walk and take pictures, breathe the clean air of Crimea and admire its natural beauties.

Fans of evening entertainment Alushta (Golden Spike, in particular) is known for its fun animated programs and dance parties. For the youngest guests, a program with animators was also thought out here, a whole children's city was built. And if parents want to take a break from their children and swim in the pool or sunbathe on the beach, then the child can safely be left in the care of qualified teachers.

Choose a sanatorium in the resort of Alushta - "Golden Ear". Visitor reviews - from positive to enthusiastic. Here everyone will like the rest: the elderly, and young people, and those who relax with their families with children or a fun company.
