City of military glory - how many are there in Russia?

There is no limit to the heroism of the people who survived the Great Patriotic War and were able to defend their homeland. People fought for their country, perished for it. And this led to a logical result. In this review, we will talk about how, in the protection of which cities, people made an invaluable contribution to the Victory.

What is hiding under such an honorary title?





Cities of military glory of Russia. This badge of honor began to assign relatively recently. It was received by individual cities for the perseverance, courage and courage that were shown by their defenders in the struggle for freedom and independence.

The provision in which the conditions and procedure for awarding a sufficiently honorary title are formed was approved in December 2006 by the president of the country.

The necessary conditions

In the city, which was awarded the title "City of Military Glory":

1. The installation of the steles is carried out, on which the emblem of the corresponding place is depicted together with the text of the decree on the conferment of the title.

2. Various events and festive volleys take place on such days as February 23, May 9 and City Day.

All these requirements must be fulfilled without fail by those cities that received such an honorary meritorious title.

Symbol of the city of military glory

The stela was approved by the Organizing Committee under the name Victory. This happened after summing up the results of the All-Russian competition. A memorial stela means a column topped with the emblem of the Russian Federation. It is installed on the corresponding pedestal, on the front side of which is the text of the decree on the appointment of the honorary title.

In the corners of the square are special bas-reliefs, which depict certain events that served as the reason for receiving the title.

Complex opening

In 2010, the opening of the architectural ensemble called "The Tomb of the Unknown Soldier". This happened after all the necessary restoration work. The complex is located in the Alexander Garden near the Moscow Kremlin. The structure contains a stele on which the names of all cities with an honorary title are marked.

Heroic cities can now enter the collection

Cities of military glory can now enter one large collection. To do this, you just need to collect all the coins. And, most likely, there will be a lot of people who want to collect such a collection.
