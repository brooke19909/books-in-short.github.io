Samsung La Fleur is a great phone for stylish ladies.

Samsung Electronics Corporation is the world-famous manufacturer of household appliances and communications in the world. She earned her fame for the reliability, functional content and accessibility of her products. At present, perhaps, any civilized person knows about the existence of such a Japanese company.

The history of the creation of this industrial giant begins back in 1938. It was founded by Lee Bong Chul as a rice trading company. Already in 1969, after the merger with SANYO, Samsung Electronics Corporation appeared. In 1972, they released their first black and white television.





The very first cell phone was developed in 1997. After almost twenty years, naturally, the products of the corporation have changed beyond recognition.
