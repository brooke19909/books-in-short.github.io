Caribbean Island Travel - Dominica

Not everyone knows that Dominica is not related to the Dominican Republic and often confuse them. In fact, Dominica is an island country located between two islands - Guadeloupe and Martinique, and the Dominican Republic is located on the island of Haiti. The island of Dominica is very famous due to the fact that it was filmed on it "Pirates of the Caribbean".

The island is very developed, although it is considered "untouched by civilization." The transport network is well developed there, so there will be no problems with moving around Dominica.
