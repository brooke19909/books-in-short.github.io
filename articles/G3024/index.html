Oven with microwave function: what is it and how does it work?

A microwave is a rather actively used kitchen appliance, which is necessary when you need to heat something, make hot dishes or prepare plain snacks. This technique is time-saving. Even those who prefer to cook in the oven sometimes use microwave ovens. In a modern house, a microwave is simply necessary, it is difficult to do without it. At the moment, there is already an oven with a microwave function. You can consider what its advantages are.



Some users appreciate the small size of the device. It is worth saying that a combined device has much greater capabilities. In the microwave, it is impossible to find some of the modes available in the oven. The microwave device is not able to use the lower and upper heating. The more functions each device is equipped with, the more diverse the diet.



The microwave oven is equipped with combined modes. It is generally believed that the microwave is not able to cook. If you use the capabilities of the oven, the speed of the first device will be an excellent assistant for achieving a common goal - fast and tasty dishes.

If the topic of the advantages of such a device is touched, then it is worth considering its disadvantages. A microwave oven can heat up lunch or thaw chicken in literally a few minutes, and for an oven it takes a little longer. However, the difference is not so great. Despite the fact that you need a little more time, the dish will be warmed up more evenly. An oven with microwave function is usually located under the hob, i.e. on the floor. If you want to warm up several plates of soup in a row, you will need to sit down several times and do not spill the contents of the plates. That is why it is important to consider the most suitable place for the device.



Why use a microwave oven?

In such a device, you can cook full-fledged dishes, and not just heat and defrost foods. The cake is usually baked for about 30-40 minutes, and if it is made from shortcrust pastry, then longer. In such a device, you can bake any meat, the cooking time will depend on the size of the pieces. You can cook the whole chicken in 20-30 minutes. To do this, spread it breast-down, and after 15 minutes of cooking, turn it over. When combining the functions of the oven and microwave, you can cook meatloaf, potato casserole, scrambled eggs and more.

If you decide to choose a microwave oven, then you should know about some points. There are a number of products not intended for microwaves: flammable food, products containing little water, objects with a hard shell. It is strictly forbidden to use aluminum cookware, pour hot water into the oven chamber, or cook popcorn at high power.
