The best hotels in Turkey for families with children - review, rating, description and reviews

Turkey is a wonderful country for families with children. Here are really ideal conditions for small tourists. Even if you buy a tour to a 5-star hotel, then the prices will not really hit the family budget. The best all inclusive hotels in Turkey are always happy for families with kids. Parents will not have to think separately about the proper nutrition of the child, nor about interesting entertainments, nor about safety. The hotel will take care of all this.

Holidays in Turkey with children

If you are going to Turkish resorts with your little ones, it is better to choose hotels with their own beaches, which are mainly located on the first beach line. There are many such on the coast of Antalya and other Mediterranean resorts of this country. Many of these beaches have the blue flag of the EU, which means that they are environmentally friendly and quite suitable for recreation even for the smallest children. In almost all such hotels, professional children's animations are constantly organized, colorful and exciting show programs are organized for younger guests, competitions, quizzes, etc. are held.

You probably thought about the language barrier, but most of the administrations of Turkish resort hotels have solved this issue by inviting animation specialists from the Russian Federation. In a word, here your child will have an interesting and eventful leisure, and you can truly enjoy your vacation, visit the hammam, spa area, swim in the pool and be absolutely confident and calm for your children.

So you can leave the beach at the hotel at any time and put him to bed, and then return when he wakes up.

In the case when the hotel is located at a greater distance from the coast, tourists to the sea, as a rule, are delivered by special buses operating on schedule. Antalya

Well, this, of course, is the most popular of the Turkish Mediterranean resorts. It is suitable for all types of tourists, including those who came to rest with their children. Children of school age who are interested in history and archeology will especially like it. The beaches in Antalya are both sandy and pebble. Naturally, parents choose for vacation those best hotels in Turkey, which are located near the coast with sand. Some like club hotels with cottages and bungalows, which are located away from the center, that is, the hustle and bustle of youth entertainment. Throughout the resort there are large and small markets where you can buy everything you need: diapers, baby food and other baby care products.

Antalya is famous for a wide range of entertainment facilities for young tourists: amusement parks, water parks, mini zoos, aquariums, etc.

Belek

A lot of people, when asked which of the Turkish resorts have the best hotels for holidays with the whole family, will answer that it is Belek. It is located 20 kilometers from Anatolia. The resort extends within the national park, so its beaches can be safely called the most environmentally friendly on the country's coast. The picturesque Taurus Mountains protect the town from the weather. The air here is simply delightful, because unlike some desert resorts, Belek has many pine, cedar and eucalyptus forests. To relax in the best hotels in Turkey on this coast, it is more correct to come in May or early June, when it is not very hot, but also not cool, as happens in the evening in April and October.
