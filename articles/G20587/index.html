Sea stone: name, description. Types of sea stones. DIY sea stone crafts (photo)

Sea stone (or pebbles), which we all loved to collect in childhood on the Black Sea beaches, has an interesting history. Millions of years ago, the formation of the Caucasus Mountains occurred with a violent eruption of volcanoes, flows of frozen lava in the form of plates, debris and small pieces were carried away by the course of mountain rivers and fell into the sea. Sea waves continued to grind and run into fragments, grinding and giving pebbles a rounded shape.

Types of sea stones

That pebble, which we all love to play on the beach, is a real rock formation, a witness to prehistoric times. Light porous pumice stone - the remains of petrified volcanic magma, frozen far from the site of eruption and trapped in air - hence the numerous pores. The name of sea stones, more smooth and weighty, is basalts and tuffs. They formed at the exit of the lava from the volcano. And the heaviest sea stone - granite - originated in its very vent.





The shiny egg-shaped rounded granite boulders that we can see on the beach are a strong and shiny (thanks to a mixture of mica, quartz and feldspar) sample of volcanic rock. Monuments and obelisks are erected from it, it is in demand by builders and designers. Other finishing stones - basalts, tuffs of different colors - are perfect for surface lining. Almost any sea stone can be used for applied or decorative purposes.

On the beach you can find samples of semiprecious minerals - syenites, porphyrites, chrysolites. These beautiful sea stones are used in the creation of jewelry and jewelry. There is also a favorite of healers - chalcedony. These stones, even more ancient than rocks, also come from volcanic craters.

How can I use stones

They look very beautiful in combination with plants, and almost any. If you lay the surface of a flower pot with stones, it will not only save the earth from drying out, but it will also look very stylish. In addition, hand-made crafts made of sea stone, these days, are popular, like any hand-made.

A garden bed decorated with stones will look no less wonderful. For masters of landscape design, they are truly irreplaceable material, especially in combination with fancy snags and grass.

The pots or flower pots can be decorated by gluing their dry non-fat surface with reliable glue with flat pebbles of a suitable size. An original stand for hot dishes will come out of the same flat small stones - they should be laid out, smeared with glue, the usual stand-napkin from the store. If you can pick up the white stones in the right amount, the perfect stand will come out. But you can take color, and combine them or come up with a pattern.





If you have a lot of stones, but they are all of different colors and look bad next to each other, make several coasters and paint with acrylic paints in the chosen color (for example, black).

We decorate the interior of the apartment

By the same principle, a bath mat or threshold is made. Stones are best glued to a rubber base, which is suitable for a regular purchased mat. By placing your feet on an exclusive product, you massage the active points and improve your health.

Another useful element of the hallway interior is a shoe stand in the form of a flat tray, in which a layer of stones is poured (even without glue). Original and practical!

An exclusive stone-age floor lamp can be made by drilling several rounded stones and planting them on the principle of a children's pyramid (large ones below for stability) on the metal leg of a disassembled lamp.

If you have already taken up the design of the hall in the "stone" style, try decorating the pebble frame of the wall mirror in a wooden frame. It should turn out very stylish and unusual. The gaps between the large pebbles are filled with small shells and stone chips.

How to decorate a refrigerator

Fridge magnets are in every family with children. But we will have unusual ones - from pebbles! Let's make monster magnets. We washed and dried flat pebbles on both sides in bright colors. After drying, draw ‚Äúmonsters‚Äù creepy mouths with bared teeth and glue eyes.

On the back of each stone we attach a magnet with superglue. If the stones are large enough and heavy - a pair of magnets. The original decoration for the refrigerator is ready!

Best of all, oval-shaped pebbles produce bright fish, as well as birds with unusual plumage. You can make a mouse. In addition to pebbles and superglue, prepare scissors, paper and colored shreds. Ears and a tail for the future mouse are cut from the fabric, glued to the elongated gray stone in the right places. Paws will be smaller pebbles, tendrils - bundles of thread. If you prefer a bird, then take a more round pebble, make a beak from a fabric of suitable color (fold the shred with a triangle with a sharp end, glue it to the pebble head with the wide side). Mark the eyes in the right place, take care of the plumage. Done!
