How to win tic tac toe? Simple and clear diagrams

In life, there are times when there is free time, but absolutely nothing to occupy yourself. If there is another bored person next to you, then together you can play the simplest game that everyone has known since childhood - tic-tac-toe. Each person has an instinctive desire to win. "How to win tic-tac-toe?" - you ask. It is very simple. This article will help you master several winning techniques.



So, let's figure out the rules of the game. The standard size of the field for tic-tac-toe is 3x3. The essence of the game is to put three of your crosses or zeros in one row diagonally, vertically or horizontally. The moves are made by the players in turn. In truth, the answer to the question of how to win tic-tac-toe, simply does not exist. After all, if both players do not make mistakes, then it is impossible to win. This article will mainly consider those cases when one of the rivals makes a serious miscalculation. This is quite enough to bring the game to a victorious ending.



Now we will consider several schemes that will help beat an opponent on a 3x3 field. So, imagine that you make the first move. We advise you to take the central cell of the field. This will give you a significant advantage. In response to this, your opponent can make two moves: put a toe (cross) diagonally from your figure or directly, horizontally or vertically. If the opponent chose the second option, then congratulations! Now the victory is yours! The next move you can put a cross (zero) in any free cell of the field, but not symmetrical to the opponent‚Äôs figure relative to your first move. After that, make sure that in two directions you have two pieces, and then the opponent will be able to prevent you from only one of them. This is one of the ways to win tic-tac-toe.

You can practice the winning technique that is now known to you not only in public, but also online. This is really very convenient, because the Internet has a wide range of applications based on the game of tic-tac-toe. You can choose not only a decent level of difficulty, but also a unique design of the field and figures that you can enjoy as much as you like, and then at any time you can stop the game if you get bored. We advise you to play tic-tac-toe on the Internet for free as often as possible if you want to defeat your opponents in reality.



As already mentioned above, the size of the field in tic-tac-toe can be any, but most often 3x3, 4x4, 5x5 are used. The larger the size of the field, the correspondingly more difficult to win. In this case, the schemes for defeating the opponent described above do not work, since these are other tic-tac-toe. 5 in a row is the goal of the 5x5 game, and to win, you need to not only know some standard positions, but also be able to analyze the position of the pieces on the field.

So, now you know how to win tic-tac-toe, so any opponent will be on your shoulder and will not pose any threat to you! We wish you good luck in the game, although in this case you will not need it: you know excellent schemes that will certainly help you win!
