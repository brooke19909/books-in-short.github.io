Architectural design

It represents the creation of a prototype of a future non-residential or residential building. Professionals call the prototype of this object an architectural project. Work on it is a laborious and lengthy process. Building design involves the development of technical and design documentation, the implementation of numerous mathematical calculations, the preparation of engineering calculations, estimates and schedules, as well as writing various descriptions and explanatory notes. These requirements are suitable for all types of construction and engineering works. Building design involves the implementation of such operations in order to obtain the desired result after completion of construction work.

Design sequence

This creative process is carried out in our country according to certain state standards and norms in various sectors of the economy. Development of project documentation is carried out at the following stages:

  • development of a preliminary design;
  • study of the material;
  • execution of working documentation;
  • approval of the finished project.

Consider the design steps. At the first stage, coordination of materials with executive authorities and state supervision is not supposed. Professionals consider the nuances of the sketch to think over the main details of the future object before a final decision is made on its implementation in real construction.

Using the preliminary design, they solve the following problems:

  • urban planning justification for the location of the new construction site on the ground;
  • demonstration of the internal layout and appearance of the created object;
  • identifying the attractiveness of the project from the point of view of investors;
  • determination of historical, cultural, urban, sanitary and environmental requirements.

Design Features

This technique is used not only in the construction industry, but also in the organizational structure of management. Risk in the managerial aspect is defined as the level of uncertainty in predicting the result. It is always associated with the choice of alternatives and the calculation of the probability of the result for each individual alternative.

The design of structures in a production and economic organization is considered as a complex object, including economic, administrative, organizational, informational, economic interactions that lend themselves to direct study and rational design, as well as socio-psychological relationships and characteristics. They are directly related to the skill level and abilities of employees, leadership style, attitude to their official duties. The peculiarity of the problem of designing the structure of organizational management is that it should not be adequately presented in the form of the task of formal selection of the ideal version of the organizational structure according to the formulated, mathematically justified optimality criterion. The problem involves several criteria at once, therefore, to solve it, combine the scientific methods of modern analysis, modeling, evaluation of organizational systems with the functioning of the leader, expert and specialist in the selection and evaluation of ideal options for organizational solutions.
