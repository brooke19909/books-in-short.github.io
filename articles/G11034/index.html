Hotels of Mirny, Yakutia: addresses, reviews and photos

Yakutia is a unique northern region that awaits tourists who want to conquer the vast expanses of the coldest region of Russia. The most visited area here is the Republic of Sakha. People come here for the purpose of fishing, hunting, outdoor activities, as well as on a business trip and at work.

This review will present to readers the most popular places to stay in Mirny (Sakha, Yakutia). There are few hotels here, but they are all ready to offer their customers a fairly high and high-quality service. And also, in addition to official places, for the night you can pick up options for rented apartments or apartments, which are presented on various message boards.

The most famous hotels

As already mentioned above, Mirny cannot boast of a large number of hotels, but nevertheless, city guests have a choice of several options.

The most famous hotels in Mirny (Yakutia) are:

  • "Azimuth".
  • "Vilyuy."
  • "Energy".
  • "Pegasus".

Below the reader can become more familiar with each of these hotels.

Hotel Azimut, formerly Zarnitsa

The former Zarnitsa Hotel in Mirny (Yakutia) is located in the very center of the capital of the diamond industry, three kilometers from the airport, at 11A Lenina Street. Near the hotel there is a city park, cinema, museum.

The number of rooms is 83 rooms of the following categories: single and double standard, deluxe, suite and apartments. Each room has satellite TV, telephone, safe, fridge, hairdryer, and toiletries.

The hotel has a restaurant for 150 people, which serves dishes of Russian, European and Far North cuisine. The cost of breakfast in the restaurant is 650 rubles, lunch - 590 rubles, dinner - 1,300. And the hotel also has a small cafe for 20 people.

Among the services that Azimut offers:

  • Business center.
  • Conference hall.
  • Souvenir shop.
  • Free parking.
  • Massage room.
  • Beauty saloon.
  • Room service.
  • Luggage room.

The cost of hotel accommodation starts from five thousand rubles per day.

Hotel reviews

Reviews about the Azimut Hotel are both positive and negative.

Guests note such advantages:

  • The number of rooms here is simple, but with everything you need for a comfortable stay.
  • The rooms are well heated, which is very important for this region.
  • There is free internet.
  • Good breakfasts are organized.
  • Cleaning is daily, unobtrusive.
  • The rooms are quiet.
  • Friendly staff who try to provide good service.

Of the shortcomings, guests often distinguish:

  • High cost.
  • Unpleasant smell of water.
  • Old furniture.
  • Smoking on site.

Many customers note that the place is suitable for short stays during business trips. The declared three stars hotel corresponds.

Hotel complex "Vilyuy"

Another famous hotel in the city of Mirny (Yakutia) is considered a three-star hotel "Vilyuy". This complex is located one kilometer from the central part of the city, on the square, not far from the Azimut Hotel. The exact address of the complex: Leningradsky Prospekt 25. A few minutes walk from the hotel there is a local history museum, several cafes and shops.

The hotel includes eighteen rooms of various categories: deluxe, junior suite, single, double and triple rooms, there are also economy rooms (without a shower). Each room has the necessary furniture, TV, refrigerator, Internet access.

The hotel has a small cafe, here guests can have a hearty and inexpensive meal of Russian and European cuisine. Opening hours - from 10 am to 6 pm.

It should also be noted that the hotel has an attentive, polite and professionally trained staff who tries to do everything to make every guest of the hotel feel comfortable.

The cost of accommodation in Vilyui starts from 2700 rubles per person, this price includes breakfast, which is served in the room until 9 a.m.

Hotel reviews

Most of the guests claim that Vilyui is fully consistent with its classification (three stars). The level of service at the hotel is good, the staff is friendly, the breakfasts are hearty and tasty, the rooms and bedding are clean, the rooms are cozy and quiet, the prices are more than adequate compared to Zarnitsa.

Among the minuses, some clients identified interruptions with hot water, a cafe closed for the weekend, an unstable Internet signal, and shabby furniture.

Bottom line: Hotel Vilyuy is a good place to stay at a very attractive price.

Guest house "Energy"

Another Mirny hotel (Yakutia), located near the center, is Energia.

Judging by the description, Energia is a modern residential complex consisting of 55 rooms of the standard, junior suite and luxury categories. Each room has air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, new furniture.

Among the main services that are provided to customers:

  • The Internet.
  • A restaurant.
  • Bar.
  • Laundry.
  • Parking.
  • Conference hall.

Prices for accommodation in Energia are quite affordable, from 2100 rubles per person. If desired, you can pay for the room with meals. Breakfast costs 200 p., Lunch and dinner - 250 p.

Hotel reviews are pretty good. The guests like the number of rooms, service, service, the possibility of three meals a day. Among the shortcomings, tourists note the lack of an elevator (a three-story building) and the remoteness of shops. Go to the nearest market for about 20 minutes.

The hotel was built last year and is worthy of competing with the hotels presented above.

Hotel complex "Pegasus"

Another well-known hotel in the city is located on Gornyakov Street (Mirny, Yakutia). The hotel has the status of two stars. A 15-minute drive from the property is the airport, within walking distance of the central market, restaurant, pool.

The hotel is a three-story building built in 2014. It offers tourists 24 rooms with all the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay, there are also VIP rooms. The hotel has a parking lot, self-service kitchen, laundry, ironing room.

The price of accommodation (from 3 thousand rubles) includes parking, internet, breakfast.

Conclusion

Summing up, we can say that despite the shortage of hotels in Mirny (Yakutia), there are decent places to stay. Here you can stay for the night in comfortable conditions and at a reasonable price.
