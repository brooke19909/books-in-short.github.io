Making a Luntik costume for a children's party

The beloved hero of all babies Luntik is, one might say, a symbol of kindness in the modern multicapital world. And of course, the kids really like to get used to the characters of the cartoons. Luntik is no exception. This image is perfect for a children's party or matinee in kindergarten. Read on how to make a Luntik costume with your own hands in our article.

Simple Luntik Outfit

For those who do not know, Luntik is a creature that has flown from space. Represents a sweet and good-natured friend of all children. Luntik has four ears, a rounded tummy, large eyes, and he is completely purple. Based on this, you can roughly imagine in which direction to move in order to make a Luntik costume.

So, to create a simple costume, prepare:

  • purple sundress;
  • pink fleece;
  • bezel;
  • purple fleece;
  • thin boa of lilac color;
  • glue gun;
  • threads.

Having prepared the necessary, we begin work:

1. From a pink fleece fabric, cut one large circle with a diameter of 15-20 cm. And five circles with a diameter of 3-4 cm. Using a machine, sew a large circle on the belly of a sundress. Then sew 5 small circles on top of it. It should be a trace.

2. From the purple and pink fleece, cut drop-shaped ears, 4 pieces of each of the flowers. Sew the pink eye with the purple together. There should be 4 bilateral ears. Since the ears in the Luntik costume should be slightly twisted, fold them and sew the edges together. Then sew the two ears together.

3. Using a glue gun, glue the paired ears to the rim. In order to hide the attachment points and traces of glue, fix a thin boa on top. Luntik's costume is over!

Luntik's hat

Instead of a rim, you can make a hat that will make the costume recognizable. You will need all the same materials that were required for Luntik's ears. In addition to them, you need a lilac cotton hat, pink fabric, threads.

Getting started:

1. Sew the ears in the same manner as described above. The difference will be only in the number of layers of lilac fabric. In this version, we will seal the ears and make them three-layer. Accordingly, the lilac fabric should be 2 layers, pink one.

2. Sew the ears sewn together among themselves on a hat.

3. From a pink cloth, cut a flower and sew it to the hat. The middle of the flower can be decorated with a button.

How much does a Luntik outfit cost?

If for some reason you did not want to make a Luntik children's costume yourself, you can always buy it. Prices, depending on the configuration, vary from 1,500 to 3,000 rubles. You can also rent a suit. Find places in your city where this service is provided.
