What is the question of the dangers of double boiler related?

The main thing that worries buyers is the effect of steam on plastic containers. Undoubtedly, plastic is convenient, both in everyday life and in production. But under the influence of elevated temperatures, the plastic releases a certain amount of chemicals, which, with constant regular ingestion into the body, can lead to disruption of the endocrine system. Such studies were recently conducted by the guys from CertiChem, PlastiPure and employees of Georgetown University. The study was based on materials that are actively used on the market, including those announced as not containing bisphenol-A. This chemical substance is highly active, and the same Braun company mentioned above, in the characteristics for double boilers, indicates the high quality of the material of the products and the absence of BPA in it. But according to the results of the experiment, it is clear that in addition to this bisphenol, there are many other substances in the plastic that negatively affect the human body.

What is the way out of this situation?

If you are very frightened by the fact of using plastic in double boiler, which is understandable, but you want healthy food, then a steamer pan may be the way out of this situation.





Its advantage is that the material from which it is made is most often stainless steel or anodized aluminum. In general, the pan that you use daily, but it is not simple, but a double boiler pan. The principle of operation is the same as that of an electric device, adjusted for the absence of a timer and warning equipment. You yourself must follow the cooking process, the availability of sufficient water and so on. It is even easier to use a steaming basket.





It can be placed in a suitable container already in the farm, and voila - the double boiler is ready. The instructions for use are simple - put the products, cover, follow the water. This is the easiest and cheapest way to get healthy food, unless, of course, you put this for yourself a priority in nutrition. Fried lovers such food will not be very familiar, and to force to consume fish or steamed vegetables is very difficult. Of course, in food cooked traditionally in sunflower oil, there are a lot of harmful substances released at elevated temperatures, which cause diseases of the cardiovascular system. But if you are thinking about your health and looking for a way to at least reduce the possible risks, steaming can be a way out.
