Solar Charger: Specifications, Purpose

Recently, a device such as a solar-powered charger has gained great popularity. In this article we will consider what such devices are, why they are needed and what parameters need to be paid attention to when choosing solar charges. But first, a little theory.



Quite powerful radiation is constantly coming to the Earth from the Sun - more than 1000 watts per square meter. Thanks to the photocells, it is possible to get 90-140 watts per meter from this luminous flux . Solar panels are assembled from such photocells, they are converters of light energy into electrical energy.



Now consider a solar-powered charger and its main technical specifications. The main parameter of such devices is the output power of the device, the charge speed of the connected batteries of the solar battery or the ability to "power" any electronic device will depend on it.
