Gremyachiy waterfall - a unique natural monument

Gremyachiy waterfall - a unique natural monument, which is a state reserve and of regional importance. They say that he is the only waterfall in the Moscow region, but this is a misconception. In fact, Gremyachiy is not a waterfall, but a spring that flows from the crevice of the slope of the Lyapinka River from a height of 25 meters. It is located on the right bank of the river Wondigi, which flows from Lake Torbeeva, located near the village of Lyapino. At a distance of 14 kilometers southeast of Sergiev Posad and 57 kilometers from Moscow is a waterfall. The rattlesnake key, or the source of Sergius of Radonezh and Roman Kirzhachsky, is sometimes called the Gremyachy waterfall.



Origin of the source

Tradition says that the Gremyachiy waterfall arose after the prayer of St. Sergius, who stopped here along the road from Trinity to the Makhrishchensky monastery. According to another version, the source appeared after a lightning strike on a hillside. This version is also confirmed by the name of the source, indicating the action of a thunder arrow.

This is a popular place of pilgrimage and a famous tourist attraction, gathering a large number of believers and tourists around it all year round.

Arrangement of the spring

A memorial sign is installed near the waterfall. A wooden staircase is laid to the spring, on which several relaxation areas are located. Near the waterfall there is a parking lot for transport, a font, a shower room, a delightful wooden temple and a chapel. The swimming pool consists of an open pond and an enclosed space.
