Lyubov Dmitrievna Mendeleev - wife of Alexander Blok. Biography, memories

Childhood story

Lyubov Dmitrievna Mendeleev was born and grew up in a large family. Her father, Dmitry Mendeleev, had six children in total. From his first marriage, he already had a daughter Olga and a son Vladimir. Lyuba was born in the second marriage of the scientist - with Anna Popova. In addition to her, the family had a sister Maria and two brothers - Ivan and Vasily. Many who studied the biography of the scientist know that the fate of Lyubin‚Äôs father, like many brilliant people, was not easy. He had a rather complicated character. After he was forced into university resignation, Mendeleev, together with his family, spent most of the time on the estate, which was located in Boblovo.



Despite the fact that in the family of an outstanding chemist, children were never especially spoiled, the future wife of Alexander Blok herself recalled that her childhood was very happy, stormy and filled with joy.

The families of Blok and Mendeleeva communicated closely enough with each other and always maintained warm relations. Blok's wife also had a serious affair. Her chosen one was a young actor named Konstantin Davidovsky. Mother survived her son only 2 years, and this time, oddly enough, she huddled in a small communal apartment with her once-hated daughter-in-law.
