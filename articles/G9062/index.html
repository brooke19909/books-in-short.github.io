Crimea, the village of Popovka: hotels, photos, reviews

The village of Popovka in the Republic of Crimea is a great place for those who like to enjoy the sunny beaches of the Black Sea in peace and tranquility. Some time ago, party-goers gathered here to come to visit the legendary Kazantip festival. Now, the village of Popovka in Crimea is considered one of the best places for families with children. Hotels, hotels, recreation centers and cottages for vacationers are not counted here.



Features of rest

The village of Popovka is located 28 km from Yevpatoriya. This place is not crowded, with wide clean beaches, on which there is never a crowd of tourists. The entrance to the sea is gentle, which is very convenient for children. From Popovka you can walk to salt lakes with therapeutic mud and arrange a free mud treatment course.

But the infrastructure of the village is practically undeveloped and consists of several small shops, bars and a pharmacy. If you walk to the village of Mirny, you can get to the market, to shops, banks and mail. The beaches are not equipped either: there are no sun loungers, no changing cabins, no canopies.

You can get to the village of Popovka in Crimea by bus or taxi from Simferopol Airport. By 2018, it is planned to build a road bridge to the Crimea, and in 2019 - a railway. In the meantime, you can get through the ferry crossing.

"Kazantip" - a place for children

The authorities of Crimea in 2017 banned the holding of the Kazantip festival. In its place in Popovka there are many that are reminiscent of the festival of constructions and decorated houses. Everything here is saturated with the atmosphere of the former country of general happiness.

At the site of the festival, the Crimean authorities plan to build a children's center. And now here is a mini-water park. The beach there is very convenient for children: the entrance to the water is gentle, and the depth near the shore is only half a meter. It is covered with fine white sand, at the entrance to the sea you can see beautiful large shells, and the water is unusually blue and crystal clear. Reviews and photos of the beach in the village of Popovka in Crimea do not convey all its beauty. In the summer season, there are small bars, a cafe and a shop on the Kazantip site.

Mini-Hotel "Sea Breeze"

A small hotel consisting of only 9 rooms, which are equipped with only the most necessary:

  • furniture;
  • a refrigerator;
  • dishes;
  • the suites are air-conditioned;
  • hot shower and toilet in each room.

In the "Sea Breeze" tourists will feel the atmosphere of home comfort. They offer 3 meals a day, washing, ironing, drying clothes. The distance from the hotel to the sea is 550 m.



Hotel Saf-Rem

Another small but cozy hotel, also consisting of 9 rooms, is located in the very center of Popovka. In this hotel you can relax inexpensively, but with comfort. Lunches can be prepared in the shared kitchen, while laundry can be put in order.

The hotel has a playground for children, a barbecue area, Wi-Fi is available everywhere. The hotel allows check-in with your pet. Free parking is available for visitors with their own car. For an additional fee, it is possible to arrange a transfer from the airport.

The standard rooms include a TV, beds, bedside tables and a fan. A shower and toilet are located on site. Superior rooms are additionally equipped with air conditioning, refrigerator and sofa. Shower and toilet in the room. Distance to the beach - 700 m.
