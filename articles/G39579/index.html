Hotel La Costa Hotel Sanya 5 * (Sanya): overview, description and reviews of tourists

The desire to make your vacation unforgettable and full of new impressions and emotions is the desire of every person after a tiring year of work. Someone wants to lie on the azure sandy beaches, someone is interested in visiting the sights and getting acquainted with their history, for someone night entertainment is in the first place. When organizing a family trip, parents primarily care about the pastime of their child. All these nuances successfully combine the resorts of the People's Republic of China, one of which is Hainan Island.

The city of Sanya and its advantages

In the picturesque natural expanses of the island, which are the only tropical climate in China, more than half of the area is occupied by forests. The prospect of the purest air, the possibility of healing the body, saturating it with oxygen, increasing immunity - this is what attracts many travelers to this resort. A large number of healing sources rich in essential microelements for the body positively affect the health of spa residents.

Sanya is the southernmost resort and the only place in this state where you can sunbathe and swim all year round under constantly scented flower beds and relentless birdsong. On three sides it is surrounded by mountains. In terms of environmental performance, it ranks second in the world. The coastline is represented by 19 bays, on the coast of which the hotel business is thriving.

The five-star hotel La Costa Hotel Sanya, located on the coast of the South China Sea, offers comfortable apartments, high-quality service, excellent dishes to everyone who stops here. Reviews of tourists about the hotel Sanya La Costa Resort 5 * are mostly positive. What has earned the hotel so close attention? This we will discuss in the article.

Accommodation

The room fund of La Costa Seaside Resort Hotel Sanya 5 * (China) is represented by 312 apartments, which vary in the number of rooms that open from the window with views, dimensions:

  • Garden View Room.
  • Deluxe Sea View.
  • Premium Deluxe Ocean.

However, they are all united in providing unprecedented comfort for each guest. Squares range from 66 to 140 square meters. The interior is decorated in a classic style, complemented by decorating elements. On free worktops there were vases with flowers, on the walls - paintings of local painters. The floors are covered with high-quality ceramic tiles, which gives the room luxury and gloss. The color scheme is dominated by warm colors and pastel colors. In each room there is access to a balcony, which allows you to admire the rising of the early sun or the appearance of the first stars in a cozy and romantic atmosphere. French glazing fills the room with warmth and light. As noted by numerous guests, it is comfortable and cozy.

There were no serious complaints about the cleanliness. Cleaning and bed linen in the rooms of La Costa Hotel Sanya 5 * are carried out daily by tidy and conscientious maids. If necessary, those who like to sleep can be woken up by staff at the appointed time. Room service is available around the clock and requires an additional charge.





Degree of comfort

Room facilities at the hotel include everything you need for a comfortable accommodation for spa guests. The furniture is made of dark wood, includes a large double bed, a TV stand, an armchair, a desk for working with a floor lamp, a wardrobe, bedside tables.

An individual air conditioning system maintains a comfortable temperature in the room. The flat-screen TV has Russian-language channels. The phone allows you to solve all your questions in the blink of an eye, contacting directly with the reception. Using the safe is free of charge, it allows you to spend a carefree vacation without worrying about the safety of important documents.

The ability to prepare a cup of hot tea or aromatic coffee without leaving the apartment, especially inspires guests. They often note this fact in their many positive reviews. A tea set, dishes and an electric kettle are what will help with this. The minibar is updated every day with two bottles of drinking water. Use of Wi-Fi is included in the price of the permit. For the full formation of comfort there are slippers and terry bathrobes.

The bathroom is equipped with a bath or shower, sink, hairdryer and necessary personal hygiene products, including shampoo, soap, gel, toothpaste.





Nutrition

La Costa Hotel Sanya 5 * reviews of the positive plan very often get for amazingly delicious food. The price includes a single meal - breakfast, which is organized on the basis of a buffet. Everyone chooses their own dishes in the quantity necessary to satisfy their hunger. The restaurant's interior has delightful elements of decor, a warm atmosphere, which contributes to an increase in appetite. Beach

The beach area is public, however, for the convenience of spa guests, the administration took care of the beach equipped with sun loungers with umbrellas, showers, changing rooms and toilets. The shore is sandy, the entrance to the water is gentle. Toddlers are enthralled by the opportunity to frolic on the coastal small fry.

Beach activities

To ensure that the time spent by the sea does not seem boring and monotonous, there is an equipped volleyball field and a wide selection of water activities. The most popular among guests are water scooters, banana riding, windsurfing and diving.

The rich, bewitching underwater world of these territories allows you to enjoy the unique flora and fauna of the South China Sea. Depth of view in some places reaches 30 meters. When diving, both an experienced diver and a beginner can meet multi-colored corals, as well as interesting inhabitants. Snorkeling is no less common, but not as impressive.

Toddler Leisure

The hotel often receives positive reviews from tourists who came to rest with their children for organizing leisure activities for children.
