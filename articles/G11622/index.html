Holiday Village "Bor", Bryansk: enjoying the benefits of nature and civilization

The base of suburban recreation "Bor", located in the Bryansk region, is a beautiful and picturesque place. Near the accommodation for visitors is a lake and beautiful landscapes. Thanks to this, vacationers can enjoy the magnificent nature and all the advantages of comfort and civilization. This organization, its features and characteristics are discussed in detail in the article.

Holiday Village "Bor" in Bryansk: enjoying the benefits of nature and civilization

This place is popular with a wide variety of customers. Both spouses with children, couples who are going to hold a wedding celebration, and groups planning to celebrate a corporate event or anniversary against the backdrop of nature come here.

The base for country recreation offers visitors a wide range of comfortable rooms and rooms for living for every taste. Here you can find various opportunities for active pastime: a sauna and a bathhouse, a gazebo and barbecue equipment, a restaurant and halls for any celebration, rental of equipment for sports and fishing.

Many are interested in where the recreation center "Bor" in Bryansk is located, how to get to this place. This organization is located seven kilometers from the city, at the address: Svensky settlement, pos. Sanatorium Snezhka.

Guest lodging

Clients of the Bor recreation center in the Bryansk region can be accommodated both in rooms and in cottages. Speaking about the varieties of rooms for guests, you can list the following:

  1. A room designed for two persons. It is a comfortable room with a modern interior. It consists of a small kitchen equipped with all necessary furniture and utensils, as well as a living room in which there is a TV, armchairs, sofa, chest of drawers, bed. The room has a toilet, shower, washbasin. The room has a balcony.
  2. Room for three persons. It consists of a hall, a kitchen, two sanitary units with modern equipment, and a balcony.
  3. Luxury room in the cottage, designed for three guests. This room has a corridor, a hall and a kitchen, as well as two sanitary units and a porch with furniture for relaxing. Not far from the cottage with these rooms there is a gazebo and all the necessary equipment for the preparation of barbecue.
  4. Room with a sauna for relaxation of large companies. This is a room that can accommodate ten or more guests. There is everything for a comfortable pastime: dishes, a microwave, a boiler, kitchen furniture, a TV with a liquid crystal display, armchairs, spacious cabinets and sofas. The room has five bedrooms.

Pastime Opportunities

Anyone who plans to spend a vacation or weekend in this institution can be sure that he will not be bored. The clients of the base are offered many types of recreation. One of them is boating. The organization is located next to the lake. Due to this, guests of the recreation center "Bor" in the Bryansk region can go fishing. Riding enthusiasts are also offered the opportunity to enjoy their time. In addition, sports equipment can be rented at the base both in summer and in winter.

The organization also provides event services.

For this, guests are offered a restaurant and a porch.

Opinions of visitors about the institution

On the recreation center "Bor" in Bryansk, the reviews of guests are dominated by positive. Visitors like the comfortable and cozy atmosphere, polite staff of the institution, the order in the premises for living. The institution has earned a good reputation and due to favorable climatic and natural conditions that improve health, cheer up and allow you to relax. It offers many opportunities for active pastime for customers of different age categories.



Some visitors respond negatively about the Bor recreation center in the Bryansk region. They believe that the premises of this institution have not been repaired for a long time.
