Best hotels in Abano Terme, Italy: photos and reviews of tourists

Abano Terme is a small resort town in northern Italy. Since ancient times, it is famous for its healing mud and mineral springs. Judging by the reviews of tourists, there are many health centers here, the best of which will be presented in this article.





Spa hotel Mioni Pezzato **** Lux

In the heart of the city is the famous spa complex Mioni Pezzato **** Lux. It is located near the main resort street of Abano Terme. Hotel "Mioni Pesato" has a huge territory, which has 3 outdoor and 2 indoor pools with hot purified water. Near the hotel there is a huge park area. Pools are equipped with modern hydromassage devices for any part of the body.

People who have been here say that the goal of the spa hotel is to make guests healthier. There are two treatment centers. One of them is a popular mud bath, the second is a physiotherapy pool with carbonated water. Also on the territory there are health caves, the presence of which can be compared with saunas, only with natural steam. The reviews of vacationers say the following: if you purchase a ticket to a spa hotel, the package includes the following range of services: minimum number of days - 7, the possibility of taking six mud sessions and baths with ozone. At your service - various types of massage, the duration of each reaches 15-20 minutes. The spa hotel Mioni Pezzato **** Lux is considered the most popular in the resort of Abano Terme. In terms of quality of service, it is one of the five best hospitals in Europe.





How to pay for your stay at Mioni Pezzato & SPA?

Tourists really like this complex. In their reviews, they call a significant plus of the hotel - this is payment for accommodation upon arrival. In addition, the hotel does not block funds on your card. Account information is only needed if the guest does not arrive at the reserved place. If you do not warn in advance, the hotel reserves the right to remove the penalty. The best hotels of Abano Terme value their reputation and carefully prepare to receive new visitors. Therefore, it is better to warn in advance that your plans have changed. Mioni Pezzato & SPA accepts payment both in cash and by bank transfer.

Abano grand hotel

Grand Hotel Abano is virtually the only five-star hotel in Padova. You will definitely not find such a rich and delightful complex. At least tourists say so. They say that there are comfortable rooms with all the necessary equipment, and wellness programs. Grand Hotel Abano Terme is located in the pedestrian part of the city. Nearby is a beautiful green park. And at nearby golf clubs, guests can get a discount on the game.

Hotel Abano has a significant plus - a specially designed program for healing and rejuvenating the whole body. On the territory there are steam saunas, hot baths, all types of massage, 3 pools with thermal water, cosmetologist and allergist services, and, of course, hydromassage equipment.
