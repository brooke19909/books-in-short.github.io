Homeliness and quality service gives its guests the resort complex "Emerald" (Vityazevo). Traveler reviews allow us to conclude that this is one of the best vacation options on the Black Sea.

General impression

Hotel "Emerald" is a fairly new institution with its advantages and disadvantages. In general, travelers are offered acceptable conditions for recreation. Well-groomed territory, beautiful buildings, freshly renovated rooms - all this contributes to the formation of a positive impression of the hotel. In addition, here guests are offered good nutrition, which is important. The hotel is suitable for both adults and children. Many guests compare this institution with similar Turkish ones.
