About the essence of the grace period

Before applying for a loan, a bank employee announces the rules for using a Sberbank credit card. The grace period for such products is 50 calendar days. But it‚Äôs not always clear to the user from what time the countdown begins and when the debt should be repaid.

The structure of the grace period includes two periods: 30 days for making purchases and 20 days for repayment. The first period of time is called the billing period, it starts from the moment the credit card is activated. You can learn it from the bank employees. This is a specific month that remains constant. Within 30 days (sometimes 31), the cardholder makes settlements, after this period comes an extract-notification about all the funds spent, the amount of debt and the minimum payment. There comes a payment period of 20 days, during which the borrower must pay the bank.

How beneficial is it to repay a loan?

Within 20 days, the cardholder must make a payment. Moreover, the profitability of the transaction depends on the borrower: if you pay exactly the amount that was spent in the billing period, then there will be no overpayment. You should be careful about the provision of preferential repayment at a rate of 0%: the rule applies only to non-cash payments. When withdrawing funds, the interest will be charged on the amount of cashing out (plus commission) daily, so you need to return this money as soon as possible.





If it is not possible to repay the entire amount within 20 days, you must make at least a minimum payment of 5% of the funds spent. This also includes commissions (for cash withdrawals, card servicing, paid services), interest on operations not included in soft loans, and penalties (for late payments, exceeding the credit limit). The situation will continue on a monthly basis until the client repays the entire amount, taking into account interest. In this case, there is no need to talk about a zero rate.
