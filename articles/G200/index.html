Which vacuum cleaner is better: with a bag or container?

Are you planning to purchase a vacuum cleaner, but the choice is difficult? What type of home assistant do you prefer? What power will ensure high-quality dust absorption? Which vacuum cleaner is better: with a bag or container?



The answer depends on the complex of individual conditions of the buyer. You should keep in mind: do you have animals in the house, on the floor - a sensitive laminate, durable tiles or carpets, are you prone to allergies. In fact, it is not so important, a vacuum cleaner with a bag or with a container, the main thing that you consider burdensome: to periodically buy disposable dust collectors or to clean the container of dirt after cleaning?

However, we will understand everything in order. To answer the question of which vacuum cleaner is better: with a bag or container, you need to find out the strengths and weaknesses of working machines.



Most buyers know firsthand about vacuum cleaners with bags. Shaking out dust from a rag container is not a pleasant experience. In addition, a fabric bag allows the smallest dust particles to pass through. By the way, they are the most dangerous for allergy sufferers. True, modern manufacturers have long replaced the fabric with synthetic materials that can easily cope with this task. And modern bags are durable, not afraid of moisture and durable.

Paper-based dust collectors are a great alternative to dirty work. A disposable bag is simply thrown away, and the paper from which it is made easily and completely decomposes. But here there is a minus - packages quickly end, and you have to go to the store again to buy a new package. As a result, there comes a moment when this duty is boring to the owner of the vacuum cleaner, and then the question again arises: after all, which vacuum cleaner is better? With a bag or container?



At first glance, the removable bowl has a significant advantage: you can forget about buying packages. In addition, if dust is just as unpleasant to shake out of the container, then the container with the aquafilter will save you from this annoying event. Contaminated water is simply drained into the sewer. It really is.

We figured out which vacuum cleaner is better - with a bag or container. It's time to navigate the power of household appliances. What suction power should your vacuum cleaner have?

If the floors of your house are durable and smooth, without carpets, then you can not overpay for the power of the model. In your case, the average option will be good. The more powerful the suction force, the more finely dispersed dust breaks through all the filters installed in the apparatus.
