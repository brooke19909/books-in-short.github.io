Several patterns of cross stitch: poppies

Floral motifs in embroidery are a predominant theme for many years. If you have grandmothers' products, you can notice the variety in ornaments and paintings: meadow flowers, field flowers, garden, forest.

In those years, it was not possible to download a ready-made diagram from the Internet, print it, or buy a ready-made one. Their cross stitch patterns of flowers (poppies, bells, roses) were intuitive. Therefore, each product was unique.

For beginners

If you have never tried to engage in such needlework, then do not despair. Cross stitching is one of the easiest activities at your leisure. The most important thing is to get a hand to do the same crosses.

For this, it is only necessary to pull the thread with equal force. It comes with time, with practice. Therefore, if the first work you may be disappointed with a little negligence, then do not despair. With each new embroidery, with each new picture, it will turn out better and better.

Ready Kits

Color patterns of cross stitch poppies can be found in many needlework stores. Usually, they are fully equipped with thread and needles. Most often, this option is suitable for beginners, because everything is ready for embroidery.

You can go the other way. Print the finished scheme, pick up suitable threads from any manufacturer and get down to business.

Black and white is distinguished by its sophistication.

A monochrome image also has its advantages: it is easier, especially for beginners, it embroiders faster, there is no confusion in the color palette, only two colors and two threads. But from this it does not become less attractive. Pay attention, for example, in the photo: black and white attract in its own way.

In addition, monochrome patterns look very original in the performance of mouline thread melange threads (two colors seamlessly fading into each other, for example, gray and blue, or yellow and red) or in the design of multicolor mouline thread threads (the same melange, only colors used not two, but more).

Cross stitch pattern of poppies according to the photo

If not one of the ready-made schemes suits, then there are computer programs. They allow you to remake the finished photograph in a pattern for cross-stitch. Poppies - flowers are popular, so it will not be difficult to find the desired image.

Such programs are convenient with a variety of features:

  • set the necessary embroidery sizes;
  • indicate the number of colors you need;
  • select the right thread manufacturers;
  • edit the circuit.

The capabilities of modern devices allow you not to limit your imagination to create the desired pattern of cross stitch poppies.
