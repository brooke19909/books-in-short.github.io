Rest house "Equator" (Olenevka, Crimea): description, beach, reviews

On the territory of the Crimean peninsula, in Olenevka, near Cape Tarkhankut, there is a wonderful vacation spot of interest to tourists of different ages. Travelers can stay in other hotels and pensions, located in abundance in the village of Olenevka. There is also a private sector. This article will describe one of the best accommodation options in the village - the Equator Holiday Home.





Having a good location in an ecologically clean place near wonderful beaches with a gentle entrance to the sea, the private boarding house ‚ÄúEquator‚Äù (Olenevka, Crimea) attracts lovers of a quiet beach holiday and diving. Clear water, an abundance of species of fish and marine flora invariably attract tourists who want to enjoy the beauty and diversity of the underwater world. In these picturesque places you can dive into the sea from steep cliffs and take wonderful pictures against the backdrop of a magnificent landscape. Many just choose the Equator Holiday Home for this.

Description of rooms





Arriving at the hotel, tourists have the opportunity to choose the most suitable accommodation option. The accommodation fund is represented by comfortable two, three, four and five-bed rooms of the category "Junior Suite" and "Suite". They consist of one room. In addition, there are two-room executive class rooms. All of them, with the exception of two-room apartments of the Junior Suite category, have balconies with sea views. Vacationers staying in "junior suites" can enjoy the surrounding landscapes on the shared balconies available on each floor.

All apartments, regardless of category, are equipped with TVs, refrigerators. Each room has dishes, electric kettles. Safes are provided for storing valuables. There is a shower and a bathroom. Hot and cold water in the holiday home is served around the clock.

The rooms of the "luxury" category have differences, expressed in the presence of two modern televisions, air conditioners. They have two bathrooms.
Rooms are cleaned daily. Towels are changed every three days. Bed linen is changed weekly.

Prices

Separately, it is necessary to talk about the cost of living in rooms of various categories in the rest house "Equator" (Olenevka). Hotel prices are very affordable. So, a day of living in a double room without a balcony will cost about 1600 rubles. The cost of a similar room with a balcony and a view of the courtyard ranges from 1800 - up to 2500 rubles, depending on the time of stay. The cost of a triple room is up to 2400 rubles. The most expensive are five-room one-room apartments. Their price per night is up to 4300 rubles. The price depends on the category of the room, the number of rooms and the availability of a balcony. Sea view rooms are slightly more expensive.

Food





Meals are organized in the cafe-restaurant "Khutorok", which is located on the territory of the holiday home. Here you can always try meat and fish dishes prepared on the grill and grill. In addition, you can eat deliciously in the cafe-cafeteria on the proposed menu with a variety of dishes. For the convenience of visitors there is a quick service line. Catering for vacationers is carried out from 8 a.m. to 21.00. The caf√© also has meals for children from one year old.

The restaurant and dining room offers all kinds of refreshing drinks and ice cream. Vacationers can enjoy chatting with each other and have the ability to access the Internet.

Infrastructure





The holiday home has a fairly developed infrastructure, which includes a guarded parking lot, a shop. For sports there is a special area. For all kinds of events and celebrations there is a banquet hall. Everyone can visit the cozy sauna.

The safety of tourists is ensured through round-the-clock video surveillance. Guests arriving in their own car look after their car parked directly from the room.

Diving Training Center





The attraction of the holiday home is the PADI Equator-Katran-K diving center, which provides training in diving. As a result, an international PADI certificate is issued, which will confirm the qualifications received. The focus of the course is on diving safety. A tourist who has taken a course in a diving center can already be considered a scuba diver who knows how to dive under water. He is able to adequately assess the situation during the dive, knows how to breathe correctly, raise various objects from the bottom, correctly carry out ascent.

The diving center provides rental and sale of equipment for scuba diving, refueling cylinders and repairing equipment. For students, test dives and underwater excursions are organized with inspection of sunken ships and grottoes. Those who wish can take a boat trip and take part in sea fishing. Reviews about the rest house "Equator" (Olenevka), below, will tell about all its advantages and disadvantages.

Guest Comments

Without exception, all vacationers who have visited the guest house note the availability of the widest opportunities for diving and water sports. According to some tourists, the entertainment industry in the village of Olenevka (Crimea) and directly in the rest house "Equator" is underdeveloped. In addition to diving, here you can still ride a scooter.

Positive sides

The guests also note the beautiful, untouched by human activity nature. But the main advantage of the guest house "Equator" (Olenevka) is the beach.





Some vacationers rate it as the best on the entire Crimean peninsula. As an advantage, guests of the holiday home note the proximity of the beach to the hotel. It is only ten minutes walk to the sea. There is a lack of a large number of tourists, which will appeal to lovers of secluded relaxation and silence.

Many guests like the well-maintained, paved with paving stones, cozy territory of the holiday home. In their reviews, guests note an abundance of flowers and vineyards. However, some complain about the lack of shade on hot days.

As a positive point, the presence of playgrounds on the territory is noted. They are open-air disco and games with small guests.

Many reviews speak of competent catering of guests and excellent quality of food prepared in the restaurant and dining room. Tourists write that the food served in the cafe is very tasty, and the menu is diverse. At the same time, according to some guests, the prices are somewhat overpriced.

Minuses

Reviews about the boarding house "Equator" are not always positive. There are those in which vacationers write about the insufficiently modern design of rooms and furniture. Some reviews say that air conditioners can not cope in hot weather. However, this drawback indicates to a greater extent that modern technology is not designed to work in hot climates.

Many tourists are not happy with the frequency of housekeeping and linen changes. In one of the reviews, the client complained that one of the nights he had to face the presence of bugs in the room. These circumstances, according to many vacationers, indicate that the hotel administration does not sufficiently control the staff working in the buildings. At the same time, as a rule, there are no complaints about the quality of guest service by the employees of the dining room and restaurant. The reviews often expressed wishes for the administration of the holiday home to tighten control over the work of the staff and more carefully carry out the processing of bed linen.

conclusions





Despite the presence of some negative reviews, most tourists generally give a positive assessment to the quality of service and organization of leisure in the rest house "Equator". Almost all the disadvantages are more than compensated by the excellent climate, the presence of a beautiful beach and clean sea water, which allows you to have a wonderful time and relax with health benefits. Children here will also be interested. Parents can not worry about the leisure of their children. The coastal zone is wonderfully equipped to stay with young children.

And for diving enthusiasts, according to most guests, it is better not to find a place on the entire peninsula. Having your own diving training center adds a huge plus to the other benefits of this placement.

Staying at the Equator Holiday Home is an excellent option for spending leisure time in the Crimea.
