Currency option with deposit coverage: features, conditions

What is such a financial enrichment instrument as a currency option with deposit coverage? What features exist here? What should be guided when making deals?

general information



Let's deal with the terminology. What is an option? This is a kind of contract that allows you to exchange one currency for another in the event that the transaction is winning, and refuse it if it is not profitable. In this case, you can act according to two schemes: ‚Äúcall‚Äù (call) and ‚Äúput‚Äù (put). In the first case, it is possible to purchase a predetermined amount of currency on a specific day and at a pre-fixed price. Put provides for the sale under the same conditions. Deposited currency options are offered by banks to their customers. They are a carefully organized financial investment product. It includes tools such as a currency option and a deposit. Why is such a product generally offered? Thus, banks provide an opportunity to earn their customers a higher percentage than offered by conventional deposits. But along with this, it is necessary to understand that savings are also subject to currency risk.

About the benefits

A standard currency option has the following advantages:

  1. An opportunity to receive a more significant income than under the terms of a simple deposit.
  2. Flexible choice of term, currency pairs, exchange rate and profitability.
  3. A short placement period, usually up to three months.
  4. Possibility of remote clearance.

Who is this suitable for?



Holders of cash savings, assets and liabilities, who have their own vision of how the rates will develop, can try options on currency pairs. Of course, if they are not afraid of risks. When choosing this type of investment, you must give preference to the base and alternative currency pairs, the carefully selected placement period, as well as the exchange rate. What should I deposit money in? The base currency is used for this purpose. But it can be converted into alternative cash according to the previously set exchange rate. The income earned under this transaction is an option premium. It is formed from the difference in exchange rates. The currency options market is quite developed, so if you want to find a niche that is convenient for yourself, it is not difficult.

What do you need to do to earn money?



Suppose a reader is interested in a currency option transaction. What needs to be done to participate in it? On the day you register the option:

  1. Place a certain amount in a pre-selected base currency. As such, the Russian ruble, the euro, the US dollar, the British pound, the Chinese yuan or the Swiss franc most often act. Options may vary slightly in different financial institutions.
  2. The second currency is selected.
  3. The placement period is set. Typically, a range of seven to ninety days is available.
  4. The exchange rate as well as the premium for the currency option are negotiated. Remember that the higher the amount you claim, the higher the risks.

When the day of option determination comes, there are several options for further actions:

  1. If the rate is less profitable or equal to the one determined at the time of conclusion of the contract, then on the day of return the person claims to receive the amount of his investments, the interest on the deposit, as well as an option premium in the base currency. Alas, this is a failure.
  2. If the rate is more profitable than a certain person, then he receives all the amounts due to him in alternative currency.
