Weather situation

The wonderful weather in Havana allows the city to host guests all year long. Tropical climatic conditions prevail here, conducive to a favorable weather situation. The average air temperature is 26-27 degrees heat. The hottest season in the capital of Cuba lasts from early December to late April. Therefore, if your vacation falls precisely at this time, then you need to take care of the hotel in advance. And what institution to choose, we will now tell.

Four Star Hotels

For travelers who do not want to spend a lot of money, but want to find quite comfortable living conditions, Havana hotels are suitable for which the corresponding authorities assigned four stars. It is worth booking the apartments in advance, and the best establishments of the Cuban capital are at your service. The most popular hotels of this category in Havana are the following.

Memories Miramar Havana. The facility as an extra convenience offers its guests a jacuzzi, outdoor tennis courts, squash courts, a sauna and a swimming pool. Each air-conditioned room has a fridge, minibar and satellite or cable TV.



Comodo Hotel Havana. Havana hotels such as this, in addition to the standard set of services, offer guests additional ones in the form of a telephone and radio in each room. The institution was built at the very beginning of the last century and is still in demand by many tourists.

Hotel Raquel. In this hotel, in addition to amazing rooms, customers will find a massage room, a sauna, a 24-hour reception and a travel agency. Additional amenities include conference rooms, currency exchange, and 24-hour room service.

Symbol of cuba

Hotel Nacional de Cuba is considered a symbol of Cuba and an important element of the culture and history of the state. It was built in 1930. At different times, he was visited by such celebrities as Winston Churchill, Ernest Hemingway, Frank Sinatra, Ava Gardner and many others. Rumor has it that customers who once visited this elegant hotel come to it again and again.

The institution is only a few meters from the sea, and therefore 80% of its apartments have an extraordinary view of the capital's coast.



One of the oldest hotels

Havana's oldest hotel list includes the Hotel Plaza, opened in 1901. Previously, the building in which this exquisite accommodation is located today was the residence of a wealthy Cuban family. Then the editors of a certain influential newspaper moved here, only at the beginning of the last century the building was converted into an apartment for tourists.



Hotel Plaza is a four-star hotel with 188 large rooms of various categories. Some rooms overlook a beautiful patio, while others overlook the city. Some apartments have a private balcony. The decoration of the rooms is dominated by wood trim and soothing shades.
