Climate of New Zealand





The country has a heterogeneous climate. It tends to change: in the north of the North Island it is warm subtropical, and in the south of the South Island it is cool temperate. New Zealand is divided in half by the Mountain Range of the high Southern Alps, which divides the country into two separate zones of climatic conditions. The wettest part of the country is the western coast of the South Island. But the driest is the eastern, located 100 kilometers from the wet.

Landscape wealth of the country

Among the landscape riches of New Zealand, Milford Sound stands out as the fjord. It is located in the southwest of South Island. The fjord can rightfully be considered the most popular and most visited place in New Zealand. It is visited annually by up to 1 million tourists. They offer boat cruises during the day and night. What are the highlights of the caves of the "Long White Cloud"?

The name of the same name Waitomo is a mountain system over 2 million years old and a small village on the North Island of New Zealand. The inhabitants of this settlement are busy working in the many labyrinths of caves. Millions of miniature fireflies illuminating the inner surface of the grottoes brought world fame.



Waitomo caves have gained such great popularity that they are included in the list of must-see attractions. And although the fireflies themselves live at the lowest, third level, there are no less people wishing to see this beauty. Firefly Grotto is also famous for the fact that this species lives only in New Zealand. But Waitomo caves are famous not only for the beautiful view of luminous insects. Cavers go down to the grottoes for the sake of many stalagmites, making the most daring sorties. On the third level of this creation of nature, concerts are held on holidays, and on the second there was a banquet hall where everyone was fed.

Hobbiton - a paradise for tourists

Another attraction that New Zealand is famous for is the Hobbiton. Peter Jackson, director and producer of the Brotherhood of the Ring trilogy, has been looking for a place to shoot the full-time film for a long time. Flying in a helicopter along the northern island of New Zealand, he saw Alexander farm. After a long inspection of the green plains, Peter Jackson decided to settle the hero of the Shire (aka Hobbiton) on the lands of Alexander farm near the town of Matamata. This 500-hectare farm has been owned by the Alexander Family since 1978. Having agreed with the owners of the farm to begin filming the film, in 1999 he began construction of the hobbit village.



When the shooting of the picture was completed, the facades of the hobbit houses, the mill, the tavern and the humpback bridge, at the request of the farm owners, were not dismantled. Today, the Hobbiton is an attraction that is very popular among tourists from around the world.

Boulders of Moeraki

On the beach of New Zealand between the cities of Moeraki and Hampden are a bizarre spherical form of education. Moeraki boulders formed in the Paleocene era. Based on the presence of iron, magnesium found in their composition, as well as stable oxygen and carbon isotopes, a conclusion was drawn about the formation of formations from sea mud. Over 5 million years of its existence, stone blocks reached 2 m in diameter. Some of them show cracks and structural damage.

The Moeraki boulders show significant differences in structural and dimensional characteristics. Locals keep, passing by word of mouth, legends about the origin of mysterious blocks of stones. By portraying them in travel guides, leisure and entertainment companies are attracting an increasing number of visitors to view boulders. Travel restrictions

For citizens of Russia and the CIS, a visa is required when visiting New Zealand. A prerequisite is the translation of the entire package of documents submitted for visiting New Zealand into English and certification by a notary.

Consideration of documents in the New Zealand Immigration Service takes an average of 14 calendar days. When traveling through a travel agency, the term for consideration of a package of documents does not exceed 14 days, and when traveling independently, it can reach 30 calendar days. The duration of the decision to open a visa is also influenced by factors such as the season and the number of people wishing to come in a particular period. In this regard, the terms of consideration of a package of documents can be extended or reduced.

Restrictions for tourists staying in New Zealand include smoking. The ban on it is quite strict: you can not smoke in public transport, in many hotels and in all public places.
