How to go online from a tablet in various ways?

Now, two methods of how to access the Internet from a tablet are most widely used. They are based on wireless data technology. The first one is using Wi-Fi. Among its advantages, one can single out a high speed of information transfer (in some cases, it can reach 150 Mbps) and ease of setup. He has only one minus - this is the need to be near the transmitter (up to 10 meters). But the second method is based on the use of 2G and 3G networks. He does not tie us to any particular place, but his speed is much lower.
