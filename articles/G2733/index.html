3 * The Natural Resort (Phuket, Thailand): hotel description, reviews

In this short article we will discuss the island of Phuket, the hotel The Natural Resort (3 stars) and much more. Believe me, if you want to have a wonderful vacation, you need to go to Thailand, this hotel. Everyone will like it here!

Location

In the heart of Phuket, the Natural Resort 3 * hotel is located. It is located about 35 kilometers from Phuket International Airport. Getting here is no big deal, as public transport in Phuket is good enough. If you want to save money, you can take the shuttle bus, which will take you from the airport to Phuket, and then take the local minibus to the place. If you want to be in place quickly and comfortably, you can take a minibus, taxi or even a limousine taxi.



Once in place, the tourist will probably first want to swim in the sea. Patong, the most vibrant and popular beach of Phuket, is about 1 kilometer from the 3 * The Natural Resort hotel (the hotel is located on the third coastline). If you walk, it will take 15-20 minutes of a leisurely step along the busy city streets past numerous shops with exotic fruits and souvenirs, as well as shops with everything you need. Approximately the same time will take the road to the most famous major shopping center Jungceylon. Here you can eat delicious seafood, which will be cooked right in front of you the way you yourself want, to buy fruits and other goodies.

In general, due to the convenient location of the Natural Resort, Phuket and its main life will not pass tourists. Believe me, you will definitely like it here!

Number of rooms

Hotel 3 * The Natural Resort consists of a three-story residential building with 85 rooms and 129 cozy bungalows. The whole territory is literally immersed in greenery, and the windows of the rooms overlook the courtyard or pool, so even the nearby road will not interfere with a comfortable stay.

Each of the spacious, tastefully decorated rooms has one double bed (or two single beds) and a desk, which is convenient for a meal or a laptop.

The furniture is modern and looks very pretty. The decoration is done in bright colors, which creates an atmosphere of lightness and freshness, and the room seems even more spacious.



In addition, all rooms are air-conditioned, which is important in a tropical climate. Returning from the beach or walking around the city, hotel guests have the opportunity to feel the pleasant coolness.

Each room has a balcony or veranda, which also has a wonderful view of the green park or pool, of which 3 * The Natural Resort has 2, albeit small ones. Guests can just sit and relax on the balcony, or they can go to the pool, take a swim or sunbathe on the free sunbeds.

In the refrigerators, which are in all rooms, you can always find several bottles of drinking water (hotel staff replenishes every day). Here you can also store fruit or other food so that it does not deteriorate in the heat.

Guests who choose to stay in bungalow rooms are provided with an extra meal table and sink.

Satellite TV with approximately 40 channels will help to brighten up your time if you had to stay in your room due to bad weather or just don't want to go anywhere.

Services

Fully compliant with its 3 *, The Natural Resort on its territory provides the following services:

- a pool bar for a pleasant relaxed pastime;

- a restaurant and cafe, where you can strengthen your strength during the day or relax in the evening;

- laundry room;

- parking for those who prefer to travel by car, even while relaxing;

- Paid safe at the reception, where you can store valuables and documents;

- taxi and transfer to the airport;

- Paid Wi-Fi in the hotel, if you urgently need access to the Internet;

- The order of excursions.

Nutrition

When booking rooms at Natural Resort 3 * (Phuket), you can choose two types of tickets: without food or with breakfast included in the bill. However, deciding to order breakfast, you should not count on a buffet. Instead, food stamps are issued upon check-in, one per day.



Breakfast includes:

- scrambled eggs or scrambled eggs as a hot meal;

- several types of corn flakes (and the most common, and even chocolate);

- as drinks to choose from - plain or chocolate milk, orange juice, tea, coffee;

- fruits and sweet pastries, as they say, in assortment.

It offers tourists who do not plan to immediately go to the city or the sea coast until late at night, there is a restaurant, cafe and bar on the hotel grounds.

Excursions

For those tourists who do not want to spend all their time relaxing on the city beach or by the hotel‚Äôs pool, The Natural Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) provides an opportunity to visit the nearest islands by buying excursions at the hotel or in the city.

You can, for example, go on a two-day or one-day excursion, during which you can visit the Phi-Phi islands, James Bond island and the islands of Krabi province, all with a Russian-speaking guide on a comfortable boat.



Tourists have the opportunity to swim in the clear turquoise waters of the Andaman Sea and soak up the snow-white sand.

If you wanted to visit separately, say, James Bond Island or Phi-Phi Islands, Natural Resort 3 * provides a chance to ride there on a speed boat or a big boat. Believe me, this cannot but please!

However, not everyone loves long boat trips, and therefore, having bought a ticket at the Natural Resort (Phuket) 3 * tour desk, you can go on a sightseeing tour of Phuket, during which visit the oceanarium, botanical garden and bird park, see the Buddha statue and admire the city from several viewing platforms, take a walk in the old city or devote time to shopping in shopping centers and markets.

For outdoor enthusiasts, there is also something to their liking. Riding an ATV off-road, going on a safari or riding an elephant, walking over an abyss along a rope bridge, flying along a rope track over the jungle or rafting on a river in a raft - all this will give fans of extreme sports an unforgettable experience.

Tourists reviews

Most guests of the Natural Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket) agree that it is fully consistent with the stated three stars. The optimal price-quality ratio will please those whose budget is limited.



The location of the hotel in the central part of the city, close to both the beach and the shops and nightlife of Phuket, is an added bonus, according to the guests. If you do not want to take walks at a distance of 1-1.5 kilometers, you can always use the buses that regularly run around the hotel.

A friendly and polite receptionist (it is worth noting that the staff here does not speak Russian, but speaks only Thai and English) is ready to move tourists into available rooms even if they arrived earlier than the specified time.

Among other things, hotel guests especially note its beautiful, green-covered area. As for breakfast, they, although quite monotonous, but very satisfying and tasty. In addition, there is a cafe of Russian cuisine nearby for those who do not like exotic food.
