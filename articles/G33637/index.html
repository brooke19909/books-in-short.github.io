Hotel Aguas Azules: reviews and photos of tourists. Holidays in Cuba

Cuba is the hot tropical sun, coral reefs, white sand beaches, clear ocean water, the widest fishing opportunities, stunningly beautiful nature, and, of course, comfortable living conditions for everyone who decided to stay at the Aguas Azules hotel. Reviews about both the country in general and this hotel in particular are mostly filled with flattering epithets and enthusiastic exclamations.

Sights of Cuba

Perhaps, acquaintance with Cuba, as well as with many other countries, is worth it from its capital - Havana. This city is located on the Gulf of Mexico. The old part of the capital of Cuba today looks almost the same as in the colonial period. Here you can see rectangular squares, churches and houses built in the Baroque style, as well as defensive fortresses at the entrance to the bay. And these are just some of the sights of Havana that each tourist center of Cuba offers to visit its customers.

Slightly inferior to Havana regarding the historical heritage of Santiago de Cuba. However, the inhabitants of this city are very hospitable and cheerful, having talked with them, tourists can learn a lot about the Cuban culture. In addition, on the territory of Santiago de Cuba there are many museums and exhibition halls that are certainly worth a visit.

Holidays in Cuba are unthinkable without a visit to the island of Cayo Coco - a nature reserve with sandy beaches stretching for 20 kilometers. The road connecting these two parts of the land deserves special attention. After all, it passes right by the sea and fits perfectly into the environment.





Active holidays in Cuba

For most tourists, vacation in Cuba is associated with sunny beaches, amazing swimming in the ocean waters and fiery evening and afternoon shows with Latin American dances and great music. However, Cuba can offer no less options for an interesting pastime to active people.

There are 30 centers for diving fans in Cuba, where you can either rent equipment for scuba diving and learn the basics of scuba diving, if you are still completely new to this business. There are excellent surfing opportunities in Cuba. However, those who plan to engage in this sport should think about this in advance. Indeed, despite the suitable natural conditions for surfing, there is nowhere to rent a board, you only need to bring it with you.

Fishing, exciting spearfishing, trips on a catamaran, yacht, boat, a trip through the jungle, a jeep safari - all this Cuba can offer its guests. Tourism in the country is developed quite well, so if you can not limit yourself in your means, you will open the most exotic corners of this country.





Varadero - a great resort for a beach holiday

Varadero is the main tourist recreation center in Cuba. Here there are luxurious snow-white beaches, and azure ocean waters, and the richest underwater world, and the dolphinarium, and the city museum, and cozy hotels, and evening shows in numerous institutions. But first things first.

Naturally, the main attractions of Varadero are magnificent beaches and the endless ocean. It is they who encourage tourists from all over the world to book rooms at the Aguas Azules hotel. Reviews about the rest in Varadero are practically devoid of negativity, because here, in fact, there is nothing to complain about. If, for example, you are tired of basking on the beach, you can go to the dolphinarium, from which not only children, but also adults, are delighted. Indeed, besides the fact that here you can look at these inhabitants of the water world, you can also swim or take pictures with them.

Attention deserves the conservation area - Hosone Park, where many tropical plants, palm trees, flowers grow, and there is also a wonderful lake. The main natural object of the park is the cactus El Patriarca, which is more than 500 years old. Speaking of natural attractions, it is also worth mentioning the caves of Cuevas de Bellamar and La Cueva de Ambrosio, located a few kilometers from Varadero.

And for a closer acquaintance with the history and culture of Cuba, you should visit the city museum of Varadero. Among its exhibits you can see a rich collection of maps of the peninsula, objects of Indian painting, culture and much more.





Aguas Azules: general information

40 km from the airport, in Varadero, Aguas Azules is located. Reviews of tourists, previously vacationing here, argue that getting to the hotel is not at all difficult. The hotel itself consists of the main four-story building, built in 1996, and in 2009 restored, and ten two-story cottages. The hotel area is well maintained, there is a lot of greenery, a swimming pool, a currency exchange, souvenir shops. For a fee, you can rent a motorcycle or car. There is also an Internet cafe, however, paid.

Hotel Aguas Azules 3 * is located on the beach. Thanks to this, its guests will need no more than two minutes to get to the beach. The length of the sandy shore is 900 meters. Therefore, no matter what time you come to it, there will always be enough space for relaxation. Beach towels, umbrellas and sun loungers are provided free of charge. Club Amigo Aguas Azules is a great option for a family vacation, because here everyone, regardless of age, can find something to their liking.





Conditions for accommodation and meals

Aguas Azules has 331 Standard rooms, 80 bungalows, 8 regular apartments and 6 apartments with a private jacuzzi. All hotel rooms, regardless of category, are designed for 3 adults or 2 children and 2 adults. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.

All rooms of Aguas Azules 3 * have a bathroom, consisting of a bath and a toilet, a hairdryer, a TV, a mini fridge and air conditioning. For extra money, vacationers have the opportunity to use the safe and telephone. The rooms are cleaned daily, and linen and towels are changed upon request. Each room has a balcony or terrace.

The hotel has 5 restaurants, in one of which guests are served breakfast, lunch and dinner according to the "buffet". In addition, Aguas Azules offers its guests 3 snack bars and 4 regular bars. Despite the abundance of choice of food and drinks of restaurants and bars of the hotel, no one forbids you to visit other catering establishments that are on the territory of Varadero. Aguas Azules, of course, is located at some distance from the center, where there are numerous restaurants and cafes, but if you want, you can get there without any problems.





Entertainment

As for entertainment, there are plenty of them at the hotel, both for adults and for children. So, everyone can enjoy swimming in the pools, which are located on the territory of the hotel 3. For young tourists - from 4 to 12 years old, there is a club in which they can have fun and have fun with their peers under the supervision of an animator.

Fans of active pastime can be invited to play volleyball, football, tennis or table tennis, billiards or engage in non-motorized water sports. In addition, Aguas Azules has a sauna, jacuzzi and fitness room. Massage services are provided. Throughout the day, all hotel guests are entertained by professional animators, but it is worth noting that there is no Russian animation there.





Hotel Aguas Azules: prices

Everyone who has decided to spend their holidays in Varadero (Cuba), prices are what interests them first. After all, you need to find out if the tourist can afford a vacation in these places. And the lion's share of the budget drawn up for the rest, naturally, is the rent of a hotel room and meals. Fortunately, the Aguas Azules hotel offers reasonable prices.

So, a double room for two adults without children can cost from 35,000 to 64,000 rubles in 7 days. It all depends on the category of room and season. At the same time, the price includes meals.

Photo in Cuba: why are they?

When choosing a hotel room, you can focus on photographs taken indoors, but you should not rely on them completely. Because often hotel owners try to put the proposed rooms in the best light. And this is quite natural. That is why it is advisable to take a photo during rest in all possible places, which are quite enough both on the territory of Aguas Azules and beyond. Firstly, you can decorate your home album with new pictures, and secondly, back up your words with evidence if you recommend or not recommend staying at this hotel to someone from your loved ones.

Do not forget to bring your camera to the beach and other places where you will go on excursions while relaxing in Cuba. The fact is that in this country there are many places and objects that are impossible to see anywhere else in the world.





Tourists reviews

Spacious grounds, clean rooms, a luxurious beach - all this is said by tourists who once rested in Aguas Azules. The reviews are also filled with many good words about the hotel staff. However, there is a "fly in the ointment": some tourists claim that the territory looks well-groomed only in residential buildings and pools, and if you go to a tennis court or soccer field, the impression is not the best.

The reviews also note that the hotel corresponds to a solid 3 stars, so if you hear somewhere about the Aguas Azules 4 * hotel, check this information. Perhaps this is a different housing option, or someone was wrong in giving a description.
