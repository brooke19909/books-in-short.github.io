LCD "Sherwood Forest": description, infrastructure, prices and reviews on the residential complex

Housing issues today are particularly acute. The acquisition of real estate in Moscow is an impermissible luxury for many. At the same time, renting an apartment costs a tidy sum. That is why, sooner or later, Muscovites and visitors begin to think about acquiring their own real estate. An apartment in the city is very expensive, but sometimes such a waste of money is completely not justified. Currently, large residential complexes with their own infrastructure located in the suburbs are very popular. This complex will be discussed in this material. We will try to make an objective review of the Sherwood Forest residential complex . Reviews of real residents will help readers to form an opinion about the mentioned object. This is an excellent information base for potential buyers facing a difficult choice.





about the project

So, the Sherwood Forest residential complex is a unique project in its format, located 30 km from Moscow in the direction of Simferopol highway. It is located in the middle of a coniferous forest, filling the complex with an amazing aroma, by the way, by the way, healing. This is a separate living space with its own protected area and infrastructure, compensating for the remoteness from the capital. Conceptually, the complex is made in the best traditions of old England, harmoniously fits into the local landscape. Want to feel like especially important people? You definitely should get to know the complex better.





Extraordinary design decisions, the opportunity to purchase both an apartment and a townhouse in an ecologically clean area are what really attracts. Well, the developed infrastructure of the complex, represented not only by shops, restaurants, but also by fitness centers, a bank and its own bakery, allows you to fully enjoy the delights of country life, without losing capital comfort. It remains only to understand how the expectations of the residents are justified.

Location

If you value comfort, ecology, you definitely need to go to Sherwood Forest. The cottage village is really remote from the city, but this is precisely its main and undeniable advantage. All those who managed to visit the facility say that their heart was conquered in the first five minutes. The air here is amazing, saturated with essential oils - this is not even in the most luxurious complex in Moscow. Residents note that the village of Sherwood Forest is a paradise for families with young children and newlyweds who are just thinking about replenishment. You can rest assured: the child will be overly grateful to you for growing up in such heavenly conditions, and not brought up in a concrete box surrounded by dust, dirt and endless noise. The place is really picturesque and ideal for living.





Transport accessibility

Given the remoteness of the Sherwood Forest residential complex from the capital, the issue of transport accessibility is relevant. The road is perhaps the main drawback of the complex. Residents note that you can get to the place only by an SUV, and public transport so far only goes to the nearest village. This feature should be taken into account when planning the purchase of an apartment or townhouse here.

Infrastructure

Residential complex "Sherwood Forest" is not a holiday village, but a small town among the forest, intended for permanent residence. That is why there is nothing strange in the fact that every potential resident wants to know what infrastructure facilities he will receive. The remoteness from the capital in this case is offset by its own infrastructure. Of course, the project provides for the construction of shops, pharmacies, even a bank branch and a fitness center will appear. The developer paid great attention to the organization of the recreation area: here there will be excellent playgrounds and sports grounds, a beach area, a barbecue area and walks. Of course, the developer is not going to stop there: he plans to build a stunning outdoor sports complex with bike paths and ski slopes. The project provides for the construction of a kindergarten, school and even a hospital, but this will not be in the very near future. That is why at the initial stage it is necessary to come to terms with some inconveniences.





Layout

"Sherwood Forest" - a cottage village occupying an impressive territory. The choice of buyers offers excellent apartments in a low-rise building and spacious town houses located directly on the riverbank. At the moment, the project is not completed, so it is impossible to assess the quality of the interior decoration. But now you can get acquainted with planning decisions.

So, many say that the developer really worked on the project, attracted professional architects and designers to it to create excellent conditions that would satisfy every taste. Here you can find very small, affordable apartments. There is housing with a spacious kitchen and isolated rooms - there are so many options that at first the eyes just run wide. And here the qualified support of managers is appreciated. Based on the wishes of customers, they select the perfect option.





Excellent townhouses and cottages for large families will be built on the territory. And here the developer also provided for every detail. In each project, he tried to isolate the living and dining areas from places of rest and sleep, thereby he managed to achieve maximum comfort for all residents.

Construction technology

So, all houses will surrender in stages. Monolithic-brick technology was chosen for construction, which allows you to quickly build warm and strong houses. High-quality materials are the main concept of the developer, and many buyers managed to verify this personally. For the construction of walls and partitions, high-quality gas silicate blocks are used, and monolithic reinforced concrete beams are used along the entire perimeter of the building, giving the entire structure even greater strength and endurance.





Construction stages

The Sherwood Forest complex is a large-scale project, the name of which is well known to many Muscovites. It involves the construction of low-rise houses and townhouses, while being long-term. The object is being leased in stages, and most of the apartments and townhouses were purchased at the stage of preparation of the site, which, of course, gave rise to a number of doubts. There are enough reviews on this topic on the respective portals. It is clear from them that the company employs real professionals, truly qualified employees who are aimed at the satisfaction of each client. That is why they do not interfere, but, on the contrary, offer to track every stage of construction: pouring the foundation, erecting walls, installing the roof, and making communications. So, all those who took an active part and controlled the construction of their own home, note that the construction technology itself and the quality of the materials used were pleasantly pleased. It is nice that the developer puts detailed photo reports with comments for those who can not independently come to the object.
