In Sergiev Posad, hotels are ready to receive guests throughout the year. The new Posadsky Hotel is located on the central square of this glorious city, founded in the 18th century. Its main attraction is the Trinity Lavra of St. Sergius, a monastery located not far from the hotel.

The distance from the Sheremetyevo Airport to the Posadsky Hotel is only 94 km, there is a public transport stop nearby, while the bus and train station are just 1.5 kilometers away. The central location of this hotel is convenient for the life of tourists and guests who visit the city for pilgrimage and business trips. In 2013, the hotel was completely renovated.

This hotel in Sergiev Posad offers spacious rooms with LCD TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, coffee and tea making facilities. The bathroom includes personal hygiene products and a hairdryer. The hotel also offers breakfasts with a huge selection of dishes. Wi-Fi is available throughout. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day.
