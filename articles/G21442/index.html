Nesterov Alexey and his creative path

Russia is a huge country in which tens of thousands of films and TV shows are released annually on large and small screens. The number of actors playing them also rolls over, so the viewer and remembers among those people only those who play the main roles. But after all, actors who play secondary and episodic roles are also very talented and try to give all the best. And one of the best among them is Alexei Nesterov, who played in 30 films and 15 performances. He managed to be remembered by many thanks to his bright appearance and charisma.





The birth of a future actor

Alyosha was born on February 14, 1963 in a loving family. His childhood and youth were cloudless, so that he could decide how to live, where to go to study and what to devote himself to. He was the most ordinary teenager who wanted to stand out among his peers (he did it well: Nesterov has an amazingly beautiful baritone), the guy decided to devote his life to cinema and theater. He wanted to play, to try on a new character every day, to surprise others, to captivate and fascinate them. Therefore, Alexei spent all his free time in cinemas and theaters, contemplating films and performances and introducing himself in the place of famous actors.

Places of study of Alexey Nesterov

After graduating from school, Alex decided not to waste time in vain and took up the realization of his dreams. After graduation, he immediately went to take exams at the Saratov Theater School, where he was immediately accepted. Studying at a university was easy for a guy, so in 1984 he successfully completed his studies at the course of R. Belyakova and graduated as a certified theater actor.

However, the training process of Nesterov Alexei did not end there. Having graduated from the theater school and having enslaved from 1984 to 1985 as an actor in the Oryol Youth Theater, the young man wanted to improve his acting skills. Therefore, he entered the acting course of master B. Golubovsky in GITIS. Studying at this institute was again easy for Alexei, so in 1990 he successfully completed the course and was able to significantly improve his qualifications.
