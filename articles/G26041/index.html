DIY do-it-yourself creative Christmas tree

I want to celebrate the New Year in a good mood. A creative New Year tree will help to spend a holiday in an interesting and original way. It is easy to make from what is at hand. You can turn napkins, sweets, tablets, and even pasta into a fluffy beauty.

Creative christmas tree

With your own hands it‚Äôs not difficult to create a Christmas tree from napkins . And it will be so unusual that not everyone will guess what it is made of. The material is very affordable - three-layer paper napkins. First you need to turn them into ‚Äúroses‚Äù, and then stick these blanks on the base.





So, first make a cone out of whatman or cardboard. To do this, cut out the triangle and round off its hypotenuse. Stick the two legs together.

Take the first napkin. It can be green or any other. Do not unfold the napkin, put a round object with a diameter of 6-7 cm on it, circle it and cut it to a measure.

The formation of roses and mounting them on a cone





Since the napkin is three-layer, folded 4 times, you immediately cut out 12 circles, which are stacked. Fasten them in the middle with a stapler. Now the fun begins. Raise the first circle and fold it in the form of a wick. This is the core of the flower.

Lift the second circle, fold it, indicating 4 faces. It should fit to the core, but not very tightly. Twist the third circle in the same way, then all the others. The last, twelfth, only bend slightly, without lifting it so that it becomes wavy. The flower is ready, glue it to the bottom of the cone.

Make a second rose, attach it in the same way. After the first bottom row, go to the second, placing the roses in a checkerboard pattern. But make flowers for him a little smaller diameter. This also applies to the following series. The closer they are to the crown of the cone, the smaller the diameter of the workpieces. Attach the last rose to the top.

You can leave the tree like that or decorate it with beads, beads, sparkles.

Colored paper is used

The following idea is suitable for those who want to quickly make a small copy of a conifer. It can be hung as a talisman and decoration on the glass of the car, put on the desktop in the office.





To make a yellow Christmas tree, cut and glue three blanks for the cone, differing in size. Put the largest on the table, put a smaller figure on it, on top - the smallest. To fasten all these parts together, put glue on the top of the two lower cones, then press for 20 seconds, placing the workpieces in the indicated order - from large to small. If you want to hang the tree, pierce the crown with a paper clip and tie a decorative cord to it . You can leave a small hole at the top of each cone through which you will pass the cord. To hold it tight, tie its two ends to a knot on the back.

Three-tiered openwork Christmas tree made of paper

In almost the same way, you can create two more small spruces. For the first cut 3 circles, the diameter of each is less than the diameter of the subsequent. Make scissors on the edges of these figures with a scissors, you can slightly bend it up to give fluffy to your creation.

On the side of each of the three blanks, make an incision to the center of the circle, lay the incised sides with a slight odor on each other and glue, but not completely, leaving small holes in the center. Through them you will also pass the cord and use it to fix the original craft in a hanging position. Here's a creative tree. Do not throw away empty bottles

Under such a tall tree will fit many gifts. The main thing is not to be naughty about him, so as not to break. To create this grandiose magnificence, you will need many green bottles, circles made of glass or hard transparent plastic, plexiglass or plywood.





Start laying out the tree from its bottom. First place the round base on the floor, then place the bottles on it with the neck up. To make the structure durable, it is better to apply a little glue for glass to the bottom of the bottles and fix the container on the base. Place them next to each other.

On top, put a circle of slightly smaller diameter than the previous one, place and fix the bottles of the next tier on it. Lay out the whole Christmas tree in the same way. Each subsequent row will have fewer bottles than the bottom. When there is one left at the top, attach to it a star glued from corks. You can admire the glass tree!

Creative pasta tree

There are many ideas for New Year's fantasies. In just half an hour, pasta will turn into a beautiful Christmas tree. In addition to them, you will need a cone-shaped plastic bottle, a glue gun, and green and gold acrylic paints.

Cut the bottom from the bottle so that the workpiece stands firmly on the table. Cover the bottom of the bottle with glue, attach the first row of pasta. To do this, use products in the form of bows, then a creative Christmas tree, decorated with your own hands, will look amazing.





Place the second one above the first tier, also pasting pasta. Attach them in a checkerboard pattern to the blanks of the first row. Place the bows vertically. After that, complete the third and subsequent tiers. Then you will get a charming creative tree. A master class with photos will help you understand how to do this.





After the whole bottle is covered with pasta, put it on the bottom. If you have a stable round support, you can attach it so that you can see the leg of the New Year's beauty. If not, the tree will look great too.

To decorate the New Year attribute with toys, take openwork pasta. They can be in the form of stars, circles, letters. Candy tree
