Picturesque embankment of Anapa

The city itself is beautiful and is actively visited by tourists. It is safe to say that the Anapa embankment is one of the most wonderful places where tourists often visit. This resort is hard to imagine functioning without its main decoration.

About this place

In the warm time of the city, it actively takes tourists to Anapa. The embankment is an extended place with a wide variety of interesting landscapes, which many people look at with pleasure. Here you can meet the freshness of the breeze from the sea.

Anapa Embankment is a great place for a walk. The sandy beaches will amaze you with their spaciousness and cleanliness. Major changes were carried out here, as a result of which it was possible to create a completely stunning complex for relaxation and pleasant pastime.





where to go

The renewed Anapa embankment has two tiers, where there are many cafes full of exotic, restaurant complexes, dance halls, clubs for discos, nightlife, where always playful music and fun. This place feels the rhythm of modernity and lives, following it all the year round.

If you ascend to the upper tier, you will have the opportunity to look at the waters of the Black Sea. Beautiful yachts, which are accepted by the local bay, moor near the shore. In Russia there is only one water stadium, and it is precisely the Anapa embankment that has it. You can go there from below. If you wish, you can rent a jet ski and devote your time to a wonderful outdoor activity. A wonderful place to relax is Anapa. Photos of beaches and the promenade make it clear how picturesque and beautiful this place is.





Historical knowledge

Here you can breathe air and gain new knowledge in the museum of antiquity called "Gorgippia", which can be reached by rising to the upper tier. Your curiosity will be fully satisfied. Not only visiting tourists come to admire the exhibits, but also the indigenous people of Anapa. Here you will open an exposition illustrating the excavation. You will be directly in the fresh air.

In addition, there is the so-called Alley of Love. This site is located on the territory of the High Bank. Thus, you can walk along the coast, admiring it. There is a lighthouse here, where local residents often meet and relax. This item is located away from the bustle of the city, disco noise and annoying lights. The sunset is perfectly visible from here, which brings a lot of pleasure to everyone who got here.





Favorite place for many

It is true that the embankment is present in every port city by the sea. Residents love to come here, regularly visit it.

The same can be said of crowding. Especially at the peak of the beach season. Even if you come here early in the morning, you can immediately see a crowd of people admiring the rising sun and blue, piles of clouds. Screaming seagulls fly overhead, flashing white wings. When the sun goes to its zenith, people become a little less. People who have come to rest shift towards the beach and splash in warm water. However, in the evening, finding a free place again becomes problematic. You can see a stunning sunset here. After a hot day, you can breathe in slightly cool air and relax. Such walks are extremely useful, because your lungs and skin absorb all the valuable substances contained in the surrounding atmosphere.

What is in the museum

We are always happy to meet you at the Gorgippia Museum in the open. The city you will be in is really very old and has more than 2.5 thousand years. There are frescoes in the museum that speak of the exploits of Hercules. You can look at the original coins, there is evidence of the existence of the slave trade. In addition, near the embankment there are Russian gates - a monument telling about how the townspeople defended Anapa from the invasion of the Turks. So the Anapa Embankment also has a great cognitive function. Museum address: Krymskaya street, 86.
