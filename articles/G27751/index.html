Deposits of Bank Vozrozhdenie for individuals

Vozrozhdenie is a large bank with a head office located in Moscow. It is one of the 50 largest credit institutions in terms of financial performance. The Bank has a fairly wide network of branches located mainly in Central Russia.

Bank Vozrozhdenie was created in 1991 by transforming one of the departments of the Agroprombank of the USSR. The founders of the credit institution and at the moment retain control over significant blocks of shares, which together account for about 60%.

The nature of the business of a financial institution is universal. Among clients there is a large number of large and medium enterprises. At the same time, the retail lending and deposits sector is actively developing.

For customers who want to place free cash with profit, Bank Vozrozhdenie offers deposits. The interest rate will be different depending on the chosen program and the period of storage of money in the account. It can be up to 11% per annum.





"Instant"

Deposits of Bank Vozrozhdenie under the Instant program can be opened round-the-clock using self-service devices or online banking. Initially, you must deposit at least 3,000 rubles into the account, while the client can choose a convenient period: 1, 3 months or six months.

At the end of the placement period, you will not need to contact the bank branch either. The entire amount will be automatically transferred back to the plastic card, the accrued interest will be transferred to it . Deposit rates in the framework of the Instant program, Bank Vozrozhdenie offers from 9.5 to 9.95% per annum.

Payment of profit occurs every month. The investor can indicate any account for the transfer of interest or choose their capitalization to increase profits.

These deposits of Bank Vozrozhdenie are distinguished by the method of opening and the maximum interest rate in rubles. Multicurrency

Clients who are afraid of losses due to a sharp change in exchange rates or want to receive additional income due to a change in the value of the currency can use Vozrozhdenie deposits under the Multicurrency program. At the time of conclusion of the contract, accounts are opened immediately in dollars, rubles and euros. Between them, you can transfer money any number of times without losing interest from one currency to another. The shelf life of money can be 6 months, 1 or 1.5 years. In rubles, the rate will be 8.1-8.4% per annum, and in dollars and euros - 0.5-1.2%.

Other products for private customers

The universal nature of the business determines a large number of services offered by the credit organization to individuals. Among them there are not only deposits, but also loans, remote services, safes, bank cards and other products.

Some products offered by Vozrozhdenie Bank have few analogues in the Russian market, for example, Individual Payroll Project. This service allows you to receive payments from the employer at Vozrozhdenie Bank. Together with convenient money management, the client also receives additional benefits:

  • Reduced rates on consumer loans and a reduced package of documents.
  • Improved conditions for making most deposits. At the same time, Bank Vozrozhdenie will offer higher deposit rates.
  • The ability to remotely manage finances, including opening deposits.

The funds placed by Vozrozhdenie Bank by individuals are insured in accordance with the laws of Russia.

A long history, large size, well-diversified assets and liabilities allow us to speak of Vozrozhdenie Bank as a reliable financial institution. Convenience of remote servicing and wide geography of offices provide the comfort of working with a credit institution. Therefore, Bank Vozrozhdenie is a good choice of modern financial organizations for most customers.
