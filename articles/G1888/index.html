Pension "Aitar" (Sukhumi): reviews of tourists about the quality of service and leisure

Pension "Aitar" (Sukhum, Abkhazia) is one of the largest resort complexes of the near abroad. People come here for active youth and a relaxing family holiday. It consists of two buildings in which two-story individual cottages are located. "Aitar" (boarding house, Sukhumi), the photo of which will be presented below, is perfect for both family vacations and leisure time with friends. In the pension there is a parking lot, a cafe, a Finnish bathhouse, a huge park area, a sports ground, as well as a pool and a sauna.





The territory on which the Aitar boarding house is located (Sukhum, Abkhazia) is fenced and is under protection. The complex is located in a subtropical park and occupies 5 hectares of its area.

Abkhazia, Sukhum. Pension "Aitar". Reviews of tourists about the area

This resort complex compares favorably with many health resorts. Gorgeous landscape, the beach, located in a quiet and comfortable place, extensive infrastructure - all this has at its disposal a boarding house "Aitar" (Sukhumi). Guest reviews are filled only with positive emotions after staying here on vacation. The arboretum deserves special attention, closed all day from the sun by densely planted trees. What else is rich in the territory on which the pension "Aitar" (Sukhumi) is located? Reviews of vacationers are undoubtedly the best confirmation of all the advantages of the complex. So, guests celebrate the splendor of nature surrounding the health resort. Here you can see squirrels and peacocks strolling between the shady pine alleys, near artificial ponds, successfully entered into the landscape. Each cottage is surrounded by unusually beautiful greenery of the park. The pride of the health resort can be called covered with small pebbles and always clean beach. Neatness is distinguished not only by the adjacent territory, but also by the boarding house itself. "Aitar" (Sukhumi) (guest reviews reliably confirm this) is considered one of the most environmentally friendly places to relax. The staff of the health resort conducts regular cleaning both around the buildings and inside them.



Accommodation

The number of rooms is one of the undoubted advantages that boarding house Aitar (Sukhumi) has. Customer reviews of prices confirm the fact that anyone can relax here. The health resort offers guests three types of rooms: standard, superior and separate 2-storey houses. All rooms have batteries, which allows you to relax comfortably in the winter season. In summer, air conditioning works. The standart rooms are equipped with a separate bathroom (bath or shower, toilet). The rooms also have a balcony. In a room of this type, the number of beds depends on the number of people living in it. There is a small wardrobe for clothes and a TV. Standard rooms are located in different sections of the hotel. The cost will also depend on the location of the room. Settling vacationers is carried out from 12-00. The health resort staff works around the clock. General cleaning is carried out every day. Superior rooms differ from previous ones in the best living conditions. The bedroom has a comfortable bed, a spacious wardrobe for clothes, a small cupboard, trellis and pouf. The room has a TV, a table and a comfortable sofa. The rooms of this category are usually two-room.

All rooms have balconies. The rooms also have an entrance hall, a bathroom with all accessories. Two-story buildings are equipped with spiral staircases. The houses have balconies with chairs and a table. The windows overlook the park and the complex. In addition, the cottages are equipped with bathrooms, consisting of a shower, toilet and sink. The bedroom has a double bed with bedside tables. There is also a ottoman, wardrobe and dressing table. The bedroom is equipped with air conditioning. On the ground floor in each house there is an entrance hall and a small hall. There is also a bathroom with toilet and sink, in the hall there is a TV, fridge, bed (2-bedroom) and two armchairs, chairs and a bedside table. Two-story houses are designed for 2, 3 and 4 people. A maximum of 6 guests can be accommodated in them. In cottages it will be quite comfortable to relax with family or in the company of friends. There are luxury houses. They can additionally use an iron and ironing board. In the pension there is a paid parking lot, which is guarded around the clock, as well as the whole complex.

Services provided by boarding house "Aitar"

Guest reviews confirm the widespread belief that in a health resort you can not only improve your health, but also have fun. The local beach invariably attracts with its cleanliness and well-groomed. There is a tour desk for lovers of sightseeing. Here you can book trips to the most interesting places in Sukhum. Aitar Pension has its own well and boiler room. So there are no interruptions in water here. In addition, there are baths and saunas on site. Three meals a day in the health resort, according to the "buffet" system.



We combine rest and treatment

In the health resort visitors are offered not only wellness treatments. On the territory of the complex are open sports grounds, tennis courts, swimming pools. Near the health resort there is "Marheul" - a well-known source of mineral waters. The resort complex "Aitar" is great for families with children. On the territory of the health resort there are special play areas, so that little guests will not be bored.



Beach

It is not only visitors to the Aytar complex that can relax and sunbathe on the beach. Vacationers who live in the nearby Anana Hotel come here. The beach itself is equipped with a solarium (covered canopy from the sun), vacationers are provided with sunbeds and umbrellas. There is also a restaurant with a summer terrace. It takes only 5 minutes to go to the beach, the beach is covered with pebbles, and the bottom is sandy, which is very convenient for families with kids.

Nutrition

On the territory of the health resort and in the vicinity there is a huge selection of restaurants and cafes worth visiting. Only along the promenade are a large number of establishments. The most famous of them: cafe "Meeting", "Caucasus", "Basla", "Artem". There is also a restaurant on the summer terrace of the beach. For guests, there is a dining room and a cafe from the military boarding house MBO.



Additional Information

The central market can easily be reached both by its own transport and by public transport. The central promenade is just a 35-minute walk from the guesthouse. A huge plus of the Aitar boarding house is the presence of a bath-and-health complex, a huge parking lot, and souvenir shops. Within a 15-minute walk there is a small market (here you can buy local fruits and vegetables) and an Internet club. Additionally, for the convenience of vacationers, a car wash and a call center are organized near the complex. All guests have access to local service providers. The shuttle stop is a few meters away.

Health Resort Hours

The Aytar complex begins its work from the end of April, and continues until the end of October. It also offers fun New Year holidays. During this period, special discounts are available for guests. Having visited the Aitar boarding house, the tourist receives a lot of positive impressions and pleasant memories of the rest.
