Cultural and historical sights

The lake was famous for the fact that it was here that the experimental Russian fleet was built. Today, some interesting exhibits have been preserved in the museum-estate. Pleshcheyevo lake: rest and tourism

In the warmer months, a huge number of campsites operate around the lake. You will have to pay for living in such a tent city, not all parking lots have access to places where you can swim. There are also full-fledged sports facilities, some of which host guests year-round. One of the most popular sports here is windsurfing. There are more than 15 species of fish in the lake; you should learn about the possibility of fishing from the administration of the reserves or from the owners of recreation and tourism facilities. In certain seasons, fishing for certain fish species is permitted.

Caution should be taken in the local forests, because many species of plants and mushrooms are also listed in the Red Book. There are also children's summer camps in the conservation area, as well as motels and health institutions for patients of different ages.
