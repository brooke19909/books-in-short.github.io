How to call an ambulance from a mobile phone?

Megafon realized this opportunity by number 030. In other words, if you suddenly felt ill or close, passerby, etc., and the mobile operator is Megafon, then you should call the indicated number. If the operator is different, then correctly call to the following numbers:

  • MTS - 030;

Emergency call

There is also another way to call an ambulance from Megafon - this is the number of the unified duty dispatch service. To call here you will need to dial the following combination of numbers from your mobile phone - 112. When calling this number, they will help to solve any problem associated with an emergency. 112 will allow you to call an ambulance, and the police, and employees of the Ministry of Emergencies. Moreover, the number is available not only within Russia, but also abroad, namely in Europe.

An important feature of a call to a single emergency service is that by dialing 112 on your phone, a citizen will go to her nearest branch. In other words, if the call is made in Moscow, it will be addressed to the Moscow service; in Ryazan - Ryazan, etc.

It should be noted that the combination of numbers does not change depending on the telecom operator; it is the same for everyone and is available to call even with a negative balance. Moreover, you can get into a single on-line dispatch service even if there is no SIM card inserted in the phone, it is enough to have a mobile phone.





Call communication

Most often, an ambulance call is in a state of shock. Therefore, the numerous questions that came from the other side of the tube seem pointless and can cause aggression. But it is important to know that the ambulance officer asks all this for a reason, it is important for him to correctly assess the situation and send to the patient exactly the specialist who can best help in this situation.

So, most likely, when calling an ambulance, you will have to answer the following questions:

  • What happened The answer should be brief: accident, unconscious person, etc.
  • Who needs help? Here you should indicate the gender, age of the victim.
  • Address? It is necessary to specify the scene as accurately as possible.
  • Who you are? It is necessary to introduce oneself: first name / surname, who the victim belongs to (relative, acquaintance, passer-by).
  • The phone number of the one who decided to call an ambulance from Megafon. It may be needed to clarify some data.

While waiting

After we managed to call an ambulance from Megafon, we should expect the appearance of specialists. This can take 20-30 minutes, and sometimes more. If there is simply no way to wait so much time, you can start moving towards the ambulance brigade. But this is not possible in all cases. For example, in an accident, it is forbidden to remove the injured person from the car independently; this can aggravate the situation.
