Reporting

The next important moment for us is the preparation of a tax return. It is she who is needed to apply to the tax authorities to pay certain contributions, and to receive certain deductions.

2-personal income tax

What else is required for all cases? Here you have to take one more certificate. It is called 2-PIT. Or, as it may still be called, a document on the income of individuals. More precisely, about wages.

Where to get it? At your employer. If you independently carry out this or that activity, you will have to use a specialized program to generate a certificate. The same one where you created 3-NDFL. Most often, the employer must give you this form as soon as possible. This is the most common option.

In what form does this tax document take? Refund of income tax is possible only when 2-PIT is the original. And nothing more. Copies are not accepted with all desire, even certified. Mortgage and apartments

Mortgage is now widespread in Russia. Income tax refund in this case takes place. True, you have to pretty prepare. The list of required documents is supplemented quite seriously. Although getting all the documents is not so difficult.



If you just bought an apartment, then you need to attach a certificate of ownership, a contract of sale, checks and payments. When it comes to mortgages, you will have to supplement the already listed list with just a few documents. You will need to attach a certificate of payment of interest on the mortgage, as well as an agreement with the bank (loan agreement). Practice shows that there are no problems with this.

Senior citizens

What kind? Here you will need a pension certificate (copy), as well as all documents that confirm your benefits. For example, disability certificates. If you were a military man, then a military card is also useful. It's all.
