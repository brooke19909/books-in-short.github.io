Brief information





Samsung Galaxy Core 2 G355H, the characteristics of which are soberly evaluated by experts on the top three with a plus, is a typical representative of the budget class and has a screen with a diagonal of 4.5 inches. A small device that can fit in one palm. At the same time, it will be convenient to operate with him in that sense that the thumb freely reaches any point on the display, right to the very corners. With permission, the developers, of course, screwed up. However, more on that later. On board, we are expecting a not-so-fresh, but quite working version of the Android operating system. The entire set of necessary communications is present, with the exception of the 4G LTE module.

Design





Samsung Galaxy Core 2, the characteristics of which can be found above, shows us the continuation of the design line, which specialists of the South Korean company are bending. In some ways, they decided to move away from their traditions, abandoning rectangular shapes. You may notice that the edges of the apparatus are rounded. Well, this is a very good solution, and there can be no complaints about this parameter. The phone lies in the hand neatly, tightly, reliably, but all this only under normal conditions. If your hands sweat or become wet from water, the device tries to slip out of your hands, which is not good.

The budget segment of the South Korean company in this regard has long been not pleasing to many users.
