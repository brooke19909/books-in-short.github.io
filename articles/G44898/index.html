Flamingo Beach Hotel 3 * in Larnaca (Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Flamingo Beach Hotel 3 * - one of the most interesting beach "three rubles" in Larnaca. No wonder its rating among tourists is quite high, and popularity is growing every year. Lovely rooms, where there is everything you need to feel comfortable, great views from either side, nice and helpful staff, very convenient location - this is a brief description of this hotel. After all, here you can fall asleep to the pleasant sound of the sea, and this is rare even in Cyprus!



Larnaca

Flamingo Beach Hotel 3 * is located in the city, which archaeologists consider the oldest in Cyprus. Although in general this resort is not much different from the rest of the island's villages - the old center, green outskirts with coastal hotels, a promenade and countless restaurants where everyone boasts a catch and has its own secrets of how to cook fish in such a way that you lick your fingers. This is a budget resort where people come with not very big money. In addition, healing sand and shallow sea attract families with children here. The most famous monument of Larnaca is the temple of St. Lazarus, where, according to legend, he was buried. And the city itself is named after a friend of Jesus Christ. At least that's what one of the translations of the name Larnaca says. From other architectural monuments in the city center, you can see the ancient ruins of ancient Greek sanctuaries and the ancient Byzantine church. Territory and what is nearby

Hotel Flamingo Beach Hotel 3 * is not very big, but comfortable. It consists of two buildings with a common hall. The last major repair was made in 2007. On the territory there is a pleasant patio, a lobby with a reception, aquariums, a games room and a TV. There is a small pond with turtles. Tourists noted a beautiful lounge with many comfortable sofas. You can sunbathe on the terrace around the rooftop pool. In one building (white) - standard rooms, in the other (pink) - apartments. This section of the hotel is named for Mackenzie. Not far from the hotel there is the reserved Larnaca salt lake, where pink flamingos live. That's why the hotel was called. There is a mosque on the lake. Near the hotel there are a lot of cafes, taverns, there is a Petros supermarket where you can buy anything you want, and at the same time cheaper than in the center. A little further - a picturesque port with yachts. To the center of Larnaca - about half an hour on foot along the promenade.



Accommodation and facilities

The hotel Flamingo Beach Hotel 3 * - sixty-four rooms. They are almost the same, but have twin or double beds. There are also single and family rooms. Inhabit for five minutes, no more. The filling of the rooms is standard. Air conditioning, bathroom with hairdryer, satellite TV. The beds are wooden with comfortable mattresses. There is a small table and a large type of writing. Plasma TV. Each room has a balcony with a plastic table and chairs in order to have a snack or drink tea while enjoying the beautiful view. The bathroom is very clean. Every day they report shampoo, soap, shower gel. Clean cleanly. Feel at home. True, sockets are very specific in shape, but this is easy to handle. The apartments have a bedroom, a living room, a terrace with sea views and a kitchen, but there is no stove. But there are a refrigerator, a toaster and an electric kettle. A safe in the room costs two euros per day, at the reception - for free. But there is no theft at the hotel, so you can safely leave things in the room.

Beach vacation

Hotel Flamingo Beach Hotel 3 * (Larnaca) is located very close to the Makenzi Municipal Beach. Between the hotel and the coast runs a beautiful, freshly finished promenade. Tourists claim that of the nearby hotels, this one has the best location in relation to the sea. Entering the water is excellent, gentle, without waves. To the depth go fifteen to twenty meters. It is highly recommended not only to vacationers with children, but also to those who are not particularly good at swimming. Sun beds and umbrellas are paid. Many different water sports - surfing, kayaking. The sea is clean, without algae, the sand is dark, fine. You can easily decompose here on towels and not pay for sunbeds. Some tourists prefer to go to the more famous central beach "Finikoudes". But here the opinions of vacationers are divided. You can sunbathe in the hotel. Since the hotel is not very large, the pool here is located on the roof. But around him - deck chairs and everything, as expected. Unless there are no umbrellas. Tourists claim that swimming there with a panoramic view of the Mediterranean Sea is a very funny thing.



Shopping

Of course, Larnaca is not Milan or Paris to specifically arrange tours here for acquiring branded items. But, coming on vacation, you always want to take something with you as a souvenir. And if you happen to make a good purchase, then vacation memories will become even more valuable. This is also believed by the guests of the Flamingo Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus). Their reviews are full of tips on what is better and cheaper to purchase in Larnaca. First of all, it is high-quality olive oil at five euros per liter. Various souvenirs and magnets can be purchased on the Fenoukides promenade. Of the food, Halloumi cheese, sweets such as Turkish delight and pastille, as well as Cypriot wines, especially Commandria, are usually popular. In addition, those who want to buy something authentic are recommended to visit the village of Lefkara, which is famous for its lace. This art is so ancient that the works of local residents, as they say, even admired Leonardo da Vinci. Jugs, mugs and other pottery products are being brought from here. But for this it is better to go to the village - there the prices will be lower than in the center of the resort, and there is more choice, and there is an opportunity to bargain.
