What size should be snud: reviews

Such a scarf can have several options: in one revolution and in two.

The size of a snook in two turns, with knitting needles, can be different. After all, such a scarf can be used both as a cape, and as a scarf, and as a scarf-cape. In practice, it is quite convenient to wear it exactly in two turns, because it will be able to protect from the cold, and not only will serve as an ornament.

What size should a snud be? This question is very often asked by knitters, especially those who take the first steps in knitting art. The approximate size of a twist of two turns, knitted with needles, depends on who will wear it. As experienced craftswomen say, such options are possible:

  • Children aged 3 to 5 years: length - 100, width - 16 cm.
  • Children from 6 to 8 years old: length - 114 cm, width - 19 cm.
  • Children from 9 to 11 years old: length - 128 cm, width - 20 cm.
  • Women: length - 142 cm, width - 22 cm.

The most convenient scarf size in one revolution:

  • For schoolchildren: width - from 45 to 50 cm.
  • For adults: width - from 50 to 60 cm.

In order for the scarf to protect the neck as much as possible from the cold, it is necessary not to miss the next moment: the wider the snood, the higher it should be.

The most suitable height for a scarf with a width of 65 cm is 40 cm.





How many loops do you need to dial?

The number of loops depends on the final scarf length required.

If you need to tie a small snood, which is planned to be worn in one revolution, then loops will need from 60 to 80. It is necessary to take into account the thickness of the thread from which you plan to knit the product, as well as the diameter of the spokes. This plays a big role. If you will wear the finished product in two turns, then loops will need from 130 pieces or more.





We knit snood on knitting needles

One turn to wear, a large scarf is not needed. Depending on how the accessory will be used, it is necessary to choose its optimal size. For a regular clamp, 15 centimeters wide will be enough. If the scarf is planned to be worn as a wrap, then the ideal width will be 30-35 centimeters.

Circular knitting needles are ideal for work. Threads need a suitable color scheme, with an admixture of wool in the composition.

When choosing a suitable pattern, one must be guided by personal preferences, and also look at the complexity of the execution.

Step by Step Description

  1. Type 60 stitches on the knitting needles. If the threads are thick, less loops will be required.
  2. Connect the loops into a ring and pin a pin at the junction.
  3. Knit three rows of purl stitches.
  4. The next three rows are facial.
  5. After several rows, try on a scarf for the child. If all goes well, you can continue knitting.
  6. Tie out the required scarf width.
  7. After that it is necessary to close all the loops.
  8. Fashionable and original accessory is ready!
