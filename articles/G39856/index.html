Tariff "All inclusive L", "Beeline" and "Megaphone"

The cellular market in Russia, despite its high competitiveness, saturation and an impressive level of penetration, continues to develop dynamically. This can be observed in a variety of aspects. First of all, this, of course, is the level of technology introduced by operators, especially in the field of mobile Internet. However, there is another area where mobile service providers are actively introducing new solutions - charging. From how competently one or another operator will build a pricing policy, its success in the market will largely depend.





The level of technology is an important, but not the only aspect of competitiveness in this segment. Much also depends on the quality of the operator‚Äôs study of tariff plans. What are some noteworthy offers for Russian subscribers today? Many experts advise paying attention to all-inclusive L rates. Particularly interesting to some analysts are those that offer Megafon and Beeline.

General characteristics of tariffs

What are the characteristics of ‚ÄúAll Inclusive L‚Äù tariff plans, offered by several Russian telecom operators at once? What unites them except the name?

First of all, this is the complexity of the provided communication services. It is assumed that in the "All Inclusive L" tariff there are options that reflect the main modern mobile services - voice, SMS, MMS, as well as the Internet. At the same time, the integrated use of each of these features will be cheaper than if the subscriber bought the same amount of the resource in the framework of a separate tariff with comparable cash costs.

Thus, the main advantage of tariffs belonging to the same class as All Inclusive L is the simultaneous benefit in respect of each of the mobile services.

Benefits of tariffs with a monthly fee

The tariff plan "All inclusive L" from "Megaphone", as well as its analogue from "Beeline", is intended for subscribers who need calls to different types of phones (including long distance). If we are talking about intra-network calls, then today's tariffs offered by most operators, including the "Big Three", allow them to be made at minimal cost: the call price is minimal, and if there is a monthly fee, it is very small, about 100-150 rubles.





The tariff plan that Megafon, All Inclusive L offers, and the similar one that is in the arsenal of offers from Beeline, differ mainly in the amount of the resource in the form of off-net minutes. If we need relatively few of them, it is more expedient to choose a tariff from Beeline. If we talk a lot, then our choice is Megaphone.

Which operator is better?

Objective data that could unambiguously answer this question in the public domain is rather difficult to detect. The level of technology involved by each of the Big Three operators is generally the same. Therefore, the practical choice of the optimal provider of cellular communication services will depend on the actual indicators - signal stability, coverage area, as well as communication standards available at a particular point of use of a mobile phone.

Thus, if the question is which operator provides the most optimal All-Inclusive L tariff - Beeline or Megafon, then it makes sense to the subscriber, if possible, to pre-test the quality of service delivery by each of the providers, having drawn up how option, cheap rate without a monthly fee. Just to see how stable the signal is and what communication technologies are supported by operators.





It may be that, for example, the most modern 4G Internet will be supported by Beeline, but it will not be Megafon - in those parts of the city where the use of the telephone is supposed to be the most frequent. And vice versa. You can switch to "All Inclusive L" at any time, and, as a rule, with minimal financial costs: the amount of money that is on the balance sheet, one way or another, will be involved in the transition to a new tariff.

It's time to move from theory to practice. What are the features of two similar tariffs offered by different operators? How much can they be considered interchangeable and is it permissible to do so in principle?

Tariffs from Megafon: Moscow

We proceed to consider specific tariff offers from the operator. Everything, of course, depends on the geographical aspect of using cellular communications. We will consider the option in which the city where the "All Inclusive L" ("Megaphone") tariff is being used is Moscow.





The monthly subscription fee at the corresponding tariff in the capital of Russia is 1290 rubles. For this amount, a user of cellular services receives 1800 minutes of calls to any numbers in Moscow, the region, as well as in Russia. Similarly, the subscriber has 1800 SMS or, which is especially interesting, MMS. It doesn't matter what type of message - and this is despite the fact that MMS is thought to be more expensive.

Inbox, of course, is free.

Tariffs for outgoing calls of those types that we have not indicated above:

- to Megafon numbers in Moscow and the region: free of charge;

- to the capital's rooms after 1800 minutes - 2 rubles;

- to the phones of Russian subscribers upon the fact of resource consumption for a monthly fee - 2 rubles. 90 kopecks;

Prices for messages of those types that we have not indicated above:

- to the numbers of Moscow and Russia upon the expenditure of 1800 SMS - 2.90 rubles;

- after 1800 MMS - 6 rubles for each subsequent message to metropolitan subscribers and to other regions.

Mobile Internet Rates: 8 gigabytes for free.

Dissimilarity of the same

We see that, despite the identical sounding, tariffs like "All inclusive L" from different operators are completely different, based on the main options. Most likely, both tariff plans are aimed at different groups of subscribers. From the general - only the aspect that we noted at the very beginning of the article - in the desire of the operator to offer its user the opportunity to save money in an integrated way. True, in the case of the tariff from Beeline, this is expressed to a greater extent for the traditional options for using mobile communications - calls and SMS. The Internet in the framework of this tariff plan, as we were able to find out, is not very profitable.

It is difficult to call both tariffs interchangeable, as well as competing. At the same time, it can be noted that both have excellent chances to find their customers both in Moscow and in other cities.

At the same time, the regional aspect of using tariffs is especially interesting. Consider it.

Regional aspects

Comparing the two regions in which Megafon offers tariffs - Moscow and the Krasnoyarsk Territory, we saw that the principles of building the structure of subscriber offers may vary, depending on the geography of the provision of services. Although this is probably not news. Not only in the aspect of these specific tariffs, but also on the example of most other offers from these and other operators, you can observe the difference in approaches in pricing policy depending on the region.





But in general, the architecture of the All Inclusive L tariff in Moscow and Siberia is quite similar. And if, for example, a metropolitan subscriber, accustomed to this tariff plan, moves to Krasnoyarsk, then, probably, without any difficulties it adapts to the regional specifics of using the corresponding mobile services.
