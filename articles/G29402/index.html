Hotel Regatta Palace 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): photos, reviews

Sunny Beach is the most popular and densely populated resort in Bulgaria. There are tourist complexes for every taste. If you are looking for an average option that combines affordable accommodation and good service, then you should pay attention to the small hotel Regatta Palace 4 * (Bulgaria). What leisure facilities does he offer his guests?

Basic information and location

Walking access to the beach and the sea is one of the main factors affecting the choice of a place to relax. Hotel Regatta Palace 4 * has a favorable location. You can get to it from Burgas Airport , which is 30 km from the resort. Travel time by bus will take a little more than an hour. At the request of the hotel guests can arrange a paid shuttle service. Antique Nessebar is located 3 km from Sunny Beach. Many tourists interested in the history of the country book excursions to this ancient city. The famous Cocoa Beach is 500 meters away, and the public area is 100 meters away. On average, the road to the sea takes tourists no more than 5 minutes. The central part of the resort is 2.5 km away. You can get to it by taxi or bus.





The hotel opened in 2004. Since that time, he takes vacationers every summer, and in winter closes for renovation. The last full repair of the complex was completed in 2010. On the territory of 2700 square meters. m is the only five-story building, built in a modern urban style. It is considered a small complex. Only 50 rooms are available for settlement. Part of the hotel staff is fluent in Russian. Following European traditions, the complex has been checking in from 14:00. You need to leave the room before noon. It should be remembered that the hotel does not accept animals.

Number of rooms

The hotel has 50 comfortable and functional rooms, which are well suited for a small family with children. In Regatta Palace 4 * you can check in with kids of all ages. All apartments have a small balcony. There is a plastic dining table and two chairs on which you can eat while admiring views of the sea or the park. The bathroom is equipped with a modern shower, toilet, washbasin and a large mirror. Maids clean up every day and change towels in the bathroom, while vacationers are not in the rooms. Sheets are also updated, but only three times a week.





Most of the rooms in the hotel are standard, designed for two adults and a child. Their area is 30 square meters. The common room has a comfortable double bed. It is possible to install an extra bed. Family apartments (55 sq. M.) Are designed for the simultaneous residence of four guests. They have a small seating area with a pull-out sofa. Junior Suite - Superior room, which is suitable for tourists who appreciate a comfortable stay. It accommodates two adults and two children, has a private balcony and a seating area with a soft sofa.

Room Amenities

Rich equipment helps to create in the room all the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay. Guests of the Regatta Palace 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) will be able to feel the atmosphere of home comfort by touching high-quality linen made of cotton. Hypoallergenic pillows, sheets and duvet covers give good sleep even to people with sensitive skin. The towels and bathrobes hanging in the bathroom are also made of high quality Egyptian cotton. A comfortable temperature for sleeping in the room is maintained using a central air conditioner, which is included on hot summer days.

Each room is equipped with a modern TV with high-quality satellite TV. Among the channels you can find several Russian speakers. Contact the hotel, call your family and friends using the phone. But it is worth remembering that calls to other countries are made at the expense of tourists. All rooms are equipped with devices for wireless Internet access. For lovers of chilled drinks, a minibar can be delivered to the room, the filling of which is provided for a separate amount. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer for free.

Nutrition Details

Regatta Palace 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) offers its guests 2 food concepts: breakfast only and half board. All main meals are served in the hotel‚Äôs main restaurant, which specializes in dishes of Bulgarian, European and Mediterranean cuisine. A buffet is served for visitors. European cuisine is the basis of breakfast. Their menu consists of scrambled eggs, boiled and fried eggs, sandwiches and fresh pastries, sausages and cheese. There are always fruits on the table: oranges, apples, watermelons, melons and peaches. For dinner, local cuisine is served. You can choose from several types of meat, fish, as well as side dishes, fresh and stewed vegetables. Drinks are also included. Tourists are offered concentrated juices, mineral and sparkling water, tea, coffee and hot milk. For an additional fee, vacationers can get a special vegetarian menu.





In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has 2 more catering establishments. The lobby bar is located on the ground floor, near the reception. It serves refreshments, including spirits, as well as light snacks, snacks , desserts and ice cream. The sky bar is on the top floor. It offers amazing sea views. Hotel guests can order the same range of dishes as in the lobby bar .

Hotel facilities

In their reviews, guests praise the developed infrastructure of the hotel. Most of the services here are provided for free, but there are several paid services. For example, a laundry only launches clothes for a fee. Near the Regatta Palace 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach) there is a spacious parking lot where guests can leave their car. It is public, so reservation of a place and payment for it are not required. In the complex at the reception, tourists can arrange car rental and travel around the country on their own.





If vacationers feel unwell, then they can call a doctor. His receptions are not included in the insurance and are paid separately. Hotel guests need not be afraid that their belongings will be stolen by servants and cleaners. To store valuables, jewelry, money and documents at the reception is an electronic safe. Its use is not included in the price and is paid daily. A gift shop and hairdresser are also on site.

Children's rest: accommodation conditions and entertainment program

The Regatta Palace 4 * complex will be a great place to relax with children. Here they are provided with all the necessary conditions for living. For the youngest guests, a free cradle is brought to the room, while restaurants have high chairs for feeding. The hotel does not rent strollers. For children under 12 years of age, a 50% discount on accommodation is available.

The hotel can also offer the following activities for children of all ages:

  • shallow section in the common pool;
  • playground with swings, sandpit and trampoline;
  • mini club and entertainment.




Key positive feedback

Tourists who have already visited the Regatta Palace 4 *, write reviews not bad. In them they talk about the advantages and disadvantages of the hotel. In their opinion, this complex has the following advantages:

  • The road to the beach passes through a beautiful garden, which is very pleasant to walk around.
  • Good location. Near the hotel there are many shops, a bakery, a bus stop, and you can exchange currency at the reception at any time.
  • Cozy but small rooms. The refrigerator and air conditioning are working properly.
  • Very clean sea without algae with clear water. The entrance to it is very convenient, without holes and sharp stones.
  • The beach is also very tidy. Litter bins are everywhere, and employees constantly walk along the shore, picking up trash.
  • The hotel has several Russian-speaking employees who will always tell you where to go for fun.
  • The restaurant always has a lot of fresh vegetables and fruits, which can be taken without restriction.
  • In the evening and at night, the hotel is quiet, although some vacationers can interfere with loud animations from a neighboring hotel.




Key negative reviews

Unfortunately, not all vacationers were satisfied with the quality of the services provided. Complex Regatta Palace 4 * (Bulgaria) sometimes gets negative reviews. In their comments, tourists talk about the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Bed linen is heavily washed, therefore needs to be replaced. Towels are also very old, some have traces of stains and sewn holes.
  • On some days, the restaurant served tasteless food. One gets the impression that a completely different cook was working at this time.
  • No cereal is prepared for breakfast, so young children have nothing to eat.
  • Dirty tables and chairs in a restaurant. Sometimes there are not enough places in it, so you have to share one table with other guests.
  • All food is either overcooked or very oily. Not suitable for people with stomach problems.

Conclusion

Hotel Regatta Palace 4 * offers tourists a budget vacation. Do not count on high quality service when coming here. The hotel has many disadvantages that are associated with the work of the restaurant, so it is better to eat in establishments outside the complex. If the above comments do not bother you, then you can safely arrange a ticket. The hotel is suitable for travelers who want to save on accommodation.
