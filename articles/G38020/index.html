Sanatoriums in Vityazevo: overview, services, addresses and reviews

Sometimes it is so important to relax in a quiet and comfortable place where not only the body, but also the human soul can relax. The village of Vityazevo is a unique place in which inimitable nature and interesting entertainments are combined. Rest at the resort is shown to people who want to relax from the hustle and bustle of the city. You can stay in one of the resorts in Vityazevo.

Vityazevo is the best place to relax

The resort is located on the Black Sea coast. On the one hand, tourists will be able to soak up the sun and swim in the clear sea waters, and on the other hand, they will be able to improve their health with the help of therapeutic muds located on the estuary. Due to the interaction of the two sides, a unique microclimate is created, thanks to which the resort never feels stuffiness and heat.

Most of the sanatoriums in Vityazevo are located near silt-sulfide healing mud. In medical institutions, patients are prescribed the intake of mineral waters, sea baths, visits to salt rooms and much more.

It should be noted that Vityazevo is located just twenty kilometers from the famous resort of Anapa.

Sanatorium treatment profiles

Tourists have the opportunity to stay in the sanatoriums of Vityazevo with treatment. A wide profile allows you to get rid of a huge number of diseases, as well as to prevent various diseases. Sanatorium complexes treat the following diseases:

  • ENT organs;
  • skin, gynecological diseases;
  • diseases of the nervous, musculoskeletal and cardiovascular systems;
  • disturbances in the functioning of the respiratory and digestive organs.

In addition, the sanatoriums provide pediatric services. Highly qualified specialists work in health-improving complexes who are looking for an individual approach to each patient. Upon arrival at the sanatorium, the therapist examines the vacationer and prescribes the complex treatment of a certain disease or the complete recovery of the body. If necessary, the doctor appoints a consultation of a specialist.

Best resorts in Vityazevo

There are many places where you can stay in Vityazevo, but sanatoriums are considered the most popular. This is due to the fact that paying for accommodation, visitors also receive a set of services, which includes a program of rehabilitation and even treatment of existing diseases. Every tourist will like the rest in the Vityazevo sanatoriums: the high quality of services, comfortable accommodation on the seashore, a set of procedures aimed at improving health - all this is guaranteed to be received by the guest of the institution.

There is a rating of the best health-improving complexes of the resort, among which the leading place is the Aquamarine sanatorium. The three leaders also include the sanatoriums Vityaz and Dune.

Sanatorium "Aquamarine"

The resort has a large number of health-improving complexes. One of the most comfortable and popular institutions is the sanatorium "Aquamarine". In addition to providing a wide range of medical services, employees of the institution are trying to maximize the conditions for visitors to stay in the complex. The guests of the sanatorium have the opportunity to visit massage, physiotherapy, dental offices, salt grottoes, gyms for physiotherapy, phyto-bars and inhalations, as well as mud treatment rooms, showers, solariums and much more.

The main medical services provided by the health-improving complex are:

  • all types of diagnostics: laboratory, gynecological, ultrasound, functional, otolaryngological, ophthalmological and urological;
  • SPA treatments and massage;
  • paraffin ozoceritotherapy;
  • individually designed wellness programs;
  • taking oxygen cocktails;
  • halotherapy.

Treatment of children is allowed from the age of four. By purchasing a spa card, the vacationer gets the opportunity to conduct a complete diagnosis of the body and undergo treatment as prescribed by the doctor. Some services are paid extra.

Sanatorium "Vityaz"

Among the leading sanatoriums in Vityazevo, the Vityaz health-improving complex is distinguished. The advantages of the institution are the availability of its own sandy beach, free services on the coast (showers, trampolines, sunbeds, a toilet, etc.), the availability of private pools and a water park, as well as animation programs, karaoke, discos and concerts. The complex has a night club.

Treatment of children is carried out from the age of three years.

The specialists of the complex are involved in the treatment of gynecological, cardiovascular diseases, as well as diseases of the ENT organs, respiratory, digestive and skin diseases.

Visitors to the sanatorium can use the services of an infrared and Finnish sauna, massage room, solarium and beauty salon.

Fans of outdoor activities are given the opportunity to do water aerobics, yoga, Nordic walking and darts. In the resort you can visit the basketball, volleyball courts, gym and tennis court, play futsal and billiards.

Children's health-improving complexes

The resort management has provided recreational facilities for children. One of the most popular medical institutions is the Globus sanatorium in Vityazevo. This is a comfortable complex that provides a lot of services and opportunities for an unforgettable vacation for the child. On the territory of the sanatorium there are cottages in which children live, as well as libraries, sports and game complexes, gazebos, facilities for holding circle classes, various venues (volleyball, football, basketball) and much more.

Children's sanatorium in Vityazevo is located at a distance of 200 meters from the beach. In addition to a variety of entertainment programs, groups can go on one of the excursions, for example, to the dolphinarium, bowling, water park, mud volcano, waterfalls, observation decks, knightly tournaments and horseback riding.

The activities of the sanatorium are aimed at improving the work of the respiratory system, nervous system, musculoskeletal system, as well as the treatment of diseases in the ophthalmological, otolaryngological, endocrine and immunological areas.

Resort Reviews

Visitors to the Vityazevo resort are definitely satisfied with their vacation. Among the reviews you can rarely find negative impressions about the rest, because the village provides a lot of opportunities, the main of which is the complete healing of the body. At the resort you can find active entertainment and relaxing, exciting excursions and relaxing treatments. In addition, a vacation in Vityazevo is perfect for families with small children (many institutions check in children from 1.5 years old).
