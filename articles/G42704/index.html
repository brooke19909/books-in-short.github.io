Stockholm Arlanda Airport: how to get to the city center?

Stockholm is Sweden's largest maritime city. It is visited annually by millions of tourists. Almost every Petersburger with a Schengen visa has ever been to this northern Scandinavian capital.

The first information about this wonderful city appeared in the middle of the thirteenth century. The territory developed very quickly and became one of the most popular and respectable settlements in Europe.

Not everyone knows that Stockholm is the place where famous corporations are based. Among them are Swedbank and H&M. And the Nobel Prize committee since the beginning of the twentieth century sits here.

In addition, Stockholm is full of attractions. By right, this settlement is often called the capital of all of Scandinavia and the pearl of Europe. By the way, local residents like to make fun of the fact that the city is mostly composed of water. Indeed, many cruise liners arrive and sail to the Baltic Sea daily. Due to this, residents of central Russia can visit Sweden inexpensively, because huge ships are constantly plying from St. Petersburg and several cities in Finland, and the price for such a trip is quite affordable.

In addition, you can get to Stockholm by plane. There are several airports in the city, but all of them are far away from the village. The largest of these is Arlanda Airport. In this article, you will learn about Stockholm Arlanda Airport and how to get to the city in the most convenient way.

Arlanda Airport

The airport appeared in the region in the mid-twentieth century and was initially used to train pilots. By the early sixties, Arlanda began to take flights, and in 1962 there was a large-scale opening. By the way, the name was awarded as a result of the competition.

The terminal is the largest in all of Sweden. Interestingly, all airports are very far from the village. Stockholm Arlanda Airport is located forty kilometers from the city. More than eighty airlines from all over the world fly out of it. Compared to other airports in Europe, it can be called medium in size. There are thirty boarding gates, as well as ninety terminals for check-in before the flight. Of course, in addition to everything, there is also Duty Free, where passengers waiting for their flight can enjoy shopping. If you need to apply for Tax-Free, then this is also done here. You can always have a bite to eat at the airport; a large food court is located on its territory.

How to get to the city center from Stockholm Arlanda Airport?

This question is asked by almost every plane descending from the ramp. There are several ways to get from Stockholm Arlanda Airport. We will tell you about all in this article.

Arlanda Express - The Fastest Way

The easiest and quickest way to get from Stockholm Arlanda Airport to the city center is by high-speed train. Of course, this method is very expensive, but many of us do not like to lose time on the road. The express runs almost every fifteen minutes, and the journey takes about half an hour. The cars are very comfortable and clean, and the seats are soft. The cost of the trip is approximately - 260 SEK. Tickets can be bought in advance online or upon arrival at the place of departure.

Express stops right at the airport. You just need to go down the elevator and you will find yourself on the platform. If there is a need for a return ticket, then you can buy it right away and save money. The total price is 490 SEK.

For students there is a discount of 50%. Group trips also have a discount.

Bus is the budget option

You can get from Arlanda Airport to Stockholm using the Flugbussarna bus. This method is much cheaper. Flights also run every fifteen minutes. The vehicle is easy to recognize. It is painted gray, and the inscription on it stands out very much. Travel time from the airport is more than 40 minutes.

The cost of the trip for adults will be 200 Swedish kronor in two directions, and for citizens from 18 to 26 years of age 150 kroons, which is much more profitable than express, but longer in time. Tickets are valid for a month after their purchase.

Suburban train

You can head to the center of Stockholm using a regular commuter train. The station is located between the fourth and fifth terminal. Travel time to the city is fifty minutes, and the cost of the trip at the moment is about 140 SEK, about 14 euros, and this is only one way.

Rent a car

As in any city, you can rent a car at Stockholm Arlanda Airport. Cost varies greatly. It depends on many factors. For example, gearboxes or vehicle models.

Comfortable taxi

There is a taxi in almost every airport in the world, and Stockholm-Arlanda Airport is no exception. You can go to any point in a comfortable taxi. The price, of course, depends on the holidays and weekends, but the usual price is 500-700 SEK.

How to get to Arlanda airport from Stockholm?

First you need to get to Stockholm Central Station, and then use the high-speed or local train. Alternatively, you can take a bus ride, which we told you about above.

Where to go from Arlanda airport besides Stockholm?

Since the airport is located very far from the city, it is absolutely logical that other settlements can be nearby. This can be done with a commuter train that stops at the Arlanda C platform and city buses.

If you want to get to the city of Uppsala, then you can use the bus number 801, which runs once an hour. He departs from the fifth terminal of the airport. Cost - 140 SEK. Travel time is usually half an hour.

You can also take a ride on a train that stops between the fourth and fifth terminals. Travel time is usually half an hour or fifty minutes. It all depends on the class of train.
