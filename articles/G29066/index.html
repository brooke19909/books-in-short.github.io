Bead Flower Weaving Pattern: Original Pendant

What a fashionista does not dream of putting on new jewelry every day, choosing them according to her mood or depending on the color scheme of her handbag or shoes. Unfortunately, many can only dream up on this topic. Those ladies who know what beadwork has long found a way out of this situation. It turns out that from beautiful, colorful stones you can weave almost anything. For example, the original pendant. The scheme of weaving a flower from beads is quite simple, so even a novice fashionista can master this technique.





Material

To make a pendant, you will need to purchase pearls, crystals and beads. Separately, you need to make a necklace of beads, for it you will need a lock, which can be purchased in the jewelry department.

The scheme of weaving a flower from beads

1. First, you should weave a circle of beads, stringing alternately a large and small bead. All you need to use 12 beads. On each side you should leave the ends of the thread 15 centimeters, then pass one end through two beads back, fixing the circle.

2. Now prepare the crystal and a small bead. Pass one end of the thread through the crystal, then through the small bead we will return the thread to the crystal. We release the thread in a circle through two beads forward. Fix the craft. Repeat this procedure 5 times to get 5 petals. The pattern of weaving a flower from beads at this stage should be completed on the outer row in order to continue work.

3. This series is simple. You need to insert a pearl between the small beads. We repeat this procedure for the whole circle, passing through the thread alternately pearls and a small bead. At the end, we pass a few more times around the circle through the beads and pearls to firmly fix the craft. If the pattern of weaving a flower from beads is made correctly, you should get a beautiful shape resembling a violet.

4. To make the product look complete, insert the pearl in the heart of the pendant, fixing the thread. Now your original pendant is ready.





Necklace weaving pattern

Now, so that our jewelry can be worn, you should hang it on a necklace. Here you can use various options. Consider one of them. Take a thread sufficient for a necklace, pull it through one small bead at the outer circle of the necklace, fix it and begin to string the same pattern on both sides of a combination of two small beads, one large bead and one pearl.

At the end of each tail, fix the last beads by stretching them through the last two stones. At the end, attach the lock - and your necklace with the pendant is ready.

Conclusion

Learning how to make simple crafts, you can go further by creating whole bouquets of beads. Try weaving a bracelet, pendants or earrings to your set. Let your imagination endless possibilities so that beautiful and fashionable bouquets of beads come to life in your hands all year round, patterns of which you can come up with yourself, based on typical beadwork techniques. Wear your jewelry with pleasure.
