Hotel Club Seatime 3 * (Turkey). Photos and reviews of tourists

Turkey is famous all over the world for its gorgeous beaches, wonderful hotels, delicious food and excellent service. Most Russian tourists are accustomed to rest in Turkey only in the five and four hotels. But the "C grade" is quite good, offering high-level service. One of such inexpensive, but quite decent hotels is Club Seatime 3 stars. Here they are deliciously fed, wonderfully entertained, and offer many interesting and useful leisure options. But first things first.



Location

The 3-star Club Seatime Hotel is 2 km from the small Turkish village of Konakli, across the road from the sea. It is 17 km from Alanya, and from the airport in Antalya, where almost all international planes arrive, including flights from Russia - 95 km, or about 1.5 hours by transfer. Comfortable buses provide travel agencies that provide trips to this hotel. You have to spend so much time on such a short distance, because tourists are brought to the hotels closest to the airport along the road. There is an excellent wide road near the hotel, minibuses stop at which you can get to Alanya and the nearest villages. Having crossed this road (on a safe crossing), vacationers get to the sea.

The whole territory is surrounded by greenery and flowers. The main entrance to the hotel is made in the form of a castle tower, which immediately sets up something fabulous and pleasant.

Infrastructure

Hotel Club Seatime 3 stars meets its guests in a cozy lobby, where comfortable lounge chairs are installed, the lobby is open. The hotel reception is open around the clock. Here you can take things to the locker room, order a taxi, an excursion, call a doctor, rent a car, put valuables in the safe for saving, order the services of a professional photographer, hand things in the laundry, and solve all pressing issues with the hotel manager Mustafa. Hotel Club Sea Time (Club Seatime) 3 stars began its work in 1998. 17 years is not such a long experience, but in 2011 there was a reconstruction. The hotel infrastructure includes a housing stock, swimming pools, two restaurants, a poolside bar, a beach bar, a private beach, a sauna, a Turkish bath, a shop, sports fields - volleyball on the beach, a tennis court on site, a five-a-side football field and a playground for playing basketball.



Rooms

To accommodate tourists 130 rooms provides Club Seatime 3 *. Reviews about them are very controversial. There are tourists who are sympathetic to the fact that the hotel has only three stars, and therefore the furnishings in the rooms are in the corresponding category. These tourists are happy with everything. Along with this, there are those who believe that the number of rooms of Club Seatime 3 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya, Konakli) is in extremely poor condition. Room Types:

1. Standard. The area declared on the official website of the hotel is 24-28 squares, but there are apartments with a much smaller area. Also, not all rooms have balconies. Equipment - beds, bedside tables, wardrobe, table, chairs, air conditioning (not in all rooms), TV (there are 2 channels in Russian), a hygiene room in which there is a toilet, shower and wash basin. Personal hygiene products (toilet paper, shower gel, shampoo) are available upon request. There are no refrigerators in the rooms.

The Standards Fund has 2 rooms for disabled guests. There are 114 units of rooms of this category in the hotel.

2. Family run. There are 16 units in this category. Area up to 48 squares. The rooms are two-room with an internal door. The equipment is the same as in the "standards", the difference lies in the number of beds, which are 1 large double and 2 single. That is, the room is designed for 4 vacationers.

If necessary, a folding bed can be installed in each room.

The view from the windows of the rooms on the sea (50% of the fund) and on the territory of the hotel. Cleaning and change of towels, linen is made once a week.

Beach

Turkey is famous for its excellent sea and wonderful coast. Hotel Club Seatime 3 stars also provides vacationers with an excellent beach holiday. The hotel's private beach is sandy, about one hundred and fifty meters long. You can get on it by going through the underpass across the road located near the hotel. The sea in this place of the coast is clean and warm. The approach to it is gentle, but pebble. At the bottom near the edge of the shore there are stones. On the beach there is a bar from the hotel, animation is carried out. There is an opportunity to ride a "banana", water skiing, jet ski. To protect from the sun, shadow canopies and umbrellas are installed, sunbeds are issued free of charge. There is one small nuance - sunbeds need to be occupied in advance, since they are not enough for everyone.

The Club Seatime 3 * hotel pays great attention to children. Here for kids there is a small pool, a playground, a kids club, where the teacher is engaged with them. In the daytime for children, competitions and games near the pool, aerobics, fun competitions are held. In the evening, ‚Äúdisco‚Äù dances are arranged for the kids, cartoons are shown.

Children from any age are admitted to the hotel. Children under 6 years old will not be charged unless a separate bed is provided.



Adult leisure at the hotel

Regarding the entertainment organized by the Club Seatime 3 * hotel, the reviews are almost unanimous. Animation is on top here. In the afternoon it is aerobics, fun exercises by the pool, multiple contests, quizzes, sporting events. There is an incendiary animation on the beach, so it‚Äôs simply impossible to get bored there. In the evening, exciting shows are arranged for vacationers, for example, dancing with lights, foam parties, striptease. Every Friday there is a Turkish Night show. Sports enthusiasts can play table and tennis, billiards, darts, volleyball, water polo and much more. There is a disco on the beach in the evening. Admission to it is free, drinks for the money.

Leisure outside the hotel

It is difficult to be in Turkey and not to leave your entire vacation to take a look at the unique sights of this country with at least one eye, especially while relaxing in an area rich in its historical past, such as Alanya. In order to diversify the leisure of the guests as much as possible, transfers to discos from the Club Seatime 3 * hotel are organized for them every other day. Alanya Center (Alanya Center) is interesting to visit at any time of the day. In the evening there are bars, restaurants, discos and night clubs, in the afternoon - magnificent shopping centers and unique attractions. Excursions to the most interesting places (Pirate Caves, Maiden Castle, Suleymaniye Mosque and others) can be carried out independently or by taking sightseeing tours. A 6-hour sailing trip on the Starcraft yacht, with a visit to Cleopatra's beach, several caves, and a great entertainment program will be exciting and unforgettable. For hotel guests to get to Alanya on their own is also not a problem, since minibuses stop in front of the main entrance. Travel time is about 10-15 minutes.
