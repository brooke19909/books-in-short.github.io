Southern neighbors: how many kilometers are from Krasnodar to Rostov?

Russia is one of the coldest countries in the world, but here you can hide from the weather at the resorts in the south of the country. The Black and Azov Sea coasts are the favorite tourist routes of most Russians. Recently, the number of tourists who prefer the Cote d'Azur vacation on the Black Sea has been increasing. The main resort areas of the country include the Krasnodar Territory, Rostov Region, Astrakhan Region and the Republic of Dagestan. The statistics of 2016 showed that most of the domestic tourists-Russians chose to relax in this way. Therefore, for trips to the sea with comfort it is very useful to know how many kilometers are from Krasnodar to Rostov.

Krasnodar



In terms of climate, Krasnodar is a very comfortable city for life. The average temperature here is 25 ¬∞ C in summer and 1.2 ¬∞ C in winter. But the average temperature does not at all confirm that a temperate climate reigns in Krasnodar. In fact, on a hot summer day it is impossible to stay out of the shade of trees for a long time, you can even try to fry the fried eggs on hot asphalt! A city with a long history is comfortably located on the right bank of the Kuban. How many kilometers from Krasnodar to Rostov is in this case a secondary question, because from Krasnodar to two seas only 150 kilometers! Each city has its own name, characterizing the purpose for which it was built. Krasnodar is proudly called the "gate of the Caucasus." The city was granted to the Don Cossacks by Catherine the Great.

Rostov-on-Don



The history of the glorious city of Rostov is no less fascinating. It was founded in 1749 by Elizabeth Petrovna. A richer city was developing at tremendous speed, they were actively engaged in trade, and the geographical position contributed to this. Before the October Revolution, Rostov-on-Don was the third city in the Empire in terms of economic development. Rostov played an extremely important role in the Great Patriotic War, it was on its borders that the Red Army replaced the defense with an offensive. The huge historical and cultural wealth of this southern city annually attracts thousands of tourists not only from Russia, but also from all over the world. But the inhabitants of Rostov are not averse to relaxing on the Black Sea coast. Therefore, all residents of Rostov and guests of this beautiful city should find out how many kilometers are from Rostov to Krasnodar.

On a journey to the sea

For a family trip from Krasnodar to Rostov and back, a car trip is perfect. How many kilometers from Krasnodar to Rostov is not so important if you have a good mood and a lot of good music on the road. There are several ways to get to Rostov: using the E-50 Don and M-4 highways or drive along the E-115 and M-4 highways. The first option will take you 3.5 hours on the way, the length of the route is about 285 kilometers, while the second of the proposed routes will require 1 hour longer (distance 350 km).
