Central Pattaya Garden Resort 3 (Pattaya, Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

It's no secret that Thailand is becoming increasingly popular among Russian tourists every year. And most often, travelers prefer to stay in the city of Pattaya. Naturally, there are a lot of hotels and resorts ready to offer their services. And the Central Pattaya Garden Resort 3 is popular. This place has some advantages that distinguish it from all others. So what kind of living conditions await tourists at the hotel?

Hotel Location





To begin with, it is worth noting that the Central Pattaya Garden Hotel 3 hotel complex stands out for its convenient location. It is located in a quiet area in the northern part of the city. The beach can be reached in just 10 minutes along walking paths. The nearest Suvarnabhumi International Airport is just one hour away.

The hotel is located in close proximity to the city center - you can get there even on foot. Nearby are museums, galleries and other attractions, as well as entertainment venues. 30 meters away is the large Big-C shopping center, where you can go to boutiques or dine in the chic restaurant. Nearby is an open market where you will have the opportunity to purchase souvenirs, as well as fresh exotic fruits and other goodies.

Brief description of the territory and infrastructure

The Central Pattaya Garden Resort 3 hotel complex is an interesting combination of ancient traditions, exotic Thai design and modern civilization amenities. In this place you can feel the real home comfort and the mysterious, magical atmosphere of ancient culture.





Central Pattaya Garden Resort Hotel consists of several one-story and two-story buildings located in the middle of an exotic garden. All buildings are built and decorated using exclusively natural materials. Here you will find wooden panels, natural fabrics, wicker garden furniture, wooden sculptures of ancient deities and vivid landscape compositions - in short, everything that will help you immerse yourself in a real fairy tale.
