Equifax: reviews of employees of the American organization

Russians can learn their credit history for free even in a commercial organization. One of the leaders among BKI in the Russian Federation is the Equifax company. The Bureau is not only a major informant for banks and individuals, but also an official employer. Reviews about Equifax allow you to evaluate its attractiveness to potential employees.

About company

The history of the bureau began almost 120 years ago in America, Georgia. Now the Equifax database has more than 293 million customer credit histories. Data is updated according to the results sent from 2 thousand companies.

The bureau works under the terms of contracts that are concluded with organizations. According to the contract, banks, MFIs and other credit organizations are required to transmit information on each client agreement to the bureau.

Based on the collected data, the borrower's credit history is formed. The Equifax bureau can request a certificate from both the banks with which the contract is drawn up, as well as third-party organizations, as well as individuals.

In addition to services for the collection, processing and provision of information about loans, BKI provides analysis and IT support programs. Success of the company:

  1. MigCredit and Gazprombank operate on the basis of the 4Score scoring model.
  2. Equifax is actively collaborating with the State Service online service, where the client can order a certificate of loans and repayments on loans.
  3. The online service "Credit History Online" is one of the best in Russia.

The presence of comfortable conditions is appreciated by all employees. But it is equally important to have additional benefits that make employees value their position and work diligently.

According to American labor practice, a healthy lifestyle has a positive effect on the ability of workers to work. Therefore, in Equifax, according to workers, there is a free fitness room on the ground floor of the office.

Wage level

Attractiveness of conditions is very important, but it is not a priority when looking for work. The salary is of interest to 100% of applicants, therefore, in the reviews about Equifax, future team members are looking for information about wages.

One of the main advantages of the company is 100% official employment. Salaries and bonuses are paid in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation.

The basic salary - up to 99% - is the salary. At the end of the year, employees are awarded a prize based on the results of their work. The most active team members receive 2-5 salaries for the New Year as a reward.

The salary is the national average. In Moscow and the region, according to reviews of Equifax Credit Services, employees receive at least 65 thousand rubles. The salary in other regions is at least 35 thousand rubles.

The company annually produces indexation of wages in connection with rising inflation in the country, so Equifax employees can count on decent wages in any economic situation in the Russian Federation.

Before applying for a job at the Equifax Business Center, all employees are interviewed. It allows you to verify the desire of applicants and test them for professional suitability.

The company does not have strict requirements for education and work experience. According to reviews of Equifax credit bureau, even a student without work experience can become a member of the team.

Since the company is based on the American approach to doing business, a lot of attention in the organization is paid to the personal development of each team member. This is reflected not only in the numerous bonuses (VHI, sports hall, free tea and coffee), but also in the training of employees in English at the office.
