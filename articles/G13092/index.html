About what podcasts are on iPhone

App developers for Apple products are always looking to surprise their users with something. That is why various useful options, programs and functions appear with each release of a new version of the iOs operating system. For example, podcasts, iMessage, Facetime and some others. What are iPhone Podcasts? What are they needed for? What does the user give? There are answers to these and other questions about the application.



What it is?

Initially, podcasts appeared during the development of Internet radio. Literally, this means "recorded message on a specific subject." As soon as the first messages of this kind began to gain momentum, Apple began to implement them in its operating system. So what are iPhone podcasts? This is definitely not a radio! These are recorded audio and video files that are sorted by topic. For example, a podcast called Trance music will definitely contain music tracks in a given direction. At the same time, several podcasts from different authors may exist on the same subject.

So what are iPhone podcasts? Why have they been implemented if there are many different applications with online radio stations? First of all, podcasts are not only music or music videos. This is humor, and business, and creativity, and news. The list goes on and on. Secondly, you can always enable the option "Make available offline."

So, what are podcasts on "iPhone" we have almost figured out. That is, you can simply download your favorite issue and listen to it again and again even where there is no Internet network! Issues and their updates

Everything is very individual here. Some podcasts come out very often (once every few days), others less often. It all depends on those who record these issues and broadcast them on the Internet.
