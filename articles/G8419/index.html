Hotel Galini Apartments Malia 3 *, Greece, about. Crete: description, rooms and reviews of tourists

If you want to have a good rest in Greece, then Galini Apartments Malia 3 * hotel is just perfect for you. Here you can not only enjoy the clear sea, but also find yourself in the epicenter of nightlife.





Brief description of the hotel

Galini Apartments Malia 3 * hotel is a luxurious apartment complex where you can take advantage of a wide range of services. So, you can swim in the outdoor pool with clean fresh water, as well as sunbathe on the sun loungers located on the outdoor terrace. During the day, you can hide from the heat in a shady palm garden. A nice addition will be the availability of free wireless Internet.

As for the rooms Galini Apartments Malia 3 *, they are quite comfortable. Modern design in soft colors makes their atmosphere warm and homely. It provides all the necessary amenities for a full stay and relaxation. You will also appreciate the presence of a furnished balcony from which you can admire the sea or the city's surroundings.

Hotel Galini Apartments Malia 3 * also takes care of the food of guests. So, during the day you will have the opportunity to satisfy your hunger in the snack bar, which presents a huge assortment of original salads and light snacks. Also on site is a restaurant where you can taste delicious local dishes (they can also be ordered directly to your room).





Resort Location

A quite favorable location is characterized by the Malia Galini Apartments 3 * hotel. Malia is a popular resort city, and this hotel is located in its very center, where the main infrastructure facilities are concentrated, as well as all nightlife. Within walking distance is the Minoan Palace, which is considered to be one of the main tourist attractions. As for the distance to the international airport, it is a little over 30 kilometers. about the same you will have to overcome to the famous port of Heraklion.

Hotel Rooms

Quite comfortable living conditions are provided by Malia Galini Apartments 3 * Resort. Crete will seem even more hospitable if you stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • Double and triple studios offer a huge living space and comfortable layout. Also, thanks to the extra sleeping area, even more guests can be accommodated. The bedroom is separated from the sitting area by a bar with a small kitchen. Also, you will certainly appreciate the presence of your own balcony.
  • Family Suite offers you to live in a spacious apartment, which consists of two bedrooms. For the convenience of guests, each of them is equipped with its own bathroom. One of the rooms has access to a private balcony with wicker rattan furniture.
  • Presidential apartments consist of a bedroom and a living room, separated by an internal door. Due to the presence of folding upholstered furniture, up to 4 guests can comfortably accommodate here. Guests will enjoy spending time on the furnished balcony overlooking the sea coast.

Useful information for hotel guests

Some rules governing accommodation and rest are valid at the Malia Galini Apartments 3 * hotel (Malia). Early booking (or early booking) allows you to receive a substantial discount on accommodation. At the same time, guests are obliged to make full or partial prepayment by credit card or electronic payment services.

Arriving in the city, please note that you can check into the hotel only after 14:00. However, subject to availability of rooms and for an additional fee, early registration service will be provided to you. As for eviction, it takes place before noon. For every hour spent in a room in excess of this standard, you will have to pay separately.

It is worth noting that, due to the availability of folding soft sofas and armchairs in the rooms, extra beds are not available. An exception is made only for babies up to two years of age (by the way, a separate fee is not charged for their stay).

General impression

If you want to relax in a quiet homely atmosphere, then this hotel is just perfect for you. Everything here is saturated with the atmosphere of coziness and comfort. The rooms are decorated in pleasant soft colors, and the area is planted with a huge number of flowers and tropical palm trees. Since the distance to the beach is significant, you will certainly be pleased with the opportunity to swim in the clean pool. And of course, an important fact is that the hotel is closed, and therefore it is very convenient to relax with children who constantly strive to escape somewhere. There is a sun terrace, as well as playgrounds and all the necessary equipment for organizing a barbecue.

It is worth noting that the hotel does not work on an all-inclusive basis. The only thing you can count on is breakfast, but they are pretty monotonous, albeit very satisfying. Nevertheless, your apartment will have its own kitchen area with all necessary household appliances, as well as dishes and other utensils. You will have the opportunity to cook food at your discretion by purchasing products in the nearest supermarket or in the market. Also nearby is a huge number of cafes and restaurants where you can eat inexpensively and tasty.

The hotel is located at a distance of about 150 meters from the sea (well-kept urban beach), and therefore the road to it can take from 5 to 10 minutes. Despite the fact that the path goes uphill, it is quite easy to overcome it, because the sidewalk is smooth and asphalted. In addition, your path will run along the rows of beautiful resort hotels. Due to the fact that the hotel is located in the city center, you will find yourself in the midst of tourist life. Also nearby is a huge number of natural, architectural and historical attractions.
