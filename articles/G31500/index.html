Hotel Olive Garden Hotel 3 * (Lardos, Greece): overview, description, reviews, prices

Greece is a country that is loved not only by numerous travelers, but also by the gods in heaven. Not in vain Helios generously endowed this paradise of the earth with sunny weather, which attracts many tourists eager for a gracious rest to this territory.

Land of the Gods

The Greek god Poseidon, endowed with the power to control the elements of water, generously endowed this region with magnificent sea depths with a gentle surf. Aeolus, the god of the wind, brings a light breeze and a romantic atmosphere to the Greek lands. Dionysus is called to closely monitor the plantations of grapes that belong to Attaviros.

Rhodes can certainly be called the largest Dodecanese island. Once upon a time, Helios was considered his patron, because the name of this geographical object now sounds like "island of the Sun." On this magical land there are many hotel complexes. One of the noteworthy is the Olive Garden Hotel in Rhodes (Greece).



Description

This excellent hotel literally attracts tourists with its extraordinary coziness, comfort and warmth reigning inside the atmosphere. Even time here slows down, not stopping vacationers from enjoying pacifying landscapes. Nearby is a wonderful garden, fully planted with various plants and equipped with benches. Very close to the hotel is a modern beach, equipped with changing rooms, rental offices, sun loungers and even its own bar with cocktails and light snacks. If you believe the reviews, this hotel will appeal to any audience - both single older people and full-fledged families with young children and even young couples in love.

Leisure

Of the entertainment, leisurely walks along the island of Rhodes with a view of the fountains and local architecture are very popular. Speni Monastery will appeal to all lovers of religion, and people who love extreme sports can have fun riding a paraglider or windsurfing board.



Location

The airport was built at a distance of 65 kilometers from the Olive Garden Hotel Rhodes 3 * (Greece), at a distance of 2 km is the central part of Lardos. The public transport stop is located just 50 meters from the hotel, and the cost of transport depends on the destination.

Beach vacation

The beach at the hotel is equipped with a convenient descent, rental of sun loungers and sunbeds is carried out for a small fee, the beach itself is strewn with small pebbles. The cost of the relaxation area, consisting of 2 sun loungers and an umbrella, is about 8 euros.

The level of the hotel is 3 stars, the year of construction is 1990. The winter months of 2014 became a time of a complete renovation of the building and hotel grounds.



Rooms

In the room stock of the hotel complex Olive Garden Hotel 3 * ("Olive Garden Hotel") there are 106 rooms of different classes. The hotel occupies 3 floors, between which you can move by elevator. Check-in is available 24 hours a day. The hotel has a large lobby with free access to the World Wide Web.

Nutrition

In terms of food, the complex has 2 restaurants with local and international cuisine. All-inclusive meals are also available, delivered directly to your room. For those guests who do not eat meat, a special vegetarian menu with a variety of dishes prepared using vegetable protein is provided. Breakfast time - from seven to half past nine in the morning, lunch - from half past two to three in the afternoon, and you can have dinner from half past seven to half past nine in the evening.
