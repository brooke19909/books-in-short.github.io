The village of Barda, Perm Territory. History and Attractions

Where is the village of Barda located? Perm region, Bardym region. In the subject there is no village with a large number of inhabitants. The population of Barda is approximately 9 thousand people, and most of them are representatives of the Bashkir and Tatar peoples.

History

The first written evidence of the existence of the village was made in 1630 in the census book. On its pages Krasnoyar and Barda are mentioned. The first mosque in the village was erected in 1750, and 10 years later a school for Muslims was created at it.

This settlement has always been large. According to the census of 1834, the population of Barda exceeded one thousand people. In the middle of the XIX century on the territory of Osinsky uyezd, the Bardym volost was established with its center in the village. Krasnoyarsk. By the beginning of the next century, the village of Barda (Perm Territory) already had 2,600 residents in 528 yards, and in 1924 it became a district center.





Where did the name come from

Barda (Perm Territory) is not only a settlement, but also a river, which is a tributary of Tulva. There are several traditions that tell the story of the name. One of the most famous is On the Floating Geese. Barda is a widespread toponym on geographical maps. This name is available in Altai, Ukraine and Azerbaijan.

Festivities

Goose is a special bird for the local population. It stands for purity, prosperity and prosperity. His image is even on the coat of arms of the district. No wedding table is complete without goose dishes. The rite of "goose help" is held annually before the start of winter. By this event, guests recognize the village.

Every year, the Barda Zien celebration is held in Barda. The celebration gathers thousands of people from the district, different cities of the region and other regions. Barda (Perm Territory) was not always the center of the holiday. Initially, it was celebrated in Krasnoyar on the summer solstice, which falls on June 22.

After the establishment of the Bardym region, Barda Zien began to be organized on the river bank in a specially equipped area. At this place, residents and guests gather. One of the most interesting shows is horse racing. Check in distance is 4 km. The winners of the competition receive awards from the governor of the region.





sights

In the village of Barda (Perm Territory) there is something to see for guests and tourists. In the middle of the village, a center of culture and recreation was built. It is located at: st. Lenin, 39. Under its roof, national celebrations and festivals are organized. Barda has its own theater. His debut took place in 1918. Later, he was awarded the title of national and exemplary.

An interesting village is Barda. The Perm Territory can be proud of it, because it has its own museum of local lore, which opened in 1974. It presents the diverse life of the Turkic people.

Holiday towels are made by masters of weaving. They combine a rich color palette and interesting patterns of cultural significance. The creations of weavers are the pride of Barda, they are widely represented in the exhibition.

Near the cultural center is Victory Square, on which Lenin stands. Here is a monument in memory of the dead soldiers during the Second World War. At the southern end of the village is Cast Iron Mountain. It offers a magnificent panoramic view of the surroundings. You can see the river and the village of Krasnoyar.
