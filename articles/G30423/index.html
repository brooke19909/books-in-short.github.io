Climatic features

Tourists and guests of Cyprus are also traditionally interested in the issue of the climate features of their chosen resort. After all, acquiring a tour to the Kkaras Hotel 2 * hotel (Ayia Napa), his guest, of course, also hopes for the weather that contributes to a good beach vacation. For Cyprus, the high tourist season lasts from May to September. Its peak occurs in the summer months, when the average air temperature is 33 0 C, and the sea 27 0 C. With the beginning of September, the weather becomes unstable, which causes a decline in the holiday season. During this period, both the average air temperature and the average water temperature are approximately 24 0 . You can swim in the Cypriot resorts until the beginning of October. The sea has not cooled yet and maintains a temperature of 22 0 , and the air still heats up to 21 0 . This period: from early September to early October is the velvet season for Cyprus.





Fans of excursions prefer to buy March and April tours. The sea has not yet warmed up at this time, but it is already possible to sunbathe. At this time, hotels with pools where water is heated are preferred.
