Where to buy bead wire

On the wire make flowers and trees, some types of jewelry. Working with a rigid base is quite suitable for beginners, since it is easier than with a needle, and even more so with a thin fishing line.

Where to buy wire? Which is better? Which one is suitable for this or that thing and how to choose an analogue? All these questions concern the craftswomen, and we will try to answer them.



We search and buy

The question of where to buy bead wire in the shops of your city is best asked at specialized forums for needlewomen. You can ask in the social network.

In the store where you buy beads, they may also know the answer. Or offer to bring the right product to order. Or maybe you just never noticed him in the window.

Hard floristic, which is suitable for flower stems, you will find in stores for florists. Take an interest in the flower pavilion, where they can buy wire. In some flower pavilions and shops you will even be sold the necessary materials, however, it will come out more expensive.

Copper wire of the most various thickness and rigidity can be reeled up from old transformers. Here you need to go for old parts to the amateur radio market or gut the faulty home appliances. True, in transformers, the wire is sometimes charred and blackened, which will immediately be visible on the product. Some craftswomen "extract" the necessary material from a new wire with a copper core, it can be purchased on any construction market.

Unpainted copper wire is visible on the folds and shines through light beads, so it is not suitable for all things. Some craftswomen solve this problem with nail polish, but if you wish, you can find a special wire for weaving any color.



It is best to buy materials in online stores. Ask your favorite search engine where to buy copper wire or silver, and many local and foreign sites will be presented to choose from. The cheapest materials are usually in Chinese stores. Moreover, silver is also cheap.

What is it like

Silver wire will allow you to become a real jeweler, but you should train with cheaper materials. That is - on copper. Copper wire for beadwork is of different sections. Stiffness depends on the thickness, which means ease of use, but not always. If the manufacturer used special alloys, the metal will be soft enough at any thickness. But, as a rule, the thicker your base for weaving, the harder it is.



Where you are going to buy wire, it would be nice to contact the seller and ask about its characteristics. The fact is that for some things you still need a very rigid base that will not deform in the sock.

The most suitable wire is a cross section from 0.3 to 0.4 mm. From it make the petals of flowers and trees, jewelry. More plastic, hanging down elements are made of wire with a cross section of 0.2 mm, and with a thickness of 0.8 or more, you can make parts that hold their shape well (flower stems, hooks for earrings).

Many places where you can buy wire can be colored, and it is from it that you will get the most beautiful things. The color base sets off the beads, silver is suitable for multi-color compositions and light beads.
