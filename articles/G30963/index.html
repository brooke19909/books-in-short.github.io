Elton Lake, Volgograd Region: rest and treatment with healing mud

Lake Elton has been known since ancient times, even then people were well aware of its healing properties and regularly used the services of a natural "doctor" in order to improve their health. At the beginning of the XX century, a sanatorium was founded here. This is a huge salt lake, comparable only to the Israeli Dead Sea, surrounded by the natural landscapes of the desert steppe. Silt mud, brine and mineral drinking springs are valuable healing resources.

Where is Lake Elton

This is the eastern part of the Volgograd region, located almost on the border with Kazakhstan, in the Volga steppes. The depth of the lake is very small, in summer - no more than 7 cm, in spring it can reach several meters. The area is 152 square meters. m. A large circle of mirror surface, to which several meters you need to go along the dried salty crust. The required lake level supports 7 rivers. At the bottom there are exits of salty springs. The lake is located 18 meters below sea level.





It‚Äôs not suitable for swimming, as it is filled not with water, but with brine - an oily liquid that tastes bitter and salty. It is oversaturated with a solution of sodium chloride, as well as macro- and microelements. Elton Lake is decorated with another attraction - Mount Ulagan, which is an ever-growing salt dome.

Another interesting feature of the reservoir is that the lake is not dead, algae live in the water, which give it a pink hue.

Climatic features of the region

If you are going to relax on Lake Elton or heal in a sanatorium, be sure to bring sunblock creams and oil. The sun is burning, almost instantly burns both the face and other exposed areas of the body. The wind almost does not stop, so dark glasses will be a useful thing. They will solve two problems at once: they will protect from the bright sun and dust transferred by the wind. The temperature difference during the day and night is very noticeable, therefore, going for a long excursion, stock up on warm clothes, it will come in handy if you return in the evening.

The heat here is difficult to bear, so when leaving the hotel room, take a hat and a supply of drinking water with you. If you suffer from heart disease, then choose spring or fall when the weather is milder.

Today the resort operates 12 months a year, the average air temperature here in the winter months is -11 degrees.





For lovers of new experiences

The natural beauties here are specific, you need to get used to them, especially the Russian person who likes the sound of the wind in the treetops and the singing of birds. Here you will not meet either one or the other. As already mentioned, the neighborhood is a bare steppe, in which the pinkish lake is located. It is not recommended to drive close by car because of the salty mud in which the wheels skid, and a large amount of salt badly spoils the metal and rubber. On a sunny day, the eyes are blinding like snow, and only in the evening, when the sunset colors Lake Elton in magical overflows of red, you can appreciate the beauty of these places.

This is the real kingdom of salt. It frames the lake in the form of a wide variety of crystals. They resemble either snow flakes or fanciful snowflakes: with sharp needles, oval, cubic. It makes sense to walk around with a camera and look for a more interesting shot. Salt gets stuck in the hair, it is felt on the entire surface of the skin, settles on the lips ... The wind wears its smallest particles constantly, which makes it seem that it is here that you can taste the air.

Magic mud and brine

Treatment on Lake Elton every year are hundreds of comers. The procedures bring significant relief, and subsequent therapy will give an even more tangible result. Therefore, in chronic diseases, people practice annual treatment at a local sanatorium.

High-salt silt, sulfide, bromine mud is a homogeneous, oily mass, slightly resembling solid oil. It is also laundered not easily, it smells of hydrogen sulfide. It is because of this amount of impurities that Elton Salt Lake is no longer used for the extraction of edible salt. Thanks to this, it has retained its original appearance and today can be a place of healing for many people.

Road to elton

You can get to Volgograd by train, car, fly by plane. Options - for every taste and budget. In the city you can take a bicycle or continue your journey by car. It is easy to get lost in the endless steppes, therefore it is better to adhere to the already known route. One of these is the path from Volgograd through the village of Volzhsky and Leninsk, but a poor steppe road leads to the lake. The second route is longer, it goes through Volzhsky, Nikolaevsk, Pallasovka, but along almost the entire route it is an asphalt road. Feel free to get ready for a trip to Lake Elton! Any local resident will tell you how to get there, even if you lose your way.

Nature Park Tours

One of the most popular services is bike rental, where you can take a tour of the surroundings. There is something to see here. The excursion usually begins with the lake itself. After listening to stories of miraculous healings, tourists head to Ulagan Mountain. This dome is unique not only in that it consists of salt, its height is fully consistent with the basin of the lake. But that's not all! Rocks come to the surface here, the history of which begins from the Jurassic period, often with preserved samples of the plant and animal world. The fossils of ancient mollusks can be found here on the surface, without excavation. This mountain is also interesting for its stunning views of the entire reserve.

If you are interested in geology, then you can visit the Grand Canyon, which is located next to Ulagan Mountain. But usually a guided tour takes place around the lake. An interesting cultural monument is the abandoned salt-mining village, Old Elton, which is located on the west bank. Here you will be told about the hard work of the villagers, who were supposed to get and load the precious goods, protect themselves from nomad raids and deliver salt unscathed through a difficult section of the steppe. An outpost was created here, which served as a fortress protecting people.

From here, the path runs north along the channel of the Khara River. Places are enchanting, with a completely unusual landscape. Even the plants here are unusual, adapted to salt soil and mineral water. Maybe that is why there are so many rare species of trees and grasses that are not found anywhere else. Surprisingly, a huge number of different types of ducks gather on this salty river, even gray cranes fly here.





A mandatory attribute of the tour is a visit to the Devil's Bridge. He is no longer there, he existed during the time of active salt mining on the lake, almost 400 years ago. Now only the ruins of a stone dam have been preserved here. Further, the route runs along the channel of the Hara to the Botanical beam. It will be interesting to connoisseurs and lovers of botany, but for the uninitiated tourist it‚Äôs just bushes and grass. About 400 plant species grow here. Among them you can find trees - apple trees and buckthorn; shrubs - thorns, rose hips, blackberries. Interesting rare species located on the slopes. These are tulips and irises, almonds, asparagus, valerian, tansy, tarragon.

If you are lucky, you can capture on the photo insects listed in the Red Book. This is a long-legged crawler, ascalaf colorful.

A walk under the hot sun makes itself felt, and tourists move towards the long-awaited Smorogdinsky mineral spring. The composition of the water resembles the water of famous sources in Kislovodsk, although the taste may not be liked out of habit.

Here, culture lovers can leave their bikes and go on a trip to the ethnographic museum. It is unique in that it was created and served by one person, a history teacher. These are four buildings located nearby. The first tells about Russian life. This is a hut, cleaned according to all traditional canons. Nearby is a Kazakh yurt, demonstrating the life of a nomadic people. The second yurt is a dining area, here, by your order, a real dastarkhan with beshbarmak, baursaks and delicious tea is covered. The fourth house is a museum of salt production.

The museum‚Äôs creator, historian, who knows and loves his land, leads the tour. He will tell you everything you want to know about Lake Elton. Volgograd region is the real passion of this enthusiastic person.
