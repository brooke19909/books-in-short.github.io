Volgograd is a hero city, one of the most beautiful cities in the country. Remoteness from the central part of the country did not affect the real estate market. Residential complex "Pioneer" (Volgograd). about the project

Seven four-story multi-section buildings, as well as its own boiler and transformer. All this is a residential complex "Pioneer" (Volgograd). It was commissioned in 2016, so today you can give it an objective assessment.

The construction was carried out using expanded clay concrete blocks 200 mm thick, modern materials were used for additional heat and sound insulation. The facades are decorated with decorative plaster, giving them a certain originality and sophistication.

Features

"Pioneer" - a residential complex (Volgograd) that meets all modern requirements. It is provided with all necessary utilities. Own autonomous gas boiler room not only provides a comfortable microclimate in each apartment, but also the opportunity to significantly save on utility bills.





For each house of the residential complex "Pioneer" (Volgograd), an individual color scheme is provided. The project turned out to be bright, life-affirming, just looking at it instantly raises the mood. The complex featured exquisite French balconies with metal fences.

Location

The residential complex "Pioneer" (Volgograd) was built a 5-minute drive from the business center of the city. Today, residents recognize the developed transport network, so that you can get to anywhere in the city both by personal and public transport within half an hour.

The main road along Zhukov Avenue is not overloaded with transport. Traffic congestion is extremely rare, which is important for those who have a private car in their family.

Infrastructure

The residential complex "Pioneer" was built in a developed area with its existing infrastructure. That is why the developer decided to pay more attention to the improvement of the adjacent territory: to lay flower beds and flower beds, organize full-fledged recreation areas, bicycle paths. All car owners got access to spacious parking lots, and in the territory of closed isolated yards there are modern playgrounds and sports grounds that meet all safety requirements.





Residents of the complex use the developed social infrastructure of the district without any problems. There are kindergartens and schools, a polyclinic, a post office, a sports complex, as well as numerous shops and service organizations.
