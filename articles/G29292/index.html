We plan a vacation with children: hotels in Turkey with a water park and a lunapark

Turkey has long been the No. 1 destination for Russian tourists traveling on vacation in the summer. In this country, both nature and people have created ideal conditions for a cloudless vacation with the whole family. The large number of hotels causes a tough but healthy competition for the consumer. That is why hotels include more and more attractive offers in their package of services. This is the Ultra All Inclusive system, spa treatments, golf courses and the like. Especially in this are luxury hotels. Turkey Hotels Five-star categories, in order to attract lodgers with families, came up with a new “trick”: the moon and water parks on its territory. Since this service is quite new, it is not in every luxury hotel.





For adults and children

Of course, an adult travels to Turkey, firstly, for the sake of the sun and the sea, and secondly, to visit interesting ancient and Byzantine antiquities, take a walk at discos and do shopping. All of the above is not very interesting for children. And if you are going on vacation with your whole family, you need to think that everyone is happy and interesting. This is the whole rest in Turkey - the best hotels, “fours”, and even “three rubles”, can teach us how to be kind to children! When the child is small, it can be taken by picking a children's shovel in the sand. Fortunately in Turkey there are many convenient beaches with a smooth entry into the sea and even more hotels where all conditions are created for such crumbs - from high chairs in restaurants and a special menu to rental strollers. But with age, the child’s life needs increase. Give him carousels, swings, water slides and other attractions. All this is in any more or less large resort. But why pay more? After all, hotels in Turkey with a water park and a lunapark have already included the use of attractions in the price of the ticket. Below you will find a brief overview of the hotels providing such services.





Turkey Hotels with Aegean Sea Waterpark and Lunapark

Oddly enough, in this region, despite the optimal climate for relaxing with schoolchildren in the summer months (it is not so humid here, so the heat is transferred easier), the new service has not yet taken root in hotels. In Marmaris, you can call the "Grand Yazichi Club Turban 5 *", and in Fethiye there are several such hotels. In the Majesty Club Tuan Park 5 * there is not only water slides, but also a real mini-zoo. "Lycia World Oludeniz HV-1" for its young guests offers a whole entertainment town, which is called - "Children's Paradise."
