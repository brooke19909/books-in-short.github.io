Hotel Bali Beach & Village 3 * (Greece, Crete): photos and reviews of tourists

Greece is a country with an ancient history, because at one time it was here that great civilizations arose and developed. Today, it is a true paradise for tourists who want to have fun on the seashore, enjoying the mild climate and the legendary Greek hospitality.

Probably every traveler knows that Crete is one of the most famous and popular islands of Greece. It is this place that many travelers choose as a temporary refuge. Of course, there are many hotels here - for every taste and budget. But when choosing experienced tourists recommend paying attention to the hotel complex Bali Beach Village. What is a resort and why did it become so popular?

Is the location convenient?





To begin with, it's worth figuring out the location of the Bali Beach Village 3 * hotel . Rethymno, namely, the cozy fishing village of Bali - it is here, almost on the seashore, that you will have the opportunity to spend an unforgettable vacation. The hotel is built on a hillside and from almost anywhere you can enjoy views of the bay surrounded by mountains.

This is a calm resort place - there are no noisy parties and nightclubs, so fans of a relaxing holiday mostly come here. Nevertheless, the town has shops, supermarkets and cafes, in a word, the infrastructure is quite developed. In 15 minutes you can walk to the bus stop, and from there - to almost anywhere in the island.

The center of lively Rethymnon is 27 km away. The capital of Heraklion is approximately 45 km away. There is also an airport here - you can get to the hotel in 30 - 40 minutes. The hotel and travel agencies provide customers with a shuttle service, so you should not worry about moving.

Hotel Bali Beach Village 3 * (Greece, Crete): features of the territory





The hotel consists of two multi-storey residential buildings. Beach Building is located almost on the beach itself. By the way, it was built in 1980. And here is the second part of the complex, namely Bali Village located on the top of the hill - a convenient stone staircase leads upstairs, and the view from there opens up wonderful. By the way, this building was built in 1996. In 2007, a complete reconstruction was carried out, which included not only repair, but also the replacement of all equipment.

The hotel area is very beautiful - everywhere there are comfortable walkways, terraces and a swimming pool, and everything is generously decorated with greenery and bright Mediterranean colors.

Can I eat at the hotel?





What food can a guest at a Bali Beach Village 3 * hotel count on? Greece, after all, is famous for its cuisine and it is quite natural that during a vacation tourists want to enjoy all its charms.

The hotel complex operates on an all-inclusive system that is advantageous for travelers. Meals are held in the hotel restaurant (it is common for both buildings). Here you will find a relaxed atmosphere, attentive waiters and, of course, delicious dishes from a talented chef. In the reviews, travelers confirm that the menu is varied and the food is delicious. You will be offered light snacks, meat, fish and seafood dishes, side dishes, salads and fresh fruits. By the way, every day guests indulge in delicious homemade ice cream and pastries.

You can also visit the bar in the lobby or by the pool. Tourists who have paid all inclusive can get locally made snacks and drinks for free (they say they serve delicious wines). There is also a bar on the beach, but you have to pay the bill.

Holidays by the sea: distance, entertainment and amenities





As already mentioned, the Bali Beach Village hotel complex is located on the seafront - you just need to go down the stairs to it. The beach here is private, so there are a lot of people only during periods of maximum settlement. The bay is protected from the winds, so the water here is always calm, clean and warm. The coast is covered with soft, clean sand, and the depth at the entrance to the sea increases gradually.

You need to pay for the use of sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas, but such a pleasure is not too much. As for entertainment, tourists here are swimming with a mask, ride boats and boats, in a word, have fun and have a good time.

Hotel Bali Beach Village 3 * (Crete): additional services





At the hotel you can count on some additional amenities. It is worth noting that at the reception desk one of the employees stays even at night, so at any time you can chat with the staff, get information or report a problem. By the way, employees are happy to help organize a transfer, order a taxi, book tickets.

Ample free parking is available near the hotel. By the way, despite the modest size of the town, there are several places where you can rent a car, bike or moped (having your own transport opens up opportunities for exploring the island).

There is no wireless Internet connection in the hotel, but there is a computer corner where you can access the World Wide Web, use a laptop or some other office equipment.

What does leisure in the hotel area look like?





The Bali Beach Village hotel complex is mainly intended for tourists who want to relax on the beach. It is full of couples, youth companies and spouses, but they spend most of their time outside the hotel.

On the other hand, you can brighten up your leisure time at the hotel. There are several pools. Some of them, by the way, are built on a slope - bathing in fresh water, you can simultaneously enjoy views of the sea. There is also a separate place for bathing children. There are sun loungers on the terraces, and there is a bar nearby offering a snack. You can have fun playing billiards or table tennis.

And, of course, do not forget about the possibilities of excursion vacations, because Crete is an island shrouded in mysteries of myths and ancient civilizations. There is something to see here. And an interesting sightseeing tour of the surroundings can be picked up both at the hotel and in the city.
