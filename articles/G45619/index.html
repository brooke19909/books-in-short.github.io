Salary advance payment

This type of loan is convenient for those who have a salary card at Halyk Bank and feel the need for a small express loan before salary. The minimum loan amount is 20,000 tenge, the upper threshold is limited to 50% of the salary, but not more than 500,000 tenge. The interest rate is set at a rate of 36%, HESV - from 43.3%. Age requirements: from 25 years to 58 years for women, 63 years - for men. Clients with a salary of at least 70,000 tenge and above are allowed to consider the loan application. To apply for a loan, it is enough to have an identification document with you. Loans of Halyk Bank of Kazakhstan in the form of an advance are repaid automatically after the receipt of wages in the borrower's account.

Emergency loan

Lending on the security of real estate is available both to the categories of clients receiving salaries at Halyk Bank and to other segments. The conditions for all are identical, except for the interest rate, and are as follows:

  • age: from 21 to 65 years old;
  • loan amount: determined by the level of solvency of the borrower and the value of collateral;
  • loan term: from 3 to 120 months;
  • proof of income: required if the requested loan amount exceeds 40% of the market value of the collateral property of the borrower;
  • remuneration rate: for participants in a salary project with a commission - from 15.5% (HESV from 17.6%), without a commission - from 25.5% (HESV - from 28.7%); for other clients with a commission - from 19% (HESV - from 21.5%), without a commission - from 27.0% (HESV - from 21.8%);
  • Down payment: not required;
  • commission for arranging a loan: 2% of the loan amount + 20,000 tenge.

Retirement loan

For pensioners, Halyk Bank offers a cash loan under the following conditions:

  • age: up to 70 years at the time of loan end;
  • loan term: from 9 to 48 months;
  • loan amount: from 150,000 to 4,000,000 tenge;
  • interest rate: with commission - from 20% (HESV - from 27.8%), without commission - from 30% (HESV - from 34.4%);
  • commission for arranging a loan - 8%.

Secured by bank deposit

A loan product targeted at the Halyk Bank depositor segment has a minimum of requirements due to a reliable collateral, the role of which is a deposit with this bank. The maximum loan amount should not exceed 90% of the size of the deposit in the bank. The loan term depends on the term of the savings deposit and is at least 1 month. The interest rate is set depending on the availability of the commission in the amount of the loan. If you include a commission of 1% for crediting credit to the client‚Äôs current account, the interest rate is calculated as follows: interest rate on the deposit + bank margin (from 1.5% to 6%). In case of commission exclusion, the amount of remuneration starts from 27.0% (HESB - from 30.6%)

Loans to the Halyk Bank of Kazakhstan: advantages and disadvantages

The advantages of Halyk Bank loan programs include:

  • a wide range of loan programs for various categories of customers;
  • affordable interest rates: from 1% (HESV from 1%) for participants in salary projects, from 7% (HESV from 25.1%) for the rest of the segment;
  • high maximum loan amount under the unsecured lending program - up to 6,000,000 tenge;
  • minimum package of documents and quick terms for considering a loan application in most cases;
  • significant marginal age of the borrower for lending - up to 70 years;
  • payment by the bank of expenses for personal insurance and collateral programs, with the exception of property insurance of vehicles under the program of preferential car loans.

Among the minuses come to the fore:

  • high fees for arranging a loan: from 2% to 8%;
  • restrictions on early repayment of the loan - there are fees for partial (full) repayment of the loan within the minimum term of use of the loan.

Halyk Bank provides consumer loans to various categories of individuals with the possibility of providing collateral. The terms and conditions of the bank's loan programs are accessible to a wide segment of the population due to reasonable interest rates and moderate requirements for the borrower. It is quite simple to apply for a loan at Halyk Bank of Kazakhstan - you need to contact the nearest bank branch or fill out a client form on the company's website. A bank consultant will help you choose the most suitable option, taking into account the solvency of the applicant, the availability of a Halyk Bank salary card and other factors.
