"Golden Babylon" on Prospect Mira: how to get to the shopping center

The Golden Babylon of Rostokino is a popular shopping and entertainment center in the north-east of Moscow. How to get to this shopping center for free

Almost from the very opening, branded buses with the symbols of a shopping center run from the nearest metro stations. With their help, you can drive to the Golden Babylon for free. How to do this? Free buses leave the Sviblovo and Otradnoe metro stations daily from 11:00 to 22:00 with an interval of 20 minutes. At the Babushkinskaya metro station, such vehicles depart from Yeniseiskaya Street.

How to get to the "Golden Babylon" from VDNH? This can also be done for free on a branded shopping mall bus. It stops between the north and south (old and new) subway exits. All Babylon buses complete their route at the East entrance to the shopping center. They also carry visitors tired of shopping back to the subway stations.

From VDNH metro station to Golden Babylon can be reached in 15 minutes by express bus 903, trolley bus 76, buses 172, 136, 344, 375. The stop is located near the southern entrance to the metro from Kibalchicha Street. Next to the northern (main) subway entrance at VDNKh station you can also take buses 375 and 578. All of these routes stop at Rostokino (Remzavod) station, from which you can quickly reach the shopping center on foot.

This can be done both from the VDNH metro station and from the Babushkinskaya and Medvedkovo stations. At the bus stop in front of the shopping center you can also find trolley bus 14 and bus 93.

How to get to the "Golden Babylon" by car

The mall is located at the address: Moscow, Prospekt Mira, 211.

If you are moving from the region by car, you need to get out onto the Yaroslavl highway (from the MKAD), move off from it to the right on the Severyaninsky bridge. The noticeable shopping center building will be located immediately to your left. Moving along the overpass, you will get to Serebryakova passage, turn in the direction of Amundsen street and turn around in a roundabout, at the traffic light turn left and move along Serebryakova passage in the opposite direction to the entrance to the shopping center (it will be on the right).

If you are heading from the center of Moscow, before reaching the Severyaninskaya flyover, move under it in the direction of Yenisei Street.
