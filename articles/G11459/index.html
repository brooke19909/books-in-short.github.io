Sapphire Hotel Turkey - the jewel of your holiday

The sapphire gem gave its name to the magnificent Turkish hotel, which has already become a familiar resting place for many domestic tourists. Not far from Sapphire there is a large shopping center, a police station and several entertaining nightlife venues - this allows us to call the location of the Sapphire hotel very successful for its residents. Tourists can always leave the complex in order to walk along the promenade or walk through the shopping center without fear of getting lost in the city.

You can drive from the complex to the port of Alanya in twenty minutes, this once again emphasizes the advantageous location of the Sapphira hotel, in the port quarter there is the largest market of the city. There you can buy everything you have long dreamed of, but did not hope to find, much less purchase at such low prices.

In the populated areas that are located near the Sapphire Hotel Turkey, there is a ban on building work during the holiday season. So, unpleasant sounds and noises of jackhammers will not be able to spoil your vacation.

Sapphire is famous for its high level of customer service. The staff speaks English, German and most importantly Russian. You can pay for hotel services in cash and with Visa, MasterCard. Additional services you can pay in three currencies: dollars, euros and rubles.

Unlike many other hotel complexes in Sapphire Hotel Turkey, you can relax with your pets, but there is a restriction on the weight of the animal, your pet should not be heavier than six kilograms.
