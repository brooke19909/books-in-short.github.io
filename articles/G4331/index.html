history of the company

The MMCIS Investments Fund is a private closed-type investment organization that began its existence in 2004. The association is a subsidiary of MMCIS Group. Until 2006, the main goal of the corporation was to professionally manage the capital of the founders. But since 2006, the department received the title of open investment fund. MMCIS investments is officially registered as a private company in Panama. Representative offices of the organization successfully operate in England and France, Israel and Turkey, Italy and Russia, Germany and Ukraine, Bulgaria and Estonia, Kazakhstan and Georgia, Latvia and Norway.
