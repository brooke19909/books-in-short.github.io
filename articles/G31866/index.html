Ruggear mobile phones: specifications, description, reviews

When working days are not in a normal environment, special equipment is needed. The well-known company RugGear makes those things that are useful for a walk in the woods or in any extreme conditions. Of course, in their assortment you can find devices with special protection. One of these models is the RugGear RG150 Traveler phone, and the RG128 Mariner will also be an excellent accessory. The article will be devoted to them.

If we compare these two options with other protected ones, then we can say that both look very commonplace. However, their functions are quite interesting.

RugGear Phone Positioning

For those who like to go climbing and other extreme sports (ATVs, bikes), RugGear phones will become best friends. These devices, after falling into sand, water or on the ground from a relatively high height, can withstand shock, pollution and continue to function, as if nothing had happened. Some manufacturers even thought about creating the so-called "float", a phone that can float. Rather, it is designed for fishermen.

Inexpensive RugGear phones are also suitable for children who are leaving for the camp. In order to prevent an incident on the beach with the main device, you can take a protected model. It should be understood that it is intended mainly for calls and messages. There are no other functions like on smartphones. If they are necessary, then it is better to purchase a special protected gadget for a higher fee.

RugGear phones are sold complete with a charger, a USB cable, a special key in order to remove the bolts from the panel if necessary, and instructions. Judging by the reviews of people, it would be nice if the manufacturer provided a cover for the hand or a carabiner for the belt.

RG128: design, dimensions, controls

The RugGear RG128 Mariner phone has a sufficient level of protection, and it looks very interesting, neat and not tough. Holds well in hand. For headphone jack located on top, there is also a flashlight. Below you can find a place for a USB cable. The connectors are closed with a special plug, and they are attached to the body and tightly insulate the nests so that water or dirt does not get there. On the side of the phone is covered with soft rubber.

The rear panel is fastened with screws, although it already fits perfectly due to the presence of grooves. There is a protective gum that does not let even a drop of water pass. Although it dries out over time, the chance to drown the phone is practically zero. Therefore, there are no problems with tightness. The model comes in black. When reading reviews, it became clear that it would be better if the RugGear phones, which are declared as floaters, were available in red or white housing.

There are no complaints about the assembly of the device, it is really good. The phone supports 2 SIM cards, the connector for which is located directly under the battery. It is possible to use a memory card up to 8 GB.
