Hardware resources of the device

The main component that determines the computing capabilities of any smartphone is the central processor. It is with its parameters that we begin the review. The Alcatel POP C5 5036D smartphone is based on a rather modest chip manufactured by MediaTek - MT6572. This is a 32-bit RISC processor, consisting of 2 cores of the Cortex-A7 architecture. The clock frequency of a given semiconductor crystal can be dynamically changed. In minimum load mode, one of the computing modules is turned off, and the second operates at 300 MHz. In turn, when launching resource-intensive applications, 2 cores operate at once at a frequency of 1.3 GHz. Among 2 crystal chips of this architecture, this is one of the highest rates. The second important component that determines the computing capabilities of the device is a video card. In this case, we are talking about the Mali-400MP2 graphics adapter. It frees up CPU resources for smooth operation of the Alcatel One Touch 5036D. The reviews of the satisfied owners of the described smartphone confirm this once again. Now to summarize. The hardware filling of this smartphone is enough to solve most everyday tasks. Starting from listening to music and surfing the Internet and ending with watching movies and mid-level games, this gadget will cope with everything. The only thing where its hardware platform will not be enough is the most demanding games that have appeared relatively recently.
