In addition to furniture, the rooms also have satellite TV, showing two Russian channels, a refrigerator and manually controlled air conditioning, a direct dial telephone, and a radio. For the use of the built-in small safe, you need to pay at the registration desk two euros per day.

All rooms are decorated in calm pastel colors. A large glass sliding door leads to the balcony, which, if necessary, can be closed with thick curtains. Curtains and bedspreads are designed in the same color scheme. On the floor is ceramic tile, which is washed daily with special disinfectants that repel insects.
