We knit a sleeveless jacket. Children's models

Knit a sleeveless jacket? A good idea! After all, a sleeveless jacket or, as our grandmothers said, a "murderer" is a very important thing in the wardrobe of any person, especially a baby.



There are many different options for knitted vests, but if you want something special, you can knit a sleeveless jacket with knitting needles. This can be a model with a relief or jacquard pattern, made of warm or fine yarn, for every day or for a way out. What you will knit, decide for yourself. And in this article you will get acquainted with the basic techniques and methods by which classic models of sleeveless jackets fit.

Knit a sleeveless jacket for son

For a vest model for a 3-4 year old boy, you will need two skeins of yarn, knitting needles, buttons (2 pcs.) And a little patience.



In order to determine the desired number of loops, take measurements. We measure the circumference of the hips and divide this value in half. The number of loops should match the resulting length. We also need to know the height of the future sleeveless. To do this, measure the distance from the hip to the shoulder seam. And another measure - the distance from the hip to the armhole. We start knitting from the bottom with an elastic band. Having knitted 4-5 cm, in the next row we make uniform additions after every third loop and continue knitting with the selected pattern. When you are tied to the armhole on both sides, you need to perform the reduction as follows. In the first row there are three loops, in the second - two, in the third - one. After you have tied it to the neck, it is necessary to close the middle loops in the center and perform reductions to round the cut. Next, we continue to knit each shoulder separately to the desired height. The shelf is ready. We knit a sleeveless jacket further.

Knitting back

We knit the back like a shelf. The difference is a higher neckline and the length of the left shoulder. We make it 2-3 cm longer, and at the end, do not forget to make a slot (1 or 2) for the fastener.





After both parts are ready, we sew them along the side seams and the right shoulder. Sew a button on the left shoulder of the shelf (1 or 2).

The neck of the vest can be tied with an elastic band on circular knitting needles or with a garter stitch. You can also use crochet posts for crocheting the neckline. Shelf vest can be decorated with thermal sticker or embroider.

A universal version of sleeveless jackets for kids

Another way with which it is easy to make knitted sleeveless jackets for children with knitting needles is suitable even for beginner needlewomen.



It is not much different from the previous one. This model is suitable for very young children. Since, due to her special fastener, she is very easy to put on. The shelf and the back fit in the same way as in the first version. Just do not forget to make both parts a little wider by adding 2-3 cm to your measurements. This is necessary to make the fastener. And you can also knit such a sleeveless jacket without elastic, starting right away with the pattern you have chosen. The photo shows an option for a girl from melange yarn. By choosing a different shade of thread, you can easily make knitted sleeveless jackets for boys.

Thermal stickers or embroidery (applique) can also be used as decoration.



Models with braids look very good, as in the photo. This option is suitable for both girls and boys. Another example is the classic sleeveless jacket, knitted with front stitch. In it, your baby will look very stylish and adult. After all, all children love to imitate and be like their parents. Well, have you already decided on the model? Then we knit a sleeveless jacket and enjoy the result.
