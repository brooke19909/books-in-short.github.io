Vacuum cleaner "Vitek": reviews, photos, instructions

It is undeniable that home cleaning appliances are being improved every year. About 30 years ago, only a bulky bag vacuum cleaner was an alternative to a broom. And now in the store you can buy models with a container, with an aquafilter, washing and even compact robots that themselves clean a given area. Water vacuum cleaners are particularly fond of Russian customers. According to the owners, the air after cleaning with such a device is much cleaner and fresher, because even the finest dust is trapped in the container.

The main disadvantage of such models is the high price. Not every average family can afford to spend 15-20 thousand rubles. on the appliance for cleaning the house. Therefore, among the entire line of vacuum cleaners with an aquafilter, the price and quality are favorably distinguished by products of the Vitek brand.



Consider one of the most popular models - VT-1886 B.

Design and principle of operation

The Vitek VT-1886 B vacuum cleaner is a model with an aquafilter, that is, garbage and large dust passing through a container of water remain at the bottom, and the cleaned air returns to the room.

Description

On the case there are two wide buttons - turning on and winding the cord. They are large enough so that the hostess can press on them with her foot, without bending over during cleaning. Between the buttons is the handle of the power regulator, which allows to reduce the suction force. It is necessary in those cases when it is necessary to clean delicate surfaces, such as curtains, from dust. If you do not use it, thin tissue will have to be pulled out of the brush, risking to get dirty or even tear.

A little further than the buttons is a wide plastic handle. It is quite wide and durable, allows you to move the vacuum cleaner from place to place.





The container with an aquafilter is installed in the vacuum cleaner body and fixed on it with a latch. The tank is made of opaque plastic, which allows you to see the fill level. To remove it, you must press the button of the transfer handle and pull up. To pour water into the container, click off the clips and pull the lid with the filter system attached to it.

Nozzles

The Vitek vacuum cleaner is equipped with five nozzles:

  1. Cleaning brush. This is a standard wide nozzle with a floor / carpet switch. When the button is pressed, the bristle extends, which prevents scratches on a smooth surface (laminate or parquet).
    When switching, the pile is removed, and the nozzle fits snugly to the surface. In this position, it is ideal for cleaning carpets or rugs.
  2. Turbo brush. This nozzle can be used both for cleaning the floor and for cleaning the carpet. The bristles, mounted in a spiral on a cylindrical rod, winds animal hair and long hair, rotating during operation under the influence of an air stream. Turbo brush has a number of limitations during operation. In particular, the nozzle cannot be used on carpets with a long pile (more than 15 mm) or brushed over electric wires.
  3. A small brush. It is equipped with a long soft pile and is ideal for cleaning furniture, smooth, polished surfaces.
  4. Slotted nozzle. Long and thin, it is designed for spot cleaning of inaccessible places, for example, skirting boards or joints of upholstered furniture.
  5. Nozzle for upholstered furniture. This is a small, lint-free brush designed to clean upholstery.

Other accessories

These include a corrugated flexible hose with a handle and an extension tube. The sleeve tip is inserted into the air intake hole until it clicks, disconnected by pressing the button locks. The extension tube allows you to vacuum the floor without bending, and the corners - without stretching and without getting up on tiptoe. It is telescopic (i.e. retractable, not composite) and can be extended by the required distance. The tube is equipped with a holder for nozzles - plastic fasteners, on which you can fix the furniture and small brushes. In this position, they will not be lost and will always be at hand.

Filters

The Vitek vacuum cleaner is equipped not only with water, but also with a number of additional fine filters. These include cyclonic, 2 foam, antifoam and HEPA. The latter is an output filter. It traps the smallest particles of dust that penetrate through water with air bubbles. The HEPA filter is detergent and does not require regular replacement. If the owner plans to vacuum the carpets pre-treated with a cleaning shampoo, the manufacturer recommends adding antifoam to the water.

Vacuum cleaner care

After each cleaning, pour water out of the container to prevent the formation of an unpleasant odor. After this, the tank must be rinsed and wiped dry. Cyclonic, antifoam and foam filters must also be washed with water and dried after each cleaning.

Before work, no more than 500 ml of water is poured into the tank. Overfilling is not allowed. Excess water can penetrate the engine and cause a short circuit.

The HEPA filter is flushed as it becomes dirty, depending on the frequency of use. To clean it, it is necessary to substitute under a stream of water and rinse. Brushes, sponges and detergents are not allowed. Drying the HEPA filter is also necessary in a natural way, without using heating devices. The foam filter in front of it is cleaned in exactly the same way.

The body of the Vitek vacuum cleaner is first cleaned with a soft, damp cloth, and then wiped dry. Washing it with abrasive detergents or immersing in water is prohibited.

Reviews

The low price and high quality are what makes the Vitek vacuum cleaner so attractive to customers. Owner reviews say that over the years, this technique has proven itself perfectly. However, it can not do without complaints.

First of all, they relate to the weight of the unit. Vacuum cleaners with an aquafilter are quite massive and heavy, especially if there is water in the container.





Therefore, despite the large wheels, this model is difficult to roll over the threshold or drag from room to room. However, this problem is typical for all units with a water filter.

Another feature of such models is that they require special care. Filters must be washed regularly, and the container emptied and dried after each cleaning. Accordingly, it will take extra time to disassemble the Vitek vacuum cleaner and then dry it. And the bag vacuum cleaner can simply be pushed into the corner after work.

Among the advantages of this model, the owners note a good suction force, the absence of dust smell after cleaning, a large number of nozzles available in the kit for different types of coatings.

Instruction manual

It includes a description of components and the principle of assembly of various components. The authors paid special attention to the description of the precautions with which the Vitek vacuum cleaner should be operated. The instruction prohibits using the unit without water, with filters not installed or improperly installed, and turning it over during operation. Manufacturers do not recommend vacuuming fine dust, flammable liquids, cigarette butts, matches, cigarettes and collecting water with it. This document also describes in detail which filters should be washed.





Often owners violate the instructions when they carry the Vitek vacuum cleaner. The photo of the model allows you to see that there are 2 handles on the case. For one you need to lift the vacuum cleaner. For another - only a container. The handle of the tank is not designed for the weight of the entire unit, therefore, it can not withstand when lifting and break.

Repairs

Occasionally, owners are faced with breakdowns of this vacuum cleaner. The most common complaint is that the suction power drops during cleaning, the equipment is very hot. As a rule, repair of the Vitek vacuum cleaner in this case is not required. Such a malfunction may be associated with severe contamination of the filters.





In this case, they must be washed as described in the instructions. A drop in power may be due to a breach of tightness: the container with water is incorrectly installed, its lid is not tightly closed, the hose or seal is broken. To determine the place of air leakage, it is enough to run a hand over the body and correct the violations, depending on their nature.

It is much more difficult when the equipment in principle does not turn on. In this case, the Vitek vacuum cleaner can be repaired at a service center or independently, dismantled with a screwdriver. Particular attention should be paid to the buttons. The fact is that they are attached to the case with latches, which can be broken if you squeeze them inaccurately.

The warranty for the vacuum cleaner is 12 months. And the service life, like all similar household appliances for the home, is 5 years.
