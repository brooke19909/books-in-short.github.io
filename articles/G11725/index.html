Majorda

This is the area where the Majorda Beach Resort 5 * hotel is located (Majorda, South Goa). On one side of it is the luxurious Mobor, and on the other, the town of Velsao. There is a fabulous atmosphere of relaxation and exoticism. No wonder most of the tourists who visited Majord fall in love with her, and from then on they want to go exclusively to Goa. An endless beach stretching for almost thirty kilometers, where eleven resort villages pass one into another - and all this is for you. On the beach you will find all kinds of living creatures, which are found here in unimaginable numbers - and gulls, and starfish, and crabs. There are practically no people here, and finding a secluded place is not a problem. The sand is clean and very shallow. How to get to the hotel and its location

Organized tourists are usually met upon arrival and delivered from the airport by transfer. The bus reaches the hotel in about twenty minutes - it is the first among the beach hotels that come across on its way. And this is an important point, especially if you are tired after an eight- or nine-hour flight. Dabolim Airport is about twenty kilometers away. The hotel can also be reached by taxi, and it costs only about five hundred rupees. If you prefer a bus, it will turn out very cheaply, but after a long journey it is better to ride in comfort. The nearest city, where there is a railway station, is ten kilometers away. Near the hotel Majorda Beach Resort 5 * (India) there are many shops selling souvenirs and jewelry (including from Tibet), pharmacies where you can buy herbal medicines, as well as oils, creams, masks and shampoos. Among vacationers, shops selling batik are popular. Very close is the famous clinic of Dr. Om, which tourists highly recommend to each other. All this is due to the fact that the village of Majorda is adjacent to the hotel. Not far from the hotel there are many famous beaches - Gonsua, Arrosim, Kansaulim, Sunset Beach. Territory

Built hotel Majorda Beach Goa 5 * in the "colonial" Portuguese style. Moreover, it is not only decorated in the form of an old fort - in its architecture all the rules of military architecture, such as natural air conditioning, are observed. The main building is built in the shape of a covered courtyard, and flowers and greenery were beautifully placed there. Newer buildings are decorated in different styles. Closest to the sea and in remoteness from other buildings are cottages. The hotel was built in the nineties, so excessive gloss should not be expected from it. But the territory itself is decorated with great taste. There is a beautiful garden with lots of squirrels. Therefore, guests are advised to be careful and close the windows in the rooms so that the animals do not "housekeeping" there during your absence. There are many lawns and gazebos at different levels, as is customary in local culture. In a word, Majorda Beach Resort 5 * (Goa) is a real "paradise" garden. Several times a week, people from a local jewelry factory come to the hotel and offer their vacationers an assortment of silver, gold, and precious stones. Moreover, you can pay for all this not only in cash, but also with a card. Tourists are very pleased with the hotel grounds - the greenery and the unusual soothing aura that surrounds it are pleasant to most.

Excursions

Very rarely, people come to Goa with the intention of staying in a hotel all the time. This country is too colorful, with indescribably beautiful landscapes, and not to see it is simply a sin.
