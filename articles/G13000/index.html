How to make a necklace with your own hands? The master class will help to master a simple needlework technique.



Want to diversify the collection of your accessories? Or maybe you want to pick up unusual beads for your summer outfit? Or none of the options offered in the store suits you? Then try to make beads with your own hands. The master class, which is presented here, will reveal to you the secret of making a summer version of a bead with a flower of five petals. You can pick up beads of any shape and color. It's so easy to make jewelry with your own hands. We have prepared a master class carefully and scrupulously for you. Having studied it, you will gain invaluable skills. Recently, it is very popular and even fashionable to wear handmade items, because, as a rule, they are very high quality made and worn for a long time. This applies to various jewelry: bracelets, beads, headbands, earrings or rings with your own hands. The master class is simple, understandable and should not cause any special difficulties.

In order to make stylish beads with your own hands, the master class is held below, you need to prepare such materials and tools:

  • wire for weaving with beads;
  • any precious or semiprecious stones with holes (some are fine with ordinary glass beads or other available materials) - 85 pieces;
  • a stone or a large bead of the original form (for the core of the flower) - 1 piece;
  • pliers.

Try to pick up stones or beads with holes that are sufficiently large compared to the thickness of the wire so that it can pass several times.

So, we make beads with our own hands. The master class recommends immediately taking up the weaving of the flower. To do this, you need to take a stone, which will be the tip of the petal. Its color may be different from the main one.





Pass the wire through it. Next, stop the stone at a distance of 1/3 of the length from the end of the wire and bend it. Then we pass the two wire ends into the next stone of a different color, as shown in the figure.



Then you need to string the following 7 stones on one of the ends of the wire.



Also add 7 stones to the other end of the wire.



Next, we take one stone, into which we thread two wire ends, and form a petal. Pliers can be used to stretch both ends of the wire more tightly.



In the next step, add 10 stones to the left wire, which should be longer, because it will be working. The last will be the second stone of a different color, which will be the edge of the second petal.



We take the same end of the wire and thread it through the second bead. Thus, we have formed the angle of the second petal.



We string another 7 stones onto the wire.



In order to complete the second petal, you need to pass the end of the working wire through the first bead from the center of the flower.



The same algorithm of actions is repeated to create 3 more petals.



Then we pass both ends of the wire through the extreme stone of the first petal, thus connecting all the petals.



Now make the core of the flower. We pass one wire end into a large bead or stone prepared in advance for these purposes.



And we return it back to the last bead of the petal fixed by us.



We draw the ends to the back of the flower and, making a loop, fix them. The remaining ends are fixed on a string of beads and clamped with pliers.

Now you can enjoy the result of your work and see how easy it was to make beads with your own hands! The master class provided by us revealed the whole secret of a rather interesting and simple technique.
