White Well (Voronezh) - what is this place?

In Russia there are a lot of different interesting places. There is such and not far from Voronezh. We are talking about the Cretaceous Lake in Semiluki. In the last century, in the 80s, in these places there was a chalk quarry, in which chalk was mined for household needs. In 1985, the development of the facility was discontinued. Years passed, and in 2003 the question arose of what to do next. There were two options: to rehabilitate the quarry by investing a lot of finances in it, or to equip its territory for sporting events. And so the “White Well” arose - Voronezh received a new sports complex.

What are they doing here





Firstly, it should be noted that the sports complex was created at the expense of initiative people who invested their own funds in this business. And now this facility is the only such complex located in the Central Black Earth region. In addition, IC “White Well” (Voronezh) has become almost the main center of Russian motor sports. Every year, athletes from all over Russia gather here to show their skills at official starts in the following disciplines: rally cross, karting, drag racing, drifting (since 2013) and cross. All routes were built in full compliance with the requirements of the Russian Federation, including safety. They are equipped with safety fences in visual places, special fences and chippers. They host many different competitions, such as the stages of the Russian Rally Championship, the White Well Cup in drag racing, the Total Extreme Fest drifting stage and others.

In order for athletes to maintain their athletic form at a high level, regular trainings are held here. Even a children's school is open for children from six years old. All events are supported by the local administration and other departments and companies. For those who wish, track days are arranged when anyone can feel like driving their own car with a real racer. “White Well” (Voronezh) in a short time became a very famous and successful place.

The beauty of the local landscape





This chalky quarry that has developed its resource has a very beautiful view, thanks to wonderful cliffs and small lakes located below. Seeing the uniqueness of the landscape of this place, its beauty, unusual landscapes, you will understand that in front of you is a real paradise for a traveler in love with his land. The rocks have a vanilla-chocolate color, the lakes are turquoise. The old quarry is framed by greenery, it just drowns in it. Nearby are fields without borders, at the top are clouds arranging their races in the blue sky. Before us is a fantastic landscape. So the “White Well” in Voronezh can also boast of its beauties.

Fishing on the lakes

Some time after the conversion of the area, it was noticed that the local lakes were teeming with fish. Experts added it and began to invite all fishermen here from May 2013 with the opportunity not to leave without a good catch and rest. This service is paid, many do not like it, but there are many who want to go fishing here. For those who, perhaps, will come here for this purpose, we will inform you of the price. Dawn costs 400 rubles per person. Fishing time: from 6 to 13 hours, from 13 to 20 hours and from 20 to 6 hours in the morning. Some places are also paid: a gazebo - 400 rubles, a bridge - 200 rubles. If you will enter the territory of the sports complex by car, then get ready to pay 200 rubles. The result of your fishing will be carp weighing from 800 grams to 3 kilograms.

Sports complex “White Well”, Voronezh: how to get to the object





Due to the fact that the sports complex is located at a distance of 12 kilometers from the city limits, it is preferable to get to it by car. We do it as follows. We leave the city on the Kursk highway A144 and drive towards the traffic police post. We pass him, then through the Don river, pass the turn to the city of Ostrogozhsk and drive about two more kilometers. Before reaching the village of Devitsa, you will see a road sign to the White Well sports complex. Voronezh has already held several sporting events there this year. On October 5, the final - stage of the Russian Championship in auto racing. The Champion of the country will be determined, therefore a sea of ​​impressions from the fight is expected. Come!
