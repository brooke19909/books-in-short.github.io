Smartphone on Windows. The best representatives

Windows smartphones, which first saw the light in 2012, diversified the choice of mobile devices. Of course, these gadgets were not able to instantly gain immense popularity and overtake such titans as Android and iOS. The most in demand and known smartphones on Windows Phone from Nokia. Moreover, this company has recently become the face of Microsoft.

The smartphone on Windows 8.1 combines all the best qualities of a mobile device. Many users are inclined to choose a gadget on this operating system. This article will cover the most popular Windows smartphones .

NOKIA LUMIA 930





Very high quality and beautiful smartphone. The body is made soundly, there is aluminum. Full HD screen. The diagonal is 5 inches. The processor is not new, but it copes with almost all tasks.

The device is endowed with 2 GB of RAM and 32 GB of permanent memory. The battery has a capacity of 2420 mAh.

This Windows smartphone is equipped with two excellent cameras. Front 20 MP and rear 1.2 MP. The camera will allow you to take high-quality pictures.

This smartphone is an excellent device that is endowed with this operating system. The gadget has a number of advantages and various features that will satisfy the needs of any user. It has a very reasonable price.

NOKIA LUMIA 630





Lumia 630 - perfectly represents smartphones on Windows Phone in the low price category. The phone has a cost of about eight thousand rubles.

At the same time, it is endowed with a good 4-core processor, which works with a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz, as well as a fairly large and high-quality 4.5-inch display.

The device does not boast volumes of memory, there is only 512 MB of RAM and 8 GB of permanent memory. However, it will not be difficult to increase the memory of the device using MicroSD. The camera, Windows Phone smartphones in a similar price category, basically have one. No exception is the Lumia 630, which received only the main camera 5 MP.

The device received a very good battery with a capacity of 1830 mAh.

First of all, this Windows smartphone is suitable for those who want to try the gadget on this operating system. It can easily compete with many Android phones that have a similar cost. With all this, the device also has a very nice appearance.

NOKIA LUMIA 635





A fairly similar model with the Lumia 630. Outwardly, they are very difficult to distinguish. Only the glossy finish that the Lumia 635 received stands out. The specifications are quite similar. A 4-core processor is also installed here. There is 512 MB of RAM and 8 GB of permanent memory. Of course, it is possible to increase it using memory cards.

The display has a diagonal of 4.5 inches. This smartphone on Windows is endowed with a single 5 MP camera. However, she is able to take quite good pictures. For those who use video calls, the lack of a front camera is a minus.

Many of the potential buyers wondered about the need to purchase this model. After all, the Lumia 635 is almost three thousand rubles more expensive than the Lumia 630. The significant difference is that the first model is endowed with 4G. This allows you to use high-speed Internet even in the absence of Wi-Fi. It is the presence of this technology that makes this smartphone an excellent choice for many users. Given the price, a smartphone is a great choice.

NOKIA LUMIA 925





Very stylish and high-quality Windows smartphone. It boasts an excellent body that was made using metal. For a long period, this device was a good example of what a high-quality smartphone should look like.

The device is endowed with a good screen with a diagonal of 4.5 inches. The image on the display does not deteriorate even in very bright sunshine.

The RAM in the device is 1 GB. Built-in choice: 16 and 32 GB. The processor in the smartphone is 2-core. It can run even demanding applications without problems.

The camera is excellent on the phone. There are two of them. Front 8 MP and rear 1.3 MP. Allows you to take excellent pictures, as well as make video calls. There is a good 2000 mAh battery.

Lumia 925 is an excellent choice for those who want to get an excellent device at a low cost.

NOKIA LUMIA 625





Beautiful and high-quality smartphone on Windows. The device received a screen with a diagonal of 4.7 inches. However, it can not boast of excellent resolution - 800-480 pixels.

A 2-core processor with a frequency of 1.2 GHz is installed in the smartphone. It is fast enough and it is quite enough to work with most popular programs. The smartphone does not boast huge volumes. There is 512 MB of RAM and 8 GB of internal memory. For those who wish to expand this amount, there is a microSD slot.

An indisputable advantage is the presence of 4G technology. For those who want to get a good smartphone at an affordable price, the Lumia 625 is an excellent choice.

Finally

The article discusses the most popular Windows smartphones today. The developers responsibly approached the creation of Lumia devices. Among the huge number of models, almost everyone will be able to choose the right one for themselves. Of course, many today prefer Android and iOS, which have gained great popularity, but there are users who were attracted by the functionality of Windows smartphones.
