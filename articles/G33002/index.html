Meridian 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): hotel description, services, reviews

Bulgaria is rich in cultural and natural attractions, which attracts millions of tourists every year. In the country you can find many resorts with different levels of service, for every taste and budget. Meridian ("Meridian") 4 * - the hotel, which is considered one of the prestigious places for recreation. We‚Äôll take a closer look at the description and services provided to them. Location and surroundings

Hotel Meridian ("Meridian") 4 * is located in the east of Bulgaria, where its borders are washed by the Black Sea. It has a central location in the largest resort called Sunny Beach, on the second beach line. The municipal beach stretches 10 kilometers in length. Golden sands and a smooth entrance into the depths of the sea have long won the sympathy of vacationers. The territory of the resort complex is harmoniously complemented by picturesque mountains, vineyards, mineral wealth.

Getting to Meridian 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is more convenient from the nearest airport in the city of Burgas, which is located at a distance of 35 kilometers from the hotel. To do this, you can take a taxi, rent a car or use the services of public transport. A comfortable bus stops just 20 meters from the hotel.

The climate in the region of Sunny Beach is very mild, without sharp temperature drops. The swimming season lasts all summer and captures the beginning of September. Average air temperatures from June to August range between 24 and 27 degrees. Water in the sea warms up early to 22-24 degrees. The resort is open for tourists in winter. Of course, at a temperature of +6 ... +8 degrees, the Black Sea does not seem very welcoming. The water is pretty cool. But you can take advantage of other entertainment, they are available all year round.



Description

Meridian Hotel 4 * (this name is most often found on advertising brochures) is a ten-story building that majestically rises above a well-groomed territory with a park, an outdoor pool, a cozy terrace and adjacent parking. The first thing a hotel guest is faced with is the original architecture, captured in unusual shapes and decoration of the building. The hotel was founded in 2005. The last time the repair was carried out in 2015, so it is equipped with modern equipment, furniture and technical devices.

The interior of the lobby and rooms creates an atmosphere of a single space of luxury and comfort. The overall design is supported by warm, bright colors, creating a mood of freedom and relaxation. To move through the floors in the hotel there are four high-speed silent elevators.

"Meridian" (hotel), according to travel agents, includes everything you need than a guest wants to treat himself to during his vacation. The staff, comfortable rooms, a range of entertainment and services - every detail here works for the successful stay of people with different requirements and tastes. And his external respectability, so widely represented in the photo in booklets and websites, corresponds to the level of service.



Rooms

Hotel Meridian 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) includes 248 rooms of several categories, which differ from each other not only in the number of beds, but also in the environment, a set of privileges and cost. The standard room includes high-quality wooden furniture, a TV, a fridge, a telephone for calling the administrator or the maid, air conditioning, a hairdryer, a cosmetic set and a bathroom. The hotel also contains non-smoking rooms and people and people with disabilities.

Family-class rooms have special technical devices and utensils for heating and preparing baby food. They can also be supplemented with a maximum of two beds. Strollers are available for transporting children.

The view from the rooms overlooks the serene sea, the surrounding area or the mountains covered with thick greenery. You can enjoy this beauty from your private balcony with a glass of sparkling champagne or a cup of fragrant herbal tea.

Housekeeping is available on a daily basis. The price includes the provision of clean bedding and towels, which are replaced twice and thrice a week, respectively. The administration works round the clock and at any time is ready to solve technical problems encountered by guests in the room.



Restaurants, bars

The service of the Meridian 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) provides for two types of food: all-inclusive breakfast and full board (breakfast + lunch + dinner). Each morning, the hotel's signature restaurant opens the door for guests and serves dishes of Bulgarian and international cuisine. For followers of a vegetable diet, a vegetarian menu is provided. In addition, the hotel has a cafe, pizzeria, two bars (by the pool and in the hotel) and a pub.

The restaurant, cafe and pizzeria have a limited schedule. The restaurant serves scheduled meals. If for certain reasons the guests are late or did not come for breakfast, for them the staff carefully prepares lunches. The hotel bar is open around the clock. The menu includes a wide range of drinks, cocktails for adults and children.



Entertainment

An incredible amount of entertainment opens up to tourists when visiting Sunny Beach. Meridian 4 * is no exception to this tourist rule and is also ready to please guests with a range of entertainment programs and attractions. Adults can visit the solarium, sauna, hammam, beauty salon, get therapeutic massage procedures, play billiards. Athletes and adherents of a healthy lifestyle will appreciate the fitness and wellness centers, table tennis. For lovers of holidays and fun there are animation programs and a night club.

The hotel has a chic outdoor pool, equipped with umbrellas and sun loungers. The water in the pool changes daily and undergoes special filter cleaning. The adjacent bar will quench your thirst and have a bite to eat. Other activities can be found on the beach.

For children, the Meridian (hotel) also provided a swimming pool, a games room and a playground. Also 300 meters from the hotel there is an entertainment complex owned by Sunny Beach.



Services

The Meridian Hotel (Bulgaria) treats vacationers with attention, therefore it has an extensive service. Here you can use the services of a medical office, dry cleaning, photocopy, fax, uninterrupted Internet connection (Wi-Fi) in the lobby, car rental, free parking. Since prices for services are set in euros, a currency exchange office is provided on site.

Business people can combine business and leisure. For this, the hotel has a conference room with 40 seats. In addition, a safe for documents and valuables and a left-luggage office are available for vacationers.

If the arrival at the resort was spontaneous, and vacationers did not have time to properly collect their luggage, you can purchase everything you need for a comfortable stay in the convenience store. It also operates on site.

Prices

As you can see, the hotel service is ready to provide its guests with maximum comfort. And, naturally, the question arises: how much does this level of service cost? Meridian 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) offers quite reasonable prices. Of course, a lot depends on the season and the influx of tourists. But the administration is sympathetic to the breadth of desires of vacationers and provides a system of discounts. Thus, children under seven years of age stay free of charge. For additional children, an additional charge of 3 euros per day per person is made. Sharing an adult in a room costs 8 euros (also per day per person).

When making an all inclusive reservation, the price includes accommodation, meals, entertainment, excellent animation for children, international cuisine twice a week, Bulgarian dishes - once a week. Other services that Smartline Meridian 4 * (Sunny Beach) offers are additionally paid by vacationers.

Payment can be made in cash or by credit card (VISA, MasterCard). Moreover, each travel agency offers an individual range of services and provides tours including flights and transfers.

  • Despite the fact that some vacationers noted the friendliness of the staff, others, on the contrary, noted a boorish attitude. Here, perhaps, we should talk about a clash of characters.
  • It‚Äôs sad, but true: some vacationers recorded cases of non-compliance with hotel rules for cleaning rooms and changing linen and towels. Several maids are assigned to each floor.
