Hotel "Sea" (Alushta): rooms

An important role in a successful vacation is played in what room you will live. Hotel "Sea" 4 * (Alushta, Crimea) offers you the following accommodation options:

  • VIP apartments are ideal for guests who love luxury and privacy. Due to the fact that the rooms are located on the upper floors, you will have the opportunity to enjoy the beautiful sea and mountain scenery. In addition, it is worth noting the presence of an individual elevator and a hall - you will not be delayed by the queues, nor will the steps outside the door bother you.
  • Three-room suite includes two bedrooms and a spacious living room. The latter has access to a huge balcony, which runs through almost the entire wall. A feature of the apartment is the presence of a fireplace in the living room, as well as its own bathroom in each bedroom.
  • Duplex is a huge room located at once on two levels. On the ground floor there is a living room, which adjoins a separate toilet, on the second there are 2 bedrooms at once, each of which has its own bathroom. It is also worth noting that on the first and second level there is a balcony.
  • Panoramic Suite is designed for those who like to enjoy the picturesque scenery. The spacious studio has 3 huge windows that overlook different parts of the city. It is especially worth noting the presence of a bar and kitchen, as well as a small separate bedroom, which can serve as a children's room.
  • Two-room suite with a small bedroom and a luxurious living room. The second has access to the balcony, which simultaneously offers views of the sea and the pool.
  • Junior suite is a compact size apartment in which one room is divided into living room and bedroom by decorative zoning. There is also a small kitchenette. You can choose the option with or without a balcony, which will have some effect on the cost of accommodation.
  • Family apartments can be either two-room or one-room. Accommodation here is made on double and one-and-a-half beds, and also soft folding sofas.
  • The Standard Room is a miniature room with a double bed. There is a bathroom with toilet and shower.
  • The smallest rooms have the category "economy". Their area is measured only 12 meters. There is also a French balcony from which you can enjoy the mountain scenery.

Modern style in combination with classics is offered to its guests by the Sea Hotel (Alushta). Photos of hotel rooms show bright, spacious rooms in which you can enjoy all the delights of a comfortable stay.

Negative reviews

It is impossible not to say about the negative aspects that characterize the hotel "Sea" (Alushta). The reviews contain a number of negative comments:

  • to get to the beach, you have to cross the roadway (traffic on it is not very intense, but not all drivers comply with the rules and speed limits);
  • the prices in the restaurant are just sky-high (it is better to go down to the city and have lunch there);
  • the spa area is very small, and the sauna is incredibly crowded;
  • very small elevators in which two people can hardly fit;
  • The SPA center works very little (I would like to visit it in the evening after a busy day);
  • no soundproofing in the rooms - even the operation of elevators is audible;
  • in buildings of a small number of storeys there are not only elevators, but also ramps, which makes it very inconvenient to carry suitcases;
  • very poor breakfast;
  • the staff is rather indifferent to the guests, despite the fact that rest in this institution is not cheap.

In general, it is worth noting the exceptional attractiveness of the Sea Hotel for tourists coming to Crimea. This place has a long history, which began with several professors' dachas. Now it is a large hotel complex with incredibly beautiful modern buildings. Perhaps the main highlight of the resort can be considered its location in the park area at the very foot of the mountain. It is always green here, it smells of the sea, juniper and cedar. Also, tourists really like to relax in the spa after a busy day.
