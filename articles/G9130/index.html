How to make a screen on the Samsung? Methods and instructions

How to make a screen on the “Samsung”? This question is often asked by the owners of the respective devices. The devices of the South Korean manufacturer operate under the control of the operating systems of the Android family. This OS is famous for providing virtually unlimited entertainment opportunities. Optimization at an average level, of course, she has gone to iOS for more than one or two years, but still I would like to note that everything is designed to be used at an intuitive level. Nevertheless, even despite such ease of use, some users still ask questions about how to make a screen on “Samsung”. Today we will try to answer such questions and tell you how to implement the process of creating screenshots on different models.

Comments from the manufacturer





First of all, we note that when answering the question of how to make a screen on Samsung, we mean the use of appropriate methods not only in the case of smartphones, but also in cases when we are dealing with tablet computers of a South Korean manufacturer. In general, the company's specialists took care of creating instructions for device users. They said that today there are three ways that with absolute probability make it possible to take a screenshot on the original devices of the company, inside which there is an official firmware. So, let's talk about them in more detail.

How to make a screen on the Samsung? Method number 1





Most often, this method is used. How to make a screen on the Samsung Galaxy? The user needs to hold down two keys at once. These are the power management and home screen buttons . The last key is also called “Home”. But there isn’t much difference, in principle. So, after you simultaneously press these controls, it is worth holding them in an active state for a couple of seconds. After that, a screenshot will be taken and sent to the appropriate folder.

Alternative





The screen on the Samsung phone can be made using other methods. Due to some features, it is used less often, but it is no less convenient than the previous one. So, the second method of taking screenshots on a tablet computer or smartphone of a South Korean manufacturer is to again hold down a combination of two keys at the same time. The power control button is also present in it, but the “Home screen” key is replaced by “Decrease the volume”. In the active state, holding the buttons is worth two to three seconds. After that, a screenshot will be taken.

Least used method





Thanks to the software features, the keys designed to take a screen shot on Samsung have changed. Now this method is used on a small number of devices, but still remains relevant to a certain extent. So, if the previous two methods did not help you take a screenshot of the device, try squeezing out the combination of the “Home screen” and “Back” keys at the same time. After you keep them active for a couple of seconds, a screenshot will be taken and sent to the system folder.

additional information

On some devices of the South Korean manufacturer, there is a function called “Take a screenshot with the palm of your hand”. It works quite simply, and the only thing that can be said here is the adjustment is carried out using the engineering menu of your device. We remind you once again that this function is not available on all Samsung devices, therefore, information on this should be clarified in a separate order.

Problems taking screenshots?

It is very important to understand that the methods that were described earlier are, in some way, a general guide for users who have Samsung devices at their disposal. It should be noted that the location of the keys on different devices may not coincide. Nevertheless, on each device from a South Korean manufacturer (namely the original one), you can take a screenshot without using third-party utilities. This is done exclusively using the firmware. Do not forget that you should not press the buttons in turn, but at the same time. If you have problems creating screenshots, then the device may not be original. Or unofficial firmware is installed on it.
