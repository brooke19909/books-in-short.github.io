What is topiary?

According to various encyclopedias and reference books, this is the first name for green plants that adorn the street interior. Topiary - shrubs and trees skillfully crafted as decorative figures. The history of their origin goes back to the distant past - during the existence of the Roman Empire. Later, a newfangled tradition spread to Europe. At the same time, decor elements began to decorate with their bizarre shapes not only gardens, alleys and parks, but also the interior decoration of the premises. Later, original man-made interior items, similar in shape to a round or conical crown of trees, began to be called topiary . Creating green sculptures is a whole art. Moreover, the embodiment of ideas and designs requires a certain time and special patience. To grow a large topiary, a professional gardener will need more than one year. Consider what methods and methods are used to form such living plantings.





Types of Topiary

To form a plant is quite difficult. You can create the desired crown in the classical way - by cutting as new branches and processes appear in the plant. Such a rather time-consuming process can be greatly facilitated by installing a special form during planting. Adhering to it, cutting the plant is easier and easier. This creates simple geometric shapes - a square, a ball, a cone. There is another, less time-consuming, method of obtaining topiary - frame. The main difference and advantage from the previous method is mobility. If classical green sculptures are usually stationary, then grown on special frames can easily be moved around the site to the right place according to the changed composition. This is due to the fact that topiary is grown according to a new method directly inside the framework on the located soil and irrigation systems. Usually, ground cover plants are used to form such landscape sculptures , braiding their shoots with the artificial base of the product in a few weeks. And if, in addition to greenery, the composition consists of flowers, then the result is amazing.

What should be the basis?

Such sweet compositions are very similar to bouquets of sweets. Topiary with its external saturation hide the internal structure of the structure. What does the foundation consist of, and how can various objects, including sweets, be strengthened on it? Firstly, you can purchase the necessary products in stores specializing in the sale of materials for applied creativity. Making a paper ball from newspapers

Most importantly, the foundation should be light enough. After all, after decorating with sweets, such a home-made crown will need to be firmly installed on the makeshift trunk of a souvenir tree. Therefore, the most suitable material in the manufacture of the base for topiary is thin paper. Take an ordinary newspaper and sewing thread. Tear the paper into small pieces that are about half the size of a notebook sheet or slightly smaller. To begin, crumple a small ball with a diameter of 3-4 cm from a small piece. Then begin to apply other newspaper blanks to it, forming a ball. The number of layers will directly depend on the desired size of the base. When the ball is ready, wrap it with threads, moving in different directions. If the future topiary of sweets is planned to be suspended, fasten the decorative braid with newspapers between layers . And in the case of making a souvenir tree, insert at the initial stage in the middle of the ball a wand, which will then be the trunk. For greater strength, you can lubricate the workpiece with a thin layer of PVA glue.





How to fix the topiary trunk in a pot?

A homemade tree usually "grows" in some small capacity. As a rule, a small pot or bucket is used for this. And so that with further work it would be more convenient to decorate the topiary by attaching various objects to the base, it is advisable to immediately fix the trunk firmly. But before that, do not forget to decorate the straw or stick with decorative elements: wrap with colored paper or bright ribbons to the tone of the whole souvenir. Then, pour a thick, freshly diluted mixture of gypsum or alabaster into the pot, filling the container by about 4/5 of the total volume. Until the mass has completely solidified, insert the topiary trunk into the middle of the pot to a depth of about 5-6 cm. Wait until the mixture has completely hardened, holding the round base in an upright position. You can also work in a slightly different way. In a finished pot with a pre-flooded mass, drill a thin hole equal in diameter to the tree trunk. Then the product will be semi-collapsible.





Master class (MK) "Topiary of sweets"

Let's consider how to fix sweets on the basis of a souvenir. To get an elegant product, use colored or shiny paper for decoration. Cut 6x6 cm squares from it. They will act as a decorative "fur coat" for sweets. Paper blanks can also be round in shape or any other. You can make a very original topiary of sweets with your own hands in the form of a bouquet. First, wrap each candy (preferably round) in shiny paper. Then, from the corrugated paper, cut the blanks in the form of petals. Wrap them around sweets, creating mini-buds. Insert one edge of the toothpick into a sweet item immediately. Fasten the other free side of the sharp stick to the round topiary base. For simple design, put paper sweets into funnel-shaped paper blanks, then attach them to the crown of the souvenir tree. The simplest manufacture of crafts is the use of ready-made lollipops. They stick into the crown with their chopsticks and, if desired, are decorated with ribbons and other decorative elements.
