Grand Central Terminal: description and architectural features

The building was rebuilt three times in its entire history.

  • The very first Grand Central Depot station lasted only 8 years, from 1871 to 1879.
  • The second, Grand Central Station, was located at this site from 1899 to 1913.
  • The latter was named Grand Central Terminal, it was opened in 1913.

The design of today's station was done by well-known American companies at that time . The first was engineering, the second - architectural.

The development of a construction plan began in 1904. When engaged in the project, the companies had to consider the wishes of the authorities. The main requirement was to hide the railway tracks in the tunnel in order to rid the townspeople of soot and excessive noise.

The idea was not just difficult for those times, but extremely difficult to implement. Nevertheless, the designers managed to achieve the desired result, even if it took a whole decade. The grand opening took place on February 2, 1913.

Secret, or Secrets of NY Central Station

Grand Central's pride, a watch with opal dials, is estimated at around $ 10-20 million. And if you look at the ceiling of the main hall, you can see the universe. It is decorated with a fresco that depicts zodiac signs and 2500 stars, 60 of which are equipped with backlight.

One of the halls (Vanderbilt) was named after its owners, people who donated about 6.5 million dollars for the construction. The family motto reads: "The great oak grows from a small acorn." Therefore, as a tribute to the memory, the station building is decorated with stucco molding with similar images.

Caring for the health of citizens of the country, workers, cleaning the ceiling of the main hall from tobacco raid (they constantly smoked at the station), a small part of it, located next to the cancer claw, was left as it is. The goal is to show people how harmful smoking is to their health.

There is even a secret basement, and rather big. Its area is about 200 thousand square meters. Part of this territory is occupied by a secret room, which is not even on the drawings. Generators are installed in it, and there is also a red button, when clicked, all trains stop. Continuing the topic of secret rooms, it is worth mentioning the platform, the whereabouts of which no one from outsiders knows. It was created back when Roosevelt, when the president needed to hide his state of health from citizens of the country: he was defeated by paralysis resulting from polio. So, he quietly got to his residence. Now this platform exists to ensure the safety of the president in the event of a threat.
