Sandy Crete Hotels - Paradise Holidays in the Mediterranean

The island of Crete ... one cannot fail to fall in love with this beautiful and mysterious land, which is full of ancient history and amazing picturesque landscapes. Crete holds mysterious myths and legends, this place was the beginning of the birth of European civilization. Modern Crete is an island with excellent opportunities and conditions for recreation. Crete hotels with a sandy beach annually attract millions of tourists from all over the world.

Island of myths and magnificent beaches

Crete is a magnificent mountain landscape, beautiful scenery, numerous monuments of ancient culture and, of course, the best golden beaches. It offers tourists a high quality service and luxury Crete hotels - with a sandy beach, comfortable rooms and all the conditions for a good rest.

Hotel complexes are located along the coast, many of them are located on the very shores of the Aegean Sea. It has numerous Crete hotels, with a sandy beach and emerald sea water, which are amazing places for a romantic getaway, for a vacation with the whole family. The unique climate of the island contributes to the long bathing season, which opens here in the month of April and lasts until mid-autumn. The island is of volcanic origin, mostly the coast is covered with sandstone, but almost everywhere sand is additionally poured. The best areas for relaxation are beaches in the areas of Stalida and Malia.

The best hotels of the island

Multiple Crete hotels with a sandy beach and well-equipped recreation areas invite tourists and are ready to offer the most comfortable conditions. A wonderful holiday complex is Candia Maris 5 *, it is famous for its well-groomed territory, apartments with sea views, a magnificent thalassotherapy center and a gorgeous beach. And the Agapi Beach 4 * hotel will be the most suitable place for families with children. An economical option and excellent conditions is the difference between Creta Beach 4 *, which offers all-inclusive meals; Children under 14 years old stay free of charge. Young people and those who want to have fun should make a choice at hotels such as Venus Melena 3 *, Marie-Kristin 3. Not far from a small fishing village is the luxurious Grecotel Club Marine Palace, which is made in light yellow and orange colors.

There are six huge pools, water slides, gorgeous beaches, children's clubs and spa centers. Those who wish to come to this place want to enjoy the peace and harmony of nature, get acquainted with the traditions of local rural residents. Crete hotels with their own beach are magnificent five- and four-star complexes with sports and health centers, restaurants and bars, a variety of entertainment programs.
One of the most popular among holidaymakers is Iberostar Creta Marin, which is located closer to the northern part of the island. Golden sands washed by the Aegean Sea and wonderful views attract this tourist center of tourists. It has everything for a family holiday, guests will be offered interesting animation, funny holidays and entertaining shows. And the restaurants are famous for excellent Greek and Mediterranean cuisine. Crete hotels with a sandy beach will give an unforgettable vacation, turn it into a real fairy tale and leave a wonderful impression.
