Sheregesh Hotels: reviews, photos. The best hotels of Sheregesh

The main criteria that affect the category of a hotel in Sheregesh are the location, focus, proximity and availability of lifts, in addition, additional services that increase the order of comfort of rest.



Depending on the level of comfort offered by the staff and the atmosphere prevailing in the hotel, in the Sheregesh resort, hotels, hotels are divided into 3 types: youth, VIP and family.

Youth are bases that offer their guests not the highest level of comfort and affordable prices. First of all, such Sheregesh hotels, reviews of which can be read in the article below, are designed for young people and students seeking drive and tireless entertainment. At the same time, they perceive the hotel room only as a room for self-care and sleep. The atmosphere that distinguishes such Sheregesh hotels (photos can be found in this article) is filled with bustle and fun, therefore, be prepared that sleepless nights and new acquaintances will become an indispensable part of your vacation.

Consider the following type. Family class hotels or Sheregesh hotels with a children's room, as the name implies, are suitable for families with kids and couples. Therefore, the presence of a measured and calm atmosphere, programs for young guests and a decent level of service is an obligatory part of the institutions of this class. A huge number of leisure options (from table tennis to a Russian bathhouse), hearty breakfasts and a homely cozy atmosphere will help restore strength after difficult workdays and looking after a fidget, as well as return you to the world of joy and carelessness.

The best VIP category Sheregesh hotels suggest sophistication, elitism and the highest level of comfort and service. The staff of the institution will surround you with attention and care and will immediately respond to your every desire. Fabulous rooms, upscale spa care programs, gourmet cuisine can emphasize your status, as well as surround you with a halo of belonging to something unusual. It is worth mentioning separately about the unique style that is inherent in each such hotel. It can be boar heads hung on the walls, or decoration with a century-old cedar, or much more, which sets a special surroundings for guests. The beauty and originality of the institution is worthy of the highest level of service.

Hotel "Snow"

Considering Sheregesh hotels, it is worth highlighting "Snowy". It has the ability to ski directly from the door. It is two hundred meters from the Cascade-Lift lift. Judging by the reviews, it offers a ski school, ski room, spa and restaurant. For guests free internet throughout, as well as private free parking.



Rooms are equipped with a kettle and a TV. Free slippers and toiletries are provided. The facilities include shops and a 24-hour front desk. The area is popular for horse riding and skiing.

Hotel "Provincial"

Looking for hotels in Sheregesh on Mount Zelenaya, pay attention to the Gubernskaya hotel. It is located right at the foot of the mountain, in the resort of Mustag. It features an indoor pool, a sauna, a spa with a steam bath and free Wi-Fi.

The rooms are decorated in bright colors and have a TV. The bathroom comes with free toiletries, a hairdryer and bathrobes.

Judging by the reviews, the restaurant serves delicious Russian cuisine, and in the morning a continental breakfast is served here . The facility is located near the ski lift, from which you have the opportunity to climb the highest part of the mountain. There is a boot dryer and a ski room.
