We determine the arithmetic mean

You have the right to try to independently determine at least an approximate payment for your housing, even if you do not have the slightest idea of ‚Äã‚Äãhow the cost of an apartment is estimated by a pro. Moreover, every sane person can do this.

Factors that affect the cost of apartments

Want to know how experts rate the cost of an apartment? Everything is not as complicated as it might seem at first glance. Valuation of real estate is carried out by analyzing the object according to several criteria, which ultimately determine its real value.

Methods for calculating the cost of an apartment used by professionals

It should be noted that the expert assessment of the value of real estate occurs through three methods of calculation:

1) Costly. It is based on the fact that the price of apartments is determined on the basis of the amount of expenses that the owner incurred in the repair of the property (transportation of building materials, their cost, salaries to contractors, taxes, etc.). Plus, the appraiser must take into account the level of inflation, depreciation. Given these factors, he adjusts the calculation.

2) Profitable. This method is specifically designed for commercial real estate. Its meaning is extremely simple. The price of the premises is determined based on the size of the potential benefits that the new owner will receive in the future. However, the method is suitable exclusively for shops and shopping centers.

3) Comparative. This method is considered by experts to be the most common. In this case, the valuation of real estate is determined by comparison with other similar offers for the sale of apartments. Since its use does not imply special knowledge, this method will not cost you so much.





By concluding an agreement with a specific company engaged in the assessment of real estate on a professional basis, it will not be superfluous to inquire through what method they determine the cost of apartments. If you don‚Äôt like this or that assessment method, you can always choose another expert.

It should be noted that the cadastral valuation of real estate is carried out by BTI, and now the tax on houses and apartments will depend on this indicator.
