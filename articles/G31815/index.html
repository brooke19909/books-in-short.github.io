How to Choose a Hotel in a Megapolis

Many visitors, arriving in a large Russian metropolis, whether it be Moscow, St. Petersburg or Novosibirsk, are forced to decide on the place of temporary residence in a foreign city. From any trip, be it an exciting trip or a banal business trip, each of us expects extremely positive emotions. In their formation, the place of residence, of course, plays one of the key roles. Currently, rented apartments have become very popular. However, many in the old manner prefer a hotel room.

The reasons for this choice are commonplace. As a rule, rooms in modern hotels are much more comfortable and cozy than empty apartments offered for rent. Hotel Ambassador , Ritz Carlton or Ararat Park Hyatt - each guest in the metropolis makes their own choices based on personal preferences and travel goals. The five-star hotel is a chic foyer, a doorman at the entrance, marble stairs with hospitable rich carpets, richly furnished apartments, helpful maids, daily wet cleaning. Of course, today there will be people for whom all these external manifestations of luxurious life matter. The number of mini-hotels in Russian open spaces is growing every year. What is a classic mini-hotel? Mini-hotel Cosiness , Neskuchny Garden, or Chenonceau (even the names themselves are so warm and soft that they have a glimpse into these pretty places) is a worthy alternative to expensive hotel rooms in capital cities. In most cases, this is a converted apartment with a small number of comfortable rooms - living rooms, designed for one or two people. And in the small comfortable lobby there is a reception, a rack with keys, upholstered furniture and chairs for clients. Current trends are such that mini-hotels in the face of fierce competition in this segment of the business strive to offer their guests the maximum number of various services, possibly satisfying all the wishes of the guests. And the prices in mini-hotels are very affordable, which is also an additional factor when choosing in their favor. Have a nice trip!
