Crochet summer beach tunic: scheme and description

An absolutely necessary wardrobe for craftswomen who love the summer sun is a crocheted beach tunic-mesh. The scheme and description of knitting the simplest models may not be required, since the procedure for their manufacture is extremely simple. To successfully implement more complex ideas, you can not do without schemes, but the good news is that such instructions are very easy to find.

What yarn to choose

Despite the fact that natural yarn is definitely better suited for knitting summer products, artificial yarns can also be used when working with mesh fabrics. Large cells will not allow the product to become hot. In addition, many synthetic types of yarn have an attractive sheen, silk texture, beautiful torsion, bright colors and other advantages.

Thus, polyamide, microfiber, cotton or linen will be the best choice for the manufacture of such a product as a crocheted beach tunic. Schemes and descriptions of summer products quite often contain recommendations regarding yarn, but often the material chosen differs significantly from those indicated. In this case, you can not rely only on the scheme, you have to knit a control sample. It must be at least ten centimeters long and high, and it must also be done using the selected yarn and pattern. Then the data will be as accurate as possible.

Measurement of the sample will allow you to calculate the size of the pattern repeat and compare them with the existing circuit.

Distinctive features of a beach tunic

Given the specifics of the product, it must meet the following criteria:

  • Canvas knitted with a mesh or openwork pattern.
  • Free style.
  • Short overall length.
  • The versatility of colors or a combination of color with a swimsuit.

The last point is of considerable importance for girls who prefer clothes of bright colors. Crochet beach tunic (the diagram and description can be for openwork or solid pattern) does not fit to hide a swimsuit, but for its aesthetic demonstration. Therefore, the dissonance created by the mismatch of these elements makes the use of tunics meaningless.

Principles of making a beach tunic

The photo below shows a very simple model that girls with any type of figure can wear and combine with all kinds of swimwear.



Such a summer beach tunic crocheted (diagrams and description are not required) is combined from four parts of a rectangular shape. Knitting of cloths is carried out using any loin net. The pattern can consist only of cells or with minor decorative elements. If desired, you can connect the armholes on the details of the front and back, as well as the ridge on the sleeves. However, this is optional. Fillet mesh allows you to get a very soft and plastic cloth, so the folds formed by square sleeves do not spoil the appearance of the product. However, when using more complex patterns, armholes and ridge are necessary.

A simple ornament that you can use.





And the circuit to it.





Fillet is called a net consisting of air loops (VP), single crochet (RLS) or double crochet (CST). The most popular are two types:

  1. A grid with square cells (CCH, 2VP).
  2. Cloth with cells of a semicircular shape (5VP, RLS).

Both the one and the other look are very convenient for the formation of paintings of almost any configuration, especially squares. The white tunic in the photo is associated with the use of the second type of mesh.

Features cut tunics

In accordance with the standards of the girl for whom the product is intended, they knit a rectangular part of the back. At production of a cloth of a forward it is necessary to think over a mouth. It can be in the form of a sharp cape, a rectangle or a semicircle. In any case, you get a beautiful crochet beach tunic. The scheme and description of the knitting of the neck, as a rule, are not needed due to the simplicity of the pattern.

Sleeves for this kind of tunic should not be made long, the length to the elbow or slightly lower will be quite sufficient. When assembling the product, the side seams can not be brought to the very edge, but small cuts can be left. Lacing placed on the sides, on the sleeves or on the neck looks very attractive.

A crocheted beach tunic (the diagram and description are above) further only partially fits. It is necessary to temporarily stop work on sections of the canvas that will become sleeves (they will be connected later, or they will not be at all).
