Mountains of Stavropol: photos and descriptions, tours and excursions

The mountains of Stavropol have their own unique landscape. For the most part, they are affordable and low, as if specially created for tourism. Any legend is associated with any of them, which locals or guides gladly talk about. The highest mountains are located in Pyatigorye, between cities such as Stavropol and Nevinnomyssk. Photos of the mountains of Stavropol, a brief description of tourist routes - later in this article.

Mount Beshtau

A unique natural monument, the highest mountain in Stavropol (1,400 m) is located in the central part of the Pyatigorsk Valley. A ring road is laid around it. You can climb the main peak along two paths, one laid from the city of Lermontov, the other goes from Zheleznovodsk.

The hiking route from Zheleznovodsk is 6 kilometers, the climb from tourists takes no more than three hours, and they get an unforgettable experience. From the top of the mountain you can observe the majestic Elbrus, the Caucasus Range and the whole panorama of the resort area. The picturesque mountain is spurs and steep cliffs. At the foot there is the Athos Monastery for men, which was founded in 1904, then was closed, but its revival began in the 90s. Many well-known people who came to Mineralnye Vody for a vacation visited here at one time, among them A.S. Pushkin, M.Yu. Lermontov.

Mount Strizhament

Located between Stavropol and Nevinomyssk, the highest point is at 831 m above sea level. The mountain is covered with sand and shell rock, there are caves, labyrinths, niches that make up a single natural complex.

On the upper part of the mountain there is a plateau without a forest, but bifurcated by the deep river Temnushka. At the foot of the mountain there are many sources of mineral water, rare plants grow here, some of them are listed in the Red Book.

The place is very popular among tourists due to the clean mountain air, healing springs and amazing landscapes: caves, rocks, grottoes, springs.

Fortress Mountain in Stavropol

On the territory of the mountain complex there are many monuments and unique objects made in various architectural styles. The fortress mountain in Stavropol has a symbolic name, since the elevation difference here is not very large. This is considered a huge plus for tourists who wish to visit these places, because climbing uphill does not require much effort.

The main attraction of these places is the monument to Pushkin. The area around it is well landscaped, tourists are happy to visit this place. Not far from the monument is the Eternal Flame, built in memory of those killed in the Great Patriotic War.

Nearby is the main temple of the North Caucasus - the Cathedral, and on the top of the Fortress Mountain there is a cross - the main symbol of the city. Tourists are happy to visit the panoramic platform, from which the whole charm of the Taman Forest, located in the north-west of Stavropol, opens. Here you can also see a spectacular light and music fountain, built at the expense of a philanthropist.

Tours and excursions

Trips to the mountains from Stavropol are carried out by many travel agencies and local carriers. Guests are invited to explore the unique natural sites. There are weekend tours organized so that in a short time you can see all the main attractions of this ancient region.

Elbrus region

Fantastic beauties, grandiose sizes of snow-white Elbrus attract thousands of tourists here. In a pine forest with the nearby river Baksan, there is a boarding house and recreation centers. The proximity to the mountains creates a unique microclimate here, conducive to good rest.

For lovers of climbing mountains and hiking trails, all conditions are created here. The tour program includes excursion routes with a lift by chairlift to Mount Cheget, visits to the glades of Azau and Cheget. After a short rest, the next day, climbing the pendulum cableway to Mount Elbrus, visit to Chegemsky waterfalls and Polyana Narzanov are offered.

Tourists are accommodated in rooms with all amenities, a caf√© and barbecue facilities are on site. The cost of the tour includes travel, accommodation in a boarding house, guided tours. Climbing by cable car to the Cheget and Azau glades is paid extra and is not considered mandatory for visiting.

Arkhyz

A lake with crystal clear water, surrounded by centuries-old pine trees and sparkling glaciers. The healing mountain air of Arkhyz earned well-deserved popularity among tourists. Snow-capped mountain peaks, alpine meadows, waterfalls, glaciers and lakes will forever remain in the memory of those who at least once visited these unique places.

Rest here for those who love clean mountain air and active tourism. Horse riding, rafting on rafts, an excursion to the astrophysical observatory, climbing to the top of the mountain along one of the longest cable cars in Russia are offered.

Tourists are accommodated in a boarding house, the cost of the trip depends on the number of people in the group and is calculated individually. The tour includes travel, accommodation and sightseeing trips. There are special offers for tourists with their own transport, they are provided with insurance, receive assistance in paperwork in the traffic police and Rospotrebnadzor. Meals are paid extra.

Dombai

This resort village, located at an altitude of 1600 meters above sea level, is considered the most popular place for mountaineering and mountaineering. The most famous winter sports center in Russia. Here paragliding competitions are held, climbers carry out their mountain skiing.

Those who do not have good ski training can have a great weekend in the mountains of Stavropol, riding horses, visiting the reserve with wild animals in aviaries. Fans of hiking will be interested in an excursion to the reserved valley of the Alibek River, to Mount Mussa-Cheri or to the Taberdinsky Reserve.

Tourists are accommodated in the hotel "Russia", the cost of a tour to the mountains from Stavropol depends on the number of people in the group. The price includes hotel accommodation, travel, sightseeing trips. Meals - for a fee.
