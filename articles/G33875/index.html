Knitted tunic for girls: schemes with description

A tunic looks great with a skirt and trousers. Knitting needles for girls, teenagers and women can be associated with interesting summer and winter models. Selecting unusual patterns, you can create things no worse than store ones. For girls, choose bright threads, for girls - a neutral gamut. For one product, it is better to use multifaceted patterns, including several patterns.

The scheme of work when knitting tunics

Start your work by choosing a model. If you are just starting to knit, then use ready-made schemes, instructions, descriptions of your size. If you have experience knitting, then draw patterns of the future tunic, choose a pattern and conduct the preparatory phase to study the properties of yarn.

To do this, knit a sample of ten centimeters on both sides or according to the size of the pattern repeat. Measure the number of loops and rows per centimeter. Next, erase the template, dry, measure again. This stage protects knitters from mistakes, blooming and low-quality yarn.

If you ignore this work, then the tunic for the girl (with knitting or crochet you knit it - it doesn‚Äôt matter) can lose shape, fade, stretch out or sit down when washing. By the way, when using several patterns, the density of knitting can vary. Therefore, it is better for beginners to use ready-made models. For warm tunics, choose patterns with diamonds, braids, cones, plaits, then the product looks more elegant, and in the warm season, openwork patterns will do.

Patterns for warm tunics

The most popular pattern among knitters is braids. Variations on this topic are huge. In any case, the tunic knitted with needles in this pattern looks elegant, feminine, bright.

The main principle of braid knitting is dividing the loops into two parts: the first is thrown onto the auxiliary knitting needle before or at work, the second is knitted with facial loops, and then the same actions are done with the remaining loops. The direction of the pattern depends on where the auxiliary spoke is located, and the number of loops affects the spike width. Standardly create a pattern of six loops: three loops for auxiliary and working knitting needles. Please note: when choosing a braid, you knit a tunic with a collar.

Do not make this pattern very dense, as it will work hard with thrown loops. Consider several patterns that are suitable for women and children‚Äôs products (not machine-made, but hand-knitted).

Tunic (knitting) with patterns of embossed patterns among women is most in demand. Only the same model can have a different designation in fashion magazines. By default, the odd rows are knitted according to the pattern, the even rows correspond to the pattern, or all are replaced with purl loops.

How to tie a tunic to a beginner?

If you don‚Äôt have original yarn at hand, and you cannot knit bumps, braids, tows and other complex elements, then you should resort to the following trick.

  • Make a product pattern immediately with sleeves and a flared bottom. That is, the tunic will look like a dress.
  • One pattern will correspond to the front and back.
  • Draw a diagonal line on the part. She will distinguish between colors.
  • Now take three colors: black, green and brown. Black narrow bands are tied, and green and brown distinguish the part in half diagonally, that is, a three-color tunic is obtained.

With knitting needles (for a girl - that‚Äôs it!) Individually knit in front and back, starting from the bottom of the product, gradually switch to the sleeves. You get two details with the sleeves down, like a kimono. Now grind them at the seam. Tie the neck, sleeves and bottom of the tunic.

If you have not learned how to change colors so that the wrong side is neat, then use melange yarn that is already dyed in several colors.

How to work with patterns?

Choose a pattern from the above schemes. Create some samples. By varying with the same yarn, knitting needles of different thicknesses, you can get a pattern of different sizes. Choose the best option for yourself. Carry out preparatory work with knitted patterns.



Now make a full-length pattern. The simplest tunic model - consisting of two shelves (front and back). If the product contains deflated sleeves or pronounced armpits, then start knitting from the bottom. If there are no sleeves, then you can start work from the neck (this method will be described below on the example of a children's model).

You start work with elastic, then even a knitted tunic keeps its shape. With knitting needles (with a description of the patterns of difficulties there will be no) we work out each individual drawing on a template. This method is highly recommended for beginners, otherwise the product will be with many folds or skewed.

Use patterns with braids for decorative purposes, for example, for vertical lines on the front shelf on both sides or throughout the tunic through a certain distance (for example, every ten centimeters). Flat patterns are most suitable for children's models.

Knitted things for children: tunic with knitting needles with patterns

For babies, choose hypoallergenic yarn. For a one-year-old baby you will need 3 skeins: 1 pc. wool and 2 pcs. mixed yarn from merino and acrylic; circular knitting needles (4 and 4.5 mm). The density of knitting with mixed yarn is 19 stitches in a ten-centimeter sample.

On 4-millimeter circular knitting needles, type 66 loops of wool. In order not to lose the beginning of the circle, you can use markers or tie with a contrasting thread. Knit elastic 1x1 - 10 rows. Then knit a circle with full face loops.

Now change the thread to a mixed yarn and alternate the front loop with a crochet. To make a crochet, you need to grab the loop of the lower row on the left knitting needle and knit it. The next circle will consist of facial loops.

Now take knitting needles with a number of 4.5 mm and 5 rows of knit facial loops. By changing the thickness of the spokes increases the volume of the product. At the end of the work, change the yarn to wool and knit 6 rows according to the pattern: odd - with facial, even - with purl stitches. Please note that this model fits from the neck down (the third photo, where the blue tunic (with knitting needles) for a girl of 1-2 years old).





Armpit and waist shaping

Now move on to shaping your armpits. To do this, knit 36 ‚Äã‚Äãfront stitches, close the next 30 stitches, knit again 36 stitches with the front stitch and close the next 30. Now you can see where the armpits and shelves of the tunic will be.

Next, knit 36 ‚Äã‚Äãwrong loops, make three crochets, without tearing off the yarn, knit the next 36 loops also inside out and again make 3 crochets. Mark with a marker or thread the end of the row. Knit a circle with full face smoothness and change the thread to mixed, with which you make literally 6 rows according to the same pattern. Proceed to the formation of the waist (row by row it turns out a trapezoidal knitted tunic with knitting needles). For a 1-2 year old girl, it is important that the clothes do not constrain movements.

Now change the thread again to wool and knit 8 rows according to the previously described pattern, where the odd rows are created with front loops, and the even rows with the back loops. Then proceed to the formation of the hem. To do this, mark the border with a thread every 13 loops. It should turn out 6 parts.



Knitting a hem in 2 ways

Replace the thread with blended yarn and add 2 loops on each part. The result should be 90 loops. Knit 9 front rows. Then repeat the knitting with increments and knitting with the front rows until the length of the tunic from the armpits is 18 centimeters.

Next, change the thread to wool and proceed to the formation of the hem. It can be connected in two ways. In the first case, 5 circles are knit either in the front or in the back. On the last lap you close the loop. In the second version, a dress is obtained (meaning a trapezoidal knitted tunic with knitting needles).
