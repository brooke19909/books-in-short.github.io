What is noteworthy is the Bohemia Hotel (Saratov)

The reason for coming to Saratov can be completely different - family circumstances, work, tourism. But, you must admit, it is not always possible to stay with friends. For this case, there is a beautiful hotel "Bohemia" on Vavilova.

Bohemia (Saratov) is a chain of hotels that guarantee the highest level of service with a European level of comfort.





Hotel Location

The city in which the Bohemia Hotel is located is Saratov. From the train station, the road to the hotel will take no more than five minutes, as it is located in the central area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city. And it is very convenient for trips, regardless of their purpose, whether it be a tourist trip or a usual business trip.

In the evening, you can also find something to do, and not just sit in the room. Within walking distance from the hotel are the drama theater , the theater of the young spectator of the city of Saratov, the opera and ballet theater , a cinema and a children's park. Booking a ticket for your favorite performance can be entrusted to the hotel staff, they will do it pretty quickly. In addition, it will also be possible to make purchases without any problems, since in the immediate vicinity, namely on Kirov Street, there are a variety of shopping centers, shops and boutiques.

Available hotel services

If you are planning to visit the city of Saratov, Bohemia on Vavilova will be happy to shelter you and provide a full range of services. Each guest room will be delivered breakfast. If there is a personal car, the hotel guest will be able to leave it in the parking lot of the complex, and absolutely free of charge.





During a business trip to Saratov, you can not worry about the upcoming meeting, as standard services include a transfer to the venue for negotiations. The return transfer service is provided only to those guests who decide to stay in Bohemia for at least three days.

In addition, you can use:

  • Wi-Fi (absolutely free for all guests);
  • a large conference room for 200 people and several small halls for banquets, anniversaries, weddings and other celebrations (there are two menu options to choose from: Italian and Russian cuisine);
  • excursion programs in the city of Saratov;
  • the services of an experienced translator;
  • visa support (for foreign citizens);
  • laundry room.

At the request of the guest, the price of the room may also include lunch and dinner.

Description of rooms

The Bohemia Hotel (Saratov) provides five types of rooms at the disposal of its guests:

  • Economy class;
  • standard (rooms consist of a bedroom and a study area, as well as a bathroom with a shower);
  • junior suite (superior hotel room, consisting of two parts: a bedroom and a study area, there is a bathroom equipped with a shower cabin);
  • Suite (the rooms have a bedroom and a study area, a bathroom with a Jacuzzi or shower, in addition there is a home theater and a DVD player);
  • VIP class.

In each room, regardless of its type, you can connect to the Internet and use the telephone services, both long-distance and international. In addition, all rooms are equipped with split systems, a TV, a minibar (here you can try the Bohemia branded beer), an autonomous heating system and an alarm system (fire and security).

In the bathroom, the guest can use a hairdryer, bathrobe and personal care products.





In bad weather you will be given an umbrella.

All hotel rooms are non-smoking. Smoking inside and inside the complex is strictly prohibited.

Hotel Conditions

Check-in and check-out takes place at 12 noon local time.

When booking a room cancellation is possible. However, in this case, if the order was canceled less than a day before the expected date of arrival, the guest must pay compensation for a simple reserved room. The amount of compensation, as a rule, is the size of the cost of the selected room for the first day of stay.

For guests with children there are separate conditions. If the child is less than three years old, extra bed is not provided, accommodation is without payment. Subject to the provision of additional space, a fee of 500 rubles will be charged , which includes the cost of breakfast.

It is possible to accommodate guests with animals, but subject to prior request.

Park Hotel Bohemia: Saratov surprises!

The Bohemia Park Hotel is a 25-minute drive from the Bohemia Hotel. This is a four-story building with 56 rooms and views of the Yellow Mountain. Here you can relax from the bustle of the city as residents of Saratov, as well as guests of the city.





A feature of the hotel is its location in the green zone. The Bohemia Hotel Chain (Saratov) is glad to see you as its guests.
