Let us consider in more detail some of the unusual inventions of man created at the beginning of the twenty-first century.

Artificial retina

This discovery belongs to Japanese scientists. The retina produced is an aluminum matrix using silicon semiconductor elements. Resolution is 100 pixels.

The retina will perform its functions if it was installed complete with special glasses and a small computer. Glasses with a built-in video camera are used to receive and transmit images to a computer, where processing takes place. A camera with glasses converts light into portions of electronic pulses. After processing the image, the computer divides it in half and transfers it to the left and right eyes, to infrared emitters located on the back of the lenses of the glasses. Glasses emit short pulses of infrared radiation, which activates the photosensors on the retina of the eye and forces them to transmit electrical pulses encoding the picture into optical neurons.





In the future, it is planned that such a retina will be able to return vision to a blind person and help to see smaller objects.

Later, Japanese scientists were able to grow the retina from the stem cells of mice, its testing has not yet been completed.

Projection keyboard

Over time, more and more new inventions appear. Unusual finds are present in human life, one of them is a projection keyboard.





With its help, it becomes possible to design keys on the surface, where they are pressed. The video projector that designs the keyboard has a sensor that can track finger movements, after which it calculates the coordinates of the pressed keys and displays correctly typed text on the display. However, such a keyboard also has disadvantages; it cannot be used outdoors.

Electronic Cigarette

This discovery was made by a Chinese scientist after his father died of lung cancer. Nicotine addiction is one of the strongest in the world. What does a person quit smoking do. He is trying to replace this habit with something else, for example, gluing a nicotine patch, buying gum, trying to find an alternative to smoking.

An electronic cigarette is a device that simulates the smoking process. When using such a novelty, a person does not give up his habit, does not seek replacements, but usually spends time. However, the smoker does not spoil his lungs with poisonous tar and combustion products, since they are not present in this type of device. Thus, a person smoking an electronic cigarette can get rid of nicotine addiction.

Brain interface

The unusual inventions of the 21st century are quite diverse, and one of them is the brain interface.

An example of managing objects with thought was demonstrated by a Japanese company. Man, by the power of thought, forced a switch mounted on a model of a train of a large-scale railway to switch.

Principle of action: in the infrared spectrum, transillumination and recording of the cerebral cortex occurs. When carrying out such a procedure, the passage through the blood vessels of hemoglobin, both with oxygen and without, is clearly visible, while the volume of blood in various parts of the brain is also visible. The machine translates such changes into voltage signals that control external devices. In this way, the train switch is controlled.





The project plans to achieve a more complex decryption of changes in the functioning of the human brain. Receiving execution signals will be the pinnacle of the development of the human-machine interface.

3D desktop scanner

The principle of operation of such a device consists in the use of two cameras, the image from which is formed and compared. With the help of such a scanner, accurate three-dimensional models of the necessary objects are created. They are reflected with the maximum accuracy of various details. Information is transmitted in mathematical, computer and digital form, carries data on the size, shape, color of the scanned element.

The computer controls image settings. All received data is analyzed, and the image appears on the screen already in three-dimensional space.

The essence of this invention is that when the chopsticks are immersed in food, information on the quality of food is reflected on the screen of the gadget on which the necessary application is installed. Invention: nanorobots

Today, many scientists are striving to create nanorobots - machines that can work at the atomic and molecular levels. Such an invention will make it possible to produce molecular materials. It will be possible, for example, to make oxygen or water. Also in the economic sphere, they will be able to create food products, fuel and participate in other processes that ensure human life. Such robots will be able to create themselves.

Nanotechnology is a symbol of the future and one of the vectors of the development of civilization. Their use is possible in almost all areas of human life.

In medicine, the appearance of nanorobots will lead to a complete cure of the human body. They can be launched into the body. Correctly programmed machines will begin to destroy viruses and other harmful substances inside the body. Using nanotechnology, you can give a beautiful and healthy look to human skin.

In ecology, electronic machines will help stop pollution of the planet. With their help, it will be possible to purify water, air and other vital sources of human health.

Such unusual inventions of mankind can help in solving complex problems, but at the moment the development is at the research stage.

To date, some components of future molecular machines have been created, various conferences are held on the issue of creating nanorobots.





There are primitive prototypes of future cars. In 2010, DNA-based molecular machines that can travel in space were first shown.

The world of nanotechnology does not stand still, and perhaps the 21st century will still be called the century in which the most unusual inventions will appear.
