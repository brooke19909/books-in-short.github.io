Museum of entertaining science "Experimentus", Chelyabinsk: address, exhibits, reviews

From the history of the museum

The Experimentus Museum of Entertaining Science in Chelyabinsk is still very young. He first opened his doors at the end of July 2011. The event took place on Ordzhonikidze Street. But, despite this, the museum became very popular not only in Chelyabinsk, but also in Russia and even beyond its borders.

Since the opening day, the museum has been visited by guests from the United Arab Emirates and the USA, Canada and Germany, Australia and Spain, Israel and France, Slovakia and Puerto Rico, Belarus and Italy, Turkey and Kazakhstan, England and China, the Netherlands and Austria.

What is the museum created for?

The activities of the Experimentus Museum are aimed at popularizing science. Its main goal is to clearly demonstrate that science is interesting and entertaining. Here they will prove to you that chemistry, physics, biology and other related sciences can cause genuine interest in kids, who in these walls feel themselves to be real scientists, and adults, and sometimes even older people.



Museum Description

An unusual institution located on Ordzhonikidze Street is located in a two-story building. The exposition occupies three large halls. A careful study of the exhibits will require at least three hours, however, especially interested visitors stay here for a whole day.

A wind tunnel, a wheel with which you can get electricity, a parabolic antenna, a magnetic labyrinth - all these exhibits not only make a huge impression, but also train. In the Experimentus Museum you can see the real tornado, see on the thermal imager screen how the human body radiates heat waves and gives off heat, easily lift a huge, twenty-four kilogram weight with two fingers, sit on a chair made of nails and climb twenty centimeters from floor surface.



And if you do not just want to understand the essence of the phenomena, you will be able to experiment yourself, of course, with the help of an instructor. For example, you can build an airplane model or learn how to tie knots in a variety of ways. Although the Experimentus Museum is intended for children, most exhibits are delighted by adult visitors.

Excursions

For an hour in the "Experimentus" (Chelyabinsk) guided tours are conducted. After completion, free time (hour) is provided during which the group can independently get acquainted with the exhibits and conduct experiments.

For group visits there is a discount of fifty rubles per ticket. For a group of children of fifteen people, one adult must be present, which is free of charge.



In general, Experimentus in Chelyabinsk is a great place to conduct practical classes in physics and chemistry. Visiting it, both children and adults learn a lot of interesting things, get vivid impressions.

Quest tour

This is a slightly different tour, it is held in a new format. The cunning anti-professor Charlatanus hid a special device creating black holes in the museum‚Äôs laboratory. Tourists have only an hour to find it and render it harmless, otherwise a huge black hole will appear at the place where the museum is located and will drag the whole city into itself.

How is the quest excursion going?

At first, the group takes the usual, but somewhat shortened tour (30-40 minutes), after which the quest takes place in the museum. After its completion, all children will receive souvenirs from the Experimentus.



Visiting Rules

The Experimentus in Chelyabinsk is an unusual place where serious scientific phenomena are presented in an easy accessible form. Nevertheless, it is a museum, so its management asks guests to observe a number of fairly simple rules.

  1. It is allowed and even recommended in the museum to touch all exhibits without exception: turn on, press, move, disassemble, experiment.
  2. Take pictures in the Experimentus. Here you get unique shots.
  3. Carefully read all explanatory plates at exhibits.
  4. Carry out the experiments carefully.
  5. Careless attitude to exhibits leads to their breakdown. Moreover, they can not always be repaired, and the manufacture of some of them takes a lot of time and is also very expensive.
  6. Feel free to ask questions.
  7. Respect the work of museum staff. It is not easy to conduct excursions both physically and morally.
  8. Share your ideas. In the museum more than once there have been situations when guests prompted a solution to a problem.

What should not be done in a museum?

  • Screaming, running, swearing.
  • To be in a state of drug or alcohol intoxication.
  • Deliberately disable exhibits.
  • To smoke.
  • To drink or eat. When you get hungry, you can go to a nearby cafe and have a bite to eat, and then return to the museum.
  • To carry flammable, explosive, flammable, corrosive and poisonous substances, piercing and cutting objects.
  • Bring pets.

"Experimentus" in Chelyabinsk: the price of entrance tickets

At the entrance to the museum, visitors are invited to collect a Rubik's cube. Anyone who successfully copes with this puzzle is given a discount on a ticket. The entrance ticket costs four hundred rubles, for children from three to seventeen years old, full-time students, retirees, as well as visitors with disabilities, the ticket will cost three hundred rubles, children under three years old visit the museum for free.

A family ticket for two adults and two children (upon presentation of a passport) costs one thousand three hundred rubles, for large families - one thousand four hundred rubles. Excursion programs are held every hour, the cost of the excursion is included in the price of the entrance ticket.

Chelyabinsk, "Experimentus": address, how to get?

The museum is located at: st. Ordzhonikidze, 58 A. You can get here by any public transport to the stop "Opera Theater".
