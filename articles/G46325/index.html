Base "Gulfstream" (Inflow): features

Recently, holidays off the Black Sea coast are becoming more and more popular. This trend is associated with the affordable cost of such a trip and the small distance to the resorts. Gulfstream (Zatoka) - recreation center 60 km from Odessa. On its territory there are more than 100 small houses, 2 cottages and a motor ship converted to hotel rooms.

Base territory

The complex covers a fairly large area off the coast. The entire territory of the recreation center is fenced with a high fence and is guarded around the clock. Tourists can feel completely safe being at the Gulf Stream (Zatoka) base . Responses of vacationers even indicate that the guards do not let out unaccompanied children out of the complex.





The base staff strictly monitors the cleanliness near the houses. Cleaning is done several times a day. In the early morning, before the guests wake up, the quality of the beach is also put in order.

On the territory there are many flower beds with plantings of a large number of flowers. A sufficient number of trees at the base makes it possible to hide in the shade from the scorching summer sun in the heat.

Room Features

The Gulf Stream (Zatoka) has various living conditions in houses. Basically, the rooms are designed for 2-3-4-5-7 people in each. Small rooms are located in the houses and on the "boat". In these rooms the necessary conditions for living are created:

  • cold and hot water;
  • TV;
  • furniture;
  • refrigerator.

More guests can rent cozy rooms in cottages. There, vacationers are given the opportunity to cook their own meals. These rooms have a lounge and a large balcony with sea views. Superior rooms also have air conditioning. Many houses have a patio with gazebos.

Particular attention should be paid to the original idea - the redevelopment of a decommissioned small ship for rooms. For guests, comfortable cabins with everything you need are presented.





Entertainment

The recreation center "Gulfstream" (Zatoka) offers its vacationers an interesting entertainment program and the opportunity to play active sports. There is a playground on the territory where in the evenings a large number of children gather from the complex for joint games.

Adults are given the opportunity to actively spend time on the volleyball court or on the field for futsal. Various events are held throughout the day with the help of professional animators.

In the evening, a fun disco with contests and prizes takes place on the beach. Children are involved in various games and quests. In the morning, the animators charge on the beach for all vacationers. For adults, special exercises in water have been developed.

Bars and restaurants are located near the recreation center. In the daytime, various attractions operate on the beach:

  • trampolines;
  • soft slides;
  • riding on bananas and tablets.

A large sandy beach makes it possible to accommodate all tourists without crowding and inconvenience. Here you can freely install small pools for babies who are afraid to go into the sea.





On the beaches there is a trade in traditional marine products: shrimp, corn, homemade cakes and souvenirs.

Infrastructure and Food

The presence of a medical office is an undoubted advantage of the Gulf Stream complex. Zatoka is a resort area, often visited by large companies and families. Therefore, the presence at the base of a medical professional is essential.

Near the territory of the complex there is a large secure parking. The base has a cinema where you can relax and unwind while watching a movie or cartoons. Throughout the territory there are picnic places with barbecue facilities and gazebos.

There is a large dining room, which offers 3 meals a day. According to tourists, the menu has a large number of fruits and vegetables. During all meals, meat or fish dishes are provided.





Meals can be ordered for the entire period of stay on vacation or daily upon the liked dishes. For lunch, several food options are available.

Large cottages have equipped kitchens. In this case, vacationers can fully or partially prepare food for themselves throughout the day. Zatoka (the Gulf Stream base) is famous for delicious homemade food in small cafes and restaurants. They can choose delicious dishes for a small price.
