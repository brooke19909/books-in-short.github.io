Needlework lessons. How to knit a plaid?

Many, even experienced, needlewomen think that knitting a plaid is very difficult. Not at all. Of course, the work takes a lot of time, but the execution technology itself is quite simple. This article provides information on how to knit a plaid with knitting needles. For beginners, this article is just a godsend. Here you can read about the necessary materials for a knitted blanket and how to make it.





Preparatory stage

To make a knitted blanket turn out to be warm and soft, choose yarn with wool or wool. The packaging should be labeled "peeled" or "baby." This means that the product knitted from such threads will not prick and cause skin irritation. Summer plaid is made from natural cotton or bamboo yarn. Knitting needles according to the thickness of the thread. The skeletal label shows the recommended tool number for use.

What are the ways to perform knitting plaid?

Hand-knitted blankets can be made with a single piece of fabric. But in this way it is convenient to make a children's plaid, the width of which is 1-1.5 meters. Larger products cannot be knitted using this method, since there are no knitting needles with a very long fishing line.

A large plaid is knitted with square, rectangular elements or long strips, which are then combined into a single fabric.

Handmade baby blanket

For beginner needlewomen, we recommend that you first learn how to knit a children's plaid with knitting needles. For work, you will need 500 grams of children's yarn and knitting needle number 4. Dial 195 stitches and knit 330 rows (75-80 centimeters) with any pattern. Then close the loops, cut the thread. The product can additionally be crocheted with the pattern "Crab step". This will strengthen the structure of the edges and prevent the plaid from stretching. As a decor for a baby blanket, you can use small bows, embroidery, textile applications.



How to knit a plaid motif?

Simple in execution, but very beautiful in appearance, a blanket is obtained made of square elements in hosiery and garter stitch. The brighter and more varied in color motives, the more original and elegant the product will look. But when choosing yarn, keep in mind that the threads in all skeins should be the same thickness.

So, we knit a motive. Type 30 stitches on the knitting needles. Knit the fabric in straight and reverse rows only with front loops until its length is 15 cm. Close the loops. As a result, you should get a square measuring 15 x 15 centimeters, made by garter stitch. Draw another motive in the same way, only with facial smoothness (hosiery). Link the required number of motives. Their quantity depends on what size you wish to receive a plaid. When all the squares are connected, proceed to join them. This process can be performed by needle stitching or crocheting. Crochet the edges .



If you want the interior of your house to radiate warmth and comfort, knit a blanket and lay it on a chair or sofa. This handmade product will decorate your home, bring bright notes and positive energy to it.
