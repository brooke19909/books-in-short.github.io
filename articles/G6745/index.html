Yankee Bar, Ulyanovsk: how to get there? Reviews and photos

The Yankee Grill Bar in Ulyanovsk is a fairly well-known establishment not only within the city, but also in some localities located nearby. People of different age categories love visiting it. As visitors themselves note in their reviews of Yankee Bar (Ulyanovsk), a relaxed atmosphere reigns here throughout the day, which has a good rest in the company of work colleagues, family or old friends.

Interior

Inside, the restaurant consists of one large hall, which, according to many of its guests, can be an ideal venue for any kind of event.

Many visitors say that the interior of the hall is made in a kind of
a masculine style in which there is a combination of dark brown and black colors, as well as materials such as wood and leather. Many details that are present in the interior have a rather massive appearance, which gives the whole design a touch of brutality.





Here, along one of the walls, there is a large bar counter, where specialty beers are bottled, as well as cocktails and drinks. Near it are tall chairs made of dark wood. The rack itself was made to order. Guests who come to Yankee Bar (Ulyanovsk) can comfortably sit at large lacquered tables made of dark wood, on soft leather armchairs and sofas. In the warm season, the establishment has a summer terrace where visitors can sit in the fresh air and enjoy views of the surroundings. Brown wicker furniture is installed on it.



Kitchen

The restaurant offers gourmets a huge selection of dishes prepared in the best traditions of American, European and Mexican cuisines. There is a rather varied menu, where guests can choose a dish for every taste, which will help the kind waiters.

The restaurant offers a wide selection of appetizers (beef barbecue, boiled pork with spicy horseradish, tortilla with chicken, chicken quesadilla, turkey skewers, baked rolls with salmon) and salads (Chinatown, Alaska, California with shrimps, with smoked suluguni and chicken), among which there are several warm options (potato sashimi from salmon, with squid, from turkey). The first on the Yankee Bar menu (Ulyanovsk) offers hot soups ( Manhattan chowder , Nevada, chicken, mushroom noodle soup). The guests of the institution pay special attention to the assortment of steaks ("Cowboy Marlboro", "Yankee", "New York").

Among the hot dishes in the restaurant, both fish and meat are served. Among them, visitors especially like sharp pork ribs, pork gratin, pasta with turkey meatballs, turkey chili, and steamed salmon.



As for the sweet tooth, the institution has a large list of desserts for them, including several types of cakes (brownie, berry cheesecake, Semilfredo, tiramisu), fruits, delicate creme brulee, as well as branded ice cream, which is prepared according to a special prescription.

Bar

The list of alcohol at Yankee Bar (Ulyanovsk) is pretty good. It contains several varieties of good vodka, cognac, whiskey, as well as tequila. In addition, aperitifs and champagne are in great demand here. The wine list offers several types of this drink brought from different countries.

As for alcohol-free drinks, branded soft drinks, as well as juice, are widely popular among them. The establishment has several types of tea and coffee.

If desired, vacationers can order cocktails at the Yankee Bar (Ulyanovsk), among which there are both alcoholic and non-alcoholic ones.
