Fixed assets of the enterprise is a complex structure





Fixed assets of the enterprise - this is a specific mapping of means of labor, expressed in value terms. The most important feature, which is rightfully considered decisive, is the transfer of the value of public funds to the output.

PF assessment methodology

Such an action can be carried out in several ways: gradually over a certain number of production cycles or parts in the course of equipment wear. The economic essence of the concept under consideration is the constant movement of financial resources, the form of which changes in a cycle: monetary-natural-commodity-monetary. Thus, we can say that the fixed assets of the enterprise are a kind of means of labor that take part in many production cycles. Such actions allow under normal economic conditions to contribute to the full recovery of the fixed assets, providing, in turn, the possibility of constant updating of the equipment park. Based on the foregoing, it can be concluded that a simple replenishment of the depreciation fund leads to the formation of a new system of implements of equal cost to retirees. However, to improve the technological process, additional capital investments are necessary, which can be attracted from the profit received, contributions of investors, loans and credits, as well as the issue of securities.

At present, the fixed assets of the enterprise are a rather complex economic system. It allows for accounting and planning of resource allocation.
