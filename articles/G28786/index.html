Sanya Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 *: description

The hotel building is only 10 minutes walk to Dadonghai Bay. Many rooms overlook the picturesque bay. Guests have easy access to the hotel as the island's airport, Sanya Phoenix, is 15.4 km away. Entering the hotel, you find yourself in the palace of some rich sheikh. A huge floor chandelier, beautiful wall decoration and spacious hall impresses visitors at first sight.





The hotel Sanya Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 * has its own covered parking + guarded parking for cars on the street. For tourists arriving by electric vehicles, there are battery charging stations.
