Such a different knitting for girls 1-2 years knitting

Little children always look surprisingly cute, no matter what clothes they are dressed in. And parents try to dress their babies smartly and beautifully always. Many types of needlework allow you to create amazing things for kids. For example, knitting for girls 1-2 years old with knitting needles is a universal opportunity to dress a child both in winter and in summer.

Unpretentious beauty

A knitted blouse for a girl with knitting needles can become an outfit at any time of the year. How, for example, to knit a summer outfit? If you take beautiful natural yarn, cotton or linen, of pure light shades, and use a simple knitting, for example, knitting, in a knitting, then the blouse for the crumb will turn out unpretentious, but very cute. So, you need:

  • 100% natural cotton yarn. A skein weighing 50 grams includes 110 meters of thread;
  • knitting needles No. 3 and No. 4;
  • 1 large button.

Payment

Knitting for girls 1-2 years old with knitting needles should begin with the calculation of loops so as not to make a mistake with the size. The selected yarn is knitted with a sample measuring 20x20 loops + 2 edging loops. Then, the length of the fabric obtained is measured and the number of loops per 1 centimeter is calculated. The size of the child calculates the required number of loops on the product as a whole, and then on the front and back.

Scheme of work

For thinner knitting needles (No. 3) you need to dial 74 loops for the back. Knit 3 rows of garter stitch. Starting to knit the 4th row, change the knitting needles to number 4. And, including the number of knitting needles, knit 39 rows with the front stitch, making such reductions: on each side, reduce 1 chain in the 7th row, in the 23rd and the 39th row. 68 stitches should remain on the knitting needles. Continue to knit until the height of the part reaches 20 centimeters. Next you need to arrange raglan. To do this, close 4 loops at the beginning of the next front, and then the next back row. The next two rows are connected without changes. Then knit 14 rows, making reductions in 1 chain first in the 1st row, and then in every second. There must be 46 stitches on the knitting needles that need to be removed on a knitting pin.





Shelves for a blouse

Tie the shelves for a simple blouse is also simple, because knitting for girls 1-2 years with needles allows you to make parts in a matter of minutes. For the left shelf on knitting needles No. 3, 40 stitches are typed. As well as on the back, the first three rows are knitted only with facial loops. Then the knitting needles are replaced with thicker knitting needles, and knitting continues with the front stitch, with the exception of 6 stitches on the inside, which are knitted with the wrong stitch. These loops fit on the entire shelf to the start of the neck. Decreases for the bevels on the sides are done in the same way as on the back.

Raglan fits at the same height with the back, but at the same time the neck also fits. So, for raglans, close the first 4 loops. Then knit 3 rows of facial. Now in the next front row, first reduce 1 eyelet = 32 eyelets remains. In the wrong row, remove 6 loops of the wrong side of the pin. Then also first reduce 1 loop = 25 loops. Further, the raglan is knotted as for the back, and for the neck, 5 loops should be closed in each second row until 15 loops remain on the knitting needles. Then reduce 2 loops in each 2 row, until 2 loops remain on the knitting needles at the height of the back. The thread must be cut off, remove the loops on it and tighten. The right shelf fits in the same way.





Mittens

A blouse for a girl with knitting needles, even for the summer, will not do without gloves. To work on them, you need to dial 46 stitches for thinner knitting needles. Knit 2 rows of garter stitch, and then change the needles to thicker ones - No. 4. Since the summer blouse has short sleeves, the raglan begins immediately after the cuff. To knit it, it is necessary to close 4 loops at the beginning of the facial and 4 loops at the beginning of the wrong rows. 2 rows to knit straight. Knit the next 14 rows, reducing 1 chain at the end of each row. There must be 24 loops on the knitting needles that must be removed with a pin.





Blouse assembly

Moisten all parts, smooth them on a flat surface and allow to dry. Then sew raglan lines. For the neck, transfer the loops of all the details to knitting needle No. 3 in this order: 5 loops of the strap (knit 2 loops at the neck together), dial 19 loops along the edge of the neck, 24 loops of the sleeve, 46 loops of the back, 24 loops of the second sleeve, dial from the edge of the neck 19 loops and again 5 loops of the strap (2 loops at the neck knit together). There must be 144 stitches on the knitting needles. 5 rows of knit garter stitch.

Knit the next row of faces as follows: 9 stitches, 2 together, * 3 stitches, 2 knit *, 9 stitches. From * to * repeat 25 times. There are 118 stitches left. Add 3 rows of garter stitch. Then knit a row in this way: * 1 front loop, 2 yarn over *, repeat throughout the row.

The next row is knitted with facial loops, but all the crochets go down without knitting. Elongated loops are obtained. Then again add 4 rows of garter stitch. In the 5th row, reductions are made: 14 loops, 2 together, * 1 loop, 2 together *, 15 loops. From * to * repeat 29 times. There are 88 stitches left, knit 4 rows and close. Sew side seams and sleeve seams. Make a buttonhole and sew a button. If 5-6 stitches are provided directly on the bar, and the sleeves are made long, then the blouse from summer will turn into a warm part of the wardrobe.

Antique outfit? And no!

Kids need to dress up in comfort. A blouse, badlon, T-shirt, and on top of a sundress - a practical and comfortable outfit. Knitting a sarafan with knitting needles for girls will create such a versatile wardrobe item for a little fashionista.

The presented model is knitted from cotton yarn, in which a 50-gram skein contains 180 meters of thread. Knitting needles for such yarn are taken No. 3. The skirt of a sarafan is knitted with one canvas for the back and front. For her, you need to dial 216 stitches on the knitting needles. Since the number of loops is large enough, it is best to choose knitting needles with fishing line. Knitting is carried out by openwork according to the scheme.





At a skirt height of 29-31 centimeters, 68 loops should be reduced evenly. The next 3 rows should only be knitted with the front stitch. Mark with a colored thread each 74 loop - the sides of the sundress. Knit 3 centimeters with the front stitch, and then knit the shelves and back separately, removing 1 loop from the marks in one direction and the other. The front and back will have 72 loops. For armholes, close the backs on both sides: 1 time for 3 loops, 1 time for 2 loops, 3 times for 1 loop = 56 loops. Knit directly along the height of the back 40-42 centimeters. Close the middle 20 loops, for the neck close from the inside 1 time 2 loops. 17 loops of a shoulder to remove on a pin. The second shoulder of the back is knitted the same way.

For the front of the armhole, knit similarly to the back, but begin to knit the neck at a height of 34-35 centimeters from the edge. Close the middle two loops, and for the bevel of the neck, reduce 1 loop in each 2 rows until 17 loops remain on the knitting needles. The second half of the front is connected similarly.

Sew the shoulders. If the skirt is knitted in a circle, then nothing needs to be done, but if it is not tightly circular, then sew the seam of the skirt. The edges of the armhole and neck are crocheted or knitted, having typed the desired number of loops and knitting 3 rows of garter stitch.
