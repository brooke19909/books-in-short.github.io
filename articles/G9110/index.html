Therapeutic measures

"Kirillovka" - a sanatorium with a wide wellness base. Arriving at the sanatorium, each patient undergoes a consultation with specialists and a full laboratory examination. The doctor prescribes a number of diagnostic measures: ultrasound, X-ray, ECG and more. Narrow-profile doctors work: neuropathologist, gastroenterologist, gynecologist, ophthalmologist, otolaryngologist. A pediatrician's office has been opened to receive children.





The mandatory list of the treatment base includes natural factors: mineral water intake, sodium chloride baths, irrigation, mud applications and other procedures. What diseases are treated in the Kirillovka health resort?

The sanatorium is pleased to offer qualified care to patients with various pathologies. Effective therapy is carried out for people with nervous diseases, including radiculitis, polyneuritis. Persons with musculoskeletal diseases are accepted, these include:

  • arthritis;
  • osteochondrosis;
  • polyarthritis;
  • bursitis;
  • myositis.

In the complex, you can take a rehabilitation course after severe injuries and operations. Qualified doctors will help cure digestive disorders and disorders. Also, in the health resort, they treat the diseases of the gallbladder, liver and ENT organs.

An individual course of treatment is indicated for women with gynecological pathologies and infertility. They visit a wellness hostel with a diagnosis of prostatitis, psoriasis, and diabetes mellitus.
