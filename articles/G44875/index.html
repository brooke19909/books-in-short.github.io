Modem "MTS Connect": modems, drivers, tariffs, installation and configuration

We all know that mobile operators often, in addition to basic telecommunication services, also promote technological products. This can be, for example, mobile phones or communication modems, issued under the order of the operator company and equipped with its logo.

We will describe an example of such a move in today's article. It will be about what the MTS Connect modem is, what advantages the service package has, what are its features, how to configure the modem and use the Internet with its help.

Mobile Internet from MTS





To begin with, almost all operators provide mobile Internet services through their network, using dedicated frequencies to transmit a signal in 3G or 4G connection format. MTS is no exception. At the same time, the company already has a developed network of tariffs, which millions of subscribers use successfully and for a long time.

In addition to the fact that the signal from the provider is well distributed throughout the coverage area, the company can also be praised for the well-developed tariffing of services, thanks to which you can enjoy the capabilities of mobile Internet connection at an affordable price for most users.

Internet + modem

In addition to affordable tariffs, another advantageous offer from the company is a comprehensive package of services, including a device for receiving a signal (modem), connected to the MTS network. Due to the fact that the company, together with the buyer of the modem, also receives a regular user with its services, the cost of such a modem is significantly reduced. In particular, now there is even a constant discount that allows you to purchase a modem along with a customized package for the price of 1 ruble. Obviously, this cannot but attract users and does not force them to purchase the MTS Connect modem. On what conditions mobile Internet services are offered to such subscribers, we will tell further.





3G or 4G tariffs

The MTS company in the set has several tariff plans designed for one or more devices. These are plans for working on a phone, tablet, and tariffs for sharing on a Wi-Fi router that will transmit a signal to other gadgets. Each of the plans assumes the presence of a certain amount of data available to the user for spending on the mobile Internet network for a certain fee.

Now let's try to figure out what settings of the MTS Connect modem you need to make in order to start using it immediately after purchase.

Customization





To work with the device, drivers for the MTS Connect modem are needed. You can download them on the MTS official website, after selecting the model and brand of the device. This is due to the fact that each of the modems has individual software.

After the drivers are loaded, we also need a program for the modem compatible with MTS Connect. With it, access to the network will be controlled - by connecting and disconnecting the device, it will be possible to change some settings necessary for the modem to work.

It is important not to forget that as new versions of the firmware become available, the device will need to be updated. This is also done using software that can be found on the MTS website.

Opportunities

You can use your MTS Connect modem as you like. The tariffs show that with it there is an opportunity not only to connect the Internet connection to your laptop, which will allow you to work in absolutely normal mode, as if we are talking about stationary network access.





Firstly, this is due to the 4G data transfer format. Unlike third-generation communications, it provides faster device operation. For example, this modem is capable of transmitting data at a speed of 100 Mbps. Thanks to this indicator, you can download movies and TV shows in minutes, not to mention watching less bulky videos.

Secondly, the huge advantage of the VIP tariff plan, which connects for 14 days immediately after the purchase of the device, is unlimited Internet at night and 30 GB of traffic for use during the day. This allows you to leave downloading large files while you sleep, with the enormous speed that 4G guarantees.

Thirdly, you can purchase an additional Wi-Fi router and distribute the Internet to several devices at the same time. So you can, for example, in addition to a personal computer, synchronize the operation of the phone and tablet.
