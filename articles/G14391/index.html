Ski slope CSKA

In Moscow in Filevsky park is the complex of the CSKA school. There are two tracks here - the training and the main (250 meters), the height difference on them is about 50 meters.

The complex is equipped with all the necessary facilities - snow-sealing equipment, snow cannons, 3 lifts, as well as excellent lighting for evening skiing.

A cozy atmosphere, a professional school of instructors, reasonable prices, affordability - all this attracts many outdoor enthusiasts.

Sparrow Hills

The ski slope on the Sparrow Hills has been opened relatively recently. The complex is equipped with three tracks for snowboarders and skiers with lengths from 170 meters to 255, as well as a ski jump. Climb to the top of the slope using a chairlift or 2 tow ropes.



There is an opportunity to engage with an instructor both in a group and individually. Also in the park there are two cross-country ski tracks and a tubing track. Lighting in the evening is organized.

On Sparrow Hills there are also rental centers for all equipment. Here you can warm up over a cup of hot tea in one of the 4 cafes.

The ski slopes of the Sparrow Hills have a convenient and affordable location for Muscovites.
