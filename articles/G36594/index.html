Recreation facilities on Lake Kumkul - description of services

Among the picturesque natural landscapes near the city of Chelyabinsk lies Lake Kumkul. Recreation facilities (including the "Seagull") will amaze you with their hospitality. The pristine nature, sandy coast and excellent fishing attract both locals and tourists. You can come here with tents or book a room, a house on the territory of one of the many recreation centers.

The recreation center "Village Chaykino" (formerly "The Seagull")

A great place to listen to the silence and soft whisper of lake waves. Such recreation centers on Lake Kumkul are perfect for a relaxing leisure time. Opening hours: from May to October. Accommodation: two-story building - four-bed rooms (two rooms for two people). The unit is equipped with a refrigerator and an electric stove. Bed linen and utensils are provided. Lodges for three or four people. There are dishes, kettle, stove, refrigerator. Near the house there is a table with benches and barbecue. Self-catering.

Infrastructure:

  • Free parking.
  • Good wood sauna.
  • Children playground.

Entertainment:

  • fishing;
  • tennis;
  • billiards;
  • beach.

Recreation facilities on Kumkul Lake differ in cost, so this issue is relevant for every tourist. Prices for accommodation (per person per day): weekdays - from 350 rubles, weekends - from 500. Free accommodation for children up to five years (without a seat), 50% discount for children up to nine years. Discounts apply for stays of more than seven days.



Water park "Kum-kul"

This is a center for outdoor activities and entertainment! Adrenaline rush and sports excitement are given to tourists in abundance. Operating mode: year-round. Accommodation: two-room and one-room suites for four people. Entrance hall with dining area. The room has a bathroom. Refrigerator, kettle, stove, dishes, TV are available in the room. Junior Suite differs from Suite in the absence of amenities in the room. Double houses, dining area, mini fridge. Self-catering. There is a cafe on site.

Infrastructure:

  • parking;
  • pier;
  • baths;
  • beach;
  • picnic area;
  • gazebo.

Entertainment:

  • trampoline;
  • waterslides;
  • climbing wall;
  • fishing;
  • catamarans;
  • boats;
  • Children's swimming pool;
  • jet ski;
  • banana.

It is this diversity of spheres that the recreation centers on Lake Kumkul differ in. Water park - a luxury that not all complexes can afford. Prices for accommodation (per day for a house): luxury - from 4 thousand rubles., Junior suite - from 3 thousand rubles., Houses without amenities from 1500 rubles. Prices should be clarified in advance.

The recreation center "Forest cottage"

Bright birch grove, clean air and comfort at affordable prices will welcome guests in the territory of the "Forest cottage". Operating mode: year-round. Accommodation: recreation centers on Lake Kumkul, like this one, can offer a double house. They have a microwave, kettle, TV, refrigerator, dishes. Toilet and shower in the room. There is also a two-room cottage with a living room and a bedroom for 4-6 people. It is equipped with a kettle, microwave, upholstered furniture, TV, refrigerator, dishes. Room amenities. One-room two-story house is designed for four people. There is a kettle, dishes, refrigerator.

Meals: independent. There is a dining room at the base. Infrastructure: arbors, beach, bathhouse, parking (free), children's territory. Attractions: Russian billiards, table tennis, badminton.

Prices for accommodation (per day): from 1500 rubles. Discounts apply for stays of 5 days or more.
