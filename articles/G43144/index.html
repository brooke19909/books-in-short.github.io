Screen

The next would like to describe the display of the device, its quality and technical parameters. What Samsung seriously decided to save on when releasing this model was the processor and the hardware as a whole. The clock frequency of each oscillates at 1.2 GHz, however, these numbers do not say enough about how slow this smartphone is.





As users describe their problems, the phone must be reset to the factory settings after several months of use. Only in this way, at least, can it be somehow helped. Otherwise, the device starts to hang very much, shows slow operation even with simple applications, and a software performance test through AnTuTu cannot pass at all due to a constant error.

Battery

An important issue in any device is its autonomy - the ability to work as long as possible without additional charge. As the technical specifications show, Samsung J1 smartphones are equipped with an 1850 mAh battery.

At first glance, this may seem like a small indicator, but you should not think so. We are talking about, do not forget, the budget model with a simple processor and TFT-screen. Therefore, the energy consumption here is relatively small, which allows you to keep the device in a healthy state for a sufficiently long period of time. At the very least, reviews confirm the 9-10 hours of working on the Internet stated in the specifications.

It is worth noting that the battery here is removable, so it can be replaced if necessary or removed if the device stops responding to commands.
