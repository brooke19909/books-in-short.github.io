Reviews of tourists: Cuba - "back to the USSR" or an incendiary vacation?

Quite often you hear that a trip to Cuba is a return to the Soviet Union. However, not everyone agrees with this. How many of us remember Soviet childhood, and especially sea vacations? If so, then reviews of tourists tell about a completely different vacation. Cuba is not the socialism we are used to. This is an eternal spring, turning into summer (what else can I call the constant plus 25 Celsius?), This is sunbathing on the white sand on the ocean to the soothing sound of the waves, this is dancing until you fall under the incendiary motifs “a la Latino”, these are stately blacks boys and girls mulattoes, this is a peculiar spirit of the "island of Freedom." All this is Cuba. Reviews of tourists in 2013 about the rest will be summarized in this article.



Cuba, of course, is not a rich country, and you will not find brilliance, luxury and gloss there. But it has a wonderful intimate atmosphere for relaxation, which is felt immediately upon arrival. There is no huge social stratification, there are not a large number of poor and homeless people, people are clean and neatly dressed, young people are cheerful, wears fashionable clothes, island residents love to sing and have fun. All this is confirmed by the reviews of tourists. Cuba begins for them, of course, with Havana. The old quarters of this city ​​with its colonial architecture have a special charm. Experienced tourists are advised to take older hotels in the center of the capital than modern and comfortable ones on the outskirts, because travel to the main streets will take too much time. In addition, living in such hotels (they are also called thematic) you can understand the spirit and history of the country.



What else are reviews of tourists telling us? Cuba often gives the impression of a small country (if you look at the map), and therefore beginners think that they only have one day to explore Havana. How wrong they are! One day is enough for a regular sightseeing tour accompanied by a guide. People who have been on such a trip are highly advised to climb a skyscraper near Revolution Square. It is vying to recommend almost all the reviews of tourists. All of Cuba, of course, will not be visible, but Havana is for sure. And after such an excursion for another two days you can just walk around the city and see museums. And also just sit in a typical Cuban cafe in some old square and relax.



What a beach holiday can recommend to us the reviews of tourists? Cuba boasts such a famous resort as Varadero. A lot of Russians prefer to relax here. Large hotels, huge rooms, spacious balconies, beautiful views. The food is good, even in not very expensive hotels. Of course, the Fives have a very large selection of dishes, but visitors to budget hotels do not suffer either. Well, the Caribbean usually exceeds all expectations. The clear sea, which you can simply admire with admiration - the color of the water changes there several times a day. The temperature is one of the best for swimming. Many say that water is like “fresh milk” - you just don’t want to leave it. And colorful dances on the beaches and in hotels simply can not be described in words! In general, the rest in Cuba, the reviews of tourists are called easy, heavenly and beautiful. When you get here, you return not in Soviet times, but rather in the nineteenth century, where there are beautiful women, bright and strong men, the sea, the sun and no problems. It’s so good here that when leaving, some even cry.
