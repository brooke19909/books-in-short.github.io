In legal science there is such a discipline as the theory of the state, law. The functions of the state are studied by future connoisseurs of the law along with various codes and documents regulating public life.

Fundamental principles of the theory

There are not so many sections in jurisprudence as important as the theory of the state, law. The functions of the state are the key and most complex subject of study for lawyers. They must be theoretically comprehended in order to improve the management of social processes. This is one of the objective needs of modern legal science. Today, this discipline is the foundation for the legal provision of freedoms and human rights.

The theory of state and law is especially important in Russia. The functions of the state in our country continue to be established after the rejection of socialism. Exactly the same processes can be observed in other post-Soviet societies that survived the fall of communism. In this vast space, there is a transition from an administrative-command to a market economy. The rule of law and civil society are gradually being created. Transformations of this kind cannot succeed without qualified specialists. They should know what the theory of the state is, law. The functions of the state are only determined and honed. If professional lawyers are engaged in this, the process is successful and painless.





Main functions

The scientific concept of the function of the theory of state and law comes from its fundamental nature. This discipline performs several important public missions. The first and starting is the ontological function. Its name is a reference to the doctrine of being, in which its laws and structure are studied. The theory, performing this function, is trying to find out how the state and law appeared, what these concepts are today, what are their prospects in the future.

The next destination is epistemological. In this spectrum, the theory of state and law performs the functions of developing legal knowledge. For this, various techniques and theoretical ideas are developed. They are necessary for the precise definition and fixing of various aspects of jurisdiction, for example, the laws by which society lives.

The functions of the theory of state and law, as a science, also consist in the fact that this theory is a connecting link between numerous sections of legal knowledge. So in different industries appears logical integrity. The theory generalizes legal and state practice, formulating key principles that are important for the work of all lawyers.





Ideology and Political Struggle

It is important that the subject and functions of the theory of state and law also lie in the formation of the scientific foundation of foreign and domestic state policy. The struggle for power in society at all times has been fierce and intense. Theory helps the state in these conditions to form and develop a system of people management. The highest point of political struggle is power over the country. Science is an important tool for linking together this government and the state machine itself.

Close to the above principle are other functions of the general theory of law and the state. For example, this is the connection of this science and ideology - a system of concepts and ideas, with the help of which the consciousness and worldview of one person, as well as the whole society, is formed. The state and people cannot coexist if there are at least no fundamental identical attitudes (for example, patriotism). Therefore, it is not surprising that when a society loses its own ideological guidelines, it enters into a period of crisis and turmoil. This course can also be called the notorious national idea.





Using theory

If the main functions of the theory of state and law are to update different political systems, then how does science develop new power mechanisms? They are born through knowledge of the experience of previous generations. Humanity over its long history has created many state systems. Some of them have taken root (like democracy), others (ancient despots) have sunk into the past. All of them are analyzed by scientists and specialists.

Useful principles and trends form the basis of new theories and models of government. They become in demand when power changes in a country. If the new government wants to adopt a different political doctrine, then the functions and methods of the theory of state and law help it.

Social practice

Public practice emphasizes the social significance of any teaching. The structure and functions of the theory of state and law also need regular application in public life. The modern world creates all the conditions for this. Firstly, the theory is tested in legal practice, and secondly, in the routine work of power structures. The constant use of different methods leads to the fact that state life falls on a strictly defined scientific basis. Everything is regulated and stipulated in it. If the actions of the institutions of power are of a similar nature, then the life of the state and society will become harmonious and productive.

People organized in groups at the dawn of humanity. The first states appeared several thousand years ago. They have become the most optimal way of coexistence of large masses of the population. Theory, on the other hand, provides clear and clear recipes on how to organize and develop such a state life. Any system stalled in its development sooner or later disappeared from the world theater. So it can be said about many ancient civilizations that died under the onslaught of barbarians, etc.

Any scientific discipline exists at the expense of its object of study. The theory of state and law is no exception in this series. The theory isolates them from this array and studies them separately from other phenomena.

People's ideas about power in their own country are also being investigated. The complex interaction of the state and society affects individual and group political consciousness. The theory makes just these connecting threads the object of its study.
