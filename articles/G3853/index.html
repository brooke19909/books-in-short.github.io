What will be required for manufacturing?

You can make a fortune teller in a matter of minutes. To work, we need:

  • a sheet of white paper;
  • scissors or paper cutter;
  • pen, colored pencils or felt-tip pens.

Detailed manufacturing instructions

So, you have stocked up with everything you need to work and now you can begin to work on making origami from paper.

The fortuneteller will be sure to leave if you follow these tips:

  1. First of all, put a sheet of paper on the table, then take the left corner and lift it up. As a result, you should get a right triangle.
  2. Now take its bottom corner and connect with its left extreme point of the base. The result is a triangle.
  3. If an A4 sheet of paper was used as the starting material, when folding you form an extra rectangle at the top, which should be carefully cut with scissors or a clerical knife. In the end, you can just tear it off with your hands.
  4. Expand the resulting square.
  5. As a result of folding the square, you form two fold lines with a common intersection point. Now add all the corners of the square to its center. You should get a slightly smaller square.
  6. Lay the sheet on the wrong side and fold the square again in the same way as in step 5.
  7. Now bend the figure in half, straighten it, then rotate it 90 degrees and bend it again, straighten it again.
  8. Lay the fortune teller with the other side facing you and carefully open the corners of the small squares. The toy will easily begin to fold over the resulting bends. Corners will begin to open, forming a kind of pocket for fingers.


Our origami fortuneteller is almost ready, it remains only to fill it with the necessary information for it to work.
