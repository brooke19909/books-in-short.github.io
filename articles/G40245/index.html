Tailor's dummy - a convenient tool for sewing

It is quite difficult to acquire a tailor's dummy (or sewing) for personal use, because you need to choose it exclusively "for yourself", that is, for your figure and its individual characteristics. Therefore, before you go shopping, you need to learn a little more how to choose a mannequin and how different models differ. Without this, it will be very difficult to purchase a truly convenient thing to use.





For example, you need to know why all imported models are sliding and how they differ from classical ones. In addition, there are children's, female and male mannequins. Only knowing all these features, you can make the right and thoughtful choice.

The sliding tailor's female mannequin is much cheaper than a professional one, but here's why - you should figure it out. Professional sewing supplies, tools and other equipment are much higher than simple sewing household appliances. Abroad, the classic mannequin, that is, non-retractable, is considered professional equipment, but there are practically no such models on the Russian market.

Fans can use regular class sewing accessories. A tailor's retractable dummy is required, as a rule, for specialized sewing enterprises and ateliers. It can be soft and hard. Solid mannequins are suitable for sewing clothes of any size. This allows them to be used in mass production. These tailor-made sliding mannequins are made of fiberglass, which is covered with knitted fabric in black or light color. The stand is made in the form of a chrome pipe. If it is necessary to sew clothes constantly, then such a mannequin should take place. Incidentally, it is not necessary to buy a new product. You can buy it from your hands and drag the skin.

The benefits of sliding mannequins can be seen even with the naked eye. It is not necessary to have several different products, it is enough to change the dimensions of the existing one with the help of regulators. Such mannequins consist of eight parts, so you can change the size from 42 to 52.





If it is supposed to sew clothes exclusively for their own needs, then it makes sense to purchase a domestic tailor dummy, which, by the way, is much cheaper than sliding. Sliding allows using the taken measurements to completely duplicate the volume of the figure independently of each other. This makes it very convenient and versatile. Since the tailor's dummy has a centimeter scale, this allows you to adjust it in accordance with the growth of the client. That is, you should set the height and measurements in accordance with the necessary, and this is done quickly and easily. The mannequin consists of glue-based nylon, which allows you to pin up sketches with pins and outline them with chalk. Incidentally, on top is a pillow for pins. By the type of figure, mannequins are female, children's and male. When buying this factor, you need to pay special attention, although outwardly they differ, which will not make it possible to confuse them.





There are mannequins attached to the table, but they have a "compressed" shape and are designed for sewing upper jackets. You can use such a device not only for sewing, but also for cleaning products and ironing.
