Primorsky Krai. Houses in Andreevka

If you want to relax on the beach with clean fine sand, unforgettable landscapes and beautiful landscapes, then you should go to the coast of Trinity Bay, in the Primorsky Territory. About the recreation center in Andreevka and houses located almost on the shore of the bay, you can find the most necessary information from this article.

Location

One of the picturesque and attractive places of Primorsky Krai for family vacations is located 220 kilometers from Vladivostok, in the north of Posyet Bay. Tourist camps, consisting of two- and three-story buildings and cozy houses, are located almost in the center of the village of Andreevka.

The famous Far Eastern Marine Reserve is located in close proximity to the village, which allows guests of tourist camps to go there on excursions. The depth of the sea in the bay is 30 meters.

Nature and climate in Andreevka

Over many millennia, a unique natural zone of the Primorsky Territory has been formed. Relict species of plants grow on the coast of the gulf, birds and animals, listed in the Red Book, live.

The climate of the Khasansky district, in which Andreevka is located, is moderately monsoon. For recreation, a stable period of warm weather from the end of June is suitable. Water at this time warms up to 18-20 Â° C, and in shallow water the temperature reaches 28-30 Â° C. August is considered the warmest summer month in Andreevka with a comfortable air temperature of +27 Â° . Nevertheless, the "velvet season" in Andreevka is called autumn. At this time, the water in the sea has a pleasant bathing temperature of 20-22 Â° C.

Tourism

Due to the unique nature and warm sea, the popularity of tourist camps with furnished houses in Andreevka of the Primorsky Territory is growing from year to year. Currently, objects of the tourism industry are growing around the village like mushrooms. The service sector is presented in a large assortment. It includes equipment rental, excursions and various types of sports entertainment. The best bases for family vacations are: "Pearl by the Sea", "Captain Flint", "555", "Fiolent".

They are located in an ecologically clean area, in an area with different species of rare plants. All kinds of water rides are organized on the territory of each base, including ATV rental. For vacationers who love water sports with adrenaline, there is a diving school. For lovers of a quiet holiday with a fishing rod in hand, there is a rental of fishing equipment, and fishing spots are organized. In almost all of the listed bases in Andreevka, the houses are located 20-50 meters from the coast of the bay.

Accommodation

Each of the above recreation centers accommodates guests in two-, three- and four-seater houses. On the territory there are playgrounds with a complex of sports equipment. There are showers, washbasins, toilets in the sanitary unit. In addition to relaxing in a camp site, you can rent a house in Andreevka almost on the shore of the bay.

The recreation center "Pearl by the Sea" has on its territory a 3-storey building with hotel type rooms. The rooms are equipped with digital TV, refrigerators, showers with hot and cold water, toilets with toilets.

At the recreation center "Captain Flint" there are two two-story buildings with double and triple rooms. They are standardly equipped with berths, bedding and necessary household appliances. You can cook both in rooms and on the street, organizing barbecue.

The recreation center "Fiolent" year-round. Located in the center of the village of Andreevka. Here you can not only relax with family and children, but also hold a corporate event. At the base there is a bathhouse, a pool, a cafe in the open air. Near the pool there is a beach area with sun loungers and tables with umbrellas. There is a car park on site.

Near the houses by the sea in Andreevka there are cafes, food shops. Here you can also buy fresh seafood, which this region is so valued for.
