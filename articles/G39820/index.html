Moscow, "Hilton" (Hilton Moscow Leningradskaya Hotel): address, description, reviews

One of the most popular cities in the world is considered to be Moscow. Hilton is an ideal accommodation option both for businessmen and wealthy tourists. The hotel is located at: st. Kalanchevskaya, 21/40.

Short hotel description

The dream city is, of course, Moscow. Hilton will give you a feeling of luxury and comfort, as well as give you the opportunity to experience the atmosphere of a metropolis. Here you can swim in the indoor pool, enjoying the panorama of the city, which becomes especially spectacular at night. You can also visit the gym and relax with a hydromassage.

Five-star hotels in Moscow impress with their chic and luxury. Hilton can be considered one of the most prominent representatives in this category. Particularly noteworthy is its number of rooms. All apartments offer a full range of necessary amenities for the most comfortable stay. It is also impossible not to say about the luxurious interior of the rooms.

A huge number of restaurants can boast of Moscow. Hilton owns one of the best establishments of its kind. His restaurant specializes in Russian cuisine, but there is also a European menu. You can not ignore the huge wine list. All dishes are prepared and decorated to the highest standard.





Where is the hotel situated

Hospitable welcomes all guests Golden-headed Moscow. Hilton is considered one of the best hotels of its kind. It boasts not only a high level of service, but also an incredibly advantageous location. Within walking distance from the hotel there are two metro stations at once: Komsomolskaya and Krasnye Vorota. In just a couple of stops you can see the main attraction of the city - the Kremlin. It is also worth noting the close proximity to the Leningrad railway station, which can be reached on foot in a few minutes.





Price policy

A huge number of accommodation options can offer visitors to the capital of the hotel in Moscow. Prices for accommodation in them range from quite small amounts (about 1000-1500 rubles) to impressive numbers, which contain five or more zeros. This indicator may depend on many factors. The first is, of course, the status and level of service, as well as the availability of accreditation (stardom). In addition, an important indicator is the area in which certain Moscow hotels are located. Prices in the central part of the city can be quite high even if the institution does not differ in high rates of service. Also, the cost may be affected by the availability of related services.

The cost of living at the Hilton Moscow Leningradskaya can also vary depending on several factors. So, for example, during the holidays, as well as during the holidays and in the warm season (tourist period), prices will be the highest. Also, rates can take off if the hotel has too few guests. On average, accommodation in this hotel will cost from 7,000 to 200,000 rubles per night (depending on the category of apartments).





Hotel Guest Apartments

Hilton can offer you a truly chic hotel room in Moscow. You have many options for resettlement, namely:

  • The Hilton corporate room offers comfortable accommodation for two guests. The highlight of these apartments can be considered a huge king size bed. This room is in greatest demand among the newlyweds.
  • The royal room is striking in its size and luxury. It consists of several rooms (including a bedroom, a living room and a dining room), which are decorated in a classic style with a predominance of gold decorative elements.
  • Deluxe apartments have variations with a large double or a pair of twin beds. The room consists of a large bedroom and a compact cozy living room. The bathroom is equipped not only with a shower, but also with a bath.
  • Executive room in its design and equipment almost completely repeats deluxe. Its peculiarity can be considered that living in it gives you the right to visit the closed lobby area, designed specifically for business negotiations.
  • Junior Suite is a large studio in which the bedroom is combined with the living room and kitchen area. You can choose the option with a king size bed or two separate.
  • Corner Suite is located on the upper floors of the hotel. Its highlight is panoramic windows that go to different sides of the world, which provides a large viewing angle. You can enjoy the most beautiful cityscapes while relaxing in king size beds.

Hotel Policies

The Hilton Garden Hotel accepts guests in accordance with a number of established rules, among which are especially worth noting the following:

  • Registration of newly arrived guests is possible from two in the afternoon until midnight, and eviction is carried out at noon;
  • upon arrival, each guest will be charged a deposit (possible both in cash and in non-cash form), which is refunded in full if you have not caused property damage to the hotel;
  • it is possible to check in only those guests who provide identification documents with a current photo;
  • no more than two children under the age of six can stay free of charge (provided that they do not require extra beds);
  • pets are allowed in the hotel, but all details must be agreed upon before arrival (in this case, an additional deposit of 5000 rubles will be charged);
  • if you want to rent connecting rooms, then this requires a preliminary reservation;
  • guests are not allowed to bring guests who were not registered at the reception in the prescribed manner to their rooms;
  • children under 15 years of age are strictly prohibited from visiting the pool without the control of parents or persons acting as guardians;

  • if you want to use the services of a massage therapist, this option must be ordered in advance at the booking stage.

Negative reviews

Despite the fact that the Hilton Hotel (Moscow) has a rather high status, there are some shortcomings in its work. They are fully reflected in negative reviews and customer comments:

  • even if at the time of booking you indicate that the room is for two guests, you will still be put only one set of slippers and a bathrobe (you will have to ask for additional ones yourself);
  • the shaving machine, which is included in the set of free hygiene products, is simply disgusting: it does not cope with bristles and leaves terrible irritation;
  • in the bathroom of some apartments in the Hilton Moscow Leningradskaya hotel there is a stale unpleasant odor (apparently, there are problems with sewage or ventilation);
  • sometimes there is confusion with the rooms (they may not be accommodated in the apartments that were originally booked);
  • the cost of dishes in the Hilton hotel restaurant is too high compared to other similar establishments;
  • despite the fact that, on the whole, the hotel staff is qualified and polite, for some reason the spa staff sometimes behaves defiantly and inappropriately;
  • there are serious problems with the pressure of water on the upper floors (this is unacceptable for a hotel of this level, and it is also worth bearing in mind the fact that the most expensive apartments are located at the top);
  • in many rooms the windows are very dirty, and since the main feature of the hotel can be considered a beautiful view from the window, this drawback is striking even more;
  • despite the fact that the reception is open around the clock, it is almost impossible to get through to it in the evening and at night (employees do not explain the reasons);
  • the elevator is very slow, making the trip to the upper floors seem endless;
  • if you want to order food in your room, then you have to go down to the restaurant.
