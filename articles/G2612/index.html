The natural heritage of Russia is the Novosibirsk region. Reserve and reserves of Siberia

The natural world of the Novosibirsk region is unique. The region is rich in mineral and geological resources. There are vast land areas without which agricultural development is unthinkable, and numerous deposits of rare metals, refractory and refractory clays have also been found . The Novosibirsk Region is rich in rare flora and fauna. The reserve of federal significance will be established this year in these places. Its name is "Barabinsky."





The main goal of creating such objects is to protect the flora and fauna from extinction. To do this, a specific set of rules has been issued, which clearly states that it is strictly forbidden to ruin and harm all living things in nature reserves. Specialists constantly keep records in which the abundance, climatic indicators and other valuable information on endangered species are recorded. Authorities plan to expand reserves in the Novosibirsk region.

According to official figures, about 80 natural protected sites are located in the region. Most of them have a rare landscape design, for which they attach high environmental importance. Protected areas include a number of endangered species of mammals, birds, plants and invertebrates listed in the Red Book.

Unique reserves and reserves of the Novosibirsk region





"The source of the Karasuk river" is a natural complex that has important scientific, environmental and educational, aesthetic and environmental significance. It is located between two villages with the names Chulymskoe and Ordynskoe. The whole territory covers 1200 hectares. It is a unique landscape zone.

Periodically, excursions and research work are conducted here. Local residents are allowed to collect hay, berries and fish (crucian carp, perches, chebakov). Tourists can engage in passive hunting (photography).

Kirzinsky Nature Reserve (Novosibirsk Region)

The reserve is located in the Baraba forest-steppe. It was created with the aim of preserving the wetland environment, reproducing hunting species of fauna and restoring endangered individuals of the forest-steppe landscape. Muskrat, elk, marten, weasel, swans, roe deer and other valuable species of the animal world live here (more than 250 species).





"Dendrological park"

This is a real living laboratory for the study and selection of planting material of rare plants. Unique flora representatives brought from different parts of the globe (China, North America, Japan) are brought here. The most important ecosystem is the natural Dendrological Park (Novosibirsk Region). The reserve attracts not only with exotic plants (securinega, blue spruce, Manchurian walnut), but also with ordinary pine and aspen trees.
