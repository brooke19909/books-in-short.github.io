disadvantages

Unfortunately, everything cannot be cloudless. The Zanussi vacuum cleaner, however, like the apparatus of other brands, has advantages as well as advantages. Let's consider which ones:

  • Noise level. Many people, leaving reviews, noticed that during operation the vacuum cleaner makes a very loud sound. Of course, this is accompanied by certain inconveniences.
  • Garbage container. As mentioned above, the devices are small. Naturally, this affects the dimensions of individual parts. The trash bin will fill up very quickly, and this leads to a decrease in efficiency during operation. Therefore, sometimes you have to stop the vacuum cleaner in order to carry out cleaning.
  • Cord length. This indicator varies from 4 to 8 meters, depending on the model. Many consumers, buying a vacuum cleaner with a four-meter cord, noticed that during the cleaning of a standard apartment the device has to be switched several times to other outlets.
  • HEPA filter for the Zanussi vacuum cleaner. This type of filter is installed on almost all models. In a clean state, it copes with its task perfectly, but when clogged, the dust suction power decreases, an unpleasant odor appears in the room. Even there are versions that there is a likelihood of contracting viral diseases.
  • The quality of the plastic. Unfortunately, the lower cost of vacuum cleaners has led some materials to be very fragile. For example, a garbage container. It is made of fragile plastic. Therefore, you must handle it with extreme care.
