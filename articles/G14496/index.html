Pencil case

Not every child has a handmade pencil case. Its pattern is nevertheless simple, and the product itself is a one-piece long canvas, a long pocket and an elastic band. Fold the fabric in length, put pencils on it, noting the distance between them. Cut fabric with allowance. You need to make the item in duplicate.



The same length you take the material for the inner deep pocket, where you insert the pencils. Therefore, it is better to take a dense fabric. Sew a pocket with a long part of a pencil case. Next, with soap, outline the dimensions of the pencil pockets. Sew the marked lines.

Insert the pencils into the pockets, see if they fall out, if the tip breaks. If fixation is needed, then sew on the elastic band from above (also stitching it through 3-4 pencils). Now sew the inner pencil case with the facial tissue. Sew a tail with a rivet to one edge, roll a pencil case with pencils into the case, mark the place of the second button. The product is ready.
