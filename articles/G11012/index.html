Hotel Club Marmara Hammamet Beach 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet): description, photos, reviews

Tunisia is a country that has long managed to win the favor of tourists. People from all over the world come here in search of beautiful beaches and interesting sights. Of course, as the number of travelers increased, the tourism infrastructure developed, so today wanderers can relax here, taking advantage of all the benefits of modern civilization.

If we talk about resort towns, then perhaps one of the most famous is considered to be Hammamet - this is the place that becomes the haven of many travelers. Local hotels offer upscale service with a light, exotic touch of oriental culture. Of course, the tourist always has a choice, because there are many hotels on the coast. And one of the good hotels is Club Marmara Hammamet Beach. Of course, for starters, it‚Äôs worth collecting as much information as possible about the chosen vacation destination.

Hotel location and its advantages





When choosing a place for a vacation, I want the hotel to be conveniently located to places of recreation and entertainment. The hotel complex Club Marmara Hammamet Beach 3 * is located in the northern part of Hammamet, almost on the seashore. This is a calm resort area, reserved mainly for the development of hotels. And there are many shops, catering establishments, entertainment complexes. By the way, there is a taxi rank and a bus stop very close by, so moving around the neighborhood is unlikely to turn into a problem. The center of North Hammamet is about 7 km away.

Airports are a little further. For example, the distance to Enfida Airport is about 60 km, and the city of Monastir is 120 km. The capital of Tunisia is located 75 km from the hotel, so tourists often go there on excursions.

Hotel Club Marmara Hammamet Beach (Tunisia): what does the area look like?





This is a rather large hotel complex, which consists of several two- and three-story buildings - snow-white walls and interesting architecture of buildings gives the hotel a peculiar flavor. Not so long ago, repairs were carried out here, so tourists are pleasantly struck by the interior decoration of the premises and, of course, the availability of cutting-edge technologies.

Club Marmara Hammamet Beach Hotel occupies a large area - it has a swimming pool, terraces for relaxation, and playgrounds for sports events. And also a motley courtyard with greenery and bright exotic flowers.

Club Marmara Hammamet Beach Hotel: photo and room information





This is a very large hotel complex that can accommodate hundreds of tourists at the same time. There are 363 rooms in his room stock. Most of them, namely 349, are standard rooms with an elegant design, which are designed for 1-3 people. But there are 14 spacious rooms, consisting of a bedroom and a large living room with folding sofas - large families or groups of 4-5 people perfectly fit here.

The decor in the rooms is simple, which creates a real home comfort. The necessary furniture is, including a large bed, bedside tables, a spacious wardrobe, a table for work. Of course, there is also a technique that will make your stay here as comfortable as possible. You can use air conditioning (it is difficult to do without it in the summer heat), a large-screen TV (there is only one Russian channel) and a telephone. There is a safe in the room, but you have to pay for using it (low prices).

There is a bathroom with a comfortable modern shower, toilet, mirror and hairdryer. Every day, employees bring clean towels to the room, making beautiful animal figures from them, cleaning the rooms and decorating them with fresh flowers. In their reviews, former guests note that it is always a pleasure to relax in the room.

Food on the territory: what to count on?





Planning a really good light break necessarily includes the issue of nutrition. What awaits the guests of the Club Marmara Hammamet Beach 3 * hotel? Reviews say that the food is really good here - there‚Äôs nothing to complain about. As is customary in most cities of Tunisia, the hotel offers an all-inclusive scheme - these are daily breakfasts, lunches, dinners that take place in the main restaurant, as well as light snacks throughout the day (they are served in the pool bar). The menu here is not bad, the dishes are delicious, and the cooks go out every evening to the guests of the restaurant to inquire about their opinions and wishes.

There is a cafe on the beach throughout the day, and many more guests spend time in the lobby bar or pool, where staff offer refreshing drinks. By the way, imported products, as well as alcoholic beverages, must be paid separately (prices from local manufacturers, of course, are much lower). And tourists are invited to a stylized, colorful Moorish cafe, where you can not only enjoy the delicacies of local cuisine, but also plunge into the atmosphere of the East.

Convenience and entertainment by the sea





What does the Club Marmara Hammamet Beach Hotel offer the customer in terms of beach vacations? Hammamet is a resort that has become popular precisely because of its beautiful coast. This hotel is located just 50 meters from the sea and owns a private section of the beach, on which only guests are allowed to relax.

You can use sun loungers and umbrellas for free, but beach towels are provided to tourists on bail. The coast here is beautiful, employees regularly clean up all garbage and algae. In the reviews, travelers talk about the incredibly transparent and warm sea.

Of course, you can spend time more actively. There are football and volleyball courts on the beach. And without any problems you can rent a non-motorized vehicle for a boat trip. If you want something more extreme, you can go waterskiing or parasailing. There is simply no time left to miss here.

Do they provide additional amenities?

Paying for a room in the Club Marmara Hammamet Beach hotel complex, tourists, of course, can count on an additional service, the purpose of which is to save guests from small household troubles. Right at the hotel, you can exchange money for local bills at a good rate. There is a luggage room, and clothes can be washed (paid separately).

On the territory there is a large parking lot, and transport rental in this city is not a problem. In the hotel lobby you can connect to the Internet for free and most of the day the speed here is not bad.

Leisure of tourists: how to spend time at the hotel?





Club Marmara Hammamet Beach Hotel was created to fully provide guests with fun and eventful leisure. Almost all day tourists are entertained with contests and performances, and in the evening they are invited to a disco (admission is free, but you have to pay for drinks). Hotel guests are happy to do morning exercises, aerobics in the fresh air, and visit the gym. It also offers riding lessons and the ability to shoot from archery.

On the territory there are two large outdoor pools for adults, and two smaller ones for children. In the evening or rainy weather, you can refresh yourself in the indoor heated pool. It offers guests - playgrounds for mini-golf and indoor soccer. In the daytime, tennis courts work (by appointment). There are ping pong and billiards tables. And for children there is a mini club and a spacious playground. And, of course, do not forget about excursions, because this country can boast of extremely interesting sights.
