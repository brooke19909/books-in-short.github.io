Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 * (Greece, Halkidiki): description of the hotel, rooms, services, reviews

Many travelers, choosing a hotel for their holidays in Greece, decide to book apartments in Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 *. Which, in principle, is correct. Tourists who have been here confirm this fact. However, about everything - in order.





Briefly about the hotel and resort

First of all, it is worth noting that Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 *, located on a private sandy beach, was built on the place where the monastery monastery used to be. The resort itself is called Kassandra - a peninsula located in the northern part of Greece. The hotel is located a five-minute walk from the very center of Kallithea. This is a famous resort village that boasts pristine white sand beaches and magnificent landscapes. Although the local beauties, the inhabitants of Greece are not surprised. After all, "Kallithea" in Greek means "beautiful view."

From the airport "Macedonia" you can get here by bus number 78 (fare - 1 euro). He walks three times an hour, starting at 5:50 a.m. The bus of the last flight leaves at 23:00. It takes about 40 minutes to get to Thessaloniki bus station. And from there - on the KTEL bus for 8.8 euros. True, one can only leave from there until 21:00. However, in any case, cheaper than a taxi (you will have to spend about 100 euros).

A few words about a topic like weather. Halkidiki has a typical Mediterranean climate. And here the air temperature, like water, is higher than in other resorts in Greece. The weather of Halkidiki pleases visitors with the hot sun, low rainfall and very warm, mild winters. The minimum temperature that has ever been recorded here was observed in February - 3 ¬∞ C. In general, in winter it reigns somewhere between +15 ... + 20 ¬∞ C. In the holiday season (which begins here in April and ends in October), the temperature varies from 23 to 34 degrees.





For leisure

Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 * is perfect not only for living, but also for relaxing. People staying here can swim in the outdoor pool, enjoy the peace and solitude in the picturesque garden, or devote time to tanning on the terrace specially designed for this purpose. By the way, various show programs are held right there in the evenings.

When you are here, you should definitely try diving. Or any other water sport. All the facilities for this are provided at the hotel. If you don‚Äôt feel like this, you can rent a bike and go explore the surroundings.

And, of course, in Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 *, as in any other good hotel, there is a SPA center. There you can visit the sauna, hammam, go for an excellent massage or relax in the jacuzzi. Naturally, other cosmetic procedures are also offered here.

And lovers of a sports lifestyle will definitely like the presence of a good gym.

Facilities and service

In Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 * everything is done so that guests are comfortable. There are two car parks - one public, free, and the other private (15 euros per day per place). Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

Guests have the right to use the services of a 24-hour reception, a nanny and teachers, laundry, ironing, as well as a business center (there is also an office with a photocopy and fax machine). By the way, while waiting for check-in, you can sit in the shared lounge for relaxation.

A shuttle service is offered to guests, but they are entitled to request a car rental. Still here are the services for the delivery of drinks and food to the apartments. And if you need to leave somewhere, guests will have lunch with them.

In general, the hotel has a variety of services. And services too. Needless to say, if there is even a church!





Nutrition

Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 * has an excellent restaurant and several bars. Breakfast at the main establishment starts at 7:30 and ends at 10:30. At this time, guests can enjoy good food here. They serve eggs, scrambled eggs, fried sausages, boiled sausages, french fries, a variety of cheeses, sausages, ham and much more. They also serve vegetables, dried fruits, olives, butter (both creamy and olive), muffins, cookies, croissants, cereals, Greek yoghurts and pasties with filling. There is even honey. And from drinks - water, tea, cocoa, juices and coffee. In general, there is diversity, it is difficult to argue with this. Many, by the way, take and make breakfast for themselves. They mix vegetables, pour olive oil, add cheese - that's the Greek salad. In general, here you can give free rein to culinary fantasies.

Standard Apartment

Now you can tell in more detail about the rooms in which travelers can stay, having arrived in a wonderful place of Kallithea. The hotels located in this resort offer apartments of different categories. And now, in Ammon Zeus, the most budgetary of them is a standard designed for two guests.

The room is 21 square meters. m has a large bed for two. There is a minibar, as well as access to a large balcony. There is a bathroom. With the toilet, of course. Toiletries are also included. Also inside the room there is a clothes hanger, good air conditioning, a table, a plasma with a large list of channels, a telephone, and a hairdryer with a refrigerator. Towels with linen are included. In addition, tourists have the right to use the ‚Äúcall-wake up‚Äù service.

For 7 days spent in such apartments, two guests will have to pay about 25,000 rubles. At the same time, the price will include breakfast and dinner. For two, it comes out at only 12,500 rubles per week. For Greece - very cheap. For three, by the way, the price will be about 35,000 rubles.

Premium

This is the name of another, more expensive category. Although the area is the same - 21 square meters. m. Premium apartments have a huge 2 double bed and a balcony with a beautiful view of the sea, for the sake of which, in fact, many people come here.

Guests who check in here are provided with not only linen with towels, but also bathrobes. The floor in the stylish, modern room is parquet. Connecting rooms can also be offered (if there are more guests than one apartment can accommodate).

For a week stay you will need to pay about 55 thousand rubles. Half board is also included. For three tourists, the price rises to 75 thousand rubles.

By the way, if you try to book a room outside the holiday season or just a few months before check-in, then there is a chance to get a discount. Often makes advantageous promotional offers Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 *. Reviews can confirm this.





For guests with children

A certain part of tourists go on vacation, taking their children with them. For such travelers, a triple room is an ideal option. There are two options - either standard or premium. The area they have is no different. Convenience is also not very. The premium room boasts marble flooring and a more stylish and modern decor. Otherwise, there are no differences. There is a minibar, a bathroom, a balcony and all other amenities.

By the way, such apartments, declared as family, are divided into two bedrooms. One has a large 2 double bed. And in the other - two standard ones. You should count on these sleeping places, as there are no extra beds in this type of apartment.

Now about the price. Family premium overlooking the sea surface will cost about 70,000 rubles. And the standard, from the windows of which you can admire the garden, is almost 2 times cheaper (about 35 thousand rubles). Naturally, in both cases, the amount includes payment for half board for two adults and one child.

Accommodations

This topic is also worth mentioning, talking about the Ammon Zeus Hotel 4 *. Halkidiki, Kassandra, Kallithea - these are very popular places for spending holidays and vacations. Many, as mentioned above, go here, taking their children with them. In the hotel you can stay with them (regardless of the age of the kids). Only extra cots are not provided. But half-board meals for children whose age does not exceed six years are free. A child over seven but under 12? He then got half-board discounts. And by the way, people who come with children under 6 years old should inform the hotel in advance. It is better to do this when filling out an application.

By the way, even in this hotel pets are allowed. But only with small ones, weighing up to 4 kilograms. And the fact that the guest plans to take a pet with him is also worth notifying the administration in advance.

What else do guests say?

If a person wants to see the sea from his balcony, then this option should be booked immediately. Many expect to ask for apartments with a view of such a landscape already in place upon arrival at the hotel. But not worth it, especially in high season. Even when reserving apartments from home, it is advisable to indicate the type requirements in the special wishes column.

By the way, if you open the balcony door in the evening, you can enjoy the sound of the surf. True, for some people who are accustomed to silence, this is a bit unusual.

Still, everyone is advised to go to the center of Kallithea. The hotel is very well located - you can get to it quickly. And there tourists will see a large number of cafes, taverns and shops. Many in these places buy gifts and souvenirs home.

By the way, if you go to the left side of the exit from the hotel, you can notice the stairs. It leads to archaeological excavations of the temple of Zeus-Amon. This is a local attraction here. Admission is free, but is open until 3 p.m.

In general, if you study the reviews, you can understand - many people like the hotel. Most travelers who have been here claim that they would like to return here. And this means that the hotel is really worthy of attention and visit.
