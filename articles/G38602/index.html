Where is Izhevsk located?

Izhevsk: attractions and interesting facts

In addition to the Kalashnikov Museum and the 19th-century arms factory mentioned above, the city has other interesting sights worth noting. For example, the Udmurt Zoo (the largest in the Volga region), where you can get acquainted with representatives of the fauna of various natural zones of Russia.

In the architecture of the city there are many beautiful buildings and structures.
