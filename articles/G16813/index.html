Resorts of Georgia: a fairy tale all year round

Georgia is a rich and unique country in which you can find absolutely everything! Here the severity of local traditions is combined with fun and revelry, and summer, which never ends on the Black Sea coast, borders on eternal snow on the peaks of the Caucasus. Therefore, the concept of "Georgian resorts" includes everything that is in this country, from mountain peaks to architectural monuments located in the center of the capital.



In summer, the most popular region is Adjara, whose cities are spread along the Black Sea. Natural riot pleases the eye all year round, and warm weather, due to a humid subtropical climate, almost never changes. Therefore, let's take a closer look at all Georgian resorts located in this wonderful area. So, the most popular city in Adjara is Kobuleti. It has everything - the gentle sea, and a huge number of hotels, and discos, and shops, and restaurants. However, it is worth noting that a large number of tourists from Armenia come to the city, especially in the summer, who have long been chosen by this place.



You can take a break from city noise in the picturesque towns of Kvariati and Sarpi. This is a kind of quiet paradise, which Georgia boasts. Resorts, the sea, beautiful penthouses and a riot of nature - all this is concentrated in small settlements on the border with Turkey. It is generally accepted that the region has the cleanest sea and air, therefore it is most favorable for families with children. Also in this area there are sanatoriums where everyone can improve their health.

Quiet, calm and at the same time incredibly chic corner - Chakvi - is located near Kobuleti. Like all Georgian resorts, it is interesting for its local flavor, natural diversity and unique architecture. It is here that the Georgian aristocracy prefers to spend their holidays. In this regard, it is often difficult to find a free room in Chakvi. And the prices here are much higher than in other cities.



Green Paradise is Mtsvane Kontskhi: a small town located near Batumi. All year round you can enjoy the greenery of herbs and trees of unprecedented beauty, as well as swim in the fabulously warm sea. The main property of the resorts is a botanical garden, protected by the state. Well, housing prices here are quite moderate, so almost everyone can afford to spend their holidays in this green corner.

As you know, Georgian resorts are famous not only for local traditions, but also for architectural monuments that have been built here for many centuries. The city of Batumi is a living museum of those, because each building in it is a work of art. New quarters are being built up in Batumi, and soon this pearl of Adjara will become not only the cultural heritage of the country, but also the center of modern architecture.



Today, Georgian resorts can provide you with an ideal and unforgettable vacation. The prices for accommodation and meals here are quite acceptable, so you can safely pack your bags and go to this country of sun and happiness at any time of the year.
