We create original gifts from money with our own hands.

Money is the best gift. But presenting them to the hero of the occasion simply in an envelope is boring and so trite. If you want to present the notes in an original way, then welcome to the master classes described in this article. Having studied them, you will learn how to make interesting gifts from money with your own hands. And believe me, very soon you will be able to surprise your friends and relatives with original presentations from banknotes.



Flowers - gifts from money

With our own hands, we will now learn how to make a rose from notes.

For its manufacture you will need such materials:

  • banknotes are clean and wrinkled;
  • Toothpicks
  • a piece of foam or cork from a bottle;
  • stationery rubber bands;
  • artificial flower on the leg.

How to make money as a gift? Follow the instructions for making a flower out of banknotes.

  1. On the cork or foam block, make vertical cuts. There should be several of them - from the top to the bottom. Gum will cling to them.
  2. Wrap all corners of notes with a toothpick. This can also be done with scissors. Pull the blade over the corners and they will wrap.
  3. Fold the bill in half. Pull the rubber band through it and wrap around the lower cut on the cortical or foam blank. Thus, draw up and attach money to all tiers of the cork. Cling bills so that all their corners are curled outward.
  4. Remove the bud from the artificial flower. Instead, attach the money crafts you made. Fasten the cork to the leg with a heat gun.
  5. Pack a rose from notes in a wrapping paper, tie a tape.


Carpets from notes - original gifts from money

With your own hands, you can complete a similar product in just half an hour. For work, prepare: bills, a plastic film 1 meter long, a stapler, satin ribbons. Use the product on a flat, even surface (on a table or on the floor).

Fold the cellophane in half and outline the fold. Expand the film. On the lower half of the polyethylene lay out exactly the bills one next to the other. Carefully cover the entire composition with the top of the film. Fasten both tiers of cellophane with a stapler, piercing it between bills. Decorate the edges of the product with satin ribbons. A carpet of money is ready!

Ship with sails from notes

Such gifts from money look very impressive. Do it yourself with your own hands is very simple. For work, you will need such materials: a toy boat (wooden, plastic or cardboard), skewers or cocktail tubes, a glue gun , a piece of polystyrene foam, clean, even bills, scotch tape.

From the foam cut the workpiece, which is glued to the bottom of the boat. On a skewer with tape, attach banknotes in the form of sails. Insert these parts into the foam. Secure them with a thermal gun. That's all, as you can see, such a gift is made quickly and easily.

Money balloon

Such a present looks very interesting, and is done in a matter of minutes. Put the bills in an empty ball, and then inflate and tie it. Tie a beautiful ribbon to it. Everything, the gift is ready. Take a transparent ball for such a presentation, so that its internal contents are clearly visible.



These are the original gifts from money you can make yourself. It is nice to create and give such presents. And what a tremendous pleasure from such a surprise the hero of the day will receive! Your gift will be remembered for him for many years.
