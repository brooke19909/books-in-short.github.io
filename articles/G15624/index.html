Great place to relax - Linden Lake (Tyumen)

The northern regions of Russia are famous for a large number of picturesque reservoirs. Such is Linden Lake (Tyumen). Photos of its surroundings will impress even the most demanding tourists. It got its strange name because it was once surrounded by a large lush forest of linden trees. Now the gentle shores of the lake are mostly covered with sparse deciduous forest, in which you can only occasionally see linden.



Geographical location and features

The pond is located northeast of the city, just beyond the ring road, on the outskirts. The distance from Tyumen is only 8 km. If you go by car, the road will take no more than 10 minutes. Tourists from Yekaterinburg will have to overcome 300 km, but a vacation in these places is worth it. Linden Lake (Tyumen) is famous for its amazingly clear, transparent waters. This is made possible due to the large number of springs that hit from the bottom of the reservoir. The depth of the lake is quite large - 15 m, and its area is 6.2 square meters. km At the bottom, sand predominates, but in some places silt is possible.

From October to April, Linden Lake is covered with ice. But in the summer it warms up well, which is ideal for swimming.



Flora and fauna

Linden Lake (Tyumen) is rich in a variety of fish species: perch, crucian carp, pike, roach, chebak, rotan. This place is a paradise for fishing enthusiasts. There are also crayfish in the lake. Currently, you will rarely find such waters in which they will live. This trait characterizes the body of water as the cleanest and most environmentally friendly, as crayfish die in dirty waters.

White swans periodically fly to the lake. Gentle shores, densely overgrown with tall reeds, are well suited for hunting. In the wild part of the lake you can relax and fish for free. Many tourists do not use the services of a recreation center, but come here "savages", sitting in tents or cars.

Infrastructure

Surrounding Linden Lake (Tyumen) recreation center with the same name and the country club "House". In their territories a whole range of various entertainments is provided for everyone. It often hosts parties for young people who prefer extreme sports. One of the main entertainments is wakeboarding (board skating on water). A daredevil who wants to take a ride sets in motion a cable stretched across the lake. Such entertainment is a bit like water skiing, only without a motor boat. Another priority area for recreation is diving. Because of the purity and transparency of the waters, Linden Lake (Tyumen) is very popular among lovers to dive under water.



The benefits of a pond

The most important advantage is the convenient location of the lake. It is only 10-15 minutes drive from the city center, and it becomes possible to enjoy all the beauties of nature. The recreation center guarantees a warm home environment in a wooden house, wonderful baths, there are hayloft for those who want to get a thrill. In the winter, after the bath, you can plunge into the ice hole.

Also at the recreation center for a wonderful pastime we offer billiards, a barbecue area, a parking lot, which is under constant guard. If there is no desire to completely break away from the bustle of the city, then throughout the territory there is the Internet and mobile communications. The signal is good, everything works without failures.

Every day spent on Lake Linden will bring pleasure from the view of gorgeous beaches, azure shores washed by the serene blue waters.



Linden Lake (Tyumen): how to get there?

Residents of Tyumen can easily get to the lake by bus number 129 on weekends and by minibus "4K" every day, stop from the "Chaika". You can also get to the Meliorator and take a little walk on foot, enjoy the peace and beauty. If the journey takes place in a private car, the path will lie along the Velizhansky highway to the Bypass Road. At the 7th kilometer behind the bridge is the beautiful Linden Lake.

Distracted from the bustle of the city and breathe in clean air, reunited with natural beauties - this is what every person sometimes needs. The inhabitants of Tyumen were incredibly lucky in this, the picturesque landscapes are almost at their side. But the rest of the inhabitants of Russia dream to come and relax in these places.
