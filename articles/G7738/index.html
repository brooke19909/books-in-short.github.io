The islands

India is one of the largest countries. Its territory occupies both the continental part and some islands. Many tourist companies for a beach holiday recommend the following islands: Lakshadvipa, Andamany and Nicobara, located in the southeast direction from the continental part of the state. The largest city in this part is Port Blair. Popular tourist sites are Chorao and Elephant.

The islands are popular for their wonderful scenic landscapes, beautiful sandy beaches and the magnificent ocean surrounding these exotic land areas. There are many different attractions (rafting, snorkeling, diving, etc.) and unusual natural attractions.

Features of the islands of India:

  1. The Andaman and Nicobar Islands is a group of island formations of different sizes, which stretches for more than 700 kilometers from north to south.
  2. Chorao is an island in the state of Goa, which is a popular tourist attraction located near the capital of the region - Panaji.
  3. Elephant is a popular tourist destination located in Mumbai Harbor.
  4. Lakshadwip is an island allied Indian territory (consists of 12 atolls and 3 reefs).

Calangute Beach

This beach stretches along the coastline, filled with numerous hotels, shops and restaurants, for 7 kilometers. The colorfulness of this place gives a large number of Indians who come here on vacation. Unusual on the beach and the surrounding landscape: on one side is the church of St. Alex, on the other - spacious fields covered with verdure.

Kerim Beach

Many beach resorts in India are wonderful. South Goa is particularly noteworthy at Kerim Beach, located at the mouth of the river. Terekol (Thiracol). Although it is the farthest in this state (south), it is recognized as the best place to relax away from the hustle and bustle of people. Very often tourists come here to retire and enjoy the unusually beautiful sunsets.

The length of the cleanest beach, covered with white sand, is 2 kilometers. This area is remarkable for its amazing silence. And the vegetation is unusual here: among banana palms here you can also find coniferous plants. For complete privacy with the surrounding nature, it is best to rent a bungalow or a room in a hostel located directly on the coast. You can eat in any restaurant.

Arambol Beach

This beach is located in the town of Arambol in North Goa. The beaches of India, located on the coast of the Arabian Sea, have been chosen by many tourists for many years. This is a fairly wide beach, covered with clean sand, and with surprisingly clear water. A feature of the area is a fresh lake with a hot spring.

In the daytime, tourists relax on the beach, and in the evenings attend night parties. In addition, mass meditation sessions are often held on the coast.

Palolem Beach

Among the best beaches in India, this place is the most popular in Goa (southern state). It is of particular interest to vacationers, since Palolem is a true paradise among the tropics. This is a wonderful site with white sand and turquoise transparent sea.

There is no infrastructure and hotels, and all this is not necessary at all. Palolem is a pristine, secluded place with a warm sunny atmosphere. It is suitable for lovers of peace and silence. There are small bungalows on the coast where any tourist can stay for a while.

Although Palolem is very popular, it is small. Its coastline is only a couple of kilometers long. There are very few people. That is why the water in the sea is unusually clean. Relaxing on the beach, you can sometimes see dolphins who are frequent guests here and are not at all afraid of people. You can also go diving or take a boat trip.

Tourists reviews

Almost all the beaches of India, according to vacationers ever there, are very clean, except for some beach areas located in cities. The general opinion of the coastal zones is the excellent beaches, covered with very soft and clean sand, as well as with a gentle entrance to the clear sea. The service is sensitive and of high quality.

Tip for tourists: when choosing a place for relaxation and swimming, pay attention to the beaches, in which there are fewer local residents. In such areas, rest will undoubtedly be calmer and it will be possible to fully plunge into the atmosphere of carelessness and pleasure without any annoying spectators.
