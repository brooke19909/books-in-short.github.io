Knitted hat with knitting needles. For the girl the most original models

Today, children's clothing stores offer a huge selection of hats for girls. But the hat, connected by mother with care and warmth, will always be the most beautiful, comfortable and unique. Want your baby to wear one? So, let's get to work. A knitted hat with knitting needles for a girl of school age is made very simply. Let's look at the most fashionable, popular and at the same time simple models.

Knitted hat with ears

It is not at all difficult to knit a headdress in the shape of the muzzle of an animal with knitting needles. There is one fairly simple and original option. To do this, you will need pastel-colored yarn and the remains of threads of a contrasting dark color (black, dark brown, dark blue). We will knit a kitty. This model is popular with younger schoolgirls, as well as with older fashionistas. This knitted hat with knitting needles for a girl is suitable for any outerwear, as it is made from threads of calm pastel colors. And for a more harmonious look, you can knit a scarf and mittens in the same style.





First, measure the circumference of the head and divide the resulting value in half. We pick up the number of loops corresponding to half the circumference of the head on the knitting needles and knit 3-4-row garter stitch. In the next row we make an increase in the amount of 10 loops distributed evenly over the entire width. Next, continue knitting with facial loops. The height of the manufactured parts is determined as follows. We measure the distance from the forehead to the lower point of the nape. We add another 7 cm to this number. The resulting value will be the height. Having reached the desired height, we make uniform reductions in 10 loops and knit 3-4 more rows of garter stitch. After that, close all the loops. We fold the resulting rectangle in half and make side seams. Next, with the seam forward, with the needle, we designate the ears, stitching through both corners of the cap. On the front part we embroider the eyes, nose, mouth and mustache. The cat hat is ready.

Knitted hats with needles with a description and various decor

Another fairly simple model. You can knit it on circular knitting needles or on ordinary - straight. On the needles, you need to dial the loops in an amount equal to the circumference of the head. Next, we knit the elastic of the future model. Options for its implementation may be different, for example 2x2, 1x1, 2x1 and so on. Choose the one you like best. The width of the gum also depends only on your desire.





After finishing her knitting, we make a uniform increase along the entire circumference. About one loop every 3-4. Next, we knit the front surface until we reach the desired height. You can determine the height using the fitting or by comparing with a hat in the wardrobe. Further, all the loops from the spokes are thrown to the thread and tightened. Another option involves a gradual decrease in four places located at regular intervals. When 5-6 stitches remain on the knitting needles, they are also thrown onto the thread with a hook and tightened.

The same knitted hat with knitting needles for a girl, made according to the scheme described above, can look completely different. It all depends on your imagination. Here are some original solutions.

Decor Options

The first photo shows a model connected in this way, without additional decoration. Its originality is due to the use of various shades of yarn.





Another photo shows models connected by the same pattern, but already decorated with flowers. Such a flower can easily be crocheted. The third option, resembling a cake, is made of yarn of two contrasting colors, and at the junction of the gum and the front surface, a row in the form of petals is made using a hook. The top of this model is decorated with a berry, also crocheted. It would seem that one scheme, but how many different options came out!





As you can see, any knitted hat with knitting needles for a girl is very fast, does not require special skills and much experience.
