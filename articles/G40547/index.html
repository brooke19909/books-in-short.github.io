Taha White Pearls Guest House 2 *: reviews

Tourists familiar with the service at the hotel report that Morjim Pearl, consisting of several villas, is within walking distance from one of the best beaches in North Goa - Morjim.





About location

The hotel is located away from the main road, so it is quite quiet there. The authors of the reviews called the hotel a very good location, many people like that the wonderful Morjim Beach is a 5-minute walk away. Nearby are cafes, supermarkets, other hotels, massage parlors.

About rooms

This hotel has gorgeous large beautiful rooms with stunning views of the surrounding countryside and the beach. The guest rooms are spacious with a large double bed. Towels and bedding are pure white, which is rare for Goa. Also in the rooms there is a wardrobe with several triplets, a coffee table, two armchairs, a red lockable fridge, air conditioning, two ceiling fans, a small flat TV (no Russian channels). There are two balconies (one is in the room, the second is on the floor), a kitchen sink, and there is no safe. Residents keep documents and valuables in a suitcase equipped with a combination lock.

The bathroom in the rooms is small, a wall-mounted water heater is equipped, upon arrival, they are given a small piece of soap and toilet paper (roll). Room cleaning and linen change is carried out exclusively on demand. It is necessary to take the room key to the reception and ask for cleaning, change of linen and towels.

Free Wi-Fi, as reported by the reviews, is available throughout the territory, in all restaurants, cafes and necks, on the beach, you only need to find out the password.

Some residents call the hotel rooms small but cozy. Often guests are surprised at their low price: a spacious room (35 sq. Meters) costs only 500 p. (breakfast included). Tourists report that in Goa in the season for such a price you can not even find hostels.

The authors of the reviews note that the territory of the hotel is beautiful, very spacious.

About nutrition

Breakfast included - not very varied, but of high quality, according to hotel guests. Breakfast, as reported by the reviewers, consists of two toasts, a piece of omelet, jam and butter, as served on airplanes. A selection of tea or coffee with milk is also available.

About staff

The hotel staff are great guys, guests say. The hotel staff is called "fantastic": here, according to the residents, there are very nice employees who speak English and are always ready to help guests in all problems. They will tell you where to find interesting places, where to go, help rent a scooter for a day or a week, offer breakfast at a very low price and bring it to your room.

About rest

Taha White Pearls Hotel is called a great place to relax, where there is not a huge number of tourists, there are several excellent local restaurants and cafes.





The review authors note that in Anjuna, guests have the opportunity to get to know the real India - with a lot of cows, goats and dogs, to the delight of those who love animals.

About the beach

Morjim, according to tourists, is a wide, not crowded, clean beach, on which there are no cows and beggars common for India. Compared to other beaches, there are usually the smallest waves. In Morjim and its neighboring beaches - Mandrem and Ashvem - mostly Russian tourists rest. Nearby is the 24-hour Russian restaurant Bora Bora. The water in the Arabian Sea, according to the authors of the reviews, is warm, very comfortable for swimming.

Tourists say about the weather that it is hot in Morjim during the day and pretty cool in the evenings (you have to wear something warm). May be intermittent rain.

Vacationers are advised to take a scooter for the entire duration of the vacation (350 rupees per day). The rental point is located at the exit of the hotel to the right, around the bend at the end of the street.

Holidays in Anjuna will fly by quickly, tourists are sharing. Time is usually taken by the fact that vacationers ride on different beaches and attractions.

About the pros

Among the advantages of the hotel, the following is noted: it is located away from the main highway, sleeps well in it, is not far from the sea, there are two good restaurants on the way to the beach - Rainbow, Tibet. Most importantly, there is no theft, there are no bedbugs, fleas, linen is clean, Wi-Fi is free. Many travelers strongly recommend a hotel to stay.
