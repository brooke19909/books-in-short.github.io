Children's Camp "Jubilee" (Berdsk) - a great option for a summer vacation

Youth and childhood are amazing times in the life of every person, especially if they pass with friends or loved ones and relatives. There are several options for spending the summer time in a small child - at home with parents, grandparents in the village or an unforgettable vacation with friends. With the implementation of the latter option, the Yubileiny camp (Berdsk) will help. Parents of children who visited here leave positive feedback about it, which is fully confirmed by the quality of the services provided.

For children, mainly from the Novosibirsk region, a vacation in the country, but relatively close to their parents, is perfect. "Anniversary" is a great option! Children's Camp "Jubilee" (Berdsk)

All young guests are accommodated in small two-story buildings, which are well heated and located throughout the camp. 4-6 children live in each room, depending on the size of the "room" itself. On each floor of all buildings there are 8 rooms, one large hall for games, morning exercises in the winter seasons and evening lights; There are also sanitary facilities with equipped showers and washbasins. Counselors live with children in buildings. A huge green area offers the camp "Jubilee" (Berdsk). Photos posted in the article confirm this. Lawns, vibrant playgrounds and horizontal bars cause a desire to play not only among children, but also among their parents.





Infrastructure for children

Camp "Jubilee" (Berdsk) offers children a fairly developed infrastructure.

  • The huge hall for concerts, performances and evening shows (the whole hall can accommodate more than 240 people) is equipped with the latest technology: projectors and video systems make mass events even more colorful and memorable.
  • An excellent dance hall for teaching kids such a wonderful sport.
  • The library will allow you to escape from the constant outdoor games and enrich the inner world. All books are in excellent condition and very interesting.
  • Rooms and classrooms well create an atmosphere for creating masterpieces. Master classes and needlework classes are held here daily.
  • Camp "Jubilee" (Berdsk) has playgrounds for volleyball, basketball, badminton and football, which will help young athletes to maintain physical fitness. And active counselors constantly organize whole competitions between units, the rewards of which are cups, diplomas and medals.

Why visit the Yubileiny camp (Berdsk)





Firstly, fresh air, a river and delicious food have not hurt any child. Improving health and the immune system is guaranteed, thanks to such summer prevention, the entire school year will fly by in the same breath, accompanied by fives.

Secondly, the camp "Jubilee" (Berdsk) gives kids a huge number of friends and, possibly, new interests and hobbies.

Thirdly, the camp staff offers a choice of several programs for recreation: fitness, tourist, local history, artistic and aesthetic, and social and pedagogical. Thus, the child will be able to "find himself" and friends of interest.





And the last: most child psychologists say that children from an early age should learn to be independent, and mothers and fathers should accustom their consciousness to the fact that their children grow up and will soon leave a warm place under the wing. And if loving parents are not yet ready to send the child alone to study a foreign language in the UK or in Malta, then "Jubilee" will be a great start.
