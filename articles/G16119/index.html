Eureka Park at Academic: find the most interesting

It so happened that the concepts of “learning” and “leisure” are rarely perceived as synonyms. Gaining new knowledge is associated with school and cramming, and entertainment with something bright, fun, but not too informative. Eureka Park undertakes to destroy these stereotypes, making scientific knowledge accessible and exciting for both children and adults.

Cognitive rest

"Eureka Park" in Moscow (m "Academic") is a modern intellectual leisure center. Setting themselves the task of instilling in children an interest in scientific knowledge, its employees choose the most effective methods for this - absolute interactivity, an informal atmosphere, and practical immersion in the research process.

In the Eureka Park at the Academic, children, together with their parents, can take part in natural science experiments, feel like discoverers, and in a playful way get acquainted with fundamental physical and chemical laws.

The center also acts as a museum, in which exhibits are not standing under glass and are available for comprehensive study by visitors. The address of Eureka Park on Akademicheskaya is Moscow, Dmitry Ulyanov Street, 1st floor of building No. 42.

Program palette

Want to instill a child’s interest in a particular field of science or deepen existing knowledge? Eureka Park at Akademicheskaya offers its visitors to join a number of ongoing programs.

“School of Future Scientists” is a circle that combines modular full-time and distance learning (you can start with any thematic block). Classes last two hours and include:

  • a demonstration during which the facilitator conducts a series of experiments;
  • independent experiments or construction in pairs and groups;
  • quest or game on the topic on a special platform;
  • joint debriefing, discussion of tasks.

The guys also receive additional video materials for independent work.

"3D-modeling" - includes creativity and modern technology. Using a 3D pen, children can depict three-dimensional heroes of fairy tales and cartoons, favorite animals. The duration of the lesson is 1 hour. Age group: 5–10 years. When you purchase a subscription for 4 classes, a discount is provided.

"Organized and school groups" - held from 11 to 19 hours on the basis of Eureka Park or an educational institution. The size of the group is from 15 people. The age of participants is from 4 to 15 years. The center offers:

  • 13 scientific programs (“Life under the microscope”, “Bioacoustics”);
  • 4 creative directions (“Secrets of cinema”, “Academy of tricks”);
  • 3 scientific marathons;
  • 7 popular workshops ("Electrorobot", "Riddles of Vision").

Useful entertainment to choose from

"Eureka Park" at the "Academic" - is not only training programs, but also a huge assortment of educational activities for adults and children. Festivals, field trips, birthdays, summer camps are far from a complete list.

  1. “Homemade Animation Kitchen” is a creative studio where visitors can independently create a cartoon, watch it on the screen and receive an entry as a gift. No special training is required.
  2. "Festival of designers" - held every Saturday for children from 3 years old and their parents. It is possible to assemble a city, castle or spaceport with your own hands (15 types of compositions).
  3. "Birthdays" - celebrating the holiday is fun and useful will help employees of Eureka Park. Original educational and entertaining programs: “The brilliant detective”, “Chocolate master class”, “Holidays of the peoples of the world”.
  4. “New Year's programs” - suggest an unforgettable meeting of the coming year at the school of the young wizard, at an ultraviolet party or ice and fire show. One and a half hour programs are provided for groups of 15 people or more.

"Eureka Park" at the "Academic": reviews

There are institutions and events whose impressions of which I really want to share. To "Eureka Park" at the "Academic" this applies in full. The vast majority of reviews are not just positive, but rather enthusiastic. Parents who came with their children to a culinary festival or master class in chemistry and plunged into the world of fascinating science are happy to share their impressions.

In their reviews, they note the following positive aspects of the complex:

  • theory turns into an exciting practice;
  • the place is distinguished by a special flavor and a bunch of programs for different ages;
  • young employees themselves are passionate about science and are able to infect children with this interest;
  • Interesting information about science is presented easily and simply;
  • lessons are presented clearly and readily.
