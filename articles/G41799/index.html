    How holiday pay is considered by the formula

    Counting vacation pay according to the formula is very simple:

    • If the billing period is fully worked out, then the amount of payments must be divided by 12 and 29.4.

    • If the billing period is not fully worked out, then the amount of payments for the billing period should be divided by the number of calendar days of the period.

    • The number of calendar days (for incomplete worked months) is calculated as follows: for a full worked month, the number is taken - 29.4. In an incomplete month, the number of worked calendar days must be multiplied by 29.4 and divided by the number of calendar days of the full month.

    • The calculation of vacation pay is made according to the formula: the average earnings per day multiplied by the due vacation days.
