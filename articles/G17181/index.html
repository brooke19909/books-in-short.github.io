The best car radio: reviews

Anyone who is going on his car on a long trip, just can not do without a car radio. This device will cheer you up with good music. With it, you can listen to any audio book or spend time on the road, learning a foreign language.



Having a car radio in a car is not just a pleasant benefit. With her, time spent on the road or in city traffic jams will become more comfortable and fly by almost unnoticed. That is why it is important for each driver to choose a good radio in the car. A reliable and high-quality device will work for many years and will delight its owner with excellent sound. What are they, the best radio in the car?

An important piece of automotive interior

Today, manufacturers offer a wide variety of car radios. Their range is so large that many drivers are simply lost when choosing a model. What is a modern car receiver? It is not just a means of reproducing sound. Modern car radios are multifunctional devices that allow you to view video, as well as connect a variety of storage media.





Choosing a good radio in a car is impossible without knowing certain nuances that are important to pay attention to when buying. Equally important for any motorist is the acquisition price. Many buyers are also targeting it. It should be borne in mind that the cost criterion for car radios is directly dependent on their functionality. The higher price of the product, as a rule, also indicates the presence of additional features and technical features in it.

Car radio sizes

This criterion, indicating the installation location of the car player, allows us to distinguish two types of such devices. Depending on the size of the car radio are single-din and two-din. The main criterion for their difference is the height of the apparatus.

Which radio is better to put in a car? Single-dome devices are suitable for domestic cars. Their height is 5 cm. The second type of car radio is installed in cars made in Germany, Japan and the USA. Their height is 10 cm. It should be borne in mind that dual-band car radios are more functional and have a high price category.

Source Characteristic

This is a fairly important criterion, which is also worth paying attention to when buying a car player. Today, manufacturers offer the following devices for reproducing sound in a car:

1. CD-recorders. This is the most common type of device in which information is read from CDs.
2. DVD recorders. These are devices with fairly wide capabilities. In addition to ordinary audio recordings, it becomes possible to play video files.
3. USB receivers. These are advanced car players. In them, audio and video information is read from external devices connected via a USB port.
4. SD radio. These devices have a combination of all the previous types of car receivers, as well as an additional connector for connecting SD-cards.
5. MP5 players. These are the most modern and best car radios. They are able to support all the formats available today, as well as reproduce photographs and images. According to customer reviews, the most attractive in these devices is their versatility.





An important criterion for the final choice is the high quality of sound reproduction.

To date, many drivers claim that the best radio tape recorders in the car are media receivers. These are devices in the design of which a mechanical drive is not provided. They display information not only from SD- and USB-carriers, but also from iPad / iPhone.

The market offers its customers a host of other radio tape recorders that have a different combination. These devices are more expensive than usual, but they are multifunctional, reproducing information from various types of media. So, according to the reviews of many drivers, car radio with an MP3 decoder are very popular. With their help, files with an MP3 extension are played.

As for the cassette radio, they have long been out of use, and meeting them in the car is a rarity.

Important details

What else should I look for when buying a player for a car? The best radio in the car must have a removable panel. This part protects the device from theft. A car radio without a removable panel is absolutely unattractive to thieves. The fact is that the cost of this important part is about 70% of the cost of the device itself. Due to such unjustified costs, the removable panel guarantees peace to its owner.

Not so important, but still the determining part of the choice is the backlight. The best car radios in the car are equipped with it, without any doubt. This part is very convenient and creates additional comfort when using the equipment at night. In addition, the backlight of the car radio, which coincides in color with the interior of the car, looks very impressive.

Choosing a player for a car, you can purchase various models. They can have either multicolor or monochrome, and interchangeable backlight. The first of them provides a very wide range of colors that automatically alternate like a chameleon. In interchangeable lighting, the choice is very small. It contains only two colors. Which radio is better in the car? According to reviews by most motorists, drivers prefer receivers with interchangeable or multi-color illumination.



Another important detail for the machine player is the built-in tuner. This is an optional feature that allows you to listen to radio signals in the FM and AM bands. Usually it is 24 stations.

Specifications

High-quality and reliable equipment must have certain parameters. They should also be taken into account when choosing a model. So, in the device, a certain ratio of signal level and noise should be observed. The value of this indicator should not be less than 90 decibels. Another important technical characteristic of the device is the ability to adjust the tone to low, medium, and high frequency.

An important parameter that should also be considered when choosing a radio in a car is the power of the amplifier. This is an indicator on which the quality of audio information depends. A good radio in a car for car audio should have high power ranging from 100 to 150 watts per channel.
