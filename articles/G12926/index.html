Sights of the Kirov region: marvelous nature, places of worship and a dinosaur cemetery

Where to find a unique land with protected nature, ancient architectural buildings and even a dinosaur cemetery? Such a place is not far away, but on the territory of Russia. The sights of the Kirov region are attractive in various aspects. Every tourist will find here rest to their liking.

The city of Kirov has a lot of interesting, but even more unusual and mysterious can be found in the vicinity.

Nature reserve

There are 192 specially protected natural territories registered in the region. These are the reserves "Nurgush", "Pizhemsky" and "Bylina", as well as resort areas. The nature of these lands strikes the imagination with the diversity of flora and fauna.

Nurgush

The Kirovsky reserve "Nurgush" is spread over more than 5.5 thousand hectares. Deciduous trees prevail here, but conifers are also found. Many plants are listed in the Red Book.





The border of the reserve is the Vyatka River. On the territory there are more than 60 lakes, interconnected by streams and channels. Each hollow in this region is filled with water. In the spring flood, almost the entire territory is flooded. Water species of birds and animals prevail here. Many species of fish come here to spawn.

"Nurgush" is a natural monument, a precious pearl of the Kirov region. Employees are active in the conservation and study of flora and fauna. Visitors come here for whom convenient routes and ecological trails are laid.

Vyatka Switzerland

Listing the sights of the Kirov region, it is impossible not to mention the Pizhemsky Reserve, which is called Vyatka Switzerland. The places here are picturesque. The coast of the Nemda River is dotted with various caves and karst voids, attracting extreme people and cavers. Steep cliffs attract climbers. In this reserve you can watch real waterfalls.





And what an amazing Lezhinsky lake is worth! It was formed 400 years ago, either as a result of the collapse of an underground cave, or from a meteorite fall. The lake is oval in perfect shape, and the water in it is so clear and clean that you can drink it without boiling. It is the deepest lake in the vicinity. Water at a depth even in the hottest months does not warm above 5 ¬∞ C.

Both reserves are ideal for walking in nature and relaxing from the bustle of the city.

Architectural masterpieces

The sights of the Kirov region are known not only for natural wealth. There are many ancient churches. The temple complex in the village of Velikoretskoe (Kirov region) is a shrine to the whole region. It was here that the miraculous icon of St. Nicholas was found. A radiant image was revealed in 1383 to the peasant Semyon Agalakov, and when one of the seriously ill residents of the village was healed from the application to the shrine, the news of this miracle spread throughout the country. Believers came here. The village was built Velikoretsky city, consisting of churches, temples, bell towers, a monastery, as well as schools, residential buildings and shopping arcades.





Not all buildings have survived to this day. But even what remains is striking in magnificence. In the 90s of the last century, the shrine was returned to the church. The monks returned here, and the revival of spiritual traditions began, one of which is the procession on June 6.

Vyatskiye Polyany

The sights of the Kirov region would not be complete without mentioning the small town of Vyatskiye Polyany. In the city itself, you can visit the historical museum and the house-museum of the designer-gunsmith Shpagin. And in the vicinity of Vyatka you can see mysterious stones. This is a real natural miracle. Huge balls of stone remained here after the descent of the glacier. Thousands of tourists rush to look at them and touch the antiquity.

Dinosaur cemetery

Few ordinary people know that a real cemetery of dinosaurs was found in the Kirov region. Millions of years ago, a full-flowing river flowed here. But now it has become shallow, and a pit has formed on the site of its channel, which gave the name to the city of Kotelnichi. A steep cliff stretches along the Vyatka River for almost 30 km, where the remains of prehistoric animals were discovered. Unique finds were made back in the 30s of the last century. And now archaeologists and paleontologists search and find in these metes the bones of extinct animals, many of which have not been found anywhere else in the world before.





A paleontological museum has been opened in Kotelnich, which contains samples of finds and tells about interesting episodes related to the search and study of animals and plants of the Permian period.

The sights of the Kirov region were also replenished with the Dinosaurs in Vyatka dinopark, where among the impenetrable forests you can see dinosaurs that are recreated from the fossils found. Surprisingly, millions of years ago, these giant lizards roamed the territory where the Vyatka Polyana is now located.

To see something unusual and surprising, it is not necessary to go to distant lands. You can go on a trip to the Kirov region and discover its secrets.
