Dubai Miracle Garden: Description

The UAE is a wonderful place to relax. In this beautiful country, there is an amazing flower park - a landmark of Dubai, which has become on a par with the Burj Khalifa skyscraper and the Jumeirah mosque. It is noteworthy that the opening of such a romantic place in nature took place on the holiday of Valentine's Day. Dubai Flower Park is the largest in the world.

Creating a flower park

Created figures, compositions from flowers of various kinds can delight even the most inveterate skeptics. When visiting this heavenly place, even adults get the feeling that they are in a fairy tale. The creators of Dubai Miracle Garden attracted the best landscape designers from around the world, including the United States and Italy, to work on shapes and flower arrangements.



Main Songs

The main flower in the created flower arrangements is weaving petunia. She wraps herself around the figures. Petunia was diluted with other plants, most of which do not grow in the Emirates, but were imported from other countries. Geranium, calendula, lobelia, coleus, tagetes and other types of flowers, which are now cultivated on the territory of Dubai Miracle Garden, gently interweave between the main flower of the compositions, if the created conditions allow it. The central composition in the park is a portrait of the founder of Dubai. The image was very realistic. Around the portrait, seven flower hearts were created, symbolizing the number of emirates in the UAE.

Dubai Miracle Garden Park is surrounded by a floral wall three meters high and eight hundred meters long. Interestingly, the Guinness Book of Records reserved a place for such a gigantic and at the same time wonderful floral work. Among other compositions in the park you can see unusual watches, which, despite the fact that they are made of flowers, are absolutely accurate, a flower pyramid ten meters high and an area of ‚Äã‚Äãone hundred forty-four square meters, flower houses, cars and other sculptures. In addition, Dabai Miracle Garden annually creates new floral arrangements, so it will be interesting for tourists visiting the park to come back here and see other works. For the convenience of moving around the park area, tracks up to four kilometers long have been laid.



Irrigation system

To care for a huge number of plants, you need to make a lot of effort in the climate of Dubai. But what can stop the Emirates on the path to creating a paradise on earth whose purpose is magnificence and luxury that amazes visitors so that it takes your breath away? Amazing technology used in the UAE. Water is very valuable here, therefore, to save on the territory of the huge flower garden in Dubai, watering with the technology of drip underground irrigation is used. It implies a method of supplying water and fertilizer to the roots of plants. This helps to save life-giving moisture and electricity up to seventy-five percent. For irrigation use wastewater. The developers of a system that maintains moisture for plants in a flower oasis created it so that the necessary level is maintained regardless of the time and conditions of the sultry climate of the Emirates. True, the park is not open to visitors year-round - its visiting time is limited: from October to May inclusive.



Impressions

Of course, those who once visited this amazing, fabulous place will once again wish to visit it and enjoy the view of the local landscape, to breathe in the scents in the air. The beauty of the Flower Garden in Dubai can not be described in words and conveyed using even the highest quality photographs and videos - it is better to see with your own eyes. Contemplating all the magnificence of the park created by human hands, you understand how much has been given to people, how man himself is significant and great among all creatures on earth. Here you forget about any hardships and problems, and you want this moment to last forever.



Visit time

In the season when the Flower Garden in Dubai is open, it can be visited both on weekdays: from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m., and on weekends: from 10 a.m. to midnight. There are buses to the park from the Mall of Emiraites metro station. Of course, you can also get to him by taxi. Children under three years old visit the park for free. For all other visitors, entrance to the flower country is paid - about 5.5 dollars (at the current rate of about 312 rubles). There is a cafe in the park. It is strictly forbidden to pick flowers, litter, walk on lawns and flower beds. If during a vacation in the UAE the purpose of the excursion will be a flower park, then it is better to call there first and clarify all the information about its work, as due to weather conditions the schedule of tourist reception may change.

Flower gamut

This wonderful park has more than forty-five million varieties of various types of flowers. There are even such cultures that were first brought to the Persian Gulf. It is hard to imagine, but the flowers of the park create more than 60 different colorful shades. Unofficially, the UAE's Miracle Garden Park is called a multi-color oasis. And this is not surprising, because it seems that a magician painted a vast territory, scattering delicate flowers instead of paints. If you look from a height at the created landscapes, it seems that the artist worked, with a wide brush, writing out various landscapes from bright stripes. Here you can see painted butterflies, a rainbow, peacocks, amazing architecture - and all this from flowers! All compositions are saturated with romance and humor. In the park you can even see a small copy of the Burj Khalifa skyscraper entwined with flowers.



Most of the flower country paths run through open areas, so it is important to have sun protection on your head. Specialists working on the creation of the park are constantly introducing something new. The park area is constantly expanding, and conditions for visitors are improving. It is safe to say that if you plan to visit the UAE, then the flower park should be included in the planned excursions - this is the place that is worthy to be on a par with the sights of wonders of the world.
