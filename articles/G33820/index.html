Hotels in Camyuva, Turkey: list, rating, reviews

The Turkish village of Camyuva is located near the city of Kemer in the resort area of ​​Antalya. Reviews this village from tourists has earned very good. Particularly praised by vacationers are local comfortable beaches and clear sea. There are many hotels in Camyuva - both three-, and four- or five-star. Some of these establishments deserve good reviews from tourists, others not so much.

3 star hotels

Hotels of this category enjoy constant popularity among domestic tourists. According to many vacationers, it is possible to place almost any three-star about Camyuva ’s body with relative convenience. Hotels of this type are usually chosen by tourists who decide to save on accommodation and at the same time relax in the popular tourist zone of Kemer.





Most three-star hotels in the village operate on an all-inclusive basis. That is, tourists, among other services, are offered a buffet. One of the features of this category of hotels in Camyuva is its large territory. Despite the low cost of living, in such hotels, tourists can use the pool, terraces for walks, and sometimes visit the private beach and.

List of the most popular 3 * hotels

Most often, domestic vacationers stay at such hotels in the village, such as:

  • M oms H otel;

  • Ipek Camyuva;

  • Ilimyra.

Hotel Moms Hotel offers its guests Standard rooms of several varieties. All rooms at this Camyuva hotel are air-conditioned and have a TV. The distance to the sea from the hotel buildings is 300 m. Tourists who fly by plane to get to this hotel will have to overcome 50 km.

Mini-hotel Ilimyra 3 * is located just 8 km from Kemer on the second coastline. In its rooms guests can use a shower, a hairdryer, a safe.





Hotel Ipek 3 * is located from Kemer at a distance of less than a kilometer. For guests here are also equipped with standard rooms. The rooms have a TV and air conditioning. At the hotel guests can use the pool, shower, telephone, TV, etc.

Three Star Hotel Reviews

Opinion about all three Camyuva hotels presented in the list above was rather good among tourists . The Moms Hotel, for example, is generally praised for its peace and quiet on site. Good reviews of Ipek Camyuva are at least due to its proximity to the beach and delicious breakfasts.

The tourists had a good opinion about the Ilimyra hotel because of the quiet rooms. However, tourists who choose Camyuva for their holidays should bear in mind that this hotel, unfortunately, is sometimes scolded for inactive air conditioning and a poor breakfast.

Turkey, Camyuva: 4 star hotels

Hotels of this category in the village are located mainly on the first coastline. The four-star Camyuva hotels are especially popular with family tourists and lovers of all kinds of water sports. Most of the rooms in these complexes offer beautiful views of the sea or mountains.





At the disposal of guests of 4 * Camyuva hotels there are private beaches, fitness clubs and lagoon pools. Also, mini-markets usually work in such hotels.

List of popular 4 * hotels

Very good reviews of our compatriots in Camyuve earned such, for example, four-star hotels, such as:

  • Pine house;

  • Rizzi

Pine House is located on a hill above the sea. Its territory is surrounded on all sides by a pine forest. This hotel is located 8 km from Kemer. From the airport in Antalya, it takes about an hour to get to it.

Hotel Rizzi (Kemer, Camyuva) was built in 2002. It represents one five-story building with elevators. In the rooms of this hotel, tourists have the opportunity to use satellite TV, air conditioning, a minibar and other amenities.





Hotel reviews of four star hotels

Russian tourists also consider such Camyuva hotels quite comfortable. The rooms here are in most cases spacious and comfortable. Judging by the reviews, there are many different amenities on the territory of four-star hotels in Camyuva. And the attendants in such complexes are usually competent, polite and helpful. In some hotels of this class, employees are able to speak including Russian.

Hotel Pine House 4 * (Kemer, Camyuva) is popular among domestic tourists for its proximity to the sea, quiet atmosphere, delicious food in the restaurant. This hotel is also highly praised for the convenience and beauty of the area and surrounding area.





Rizzi complex has earned good reviews from tourists, primarily for the convenience of the beach. The sea in the area of ​​this hotel is very clean, and besides, the depth of it in this place begins close. Judging by the reviews, there are always enough loungers on the beach of the Rizzi hotel for all guests. The menu in the restaurant of this hotel, according to most tourists, is not too diverse. However, the cooks at the hotel cook quite tasty.

Five star complexes

Hotels of this class in Camyuva, of course, offer tourists the most complete range of amenities. They are located, of course, directly on the coastline. Such hotels offer guests all-inclusive or ultra all-inclusive services. Most of these hotels have a huge territory.

Five-star class in this village can also include club hotels of the group HV-1 and HV-2. Such hotels provide their guests with a standard set of amenities. They differ from ordinary five-star hotels only in that the guests here are accommodated not in rooms, but in separate bungalows.

List of popular 5 * hotels
