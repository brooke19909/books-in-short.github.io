How to open Kinder Surprise and reassemble it

Kinder Surprise is one of our favorite children's treats. Delicious chocolate, and even a surprise inside. What will be the joy if there is an unexpected personal gift inside. It remains only to find out: how to open Kinder Surprise.

Such a present can be presented not only to a child, but also to an adult. A great way to give a small toy, a touching note, jewelry or even an engagement ring.

How to open Kinder Surprise quietly

To make the gift unexpected, you need to replace the contents of the chocolate egg very carefully.

  1. Before starting the process, it is recommended to cool the chocolate a little so that it does not melt from the heat of the hands and is not covered with fingerprints.
  2. Then you need to carefully unwrap the wrapping foil without wrinkling or tearing it. First you need to straighten all the fold lines, then remove the foil completely.
  3. Now you need to open the "Kinder Surprise" as a box, separating the halves from each other. This is easier to do with a knife: you need to carefully walk them along the seam between the halves.

Now it will not be difficult to open a plastic capsule and replace the surprise inside.

How to connect the halves of a chocolate egg again

The question of how to open Kinder Surprise has been resolved. Now you need to connect the chocolate pieces.

  1. To do this, you will need a knife again. It must be immersed in hot water and kept there for about 30 seconds.
  2. When the metal becomes hot, you need to attach the knife with a flat surface to the edges of the chocolate halves.

  4. As soon as the seam is frozen and the egg is fastened, you can return the foil to its place. First wrap the center of the chocolate egg, then bend the edges, as it was originally.
