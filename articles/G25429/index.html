Excursions In Western Ukraine: All Beauty In One Day

Western Ukraine is a unique corner of the earth, where in a small space almost all the attractions that tourists would like to see during the tour are collected.

There are also beautiful ancient castles, the walls of which breathe history. Castle tours are generally an unforgettable experience. Here, ancient monasteries and temples are the same age as Christianity in Russia and Slavic writing. Here and the incomparable peaks of the Carpathians, rapid mountain rivers and raging waterfalls. Here are the most beautiful coniferous and oak forests. Here are the narrow streets of ancient Lviv and Kamenetz-Podolsky. And one of the largest cave massifs in the world.

And most importantly - all this is very close. Literally within walking distance.

Travel agency "Radosvit" is one of the operators that specializes in organizing excursions that combine the most diverse beauty of the region. So that in one trip you can see as many different beauties as possible and gain maximum indescribable impressions.

For one day of excursion in Western Ukraine, you can visit the beautiful plain Dzhurinsky waterfall, and the old stronghold - Khotyn fortress, wander along the streets of Kamenets, visit karst caves near the village of Mlynky, Ternopol region.

Or during a tour of the castles of the Golden Horseshoe, visit the Zolochiv Pink Palace - a small corner of the East in the vast Galicia. Stop in at the castle in Podgortsy, where the Three Musketeers were filmed and when he sees it, the Third Part of the Marlezon Ballet comes to mind. To visit the Olesky castle, which served as a refuge from enemy raids back in the days of Kievan Rus. And along the way you will also see one of the greatest relics of the Orthodox world - the Pochaev Lavra. Look at the ruins of the old fortress on Mount Bonn and the Jesuit monasteries in Kremenets.

Or another option for excursions in Western Ukraine - to go to the West in the Carpathians. Meet the real fairytale atmosphere that surrounds the cascade of mountain waterfalls in Rostock, admire the beauty of the Dead Lake, admire the majestic landscapes in the tract stretched stones or on the rocks "Hawkeye".

The great thing is that all this can be done in one day. Whatever type of excursions in Western Ukraine you choose: castle tours Lviv, excursions to the Carpathians, excursions to waterfalls - you are guaranteed a maximum of impressions and positive emotions. The beauty of nature, ancient monuments of architecture, incomparable landscapes, romantic stories. All of this is compiled here. And all this you can see now.
