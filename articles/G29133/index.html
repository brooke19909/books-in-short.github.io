Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 * (India, Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 * is a beach hotel in the south of Goa (India). The hotel positions itself as a five-star. It is two hundred meters from the sea. It opened in 2005. It is believed that the hotel is in the most idyllic and romantic place. Holidays in Utord - the so-called village and the beach of the same name - does not at all resemble the usual pastime in the state of Goa, about which so many legends go. The Russians began to travel there only recently, therefore, in order to study comfort and service in this complex, we analyzed only the reviews of recent years. So, what attracts tourists to the hotel Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 *?



India, South Goa. Utorda

This is a great place for a relaxing holiday. Utorda Beach is called by many the pearl of South Goa. Despite this seemingly banal characteristic, many tourists share this opinion. The fact is that on this beach you can still find that pristine exotic beauty, for which tourists go to Goa. The beach is located between two coasts - Majorda and Arossim. Its natural boundaries are rivers flowing into the ocean. There are many palm trees, which not only creates the shadow necessary for vacationers, but also gives the beach a resemblance to advertising posters. The scenery here is simply incredible. Unlike the ‚Äúhippar‚Äù North Goa, people with average incomes, couples with children, come here. Hotels are often located directly on the beach or in the immediate vicinity. The real resort atmosphere reigns here.

Where is

Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 is located among tropical greenery, near the ocean. Next to it is Calangute and Colvia. Goa International Airport and Magdaon Railway Station are not too far, no more than twenty kilometers. This means that you do not have to worry about a long transfer, and if you get to the hotel on your own, then this is easy enough. From Dabolim Airport, you take a bus to Majorda, and from there you will be there by rickshaw or other public transport in a few minutes. The hotel is somewhat out of the way. Therefore, if you want to buy fruit at the local market, then you will have to get to the nearest village either by taxi or on foot along the shore. And this is about an hour's go. Therefore, it is better to carry out such sorties in the evenings when it is not too hot.



Territory

Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 is a range of stylish snow-white cottages. They are located on an area of ‚Äã‚Äãfive acres. The territory is very beautiful, well-groomed. The hotel provides vacationers with a parking place for cars, playgrounds and equipment for cooking meat and fish in the open air (barbecue). If you want to organize a seminar, then the hotel has five rooms of different capacities with all the necessary equipment. Closer to the beach entrance are large lawns with thick soft grass, where Indian wedding ceremonies are often held. Throughout the hotel there are stylish flower beds with tropical flowers, as well as various decorative compositions.

Where holidaymakers settle

Hotel Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 is not too large. It has a little over seventy rooms located in cottages of the "bungalow" type. They are comfortable and elegantly decorated. The rooms on the ground floor often have terraces and private gardens. Most of the rooms are the so-called club ones. There are also suites and royal suites. Some rooms have balconies and chic ocean views. You can sit there with rum and fruit in the evenings. The furniture is finished with dark wood. The beds are comfortable, orthopedic. All rooms are air-conditioned, with LCD flat-screen TVs and cable channels. Bathrooms feature a hairdryer. Each room is equipped with a fridge, kettle, coffee maker, DVD player, ironing board and iron. Every day, cleaners put two bottles of drinking water in the guest rooms, as well as various little things - shower gel, shampoo, soap. There is enough toilet paper. Maids make swans and other figures from towels. Soundproofing is good, noise from other rooms is not audible.



Service

Hotel Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 * (India, Uttorda), like other hotels of this class, gives guests the opportunity to use free "Wi-Fi." The reception is open around the clock. In addition to services for the resettlement and solving the problems of guests, its employees can put your valuables in a safe. There is also an information desk where you can find out everything you need both about the hotel services, and about the surrounding area and attractions. At the hotel you are washed with linen and clothes. You can exchange currency here. If you have small children, and you want to go somewhere alone, then the administrator will call the nanny. The hotel staff is very attentive and always ready to help. The rooms are cleaned with sufficient quality - tourists write that the scourge of Indian hotels is mold in the rooms. But in "Orchid" you will not see anything like it.



Entertainment

Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 * hotel (India) is loved by tourists who are fond of sports and oriental methods of healing. Here you can play such a noble game as mini-golf. Many guests, especially children, appreciated the giant chess. The hotel has a yoga center. There is a very good SPA center with professional masseurs and cosmetologists, as well as Ayurvedic treatments for both the face and body. For children there is a modern, safe playground. The hotel organizes excursions and trips in the surrounding area. Sometimes in the evening there are entertainment programs - songs and dances, although there is no animation as such. But many hotel guests prefer to leave after sunset on the beach necks. With the onset of the evening, fiery amusements begin here, the temptation of which is difficult to resist.



Nutrition

Those who have visited Goa know that most often the concept of included in the price of food that the tourist hotel provides vacationers includes only morning meals. Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 * is no exception. Breakfast is served for tourists in the Cosmos restaurant, from where you can see the ocean. There are always fruits, vegetables, several types of Indian hot dishes, lots of pastries, yoghurts and fruits. Fried eggs and omelets are cooked right in front of you, with various additives. In the same restaurant, tourists can have lunch and dinner. There is also a Sanken bar in the hotel, where good cocktails with exotic ingredients are poured. The restaurant has a Boat Quay grill, the cooks of which prepare excellent meat dishes. But most hotel guests prefer to dine on the beach, in bars and necks. Rice soup with seafood is especially good at such beach establishments. If restaurants are far from the hotel, then often vacationers are taken to the hotel for free. There are a lot of cafes outside the hotel, the choice is huge, and the prices are very different. One of the best restaurants outside the hotel, many vacationers called "Zibop", immediately outside the gate. But practically in any institution they cook excellently, and vacationers did not have any stomach problems, regardless of whether they ate in cheap necks or more expensive cafes.



Sea vacation

To get to the beach of the Royal Orchid Beach Resort 5 * hotel (India, Goa), you will have to go no more than five minutes through the tropical garden. On the sandy shore of the ocean are sun loungers and deck chairs in the shade of palm trees. The beach is gorgeous. The sand is small, clean and white, like powder. The hotel is the last on the beach strip, because there are always free sunbeds, clean and few outsiders. But you need to take strong suntan creams with you. The sun is evil, not even a couple of Turkey and Egypt, you can burn up instantly. The water is clear even when big waves begin. Most vacationers spend the whole day on the beach, and lovers of a healthy lifestyle use a strip of sand for morning jogging. Garbage on the beach is cleaned regularly, and lifeguards are on duty all day on the sea. There is an outdoor pool at the hotel. Around him are sunbeds, give beach towels. The bar is right in the water. While swimming, there you can order cocktails, juices, fruits, beer. There is also a children's pool with circles and water toys. Chlorine does not smell, very clean.

Excursions

For starters, you can walk around the neighborhood, visit the nearby beaches - especially Madjorda - and also explore the village itself. Here, in addition to the traditional set of "pharmacy-shops-bank" there is a beautiful Catholic church of the seventeenth century. If you want to see more attractions, the Royal Orchid Beach Resort spa 5 * hotel (South Goa, Utorda) can organize excursions. For all sorts of travel, prices are quite acceptable here. You will be taken in cozy, air-conditioned mini-vans, not in large buses. Most of the excursions are two-day. These are sightseeing trips to the cities, beaches and temples of Goa. But if you prefer close excursions, then you have at your service the Utord bus line, the stop of which is not far from the hotel, as well as a taxi and various rickshaws.
