How to apply for a loan at Post Bank: instructions and recommendations

You can always find a form for an online request on the official website of the financial institution in question. There is another service, namely a loan calculator, through which you can calculate loans online. Simply select the amount of the loan, set the terms and calculate monthly payments. An online loan application at Post Bank is filled out in accordance with some parameters. It indicates:

  • Information about the loan.
  • Passport details of the borrower.
  • Information about the official place of work.
  • Personal information about the composition of the family, contact phone numbers and more.

Filling out an online loan application at Post Bank, as a rule, takes about ten to fifteen minutes for customers. After the questionnaire is sent for verification, which lasts twenty minutes. In the event that the online application is approved, and the client is confirmed with a loan, he will need to contact one of the branches of this financial organization in order to sign documents and receive money. It is required to come to the bank no later than seven days after the approval of the request. In the event of a later deadline, the approval is simply canceled, and in order to obtain credit, the borrower will have to go through the entire verification procedure again. Now let's talk about the requirements that apply to customers.

Who can take loans on an online application?

Every Russian citizen who meets the following conditions will be able to get a loan through an online request:

  • Has a civil passport.
  • It is registered in any region of the country.
  • Has a permanent place of work or receives a pension.
  • May present the bank with its individual account insurance number.
  • Suitable for the financial structure by age (i.e., from eighteen to seventy years for women and from twenty-one years to sixty-five years for men).

How to check the loan application "Mail Bank", often asked. You can get comprehensive information by calling the numbers indicated on the official website of a financial institution, or by contacting the office. Also, SMS with the application status will come to your number.

Methods of obtaining and paying a loan

The procedure for receiving cash and paying the monthly installment for a loan that was issued online is absolutely no different from loans in a branch of a financial institution. Most often, money is transferred to the Bank Post card, which is issued simultaneously with the loan. You can cash out the amount at ATMs of the same bank without any commission or by contacting any other financial institution for a nominal fee. You can repay loans in the following ways:

  • By transfer from a card or from another bank account online.
  • By postal order.
  • By depositing cash through the terminals.
  • Through automatic charges.

Thus, each client can independently choose how to pay his loan.
