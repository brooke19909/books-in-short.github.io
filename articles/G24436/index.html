Camping Gelendzhik: description, photo

Wild rest has recently gained unprecedented popularity. A tent near the sea, fresh salty air and midnight gatherings with a guitar by the fire - for many this is enough for a good holiday. To tourists who prefer car travel, it was more convenient, in many resorts there are campsites. Gelendzhik is no exception. Consider the most popular holiday destinations for car travelers!

"Thin Cape"

It is in this place that we recommend going to all lovers of diving and spearfishing! Indeed, here the Black Sea is simply replete with wildlife and vegetation!

The sea in this place is quite clean, but the descent into the water complicates the rocky bottom.

And in the holiday season, right on the territory of the "Thin Cape" there are mini markets with a variety of souvenirs, water attractions. Scuba diving is also organized here.

"Blue Bay"

Autocamping "Blue Bay" in Gelendzhik is able to accommodate at least a hundred cars! And it is located just a three-minute walk from the beach belonging to the sanatorium of the same name. On the beach, by the way, you can rent catamarans and all beach equipment. There is a large sports ground in the Blue Bay. Shops and a wild beach are nearby. In addition, next to this Gelendzhik campsite is a ship stranded.

Vacationers say that the campsite is extremely picturesque. It is surrounded by deciduous trees, beautiful shrubs and pines. And quite nearby, a mountain river called Yashamba flows with amazing beauty.





Green Harbor

The name of this campsite in Gelendzhik speaks for itself - the territory was filled with acacias! And therefore, even on the hottest day, tourists who choose the Green Harbor can hide from the incinerating sunlight.

The "Harbor" is located between Gelendzhik and the small village of Divnomorskoye. To get to the city center, you need to drive only 10 minutes by car. The access to the Green Harbor is asphalted. And there is a campsite just five minutes of an unhurried walk from a wild beach. On the territory of this camping in Gelendzhik are located: a bar, rental of tents and hammocks.
