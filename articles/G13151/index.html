Important points

You need to be aware of some points when planning to visit the Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * Hurghada. Reviews of tourists indicate that sometimes check in a room has to wait long enough. Mostly newly arrived guests manage to get into their room no earlier than two in the afternoon. But this is in no way connected with the negligent or dismissive attitude of the staff. In fact, after the previous guests left the room at noon, it takes another two hours to bring it in perfect condition to meet new guests.

Another important point that you need to know about is the features of the Internet at the Hurghada Hotel Grand Plaza Hotel. Reviews indicate that far from the entire territory of the hotel is covered by the network. If you need to use wireless Internet in the pool or lobby, you will have to pay for this service. At the reception you can purchase special cards that give you the opportunity to use the global network for 30 minutes, one hour or a day.
