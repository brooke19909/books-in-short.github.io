Do-it-yourself jeans bag pattern: do it by eye, decorate with soul

If you have littered with unnecessary old jeans, then in one evening you can make yourself a new bag. The pattern of a bag of jeans with your own hands is done by eye, you need a minimum of effort, and special sewing skills are not needed. So, let's get to work.





In a couple of minutes

Do-it-yourself handbag will be ready in no time. Just take your old jeans, cut off your trousers in the manner of shorts. Spread the step seams, carefully trim the sides, rounding the edges, and stitch. It remains to add a handle to the bag. It can also be sewn from the remaining material or attached to it already made of leather, fabric or beads. Various hand-made jewelry will suit such a handbag. For example, brooches made of fabric in the form of flowers, badges, embroidery. So simple and unobtrusively we have extended the life of jeans almost to infinity.

Trouser Backpack





The source material, I think, is clear - jeans. This time we cut off the legs, unzip the step seams and sew them in pairs. This is how a jeans bag pattern looks like. With your own hands you will make a backpack for a school shift or sports uniform. Once you have a straight rectangle, consider it a practically ready backpack. It remains to decorate it and add ties. We will fasten the backpack with tapes. Just tuck the top of the fabric, insert the ribbons and sew. Tightening them, you will see that the backpack will take the correct bag-like shape. Instead of ribbons, you can use special rubber bands and mounts for them, which you can buy in the store for goods for needlewomen. Sewing bags with your own hands in this case will not be any different from sewing store analogues. You can decorate with sequins, rhinestones or buy ready-made decals. You can paint the finished model with acrylic paints on the fabric. You can choose the most unusual pattern, the main thing is that it is bright. Do not be afraid to go too far with paint. And even if you do not know how to draw, then on denim and ordinary blots will look appropriate and cool.

For our smaller brothers



Here we are talking about laptops, tablets and cell phones. For them, too, you can easily and quickly make a new thing. And we do not need a pattern of a bag of jeans, with our own hands we will make it even easier. Attach the object to the denim, outline with chalk. So you get one side. If we talk about a laptop, then a double case on a synthetic winterizer would be more appropriate here. This will protect your computer from mechanical stress and protect it from crashes. The lining is best made from cotton fabric. Remember to insert a zipper or buttons. Here it is necessary to think over a question of usability in more detail.

Variations on a Theme

Do-it-yourself jeans bag pattern is not the only thing you can do from old pants. Often, organizers for office supplies are made from pockets, comfortable aprons for the kitchen, and even a chair cover. True, here you will have to collect material from your neighbors as well. So do not rush to throw jeans, they can serve you for more than a dozen years, but in a different guise.
