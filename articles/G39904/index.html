Hotel Sunset Mansion 3 *: tourist reviews

Hotel Sunset Mansion 3 * is a cozy guest complex in which tourists will feel no less comfortable0 than at home.

General characteristics of the hotel

Sunset Mansion 3 * provides its guests with the opportunity to stay in modern rooms with an excellent layout. They have comfortable wooden furniture, as well as all sorts of amenities and household appliances. The bathroom is combined with the toilet. In addition, a pleasant surprise for hotel guests will be the presence of a private balcony in the apartment.

The hotel makes sure that its guests feel complete comfort and hospitality. For this purpose, the hotel has a 24-hour reception desk, the staff of which is always ready to help you. The services of a travel agency are also in great demand, where you can pick up an excursion tour for you according to individual parameters.

An important point of any holiday is a full and high-quality food. The hotel has an international restaurant. Here you can also get acquainted with the specific local culinary. There is a cozy cafe on the territory of the hotel complex, it specializes in fragrant pastries, as well as a variety of coffees.





Hotel Location

Sunset Mansion 3 * Resort (Phuket) is located just 500 meters from Patong Beach. And to get to the city center, you have to travel by bus for no more than 25 minutes. Just a 10-minute leisurely walk from the hotel is a popular nightclub.

Guest apartments

Guests of Sunset Mansion Resort Hotel 3 * (Phuket Island) have the opportunity to stay in one of the following comfortable rooms:

  • Standard apartment with a large double bed is a compact bedroom that can comfortably accommodate two guests. It has a sleek design and a high level of comfort.
  • Two-room rooms are perfect for travelers who like to relax with their families. These are 2 full bedrooms, each of which has its own exit to a small balcony. Also a feature of these apartments can be considered that the bathroom is equipped with a bath.
  • Double Suite - This is a spacious bedroom with a luxurious decoration, made in local traditions. In addition to it, there is also a cozy kitchen.




The amenities provided in the apartment

Resort Guest House Sunset Mansion 3 * provides its guests with the opportunity to use the following list of amenities:

  • individual air conditioning to create a comfortable microclimate in the room;
  • a modern TV that broadcasts a wide range of satellite channels (including several Russian-language ones);
  • a telephone that is connected to both an internal and an international line;
  • a minibar filled with a variety of drinks for an additional fee (can also be used as a refrigerator);
  • the bathroom has a hairdryer;
  • the apartments have standard 220 V sockets




Resort Infrastructure

Great attention is paid to the comfortable accommodation of guests of the Sunset Mansion 3 * hotel. Reviews of tourists indicate the presence of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • car parking, which all guests can use for free;
  • a small gym, which provides all the necessary equipment for a full workout;
  • on the ground floor there is a small convenience store where you can buy food and other essential goods;
  • You can use hairdressing services without leaving the hotel;
  • hotel guests are given the opportunity to rent a car or other vehicle;
  • there is a large conference room with modern furniture, computer and office equipment for meetings, seminars and other business events;
  • international restaurant
  • cafe bakery.




Hotel Policies

Many tourists stay at the Sunset Mansion 3 * hotel. O. Phuket boasts a huge number of hotels, each of which has its own characteristics and rules of accommodation. Regarding this guest house, they sound as follows:

  • registration and eviction of guests of this hotel is carried out strictly after two in the afternoon and before noon, respectively;
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring any animals to the hotel (regardless of breed and size);
  • Children under the age of four stay in this hotel complex for free (provided that they do not require extra beds);
  • an extra bed will cost 400 baht per night (both for adults and for children);
  • When going on vacation with babies, please note that the hotel does not provide for special cots;
  • You can use bank transfer to pay for the services received.




Positive feedback from tourists

Resort Mansion 3 * Resort (Phuket) is quite popular among tourists. Reviews reflect all the positive moments of rest in this hotel:

  • the hotel has a very quiet and calm atmosphere;
  • The staff who, in addition to the high level of professionalism, also demonstrate courtesy and oriental hospitality deserve the highest praise;
  • very fast registration and check-in procedure without unnecessary delays;
  • daily rooms are thoroughly cleaned and replenished with drinking water;
  • the beach is very close - literally 5 minutes walk by walking;
  • each room has a small kitchen where there is everything necessary for cooking (this is extremely important for those tourists who do not want to overpay for restaurant meals);
  • if there are free apartments in the hotel, you have the opportunity to check in without waiting for the check-in time (without any surcharges);
  • due to the presence of a balcony, you can dry things on the street without creating dampness in the room;
  • quite reasonable prices in the hotel cafe (the quality of food is excellent).




Negative reviews of tourists

Hotel Sunset Mansion 3 * (Phuket Island) leaves not only positive, but also negative impressions. Guest reviews indicate the following negative points:

  • a negative point is the lack of a swimming pool on the hotel‚Äôs territory (but this is offset by a rather low price for accommodation);
  • neither in the rooms nor at the reception of the safe is provided (despite the fact that no cases of theft were observed, there was still some concern for valuables);
  • the stairs leading to the upper floors are very steep, and therefore there is a high probability of falling and getting injured;
  • the hotel is located above the carriageway, which, in addition to noise, is also accompanied by an unpleasant smell of automobile exhaust;
  • sometimes there are overlays with availability of rooms, which is why you can temporarily be accommodated in a hotel of a much lower category;
  • virtually no own territory and any greenery.

It is worth noting that the Sunset Mansion 3 * guest house is an ideal place for unpretentious tourists who do not seek luxury, but come for new experiences. For relatively little money you can get quite comfortable, albeit modest, living conditions. But the money saved at the hotel is recommended to spend on fascinating excursions, thanks to which you can get to know more about local attractions and culture.
