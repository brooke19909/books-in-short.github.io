Palau Islands in the Pacific

The Palau Islands are a fairly new and unusual tourist destination. For residents of most Russian regions, it remains little known due to the significant distance from the usual main routes to places of beach recreation. But this country is unique and inimitable. And there are many reasons to put it on the list of places where you should definitely visit, if not in the first place, then a little later. Those who are lucky enough to go there, call the Palau Islands "a place where you want to take a one-way ticket and emigrate for permanent residence." Let it be said with a slight irony, but common sense in this statement, of course, is.



Palau Islands on a world map

Although this is not believed right away, an independent state is located on these islands with a population of less than twenty thousand people. He has officially established diplomatic relations with the Russian Federation. This proud and independent state is located on more than three hundred islands in the Pacific Ocean, east of the Philippine Archipelago. Most of these pieces of land are of coral origin. They are grouped around several peaks of underwater volcanoes. Many consider the islands of Palau a kind of paradise on earth. First of all, this point of view is shared by divers, pearl divers and other explorers of the ocean depths. Indeed, the underwater world of coral reefs is difficult to verbally describe. You need to see it with your own eyes. The diversity and brightness of the underwater flora and fauna, according to eyewitnesses, are superior to everything seen before.

But even if you do not want to go deeper, the Palau Islands provide all their guests with the opportunity of a luxurious beach holiday. The animal and plant world is in no way inferior to the underwater coral atolls. It is just as unique. The islands maintain high environmental standards, and there is no significant industrial production. The basis of the country's economy is tourism. The natural and climatic conditions of this part of the Pacific Ocean are stable and allow tourists to be received throughout the year without special regard to the season. This is a country of eternal summer with an average air temperature of just below thirty. The rainy season (from May to October) in the tropical climate of the islands does not have a special effect on the quality of beach holidays. Precipitation is light and short. In recent years, much has been done in the development of tourism infrastructure.


Island of palau. Tours from Russia

The Pacific direction is one of the most promising in the modern tourism business. Tours are in demand here, and attention to them has a clear tendency to increase. This is due, not least, to the fact that those Russians who have already visited the tropical islands share their impressions with those who are still considering the routes of their future trips. And this is the best advertisement you can imagine.
