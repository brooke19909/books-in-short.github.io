Guest houses in Golubitskaya by the sea: holiday reviews

The village of Golubitskaya is located in the suburbs of the city of Temryuk, on the isthmus that separates the Temryuk Bay from the Akhtanizovsky estuary. It stretched from east to west for four and a half kilometers along the Azov coast.

A sharp difference in height divided the village into two parts: lower and upper. The lower one is closer to the sea, while the upper one offers stunning views of the Sea of ​​Azov and the mud lake. The upper part is much calmer at night, as the noisy beach infrastructure is located in the lower part of the village.



Climatic features

The climate in this region is temperate continental, with mild winters and hot summers. In the village of Golubitskaya more than 270 sunny days a year. The average water temperature in the summer months is +24 ° C, and the air warms up in July to +30 ° C. However, the weather is often changeable, and even in the summer quite cool days are possible. The beach season begins in early June and ends in mid-October.

Beach

The village of Golubitskaya is a popular developing resort, which is preferred by lovers of a quiet secluded holiday, as well as families with children. There are many beaches stretching along the banks of the village. The beaches and bottom of the sea are sandy, in some areas - shell rock. The most popular central beaches are Sea Breeze and Youth. Here you will find plenty of water activities, you can relax in the many cafes and refresh yourself with cold drinks and ice cream.



At the very end of the village is a wild, or rather a nudist beach, which is chosen by lovers of a full tan.

Guest houses in Golubitskaya stanitsa

If you decide to spend your vacation in the village of Golubitskaya, then you are certainly interested in the question of where you can stay at this resort. You can rent a room in the private sector or settle in a hotel, but in recent years, guest houses have become increasingly popular. There are a large number of them in the Golubitskaya stanitsa . We will present some of them in this article.



"On the New West"

This modern guest house is located in the center of the village. The road to the beach takes no more than two minutes of a quiet walk. To accommodate guests are offered rooms with individual kitchens, which are equipped with modern appliances and a set of necessary utensils.

When decorating the rooms used high-quality, environmentally friendly materials and wood. Guests have access to free Wi-Fi. On a landscaped and green area there is a barbecue area, a playground for kids, free parking at the house.



Reviews guest houses Golubitskaya stanitsa, usually positive. For example, most vacationers believe that “On Novozapadnaya” is suitable for families with children. There is always a very calm and pleasant atmosphere. Children enjoy spending time on the playground. The rooms are very comfortable and well decorated.

Village Golubitskaya: guest house "Mayak"

A well-maintained and modern house is a ten-minute walk from the sea coast and the mud lake. Here you can choose the number of the category "comfort", "standard" and "economy". The rooms are equipped with new furniture and necessary appliances. On the well-groomed territory there is a large swimming pool, a gazebo entwined with ivy, a barbecue area with barbecue, an excellent playground, a kitchen with cooking utensils necessary for cooking, as well as a free guarded parking for twenty-five cars.



Near the guest house are:

  • dolphinarium;
  • aquapark;
  • entertainment center;
  • grocery store;
  • Cafe;
  • market.

The guests of Mayak are pleased that they spent their holidays here - the rooms are cozy, well decorated, the beach is nearby, there is the opportunity to eat cheaply in a nearby cafe or prepare food yourself.

"Azov"

On the territory of the recreation center "Golden Fish" is a wonderful place for summer holidays - this is the guest house "Azovsky" (Golubitskaya stanitsa). According to vacationers, it is simply created for a quiet family holiday. Families with very young children feel comfortable here, as the hotel is far from nightclubs and restaurants.

Many guest houses in Golubitskaya are located near the sea, and Azov is located on its very shore. Nearby there is a wonderful playground with swings, carousels, sandboxes, etc. The beach is sandy and shell. On the shore there are trees that create a shadow in the summer heat. The bottom here is flat without stones.



For regular customers in the "Azov" system of discounts. If you book rooms for ten days, you will receive a “Set for a tired traveler” as a gift, and for guests who come for three weeks, a discount on accommodation is 3% of the cost.

Meals in this guest house are not provided, but in two-room rooms there is a kitchen, and for one-room rooms - one for four rooms.

Reviews

Mostly guests are happy with their stay in this resort village. Guest houses by the sea, which include the "Azov", attract a convenient location, well-groomed territory. True, this guest house has serious complaints. For example, the rooms are not cleaned, and guests are forced to carry it out on their own. There are complaints of a rare change of bed linen and towels. We hope that the owners of this guest house will take note of these reviews and eliminate the shortcomings.

"Embankment, 14"

Not all guest houses in Golubitskaya receive guests year-round. “Embankment 14” is a pleasant exception. The modern guest house, where you can relax not only in the summer, but also to celebrate the New Year, is located almost on the seashore.

For accommodation, spacious rooms are offered “standard” and “comfort”, each of which has an individual design, they are furnished with modern furniture and household appliances. One-room rooms are designed for two vacationers, two-room rooms for five.

The landscaped landscaped area has created excellent conditions for relaxation (gazebos, swings, benches under a canopy), there is also a playground with slides, sandboxes, large inflatable toys and a decorative pond. The guest house has a sauna and massage room, a small gym with exercise equipment.



The owners of the hotel did not ignore lovers of an active lifestyle. They can play table tennis, volleyball, table football. Guests who come to rest by car do not have to worry about their car - there is a free secure parking on site.

"Amber bunch"

Today, many Russian tourists are attracted by the village of Golubitskaya. Guest houses by the sea can be chosen for every taste. For example, the three-story guest house "Amber Cluster", offering its guests standard and luxury rooms, is a ten-minute walk from the beach. But it is also notable for the fact that on its territory there is its own liquor store, a bar and a tasting room.



The territory is beautifully landscaped and decorated with flowers. There is a large outdoor pool, gazebos, a playground for young guests and a barbecue area. This hotel provides organized, full-charge meals in the dining room.

"Bermuda Triangle"

This guest house enjoys a picturesque, quiet location. It offers comfortable rooms, as well as a Flying Dutchman room on the third floor. It consists of a bedroom, living room, kitchen, toilet room and a glass "deck" (relaxation room). This is a great option for a honeymoon or a family vacation.



The hotel has self-catering, but if you do not want to cook, you can visit the neighboring cafes and dining rooms. The guest house has its own sandy beach with sun loungers and parasols.

This guest house has already won many regular visitors. Reviews about him leave people who have been vacationing here for several years in a row. And I must say that they are all just enthusiastic: perfect cleanliness, tranquility and comfort, all the conditions for a vacation with children. Many kind words are addressed to the owners of the hotel.

Reviews about the rest in the village

The village of Golubitskaya is a modern developing resort, which may well compete with many villages on the Black Sea coast. Rest here has several advantages: the absence of a large number of vacationers, a clean sea and a beach, quite affordable prices. In the village there are night clubs, a cafe, a restaurant. Families with children will be pleased with the show programs in the dolphinarium and relaxation in the water park. There are also sights here, the main of which are a mud lake and an active mud volcano, from time to time frightening vacationers who have arrived in the village for the first time.
