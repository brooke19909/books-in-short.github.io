Crocheting squares begins with a chain of 20 stitches in length. Then the knitting is rotated and the columns of the next row are knitted from each air loop. When turning the knitting and moving to the next row, you need to knit 1 air loop - it will lift the work up without reducing the width of the knitting. But you need to make sure that from this loop then you do not inadvertently tie a new column, because then the width of the product will increase.

Now you should learn the secrets of crocheting ornaments. In the place where the first column of a different color begins according to the scheme, you need to bind another to the main thread. We carefully lay the main thread on the edge along the knitting and knit the string with a thread of a different color so that the thread that is no longer needed is inside the knitting. As soon as the required number of columns of a different color ends, we also lay along the edge (extending it to the left) a free thread, and the main color thread comes into play again. Indeed, while working in a different color, although she was not included in the case, she walked along the course of knitting, simply hiding between the sides of the column.

For a T-shirt, however, it will be necessary to make additional tying in the side seams, since the T-shirt pattern cannot be a multiple of a certain number of squares, like a plaid or rug.
