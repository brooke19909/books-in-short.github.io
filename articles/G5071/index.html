Hotel Avra ​​Budget Beach Hotel 1 * (Corfu, Greece): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Tourists can find comfortable accommodation here without any problems. For example, Avra Budget Beach Hotel 1 * offers quite comfortable rooms to vacationers .

This budget hotel complex is located directly in the village of Benitses, not far from its center. The beach is just 40 meters from the hotel. That is, tourists can reach the sea in just a couple of minutes.

What is a complex

The resort welcomes guests of Benitses Hotel Avra Budget Beach Hotel 1 * in a small cozy two-story building. This hotel was built a very long time ago - in the 40s of the last century. Originally owned by the Spinoulas family. The hotel was later sold by them. Representatives of the Monro family bought the hotel. At the moment, the complex is completely renovated, but still retains home comfort and a quiet atmosphere of the middle of the last century.





From most other Greek hotels, Avra Budget Beach Hotel (Corfu) is distinguished by the fact that it is allowed to stay with cats or dogs. Smoking on the territory of this hotel is possible only in places specially designated for this purpose.
