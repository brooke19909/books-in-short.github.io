Makadi Bay (Hurghada) - a resort for a relaxing family vacation

Egypt is one of the oldest countries. It attracts many travelers with the richness of its cultural traditions and well-developed tourist infrastructure. Egypt is good for relaxing throughout the year. However, the most popular times are Christmas holidays and New Years.

Those who have chosen Egypt for their holidays are welcomed by the popular Hurghada, the magnificent Sharm El Sheikh. Greets tourists sanatorium "Safaga", Makadi Bay. A popular holiday destination is the so-called Egyptian Venice - El Gouna.



An amazing and hospitable country is an inexpensive place for a massive beach holiday. On the coast, located along the Red Sea, divers and surfers like to relax. Some admire the coral, others are happy to catch the wave. The warm sea, wonderful climate, sandy beaches, magnificent hotels, numerous excursions, as well as the exotic of the ancient country make the vacation truly unforgettable.

New tourist destination

Resorts of Egypt, located on the Red Sea coast, provide numerous tourists with a great opportunity for an affordable, enjoyable and unforgettable vacation. One of them is Makadi Bay. Hurghada is located very close to it, at a distance of thirty kilometers. The history of one of the most fashionable resorts of exotic Egypt began in the mid-nineties of the last century. Until that time, there was only a boundless coast and desert on the territory. More recently, numerous crowds of tourists visited Hurghada. However, in Makadi Bay, the water in which there were a great many marine animals was much cleaner. That is why this territory began to be developed.

Hotels

Currently, Makadi Bay (Hurghada) is a small resort village. It is intended mainly for family vacations. Those for whom the purpose of travel is Egypt (Hurghada, Makadi Bay), hotels can choose only four- and five-star hotels . In this resort, by Egyptian standards, vacationers are offered a high level of service.





In a large area with sandy beaches and sea reefs are hotel chains. Among them are the Swiss Inn, Le Meridien, Iberote, and Sol Y Mar. Many hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis. Their architectural solutions are amazing. On the territory of Makadi Bay (Hurghada), all hotels are built taking into account Egyptian traditions and look like bungalows.

Entertainment

Makadi Bay (Hurghada) is the choice of those who love a calm and secluded family vacation. Youth at this resort will be bored. All entertainment in Makadi Bay is located on site. Outside of their territories you can find only a few souvenir shops. Makadi Bay (Hurghada) is a resort that does not have nightclubs and discos, numerous restaurants and bars. However, this is the highlight. In Makadi Bay, you will find a serene and relaxing holiday.

Excursions

While relaxing in Makadi Bay, you can go on an excursion to Hurghada. Children will love the Sinbad or Titanic water park. One of the most popular excursions in Hurghada is a bathyscaphe dive.



Divers Makadi Bay offers a trip to sea caves and coral reefs. During this amazing tour you can admire the beauty of the inhabitants of coastal waters.

From Makadi Bay, trips to the Bedouins are organized. In addition to acquaintance with the life of the Egyptians, vacationers are invited to ride in jeeps in the desert and enjoy delicious dishes of national cuisine.

One of the most interesting excursions is a trip to Cairo, during which you can admire the Egyptian pyramids and visit the National Museum with your own eyes. No less popular is the trip to the city of Luxor, located in the Valley of the Pharaohs. From Hurghada you can go to Israel and visit Jerusalem, see the Wailing Wall and the Road of Suffering, on which Jesus walked to the place of his execution.
