Rose Resort 4 * (Turkey / Kemer): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The coast of Kemer is very popular among Russian tourists, as it offers not only a beach holiday, but also a lot of opportunities to have fun and have fun. And today, many people going on a trip are interested in the Rose Resort 4 * hotel complex. What services does the hotel offer? Is it possible to come here with children? Who needs a holiday in the complex?

Hotel Location





The hotel complex Rose Resort 4 * is located in the famous resort town called Kemer. This is a picturesque place on the seashore, which is surrounded on all sides by lush gardens and mountains. The distance to the nearest international airport is fifty kilometers - the transfer is provided by either a hotel or a travel company of your choice. And another famous resort area - Antalya - is located forty kilometers from the city.

In fact, one of the advantages is precisely the location of the Rose Resort 4 * hotel. Kemer-center is only 1 km from the hotel - it can be reached even on foot. And on the territory of the city center, of course, noisy markets, large shopping centers, exciting attractions and interesting sights await you.

Brief description of the hotel complex

Rose Resort Hotel 4 * is a small hotel complex. Its area is about four thousand square meters. Nevertheless, the hotel stands out for its convenient and well-thought out infrastructure. Naturally, there is a swimming pool, as well as sun terraces, secluded arbors. The whole territory is decorated with palm trees, flower beds and ornamental plants. Moreover, despite its modest size, the hotel even has its own garden area. On the one hand, the complex adjoins the mountains, and on the other, a large orange garden. In fact, many travelers admire the exotic beauty of the Rose Resort 4 * hotel.

Kemer is a great place to relax. And this hotel is suitable for a quiet family vacation, as well as for a fun youth and even a rich excursion. Here, every traveler will be able to find an interesting occupation and return home, full of vivid impressions and pleasant memories.

What do the numbers look like?

The hotel complex Rose Resort 4 * is considered not too big. Nevertheless, it offers its customers accommodation in one of 125 spacious, comfortable rooms. By the way, for the most part they offer standard rooms of different capacities - single, double and triple. But there are several family rooms designed for a large family or company.





It is immediately worth noting that the rooms here are really large and bright, furnished in a modern style. In the rooms you will find all the necessary furniture for comfort, including soft beds, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a dressing table with a mirror and chairs. Each room has a private balcony with breathtaking views of the sea, mountains or the blooming orange garden.

There is also a separate bathroom. In the spacious bathroom you can always take a shower, as hot and cold water is available around the clock. A large wall mirror and a washbasin are also available. For added convenience, hair dryers are provided. In addition, each client receives a set of towels, as well as branded personal care products, which are replenished every day.

Of course, the rooms are equipped with the necessary appliances for comfort. In particular, the air conditioning system will help to create optimal living conditions in the room. The management system is individual. You can watch satellite channels on a large-screen TV, including several Russian ones. There is also a telephone with direct dialing function in the room, but it is worth considering that you will have to pay extra for long-distance or international calls. And if we are talking about a paid service, then you also have to pay for using the safe, but the price here is symbolic. The minibar is filled only at the request of residents - the cost of drinks delivered to the room is paid separately.

That is how the rooms at the territory of the Rose Resort 4 * hotel look. Reviews of Russian tourists confirm that the quality claimed by the hotelier is true. The cleaners do their job perfectly - the rooms are always clean and regularly ventilated, and towels are changed every day. New bedding is delivered to the rooms twice a week. By the way, room service is available around the clock, but this service also applies to paid services.

Meal plan for guests

What kind of food does the hotel complex Rose Resort 4 * offer its guests? Reviews of Russian tourists indicate that the cuisine here is really at a high level. This hotel offers its guests all-inclusive meals. This means that the traveler can count on breakfast, lunch, dinner, as well as light snacks during the day and local drinks (alcoholic and non-alcoholic). But fresh juices and imported drinks are paid separately.





Three times a day, Rose Resort 4 * hotel invites its customers to the main restaurant. There is a large outdoor terrace for 300 people, as well as a no less spacious indoor hall that can accommodate up to 350 guests at a time. The restaurant operates according to the "buffet" system - visitors themselves choose their favorite dishes and drinks. By the way, the cuisine here is original, as local chefs delight guests every day with incomparable culinary masterpieces, which are a very original mixture of Western and Eastern traditions. Here you will find meat, fish and seafood dishes, as well as fresh salads, plenty of fruits, traditional Turkish delicacies, as well as homemade ice cream and fresh pastries.

During the day, you can also visit the bar located in the lobby of the main building - here you will find desserts, light snacks, real Turkish coffee, as well as a huge selection of spirits and original cocktails. And at the pool bar, you will always be treated to chilled drinks.

Conditions for beach and water recreation

Beautiful sandy beaches are just what Turkey can offer the traveler. Hotel Rose Resort 4 * owns its own stretch of the Mediterranean coast about a hundred meters long. The beach is located just 250 meters from the hotel complex. It is worth noting that residents of a neighboring hotel, which is part of the same Rose hotel chain, can also use it.





The beach here is sandy and pebble, so you should take care of suitable shoes. Descent into the sea is gentle and safe. The coast is not too deep, so even children can swim here. On the shore of the hotel guests are waiting for comfortable sunbeds, protective umbrellas and changing cabins. They can be used for free.

If you do not have enough sun baths and transparent sea waves, then you should pay attention to more active types of recreation. In particular, the beach has a well-equipped volleyball court. And for a very reasonable price you can do water sports. For example, you will have the opportunity to go boating or pedal boats, boats or jet skis, as well as water skiing. There is also the opportunity to go scuba diving or parasailing.

Is a children's vacation possible here?

The availability of appropriate conditions for children's recreation for parents traveling with children is a matter of first importance. And the hotel complex Rose Resort 4 * is ready to provide your child with a lot of fun and entertainment.





For starters, it's worth talking about amenities. Upon request of parents, an extra rollaway bed will be delivered to the room. As for nutrition, the restaurant has a huge selection of dishes that will not only please the child, but also, undoubtedly, will benefit the growing body.

As for leisure, it is perfectly organized at the hotel. Children between the ages of four and twelve can spend time at the kids club. Here professional educators will be engaged with them. Children will be able to engage in creativity, take part in special educational games, or even enroll in educational courses.

For young travelers, the hotel has a special children's pool, where children can swim and have fun under the supervision of vigilant staff. And here, animators work with children, who arrange fun games and real water competitions. Your child will also be able to have a great time on the playground with a variety of swings and slides. And in the evening, a small disco is organized for children.

Hotel Rose Resort 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): additional service





Like any other good hotel, Rose Resort offers its customers some additional services that will help to eliminate all arising domestic problems. To begin with, it is worth noting that the reception is open around the clock. In addition, the hotel accepts credit cards. And if necessary, you can use the services of a currency exchange.

Clothes can be taken to the hotel laundry - this service is paid. If you wish, you can rent a car here. In fact, car rental in the resort town is a completely common thing. After all, this greatly facilitates movement around the neighborhood, and it is quite inexpensive. A rented car can be left in the hotel parking, which is guarded around the clock.

And the hotel has a fairly large business center. In the local spacious conference room, business meetings or seminars are regularly held - by the way, hotel staff will be happy to take care of organizing an event. Here you can also fax, use a computer, the Internet, as well as a copier, printer or scanner.

In case of health problems you can always call a doctor. The hotel also has shops selling essential goods and interesting souvenirs. And at the hotel you can use the services of a professional photographer who will happily hold a photo shoot.

Entertainment and leisure

What entertainments can the hotel complex Rose Resort 4 * offer you? Kemer is an amazing city that has been tourism for many years. Here they know exactly what foreign travelers need.

To begin with, it is worth noting that the hotel complex has a large outdoor pool, around which there are sun loungers and umbrellas. By the way, almost every day fun and healthy classes in water aerobics are held here . And in the evening or cold weather, you can swim and relax in the indoor pool with a water heating system.





In addition, the hotel offers some wellness treatments. In particular, you should definitely visit the local Turkish bath - this is a recommendation from all tourists who visited this hotel. Massage rooms are constantly working - here, professional, experienced massage therapists are ready to provide their services. In addition, customers are offered other procedures, including peeling, body wraps, therapeutic baths, etc. In the beauty parlor you will be provided with high-quality facial skin care. And you will have the opportunity to visit the sauna.

Women will be delighted at the presence of a beauty salon, where qualified cosmetologists and talented stylists work. And on the territory of the hotel there is a gym and a fitness center, where visitors are offered various group and individual sports classes.

You can also always visit a small cinema and enjoy watching your favorite movie. In the evening, a disco club opens its doors where you can have fun. And do not forget about the animation, as a team of cheerful, groovy guys works on the hotel territory - you will find comedy performances, oriental dances, shows with fire, acrobatic performances and, of course, live music.

Excursion holidays in the territory of Kemer

What else can a tourist hotel complex Rose Resort 4 * offer? Kemer center is within walking distance, so each guest will have the opportunity to stroll through the city center, look at local shops, try national delicacies in one of the restaurants or visit interesting entertainment centers.

Of course, this is not all that Turkey can offer the traveler. Rose Resort 4 * has its own tour desk. If you are a history buff, then you should definitely use this service, because a country with such a rich tradition really has something to show.

The most popular place for tourists is the so-called Fiery Mountain - a unique natural phenomenon worth seeing. Legend has it that in ancient times there lived a terrible monster - Chimera, which spewed flames of fire on the inhabitants of the city. The ruins of Phaselis, one of the most ancient cities in Turkey, are located near the resort town. It is believed that the first settlements on this site were created in the sixth century BC by colonists from the island of Rhodes. You will also have the opportunity to explore the ruins of the city of Olympus, located at the foot of the mountain of the same name. You can also have a great time on the top of Tahtali Mountain, where tourists climb the cable car, enjoying the breathtaking scenery.

If you are interested in more modern attractions, then you should definitely visit the dolphinarium, where vibrant performances with the participation of marine life are regularly held. A large water park with water slides is sure to appeal to children. Tourists also like to stroll through the park complex with the romantic name "Moonlight". In fact, there are many interesting places on the territory of the city and its environs, so tourists are provided with a rich excursion vacation.

Hotel prices

The cost of living is one of the most important issues for tourists. And in this regard, the Rose Resort 4 * hotel offers pretty favorable conditions. Of course, the final cost is influenced by many factors, including the price policy of the travel agency you have chosen, as well as the time of booking, the date of arrival, the selected room, etc. Nevertheless, on average a day at the hotel will cost you about 3000 rubles. Agree, this is not so much, given the service offered by the hotel and the level of service. And, of course, do not forget that some services, including excursions, are paid separately.
