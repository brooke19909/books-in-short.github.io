Rusfinance Bank. Reviews

Rusfinance Bank is a newly formed company, one of the best organizations of this type. It is developing in the field of consumer lending and offers a wide range of services, among which there are car loans, which became popular with the development of technology. You can also use loans at points of sale, get a loan in cash, which is very convenient. The company also issues credit cards. Reviews about Rusfinance Bank can be viewed on the Internet.

The company has office points located throughout Russia - in 110 cities, from the remote Kaliningrad to the city at the opposite end - Vladivostok. The Rusfinance Bank Society is included in almost the largest international financial group in the Eurozone - Societe Generale. The results of 2012 showed that this company ranks third in the ranking in terms of volume and number of issued car loans. The first and second places were taken by Sberbank and VTB24.

The head office of the credit organization is located in Samara. Currently, it has about 14,800 partners and has its own representative offices in 63 regions of the Russian Federation. There are 10 additional offices, and credit and cash offices - 127. The bank is gaining an increasing number of customers. And with their growth, the curiosity of those who have not yet used the services of the bank, and more and more often in the search engines there is a request: "Rusfinance Bank reviews." The institution is dynamically developing, supplementing and updating programs, the number of loans granted is growing, and consumers are attracted.

The company's policy is simple: to provide loans to customers under favorable conditions for both parties. There are a number of consumer lending programs. For some of them, you can purchase furniture, appliances, household and personal goods, and much more with a small overpayment.

Every citizen of the Russian Federation can improve the quality of his life thanks to an organization that provides financial services at a global level with a stable long-term basis. Communicating with customers, Rusfinance Bank (reviews confirm this) uses the principles of the market, complies with business standards and current legislation.

Car loans

For flexible terms, the bank can provide a loan that allows you to purchase a car of any brand. Including the sale can be carried out with a private person. With a minimum set of documents, it is possible to obtain a loan in the amount of 90% of the cost of the car itself. The company cooperates with many car manufacturers, which speaks of wide connections that allow creating the most favorable conditions for signing the contract. Rusfinance Bank has very positive reviews about this type of activity. No cheating and gray schemes have ever been noticed.

Credit card issuance

Rusfinance Bank issues VISA credit cards. There is a grace period of 50 days. Interest is accrued only on real debt. The company has developed a flexible system that allows repaying a loan and reusing it without any difficulties.

Consumer loan

In Rusfinance, the loan has the peculiarities that, having issued it, you can purchase almost any consumer goods in stores, the number of which exceeds 7 thousand. Loan terms are 3-24 months.

Cash loan

The client can get a cash loan in the amount of 5 to 50 thousand rubles from the company Rusfinance Bank. The feedback from its many clients can help a newcomer to better understand the system of the institution, although it is already so simple. The procedure for obtaining a cash loan was simplified: the amount of all payments that are required is fixed initially and then does not change at all during the entire repayment period.
