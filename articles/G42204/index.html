Hotel Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 * (Nabeul, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

The atmosphere of luxury and comfort will pamper travelers with the Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 * resort complex. Choosing this hotel for your vacation, you will experience a lot of pleasant and unforgettable moments.





A little bit about the institution

Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 * is the newest hotel in the Novostar Hotels chain. He opened his doors for tourists for the first time in 2012. It is worth noting that the hotel staff went through a rigorous selection process, and therefore you can be sure that you will be served by real professionals. There are also Russian-speaking employees here.





Hotel location

Hotel Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 * is located in the popular resort of Nabeul (almost in its very center). It is 125 km from Monastir and 65 km from Enfid.

Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 *: room description

The quality of rest is largely determined by the living conditions. Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 * offers travelers 361 rooms. Apartments can be divided into the following categories:

  • Standard rooms are designed for single and double occupancy. The room has everything you need: orthopedic beds, a desk and an easy chair for relaxation.
  • Family apartments can accommodate up to 6 people (subject to extra beds). The adult bedroom has a king size bed, and the children's bedroom has folding sofas. Each room has its own bathroom.
  • Suite - luxury apartments. The bright bedroom has a large orthopedic bed opposite the panoramic window. The living room is equipped with a seating area and a coffee table.

It is worth paying special attention to the interior solution of the Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 * complex. Photos show a modern, concise design in soothing colors.





Apartment Amenities

Hotel Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 * cares about the comfort of guests. For this purpose, the rooms are equipped with the following list of amenities:

  • combined bathroom, which is additionally equipped with a hairdryer;
  • some rooms have access to a private furnished balcony;
  • thanks to the air conditioner, you can take refuge in the room from the Tunisian heat;
  • satellite television with a package of Russian channels;
  • a landline telephone provides round-the-clock communication with the reception, and also makes it possible to make international calls;
  • the safe is responsible for the safety of your valuables;
  • wireless Internet;
  • in a small refrigerator you can always store a stock of food and drinks.

Hotel Policies

Each hotel sets its own rules regarding accommodation. In this institution the following apply:

  • use of air conditioning is available to guests only from June to September;
  • the schedule of bars and restaurants can be changed by the administration depending on the occupancy of the hotel;
  • registration is made from 14:00 to 16:00;
  • to leave a room on the day of departure, guests are required to noon;
  • smoking is strictly prohibited in all areas of the hotel (this is only possible on open areas and balconies);
  • a visit to the playground is allowed only for babies under 12 years old;
  • if the hotel has less than 20 children resting, the dietary line of the buffet is abolished;
  • it is not recommended to appear in bathing suits anywhere except the beach and pools;
  • taking out towels from the rooms is strictly prohibited (beach towels can be taken from the administrator);
  • the removal of food from the restaurant is not allowed;
  • visiting catering establishments requires compliance with a dress code (elegant, closed clothing);
  • use of the pool after 18:00 is strictly prohibited (at this time, cleaning devices begin to work);
  • in order to avoid injuries and unpleasant situations, it is forbidden to enter the hall in wet clothes and shoes;
  • if for health reasons the guest cannot go down to the restaurant, then the food will be delivered to his room.

General impression

Based on reviews of Novostar Khayam Garden Beach & Spa 4 * hotel, we can conclude that it deserves five stars. Of course, there are a lot of shortcomings in it, but, compared with numerous advantages, they are not so significant. The hotel is very beautiful and well-groomed (both inside and out), the staff is polite and competent, the atmosphere of relaxation is light and relaxed.

If we talk about the advantages of the hotel, then in the first place, of course, it is worth putting animation. The guys literally go out of their way so that vacationers do not have to get bored for a second. At the same time, entertainment is not imposed on anyone. It is also worth noting a lot of opportunities for children's recreation. The mini-club is significantly different from those that can be found in other hotels. Here, kids are immersed in creativity and fun.

The hotel staff is very welcoming and helpful. Especially touching look Tunisian attempts to communicate with guests in your own language. However, if you have a serious problem, you can always contact the Russian-speaking administrator. Particular attention is paid to the safety of guests, and therefore there is a large team of security guards and rescuers.

If we talk about the shortcomings of the hotel, the main one is the quality of cleaning, or rather, problems with replacing bedding. Perhaps, in no hotel with such perseverance they will not hang towels thrown to the floor back onto the hook. However, this problem is quickly resolved by contacting the administrator. In addition, arriving in the room in the evening after a busy and bright resort day, you will most likely not notice any flaws in the room.
