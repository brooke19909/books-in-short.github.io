Hotel Margaret Hotel 3 * (Rhodes, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Margaret Hotel 3 * hotel is located almost in the center of Rhodes. The center is only 1 kilometer away. Nearby attractions include the Temple of Apollo and the Acropolis. Places worth visiting include the Yeni Hammam, Diagoras Stadium and the Kollakium district, and you will have to drive about 10 km. Diagoras Airport is just a stone's throw away - a couple of minutes by car. On the one hand, it is convenient, but on the other hand, no. After all, residents of hotels almost around the clock hear the noise of taking off and landing aircraft. It interferes with both sleeping and just relaxing. The hotel windows offer a gorgeous view of the beautiful garden with exotic trees.

The hotel has three stars. It has 259 rooms, including standard rooms and studios. There are no rooms for one person in the hotel.





Hotel facilities

Margaret Hotel 3 * reviews received mostly positive. Guests note that each room has everything you need for a comfortable stay:

  • Hot and cold water.
  • Towels, hairdryer and soap products.
  • Balcony with a gorgeous view.
  • Radio, TV, kettle, fridge.
  • Wi-Fi is available only in public areas.
  • All necessary furniture: wardrobe, bedside table, bed, chairs, mirror. All furniture is not new, but in good condition.
  • For a pleasant microclimate, each room has air conditioning, and for the safety of expensive items, each room has a safe. Air conditioning and a safe are charged separately - 5 euros per day.
  • Meals are not included in the price, but there is the opportunity to cook yourself.
  • Rooms are not cleaned every day.
  • Kitchen with everything you need for cooking and eating.

Room prices at Margaret Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) are relatively low, they justify their three stars.

Many residents recommend taking rooms on the ground floor. They have small terraces with tables, and most importantly, a gorgeous view of the sea and the nature of the island of Rhodes. On the terrace in the evenings you can have dinner. Breakfast there will not work, because in Rhodes already at 7 in the morning there is heat.





Hotel infrastructure

There is a reception, where you can always come up on any issue. You will be politely served and solve your problems. Guests among the positive moments of their stay at the hotel note that you can communicate in English and Russian, which is very convenient. Near the hotel there is a free secure parking.

disadvantages

Margaret Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) reviews receive both positive and negative. Many residents complain about the presence of cockroaches in the rooms. They are trying to fight them, but so far without success. Another indisputable minus of the hotel can be called poor soundproofing, because all the partitions are made of drywall. In the evenings, people gather in the hotel lobby, there is a bar for everyone, even for those who do not live in the hotel. They play billiards, music sounds, and everyone just talks loudly. It lasts until late at night. It is useless to complain to the administration, because they themselves often rest there.





Entertainment

Margaret Hotel 3 * (Rhodes, Greece) has an outdoor pool where you can swim and sunbathe on a sun lounger at any time. Next to the adult is a children's pool. Nearby there is a bar with a variety of drinks and cocktails, as well as light snacks and desserts. Pleasant light music plays.

Open sea

If you want to swim in open water, visit the beach with sun loungers and sun loungers for 3 euros per day. The beach is pebble. Tourists recommend going into the sea exclusively in shoes, because pebbles are large, and sometimes sharp. You can injure your legs. From the hotel to him on foot 10 minutes walk. This beach belongs to Margaret Hotel 3 *. If you go a little to the side, you will find yourself on a more comfortable and equipped beach with cleaned sand, new sunbeds, a bar and a rental of surfing equipment. By the way, the weather for surfing is ideal, because in the town there is often a storm and wind blowing. Therefore, there are much more surfers here than just swimmers. Due to strong winds, it will not be possible to sunbathe or swim normally. Is that a plunge and run ashore. It is worth noting that it is in this place that the two seas join, the Aegean and the Mediterranean.





Where to eat better?

Around the Margaret Hotel 3 * (Greece) there are a lot of different bakeries and bakeries, from where a very pleasant smell of fresh pastries is constantly heard. Nearby there is a small market where you can buy fresh vegetables and fruits. Tomatoes, peaches and melons on the island are simply divinely delicious, because the sunny climate is ideal for growing these crops. Also nearby is a supermarket where food prices are relatively low. It is also worth visiting the fish shop, where there is always fresh seafood. There you can try them or buy and cook yourself.





What to visit in Rhodes?

Where else can I go to Rhodes? If you want a really good beach holiday, you should go to Feliraki. The road will take 15-20 minutes. This is a beautiful well-kept beach with clean sand. There are a lot of entertainment on the beach, rental of beach equipment, cafes, attractions.

In Rhodes, people will literally go everywhere and offer the services of guides and tour operators. They will assure you that without their services you cannot travel and see the most interesting. Do not believe it. Armed with a map of the city, the Internet and the ability to communicate, you can visit all the sights yourself, while saving a decent amount. Moreover, transport on the island runs regularly and is inexpensive, you can easily get from Margaret Hotel to any place of interest to you. In two days you can see the old Rhodes in full. Be sure to visit the ancient fortress of the island. She really deserves attention. In the old town you will be surprised by narrow streets paved with paving stones, breathtaking architecture, carved doors. Everywhere there is a lot of greenery weaving around the houses of flowers. Here you can truly experience the spirit of the Middle Ages. Be sure to sit in the cafe and try the local cuisine.





If you have already seen Rhodes, take a trip to Lindos. This is an incredibly beautiful city, which is located on the neighboring island of Symi.
