Negative reviews

Despite the overall positive picture of reviews, this hotel also received a number of negative comments:

  • The menu of the restaurant is very poor choice, and the prices are quite high.
  • The rooms are incredibly stuffy.
  • A very small assortment of dishes on the buffet menu.
  • The room has very pale lighting, and therefore it is problematic to read in the evening.
  • In the bathroom, the hood does not work well, because of which condensation collects and smells of damp.
  • Even in double rooms, a set of towels is provided for only one guest.
  • Noise isolation in the room can be said to be absent - you will hear the street, the corridor, and the neighboring rooms.
  • In standard rooms there are problems with electrical appliances - something is not working, something is clumsily wrapped in electrical tape.
  • Plumbing is poorly functioning, and the sink drain is clogged.
  • The staff responds politely and with understanding to all requests and comments, but in fact does nothing.
  • It seems that the bath has not been washed since the construction of the hotel.
  • The entire scenic view of the embankment is spoiled by a nearby construction project.

General impression

An indisputable advantage can be considered the picturesque area in which the Golden Ring Hotel is located. Kostroma, whose photo is simply mesmerizing, opens in a new light if you look at it from the window of a luxury hotel. It dominates the classic design with luxurious decor. Nevertheless, some rooms (especially standard ones) have many shortcomings - breakdowns, poor ventilation and more. In general, guests are satisfied with the living conditions, as well as their value for money.

As for the work of the staff, there is a dual impression. On the one hand, everyone is very polite, smiles warmly and listens attentively to every request. Nevertheless, almost no response occurs. True, few people pay attention to this, because here practically no one stops for a long time. As for the restaurant, it would not hurt to lower prices a bit and expand the menu. Nevertheless, the beautiful landscapes around the hotel and the incredible view from the window cover all of its shortcomings.
