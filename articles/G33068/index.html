Hotel Sunwing Waterworld Makadi (Egypt / Hurghada): detailed description and features

On the shores of the Red Sea, in the large tourist city of Hurghada, the cozy Sunwing Waterworld Makadi Hotel is located. Only 25 km separates the hotel from the airport. The boarding house is only a year old, but in this short period of time it has gained an excellent reputation and fell in love with foreigners. On the same territory there are several resort complexes, and guests are free to use all services.





This is a true paradise and entertainment center, where you will be able to spend a comfortable, interesting and fun vacation. Right on the territory, surrounded by tropical vegetation, there is a whole water park with water slides, a breakwater and an artificial waterfall. The vast majority of vacationers are families with children, couples in love and pensioners.

It combines an active, calm and sightseeing vacation. Exciting trips to the sights of the resort, numerous concert programs, amazingly clean beaches and the sea warm as fresh milk are waiting for you. You can pay for your stay at Sunwing Waterworld Makadi by cash and credit card (Mastercard, Eurocard, Visa).

Number of rooms

600 comfortable rooms await their guests. There are standard rooms, Junior Suite - improved two-room apartments, Family Suite - with a living room and a bedroom, designed for 6 people. All rooms are cleaned daily. Each of them adjoins a terrace with a picturesque panorama of the Red Sea and rich internal infrastructure.





Each room has a bathroom, a personal air-conditioning system, a paid bar with drinks (to be replenished upon request). In addition, you have a TV with connected satellite TV (broadcasting Russian channels), a telephone and a paid safe. Cots are available for children at the Sunwing Waterworld Makadi 5 with their parents.

Nutrition

Hotel guests eat 3 times a day, in addition - snacks throughout the day. Table setting is carried out in the main restaurant according to the "Swedish" concept. A children's menu has been developed separately for babies (highchairs are provided).





Guests can visit local restaurants at any time. Here they can taste Italian, Asian and national dishes. A fish restaurant is open for lovers of various delicacies. There is a tapas bar, a snack bar, a steakhouse and a lobby bar. A lot of establishments are concentrated nearby, where you will be fed inexpensively and efficiently.

Beach

Sunwing Waterworld Makadi Hotel has its own private beach. The coast is just a couple of miles from the building, strewn with fine sand. Nearby there are fenced coral islands, from where you can watch the life of marine mammals. Descent into the sea is quite comfortable and even - right from the shore.

For fans of diving, there are all the possibilities. On the beach it is better to wear special shoes. There is a water entertainment center and a free bar on the shore. Beach amenities are free for every tourist.





Additional Information

Sunwing Waterworld Makadi provides security and concierge services. In the open area for younger guests built a pool with five water slides and a sand embankment. There is also a developing club for children under 12 years old, a disco, a playground. Every day there is a fun animation program designed for different age groups. If desired, you can order a nanny to look after the child.

For adults: a gym equipped with a tennis court, a wave pool with a beautiful waterfall, basketball courts, football, aerobics (water aerobics), a billiard room. You can improve your health in the SPA salon, where you will be served at a high level, individual procedures will be recommended.

For business travelers, you can hold meetings in the conference room at any time, use fax, business center, photocopying equipment and the Internet. For solemn trips there is a limousine. For personal cars - parking. It offers transport rental, a medical office, an exchange office, dry cleaning / laundry service, and a shuttle service to the station and airport.





Digest

The chic 5-star Sunwing Waterworld Makadi Center is ideal for families. A landscaped area with rich greenery pleasantly pleases the eye and adorns the adjacent interior. Well-designed entertainment, professional animation will make the most daring tourist laugh.

All year round, visitors can enjoy all the amenities provided at this guesthouse. This is an ideal place for an informative and exciting vacation. Good food, tidy and neat apartments, friendly service and quality service - these are essential attributes of a hotel.
