The best hotels of Transylvania

In the northwestern part of Romania, the beautiful Transylvania is located. She is famous thanks to the famous work of Stoker "Dracula".



Therefore, in addition to magnificent landscapes and stunning architecture, many tourists are attracted by a certain mystical atmosphere. But in order to relax, you need to choose a good hotel in Transylvania. And to do this, it is better to consider several options.

Transylvania Hotels

Pension tempo

Located in the city of Tirgu Murezh. You can get to it from the center in a few minutes by any transport, because the distance to the hotel is only 750 meters.

Pension Tempo, like many hotels in Transylvania, is well equipped. Each room has a comfortable bathroom, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and an excellent minibar. It has good heating in winter.

Free parking is available near the hotel. In this case, pre-ordering is not required. In addition, a clear plus is the free Wi-Fi network available throughout the hotel.

Pension Tempo offers international and Hungarian restaurants, as well as a bar.

Pensiunea el passo



Many Transylvania hotels are located in Tirgu Murezh, and Pensiunea El Passo is no exception.

All rooms are decorated with wood and beautiful furniture. Each of them has a shower and a bathroom, as well as free toiletries (including a hairdryer).

Free parking is possible on site right next to the hotel.

A widescreen TV is ideal for leisure activities. In addition, Wi-Fi is available without payment for each guest.

Pensiunea El Passo has an excellent Italian restaurant, which, in addition to gourmet dishes, serves signature pizza. And near the hotel there is an Exit Entertainment Center. You can go there with the whole family and have a great time.

Hotel szeifert



This 3-star hotel is located 260 meters from the famous salt lake of Bear Lake. You can get here without any problems.

The floors here are wooden. The rooms are beautifully furnished. Each has a modern flat-screen TV, a seating area and a comfortable minibar. Hotels in Transylvania are generally comfortable.

Here you can use a separate shower (or bath) and a bathroom. Each guest is provided with the necessary bathroom amenities. Almost every room has a small balcony. Going out on it, you can enjoy a magnificent view of wildlife.

You can eat in the room or on the hotel terrace. In the summer, this option becomes truly relevant.

Hotel Szeifert offers free parking. Of the free joys, you can also highlight Wi-Fi and wired Internet in each room. Vacationers have at their disposal an excellent sauna and jacuzzi, massage room, there is the opportunity to play table tennis. Hotel Szeifert has an excellent restaurant and a bar. There is a small, beautiful lake right next to the hotel.

Still, a magnificent region is Transylvania. Tours here are always snapping up like hot cakes. If you want to not only relax, but also enjoy the beautiful views, be sure to go to this wonderful place!
