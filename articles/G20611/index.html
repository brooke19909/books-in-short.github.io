Jasmine from beads: a master class and a weaving pattern

The floral world is rich enough with a variety of beautiful flowers. Of course, many people want delicate plants to please us constantly. A living flower is able to give aromas and joy, but not for long. A made plant from beads will give satisfaction from the work done and will decorate a corner in the house. You will have a natural question: how can I get a beautiful man-made work? There are several options, and one of them is to try making jasmine from beads.



The weaving pattern presented in this article will tell you what to do and in what sequence. Let's get to work.

Necessary materials and tools

To make jasmine, you will need to prepare. Pay attention to the list below and collect everything you need to work:

  • beads of white, green, yellow colors;
  • green and silver wire thread;
  • strong wire;
  • floristic ribbon;
  • threads.

Jasmine from beads: a master class

Creating a delicate branch is performed using a parallel technique. Jasmine weaving should start with the manufacture of flowers. Let's consider everything in detail:

  • For one bud you need to weave four beaded petals, where each of them should be made up of two halves.




  • First make two jasmine leaflets. The first half of the petal is woven in a light color on a wire with a length of at least forty centimeters.
  • Then you should stretch the wire between both lines of the main half of jasmine, then make the second, similar to the first.
  • After all tiers, the second half connects with the first. The next two sheets of beads are braided in exactly the same way.

Making pestle and stamens

Jasmine pestle is created using needle weaving technique. Use the green tone of the beads:

  • Harvest three loops. Each consists of three beads and includes three beads.
  • Now make jasmine stamens. They are braided by the same technique. They are based on three yellow and twelve white beads. One flower includes 33 stamens.
  • Make the first petal by collecting 3, 5, 6, 5-3 and 1 beads (the number of beads in each row). In the future, similar digital designations will be decrypted in the same way.
  • After that, the wire is passed between the two lines of the main half of the jasmine petal. Then the second half is braided with beads. Both parts of the flower are fastened together on all tiers.
  • The sheets must be joined after the completion of the seventh line.

For beginner craftsmen, the weaving presented may seem complicated. In fact, jasmine from beads does not belong to the class of hard work.

Create a bud and sepals

Next, we will study in detail the technique of weaving a bud:

  • To do this, make four petals. One sheet will require a wire about 30 centimeters long. Beads should be strung in the following order: 3, 5, 6 (5), 5-3, 1.
  • The sepals are performed in the following sequence: 1-4, 5 (2), 4-1.
  • Then make two jasmine petals from beads. Combine the fourth sheet with the first and third at the same time.




  • Harvesting of leaves is woven from two halves. First, make a voluminous leaf, typing according to the scheme: 1, 1-9, 10 (6), 9-7, 5-1.
  • The middle jasmine leaf consists of a set of the following beads: 1, 1-9 (3), 8-1.
  • The sheet of smaller beads consists of the following combination: 1; 1-6 (5); 5-1.

Flower assembly

Now consider how jasmine is made from beads:

  • Using a floral tape, attach the pestle to a sturdy wire. Then set stamens around its circumference.
  • Lock the small leaves and place them opposite each other. Tie large petals in the same way as small ones.
  • Pass the sepals through the bottom of the jasmine and secure it properly under the petals.
  • Flower wrap with a floral ribbon.




The master class " Jasmine from beads " is completed.

Conclusion

You probably managed to notice that the weaving process is somewhat time-consuming. Jasmine from beads does not contain complex styles of weaving, in contrast to the creation of plants such as violet. Therefore, we recommend that you study the jasmine weaving style well. The full development of the proposed material in the article will increase your skill level by an order of magnitude.
