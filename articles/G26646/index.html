May Room Hotel 3 * (Kemer, Turkey) - an excellent hotel for an economical vacation

If you are short of money, but want to spend a vacation in conditions that are close to comfortable, on the beach or by the pool with a glass of wine or a glass of cool beer, then May Room Hotel 3 * (Kemer, Turkey) will be an excellent option for relaxation.





A bit about Beldibi

The place where this hotel has spread its small possessions is the village of Beldibi. This is a suburb of the resort town of Kemer. Beldibi stretches along the Mediterranean coast. Its location is attractive for tourists: on the one hand the Taurus Mountains, covered with pine forests, on the other - the warm waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Fresh air, picturesque landscapes, a friendly population - all this makes an attractive holiday in Beldibi. This is what prompted German investors to invest in the construction of hotels and the creation of appropriate tourism infrastructure in this place. The beauty surrounding Beldibi, the proximity of cultural, shopping and entertainment centers of the coast of Turkey make this resort attractive for tourists from various countries.





The village of Beldibi is located near secluded bays, where you can spend time in a romantic setting. And rising to the mountains, you can calmly admire the beauty that opens to the eye: banana groves, orange orchards, quiet sea surface.

Location

May Room Hotel 3 * is perfect for a relaxing beach holiday amid beautiful nature. Kemer is nearby - at a distance of 15 kilometers, which allows vacationers to visit shopping centers and, if desired, lead an active nightlife. Many disco bars and dance floors in Kemer more than compensate for the quiet pastime during the day.

May Room Hotel 3 * hotel offers a decent budget vacation for people of different age categories and status. This hotel is perfect for couples, tourists traveling alone, as well as for families with children. The favorable location of the hotel and the infrastructure make it comfortable and convenient.





Service

A small three-story hotel building, built in 2007, is located in the center of the village, 100 meters from its own sandy and pebble beach. The building has 27 standard rooms equipped for a comfortable stay. Each apartment has individual air conditioning, a balcony equipped with furniture for relaxing, a TV with Russian-language channels, a hairdryer, a shower, a telephone. Rooms are cleaned daily, and bed linen and towels are changed 2 times a week.

Features of rest

There is no animation at the hotel, however, you can find the opportunity to spend time richly and interestingly, occupying yourself by riding on ATVs, motorcycles, horses, boats. A worthy hobby will be fishing. Many shops, supermarkets and shops in the area will appeal to shoppers. The mobile bazaar will please with a variety of goods and low prices.

The food, according to tourists, is full, high-quality and varied. The bar serves cocktails and delicious Turkish coffee. The hotel restaurant always has oriental sweets for dessert.

A small pool on site allows guests to enjoy the cool water at any time of the day. Part of the pool is specially equipped for children. Sun loungers and umbrellas by the pool and bar are at the complete disposal of vacationers.



For those who are tired of the hustle and bustle of city life, from work and rush, an economical vacation will be very useful in May Room Hotel 3 *. Turkey is always friendly to its guests, regardless of type and category of vacation.
