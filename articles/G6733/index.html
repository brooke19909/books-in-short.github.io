Films with Cameron Diaz: all acting and full filmography

Movies with Cameron Diaz are a wonderful choice for viewers who are looking for fascinating paintings for a pleasant pastime. The main "cutie" of Hollywood is removed in thrillers, and in action films, and in dramas, but is best known for comedy roles. By the age of 43, the actress and the former model managed to appear in 48 film projects, create many memorable images. What tapes with her participation can be seen?

Cameron Diaz: the first movie of the star

The girl who worked as a model for several years came to the Masks casting by accident. The director of the comedy, which was originally supposed to be a horror movie, immediately liked the 22-year-old blonde Cameron Diaz. The first film in which the aspiring actress appeared, made her a star. The image of her heroine Tina “stuck” to her for many years, the directors basically invited the American to play frivolous blondes clapping eyelashes.





The comedy The Mask, in which Jim Carrey became Cameron's partner , was released in 1994. The heroine of the actress is a singer who meets with the manager of a nightclub. The boyfriend uses Tina, trying to turn her into a pawn in his dangerous games with the law. Like many other films with Cameron Diaz, the comedy was a great success with the audience. Since its release, the newly-minted star has not lacked offers to star.

The funniest comedies

It's no secret that Cameron Diaz is primarily a comedian. The film “Everything Is Mad About Mary”, presented to the public in 1998, helped the “cutie” gain a foothold in this status. The heroine she played becomes a kind of “magnet”, attracting absolutely all the representatives of the stronger sex, into the field of vision of which she gets. Despite numerous critical reviews of the plot of the comedy, she was a success.





Listing the funny films with Cameron Diaz in the title role, one can not help but remember about the film "Very Bad Teacher", in which the actress appeared in 2011. In this picture, the star officially parted with the “cutie” image that bored her, portraying a hunter for rich husbands. The blonde in any situation wears shoes with 13-centimeter stilettos, intends to increase her breasts and does not feel pity for her victims.

The brightest role

"Being John Malkovich" is a fantastic drama released in 1999, which critics invariably call, listing the best films with Cameron Diaz. Fans will not immediately be able to recognize the actress, accustomed to seeing her in the image of a sexy blonde. In this drama, she plays a slightly crazy loser with a terrible haircut, wearing voluminous sweaters surrounded by monkeys.





The story begins with the fact that the puppeteer, who is unlucky in life, finally finds work in a mysterious office. Having examined his own office, a man notices a tiny door. It turns out that behind it is a move, taking advantage of which, everyone can get into the brain of the famous Hollywood actor. The newly-made clerk does not intend to hide such an amazing discovery from the world.

Spy and princess

Remembering the fascinating films with the participation of Cameron Diaz, it is impossible to ignore the film “Charlie's Angels”. Get one of the main roles in this picture, the actress helped model parameters and a charming smile. This story is about spy ladies doing secret errands. The film will appeal to all viewers who love the abundance of modern weapons, well-set fights in the film. The roles of two other "angels" went to Barrymore and Lew.





“Shrek” is a fabulous story in which fans will not be able to see the actress, but they will enjoy the opportunity to listen to her pleasant voice. The character she voiced was Princess Fiona. The first part of "Shrek" was so successful that the creators released a few more.

"Gangs of New York"

The thief prostitute in the crime drama "Gangs of New York" - one of the largest roles of Cameron Diaz. The best films with her participation are far from all funny. In "Gangs", the actress abandoned her "signature" blond hair, repainted in red color. She also temporarily forgot about her talent to make the audience laugh, taking on a serious role. Diaz became a partner of Diaz, whose friend she plays.





The heroine Cameron must make a difficult choice. Duty tells the girl to serve criminal authority, but she suddenly falls in love with a guy who is trying to kill her boss. Interestingly, the events take place in 1863, the center of action is New York at that time, whose inhabitants obey only the law of power. Leonardo DiCaprio in this picture got the role of a son who wants to pay for his father, who died in a fierce skirmish many years ago.

Work with Tom Cruise

Hollywood "cutie" often have to meet on the set with other stars. Tom Cruise and Cameron Diaz in the film "Vanilla Sky" composed a spectacular duet. The actress got the role of a deceived rich playboy lover. Cruz acts as a playboy, his character has absolutely everything from a thriving business to a huge bank account. His life completely changes when a mysterious girl, performed by Penelope Cruz, bursts into it.

The drama became a remake, the original version was shot at the time by Alejandro Amenabara. All critics insist that the remake has become much worse than the original. However, the role of the tragically deceived girl Diaz played brilliantly, laid out one hundred percent.





Vanilla Sky is not the only film in which Tom and Cameron starred together. Already in 2010 saw the light of the tape "Knight of the Day", again uniting the stars. Hardly a comedy action movie can be called a breakthrough of the year, but the constant alterations in which the heroes Diaz and Cruz found themselves impressed the public.

The most romantic stories

Many films with Cameron Diaz can be called romantic. A striking example of such a picture is the work of 2006 "Vacation on the exchange." Two ladies, one of whom is played by an actress, are disappointed in their own boyfriends. They come up with an original decision - to swap houses for the Christmas holidays and how to relax. An unusual vacation will give them not only fresh impressions, but also long-awaited love.





Noteworthy is the comedy Once Upon a Time in Vegas, in which handsome Ashton Kutcher became Cameron's partner. Two young people find themselves in the same bed after spending a fun night in Las Vegas. The memories of yesterday's adventures are both very vague. It turns out that they hit the jackpot at the casino and got married. Now each of them wants to take possession of a huge amount of money, leaving the newly minted “second half” with his nose.

It is worth looking at the picture “The Wedding of a Best Friend”, in which Diaz starred with Julia Roberts. The heroine of the blonde is Kimberly, who is going to get married. Suddenly, an old friend arrives to her fiance, who suddenly realized that she had been in love with him all her life. But Kimberly does not believe that her rival will be able to upset the upcoming wedding.

What else to see

Gambit is a criminally adventurous story presented to the public in 2012, which has become a remake of the 1966 work. A swindler, a rodeo participant, a resident of Texas - this is another heroine Cameron Diaz. Comedy films are brilliantly successful for the actress, and Gambit was no exception.

The film “Away from You”, released in 2005, introduces viewers to the story of sisters who are unlike each other. Diaz plays reckless Maggie, whose life consists of going to night clubs and meaningless novels. Once she is forced to move to her sister - an educated, serious girl lawyer. The relations of relatives do not add up from early childhood, they reach maximum stress when Rose discovers Maggie in bed with her own boyfriend. How will events develop after that, will the sisters part forever?

The most recent films with Cameron Diaz were released in 2014: Another Woman, Emmy. Fans can only hope for new bright projects with her participation.
