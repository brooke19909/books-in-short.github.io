Abkhazia All Inclusive Hotels

According to the all inclusive concept, most hotels in the region work. These are Inter, Alexandrina, Anana, etc. Moreover, the all-inclusive system in Abkhazia differs from the Egyptian or Turkish service that has already become familiar. Here in hotels and boarding houses alcoholic drinks, even locally produced, are not included in the price of the permit. However, any high-quality wines, including expensive and well-known far beyond the borders of the country, as well as champagne, cognacs and chacha are sold everywhere and at affordable prices. Of non-alcoholic drinks, the concept of all inclusive most often includes tea and coffee, fruit drinks and fruit drinks, water and soft drinks. Therefore, as a rule, all-inclusive hotels in Abkhazia mean three meals a day, including the buffet.
