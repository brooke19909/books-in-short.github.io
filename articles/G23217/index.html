Sherwood Dreams Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Belek, Bogazkent): hotel description, service, reviews

In our article we want to talk about the five-star hotel Sherwood Dreams Resort Hotel 5 * of the famous Turkish resort Belek.

A little bit about the resort ...

Sherwood Dreams Resort Hotel 5 * is located just three kilometers from the famous Belek. The city has become a common resort recently. In the tourist direction, it began to develop in 1984. In such a short time Belek has become a famous resort destination not only within Turkey, but has also gained popularity among thousands of tourists around the world. There are no historical sights in the town itself, but it is easy to get to Antalya and Side from it, where vacationers will find many interesting things for themselves. In Belek, golf is incredibly popular.



The town is located in beautiful places with eucalyptus, pine and cedar trees. In the local forests rare birds are found. Currently, Belek is considered the most presentable, and therefore expensive resort in Turkey. There are not so many youth in these places. The resort is interesting, first of all, for its wide long beach stretching for twenty kilometers. Here the swimming season lasts from May to the end of October. Surprisingly, the weather in Belek in October allows tourists to bask on the beach and splash around in the water.

Almost all resort hotels have a category of 4 or 5 stars. All of them have good territories and are located on the first line. Such prestigious complexes are well equipped and offer guests impeccable service.

Weather in Belek

Many tourists are interested in what is the weather like in Belek in October? This is a very relevant and popular topic. So October is quite an acceptable month for a vacation. Especially in the first half in Belek it is very warm, the sea did not have time to cool down at all, therefore it is comfortable to swim in it. But the prices for rest in this period are almost the lowest in the year. For this reason, tourists are interested in October weather conditions. The only negative is the fact that at this time the probability of storms and rains increases.

In Belek, the velvet season ends in October, but in November there are sometimes very warm days. In the first half of October, there is always good weather, and sometimes even summer heat. But autumn is autumn. And during this period it can always cool for a couple of days, rain or a storm. In the resort, the average air temperature in the daytime is +26 degrees, and at night - +15. And the water temperature can vary between +24 - +27 degrees. If you plan to spend your vacation in October, it is better to choose the first half of the month. At this time, there are practically no whims of nature.

Belek has long won the title of the best resort in Turkey due to its huge sandy beaches, beautiful natural parks, eucalyptus, juniper, coniferous forests and luxurious hotels equipped with sports and entertainment infrastructure. This resort is chosen for sports training and recreation, even professional teams. Here are some of the best golf clubs in the world. And in October, the golf season begins. Since all competitions are held in open fields, during the summer heat this is simply not possible. For this reason, a more comfortable time was chosen.



In October, the animation in the hotels is still working, but the staff is partially starting to go home. Such nuances must be learned from tour operators. If the weather on some day is not very favorable, then it is better to devote this time to sightseeing.

The cost of a vacation in Belek in mid-autumn will certainly please those tourists who cannot spend a lot of money on vacation, or are simply limited to a certain amount. In May and October you can buy the cheapest tours, and the weather at the same time most of the time is quite good. Of course, there is a chance of rain, but the sea is warm. But in the second half of October, the weather is more moody, and can bring unpleasant surprises.
