Frigola 3 * (Spain / Costa Brava) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

This private Spanish hotel on the Costa Brava is one of the most popular among Russian tourists. Frigola 3 * is located in Catalonia, in the wonderful resort town of Lloret de Mar. It stands a few tens of meters from the Mediterranean Sea. Despite the fact that the hotel is quite large, it is not only a beach hotel, but also a city one, since it is actually located in the center of the village. Having settled here, you can not only enjoy a beach holiday on the famous Spanish coast, but also explore local attractions and easily visit Barcelona and Girona. The hotel is very functional, comfortable and suitable for travelers of almost any age and temperament.



Location

Frigola 3 * is built extremely well. This hotel is close to everything. We can say that the location is one of the main bonuses of the hotel, which attracts Russian tourists. Two to three minutes walk to the beach, five minutes walk to the bus station, from where you can get to the two main major Catalan cities, where the main sightseeing trips are made and where the nearest airports are - Barcelona and Girona. From the bus station there are also small minibuses to the railway station. In addition, immediately after the entrance to the Frigola 3 * hotel begins an endless string of bars, restaurants and eateries, a beautiful promenade and many shops where you can spend your time with pleasure. Not too far from here to the Water World water park - just over three kilometers.

Lloret de mar

Frigola Lloret 3 * is located in the center of one of the most rave resorts on the Costa Brava. This is Lloret de Mar - a place where almost all the youth of Europe seek to rest. On the local embankment you will find a very democratic and variegated audience, sometimes quite unconventional. But these are the features of this resort. It is also famous for its picturesque surroundings - cliffs of bizarre shapes that rise from the clear blue water. If you want to go to the water park, then next to the hotel there is a stop of the free bus that goes there. The main walking street, which is located near the hotel, is Morskoy Boulevard. There you can not only walk along the coast and wander around the shops, but also get drunk from the Canale fountain. Then, as the local legend says, someday you will certainly return to Lloret.





Territory and infrastructure

Frigola 3 * (Lloret de Mar) is a three large seven-story building in light beige color. The hotel was opened a long time ago, in 1967, but in 2002 there was a complete update and a good repair was made. To reach the upper levels, you can use the elevator. The hotel has its own territory, where there is a parking lot, minimarket (there you can buy souvenirs, products, beach accessories and other trifles useful for guests). Wi-Fi is free throughout. In addition, hotel guests can use the territory of the neighboring Flamingo Hotel, which has practically merged with Frigola. On the neighboring streets there are a lot of cafes and shops that you can look into on the way to the beach. And at night, a completely different life begins there - incendiary and fun, which boils until the morning. Five steps from the hotel there is a good supermarket where you can buy whatever your heart desires.





Rooms

The hotel Frigola 3 * (Spain), there are five hundred thirty-four rooms. All of them are simple in design, but comfortable, with balconies or terraces. The rooms have everything that travelers need - a decent bathroom in good condition, a bathroom, a telephone, a TV, a safe. The views from the windows are very different - on the sea, on the roofs and streets of Lloret, on the pool and the hotel grounds. The balconies are large, with a table and chairs. The room has Wi-Fi, but wireless Internet access from your own room is at an additional cost. If you arrive at this hotel in the cold season, you can use the heating - you will not have to freeze. The rooms are cleaned every day. Most rooms are designed for four guests, but there are forty-two rooms for only two.





Services

Hotel Frigola 3 * provides an opportunity for its guests to make photocopies of documents or send them by fax. Russian tourists claim that you can also print electronic tickets. Since the local places, especially the Costa Brava, are better to inspect on their own "on wheels", a car rental service is available at the hotel. This is a very convenient and useful service. At the reception you can order tickets for trains and buses. There is a playroom for children. You can use the parking lot, but this service needs to be ordered specially and in advance. The hotel also provides its own transfer from the airport or other place (for a fee). Registration of customers is carried out twenty-four hours a day. For late check-out or early arrival, you can leave your belongings in a special room.

What do guests in the Frigola 3 * hotel occupy in their free time? For tourists who want to see the sights of Catalonia, there is a special bureau that arranges trips and various excursions. Here you can organize a banquet, birthday party or wedding. But most importantly - this hotel is located in a city that has fun all day and never gets discouraged.
