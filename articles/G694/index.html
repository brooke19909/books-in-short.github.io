Children of any age in Side Alegria Hotel & Spa 4 * are very good. They find a lot of activities for themselves in addition to the much-loved bathing and water slide rides. Beach area

Hotel guests claim that the beach here is a real fairy tale. It consists of a small sand, and the entrance to the water is safe even for kids. The beach area is equipped with sun loungers and parasols, which can be used for free. Nearby is a bar, open until half past four in the evening. There you can refresh yourself with water and other soft drinks, as well as dine. From twelve to three in the afternoon, fresh cakes with chocolate and cheese are baked on the beach. Vacationers claim that they have a unique taste and are worth it to try this oriental delicacy.

The only minus of the beach is the abundance of people on it. The fact is that the coast in this place is quite narrow, so the influx of vacationers turns a pleasant and measured vacation into sea swims among a real crowd of the same tourists. However, approximately five hundred meters from the hotel beach is a pretty cozy place where you can relax in a more secluded atmosphere.
