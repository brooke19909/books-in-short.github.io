Cardstock: what is it, features of its manufacture, methods of application

Among modern stationery materials there are a lot of designer paper. Quite popular is the cardstock. What it is? A similar name has a variety of thick paper, which is used for creative craftsmanship. We suggest that you learn in more detail what it is - a cardstock, what it is like, and how it is used. After all, crafts made from such cardboard are affordable, simple and fast. It is enough to purchase a set of color cardstock only in the store.

Cardstock: what is it?

This material is produced in different shades and textures, density and texture. So what is it - cardstock? So called dense cardboard used for scrapbooking and other types of creativity. The name "cardstock" appeared in English. It matters paper of a certain type and density. This thick paper is used to design albums, cards, pages.

Although this material is quite dense, it is still quite thin and fragile. The synonyms of this cardboard are scrapbooking, quilling, card making. This type of dense colored paper can be in the form of a plain background or with a textured pattern. There is a textured cardstock with a stylized surface under the skin, stone, wood. Sometimes it may resemble an orange peel. In appearance, this material looks original.

Features of cardstock paper technology

This textured cardboard is produced using special technology. In its general composition, acid and linin are not present. This allows you to use it as material for archives. Even if you store a photo album made from a cardstock for a long time, it will still not change its color. It also does not show yellowness, and for a long time keeps its original appearance.

Today on sale you can find not only a rectangular-shaped cardstock, which you see in the photo of the article. Sometimes you can meet the curly shape of the edges. Such cardboard allows you to fantasize with crafts.

Two-layer cardstock

The production of two-layer cardboard has also been established. The bottom layer has a different shade. It can be a contrasting or related color. This makes it possible to pick up quite original color combinations, to create author's unique products in different ways.

If you take sandpaper and rub it on this cardboard sheet, an inner layer will appear. In this way, you can achieve the original effect of scuffing. The product after that takes an old look.

The two-layer cardstock allows using it in the torn edge technique. If you tear off the pieces of the outer layer, you get a unique frame.

A very interesting effect can be achieved using the technique of embedding (embossing). To do this, the upper layer of cardboard is erased, under which a pattern of the lower layer appears. You can give the paper a special charm if you just crush it. Then it is straightened and polished a little. Such a sheet can be an excellent page for an album.

Ways to use designer cardboard

A set of cardstock allows you to implement various design ideas. The popularity of such creativity brought a simple technique. Some people buy cardboard with the most elaborate designs and patterns. It is suitable for making jewelry. To do this, use curly holes. They allow you to make decorative garlands, hearts, stars. Some figures resemble 3-D decorations.

Also, a less dense cardstock is suitable for making flowers in the form of a fan. Decorative cards, albums, covers, gift wrapping are made from it. Festive garlands look great from color cardboard.

Cardstock is used as the basis for the manufacture of bags and boxes. Sometimes even metal boxes are pasted over with colored cardboard. Suitable capacity for candy, coffee and other goodies. They can be transformed into pencil cases, small caskets. For kids, you can make a spinner-windmill on a stick. When working with a cardstock, patience and accuracy are important.
