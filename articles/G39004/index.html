Hotel "Dana Beach" (Hurghada) - one of the best options for a relaxing stay

Hotel "Dana Beach Resort" (Hurghada) is an excellent resort complex, which has a category of 5 stars. It occupies an area on the picturesque coast of the Red Sea with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã181,000 square meters. m. From the city of Hurghada it is separated by a distance of 28 kilometers, and the international airport is only 15 minutes away. The opening of the complex took place in 2004. It includes one main building and a number of three-story buildings. Currently, partial reconstruction and minor repairs have been completed.



Rooms

For its guests, the hotel "Dana Beach" (Hurghada) provides 640 rooms, including 282 standard, 232 superior and 126 family. It should be noted right away that pets are not allowed. Each room has a shower, hairdryer, safe, individual air conditioning. There is a minibar, which is filled at an additional cost. There is satellite TV, a telephone. The floor is covered with ceramic tiles. Each room has a balcony or terrace. All of them are decorated in European style, mainly in bright colors, quite comfortable.



Nutrition

The Dana Beach Hotel (Hurghada) offers all-inclusive buffet meals to its guests in the main restaurant of La Clef de L Orient complex. It has a pleasant atmosphere. Equally delicious cuisine is served at the Castello Restaurant, which invites guests to spend time in a relaxing environment. There are two other similar establishments offering both local and European cuisine. In addition to restaurants, in the lobby of the main building is the bar Le Rendez Vous, which has comfortable and upholstered furniture. It offers a large selection of drinks as well as light snacks. There are 3 more bars with similar features.

Those who at least once chose the Dana Beach Hotel (Hurghada) to stay, reviews leave only positive. The main advantage of this complex is its high-level service and well-developed infrastructure that helps to make the rest full and interesting. The hotel has three swimming pools, two of which are heated. For those who have arrived or rented their own car, there is free parking. There are several different currency exchange shops.

Additional Information

The hotel "Dana Beach" (Hurghada) offers its guests a number of services. For those who come not only to relax, but also to work, there is a conference room that seats up to 120 people. For an additional fee, you can use the services of a doctor, laundry, hairdresser, dry cleaning. In addition, a number of entertainments are provided here, including an animation show, a catamaran on a lazy river, a boat on a lazy river. For a fee, you can ride a camel or horse. In the evening, a disco awaits all guests. For those who seek to keep fit even during their holidays, a number of sports activities are offered, a gym is open. After an active day, you can relax in the steam room or sauna, as well as use the services of a qualified massage therapist.



Hotel "Dana Beach" (Hurghada) did not forget about its young guests. For them, there is a special playground for games, a mini club. If necessary, a cot is placed in the room, and a children's menu is prepared in the restaurant. There is no separate pool for babies, but there are specially designated sections in ordinary ones.

Beach

The hotel has its own sand lagoon. A pier is built near the shore. There are corals, so when swimming, it is recommended to use special shoes.
