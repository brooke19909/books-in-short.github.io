Hotel "Sirius" (Tekirova, Turkey): description of rooms, service, reviews. Sirius Hotel 4 *

The nice and quiet four-star Sirius Hotel (Tekirova, Turkey) was built in 1995, and its modernization took place in 2012. This is a small hotel, chosen by families vacationing with children. Many of them come here again. It provides its guests with a good budget vacation and acceptable food.





In September, the price of a tour with accommodation in Sirius for 7 nights costs from 49 177 rubles. The contingent of vacationers in it is mainly from the CIS countries. There is no language problem at all. The staff understands well and speaks fluently in Russian.

The hotel is on the second line. A walk from its territory to the beach can be reached in 4 minutes.

Location

Hotel Sirius (Tekirova, Turkey) is located in the specialized tourist village of Tekirova, an hour's drive from Antalya Airport and a 15-minute drive from the resort town of Kemer. The village is surrounded by picturesque Taurus Mountains, overgrown with fragrant pine forests. The air is special: Mediterranean rich freshness mixes with a specific mountain, permeated with pine volatile.

The village itself is buried in gardens. The surrounding banana groves are cool and shady, appetizing fruits ripen on the branches of pomegranate and orange trees.

Around the hotel under consideration, another ten hotel complexes were erected, most of which are five-star. Their joint infrastructure provides vacationers with a boring holiday.

Just 7 kilometers from the hotel there is a historical attraction - the ruins of Phaselis, the ancient capital of Lycia. Half an hour away from Sirius lies Cirali - one of the best Mediterranean beaches.

Hotel Territory

Hotel "Sirius" (Tekirova, Turkey) has a fairly compact, but well-planned area. It contains the main classical components of the resort infrastructure: outdoor pool, bar, stage, playground. There is also a private garden, planted around the perimeter, and a cozy well-groomed terrace.





When the air is hot up to 45 degrees, some vacationers prefer not to fry in the beach sun, but to be in the shade by the pool. A small game waterslide was installed there, along which chilled water flows into the heat. In addition, you can order refreshments and pastries in the stylish bar, which has a round counter and a dense sun canopy.

In the pool itself, animators organize water polo games for vacationers, and yoga and aerobics classes for those wishing to do so.

In the daytime, leisure activities at the hotel are organized daily by a friendly team of Russian-speaking animators.

Building

In two specialized boarding four-story buildings accommodates its guests Sirius Hotel 4 *. The buildings are equipped with shaded loggias, have spacious and bright rooms. The newcomers are immediately struck by the arrangement of the reception area, the vaulted ceiling of this main hotel public zone, the stylish cladding with artificial stone. It has Wi-Fi, equipped recreation areas.





On the ground floor of the main building is the main restaurant of the hotel, where guests are provided with full board-type meals. There is also a small but stylish lobby bar, gym, sauna and massage room.

Rooms

Mighty economy class of premises offers its guests the hotel "Sirius". Description of the three types of rooms can be found in reviews of tourists:

  • standard, designed for two people, with one double or two single beds;
  • triple, increased area;
  • family room with two connecting rooms.

The rooms are furnished with modern beds, wardrobes, tables, nightstands. On the balcony there is a standard plastic set: a table and a pair of half-chairs. Household appliances in the rooms are working: air conditioning, TV, minibar, hairdryer.



The bathroom is equipped with either a shower or bath. Cleaning is carried out daily, but with flaws. It is advisable to leave the dollar to cleaners in a day. Towels are changed regularly; if urgent replacement is necessary, just put them on the floor.

Hotel infrastructure

In addition to the infrastructure described above (pool, summer stage, playground), there are also additional wellness opportunities: compact, but adequately equipped spa and fitness centers in the Sirius Hotel. The service of the hotel complex also includes the services of its own spa center, fitness center. Vacationers of middle and older age can enjoy moderate motor entertainment: billiards, table tennis, darts.



Active, sports warehouse people will certainly be interested in paid, but inexpensive diving classes conducted by an experienced Russian-speaking instructor.

Vacationers can use the parking lot for rented cars. They also have access to classic hotel services: currency exchange, airport shuttle, laundry, ironing.

Resort infrastructure

In the most picturesque place of the resort Kemer, in the village of Tekirova, is the hotel "Sirius". The entertainment that is offered to tourists is partly organized by animators (beach volleyball competitions), partly by sightseeing and travel agencies.

The hotels operate food, manufactured goods and souvenir shops.

In the village, which has a developed hotel economy, with five-star hotels there are discos and night clubs. However, companies of nightlife connoisseurs usually take a taxi to Kemer in the evenings. Here are the nightclubs known throughout the Mediterranean coast: Aura, Crystal, Inferno.

Beach vacation

Two hundred meters from the water's edge is the Sirius Hotel. The beach on which its guests relax is long, pebble, with a gentle entrance to the water. Pebbles are so small that in places they resemble sand. These places are considered to be elite and healing: the turquoise transparent and warm sea attracts many tourists from Western Europe. In the sectors of five-star hotels, beaches are covered with imported sand.

The coast is divided into sectors. Sirius also serves its site with umbrellas, sunbeds and deck chairs, a volleyball court and free exercise equipment. Every day, animators organize mini-volleyball tournaments for vacationers. However, vacationers are not forbidden to move to neighboring areas. There are pontoons on the beach, from which diving is prohibited.

Nutrition

Judging by the reviews of the guests, the Sirius Hotel is very worthy in terms of cooking. The food provided by the main restaurant is full and satisfying. Meals are served in buffet style.

Breakfast is quite light. The menu includes omelets, sausages, sausages, salads, several types of cheeses, yogurts, tea, coffee, pastries, fruits. Lunch is full and satisfying. Dinner resembles lunch, only there are no first courses, but there is meat and fish on the grill.

The main dishes are prepared from chicken, fish. Less often - from lamb. There are always soups on the menu. Garnished with potatoes, pasta, rice, stewed vegetables. A lot of salads, pastries, fruits. In the evening, ice cream is served by the bar. The drinks on the menu include sprite, cola, fantasy, as well as tea and coffee. From alcohol to the menu as part of the "all inclusive" there are beer, local wine. You can take imported spirits for a fee.

There are bars in the reception and pool areas. The work of the latter is highly demanded and appropriate for bathing and sunbathing in the hotel.

Excursions

Like any hotel complex, the Sirius Hotel offers its guests sightseeing tours. Reviews of tourists, however, indicates their overpriced by 30-50% of the cost. Experienced tourists buy sightseeing tours at specialized tour agencies.





Among the places most visited by Turkish guests are its capital, Istanbul, Pamukkale baths, the stone city of Cappadocia. Families who come with children seek to visit the central water park. Fishing is also in demand with the instructor at the local trout farm.

A significant advantage in terms of visiting historical sights are those tourists who rented cars. After all, the hotel complex itself is located on the ancient land of Lycia. There are many cave fortresses and monasteries on it, there are even cities built underground.

Just a few kilometers from the hotel are the ruins of Phaselis, the ancient capital of this kingdom, built almost three thousand years ago. Tourists can see an impressive fragment of the ancient arcade and the ruins of the amphitheater, planned streets, the remaining fragments of the walls of the dwellings.

Another nearby and therefore popular excursion is a cable car ride in the Olympos Teleferik massif. The climb itself lasts about 10 minutes. But the beauty of the Western Taurus ridge leaves an impression even for children.

Shopping

For shopping, the village of Tekirova - a resort coast with a unique nature - is not the best place. After all, even in grocery stores, prices are too high. Souvenirs and local light industry products are also expensively sold.

Therefore, hotel guests go shopping in Kemer by bus / dolmush (ticket fee is $ 1.5-2) or by taxi ($ 3-5). Moreover, if a tourist is going to make a major purchase in the Hadrian Kemer shopping center, the store itself sends a car for it.

Tourists buy most of the goods in the shopping center Mirgos, Waikiki, Kaya Leather Bag. The largest share falls on fur, leather products, gold jewelry.

Conclusion

For a long time, the Sirius Hotel (Tekirova, Turkey) has occupied a worthy market position in the tourism business. Inexpensive tours and at the same time a decent level of service provide him with popularity. Vacationers like its isolation and good hotel service, genuine hospitality and hospitality of the owner, the skill of an outgoing and textured chef.





In addition, the powerful recreational potential of the local mountainous Mediterranean coast plays an important role in the demand for the hotel complex. Vacationers here receive a double bonus: an inexpensive economy vacation and good transport interchange, which allows you to visit the shopping and party Kemer.

Guests note the professionalism of the hotel staff, his serious attitude to their work.
