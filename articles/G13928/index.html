Lazarevskoye: Starfish Waterpark

Another miracle that a small Sochi village can boast of. Sometimes it seems that a whole scattering of stars has gathered here. And each of them is able to captivate and surprise.



What is attractive water park?

  • Firstly, it is located near the sanatorium of the same name and is a universal institution for lovers of the water element. Here both adults and children will find classes.
  • Secondly, you can spend all day in the park. It works until 8 pm.
  • Thirdly, the water oasis provides entertainment for every taste. Do you like extreme sports and crave unusual sensations? At your service are attractions "Kamikaze" and "Space Ball". Fans of a more relaxed holiday can be advised by the Black Pipe.
  • Fourthly, in the water park you will find several pools. One of them with waterfalls, the other with artificial waves. So what about the fact that the sea is near? Splashing around in such a pool is no less fun.
  • Fifthly, on the territory of this kingdom of water there are several catering establishments where you can have a tasty snack.

In a word, the Starfish water park has everything in order to feel happy.
