Hotel Resort Village Royale 2 - a picturesque corner in North Goa

To get away from the hustle and bustle, enjoy the sweet spirit of Indian exoticism and recharge with the powerful energy of the distant East, at least once in a lifetime, every person dreams of. You can fulfill this dream in Calangute by staying at Resort Village Royale 2. Calangute is a popular beach in the city of the same name in North Goa.





Rhythmic music, disco until the morning, interesting offers of massages of various kinds, a colorful mosaic of goods and souvenirs, Indian spices, the smell of which drives you crazy - all this is a general picture of the surroundings of Resort Village Royale.

Fans of beach vacations will find here everything for a fun and unforgettable pastime. The coast has a vibrant nightlife with all kinds of entertainment.

Location

The city of Calangute is located on the coastline of the Arabian Sea. Resort Village Royale 2 is two kilometers from a sunny and welcoming beach. The international airport is 50 km from the hotel.

Hotel Description





So close to nature and exotic , Resort Village Royale 2 is not without all the modern amenities. The interiors of the rooms (more than 150 rooms) are decorated taking into account modern trends, although quite modestly. The apartments are equipped with only the necessary items without claiming luxury. There are fans, satellite TV, shower or bath, maybe a refrigerator, as well as a balcony. It should be prepared for the fact that not all air conditioners are available. Therefore, it is better to immediately clarify this nuance at registration.

In search of entertainment or active leisure, you can visit the on-site bar or restaurant, or work out in the fitness center.

It is worth paying special attention to the fact that Resort Village Royale 2 is located in the countryside. But, despite this, there is no complete solitude, silence and tranquility. This is a very busy village where the fun continues until late at night. The atmosphere is suitable for family or economical youth vacations.

The hotel level is simple enough to satisfy the hopes and appetites of the most demanding gourmets. Lunches and breakfasts are modest, and therefore you will need to take care of additional food.

So, tourist tips for choosing a hotel Resort Village Royale 2 may be as follows.

If you are ready:

  • Do not rely on exclusive comfort;
  • Do not find fault with the maintenance staff;
  • ignore some aesthetic flaws in the room;
  • Remember that there is no all-inclusive system.

Feel free to head to Resort Village Rroyale 2.

If you need supercomfort, amenities, all the benefits of civilization, very refined food and service at the highest level, then think very carefully before you go to this place.

The rustic two-star hotel is more suitable for unburdened people who, having settled in it, plan to spend most of their time on excursions in India, enjoy the flavor of the surroundings and the unsurpassed nature of North Goa.

Have a good trip and make the right choice!
