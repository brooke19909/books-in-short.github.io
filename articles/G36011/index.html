Wonderful yarn - openwork pehora

Needlework is one of the most beloved occupations of creative people, which brings moral satisfaction from the work done and allows you to create charming crafts for every taste. Among a wide variety of types of handmade, knitting and crocheting are in great demand. This type of creativity is beautiful in its versatility, because there are a lot of styles and techniques in knitting, which make it possible to weave things of magical beauty from threads.

Of course, in knitting, as in any other kind of creativity, there are pitfalls that often cause many beginners to quit the job they started. But for those who purposefully stride to their dream, no obstacles are scary, the main thing is to correctly follow all the recommendations of the masters.

Professionals recommend paying great attention not only to remembering the progress of work, but also advising that you carefully consider the choice of tools and materials for knitting. And if the sizes of knitting needles and hooks are easy to figure out, then selecting high-quality threads for products can be very difficult. But it is precisely on the quality of the threads and their possible deformation during washing that the appearance of the finished product will depend.

Magic threads

Very popular among the craftswomen are the openwork pehora threads, which managed to earn great praise for their excellent quality and ease of work. After all, it is this type of yarn that allows you to create very thin, like a cobweb, things. These threads are one hundred percent mercerized cotton, part of the summer series of yarn made using Italian technology. Due to the natural composition, such material is very pleasant to the body and does not cause irritation, which makes it possible to wear things from it even by young children.





Most often, pehora openwork yarn is used to weave products crochet, because its fibers are very thin and airy, so it is rarely used in knitting. Although there are such needlewomen who knit with her knitting needles very chic and creative things. This type of yarn is suitable for creating a large number of outfits, such as dresses, tops, sweaters, skirts and other stunning exhibits. She looks great in products for graduation balls and many other social events, since all the decorations from the "magic threads" look very elegant and rich.
