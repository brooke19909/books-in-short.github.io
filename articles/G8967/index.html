Novosibirsk: Central Park - the best oasis for families

Large modern cities have many attractions and interesting entertainment facilities. Where to go and what to see if you have a trip to Novosibirsk? Central Park is known as the oldest and one of the most beautiful green areas in the region. Indeed, this is a real oasis among the stone jungle, ideal for family vacations and walks alone. The park is constantly being ennobled, new attractions and landscape decor appear here almost seasonally.





When did the Central Park appear in the city?

The favorite vacation spot of the inhabitants of Novosibirsk has a very interesting and exciting story. The park was built in 1925 on the site of an old cemetery. Later, the park was renamed the "Central Garden", and then in the "Garden them. Stalin. " Over time, that earlier there was a cemetery in place of the green zone, everyone forgot. It is worth noting that the transfer and liquidation of old churchyards within the boundaries of large settlements is a common practice for our country, carried out according to proven technologies. In the 60-70s of the last century, many names of streets, squares and squares changed, but the name of the city - Novosibirsk remained the same. Central Park officially received its modern name in 1963. On 10 hectares in the very center of the city there are green plantations of trees and shrubs of various species and various entertainments for vacationers. The first attractions appeared here in 1944.





Entertainment for vacationers

Novosibirsk Central Park is famous for its variety of carousels for visitors of all ages. There are attractions for very young guests of the recreation area, who have just started to walk: these are mechanical rocking chairs, trains and swans. Schoolchildren and their parents will not be bored. The Central Park in Novosibirsk has classic attractions, for example, the Ferris Wheel and super-modern extreme, which even adult men sometimes refuse to ride. Another entertainment is the Laughter Room. Today, the park has only about 50 attractions and the same number of other entertainment facilities. The real pride of the recreation area is the Musical Comedy Theater, founded back in 1959 and received many significant awards in the history of its existence, including the eight-time winner of the Golden Mask. There is also a children's theater and a planetarium in the park.





Infrastructure and leisure facilities

It will be useful to know for everyone who is going to visit Novosibirsk: Central Park is open daily from 09:00 to 23:00, in winter and summer. In the warm season, you can ride a bicycle, walk along the old alleys, admire nature or just sit on a bench in the shade of centuries-old trees. In winter, all outdoor enthusiasts are invited to ride an ice rink in Central Park. Novosibirsk is a city whose inhabitants choose a healthy lifestyle, in winter many go skiing or continue daily jogging. There are enough cafes, restaurants and pavilions in the park where you can buy snacks and drinks. There are children's and sports grounds, gazebos and cozy benches for relaxation.
