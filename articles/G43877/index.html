Recreation centers in Gorno-Altaisk: accommodation options for tourists

Every year, thousands of tourists from different parts of the world flock to Altai Mountains in search of vivid impressions and an unforgettable vacation in stunning nature. In the Altai Republic there are many places for tourists and guests to suit every taste and budget: recreation centers, apartments, guest houses, campsites, camp sites, estates, hotels. All of them are united by the fact that they are located in one of the most environmentally friendly regions of the country - in the Altai Mountains.

Stunning fabulous nature, fresh air, clean water, unique energy, rich flora and fauna - all this will not leave indifferent the most demanding tourist. Among other things, guests are offered various services and entertainment, ranging from rafting, horseback riding and various excursions to spa treatments (for example, useful antler baths and phytobarrel). What are the recreation centers in Gorno-Altaisk?

Where can a tourist stay in Gorno-Altaysk?

Gorno-Altaysk is the only city in the Altai Republic, its administrative center. Of course, the best option is to pre-book a place in a hotel, camp site or guest house. But even if they did not manage to do this, housing can be found in Gorno-Altaysk itself.

However, there are no recreation centers in Gorno-Altaysk. Usually in the city tourists can stay in hotels, hotels, apartments and guest houses. A plus stop in the city itself is that you can get acquainted with the cultural objects of the indigenous population, their traditions. For example, you can culturally enrich yourself by going to the A.V. National Museum Anokhin, take a walk in the parks or the main avenue of the city.

So, 9 kilometers from the airport is the Gorny Altai Hotel, located in the very center of Gorno-Altaysk, on Erkemen Palkin Street 5. However, this is an option for undemanding tourists, since the hotel has been operating since Soviet times. But this is a way out for those who urgently need to stop somewhere to spend the night or relax. Nowadays, there are many different recreation centers in the republic. Gorno-Altaysk is usually a sort of transit point on the way to the main tourist destination.

"Golden Key"

One of the vacationers' accommodation is the year-round recreation center "Golden Key" in the village of Aya, on the street. Soviet, 40a. It is located on the banks of the Katun River and is suitable for families with children, and for hanging out with friends.

For budget travelers, budget summer houses are suitable. There are also more expensive rooms - in cottages and in a large winter house. All houses are made of natural wood, the rooms have a refrigerator, TV and electric kettle.

You can eat in a nearby cafe. The plus is that food can be prepared independently:

  • on an equipped stone hearth;
  • in cooking areas located on summer verandas with a gas stove or microwave.

In the vicinity of the recreation center, villagers sell natural local products, including vegetables and fruits, seasonally.

Free of charge for residents: a swimming pool with warm water, secure parking, a playground, gazebos overlooking the river, barbecue and, of course, the Katun coast itself and beautiful nature with mountain views. Nearby is a rustic beach. A good playground has been built for children: carousels, swings, houses and pools.

For lovers of active pastime, car tours in the Altai Mountains, trips in the mountains on ATVs and UAZs, rafting on the Katun River in rafts, trips on a motor boat, flights on a motor hang glider and cycling are offered.

In addition, guests of the Golden Key base can enjoy horse riding and fishing, as well as therapeutic antler baths. There are also two saunas.

Globe Plus

Another great place to relax is the comfortable recreation center Globus Plus, located in the village of Katun, on Nagornaya Street 17. Such unique natural monuments of the Altai Republic as Tavdinsky caves and the holy spring Arzhan Suu are nearby. The hostel is designed for any audience: from families with children to fun groups of friends.

Globus Plus offers a variety of accommodation options: from economy rooms to luxury apartments. The complex includes: parking, sauna, pool, spa complex, gazebos, campfire with barbecue, sports and children's playgrounds, bike rental, banquet and conference room. You can eat in the cozy cafe-bar. Across the territory are gazebos for recreation, places for cooking barbecue.

From entertainment, billiards, table tennis, air hockey, shooting range, rafting, a variety of excursions, fishing, a disco, live music are offered. In addition, the recreation center has active leisure: bicycles, volleyball, badminton, river rafting, cycling, swimming in the river or pool, various excursions. From spa treatments offer antler baths and a phytobarrel.

Thus, whatever type of vacation a tourist chooses, he has at his disposal not only a variety of recreation centers in Gorno-Altaysk, but also places even in the most remote corners of the republic. In any case, the purest air and water, the stunning nature and energy of these places will make your vacation unforgettable.
