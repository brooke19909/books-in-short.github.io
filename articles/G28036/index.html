First of all, you should think about three important components:

  • what will happen to your camera if you change your mind about taking pictures.


If you choose a SLR camera for a beginner, after answering the first question you can determine several important points: which lens is best for you, as well as what else you need, except for the camera and lens? You have already given yourself the answers to these questions, so you can choose a SLR camera for a beginner.



It is worth considering the situation in light of the technology released by Canon. The line of models for beginners is not too large, and the cameras differ from each other very slightly. At the moment, on store shelves you can find 5-6 camera models of this manufacturer, which are suitable for beginners. You can consider several models so that you decide which SLR camera for the beginner is most suitable for you.

The Canon1100D SLR is positioned as a model for beginners. This is expressed not in the fact that its functionality is very truncated, but in the fact that it was initially focused on a person who is poorly versed in this type of technology. This model has a built-in function guide that allows you to easily and quickly cope with any task. The camera works with a number of EF and EF-S lenses. The turn-on time is only a second, one battery charge is enough for a long time, the processor is similar to the older models, which speaks of quite significant opportunities. There are also disadvantages, such SLR cameras for beginners are not able to shoot FullHD video, and this option is quite important, there are no rubberized hand inserts on the case, but this is not a problem, since the camera is quite light.

Another successful model is the Canon 550D. This device is considered to be the most popular among beginner photographers. Compared with the previous model, this one is more advanced, there are a lot of pleasant moments, for example, an HDMI connector, a large screen, the ability to shoot high-quality video, high speed burst shooting.

The Canon 600D SLR differs from the previous cost associated with having a rotary display. Otherwise, the difference is not too significant.

These points can be called the main ones that interest the novice photographer. Of course, you can consider Sony DSLR cameras, but they are worthy of a separate article.
