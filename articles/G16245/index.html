Nelia Gardens 4 * (Cyprus / Ayia Napa) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Ayia Napa is perhaps the most popular resort in Cyprus. This city is famous for its vibrant nightlife and lots of entertainment. It is ideal for youth recreation. However, family tourists, as well as older people, will be happy with their stay in this picturesque place with magnificent beaches. If you want to spend your vacation here and are looking for a comfortable, but not too expensive hotel on the beach, then pay attention to Nelia Gardens 4 *.





Nelia Gardens 4 * on the map

This comfortable hotel is located almost in the center of the Cypriot resort town of Ayia Napa. The nearest beach is 100 meters away. In the vicinity of the hotel there are a lot of various tourist infrastructure facilities: taverns, bars, discos, shops, retail shops, tour desks, car rental points and much more. Also in the vicinity of "Nelia Gardens" there is an amusement park, port and many more interesting places to visit. The nearest airport to the hotel is in Larnaca, 45 kilometers away. So the path to the hotel will take you no more than an hour.

Description and photo of the hotel

Nelia Gardens 4 * is a new modern hotel offering tourists to stay in comfortable and equipped with all necessary spacious rooms. It was built in 2013. The hotel has six two-story buildings, which include 74 comfortable apartments. Each room has air conditioning, a TV with Russian channels, a telephone, a tea / coffee maker, a safe, a bathroom with a hairdryer, a spacious terrace or balcony. Meals at Nelia Gardens are carried out according to one of the following schemes: breakfast only, half board or all inclusive. The hotel has a swimming pool with a sun terrace and a children's playground.





Prices

As for the cost of living in Nelia Gardens Hotel 4 *, then, according to tourists from Russia and other countries of the world, it is quite consistent with its category, accommodation conditions and level of service. So, a seven-day stay here in the month of August will cost you from 770 euros for double occupancy in a deluxe studio and from 1260 euros for double occupancy in two-room apartments. Prices shown include breakfast only.

Impressions of our compatriots about rest in Nelia Gardens 4 * (Ayia Napa)

Since modern tourists, when planning a trip to a particular country, are guided not only by the official description of the hotel, but also by the reviews of other travelers who have already been here, we decided to make it easier for you and invite you to familiarize yourself with the generalized comments of our compatriots who stayed during their Holidays in Cyprus at the four-star Hotel Nelia Gardens (Ayia Napa). Just note that most tourists were more than satisfied with their choice. In their opinion, the hotel meets its category and cost and is a great place to relax on the beach. But we will understand in more detail.





Comments from travelers about the hotel itself, its location and territory

Our compatriots described Nelia Gardens as a small and comfortable hotel. The tourists considered the proximity to the sea to be a big plus for it. So, go to the nearest beach no more than five minutes. The territory of Nelia Gardens 4 * is small and very well-groomed. There is a swimming pool (it is regularly cleaned), a sun terrace with new comfortable sun loungers and a children's playground.

The rooms in the hotel are very spacious and stylishly decorated. The apartments are furnished with modern furniture and appliances. A big plus, our compatriots considered the excellent soundproofing in the rooms. So no noise from discos from nearby establishments will not interfere with your vacation. There is also a safe in the rooms. Almost all apartments have spacious balconies with beautiful views. They are equipped with convenient clothes dryers. Room cleaning is carried out every day. Location Nelia Gardens 4 * (Cyprus) is also recognized by travelers as very successful. So, the hotel is located not only near the beach, but also the main tourist street of Ayia Napa with many bars, restaurants, clubs and other infrastructure can be reached in just 5-10 minutes. In half an hour on foot you can reach the magnificent Nissi Beach. Also in the vicinity of the hotel there is an amusement park, a port (experienced tourists recommend purchasing sea excursions), churches, souvenir shops and much more. It is also convenient to get to the water park from the hotel, where both children and adults will definitely enjoy it.

Travelers' opinions on food at Nelia Gardens

If you stayed at Nelia Gardens 4 * and chose all-inclusive meals, then the hotel itself will offer you only breakfast. You will have to go to lunch and dinner to a neighboring hotel of the same chain called Nelia Beach. However, it is literally two steps away, so this fact will not cause you any trouble or inconvenience. As for the local menu, according to the guests it is impossible to call it very diverse. However, everyone will always find something to their taste and will not remain hungry. Many tourists were very pleased with both the national dishes served to them here and the dishes that are familiar to us. Everyone also liked the fact that they serve ice cream as a dessert for dinner in the hotel restaurant.

If you plan to eat on the basis of "breakfast only" or "half board", then you can go to lunch and dinner in one of the restaurants located in the vicinity of the hotel. All local catering establishments offer deliciously prepared dishes.





Impressions of tourists about the hotel staff

As for the employees of Nelia Gardens 4 *, according to our compatriots, they are very kind, hospitable, friendly and helpful people. The hotel staff will always do everything possible to promptly resolve any problems that arise. Also, some travelers noted the fact that at an early arrival, administrators always try to populate tourists as early as possible, without condemning them to waiting for the checkout time. In addition, to the delight of our compatriots, most of the Nelia Gardens staff speaks Russian. So you will not have any communication problems here.





Impressions of our compatriots from beach recreation and entertainment

The Nelia Gardens 4 * hotel does not offer special entertainment. On its territory you can only have a good time by the small pool on the sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers. In addition, experienced travelers recommend that all tourists coming to Cyprus rent a car, scooter or other vehicle and make trips to different parts of this island with an amazing history, culture and magnificent nature.

As for the beaches of Ayia Napa, they are all in municipal ownership. Therefore, you will have to pay for the rental of sun loungers and parasols. The beach closest to Nelia Gardens is just a five-minute walk away. It is sandy and very clean. When entering the water there is a narrow strip where stones are found. You can either carefully follow them, or go into the sea along a specially equipped path that stretches to the sandy bottom.
