Fashionable crochet headband for girls

Many mothers would like to know how a headband for a girl fits.

It is not difficult to make such a fashion accessory with a hook, even if the knitting experience is small, the main thing is the desire to see your princess the most beautiful. Such a thing is very practical - it will help the young fashionista look good and neat in any situation and emphasize the features of her style.

How to tie a bandage on the head of a girl?
If you prefer simplicity and elegance, pay attention to this model. The photographs show the sequence of work. The headband for the girl is crocheted 2 mm thick. You will need a little cotton thread (you can use the rest of the yarn), an elastic hair band, one button if you want to decorate your product with a flower, and a sewing needle. An experienced knitter will immediately determine the sequence of work. For beginner needlewomen, we present a more detailed description of all the stages of knitting:

  • First, determine how long the headband for the girl will be.
  • Crochet a loop of thread and begin to tie the edge of the elastic, as shown in the photo. Perform 5 loops - this is the first row from which work will begin.
  • From each loop, knit a double crochet and make a turn. Move in the opposite direction. Continue this way until you get about 9 cm. This part of the bandage will be hidden under the hair, so it has a simple look.
  • We pass to the main part of the rim. We knit similarly with the only difference: at the end of each row after the turn we knit 4 air loops to make a small arc, in place of which there will be a festoon. The length of this section is calculated as follows: total head circumference - 18cm - the length of the folded elastic.
  • Knit another 9 cm like the first section and connect the end of the dressing to the other end of the elastic. How to do this was described at the beginning.
  • Now it is time to make openwork festoons from two sides on the central part of the rim. To do this, knit 9 double crochet stitches and a single crochet stitch through one arc of air loops around the edge of the work. Repeat the same action on the opposite side.
  • Connect the center of the elastic as shown in the illustration. On the front side, the ligament should be very neat. Fasten and cut all the threads so that the headband for the girl, crocheted, takes on a finished look.

If you like the extra details, you can separately tie a small flower. Two-layer elements made of threads of a contrasting color look very impressive, as in the photo. You can attach such a decoration using a button or bead.

A few more options for headbands for girls are presented in the form of a variety of patterns in the central part of the product.
