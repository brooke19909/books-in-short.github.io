Rolsen TVs: advantages and disadvantages, reviews

The need for inexpensive and high-quality equipment in our country remains invariably high. On the one hand, this is due to the low incomes of the population. On the other hand, the widespread distribution of suburban areas, as well as the fact that a significant part of the population lives in villages and towns in which ultra-modern multifunctional household appliances are simply not required. Not to mention the fact that the percentage of thefts in the private sector is much higher than in the city. That is why many buyers pay attention to the "Rolsen" technique, namely, to the televisions of this brand.

history of the company

The company Rolsen Electronics can not boast a century of history, like many famous brands. It takes its beginning in 1990. It was then that in South Korea appeared an independent company that produces televisions.

5 years later, production was launched in Russia under well-known brands, and another 4 years later our own brand, Rolsen, was released, under which we still know this technique. Now the company produces a number of TV equipment, which is dominated by liquid crystal televisions. As well as several items of audio products and kitchen appliances.

CRT TVs

The company Rolsen for a long time produced kinescope TVs. Even when other firms switched to liquid crystal and plasma models. Rolsen CRT TVs can still be found in storehouses or in private sales announcements. Small and cheap relative to modern models, they are perfect for a summer house or kitchen.

Advantages of CRT brand Rolsen TVs



Consider the advantages of some models produced under this brand:

  1. Affordable price.
  2. High image quality.
  3. Built-in player that allows you to listen to and view recordings in DVD, SVCD, MPEG4 (DivX) and MP3. To control the player, the remote control for the Rolsen TV is used.
  4. Absolutely flat screen in the latest generation models.
  5. Ultra-thin telescope.
  6. Stereo sound.
  7. Built-in games, a wide range of settings.

Disadvantages CRT TVs "Rolsen"

The disadvantages of the models of this company are the same as that of CRT TVs in general. It:

  1. Dimensions and big weight. Such an instance can only be installed on a pedestal, but it is impossible to hang it on a wall, like liquid crystal models.
  2. The lack of modern connectors.
  3. Outdated technology.

Advantages and disadvantages of plasma models

As well as kinescope models, the Rolsen company continued to produce plasma TVs at a time when all other manufacturers switched to more modern technology.

The main advantages of plasma TVs Rolsen:

  1. High contrast and brightness.
  2. Excellent color rendering.

Disadvantages:

  1. Burnout of pixels over time.
  2. Small, by modern standards, resolution.
  3. There are no connectors for connecting additional devices, for example, an external hard drive.

LCD TVs



To date, all the development of the company "Rolsen" associated with LCD televisions. Several product lines have been released, including devices with LED backlighting, as well as the ability to watch movies in 3D. The most advanced copies, such as the RL-32L1005U, are no different in technical specifications from the widely advertised advanced brands.

Advantages of Rolsen LCD TVs

  1. Using LED backlighting allows you to maximize the picture to natural colors.
  2. The refresh rate of the frame increased to 120 Hz, which increases the smoothness of the characters in dynamic scenes.
  3. Using SuperSlim Frame technology (thin aluminum frame) around the perimeter. It visually enlarges the screen, allowing you to improve the perception of the image.
  4. Screen resolution allows you to watch movies in FullHD format.
  5. Some Rolsen LCD televisions come with a Personal Video Recorder feature that allows you to record video while watching digital channels for later HD playback.

Disadvantages of Rolsen LCD TVs

  1. Low resolution of most budget models.
  2. Weak speakers that do not provide the necessary clarity and volume.
  3. The viewing angle does not exceed 170 degrees, and with a significant deviation from the line perpendicular to the screen, the picture begins to distort.

LED TVs "Rolsen"



The company "Rolsen" and LED models did not bypass its attention. Through the use of a large number of LEDs, manufacturers have achieved the ability to locally darken and highlight portions of the screen for greater contrast.

Advantages of Rolsen LED TVs

The advantages of such models include:

  1. Three-year warranty on most Rolsen LED TVs.
  2. Using TruSurround XT technology allows you to achieve surround sound.
  3. The presence of at least one USB connector makes it possible to connect multimedia devices and watch movies from removable media.
  4. Implementation of the PNC system allowed to obtain colors close to natural.

Disadvantages of Rolsen LED TVs

Among the shortcomings are:

  1. The maximum resolution (1366x768) does not allow you to watch a movie in high quality.
  2. Limited functionality.


It should be noted that the characteristics of specific models in different lines, even belonging to the same series, can vary significantly. And those features and capabilities that are not available in basic televisions may appear in premium devices.

Reviews

According to the reviews of the owners, the main thing that attracts buyers in Rolsen televisions is the low price. For those who do not plan to connect the equipment to the computer or use the Smart TV function, they will be an ideal option. Reliable, stylish, meeting the requirements of our time, they are perfect for both daily viewing in the apartment, and for periodic use in the country.

Despite the low cost of special claims, the owners do not have Rolsen TVs. Reviews indicate that the equipment is reliable enough, and breakdowns occur in isolated cases. However, many have claims to image quality. Some people notice picture distortion, lack of clarity not only in dynamic, but also in static scenes. Lame sound quality. As a rule, the built-in speakers have sufficient power, but when you increase the volume, the clarity of the sound disappears.

Also among the features of TVs, the owners note the braking of the device when working with the menu, selecting channels, settings. Such a nuance has no effect on the work of equipment as a whole and is not a malfunction.

Portable TV "Rolsen"

The company "Rolsen" took care of the release of not only stationary but also portable televisions. Such devices can be taken on the road for a long trip, on vacation or to the country. The company produces only 3 models: RTV 700, 900 and 1000, differing from each other in resolution and screen size. The Rolsen portable TV is equipped with a digital TV tuner for receiving DVB-T2 and DVB-T signals. The built-in battery allows you to watch videos for three hours without recharging the device. If you wish, you can upload your favorite cartoons and movies to a flash card or memory card and watch them on the go through a USB port.

There is no mount to the car. You can watch the video only by holding the device in your hands.

Reviews on the portable Rolsen TV

Portable TVs of this company are not much different from models released by other manufacturers. Small resolution, small viewing angle (it will be quite difficult to watch the screen together), weak speakers - these are the main disadvantages of the Rolsen portable TV.

The manual describes in detail the principle of device setup, purpose and precautions. Complete with a portable TV there is a remote control with which you can switch channels, add sound and change settings. Price is another argument in favor of buying such a model. For little money, the buyer gets the opportunity in decent quality to watch favorite shows and films anywhere.
