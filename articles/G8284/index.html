Anchor slot, private sector. Description, leisure, services

Resting in Sochi, you can hear or see the name Anchor slot on road signs. Such an unusual phrase was called a microdistrict located in the center of the Lazarevsky district (Lazarevskoye) of the city of Sochi. About the Anchor Slit, the private sector located in this place, about the rest there and its features will be discussed in this article.

Description

As mentioned earlier, the Anchor Slot is located in the Lazarevsky District of Sochi. It got its name thanks to the river of the same name and is located 32 km southeast of Lazarevsky, which is the administrative center. In the western part, the village of Yakornaya Slit is washed by the Black Sea, and in the eastern part it is surrounded by mountains and forest, due to which it is reliably protected from winds.

In these places there is a special purity of mountain fresh air, the warmth of the sea, and home comfort is felt. This is what attracts many guests from all over Russia. Also here you will not meet a large number of people, as, for example, in Sochi, Adler or Lazarevsky. In the Anchor Slot, in the private sector, comfortable conditions for relaxation are created. The village itself is not very large in size, but quite cozy and comfortable.

Private Hotels Anchor Slots

More than 20 mini-hotels, as well as guest houses and hostels, have been erected here. On the territory of the village and next to it there are recreation centers, pensions and large hotels. It is worth noting that the price of accommodation in this place is significantly lower than in Sochi and Lazarevsky.

In the Anchor Slot, absolutely all the infrastructure is built up, which is necessary for a comfortable and pleasant stay. The village is fully connected to the central electricity network, has gasification, including of the private sector, as well as a central sewer and collector system. Cellular communications work great here and there is 4G coverage.

Service system

In the Anchor Slot, in the private sector, a system of tourist services during the holiday season is developed at a high level. For example, there are several restaurants, bars, cafes and canteens. A tourist who came here will not remain hungry, as in the above institutions a wide selection of dishes. You can enjoy dishes not only of traditional Russian cuisine, but also of Caucasian, Oriental and Japanese.

There are no large shopping centers and hypermarkets in the village, however there are shops where you can purchase everything you need. Any food: meat, fish, vegetables, fruits, as well as drugs and household goods.

Private Anchor Holidays

In addition to relaxing on a beautiful beach, guests who come to the village enjoy spending time fishing. You can go to the open sea on a rented boat and go fishing. Boat trips on boats, yachts, scooters and catamarans are offered.

Those who love the amazing underwater world can enjoy the diversity of marine life by scuba diving under the supervision of experienced instructors.

For local wildlife lovers, local tour operators and companies offer trekking in the mountains, visiting caves, including underwater ones. Horse and bike rides can also be enjoyed while enjoying the magnificent scenery of this beautiful place. Fans of extreme sports can take an exciting ATV ride on mountain and rough terrain.

On a warm, calm evening, you can visit one of the restaurants or cafes, for those who want noisy fun - a direct route to Sochi or Lazarevskoye. Logistics in the village is thought out quite competently, and you can get to the regional center or Sochi by shuttle bus. In the summer, they run until one in the morning.

In general, rest in the Anchor Slit is very comfortable and pleasant. In this case, the tourist does not have to pay dearly for the time spent here. This is a place that you must visit to get a lot of pleasant emotions.
