How to make flowers from balls and decorate a festive interior with them?

When the holidays come, we certainly want to somehow decorate the interior of the room in which the celebration will take place, thereby arranging an original surprise for our loved ones. Of course, today there are many companies that do this professionally, at a high level, but their services are so expensive that not everyone can afford such a luxury. Some secrets on how to make flowers from balls

It is advisable to start your work in the art of modeling from balloons (twisting) with the most simple, but at the same time the most popular shapes - colors, as they are always the most welcome guests both at adults and at children's parties. Before making flowers from balls, you need to purchase a hand pump for inflating balloons and a set of special balloons (balloons for modeling). Further, when creating your own composition, it is necessary to take into account that it is necessary to inflate the balls in such a way that they all turn out to be the same in size, for this they usually use a homemade cardboard or foam template with a certain diameter. You can also count the number of full pump strokes.

How to make flowers from balls? An easy way for beginner twisters

Consider the process of making a flower with our own hands on a specific example - we will try to collect a noble and elegant iris.

Since you want to make a flower of balls as similar as possible to the original, then for the petals we select a ball of pink or purple hue, and for the stem, of course, green. Inflate the ball so that there remains a small tail (3-5 cm long), unfilled with air.



Fold the ball in half and tie the ends.



Then the resulting circle must be twisted in the center to get two loops of the same size.





Having folded the loops, twist them again, but so that one loop is one third, and the second two.


Thus, we get a bud very similar to a real iris.



The green ball should be inflated in the same way, leaving a small ponytail.



Having made several twists, as shown in the figure, we form leaves on the stem.



Then we connect the bud with the stem - and our iris comes to life.



If before you had no idea how to make flowers from balls, now by turning on your imagination, you can come up with many different colors and decorate any interior. A great idea would be to decorate the path, the perimeter of the room or terrace for the celebration with a border of airy flowers. Also, flowerbeds made of such flowers, arranged between tables, in the corners of the hall or stage, will look great. And panels attached to the walls, composed of large flowers, or bouquets hanging from the ceiling on a fishing line, will be your excellent find as a novice designer. And do not forget to make a memorable photo of the flowers from the balls - a masterpiece made by your own hands. Such a bright photo will certainly warm you, dispel melancholy and cheer you up on any gloomy and cloudy day.
