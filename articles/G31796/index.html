Guest House Sofia, Adler: guest reviews

Sofia Guest House (Adler) is a great alternative to all major hotel complexes, which cost a lot to stay. Cozy rooms at low prices, high-quality service, great views, proximity to the sea - all this is a clear advantage of the guest house.

What is a guest house?

The phenomenon of the guest house has existed for a very long time. It takes its roots from ancient times. All guest houses in Adler - “Sofia”, “Lazur”, “By the Sea”, operate on the same principle - the building itself is a private property, the owner of which rents it in whole or separately by room. Very often, the owners of such houses live there and are directly involved in servicing their guests.





About all services that are provided in the guest house, its owner must report to the relevant government agencies. This for tourists has its positive aspects: if you provide low-quality services, you have every right to contact the regulatory authorities and demand that an inspection be carried out in this house.

The place where the Sofia guest house is located (Adler, Medova, 2) is very convenient for tourists, since the Black Sea coast is located 500 meters from it. A four-story house is often called a hotel, although it is not. Across the road is the Adlerkurort association, which includes an oceanarium, water park, night club, dolphinarium and other entertainment facilities.

In total, 44 places are offered here, in each room there is a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, dishes, as well as all the necessary furniture. The guest house, unfortunately, does not accept visitors with animals, so if you are going to relax with your pets, it is best to look for another place for them. The cost of daily living in such a guest house ranges from 800 to 1400 rubles, depending on the time of year in which you relax.

Reviews

If you intend to relax in the south, be sure to pay attention to the guest house "Sofia" (Adler). Reviews about this place, located on Medova Street, are mostly positive. Many in the house are attracted by its convenient location in relation to the sea and all the necessary infrastructure, low prices - another reason to relax here.





Among the negative aspects, vacationers note too few rooms, which is why rooms have to be booked almost a month in advance. In addition, the guest house only accepts guests from May 1 to October 31, although many other similar establishments work year-round. The management explains this by the small number of guests in the winter season, and so far does not plan to switch to a year-round mode of operation.

Conclusion

Those who have been vacationing in this resort city for more than one season recommend visiting the Sofia guest house (Adler), which is located on Medova Street. The main reason is the high-quality service, which is now widely appreciated in the tourism industry. Vacation prices here are average compared to other travel agencies, but vacationers prefer to pay a little more and live as close to the sea coast and the beach as possible. Another plus of this particular guest house is the following: just cross the road and you will immediately find yourself in the center with a lot of entertainment facilities.

Conclusion

It is recommended to clarify the cost of accommodation in the guest house, hotel or mini-hotel you are interested in, since prices may change periodically. The same applies to rail and air tickets, if you wish, you can book them 30-45 days before the departure date. It should be remembered that, depending on the time of year, the fare can both increase and decrease. Have a good rest!
