Preferential mortgage

New buildings in Saransk are different, there are many offers. It was possible to maintain growth rates in the construction sector thanks to support from the government of the Republic of Mordovia. The head of the republic proposed and implemented a program of preferential support for mortgages. In fact, a mortgage in Saransk is offered at 5%, the difference between the bank interest is compensated from the regional budget. This allowed a large number of families to improve their living conditions. Since the program had a response and increased demand for apartments in new buildings in Saransk, its action has already been extended, now it is valid until the end of 2018. To become a member of the preferential program is simple: you need to live and work in the republic, the cost per square meter in the purchased housing should not be higher than 44 thousand rubles and the bank rate should not exceed 12%. The conditions are feasible, since the average cost per square meter in Saransk is about 42 thousand.
