How to sign up through the Public Services to the Pension Fund: rules for registering and using the portal

Pension fund is a demanded organization, the services of which are used by almost every person. It transfers funds to working citizens, and people who have reached retirement age receive a pension from this fund. This payment can be assigned not only by age, but also for other reasons.

Often you have to take different information from the organization or get advice. To do this, you need to visit the PF branch, and you can pre-register at this institution. This can be done over the Internet. Therefore, you should figure out how to sign up through the "State Services" to the Pension Fund using the official website of this institution.

What does PF offer?

The Pension Fund of the Russian Federation is considered to be a progressive and demanded organization, trying to keep up to date, therefore it has its own official website. It is constantly being modernized and developed, so it is convenient to use. Each visitor can get a personal account. Due to this, you can access various services and documentation of the organization. Numerous reference data are also offered. Getting access to the personal account of the insured person on the PF website is quite simple. To do this, you just have to go through a simple registration procedure.

Features of a personal account on the PF website

Your personal account is presented with special pages created exclusively for a particular visitor. They have information available only to some individuals, since the personal data of each citizen must be confidential.





On the PF website, the personal account of the insured is protected by a password and login, which is invented by the citizen himself. To gain access to it, you need to register, and the process is performed through the "State services".

How to register on the site of the PF and "State services"?

To understand how to sign up through the Public Services to the Pension Fund, you should correctly register on this site. For this, sequential actions are performed:

  • initially you need to go to the official website of the PF;
  • there is a special tab "Registration";
  • after its opening, the user will be redirected to the EGAIS system of the State Services site;
  • in the form that opens, you must enter the name and surname of the citizen, as well as a phone number and email address;
  • To confirm registration, a message will be sent to the phone with a one-time code, which must be entered into the form.




After completing these steps, the registration procedure at the State Services will be carried out, so you can get a lot of information about a particular citizen from the Pension Fund. But such registration is only the initial and easiest, so many services will not be available to the user. How to enroll through the State Services in the Pension Fund? To do this, you need to get a standard account.

How to enroll in the PF?

After completing all the necessary preliminary steps, you need to figure out how to make an appointment with the Pension Fund via the Internet. For this, sequential actions are performed:

  • initially you need to go to the official website of the institution - pfrf.ru;
  • it must be authorized, for which a username and password are entered;
  • at the bottom of the page there is access to your personal account;
  • after its opening, a list of services that can be used using the site will appear;

  • a window opens in which it is necessary to fill in all available lines, and it is important to choose the region and territorial authority of the FIU correctly, as well as enter the name and contact details of the applicant;
