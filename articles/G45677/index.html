The amazing town of Kindness in Montenegro

Traveling around Montenegro, you can discover new facets of this picturesque country. In each new village you will not get tired of wondering and admiring the beauty of nature and architecture. Where to find Kindness?

A settlement with such a nice name for Russian perception stands out in a separate village only conditionally. In fact, it is part of the city of Kotor and smoothly passes into it.

Dobrota (Montenegro) is a small town with a population of just over 8 thousand people. Locals are popular in their village and are actively renting out apartments for visitors. At the same time, on the beaches and in the active tourist areas, during the season, you will not find the indigenous population. They patiently "wait" for the influx of visitors, and then return to their measured life.





Natural resources

Kindness is located on the shore of the Bay of Kotor. The village has an elongated, long shape. There is a promenade on the shore of the bay, with a length of about 7 kilometers, and residential buildings around it.

There are no sandy or pebble beaches in Kindness. Tourists rest right on the numerous piers, placing sun loungers and sunbeds there. You can swim in the water diving from the piers or going down the specially equipped steps. The Bay of Kotor is one of the favorite vacation spots in the Adriatic region for many tourists, although there are no crowded beaches.

Due to the lack of a shallow sandy or pebble bottom, Kindness in Montenegro is popular for owners of yachts and other water transport. Watercraft "park" right under the windows of the apartments, which can often be observed at this resort.

The amazing climate of these places is provided by the mountain slopes that surround Kindness. The air here is always clean and transparent, and the climate is moderately mild. The area is surrounded by olive and citrus groves, which is not only beautiful, but also extremely healthy. One walk through these amazing places and you can recharge with energy and positive for a long time.





What to see in these places?

The kindness of Montenegro owes its sights to the heyday of this area, which fell at the beginning of the XVIII century. Then the local coast was popular among wealthy sailors and shipowners who not only stayed in a picturesque corner of Montenegro, but also built luxurious houses and temples here.

Until today, the buildings of those times came in the form of historical monuments, interesting structures and castles, which invariably attract tourists with their antiquity and authenticity.





But the religious buildings, temples and churches, remain a constant highlight of Kindness. They are striking in their monumentality combined with simplicity. Reviews about Kindness (Montenegro) in most cases are positive. Everyone who has been here at least once wants to come back here again. Of the iconic places often visited by tourists, it is worth noting:

  • Church of St. Eustathius;
  • palaces of Radonichi and Milosevici;
  • Church of St. Matthew.

What do you need to know about?

Due to its location and historical development, the infrastructure of Kindness may not be well developed for sophisticated tourists.

In this area there are shops and restaurants, but in small quantities and with a limited range. Also at this resort, it may seem somewhat boring to lovers of active nightlife.





Kindness in Montenegro is an ideal place for a family vacation, as well as for those who are tired of the daily hustle and bustle of tourist attractions. Arriving in this calm corner of Montenegro, you can enjoy the unique air, stay in solitude among citrus and olive trees and touch the history of these amazing places.
