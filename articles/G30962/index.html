The best brands of Chinese phones: review, specifications, models and reviews

The smartphone market has developed rapidly over the past 3 years, large companies have already made great strides in this field, but stagnation has recently been observed - innovations are appearing less and less and people are less interested in acquiring ‚Äúflagship‚Äù gadgets. One of the reasons for the fall of large manufacturers can be called the expansion of devices from the Middle Kingdom. Chinese phone brands occupy an increasing part of the market, and restless manufacturers are actively introducing modern technical equipment, while offering dumping prices that attract both advanced users and not too biased ones.

Main models: the pros and cons of Chinese brands of smartphones

Brands of Chinese Android phones vary widely. There are both fairly well-known players, and "nameless", who showed the world one or two gadgets. Not all budget manufacturers take quantity and price. Well-known brands of Chinese phones have managed to enter the vast global market thanks to the desperate copying of eminent competitors - similar devices at low prices disperse like hotcakes.





These devices, of course, have both their pros and cons. The advantages of such gadgets include their price. Brands of Chinese phones are independent of retail, they do not need advertising, therefore, having saved on these components, the creators of gadgets save significant funds, which ultimately manifests itself in the form of reduced cost smartphones. It is clear that this fact will certainly please potential buyers.

Also, the advantages of such devices include their "top" filling. Chinese brands of mobile phones, despite their commitment to the middle price segment, equip their offspring with the latest technology. If you see a 10 or 20 core chip anywhere, then this will be a newcomer from China. Recently, Chinese phone brands can boast of an interesting design: if before all the gadgets from the Middle Kingdom looked like randomly and carelessly collected pieces of plastic, now the creators are increasingly paying attention to the appearance of smartphones. Often there is the use of more solid materials, such as steel, glass, aluminum, sometimes even wood.

Unfortunately, inexpensive gadgets have a number of disadvantages. First of all, you need to be aware that phones whose price threshold is especially low will not be able to boast of high-quality assembly. That is, those smartphones that attract less than 3 or 4 thousand rubles, still look like those same faded and cheap plastic Chinese fakes.





In addition to low-quality components, you must remember that the software content of such devices leaves much to be desired. For example, often there is a huge amount of pre-installed, terrible in quality or completely unnecessary software, which is crammed with all the cheap brands of Chinese phones: with changing voice applications, demos of games, "national software" and the like. The developers do not really care about optimization. "Android" - the system is finicky and requires a powerful filling or good straightening on the part of the developer in order to ensure proper gadget performance.

And finally, even large manufacturers are not averse to deceive their fans. If your new, inexpensive smartphone has 8 cores, do not be surprised that only 4 are actually active, and a 13 megapixel camera, coupled with interpolation, also falls short of an 8 megapixel photomodule.





All that can be advised is to choose those ‚ÄúChinese‚Äù who have already gained a good reputation. But you need to avoid those who, using the inexperience of users, deceive them. Actually, more about devices.

Reviews and conclusions

It should be said that most users perceive Chinese phone brands as a worthy product. This is not consumer goods - now such gadgets are full-fledged market players. The quality of budget devices is really surprising; they work and sell well. In addition, these models are fast, practical and beautiful.





Of course, there are those who say that such devices are of poor quality and people make the mistake of buying them. These are mainly users who are used to flagships or have had negative experiences with acquiring Chinese phones. As the saying goes, once a time is not necessary - the level of marriage at such manufacturers is higher than that of serious corporations. But the chance to get a decent device is also great. After all, not everyone wants to overpay for gigabytes, which may not be useful at all, since many do not spend time playing or using bulky applications.

Therefore, the opinions of people are very different: someone thinks that budget gadgets are not worth attention, and someone, on the contrary, is delighted with the new product and is glad that he did not spend money on a more status model. This largely depends on personal preferences. Basically, buyers respond positively to their low-cost purchases and the Chinese gadget market is gradually developing. The user votes in rubles.
