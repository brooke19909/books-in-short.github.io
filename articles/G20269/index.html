Hotel "Nadezhdinsky", Serov: address, service, reviews

Hotel "Nadezhdinsky" in Serov has a long history and rich experience in servicing guests. This is a cozy hotel complex with comfortable living conditions and friendly staff. This is a good place for a relaxing family vacation, as well as for celebrations and business events.

Location

The address of the hotel "Nadezhdinsky" in Serov: Leo Tolstoy street, 18. It is only a kilometer from the Kakva River.

Within walking distance are two supermarkets and several catering establishments with reasonable prices.

Services

A wide range of services is provided to tourists by employees of the Nadezhdinsky Hotel in Serov. The service included in the price is as follows:

  • free Wi-Fi access in the rooms and in the rooms;
  • umbrella rental from the rain;
  • wake up service;
  • use of a safe in the room (for living in a suite);
  • taxi call;
  • Ironing room
  • emergency call if necessary.

The following services are available for an additional fee:

  • food in the hotel cafe;
  • massage room;
  • room service from 7:00 to midnight;
  • drinks and snacks from the minibar;
  • conference room rental and organization of business events;
  • rent of a banquet hall and organization of celebrations;
  • washing and ironing of things of guests;
  • purchase of goods in a souvenir shop;
  • billiards;
  • use of secure parking;
  • use of a safe by the administrator;
  • transfer organization.

Catering establishments

The hotel "Nadezhdinsky" in Serov has a restaurant of the same name, which will delight guests with delicious and high-quality food. In the rooms with a cozy classic interior, guests can enjoy delicious Russian and European dishes. It is also possible to hold celebratory and corporate events in the institution. For these purposes, three rooms are provided:

  • a small hall for 13 guests (+ 5 seats at the bar);
  • large hall for 44 guests;
  • banquet hall for 16 guests.

Also, the hotel "Nadezhdinsky" in Serov has a bar, designed for 20 seats. Here is a wide selection of drinks and light snacks. In a fun atmosphere, you can spend time with friends.

Billiards

If you are planning a visit to Serov, this hotel will be an excellent accommodation option. In addition to comfortable living conditions, the administration also took care of leisure activities. Guests can enjoy a game of billiards. The cost of the service is as follows:

  • Russian billiards (from 8:00 to 17:00) - 160 rubles per hour.
  • Russian billiards (from 17:00 to 8:00) - 200 rubles per hour.
  • American billiards (from 8:00 to 17:00) - 110 rubles per hour.
  • American billiards (from 17:00 to 8:00) - 160 rubles per hour.

Conference hall

If you arrived in the city of Serov for business purposes, the hotel will provide you with the necessary conditions for conducting business events. A conference room for 30 people is presented to the attention of the guests. It features a multimedia projector, plasma screen and flip chart. Also in the room there is air conditioning.

If you plan to hold an event without changing the interior of the conference room, rent will cost you 500 rubles per hour. If you need a rearrangement or decoration, the cost rises to 1000 rubles per hour.

Special offers

For guests of the hotel complex "Nadezhdinsky" in Serov, a number of special offers. Namely:

  • For regular and transit guests discounts on accommodation. From 5 days a discount of 3%. The more accumulated days, the greater the discount.
  • For residents of the city of Serov there is a 50% discount on accommodation in suites, junior suites and mini-suites.
  • When ordering a wedding banquet, guests receive a 50% discount on accommodation in all rooms except triple and five-seater.
  • When staying in the suite on weekends, guests enjoy free late check out until 15:00.
  • If you rent a pool table from 2 hours from Monday to Thursday, you will receive a 15% discount on food and drinks in the restaurant.

Positive reviews

The main source of objective information about the Nadezhdinsky Hotel in Serov is guest reviews. Here are some approving comments they leave about this institution:

  • I am glad that the suite has a mini-library with a good selection of classical literature;
  • a very comfortable bed with an orthopedic mattress (you can fully sleep without pain in the back and neck);
  • perfectly clean bedding, which has almost no powder smell;
  • on the second floor there is an ironing room, which always has free access;
  • good open parking (true, paid);
  • uninterrupted operation of wireless Internet (a good signal both in public areas and in rooms);
  • attentive and helpful staff;
  • quite acceptable breakfasts - everything is quite satisfying and cooked at home;
  • convenient location near a good traffic intersection;
  • wide beds, which is especially important for tall people of large physique.

Negative reviews

Not without negative points. They are reflected in such negative comments:

  • sluggish work of restaurant employees - an order has to wait a very long time;
  • the quality of food in the restaurant leaves much to be desired, but the prices can not be called low;
  • small showers, in which it is especially inconvenient for people of large physique to bathe (unpleasantly, the skin touches the walls);
  • the drain in the bathroom does not work well - it is worth cleaning the drain;
  • maids do housekeeping irregularly (you have to constantly remind about this at the reception);
  • due to poor ventilation, a strong smell of sewage is periodically present in the bathroom;
  • difficulties with booking rooms through the official website (this function periodically fails);
  • very small pillows - sleeping on them is completely uncomfortable;
  • cramped room area (guests constantly kick their feet on the edges of furniture);
  • in the rooms and corridors there is a distinct smell of tobacco smoke;
  • lack of soundproofing in the rooms (the conversations of neighbors and the tramp from the corridor interfere with rest);
  • during breakfast it can be problematic to find an empty seat at the table;
  • high prices for accommodation (apparently, this is due to the lack of alternative options);
  • breakfast is not included in the price;
  • old leaking plumbing in the bathroom needs to be repaired or completely replaced;
  • the rooms need redecorating;
  • the temperature of the water in the shower is poorly regulated (it is either icy or immediately very hot);
  • very high prices for laundry services;
  • a few days after the reservation, they can call and inform that the cost of living has increased.
