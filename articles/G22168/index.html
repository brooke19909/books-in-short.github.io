Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos): hotel description, services, reviews

Cyprus is one of the most popular tourist destinations. After all, here you can not only relax on the sandy beach, enjoying the fresh sea air, but also immerse yourself in the local history and interesting culture.

If we talk about the cities of Cyprus, then one of the most famous and popular resorts is Paphos. Of course, a large number of hotels operate in this area all year round. One of the comfortable and inexpensive places to stay is considered the hotel Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 *.

Of course, for starters, tourists are looking for any interesting information, including data on the exact location and infrastructure, living conditions in the rooms. It is worth learning more about food and leisure activities on site. So what is a hotel?

Where is the hotel complex located?





First you need to find out more about the location of the hotel, because this factor is important. Where to look for the hotel Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 *? Paphos, one of the most vibrant and vibrant resorts in the country, is here, in a quiet area, and you have to spend a vacation.

Immediately it is worth saying that the beach is not very close - the distance to it is about 1000 meters. However, the road does not take much time. For those who are burdened by daily walks, a shuttle service is provided. The coast, as well as the city center, can be reached by public transport, the stop of which is located very close to the hotel.

Within walking distance are shops and cafes, which is very convenient. By the way, the airport is located 15 km, so the road to the hotel does not take much time.

Information about the territory and infrastructure

Hotel Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 * is small but cozy. It consists of one large three-story building, built in the Cypriot style. Despite the fact that the hotel has been operating for several decades, it meets international requirements, and repairs are carried out quite often.

By the way, the hotel courtyard is quite spacious. Here, tourists will find terraces for relaxation, a walkway, a swimming pool, sports grounds, and even a small but very beautiful Mediterranean garden. The territory pleases with an abundance of greenery, flowers and discreet scenery.

Hotel complex Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 * Standard (Paphos): room description





This is a small hotel consisting of 35 rooms. Here, tourists are offered standard double rooms, more spacious "studios", as well as apartments in which guests can use not only the bedroom, but also the living room and kitchen corner.

What living conditions can you expect when you stay at the Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 *? Reviews say that the rooms are comfortable, despite the relatively modest arrangement. It has comfortable furniture, as well as household appliances, including a TV (in some rooms it is old, but it works and gives you the opportunity to watch the usual satellite channels), individual air conditioning and a radio. If you paid for a room with a kitchen, then, of course, you can count on household appliances and cooking utensils.

The bathroom is small but clean. Water is supplied regularly. At your service - shower, washbasin, mirror and a set of large towels.

What kind of food should a traveler count on?





When planning a trip, food must be taken into account, because otherwise the vacation can be ruined. So what can a guest at Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 * count on?

The hotel provides an all-inclusive system, which means that tourists are provided with full three meals a day. Reviews say that the menu is quite diverse, the dishes are always fresh and tasty, and the service staff copes with the work. You can count on snacks, fresh salads and fruits, meat and fish dishes, fresh seafood. The price includes drinks, but only from local manufacturers.

Guests enjoy spending time in the local cafeteria, where they are offered cocktails, light snacks and delicious homemade desserts. You can have a good time in local bars (there are two of them). Tourists can count on chilled drinks and strong drinks.

Beach: distance, entertainment and facilities for tourists





If you decide to relax in this resort, you probably expect to spend at least some time on one of the picturesque beaches. What can the hotel complex Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 * offer to its tourists?

Cyprus is famous for its beautiful coast. And just 1000 m is one of the most beautiful and lively public beaches of Paphos. It can be easily reached on foot, but travelers also use shuttles and public buses.

Since the beach here is public, then, of course, renting sun loungers and umbrellas is paid separately. There are several bars and cafes on the shore, where travelers are offered chilled drinks and traditional Cypriot snacks.

The coast is covered with soft sand, the water is clean, and the entrance to the sea is convenient - many tourists do not use special shoes, although it is better to stock up on them. The waters are calm, but from time to time waves rise, which pleases fans of windsurfing. A team of experienced lifeguards works on the beach.

Of course, people who prefer a more active holiday, are also not bored here. Tourists have the opportunity to rent a canoe or boat, take an exciting trip on a boat or yacht, get a lot of vivid impressions from water skiing and parasailing. A spacious playground for volleyball and other sporting events is equipped on the shore. Nobody has time for boredom here.

Additional service: what does the hotel offer?





The hotel is perfect for overnight stays for tourists who spend most of the day in the city or on the beach. Nevertheless, there are some additional amenities. For example, the reception desk is constantly working here, where you can not only get information, but also call a taxi or make an appointment with a doctor (the staff will help you).

You can rent a safe from the administrator for a small fee, in which your valuables and documents will be completely safe under constant supervision. For your convenience, a luggage storage room is also available.

Hotel Paphiessa Hotel and Apts 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos): how to spend your free time?





In the courtyard of the hotel for tourists is equipped with a small pool. There is clear water, and comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas are arranged around. Here tourists sunbathe, swim and have a good time in communication. In cold weather or in the evening, you can relax by the indoor pool, where the temperature is always as pleasant as possible.

For guests at the Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 * there is a sauna and even a traditional bathhouse. Here, tourists can relax in the spacious jacuzzi. You can spend time playing tennis. There is a TV room with a large collection of films, as well as a karaoke bar where travelers love to have fun. From time to time, live music is played at the hotel, and various performances are arranged for guests.

Conditions for a vacation with a child

Since many parents today prefer to spend holidays with children, the availability of appropriate amenities is also considered an important issue. Hotel Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 * is often used for family holidays. On the other hand, tourists say that with small children it may not be so convenient here.

Of course, you can count on some degree of comfort, including the possibility of delivering an extra bed to the room, as well as the availability of a chair for feeding in the restaurant. By the way, a child can also eat at the hotel, because the menu is quite diverse. As for entertainment, there is a shallow pool for children in the courtyard, where they have fun. For a reasonable price, parents use the services of an experienced nanny.

Reviews and recommendations from former guests





Check out official hotel information is always helpful. What are the actual conditions for the guests of the Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 *? The reviews are mostly positive, but it should be noted right away that the majority of travelers use the hotel complex only for bed and breakfast.

Renovation was carried out recently, so the rooms are quite clean and comfortable (by the way, they clean here regularly). Not everywhere there is a new household appliance, but everything works, and the appliances perform their main functions. The bathroom shines with cleanliness, and hot water is available around the clock. The food is at a decent level, since there is always plenty to choose from - even fastidious tourists do not go hungry.

The disadvantages include the distance to the beach. On the other hand, the cost of living at the hotel is very low, which makes it so popular.
