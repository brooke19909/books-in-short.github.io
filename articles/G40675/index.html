Deposits, losses and income of investment companies

When free money appears in your wallet, you can think about investing. For long-term investors, business plans are developed, and the payback period of projects is calculated. The average Russian is more difficult to determine the direction of investment. The task is not only to generate income, but also to minimize the risk of losses.

Investment contribution

Many Russians open bank accounts. With a deposit, you can get a stable, but small income. In the best case, the amount of interest will cover inflation and will save personal savings. This drawback forces investors to search for profitable instruments, for example, investment deposits. It consists of two parts: a basic bank deposit and additional investments in mutual funds (unit investment funds). These funds are used by companies to trade on the stock exchange.

Non-state pension funds also receive investment income. Money transferred by the insured is used to purchase securities. The income received is distributed between the fund and the client.

Features

Do not confuse an individual investment account (IIA) and investment contribution. In the first case, the client independently manages all operations in the stock market. In the second - the client transfers the money for temporary use to the bank, but he chooses the instruments in which the funds will be invested. Also, the financial institution independently decides in what proportion to distribute profits. To begin to receive income from investment activities, you must begin to invest at least 100 thousand rubles. However, the company does not guarantee net profit. As a result of operations, you can get both profit and loss. The essence of the operation is that the company accumulates funds from the capital of several investors, invests them in the stock market. The profit is distributed among investors, the company remains the percentage remuneration of the manager.

Benefits of Deposits

Here are some advantages of such contributions:

  • After the expiration of the contract, the client is guaranteed to receive the amount of the initial deposit.
  • Revenues from projects exceed the interest of bank deposits.
  • To conclude an agreement, it is enough to submit a passport, application and invest money.
  • If an urgent need arises, then at any time you can break the contract and return the invested funds.

Cons of deposits

Disadvantages:

  • In case of early termination of the contract, you can withdraw only the entire deposit amount (even if you need ‚Öì). Interest on the transaction is not charged.
  • If the transaction turned out to be unprofitable, then in addition to the loss of investment income, the client in any case pays the commission of the management company.
  • The maximum duration of the contract with the management company is 12 months.
  • Interest is not accrued on the deposit part of the transaction. It is impossible to replenish the initial deposit amount.
  • The extension of the contract is not provided. You can only open a new account.
  • Profit is accrued at the end of the contract. 13% of taxes are paid on income.

For whom?

After analyzing all the advantages and disadvantages of the contribution, we can conclude that investments and investment income are suitable for people who like to take risks, earn money from the stock market, but are limited in time. Or novice investors who do not yet have trading skills. The tool is not suitable for conservative investors who do not allow the possibility of loss of funds, and people who want to invest the last money.

Where and how can I open an account?

You can become an investor by opening an account with a bank or investment company. The requirements for potential investors in these organizations are different. Only adult citizens and residents of the Russian Federation can open an account and receive income from an investment company (tax payments must go to the treasury of Russia). The deposit amount should not exceed the investment in the mutual fund, otherwise the transaction will be unprofitable for the management company. When investing a large amount of funds, an additional income statement is required.

What does IR do?

An investment company is an organization that invests in assets in order to increase them. Funds can be either own or borrowed from banks and investors. Unlike mutual funds, investment funds have legal registration and a license; their activities are regulated at the legislative level.

Before investing in order to generate income, investment companies analyze the market: market conditions, the price dynamics of the asset, the political situation are examined, and the exchange rate is monitored. That is, all indicators that can affect the price are analyzed.

The income of investment companies may include not only the profit from transactions in the stock market, but also the commission from brokerage operations. After all, only those who have a license for this can become a participant in the securities market. Brokers act as intermediaries between the market and investors. They provide access to quotes and guarantee the security of transactions. They charge a fee for these services. To place funds in the stock market, an investment company needs to use the services of intermediaries or acquire a license for broker operations.

Investment fund earnings also include mergers and acquisitions. The acquisition of one company by another takes place to unite interests, to form an influential market participant. IR is acquired by other organizations for resale purposes. The fact is that during the merger, the price of the company's capital is lower than the market. If you sell it in parts, you can make a profit.

How to choose IR?

The success of operations depends on the choice of an investment company. For a long time, there are organizations on the Russian market that are steadily developing and making profit for their investors. To choose the right company that will manage the money, it is important to consider a number of factors.

Analyze the maximum information on the company website. In particular, pay attention to the duration and date of registration. Usually these data are published on the main page or in the section "About us". The first IRs in the Russian Federation appeared in 1992. If an earlier date is indicated on the site or it is absent at all, then it is better to exclude the company from monitoring.

A registration document must also be published on the site. His absence is a warning signal.

A small number of investors indicates that the company has recently appeared on the market, has not yet gained experience in conducting profitable operations. Therefore, it is better to pay attention to organizations with a large number of investors.

The more ways a company provides a deposit and withdrawal of funds, the more comfortable and profitable it will be to cooperate with it. Some investors prefer bank transfers, others prefer cards, and others prefer electronic systems.

Although no company can name the net investment income from the transaction, it must indicate the approximate percentage. The average return on transactions for each asset is displayed on the site.

Therefore, before concluding a transaction, you should familiarize yourself with the company's statements to make sure that you are not cooperating with MLM (multi-level marketing), but with IR. IR costs

Having figured out how the income of investment companies is formed, we turn to the issue of costs. Investment expenses are expenses incurred by a legal entity in the course of business. These costs are the purchase of assets and the formation of a portfolio with the required rates of return. By purchasing a security, the investor pays its face value, the services of a broker and a bank for the transfer. These are variable costs. Permanent assets include rental of premises, other administrative payments, salaries of employees of ICs, etc. The investments themselves are classified as direct expenses, since they are the main activity for ICs.

Investment Cost Structure:

  • Mandatory (for example, the purchase of securities, the cost of money transfer);
  • indirect (for example, consultation before the conclusion of the transaction);
  • hidden (for example, expenses caused by the early termination of the contract with the client);
  • unreimbursed (all other expenses).

Calculation of investment income

The rate of return is determined in relative terms, that is, it shows how much income one invested monetary unit will bring. By absolute values, it is impossible to evaluate the effectiveness of the project. For example, an investment brings $ 100. And how much was invested? 1000 dollars or 10,000 dollars? How to protect investment income of NPFs?

In 2018, a pension fund client protection program began to operate on the pension market. Its purpose is to protect clients' funds from financial losses of early termination of the contract. If the client transfers the PF savings for three years, then all the income received is guaranteed by the state. But in case of early termination of the contract, net investment income ‚Äúburns out‚Äù. How can this situation be avoided?

Between the client and the PF there is always an intermediary - a bank, broker, agent. It was through them that the majority of Russians learned about the possibility of accumulating pension savings. The sales scheme is built on agents. The latter receive 1.3-1.5 thousand rubles for the attracted client. Therefore, the obligation to inform the insured about the risk of loss of investment income from pension savings lies with the agent. Would a person want to sign a contract for 5 years if he finds out about the loss of income? Most will refuse. Therefore, not all agents notify clients of potential risks. As a result, the Central Bank, PFR and NPF regularly receive complaints from insured persons. Although the conflict lies in the model of attracting customers, the latter are required to study the terms of the contract in detail.

Conclusion

Investment costs and revenues are very difficult to predict. Although the company conducts a detailed analysis of the market before entering into a transaction, the economic situation can change dramatically. In addition, one should not forget about force majeure circumstances, for example, an urgent return of capital by a major investor. Despite significant risks, net investment income usually exceeds the profit from the deposit. Therefore, it makes sense to invest in ICs that have proven themselves in the market.
