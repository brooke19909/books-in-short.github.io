Insurance policy

But back to the classical understanding of this concept. An insurance policy is a document that can be issued for a specific person or bearer. This document certifies the fact of payment of insurance services and guarantees that the insurance organization fulfills its obligations upon the occurrence of an insured event. In most cases, the obligation of the insurer includes the payment of a mandatory agreed amount. The beneficiary is the beneficiary. It can be both an insured citizen and third parties. The insurance premium payable is called insurance compensation (or compensation).

What determines the policy in modern insurance

The main purpose of the policy is to list the conditions under which an insured event will be recognized as such. Therefore, it must necessarily indicate the rights and obligations of both the insurance organization and the policyholder.

The main value of the policy is that it is a document according to which the regulation of relations between the insurer and the policyholder is carried out. The system of modern insurance legislation defines an insurance policy as evidence of the signing of an insurance agreement. It is from this point of view that the insurance policy becomes important evidence when considering insurance disputes in court.

The main details of the policy

The Law "On Insurance" defines a list of basic details that must be indicated in each insurance policy. It:

  • Detailed name of the document;
  • full name and details of the insurance company;
  • Name, date of birth, full address of the policy holder;
  • if the insurance policy is concluded in favor of third parties, the details of the beneficiary are needed;
  • detailed data on the insurance object;
  • the agreed amount of the amount to be paid upon the occurrence of an insured event;
  • a complete list of possible insured events;
  • insurance rate indicating insurance premium;
  • validity period of the insurance policy.

This is a basic list of details required for an insurance document. Consider the additional conditions that must be spelled out in the insurance certificate of compulsory medical insurance.

Medical policy

The MHI policy is designed to receive basic medical care throughout the Russian Federation. It is issued once and for free. In this case, the mandatory insurance contributions are taxes levied on wages and other income. The amount of contributions to the MHI policy is regulated by law. But the reimbursement for medical insurance is no longer paid to the holder, but to the medical institution or private doctor who provided medical services.





A medical policy entitles each holder to take advantage of medical care. The refusal to provide medical services in the presence of a policy is unfounded and may lead to criminal liability. A medical certificate is available to citizens of our country, foreigners or stateless persons.
