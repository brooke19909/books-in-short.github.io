Sheksna hotels: description and reviews

Sheksna is an urban-type settlement in the Vologda Oblast, located on the left bank of the river of the same name. The name of the settlement is of Finno-Ugric origin and translates as "left tributary overgrown with sedge."

The village attracts with developed industry and cultural attractions. Sheksna hotels will provide a warm welcome for both business travelers and tourists.

Hotel "Comfort"

On the ground floor of a five-story building, the Uyut Hotel is equipped. Sheksna, Pervomaiskaya Street 10 - the address of the institution in question. It has 8 double rooms that correspond to different categories. Each is equipped with a TV and fridge. There are rooms with both individual and general amenities. The cost of accommodation is from 700 rubles per day.

It is also worth noting the presence of such advantages:

  • fully equipped shared kitchen;
  • outdoor parking;
  • wireless Internet.

Reviews about the hotel "Comfort"

A number of positive reviews can be heard about the Sheksna hotel in question, namely:

  • cozy calm atmosphere;
  • budget option for short-term accommodation;
  • pleasant outgoing staff;
  • there is a kitchen equipped with everything necessary for cooking;
  • There are shops and cafes nearby.

But there are also negative comments:

  • very old repairs and furnishings;
  • purity is far from ideal.

Hotel "Parus"

The whole tourist complex is the Parus Hotel. Sheksna, Shlyuzovaya street, 11 - address of the institution. It is located in the middle of a beautiful forest on a picturesque coast. The facility is designed for 30 seats, room categories are as follows:

  • one-room number 1 and number 2 - from 2500 rubles;
  • one-room number 3, number 4 and number 5 - from 2000 rubles;
  • number above the bath - from 3000 rubles;
  • individual houses - from 1500 rubles.

Resting here, tourists can take advantage of the following set of advantages:

  • children playground;
  • outdoor sports grounds;
  • billiards;
  • solarium;
  • table tennis;
  • rental of water equipment (catamarans, boats, canoes, surfboards);
  • water sports briefing;
  • boat trips;
  • secure parking;
  • fishing organization;
  • karaoke bar;
  • Russian sauna;
  • animation programs;
  • Cafe;
  • barbecue areas;
  • organization of business, ceremonial and corporate events;
  • well-maintained beach;
  • wireless Internet.

The institution welcomes guests year-round.

Reviews about the hotel "Parus"

You can hear such approving reviews about this Sheksna hotel:

  • convenient signpost system helps you quickly find the way to the hotel;
  • all banquets end at 23:00, so the night is quiet in the territory;
  • polite and attentive staff;
  • a good cafe with a large selection of Russian and international dishes;
  • in summer you can go water sports;
  • convenient access to shops;
  • organization of events at an excellent level;
  • picturesque area with a view of the reservoir;
  • Cleanliness and comfort of the rooms.

And such negative ones:

  • there are no slippers in the rooms;
  • in the cold season it is quite boring here (everything is aimed at summer entertainment and events);
  • the karaoke bar has poor equipment and a small selection of backing tracks.

Hotel "Paradise"

"Paradise" - Sheksna hotel, located at: Vodnikov street, 3. The facility consists of 35 rooms, designed for 65 people. The following options are provided for placement:

  • double standard of increased comfort - from 1600 rubles (from 800 rubles for a place with shared accommodation);
  • triple standard of increased comfort - from 2400 rubles (from 800 rubles for a place with sharing);
  • four-seater standard of increased comfort - from 4,400 rubles (from 1,100 rubles per seat with sharing);
  • single standard of increased comfort - from 1600 rubles.

You can also use the following services:

  • Cafe;
  • outdoor parking;
  • organization of excursions;
  • Russian sauna.
