Unforgettable holidays at the Karelta Beach Hotel (Turkey)

The hotel is located on the Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea. The resort village of Beldibi is located nearby, and the city of Kemer is seventeen kilometers away . Hotel "Karelta Beach" (Turkey) was built in 1999, the last reconstruction was carried out in 2008. Its area is 22,000 square meters.

Turkey. Hotel Karelta Beach: description



The hotel complex consists of one four-story building. The hotel is pleased to offer accommodation to its guests in 205 comfortable rooms: 172 standard, 5 suite rooms, 11 corner rooms and 5 connections rooms. In each room of guests expect:

- shower room;

- balcony (sea, pool or mountain view);

- central air conditioning;

- satellite antenna;

- hair dryer;



- linen change (three times a week);

- mini-bar (for a fee);

- daily cleaning;

- carpet on the floor;

- safe (paid);

- slippers;

- TV with the Russian channel;

- room service (paid);

- telephone (paid);

- restroom;

- cot (on request);

- a towel.

Dining at the Karelta Beach Hotel (Turkey)



From seven in the morning until ten in the evening, guests can enjoy the main restaurant, which serves international dishes. It accommodates up to two hundred and fifty people. There is also an A la carte (fish restaurant), a beach bar, poolside, hotel lobby and a disco bar. Meals at Karelta Beach (Turkey) are organized on an all-inclusive basis. Includes: breakfast from seven to ten in the morning, lunch from twelve to fourteen in the afternoon, dinner from seven to ten in the evening, coffee, freshly squeezed juices, tea. As well as non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks, only locally produced, from ten in the morning to eleven in the evening.

Turkey Kemer). Karelta Beach Hotel: Things to Do

Vacationers with children will like this hotel. Little guests will not be bored, a children's disco is organized for them, games are held in the mini-club. Babysitting services are also available. And adults can still do water aerobics, play mini-golf, football, chess. They are also waiting for water activities on the beach: diving, surfing, volleyball, canoeing, water skiing, windsurfing. For those who love speed, a jet ski, scooter, flight and parachuting are quite suitable. For an additional fee, you can book excursions and visit, for example:

- Beldibi cave (traces of the Paleolithic man are stored there);

- the ruins of the city of Phaselis (there you can see the theater, two harbors, the main street, Byzantine necropolises and so on).

Some guests write that the hotel is located forty-five kilometers from Antalya Airport, so the path to it is not too close. However, the surrounding nature, excellent weather conditions, a comfortable stay and local attractions are worth it (all the more so since you have to go to many other places of rest much longer).
