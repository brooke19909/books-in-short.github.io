"Grand Hotel Astrakhan": description, reviews and photos

"Grand Hotel Astrakhan" is a modern hotel with a huge number of advantages. Today we will discuss the interior of the hotel, its location, reviews and much more. Getting started?

About hotel

The Grand Hotel Astrakhan Hotel is a pearl of local architecture and a wonderful place to stay for a vacation. Polite staff, nice neighbors and fairly comfortable apartments - all this makes the hotel a good reputation. He deservedly proudly wears his five stars.



There are many events held at the hotel, you can have a good time here: the local administration provides its guests with two restaurants with cuisine of different countries, various events with animators for adults and children are held here on an ongoing basis (some of them are free, some require additional fees admission ticket). What else is remarkable about Al Pash Grand Hotel Astrakhan? The pool located on its territory is one of the best in the city. In addition, guests can visit the spa, sauna, wellness area.

Description "Grand Hotel Astrakhan" says that it is permissible luxury. Is it really? Let's find out.

Location

The hotel is located at Astrakhan, Kuibyshev street, 69.

From its windows a wonderful view opens: many guests, peering out onto the street, see the Volga River. The hotel is located in the city center, so it is not so far from the main attractions. The singing fountain, located two kilometers away, is especially beautiful at night when the lights turn on. You can sit around him even until the morning and not get tired ...

In addition to the Astrakhan Kremlin, which is located 1.8 kilometers from the hotel, guests can visit the local museum of local lore 2.5 kilometers away.
