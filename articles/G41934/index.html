Hilton Dreams Resort (Sharm el-Sheikh): review, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Sharm el Sheikh annually attracts millions of tourists who want to spend their holidays here. Such popularity of this resort is associated not only with comfortable natural and climatic conditions, but also with the traditions of oriental hospitality. These customs are fully reflected in the quality service that the Hilton Dreams Resort Sharm provides its guests.

Hotel Description

Hilton Dreams Resort Sharm is a real dream of a tourist, because it has everything you need for a good rest. Polite staff, comfortable rooms, restaurants and bars, as well as great entertainment opportunities are at the disposal of the guests of this hotel complex. Of particular note is the well-kept private beach of the hotel, as well as the picturesque view of Mount Sinai, which opens from the windows of the apartments.





Hotel location

Hilton Dreams Resort Sharm is located on the beautiful shores of Naama Bay, the waters of which are considered the most environmentally friendly in all of Egypt. Sharm el-Sheikh International Airport is only twelve kilometers from the hotel, and the journey to the city center will take no more than fifteen minutes. To plunge into the azure waves of the Red Sea, you have to walk only 200 meters.

What is included in the price

When purchasing or booking a tour, special attention should be paid to the food system in Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort 5 *. "All inclusive" does not apply to all guests and is divided into three varieties:

  • Bed & Breakfast - includes room and breakfast buffet. Drinks are subject to a surcharge.
  • Half Board - this system implies that the guest receives a full breakfast and dinner. As in the previous case, the cost of drinks is not included in the price of the ticket.
  • Full Board - includes three meals a day on the buffet system.

In addition to food, each of the three systems includes visits to swimming pools, a gym, as well as table tennis and beach volleyball.

Tourists reviews

Reviews of the Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort hotel testify to the high quality of service in this hotel complex. Tourists who have previously visited this resort, note the following positive points:

  • large well-groomed area with a huge number of flowering exotic plants;
  • spacious, clean, bright and comfortable rooms;
  • a large assortment of food products offered for the buffet;
  • friendly, understanding and qualified staff, ready to provide assistance, as well as make concessions and compromises;
  • quick resettlement;
  • rich animation program;
  • a nearby well-groomed beach, not far from which are picturesque coral reefs;
  • it is possible to get a room of a higher category for a small fee to the reception staff (this is much cheaper than paying for a room during the purchase of the tour);
  • Pleasant value for money.

A number of tourists note several negative aspects of living in this hotel, namely:

  • some rooms have insects;
  • poorly washed dishes were seen in the hotel restaurants;
  • a pronounced smell of bleach is present in the pools, as well as near some fountains;
  • paid bars on the beach;
  • paid wireless internet.

Helpful information

Going on vacation at the Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort (Sharm El Sheikh), you need to familiarize yourself with some of the provisions regarding the rules of living at this hotel, in particular:

  • Check-in of newly arrived guests to the rooms is after 15:00, but if you want to register earlier, you will have to pay an amount equal to the cost of a full day in the apartment
  • check out from the hotel before 12:00; late check-out is charged extra;
  • no pets are allowed in the hotel, including guide dogs;
  • smoking in the hotel is allowed throughout the territory with the exception of bars and restaurants;
  • there are terminals for cashless payments with plastic cards of international payment systems;
  • Allowed to relax in the hotel with children of any age, but no cots are provided;
  • if the child does not require an extra bed, then he lives in the room free of charge.




So that your vacation goes without excesses and leaves you with only good impressions, carefully approach the choice of a hotel complex. When planning a trip, be sure to check out the reviews of tourists who have previously visited a particular hotel.
