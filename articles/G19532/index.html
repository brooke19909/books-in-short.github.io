Hotel Atlantica Holiday Village Kos 5 * (Kos, Greece): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Atlantica Holiday Village Kos is a 5-star hotel, rightfully considered one of the best in Greece. And you can verify this by at least looking at the reviews left by people who were lucky enough to relax here. However, since the hotel receives "nine" and "tens" on a 10-point rating scale from guests, it is worth telling about it in more detail.





general information

Atlantica Holiday Village Kos, as the name implies, is located on the island of Kos, which is located in the southeastern Aegean Sea. The main highlight of this hotel is the beach on which it is located. It is called Surfers Paradise and is located in the village of Kefalos.

No wonder this beach is considered a paradise. After all, it is covered with fine and clean sand, and the water in the sea is so clear that you can even see the fish floating in schools. It is also interesting that you need to go to a depth of about 200-300 meters.

It takes about 25 minutes to get here from the capital of Kos. You can get there by taxi or public transport (travel costs about 1.5-3 euros per person). By the way, the time to go to the airport is about the same. The infrastructure in Kefalos is developed, so tourists will not have any problems with transport.

Restaurants

Atlantica Holiday Village Kos 4 * (Kos) has several fashionable establishments in which guests are offered a high level of service and excellent food.

The main restaurant serves a wide selection of dishes and serves a buffet. The restaurant operates from 7:00 to 10:30 for breakfast, from 12:30 to 14:30 for lunch, and from 18:30 to 21:00 for dinner.

I would also like to talk about American Restaurant. Between 10:30 and 17:30, snacks are served at the restaurant, and dinner is served from 19:00 until 21:30. There is everything and in large numbers - from grilled ribs to fish and seafood.

Greek & Grill Restaurant is another noteworthy establishment. After all, it is in it that you can try real Greek cuisine. The restaurant is open from 19:30 to 21:00. Be sure to comply with the evening dress code and book a table.

The Italian Stone Grill Restaurant, as the name implies, serves delicious Italian dishes - pasta, salads, pizza, meat and more. A visit to this institution is not included in the all-inclusive package, but at least once you need to look here. It works from 19:30 to 22:00, and a table must be reserved in advance.

An ideal place to watch a football match with a pleasant company for a beer with snacks.
