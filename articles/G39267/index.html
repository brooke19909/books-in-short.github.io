Modern Audit Services

The chief accountant of the organization can carry out verification of accounting, however, audit services provide a higher level of professionalism. Employees of specialized firms know all the subtleties and nuances associated with the documentation of enterprises. A timely audit helps the customer achieve a serious economic result and implement any business project. In addition, audit services allow you to demonstrate to potential partners and customers the openness and transparency of the company. Independent conclusion enhances the company's position in the market of goods and services. Professionals work in complete confidentiality and only taking into account the interests of the customer.

Currently auditing services include a whole list of operations with company documents. This is a check of accounting, its restoration if necessary, analysis of financial condition, assessment of the effectiveness of enterprise management. The auditor can develop recommendations for improving the performance of the company, while the form of ownership or the specifics of the work do not matter.

At the end of the audit, the customer receives a conclusion that strengthens the company's reputation among competitors, makes it possible to obtain the status of a reliable company in the eyes of state bodies. The main advantage that audit services have is the ability to prevent controversial issues, fines and penalties that may arise during the inspection by regulatory authorities.

Based on the conclusion of the auditors, the manager will be able to choose the best way to distribute profits, quickly take measures to timely correct deficiencies in the documentation without violating tax laws. Even if it turns out that the balance is normal, audit services will be useful in any case. The presence of an audit opinion on the financial condition will affect the decision making by investors and partners in your favor.

Another important issue is the correctness of tax calculation and payment, which is especially relevant in the face of constantly changing tax legislation. The penalties for incorrect and untimely calculation of tax payments are quite high. Therefore, tax audit has recently gained great popularity. This is an objective check of the state of accounting and tax accounting, as well as the organization‚Äôs calculations for mandatory payments.

Usually, tax audit conducted on the eve of the inspection of the enterprise. In accordance with the law, a documentary audit of a company is carried out at least once every three years. Specialized firms are often approached by company executives who are interested in reducing tax risks that usually arise in the process of inspections by regulatory authorities.

A tax audit is necessary if the organization pays unreasonably high taxes and fees or, conversely, small amounts at high speeds, tax optimization schemes are applied, and its own accounting system is built. The main purpose of the audit is to check the tax base and declarations according to the methodology of state bodies, identify errors and develop recommendations for their elimination.

Performing a tax audit, specialists will carefully study the accounting of the enterprise, give advice on correcting shortcomings, making changes to the reporting. This will significantly save your money. At the same time, auditors take responsibility for their actions, provided that the organization guarantees the authenticity of the documents submitted for verification and timely correction of errors in accordance with the recommendations.

The main result of a tax audit is to reduce the tax risks of the enterprise. Based on the results of the audit, a conclusion is drawn up regarding the correctness of calculating the tax base for a specific period. In addition, support is provided for the audit conducted by regulatory authorities, and participation in the settlement of disputes.

Today, tax audit is one of the most popular types of audit services.
