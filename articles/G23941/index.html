Tablets "Lenovo" 10 inches: reviews, photos, instructions and specifications

The Lenovo brand is among the leaders in the global market for mobile gadgets. This Chinese corporation produces stylish and productive smartphones and tablets running various operating systems. Many users and experts in the mobile market believe that the optimal diagonal of the tablet screen is 10 inches. The brand in question produces a large number of devices that match this parameter. These can be classified immediately on several lines. What is the specificity of devices related to them?





What 10-inch tablets does Lenovo produce?

The Chinese company Lenovo supplies the market with several lines of tablets with a diagonal of 10 inches. Among the most popular in Russia:

  • IdeaPad;
  • TAB
  • ThinkPad
  • Yoga Tablet;
  • Miix
  • IdeaTab.

The main thing that combines these Lenovo tablets is 10 inches in the display. Otherwise, the differences between the marked lines are significant. Let's try to explore the specifics of each of them, having studied the features of the popular devices related to them.

IdeaPad line on the example of K1 device

Let's start with the IdeaPad line. Let's study it using the example of the K1 device, also called LePad.





This Lenovo tablet is a 10-inch, it was first introduced on the market in 2011. It is considered relatively outdated, but at the time it went on sale it was considered as one of the most competitive in its segment. The device is controlled by the Android OS in version 3.1. The width of the device is 264 mm, the height is 189 mm, the thickness is 13 mm, the weight is 726 g. The tablet is rather massive in comparison with other rulers from Lenovo. The device is equipped with an NVIDIA Tegra 2 T20 processor, operating at a frequency of 1 GHz. The amount of RAM installed in the tablet is 1 GB, the built-in flash memory is 16 GB. The main communication standards supported by the device: GSM, 3G, Bluetooth in version 2.1, Wi-Fi. The screen resolution of the device is 800 by 1280 pixels. Type of display - capacitive. The tablet is equipped with an NVIDIA ULP GeForce video module. The device has 2 cameras - a front camera with a resolution of 2 MP, as well as a rear one - 5 MP. There is a flash, as well as autofocus. A stereo speaker is installed on the tablet, there is a connector for connecting audio devices. The device in question is equipped with sensors: light, G-Sensor. The device supports additional microSD memory cards. It is possible to connect other devices through the microHDMI slot.

K1 tablet: features and reviews

Key features of the K1 device as a representative of the IdeaPad line:

  • high resolution pictures taken with the main camera;
  • large amount of built-in flash memory;
  • productive video module.

What do lovers of mobile gadgets who have used this Lenovo tablet (10 inches) say? The reviews of the owners of the device in question, first of all, characterize the device as sufficiently productive and stable. Many experts on the mobile gadget market also consider the speed of the K1 tablet, as well as the absence of noticeable failures in its work, to be its strongest points. Which, at the same time, characterize the entire IdeaPad line in the same way.

Models of the same line: how noticeable are the differences?

In the market of mobile devices, a situation may well arise when, within the same line of the manufacturer, the differences between the devices will be as significant as when compared with devices of other concepts. However, as for Lenovo products, there are usually more similarities between tablets of the same row. Especially in the design.

So, for example, the Lenovo 10 inch A7600 tablet is popular within the IdeaTab line. It can be noted that it is very similar in design to the S6000 we reviewed above, as well as the TAB 2 A10-70, although the second device belongs to a different line.





There are also few hardware differences between the A7600 and S6000. Among the most obvious - in the A7600 model, the front camera has a higher resolution - 2 MP. It can also be noted that the A7600 tablet costs a little more. Thus, Lenovo can supply devices with minimal differences in characteristics to the market, however, this adapts specific models to the needs of certain user groups. There are lovers of mobile devices for whom a high-resolution front camera is a secondary criterion for choosing a device, and they may prefer to save money by acquiring an analogue that is no worse in other indicators within the same Lenovo line.
