Hotel location

In the outdated brochures you can still see the old name of the same hotel complex: Leganya Beach Ex Mari Beach. To avoid possible confusion, we note that, despite a different name, this is the same hotel. Transfer from the airport to the hotel complex takes 35 minutes. On the way, you can see almost the entire Mahmutlar. After all, he, in fact, is buildings along the highway, as well as on two or three streets perpendicular to it.

However, for a tourist in Mahmutlar to buy fresh fruits, cheese, oriental sweets is not a problem. Four times a week there is a market here, where guests of Leganya Beach regularly buy products.

According to established practice, tourists are accommodated in rooms immediately upon arrival.

The hotel is located in a 6-story building. Leganya Beach Hotel 3 * - the hotel is small, only 30 rooms in it. It features a garden and an outdoor compact pool. Around it there is a sun terrace: sun loungers and sun umbrellas are installed. Vacationers in the pool area buy refreshing drinks in the hotel bar.

Rooms

All hotel rooms are two-room, spacious, with wonderful sea views. Each has a balcony with a table and two chairs.



The furniture in the rooms is not new, but in good condition: wardrobe, bedside tables, beds, table, chairs. The rooms of Leganya Beach Hotel 3 * have air conditioning, a TV, a minibar, a hairdryer and an electric kettle. The bathroom is comfortable, there is a shower and a toilet. Rooms are cleaned daily, and towels and bed linen are changed every other day. The rooms have Wi-Fi.

The hotel staff is friendly, guests' requests are fulfilled immediately.

Hotel infrastructure

A small hotel is designed for basic services to guests. His clients are active holidaymakers who organize their own holidays for themselves. Nevertheless, at the hotel you can buy a guided tour at the hotel; cards are served; there is a laundry service;

Excursion vacations are the hobby of most guests of Leganya Beach Hotel. Guest reviews are adequate to the specifics of this hotel complex: saving money, the majority acquires excursions at city tour agencies.

You can also rent a bicycle directly at the hotel to get to know Mahmutlar better. There are a couple of tennis tables.

In addition, one of 9 cars can be rented at the hotel reception.

Directly opposite the hotel is a motorway with a bus stop. The most requested route for hotel guests: Mahmutlar - Alanya.

Most of the guests of the three-star Leganya Beach Hotel (Turkey) use it as a platform for exploring the beauties of the resort of Alanya.

Nutrition

Catering for tourists provides the main restaurant of the hotel Leganya Beach Hotel 3 *. European and local dishes are prepared. And more - local. After all, the hotel has almost no tourists from the New World and Western Europe. Mostly Russians and Turks rest here. The hotel restaurant practices a half board type of food. For breakfast, the hotel has a separate room. You can ask the waiters to bring food to your room for an extra charge. This service is paid.

There is also a bar at the hotel complex.

According to tourists, the hotel restaurant is very moderate and portioned spreads meat and fish. But a lot of vegetable dishes, pastries. Very tasty local coffee.
