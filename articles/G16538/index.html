Hotels of Reutov. Description and photo

On the way to Moscow there is a small town Reutov. Almost always, it is filled with a large number of guests. Many of them are businessmen or researchers. They can come for a few days and even stay in the city for weeks. Tourists here practically do not stay, and if so, then for a maximum of half a day - for rest and lunch.

Through the city of Reutov passes the way to the capital of Russia, so there may be many tourists, but almost all of them are passing through here. A large cluster of hotels is located in the city center, which allows you to comfortably get to any desired point.

Reutov hotels offer comfortable accommodation for relaxation. The prices here are quite affordable. But besides the basic amenities, nothing more should be expected. Only Reutov hotels of the highest category can provide a wide range of services. Prices for accommodation in them, respectively, are an order of magnitude higher. Therefore, where you live and how to relax in the city - choose for yourself.





Despite the fact that Reutov is a suburb of Moscow, this does not prevent such a small city from hosting a large number of different tourists every day. Reutov hotels are most often filled with people who have come for business matters, so special attention is paid to creating comfortable conditions in the rooms so that a person away from home can feel warmth and comfort and return to their homeland only with good emotions. Travelers who come for a few hours are invited to relax in the suburban sectors or private homes.





Moscow is a very expensive city. In order to save money, guests of the capital can pay attention to the city of Reutov. After all, Moscow is not so far away, and here you can visit many interesting places. In the Moscow region you can find a lot of unique attractions, so everything is here for an interesting vacation. The city has a local history museum, a museum of local manufactory, cozy parks and squares. Even there is a wonderful clean pond, in the summer you can see many vacationers who like to sunbathe in the sun. On warm evenings, you can stroll around the city and watch the night scenery.

All Reutov hotels are located in advantageous places, you can get to the railway station of the capital in 30-35 minutes, there are several metro stations, which sometimes facilitates movement in busy Moscow. There may not be many places to accommodate, but there are conditions for a comfortable stay. Basically, hotels offer standard and superior rooms. Since the city is small and tourists come more for business, Reutov sets affordable prices for accommodation, which becomes available to any guest.

After settling in one of the local hotels, you can visit a lot of places necessary for tourists. The city has many wonderful beauty salons, the prices of which compare favorably with Moscow. For lovers of tasty food there are a sufficient number of restaurants and cafes with national and European cuisine. If you come to see Russia in all its colors, there is the opportunity to use the services of a tour desk. The hotel complexes can comfortably accommodate, some include breakfast.

Hotel NPO Engineering

The hotel is located in the suburbs of Moscow - Reutov. This allows the hotel administration not to set high prices, but at the same time give its guests the maximum level of service. On the territory there is a small cozy cafe where you can eat and drink a couple of light cocktails. By the standards of Moscow, all this will cost a mere penny. Near the hotel there is a railway station.

There are about 60 hotel rooms.

There is no parking space.

Service





In the rooms:

  • sleeping place;
  • cupboard;
  • minimum amount of furniture;
  • air conditioning;
  • a separate bathroom with the necessary accessories;
  • linens.

The hotel does not provide rooms for pets.

There is no Wi-Fi distribution, there is no Internet on the hotel territory.

There is no possibility to put an extra bed in the rooms, therefore it is not possible to accommodate children.

Additional services

Also offered:

  • Halls for ceremonial or business meetings.
  • Access to the administrator within 24 hours a day.
  • Help in purchasing tickets.
  • Driver services for a fee.
  • Wet cleaning every day.

Registration and settlement - from 13:00.

Eviction - until 12:00.

Reviews





The hotel from the machine-building scientific association is located near the railway station, from here you can safely get to Moscow and to the center of Reutov itself. If you take a calm step for 15-20 minutes, you can get to the Novokosino metro station. The rooms are equipped with minimal appliances, TV, air conditioning. There is a small store with the necessary products on site. For additional purchases, you can stroll to the mall, which takes approximately 10 minutes. Sheremetyevo International Airport - 40 minutes, to the center of the capital - a maximum of half an hour.

There are other noteworthy hotels in Reutov. Inexpensive you can stay at the next hotel.

Mini-Hotel Asteria





Hotel Asteria is located 20 km from the capital. The complex provides a fairly wide range of services. The on-site restaurant offers delicious dishes of national cuisine, a sauna and a hammam are warmly waiting for their guests. There is free access to the Internet. Private parking is available at an additional cost.

The rooms have private shower rooms with spa baths and bath sets. Superior apartments have a private sauna. Each room is equipped with new modern technology.

Access to the reception desk around the clock.

How to get there

If you travel by car, the hotel is very easy to reach. You need to leave on the Nosovikhinskoe highway, after 7 km on the left side there will be a beautiful three-story building with the name of the hotel.

All about the hotel:

  • Settlement - from 12:00 to 13:00.
  • Eviction - until 11:00.
  • Only 7 rooms, in the form of a guest house.
  • There is a private parking.
  • There is the possibility of settlement with animals.
  • Free internet access.

Mini-hotel "Asteria" deserves positive reviews. Not only guests of the city, but even local residents are eager to get here. Therefore, it is better to book rooms in advance.

Reutov hotels are an ideal option for a budget tourist who wants to see the surroundings of Moscow.
