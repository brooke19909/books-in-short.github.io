Christmas tree toy: we create decorations with our own hands

The tradition of decorating a Christmas tree on New Year's Eve has remained unchanged for many decades. Each person is seized with excitement and trembling feelings when he pulls out a box with garlands and toys from the bins. All of us adults immediately turn into children, admiring the beauty of Christmas-tree decorations and plunging into memories. How many of you, as a child, did not make garlands and toys with your own hands? Let's remember these moments together and make decorations for our New Year tree. There are many ideas for their implementation. Our article will tell you how to create a Christmas tree toy with your own hands. Here are examples of making Christmas decorations, which are made very quickly from available materials.



Textile toy for the Christmas tree. With our own hands we create beauty!

New Year's decorations made of fabric are relevant and fashionable today. They will be very useful in homes where there are small children. Products of this kind are absolutely safe and are a small soft toy.

Next, patterns of toys for the Christmas tree are performed on paper. The pattern is transferred to a double folded fabric, encircled and cut. The detail of the front of the toy is decorated to your liking. For example, if you make a Christmas tree, then you can decorate it with colored buttons and beads. Handmade Christmas balls

A ball is considered a traditional decoration for a Christmas tree. We offer you to make your own exclusive balls-cobwebs. They will become the original decoration of your home. How to make a Christmas ball toy from threads? Read and remember instructions.

Inflate the balloon to the size you need. Lubricate its surface with any cream or petroleum jelly. Dip any threads (cotton, floss, yarn) into a solution of PVA glue (50 g) and water (10 g) and wrap the ball with them in all directions. Suspend the product and leave to dry until morning. With a needle, pierce a balloon, pull it out. Decorate a toy from threads to your liking. It can be decor from bows, beads, artificial flowers. Perform a loop from the ribbon . The spider ball is ready! Such a product can be hung not only on the Christmas tree, but also decorate it with a chandelier, walls in the house.

Plastic balls (tennis, for a dry pool) can also be turned into Christmas tree decorations. Pick up beautiful pieces of square-shaped fabric. Put the balls in them, pick up all the corners up and tie a bright bow. Use a needle to pull the tape through the place where the tape is tied and tie a loop. Everything, the ball is ready. The brighter and more diverse the ornament on the fabric, the more beautiful the ball turns out. If your patches are plain, then you can decorate the product with shiny rhinestones and painted with acrylics.
