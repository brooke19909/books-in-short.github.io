"Arthurs", hotel on Dmitrovskoe highway: description, service, entertainment, reviews

Russian people are accustomed to a variety of country pensions and holiday homes. As a rule, they all have the usual style of design and nutrition. Not much different from each other and services in such institutions. But still in this whole routine you can find several unique and unusual places. For example, Arthurs is a hotel on Dmitrovskoye Shosse. It is here that the atmosphere of Europe reigns. The institution has an unusual design style, as well as top-class service. Getting to him is easy. The hotel is located just 20 km from Moscow.

general information





The administration of "Arthurs Village & SPA Hotel" offers its guests all the necessary conditions for a perfect holiday, caring for children and much more.
