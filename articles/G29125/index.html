The Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort 5 * (United Arab Emirates / Fujairah): hotel reviews

The Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort is a modern five - star hotel, designed in an interesting Moroccan style , located between the foothills of the Omani Mountains and the Indian Ocean. This hotel is quite popular and frequently visited. Tens of thousands of tourists from all over the world come here every year, and Russia is no exception.





Accommodation options

If a person plans to stay at The Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort, then he will have a lot of options. The hotel offers more than ten different types of apartments. It is worth listing them all. So, there are Al Dana suites - overlooking the pool, ocean or desert. Family apartments are also available in the hotel - for 2-4 people, Promo category. Connecting rooms (2 connected into one) for 4-6 guests are also offered. There are also Al Bawadi suites with pool or ocean views. Standard 2-bed apartments are also available. Standard family rooms are also available. You can choose apartments with pool, ocean or mountain views.

There are standard rooms with special offers, and Al Qasr, Al Rawda and Al Dou suites. All these apartments differ not only in name, but also in terms, area, prices. And the most important thing in any trip is to stay in a comfortable room. Well, it is worth paying more attention to this topic and telling about the conditions offered by the hotel, and about the opinion that people who have already visited here have developed about the hotel.

How to save?

The question is interesting and relevant for many. And it consists in one completely elementary feature. Guests at The Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort are advised to book rooms with mountain or desert views. Why? It's simple: because they are cheaper than those from where the ocean is visible, by 15-20 thousand rubles. Of course, this difference is unlikely to confuse people who went to the Emirates, but still it is worth bearing in mind. The beach here is literally a couple of tens of meters, because the ocean surface can be seen every day, at least several times.





Standard 2-bed rooms

This is the first category of the most popular at The Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort, which will be discussed. The cost of such apartments for 2-3 people for 14 full nights is from 200 to 270 thousand rubles. Why could there be such a difference? Because often the hotel arranges promotions and days of super-offers. And if you manage to get at this moment, you will be able to reserve apartments with a good discount. But if you want breakfast to be included in the price, you will have to overpay. Then the cost will be 280-350 thousand rubles.

And what about the conditions? The guests who have been here assure that they are really good and are quite consistent with the stated stardom. Firstly, the beds are not standard, but queen-size (i.e. incredibly huge). There is an opportunity to get a room with such a luxurious bed, only this must be indicated in the commentary on the reservation.

The room also has satellite TV, a flat-screen plasma TV, a work desk, an iron with ironing facilities, and even a radio with a safe. But that is not all. Air conditioning, a bathroom, shower, bathtub, minibar, as well as slippers with bathrobes and linen - all this is also provided to guests. Guests assure that the apartments are comfortable (although some are still confused by the declared price).

Nutrition

Now a few words about the level of food in this 5-star hotel. Well, the establishments of the hotel complex The Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort get decent reviews. And this is not surprising. The large, stylishly decorated restaurant welcomes guests with a pleasant atmosphere and a spacious terrace. The menu is varied, which definitely will appeal to lovers to please themselves with delights. The restaurant serves fresh and delicious dishes of national and European cuisine.

Also, guests are strongly recommended to visit another institution. Namely - an Italian restaurant, which is located by the pool. There really is excellent cuisine and fine wines.

And in the lobby of the hotel there is a cozy stylish bar. It is very pleasant to sit down in an easy chair in the evening and, having chosen alcoholic drinks and light snacks, relax in good company.





Leisure and entertainment

So, what else can guests who come here have a rest? In fact, at The Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort 5 * (Fujairah) hotel you can really have some good fun. In addition to the outdoor pool, garden and sun terrace, there is plenty of everything else. Tennis court, billiard room, darts, the opportunity to go diving, windsurfing, fishing and other other water sports. You can take a steam bath in the sauna, in the Turkish bath, visit the wellness center, relax with a massage or use the SPA services. For children, by the way, leisure is also organized. There are animation staff, a games room and a playground, as well as a kids club. So, surely you will not be bored either by adult guests or small ones.

Although few people are involved in all of the above, since most tourists come here to swim, sunbathe and get acquainted with local attractions, which are more than enough here.





Service

Finally, a few words about how in such an expensive hotel the staff treat their guests. Guests who have already stayed here say that the service is organized at a decent level. Food and drinks can be delivered to the room at the request of one (like fresh newspapers), if you need a transfer, it will be provided (though you will have to pay extra). The registration desk is open around the clock, and the complex has everything that can come in handy. This is a currency exchange, tour desk, luggage storage, ATM. There is also a laundry with dry cleaning. Guests can also iron clothes. There are even places on the territory such as a business center, a hall for banquets and conferences, a gift shop and a fax reception point. And guests can hire a nanny - right there at the hotel.

In general, if we draw conclusions, then we can confidently say that this hotel is from the category of good ones. Service, maintenance, food, cleanliness - everything is organized at a decent level for the Emirates. So, in any case, say guests who have booked rooms here. True, rest in this place is very expensive. This is a significant minus for those people who value the price.
