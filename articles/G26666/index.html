How to make a shirt out of a bill with or without a tie

The ancient art of folding various and very intricate figures of paper, which came from Japan and never ceases to amaze with its grace, inimitable subtlety and airiness, is becoming more and more popular every year. And this is not surprising. After all, origami offers schemes for combining the most interesting figures - both geometric and masterpieces from the animal and plant world. In principle, you can create anything from paper. For example, a small shirt made of a banknote is considered a lucky talisman that brings good luck. You can keep it for yourself or give it to friends. So how to make a shirt out of a bill? Very simple. All that is needed is material, patience and about half an hour of free time.





Where to begin?

Before you spoil the money, you should take a simple sheet of clean white paper and practice, strictly following all the points in the instructions. From the first time, of course, something sensible is unlikely to work out. However, a few attempts - and the talisman will be ready. A banknote shirt will not only demonstrate the exuberant imagination of its creator, but will also entertain others.

Which bill is better to take for a shirt?

Having decided to learn how to make a shirt out of a bill, you should immediately consider the money available. In principle, you can take anything, but the most even and beautiful copy will come out of the dollar. The fact is that he has the most suitable proportions for this. Shirts from any other notes will turn out to be of a different form.





Talisman Production Scheme: Beginning

So, a dollar is taken, or, say, a bill of ten or fifty rubles. On the left side you need to bend less than a third of the banknote. Now, without unbending anything, you should bend the paper in half along its long side, and then unfold the fold. Now the upper and lower long sides are bent to this center line. The next step is to deploy the two folds that have just been bent. The bill needs to be turned over and on its right side bend a thin strip no more than a centimeter wide. How to make a shirt out of a bill, having done everything already described? Turn it over and bend the long edges again to the center line. On the right side of the banknote, another thin strip is bent.





Cuffs and shutdown

The entire left side of the bill is now unbent, and thin stripes are bent around the edges, which will serve as the shirt cuffs. Now the long edges are bent inward again. On the right side, the corners bend forward so that their lines intersect with the central horizontal. Thus, the collar of the future shirt will turn out. And on the left side, sleeves are made: the internal valves must be turned out. At the end of the work, the left edge is bent so that it can be passed through the collar. Everything - a shirt from a bill, the scheme of which is quite simple, ready.





Shirt options

When figuring out how to make a shirt out of a bill, you should pay attention to the fact that you can add not only a talisman, but also additions to it. This, for example, pants from the same money, a skirt, paper flowers, small men. A unique craft will be a real work of art. By the way, you can fold a more complex shirt.





Complicated Option

If you want to create something more interesting, a shirt with a tie from a bill will be an excellent option. The material is taken, bent in half along the long side, then the fold is unbent. Now the upper corners should be folded to this fold, making a triangle, and bend it inward, to the center of the sheet. From the center of the top, the bills are drawn with a pencil and a ruler two lines to the edges of the resulting triangle. This is a blank for the future tie. The next step is to gently bend the bill along these pencil lines and straighten it again, forming quite noticeable folds. At the same time, the rest of the field is slightly bent to make the tie convex against the background of the shirt. Now the long edges of the future talisman are bent inward, to the central fold. At the same time, they need to be folded so that the edges are under the tie without covering it.

Second part of the work

All that needs to be done next, in general terms, coincides with the scheme for folding a traditional shirt without a tie. The lower part of the bill is bent by a centimeter, then you need to fold its side faces to the center. Then you need to bend the paper, folding the lower third inward. Now the inner edges are twisted from the center, the shirt is folded in half and sleeves are obtained. Everything, the talisman with a tie is ready. In principle, if you really want to, you can fold a shirt not from a banknote, but from plain paper, color it, even paste over it with sequins or rhinestones. It all depends on the flight of fancy. However, it is a money shirt, according to many, that bestows luck on its owner not only in money, but in all other matters. And it looks much more interesting than all other options.
