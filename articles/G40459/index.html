"Peterland" - water park and entertainment for the whole family

In the Primorsky district of St. Petersburg, on the shores of the Gulf of Finland, there is a unique shopping center that unites under its roof many opportunities for shopping, recreation and entertainment for the whole family. What does Peterland shopping mall offer its guests? Water park, restaurants, cafes and bars, shops, as well as fun events for the whole family, fashion shows and concerts.



Peterland (water park) is a unique project, the largest in Russia. The huge space inside is designed for up to 2000 people and offers its guests a wide selection of entertainment and enjoyable procedures. The dome of the water park, with a diameter of 45 meters, is made of glass and coated with a special composition that transmits ultraviolet light. Thanks to this, all visitors can sunbathe year-round!

For adults and children

So, how is Peterland different from others ? The water park is equipped with a large number of slides, the maximum height of which reaches 12 meters. For visitors are also available traditional pools, saunas, baths and beauty salons. For lovers of thrills and unusual entertainment in the water park has a special wave pool. Bathing in it will bring a lot of unforgettable emotions.



For athletes and those who do not mind learning something new there is also a reason to come to Peterland. The water park is equipped with a special room for wind surfing. It is here that you can indulge in your favorite sport at any time of the year and exercise, regardless of the weather.

Waterpark is a great option for spending leisure time with the whole family. And be sure to take your child to Peterland! The water park will delight the child with an abundance of entertainment! Here, a special island is equipped for children, on which everything is provided for young visitors. And parents don‚Äôt have to worry about their children!



In addition to outdoor activities in the water park provides everything for relaxation. That is why most beautiful ladies come to Peterland. The water park provides its visitors the opportunity to visit several types of baths and saunas - Turkish, Roman, Finnish, Russian. All this, as well as beauty salons with a full range of services for every taste, will help to relax and transform in a pleasant atmosphere.

Cost

It remains to find out an important question. How much does a trip to Peterland Waterpark cost? The cost of tickets for children varies from 550 to 700 rubles. You can purchase a ticket for this amount if your child is less than 12 years old. For adults, the cost of visiting is from 800 to 1800 rubles. The price depends on the time of day and increases on weekends and holidays. For all visitors there is the opportunity to purchase annual membership cards. The water park is open every day from 10 am to 11 pm. On Monday, the work schedule changes slightly - from 15 to 23.

How to get there

There are several convenient ways to visit Peterland. The water park, whose address is Primorsky Prospekt, house 72, launched special free buses that are able to deliver visitors as quickly as possible from the nearest metro stations - Staraya Derevnya and Chernaya Rechka. In the future, it is planned to organize a pier from the side of the bay, where meteors can be moored.
