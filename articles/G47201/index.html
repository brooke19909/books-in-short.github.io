Huawei Nova Smartphone: Owner Reviews

The mobile device market is not lacking in new models. Most manufacturers are constantly updating their product range. Customers are happy and even with some trepidation await the release of new products. In addition to well-known world-famous brands (Samsung, Apple), Chinese manufacturers have also become leaders. Previously, buyers were wary of such devices, preferring to overpay a certain amount, but buy quality products. Now everything has changed dramatically. Gadgets from the Middle Kingdom please not only with a beautiful design, hardware, good quality, but also low cost. It is worth noting that such devices fill the niches of not only the budget segment, but also the middle class. These are the ones that Huawei produces.

The hero of our review debuted in 2016. This is a smartphone Huawei Nova (CAN-L11). The expert reviews about this model are mixed. "Why?" - you ask. The device has some features that underestimate its assessment. Noticed the shortcomings and the owners. They share their impressions. Those who are seriously interested in this model, it is very useful to read the reviews of real people. At the start of sales, the price tag for the gadget reached $ 370. If you pay that kind of money, then you need to know why.

The review below is based on owner reviews and specifications announced by the manufacturer.



Distinctive feature

The phone is a compact device that may interest those customers who, for whatever reason, do not want to switch to bulky "shovels." It can be called "average", as the gadget is equipped with a typical "stuffing" for its price category. However, you can often find such reviews about the Huawei Nova 32Gb, which state that, against the background of other Chinese gadgets, its price seems somewhat overpriced. Did the manufacturer justify the difference in cost? This is what we have to deal with.

The first thing customers pay attention to is the specifications. A detailed description of them can be read below. Some features distinguish this model from devices of the budget segment, for example, Qualcomm chipset. But smartphones with a class below are based on their own Kirin platform.

What other features interested users? Many have focused on the Huawei Nova fingerprint scanner. In the reviews, the owners highlight its versatility. In addition to the main purpose, it can be used as a button. Settings are available to the user, thanks to which the scanner can be programmed to receive an incoming call or to activate the front camera. The phone is controlled by both standard movements (swipe) and gestures. With the help of the latter, the user will be able to open the curtain and view pictures. Gestures are recognized thanks to sensors installed on the smartphone case.

In the comments, users included design features as well. But we will talk about it in more detail.

Hardware equipment

What characteristics did the manufacturer indicate? Qualcomm Snapdragon 625 is responsible for the device‚Äôs performance. Undoubtedly, this processor is considered more powerful than the one used in budget models of this brand.

The chipset is based on eight cores. Their type is Cortex-A53. Now attention! The maximum clock frequency reaches 2000 MHz. For the middle price category this is a pretty good result. Advanced users claim that the developers were able to optimally combine energy efficiency and the level of productivity. The process is indicated by the process technology (14 nm).

So, what do all the above characteristics of the hardware platform testify to? The smartphone can easily cope with all modern applications. Huawei Nova Gold (CAN-L11): battery life reviews

The manufacturer equipped the gadget with a pretty good battery. Its capacity is 3020 milliamps per hour. Given the thickness of the smartphone, such a battery is the best solution. In their reviews, users say that with a low load (no more than 2-3 hours per day of activity), the battery life is approximately three days. When watching movies and other videos, the battery lasts for 8 hours. It is these characteristics that almost all mid-range smartphones have.

To restore the charge to 100% will have to spend about 2 hours. In the official firmware, energy-saving modes are not provided.

Cameras

About Huawei Nova cameras, the reviews are mostly positive. For the main manufacturer, I chose the matrix of the famous manufacturer Sony. It's about the IMX286 model. The lens is based on six wide-angle lenses. To improve the quality, the developers implemented phase autofocus. There is also a LED flash.

The shooting quality is adequate. It is quite justified by the cost of the device. However, users claim that, for example, for Samsung, Huawei gadgets are not competitors. If you activate the HDR mode when shooting outdoors, then the photos are detailed with good color reproduction. I liked the users and the camera in the room. However, there are still flaws, which is confirmed by the reviews. Huawei Nova is not the only smartphone whose optics are not complete. For example, the most common problem is a jump in sharpness, noticeable when the photo is enlarged. Pictures taken at night, although not very obtained, but if you compare, for example, with the Meizu M3, the difference is noticeable to the naked eye. Definitely, Huawei wins. The main camera is capable of recording video in 4K quality.

The front module also has a good matrix. Its resolution is 8 megapixels. According to users, this camera is the most balanced selfie solution. Surprisingly, when compared to the Samsung Galaxy A5, the winner was the Nova.



Communication opportunities

This phone model supports the work with two SIM cards of mobile operators. The special tray has two slots. They are oriented to the nano format. There is one important point that you should pay attention to. It's about working with memory cards in the Huawei Nova 32Gb LTE. Reviews on this subject can hardly be called positive. The fact is that to install it you will need to sacrifice one of the SIM cards, as the developers combined their slots.

The phone, as befits a modern device, works not only with networks of the second and third generations, but also with 4G. Theoretically, the maximum available speed is 300 Mbps. However, it is worthwhile to understand that the LTE network still does not catch all over Russia, so if you have connection problems, you must first check the coverage.

Gadgets that are sold at official locations work great with all frequencies. Nobody can guarantee such a result of phones bought directly from China.

Among the communication capabilities, it is worth highlighting the presence of an NFC transmitter, GLONASS, GPS, Bluetooth modules. The version of the latter is 4.1, although at present most manufacturers already install the most effective 4.2. There are comments on the Wi-Fi module. It only works in the 2.4 GHz frequency range.
