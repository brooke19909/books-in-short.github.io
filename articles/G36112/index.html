Ceramic-coated crock-pot: pros and cons

A multicooker with a ceramic bowl coating is now becoming an increasingly popular product among consumers. Manufacturers are actively promoting just such an option as the best alternative to traditional pots with non-stick Teflon coating . It is worthwhile to figure out what are the advantages and disadvantages of this option before you run and buy it.



On sale now there are several types of bowls for multicookers: with Teflon non-stick coating; with ceramic coating; completely made of stainless medical steel. Most often it is the first option. There is ongoing debate among experts over the durability and safety of such coverage. Studies conducted at the Institute for Risk Assessment in Germany showed that it poses no threat. If we talk about durability, then it is important that the manufacturer respects all the subtleties of the process. A crock-pot with a ceramic coating of the bowl guarantees higher non-stick properties at the beginning of use, but this advantage will remain in normal use for no more than 3-4 months. In the case of high-quality Teflon spraying, these properties will remain unchanged for two years.



A crock-pot with a ceramic coating of the bowl requires careful handling, as this material is quite fragile, as a result of improper use, cracks and chips can quickly appear on it. Teflon is not so afraid of such influences. Stainless steel bowls also have certain disadvantages. The composition of the material includes nickel, which some people may have an allergy to. In addition, such a material conducts heat worse, and with one-sided heating it does not have the property of uniformly warming up. In such a bowl it is impossible to cook acidic dishes, as this material is very afraid of the effects of acids.



A crock-pot with a ceramic bowl coating has its own advantages and disadvantages, as well as a device with a different bowl coating. Do not believe the catchy advertising statements that urge you to buy just such a product. A Teflon bowl can last a very long time, properly preparing all your favorite dishes, and when it fails, then you can think about buying a new one. However, if you decide to choose a crock-pot with a ceramic bowl, then finding the right model will not be difficult. Various manufacturers offer a lot of options with both ceramic and teflon coating. Redmond's products have proven themselves very well, having received a lot of positive feedback from users.

The Redmond crock-pot with a ceramic bowl of the RMC 4502 model perfectly shows itself in operation. This product is intended for continuous use. In this model, you can see the optimal ratio of price, functionality and quality.
