Hotels

Sharm el Sheikh is a beautiful, very picturesque place. There is everything that, according to tourists, a resort of this level should have. Sharm el Sheikh hotels, and there are a lot of them, offer first-class service and have a very developed infrastructure. In a word, there is everything for entertainment and relaxation. general information

The hotel is located on the first line from the sea in Sharks Bay. This is the north of Sharm El Sheikh - the heart of the Sinai Peninsula. There are beautiful coral reefs and clear water. The architecture of the hotel traces the "style of Arabian tales." Its buildings are completely surrounded by greenery. The territory of the Savita Resort Spa smoothly descends to the golden sandy beach of White Knight Beach.

The history of the foundation of the hotel dates back to 2006. Then this hotel complex was called differently. The new Savita Resort 5 * was inaugurated in 2010, and since then this updated hotel has been hosting both families and youth companies.

The international airport is only ten kilometers away. This is especially appreciated by those tourists who come to the hotel with children, who often do not tolerate the road.

Territory

The area on which the buildings of the hotel complex are located is quite large. We must pay tribute to those architects who created the Savita Resort 5 * project. With its impressive dimensions it is equipped very compactly. The whole territory is planted with exotic flowers, palm trees. All free space is a lawn grass pleasing to the eye. Numerous paths lead to six buildings - eight-story and five two-story buildings. And nearby are three two-story villas.





The territory is fully equipped. It is kept clean. There is where to walk or sit in the shade of palm trees, admiring the surrounding Sinai mountains and the emerald sea. Signs are posted everywhere for the convenience of vacationers.

Infrastructure

Hotel Savita Resort has a convenient parking lot, which you do not need to book in advance. On its territory there is a first-aid post where they can provide emergency assistance, souvenir stalls, where people can buy something for memory. There are also dry cleaning and laundry facilities. The hotel infrastructure also includes two conference rooms with a total capacity of about four hundred people.





At the round-the-clock registration desk, you can arrange a transfer to the nearest airport terminal, use a fax or photocopy machine, call a taxi, rent a car to travel around the peninsula on your own.

Number of rooms

In Savita Resort Spa 5 * three hundred and sixty five rooms with all amenities. Most of them overlook the sea, the rest into the garden.

Room categories are as follows: Standard Rooms are forty-two square meters in size for two adults and one child at a time, Connection and Family Rooms. Royal Suite apartments, located in separate villas, have two floors: on the first floor there is a living room and kitchenette, a toilet and a terrace, on the second floor there is a bedroom with a balcony, a combined bathroom with a jacuzzi. Their area is one hundred twenty square meters.

The rooms, decorated in pastel colors, have a TV with satellite output and three Russian channels, a paid minibar, a refrigerator, filled on request and payable for each order. For comfort, each room has a controlled air conditioning. For storing money, documents or jewelry there is a free safe, which is very rare for five-star hotels.

The rooms at Savita Resort Spa are tiled. Each room has a balcony or a furnished terrace. Superior rooms have a computer, which is especially convenient for those who work or play on the Web. Internet is available on request at an additional cost. Daily cleaning of the premises is carried out unobtrusively, in the morning, when vacationers leave for breakfast.



On the ground floor of one of the buildings there are rooms designed for people living with problems with independent movement. The hotel is positioned as smoke-free. Check-in with small pets must be arranged with the property in advance.

Beach

For those who come to rest in Egypt, Savita Resort Spa 5 * makes an indelible impression of the beauty of its beach and its surrounding nature. Clear water and bulky coral reefs leave their mark on tourists. Many find Sharks Bay the most beautiful on the coast.

Own sandy beach, which is three minutes walk, has a length of five hundred and fifty meters. It has a pontoon with steps to the sea and a pier. Towels, sun loungers and mattresses vacationers receive for free. Since there are many corals in the Red Sea, entering the water is somewhat difficult. Special shoes are a must.

For lovers of water entertainment, there are sailing ships by the sea. Here you can ride with a breeze on water skiing and canoeing. Beach volleyball is very popular among vacationers. Those who come to the Red Sea to go scuba diving can sign up for the appropriate courses, where licensed instructors teach. For more advanced divers, there is a diving center on the beach. For many, the prices for vacations in this Egyptian resort seem low, which is the biggest plus.

In matters of service and cleaning in the rooms, opinions of vacationers differ.
