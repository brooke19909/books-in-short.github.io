Hotel "Tourist", Chita: description, cost of living, reviews

The Trans-Baikal Territory is a territory of pristine nature, attracting travelers tired of the bustle of the city. Various natural zones are represented here: plain steppes and spurs of the Apple-tree ridge, taiga and birch forests. From here, the Shilka and Argun rivers begin, forming the Amur at the confluence. A few hours away is Lake Baikal. But all journeys begin with the capital of the region - the city of Chita. Hotel "Tourist" offers city guests a variety of accommodation options: from ultra economy to rooms of the highest category.

Description

The hotel is located in the west of the city, in the historical district, surrounded by the main attractions of Chita. The five-story building was built during the Soviet era, in 1985, and is made in the spirit of minimalism. The owners tried to compensate for the lack of architectural zest by the abundance of greenery, modern renovation of the interior and goodwill towards customers.

Given the popularity of the hotel, it is recommended to book a place in advance using the telephone numbers indicated on the official website and partner sites. Hotel "Tourist" in Chita not only offers accommodation, but also organizes leisure activities.

How to get there

The building is located on one of the main arteries of the city, at the intersection of Babushkina and January 9th streets. For this reason, getting here by taxi or by personal transport will not be a problem. Hotel address "Tourist": Chita, st. Grandmother, 42A.

The Chita-2 railway station and the nearby bus station are only one and a half kilometers away, which allows you to walk the entire route in the absence of heavy luggage. You should move northeast along the street Polina Osipenko. Having left on Babushkina street, you should cross the road and turn right, after 140 m there will be an entrance to the Tourist Hotel (Chita).

You can also take minibuses number 24 to the stop "Children's Clinic" and number 49 to the stop "Art Museum". However, in this case, you still have to walk about 500-700 m on foot.

Shuttle taxis No. 7, 12, 25, 27, 45 are taken from the Chita airport to the Tourist Hotel. You should leave at the stop. "Skorokhod". Then you can either drive up by other means of transport or walk for 15-20 minutes: first along the street. Zhuravlev to the north-east to the intersection with the street. Babushkina (500 m), then turn right and go in a straight line (1000 m).

Number of rooms

Hotel "Tourist" in Chita offers a variety of accommodation options. The cheapest overnight stay will cost 400 rubles. For this price, the client gets the opportunity to sleep on a bed in a common room. Shower and toilet are shared. In addition to the bed, the administration offers separate 1-3-bed rooms of the 2nd, 1st and highest categories. The average daily cost of a double room (for two) is:

  • 1500 rub. for the number of the 3rd category;
  • 1800 rub. (2nd);
  • 1900 rub. (1st).

Of course, this is not a five-star hotel, you should not expect marble heated floors and crystal chandeliers. Meanwhile, even in inexpensive rooms, neat repairs were made, the furniture is new and quite comfortable. You can eat in the cafe at the hotel.

For lovers of comfort, it is recommended to book a two-room suite. The seating area has a seating area with an LCD TV, and the bedroom has comfortable beds and a dressing table with a mirror. The cost of living for two will be 4200 rubles.

Reviews

Tourist Hotel in Chita is a typical example of provincial hotels inherited from the Soviet period. Although the architecture of the building cannot be changed, the staff tries to fill in the gaps with a respectful attitude and service.

Pleasant impressions begin with a spacious, bright lobby, where at the reception skilled workers will help you choose a room and talk about the services included in the price. Also, right at the hotel you can order a vacation on Lake Arey - the pearl of Transbaikalia. Among the shortcomings, users note a periodic shutdown of hot water. Someone seems rustic design rooms.
