Ideal Prime Beach Hotel

The elegant and beautiful hotel Ideal Prime Beach Hotel 5 * of an elite level is located in one of the busiest resort cities - Marmaris, on the very shore of the dazzling warm sea. The resort center is only 3 kilometers from it, and the international airport (Dalaman) is an hour away by car. Near the hotel complex there are a large number of local restaurants, cafeterias, shopping centers and shops, and the "old market" can be reached in fifteen minutes. There, travelers can purchase local products, sophisticated jewelry, fabrics and leather wardrobe items. Also nearby are many places for entertainment and nightlife, where young and active companies can spend their holidays perfectly. The hotel first opened its doors to tourists in 1965, but several years ago a complete renovation was carried out, as a result of which guests of the resort are greeted with new, modern apartments with high-quality infrastructure and fresh appearance.





On the well-groomed landscaped grounds of Ideal Prime Beach Hotel 5 *, which is 20,000 square meters, there are four snow-white six-story buildings, decorated in a classic moderate style. Additionally, guests of the resort are accommodated in four cozy bungalows. The comfortable hotel complex is surrounded by a beautiful green garden where you can enjoy the beauty of exotic nature and the local landscape from charming gazebos. This luxury hotel is perfect for young people and couples, especially for those who spend their honeymoon or celebrate their wedding anniversaries. However, single men without an accompanying representative of the fair sex, the resort administration does not accept.

Reviews

An interesting vacation in an exotic country with incredible climatic conditions and magnificent natural landscape can be spent at Ideal Prime Beach. Reviews about the resort are mostly positive, many are attracted by its high level of service and reputation. But there are also negative opinions.

Among the positive aspects, tourists note the excellent location, proximity to the center and city infrastructure, beautiful landscapes of local nature, large and luxurious territory, good and varied food, many restaurants and bars, modern apartments equipped with everything necessary for relaxation.
