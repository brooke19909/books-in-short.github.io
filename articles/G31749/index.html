Greenwood Business Park: how to get, modes of transport

To get to an unfamiliar place is an overwhelming task, especially if this is the first place of work, and it is important to be in time there too. This article will discuss how to get to the Greenwood Business Park in Moscow by various means of transport, including both personal and public.

By car

It is not difficult for vehicle owners to get to the business park due to its convenient location near the Moscow Ring Road.

Entrance to it can be carried out immediately from two sides.

The first option - from 72 kilometers of the Moscow Ring Road. But in this case, the entry will be limited by the checkpoint, since the arrival takes place only by passes.

Another way to get to the Greenwood Business Park is to drive through the second checkpoint, which is opposite the O'Key grocery store. Entrance from the Putilkovskoye Highway, the second entrance to the shopping center, is also possible there. In this case, it is necessary to turn from the highway towards the Moscow Ring Road and then turn in the opposite direction.

Parking is located on the territory of the business park in front of buildings 1-31 and between the checkpoint "North" and "South". It is provided free of charge.

By public transport

Guests of the capital who are considering how to get to Greenwood Business Park by public transport should immediately take care to get to the Skhodnenskaya metro station in Moscow. From there, a free shuttle bus runs to the business park.

Its stop is opposite the Kaleidoscope shopping center. The bus leaves every 10 minutes. The exception is some flights: they follow from the Pension Fund stop without checking into the Microdistrict 2B point. On weekends, the bus leaves with the final every 30 minutes. From 16 o'clock - rides non-stop. On weekdays, passengers of the free bus can board at any public transport stop that will be along the route of the bus.
