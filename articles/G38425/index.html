  2. How many windows will there be in a room? Draw the appropriate number of rectangles and cut out the holes. As glass, you can use transparent tape. And glue the tulle pieces from the old curtain, too, with adhesive tape inside - here you have the curtains! Also, if desired, make curtains. Only at first, if the daughter has a desire, paste the box on the inside with wallpaper. So the house will look much more beautiful and more comfortable!

In a word, when dealing with a chest, experiment how to make furniture for Barbie with your own hands, how to decorate it better to get stylish and elegant.
