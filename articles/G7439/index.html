Peda Hotels Gumbet Holiday Beach: general description

This hotel was built at the end of the 20th century, namely in 1998. Initially, it bore a different name - Litera Gumbet Beach Resort. After the entire hotel complex was reconstructed in 2011, he switched to the Peda Hotels chain of hotels and began to be called as it is now. Its territory is quite large, is 12,000 square meters. The hotel complex consists of 29 three-story buildings surrounded by greenery of the park and garden. It is located on the first coastline and has its own beach, equipped with all necessary equipment. The hotel offers beautiful views of Gumbet Bay. For diving enthusiasts, paid diving lessons are arranged.



Number of rooms

As already noted, the hotel consists of almost three dozen small three-story buildings. The total number of rooms in the complex is 204. There is no wide variety of room categories in the hotel. There are only double living rooms with one double or two single beds or triple rooms with one double and one single (for children) or three single beds. The cost of living in this hotel is from 2000 rubles per day.



Description of rooms

Most of the rooms have balconies or terraces overlooking the sea or the park area with lush flowering vegetation. All rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning, satellite TV and Internet. There is a safe and mini fridge (for a fee), heating system. For those who want to make tea or coffee with their own hands, there are kits designed for this. Of course, the rooms have a private bathroom with shower, toiletries, towels and a hairdryer.
