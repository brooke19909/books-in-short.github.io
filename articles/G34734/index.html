Cross stitch kits “Riolis”: features, advantages, new items

In Russia, there are not many manufacturers of cross-stitch kits popular around the world. "Riolis" is one of the domestic companies that creates products for creativity, widely known abroad. The company has existed on the market for more than 20 years and was one of the first to produce ready-made sets of threads of floss, canvas and color scheme.

Assortment of the company "Riolis"

The company creates its own original designs in its own art department. The assortment includes kits from the “Create Itself” series with Italian-made Safil woolen threads, a premium collection with Anchor floss, pillows, bags, cases and postcards for embroidery. For needlewomen who prefer other techniques, delirium offers sets for working with beads, ribbons, articles from soutache, zippers, felt and related products.



Advantages and disadvantages of cross stitch kits “Riolis”

Many Russian needlewomen first felt the charm of this kind of hobby with the Riolis cross-stitch kit. In the series "Create it yourself" presents some of the highest quality sets with woolen threads.

Brand products have several important advantages:

  1. Bright and interesting design. Finished works look very beautiful in the interior, perfectly fit as a gift.
  2. Low cost. Even large kits are affordable.
  3. Samples of floss in sets for the convenience of craftswomen. Some kits have separate tablets with a small number of threads, which makes it easier to find the right color.
  4. A variety of topics. Designs are issued for beginners and experienced needlewomen with a high level of skill.
  5. Availability. Cross stitch kits "Riolis" is easy to find on the store counter for creativity, sold everywhere.
  6. Customer care. The thread in the sets is always with a large margin: enough, even if the embroiderer made mistakes during the work.




The disadvantages of the brand's products include not too convenient for unwinding bundles of woolen threads and tablets for them. Before work, it is advisable to distribute all the colors on special bobbins for storing floss or use an organizer. Some needlewomen note the friability and softness of linen canvas in the new “Create it yourself” sets. The difficulty is caused by the scheme, especially after working with other cross-stitch kits. At "Riolis" the shade of threads and its color designation do not match.

Unusual stories in the Riolis sets

The company is able to surprise its fans with special plots for cross-stitch. The 2014 Riolis novelties were a series of sets with sports themes: Biathlon, Curling, Pair Skating, Hockey. They are very easy to perform and are suitable even for beginner needlewomen. The “snowboarder” in this series is designed for experienced embroiderers.

In 2016, Riolis celebrated its twentieth anniversary. Many paintings based on the works of famous artists and plots devoted to the traditions and scenic spots of Russia appeared in the catalog: sights of Moscow, St. Petersburg and Crimea, Zhostovo painting, Russian hunting, ballet.



Each set, regardless of size and complexity of execution, is designed to the smallest detail. Designers bring to perfection even the most ordinary floral plot, masterfully selecting transitions and midtones so that the finished work looks voluminous and “lively”. The patterns are so simple that the needlewoman can only enjoy the embroidery process, abandoning everyday worries.

New in the catalog of 2017

In the catalog of 2017, among the cross-stitch from Riolis, the novelty was a series of miniature paintings with the inhabitants of the rural farmstead “Khutorok” and several plots with pirate themes. Designers have not forgotten about lovers of plant motifs: bright bouquets, unusual wreaths attract attention and are able to decorate the interior in any style. Craftswomen who prefer pastel colors and smooth transitions of color can also find a set for themselves. The list of new products is constantly expanding. The manufacturer follows the trends in the world of needlework and constantly supplements its sets with new, previously unused techniques: embroidered on canvas with a printed pattern and a plastic canvas, embroidered stitch, ribbons.



Tips for beginner embroiderers

A few recommendations for last:

  1. Before starting work, you must always wash your hands with soap, so as not to stain the canvas and drawing.
  2. Glue the folds and corners of the circuit with tape. This will help prevent her premature damage.
  3. If you want to reuse the circuit from the set, it is better to make a copy and work with it. Thanks to the appropriate color symbols, the lack of color will not interfere with operation. The difference between the shades of thread and its transfer on paper can only confuse an inexperienced needlewoman.
  4. When working on the hoop, try not to pull the embroidery so as not to damage it.

Cross stitch kits “Riolis” will suit both beginners and experienced embroiderers. The company catalog will be an excellent source of inspiration and will help you make a good choice.
