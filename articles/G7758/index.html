Vacuum cleaner Zelmer ZVC752STRU: reviews and specifications

Design and appearance

Let's start with the most obvious. The design of the Zelmer ZVC752STRU (whose review we started) is classic, without any frills. There is no giant bulging reservoir for water. The case has a solid structure and discreet colors. Most released models in white and blue colors. And this is not bad, as these colors fit perfectly into any interior. On the top of the vacuum cleaner is a control panel consisting of several buttons. It is made in the shape of a rectangle of dark color. Also on the panel there are LEDs that notify the user about a particular mode of operation of the vacuum cleaner.

Also on the top of the unit is a button to block the water supply and a convenient handle for carrying the device. The water tank itself is located in the rear of the Zelmer ZVC752STRU vacuum cleaner (reviews of which we will discuss a bit later). In order to get to the tank, you just need to raise the upper part. Also, the unit is equipped with rather large wheels, which allow it to easily move on any surface. Complete with a vacuum cleaner there are several nozzles, a telescopic tube and a shiny hose for connecting to the vacuum cleaner itself. In general, this unit looks decent. Suction power. Nuances

A key feature of the Zelmer ZVC752STRU is its suction power. She is really great here. The power of the engine itself is 1600 watts.
