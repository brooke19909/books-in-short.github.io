Sewing materials

  1. Patterns and various drawings.
  2. Selected fabric.
  3. Threads in tone of material and a needle.

Sewing a dress with your own hands is necessary taking into account the high-quality processing of all seams. The appearance and accuracy of the result of the work will depend on this. Untidy seams will immediately reveal that the dress was not only made by one‚Äôs own hands, but also absolute lack of effort. Therefore, this stage of work must be treated with great accuracy. The sewing process always starts with taking measurements. The girth of the chest and belt, the distance from the lower edge of the dress to the knee and from the knee to the clavicle. The dress will consist of three parts: the base, the belt and, of course, the ties. For the main part, a rectangle of fabric is cut out according to the pattern. Then it is folded in half and an incision is made along the fold to place the belt there. For him, you will need to take a strip of fabric, which is equal to the circumference of the chest. The ties should be made of the main material with a width of 1.5 cm and a length of 3 m. Next, yourself try on the result in front of the mirror and adjust how best to drape the folds.
