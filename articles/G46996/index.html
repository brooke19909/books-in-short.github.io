General characteristics of the project

The unique project "Business Angels" is a special program designed for aspiring entrepreneurs. Its author is Matrosov Yuri Vladimirovich (pictured below). According to him, the goal of the project was to create a virtual platform on which ideas for startups could be found with the further investment of free cash in them. In other words, the author has created a large information and search portal that helps novice businessmen find investors.

This is a kind of catalog of all kinds of financiers and entrepreneurs who are ready to provide financial support to beginners. The company presenting the project itself is a non-profit organization working as an intermediary between business experts, start-up entrepreneurs and potential investors.
