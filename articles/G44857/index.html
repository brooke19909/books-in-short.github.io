Alchemical circle as a source of natural energy

Alchemy was one of the most popular occupations in the Middle Ages. Even today, lovers of everything unusual, mysterious and magical are interested in this science, considering it not so much entertainment and the search for something non-existent, but as a very real way to penetrate the secret of the universe. The most important concept in it is the so-called alchemical circle of transformation.





And today, as in the Middle Ages, alchemy studies remain quite closed to the general public. If earlier this was explained by the general ignorance of the vast majority of ordinary people, now many do not confess to their studies, fearing ridicule from others. It is known for certain that the main object of alchemy is the process of transformation. In this case, both completely material objects and intangible substances can turn. The main role here is played by the alchemical circle.

This concept is an important symbol, which has long and firmly become one of the attributes of fans of this science. At its core, the alchemical circle is a system of special signs arranged in a certain way, possessing, according to the followers, magical power and properties. A huge role here is played by the graphic design of various figures.





It should be borne in mind that the alchemical circle does not have to fully comply with all the requirements of this geometric figure. Most importantly, to maintain the general appearance of the circle, all the required proportions are clearly visible. That is why quite often there is a circle consisting, as it were, of several sectors in the form of triangles, an octahedron or a pentacle. A great role here is played by the mastery of the creator, as well as his belonging to a particular direction.

Novice researchers most often use the alchemical circle, in which the main role is played by the symbols of the four main elements - air, fire, water and earth. Apply it mainly on a hard surface, pursuing any cognitive purpose. So, for example, drawing a circle on the door in which the symbols of water play a predominant role, a person hopes that all the basic secrets of the world will be revealed to him, that the path to them will be thorny but lucky.

A special role is played by circles in which symbols of all elements are used. With the help of them you can cause global cataclysms or return to life a person who has already gone to another world.

How many secrets does alchemy hold in itself ?! A person, applying an image of a magic circle to one or another surface, thus calls for help all powerful natural forces, mobilizing the surrounding space. Combining his efforts with the energy of nature, he can ultimately achieve very significant results.
