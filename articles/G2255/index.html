Credit Card Bank Post: types, conditions, design and requirements

Post Bank was founded recently, in January 2016. The new banking structure makes maximum use of the opportunities provided by its shareholders - Russian Post and VTB Bank. In this article, we will consider how to get a Post Bank credit card.



Bank Details

Despite the fact that the bank was created recently, it is gaining more and more popularity among residents of the country. There are branches of the Russian Post even in the most remote corners of our state, which means that the card can be accessible to everyone. The bank specializes in issuing both credit and debit cards, as well as deposits. Naturally, there are many banking institutions that can offer similar services, but they cannot reach such a prevalence and ubiquity.

So, what do you need to get a Post Bank credit card?

How to order a credit card?

On the Internet you can find a lot of positive reviews that this card can be obtained very quickly. You can also order it, without committing unnecessary actions, it is enough to take the following steps:

  1. You must register on the site and leave an online application for a credit card at the Post Bank.
  2. After making the card, you will be notified of its readiness. You can pick it up yourself in any department.

Replenishment

Credit account replenishment is possible in several ways:

  • Money transfer to the account.
  • Auto payment with debit card. Occurs with a commission of 1.9%, at least 29 rubles.
  • Depositing funds through the VTB 24 ATM.




Full or partial repayment of debt is a necessary condition for fulfilling credit obligations. The regular and complete fulfillment of all conditions creates a reputation, creates the image of a bona fide borrower and causes a tolerant attitude towards him in the future.

Deferment of payment

Using a credit card Bank Post also involves changing the date of repayment of the debt. Deferral can be obtained from the 4th to the 28th. This service does not provide for late payment interest. Closing a credit card implies a personal appeal to a branch of a banking institution or to a sales desk in the branches of Russian Post. It is impossible to close the card without a personal presence.

Features of use

So, it is not difficult to get a Post Bank credit card.

Having received the money, the client must be ready to fulfill certain conditions of use and repayment of the money received:

1. Debt payments must be paid monthly. Not only the borrower can pay the loan, but also a third party.



2. All amounts are monthly written in the schedule, which is prepared and issued to the client along with a package of documents for a credit card upon first contact with the bank.

3. You can deposit amounts of any size, but at least 5% of the amount owed at the time of payment.

4. Card account can be replenished with your own money. This can be done by transfer or cash payment after the full repayment of debt to the bank.
