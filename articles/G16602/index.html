Hotels in Obninsk: hotel overview

Obninsk is a quiet and small city. On a minibus, it costs nothing to go round it all in 20-30 minutes, and it will open to the guest in full view. For a long time there were three or four hotels in it, and mostly departmental. Not so now.

General overview of places where a city guest can stay

Always ready to receive visitors to the hotel Obninsk. Phones to contact them, we also provide:

  • Hotel Triumph Hotel: +7 (910) 911-33-46.
  • TsIPK Hotel: +7 (484) 392-91-20.
  • Hotel Versailles: +7 (484) 392-32-23.

  • When FNIFHI: +7 (484) 396-43-44.
  • Hotel owned by the IPPE: +7 (484) 399-56-22.

What is not included in the general list

Not in the list of those institutions that put the address of Obninsk, but are actually located on the Kiev highway. From there, without personal transport, it is quite troublesome to get to the city. By the way, those who arrive by car prefer to find hotels in Obninsk closer to the required place.

Hotel Description

Currently, hotels in Obninsk are competing among themselves in the quality of services provided and comfort. This is the trend of the time.

The Yubileinaya Hotel considers its location three minutes from the station to be one of its advantages, and this is true, as well as rooms at various prices and categories.

In addition, there is a gym, in the summer there are bicycles for rent, a sauna, a beauty salon, a restaurant and a pizzeria, a business center where you can rent a PC or laptop. Special offers include discounts for groups of more than 10 people, tariff reduction on weekends by 20%. The rooms in it are single, double and superior. They have TV, fridge, in the suites - air conditioning.

Downtown

Hotels in Obninsk, which are located in busy places with many shops and entertainment centers, are the Triumph Hotel and Like Hostel. A little further away is Versailles.

Triumph Hotel is one of the most modern establishments in which the staff speaks English. It is located on the upper floors of the large shopping and entertainment complex Triumph Plaza. Soundproofing guarantees silence, relaxation and a peaceful sleep after a day.

You can visit the numerous shops and boutiques popular with the residents of the city, a beauty salon, an excellent coffee shop with excellent coffee, a restaurant on the 6th floor with excellent cuisine and panoramic views of the city. The main body meets the requirements of 4 stars. The first building is an inexpensive hotel with a 3-star level. It has all classic rooms, including a suite.

Likehostels is also centrally located and designed for those who need to spend inexpensively just a night in the city. It necessarily has a shower and towels are provided, in addition, there are household appliances and a kitchen where they will feed the guest or he can cook his own meals if he wishes. The rooms are large. Double beds. There is also a family room with a double bed and a sofa.

Versailles is a modern enterprise that offers free Wi-Fi, round-the-clock room service, free breakfast, taxi and shuttle transfers, paid secure parking, as well as alarm clock, laundry and dry cleaning services, fax and document sending, and scanning.

The staff is fluent in English. The rooms are superbly equipped, there is a plasma screen, suitable for one, as well as for two or three people. The suite also has a safe.

Quiet green places

Obninsk hotels and hotels is away from the noise in its green part. First of all, one should mention the Greenway Park Hotel. It will provide the most worthy welcome to any dignitaries who arrive in the science city. This hotel has its own face: unconventional romance and comfort. Greenway offers three types of rooms.

Through the use of natural silk, satin, wood, they are easy to work and relax. Breakfast is a tradition from the opening. The restaurant surprises with Mediterranean and Russian cuisine. Its interior is a work of authorship.

In a private three-story house in the village of Mirny, a hotel with 25 rooms is located. Rooms of different categories are cozy, elegant, sophisticated.

What is located next to the Research Institute of Medradiology RAS

There are only two hotels in Obninsk near the radiological center: the already mentioned Versailles, which is more suitable for business people, and the TsIPK hotel. We will consider it in more detail. Previously, it was intended for people coming from all over the country to improve their skills. Nowadays, old ties have been largely lost, and businessmen do not send employees to Obninsk en masse. Hotels near the radiological center are readily filled by people who have come to their sick relatives or close friends.

It is a 12-20 minute walk from the station and five minutes from the Institute of Medical Radiology. The rooms in it are of various quality classes: from economy to luxury. Even in the simplest room there is Wi-Fi, a refrigerator, a TV, a shower, a toilet, personal hygiene products and a set of towels. There is a guarded parking lot for the car.

This is a brief description of the hotels in Obninsk, which have a different level of service.
