How to leave Russia by regular bus to Germany

It is known that buses, despite the high cost of fuel, are the cheapest means of transportation over long distances. Of course, getting to your destination faster by plane. But firstly, it is expensive, and secondly, there are people who are panicky afraid to travel through the air. And land transport has the advantage that you can see different countries in one trip. This is what travel agencies lure their customers by offering bus tours. But such trips do not quite please independent travelers. After all, agencies want to sell the entire "package", which includes the services of a guide, hotels, tours. And if you just need to get from Russia to some city in Germany? And cheaper and without long stops? For this, there are direct buses to Germany. We will discuss them in this article.

Ecolines

Many companies send their cars in the direction we are interested in. And in many ways, the price of a bus depends on the appetite of the carriers. You should compare the fare, choose convenient dates, routes and destinations. The first in our review we will consider the company Ecolines. She sends her buses to Germany from the Tushinskaya station (Moscow, 9 Stratonavtov passage). The Ecolines cars go to Berlin, to Hamburg, Hanover, Munich, Dusseldorf, Frankfurt. Moreover, buses depart not only from Moscow, but also from St. Petersburg (to the capital of Germany) and Kaliningrad (to Kiel, Munster and Hamburg). What are the conditions on board the buses? Dry closet, folding backs of comfortable seats, multimedia screens for each passenger, personal outlets, free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, hot drinks. Such a bus takes 36 hours to get to Berlin from Moscow. What are the fares? For example, on the Moscow-Berlin route, the cost of a ticket is six thousand rubles. The timetable is designed so that passengers spend two nights and one day in the car.

Basfor

This reputable international company sends most of its buses to Germany from Ukraine. But there are flights that connect Germany with Russia. True, Busfor's prices are slightly higher than those of the Ecolines. For comparison: a ticket on the route Moscow - Berlin will cost an adult passenger 7200 rubles. From Kaliningrad to the capital of Germany you can go for 4825 rubles. Berlin is not the only point on the map of Germany that can be reached with the help of the Basfor company. Reviews note the extremely wide geography of flights, as well as their frequency. On board the bus, passengers can count on comfortable soft chairs, air conditioning, and a TV. Tickets can be bought online, and it is also easy to refuse a trip and return the full price.

Deutsche Touring

The German company is known for its wide network of routes, which covers not only Germany, but also all of Europe, as well as the CIS countries. About 40 flights were opened in Russia alone. Buses to Germany from Deutsche Touring will deliver you to Berlin, Aachen, Bielefeld, Dresden, Frankfurt, Essen, Hanover, Cologne, Karlsruhe, Magdeburg, Stuttgart and Nuremberg. At the same time, the prices of the German carrier are even lower than domestic, and the level of comfort in cars is higher. Tickets can be purchased online, and students, retirees, schoolchildren and groups of more than ten people receive a significant discount.

Europa Light

A bus trip to Germany from Moscow will be a pleasant pastime due to the comfortable conditions on board. Smiling stewardesses, hot meals and drinks, media devices built into the back of the front seat, the ability to reserve a seat in the cabin and other amenities will make the long journey shorter. With this company you can easily get from Moscow to Nuremberg, Munich, Ulm, Augsburg, Stuttgart, Cologne, Hanover, Magdeburg and other big and small cities in Germany.
