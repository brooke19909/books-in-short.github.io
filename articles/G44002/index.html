A fabulous vacation in Dubai - oriental exoticism and modern technology.

Today, vacation in Dubai is becoming more and more prestigious. A beautiful city on the coast of the Persian Gulf, an amazing fairy tale in the middle of a faceless desert - attract thousands of vacationers here.

Dubai is the most modern emirate of the UAE, an international center of tourism, business, sports. At first glance, Dubai impresses with its exquisite architecture, modern engineering achievements, and high level of service. Dubai is the largest port in the Persian Gulf, where ancient mosques and palaces are located in combination with cutting-edge architectural complexes. The bay divided the city into two parts: Bar Dubai and Deira.

One of the areas of the tourism industry of the emirate of Dubai is business tourism. Thousands of businessmen from all over the world come here. Holidays in Dubai - this is an opportunity for amazing shopping. The traditions of the oriental bazaar are combined with a modern trading business. It is here that you can purchase goods from world manufacturers at low prices. In the spring, Dubai hosts an annual shopping festival gathering thousands of buyers eager to take advantage of substantial discounts.

Sports in Dubai is a separate issue. Today, high-level international competitions are held here. Dubai is waiting for everyone who wants to actively relax. Here you will find upscale sports fields, golf courses, a ski complex, diving and yacht clubs. Equestrian sports and camel racing are very popular in Dubai.

Holidays in Dubai are a lot of fascinating entertainments and excursions. Dubai Zoo has collected rare specimens of animals and birds. The amusement park will conquer both adults and children with its interesting unusual attractions, games, and entertainment. Singing fountains are an extraordinary beauty, a creation of modern technology.

Do not forget that Dubai is a traditional Arab world. Despite its democracy compared to other Emirates, Dubai maintains Islamic traditions and oriental customs. Therefore, lovers of the East, who went on vacation in Dubai, it will be extremely interesting to get acquainted with the history of the Arabsky state, look at the life of the desert Bedouins, touch the luxury of Arab sheikhs and see their unusual palaces.

Oriental exoticism and modern achievements of science and technology - this is what the Dubai resort is today.

Last-minute tours to Dubai are popular in the Russian tourist market from October to May. At this time, it is incredibly nice to relax from the cold weather on the cozy Dubai beaches.

Hotels located in close proximity to the coastline have their own private beaches. City hotels often arrange transfers to the beach. Vacationers can choose both paid and free beaches. In Dubai, there are two huge beach complexes with developed internal infrastructure. The peculiarity of the work of beaches in the UAE is the so-called women's days, when men are not allowed to enter the beach, and attendants consist only of women. You should also know that topless sunbathing is prohibited on the territory of beaches in the Emirates.

Dubai hotels are famous for their luxury and comfort, traditional hospitality. However, here you rarely see such a variety of animation as in Europe. In Dubai hotels, you can probably find a lot of offers for active sports than for entertainment. It is interesting that in other emirates the hotel base is not as large as in Dubai.

Choosing last minute packages to Dubai, tourists need to know some of the features of a holiday in the UAE. Dubai is tolerant of other religions, but some visitors must observe certain rules of conduct in public places . Being drunk is punishable by heavy fines. It is not acceptable to eat while standing or on the go. Muslims cannot be circumvented during prayer from the front. Entering the mosque, you need to take off your shoes. When talking with the local population, remember that you can not look into the eyes of a person with a handshake. You can not look in the face of a person who is busy eating.
