What can not be depicted on plastic?

It is forbidden to use the following topics on maps:

  • coats of arms, flags;
  • weapons;
  • pornographic content;
  • drugs
  • textual information;
  • brand names;
  • photographs of famous people;
  • images of notes, currencies;
  • alcohol;
  • tobacco;
  • faces in uniform and other illustrations (a full list of restrictions is provided on the website of Sberbank).

Where to choose a finished design

In the bank branch or on the Internet, you can choose ready-made individual designs of Sberbank cards. These images are made by representatives of the bank and can be transferred to plastic. This function is convenient for those people who do not want to place their own photos on the front side of the card or cannot choose something suitable from their options. When contacting a consultant at a branch, you can ask to see the available individual designs of Sberbank cards in the form of a catalog. Usually they are presented as small copies of real cards: they can be viewed and touched. It happens that cards with an individual design are printed in a special album, which is available for study to all visitors.





What can be shown on the map?

You can draw up a Sberbank card with an individual design in a variety of directions. It all depends on personal imagination and creativity. How long does it take to wait for a card?

A relatively short card is produced with an individual design (Sberbank). The terms of its development vary from three days to two weeks. The minimum production time is occupied by cards with the design offered by the bank. Such work does not require much effort from employees, and therefore the timing of its release pleases every potential owner. If the client wants to order a Sberbank card with an individual design, which he invented independently, then it can come to his disposal only after two weeks. It all depends on the complexity of the image, color palette and small details. About two weeks is the standard time for the manufacture of plastic, but each case is individual and requires a certain time frame. Banking specialist during filling out the application will announce the approximate waiting time for the card.





As soon as the card arrives at the selected department, a message with the necessary information will be sent to the number indicated during registration of the application. Do not delay with going to the bank, as the card cannot be on the balance sheet of a banking organization for too long.

Usually the cost of a card is debited from the account to which it is attached. Moreover, on the day of filing the application for the issue, the required amount should already be in this account. Otherwise, the card cannot be issued, as the bank does not issue cards with an individual design at its own expense.
