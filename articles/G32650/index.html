"Red Lake", Korobitsyno. Resort "Red Lake" in Korobitsino (Leningrad Oblast): reviews, prices

At a distance of 90 km from St. Petersburg is one of the most interesting ski resorts in the country - "Red Lake". Reviews of people vacationing here characterize this resort as a great place for independent and family vacations. The resort complex is located on the top of Zalesnaya Mountain, and offers ski enthusiasts trails for every taste, from gentle slopes that are perfect for beginners to steep slopes that can interest an experienced skier. Not far from the resort is the village of Korobitsyno.



Infrastructure

Excellent infrastructure is one of the advantages that can be offered to vacationers by the ski resort "Red Lake", Korobitsyno. In the season, several runs are constantly working here, ready to accept fans of extreme sports. There is one chairlift and several towbars. There are no problems with snow cover at the "Red Lake" - high-quality equipment for artificial snow is responsible for this. If there is a desire to ride in the dark, the "Red Lake" (Korobitsyno) is perfect for this, because the local routes are equipped with night lighting.

For lovers of cross-country skiing, their own running track is equipped. Snowboarders are also not deprived of attention, the Ski & board cross track leaves them with the warmest experience. Children can ride a toboggan ride, which definitely will not leave young fans of winter recreation indifferent.



Entertainment

"Red Lake" offers a lot of entertainment not only for skiers, but also for more lazy vacationers. Cafes and restaurants are ready to treat visitors with delicious dishes and drinks. There are also bars and comfortable banquet rooms in which you can comfortably celebrate any holiday. To stay at the resort in Korobitsyno there is a small cozy hotel, as well as several cottages. The average capacity of each cottage is from 2 to 8 people.



Tourists assure that each cottage has its own kitchen, shower, a set of quality dishes and even a sauna. On the territory of the holidaymakers there is a children's playground, as well as a gazebo with a canopy and barbecue.

Summer rest

You can come to rest at the "Red Lake" not only in winter, but also in summer. On the shore there is a clean sandy beach with comfortable dressing rooms and sunbeds. There is an opportunity to fish in the lake - experienced fishermen will be able to get hold of an excellent catch here. Even skiers and snowboarders will be able to find for themselves suitable entertainment in the summer. The newly built water ski jump will allow you to ride even on sunny summer days.

People who have already been here assure that for boat trips on the lake you can rent a boat or catamaran. There is a decent tennis court, as well as everything you need to play paintball. All year round "Red Lake" (Korobitsyno) pleases happy visitors with a wide variety of entertainments that can satisfy even the most sophisticated lovers of country recreation.

Ski slopes overview

In the ski resort of Korobitsyno there are as many as 12 tracks for various types of skiing. For skiers, there are three ski lifts and one chairlift. According to the choice of tracks, the "Red Lake" surpasses many ski resorts in the Leningrad Region, the prices here are also quite reasonable.



"Red Lake" offers tracks with a height difference of 100 to 120 meters, and a length of up to 1100 meters. For those who prefer cross-country skiing, a 3 km long circular track has been created on the plain. Some of the slopes are long and gentle, ideal for those who have just started to get involved in skiing. For experienced athletes who can get bored on the gentle slopes, there are several steeper slopes that can provide extreme sports enthusiasts. The quality of the slopes will pleasantly surprise those who come here to ride - all the tracks are well-rolled and always covered with a decent snow cover.

The duration of the season in Korobitsyno

The ski season in the resort "Red Lake" begins somewhere in December and lasts until March, sometimes April. This is the standard duration of the winter season for the entire Leningrad region. Sometimes you have to ride in the rain, but the thaw does not interfere, since the latest snow-making equipment is always ready to fulfill its duty. As you already know, "Red Lake" (Korobitsyno) provides its services all year round, so if you have not had time for a winter vacation, you do not need to be upset, because this resort is always at your service.



Kerosene games

The opportunity to participate in an interesting and beautiful tradition is given to everyone who came to rest in Korobitsyno. Ski resort "Red Lake" every year, following the traditions, arranges "Kerosene Games." Vacationers say that this unusual event begins with a torchlight procession, then to develop into a slalom competition. Athletes who dare to take part will have to drive along a track lit only by kerosene burners. The best will participate in downhill, according to the results of which the strongest athletes among men and women will be determined.

How to get there Prices

Every year more and more people strive to get to the "Red Lake". Korobitsyno attracts with beautiful natural landscapes and decent infrastructure for skiing. Korobitsyno is located about a hundred kilometers from St. Petersburg. If you decide to travel by car, the route to Vyborg or Novopriozerskoye Shosse will be the shortest way for you. In this case, the road will take about an hour and a half. For those who do not have their own transport, there are bus routes, but they will take a little longer than a trip by car.

The cost of the ski pass for 2015 is acceptable: 3500 rubles. per adult for the weekend and 2400 rubles. for children under 12 years old. As much as five whole weekdays rest in this wonderful resort. You can also buy a seasonal ski pass, worth 7,000 rubles. for weekdays and 19,000 rubles. for a full seasonal ski pass.



Tourists say that an hour with a professional instructor in Korobitsyno costs 1,500 rubles. per hour, but you can buy a subscription for 3 classes for 4300 rubles. and for 6 - classes for 8400 rubles. You can rent ski equipment, which will cost you 400 rubles. per set on weekdays and 500 rubles. on the weekend. There is equipment for snowboarding, but its rental costs 100 rubles. more than a ski kit.

Prices are affordable, which only adds to the popularity of the ski resort "Red Lake". The Leningrad region is not too rich in such places, so the resort in Korobitsyno can be a real find for lovers of winter holidays from St. Petersburg.
