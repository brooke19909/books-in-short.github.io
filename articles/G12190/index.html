Volgograd: beaches. Volgograd, the beach "Beavers"

Probably, many of our compatriots dream of relaxing on the Volga. The great Russian river, praised by artists, poets, musicians, attracts travelers and tourists to its banks. Today we will go to Volgograd. The beaches of this city are ideal for a relaxing family vacation. Here, townspeople and tourists from other cities spend their time in the summer heat with pleasure.



Volgograd: Crete beach

It is located on the left bank of the Volga. Here, the townspeople are very fond of spending their free time. It belongs to the Sredneahtubinsk district of the city of Krasnoslobodsk. However, most residents of Volgograd consider this beach their own. The coast here is gentle, sandy, but sometimes there are underwater pits.

Rest on the beach is well organized - you can rent air mattresses and children's rafts, umbrellas and sunbeds. There are volleyball and basketball courts. You can get here by boat from the berth No. 6 of the river port daily from nine to nineteen hours. On weekdays, such flights are made every hour, and on weekends - every half hour.

Volzhsky - city beach

It is located near the island of Green in the vicinity of the abundance gardening partnership. Most of the townspeople spend their leisure time here on stuffy summer days. They are attracted here by golden sand, on which you can get a beautiful tan, the gentle waters of the great river, the opportunity to completely relax and forget your daily worries.



City beach at TsPKiO

This is the official city beach of Volgograd, it is the most comfortable of all available. Fixed canopies are built here, umbrellas are placed on the territory. For kids there are inflatable boats, a volleyball court. There are many vacationers here on weekends. During the day and after the beach is closed, the area is cleaned. The bottom of the river and the shore are covered with small pebbles. The territory is fenced. You can come here by car. But there is no special, guarded parking, so all cars park just at the fence.

You can come here by tram or trolleybus number 8, number 8A, number 12. In addition, you can take a minibus to the stop "Central Park of Culture".

In the second case, finding the way to the beach is not difficult - the signs will tell you the way. True, this may seem incredible, but not all taxi drivers know the way here. Chalet "Lukoil"

It should be noted that in the city of Volgograd there are various beaches - urban, paid, free, wild. All of them are visited by Volgograd residents - some of them are more popular, others less. The beach of the Lukoil camp site is one of the most visited. Vacationers are primarily attracted here by clean sand and a flat sand bottom. This is the Bakaldy district, long loved by Volgograd residents. They not only rest here as families, but also bring their guests here from other cities with pleasure.

Chalet "Ruble"

For everyone who came to the city of Volgograd in summer, the beaches will surely be interesting. We want to introduce you to a picturesque place located a 15-minute drive from the city center. This is the recreation center "Rublevka", located in a beautiful oak forest. For those who want to swim in the cool water on a hot summer day, there is not only a well-equipped beach, but also an outdoor pool with sun loungers.

If you decide to stay here for a few days, then at your service are two-, four- and six-seater log cabins that will make your stay very comfortable.



The hostel "Anyuta"

If you want to spend a weekend in nature with your children, then you will not find a better place. Golden sandy beach, magnificent view of the Volga, a very cozy area. Here you can use the grills. Cozy and comfortable houses are designed to accommodate from two to seven guests. You will need only half an hour to get to the city - and now you are already resting on a great sandy beach. At your service - friendly and friendly staff and a warm homely atmosphere.

Arriving in Volgograd, you can choose the beaches to your liking. It is up to you to decide whether to rest on the beach or have fun in the company of friends and engage in water sports.
