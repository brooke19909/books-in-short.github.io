Hotel Art Deluxe Hotel 3 *, Nha Trang: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Nha Trang - the capital of the Vietnamese province of Khan Hoa - is also considered the center of beach holidays in this country. The whole life of this popular resort is connected with tourism. There are three beaches in Nha Trang, which are considered one of the best in the country. The reason is that in the bay, partially covered by islands, there are practically no strong waves.



Holidays in Nha Trang

Many Russians come here every year who prefer to spend their vacations at sea, surrounded by marvelous nature. In addition, due to climatic features, here you can not only swim and sunbathe on the white beaches, but also improve your health with the help of mud procedures. Sand on the coast of Nha Trang is healing: it has healing properties due to its special composition.

You can come to this resort at any time of the year. The sea here is quite deep: you can dive already from three meters from the shore. The beaches in Nha Trang are municipal, but this does not stop them from offering tourists many attractions, from water skiing to balloon rides. There are many diving centers on the coast.



Hotel Overview

A huge army of tourists coming from all over the world, including Russians, rests in Nha Trang every year. Several hundred hotels have been built along the coast. There are luxury apartments in luxury Ana Mandara hotels, in five-star complexes with expensive restaurants and spa centers. Those who are not satisfied with such a holiday may well book rooms in the "treshki". There are about fifty of them in this popular Vietnamese resort. All of them offer a comfortable and decent vacation. The rooms in them, such as in the Art Deluxe Hotel (Nha Trang) 3 * are well furnished, they have everything you need.

For the convenience of tourists, almost everywhere there are tour agencies that organize tours of the sights of this resort. Without exception, all three-star hotels in Nha Trang have their own pool, and beach areas are literally a few steps away or across the road, such as from the Art Deluxe Hotel 3 *, which is quite popular among our compatriots. Here you can relax comfortably and inexpensively, having received all the necessary range of services for a normal stay.

Infrastructure

Art Deluxe Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) has its own parking lot, which is guarded. Its infrastructure also includes a lobby, a compact business center. Paid services are offered to residents, such as washing and ironing of outer clothing, the possibility of renting cells in a depository safe. Free luggage storage is available.

The hotel has an elevator and concierge services. The reception is open around the clock. Here you can arrange a transfer, buy sightseeing tours or rent a vehicle.



Housing stock

In Art Deluxe Hotel 3 *, which is considered small in size, only fifty rooms of the following categories: standard, deluxe, family. They are equipped in a seven-story building, which also houses the administrative infrastructure. There are two modern elevators in the building for tourists. In apartments with increased comfort, 2 + 2 people can be accommodated at the same time. At the request of parents, an extra playpen is available. The rooms have everything necessary for living: a plasma TV, high-quality dark wood furniture, armchairs, a sofa, couches, dressing table, a work table, a dining area and a minibar. For using the contents of the latter should be paid. There are balconies in front of the rooms, dark curtains on the windows.

The combined bathrooms have excellent plumbing, the apartments have a jacuzzi. On the floor - ceramic tiles. All public areas of Art Deluxe Hotel 3 * are air-conditioned, and individual split-systems are installed in the rooms. According to the rules of the hotel, pets are not allowed.

Beach

Art Deluxe Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) is located on the first line. To the sea - a maximum of three minutes. To the municipal beach you need to go across the road. For sunbeds and mattresses you need to pay extra. Beach towels can be obtained by leaving a deposit - 100 dong.

Additional Information

Art Deluxe Hotel 3 * offers its tourists sightseeing tours, which you can buy in an office working in the administrative building. Here you can book a trip to a mountain resort in Dolat, a safari to the Vietnamese jungle, to a factory where craftswomen paint silk products, etc. A wide range of entertainment can be obtained on the beach, including renting snorkeling equipment.

Reviews

The hotel has for some time been very popular with those tour operators who sell tours to Vietnam. It is perfect for a relaxing holiday. There are no animations or noisy bars on site. Tourists say many positive words about the rooms of Art Deluxe Hotel 3 *. Russian reviews indicate that they are perfectly furnished: they have absolutely everything for a comfortable stay. The hotel does not offer noisy entertainment, it does not have a spa center, sauna and other similar services, but this, according to many, is not a minus, but rather a plus, contributing to a peaceful pastime.



The staff works harmoniously, quickly solving problems. As for nutrition, opinions differ: some consider it monotonous, others are satisfied.

Many Russians were pleasantly surprised by the low prices in restaurants. Excursions are also inexpensive. However, the hotel has several drawbacks: some believe that it is far from the European quarter, in addition - and most reviews testify to this - the hotel decided to charge a separate fee for washing used beach towels, which many Russians did not like.

Another significant drawback of our compatriots consider an unfavorable road, which is all in potholes and blurred. It is on it that you have to go from the hotel to the city center.
