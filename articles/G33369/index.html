Park May 1, Nizhny Novgorod: photo, address, how to get

Very pleased with the current trend. The oldest city parks are gradually reviving, rejuvenating and becoming more beautiful than they were. And indeed, where else to go on a summer, fine day, how not to walk along the shady alleys. And if there are also opportunities for pleasant leisure, then it is worth making it a weekly tradition. Than to spend the evening at the TV, it is much better to get out into the fresh air. Today in the focus of our attention is the park on May 1 in Nizhny Novgorod. Huge, beautiful and amazing, with whole alleys of trees that have survived for decades, he invites you to visit.



A bit of history

The first decision to establish a recreation area was made by the city authorities in 1896, when various exhibitions were held at this place. By decision of the merchants, the park received its initial appearance on May 1. Nizhny Novgorod was then completely different, and the recreation area was very different from modern.

In the wasteland between the Moscow-Nikolaev railway and the forest of Count Shuvalov , a walking area with 50 fountains and a pond in which swans lived was divided. Over 30 entertainment venues were built throughout the territory. That was the park on May 1. Nizhny Novgorod has changed a lot since then, only a pond and the territory itself, which they decided not to rename, have reached our time.



What is he like today

How long have you been out for a walk? If so, drop everything, take your family and recover in nature. It is not necessary to travel out of town, you can go to the park on May 1. Nizhny Novgorod itself is very beautiful, but the presence of such places makes it simply wonderful. Walking area is 15 hectares. The nature is very beautiful, all the alleys are well-groomed, and mainly linden and birch grow, flowerbeds are broken.

It is especially interesting here on weekends. Various creative groups, musical and dance perform on stage. The city administration often arranges children's parties with awards and valuable prizes. The park named after May 1 in Nizhny Novgorod invites you to visit interesting and exciting attractions, go for a bite to eat in a cafe and learn horse riding in a riding school.

Contact zoo

More precisely, this is a living corner called "Cat Farm". There are not many inhabitants, but each of them is very affectionate and does not mind communicating. Several geese and chickens, as well as stunning sheep, meet their guests and beg for goodies. Hamsters and rats, turtles and hedgehogs live under the same roof. They also will not refuse food, but in the daytime the activity of nocturnal animals decreases, so sometimes they may not leave the houses.

Labyrinth of fear

Want to tickle your nerves? Then welcome. You will be scared in every possible way. The labyrinth itself is stylized as a creepy hut. Robotic scenery and mystical attributes cause a storm of emotions and an explosion of adrenaline. It is impossible to remain indifferent. By the way, there are three modes that the visitor selects:

  • Lighting is on, while the scenery is stopped .
  • The light works in full force and the movement of all mechanisms is connected.
  • The lighting is turned off, not only the scenery come to life , but also the operator is included in the process. It is undesirable to take children on such an adventure.




Dolphinarium

This is where the whole family should go. More recently, a stunning dolphinarium was equipped here. Park May 1 (Nizhny Novgorod) immediately gained extraordinary popularity. Still, who does not want to look at these amazing creatures and get the opportunity to swim with them.

The main hall is designed for 500 seats. There are vivid performances with the participation of two dolphins and a couple of fur seals. After each show, you can take a photo on the platform and in the water, as well as swim, accompanied by amazing creatures. Children under four are admitted free of charge, but must provide a birth certificate. The price of basic tickets is from 500 to 700 rubles.



Winter adventures

With the advent of cold weather, the park does not close. He is still ready to meet his guests. Now, in addition to walking along the snowy alleys, you can spend time at the rink. Its usual working hours are from 11:00 to 23:00. Due to weather conditions, it may change. The cost of going out on ice is relatively small. A ticket for children up to 7 years old will cost 100 rubles, for adults - 200. A skate rental is also available here, the cost is the same. There is a sharpening service.



Family trip to the park

To get here, it is enough to have a free day and a desire to spend it with your family. Everything else you will find on its territory. Probably not among the locals those who do not know the address of the park on May 1. Nizhny Novgorod is a big city, but there is only one similar object. But for those who come to visit, this information can be useful. So, it is located on the street. October Revolution, 31. The nearest metro station is Chkalovskaya, if you decide to use the metro.

Entrance to the park is free from 06:00 to 23:00. Of course, not all attractions work until late, but they are usually quite enough. How to get to the park on May 1 (Nizhny Novgorod) by public transport? There are buses number 26, 40, 51, minibus T-37. Choose the one that is most convenient for you.
