Ski resort Arkhyz. Ski resort "Arkhyz - Romance"

Surely you heard and even tried melt mineral water called "Arkhyz", which is named after a village located in the Karachay-Cherkess Republic. However, Arkhyz is not only a healing and very tasty mineral water, but also an all-weather resort area that harmoniously combines summer and winter types of tourism. Today we take a closer look at this place and find out why it is unique.



Where is Arkhyz ski resort located?

The picturesque mountain valley is 109 km from the railway station "Dzheguta" at an altitude of more than 1000 meters above sea level. In the village are concentrated more than 60 mountain lakes and glaciers. On all sides, the area is surrounded by steep hills. The resort has 12 beautiful waterfalls.

What to get to?

You can get to the Arkhyz ski resort by train, private car or public transport. In the first case, by train, this, by the way, is the best option, you need to get to the Cherkessk and Nevinnomyssk stations, and then by taxi. If you decide to travel by your own car, you need to follow the following path: Nevinnomyssk - Cherkessk - Kumysh - Zelenchukskaya. Public transport (bus) extends from Labinsk, Cherkessk, Nevinnomyssk and Krasnodar.

Arkhyz ski resort was created taking into account all the needs of the client. Even the most sophisticated tourists will be delighted with the quality service. A ski drive or unhurried walks along the slopes of fast-moving rivers, interesting excursions to historical places or horseback riding along mountain lakes - the village has everything for an unforgettable and extreme vacation.



Future plans

The mountains are known for their healing climate and healing water, so in the future it is planned to build sanatoriums with balneological procedures, medical centers and SPA salons here. Tourists will be offered to stay in separate comfortable chalets, chic apartments or budget rooms - everyone will be able to choose an alternative for themselves based on their preferences and affluence.

It is also planned to equip retail facilities, recreation and leisure centers, schools for training beginners and cozy restaurants. Over time, the territory of the village will be enriched with children's zones, points for the sale of sports equipment, swimming pools. Local hospitality and color will be highly appreciated by vacationers. Arkhyz (ski resort) receives only positive reviews and recommendations, despite the fact that construction work is still underway.

What to see?

Arkhyz ski resort is famous for its unique facilities. The road to the village is already an excursion tour. While at the resort, first of all visit the Nizhnearkhiz Gorge - an ancient monument directly under the sky. The next object is the Astrophysical Observatory of the Russian Academy of Sciences, in which the huge ‚ÄúRatan-600‚Äù radio telescope is installed - the largest in the world.

An unforgettable experience will remain with you from the cave image of the face of Jesus Christ. This object is considered a local shrine and a place of pilgrimage for Christians; it was discovered on the banks of the Bolshoy Zelenchuk River in 1999.

Visit the Teberda Nature Reserve, on the territory of which majestic cliffs and ridges rise: Cheget Chag, Abishira-Akhuba, Uzhum, Ehresku.
