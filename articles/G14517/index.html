Official statistics



Why Guantamo? The prison is built by the United States and is indefinitely rented from Cuba. In the same territory is the military base of America. It bears the same name as the bay, river, province and city: Guantamo. Prison is its natural extension. The first "tenants" were brought here in 2002. 20 Afghans fighting on the side of the Taliban became the first prisoners of the casemate. Since then, more than 750 foreigners passed through it, among which there were eight Russians. Some of them were extradited, some were transferred to other prisons, some were released. But Guantanamo was not famous for this. The prison is notorious for the fact that many prisoners have been held there for years without charge.
