Be sure to enter the image when photographing the first acting portfolio?
As a rule, a novice actor does not yet have sufficient experience in entering the image and, importantly, still does not really know how he looks from the side when entering the image.

Actor Type
An actor‚Äôs type is the main element by which an actor is selected for a particular role. Therefore, it must be disclosed as well as possible.

When revealing the type in the photos, you should not play. It is very important to be and remain yourself. It is necessary to show your natural emotions, your manner of behavior and movements. Also, what the director is looking at is the proportion of the body and the features of the face and gaze. Try to show it as detailed as possible.

Should I ask the photographer to put you in?
This is obviously a losing option for the actor. Remember that when you come to an audition or get a role, you will not have a person who will put and guide you. Have to play yourself. And when photographing an acting portfolio, you need to behave the same way - play and move yourself. The only thing you can agree with the photographer when photographing your acting portfolio is to tell you how and in what light you look most advantageous. In this case - the better you set up a tandem with a photographer, the greater chance you get to get good photos.

What to bring with you to a photo studio.
A well-known fact is that clothing can change a person quite strongly. Therefore, do not be lazy to bring several clothing options to the photo studio: a business suit, a sports suit , an evening outfit ...

Clothes should not be bright, so as not to distract attention to themselves, without bright spots. It will be better if the actor refuses jewelry and jewelry when photographing the acting portfolio.

Portfolio Requirements
Actors should have a minimum of makeup, a minimum of retouching. Do not embellish photos - they should be true and show you. Be sure to attend full-length photographs, close-up portraits, shoulder portraits, half-height photographs.

Pictures should be taken on a plain background (white, black or gray). The light should be even, without bright light spots.
