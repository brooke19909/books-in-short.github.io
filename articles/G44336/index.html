Residential complex "Nikolin Park": reviews, photos

Modern residential complexes impress with their diversity. Developers manage to foresee every little thing to make life as convenient, comfortable and modern as possible. If you decide to purchase an apartment in the capital, we recommend that you take a closer look at the new residential complexes. Why, you ask? Everything is extremely simple and clear: the complexes are represented not just by apartment buildings, but by all infrastructure facilities. This is a kind of "city in the city", where all the necessary conditions are created. "Nicolin Park" is a vivid representative of such complexes. It is about him that will be discussed in this material. Feedback from real residents and those who managed to visit the facility will help evaluate the project from all sides, determine its strengths and weaknesses.





about the project

So, the residential complex "Nicolin Park", according to customers, is the embodiment of comfortable modern life in the territory of New Moscow. This is a great option for those who want to appreciate the delights of suburban life in the fresh air, but are not ready to abandon the facilities of the capital's infrastructure.

Among the main advantages of the Nicolin Park residential complex that real estate buyers have noticed and appreciated are a wide selection of planning solutions, well-developed and well-thought out infrastructure, including social facilities, as well as security provided by a fenced area, and transport accessibility. Buyers also attributed the Moscow registration to the main advantages. Within the framework of the project, 11 buildings of variable number of storeys (from 4 to 9 floors) will be built.

Transport accessibility

We already managed to mention the transport accessibility promised by the developer. But how comfortable is it to live in the LCD "Nicolin Park"? Responses of residents who do not have a car emphasize that getting to the metro is not difficult. The fact is that specifically for the residents of the new complex, a separate public transport route was launched connecting it with the Moscow metro station. Moreover, the development plan of the Leninsky district includes the construction of a metro station.





So what can the LCD "Nicolin Park" offer? How to get to the complex? If you are traveling in a private car, then it is enough to move in the direction of the Kaluga highway. At 3 km of the MKAD, turn right and move in the direction of Nikolo-Khovansky. From the Teply Stan metro station to the complex, buses No. 526, 577, 433 run to the Gas Pipeline stop, as well as minibuses No. 894 and 27 to the turn to the village of Nikolo-Khovanskoye.

Infrastructure

The complex is in close proximity to all necessary infrastructure. Not far from the new buildings , the Mega. Teply Stan shopping and entertainment complex , the construction market, and the wholesale and food base.





Embodying modern technology and the concept of comfortable housing, the developer laid the construction of his own infrastructure facilities in the project. A kindergarten, a medical center, children's, playgrounds and sports grounds, recreation areas for each resident, walking and bicycle paths, shops, and a pharmacy will appear on the territory of the complex. Residents of the rented houses note that at the moment it is difficult to call the infrastructure of the complex developed, there are already not enough places in the kindergarten, and even the nearest school is far away.
