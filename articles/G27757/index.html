Hotels of Irbit. Description. Reviews

Irbit is one of the oldest cities in the Sverdlovsk region. Unfortunately, its former greatness has now been lost, but the revived Irbit Fair attracts many participants, buyers and tourists. If you have to stay here, you can stay at one of the Irbit hotels.

Hotel Nitsa

The hotel occupies the third floor of a five-story building located in the city center. Walking distance to the Museum and Exhibition Center, Museum of Education, Center for Motoculture of Irbit. In the same building with the hotel there is a restaurant and shops. For guests with a private car, free parking is provided. The hotel has a small cafe. Guests can use the services of the dining room, which has an equipped kitchen. Each room has its own bathroom. Without payment, they will call you a taxi, deliver correspondence to your room, and wake you up at the indicated time. The hotel offers several categories of rooms:

  • single - 1100 rubles per day;
  • double - 1500 rubles per day;
  • six-seater - 2400 rubles per day;
  • double junior suite - 2000 rubles per day;
  • double suite - 2200 rubles per day.

The cost for placing a guest on an extra bed is 500 rubles per day. Each room is equipped with a TV. There is a system of discounts. The Nitsa Hotel is located in Irbit at the address: Pervomaiskaya Street, 46. Check-in time is 12.00, check-out time is also 12.00.
