Loans without collateral

In this bank you can get a cash loan in the amount of from 100 thousand to 6 million tenge, for a period of up to 5 years. If the client is ready to provide collateral movable or immovable property, then in this case a loan of up to 30 million tenge is available to him. What are the requirements for borrowers

In order to get a loan, you must provide a document confirming the citizenship of the Republic of Kazakhstan. In this case, funds are issued to citizens aged 21 years. In addition to the main package of documents, it is necessary to provide a statement of income confirming a permanent place of work. In addition, a document on marital status may also be required.

If we are talking about obtaining a loan on bail, then in this case the assessment of the collateral property is carried out. Repayment of the loan can be carried out both with a credit card and using cash.

Judging by the comments of the clients of the financial institution, it took them a lot of time to get the refinancing service. For example, if the client did not provide the details of Kazkommertsbank or another financial organization to whose account it is planned to transfer money, then in this case the manager will not be able to correctly execute the transaction.

Also, in some situations, regardless of employee qualifications, additional documents may be required. At the first stages, the manager sends the client data to the head office, where another operator can request additional securities.

You need to understand that getting a loan can take a lot more time than planned. This is because the bank employee needs to make sure that the financial institution does not incur losses, and that the borrower will timely make all the required payments.

Yes, it really cannot be ruled out that in some branches of the bank employees do not always possess information. However, in other situations, everything only looks like this in the eyes of customers. To avoid such situations, before going to the bank, it is recommended to familiarize yourself with information on lending on the official website of a financial institution. Thanks to this, you can come to the bank with a ready-made package of documents and significantly speed up the process of receiving funds.

Card Opening Reviews

If we are talking about the standard procedure for opening an account, then, as a rule, everything happens very quickly. However, if the issue of an additional Kazkommertsbank card is required, some customers are faced with rather serious problems, which again can be attributed to the unpreparedness of the plastic holders themselves.

Some complain that opening a new account or issuing additional cards took a lot more time. However, it should be borne in mind that this procedure is also performed with the participation of the head office. Thus, applications and a package of documents are initially sent there. Accordingly, the farther the office is from the head office, the longer it will take to wait for the finished card. The same thing happens in almost all financial institutions. Therefore, you should not blame the bank itself or its employees for everything.

Also, some of the bank's customers began to respond negatively about it after the events that took place several years ago, when the global crisis began.

What has happened since 2016

In the fall of 2016, the banking issue in Kazakhstan became very acute. First of all, it was about the financial well-being of some organizations. At the same time, the media that gave information was very actively adding fuel to the fire, referring to sources, and not to real numbers. This negatively affected many banks.

The headlines said that Kazkommertsbank was on the verge of default and would soon cease to exist. In fact, it was not quite like that. The fact is that this financial organization simply merged with Halyk Bank, while continuing to operate. At the same time, both the Kazkommertsbank hotline and other services are still active. Therefore, investors and borrowers have nothing to worry about. If we talk about the need to reissue a loan, then, according to representatives of the organization, this procedure, if it happens, is without problems for payers.

Many are also interested in what will happen with the onlinebank kz service of Kazkommertsbank. According to representatives, online banking and other resources will function as usual. However, it is believed that over time these services will merge with similar applications from Halyk Bank.

Thus, the services of Kazkommertsbank online can still be safely used. However, it is better to follow the news of a financial organization so as not to miss important changes regarding its mode of operation.
