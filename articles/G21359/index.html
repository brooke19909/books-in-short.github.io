Location

Immediately two seas, Black and Azov, washed the glorious peninsula of Crimea. Beregovoe - a small village lying in its eastern side - extends to the coast of the Black Sea. From Yalta it is about 170 km, from Simferopol 125 km from the railway. station and 135 km from the airport or 1.5-2.2 hours by taxi, and from Feodosia - only 8 km, which can be covered by bus in 20 minutes. Its eastern border is the small salt lake Kuchuk-Adzhigol (Aschigol). It does not have therapeutic mud, therefore it is not of great interest to vacationers. It is separated from the Black Sea by a narrow Ak-Monai Isthmus, along which the Kerch highway is laid, crossing the village. About a kilometer from the first lake lies the second, called Adzhigol. It is slightly larger in size, also salty and also not medicinal. Directly adjacent to this lake is the village of Primorsky.



How to get there by public transport

Most people arrive by train or plane to Crimea. The coastal area near Feodosia is relatively close to the capital of the Simferopol Peninsula and connected to it by the Theodosian P23 highway. Get from the railway Station or airport can be a direct "flight" by taking a taxi. The trip will cost about 5000 rubles from the airport and about 3000 rubles from the station. The official cost of a taxi (if ordered through a dispatcher) is 22 rubles / km. You can get cheaper, but with transfers. If you arrived in Simferopol by plane, you need to get to the central railway line by buses No. 49, 49a, minibuses No. 115, 98, 100 or trolleybuses No. 9, 55, 54 train station. Ticket price - from 9 to 15 rubles. From the station, walk on foot to the Spa Station, located 30-50 meters away. From there, in the summer, literally every 10-12 minutes, direct or passing buses leave for Feodosia. Ticket price from 350 rubles per person. The journey takes about 2 hours. In Feodosia you will have to change to a bus (minibus) No. 106 to Beregovoy. In the afternoon they ‚Äúrun‚Äù every 7-10 minutes, and in the evening, that is, after 20-00, every 25-30 minutes. Lying on such "gold" is also very pleasant, although sunbeds are always available throughout the entire beach (from 150 rubles per day).
