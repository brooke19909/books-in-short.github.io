"Clover Park" Yekaterinburg - a modern solution for urban life

More than a thousand kilometers of distance separate the capital of the state from another, much smaller in area and population, but nonetheless a major economic center - Yekaterinburg. This city is also called the capital of the Urals.





A little about Yekaterinburg

For over 12 years, the population of the city has only increased; it occupies the fourth position in this matter among the cities of Russia. Many production and manufacturing enterprises make it possible to provide jobs for everyone. And to provide residents of the city with comfortable housing, developers are building ever new residential complexes. One such project is Clover Park in Yekaterinburg.

Location of the residential complex

For the construction of "Clover Park" in Yekaterinburg, the city authorities allocated one of the most attractive and promising land plots to the developer. The residential complex is being built on the banks of the Iset River, which provides its future residents with a beautiful view from the windows of new housing. It is located just six minutes from the center of Yekaterinburg, which makes it possible to stay in modern housing with all the necessary infrastructure.





The residential complex "Clover Park" in Yekaterinburg is located at the address: Tkachev street. A business center of the same name was erected nearby. This location gives the complex excellent transport accessibility. Residents will be able to get home on any form of public transport plying in the city.

Description of the residential complex

"Clover Park" in Yekaterinburg was designed as eight residential buildings of different floors, built using monolithic technology. Due to so many houses, apartments of various sizes will be presented in them: from studios to seven-room ones. The developer has provided all the smallest nuances in the layouts of apartments for the most comfortable life of their future customers. They will have improved sound insulation, large windows with French window sills, spacious rooms and kitchens, a wardrobe room. Residents who bought apartments on the upper floors are guaranteed an additional advantage - the possibility of equipping the apartment with a real fireplace, since these floors will have a chimney. Thus, everyone can purchase housing in accordance with their desires and needs of their family.





The residential quarter is equipped with its own access to the Mayakovsky park, which will allow those who wish to engage in morning jogging and sports, without experiencing any inconvenience. The developer also integrated the Iset River flowing nearby and provided for the arrangement of its own embankment.

Internal and external infrastructure

The territory of the Clover Park residential complex in Yekaterinburg will be fenced and guarded by CCTV cameras, which will provide its residents with the greatest security. Internal infrastructure includes the arrangement of children's and sports grounds, recreation areas for adults and children, landscape gardening of the territory. The project provides for an underground parking for 2667 cars, as well as open guest car parking.

Since the developer has planned a very large-scale project for the construction of residential buildings, for the convenience and comfort of the residents of the Clover Park complex in Yekaterinburg, it is planned to build all the necessary infrastructure to accommodate facilities for services, development and training of children of different ages, the purchase of food, and leisure activities. This means the opening of kindergartens, schools, pharmacies, a fitness center with a swimming pool, a beauty salon, a dental office, and shops in the future. Nearby are a huge Mayakovsky park and the Iset River, which can give minutes of unforgettable relaxation and entertainment.





Thanks to such a successful location of the residential complex, within walking distance from it are already existing and existing all the necessary infrastructure. Many kindergartens and schools will gladly accept new arrivals of children and schoolchildren. For those older, there is a teacher training college. It is enough to cross the street of Weavers - and you can get into the huge hypermarket "Maksidom", where there are any goods and food products.

Reviews about the residential complex

Since the residential complex has not yet been commissioned for settlement, reviews about the "Clover Park" in Yekaterinburg are scarce. This is mainly the discontent of residents about the ongoing construction and the inconvenience associated with it. But there are also satisfied with the business center of the same name and the location of the LCD.
