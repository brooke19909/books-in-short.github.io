Hotel Dream World Resort & Spa 5 * (Side, Turkey): photos and reviews of tourists

Number of rooms

The Dream World Resort & Spa offers 232 rooms with a total capacity of 510 people. Here are the categories of apartments available for guests:

  • Standard rooms are designed for two guests with the possibility of additional accommodation. The cozy bedroom is equipped with large or twin beds, as well as a place to relax. Three rooms of this category are specially equipped for the disabled (wide doorways, handrails in the bathroom, sockets and switches at a convenient height).
  • Family rooms can accommodate up to four people. The apartment consists of two bedrooms separated by an internal door. One room is equipped with a large bed, and the second - a pair of separate.
  • Family Duplex is a spacious two-story room. On the first level there is a bathroom and a bedroom with twin beds. The second floor is a bedroom with a large French bed.
