Goryachiy Klyuch Resort (Krasnodar Territory): reviews of people who have moved

Kuban has long been famous for the beauty of its nature and its healing powers. One of the pearls of the region is the Goryachiy Klyuch resort (Krasnodar Territory). Reviews of people who moved here note a sharp improvement in their health, thanks to the cleanest air and medicinal herbs, the fame of which has long gone beyond the boundaries of the region.

Hot key. General information

The Goryachy Klyuch resort in the Krasnodar Territory, the description of which must begin with its amazing nature, is rightly called a unique place in the Kuban. It is located on the banks of the Psekups River, which flows along the northern foothills of the Caucasus. The nature of this quiet place, sheltered by mountains on three sides, is truly unique. The valley of the mountain river, covered with dense forest, softens the summer heat and makes the winter especially mild. Hot Key (Krasnodar Territory) boasts an unusually large number of warm days. Only Anapa, rightfully considered the sunniest resort in Russia, surpasses it in this parameter. However, unlike him, in the Hot Key, the southern sun is not so scorching. It softens the fresh air of the mountains and the coolness of the river.





This resort town is located 65 km from Krasnodar and the same distance lies between it and the Black Sea coast. Goryachy Klyuch consists of one urban-type village and 6 administrative districts, which include 28 rural settlements. The total number of residents is more than 52 thousand: almost 30 thousand of them live in urban areas, and 22 thousand live in rural areas. It is noteworthy that there are almost no young people who leave to work in large cities. However, few of them can completely disconnect from their hometown. Every year young people come here to visit their parents and enjoy their holidays in their small homeland. Despite the lack of noisy entertainment, young people say that they are never bored in their hometown.

Resort History

The history of the city of Goryachy Klyuch (Krasnodar Territory) began from the moment a military hospital was built on its current territory and began to equip it. First of all, they built hospitals and equipped cranes designed for the extraction of underground mineral waters.

In the description of the history of this resort city there is one interesting case that allows its residents to consider their city a blessed heaven. This is due to the fact that at the moment when the priest consecrated the sources of mineral waters, in response to the words of blessing, a bright ray of sunshine pierced through the cloudy sky. He lit up the whole clearing and every person who was on it at that moment. This day, July 15, 1864 is called the birthday of the Goryachiy Klyuch resort (Krasnodar Territory). The reviews of those who moved to this area mark the strength of the faith of the indigenous people in the importance of this phenomenon, which is considered a real sign here.

Three years later, in 1867, Grand Duke Mikhail Nikolaevich came to a new treatment site. It was he who named the main street of the city - Mikhailovskaya.

Even cars do not have the right to enter the resort area, an imposing fine is provided for violating this rule. However, such fines are rare. Local residents, as well as guests of the resort who came to relax, appreciate the cleanliness of this unique place and do not violate existing prohibitions.

The healing power of the Hot Spring mineral springs

This resort is a unique place where the healing properties of Essentuki mineral water for drinking are organically combined with hydrogen sulfide baths such as Sochi-Matsesta. After a person begins to drink water from local mineral springs, the healing effect is achieved very quickly. There is an improvement in respiration, blood circulation, skin and general metabolism. More oxygen begins to flow into the body, protein metabolism, as well as the tissue's regenerative ability, also improves.





Today, the Goryachiy Klyuch resort in the Krasnodar Territory uses 6 types of mineral waters in its health resorts, which are extracted from 17 wells. In this case, both their external application and internal are provided. Due to such a variety of types of mineral waters, Goryachy Klyuch is rightly called the unique resort, balneological and drinking. Table mineral water, which is named after this amazing place, meets the highest standards of the World Health Organization.

Types of Mineral Water

Among a large number of mineral springs of various types, the treatment at the resort is carried out by such waters as:

  1. Thermal, sulfide, chloride-bicarbonate sodium - intended for baths that help with problems with many diseases, such as the digestive and nervous systems, the musculoskeletal system, gynecological problems. The temperature of such waters reaches 60C.
  2. Sulphide, bicarbonate, sodium and chloride sodium, the temperature and the presence of which hydrogen sulfide is less, are used for drinking treatment and balneotherapy procedures.

Thus, this resort is suitable for the treatment of almost all diseases known to science at the present stage. It is this feature that is inherent in the city of Goryachy Klyuch (Krasnodar Territory), the reviews of those who have moved note as the main reason for the change of permanent residence.

Sanatoriums of the resort

This small town has over 10 sanatoriums, the largest of which is named after the resort itself. They accept for treatment people from all over our country, as well as from neighboring countries.

If you want to improve your health without taking expensive medications, the Goryachy Klyuch resort (Krasnodar Territory) will be your best choice. Sanatoriums here offer hydrotherapy facilities for their visitors, in which there are more than 70 bathtubs. Modern boarding houses, equipped with expensive equipment for the diagnosis of diseases and their successful treatment, can host up to 1,500 people at a time.

Private sector at the resort

In addition to the sanatoriums, the private sector is presented for vacationers in the Hot Key of the Krasnodar Territory. This is a lot of mini-hotels, apartments and houses that can be rented during your stay on treatment. The undoubted advantage of this choice of housing is a significant cost savings. At the same time, you can visit the sanatorium to receive medical procedures, enjoying the rest of the time at home in a cozy atmosphere. If you are going to visit the resort Goryachy Klyuch (Krasnodar Territory), you can look at the photos of the future temporary home in advance to determine a more suitable place of residence during your stay in the city. A variety of positions in the private sector allows you to choose the place of residence located closest to the sanatorium in which you are treating. In addition, you can know in advance the route, the time that it takes to get to the hospital and other important factors that affect the choice of housing. You can find housing even after arriving in the city. However, in this case, you run the risk of not finding a suitable location or price. Therefore, it is advisable to take care of the place of stay in advance, paying for medical procedures in the sanatorium.





How to get to the resort?

You can get to the Hot Key only through Krasnodar. This city, being the capital of the region, attracts many with its combination of developed infrastructure and beautiful nature. You can get to Krasnodar by any means of transport: airplane, train, bus or ride your own car. The time spent on the road, and its cost depends on the chosen mode of transport. A plane will be the fastest way to get to your destination, but it will cost a lot.

From Krasnodar, you can get to Goryachiy Klyuch by intercity bus, train or train from the city railway station. After arriving at your destination, you can take a taxi or minibus to the sanatorium where you have reserved seats.

Holiday at the resort

As for spending time in the resort Goryachy Klyuch (Krasnodar Territory), reviews of vacationers note that this city is not suitable for lovers of noisy entertainment and large companies. This is a place for a quiet break from the bustle of the city. Everything here breathes calm and measured.

That is why, if you want to not only enjoy the healthy and clean air in combination with the healing properties of mineral springs, but also take a break from the bustle of noisy cities, Goryachy Klyuch is exactly the place you need.

Walking in the picturesque places of the city and sightseeing are what this resort offers for its guests.

Excursions in the Hot Key

To entertain holidaymakers, numerous excursions are organized in the city. The most beautiful places of the area that will be presented during trips are worth visiting this beautiful resort.

One of the most famous attractions is the Underground Lake, which emerges on the earth's surface in the form of a spring, which then flows into the Psekups River. The peculiarity of this place is the healing and anti-aging properties of water. On the river bank made stone baths. They were erected by residents of the resort Goryachiy Klyuch (Krasnodar Territory). A photo of the beach, where you will experience the healing power of the waters of the Underground Lake, will be an excellent memorable souvenir from the rest.

Another famous local attraction is Adov Waterfall, which is a truly mesmerizing place. Its waters fall from a height of 12 meters into the basin of the Chepsi River, located on the right tributary of the Adov Stream.





Outside the city is the Valley of Charm - a piedmont glade covered with oak forest.

Also, having arrived at the resort, you can visit places like Dantovo Gorge, Petushok Rock, Bogatyr Gorges, Monastery Lake. The names of attractions themselves carry a touch of poetry and romanticism. As city guests say, nature itself helps to heal here.

A walk through the Hot Spring Healing Park will bring real peace. Wide alleys, hundreds of plant species buried in verdure from all corners of the earth, can not leave anyone indifferent. The park has no clear boundaries, and a walk along it can end in a nearby forest. In the center of the healing park is Mineralnaya Polyana. The water that flows in the Drinking Gallery and the hydropathic institution passes through its wells. Here is also the Pillar of Memory - the oldest Adyghe monument.

Describing the entertainment that the Goryachiy Klyuch resort (Krasnodar Territory) offers, horse riding along mountain trails cannot be noted. This is an unforgettable feeling of unity with nature, which delights every visitor to the city. For the smallest vacationers a pony ride is provided. Such entertainment will not leave indifferent any child.





Thus, it is impossible to talk about boredom, having arrived in the city of Goryachy Klyuch (Krasnodar Territory). A photo of the most beautiful places you have visited will remind you of the most calm and measured rest.

Hot Spring Resort (Krasnodar Territory). Responses of the moved

Authorities say that in recent years, the birth rate has declined. However, this fact does not affect the demographic situation of the district, because every year dozens of people move to Goryachy Klyuch for permanent residence. Such a move is associated, as a rule, with a state of health, when, for medical reasons, it is necessary to change the climate. However, there are people who have moved among those who came to rest here and no longer wanted to leave this cozy city. The reasons that prompted people to change their place of residence to Goryachy Klyuch, according to the reviews of those who moved, are as follows:

  • unique healing properties of mineral waters;
  • mild climate without sweltering heat in summer and extreme cold in winter;
  • the proximity of the mountains;
  • fresh air;
  • lack of industrial enterprises;
  • desire to change the bustle of the city with the calm rhythm of the resort and be closer to nature.

It is noteworthy that the opinions of people who were vacationing here and who moved to permanent residence agree on one thing. Both those and others do not cease to celebrate the generosity and primordial nature of this quiet town.





Thus, if you want to not only improve your health, but also enjoy a relaxing holiday, see the most beautiful natural sites, then the resort of Goryachiy Klyuch (Krasnodar Territory), reviews of which are only positive, will be an ideal choice for you.
