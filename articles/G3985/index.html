Clothing for cats: do-it-yourself pet dresses

People and cats live together solely because they can forgive each other and find compromises. We often close our eyes to many of their pranks, to the fact that they spoil furniture, prevent us from sleeping and occasionally express our dissatisfaction with scratches, and our pets, in turn, are willing to dutifully endure the fact that we try to dress them in cute, in our opinion, clothes. Although, usually, these are just pretty little things. But in cold weather, the pet may actually need clothes. For a cat with your own hands, you can make it fast enough. We will give some recommendations thanks to which you will succeed.

Clothing for a cat: we make an outfit with our own hands

First you need to decide what exactly you want from such things. It can be just outfits for photos - and then they must be bright and cute to look beautiful in the picture. Or there is a second option: they should be practical and perform protective functions - to protect the pet from the cold or possible injuries. Therefore, clothing for cats can be made of various materials.



A few requirements for things:

  • They should have been very comfortable, not to constrain movements, not to press, and in no case to interfere.
  • Clothing for a cat, sewn or knitted, should absolutely fit her and not fall off her on the go.

If you have not tried sewing clothes for yourself before, then you may have problems. Of course, you can always make two pairs of holes in old golfs, but here you will need to try, because these holes need to be swept aside so that they do not crawl. True, now there are quite a few offers of ready-made things so that you do not have to think about how to sew clothes for a cat. And it is quite affordable.

We make a thing

But if you are prone to creativity and still decide to do everything yourself, you need to study this issue thoroughly, perhaps even practice it for a start, on some unnecessary materials. Patterns for such things can be found on forums or in specialized literature. We take the model we like and get to work. It is better to wash, dry and iron the fabric from which we will sew, in order to make sure that it does not shed and does not sit down in the future. So, clothes for a cat ... With their own hands, it is cut out, as a rule, from a material folded in half, but inside out. To avoid displacement, cleave the fabric with pins.

First, we make patterns of the largest parts. Do not forget to leave an allowance for the seam - about one centimeter. On the back we leave it depending on the type of fastener. Zipper is just a hem, but Velcro and buttons are sewn with an overlap. The direction of the shared thread should go parallel to the bend. Paper patterns are laid out upside down. Next, we sweep away parts, starting with larger ones. We do this with large stitches, a thread of contrasting color. And you can start trying on. If everything fits, then we sew on a typewriter, after having swept over the edges. If something pulls, then a little let go. Make the fasteners out of the reach of the teeth and claws of the pet.



If you do not have sewing skills, but you knit tolerably well, and there are no problems, in special publications you will find many outfit patterns for your favorites, it will also describe which threads and knitting needles are most suitable. Therefore, choose your favorite model and go for it.

Conclusion

And if you are concerned about the question of how to make clothes for cats beautiful, then everything is as simple as that. You can decorate it with embroidery or sew ready-made jewelry or initially choose material of bright colors. Or you can associate something with the picture. The main thing is to apply imagination and patience. Now you know what clothes for cats are. With our own hands, as we see, it is not difficult to make it.
