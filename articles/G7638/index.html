Hotel "Cleopatra" (Hurghada, Egypt): description, photos and reviews of tourists

If you want to spend an excellent vacation on the coast of the delightful Red Sea and prefer comfortable accommodation, then the Cleopatra Hotel (Makadi Bay, Hurghada) can be an excellent option.



Location

This five-star hotel is located in Makadi Bay, 25 kilometers from the center of Hurghada, on the very shore of the Red Sea. The nearest airport is 30 kilometers away.

Description and photo

Hotel Cleopatra (Hurghada) is located on a large territory of 300 thousand square meters. meters. Its housing stock includes 530 rooms, which can simultaneously accommodate 1,132 guests. The apartments are located in several two- and three-story buildings, surrounded by a garden. The rooms here are presented in the following categories: standard, family, executive suite and junior suite.

Hotel Cleopatra (Hurghada) has at its disposal several swimming pools (including heated), sun terraces, bars and restaurants (buffet and √† la carte), spa center (saunas, hammam, cosmetology procedures, massage room), diving center, gift shop, tour desk, conference room, gym, sports grounds and tennis courts, as well as much more. A team of animators works here throughout the day. Meals are organized on an all-inclusive basis.

The hotel has its own sandy beach with a pier and a pontoon. Near the coast is a coral reef.





According to tourists, this hotel complex is perfect for a wide variety of vacationers - family people with children, senior citizens and youth.
