Rest in the Omsk region. Recreation facilities in the Omsk region

Despite the not very favorable climate, you can spend a good rest in the Omsk region. There is a large selection of recreation centers, boarding houses and resorts, different in price, location and quality. Some of the resort areas of Omsk are located in the immediate vicinity of the city, while others are located in its regional centers.



Places of rest in the Omsk region

  • Krasnoyarsk.

Black ray

Perhaps one of the most popular vacation spots in Omsk. This village is located 50 kilometers from the city of Omsk, in a pine forest, on the picturesque bank of the Irtysh.

Clean air, the opportunity to swim in the warm river waves, places for walking provide a wonderful holiday in the Omsk region. Chernoluchye is a small village. You can get here from the bus station: about once an hour shuttle buses leave here. You can also buy a ticket for a bus at the box office, which runs on schedule, starting from 7 in the morning with an interval of 2-3 hours.

Chernoluchye offers a variety of cost recreation centers. In the Omsk region in general, prices for accommodation in boarding houses and hotels are not so high.

Holiday Village "Rainbow"

It consists of 3 three-story buildings, there is also the so-called "Village House". The rooms of four classes: economy, junior suite, suite and VIP. In all rooms without exception - a bathroom, a bathroom. The cost of living for one person in the cheapest room and with four meals a day is 1300 rubles. Further prices rise. You can rent a double room with four meals a day for two for 4000 rubles. The recreation center offers the widest range of entertainment: table tennis, badminton, sauna, bathhouse. The complex has a swimming pool with sun loungers, barbecue facilities and much more.

Park Hotel "Dream"

It is one building in which there are 36 luxury rooms and 6 cottages in which there are rooms of different classes. The cost of the cheapest double room with meals is about 5000 rubles.

The recreation center has a restaurant, a bar, a cinema, a bathhouse, a sauna, bowling, billiards and much more. "Dream" offers relaxation in the Omsk region at a European level.

Sanatorium "Russian Forest"

There are three classes of rooms in the sanatorium: standard, improved and improved with a bathroom and shower. The cost depends on comfort, number of people, includes treatment or not. Sanatorium services: speleotherapy, heat therapy, physiotherapy exercises, massage, mud and hydrotherapy. For children under three years of age, the rest is free, and there are also discounts for senior citizens.

Hotel complex "Fairy Tale"

This is a complex of buildings of different classes. The rooms are very diverse, accommodating up to 6 people. There are special family or wedding rooms. All rooms, without exception, have a TV, a bathroom, a shower. In the zone of the complex there are billiards, basketball, volleyball courts. All necessary sports equipment is rented. This is an opportunity to spend an active holiday in the Omsk region. "Fairy Tale" also offers to play paintball or visit the large indoor water park with a jacuzzi and hydromassage. On the territory there is a restaurant where you can hold any banquet.



Krasnoyarsk

A small village near Omsk. You can get here by personal transport along the Krasnoyarsk tract or from the bus station. At intervals of 60-30 minutes, minibuses and buses go from here.

Health complex "Admiral Makarov"

The complex has billiards, a medium-sized pool, a bathhouse and a sauna. Here you can spend a good family vacation. There are many opportunities for young people. Various sports equipment is rented: bicycles, balls, rackets, etc.

Sanatorium "Kolos"

Located in a mixed forest, 50 kilometers from Omsk. Sanatorium services: physiotherapy, acupuncture, massage therapy, balneotherapy, mud therapy, spa treatments. There are special offers for men and women. In the sanatorium "Kolos" you can not only improve your health, but also take a course of anti-aging therapy for face and body skin. A number of procedures are also performed to improve the condition of the scalp and hair. This is a rest for body and soul.

Holiday Village "Artyn"

It is located 200 kilometers from Omsk, near the village of Artyn. Represents several Finnish houses, well insulated. Tourist parking "Linevo"

Linevo is one of the five lakes. It has a perfectly round shape. The water in the lake is very clear, bluish in color. On the territory of the lake there are guest houses, you can also settle in tents. Excursion programs are offered in Okunevo, across all five lakes. Here you can go to the bathhouse, ride catamarans, take a walk through the forest, take a swim in the lake. There are evening discos on the shore, there is a bar and karaoke. The water in the lake warms up well, so swimming in it is very comfortable. In some parts of the coast there is white healing clay. In the forest around the lake you can see squirrels, pick berries.

Guest house "Okunevsky"

Rest houses in the Omsk region are particularly comfortable, and Okunevsky is no exception. This is a wooden one-story building in a rustic style, located in the village of Okunevo. This village is world famous as the energy center of the whole world. When everyone believed that the end of the world would come, people believed that only this place would be preserved. Pilgrims from all over the world come here. Many houses have already been bought by foreigners. The atmosphere of the village is amazing. All the people in her are kind and friendly. Guest house "Ermak"

Located in close proximity to Okunevo, in a picturesque location on the banks of the Irtysh. This house is fully equipped for a good holiday. There is a bar, a hookah bar. On the territory there is a bathhouse with a swimming pool. Here you can hunt, go fishing, and relax with a barbecue or barbecue, and ski or ice skate, snowmobile and scooter. In general, the rest here will be good both in winter and in summer.



Recreation center "Biathlon"

It is located on the banks of the Shaitanka River. There are constantly held discos and various sports competitions. The rental is given all the necessary equipment. On the territory there is a bathhouse, ice rink. Special trips are organized to the lakes Danilovo, Schuchye, Linevo, Shaitan Lake and to the village of Okunevo.

The farmstead "Gribanovo"

This is a small house-hotel with only about 10 rooms. You can come here in winter to go skating and skiing. In summer you can walk through the woods, relax in the gazebo, make a barbecue, play tennis, paintball, billiards. On the estate there is a bathhouse, a sauna with a swimming pool, a restaurant.



Hotel complex "Serebryany Bor"

It consists of three buildings: the main building and two cottages of different classes. A distinctive feature of Silverpine is the presence of stylized numbers. There are rooms in the style of "Europe", "Africa", "Greece", "Marrakesh", etc. On the territory there is a restaurant, several baths and saunas. Those who wish can ride horses, play football, billiards, ride bicycles. In winter, skis and skates are available for hire.
