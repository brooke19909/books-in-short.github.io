SEC Talisman in Izhevsk: address, description and services

SEC "Talisman" in Izhevsk is located in the city center. Therefore, getting here from any area will not be problematic. The building of the complex was built in accordance with all international requirements in 2009. SEC "Talisman" in Izhevsk is the largest institution of this type. Here are concentrated the most popular shops, cafes and entertainment centers in the city.

Where is?

The mall is located on the street. Kholmogorova, 11. The building consists of three above ground floors and one underground. Around the center is equipped with a spacious parking lot, which can accommodate up to 840 cars. In the underground - up to 192.

How to get to the Talisman shopping center in Izhevsk? On personal vehicles, you need to move towards the center. By public transport - by buses No. 28, 29, 18, 52.

The Talisman shopping center in Izhevsk is open daily from 9:00 to 22:00. And only the Kinomax cinema has a different mode - from 9:00 to 03:00.

The shops

The mall houses the majority of popular outlets and brands in the city:

  • Hypermarket "Carousel" - here customers are invited to purchase goods for every taste - from food to bedding and utensils;
  • "M. Video" - this trading network selling electronic goods and household appliances is considered one of the largest throughout Russia;
  • "Mango" - a famous brand of fashionable Spanish clothing;
  • "Adidas" - a world-famous brand of sporting goods;
  • Lime - a famous brand of fashionable and stylish women's clothing and accessories;
  • Gerry Weber is one of the modern clothing brands of any size, even the most extraordinary;
  • Geox - a brand for the manufacture and sale of home clothes and shoes;
  • Chester - a store selling quality shoes for every taste;
  • Pandora is a well-known brand for the manufacture and sale of Danish jewelry.

This is not the whole list of outlets that operate on the territory of the Talisman shopping center in Izhevsk. Every month the number of existing stores is increasing.

Entertainment centers

Modern residents of the city come here not only to make purchases, but also to relax and have fun. The complex has several entertainment centers that can be visited by the whole family.

Play Day is a playground for children where young visitors can actively spend time. In this area there is a large soft labyrinth with all kinds of slides and a dry pool. There are also gaming machines for children of different ages.

Trampolines, carousels and other small attractions are set in the play area. This center also has a room for mother and baby. There are several cafes in the games area where parents can relax while their child is engaged in active games.

Kinomax is a modern cinema halls with 3D technology. The center has 8 such facilities. Only here in the city are the premieres of various films and cartoons. The halls are equipped with modern comfortable chairs, popcorn and various soft drinks are sold.

On the territory of the shopping center there are several restaurants and cafes. There are branches of some banks in Russia and terminals are installed to replenish a mobile account on the phone.
