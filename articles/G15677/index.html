Modular origami machine: assembly diagram

The assembly scheme of the product is very simple and understandable, especially if you consider it step by step. So, even a child will independently perform this process.

We will prepare paper fragments for the further creation of the figure. Since this is a machine of modules, origami, the scheme of which is striking, you should use bright and rich colors. So, for wheels and strokes you will need black parts, for glasses and metal parts - white or blue options, and choose the base of the machine based on personal taste. In our case, red.





  1. We begin the assembly process from the bottom edge of the picture. To do this, we carefully consider the scheme, consider how many modules of different colors we will need, and put them close together on a flat surface. Usually the first row is not fixed. But if it is more convenient for you, then coat the parts with each other and carefully lay out in the correct order.
  2. It's time to start the next row. Modular origami assembly schemes are specially created in such a way that the buyer, whether he is an avid creator of paper paintings or just a beginner who is interested, is simply and understandable while working with a picture. Therefore, you can easily find out what is needed for the second row and fix the parts in the right places. Again, if it is more convenient for you, fix it with glue, and then dry the remaining fragments in the desired position so that extra bends and creases do not form.
  3. So, row by row we recreate the car according to the scheme. The number of modules should not be changed throughout the process, and if this happened, then it is worth recounting all the previous rows and, having found an error, disassemble to the desired row and fix it.
  4. When all rows are finished, you can carefully glue the back side to give the product strength and strength. As it turned out, the origami ‚ÄúMachine‚Äù of modules, the scheme and the whole process of creating which is unusually simple, can be folded independently.
