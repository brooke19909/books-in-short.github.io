Sea Breeze Hotel 2 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): hotel reviews

Sea Breeze Hotel 2 * is a modern hotel that offers an inexpensive and comfortable stay near the public sandy beach.





Brief description of the resort hotel

Sea Breeze Hotel 2 * offers its guests comfortable accommodation near the public beach. The rooms of this hotel, although modest, can give the traveler comfort and a sense of domestic warmth. It provides the most necessary set of furniture, as well as modern amenities and appliances, without which no tourist can imagine his vacation.

Resort Sea Breeze Hotel 2 * has developed an excellent infrastructure for the comfort of its guests. So, the central place in the hotel is occupied by the reception, which is responsible not only for the resettlement of guests, but also for solving problems and issues that arise in the process of living and relaxing. It is important to have your own car parking, where you can leave your rented vehicles. You can also rent a bike for temporary use here. It is also important that tourists enjoy the use of free wireless Internet.

Of great importance during any vacation is food. The same applies to staying at the Sea Breeze Hotel 2 *. The price of the tour includes delicious and hearty breakfasts. Also, looking at the extensive menu, you can try the local cuisine, which may seem exotic to you. In addition, the chefs offer you many familiar European dishes. If you want to drink a refreshing or alcoholic drink, as well as enjoy a cocktail and light snacks, then the bar will provide you with such an opportunity.





Resort Location

Hotel Sea Breeze Hotel 2 * is located very close to the public beach of Pham Wam Dong. Thanks to this convenient location, you can also visit a number of nearby attractions. So, Cape Hon-Chong is 1 km away from the hotel, and Banshee Po-Nagar-Cham is 2 km away. Also, after driving 3 km, you can plunge into the hot thermal springs of Tap Ba. The airport is also not very far away - only 32 km.

Guest rooms

Hotel Sea Breeze Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang) has a number of rooms, which includes the following categories of apartments:

  • Standard rooms offer double occupancy. Since they have only one large bed, these apartments are well suited for a romantic getaway.
  • Another variety of standard apartments can be considered four-bed. This is one spacious room with two large beds. Some rooms of this modification provide sea views from the windows and are considered superior.
  • The hotel also has huge family rooms, which are also great for relaxing with large groups. Unlike other hotels where such apartments are designed for 4 people, six guests can accommodate here.




Hotel facilities

One of the favorite travel destinations for travelers is Vietnam. Hotel Sea Breeze Hotel 2 * can offer its guests comfortable accommodation, as well as access to the use of a huge number of infrastructure facilities:

  • You have the opportunity to rent a bicycle to explore the city or go to nearby attractions;
  • the entire resort is covered by wireless Internet coverage;
  • you can leave your vehicles in a free guarded parking lot;
  • since the reception is open around the clock, at any time you can contact the hotel with questions you are interested in or to solve problems that arise during the stay;
  • since the hotel rooms are compact enough, you can hand over your bulky suitcases to the left luggage office so that your luggage does not take up too much space;
  • in the laundry you can order not only the laundry service, but also ironing;
  • the hotel has an elevator to make it convenient for guests to get to the rooms located on the upper floors; on the territory there are special places and rooms for smoking.




Positive hotel reviews

A great option for a budget holiday can be considered a resort hotel Sea Breeze Hotel 2 *. Reviews of tourists say that such accommodation has a lot of advantages:

  • the hotel is located quite conveniently relative to the sea in a very quiet and calm area;
  • friendly and polite staff;
  • good menu in the restaurant, quite affordable prices.




Negative Resort Reviews

The following negative aspects are present in the work of this hotel:

  • most standard rooms with a double bed do not have windows, which is not indicated in the brochures (when checking in and booking, be careful and specify this information in advance);
  • due to the lack of windows in some rooms (or too small), the apartments are very warm and damp, which contributes to the appearance of an unpleasant odor, mold, as well as a huge number of insects;
  • even if you are not satisfied with your number, you will not be able to replace it (reception staff refuse to do this even if a supplement is paid);
  • the apartments are incredibly small - it is impossible to approach the bed from any direction, and there is practically nowhere to put bags, which forces you to use a paid storage room;
  • construction is being carried out literally next to this hotel, which is why the rooms are always noisy and dusty, and around the hotel it is sloppy and dirty;
  • the walls of the rooms are whitewashed with lime, which is why there is a great risk of staining clothes (and especially given the small area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe rooms);
  • since the water is heated from the sun in the tanks on the roof, you can not count on a hot shower in the early morning or in cloudy weather;
  • Room cleaning is very poor, and bed linen and towels for the entire period of rest never change.

This hotel is a rather specific institution. Special care should be taken when booking a room. Be sure to check the availability of windows in the apartment. An undoubted advantage of this hotel can be considered that it is located in a quiet area and is located quite close to the sea.
