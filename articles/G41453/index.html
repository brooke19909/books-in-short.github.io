Shelekhov city, water park: photos, opening hours, opening date

Recently, water parks have become extremely popular not only in the USA, where entertainment was invented, but also throughout the world. Europeans consider visiting this institution a mandatory attribute of a healthy lifestyle. Especially popular are water parks, combined with large shopping centers (shopping and entertainment centers). Here, guests can spend an entire day entertaining, combining water slide trips with activities in the game rooms, etc.





More recently, water parks have become widespread in Russia. In almost every relatively small settlement of the Russian Federation there is a similar water attraction. Almost always, they are crowded with visitors. According to reviews, the water park in Shelekhov (a city in the Irkutsk region) is no exception. The opening date of the water park in Shelekhov is February 2002. A visit to this water attraction has long been popular among locals who come here in any season.

On the popularity of the water park in the city of Shelekhov

If you do not know where in Shelekhov you can usefully and have fun with your whole weekend, we recommend that you go to a local water park. The water park in Shelekhov (photos are presented in the article) is located at the address: 20th quarter, 100, stop. "Builder" in the city municipal unitary enterprise "Health Center". The institution will provide an interesting pastime for everyone.

Mom with children can splash around in the pool or bask in the sauna, dad will enjoy playing billiards or working out in the gym, and grandmother can be offered a visit to the dental office or massage therapist.

It is known that in Shelekhov there are no open natural reservoirs, therefore this entertainment quickly gained fame and popularity among the townspeople, as well as residents of the region. And this is not surprising. A visit to the water park is a great opportunity for everyone to have a great time with their family.

Infrastructure

In addition to the swimming pools, which are loved by both children and adults, the water park infrastructure also includes a jacuzzi with hydromassage and a sauna. Wi-Fi is available throughout the entertainment center. The water park also has its own free parking. There is a pool table, a rental center for laps, swimming caps, inflatable toys and vests.





What to do with little guests?

If the visitors are still very young and do not know how to swim, and parents want to escape from their worries and at least have a little rest, the kids can be left in a soft children's room with the "talking" name "Fidget" under the supervision of a qualified teacher. There is everything necessary for a fun and interesting pastime of the child. For example, a slide descending into a pool that is filled not with water but with balls. This service is appreciated by visitors.

Pools

In the water park (Shelekhov) several pools are offered at once. In one of them you can just swim, and in the other you can ride from one of the two slides. This pool is also equipped with luminous fountains, causing indescribable delight in children.



Immediately before visiting the pool, all visitors to the water park must undergo a medical examination. This is a prerequisite for all guests, due to the need to ensure the safety of both children and pregnant women.

A visit to the water park in Shelekhov will be useful for the ladies: on Tuesdays and Thursdays, from 17:45 to 18:45, they are invited to aerobics. In addition, classes for pregnant women are held. So that the children do not get bored while their mothers are engaged, they can be sent to have fun in the spacious game maze. For those who want to learn to swim or improve their swimmer skills, the Shelekhov water park on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 9:45 to 17:45 provides appropriate training.

The number of visitors to the pool is strictly controlled by the administration, so it is never crowded. After swimming in the Shelekhov water park, you can visit the solarium, vertical or horizontal, after which you can have a tasty snack in a local cafe, whose menu always has a lot of children's dishes.



What do you need to carry with you?

Those wishing to visit the water park should stock up on:

  • washcloth;
  • soap
  • a towel;
  • replaceable shoes;
  • swimming cap;
  • swimsuit.

Do not be upset if anything from this list was not with you. Missing can be rented. A physical examination is also carried out on site.

Wellness

In the wellness center, guests are invited to enjoy some types of massage (reservation is required):

  • general;
  • relaxing;
  • anti-cellulite.

In addition, a specialist can massage problem areas:

  • cervical-collar;
  • back
  • lower limbs;
  • upper limbs;
  • thoracic region;
  • a head;
  • feet.

For those wishing to undergo wellness treatments, it is proposed to use a massage couch, massage chair and a low-frequency myostimulation belt.

Shelekhov, water park: mode of operation

The pool is open from 10:00 to 22:00. Prices for entrance tickets to the water park depend on the day of the week and time of visit. The cost of tickets on weekdays from 10:00 to 19:00 and on weekends and holidays from 10:00 to 12:00 is 150 rubles per hour. From 19:00 to 22:00 on weekdays and from 12:00 to 22:00 on weekends and holidays you will have to pay more - 350 rubles per hour.



Children under 5 years of age upon presentation of a birth certificate or a copy thereof are admitted free of charge.

Other rates

Paid is also the rental of hats, circles, etc., a compulsory medical examination, a visit to the gym, children's room and billiards. Cost:

  • classes in the gym without the presence of an instructor - 120 rubles .;
  • subscription - 1000 rubles. in 10 hours;
  • classes with an instructor - 150 rubles .;
  • subscription - 1300 rubles. in 10 hours;
  • Solarium - 15 rubles. per min .;
  • billiards - 150 rubles .;
  • visits to the game room - 120 rubles .;
  • medical examination (without issuing a certificate) - 20 rubles .;
  • rental (towels, slippers, hats, sheets, rubber ring, life jacket, toys and other) - 30 rubles. per unit

Additional Information

Those wishing to visit the water park at the weekend must sign up in advance. Children under 3 years old are allowed into the pool exclusively with a waterproof diaper (sold at the Center). The depth of the children's pool is up to 80 cm.

Reviews

Mostly visitors about their stay at the Shelekhov water park respond very positively. Guests love to come here as a family on holidays and weekends. The authors of the reviews note that in the water park it is interesting for both children and adults. Some visitors respond negatively to visiting the water park. But their assessments are purely personal in nature. Some are dissatisfied with the behavior of individual guests, others consider prices to be too high, etc.

Most reviewers admit that they are regulars at this entertainment center. Many people like the fact that the water park in Shelekhov is never overcrowded. The administration clearly monitors the implementation of the established regulations, so visitors have the necessary comfortable conditions for relaxation. The reviewers are pleased to note the presence of a mandatory physical examination, and also report the harmlessness of swimming in the local pools for both children and expectant mothers (the water purification system in the water park does not include chlorine).

The regulars of the water park urge residents of Shelekhov and guests of the city to arrange fun and health holidays more often and wish that leisure time in this entertainment center would become a real family tradition of the townspeople.
