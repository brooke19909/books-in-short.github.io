Apple story

Apple is one of the most expensive today. It has a tremendous impact on the development of society along with such giants as Microsoft, Coca-Cola, IBM.

It was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs and Robert Wayne. At first, none of them was sure about the future of the company. But over time, the position strengthened and grew, in 1977 the first computer was released.

Apple has become an innovator in many areas. When the first iPhone went on sale at the beginning of the 21st century, Steve Jobs called it the exclusive work of the apple company.

The main breakthrough was in 1996. If before that Apple tried itself in different directions, then after the restructuring, it decided to focus on the manufacture of portable and stationary computers iMac and iBook.

More than 300 employees were laid off during the same period. Thanks to the decisive actions of the management, the company has become what it is now known. And in 2007, the first iPhone was released, which had the effect of an exploding bomb.

Due to its popularity and growing sales, Apple stocks are now in high demand.

Expert Advice

Many do not understand how and how much they need to buy securities, what to do if Apple shares have fallen in price. When calculating medium-term stock holdings, the most promising strategy is to use the Buy & Hold strategy. That is, you can buy stocks and store them long enough in order to make a substantial profit.

During medium-term storage, possible drop in quotes is smoothed out, which favorably affects the amount of your earnings. If you have a significant amount of money and are ready to purchase a lot of shares, then it is best to follow the short term. For a profitable sale, it is most reasonable to resort to a technical and fundamental analysis of the market, which will help to form a complete picture.

If you decide to use a long-term or medium-term strategy, then always remember that today in many countries the government raises taxes and actively fights offshore organizations and legal entities. Ways to buy company shares

If you have calculated and considered everything, then you will probably be puzzled by the Apple stock purchase system. How to buy shares to an individual who does not have any relations with brokers? No wonder we live in the age of Internet technology. Now you can buy anything with the help of a worldwide network, including promotions.

There are a huge number of online stock stores. You need to choose your company carefully: read customer reviews, study registration information, TIN and other available data. You can make a purchase using a bank card or electronic wallet.

Another way to purchase shares is to buy them directly through the stock market. This is the most obvious and understandable way. Apple shares are listed on all major exchanges. You can contact AMEX, NYSE or LSE. Recent amendments to the law allowed individuals to make financial transactions with foreign funds, so shares can be purchased without problems in just a couple of minutes.

To purchase on some exchanges, you first need to conclude an agreement with a broker who acts as an intermediary between the buyer and the exchange. Please note that not all of them are licensed for foreign trade.

The last way is to buy them using binary options. If the securities themselves can be quite expensive, you can buy binary options without even having a lot of capital.

What it is? This is a contract with a specific exchange, which is used to generate income from asset value movements. Some experts consider this not the most reliable method. But then it allows you to start investing even with a small amount.
