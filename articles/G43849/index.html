Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): reviews, photos

In the quiet sea bay of Nabq Bay, among the green gardens, the chic hotel The Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * is spread. Photos taken by tourists demonstrate the luxury of the rooms and the well-groomed area. There is always an atmosphere of carefree fun and leisurely relaxation.

Hotel Summary

Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * hotel has all the necessary attributes of a complete and vibrant vacation. Comfortable rooms with a full range of all necessary furniture and appliances, blue clean pools, and a private sandy beach make your stay in the hotel complex comfortable and cozy. Special attention deserves a network of restaurants operating on the territory of this hotel. Here you can not only have a delicious dinner, but also have a good time enjoying the oriental atmosphere and live music.

Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * hotel is a complex of several one-, two- and three-story buildings. It has been operating since 2002, and already in 2005 a partial reconstruction was carried out.





Where is the hotel situated

Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * hotel is located very close to Sharm El Sheikh International Airport - just 5 km. Naama Bay Resort Center is 20 km away. The nearest beach will have to be overcome 2 km. You can walk this distance or use the free bus.

Rooms

331 spacious cozy room is available in the Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * hotel. Standard is the largest and most demanded category of apartments, which is designed to accommodate two guests (it is also possible to provide two additional beds). The room is a bright bedroom with a bathroom and a cozy balcony.





Family apartments consist of two rooms, interconnected by an internal door. A large and two small beds are designed to accommodate 4 guests, and it is also possible to occupy two more people by placing them on a soft sofa or folding beds. One of the biggest advantages of this room is that each of the bedrooms is combined with a private bathroom, which creates additional convenience when relaxing with a large family.

Junior Suite is an apartment of a superior category of comfort. Here, in addition to the spacious bedroom, there is also a living room with a large set of upholstered furniture. A spacious terrace on which comfortable wicker chairs are placed deserves special attention.





Infrastructure facilities

Entertainment is one of the main goals for which tourists visit Egypt. Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * is located quite far from the benefits of civilization and entertainment facilities, so the organization of its own infrastructure plays almost the main role in creating the image of the hotel. The following facilities operate on the territory of the resort complex:

  • guests of the resort complex have the opportunity to visit two restaurants, one of which is the main dining room, and the second works on the menu;
  • there are five bars with a large selection of drinks, as well as fast food;
  • there are as many as seven outdoor pools;
  • the reception is open around the clock not only for the reception and eviction of guests, but also for appeals, requests and complaints;
  • there is a mini market where you can buy products and household chemicals, as well as many souvenir shops and stalls, a jewelry store selling products from local craftsmen is very popular;
  • in a medical center you can get first aid for injuries mlm malaise, as well as call a doctor;
  • There is a private guarded parking area;
  • laundry and dry cleaning;
  • rental of equipment and inventory for scuba diving and diving;
  • barbershop;
  • buses running to the beach and back, as well as transport to the city center;
  • a bank services point where you can exchange currency, withdraw money from a card or pay through a terminal.




Services for children

Three Corners Palmyra Resort Sharm 4 * hotel makes every effort to ensure that children's holidays in this resort are as comfortable, bright, interesting and eventful as adults. To this end, the following services are provided to young tourists:

  • Coming to rest with children 4-11 years old, parents can leave them for a day at the mini-club. Here they will not only be under the supervision of animators, but also will be able to participate in interesting game programs and competitions. The room is equipped with safe furniture and has a huge number of toys.
  • The beach has a separate play area for children, and a special animation program has been developed.
  • There is a shallow heated pool with slides.
  • Playground.
  • The children's restaurant has special high chairs.
  • It is possible to leave children with a nanny.

restaurants and bars

Special attention is paid to the nutrition of guests at Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * hotel. Guest reviews are especially noted by the following establishments:

  • The main restaurant of the hotel with a large bright room and a spacious outdoor terrace. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are held here. From time to time, restaurant chefs hold theme nights devoted to local, Italian or Asian cuisines.
  • The brand restaurant on its menu offers dishes from different nations of the world.
  • The lobby bar has a large assortment of desserts, as well as tea and coffee.
  • The disco bar hosts dance evenings.
  • Several poolside bars offer refreshing drinks and snacks.




All inclusive

The all-inclusive system at the Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * hotel implies:

  • breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main restaurant;
  • in the afternoon - snacks and sweets in the lobby bar;
  • from 10 a.m. to midnight in all bars alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are provided free of charge (except for juices and wine), as well as cocktails and snacks;
  • mineral water, tea / coffee and refreshing carbonated drinks are provided on the beach.




Positive reviews

Thousands of tourists who annually relax at the Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 * hotel. Reviews about it allow you to make a complete picture, which contains both positive and negative sides. Perhaps you should start with the advantages of this hotel complex, which can be described as follows:

  • a large beautiful area, where there is where to walk, as well as take original photographs as a keepsake;
  • several large pools, so there is a lot of space for comfortable swimming;
  • free wireless Internet near the reception;
  • immediately upon arrival at the hotel (regardless of whether the guests are already registered or not) a special bracelet is issued, which gives the right to use the All Inclusive system;
  • you need to hand in the bracelet only when leaving the hotel, even if it occurs several hours later than the delivery of the room keys;
  • the hotel staff is very responsive and quickly enough responds to all questions, requests or complaints;
  • due to the fact that the hotel is located far from the beach, a special bus is provided that runs between the main building and the sea coast;
  • in the pools there are high-quality water slides, in connection with which there is no need to visit paid attractions in water parks;
  • daily, comfortable buses take tourists absolutely free to the center of Naama Bay, to Sharm El Sheikh and vice versa, despite the fact that the hotel is located near the airport (which can already be considered a positive point);
  • high-level staff speaks Russian;
  • a beautiful beach with coral reefs and many exotic fish;
  • many bars with a diverse assortment of drinks and snacks;
  • due to the fact that in the room where the disco is held, good isolation, loud music does not bother guests;
  • relatively fresh repairs and good furniture in the rooms;
  • a lot of greenery and flowers in the territory;
  • Pleasant clean and well-kept pools, the water in which practically does not emit the smell of bleach.




Negative reviews

As you know, it is impossible to organize everything perfectly due to a number of factors specific to the tourism industry. Naturally, there are some shortcomings in the work of Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 *. Reviews note the following disadvantages:

  • the hotel is too far from the sea;
  • the private beach owned by the hotel is very small, which often causes crowds;
  • there are few lighting devices on the hotel territory, and therefore moving around it at night is quite uncomfortable and unsafe;
  • too few fruits on the menu, which is unacceptable for a country with a tropical climate;
  • monotonous diet;
  • without a tip it is impossible to achieve quality room service;
  • poor animation and boring entertainment;
  • during cleaning, hotel staff do not report items of a cosmetic set for a shower;
  • high noise levels from take-off and landing aircraft;
  • poor lighting in the rooms;
  • untidy area around the hotel.

Useful hotel information

All hotels are simultaneously similar and different from each other. This feature is associated not only with the interior and service system, but also with some internal rules in accordance with which the hotel operates. Going to Three Corners Palmyra Resort 4 *, it is useful to familiarize yourself with the following information:

  • Check-in and check-out are carried out at 14:00 and 12:00, respectively.
  • Children under 12 years old get extra bed for free. Subject to check-in, two children are not charged only if they have not yet turned 6. Otherwise, a fee of half the cost of an adult voucher is taken.
  • Each room can accommodate two extra beds.
  • When going to the hotel with a child, be sure to inform the property in advance, especially if you require a cot.
  • In accordance with the all-inclusive system, drinks are available to guests from 10 a.m. to midnight. Fresh juices and wine are not covered by this program - they are subject to an additional charge.
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring any pets to the hotel.
