Golden Dragon Hotel 2 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

Golden Dragon Hotel 2 * is a typical inexpensive Vietnamese hotel that can be ranked both as a city and a beach. And this is not surprising. Since it is located almost in the center of Nha Trang resort, and not in the park or in the vicinity, it can be attributed to the city. But proximity to the sea suggests that people come here not so much on business or on a business trip, but on vacation. Why? Now let's figure it out.

Tours in Nha Trang

This name is familiar to all lovers of sea holidays. After all, this city is the king of Vietnamese beaches. Three of them are considered the best in the country. In addition, the bay in which this resort is located is literally dotted with small islands, which, in addition to the exceptional beauty of the landscape, is also of practical importance - the waves on the shore are not so large, so the average and even beginner swimmer can feel safe.





In addition, excellent weather reigns here all year round, although typhoons sometimes come over. But there is no pronounced rainy season. Therefore, tours are often organized in Nha Trang - beach, sightseeing, wellness, diving. Often, the Golden Dragon Hotel 2 * becomes the base for travelers who have chosen a package vacation with an operator. Pegast ("Pegas Touristik") and other companies, as a rule, offer accommodation in this hotel for a week, ten nights or fourteen days.

Nha Trang Hotels

As in any resort, there are chic five-star palaces, for example, the famous "Winperl Resort" or a hotel located in the former villa of the last emperor Bao Dai. Three- and four-star hotels, as a rule, have a large territory, good infrastructure and excellent breakfasts, but they are not very cheap. Therefore, the most popular and popular in the resort are mini-hotels, such as Golden Dragon Hotel 2 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang). They can provide customers with almost the same amenities as treshki, but at a lower price. They are located in quiet places and are aimed not at wealthy tourists, but at local residents.

How to get there and what is nearby?

To come to Nha Trang, you first need to get to Ho Chi Minh City. From Russia to this city flights of many airlines fly, both direct and with transfers. From there you can get on local planes to Kamran Airport, and a transfer will deliver you to the hotel. Another option is to use the services of intercity buses, and they will take you to Nha Trang not only from Ho Chi Minh City, but also from Hanoi. The railway connection is well developed here. Not only ordinary trains, but also express trains run from Ho Chi Minh City to Nha Trang and back.





This hotel is located in the southern part of Nha Trang, in a comfortable and picturesque area, built up with fairly budget hotels. There are very clean and relatively deserted beaches. Tue Tinh Lane, on which the Golden Dragon Hotel 2 * stands, is very quiet and calm, and walking from the hotel to the sea is just a few minutes. Tourists consider this location to be very successful. Opposite the lane is the so-called Gorky Park with rides and pools. The center is about seven minutes walk.

The hotel is a five-story building with an elevator. Near it is a pond where the turtle lives. Near the hotel you can find a cheap and good massage parlor, as well as an excellent coffee shop, open from half past six in the morning. Literally through two streets there is a night market, which is open daily.
