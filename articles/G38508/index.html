Bodrum Bay Resort (Turkey, Bodrum): traveler reviews

Turkey is one of the most accessible countries for tourists. It combines a pleasant climate, snow-white beaches, high-class service and, of course, oriental exoticism. A traveler can relax in one of the huge hotel complexes, taking advantage of all the benefits of modern civilization, and enjoy an interesting history, study the local culture and traditions.

The coast of Turkey is replete with resorts of different sizes and categories. One of the most popular cities is Bodrum - here tourists can not only relax, but also fully enjoy excursions and the study of antiquities. Of course, there are more than enough hotels here, but many experienced travelers recommend staying at the Bodrum Bay Resort hotel complex.

Of course, before the trip, you should learn more about the chosen stopping place. What is a hotel like? What type of vacation is it suitable for? What do former guests say about him? The answers to these questions are sought by many tourists who are first going to spend a vacation in the resort.

Hotel Location Information





It's no secret that when choosing a hotel, its location plays an important role, because each traveler has his own preferences and requirements. Bodrum Bay Resort is located in the cozy resort village of Bardrum, just 1.5 km from the famous Bodrum resort.

In walking distance of tourists the most famous attractions of the surroundings, including St. Peter's Fortress and windmills. Milos International Airport is about 39 km away. Ready tours, as a rule, already include a convenient transfer from the airport. In any case, the road to the hotel will take no more than 40 minutes.

What does the territory look like? Description of infrastructure

Bodrum Bay Resort 5 is a large hotel complex located on a hillside. The hotel consists of one main six-story building and twenty two-story bungalows. The total area is 57 thousand square meters.





According to reviews, the hotel grounds are very beautiful - there are sports fields, colorful flowerbeds, and cozy terraces, which offer amazing views of the Aegean Sea. By the way, the hotel has been hosting guests since 1987. In 2014, a complete reconstruction was carried out here.

Tourist Accommodation: Room Categories

Bodrum Bay Resort is a relatively large hotel consisting of 268 rooms. 80 of them are standard rooms located in the main building. Their area is 28 square meters. Designed accommodation for 2, maximum 3 people.

Hotel Bodrum Bay Resort 5 *: photo of rooms and description of amenities





A good vacation is impossible to imagine without the benefits of modern civilization. What can the hotel complex Bodrum Bay Resort 5 * offer? Tophotels, or rather, the reviews left by tourists on this site, suggest that the living conditions here are quite decent. The rooms are spacious and clean with large windows and tiled floors. Guests can use their private balconies or terraces.

The room has a large comfortable bed, wardrobe, bedside tables and other necessary furniture. You can also use air conditioning, and before going to bed it is always nice to relax and watch your favorite TV show. There is a safe in the room, but it can be used at an additional cost. A minibar is also available upon request - only drinking water is provided free of charge.

Bathrooms are equipped with modern fixtures. Guests are provided with personal hygiene products, towels (changed to clean daily), as well as a hair dryer (on request).

Meals: what does the hotel offer?

Most hotels in Turkey operate on an all-inclusive scheme that is convenient for vacationers. The Bodrum Bay Resort 5 * hotel complex is no exception. Reviews indicate that the food here is at a decent level.

At the foot of the hill is a spacious restaurant, which serves buffet-style breakfast, lunch and dinner. The menu here is quite diverse - there are light salads and snacks, meat and fish dishes, freshly prepared scrambled eggs, delicious desserts and drinks. Some traditional Turkish or Mediterranean dishes are also offered.





There is also a snack bar serving light snacks and salads. Many tourists respond well to the beach bar - here visitors are offered not only chilled drinks, but also delicious desserts and homemade ice cream. In the lobby there is another bar with a large wine and cocktail menu (real Turkish coffee is also served here).

Beach and water activities for guests

The Bodrum Bay Resort hotel complex has its own coastal area. Platform beach is located at the foot of the hill. You can go down to it by steps, although for greater convenience the funicular works for guests.

The beach is spacious and clean, covered with soft fine sand. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient, there is a sandbank for tourists who are not too good at swimming. A sufficient number of comfortable sun loungers are located on the shore. Guests enjoy free sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses. Here you can always get a clean large towel.

As for entertainment, vacationers like to ride boats and catamarans, take boat rides, go scuba diving, try more extreme water sports, including water skiing.

Additional services offered by the hotel





When choosing a place for a vacation, tourists first of all pay attention to convenience. Residential buildings, according to reviews, are clean, beautifully decorated and comfortable. It is always a pleasure to spend time in the halls and on the terraces, and here you can also connect to the Internet, which is important for many modern travelers who want to stay in touch.

Guests enjoy free parking. By the way, if you prefer to move freely, then it is worth considering the option of renting a car or motorcycle - this service is offered on site, and the prices here are not too high.

For a small fee, clothes can be taken to the laundry room. Currency is exchanged in the main building - the rate at the hotel is not bad. For a while, unnecessary things can be left in the storage room. For an additional fee, guests often rent a small safe at the reception.

Leisure of tourists: how do guests spend their free time?





The hotel complex is ideal for people who do not want to particularly think about leisure and plan a vacation to the smallest detail. The hotel has everything in order to have fun and even usefully spend time. The terraces are equipped with spacious pools of different depths. Of course, comfortable loungers with mattresses and wide umbrellas are arranged around. Here you can swim, relax, sunbathe, while admiring the breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. By the way, in the mornings in the pool group classes in water aerobics are held, and in the daytime animators often organize fun competitions in water polo.

Former guests recommend visiting the SPA center at least once. Of course, it is worth trying on all the charms of a real Turkish bath, and after water procedures you can enjoy a relaxing massage (qualified and experienced workers offering different types of massage work in the salon). Many tourists like having a library with a good collection of books in different languages. There is a games room and a TV lounge. There are billiard tables with hourly pay. Guests have a good time playing table tennis. And in the evenings, all guests are entertained by animators, organizing vivid shows and groovy performances. Do not ignore the possibilities of excursion vacations. Turkey is a country with an interesting culture and traditions. In Bodrum itself, there are many attractions that are worth a look. Is the hotel suitable for a child?





Tourists who prefer traveling with a child should know that the Bodrum Bay Resort 5 * hotel is not intended for families. At the time of booking, the administrator will tell you that only teenagers over 16 stay in the hotel. Accordingly, parents with children are better off looking for another option.

Who is the hotel suitable for?

Who needs a rest at the Bodrum Bay Resort 5 * hotel (Bodrum, Turkey)? Ratings indicate that young people feel quite comfortable here. Fun, entertainment, noisy parties, relaxing on the beach - all this is ideal for noisy and restless youth companies.

Often, honeymooners spend their honeymoon here, and more mature couples are always able to find an interesting occupation for themselves. There are no families with children, so the noise does not bother anyone and young people can have fun from the heart. By the way, often on the territory they organize various festive events, including weddings.
