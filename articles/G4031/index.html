Extreme Park (Perm) - youth entertainment center

In Perm, not far from the railway station, there is an extreme amusement park, analogues of which in Russia at the moment do not exist. This place immediately became popular among fans to tickle their nerves and enjoy the release of a shock dose of adrenaline into the blood. With his advent, entertainment in Perm has become more diverse, and young people have an alternative to many hours of meaningless communication on social networks.





How it all began

Extreme Park (Perm) has opened its doors for fans of street sports and just for those who want to take a walk and look at an unusual architectural and technical creation in 2009.

It was designed taking into account modern trends in the field of youth urban active entertainment.

Concrete amusement park (Perm) offers all conditions for:

  • skateboarding;
  • freestyle;
  • parkour;
  • roller skating.

The project to create a modern extreme zone was developed in the American city of Louisville. The size of the site made of high quality concrete that meets all the strength characteristics is 12 thousand square meters. m

The construction and equipment of an extreme park cost 50 million rubles. And this is only according to the most preliminary estimates!





Extreme Zone Description

Upon entering an unusual park, the guest will immediately pay attention to two sculptures: the Perm period and the Tron. They were made by sculptors Andrei Barakhvostov from Nizhny Tagil and Andrius Petkus from Lithuania.

In addition, the park has a large stand with a map of the park and information that all visitors should use protective equipment in order to avoid injuries when skiing. Unfortunately, most extreme people neglect these recommendations and, through negligence, can get scratched, bruised, get dislocated or sprained, or even fractured.

The only thing that upsets is the lack of a reliable canopy that protects athletes from rain. In bad weather, this makes the extreme site unsuitable for use. In addition, during heavy rains, rain accumulates in some structures.

The "filling" of the park

Those who prefer active entertainment in Perm have everything necessary for practicing their favorite hobby or preparing for various competitions. Designers took care of this, taking as a model the most famous similar complexes in the United States.

Extreme Park (Perm) has several large-scale concrete structures designed for use by fans of extreme sports. Among them:

  • inclined pipe, which allows you to slide and jump;
  • fan box, which is a special springboard;
  • a platform having a flat approach and an inclined exit;
  • ramp - a convenient design for skiing for both beginner extreme sports lovers and professionals;
  • a pyramid representing a platform with three gentle exits;
  • a concrete face designed for side skating;
  • mini-ramp (a small platform for jumping).
