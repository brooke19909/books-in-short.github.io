How to buy an apartment if there is no money for a mortgage?

Many banks do not miss young families because of their low solvency. For example, Sberbank has strict requirements for a mortgage loan: for a family of 3 people, income must be at least 45 thousand rubles, two guarantors with no less income and no loans and a bunch of reference documents.



In this case, how to buy an apartment without money? Look for banks where the campaign for new buildings takes place (rates are higher for resales and requirements are tougher), when a family with a monthly income of 20 thousand rubles can get a mortgage for 15 years with a 12% interest rate without guarantors with a minimum package of documents (personal income tax certificate for half a year, copies of passport, work book and diploma of education). This is due to the fact that during the period of the action, banks do not take into account family expenses, but look only at profitability.

We take a mortgage, where can I get money for a contribution?

Mortgages - this is the main way how to buy an apartment if there is no money. A down payment requires a minimum of 10% of the cost of housing. Currently, there are companies that are building new buildings from 950,000 rubles. Then for the down payment it is enough to collect 95 thousand rubles.

This amount can be collected in cash on your own. For example, maternity and maternity leave benefits may amount to a down payment. If the family is expecting a second child, then it can receive maternity capital, due to which you can reduce the mortgage rate.



Once you have decided on the bank, hand over the documents and wait for the management decision. After obtaining a loan for several months (3-6), gradually collect the documents for obtaining an apartment in the property, which you will be advised by various experts from the developer and the bank.
