We knit a cardigan-cocoon with our own hands

The fashion industry offers us many interesting and original things that are so simple that they are accessible even to beginners. In the material presented below, we study the technology for making a cardigan-cocoon. The reader can choose the tool by himself. Our master class is universal and will help to fulfill the idea using both knitting needles and a hook.

Training

To create a truly beautiful product, you need to work hard at the preparatory stage. Start by choosing a pattern. In this case, each needlewoman can rely on her own taste. The main thing is to compare the rapport of the selected pattern with the calculations, the technology of which we will study a little later. And it is also worth considering that for knitting patterned cardigan-cocoons, you should choose plain yarn. In this case, it can be pastel shades or saturated acid.

If, however, a person who is just beginning to get acquainted with the basics of knitting is going to associate the studied product, it is more reasonable to focus on the unusual yarn. For example, patchwork, motley, gradient and others. In this case, it is permissible to alternate the front and back rows or to knit on both sides only with front loops.

Features of model measurement

Cardigan-cocoon, of course, refers to products called oversize. But despite this, knitting it, as they say, at random, is completely unreasonable. Therefore, further we will find out what parameters are required in order to produce a product that will fit the figure exactly. There are only two of them:

  • product width - from the base of the neck to the proposed lower edge;
  • product length - from the intended end of one sleeve to the other (hands spread apart parallel to the floor).

Loop and row calculation technology

Regardless of whether crochet or knitting a cardigan-cocoon will be performed, the previous mathematical calculations will be identical. But first you need to prepare a square sample of the selected pattern. For knitting the studied product, a fragment of 10x10 centimeters is enough.

We prepare it, count the number of loops and rows, and then divide by 10. As a result, we find out how many necessary units fit in one centimeter. After all, they will help us to knit the coveted wardrobe item, which will definitely fit the figure.

We knit a cocoon cardigan as described

With knitting or crochet, the intended product will be made, not so important. If desired, you can combine the work of two tools. For example, knitting the cuffs and collar with knitting needles, and the main part - crochet. In any case, the technology implies the following actions:

  1. We collect the number of loops equal to the width of the product.
  2. We knit a flat cloth, without making increases and decreases.
  3. When we reach the intended length, close the loop.
  4. We outline the width of the cuff in the middle of the smaller side.
  5. We take a needle with a thread and sew the fabric along the lines shown in the diagram in green.
  6. If desired, this can be completed. Or add a cardigan with cuffs and a collar.
  7. To do this, prepare the hook and pick up new loops in the necessary zones. If we continue to use knitting needles. Or immediately crochet cuffs of the desired length.
  8. In the same way we make out the gate. But in this case, we move not in a circle, but back and forth.

Cardigan from bottom to top

To tie this version of the cardigan-cocoon with a crochet or knitting needles, you should perform several other manipulations:

  1. Multiply the number of loops in one centimeter by the length of the product, and the number of rows in one centimeter by the width.
  2. We collect the calculated loops.
  3. And we knit without increases and decreases the fabric of the desired length.
  4. Then we collect it in the same way as described in the previous paragraph.
  5. However, it is important to note that if desired, the reader can make a two-color cardigan. To do this, tie the product to half, and then switch to a different thread color.
