Ski complex "Manzherok": reviews and photos

Altai Territory is known for its natural beauties both in Russia and beyond. Locals enjoy gorgeous views and the rich natural heritage of these lands. One of the Altai lakes - Manzherok - sheltered in the neighborhood of the same name ski complex "Manzherok". It is located forty-five kilometers from the village of Gorny Altaysk, at the foot of the Malaya Sinyuhi mountain. Due to the fact that a vacation surrounded by the natural beauties of Altai is pleasant in any weather, the family recreation center is open to its visitors throughout the year.



What is a review center?

Differences in altitude of one hundred and sixty-seven meters will not leave indifferent even the most avid and experienced skiers. And inexperienced beginners are waiting for a training track with a length of two hundred and fifty-five meters with a height difference of thirty-three meters. Both tracks are equipped with modern lifts. Absolutely everyone can try themselves on the cross-country skiing track.

Those who prefer sledding to skiing will also not be bored: they are waiting for a tubing track, the length of which is one hundred sixty meters, and the vertical drop is twenty meters. Tubing is a special type of sled, which is a durable rubber circles. They need to sit on top and, holding on to the handles, go down from the snowy slope. Climbing the mountain does not require effort, because at the foot awaits a tubing lift that will quickly deliver to the top. The cost of the lift is calculated by a complex system of discounts. Children under eight years of age are provided with a free climb to the top.

Reviews say that Manzherok is a tourist complex that includes a full range of services. Anyone can take the advice of an experienced mentor who will easily instruct the vacationer on snowboarding or skiing. In order to provide all tourists with equipment, a very convenient rental of equipment is available, where you can get skis, boards, special boots, glasses. Plains skiing and snowmobiling will complement the entertainment menu.



Children's recreation area

"Manzherok" is a ski resort, reviews of which are only positive. Here you can fully relax with the whole family, and everyone will have fun. The doors of the Fan Park are open for children, which is a territory fenced by a safety net, divided into separate areas. In the first section, which is educational, children are engaged under the guidance of professional instructors. They will teach the child the correct riding technique both in a group and individually. Another area is equipped with a special relief and tracks where, under the supervision of instructors, children can ride on their own. The children's area is equipped with a baby lift. Soft gates, figures and fences that are not traumatic are located everywhere.



Accommodation for tourists

Ski complex "Manzherok" offers its guests accommodation in a modern hotel complex, which includes three mini-hotels with standard and studio rooms, as well as hotels for economy class accommodation. In total, hotels can accommodate one hundred and twenty people at a time. Each of them has its own restaurants, in which there is an opportunity to just have lunch, and organize a celebration or banquet. In addition, there are cafes where, after skiing, you can quickly and inexpensively eat. Young people can have fun at a disco nightclub.



The entire hotel complex has both cold and hot water supply. This fact makes the stay more comfortable.

Standard rooms are double. Here is one room, which includes a double bed, bedside table, armchair, dining and desk tables, chairs, TV, wardrobe. There is also a mirror, additional lighting, a small bar, a kitchen set: a suite, a kettle, dishes.

Since the ski complex "Manzherok" is a center for family vacations, standard and studio rooms are equipped with a common door. This will allow large companies or families with children to open the connecting door when checking in and use a four- or five-seater two-room suite.
Studio rooms are also double. However, there is a difference in the fact that an extra bed is added in the studio number. There are two beds, each one and a half. For the convenience of vacationers, there are bedside tables, an armchair-bed, a wardrobe. Otherwise, the room is equipped in the same way as the standard.

Economy accommodation requires a common entrance hall, a wardrobe, a bathroom with a shower. The rooms are equipped with bedside tables, lamps, as well as double and single beds in each unit, respectively.



Security

According to reviews, "Manzherok" is a ski resort, the prices for vacations in which justify themselves. Being here, you can not worry about security, because the whole territory is well guarded round the clock. It will not be a problem to leave your car, as parking spaces are provided here. The complex has its own medical service, as well as a rescue service. When descending from the track, you can not be afraid to earn an injury, since extreme sections on the sides are fenced with a protective net, and the snow itself is regularly treated with snow groomers.

Acquaintance with Altai Territory

Altai invites all visitors to the Manzherok complex for excursions. The programs involve acquaintance with the mountain complex and a lot of interesting useful information about this wonderful region. Near the center there are a large number of attractions that have both natural and man-made origin. These include gorgeous waterfalls, deep caves, beautiful lakes, a water park.

How to get there

The following buses deliver vacationers to the Manzherok ski resort: Novosibirsk-Chemal, Barnaul-Chemal, Biysk-Chemal. Having reached the village with the complex of the same name, it will be necessary to transfer by taxi, which will take you to the recreation center. If you have a car, you need to follow to the village of Manzherok, from where you need to turn towards the village of Ozernoye, where only two kilometers will remain to the complex.

Thus, the Manzherok ski resort is recommended to be visited both by small companies and entire families. Rest here will be remembered for a long time, because the organizers took into account the needs of vacationers with any riding preferences and skills.
