Sosnovy Bor in Kamensk-Uralsky: description, prices, reviews

The recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" in Kamensk-Uralsky is located on the territory of the former sanatorium. However, today employees of the institution do not provide clients with wellness services. Visitors come here to enjoy beautiful scenery, sports and other entertainment.

Organization Location

The recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" (Kamensk-Uralsky) is located ten kilometers from the city, in a forested area. Photographers and newlyweds love to visit this place. Here you can take many wonderful pictures against the backdrop of nature.

The landscapes on the territory of the holiday home are really magnificent: huge coniferous trees, a mountain river and amazing cliffs that have bizarre shapes. In the city of Kamensk-Uralsky recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" is located at: Sanatorium Lane, 28.

Lodging

Clients are offered three buildings with rooms of different types. The territory can accommodate 90 visitors.

In the old base building there are rooms for one or two people. This building also has rooms for three people. These are standard rooms with a single bed. There are rooms for families with sofas for two people. A toilet and shower are located on the floor. In addition, the old building has deluxe rooms. Each room of this type is equipped with a separate toilet, bath, jacuzzi, balcony and TV. The second building is a house, designed for a large number of people (twelve to fifteen vacationers). There are single rooms, rooms for two or three persons, a hall for celebrations, a sauna.

The third building is a wooden cottage. It is designed for twenty to thirty customers. There are sleeping rooms, there is a sauna, shower, banquet room equipped with a fireplace.

The cost of living at the recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" in Kamensk-Uralsky depends on the type of room. The cost of a standard category building is from 630 to 750 rubles per day, for a suite - from 2700 to 3000, for a family - 800 rubles. A day spent in the cottage will cost visitors more. The rent of the second building is 15,000 rubles, the third - 30,000 rubles.
