Best auto insurance companies: rating

Car insurance is a prerequisite for the maintenance of the car. Get insurance at the present time is not difficult. You can do it through online services or with a personal appeal to the office. It should be noted that for all companies, the conditions for registration for the same car vary significantly. Therefore, before concluding an agreement, it is necessary to choose the most profitable company.

What is car insurance?

Each owner must take out insurance for a vehicle for their own peace of mind. The point is that in the event of property damage to the vehicle, the costs are not borne by the owner, but by the insurance company. Thus, even the most unforeseen situations on the road will not hit the wallet of a motorist.

Types of car insurance

Today, insurance companies offer a variety of contract options. Each of them is limited to a certain amount of risk. The main types include:

  1. CTP - this type in the Russian Federation is mandatory. Under the MTPL agreement, the car owner is entitled to compensation for damage caused to passengers or to the vehicle. It is worth noting that the payment is made only if the injured party is not to blame for the accident. MTPL is issued once a year.
  2. Casco - this agreement is signed on a voluntary basis. Within its framework, full compensation for damage to the car is assumed. But you should know that this agreement does not insure passengers or transported property in any way.
  3. DSAGO - voluntary car insurance. It is drawn up as an addition to the main contract and ensures the reduction of the most unforeseen risks.

Thus, OSAGO must be issued to all car owners. Casco does not interchange OSAGO.
