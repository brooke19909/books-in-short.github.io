Yellow Sea Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): hotel description and reviews

Located on the coast of the South China Sea, the city of Nha Trang is one of the most popular among tourists from Russia and the countries of the former CIS. Domestic vacationers began to go on vacation here not so long ago. However, Nha Trang itself has become a resort since colonial times. And, of course, over many decades, the tourist infrastructure of this city has developed simply superbly. At least, hotels over the years of the resort's existence have been built a huge number. Tourists who choose Nha Trang as a beach vacation destination can easily rent both luxury luxury rooms in five-star hotels and less comfortable budget rooms in two- or three-star hotels.

Many Russian tour operators work in Nha Trang, for example, with the Yellow Sea Hotel 3 * hotel. This three-star hotel offers vacationers quite comfortable, judging by the reviews, rooms at a relatively low cost.





Location

The hotel is located Yellow Sea Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) directly in the city of Nha Trang. It was built at some distance from the ocean. To get to the beach, its residents should go through a rather busy highway. However, many former residents of this hotel do not consider this location to be too much inconvenience. Indeed, in any case, vacationers do not need to get to the beach for a long time. Tourists who settled in this complex can reach the seashore in just five minutes.

The closest airport to Nha Trang and the hotel is Kamran Airport, approximately 35 km away. Usually, Russian holidaymakers, arriving in Vietnam by plane, get to this hotel, of course, on the operator's transfer bus. But if you wish, you can also get to the hotel from Kamran Airport by taxi. Such a trip will cost about 20-25 dollars.

Also, some tourists travel to Nha Trang from the airport by minibus. A trip on this type of transport costs only about $ 3. However, the minibus, unfortunately, is not going to the city itself, but to the old military airfield. The convenience of such a trip is that the latter is located in close proximity to the tourist areas of the city. You can also get from the military airport to Yellow Sea by taxi. In total, a trip to the hotel with this method will still cost much cheaper.
