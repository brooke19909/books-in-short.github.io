Hotel Corfu Maris Bellos 3 *: description, reviews

To tell about the hotel Corfu Maris Bellos 3 *, without mentioning its excellent location, it means not to talk about anything.

Corfu

Corfu Island (or Kerkyra) is the best resort in Greece. It is located north of all the islands of the Ionian Sea. It is this good location that makes Corfu a favorite destination for many tourists. The weather at the resort is favorable for recreation throughout the year. But the best time to spend your vacation on this island is the season from May to October.

Corfu has long been glorified by many famous people. His beauty and uniqueness were admired by such personalities as Oscar Wilde, Napoleon, Goethe. The island is colorful and diverse in terms of landscapes, attractions and historical values. Corfu has long been a place of elite relaxation.



On the land, not more than 60 kilometers long and up to 30 kilometers wide, are resort areas, many large hotel complexes, and historical monuments. Spending a vacation on the island of Corfu, in any of its corners, including the Corfu Maris Bellos Hotel 3 *, it is impossible to ignore its historical values.

sights

The Museum of Asian Art presents the best works of the greatest Byzantine masters, unique exhibits of archeology.

In the center of the island's capital, bearing the same name, is an old naval fortress, which was erected by Byzantine craftsmen in 1546. Later it was updated by the Venetians. Now it is an art museum where art exhibitions, shows and theme nights are constantly held. Corfu has a coastal fortress that was built in the 16th century to protect against Turkish attack. At present, this fortress is a training building of the Greek Navy. Nearby is the central market. The Cathedral is famous for its original ancient icons. The most beautiful bell tower in Greece is the one located on the territory of the church of St. Spyridon in Corfu.

Symbols of the resort are Kanoni with the Mouse Island, the famous Vlacherna Monastery and the summer palace of the Austrian Empress Elizabeth on Achillio Hill.

Hotel

Hotel Corfu Maris Bellos 3 * is a cozy nice little hotel located 3 kilometers from the picturesque village of Benitses. The capital is only 10-15 minutes away. This is a complex consisting of two 4-story buildings, it is quite suitable for a budget, economical family vacation. It is suitable for lovers of a relaxing pastime and for those who wish to spend bright nights on the dance floor. The proximity of a large city allows you to plunge headlong into a hectic life. Corfu has many night clubs, entertainment centers, restaurants, taverns, discos on the beach.



Main
