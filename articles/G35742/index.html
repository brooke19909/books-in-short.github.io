Microsoft Lumia 640: review, specifications and reviews

Nokia, which has recently lost its leadership position in the mobile market, is actively expanding its presence. In particular, a number of smartphones can testify to this, comparing favorably with their competitors due to their special technical characteristics.

The same can be said about the model that we describe in this article. Meet: this is a Lumia 640 DS, a review of which we have prepared specifically for you. It should be noted that under this brand two different devices have been released, which, first of all, differ in their dimensions and a number of features. This is a model 640 and Lumia 640 XL. The review that we prepared today will tell about the main advantages and disadvantages of this smartphone, describe some user reviews about it. We will also draw a number of conclusions regarding this gadget.





Positioning

Let's start with a general description of how the device is presented in the Nokia model line and how the manufacturer positions the Lumia 640. The review we prepared showed that the smartphone belongs to the lower middle price class - prices for it start at 10 thousand rubles. Despite this, the capabilities of the device can indeed be called wide, based on the technical specifications indicated by the developers. In particular, we are talking about a powerful processor, colorful display, build quality of the Lumia 640. The review that you are reading now contains more detailed information about all of the above points, so you just have to be patient and you will learn everything about this device very soon . In the meantime, we give a description of the packaging of the device and its kit.

Equipment





First of all, I would like to note the special flat shape of the box in which the device is offered on the market. It is made of special cardboard that is not quite familiar in texture with drawing images of a smartphone and Microsoft logos. Opening the package, we meet a neatly arranged device that comes in a modest kit with a charger. The developers did not equip the model with a cord for connecting to a PC, so it will have to be purchased in a separate order. Also inside you can find documentation for the model, which describes the features of working with the Lumia 640.

We will not stop our review on the kit - let's move on to the exterior of the smartphone.

Design

It is hardly worth noting that Lumia has its own corporate identity in which all devices of this line are manufactured. The same can be said about Microsoft Lumia 640. The navigation elements of the device are represented by a classic set consisting of soft keys under the screen and physical ones on the right side face. We are talking about the buttons to lock the display and rocker-volume control.





In general, the assembly of the device can be noted on the positive side - the quality of plastic is high here, and all the elements are fitted closely. The phone is in the hand successfully - despite the glossy texture, the model does not try to slip out of the hands. So, while we were preparing a review for the Lumia 640, we could not find any defects, backlash or creaks.

Screen

The phone is presented in a popular (recently) class of devices that consist of 5-inch phablets. This is the optimal size for the device screen, for which there is considerable demand today. We can say that such models are both quite functional and not too bulky at the same time.

The XL version received a display whose diagonal is slightly larger and reaches 5.9 inches.

The resolution of the display, which is based on IPS technology, is 1280 by 720 pixels, which allows us to talk about a clear and rich picture. In addition, the image on the screen of the device does not change its color gamut even when turning and tilting, which is a positive feature.

Outside, the screen is covered with an oleophobic coating (which significantly reduces the friction force of the finger on the screen and, in addition, makes it easy to remove emerging handprints on the Microsoft Lumia 640. The review also showed that the display is equipped with a third-generation Gorilla Glass protective glass, which also has increased resistance to mechanical impacts and damage.

Battery

Our review is not about an Android device, about which there are so many rumors about their low level of autonomy. Nevertheless, the level of battery consumption (whose capacity, according to official documentation, is 2500 mAh) is here at about the same level. The enlarged XL version is different in this regard, as it has a 3000 mAh battery. This allows us to talk about a higher level of device autonomy compared to the basic version, even despite the enlarged display and, as a result, a slightly different level of charge consumption.





If you actively use this device (meaning working with access networks to the Internet, about watching videos, games and other applications), then one charge is enough for 1-1.5 days. Therefore, most likely, more advanced users using a wide range of device functions will need to charge it every day.

For those who need the Lumia 640 LTE (the review of which we are conducting) exclusively as a phone (meaning the use of its most basic functions and capabilities), it should be said that in standby mode the model is ready to work 3-4 days.

Communication





There are no unique features that could be attributed to the communication side of the device on the Lumia 640 Dual SIM (the review proves this). Yes, both smartphones support the work of two SIM cards (as the full name of the device may indicate). Reviews

conclusions
