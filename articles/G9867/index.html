This region is not the most popular among tourists, but recently the number of vacationers here has increased significantly.

Casa Rofina Hideaway Guest House 1 * is located just 45 km from the international airport and is a colonial-style building traditional for Goa. There are not many such hotels here. Beach and sea

Of course, tourists go to Goa not to spend their whole vacation at the hotel, they want to fully enjoy a chic beach holiday. Casa Rofina Guest House is no exception. Reviews of tourists confirm that the sea from the hotel is just a stone's throw away. All you need to do is get out of the gate, cross the central road and you are on the beach. The beach here is municipal, as in most other areas of North Goa. But this does not detract from its merits: pure fine sand, clear sea water, gorgeous nature. Here you can rent a sunbed and umbrella to protect from the scorching sun. I must admit that Candolim beach is considered the best on the coast, here you can not only sunbathe and swim, but also rent sports equipment, dive under water and even get on a water board.
