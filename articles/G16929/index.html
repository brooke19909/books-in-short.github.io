Guest Accommodation



The hotel has 1536 rooms located in different parts of the complex:

  • Junior Suite - in the remote;
  • Junior Suite Superior - in the main;
  • Junior Suite Club Hacienda and Golden - in two- and three-story cottages, close to the beach.

The cost of living in Junior Suites is an average of $ 62 (all-inclusive principle). This type of room offers 24-hour room service, concierge services, personal check-in, slippers and bathrobes in the room, as well as Internet access (the first 15 minutes are free). Absolutely all rooms of the Grand Bahia Bavaro Principle complex are equipped with air conditioning, a fan, satellite TV, a minibar and a telephone. Guests can enjoy the jacuzzi in the spacious bathroom. There is a coffee maker. There is access to a balcony or terrace. A safety deposit box is available at an extra charge.

Infrastructure



The Grand Bahia Bavaro Principle Hotel offers vacationers a pleasant time in three pools. On the territory near each of them there are bars and a jacuzzi.   • bathhouse;
  • cosmetic services;
  • sauna;
  • massage;
  • barbershop;
  • gym;
  • aromatherapy.

This center is recognized as the best in Bavaro.

Dominican Republic, Grand Bahia Principle of Bavaro: entertainment

Here, every vacationer will find something to do, because the hotel offers evening and afternoon entertainment programs, sports events for both adults and children. The hotel has a casino, a photo salon, a mini market, a business and Internet center. On the beach, vacationers are free to use sun loungers, umbrellas.



Sport

The hotel offers an extensive range of sports services. For example, visit a gym, aerobics or aqua aerobics classes, learn tango in dance classes, play tennis on one of the three courts, take part in water polo or volleyball competitions on the coast. In addition, the hotel has a diving center. On the coast, vacationers can rent pedal boats, a catamaran or a kayak, or ride a banana boat.
