Cheap hostels in St. Petersburg with prices and addresses

Are you dreaming of visiting the cultural capital of Russia, but are not ready to give a lot of money for the trip? A significant item of travel expenses are living expenses. Cheap hostels will help to save , there are a lot of them in St. Petersburg . In the article, we consider inexpensive options, but with high ratings, based on guest reviews. The cost of living in hostels of St. Petersburg starts from 200 rubles with a small one.

Mini-hotel "Lira"

Located in the city center, within walking distance of the most famous sights: Kazan and St. Isaac's Cathedrals, the Hermitage and others. To the metro station go 2-3 minutes.

The mini-hotel occupies 3 floors of a 5-storey building. The rooms have free Wi-Fi. Each floor has an equipped kitchen, showers, toilets, an iron with an ironing board.

The cost of accommodation depends on the season, number of guests, length of stay and room category. The cheapest option is a bed in a 10-bed room. In the low season, they ask for her 250 rubles per day. Hotel Booking Score 7.6 out of 10. Address: Moskovsky Prospekt, 8.
