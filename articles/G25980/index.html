DIY jeans bag

The most comfortable and popular clothing for many people of the current generation is jeans. Many people wear jeans to holes, then they put them on at home or leave them in case of going out into the country, at barbecue, at a summer cottage. In the article, we will offer readers another option for using old jeans, namely sewing a fashionable bag. Basically, such a cotton bag is used in the summer or in the warm autumn-spring off-season.

Bags from old jeans are easy to sew, because most of the product is already ready-made. Often for sewing choose the upper half, in which pockets are sewn. Some types of bags are sewn from trousers. It is not difficult to draw a bag pattern from old jeans; in most products it is a bottom rectangle and thin strips of which the handles are made.

Simple flower bag

As you can see in the photo below, for sewing this version of the bag, the upper part of the jeans is cut, namely, the distance from the belt to the beginning of the legs is cut. Front and rear pockets remain intact. The bottom of the bag from old jeans can be made separately by cutting out a strip of 10 cm trouser legs. From the wrong side, you must first draw it on the front and back of the bag, and then sew it on with a sewing machine.

If overlock is included in the set of paws, then all seams must be additionally processed so that the fabric does not split. If you expect to fully load a bag of old jeans, then the bottom can be sealed by adding a strip of dense fabric. It remains to sew the pens, which are composed of a wide strip, folded four times. Do this so that the raw edges do not peek out. The length of the handles can be different, depending on the wishes of the hostess of the product. After all, bags can be worn on the shoulder and in the hands. Seams are made on both sides of the handles. In places of attachment to the belt, reinforcement of the seams is made on them. The photo shows that the line has the shape of a square with a cross inside. To bag from old jeans had a bright element, on the front you can sew a beautiful red flower made of fabric. It can simply be sewn to the bag itself, or you can fasten it on a contrasting button color.

Shoulder bag

The next version of the bag from old jeans with their own hands is made similar to the previous version. The bottom is not sewn separately, and the bottom cut is simply attached with a double seam from the inside. The handle is designed to be worn on one shoulder, so instead of two strips, one is cut out, but long.

Its length can be measured on another bag. She breaks out of her jeans leg. A handle is attached on both sides of the bag, sewn with a similar seam. A decorative element of a denim bag is a thin scarf or scarf threaded into the straps of a belt. Changing the color of the insert, you can change the appearance of the bag.

Product with wooden handles

A pattern of a bag from old jeans with your own hands is made from two rectangles of the front and back. To form the bottom, you need to cut a strip from the leg, the length of which is equal to the width of the bag, and the width is taken at your discretion. However, the middle variant of the bottom width will be 10 cm. The edges of the strip can be made rounded, then there will be no corners on the bottom. The bag itself is cut out either whole or from several parts. You can use fabric from different pants.

The combination of different types of denim looks very impressive. One of the flaps can take the wrong side of the denim, then the piece will be almost white. When all the flaps are assembled according to the pattern, the rectangle of the front and back of the product is cut out. At first it is more convenient to sketch all the seams manually. These are the side seams and the bottom. Then work is done on the pens. These are round wooden sticks. They can be covered with a layer of stain, and then clean well with sandpaper and re-open with acrylic varnish. So that the bag fits well on wooden handles, you need to sew two valves. Four identical squares are cut out, folded in half and the back of the pocket is attached. At the end, all four parts are attached to the top of the bag. Sticks are inserted before work, so that they are tightly held inside and do not fall out when moving the bag.

Small strap bag

To make such a bag from old jeans (the photo is located below in the article), you need only one half of the pants. Starting from the waistband, we make an incision to the end of the pocket. You do not need to get to the fly; the pattern is done to it. Next, two sides are sewn together - the lower and the side. Seams are made on the wrong side of the craft.

The bag is small and flat. However, all the necessary things for a woman will fit in completely there: a wallet, a phone, cosmetics, documents for a car, keys and other little things. Even in such a small product, there are three pockets. This is a semicircular front pocket of jeans, a square back and a little hood for little things. To make the bag close, a button strap is sewn onto the jeans belt. The bag in which the clasp and strap are made of leather looks interesting. You can use an unnecessary leather bag. Her handle is perfect for denim, and you can sew a clasp from the leather of the bag itself. A button is best chosen metal, in shape resembling rivets.

Leg bag

You can sew a bag of old jeans with your own hands not only from the upper part of the pants, where the belt is located, but also to cut out the fabric from the pants. Side seams are already there, it remains to bend the fabric from above so that the threads do not come out, and stitch the bottom. The seams on the bottom of the crafts are double. First, two legs of the leg are attached on the wrong side.

Then, small seams are made on the sides of the bottom. To do this, on the inside, the jeans fabric is straightened and stitched about 6 cm perpendicular to the bottom seam on one and the other side of the bottom. Externally, the seams at the bottom look as follows: I ----- I.

It remains to sew in the center strap with a clasp. Such a bag is carried under the armpit, since it does not have handles. If the master has a desire, they can be sewn on the sides of the product.

Woven Stripes Bag

If you have not yet chosen the option of how to sew a bag of old jeans, consider the following type of product. This bag is made of strips of denim cut from the same size of pants. In the photo below you can clearly see the step-by-step execution of fabric cutting. First, identical strips are cut from long parts. Then they need to be ironed with a hot iron and laid out on a flat surface.

Weaving is performed with alternating arrangement of strips above and below. So that during attachment the strips do not crawl out, it is better to mark all the edges with a thread with a needle manually. When the rectangles of the front and back sides are assembled, seams are made. Bending of the fabric is done on top of each detail, the sides are sewn along the wrong side, like the bottom. It remains to carve out long strips for pens. Sew them in the same way as the first method already described earlier. With such a spacious bag, you can go not only to the store, but also go sunbathing to the beach. The clasp can be made at will, but in such bags they are usually not sewn.

Pleated bag

You can sew a bag of old jeans according to the pattern and the next style. It is sewn from the extended part of the legs. Two segments 25-30 cm long are cut off at the same level. Seams from one side are cut off to make the fabric smooth. The same details are cut from the lining fabric according to the available patterns. Then, all four patterns are first sewn with manual stitches, then attached to the sewing machine. The bottom is made out as follows.

On denim, the bottom of the pieces is sewn along the wrong side. The lining is sewn separately and is applied inward, face up. Further work takes place on the planks. First, two wide strips are cut from the legs of old jeans, which are wrapped around the front and back of the bag. Then the workpiece is rotated laterally forwards. Seams are obtained in the middle of the future bag.

Pen work

The next stage of work on the bag consists of measuring the rim with a handle. A long strip of fabric is cut from the trouser leg, equal to the length of both sides, plus space for pens. The photo shows how the work is performed step by step. The bezel is collected immediately on the bag, the raw edges of the fabric are bent inward. Finally, a button is sewn and a loop of thick dark blue rope is gathered. It will be a clasp for a bag.

Dressing jeans bag
