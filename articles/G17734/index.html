"Fast" bear costume

This costume is suitable for a matinee in the garden and can be made in literally an hour. The outfit consists of ears, a mitten, a ponytail and a bow on the neck, like a bear from the cartoon "Masha and the Bear." We will need a small flap of satin, brown faux fur, a thick bezel and a pair of safety pins. We make a bow tie from chintz. From the fur you need to cut mittens, ears and the tail of a bear. Then cut off a strip of material, sheathe its rim, attach ears on top. Previously, you can seal the ears and tail with synthetic winterizer or cardboard. We attach the butterfly and ponytail to the child‚Äôs clothes with safety pins. The bear costume is ready!
