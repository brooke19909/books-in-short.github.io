Apple

There are currently four tablets running iOS on the market. Three of them have a diagonal of 10 inches and one - 7.9. The iPad 3 and 4 are of real interest. This is the answer to what kind of tablet is good among this kind of Apple products. The only negative of this solution is its high cost, which is an order of magnitude higher than that of competitors.

Green robot
