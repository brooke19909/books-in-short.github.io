How to transfer money to Ukraine quickly and with minimal losses

Long gone are the days when, in order to transfer money from one city to another (or even to a neighboring country), you had to wait for a grand-nephew to be given in the form of a second cousin or to negotiate with a guide or bus driver, hoping solely for the honesty and decency of the latter . To do this, you need to have a passport, money, and the contact details of the recipient (his last name, first name, middle name and full home address).
