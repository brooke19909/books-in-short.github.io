Credit at Tinkoff Bank: customer reviews

Lending today has become relevant for all segments of the population. People borrow money in order to buy new household appliances, go on a vacation, buy real estate or a car, open their own business. At the same time, you need to choose a bank very well so as not to overpay too much on interest. A huge number of Russians get a loan at Tinkoff Bank. Reviews in this case are very different from each other. Someone in this banking system really likes lending. Others call the terms predatory. Let's try to understand this issue together.



Lending terms

The consumer has a choice, therefore, if you are not in a hurry to get cash, then it is better to collect complete information and based on this make the final choice. Who can get a loan at Tinkoff Bank? Reviews suggest that this system has minimal requirements for the consumer. Almost everyone can get cash on hand. To do this, you must be an adult citizen of the Russian Federation not older than 70 years.

The main differences

In each city of Russia, you can see dozens of branches of bank offices. But among them there is not a single Tinkoff bank. How does it exist and carries out work with clients? This is a unique financial structure that does not have offices, branches and ATMs. This significantly reduces the costs that somehow fall on the shoulders of the credited.

How can I get a loan at Tinkoff Bank? Reviews say this is a fairly simple operation. In fact, it is a virtual bank that accepts applications from customers remotely. The application is submitted through a special service service. You can send it anywhere in Russia by entering your passport details. The decision is communicated by telephone. A ready-made card will be delivered to you by a bank employee along with all documents. That is, anyone can quickly and easily get a loan at Tinkoff Bank. Reviews, however, warn that employees will definitely check your solvency. If you have low income and already have loans, then you should not rely too much on a loan.



Check

After submitting an application, passport data is received in the bank, as well as information about where the borrower works. All this will be checked through special services. As a result, employees will decide not only to approve or refuse, but will also determine the maximum amount that you can use. Usually it will be small on first use. If the credit history is good, then next time it will be possible to count on a more serious amount. The main criterion will be the level of wages, as well as the presence of dependents and debt on loans. The bank will carry out all checks on its own, without requesting additional information from you.

Bank help

In a difficult life situation, everyone can be. If you lost your job, ended up in a hospital, then you should not hide from the bank managers. On the contrary, the faster you get in touch with employees and explain the situation, the easier it will be to find a way out. Most often, you will be offered a loan restructuring at Tinkoff Bank. Reviews say that this system has its pitfalls, but is a good solution to the problem.

The main thing is to try to prevent delays. Contact a financial institution and try to convince the lender that you have good reasons and you do not shy away from paying. Fill in the official paper, in which you need to indicate the number of the debt agreement, the size of the debt limit, the amount paid and the rest of the debt, the time of the last payment, the reasons why the payments stopped.

Special programs

If the bank makes a positive decision, then you will be offered new credit decisions with a more convenient repayment schedule. There are two restructuring programs:

  • Tunnel - for a certain period of time, the minimum monthly payment is reduced to 200-500 rubles per month. The credit card is blocked for this time. When your financial condition stabilizes, you continue to pay as before.
  • Installment - credit card payments are completely stopped. The debt is fixed and a new loan is issued for this amount, the repayment schedule is determined.

This service has its drawbacks, because when it is issued, the bank will calculate the commission, distributing it for future payments. But the situation in this case will be better than if you do not pay Tinkoff Bank a loan. Reviews say that in this case, round-the-clock calls from collectors are provided, with all the ensuing consequences.
