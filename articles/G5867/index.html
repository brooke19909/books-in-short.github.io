Hotel Kamenec Nessebar 2 * (Nessebar, Bulgaria): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Nessebar is considered one of the most ancient cities in Europe. On its territory there are many historical monuments that attract tourists from year to year. The resort is located on the coast, so sightseeing holidays here can easily be combined with a beach. In addition, vacation here is considered extremely budget. Most hotels have only two or three stars. One of these hotels is Kamenec Nessebar 2 * (Bulgaria). What service can he offer his guests?

basic information

The hotel was built in 1972. However, you can not worry that the hotel is very old. Complete repair of all residential and public buildings was carried out in 2011. After it, the re-opening of the Kamenec Nessebar 2 * complex (Bulgaria, Nessebar) took place. It consists of two buildings, which are located in a small landscaped area. A small three-story building is considered the main one. Here are located not only the rooms, but also the reception with the lobby. The second building is a high-rise building. In total there are 168 rooms. Check-in here starts as standard at 14:00. Registration usually lasts until midnight.





Pets of any size are strictly forbidden. Children, on the contrary, are welcome here. They can be provided with a cradle or an extra bed for a fee. In addition, for their accommodation you need to pay less. The staff partially speaks Russian. It is worth knowing that when booking a room, the administration of the Kamenec Hotel (Kamenec Nessebar) 2 * hotel has the right to ask you to partially pay for the stay in advance. This can be done using a bank card or money transfer. Checkout time here is noon. Until this time, tourists must leave their room or extend their stay for a day or several hours.

Location

Tourists believe that the main advantage of this hotel is its advantageous position. Indeed, it is located in the very center of the resort town of Nessebar. From here you can quickly walk to the city beach. The path to it will take no more than 5 minutes. A public transport stop is 100 meters from the Kamenec Nessebar 2 * hotel. Bus routes run to Burgas, Sunny Beach, Sveti Vlas and the village of Ravda. Near the hotel is well developed local infrastructure. Tourists can visit grocery stores, souvenir and vegetable shops. There are also several restaurants and cafes that specialize in local cuisine. The old part of the city is 1.5 km away. You can walk to it or take a taxi. Cacao Beach is 2 km away and the famous Grand Night Club is 4 km away.





The nearest airport is in Burgas, which is approximately 30-35 km away. There are regular buses and taxis to Nessebar from here. Paid transfers are also organized for tourists. Travel time is 30-40 minutes.

Description of rooms

The apartments of this hotel are simply furnished. Here you will not see a large number of decorative elements. Some rooms additionally have an open balcony. If you stay on the upper floors of a high-rise building, you can enjoy magnificent views of the sea. Guests from the lower floors can enjoy the garden or the surrounding buildings. Housekeeping is provided daily by maids. They regularly update bed linen and towels.





The room fund of the Kamenec Nessebar 2 * hotel is represented by the following types of apartments:

  • Double room in a small building. Its area is 18 square meters. m. Consists of a living room, which houses two separate beds, and a bathroom. Suitable for a family of two parents and a child. A small sofa is used as an extra bed.
  • Triple room in a small building. Almost no different from its double counterpart. However, instead of a sofa, there is a full single bed on which an adult can be accommodated.
  • Junior Suite in a high-rise building. It is designed for two parents and two children. It is a studio in which there is a bedroom and living area. There is a combined bathroom. Rooms are 30 square meters. m. Children are located on a folding sofa.
  • Double room in a high-rise building. Small apartments suitable for an adult couple without children. Guests are accommodated in two single beds that are pushed together. The living room area is 14 square meters. m
  • Suite It is designed to accommodate four adults. Two of them can sleep in a double bed, while the rest are offered folding chairs and a sofa bed. There is a seating area and a bathroom. The suites are 35 square meters. m

Room amenities

The arrangement of the apartments is not very diverse. Kamenec Nessebar 2 *, reviews of which are often positive, offers guests equipment that is not inferior to that characteristic of hotels of higher categories. Guests can enjoy the modern flat-screen TV, which broadcasts a large number of cable channels. The rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning in a small building, while the central building is located in a high-rise building. There is also a refrigerator in the apartment, which can also be used as a minibar. Iron and ironing facilities are available at the reception free of charge. True, after use, they must be returned.





The bathroom is equipped with a washbasin, toilet and shower, although some rooms have a full bath. The price also includes a set of toiletries. The bathroom also has a hairdryer, bath towels for face and body, as well as a large mirror, with which it is convenient to shave or apply makeup.

Infrastructure

Developed infrastructure for tourists boasts of Nessebar. Kamenec Nessebar 2 * offers a standard set of services and facilities, which is consistent with the status of a two-star hotel. So, guests can always take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • Public car parking located near the hotel. You can use it for free, but you cannot reserve a seat in advance, unfortunately.
  • Two outdoor pools that work in fine weather. Near them is a sun terrace with free umbrellas and sunbeds.
  • Children's playground with slides and swings.
  • A luggage storage room where you can leave your bags if you have arrived before 14:00.
  • Specially equipped smoking areas.
  • Free wifi that works in all public areas of the hotel but catches best in the lobby.
  • A conference room equipped with a projector and computers. Located in a high-rise building.




Nutrition

Hotel Kamenec Nessebar 2 * offers guests all-inclusive meals. It includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, which includes dishes of Bulgarian and international cuisine, as well as soups, salads, desserts and fresh fruits. The morning menu consists of slices of different types of sausages and cheeses, fruits, vegetables, pasta, fresh pastries. Soft drinks, hot drinks and mineral water are served. Local alcohol is also provided free of charge: homemade wine, draft beer, whiskey, gin and mastic. Meals are served on schedule at the hotel‚Äôs main restaurant. It is forbidden to take food and drinks out of it.





Positive reviews

Travelers having a rest in hotel Kamenec Nessebar 2 * (Bulgaria), leave good reviews. They believe that the price at this hotel is consistent with the level of service provided. In their opinion, this complex has the following advantages:

  • New, just refurbished rooms that have everything you need for a comfortable stay. Furniture and plumbing are also in excellent condition.
  • Great location for tourists who like to spend time outside the hotel. Nearby there is a bus stop, an ATM, a restaurant serving Bulgarian cuisine and a cafe.
  • It is quite quiet here at night and in the evenings. Music in nearby restaurants and bars turns off at about 22:00.
  • The Internet works not only in public places, but also in some rooms. At the same time, you do not need to pay extra for it.
  • Even on the ground floor, the window offers beautiful views of the garden and the huge chestnut tree.




Negative reviews

But not only its positive opinions are famous for the Kamenec Nessebar 2 * hotel (Nessebar). Reviews about him are sometimes found negative. Tourists in their comments talk about the following disadvantages of the hotel, which may well affect the rest:

  • Elevators in a high-rise building often break, so you often have to go up to your floor on foot.
  • The hotel staff speaks Russian and English poorly. Sometimes it is very difficult to explain to the staff about the problems encountered.
  • On the day of arrival, tourists are entitled only to dinner. If you arrived early in the morning, then you will have to eat at the nearest restaurants on your own for your money.
  • Huge lines in the restaurant for lunch and dinner. Also not always enough dining tables.
  • Local alcohol on an all-inclusive basis is not of the best quality. Gourmets are unlikely to like it.

Draw conclusions

Unlike many other hotels, Kamenec Nessebar 2 * has renovated rooms, friendly staff, quality service and varied food. Tourists note that this complex is much better than other three-star hotels in Nessebar. It is suitable for tourists who love a quiet excursion or beach holiday. Tourists with children, elderly people and adult couples often stop here.
