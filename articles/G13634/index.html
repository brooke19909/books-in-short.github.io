Tbilisi hotels: addresses, descriptions, reviews of tourists and experts

Among the avid travelers and lovers of good rest outside the borders of their city, there are not only people who prefer to bask in the sun on the shores of the warm sea. There are those who prefer a cultural holiday, during which you can get acquainted with some new city for yourself, see its sights and try to feel its special atmosphere.

A great option for such a trip could be the capital of Georgia - sunny Tbilisi. This city has enough interesting places that will not leave indifferent any tourist. And hotels in Tbilisi will always welcome new guests from all over the world with pleasure and hospitality.

What to do for tourists in Tbilisi

The city got its name thanks to the warm sulfuric springs that are on its territory (from the Georgian ‚ÄúTbili‚Äù - ‚Äúwarm‚Äù). Due to its location, the city can offer tourists to soak up real sulfur baths in the region of Abanotubani. Here you can relax thanks to the massage.

Of course, Tbilisi is famous not only for bathhouses. The cultural heritage of the Georgian capital is known to many, so those who like to explore all kinds of sights and cultural places have something to do here. Guests of the city should definitely see the local churches and cathedrals, the Narikala fortress, the Old Town and museums. You should definitely go to one of the Georgian restaurants and try the local khachapuri, khinkali and, of course, real Georgian wine.

Where can guests stay in Tbilisi

Modern Tbilisi is a great place for those who want to get acquainted with the culture and history of Georgia. On the territory of the city there are a lot of hotels and hostels in which tourists coming here can settle. There are both inexpensive hotels and hotels for wealthy guests of the city. Well developed in the city and the private sector. Often, guests of the Georgian capital choose this type of housing.





However, Tbilisi hotels are very diverse, so finding an option suitable for yourself will not be difficult. Everything will depend only on where the hotel of your choice will be located. Housing prices in Tbilisi largely depend on the area in which the hotel is located. Expensive hotels prevail in the city center. A significant factor affecting the value of the room may also be the view that the guest will observe from his window. If you dream of a panorama of the city, then be prepared to pay extra money for such a luxury.

What hotels does Tbilisi offer its guests?

The capital, modern and popular with many tourists, boasts that on its territory several hotels with world names can host guests. Choosing such hotels in Tbilisi, any guest can have no doubt that he is waiting for the highest quality service and incomparable comfort. These hotels include: Marriott on Rustaveli Street, Courtyard by Marriott on Freedom Square, Sheraton on Telavskaya Street, Radisson Blue on Republic Square and Holiday Inn in Saburtalo.

Of course, in the capital of Georgia there are a large number of hotel options that do not have world names, but which have worked well here. Those who are looking for very good hotels in Tbilisi, in the city center, can choose the beautiful Narikala Citadel or Orion, located near the State University. The prices in them, of course, are not very affordable, but both of these hotels have 4 stars. Of the inexpensive options that are located quite well, it is worth highlighting the Victoria Hotel and Diamond.

Great views from the Narikala Citadel Hotel

Choosing the Old City for your stay in Tbilisi is the right decision, especially for those who are visiting the capital of Georgia for the first time. After all, the central temples and the best views of the whole city are concentrated here. And it is thanks to the amazing panoramic view that opens from the windows of his rooms that the Hotel ‚ÄúNarikala Citadel‚Äù collects many rave reviews.





The hotel is located at 20. J. Azhiashvili Street. This place is located in close proximity to the Narikala fortress, which appeared here in the 7th century. The Narikala Citadel itself is a unique small boutique hotel with a roof terrace. From it you can admire magnificent views of the city and its attractions, as well as taste delicious Georgian dishes in the restaurant located right here.

Both ordinary tourists and experts agree that this hotel is simply amazing. Everything here has a good rest and immersion in the real Georgian flavor. Of course, it is more suitable for physically prepared holidaymakers, since it is located high enough on a hill and it‚Äôs hard to climb up to it. So if you are ready for such a small difficulty and are looking for good hotels in Tbilisi, then the Narikala Citadel will be an excellent choice for living in the Old City.

Services and rooms at the Citadel Narikala hotel

This boutique hotel has very few rooms - only 16. Each of them is designed for two guests and is made in white or gray colors. It offers soft bathrobes and slippers, a minibar, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning, a hairdryer, a large mirror and a balcony with amazing views.

For guests at the hotel there is also a small fitness room with the necessary equipment, as well as its own museum. It is located right in the lobby and introduces guests to archaeological finds discovered during the construction of the hotel.





The prices for rooms in the hotel are rather high, but this is directly explained by its location, the views and the quality of the rooms and service. Therefore, few vacationers here complain about the price tag.

Orion in the center of Tbilisi

Just 100 meters from Chavchavadze Avenue, on Napareuli Street, 5, is the 4-star Orion Hotel. Due to its favorable location in the very center of the city, this hotel will be a great place for all those who come to get acquainted with the capital of Georgia. Very close to it are the city philharmonic society, Tbilisi State University and the zoo. Getting here is also convenient, because the distance to the railway station is 3 km, and the hotel is only 19 km from Tbilisi Airport .

For guests, the hotel offers an excellent restaurant where you can taste dishes of both national and European cuisine, a 24-hour bar, a conference room for business meetings and meetings, its own sauna and billiards.





Among the apartments offered to tourists, there are both double and triple standard rooms, family rooms, which can comfortably accommodate up to four guests, deluxe rooms with one or two beds and even suites with magnificent terraces. Each of them has free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a minibar and a safe, a flat-screen TV and comfortable new furniture.

What guests think about the Orion Hotel

Everyone who has ever stayed at this hotel speaks great of local breakfasts and comfortably furnished rooms. Friendly guests and a very good location of the hotel delight guests.

Some, however, say that Orion is clearly not up to 4 stars, but solid 3 stars can be given to it. Local Wi-Fi guests also scold at times, because it sometimes disappears. But this happens with almost any Internet, isn't it?





On the whole, if you are interested in good Tbilisi hotels located in the center, then be sure to pay attention to Orion.

Diamond in Tbilisi

Located on V. Iverieli Street, at number 20, Diamond Hotel has recently opened its doors to all guests of the Georgian capital. Nearby is the 300 Aragvintsev metro station, so every guest can easily get to the most interesting and popular places in the city. It takes only 15 minutes to get to the international airport, so that even after a long flight, a tourist can quickly enjoy the peace and comfort in the purchased room.

The hotel itself occupies a small four-story building. On its territory there is free parking, which is sure to appeal to those guests who cannot travel without a car. You can have a tasty breakfast here in a cozy bright cafe. By the way, guests can pick up breakfast in their room.





If one of the things vacationers got dirty or wrinkled, do not despair. Here you can always use the laundry and ironing services, which will allow to tidy up any clothes.

Diamond Hotel Rooms and Reviews

This small but cozy building has only 11 rooms. They are all equipped with air conditioning, which will not allow any guest to languish from the heat. Modern televisions with cable channels and complimentary wireless Internet access will definitely brighten up any boring evening. The rooms also have a hairdryer and a telephone, and the bathrooms are equipped with comfortable showers. Some rooms have balconies.

According to tourists and experts, the rooms here are always clean and very comfortable. The local staff and delicious and varied breakfasts are also praised. Guests also note the convenient location of the hotel and very affordable prices.





Some, however, complain about the not very beautiful view from the window: in several places nearby are quite lengthy construction projects, which spoil the whole impression of viewing the city.

Cozy Victoria in Tbilisi

If you plan to relax in Tbilisi and spend a little money on this, then finding cheap hotels in the city will not be difficult. For example, there is a hotel called Victoria, which prices are available to many tourists.

"Victoria" is located near Rustaveli Avenue, which stretches in the city center. The address of this hotel is 42 Petriashvili Street. Just a 15-minute walk is the Rustaveli metro station, so it‚Äôs quite convenient to leave here for tourists. Although you do not have to go far to enjoy some of the main attractions. To see Sameba Cathedral or Narikala Fortress, you need to spend only about 10 minutes on foot.





Hotel Victoria itself is a low beige building located on a hill. In their reviews, guests of this hotel call it rather a guest house or a small family hotel, where workers meet everyone as members of their family. A delicious continental breakfast is served daily at the local fireplace restaurant. In the evenings, you can listen to the sounds of a real piano.

Rooms at the Victoria Hotel

There are few hotel rooms: only 9 pieces. But all of them have beautiful design, antique furniture and carpeted. In each of them, according to vacationers, you can feel as comfortable as at home.

Each room is equipped with air conditioning, telephone, cable TV and wireless internet. There is also a minibar. As for the categories of rooms available at the hotel, there are suites with magnificent terraces, as well as standard rooms with one double or two single beds.





Tourists staying at this hotel note that all rooms are very spacious and clean. The opinions of most vacationers also agree that the equipment in the bathrooms of this hotel is old and needs to be updated.

Tbilisi hotels are waiting daily for guests of all those who wish to get acquainted with this beautiful ancient city. Among them, you can find both cheaper options and pompous expensive apartments in which it would be comfortable even for kings. Whichever of them the choice of the future tourist fell, he will certainly be satisfied with the local hospitality, cordiality and delicious local cuisine.
