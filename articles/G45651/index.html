Hotels Crete (Hersonissos). Description

One of the largest resort cities in Crete is Hersonissos. It is located just a 30-minute drive from Heraklion. Every year the popularity of this amazing resort is growing, and with it the variety of entertainment on offer. The city is famous for its clean sandy beaches and a huge number of various bars and taverns. During the day, vacationers chill by the sea, and at night there is a romantic atmosphere. Hotels of Crete (Hersonissos), offering vacationers quality living conditions, take care of the leisure of tourists. The hotels have entertainment programs and discos, excursions are carried out. Hotel guests have the opportunity to engage in a wide variety of sports. And those who are tired of the hustle and bustle of the day can just walk along the sparsely populated streets of mountain villages. Near the resort town is a water park.





Crete hotels. Hersonissos

Anissa Beach & Village 4 *

Hotels of Crete (Hersonissos) create all conditions for a carefree and memorable vacation for tourists. Once in the hotel Anissa Beach & Village 4 *, you can simply forget about the time and enjoy the beauty of beautiful nature. The hotel is located along Anissaras Beach and consists of modern three-story buildings. The hotel has 311 rooms. The hotel infrastructure includes an amphitheater, two restaurants, an Internet cafe, indoor and outdoor pools, and a TV room. For kids there is a club, a playroom, high chairs, cots and a separate water area. For recreation, a mini-football field, tennis courts, a golf club, massage room, as well as aerobics and volleyball, animation are provided.





Hotel Hersonissos 4 (Crete)

This luxury hotel is located in the very center of the spa town. Decorated in a neoclassical Greek style, it surprises with an elegant interior and well-groomed territory. The most popular Hersonissos 4 is among young people. The hotel consists of a main four-story building and a small separate building. The hotel has 200 rooms, there are also two swimming pools, conference rooms, a restaurant, two bars, a parking lot, a TV room, an Internet cafe, a gym, billiards, a tennis court, a sauna, and animation. For children, the restaurant has high chairs, and the room has a cot.





Dimitron Central Hotel 3 *

For tourists visiting the island of Crete (Hersonissos), hotels of the city of various categories offer their services. The budget hotel Dimitron Central Hotel is very popular among vacationers. It is located in the center of the resort town, which allows tourists to choose their own entertainment. At 100 meters there is a sandy beach with umbrellas and sun loungers. The hotel has a bar, TV room, restaurant, park, outdoor pool, billiards. Water sports are available at the beach.





Central Hersonissos 2 *

Some Crete hotels (Hersonissos is no exception) are oriented towards economical vacations. Central Hersonissos 2 * belongs to these. It is located near the water park and the city port. The hotel offers its guests spacious rooms, traditional furnishings and quality service. A beach with umbrellas and sun loungers is located 200 meters from the hotel. The hotel has a left-luggage office, a bar, a swimming pool, an Internet cafe, a parking lot, an elevator, a laundry, a TV room, and billiards. For babies in the room there is a cot.
