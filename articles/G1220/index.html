Mask of the monkey. How to do it yourself?

After reading this article, you will learn several ways to create a cute monkey mask with your own hands. You can make such a product with your child and use it at school or kindergarten.

Paper monkey mask

If you want to make a mask for your child in kindergarten for the holiday, then you can make it out of paper. You will not spend much time and money. It is best to buy colored cardboard for children's creativity for such a mask. If you cannot do this, then any thick paper will do, which you will then need to paint with pencils, felt-tip pens or paints (gouache, watercolor or acrylic).





First draw a monkey face on cardboard. As a template, you can use the photo above. First, cut the silhouette of the muzzle out of dark brown cardboard. Carefully measure the distance between the eyes, otherwise the monkey mask may not fit in size. Then cut out the mouth, ears and bangs from the light brown cardboard, and from the yellow - the contours around the eyes. Make small holes near the ears and tie an elastic band to them. Glue all parts together. The monkey mask is ready!

More complicated option: felt mask

To create such a mask, you need a brown, sand and white felt, thread, needle and scissors. You can use a ready-made template to create such a product, or you can make a pattern on paper with your own hands, so that later you can cut out a mask from felt. First make a monkey face out of the dark brown felt. You can notice the edges of the future mask so that they are stronger. Then cut the monkey‚Äôs face out of white felt and carefully sew it to the brown part. With a dark thread, mark the line of the nose as shown in the photograph.





Then cut two semicircles of sand-colored felt and sew them to the ears. If you have a high-quality machine, then you can embroider these semicircles. Now it remains only to attach the elastic to the mask. To make such a product, it is best to take a thick and hard felt.

How to make a mask of polymer clay and faux fur?
