For those who want to know how to crochet

One of the main occupations of women since ancient times is needlework. At the same time, knitting is not only a hobby, it is also a business that benefits both the person and his family. To answer the question about how to start crocheting, you should learn some tips.





How to choose a hook and material for knitting

First of all, you should choose the necessary hook and yarn. The hook is the main tool in knitting, and if you choose it incorrectly, then your hands will begin to get tired. The hook is made of wood, bone, plastic and metal. It is also thick.





The type of material is also important in answering the question of how to crochet. Usually yarn of any kind is used for this. However, it should be understood that in order to tie a lace fabric, the presence of twisted threads, which are made of cotton or artificial material, is required. There are a lot of yarn at present, so it‚Äôs very difficult to choose a particular type.

Cotton, synthetics, wool - all these materials can be used to create yarn. It can also be made by mixing different materials in various proportions. How to choose the right yarn, which hook to choose, and how to crochet? These questions torment any beginner needlewoman.





If synthetic yarn is chosen, then the hook must be purchased one with a thickness of about six millimeters. If the yarn is thin, then the hook should not be too thick. In order to knit smoothly, you should pick up a hook, the thickness of which is approximately two times thicker than the thread. If you make the choice the other way around, you get a denser product. An openwork cloth will turn out if you use a thick hook and a very thin thread.

How to start knitting

Now it is worth considering in detail the question of how to crochet. To do this, take the tool in your right hand. It is necessary to take it as if they took a pen, resting on the middle finger. In the left hand you need to take a ball. Put the thread of yarn in your palm, passing it between two fingers - index and middle. Then it is worth wrapping it twice around the thumb. The end that goes to the ball, you need to fix on the middle finger and skip the thread between it and the ring finger. It is not worth clamping it. The short end of the thread should be pressed lightly with the little finger and ring finger.

How to knit beautifully and naturally

Now, most likely, the question will arise about how to crochet beautifully. The following recommendations exist for this:

  1. You should always make a sample on which you can make all the calculations. In addition, you can see how the image of your choice lies.
  2. Begin from the beginning of the row. The long end of the thread should be left behind. This is necessary so that the yarn is fixed neatly.
  3. Do not forget to knit a lifting loop.
  4. Before you start knitting, you should take a comfortable pose.
  5. Good lighting needed. It must be borne in mind that it should not shine in the eyes.
  6. You should take the box and put a ball in it. Otherwise, he will begin to ride around the room.
  7. Do not rush.
  8. It is necessary to periodically take breaks.
  9. Do not knit with dark threads in the evening, otherwise your eyes will get tired.
  10. It is best to start learning how to knit with light threads, as it will be easier to count the number of loops.

On this, the question of how to crochet can be considered considered.
