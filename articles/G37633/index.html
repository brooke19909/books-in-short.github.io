Ice Palace in Klin: description and address

Sports facilities instill in the population a love of an active lifestyle. In modern centers it is available to enroll in a variety of sections, choosing the best option for yourself. In addition, you can come to the complex to watch competitions between teams, for various show programs and even performances by artists. All this suggests making an ice palace in Klin, which is a rather multifunctional center. It is available to come here to relax with the whole family or with a group of friends.

General information

Ice Palace named after Valery Kharlamov is well known throughout the city. It is considered a local attraction, as well as a great place to relax. The building itself has an interesting and modern look, so it looks very attractive. It was built in 2003. The center regularly trains hockey and figure skating athletes. Spectators can also enter competitions between hockey teams. All halls and rooms are impressive in size, so they are ideal for training. On the way to the building, visitors can see the monument to hockey player V. Kharlamov. It was in honor of him that the complex was named.

Mass skating in the ice palace in Klin takes place quite often. Visitors are advised to know in advance the time of the sessions, as it depends on hockey matches. You can rent skates right on the spot if you don‚Äôt have your own. Many guests of the complex are very happy with the skating rink and write that this is one of the main places in the city. Under the roof of the building often hosts exhibitions, fairs and conferences. Stars of not only Russian, but also foreign pop music are invited here. For children and adults arrange festive events. In the arena, you can see ice shows, a circus comes on tour.

Prices of the ice palace in Klin start from 250 rubles. Available to purchase a subscription in the section. For 3 months it will cost 7500 rubles, and for six months you need to pay 10 800 rubles. If you immediately purchase a subscription for a year, then it costs 15,000 rubles.

Address

The 99th Ice Palace in Klin can be found on Karl Marx Street, building 99. Next to the complex is the Sestroretsky Park and the Sestra River. They can become a guide for the search.

You can get to the sports facility both by private car and by public transport. Nearby there is a stop that goes to:

  • Buses No. 2, 6, 18.
  • Minibuses No. 9 and 54.

You can also come here by train. Get off at the station, which is named after the city. From the station to the complex is accessible by bus 18 or bus 9.

Mode of operation

Ice Palace (Wedge) is open every day. For the convenience of visitors, it opens from 9.00 and closes only at 22.00. Therefore, many citizens can attend sports groups at a convenient time for them. Clarify the work of each section is available on site or by phone, which is listed on the official website.

Additional Information

The sports facility is known not only for hockey matches, but also for its excellent selection of sports sections. Here you can enroll in weightlifting, figure skating, Greco-Roman wrestling groups. Aerobics and fitness classes are held, it is available to get to the dance studio in various directions. Guests can enjoy table tennis and billiards. Sports equipment rental is open. Most people are now engaged in the figure skating section. Trainers with extensive experience work with students. Groups are of different ages. The youngest students are only four years old. Many participants regularly qualify and compete.

The Titan GYM gym in the complex is in great demand. In it you can turn with all the necessary questions to the trainer who will work out the lesson plan. In the hall, power and cardio workouts are held, there are many modern simulators.

Even from Moscow come to classes at the Ice Palace (Klin). This is due to the presence of an excellent composition of teachers, as well as with a sports center, which is equipped with everything necessary. Expectant mothers can attend separate sections. Especially for them, instructors work for free, who can choose the right program. For visitors are open: massage room, solarium, hairdresser. You can make an appointment with a psychologist. A sports shop is open on the territory of the center, there is a video rental and a communication salon.
