Seychelles Islands. Climate features

A huge plus for tourists is the fact that this vacation spot is located in the same time zone with central Russia. Therefore, you do not have to endure the inconvenience associated with the restructuring in time. The Seychelles Islands are located in the oceanic zone just south of the equator and have a mild tropical climate. It is not too cold or too hot here, the temperature is always almost the same, without significant fluctuations. Between December and May, most of the precipitation falls on the islands. The warmest period is considered the season from November to March, and the coldest - from May to September. The main tourist islands are Praslin, Mahe, La Digue, St. Anne, Frigate, Surf, Denise and Silhouette.

Tourists visiting this paradise, travel with great pleasure, discovering new corners of a unique nature. Holidays in the Seychelles can not be called purely beach or sightseeing, it is rather observant. By bicycle, car or just on foot, you can explore all the new islands, bays and marine parks daily. Some hotels offer tourists to hike with fellow travelers to fabulous places or visit the parking of birds and turtles.
