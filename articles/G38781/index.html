Primorsky Kiliysky area: recreation centers and reviews of tourists

In the Odessa region there is a seemingly inconspicuous village of Primorsky (Kiliysky district). Its center itself is located about one kilometer from the beaches of Zhebriyanskaya bay. However, the resort area is increasingly being built up with various complexes, bases and holiday homes. Those who find spending a vacation on the sea in Odessa itself seem a little expensive may well consider this option. Moreover, getting from the regional center here is not at all a problem. Although the ride is quite a long time - about a hundred and eighty kilometers. A lot of minibuses and suburban buses go to Primorsk. They depart from the Privoz market. The main thing is not to mix up settlements. In the Odessa region there are at least two more towns, which are called exactly the same, only they are in other areas. Therefore, carefully consider the route that you are following.



Rest in Primorsky Kiliysky area

The remoteness of the village from Odessa can be a plus, because this means that the resort is environmentally friendly. And in fact, it is located on the territory of the Danube Reserve. As in many sea ​​resorts located around Odessa, there is clear water, a shallow entrance convenient for children, and huge sandy beaches. The number of vacationers is not so large, so you can always retire. And the prices here are much lower than in Odessa or areas that are closer to the city. For families with children, this is a good budget option for a seaside vacation. Moreover, you can swim here from May to October, the water warms up so well. There are also many estuaries, and the air contains a useful salty suspension. There are in the village Primorsky (Odessa region) recreation centers, pensions. You can stay in the private sector.



Recreation centers in Primorsky

Despite the fact that it is a small town, there are a lot of offers for housing. About twenty tourist camps have been built in its resort area. They are quite far from each other. Since traditionally the inhabitants of the village were not built by the sea, the entire coast for twenty kilometers is occupied by these bases. The vast majority of them are on the so-called “first line”. Yes, and they have their own beaches. That is why the village of Primorsky of the Kiliysky region is interesting. Recreation facilities can offer you a very good service. The rooms in them are of different prices - there are small budget rooms, and there are comfortable luxurious suites.



Leisure opportunities

If you want sun, sea, privacy and simple basic amenities with cheap accommodation, then you should stay in ordinary wooden houses of the "Soviet" type. Then you will use the shower and toilet on site. Hot water in this case will come from solar panels. If comfort plays a decisive role in your journey, book superior rooms. All this will provide you with the village of Primorsky Kiliysky district. Recreation facilities here have large areas where you can park a car, fry barbecue on the grill. They are equipped with modern playgrounds, some have tennis courts. There are bases with dining rooms and three meals a day. The food is not only comprehensive - you can order dishes. Others offer guests to cook on their own and provide equipped kitchens and refrigerators.



Best offers

As mentioned above, the village of Primorsky of the Kiliysky district can offer tourists a lot of places to stay and eat. Recreation facilities here are of different categories, but we will mention here those that have received the best reviews from travelers. For example, the Meliorator complex has a guarded territory, a playground for children, billiards, a bar, and Wi-Fi. There are rooms decorated by designers. Tourists believe that the food here is complex, but not bad. There is a tour desk. Using its services, you can go to Izmail, as well as ride along the Danube Delta. And the base "Lambada" has a whole children's town. Live music is played in the summer café, and massage beds are available on site . The rooms of the second floor are with sea view. Base "Mercury" boasts a sauna and separate cottages with amenities. Thus, vacationers will not interfere with each other.



Something special

For a long time, the village of Primorskoe of the Kiliysky district has been attracting guests. The recreation centers located here are trying to lure tourists with a variety of innovations and bonuses. So, in the complex "Marina" mini-parking is equipped with each room. And some rooms for guests are not only air-conditioned, but also have kitchens. The location of this base is the most successful regarding the tourist infrastructure of Primorsky: market, cafes, entertainment - all around the corner. Frigate has a large, beautiful and green area. There are many flowers, everywhere arbors in the shade, there is a pond with a bridge and a fountain. Outsiders are not allowed here. They take from here on excursions to Vilkovo - Ukrainian Venice, to an ostrich farm, where you will be treated to huge eggs. A "Veterok" is located thirty meters from the sea and has its own beach. The local cafe specializes in Bessarabian cuisine.

Vacation Reviews

Despite the fact that this is a small village remote from Odessa, tourists love Primorsky and dream of coming here on vacation. Despite various political upheavals in Ukraine, practically nothing has changed here. Locals are friendly, honest and helpful. Very reasonable prices, calm, safe. Very clean and warm sea, good entrance, sand with shell rock. Excellent food, especially local fish - fried, smoked. The portions are large, the products are fresh. Everything is very clean, rooms and houses are cleaned constantly. The rooms have everything you need. Although everything is very simple, but convenient and comfortable. The beaches are equipped, you can ride on the "bananas", water skiing, catamarans. Children are especially delighted. The village of Primorsk (Odessa region), whose recreation facilities we have described above, has a fairly high rating among tourists. Prices here are higher than in the private sector, but if you live in a rented apartment or in a room, then you have to go a little to the sea. And at the base you are almost a few steps from the water. Moreover, the beaches are cleaned and leveled with a rake, like somewhere else in Egypt or Turkey.
