Description of the hotel Heliopark Nebug. Heliopark Nebug: reviews of tourists

The hotel complex "Nebug Heliopark" is the only first-class hotel near Tuapse. It offers an amazing panorama of the Black Sea coast. The hotel is ideal for children.

Location

Located "Nebug Heliopark" in the Krasnodar Territory. It was built in the village of Nebug. The hotel complex and the city of Tuapse are separated by 16 kilometers. In the small village of Nebug there are many boarding houses, rest houses, hotels and resorts.





Supermarkets, shops, market and cafes are within walking distance. The hotel is adjacent to a water park, a dolphinarium and the Ice Palace. It is built on the first beach line. Minibuses and buses run from Nebug to Tuapse.

Description

The hotel complex belongs to the HELIOPARK Hotels & Resorts chain. Families, romantic couples, business partners come to it to relax. It is suitable for corporate leisure. The fashionable Heliopark Hotel Nebug provides 90 stylish apartments for tourists.

Guests enjoy a private landscaped beach. They bathe in a large pool. Relax in a Finnish sauna. They take anti-aging treatments in a beauty salon. Hold business meetings in the halls for business events and special occasions.

They are invited to take informative excursions, relax at discos, theme parties. The hotel complex "Nebug Heliopark" is famous for its amazing animation programs.

Number of rooms

The apartments are located in a seven-story building, equipped on the facade with balcony galleries. It offers rooms with stylish interiors. The highlight of the apartment design is bright, cheerful elements of eco-design.

The rooms with huge windows, which offer picturesque landscapes with sea and mountains, include a TV and a minibar. Guests have the opportunity to use Wi-Fi. The bathrooms have a shower, built-in hairdryer and make-up mirror. The toilets in the superior apartments are equipped with a bidet.





For guests prepared sets of towels, hygiene items. For those who have settled in a studio of suites, bathrobes are available. The rooms are furnished with good furniture for relaxation and work. Private rooms have furnished balconies.

Double standard double / twin apartments are the basis of the number of rooms in the hotel complex “Nebug Heliopark”. Superior Superior double rooms have panoramic stained glass windows overlooking the Black Sea coast. If necessary, they organize an extra bed.

The space of two junior suites is well-zoned. They are designed for a family of 4 people. Studio apartments have a modern layout and comfortable furnishings.

In the most presentable room - the only suite - the living room is furnished with upholstered furniture, it has a large LCD TV and a table for meals for 6 people.

Business services

The hotel has a well-organized corporate service. For business guests, the Heliopark Nebug Hotel provides a shuttle service. Participants in business meetings are accommodated in comfort. They are invited to coffee breaks, receptions, business lunches and dinners. They are preparing entertainment programs for them.

Events are arranged in a small hall and a cozy meeting room. They have at their disposal a conference room overlooking the Black Sea. They are provided with technical support, providing them with the necessary sound and demonstration equipment (flipchart, microphones, multimedia projector, etc.).

Nutrition

The main Sorrento restaurant has a banquet room, a bar and a patio, a summer terrace and a relaxing lounge area. It serves Mediterranean-style recipes based on a seasonal menu.

In summer, the restaurant opens onto a rooftop terrace, indulges guests with masterpieces of Indian and Oriental cuisine and offers dishes from the grill menu. The terrace offers wonderful views.

The panoramic Café-Park serves delicious, varied dishes for daily breakfast. In summer, the cafe serves breakfast and lunch. The lobby bar, with access to the pool, offers delicious coffee and delicious cocktails. There is a Sorrentino cafe on the beach.

One part of the guests chose packages with breakfast only. The second preferred vouchers with breakfast and lunch. Food from the restaurant and cafe on request is brought to the apartment.

Beach





Hotel "Heliopark Nebug", reviews of which are left on many tourist sites, has an ennobled beach. A staircase leads to the beach spit. You can go down to it using the funicular. Many tourists use the free shuttle service. The beach is furnished with sun loungers with umbrellas. It has children's inflatable slides and swings.

Vacationers use showers, toilets. Take water transport at a rental point. Stop by for a snack at a coastal cafe. They use the services of a first-aid post and buy souvenirs at a beach shop.

Entertainment

The hotel has an outdoor pool with heated water. Guests relax in the Finnish sauna and jacuzzi, go for a massage. They use the services of a beauty salon and hairdresser. Visit cosmetologist's offices and sign up to the master of nail design.

The restaurant's patio hosts discos and theme nights. A karaoke room is open for guests. They are invited on excursions. Children have fun in the playroom, on the playground with swings and slides. Animators engage tourists in entertainment programs.

The cost of trips





The cheapest thing is to rest on weekdays, during the off-season, with breakfast. The price of tickets varies depending on the selected apartments and amounts to 1900-8000 rubles. On weekends, it rises to 2100-8000 rubles. In high season, rest costs 7000-13 700 rubles. There are still two periods when the Heliopark Nebug hotel prices fluctuate in the range of 4000-10 300 rubles.

Reviews

According to vacationers, the hotel corresponds to the declared stardom. They celebrate its good location, thoughtful descent to the beach and gorgeous panoramic views from the windows and terraces. Guests also like the fact that the Heliopark Nebug hotel has spacious comfortable apartments.

Reviews emphasize moderation of prices and diligence of staff. The maids clean the rooms flawlessly. However, guests would like to see more sumptuous breakfasts. True, no one feels hungry (with all the modesty of breakfasts).





Tourists are happy with the pool, sauna, beach and the fact that there is a dolphinarium and an aqua park nearby. They note that the hotel is comfortable to relax with children. In general, as the guests assure, in HELIOPARK Nebug relax no worse than in foreign hotels.
