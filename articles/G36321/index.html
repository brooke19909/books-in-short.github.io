Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime: review, specifications and reviews

Models from China that are similar in their technical characteristics are sold much cheaper, so the company is simply forced to answer this not only with massive staff cuts, but also with lower prices for its lines.





One of these answers was the Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime. The review of this smartphone, of course, is worth starting with the price: for the characteristics that the device offers, it is quite justified. The average cost of the model range on the Internet market ranges from 10,000 rubles, which may already interest potential buyers.

Generally speaking, the Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime smartphone is suitable for those people who need a hardy battery and high-quality selfie. You can buy a line in white and gray, White and Gray, respectively.

Appearance

In appearance, the Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime VE model is easily identified as a representative of Samsung, even if it did not have a distinctive logo. The line has a typical Samsung design and style: the side frame is made in silver, rounded edges, a classic speaker just above the logo and an oval button in the control place.





There is a slightly protruding lens on the back of the smartphone, a backlight is located to the left, and to the right of the camera is a speaker divided in two by a strip of plastic.

The power key and volume rocker are located on the sides of the device, there are also a headphone jack and micro-port for SD cards. In general, the smartphone gives the impression of an attractive and solid gadget.

Dimensions

The device has the following dimensions: 145x72 mm with a thickness of 9 mm. They are very similar to the Sony Xperia Z3. But with a diagonal of five inches, the body seems too thin and too tall. Nevertheless, the smartphone is convenient to hold in your hand.

The material of the device is plastic, it is assembled quite qualitatively, which is typical for the company's products, in particular for the Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime VE. Reviews about the device sound mostly in a positive way, and there were no serious flaws: nothing backlash, crunches and does not bend. The case is collapsible, the cover under which the batteries and SIM slots are located is easily removable and also put in place. You can remove the battery or replace the SIM card without problems, without fear of breaking anything.

Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime Series Screens

The review of the screen should begin with not the most pleasant moments, as the company clearly decided to save on it. The resolution of the display is 960 by 540 pixels, as on an old mini-flagship, but with a smaller screen. The density of dots per inch is very small - 220 ppi, and plus there are no light sensors, and at the same time an adjustable brightness.





The only thing that pleases is the lack of a TN-matrix in the Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime. User reviews were still heard, and the line was equipped with an IPS analogue. Although the display of the device does not have sufficient (by the standards of modern smartphones) clarity, viewing angles are very good.

Among the few advantages of the display, a rather high brightness of 400 cd / m2 can be noted, which allows you to work normally with the device in the daytime in the fresh air. You should immediately warn that the display does not have an oleophobic coating or protective glass, so the screen ‚Äúcollects‚Äù fingerprints and small scratches. Camera

The device is positioned by the company as a selfie device, which is facilitated by the main 8-megapixel and front 5-megapixel cameras, specially included by engineers in the Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime line. The camera overview is within 85 degrees, which allows them to take high-quality atmospheric and panoramic images.

Pictures are obtained of quite acceptable quality, deserving all praise.

The maximum resolution for video formats is 1920 by 1080 pixels with a recording speed of 30 frames / second. The camera has a tracking autofocus, coupled with a microphone records sound in stereo mode.

The front row is designed not only for calls, but also for wide-angle selfies. Separately, it is worth noting the group picture mode, where it is possible to create a mini-panorama where your entire company will fit.

Multimedia

The company in its devices pays much attention to the multimedia capabilities of gadgets, but not in the case of the Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime.
