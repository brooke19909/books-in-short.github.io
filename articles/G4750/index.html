Beeline Fast Smartphone - Review and Overview of Features

Budget mobile devices with LTE support are no longer considered a miracle. They even began to produce mobile operators. In general, this article describes the Beeline Fast smartphone - reviews about the model and its characteristics.

Equipment

The manufacturer is not too generous and generous - a white cardboard box contains a meager set of items: a charger, a user manual, a smartphone itself and a data cable. The headset is not included, and the power supply is only 1A, so charging will be quite long (about 3 hours), unless you urgently need to buy something more powerful.





The appearance of the smartphone is nothing unique, so it is easily lost in the mass of other budget devices. The Beeline Fast phone is relatively small and light. Rounded corners make the device neat in appearance, convenient to hold in your hands, and it comes in three colors: gold, silver and black.

Assembly and controls

The assembly is of high quality: all parts come into contact tightly, stability is not broken and there are no extraneous sounds. Although the comments left on the Beeline Fast phone note a slight backlash of the control buttons.

Under the back cover is a removable battery. There is also a camera and speaker. On the top there is a headphone jack, and on the opposite side there is a microphone and a micro-usb hole. The buttons to launch the smartphone and change the volume level are made in the color of the case and are located on the right and left side, respectively.





Strange, but the three hardware buttons under the large screen are devoid of backlight. They are not even white, but a mirror shade, so in the dark it will be difficult to notice them. But there is an indicator of events, respectively, you do not have to constantly turn on the screen to check for missed calls and messages.

Screen

The Beeline Fast smartphone is equipped with a weak 5-inch display, the resolution of which is only 854 √ó 480 pixels. Saturated colors and a good matrix for a budget model save the situation. And spoil all the small viewing angles and insufficient supply of brightness, which is critically lacking in bright sunlight.





There is also Mira Vision technology, which adjusts the image on the screen based on the environment. Of course, miracles should not be expected, but still nice. The sensor is quite responsive and responds well to touch, which is confirmed by the comments left on the Beeline Fast smartphone.

Cameras

The smartphone has two cameras in its arsenal. The main module (5MP) has a flash and autofocus. The problem is that the flash is too weak to take good photos in low light, and autofocus is too slow. So the slightest movement during shooting can instantly ruin the picture. Of the settings, there are only the most standard: panorama, HDR and video.

The front camera for its resolution (2MP) makes pretty good pictures. White balance and color reproduction correspond to the level of the device itself. The capture angle is small, so for a group photo you will have to use a selfie stick.

Beeline Fast: Features

The smartphone runs on a 4-core MediaTek processor, Mali T720 graphics accelerator, while it has 1 GB of RAM and OS Android 5.1. The internal memory is 8 GB, but only half of them will be free. A good solution was that it can be increased to 32 GB.

Despite the weak characteristics, the device is fast enough. At least applications and folders open in a split second. Although in this regard, there are still some complaints about the Beeline Fast smartphone. The reviews left by users often indicate a strong overheating of the device while working with resource-intensive applications. Things are not going smoothly with games either. They, of course, will start, but not all will function at maximum settings.

With other multimedia tasks, the smartphone copes much better - when watching video artifacts and audio delays were not noticed. And the browser is pretty smart, scaling and scrolling pages work smoothly, and pages open quickly.

Wireless interfaces

The smartphone was awarded the usual set of wireless functions: bluetooth 4.0, Wi-Fi, high-speed connection and GPS. And it is worth noting that each technology works stably. Indeed, the phone holds a 4G network well, switching to 3G is extremely rare. There are no complaints about the highly sensitive GPS module, which instantly catches satellite signals.





A big surprise for many will be the FM-receiver. True, as an antenna, he wants to see a headset or headphones, which could not be found in the configuration.

Autonomy

In offline mode , the Beeline Fast smartphone supports a rechargeable battery with a capacity of 2200 mAh. In theory, this should be enough for a long time, because the low characteristics of the smartphone and a low-resolution display will not require much energy. In fact, everything is not so simple.





For example, a user has about 8 hours to watch a video in high resolution. Half the time allocated to games is about 4 hours. And everyday use of the device - viewing social networks, short listening to music and watching videos - will allow the smartphone to hold out until the next day.

Conclusion

It should be noted right away that the Beeline company slightly overestimated the value of its device, deciding that its cost is about 6,000 rubles. In fact, it has only two advantages - Android Lollipop 5.1 and 4G connection. The rest leaves much to be desired. A large display with indecently low resolution, a small amount of RAM, a weak "stuffing" sometimes too spoil the impression of using the device. This is often evidenced by reviews, and many users say that for the money you can now choose a more fundamental model.
