Kan - a river in the Krasnoyarsk Territory

The Kan River is located in the center of the Krasnoyarsk Territory, is a large tributary of the river. Yenisei. It begins on the territory of the East Sayan, on the north side of the Kansky Belogorye in the place of the union of the river. Quiet Kahn and Wild Kahn.

Characteristic

A wide floodplain and meandering channel make the river. Kahn is attractive to kayaking enthusiasts. On its sandy shores it is pleasant to relax with a tent, if you find a gentle, safe place where there will be no cliff.





Tourists and residents of nearby territories like to swim in the water and admire the surrounding landscapes. It is interesting to swim past the islands enveloped by the current, to get acquainted with mountain peaks and hills.

There are places through which it is easy to drive to the bed by car or walk. Due to the large amount of sludge, the water has a brownish tint. At the bottom are pebbles, clay and sand, algae.

The Kan River in the Krasnoyarsk Territory passes the deepening of the Kansk-Rybinsk Basin and the spurs in the south of the Yenisei Ridge. Mountain part

The most watery phase in the water regime is high water in summer and spring. There were years when floods reached catastrophic proportions due to heavy rains.

The Kan is a river with large tributaries: on the left is Big Urey, Peso, Anzha, Kirel, Rybnaya, on the right is Agul, Kungus, Nemkina, Kurysh, God-nai.

The upper part of the course is an ordinary mountainous waterway. Water flows quickly along the rapids, bypassing the steep and rocky shores. Athletes, as well as people who are interested in non-trivial obstacles, are advised to visit between the tributaries of the Tuksha and Yanga. History of the surroundings

In ancient times, cats and Kamasians lived on the riverbank, communicating in Celtic. The Slavic population settled here with the construction of the Krasnoyarsk prison in 1628. In the first half of the 18th century, people were more drawn here. Today, on local shores, people live in small villages: Sayansky district, Irbeysky (Ivanovka village, Aleksandrovka).

There are also a lot of residents in the village of Orye, Yudino, and Can-Oklare. The geographical location of this area is in the hands of its residents, so the Kansky district is especially densely populated. The city of Kansk takes 4 place in the number of citizens in the region. The greatest emphasis is placed on the food and light industry.

Big catch

Fishing on the Kan River is a common way to spend a day off for residents and visitors. Riding on rowing and motor boats is carried out. Many managed to identify fishing spots for themselves.

Good biting is observed throughout the year. In the spring, spawning occurs, so only one fishing rod is allowed. The predefined number of hooks is pre-set.





Having been here, the fisherman has a great chance to go home with excellent roach (silver-horned), dace, carp, pike, lenok, grayling, perch, ruff, bream, tench, burbot or ide. The selection is pretty wide. 20 years ago, sterlet and Siberian sturgeon were caught here. After they built power plants on the rivers, it is extremely difficult to meet these species; they are listed in the local Red Book.
