The subtleties of a beach holiday

The trip to the beaches of Egypt should be approached with all responsibility: the percentage of risk of harm to health is quite high. Coral reefs and not always sandy bottom make visitors swim in specialized shoes (it is inexpensive, it is sold at almost every corner, most often these are simple rubber slippers). Therefore, when vacationing with children, you should think about choosing the most convenient place for their safety. If even this did not work out, then almost all hotels provide a free shuttle service to the beach at the choice of the vacationer (this is indicated by reviews, Iberotel Il mercato 5 * among such hotels).
