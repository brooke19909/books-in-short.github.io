The best monochrome printers for home and office

Printers are matrix, inkjet, sublimation and laser, and the color of the print - monochrome and multicolor.

Monochrome printers that work according to the laser principle reproduce images using the method of xerography. From their color "brothers" they differ only in the presence of only one black paint.

There are monochrome devices that are designed for home use, but there are also what are most suitable for large companies and offices.

Features of monochrome printers

Black and white printers are more compact than color printers. In addition, their difference is profitability.

Such printers, on average, can print 20-60 pages per minute. Almost everyone has network support, paper trays for receiving and receiving, and finishing devices.

Monochrome laser printers can provide good print quality.

If we compare inkjet and laser printers with a black and white device, then the laser model will show the best print speed and the price of the print will be much lower.

For home use

An inexpensive monochrome printer will be a bargain for use by schoolchildren and students, teachers and teachers.

And mothers of babies can use such a printer to print textbooks and coloring pictures.

As a rule, a laser printer is a bit more expensive than an inkjet. But its advantage is cheaper maintenance and operation.

For institutions, firms and offices

Black and white printing equipment is widely used by many companies. Contracts and other legal documents are freely printed. In addition, owners of such devices can provide printing services or sell copies of forms.
