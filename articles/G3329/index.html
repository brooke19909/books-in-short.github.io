How to make a do-it-yourself elf costume

The traditions of the celebration of Christmas came to us from distant times. In Western European culture, the good wizard - Santa Claus and his assistants - reigns supreme. Dressing up in an elf costume for a baby is like touching the main character of a fairy tale.

Christmas Elf Costume Story

The Christmas elf, as a character in folklore, appeared a long time ago. These were the little creatures that accompanied Santa Claus. Chief assistants and advisers. They distributed gifts to obedient children and helped craft souvenirs.

If you look at old postcards, you can see little men in plain dresses. These little toilers are dressed in comfortable, non-marking clothes. Over time, ideas about magical creatures changed. They were credited with more and more opportunities, and their outfits became brighter.

Modern Christmas Elf Costume

If you carefully analyze modern Christmas cards, you can see that the elf costume consists of:

  • Caps.
  • A shoe with a bent toe.
  • Green or red jacket.

Quite often, the ends of hats and socks are decorated with bells or pompons. This is what the traditional festive attire of the inhabitants of the north looks like. An elf costume for a girl is complemented with either a longer tunic or a green skirt.

Colors are also traditional. It is green, red and white. They can be placed anywhere alongside, creating a unique elf costume. The photo shows what color options can be.

Looking at old Christmas cards, you can see Santa's helpers of both sexes. If your baby no longer wants to dress up as a princess, then sew her an elf costume for the girl.

Choose primary colors and matching fabrics. As mentioned above, the colors will be mostly green and red. For a girl, you can diversify with silver and golden fabrics and various tinsel, beads, and rhinestones.

Elf costume for boy

Children love to dress up in a variety of carnival costumes. But the main criterion must be taken into account - it must be an active and courageous character. The elf costume for the boy from this point of view fits perfectly.

You can order a ready-made kit, their choice is quite large. But it is not difficult to make a carnival outfit for a child on your own. You will need a green T-shirt or sweatshirt, a little red fabric and a wide belt. You can see what an elf costume looks like for a boy (photo below).





The green T-shirt should be remade a little to make it look like an elven one. It is enough to sew a red collar. If there is a desire and time, you can make uneven edges on the bottom and on the sleeves. Put on your belt and the main part of the costume is ready.

The cap and boots are best made from a dense fabric, such as felt. Their patterns are very simple and do not require special skills. The hat is just two stitched triangles with a lapel.





Shoes are usually made in the form of socks, which are worn over ordinary shoes. However, this detail of the costume can be missed so that the child can safely run and play.

Christmas Elf Costume for Little Kids

Do not deny yourself the pleasure of dressing up a baby. Little children in Christmas costumes look especially cute and touching. Be sure to take a series of quality photos. They will become the real decoration of the family photo archive.

Do-it-yourself elf costume for a baby or baby is not difficult to do. If you know how to knit, then make pants and a jacket in a red-green strip. Garnish them with white pompons and bells.





For those who can neither knit nor sew, there is also a way out. Buy a white jumpsuit with a hood for your baby. In addition, you need ribbons of red and green. Now embroider the future suit with ribbons and decorate the top of the cap with a pompon.

If your choice fell on a finished suit, then you should be very careful about the material from which it is made. After all, the skin of the baby is very delicate and sensitive. In any case, it should be well removed and all details checked. So that nothing hangs, and the child does not choke on an accidentally torn button.

DIY elf costume for adult

The spirit of New Year's magic affects everyone without exception. And adults, like babies, want to be transformed into a holiday. There is nothing easier to create an excellent mood than to dress up in a carnival costume.

Any green jacket, sweater or T-shirt is complemented by a red scarf. The elven cap can be remade from the cap of Santa Claus, wash the green ribbons on it, and fasten the bells on the crown.

If there is time and desire, then you can independently sew an elf carnival costume for the whole family. Buy everyone long t-shirts of the same green color. Sew red collars on them. Be sure to wear wide belts.

Red or green pants can be sewn on a simple pajama pattern. They should be free enough and not restrict movements. And for mothers and daughters, striped white-green "Tatyana" skirts are well suited. Quite a bit of effort and a fun little onion is ready.

How to make a Christmas elf costume if there is no sewing machine

The lack of special devices for sewing is not a reason to abandon creativity. Almost any thing can be knitted or sewn together.





Each carnival costume has the main details on which emphasis is placed. In a Christmas elf costume, this is a combination of colors, a cap and slippers. You can, of course, buy everything ready-made, but the quality of the purchased items, as a rule, is not the best.

Let's start with a hat. You will need green and red felt, scissors, a needle and thread. Cut out fabric of different colors along the same long triangle. Trim the edges, and sew them together in small stitches. Now do a little lapel. On the crown you can sew a bells. The elf's hat is ready.

Sewing shoes is a little more difficult, and it will be difficult for a child to walk in them. For a home holiday, tie your kid red-green socks. And for the children's party, the rest of the costume will be enough.

Elf jacket can be made from any green or red T-shirt. Make her an uneven bottom, sew in a collar and add a belt.

With very little effort, your baby will become one of the main characters of Christmas.
