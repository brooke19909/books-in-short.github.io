Madeira Reviews of tourists about this flowering island

In the northern waters of the Atlantic Ocean lies the picturesque island of Madeira. Reviews of tourists about this resort are only the most positive. Everyone who has been here at least once remains in love with this island. Noisy streams running from the mountains, powerful waterfalls, a riot of greenery and exotic flowers - all this attracts vacationers from all over the world.

Location

Madeira is an island of volcanic origin, its entire territory is covered by mountains and forests. It is located just 545 kilometers from the African coast, in the northern Atlantic. It is part of the Porto Santo archipelago and, together with the islands of Selvagenes and Desertas, forms the autonomous region of Portugal.



Capital of the island

Madeira was discovered at the beginning of the 15th century, in the era of sailors and great geographical discoveries. Currently, it has six large cities, the capital is Funchal. An amazing green paradise is the island of Madeira. Reviews of tourists who have been here on vacation will help make the right choice.

Climate

This excellent land, according to legend, is considered a miracle of the surviving part of Atlantis. Throughout the island there is a mild climate, which is significantly influenced by the warm waters of the Gulf Stream. Here, the summer season lasts a whole year, in January-month the air temperature is in the range of 19-20 degrees. Madeira has all the necessary conditions for healing. Reviews of tourists about the excellent climate of this area prompt you to choose this particular island for a vacation.

Beaches

Madeira delights with its amazing natural landscapes. The beaches on the island are covered with basalt stones and pebbles. The rocky shores have their own charm. When ocean waters are washed by sun-drenched beaches, you forget about all the inconveniences. Sandy recreation areas with black volcanic sand are located in the Kanisal area, they are surrounded by bare black-brown cliffs. A beach called Porto da Cruz is famous for its very well-developed entertainment area. Remarkable service differs Madeira. Reviews of tourists about this resort are only positive, there is a high quality of services and numerous entertainments have been created for both adults and children. In the midst of the season, prices for accommodation in three- and four-star hotels, including breakfast, range from 26 to 55 Euro per person.





The beauties and sights of Madeira

This island can be called a real blooming paradise, amazing roses and many other beautiful flowers grow throughout its territory. One of the main attractions of Madeira are the botanical gardens. Here plants and flowers grow, collected by Portuguese sailors for three hundred years. Two thirds of the island's territory is occupied by a protected area, which is strictly protected.

Of the architectural sights, it should be noted the Franciscan monastery, built in the 16th century. At this place, tourists are given not only the opportunity to see the manufacturing process, but also try the famous Madeira wine. The real blooming fairy tale can be called the island of Madeira. Reviews speak of its growing popularity among tourists around the world. Such a vacation will bring a lot of positive emotions and pleasant memories. Walking along mountain paths, visiting waterfalls and other exotic places - all this offers Madeira. The prices here are moderate and affordable, which makes holidays even more attractive.
