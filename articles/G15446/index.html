Hotel Meraden Opus 3 * (India, Anjuna): description and reviews of tourists

India is a country that is becoming more and more popular with travelers every year. And tourists from all over the world seek to spend at least a few days in Goa. This is a paradise island with beautiful beaches and incredibly beautiful landscapes. Of course, when planning a trip, an important point is the choice of hotel, because comfort depends on it. One of the rather popular stopping places is the Meraden Opus 3 * hotel. This hotel stands out for its favorable location, good service and pleasantly low prices.

Description of infrastructure and location





A small city hotel Meraden Opus 3 * is located in the northern part of Goa, very close to the center of the colorful resort town of Anjuna. The beach of the same name is only 700 m from the hotel building. By the way, the distance to Dabolim International Airport is also not too big - 50 km. Tourists should not worry about a long trip, as the hotel can be reached in 40 minutes by convenient transfer. And at 19 km there is a large railway station Tivim.

The hotel itself is a five-story building with modern renovation. Own territory here is very small, but decorated with greenery, exotic flowers and palm trees. Tourists like to spend time in a hotel - here you can count on modern comfort.

Hotel Meraden Opus 3 * (India, Anjuna): photo and description of rooms





As already mentioned, this is a very small hotel - on its territory there are 22 standard rooms. Guest reviews suggest that they are all comfortable, cozy, with a discreet, but very elegant design. In addition to large comfortable beds and a spacious wardrobe, the room also has a convenient desk, which is ideal for work. At your disposal will be a spacious balcony with wooden furniture - you can enjoy the fresh sea air and city views.

It is difficult to imagine the life of a modern traveler without at least a basic set of household appliances. And the Meraden Opus 3 * hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable pastime. You can watch TV, use the phone, regulate the temperature in the room with the help of an air conditioner, and keep valuables in a small safe. The minibar is filled with drinking water, but other drinks can also be delivered at an additional cost. Guests can enjoy free internet. The room also has a set for making drinks (tea, coffee), as well as an electric kettle.

The bathroom is also quite spacious - tiled floors and walls, modern plumbing, a shower and round-the-clock availability of hot water. Guests can also use a hairdryer, clean towels (changed daily) and quality branded cosmetics from the hotel.

Do guests offer food?





The issue of nutrition is very important, because good food is part of relaxation. Paying for accommodation at the Meraden Opus 3 * hotel, tourists get the right to attend breakfast daily, which are held in a buffet restaurant. According to reviews, the menu here is straightforward, but there is a choice of dishes, and the food is fresh and tasty. You will be offered several types of fried eggs, toasts, side dishes, light salads, fruits, pastries and some traditional Indian dishes.

In the same restaurant you can have lunch or dinner, but already ordering from the menu and paying the bill. In any case, do not worry about food, because the city has a lot of restaurants, cafes, fast food establishments and even markets where you can buy some local delicacies at a good price.

Beach: amenities and entertainment on the beach





Of course, arriving on the island of Goa, you expect to have fun on the beach. What does the hotel Meraden Opus 3 * offer its customers? Anjuna, a picturesque beach, is located about 700 m from the hotel. A direct road leads to it, which takes no more than 10-15 minutes. Most travelers do not mind daily walks.

The coast here is very beautiful, the water is clean, and the entrance to the sea is convenient. This is a public beach, so renting sun loungers, umbrellas and beach towels is charged separately. On the other hand, the prices here are not high, and you can have a good snack at the beach bars at any time of the day.

Tourists who want a more active holiday will also not be disappointed. On the shore, you can rent a boat or a catamaran without a problem, take a boat trip, go water skiing. Also popular among travelers are diving, sailing and windsurfing (on the beach you can take instruction and training courses).

Hotel Meraden Opus 3 * (Anjuna): additional service and leisure for tourists





Of course, you can count on some additional conditions. In almost the entire building you can connect to the Internet, and its speed is quite good. A small but compact parking lot is equipped near the building (by the way, renting transport in the city is easy and inexpensive). The hotel also offers currency exchange, dry cleaning and laundry services, as well as asking for a doctor in case of health problems.

On the roof there is a small pool with clean, fresh water, and nearby, on the terrace, there are comfortable sun loungers. Here, guests can refresh themselves, sunbathe, enjoy the scenery and just chat with each other.

Hotel reviews: travel recommendations

Of course, an important question is what tourists think about the Meraden Opus 3 * hotel? Reviews are by and large positive. Despite its modest size, the hotel is very comfortable and cozy. All employees communicate politely and are ready to help with the solution of any problems at any time.

The location is not bad - near the beach, and cafes, and shops. And the prices for accommodation are affordable, which makes the hotel extremely popular among lovers of economical holidays.
