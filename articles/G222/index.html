Aslanis Village Hotel 3 * (Kos island, Tigaki, Marmari, Mastichari): description and reviews

Located 15 km from the capital of. Kos, the town of Marmari is one of the most popular resorts among tourists around the world. Especially a lot of surfers come here. The winds in Marmari are often blowing, and for lovers of this sport, it is a perfect place. But among ordinary vacationers, this resort is, of course, very popular.

The tourist infrastructure in Marmari is developed simply superbly. Finding a good hotel here will not be difficult if necessary. For example, tourists can rent comfortable rooms in Marmari in the Aslanis Village Hotel 3 * hotel.





Location Features

This complex is located not in the village of Marmari itself, but about 2 km from it among the dense tropical forest near the village of Mastikhari. At 3 km from the hotel is another popular holiday resort Tigaki. The distance from the nearest airport to this hotel is only about 15 km. In this regard, many tourists find the location of the hotel, of course, very convenient. To get to the place for vacationers who rent rooms in Aslanis Village Hotel 3 *, you need no more than half an hour. At the same time, planes over the hotel do not fly with a roar and do not interfere with the rest of the guests.

Guests of the complex can reach Marmari with its busy tourist life in just a few minutes by public transport. A bus stop is only about 50 meters from the hotel buildings. If desired, guests can even simply make a pleasant walk in Marmari. The road to this town from the hotel with a calm step will take no more than 30-40 minutes.





Regarding the sea, this complex is, unfortunately, not very well located. It is located on the third line and the distance from its buildings to the seashore is about 800 m. Hotel guests have to walk to the beach on foot, thus, it takes quite a long time. But the cost of living in this hotel is less than in three-star recreation centers built on the first coastline.
