Picturesque Inal Bay: Inal Hotel and other tourist destinations

In the Tuapse district on the Black Sea coast there are many corners that are simply created for relaxation. But the most popular, judging by the annual attendance, is still considered Inal Bay. Hotel Inal is one of the many establishments that were built so that hundreds of thousands of tourists could spend their holidays here, admiring the unique beauty of the nature of the Krasnodar Territory. But many people come here not only to enjoy the gentle sea and picturesque landscapes. Some tourists also seek here in the hope of improving their health. This is not surprising, because a deposit of blue clay was discovered on the shore of the bay. A small lake is equipped for visitors here, where everyone can take therapeutic mud baths.

Complex "Inal"

Fifteen kilometers from the village of Dzhubga is the Inal Hotel, built on the coast of Inal Bay. This is a hotel complex, which is located in a mountain forest, away from the private sector and industrial zone. For tourists, cozy wooden houses are provided here, where there is everything you need for a comfortable stay.





Located near the famous Inal Bay, the Inal Hotel offers its visitors to sunbathe on a spacious pebble beach, swim in the clear sea and take wellness treatments in mud baths. Guests are constantly entertained by animators. Kids can spend time on the playground, and for adults, discos are organized in the evening. The main attraction here, of course, is the Inal Bay itself. The Inal Hotel, in addition, offers tourists a rich excursion program. They can visit the dolphinarium or penguinarium located nearby in the village, the water park in Nebug, the safari park in Gelendzhik and other interesting places.

"Villa Alla"

Despite the fact that Inal Bay is nearby, hotels with a swimming pool are especially popular with tourists. Not everyone likes to walk to the beach. And in extreme heat, you often want to freshen up without leaving your home. An outdoor pool is ideal for this. There are several such establishments in the vicinity of the bay. Among them are the Infanta Club, Oasis Park hotels, as well as the Paradise Valley hotels Breeze, Adriatic Inn and Altai. These are mainly establishments from which more than 200 meters from the sea.   • comfort (double);
  • Suite (family).

Every year there are hundreds who want to visit such an excellent institution. Therefore, you need to plan a trip in advance using the reservation service.

"Old lock"

In Tuapse district, Inal Bay is considered the best place to relax. Hotels by the sea in this place are always very popular. They are preferred by those who like to swim, sunbathe and enjoy all the charms of a beach holiday.





An excellent example of such an institution is the hotel "Old Castle". This is a two-story building, which is located just five minutes walk from the sea. Guests are offered to stay in comfortable rooms for 2, 3 and 5 people with comfortable furniture, which include a TV, fan, refrigerator and a private bathroom. Those who prefer to eat on their own are settled on the second floor. A shared kitchen is specially provided for them. The rest can eat in the cafe located next to the hotel. Its terrace offers amazing views of the sea and the surrounding area. Getting here is easy. For an additional fee, the owners can provide a transfer and meet their guests in any of the nearest settlements. They guarantee future residents a wonderful stay and excellent living conditions.
