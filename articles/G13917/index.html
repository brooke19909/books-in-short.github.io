Holidays here are quite popular among residents of the Taman Peninsula, as well as among visiting guests. What attracts tourists to this area? Let's get a look.





Constantly expanding

For quite a long time, vacations on the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov have been inextricably linked with a specific list of settlements. One of them is the Golubitskaya stanitsa. Rest on the territory of this resort involves a stop at the following points:

  1. Sanatoriums and medical institutions.
  2. Recreation facilities and pensions.
  3. Private housing.

By the number of various recreational facilities, the first place on the Taman Peninsula is precisely occupied by the Golubitskaya stanitsa. Vacations for a huge number of guests are organized by both commercial enterprises and ordinary residents. Every year, new pensions and hotels appear on the territory of this resort. The coastal line is literally strewn with them. Private sector residents also keep up with commercial projects. They invest their own money in the construction of small hotels and houses for tourists.





Relaxation for every taste.

For those who are accustomed to comfort, a boarding house or recreation center is perfect. The village of Golubitskaya has many institutions of this type in various categories. For couples with children, such complexes as Altair, Antey, Sea Wave, Kuban and many others are perfect. It offers cozy rooms and houses in different price categories. Moreover, the cost of living depends on the season of arrival. Cozy gazebos located on the territory of boarding houses, cafes, sports grounds and playrooms for children, specially equipped platforms for cooking barbecue, fitness equipment, a private beach, parking, equipment rental and many other services are offered by almost any recreation center. The village of Golubitskaya also posted on its territory pensions that entice guests to stay in attic-type rooms. For example, an institution called "Cypress".





Would you like to settle with a dog? You are welcome!

It is worth noting that, compared with other settlements of the Taman Peninsula, the cost of rest on the territory of the village of Golubitskaya is somewhat overstated. The reason for this is the high popularity of the resort. Pensions in various ways try to attract the attention of customers. For example, in some establishments pets are allowed. Such a service offers the recreation center "Christina." The village of Golubitskaya is famous for the hospitality of local residents. On the territory of almost every private house there is a guest house or even a small hotel complex. Additionally, you can use the food services.





Shell invasion

The main reason why tourists come here is the sea, the sun and the beach. The latter, I must say, in the morning is a stunning sight. During the night, a huge amount of large shell rock is thrown onto the coast, which makes up the top layer of the beach. During the day, large fragments are crushed and turned into fine sand. You can and should walk on it barefoot: this is the perfect acupressure for the feet. It should be noted that the largest and most famous is the central beach. For him, for the most part, a crowd of vacationers flocked to the private sector. If we go towards the Peresyp, the number of people decreases. Near the sea, various events are often held to coincide with any event: whether it is the opening of the holiday season or Neptune's day.





Caught a fish...

Fishermen will also be able to enjoy their holidays in this paradise of the Krasnodar Territory. Of course, for a leisurely pursuit of what you love, you need an appropriate quiet atmosphere. You can fully enjoy fishing in several places:

  1. The so-called ditch, located next to the village, near the highway leading to Kerch.
  2. Eric, located right behind the first fishing spot, about two kilometers from Golubitskaya.
  3. Estuary. It is located near Eric. The estuary is shallow, and there are a huge number of different fish in it: pike, bream, catfish, tench, common carp and others.
  4. The mouth of the Kuban River.
  5. And, of course, the sea.




Have fun until the morning

In addition to passive relaxation on the beach, swimming in warm sea waters and fishing, many tourists are interested in entertainment. Guests who come to such a place as the Golubitskaya stanitsa are no exception. Rest here, to the delight of visitors, is quite diverse and interesting. On the territory of this small settlement there are many different places of entertainment. Many restaurants and bars are open until the morning. Often from the depths of the cafe comes amazing live music. In addition, great shows are regularly held here.





Water and dolphins

Fans of outdoor activities will surely enjoy the Amazon water park. It is worth noting that on the Taman Peninsula this institution is one of a kind. In addition to the water park, a dolphinarium is located near the resort. To the delight of adults and children, communication with representatives of the marine world, visiting this place is not limited. There are other entertainments here, such as: Planet of the Apes, an amusement park, etc. If you like animals, then you will probably be interested in visiting the equestrian center and crocodile farm.

Sports such as windsurfing and paragliding allow tourists to soar to the clouds. If you are afraid of these extreme entertainments, but still want to see the landscape from a bird's eye view, a panoramic walk on a small plane is at your service.





Lotus, museums and healing mud

In addition, many institutions are ready to diversify the rest of their guests with all kinds of excursions. A trip to the mud volcano located in the village of Za Rodina, a visit to the recently opened dolphinarium, a visit to the Lotus Valley (Akhtanizovsky estuary) and museums ... The latter are located in the village, better known as the port of Temryuk. The village of Golubitskaya also has an attraction on its territory: a mud lake. This pond is located near the main beach of the resort. It is noteworthy that its size is very large: about half a kilometer wide. However, the maximum depth is only one and a half meters, which allows bathing even for children and citizens who cannot swim without any problems. The mud of the lake is characterized by an amazing healing effect, which is explained by the content of calcium, bromine, hydrogen sulfide and iodine in it. This healing composition helps to cope with skin problems.

Every year a huge number of guests come in order to fully enjoy the delights of southern life that the village of Golubitskaya offers (Krasnodar Territory). Visitors will find a vacation for every taste: active entertainment, lazy pastime, and rich excursion programs - all this is welcomed by friendly locals. And, of course, the warm sea will warm tourists in their arms, yearning for winter.
