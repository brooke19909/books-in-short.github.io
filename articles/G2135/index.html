Where to go with children in Karaganda? Where to go with a girl in Karaganda?

Karaganda is one of the largest cities in Kazakhstan. A large number of minerals are mined here, and besides this, there is an international airport. Every year, a large number of tourists come from various cities of our planet. Therefore, the question of where to go in Karaganda is very relevant. In this article we will tell you about the most interesting places that you can visit with your children. Let's start our review.

Safari park

If you want to have a great rest with your whole family, we suggest paying attention to this option. Safari Park is a place where both adults and children will have fun. For the smallest there are pools with bright balls, a fabulous train, carousels, as well as small slides. Experienced animators organize various games and performances in which children of all ages participate with great pleasure. In the park you can play slot machines and even arrange exciting competitions between family members. There are also various entertainment shows, which are very popular with visitors.

Center address: Cosmonauts street, 1v.

City leisure park

We invite you to get acquainted with one of the most popular holiday destinations among residents of Karaganda. The construction of the city park began long before the Great Patriotic War, and ended after its completion. Every day a large number of people come here. Here you can not only enjoy the fresh air and admire the beauty of the surrounding nature, but also do the following:

  • admire the fountains that are in the park;
  • visit a children's complex with Soviet carousels;
  • swim in a boat or catamaran;
  • workout;
  • taste delicious and varied dishes in a restaurant or cafe;
  • visit children's playrooms with colorful trampolines and intricate mazes;
  • listen to good music;
  • take part in interesting entertainment programs;
  • play machines and much more.

You can spend the whole day in the park, completely forgetting about time. So do not deny yourself the pleasure and visit with your children one of the most popular places in Karaganda.

Address: Kazybekbiysky district, Bukla river valley.

Waterpark "Dolphin"

This is one of the great options where to go with a child in Karaganda. The water park is loved by adults and children. Here you can forget about all the problems and get a huge amount of the most positive emotions. For visitors, a huge number of slides, two children's pools of different depths (they are decorated with large inflatable toys in the form of dolphins and outlandish fish) and one adult, a Turkish sauna, a jacuzzi, a Russian bath. In the water park you can enroll in a group on water aerobics. These classes are very popular with beautiful ladies. If you are hungry, then it does not matter, because in the water park there is a cafe with delicious food and affordable prices.

Address: 33 Boulevard of the World.

Circus

Where to go in Karaganda to be interesting not only for children but also for adults? A great option is to attend a circus performance. This is a place with an incredibly magical atmosphere in which various miracles take place. What you will not see here: gymnasts with acrobatic stunts right under the circus dome, trainers who managed to tame such animals as lions and tigers, funny clowns and much more. Address: Komissarov street, 45.

Zoo

Another option where to go in Karaganda is to visit the city zoo. This entertainment will appeal to both kids and adults. The zoo is one of the main attractions of the city; the local budget annually allocates a large sum for its maintenance. Here you can see especially rare species of animals that are listed in the Red Book. Children will be able to learn interesting facts about such representatives of the fauna world as the black fox, Siberian lynx, and zebra. If you have never ridden a horse, you can fill this gap. Purebred white and black horses will be welcome to all comers. For young children there is a pony. On the territory there is a contact zoo. This is one of the few options where you can go in Karaganda to pet and feed cute animals.

Address: 111 Ermekova street.

Where to go with a girl in Karaganda?

A date with a girl you like is always exciting. And if you want everything to go well, think in advance how to surprise your sweetheart. We offer you the best options where to go in Karaganda:

  • You can visit one of the many cafes or restaurants in the city, depending on how much money you have. We recommend "Cinnamon" and "Ancient Rome". There is always delicious food and a pleasant atmosphere.
  • If you do not know where to go with a girl in Karaganda, we recommend that you visit a local cinema. Choose your favorite movie and stock up on popcorn.
  • You can just take a walk around the evening city. After all, winter, when everything shines with beautiful illumination, is the time for romantic walks. Take spray cans and have fun drawing funny pictures in the snow.
