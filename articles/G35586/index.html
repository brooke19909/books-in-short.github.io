Description of the hotel Olesya (Loo)

The institution is located in a village called Loo, 18 kilometers from Sochi. It got its name from the name of the Abazin feudal family, who lived here in the 13-14 centuries. - Lau (Low, Loova).

Hotel "Olesya" (Loo) is a modern four-story building with a landscaped area. Sunbathers can already take sunbaths by the pool in the hotel in April. You can also use umbrellas and sun loungers. The opening of the beach-bathing season is in May, it ends in late October. The beaches of Loo (pebble) are closed from cold winds and are distinguished by their unusual purity of water. A full relaxation of guests will be complemented by a visit to the pool. At the service of fans of maintaining physical fitness - a variety of weight training equipment. Hotel "Olesya" (Loo) is equipped with a cozy courtyard, which next to the pool is a modern cafe "Olesya". It offers original gourmet dishes of European and Russian cuisine. In addition, from the chef you can get exclusive offers. Three meals a day (buffet system) are included in the price. In summer, you can spend time on the terrace, breathe in the fresh sea air, admiring the beautiful view. The cafe offers a wide range of drinks, original snacks and shisha of various kinds. Location

Olesya (Loo) is a hotel located 150 meters from the AquaLoo (entertainment center) and the water park. The Black Sea coast can be reached on foot in about 5 minutes. The shortest distance from the hotel to the water's edge is 315 m. It features an on-site restaurant, an outdoor pool and free private parking. The railway station "Mountain Air" is located from the hotel in a 3-minute drive. The international airport is 45 km away.

Excursions

Hotel Olesya (Loo) offers many interesting excursions. The most popular are:

  • The Agursky Gorge is a rather attractive place for tourists, located near Sochi, four kilometers from the Black Sea coast. Here are amazing Agursky waterfalls, a visit to which will not leave anyone indifferent. Agura (a mountain river) begins on the slopes of the Alec ridge and rushes to the sea, creating cascades of waterfalls. The most famous of them is a waterfall of 2 cascades, 12 and 18 m. Under them there is a deep vast reservoir. Those who want to go to the waterfall must go through a metal bridge. Another low multi-jet waterfall is located at a distance of 500 m. Its flows fall into a pool of 5-meter depth. Lumps of limestone are piled at the bottom of the gorge. Upstream the river you can see the third waterfall, roaring down with a roar.
  • Water Park "Aqua Loo", according to guest reviews, is a terrific place. Here, under one roof, a lot of entertainment and wellness structures are collected, necessary for an unforgettable full and relaxation. The medical sports and entertainment center consists of a boarding house (900 European-class rooms), a concert hall, a bowling center, a sanatorium, a fitness complex, bars, restaurants, night clubs and an indoor water park, the only one in the south of the Russian Federation. It has 10 super-fast slides and 5 pools. Here is the famous eco-friendly beach 250 m long - sand and pebble, with sun loungers, umbrellas and fountains. A bewitching panorama of the Caucasus Mountains with their magnificent sunsets and sunrises, clean air completes the picture of an unforgettable vacation.
  • Writers Alley, which is located near the Green Theater (Riviera Park, Sochi). It contains 21 sculptural portraits of famous writers (Russian and Soviet). Monuments of writers are forged from copper. The alley is a historical and cultural monument of local importance. She is a favorite method of vacationers walking.
  • The Berendeevo Tsarstvo National Park is located in the Lazarevsky District of Sochi, on a tributary of the Kuapse River. Its length is 1200 m. Tourists prefer to visit this place with children, the route is easy and interesting for them, and in the evening the kids will be entertained by the characters of their favorite cartoons. This place is amazing for its picturesque and beautiful nature.

Sochi has a lot of interesting, noteworthy tourists. Visiting excursions enriches the rest with impressions, entertains and broadens one's horizons.
