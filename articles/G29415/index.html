Hotel Kassavetis Hotel Aparts 3 * (Greece, Crete): overview, description, rooms and reviews

The island of Crete, located in the Mediterranean Sea between Europe, Asia and Africa, possesses perhaps the best resort infrastructure among the islands of Greece. The famous island of Zeus and the Minotaur is simultaneously one of the leading cultural and economic regions of modern Greece. He certainly has his own face: the immediately clear crystal clear beauty of the sea, picturesque cliffs overgrown with pine trees, golden sandy beaches, heady air, incomprehensible hospitality and kindness of local residents, delicious local cuisine.





Many small cozy economy hotels on this island make vacation on it affordable for budget travelers.

Climate and seasonality

When is the best time to go to the Land of Zeus and the Minotaur? The answer to this question depends on the climatic preferences of tourists. Generally speaking, the first tourists begin to arrive on the island in April, and the latter leave the island in late October. The Mediterranean subtropical climate is characterized by dry and hot summers and mild windy winters.

High tourist season occurs in the summer months. It was then that a stream of vacationers from Western Europe sent to the Mediterranean countries. The air temperature, on average, is 28 - 30 0 C. You should buy tours for this time ahead of time, for four to five months, including the hotel - the subject of this article. The low season is in the winter months. At this time, lovers of excursions indifferent to a beach holiday go to Crete. At this time, the air temperature is set at 15 - 16 0 C. The weather is well suited for excursions.
