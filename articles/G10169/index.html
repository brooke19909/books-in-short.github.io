Vinyl record butterflies

If you want to create something original and non-banal, you need to use an unusual material, an excellent solution is old vinyl records. However, you need to prepare yourself in advance for the fact that you will have to tinker with creating a single butterfly for quite some time. After all, this process is very painstaking. But the product will turn out very extraordinary.

So, how to cut a butterfly from a vinyl record:

  1. The first step is to prepare a plate, plain foil, scissors, chalk or a contrast marker. You‚Äôll have to work with the oven, so it‚Äôs wiser not to bring the kids to work.
  2. Spread the foil over the surface of the pan.
  3. Put a plate on top on which first outline the silhouette of the desired butterfly.
  4. Place the pan in the oven and lightly heat it.
  5. Take out and immediately cut out the butterfly using ordinary scissors.
  6. Repeat the procedure until the marvelous creation is ready.
  7. Finally, reinsert the plate fragment into the oven. This is necessary so that it softens, then you can give the butterfly the desired shape.
