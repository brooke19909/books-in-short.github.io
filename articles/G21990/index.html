Reviews about the rest in the UAE. Journey to a fairy tale

Reviews about holidays in the UAE prompt many tourists to travel to this young, but very promising country in terms of tourism. The state was founded in 1971 and consists of 7 emirates. Although the president is considered the main thing, each region has its own ruler and its own rules of behavior and stay of foreigners. You can relax in the UAE throughout the year, but you need to remember the rainy season, which occurs in the winter months. Most of the country is occupied by a sandy plain that passes into the Jafur desert. In the east there is a rocky plateau, there are no lakes or rivers at all, only in the period of rains dry channels are filled with water.



Reviews about the holidays in the UAE are mostly positive, because this country has done everything for a comfortable and interesting pastime for tourists. It offers a lot of educational excursions that will familiarize foreigners with the history of the state, the main architectural and natural attractions. In the Emirates, it will be very interesting for tourists who prefer an active lifestyle. Here you can enjoy water skiing, archery, windsurfing, scuba diving, golf, tennis, horse riding and other sports.



None of the country's guests are indifferent to the national dishes offered in local cafes and restaurants. Reviews about vacations in the UAE relate in many respects to Arabic cuisine, because where else can you taste amazing seafood, sweets, meat dishes? Lebanese culinary masterpieces are especially popular; for lovers of variety, a choice of Chinese, European, Mediterranean, Japanese, Indian, Mexican, Iranian, Thai, Irish and even Russian cuisine is offered. Such a variety of dishes makes holidays in the UAE even more attractive. Reviews (prices play an important role) about the service in restaurants are the most positive, because here you can eat well for a moderate fee.

Eastern markets have always attracted the attention of travelers. In the UAE, you can buy a lot of interesting things: various spices, items for coffee drinking, smoking accessories, carpets, fabrics, gold jewelry, precious stones. You always need to bargain in the markets, even if the seller is already making a discount. When buying equipment, you must definitely demand to check it on the spot and ask to be delivered to the hotel, the delivery fee is included in the price of the goods.

Reviews about the rest in the UAE indicate the importance of following the rules of conduct here. Since the country is Muslim, women need to wear modest clothes, which in no case should be tight or short. Do not drink alcohol or appear in public in a state of extreme intoxication. Beachwear is allowed only in appropriate places and near the pool on site. During Ramadan, one cannot eat or drink in the presence of Muslims from sunrise to sunset. Tourists really like a beach holiday in the UAE. Reviews about traveling to this beautiful eastern country are only positive.
