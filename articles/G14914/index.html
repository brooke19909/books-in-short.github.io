How to make a rim with flowers with your own hands (photo)

Want to renew your hairstyle with the original jewelry? Learn how to make a flower rim with your own hands. By the simplest technology, you can make this accessory in half an hour. There are options for advanced craftswomen. If you work hard, you get a real masterpiece that everyone will admire.





Techniques and Embodiments

If you are interested in how to make a rim with flowers with your own hands quickly and beautifully, here you will find the answer to this question. There are options that require a lot of painstaking work, but the result is worth it.

So, you can perform the product in the following ways:

  • From the decor, taken from the purchased hairpins.
  • Using satin ribbons.
  • From artificial finished flowers.
  • By doing your own blanks from paper or fabric.
  • Applying the kanzashi or quilling technique.
  • Crocheting the necessary elements.




If, after reading the list, you are confused and do not know how to make a rim with flowers with your own hands, the photos and a brief description of the methods that are listed below will help you make a choice.

Easiest method

If you have very little time, or you have absolutely no experience in needlework, then this option is a great opportunity to make yourself an original jewelry quickly. The sequence of actions is as follows:

  1. Buy several elastic bands for hair with flowers of the kind you like, size and shade, as well as a headband, better than the right tone right away.
  2. Cut or peel the flowers from the elastic bands, make a composition of them and glue them on the rim.

All is ready. To make such a decoration is available to anyone.

How to make a rim with flowers with your own hands: master class

From tapes you can make a very beautiful product. In this method, tapes of the same or different shades and widths are used as the main consumable. As a basis - a purchase rim of any tone and look.





The sequence of work is as follows:

  1. Take the rim blank and wrap it in satin ribbons in a spiral. For reliability, it is better to apply glue to the rim surface (at least in several places).
  2. Make some bows or twist roses. Better when the items are of different sizes. They will make a beautiful composition. The middle of the bows is easy to decorate with beads.
  3. Assemble the product by gluing the decor onto the prepared rim. Its free surface can be decorated with beads.

This option allows you to make several accessories that differ in design in a short time. It is enough to take tapes of different widths and shades.

How to use ready-made flowers?

If you want to perform floral motifs on the decoration, you can use artificial bouquets and compositions for the interior.



Buy several branches and disassemble them into separate elements. Prepare the rim, you can wrap it with satin ribbon, fabric, braid, yarn. The following describes how to make a rim with flowers with your own hands. Step by step, the work looks like this:

  1. After preparing all the starting materials, take the bezel and glue the large flowers first.
  2. In between place small details: leaves, buds.
  3. Glue extra decor.

So quickly you can create a beautiful accessory. Unlike the first method, where you are limited by the colors with which you will find elastic bands, here the assortment of initial blanks is much wider. This will allow you to diversify the created jewelry for the hair.

Paper flowers

You have already read how to make a rim with flowers with your own hands in several ways. These are quick and easy options. If you want more creativity and originality, make a floral decor yourself. Then your product will be completely unique. It is very simple to make flowers out of paper. They look natural, just like real ones. It will take more time and patience, but in this way you can make a bezel with any colors you want, and not with those that you could buy.

The assembly scheme of the product is the same in all cases. You have already studied it. Flowers are made as follows:

  1. Prepare paper in the desired shades. As a rule, corrugated crepe is used, although spectacular floral elements can also be obtained from a regular sheet that does not stretch and without texture. If you are going to carry the leaves, you definitely need green paper or tape. You will also need scissors, glue, a pencil and a small piece of cardboard.
  2. When everything you need is in front of you, you can start creating blanks. On the cardboard, draw petal patterns and cut them out.
  3. Give flat parts volume. Corrugated and crepe paper is easily stretched with your fingers.
  4. Make a center and leaves.
  5. Gather all the details of the flower in one piece and do this to everyone.
  6. When all the parts are assembled, proceed with the assembly of the rim.

As you can see, from improvised inexpensive materials you can create amazing beauty and fashionable jewelry.

Textile headband with flowers

How to make a rim with flowers with your own hands? A master class (photo of the finished product is below) will help you with this. The previous section examined the technology of making paper decor. It uses fabric. Depending on what kind of appearance of the flowers you want to get, you can use both natural and synthetic materials.





To create realistic elements, chiffon, nylon are suitable. For simplified colors, you can even take fleece or felt. Work with fabric goes like this:

  1. Like paper, blanks of petals and other details are cut from chiffon.
  2. Here it is imperative to process the edges of the elements above the candle flame so that the threads do not crumble. This operation also adds volume to the petals.
  3. Separately, make the center (pestle) and stamens, if necessary.
  4. Fabric flower elements can be neatly stitched together or glued together.

Assembly and decoration of the rim are performed similarly to the previous options.

Kansashi Technique

In addition to the above, there is another way how to make a rim with flowers with your own hands from ribbons. This is a very time-consuming method compared to all previously considered. Flowers made using the kanzashi technique are decorative (unrealistic), but very unusual, bright and beautiful.



For production, as a rule, wide satin ribbons are used. They are cut into squares, in which it is necessary to carefully process the edges above the flame. The resulting blanks are stacked in different ways into details similar to the petals. Small workpieces can be glued together, stitched or joined by fusing the material with fire. Using different colors and folding patterns, very diverse compositions are obtained. These jewelry look impressive on the rim, and on hairpins, and on elastic bands. Initially, it will create a single set in this way.

You learned how to make a rim with flowers with your own hands. Choose your favorite method, prepare materials and tools. Create spectacular jewelry yourself.
