Hotel "Water Area", Gorky Sea. Reviews, prices. Holidays on the Gorky Sea

Are you tired of the fast pace of life, constant lack of sleep and stressful situations? Quickly restore strength and emotional balance, abstract from fussy everyday life can be in nature, namely on the Gorky reservoir in the Nizhny Novgorod region. Near the coast there are comfortable hotel complexes offering quality services at an affordable price.

The country hotel "Water Area" (Gorky Sea) is very popular. In this life-giving corner, the water element and the pine forest are harmoniously combined. The air here is so clean that your head is spinning. To spend a vacation or a weekend in a hotel means to improve your body and soul, pursue your favorite hobby and spend time in a friendly atmosphere. How do you like this original scenario of your vacation?

I would like to note that the sanatoriums and recreation centers of the Gorky Sea are focused on a very different category of tourists and provide a wide range of entertainment and business services. You can choose the accommodation option from economy class to luxury category - it all depends on the budget.

General information

The complex "Aquatoria" (Gorky Sea / Nizhny Novgorod) is located in the Gorodetsky district, not far from the landscaped promenade and beach. A spacious area with an abundance of green spaces will captivate with beauty and well-groomed. Clean air, flowering flower beds with paved alleys create a comfortable microclimate for healing and good rest. The developed infrastructure meets European quality standards and allows guests to safely engage in active sports in the lap of nature.





The hotel is ideal for a romantic weekend, honeymoon, family or corporate vacation. The price includes: two meals a day, the use of a safe and an electronic key to the room, as well as an underground parking lot. The following resorts of the Gorky Sea have similar conditions: "Petrel", "Emerald", "Seagull". Each one is good in his own way. But now we will talk about the "Water Area".

Where to stay?





The modern hotel "Aquatoria" is pleased to offer its visitors comfortable accommodation in comfortable rooms of various categories. All rooms are decorated in calm pastel colors and furnished with necessary furniture. The rooms comply with regulatory and technical standards. 24-hour room service is provided, daily housekeeping and towel change are provided.

From the windows of their rooms, guests can enjoy the picturesque scenery of a pine forest and the Gorky reservoir. At the disposal of tourists will be a small bar with drinks, electronic safe, satellite TV, private bathroom with a set of toilet and hygiene products. The price of a standard room per day starts from 2800 p., Superior apartments - from 8400 p.

Nutrition





A varied and balanced food is offered by the club hotel "Aquatoria" to its guests. On the territory there is a luxurious restaurant with 80 seats and a cozy karaoke bar for 30 people. There are also summer pavilions for 150 people. A cafe with barbecue facilities and a stove is open right on the seashore. The menu includes children's, banquet and diet meals.





In the institutions you will be treated to delicious delicacies of European, Russian and Mediterranean cuisine. For preparation, pure and healthy water from an artesian well is used. A professional chef will surprise and amaze with his culinary masterpieces even the most sophisticated gourmet.

Orders for large-scale celebrations are accepted. For these purposes, a banquet room is provided. Attentive and experienced staff will be happy to help in organizing the holiday and its design.

Coastline





Landscaped beach area is owned by the hotel "Water Area". The Gorky Sea is located within walking distance of the residential building (100 meters). On the shore are comfortable sun loungers and sunshades (for free use). There is a pier and a boat station. It offers vacationers a variety of active games and water activities: surfing, volleyball, boating, jet skiing, catamaran, kayaking.

Winter holidays





Informative and interesting rest in the cold. Club Hotel "Water Area" offers a versatile leisure for every visitor. The source of fun and vivid impressions will be a ride on a snowmobile, snowkiting, as well as sledding, skiing and ice skating. The countryside complex has its own ski run and children's slides. Tours around the area are organized. You can arrange hiking on a snowy forest and admire the indescribable beauty. In the off-season and in winter, the price of a room starts from 2000 rubles per day (per person).

Service maintenance

A Finnish sauna, Russian and Turkish baths will help keep warm after a frosty winter day. Be sure to visit the massage room. You can restore lost strength and cleanse the body with a herbal barrel. The herbal components used during the procedures have a sedative, anti-inflammatory, sedative and antiseptic effect.

In addition to healing sessions, the Aquatoria complex (Gorky Sea) offers interesting entertainment programs with live music, a dance hall and various competitions. Original shows are held on the eve of the holidays. Unforgettable, bright and fun you can spend here New Year holidays with friends.

Business services

Qualitatively organized business tourism. The hotel is comfortable not only to relax, but also to decide on working moments. Roomy conference rooms with excellent technical equipment are for rent. Businessmen come here for presentations, promotions, important meetings and negotiations.





In the halls you will find the most necessary machinery and equipment: a large plasma TV, flipchart, the Internet, a powerful projector, printer, fax and office supplies. For comfort and convenience, air conditioning systems are installed in the premises, there are large round tables and chairs with folding stands. Lunch, drinking water and coffee will be provided for your partners.
