Hotel Monart City Hotel 4 *, Turkey, Alanya: traveler reviews

Monart City Hotel 4 * complex is located in the center of Alanya resort. The remoteness of the city from the international airports of Turkey did not affect the popularity of the hotel among holidaymakers. Travelers claim that the journey from the passenger terminal of Antalya to the Mediterranean coast takes no more than three hours.





Towards the sun

The region is famous for its abundance of beaches, highly praised by European experts. One of them stretched along the multi-storey buildings of Monart City Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya). Alanya has recreational areas that are equipped with ergonomic sunbeds, wide umbrellas, changing cabins, showers and toilet boxes. There are aerarii and solariums, platforms for massage and leisurely relaxation in the shade of lush southern vegetation.

A gradual entrance to the sea and an extended sandbank contribute to the rapid heating of the water. The resort has all the conditions for a family holiday and noisy fun with friends. The city marina is the epicenter of entertainment and nightlife. The rich historical heritage of the Mediterranean involves a wide selection of excursion programs.

A couple of minutes drive from the Monart City Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) towers ancient towers, the remains of an Ottoman defensive structure. Fortresses with empty eye sockets look down on fussy tourists. The mournful singing of the muezzin anticipates the dawn hours.
