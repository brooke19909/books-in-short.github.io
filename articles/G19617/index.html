Hotel Hotel Club La Playa 3 * (Hammamet, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is one of the leading countries that offer tourists a beach holiday. Hammamet Resort is suitable for travelers who prefer a relaxing pastime. Hotels are located on picturesque sandy beaches. Often people come here for treatment, because here you can find many health centers of thalassotherapy. If you are looking for a cheap place to stay, then pay your attention to La Playa Hotel Club 3 * (Tunisia). But is it worth saving on vacation in this country?

Important information and location

The modern and affordable hotel was opened in 2013 after a long reconstruction of the building. During the repair, both the living and public areas were updated, and all the plumbing and furniture in the rooms were completely replaced. The snow-white two-story complex has only 2 floors, which house 90 comfortable apartments, designed to accommodate no more than 2-3 people. It is worth knowing that La Playa Hotel Club 3 * ( Hammamet ) is a hotel for adults, so families with children can be uncomfortable here. Self-registration is only possible from the age of 18. The complex does not accept tourists with pets. Hotel staff will surely ask tourists for a document with a photo confirming their identity, as well as a credit card or cash as collateral.





A small hotel is located on the first line and has its own beach area. Unfortunately, there is no developed tourist infrastructure nearby. To go shopping or visit a restaurant, guests will have to call a taxi and go to the center of Hammamet , which is 6 km from the hotel. The airport is also not far away. Most often people come here from Enfida , which is 44 km away. The capital's airport is 66 km from the complex. Thus, the approximate travel time to the hotel will be about an hour.

Number of rooms

This is not to say that the rooms at Hotel Club La Playa feature luxurious interiors or many amenities. The three-star complex provides an economical accommodation option. Standard rooms consist of one small room and a bathroom, which has a shower, toilet, washbasin and mirror. The bedroom has a large double bed, a desk, wardrobes and bedside tables.

More about room amenities

Like other hotels, La Playa Hotel Club 3 * offers its guests a standard list of amenities that are necessary for a comfortable stay. So, in each room there is a modern TV. Satellite TV mainly broadcasts TV channels in French and English, but you can also find a couple of channels in Russian here. For example, the First or "Russia". In high season, air conditioning is included in the rooms. They work until the end of September. For an additional fee, you can rent a small safe in which you can leave valuables.

The bathroom must have a hairdryer and a set of towels. Cosmetics are not provided, so they can be brought with you or bought on your own in Tunisia. Upon your request, a minibar is also provided in the room, where you can cool drinks. True, its regular filling will have to be paid separately. There is also a telephone in the room, which is usually used to communicate with the manager.

Hotel infrastructure

The updated complex La Playa Hotel Club (Tunisia) offers guests a wide selection of services and services that they may need during their vacation. For tourists who are accustomed to traveling by rented car, the hotel has free parking. You can leave your car here at any time without additional reservation. You can contact relatives or simply check social networks using Wi-Fi , which works in all public places for free. For smokers, there are special areas for the use of tobacco products. Smoking is also available in the rooms.





Businessmen are offered a small meeting room equipped with a computer and a projector. Fax and photocopying services are also available here. The front desk is open 24 hours a day. At the reception, tourists can check in their luggage for luggage storage, use a paid safe, and exchange currency. Guests are assisted in the selection and purchase of excursions, tickets, as well as in arranging a transfer to the airport.

Entertainment

Tourists come to Tunisia in search of a classic beach holiday. Hotel Club La Playa offers them a private beach area, equipped with a shower, changing cabins, toilet and musical accompaniment. Guests can also take a sun lounger and sun umbrella for free. An excellent alternative to the sea is an outdoor freshwater pool, which is located very close to the hull. The hotel has animators who organize daytime and evening shows. True, they are held only in English and French. On the beach you can also play volleyball or water polo with other guests for free. Young people can visit the local night club, which is located on site.





A lot of hotel and paid entertainment. For example, a SPA salon, which has a massage room, a wellness salon and a hairdresser. In winter, you can visit the indoor saltwater pool. Near the hotel there is a golf course or paintball field . A gym and water aerobics classes are also available for a fee .

Nutrition Details

The quality of food in Tunisia is often answered negatively. Dishes here are most often heavily seasoned with pepper, and their choice is not as great as in Turkey or Egypt. The cost of living at La Playa Hotel Club ( Hammamet ) includes all-inclusive meals. This includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as snacks, desserts and local drinks. Alcohol is provided daily, but only until midnight. Imported alcohol can be purchased at the bar, but for a fee.





The hotel has several catering facilities. The main one is the main restaurant, which serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. They are served buffet style with hot and cold appetizers. You can also grab a bite to eat at the pizzeria or lobby bar , which serves pastries, ice cream, fruits, snacks , sandwiches, and more. Positive reviews

This is not to say that La Playa Hotel Club receives only positive reviews. Nevertheless, tourists note many advantages of the hotel. We list the main ones:

  • Hospitable staff who greet their guests with a smile. Some of them have already learned several Russian words, but there are still difficulties in communication.
  • The cleaners do their job well. Without a tip, they clean the rooms thoroughly daily and change towels .
  • A good selection of local alcohol. It is of fairly good quality, although inferior to imported alcohol.
  • There are no queues in the restaurant, there are always free tables.
  • Sun beds on the beach are new. There are many of them, so guests do not need to occupy them in advance in the early morning.




Negative reviews

Despite a good description, Hotel Club La Playa is often criticized. Tourists talk about the many shortcomings that ruined their vacation. They also advise never to come to this hotel. As arguments, they call the following disadvantages:

  • The staff at the complex speaks French only. Therefore, Russian tourists very often have difficulty understanding when communicating with the administrator.
  • Very dirty beach. Cigarette butts, plastic glasses and bags are constantly lying in the sand. The stools of camels are rarely cleaned, therefore, the smell here is appropriate.
  • The hotel constantly plays loud music. In the evenings, even closed windows do not save.
  • The hotel has almost no Russian tourists, but there are many local tourists who are very noisy and often behave indecently.
  • The rooms are in terrible condition and in need of repair. Plumbing in the bathroom is leaking, and salt water is running from the tap. In addition, it can be turned off for the whole day without warning.
  • Spicy food that is not suitable for tourists with stomach problems. For lunch and dinner, only carbonated water is served from drinks.




Conclusion

Hotel Club La Playa has a good description, but reviews about the rest here are more often negative. Russian tourists who do not know French, it is better to choose another hotel, where there is at least English-speaking staff. The complex is suitable for young people who are not afraid of loud music and noisy guests.
