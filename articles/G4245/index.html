The nuances of using a running meter



A running meter is one meter of a product, regardless of what height or width it will have. If the cost of products is expressed in this unit, then you just need to choose the most suitable color, texture, type of product and a suitable width. Payment will be made only for the length, without any conversion to pieces or square meters.

There is a special talk about furniture.
