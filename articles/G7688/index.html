What is a mortgage

Perhaps one of the fastest and most convenient ways of acquiring real estate, which does not require a one-time deposit of a large amount, but allows you to pay for the purchase over a long time, is a mortgage. This is a certain form of collateral, which implies the purchase of housing in the property before the actual payment, where the guarantee of repayment of the loan is either the property itself or the property equal to the price.

The procedure for approving and issuing a loan may differ depending on the terms of the contract and the bank that provides it. In Novosibirsk, there are up to 32 such financial institutions. What is important when making

A mortgage in Novosibirsk without a down payment is a rather complicated procedure, which banks rarely agree to. Since the down payment, in fact, is a confirmation of the serious intentions of the borrower to repay the loan within the agreed period, and also removes a significant debt burden, allowing you to reduce the collateral amount and reduce the overpayment on the loan. Accordingly, a zero down payment may cause a client to default on a loan due to an increased load as a result of increased ratios under the terms of the contract.





Given the heavy load for the client when applying for a mortgage, not all banks are willing to take risks and give their consent to this condition under the contract. More often they offer a down payment, the average size of which is from 10 to 30%. But still, a mortgage without a down payment in Novosibirsk is possible. For this, the client needs to prove his worth, and his high income will be sufficient evidence. Next, you need to choose a property for purchase and decide on a bank that can provide the most favorable conditions for this particular case.

Offers from developers

What is the advantage of new buildings

It is profitable for construction companies to sell apartments under construction, bypassing intermediaries and thereby reducing the cost of housing. Yes, and housing itself at the stage of wall construction has a low price, which increases significantly after completion of construction work.
