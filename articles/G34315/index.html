Action camera - what is it? Action camera: description, reviews, prices

Modern video technology mainly develops in the characteristics of the internal "filling" and its functionality. Improved properties of matrix modules, processors and lenses allow to obtain materials of a qualitatively new level. However, the features of the operation of digital technology in extreme conditions led to the formation of a whole segment of devices of a different type. So there was a compact and at the same time easy-to-use action camera. What is this from the point of view of the direct consumer? First of all, a wide range of protective equipment that protects the structure from external influences of various kinds. But the features of models of this type do not end there.





Action Camera Overview

Most models are small-sized cameras enclosed in a rugged and dust-proof housing. By the way, a wide range of insulation coatings and shells, even in budget models, allows you to protect the filling of the device also from water and small particles of dust. There are differences and mounting systems, which are already in the basic configuration complemented by a standard action camera. What are these devices? On the one hand, these are typical devices for attaching the case to different parts of structures and even clothes. On the other hand, damping means that soften lens vibrations.

An important structural feature is the equipment with an ultra-wide-angle lens, which is characterized by a high degree of sharpness. Such a device also eliminates the need for autofocus, which in itself is sensitive to vibration. By the way, manufacturers practice and supply models with fisheye lenses. For example, the third-generation GoPro action camera allows you to shoot with a 170 ¬∞ view.

Recording Features





The recording process is carried out at high speed - in this aspect, this is the main difference between such cameras on conventional models. Almost all devices of this class operate at a frequency of 60 frames per second, while the traditional line is characterized by a speed of 25-30 frames per second. What is especially important, the recording format and resolution of the matrices practically do not lose to traditional devices. Sony's modern action camera, in particular, supports not only FULL HD recording mode, but also modern 4K technology. Another thing is that the operating conditions of such models nevertheless leave their mark on the final quality of the video material, which sometimes radically differs from shooting in normal mode. We are talking about characteristic shaking, vibrations and vibrations. Just damping devices are aimed at eliminating such negative effects, but their function is not enough.

Panasonic HX-500E Reviews





Panasonic developers also paid special attention not to the basic quality of shooting, but to means of eliminating interference arising in extreme conditions. Owners of the HX-500E model point to a well-thought-out split-camera design, in which the lens and the main unit with the controller and technological stuffing are connected by cable and mounted in different places. This solution allows to improve both the quality of the recording and the reliability in handling the equipment. However, the functional advantages such action cameras have are also noted. How much does an action camera cost?

The most affordable action cameras can be purchased for 5-7 thousand rubles. These are models with a minimum set of options, which also demonstrate not the best structural ergonomics. This is followed by a segment of 10-12 thousand, which presents a better technical camera, but still poor in functionality, an action camera. What does this mean in practice of use? In terms of shooting, such a device is significantly inferior to more expensive premium versions, but in terms of the reliability of the element base it is quite suitable for a wide variety of application conditions. The higher segment, in turn, shows attractive cameras in all respects, like the latest GoPro generations, worth about 20 thousand.

Conclusion





Despite all the differences from traditional video cameras, action models also provide an example of the dynamic development of a separate industry in the field of digital technology. Advanced control systems, intelligent sensors, wireless data transmission channels - these and other technological innovations have long been incorporated in an action camera. What does this give the end user? First of all, freedom of action in the process of creating video content. Kits of clamps not only allow you to securely mount the camera, but at the same time do not reduce the quality of shooting. In addition, the latest technical ideas have saved the action camera operators from solving many secondary tasks related to data transmission in the mode of remote access to the network.
