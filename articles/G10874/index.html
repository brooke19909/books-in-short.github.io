Hotel "Djerba Sun Club": reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resorts of the state of Tunisia, the island of Djerba, is known all over the world for the combination of exotic nature, delightful mild climate, monuments of ancient culture and excellent service of European level. Hotel "Sun Club", reviews of which are left by Russian tourists who visited the island resort, offers its guests an excellent all-inclusive stay at a relatively low cost. What living conditions can this hotel complex provide? And what can the reviews of 2016 on tourist forums say about the San Club Club hotel on the island of Djerba?





About the hotel complex

The hotel complex "San Club" 3 * is located on the island of Djerba in the south of the African state of Tunisia. Djerba Sun Club, reviews of which can be found at most Russian tourism forums, includes a complex of several hotel buildings in a fenced and guarded area.

A special feature and visiting card of the hotel complex is the unique local flavor, visible in everything: in the interior of the premises, the landscape design of the territory, the hotel kitchen and the decoration of dishes.

The hotel is located near the small village of Medun, near the island capital - Houmt Souk, only 20 km from the city and the international airport. By car, the main city of Djerba can be reached in 10-15 minutes.

What, in addition to residential buildings, is located on the territory of one of the most popular hotels on the island of Djerba - "Sun Club"? The hotel has an indoor pool, where you can comfortably swim even in the cool winter months, and outdoor children's and adult pools, equipped with water attractions - slides. Hotel beach

Separately, it is worth talking about the conditions for a beach holiday at the Djerba Sun Club Hotel. Reviews about the beach of this hotel complex guests leave mostly positive. Here's the difference between a vacation here:

  • The hotel is on the second coastline. The beach is located at a distance of about 50 m from the nearest residential buildings, but no further than 350 m from the farthest buildings of the island hotel. There is a road between the beach and the hotel complex.
  • The beach is sandy, the sea is of medium depth, which is especially good for families with children or for the elderly.
  • Sun loungers and parasols at the hotel beach are provided free of charge. You can pay extra if you wish for a soft mattress for a deck chair and beach towels.
  • Less - a large number of algae in the sea. All marine vegetation carried out on coastal sand is cleaned daily by tractors in the morning, simply raking it in heaps and dragging it to the territory of neighboring beaches. Moreover, the hills of algae lie for several days. It is for this reason that guests of the Djerba San Club 3 * complex express their dissatisfaction, reviews contain references to the unpleasant smell of musty algae and the spoiled sea landscape. It is worth noting that many algae do not interfere with many.
  • Animals on the beach - the most controversial "attraction" of the beach of the hotel "Djerba Sun Club". Reviews of hotel guests on this subject are very different. Someone like camels walking along the seashore, horses and a small donkey very much. Animals, according to tourists, are very peaceful, calm and, in addition, for a relatively small fee you can ride them or take some photos. In addition, all traces of the vital activity of animals are removed by their owners. But there are also many people who are extremely indignant at the presence of animals on the beach.

What to see? Excursions, sights

At different periods on the island of Djerba and on the nearby coastal territories of Tunisia, the French, Spaniards, Turks, Jews, Sicilians, Berbers lived, built, fought, fished and traded. The Tunisian culture and architecture also reflected the ancient Carthaginian civilization, the raids of the Phoenician navigators, the era of the conquest of the Roman Empire. Tunisia and the island of Djerba for centuries have absorbed and preserved the ancient heritage of the peoples inhabiting these lands. The historical monuments of this African state are not so well known to Russian travelers, as, for example, Egypt with its pyramids and Bedouin settlements. But both in Tunisia and on the island of Djerba, there are many cultural and historical sites, which will be very interesting to look at.

Guests of the San Club hotel complex can see the sights either as part of an organized excursion, or simply by renting a transport and buying a guide. The second option will cost much cheaper. You can start with the island capital - the city of Houmt Souk. One of his business cards is the stone fort of Bordj el Kebir. Tourists will certainly be interested in a familiarization walk to the medina, the old part of the main city of Djerba. These neighborhoods are a real labyrinth of low houses in the Arabic style and intricate narrow streets.

In the medina there are beautiful monuments of Muslim architecture - three ancient mosques. Is it possible to get acquainted with the traditional life of the population of Djerba? visiting the local history museum of the city of Houmt Souk. A few kilometers from the capital is a former Jewish settlement. Here is one of the oldest religious buildings of the El Ghrib synagogue, whose age is more than two thousand years.

In addition to the capital, tourists are usually recommended to visit the small village of Gellala in the southern part of the island. It has been producing famous ceramics for many centuries - the main souvenir product of the island. In a small village there are more than 450 traditional pottery workshops that carefully preserve the ancient secrets of the production of light and amazingly beautiful pottery. In Gellala there is a museum of ethnography, which presents elements of the traditional life of the Djerbins: samples of ancient ceramic products, traditional jewelry, national clothes and household items.

The most popular excursion route among tourists is a trip to the Sahara desert. As a rule, the trip lasts at least two days with an overnight stay in one of the local hotels. Tourists will be able to admire the unusually beautiful desert landscapes at sunset, get acquainted with the traditional life of the Berbers, visit the legendary location of the Star Wars saga, an oasis with date palms, a zoo and a crocodile farm, the ruins of the Roman amphitheater and much, much more.
