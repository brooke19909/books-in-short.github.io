Several ways to close the stitches with knitting needles

Proper closing of the loops with knitting needles at the end of knitting will ensure that the product maintains its correct shape. The school taught only one way, but in fact, for each pattern a different approach. If the knitter creates his product for the first time according to the scheme, then he is faced with a lack of a description of the end of the work. Learning this is easy.

Close edge one loop

This method produces a loose pigtail that stretches. Therefore, such an elastic closing of loops with knitting needles is used in order to finish the last row of knitting things with any pattern and elastic. The sequence of actions is as follows:

  1. Remove the first and knit the next one according to the picture.
  2. Use a knitting needle in your left hand to hook the edge and drag a knitted loop through it. Only one loop will remain on the right.
  3. Knit the next loop according to the drawing and repeat the steps.


This should be done to the last loop so that only the loop always remains on the right side. Tear off the thread, leaving the end at 4-5 cm, and thread through the loop, tying a knot on it. During operation, you should ensure that the pigtail does not drag on, but remains free.

Close the edge in two loops

Such closing of the loops with knitting needles should not be used for the finished gum pattern, since the obtained pigtail will not stretch. It is also important to consider the thickness of the yarn and its friability, being careful not to tighten the product. To finish a thing, you need:

  1. Knit the first and subsequent loops together for the walls behind the knitting needles.
  2. Return the resulting loop to the left knitting needle and again make one loop out of two.
  3. So do it to the end.


Pull the edge of the product slightly when the last loop remains and, as in the previous example, tear the thread so that you can tie a knot. On the right side, the product should be completed with facial loops, and on the reverse - with the wrong ones. This is the most common method.

Close gum 2x2 without a needle

In order for the end of the 2x2 elastic band to merge with the pattern itself and stretch, but not use a needle, the following steps should be performed:

  1. Knit the first two loops with the help of the pattern.
  2. Throw the first loop onto the knitting needle in your left hand and pass the other loop through it.
  3. Knit the next with the wrong loop and do the previous steps.
  4. To the last loop, knit according to the pattern, while only one loop should remain on the right knitting needle after stretching.

Finish in the usual way. So you can finish and 1x1 gum.

Closure of loops on the neck and sleeve



For this, the required number of loops is first closed. On the reverse side, the rows are knitted according to the figure, and at the beginning of the next it is necessary to reduce one loop, stretching the edge. Beautiful closing of the loops with knitting needles on these elements of clothing is simply necessary, since the sleeve and collar on some models are missing.

Close loops in circular knitting with a hollow cord

Closing loops with needles in this way is also called I-Cord. It is used only to complete work on a circular product. We do the following:

  1. We take the auxiliary knitting needle and pick up an additional three loops. To do this, knit the first loop first, leaving it on the working needle. Use the left knitting needle to pick up the loop from the front wall from you. Also make the other two loops.
  2. Knit the resulting two loops face.
  3. Knit the third with the subsequent loop for the back wall.
  4. Return all loops to the left knitting needle.
  5. Repeat movements until the row is closed.
  6. Leave the remaining three loops on the right knitting needle.
  7. Cut the thread, leaving about 20 cm. Insert into the needle.
  8. Remove the first loop, thread the needle and thread and rotate the product.
  9. Grab the needle under both ears from the bottom up to the loop from the other edge.
  10. Return the needle to the open loop.
  11. Do the same with the remaining loops and close the last.

It may seem that such an elastic closing of loops with knitting needles is very difficult, but try, get a beautiful edge, and this method will become your favorite.

Closing the last row of the jagged pico

Knitting ended. Closing the loops with a patterned pattern will look quite original. Make it simple:

  1. Transfer the first loop to the knitting needle in your right hand.
  2. The next knit with a facial loop.
  3. Insert the left knitting needle into the first and thread the other.
  4. Repeat the steps.
  5. Insert the needle in the left hand into the loop, and drag the working thread with the right to make a loop. So do it twice.
  6. Insert the left knitting needle into the loop behind the pigtail of the third row, and knit the right loop with the right one.
  7. Perform steps 3.
  8. We start to knit from the 2nd point.

It turns out a simple, simple design that will decorate the product itself. This method is not suitable if this edge is meant to stitch.



Sometimes a large needle or hook is used to collect the loops. Only the appropriate method should be selected. So, for example, if the elastic was knitted with knitting needles, the closure of the loops should be elastic so as not to tighten the product. Proper execution of the work provides an excellent result.
