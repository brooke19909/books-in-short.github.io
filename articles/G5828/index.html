Waterproof smartphones: rating, review of the best models, specifications, selection tips and manufacturer reviews

A few years ago, the shockproof and waterproof smartphone segment stood apart and was considered a highly specialized exotic for extreme people and tourists. With the advent of new and more advanced technologies, protection began to appear in more "down to earth" gadgets.

Of course, a good half of shockproof and waterproof smartphones still have a fat and unattractive look, but manufacturers have made the main emphasis on protection against water. While the physical part fell on the shoulders of films, cases, handbags and other accessories, without which the phone looks quite presentable and does not differ much from its usual counterparts.

We will try to understand this issue and outline the list of the best waterproof smartphones. Consider the important technical characteristics of the models, their advantages and disadvantages, and also pay attention to the main selection criteria.

Difficulty choosing

All waterproof smartphones, both inexpensive and premium, fall under the international standard, which just indicates the degree of protection of the gadget from water. The general classification is quite impressive, and it does not include a small list of conditions, but we are only interested in two classes - IP67 and IP68. The latter are just related to the segment of smartphones and fully reflect the characteristics we need.

IP67

IP67 waterproof smartphones can survive the short-term immersion of the device to a depth of 1 meter, and are also completely protected from dust. That is, the continuous operation of the phone in this mode is not assumed, nor is it guaranteed by this standard.

In simple terms: dropped into the water - picked up, wiped with a handkerchief and went on. In addition, waterproof smartphones that support this standard can work in the rain, that is, it is like water, but not immersion, but something in between. But, again, abusing conversations on a cell phone in a downpour is not worth it.

IP68

Here we have maximum protection against moisture, which implies immersion and correct operation of the device at a depth of more than 1 meter. In this case, you can safely go diving and at the same time respond to SMS.

Neither moisture nor dust can get into a waterproof smartphone and disrupt its operation. You can even talk for hours on end in the pouring rain, and your gadget will not care. Naturally, devices that meet this standard are much more expensive than devices of class IP67.

Manufacturers

Many companies are engaged in the production of waterproof smartphones, but not everyone gets really high-quality gadgets in all respects. For some, for the sake of protection, functionality or performance suffers, while others even put a fake mark on the received standard. So in this case, it is better to trust trusted and well-established brands.

Here we have already familiar companies that have repeatedly proved their worth and have launched more than worthy gadgets on the market. These are devices from Samsung, Sony, Apple and LG. Reviews about waterproof smartphones from these manufacturers are completely positive. Moreover, the devices are obtained not only protected, but quite complete - with a good set of chipsets and attractive appearance.

Reviews about other manufacturers, alas, are not so flattering, so the choice of series should be approached especially carefully. Otherwise, you will get either no performance with proper protection, or a plain brick that barely fits in your pocket.
