Dolphinarium in the Sormovsky park of Nizhny Novgorod: description, address, reviews

In May 2015, a joyful event occurred in Nizhny Novgorod . For a long time, residents and guests of the city were waiting for him. A unique mobile Moscow dolphinarium was opened in Sormovsky Park (Nizhny Novgorod).

Description





This complex is part of the Moscow structure and, accordingly, operates under the famous brand. A feature of the dolphinarium is its mobility. Indeed, not every city can boast the presence of a dolphinarium or an aquarium. Moscow is distinguished by a high level of qualification of trainers and excellent preparation of animals. His mobile follower tries with honor to bear the name of his main institution. Therefore, programs are constantly updated, and the views are always interesting. Artists and tricks

Funny artists are happy to work for the public, walruses and seals twist rings, throw up the ball and perform hilarious racks, while funny bottlenose dolphins along with a white whale show complex tricks in the form of jumps and somersaults, play with the ball and rings, ride and throw up trainers, surprise with vocals. They can also draw a picture, which will no doubt become not only a wonderful decor for any home, but will also carry a powerful positive charge.





The ability of these beautiful animals to positively influence the human psyche has been known for a very long time, and is widely used in many countries of the world. Dolphins are much smarter than other mammals. They are at the top of the smart parade among animals. Therefore, it is so interesting to watch them. Zoologists say that dolphins are much like humans, they can experience feelings such as love and affection, and some even jealousy. There are many legends about how dolphins helped sailors in a shipwreck to get to the shore. The military has long appreciated their intelligence. Now these marine mammals are in the service of the fleet of many countries. It is known that fighting dolphins helped our fleet in clearing the Black Sea during the Second World War. So communication with our smaller intelligent brothers living in the seas and oceans will benefit absolutely everyone.

Dolphin therapy

As already mentioned, the dolphinarium in Sormovsky Park in Nizhny Novgorod offers a swim with these amazing animals. Such a procedure will bring not only fun, but also benefit. Many have probably heard about such a direction in medicine as dolphin therapy. As it turned out, the ultrasound published by these mammals is very useful for the human body. After sessions of dolphin therapy, it became better even for people who are sick with serious diseases such as cerebral palsy and a disorder of the central nervous system.





Psychologists often advise dolphin therapy to people who have experienced severe stress or received an injury that affected the central nervous system. It was noted that such procedures provided great assistance in the recovery process. Dolphins are very friendly and can quickly make friends with humans. When swimming with them, you can easily imagine yourself as a marine inhabitant or master of the seas. Experienced instructors will help and advise you in everything, so swimming will definitely be unforgettable, not to mention the benefits.

Dolphinarium in Sormovsky Park (Nizhny Novgorod). Reviews

People who attended a fascinating performance featuring funny dolphins, a white whale and funny fur seals were happy to share their impressions of what they saw. The colorful show, which shows the dolphinarium in Sormovsky Park in Nizhny Novgorod, brought them great joy. Especially satisfied are the small spectators and their parents. Indeed, funny marine artists delight not only with a variety of tricks, the ability to sing and draw, but also with a fountain of splatters scattering in all directions, the kids are delighted with the dizzying jumps made by dolphins right under the dome of the water arena.





Adults note the care of the staff about their viewers, because the issued oilcloths are not at all superfluous after such jumps. Many thanks to people not only to the organizers of the show, but also to the employees of the dolphinarium, their friendliness and responsiveness are worthy of all praise. Separately, the purity and lack of smell of chlorine, which, unfortunately, is characteristic of pools, was noted, so the dolphinarium in Sormovsky Park received the most flattering reviews. For those who have not had time to enjoy the performance, I advise you to do it without fail. After all, the pleasure received by the child is a double pleasure for parents.

Address

Where is the Dolphinarium in Sormovsky Park? His address: Nizhny Novgorod, 34 Yubileiny Boulevard, next to the Madagascar Zoo. Phone for inquiries: 8 (831) 414-90-53, 8 (831) 414-90-43.

Ticket price and show duration

Tickets cost from 400 to 800 rubles, depending on the chosen place.

The cost of photographs is from 300 to 700 rubles.

The duration of the presentation is from 40 to 45 minutes.

The cash desk is open daily from 10.00 to 20.00
