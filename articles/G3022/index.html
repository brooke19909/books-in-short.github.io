Sheregesh hotels: overview, description, prices, photos and reviews of tourists

Sheregesh is one of the most famous ski resorts not only in our country, but also abroad. Sheregesh hotels will provide you and your family a cozy and comfortable stay. Clean mountain air and active entertainment will make your vacation unforgettable. The high level of service and helpful staff will pleasantly please you, and the opportunity to enjoy beautiful views of nature will complement an already positive experience.

Weather Sheregesh





It is very important for vacationers who plan to visit the resort, to know what climate awaits them at their destination. As for the climate of Sheregesh, it is brightly continental here, but the mountain ranges protect the area from strong winds and frosts. Even when the thermometer shows twenty degrees of frost, being here is very comfortable. Most of the year, snow covers the earth, starting in November and ending in May. The peculiarity of snow is that it is very fluffy and soft. Most tourists go to the resort in the winter.

Variety of entertainment





The main treasure of the resort is the ski slopes. There are a huge number of them with different levels of difficulty. Novice skiers choose simpler tracks, and professionals are not afraid to drive along the most difficult slopes. Sometimes skiers like to ride on pristine snow.

In addition to the slopes, the resort provides a huge amount of entertainment for every taste:

  • night bars, clubs and restaurants;
  • the opportunity to play billiards, bowling;
  • variety of saunas and baths;
  • training schools for lovers of skiing;
  • large selection of shops;
  • rental of various sports equipment;
  • children's clubs where they look after the children while their parents are busy;
  • organization of excursions to the nearest picturesque mountain places;
  • horseback riding;
  • helicopter ride and much more.

Holiday at the resort in summer





Not only in winter, people come to this wonderful resort. Sheregesh hotels in the summer offer discounts on accommodation, and this attracts people with average incomes here. As for entertainment, in summer there are no fewer than in winter. The only difference is that the snow is created artificially. Otherwise, the summer Sheregesh is not inferior to the winter. The weather here is warm and comfortable in summer. In addition to the usual skis and snowmobile rides, guests are offered to engage in various types of outdoor activities:

  • paragliding;
  • freeride
  • jumping from a cliff;
  • skiing on a mountain river.
