How to check an account on a Sberbank card: options, instructions

Sberbank clients have as many as five ways to check their account on a Sberbank card. Some of them are quite convenient, but require the preliminary connection of additional services, others are completely free, but they do not differ in special convenience.

Checking a card account using a mobile bank

A mobile bank service is a set of specific services that a financial institution provides using mobile communication devices. By using short commands, it is possible for a small fee or completely free of charge (it all depends on bank conditions) to request almost any data on the card. In response to such commands, SMS with the necessary information comes. This service is available to subscribers of all major mobile operators.

To find out the rest of the account on the card of Sberbank, a client with a mobile phone can To which the mobile bank service is connected, and its number is attached to the card in question. Many people are interested in how to check the balance of a Sberbank card after 900. To do this, send a short message from the phone to the appropriate number. In this case, the command "BALANCE XXXX" should be indicated in the message, where XXXX is the last 4 digits of the card number.

In addition to the word "BALANCE", you can also use the following commands: "01", "OSTATOK", "RESIDUE", "BALANCE", "BALANS".

Bank customers can use such commands to perform operations such as: check the account on the Sberbank card, receive a mini statement, which will show the balance of available funds, put money on the phone and much more. You can get a mini statement by sending any of the commands: "02", "HISTORY", "EXTRACT", "HISTORY", "ISTORIA", "VYPISKA". After each of the teams, the last 4 digits of the card on which the request is made must be indicated. The command should also be sent to 900.

Advantages of a mobile bank

Using a mobile bank has several advantages:

  1. Getting information about actions that are performed with a card account.
  2. Checking the data on the limit of available funds and recent transactions.
  3. Making transfers and payments online.
  4. Repayment of credit obligations from the card account, card blocking.

Knowing the information about the method of requesting a card balance using SMS allows bank customers to significantly simplify their own lives. By sending just one team to a special number, you can get all the necessary data without breaking away from important matters and without leaving the room.

To use this opportunity, the client should activate the mobile bank service. The cost of this service is determined by the banking version and tariffs of mobile operators for using SMS.

The client may need to check the balance at any time. Sometimes it happens that there is no opportunity to visit a branch, an ATM is far away, and there is no Internet access. Checking the balance in the bank branch. How to implement?

Checking an account on a Sberbank card is free. In order to do this, you must contact the bank branch. This method of finding out information is the most inconvenient and time consuming. To find out the balance with a bank employee, you must have a passport with you, and only the account holder can request data. Otherwise, the bank representative will not give an answer to this question.

But when contacting the branch, the client has the opportunity to receive official bank paper, which will reflect the number and validity of the card, card account number, statement for the required period of time. That is all the necessary information. It should be noted that the account number on the card of Sberbank is not displayed.

Checking card balance via ATM

You can request the available balance on the card through an ATM. To do this, insert the card into the self-service terminal, enter a personal PIN code, go to the balance request section. After performing these steps, the ATM will offer the choice of displaying the balance on the card - on the device screen or on the check. Each of these methods of requesting a balance is free, so the client can choose any of the most convenient of them.

It is important to remember that some banking organizations charge a fee if the customer uses the card through other people's self-service terminals and ATMs. If there is not much money on the card, then it is better to check the balance and withdraw money from Sberbank ATMs. So many and much more convenient.

Before checking an account on a Sberbank card using an ATM, it is recommended that each client familiarize themselves with simpler and more convenient ways of managing an account and receiving financial data. If you connect the convenient services provided by digging, you can significantly save your time without spending it in line with a bank employee or ATM.
