Residential complex "Seasons" (Astrakhan): description, reviews

Astrakhan is a southern city that is currently being actively built up. The best construction companies offer modern projects of comfortable housing, which are very popular among residents. The residential complex "Seasons" (Astrakhan) is a bright representative of the project, where all conditions for a comfortable and safe life are created. Our task is to evaluate it from all sides and identify the main advantages.





about the project

"Seasons" (Astrakhan) is a comfort class residential complex, which distinguishes it from the background of numerous new buildings. Four monolithic brick houses of 12 floors each personify summer, autumn, winter and spring. To date, all houses are commissioned and settled, but you still have a chance to become the owner of a great apartment.

Location

The residential complex "Seasons" (Astrakhan) is located on Balashovskaya Street, a few minutes drive from the city center. The happy owners of apartments here focus on a good location: they live in a developed and well-maintained area, have access to all infrastructure, and at the same time have a comfortable atmosphere, some isolation from the noise of the street and their own park of greenery, which surrounds the new buildings.

Transport accessibility

Near the residential complex "Seasons" (Astrakhan) there are public transport stops: using minibuses and a bus, you can reach any point of the city within half an hour. For car owners, a trip to Yablochkova Street, running through the whole city, is provided. By the way, the reviews of residents focus on the parking lot that every home has: it is under 24-hour video surveillance, ensuring peace of mind for every car owner.

The project does not provide for the availability of its own infrastructure in the complex, but within walking distance from new buildings there are two private and four state kindergartens, two secondary schools, all the necessary shops, cafes, restaurants, and service organizations.
