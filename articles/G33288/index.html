How to make a cat out of paper using origami technique is quick and easy

A cat is a very beautiful animal. In many cultures, magical traits are attributed to her. For example, in Russia it is customary to let a cat into the new house first. It is believed that it will certainly bring happiness and prosperity to the home. If you like to do crafts with kids, then you can perform a simple and cute model. Let's see how to make a cat out of paper using the origami technique.

Materials

To work, you need a minimum amount of preparation. All you need is paper. Preferably color so that the kitty is original. Also stock up with colored felt-tip pens or markers. If the paper is white, then you can use paints, such as gouache. If desired, parts of the muzzle can be decorated with colored paper according to the type of application. That is, you need PVA glue.

Instruction manual

If you have never previously made origami from paper, a cat is a great chance to learn this interesting technique. The most important thing here is to follow the instructions exactly. Gently fold the paper and smooth the folds. First, do it yourself, and then you can explain the sequence of work to your child. The proposed model is the head of an animal. However, if desired, it is very simple to make the body.





Make the head

Cut a square from a piece of colored paper whose sides will be equal to 15 centimeters. Lay the figure directly in front of you and bend diagonally so that the opposite corners close. Now, to understand how to make a cat out of paper, lay the blank with the base down. Bend both lower corners to the top, and then push the future ears apart so that they can be seen from behind the top. Flip the model with the back to you. It remains to shape our muzzle and bend the triangle on the crown back. Carefully smooth out all folds. Now you can start decorating the muzzle. Marker draw eyes, nose, mouth and mustache. Eyes can be cut out of colored paper and glued to PVA. The cat's head is ready. You can stop here. You can play or decorate a children's room with such a toy.





Make the torso

If you want to figure out how to make a cat out of paper entirely, then get ready to complete one more detail. Make it very simple. Prepare a square exactly the same as for making the head. Bend the sheet diagonally, iron the fold carefully. Bend the left corner to the right to get the tail of a cat. To understand how to make a cat out of paper, attach the head to the body by gluing the upper corner of the lower part to the head.





Conclusion

So, now you have learned a new technique - origami. You know how to make a cat out of paper. Making crafts can be turned into an exciting game. Make some shapes from multi-colored paper, color them all in different ways. Each model takes only a few minutes. And cats are fun and cute.
