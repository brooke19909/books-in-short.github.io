Where in Novosibirsk you can take a walk. Monuments, parks, museums

You were visiting the largest Siberian city and do not know how to spend time? Our article will tell you where to go for a walk in Novosibirsk and what to do.



As in most cities, in Novosibirsk there are always parks, squares, a promenade, many shops, cafes, theaters and cinema halls. So, where in Novosibirsk can you take a walk in the summer? The traditional place for a walk in the warm season is the embankment of the Ob River. The widest river divides the city into two parts, connecting them with a long bridge. The Ob promenade is a fairly extensive place for walking; to visit all the entertainment located by the river, it will take all day. Everything is arranged here for a perfect holiday: green lawns, cozy benches, gentle stairs and dim lights.



For lovers of vivid impressions, walks are organized on a river boat with a visit to the Korablik island. There are many carousels and attractions on the promenade, including karting. You can grab a bite to eat at cafes and restaurants located on the banks of the river.

In the evening you can admire the colorful fountains arranged right in the riverbed.

Monuments and architectural ensembles - about a hundred more places where you can walk in Novosibirsk. Unusual buildings are the heritage of past centuries. For example, a fabulous house on Sibrevkoma street. The owner of this log house with carved shutters, now bearing the title of city monument, was Vyhodtsev Alexander Fedorovich. The highlights of the wooden architecture of the Siberian city include: an interesting-shaped house on Ordzhonikidze Street, the house of Vasily Nikitin, Kopylov's estate, a two-story house on Krasnoyarsk Street and many others. Of the unusual and modern - monuments to the Business Woman and the Sewing Machine. As well as a monument to the Laboratory mouse near the Research Institute of Cytology and Genetics.

If you are interested in where you can take a walk in Novosibirsk to increase erudition, then the list of museums in the city is not out of place. The Museum of Local Lore, the Museum of Railway Engineering, the Russian Birch Bark, the Wax Museum and the World Burial Culture, as well as the institution with the interesting name The Museum of Happiness - these are the most interesting walking routes. Glory Monument, Trinity Vladimir Cathedral, Alley of the Bards - and that's not all!



Where to go if you come as a family? Where in Novosibirsk can I go for a walk with my children? Worth a visit to the Galileo Science Park. Here, in visual exhibits, the basic laws of nature are unobtrusively explained to children. Zoo, puppet theater, city for the smallest, and finally, ordinary parks of culture, recreation and entertainment - something from this list will surely appeal to the kids. In addition, the city has a chic water park, bowling for children, an equestrian club and a dolphinarium.

Where to walk in Novosibirsk in the winter? There are always snow slides and ice rinks. In addition, in Novosibirsk it would be interesting to admire the ice figures that adorn the entire city at this time of year.
