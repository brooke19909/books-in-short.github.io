Ariston washing machines - the best choice for the hostess

Household appliances for the home, for the family must necessarily meet the main principles: to be economical, easy to use and have a beautiful design. All this applies to the choice of washing machines. Indeed, for the hostess, washing along with ironing and cooking meals is one of the most important things. Modern manufacturers offer a wide selection of models. The Ariston washing machines, which are produced by the well-known Italian company of household appliances, are credible.



A little bit about the company

A popular brand of household appliances is owned by the Italian company Merloni Elettrodomestici. It was founded in 1975, Vittorio Merloni was at its source. He still leads the company, which now ranks third in the world in the production of home appliances.

How to choose a washing machine

When choosing household appliances, you need to decide what its functions will be most in demand for you. If you plan to install a washing machine in the kitchen, it must be built-in. In the bathroom, a freestanding model is suitable. The laundry loading method is also an important parameter. It can be frontal or vertical. Estimate what capacity the drum should have. As a rule, the best option is five kilograms. In addition, it is necessary to take into account the dimensions of household appliances, washing modes. By type of control, there are automatic washing machines or semi-automatic washing machines . The best option is the first. The material from which the drum of the machine is made is also important.

Differences of washing machines " Ariston"

If you settled on the models of this company, then you did not lose. Ariston washing machines are of high quality. The manufacturer offers a wide range of household units. Thanks to special washing modes, they gently and easily wash almost any fabric, they can even be trusted with lace. During the process, depending on the models, six hundred to 1200 rpm is performed.

The unit does not jump around the bathroom, but works quietly and silently. This is facilitated by a special balancing system and the Super Silent function. In addition to washing programs, the manufacturer has provided several modes of drying clothes. Washing machines are economical. When working with them, less detergent is consumed . There are features "Easy Ironing" and "Whitening."

Description of models of washing machines "Ariston"

The European home appliance company offers consumers high-quality units. The beautiful design, ease of use, which distinguish the Ariston washing machines, have already been appreciated throughout the world. Models are equipped with a large number of functions for washing things from various fabrics.

You can monitor the process on the LCD screen. The selected mode is indicated by the LED indication. Special features of household appliances will also please consumers. There is protection against accidental turning on of the machine by children or from overflow of water. Undoubtedly, the hostesses will like the Delayed Start function. It allows you to program the unit to turn on at a specific time. New models represent a combination of the latest achievements of developers. But good old appliances are also good, for example, the Ariston 2000 washing machine, which can now be purchased only with the hands of an ad. The model appeared on the market in the early 2000s. The owner of this unit received a very convenient and economical device that does not consume much water. It has a very simple and intuitive operation using the buttons on the front panel. Of the useful features you can mention "Delayed start" (from one to twelve hours), washing wool and products from various fabrics.
If the hose is defective, the water supply is blocked. The Golden Cashmere function is actually universal and allows you to wash not only wool products, but also things from other materials that need careful care. Another model that deserves attention is the Ariston 129 washing machine, which has a lot of positive properties. This built-in unit allows you to wash seven and dry - five kilograms of laundry. If you do not want to spend a long time on washing, there are modes for fifteen and thirty minutes. Delayed start allows you to program the unit to turn on after three, six or nine hours. Automatic control is responsible for the formation of foam and water consumption .
