Dinopark (Magnitogorsk): a journey into the prehistoric world, accessible to everyone

Water park or amusement park today does not surprise residents of a large city. Would you like to visit the dinopark? Magnitogorsk offers this opportunity to its residents and guests. Just 40 kilometers from the city, not on the banks of a picturesque lake, there is a real prehistoric world in which everyone can touch a dinosaur by the tail and listen to the growl of ancient reptiles.

The concept of the park is original: children without adults and adults without children are not allowed here. If you decide to visit the dinopark (Magnitogorsk), you should not expect something sinister, as in the films from the series "Jurassic Park". On the contrary, all the animals here look realistic, but at the same time not at all frightening, but, on the contrary, quite cute and friendly. And this means that even the youngest visitors will not be afraid and will be delighted with such an excursion.

Comfortable stay for the whole family



The prehistoric park is located on the shore of a large and incredibly beautiful lake. In the warmer months you can swim here. Also on the territory of the park there are children's and sports grounds in the open air, various attractions work. It offers other additional services dinopark (Magnitogorsk). Hotel, restaurant, cafe - what else is needed for a comfortable stay with the whole family? Many residents come here for the whole weekend. The accommodation conditions are decent, there are enough options for organizing leisure.

Future plans



The dinosaur park is striking in its realism. The figures of animals are set in the most natural environment and organically fit into the surrounding landscape. The creator of the park claims that the dinopark is only the beginning of a huge family recreation area with entertainment for every taste. Today, next to the giant lizards, one can already see realistic sculptures of insects enlarged by several hundred times.

In the near future it is planned to open a water park and other entertainment facilities. At the same time, prices will remain at a relatively low and affordable level. The owner of the entertainment complex emphasizes that the main goal is not to make a profit, but to give joy and new impressions to the residents and guests of the city.

Today dinopark (Magnitogorsk) is one of the largest in the country and the only one of its kind in the region. All residents of the city who have already managed to visit this place share exclusively positive impressions. What is remarkable: the dinosaur park is liked not only by children, but also by their parents. Many adults enthusiastically respond to the movements of prehistoric dinosaurs and are happily photographed with sculptures. Children do not lag behind them.
