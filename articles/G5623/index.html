Location, how to get there

The location of the Chistye Prudy Hotel (Adler) is wonderful. The Black Sea splashes on one side, and on the other two large ponds surrounded by an emerald green lawn and still small young trees. A racing track was laid nearby, several parks were opened (Olympic, ornithological, botanical "Southern cultures", the famous Adventure Park), a temple of the image of the Savior Christ was built, there is a wonderful promenade with recreation areas. All these facilities are within walking distance of the Chistye Prudy Hotel. So, you can leisurely walk to the Olympic Park in just 10 minutes. Nearby is the railway station of the same name.

Getting to the Chistye Prudy Hotel (Adler) is very convenient from the local railway station. You just need to transfer to the "Swallow" train (runs along the coast) and get off at the stop "Olympic Park". From there to the hotel - walk about 20 minutes, but you can take a taxi or book a transfer. In addition, from the railway station to the hotel there are buses and minibuses No. 57, 125 and 100. The route from Adler Airport is also easy. You need to take the minibus number 135, get on it to the television center (called "New Century"), and then transfer to all the same buses No. 57, 125 or 100.

Sea

The Black Sea coast is famous for its beaches, clean and gentle sea and excellent weather for almost the entire season. Many vacationers who intend to spend a vacation in the institution we are considering are wondering if the Chistye Prudy (Adler) hotel has a beach. We answer: there is, it is called "South Star". Located, if you go from checkpoint 2, about 150 meters. The beach is small in size, about 100 meters, but well-groomed. Umbrella fungi are installed here, sun loungers are issued for the price of 200 rubles per day. Rest on the city beach (from checkpoint 1 to 250 m) costs 100 rubles per day. The soil on both beaches is medium-sized pebbles, so you need special rubber bathing sandals, which can be purchased at the local market or in one of the many shops. The average price of such shoes is 200 rubles. The entrance to the water on the beach is not quite gentle. The second inconvenience is stones underfoot. Third, sometimes there are many jellyfish in the sea. But, as vacationers say, all these shortcomings are more than covered by a clean, warm and gentle sea. The water temperature here in the summer months is +28 degrees, in May it is already +19 ... + 20 , and in September-October it still keeps at +23 ... + 25 .
