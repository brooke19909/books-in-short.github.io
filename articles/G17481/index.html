Freezer chest for home: selection tips and manufacturer reviews

A chest freezer is a device that is designed to store frozen food. As a rule, such units are used in various small stores, as well as in large supermarkets. A feature of freezing chests is considered to be high power, compactness and, as a result, mobility. Today they are also in demand for homes. Their owners have the opportunity to stock up, for example, with a large amount of meat. The refrigerator is not able to accommodate a lot of products.





First of all, people who live in the countryside began to acquire a freezer chest for the house. This is due to the abundance of meat and dairy products, which they accumulate. Citizens just look at these devices, but some are seriously thinking about buying.

How to choose the right one?

First of all, you should know that there are various types of chest freezers. For ice cream in trays provides a separate subspecies. There are also models with glass. Moreover, they can be curved from above or not. If you consider the option for the home, the most appropriate is considered to be freezing chests with a conventional lid.

Of the characteristics primarily pay attention to the power of freezing. This indicator is measured in kg / day. Usually it fluctuates around 12 kg / day, but much depends on the manufacturer. Next, you need to evaluate the management of the freezer. For the home, it is best to look closely at models with a conventional mechanical controller without a display. In this case, the quick freeze function is welcome. Also important is the ability to maintain temperature when a power outage occurs. To date, no one is safe from this, so the manufacturer needs to check the exact time.

The noise level of the device also plays an important role. Typically, this indicator fluctuates around 45 dB. In general, it is considered normal to be close to such a device, feeling quite comfortable. Lastly, the dimensions of the freezer are evaluated, because we are talking about a rather voluminous device that will be placed in the house. On average, the height of the model is 800 mm, the width is 1000 mm, and the depth is 700 mm. There are good freezing chests (market price) of about 20 thousand rubles.
