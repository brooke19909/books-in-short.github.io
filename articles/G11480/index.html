Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 * (Hainan): reviews, hotel description, service

Friendly and unusually original China, Russian tourists visit with pleasure. Some come here for the sake of profitable shopping, others seek to see unusual and very interesting sights, while others dream of just having a great rest on the always warm South China Sea. We invite you to have a wonderful time on the largest Chinese island of Hainan at Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 *. The reviews about this hotel are mixed. Someone here liked everything, but someone found some flaws. Note that Chinese 4-star hotels are slightly different from those in European countries, which must be taken into account when choosing a tour. But Hainan Island itself, the Bay of Pure Water, where the hotel is located, will certainly not leave anyone indifferent. The mild climate, fantastically beautiful nature, wonderful beaches, lots of entertainment - all this awaits those who choose this amazing place for relaxation. And the hotel staff will try to create all conditions so that tourists will certainly want to return here again.



Location

Infinity Ocean Beach Resort is located in a quiet, beautiful Bay of Clear Water at the southern tip of Hainan. This place is famous for its unique coastline with a length of 12,000 km with golden singing sands. These are only in Hawaii and in Australia. Many people call the island of Hainan East Hawaii, because it lies at the same latitude as this famous resort. The hotel is located at some distance from the large city of Sanya, which is 35 km. Other important and interesting objects are approximately the same distance from the hotel. So, to the island of monkeys, to the island of Wuzhizhou and to the railway. Lingshui Station can be reached in 35-40 minutes. At Lingshui station, you can take a ticket for a high-speed or regular train and go around the entire eastern part of the island, as well as get to the mainland.

How to get there

Basically, only Russian tourists travel by plane to Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 *. There are several international airports in China. One of them is located on Hainan Island. This airport is called Phoenix. From it to the described hotel is 45 km. From Moscow, a little over 11 hours fly here. From the airport to the hotel tourists deliver modern transfers. The road takes about an hour. It is important to note that buses do not reach the entrance to the central building of the hotel, because it is prohibited by local regulations. Tourists have to walk a few tens of meters with things, as well as climb the stairs in two flights.



General description of the hotel Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 *

All without exception, tourists in their reviews note that this hotel impresses with its scale and its beautiful territory. Some tour operators are positioning him as a five-star, but in reality he has four stars. This hotel began to take tourists in 2012, so everything here is still in fairly good condition. The territory of the hotel, according to tourists, is large and very green. There are many flowers on it, walking paths are laid. Under the canopy of tropical palm trees and evergreen trees, five tall buildings are harmoniously located. Each has a very spacious exclusive lounge. The design is different everywhere, but each hall is furnished with many comfortable soft sofas with pillows and tables. Each lobby has a bar, there is free Wi-Fi, air conditioning. Also on the territory there is a children's playground, there is a large swimming pool, there are sports fields.

Infrastructure

Those who decide to spend their vacation or celebrate an important event in China at the Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 * will not fail at all. The hotel provides all its guests with a decent vacation, and in addition, provides the opportunity to combine a pleasant stay with wellness or work. The infrastructure of this hotel has a spacious business center with a spacious conference room equipped with the necessary equipment (microphones, voice recorders, cinema and video equipment, photocopy, fax). There is also a small room for important meetings and negotiations. For those who wish to celebrate the celebration, the hotel provides a chic banquet room and service. Reception staff are meeting guests in the central building. Unfortunately, they only speak good Chinese. According to tourists in their reviews of Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 * (Hainan), only one receptionist understands Russian a little, but she does not work every day. Therefore, Russian guests have to solve current issues with gestures, but this does not affect the quality of service. At the reception you can exchange currency, rent a car or bike, buy a sightseeing tour, call a taxi. On the ground floor of the central building there are gaming halls. One has billiard tables, the other has tennis, and the third offers the opportunity to play the popular game of mahjong. In addition to entertainment, in this hotel you can improve your health using Chinese medicine and undergo examination by local experts. To do this, there is a spa, sauna, medical center, massage rooms.



Residence

The Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 * hotel (Hainan) has 406 superior rooms. According to our compatriots, tourists from Russia are placed here only on the first four floors. On the floors starting from the fifth, they provide rooms only to Chinese tourists. On the ground floor of the central building there is a parking lot. The views from the windows of all rooms are mainly on the territory. Only those who will live on the fourth floor can enjoy the surroundings.

Nutrition

In Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 * food, according to Russian tourists, is based only on the principles of traditional Chinese cuisine, which is not suitable for all Russians. Most tours to this hotel are only offered with breakfast included in the room rate. Breakfast is served in a beautiful restaurant. Opinions on the quality of dishes are different, which depends on the individual preferences of each person. Every day, the menu has a lot of salads, several types of side dish (mainly rice with various sauces), sausages, corn, eggs, fruits. From drinks you can take tea, coffee or water. Tourists can have lunch and breakfast in one of the many cafes located nearby. Unfortunately, in each of them, information about food and ingredients is written in Chinese. Only in some cafes photos of dishes are attached to the description.

Hotel for children

A lot of tourists come with children on holiday to China to Hainan Island with their children. Reviews of the service for babies, which provides Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 *, are very restrained. On the territory for young guests is equipped with a playground with various slides and swings. Guestrooms provide cots upon request. That, perhaps, is all that is here for the children.



Leisure for adults

In Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 * entertainment for young people and older people is more diverse than for kids. It features a spacious outdoor pool. Unfortunately, the installation of sunbeds around it is not provided. Beach towels are also not provided. Swimming in the pool is allowed only in special swimming caps. Sports enthusiasts have free access to the fitness center, which has modern sports equipment. Guests can relax in the sauna or in the massage room. All those who want to experience the healing effects of Chinese herbs are waiting for folk healers. They are taken in a separate treatment center. Animations and discos in the described hotel are not provided, but there is a cinema hall where popular films are shown every day.

Beach

It's no secret that for the sake of a wonderful warm sea, many tourists go to Infinity Ocean Beach Resort 4 *. The beach from the hotel is only a hundred meters, which is reported by many tour operators. In fact, it is, but there is no direct road to the coast. To get to the beach, hotel guests must follow the road surrounding several villas. As a result, the path to the sea takes 12-15 minutes. On Hainan Island in the Bay of Pure Water, the beach is amazing. It is municipal, but very clean. Here, even at the peak of the season, there is not a large concentration of people, because the length of the coastline is very large. Therefore, you can always find a quiet cozy corner. Sun loungers and parasols are not here, vacationers are located right on the sand. They are unusual because they know how to ‚Äúsing‚Äù with every movement of golden grains of sand. The water in the sea near the coast is always clear, there are almost no waves. This is the best place to relax with young children. But for those who love long-distance swimming, this beach may seem boring, as local rescuers very eagerly perform their duties and do not allow far to go far from the coast. Otherwise, they not only begin to give signals, forcing violators to return to the permitted territory, but they also call the police.
