DIY cat costume for a boy

All children love costume parties because they have fun. Often after such events gifts are given. Also, children get the opportunity to express themselves through their outfit. Especially if it's a New Year's suit of a cat in boots for a boy.

Why is the outfit so popular

Interesting fact: a cat costume for a boy is one of the five favorite children's images. That's why:

  • The outfit has a large selection of options. Leopold, Puss in Boots, A kitten named Gav, a cat from the Bremen Town Musicians, Cheshire Cat , etc. All these characters vary in character, but they also have one thing in common. After all, they are all cats.




  • The costume is the personification of a graceful, smart and dexterous animal. The cat is the unchanging hero of a large number of Russian folk tales. He is a purr, and a mousetrap, and a protector. Therefore, representatives of the cat family like boys and girls.
  • The carnival image includes a tail, and children like to imagine themselves as cute animals.

First of all, regardless of the age of the child, any cat costume for a boy should be made of quality material.

How to choose a beautiful suit

So, how to choose the New Year's cat costume for the boy. It is worth paying attention to the following factors:

  • to know the opinion of the baby about his New Year's outfit;
  • tell the child what the adult wants to buy for him;
  • go to the store with the boy to try on a suit on him and immediately find out his opinion.

In the event that the desired image could not be found in the store, you can choose the model you like on the Internet with your child. However, such a purchase should be made in advance, because if the cat costume for the boy does not fit, he will have nothing to go for the holiday.
