Useful information on how to get to the metro from Pulkovo

How to get to the metro from Pulkovo? Perhaps this question interests everyone who has ever come to the Northern capital through the air gates of the Russian Federation.

But is it real at all? Does any public transport go there or the only option is to resort to taxi services and pay a huge amount of money for travel?

This will be discussed in this article. However, the reader will learn much more than just a set of information on how to get to the metro from Pulkovo, he will get acquainted with the services offered by the airport, its achievements and bandwidth.

Section 1. General description of the object





Pulkovo is actually the only airport in St. Petersburg, which is at the same time the international air gate of the North-West Federal District. By the way, few people know that it was called "Highway" until 1973. Today it is located at a distance of twenty kilometers from the city center.

Having served almost thirteen million people in 2013, the airport took third place in the number of passengers brought. Pulkovo Airport is also the base airport for Transaero and Russia airlines.

Now all, both international and domestic flights, are operated through the new Terminal-1. And the Pulkovo-1 terminal, well-known to many, was closed for repairs, which they plan to finish in 2014. After repair work, the Pulkovo-1 building will be connected to the newly-built terminal, where they will serve passengers of domestic flights. At the end of March this year, the Pulkovo-2 terminal, which sent international flights, completely stopped working.

Express buses from Pulkovo-1 (No. K800) and from Pulkovo-2 (No. K900) run every hour even at night. It is possible to get to several metro stations at once: Technological Institute, Moskovskaya, Vladimirskaya, Dostoevskaya, which is the final one.

Section 4. How to get to the metro from Pulkovo (buses and minibuses)

Public transport is also abundant. Of course, it moves around the city more slowly, but its prices are much lower.

A shuttle taxi No. K39 and a bus No. 39 leave from the Moskovskaya station.

From the station "Kupchino" - minibus number K-113.

Minibuses No. K-213 and No. K-3 depart from Sadovaya metro station.

From Vladimirskaya square - minibus number K-900.

Now, for visitors to this event, Pulkovo Airport will take the entire third platform. Therefore, there will be no problems with the registration and baggage claim for other passengers.
