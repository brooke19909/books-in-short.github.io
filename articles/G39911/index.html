about the project

The Stockholm residential complex is being built in close proximity to the banks of the Bolshaya Nevka River, on Primorsky Boulevard. Despite the fact that the plans include the construction of only two buildings of different floors (from 5 to 7), the project can be considered large-scale, since complex development involves not only the construction of residential buildings, but also a kindergarten, underground parking, as well as a complete arrangement of the territory in three more than a hectare in order to provide a truly comfortable living environment.

The cases are arranged in the shape of a horseshoe, which not only protects the interior of the territory from cold winds and noise, but also makes the appearance of buildings very unusual and original. About 270 apartments (from one to five-room apartments) will be put up for sale in houses. Penthouses will be located on the upper floors, and commercial objects on the lower floors. The construction of the Stockholm residential complex (St. Petersburg) began in 2014. Construction is carried out in one turn, using brick-monolithic technology.
