Bloomers for a pattern for a suit for a boy for a holiday

Harem pants are wide, loose pants that are pulled together at the waist and bottom with elastic bands. This word came to our language from Turkic and sounded earlier like shalvars. Then it acquired an easier pronunciation of the word for us. These pants were loved by residents of India and other eastern countries, worn by Zaporozhye Cossacks. They still remain an element of the national men's costume of Ukrainians.

For holidays, children for carnival dresses, it happens, need such pants. In the article we will tell in detail how to sew harem pants for a pattern. It will be useful to find out to any masters who do not trust the rental studio, but prefer to sew costumes for holiday events for their child.

What costume can be used for harem pants?

1. When we hear the word "harem pants", the first thing that comes to mind is the Ukrainian national costume. You also need to add an embroidered shirt and a wide belt to complete the image.

2. The image of the wizard, the eastern sultan. It remains to add a spare dressing gown, tied with a long belt, and a turban on his head.





3. Any costume animals. Orange harem pants - fox or squirrel. Gray or black is a wolf. Browns are a bear. It remains to add a turtleneck of the same color or a white fur vest and sew a tail on the harem pants. Put a mask on your head or sew a hat with ears. The outfit is ready.

4. Costume Parsley or Pierrot.

If you think about it, such harem pants are used even for girls (oriental costume). In general, there are many options, as you can see, and it‚Äôs easy to sew harem pants on a pattern. Next, we will take a step-by-step look at how best to do this.

Sharovar style

These peculiar pants are easy to recognize and highlight among other types of clothing. This is because they have a colorful appearance. These are wide, loose pants, which are gathered from above on the waist and are taken on elastic in several rows. At the bottom of each leg, too, the material is collected and assembled with elastic. Sometimes they make a single, and often double.





For carnival costumes, harem pants according to the pattern are sewn mainly of sparkling satin fabric. It gives along a festive look. The colors used are different. For the Ukrainian national costume, trousers for the pattern are sewn in blue or light blue, although the red option is also possible. For other characters, different color schemes are selected.

Pattern

To prepare the pattern, you must first make the necessary measurements. Measure the child‚Äôs hip circumference, the length of the trousers along the line of the outer side of the thigh, the length of the trouser legs along the inside of the thigh, thus calculating the height of the seat. If you manage to sew harem pants for a child‚Äôs pattern, then you can also sew light summer beach harem pants made of chintz or linen.





Leg width is optional. You can measure the boy‚Äôs trousers and add a few centimeters for a loose fit. On the belt, you can make separately sewn elastic, or you can extend the length and tuck the material inward. The same thing is with the cuffs on the legs. They can be made separate, or you can extend the length of the trousers for the boy in the pattern and bend the fabric.

How to sew harem pants?

If you know how to make a pattern by measurements, then after taking measurements and drawing a drawing on paper, everything is transferred to the fabric. It remains only to sew the details along the seamy side and arrange the belt and lower cuffs. You can make a single elastic band, or you can stitch the material twice and insert two elastic bands.

If you do not know how to make a pattern of bloomers, it does not matter. Such light pants can be sewn using any pants or leggings of the child. To do this, they need to be laid out on a table and bent in half, with the width inward. Hand gently smooth all the folds. Circle the trousers along the contours. The pattern will be the back half of the legs. Then we turn the trousers outward with the fly and fold in half in the opposite direction.





Also, you need to carefully smooth out all the folds. We put it on the material and draw a chalk around the front of the pattern. So that the trousers are wide, on each side we add a few centimeters to the allowance. Do not forget to additionally leave 1 cm at the seams. The length of the legs also increases, as does the height of the waist.

Then two identical parts of the back and front parts of the bloomers are cut out. The details are sewn on the wrong side. The belt is tucked in half, and several strips for elastic are sewn. The same thing is done with the bottom of the legs to stitch the cuffs.

It remains only to iron the thing - and you can wear it. As you can see, it‚Äôs not difficult to sew bloomers with your own hands for a child for a holiday in kindergarten. The main thing is to have a desire to do something for your baby.
