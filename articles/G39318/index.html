Air carriers

Absolutely the majority of aircraft in this direction are operated by charter flights. This means that the tour operator that generated this tourist product has signed an agreement with the airline that buys a certain number of seats on each flight. Depending on the projected demand, this quantity may vary each year. If the tour operator cannot sell this number of seats, then he will still pay the air carrier for them, and thus incur financial losses.

Almost all major airlines operate on charter programs. How much to fly to Antalya from Moscow? It does not depend on whether a scheduled flight or a charter flight.
