A modern mobile phone or smartphone is that friend and assistant who is constantly with the person. Therefore, it is a reflection of our personality. The most important part of setting up a personal mobile device is setting individual ringtones. There are several ways to solve this problem. The question of how to put a melody on a call on Android almost completely depends on the version of the system.

Set custom ringtones on Android 2 and 3

These versions were released 5 years ago, in mid-2009. The main efforts of their creators were aimed mainly at improving the level of security, as well as optimizing the available memory and system performance. Then the possibility of establishing non-standard ringtones through the usual setting of personal calls was not yet realized. That is why, before putting the melody on the bell in the old version of "Android", you must first prepare. The most common and simple ways are to create special folders or set the soundtrack through a standard player.

How on Android to put a ringtone using special folders

If your smartphone has a USB cable for connecting to your home computer, then just create special audio folders for incoming calls, an alarm clock, SMS or any kind of reminder.



In order to understand how to put a melody on a call on Android, you need to follow a few steps. The whole process can be reflected as follows:

  1. Using a standard cable, the smartphone connects to an existing computer.
  2. A special folder for music and Media ringtones is created in the root directory. Or, if it is already present, its contents are revealed.
  3. The Audio subdirectory is created in the selected folder.
  4. To add your own tunes, the following directories are created: Alarm, Ringtones, Notification. To put a melody on a call, the Ringtones directory is necessary.
  5. This is where the necessary songs or soundtracks for Android are downloaded. You can put a melody on a call just from the created Ringtones root folder.
  6. For correct and stable operation, the phone is disconnected from the computer and rebooted.
