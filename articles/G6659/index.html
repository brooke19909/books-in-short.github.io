San Juan Park Hotel 2 * (Spain / Costa Brava) - photos, prices and reviews

Lloret de Mar has long been recognized as the tourist capital of the Spanish resort of Costa Brava. Every year, tens of thousands of vacationers from various countries, including Russia, flock to this seaside town. The bulk of tourists are young people, attracted by many nightclubs, discos and bars. But also here you can meet family tourists with children, as well as travelers of an older age category. If you want to spend a vacation in this resort and are looking for a budget option for accommodation, then consider the San Juan Park Hotel 2 *.





Location

The two-star San Juan Park Hotel is located in the very center of the resort town of Lloret de Mar on the Spanish coast. Girona is 40 kilometers away, and an air harbor located in the vicinity of this city is 23 kilometers away. Thus, upon arrival at the Girona airport, it will take you no more than half an hour to get to the hotel. Some tourists also come here from Barcelona 's air harbor. The distance to it is about 70 km. The central streets of Lloret de Mar with clubs, restaurants, bars and many other attractions can be reached in just ten minutes from the hotel. The beach closest to the hotel is at the same distance.

Photo and description

San Juan Park Hotel 2 * (Spain) began its work several decades ago, in 1970. The last large-scale reconstruction here was carried out in 2000. The entire housing stock of the hotel, consisting of 75 rooms, is located in a five-story building equipped with an elevator. In addition to the rooms, the restaurant and the bar, there is also a rooftop pool with wonderful views of the surroundings. The rooms here are standard. They are decorated in a simple but pleasant style. They have everything you need: a private bathroom, a TV, a fridge and a balcony. This hotel is equally well suited both for young people and for accommodating tourists arriving on vacation with their entire families with children, as well as for older people.





Prices

The cost of accommodation in San Juan Park Hotel 2 *, according to most tourists who have been here, is consistent with the stardom of the hotel, the conditions of accommodation and the level of service. So, a seven-day stay in this hotel at the height of summer will cost you from 365 euros for a single room, from 580 euros for a double room, from 826 euros for a triple room and from 1073 euros for a four-person room.

San Juan Park Hotel 2 * (Spain): traveler reviews from Russia

Since modern tourists, when choosing a place to stay in a particular country, are guided not so much by the description of hotels as by the impressions of people who have already stayed in them, we decided to slightly ease your task and bring to your attention the generalized comments of the Russians who this year had a rest in San Juan Park. Having run a little ahead, we note that most travelers were very pleased with their choice. In their opinion, this hotel not only meets its two-star category, but also draws on all three stars. In a word, if you are looking for a budget hotel in a city with developed tourist infrastructure on the Spanish coast, then San Juan Park Hotel 2 * will be a good option.





Reviews about the hotel and its rooms

According to our compatriots, the rooms in this hotel are not very large, but there is enough space here. They are decorated in a pleasant style. Bathrooms are also not very spacious, but quite comfortable. They either have a shower with a curtain, or a full bath. There is a hairdryer. Problems with hot water, according to guests, were not noted. The furniture and appliances in the rooms are not new, but everything is in good, working condition. Some rooms (mainly on the ground floor) have Wi-Fi. If the Internet does not work in your room, then you can always connect to it in the hotel lobby.

San Juan Park rooms are cleaned every day. The quality of cleaning in general, all guests were satisfied. However, according to them, maids sometimes forget to add soap in the bathroom. Also, experienced travelers pay attention that if you want a towel to be changed, then you should throw it on the floor. Otherwise, the maid will consider that she does not need a replacement.





Impressions of tourists about the location and contingent of the hotel

As for the location, the San Juan Park Hotel 2 * is in a quiet location near residential buildings. This is a big plus, because here your night's rest will not be disturbed by the noise of discos. Nearby there are supermarkets, restaurants, shops and a host of other tourist infrastructure. A seven-minute walk from the hotel there is a bus station with links to neighboring Blanes or Barcelona and other cities in Spain. Free buses to the water park and go-karts depart from here. According to the guests, you can walk from the hotel both to the sea and to the city center in just ten minutes.

The contingent of vacationers in this hotel is very diverse. So, here you can meet both families with children of all ages, as well as youth, as well as pensioners. Most tourists come here from Eastern Europe. There are not so many of our compatriots and residents of other countries of the former USSR as in hotels, for example, Turkey or Montenegro.





Travelers' opinions on food and staff

As for food in the San Juan Park Hotel 2 * (Costa Brava, Spain), in general, the guests were satisfied with its level. So, breakfasts here are standard for European hotels. In the morning, the restaurant offers several types of cheese and sausages, granola, toasts, eggs, fruits, rolls and croissants, jam, coffee, tea and milk. The lunch and dinner menu is more diverse: cream soup, several main dishes, salads, dessert. By the way, ice cream is often offered for dessert for dinner. In a word, nobody will remain hungry here. In addition, it is possible to ask for a lunch box if you plan to go on an excursion in the early morning. In addition, if for some reason you skip dinner, and you have paid half-board, then the next day you can replace it by having lunch in the hotel restaurant.

The impression of the staff at San Juan Park Hotel 2 * was also positive for most tourists. So, according to them, upon arrival, administrators always try to quickly fill out all the papers and check you in the room as soon as possible. All hotel staff are friendly, welcoming and always ready to help. True, no one speaks Russian here, however, you can always easily explain yourself by recalling a little curriculum in English. And if you know at least a little Spanish, then you will become the most welcome guests at the San Juan Park.





The opinion of our compatriots about beach vacations and entertainment

This hotel does not offer any special entertainments to its guests. However, there is a small pool with a sun terrace on the roof of the hotel. Many guests enjoyed spending time here, especially since it offers a nice view of the surroundings. Basically, people come here for a beach holiday, as well as for trips to night clubs, bars and discos and visits to attractions. Since the city center is literally a ten-minute walk from San Juan Park Hotel 2 * (Costa Brava), all the entertainment you have, as they say, is at hand. The nearest municipal beach can also be reached in a few minutes on foot.
