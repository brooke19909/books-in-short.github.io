Hotel Laguna Park 4 * (Croatia, Istria): tourist reviews

Modern tourists have become more demanding and selective, more attentive to the choice of resort. All roads are open before us, which is why both beach lovers and fans of the excursion program will find the ideal resort for themselves. If it seems to you that you have managed to travel around all the best countries of the world, to visit remote continents, think about whether you have been to Croatia? This resort can hardly be called popular among tourists, but most of those vacationers who at least once had the opportunity to visit here will definitely return to this country again and again.





The Istrian Peninsula is the pearl of the Adriatic coast of Croatia. This is the cultural, historical center of the country, where modern tourists prefer to relax. It is from here, from the peninsula, that you can go to the fabulous Italian city of Venice. And not only this attracts vacationers: a stunning sea with crystal water, chic nature, hospitable people, a huge cultural heritage. As for the hotels of the peninsula, there are enough of them. There are huge tourist complexes represented by several hotels, there are more modest options. It all depends on the taste preferences and financial capabilities of tourists. And we suggest paying attention to a good one, if you believe the description of the hotel - Laguna Park 4 * (Croatia). Based on the reviews of real vacationers, we will try to give him an objective assessment and determine what conditions he can offer potential guests.

About hotel

Of course, going to the resort, it would be nice to know in advance which hotels and hotels are located on it. Hotel Laguna Park 4 * (Croatia) is a hotel that managed to win love among modern tourists and gain a good rating on the largest tourist portals. It is located just 2 km from the city of Porec, on the first coastline. Only 100 meters and you are on the coast. Near the hotel there is a bus stop, from where you can reach the nearest city in just 15 minutes. The airport in Pula is only 60 km, so the trip will not be tiring for tourists.





Location

Real vacationers note that the hotel is well located. Near it there are many shops, souvenir shops, small restaurants and cafes where you can taste the local culinary masterpieces performed by the best chefs. All vacationers with children can go to an excellent playground, located just a few minutes walk from the hotel. Athletes will like a chic sports complex equipped with modern fitness equipment.

Nutrition

Surely all guests of Croatia are concerned about the issue related to food at the Hotel Laguna Park 4 * hotel (Croatia). Reviews of tourists note that the hotel offers three meals a day on a buffet basis. Everyone can choose the best option for themselves, for example, only breakfast, lunch with dinner or full board.





The food at the hotel can hardly be called diverse, but all the products and dishes are very tasty. A lot of fish, meat, seafood, fresh vegetables, fruits of local pastries. Nobody will be left hungry for sure. For children there is a special children's menu: milk porridge in the morning, pasta with cheese, pancakes with cottage cheese.

Tourists going on a morning excursion can order lunches with them - for this, the evening before, you need to contact the hotel reception.

Entertainment on site

All guests of the Laguna Park 4 * hotel (Croatia) have access to an excellent outdoor pool with fresh water measuring 21x15 meters, equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. There is also a 4x4 meter children's pool with small slides - a favorite vacation spot for the smallest tourists and vacationers.





Sports equipment hire is available on site: for an additional fee you can play badminton, tennis, mini-golf and even ride a water bike.

Animation is a separate story. Of course, many tourists do not expect something chic from a mid-range European hotel. But at the Laguna Park 4 * hotel (Croatia), the entertainment program for adults and children is organized at the highest level: all kinds of sports competitions, morning gymnastics, water aerobics, live music in the evenings, bright and enchanting show programs are held. In a word, if you, as a tourist, are attracted exclusively to beach vacations, you are not going to leave the hotel, here you will definitely not be bored.

Beach

The beach is 100 meters away. All tourists traveling to Croatia should understand that here you will definitely not see sandy beaches, on the contrary, the beaches here are rocky, with huge rocks, boulders. That is why it is necessary to stock up on special shoes in advance. But the beach is clean, equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas, the sea is transparent even at considerable depths. In a storm, you can always take advantage of the alternative - a swimming pool on site.
