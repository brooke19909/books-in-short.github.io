Anapa Sights and nature of the resort of the Krasnodar Territory

The noise of the gentle sea, the beauty of nature, the presence of clean sandy and pebble beaches, an abundance of various attractions - this is only a small part of what attracts tourists from different countries to visit Anapa. This beautiful resort is located near the Caucasus Mountains. On the other hand, Anapa, you can see the endless steppes of the Taman Peninsula.

Anapa sights

In Anapa, there are many places worth seeing. So what is known for the resort city of Anapa, whose attractions are the greatest monuments of architecture?

Many modern health resorts, children's camps, cozy hotels and pensions can be visited upon arrival in this magnificent city. Anyone who wants to save money can stay in one of the resort villages - Big and Small Utrishche, Dzhemete, Sous-Psekhe. The village of Vityazevo and the village of Blagoveshchensk are also located near Anapa.

Attractions, Anapa: photos

You can start sightseeing with a visit to the beaches. Here you can see many different cafes, bars, rental points for catamarans, scooters, deck chairs and even air mattresses. Also, if you wish, you can use showers and massage cabins, locker rooms. And diving and yachting will help to make your vacation truly unforgettable.

In addition to paid beaches, you can visit free ones (after all, their total length is 50 kilometers). Those who like to enjoy the sunrises and sunsets will be interested in visiting the old lighthouse, built in 1898. Next to it is a small observation deck, which will allow you to fully enjoy the beauty and grandeur of the nature of this area.
Various water parks and a dolphinarium will not let both adult tourists and children who come to relax get bored.

Monuments of architecture

Those who like architectural monuments can start their journey with a tour of the ruins of Gorgippia. This ancient Greek policy was a major city during the time of the Bosporus kingdom. And today, visiting this museum, you can plunge into the era of antiquity, see the remains of the foundations of various buildings, wine complexes, wells and much more. Another famous monument of antiquity is the Crypt of Hercules, which contained the ashes of the aristocrats of Gorgippia. The former power of the Turkish fortress is represented by the Russian Gate, a quadrangular square-shaped structure. The historical and cultural heritage of Anapa includes various museums, the temple of St. Anufry, the reserve "Big Utrish", numerous monuments (including the monument to AS Pushkin). Not far from Anapa is the Sukko Valley, on the shore of which there is a beach.
