Socium is the largest subject of the historical process

What is society? In everyday life, this term is often used as a synonym for the words "people", "country", "state". However, they all mean what we need, only in a synonymous sense. Each of them has different, particular values.

Socium is a Latin word denoting a certain type of collective. In Russian, its exact equivalent is the word "society". His life is built around the conduct of a single economic activity, starting from primitive times. In historical times, compulsory taxes and duties were added to this. In turn, society represented by the state provides assistance in the form of subsidies to support the economy of individual lands (regions).

Society is the most complex and largest subject of the historical process. This is not just a collection of people, but their organized group. Within society, people coordinate their actions and coordinate them according to certain rules. That is, society is endowed with a unity of actions that will allow us to consider it as an independent subject of historical development.

What is society for individuals? In this aspect, several main features of this association of individuals can be distinguished. Distinctive features of society are the following. First of all, the fact that the regulation of all forms of human life in it occurs on a comprehensive scale and concerns the most important aspects of the existence of people. Society is a system that regulates family ties, security, the ability to enrich and strengthen the power of individuals.

Belonging to it is a hereditary property: its individual members are interconnected and cannot terminate this existing unity only at the request of a specific person. It, for example, cannot be abandoned as a party or a sports club.
