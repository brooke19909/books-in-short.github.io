How to spend your vacation: the village of Blagoveshchenskaya, recreation

Not so long ago, few people knew about the village of Blagoveshchensk. This resort is located near Anapa, but began to develop on its own only a decade ago. Many people liked the place of rest, and thanks to the positive reviews of tourists who spent their holidays here, the village of Blagoveshchenskaya, the recreation centers located here began to gain unprecedented popularity.

Resort Feature

The village is located near the world famous resort town of Anapa. From two sides it is washed by the estuaries of Kiziltash and Vityazevsky. However, this is not only attracted by local recreation: the village of Blagoveshchenskaya, recreation centers located here have access to the open Black Sea. It is very welcoming, pampers with clean water. A good place for families with children. If you want noisy parties and cultural pastime, then transport will quickly deliver to Anapa, where discos, bars, restaurants and other benefits of civilization are at your service.

Rest in the village

Despite the fact that the world-famous resort of Anapa, the village of Blagoveshchenskaya, is very close , the recreation centers here are distinguished by relative silence and tranquility. There are practically no noisy discos and parties, everything is designed for a quiet and relaxing holiday. The population here is also not very large, mainly the employment of local residents is aimed at renting out housing and providing services to vacationers. Income from the holiday season is the main type of earnings for the locals, so guests and vacationers are very friendly here.

Climatic conditions

Despite the fact that the village is located in a rather arid zone, the vegetation in which is usually sparse, the locals tried and greened their land. A large number of fruit trees and vegetables are grown in the private sector; viticulture and winemaking are very well developed.





It is noteworthy that the village is much warmer than in the same Anapa, although they are located very close by. For those who are going here on vacation, the village of Blagoveshchenskaya, the recreation centers available here can offer not only the gentle sea, but also the healing air. Often in these places, vacationers can admire the dolphins, which are quite frequent guests.

Temperature and Weather

An important factor for tourists are temperature indicators. Those who decided to spend their holidays in these places, the village of Blagoveshchenskaya (recreation centers) will be pleased with the wonderful weather conditions that prevail here for almost the entire holiday season. The average air temperature in the summer reaches 30 degrees, and the water in the sea usually warms up to 26 degrees. Even in winter in this region, the minimum temperature is +4 degrees. The holiday season lasts from June to September.





If you like a relaxing holiday, the village of Blagoveshchenskaya (recreation center) will certainly satisfy your needs - in these places you will get a lot of positive emotions. The most measured and calm here in mid-June.

Beaches and the sea

Beaches surround the village on three sides. They are very similar in type: clean, sandy, some have dunes. A flat and gentle beach is ideal for children and people who cannot swim. The beaches are vast and reach almost Anapa itself.

In Blagoveshchensk there are also equipped beaches, but for the most part they belong to recreation centers. Mostly people come here to mingle with wildlife. There is not much entertainment here, however, if you wish, you can find something to your liking, in addition, the water in the sea is clear. This is what attracts thousands of tourists here annually.





There is practically no algae in the water, only with a strong wind a small amount of grass can come out. However, such cases are a real rarity. The beaches are quite free, even in the midst of the season you do not need to worry about taking a seat and putting on a sun lounger. This distinguishes Blagoveshchenskaya from neighboring expensive resorts.
