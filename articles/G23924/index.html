Miami: sights of the warmest American resort

One of the most favorite vacation spots of all Americans is the state of Florida. A favorable climate, golden beaches, magnificent parks, an abundance of entertainment, an atmosphere of joy and fun - all this attracts not only residents of nearby cities, tourists from all over the world are also happy to come to Miami. The sights of this American tourist center are striking; neither children nor adults will be bored here.



The history of Miami began in 1891, when the widowed Julia Datol acquired the estate here. The woman always wanted to live where it is always warm and oranges grow. Travelers found refuge with Julia, and paid for the hospitality shown to them by work. Very little time passed, and a whole village grew from a small estate. In the 30s of the twentieth century, Miami became a full-fledged city, and after the Cuban revolution, when emigrants who understand the travel business began to come here, it was also the tourist center of the United States.

Today it is no longer a city, but separate administrative units. Of course, Miami is not divided by any borders. The sights of this region are striking in their bright colors, modernity and interesting ideas. It will not be bored for lovers of beach or outdoor activities. The resort offers many tours and excursions to unique places, you can visit museums, zoos, safaris, aquariums.
