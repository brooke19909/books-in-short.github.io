Karlovy Vary, Colonnade (hotel): overview, features and reviews

Health tourism has existed since ancient times. In ancient Greece, the city of Epidavros enjoyed particular fame. People gathered there to improve their health. This could be done in the sanctuary of the god of medicine Asclepius. Near the temple there were baths, gymnastics schools and hotels. In the modern world, the resort industry has covered all countries and continents.

The best resort of Bohemia

European treatment centers are famous all over the world. By the number of tourists arriving in the country to improve their health, the Czech Republic leads. The famous health resort of Karlovy Vary welcomes patients from all five continents. Medical tourists from 70 countries of the world come here annually.

The most famous Czech resort is located in the western part of the historical region of Bohemia. There is a legend that the Roman emperor, who was also part of the Czech king Charles IV, severely injured his leg during the hunt. In the forest, he discovered a source of warm water. In it, the king washed his leg. A terrible wound healed without a trace. Karl was so surprised by the healing properties of water that he ordered the establishment of a resort on this site.

Throughout its existence, the city has known prosperity and decline. He survived floods, fires and wars. A huge advertisement to the resort was made at the time by Peter the Great. He came to Karlovy Vary twice.

A new flowering began in the XVIII century, when significant amounts were allocated by the treasury for the development of the city. More and more guests are coming to the city. And the local bourgeoisie begins to invest in the construction of real estate.

The prosperity of the city continued until World War I. This time is often called the golden age of Karsbad.

A new stage in the development of the city came after the "velvet revolution." Well-established treatments have begun to revive. Karlovy Vary has again become a favorite place of rest and recovery for people from all over the world.

Depending on the disease, the doctor determines what kind of water the patient should consume. For treatment, only 13 sources are used:

  1. "Geyser". Depending on the temperature of the water, it may have a laxative or binding effect. Inside is used in diluted form. Often used for bathtubs. The water is ecologically clean, rises from a depth of 2 thousand meters.
  2. Source of Charles IV. It is believed that it was in him that the emperor washed the wound.
  3. Upper castle. The composition is the same as that of a geyser.
  4. Lower castle. Its temperature is 55.6 degrees.
  5. Serpentine. This water has more carbon dioxide, but less minerals.
  6. Mill. It is the oldest source.
  7. Prince Wenceslas. Initially, mineral salt was extracted from the source.
  8. "Mermaid". Water temperature - 60.2 degrees. In the mid-19th century, doctors received their patients right near this source.
  9. "Libushi". One of the most popular hot springs.
  10. Rocky. Water for him rises through several wells from under the rock.
  11. Garden. This source made its way during the construction of a military sanatorium in 1852.
  12. Glandular. Often used for bathtubs. It has a high content of iron and arsenic.
  13. Market.

Hotel Kolonada

Hotel "Colonnade" in Karlovy Vary is located in the heart of the spa area. It is located on the waterfront, opposite the Mill Colonnade.

Hotel Kolonada Karlovy Vary arose on the basis of the hotels "Patria" and "Otava". Hotels have a rich history. For example, Otava was formerly called the Golden Lion. The first mention of it dates back to 1802. N.V. Gogol stayed in it. The hotel received its current name almost 70 years ago.

Now the Colonnade (hotel in Karlovy Vary) is a modern spa complex. It is located in close proximity to thermal springs. Equipped with the most modern equipment.

The sanatorium Bristol Kolonada 4 * in Karlovy Vary, in the Czech Republic, is one of the treatment centers with the highest quality medical base. All procedures are carried out in the hotel building on three floors. They are interconnected by an elevator.

Hotel "Colonnade" in Karlovy Vary specializes in the treatment of the digestive tract, musculoskeletal system, neurological diseases, as well as pathologies of the cardiovascular system.

Accommodation and infrastructure

Hotel Kolonada 4 * in Karlovy Vary, in the Czech Republic, can offer its guests 86 double and 46 single rooms. For people who value a special attitude and comfort, it is possible to book an apartment. All rooms have a TV, telephone, slippers with bathrobes and a hairdryer. There is also a safe and minibar.

High-quality daily cleaning at the Hotel Colonnade in Karlovy Vary is of particular importance. The staff does everything so that no domestic problems distract guests from wellness and relaxation.

All rooms are not only stylish, but also very comfortable. All of them are equipped with new furniture and modern fixtures. The Kolonada Hotel in Karlovy Vary, Czech Republic, offers magnificent views of the Mill Colonnade and the river.

Guests with pets can bring them to rest with them. All that is necessary for the settlement of a four-legged friend is his veterinary passport and a certificate of international standard about the availability of all necessary vaccinations.

The Hotel Colonnade has a swimming pool, a fitness club, a solarium, two restaurants and a lobby bar. In addition, you can visit beauty salons, several shops or book tours in the travel agency.

Right in the treatment center of the sanatorium, you can take a blood test and make an electrocardiogram. In addition, the price includes an entrance, control and final examination by a doctor.

Spa at Kolonada

In their reviews about the Hotel Colonnade in Karlovy Vary, guests pay special attention to spa treatments. Most are available at an additional cost. These include:

  • different types of massages (feet, classic, aromatic, underwater, hot stones);
  • electrotherapy;
  • mesotherapy;
  • face lifting.

At the Hotel Colonnade, the mesotherapy procedure is carried out without the injection method. With the help of electrical pulses, transdermal administration of hyaluronic acid, peptides and vitamins occurs. The duration of the procedure is 50 minutes.

Face lifting includes a massage that smoothes wrinkles and a vitamin mask based on water from the Geyser spring. Sessions are recommended to take place every other day. In combination with wellness procedures and hydrotherapy, face lifting and mesotherapy give a striking anti-aging effect.

Free of charge in the Spa & Wellness department you can visit:

  • pool;
  • jacuzzi;
  • a sauna.

The size of the pool at the Colonnade Hotel is 10 by 5 meters. It has a counterflow of water. The spa is open daily from 9.00 to 21.00. Between 14.00 and 15.00 for men, the entrance to the pool and sauna is closed. This time is exclusively for female visits.

At the Colonnade Spa you can do all kinds of manicures and pedicures:

Special offers

From time to time, the Hotel Colonnade in Karlovy Vary offers its guests a choice of special packages. You can independently choose the number of necessary medical procedures and the nutrition option.

The most expensive package with the most treatment options is Active Slim. It provides 22 treatments per week. The minimum number of nights is 14. The price includes breakfast only. The price is 139 euros per day.

The Basic Slim package differs from the previous one only in the number of procedures, there are 5 less of them. Only 17 per week. But the price is much lower, only 96 euros.

Guests have the opportunity to choose a package without procedures, only accommodation with breakfast. The price for this type of accommodation is 71 euros. This option is suitable for those patients who are exclusively interested in medical sources. Most of them are open to all guests of the city.
