How to make men's shorts out of jeans yourself?

How to remake jeans? Probably, in the wardrobe of any man there is far from one pair of jeans. There is a couple to go on a visit, to go bowling with friends, to give or repair a car. And the most beloved, carefully preserved for several years. All in holes, splashes of paint, shabby ... But it is precisely it that can never be thrown away. It is impossible to wear, and the slightest attempt to free up space in the closet causes a storm of emotions. In this situation, there is only one way out - to modify this thing! How to make men's shorts out of jeans ? It's simple, the main thing - warn your husband so as not to cause him a nervous breakdown!

So, how to make men's shorts out of jeans?



In order to turn this miserable semblance of a wardrobe item into a thing that can be worn for some time, you will need patience, scissors, threads, and then on the circumstances ... First, try to remove as much as possible all dirt, greasy stains, traces from fuel oil and paint. If you manage to get your husband to put on jeans and measure the future length of the shorts on it - great. However, the hope that the average man will spin in front of a mirror is extremely small. In this case, in order not to be mistaken, and instead of gratitude not to hear reproaches, it is worth taking shorts from his wardrobe and, based on the sample, cut off his trousers. Do not forget to leave a small margin, literally four to five centimeters. This will not only hem, but also adjust the length of the finished product.

How to make men's shorts from jeans: holes





After the legs have been cut off, the next stage begins. This is no less crucial moment. How to eliminate the natural ventilation created by numerous holes and tears on the fabric? If the holes are small and there are only a couple of pieces, then you can cover them with some neutral applique with the image of a brand or, for example, a ball. If the husband is an opponent of labels and jewelry, just leave the hole, after giving it a stylized look. In this case, the spots of paint will also look more organically. In extreme cases, you can put a patch of the same fabric, leaving its edges shaggy or carefully tucked and several times stitched diagonally. You almost managed to make men's shorts from jeans. Next is the last stage of work.

How to make men's shorts from jeans: edge treatment

Some men prefer well-trimmed shorts. Others, on the contrary, recognize only a carelessly cut bottom, from which the bright warp threads are visible. The edge should be hemmed very carefully, having previously processed it with an overlock.





After the jeans are hemmed, you can sew a stitch over the fold line. It all depends on the preferences and style of clothing. In this case, it is useless to try to convince or dissuade a man. It is better to immediately agree and do as he wants. Satisfied, perhaps he will allow some other old things to be modernized, thereby freeing up space on the cabinet shelf.
