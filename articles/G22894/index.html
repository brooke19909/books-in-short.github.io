What is a virtual card?





So, in general, virtual cards of the Visa or Master Card payment system are special types of cards intended for making payments for purchases or making other payments via the Internet. Typically, such cards do not have tangible media, respectively, with their help you can not pay in a regular store or get money at an ATM.

Why is Yandex.Money virtual bank card best suited for online payments? Its main advantage is an increased level of security when making payments, as well as an additional guarantee that your main card will not suffer from the disappearance of money in an unknown direction.

You've probably already heard about phishing, which is a separate type of plastic card fraud when making payments via the Internet. What dangers may lie in wait for a simple card holder? For example, you have decided to purchase goods through an online store and are going to pay for the purchase with a simple card. To confirm the payment, you will have to indicate the card number in full, and in most situations also a special three-digit security code. It is understandable that, theoretically, the store can use the information received for its own purposes, and an attacker who intercepts the data will be able to make a payment for a purchase in another online store.





If you use Yandex.Money, a virtual MasterCard or Visa card, the risks are minimized. First of all, most of these cards have a limit and a limited duration. Some cards in general can be used only once, due to which, even with the knowledge of the number and secret code, they cannot be reused. Other virtual cards with the possibility of reusable use can last for several months, but they have a limited cash limit, that is, attackers, even if they wish, will not cause you significant damage.

Yandex.Money is a virtual card (Visa or MasterCard), which can be used only once. For example, you need to pay something - created a card, set a certain limit for payments, paid for the purchase.

When issuing virtual cards, the financial aspect is also important, that is, they are not issued for free, so for each specific case, it should be compared what will be more profitable and safe, to constantly purchase a new card or buy a reusable one.
