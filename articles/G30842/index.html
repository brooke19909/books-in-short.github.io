The first way is traditional

The first thing you need to remember: the ‚Äústep of step‚Äù technique is crocheted from left to right. This is important, because otherwise you will get the usual single crochet. In principle, it is practically no different from the above method. The only thing you should pay attention to is the number of loops on the hook. Instead of introducing the hook under the arcs of the previous row once, we perform this procedure, for example, three times. You can do more, it all depends on what thickness and splendor you need to finish.



Such a magnificent crochet step, made with a crochet, is most suitable for warm clothes: winter hats, sweaters and so on.
