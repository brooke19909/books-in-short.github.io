The most popular cheap hotels in Minsk

Minsk is a city of cozy parks and huge squares. It does not look like most other capitals of the world, because there is no such fuss and noise, there is no brilliance inherent in many large cities of Europe. It is a calm city for the soul. Many who have been here want to return to this place to take a walk in this slightly slow, yet incredibly romantic city.



Minsk: hotels, prices

Inexpensive, you can stay in small cozy hotels (from 1,500 rubles per day) and in hostels (from 500 rubles per day). For those who are not accustomed to save on comfort and have enough money, there are also luxurious 4-5-star hotels, but accommodation in them, of course, is much more expensive (from 4,500 rubles per day). Both luxury and affordable hotels in Minsk are always happy to welcome their guests. In this place there is an opportunity to find expensive hotels, but in this article we will consider budget options for accommodation.

Hotel Sputnik

Considering inexpensive hotels in Minsk, you should immediately highlight the "Sputnik". This hotel has a cozy restaurant where you can order various dishes of European and Belarusian cuisine. In the cozy bar you can chat with neighbors and enjoy a glass of your favorite drink.



A great place to gain strength and relax after a hard day is the sauna. If necessary, you can also use the laundry services. The hospitable staff will help you with pleasure to decide on the selection of excursions, as well as provide tourist information you need. The front desk is open around the clock.

Cost: from 2499 rubles per day.

MinskForMe Apartments

These apartments are located in the city center at various addresses. These are cheap hotels in Minsk, located near the main attractions. You can reach Independence Square in 15 minutes by public transport.



MinskForMe is a great choice for a stay in the city center. There is a modern kitchen, which is equipped with everything necessary for self-cooking. In addition, in the vicinity of these apartments there are a huge number of cafes, restaurants and bars.

If you are considering these inexpensive hotels in Minsk for your stay, it is worth noting that here you can order a transfer for an additional fee. Wi-Fi access is provided here, so you can stay in regular touch with your home and plan your future trip. The apartment staff will help you determine the selection of excursions, as well as provide the necessary tourist information.

Cost: from 2499 rubles per day.

Hotel East Time

If you are interested in inexpensive Minsk hotels in the city center, then pay attention to the East Time Hotel.



The restaurant in the restaurant serves European and Belarusian dishes. Breakfast is served here in the mornings and you can relax with a drink in the cozy bar.

Friendly staff will help you organize excursions and book tickets. A shuttle service to the airport or train station can be arranged for you at an additional cost.

Free internet is available in the lobby. This hotel also has parking.

Cost: from 1899 rubles per day.

Central Minsk Apartments

Today it’s not difficult to find cheap hotels in Minsk in the center. Among them can be distinguished and apartments "Central Minsk". The hotels are located in the center of Minsk at various addresses, not far from the Yakub Kolas Square and Oktyabrskaya stations. Near the apartments are parks named after Gorky and them. Yanki Kupala, Trinity Suburb, concert hall of the Philharmonic and the Palace of the Republic. Nearby are the shopping centers GUM, TSUM, Komarovsky central market, in addition, restaurants where you can taste the cuisines of different countries. Independence Square is a 10-minute walk away.



At your service in all apartments is a comfortable kitchen, equipped with everything you need for self-cooking - as pleasant and simple as possible. The apartments have a balcony - it offers stunning views of the streets of the Belarusian capital.

Cost: from 1599 rubles per day.

Apartments FlatsBy

When looking for cheap hotels in Minsk in the center, you can stop at FlatsBy Apartments. They are located 2 steps from various bus stops, not far from the stations “Pervomaiskaya”, “Victory Square”, “Academy of Sciences” or “Nemiga”. The journey from the airport takes about 15 minutes. The main attractions are about 20 minutes from the apartments.



After a busy day, guests of the apartment can relax in the very comfortable living room while watching a fascinating TV program. To keep in touch with relatives and friends, use Wi-Fi access. For your convenience, the apartment includes free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Cost: from 1899 rubles per day.
