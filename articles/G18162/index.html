Housing stock

The rooms in this hotel guests were quite satisfied. According to them, all the apartments here are newly renovated, spacious, bright and stylishly decorated. Our compatriots liked that they have everything that you may need, from a minibar and hairdryer to a set for making tea and coffee, bath accessories, a bathrobe, slippers and even an umbrella. By the way, some rooms also have a kitchen with a large fridge, microwave and full kitchenware. Very pleased with the tourists good insulation in the apartment. According to them, of course, if you come across too noisy neighbors, you will still hear them, but basically no noise will interfere with your vacation. Some discontent was expressed by the guests, whose windows overlooked the construction site. But the fact is that on the territory of The Beach Heights Resort 4 * (Phuket, Kata Beach), another building is being built. Its construction will be completed soon, so that all guests will enjoy a pleasant view from the room. As for the pluses, many travelers were satisfied with the fact that both in the apartments and throughout the hotel, it is possible to use free wireless Internet. Wi-Fi password is issued upon arrival separately for each of your devices.



Cleaning

Judging by the comments of our compatriots, they did not have any complaints about the work of the maids at Beach Heights Resort. On the contrary, according to Russian tourists, the cleaning of the rooms was carried out very carefully. The maids also changed bed and linen and towels several times a week and replenished personal hygiene supplies in the bathroom as they were spent.

Check in, departure

Tourists vacationing at this hotel warn that here they strictly follow the rules of the estimated time. So, if you arrive at the hotel in the morning, the room keys will be issued to you no earlier than 14:00. If you wish, you can wait for check-in in the lobby (here you will be offered soft drinks) or leave things in the luggage room and go to the pool or for a walk in the vicinity. After two in the afternoon, a hotel employee will come to you, who will lead you to your room and bring your things. Also note that when you check in you will be asked to make a deposit of $ 100. Upon discharge from the hotel, this amount will be returned to you. As for the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon.



Territory, infrastructure

According to our compatriots, The Beach Heights Resort 4 * hotel (Phuket, Thailand) has a small, but very thoughtfully planned territory. Here is a separate building reception, two residential buildings, between which is a swimming pool, restaurant, bar and spa. An interesting feature of the hotel is the fact that the rooms located on the ground floors have access to the pool instead of a balcony. There are also several jacuzzis. The hotel also has a children's playroom and a shallow pool for toddlers.



Nutrition

The cost of living in this hotel includes breakfast only. They are held in buffet style. Most of our compatriots, judging by their reviews, were quite satisfied with the breakfasts. So, guests note that in the mornings you can eat here with scrambled eggs, omelettes, boiled eggs, soup, which includes noodles, vegetables and chicken or meat, potatoes, bacon, sausages, fresh vegetables, rice or noodles in spicy meat sauce, sausage , cereals, pastries, fresh fruits, juices, milk, tea and coffee. The choice of dishes every day is unchanged, but there is always something to choose from. So you can always diversify your breakfasts. Also a big plus, according to many tourists, is the fact that in the morning you can eat from 6:30 to 10:30. Therefore, you are unlikely to oversleep breakfast. In addition, as foods and dishes run out, cooks replenish their supplies. Therefore, even if you come to the end of breakfast, you can taste the same as those who visited the restaurant early in the morning.

Also, tourists have the opportunity to dine at the hotel for an additional fee. An evening meal is also served in buffet style, which serves local cuisine. However, most of the guests preferred to eat outside the hotel during the day. According to them, in the vicinity of The Beach Heights Resort 4 * (Phuket, Kata Beach) there are many cafes and restaurants where you can have an inexpensive and very tasty meal. In addition, many tourists gladly went to the local market, where you can buy fresh seafood, which will be prepared here with you. Also, travelers were happy to buy food at mobile stalls.



Sea

As our compatriots note, the closest beach to the hotel in question is Kata Beach. You can walk to it in just 5-7 minutes at a leisurely pace. The road runs through the city streets. According to seasoned travelers, Kata Beach is great for families with children. After all, there is fine clean sand and a fairly calm sea. There are cafes and bars on the beach where you can enjoy a drink and a snack. As for beach towels, they can be taken on receipt at the reception at Beach Heights Resort. After returning to the hotel, they should be returned.

Also, tourists enjoyed visiting other beaches. So, they advise to definitely go to Patong and Kata Noi. They can be reached on foot (about 30 minutes) or reached by tuk-tuk.
