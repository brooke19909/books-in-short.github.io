Chebarkul hotels: rating of the best, addresses, choice of room, convenience of booking, quality of service, additional services and reviews of visitors and customers

In the Southern Urals, a two-hour drive from Chelyabinsk, the city of Chebarkul is located. This place has a rich history, unique nature, the destinies of great people touched it, and only recently it became famous throughout the world for a meteorite falling in the lake of the same name. Hotels in Chebarkul are in demand among numerous visitors to the city. Each hotel provides reservation services via the Internet, in the summer season you need to take care of the rooms for the dates of your trip or vacation in advance.

The city is located on the slopes of the Ilmensky ridge. Pine and mixed forests, mushrooms, berries, meadows surrounded with birch groves, a silver placer of lakes, radon springs make it a great place to relax and restore health. Many hotels in Chebarkul are located on the territory of sanatorium complexes, preserved from Soviet times.

The history of Chebarkul and famous people

As a Cossack fortress to protect the road to Orenburg, Chebarkul was founded in the 18th century with the consent of the Bashkir Khan Taymas Shaimov, who was the owner of this land. During the Pugachevsky uprising, after an unsuccessful attempt to seize Chelyabinsk, the Pugachevs returned to Chebarkul, captured it, hanged several Cossacks and officers, this place is now one of the sights of the city.

White clay was mined in the Misyash lake of the Chebarkul fortress, which was delivered to St. Petersburg at the famous Imperial Porcelain Factory.

The Chebarkul district is the birthplace of the brilliant director S. A. Gerasimov, who in his films sang the beauty of the Urals, reproduced the atmosphere of Cossack houses, and wrote out characters from fellow countrymen.

The famous hockey player Valery Kharlamov trained under the leadership of the outstanding Vladimir Alfer in the Chebarkulsky Zvezda.

In the early 2000s, the majestic Transfiguration Cathedral was restored in the city, in which a large number of people gather on big holidays.

The city has many factories and combines, objects of the military-industrial complex.

Another reason to visit Chebarkul is the famous Malkovo Legends festival, which takes place in July on the remains of an 18th-century redoubt, between the southeastern coastline of Lake Chebarkul and the western outskirts of the village of Malkovo.

The fall of the meteorite, fragments of which can be seen in the local museum, glorified the city throughout the world.

Many come to Chebarkul on business trips to visit interesting tourist places, relax on magnificent lakes.

The resort complex "Utes"

The family resort "Utes" is located next to the lakes B. Kisegach and M. Terenkul, occupies 8.5 hectares of forest area. To get to the hotel, you need to travel 10 minutes by taxi from the Chebarkul railway station. On the forest territory there are 8 two-story and three-story buildings with 138 rooms, numerous barbecue areas, gazebos, a rotunda, a sports ground. From 12:00 to 24:00 the restaurant "Jay".

Rooms are provided:

  • family comfort with two rooms, a double bed and a seating area;
  • single and double standards
  • Suite in the style of "Provence";
  • Suite in the style of "Oriental Tale";
  • two-level suite;
  • Apartments 87 m 2.

The rooms have televisions, refrigerators, kettles, showers, hair dryers, and towels are provided for each guest.

When booking rooms, breakfast is a gift to each client. The restaurant organizes lunches and dinners according to the "buffet" system, children under 5 years old receive meals free of charge, from 5 to 12 years old with a 50% discount.

The restaurant has a children's area with bright furniture, a playground. There is a salt cave, which is recommended to visit for the prevention of respiratory diseases. Utes has a spa with a heated outdoor pool, a Fresh bathtub, a hydromassage, a shower alley, a hammam, and a Finnish sauna. The hotel has a conference room.

According to tourists' reviews, the resort staff has something else to work on, but repairs are being gradually carried out and the services of the spa, which is very popular with guests, are developing. Nevertheless, the quality of service is at its best, the staff is friendly and always ready to help the guest.

Hotel on Spruce Lake

The Hotel on Elovoy is located 4.5 meters from the center of Chebarkul in a westerly direction on the lake in a forest zone. On the territory there is Wi-Fi, free parking, an outdoor pool, a steam room, a restaurant. Children can spend time in the games room. The cost of rest is about 3,500 rubles. per day, kids up to five years stay free.

There are 14 rooms in the building. The entrance to the lake is from the pontoon, so swimming with young children is not very convenient. 30 km away is the Sun Valley ski lift.

Tourists note good food and service, cleanliness in the rooms, as well as the beautiful nature surrounding the hotel in Chebarkul.
