"Karpov Pond" (Chelyabinsk): characteristics of the housing estate

The village of "Karpov Pond" (Chelyabinsk) is not in vain received that name - it is located on the shores of the reservoir of the same name. The settlement is located in the Sosnovsky district near Chelyabinsk.

Arrangement of communication networks

The Karpov Prud settlement (Chelyabinsk) is fully powered, there is a backup generator that provides uninterrupted power supply throughout the village. Water and gas pipelines. A well was drilled at each site for the extraction of drinking water. Centralized sewerage, individual heating.

Features of the territory

Carp pond - a pond intended for watering breeding sites. The name of the lake came from the fact that the pond was stocked up - it was launched into the reservoir of carp. The waters are clean, and the banks are densely overgrown with a plant layer of organic matter. The pond is surrounded on all sides by buildings with the exception of the southern coast - this is the territory of the garden community. The village of "Karpov Pond" (Chelyabinsk) is located on the northern shores of the lake.

In the summertime, a considerable number of vacationers gather off the coast of the reservoir. "Karpov Pond" (Chelyabinsk) is a resort area, because real estate here, despite the considerable price, is very popular. They rarely fish here, mainly they come to relax in nature: it is quiet, calm, and the environment is favorable.
