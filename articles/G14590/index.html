Hotel Grand Resort Hurghada 5 * - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Hurghada is one of the most popular resort areas in Egypt. And today the Grand Resort Hurghada 5 hotel is in great demand among tourists. This place provides its guests not only with comfort and homeliness, but also with a lot of entertainment and, of course, professional service.

Hotel Location





Hurghada is a large resort city that hosts thousands of tourists from all over Europe and Asia every year. It is here, practically on the very shore of the Red Sea, that the Grand Resort Hurghada 5 hotel is located. By the way, this area, called the Promenade, is considered one of the most beautiful and lively areas of the city - markets, shops, shopping centers and catering establishments are located around the hotel complex . The international airport, as well as the center of Hurghada, is ten kilometers from the hotel.

The beach is approximately 350 meters away. You can get to it by going through the courtyard of a neighboring hotel called Grand Plaza Resort Hurghada 5. By the way, this hotel belongs to the same chain of hotel complexes.

The Grand Resort Red Sea Hotels Hotel Chain Summary

Hurghada 5 belongs to the well-known chain of hotels located throughout the country. Several of them are located in Hurghada. In addition, the hotel complexes of this line are available in Sharm el-Sheikh, Makadi and other large resort cities of Egypt.

In fact, these hotels are famous for their first-class service and traditional oriental atmosphere, which so attracts tourists. Their undoubted advantage is the presence of a large territory surrounded by greenery. And today, the Grand Resort Red Sea Hotels Hurghada 5 is in great demand among tourists. Guest reviews indicate that this hotel is an ideal option for a fun youthful, relaxing family or eventful sightseeing vacation.

Infrastructure and description of the hotel area

On a relatively large territory of the hotel there are several residential buildings, which are multi-storey buildings built in the traditional Egyptian style. In the courtyard there are swimming pools, bars, terraces with tables, as well as cozy gazebos where you can have a great time enjoying the peace and sea air. A picturesque garden full of flowering exotic plants and palm trees gives the hotel complex an incomparable charm.

What accommodation conditions does the hotel offer?





What conditions of accommodation does the Grand Resort Hurghada 5 offer? Reviews in 2014 confirm that the rooms here are really spacious, bright and comfortable. The hotel has 828 rooms of the following categories:

  • 474 Deluxe rooms, in which no more than 2 adults can live at a time;
  • 200 Junior Suites for 2-4 people;
  • 93 Standard rooms for 1-2 tourists.

Regardless of the category, the rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay. The spacious bright rooms are decorated in a traditional oriental style - carved furniture, a canopy above the bed , etc. Almost every room has its own balcony (or terrace on the ground floor), which overlooks the pool or garden.

In the bathroom, you will find a spacious shower, washbasin, as well as branded hotel cosmetics and a set of clean snow-white towels that are changed every day.

Of course, the rooms are equipped with modern air conditioning. On TV you can watch satellite channels (there are several Russian). Valuables and documents can be stored in a small safe. Your phone has a direct dial feature. There is also a minibar with a cooling system for storing drinks.

It is worth noting that the rooms are cleaned every day, so there is no need to complain about cleanliness. Bed linen is changed three times a week.

Meal plan for guests





Hotel Grand Resort Hurghada 5 offers its guests an all-inclusive system. The hotel has two large restaurants - Mahara and Marakesh. This is where breakfast and dinner are served for travelers. The hotel menu includes cold snacks, meat, fish, seafood, fresh pastries, desserts and fruits. In fine weather, lunch is served on the hotel beach - guests have the opportunity to enjoy the beautiful scenery and the soft sea breeze during meals.

In addition, the hotel has two a la carte restaurants , which must be booked in advance - Nigma and Mediterraneo. Guests can also enjoy light snacks and local drinks in the bars located in the lobby and by the pool. There is also a beautiful pub, decorated in a traditional oriental style, where you can enjoy national drinks and some traditional Egyptian dishes.

If you did not like the food at the hotel, for a modest fee you can eat in one of the city cafes - here everyone will find something to their taste.

Beach and water recreation in the city





As already mentioned, the Grand Resort Hurghada 5 hotel has its own stretch of coast - the beach is 350 meters from the residential buildings. On the shore there are sun loungers with comfortable mattresses, as well as bright umbrellas that perfectly protect you from the sun. By the way, they can be used for free. The entrance to the sea is wide, convenient and safe, and the coastal aground makes this place suitable for bathing children. There is a rescue tower on the shore, where several hotel staff are constantly on duty. In addition, here you can dine or enjoy delicious exotic cocktails offered by the beach bar.

If bathing and sunbathing are not enough for you, then there is always the opportunity to try out more active types of recreation. For example, on a volleyball court, competitions are constantly held between hotel residents. There is also a diving school, whose services are paid separately - here beginners are offered appropriate training, as well as the rental of necessary equipment. Naturally, you will have the opportunity to surf, go boating or water skiing. In a word, nobody will be bored here.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?





In fact, parents with children quite often stay at the Grand Resort Hurghada 5. After all, this hotel complex provides the most comfortable and fun family vacation. For example, if necessary, a cradle or a rollaway bed for an older child will be delivered to the room. By the way, children under twelve years of age can stay at the hotel with their parents for free.

The restaurant menu has a lot of dishes that will appeal to the child. Naturally, the waiters will certainly offer the baby a highchair. And baby food and other items can be bought in one of the stores located on the territory of the resort center.

As for entertainment, here children do not have to be bored. For example, they can have a great time on the territory of the playground with slides, labyrinths, trampolines and swings. Here the children will have the opportunity to get to know each other and arrange real water competitions under the supervision of rescuers. Additional service for tourists

Many travelers are interested in questions about what additional services the Grand Resort Hurghada 5 hotel offers. Hurghada is a well-known resort city that has been engaged in the tourism business for decades. Naturally, all the hotels here, especially five-star ones, offer their customers everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay.

Of course, the airport transfer is provided by the administration of the Grand Resort Hurghada 5. The tour operator will make sure that you are accommodated in a clean, spacious room. Also, an employee of a travel company will introduce you to the infrastructure of the hotel complex.

For guests there is a laundry service, the services of which are paid separately. In addition, there is a small parking lot, and you can rent a car, bike or motorcycle in the city. There is free internet access in the lobby and other public areas.

The hotel also has a currency exchange office. There are several shops where, at very reasonable prices, you can buy essentials, as well as souvenirs and trinkets for yourself and loved ones. If health problems arise, administrators will immediately call you a qualified doctor.

Entertainment and sport for vacationers at the hotel





Of course, there are plenty of ways to have a good time - this is what the Grand Resort Hurghada 5 provides its guests with. The photo shows that the entire courtyard is occupied by figured pools. There are two outdoor pools for adults, two for bathing children. There are plenty of umbrellas and sun loungers around. By the way, there is another indoor pool with a heating system, which hotel guests can use in cold weather or in the evening.

On the other hand, the resort complex offers some wellness treatments. For example, guests can always relax in the indoor or outdoor jacuzzi. There is also a sauna and Turkish bath, decorated in the best oriental traditions. In addition, massage rooms are located at the hotel - you can have a great time and relieve fatigue at the hands of experienced professionals. By the way, on the territory of the neighboring hotel (Grand Plaza Resort) there is a large SPA center that offers a lot of wellness and anti-aging treatments - the guests of this hotel can also use the center's services for a fee. And women will certainly be pleased with the beauty salon and hairdresser, where experienced stylists work.

If you are a fan of a healthy lifestyle and always strive to keep fit, a fitness center with a well-equipped gym is ready to offer its services - if necessary, you can use the services of a trainer.

The hotel also has table tennis tables. You can have a great time with old friends or new acquaintances in the billiards room. The hotel also offers customers to visit the local TV lounge. For a small fee, you will have access to a large tennis court - here you can take a few lessons, rent the necessary equipment. In the evening, the court is lit. There is also a sports field for volleyball and futsal.

Throughout the day, guests are entertained by a team of talented and groovy animators. Great performances are regularly held here, including performances by local groups, oriental dances, vibrant shows, karaoke nights, various competitions for guests, etc.

Of course, there are many other events that the Grand Resort Hurghada 5 hotel can offer. Hurghada is an ancient city rich in secrets, riddles and interesting sights. And on site you will be helped to choose a suitable tour. Most tourists book a trip to the Luxor Temple first. This is the greatest architectural structure of antiquity will allow you to touch for a moment the mysterious history of the great ancient Egyptian state.

No less interesting can be a trip to one of the most famous Christian buildings in Egypt - St. Paul's Monastery, which was built in the fifth century AD and has been well preserved to this day. Tourists are often offered to spend a day on the Filet Peninsula and visit the monumental temple complex built during the Ptolemaic dynasty - by the way, the temple is considered one of the best-preserved architectural monuments.

It is worth visiting the temple of Abydos, which was supposedly built in the eighth century BC. In ancient times, such temple complexes were most revered by the Egyptians, as these were not only the places of worship of Osiris, but also the doors to the other world. If you are interested in more modern attractions, then you should go to the Museum of Marine Biology - here you can see firsthand the representatives of the local marine fauna and flora. By the way, this place is also a research station.
