Iriklinskoe reservoir - rest in the Orenburg region

In the 1950s, the Orenburg region felt a sharp need for a constant water supply and sanitation in the eastern and central parts of the Southern Urals. In this regard, in 1958 it was begun, and in 1966 the construction of the largest reservoir within the Urals, located at an altitude of 245 m above sea level, was completed. By the way, the Iriklinskoe reservoir was built on the river of the same name. Its length is 73 km, and the length of the coastline is approximately 415 km. The average depth is 12.5 m, the greatest - 40 m. Below is a map of the Iriklinsky reservoir, which will help tourists get to the reservoir.



A few words about the reservoir

The appearance of the reservoir is accompanied by many legends that the locals will willingly tell you. They say the name of this reservoir in translation means "heavenly place." Indeed, the time spent here is similar to the time spent in paradise - clean air, clear water, wonderful views ...

A huge deep lake with rocky shores and many bays - this is the Iriklinskoe reservoir. Rest in these places is remembered for a long time. One who once saw this splendor will remain impressed with him forever. So say the locals and travelers, there is no end to it in all seasons.

Fishing

About 40 species of fish live in the reservoir, the diversity of which will also impress the experienced fisherman: burbot, bream, roach, pike, whitefish, trout, silver carp, carp, common carp, sterlet ... And this is not the whole list! It is worth noting that earlier the Iriklinskoe reservoir (recreation with a fishing rod in these places is quite popular) was famous for populations of predatory fish species. Who knows, maybe they will meet a lucky fisherman.

The huge diversity of the underwater world allows fishing companies to exist successfully, of which there are several: the Volna fishing farm, the state fish breeding nursery, the fishery community and the state-owned fish processing enterprise.

Vegetable world

Also in this area you can find many small islands, peninsulas, lagoons, capes, braids and bays. Flora consists mainly of poplar, alder, elm, juniper, birch and aspen. Artificial gardening is actively practiced here, new trees and shrubs are constantly planted.

In the summer, the Iriklinskoe reservoir (the most sought after holiday this season) acquires an emerald green hue. This transformation is due to the blooming of water. And the reason for this phenomenon is the high content of various nutrients in it . No wonder this place is called the pearl of the Orenburg region!



Infrastructure

In these parts the infrastructure is very developed. Numerous houses equipped with everything necessary, clean beaches, in a word, the most comfortable conditions await tourists coming to the Iriklinskoe reservoir. Recreation facilities, which are located in close proximity to the water, are the famous "Seagull" and "Cherry Slides". They are suitable for lovers of thoughtful relaxation. There is a cafe-bar with karaoke on site. Equipped with a dance floor. For lovers of sports, there is a volleyball and soccer field. Also at the recreation centers you can use the barbecue and grill.

And for those who do not want to enjoy the benefits of civilization, preferring to them unity with nature, there is the opportunity to set up a camp.

It is not surprising that the place is constantly filled with tourists and nature lovers. Also, for lovers of yacht sports there is the opportunity to visit a local club, also located in the pond. If you choose the Iriklinskoe reservoir, rest, you can be 100% sure that it will be fun and unforgettable.



The creation of this reservoir made it possible to prevent and reduce the effects of floods in the Orenburg region, significantly increased the chances of developing fishing and improved the ecological situation in the area.
