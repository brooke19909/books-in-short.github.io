Car radio JVC KW-AV51: description, specifications, instructions

One of them is the JVC KW-AV51, which has a number of advantages and allows you to turn part of the car panel into a real multimedia center. A large number of connectors for connecting additional devices expands its capabilities and leaves prospects for improving sound and selecting the appropriate source for playing music files. In order to understand its advantages and disadvantages, you should first familiarize yourself with the basic functionality, and then analyze user reviews.

Playing video files

The radio supports the playback of most modern and outdated formats, so that drivers have the opportunity to watch their favorite movies during parking. In addition, there is a connector on the rear panel that allows you to install and connect an additional monitor for passengers. Key Features

The main task of any radio, regardless of its functionality, is to play music. It is the quality of the sound played that can be called the criterion by which the final selection is made. The radio in question, according to reviews about it, received a sound path that allows you to produce surround, deep sound. The presence of an equalizer and a low-frequency amplification system made it possible to connect even a budget or stock speaker system to it and tune it in such a way that familiar melodies could please your hearing.

The basic characteristics of the JVC KW-AV51 are about the same as other devices of this type. The built-in amplifier supports up to 4 speakers with a power of 50 watts each. If you need deep bass, you can connect a subwoofer with its own amplifier and capacitor. For this, a linear output is provided on the rear panel.

Positive and negative sides of the model

If we analyze in detail the reviews left by the drivers about this model, we can highlight its main pros and cons. Among the positive aspects, the following are most often noted:

  • The ability to expand functionality due to numerous additional inputs and outputs.
  • A removable panel that allows you to protect the car from illegal entry in order to seize the radio.
  • A nice and clear menu through which the initial setup. Even without instructions, it can be configured intuitively, since all parameters are written in simple language.
  • Simple connection. JVC KW-AV51 can be mounted by anyone who reads the instructions, and all wires of the proprietary connector are signed in accordance with their purpose.
  • The ability to fine-tune sound quality to the existing speaker system.
  • A wide selection of audio sources, ranging from classic radio to various portable devices with wired and wireless connections.

Among the minuses of the JVC KW-AV51 model, one can single out a slightly slow switching between menu items and folders on external media, as well as a rather loud fan designed to cool components. The rest of the drivers did not find critical problems and are happy with their purchase.
