Digma S675 e-book: review, specifications, instructions and reviews

In 2014, the notorious company Digma announced the release of a new line of e-books. It consisted of three gadgets. Digma S675 is the youngest model in the series. Want to know more about this device, its advantages and disadvantages? Be sure to read this article!





Appearance

The first thing that catches your eye is the design of the device. Digma experts decided to follow the classics. The device looks very restrained and stylish. The e-book is sold in two colors: black and silver. The gadget's corners are rounded. The case is made of high quality material. Thanks to this, the device is pleasant to the touch. He does not slip in his hands. With all this, the e-book does not collect fingerprints and dust. Various backlashes, creaks and gaps were not noticed. The body fits very tightly. In terms of design, the Digma S675 is a universal device. The e-book looks great with both everyday and business clothes.

Display

The Digma S675 e-book boasts an E-Ink Pearl HD screen with a resolution of 1024 by 758 pixels. The rate is quite high. And this is more than enough to read fiction. Even PDF documents that are incredibly demanding on the screen are readable. Perhaps one of the main advantages of the device is the presence of a backlight. Thanks to this, you can read books even in the dark.

In addition, the presence of a touch screen pleases. Using it is very simple to use the device. Also pleased with the speed of updating pages. At the same time, dark stripes do not slip on the screen, as it was in the E625 model. It can not but rejoice in the high quality of the text. Characters and letters are not pixelated. The background color for the display is light gray. The text on it is very clearly visible.





Main screen

The main screen is made at a decent level. When the device is in standby mode, then it displays all the information the user needs. At the top of the display, you can see the current time and date. In the upper right corner is a battery indicator. Most of the screen space is occupied by two zones.

In the area called "The Last Book" is the cover of the last work that the user opened. Next to the cover you can see a brief informational summary about the book: title, author, number of pages and volume of reading. In the second zone there are 8 main menu items. This includes sections such as Recent Books, Photos, Notepad, Books, Explorer, Music, Preferences, and More. Switching on the main screen is from the touch.

Library

Digma S675 supports all popular formats like FB2, ERUP, MOBI, etc. The library is represented by a directory in which the user's books are included. One of its most important functions is sorting. With this, the Digma S675 is all right. Sorting can be done by author, title of work, date of addition, etc. Also, the library has an option with which you can turn on the music player. Upon transition, the last listened song or the first song in the list is automatically played.





A photo

Also, using Digma S675, you can view various files in graphic format. The device supports JPG, GIF, BMP and PNG. Unlike competitors' gadgets, the device reads a less popular format called TIFF. The display quality of graphic files is at an acceptable level for an e-book. Photos, like books, can be sorted by various criteria.

Music

In addition, the Digma S675 Black even supports music tracks. Formats are quite standard: MP3, WMA, OGG, WAV. The music menu interface is pretty minimalistic. And there is nothing superfluous in it. When playing an audio file, a window can be seen at the bottom of the screen. It indicates the name of the composition, the countdown. Management in this mode is carried out using the sensor. Thanks to him, you can flip through songs, pause them and turn them on again, adjust the volume. In addition, you can rewind the audio file at the right time. It just can not but rejoice.





Previous models from Digma suffered from a bug that is quite common in Chinese-made devices. The essence of the flaw was as follows: if the user tries to start a composition whose name contains problems or Cyrillic, then nothing will work out for him - the device will either freeze or will be thrown to the initial screen. Fortunately, experts sorted out this problem. And Digma S675 is deprived of such a bug.

Notes

Digma S675 has a pretty interesting feature. An e-book can be used to create notes. This is done using a section called Notepad. It is worth recognizing that in previous devices this function was greatly underdeveloped. Nevertheless, in the Digma S675 it still brought to mind. Thanks to the touch screen, taking notes is very simple. Such an action does not take much time.

Digma s675 reviews

Feedback on this device is extremely positive. First of all, customers are pleased with the high ergonomics of the gadget, stylish design, high quality playback of various formats, multifunctionality, etc. In addition, one of the main advantages of this device is the price. The cost of Digma S675 is a cut lower than that of competitors with similar characteristics.

The lack of an official peripheral is the main drawback of the Digma S675. It is incredibly difficult to find a cover that would fit the device. After all, Digma is a Chinese manufacturer that does not produce accessories for its devices in our country. And to get the same case, you have to carefully search the vast expanses of the World Wide Web.
