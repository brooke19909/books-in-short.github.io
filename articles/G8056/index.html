Mari Evans Apartments 3 * (Crete, Greece): description, service, reviews

Crete is the largest island in Greece and one of the most popular tourist destinations in this country. Travelers will not be bored here, because they have golden sandy beaches and plenty of water activities. Adherents of excursion tourism are unlikely to refuse to visit the island, which, according to legend, became the birthplace of Zeus and the habitat of the Minotaur.





Those who decide to fully enjoy the natural beauty of this paradise, it is best to choose small hotels, for example, Mari Evans Apartments. There is no influx of tourists, but there is something to see.

General information about Mari Evans Apartments

This hotel is interesting for several features. Hotel Mari Evans Apartments (3 stars) is a small, homely hotel. It has only 10 rooms, so guests can enjoy their holidays as much as possible and not suffer from constant fuss and noise.

Convenient location, reasonable prices and high quality service distinguishes this hotel from many others on the island. The building is made in the spirit of traditional Cretan buildings, and the territory is rich in green spaces.

Hotel location

Mari Evans Apartments is located in one of the most beautiful tourist areas of Crete - in the resort area of ​​Gouves. The city of Heraklion, which has the Nikos Kazantzakis International Airport, is 17 km from the hotel.

You can get to the city by choosing air travel from many major European cities. There is a direct flight Moscow-Heraklion. From the airport, the hotel can be reached by public transport or taxi.





Hotel Facilities

The attendants make a lot of efforts to make guests feel at the hotel cozy and comfortable. Among the services marked:

  • reception (it works around the clock);
  • laundry service;
  • housekeeping;
  • linen change.

In addition, the entire hotel staff is very courteous and polite.

Hotel Accommodation Mari Evans Apartments

This 3-star hotel is a small building with several floors. The administration can offer travelers 10 rooms. Depending on the area and comfort level, the rooms are divided into several types.

  • Single studio. This studio is for single occupancy.
  • Studio 2 pax / Studio 3 pax. Accommodation in a single room with double and triple occupancy.
  • Apartament. This room has a living room and bedroom. The apartment can accommodate no more than 4 people.




Room Amenities

The hotel has a 3 star status and the equipment of the rooms here fully corresponds to this name. All rooms, regardless of the type of accommodation, are quite spacious, clean with a fresh repair.

The rooms include:

  • balcony;
  • well-equipped kitchenette;
  • a bathroom with a shower;
  • hair dryer;
  • direct dial telephone;
  • small fridge;
  • kettle;
  • TV;
  • air conditioning (use of air conditioning is offered for a fee);
  • Internet (Wi-Fi is available on site).




Entertainment at the hotel

Most vacationers at the Mari Evans Apartments 3 stars spend a lot of time on the beach or on excursions. There are no opportunities for outdoor activities at the hotel. However, here you can always take a break from walking.

For this, the following are provided:

  • outdoor pool in the courtyard of the hotel;
  • well-equipped bar;
  • internet bar;
  • a restaurant.

This is enough for a quiet relaxed holiday.

Beach vacation

Mari Evans Apartments 3 is located on the third coastline - 250 m to the beach. Most of the beaches in the area are urban sandy. It is regularly cleaned, so tourists can enjoy the cleanliness of the sand and the azure waves.

On the beach you can use a sun lounger and an umbrella, however, you will have to pay for their rental. Entrance to the beach is free.

Each trip to Crete promises travelers a vivid experience and active pastime. In the list of beach activities:

  • boating;
  • yachting;
  • waterslides;
  • diving (many beaches in Greece are perfect for scuba diving);
  • beach sports (outdoor enthusiasts can always do beach volleyball);
  • surfing.

There are excellent bars on the beach where you can get refreshing drinks, and a cafe with local dishes.

Dining at the hotel

The hotel is self-catering or breakfast-based. In other words, meals in the hotel as such are not provided. However, in the hotel bar and restaurant you can always choose the right dish.

Whatever choice customers make, the food will always be fresh, well-served and delicious at home. During the day, many tourists prefer to eat in the city.





Features of accommodation

Tourists who travel alone and entire groups can stay at the Mari Evans Apartments (mainly those who travel along the excursion route).

Many guests are couples with children. Additional amenities are offered for them. At the request of the residents, a children's bed can be placed in the room, and a children's pool is located on the hotel premises.

Who is the hotel suitable for?

The quality of rest depends on the correct selection of the hotel. In Crete, Mari Evans Apartments is mainly suitable for travelers who do not like noisy hotels and resorts.

You can safely reserve a room here:

  • couples with children (the territory of the hotel is as quiet and safe as possible, and there will be a lesson for children);
  • newlyweds (for a honeymoon, Crete is just the perfect solution);
  • people of age;
  • lovers of sightseeing walks;
  • those who want to spend most of their holidays on the beach;
  • Those who want to save on accommodation.

Mari Evans Apartments: reviews

Studying the reviews of tourists who have already stayed here, we can come to the following conclusion: most of them were completely satisfied with the conditions of accommodation.

In the list of the most obvious advantages:

  • relatively low prices for accommodation (in general, holidays on the island of Crete are considered a rather expensive pleasure);
  • comfortable rooms that fully comply with the status of 3 stars;
  • helpful staff.

Not without negative reviews. Most often, guests expressed dissatisfaction with:

  • great remoteness of the beach;
  • poor food at the hotel;
  • low level of service.

Given all the characteristics of the hotel, we can conclude: those who like quiet, calm travel should stay in this hotel. Mari Evans Apartments is far from the last in the ranking of budget hotels. It should be borne in mind that the low cost of accommodation is achieved due to the lack of many types of service.
