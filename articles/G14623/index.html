Ski complex "Stone Cape", Surgut: description, reviews

When it comes to holidays in Surgut, a place for real extreme sportsmen immediately appears, which is sixty kilometers from the city itself. Skiers and snowboarders from all over the Tyumen region gather here. This resort is known throughout Russia due to the excellent equipment of the tracks. Moreover, both experienced and novice athletes will like it here. But consider everything in order.

General description of the resort

The Kamenny Cape ski complex is the most popular and favorite place for outdoor enthusiasts from all over the Khanty-Mansiysk Autonomous Okrug.

The resort began its work in 2001 and has since been improved and delighted with any new products of its customers every year. Here you can not only enjoy skiing and snowboarding, but also enjoy homemade dishes in wooden huts decorated in the Ukrainian style, or have a good rest at the bases located in the center of the Kamenny Cape complex (Surgut).

For those who did not bring their skis, the resort has prudently located rental locations for all ski equipment and professional instructors who will help beginners learn the basics of skiing. Children will find plenty of fun in the children's snow town. Customers arriving in their own cars can use the services of secure parking.

Climatic features

Before you visit this resort, it will be better to find out what the weather is there. "Cape Kamenny" is located between the cities of Surgut and Nefteyugansk, where the average monthly winter temperature ranges from -8 to -12 degrees Celsius. In March, the thermometer may already rise to zero.

Due to the weather in this region, Cape Kamenny opens its season at the end of November, and it lasts until April. If precipitation is insignificant and does not contribute to good skiing, snow cannons are provided on the territory of the complex, which throw artificial snow on the tracks. Therefore, even in the spring months you can have a great time.

Leisure at the resort

Most skiers cannot satisfy their need for skiing in just one day, so it is worth considering as an option to stay at a resort recreation center (Surgut). Kamenny Cape can offer its vacationers accommodation in the Kedr guest house or in the Rus or Northern Lights hotel, which are located directly on the territory of the complex. Thus, lovers of active and extreme types of recreation will be able to enjoy their vacation for several days.

For a more comfortable and interesting pastime for its customers, the ski complex has provided many more interesting and useful things. For example, they can take a steam bath in a Russian bath or visit some sightseeing tours. It will be informative to see the museums of Surgutgazprom or the Merchant's Estate, as well as visit the Church of the Transfiguration.





Feedback from grateful customers

Skiers and snowboarders who already had the opportunity to visit the slopes and ski lifts of the resort leave only positive comments. They say that it is so comfortable and safe to ride nowhere in the whole district. There is always a lot of snow on the slopes of the complex, which contributes to a pleasant descent, and you can only enjoy the ride, not being afraid that you can fall.

The entire resort has only friendly and helpful staff, always in a hurry to come to the rescue. Professional instructors who teach beginners are very accessible and understandable, they show everything, after which it will not be difficult to roll off any simple route yourself. Those customers who rested there at least once in their lives will undoubtedly return there again and will not forget to bring a couple of their friends with them.

Cost of services and rental

Prices at the resort are quite low and affordable, so many people can afford this type of vacation. For example, an hourly ski pass costs only 250 rubles, but even in this case, you can still save by purchasing just three hours for 550 rubles. or six hours for 750 rubles.





Customers who come to relax at the resort from year to year can buy a discount card. With its frequent use, the discount on rental of all equipment and payment for descents will accordingly increase.

It is still possible in such an original way to please your near or dear person by purchasing a gift certificate for skiing at the Kamenny Cape resort (Surgut). Prices for this type of gift vary from one to five thousand rubles.

Beginners can use the service of an instructor for 800 rubles per hour, this is if he will teach personally. The larger the composition of the training group, the less will need to pay the coach. For example, if two skiers learn the basics of skiing, then the instructor will take 650 rubles per hour, and when a group of three people is recruited, the cost of training drops to 550 rubles.
