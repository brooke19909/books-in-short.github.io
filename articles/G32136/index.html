Lightweight shawl scheme (knitting): photo and job description

Successful, that is, simple, interesting and understandable, the scheme of shawl with knitting needles is quite rare. Often you can see something too ordinary and boring or, conversely, so complicated that it is completely impossible to understand the instructions. The scheme of knitting an openwork shawl with the needles proposed in this article allows you to get an extremely beautiful product and does not require a high level of skill from the knitter. To bring it to life, it is enough to possess basic skills, know the front, wrong loops, their reduction and addition with the help of yarn.



Where to begin?

First of all, you should choose the right yarn. The material should be either entirely cotton, or contain about 50% wool, mohair or angora (the rest is acrylic). In the first case, the product will be dense, but not hot. Such things are suitable for cool summer evenings.

If the craftswoman decides to use warm materials, then she can knit autumn or even winter shawls with knitting needles (the diagram and description can remain the same).

The optimal thickness of the yarn is in the range of 200-400 m / 100 grams. When using a thicker thread (200m / 100 grams), the shawl scheme with knitting needles will allow you to create a dense and large product. If you choose a thinner material (400 m / 100 grams), an openwork and light accessory will come out.

Knitting needles should be selected for yarn (No. 2, 2.5, 3 or 4).

Getting to work

The shawl canvas will consist of five full-fledged rapports. In order to proceed to knitting the first row, you need to dial 15 loops + 2 hem.

Next, the craftswoman only needs to follow the scheme A.1 and perform five successive rapports.

When this knitting pattern is finished with knitting needles, the knitter may find that the product is ready. You should try on a shawl and see if you need to knit further. Depending on the thickness of the thread and the density of knitting, the fabric can be either medium size or quite large.

How to make a shawl edge?

In the event that the first two steps were enough to complete the product, the knitter can safely close all the loops. This work must be done carefully, because you can neither tighten the edge nor weaken it.

It is best to use a slightly larger hook than knitting needles: for example, if knitting with number 3, you need to take a hook number 4.

The finished shawl should be tied in several rows of single crochet posts, taking care that the corners are correctly framed.

Shaping corners for tying shawls

The peculiarity of crocheting is that it can either emphasize the geometric shape of the product, or lubricate it. In order for the shawl to have neat contours, its corners must remain sharp.

This can be achieved if three single crochet stitches are knitted from one base loop. In the same way, when performing all rows of strapping.

To further emphasize the geometry of the shawl, you can add columns at the tops of the waves. However, for balance, it will be necessary to reduce the same number of columns in the process of tying concave sections. If you only add columns, the edge of the shawl will turn into ruffles.

We knit a big shawl

For those who chose thin threads or want to get a product of impressive size, the designers developed another stage of the scheme. Here are all the familiar elements in the familiar combination. The only difference is that the openwork shawl scheme (knitting needles) is divided into two parts.

The first side (A.3a) represents the left side of the rapport, and the second (A.3b) represents the right side. To finish the shawl, the craftswoman must sequentially perform five rapports, starting each row according to scheme A.3a and ending in the pattern A.3b.
All purl rows are traditionally knitted according to the pattern. If the last scheme still turned out to be too large, it can only be completed in half. In fact, you can stop knitting at any stage, the model will not suffer from this. However, in the case when the craftswoman wants to increase the scheme, she will have to work hard to extend the rapport. It is important to maintain proportions and ensure uniform expansion.

When the product is ready, it should be processed as described in the previous paragraph.

This knitting pattern for shawls is perfect for those who want to quickly and effectively knit an accessory for themselves or as a gift for the holidays. You need to remember how to care for knitted things: they are washed only in warm water and dried unfolded. To make the hair softer, you can add shampoo or special conditioner when washing. You can straighten the crumpled knitting by dipping the shawl with steam from the iron. At the same time, it is impossible to put the sole of the device on the canvas.
