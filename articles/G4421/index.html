Overview of trampoline parks in Yekaterinburg

Trampoline parks are a favorite pastime not only for children but also for adults. After all, this is a great way to get a boost of vigor, relieve stress after a working day and keep your muscles in good shape. In Yekaterinburg, trampoline parks are becoming more popular, so several such establishments have opened. Below is an overview of the most popular of them.

Razgon

Most often, such entertainment is placed in shopping centers. One of the most popular trampoline parks in Yekaterinburg is Acceleration. It is located in the shopping and entertainment center "Alatyr". This park occupies a large area, where you can jump as much as you like on more than 100 trampolines. In addition to trampolines, the park has 4 foam pits - one of the favorite entertainments of young and adult visitors.

The trampoline park of Yekaterinburg in "Alatyr" is divided into 12 active zones, which allows visitors not to interfere with each other during jumps. And if you want to not only jump, then at your service a climbing wall. For the youngest guests there are special mini trampolines.

An additional service appeared in the trampoline park of Yekaterinburg in Alatyr - the Nerf quest. This is a team game using blasters - a great alternative to jumping and climbing. It offers visitors instructors who monitor safety and conduct training.

After an active holiday, you can relax in a cafe, try delicious necks and soft drinks, as well as have a bite to eat with pizza and other fast food. Reasonable prices, as well as the possibility of another option for recreation, except for jumping, make this place one of the most popular in the city.

Fun jump

This trampoline park of Yekaterinburg is located in the Academic microdistrict. In addition to trampolines and a foam pit with a tower, the institution has many other attractions. Younger guests can visit the three-level labyrinth, a ship with air guns, a bungee, a climbing wall and other interesting attractions.

Experienced instructors work with visitors who conduct classes in acrobatics, fitness and jumping. In addition, there is a restaurant on the top floor of the trampoline park, where guests can relax from active entertainment.

"Base of heroes"

This is a new trampoline park in Yekaterinburg, opened in the Shirokaya Rechka microdistrict. It is not as big as the others, but it has a cozy and homely atmosphere. This park has 12 small trampolines and 4 voluminous nets. Also there are two foam pits.

In addition, experienced trainers conduct classes on the basics of acrobatics and gymnastics. A distinctive feature - courses of aerial gymnastics on canvases. In addition, holidays are organized for children with the invitation of various popular heroes. Visitors also note the good location of the recreation area and the cleanliness of the establishment. Therefore, the new trampoline park is becoming increasingly popular.

In order to stand out in a competitive environment, the institution needs, in addition to trampolines, to organize other activities for children and adults. This greatly diversifies the rest. An essential component of success is experienced instructors who not only monitor the safety of visitors, create an atmosphere of fun and outdoor activities, but also teach the basics of acrobatics and gymnastics.

Prices in trampoline parks in Yekaterinburg are affordable. In some institutions, you can purchase membership cards for visiting. The cost of unlimited visits to the park is from 700 to 800 rubles on weekdays, and on weekends it is slightly higher. An individual lesson with a trainer costs 1,000 rubles. A group training - from 400 rubles.

In addition, in all trampoline parks, various promotions are often arranged, which allows you to attract new customers. Trampoline parks now open not only in shopping centers, but also in new residential complexes, which makes them even more convenient for visiting. Trampoline parks are a great opportunity to get a boost of vigor, return to childhood and bring muscles to tone.
