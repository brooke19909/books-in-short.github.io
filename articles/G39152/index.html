Holiday Village "Albatross", Nizhny Novgorod Region: description, reviews, prices

Many citizens would surely like to go out on a weekend in the countryside. Great service and a lot of entertainment today offers just a huge number of tourist camps and holiday homes. Residents of the Nizhny Novgorod region, for example, have an excellent opportunity to visit the Albatros complex, located in the forest on the banks of the Volga, 25 km from the regional center.



General description of the recreation center "Albatross" in the Nizhny Novgorod region

This tourist base accepts guests in a large cottage-type building for up to 42 people. You can park your car in the parking lot next to it. Clean air, unusually beautiful nature, the opportunity to have some good fun - all this makes Albatros very popular among residents of the Nizhny Novgorod region. Vacationers have the opportunity to take walks in the surroundings or a beautiful grape alley in the complex.





Number of rooms

In the hotel building of the camp site you can rent an improved room, ordinary, standard two-room or one-room. All rooms are designed for two guests. Each room has comfortable beds, there is all necessary appliances for a comfortable pastime, a bathroom, a shower. If desired, the cottage can be rented as a whole for a large company.





Infrastructure

In the complex, in addition to parking, there is a boat station. Here you can rent a boat, boat or pedalo. There is a windsurfing school on the shore. Also, the recreation center "Albatros" (Nizhny Novgorod region) offers its guests to visit the summer cafe-bar. Light breakfast and drinks are available here. Also, guests are waiting for a comfortable Russian bath. Barbecue lovers can conveniently sit in a place specially equipped for picnics.

In summer, a beach on the Volga is equipped for guests of the complex. You can also visit the outdoor pool. Bicycle rental is also available. There are also stables on site. Those who wish can enjoy horseback riding. In winter, an ice rink operates at the Albatross base. But only in fine weather. Fishing enthusiasts have the opportunity to rent any gear and boat. The territory of the park itself is guarded by licensed specialists around the clock.





sights

Visiting all sorts of interesting historical or cultural places is also part of the range of activities that Albatros (recreation center) offers its visitors. Nizhny Novgorod region (reviews of these places are good for all tourists without exception) - a region with a huge number of attractions. Vacationers have the opportunity to book a sightseeing tour of the surrounding areas. In addition, guests of the base can visit:

  • Makaryevsky monastery (on a yacht or by bus).

  • Ostrich farm.

  • Sightseeing in N. Novgorod itself.

Treatment

Although the tourist base "Albatros" as a sanatorium does not position itself, here you can not only relax well, but also heal. In the immediate vicinity of the complex there are two artesian wells with healing mineral water. Drinking it is recommended for people suffering from diseases such as increased or decreased secretory function of the stomach, chronic colitis, all kinds of problems with the liver and kidneys.

Also, the recreation center "Albatros" (Nizhny Novgorod region) offers its customers to visit:

  • shungite cave with marine microclimate;

  • massage room;

  • ozonized pool;

  • IR cab

Family holiday

Many residents of the Nizhny Novgorod region come to the tourist base "Albatross" with their children. For younger guests of the complex, the administration also provided a lot of entertainment. Children can play on the specially equipped playground, take a swim in the small pool, ride a pony or go to the mini zoo with their parents.

Accommodation Features

Check-in at this tourist base is from 14 hours, check-out is from 12 hours. However, by prior arrangement with the administration, you can order another time of arrival or departure. For example, early check-in is possible - before 9 am, and late check-out (after 20.00). However, in both cases, you will have to add 500 rubles per person to the cost of living.
