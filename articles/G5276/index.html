Sauber Bank: customer reviews, services, loans, deposits, interest rates and terms of payments

Reviews about Sauber Bank are of great importance for all customers who are considering the opportunity to use the services of this financial institution. How real users relate to it, it is important to know both individuals and legal entities. In addition, potential employees are also interested in impressions about working in the company. This is a fairly large bank, in which a large number of the most diverse vacancies are open almost all year round. Therefore, many can find a job in their specialty.

About company

There are various reviews of Sauber Bank, in order to understand them in detail, we will first tell about the history of the company itself, when it appeared, what services it can provide at present.

The Sauber Bank joint-stock company was founded shortly after the collapse of the Soviet Union, in 1992. It appeared on the basis of one of the financial institutions included in the ramified system of Promstroybank with a 75-year history. For a long time, the company has already formed corporate traditions that provided it with exceptional authority in the financial services market.

In 2012, the company conducted a full-scale rebranding, which resulted in the bank getting a new name, by which it is known to everyone today, as well as a significant increase in its capital by attracting additional issues and shares.

The official opening of the renewed Sauber Bank office in St. Petersburg took place at the end of 2012. Currently, it is located at Bolshoy Prospekt Petrogradskaya side, house 25/2.

At the next stage of development, its own division was opened in Moscow, soon other regions began to appear in the geography of this financial institution. The systematic growth of capital made it possible to provide clients with fundamentally new opportunities in the field of lending, the client base has grown significantly, and the risk has been minimized due to the diversification of the loan portfolio.

The Bank provides services for individuals and legal entities. Separate conditions are provided for corporate clients. In particular, they can count on an individual approach when lending, rendering services in the field of settlement and cash services in foreign and domestic currencies, when renting safes, issuing credit plastic cards, remote services, currency control, making payments using a customs card.

Private individuals are provided with the services of placing their free cash funds on deposits, they can also rent safe deposit boxes, receive car loans and cash loans, carry out currency exchange operations and bank money transfers.

Loans

In the customer reviews of Sauber Bank, private individuals note that, most often, they turn to a financial institution for services in the field of lending. For example, you can get a loan for a new or used car.

When purchasing a used car, you will need to make an initial deposit of up to 80 percent of the cost of the car (while there are options when a deposit is not required at all), the loan is issued according to two documents, including a passport and a driver‚Äôs license. A preliminary decision is made within one hour. After that, the client has thirty days to find a car that meets his requirements. It is important that the company ensures the safe execution of the transaction.

The borrower is subject to certain requirements. He must be a citizen of the Russian Federation from the age of 21 at the time of conclusion of the loan agreement. Moreover, at the time the agreement expires, he must be no more than 65 years old (for women - no more than 60). He must carry out his labor activity in the regions where the branches and branches of the bank are represented. It is required to provide information on official employment, which can be confirmed by the presence of the corresponding entry in the work book or by the concluded labor contract.

Continuous work experience at the last place of work should be at least three months, if a loan is issued in the amount of more than 1.2 million rubles, then at least six months.

When drawing up this agreement, it is necessary to provide a completed credit questionnaire, a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation and a valid driver's license.

According to customer reviews of Sauber Bank on car loans, the design of loans for new cars is popular. In this case, you can count on up to one and a half million rubles at an interest rate of 12.5 percent per annum. The loan term will be from one to six years.

A cash loan is issued for a period of up to three years within a quarter of an hour after applying. The validity of this decision is one month, and you can first learn about the result within an hour after the submission of all necessary documents. Early repayment of additional fees and commissions is possible. The only fundamental condition is that the borrower must live in St. Petersburg or the Leningrad Region. He can count on the amount from thirty to 299 thousand rubles.

If you take a loan secured by a car, then the maximum amount increases to one and a half million rubles, and the loan term grows to five years.

Deposits

According to customer reviews of Sauber Bank, many people prefer to open deposits here, since they are attracted by rather high interest rates.

The company offers six types of deposits that should satisfy any customer needs. On the Sauber-Fortune deposit, the user is offered convenient and favorable storage conditions and growth of accumulations. It is noteworthy that the conditions of the deposit provide for its early termination at the request of the client.

The minimum deposit amount is one hundred thousand rubles, the base rate is 7.55 percent per annum, with early termination it is reduced to 5%. There is no capitalization of interest and additional contributions. Interest is paid monthly; auto-prolongation is provided.

When making a Sauber-Fortuna Long deposit for a period of more than three years, the base rate increases to 7.65 percent per annum. In case of early termination, it will amount to 5.7%.

If the two previous deposits were opened only in rubles, then Sauber - Currency Growth is intended for foreign currency. The minimum amount is one thousand dollars or euros. The base rate for a period of more than three years will be 0.2% per annum if you keep money in dollars. And if in euros, then the rate will be 0.01%.

The Sauber-Infinity deposit is opened from the amount of one hundred thousand rubles at a rate of 7.85 percent per annum. Interest is paid monthly to the card account on a certain date, which corresponds to the date of conclusion of the agreement with the financial institution. When servicing this deposit, a Visa Infinite card is issued for the client .

The Sauber - VIP Classic deposit involves a maximum income with the ability to spend money, as well as early terminate the contract with minimal loss. You can open it with a minimum amount of five million rubles at seven and a half percent per annum. Moreover, upon termination of the contract after more than six months, the rate is reduced to six percent per annum, and if earlier, then to 3%.

Finally, there is a Sauber-Partner contribution. It is intended for customers with deposits closed from November 1 to December 28, 2018. In this case, users can count on special favorable conditions. With a minimum deposit amount of one hundred thousand rubles, they are given a rate of 7.8% per annum when making a deposit for three years. If the contract is terminated ahead of schedule, then the rate is reduced to 7.4%.

Cell rental

Many customers use the safe deposit box rental service. This is a proven, reliable and versatile way to preserve any values.

Users are provided with a wide choice of cell sizes, various rental periods, the possibility of online booking this service immediately on the site, as well as safe rent by several tenants.

For individuals and legal entities, when concluding a contract for a year, a discount of twenty percent is given, and when renting a cell for a period of six months, upon presentation of a bonus card of the Sauber Club loyalty program, it is possible to get a 30 percent discount.

The minimum rental period is one week. The rental price is from 15 rubles per day.

other services
