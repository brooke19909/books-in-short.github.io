Hotel Dream Beach Resort Sharm 5 *: overview, description, features and reviews

Resorts of Egypt, especially Sharm el-Sheikh, are one of the most popular places for both winter and summer holidays of Russian tourists. However, they are especially popular during the winter period, because in our temperate and northern latitudes, there is slush and frosty weather, and the sun is in great shortage. Meanwhile, there, on the African continent, on some turquoise beach, next to a comfortable and starry hotel, for example, Dreams Beach Resort Sharm El Sheikh Egypt, on cozy sun loungers, under bamboo umbrellas, thousands of tourists from all over bask in the gentle southern sun Sveta. Naturally, they very quickly forget about the cold, and about subzero temperatures.



Egypt is the land of the sun!

Probably everyone knows about Egypt without exception. Indeed, our textbooks of ancient history begin to narrate precisely from the story of this country. Naturally, a state with a thousand-year history should be rich in sights. And so it is. Nevertheless, not only the famous pyramids of the pharaohs, sphinxes and other ancient monuments of architecture attract many travelers and tourists here. Egypt is popular for its magnificent beaches, on which dozens of comfortable hotel complexes from two to five stars are located, such as the Dreams Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Dreams Beach Resort) in Sharm El Sheikh, as well as incredible beauty underwater world. That is why there are many diving training centers on the Egyptian coast.



Sharm el Sheikh - the pearl of the Red Sea

This seaside resort has been the most sought after on the Egyptian coast for several decades. Tourists from both Europe and Asia come here. Charm is especially popular among lovers of lazy beach holidays, as well as lovers of wonderful seascape and diving fans. How to get there

So, if you decide to go to the best of the Egyptian resorts located on the shores of the Red Sea to relax in one of the fashionable hotel complexes, for example, in the Dreams Beach Resort 5 * ("Dreams Beach Resort"), then you need to buy a ticket for one of charter flights to the only airport of Sharm - Ras Nazran. The flight time is 4 hours 30 minutes. As for regular flights, they are only in two directions: to the capital of Egypt, Cairo or the large city of Alexandria. From there, the resort can be reached either by bus, or by taxi or a rented car, or by some kind of domestic flight. For those who are going to buy a tour: almost all packages include a transfer, that is, delivery from the Sharm el-Sheikh airport to the desired hotel.



Sharm El Sheikh

The coast of this resort is indented by bays along which picturesque areas are located. They are united by the central street Peace Road. The oldest of them is Naama Bay. He is the most comfortable. There is a fairly developed infrastructure, there are many upscale hotels, restaurants and bars. Sharm El Maya is famous for its magnificent sandy beaches and the colorful old eastern market, while Sharks Bay is famous for its incredible beauty of coral reefs. As for Nabq, it is a rather young and rapidly developing area, and the Ras Um al-Sid, or Khadoba bay, where the aforementioned Dreams Beach Resort (Sharm El Sheikh, 5 stars) is located, is famous for its wonderful coral gardens, in which huddled a great many marine inhabitants. Of course, corals starting directly from the coast interfere with free access to the sea, so there are pontoons on the beaches that ensure the safety of tourists. In addition, guests are advised to wear special rubber slippers to protect their feet from damage.

Climatic conditions

As noted above, in the resorts of the Red Sea, the beach period continues throughout the year. Nevertheless, experts distinguish between two seasons: winter (mild) and summer (hot). The first is suitable for those who are not accustomed to very high temperatures, and the second is for those who can‚Äôt warm up or tolerate hot temperatures. Nevertheless, low humidity and periodically blowing winds help tourists easily tolerate such heat. But in the winter, it is cool here at night, and the thermometer drops to 14-16 degrees. And in the daytime it is warm enough to soak up the gentle sun and swim in the gentle waters of the Red Sea. In a word, rest at the Dream Beach Resort Sharm 5 * hotel in winter is a great choice for tourists of all age categories. Number of rooms

In total, Dream Beach Resort Sharm has 5 * 467 rooms of three categories: suites (suite), standard (standard) and family (standard connected), consisting of two standard rooms, interconnected. The hotel also has a room for disabled travelers.

Description of apartments and service

All rooms have balconies or terraces with outdoor furniture. Each room has a kitchenette equipped with a refrigerator and a special set for making coffee and tea. The bathroom has a shower, washbasin, toilet, full bath and toilet set. The room is also equipped with modern appliances: a TV showing satellite channels, air conditioning, a minibar, a telephone, a hairdryer, etc. As for the service, bed linen is changed every other day, and room service is daily. Although there is information that not everything is so smooth at the Dreams Beach Hotel (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh). Reviews of tourists say that although cleaning is carried out daily, it does not bring great cleanliness, and staff constantly forgets to change their linen, and if they do not remind them, they will not even remember about it. Room service is available for a fee. By the way, a 1.5-liter bottle of clean water is delivered daily to each room. The cost of living in rooms for one person is from 1700 rubles.

Infrastructure

The hotel has two conference rooms and a meeting room, thanks to which quite often various events are held here, such as seminars and presentations. There is also a business center with the necessary office equipment. Wireless internet is available on site. Entertainment

Hotel Dream Beach Resort Sharm 5 * is rich in water activities. There are 5 pools. Right in the center of the complex is the main pool with a 32-meter slide. Nearby is a children's pool. There are also pools in the beach area: children and adults. The length of the children's slide is 6 meters. By the way, in the winter season, the water in the pools is heated. An international team of experienced animators is what the Dreams Beach Resort Sharm 5 * hotel is truly proud of. Reviews of tourists about this segment of the service are always positive.
