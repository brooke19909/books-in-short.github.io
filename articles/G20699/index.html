A beautiful and stylish accessory for hair is a bezel with flowers. With our own hands we will make an original decoration

Handmade hair jewelry is distinguished by its individuality and originality. Handmade accessories are in fashion today, so we suggest that all of you learn how to do them yourself. In this article, we will tell you how to make a rim with flowers from the most affordable materials. For work you need only an hour of time, and the resulting decoration will delight you more than one day.



Master class "Headband with flowers." With our own hands we learn to make accessories from fabric

Preparation of materials

For work, we need:

  • plastic rim;
  • organza fabric and silk (small segments);
  • round cardboard templates of different sizes (4-5 pieces);
  • beads;
  • thermal gun;
  • scissors;
  • needle with thread;
  • candle or lighter.

The basics

To make a beautiful rim with flowers with your own hands, you need to start by framing the plastic base into a fabric. From a silk cut a strip about 4 centimeters wide. Wrap this blank around the rim, fasten the ends with hot glue. Try to keep the fabric lying evenly, so the product will look neat and beautiful.

Flower making

Place patterns on the organza, circle them with a pencil. Cut 3-4 mugs of each size. These will be the flower petals. Gently over the candle or using a lighter, scorch the edges of these blanks. As a result of this procedure, they will become dense, will not bloom, and the part itself will bend a little and become like a plate. Next, fold all the petals one on one, starting with large ones and ending with smaller ones. Sew the entire structure in the middle and attach one large bead or several small ones. A fabric flower is ready. You can make such parts several or one large.

We collect a rim with flowers

With our own hands, we have already designed the base and made decorations for it. Now connect these parts. Apply a couple drops of glue to the flower and attach it to the hoop. Hold the product for a minute and set to dry. This is how you simply make a rim with flowers with your own hands. This decoration will be a great addition to summer air dresses and sundresses.

Satin ribbon flowers are a wonderful bezel decoration. Learning to make an original decor from simple materials

The most common thin or wide hoop can become an exclusive accessory for hair, if decorated with flowers made of ribbons. What do we need for work? Decorate the hoop as described above, only instead of fabric you can wrap it with tape. To make a flower, prepare a needle with a thread, a satin ribbon, scissors, a lighter, a beautiful button or bead, and glue. So, let's get down to creativity. Cut the tape into segments of 6-10 centimeters, burn their ends with a lighter. Make four such details. Next, fold each piece of tape in half and sew the ends together. The result is four rings of ribbons. Now fold one piece so that the junction of the ends is in the middle. Sew a bead or button in the center, capturing all the blanks with a needle at once. This will be the middle of the product. As a result, you get a flower with eight petals in the form of loops. You can perform several of these elements, then you get a rim of flowers on your head in the form of a wreath. Please note that the wider you take the ribbon and the longer the cuts from it, the larger the size and volume of your decor.



Such hair accessories will decorate you, give an image of charm and elegance. I wish you creative success!
