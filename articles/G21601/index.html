Poseidon Hotel 4 * (Marmaris): reviews, photos

Marmaris is one of the most beloved Russian tourists city-resort in Turkey. Thousands of travelers come here every year who love exotics and hot countries. Not surprisingly, the hotels of this town are in great demand. One of the most popular hotels in Marmaris is Poseidon Hotel 4 *, which offers excellent conditions for recreation to every guest.

Location

The hotel is located on the very shore of the Aegean Sea, in a beautiful and clean place. Not far from it is a huge entertainment center, which includes a large number of city restaurants, cafes, bars, nightclubs.





Also, tourists living in it can go to look at various sights, many of which are of historical value. The nearest major airport is 90 kilometers from the location of the Poseidon Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), in another resort city - Antalya.

general information

The hotel is located on its territory as compact as possible - a whole range of entertainment and buildings occupy only 3900 square meters. This resting place was built in 2000, and since then the territory has been maintained in its original condition and modern look. Tourists, arriving at the Poseidon Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), can enjoy the beautiful garden, which is spread between the building and the pool, it often hosts photo shoots.

Rooms

All 232 rooms available in the hotel fund are located in a large and modern six-story building. All of them are divided into several categories: economy, standard, family and apartments for people with disabilities.





In each of them there are absolutely all conditions suitable for a comfortable stay. The interior of the apartment was made in accordance with an individual design project; modern furniture, electronics and plumbing were selected for equipment. Each room, regardless of its category, has a bathroom and shower. In addition, all apartments have individual air conditioning, with which residents can independently adjust for themselves the most suitable climatic conditions, regardless of the weather on the street.

Living conditions for children

Hotel Poseidon Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Marmaris), even its owners can hardly be classified as family-type vacation spots. Despite this, there are some conditions for small children. In particular, a special swimming pool is equipped for them, made taking into account all safety requirements.

SPA area and sport

Especially popular among hotel guests are such areas of the hotel as a gym and spa. The gym has a small number of simulators designed for different muscle groups. The hotel also offers bike hire for rides. As for the SPA zone, it consists of a large sauna and a traditional Turkish bath - hammam. In addition, everyone can visit the massage room, but all the services provided in it are paid.

Nutrition

A large number of tourists in their reviews of the Poseidon Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) report that the local restaurant serves excellent European and Turkish dishes prepared by professional chefs. A daily buffet is organized here, for which guests are invited for breakfast and dinner.





Drinks are available at an additional cost. Hotel guests can also order light snacks and salads, as well as Turkish desserts in local bars located near the pool, on the beach and in the building. It offers a huge amount of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as cocktails.

Beach

On some photos of the Poseidon Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) you can observe the hotel's own beach, which is famous for its beauty. From its territory you can observe a beautiful surrounding view. Here everything is arranged for a comfortable rest of guests: there are locker rooms, showers and toilets. Guests can sit on large sun loungers under huge umbrellas that protect against direct sunlight. Terry towels and air mattresses are available free of charge here.

Tourists who like to sunbathe on the beach, have the opportunity to have fun with any available methods. Here you can rent a small boat, catamaran or yacht and go for a while to the open Aegean Sea. Fans of extreme sports will not miss the opportunity to ride a hovercraft or water ski, and all fans of the underwater world can go scuba diving under the guidance of a professional instructor.

Services

The hotel provides a range of services that make the lives of guests more comfortable. In particular, any guest can apply for a free consultation to the administrator, who is located at the reception desk - this can be done around the clock. Also, all guests can enjoy free access to the Internet - this ensures constant communication with family and friends.





For a fee, guests can use the dry cleaning and laundry services, as well as call the doctor in the room (if necessary). For tourists, whose life is connected with doing business, it often comes in handy to have a conference room that can be rented for a certain time. It has everything you need for meetings, negotiations and presentations.

Guest reviews

Hotel guests who spent their holidays on its territory, as a rule, are satisfied with the results, because they return home rested, full of vitality and energy. In their reviews of Poseidon Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), tourists generally share their impressions and photos taken during their holidays.

Here administrators are always ready to answer any exciting question and help in solving problems, and the maids from the room service leave behind themselves impeccably clean rooms.

Tourists who like to take care of themselves note that the hotel has all the necessary conditions for maintaining health and body sculpting: here you can first go to the gym, then swim in the clean pool and finally relax in the bath.
