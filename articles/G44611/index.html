How to fix birch and other trees

Put a birch in a bowl, fasten. Other bead trees are formed in the same way, the weaving patterns of which are so simple that even novice craftswomen can reproduce them.

You can fix the barrel with plasticine or first cut a piece of thick foam of suitable diameter, put it in a bowl and stick the end of the aluminum wire into it.

To keep the birch or other tree tight, in a separate bowl mix 1 part PVA glue, two - alabaster. Add so much water to make a solution that looks like liquid curd.

To apply it on a tree trunk previously moistened with water , it is necessary without delay, since the alabaster hardens very quickly. Put the remaining solution in the bowl in which the tree is located to fix the birch in this position.

When it dries, cover it with 2-3 layers of white paint. When each of them dries, make a few strokes of black paint to make the birch look like a real one. Paint and paint the surface of the alabaster in a bowl, decorate it with colored pebbles.

Bonsai

If you wish, use the simpler method and make not only money, but also other trees from beads. Bonsai weaving patterns are also simple.





Cut pieces of 45 cm long from a wire 0.3-0.4 mm thick. Each of them will soon turn into a branch. Take a little more than 200 g of green beads, as well as yellow, mix them in a container.

Pick up so many beads on the first wire so that it covers it by 15 cm. Take a wooden stick for sushi, wrap a part of the wire coated with beads on it. Insert one through the resulting rings and tighten the other end of the wire to the other side.

In the same way, arrange 2 more branches, twist them together, as shown in the photo. Using a floral ribbon, connect 3-4 branches. Then take 4 such blanks, tape them.

It remains to connect the branches and, observing the asymmetry, fix the bonsai in the container. This is how trees are made from beads, the weaving patterns of which are simple and understandable.
