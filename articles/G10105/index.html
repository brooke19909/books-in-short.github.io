Shambhala beads and their own making

Jewelry that is made independently gives a special charm to its owner, helping to create a unique image. Much depends not only on the model, but also on the material that is used to create this or that work of art. Craftswomen will undoubtedly be attracted by shambhala beads, which in themselves are remarkable gizmos. As a rule, they have a round shape and are decorated with rhinestones. The color palette is very rich: you can find pink, peach, turquoise balls, in texture resembling crystals.





A bracelet made of these beads is not only a stylish thing, it is also curious because, according to existing beliefs, it protects its owner from misfortunes. You can purchase this jewelry through the online store. But it is doubly pleasant to create it yourself!

Even for beginners, weaving bracelets from shamballa beads will not be difficult. The use of minerals and rhinestones in the decoration will give your product a truly chic look. Round decorative elements can be made of natural stones, glass or wood.

It is quite possible to make beads at home. Goat milk can be mentioned as alternative materials from which shambhala beads are made . It must be mixed with food vinegar. Casein stands out. Already from it you can form decorative elements for future decoration. They can be painted with food colors.





Perfect for creating original plastic beads. To obtain a glassy mass, it is necessary to melt the polymer clay or thermoplastic. The latter type of material is most convenient for the manufacture of jewelry, as it can be remelted. Shambhala beads made from it can have a variety of shapes and sizes. For example, there are flat, round, or drop-like. Professional craftsmen perform them in the form of leaves, flowers, coins, figures of birds or animals.





The traditional bracelet is made of large round beads. Translucent decoration create interspersed with rhinestones, which are placed on its outer and inner sides. Each decorative element is equipped with small pearls or interspersed with sparkles. All of them should be distributed evenly. A plastic material such as polymer clay will help in this matter . Shambhala beads made of it are decorated with small pebbles, each of them needs to be pressed a little into the material with a match, and then for a better fit of rhinestones it is necessary to roll a decorative ball. A bracelet will need 9 beads. They need to be pierced with a toothpick and placed in the oven, which must first be heated to a temperature of 100-130 degrees.

Apply weaving zigzag, single, 2, 3-row and so on. And for the manufacture of jewelry you need only a nylon cord and the beads themselves. The technique in which the bracelet is performed is quite simple, and every craftswoman can master it.
