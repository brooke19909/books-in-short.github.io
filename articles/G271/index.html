The recreation center "Swan": an unforgettable outdoor recreation

Very close to the capital of Tatarstan, Kazan, in protected areas, there is a recreation center "Lebyazhye". Easy to get there. Settled tourists in comfortable rooms. This base is worth noting as an option for outdoor recreation suitable for any, including budget, wallet. And guests will not have to get bored: the base staff offers guests a lot of entertainment. Now we will tell you everything in detail.



Location How to get there

The Lebyazhye recreation center is located on the territory of the Nizhny Kama nature reserve in the Borovets Forest. Administratively, it belongs to the city of Naberezhnye Chelny. From Kazan, you can reach the base by several buses. These are numbers 29, 36, 46, 72, 91 and 97. The stop is called "Swan Lake". But most of the guests arrive here in their own cars. After leaving Kazan, you need to move along the Gorky highway. Stop at the Swan Lake store and then turn onto the road that leads to the right. You will ride on it until it ends right at the base gate.



Where do tourists live?

Those who come to the Lebyazhye recreation center to spend a weekend or even a short vacation will find comfortable cottages. It can accommodate a different number of people - from a married couple to a large company of ten people. These guest houses are two-story. On the first level there is a kitchen, a living room, as well as amenities: a bathroom and a shower. On the second floor - one or two bedrooms, depending on the number of guests. Each cottage has a sauna, hot and cold water. The bedrooms have balconies.

Large groups of vacationers settle in the so-called hockey houses. They are designed for ten guests. They have all the necessary kitchen appliances for cooking, as well as amenities and televisions. Around the houses there are barbecue grill and barbecue areas. Many houses, especially for large companies, have their own names: “Birds”, “Music”, “Paris”, “Venice”.

On the territory of the recreation center there is also a hotel building with seven six-bed rooms, each of which is also equipped with a kitchen and even a sauna. Tourists can also settle in the bath complex. This is a two-story house, where on the first level there is a steam room, a swimming pool, a lounge with a TV and a kitchenette, and on the second - bedrooms.

Nutrition

All the houses and cottages of the Lebyazhye recreation center are equipped with kitchens. Therefore, if guests bring food with them, they will be able to cook them on their own. But there is also a summer cafe, a restaurant on the territory of the base , as well as tents for those occasions when a banquet, corporate party or wedding is held. If you want to make kebabs, then you don’t even have to worry about meat. At the base you will be offered pork, and beef, and lamb, and chicken. If you want to have a holiday, the restaurant will offer you a banquet menu, including a pie with salmon, a goose stuffed with apples, a rabbit in sour cream. There are dishes of Korean, Italian, Tatar cuisine: pork ribs in honey, pilaf with lamb, echpochmaki, pizza.



Services and prices

The territory of the base is guarded around the clock, it is completely closed, so that guests can not worry about their safety. Guests can leave cars at a special parking lot. The recreation center "Lebyazhye" (Naberezhnye Chelny) has a lot of entertainment for children and adults. For kids there is a playground. For older children - a rope town. There is an opportunity to play paintball. On the base there is a rental of balls and rackets for tennis and badminton, and in winter - skates and skis. Lovers of steam will like a good, modern bath complex. Sun loungers are provided throughout the summer for sunbathing.

The recreation center "Lebyazhye" (Chelny) stands on the shores of the lake of the same name. When the water warms up, you can swim there. There is a nice sandy beach. And winter holidays and New Year's holidays are a continuous kaleidoscope of performances, various outdoor games and competitions. Prices for accommodation at the base are different. So, a cabin for two people will cost 3 thousand rubles on a weekday, and on weekends or holidays - 3,500. Guest house “Venice” for 9 people will cost 11 thousand. Rent a sauna for two hours - 1600 rubles.



Reviews

Tourists recommend this recreation center, because it is located near the city and at the same time in nature. The prices are moderate and the service is of high quality. Everywhere cleanliness and order, bedding smells good, excellent cuisine, fresh products. Braziers and tables are enough for everyone. The administration is friendly and very responsive to questions, requests and problems. Chic bath and picturesque surroundings.

Tourists note that when booking cottages and rooms online this place is confused with another recreation center - “Swan Paradise”. It is located in a completely different region of Russia, near the city of Anzhero-Sudzhensk, not far from Kemerovo. This is the urban village of Yaya. This base is also intended for family vacations and corporate events. She stands on the shore of the lake, there are summer houses along the beach and a large building. The base offers fishing, a Siberian bath and various water activities. On the beach you can rent inflatable boats and fishing equipment.
