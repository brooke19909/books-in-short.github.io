Armenia hotels: overview and prices

Many tourists visit Armenia, where you can relax. Not only Yerevan is popular, but also Sevan, Tsaghkadzor, Jermuk. Next, consider the most famous hotels in these cities.

Guest House at Keri street

Average cost per day: about 11,500 rubles.

This hotel in Yerevan (Armenia) is a two-story villa, in the territory of which there is a garden, a swimming pool and everything necessary for barbecue. A metro line is 10 minutes away. The historic city center is 3 km away.

Private parking is available on site as well as free internet. There is a private beach. The building itself is fenced with gates. A shuttle service is available at a surcharge.

The hotel consists of a bathroom, a shared living room, a dining room and an equipped kitchen. There is a playroom for children. The staff speaks Russian, English and French.

Golden Palace Boutique

Average cost per day: about 9 thousand rubles.

This hotel in Armenia is located right in the center of Yerevan. Guest reviews suggest that the staff is welcoming. The interior is as modern as possible. All rooms are comfortably furnished. The bathroom has marble walls. The windows offer a beautiful view of the city.

There is a restaurant on the roof of the building. It serves both Armenian and European cuisine. You can order food and drinks in your room. The complex has a conference room. It is equipped with modern technology. Near the hotel there is a square and metro line. The zoo is 3 km away.

Allowed to come with children. If necessary, you can order a babysitting service.

Cross sevan villa

Average cost per day: about 35 thousand rubles.

The described hotel in Sevan (Armenia) is very popular. A lake is located 10 minutes from it. The complex has a garden and internet. The villa has a balcony with lake, living area, washing machine and TV. The bathroom has a hairdryer for guests.

In the kitchen you can cook your own food. A fridge, stove, microwave and kettle are also provided. Barbecue facilities can be used if desired. A continental breakfast is available. The train station is 4 km from the villa, and the airport is 80 km away.

There is a play area for children. The staff speaks Russian.

Tsovasar

Average cost per day: about 4 thousand rubles.

This hotel in Armenia is located in Sevan. Next to it is the lake of the same name. The rooms are designed for couples. Living room, dining area, bathroom - all of them are as cozy and comfortable as possible. Visitors are allowed to visit the pool, pool, tennis. The hotel organizes city tours. The kitchen has a refrigerator, stove, dishes. If you wish, you can order breakfast in your room.

The monastery is 6 km away, the railway station is 60 km away, and the airport is 67 km away. The staff speaks Russian and English.

Holiday Home 2 On Harutyunyan

Average cost per day: about 60 thousand rubles.

This hotel in Tsaghkadzor (Armenia) is located 1 km from the ski lift of the same name. Private parking and free internet are available on site. The complex has a TV, kitchen, sauna and terrace. The airport is 50 km from the hotel.

You can come with pets. There is a place where skiing is allowed. Housekeeping and laundry services are provided. The staff speaks Russian and English.

Viardo house

Average cost per day: about 42 thousand rubles.

This hotel of Armenia is located in Tsaghkadzor. Next to it are two lifts. If you wish, you can use the dining area and the kitchen, which includes a dishwasher and oven. The hotel has a TV, as well as DVD, CD, Blu-ray players. Private parking is available on site. For guests it is free. The airport is 50 km from the hotel.

A shuttle service is available at a surcharge. The staff speaks English and Russian.

Hyatt place jermuk

Average cost per day: about 5500 rubles.

Located in Jermuk (Armenia), the hotel is very popular. The complex has 92 rooms. They have a TV, kettle, safe, desk, bathroom. Guests have free access to the fitness room and sauna. The hotel has a restaurant, bar and shop. Breakfast is served in the morning.

If you need to conduct negotiations or a seminar, you can use the conference room. The train station is 170 km from the hotel. Nearby there is a church and a beauty salon. Free press delivery. A massage service is available. The jacuzzi is allowed to visit for free. The staff speaks Russian and English.

Nairi

Average cost per day: about 3,500 rubles.

The described hotel in Armenia is located in the city of Jermuk. Can be populated with animals. On the site there is parking.

The rooms have TV, internet and private bathroom. Some have a balcony, bathroom. Guests can enjoy tennis, billiards and bowling. A buffet breakfast is available in the mornings. It is included in the price. There is a cable car 1 km from the hotel, a railway station 106 km, and an airport 114 km.

There is a playground for children. Shoe shine and room cleaning services are provided free of charge. The staff speaks Russian.

Summary

Arriving to rest in Armenia, anyone can find the hotel that will appeal to them. The article describes the most popular options to date. Some complexes may seem too expensive, but the price-quality ratio is maintained at the best level. Therefore, you can safely populate. Most are allowed to come with children and pets.
