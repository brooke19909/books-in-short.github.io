Making exclusive items: how to make a pencil case for a school with your own hands

If you have children who go to school, then you are constantly buying any stationery. And if something can only be purchased at the store, then there are such items that can be made at home. Moreover, things made independently look exclusive and original.





Cardboard cylinder case

There are many workshops on how to make a pencil case for the school with your own hands. However, an unusual organizer can be made from the usual box. It is enough just to paste over its walls with colored paper and place a velvet or leather lining on the bottom.

An organizer for pens and pencils made of fabric and a cardboard cylinder will look more original. Additionally, you will need lightning, a pencil, scissors, threads, a needle and an office knife.





If you do not know how to sew a school pencil case with your own hands, then first cut the cylinder into two parts (one should be longer than the other). The bottom of the cylinder must be circled on fabric and cardboard. Two circles of fabric and thick cardboard should be cut.

To make the base, you will need a piece of fabric, which will be enough to wrap the cardboard part, leaving room for a seam. Next, on the fabric, you need to outline a distance equal to the width of the future cover of the pencil case, make an incision in this place and sew a zipper there.

After this, a piece of fabric needs to be folded in half face inward and stitch the part along the edge. In the resulting cover (without turning out) you need to insert cardboard cylinders, matching them with a zipper.

Cardboard and fabric circles are inserted on both sides of the cylinder and sewed with a needle and thread. When the pencil case is ready, you need to remove it from the cardboard part, turn it on the front side and put it on the cylinder again, gluing the fabric with cardboard.

How to sew a pencil case for school with your own hands from felt

This master class is also not very difficult. Such a product can be made with a child. For work, you need to use sheet felt or a piece of leather. Using an office knife and a ruler, you need to make parallel cuts.

Pencils and pens are inserted into these incisions like rods. The pencil case is rolled up and tied with a ribbon or decorative cord. The edges of the ribbon can be sewn to the details of felt.





Manufacturing instruction

If you do not know how to sew a pencil case for a school with your own hands, but you have a desire to get acquainted with various options for execution, then one more organizer can be made from felt. For its manufacture, you need to prepare a braid and a glue gun. First of all, a rectangular base is cut out of felt, the size of which is determined by spreading on it parallel writing instruments. Between them a little glue is applied, the lower part of the felt is bent and glued between the handles.

Further, colored ribbons are glued on top of the edges for decor, the excess must be cut off. One of the tapes should be longer; it will serve as a tie-in case. When the organizer is ready, the handles are laid out in places, they turn the part into a tube and tie a ribbon.

How to easily make a pencil case for school with your own hands

It turns out that making a school pencil case is faster and easier than it might seem at first glance. Of course, it is hardly suitable for boys, but girls will be delighted with a cute and original little thing.

To make such an organizer for writing instruments yourself, you need to prepare two pieces of fabric 30 by 29 cm, a braid for decoration, a zipper, a non-woven fabric, a ruler and scissors.

Step-by-step instruction for manufacturing a fabric pencil case

From non-woven fabric, you need to cut two 30 by 29 cm rectangles and pin them to two pieces of fabric. In work, you can use adhesive non-woven, which is fixed on the fabric with an iron. If you do not know how to make a pencil case with your own hands from a fabric, the pattern presented below will help you.



One of the pieces of fabric that is brighter will be the front side of the pencil case - it must be decorated with braid. Another fabric will serve as a lining.

Now you need to sew in the zipper. For these purposes, on the side of 30 cm from above, the front tissue must be wrapped inward by 5 mm and ironed. After that, the parts are put in with non-woven inside, the zipper is placed between the lining and facial tissue, wound and stitched on a typewriter. It remains to stitch the pencil case on the remaining sides face inward and turn the organizer on the front side.

Case out of the box

Thinking about how to make a school pencil case with your own hands, do not give up one of the simplest options. To make a beautiful organizer out of the box, decorative paper, glue, stickers, scissors and the box itself will come in handy.

The first step is to pick up boxes of a size that can fit all the student's writing instruments. It needs to be decorated with multi-colored paper with a variety of drawings and stickers with cartoons. The child will be delighted with the bright little thing.
