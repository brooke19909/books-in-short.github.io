Hospitable Turkey. Konakli - a quiet and comfortable resort

Favorable climatic conditions, picturesque landscapes, warm sea, golden beaches, clean mountain air, interesting entertainment programs attract tourists to Turkey. Konakli is a small village with a well-developed infrastructure. It is located just 12 km from the popular town of Alanya and 120 km from Antalya. Seekers of a relaxing holiday will like the village, it provides a unique opportunity to be alone with nature, enjoy the silence, forget about problems and worries.



Turkey recalls its centuries-old history with numerous architectural monuments. Konaklı differs from other resort towns and villages in that it was founded relatively recently and only thanks to tourism. Of all the architectural sights, only the chapel and the mosque deserve special attention. You can relax on a bench near the fountain, visit an Internet cafe, a golf club, a park, stadiums, a caravanserai. All other facilities are 4 and 5-star hotels, entertainment venues. Holidays are usually held near the chapel.

Turkey is open for people of different ages and temperaments. Konakli is more suitable for families, as well as for tourists aged. Here you can stroll along the quiet streets, sit in a cafe or restaurant, enjoy a unique relaxing atmosphere. Every Wednesday for shopping lovers a holiday is held - a huge bazaar unfolds, on which you can buy everything that your heart desires. Small souvenirs, chic rugs, jewelry, and dishes are also sold here. All products are offered at affordable prices.



Turkey also tried to arrange the perfect vacation for young people. Konakli, although it offers more calm and quiet entertainment, compared to other resorts, but here you can find small nightclubs. If you want a greater variety of entertainment programs, you can go to Alanya, located in the neighborhood. Although the hotels of the village provide everything for families with children, the beaches here are not the best. Going down to the sea from the pontoon will be very difficult for kids and people who do not know how to swim.

Previously, only Konakli (Turkey) was strengthened with concrete slabs , hotels here grew like mushrooms after the rain, and many beaches remained concrete. But still sand lovers can find a paradise for themselves here - this is a “turtle beach”. It is located on the outskirts of the village. Tourists like to come to this secluded place surrounded by rocks. The beach got its name due to the fact that earlier here small sea ​​turtles were basking in the sun .



Everyone who wants to be alone with nature, take a break from the noisy and dusty cities, have plenty of fun in the clear sea, you need to come to Konakli. Turkey, whose attractions make you want to relax here every year, takes care of the comfort of its guests. The resort is attractive for its high level of service, developed infrastructure, picturesque landscapes. Everyone who has been here once will want to come again and again.
