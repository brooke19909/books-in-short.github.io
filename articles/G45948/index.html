How to make a clock out of cardboard: a master class

It is not so simple to teach a child to understand time in a clock. But you need to do this. To make the kid interested during such classes, make a visual aid with him - a cardboard clock. With your own hands, invite the baby to make arrows, write numbers. Believe me, with such a developmental toy the child will be happy to do. The master class proposed in this article will tell you how to make a clock out of cardboard for teaching children about time.



Materials and Tools

To work on the manufacture of watch models you will need:

  • thick cardboard in three colors;
  • a compass or two plates;
  • scissors;
  • pencil;
  • bolt with nut;
  • PVA glue;
  • markers;
  • decorative elements.

How to make a clock out of cardboard: a description of the process

  1. On cardboard sheets of different colors, draw two circles with a compass (or circle two plates). The second part should be slightly smaller than the first. Cut them and stick them on top of each other. Try to match the centers of both circles.
  2. Draw arrows on the cardboard of the desired shape and cut them out. If the cardboard is not very tight, then glue it together twice. It is important that this watch part is durable.
  3. Glue a round blank onto a whole sheet of cardboard of rectangular shape. Put something flat and firm on it, leave to dry. This is necessary so that the product does not deform from moisture that the glue gives.
  4. Pierce a hole in the center of the circle, make the same holes on the arrows. Use a small bolt and a nut to attach the arrows to the base of the product.
  5. Markers write numbers from 1 to 12 along the edge of the outer circle. In the future, when the child learns to understand the time by these symbols, you can add values ‚Äã‚Äãfrom 13 to 24 on the side.
  6. Decorate the product as your little Know-it-all wishes. It can be stickers, drawings, applique.


So you know now how to make a clock out of cardboard. This product option is perhaps the simplest and most affordable. With older children, you can perform a different model.

An interesting idea: how to make a clock out of cardboard, and not only out of it?

A watch model with a real movement will really please your child. He will be able to translate the hands and set the time on his own. For the manufacture of such a product will need the following materials:

  • corrugated cardboard;
  • clockwork with hands;
  • plastic caps (from bottles, jars of vitamins, gouache paint boxes) - 12 pieces;
  • glue gun;
  • scissors;
  • pencil.

Step-by-step instruction: how to make a clock out of cardboard

  1. Cut a large circle out of cardboard.
  2. Spread the lids at about the same distance from each other and glue them with a heat gun.
  3. Pierce a hole in the center of the product. This can easily be done with a pencil, because corrugated cardboard is punctured without difficulty.
  4. From the inside, install the clock mechanism, from the outside, fix the hands.
  5. In each cover, write a number with a marker or stick it out of paper.


That's all. The watch is ready. If the mechanism is working, then such a dummy can even correctly show the time and serve not only as a developing toy, but also as usual wall clock in the children's room.
