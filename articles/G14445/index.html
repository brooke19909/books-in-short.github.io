What is the black forest?

Black Forest - a mountain range in Germany, forested, located in the south-west of the country. Its lands rub along the Rhine river from north to south. The highest point of the entire massif is Mount Feldberg. Its height is 1493 meters. The territory of the massif has a rectangular shape, its length is 200 kilometers and its width is 60. The very name Black Forest in translation means black or dark forest. It is believed that the name is of Roman origin. The black forest got its name due to the fact that the dense needles of trees practically do not let light through.

Gastronomic paradise

Going to the Black Forest (Germany) is not only for viewing local beauties, but also for culinary delights. The amazing region is famous not only for resorts, but also for gastronomic delights. Nowhere else will you find so many famous cooks. The Black Forest is known for ham, a stunningly delicious Black Forest cake, spiced beer, sparkling wine, schnapps and mineral water. Museums

The Black Forest has a long history and traditions. On its territory there are small and large museums that everyone can visit. In Gutakh - an open-air museum you can get acquainted with local traditions and way of life. Tourists should watch the largest cuckoo clock in the world in Triberg. If you wish, you can ride a historic steam train through villages and forest glades.

Interesting places

Among the attractions of the Black Forest it is worth highlighting the Mercedes-Benz and Porsche Museum. In addition, tourists should visit the famous Stuttgart ballet. Stunningly interesting castles and temples are located throughout the city.

Mines

In ancient times, mines existed in the Black Forest. They extracted cobalt, silver, and lead. But most attention was paid to ore. It is believed that for this reason the forest was named Black. Currently, the mines are abandoned, so you can always meet tourists here. In some of them you can go down and see the underworld from the inside.

Hohenzollern Castle

The fairytale place of the Black Forest is famous for its historical buildings. Among them are many castles. One of them is the Hohenzollern. The castle delights with its beautiful towers, ramparts, fortifications.

The large building impresses with its appearance. It looks especially majestic in snowfall and fog.

Hochburg

Not only castles are attractive, but also their ruins. Especially if the ruins were formed during long battles. Such is the castle of Hochburg. For a long time he was a real defensive stronghold. It was rebuilt and strengthened several times.

It was finally destroyed by the French. They wanted to restore the castle for a long time, but to this day the fortress remains only ruins.

Waterfalls

The natural attractions of the Black Forest are the All Saints Waterfall and Triberg. Huge streams of water fall down from 150 meters high. The Black Forest authorities highlighted the waterfalls so that tourists can enjoy the natural beauty at night.

The famous Danube River originates from the mountain rivers of the Black Forest. The Black Forest is an amazing and fabulous place that is breathtaking.
