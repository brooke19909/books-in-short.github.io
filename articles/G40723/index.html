Decals on clothes - a new life of your favorite things

What to do if your clothes have deteriorated for some reason? For example, they put a stain on wine or a baby fell unsuccessfully and tore new jeans. Really throw things away? Of course not. Today there are new modern technologies that will help preserve things or just create a new design. From the article you will learn how to stick decal on clothes.

Fabric stickers

Want to add a twist to your things or hide a noticeable defect on them? The right option is a decal on clothes. As a rule, special stickers are made of thick fabric, and on the back there is an adhesive layer. Most often they are used to hide defects in clothing.

For work you need: ironing board, iron and cotton cloth. First you need to remove the protective layer, which is located on the back of the sticker. Then put the iron on the ironing board, but make sure that there is no water in it and the steam function is turned off. Otherwise, you will ruin your clothes. Turn on the iron and let it heat. It must be very hot.

Put the item you need on the ironing board. It must be thoroughly smoothed with your hands so that there are no wrinkled areas. In the place where the application will be, put the sticker without a protective film. Cover with a single layer of cotton cloth . If you glue jeans, then you can do without additional material.





You need to do this procedure very carefully, as the sticker may shift. At this point, iron thoroughly. If the sticker has not yet been stuck, do not stop. Iron until you see the result.

Decal rhinestone on clothes

It is not always necessary to hide a defect in things. Very often you just want to decorate and update your clothes, give them a new, original design that no one has. For this, rhinestones are used. Thanks to them, you will add chic and shine to your appearance.

Strass decals on clothes are made a little more complicated than fabric ones. Many are sure that this is a delicate and even jewelry work that requires perseverance and attentiveness.

Before gluing rhinestones with an iron, first try on a small shred so as not to spoil your thing. Then go directly to your clothes.





Put the item on the ironing board and heat the iron well. Do not forget that there should not be water in it. Then put clothes on the board. On the area where you plan to glue the rhinestones, put a piece of fabric between them. This is necessary so that the glue does not spoil your thing. And then lay out the rhinestones. Cover them with a small shred, preferably wet. Now put the iron on the rhinestones and hold it for about 12 seconds. During this time, hot melt will melt. If you use large jewelry, then you need to hold the iron for at least 30 seconds.

After decals of rhinestones on clothes are ready, leave the products for 1 hour. Do not touch things. Wait for the rhinestones to cool well. Now you can be calm, as you should get a beautiful, festive and bright outfit.

Advice

Decals on clothes do not require much time and effort. But still there are some secrets that every needlewoman should know. Before you do the applique, find out what kind of fabric your garment has. Be sure to look at the label. If a crossed iron is painted on it, then such a product cannot be ironed, as the fabric may deteriorate.

Decals on clothes with rhinestones are more delicate. When holding the iron on the products, do not press on it.





Lightly touch the fabric surface. Under the hot iron glue from rhinestones will begin to spread, respectively, and stick to the product.
