The resort city of Kerch: the beaches of "Moscow", "Youth", "Traps"

This beautiful and very green city is conveniently located on the shore of the Kerch Strait, between the Black and Azov Seas, on the Crimean Peninsula. The former capital of the Bosporan kingdom, the modern city of Kerch is very popular. This is the largest industrial settlement of the peninsula, and most importantly, the famous Crimean resort, which attracts millions of vacationers.





Relaxation

Kerch is one of the oldest cities on the peninsula, because here you can easily combine a beach holiday with sightseeing and educational. The city and its environs are full of natural, cultural and historical monuments. Among them are Mount Mithridates and the Great Mithridates Stairs, excavations of the ancient city of Panticapaeum and a more modern historical monument - the obelisk of Glory.

You can visit Adzhimushkaysky quarries, which impress all guests. Fans of religious buildings will also not be disappointed by the city of Kerch. Here is the oldest church on the peninsula of St. John the Baptist, a recognized monument of Byzantine architecture.

Well, for everyone who came to rest with children and just wants to unwind, we recommend you to go to the Exotic Ostrich Breeding Farm or visit the unique museum of marine fauna and flora.

Kerch beaches

And although there are really many attractions in the city, tourists are usually very interested in the beaches of Kerch (reviews confirm this). In the city and its immediate surroundings there are both well-equipped coastal areas with well-developed infrastructure, and almost wild, where people who are tired of the hustle and bustle of the big city like to relax.

Kerch: city beach

In 2011, the city beach was renovated, and it was given the name Sun Dali. It should be noted that he was among the twenty best beaches of the peninsula. But in the Crimea there are over 500 of them.

Sun Dali is located on the outskirts of the city, so it is better to go by taxi. According to the townspeople, this is the most equipped and attractive beach of the city. Here for vacationers there are showers with fresh water, changing cabins, toilets. There is a cafe-bar on site.



Most guests arriving in Kerch must visit the city beach. It is very wide, sandy, with a flat bottom. Guests can enjoy a variety of water activities, and you can rent everything you need for water sports. Inflatable slides and other attractions are installed on the beach, which will be interesting not only for children but also for adults.
