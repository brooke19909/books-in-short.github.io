Game rating for Android: list of the most popular

When your favorite game is on your phone and available at any desired time, it becomes more than just entertainment. Games come in different categories, and therefore comparing them with each other will be an illogical and controversial matter - each game has its own audience. Some games can gather an army of fans around them, while others can unite strangers from around the world with a single idea. However, there are games that have become popular among generations, regardless of age, status and preference. The following is an overview of the games for Android, which have won universal love, and their advantages and disadvantages are described.

Pubg mobile

This game in the Royal Battle genre deserves first place not only in its own way, but also in the general list of games for Android. In a short time, at the end of 2018, she received the highest download rating among the rest. It is based on the PC game of the same name. Combines the tactics and freedom of action of war and "survival".

100 unarmed players land on a large island. The main goal is to survive. A dangerous field periodically swallows the island, taking life from those who failed to cross the exclusion zone. Players are forced to fight each other in a tapering safe zone to become a winner.

Gameplay Features:

  1. View from the first or third person.
  2. In addition to the single player competition is offered in a squad of four and two players with voice and print chats.
  3. Dozens of types of land and water transport.
  4. Large selection of rifles, rifles, machine guns.
  5. Different ways to restore health - bandages, first aid kits, adrenaline and so on.
  6. 4 maps with different terrain: desert, forest, jungle and snow.
  7. Several arcade game modes.
  8. Constant updates and incentives from the developers in the form of coloring weapons and equipment.
  9. In-game tournaments, lineups of achievements, championships and competitions for the holidays.

Advantages:

  • Open world.
  • The unpredictability of the gameplay.
  • Game in a team.
  • Great graphics.
  • Donat does not give the player advantages in battle.

Disadvantages:

  • Great requirements for the technical data of the smartphone.
  • Weak internet negatively affects the process.
  • High battery consumption.
  • Long classic fights.
  • The presence of bots.
  • The presence of bugs and cheats.

Asphalt 8: airborne

Asphalt 8 takes the highest place in the ranking of games on the "Android" of this line. The game was created back in 2013, but it does not lag behind modern variations.

Gameplay Features:

  1. More than 220 cars and motorcycles of different classes with the possibility of tuning.
  2. A variety of game modes.
  3. Large selection of maps and routes in the most beautiful cities in the world.
  4. Performing certain tasks for additional incentives from developers.
  5. There is the possibility of online racing with players from different countries, as well as from their social networks.
  6. Often in the game tournaments and championships are held, which gives additional motivation to users.

Advantages:

  • Great graphics and soundtrack.
  • Large selection of cars and tracks.
  • Network races.

Disadvantages:

  • It is difficult to rise without a donut.
  • Lack of storyline.
  • The availability of mods.

Terraria

The game in the sandbox genre was originally created for the PC, but after a while it was released specifically for Android, IOS and Windows Phone. To date, the latest version of Terraria on Android is available on the Play Market and has over 10 million downloads.

The game is a platformer, the main purpose of which is development and entertainment. Many people compare it with Minecraft to Android, but although their genres are very similar, the purpose of each game is different.

Gameplay Features:

  1. Create your own, unique character.
  2. Exploration of dungeons and maps, of which the game has more than 25.
  3. Find artifacts, get resources, fight bosses and transform your world.
  4. Dynamic change of day and night.
  5. The game has a huge amount of weapons, pets and enemies.

Advantages:

  • An unpredictable plot.
  • An interesting adventure simulator.
  • Open world.

Disadvantages:

  • Pixel graphics in 2D.
  • The game is not just paid, but expensive.
  • Promotion is influenced by luck rather than experience.

World war heroes

World War Heroes on Android is a team shooter and talks about the Second World War. Weapons, uniforms, military equipment - all the details of the game thoroughly convey the atmosphere of that time.

Gameplay Features:

  1. Locations of the seven most high-profile and significant battles of the Second World War.
  2. 7 game modes and the ability to create your own rooms for the match according to your own rules.
  3. More than 55 types of weapons and the ability to customize them.
  4. The ability to choose the country for which you want to fight and your shaky.
  5. Developers constantly update maps, weapons, conduct various tournaments and various promotions for players.

Advantages:

  • The ability to level the hero without donat.
  • The use of military equipment.
  • Quality graphics.
  • Team game.

Disadvantages:

  • The availability of mods.
  • Small capacity relative to each type of inventory.
  • Weapons cost a large amount of in-game currency.

Wolrd of tanks blitz

This game is an MMO action game with online PVP battles. It owes its popularity to its variation of the same name on a PC. She occupies a high place in the ranking of games for Android. The battle in it is built on the basis of the tactics and strategy of the players, which does not predict the outcome of the battle that takes place on the battlefield of World War II.

The main point of the game is to keep the central zone at your disposal for 100 seconds, or to destroy the opponent. The battle takes place between two teams of 7 players.

Gameplay Features:

  1. There is an opportunity to play for Russia, the USA and other countries.
  2. More than 90 types of tanks that you can pump and explore, change its guns and colors.
  3. More than 23 gaming locations with a unique landscape and design.
  4. In-game tournaments and competitions.

Advantages:

  • Good graphics.
  • Lots of servers.
  • Constant play with partners.
  • Short fights.

Disadvantages:

  • Donat gives an advantage over other players.
  • Dear Donat.
  • The relief of randomly occupied places on the map affects the outcome of the battle.

Township

The game was first launched on Facebook, but managed to fall in love with many in a short time. That is why Township soon appeared on Android, IOS and other platforms.

The game is a mixed simulator of a farm and a city, it starts with a ready-made playing field with various buildings that need to be improved and the rest of the city built up with infrastructure.

Gameplay Features:

  1. The ability to build cafes, restaurants, zoos, farms and other buildings.
  2. Receiving raw materials, their sale, processing.
  3. Study of the city and its bowels.
  4. Construction of architectural monuments and structures.
  5. You can build buildings, grow and process products.

Advantages:

  • Great simulator.
  • Nice interface and graphics.
  • The presence of an assistant.

Disadvantages:

  • Lack of motivation in passing.

FIFA

FIFA - football on Android. The game has made a splash like on other consoles and in real life it has over 100 million downloads. The game requires a constant connection to the Internet, which means that it allows players to compete among themselves constantly.

Gameplay Features:

  1. Game against each other in real time.
  2. Creating your own team of world famous players.
  3. More than 550 football teams.
  4. The opportunity to participate in world tournaments.
  5. Several game modes.

Advantages:

  • Several types of management.
  • Good graphics.

Disadvantages:

  • Requires permanent internet.
  • Big size.

Subway surfers

Subway Surfers on Android broke the Play market record with 1 billion downloads. A simple interface and a relaxed atmosphere make it possible to just relax and spend time behind the usual legwork to meet the unknown.

The main point of the game is to run away as far as possible from the guard, who is trying to catch the player. The character‚Äôs route passes along rails, on which you need to dodge standing cars, as well as their oncoming directions.

  1. A set of characters that can replace the current one.
  2. Collect coins, which subsequently make it possible to unlock new characters and buy boxes and power-ups.
  3. Venues of the game very often change and take place in the most beautiful cities in the world.
  4. When connected to the Internet, it is possible to complete weekly promotional tasks.

Clash of clans

Clash of Clans on Android is a mix of Scandinavian-style farm and tactical strategy. The main goal is development and conquest. The fights in this game take place in the present tense.

The game begins with an empty field that needs to be filled with infrastructure. The city can be surrounded by walls, crammed with traps and put on the tower defense: guns and mortars. Resources are extracted from mines and wells. Every city must have a town hall building.

Gameplay Features:

  1. Improving the quality of buildings due to the constant update of developers.
  2. Clan games along with teammates are rewarded with magic items.
  3. An army with different types of warriors, which is being improved as the game progresses.
  4. Updates allow players to take advantage of new opportunities.

Advantages:

  • Frequent updates.
  • Simple graphics.
  • You can play without donat.

Minuses:

  • Difficult perception at first.
  • Imbalance between players.
  • Long pumping.

Warhammer 40,000: Freeblade

Despite its absence in the ratings of games on Android, the game should not be overlooked. It is built on the basis of the fictional universe of a board game. She has a huge number of followers, a low-budget film from fans, dozens of books, games on different platforms and much more.

40 levels of the game are built around the history of the Imperial Knight, whose brothers were killed and the house destroyed. Now, being in the service of the Dark Angels, he takes revenge on his wrongdoers. The unusualness of the game lies in the fact that the sequence of actions is more important here - the game itself moves the character, you only need to set priorities on time and attack them in time.

Gameplay Features:

  1. Obtaining weapons and armor for donat, through the passage of missions and levels.
  2. The ability to increase the quality of armor and weapons by improving it, or by merging from several other things.
  3. Daily awarded assignments. One-on-one tournaments (not occurring in the present tense). The victory of the player is determined by his own skills, and not the strength of the enemy.
  4. Customization of your knight.

Advantages:

  • Graphics.
  • Customization of the knight.
  • Daily bonuses and tasks.

Disadvantages:

  • Monotonous gameplay.
  • The player only shoots. The computer is responsible for the movements.
  • PvP battles do not occur in the present tense.
  • After completing the story, motivation is lost.

Minecraft

Minecraft on Android was created after it was widely recognized on the PC. Three-dimensional game in the genre of "sandbox" is an open world, which allows the player to fully use his creative thinking.

Interface Features:

  1. The game has 2 modes: creative mode and survival.
  2. The player can collect their own buildings and weapons from found blocks and materials.
  3. The change of night and day.
  4. Experience in mining and crafting things.

Advantages:

  • Great scope for imagination.
  • Gameplay in 3D.

Disadvantages:

  • Pixel graphics.
  • Lack of plot.

Mortal combat x

This game began its journey with arcade slot machines, but after a while it gained a large number of fans and received not only universal recognition, but also an honorable place in the ranking of games on Android.

The campaign mode map is divided into towers, in each of which the player expects 6-8 enemies. You can take 3 of the available fighters to the tower with you.

Gameplay Features:

  1. Over 50 characters in the form of cards.
  2. All familiar combo attacks, special hits and the accumulation of rage.
  3. The ability to change the fighter during the battle.
  4. The ability to pump each fighter, his equipment.
  5. Bonus missions with breaking items are given.
  6. Network mode.
  7. X-ray is given after lvl 20.

Advantages:

  • Large selection of characters and their variations in their appearance.
  • Network mode.

Disadvantages:

  • Boring swipe based gameplay.
  • Monotonous and short storyline.

Conclusion

As you can see, most games are based on open world, team play and rivalry. Perhaps, along with graphics, these are the main reasons for their popularity. Many of these games made it possible to unlock the potential of users and realize their fantasies.
