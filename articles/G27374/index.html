Mortgage insurance: how and where it is cheaper to get

When applying for mortgage lending, one of the main conditions of almost any bank is the execution of health and life insurance. If a bank client refuses to prepare this paper, the interest rate per annum can be significantly increased. Therefore, it is much more profitable to go on the terms of the bank. Is it mandatory to take out health insurance

Before filling out loan obligations with a bank, it will be useful to find out what things need to be done and what procedures can be legally abandoned. For example, it is not necessary to draw up a policy that will guarantee that he will not be able to repay the loan in the process of the borrower's painful condition.





It is also worth considering that no credit institution has any legal right to impose such a service. Accordingly, any borrower has the right:

  • refuse to take out insurance;
  • agree to these terms;
  • conclude an appropriate agreement, but terminate it unilaterally immediately after he receives a loan.

The latter option is not a scam, as these are legal actions by a citizen who wants to get a loan.

However, it must be understood that without insurance in the event of default on the loan, all legal costs will be incurred only by the borrower. This can be avoided. If the insurance is formalized, then financial claims will be presented to both the insurer and the borrower. Therefore, it helps to protect yourself from unnecessary financial costs in case of emergency.

Based on this, it is recommended to choose where mortgage insurance is cheaper and what conditions the UK offers.

What problems can life insurance solve?
