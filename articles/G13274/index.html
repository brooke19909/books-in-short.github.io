Package from Beeline "All for 400": a detailed description of the offer

Today, almost everyone has a mobile phone: from preschool children to older people. And this entails a huge demand for the services of telecom operators. There is a demand - there must be a supply. And there are a lot of them!

Wanting to get ahead of their competitors, many operators offer profitable “package” services. They mean the provision of a certain number of minutes, SMS messages and traffic at a low cost. Beeline did not stand aside. He offered a rather interesting and economical product called "All for 400." This article will be devoted to its detailed consideration, connection methods and other important points proposed by the Beeline operator.





All for 400: Product Details

As you can understand by the name of the tariff, the monthly subscription fee for using it is 400 rubles. This price includes:

  • Non-charged calls within the network;
  • 400 free minutes for talking with subscribers of other telecom operators;
  • 2 Gb high speed internet;
  • 100 SMS messages within the network.

If the subscriber will use the provided “package” from Beeline “All for 400” earlier than a month from the moment of connecting the service, then he will have to pay extra for all outgoing calls and SMS messages. Moreover, the first service for numbers of other operators within the home region will cost 1.6 rubles per minute, and the second - 2 rubles apiece. But such prices are not relevant for calls to numbers of “competitors” operators, and calls to numbers within the network outside of Moscow and the Moscow Region. In this case, the subscriber will have to "fork out" for much more money. Their exact cost will be described below.





Tariff benefits

To make it easier for the reader to decide on the need to connect a tariff from Beeline “All for 400”, its main advantages should be given:

  • Big cost savings. Indeed, for only 400 rubles a month, a subscriber receives unlimited calls within the network, 400 minutes to the numbers of other operators, 100 free SMS, 2GB of fast Internet.
  • Time saving. It is enough for the user to put the required amount once a month and for the whole 30 days forget about the need to replenish the balance of the phone number.

It should be noted that not every telecom operator is ready to offer such a benefit. Beeline, rather, is one of the representatives who was able to present the most attractive product for residents of Moscow and the Moscow region.





Internet traffic charging

As already noted, the tariff from Beeline "All for 400" provides for the provision of free 2 GB of traffic. Its “accrual" is made once a month at the beginning of the billing period. Their subscriber can be freely spent within the local connection.

When the proposed "package" is used up, the service "Auto-renew speed" is automatically activated. It means providing an additional 150 MB of traffic for only 20 rubles. In addition to the subscriber, other ways of extending traffic are available, namely connecting the service “Prolong speed” or “Highway”.

Billing of other services

What awaits the consumer when the “package” from Beeline “All for 400” is used up in relation to the rest of the services provided: calls and SMS messages. In this case, debiting for “over” calls and SMS will be charged at the following rate:

  • Calls to numbers of other operators within the home region: 1.6 rubles / minute;
  • Calls within the network are free;
  • Calls to numbers of other operators outside the home region (in addition to the Republic of Crimea and the city of Sevastopol) - from 5 rubles / minute;
  • Calls to Kyivstar numbers in Crimea and Sevastopol - 12-24 rubles / minute;
  • Calls to numbers within the network to the CIS countries and Georgia - from 5 rubles, to other countries - from 24 rubles;
  • Sending SMS messages will cost 2 rubles, MMS - 7.95 rubles apiece.




Connection of the tariff "All for 400"

Beeline (the product description has already been described in detail above) allows you to perform this action in several ways:

  • By making a call to 067410260. Next, you will need to act according to the actions specified in the instructions;
  • Change the tariff on the official website of the company, after having logged into your Personal Account;
  • Order a SIM card with a connected tariff in the online store of the company;
  • Contact the operator’s office with this question.

It should be noted that before changing the tariff it is necessary to ensure that there is at least 155 rubles in the account. This is because the subscriber will have to pay a commission of 150 rubles for the procedure itself.

Finally, the reader will be correctly informed about how you can check the remnants of “free” minutes, SMS messages and traffic. You can do this very simply and quickly, simply by typing the following combination of numbers on your phone: 06745. As a result, the Beeline telecom operator’s client will receive all the necessary information.

Most likely, many will be interested in such an offer from Beeline. 400 rubles per month for a lot of free minutes and SMS - this is practically nothing compared to the offers of other operators!
