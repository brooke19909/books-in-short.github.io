Hotel Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort: description, reviews, photos

Thousands of tourists annually choose Egypt as their vacation spot. This country is famous not only for its warm sea and picturesque beaches, but also for its ancient culture and luxurious resorts. One of the most popular tourist areas is Hurghada. It is here that the large hotel complex called Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort is located.

Of course, before booking travelers try to get as much useful data as possible about a particular place. Where exactly is the hotel located? What level of service can you expect? What quality of accommodation can a hotel provide? What do tourists think, having already managed to spend some time here?

Description of territory and infrastructure





The hotel complex Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort 4 * is considered quite large - its area is 61,000 square meters. In the center is a multi-story building, built in the form of a cruise ship. The entire territory of the hotel is well-groomed and properly equipped - it is always a pleasure to spend time here. Pools, well-groomed walking paths, sports fields and terraces for relaxation, exotic flowers and tall palm trees, secluded arbors - all this will give your vacation an unforgettable charm.

By the way, the Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort is part of the popular Primasol hotel chain. It has been successfully hosting tourists from all over the world since 2005. By the way, every year in the hotel there are various innovations, so today it meets all international standard requirements.

What type of food does the hotel offer?

For many travelers, the issue of nutrition is very important. The Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort hotel complex operates according to the all-inclusive system generally accepted in Egypt. Three times a day, buffet tables are organized for guests at the main restaurant. By the way, the room here is spacious enough to accommodate all vacationers. The menu here is quite varied, as the cooks every day try to please the guests with something new, and the dishes are always fresh and really tasty. This is what the guests say in their reviews.





In addition, the hotel has several a la carte restaurants that offer their visitors dishes from around the world. By the way, you can visit each of them once for free, but only after pre-booking the table with the administrator. And during the day you can get light snacks, delicious desserts, chilled drinks and alcohol in one of the local bars. The hotel also offers homemade ice cream and freshly squeezed juices, but they need to be paid separately.

Beach and water recreation for guests

The Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort hotel complex is located on the second line. Nevertheless, he owns his own section of the beach, which is located about 800 meters. This distance can be overcome independently. In addition, there is a bus every 15 minutes that takes tourists to the beach.





The sea here is warm, with a comfortable entry. There is a small pontoon. Despite the fact that the beach here is sandy, because of the proximity of the coral reef, travelers are advised to use special swimming shoes. Naturally, on the shore there is a sufficient number of sunbeds, sun umbrellas. There is also a bar where you can get drinks and a snack.

If you are a supporter of a more active holiday, then here you are sure to find yourself an occupation. On the shore there are rental points for catamarans, boats and other non-motorized water transport. For a reasonable price, guests can also ride a boat, water ski, jet ski, etc. A scuba diving center is constantly working, where you can rent the necessary equipment or take short training courses before the first dive.

Family holidays with children

Of course, today many people prefer to relax with the whole family. Therefore, many travelers are interested in the question of the existence of certain conditions for living with a child. So what can the hotel complex Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort offer? Guest reviews indicate that this place is the best suited for children.





For starters, it's worth talking about amenities. At the reception you can request an extra bed in the room, and in the restaurant you can get a high chair (these services are completely free). From a fairly extensive daily menu, you can choose something useful for the baby.

But in terms of entertainment, the hotel complex Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort will seem like a real paradise for a child. After all, there is a huge water park with slides for every taste. Children can choose attractions depending on age and wishes. In addition, there are three spacious children's pools of different depths, which are heated in cool weather.

In addition, the hotel has a well-equipped playground with swings, where children can have fun. And, of course, every day they are expected at the kids club, where they offer to do art and dance, take part in fun games and competitions.

Additional service on site





Of course, the hotel tried to create all conditions for relaxation, not overshadowed by domestic problems. For a reasonable price, you can use the laundry and dry cleaning services. The hotel has a constantly working medical office, where in case of problems you will be examined by a qualified doctor.

There is also a currency exchange point. Those who wish can rent a car, which is then conveniently left in the hotel parking. In addition, a shopping arcade has been organized here, where at an affordable price you can buy almost everything, from household trifles and small souvenirs to branded clothes and jewelry.

Hotel complex Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort 4 * (Hurghada): entertainment and leisure

The hotel is perfect for a truly relaxing stay. On its territory there are several pools, one of which is equipped with a wave simulation system. Naturally, the most striking attraction of the complex is the water park, whose area is 15 thousand square meters. By the way, it is rightfully considered the largest in Hurghada. There are slides and attractions for every taste, including even a river with a whirlpool.





Here you can play billiards or have a good time on the tennis court. There are several sports fields and a gym for those who prefer to always stay in shape. Various group exercises are regularly held, including water aerobics.

During the day, guests are entertained by a team of fun animators. And in the evenings you will find real shows and unforgettable performances. Those who wish can attend parties and discos, which are often organized at night. And, of course, you can go on excursions, the choice of which is really great here.

SPA center and its services

The hotel owns a large spa center, which must be visited at least once. Here you can visit a real bathhouse or take a steam in a more familiar sauna. Also in the center there are several Jacuzzis. And guests are offered several types of massage, including anti-cellulite, sports, relaxing, as well as massage of the pharaohs. Here you can sign up for several aromatherapy sessions and some cosmetic procedures. Prices for services here are quite reasonable.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

As you can see from the description, this hotel complex is designed primarily for families with children of different ages, as the place is ideal for both kids and older children. On the other hand, youth companies as well as older tourists often stop here. Fans of a relaxing beach holiday will love it here. But, of course, there are other categories of tourists who will feel comfortable at the Dessole Titanic Aqua Park Resort. Hurghada is a suitable city for lovers of excursions. And here from time to time various holidays are held, including weddings.
