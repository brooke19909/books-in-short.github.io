Navigator TeXet TN-515DVR - universal media combine

In this article, we will consider the TeXet TN-515DVR GPS navigator, which successfully combined a positioning device and a DVR.

Appearance of the device and mount

To manufacture the device case, the manufacturer used good soft plastic. This can be instantly felt after removing the protective shell from the navigator. In the hands of this model does not slip, which simplifies fixing the device to the windshield of the vehicle. Another distinctive feature of the navigator is its accurate execution. The device is equipped with a connector for connecting an AV cable and a headset in the left end part. In addition, the navigator has a miniUSB connector and a place for a microSD memory card.





At the top end of the equipment is an input for connecting a power cable and another slot for installing a memory card. By the way, it will be said that it is on a card located in the upper honor of the device that a video about the situation on the road will subsequently be recorded. The lens of the camcorder is located on the back of the device, and nearby are the speaker of the speaker, a pencil case with a stylus and a stub.

On the glass, the portable navigator is fixed by means of a bracket. This device is made of high-quality durable plastic with high-quality articulation.

About the menu and display

The main menu of the navigator consists of eight sections and is almost the same as that of other devices of the manufacturer. The only difference is the presence of an additional ‚ÄúDVR‚Äù icon.

Among the advantages of the menu, you can highlight the presence of a currency converter, a convenient calendar, as well as an engineering calculator. In addition, there are as many as six gaming applications. It is also worth noting that the menu icons are large enough and convenient for pressing.





The TeXet is equipped with a five-inch display, while it is widescreen. It should be noted a sufficient margin of brightness control, as well as convenient control settings. In general, the display makes a positive impression of its work. Of course, the viewing angles of the screen are inferior to other more expensive devices like the Samsung Galaxy Tab. Nevertheless, they are good enough for such an inexpensive model.

Among the other advantages of the screen can be noted good sensitivity when in contact with a finger or stylus. The quality of the photos is poor. But do not forget that we are talking about a GPS-navigator, and not about the camera.

On my way

The icon of the navigation application is called "Go". By clicking on this menu item, the Navitel application is launched. If necessary, you can install other navigation programs or TeXet navigator maps on the device. It is worth noting that Navitel is regularly improved and supplemented, it is possible to add various maps depending on the country in which the motorist will use the navigator.

Of the few shortcomings of this device, the lack of a Bluetooth interface can be distinguished, which makes it impossible to obtain the necessary information about traffic jams. By the way, it will be said that this TeXet navigator is equipped with a GPS receiver of increased sensitivity. This device has shown its effectiveness on the roads between high-rise buildings, when the horizon view does not allow to receive a signal from a large number of satellites. The same goes for work in the forest.

Video recording

The advantage of this device is the presence of a built-in DVR. You can start shooting through the navigator menu or by pressing the button in the upper end part of the case. Recording is in VGA format. In this case, the recorded clips are saved on the inserted memory card. By the way, it will be emphasized that each video carries information about the date and time of recording. The quality of video shooting is at a fairly good level, however, it will be possible to consider car numbers only at close range.





In conclusion, I would like to note that the TeXet TN-515DVR GPS navigator is an inexpensive solution that has an undeniable advantage in the form of a complete set with an integrated video recorder.
