A bit of development history





The founder of the company, he is also its owner, an engineer and designer from England named James Dyson, really, thought about the importance of creating a powerful and convenient vacuum cleaner back in the distant 1970s. He was annoyed by the then design of these devices, or rather, the presence in them of terribly inconvenient and quickly clogging dust bags. Already in 1978, he realized that it was possible to upgrade a vacuum cleaner using a phenomenon such as a cyclone. The result of such thoughts was his first device model. It was equipped with a cyclone system that performs the function of dust absorption. The first Dyson vacuum cleaner, reviews of which were positive, was able to maintain unchanged retraction force. At the same time, he still managed to delay dust particles.

As a result, the invention created by Dyson was later called Root Cyclone. In today's assortment of these devices, one can distinguish such models as: Dyson Allergy, Dyson Animal, as well as the Dyson Digital Slim vacuum cleaner and Dyson All Floors. All of them are very popular.
