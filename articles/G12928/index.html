Classification

To make such a list, you must immediately clarify what is meant by quality and what are the evaluation criteria. The most important judge in this distribution is always the buyer. The technical characteristics indicated by the manufacturer do not always correspond to the real indicators. Therefore, all that the user is talking about is the main reason to place the model at one or another step.

As usual, the most sensational was the race between Samsung and Apple. The developers tried to get ahead of each other once again and release smartphones faster.
