Dusit D2 Resort Phuket: hotel description and reviews

Of course, over the past few years, Thailand has become a fairly popular place for a beach holiday. Here, travelers will find the gentle sea, the breathtaking beauty of nature, luxury hotels and, of course, sincere hospitality. One of the rather popular stopping places is the Dusit D2 Resort Phuket. So what can a tourist who has chosen this hotel as a vacation destination expect?

Where is the hotel located? Description of the Territory





A small but cozy hotel Dusit D2 Phuket Resort 4 * is located in Phuket, one of the most popular resort cities in the country. This is a peaceful place in the southern part of Patong Beach, very close to the sea coast. The international airport is located about 40 km, but you should not worry about this, as tourists are usually provided with a transfer. Nevertheless, it is worth preparing for the move.

The hotel itself is a beautiful building built in an interesting colonial style. The area around is small, but very beautiful - everywhere there are dense crowns of trees, bright exotic bushes, elegant paving stones, in a word, there is everything in order to feel like in a fairy tale.

Hotel Dusit D2 Phuket Resort 4 * (Phuket): brief description of rooms





This hotel is considered quite large and consists of 383 rooms of various categories, including:

  • 283 Deluxe Rooms;
  • 113 Premier Rooms;
  • 16 Studio Suites;
  • 10 One Bedroom Suites;
  • 13 Premier Pool Access Rooms.

All rooms are quite spacious, consisting of at least a sleeping and living area. Some have private rooms and even fully equipped kitchenettes. Regardless of the category of apartments, guests can count on comfort, a pleasant design and cleanliness.

Naturally, there is the necessary equipment for a comfortable stay. You can spend your free time watching TV (mainly local channels are shown here, although there are some satellite and even Russian ones). The air conditioner is working properly, so you can maintain a temperature that is comfortable for you. There is a safe, telephone and minibar, which is filled with drinking water. But additional drinks in the room will be delivered only at the request of the tenant. Other guests are provided with everything necessary for making coffee and tea.

The bathroom is quite spacious and clean, and the plumbing is serviceable and modern. At your service are a shower cabin (in some rooms a bathroom), a hair dryer, a washbasin, a set of towels and high-quality cosmetics.

Can I eat at the hotel?





Since the issue of nutrition is important for many travelers, it is worth stopping at this point. It should be said right away that the Dusit D2 Phuket Resort hotel provides its guests with breakfast. The beautiful restaurant serves a daily buffet. By the way, the menu is quite diverse, as evidenced by the reviews. You will find an abundance of snacks, delicious soups, vegetable dishes, fruits and unsurpassed desserts.

You can also eat at the rest of the day in the restaurant, however, for an additional fee. You can enjoy superbly prepared international and Asian dishes. In addition, there are two bars on the territory, one of which is located on the roof, from where you can enjoy not only drinks, but also breathtaking sea views. There is also a cafe that works around the clock - here visitors are offered snacks, aromatic coffee and quality drinks.

Where is the beach located? Are there opportunities for water recreation?





Dusit D2 Resort Phuket is located very close to the beach - the distance to the popular Patong Beach is only 200 meters, so you can walk here in a few minutes. Since the beach is public, you will need to pay for the rental of sunbeds, mattresses, umbrellas, beach towels . The coast and the seabed are covered with soft sand, the water is warm all year round. However, according to reviews, sometimes on the beach it is crowded.

There will be an opportunity to do water sports. This beach is quite popular among fans of windsurfing, sailing. You can also ride a boat, water skiing, boats, catamarans, in short, have fun.

Additional services for guests

Of course, guests of the Dusit D2 Phuket Resort (ex Destination Patong) can expect some additional services. For example, the necessary little things and original souvenirs can be bought at a local store. There will also be no problems with local money - currency can be exchanged at the hotel, as well as in the city, in particular, in jewelry shops, banks, pharmacies. Tourists can also use the parking lot. By the way, rented transport is the most popular and cheapest way to move around the area without limiting yourself in time and distance. The hotel also has laundry and dry cleaning services, whose services are not so expensive.





Dusit D2 Resort Phuket is also focused on business travelers. The hotel has several meeting rooms. There is a business center with the necessary equipment, a spacious conference room, as well as constant access to the Internet, which is very important for many travelers. By the way, for a small fee, employees can quickly organize almost any event, from small business meetings to happy holidays and even weddings.

Leisure and entertainment





Hotel Dusit D2 Resort Phuket is primarily intended for beach holidays. Nevertheless, on its territory there are some conditions for an interesting pastime. For example, guests can have fun by the outdoor pool in the courtyard. Another pool is located on the roof of the residential building - it offers a breathtaking view of the sea waters and the surrounding area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city.

There is also a fitness center where tourists can use the gym, as well as attend some group classes. There is also a sauna and several massage rooms where you can try on yourself a real oriental massage.

Separately, it is worth mentioning children's amenities, including a high chair and special dishes in the restaurant, an extra bed. Children can have fun at the playground with a swing or in the games room. There is also a children's pool with clean water.

Hotel Dusit D2 Phuket Resort 4 *: tourist reviews

If you talk with tourists who have already managed to visit a particular place, you can get a lot of useful information. So what are they saying about the Dusit D2 Phuket Resort? Travelers leave positive reviews, as the hotel is really good. Beautiful territory, exquisite design and interior decoration, modern furniture and working appliances - this is enough for a really comfortable pastime.

As for nutrition, it is not a problem here. Breakfasts are delicious and portions are large. Dishes ordered by the restaurant are also good, and finding a cozy cafe with a unique menu is not a problem in the city. The service is also good, the staff are smiling and welcoming. In short, the hotel is perfect for a relaxing beach or an interesting excursion vacation.
