Ostankino Tower

The television center in Ostankino was built simultaneously with the tower in the period from 1963 to 1967. The center was named after the 50th anniversary of the October Revolution. That year, the half-century date from the day of the 1917 revolution was just celebrated.





Designing a telecentre began during the reign of Khrushchev. It was planned to rebuild 26 floors, but approved the project with 13 floors. Initially, they wanted to place it on the Lenin Hills, since this is the highest point in Moscow. But later it was decided to build a center in Ostankino, there was enough space for it.

In November 1967, the construction of the Ostankino tower was completed. The construction was 540 meters high. At that time, it was considered the highest on the entire planet. At the moment, the tower remains in the top ten world skyscrapers.

The supporting structures are made of reinforced concrete. Designer Nikitin took an inverted lily flower as the basis for the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãconstruction. In 2017, the Ostankino Tower celebrates its half-century anniversary.

Monument "Motherland"

World-famous monument to Motherland, which is located in Volgograd, was opened in 1967. A photo of this huge monument confirms its greatness and power.





At the main height of Russia stands a woman in a decisive impulse, in the hands of her heavy sword. A strong-willed, expressive face, fluttering hair, a turned torso create a feeling of significant strength of the Russian spirit. Her silent cry calls on all the sons of the country to defend their homeland, fight enemies, and stand to the end. The sculpture of the Motherland is the most majestic and unusual in the ensemble of the Mamaev Kurgan.

Upon completion, the sculpture of the woman was the tallest statue in the world. Above the pedestal, the monument rises to 52 meters. Together with the sword, the height of the Motherland is 85 meters. The weight of the monument is 8 thousand tons, and this does not include the sword.

Fifty powerful floodlights illuminate the sculpture, this makes it possible to see the monument from afar at night.
