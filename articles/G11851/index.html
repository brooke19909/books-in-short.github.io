Which radar detectors are better? Three selection criteria

Before you talk about which radar detectors are better, you need to clarify one point. This is to ensure that you do not have any confusion during the purchase. Many believe that a radar detector and a radar detector are one and the same. In fact, this is not so at all. The principle of operation of the radar detector is based on the decompression of the signal and its suppression in the operating frequency range, that is, if the radar detector detects extraneous signals from nearby sources, it will "drown out" them. A ban has been imposed on the use of this device in the Russian Federation; in case of its use, large fines and confiscation of the device are threatened. A radar detector only detects signals within its radius of action (400-800 m). In other words, it only detects other devices at available frequencies and warns the driver with a beep. Legally, its use is not prohibited. Now that we’ve found out, let's figure out which radar detectors are better.





Excess fines

Currently, the size of fines for speeding ranges from 500 to 5000 rubles. The maximum punishment is deprivation of rights for six months. That is, in most cases, you will give back the amount for which you can buy an inexpensive radar detector. What is the best option, we will describe below.

Three selection criteria

1. Range and immunity to interference.

The first is the radius of detection of an "ambush" on the road, and the second is false positives, which ideally should not exist at all. Help in achieving these goals is achieved by switching the radar detector to the "City" and "Track" modes. In the "City" mode, speed is measured from small distances, so that the device does not give false signals, its sensitivity decreases. In the “Track” mode, the sensitivity, on the contrary, increases, which makes it possible to detect a police radar from long distances. This gives the driver extra time to slow down. So if you plan to buy a device and think about which radar detectors are better, then be sure to take this criterion into service.





2. The ability to detect all types of impulse police devices.

Recently, police radars have been regularly upgraded, starting from almost unused permanent devices and ending with modern pulse models. Now in Russia, 90-95% of police radars are pulse-type devices. These devices operate in two ranges (X and K). Developers, who probably know which radar detectors are better, install the latest software firmware on the device, allowing them to track police devices in these ranges. These modes are called Ultra-K and Ultra-X. Before buying, make sure that they are on the radar.





3. Additional features:

- backlight adjustment (will be useful during night driving);

- function to automatically reduce the volume (after detecting a police radar, the signal subsides after a couple of seconds);

- When deciding which one is better to buy a radar detector, be sure to think about an acceptable method of notification for you. There are models with a tone alert (loud speaker), but there are voice-activated ones. Choose according to your preferences;

- A digital compass showing 8 directions of the car.
