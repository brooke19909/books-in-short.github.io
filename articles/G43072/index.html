Alapaevsk hotels: description and reviews

Alapaevsk is a city in the Sverdlovsk region, which was once the village of Alapaikh. The village is located on the banks of the Neiva River. Alapaevsk attracts tourists with temples and museums. There are also several industrial enterprises. If you are interested in Alapaevsk, the hotels of the city will provide you with a warm welcome.

Hotel "Town"

On Volodarsky Street, 66 is located the hotel "Town" in Alapaevsk. It is about a quarter hour walk from the city center. A few steps from the institution are such attractions as the Tchaikovsky Museum, City Library, Holy Trinity Cathedral, Floor School.

This is a mini-hotel consisting of only four rooms. Each room has a TV, fridge, kettle, dishes, wireless Internet, a combined bathroom. There is a quiet and peaceful environment, guests are available such amenities:

  • drinking water cooler;
  • wireless Internet;
  • parking.

Security is provided by a security system and fire alarms.

The cost of accommodation is from 1500 rubles per day.
