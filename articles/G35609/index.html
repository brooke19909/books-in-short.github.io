"Alpen Grotto" (Novosibirsk): show program, location and reviews

In the center of Novosibirsk, a lot of remarkable places and entertainment venues are concentrated, but the legendary Alpen Grotto club-cabaret with its incredible program stands out from their background. This place is visited by both date admirers and night party lovers. Many guests, having come on a romantic evening, stay here to take part in the stormy night of a magnificent show, which will give each viewer an unprecedented glow of passions, positive and drive.





About the institution

According to the local administrators, it is Alpen Grot (Novosibirsk ) that fully meets all the standards of clubs of this format. Its design is rather modest, but it is tastefully executed and emphasizes all the spectacular performances. As is characteristic of institutions of such a plan, a big role is played by the play of light, which is reflected on a semicircular stage surrounded by pylons and parapets. The club is unique in its flexible atmosphere of relaxation, as it is relevant for any category of visitors. For those who are looking for communication and new acquaintances, there is a large hall, and for couples who want to retire, there are separate cozy sofas. From 9 pm to 12 noon, the club has a meeting time, a relaxed atmosphere and light music that allows you to have a quiet and tasty dinner, enjoy communication, the comfort of service, a fashionable and cozy interior.

Location and working hours

For thirteen years, the institution has held a worthy place in the ranking of the most famous and visited entertainment establishments in Novosibirsk. Club "Alpen Grotto" is located in the heart of this big city at the address: Vokzalnaya Magistral, 16 (metro Ploshchad Lenina).





You can get acquainted with photo reports from parties, a detailed poster, as well as a bar and kitchen menu on the official website of the night club, the entrance to which is open daily from nine in the evening. From Sunday to Thursday, the institution is open until six in the morning, and on Friday and Saturday the club closes its doors at ten in the morning. When visiting, face control and a strict dress code apply.

Alpen Grotto (Novosibirsk): reviews

Judging by the numerous reviews of the club, this place is considered one of the best nightlife in the city. It has a great fresh repair, beautiful interior, good air conditioning, comfortable and soft sofas, a good bar, excellent cuisine and reasonable prices on the menu. The contingent of visitors is 23+. The show program of dancers admires with strong choreography, there are always several contests. Incendiary club music always sounds, like on Record radio.

Alpen Grotto impresses the audience during each visit. Young guys say that the club has a lot of beautiful girls, a DJ harness, the staff is cool, very friendly. The establishment is excellent with a positive atmosphere. "Grotto " is one of the favorite clubs among young people, it always feels that you are truly welcome. Club guests also note the convenient location of the establishment, among the pluses are the fact that there is parking for cars, you can pay with cards, serious men are on guard, so the contingent of visitors is only decent people.





Among the minuses, guests of the club note the dance floor, since on the weekend it does not always have a place to move around. During its existence, the staff of the Alpen Grot club studied all the wishes of its guests regarding leisure, which is why the institution became especially loved and regularly visited by local youth and guests of Novosibirsk.
