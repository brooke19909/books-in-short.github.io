Finishing the basement base in elite new buildings in the city of Samara

According to statistics, Samara is one of the ten largest cities in Russia in terms of construction and population. Office premises, multi-storey residential buildings, shopping malls and administrative buildings are the most popular objects on the housing market of the city. New buildings in Samara are particularly popular among the residents of the city, because they correspond to the ratio of price and quality and meet all modern requirements. Leading construction companies of the city offer a huge selection of real estate for their clients, from economy class apartments to luxury apartments and townhouses.

Apartments in new buildings of economy class

Economy class apartments are especially popular at all times, which is why there is always a huge selection of affordable housing on the city's housing market. The residential complex "Samara" is represented by modern multi-storey buildings corresponding to the highest level of quality. The heating of the complex is centralized, thanks to the centralized boiler room, which is located on the roof. Finishing the basement base - artificial stone. Commercial and office premises are located on the lower floors of the complex, and convenient and practical housing for any population groups is conveniently located on the higher floors.

The residential complex "Dynasty" is located in one of the most picturesque and environmentally friendly areas of the city of Samara. Convenient transportation and developed infrastructure of the micro-district allow residents to quickly get to their homes from any part of the city. The complex consists of one-room, two-room and three-room apartments of economy class, equipped according to the latest technical requirements. On the ground floors there are shops, cafes and service points, over time it is planned to build a garage and a sports center in the complex. The basement base is in perfect harmony with the whole complex and creates a completely aesthetic and attractive design.

Business-class housing in new buildings in Samara

Any businessman and entrepreneur tries to provide his family with quality comfortable housing, so business-class apartments are always very popular among business people. The residential complex "Samara-Twins" is of particular interest to people who value high-quality and convenient business-class housing. In the immediate vicinity of the premises are the cultural and historical monuments of the city, the developed infrastructure of the new building with a wellness center, shops and cafes is distinguished by a convenient entrance and a picturesque design.

The complex is comfortably located in two seventeen-story houses connected by an office center. The houses are equipped with the most modern silent elevators, a convenient five-story parking and underground parking, and a modern playground is equipped in the courtyard . Such complexes provide for the construction of a basement floor, where office premises and small commercial organizations are usually located, which is very convenient for business and practical people who know how to value their time and money. The basement basement is provided in natural stone or granite chips, which is very original fits into the overall structure of the complex.

Elite housing in the housing market of Samara

"Portal Building" is an elite residential complex of particular interest to people who value comfort and convenience. It is located in close proximity to the metro station and from the Volga river embankment, from the upper windows of the penthouses there are beautiful panoramic views that allow you to plunge into the world of dreams and relax from the everyday worldly bustle. The loggias and windows of the complex are glazed in a single architectural style and represent a single system focused on true connoisseurs of harmony and great design.

The monolithic 22-storey building represents the basis of the complex, on the first floors of which elite boutiques and service centers of the highest level are firmly established, the basement basement is made of granite or artificial stone. Underground parking is intended for the convenience of residents, over time it is planned to build its own water park, restaurant, clinic and kindergarten. Service and household organizations will take their place of honor in the complex, since without them the functioning of the whole complex is completely impossible. Dispatching the complex plays an important role in the formation of ideal housing, because timely control over all life support systems will never hurt.
