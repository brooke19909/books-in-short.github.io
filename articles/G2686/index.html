Security and search module StarLine M20

StarLine M20 is a security and search module that can protect any car from the threat of hacking and theft. Using it is quite simple - you just need to insert a SIM card into this device and put this relatively small block in the car so that it is practically not visible. The device will call or send an SMS message in case of danger to one of four mobile numbers that have been previously driven into the device. At the same time, you will receive not only an alarm signal, but you will also find out the reason why the StarLine M20 system worked, whether it was an open door, an open hood, a disabled parking brake, a shock sensor operation , etc.





You also get a unique opportunity - to drive a car from a distance. You can stop or start the engine, turn off and turn on various additional equipment of your car, for example, a heating system and so on. To do this, just call your beacon or write an SMS message with the desired order number (forty-two teams are supported). In this case, the device always reports on the implementation of this instruction. The StarLine M20 can be connected to other types of alarms, adding to the functionality of this device.





It is impossible not to say about the main functions and advantages of such a device as the StarLine M20. Customer reviews also complement the picture of the great versatility of this device.

One of the main advantages is collaboration with other types of alarms. Undoubtedly, StarLine M20 can also be used as a full-fledged independent car security system. However, the ability to combine functions is very attractive for car owners.

Alarm management in the remote dialogue mode using the appropriate commands that are dialed directly from the phone to the module number or sent as a text SMS message is another undoubtedly important function.





The execution or non-execution of the command will be confirmed by SMS or voice prompt. It is also possible to remotely monitor the status of the alarm and receive the necessary information about the launch of devices in the form of SMS alerts.

Using the StarLine M20 device control commands, you can set and configure various security modes, quickly enable or disable shock sensors and additional resources. Remote start and stop of the engine is also possible. You can also at a distance to open and close doors, hood, trunk. Turning the ignition, parking brake on and off, pressing the foot brake, turning off and on the power and various additional equipment of the car - all this can be done with the Starline M20. The price for such a device in online stores ranges from three to four thousand rubles, which is pretty cheap for such an indispensable tool that protects the car from unwanted intrusions.
