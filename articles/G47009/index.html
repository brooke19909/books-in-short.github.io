Rhodes is the fourth largest island in Greece, quite popular among modern tourists. From the southeast, it is washed by the waters of the calm and gentle Mediterranean Sea, and from the north-west it is more characteristic and wavy Aegean. This is one of the most affordable Greek resorts, which makes it so loved and popular.

Most often beach lovers come to Rhodes who want to spend their holidays alone with nature. Many experts call this place one of the most beautiful in Greece for the abundance of greenery. Rhodes has long been chosen by couples, including with young children, young people, lovers of both beach and more active and eventful relaxation.





It is clear that, arriving in such a beautiful place, it is unlikely that any of the tourists will spend all their free time at the hotel, however, I want to come to a clean, bright and comfortable establishment with good service and maintenance. In Rhodes, accommodation problems will definitely not arise. This is a large resort with luxury and luxury hotels, mid-range hotels and very simple budget hostels and villas. About hotel

So, in January 2013, the Kiotari Garden hotel was renamed Miraluna Garden Villas 3 * (Rhodes). Not all tourists are familiar with this information, the presence of a small number of reviews about the "new" hotel confuses and alarms them. By local standards, the hotel is quite small, so it is ideal for tourists looking for a calm and measured vacation. It is only 1-1.5 hours drive from the airport.





Location

Hotel Miraluna Garden 3 * (Greece, Rhodes) has a good location - between the villages of Largos and Kiotari. It is located in the southern part of the island, so the season here lasts longer than usual. The beach is only 300 meters from the hotel, which is why beach lovers who plan to spend most of their time on the coast so often pay attention to this option.

Population

Check-out time at the hotel is 14:00. But many of those tourists who arrived earlier than the indicated time indicated that they were settled earlier. The staff meets all the guests, especially with young children, understands that everyone is tired of the road, they want to put themselves in order and start getting to know the resort. They will refuse to check in to newly arrived guests only if at the time of their arrival there will not be a single free and prepared room.

Rooms

The room fund of the Miraluna Garden Villas 3 * hotel (Greece, Rhodes island) is represented by only 40 rooms: one-room double rooms, designed for 2-3 people, and two-room superior apartments, designed for 3-4 people.





The rooms are fairly bright and spacious, each with a private bathroom, a balcony with stunning views of the island‚Äôs beauty, satellite TV, air conditioning, telephone and mini fridge. If you rely on the reviews of vacationers, there were no interruptions in the operation of the equipment: the refrigerator freezes, the air conditioner cools the air in the room - what else is needed for a good rest. Important note: use of air conditioning is included in the room rate. Moreover, all vacationers with children will be provided with a special bed upon arrival.

The rooms are fresh, with traces of cosmetic repairs. Furniture is not of the highest quality (particleboard), but stylish and modern. The furniture set is standard: two beds, a table with a chair, a mirror. I would like to see a wardrobe, and so you have to keep things in a suitcase. The mattresses are clean, even, the sheets are snow-white.

The bathroom is small, but clean and comfortable: a marble table, sink, toilet, hairdryer, a full bath with a curtain - after each reception of water procedures, tourists will not have to wipe the puddle formed on the floor. When checking into the room, all vacationers will find shampoo and soap. The only drawback of the hotel is the lack of bath towels. That is why be sure to take them with you.

Room service

Rooms are cleaned every 2 days: they throw out trash, wash the floor, clean the plumbing, and even wipe the dust that has accumulated on the furniture. Bed linen is changed every 3 days. Cleaning is done during the absence of tourists, so nothing will disturb your peace.





Hotel infrastructure

The territory of the Miraluna Garden VillaS 3 * hotel (Greece, Rodos) is quite small, but well-maintained. Vacationers can take advantage of the mini-market and purchase small things necessary for rest, some souvenirs there. Rhodes is the place where tourists rent cars. A car is a great opportunity to get to know the island better, to look into all its interesting places. So, the hotel administration arranged for all vacationers car rental of various categories.

Entertainment

The hotel has several excellent pools - enough space for everyone. Sunbeds and umbrellas by the pool are free for all vacationers. Evenings can be spent in the billiard room, organizing tournaments. The hotel is ideal for families with children: a good children's pool is built for them, there is an excellent playground. For children, the Miraluna Garden 3 * Hotel (Rhodes) organized a good animation. Children are busy, parents get the opportunity to take care of themselves, relax and unwind. For children the club "Zebra" works. Many parents hope that they will really be engaged in with the children here, teach them something new, but in fact this is a place where children are simply entertained. The enthusiastic cheers of the children, laughter and joy are the main indicators of the quality of staff work.





Service

Hotel Miraluna Garden 3 * (Rhodes, Greece) operates on an all-inclusive basis. It turns out that all vacationers can eat around the clock in the restaurants and bars of the hotel, consume soft drinks and local alcoholic beverages without restrictions.

At the reception, all guests are greeted by polite and friendly staff. The information desk is open around the clock, so tourists can solve any question. If you are an active Internet user, you will be disappointed, as the only access point to the wireless connection is at the reception. The signal level is low, it is barely enough to exchange messages on social networks.





Nutrition

Let's evaluate what food the Miraluna Garden 3 * hotel (Rhodes) can offer. Reviews of tourists note that it is - for a 3 * hotel - excellent, not all fives can offer such a variety of dishes. For lunch and dinner, always served meat, sea fish, vegetables, lots of greens and salads. Of the side dishes, you can often see spaghetti, rice, mashed potatoes, steamed and grilled vegetables.

We were pleased with the children's menu: in the morning, cooks prepare porridge for milk for kids, bake pancakes and pancakes, and for children they serve their favorite delicacy - pasta with cheese. During the day, an excellent pizza is prepared at the bar - there is a line for it. The bar has enough alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, you can drink them in unlimited quantities. Vacationers praise local wine.

Be sure to visit the local cafe or restaurant at least once. Rhodes is a place where you can taste delicious Mediterranean cuisine. Do not deny yourself such a pleasure.

Beach

Hotel Miraluna Garden 3 * (Rhodes island, Greece) does not have its own beach, but only 300 meters from it there is a beautiful public beach with small pebbles. The entrance to the sea is sometimes rocky, but this does not bother tourists at all. The beach is also suitable for families with small children. Some tourists are advised to purchase special shoes, which are sold in all shops of the resort. There is a small cafe on the beach where you can buy soft drinks, ice cream, drink aromatic coffee with fresh pastries. For an additional fee (8 euros), you can rent a sunbed with an umbrella. But in the season there are far from enough sun loungers: tourists have to go to the beach in the early morning to take a seat.
