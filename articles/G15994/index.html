"Urgun", a recreation center in Bashkiria: description, photos and reviews

In Bashkiria there are many picturesque places and virgin corners of nature. Residents of neighboring regions can always spend a weekend in nature, without giving up a high level of comfort. Urgun is a recreation center located on the shores of the lake of the same name. What conditions await vacationers here and what do those who have already managed to rest here say about this place?

Holidays in the Ural foothills are available to everyone!



Urgun is the largest natural freshwater reservoir in the group of Uchalin lakes. On its shore is the recreation center of the same name. Vacationers come here in order to enjoy the natural beauties and the cleanest air without abandoning the modern benefits of civilization. Urgun is a recreation center ideal for spending a weekend, a whole vacation alone or as a family. The complex has a restaurant in which there are all conditions for banquets and corporate events.

The recreation center "Urgun" awaits guests all year round



The tourist complex operates year-round. The recreation center "Urgun" offers its guests a large number of different entertainments and leisure options at any time of the year. In summer, you can swim and go fishing, ride boats, ATVs, horses, bicycles. In the winter season, holidaymakers are offered sleigh rides, tubing, snowmobiling and snowboarding. An ice rink is poured on the territory of the recreation center and skate rental begins its work. In winter, you can also go fishing or horse riding. At any time of the year, vacationers are offered excursions to the most interesting local attractions. This is the Church of the Ascension, the spring of Aulia, the mountains of Aushtau and Iremel.

Guest accommodation options



"Urgun" is a recreation center capable of hosting 130 tourists at a time. Guests are offered accommodation in a three-story residential complex. Each vacationer can choose an option that is ideal for him. These are three categories: economy, standard and luxury. Each room has a balcony, furniture and appliances. The tourist complex welcomes guests with children. If you decide to relax with the whole family, you will definitely like one-bedroom apartments. In summer, a shift camp for children from seven to fifteen years of age operates at the recreation center.

Infrastructure and entertainment



On the territory of the recreation center there are sports grounds, a children's area, barbecue places. In bad weather, you can visit the games room and play air hockey or table tennis. It has its own sauna with pool. The recreation center "Urgun" (Bashkiria) is surrounded by a pine forest, where throughout the warm season you can pick berries, mushrooms and herbs. There are paths on the territory of the tourist complex, which are very convenient for walking or cycling. The recreation center offers its guests a choice of food in a local restaurant or cafe. There is a banquet hall with a capacity of 60 people for celebrations.

Accommodation prices and related services

Base "Urgun" pleases guests with affordable prices for accommodation and related services. In the summer, a deluxe room starts at 2,800 rubles per day. Standard apartments (up to two people) can be rented at a price of 1800 rubles. Economy double room - from 800 rubles. The most budget accommodation option - one place in an economy room - only 400 rubles. Three meals a day in a restaurant in the recreation center will cost 650 rubles per person per day. If desired, you can eat in a cafe, ordering from the menu (paid according to the price list of the institution). Urgun is a recreation center where most services are free for vacationers. Separately paid excursions and rental of tourist and sports equipment.
