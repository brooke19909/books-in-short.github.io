The Rurik dynasty on the Russian throne





The Rurik Dynasty is the very first Grand Dynasty on the Russian throne. She established herself, according to the text of the Tale of Bygone Years, in 862. This date has a symbolic name "calling of the Varangians."

The Rurik dynasty lasted 8 centuries. During this time there was a mass of displacements, distrust, conspiracies against its representatives. Rurik laid the foundation of statehood in Russia, became the founder of the first grand dynasty. So, the Rurik dynasty, a list of which will be presented below with all the characteristics of its leaders, has its own ramified system. The second representative was Oleg. He was the governor of Rurik and ruled during the infancy of his son. He is known for uniting Novgorod and Kiev, and also signed the first treaty of Russia with Byzantium. When the son of Olga and Igor Svyatoslav grew up, naturally, all power went to him.





But this prince was distinguished by his military thinking and was constantly on campaigns. After Svyatoslav, Vladimir 1, better known as St. Vladimir, ascended the throne.

He baptized Russia at the end of the 10th century. After Vladimir, Svyatopolk ruled, he was in a civil war with his brothers, the victory of which was won by Yaroslav the Wise. The Rurik dynasty, whose tree was very complex, received the next great ruler after almost 100 years. They became Vladimir Monomakh. He was the organizer of the Lubey Congress, he defeated the Polovtsy and preserved the relative unity of Russia. The dynasty of the Rurikovich after his reign forked again.

Yuri Dolgoruky and Andrei Bogolyubsky can be distinguished from this period. Both princes were prominent figures in the era of fragmentation of Russia. The remaining period of the existence of this dynasty will be remembered by several names: Vasily 1, Ivan Kalita, Ivan 3, Vasily 3 and Ivan the Terrible. It is with the names of these figures that the creation of a single Russian state is connected, it was they who began the annexation of all lands to Moscow and they completed.
