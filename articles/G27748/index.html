Hotel "Hilton", Perm: address, description of rooms, infrastructure, photo

Hotel "Hilton" in Perm is one of the best places in the city where you can relax and just have a good time visiting a large settlement. "Hilton Garden" is a hotel in Perm, which offers its guests wonderful conditions for business class accommodation. Often guests of the city who are in it on a business visit stop there. It is the rooms of the hotel in question that offer vacationers all the conditions that are needed not only for relaxation, but also for successful business and business matters.

The hotel administration also notes that newlyweds often stay in the place in question, wanting to spend time in a romantic setting, which, if necessary, will be organized by the staff. In addition, many of the guests who stayed at the Hilton with the whole family (along with their children) also note the convenience of the location.

Rooms

Hotel "Hilton" (Perm) offers its guests a luxurious number of rooms, in which there are 99 apartments of different categories: standard, suite and suite. They are equipped with everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay of guests. It should be noted that some apartments provide facilities for people with disabilities, which is positively evaluated by many guests of this place.

All apartments have a modern climate control system, presented in the form of air conditioners. With its help, vacationers can maintain in their rooms only the optimum temperature for them.

The rooms of any group are equipped with a modern bathroom, in which plumbing is installed, as well as furniture designed to store bath accessories provided to tourists. In addition, there is also a hairdryer designed for drying hair.

Each room has wireless Internet access, as well as an iron with ironing board and a small refrigerator. In addition to all this, while staying in a standard type apartment at the Hilton Garden Hotel (Perm), tourists can watch their favorite TV shows on a plasma TV, which is connected to a satellite broadcasting system. Guests can request the installation of an electronic safe directly in the room or use the cell in the left-luggage office located behind the reception desk.

Staying in rooms of any category, guests can independently prepare for themselves their favorite hot drinks (coffee, tea, chocolate, cocoa), for which electric accessories and utensils are provided.

Standard

Apartments of the standard type inside the main category are divided into three different groups, depending on the number of beds installed in them for guests. So, here we distinguish a room with one double bed, with two single beds, as well as an improved standard in which modern furniture of the latest models, made of high quality materials, is installed.

As for capacity, the furniture equipment of any room of this category is designed for a couple of guests. In addition to sleeping places, comfortable work areas are equipped for them, as well as relaxation corners located near the balconies.

As mentioned above, the Hilton Garden Inn Hotel (Perm) has excellent facilities that help you do business. What are they expressed in?

Hotel infrastructure

It should be noted that the hotel in question has an excellent infrastructure, which allows its guests to fully enjoy a good vacation. Here, a small fitness room, which operates around the clock, is presented to guests. On its site is everything that is necessary to maintain the body in excellent physical shape.

On one of the tiers of the hotel there is a spa center, which is presented in the form of a small sauna. This is where many guests come after a hard day. In addition to the sauna, the Hilton Garden Inn has a hammam, an infrared cabin, and a comfortable relaxation room.
