Hotel Omirikon Hotel Apartments 2 * (Greece, Corfu): description and reviews of tourists

Many travelers dream of spending their holidays in Corfu, one of the most beautiful and romantic islands of Greece. Here, the mysterious atmosphere of ancient myths goes well with the beauty of nature, convenient beaches and good service. Without a doubt, many hotels and pensions have been built on the coast, so travelers always have a choice. And often the gaze of a tourist stops at a small cozy hotel Omirikon Hotel Apartments 2 *. Of course, before paying for a room, you should find out more about the chosen place.

General information about the hotel: infrastructure and location

As you know, when choosing a hotel, many people are guided primarily by its location. Hotel Corfu Omirikon Hotel Apartments 2 * is located on one of the most beautiful coasts of the island of Corfu, in the cozy resort village of Gouvia. This location is considered convenient by tourists, because in walking distance not only the beach (the distance to the coast is about 200 m), but also cafes and shops. The city of Kofu, the capital of the island, is located only 8 km from the hotel, so many travelers spend time in the busy resort. The airport is 10 km away, which is also a bit.

But the hotel area is relatively large and, as the travelers themselves say, very beautiful, because it resembles an ever-flowering Mediterranean garden. Everywhere, emerald lawns, flowering bushes, bright plants and tall trees, in the shade of which you can hide from the heat, delight the eye.

Hotel Omirikon Hotel Apartments 2 * (Corfu): description and photos of rooms

As already mentioned, this is a small hotel, even rather a boarding house, consisting of 11 rooms. There are standard rooms for 2 or 3 people, as well as more spacious studios. The rooms are simply furnished, but tourists feel comfortable, almost at home. You will have your own balcony or terrace.





Of course, you can count on technology without which it is difficult to imagine a pleasant vacation. There is air conditioning, TV and telephone. By the way, a huge advantage is the availability of your own kitchen - at your disposal will be a stove with an oven, a roomy refrigerator, a toaster, a kettle and, of course, the necessary utensils. There is also a dining table - guests can dine directly in the room.

The bathroom also has everything you need, including a wash basin, shower, toilet and clean towels. Tourists do not complain about the condition of the rooms - everything is very clean, and the cleaning is of high quality.

Food: what do hotel guests offer?

Guests can choose their own meals, whether it is only breakfast or double meals. Immediately it is worth saying that in the territory of Omirikon Hotel Apartments 2 * there is no own restaurant - you can eat in the neighboring hotel Iliada Hotel. In the reviews, tourists say that they feed the hotel deliciously - there is always a choice of fresh dishes, and the staff is very hospitable and welcoming.





You should not worry about food in any case, as there are traditional island taverns and cafes nearby, where they offer delicious food at a convenient price. Yes, and you can cook if you wish, because in the room you will have your own kitchen.

Convenience and entertainment by the sea

The distance from the Omirikon Hotel Apartments 2 * to the sea is not more than 200 meters. Of course, this is a public beach, but a large influx of people is observed only during the high season. For a couple of euros you can take a comfortable lounger, in the beach bar - order a chilled drink or a sandwich.

The coast is covered with small pebbles, so you should get a suitable pair of beach shoes. The water here is clean, and there are practically no waves - it‚Äôs nice to sunbathe and swim here. If you wish, of course, you can find more active types of recreation or play sports.





Additional amenities for guests

According to reviews, the hotel for guests is a place where you can relax comfortably after a day on the beach or in the city, so there is no special entertainment here. As for amenities, at the reception you are always ready to provide the necessary information, call a taxi, book tickets. Currency can also be exchanged, and cards are accepted for payment.

In the courtyard there is a spacious terrace - a solarium with sun loungers. Guests here have a rest, take sun baths, communicate with each other. Internet access is also available.

Hotel Omirikon Hotel Apartments 2 *: reviews and experiences of tourists

Most tourists are completely satisfied with their stay at the hotel. This is a really peaceful place with a green, beautiful territory and hospitable hosts. The rooms are clean, with modern furniture and appliances. The kitchen has everything in order to organize a family breakfast or a romantic dinner for two. And in a nearby hotel they feed well - tourists do not stay hungry.

Immediately it is worth saying that the Omirikon Hotel Apartments 2 * is ideal for tourists who want to completely relax and spend their holidays calmly, enjoying the sea and warm weather. But lovers of noisy parties and midnight fun may be slightly disappointed. By the way, the prices for accommodation here are very pleasant.
