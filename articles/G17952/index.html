Infrastructure

Architects would like the new quarter to become completely independent, like a separate city. Will this be the case with the residential complex "Spanish Quarters"? Photos of the current construction and computer models of the designed objects demonstrate the active desire of the developer to implement this plan. The infrastructure of the district promises to be developed and diverse. All houses will form small quarters that are closed in cozy courtyards. Between the groups of houses a spacious boulevard has been designed, which will be framed by shops, offices, restaurants and cafes. A comprehensive school will be built for more than a thousand places, as well as two kindergartens equipped with everything necessary for the development of children.

For many, parking is important. The developer says: parking will be built. It will be underground and will consist of several levels. Thus, such a parking can accommodate about two and a half thousand cars. Several buildings will be equipped with their own underground parking. Overground parking was not designed.

In addition, a pool, tennis court, fitness club, skating rink, bicycle, as well as jogging tracks, a number of sports grounds will be equipped for residents of the housing complex under consideration. On its territory there will be a shopping and entertainment center, shops and, of course, a clinic.

Landscaping will also be done by professionals. The quarta will become pleasant and incredibly beautiful due to the numerous green spaces, flower beds, benches, cozy and safe playgrounds.





Ecology

Shareholders' opinions on how environmentally friendly the area is in which the construction of buildings under the Spanish Quarters (LCD) project continues. Most of all future residents are worried about the neighborhood with the Khovansky cemetery, which at the moment is less than two kilometers from the complex in question. In addition to being considered the largest in the entire region, it still continues to expand annually. An integral part of it is the crematorium. Construction progress

According to eyewitnesses, the construction of buildings is moving at a fairly brisk pace, which gives hope for the timely completion of the project. The first phase should be completed in the fourth quarter of this year. It implies the presence of seven buildings. In mid-February, all construction work with the buildings was completed and the implementation of internal and external decoration of the residential complex "Spanish Quarters" continues. Phase 2 will be completed in the third quarter of 2018. It will consist of nine buildings. Each is a business class level. Their construction continues. The third phase with the planned nine buildings will be completed in the first quarter of 2019.

There are several ways to pay for housing. Among them: full prepayment; installment plan; mortgage. Banks set their own payment terms, so they may vary slightly. On average, the first installment should be at least twenty percent of the total, and the mortgage should not exceed twenty years.
