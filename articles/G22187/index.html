DIY soap bubble generator

When the standard holding of family holidays is boring, we advise you to find a way to diversify them. One of the exciting activities that will appeal to both children and adults is blowing bubbles. You can do it yourself, as you did in childhood, or use a soap bubble generator.

How to make soap bubble liquid

Previously, we did not suspect that shampoo and water for the manufacture of high-quality liquid for bubbles is not enough. The secret of bizarre, large figures when blowing them depends not only on your skill, but also on additional components in the liquid itself. One of the recipes for soap bubbles contains glycerin:

In 100 ml of shampoo add 50 ml of glycerin and 300 ml of water. In this solution, you can lower the loops to blow bubbles or use liquid for bubble generating devices.

DIY soap bubble generator

  • One of the easiest ways to create a soap bubble show is a spray can filled with compressed air. It is recommended to use spray with varnish, deodorant.
  • Another way is to buy aquarium spray guns at the pet store. Add a few hoses and an oxygen tank to them. You will only have to attach the hoses to the bottle, and put sprayers on the end of the tubes and lower them into a container with bubble fluid. When you open the cylinder vent, your homemade soap bubble generator will start a colorful show.
  • The third method of making such a machine with your own hands will require some skills in mechanics and a bunch of spare parts. Finally, your suitcase with parts from old players, fans and other devices will live up to its existence. Take a knife and scissors, thick cardboard from under the covers of books, from 1 mm thick, glu gan, ruler, corrugated cardboard, spindle from the box for CDs, a motor from any player, a sleeve from a large adhesive tape, a fan measuring 120 by 120 mm . You will also need a pair of compasses and a pencil, adhesive tape, rubber bands for money, a food stretch film, four screws with nuts, six bottles with soap bubbles.

Soap Bubble Generator: Assembly



With the help of a rubber band for money, attach the motor from the player to the roller of the three-stage gearbox, this mechanism transfers rotation to the receiving roller, made of 2 disks. Fill the gap between the discs with glu gan glue. On the disc, evenly stick on one side 6 plastic loops of soap bubble bottles. Thus, when the disk rotates with a roller, the loops will fall into the container with the solution and, blowing past the fan, blow bubbles.



The fan is powered by a voltage of 12V, the motor - from 5V. For stationary power use the power supply from the computer, where the yellow wire gives 12V, and the red cable gives 5V. Use clerical paper clips as axles for the gearbox rollers, and the mechanism itself is made of cardboard. To prevent paper parts from being rubbed off by friction, we recommend wrapping each with tape.

Soap Bubble Generator: Ideas for Use

Such devices for foam show can decorate any party, birthday, anniversary or wedding. A little ingenuity and a few preliminary trainings will allow the craftsman to become a real "host" and the soul of the holiday. Those who have already tried to decorate the holidays with such tricks advise beginners to watch videos that show how masters create masterpieces from solutions with shampoo.

Definitely, soap bubble generators make it easy for your task to surprise and delight everyone, as long as there is enough solution!
