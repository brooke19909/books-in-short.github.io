The best hotels in Dubai: list, description, reviews of tourists, photos

How many hotels in Dubai? There are more than five hundred of them. From student hostels and guest houses in the city to luxury luxury Gulf Coast hotels. Millions of tourists come to the city of oil magnates and sheikhs every year, and everyone is interested in which hotel in Dubai is the best.

Burj al arab jumeirah

Burj Al Arab or the Arab Tower is the most expensive hotel in Dubai. The price for a night in a double room starts from one hundred thousand rubles. This is a fee for accommodation in a unique symbol of Dubai - the sail. The Burj Al Arab Hotel has 28 floors, but keep in mind that all 202 rooms are exquisite duplex suites with a royal atmosphere. The hotel has six restaurants, three bars, a SPA center, tennis court, billiards, kids club, helipad and beach with a total area of ‚Äã‚Äã10,000 square meters with pools, gazebos and sun loungers.

Millions of guests have appreciated the high-quality service and service of the hotel, whose reviews merge into the common opinion that Burj Al Arab is one of the best hotels in the world. The Arab Tower is located on Jumeirah Beach Road.

Atlantis The Palm

The five-star Atlantis The Palm Hotel is at the top of the popular Jumeirah Palm Island. The entire hotel is imbued with an Arabic style that combines with the theme of the ocean. The suites offer stunning views of the Persian Gulf. The hotel provides special rooms that are located below the ground floor. The huge windows of this type of apartment offer a panoramic view of the diverse underwater world of the bay. All hotel guests have the opportunity to visit Aquaventure Waterpark, the largest in the Middle East, for free. Atlantis The Palm fulfilled a large number of tourists' desires and earned thousands of positive reviews. The hotel in Dubai is located at Crescent Rd.

Address Downtown

Address Downtown Hotel is located in the heart of Dubai in a 306 meter high skyscraper. The building has 63 floors; 220 cozy and modernly equipped rooms and suites are located on them. The hotel offers breathtaking views of the musical fountain - one of the largest and highest in the world. Guests can satisfy their gastronomic whims in nine restaurants where you can try national dishes of a dozen world cuisines. Also on site is a SPA-center, gym and children's entertainment center. Service and location are the most rated address Downtown guests. Last of all, tourists like the price of a room per night. Location: Sheikh Mohamed Bin Rashid Boulevard, Downtown.

Jumeirah al qasr

The five-star Dubai hotel Jumeirah Al Qasr offers its guests a choice of 290 spacious rooms with chic antique furniture. To a private sandy beach, stretching over two kilometers, go no more than a couple of minutes. There, the guest is invited to visit the Talise SPA-center, a pool complex, as well as a relaxation area located in a palm garden. Jumeirah Al Qasr prefers 70 percent of couples with children. The hotel offers a great time in the playground or in the games room. It also offers babysitting services. Address: Jumeirah Beach Road, Madinat Jumeirah Resort.

Jumeirah emirates towers

Jumeirah Emirates Towers is located in two towers in downtown Dubai. It offers four hundred stylish and elegant rooms and suites equipped with the latest technology. In addition to impeccable service, the hotel provides free use of pools, gyms and a SPA center, as well as access to Wild Wadi Water Park. Additional free opportunities are available to guests and in the club zone of the hotel. The hotel in Dubai, which is chosen by many businessmen, organizes various banquets and conferences in it. Oriental hospitality and stunning views are mentioned by tourists, speaking about this place. Address: Sheikh Zayed Road.

Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah

On the island of Palm Jumeirah is another luxury hotel - Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah, whose policy is to satisfy the whims of the most fastidious tourist. The palace corridors lead the way to the doors of three hundred rooms and suites, in which guests will find exquisite furnishings with comfortable furniture and modern appliances. For real spa relaxation, the hotel offers a snow-white beach with swimming pools, a tennis court, SPA-salon and a water sports center. In reviews, guests write about the unique style of the hotel, about the amazing service and the cleanest beach. Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah is located on Palm Jumeirah, Crescent East, Plot C-34.

Grosvenor house dubai

Grosvenor House Dubai is called the pearl of the Arabian coast. The hotel is located on the 45 floors of the twin towers. Technological modernity and oriental sophistication are blended into the original design of the rooms and suites, from whose windows a breathtaking panorama of the coast opens. The hotel is at the epicenter of such tourist places as a beach with pools, a golf club, shopping centers, a city museum and complexes for business and leisure. Grosvenor House Dubai is the perfect choice for business trips and families. The staff, food and location praised the travelers for their praise. Address: Al Emreef Street.

Jumeirah zabeel saray

Hotel Jumeirah Zabeel Saray is located in the west of Palm Jumeirah. This is not just a resort hotel, but a real palace, the interior of which is saturated with oriental mood, consisting of works of art on the walls, frescoes, painted ceilings, marble bathtubs and crystal chandeliers. The hotel has 405 rooms, 10 restaurants, bars, pools, tropical gardens, a fitness center and tennis court. Jumeirah Zabeel Saray also has 38 private villas for private use. Positive reviews indicate the uniqueness of the place and the excellent work of the staff. Address: The Crescent (West), Palm Jumeirah.

Jumeirah beach hotel

Jumeirah Beach Hotel is one of the most popular and exclusive hotels in Dubai. The resort 26-storey "Wave" has a length of 275 meters. The glass structure reflects the waters of the Persian Gulf. The uniqueness of the design of the spacious rooms lies in the combination of the colors of the elements (earth, fire, air and water). The hotel has a kilometer-long beach where guests can play volleyball, polo, etc., as well as attend diving or surfing classes. Guests also have unlimited access to Wild Wadi Waterpark. Tourists write in reviews that Jumeirah Beach Hotel is one of the best hotels in Dubai with the highest level of service. Address: Jumeirah Beach Road.

Rixos The Palm Dubai

Rixos The Palm Dubai is a magnificent multi-level hotel in Dubai. The most spacious hotel suites have an area of ‚Äã‚Äã580 square meters. All 230 rooms comply with the technological and sanitary requirements of modern apartments. Three restaurants and bars are open on the territory, a golf club and a children's center, a beauty salon, a gym, a diving center, as well as swimming pools and a sandy beach, on which there are playgrounds for beach games. Positive reviews about Rixos The Palm Dubai note the quality work of staff and children's animators, the sophistication and exoticism of local cuisine, the cleanliness of rooms and the beach area. Address: The Palm Jumeirah, East Crescent, Plot C40.

Kempinski Hotel Mall of the Emirates

Kempinski Hotel Mall of the Emirates is located in the popular Mall of the Emirates, which includes a huge shopping center and the largest Middle East indoor ski resort, Ski Dubai. The snowy slopes make the hotel one of the most unique in Dubai. After all, it is here that you can settle in one of the 15 chalets, estranged from the hot Arabian desert by Austrian snows. The hotel also offers a choice of 380 rooms and suites overlooking the Persian Gulf. Tourists willingly share their stories on the network about the uniqueness of the proposed entertainment list. Kempinski Hotel Mall of the Emirates is praised for its location and service. Address: Sheikh Zayed Road.

Jumeirah al naseem

The luxury hotel Jumeirah Al Naseem offers its guests a choice of 440 rooms for a luxurious stay. Desert and wave combined in the design of this hotel. All apartments are decorated in light colors with light Arabic notes. On the territory of Jumeirah Al Naseem, 11 restaurants and bars, a fitness club and SPA-salon, a children's entertainment center and one of the best private beaches with many swimming pools are open. The hotel also offers free access to the Wild Wadi Waterpark. Guests praise the cleanliness of the rooms and the professionalism of the staff in the reviews. The hotel is located at: King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud Street, Madinat Jumeirah Resort.

Hilton Dubai Jumeirah Beach

Hilton Dubai Jumeirah is one of Dubai's favorite tourist hotels. The reviews write that the hotel not only meets the high level of service, but also provides a pleasant pricing policy. Considering that Hilton has five stars and is located in the center of shopping and entertainment venues, the price of a room that is equipped with a wide soft bed, various household appliances and large windows with sea views is less than ten thousand rubles per night. The hotel is located at Jumeirah Beach Road.

One and Only Royal Mirage

One and Only Royal Mirage Hotel offers one of 452 luxurious rooms with handmade furniture and carpets, bas-reliefs and mosaics. On the kilometer-long beach of the hotel are palm gardens, fountains and pools. Eight theme restaurants, tennis courts and a kids club are also on site. Guests are offered the services of a water entertainment center: diving, surfing and fishing. Hotel guests also have the opportunity to visit the Aquaventure water park for free. Tourists note in positive reviews the work of the staff, room amenities and the cleanliness of the pools. One and Only Royal Mirage is one of the few five-star hotels that visitors have enjoyed for its value for money.
