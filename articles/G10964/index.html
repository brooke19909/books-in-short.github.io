What is an insurance company? Structure and function

An insurance company is a financial body that provides insurance services to individuals and organizations of various forms of ownership. In order to understand the mechanisms of work of insurance companies, it is necessary to consider the principles of providing monetary compensation to insured persons.

What is insurance?

Insurance is a way of protecting one‚Äôs own interests, welfare in case of adverse circumstances. The probability of occurrence of such events is relatively small, but in general is quite real. Based on statistical data, insurance risk, insurance amount and tariff are determined. Compensation for damage only occurs if an insurance contract is entered into. The source of funds is the reserves and funds of the insurance company (IC).





In our country, the legal basis for the provision of such services are:

  • Law of 1992 on the organization of insurance in the Russian Federation.
  • Civil Code of the Russian Federation.
  • Other documents.

The result of the work is an insurance product, which is a list of the provided insurance services. Certificate of insurance is an insurance policy. It serves as a kind of guarantee that, in the event of certain circumstances, the insurance company undertakes to pay the indicated amount of money in favor of the final beneficiary.





What are insurance companies

A standard insurance company is a separate organization concluding contracts for the provision of insurance services and their servicing. In our country, any insurance company is a legal entity that has a certain authorized capital and offers its services on the basis of current legislation.

The provision of insurance products is the direct responsibility of insurance companies. Based on the probable occurrence of events and risk assessment, SK agents offer insurance policies and guarantee compensation for damage in the event of an unfavorable combination of circumstances.

Of course, like any commercial structure, the company's goal is to profit. But a special kind of activity allows you to accumulate huge amounts of money on accounts, which the insurance company can rightfully dispose of. This financial splendor may be granted to other commercial organizations on credit terms. The terms of such investments are regulated by national insurance legislation and financial authorities. In the Russian Federation it is the Ministry of Finance and the Central Bank.





Types of Insurance Companies

The classification of insurance companies is quite diverse. For example, Moscow insurance companies in their statutory documents define themselves as enterprises that are in state, private or municipal ownership. Similar organizations differ in the nature of the services provided. There are narrowly specialized companies that have made a business of providing several insurance services, for example, car insurance.





There are multidisciplinary insurance companies that can insure everything and from everything. There are reinsurance insurance companies and non-state pension funds, which , incidentally, are also related to insurance organizations. SK also differ in the service area in which they offer insurance. The insurance company needs buyers of policies - because the more contributions the agents collect, the faster the company's profit will increase. Insurance companies operate in a certain territory, opening branches and representative offices. Geographically, such enterprises are divided into:

  • local - offer insurance policies in a particular area or area;
  • regional - have representative offices in several areas, autonomous centers;
  • national - operate throughout the country;
  • international - have representative offices or branches in other states.
