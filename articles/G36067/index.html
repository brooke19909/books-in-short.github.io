Applique of geometric shapes - fun with learning

One way to familiarize yourself with the simplest geometric concepts in a playful way is to use an application of geometric shapes. Such an interesting and fascinating lesson as geometric application, in addition to knowledge in mathematics, develops the child's practical skills in working with scissors, glue and paper. And, as you know, the development of children's finger motility has a huge positive effect on the center of speech. Also, the application of geometric figures helps to awaken the artistic and spatial perception of the world among the growing personality, teaches us to see the components in the complex, and the smaller in the large.

The easiest way for very young people can be the lesson "Seedlings in the garden of flowers." For a picture with a flowerbed, you may need yellow circles - for the middle of the flowers - and circles of different colors for the petals. You can also prepare a window rectangle, for example, pink, and a door rectangle, for example, green.

For more developed children, it is perfectly acceptable to propose that trees also be attached to the picture. For deciduous, a long brown rectangle is suitable - the trunk and the green circle - the crown of the tree.

And you can still make a Christmas tree! Then you need to prepare also the trunk - a brown rectangle - and several green triangles of different sizes. Green triangles will schematically depict rows of spruce branches. They must be placed on the applique from the bottom up taking into account the reduction in the size of each next triangle. First, we stick the torso on a clean sheet. But for children for whom such an activity as an application from geometric figures is not a discovery, they can actually cut out eyes and nostrils from black paper for their piglet.

By the way, when working with such applications, the method for the simplest sketchy drawing is subconsciously laid in the brain in children, which is also extremely important for its development.
