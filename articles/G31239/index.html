Review of mobile phone Nokia 1208

Currently, every second has an expensive and functional smartphone. It is a minicomputer, thanks to which you can access the Internet from anywhere. And if you just need a phone to make calls? Then it is better to purchase a high-quality model, for example, Nokia 1208. It is suitable as an additional device. Also, such a phone can be purchased by the elderly, who are not very versed in modern technology.

The cost of the device varies within 1300 rubles. The model went on sale in 2007 and is relevant to this day. The menu is clear on an intuitive level, long battery life, good screen backlighting, large print, high-quality assembly, loud sound - all these are the advantages of the Nokia 1208 phone.



Description

This model has a classic look. The front panel is divided in half: the keyboard block is located at the bottom, and the display and speaker are at the top. The manufacturer presents in the assortment several color schemes. The front panel is black or silver, and the frame is blue, red or black.

At the ends there are three connectors:

The model with the index 1208 is a budget one, so in the box the buyer will find only the device itself, the battery and the charger. There is also an instruction in Russian. Accessories are not provided by the manufacturer, therefore it is recommended to purchase headphones (type HS-40).
