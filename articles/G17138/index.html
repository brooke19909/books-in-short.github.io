Sony Ericsson K750i is not just a phone

Sony Ericsson K750i is one of the best-selling phones in Russia, which you can buy to this day. Even before the appearance of suppliers on the shelves, it was massively untwisted by all possible advertising ways. The flagship, the best value for money, great functionality - this is just part of its advantages, which was generously used in advertising.





The phone appeared in 2005 and, according to Western sources, has become one of the best options for its time. The release of this device allowed the recently appeared elite company to take its place in the market. Sometimes notes appeared in the press: the quality of the Erickson connection and the camera from Sony. In fairness, it is worth noting that this was not far from the truth.

Battery and Charging

The battery for Sony Ericsson K750i is made in a separate unit, which is inserted under the cover located on the back of the phone. For proper insertion, there are small stops on the side of the contacts, which at the same time serve as guides during installation. Simka is under it, slightly recessed into the case, and it is impossible to remove it without disconnecting the battery and a sharp object to pry off the edge.





If the phone is used only for calls, cameras, radio - the battery capacity may last for a week or more (for comparison: the smartphone needs to be charged 2-3 times a week). Like other modern devices, charging from a computer is steadily supported, but another problem arises - the PC will not see the phone (and therefore will not charge) until all drivers are installed, which are separately divided into drivers for cable and phone. Therefore, it is easier to use the Sony Ericsson K750i charger that comes with the kit.

Screen

For all its variety of functions, the Sony Ericsson K750i display has only 256 thousand colors and a resolution of 176x220. The screen itself is under the glass of the case. Glass does not glare, but on a bright sunny day, the image fades slightly, although it remains visible.





The backlight is quite pleasant blue tones, the brightness is selected manually, but, as a rule, the phone out of the box is already configured correctly. The screen is well lit, visible in the same way as during the day. Under the keys, the backlight is white, however, the "asterisk" and "pound" remain in the shade.
