Polish gum: knitting pattern. How to knit a Polish gum with knitting needles

The pattern of Polish gum is presented in several forms. The first is the classic version, which is the most common, and the second is the "Leningrad" one, it is an almost forgotten "Polish gum". The knitting pattern of these patterns has slight differences. Also for knitting circular products, there are several other ways in which Polish gum is made.



Patterning

Any gum is obtained by periodically repeating the front loops with the wrong ones. They line up in even stripes vertically, which help stretch the canvas in width. To obtain a pronounced texture and provide tensile properties, knitting needles are used in needles two sizes smaller than for the main pattern. The relief of the pattern is obtained due to facial loops, which line up in convex lines, and concavities are formed, on the contrary, purl. As a result, we obtain an elastic web, while this may cause difficulties in measuring the height of the product. Because when the part is stretched, the height size decreases, and in the contracted state it increases. The best option would be to measure the size of the product in the state of the stretched gum by half.

Classical Polish gum

Using this pattern, when typing, you need to remember the following. The total value of the loops should be divided into four. Two edge loops are also added to the total . What are the features of the relief pattern - the Polish gum? How to knit this pattern in accordance with the scheme described below:



We carry out a set of the required number of loops, depending on the length of the product.

1st row. We shift the edge of the loop on the working needle. Next, we combine two purl with 2 facial loops along the entire length. We end the row with an extreme loop. Turn the canvas over.

2nd row. We transfer the edge loop to the working knitting needle and knit 1 wrong side. Then we perform the next rapport - we knit 2 facial ones, then 2 wrong ones. We carry out the last front and close the row with an edge loop. Turn over the knitting.

Then the technology of the first and second rows is repeated over the entire height of the canvas.

"Leningrad" gum

As in the previous case, the number of loops to be dialed should also be a multiple of four, with the addition of two edge loops, from which the Polish gum will form. The knitting pattern has the following sequence.

Depending on how long the product will be, loops are calculated and dialed.

1st row. We throw the hem and knit 1 front, 1 wrong side and 2 front loops, repeating the combination along the entire length. We finish the row with an extreme loop, turn the knitting needle over.

2nd row. Remove the edge loop. Then do rapport * 1 wrong side 3 facial *. The last is the edge loop. We make a turn of a working cloth.

Repeat the first and second rows along the entire height to form the necessary pattern.

Polish elastic for circular knitting needles No. 1

Since the process in this case is carried out in a vicious circle, all rows need to be knitted only with faces. In order for the correct Polish gum to form, the knitting pattern shown in the figure is slightly different from the ones discussed above.

A set of rows is performed.

2nd row. It is carried out in the following order - we remove an edge loop. We perform further such rapport - 2 facial, 1 wrong side, 1 facial. We end the row with an edge loop.

We continue the formation of the pattern by repeating the first and second rows.

Polish circular knitting elastic, option number 2

To obtain the correct circular pattern, you can use knitting technology that is different from option No. 1. Consider in more detail? How to knit a Polish gum with knitting needles:

We carry out a set of the required number of loops (the number should be a multiple of four, with the addition of two edge loops to this number).

1 and 2 rows. Knitted with a classic double elastic band. The rapport fits so - 2 front and 2 wrong loops.

3rd row. To complete the pattern of Polish gum, it is necessary to determine the central loop. Its location is determined by the adjacent wrong side, which should be located to the left of it. The central loop and the purl following it are knitted only in the facial way. After them, the following stitches are sewn on the wrong side. This alternation - 2 facial and 2 wrong - must be continued until the end of the series.

4th row. It also begins with the definition of the central loop. Then a number of knits are knitted similarly to the third.

By this technology, the entire fabric is knitted. After a few rows there will be a clear selection of the central loops that line up in a vertical line.

With this pattern, you can perform a variety of models. Even for the smallest, you can knit sliders and blouses using this version of the picture. This is because the texture of the canvas is quite loose. Also, Polish gum has poor elastic properties and will not hold a good shape.
