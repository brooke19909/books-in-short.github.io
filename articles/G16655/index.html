The best Malta hotels for holidays

Holidays in Malta - one of the fashion trends of recent times. In this small European country they go not only to sunbathe on the beaches and swim in the sea. Tourists rush here to learn the language, hang out in the best night clubs, explore the numerous castles and bays, take a walk in the cities that have not lost their medieval charm. In this article we will try to describe what kind of accommodation can be found in this country, which is more suitable for relaxation and how to approach the choice of a hotel in Malta. Let's start with general advice.

Description of Malta Hotels

This state is located on several islands. Malta is the largest of them. But there is also Comino and Gozo. Of course, many tourists go to the largest island. Although hotels are available on all three. When choosing a hotel in Malta, you also need to decide what is more important for you - a vacation at sea or proximity to nightlife. On this island you have to choose: there are no hotels that would satisfy both needs. But this country is small in size. So if you prefer a hotel on the beach, then you can go to a disco or a nightclub by public transport and spend no more than thirty minutes on the road. For lovers of partying and nightlife, the St. Julian's area is suitable. In season, he never sleeps at all. Well, for those who came here to enjoy the scenery and relax in solitude, experts advise the island of Gozo.

Even some of the guesthouses are of a similar type on the islands. The rooms are usually clean and comfortable. Food can be at the level of "five", it all depends on the hotel. Since water in Malta is greatly saved, towels will not be changed every day, but only if you throw them on the floor. Of Malta's hotels of this class, the most popular are hotels in the north of the island. These are Ramla and Mellieha Bay. Good reviews about the Dolmen Resort hotel. It is located in the resort of Aura, near the old pagan altar, between two picturesque parks. 5 star hotels

On the islands of the state, hotel lines of international class are very widely represented. The most comfortable and luxurious hotels in Malta are Hilton, Kempinski, Radisson and Intercontinental. Here you will not only be offered a huge range of services, but also a very fast and professional service. Since international seminars are often held at such hotels, they are equipped with huge conference rooms. Almost all employees understand English. These hotels have gyms and spas, as well as several pools. Some of them are heated in the winter. If you want to relax inexpensively in the suite, and even in the "five", come out of season. Then housing prices and services fall sharply. Most high-end hotels are located in the St. Julian's area. The Excelsior Hotel in the capital of Valletta is also very good. It is located in the city center, close to historical sites. At the same time, all conditions for a beach holiday are created there, there is a private yacht marina, 3 gourmet restaurants and five bars, as well as infinity pools known throughout Malta.
