New Year's surprise



Gifts for a magical holiday await everyone with the same thrill. And to make this process less stressful and more fun, you can make a wonderful postcard with your child.

For such a craft for children, paper will require: cardboard (smooth blue, red, green and brown, as well as white corrugated), gold foil, felt-tip pens of white, red and black colors, glue stick, a sheet of thick white paper, scissors.

On a piece of thick paper, you should print the presented template for the future postcard. Then, on the corrugated cardboard, transfer the contour of the beard, mustache, edge of the cap and fur coat. The silhouette of the bag is copied on brown cardboard, on the red - the main elements of a fur coat and hats, on green - gloves and spruce for the next New Year greeting card-paper crafts for children. Stars are cut out of gold foil, but to facilitate the process, you can use ready-made stickers of different sizes.

A Christmas tree is glued onto a blue cardboard. Next to her is attached the silhouette of Santa Claus with a bag. Next comes the decor: in accordance with the template, a beard, edge, gloves and stars are applied. With the help of felt-tip pens, a decorative seam is made on the office of spruce, fur coat and bag, and also eyes and nose are drawn.

Such a postcard can be independently built by a first grader, but younger children will need the help of their parents.

With love



Paper crafts for children will help express their feelings on Valentine's Day. To do this, you will need paper molds for cupcakes of different diameters, pastel shades of blue, pink or orange, cardboard with a glazed surface, and glue.

Using glue, it is necessary to fasten together three molds for cupcakes of different diameters, starting with the largest and ending with the smallest. A heart is cut out of the cardboard, which fits into the smaller of the shapes in volume, and is attached to the middle of the resulting composition. The tape should be folded in half and glued to the back. This craft will be a wonderful expression of love.

Summer has come

They are looking forward to this time. And the best gift for her offensive may be paper crafts for children in the form of butterflies. They will need a tube of baking foil, colored cardboard, thin ribbons, doll eyes, glue and scissors.



A 15-cm pipe is cut from the tube and glued with colored paper. Butterfly wings are cut from the same sheet , on which circles and flowers of contrasting tones are applied. Further, with the help of glue, the wings and body of the butterfly are fastened, as well as the eyes. 7-centimeter segments of ribbons are twisted each at one end by a knot and are attached to the body of the butterfly from the inside above the eyes.

DIY paper crafts for children will bring not only pleasure, but also significant benefits. Such games will help develop creative thinking, mindfulness and perseverance.
