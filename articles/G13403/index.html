How to repay a debt

All existing programs of the bank provide for the repayment of debt by the borrower according to the annuity scheme, which involves making monthly payments in equal amounts throughout the entire term of the loan. Paying interest on the use of the loan and returning money for the loan body is necessary according to the schedule approved at the time of signing the contract. The required amount can be paid in various ways:

  • transfer from a card of another bank through an ATM or terminal;
  • cash at the bank office;
  • by direct transfer from the employer of the borrower by deduction of the required amount from the salary of the latter.

In case of late payment of the next payment, the bank shall calculate a penalty in the amount of 20% of the monthly debt. In addition, the bank provides for the possibility of recovering funds in the amount of 2% of the total loan debt in case of loss of the guarantor. As with any other credit institution, you can also pay off the debt ahead of schedule - there is no penalty or commission for this.
