Main Attractions Arkhipo-Osipovka

Arkhipo-Osipovka is located on the Black Sea coast, between Novorossiysk and Gelendzhik. This place has long been a favorite resort of many Russians. Beautiful nature, warm sea, comfortable beaches and low prices contribute to the growth of attendance of the village from year to year.





The village has existed on this site for more than two hundred years, and during this time many changes have taken place in it. A resort of an all-Russian scale grew out of an ordinary village. The name of the settlement was given in honor of the hero of World War II, a monument to which still adorns the surroundings.

The main attractions of Arkhipo-Osipovka are nature reserves, vegetation, waterfalls and dolmens. The unique nature, climate and air made this place ideal for restoring health and strength. Several resorts and boarding houses are located near the village, there are also campsites and recreation centers for holidaymakers of different ages nearby.





Sights of Arkhipo-Osipovka include unique Teshebsky waterfalls. They consist of six cascades, some of which can be seen nearby. Observation platforms installed near the waterfall can be visited on their own or as part of a group. Over the years, water has created a small pond at the foot, which is filled with beautiful azure water and is suitable for swimming.

Vacationers should definitely visit the park surrounding the Arkhipo-Osipovka sanatorium. The sights of this place are known throughout the district and attract many tourists every year. More than a hundred species of exotic plants grow in the park.

The most famous sights of Arkhipo-Osipovka are dolmens. This is the name of the ancient structures of heavy slabs weighing several tons, which were used for burials. They were built in the Bronze Age. Many of them can be seen in the photo. Arkhipo-Osipovka is known for a large accumulation of dolmens and burial grounds. These places are very famous, every year many tourists visit them.

Some of the sights of Arkhipo-Osipovka carry the memory of the hostilities that took place in the area. The Mikhailovsky fortification played an important role in the defense of the territory during the Caucasian War. Currently, a museum is operating here. You will also see a large cross of a snow-white color, which was installed in memory of the war hero Arkhip Osipov.

Numerous attractions of Arkhipo-Osipovka will allow you to learn something new every day and study the history of this place with interest. In addition, the village offers a lot of entertainment for every taste. For children there is a dolphinarium and a water park. For teens, karting will be interesting. A special track is installed for him.



One of the most beautiful places in the village can be called the embankment, which every day with the onset of dusk is filled with people walking. Here are interesting places, cafes and bars. And, of course, one cannot fail to note the wide pebble beach, which invites adults and children to relax on the shores of the gentle sea.
