Zaporozhye hotels: a list of the best. Photos and reviews of tourists

Zaporozhye is one of the largest cities in Ukraine, which is located on the banks of the Dnieper River. Many tourists who want to admire local attractions often come here. During the arrival of any guest of the city the question arises: "Where to stay?" Here hotels come to the rescue, the six best of which are mentioned in this review.

Hotel "Theater"

This hotel is one of the oldest buildings in the city, which in 2011 was subject to complete restoration. It is located in the most convenient and easily accessible place in the city - in its center. Tourists who visit the Teatralny Hotel have the opportunity to stay in one of 109 comfortable rooms of different categories: single standard Single, double standard Double, triple standard Twin, Business standard, Junior Suite and Suite. In addition to comfortable rooms, the hotel has a large restaurant and a lobby bar for guests.

If it is necessary to conduct negotiations or presentations, there are three conference rooms in the building of the Teatralny Hotel (Zaporozhye) that are equipped with modern sound equipment. In addition, there is a separate small room for negotiation.

Guests of this hotel in Zaporozhye can use the parking services, which are also available here.

Hotel Theater "is located at the address: Zaporozhye, Troitskaya street, 23.



Hotel "Cathedral"

"Cathedral" is a hotel, which is located in a four-story building, which is built and equipped with the latest technology and architectural construction. It is provided with independent heating and a good ventilation system, which allows guests to feel completely comfortable.

On the ground floor of the hotel there is a spacious hall, the interior of which is dominated by a large number of marble details. There is also a huge restaurant room for guests to eat, and besides this, there is a sauna and massage room - all this is arranged for greater comfort of guests.

On the upper floors of the building are rooms for guests. It should be noted that "Cathedral" is the most expensive of all Zaporozhye hotels. This is due to the fact that it has exclusively luxury rooms, the total number of which is 16. They are a team of designers and are equipped with the latest technology.

Tourists resting here respond very well to the staff working here. Analyzing the reviews they left on the Internet, we can conclude that each guest here is surrounded by attention and care, which makes staying in the hotel even more comfortable.

The address at which the Soborny hotel is located: Zaporozhye, Soborny prospect, 28.



Hotel "Venice"

Hotel "Venice" - a place where there are conditions designed for even the most demanding guests. There are several categories of rooms: Junior Suite with two beds, Standard with a large bed, Junior Suite with a large bed, Economy, Suite and Family Two-Room Suite. In total, the hotel "Venice" in Zaporozhye has 19 ideally equipped rooms.

All guests have the opportunity to visit the large bath complex, which is also part of the hotel structure. Here, guests are offered steam rooms of the Russian and Turkish baths with a wide range of services that the guest can choose on their own. There is also a small cafe where you can have a snack and a cup of hot coffee or tea.

From the windows of the hotel rooms you can see the picturesque banks of the Dnieper and the island of Khortytsya.

Address at which Venice is located (hotel): Zaporozhye, Nizhnedneprovskaya street, 1-B (Naberezhnaya highway).



Hotel Intourist

This hotel is within walking distance from the main transport hubs of the city - in its central part. Within the walls of the hotel are both budget and more expensive rooms, the cost of living in which starts from the amount of 990 hryvnia per day. There are rooms of the following categories: Standard Double, Standard Twin, Junior Suite, Junior Suite Twin, as well as Suite.

Intourist is one of the best hotels in Zaporozhye, on the ground floor of which there is a large spacious lounge, as well as several restaurants where you can eat and drink a cup of hot tea or coffee. Each restaurant has a beautiful view of the central street of Zaporozhye - Soborny Avenue.

Children under 12 years old enjoy free accommodation at the Intourist Hotel (Zaporozhye). Adults are given a 5% discount in case of booking a room through the hotel website. Judging by the reviews of guests, who are often found in different forums, this is a very advantageous offer that interests tourists.

Hotel Intourist is located at: Zaporozhye, 135 Soborny Avenue.



Hotel "Khortytsa Palace"

"Khortytsa Palace" is one of the most prestigious and expensive hotels in Zaporozhye. It hosts government officials, visiting stars, businessmen and just rich people. The conditions provided by the hotel are really worth a lot.

The building of this hotel is located a five-minute walk from the city hall, in the very center of the city, and also half an hour from the airport. The windows of the rooms of this hotel demonstrate wonderful views of the mighty Dnieper and the island of Khortytsya.

The hotel building has rooms of different categories, the cost of living in them starts at $ 80 per day. In addition to luxurious rooms, the Khortytsa Palace Hotel has a spa center and a large gym - according to guests, this is a great solution that makes sports for business people within walking distance.

Also in the hotel building there are several restaurants: Flavors, River Lounge, as well as two lobby bars, one of which is a summer terrace.

Address where you can find the "Khortytsa Palace" (hotel): Zaporozhye, Shevchenko Boulevard, 71A.



Hotel "Reikartz Zaporozhye"

"Reikartz Zaporozhye" is a hotel located on the banks of the Dnieper. Within walking distance from it is the beach and the Kozak Palace Exhibition Center, popular with tourists. This is an ideal place for both tourist and business trips - there are many conditions for the comfort of guests.





The hotel offers a choice of 69 comfortable rooms, each of which is equipped in the best way - they have comfortable furniture and good appliances. Each of the rooms has a bed, a bathroom, a TV, air conditioning, a hairdryer, a minibar, and various necessary accessories. As for the categories of rooms, there is a Classic, Standard, Studio, Suite, as well as a room for people with disabilities - Superior.

Hotel guests can use the services of the Riviera grill bar, which is located on its territory. There is also a well-equipped summer terrace with a pool.

Hotel "Reikartz Zaporozhye" is located at the address: Zaporozhye, Mayakovsky Avenue, 19.
