Ilyssion Resort 3 *. Hotel Greece Ilyssion Resort (Rhodes): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The island of Rhodes, belonging to Greece, is one of the most popular places for summer holidays of our compatriots. This is not at all surprising, because it has a wonderful climate, picturesque nature, as well as a mass of historical and architectural monuments, many of which are under the protection of UNESCO. Rhodes is also often called the pearl of the Mediterranean. By the way, this island is washed by the waters of not only the Mediterranean, but also the Aegean Sea. So, if you decide to spend a week or two in Rhodes (Greece) and at the same time count on a budget, but comfortable rest, we can recommend you to stay at the Ilyssion Resort 3 * hotel. We propose today to find out together what is waiting for tourists here, and also we will figure out what impressions our compatriots left after living in this hotel.





Location

The hotel in question boasts a good location. So, it is located between two small settlements - Pefki and Lardos, just seven kilometers from the popular Greek resort of Lindos. As for the surroundings of the hotel, this area is quite quiet, although it has all the necessary tourist infrastructure. Fans of noisy and active leisure can always take a bus, taxi or rented car to Lindos, famous for its many shops, nightclubs, restaurants, bars and other institutions.

Three-star hotel of Greece Ilyssion Resort (Rhodes): photos and description

The hotel in question first opened its doors to all comers in 1989. A few years ago (in 2012), a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here. So now "Ilission Resort" is a modern comfortable hotel. It is located almost on the beach and has its own territory with pools, a restaurant, a bar and other infrastructure. Basically, the hotel is designed for family people who want to spend their holidays in a relaxed atmosphere. However, young people will also like it here, since all the entertainment of Lindos can be reached in just a quarter of an hour by bus or taxi. The hotel has 110 comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary for guests to stay and relax.

Posting Rules

As with most hotels around the world, Ilyssion Beach Resort 3 * has a clearly defined time for check-in and check-out. In accordance with the rules of the hotel, check-in of travelers who are staying in rooms of the category they book is carried out starting from two in the afternoon. However, this rule is not immutable. The hotel administration always tries to meet the guests and make their stay as convenient and comfortable as possible. Therefore, if you arrive at the hotel before the indicated time, everything will be done to accommodate you as quickly as possible. However, this may not be feasible in high season when the hotel is 100% full. In this case, you will have to wait until the previous guests vacate the room and the maids prepare it for check-in. In order not to lose time, but to start your vacation as soon as possible, you can leave your things in the luggage room and have a snack in the hotel restaurant, refresh yourself with a drink in the bar, have fun in the pool or go straight to the sea.

As for the date of departure, it is necessary to vacate the occupied room before noon. At the same time, you should hand over the keys at the reception and pay for the entire stay (if you have not previously paid for the tour to your travel agency). Hotel Ilyssion Resort 3 * (Rhodes, Greece) accepts both cash and plastic cards of such popular systems as Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Maestro and Diners Club. Also note that when booking an online room via credit card, the hotel may deduct the prepayment amount from you. In this case, at checkout you will only have to pay the difference between the full cost of living and withheld prepayment.

Accommodation for guests with children

Since the Ilyssion Resort 3 * hotel (Rhodes, Greece) positions itself as a family institution, guests who are on holiday with children of all ages are always welcome here. Moreover, if you place a child in an extra bed in your room, his stay will be free. By the way, a playpen is provided for children under two years old. Please note that only one extra or baby cot can be accommodated in each room. If you need this service, you should notify the hotel administration in advance, and also wait for confirmation from it.

Pets welcome

This point should be paid special attention to those tourists who prefer to go on vacation in the company of their four-legged pets. So, in accordance with the rules of Ilyssion Resort 3 * (Rhodes, Greece), pets are not allowed in the hotel. Therefore, you will either have to leave your pet at home during your vacation, or find another hotel in the Pets Friend category to accommodate.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the Ilission Resort Hotel has 110 comfortable rooms. There are rooms of the following categories: standard single, standard double (with the possibility of an extra bed) and suites. The rooms of the last two types have either one double or two single beds. The hotel also has rooms specially equipped for disabled guests. In addition, there are rooms for honeymooners. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a private bathroom with necessary bath amenities, a TV, a telephone, a refrigerator and a balcony. From the windows of the rooms you can either see the sea or the hotel garden and the surrounding area. Cleaning in the rooms of the hotel Ilyssion Resort 3 * (Lindos) is carried out on a daily basis. Bed linen and towels are changed three times a week in the rooms. In addition, all hotel guests can order a room service at any time.

Nutrition

Travelers who reserve a room at the Ilission Resort Hotel can choose one of two catering systems: all-inclusive and half-board. In the first case, tourists will be able to eat in the hotel restaurant three times a day, and in the second, guests will be provided with breakfast and dinner. Breakfast is continental, while for lunch and dinner, chefs prepare a variety of Greek and international dishes. Please note that, regardless of the type of food chosen, all drinks are provided for a fee.

In addition to the restaurant, Ilyssion Resort 3 * (Rhodes island, Lindos) has two bars. One of them is in the hotel lobby, and the other is near the pool. Here you can enjoy your favorite cold and soft drinks, as well as snacks.





Sea and beach

Just 20 meters from the hotel is a small beach belonging to him. A couple of hundred meters from the hotel there is a large sandy beach where there is everything you need to relax: sun loungers, sun umbrellas and equipment for entertainment and water sports. The beach is municipal, so you will have to pay extra for using its infrastructure.

Infrastructure

Ilyssion Resort 3 * hotel (Lindos) has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay and an excellent holiday for guests. So, the hotel has two outdoor pools, as well as a pool for children. Near them is a large sun terrace with comfortable sunbeds and tents, as well as a bar with a wide selection of soft and alcoholic drinks.

Fans of outdoor activities can play mini-golf, volleyball, tennis, ping pong, as well as billiards or chess. You can relax by going to the jacuzzi. You can also spend time in the hotel's TV room. For the youngest guests, the hotel has an outdoor playground, as well as a games room.

At the reception of Ilyssion Resort 3 *, if necessary, you can leave valuables in a safe, exchange currency, book an excursion or rent a car. In addition, the hotel has a shop with everything you need for a beach holiday, as well as a souvenir shop.





Cost of living

We offer you to get acquainted with the prices for accommodation at the Ilission Resort Hotel (Rhodes, Greece). The cost of accommodation varies by month. So, hotel accommodation in the low season (May and October) will cost from 53 (for a single room) to 79 euros (for a triple room) per night. For accommodation in June and September you will have to pay from 65 to 96 euros per night, and in July and August - from 77 to 114 euros. Prices shown include half board in the hotel restaurant.
