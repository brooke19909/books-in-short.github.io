Mitts - what is it? How to knit mitts with knitting needles?

Recently, more and more popular knitwear. It can be a hat, scarf, mittens or sweater. Any element of the wardrobe can be created from woolen threads. Handwork is especially valuable. If you know how to knit, then this will be a huge plus. A woolen product will be an excellent gift for any celebration. It is worth noting that do-it-yourself things allow you to save money well . This article will discuss how to knit mitts. You will find out what kind of clothing item is and when to wear it.





Mitts - what is it?

This item of clothing can be created from almost any material. Mittens are clothing for hands, which is often created from dense fabric. So, this wardrobe item can be velvet or velor, tweed or fur. Recently, mittens knitted with needles of woolen threads are very common. You can also use a hook to create this outfit.

Mittens are a wardrobe item worn in any season. Do not think that clothes for hands are needed only in the cold season. In summer or spring you can wear mittens. They will become an original accessory to complement your look.

The peculiarity of this product is that the mitts have an incomplete appearance of mittens. All your fingers will be open in them. Many fashionistas prefer to decorate mittens with additional flowers, bows, beads and stones. It is worth noting that this item is more decoration than warming clothes.





How to knit mitts with knitting needles?

For beginners, there is a simple knitting instruction, which will be presented in this article. If you are just learning to create woolen products, then give preference to the simplest pattern. Knitting needles are created in several stages. Let's consider each of them.

The first stage: prepare the material for work

Before knitting the mitts with knitting needles, it is necessary to choose woolen threads and knitting needles. If you want to combine several colors, try to choose materials of the same thickness. Only in this case the product will be even and the pattern harmonious.

Be sure to consider the size of your hand. The smaller the brush, the finer the threads and knitting needles. You also need to tie a probe from the selected material. It will be needed to calculate the number of loops and rows in the future. Type 10 stitches on the knitting needles and knit 10 rows. After that, using mathematical calculations, find out how many loops and rows are in one centimeter.





Second stage: determine the size

Before knitting, determine the height level of the product. Mittens are a universal wardrobe item that can be low (to the wrist) or high (to the shoulder). Most often, mitts are created that are up to the elbow or slightly lower.

After you have decided on the height of the product, you need to take the dimensions off your hand. Measure the girth of the arm in the place where the mitten will end. Most often this is the base or middle of the fingers. Next, determine the length of the sleeve.





The third stage: the beginning of knitting

When the exact number of loops is known (calculate it with a probe), you need to dial them on the knitting needles. Use stocking tools. The number of loops should be a multiple of four. The first rows of the product are best knitted with an elastic band. Otherwise, the edge may begin to curl.

Tie several rows with the Double Gum pattern. At the same time, the work is performed as follows: two purl loops, two front loops behind them, then two purl loops again, and so on. In the next row, knit the wrong loops over the wrong side , and the front loops over the face. The height of such a gum depends on the size of the product. If your mitts are short, then you should tie no more than two centimeters. With a higher working height, you can independently choose the size of the gum.

When the first steps are taken, move on to a smooth drawing. Knit with the front or back loops at your discretion. The technique for performing the pattern should be as follows: the first row - all the front loops. In the next row, face parts are knitted over the front. Using this method, bring the work to the base of the thumb. In the process, you need to try on an unfinished product several times.





Fourth stage: give a thumb up

When the work is done to the desired level, put the product on your hand and note how many loops you need to leave to get your finger out. Transfer the marked elements to additional knitting needles or use a separate thread of a different color.

Fifth stage: the final touches in the work

In a similar way, a second mitten is created. Remember that both products must have the same number of loops and rows, that is, be absolutely symmetrical.

After the accessory is ready, you need to carefully hide the remaining threads. Do not cut them too close to the product, otherwise the mitten will begin to blossom over time. Using a hook or a large needle, thread the thread through the loops on the wrong side and fasten with a knot.

Next, wash the mitts. Be sure to consider the temperature regime and method of cleaning. It is better to choose special gels for washing wool and process the product manually. Dry the mitts on a thick towel in a horizontal position. Do not hang woolen products on a clothespin; the accessory will easily lose its shape and acquire defects.





Conclusion

Now you know what mitts are. You also learned the easiest way to create this accessory. If you wish, you can supplement the instructions with your own strokes, for example, choose your own knitting method and pattern. Some experienced needlewomen create mitts using circular knitting needles or on two knitting needles - in the form of a fabric, which is then sewn together. Beginner knitters should still use this instruction and do the work on the knitting needles.

Knit with pleasure for yourself and your loved ones. Mittens will be a great gift for the New Year, Christmas or any other celebration. Your present will not only become the most original, but will also be remembered for a long time by your new mistress. The product can be gifted to both an adult woman and a little girl who loves to imitate adults and put on different clothes.
