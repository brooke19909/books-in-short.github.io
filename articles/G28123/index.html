Sea Sun Hotel 4 * (Egypt / Dahab) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Dahab

The town is located in the desert, at the foot of the Sinai Mountains, next to the resorts of Egyptian Sharm El Sheikh (just 100 km) and Israeli Eilat (150 km), so Sea Sun Hotel 4 * Dahab is located in a vibrant tourist area.

In ancient times, Dahab was an important trading port, which is mentioned in the Bible, in the Book of Moses, as the settlement of Dizahab. The ruins of a sea fortress and a lighthouse have survived to our times.

Dizahab is translated from Hebrew as ‚Äúplace of gold‚Äù, and Dahab in Arabic means ‚Äúgold‚Äù. The origin of this name has traditionally been associated with the color of sand, which fills the entire valley to the Red Sea.

But recently, geologists have indeed found gold in the vicinity of Dahab. And now, tourists, vacationing at Sea Sun Hotel 4 *, can say that they live in a real "golden city".

Despite the fact that the local population in Dahab is only 10 thousand people, this town is a center of diving, windsurfing and kitesurfing due to its climate, location and structure of the seabed.

Climate

Most of the year on the coast, where the Hotel Sea Sun 4 * (Egypt, Dahab) is located, is dry and warm. Rains are rare, as constant winds simply blow away the moisture evaporating from the sea and prevent rain clouds from forming.

Winds are born in the Sinai mountains, which rise on the coast of the Gulf of Aqaba, they blow away not only the clouds, but also the heat, so the Sea Sun Hotel 4 * is not as stuffy as in Sharm. To the delight of numerous surfers, a "tailwind" blows in Dahab 270 days a year.

It is warm in winter (+ 20-25 o ), and in summer + 35-40 o . Water (even at depth) - +21 degrees in the winter and +28 degrees in the summer.

Sea and diving

The width of the Gulf of Aqaba is about 25 km, so on the beaches around the Sea Sun Hotel 4 * there is no strong surf and high waves.

There are many coral reefs located close to the coast, so you can dive not from a boat, but from pontoons off the coast. Due to this, sea diving in Dahab are cheaper than in other Egyptian resorts.

The beauty of Dahab's coral reefs is recognized around the world, and many guests from different countries come here to see the real world of the sea.





Not far from the Sea Sun Hotel 4 * is a famous natural monument - the Blue Hole, which attracts divers from all over the world. This is an underwater cave with a diameter of 50 meters, extending 130 meters vertically down. If you dive to a depth of about 55 meters, you can find a passage that connects this "lost world" with the sea.

But for ordinary tourists an easier route has been developed, and they can penetrate the Blue Hole through the isthmus, descending only 7 meters under the water. Inexperienced divers are advised to dive into the Blue Hole only with professional instructors.





In the area of ‚Äã‚Äãbeaches adjacent to the Sea Sun Hotel 4 *, there are many other underwater grottoes and coral reefs, where you can see interesting animals and fish.

Dahab seems to have been created for diving, as the reefs are located close to the shore, there are no big waves on the surface, and there are no strong currents under water.

More than 60 dive centers and 30 dive sites operate for tourists, where you can dive to a depth of 200 meters.

Windsurfing, Kitesurfing and Snorkeling

Tourists from different countries come to Dahab year-round to ride the sea on the boards with a sail or a kite.

Not far from Sea Sun Hotel 4 * Dahab is the famous lagoon. This is a coastal bay, separated from the sea by a sand spit, which protects and protects surfers. Therefore, the water in the lagoon is always calm, and the constant wind is directed along the coast and does not carry the board into the open sea.





There are no corals on the lagoon beach (1 km long), unlike other Dahab beaches. There is a wide sandy beach, very convenient for swimming. And after low tide in shallow water you can find beautiful shells of various mollusks and bright starfish.

On the beaches of the Sea Sun Hotel 4 * snorkeling is also popular. Unlike diving, this is not diving, but swimming under the surface of the water with a snorkel, mask and fins.

Services

Dahab can be reached in 15 minutes by the free shuttle bus, which the hotel provides twice a day.

The hotel has an outdoor pool (300 square meters), several restaurants, a hookah room, billiards, a spa, an Internet cafe, a massage parlor, a first-aid post, a gym with exercise equipment, sports grounds and a children's play area.

Nutrition

Meals are offered according to typical resort standards BB (Bed and Breakfast), HB (Half Board) and AI (All Inclusive).

The package with the BB food system includes only breakfast, which usually consists of vegetable salads, bread, cheese and sausages (without hot dishes).

The HB system means the so-called half board, when a tourist can have breakfast and dinner for free. Lunch is not included, it can be obtained at an additional cost.

Half-board is the most popular since people come to hotels of this type for the sake of outdoor activities and leave the hotel for the whole day, returning only in the evening.

AI means ‚Äúall inclusive‚Äù (until ten in the evening). As part of the AI, you can have breakfast, lunch and dinner for free, as well as get local soft and alcoholic drinks poured into glasses. For all drinks (including plain water) in bottles, imported, as well as wine, fresh juices and drinks in bars, you must pay extra.

Sea Sun Hotel 4 * - reviews and opinions

Over the years, thousands of tourists have relaxed at the hotel with different expectations and ideas about the international and local level of relaxation, so the reviews are also very diverse.

Most tourists note the economy of vacation at Sea Sun Hotel 4 *, reviews of which are quite favorable given the cost of the trip and the quality of services.

Considering reasonable prices for vouchers, the hotel is fully suitable for people who prefer active recreation, go in for sports, diving, surfing and go on excursions, returning to the hotel mainly for overnight stays.

As tourists visiting Egypt say, Sea Sun Hotel 4 * is not very suitable for those who come to sleep, drink and lie on the beach, it is better to stay in other hotels with a developed entertainment system.

Among the advantages of the hotel, tourists indicate peace and quiet, as well as the proximity of the sea and diving centers.





Meals also correspond to the price of the voucher. The food is simple and satisfying, although a bit monotonous. The menu includes meat, vegetables, fish, sweets and fruits.

Often in positive reviews there is a small size and compactness of the hotel and infrastructure, when everything is nearby and there is no need to spend extra time and effort walking on long halls, stairs and passages, as in many other hotels.

The hotel has excellent massage therapists. According to tourists, massage sessions in Sea San are much better than in other hotels.

True, there are also not quite positive reviews about the hotel Sea Sun Hotel 4 * (Dahab). They include a description of some of the disadvantages.

Tips for travelers at Sea Sun Hotel 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh)

In connection with shallow water, it is better to enter the sea not from the coast, but from pontoons.

You should not go barefoot on the beach, as the coastal area is covered with corals, stones and sea urchins. You need to use special slippers, they can be bought right on the spot for $ 5-7 or brought with you.

The hotel does not accept Russian-language television channels, but there is Internet access. In this regard, many tourists bring laptops so as not to miss in the evenings.

The hotel staff only speaks Arabic or English, so you should bring along a dictionary or phrasebook.

It is best to bring instant tea and coffee bags (boiling water can be taken at the bar), and fruits, soft drinks and spirits to buy in Dahab (the cheapest is in the Assala area).

Diving and snorkeling equipment can be rented, but it is better to bring your mask and snorkel.

No need to take tours for excursions at the hotel or the Pegasus agency, it is much cheaper to buy tickets in Dahab.

The staff will be very friendly with tourists, if tourists are friendly themselves. The smile returns to those who smile.

On vacation it is always more fun to be in a big company, so you should try to make friends with other tourists.
