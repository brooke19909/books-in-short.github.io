How does an IP withdraw money from a current account? Methods for withdrawing cash from the current account of IP

Before registering yourself as an individual entrepreneur, you should consider that it is not very easy to withdraw funds from the current account of an individual entrepreneur, especially at first. How does an IP withdraw money from a current account?

But, despite this, there are several legal and convenient ways to cash out money from the current account of the individual entrepreneur. This article describes the withdrawal procedure and recommendations that will allow you not to attract excessive attention of the tax authorities and not fall under the high percentage of the bank's commission.

How to withdraw funds from a current account

Often in entrepreneurial activities there are situations when it is necessary to withdraw money from the account. There are 4 main ways to do this. How does an IP withdraw money from a current account? Each method should be considered separately.

Checkbook

This method was popular several years ago, but at the moment it is gradually losing its relevance. The withdrawal procedure can last three days, which causes inconvenience to the entrepreneur, especially if he urgently needs cash. Another feature of this method is that the bank is obligated to provide the client with documents confirming the withdrawal of funds from the account, as well as the write-off of VAT. A checkbook for IP can be used today, but other methods will be much more convenient in practice.

Bank card

If the amount is large, then you must first write an application, because sufficient funds may not be in the bank branch. You will be issued a cash settlement account with a current account. Then, as an individual entrepreneur, you must transfer the funds received at the bank to the balance sheet of your enterprise and only then can you use them for your own needs. This is one of the most common methods of withdrawing cash from the current account of IP.

Cash card

Similar cards have one more name - corporate. They are attached to your IP account and provide the full functionality of ordinary plastic cards, that is, you can cash out funds in any terminal at any time. But it is worth noting here that large amounts should not be withdrawn, especially at first. Otherwise, it will attract close attention from the tax authorities.

Individual card

To do this, you must first transfer the funds from the IP account to the account or card of an individual. You can transfer to an account belonging to the same bank as the current one. And then the entrepreneur can transfer funds via the Internet and cash them in the nearest machine. However, this can be done no earlier than a day after the transfer of money from the settlement account of the individual entrepreneur.
