How to calculate the cost of repairing an apartment?

Before you begin the long-awaited redevelopment of your home, you must correctly calculate the cost of repairing the premises. After all, the budget that we are going to allocate for updating should be clearly and competently distributed in order to avoid additional material costs. To more accurately and specifically answer the question of how to calculate the cost of repairing an apartment, you need to know what level of repair you plan to do.

People live in times of market relations, and there is competition among organizations in the construction market, but there are also average prices for finishing work that are already well established.

If you decide to calculate the cost of repairing an apartment yourself, then you need to consider some nuances. The least amount of money will be required to carry out cosmetic repairs - these are the types of work that you can do yourself. These include replacing wallpaper and leveling walls, covering the floor with carpet or linoleum and changing the floor level , replacing electrical outlets, installing interior doors and other types of work that do not require special training or professional education.





From the very beginning, it is necessary to calculate the right amount of building consumables - mortars for floor and wall treatment, coating for all substrates, fixing and adhesive mortars, improvised products (masking tape, tools) and the like. If you want to replace windows, then it is worth including in the estimate the cost of window structures and cash costs for paying for the work of builders.

To carry out the installation of electrical wiring, you need special tools - they will have to be purchased if you want to carry out repairs yourself.





All consumables, including screws, must be entered in the table in a column. To the right you will write the desired quantity, two other columns - the cost of a conventional unit and the amount for the entire quantity. Total will result in a figure that will mean all your costs for products.

In order to correctly calculate the cost of repairing an apartment, it is worth revising your list - maybe you need to replace some materials with inexpensive products that are not inferior in quality. Construction markets offer a fairly wide range of finishing products, so take a closer look at analogues - this way you can save a little and use the money for its intended purpose.





The purchase of a construction tool is the most important part of the estimate. If you want to correctly calculate the cost of repairing an apartment, then you do not need to save on this part of the costs - the final result depends on how efficiently and quickly the work is carried out. Choose only modern tools and products, the use of which helps to reduce the repair time and reduce the likelihood of injury.

A separate amount should be allocated costs for the purchase of specialized clothing (gloves, overalls, goggles, helmet, mask and the like) - take care of your health.

If you want to hire other specialists, then first stipulate the cost of their services, work details. Thus, you will prevent the occurrence of surprises and be able to prepare in the material plane. In order to correctly calculate the cost of repairing a room, do not forget to include in the budget the costs of additional work - construction waste removal, product delivery, and the like.
