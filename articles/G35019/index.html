What is lymph?



In humans, it is very closely associated with the circulatory system. Its tasks include neutralization, as well as the disposal of the most harmful waste. It also has the responsibility of pumping excess fluid, which is in the intercellular space, back into the vascular bed. Lymph works with the remains of decayed cells, various microbes, viruses, toxins, end products of human cells, tissues, and organs. She, like blood, pierced the whole body with her vessels.
