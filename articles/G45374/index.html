How to restart the tablet if it freezes?

A tablet is a multi-functional compact gadget that can easily compete in performance even with laptops. It can be used for calls, games, the Internet, as well as an e-book. But, unfortunately, it has long been clear that no computer hardware is safe from failures. And at any time you may encounter the fact that your tablet will freeze and will not respond to any manipulations with the On / Off button. What to do? How to restart the tablet? I will try to answer you these and other questions.

Possible reasons

Depending on the nature of the failure, the problems can be divided into two types:

  • Software. Perhaps you recently installed some new application, most likely it was it that crashed. A large number of simultaneously running programs can also affect the operation of your gadget. Another reason may be the presence of viruses in the device.
  • Hardware Many things can be attributed to this: damage to the board, connection of faulty or incompatible devices to the tablet, shock, moisture, etc.

How to restart the tablet? First way

So, if you were able to find out that the cause is still a software failure, then do the following:

  1. Try turning the tablet off by long pressing the On / Off button. If it doesn‚Äôt work, then carefully examine your gadget,
    it should have a small hole labeled Reset. Insert a needle, pin or paper clip into it. The tablet should turn off.
  2. Turn on the device. Wait for the full download.
  3. Copy all the important information to a flash card, and then extract it.
  4. Go to the "Settings" menu, then "Restore and reset" (in some versions this item may be called "Confidentiality").
  5. Complete a factory reset.

After all these steps, the tablet will restart. All settings will return to the factory settings. Important! Before resetting, connect your gadget to the network. It may take some time to reboot, so you cannot allow the battery to completely discharge. After all, if system recovery is not completed to the end, a flashing will be inevitable! This was the first way to restart the tablet.

Hard Reset Second way

This method is usually used when pressing Reset did not help, and also if you have a Chinese tablet. How to restart the gadget and return it to working condition? So, we pass to the most rigid procedure Hard Reset. I warn you right away: all the information that you yourself downloaded will disappear without a trace! All settings will return to the factory settings. We do the following:

  1. Remove the SIM card and flash card from the tablet.
    Thus, at least some information you have saved.
  2. Press the On / Off buttons at the same time. and volume control. Hold for 10-15 seconds, after which the device should vibrate.
  3. A menu will appear on the screen, in which the volume control button will need to select "Settings" and then "Format System".
  4. We stop on the line "Reset". We are waiting for the tablet to restart.

If the first time you did not succeed, try this procedure again. And if you are interested in how to restart the Texet tablet, then keep in mind that this is done in the same way, only the items in the menu that appears will be spelled out a little differently. You have to select first "Wipe Data" / "Factory Reset", and then "Reboot".

Conclusion

If the problem lies only in the software, then these two ways of how to restart the tablet will definitely help you. And if this is not so, then it would be better for you to go to the service center. Only experienced professionals can help your tablet.
