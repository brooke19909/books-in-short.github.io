Details on how to fold an elastic sheet

In this material we will describe how to fold a sheet on an elastic band. Step by step this process is described below. This approach allows you to significantly save space. This skill can be very useful.





Turnover

We turn to the practical part of solving the question of how to fold a sheet on an elastic band. We take it in our hands and hold it in length. Poke your fingertips into the far corner of the sheet, into the corner seam. Thus, we have in our hands one lower and upper part. As a result, the material must be completely turned inside out.

Angles

We move on to the next step in resolving the issue of how to fold a sheet on an elastic band. We put the first corner into the second. Bring your fingertips together. Turn the corner so that the outside is outside. Go to the next step.





So, how to fold a sheet on an elastic band? We hold both corners with one hand. As a result, on one side we should get a short loop, and on the other, a long loop. Put your fingers on the undercut seam. He should touch his hand. Next we get to the next corner of our sheet. Go to the next step. Poke the corner. We take part of the sheet. We poke at two angles.

Further instruction

Before you fold the sheet on an elastic band, you need to practice several times. We take the last previously unused corner. The rest should be folded by this time. Poke this corner. We also place it under the three that are already in the hand. The wrong side of the fabric should touch the inside of our stack. Shake the sheet. In this case, the long edge should hang from one side, the short one from the opposite. Go to the next step. Take the short side around the corner or bend. Shake the whole sheet. This will help to straighten it. Put it in a rectangle or square. The edge with the elastic should pass on two sides. We straighten the sheet. Put it on a flat surface. We straighten it in the form of a square. You may have to iron out one of the edges. We straighten the maximum number of folds. Keep the corners together. Fold the sheet in parts. There may be 3 or 4. The number in this case depends on the size of the sheet.





The thickness of the fabric can also affect the finished result. We straighten the folded sheet. We fold it in such a way that it turns out flat. Such an approach will avoid wrinkles in the future. Now the main task is completed. We recommend folding pillowcases and sheets in the same way. This will keep all bedding items together. They can be stacked and tied with a rope or ribbon. The approach described above allows you to store bedding in order. At the same time, you can more effectively use the space in the cabinet. Outwardly, the result of such folding looks more diligently. In addition, for greater convenience, all sets of sheets can be stored inside one of the pillowcases.
