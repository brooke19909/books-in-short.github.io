Economy boarding houses of Crimea. Inexpensive rest in Crimea. Photos and reviews of tourists

One of the most famous health resorts is the Crimean Peninsula. Its pensions and resorts are famous for the comfortable accommodation of vacationers and a variety of wellness programs. The peninsula has the status of a health resort zone, the main value of which is considered to be unique natural resources and climatic conditions. The main advantage that distinguishes local modern pensions is the ability to take care of their own health at the same time and enjoy the full range of entertainment at the resort.



The optimal location of institutions near medical resources (mud lakes, mineral springs) and the coast of the sea also distinguishes them favorably. Economy boarding houses of Crimea on popular beaches near entertainment facilities give their guests the opportunity to plan a good rest program. The Tangier boarding house located in the city of Saki enjoys great popularity among the guests of the peninsula. It offers all the necessary conditions for a comfortable family vacation. Just imagine that you can visit a first-class medical center every day, enjoying therapeutic pleasant procedures, after which you can go on an exciting excursion or relax on a beautiful beach - you can only dream of such a wellness holiday.

Another important feature that distinguishes Crimea is the economy class boarding houses, which are abundant here. Even in high season, you can rent a comfortable room at attractive prices. For these establishments, a multiple increase in prices in the summer is uncharacteristic.

Pension "Brigantine"

Pension "Brigantine" is a holiday complex. It was opened in 1999. The boarding house is located in the village of Beregovoe, near Feodosia, not far from one of the best sandy beaches of Crimea - Zolotoy. Five three-four-story comfortable buildings are located on the seashore, in a real oasis, as you can find out by reading reviews about the institution.



On its territory among the trees is a fountain-waterfall and gazebos. The territory is divided into 2 parts, while on one of them are the first 3 buildings, on the other - the second 2. Economy rooms, with amenities, double, 1- and 2-bedroom.

Cost: from 1350 rubles / person.

Pension "Massandra"

Pension "Massandra" is located in the eponymous eastern region of Yalta, in a cozy, quiet and picturesque corner of the Massandra Ancient Park. It appeared in the 1830s, while the boarding house, then called the Massandra Hotel, opened its doors to guests in 1958.



The institution received the status of a boarding house in 2005, after a complete reconstruction and privatization of two 4-storey buildings. Judging by the reviews, conference rooms, cafes and office rooms were added to the renovated, as well as furnished in a neoclassical modern style rooms.
