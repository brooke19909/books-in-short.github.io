Seeka Boutique 3 * (Thailand / Phuket) - photos, prices, description and reviews

Patong is one of the most popular resort regions of the island of Phuket, owned by Thailand. Thousands of travelers from different countries come here every year, including from Russia and other states of the former Soviet Union. Basically, this place was chosen by young people, since there is always an atmosphere of unrestrained fun. Patong is often even called the "Asian party capital", in addition, there is a wide selection of hotels for every taste and budget. If you are planning an economical vacation in Patong, then consider Seeka Boutique 3 * hotel as an option for accommodation. Next, we offer to learn more about this hotel, as well as to find out whether our compatriots liked it here.





Where is

This hotel is located on Patong Hill, some distance from the city center. So, the distance to Bangla Road, famous for its nightclubs, restaurants, bars, and also the sex industry, is one and a half kilometers. About the same distance separates the hotel from the nearest beach. Due to the fact that you can get bored of walking these distances within a couple of days after arrival, experienced travelers recommend renting a motorbike or using a taxi.

How to get there

The nearest international airport to Seeka Boutique 3 * is located in Phuket. The distance to it is about 35 kilometers. So after arrival, your journey to the hotel will take about 30-40 minutes. If you travel with the assistance of a travel agency, then a transfer will most likely be provided at the airport. If you went to Thailand on your own, you can get to your hotel from the air harbor by taxi limo, ordinary taxi or bus. The first option is the most expensive, but, as practice shows, the most attractive for our compatriots.





Phuket, Seeka Boutique Resort 3 *: photo and description

Seeka Boutique is a small three-star hotel located in Patong, Phuket. Due to the fact that it is located some distance from the center, the guests will not be disturbed by noise, and they will be able to relax and unwind in peace and quiet.
