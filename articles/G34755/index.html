Calypso Patong 3 Beach Hotel (Thailand / Phuket): description, photos and reviews

For a long time, Thailand has been a leader in the ranking of the most popular holiday destinations among our compatriots. In this wonderful corner of the globe, where there are so many sunny days in the year and even in winter the temperature remains comfortable for swimming, tourists are attracted by the picturesque tropical landscape, the blue windless bays with bays and the sparkling warm sea.

According to statistics, most often guests visit the island of Phuket, which is famous for its long white beaches, active entertainment, high hospitality, magnificent architectural buildings and inexpensive hotels. Even those who do not have large incomes can choose an acceptable accommodation option with high quality service and amenities.





The Calypso Patong 3 hotel complex opens its doors, designed for a picky client who wants to spend time at an affordable price in an interesting, informative and serene way. Convenient location makes the hotel even more popular. It is located in the central part of Phuket, close to business centers, sports schools, shopping and entertainment facilities. Opposite the complex there is a currency exchange with a favorable rate.

Getting to the hotel is not difficult - from the airport the road does not take an hour. Let your dreams come true, give your children the excitement and joy of visiting the water park, elephant trekking, and scuba diving. There will be creative entertainment for an adult audience: rafting, a tour of the pearl farm, diving, surfing on artificial waves.

A rich list of creative events will not leave indifferent either a calm tourist or a zealous activist. Arriving at the complex Calypso Patong 3 (Thailand), you will not be deprived of attention, you will be satisfied with the service and quality of service. For a little money, you can stay in a comfortable room, a daily hot breakfast and a shuttle service.

Residence





The modern five-story building of the Calypso Patong 3 Hotel (Phuket) is decorated in a classic style, it is snow-white in color and has balconies. Only 28 rooms of various categories are concentrated in it: standard and deluxe rooms. Children under eight years old can stay for free - sharing the same bed with their parents. There is a discount for older children.

All rooms are decorated in warm colors, equipped with air conditioning, a private bathroom and the necessary furniture. Each room has satellite TV, free high-speed Wi-Fi. A compact fridge is available, and some rooms have an electric kettle with kitchenware and iron.

If necessary, all household appliances (small) can be requested at the reception. Daily maid service is provided. On certain days, bedding and bath towels are replaced (more often, on request).

Catering





If you wish, guests arriving at Calypso Patong 3 can pay for the morning meal, which is served in the local restaurant - √† la carte. The diet is not too rich, but you can eat: toast, sausages, fresh vegetables, scrambled eggs and hot drinks. Near the hotel there are many cozy establishments for different tastes and affluence, where they feed excellently and tasty.

Coastline

Unfortunately, Calypso Patong 3 does not have its own beach area, but this does not prevent it from being one of the best in its category in Thailand. Nearby - about a kilometer - stretches a sandy coastline owned by the municipality. You can walk to it on your own (the road will take about 15-20 minutes) or take the bus (for a fee).

Admission is absolutely free. The beach is clean, strewn with pleasant fine sand, cabins for changing clothes are working on the shore, there are equipment rental points. It offers vacationers diverse entertainment - from volleyball to riding a scooter. Refreshing kiosks are located everywhere and bars are open.

Leisure activities

For affordable payment, tourists will be offered the best conditions for excursion, beach, peaceful and active recreation. Guests at Calypso Patong 3 will not have to think about parking (those who drive), as there is a public guarded parking nearby. The front desk has a safe and luggage storage.

For a surcharge, they will offer professional dry cleaning and laundry services. A travel agency with a wide range of excursion programs is open in the building. In your free time from routes and bathing, visit the massage room or rent a bicycle and ride around. Services are provided (for a fee) delivery to the airport.

Calypso Patong 3 Hotel: guest reviews





A lot of positive reviews have been written about this complex. Tourists highlight several of the main advantages of the hotel: cleanliness, tidy rooms, hospitable staff and affordable ticket prices. Also, many liked the region and location.

All interesting objects are within easy reach - there is no need to overcome hundreds of kilometers. The hotel can be recommended to active travelers eager for new discoveries and constant movement, those who will not be constantly in the hotel building.
