Beach holidays in Italy in October: weather and reviews of tourists

Is a beach holiday in Italy possible in October? This question cannot be answered unequivocally. Indeed, during the transitional periods, which include spring and autumn, the weather changes day by day, and therefore the climatic conditions in the first days of October are significantly different from those that come at the end of the month. Yes, and the Apennine peninsula, which is occupied by Italy, stretches in the form of a boot far from north to south. And if it can be said that it is hot in the whole territory of this South European country in summer, then in the middle of autumn everything is not so simple. Well, let's look at what Italy will please a tourist in October. Weather, beach vacations and reviews of tourists about it will tell us whether to go to this country in the fall and which route to choose for travel. Note that they are sent to Italy not only for the sake of sea bathing and tanning. Beach holidays in this country are rather an additional bonus to a rich excursion program. Beach holidays in Italy in early October in the north of the country

While the breath of autumn is already felt in the countries of Western Europe north of the Alps, summer continues on the Apennine Peninsula. But coolness comes to the northern regions of Italy. On the great lakes - Garda, Lago Maggiore, Como - beach vacation is already unthinkable. Seas, Ligurian and Adriatic, due to the large volume of water still store summer heat. But the number of rainy days is increasing. Also, storms are increasingly being felt. In principle, in early October you can still catch the last tan in the elite resort of the Ligurian Riviera Portofino. But hardened natures can only buy. South of Italy in early October

Gastronomic tours

Do not forget about another tourist destination, which Italy beckons to travelers in October. Other events

In mid-autumn, when there is no heat, and in some places a slight coolness reigns, you can realize your dream and travel around the cultural treasury of the world, which is Italy. A tour (October for this is one of the best months) can cover the whole country or some separate region of it. Especially popular are trips to Rome. In October, the Eternal City hosts the Festival of Art and Sacred Music, which attracts celebrities from all over the world. In the second month of autumn, International Museum Day is also celebrated. It is very important to be on this date somewhere in Florence, Naples, Verona, Rome. After all, then all museums hospitably open their doors for tourists and work on their professional holiday for free. In general, the golden autumn allows tourists to plunge into the ancient and the beautiful: to devote all time to the contemplation of historical and cultural attractions.
