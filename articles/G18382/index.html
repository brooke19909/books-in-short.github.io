Bielefeld (Germany): Local attractions of the largest city in the region of Ostwestfalen-Lipe

In the north-eastern part of North Rhine-Westphalia, there is such a wonderful city as Bielefeld. Germany is famous for its various beautiful places, therefore many tourists go to this country. And Bielefeld, the largest city in the entire Ostwestfalen-Lipe region, is also worth a visit for travel lovers.





For lovers of architecture

This city has a very rich and ancient history. This is famous for Bielefeld. Germany, in turn, is known for its luxurious castles. And this architectural work of art is located in this city. Sparenburg Castle - that's what it is called. The oldest building in the whole city. It began to be built almost immediately after the foundation of Bielefeld - in 1240. True, the construction was carried out for 10 years. This castle is the main defensive structure of the city. More than once the fortress was besieged, however, Sparenburg withstood any attacks. Not so long ago, the castle was reconstructed, and several observation platforms were opened specifically for tourists.





Museums

The city in Germany Bielefeld is also famous for its museums. One of the most attractive for tourists is located in the estate, built in the distant XVII century. This is the Museum of Natural History. Inside is a rich collection of minerals. Also, various exhibitions are often held there.

The Museum of Peasant Life is also interesting. Opened it in 1917. It is interesting that the exposition itself is in the open. Arriving at this place, you can look at weaving looms and other antique products. In the museum there are even a bakery of the XVIII century and a windmill.

In general, there are many wonderful museums in the city of Bielefeld. Germany is interesting because it has something to see, and all the sights are not alike. And local museums (there are only 13 in this city) will appeal to even those people who are not used to going to such establishments. By the way, if you just want to take a walk, you can go to Alter Markt Square. There is a picturesque fountain of Mercury, which immediately attracts attention.

And if you want to look at something modern, then you can pay attention to the University of Bielefeld.





Other entertainments

What else, in addition to the above, can Bielefeld (Germany) boast? The photos provided above demonstrate that this town is very attractive. And there is entertainment for every taste. If you want to relax in nature, but not leave the city, you should go to the City Park. It is beautiful and picturesque, and the rose garden is nearby.

But this is not all that Bielefeld is famous for. Germany is a country of high quality products. And in this city there are many fashion boutiques and shopping centers. Therefore, you should take a spare empty suitcase with you to Bielefeld, so that you can take away purchased gifts, jewelry and things. By the way, unforgettable shopping can happen during Christmas. During this period, a huge number of wooden stalls appear on the streets of the city, where you can buy local sweets, wine, as well as Christmas decorations and souvenirs.
