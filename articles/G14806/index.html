Hotel Rahoni Cronwell Park Hotel 5 * (Greece, Halkidiki, Kassandra): description, services, reviews

Location

To begin with, it is worth saying a few words about the location of the Rahoni Cronwell Park Hotel 5. This complex is located on the second coastline. The hotel rooms offer scenic views of the Aegean Sea, which can be reached in just a few minutes.

Neo Skioni is a former fishing village, which is located in a very cozy, quiet, secluded place. The only negative is the airport is far away. It is located in Thessaloniki. Get from there to the hotel for about an hour and forty minutes. But there is a definite plus. Airplanes do not interfere with the guests with their characteristic noise during takeoff and landing.

In general, these places are an ideal recreation area. After all, a 5-star boutique hotel is located on a hill, where olive and coniferous trees grow on each meter. In addition, the hotel complex has a very beautiful and well-groomed surrounding area. Exotic and fruit trees are planted here, which pleases the eye and creates an atmosphere of comfort. By the way, it is interesting that the Skioni coast has been awarded the EU Blue Flag for its cleanliness for several years in a row.





What is in the complex?

Rahoni Cronwell Park Hotel 5 * is also interesting in that it meets the highest environmental standards. But the rooms are especially pleasing. Also on site is a restaurant called Skionic. Upon arrival, hotel guests can enjoy Mediterranean and European cuisine in a cozy atmosphere. In Rahoni Cronwell Park Hotel 5 * there are many ways to have fun. The complex has an open-air swimming pool that has been operating for all 12 months, as well as a sun terrace and a stunning, picturesque garden.
