IPod - what is it? Introducing the line of devices

Today we will answer the question: "iPod - what is it?" This is one of the masterpieces of the great electronics artists of Apple. Designed to showcase music, videos and photos. It was created during the life of Steve Jobs and was one of his best creations.

So iPod - what is it? We will discuss the development of these devices and consider the modern line of iPods, which are now available for sale.

iPod shuffle: the beginning of evolutionary development

This device, which was launched in 2005, has a memory capacity of up to 4 gigabytes, which was an excellent feature for a small piece of metal of that time. Without a display, but with a very convenient attachment to clothes, this iPod works without stopping for about 12 hours. The kit includes a USB cable, headphones, two stickers with the company logo of the appropriate size. Very small, comfortable, he once made a big "explosion" in the market of mobile music players.



iPod classic - the first "wise man" among iPod models

Indeed, this is one of the first models of players with a display and an operating system. It has 160 gigabytes of memory, which is very suitable for real music lovers. This device can be used as a portable hard disk due to its capacity. Of course, it has an excellent stylish design, like the entire iPod lineup presented, as well as a display with the ability to view video files. It can play music without stopping for up to 36 hours, which is also very remarkable: out of the analogues presented in Russia, units have such battery capacity.

iPod nano 6 - the most common of this line

This iPod is not yet ideal, but close to it. The same small aluminum box as the previous model, but already with a touch screen and all the standard features for these devices (radio, Bluetooth, and so on), it has a memory of 4 gigabytes to 16 gigabytes. The biggest plus of this device is the ability to use it as a watch, and the screen can be customized. Putting on such a watch, you seem to find yourself in the near future, which is very pleasant to feel.



iPod nano 7 - one of the new heights in the evolution of Apple devices

The shape of this iPod is somewhat reminiscent of the small iPhone 5. With an updated operating system and a slightly larger touch screen, the iPod has become more suitable for watching videos. The new earpods are very stylish, but not everyone fits because of their unusual shape.

iPod touch - the evolutionary king of iPod

Ordinary people call it an iPhone without a SIM card. It has everything: wifi, AppStore, a camera with 5 megapixels, a display with Retina screen support. There is even a strap for convenience, so that it is always at hand.

About iPod Disadvantages

There are few flaws in the entire iPod line, and even those are small. First, mandatory synchronization with iTunes and Apple ID. Secondly, none of the presented iPods is fully compatible with other High-end headphones, except for the series of famous Beats headphones.

To summarize

How can I answer the question: "iPod - what is it?" Something like this: "iPod is a small entertainment station designed for the average user, which can replace a large number of different devices." If you want to keep up with the times, then buy an iPod, and then you will get the most accurate answer to the question: "iPod - what is it?"
