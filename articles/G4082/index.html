Blouses are summer, models for magnificent beauty

For some reason, the ideal of female beauty is considered to be very thin, masculine female figures. Unfortunately, only thin girls remain the favorite models of designers for a long time. How to choose a color

When choosing models of blouses for obese women, it is worthwhile to dwell on solid, but rich colors. But if you want something with a pattern, then the main rule for colored clothes is that the pattern should be vertical, such blouses, summer sundresses and blouses look original. If these are floral motifs, then they should not be too large.





Where to sew clothes

Patterns of summer blouses, trousers, skirts and other clothes can be found in special magazines. As a rule, they include ready-made patterns with a dimensional grid and detailed sewing instructions. But if you are not ready to take up sewing on your own, then it is better to turn to a professional seamstress. To save money, it is better to find not an atelier, but a homeworker, the quality of tailoring will be the same, but the price for work will be much lower.
