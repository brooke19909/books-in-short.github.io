St. George Hotel 4 * (Cyprus) - photos, prices and reviews

St. George Hotel 4 * - a luxurious, beautiful and elegant hotel, located in the heart of Greece - on the island of Crete. Tourists constantly come here to relax, sunbathe and swim in the warm sea.





general information

First of all, I would like to note the attractive appearance of the hotel complex. This chic building immediately catches your eye and attracts attention. And, I must say, in this case, the appearance is not deceiving - inside the hotel is decorated in the same stylish and beautiful way. It is really nice to be in such a place. The hotel administration took care of creating the most comfortable conditions, both for leisure and for official meetings. The hotel territory is well-groomed, clean, it is decorated with shady palm trees and a spacious pool in which guests can swim at any time or sunbathe nearby, on the terrace. By the way, one more pleasant moment should be noted - this complex is located on the seashore, and the windows of the hotel rooms offer simply stunning views. In principle, the hotel is in a rather favorable location, because next to it there is everything you need - various taverns, shops and much more, recommended for visiting.





Rest for the soul

I want to say that St. George Hotel 4 * is not just a hotel where people stay to spend the night. This is a truly full-fledged hotel complex with all the conditions that are needed for both leisure and entertainment. For example, the hotel offers a huge selection of bars and restaurants of various formats. Guests can drink aromatic coffee alone in a cozy cafe or take part in an incendiary dance program. Organizers working at the hotel always offer interesting and non-standard events. All guests staying at St. George Hotel 4 *, left with a lot of impressions left by local shows. About institutions should say a little more. People sitting at tables in the restaurant can enjoy stunning views of the sea. Only the best cocktails are served in the pool bar - any connoisseur of strong and weak drinks will find the one that he will like. And, of course, it is worth noting the attention of the amphitheater, because there are twice a day there are various dance shows and musical performances.





For lovers of an active lifestyle

Many people cannot imagine their life without sports and everything related to it. In St. George Beach Hotel 4 * such individuals will not be bored for sure, if only because there are two whole tennis courts with lighting, a hall designed for squash, and a mini-golf course. Here you can play beach volleyball or swim in the pool. Men will definitely enjoy the billiards room, and people who like to explore the area can rent a bike and go for a drive. On the territory of the hotel complex there is a real paradise called the Renaissance Center. There is a magnificent gym, equipped with everything you need. And, of course, guests are offered a jacuzzi, a sauna, a bathhouse, an indoor pool and even a massage parlor. As you can see, in St. George Hotel 4 * (Paphos) will not be bored even if you want to stay at the hotel one day and not go anywhere.

Formality

It was said that St. George Hotel 4 * (Paphos) is a good place not only for life and relaxation, but also for official events with the participation of senior VIPs. Indeed, business meetings and conferences are often held here. Influential businessmen who at least once organized events here were pleased with the level of decoration of the hall and rooms for private meetings. There is everything necessary for this - professional equipment, equipment, comfortable conditions of stay and much more. The level will delight any entrepreneur.

Accommodation

St. George Hotel 4 * (Cyprus) has 255 rooms. All of them are tastefully decorated, distinguished by special luxury and undeniable comfort. These rooms have a good time. The apartments fully comply with all the requirements for four-star hotels. They have different levels of comfort, but the conditions are excellent everywhere. Some rooms, by the way, can be combined with others. Thus, apartments of the "1 + 1" type are obtained.





What is worth noting regarding the conditions? Guests can choose rooms with a double bed or with two single beds - at their discretion. A few words should be said about the studios. All apartments have sea views. In addition, such rooms are much more spacious than standard ones (area 32 sq. M). And finally, luxury apartments. Their area is about 50 square meters. m, and the room itself is equipped with everything for a comfortable life and even more - a bedroom, living room, bathroom, toilet, exit to a balcony or terrace, etc. The rooms also have: air conditioning, coffee maker, refrigerator, satellite TV, minibar and even such pleasant things as a safe, hairdryer and telephone.

Care for children

Guests who choose to stay at St. George Hotel 4 *, along with their children, can not worry about how they will feel. For such guests, the hotel provides an additional service called friendly service. This is a real find for parents who want to be alone on vacation. The service provides them with complete freedom - if they want to be alone, go somewhere, relax, then professional specialists with a pedagogical education will take their children under their control. Children will feel relaxed, as the atmosphere of the apartment is filled with home comfort. Therefore, parents can safely enjoy their vacation, while their child will develop with the teacher. The hotel also organizes classes designed for children from 4 to 10 years old. The program is called Soft play center, and anyone can use it. By the way, it is worth noting that for children at the hotel there is even a separate pool and a playground for games.





Sights and cultural heritage

People who like various archaeological finds, sights and much more will definitely be delighted when staying at the St. George. A five-minute walk separates guests from the stunning harbor of Paphos. You can not ignore the church of St. George Chapel, which is the most important historical monument. By the way, it was she who was guided by the designers who developed the architectural plan of the hotel. The complex itself is located between the picturesque hills and the Mediterranean Sea. The hotel looks like a blooming garden - most of it is occupied by tall trees. Museums, exhibitions, monuments, ancient architecture - all this is just a stone's throw from the hotel. Certainly, while staying here on vacation, one cannot fail to devote at least one day to exploring local attractions. By the way, the hotel even has a service for organizing excursions and hiking - this is especially true for those people who are poorly oriented in other cities, but want to enjoy the surroundings.

For body and soul

Girls who like to take care of themselves and cannot imagine life without cosmetic procedures will enjoy spending time in the spa. In St. George Hotel SPA 4 * has a professional beauty salon, which offers to go through a variety of procedures. These are face masks, pedicures and manicures, instant tans. You can combine business with pleasure and undergo procedures that help improve vision. If you want to change something in yourself, then professional hairdressers will help with this. By the way, girls can even undergo thalassotherapy, offered here, or sign up for yoga. And finally, I would like to note that the hotel also has a fitness center. Based on all of the above, it is safe to say that a good rest is possible even if guests do not want to leave the territory of the hotel complex.

Service and Facilities

The staff that serves guests is distinguished by friendliness, friendliness and understanding. This is important, because the quality of rest depends on how the hotel treats its guests. Here, guests can offer a variety of services, from early registration to the provision of individual services. The hotel administration made sure that the guests did not worry about anything, and arranged parking at the hotel - especially for those people who do not part with the "iron horse" even on vacation. The complex also has dry cleaning and laundry services, a point where you can exchange currency, car rental, as well as fax and mail. A couple of words I want to say about room service - cleaning is regularly carried out and bed linen is changed. All at the highest level.





Guest Comments

People who came to Paphos and stayed at this place were satisfied with the St. George Hotel 4 *. Reviews, at least, are direct proof of this. Some even claim that the hotel matches all five stars. First of all, the hotel staff is noted - friendly and polite, always ready to help in solving questions or problems. Also, guests praise local dishes and drinks on offer - everything is tasty, high-quality and in large quantities. The guests liked the rooms - clean, neat, bright. They have everything you need to relax. All in all, it is an ideal place for those people who love comfort and luxury. And finally, it is worth noting the most important thing - the cost. For a hotel of this level, it is very small. For example, a luxurious Internet Rate with all conveniences will cost two people 66 euros per day. If we summarize all the many reviews left by guests, then we can say something like the following: George Hotels - this is an excellent cuisine, excellent service, first-class bars and restaurants, a huge selection of classes for various interests, and all this at an affordable price.
