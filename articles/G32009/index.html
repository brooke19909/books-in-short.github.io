Primorsky (Abkhazia): rest for tourists

For decades, the attention of tourists has been attracted by the village of Primorsk. Abkhazia is the place of its localization. Unique hydrogen sulfide springs and healing clay have provided this small village with the status of one of the highest-quality healing resorts in the Caucasus.

Seaside, or Tskuara, as locals call it in their own language, has all the features of a first-class medical resort: a mirror-clear sea, clean and not crowded beaches of the Black Sea, mountains and beautiful landscapes. All this is available to visitors at very low prices. Excursions, special programs for rehabilitation and a minimum of city bustle of the modern metropolis - this is the formula that can be used to describe the village of Primorsky in Abkhazia.





Seaside, or Tskuara

You will find the village on the map if you pay attention to the Gudauta district of Abkhazia. It is located in close proximity to New Athos - only seven kilometers. The name of the village changed over time. Today, for example, Tskuara is the official name, which is more convenient for Georgian visitors and Abkhazians, but not for Russian tourists. For the latter, the old Soviet name, which the village bore from 1925 to 1948, Primorskoe, is more familiar.

Abkhazia, in comparison with other countries (Turkey and Egypt, in particular), is not a very popular resort. Therefore, Russian tourists do not get here very often, although they are still the main part of visitors.

Hydrogen sulfide baths

What can offer its visitors a vacation in Primorsky? Abkhazia has a unique geological environment, so that hydrogen sulfide water - the main trump card of Primorsky - will help you spend a unique vacation. This water is popularly called "living." And such a comparison was used not in vain: although a special course of ten hydrogen sulfide therapeutic baths can not be called a panacea, a cure for all diseases, it will help to raise the level of self-awareness qualitatively for sure.

"Live" baths for 15 minutes a day help with problems with the cardiovascular and nervous systems, digestive organs, musculoskeletal system, skin. Obesity, insomnia, radiation sickness are also on the list of those ailments that can be overcome by a hydrogen sulfide bath. No wonder this hospital was very popular in Soviet times.
