Hotel One Resort Monastir 4 * (Monastir, Tunisia): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Monastir is a famous resort city, which is located on the shores of the gentle Mediterranean Sea. The name of this place is translated from Latin as "monastery". This is due to the fact that before the main object of this city was considered a fortress with warrior-monks who defended the castle from enemy attacks.

This resort offers a wide variety of recreational opportunities for tourists from all over the world. If you compare Monastir with Sousse and Hammamet, it turns out that there is an amazing atmosphere of calm and tranquility. The main public of the town are couples and senior citizens.

Many people who came to Monastir manage to combine both beach, and active, and sightseeing vacations at the same time. As for the latter, the most visited place of the resort is the ruins of Rabat Hortem, which at one time served as a defensive structure and was built in the 8th century BC. e. Climbing to the observation deck of the fortress, you can see in all its glory the city of Monastir and the Muslim cemetery of Kubba.

At this place, among other things, is the Imam's mosque, whose construction dates back to the 12th century. She is called Sidi El Meseri. Among the inhabitants of the resort town there are many interesting legends that children who have been inside the church, who suffer from any ailment, are sure to be healed. The observation tower of Nador is another famous place with a beautiful view of the whole fabulous landscape of the city.

The central territory of Monastir is interesting in a landmark called Mortir, which has the shape of a multifaceted figure. This same polyhedron plays the role of a memorial erected in memory of the dead inhabitants of the city.

In Monastir there are a huge number of various ways of leisure: it is yachting, and horse racing, and golf, and diving, and surfing, and windsurfing, and tennis, and much, much more. In a word, there is everything for a full fun pastime in a pleasant company.

In this article, the most popular among tourists hotel complexes Delphin Monastir Resort 4 * and One Resort Monastir will be presented. Let's consider them in more detail.

This cozy and comfortable hotel has a high classification among competitors for the ultra all-inclusive service system. The complex is located in close proximity to a beautifully equipped sandy beach, on which for a fee vacationers are offered sun loungers, deck chairs, sports equipment, the use of changing rooms and a variety of soft drinks.
The location of the One Resort Monastir 4 * hotel (Tunisia) is considered to be very advantageous, since the international airport of Monastir is located 2 kilometers from it, and the central part of the city is only 8 km away.
