Hotels in Kaluga in the city center: overview, addresses, reviews

Kaluga is an old Russian city located on the banks of the Oka River, included in the list of cities included in the Golden Ring. In addition, it is a large industrial center. Many tourists and business people come here, so there is a need to create comfortable conditions for them to relax. Hotels in Kaluga in the city center will welcome their guests and create a truly homely atmosphere for them.

Hotel Oka

This hotel is located in the historical center of Kaluga. Hotel "Oka" is located next to administrative buildings, a park of culture and leisure, a river station and other interesting places. In just 25 minutes you can walk from here to the Tsiolkovsky Museum of the History of Cosmonautics. The train station is 3.5 kilometers from the hotel. The on-site caf√© serves European cuisine. You can park your car in the free, private, guarded parking lot. Free internet access is available in public areas. A vending machine with drinks is on site. For a fee, guests can relax in the sauna. Conference and banquet facilities are available. To accommodate guests in the hotel there are such room options:

  • double with two single beds - 1600 rubles per day;
  • Superior double with two single beds - 1800 rubles per day;
  • double with one double bed - 2000 rubles per day;
  • Junior Suite - 2200 rubles per day;
  • Deluxe - 3000 rubles per day;
  • Suite - 3500 rubles per day.

Room rates include accommodation only. Upon request, guests can additionally order breakfast. The hotel is located in Kaluga in the center at 10 Karpova Street.

Hotel "Scarlet Sails"

This hotel is also located in the historic city center. In just five minutes you will reach the Holy Trinity Cathedral, and in ten minutes - to the Kaluga Museum of Local Lore. At Scarlet Sails Hotel, guests can relax in the sauna with a plunge pool. The restaurant offers Russian, European and Mexican cuisines. The restaurant has a maritime theme. The hotel has a library. Guests can leave their car in the free private secure parking. You can use the internet for free throughout. A shuttle service to or from the airport can be arranged upon request. Laundry and ironing services are available at a surcharge. The hotel staff will help you organize a banquet. The following room categories are offered to accommodate guests:

  • single standard - 2400 rubles per day;
  • single comfort - 2800 rubles per day;
  • double - 3400 rubles per day;
  • Studio - 3900 rubles per day for two and 3300 rubles for one person;
  • Suite - 3900 rubles per day for two and 3300 rubles per person.

Room rates include breakfast and dinner. Upon request, payment is only possible with breakfast or without any meals. The hotel is located at 37/2 Teatralnaya Street.

Hotel Vintage

This hotel is located in the old part of the city near the Drama Theater, the park of culture and leisure, the museum of local lore. Within walking distance there is a supermarket, market, cafes and restaurants. Free private guarded parking is available at the hotel. Wireless internet is also free for guests. Laundry, ironing and shoe shine services are available at an extra charge. Guests have access to a shared kitchen. For accommodation, guests are offered the following rooms:

  • single superior - 2650 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 3325 rubles per day;
  • improved family - 3689 rubles per day;
  • double comfort - 3845 rubles per day.

The indicated price includes accommodation and breakfast, which is served according to the buffet system. At the request of guests, accommodation without breakfast is possible. All rooms are air-conditioned and have a safe. The Vintage Hotel is located at 17a Teatralnaya Street.
