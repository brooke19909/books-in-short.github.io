Therefore, before setting the price, it is necessary to monitor the secondary real estate market and carefully study the main criteria that affect pricing.

Giving real estate presentation

When you decide on the cost, it is recommended to start buying / selling an apartment with a repair. This is especially true if it is in an old house or not in the best condition. Appearance has a very strong influence on decision-making by potential buyers. According to statistics, the vast majority of buyers when choosing a home pay first attention to the condition of the bathroom. Therefore, the toilet should be in perfect condition and not exude stench. It is because of the poor bathroom that the sale of housing most often breaks down.

Paperwork

Let's dwell on this aspect in more detail. If you want the transaction to proceed normally, it is very important to formalize it in accordance with all the norms of the current legislation. Where to start selling an apartment? Paperwork is one of the important stages to which most people do not pay due attention. You will need to collect the following:

  • documents confirming the legality of ownership of real estate;
  • technical certificate;
  • title document received at the Unified State Register;
  • extract from the state real estate cadastre;
  • information on form No. 9;
  • statement that the seller has no debts to utilities;
  • the consent of the husband or wife to the sale of housing in writing, certified by a notary;
  • if you have children under the age of 18, then permission from the guardianship authorities will also be required.

Clearance transaction

How does it happen and what is the peculiarity of this process? If you want to sell real estate in a house that has not yet been commissioned, there are many nuances to consider. According to experts, the start of sales of apartments in new buildings, after documents are drawn up and a buyer is found, this is the preparation of a preliminary contract. This is necessary so that the interested person confirms his intentions to complete the transaction. This document insures the seller in case the buyer may change his mind for any reason. In this case, an advance is paid, the size of which is negotiated individually.

Further, it will already be possible to start official registration of the purchase / sale of real estate. The main thing here is to draw up a contract correctly. In order to prevent mistakes, you can take a ready-made form from the reference legal base or seek the help of qualified lawyers. The document in the smallest details should be spelled out all the conditions of the transaction, a full description of the signs indicating the subject of the transaction, as well as its value. The contract is filled in two copies - for the buyer and seller.

At the final stage, the rights of the new owner are registered and the apartment is transferred to him. To do this, you must submit to the Rosreestr an application of the established form, attaching the following to it:

  • contract of sale;
  • copies of passports of all participants in the transaction;
  • extract from the state register on the right to own real estate;
  • checks confirming the payment of all fees and charges.

Only after this the act on the transfer of housing is signed and the new owner can move into his apartment. The sale of housing in the secondary and primary market is completely similar, so you can safely follow the instructions described above.

What if real estate has an encumbrance?

Where to start buying / selling an apartment if it is pledged by a financial institution, under arrest or on a lease with a life sentence? Immediately it is worth noting that the chances of finding buyers in this case are very much reduced, and you will not be able to get a favorable price. Any residential or commercial property that has any encumbrance is sold at a huge discount. But even in this case, the vast majority of buyers prefer to stay away from it.

As a rule, most often housing is used as collateral for a loan. In this case, not everything is so scary, since you can always agree with the other side on mutually beneficial conditions. However, the money received from the sale will go to fulfill its obligations to a financial institution. If neighbors look after the owner of the real estate, then it will not work to conclude a purchase / sale transaction, since it is prohibited by law.

But where to start selling an apartment yourself if it is seized? This case is the most problematic because it requires the settlement of disputes with the organization that imposed it. Repayment of all debts will usually suffice, but this is not always possible. In some cases, their size can be so high that the sale of housing becomes simply unprofitable.

Alienation of property

If you own residential property together with another person, then for its implementation you will need to go through four stages. Where to start the sale of shares in the apartment? First of all, you need to determine the price and agree on all the terms of the transaction with a person interested in buying. In this case, you do not need to coordinate anything with another property owner. However, you must notify him of your intentions to sell in accordance with applicable law. This is done by sending a written notice indicating the price and terms of sale. From the moment it is received, the solicitors have 30 calendar days to decide on the redemption of your part. If no action is taken on their part, then this is automatically equated to consent.

If everyone is happy with everything, then you can proceed to the execution of notarization. For this, the following documents will be required:

  • written refusal of owners to redeem a share or evidence of the absence of their reaction;
  • EGRP statement;
  • technical passport for the property;
  • housing agreement;
  • original passports of the buyer and seller.

After the settlement of all legal formalities, you can proceed to state registration. To do this, you need to contact the Rosreestr or the Multifunctional Center, write a statement of the established form and pay the state fee in the amount of 2000 rubles.
