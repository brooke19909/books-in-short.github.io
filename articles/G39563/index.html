Knitting needles

What kind of knitting needles to choose if you need a knitted hat for a girl with knitting needles in a fashion magazine ? The diagrams and description, as a rule, contain information about the necessary tools. For experienced masters, deciphering this information will not take much time, for beginners it is a small problem. We will deal with the types of knitting needles. They are straight and circular. Straight knitting needles have stops at the ends. They knit scarves, sweaters, hats, mittens. A feature of finished products obtained with their use is the presence of a seam. Circular knitting needles are interconnected by fishing line or other flexible material. Their advantage is that it is possible to knit them in a circle, and then the output will be a seamless product. In the absence of straight knitting needles, circular ones can be used as straight, but on the contrary it will not work. There are so-called stocking knitting needles, there are 5 of them in a set, and most often they knit seamless hosiery. The numbers of the needles are directly proportional to their thickness and the chosen yarn.

Buying yarn

It is necessary to purchase high-quality material to make a good knitted winter hat for a girl with knitting needles. The description, pattern, consumption and quality of yarn can be found in the specialized literature, but sometimes you have to deviate from the proposed parameters. What should I do if my child is allergic to Angora, and this type of yarn is recommended in the description? Do not be upset. You can choose a safe analogue using your own knowledge and advice of experienced needlewomen.

It is believed that pure wool or fluff is the best choice for knitting children's mittens and gloves. In practice, it turned out that blended yarn (wool, acrylic) is better suited for these products. Synthetic additives have a water repellent property. That is why the mittens from the combined yarn remain dry longer than from natural wool. The main thing is to monitor the percentage of raw materials, which is always indicated on the package.
