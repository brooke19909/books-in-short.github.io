The most popular hotels in Ulan-Ude

Ulan-Ude is the capital of the Republic of Buryatia. It has a well-developed economy, culture, hotel business. The city has a huge number of hotels - from hostels and economy class to elite and expensive establishments. In this article we will look at the most popular hotels in Ulan-Ude.



Hotel "Golden Ear"

Considering cheap hotels in Ulan-Ude, it is worth highlighting the "Golden Ear". This is an economy hotel located in the commercial and historical center of the city, near shopping centers and close to the main street. Nearby are cultural objects and architectural monuments. The hotel has a hairdresser, cafe, Wi-Fi, massage room, free parking.

People staying here say that price and quality are ideally combined in this place - a fairly comfortable room here will cost quite a bit.

Cost: from 900 rubles per day.

Hotel Odon

The Odon hotel complex is located in the city center, near the railway station, a large shopping center, as well as a sports complex. At this place, the guests are offered 75 rooms of various classes, a restaurant, cafe, laundry, beauty salon, parking, sauna. The friendly and attentive staff of this hotel Ulan-Ude will do everything to make guests here comfortable, as well as to want to return to the capital of Buryatia again.

When you read reviews about this institution, it becomes clear that there is a very cozy, warm atmosphere. Many guests are pleased to note the availability of free additional services.

Cost: from 950 rubles per day.

Hotel Baikal

The Baikal Hotel (Ulan-Ude) is the first four-star hotel in Buryatia. It meets the requirements of even the most demanding guests. Cosiness, comfort, high service and luxury of service are the hallmark of the institution. Advantageous location in the very center of the city is an additional advantage of the hotel. Nearby are cinemas, museums, numerous shops, architectural monuments, etc.



Reviews of this hotel suggest that this is a hotel with a high level of service, a home-like atmosphere, good cuisine and at the same time affordable price level.

Cost: from 2200 rubles per day.

Hotel "Buryatia"

This hotel is one of the tallest in the city. Located in a cultural and business center, it is very popular among guests of the capital of Buryatia.

Reasonable prices and comfortable rooms of this hotel in Ulan-Ude are what all guests of this hotel emphasize in their reviews.

Cost: from 1300 rubles per day.

Hotel Boston

Boston is located in a calm and quiet place, away from the hustle and bustle of the main streets. The hotel has ten rooms for every budget and taste. Each of them has everything that is necessary for a comfortable and pleasant stay. The hospitable and polite staff of this hotel Ulan-Ude will make your stay here unforgettable.

Reviews about this place indicate that it is suitable for relaxing with friends, as well as for romantic meetings.

Cost: from 1000 rubles per day.

GESER Hotel (Ulan-Ude), reviews

This hotel is located in the city center, but away from roads and noise. This is the only hotel in the Siberian region, which since 2007 meets the ISO quality standards. A third of the guests are regular customers, trusting the hotel service for more than 10 years.



Guests staying here say that GESER is an impeccable service, excellent service and high quality. Many say that this is an ideal choice for business trips and business trips with a long stay.

Cost: from 3300 rubles per day.

Hotel Camping

The hotel is located near the center of Ulan-Ude, near the monument "Hospitable Buryatia". In addition, there are bus and train stations nearby. The airport is 8 kilometers from the hotel. A small roadside hotel will give travelers home coziness and comfort, while polite and welcoming staff will help with check-in around the clock. Home atmosphere, comfort and coziness, as well as affordability will give a huge amount of positive emotions. There is a guarded parking lot and service stations, in addition - a sauna for 5 people. There is a cafe with a varied and delicious cuisine.

Reviews about this institution suggest that it surprisingly combined comfort and cheapness. Many are surprised to note that at such a low cost there is even a list of additional services that you can not always find in a more expensive hotel.

Cost: from 1000 rubles per day.
