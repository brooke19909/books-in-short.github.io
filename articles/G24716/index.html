About weather

The weather here is very reminiscent of the conditions of the Mediterranean cities of Croatia and northern Italy. The air temperature in the summer is between 27-30 degrees, and sea water warms up to 22-26 degrees.





The beach season begins in early July and lasts until mid-September, but experienced tourists drop in the city and the peninsula in March, at the beginning of almond blossom. The chic climate makes this place one of the most sought after tourist destinations.

Country and manners

Turkey is a country that combines elements of Europe and Asia, and there are many stereotypes around it. It was formed in 1923 as a result of the division of the Ottoman Empire. The country is washed by four seas - the Mediterranean, Aegean, Marmara and Black. The capital is Ankara, but, of course, Istanbul is Turkey 's largest city, its industrial and financial center. And everyone knows the resort of Antalya, surrounded by large hotels. Almost all industries are developed here, the climate is warm, there are fresh fruits and vegetables all year round, nutmegs, cherries, apricots, pomegranates and dates.

The richest regions of the country are the north-west and west, the cities of Istanbul and Ankara, where the main industries are concentrated. Turkey has achieved impressive results in the field of tourism, introducing the world to new formats of recreation, namely: all inclusive club hotels. There are many restaurants, pool bars, noisy parties and fiery dances. Mostly Europeans (Germans, Scandinavians, British) rest here, as well as Ukrainians and Russians.

Turkey attracts Russians with its geographical proximity, quality of service, cheapness, and the absence of a visa requirement. Even the Turks deal with the language barrier quite quickly. The best hotels in the world are in Turkey!

It is also a historical region. There are no less attractions than in Europe: ancient cities, hot springs, ruins of castles and, of course, magnificent nature with mountains and endless beaches.

The healing properties of thermal springs have been known since ancient times - the temperature of the water is 36 degrees, it is saturated with calcium carbonate and carbon dioxide, which helps a lot with heart diseases, and also the water is good for the skin and helps to recover quickly after operations. Many come here for treatment, take baths, and the skin becomes ruddy like a peach and soft like silk. Here you can definitely relax and calm down.
