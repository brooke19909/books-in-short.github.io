Where to stay in the city of Boguchar? Hotel or mini-hotel?

In the south of the Voronezh region there is a small town called Boguchar. The hotel in it provides rooms for those who decide to stay in this place or come on a business trip. In addition to the hotel, there are hotels here, you can rent a house in the private sector for rent. It is recommended to book rooms in advance, which will create maximum comfort upon check-in.



There are different types of hotels in Boguchar. The largest of them were erected in the nineteenth century. At that time it was the inns where travelers staying, passing through Boguchar to other cities of Russia , stayed.

Hotel Types

Hotels in Boguchar are small. Usually they have few rooms, which allows each guest to pay maximum attention.

Almost all rooms have TV and air conditioning, shower. Some hotels have a parking lot, a 24-hour cafe, and Internet. If necessary, an extra bed can be added to the booked room.

The cost of hotel rooms is almost the same and depends on the number of beds and the level of comfort. The price on average varies from 1000 to 3000 r per day per person.
