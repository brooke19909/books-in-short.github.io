Crochet beach dresses: patterns, patterns and description

Every woman, while on vacation, wants to look great on the beach. A beautiful swimsuit is undoubtedly a beach attribute, but is it not in a swimsuit to go to the beach? But a beautiful, crocheted beach dress made of linen or cotton thread will actually attract the attention of vacationers. This article is about how to knit a beach dress and look amazing in it.

What do fashion designers offer?

Outfits for the summer of 2018 are beautiful, colorful beach sundresses, tunics and a pair of light, airy materials. But a special emphasis is made, as in previous summer seasons, on knitted dresses. But this does not mean that dresses of other colors will not be in trend. Summer is full of different colors, including bright colors of dresses, tunics, sundresses.

Crochet Beach Dress

We begin the job description with the preparation of the yarn necessary for knitting. It will need about 600 grams at the rate of 120 m / 50 g. For work, we use a 3 or 3.5 hook and a needle for stitching knitwear.

Before knitting, calculate the density of the fabric. For this, a sample of half columns and a sample of lace are knitted. A sample of 10 * 10 cm should contain a certain number of loops and rows. With these calculations, you will always knit a product that will be perfectly tailored to your size.

For example, the density of knitting lace, you get 20 loops and 12 rows in a square of 10 * 10 cm. So, in 1 cm - 2 loops and 1.2 rows. We start work with a chain for the bottom of the dress. With a bottom size of 60 cm, 120 loops are typed.

In the future, all calculations must be carried out based on the sizes indicated on the pattern. It is necessary to calculate how many rows you need to knit to the armhole. If you consider that the density of knitting of half-columns and lace is different, you need to calculate how many repeating motifs will be from the hem of the skirt to the armhole.

For example, the number of rows of a crocheted openwork pattern of 10 cm is 12 rows, and in half-columns it is 16 rows. It turns out that the motif of the two patterns will be of 28 rows (20 cm). If the length should be 80 cm, then you need to knit 4 motifs in 28 rows.

The dress consists of stripes. Choose the width of the strip that suits your opinion. It can be the same strip width or more openwork and less than half columns, or vice versa.

Knitting dresses

The front (back) is knitted in straight and reverse rows from bottom to top of the length that is needed before the armhole (in photo No. 1, the parameter is 60 or longer). At the height of the armhole on both sides of the knitting, you need to add such a number of chain loops that is equal to the size of the sleeve (in photo No. 1 it is 25 cm). By tying 25 cm, you can finish knitting.

The cutout on the dress will look beautiful if the work is finished with half columns. Having connected 2 details of the dress, they are sewn together with a needle or from the wrong side with columns without a crochet. Sleeves are sewn 25 cm on both sides. The neckline, the edges of the sleeves and the hem are tied with a "dog step", that is, columns without a crochet from left to right.

Crochet openwork pattern begins on an already connected fabric consisting of half columns. Let us arbitrarily denote the motives:

  • A - 3 tbsp. with nak., 2p. air., 3 tbsp. with nak .;
  • B - 1 tbsp. With nak., 1p. air., 1 tbsp. with nak.

1st row: * in the loop of a row of columns we knit the motif A; skip 4 p. on the chain; in 5 p. knit motive B; skip 4p * (repeat knitting from * to *).

2nd row: knit 2 p. for lifting; * in the arch of 2 p. knit motive A; in the arch of 1 p. knit motive B * (repetition of knitting from * to *).

The spruce decided to perform 4 rows of lace, you should connect 1 and 2 rows and repeat the pattern of 1 and 2 rows again.
