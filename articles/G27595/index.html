Manufacturing technique

A papier-m√¢ch√© vase is made according to the following algorithm:

  1. The mold for the product is wrapped on the outside with cling film. Such a protective layer will make it possible to easily remove coarse paper without damage.
  2. Glue is bred with water. It is worth adhering to the following proportion: dilute one part of the glue with three parts of water.
  3. Tearing newspapers. In this case, you should have both small pieces and long rectangular ones. Small pieces are the basis of the form, and large ones will strengthen the frame.
  4. Long pieces of newspapers are dipped in diluted glue and stacked tightly in a circular shape.
  5. When the first layer dries a little, you need to lay the next. There should be at least eight such layers.
  6. After complete drying, the outside should be glued with pieces of white paper. Carefully pull the product out of the mold and glue it with white paper inside (all this dries up for about a day).
  7. The basis is to cover with gouache. The color is best chosen in accordance with further decoration.
  8. After 2 hours, the gouache will dry and then it will be possible to treat the surface with varnish.

Other decorative elements can be attached with a glue gun already on the dried varnish.
