Heart rate monitor with chest sensor: overview, description, reviews

If you carefully monitor your health, want to lose weight or just go in for sports, then the segment of heart rate monitors (trackers) is probably familiar and interesting to you. The first devices of this kind appeared in the distant 80s. They were quite massive and performed only one function - they measured heart rate.

Today's gadgets have stepped far ahead in technical terms and can offer a lot of interesting things that greatly increase the effectiveness of training. The modern market for sports devices offers a wide range of models that differ from each other not only in price, but also in functionality, size and other characteristics.

What are heart rate monitors?

Almost all sports trackers can be divided into two types - wrist and chest. Both varieties have both their pros and cons. Let's start with the most important parameter - measurement accuracy.

Independent experts in this field unanimously declare that the models mounted on the torso are much more accurate than all others. Judging by the reviews of wrist heart rate monitors without a chest sensor, they lose to their counterparts by an average of 10-15%.

But torso models are somewhat limited in applications. They are not suitable for use in fitness rooms, and especially for weightlifting. Yes, and swimmers experience discomfort from the pressure belt, when it is necessary to work hard with the chest on inhalation and exhalation.

But if we are talking about cycling, running and other similar disciplines, then even the best heart rate monitor on hand without a chest sensor will give only approximate data. The fact is that a belt attached to a torso is less susceptible to vibrations than arms or legs. Hence, more accurate indicators.

In our article, we will just consider models with fastening on the torso. So, we bring to your attention an overview of heart rate monitors with a chest sensor. Our list includes the most popular devices, distinguished by their effectiveness and a considerable amount of positive feedback from consumers.
