Hotel Grand Sunlife Hotel 4 *, Turkey, Alanya: overview, description and reviews

Grand Sunlife Hotel 4 * is one of the most popular hotels among travelers in Turkey. It attracts a lot of guests from abroad with its excellent conditions, not only those that are on the territory, but also those that are outside it. Often, tour operators offer it as a vacation spot for anyone who wants to relax with their families in an exotic atmosphere.

general information

Grand Sunlife Hotel 4 * (Turkey) was built in 1998. At that time, it was not so developed, but over the years, everything has changed radically, because as a result of numerous renovations, its body has become more modern in appearance, and the landscape design has become more pleasing to the eye. A lot of attention during their holding was also paid to the technical equipment of the rooms. One of the last such reconstructions was carried out in 2014.

At the moment, the hotel occupies a fairly large area - more than 20,000 square meters. m. It harmoniously combines a place for apartments in three seven-story and one six-story buildings, as well as several options for places for recreation and entertainment.



Location

The hotel is located near the coastline of the Mediterranean Sea, in the tourist region of Alanya. This place is known for its picturesqueness, here the shores are rather gentle and, accordingly, the sea is warm for a long period, which is very favorable for recreation. The center of Alanya is 30 kilometers from the location of the Grand Sunlife Hotel 4 *, and the nearest airport is 90 kilometers from another large resort city - Antalya. It is the great distance between the airport and the resting place that is the main reason for inclusion in the standard set of any travel transfer vouchers.



Rooms

The news having the number of rooms is located in four beautiful and modern buildings, which are arranged with the latest technology. Inside each of them, everything is arranged taking into account the taste, the interior is bright and quite attractive for tourists everywhere, as they themselves note in their reviews. The total number of apartments presented here is 469, due to which, by all international standards, Grand Sunlife Hotel 4 * (Alanya) belongs to the category of large vacation spots. All rooms presented here are divided into two categories: standard and family.

Each apartment is equipped with a bathroom, and there is also air conditioning to adjust the air temperature to the optimum level.

Sports and spa

Guests often notice that within the Grand Sunlife Hotel 4 * (low cost), you can successfully monitor your health and maintain a normal body shape. The hotel offers a wide range of entertainment for the different categories of tourists who live in it. Beach

The mass attention of tourists is paid to a large section of the beach, which is under the jurisdiction of the Grand Sunlife Hotel 4 *. Its length is more than two hundred meters, which is enough to accommodate a couple of hundreds of sun loungers and umbrellas in this space. If desired, guests can borrow them completely free of charge, as well as several locker rooms, showers and public toilets are presented here - these objects can also be used free of charge at any time.

On the beach, the hotel guests are offered a lot of water activities, which are paid. Subject to payment by a professional instructor and equipment rental, guests can engage in water sports, most of which are extreme.

The main feature of the beach strip is that it does not work in the winter, due to the rather low temperature of the water in the sea.
