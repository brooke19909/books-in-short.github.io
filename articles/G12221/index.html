The Jumeirah Beach, United Arab Emirates: beach, holidays, reviews

The United Arab Emirates is an extremely popular tourist destination. After all, here travelers are expected not only to rest on the seashore, but also modern entertainment complexes, shopping, business centers, interesting excursions and lots of other ways to have fun and usefully. One of the most popular and luxurious vacation spots is the Jumeirah Beach Hotel. Naturally, people planning a trip to an exotic country are interested in additional information about this place.

Hotel Location Description





The Jumeirah Beach Hotel stands out for its advantageous location, as it was built almost on the shore of the Persian Gulf. The airport is about 20 km away. You should not worry about this, as tourists are provided with a shuttle service - in just 20-30 minutes you can get to the hotel building. This hotel perfectly combines the comfort and coziness of the suburbs, leaving the opportunity to plunge into the active life of a big city. By the way, more and more tourists choose the Emirates. Rest here can be truly unforgettable. A luxurious hotel will create for you an atmosphere of a true oriental fairy tale.

What does the hotel look like?

The Jumeirah Beach Hotel was built in 1997. It consists of a giant building with twenty-six floors, which in shape resembles a wave. Naturally, the hotel has a large territory, which is reserved for gardens, terraces for relaxation, swimming pools and even its own water park.

Jumeirah Beach Hotel Complex (Dubai): how many rooms are there on the territory?

The hotel can be considered quite large, as it consists of 600 rooms of different categories and spaciousness:

  • 448 Standard Deluxe, which are designed for 2 people (139 of them have a private balcony);
  • 103 Club Executive - spacious rooms on special floors that are designed specifically for business people. Breakfast is served in the spacious lobby, during the day you can get drinks, there is a place for work.
  • 48 Suites - one, two and three room rooms with bedrooms, living areas, terraces.
  • 1 Presidental Suite with private jacuzzi and bar.

Brief description of rooms





You can be sure that the reserved room will delight you with space, modern furnishings and wonderful views. Some rooms have access to a private balcony or terrace leading to the courtyard.

Guests are provided with their own home appliances, including a plasma TV, on which you can watch your favorite satellite channels, as well as a convenient and powerful air conditioning system, telephone, minibar, safe. Some rooms even have their own fax machine. Of course, you can use the Internet access.

In the spacious bathroom you will find a comfortable shower or a bathtub where you can relax in the aromatic water. Guests receive a hair dryer, clean towels, shampoos, soap and other high-quality cosmetics. Comfort is what Emirates are famous for. Rest here will be unforgettable. By the way, cleaning is carried out regularly and, indeed, of high quality.

What kind of food is offered to guests?





Jumeirah Beach is a hotel that offers a variety of dining options. For example, guests can choose only breakfast, and have lunch and dinner in the city, or choose any hotel establishment. There is a fairly popular all-inclusive scheme, paying for which, the tourist gets the right to good food at the hotel, drinks and snacks throughout the day. It is worth paying attention to the fact that practically no alcohol is served at the hotel.

Hotel "Jumeirah Beach": beach and water recreation for tourists





It's no secret that most travelers visit the country, wanting to have a good time on the bay. What can the Jumeirah Beach Hotel offer in this regard? United Arab Emirates are famous for their clean beaches. And the hotel owns its own stretch of coast, which is located next to the hotel. Its length is 900 meters. Here guests can use umbrellas, sunbeds, hammocks for free, and get clean towels. Naturally, fans of more active leisure will also find something to do. Tourists have the opportunity to engage in windsurfing and sailing, go boating and boating, water skiing, try scuba diving or even, having bought a sightseeing tour, go on a yacht on a real trip.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?

It's no secret that people today increasingly prefer to travel with their children. And for such tourists, the main ones are questions about whether the hotel has the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay for a child. To begin with, it is worth saying that an extra bed will be delivered to the booked room. In any chosen restaurant and bar you can count on a chair for feeding. Some cafes have a special children's menu. Here you can get quality baby food for the youngest guests.

The Jumeirah Beach Hotel is a great place for children to spend time. For small tourists there is a spacious playground with swings and other attractions. A club is constantly open, where experienced animators, educators and teachers are engaged with the guys. And, of course, do not forget about the beach and city attractions that the child will definitely like.

Additional service: what services can a guest expect?





Naturally, in such a high-class hotel, guests can count on additional services necessary for comfort. For example, you can dry clean and laundry, and upon request you will be provided with ironing equipment.

A medical office is constantly working, and the necessary medicines can be purchased at a hotel pharmacy. There are also small shops. Here you can rent a car and use parking for free. And from the hotel constantly runs a bus that delivers tourists to the city center and back.

Internet access is available, as well as a well-equipped business center. Business people can use the spacious conference rooms for meetings, seminars, presentations, etc. There are banquet rooms where you can celebrate an important event. The hotel staff is happy to organize various events, including even weddings.

Tourist Attractions

Today, many travelers go to the Emirates, a vacation here can be magnificent. The hotel has all the conditions for a rich leisure. In the courtyard there are three large pools, the water in which is heated if necessary. You can use towels, umbrellas and sunbeds. Children and their parents get a lot of fun while visiting a large water park with a variety of water slides.





For tourists, a two-story sports club is equipped with a gym with modern equipment, a room for aerobics, saunas, a jacuzzi and massage rooms. Here you can study independently or with a trainer, as well as sign up for group lessons. The local health club will offer you dozens of wellness, relaxing and anti-aging treatments. And women, for sure, will enjoy the beauty salon, where you can use the services of qualified stylists. There are several squash courts, as well as sports fields.

Do not forget that in the city you will find no less entertainment, including a variety of excursions and, of course, shopping in giant shopping centers. The hotel staff will be happy to help you plan an itinerary for independent travel, advise a good travel company, and talk about the most popular shops in the city.
