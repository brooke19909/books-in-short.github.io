Full review of the smartphone Explay Diamond

Media Tek launched a low-cost, but very powerful eight-core smartphone. Each manufacturer tries to make his device something different from the models sent to the market by other companies. Explay Diamond is a very competitive tablet with a six-inch screen and the presence of an eight-core processor. Strengths

In appearance, the Explay Diamond smartphone looks quite stylish, presentable and original. The case is completely made of high quality metal. The device itself is very thin, its thickness is only 7 mm. The case is made in accordance with the classical forms of all modern smartphones, the screen frames are very narrow, the corners are smooth, and the edges of the device are slightly sloping.

With the impressive dimensions of the product (161x80x7 mm), the phone is very convenient to hold in your hands, this is facilitated by the small width and thickness of the device. Another strength of Explay Diamond is its price. It looks very expensive, despite its low cost. The tablet weighs 185 grams. It supports the work of two SIM cards at once, which can work simultaneously. Such characteristics are quite a good indicator for this kind of mobile devices.





Explay Diamond: phone review

Consider the device in detail. The Explay Diamond front panel is covered with a protective glass that protects the phone from cracks and other breakdowns during collisions or bumps. The main function buttons are outside the screen. The front camera window is located above the display, which has already housed the speaker grilles and proximity sensors, as well as lighting when taking photos or videos. The tablet lock button is located on the top of the case. There is also a special connector for connecting audio devices. To place the lock button on the top panel of the device was not the most successful solution, because with such a large size, reaching it is very inconvenient.

On the bottom of the case there is a data interface connector and a microphone hole. The speech speaker has a slightly higher volume than a regular smartphone. When talking, audibility is very clear. At the maximum allowable volume, the speaker does not emit any extraneous sounds. Due to its coating, the display is able to withstand long-term transportation and some extreme situations. There is black plastic under the protective glass. In appearance, the display material merges almost completely with the front camera and looks quite attractive.





If you need to remove the back cover of your smartphone, first move the plastic insert. Pull it to the side for a special recess. Then pull the back cover of the phone out of the grooves and lift it up. Fingerprints on the screen, if they remain, they are not very noticeable, moreover, they are very easily erased from the display. The manufacturers of the Explay Diamond tabletphone have made sure that with a six-inch screen the mobile device does not look bulky. This they achieved by minimizing the gaps between the case and the screen.

Explay Diamond: Quality Reviews

Users note that the assembly is very high-quality, with a physical impact on the device, nothing bends, does not crackle or creak. Explay Diamond Display Image

The review will continue with a discussion of the quality of the graphics. The image reproduced by the tablet will appreciate the clarity and brightness of the colors. If you slightly tilt the display to the side, then a violet hue appears on the right, and on the left side the image gives off yellow. The touch screen is highly sensitive, supporting up to five finger touches at the same time. The device has a function that enhances the smoothness of the video and captured images.

Battery

The Explay Diamond mobile device uses a removable 2300 mAh lithium-polymer battery. The operating time is approximately the same as that of other products of this plan. The tablet phone can withstand 10 hours of weak activity of the device without recharging, the hour of using the camera. At maximum brightness of downloaded games, you can count on 1.5-2 hours of work at full strength. Of course, manufacturers should put a more powerful battery in the communicator, but then they would have to increase the thickness of the smartphone by several millimeters.
