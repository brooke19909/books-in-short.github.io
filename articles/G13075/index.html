What are the conditions for MTS roaming in Abkhazia?

When traveling outside the Russian Federation for a short period, for example, on vacation or on a business trip, most often people prefer to bring along SIM cards that they use in their region. Of course, in order to save on communication services, it is recommended to purchase local SIM cards in the country of arrival. However, this is not very convenient and often when traveling, subscribers rarely make calls, send SMS, and even more so use the mobile Internet. Nevertheless, it is recommended to clarify in advance what tariffing will be in a particular country before the trip and deposit the amount taking into account roaming expenses. Under what conditions is MTS roaming in Abkhazia provided, is there an opportunity to optimize the cost of communication services?





Basic roaming prices for voice services

Each country has its own communication rates. For a number of countries, the conditions may be identical, for example, for European countries. As a rule, prices for voice calls for international roaming are much higher than those implied by roaming in Russia. Consider what tariffing is in Abkhazia.

You should start with voice communication: a single cost is established - 155 rubles per minute of connection. It applies to calls to any destination: local calls (to the numbers of operators in Abkhazia or landlines), calls to Russia (to cell numbers and home phones). At the same time, MTS roaming in Abkhazia also implies a fee for receiving calls - just like roaming in any other country. The cost of a minute of an incoming call is also estimated at 155 rubles.





The cost of MTS in Abkhazia: SMS messages

You can send a text message for only nineteen rubles - this price is relevant when transferring to any mobile number. Incoming messages are not paid. There are also several options that reduce the cost of SMS. They will be discussed later.

Internet using

Using mobile Internet abroad is not recommended - as its cost is quite high. At the moment, many telecom operators, including MTS, offer an Internet package for a fee.   • Package 50 SMS - allows you to reduce the price of an outgoing message to 10 rubles in the framework of fifty messages (connected for 500 rubles, also valid for thirty days).
