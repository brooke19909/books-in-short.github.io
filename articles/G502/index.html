A-One Star Hotel, Pattaya: description of hotel rooms, reviews

Pattaya is a popular resort for vacationers around the world, offering its guests not only comfortable beaches and the warm sea, but also a lot of all kinds of entertainment. The tourist infrastructure in this city is developed excellently, and there are many excellent hotels here. One of the most frequently visited local tourists by Russian tourists is the A-One Star Hotel.

Where is the complex

This hotel is located very close to the center of Pattaya (in the northern part) at the very edge of the sea. Within walking distance from the complex there are many shopping centers, cafes and restaurants. So, just about a 5-minute drive from the hotel, the Central Festival Pattaya Beach shopping center is very popular with tourists.

Located near the complex and the airport "U-Tapao - Pattaya." A-One Star Hotel is about 25 km away from it . Also , some vacationers who have rented rooms in this complex arrive in Thailand at Suvarnabhumi Airport. Ra is located about an hour away from the hotel (by shuttle bus).





To the capital of Thailand, the city of Bangkok, hotel guests can reach literally a couple of hours by public transport. The residents of the complex need to go to the beach no more than 2-5 minutes.

What is a hotel

Reviews on the complex A-One Star Hotel (Pattaya) on the web are different. Some guests find this hotel quite comfortable. Others do not recommend staying in it for various reasons. In any case, all residents of the complex agree that the service provided in it fully corresponds to the low cost of rooms.

This apartment hotel accepts tourists in a multi-storey building of modern construction. The hotel building looks very solid and presentable. There are good reviews on the Web about its internal design.

The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. When checking in, tourists must leave a deposit of $ 50 at the reception. At the end of the paid period of residence, this money is fully refunded to guests.

If necessary, the residents of the hotel are provided, including the service of accelerated check-in and check-out. But you need to pay for it, of course, separately. On many sites of tour operators, this complex is classified as an apart-hotel. That is, basically , rooms equipped as apartments are not rented out, and not just hotel rooms. This hotel belongs to the class of three stars. That is, the amenities here are quite a lot.





Short description of a standard hotel room

In total, this budget hotel offers 142 rooms for tourists who choose Pattaya for holidays. All rooms for rent at the hotel are equipped with:

  • air conditioners;

  • safes;

  • mini-bars.

Of course, in every room rented in this three-star hotel, guests can use individual showers. The keys to the safes in the apartments of this hotel are given to residents for free. In order for a hotel employee to open a minibar, tourists need to pay a certain amount. Each room has, among other things, a small refrigerator. Unfortunately, there are no balconies in the rooms of this complex. The views from the hotel windows open mainly to the city.

Guest opinion on hotel rooms

There are various reviews on the A-One Star Hotel (Pattaya) rental property on the Web. The main drawback of the rooms of this complex is the guests consider their very small size. The area around the rooms in the hotel is really small. But many residents still do not consider this a special problem. In Thailand, for obvious reasons, tourists rent rooms in a hotel mainly just to spend the night in them.





Also, vacationers consider the absence of a closet to be a drawback of the rooms at A-One Star Hotel (Pattya) . Tourists who have rented rooms at this hotel often have to keep their belongings in a suitcase. All rooms have several hooks on the walls. However, too much clothes can not be hung on them, of course.

The disadvantages of the hotel, according to many vacationers, include the poor soundproofing of the rooms. Some hotel guests talk on specialized forums on the Web that they perfectly heard even the conversations of their neighbors from adjacent rooms. Many vacationers advise newcomers, going to this hotel, to take ear plugs with them from home.

Most of the guests consider the cleanliness of the rooms to be the advantages of this hotel. Room cleaning is very common here. At the same time, according to many residents, maids relate to their duties well. The interiors in the rooms rented in this complex, according to most tourists, are quite beautiful and stylish. Bed linen and towels are changed daily.

According to many vacationers, it is more or less convenient to take water treatments in the hotel showers. In addition to the traditional set of toiletries, a hairdryer is provided in the bathrooms of the hotel. Guests can also use slippers in the rooms. The only drawback of the bathtubs in this hotel is the tenants consider a not too good pressure of water in the pipes. However, a similar problem exists in almost all hotels in Thailand.

Hotel infrastructure

For the convenience of tourists staying at A-One Star Hotel (Pattaya) , among other things, there are:

  • parking;

  • laundry;

  • SPA-center.

The hotel also has a currency exchange service. Tourists can use Wi-Fi in the hotel for free. Of course, this three-star hotel also has an outdoor pool. Also, tourists can, if desired, visit the massage room. For various business events, the hotel provides a conference room. The hotel also has a business center with fax and photocopying services.





Infrastructure Reviews: Benefits

Accommodation at the A-One Star Hotel 3 * hotel most tourists find quite convenient. In particular, guests praise the location of this hotel. It was built on a very quiet street. Moreover, the entire infrastructure of the city is within walking distance from it. And the residents of the complex do not need to go too far to the sea.

Also a plus of this hotel is the attitude of the staff towards the guests. Hotel staff behave very politely and always try to quickly solve all problems that arise from customers. Unfortunately, hotel workers do not understand the Russian language. However, many of them speak pretty good English. Therefore, you can communicate with the employees of the complex if necessary using, for example, the same translation program on your smartphone.

Another advantage of this hotel, many tourists believe that it is literally crammed with all kinds of electronics. Even elevators in the hotel are called exclusively by the guest‚Äôs card. All this, of course, significantly increases the safety of residents.





Smoking in the rooms of this hotel, as already mentioned, is not allowed. However, there are special places in the courtyard for cigarette lovers.

Cons of the hotel

The main disadvantage of the hotel, most guests believe that there are always a lot of Indians and Chinese. Such tourists, unfortunately, almost always behave very noisy. Also a minus of the hotel is the fact that the parking lot is located right next to the building - under the windows of some rooms.





The pool at this hotel is not too comfortable. It is located on the north side of the building. The sun illuminates it only in the morning. Therefore, the water in the pool often just does not have time to warm up to a comfortable bathing temperature. Guests can use sun loungers and umbrellas nearby on the hotel terrace for free. Installed them by the pool a lot. However, sometimes, unfortunately, it still happens that there are not enough sunbeds for all the residents.

Hotel Food Reviews

Rooms at the A-One Star Hotel (Pattaya) can be rented with or without breakfast. The hotel also offers full board. Most of the former guests speak about eating in the dining room of the complex not bad. Food is served in the hotel buffet in the convenient buffet system. If desired, tenants can take first, second courses, salads, various drinks, pastries, fruits in the dining room .

The menu in the hotel restaurant is thus very good. However, experienced tourists advise newcomers to come here early. Otherwise, all the most delicious and fresh can simply make out the other residents of the hotel.

A-One Star Hotel Beach Reviews

The coastline is in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. Unfortunately, this hotel does not have its own beach. Therefore, its guests are mainly urban public. Fortunately, there are many good beaches in Pattaya. Most often, hotel guests rest on Wongamat or Don Tang. In any case, all the beaches of Pattaya are very similar to each other, and the fundamental difference in where exactly to sunbathe, according to most tourists, simply does not exist. The infrastructure on the coastline in this resort is well developed everywhere. In any case, it will not be difficult for tourists to find a cafe with a good menu and low prices for dishes.

The city of Pattaya is located in the bay. Therefore, the sea is especially transparent, unfortunately, never happens here. But at least the water on the local beaches is always warm. At the same time, there are almost never too large waves here.





Instead of a conclusion

Of course, a very well-developed tourist infrastructure offers guests of Thailand in the city of Pattaya Northern part. The hotels here, even budget ones, are mostly comfortable. Tourists can rent rooms, for example, in hotels such as Kly Unique Pfttya 3 *, SN Plus Hotel 4 *, Nantra Pattaya Baan Ampoe Beach 3 *.

Actually, the A-One Star Hotel 3 * complex itself , if you focus on the reviews of vacationers, is also very good for relaxation. But renting rooms at A-One Star Hotel is only for people with not too sensitive sleep. Also, this hotel is not particularly good, according to many travelers, is suitable for families with children. No entertainment is provided for young guests at the hotel. In addition, Pattaya itself is oriented more towards vacationers who prefer an active holiday to a relaxing beach holiday. This city is best suited for young people or for those who are looking for entertainment in the resort.
