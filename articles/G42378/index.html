Kos island, Greece: tourist reviews

Holidays in Greece are an opportunity for complete, physical and mental relaxation. Here you will find beautiful nature. The magnificent climate ensures that the influx of tourists does not end almost all year round. Of course, each visitor has his own goal. Someone has to sunbathe on the beach, others want to conquer the waves, surf. You can end the list with agro and ecotourism, that is, a visit to national parks, reserves. Here you can add alternative types of recreation: mountain and winter. That is, the country can accept tourists all year round. Today we will talk about the island of Kos (Greece). Reviews call it one of the most comfortable places in the Aegean. Let's look at what it attracts tourists.



general characteristics

The island of Kos is not in vain called the "Garden of Eden." If you want to spend your vacation amid a riot of greens and colors, then here you are. The unique nature and ecology is what all reviews emphasize. Kos island (Greece) attracts tourists and a huge number of attractions. A nightlife will allow you to have fun the youngest and most active. In general, Greece is a world of harmony and silence, for this people of all ages come here.

Resort Regions

There are seven of them, although in fact much more. It means that now the conversation is about particular regions, and not beautiful and attractive places. Now we will consider each of them in detail, so that tourists have a complete picture. Sandy beaches, an island of fantastic beauty, greenery, a huge amount of ancient architecture, all this is an indisputable advantage when choosing a place to stay on the island of Kos (Greece). Reviews emphasize the highest quality of service at local resorts.

It has a mild climate, most of the days are sunny. Quiet bays with silvery water in the sun, small chapels saturated with aromas of citrus trees will surely conquer you. You can talk for a long time about how the island of Kos (Greece) charms its tourists. Reviews say that each of the seven regions has its own zest, special and unique. However, you can not torment yourself with doubts for a long time. Choosing any hotel you can walk around the island and see all its attractions.



Kardamena

The resort town is cozy and very beautiful. Have you ever played chess on black, volcanic sand? If not, then welcome to the island of Kos (Greece, Kardamena). Reviews from tourists suggest that here you will find a rich, cultural life. Various championships and competitions are held here. Well-groomed, sandy beach, gently shelving entrance to the clear sea, plenty of entertainment, all this attracts people.

Travelers with great pleasure study the monuments of ancient architecture. Lovers of ancient Greek architecture will definitely find a lot of old, restored buildings, if you choose the resort of Kardamena (Kos island, Greece). Reviews emphasize that this is one of the most successful choices for youth recreation.

Kefalos

And if you are looking for a calm, quiet holiday with your family, is the island of Kos (Greece) suitable for you? Reviews from tourists say that being alone with nature, away from noisy parties, you will best succeed in Kefalos. Not for nothing that a huge number of tourists come here every year. Developed infrastructure, fantastic beaches, beautiful landscapes and lots of attractions.

The historic center of Kefalos is a great choice for leisurely walks. This is a typical Greek settlement with narrow streets and old stone houses, castle ruins and old, windmills. In the coastal part of a huge number of restaurants, taverns and bars, the perfect place to sit well in a pleasant company. Among the attractions of Kefalos, I would like to mention the folklore museum, as well as the tiny island of Kastri with a charming church.



Peace and quiet in Lumpy

Another charming resort town, which is conveniently located on the outskirts of the capital. This is one of the most beautiful corners of the island of Kos (Greece). Reviews of tourists say that peace is just in the air. In few places you can find such an amazing world of peace and quiet, peace, which is simply spilled around. Harmony, comfort and tranquility, which we are so lacking in everyday life, are natural here.

Lumpy is the best place in the world for windsurfers. Water sports are held in high esteem, so the choice will be fantastic. Those who wish to travel can visit other groups of islands and spend time interestingly and profitably. Beach holidays here are really the best on the island. Tourists note that the crystal clear, blue water and pleasant, sunny beaches they remembered for many more years.

Marmari

The resort is 15 km from the capital of the island of Kos. It is quiet, calm, but there are no attractions and infrastructure. Marmari Beach is best for surfing enthusiasts. Actually for this reason, a large number of tourists visit Greece. The island of Kos (photos, reviews of tourists is confirmed by a large number of surfers) is great for athletes. Rental offices give you the opportunity to take any equipment that interests you. From beach balls to boats and yachts. In addition, there are various schools of water sports, which will allow during the holidays quite tolerably to learn new art.

We plan a route

It is very convenient that you can make excursions here in all directions. A travel agency works for you every day to make it rich and interesting. Think in advance what to see on the island of Kos (Greece). Reviews with great warmth describe the vacation spent on these sunny beaches. The bus station is located in the very center of the island, which allows easy access to any hotel.

Travel around the island by city and suburban buses. You can rent any type of ground transport. Judging by the reviews, this is the best way to travel around the island, which many enjoy using. Biking on the island is the main means of transportation. By the way, be very careful, they can be fined for walking on cycle paths. Experienced tourists say that you can relax here no worse than on the more expensive and fashionable Rhodes. Everything is very close here, you do not need to take a car to get to places of interest to tourists.



sights

Upon arrival, you will find out that it was here that the father of medicine Hippocrates was born. Every year, the best doctors from around the world gather at the center named after him. Here, on the island, an ancient plane tree grows. Some experts claim that he is already 2,000 years old, and under him an ancient doctor taught followers. What other attractions has Kos prepared for you:

  • Asklepion - this is the first attraction that is found by tourists on the island. These are the ruins of a former temple and hospital. They still look very magnificent. It is located in a beautiful place of the island, from where a wonderful view of the sea opens.
  • The castle of the Knights of St. John is a real fortress, which was built to protect against the Turks. The island has many ancient buildings, and the castle is no exception. Here are the tombs and altars, columns and a stone bridge. The interior of the castle suffered, but you can still understand how the knights lived in those days.
  • Altar of Dionysus . It is located near the Agora. It was not preserved in the best way. To be more precise, it was the temple of the god of winemaking. It was built in the form of the letter "P". It was destroyed by an earthquake, and since no one was restoring it, it gradually became desolate. There are few surviving fragments of walls and other structures, but they convey the beauty of the temple and the altar.
  • The fortress of Antimachia is one of the best examples of medieval architecture. It is located in the center of the island. It was laid in the early Middle Ages.
  • Colorful mosques .
  • Peacock forest . This is one of the most colorful attractions on the island. In a stunning coniferous grove inhabited by hundreds of peacocks. They live here without any restrictions and fences. Birds walk along the fringes, spreading their luxurious tails. They are used to the constant influx of tourists, so they calmly take food from their hands.

Experienced tourists recommend not taking excursions from the hotel. It comes out pretty expensive. Moreover, Kos is a very small island. Take a bike and calmly go round beautiful places almost for free. In addition, breathe in fresh air and recharge your batteries for the whole year ahead.
