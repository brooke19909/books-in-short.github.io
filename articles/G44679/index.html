How to borrow money on Beeline and continue communication



Money on a cell phone account always ends unexpectedly. Often zeroing the balance interrupts very important conversations. In addition, circumstances may develop so that it will not work to replenish the account right now. Suddenly you will find yourself in the country or go camping. Definitely worth taking care of the balance of the phone in advance. But if suddenly the money ran out at the most inopportune moment, then you can always borrow money from a telecom operator. Moreover, it is very easy. It is enough to type one team and wait for the SMS with the result. About how to borrow money on Beeline, this article will tell.

Service "Trust payment"

This is the name of the service from the Beeline operator. It is provided in the home region and roaming. With it, you can replenish the balance of your mobile account at any time of the day, because the system makes transfers regardless of the working day of the mobile operator's company. How to borrow money on Beeline: rules

  2. The replenishment amount is calculated taking into account the total amount of expenses for calls for the next past month. The minimum loan amount is 30 rubles, and the maximum is 300 rubles. The exact figure of a possible loan can be obtained from the telecom operator or in fact from SMS about the provision of the service. Or dial * 141 * 7 # call.
  3. The amount of your balance should be from zero to 90 rubles. If you "go negative", the service will not connect.
  4. The amount will be provided for a period of two days. During this time, the balance must be replenished.
  5. You can reuse this service only after a day from the moment the debt was written off and only with a positive balance.
  6. The service is not free. It will cost you 10 rubles for each appeal.

How to borrow money on Beeline if you are in roaming? In the same way, but the calculation of the amount provided will be doubled, and the period of use will be seven days.
