Dalaman Lykia Resort 4 *. Detailed description of the hotel: rooms, food, services, entertainment

If you want a bright and unforgettable, but at the same time not very expensive vacation, come to Dalaman. This colorful, but still little mastered by our tourists Turkish town knows how to surprise, delight, conquer. The chic Dalaman Lykia Resort 4 * hotel, hospitably opening the doors in winter and summer, will no doubt complement your vacation with pleasant memories, help you wait for a flight, and give you a wonderful vacation after a difficult flight.



Location

The city of Dalaman became widely known to tourists only after the opening of an international airport here. Near this epoch-making structure several modern hotels immediately grew up, providing a decent rest to all those who came to the city and left it. Hotel Dalaman Lykia Resort 4 * compares favorably with others in its multifunctional infrastructure, quality of service and provided services. It is located only 8-10 minutes from the airport (approximately 3.5 km). It is also not far from Dalaman, about 2.5 km. You can cover the distance on foot, by minibus or taxi. The world famous Fethiye resort is 40 km away, while Marmaris is 95 km away. Nearby there is a highway, along which buses run to both of these resorts.



Description

Hotel Dalaman Lykia Resort 4 * is a modern building made of glass and concrete, with spacious foyers, comfortably furnished and equipped with panoramic verandas. A beautiful elevator lifts the floors of tourists. Reception is open around the clock. Parking at the hotel is free. For a great stay, Dalaman Lykia Resort 4 * has a spacious outdoor pool for adults, a small tank for children and a heated pool in the building, sauna, hammam, spa, beauty salon, fitness room, night club, currency exchange, laundry. For leisure activities, the hotel offers tennis and billiard tables, a dart board, table football. For business meetings, 2 conference rooms are equipped with all necessary equipment. There is also a stylish restaurant and two bars. The territory of the hotel in question is not very large. Rooms

The hotel has 174 spacious rooms, 2 of which are equipped for disabled guests. Their area is from 20 to 35 square meters. The rooms are furnished with new furniture, on the floor - soft flooring (carpet). The view from the windows, however, is not very picturesque: either on the road, or on the houses of local residents against the backdrop of the mountains. Each room has a fairly spacious balcony with light furniture. From technology there is a TV with channels in Russian, air conditioning, telephone (paid), hairdryer, safe with combination lock, small refrigerator, Internet. The toilet rooms in each room are spacious, tiled, equipped with a bathtub, washbasin and toilet. The Dalaman Lykia Resort 4 * hotel (guest reviews always indicate this) provides a rich set of toiletries, including soap, shampoo, bath foam, makeup sponges, ear sticks, toilet paper, a bathing cap, slippers.



Nutrition

Two types of food can offer guests the hotel Dalaman Lykia Resort 4 *. Turkey is famous for its pickles, delicious pastries and wonderful national dishes. This hotel also has a very varied menu. According to the "full board" system, guests are provided with breakfast (buffet), lunch and dinner. Important! Free drinks are only served for breakfast. The menu includes dishes from vegetables, rice, pasta, meat (chicken), soups, sausages, cheeses, sweets, fruits. If you wish, you can order lunch in a restaurant or ask for room service. Unfortunately, there are no cafes or restaurants near the hotel. If you want to spend dinner somewhere else, you have to go to the city.

For a short stay, for example, during a business trip or during a transit flight, the Dalaman Lykia Resort 4 * hotel is most suitable. Fethiye, located just half an hour away, will bring a pleasant variety to the rest. But Dalaman himself is able to give a lot of unforgettable impressions. Its main attraction is the fast river of the same name, on which rafting is organized. There are two routes, simple and complex, and at the end of the travelers there are magnificent beaches. And if you book a boat trip, you can stay in one of the dozens of deserted bays of fabulous beauty. But that is not all. There is a turtle sanctuary in Dalaman. The beach is located on its territory.
