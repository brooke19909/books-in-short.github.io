Aquarium on Chistye Prudy: address, reviews, official site

There are many interesting places in Moscow. However, the Aquarium on Chistye Prudy (see photo below) is one of the most amazing.





In this paradise, summer stands year round. Having visited the oceanarium located in the heart of the noisy capital, not far from the metro station Chistye Prudy, you find yourself not just in the tropics, but in the fantastic element of the queen of water. The guests of this establishment are immersed in a bright and unusual world that has nothing to do with city landscapes.

Location

The Oceanarium on Chistye Prudy (address - Chistoprudny Boulevard, Building 14, Building 3) is located inside the boulevard ring. Here stands an old mansion, decorated with stucco made of fantastic creatures. The windows of the house look at the Moscow pond, which is called in the plural - "Clean Ponds". The old mansion is inhabited by inhabitants of the salty warm depths. Functions

The Oceanarium at Chistye Prudy was originally conceived as a pet store. Currently, he continues to perform the aforementioned function. Visitors can order an aquarium here on an individual project, buy fish food and a closed ecosystem of the deep sea that does not require outside intervention. They also sell aquatic inhabitants for keeping at home.

What does the oceanarium offer visitors?

The pet store, which is located in the building of an old mansion on Chistye Prudy, sells more than two hundred species of various aquarium complexes, marine and freshwater aquariums, equipment, as well as aquaterrariums. For visitors there is an aqua design studio. Its specialists will help to stylishly and harmoniously fit into the buyer‚Äôs interior an acquired house for fish and other marine life.





The pet store offers a wide selection of fish and invertebrates from all over the planet. There is an installation and maintenance department in the aquarium. He provides clients with installation services for aquariums. In addition, there is the possibility of warranty service.





The pet store implements closed ecosystems. These are small cubes or balls, in which there are symbioses of animals and plants, which are living closed communities. The existence of such ecosystems is supported by interrelated processes of life. Such symbioses do not need to be cleaned, fertilized or replaced. Subject to the necessary external conditions, the community is capable of long-term existence.

Expositions

What especially attracts visitors to the Aquarium at Chistye Prudy? Reviews testify that the exhibition of expositions of oceanic and sea landscapes is of particular interest. In this regard, the territory of the store-hall can only be reached after paying for the ticket. Its price is by no means prohibitive, in addition, there are discounts for sightseeing groups and children.





The proposed programs are useful for students and those who are simply interested in the underwater world. The cost of tickets can be found on the official website of the aquarium.

The marine aquarium at Chistye Prudy has large aquarium complexes, each of which is dedicated to certain marine and oceanic zones that exist on our planet. Here you can see amazing examples of ecosystems. The visitors are offered coral reefs of the Indian and Pacific Oceans. Inhabitants Collection

The Oceanarium on Chistye Prudy invites its visitors to admire the unusual and amazing representatives of the oceanic and marine fauna, which are located in more than two hundred separate aquariums.

The collection of underwater inhabitants can amaze with its diversity even the most demanding guest. Over one thousand species of various exotic fish and marine life are represented here .

Visitors can admire the magical sea anemones with poisonous tentacles, toothy moraines and a variety of invertebrate animals. Among the inhabitants of the aquariums are jellyfish and nautiluses, octopuses and turtles, Kamchatka crabs and stingrays, gluttonous piranhas and toothy sharks.





The Oceanarium at Chistye Prudy placed a predatory "fraternity" in a twenty-five-meter circular bathyscaphe. Entering a separate darkened room, visitors fall into the heart of the pack. Here, gracefully, looking strictly around, swim past the shark. They captivate visitors with their proximity, precise and swift movements, as well as streamlined flowing shapes.

The Oceanarium at Chistye Prudy every week on Tuesdays and Fridays holds a unique and interesting show - feeding sharks. It enjoys constant success, especially among young visitors.

Visit program
