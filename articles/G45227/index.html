How to transfer money from card to card of Sberbank: all methods

Every second resident of Russia has a bank card of Sberbank today. This is a convenient tool for storing and receiving funds, as well as making payments. Today, even older people, accustomed to cash, are learning to use plastic rectangles and find many advantages. But, nevertheless, even experienced users sometimes have questions, and one of the most popular among them is: "How to transfer money from card to card?" Sberbank offers several options for the implementation of this type of transfer.

Virtual transfer via online banking



Sberbank offers its customers a lot of convenient services for more convenient payment transactions. Sberbank-Online allows you to manage your finances using a computer with Internet access. How to transfer money to a Sberbank card using this service? You will need to log in to the system using a personal login and password - they can be obtained at the ATM. Attention: the transfer is confirmed by SMS with a one-time password sent by the system to the phone number on which Mobile Bank is connected. The Sberbank-Online service is now available not only from computers, but also from modern smartphones.

Transfer via short mobile messages



Is it possible to transfer money from one card to another using one telephone? Sberbank offers users of the Mobile Bank service to transfer funds using two SMS. From the phone to which the card is attached, you need to send a message to the service number 900. SMS text: "TRANSFER <phone number of the person to whom we transfer> <transfer amount>". The recipient will receive the name and patronymic of the recipient and a confirmation code, as well as instructions for completing the operation. If you transfer money to a person who does not have a Mobile Bank connected, instead of a phone number, dial the card number. The word "TRANSFER" in SMS can be replaced by "TRANSFER", "Translation", "PEREVESTI" or "PEREVOD".

Transfer via terminal or ATM



You can send money to a relative or friend using an ATM or terminal. To perform this operation, you need to know the card number of the person to whom you are transferring funds. This is a combination of numbers on the front of the plastic. Insert your card into the device and enter the pin code. Select the ‚ÄúPayments and Transfers‚Äù menu. Next, fill in the empty fields in the form that appears: enter the card number and the amount to transfer. The system will prompt you to check the details and confirm the operation. As you can see, Sberbank offers to transfer money from card to card in just a few clicks.

Can I translate at the office?



Through a bank branch, you can replenish someone else's card account without even being a client of the organization. Refer to the window with the sign "Acceptance of payments." Provide the operator with the card number of the person to whom you want to transfer money, and select the payment option: cash deposit, transfer from a personal bank account or card. After a few seconds, your operation will be completed, do not forget to take a receipt. In addition, at any office there are ATMs and terminals. You can contact the employees and ask how to transfer money to a Sberbank card using this device if you have never previously performed the operation yourself. In principle, there is nothing archaic in this; the interface for most equipment models is quite understandable.

How to withdraw money from Sberbank card after transfer?

Despite the popularization of cashless payments, in many cases cash is still required in our country. How to receive money after you received a transfer to the card? The easiest way is to find an ATM. You need to insert your card into it, dial the PIN code. Attention: ATMs issue denominations in the face value of one hundred rubles.

No less popular question: "How to withdraw money from a Sberbank card if it is not at hand?" You can get a transfer in this case too. Contact the office of Sberbank with a passport, in case of loss of the card or the expiration of its validity period, you can also order its reissue.

Helpful information



We considered all the options for transferring money from card to card. Sberbank promises its customers crediting funds to the account within 24 hours. Sometimes (for example, on weekends and holidays) transfers take longer. So when to start to panic and turn to Sberbank? Did not send money to the card during the day? Check the check, SMS messages or history in the online service. It is possible that the problem arose because you made a mistake when filling out the details. In this case, you can try to return the money. If the transfer does not reach the recipient, the most reliable solution to the problem is to contact the office of Sberbank. It is also good to know that there are some limitations. You can not transfer more than 100,000 rubles a day without contacting the office. Using the Mobile Bank, you can only make 10 operations per day. Remember that you can always transfer funds from one of your cards to another, if you have two or more. In general, the procedure is similar to transferring to another person, but in the ATM or online service you need to select the option "Operations with your accounts and cards" in the payment menu.

How to transfer money to another Sberbank card that belongs to you? In the form that opens, select the last digits of the numbers, from where and where you are sending the funds, and indicate the amount. Then it remains only to confirm the operation.
