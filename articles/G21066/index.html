The best ski resorts in the USA: list

Skiing is one of the most popular activities in the world. Such an active vacation is suitable for any person of any age at any time of the year. In the USA, the ski business is extremely responsible, despite the fact that not every state has suitable conditions. Recreation centers are appreciated for their accessibility and, of course, the duration of the slopes. The list of US ski resorts in the article below.

1. Aspen

Aspen is arguably the most popular ski resort in Colorado, USA. It is also the most prestigious and expensive. Despite this fact, the center is accessible to people with any budget. The resort town is built in Victorian style. It is located in a scenic spot in the Roaring Fork Valley.

The resort combines four ski areas, including Aspen Mountain, Aspen Highlands, Buttermilk and Snowmass. All of them offer two hundred kilometers of tracks for every taste. Both beginners and professional skiers will be able to enjoy skiing here.

Maroon Bells and Pyramid Peak are the highest peaks in the area, with heights of 4247 and 4205 meters respectively.

In Aspen, there are a huge variety of different hotels, shops, as well as all sorts of entertainment, in addition to skiing.

2. Deer Valley

Deer Valley Resort in 2014 was recognized as the best ski resort in the United States. Visitors called it the best conditions for service, family programs, restaurants, hotels and well-groomed trails. The most respectable center of North America, Deer Valley was called five years in a row, starting in 2007.

The resort is located in the Wasatch Mountains, thanks to which it is known worldwide for its upscale slopes, of which there are more than a hundred in the center. The total ski area is 820 hectares, including six peaks. The highest point of skiing is almost three kilometers. The longest track stretched for 4.5 kilometers.

One of the best ski resorts in America does not accept snowboarders and has limitations on the total number of skiers present.

3. Vail

This ski resort is the most bohemian and pathos of all. Mostly wealthy outdoor enthusiasts flock here to rush once on the highway, and the rest of the time to create the image of the richest person.

Vale is a two-hour drive from Denver. It has existed here since 1962 and is currently one of the five best ski resorts in the world. Also, this place is one of the most beloved among Americans due to the huge free space for skiing and the fastest lifts. At one of the best ski resorts in the United States, each guest can find something to do. The center is intended not only for rich people and professionals - skiers of any level of training will be able to enjoy outdoor activities.

4. Breckenridge

Breckenridge is the highest skiing center in the USA - the highest point is at an altitude of almost four kilometers. Here you can not only feel all the pleasures of skiing and snowboarding, but also see historical sights. The city has preserved the spirit of old America, and new buildings are being built in the style of the "gold rush" period. Breckenridge can be called a unique ski resort.

It is an hour and a half drive from Denver Airport.

5. Winter park

This ski center is located on the territory of the National Forest of Erepejo, located an hour's drive from Denver, and also three kilometers from the city of the same name. Winter park is one of the oldest resorts in Colorado - the first guests he received in 1940.

Locals and citizens from all over the country love to spend time here. The main advantage of the center is its close proximity to Denver, as well as a large selection of slopes for skiers with a wide variety of experiences. Winter park is first and foremost a homely atmosphere and unique hospitality, which is rarely seen in the ski resorts of the United States.

6. Park City Mountain

Park City Mountain is one of the largest ski resorts in the United States. Many call it the best place for families. He is one of the five best resorts in the country and ranks first in terms of location. Park City Mountain is a thirty-minute drive from Salt Lake City Airport. The center has a large number of jumping areas.

7. Canyons

Canyons Resort is Utah's largest ski resort by area. Here, anyone can experience the choice of a route of any complexity from nine mountain peaks. Canyons is one of the five most popular centers among Americans. Regular visitors appreciate this ski resort for the abundance of snow and for the best service. Over thirty million dollars were invested in the development of the center.

8. Beaver Creek

This US ski resort is a two and a half hour drive from Denver. Here, in 1998 and 1999, the International Alpine Skiing Championships were held, which is indisputable evidence of the high level and quality of service and tracks.

The prestigious resort is located in a village founded in 1881, with the pleasant name "Beaver Creek".

The track on the Slope of the Birds of Prey, which is one of the three most difficult in the world, is the most favorite among extreme skiing.

9. Mammoth Mountain

Mammoth Mountain is a five-hour drive from Los Angeles. The center is the largest in California. Mount Mammoth Mountain is so named for its shape resembling an ancient hairy beast. Such outlines gave rise to many diverse slopes and descents that can satisfy the desire of any adrenaline fan.

10. Heavenly

Heavenly Resort is located on the border of Nevada and California. At the foot of the mountain is the largest mountain lake in America, Lake Tahoe. This versatile resort offers to combine an active vacation with excitement, because there is a casino here.

Hevenly combines three main ingredients that allow you to feel the rich taste of relaxation: nature, skiing on the picturesque slopes, endless nightlife. Various bars, restaurants and clubs are open around the clock.

The resort boasts unique weather conditions, because there are three hundred sunny days a year.

Heavenly is a four-hour drive from San Francisco.

11. Lake Placid

Many people call the Lake Placid village the capital of the Winter Olympic Games, although the last time they took place here was in 1932 and 1980. This resort is very popular due to the fact that here not only skiing is possible. The center has created ideal conditions for practicing almost all winter sports.

The high season begins here in November and ends in April. The best conditions for skiing in the spring, because winter brings a lot of winds.

America's ski resort is an hour's drive from New York.

12. Telluride

A cozy ski resort is located in Colorado. Due to its location, the historical spirit of the mountain village is preserved here, in which traditions are still observed, and life flows slowly and charmingly. Almost all the buildings and bars are made in the style of the Wild West, but this does not prevent them from side by side with newfangled hotels and elite restaurants.

The nearest airport to the resort is Montrose, which will take you about an hour and a half to drive from. The road from Denver will take about seven hours.

13. Keystone

This US ski resort is one of the largest in Colorado. Here guests are invited to plunge into the atmosphere of a happy holiday with family or friends. Adventure Point was created for slopes on an inflatable "cheesecake", Keystone Lake is a huge ice rink, and at the top of Dercum there is a large snow fortress.

Keystone Resort offers a variety of accommodation options, from budget hotels to luxury apartments.

Keystone Resort is located 145 kilometers from Denver. The road from the airport to the ski center takes about an hour and a half.

14. Northstar

Northstar ski resort offers its potential guests many attractive opportunities. Here you can take a ride from Lookout Mountain and try to go through nine levels of difficulty, two of which are two-kilometer descents on Backside and Magic Moguls. In addition to traditional alpine skiing and snowboarding, in Northstar you can try out cross-country, telemark and even snowshoes.

At the Nothstar Resort, visitors of all ages can enjoy their holidays and spend time with pleasure, even if no one is skiing. The village has a huge number of restaurants, various entertainment venues located around a large ice rink.

15. Las Lenas

At the end of the list of ski centers in the United States, I would like to travel to another continent and note the best ski resort in South America - Las Lenas, located in Argentina. It has an area equal to sixteen thousand hectares. For comparison, the largest resort in North America reaches only three and a half thousand hectares. The only problem is the cost of time and effort to get to the center, however many skiers and snowboarders note that Las Lenas is worth such funds.
