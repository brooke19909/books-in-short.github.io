Bank "Russia": customer reviews, services, deposits and services

In the article, we consider customer reviews about the bank "Russia".

This institution has a general license, which allows it to carry out financial transactions. This structure is a professional participant in the securities market. She has the right to carry out dealer, brokerage and depository manipulations. The Bank is engaged in securities management activities and interacts with stock brokers making option and futures commodity transactions in the framework of general trading. Reviews about the bank "Russia" are presented in the article.

Bank Information and Customer Service

This institution has a developed network of offices and branches. In total, their number today is approximately sixty. The main clients of the financial institution represented are corporations with various enterprises. The list of bank services for corporate clients is very wide. Individuals can choose financial products from the standard list. For example, you can open an account, order the issue of plastic cards, use the rental of safes and so on. It is worth noting that in recent years the assets of this financial organization have increased significantly. Also, the level of the currency balance increased by as much as 32%.

Rating

According to various prestigious financial publications, the rating of the Bank of Russia is quite high. With an asset indicator of almost four hundred and fifty billion rubles, this structure occupies the third place within its region and the fifteenth in the Russian Federation. The St. Petersburg region is in second place in terms of net profit, and the twenty-first in the country. Credit rating is stable. By unofficial popular vote, this bank is placed on 117th place. Reviews about the bank "Russia" are mostly positive.

Mortgage

According to reviews, the mortgage bank "Russia" also has advantages. Housing lending in this bank is provided to three categories of citizens. Loans

Loans from Russia Bank can also be selected according to your needs. You can issue one of three options. For example, take a loan secured by real estate. It can be taken on various offers from twelve to twenty-four months. The rate also varies and ranges from 14 to 21 percent.

This bank also presents two types of car loans. One of them can be issued together with CASCO, and the other without insurance. In the second case, the rate rises by five percent and will be 17.7%. You can return the money within five years.

Customer reviews

Client reviews about the bank "Russia" are quite diverse. According to positive comments, this financial institution conscientiously serves salary cards, provides favorable loan conditions with attractive deposit packages.

At the same time, there are also not quite positive reviews about Russia Bank. For example, in some situations, customers are unhappy with the loss of funds from their cards, which may indicate some gaps in the security system. Repeatedly in the discussions, an unfavorable rate is also noted in the framework of withdrawing currency from plastics upon receipt of the amount in rubles.

But it is worth emphasizing that almost all customers are satisfied with politeness, and at the same time, with the attention of the staff. They are also pleased that this institution provides an opportunity to resolve some issues by sending their representatives to clients.
