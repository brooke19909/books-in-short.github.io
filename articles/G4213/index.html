Hotels in Saki: addresses, description of rooms, infrastructure, photos

People traveling on holidays to Crimea often choose hotels in Saki. Although Saki is a small town, it has a good location. The city is located on the shores of the Kalamitsky Gulf, in which the water warms up earlier than in the Black Sea, and the beaches delight with fine sand.

On the other hand, the city borders Saki Lake, the mud of which is used for medicinal purposes. In a 20-minute drive is the large resort city of Yevpatoriya with all kinds of entertainment. Not only beach vacations, but also medical tourism is in demand in Saki, because there are several sanatoriums with an excellent reputation in the city that treat various diseases.

This article provides information about where you can stay in Saki, which hotel to choose, which price will be optimal.

Important nuance

Whatever hotel in Saki attracts attention, you must definitely specify the cost in advance. In all Crimean hotels, the price of a room depends on the season: in June it is cheaper, in July-August it is more expensive. Some hotels operate year-round, but many - seasonal - have been welcoming guests from April-May.

Novofedorovka

The village belongs to the Saki region and is surrounded by salt lakes on the Black Sea. The decoration of the village is an extended embankment. From Novofedorovka it is easy to get to Saki, Yevpatoriya and other cities of Crimea.

There are about 20 Novofedorovsky hotels, and all of them are diverse in price level.

The economy segment is represented by the small hotels "Guest House Summer", "Tatyana", "Victoria". Rest will cost 250-500 rubles per person.

Various types of rooms, many of which are equipped with air conditioning, are presented in the private hotels "Edem", "Marikon", "Villa Elena", "Irina". At Gala Paradise, Aquilon, Elite and Oasis, you can use the pool, and at Villa Magic you can use the sauna.
