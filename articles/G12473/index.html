Several ways to make satin ribbon bows simple and fast

Ribbons, bows, hairpins - this is not a complete list of all kinds of fashionable pieces of modern girls used by them to decorate their hair. But very often all of these accessories purchased in a store are bored with their uniformity, uniformity. But even small fashionistas want to look beautiful, stylish. The article provides several ways to make satin ribbon bows . A step-by-step guide will help with this.

How to tie a ribbon in the shape of a lush layered flower?





There are a great many ways to make bows from satin ribbons. Below is a description of one of them, on the basis of which you can come up with other options. If the tape is of medium width (4-6 cm), not very dense and has a fairly soft structure, this method is suitable.

  1. Leave the free end of the desired length on one side.
  2. Fold the accordion-shaped bow, laying each next layer exactly on the previous one.
  3. Having thus obtained five to eight loops on each side, bend the second free end under the bottom of the product.
  4. Using a thin wire or threads folded in several layers, pull the bow in the middle.
  5. Scroll all the loops received left and right, evenly distributing in a circle.
  6. Decorate the middle of the flower with a beautiful brooch or beads.

The loose ends can be used instead of a headband for tying around the head or left in a fluttering position for decoration.
