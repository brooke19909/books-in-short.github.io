"Express Infinity" - budget modern technologies

The current generation of people has long forgotten about the simple and necessary features of phones. Absolutely no one pays attention to the overall dimensions, the backlight of the buttons or, for example, an antenna with an extended range. 21st century cell phones are chasing only the latest technology. Any work is paid, thereby making luxury items out of many phones. Not everyone can afford to make a purchase, the cost of which is a monthly income or even more. Expanding their circle of consumers, many manufacturers focus on phones designed for the middle class.



One of these products was the smartphone "Infinity Express". The device was created using the latest technologies, while its layout and low cost will make it possible to purchase a modern phone without spending a lot of money on it.
