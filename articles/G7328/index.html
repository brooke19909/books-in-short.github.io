Sinatra Hotel 4 * (Kemer): description

This hotel was built in the late nineties of the twentieth century. During this period, in Turkey, multi-storey hotel complexes with a huge territory and elaborate design were mainly built. Against their background, Hotel Sinatra 4 * in Kemer (Camyuva) seems like a real gem.

It is a five-story complex designed by the corner, where all rooms, a restaurant, reception and other infrastructure facilities are located. It is noteworthy that the entire hotel is designed in a restrained style that evokes thoughts of the sixties. The fact is that the Sinatra Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Kemer) is named after the famous Frank Sinatra, which can be traced in literally every detail of the interior. Some tourists claim that the hotel is so different from the usual Turkish hotels that it gives the impression of being in a small seaside European town where you are always welcome, and the service in the complex is absolutely unobtrusive.

The last repair at the Sinatra Hotel 4 * in Kemer (guest reviews often contain information about comfortable rooms and their excellent condition) was carried out twelve years ago. However, the hotel still looks very dignified and fully justifies its four stars. It is noteworthy, in many 2017 reviews of Sinatra Hotel 4 * (Kemer), its guests claim that the hotel complex can be assigned five stars, despite the fact that in the current holiday season it almost completely changed its staff and management.

The hotel area is quite small - just over two thousand square meters. Of course, this is not enough to accommodate the mass of infrastructure. Hotel infrastructure

Usually, the infrastructure of Turkish hotels can be written indefinitely, but in our case there is nothing superfluous on the territory of Hotel Sinatra 4 *, but at the same time there is everything you need to make guests feel comfortable.

The hotel has one large swimming pool with a separate section for babies. It has no slides, which distinguishes this complex from many others located in Turkey. Around the pool is equipped with a relaxation area with an abundance of sunbeds and umbrellas. They are always enough for all guests, however, some comments mention their unpresentable appearance. Guests write that sun loungers need to be washed well, as many of them clearly show dirt stains.

It offers two small bars and one restaurant, where breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in buffet style.

There is one conference room at the hotel, it can accommodate up to eighty people and is equipped with all the necessary equipment and technology for events. There is also a banquet room nearby, where hotel staff can set tables for you in honor of an anniversary or any other festive event.

Near the hotel there is free parking. There are usually quite a lot of empty seats, so guests do not experience problems with leaving vehicles for the night.

Also, many guests enjoy the Turkish hammam. When checking into a tourist room, his mistress meets him, offering a range of services at a very reasonable price. Vacationers will be able to enjoy the procedures provided by professional bathhouse attendants and masseurs.

Dining at the hotel complex

About the food in the hotel in the reviews mentioned very often. It is at its best here, so it‚Äôs difficult to stay hungry in a hotel complex. Keep in mind that Hotel Sinatra 4 * operates on an all-inclusive basis. But there is always a six-hour break between lunch and dinner. The only drawback of the restaurant is tourists consider a small selection of pastries and sweets. If you do not have time to take them at the beginning of the dinner, then by the end of the choice there may no longer be any.

All reviews noted that the restaurant is very clean. Waiters quickly bring appliances and change dirty tablecloths. All staff are extremely polite and smiling.





Number of rooms

The most common room type at Sinatra Hotel 4 * is standard. Kemer is distinguished by an abundance of similar rooms in various hotel complexes. The hotel described by us has about forty-eight such apartments, in addition to them, there are twelve more corner rooms and two suites.

Each room has a minibar and a safe. However, you must pay for using them. For example, one day of using the safe costs two dollars. They also include air conditioning and satellite TV. Usually vacationers spend a lot of time in the evening on the balcony, but in this hotel it will be impossible to do this - the balcony is so small that it can accommodate only one person.

The bathroom has a shower and hairdryer. However, quite often, guests note that the shower is extremely narrow, so overall people will be very uncomfortable there.

The beds in the rooms are quite wide and it is very pleasant to sleep on them. Guests rate the apartment cleaning very highly. Towels and bed linen are changed daily, this is not always found even in five-star hotels.
