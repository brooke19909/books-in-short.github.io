Hotel Fame Hotel 3 * (Kemer, Turkey) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

Fame Hotel 3 * in Kemer is a hotel offering excellent living conditions and a rather high level of service in return for affordable payment for services. Every year several hundred tourists from all over the world come here to spend their holidays in an exotic atmosphere of warmth, tropical vegetation, and walking distance to the sea.

general information

Many travelers consider this hotel in their comments to be one of the best places for an inexpensive vacation in Kemer. In the reviews of tourists Fame Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kemer) is often described by positive words addressed to the beautiful interior of the rooms presented and the excellent arrangement of the territory.

The hotel was built in 1989, since then both large and small renovations have been made on its territory. During their conduct, the conditions for accommodation of tourists, as well as the external and internal appearance of the hotel, improved. In 2015, major repairs were made in its buildings and the interior of the apartments was updated.

Location

The hotel is located in a rather lively area, in the central part of Kemer, which is the capital of one of the resort regions of Turkey. Not far from its territory various entertainment venues are concentrated: bars, restaurants, karaoke clubs, cafes, discos. There is even a yacht club next door to it, which you can visit by reaching the marina. Nearby there are several attractions that you can visit on your own or by ordering a guided tour at the hotel, which is paid separately from the ticket.

In the city of Antalya, which is located 50 kilometers from this place, there is an international airport where all foreign travelers arrive. Tourists note that a passenger car provided by the hotel as part of the transfer service takes them from here. It is equipped with modern air conditioning, so that all guests can quickly and comfortably drive to the hotel Fame Hotel 3 * (Kemer).





Rooms

The attention of every tourist resting in this place is presented with a considerable list of rooms, consisting of 108 apartments with ceramic flooring. All of them are decorated in the best possible way, each made a fresh repair. The reviews about the hotel Fame Hotel 3 * (Kemer), left by guests, often say that the general atmosphere of coziness and warmth prevailing here sets you up for a comfortable stay and has a pleasant pastime.

All rooms are presented in one category - standard. In each of them, from the moment of one of the latest renovations, climatic equipment has been installed, with which you can increase or lower the total temperature in the room and maintain it at a certain level.

The apartments adjoin shower rooms combined with bathrooms. They have all the plumbing that is necessary to maintain a high level of hygiene. In addition, tourists can also use the bath accessories provided for each traveler individually at the time of arrival.

Apartment equipment

All rooms have only modern furniture for two guests. It is represented by two beds in the form of one large or two small beds. The spacious room also includes a walk-in wardrobe for storing clothes and personal belongings. The apartments have a desk and chair - they can serve as a working area.

Guests note that the hotel rooms are well decorated. Each of them has a large mirror, as well as several lamps. In addition, the apartments feature a considerable amount of wood details.

In their free time, guests have the opportunity to watch Russian-language channels that are broadcast on a plasma TV as part of a satellite broadcasting system. Here, anyone can use the phone connected to the international line, as well as a safe and a minibar. These objects are provided for use subject to payment of their daily rent.

Nutrition

In order for guests of the Fame Hotel 3 * (Kemer) to be able to eat without going outside the hotel, it has several establishments where, according to vacationers, they prepare rather delicious dishes in the best traditions of European and Turkish cuisines. A daily buffet is served in the main restaurant, where all those guests who live according to the all-inclusive concept are invited. The room's hall is divided into two parts: open and closed, both are free to visit. In total, the restaurant can accommodate up to 300 people at a time.

In addition to a large establishment, the hotel has two small, represented by bar counters. Their menu places a great emphasis on various drinks and cocktails, which are distinguished by their brightness and uniqueness. Travelers note that they are characterized by original taste. In addition to cocktails, the bars serve snacks and salads. You can also enjoy desserts in them.





Entertainment

Additional features

Fame Hotel 3 * (Kemer / Kemer-center) offers a number of features that, if necessary, can be used by all hotel guests. Services are paid.

If desired, any tourist can take their things to the laundry or dry cleaning - there professional craftsmen will bring them to perfection and give their original fresh look. Also, guests staying here can use the medical services provided by a qualified doctor. They are available only on request.

Special attention is paid to the possibility of renting a car without leaving the hotel. Many travelers recommend going to interesting places and to neighboring cities on it. For such a machine for the entire period of its use, the hotel allocates a free parking space in an open parking lot.





Beach

On the coastline, which is located 300 meters from the Fame Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kemer / Kemer-center), the hotel has its own plot, the total length of which is 50 meters. As guests of the region have noticed, everything is arranged here for a rather comfortable pastime.

Small sun loungers with umbrellas are placed here to accommodate vacationers, and for their convenience there are several booths in which there is a changing room, a shower room and a public toilet. There is no towel collection point on the beach, so it is advisable to bring such items if necessary.

If desired, tourists can have fun with water skiing or scuba diving - this is done only under the guidance of an instructor, whose services are paid. Here you can also rent a boat or yacht and go on a short trip through the open sea.

Prices

Fame Hotel 3 * (Kemer) has an acceptable price policy for many, which provides for the democratic cost of the services provided in the hotel and the entire travel package.
