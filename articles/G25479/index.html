The more expensive the dollar becomes, the lower the cost of our favorite gadgets we can afford. In this case, they enter the battlefield - inexpensive budget models. Among them is the HomTom HT7, reviews of which make you think about buying it.

It is an ultra-budget model of the smartphone. Which, however, remains quite attractive and productive, despite the low cost. But first things first.

Appearance

The smartphone received a high-quality plastic case with two color options - black and white. The casing has nice arched fillets in the upper and lower parts. The back cover got corrugated, which makes it look good, does not get dirty, and the smartphone does not slip out of the hand. About the case HomTom HT7 reviews are all positive, they emphasize the ergonomics and quality of materials. The volume and power buttons are located on the left side, which gives some discomfort to the right-hander, but you can get used to it during operation. The rest of the device is no different from similar state employees from this price category.

Specifications

The smartphone received a classic MT6580A budget processor for this price category, which has 4 cores operating at a frequency of 1.3 GHz. The Mali-400 video chip is integrated into it, which will be enough for most simple games from the application store, but it can‚Äôt cope with complex 3D games at all, or they will hang noticeably. The smartphone is equipped with two cameras that have a resolution of 5 MP and 2 MP. For storing photos and other files of the user, as well as the system itself, a non-volatile memory of 8 GB is provided, which, if desired, can be expanded using a MicroSD memory card up to 64 GB.

Display

I would like to pay special attention to the display, which is both a strong and a weak side of the device. On the one hand, the smartphone has a chic IPS-matrix with a diagonal of 5.5 inches and a resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels. This is enough to watch movies and photos, while pixelation is almost invisible. In addition, there is enough margin of brightness to comfortably use a smartphone even on a bright sunny day.





However, the sensor in most cases is not of good quality. The multi-touch of this smartphone is designed for just two touches, as a result of which, with a fast typing of messages, false alarms or sticking of the sensor are very often noticed. Battery

The battery capacity declared by the manufacturer is 3000 mAh. But, as shown by most tests, almost always the capacity is lower, sometimes significantly. Well, you can only come to terms with this, since almost all manufacturers of budget devices overestimate their characteristics.





In this case, it is simply best to rely on the capacity determined by Doogee HomTom HT7 users, whose reviews speak of average battery performance - about 2300-2700 mAh. According to them, even this is enough to comfortably use the smartphone in active mode during the day, and not live near the outlet.

Software

The firmware from the factory comes with Android 5.1. It is quite well optimized and, despite the budget hardware, it works smartly. Most users who bought a HomTom HT7 phone write positive reviews about the firmware, noting that the scrolling in the menu is smooth, and in general, using the smartphone creates a pleasant feeling.

Smartphone Reviews

In principle, the HomTom HT7 smartphone has earned positive reviews, but consumers have indicated a number of shortcomings. The disadvantages definitely include the speed and accuracy of the sensor and the quality of the received photos from cameras. For some users, the inconvenient location of the keys on the side of the phone was also critical.

General impression of the device

In general, this unit is suitable for those who want for a little money to get a functional device and use all modern communication methods. However, it should be borne in mind that it is the state employee who is being bought, which means that some shortcomings simply cannot be avoided.
