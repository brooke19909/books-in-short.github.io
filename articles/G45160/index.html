Hardware platform performance

So, let's see what hardware the Oukitel K10000 Pro Black smartphone is equipped with. User reviews are full of positive gadget performance ratings. The smartphone supports LTE Cat.6 networks, has advanced Wi-Fi and Bluetooth transmitters and is equipped with a smart navigation module. The latter connects to satellites within 10 seconds.
