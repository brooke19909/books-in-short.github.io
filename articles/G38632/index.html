Guest House Rodnichok (Anapa, Dzhemete). Guest reviews

The private guest house "Rodnichok" (Anapa, Dzhemete) during its operation has established itself as a comfortable hotel for a relaxing family vacation.





Private hotels of this kind have become popular in the south of Russia (Dzhemete, Vityazevo, Sukko). Rodnichok is located in the middle of Pioneer Avenue, 20 meters from the sandy beach with dunes.

About hotel

Rodnichok Guest House (Anapa) is a three-story building with forty guest rooms. Near this private hotel there is a paid car park that can accommodate up to 30 cars. A hairdresser, laundry. The guest house has its own small sea pier.

Rodnichok provides its guests with double, triple and quadruple rooms. The rooms are furnished in the "standard" style with new furniture, equipped with working appliances: air conditioning, TV. Wi-Fi is available throughout the guest house. For every two rooms there is a single bathroom equipped with a shower. The guest house provides round-the-clock hot and cold water supply.

Service and food

Rodnichok Guest House (Anapa) provides a transfer service for its guests from the station to the guest house and vice versa. Guests use the service of storing valuables in a safe, located at the reception. If necessary, they order an ironing board in the room. Here they can sign up for a massage. For this type of private hotel, Rodnichok, a guesthouse 2 * (Anapa), demonstrates a sufficiently thoughtful and convenient service for guests. Their feedback on the rest in it is positive.





Catering for tourists at the guest house is organized as follows. Those who paid for the guesthouse receive a full breakfast, lunch and dinner in the cafe of the same house.

Breakfast menu includes cottage cheese casserole, oatmeal or rice porridge, scrambled eggs with sausage, salads, cheese sandwiches, fruits, tea, cocoa. For lunch, guests are served a choice of two first courses, two or three second courses. The main dishes are prepared from chicken, beef, fish. The dinner is varied and quite satisfying. The dinner menu is reminiscent of lunch.

On the ground floor, a kitchen and a furnished dining room are equipped to cater for tourists who want to cook their own meals.

Employees of the guest house (such is the order established here) do not clean guest rooms every day, as they do in "star" hotels, but only after receiving a corresponding request from them. Linen and towels are changed once a week.





In addition, at the request of tourists, they organize excursions from the guest house.

Directly next to the guest house there is a minibus and a grocery store. Tiki-tak Water Park is a 10-minute walk, and the local market is at the same distance. To the noisy and crowded regional resort of Anapa from the hotel in question to go only 10 minutes.

The prices of guest rooms, relative to similar rooms in hotels, are low. For a night, residents of a double room pay depending on the month from 1,400 rubles (in May-June) to 2,000 rubles (in July-August). For accommodation in a triple room with guests will be charged, respectively, 1800 and 2700 rubles; in a four-seater - from 3500 rubles.

Beach and outdoor activities

Rodnichok Guest House (Anapa, Dzhemete) is a two-minute walk from a clean, sandy, secluded beach. The entry into the water is smooth, accessible to children. The water area is clear, the water is clear. Vacationers often see frolicking dolphins in it.





Those who wish to visit a more comfortable beach (with changing cabins, sunbeds, umbrellas, cafes, water rides, etc.) are not deprived as well. You can walk to the pebble Anapa beach in 40 minutes from the guest house or, more conveniently, drive to it 5 minutes by taxi.
