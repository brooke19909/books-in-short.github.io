But what about the processor?

The time-tested central processor is used in LG Magna. Reviews about MT6582 (namely, such a chip is used in this gadget) confirm this. Its hallmark is a very high degree of reliability. Smartphones based on it have been used for more than one year and characterize it only from the best side. Another plus is excellent energy efficiency. Unused computing power is disabled when idle, and modules that do not need a maximum level of performance automatically reduce their clock frequency to 300 MHz. But the disadvantages include a low level of performance. The composition of this semiconductor crystal includes four computing modules of the architecture "A7". Each of them can accelerate when solving the most complex problems to 1.3 GHz. The chip itself is manufactured using a 28-nm process. The capabilities of this chip are enough to solve most everyday tasks. An exception to it are the most demanding applications of the latest generation, which are based on 64-bit computing. This CPU can only process 32 bits of information. This is where problems arise with the launch of new software.

Battery

The battery capacity of 2540 mAh boasts LG Magna H502F. Reviews clearly indicate that this battery capacity is enough for 2-3 days of operation with an average level of load on the gadget. If you activate the maximum energy saving mode, you can count on 4 days of confident and stable operation of the device. Well, in the case of maximum load on the smartphone, one charge of the battery will be enough for 7-9 hours. As a result, it can be noted that in terms of autonomy, this device does not stand out from the competition. A certain solution, which will significantly increase the battery life of this device, may be the purchase of an additional external battery. This will allow you to avoid problems with discharging the battery at the most inopportune moment for this.





Interface set

An impressive set of information transfer tools is installed in the smartphone LG Magna H502. Reviews highlight such of them:

  • Also this device is equipped with a Wi-Fi transmitter. This is the main way to receive and send data to the "global web." In this case, the speed can reach impressive today 150 Mbit / s.
  • The navigation system in this smart phone uses two methods at once to determine its location. The first one is GPS. It uses a signal from satellites and allows you to accurately determine the location of the device. The second is A-GPS. In this case, you need to connect to the Internet.

  • In addition, there is an audio port. It outputs an audio signal to an external stereo system. Also, if there is an appropriate plug, it can receive an audio signal from an external microphone.
