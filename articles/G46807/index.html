Hotel "Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club" (Turkey, Kemer): description, service, reviews. Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club 5 *

Kemer is a major entertainment center in Turkey, which is visited every year by tourists from different regions of the world. Vacation prices here are relatively low, but the level of service is pleasantly pleasing. There are many excellent hotels here that are always happy to welcome tourists. One of them is Ulus Kemer Holiday Club.

Location

This hotel is located on the first line of the sea, near the center of the small village of Geynuk. It is literally 20 minutes drive from the bustling central part of Kemer - it is 7 kilometers away. As for the transport interchange nodes, the nearest airport is located 45 kilometers from Ulusoy Kemer, and you can get to it by a comfortable bus provided by the hotel.

As for the weather, it is not hot at all in this part of the Mediterranean coast; guests can enjoy the temperature softened by the air masses of the Mediterranean. Here during the entire resort period there is a huge number of sunny days, which also pleases tourists - they often mention this in their reviews about the rest.



general information

"Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club" - the hotel is quite large not only in its territory, whose area is almost 43,000 square meters. m, but also by the number of rooms in the fund. It was built in 1989, since then a number of various reconstructions have been made here, both of the entire territory as a whole and of some parts of the buildings. At the moment, its buildings in the form of bungalows are beautiful buildings that can attract the attention of vacationers.

Rooms

All apartments in Ulusoy Kemer are in small bungalows, which are buildings one, two and three floors. Each of them has a separate room or a complex of those. In total, the hotel presents to the attention of tourists 465 apartments of various categories: "economy", "standard", "family" and "suite".

All apartments have a private bathroom, equipped with everything necessary to maintain a state of cleanliness of the whole body. It also has additional furniture designed to store bath accessories, bathrobes and towels, which are provided for use by every tourist.

The apartments have personal air conditioning, which are installed in each room separately.



"Economy"

There are 40 economy rooms in the hotel. Each of them is presented in the form of one room, designed for a couple of people. In total, its territory is small - only 12-14 square meters. m

On the provided quadrature there is a minimal furniture set, which consists of a light double bed, a pair of hanging shelves near the bed, as well as a small wardrobe in which to bring your brought things. If you wish, you can watch a TV that is connected to a satellite broadcasting system.

Tourists who stay in these apartments note that you can have a great time together, being, for example, on a honeymoon. Experienced tourists note that from the windows of these rooms you can enjoy stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.

"Standard"

Standard rooms are also designed to accommodate a couple of guests, but they, unlike the previously considered category, are presented on a more spacious area - about 20 square meters. It also has a large French bed, a pair of nightstands and a wardrobe. These apartments also have a balcony, and some have an outdoor terrace where you can sunbathe. If you wish, you can move into such apartments with two children as a teenager - for them you can set up beds for free here.

"Suite"

Suite apartments offer vacationers superior comfort. They are represented on a fairly large territory - 42 square meters. m, which is also divided into two rooms, one of which is a living room, and the second - a relaxation area.

The bedroom has a large European-style bed, covered with expensive hypoallergenic linen and an orthopedic mattress. The living room has a furniture set, which consists of a large sofa and a pair of armchairs. Between them there is a low table made of shockproof glass. It also has a plasma TV, safe and minibar. Staying in the room, guests can use the telephone, which is connected to the international line.

Tourists note a very convenient location of this type of room: they are in one-story bungalows and have direct access to the garden, where you can admire the beauty of the surrounding nature.

Services for children

Often, tourists themselves represent in their reviews Ulusoy Kemer as a hotel, perfect for families with children. All this is due to the fact that on its territory a number of conditions are provided for comfortable living of this category of guests.

In particular, in the open area there is a large pool, which is made taking into account all safety requirements. If desired, children can visit a game club that operates at certain times, but many parents make the remark that if you wish, you can hire a nanny for your child, whose services are charged hourly.

In their comments, parents resting in Ulusoy Kemer with children note that in almost all of his rooms, the basic furniture is included in the bed, which is perfect for teens under 12 years old. Also, if you wish, you can rent a stroller here.

Pool

Fans of water procedures every day gather on the outdoor terrace near the pool, which is furnished with a large number of sun loungers and beach umbrellas. It is located in a very picturesque place, which is a green flowering garden. There is a bar nearby, where tourists have the opportunity to enjoy delicious cocktails and cold drinks, as well as a number of snacks to them.



The pool itself is located in the middle of the sun terrace, its area is just over 1000 square meters. m, and the water here is constantly clean, as its staff is always monitored by its condition. Throughout the day, it heats up under the influence of sunlight, since an automatic heating system is not installed in the niche. Slides are installed in the pool, the number of which is 4. You can visit the aquazone at any time from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m.



Sport

A large number of reviews left by the guests of Ulusa Kemer indicate that there is a beautiful gym in which there are a considerable number of units designed to work with different muscle groups. You can visit it only for a fee. In the open area, the hotel has several sports fields that are well equipped for playing volleyball, soccer, basketball, and in one of the buildings there is a hall where tables for playing table tennis are installed.

For a fee, guests can visit the billiards room, which is paid hourly. For a fee, you can also use the services of a windsurfing instructor and try to master this sport, which, as practice shows, many people like.

SPA

The spa center of the Ulusoy Kemer hotel deserves great attention among vacationers. Judging by the reviews, representatives of different age categories who love relaxation and tend to the ideal state of their health like to visit. A visit to the SPA center is paid, regardless of the type of service provided.

Guests enjoy the large massage room that is equipped here. Guests note that it has a light, unobtrusive interior, staying in which you can truly relax. There is a team of professional massage therapists who know a lot about relaxation.

Also in the SPA there is an excellent thermal zone, which is also said a lot of enthusiastic comments. Attention tourists are offered a sauna and a traditional hammam, where a team of parmeisters works. There is also a small pool with a jacuzzi.

Nutrition

In the reviews of tourists about the Ulusoy Kemer Hotel, it is constantly said that there are excellent catering establishments within its walls, serving amazing dishes prepared in the traditions of European cuisine. The Italian restaurant deserves special attention, where a buffet is held every day, and it is served three times a day. To visit it you need to make a preliminary reservation.

In addition to the restaurant, the Ulusoy Kemer Hotel (Turkey) has five bars, one of which is located directly in the restaurant, the other in the building, and the rest near the pool. Here guests are offered light snacks, salads, desserts and, of course, drinks of various kinds.

Tourists note that the catering facilities of this hotel offer diet menus in a separate menu, and there is also a children's list.



Beach

The coastline is less than 180 meters from its buildings and is adjacent to the territory of Ulusoy Kemer. Its description says that the beach is covered with fine and soft sand, which is favorable for families with small children, as tourists who have a rest with their families notice.

If desired, guests can sit here on comfortable sun loungers under umbrellas, as well as use inflatable mattresses. The beach area is quite well-maintained: there are several public toilets, locker rooms, as well as showers.



Prices

The cost of a tour to Ulusoy Kemer for Muscovites on average costs 65,000 rubles if tourists want to relax together for seven days. According to tour operators, this price already includes flights from one country to another, transportation of tourists from the airport to the hotel, as well as rental of a standard class room.

If necessary, the cost of a ticket can include additional services that are available in the Ulusoy Kemer service, but experienced tourists recommend ordering them on the spot, as in the hotel their cost is much cheaper than that of the operators.
