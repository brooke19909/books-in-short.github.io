Location

Many travel directories give information about the Sentido Numa Bay Hotel 5 * in Alanya. In fact, the hotel complex was built near the resort village of Avsallar. From the hotel to its center the distance does not exceed two kilometers. Vacationers say that in the village there are many shops and markets where various souvenirs are sold.

If you want to go on an excursion to Antalya, then think a few times - this city has a long way to go. The distance between the resort and the hotel complex exceeds one hundred kilometers. Pros and Cons of the Beach

The bay, on the shore of which the hotel complex is located, is very picturesque. From the windows of the rooms you can see a bewitching view of large boulders, calm waters rolling on fine sand, and ancient ruins inherited from the ancient inhabitants of these places.

Reviews of Sentido Numa Bay Hotel 5 * contain a lot of information about the beach area. It is the property of the hotel and is well equipped. However, the coast in this part of the bay is extremely narrow, so by noon, when most of the guests go down to the sea, it becomes very crowded. Many vacationers go to neighboring beaches and spend time there.

Despite the fact that the beach consists of sand, it is not suitable for small children. The fact is that entering the sea is replete with sharp stones. This makes bathing difficult for babies. Parents try to relax with them by the pool, where the kids often feel more comfortable.

Accommodation with children

Sentido Numa Bay 5 * (Turkey / Alanya) belongs to family hotels, therefore children of any age are always welcome here. If you live with an older child, you will need to pay extra for it in the amount of seventy euros per night. One room can accommodate two extra beds.
