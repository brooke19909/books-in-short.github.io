How is it different from other devices

There are many good or even great Android phones released during 2016. IPhone 7 surpasses each of them in speed, but not in battery life. Its size, performance and camera are unique features, along with the ability to use iOS, of course. The most important thing is that this smartphone is waterproof, that is, it can be immersed in water for a while without damage.

Compared with the previous model, the camera takes significantly better photos, especially in low light, and adds the function of optical image stabilization. The battery lasts a little longer, and sometimes it is very noticeable, especially compared to the iPhone 6S. The processor in the smartphone is definitely faster when compared with previous-generation iPhones.

He also has a screen with a "wide color gamut" with increased color accuracy and improved stereo speakers. According to user reviews, these improvements are not as important as those described above. In addition, the Home button is no longer clickable - it has the same pressure sensitivity and vibration feedback as the touch screen. The key works fine, but it takes some getting used to, because when it is pressed there is no mechanical click.

New A10 Fusion Processor

This is the biggest advancement in technical parameters. Shortly before the release of iPhone 7, Apple announced that the A10 Fusion chip has four cores: two high-performance for the most intensive tasks and two low-energy to perform simpler functions while saving energy. According to user reviews, when using the device, its speed is noticeable from the first clicks.

Applications start up quickly, updates are installed quickly, and the camera is ready to shoot right after switching on. According to many users, any performance differences are not noticeable in resource-intensive (such as Pixelmator) and lightweight applications (such as Mail).

The best screen, memory and speakers

Many have been wondering for a long time when iPhone 7 will be released. The exact release date of the device for sale was so interesting to the mass consumer for various reasons, including because the developers promised to introduce an improved screen of a new generation.

When the smartphone became available, numerous reviews confirmed that the update was indeed present. The screen has become brighter, which makes it easier to read in bright sunlight. It also supports a wider color gamut, making photos and videos look rich, vibrant.

Sound features

When the iPhone 7 came out, Apple developers removed the analog headphone port and added a second speaker for stereo sound. It is located next to the FaceTime camera, and the sound improvement is noticeable immediately.

Inner memory

Another nice addition is doubling the size of storage. The entry-level iPhone 7 now has 32 GB instead of 16. Other models offer 128 and 256 GB. This is very important if you always have to manage the available storage by deleting photos and videos. For comparison: IPhone SE has a maximum capacity of 64 GB, and iPhone 6s - 128 GB, so if you need a large amount of memory, model 7 is what you need.

Camera

The iPhone 7 has one 12-megapixel iSight camera, but its performance is significantly improved compared to the 6s. The lens has a wider aperture - f 1.8, which gives more light for better photos in low light.

The iPhone 7 also has optical image stabilization, which was previously limited to the larger Plus models. The TrueTone flash is also 50% brighter thanks to four LEDs. Apple said it could even compensate for the faint flickering of interior lighting.

On the front, the FaceTime camera has grown from 5 megapixels (as it was in the iPhone 6s) to 7 megapixels, although it retains the same f 2.2 aperture. Now it can record 1080p video, and its performance in low light is also improved. In general, it works more like a rear view camera, so your selfies will always look good.

Waterproof smartphone

When the iPhone 7 came out, its declared water resistance immediately began to be discussed. This is not surprising: an accidental fall of the device into the water can occur in everyone. However, Apple support recommends that you do not wet the iPhone specifically, and if you do, unplug all the cables and make sure it is completely dry before trying to charge it again.
