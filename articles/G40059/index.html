Yuzhnouralsk hotels: list, rating of the best, addresses, room reservation, services, pricing, visitor reviews and tourist tips

Many people who come to a foreign city for work or for tourist purposes have not used rented apartments for a long time. The most popular is the removal of rooms in the hotel. Yuzhnouralsk hotels in the Chelyabinsk region are not inferior to European standards: in this city it is possible to find both an economical option and a luxurious business class. Everything is done for the comfort of customers: reasonable prices, any payment method, convenient parking and much more.

When choosing a temporary accommodation near Balandino Airport, you can also find a suitable hotel. Below is a short list of the most popular hotels in Yuzhnouralsk with high rating and low prices. The institutions here are diverse, so even the most fastidious visitors will like it. Please note that hotel rates may vary depending on the room options selected.

The list and rating of the best hotels in Yuzhnouralsk is presented below. You just need to familiarize yourself with it and choose the one that best meets the requirements and financial capabilities.

Hotel "Persona", the leader in the ranking

It is 1.1 km from the center (no more than 12 minutes walk). "Persona" is located near the central square, which is why visitors who have settled have a beautiful view from the window. Connecting to a Wi-Fi point is completely free. There is air conditioning and a public lounge. A central library is located next to the hotel. Frequent guests speak of the hotel as a clean, quiet establishment, with polite staff and high quality service.

The rooms are heated and are offered in two types: non-smoking and family. Room service is also available for breakfast and additional products. Cleaning is scheduled daily.

Prices - from 2 295 rubles per day. Transfer from the airport must be paid separately. Has a rating of 9.6 - the best indicator among hotels in Yuzhnouralsk, according to guests.

Hotel Apartment on ulitsa Sergeya Bulando: second place in the rating

Located 1.8 km from the center, a walk takes about 15 minutes. The hotel is located in a region with a picturesque view of the city of Yuzhnouralsk. It offers a varied cuisine, as well as free Wi-Fi. Free use of a washing machine, cable TV with a large selection of TV channels. The room has a kitchen, an ergonomic workplace and a sofa bed. Pets are allowed. Cleaning is done every day, there is a dry cleaning service and laundry. Separate ironing service. Near the hotel is a restaurant.

Please note that smoking at the hotel is strictly prohibited and subject to a fine.

Room price - from 1 312 rubles per day. Separate payment for transfer from and to the airport. Visitors rated the hotel a rating of 9.5.

Comfortable rooms at the Versailles Hotel

It is located 900 m from the center, about 10 minutes walk. Visitors to the Versailles Hotel in Yuzhnouralsk can spend their free time in the bar of their own room directly in the bathroom. There is a TV with many satellite channels. The on-site restaurant serves Russian traditional cuisine and includes a children's menu. There is a service for ordering breakfast directly in your room. Frequent visitors praise the rooms and service. Accommodation in VIP rooms is possible, each with soundproofing. Of the other types of rooms there is a family suite. Pets are allowed under the responsibility of the owner.

It also has a pool, laundry facilities and air conditioning. Parking is available. There is a mini market and a souvenir shop on site. Room price - from 1 967 rubles per day. User rating - 9.2.

Hotel Vivaldi

Located 2.5 km from the city center, walking time - 20 minutes. Among the main range of services are free Internet, a 24-hour reception and a restaurant, combined with a bar. As entertainment, billiards are offered. There is an air conditioning system. For businessmen who visited the city at work, conference rooms and offices for organizing banquets are provided, equipped with printers and faxes. The rooms are heated and are cleaned daily. Laundry service is available at the property. There is air conditioning.

The price of a hotel room in Yuzhnouralsk is from 4 459 rubles per day. Parking is private, located on the territory of the hotel, free for guests. The rating reaches 8.8.

Hotel Olympus

The distance of 2.5 km from the center, the road will have to spend about 20 minutes. Less than a kilometer from the hotel is the Church of the Intercession of the Holy Virgin. For tourists there is a cozy terrace, a restaurant. Reception is open 24 hours a day. Internet connection is free, everyone can freely use the laundry room or spend time with children in a specially designated area. The hotel staff is praised for responsibility and integrity, a friendly attitude towards the animals with which visitors wish to be accommodated.

There are several types of rooms: non-smoking, honeymoon, family, soundproofed. Rooms are cleaned every day.

Price - from 2 295 rubles per day. The rating reached 8.5.

Mini-hotel Zhemchuzhina

The distance from the center is 2.7 km (about 18 minutes on foot). Mini-Hotel Zhemchuzhina is equipped with a cozy terrace, a bathroom with a hydromassage and an open-air pool. Internet connection is free, as well as parking spaces on site. "Pearl" - a budget option for temporary housing. Nevertheless, the hotel is in great demand due to the high quality of service. The recommendations of visitors are mostly positive.

There is a restaurant with a wide range of dishes, breakfast can be ordered directly to your room. There is a special playground for children. There is a number of wellness services: hydromassage, jacuzzi, sauna. Designated smoking areas. Room price is from 1 518 rubles per day. Rating from visitors - 8.2.

Hotel Grace Yuzhnouralsk

The distance of 980 meters from the center is about 10 minutes on foot. As services, we offer everything you need for a barbecue, wireless Internet. Reception is open 24 hours a day. There is a restaurant with both an adult and a children's menu. Delivery of products is possible. On the territory there is a playground for children, cheap parking and laundry. The air conditioning system works.

Visitors can check their luggage in special storage departments. There are places for smokers. Settlement is possible with small pets. A conference room is reserved for businessmen.

Prices from 721 rubles per day. Transfer payment separately. The rating of visitors reaches 8.2.
