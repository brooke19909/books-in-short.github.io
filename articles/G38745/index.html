Hotel NaiYang Beach Resort Phuket (Thailand): photos, descriptions, reviews of tourists

Many lovers of Thailand know the island of Phuket as a place of fun and noisy parties. Patong city, resort places Karon and Kata are very popular among Russian tourists. But the northwest coast of Phuket is less known. However, there is a unique place called Nai Yang. This beach is interesting because casuarins grow there directly on the sand. They look like conifers, only their needles are soft as leaves. Thus, the beach is perfectly shaded.

Nai Yang Bay is more suitable for a relaxing, relaxing holiday. There are wonderful sunsets and beautiful nature. But this is not the backyard of civilization. There are several 4-5 star hotels in the bay. In this article we will talk about one of them - Nai Yang Beach Resort 4 *. Reviews and photos of tourists served as an informative base for our article.

Location

Nai Young Beach Resort is just a kilometer from Phuket International Airport. But the planes taking off and landing did not interfere with rest. The airport does not accept flights at night. Very close to the hotel we are describing is the vast Sirinat Park - a fabulous place, judging by the reviews. Tourism infrastructure on the Nai Yang coast is well developed. There is a pharmacy, several shops, fruit and fish markets, cafes and restaurants. True, all this trading life is concentrated near the Phuket Marriott Resort & Spa Naiyang Beach 5 * hotel.

Chic "five" is located on the first line. It can not be called the closest neighbor of our hotel, because between it and the Marriott is still a small hotel L'Esprit de Naiyang Beach Resort. To the north, towards the airport, there are more hotels. But they are separated from Nai Young Beach Resort by Sirinat Park. To Patong and Phuket Town from the hotel - about 40 kilometers. Thus, tourists claim that this resort is suitable for those who do not suffer from noisy parties and discos. But this place cannot be called wild either.

Naiyang Beach Resort Phuket: hotel description

The hotel was built in 1997. But do not be afraid of dilapidated buildings and rooms. The last renovation was carried out in 2015. Those tourists who have visited the hotel more than once (and there are quite a few) say that in the low season something is always under construction or repair, and a new building has recently been erected. The territory of the hotel is quite significant - 24 thousand square meters. It stretched out into a narrow strip to the road, beyond which Nai Yang Beach immediately stretched.

The hotel has several buildings. In the middle stretched in a row single-storey bungalows connected among themselves, classified as oriental wing and tropical wing. On one side of this row of cottages are two three-story buildings. Between them are three pools. On the other hand, two-story buildings and a building on 4 levels extend from a number of bungalows. The whole territory is very well-groomed, landscaped. From the Sirinat park, chipmunks, lizards, geckos run into the hotel garden, you can see turtles.

Beach, sea, pools

Nai Yang is little known among vacationers in Phuket. Tourists from Patong go to the popular Kathu and Karon, and almost no one gets to this beach, which is located on the northwest coast of the island. Therefore, only residents of local hotels have a rest here. Only Marriott's representative office has its own dedicated area with sunbeds and umbrellas. Since the NaiYang Beach Resort Phuket is located on the second line (across the road), its guests must pay for beach equipment. It costs 200 rubles a day. But many tourists report that they were perfectly arranged on a hotel beach towel on a soft white sand in the shade of casuarina.

Low tides here are poorly identified. Sunset at sea is very convenient for bathing young children. The beach is protected by a rugged coast from winds and storms. There is no biting plankton in the sea, but the water is not completely transparent. You can see the fish only at full tide. The hotel has three swimming pools. The water in them is fresh and clean. The pools have a sun terrace, with free sunbeds and umbrellas. According to tourists, there is almost always deserted.

Hotel services and infrastructure

The administration of NaiYang Beach Resort Phuket is doing everything possible to make people with disabilities (including wheelchairs) feel comfortable here. Tourists with small children will be cradled in the room at no extra charge, while the restaurant has a special menu and high chairs for feeding babies. In one of the pools there is a department with shallow water. But the playground, as well as the mini-club, is not in the hotel.

In general, the hotel positions itself as a place of quiet relaxation, and guests are invited to entertain themselves. But for this, a suitable infrastructure has been created. There is a gym, a tennis table with rackets and balls, for a fee you can play billiards. Like many hotels in Thailand, Nai Yang Beach Resort has a small spa with various types of massage. Parking at the hotel is free for guests. At the 24-hour reception, you can order tickets and excursions, call a taxi, send a fax or print documents, change currency, rent a scooter or bicycle, put things in a locker. There is a shop and a beauty salon on site.

Excursions from Nai Yang Beach Resort Phuket 4 * (Thailand)

Phuket is the largest island in the country, and it is full of attractions. Tourists recommend to travel independently in the evening to the city of Patong.
