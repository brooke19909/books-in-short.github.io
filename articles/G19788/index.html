One of the places to do this is the Kant sports complex and the Nagornaya school of young skiers operating on its basis.

Kant: contact information

The sports complex is located in the Southern Autonomous Okrug of Moscow, in the immediate vicinity of the Nagornaya metro station at the address: Electrolyte passage, house 7, building 2 . For those interested in how to get to the Kant ski resort on Nagornaya, the traffic pattern is simple:

  • get out of the last metro car following from the center at the Nagornaya station;
  • walk 500-600 m on Electrolyte passage, guided by signboards.




The complex operates daily, from 10.00 to 22.00.

Education in the sports school "Nagornaya"

The Nagornaya ski school is considered the largest in the capital: about 80 experienced trainers and instructors work here, it has 9 training tracks at its disposal with a gradual increase in the difficulty of the descent. A positive point is that these routes are available only to students of a sports school. A convenient advantage is the moment that the cost of training includes the cost of a ski pass - a ski pass.

In addition, the Nagornaya sports school has 20 years of experience transforming its students into experienced snowboarders and skiers. You can train here both individually - one on one with the trainer, and in groups - small (up to 5 people) and large. Class schedule is free - in "Kant" it is possible to choose a convenient training time for yourself.





The training in the Nagornaya ski resort is phased: for the first time skiing or snowboarding, introductory classes are provided on the basic skills of skiing, then the students will go through the confident skiing stage, and, in the end, the real sports. In addition, school graduates and all comers can take part in alpine skiing sports competitions.

You can become a young skier here from the age of three, and a snowboarder from six. There are no age thresholds for the adult category of students.

Ski simulator

For those visitors who want to learn the skills necessary for a skier during off-season time, the ski complex on Nagornaya offers a SkyTec simulator. You can use it daily from 7.00 to 23.00, going to the building of the sports club "SkyTehcSport - Kant" (in the building where the indoor tennis courts are located).





These snowboard and ski simulators surprisingly accurately reproduce all the accelerations, obstacles that arise when descending from a snowy slope. The biomechanics and physics of such a peculiar "skiing" on the simulator exactly corresponds to the loads obtained during real skiing or boarding. This is achieved due to the fact that the simulators were designed based on the work of the tandem of scientists - mathematicians, physicists, engineers. Each device is equipped with meticulously verified mechanics, a large-scale sensor system, powerful power drives, and modern computer control.

Events based on "Kant"

The ski complex on Nagornaya is also a center of sports competitions, holidays and entertainment. On its basis are held:

  1. Children's holidays - birthdays, school graduations, etc. They include interactive programs with animators, quests, sports and master classes, intellectual games, a festive feast from the chef.
  2. "Kids Club" is a sports and educational alternative to kindergarten.
  3. Sports city summer camp "While mom is at work."
  4. Organization of sports trips for children to the Rosa Khutor ski resort.
  5. More than 30 competitions of various levels in a wide range of sports, in which anyone can take part with the appropriate training. The participants are judged by professional judges, and the winners receive not only medals and cups, but also prizes from sports brands.




The ski complex on Nagornaya (Kant) is known as a fully-fledged modern ski center with its own sports school and a year-round training system for future snowboarders and skiers. In addition to this, it is a multifunctional structure providing active recreation for fans of various sports of all ages.
