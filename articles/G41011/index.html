Bosch BGS 62530: overview

The BGS 62530 model was developed with an emphasis on the most convenient use. It is produced under the famous Bosch brand, which is known throughout the world. In this device, criteria such as reliability and practicality are in the first place. An important detail is the design. Many original solutions are used in appearance. For example, broken lines, a combination of smoothness and straightforwardness, a combination of colors - all this makes the device unique. But many buyers, not understanding the technical characteristics, consider appearance as the main criterion. And in this practically there are no competitors for the Bosch BGS 62530 vacuum cleaner. Its price starts from 16,000 rubles. For this money, the buyer gets a powerful and functional device.

For the manufacture of the body used high-quality plastic, which is characterized by a high level of strength. There is no unpleasant odor even during prolonged use. For good mobility, the vacuum cleaner is equipped with rubber wheels. They move perfectly on any type of coating, leaving no traces or mechanical damage. The device does not differ in large dimensions, therefore, for its storage, you can find a place without much difficulty and inconvenience.



Technical equipment

Specifications are an important criterion for choosing this kind of technology. Model BGS 62530 is a powerful vacuum cleaner with which you can clean any kind of surface. He got such a reputation for a reason. During operation, dust and dirt are absorbed with a power of 550 watts. This indicator is above average. When turned on, it consumes 2500 watts. Designed only for dry cleaning. The capacity of the dust bag is 3 liters. Dust is collected in a plastic container that is equipped with the latest cyclonic system. Thanks to her, after cleaning, the air in the room remains clean. You can set the power manually using the regulator, which is located on the case. Equipped with a SensorBagless system that monitors the productivity of the device. The device works from a 220 V network. A long power cord (9 m) allows you to increase the cleaning radius to 11 meters. The kit includes a telescopic tube and four nozzles. The weight of the vacuum cleaner is 8.5 kg. The device is additionally equipped with a storage space for nozzles, an automatic cable winding system, a foot switch, vertical parking, and a fine filter.



Work Highlights

Before turning on the Bosch BGS 62530 vacuum cleaner, it is necessary to insert the hose pipe into a special hole on the body. If done correctly, a characteristic click will sound. After that, you need to connect the handle to the telescopic tube. Reliable fixation provides an unlocking clutch. You can adjust the height by clicking on a special slider, the arrows shown on it show the direction. The nozzle is mounted on the free end of the tube. It does not have additional fasteners, so you need to insert it until it locks firmly.

After assembly, you can turn on the device. To do this, the network cable of the required length is unwound, the plug is inserted into the outlet. On / off button located on the body. By pressing it, the device turns on or off.

Power is regulated by a switch. It rotates smoothly in the direction of the arrow. The device is equipped with three power levels:

  • The first is designed to clean delicate fabrics (curtains).
  • The second is used for light pollution.
  • The third is for flooring, durable fabrics and heavy soiling.

After completing work, unplug the appliance from the outlet. Pull a little on the network cable and it will automatically rewind.



Nozzles

The Bosch BGS 62530 vacuum cleaner is equipped with four different nozzles. They are designed for different types of cleaning.

  • Floor / carpet. On the nozzle is a switch with which to adjust the length of the pile.
  • Cushioned furniture. It can be used to clean fabric surfaces, for example, curtains, upholstery.
  • Crevice. Ideal for cleaning bottlenecks, stairs and corners.
  • Parquet flooring . It is used for cleaning hard surfaces, such as ceramic tiles, parquet and others.

disadvantages

Service maintenance

A warranty card is included with each vacuum cleaner, and the manual contains a complete list of service centers. Do not forget to check the correctness of filling out the documentation at the time of purchase, since even though this model of vacuum cleaners rarely breaks, there are still such cases. Free warranty repair is carried out within one year. During this time, service is provided. If the buyer finds a breakdown, then you must contact the store. After that, the device is commissioned; the decision is usually made within one month. If a factory defect is detected, the vacuum cleaner will be replaced with a new one without any problems. However, if the buyer violates safety precautions and working conditions, as well as the tightness of the case, free maintenance will be denied.
