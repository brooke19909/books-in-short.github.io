Where is it warm in April? Where the sea is warmer in April

After an unpleasant long Russian winter, I want to finally forget about the vagaries of my native nature and soak up the sun. When spring slush is observed on the streets of Russian cities, many decide to go to the warm, gentle sea. April is a kind of bridge between winter, during which holidays in ski resorts and in various exotic countries are very popular , and in summer, when the time comes for vacations, and therefore travel. Thus, the second month of spring is an ideal time for relaxation: in most resorts there is still no influx of tourists, and the cost of tours is lower. So, it is decided, we are going on vacation! It remains only to determine the place of rest, and for this it is necessary to know where it is warm in April. We will talk about resorts that are ready to please you this spring month with the gentle sun and warm sea, in the article.





Carefree Egypt

When planning a vacation with children, we thought about where the sea is warmer in April? Stop thinking, rather buy a tour to Egypt - the most proven and cheapest traditional resort. In the Arab state, spread out on the shores of the Red Sea, this month the weather is set almost ideal for a beach holiday. Hotels in Sharm El Sheikh and Hurghada are not yet crowded, so you can feel as relaxed as possible. For spending time with children, this country with a developed infrastructure is an ideal place. In April, the wind season just ends here, comfortable weather sets in - the average air temperature is 27 degrees. The water is still not very warmed up (on average up to 23 degrees), but it is already quite suitable for swimming.





Hot thailand

This is a place where it is warm in early April, late April, September, January and other months, in general, always! However, during our spring in Thailand, it‚Äôs not just warm weather, real heat is established here - the air warms up to 35-40 degrees. If you are not afraid of high temperatures and humidity, then the "land of smiles" will become your paradise. Beach vacations here during this period still remain popular, but the intensity of tourists is already lower than in the winter months. Phuket, Samui, Pattaya, Koh Chang - you can not distinguish a resort where the sea is warmer in April. Wherever you go, great beaches and gentle waters of the Gulf of Thailand await you everywhere .





Cozy Tunisia

One cannot but mention this place when talking about warm countries. In April, a vacation in Tunisia is almost perfect, both in terms of weather conditions and in terms of economy. The Mediterranean coastal waters in the middle of spring, perhaps, are still not warmed up enough for swimming - usually the temperature of the water does not exceed 16-17 degrees, but the air temperature rises to 24-25 degrees, which makes it comfortable for people who can not tolerate the heat, as well as who wants to get acquainted with the sights of the country. At such resorts as Monastir, Sousse, Djerba, in April it is already quite possible to sunbathe, and perhaps even get a swim. At the same time, you are guaranteed the attention and helpfulness of hotel and bar staff - local residents have not yet had time to get tired of the influx of tourists.





Mysterious Morocco

This is another North African state where it is warm in April. Having bought a tour to Morocco, you will get an amazing opportunity to spend a vacation on the Atlantic coast. The air temperature and the bright sun already allow you to enjoy a tan, but the water in the sea is, of course, not akin to summer (up to 18-20 degrees). The most important resort in the country - Agadir - has good hotels and the longest beach in Morocco. It is best to come here in the twentieth of the month: at this time in Morocco, oranges and other fruit trees are blooming, and, of course, it's worth a look.



Paradise Canaries

These islands, located relatively close to the colorful Morocco, are also better to visit by the end of the month. The most famous resort of the Tenerife archipelago is a place where heat is already firmly installed in late April (the air warms up to an average of 22 degrees). Here, as elsewhere in the Mediterranean region, the tourist season is just starting at this time, so there are relatively few vacationers. In general, the Canaries can be described as islands of eternal spring: in autumn, spring, and summer, because of the peculiarities of ocean currents, it is cooler here than in similar latitudes of other regions. Nevertheless, some tourists already swim in the ocean in April.





Amazing Turkey

In the middle of spring, rest on the Turkish coast is not as popular as in summer - after all, the sea does not warm up as fast as we would like. Nevertheless, many tourists, attracted by a calm environment, excellent service and low prices, choose this time for a trip to the country. After all, how pleasant it is to wander through the historical places of Asia Minor without the inherent summer fuss . Amazing Turkey in April! Where it is warmer in the spring in this Mediterranean region, so it is in Alanya and Side - these are leaders in the rate of warming. Resorts such as Antalya, Fenike, Kemer, Fethiye are also comfortable in terms of temperature in mid-spring. During the day the air warms up to 23 degrees, at night - up to 13. The water temperature reaches 17-20 degrees.





Luxury Emirates

Those who are attracted by the Persian waters more than the Atlantic ones will surely enjoy a vacation in the UAE. This is a country where it is warm in April and the sun shines all days. The tourist season is still in full swing, but a large number of diverse hotels and beautiful beaches will satisfy everyone's needs. Of course, most often, deciding to visit the Emirates, travelers choose Dubai as the vacation destination - the country's main resort and the largest economic center. In April, the weather is comfortable for swimming and for visiting excursions - the air warms up to 26-30 degrees. It will be interesting to climb the tallest building in the world - the Burj Khalifa tower, indulge in a desert jeep safari, have fun in local water parks. Do you like shopping? And here duty free Dubai is at your service.





Other leisure options

Of course, in the article we did not list all the places where it is warm in April, but only the most popular from the standpoint of tourist attendance. Among the more exotic destinations are Cuba, Maldives, Dominican Republic, Mexico, Sri Lanka. Whichever of these countries you go to, you can everywhere not only sunbathe and swim in the gentle sea, but also visit numerous sights, which will allow you to join the culture of these countries. Are you attracted to Asia? Then do not forget that, besides Thailand, there is Vietnam, not yet popular with crowds of tourists, with its amazing beaches, as well as two islands that enjoy steady popularity among holidaymakers - Indonesian Bali and Chinese Hainan. Have a nice April holiday!
