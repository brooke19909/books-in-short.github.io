The appearance of the peony

To make a large and lush flower, you need to cut a huge number of petals, all of which are of different shapes. In a real flower, when the bud opens, the petal bends in the opposite direction and shallow tears appear on the outer edge.

We draw a pattern of petals

Petals for peonies from corrugated paper with sweets (see the photo below in the article) are painted first on cardboard, making a template. Then fold the paper several times and outline the template with a simple pencil along the contours. Then cut out different parts, several of the same.

Making the middle

First, we‚Äôll cut out the core, that is, stamens with yellow paper pestles. A strip of 20 cm long and 6.5 cm wide is cut and its lower edge is cut with "noodles".

For cutting, it is recommended to fold the strip in several layers to make fewer incisions. Then rotate the workpiece around a flat part. "Noodles" will look after such a procedure in different directions, which will add naturalness to the craft. Then the workpiece is glued to the bud with candy.

Flower assembly

In a real peony, the flower has a rounded shape, so the petals also need to be slightly twisted. This is easy to do by stretching each part along the side surface of a simple pencil of cylindrical shape. Additionally, you can apply the carved edge of the petal by pressing the same pencil or do it by hand.

With a hot glue, a drop is placed on the even edge of the element and the prepared petal is immediately placed on the twisted lower part of the bud. Each next part is attached with a slight shift to the side. Gluing is carried out until the flower acquires the required size.

The closer to the outer edge, the larger the petals you need to choose. At the end, the stem is formed. Cut a strip of 1 cm wide from green paper. The length depends on the height of the flower. Attach the edge from the bottom of the wire with hot-melt adhesive, and then spiral-wrap the entire rod to the top. At the end, reinforce the edge in the same way as the bottom. When the stem is decorated, stick the leaflets of elongated shape, placing them in a circle, as in the photo above in the article. Everything, the flower is ready!
