Hotel Orlov 2 (Italy, Rimini): traveler reviews

Hotel Orlov 2 is a two-star hotel in Rimini, a city that has earned fame as one of Italy 's best beach resorts. The hotel has 31 rooms. Despite the name, the hotel does not speak Russian. Use only English and Italian.

Advantages of the hotel:

  1. Located in the center of Rimini.
  2. The beach from Hotel Orlov 2 (Italy, Adriatic Riviera, Rimini) is only 100 meters, that is, five minutes walk, it is easy to walk even with young children.
  3. The hotel has a traditional restaurant with homemade dishes (typical Italian cuisine).
  4. Breakfast is buffet style.
  5. Discounts at some beaches and theme parks nearby.
  6. Nearby are all the major transport interchanges: the airport and the railway station.




2 Star Hotels Rimini

It makes sense to compare it with others in order to understand what exactly is Hotel Orlov 2 (Italy, Rimini). In this city, you can choose from almost six hundred hotels. Of these, 69 are two-star. Unfortunately, Hotel Orlov 2 stands out for its leading position ... from the end. Of the rated hotels, it has the lowest average rating. It also does not apply to the cheapest two-star hotels.

The only thing that distinguishes it in a positive sense is that it is really close to the center. Although there are still hotels that are even closer, such as Hotel Eriale or Villa Mirna, the overall rating is very high. The most successful competitors of the Orlov Hotel are: Residence Maryel, Hotel Mary Fleur, Green Residence, Hotel Eriale, Hotel Greta, Hotel Holiday Beach, Albergo Colonna, Hotel Lora, Hotel Eriale. Each of these two-star hotels has a lot of rave reviews and very high ratings.

It is about Hotel Orlov 2 that the reviews are extremely mixed: from average ratings to enthusiastic and sharply negative. The average score is 3 or 4 on a five-point scale. On some resources, the average rating is 2 points out of five.

The rooms

The rooms are not as cramped as some too picky travelers seem, albeit small. In addition, many reviews mention that the rooms are cozy and well equipped with furniture, for example: a spacious double bed, bedside tables, huge wardrobes with mirrors, tables and chairs, a TV and even a safe. Balcony - 3 meters, it offers a beautiful view of San Marino, Mount Monte Titano and the Church of St. Mary.

As for the quality of the odds of sleeping. It is not so rarely mentioned that mattresses are hard and uncomfortable. One review said that one bed had a good mattress, and the other one was uncomfortable.

Noise

The nearby railway really makes a noise. For noise-sensitive natures, this can be a serious drawback, but some of the reviews that wrote about Hotel Orlov 2 (Rimini) reported that they were so tired of walking and impressions for the day that they did not pay any attention to the noise, even ear plugs were not needed.

The hotel has a bar where you can enjoy coffee with something sweet even in the early morning. For breakfast, in Hotel Orlov 2 (Italy), in addition to coffee, cheese, sausage, sweets, jams, butter, toasters are served. Plates replenish on time. That is, no servings and no restrictions. What to say about dinner? The owner (Emilio) personally finds out the wishes of the guests about dinner. The cuisine is typical Italian. The lack of soups on the menu and the abundance of pasta, fish, meat, lasagna, caprese - this is a feature not only of the hotel itself, but of the country itself. If someone has individual wishes and needs to be prepared separately, for example, for a child, the hotel management will meet you without question.

The portions are huge. It would seem that this depends solely on the authors' appetites, and such an assessment is subjective. However, regardless of who wrote the review - man or woman, what age, marital status, all unanimously noted that the portions are really huge.

Dinner at Hotel Orlov 2 is not taken by all tourists, because numerous cafes are within easy reach, however, it should be borne in mind that the hotel is cheaper: here dinner will cost 10 euros, while in any of the street cafes - 15-20 euros.

They cook deliciously. But in assessing the taste of dishes, opinions diverged, although this is expected. What could be more subjective than taste? Some argue that the cook is a genius and created real masterpieces, others say that the food was very tasty, but at the same time they ate at the nearest cafes, and the restaurant visited the hotel only occasionally, while others said that the kitchen was very amateur and preferred Lunch and dinner exclusively in cafes and restaurants near Hotel Orlov 2 (Rimini).

Reviews of some tourists are even sharper. Some say that the food in the hotel restaurant was not always fresh, sour, poorly prepared, such as burnt potatoes, or unheated - even the most passionate pasta fans are unlikely to appreciate them cold.

conclusions

Many experienced tourists directly write that this hotel is not for those who are used to passive beach holidays in Egypt or Tunisia and sometimes want to lie comfortably in a spacious room. Such lovers will be annoyed by the noise of passing trains, the crowded rooms and the typically Italian cuisine presented here is satisfying, but monotonous. This hotel is a convenient inexpensive place for very active tourists who are inclined to explore the surrounding area up and down and do not sit for a minute, spending time on walks, excursions, and come to the hotel only then to have a quick meal, to exchange a few words with a kind master and fall asleep. Many tourists rightly noticed that for such a low price (1700 rubles per day) you simply don‚Äôt notice any inconvenience.

What is noteworthy, the hotel received the highest ratings from experienced tourists who have seen many hotels of different degrees of stardom in their lifetime. They claim that the hotel is very good for their stars, some even said that the hotel is not inferior to other four-star hotels. However, on the other hand, if you evaluate all two-star hotels in Rimini, the Orlov hotel does not look the best way against their background: there are many similar two-star hotels near the center of Rimini with much higher ratings. Therefore, it depends only on tourists which hotel they choose.
