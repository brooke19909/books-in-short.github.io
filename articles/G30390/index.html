Short Bank Summary

Probusinessbank was founded in early 1993. Since its inception, this lending institution has embarked on lending to small and medium-sized businesses. Deprivation of the license: what are the reasons

Despite the high ratings and great popularity among business representatives, the bank still lost its license. It happened in early August 2015. The main reason for this decision by the regulator was a systematic violation by Probusinessbank of federal law. And of course, the financial institution could not do without conducting a high-risk credit policy with the subsequent investment of money in low-quality assets. In a word, "finita la comedy." And so the activity of the center player of the credit policy of the Life group, better known as Probusinessbank, ended.
