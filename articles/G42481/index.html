Guest house "Olympus", Vityazevo: photos, description, reviews

In the village of Vityazevo, in its quietest and most comfortable area, Olimp is located , a guest house. Vityazevo is located on the shore of a picturesque estuary, so guests of Olympus can not only relax in silence, away from the bustle of the city, but also breathe in the fresh sea air and enjoy the beauty of the local nature.





Description

Olimp Guesthouse is a three-story building that includes 15 rooms. Although it is located far from the noisy center of the resort village, very close to it are shops, pharmacies, tents with souvenirs. A market is a five-minute walk from the guest house, a promenade is seven minutes away, and any local entertainment can be reached in 5-15 minutes. Nearby there is a minibus stop.

"Olympus" is a guest house (Vityazevo), which on its territory has an equipped playground, a summer cafe. For lovers of cooking barbecue themselves, there is a barbecue in the courtyard. The guest house has its own kitchen, equipped with everything necessary for cooking. Those who like to walk on foot can reach the sea in 15-20 minutes. A free shuttle service to the sea is also organized twice a day for hotel guests. The private guest house has its own beautiful shady courtyard with a gazebo and tables for relaxing. Accommodation

The room fund of the Olimp Guest House includes 15 rooms:

  • In 3-bed rooms there are three beds, a plasma TV, air conditioning, necessary furniture, a combined bathroom (shower and toilet). The cost of such a room ranges from 300 to 750 rubles per person per day, depending on the season.
  • The 4-bed rooms have four beds, air conditioning, a plasma TV, furniture and also a combined bathroom, which includes a shower and toilet. In such a room, the price for one person per day is from 250 to 600 rubles.

It is possible to place additional space, it will cost from 150 to 300 rubles per person, depending on the season. If the guest house has children under three years of age who do not occupy a separate place, they will be charged 100 rubles of community fee per day.

Guest house "Olympus": negative impressions

Unfortunately, the Olimp Guest House (Vityazevo, Krasnoarmeyskaya) has left a bad impression about itself in some respects:

  • Some guests complain about the old plumbing in the rooms.
  • Also, many do not like the lack of teapots in the rooms. Water coolers are only on the first and third floors.
  • Almost every day there are interruptions in electricity and water.
  • Cleaning is only required.
  • Many guests are unhappy with the old furniture.
  • There was a precedent for settling guests who arrived in an unclean room.
