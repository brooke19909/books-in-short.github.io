Pullover for a boy - some recommendations for knitting

Knitting is a grateful occupation. With the help of threads and knitting needles, as well as zeal, you can create beautiful unique things for all family members - both large and small. For example, a pullover for a boy will become a useful and elegant thing if his mother or grandmother connects him.

Neckline by age

Children grow up, and the things that they wear also grow and change, because the clothes that suit a one-year-old baby are not needed by a three-year-old researcher. Moreover, it is not a matter of size at all. A pullover for a boy of a certain age must meet certain requirements.

The clothes of young children who absolutely do not like to be worn (especially things over their heads) need a wide neck. But after all, a pullover is a warm thing, so the neck and chest of the crumbs should be covered first. To make life easier for parents and not to torment the baby with uncomfortable clothes, the pullover for a little boy, and a girl, of course, needs to be knitted with a special clasp along the shoulder seam. The polo clasp for babies is unacceptable, since the button of the baby can be thrust into the mouth, and it can come off. Older children do not need clothes with a clasp on the shoulder seam. For them, a sweater-badlon will not be a problem.





Smart pullover for a child

Children's clothing should be smart. This applies not only to holiday sets, but also to casual panties. Children are the most positive creatures in the world, they are always happy about the world around them, and the kids need the same joyful clothes. But a pullover for a boy of 3 years old will be different from similar clothes for a schoolboy.

Pullovers with drawings depicting the heroes of your favorite cartoons and fairy tales in gentle, light shades are suitable for the baby. The older boy will probably prefer drawings with the symbols of a sports team or a computer game. Drawing picture for baby

Knitting a pullover for a boy with knitting needles will be much easier if you use the knitting scheme borrowed from the popular needlework magazine or the recommendations of experienced knitters. The picture can be anything, it all depends on the desire and skill of the needlewoman in working with colored threads and a complex pattern.

A drawing can be developed independently, if you put the picture you like on a grid scheme. It should be noted that for the drawing it is not necessary to take the markup from even squares, as on a school notebook in a box. Best of all, if the cell size is equal to the ratio: 1 part in height and 1.5 parts in width - it is more suitable for knitting. You can use special computer programs to help needlewomen process drawings in various ways and for a variety of needs.





How to work with a multi-colored pattern?

A knitted pullover for a boy will become a real gift for a child, if a drawing is made on it at the request of the baby, it will be convenient to put on and take off, the yarn will be soft and will not prick. In addition, the work should be as accurate and high-quality as possible. To make the pullover for the boy neat, you must not forget to make peroxides at the junction of the two colors, then there will be no gaps between the individual sections of the pattern, and the pattern will be neat. If the drawing needs broaches, then the thread cannot be either tightened or loosened strongly. In one case, the place with the pattern will be very tightened and smaller than the rest of the canvas, in the other case, long broaches will interfere with putting on and taking off the pullover, and the loops when worn and washed will come out over time in real holes.





Men's classic

A pullover for a boy, even the smallest one, can be connected, like a real men's sweater, with Norwegian braids and jacquard. Small "braids" and weaves, a pattern of "beads" or "rice" will harmoniously decorate a knitted pullover for a boy of any age. And if mom connects the classic men's pullover for dad and son in the same color scheme and the same pattern, then they will look great, causing the envy of everyone around.





Pattern for a pullover - it can not be easier

Those who love and know how to knit know that to work in joy, you must strictly follow the knitting pattern. This applies not only to the drawing, but also to the size of the thing. To use the developed description of a particular model, you should choose exactly the same threads that are indicated in the description, and exactly the same knitting needles. Then the pullover for the boy will be knitted exactly in size. If you want to choose the threads finer or thicker than specified in the specific description for a specific size, you should change the scheme by counting the loops according to the sample, or use the description for other sizes - more or less, respectively. You can knit a children's pullover with knitting needles for a boy or girl from 4 main parts - rectangular backs and front with cutouts for the neck and trapezoidal sleeves. Knitted details are sewn with threads in tone, and then the neck and straps are fastened under the clasp, if it is provided according to the scheme. For babies, models with a similar shoulder clasp are the most preferred option.





If the pullover for the boy will be associated with love, thought out for the child in all details, then the baby will wear a knitted item with pleasure.
