Crafts from the branches: the best ideas for eco-decora

You can transform the interior using the simplest and most affordable materials. Why not remember the labor lessons in elementary school and try to make interesting and unusual crafts from the branches? We bring to your attention the best ideas for inspiration.

Bouquets that are not faded



What to make of dry branches? The simplest answer to this question is composition in a vase. We select the branches of the desired length and, if necessary, cut off all unnecessary from them. Then we collect a beautiful "bouquet". Then the most interesting part begins - if you wish, you can paint the branches, it is more convenient to do this with paint in a spray can. If you want to make crafts from the branches for the New Year, you can depict snow and hoarfrost. There are several ways - you can simply spread the necessary areas with glue and sprinkle them with sugar, salt or polystyrene foam. A special "artificial snow" for decoration is also suitable. You can get real crystals on the branches by immersing them in a strong saline solution and leaving them to dry completely. The composition will look interesting and if you add it with some thematic decor. It can be artificial flowers, natural or decorative fruits, bird figures, pendants in the form of hearts or stars, bows or other elements. Add autumn crafts from branches in the form of bouquets with bright leaves, rowan berries and hawthorn berries.

Eco Frames



Photo frames decorated with natural branches look incredibly beautiful and interesting. The base of the desired size can be cut out of cardboard or sawn out of plywood. A regular purchase frame with a smooth baguette is also suitable. Arrange the branches in neat rows and cut the ends or create a creative mess by lapping them. You can fix using any universal glue. If desired, the finished frame can be varnished or decorated with some decorative elements. According to this principle, crafts from branches with their own hands can be done to design a whole wall or shelf. A group of similar frames with a variety of photographs or reproductions of paintings will enliven a boring corner and make it more comfortable. You can also place a mirror in the frame of twigs.

Decorative panels

You can use dry branches to create paintings or volumetric compositions in frames. This is a great solution if no vases were found for the bouquet of twigs. The panel may be more complex, lay out the landscape, animal silhouettes, or try to collect flowers from small pieces. As a background, you can use fabric or beautiful paper. Dry seeds and fruits of various trees, birch bark, and moss will look great in a panel of natural materials . Crafts from branches of this type can be done without a background-base. Make individual figurines of twigs and hang them on a frame.

Crafts from branches for the interior: vases, candlesticks and much more



Have you accumulated several jars of the same size or found an old vase that asks for an update? It is not difficult to make original candle holders or containers for flowers. Option one - just glue the existing base with even branches, placing them close to each other. If such vases and candlesticks seem too boring to you, try randomly sticking several branches to the container, and then tie them around with decorative braid or rope. Candlesticks look especially impressive in this design; when a candle is lit, the light will gently break through the gaps between the rods. You can make similar crafts from leaves and branches, but keep in mind that in this case your decor will fade in a few days. This idea is great for a festive interior decoration. If the branches are fresh, cut in spring or summer, the vase decorated with them can be put on a pallet with water, and then the composition will retain its fresh appearance a little longer. Autumn branches with berries or bright leaves also look great. From dry rods, it is not difficult to assemble supports for hot. To do this, take as a basis a small piece of plywood or a cork of a square shape and glue branches on it, cutting them if necessary.

Large-scale ideas and unusual solutions



In home creativity and decor, it is appropriate to use not only small twigs and twigs. If there is an opportunity and desire, try to transform large driftwood, elements of trunks. Having fixed several large branches or young trees vertically on the floor, you can divide the room into two zones. Large knots, neatly sawn and varnished, will be an excellent hanger for clothes or jewelry. From curved branches, you can also make a curtain rod for curtains or decorative decoration that decorates the doorway. Attention: it is better to do large crafts from branches with your own hands, observing all the rules of wood processing. And this means that previously the wood should be well dried and treated with protective compounds. All that remains is to show your imagination and let the natural forms into the existing interior.
