The recreation center "Arcadia" in Maslovoy Pristan: where it is, accommodation, entertainment and reviews

City life often consists of hustle and bustle of everyday work. Children grow up surrounded by asphalt and lots of exhaust fumes. Therefore, city dwellers are increasingly trying to travel to nature to enjoy the surrounding beauty and clean air.

Camping trips with tents do not attract many, so people try to find hotel complexes in which comfortable conditions for recreation are created. One of the wishes of tourists is the availability of a good dining room or restaurant and several entertainment services. All the necessary conditions for an interesting and memorable pastime are created at the recreation centers "Arcadia" in Maslovaya Pristan.

Where is it and how to get there?

The hotel complex is located in the Shebekinsky district of the Belgorod region. The recreation center itself is located near the small village of Maslova Pristan.





From Belgorod, the road by car takes about 25-30 minutes. You need to move in the direction of the village of Razumnoye through the forest park Sosnovka.

Description of the complex

The recreation center "Arcadia" in Maslovaya Pristan is surrounded by a pine forest. Nearby organized a beach on the Belgorod reservoir. The noise of water and the twittering of birds can immerse a person in a world of calm and relaxation.





All buildings are made of wood and glass. Thus, the material is environmentally friendly and does not harm the health of vacationers. The complex has a main hotel building and several cottages.

Accommodations

The hotel tourists can stay in comfortable rooms for 2 and 3 people. The rooms have two single or one double bed. In the triple room, an additional transforming chair is provided.





The room has a TV, fridge and air conditioning. Each room has its own combined bathroom with shower. The rooms have access to a spacious terrace with chairs and tables.

The hotel has rooms with a high level of comfort. They consist of two rooms. The bedrooms have large beds and sofa beds. The bathroom and toilet are separate.

Accommodation in cottages

For relaxation of large companies or families, rooms in detached houses are provided. Cottages for 6 people have a spacious sleeping area on the second floor. It has a double bed and a sofa bed.

The guest room on the ground floor has a seating area, a plasma TV, air conditioning and a coffee table. There is also a dining area. It has a fridge, microwave, sink and electric kettle.

The house has a combined bathroom with shower. Bathrobes, slippers and towels are provided.

To relax a large company in the complex built a cottage for 15 guests. It consists of 4 bedrooms on the first floor and two on the second. Each room has double beds, dressers, mirrors and air conditioning.

The house has a kitchenette. It contains:

  • refrigerator;
  • microwave;
  • washing;
  • electric kettle;
  • set of necessary utensils.

On the second floor in the hall there is a folding sofa and TV, there is a combined bathroom. This part of the house has a separate entrance.

Entertainment

At the recreation center "Arcadia" in Maslovaya Pristan (photo), all conditions for active pastime are created. It has a modern sports field where tourists can play basketball, football and volleyball.

There is also a playground on site. It has bright slides, swings, stairs and other attractions. The main hotel building has a modern playroom, where children can have fun at any time of the year.





A rope road consisting of 17 stages was built on the street. Adults and teenagers can test their courage and endurance on it. There is another rope road for children. It is located not so high and has easier stages of transitions.

Most recently, a modern game room has opened on the recreation center "Arcadia" in Maslovaya Pristan. Here, guests of the complex can fight among themselves in the costumes of sumo wrestlers or ride a race on inflatable horses. The hall has air hockey, a children's labyrinth and a place for playing hockey with a ball.

On the territory of the recreation center "Arcadia" in Maslovoy Pristan wooden arbors are installed. They have barbecue facilities, long tables and benches, and lighting is available in the evening. In case of bad weather, guests can use the glass gazebo, which will protect from wind, rain or frost.

The hotel complex has a Russian bathhouse. It has a swimming pool, steam room, lounge with TV, showers. There are brooms and herbal tea with honey for sale. You can rent the necessary accessories for soaring.

The complex has a restaurant with a large banquet room. In the main room, tourists are organized daily meals. In the banquet hall, you can celebrate any celebration.

The recreation center "Arcadia" in Maslovoy Pristan: reviews

If we analyze most of the comments of guests who were vacationing here, we can conclude that the administration is trying to arrange comfortable accommodation for each client.





Tourists are happy with the cleanliness of the rooms and on site. They claim that the restaurant serves delicious food. There are also many positive reviews regarding the arrangement of banquets and the menu on them.

Guests note that the complex at any time of the year is fun because there is a lot of entertainment organized for different tastes. Tourists are also happy that the check-in time at the Arcadia recreation center in Maslovaya Pristan has not been officially established and this point can be agreed with the administration when booking a stay or vacation in the gazebos.
