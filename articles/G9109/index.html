Petrinos Aparts 3 * (Rethymno) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Greece has many islands. And almost all of them are wonderful resort areas that welcome guests from all over the world all year round. The largest and southern island of Greece is Crete. It is divided into four regions: Heraklion, Chania, Lassithi and Rethymnon. Nature has retained its original appearance, and the sea is always warm and calm.

Many travel agencies offer tourists to visit the hotel Petrinos Aparts 3 * (Greece), which is located in Rethymnon. Picturesque places, amazing nature and breathtaking views of this region attract travelers from all over the globe to enjoy this unique beauty.

Magical island of Crete

This place reveals the true beauty of Greece. The southern resorts of Crete are famous for their spacious sand and pebble beaches and breathtaking natural landscapes. The weather in the northern regions of the island is always excellent, and the sea here is always very warm and warms up earlier than in Turkey.





The eastern regions are covered with wide bays, hills and plateaus. In the central part of Crete, powerful mountains rise, the snow caps of which are visible until the end of spring. The western regions of the island are densely covered with bright vegetation; the climate is milder and more humid.

Holidays for everyone

In resort towns there is always an atmosphere of fun and carelessness. Everyone can find a suitable place to stay here: a poor student, a respectable businessman, and a family with children. For young people looking for fun, the resort of Hersonissos is ideal. There is always a holiday and a siesta time, a vibrant nightlife and noisy discos.

For families with children, it is better to choose Rethymnon hotels. The hotels of this place are always cozy and calm, remote from the hustle and bustle. Those who value comfort the most are invited by the resort of Elounda. The coast of this city consists only of hotels of only five stars, whose fashionability and wealth are amazing.

Hotel Petrinos Aparts 3 * (Rethymno)

It is an ideal place for a relaxing family vacation. The hotel is located in the village of Sfakaki, which is located 11 km from the city of Rethymnon. Most of all, the Petrinos Aparts 3 * complex appeals to residents of megacities. It is here that you can retire, forgetting about the frantic pace of life in a noisy city and enjoy the rest.





The complex has several small cottages, and in total the hotel provides its guests with 17 comfortable apartments. In the central part there are two luxurious pools, one of which is for children. If guests get tired of the scorching sun, they can always take refuge in a cozy garden, relax in the shade of orange and olive trees.

Rooms

Hotel Petrinos Aparts 3 * (Rethymno) provides its guests with rooms of the following categories:

  • Superior apartments
  • Studio.

Each room is equipped with comfortable furniture, has a kitchen area with the necessary set of dishes, a bathroom with shower, and a bathroom. Absolutely all apartments have spacious balconies with a beautiful view of the sea. The sound of the surf is heard at any time of the day. The evening sea becomes especially exciting when in the dark blue of waves the bewitching reflections of bright stars flicker.





The apartment consists of two spacious bedrooms and an equipped kitchen. In the studio category rooms, the sleeping area is adjacent to the seating area. For your convenience, each room has: powerful air conditioning, a small TV with satellite TV, a radio, a spacious refrigerator. A safe is available for an extra charge.

Beach area

To swim in the sea and relax, you do not need to go far. The beach is located a few meters from a number of buildings. The wide shore is covered with fine golden sand, there are also a number of sun loungers and umbrellas, which are available at an additional cost. The Petrinos Aparts 3 * complex does not have its own recreation area, its coast is a city beach.

However, the presence of sea urchins and a pile of rocks impede comfortable entry into the water. Therefore, in order to swim, you need to go a little to the side, where large sandy beaches with a gently sloping bottom and convenient descent into the sea extend.

Nutrition

There is no restaurant in the Petrinos Aparts 3 * hotel. But the friendly hosts independently organize breakfasts for their guests. But if the excursion is not expected and you have to stay at the hotel all day, you can go to the local taverns - Eleven and Talasi. Here, very tasty food and a wide variety of national drinks.

Entertainment

If you are accustomed to a passive vacation with a permanent stay in the complex, rest at the Petrinos Aparts 3 * hotel is not for you. There is no entertainment in the form of animators who amuse guests all day. Lovers of outdoor activities come here to admire the beauties of the island and enjoy the picturesque nature, whose paradises are created for admiration.





Mostly visitors rent a car and travel all day long to Greek attractions. The nearest major city to visit is Rethymnon. On its territory are located:

  • Venetian harbor with an old port;
  • a beautiful promenade with a number of Greek taverns;
  • Antique Venetian fortress;
  • Old city;
  • Museum of Cretan Music;
  • freshwater lake Kournas ;
  • souvenir shops and shops.

Hotel Petrinos Aparts 3 *: reviews and prices

Holidays in this complex are suitable for active tourists who prefer car trips to the sights of Crete. The living conditions and service at the Petrinos Aparts 3 * hotel are at an average level and fully correspond to its three-star status.





Reviews about the complex are mostly positive. The calm home environment, the hospitality of the owners and the amazing nature did not leave indifferent any guest.

Not without negative reviews. Some residents lacked service and entertainment, some guests were unhappy with the rocky entrance to the sea and dangerous sea urchins.

Hotel Petrinos Aparts 3 * has a convenient location for traveling around Crete by car. And given the low cost of living (about 800 euros per week for two), the complex is very popular. The prices in taverns and small restaurants are reasonable, the food is very tasty, and the portions are large. The cost of renting a car is about 35 euros per day, so you can travel around the island and explore its heavenly corners.

The Petrinos Aparts 3 * complex (Crete) is an ideal place for tourists whose goal is to visit a delightful Greek island, plunge into a carefree vacation and the enchanting atmosphere of this region.
