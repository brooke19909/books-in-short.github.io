Hotel Shine House, Adler: photos and reviews

In the squeals of an ideal place to relax, pay attention to the Shine House Hotel. Adler will give you only the best experience if you stay in a new modern business-class hotel.





a brief description of

Hotel Shine House (Adler) is a business-class resort, which in its activities is guided by European standards of service. This hotel is perfect for both leisure travelers and business travelers.

Distinctive features of this institution can be considered the following:

  • own pool on the roof of a multi-storey building (provides a good panoramic view);
  • authoring of rooms and other rooms;
  • reasonable prices, in comparison with other similar institutions;
  • panoramic windows in the rooms;
  • food in a status restaurant;
  • convenient booking procedure;
  • wide range of services.




Location

In the heart of the spa town is the Shine House Hotel. Adler, Lenin Street, 63 - the address of this institution. It is about 3 km from the train station. The road to the airport will take you no more than 15 minutes.

A great place for travelers is the Shine House Hotel. Adler is interesting not only in terms of sea vacations, but also in terms of attractions. Tourists are recommended to visit the following facilities:

  • shopping center "New Century" (1 km);
  • Adler District Museum of History (1 km);
  • St. Sarkis Cathedral (1 km);
  • shopping center "Mandarin" (2 km);
  • aquarium (5 km);
  • Caucasian Biosphere Reserve (12 km);
  • Mount Akhun (15 km);
  • Agursky waterfalls (16 km).

Room Amenities

Comfortable accommodation provides its guests with the hotel "Shine House" (Adler). The photo shows a pleasant, cozy interior. In addition, it is worth noting the presence of such amenities:





  • built-in wardrobe;
  • TV with satellite receiver;

Additional Information

It is worth taking care of booking a room in advance when leaving for the Shine House Hotel (Adler). Reservation is carried out through a special form on the official website. Also about this institution you should know the following:





  • within 5 days from the moment of ordering the guest is required to make an advance payment;
  • upon cancellation of the reservation earlier than after 5 days, the deposit will be refunded in full (otherwise a penalty of 100% of the cost of the first night will be charged)
  • minimum stay at the hotel - 3 days;
  • a child under the age of 12 years stays in a room free of charge without meals and extra bed (when placing 2 children on similar conditions, 1000 rubles per day will be charged);
  • the cost of an extra bed is 1000 rubles;
  • early check-in from midnight to 06:00 pays 50% of the daily cost of living, and after 06:00 hourly billing is carried out;
  • for late evictions until 18:00, an hourly fee is charged, and from 18:00 until midnight - 50% of the cost;
  • the need to provide a parking space should notify the administration in advance.




Special offers

For its guests, the Shine House hotel has developed a number of promotional offers. Of greatest interest are the following:

  • all guests of this hotel receive a 10% discount on the purchase of fur products in Lama salons;
  • when booking a room for more than 10 days, a transfer from the airport (railway station) and back is provided free of charge;
  • newlyweds get a bottle of champagne as a gift.




Positive reviews

Many travelers and business people choose the Shine House Hotel (Adler) for accommodation. Reviews note the following advantages of this institution:

  • in the cold season rooms are well heated;
  • fresh repair;
  • delicious, nutritious breakfasts;
  • advantageous location;
  • good traffic intersection;
  • the staff is polite, helpful and competent;
  • perfect cleanliness of the rooms;
  • at night the building and hotel grounds are beautifully highlighted;
  • inexpensive transfer (much cheaper than ordering a taxi);
  • from the windows of the rooms on the upper floors the sea is partially visible;
  • large orthopedic beds;
  • if it rains outside, you will be provided with an umbrella for free;
  • good hygiene set;
  • nice rooftop pool (swimming, you can enjoy beautiful views);
  • beautiful facade of the hotel;
  • bathrobes and slippers are provided in the rooms;
  • kids are provided with free children's furniture;
  • The hotel has bicycle rental.




Negative reviews

Even such a status institution as the Shine House Hotel (Adler) is not without drawbacks. Reviews of tourists give reason to talk about such negative points:

  • there is no partition or curtain in the soul, and therefore all water flows to the floor;
  • despite the fact that the windows are plastic, it blows strongly through them;
  • poor sound insulation in the rooms;
  • the parking lot can accommodate no more than 5 cars (even if you book a place in advance, it is not a fact that you will get it);
  • breakfast starts at 8:00, which is very late for business people;
  • poor quality instant coffee is served for breakfast;
  • drinking water in the room is available only on the day of arrival;
  • there are no coolers with drinking water on the floors;
  • the restaurant is not very clean dishes;
  • to make an order in a restaurant with room service, you have to dial for a long time;
  • cleaning is done at different times (in the morning, then at lunch, then at bedtime);
  • the floor in the lobby area is very slippery, which is especially felt in cold weather;
  • wireless internet is slow;
  • early check-in will be paid, even if the hotel is almost empty;
  • ice water in the pool;
  • restaurant staff are some gloomy and unsociable;
  • there are only three clothes hangers in the room, which is not enough for two adult guests;
  • cramped bathroom;
  • the air conditioner is too noisy;
  • there are few waiters in the restaurant, and therefore they do not have time to serve all the guests;
  • too small wardrobe in the room;
  • at the entrance to the hotel there is no ramp, and therefore it is inconvenient to carry suitcases (it also gives discomfort to vacationers with children, people with disabilities);
  • when booking, full prepayment is required;
  • Most of the standard rooms face the wall of a neighboring building.

Shine House is a good place to relax in the center of Adler. The advantages of this hotel outweigh its shortcomings, and therefore 3 stars can be considered quite deserved. This place will appeal to travelers for whom good transport interchange is important, as well as business people who value comfort.
