Specifications

The model is equipped with a matrix with a resolution of 16 megapixels. Compared with the previous modification (AW110), more enlightened optics are used here. The device is capable of bringing photographed objects up to five times, while maintaining a fairly high degree of wide-angle. Full-resolution burst speed is almost seven frames per second. Clear and blurry photos are created even when moving, which is facilitated by the electronic stabilizer used in the Nikon Coolpix AW120. Durability

The operability of the device and its body will be preserved even if it falls from a two-meter height. However, it is undesirable to conduct such experiments on your own, because any technique requires a careful attitude to yourself. The model works well not only in cold weather, but also in hot hot deserts.



Battery

Nikon Coolpix AW120 comes standard with a lithium-ion battery, which has a full charge of about 350 shots or one hour of continuous video shooting. It has a removable type, in connection with which it is recommended to additionally purchase another or two copies and constantly carry them with you, especially during long trips. The battery charges very quickly through the USB port. You can view and take pictures even at this time.

Device features

The main feature of the camera is its ability to shoot under water at a depth of up to 18 meters. The device is equipped with a GPS navigator, which tracks the coordinates of the place where pictures or videos are taken, and writes them to file properties. If such a need arises, then on a computer you can overlay them on a map and compare them with the travel route. It also allows you to navigate in such parameters as the location of the cardinal points, pressure, altitude or immersion depth at the shooting location. Among other things, one can not fail to note the high frost resistance of the Nikon Coolpix AW120. Customer reviews and representatives of the manufacturer indicate that the device functions normally even when the ambient temperature is 10 degrees below zero. You can control the camera even with gloves on.
