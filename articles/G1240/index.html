How to remove Vatsap from your phone: detailed instructions

Among the huge number of instant messengers, Vatsap is considered one of the most popular. It is simple and convenient to use, with the help of it you can exchange multimedia files, as well as make video calls. But sometimes for some reason the application needs to be uninstalled or reinstalled. In this article we will look at how to remove Vatsap from the phone completely.

There are two ways to do this:

  1. Directly in the smartphone operating system.
  2. Using third-party utilities.

Start

When deleting a Vatsap account, you need to understand that if you get rid of it, the entire history of correspondence in the messenger will disappear. You will be removed from all communities and will no longer see payment information. In addition, the account will be deactivated.

How to remove Vatsap from the phone using the operating system?

Make it easy. It is enough to go into the settings of the smartphone, then into the installed applications. Find "WhatsAp" in the list, click on "Delete". After the operation, do not forget to restart the smartphone, as well as clear the cache.

Removal using third-party utilities

How to remove "WhatsApp" from the phone using auxiliary applications? The messenger does not always work out manually. For such cases, there are special programs. But you should be careful when downloading them: use only virus-tested utilities and also avoid malicious services. Here are some utilities to help you remove the messenger:

  • Root Uninstaller. Allows you to erase both downloaded and system programs.
  • Uninstaller This utility is able to remove any program from a smartphone running on the Android platform.
  • Root App Delete. Allows you to get rid of any application or freeze it).
  • System App Remover. Allows you to delete and move applications, including system ones.
  • Cleaner. Not only clears the device‚Äôs cache, but also helps speed up the smartphone.

To use some of the above utilities, you must be root.

Uninstall on Android

Many people ask how to restore the deleted Vatsap on the phone. Unfortunately, this cannot be done. But if you still decide to remove it, then this is done in the following way:

  1. On the Android smartphone, launch Vatsap.
  2. In the menu list, select "Settings", then "Account", at the end click on "Delete account".
  3. By clicking on this button, you must enter your phone number in international format.

Please note that only the account owner can delete an account. Neither the other user nor the messenger support service can do this.

On iphone

First you need to check which version of the application was used earlier. To do this, go to the store. Launch Vatsap, go to Settings, and then to Account. Select "Delete Account." In the window that appears, enter your phone number without forgetting the country code. To completely delete an account, click on "Delete Account".

Following this simple instruction, you will no longer have a question how to remove Vatsap from your phone.

Reinstall

Sometimes the described action is required to reinstall. How to remove Vatsap from the phone to install it again?

  1. First you need to uninstall the application using any of the methods mentioned above. After restarting the smartphone.
  2. In the application store, re-download Vatsap and install it.
  3. Start up, enter your phone number.
  4. Indicate the name that will be displayed by your interlocutors during the dialogue with you. You can do this this way: in the settings, select "Profile", then "Name".

Since Vatsap has already been installed on the smartphone, then perhaps the device will ask you whether to restore message history. If you are for recovery, just click on the button. That's all, the messenger is reinstalled, and you can communicate again.

Following the above instructions, you yourself can both delete your account and the Vatsap application, and restore your profile if necessary.
