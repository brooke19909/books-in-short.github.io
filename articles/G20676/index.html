Hotel Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey): tourist reviews, photos

Turkey is a country that receives tens of thousands of tourists from all over the world every year. Travelers are very fond of staying in hotels located in the coastal strip, enjoying the hot, but rather soft rays of the Turkish sun.

One of the most popular mid-range hotels in Turkey is Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Alanya), which offers a budget vacation near the shores of the Mediterranean Sea.

general information

This vacation spot is perfect for a family trip, and it is very often chosen by those who want to relax with their children - for this category of guests there are special conditions.

The hotel was built in 1998, however, over the entire period of its existence in the territory and in the buildings, a number of reconstructions were made. One of the last was completed in 2016 - during this period the project management was engaged in updating the interior, as well as the technical equipment of the apartments.

Location

The hotel is located in the coastal part, 70 meters from the beach. In the place where it was built, a wonderful mild climate and rather hot, sunny weather prevail. The air temperature here is perfectly softened under the influence of the air masses of the Mediterranean.

As for the location from a geographical point of view, the hotel is located within the region of Alanya, in the resort of Avsallar. In order to get to the central part of Alanya, travelers need to overcome an impressive distance of 20 km, but many prefer to do this, since a huge amount of entertainment for every taste is concentrated in this place.

In their reviews of Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya / Avsallar), tourists often recommend visiting the dolphinarium, Alar bazaar and Konakli mosque. These facilities are located relatively close to the location of the hotel.



Rooms

The large and rather modern-looking six-story building of the hotel houses its entire number of rooms, which includes 113 apartments. Each of them is currently undergoing modern repairs, as well as excellent technical equipment. Guests note that an excellent decision by the team of builders is that all rooms have a new type of climatic equipment. With its help, guests have the opportunity to independently set the desired temperature conditions in their rooms, regardless of what weather conditions are currently on the street.

Also, all apartments have a private bathroom, which features modern plumbing. Here, guests will find sets of bath accessories, which include bathrobes, towels, as well as soap, shampoo, etc.

All numbers are presented in two variations: standard and promo.

On the wall of the room there is a large plasma TV with the possibility of broadcasting satellite channels, among which there are a couple of Russian speakers, which guests from the CIS countries consider a positive moment.



Promo

Promo class apartments offer superior conditions, designed for two guests. Here the furniture set is exactly the same as in the Standard rooms, but the technical equipment is much better. On a paid basis, in rooms of this type, you can install a landline telephone, a safe and a minibar, the assortment of which, if paid, will be updated every day with soft drinks. The cost of their rental is about 2-3 dollars a day.

The apartments are cleaned almost every day and bed linen and towels are replaced twice a week.



Conditions for children

The reviews about Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya) contain many comments that this place is perfect for families with children. All this is due to the fact that for this category of guests there are a number of conditions due to which their stay within the hotel becomes as comfortable as possible. In particular, one of them is the possibility of providing free cots to rooms of any class for guests aged 4 to 12 years. The catering facilities for babies offer high chairs designed for their convenient location.

Nutrition

In the tourist reviews of Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Turkey), there are many comments that the hotel provides excellent food. They are fed in the main restaurant, where a buffet is served three times a day for guests staying on the all-inclusive concept. The menu offers original and delicious dishes prepared in the tradition of Mediterranean and Turkish cuisines. Here you can also visit two bars, one of which is located within the aquazone, and the second - in the building. They offer vibrant cocktails, alcohol, mouth-watering snacks and fruits.



Spa and aquazone

Fans of relaxation prefer to drop in at the spa, which features a beautiful thermal area. It consists of a bath and a sauna. There is also a massage room in which professional masters working using unique technologies to immerse the client in a state of complete relaxation can offer their services.



As for outdoor enthusiasts, for them the Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya) has an aquazone, which is located in an open area. It consists of a terrace and a pool with clean water. There are several dozens of sun loungers all over the open terrace, which guests can borrow for free at any time. In addition, there is a bar within it where you can enjoy refreshing cocktails.

Beach

A large number of comments made by tourists indicate that, while staying at the hotel, you can spend quite a long time on a sandy beach, which belongs to the Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Turkey). Photos taken by tourists perfectly reflect the beauty of the surrounding nature, which can be observed here.

The total length of the beach line is 50 meters. Throughout its area there are several dozens of sun loungers on which travelers can relax for free. Huge umbrellas are equipped above them, which are able to protect vacationers from the harmful effects of sunlight.

If desired, guests can rent beach towels. The cost of this service is about 120 rubles per day.



Reviews

Tourists often leave photos with reviews of Kolibri Hotel 4 * (Turkey). They can be found on travel sites, as well as on the pages of travelers available on social networks. In them, they often share their impressions that were obtained as a result of their stay in this place.

Often, hotel guests talk about the excellent service that is provided in it. Particular attention is drawn to the amazing work of administrators who are around the clock at the reception. In addition, they note the excellent activity of the maids, who leave behind perfectly cleaned apartments every day.

The interior of the rooms is also appreciated by positive comments. According to the guests, in the apartments you can relax and have a great rest in a cozy and bright environment.



Prices

The cost of traveling with accommodation in this hotel is relatively low. It is about 40-45 thousand rubles. The tour includes not only a fee for staying together for a week in standard class apartments, but also all the necessary transportation costs that are associated with the flight and transfer.

If desired, travelers can include sightseeing trips in the travel package, but experienced tourists do not recommend this, since in Turkey their cost is much lower.
