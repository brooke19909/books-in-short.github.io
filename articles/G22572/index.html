The magic button "Start-Stop" makes the world cleaner!

Today we will consider an innovative technology to reduce the amount of harmful substances from a car while driving. It is called "Start-Stop." This system has long been available to residents of America and other European countries, but it has come to the CIS quite recently. Today, this technology is present only in business-class cars, but soon manufacturers will equip middle-class cars with the novelty as well. The principle of the system is that when you click on a special panel, the ‚ÄúStart-Stop‚Äù button in the car activates an option that shuts off the engine at traffic lights and starts it again during the ride. All these actions occur lightning fast, so you will not feel any discomfort. These processes are effective in reducing the emission of harmful substances into the environment. Scientists have proved that it is precisely downtime at traffic lights of cars with the engine turned on that additionally pollutes the environment by 10-15%. Let's take a closer look at the Start-Stop system.





Different manufacturers have slightly different operating principles for this system. The pioneer was the Bosch company, which first began to integrate this system into automobiles. She provided a reinforced starter, calculated by the manufacturer for at least 150,000 cycles. The Start-Stop button, when pressed, before stopping the engine, controls and checks all the functional units of the machine: the gear lever, which should be in the neutral position, the position of the accelerator pedals, brakes and clutches. A control check of the battery charge is necessarily carried out, since the system is powered by electricity. If "Start-Stop" notices any deviation from the norm in these indicators, you will not wait for the long-awaited stop at the traffic light.

In addition, they are equipped with reinforced generators, starter and batteries that can withstand longer and more intense loads.
