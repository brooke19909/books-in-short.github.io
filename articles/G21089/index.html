We travel across Russia. Solovki. How to get to the archipelago?

The most popular archipelago in the White Sea are Solovki (Solovetsky Islands). They attract a large number of tourists every year, shock with their beauty and mystery. The entire archipelago is divided into six large and about a hundred small islands. The most famous is Solovetsky. There is a conservation area on it, so the territory is carefully guarded. Many people like to visit Solovki. How to get to them, you will learn further. In the meantime, pack your bags.



How to get to Solovki?

There are many ways to arrive on the islands. Often, tourists prefer to fly there by plane: this can be done through the Sheremetyevo-1 airports in Moscow and Arkhangelsk in Arkhangelsk. Luxury lovers use a helicopter to fly to Solovki. How to get to them, saving money as much as possible? Tourists are very fond of the sea mode of transport, it is convenient: the service on the ships is of high quality, and it seems that you are on a wonderful trip. Motor ships sail from the cities of Kem and Belomorsk. Flights are daily, prices are inexpensive and there are enough seats.



Solovki: how to get there by other means of transport?

In addition to motor ships and aircraft, do not forget about other types of transport. The train is used by many people, it is quite comfortable, fast, and tickets can be purchased at affordable prices. For example, it is possible to take the following route: from Moscow or St. Petersburg, go in a reserved seat car (the cost of a ticket for which is 1,500 rubles), and after arriving in Kem, transfer to the ship. Another option is to get to Arkhangelsk by train or car, and from there fly by plane (the time spent on the road will be 50 minutes). As with the first option, you can come to Belomorsk. Today the route through Petrozavodsk is very popular. It opened recently, but has already managed to prove itself from the best side, departures are made twice a week. Unfortunately, in winter it is best to use only an airplane. Thus, it becomes clear how to arrive in Solovki (how to get from Moscow, described above).

Solovki: how to get to them without transfers?

The easiest way to do this is by land transport, for example, by bus. Such flights have been practiced for a long time, they are safe and acceptable in service. This method is much cheaper, but longer in time than traveling by plane. So let the tourist choose the most optimal path, and in the future he will have an amazing pastime and outlandish sights of the famous archipelago.
