Hotel Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 *: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Surely every traveler knows that one of the most popular Egyptian resorts is Hurghada. This place is chosen by tourists who want to enjoy a beach holiday and the exotic beauty of nature. Here is the large hotel Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 *. What level of accommodation and service can you expect? Are there any conditions for having fun with children? How tourists respond about the hotel?





Description of the territory and location of the hotel

First of all, tourists are interested in exactly where the Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * hotel is located. Egypt, Makadi, a picturesque bay, which is famous for its large coral reef - it is here, very close to the seashore, that the hotel is located. By the way, it was built in 2006, and in 2013 a complete reconstruction was carried out, after which the complex became even more comfortable and modern.

Hurghada city center is about 43 km away. And the international airport is 35 km from the hotel complex.

The hotel occupies a large territory of 85 thousand square meters. It consists of a main four-story building and several three-story buildings, which are reserved for residential rooms. Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort reviews indicate that the area here is very beautiful, well-groomed. It is decorated with lush shrubs, tall palm trees and exotic flowers.





Hotel Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * (Egypt, Makady): description and photos of rooms

This hotel complex can be considered quite large - there are 552 rooms, including single, double, triple rooms, connected rooms. It is spacious, clean, there is a sufficient amount of upholstered furniture. Windows and balconies offer views of the flowering garden or the breathtaking panorama of the sea shore.

Hotel Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * provides guests with the necessary household appliances, including TV (you can watch some Russian channels), air conditioning (cools and warms the air), safe (small, suitable for storing documents), telephone and Internet access (paid, but is inexpensive). Here you will also find a minibar, which is filled with drinking water every day.

In the bathroom, guests will also find everything you need, including a shower, washbasin, mirrors, clean towels and a set of hygiene products.





What kind of food do guests offer?

As is customary in Egypt, the Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * hotel operates on an all-inclusive system, which means that it provides guests with full-fledged meals. In the main restaurant three times a day for guests organize a buffet. The choice of dishes is quite large, and the menu includes fresh fruits, sweet pastries, and several varieties of meat and fish. In one of the three local bars you can get chilled drinks or alcoholic cocktails at any time of the day.

In addition, the hotel has four a la carte restaurants where you can enjoy all the charm of Italian, Mediterranean and Oriental cuisine. One of the restaurants offers a large selection of seafood. By the way, a place in such an institution needs to be booked in advance.





Where is the beach located? Water activities for tourists

What beach vacation can the hotel Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * offer? "Harmony Makadi" owns a private beach stretch of 600 meters, directly to it and overlooks the hotel courtyard. There is a fairly convenient, cleared entry into the sea, as well as a cozy backwater, which is suitable for bathing children. On the shore there are sun loungers with mattresses, umbrellas, in short, everything you need for a comfortable stay.

The Red Sea is world famous for its coral reefs. One of them is located near the coast. A favorite activity of tourists is swimming with a mask, because the underwater world here is very beautiful. Moreover, the diving center is constantly working, where you can buy a trip to the brightest corals, rent equipment or undergo training before the first swim. And tourists can ride boats, boats, catamarans, water skiing, "bananas", in a word, engage in a variety of water sports.





Vacation with a child: are there any conditions?

For parents traveling with children, it is important to have the necessary conditions for the child. In fact, the hotel has something to offer children, as evidenced by reviews. Harmony Makadi Bay Resort 5 * hotel provides small tourists and their parents with all necessary amenities. For example, guests can count on a cot, high chair. In the restaurant menu you can choose tasty and wholesome food for a child of any age. There are also several professional nannies working here.

In terms of entertainment, the hotel also has a lot to offer. In the courtyard for younger travelers there is a swimming pool where the water is always well warmed up. Your child will get a lot of pleasure on small water slides. There is a spacious playground with a lot of swings, where the guys like to spend time. A kids club is constantly working, where your child will be able to play, dance and draw, take part in various team games and productions. And in the evenings, the animators organize a fun mini-disco.

Additional service for hotel guests

Hotel Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * provides guests with everything you need for a pleasant stay, not overshadowed by domestic turmoil. The hotel has parking. By the way, here you can rent a car.





Do not worry about the local currency, as it can be exchanged at the hotel, and the ATM is always on. In case of deterioration of health, the assistance of a qualified doctor is provided to tourists. Clothes can be taken to the laundry or dry cleaning - this service is inexpensive.

The hotel owns its own beauty salon and hairdresser, where extremely talented stylists and professional cosmetologists work. Guests can enjoy access to the World Wide Web at a local Internet cafe. There is also a well-equipped business center with the necessary technical equipment and a spacious conference room.

Leisure of vacationers: entertainment on site

According to traveler reviews, this hotel always has something to do. For example, in the courtyard there are several large pools. One of them works in cold weather, as it is equipped with a heating system. The other pool has a jacuzzi compartment. You can have fun on water slides, which work constantly.





Fitness center services are free. You can visit the gym at any convenient time, sign up for water aerobics, morning exercises or yoga, take several lessons of passionate oriental dances. You can have a good evening playing table tennis and billiards. There are several sports fields. The hotel also invites tourists to play on local tennis courts. By the way, nearby for a small fee you can rent balls and rackets, as well as pay for lighting in the evening.

Naturally, a sauna and a Turkish bath are always open for hotel guests. You should definitely go for a massage, as real professionals work here.

During the day, tourists are entertained by animators who constantly organize new contests, performances, competitions, etc. And in the evenings, guests are invited to the amphitheater, where they can enjoy exciting shows, performances by singing and dancing groups, acrobatic performances, etc.

Hotel Harmony Makadi Bay 5 * (Makadi Bay, Egypt): reviews

Immediately it is worth saying that this hotel is in demand among tourists. Reviews indicate that the complex often becomes a vacation destination for couples and the elderly, as well as youth companies. After all, regardless of age, you will always find an interesting lesson.

The rooms are really spacious, cleaned here every day, all the furniture and appliances are in good condition. Tourists also note the quality of food - the restaurant always has enough food, the dishes are fresh, some even cooked to order. In the hotel you can have a wonderful time, as the animators constantly organize various events. The undoubted advantage of the hotel is the availability of its own beach, and also - the affordable cost of living.
