Aybel Inn Hotel (Turkey, Kemer, Beldibi): description, reviews, photos

Beldibi resort is popular not only among Europeans, but also among Russians. It stretches along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. Located in the Lycia region and is halfway from Antalya to Kemer. The boundary of the municipality lies at the foot of the Taurus mountain range. A series of multi-story hotels occupies the first line along the beach.

Turkish nights





The main industry of the region is tourism. The optimal conditions for a rich and interesting holiday are created here. There are night clubs and discos. There are diving centers, rental yachts and boats. Tour agencies offer a wide range of trips and travels.

The first guest houses in Beldibi appeared in the late eighties of the XX century. Today, the village is conditionally divided into three districts that have a digital designation. Their exact administrative boundaries have not been established.

The crowded central streets of the resort are crowded with holidaymakers. Near the Aybel Inn Hotel, an oriental bazaar operates twice a week. Its counters abound in herbs, spices, nuts, fruits and vegetables. You can also purchase clothes and leather goods. Prices are affordable, but recommend bargaining.

Swimming season





The first wave of holidaymakers in the hotel appears at the end of May. At this time, water is already warming up to 20 ¬∞ C, air - up to 25 ¬∞ C. The nights are still cool. In June, the sea temperature reaches 23 ¬∞ C, and in August - 27 ¬∞ C. You can swim in the water until the end of October. Therefore, the last tourists leave Aybel Inn Hotel in early November. Water finally cools in December.

The proximity of the mountains provides coolness that descends to the resort in the evening. Even on the hottest days of the night, the coast is not warmer than 21 ¬∞ C. The midday breeze softens the Mediterranean heat. Afternoon hours are best spent in the shade. Guests at Aybel Inn Hotel while away in the parks and gardens or around the pool.

Coast





150 meters from the residential buildings of the hotel is a private beach. Initially, it was pebble. But the administration brought yellow sand for the convenience of travelers. They filled up the entire coast and partially the entrance to the water. At the bottom, the bottom is rocky. It is recommended to wear special rubber slippers. Aybel Inn Hotel provides free umbrellas, sun loungers and mattresses.

At their disposal are awnings, showers, toilets, changing rooms. The path to the sea runs along an asphalt path. We have to cross the highway. There is no underground tunnel. How to get there

Aybel Inn Hotel 3 * is located near the highway. Finding it is not difficult. Comfortable buses deliver tourists from Antalya International Airport. Those travelers who come to the resort on their own choose minibuses. There may be traffic jams at the exit of the district center towards Beldibi.

Judging by the reviews of Aybel Inn Hotel 3 *, you can get to these parts from Kemer. Follow the D400 Highway. Travel time is not more than one hour. Ticket price - 200 rubles, in Antalya - 300. When calling a taxi, tourists recommend that you agree on a price in advance. If you pay after the trip, the overpayment will be fifty percent.

In high season, congestion on the roads of Turkey. If you are going to Beldibi for the first time, do not take any chances, book an appointment. The vehicles of Aybel Inn Hotel 3 *, reviews confirm this, equipped with air conditioning and soft chairs. Experienced drivers guarantee safety.

Accommodations





Registration of new customers begins at 14:00 and lasts until late at night. You need to free the room before 12:00. At the cash desk of the hotel they accept plastic cards issued by Maestro, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, as well as cash.

In their reviews of Aybel Inn Hotel, tourists indicate that hotel staff sometimes extort a tip. An extra charge is requested for providing a better room or early check-in. If guests refuse, an unpleasant surprise awaits them. Only a shift supervisor or administrator can resolve a conflict situation.

Aybel Inn Hotel offers sightseeing tours to nearby towns and Kemer. For service you need to contact the information desk staff. Front desk is located in the lobby. It operates around the clock. The hotel employees are engaged in solving all the pressing problems that guests face. They call doctors, contact representatives of insurance companies, and help with the rental of cars and motorbikes.

Aybel Inn Hotel (Kemer) has ceramic tile flooring. The main room has beds, a nightstand, a table and chairs. The maid is cleaning up every day. Washes floors and vacuums. Bed linen is changed twice a week. There is no refrigerator in the rooms.

Reviews

If you believe experienced travelers, the hotel used to be called the "Dream." At one time in tourist guides it was called Aybel Inn Hotel (ex. Mechta). After changing the sign in the rooms, a major overhaul was made. Public and recreational areas updated. That's all.

The quality of service has not changed. Guests complain about the slowness of cooks and waiters who work at the distribution counter. Even in the most stressful hours, they were in no hurry to fulfill their duties. As a result, long lines formed.

But the guests have no complaints about the cleanliness of the beach and pool. Technical staff constantly cleaned up, collected garbage, looked after the garden. Tourists noted a quick check-in. Sometimes rooms were provided earlier than the time set by the administration. Many were unsatisfied with the inflated prices in the hotel bar.

Parents praised the location of the hotel. The mountains could be seen from the windows. The abundance of greenery visually cut off nearby buildings. It was always quiet at night. No loud music and animation.
