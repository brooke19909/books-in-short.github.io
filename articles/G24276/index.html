Wonderland Hotel Nha Trang in Nha Trang: photos and reviews of tourists

It's no secret that over the past few years, among travelers, the number of people who prefer to relax on the picturesque white beaches of Vietnam has increased significantly . And one of the most popular resorts is the city of Nha Trang. This place is developing rapidly - every year new hotels, restaurants and entertainment complexes open on its territory. This is where the Wonderland Hotel Nha Trang is located.

Of course, during vacation planning, every tourist tries to get the maximum amount of useful and relevant information about a particular place. So what does a vacation at the Nha Trang Wonderland Hotel 3 * look like? Is there anything to do here? Can you expect a pleasant stay?

Description, photo and location of the hotel





The modern Nha Trang Wonderland Hotel 3 * hotel is located on the territory of the famous resort city of Nha Trang, in one of its new areas, which is actively developing today. The advantage is the proximity to historical sites, a picturesque beach and, of course, a colorful open market, where you can buy almost anything. Nah Trang Airport is 32 km away. Another airport is located in the city of Tui-Hoa, which is approximately 87 km away.

The hotel itself is a large seventeen-story building, built in a modern style. This is a city hotel. Nevertheless, a small area decorated with greenery and flowers is still available.

Can I eat at the hotel?





Paying for accommodation at the Nha Trang Wonderland Hotel, travelers can choose their own meal plan. Breakfast is usually the standard. On one of the upper floors there is a spacious Wonderland restaurant, which offers wonderful views of the surroundings.

Breakfasts are standard here - they offer vegetable dishes, fresh vegetables, delicious pastries and rolls, scrambled eggs, pancakes and some Vietnamese dishes. Are there conditions for water and beach holidays?





Nha Trang Wonderland Hotel is located in the resort town. The scenic Bai Ruong public beach is located about 1000 meters from the hotel building. Naturally, you can take a taxi or bus and get to the shore in 5 minutes. But many guests do not refuse from walking.

This beach was recently equipped, so it is clean, beautiful and rarely crowded. On the shore you can rent a comfortable lounger with a soft mattress, and you can relax in the shade of beautiful straw umbrellas. The water here is warm, the entry into the sea is comfortable and clean. In fact, it is the beach that is one of the main advantages of the hotel.

There will be activities for travelers who prefer to spend time actively. Vacationers can ride a boat, canoe and catamaran, as well as make an unforgettable boat trip, receive a dose of adrenaline, doing water skiing and parasailing. The city has windsurfing and diving clubs where you can rent the necessary equipment and even take an express training course.

Range of additional services for guests





At the Wonderland Hotel Nha Trang, guests are offered some additional services, although the list is not so big. To begin with, it is worth saying that you can get a safe from the administrator, which will ensure maximum safety of your belongings. There are luggage storage facilities, which is convenient if you stay at the hotel only for a while, and not for the whole vacation. Laundry / dry cleaning services are available for a small fee.

The hotel also has a spacious conference room, fully equipped with modern technology, seating up to 80 people.

Leisure: how to have fun at the hotel?





On one of the floors of the Wonderland Hotel Nha Trang there is a large pool with clean water, where you can refresh yourself, relax and just chat with other guests. If you wish, you can visit the gym at any time, where, of course, you will find modern equipment. There are massage rooms at the hotel - only real professionals offer their services.

Naturally, this is not all that Nha Trang Wonderland Hotel 3 * can offer. Vietnam is a country with a rich culture and interesting history. Only three kilometers away are attractions such as the Ponagar Towers and the Cathedral. Nearby there is a huge market that fans of shopping will enjoy.

Nha Trang Wonderland Hotel: guest reviews

After talking with people who have already managed to visit this or that place, you can get a huge amount of valuable information. Reviews of the Wonderland Hotel Nha Trang are usually positive. Its main advantage is its convenient location - from here you can quickly get to the city center, the beach, the market and the main attractions.

The hotel itself stands out for its contemporary design and decor. The rooms are always cleaned, supplies of water, tea and coffee are replenished daily. Hot and cold water is supplied constantly. Guests can rely on quality cosmetics, as well as daily delivery of clean towels. The hotel service is good - all staff are polite, smiling, pleasant in communication.

The advantages of the hotel include the quite affordable cost of living. Today this place is in demand among tourists who prefer to spend free time on the beach or excursions.
