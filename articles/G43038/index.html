Hotel White Gold Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey): overview, description and reviews

Alanya is one of the most visited tourist region in Turkey. Every year guests from Europe, the CIS countries, as well as from Russia, relax here. According to the results of the vacation, travelers leave a number of reviews on their personal pages on social networks and on thematic sites. In them they talk about which hotel they were relaxing in, what living conditions and services were provided. Tourists often give a huge number of positive reviews to White Gold Hotel & Spa - a hotel that is located almost on the beach, washed by the Mediterranean Sea.



general information

The hotel is located in Alanya, where the weather is warm throughout the year. In the midst of the spa season, the temperature on the thermometer in the daytime is at the level of 35-45 degrees, however, tourists who relax here do not experience the full heat, as the weather conditions here are perfectly softened by the air masses of the Mediterranean.

As for the transport interchange, the hotel is located relatively far from the large airport, which is located in another large Turkish city - Antalya - in order to get to it, you need to stay on the road for about 2.5 hours.

White Gold Hotel is in a great location - almost in the center of Alanya, where all night and daytime activities are concentrated - you can get to it in just 10 minutes. Also nearby is one of the main attractions of the region - the largest waterfall Manavgat, which is visited annually by crowds of tourists.

The total number of apartments in the hotel is 171. All of them are located in one building and are mainly represented by standard rooms. For a change 4 apartments of the "family" group are also presented and the same number are intended for people with disabilities. All the rooms available in the White Gold Hotel 5 * in the reviews of tourists are presented as cozy rooms, which contain everything necessary for guests to have a great time. All apartments have a private bathroom with modern plumbing, as well as a set of bath accessories for showering. Each guest at the time of arrival receives a set consisting of a bathrobe, slippers and towels.

The peculiarity of these rooms is primarily in the installed furniture - it was made by special order, taking into account all European standards. The apartments are equipped with a system of "smart home", which allows people living here to minimize movement, automating many functions of furniture.

Entertainment

Most travelers who relax at the White Gold Hotel & Spa note that, being on its territory, it is impossible to get bored. This is because different show programs and events are held here every day. So, guests can participate in morning sporting events, which are held near the aquazone, and in the evening a disco opens for them, which lasts all night. Attendance at such events is completely free.

During the day, guests are entertained by a team of animators who can find an approach to guests of any age. Travelers on the positive side note the fact that most of its participants know Russian.

Nutrition

For guests of the White Gold Hotel 5 * hotel, meals are provided in an upscale restaurant, where a daily buffet is served for visitors. Many travelers, when sharing their impressions of the rest, often note that they serve well-prepared dishes and recommend that upon arrival at the hotel you should definitely try the grilled specialties.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has 4 bars, two of which are paid, and the rest are free. They offer delicious snacks and desserts. Guests in the reviews praise the taste of the pastries with laudatory comments, and also recommend that they taste at least a couple of the original cocktails that the bar menu offers.



Sports and SPA

All tourists who prefer to monitor their health and condition of the figure, certainly visit the spa treatments that are offered at the hotel. Fans of relaxation note in their reviews the presence of a massage room, where not only special chairs are installed, but also professional massage therapists work. Their services are paid, but, as guests say, they are very upscale.

Fans of a ‚Äúhotter‚Äù vacation can visit the thermal part of the spa complex, which includes a hammam and sauna. At the exit of the thermal zone there is a small pool where you can swim. All connoisseurs of an active lifestyle can look into the gym and engage in the formation of their figure. His visit is paid, but nevertheless, the place among the guests is very popular. If desired, guests can go to the volleyball court for free, as well as play billiards or table tennis.

Aquazone

Near the hotel building there is a small aquazone, presented in the form of a pool, whose area is 168 square meters. m. It is not installed heating, but there is an automatic cleaning system that allows you to keep the water crystal clear. It has one high slide, where guests can actively spend time.

Around the pool is a small sun terrace, where there are several dozen sun loungers and several beach umbrellas. If desired, vacationers can rent a towel or an air mattress.

Beach

White Gold Hotel has a personal stretch of coastline, which is 50 meters from its territory. In order to get into the coastal recreation area, tourists need to overcome the road, which can be done through the underpass.

As vacationers note, the beach here is well maintained and very clean. It is completely covered with fine sand interspersed with small pebbles. Over the entire area there are several dozens of sunbeds, above each of which there is an umbrella - you can take such places absolutely free. If desired, guests can take an air mattress. Towels, unlike everything else, are provided completely free of charge - their rental cost is about 2 euros per day.



For business

Hotel guests, whose life is connected with doing business, are often forced to do work even during their holidays. For holding business meetings, presentations and other business events, the White Gold Hotel has a large conference room that can accommodate up to 140 people at a time. It is fully equipped with all the necessary audio equipment, as well as video equipment; In addition, there is office equipment. The furniture in the White Gold Hotel conference room includes comfortable chairs and tables made of dark wood.



Tour Prices

The cost of relaxing at the White Gold Hotel (Turkey / Alanya), according to guest reviews, is on average about 55-60 thousand rubles for two. Operators, as a rule, include a ticket for a trip for such a price, transportation of travelers from the airport to the hotel, as well as accommodation for a week. Tourists should remember that a visa is required for a Russian citizen to stay in Turkey.
