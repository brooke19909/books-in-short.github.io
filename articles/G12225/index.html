Balloon Compositions - Show Fantasy

All children and adults are just crazy about balloons that always remind you of a holiday. It is for this reason that they are used to decorate rooms during a wedding or birthday. Using compositions from balloons, you can easily create elated mood.

These incredible decorative solutions can be invented and implemented independently. The main thing is to have desire and free time. Let's look at how to make balloon compositions with your own hands.

A few words about balloons



Balloons can be of various colors, sizes and shapes. But to create jewelry very often use ordinary balloons. Compositions of them never need to be tied with a thread. It is necessary to make an ordinary knot. This rule should be remembered. To learn how to make such knots, you can practice on already inflated balls. To do this, wrap the end of the inflated ball around your middle and forefinger and pass its tip through the loop that has formed. Then you need to tighten the resulting knot well, which will perfectly retain air in the ball. The latter, in turn, will not be able to deflate for a long time. For the composition, you can choose three sizes of balls: small - about 12.5 centimeters, medium - about 22.5 centimeters, and large - about 30 centimeters in diameter. Using such balls, you can create a huge number of various shapes without any problems.

Flower arrangement of balloons



In order to make any flower, you need to purchase one small ball and four medium ones. Such compositions of balloons are often called "fours". They need to be inflated to exactly the same size. Here, science is simple: just attach the balls to each other and compare the sizes. Now you need to connect two balls in an ordinary knot. So you can make the petals. To form a flower, you need to twist together pairs of connected balls. The last (the smallest ball) must be tied to the center of the flower. If desired, such compositions of balloons can be decorated with ribbons.

Ball Decor

Flowers from the balls can be hung without problems at home on the curtains and walls or to make a decoration for the ceilings based on such flowers. For this, four more large balls are added to the already prepared flower. Inflate and fasten them in the manner described above. Then you can collect the whole composition. Due to the fact that the balloons are incredibly light, and the most inconspicuous movement of air or a breeze in the room can rock the composition, you need to draw a little water into the smallest central ball. A similar load will fix the entire composition.

There are many more widespread decorative decisions from spheres with which it is possible to decorate any room, having completely transformed it. The main thing is to stock up with the right amount of balls and, of course, fantasy.
