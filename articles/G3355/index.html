New Year's compositions from candies: a tasty gift

New Year is a time of magic and fulfillment of desires, a time to give gifts to everyone who matters in our lives. Before this holiday, the question arises especially sharply: what to present to friends, relatives and relatives? If you cannot decide which gift to buy, try to make it yourself. The best gift is one that is made with your own hands, they put warmth and a piece of soul into it. We offer several ideas on how to create unusual New Year's compositions from sweets: everyone will surely like them, because what kind of holiday does without sweets?



Options for candy compositions for the New Year

If you include imagination and remember all the attributes of the New Year, you can come up with many options for the design of candy compositions. The most common way is to make a Christmas tree of sweets. They can be fixed on a simple adhesive tape, as a basis to take a bottle of champagne or a cardboard cone. The advantage is to give sweets in green packaging or decorate them with green tinsel - so the Christmas tree will look like a living one. Another option: decorate a small decorative spruce with the help of sweets fixed on a beads: you only need to place a garland on a tree beautifully, and the New Year's composition is ready. You can come up with a large ensemble: dress a doll in a Snow Maiden costume decorated with sweets, make decorative sledges carrying spruce twigs and candy gifts for children. Another popular option: a bouquet of sweets. In this case, let it be decorated in the New Year's style, use golden and silver colors, diluting them with bright shades. Another idea: Christmas candy compositions in the form of a clock, showing 23.55. In general, there are many options, and the choice is yours.

How to make a candy composition and what you need for this



To make an unusual sweet gift, you may need:

  • candies in a wrapper of a suitable color;
  • single and double sided tape;
  • glue gun;
  • tape tape;
  • wooden skewers;
  • floristic foam or polystyrene;
  • containers for bouquets, wicker baskets or sledges, champagne bottles;
  • tinsel, beads, ribbons, colored paper or fabric;
  • spruce twigs or artificial Christmas tree.

Further, the process depends on which version of the composition you have chosen. We will describe the option with a clock. You will need a round box in which you can put small gifts.

It is wrapped in corrugated paper, the dial and hands are laid out with beads, tinsel is fixed around the perimeter. On the side surface of the watch, candy is fixed on a double-sided tape. A beautiful ribbon of silk or brocade is tied over the sweets. Cones, fir branches arranged with a pair of sweets in wrappers of a different color are glued to the front of the watch.
