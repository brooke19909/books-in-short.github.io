Screen

The device received a pretty good IPS-matrix with an HD-resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels. This resolution is quite suitable for the 5.5-inch diagonal of the screen, and the average grain size (267 ppi) does not hurt the eyes at all.





The output image is good: the colors are natural and saturated, the margin of contrast and brightness is at a high level, and the viewing angles are maximum for the IPS matrix. The image is not distorted even at a significant change in angle and does not fade under the rays of light, as well as in well-lit rooms.

The only thing users sometimes complained about in their reviews of the Prestigio Grace S7 LTE was the color temperature. The figures are slightly higher than normal, which makes the colors appear cold.
