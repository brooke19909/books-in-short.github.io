This item did not cause any complaints from almost all guests. So, according to our compatriots, the territory at Otium Hotel Life 5 * (Kemer, Goynuk) is quite large and well-groomed. On the whole, there were no special complaints from the guests about the numbers of Otium Hotel Life.
