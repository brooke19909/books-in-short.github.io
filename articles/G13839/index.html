The recreation center "Montazhnik" - rest on the shore of Lake Kaldy

The main advantage of the recreation center "Montazhnik" is Kaldy Lake. It is surrounded by dense forest. The administration has created all the conditions so that vacationers can feel comfortable. The base is open all year. It provides a wide range of services. Ideal for families and for those who come in a noisy big company. For living there are houses and cottages of different levels of comfort. The sandy beach is equipped with comfortable sun loungers and parasols. The water in the lake is clean, warms up well in the summer. A slide is installed for children. There is a dressing room. In a word, rest at this base will not be boring.

Residence

For the settlement on the recreation center "Montazhnik" different houses are provided. There is a two-story building that works all year round. It can accommodate up to 100 people. In addition to rooms, the building has a banquet and children's room, a dining room, a conference room. The administration is located here. Each room has upholstered furniture, a wardrobe, a TV, a shower. Bed linen is provided. The price includes meals.

Those who want to enjoy the water of the lake should choose a two-story cottage. It is built of wood. Designed to accommodate 9 people. There is a kitchen, a bathroom and a shower. The living room is shared, located on the ground floor. You can dine on the veranda, there is also a balcony. At the recreation center "Montazhnik" there are also one-story houses. They are equipped with a kitchen. Braziers are located near them. There is a veranda with a dining area.

Block houses for vacationers have been built on the territory. They have all the necessary furniture, household appliances. There is a bathroom with a shower and a washbasin. Some of them have benches with a table. In the houses of the first category there is no bathroom, so you have to visit a shared separate toilet located on the street.
