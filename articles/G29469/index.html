Crafts for giving with your own hands made of wood

Wood is a unique material from which you can make many crafts and ornaments. For many years, the tree has not ceased to delight with its variety of colors and textures. In addition to tactile sensations, this material retains its divine aroma in products. Apparently, for these reasons, the tree is highly regarded among creative people. In the country, wood is probably the most widely distributed and used.

So, making wooden crafts for the garden with your own hands will not be difficult. For example, you can easily make an outdoor flower pot. To do this, you will need a stump of the right size and height, a drill, drills, a chisel, a hammer, sandpaper and varnish. First, we mark on the hemp the places of future holes. After that, we drill the indicated holes, and combine them with a drill. It is also necessary to do not only around the circumference, but also in the center of the workpiece. Next, with the help of a chisel and a hammer, it is necessary to remove the core of the stump. When this is done, take sandpaper and grind the edges and insides of our hemp. In the end, of course, several times we coat the entire wooden pot with varnish. That's it, the work is over, it's time to plant flowers.

Crafts for giving with your own hands are not limited to only wooden pots. From a suitable branch, consisting of a handle and three branches, it is quite possible to build a flower stand. To do this, you just need to nail a circle of plywood of the desired diameter to the handle, clean the entire stand with sandpaper and cover with two layers of varnish.

Those craftsmen who are more experienced in handling and working with wood can easily make such crafts for giving their own hands from wood, as figures of various fairy-tale or cartoon characters. Children who come with their family to the country will be delighted with such crafts.

One cannot but mention the beauty and practicality of the wooden arbor. Professionals who can afford to take up such work will immortalize their work and skill for many years. One has only to imagine how the whole family gathers at a table in a wooden carved arbor on a quiet summer evening. This place will serve not only for family vacations, but also an occasion to gather a large and noisy company of friends.

If a wooden arbor is within the power of the owner, then a shower for giving with your own hands can not be a problem at all. Such construction will take much less time and effort.
