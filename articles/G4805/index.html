Side Ally Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular and lively resorts in the south of the Republic of Turkey today is Side. In the vicinity of this city is concentrated a lot of historical, cultural and natural monuments, carefully guarded by the government. In addition, Side boasts a wide base of hotels, entertainment for every taste and budget, as well as luxurious beaches, clear sea, beautiful scenery and wonderful climate.

If you, like tens of thousands of tourists from all over the world, have decided to discover this resort region of Turkey, then as a place for accommodation, we recommend considering Side Ally Hotel 4 *. Further, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with this hotel closer, and also find out what our compatriots remembered for the rest in it.





Location

The hotel complex under consideration is located seven kilometers from the center of Side in the small resort village of Sorgun. The famous local landmark, Manavgat Waterfall, is just five kilometers from the hotel.

The nearest international airport is in Antalya, 70 kilometers from Side Ally Hotel 4 * (Side). After your plane lands in this air harbor, it takes about 1.5 hours to get to the hotel complex. This time you can spend interestingly and profitably, listening to the guide‚Äôs story about the history, culture and features of the region (in case the transfer is organized by a travel agency), as well as admiring the picturesque scenery from the window of the bus or car.

This hotel has its own beach. It is located five hundred meters from the hotel complex. The path to it passes through the promenade. We draw your attention to the fact that it is not necessary to overcome this distance on foot, since a special bus regularly runs between the hotel and the beach, delivering guests to the very shore of the sea.





How to get to the hotel yourself

If you plan your trip to Side yourself, we recommend choosing flights that land at Antalya Airport. From here you can easily get the intercity bus to Manavgat. The distance from it to the hotel is only a few kilometers, which can be overcome either by taxi or by minibus. If you want to travel independently to different regions of Turkey, then in the summer there will be no problems with this. So, between Side and Alanya, Istanbul, Izmir and other cities regular bus service is established.

This hotel, formerly called Belinda, was built in 1989. In 2014, large-scale renovations were carried out here. Thus, guests have the opportunity to stay in comfortable, stylishly decorated and equipped with all necessary rooms, located in a large five-story main building and a one-story additional unit.

There are restaurants, bars, swimming pools, sports fields, a fitness center, spa, parking and much more. This hotel complex is ideal for tourists who want to spend their summer vacation in a pleasant and peaceful place away from the noise and stormy entertainment of the big city. Also, there are all conditions for children.

If you want vivid impressions and stormy fun, then you can always go by taxi or shuttle bus to the city of Side or Antalya.





Posting Rules

Side Ally Hotel 4 * (Turkey) has a set time for check-in and check-out of vacationers (the so-called checkout time). In accordance with this rule, check-in for guests arriving after 14:00. It is necessary to free your room on the day of departure before noon.

If you arrive at the hotel earlier than two in the afternoon, then if you have free rooms you will be settled immediately. However, in the midst of the season, you may have to wait until the previous guests have vacated the room and the hotel staff have prepared it properly.

As for the departure, when handing over the keys to the reception it is necessary to pay for the entire stay, as well as for using additional services. You can pay both in cash and credit cards "Visa" and "MasterCard".

Accommodation for guests traveling with children

Since Side Ally Hotel 4 * is a family hotel, tourists who come to rest with their children are only welcome here. Moreover, if you place children under the age of twelve in your room on existing beds, then nothing extra will be required. In addition, playpens are provided free of charge for children up to two years old.

If your room will accommodate a child over 12 years old or an adult in an extra bed, then you will have to pay 70% of the cost of apartments per person per night for this. The administration of Ally Hotel Side 4 * (Side, Turkey) should be notified in advance of the need for an extra bed , and then wait for confirmation from it. Also note that each room can only have one extra bed.





Accommodation for tourists with pets

As you know, not all hotels have the opportunity to stay if you travel in the company of your four-legged pet. Side Ellie Hotel is one of those. Therefore, if you plan to stay here, then you have to leave the animal at home for the holidays.

Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the Ally Hotel Side 4 * hotel has 149 functional and stylishly decorated rooms, which are located in the main five-story building, equipped with an elevator, and a one-story additional unit. Most rooms are standard. Family apartments are also available. The hotel also has several rooms specially equipped for the comfortable accommodation of people with disabilities. In total, the hotel complex can simultaneously accommodate 320 guests.

Each hotel room is decorated in a modern style and has a private bathroom (with a hairdryer and necessary toiletries), a balcony, a TV (there are Russian-language channels), a refrigerator, a minibar, a safe, air conditioning. Wireless internet access is also available. Rooms are cleaned on a daily basis. Bed linen and towels are changed several times a week. In addition, all guests of Side Ally Hotel 4 * can always order food delivery directly to your room.





Nutrition

Meals in the hotel in question are organized on an all-inclusive basis. In the restaurant of the hotel complex during the day you can taste delicious dishes of local and international cuisines. In addition, several bars with a wide selection of drinks and snacks are available on site and on the beach.

Sea and beach

Despite the fact that the Side Ally Hotel 4 * hotel is 500 meters from the coastline, it has its own nice sandy beach. There is a regular bus service from the hotel to the beach, so there is no need to walk. The beach has everything you need for a great holiday: deck chairs, mattresses, tents, a bar, as well as equipment for water activities.





Entertainment

The hotel complex has a large outdoor pool with a sun terrace. There is also a bar with a wide selection of drinks for every taste. In addition, you can play darts, billiards, basketball, ping pong, tennis and golf at the hotel. Water sports equipment rental is available on the beach. Animators work here all day, and in the evenings there are bright shows and discos. Guests can relax with a hammam and massage treatments.

For the youngest guests there is a children's pool and a playground. There is also a mini club.

Services

Hotel Side Elli has a developed infrastructure that allows you to make your stay as comfortable and convenient as possible. So, the reception desk is open around the clock. The hotel has an ATM, wireless Internet access. The guests of the hotel complex can book a transfer, a tour, rent a car, use the laundry, dry cleaning, ironing and room service.

Cost of living

As for the prices of rooms in the hotel in question, they are average for hotels of this level of stardom. So, a ten-day placement at the height of the season will cost you in the amount of 50 thousand rubles.





Side Ally Hotel 4 *: tourist reviews

It should be noted that the hotel in general leaves a positive impression on our compatriots. But we offer to understand everything in more detail.

So, tourists like the location of the hotel complex. It is located away from the bustling city streets, so you can sleep well at night. The hotel itself, although not new, is in good repair. All equipment in the rooms works. If there are any malfunctions, they are very quickly eliminated after contacting the reception. However, some tourists recommend to settle in the main building of the hotel, if possible, because, in their opinion, the rooms in the bungalow are slightly worse. So, they do not have laminate flooring, and tiles, balconies are not so large, and they are also located closer to bars and restaurants, where the noise does not subside until late in the evening.

As for food, almost all travelers are satisfied with it. So, in the restaurant here both adults and children are happy to eat. The menu is quite varied, the food is tasty and of high quality.

I liked our compatriots and the beach of the hotel. Despite the fact that it was several hundred meters away, many enjoyed walking along the road surrounded by eucalyptus trees and other vegetation. Alternatively, you could always get to the beach with a hotel car, which constantly runs between Side Elli and the coastline.
