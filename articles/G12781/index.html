General information about the Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic is a fabulously beautiful, warm and sunny tropical corner located on about. Haiti. The territory of this paradise island is occupied by another republic of the same name (Haiti), which owns a smaller part of it.

Where is the Dominican Republic? On a world map, the Republic is visible in the eastern part of the island of Haiti. In the west is the Republic of Haiti. On the north side, Dominican Republic is washed by the waters of the Atlantic Ocean, from the south - the warm waters of the Caribbean Sea.

This country for many people is associated with beautiful exotic palm trees, white sand and turquoise waters of the ocean and sea. However, these places are more multifaceted. In addition to beaches, palm trees and the sea, there are wonderful national parks where a wide variety of vegetation grows in abundance, many historical and other attractions, as well as equipped places for a variety of water activities.

A brief history of the resort

Until the 80s of the XX century, the jungle and impassable mangroves spread in this place. There were no houses, no roads, much less infrastructure. Only golden beaches and the sea.

And then one day the American Theodore Hill and the investor Frank Ranieri from the Dominican Republic decided to create a beautiful resort in this place, but few believed in the realization of such an idea. A lot of effort was made, but as a result, the first Puntacana Resort and Club hotel was built on the lands bought out by investors, which laid the foundation for the development of tourism in this paradise on earth.

Punta Cana Coral Reefs

The resort of Punta Cana is recognized as a great place for diving, because along the entire coastline there is a coral reef, which is not only picturesque, but also rich in underwater fauna and flora. Thanks to this huge wall, all the beaches of Punta Cana and Bavaro are quite well protected from the surf.

This unique ecosystem attracts a large number of diving enthusiasts from all over the world in Punta Cana. The length of the coral reef is about 40 kilometers. It has picturesque crevices and unique underwater caves located near the Macau beach, created by nature itself for divers, both beginners and professionals. The depth of immersion is not more than 18 meters, underwater visibility in summer reaches 30 meters.

Diving in Punta Cana in the most picturesque part of the reef Marinarium among divers is quite popular. This place gives vacationers an unforgettable encounter with fish of various colors. Here you can swim next to the stingrays, nanny sharks and stingrays.

It should be noted that the beaches of Punta Cana and on the island of Catalinita are perfect for sea diving. Here is the most beautiful marine life in the entire Dominican Republic, recommended for fans of diving and snorkeling.

Sights and excursions

And what to see in Punta Cana, except for the wonderful beaches? Relaxation under the sun near the sea can be perfectly combined with interesting excursions.

The best time to relax in Punta Cana, as noted above, is from November to April. Although here at any time of the year you can have a wonderful and profitable time due to the presence of many excursions to such exotic places.
