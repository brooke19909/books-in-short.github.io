Health resort Omsk "Dawn": medical profile and services

Nature generously awarded the city of Omsk picturesque places and favorable conditions for a relaxing holiday. It is not surprising why the tourism industry is so developed here, numerous hotel complexes and health resorts have been built. Here you can enjoy the natural beauties of wildlife, inhale a breath of clean fresh air filled with the healing aroma of coniferous forest, and, of course, protect yourself from the endless bustle of the city.

Medical services

High-quality medical care using advanced technologies and specially designed programs is carried out by doctors of the highest category. The rooms of the following doctors work in the medical building: cardiologist, neuropathologist, therapist, psychotherapist, urologist, gynecologist and many others. Omsk sanatorium "Dawn" offers patients to undergo a functional diagnosis and treatment in a reflexology clinic.





Competent specialists using modern research methods will help to identify the existing pathology and get rid of it as quickly as possible. Patients who have undergone oncology are accepted for recovery. There is a gynecological department. Since 2006, the dispensary has been accepting pregnant women for rehabilitation.

People with heart, respiratory and digestive diseases come to the Rassvet sanatorium (Omsk). Photos of the residential building can be viewed on the official website. In addition, the resort specializes in nervous, skin, genital, endocrine and psychological pathologies.

Treatment programs

The boarding house uses the latest achievements in the field of cosmetology and medicine. Phytotherapy is actively used in combination with drug treatment. For each patient, a course of therapeutic exercises for every day is developed. There is a shower department in the boarding house, a department of practical psychology to help emotionally depleted people, radon therapy, and magnetotherapy.



For those who want to lose weight, correctional programs and diets are provided. Dermatological and sexual problems will help quickly and permanently solve the sanatorium "Dawn" (Omsk). Cosmetology includes modern methods, such as cryosauna, biorevitalization, mesotherapy, aromatherapy, physiotherapy (vaporization, darsonval, brosage), as well as waxing and various cleansing masks. After passing such sessions, patients rejuvenate, gain strength and get rid of skin problems.

Procedures for small children are provided - from 2 years of age. To strengthen immunity, protect against weakness and emotional overload, pantohematogen baths are offered that increase the flow of nutrients and oxygen to our tissues. In addition, they are absolutely harmless, do not provoke allergic reactions.

Accommodations





Comfortable, beautifully furnished and renovated rooms of various categories are offered by the Omsk "Dawn" sanatorium. The boarding house is designed for 160 places. Available standard and luxury rooms with air conditioning. The windows and balconies of the rooms offer a magnificent view of the adjacent park area. Each room has new furniture, modern appliances, the necessary hygiene and bedding accessories.

Supply system

Balanced and fortified 3 meals a day awaits guests in the health complex. The list and composition of dishes are carefully thought out by nutritionists. Mineral water and fresh vegetables with fruits are always present on the tables. For those who are shown a strict diet in connection with gastrointestinal diseases, a separate menu is being developed. The building has a bar and offers free internet. Every day there are exciting events and a disco.

Infrastructure





Excellent conditions for a pleasant pastime are provided by the Rassvet sanatorium (Omsk). Reviews of tourists confirm the high quality of service and the availability of versatile leisure activities. The pearl and the main object of entertainment, according to the guests, is a large swimming pool in which different types of hydromassage are built in (countercurrent, cascade, bubble air massage). In water, you can do aqua fitness and hydroaerobics.

In their free time from medical procedures, vacationers can visit the gym, Finnish sauna or hammam. You will get great pleasure from reading the literature in the reading room of the library. For active leisure there are computer and billiard rooms. Well, in the evenings everyone gathers on the territory of the complex to participate in interesting contests, watch a concert program and dance.





Fun for kids

From the age of two, babies are admitted to the Rassvet sanatorium (Omsk). For children, special recreational, educational, cultural and sports events are held. The guys learn swimming and dancing. A playground with swings and sports facilities is equipped on the adjacent playground.

How to get there

Omsk sanatorium "Dawn" is located at the right bank of the Irtysh River, No. 153. You can get to the dispensary by intercity transport (bus, minibus) - Sibadi stop.
