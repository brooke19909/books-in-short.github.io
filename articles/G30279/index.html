LCD "Tsarskaya Square": features of the complex

LCD "Tsarskaya Square" is a combination of a comfortable living environment, a legacy of the history of the city of Moscow, non-trivial architecture and the most modern technologies. This business class home will be the place where you want to return again and again.

LCD location

The Tsarskaya Square residential complex is located in Moscow, Northern Administrative District , at the address: Leningradsky Prospekt, 31. The location of the complex is convenient in terms of both infrastructure and road structure.





The residential complex can be reached both by public transport and by private car. In the first case, it is enough for a resident to walk to the Belorussky railway station or to the Dynamo metro station in Moscow. Next to the house lies Leningradsky Prospekt, from where you can go to the center through Tverskaya-Yamskaya Street or through the Third Transport Ring, as well as into the suburbs via Volokolamsk Highway. In addition to these directions, the presented roads allow you to get to anywhere in the city and suburbs.

Infrastructure LCD "Tsarskaya Square"

The internal arrangement of the complex is thought out to the smallest detail - it's a pastry shop, grocery store, fitness center, restaurant. In walking distance from the LCD there are numerous schools and lyceums, a large shopping center "Aviapark", clinics.

The peculiarity of the Tsarskaya Ploshchad residential complex is the projected green zone, which will be equipped for pedestrians with cozy boulevards, large lawns, intricate architectural complexes, fountains, and in the winter, residents will be able to use the ice rink. The ecology of the district where the residential complex is located is also at a high level in comparison with other microdistricts of the city, even despite the proximity to major highways and the constant traffic of cars. The proximity to the Petrovsky Park complex makes housing in the residential complex even more comfortable and useful.

Reviews about LCD "Tsarskaya Square" and the results

There is no point in doubting that the funds spent on the purchase will be in danger - the company is verified. LCD "Tsarskaya Ploshchad" is not the first MR Group facility; it is a rather large construction company in Moscow.

Reviews about the LCD are mostly positive - close Moscow and Grushevskaya alleys where you can walk; lack of delays in the construction of the complex; comfortable territorial location and developed infrastructure; the presence of underground parking, which will provide round-the-clock availability of space for a personal car; the absence of any legal problems with the developer and paperwork.
