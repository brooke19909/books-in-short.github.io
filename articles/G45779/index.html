Which vacuum cleaner company is better? Rating of vacuum cleaners

House cleaning takes up a significant part of the free time of any housewife. The modern home appliance market with its assortment facilitates this task. A vacuum cleaner is an indispensable thing in any modern family, with its help you can remove trash, dust and dirt not only from flooring, carpets, but also furniture, curtains, bookshelves, and washing can even refresh windows. At the same time, a representative of the household appliance family is able to effectively remove not only dust and large particles from any surface, but also difficult to collect threads, hair, pet hair, fluff, microparticles, microorganisms.





Which manufacturer's vacuum cleaner is better to choose so that it performs its functions perfectly? Let's try to figure out that article.

Features worth choosing

There are a lot of categories of vacuum cleaners that you need to determine when choosing.

  • Method of cleaning: dry cleaning, washing, steam.
  • Dimensions: classic (floor), manual, portable, vertical, backpacks.
  • Dust collection options: container, with a water filter, with a bag.
  • Control method: manual, remote control with infrared or radio control, robots (automatic).
  • Power: wired, battery.
  • Application: domestic, professional, stationary

Another selection criterion is to solve the problem of which vacuum cleaner company is better, since many popular companies offer all kinds of models of these devices.

Manufacturers of quality vacuum cleaners

Initially, you need to determine the list of manufacturers of quality vacuum cleaners. The question of which vacuum cleaner company is better will become clearer if we classify well-known manufacturers. For example, in parts of the world: Asian, American, European.

European brands





Among European, the highest quality vacuum cleaner is considered Bosch. This company has existed for over 120 years and offers reliable and affordable equipment. The disadvantages that users of individual devices highlight are not significant (inconveniently located controller, there is no option to store nozzles).

Another popular company is the German manufacturer Thomas, whose vacuum cleaners have an unchanged water filter that helps clean the air from the smallest particles, including microbes. A good vacuum cleaner for the home is offered by the Swedish and Polish companies Electrolux and Zelmer, respectively.





Karcher is known as a manufacturer of reliable professional cleaning equipment. This technique has high power, which allows it to be used to remove garbage after repair.

Asia and America

Among Asian brands, the highest quality is the LG vacuum cleaner and Hyundai technology. Japanese and Korean cleaning devices are not only reliable, but also affordable. Vacuum cleaners of these companies do not have a very high durability, but it is quite enough (about 5 years) for use in everyday life. The specific period depends on the frequency of use and neat attitude.





The most durable equipment is offered by American companies. Vacuum cleaners Kirby and Rainbow are reliable and durable, but their cost is significantly higher than analogues of other companies.

Popular home vacuum cleaner manufacturers

It will become easier to answer the question which vacuum cleaner company is better if you evaluate brands according to the parameters used by experts and market analysis experts. In descending order of models, Samsung came first, followed by Philips. This is followed by Electrolux, Zelmer, Thomas and Rowenta. However, a large selection and a high degree of quality do not have a direct connection, therefore, the technique of market leaders should also be evaluated according to the main features.





Expert opinion

The rating of vacuum cleaners among popular brands was helped by experts characterizing brands in the following ways:

  • Cleansing ability.
  • Noise level.
  • Cleanliness (degree of dust emissions).
  • Convenience of operation.




It turned out that the Electrolux vacuum cleaner is the best option for the home. Advantages: low noise and dust emission, cleans surfaces well. Unlike the models of the Swedish company, the LG (and Samsung) vacuum cleaner is noisy. Experts noted that the power lever of the first brand is inconvenient and there is no indication of filling the dust bag, which increases the likelihood of breakage.

Some users note that the Zelmer vacuum cleaner is also a bit noisy. Some models of this brand, for example, Solaris Twix 5500.3 HT, will be uncomfortable for women due to the large weight.

The best models with a water filter

Despite the wide variety of varieties of vacuum cleaners, models with an aquafilter are considered the best for use at home. They are used for general cleaning of surfaces. Their feature is their weighty dimensions, as these are floor models that are ideally suited for cleaning carpets. Which vacuum cleaner company is better in this category of products in terms of cleaning, air filtration and dust retention functions?





The Zelmer 919.0ST vacuum cleaner (8.5 kg) allows you to change the filter to a bag for collecting dust, performs dry and wet cleaning. Unlike the first option, Thomas TWIN T1 (8.4 kg) does not have a bag in the kit, but is equipped with the ability to supply water under pressure and the option of installing the pipe vertically. Karcher DS 6.000 (7.5 kg) weighs a little and consumes electricity moderately. The most expensive option among those presented, performs only dry cleaning, there is a vertical mount. Zelmer and Karcher vacuum cleaners also have an accessory storage area.

The best models with a bag

The rating of vacuum cleaners with a bag will help fans of the classics of the genre to choose the right model. It is believed that in this category the most silent is the Electrolux UltraSilencer USENERGY vacuum cleaner. This is a relatively inexpensive vacuum cleaner, but its cost is higher than noisy analogues. The system is equipped with a high-quality filter, which is convenient for allergy sufferers. A vacuum cleaner is not a champion in terms of bag volume (3.5 L) and suction power (240 W). The parameters of the model are above average, and are quite suitable for use in the apartment.

Among vacuum cleaners with a bag, several Samsung products were included in the ranking of the best models. Their features:

  • The SC4180 is compact (27.5 x 23 x 36.5 cm), while the rest of the product is at a high level.
  • SC61B4 has a large set of nozzles, power - 420 W, is easy to operate.
  • SC21F60YG - the most powerful (520 W) vacuum cleaner from inexpensive models.
  • SC5491 has a low price compared to the above devices, the most popular in the CIS.

Cyclone Vacuum Cleaners

Container models with the development of technology have replaced vacuum cleaners with bags. To ensure good suction, the air flow in the system occurs as a ‚Äúcyclone‚Äù (in a spiral), which ensures dust settling in the container. One of the best models in this category is the Panasonic MC-E8035 with a noise level of 68 dB. Bosch BSG 62085 is characterized by compact dimensions, due to which the model has drawbacks associated with a small (1.2 L) container volume, lack of space for nozzles.





Conclusion

There are several manufacturers offering good vacuum cleaners: Bosch, Electrolux, LG, Panasonic, Samsung, Thomas, Zelmer. The exact choice depends on your preferred features. For example, for cleaning rooms with young children, a silent option is needed. Such models can be found at Panasonic and Electrolux. Vacuum cleaners with aquafilters are suitable for allergy sufferers, you can leave your choice on the Zelmer technique. Bosch and Samsung equipment have high power. The latter option is represented by a wide range of budget models. All these brands are quite popular, so if problems arise, it is easy to replace parts and purchase accessories, and create a stock of interchangeable bags. American models are too expensive for the average buyer, so not so popular.
