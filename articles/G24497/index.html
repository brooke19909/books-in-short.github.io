What cities are on the country's banknotes

Any national currency differs not only in its design. The banknotes depict prominent figures, architectural monuments and whole landscapes. But not everyone knows which cities on the banknotes of Russia and parts of Russia are indicated on its banknotes.

Krasnoyarsk open spaces

A denomination of 10 rubles today is the minimum bank ticket. It depicts the views and attractions of the city of Krasnoyarsk. His railway bridge over the Yenisei, displayed on a banknote, was included in the list of the best such structures in the world. On the same side of the bill is the chapel of the great healer, St. Paraskeva Friday.



On the back of the banknote is part of the Russian hydroelectric station, the second largest in the country in terms of power. The bill gradually leaves household. It is found less and less, and most people use coins with the same denomination.

Views of Arkhangelsk

City of the Golden Ring

What are the cities on the bills? Each Russian banknote reflects something special - events, people, etc. A banknote of 1000 rubles glorifies the city of Yaroslavl. It was founded by Yaroslav the Wise. He is depicted on banknotes. In the hands of Yaroslav the Wise is the temple. Residents of Yaroslavl gave this monument the affectionate name "Uncle with a Cake." It is located next to the chapel of Our Lady of Kazan. On the back of the banknote is another cultural heritage - the temple of the Baptist.

Amur wonders

Few people think about which cities are on the banknotes of Russia. And each locality is distinguished by its miracles and outstanding personalities. The largest Russian bill today has a face value of 5,000 rubles. The banknote is bright, beautiful, it shows the views and sights of Khabarovsk.



Count Nikolay Muravyov, Governor-General of Eastern Siberia, is immortalized on the bill. Thanks to this man, Russia was able to regain the Amur, given in 1989 to China. On the other side of the banknote is the Amur Miracle - the Khabarovsk Bridge. Its length reaches 2700 meters.
