Cambrils - Catalan Spain

More recently, this village was a perfect exotic for tourists. Only the fishermen, who at exactly half past four in the morning sailed to the shore to sell their goods, disturbed the quiet and calm life of the town of Cambrils. Spain is a tourist country, which means that sooner or later tourists should find out about this amazing village, making it forever a favorite vacation spot.

This resort is especially popular today among couples with children. The mild climate, the absence of sudden changes in the weather, the clear sea with gentle slopes and well-equipped beaches are excellent conditions for families with children.

Five kilometers from the city is the resort town of Salou, known for its fashionable entertainment. Thus, Park Aventura attracts tourists from all over Europe. This is a place that has united on its territory five different worlds: the Mediterranean village of the last century, the stunning China, the Wild West in all its glory, exotic Polynesia and colonial Mexico. There is also a traditionally huge Caribbean Beach water park in Salou, which is also often visited by holidaymakers in Cambrils.



Spain seems to have frozen here in the last century. Despite the developed tourist infrastructure, the city has preserved centuries-old national traditions and an old way of life. In the port, fashionable yachts are adjacent to the fishing "dishes", expensive restaurants and nightclubs located on the promenade have simple fish dishes of inexpensive taverns that Cambrils is so rich in on its menu.

Spain at this resort is very clearly represented precisely by national cuisine. Moreover, this village is considered the gastronomic capital of the Mediterranean coast. All kinds of thematic festivals and fairs are regularly held here, where you can not only try, but also buy delicious cheese, honey, wine, olive oil and other products typical of Spanish cuisine.



Outdoor enthusiasts will also appreciate the Cambrils. Spain is a sports country, which can be easily seen here. Windsurfing, diving, yachting, beach soccer and volleyball, motor boating or a catamaran ... The list of active entertainments offered by the resort goes on and on.

Unrecognizable nightlife changes the look that Cambrils “wears” in the sunlight. Spain, the sights of which we are accustomed to assessing with geographical and historical values, is also a developed infrastructure of entertainment, including nightlife. Youth discos, amazing live music of restaurants and summer cafes, all kinds of contests and performances will not let anyone get bored after sunset.

In general, here any tourist will find all the best that Spain has to offer. Cambrils hotels have a variety of - from budget to fashionable five-star hotels. However, wherever you stay, you can be sure of the service of the highest European level. In Cambrils, a tourist of any income will find both accommodation and entertainment available to his wallet.
