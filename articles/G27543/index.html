Basically they have a good opinion about this accommodation option. Well, the rating of Presa Di Finica Hotel Suites 5 * is quite good (4 out of 5 possible points). Beach

The 5-star Presa Di Finica Hotel Suites is located on the Mediterranean coast. To get to the beach, you must overcome the distance of 200 meters and the underpass.

Sand and pebble coastal zone has a gentle entrance to the water. This allows young children to independently frolic aground. Right on the beach you can see the crawling sea turtles. In the coastal waters live swordfish, tuna and several other species.

For comfortable tanning, taking air and sun baths, there are comfortable sun loungers with soft mattresses. Enjoy the sound of the surf for those who prefer areas in the shade, under numerous umbrellas and awnings. Beach towels are free. The components of the infrastructure are showers, changing cabins, a bar and a toilet.

The safety of swimmers and the order of sunbathers is constantly watched by attentive rescuers.





Beach fun

The presence of the pier allows boats to moor holidaymakers to the open sea to get new emotions. One of the most popular in these parts is diving. Having sunk to the bottom of the sea, getting acquainted with the unique relief, flora and fauna of the underwater world, you will get an unforgettable experience.

For those who prefer to spend their free time on land, there is an equipped volleyball court. In addition, holidaymakers will like snorkeling, banana riding, canoeing, windsurfing, jet skis, the ability to parachute after a motor boat.

Aquazone

Many people prefer active swimming in pools to swimming in the salty waters of the seas. For this, in the hotel complex Presa Di Finica Hotel Suites 5 * ("Tophotels" gives the complex a rather high rating - 4 out of 5 possible) three outdoor pools were built, one of which is equipped with four slides. Water slopes of various structures diversify and saturate your stay with bright colors and unforgettable memories.

The main swimming pool is surrounded by sunbeds with sunshades. He - without heating, has a gradual increase in depth. Numerous tropical palm trees and leafy shrubs complement the swimming area and create a beautiful view.

A children's pool is located nearby, where the kids, under the constant supervision of their parents, are ready to swim for a day and night.

Here you can play water polo or do water aerobics. Simple exercises from instructors to raise the mood and relieve tension will throw a couple of extra pounds.

In order for the children not to have time for boredom, their leisure is thought out to the smallest detail. The kids club, whose opening hours are available at the entrance, is pleased to meet guests from four to twelve years old. They will have a great time here. Under the guidance of an experienced educator, at a small children's round table, they will be invited to play board games, draw and make original crafts. A wooden two-story house allows you to fantasize and play role-playing games. Puppet theater, watching cartoons and master classes are part of the entertainment schedule for young guests.

Throughout the day, cheerful animators entertain and accompany the children both in the club and in the fresh air. The latter is incredibly healthy. For games in the air built a game complex of high-quality and safe for children materials. Slides and swings, sandboxes and carousels allow kids to spend time actively and not sit still. In restaurants, babies are provided with high chairs, and a playpen is available in the room upon request. Babysitting services are paid.
