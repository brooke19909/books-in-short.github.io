How to get from Kutaisi to Tbilisi: consider various types of transport

Georgia is a small country. Traveling along it is a pleasure. Ancient castles and monasteries, picturesque nature - I just want to photograph everything around. Roads in Georgia are in good condition. In this article we will describe how to get from Kutaisi to Tbilisi. The country is quite elongated from west to east. Therefore, these two cities are separated by a considerable distance - 226 kilometers. How to overcome it? We will tell you. Read our article and go explore the capital of Georgia from the ancient resort city of Kutaisi.



Airplane

The air mode of transport is rightly considered the fastest. But not on this route. In solving the problem of how to get from Kutaisi to Tbilisi, the plane should be completely rejected. Why - after all, in both cities there are airports? The reason is pretty simple. There is no direct flight connecting the capital of Georgia with the western city. You will have to get with transfers. The journey will take about 15 hours. And such a trip will cost a lot - at least four hundred lari (9,400 rubles). Not to mention the hassle of getting to the airport and waiting an hour and a half before departure. Kutaisi air gates are located 17 kilometers from the city. And another ten lari (235 rubles) will need to be laid out for the road to it. And taking into account the fact that you still have to admire the transit hall at the transfer point for about five hours, you cannot call such a trip a pleasure. In a word, D&G is Expensive and Stupid.



How to get from Tbilisi to Kutaisi: train

The traveler has several trains to choose from - night and several daytime, as well as trains. In Kutaisi, the railway station is located almost in the center, a ten-minute walk from the restored old quarters. If you want to drive up, then take the city bus number 1. The distance from Kutaisi to the capital of Georgia by rail is about 250 kilometers. This route night passenger train number 602 overcomes in five hours. An alternative to it is the daily express train No. 24. Electric trains are suitable for a traveler only if he wants to fully plunge into the local life. These trains make too many stops along the way, so the road to the capital will stretch for six hours. On the official website of the Railways, you can not only clarify the schedule, but also purchase tickets online. By train, the price of the issue is three lari (70 rubles). The fare on trains of the Georgian railway depends on the class of train and wagon.



Trains with transfers

There is one secret regarding how to get from Kutaisi to Tbilisi. Only local residents know him, and actively use it. Five kilometers from Kutaisi is the Rioni railway station . Through it, a mass of convenient high-speed trains, including double-decker Stadler-Kiss from Batumi, follows to the capital of Georgia. Also suitable compositions from Poti, Zugdidi, Ozurgeti. You can even get to Rioni by taxi, five kilometers is not the distance. But in Tbilisi you will be in three to four hours. A trailed car rides from Kutaisi to Rioni, which is attached to long-distance trains in a transport hub . The transfer option is the fastest and not so expensive. One-way trip will cost 10-15 GEL or 235-355 rubles (depending on the class of train).



How to get from Tbilisi to Kutaisi: bus

The road between these cities is close to the straight line laid on the map. Therefore, many choose public transport such as minibuses. Their disadvantage lies only in the fact that they are in demand among the population and will have to go in cramped conditions, but not in insult. Therefore, you should arrive at the bus station in advance - the minibus is still not rubber. In addition, having typed a full car of the people, the driver does not wait for the appointed departure time. But minibuses ply between two cities very often. Yes, and go only three hours. Directions - a little more than a train - ten lari.

In Kutaisi, the bus station is located at McDonald's at the intersection of Chavchavadze and Zviad Gamsakhurdia. It is easy to find by a variety of minibuses. If we consider the question of how to get from Tbilisi to Kutaisi, then the station from which minibuses depart is located in the capital of Georgia near the Didube metro station. Having paid a little more expensive, fourteen lari (240 rubles), you can get on a large long-distance bus. You will be in Kutaisi in two hours.

For 15 GEL there is a great opportunity to use the services of Georgian Bus and Metro Georgia carriers. The same three hours on the road, but much more comfort. The buses of these companies have air conditioning, TV, Wi-Fi. And finally, great news. Most recently, a bus was launched that follows Kutaisi directly from Tbilisi Airport. Ticket price - twenty lari (470 rubles).

To do this, follow the E60 highway through Zestafoni, Khashuri and Gori. There is a highway from Leselidze to Tbilisi. Want to make the trip cheaper? Take fellow travelers!
