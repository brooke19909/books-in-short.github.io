Territory

Gran Hotel Don Juan Palace 4 * - chain hotel. She belongs to the Don Juan Hotels. The representative office of this network in Lloret de Mar was built in 1972. Since then, the hotel has been renovated several times. The latter took place in early 2017. The hotel consists of one six-story building. It is easy to recognize from afar by its beautiful white facade with a semicircular trellised ledge. The building faces the road. Thus, very few rooms face the street. The hotel building stretched like a half-open book deep into its territory. Next to the building are two outdoor swimming pools with a sun terrace and jacuzzi. Since the Grand Hotel Don Juan Palace is located in the very center of the resort, its territory is small. There is a walking alley past the pools, very beautifully designed. Reviews claim that it creates the illusion of a large space. The greenery of palm trees and Mediterranean vegetation creates a wonderful shadow.



Hotel Rooms

Gran Hotel Don Juan Palace 4 * can offer guests several categories of rooms. The simplest is the standard. There are more than half of such rooms in the hotel. Some tourists complain that it is not easy to travel alone. Often you have to pay for a double room. But the Don Juan hotels have prepared standards for one guest. There are also double rooms - both for couples and for two friends, with separate beds. Reviews say that the beds are so wide that three people can live in a room - for example, parents and a child. Families of four are provided for families of four. These rooms include a bedroom with a living room with a seating area and a sofa. There is also a junior suite category. The room consists of two bedrooms without an adjacent door and a living room. The upper class is the "family suite". These rooms are located on the top floor and have a spacious wooden terrace, two bedrooms and a living room. However, balconies have all the facilities for guests.
