Karafuu Beach Resort & Spa 5 * (Tanzania, Zanzibar): description of rooms, service, reviews

In Tanzania, on the Zanzibar archipelago, right on the coast of the famous Pingu beach, there is a resort spa hotel called Karafuu Beach Resort & Spa 5 *. It is an ideal place for a paradise vacation. Yes, and not far from the airport - a little more than 30 kilometers separates the hotel from Abeid Amani Karume International Airport. Many people come here, as the complex offers high-class service and maximum comfort to its guests. However, you can tell about its advantages in more detail.





Leisure

First of all, it is worth noting that Karafuu Beach Resort & Spa 5 * has its own private beach with sun loungers and umbrellas, where access is exclusively allowed to guests of this hotel.

On the territory of the complex there are several swimming pools (including a children's one), sun terraces, a fitness center with a gym, two outdoor tennis courts, a volleyball court, and a health club. And, of course, Samawati SPA-center with a wide range of services and procedures offered, of which tourists enjoy massage.

In addition, the hotel has modern equipment necessary for water sports (diving, by the way, too). There is also a games room with billiards, table tennis and darts. And in the evenings on the territory interesting entertaining show programs are organized by a professional team of animators.





Facilities and service

In Karafuu Beach Resort & Spa 5 * there is everything that can be useful to tourists who are on vacation. This includes free Wi-Fi, private parking, airport transfers, luggage storage, tour desk, currency exchange, 24-hour reception ... and that's not all! There is also a laundry room, several shops and a conference room.

Of course, the hotel has several facilities serving guests. There are three, to be more precise:

  • Restaurant Eatalian. The main specialty is Italian cuisine. This establishment offers lunch and dinner.
  • Restaurant Grand Bleu. Here guests are offered a fish menu. A wide selection of seafood dishes will delight any gourmet. It is only necessary to pre-book a table.
  • Zanzi-Bar. Based on the name, we can make a logical conclusion - this is a bar. Yes, and it works daily. Here, guests are offered a wide selection of soft drinks and strong drinks, cocktails, etc.

Beach

It is necessary to tell about it separately. The beaches of Zanzibar are well known throughout the world, and the one on which the hotel is located is even more so.

Pingu meets vacationers with soft, pleasantly silvery sand, in which I want to bury my head. The beach is not bad, low tides, barely perceptible. There are few people here, and various cafes and taverns are scattered along the coast.

Pingu Beach smoothly flows into the neighboring, almost the same name - Pingwe. It is not so good, because the bottom in its area is pebbles mixed with sand. Yes, and the ebbs are strong here. But on the other hand, next to Pingwe is the famous The Rock restaurant, which serves great seafood and exotic cocktails.

If you want to drive along Zanzibar, then you can go to Pwani Mchangani beach, which is one of the best on the east coast.

What is worth a visit?

In Zanzibar, of course, there is something to see. The question is different - where is it really worth going? After all, time is not always enough, and you do not want to lose it on uninteresting or tiring excursions.

Tastes are different for everyone, but most definitely recommend fishing. Vacationers are taken to the north coast, to the unique Zanzibar beach - this is an amazing place where there are practically no underwater "barriers" in the form of sea urchins, and low tides rarely occur. And the nibble is excellent. Emotions are provided, as is the catch.

You can also go to the island of turtles - this is an exciting sea voyage across the ocean with a visit to an abandoned prison. And lovers of everything original should go to the village of Kizimkazi and to the Jozani rainforest.
