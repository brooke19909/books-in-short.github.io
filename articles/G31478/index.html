Vietnam: is it worth going to the country's resorts in August? Features of rest, recommendations and reviews of tourists

The charming country of Southeast Asia, Vietnam was recently "discovered" by Russian tourists. A long flight, perhaps, is the only minus of a trip to rest in this state. But there are no pluses. These are relatively low prices, wonderful beaches, excellent service, hospitable people, exotic customs, nature enchanting with its beauty, rich excursion program at each resort. The advantages of Vietnam should be added both security in the country and a visa-free regime for Russians arriving for a period of not more than fifteen days. But when to go on vacation to this state? In this article, we will look at Vietnam in August. Is it worth going to the resorts of the country in the last month of summer, or is it worth waiting for a more comfortable time of the year? What do tourists say about this? We examined their weather reports in Vietnam and summarized them here.



Vietnam in August: reviews and features

If you look at the map of the country, it is easy to notice that this state has a great length from north to south. A relatively narrow strip is Vietnam along the Pacific Ocean. Therefore, in a small country there are three climatic zones. This makes it possible to relax in Vietnam at least all year round. When in the winter in the north, in the Hanoi region, nasty rains and pretty cool, on the island of Phu Quoc, which lies in the same latitudes with the resorts of Thailand, a real hot summer reigns. But from June to August, the tropics of southern Vietnam covers the rainy season. But in the north of the country the sky is not cloudless. So is it worth going to Vietnam in August? Should I go to the fabulous Halong Bay (in the north of the country) in general? Let's look at this issue in more detail. According to climatic conditions, Vietnam can be divided into northern, central and southern parts. What is the climate in all of them at the end of summer?



North

When asked about whether to go to Vietnam in August, reviews respond that it all depends on the chosen vacation spot. Summer - it is summer in Southeast Asia, and, consequently, air and water temperatures are quite high. The climatic zones of Vietnam in August differ only in the amount of precipitation. What can be said about the north of the country in the last month of summer? There, the wet season is almost over. There are more and more clear days. Rains can still happen, but they mostly occur in the evening and night. Meteorologists observing the weather in the most beautiful bay on Halong land say: for the whole of August, the sky is covered with clouds for eleven days. Clouds cannot diminish the effect of the southern sun. It warms the air to thirty-two degrees during the day, and at night the temperature does not fall below + 24 C.
