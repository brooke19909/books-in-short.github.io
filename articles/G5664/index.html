Transport accessibility

It is believed that one of the advantages of this residential complex is that getting to it is as convenient as possible. It is located five kilometers from the Ring Road. As a result, the journey by car to the city does not take more than 30 minutes.

Since 2015, a new bus route has been operating from the Novy Petergof railway station to the Kirovsky Zavod metro station. The number of this route is 200A.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is one of the main advantages of this residential complex. It is located in a truly unique place, where picturesque landscapes and fresh air are combined with developed urban infrastructure and an amazing historical atmosphere.

Petrodvorets district has long been recognized as the most environmentally friendly in the city, almost half of its area is occupied by squares and parks. For example, right behind the railway station, within walking distance from New Peterhof, is the Alexandria Park, through which you can go to the Lower Park with fountains and the Gulf of Finland.

In the west of the new buildings there is a small forest with a meadow park, rivers and ponds, on the banks of which you can take a break from the bustle of the city. A little further, the Belvedere Palace landscape park with picturesque ponds. Meadow Park can be reached by car or bicycle in just 5-10 minutes. Walking does not take more than half an hour.

It is worth noting that since 2005, the Petersburg suburb of Peterhof received the status of a science city, which has become an official recognition of it as a unique center of science, culture and education. For example, it is here that the educational building of St. Petersburg University is located, as well as the Naval Institute of Radio Electronics, which bears the name of Popov.

In addition to the shopping center and small shops that are located on the territory of the complex itself, you can go shopping at nearby supermarkets Magnit and NETTO, the trading house Real, grocery stores 21 Century and Ropsha, a state pharmacy and a private pharmacy "Pearls".
