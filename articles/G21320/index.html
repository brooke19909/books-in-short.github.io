Hotel location

Iberotel Makadi Oasis & Family Resort 4 * is a hotel complex, which consists of three-story buildings spread across the beach in Makadi Bay. It resembles an Arab village, which allows you to feel the charm of the East from the moment of settlement. The nearest airport is 30 km away. Nearby is the city of Hurghada.



Territory and infrastructure of the hotel

The main pride of the hotel is its territory. It smells of lush greenery, shrubs of bizarre shapes and flower beds. It looks especially impressive against the desert. There are tile tracks everywhere, which helps guests to move around comfortably. The order is monitored very carefully, and the cleanliness everywhere is just perfect.

The attraction of the hotel is the impressive pool, which is very similar to the lake. Growing near the palm trees give an oriental flavor to the reservoir. They clean it every day, so there are no complaints about pollution. Also near the hotel there is also a pool for young guests.



In Iberotel Makadi Oasis & Family Resort 4 * attentive to the leisure of guests. Hotel guests can visit the amphitheater, where animators organize various performances. There are also regular discos for young guests and adults. In the evenings here you can enjoy live music, karaoke or all kinds of show programs.

Hotel guests can replenish their knowledge in the library, use parking. For an additional fee, you can visit the hairdresser, use the laundry, rent a car. For a certain price, the doctor provides his services.

Business people are given the opportunity to use the conference room for negotiations or conferences.

Number of rooms of the hotel Iberotel Makadi Oasis & Family Resort 4 *

Makadi is a great place to spend a vacation, so a huge number of tourists come here every year. For their accommodation, the hotel provides 521 rooms, which are divided into the following categories:

  • standard double - 318;
  • family - 196;
  • junior suites - 7.

The hotel also has rooms for people with disabilities.



The rooms have ample space. From the window you can enjoy the pools or gardens. There are balconies where you can spend time admiring the views. Each room is equipped with satellite TV, air conditioning, minibar. There is also a telephone and a safe for storing valuables.

The bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer and toiletries.

The rooms are cleaned daily and linen is changed twice a week.

Features of a beach holiday

The hotel complex Iberotel Makadi Oasis & Family Resort 4 * owns a beach, which is located on the seafront. Like the entire complex, the beach is always very clean. You can reach it in a matter of minutes. There is always enough free space. Vacationers can get mattresses or umbrellas here. As for the towels, they are given out only near the pool.

The sea bottom is dotted with pebbles and shells, so when bathing it is better to use special shoes that will avoid visits to the doctor.

Of particular interest to vacationers is the coral reef, located near the beach. Here you can watch the marine life.



On the beach, vacationers can brighten up their leisure time with a game of volleyball, engage in darts or throwing balls. Fans of a more passive holiday will have a great time in the beach bar, where you can have a snack or order a drink.

restaurants and bars

For fans of international cuisine there is an opportunity to visit restaurants located on the territory of Madinat Makadi, where guests of the Iberotel Makadi Oasis & Family Resort 4 * hotel are provided with free movement.

Entertainment

A pleasant pastime is provided by a wide selection of various entertainments that Iberotel Makadi Oasis & Family Resort 4 * provides its guests with. Reviews of tourists confirm that here you can play billiards, volleyball, tennis or squash. You can also learn how to shoot from a bow. There is a golf course on site. There is an equestrian center here, where everyone is given the opportunity to ride a horse. True, for such entertainment you have to pay extra. You can also use the fitness center for a certain amount.



Fans of the water element were not deprived of attention here, providing them with a large selection of water sports. The hotel has a diving school, where everyone can go diving. You can learn water skiing, surfing, learn to sail or canoe.

Hotel guests for a certain price can use the services of a massage therapist, visit the SPA center, sauna or jacuzzi.

An interesting pastime awaits the young guests of the Iberotel Makadi Oasis & Family Resort 4 * hotel. Here they can have fun on the playground, attend a kids club or a mini-disco. If necessary, you can use the services of an experienced nanny who can look after your baby at any time.

Nearby Activities

Especially good tourists say about the water park, which is located near the hotel. Here adults and children can relax. It is no less exciting to spend time on excursions, which are abound here. Guests can head to the desert to fully experience the beauty of this fabulous place and visit the Bedouin village. It will be very exciting to sail on a boat with a glass bottom. You can simply swim in the sea, watching the marine fauna or go fishing.

The hotel provides an opportunity to visit the monasteries of St. Anthony, St. Paul or St. Catherine, the pyramids of Giza and see the Great Sphinx.

Connoisseurs of oriental culture can visit nearby cities. Excursions to Hurghada are often held, where you can see many attractions. Guests will be enchanted by the beauty of the singing fountains that are in this city.

You can also book a trip to Luxor - a very beautiful city, spread out on the banks of the Nile. But it is far away, and tours there are carried out only by appointment.

Staff

Many of the reviews of tourists who had the pleasure of visiting the hotel Iberotel Makadi Oasis & Family Resort 4 * note the friendliness and professionalism of the local staff. All guests here are treated very carefully and respectfully. When meeting, they always welcome guests and are interested in their affairs. True, for people who prefer solitude, this may seem a little intrusive.

Especially good here are children. Moms are very pleased that their babies are always called by name, although remembering everyone is quite difficult.

It is necessary to take into account the fact that the overwhelming majority of people here are Arabs, so it is necessary to respect their faith. There is no need to disturb anyone during prayer.

Hotel Features

The hotel is perfect for family vacations, as its name suggests. But no less exciting here will be able to relax single people, couples or companies of several people.



Well-established infrastructure allows you to feel quite comfortable. The friendly atmosphere that prevails in the hotel complex contributes to a pleasant stay. An indelible impression will be left by the Arabian architecture and the Mediterranean flora.
