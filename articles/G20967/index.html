Kamensk-Shakhtinsky bike hotel: description, services and reviews of tourists

Traveling through the vast expanses of our country, motorists often have difficulty finding an overnight stay. However, recently mini-hotels have begun to open on Russian roads, offering tourists comfortable rooms at affordable prices. Among such hotels there are also stylized, originally and unusually decorated. There is such an institution in the city of Kamensk-Shakhtinsky. The bike hotel, the only one in the country, located on 932 km of the M-4 Don highway, offers its visitors comfortable, inexpensive rooms.

Location Features

In order to become a guest of this interesting hotel, you do not need to turn off the road and loop around the city streets. The hotel was built right on the road. Of course, it is very convenient for non-resident road travelers. Of course, this arrangement does not imply a special silence and environmental friendliness. However, most tourists consider this a minor trifle. Moreover, the hotel is decorated really creatively, it‚Äôs comfortable to live in it.





General view of the hotel

The original hotel building cannot leave indifferent any car enthusiast passing the city of Kamensk-Shakhtinsky. 932 km long bike hotel is a multi-storey building with large windows and a blue and white facade, decorated in a modern style. Biker themes are widely used not only inside the hotel, but also outside. At the entrance to the hotel there is a traffic light at which a green light is always on for visitors.

Near the hotel there is a monument to a tired biker in the form of a large scary skeleton on a motorcycle. Under the massive billboard with the name of the hotel pyramid are barrels of ‚Äúout of fuel‚Äù.

There is another monument in the courtyard of the hotel - a completely rusty three-wheeled bike, which is nevertheless on the go. The sculptural composition, made in the technogenic style, which is a huge skull with a truck leaving its mouth, also looks very interesting.





A concrete staircase finished with tiles leads to the building itself, on the steps of which visitors are greeted by a sculpture of a jazzman. Near the hotel for guests there is a free secure parking.

Hotel lobby

Entering the building, the visitor finds himself in a real bike museum. The first thing that catches your eye is the red vending machines for drinks, stylized as a gas station. Hotel visitors have the opportunity to buy coffee, tea, beer and even vodka. Biker attributes are placed throughout the hall: a motorcycle, wheelchairs with rubber tires, fuel barrels, etc.

A stylized clock hangs above the reception desk, showing time in different parts of the world. In the corner of the hall is an old Maxim machine gun painted green. Visitors wishing to purchase an original gift have the opportunity to do this in a souvenir shop.





Hotel rooms

For motorists traveling at night through the city of Kamensk-Shakhtinsky, a 932-km bike hotel is worth a visit. You can relax in this hotel with great comfort. The hotel offers its visitors standard and junior suites. Room keys are issued on the original keychain in the form of a motorcycle number. On the floors you need to climb a massive staircase from a steel corner. The corridors are decorated in biker style. There are posters, metal and rivets everywhere. The doors to the rooms are steel with a dusting in the form of skulls, motorcycles and other patterns of biker theme.

The rooms themselves have air conditioning, refrigerators with bars, desks, beds, bedside tables and televisions. Of course, all this is framed in a biker style. So, the tables are a tire covered with glass. Headboards are made in the form of a motorcycle or biker. There is no general lighting in the rooms, because the hotel is mostly nightly. However, this does not cause inconvenience to visitors. A sconce in the form of motorcycle headlights is hung on the walls in the rooms, there are floor lamps and lights for nightstands.

The place is also pleasant in that it offers an inexpensive bike hotel (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky) to relax. Room prices range from 2300-3000 rubles per night. Those who decide to book a room for several days in this hotel should bear in mind that in case of premature departure, money for unused time spent by the guest is not returned.





Hotel bathrooms

Shower rooms are decorated very brightly. The doors leading to them are decorated with metal gears and original handles. The walls in the bathrooms are tiled with colorful tiles.

Judging by the reviews of visitors, the water in the shower comes with good pressure, and the rooms themselves are perfectly clean. For the convenience of guests in the bathrooms there are bags with shampoo, pieces of soap in individual packaging, new towels, toilet paper, etc.

Dining at the hotel

The bike hotel in Kamensk-Shakhtinsky is also convenient because breakfast is included in the room rate. You can eat in the nearby Garage, a small cafe. This establishment is also decorated in a biker style. The sign is made in the form of a skull with wings, huge tires are installed on the roof, and a large white motorcycle above the entrance.

The food in the cafe, judging by the reviews of the hotel guests, is very good. All dishes are delicious, hot, prepared with the freshest ingredients.





Opinion of the guests

The bike-hotel hotel (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky) has rightfully earned rave reviews from visitors. Almost all motorists who have ever stayed at this original establishment recommend it to friends and acquaintances. The rooms here are really comfortable - spacious and well equipped. To pay for spending the night in them is affordable for any motorist. The rooms and the hotel are cleaned very often. Therefore, it is always very clean and comfortable here.

Many car travelers also note the fact that the hotel staff is polite and helpful. If desired, you can ask the staff of the institution both about the hotel itself and about the sights of Kamensk-Shakhtinsky. They provide all the necessary information very willingly.

In general, in a bike hotel you can spend time not only with comfort, but also very interesting. Many travelers rent rooms here all the time. Museum of the legend of the USSR

The presence of a fairly large number of attractions is what the city of Kamensk-Shakhtinsky can boast of. The bike hotel and the place itself is quite unusual. However, those travelers who want to gain more experience should visit the USSR Legend Museum located next to this hotel. It is a magnificent building, decorated in a modern style. The main theme of the museum are cars produced during the Soviet era. Models of Moskvich, Seagull, Gas, etc. brands are arranged in the halls. Here you can also watch Soviet TVs, tape recorders and other household appliances and utensils.
