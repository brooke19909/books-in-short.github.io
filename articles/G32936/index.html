Hotel Otium Hotel Aloha Sharm 4 *, Egypt: description, photos and reviews

Surely everyone will agree that Egypt is one of the most popular tourist countries for our compatriots, as they offer a carefree beach holiday, high-quality service, and at affordable prices. The famous resort city is Sharm el Sheikh - the pearl of the country, located on the picturesque coast of the Red Sea. It is here that the spacious hotel complex Otium Hotel Aloha Sharm 4 * is located.

What kind of living conditions do tourists offer? Is the beach far away? Can I take a child with me? What reviews do travelers leave about the hotel? Answers to these questions will be of interest to readers.

Where is the hotel located?





Quite often, this hotel is chosen by tourists going to Egypt. Otium Hotel Aloha Sharm 4 * is located in a picturesque corner of the famous city of Sharm El Sheikh, almost on the seashore. Very close is the cozy bay of Ras Um Sid.

By the way, the distance to the city center with all its attractions and entertainment is only 4 km. About 7 kilometers away is Naama Bay, a well-known among travelers. The airport is 18 kilometers away, so the road to the hotel is unlikely to take much time. The location of the hotel is one of its advantages, since it is easy to get to almost all interesting places of the city.

How many rooms are on site?

By the standards of Egypt, the Otium Hotel Aloha Sharm 4 * hotel can be considered relatively small, since there are 206 rooms on its territory. Tourists can choose rooms of different categories depending on their needs and preferences:

  • 106 double rooms with one large or two single beds;
  • 43 triple rooms for three adults and a child;
  • 6 large quadruple rooms.

In addition, on the territory of the Otium Hotel Aloha Sharm 4 * there are also connected rooms that are perfect for large families and youth companies. There are rooms for both smokers and non-smokers. On the first floors of the residential building there are several rooms designed for the comfortable living of people with disabilities.

Naturally, many tourists are interested in questions about what living conditions can be expected. What do the rooms at the Otium Hotel Aloha Sharm 4 * look like? There is the necessary furniture, including comfortable beds, tables, wardrobes and soft sofas. Some rooms have a private terrace or balcony.

From household appliances there is a telephone, an air conditioning system with an individual control panel, and a TV on which you can watch satellite channels (there are also some Russians). The bathroom also has clean towels and personal care products. The rooms are cleaned every day and most tourists do not complain about cleanliness. Bed linen is changed approximately three times a week.

As already mentioned, this resort complex is located on the first line - its territory goes directly to the seashore, which means that the beach can be reached in a few minutes.

Water recreation for tourists





What quality of beach and water recreation can the hotel complex Otium Hotel Aloha Sharm 4 * offer? Sharm el Sheikh is a city famous for its warm waters and picturesque coral reefs, so travelers will definitely not be bored here.

Here, next to the hotel, you can rent a boat, pedalo or canoe. For a relatively small price you will have the opportunity to ride a boat, banana, water skiing or even take a short boat trip.

Naturally, near the coast there is a picturesque coral reef, so swimming with a mask is a favorite activity of tourists. If you want to study the local underwater life in more detail, you can visit the diving school. Travelers with the appropriate certificate can rent the equipment necessary for diving and get an excursion to the most interesting places in the Red Sea. Beginners, on the other hand, have the opportunity to take a few lessons from professionals, in whose company they can then make their first diving.
