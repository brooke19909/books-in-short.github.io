Sun & Sands Beach Hotel 3 *, Vietnam: hotel description, traveler reviews

Hotel Sun & Sands Beach Hotel 3 * provides comfortable accommodation, many services, as well as proximity to the main local attractions and objects of city life.





Resort Description

Hotel Sun & Sands Beach Hotel 3 * has an incredibly favorable location. Just a few meters away is the wonderful Mui Ne beach. And on the other coast, about 2 km from the hotel, there is a beautiful center where you will be taught all the tricks of surfing.

As for the attractions located near the Sun & Sands Beach Hotel 3 *, it is especially worth highlighting the Sand Dunes, the large Phan Thiet Market and the Fairy Stream. You can also go on longer trips using the services offered by the local tour desk.

One of the main features of Vietnam can be considered its unusual cuisine. In this regard, Sun & Sands Beach Hotel 3 * can offer many local dishes in its restaurant. This hotel also offers such pleasant moments as free Wi-Fi, comfortable rooms, private parking and much, much more.





Hotel complex rooms

Sun & Sands Beach (Vietnam) can offer its guests the following options for accommodation:

  • Separate bungalows for 4 people. There are two bedrooms, as well as a small living room with upholstered furniture (can serve as an extra bed for two guests), a dining table and chairs. The bungalow also has a cozy terrace with wicker chairs. Nearby is a small garden.
  • Standard rooms are designed for 2 guests. If necessary, an additional clamshell can be installed. The apartments also have a small balcony for drying clothes and bath towels.
  • The huge quadruple room stands out among the rest not only in impressive size, but also with a beautiful view of the sea, which opens from its windows. If necessary, the apartment can also accommodate two beds.




Service for guests

The Sun & Sands Beach Hotel (Phan Thiet) provides the following services for guests:

  • free wireless Internet, the signal of which extends to all rooms of the hotel, as well as to its territory;
  • paid transfer to the airport or to any other communication centers or sightseeing sites;
  • The reception desk is open around the clock so that you can contact the staff with questions or for help as soon as possible (you can also call);
  • the hotel administration will provide you with all assistance and assistance in purchasing or booking tickets for any means of transport;
  • in the travel agency you will be helped to choose the most interesting routes for study tours;
  • guests have the opportunity to check their luggage in a specialized left-luggage office;
  • a safe consisting of several cells is installed at the reception so that each guest can deposit their valuables there;
  • Hotel guests are given the opportunity to rent a bicycle to travel around the city or to explore for themselves.




Hotel Infrastructure

One of the most favorite tourist destinations at the moment is Vietnam. Sun & Sands Beach Hotel 3 * offers its guests a rich infrastructure so that they do not feel a lack of any services. So, the following objects are available to hotel guests:

  • outdoor pool, which is located in the courtyard of the hotel;
  • public beach, which is literally a couple of steps from the hotel;
  • a restaurant serving local cuisine, which, however, can offer more familiar European dishes;
  • a wellness center that specializes in massage;
  • a bar with a lot of drinks, where television broadcasts of sports matches and all kinds of entertainment programs are also held;
  • car parking, the use of which is completely free for hotel guests;
  • a laundry room where you can order not only laundry, but also ironing services;
  • exchange office that works for commission payments;
  • On the ground floor of the hotel there is a pharmacy where you will have access to the most necessary medicines at any time.




additional information

The hotel Sun & Sands Beach Hotel 3 * (Phan Thiet) has the following internal rules that govern the stay of guests:

  • registration and check-in in the rooms is strictly in the period from two in the afternoon to nine in the evening;
  • You must leave your room on the day of departure from 9 am to noon;
  • the hotel is not intended for children under 12 years old, and therefore cots and other relevant infrastructure are not provided in it;
  • The hotel is strictly forbidden to live with any pets;
  • You can pay for your services with a credit card using a bank transfer system.

Positive reviews

Many tourists from all over the world managed to visit the Sun & Sands Beach Hotel. Reviews give a good idea about this institution:

  • on the territory there are two whole pools, the purity of which is maintained very carefully;
  • the green territory, which is densely planted with greenery, palm trees and flowers, pleases the eye;
  • quite nice and neat rooms, which are quite clean and tidy, despite the modest interior;
  • every day, bath towels are replaced, and, as necessary, stocks of soap and other cosmetic accessories are replenished;
  • thorough cleaning, the schedule of which must be agreed with the guests;
  • good hearty breakfasts;
  • in the restaurant you can try many delicious and unusual dishes related to the national cuisine of Vietnam;
  • budget rates for accommodation.




Negative reviews

Hotel guests are generally not happy with the following points:

  • the road from the airport to this complex is very exhausting, because it takes about 6 hours in time;
  • very uncomfortable and hard pillows lie on the beds, after sleep on which the back and neck are terribly sore;
  • breakfast is held too early (from 6 to 9 in the morning), so many tourists simply did not have time to wake up at the appointed time;
  • the hotel restaurant is more like a cafe and looks very untidy;
  • too intrusive and not polite with the guests hotel guide.

In general, the hotel leaves its guests a fairly pleasant impression. Despite the budget prices and small size, there is everything that a tourist may need. It is also worth noting the advantageous location of the hotel. It is located in a small village, so the prices for food, food and souvenirs here are much lower than in resort towns. And all sorts of natural and architectural sights are just a stone's throw away. Also, you will certainly appreciate the proximity to the beach, as well as the cordial attitude of staff and local people.
