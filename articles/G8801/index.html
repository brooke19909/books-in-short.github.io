Hotel Ocean Varadero El Patriarca 5 *, Cuba: photos and reviews of tourists

Cuba is an incredibly interesting country with original traditions and history. In addition, here you can relax on the beach, not only in the summer, but also in the winter, which many travelers prefer to do.

Not the whole country is well adapted for tourism, but there are resort regions with developed infrastructure. One such city is Varadero. This is where thousands of foreign tourists flock here every month. Of course, at the time, a huge number of hotels were built on the coast. And one of the popular holiday destinations is the Ocean Varadero El Patriarca 5 * hotel.

What does the hotel look like and what quality of accommodation can you offer here? Is the hotel suitable for families with children? Can you count on good nutrition and entertainment? What impressions did tourists have already managed to spend here a few days?

Where to look for a hotel? Location Features





In fact, the Ocean Varadero El Patriarca 5 * hotel stands out among others precisely in a convenient location. It is located almost in the center of the famous Varahicacos nature reserve. The distance to the beach can be overcome in a few minutes of leisurely walking. Within walking distance are shops, shopping centers, sports complexes and even a golf club. Just 2 km away is Gaviota, a marina.

By the way, the distance to the center of Varadera is 12 km. This distance can be overcome using special tourist transport, the schedule of which can be found at the reception. The international airport is 34 km from the hotel grounds, so do not worry about a long trip.

Hotel Ocean Varadero El Patriarca 5 *: photo and room information





The hotel is rightfully considered large - on its territory there are 420 spacious, comfortable rooms with a modern renovation and a pleasant design. Naturally, the room has a covered terrace or balcony, from where you can enjoy views of the pool, garden or sea shore. The rooms are fully furnished - you are waiting for a large bed, wardrobe, bedside tables and upholstered furniture, even a comfortable desk.

Also, guests are provided with a set of household appliances. There is a telephone, a TV (you can watch some satellite channels), an electronic safe, as well as a fan and air conditioning. At your service - a roomy refrigerator and coffee maker (a set of necessary utensils is included). Iron and ironing board can be delivered to the room upon request.

The bathroom is very spacious. There is not only a bathtub where you can relax in the foamy water, but also a separate shower. Also, guests are provided with branded bathrobes, slippers, a set of towels and quality cosmetics.

How do hotel guests eat? Bars and Restaurants





Of course, when traveling to exotic countries, I want to please myself with delicious food. The hotel provides its guests with good food. Breakfasts, lunches and dinners are held in the form of a buffet - you yourself are free to choose your own food to your liking. By the way, guests have the opportunity to enjoy international, Italian and Cuban dishes. Of course, the restaurant serves drinks, desserts, ice cream and fresh pastries.

Also on the territory there are several cafes and bars, where at any time of the day you can order a cocktail and even a snack. According to reviews, there are very good alcoholic drinks, especially Caribbean rum, which is worth at least one try.

Are there any amenities on the beach?





Is it possible to count on a quality beach vacation for a guest at the Ocean Varadero El Patriarca 5 * hotel? Cuba, Varadero is famous for its picturesque sandy beaches. And one of them is located just 100 meters from the hotel grounds. According to reviews, it is rarely possible to observe a strong concentration of tourists, so you can always find a secluded place. The tides are not very pronounced, so you can always swim.

The coast is covered with soft sand, and the entrance to the sea is gentle and comfortable. Garbage and algae are cleaned daily. At your service - comfortable sunbeds with soft mattresses, located under thatched sun umbrellas. This place was created for a leisurely and enjoyable vacation.

Rest by the water: how to have fun?

If bathing and sunbathing over time make you bored, you can contact the sports recreation center located here on the beach. For a reasonable price, you can rent a boat or other non-motorized water transport. Also, travelers like to ride boats, jet skis, bananas. Parasailing and water skiing will provide a lot of vivid impressions.

The underwater world is bright and rich. Swimming with a mask, you can enjoy the bright warm-water fish and other inhabitants of the deep sea. And there is also the opportunity to try diving - tourists take training courses here. Soft waves are great for travelers looking to windsurf.

Do tourists offer additional services?





The owners and employees of Ocean Varadero El Patriarca 5 are trying to save their guests from small household problems. To begin with, it is worth saying that here at any time you can exchange currency for local bills. Laundry facilities are also available. There are several shops selling various household items and traditional souvenirs. In case of health problems you can count on the help of a qualified doctor.

In the city, as well as in the hotel itself, it is easy to rent a bicycle, car or other means of transport. This service is used by many people who plan to travel around the county. The hotel has a spacious parking lot with 24-hour security, where you can leave free transport.

As you know, in Cuba there is a problem with the Internet. Nevertheless, access to it at the hotel is, however, only in the lobby, and this service is paid separately. At your service is a spacious conference room, which is used for various events, ranging from business meetings to celebrations. By the way, a hotel often becomes a venue for weddings, because what could be better than a ceremony on the picturesque Caribbean coast?

Entertainment at the hotel: how do tourists spend their leisure time?





Despite the fact that most travelers arrive in Varadero, wanting to have fun on the beach, the hotel also tried to create conditions for an interesting, eventful leisure.

In the courtyard there is a large pool - this is a kind of center of public life of the hotel. Here people bathe, relax, get acquainted and communicate with each other. For a small fee, you can visit the sauna or relax your muscles in the large jacuzzi. The spa offers a large selection of different treatments - you can improve your body, improve your appearance and just relax. Experienced, professional masseurs offer their services here.

The hotel has several tennis courts, and nearby (5 km) - large golf courses. There is also a fitness center with a gym. Instructors often organize some kind of group sports classes. And, of course, at the hotel you can take some lessons of passionate Latin American dances.

In the evening, you can demonstrate your skills on the dance floor of the disco - admission is free, but drinks are not included in the price. The guests have a great time here, having fun under the fiery Cuban tunes. The local amphitheater regularly hosts shows and shows worth seeing. And you can reap while enjoying live music from local bands. By the way, the animation works constantly here - in the daytime the guys organize team games and fun contests, and in the evenings they invite everyone to dance.

Entertainment can be found outside the territory of Ocean Varadero El Patriarca 5 *. Cuba is an amazing place to explore. In the hotel or in the city you can easily pick up and purchase an interesting tour. Many tourists spend at least a day in Havana, because the capital of Cuba is a unique city with rich traditions and hundreds of opportunities to have fun.

Family holidays: comfort and fun for children





If you are going on a trip with a child, then surely the foreground questions for you about conveniences specifically for your child. So is it worth taking children with you when going to the Ocean Varadero El Patriarca 5 * hotel?

Varadero is a wonderful place full of entertainment and fun for tourists of any age. And at the hotel they tried to make families with children as comfortable as possible. You can count on an extra bed in the room (it is folding, but comfortable), as well as a chair for feeding in the restaurant (for the youngest guests). The menu always contains simple but healthy dishes that are suitable for the child.

As for entertainment, a special shallow pool with a heating system has been built for children. There is an outdoor area with slides, swings and other attractions. Young children love spending time in a mini-club, where experienced animators and educators organize their leisure time. A club for teenagers (over 13 years old) also works during the season, where children can get to know each other, practice dance and art, and even learn Spanish.
