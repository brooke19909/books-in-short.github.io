General description of the CTK line

This line includes both advanced models and beginner level. Basically, almost all products with the CTK index are intended for beginners or musicians with average skills. Specifications

This model is a synthesizer that has 49 buttons. 100 tones integrated. Polyphony is designed for 12 notes. For some timbres, only 6 of them are suitable.

What about auto accompaniment, the Casio CTK-240 has about 100 rhythmic patterns. The mode of this function can be selected. There are only 2. There are two controllers. The volume is designed for 10 steps. 50 melodies are built into the internal memory, there are only three controllers for them.

The display is installed. It displays the playback tone, melody, name or song number that sounds from the built-in memory, as well as the keyboard settings used by the user.

What about the tempo, the display shows its value, the work of the metronome, the synchronization process. In addition, the name of the chord may be displayed on the screen. Transpose has 12 steps. The volume of the Casio CTK-240 is adjustable from 0 to 9 levels. The ability to connect headphones using the standard stereo jack is available. The resistance is 78 ohms, the voltage does not exceed 2 watts. There are two options for power, we are talking about the battery and mains operation. The battery can give the opportunity to interact with the synthesizer for no more than 6 hours in a row. Six AA-type zinc batteries are installed. The tool turns off automatically after 6 minutes of inactivity. Without a battery, the device weighs about 3 kg.
