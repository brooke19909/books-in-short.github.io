Hotel H2O, Vityazevo: reviews, photos, description, how to get

Anapa has long attracted the attention of tourists from all over Russia and beyond. Here is a very large selection of hotels with different levels of comfort. Service is gradually moving to a new level and thereby attracting new vacationers. Hotel H2o in Vityazevo (Anapa) is one of the institutions that are developing more and more every year and are waiting for new tourists.

Where is it and how to get there?

The hotel is located in the resort village of Vityazevo. It is 11 km from Anapa. The exact address of the H2o Hotel in Vityazevo is: Beautiful Avenue 18. It can be reached from the airport or Anapa railway station by shuttle bus or book a transfer. The hotel provides this service for a separate payment in both directions. Check-in time is 14.00.

Description

Hotel H2o in Vityazevo has an attractive exterior for tourists. The building was completely completed in 2017, all the furniture and decor are still new. The territory of the complex is quite spacious.





Near the hotel can accommodate no more than 5 cars. Other guests can park their cars in a paid car park located in the immediate vicinity of the H2o Hotel. Responses of vacationers indicate that security is on duty around the clock, and the cars are fully protected.

On the ground floor there is a reception desk. Her employees are here around the clock. They are always ready to help vacationers on everyday issues and can tell how to get from the H2o Hotel in Vityazevo to any entertainment center or to Anapa. The reception area has large blue sofas where guests can relax and chat with their neighbors. The building also has a modern elevator, which operates around the clock.

Hotel H2o in Vityazevo: room description

The hotel offers a variety of rooms with different levels of comfort:

  • Deluxe for 4 people - a room in which there is one large double bed and two single beds, a wardrobe, a desk, a small sofa, cable TV, a refrigerator, a toilet and shower, a balcony with sea or pool views, chairs and a table on the balcony .
  • Deluxe for 3 people - a spacious room with one large double bed and one single. There is a wardrobe, cable TV, refrigerator, toilet and shower, a balcony with sea or pool views.
  • Large double room, which has one double bed, wardrobe, TV with cable channels, refrigerator, toilet and shower, balcony.




All rooms are air-conditioned and guests can use for free:

  • a hairdryer;
  • The Internet;
  • phone (calls only within the country);
  • mini-bar.

All rooms are decorated in a restrained modern style in beige tones. The pillows and mattresses in the rooms are not feather and are suitable for people with allergies.

Hotel service

In the H2o hotel (Vityazevo), according to 2017 reviews, tourist services are at a very high level. Hotel guests note that the staff is friendly and friendly. By the time of arrival, rooms are already prepared, and check-in takes no more than 10 minutes. The rooms are always clean, daily cleaning. Bed linen is changed every three days. For families with small children, a special cot with sides is provided upon request. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms or in the hotel building.

Nutrition

The price does not include meals. The hotel has a modern dining room, which operates on a buffet basis. It is decorated in a modern style and looks like a small restaurant. Here, for each meal at the counter, a sufficient variety of dishes is offered. According to reviews of guests of the H2o hotel in Vityazevo, the food in the dining room is very tasty, and the menu is quite varied.





In the hall there is always an electric kettle with boiled water. Moms often use it to prepare meals for young children. The menu is designed in such a way that children from 1 year old can eat here. There are special high chairs for babies in the dining room.

At the food counter, fruits and vegetables are always laid out for each meal. A variety of desserts are also available. Visitors can take portions in the required quantity. According to reviews, in the H2o hotel in Vityazevo in 2017, the average cost of lunch for 3 people was 500 rubles, and breakfast - 250-300. Three meals a day for a family cost vacationers 1000-1200 rubles.

Hotel Territory

Around the hotel is always clean, garbage is taken out several times a day. The territory is lined with special paving tiles. It is divided into small flower beds in which ornamental bushes and flowers are grown. It has an outdoor terrace. It has tables and modern benches. Hotel guests can stay here while eating or just relax after relaxing on the beach or swimming in the pool. The terrace has a canopy that protects vacationers from the scorching sun on a hot day or from rain - on a cloudy day.





Also on the territory of the hotel there is a special zone for tourists who like to sunbathe. It has modern sun loungers in several rows. Barbecue lovers can rent a gazebo with all the necessary barbecue equipment for an additional fee. It is located in a separate area, and smoke does not interfere with other vacationers.

Beach

The hotel is located on the first coastline in the resort village. A decorated sidewalk leads to the beach; a leisurely step takes no more than 5-7 minutes. According to tourists reviews of the H2o hotel in Vityazevo, even with young children, the path to the beach takes no more than 10-15 minutes. Along the way, there are many outlets where you can buy fresh fruits, souvenirs and small things necessary for a good rest. The beach has a sandy beach. It has a variety of entertainment:

  • inflatable slides;
  • trampolines;
  • riding points on the "banana" and "cheesecake";
  • shooting ranges;
  • small cafes.

A rescue service works around the clock. Changing cabins are also installed.

Pool

The hotel has a large swimming pool. It has a special children's area. It has a shallow depth, so parents may not worry about the safety of their children. The pool has an impressive size, so adults can freely swim in it. Water in it passes through several stages of purification through special multi-level filters.





Around the pool laid a special coating that does not slip. Thanks to this, hotel guests can not be afraid of falls after swimming. Around are comfortable sun loungers that do not absorb moisture and remain dry.

Area for children

Families with children of different ages often come to the hotel, so the administration worried about their leisure. A playground has been built on the territory of the complex. It has slides, swings, carousels, ladders. It is made of material safe for children and decorated with bright colors. There are benches near the playground where adults can relax. The play area is built far from the pool to ensure the safety of the children. Animators work with guests three times a week. They organize contests and shows. An entertainment program is provided for tourists of different ages.

Entertainment

The hotel is 5 minutes away from a variety of entertainment centers. In the resort village you can visit:

  • dolphinarium;
  • aquarium
  • amusement park;
  • the embankment.




Near the H2o Hotel in Vityazevo, the photo of which is presented in the article, there is a market and grocery stores. Also in this area are several pharmacies and Sberbank. The hotel has a bar. In it, guests of the complex can sit in a cozy company in the evening and enjoy the taste of wine or beer. The bar serves a variety of snacks, plays beautiful music.

Hotel H2o in Vityazevo: reviews

Despite the fact that the hotel only accepts guests for the first season, there are a lot of statements about its work on the Internet. Most often there are reviews in which visitors note perfect cleanliness. Due to the fact that the hotel is new, tourists note that the rooms still retain the perfect atmosphere. The furniture and communications are in excellent condition. The guests of the complex note that the settlement process takes place as quickly as possible, the rooms are prepared in advance for the arrival of visitors.

The staff is very friendly to the residents of the hotel and tries to solve any problems that arise very quickly. Professional chefs also work in the dining room, monitoring the freshness of the products from which the dishes are prepared. According to reviews, vacationers in the dining room are deliciously fed, and a variety of dishes suits almost everyone. Parents are pleased that babies are provided with high chairs with safety belts.

Tourists note that the water in the pool is always clean, and the sun loungers around it are enough for everyone. Also, almost everyone is satisfied with the location of the hotel. Next to it are all the necessary establishments and shops. Also nearby are entertainment centers. In addition, guests are comfortable with soundproofing in the rooms. They claim that audibility is almost zero. Tourists are also pleased that the territory of the complex is completely fenced.



Despite all the pluses, on various resources on the Internet you can find some comments about the arrangement of the hotel and the service in it. Most often, guests are unhappy with the lack of their own parking in the complex. Many visiting tourists using their own vehicles have to use the services of paid parking, which is located near the hotel.

Also, guests note that the balconies are not closed by a partition, which is not very convenient if vacationers want to retire on a warm evening with a glass of wine and do not want other residents of the complex to watch them. Some guests are not happy that only one room key is issued. They also note that there is no table and chairs in the room, so they had to be brought in from the balcony, which is not very convenient to do daily.

Guests also note that access to the sea lies through the parking lot, and in order to get to the beach, you have to step over the barriers. Even vacationers focus on the fact that the separation of zones in the hotel was made irrationally. Places for tourists from certain sanatoriums are fenced off by an ugly and rough fence.

The administration takes into account all the comments and wishes of vacationers and will try to respond to all negative reviews. Due to the fact that the hotel worked the first season, certain shortcomings appeared in the organization of a comfortable stay, which promise to completely eliminate by the next season.
