The year-round recreation center "Gremyachevo" (Cheboksary) is located near the shore of the beautiful Volga. This is a very popular recreation area among locals who like to take a steam bath, swim in the river or ride a quad bike in the picturesque surroundings.

Features of the recreation center "Gremyachevo" in Cheboksary

Tourists are attracted not only by beautiful nature, comfortable accommodation and excellent service, but also by transport accessibility. The recreation center "Gremyachevo" (Cheboksary) is located in the suburban area. It is easily accessible by public transport, buses and taxis regularly go all year.





The full-flowing Volga is only 10-15 minutes of a quiet walking step. Here you can not only swim, but also fish.

Infrastructure and leisure

The territory of the recreation center "Gremyachevo" in Cheboksary is very neat and well-groomed. To accommodate tourists there are two-story comfortable cottages made of natural wood. Near each house there are wonderful gazebos (open and insulated) with barbecue facilities. "Gremyachevo" in Cheboksary is famous for Russian baths, in which guests like to bathe with a broom.

Cottages can accommodate up to 15 vacationers, they have air conditioning and heating systems, televisions, refrigerators and other appliances, modern, comfortable furniture, bathrooms. The interior is decorated in Russian traditional style. Zoning to bedrooms, living rooms and kitchen rooms is very convenient for tourists.





Meals are provided in a cozy cafe. Here you can hold corporate events, banquets, any holidays and celebrations. You can brighten up your leisure time in the billiard room. Fun companies gather in the evenings to soft music in the gazebos, grill kebabs and have a great time.
