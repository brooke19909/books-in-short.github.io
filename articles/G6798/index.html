How to photograph children at home, at school and in nature? Children's photo shoot

A children's photo shoot is a laborious and very difficult job. If an adult can be explained what and how to do, then working with children is quite difficult. If a newborn and a baby up to a year can be laid and set as the photographer needs, then this will not work with an older child.

However, young children can be fascinated by something interesting and make the time of the photo shoot simply unforgettable. From the very birth of a child, parents take pictures of literally every step, however, it is professional images that are obtained in high quality and unusual. There are many places for a children's photo shoot, so if you want, you can turn all the most daring ideas into reality.

Photo session planning

The question of how to photograph children is of interest to so many parents who want to get very interesting and original pictures. Equipment preparation largely depends on where the shooting will take place. The frames are very interesting:

  • outside;
  • at home;
  • in a studio;
  • in kindergarten or school.

If shooting is done at school, at home or in the studio, then you need a wide-angle lens. In addition, the room will require additional lighting, without which even the most powerful optics will not provide high-quality images. It is worth considering that the simplest soap dishes are simply unsuitable for holding a photo shoot at home. Such cameras will deprive photographs of artistry, and they will turn out too grainy. Using a SLR camera, it is worth using an external flash and additional accessories for light reflection.

When conducting a photo shoot in nature, you do not need to use portable lighting, but you should still take a reflector with you, since when working with sunlight it will turn out to be a necessary accessory.

Preparing for a photo shoot

Many do not know at all how to take pictures of children so that the pictures turn out to be bright, colorful and saturated. The main component of a successful photo shoot is a good mood, as well as the physical condition of the baby. It is necessary that the child feels well, sleeps and is full. However, shooting immediately after eating is not recommended.

It is important to understand exactly how to photograph children for group shots and how to take an individual photo. If the photo shoot is held in a school or kindergarten, it is advisable to plan its holding in the first half of the day. With individual shooting, you need to adapt to the characteristics of the child. First of all, the baby should be comfortable so that he is always in a relaxed state.

It is best if the photo session is held at home, on the playground, in kindergarten, as well as in another familiar place. If this happens in an unfamiliar place, then it is best to give the child time to get comfortable and get used to it.

Parents must prepare in advance for the arrival of a child photographer. They can pick up costumes and props. In addition, you need to prepare the room, remove unnecessary things so that they do not interfere and do not distract attention.

In order for the child to show the necessary emotions, you should talk with him during the shooting. It is important that the baby behaves naturally, so do not force him to pose if he does not want to. It is advisable to go to an event with the baby before holding a photo shoot, and ask him to share his impressions during the shooting. Guaranteed to be a lot of emotions.

Home photography

It is important to understand exactly how to photograph children at home in order to get really high-quality and original shots. It is at home that the baby can relax and behave naturally. You can capture his games with your favorite toys, catching the sincere emotions and smile of the baby. The most important thing is that the child has a good mood and he perceives everything that happens as a game. The next day, you can invite several friends of the same age and watch them.

How to photograph children up to a year? It is safe to say that it is best to do this at home. You can take some pictures with your parents, as the result is a very interesting and touching selfie. Ideas for a home photo shoot can be very different. From the things of newborns, you can create the most extraordinary images.

A no-lose option is balloons, which are appropriate both at home and in nature. They personify joy, celebration, fun. You can have the child throw them up or directly into the lens.

A good occasion for taking pictures of children at home will be the birthday of the baby. In this case, you do not need to invent anything, you just need to dress the baby in elegant clothes and put a cake with candles in front of him. In addition, you can invite animators to the celebration, and the celebration will be simply unforgettable.

Professional studio shooting

A child photographer can offer many ideas for a photo shoot in the studio. Here you can make very high-quality and original family and portrait shots. The most difficult thing in the studio is for children aged 1-3 years. Around everything else, the baby does not want to become or sit in the right position. In this case, it is advisable to prepare in advance to understand exactly how to photograph children in the studio.

Initially, you need to get acquainted with the situation, the photographer, so that the baby is a little used. With older children and adolescents, everything is much simpler. Parents should prepare their child for the upcoming shoot. The photographer should also try to find a common language with him, to win over him, since children are very distrustful of strangers.

Ideas for a photo shoot can be very different. Children are very fond of imitating adults and trying on their images. In addition, stylized images look unusual. In specially selected costumes, it is very simple to create the necessary poses for the camera.

Parent Tips

Very often, parents are very nervous before a photo shoot with their child. For all questions of interest, you should definitely consult a photographer to know how to behave properly. Photography must be vibrant, vibrant and vibrant. It is necessary to convey all the feelings, emotions and feelings.

Children do not know how to pretend, so they behave in front of the camera as naturally as possible. This can be a minus and a plus. Since the baby does not hide his emotions, he may be shy, be wary or scared.

No need to perform sudden movements and force the child to do what he does not want, because as a result, you can simply lose time and not achieve anything good. If the child snuggles up to the parents, then you can start with a family photo shoot until the baby calms down. In addition, such photographs demonstrate love and trust.

It is best to start shooting a child using telephoto lenses. The minimum distance should be 3 m. This will allow you to smoothly enter the process, and the child experiences much less.

School photo shoot

It is very important to understand how to photograph children in school so that the graduation album is very beautiful, original and colorful. First you need to conduct thorough preparation, choose a place, costumes, as well as the most successful poses. Before holding a photo shoot, children should have a good night's sleep, prepare shoes and clothes, and also make a hairstyle.

Do not be afraid of the camera and be shy, you need to be as free as possible. Before taking pictures, you can rehearse several poses and choose the most successful ones. During individual shooting, you need to communicate with the photographer, not be afraid to ask him questions.

It is important to show all your imagination and creativity. If you have favorite things, then you definitely need to take them with you to a photo shoot, this will allow you to make some successful shots. The more creativity there will be in the pictures, the brighter and more interesting the album will look.

Photo processing

During the photo shoot, you need to try to take as many frames as possible so that you can then choose the most successful photos. You need to constantly change the proximity of the plan and angles, experiment and play with the baby during work.

When processing the resulting images, it is important not to overdo it with retouching. Usually the baby's skin is light and clean, so an extra correction will give it some pallor. When processing photos, you need to select a shade that will look most advantageous.
