The recreation center "Aquamarine". Rest in the Northern Black Sea Region (Ukraine)

The infrastructure of beach holidays in the Ukrainian Black Sea coast is developing rapidly. Every year there are new pensions and private hotels. And the old holiday houses of the times of the USSR undergo a complete reconstruction. This booming growth of private resort hotels and bases is sometimes confusing. There are several boarding houses with the same name. This article is intended to clarify the confusion with a recreation center called Aquamarine. One of them is located in the Nikolaev region, in the city of Koblevo. Also in large numbers offers Odessa Inflow recreation. Aquamarine is one of them. This is a very good name for resort hotels. We will talk about the conditions at both bases - in Odessa and Nikolaev regions.

Conditions for relaxation in the "Aquamarine" in Zatoka

The boarding house consists of two three-story buildings. They are new (2007 and 2015 built). In these buildings there are numbers of different price segments. Economy rooms are designed for three or four people. They are equipped with a fan, refrigerator, TV. The bathroom is in the building. Standard rooms are air-conditioned. They adjoin a cozy balcony with furniture or a small terrace. The recreation center "Aquamarine" has a swimming pool on its territory, which is heated in May and June. So if the Black Sea in the beginning of summer will not please you with warmth, rest will not be spoiled. Another feature of Aquamarine is free Wi-Fi, covering the entire territory of the boarding house. There is a guarded parking lot. The boarding house has a flexible system of discounts. For a child under five, you need to pay only 70 hryvnia (about 200 rubles) per day. The location of the recreation center in the center of the village makes it possible to visit the grocery and clothing markets, as well as shops and restaurants. You can also agree on food at the recreation center. You can order three meals, breakfast or half board.





Reviews

About the recreation center "Aquamarine" there are only positive reviews. The territory is small but well-groomed. The cafe is tasty and of high quality. Full board available. In other cafes, meals will cost more. Those who rested in "Aquamarine" are advised to book standard rooms. Many tourists noted the cleanliness of the pool. The Internet is fast, you can chat with relatives and friends on Skype. There is a playground equipped with swings and slides. In general, this is a good place for a family budget vacation.





The recreation center "Aquamarine" (Koblevo)

This resort is located on the very border of the Nikolaev and Odessa regions. Pension "Aquamarine" is located in the Moldavian part of the resort of Koblevo, on Chisinau street. From the sea - on the second line. Nearby is the famous water park. The only minus of Koblevo as a resort is the distance from the highway. You need to go to the bus station on the highway "Odessa-Nikolaev", and there change trains to the local minibus. At the height of the summer season, the Moldavian part of Koblevo (the one to the east, closer to the estuary) turns into a bustling resort. But all the fun is concentrated on the waterfront. The second line, where the Aquamarine recreation center is located, pleases tired tourists with silence. Koblevo offers a lot of entertainment. In addition to the water park, there are water slides directly on the public beach. There is a travel agency where you can order excursions to Odessa, Nikolaev Zoo and other interesting places in two regions of Ukraine.





Conditions at the boarding house "Aquamarine" in Koblevo

A large landscaped area is occupied by several buildings. This is a three-story building with fifty rooms, inherited from the Soviet Union, three houses (25 rooms) and a new hotel (12 superior rooms). The rooms for guests are divided into categories: economy (bathroom on the floor), standard, one-room junior suite and two-room suite. The recreation center "Aquamarine" on its territory has a playground, gazebos. Vacationers can use the barbecue for free. Internet is freely available at the reception and in some rooms. This guesthouse is located on the second line from the sea. You need to walk about a hundred and twenty meters to the sandy beach. Prices in a boarding house depend not only on the room category, but also on the season. Guests "Aquamarine" in Koblevo begins to receive from the first of June. Throughout the first month of summer, the lowest prices are held. So, a place in an economy room costs only five dollars. At the peak of the season (second half of July and August), the double standard is already estimated at $ 50.
