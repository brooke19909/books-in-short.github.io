Ski complex "Fedotovo": photos, reviews, address

With the onset of winter, many go to ski resorts. Outdoor activities are becoming increasingly popular. It is not necessary to leave Russia for skiing and sleigh rides; there are many beautiful resorts with picturesque natural resources and a wide selection of slopes and slopes in the vast country.

We present to your attention the modern ski complex "Fedotovo", located in the Republic of Tatarstan, the large industrial city of Naberezhnye Chelny (5-7 km from Nizhnekamsk Airport). Rest at the base will allow you to immerse yourself in a fabulous atmosphere, leaving the city bustle, plenty to enjoy high-speed skiing on winding snowy hills. You will experience tremendous pleasure from the holidays spent in this place.





It is an indisputable fact that active outdoor activities, especially skiing and ice skating, have a healing effect on the whole body. This type of relaxation fills with energy, energizes and prolongs youth. Such sorties help strengthen the spine, learn to maintain balance, improve the activity of the vestibular apparatus. When skiing, you establish coordination of movement, develop plasticity and acquire a certain grace. And finally, it is useful for blood circulation.

A few words about KFOR

Having gone to the sports and business complex "Fedotovo", which is conveniently located in the village of the same name, you will discover opportunities not only to ski, snowboard and sled, but also to walk in the local surroundings, breathe in the fresh mountain air, take a steam bath and try national delicacies.

The resort area is absolutely safe, so whole families come here with small children to get vivid emotions from winter entertainment. At a high level, business tourism is organized here. Comfortable conditions have been created for corporate events and celebrations.





Sports ski complex "Fedotovo": address of the base and how to get

Exact location: Zainsky district, village Fedotovo. You can get to the complex by your own transport, as well as by bus or taxi. From Nizhnekamsk airport or railway station you need to get to the city of Naberezhnye Chelny, from there on a weekend (Saturday, Sunday) there is a minibus to the destination. You can also rent a car, which will comfortably bring to the Fedotovo ski resort. How to get there by car? You must follow the highway towards Naberezhnye Chelny, the pointer will lead to the base.





About the ski slopes

Highly qualified instructors work at KFOR, ready to come to the rescue, to teach beginners all the basics of skiing. Professionals will also not be left without attention, they can always get advice on issues of interest, thereby increasing their level. On the territory there are five slopes of varying complexity and colors. There are modern tracks for snowboarding and snow tubing (inflatable rubber tires).





If you are at a loss in choosing a descent, you can contact the staff. It‚Äôs safe to ride in the evening, as lighting fixtures protected by fences are installed everywhere. To maintain the snow cover, special guns are used. Two tow lifts work at the base : for beginners and children.

Everyone will be delivered without problems to a gentle hill, from which a breathtaking panorama of the picturesque landscapes opens. The slides here are even, safe even for young children. There are no dangerous obstacles and winding slopes, so it will be easy for beginners to master this sport.

Ski complex "Fedotovo" offers to feel like a child again, to experience a storm of adrenaline ride on a "cheesecake". Many tourists here organize real competitions and fight for the championship. For true extreme sports and professional skiers built high slopes with intricate slopes.





What else to do at the base?

In their free time, guests can play at the pool table. There is a tennis table, football, badminton and paintball fields. For athletes open a fitness center with modern fitness equipment. A real steam room (sauna) will help to cleanse your body and relax.





If you want to please the kids - ride them on a pony or an adult horse. Communication with these good-natured animals leaves a positive impression, calms and pacifies. Riding lessons for adults are provided.





Catering

The Fedotovo ski resort (photos are illustrated by the services provided here) has its own restaurant and cafe. After outdoor activities, you will undoubtedly have a brutal appetite, and the culinary masters working in these institutions will be able to extinguish it. The menu will enchant you with a wealth of choices. Here you can find a variety of dishes of national cuisine and European delicacies.





Fans of elite alcoholic beverages will be delighted with the wide range of wines and other alcohol. Brazier platforms with the necessary equipment for rent for those who wish to cook meat, fish or vegetables at the stake with their own hands. All arbors have electricity, they are located under the roof, equipped with comfortable benches and tables. Even in bad weather, tourists will be comfortable and warm.

For corporate parties, business weekends and holidays

The ski complex "Fedotovo" accepts orders for various events from individuals and legal entities. Businessmen can use a cozy conference room (for 60 seats) and profitably present their goods or arrange negotiations at a high level. For large companies, they will offer a banquet room, a hall in a restaurant or an indoor tent.





Experienced base staff will create a real celebration for you with music and dancing. Chefs will create a harmonious and delicious menu. A country celebration and a corporate party are the best alternative to a classic vacation in an urban setting. Rental prices are acceptable and affordable for different social groups.

Accommodation offer

Tourists who come to the Fedotovo ski resort (Naberezhnye Chelny) will be offered to stay in a comfortable hotel. Those choosing luxury apartments and suites can count on a hot breakfast. Available and economical options for two people with beds and bedside tables, a shared bathroom.

All rooms are pleasantly designed to please the eye. They include a TV, a landline telephone, air conditioning and household appliances (except for the "economy" category). Daily housekeeping and maintenance are provided.





Infrastructure

High-speed Internet is available in the entire hotel - use of the network is free of charge. There are ATMs and payment acceptance terminals right in the lobby. It should be noted that the Fedotovo ski resort is under protection, private parking is provided for tourists' cars (free of charge). There is a rental point and a workshop for sports equipment.
