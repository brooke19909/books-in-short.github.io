Hotel "Cardinal", Lazarevskoye: description and reviews

Sochi has recently been choosing an increasing number of domestic tourists for summer vacations. The infrastructure in this southern city is well developed, and you can spend your vacation here comfortably. One of the most popular tourist areas of Greater Sochi is Lazarevskoye. Here, vacationers are waiting for clean beaches, a warm sea, magnificent nature and, of course, comfortable accommodation. Tourists can rent a room in Lazarevsky, for example, at the Cardinal Hotel. This three-star hotel has rightfully earned excellent reviews from its guests.

Location Features

The Hotel Cardinal (Lazarevskoye) is located 2.4 km from the center of the village. You can get to him from Sochi by minibus. Many tourists come here by train. The hotel is located in the immediate vicinity of the station. The total distance from Sochi (Adler) Airport to Lazarevsky is 72 km. If desired, tourists have the opportunity to book a transfer directly to the Cardinal Hotel.





The beach from this hotel is within walking distance (260 m). Tourists certainly find this location of the hotel very convenient. Her exact address: Sochi, Lazarevskoye, Hotel Cardinal, st. Victory, 153.

The Hotel Cardinal is a new six-story building with a modern attractive design. Just come to Lazarevskoye and rent a room in this hotel will not work. Room must be booked in advance. In this case, the prepayment is stipulated by the rules. Money should be transferred to the future hotel guest to the bank account of the hotel. You can book a room, for example, by phone of the hotel +7 (918) 001 9898. Some tourists book rooms in this hotel and through specialized sites. In this case, immediately after booking, the administration usually contacts the potential customer by phone and provides him with all the necessary information.

The Cardinal Hotel 3 * (Lazarevskoye) offers its guests a variety of rooms for every income. Unfortunately, there are no single rooms in this hotel. But tourists can always rent a double or quadruple. The hotel rents standard, deluxe and deluxe rooms. Also, if you wish, you can rent a special room with a king-size bed (very wide and comfortable) and a balcony.

The bathroom in each room has a separate. Here, for the convenience of tourists, toiletries and a hairdryer are provided. Judging by the reviews of the guests, the rooms at the Cardinal Hotel are simply well equipped. The furniture is brand new, and household appliances and sockets are working properly.





Cost of living

Hotel "Cardinal" (Lazarevskoye) holidaymakers deserved good reviews. Spending a vacation here is relatively comfortable. In addition, the cost of living in this hotel is quite inexpensive. For renting a standard room (from 2 seats), they take about 5,000 rubles, for three days or so - 1700-2700 per day. Luxury apartments, of course, are more expensive. For such a room you will need to pay about 6000 (per night). Rooms with a special large bed cost about 4000 rubles. per day.

Accommodations

Children at the Hotel Cardinal (Lazarevskoye) are accepted at any age. Parents can be provided with a cot upon request. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. One of the undoubted advantages of this hotel is that it is conveniently located for people with disabilities. They have special rooms. Non-smoking rooms are also available. Check-in at this hotel is at 14.00, check-out is at 12.00.
