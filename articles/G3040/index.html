Hotel "Slavic Radisson" (Radisson Slavyanskaya) and a business center: address, reviews. Radisson Slavyanskaya Hotel (Moscow): how to get there

Hotel "Slavic Radisson" is one of the favorite places for businessmen who arrive in Moscow for business purposes. And in view of the incredibly convenient location, tourists and travelers also stop here quite often.





Brief description of the hotel

Hotel "Slavic Radisson" is one of the best accommodation options in the capital. The building is located on the banks of the Moskva River, and its windows offer a beautiful city panorama. A pleasant moment is also the availability of a wide range of amenities, among which the most popular are such as a swimming pool, a beauty center, as well as a gym and wireless Internet.

Special attention should be paid to the guest apartments, which the Slavyanskaya Radisson hotel offers its guests. All rooms are quite spacious, and large windows let in a lot of sunlight. The traditional classic style and warm colors in the design will give the atmosphere of home comfort, and modern amenities will provide absolute comfort.

Hotel "Slavic Radisson" can be proud of its restaurant, which serves the best dishes from different regions of Italy. Also here you can visit an institution that specializes in Japanese cuisine. In addition, the hotel has numerous shops where you can buy souvenirs, clothes, groceries and much more.





Hotel "Radisson Slavyanskaya": address

This hotel is located in the central part of the city. Hotel address - Moscow, pl. Europe, 2. The hotel windows overlook the river, as well as the building of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Very close is the metro station, as well as the Kiev railway station. As for international airports, the closest is Vnukovo (25 km), but Sheremetyevo and Domodedovo are removed, respectively, by 35 and 50 kilometers. Consulting services are provided by the Radisson Slavyanskaya Hotel (Moscow) to its guests. Hotel staff will explain how to get there if you call them upon arrival. From the station, the easiest way is to get to the metro, but from the airport you have to take a taxi or order a transfer.

Number of rooms

The following room categories are presented to your attention in this hotel:

  • The standard apartment is a spacious room of 25 square meters. m. Here you can stay on a double or a pair of one and a half beds. Within the framework of one room, there is a bedroom area, a work desk, and a small corner for relaxation.
  • Business class apartments are ideal for those guests who have arrived in the capital for business purposes. It has a full-fledged workplace, as well as the highest quality free Wi-Fi in these rooms. You can fully relax on a huge orthopedic bed.
  • Junior Suite is a large two-room room, which is often chosen for family living. The bedroom has a large bed and a small reading corner (with soft chairs and a floor lamp). In addition to upholstered furniture, the living room also has a dining table and a bar counter.
  • Suite is a luxury apartment, the design of which is dominated by warm golden shades. The living room is a relaxation area with a seating area, as well as a full-fledged study. The bathroom includes a spa bath.

Useful information about the hotel

If you have chosen this hotel for accommodation in the capital, check out some of the rules that govern accommodation:

  • The estimated hours associated with the registration of newly arrived guests, as well as the check-out from the hotel, are set at two in the afternoon and noon, respectively;
  • if a child under 12 years old does not need an extra bed, then there is no charge for his stay;
  • cots for babies up to two years old are provided free of charge;
  • an extra bed will cost you 1000 rubles for each day of stay;
  • pets are strictly prohibited in the hotel, and in case of violation of this rule, the administration has the right to refuse to check in;
  • guests have the opportunity to pay with a plastic card for services received at the hotel;
  • during registration, each guest is required to pay 3,000 rubles of deposit (possibly in non-cash form);
  • smoking is strictly prohibited throughout the hotel.

General impression

If you want to settle in a presentable institution in the center of Moscow, then the Slavyanskaya Radisson hotel is ideal for you. It is characterized by a convenient location, and also boasts an excellent business center. Of course, you can not ignore two wonderful restaurants that specialize in Japanese and Italian cuisines. You can admire the beautiful Moscow landscapes from your window, as well as take advantage of the huge number of services for recreation and entertainment.

Nevertheless, as already mentioned above, there were some shortcomings. It is worth noting, in particular, slightly outdated furniture and plumbing in the rooms.
