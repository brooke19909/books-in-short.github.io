Tomsk Region: attractions, historical places, interesting facts, excursions, beautiful nature and national tourism

The rich Tomsk land is a wonderful place with the majestic beauty of Siberian nature, historical places and monuments of wooden architecture. Having chosen this destination for rest outside the Urals, you will undoubtedly receive a lot of new impressions. In this article, we will consider some noteworthy sights of the Tomsk region. You will find a photo with a description below.

Siberian Botanical Garden

In the regional center, in the green grove adjacent to the state university, the main natural attraction of the Tomsk Region is located - the oldest botanical garden in Siberia, which has been pleasing visitors for more than a century. The territory of the garden includes greenhouse-greenhouse and picturesque park complexes. The garden collection includes over 6,000 species of various plants: from local to rare exotic and even listed in the Red Book. Spreading palm trees, dense creepers, evergreen cypresses, giant cacti, a variety of outlandish flowers - a real tropical oasis in the harsh climate of Siberia.

You can simply wander through the shady groomed alleys of the garden, looking at street expositions or order interesting excursions to greenhouses and enjoy the magnificent riot of colors. On the territory there are small ponds with improvised bridges, stone fences and various decorative elements that perfectly fit into the natural landscape. The Siberian Botanical Garden is an excellent place where you can plunge into the atmosphere of wildlife without leaving the borders of a noisy metropolis. Peace of mind, aesthetic pleasure and an unprecedented rise in energy after visiting the sights of the Tomsk region are guaranteed.

Merchant Mansion

The architectural heritage of Tomsk has a huge number of beautiful old buildings. One of them was built in 1904 by order of the Tomsk merchant Fleer. The Art Nouveau building stands out for its rich decor: moldings, an elegant bas-relief depicting a female face, openwork metal fencing of balconies, forged lattices. Today, in the mansion with a blue facade, the Wedding Palace is located.

Seversk Museum

Not far from Tomsk is the closed city of Seversk with a stele installed at the entrance, the top of which is crowned by a model of the atomic nucleus, which characterizes the city-forming enterprise. Previously, only the inhabitants and experts of the nuclear industry who conducted their research there knew about the existence of this city. Entry is restricted due to privacy and is only possible with a special pass.

Despite the fact that the town is relatively small and young, its sights in Seversk, Tomsk Oblast, are also available. The museum, which opened in 1987, is the center of the city, the largest among nuclear cities, since it houses the fourth part of the museum fund of the Russian Federation. The museum has more than 130 thousand memorabilia and photographs about the history of the city. Exhibits are combined in collections by direction. Items of archeology and ethnology, a valuable selection of awards and coins, and samples of applied art are presented. The Seversk Museum, in addition to excursions, attracts with general education programs and various master classes.

Thanks to sponsorship and assistance of residents who are not indifferent to the history and culture of their city, the museum celebrated its 30th anniversary.

Museum of Local Lore in Asino

There is Asino city with a local landmark, created in 1989, 100 km from the center of the Tomsk Region . This is a local history museum with a collection of over 13,000 exhibits characterizing the history and natural wealth of the native land. The exhibition ‚ÄúAsinovskaya Antiquity‚Äù shows clothes, household utensils, household items and tools of the peasants who once inhabited the village. There is an exposition dedicated to the life and work of the writer G.M. Markov, a native of the region. The town, which received its status only in the 70s, is known as the center of birch bark fishing. The museum works closely with craftsmen who exhibit their original works of wood and birch bark.

Cathedral

The architectural decoration and historical landmark of Tomsk is a beautiful, unique peach-colored structure - the Epiphany Cathedral. It was erected on the site of a small wooden church at the end of the 17th century. In 1804, it was in this temple that the formation of the Tomsk province was publicly announced . The main cathedral shrines - a particle of the relics of the great martyr Panteleimon and the Crucifixion with a tiny particle of the Cross, where Jesus was crucified. On holidays, shrines are taken out to the center of the temple for general worship.

Kirek Lake

The regional nature monument is a small beautiful lake an hour away from Tomsk. The mirror surface is abundantly dotted with white water lilies, it is simply impossible to take your eyes off the picturesque landscape. The pond is famous for its clear, warm water, a sandy bottom and a thickness of healing silt. In the coastal area there are picnic areas and a mud bath. Guests from all around come here to take a break from civilization.

Rural park

Not far from Tomsk, on the outskirts of the village of Zorkaltsevo, there is a place beloved by city residents of all ages - the Okolitsa park. An unusual attraction of the Tomsk region is located on the forest edge, surrounded by pines and birches, and occupies a site of 16 hectares.

Since 2013, the park has held the annual "Ax Festival" - an international festival of craftsmen of wooden art, who work tirelessly for three days and give ordinary logs a second life. Tens of thousands of guests from all over the Siberian region come to see this spectacle. Participants' works remain in the park and adorn its green territory. There are more and more mythical characters and heroes of famous fairy tales frozen in a tree from year to year.

In "Okolitsa" there is an extreme park, a trampoline, playgrounds, a mini-farm where pets live, which you can feed and pick up, gazebos for relaxing, a small hotel and parking.

Nature and fresh air are the best rest for the soul.
