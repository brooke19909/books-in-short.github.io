Refrigerator Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W: reviews, specifications, features of the model

A wide variety of modern refrigerator models presents consumers with a difficult choice. Often on the shelves of large retail outlets of household appliances there are several similar models in the same price category. But how to get right with the choice of a high-quality and reliable refrigerator? You should start by familiarizing yourself with the characteristics of the model, the opinions of specialists (sales consultants), and, if possible, with the feedback from customers who have already tested the device in operation.

In connection with the economic situation, many try to choose budget proposals, but it is worth remembering that savings should not be at the expense of quality. Many experts recommend paying attention to the Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W refrigerator, the price of which varies from 20,000 to 25,000 rubles, depending on the offer of retail chains. And if you take advantage of promotions or a bonus program, then you can buy it and cheaper.





The main characteristics of the model

The Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W two-chamber refrigerator is designed for the financial capabilities of the middle class, but it does not lose to other devices by similar characteristics. It performs its main functions perfectly. The freezer holds about 75 liters, which is suitable for everyday use. Enough for small frosts harvested in the summer. Of course, large-sized blanks are unlikely to be placed here. The entire volume can be evenly distributed across three drawers.

The refrigerator compartment is also divided into four compartments. There is an additional box for special storage of vegetables and fruits.



This is a great opportunity to protect products from foreign odors and keep them separate. In addition, a special egg stand is provided on the door, which also improves storage conditions. Thanks to the active air circulation, the space inside the refrigerator cools evenly.

Defrost system

Both the refrigerator and the freezer use the modern No Frost principle. According to many consumers, this is very convenient. You won‚Äôt have to defrost the Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W refrigerator yourself . It is enough to turn off the device once a year and just rinse it. Due to the jet supply of cold and actively circulating air, ice does not form on the walls.

However, it is worth remembering that in units whose refrigeration chambers are equipped with the No Frost system, products that are stored in open form are quickly ventilated. To avoid such situations, it is worth storing them in a sealed container or bags (in the case of vegetables and fruits that are stored outside a box specially provided for this).





Model benefits

The Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W refrigerator is an excellent choice among budget options. He will undoubtedly become the main au pair. In addition to an affordable price, the model has other advantages.

If you analyze the reviews available about the Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W refrigerator, you can highlight the following advantages:

  1. Convenient defrosting system No Frost in the lower and upper chambers. For many modern consumers, this is the main argument when choosing such a technique.
  2. Shelves are made of high quality impact resistant glass. There are four of them in the refrigerator compartment, two of them can be changed in height, which is quite convenient when using tall dishes.
  3. The Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W refrigerator has an attractive design without unnecessary details and electromechanical control. It is possible to adjust the temperature in the refrigerator, in the freezer a standard one is provided - minus 18 degrees. There is a beautiful bright backlight. Both mature people and children will be able to use the model, since there is nothing complicated in the management and operation.
  4. It copes with the main functions with high quality: it freezes quickly and well, it keeps products in good quality.

Are there any drawbacks to this model?

Also among the user reviews you can find the following complaints about the operation of the device of this model :

  1. It produces a large amount of noise during operation. Regarding this point, the reviews on the Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W refrigerator are contradictory. Regarding the noise level, the manufacturer announced a figure of 43 dB, which should correspond to comfortable operation for the consumer. In the event of a deficiency, experts recommend checking the correct installation of the device by level. If necessary, align by twisting the legs.
  2. Of the additional arsenal there is only superfrost. But, as practice shows, not every consumer needs additional functions.




As a conclusion, it can be noted that the Hotpoint-Ariston HF 4180 W refrigerator is a good option for value for money without unnecessary frills. An automatic defrosting system makes it easy to maintain, and an attractive design complements any interior.
