Hotel 3 * Natural Park Resort (Thailand / Pattaya): reviews, photos

Thailand is a great place to spend an exciting holiday. Thanks to the warm and humid climate, here you can plenty to swim in the sea. Travelers are given the opportunity to see many interesting places and get acquainted with the life of this amazing country. To make the holidays a truly good memory, you should pay attention to the 3 * Natural Park Resort hotel. Many vacationers speak very warmly about this place and would not mind returning here again.

Hotel location

On the picturesque coast of the Gulf of Thailand is a hotel 3 * Natural Park Resort. It is located on the outskirts of Pattaya, in its southern part. The international airport is 35 km, you can reach the hotel in just 10-20 minutes. Bangkok Airport is 135 km away. You will have to spend about two hours on the road from it to the hotel.

Pattaya city center is 7.5 km from the hotel. Tourists get there by public transport or taxi. According to many guests, you need to walk a bit to the bus stop. Public transport near the hotel does not stop. In some cases, it is possible to negotiate with the driver, and for a surcharge, he takes vacationers directly to the hotel.



The sea is 100 meters from the Natural Park Resort 3 * hotel (Pattaya). Jomtien Beach is a magnificent beach where guests can sunbathe. There is a very beautiful area, which has a pretty pleasant rest.

Description of the hotel complex

The hotel first opened its doors to guests in 1990. This is a complex consisting of separately located two- and three-story buildings. There is also a bungalow. All buildings are designed in national style. The hotel is very similar to a village in which its hectic life boils. The last renovation was carried out here in 2011.



The interior of the hotel is also designed in Thai style. Here, each decorative element reminds you of the guest of which country you are currently at. The hall is quite spacious, furnished with comfortable upholstered furniture.

Description of the Territory

Hotel 3 * Natural Park Resort has a small but very well-groomed area, which can be called its main attraction. Each house is simply immersed in greenery, very beautiful colors. Guests note that the flowers come from a stunning, truly magical fragrance. It feels especially good in the evenings.



Gardeners daily look after magnificent lawns and bushes, decorative trees. Thanks to their tireless work, impeccable cleanliness always reigns here. So that vacationers can enjoy the beauty of the local area, mushrooms in the form of tables are located throughout the garden. There are also gazebos. In these places for recreation there is a backlight, which allows guests to sit in the evening in a cozy atmosphere. Here you can drink coffee or tea.

But the gardens at night really look amazing. The hotel staff has decorated the trees with lights, thanks to which the place turns into a fabulous corner.

Room cleaning is carried out every day. Its quality is up to standard, and vacationers do not have to deal with dust or dirt. Bed sheets are changed twice a week.

Infrastructure

Hotel guests can contact the reception at any time. There are always managers who are fluent in Russian. On arrival, large and very valuable items can be left in the safe located in the hotel lobby. Here you can also exchange money, book a transfer to the airport. Near the reception there are representatives of tour operators who will offer guests a wide variety of excursions.



The hotel has an outdoor pool. This pond is kept in absolute purity. Sun beds by the pool are free of charge for hotel guests. With the full occupancy of the hotel, there are not enough sun loungers for everyone, so you need to occupy them in the morning. Towels are issued free of charge on receipt.

Hotel Natural Park Resort 3 * (Pattaya) provides tourists with the opportunity to leave their personal transport in free parking. People who have arrived in the country on business can use the conference rooms, equipped with everything necessary for meetings or negotiations. The halls accommodate up to 120 people.

Hotel guests can rent cars and explore all the sights nearby. It will also be very interesting to admire the resort city of Pattaya.

Pattaya, Natural Park Resort 3 *: description of a beach holiday

The beach is located across the road from the hotel, which must be crossed carefully, since there is quite a busy traffic. The beach is municipal, so there are always a lot of visitors. But there is always enough space for everyone. Towels are provided for hotel guests. You can sit on a sunbed under an umbrella for an additional fee.

The beach is sandy, pretty well maintained. But in many reviews, tourists indicate not very clear water, so swimming in the sea can be somewhat problematic. This situation is not observed every day. But there are times when duckweed and other garbage accumulate near the shore.



There are a lot of water transport on the beach. Due to its frequent use, the smell of petroleum products comes from the water, which is also not very pleasant for vacationers. Fishes rarely swim to the shore, so it‚Äôs unlikely to admire their beauty.

Many hotel guests prefer to swim on islets located near the coast. There the water is much cleaner, there are a huge number of marine life. To get to the islets, you can take advantage of the offers of local carriers, the fare is within reasonable limits. You can get to the islands as part of excursion programs.

Dining at the hotel

When booking a tour to the Natural Park Resort 3 * hotel, the photo of which can be seen in the article, you can choose one of two meal plans: breakfast or half board, which includes breakfast and dinner. The food here is very good. A variety of dishes will delight even very demanding tourists. Food quality is also quite decent. Fruits are served in sufficient quantities. From drinks, hotel guests can choose tea, coffee, sweet water made from powders. Hotel guests are not particularly enthusiastic about coffee. Due to the taste of the local water, this drink has a strange aftertaste.

Dine in the many restaurants and cafeterias. They are located near the place where the Natural Park Resort 3 * hotel is located. Reviews from tourists suggest that the food served here is quite tasty, albeit a little spicy. When placing an order, you can ask the waiters not to put too many spices in the dishes you ordered.



Near the hotel there is a supermarket where you can buy ready-made meals at any time. But you need to choose very carefully, because not all products indicate their name and ingredients that are part of the composition.

Near the hotel there is a shop with a huge selection of different fruits. The prices here are low, which allows you to enjoy plenty of overseas exotic.

Entertainment at the hotel

For fans of active pastime, the hotel offers the opportunity to test themselves in a variety of water sports. Their cost is not very high, so everyone can swim in water skis, a banana, a catamaran, or learn the intricacies of diving.

The hotel has a beauty salon. According to vacationers, they are doing an excellent Thai massage, which allows you to relax and promotes great well-being. There is also a sauna in the lounge. For all services provided here, you need to pay extra.

A little boring in the hotel will be for young people. There is no animation. You can have fun only on the dance floor, located in the restaurant. But if you wish, you can find enough entertainment in the clubs of the city, where there is the opportunity to have a lot of fun.

Often, hotel guests prefer to spend time ordering various excursions, of which there are a huge number. You can admire the beauty of various islands, the sights of cities or visit the local park. Tourists are advised to take excursions from local agencies, which will save a significant amount of money.

Features of children's leisure

Children can have fun at the hotel's well-equipped playground or go swimming in the dedicated pool. Parents can request a cot for their babies upon arrival. It is provided for free.

Young guests of the hotel can also have fun in the specially equipped computer room. Here you can entertain yourself with various video games.

Pattaya, Hotel Natural Park Resort 3 *: tourist reviews

According to many tourists, the hotel fully meets its category. For a pretty decent price you can have a pretty good time here. Favors this wonderful climate, beautiful countryside, friendly staff and comfortable rooms.

Of the negative aspects, tourists note the possibility of theft. Also, many do not like that at night the hotel is practically not guarded, and local people can walk here. Video surveillance is conducted only at the reception.

But still, tourists claim that no one will be disappointed by choosing the Natural Park Resort 3 * hotel (Thailand) for relaxation. Pattaya is an amazing place that will amaze anyone with its beauty.
