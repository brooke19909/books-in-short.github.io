Healing bath

Starting from the 16th century, longing for healing and purification were plunged into the Okovetsky springs. For several centuries, the fame of miraculous healings from various ailments and ailments was spread not only in the Tver province, but throughout the Russian land. In the twentieth century, the flow of people who wanted to take holy baths somewhat decreased, but from the beginning of the 21st century, the restoration of temples, the construction of baths from wood, the foundation of the courtyard began. At the same time, a day was celebrated to celebrate the acquisition of wonderful icons in the Okovets Forest.

Since then, every year on July 24, the Holy Okovetsky spring becomes especially crowded: clergy conduct a special service, after which all those present (both adults and children) are immersed in clean healing waters.

You need to dive at least three times, always with your head. A person goes through three bathing pools. It is believed that immersion in the waters of the Okovetsky spring at the height of summer is equal in importance and effectiveness to the Epiphany bathing. Moreover, the temperature of the water in the spring is not high - no more than four degrees, the spring does not freeze in winter, and the water does not heat up in summer. The fact that burning cold water really has healing properties is evidenced by many who have visited these holy places.
