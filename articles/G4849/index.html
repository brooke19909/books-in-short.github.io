Novotroitskoe Reservoir - Recreation and Fishing

On the Yegorlyk River in the Izobilnensky district of the Stavropol Territory, a reservoir was formed, which is now called Novotroitskoye. It occupies the 4th place in the area in the Egorlyksky cascade. The length of the pond is approximately 6 km, and the officially recorded maximum width is 8 km.

Novotroitskoe reservoir belongs to the Egorlyk river basin. In 1949, the construction of platinum began. In 1952, the work was completed and the reservoir was filled with water along the Nevinnomyssky Canal. Currently, it is the most favorite place for vacationers. A lot of people in the surrounding areas like to go fishing. The coastline is convenient for relaxation, so in the summer on the beaches you can meet a large number of vacationers.



Features of the Novotroitsk Reservoir

In winter, the Novotroitsk reservoir does not freeze - a good condition for waterfowl to constantly live there . And for those who like to spend time with a fishing rod, here is just expanse. Fishing in these parts is popular all year round. The entire population living in this area takes water for life and providing the economy from the Novotroitsk reservoir.

Animal and plant world

In this reservoir, such species of fish as crucian carp, pike perch, catfish, bream, black and white grass carp, perch prevail. Novotroitskoe reservoir is surrounded by dense forest. The nature in these parts is truly beautiful. Often you can see a small egret, mallard, hoopoe and other birds. Those who come to these parts in the winter can meet with a big boar, a wolf and other predators.

Of the mammals, the most common species here are the brown hare, the fox and the raccoon dog.

Recreation facilities

In order to have a good rest and have fun, numerous recreation centers have been built near the Novotroitsky reservoir. The most famous among tourists are such as "Yacht Club", "Cheerfulness" and "Sunshine". Here tourists are offered a wide range of services. But "Energetik" or "Dream" is less comfortable, but their indisputable advantage is low cost. For example, a single room will cost only 400 rubles. (meals are not included). The bases of the Novotroitsky reservoir are intended for people of different layers, so there is no shortage of vacationers.



Currently, many people hold celebrations at the local bases. There are sports and playgrounds, gazebos, rooms for children and much more.

This place has become the most popular among tourists and travelers due to its unusually beautiful nature. You will not regret if you go to relax on this pond. Everyone who has visited these places at least once has returned with positive emotions and good mood.

Residents of the Izobilnensky district are very fond of the Novotroitskoe reservoir. They come to rest there almost every weekend.
