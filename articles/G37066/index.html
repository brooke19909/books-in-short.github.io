Where to relax in Vietnam - seasonal overview

Now Vietnam is becoming an increasingly popular destination among Russian tourists. This is a country with a glorious past, beautiful nature, interesting culture and hospitable locals. Travel services are growing every year, and the prices for services pleasantly surprise. Here excursion trips are perfectly arranged. There are many opportunities for independent study of the country, and for traveling around the edge. But most visitors choose a beach holiday in Vietnam. Where to stay and which places to visit - read this article.



If you are focused on swimming in warm waters and sunbathing under the tropical sun, but at the same time do not mind learning a lot about the country, then focus on a factor such as climate. Vietnam is ready to receive tourists year-round. The country stretched from north to south lies in several climatic zones at once, therefore, when it is damp and cool in the north, real summer reigns in the south. Conversely, when the rainy season is in the summer months in Ho Chi Minh City and its environs, there is no question for tourists about where it is better to relax in Vietnam. Of course, in the north of the country.

In this climate zone, the season begins in May and lasts through October. It is dry at this time, the sea is calm and calm. Most tourists agree on where the best vacation in Vietnam in the summer is Halong Bay. This unique natural complex is rightfully included in the list of world heritage. Thousands of small and simply tiny islets of bizarre shape seem to soar above the water surface of the bay. Look at this beauty, imbued with the amazing energy of this place attracts millions of tourists from all over the world.



Nearby is the capital of the country - Hanoi, as well as the very interesting excursion city of Haiphong. A week is not enough to see all the sights of these cities. The key places are the capital's pagodas Chua-Mot-Kot and Bach-Ma, the gates of Kuanchang, the imperial "Forbidden City". Where is it better to relax in Vietnam on the north coast? In the vicinity of Thaibin, Gimson and Thanh Hoa at the Gulf of Thin.



In spring and autumn, when it is still too cold in the north, and tropical rains are already beginning in the south, the center of the country is ready to receive guests. You are not fixated on a beach holiday and are ready to devote a lot of time to excursions? Then, in winter and summer, amazing adventures await you: riding on elephants, visiting snake and crocodile farms, jeep safari in the mountains and the like. Where is it better to relax in Vietnam in its central part? Of course, in Da Nang. There are unique Marble Mountains, the most beautiful beaches of the country. This is the mecca of surfers. Also nearby is the Hoi An resort, which divers have chosen.

Where is it better to relax in Vietnam in winter? Tourists break their spears, deciding which of the resorts in the southern part of the country to call the most-most-most. Opinions were divided between Phan Thiet, Long Hai, Vung Tau, Nya Chang and Fukuoka. If you are a fan of diving into the world of coral reefs, then choose an island near the Cambodian border. Fukuoka has a decent level of diving schools, and the fauna and flora of the underwater world is fascinating. The best time for diving here is November-May, and in Nha Trang - March-October.
