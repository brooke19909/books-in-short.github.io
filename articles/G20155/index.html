Hotel LTI Djerba Plaza Thalasso & Spa: overview, description, rooms and reviews

If you want to visit such an original country as Tunisia, it is important to approach the choice of a place to stay as responsibly as possible. So, the love of travelers from around the world managed to win the Djerba Plaza Thalasso & Spa.



a brief description of

A real paradise in the middle of the desert is the resort complex Djerba Plaza Thalasso & Spa. Built in 1992, it was constantly improved and reconstructed to meet the needs of guests. The last overhaul dated 2014. On the territory of 14,000 square meters. m. there are 3 comfortable residential buildings. Travelers can stay here any time of the year.

Location

Only a 6-minute walk from the luxurious beach is the resort Djerba Plaza Thalasso & Spa 4 *. Tunisia, Djerba, tourist area Midoun, 4116 - the address of this institution. It is 17 km from the island‚Äôs capital, Houmt Souk, and 30 km from the international airport. Nearby is a large crocodile farm.





Number of rooms

Dreaming of a cozy and comfortable environment, pay attention to the rooms of the resort complex Djerba Plaza Thalasso & Spa. The following accommodation options are available to guests:

  • Standard rooms are 30 square meters. m. provide views of the large palm garden or the sea coast. You can sit in large or twin beds. For relaxation, a wicker chair with soft pillows is provided. Comfortable garden furniture is also provided on the balcony or private terrace. This category includes rooms for the disabled, as well as removable rooms.
  • Junior Suite provides one king bed or two twin. A comfortable sofa is provided to accommodate the extra guest, and a clamshell can also be installed. The luxurious terrace, equipped with comfortable armchairs, overlooks the garden, the sea or the luxurious golf course.
  • The family room can accommodate up to 4 adults and 1 child (another combination of guests is also possible). Two bedrooms have large and one and a half beds. The cozy sitting area is equipped with wicker chairs and a coffee table. There is also a workplace. It has a balcony or terrace overlooking the palm garden.
  • Suite is a spacious apartment of 63 square meters. m., designed for 2 adults. It is possible to provide an extra bed or cot. There is a bedroom and a living room. On a spacious terrace of 15 square meters. m. there are 2 comfortable sunbeds. From the windows you can see the pool, garden and partially the sea.
  • Presidential Room is the best accommodation option in this hotel. Room is 120 square meters. m. Plasma TVs are installed in the living room and main bedroom. There is also a study and an additional room with twin beds. The room is equipped with two bathrooms.

Hotel Bars

LTI Djerba Plaza Thalasso & Spa Resort provides its guests with quality drinks and light snacks. You can visit the following places:

  • Bar-cafe on the beach is not only a place where you can refresh yourself with cool beer or carbonated drinks. It is also possible to have a delicious meal in the afternoon.
  • The coral bar is located in the main lobby of the hotel. Here, a calm atmosphere reigns and pleasant music always plays. The elegant interior and quality drinks contribute to a pleasant pastime.
  • The Moorish bar is decorated in a traditional oriental style. The interior contains handmade elements. You can also enjoy local drinks here.

Thalasso Center

One of the main features of the resort complex Djerba Plaza Thalasso & Spa 4 * is a luxurious thalasso center. Here, qualified specialists will take care of your health and beauty. Thalasso procedures are based on the use of sea water heated to a certain temperature. Here you have the following options:

  • large pool with purified sea water;
  • aerobics classes in water;
  • Nordic procedures, which involve the alternation of high and low temperatures;
  • salt room for rest;
  • herbal teas made from unique medicinal herbs;
  • Turkish hammam
  • aromatic and ordinary steam saunas;
  • chromotherapy based on the effects of a whirlpool of water;
  • hot tub, as well as baths for arms and legs;
  • jet massage;
  • cosmetology zone;
  • massage parlor.

Golf Club

Golf is a game of aristocrats. All guests of LTI Djerba Plaza Thalasso & Spa 4 * can take part in this entertainment. Les Parmies is a 9-hole course. Playing on such a site is the most interesting and challenging. The landscape is characterized by smooth changes in height, and is also decorated with tropical palm trees.

La Mer Field is located on the seafront. In addition to the game, you can enjoy the beautiful scenery. Despite 9 holes, this field is easier to pass than the previous one. But the Les Acacias field is ideal for beginners, as it is the smallest in size.
