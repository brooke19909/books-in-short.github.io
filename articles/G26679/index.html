What can be done out of the boxes with the children?

How do they usually use boxes from equipment, shoes, juices, matches ?! In 80 percent they simply throw out, sometimes they spread cardboard in stores under their feet so that they do not spread the dirt, and they also make bird feeders. However, this is not all that can be done out of the boxes, as we will discuss in more detail in the article.

What can be the use of boxes in the development of children?

In educational institutions, the task of psychologists and teachers is to contribute to the development of children's imagination. The simplest test to identify the level of imagination is to give the child a simple subject and task: ‚ÄúWhat can be done with it? Name as many options as possible. ‚Äù

Daily exercises develop thinking, imagination, speech, fantasy, as the psychologist achieves the non-standard use of ordinary objects.

For example, crafts: box houses, a tractor, a carriage, a truck, a booth, a closet, a TV are standard templates, and a cockerel, a dog, a rocket, a well, a built-in kitchen - these answer options speak of imaginative thinking. Volumetric and complex crafts contribute to the development of spatial and abstract thinking, artistic and aesthetic taste.

What can be made of medium-sized boxes?

  • Gift wrapping. The easiest option is to decorate shoe boxes with scrapbooking paper and decorate with bows.
  • Transport for dolls. Using cardboard and bottle caps, you can build a variety of cars and trucks.
  • TV and mini-theater. With the help of boxes and applications of colored paper, you can play various tales and cartoons. If you do not know how to draw, it is enough to stick pictures from magazines and coloring books on a solid foundation.
  • Dollhouse houses. You can build a tall building, where each box defines a room, or make a flat dwelling for small toys from one.
  • Furniture for games. Beds, dressers, wardrobes, sideboards, sofas, armchairs, tables, chairs and other attributes of everyday life can be made with your own hands from ordinary things.
  • Photo frames. The bottom of candy boxes can be used as a photo frame for photographs, drawings or crafts of a child. At the same time, it can be decorated on the outside with various decorative trifles and paints. Such "paintings" perfectly decorate the nursery.
  • Unusual gifts. A simple gift for mom or grandmother: paint and paste over the bottom of the candy box with flowers, butterflies-applications. Imagine how dear the little man will be delighted, having received a piece of summer in the winter evening!

Crafts from small boxes

With large objects, everything is clear, but what can be done from boxes of juice, milk, matches and other little things? All the same: cars, ships, houses, cribs, furniture, TV, chest of drawers. Can be used for crafts with various applications and improvised material.



Even useful and practical things come from boxes. For example, cup holders for office supplies. If you take several boxes of different sizes, decorate them with paper or tie them with threads, connect them in a single line, you will get different containers for brushes, pens, pencils and even erasers.

Caskets for thread and jewelry can also be made from small boxes. Create vases and make paper roses and flowers. You can make animals and decorate them. In any case, children offer a lot of ideas for making crafts from boxes. Organizers for needlewomen are especially relevant, just decorate the boxes, glue them together and get small compartments for beads, floss and other accessories.

As you can see, there are a lot of ideas for joint creativity with a child at minimal cost, and what can be done from the boxes - the children will tell you themselves!
