The best hotels of La Pineda (Spain): description, photos, reviews

The tourism industry in the resort began to develop in the middle of the 20th century. This is a young but well-known Spanish town located in Catalonia, near Salou and right on the Costa Dorada. A large amount of vegetation grows here, in particular Mediterranean pine, in honor of which the name La Pineda is given. There are many hotels in the resort, among the available options you can choose the one that will meet both personal wishes and budget.

The city itself is small and cozy, and therefore is great for family and romantic getaways. Almost every hotel has furniture for babies, as well as many recreational activities for children and adults.

For secluded relaxation: Palas Pineda





The hotel is in a quiet corner of La Pineda. It has 504 rooms that are equipped with a balcony, a bathroom, a minibar, a safe, a TV and a telephone. A choice of rooms of various categories, differing in the level of comfort and spaciousness.

For children there is a playground, a mini club, babysitting services are offered. Adults can visit the fitness room, beauty salon, TV room or go shopping.

For business clients there is a banquet and conference room where you can conduct business negotiations and meetings in an informal setting.

The beach is sandy, located 150 m. The conditions for a relaxing and active holiday are created, and you can also go on a boat trip on a catamaran or on a boat - for fishing. Among other things, the hotel offers a sauna, a bathhouse and a solarium, as well as tennis, volleyball and billiards.

Huge Hotel Estival (La Pineda)





Among Russian tourists, this complex is most popular. The hotel consists of 10 buildings, including apartments, rooms of various categories, a mini club and a spa. As for the living conditions, you can choose both a simple standard type room and a luxurious two-story duplex.

The complex occupies a vast territory. Great for families with children, as for kids there are many attractions and services, such as several pools, playgrounds and rooms, a mini club.

Cozy Gran Palas





Among the hotels of La Pineda, it is Gran Palas that is famous for comfort and tranquility. It is small and cozy, located 700 m from the resort center. Equipped with 102 rooms equipped with the necessary appliances and furniture. Meals are offered at choice:

  • breakfast only;
  • half board;
  • full board.

For children there is a swimming pool, a mini club, a disco is held, an animation program is developed. Adults can visit the spa, which offers full relaxation with massages and other wellness treatments.

Just 3 km from the hotel is the popular Port Aventura Theme Park, which houses the equally famous Ferrari Land theme park.

Comfortable Golden Donaire Beach





The huge advantage of the hotel is that it is located right on the coast, which is very rare for La Pineda. Although the hotel is considered economical, there are all conditions for a comfortable stay. Most of the tourists at Golden Donaire Beach are Russians, so even children's animation here is carried out mainly in our language.

Well-developed infrastructure, provides a lot of entertainment for children and adults. Active youth also likes to relax here, so it can be very noisy.

Well located La Hacienda Gran Hotel

This is one of the hotels in La Pineda, where you can relax, as it is located in a quiet area. The territory is well landscaped, there is a large pool, loungers are around it. Also, adult vacationers are invited to go to the gym, watch a movie in the TV lounge, and children can play in the club or on the playground. For business customers, the doors of a fully-equipped conference room are always open.

Best Sol D`or - hotel for a relaxing holiday





Quiet and comfortable hotel, next to which there is a beautiful landscaped park. You need to walk only 100 m to the beach. Due to the remoteness from the resort center, 1 km around the hotel is always calm. In addition, living here is relatively cheap.

A special pool for children, a mini club and a playground are on site. It is possible to place a cot in the room, the restaurant offers the use of a high chair. The hotel infrastructure includes 2 outdoor pools, a sauna, a restaurant, a bar, a terrace and a car rental service.

Hotel reviews of La Pineda

About all the above hotels, the reviews of tourists are mostly only positive. Vacationers write about each of them that the hotels have a convenient location, comfortable rooms, delicious cuisine and a sufficient amount of entertainment for children and adults. A high level of service and the ability to relax under the influence of massage and other procedures are additional pluses. The resort itself is also pleasant to relax. Well-developed infrastructure: restaurants, bars, shops, souvenir shops, parks and much more. In general, La Pineda hotels in Spain are ideal for a comfortable stay.
