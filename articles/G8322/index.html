Market "Gardener": how to get there. Metro and bus

Not everyone has their own car. But even if it is available, in Moscow it is not always the most convenient and profitable mode of transport. There are several ways to get to the popular Gardener Market. How to get to it from the metro is not a big problem. The administration of the trading company provides its visitors with the opportunity to use the free bus route. A free bus runs in the direction of the Sadovod market from Vykhino and Lyublino metro stations every twenty minutes. But it is quite possible to get to the goal and by minibus.
