Meshchersky Park: description and address

Each park can be considered a real breath of fresh air. Life in the city does not allow a person to fully relax, so he always seeks to communicate with nature. Meshchersky National Park has not only beautiful nature, but also its own history, which not all Muscovites even know about.

General information

From distant times, mixed-type forests grew in the park. If you recall a little history, Meshchersky Park began to be described for the first time in the 13th century. Parts of the land were in the possession of many famous personalities, for example, such as Alexander Nevsky. Ivan the Terrible himself came here to hunt, because the forests were well placed for this leisure. During its existence, the park has undergone destruction less than others, so it has come down to our time in the form of full forests.

Another property of the park is Meshchersky Pond. For a comfortable rest of the townspeople, a special sandy beach is offered. He suggests the possibility of swimming. There are cabins and showers on the beach, so you can relax quite comfortably. Children can not only swim, but also ride on a swing. There are quite a few options for their entertainment in the park. Adults here are also not bored. In addition to sports fields, there are plenty of places for picnics and walks. Therefore, those who do not go to the cafe often themselves cook kebabs in the clearings, which are specially provided for this. Meshchersky Park is regularly cleaned so that visitors can spend their free time.

How to get there

Find Meshchersky Park is quite simple. It is located at Voskresenskaya street, 1as1, in the Solntsevo area. You can get to it in several convenient ways:

  • By bus number 883 from Filevsky Park metro station. Get off at the Meshchersky Pond stop.
  • Minibus 753 from the metro station "Slavyanskiy Boulevard".
  • Minibus at number 500 from the Kievskaya metro station.
  • By electric train from Kievsky station. Get off at the Skolkovo Platform station.
  • On personal vehicles, you can choose the road along Skolkovsky, Minsk or Borovsky highway.

Why is the park so popular?

Meshchersky Park in Moscow is well known to both citizens and many tourists. It attracts people with its beauty, as well as the opportunity to relax from the noise of the city. But besides simple walks, this place has become popular also because it unites lovers of a healthy lifestyle. Especially to improve physical fitness you can always visit the site for workout. In addition to it, the park has a football field, as well as bike paths. Meshchersky Park is especially appreciated by those people who like to run. Here you can find a lot of tracks, thanks to which each run will be very effective. On the Internet, runners actively communicate and meet in the park to practice together.

In the cold season, the park is also not empty. Indeed, the place of runners is actively occupied by skiers. Many people like to ride through the snowy territory, enjoying beautiful views. The park is used for photo shoots at any time of the year, because many photographers and amateurs cannot help but capture the marvelous nature.
