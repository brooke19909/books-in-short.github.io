Holidays in hotels in the Philippines: where is the best place to stay?

The Philippines is located on 7641 islands between Taiwan and Indonesia, which are divided into three groups: Mindanao, Visayas , Luzon. The total length of the coast is 36,300 km. Mountain ranges of volcanic origin with tropical thickets pass through the islands.

The marvelous beauty of the ocean, the rich underwater world, interesting excursions make it a great place to relax.

Climate on the islands

The weather in the Philippines is determined by the tropical marine climate. There are 3 seasons here in the year: in the summer 3 months from March to May are considered, then the rainy season begins, which lasts until November, then the wind changes from the south-west to the north-east monsoon. Typhoons and tsunamis are not uncommon here.

Where to stay in the Philippines?

The beautiful ocean, sandy beaches and warm weather attract tourists from all over the world. This contributes to the development of the tourist area, here everyone can choose hotels to taste. The best hotels in the Philippines are very popular in 2018:

  • Shangri-La's Mactan Resort & Spa;
  • Jpark Island Resort & Waterpark, Cebu;
  • AG New World Manila Bay Hotel in Manila with a casino;
  • Crimson Resort and Spa, Mactan;
  • South Palms Resort Panglao and many others.

Palm paradise

On the island of Pangalao, there are magnificent white beaches, the most popular of which are Alona Beach, Doljo Beach and Bikini Beach, with many cafes, restaurants, dive clubs.

In the radiance of snow-white sands, a tourist tries in vain to find peace: hundreds of boats, boats and ships are constantly mooring and sailing, taking the afflicted on boat trips, cleverly maneuvering between bathers splashing in the ocean waves.

Beaches that are not specifically cared for, quickly turn green from algae. Strong low tides after dinner also do not contribute to swimming. Given that algae begins to bloom in winter, there is no better way than to settle in one of the excellent hotels in the Philippines with pools.

Hotels in Cebu Philippines

Cebu is one of the islands discovered in 1521 by Fernando Magellan. This is a narrow stretch of land with a length of 225 km, the shores of which are white sandy beaches. A lot of chain, private hotels have been built here, the price per night in which is from 20 to 100 dollars. The most popular are:

  • Shangri-La's Mactan Resort & Spa;
  • Radisson Blue Cebu;
  • Jpark Island Resort & Waterpark;
  • Movenpick Hotel Mactan Island Cebu and many others.

Most hotels receive positive reviews from tourists. Attention to Cebu attracts Maayo Hotel 4 *, opened at the end of 2017. This is a great option for businessmen, travelers, it is suitable for those who, after visiting the beach, want to do exploration of the surroundings and fly to other islands, as it is located 10 minutes from the airport.

It has 229 standard rooms, excellent restaurants, a panoramic pool, and live piano music at the reception. Magnificent new rooms decorated in soothing pastel colors.

Cebu is a real paradise with quiet lagoons, picturesque reefs, historical sights.

Having been to hotels in the Philippines, they note excellent service, affordable prices, and a friendly atmosphere. The main problem is the road, which includes several flights, as well as frequent tsunamis, typhoons and hurricanes.
