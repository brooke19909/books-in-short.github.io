CPU Reviews

Let's move on to the performance description of the Samsung Galaxy J2 Prime Black. User reviews on this issue are 90% positive. Shortly before the release of this model, the Korean company began cooperation with the Chinese chipset manufacturer MediaTek. Therefore, the choice of developers was quite expected. The smartphone is based on the MT6737T processor. It is based on four computing modules. Type - Cortex-A53. Under heavy load, they are able to accelerate to 1400 MHz. Compared to previous models, productivity has increased by approximately 15%. This, of course, pleased brand connoisseurs. The manufacturing process is 28 nm. This is not a good indicator, but the platform can be safely called energy efficient. It was possible to achieve such results thanks to correctly selected software algorithms. Also, the improved SoC design played a significant role.

The manufacturer was still able to surprise buyers by releasing the Samsung Galaxy J2 Prime SM-G532F. Reviews indicate that this model in some cases is quite superior to its competitors.

The Mali T720 GPU also performed well. In order to evaluate its work, you can run the popular game World of Tanks Blitz. It is quite demanding, so you will need to immediately set the phone settings to maximum. The game starts at up to 50 frames per second. Memory

Samsung Galaxy J2 Prime reviews often mention the amount of RAM. The fact is that the manufacturer is gradually abandoning high performance due to the improvement of software. The company directly stated at a press conference that it would not chase a powerful hardware platform. Users decided to test this in practice. For this, the WoT game was used and at the same time ten tabs of the Internet browser were launched. Surprisingly, the phone switched between them quite quickly. There were no freezes. This result pleased and users decided to complicate the task by launching a video on YouTube as well. After 5 minutes of viewing, returning to the game took a matter of seconds. But still, one point deserves attention. Open tabs after such manipulations began to reboot spontaneously.

What conclusion can be made? For such a model, 1.5 GB of "RAM" is enough. The manufacturer forgets to indicate this point in the specifications. But thanks to the reviews of advanced users who thoroughly test the phones, for detailed information.

Camera Features & Reviews

There is a category of buyers for which camera resolution is one of the most important criteria when choosing a phone. What does this say about the Samsung Galaxy J2 Prime SM-G532F? The main module is based on an 8-megapixel matrix. There is a focus that works in automatic mode. But here you need to pay attention to one point: best of all, autofocus performs its functions at a distance of 12 cm. If you bring the camera closer to the object, you will not succeed in achieving a clear picture.

You can focus on the subject manually. To do this, just click on the display in the right place. Users left a few comments about the face recognition option. It works only with the full-face image. If the face is shifted at least a couple of millimeters, then the shooting will have to be done manually.

In order to start the viewfinder, you will need to press the Home button twice. Be sure to remove the lock before doing this. For settings, you can use the light filters, exposure, change the mode by choosing, for example, burst or panoramic shooting.

What do users say about the front module of the Samsung Galaxy J2 Prime? Reviews, it is worth noting, are positive. The first thing that caught the eye was the flash.
