The most famous hotels of Kyshtym

The small town of Kyshtym is located in the Chelyabinsk region. There are few hotels here, since the area is not a resort. Although from a tourist point of view it is considered promising. Indeed, back in 1990, Kyshtym was included in the list of historical cities of the Russian Federation.

The large advertising signs that usually decorate hotel buildings are practically absent on the streets. Therefore, in order to find accommodation for you it has become easier, we offer a list of small but cozy local hotels.

Hotel Acropol

The hotel is located, which is popularly called "Manhattan", on Second South Street, 2B.

Despite its modest size, this Kyshtym hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay. In a two-story complex are located:

  • nine residential rooms;
  • Cafe;
  • sauna with pool.

For those who come to the city on a business trip, the hotel provides a small conference room with a printer and fax.

The number of rooms of the Acropolis consists of the following rooms:

  • single double;
  • doubles with single beds;
  • triple with double bed and sofa bed.

The cost of living is from 1500 rubles per day.

The cafe serves Greek, Georgian and European dishes. The capacity of the hall is up to 50 people.

The main feature of the Acropol hotel complex is that when organizing a wedding banquet, the newlyweds receive a room as a gift.

Other hotel services:

  • Free Wi-Fi, which is valid throughout the complex;
  • satellite TV in the rooms;
  • set of towels and bath accessories;
  • free parking.

Hotel Rodnik

It is a whole shopping and hotel complex. On the ground floor there is the Trattoria restaurant with two spacious banquet rooms for 60 and 90 people, a bakery, a supermarket, and a beauty salon.

Residential apartments are located on the second floor. In total there are 15 cozy single and double rooms in Rodnik. Each has a comfortable double bed (or two single beds), TV, fridge, bedside tables, table, wardrobe. The bathroom is equipped with a shower or bath.

Hotel complex services:

  • free wifi in rooms;
  • taxi call;
  • digital television;
  • the provision of a microwave and utensils;
  • delivery to the correspondence number;
  • provision of printer and fax.

The cost of accommodation is from 700 rubles per day.

One of the main advantages of the hotel is its minus at the same time - its location. Hotel address: Kyshtym, 137a Karl Liebknecht Street. This is the very center of the city, so the windows overlook the busy highway.

Hotel Billiards

The people bears the name "Zhilinsky Hotel". It offers guests and residents of the city a vacation in an affordable price segment. In total, this hotel in Kyshtym has five rooms designed for ten people.

The apartments are located on the second floor of the complex. They are small but comfortable. Equipped with comfortable double beds, bedside tables, wardrobe, dressing table. Each room has a private bathroom with shower and toiletries. The cost of accommodation is from 1200 rubles per day.

On the ground floor of the hotel there is a cafe for thirty people and a large billiard room. Hotel guests especially note the delicious cuisine, clean apartments, friendly staff.

Hotel address: Frunze street, 2a.

Hotel and Bath Complex "Coliseum"

Located on Pervaya Yuzhnaya Street, 2d.

The hotel can accommodate up to twenty guests at a time. The rooms are made in a modern style, equipped with everything you need: comfortable beds, a table, a wardrobe, a TV, a mini fridge. Each has a split system and a bathroom with shower. Both single and double occupancy are possible.

In the same building as the hotel, there is a Russian bathhouse. It is decorated in a rustic style. You can relax in the relaxation room or the billiards room.

Next to the hotel-bath complex is the cafe "Sirtaki", which serves European and Georgian cuisine. The banquet room is designed for 70 people, making it ideal for family celebrations, weddings and corporate parties.

Hotel "Malachite"

It has a long history, because the hotel has been open since 1963. Initially, the hotel room stock included as many as forty rooms of various categories. To date, only seven are left to function:

  • four single;
  • two doubles;
  • one triple.

Each room has its own bathroom with a bathroom. Hot and cold water is supplied around the clock. There is also cable TV everywhere. One refrigerator for all seven apartments is located in the corridor. The cost of accommodation is from 480 rubles per bed.

Residential rooms are located in a three-story building, on the ground floor of which there is a photo studio and a copy center, a supermarket. The complex also has a hotel cafe, open from 9 to 22. It serves simple but tasty dishes made with the freshest produce.

The address of the Kyshtym Hotel "Malachite": Frunze Street, 3.
