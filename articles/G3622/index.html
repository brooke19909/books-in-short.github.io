Vityazevsky estuary: mud treatment, ecotourism and fishing

Over the years, the Krasnodar Territory has been famous for its hospitable lands and beautiful reservoirs. Today, its territory is often attracted by such water resources as estuaries. Separated from the Black Sea by a narrow sandy scythe with small hills and heaps of grass, these brackish bays do not have surface vegetation and lie among ridges and rounded hills. Such natural beauties include the Vityazevsky estuary.

Reservoir characteristic

This small, shallow, triangular-shaped bay has a size of about sixty-four square kilometers and a depth of up to two meters. A small small river Gostagayka flows into it.



Unlike other similar bays, such as Kiziltash, Bugaz and Tsokur, which are connected with each other and with the Black Sea, the Vityazevsky estuary is separated by the Annunciation ridge. Some sections of its shores are quite steep, so they are convenient places for creating burrows by wild animals and nesting pegans.

A large number of islands and sand-shell braids contributes to the arrangement of nests with gulls and waders. And the surrounding agricultural fields create favorable conditions for the feeding of waterfowl. Some of them nest here, molt and remain wintering.

Healing mud

The estuary in Vityazevo is also therapeutic, as it contains mineral muds that are beneficial to the human body. They are often used in Anapa sanatoriums and mud baths in the treatment of various diseases. The bottom of the estuary is silt and dirt, exuding an unpleasant odor, but having a huge amount of useful properties. Due to their absorbable and anti-inflammatory effect, they are used not only for the skin, but also for the joints. However, it should be remembered that not everyone can be treated with these muds. Therefore, in order not to harm your body when planning a vacation in these places, it is best to consult a doctor.



The reason for the appearance of therapeutic mud is in the waters of the Gostagayka River, which carry fine clay that settles in the eastern part of its mouth. The thickness of the layer of sludge at the bottom of the bay is from twenty to sixty centimeters.

Ecotourism

Due to its wonderful properties, Vityazevsky estuary is a great place for eco-tourism and recreation. Near it is the resort village of Vityazevo, located in the southwestern part of the Taman Peninsula, only twenty kilometers from Anapa. Some time ago, the estuary was a sea bay in the delta of the ancient Kuban, and today it is a closed reservoir in the shape of a triangle. Gostagayka remains the main fresh residential for him, but not the only water resource. In addition to it, the estuary is replenished with atmospheric precipitation and the waters of the Black Sea. The latter, before entering the bay, pass through a kind of filter in the form of a scattering.

It should be noted that lovers of eco-tourism, especially barding, often visit the Vityazevsky estuary. Photos and videos showing a wonderful vacation here are a vivid confirmation of this.



Although the coast of the estuary is not as spacious as the sea, it has its own natural charm, which attracts large flocks of birds. Therefore, pelicans, gulls, herons and cormorants are often found on the shell-sand coast.

Throughout the summer months and September, the doors of bases and rest houses, such as Elite, Seagull, Builder, Glory, and others, are open to visitors in the village. In addition, the private sector also accommodates guests, trying to provide the most comfortable conditions at reasonable prices.

Fishing in the estuary

The Vityazevsky estuary is also a great holiday destination for those who like to go fishing. Fishing in this area will bring a lot of pleasure and unforgettable experiences. For this purpose, you can even rent a boat or boat, but this option is not the cheapest.



Fish in the bay are found in sufficient quantities. But when choosing gear you need to consider that mainly perch, carps, carp, bream, rudd, crucian carp, pike and others live here. For bait, it is better to use worms, maggots, makuku or corn.

You can also visit the trout farm to look at a different perspective on the Vityazevsky estuary. Fishing is organized at the highest level. And if you wish, in the cafe located on the farm you can prepare delicious dishes from the caught catch.
