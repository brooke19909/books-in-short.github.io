Soft toys from socks - beautiful and easy!

Why buy stuffed animals when you can make them yourself?

Not only children, but also adults can make various funny animals with their own hands. Toys from socks are made easily, simply and quickly. Almost any toy can be made from this piece of clothing: a bunny, a doll, an elephant, a giraffe, a snake, and many others. Some more materials will be needed: a synthetic winterizer or cotton wool for stuffing, buttons, threads, needles, bows for decorating a finished toy.

Plus, such a craft at low cost for its manufacture, which means that it will not be a pity if something happens to her during the game. Toys from socks - this is an occasion once again to please your baby, to give him a gift. Such a nice craft can be given as a guest on a visit as a small souvenir.

Quite simply, toys are made from socks. A master class with step-by-step description can be found in any sources. And you can show imagination and do everything yourself.



What if you try to make a bunny? To do this, you need two identical socks. From one we make a head with ears, and from the other we make a trunk with legs. We cut off the elastic from both socks: in one case a little more, in the other - a little less. It is good to take socks with a white heel and toe. Then we cut these socks along the bottom, without cutting a couple of centimeters to the heel. We take a sock, which in the future will be the head of a hare. We cut off the lower white tips from it, from them we will make a tail, stitching it round and stuffing it with a cone. We sew the resulting ears, it should be noted that you need to stitch from the wrong side. We fill the resulting form with a filler, the heel with us will be the muzzle of a hare. Having pulled the heel in a certain way, we get a "mustache" and the nose of a hare. Sew on the nose, eyes from buttons or material of a different color.

From another sock we make the body. Also, we sew the ‚Äúlegs‚Äù from the inside, twist, stuff, sew. The heel in this case is the back of the hare. We sew a tail made in advance to it. Then we sew the toes with a needle and thread. Sew together the body and head.

From the cut gum, you will need to make the front legs of the hare and sew to the body. Everything, the bunny is ready!



Such soft toys are made from socks as baby dolls. From the sock you need to cut off the upper part with an elastic band and a heel. The gum will act as a cap, and we‚Äôll make a head out of the heel. So, the cut off heel needs to be aligned to a circle, to pass a thread into it and to tighten, the ball will turn out. We fill it, sew on the eyes - beads, you can embroider them with thread, mouth and nose. To create a hat, you need to overcast the cut gum, then thread the thread, stepping back about a centimeter from the edge, and pull it off. It turns out a cap-cock. We put it on the head.

Now you need to make the body of a baby doll. To do this, in the lower part of the sock, note the position of the legs and arms. For the legs, sew a seam about 6 centimeters long in the middle of the toe, for the hands - stepping back one centimeter from the edge, make seams five centimeters deep. We fill the body with filler, tighten it from above. The final touch - we sew the head with the body. The seam can be made invisible by covering it with a bow.
