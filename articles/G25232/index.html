Territorial and climatic features

Pitsunda is one of the most amazing places in Abkhazia. Here is a unique microclimate. Association of boarding houses "Pitsunda Resort" is located on the seashore. It is surrounded by a pine grove. Plants, in turn, contribute to the creation of a healing microclimate. Here, even in the hot summers, you can enjoy the cool. The resort coast is located in a picturesque bay. Thanks to this, the sea is calm and crystal clear. Pitsunda Resort is an ideal holiday destination for those who like to enjoy the beauty of nature and appreciate the ecology. This resort is for people with an unassuming attitude to comfortable living. Few people know where the cleanest water on the Black Sea is. It can be found at Cape Pitsunda, in the Ldzaa area. Even in the hottest heat, there are corners where you can enjoy amazing coolness. This is largely due to cypress and pine alleys, as well as sea breezes.
