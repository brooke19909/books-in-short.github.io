Hotel "Iren" in Kungur: description, address, mode of operation, reviews and photos

Hotel "Iren" in Kungur is a cozy establishment with a welcoming atmosphere, comfortable accommodation and high standards of service. The hotel is already working. Where is the hotel located? What categories of rooms does it offer guests and what services does it provide? What do travelers think of him?

Location

The address of the Iren Hotel in Kungur is Lenin Street 30. This is the historical center of the city. In walking distance are administrative and infrastructure facilities, many enterprises. The distance from Bolshoye Savino Airport is 100 km.

From the bus station and Kungur railway station to the Iren Hotel you can get by buses and minibuses No. 1, 7, 8, 9. Get off at the Hotel stop.

Accommodation options

The Iren Hotel in Kungur provides 54 rooms with a total capacity of 73 beds to accommodate guests. All rooms were renovated from 2006 to 2010. Comfort categories are as follows:

  • Suite - two-room suite, consisting of a bedroom with a large bed and a living room with upholstered furniture.
  • The first category is one-room rooms with large or twin beds.
  • The second category - one-room rooms with large or twin beds. The bathroom is shared by two rooms.
  • The fourth category is a compact room with a minimum set of amenities. The room has a washbasin. Bathroom on the floor.
  • The fifth category is a compact room with a minimum set of amenities. Bathroom on the floor.

Booking Policy

If you plan to stay at the Iren Hotel, read the reservation policy. Here are some points to consider:

  • Reservations can be made through the booking form on the official website or by phone (the number is also indicated on the website).
  • Within a day from the moment of sending the application, the administrator will contact you by phone to clarify organizational issues, as well as confirm the order.
  • Reservations must be made at least 5 days prior to the expected arrival date.
  • It is possible to change the application by sending a new one (be sure to make a note about the cancellation of the previous order).
  • Reservations are charged at 25% of the daily price.
