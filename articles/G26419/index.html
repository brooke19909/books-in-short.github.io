Complex "Cascadas", Sunny Beach, Bulgaria - overview, description and reviews

The complex "Cascadas" in Sunny Beach in Bulgaria is a lot of buildings for vacationers located in a protected area. In multi-storey buildings, you can rent apartments at any time of the year. The resort "Sunny Beach" is the first largest and most popular in the country. Tourists from all over the world come here to spend their holidays brightly and cheerfully, or just to relax from the bustle of the city in picturesque places.





Location

The resort area "Sunny Beach" is located a hundred kilometers from Varna, forty - from Burgas. Its construction began in the 1950s. Now the resort has a developed infrastructure and the status of an international holiday destination. "Cascadas" of Sunny Beach is one of the most modern projects on the coast. The proximity to the Black Sea is the main reason why tourists choose this apartment.

You can get to the recreation area by bus, a bus stop is nearby. If you go a little further, you can see the bus station, where tickets are sold to the nearest cities, including Sofia. It is also possible to rent a car and drive to your destination on your own.

Description

Apartments "Cascadas" in Sunny Beach is divided into several housing options, depending on the desires and financial capabilities of vacationers. Apartments for rent of various sizes, from small studios to multi-room premises. In the new buildings there is still the opportunity to buy your own apartments. This will be an excellent investment, which, in the future, will bring permanent profit to homeowners if they wish to rent an apartment. The location is as good as possible. You can walk to a good sandy beach in about 15 minutes. In addition, just 70 meters from the Cascadas buildings in Sunny Beach there is a water park where you can swim and have fun with the whole family or in the company of friends in any weather.





During the construction of houses used only high quality materials. Heat and sound insulation is mandatory, therefore extraneous noise will not interfere with a relaxing rest and sleep. Due to the presence of air conditioners in the rooms, the optimum air temperature will be constantly maintained. The facade of the buildings is made of brick in combination with natural stone, which makes the exteriors more presentable and original.

Benefits

Near the Cascadas complex in Sunny Beach there is a huge pool filled with salt water, as well as about a dozen smaller artificial reservoirs. On the territory they are engaged not only in swimming, but also in other sports. For this purpose, sports grounds and fitness centers have been established where visitors can use fitness equipment for a fee. It is possible to rent a bike. The infrastructure is complemented by beauty salons and SPA centers. Also, guests will enjoy numerous cafes and restaurants that have Russian, Italian cuisine and other varieties. It is worth noting that in these institutions in the warm season there are always dishes from fresh fruits and vegetables that are grown directly on the territory of the complex. There is a tour desk in the relaxation area.





This is the best place to spend a vacation with your family. There are playgrounds for children, as well as barbecue areas with gazebos. Among the various types of entertainment, you can choose more relaxed. For example, an ordinary walk in the park. On its territory you can sit on a bench in the shade of trees, admire flowering plants near the pond.

"Cascadas" in Sunny Beach in Bulgaria: reviews

The apartments are recommended as a good place for family vacations in the summer. The range of services provided varies: it all depends on the specific type of building. In hotel rooms and some rented apartments, room cleaning is standard. Sometimes breakfast is also included in the price at the nearest restaurant or cafe. Prices are affected by the booking time (if you take care of this in advance, it will be cheaper) and the total number of days that vacationers are going to spend in Cascadas in Sunny Beach. In the reviews of those who have already visited this place, a lot is said about the convenience of apartment layouts.

Visitors highly appreciate the service, the friendliness of the staff, and pay particular attention to the quiet and calm atmosphere on the territory of the Cascadas buildings in Sunny Beach. On the beach you can always find free sunbeds. The park is especially well-groomed. It is very beautiful here in summer: fountains work, in the pond you can see turtles and watch the feeding of fish.





Apartments

Modern furniture and quality finishes create a special, "home" comfort in the rooms. From the equipment in the rooms there are new televisions, microwaves, electric kettles, ultramodern refrigerators and stoves. Guests can cook and wash their own meals. There are economy rooms for guests who are willing to pay only a limited amount for accommodation in the complex. In this case, cleaning the premises will need to be done independently, and if necessary, go to the laundry room or order the ironing service, but for a fee. If you do not want to do this, you can book a superior room, where all additional services are included in the price.

Service

On the territory of "Cascadas" of Sunny Beach in Bulgaria, a video surveillance system is constantly operating. Here you can not worry about security, yours or your loved ones. If the residents have any problems, you can always call home specialists: plumbers, electricians, security. Professionals in the shortest possible time will help to fix the equipment, and give advice on further operation.





A big plus of buying an apartment in the Cascadas buildings in Sunny Beach is the opportunity to live on the territory all year round and breathe in the fresh sea air. Thanks to the developed infrastructure, here and in the cold periods everything that is necessary for living in comfort is available: shops, bars, laundries and service departments continue to work.
