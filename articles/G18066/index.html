Hotel Dessole Lippia Golf Resort 4 * (Greece): traveler reviews

Beach holidays are a favorite pastime of almost any person. That is why the coastal cities of many countries have long turned into resorts. One of the most popular resort destinations is Greece, which has at its disposal not only hundreds of miles of sandy beaches, but also dozens of islands of different sizes, which can offer the traveler not only relaxation by the water, but also a rich excursion program.

One of the most popular Greek islands is Rhodes. Every year thousands of tourists come here from all over the world to not only relax on the beach, but also to plunge headlong into an interesting culture and ancient, rich history. Naturally, on the territory of the island over the past decades, many hotels have been built for every taste and budget. One of them is the Dessole Lippia Golf Resort.

Naturally, every traveler is looking for additional information about this place. What does the Dessole Lippia Golf Resort look like. Hotel Dessole Lippia Golf Resort: where is it located?





Favorable location - one of the advantages of the hotel complex. Where exactly is the hotel Dessole Lippia Golf Resort 4 *? Afandou beach gr-85103 is the address of the hotel. This is a picturesque place in the north-eastern part of the island of Rhodes, just 200 meters from the picturesque long beach. It is worth mentioning right away that most often guests move around the island by taxi or rented transport, since there are practically no public buses here.

Hotel Dessole Lippia Golf Resort 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): Afandou and beach holidays





Rhodes is a place that is popular among fans of relaxation by the water. What does the hotel Dessole Lippia Golf Resort 4 * offer its customers? Afandou, one of the most famous beaches on the island, is located just 200 meters from the main hotel building. A convenient path leads to it; 5-10 minutes that you spend on the road is enough to enjoy the beautiful nature.

Hotel Dessole Lippia Golf Resort owns its own stretch of coast. You can be sure that your vacation will not be disturbed by the visit of strangers - only guests rest here. The beach is covered with fine pebbles and sand, as well as descent into the sea. The entrance is cleared of large stones, but barefoot walking here is not too comfortable. On the shore in several rows there are sun loungers and umbrellas for guests of the Dessole Lippia Golf Resort 4 * hotel. Reviews say that there are always enough sun loungers, regardless of what time you arrived at the beach. By the way, hotel guests use them for free. Here you can get a clean beach towel.

Sunbathing is not the only activity on the seashore. They will find interesting adventures and fans of outdoor activities. Nearby, in the center of water sports, for a small fee you can rent, for example, a catamaran, canoe or boat. The sea here is mostly calm, and therefore suitable for such independent sailing. In addition, tourists are happy to ride on boats, water skiing, banana, etc. The prices of attractions are no higher than on other beaches of the island. Fans of sailing and scuba diving will find something for themselves.

It is also worth saying that small sea excursions on yachts are very popular among tourists - you can fully enjoy all the fabulous beauty of the island.
