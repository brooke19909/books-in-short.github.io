Abano Terme (Italy) - a unique thermal resort

15 km from Padua and 60 km from beautiful Venice, among the picturesque green hills, lies the quiet and very beautiful thermal resort of Abano Terme. Italy pampers tourists with stunning landscapes, interesting sights, favorable climatic conditions, and in this beautiful country you can relax and improve your body. In Abano Terme, a smooth and mild climate is maintained all year round, the average temperature is +22 ° C, in summer it can rise to +32 ° C.



The resort in its lifetime has seen many famous, talented and empowered people. Back in the days of the Roman Empire, the town of Abano Terme was famous. Italy as a resort country began to develop in the XVIII century. It was during this period that the infrastructure was being established, aristocrats from all over Europe wanted to relax in this warm wonderful region. Thermal springs have long been famous for their unique healing properties, so it is not surprising that many tourists come to rest in Abano Terme (Italy).

Reviews about the resort are the most positive, people here relax in body and soul, heal the body, recover from serious diseases, rejuvenate the skin of the face and body. Local healing waters are geothermal in origin, they include mineral salts, iodine, bromine, iron, calcium, sodium, soda, magnesium, sulfur, ammonia, and potassium. The resort has 106 outdoor and 103 indoor thermal pools. To gain healing properties, water must go a long way in 50 years and 100 km. Rain water is collected in the Alps, seeps through the soil, enriched with trace elements and heated.



There are not many unique places like Abano Terme on the planet. Italy offers tourists comfortable relaxation conditions, accommodation in luxury spa hotels. Local thermal springs are unique in their chemical composition and temperature, in addition to water, mud is also used for treatment, in the formation of which microorganisms and microflora play an important role. All vacationers can get a doctor's consultation, which, depending on the existing diseases, will prescribe a course of treatment. Wellness procedures consist of thermal and ozone baths, mud compresses , hydromassage, inhalations.

In addition to balneotherapy and mud therapy, you can visit the spa programs in Abano Terme. Italy pays great attention to preserving the beauty and youth of women. Therefore, the resort has special programs: “anti-cellulitis”, “anti-aging”, “weight loss”, “anti-stress”, “pressure therapy”, “zone massage”, “lymphatic drainage”, etc. For the treatment of gynecological diseases, musculoskeletal system, joints, respiratory system, chronic fatigue, neuralgia is also suitable resort Abano Terme. Italy will put everyone on their feet, help to cope with excess weight, and will tone the body.



In addition to treatment, in Abano Terme you can visit many interesting sights, take excursions to Lake Garda, Venice, Verona, Padua. In the town itself there are many entertainment programs. Tourists can take part in sport fishing, play golf, tennis or bowling, do gliding or mountain climbing, and ride horses.
