How to choose a 100W LED spotlight: tips and reviews

Spotlight technology on diodes has been a leader in the Russian market for several years in a row. The assortment of such a product is quite large, and it can be purchased at any nearby specialized store. Consider the types of lighting devices on diodes, give tips for choosing, and also note what you need to consider when buying a 100 W LED floodlight.

Types of LED Spotlights

Depending on the scope and design features, you can divide all the floodlight technology on diodes into several types. This must be considered before making a purchase.

Types of LED lights:

  • for external lighting - a street LED floodlight (100 W) is used to illuminate the space outside the house, it has increased power and wear resistance;
  • for installation on the ground (unpaved) - this type of lamp has a rounded shape and is equipped with a leg that is mounted in the ground;
  • matrix spotlight - the LEDs in it are interconnected in a certain order, forming a matrix, due to this a high quality of light output is achieved (most often such lights are produced in a rectangular or square case);
  • linear device - all the diodes in the projector are installed in a row, and a slightly elongated light source is obtained.

There are also varieties of LED spotlights (100 W), which are used to decorate the landscape of the territory, underwater options or solar-powered lights.

How to choose a LED spotlight 100 W street?

The street lamp is highly durable, as it is additionally protected from the adverse effects of various weather conditions, dust and mechanical damage.

What to look for when choosing a street lamp on diodes?

  • degree of protection against moisture and dust - this indicator (IP) is indicated on the package and should vary between 65-68;
  • light emission angle - from 135 to 350 degrees;
  • ambient temperature - the lantern must be operational when the temperature fluctuates from -40 ¬∞ C to +40 ¬∞ C;
  • remote adjustment - it may be needed when lighting the area near the house (using the remote control);
  • motion sensors - a convenient function that allows you to save on lighting (reacts to sound or movement);
  • the material of the case and protective lens - this should be heat-resistant metal or stainless steel, and the lens should be of any transparent plastic.

How to choose a LED spotlight for indoor use?

When choosing a 100 W LED floodlight for indoor lighting, it is also important to consider certain criteria.

Tips on how to choose a spotlight on diodes internal:

  • acoustic motion sensors - most often selected for lighting porches, stairs, corridors and elevators (significantly saves energy costs);
  • degree of protection against dust and moisture - for indoor use, IP should vary between 20-40, for production warehouses up to 65;
  • radiation angle - about 120 degrees will be enough for the room;
  • cooling system - a device with a power of 70-100 W should be cooled and additionally protected from overheating;
  • installation type - the flashlight can be removable, mobile or stationary, which depends on the scope of use;
  • light emission range - there are spotlights with a lighting range of up to 5 m, there are also those that illuminate 15 m forward;
  • remote switching - it all depends on personal preferences.
