Hotel Location

Riu Palace Hammamet Marhaba 5 * is located in the southern part of Hammamet. It is 3 km from the Old Town, where a huge number of historical attractions are concentrated. The nearest airport is Carthage (60 km), while Monastir Airport is 90 km away. If we talk about the nearest tourist sites, it is worth noting the following:

  • Entertainment complex Carthageland Hammamet (3 km).
  • House of George Sebastian (2 km).
  • Yasmine Golf Club (3 km).
  • Medina of Hammamet (4 km).
  • Museum of Religion (5 km).
  • Archaeological Museum (16 km).

Hotel Rooms

The Riu Palace Hammamet Marhaba 5 * hotel has 371 comfortable rooms. For accommodation is the main building with a height of 3 floors, as well as 150 separate bungalows. The following categories of apartments are available for travelers:

  • Standard rooms provide guests with the minimum necessary set of amenities for a good rest. This bedroom has a bathroom with shower. There is also access to a balcony from which you can enjoy the sea, garden or city views.
  • The suite pleases guests with a spacious area, as well as a convenient layout. The main space is reserved for the bedroom. There is also a small living room area equipped with a soft sofa (unfolded can serve to accommodate additional guests).
  • Type B Bungalow consists of a small bedroom and a spacious living area with direct access to the terrace. Outside, guests are provided with comfortable sun loungers with umbrellas and a plastic table.
  • Type A bungalows differ from the previous category in a more spacious area. In the living area there are two large folding sofas. An additional bedroom is also provided. The bathroom is equipped with a bath.

Negative reviews

In order not to make a mistake in choosing a hotel, be sure to read the reviews about the Riu Palace Hammamet Marhaba 5 * hotel. Tunisia, Hammamet is a rather specific tourist destination, and therefore you can be prepared for some shortcomings. Based on the comments of travelers, the following negative points can be distinguished:

  • the buffet menu has a limited number of fish dishes (nevertheless, the variety of meat types compensates for this drawback);
  • there are practically no green spaces on the territory, which further aggravates the feeling of heat;
  • in the midst of the holiday season, the hotel is undergoing renovations (it was necessary to close the establishment for a while or to postpone the restoration);
  • the beach is cleaned only in the morning, and during the day candy wrappers, cigarette butts and other garbage accumulate on the sand;
  • there is no infrastructure and places for walking near the hotel (you will have to walk at least 15 minutes to the nearest store);
  • Wireless Internet is only in the lobby (in the evening it is almost impossible to use it due to the large influx of people);
  • Before checking in new guests, it would be nice to ventilate the rooms, as they have very stale air;
  • smoking is allowed in the hotel lobby, which is why there is always a "smoke screen" (apparently, the ventilation can not cope).
