Atlant Hotel, Adler: photos, reviews

If you like luxury and comfort, then give preference to such an institution as a resort hotel "Atlant". Adler will provide you with a gentle sea and beautiful nature, and this hotel will give home comfort and high standards of service.





Brief description of the resort hotel

Tourists at Atlant Resort Hotel have long been fond of tourists. Adler attracts guests with its beautiful nature and azure beaches, and this institution with high standards of service. Here you will have access to such features as free Wi-Fi, a guarded parking area, a clean pool in the courtyard, a massage parlor and even your own beauty studio. Here, every guest will feel a warm welcome.

Particularly noteworthy are the guest apartments, which the Atlant Hotel offers its guests. Adler will seem even more beautiful and welcoming if you look at him from the windows of a clean and comfortable room. It has a homely atmosphere. To ensure a high level of comfort, there is a full range of comfortable furniture, as well as household appliances and other amenities necessary for a modern tourist.

The Atlant Hotel can be proud of its restaurant. Adler has many similar establishments, but only here a wide range of dishes of different peoples of the world is served. Resort Location

In the very center of the city is located "Atlant", a 3 * hotel. Adler, Ulyanov Street, 14 - this is the address where you can find this institution. It is literally a few steps from the sea coast. As for the distance to the train station, it is about 3 kilometers. But to get to the nearest international airport, you have to overcome almost 6 km.





Numbers of the Atlant case

If you choose the Atlant Hotel (Sochi, Adler) for your accommodation, then in its first building the following room categories will be available to you:

  • Comfort Plus is a one-room apartment designed to accommodate two guests. There is also the possibility of installing an extra bed. Despite the lack of a balcony, you can admire the surroundings, thanks to the large window over the wall.
  • Comfort apartments are slightly smaller than the previous ones. However, this did not affect the number of amenities. It is worth noting that accommodation is possible both on a double bed and on a pair of small beds.
  • Suite is a spacious two-room apartment. The bedroom has a large orthopedic bed, as well as a desk. As for the living room, it is equipped with soft leather furniture, which when unfolded acts as an extra bed.
  • Deluxe Suite is a huge two-room suite. From the previous category, it differs not only in increased area, but also in the presence of its own balcony.




Apartment building "Atlant-Lux"

Apartments of high comfort have been prepared for you in the second building by the Atlant Hotel (Adler). The photos of the rooms demonstrate a luxurious decoration, as well as a large number of amenities. So, you can choose the following accommodation options:

  • The Comfort Plus rooms are similar to those in the first building, with the only difference being that there is a private balcony.
  • The suite has two rooms - a bedroom and a living room. It is worth noting the presence of large panoramic windows, as well as a private balcony. The bedroom has a large comfortable bed, and the living room has a couch, a coffee table and other necessary amenities.
  • Luxury apartments are similar to those presented in the Atlant building.
  • The honeymoon suite is decorated in soft gold tones that create a romantic atmosphere. The highlight of the room can be considered a large orthopedic canopy bed. The living room has soft leather furniture. It also has large panoramic windows and a private French balcony.

Facilities at the guest apartment

The rooms of this hotel will offer you the following amenities:

  • combined bathroom, which, depending on the category of apartments, is equipped with a shower or bath;
  • a landline telephone is used to communicate with the administration and restaurant, and is also connected to the city and intercity lines;
  • Each room has a modern wide-screen flat-screen TV connected to a satellite dish;
  • the minibar always has a supply of drinking water, and for a fee it will be filled with alcohol and soft drinks (can also be used as a refrigerator);
  • thanks to the presence of an individual air conditioner, you can cool or warm the air in the room, depending on the vagaries of the weather;
  • a wireless Internet signal is active, the use of which is included in the price;
  • Some rooms have a French balcony.




Atlant Hotel (Adler) - reviews, advantages

This hotel has received the following positive comments from guests:

  • good comfortable furniture in the rooms;
  • excellent location near the sea;
  • pleased that the rooms have high ceilings;
  • high-quality cleaning is carried out every day, during which towels and bed linen are also changed;
  • quick registration procedure without red tape;
  • beautiful design of both the hotel itself and the surrounding area;
  • Sea view from the windows of some apartments.




Negative reviews about the hotel Atlant (Adler)

Of the negative aspects of living in this hotel, the following can be distinguished:

  • the staff is somehow indifferent and cold not only to the guests, but also to their work in general;
  • the rooms really lack safes;
  • huge lines are always created at breakfast, and very little is laid out (it is not enough for everyone);
  • if you book a room at a promotional price, then be prepared for the fact that on the spot they will demand money from you even for swimming in the pool;
  • poor insulation, due to which everything that happens on the street, as well as in neighboring rooms, is audible;
  • constant inconsistencies with the information indicated on the reservation service (they can be accommodated in a room of a different category, as well as request money for free parking);
  • as for a resort hotel, breakfasts start very early (at 8 a.m.);
  • the rooms located above the third floor do not have wireless Internet;
  • A cosmetic kit in the bathroom is provided only on the day of arrival, after which the maids do not replenish it.

General impression

Speaking about the Atlant Hotel, first of all I would like to note its location. From here it is a stone's throw from the sea, to the bus stop, and from a large number of infrastructure facilities. Also, the design of a hotel with a claim for luxury cannot go unnoticed. Nevertheless, the service spoils the whole impression. It all starts with the unfriendly and cold attitude of the staff. Even if there are any problems, then there is no desire to seek help. It is also worth paying attention to such details as room service and lack of food for breakfast. Attempts by staff to demand money for services that are indicated on the reservation service as free are also unpleasant.
