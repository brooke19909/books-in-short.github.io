Where is the hotel "River Garden Holiday Village 5 *"

River Garden Holiday Village is located in the resort region of Turkey Belek. The hotel has a very good location - surrounded by fragrant pine trees, lush foliage of eucalyptus trees and citrus trees. The aroma of coniferous trees, mixing with the sea air, makes literally every breath in this cozy and quiet place pleasant.

The nearest village called Kadriye is two kilometers from River Garden Holiday Village. The central resort city - Belek - is seven kilometers away from the hotel. The nearest airport is Antalya Airport, which is only thirty kilometers away. Thus, upon arrival you can reach the hotel in less than an hour, which is a very important factor if you are traveling with a small child.

River Garden Holiday Village HV-1 has a private beach. The distance to it is one and a half kilometers. Throughout the day, a free bus runs between the beach and the hotel, which literally takes you ten minutes to the luxurious sandy shore with clear water.





Turkey: River Garden Holiday Village - what a hotel is

This hotel complex is very original and not like most hotels that can be found on the Turkish coast. So, River Garden Holiday Village HV1 consists of several dozen two-story houses located on a large territory of sixty-five thousand square meters, surrounded by beautiful nature. The hotel area is very well maintained and beautiful. There are winding pedestrian paths neatly tiled, graceful secluded arbors, and velvet lawns, and green lawns, and bright, tastefully decorated flower beds. All design elements of the hotel create an indescribable atmosphere of rustic comfort. Excellent service and a variety of entertainment will make your stay in the lap of nature unforgettable and complete.

River Garden Holiday Village is a great place to stay for people of all ages. Indeed, a calm, but at the same time stylish and vibrant atmosphere, as well as a variety of entertainment, will satisfy the needs of representatives of all generations.

River Garden Holiday Village Hotel Policies

In accordance with the rules of accommodation adopted by River Garden Holiday Village HV1, check-in for arriving guests takes place after two in the afternoon. However, if you arrived at the hotel earlier, then, provided that the previous guests have already vacated the room, and the housekeeping staff has managed to clean, you will be checked into the booked room immediately. If the room is not ready yet, then you can leave your luggage in the luggage room and go for a walk around the territory and lunch or take a swim in one of the pools of the hotel complex.

Check out from the hotel on the day of departure until noon. Then it is also necessary to make payment for accommodation, as well as the use of additional services (if you have resorted to them). If you have already paid for the hotel to a travel agency, then you will only have to pay for the additional service. Payment can be made both in cash and with a plastic card of the Visa and Mastercard systems.





As the hotel is family oriented, children of any age are allowed here. At the same time, if a baby under two years of age will be living in your room for whom a crib will be installed, you will not have to pay extra for this. Also free is the placement of a child from two to twelve years of age on existing beds in the room. Please note that only one extra or cot can be put in each room. However, River Garden Holiday Village (Belek) must be notified in advance of the need for this service.

We also draw the attention of tourists who prefer to travel in the company of their four-legged pets to the fact that this hotel does not allow guests with pets.

Entertainment

River Garden Holiday Village: traveler reviews

In general, this hotel for most vacationers left an extremely positive impression. However, some tourists believe that the River Garden Holiday Village is a 4-star hotel that does not stretch for five stars. Mostly their dissatisfaction is due to the distance from the beach. They assure that buses from the hotel to the beach do not run regularly, and sometimes waiting for transport is inconvenient, especially when returning to the hotel.

The rest, the vast majority of tourists were very pleased with both their rooms and food, and service. Many of them would recommend this hotel to their loved ones and relatives.

Cost of living
