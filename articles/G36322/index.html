To the capital at hand

The complex is located in the city of Krasnogorsk near Moscow, just three kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road. The developer is the well-established Progress LLC. It is considered a full-cycle developer. Since 2012, three new buildings have been commissioned by this company, two more are planned to be completed by the end of the second quarter of 2018. Prior to this, Progress LLC successfully implemented residential projects in St. Petersburg and the Moscow Region.





Attractive conditions

The complex harmoniously combines infrastructure and location, high-quality decoration of common areas and high quality construction and finishing materials, modern technical systems and elevator equipment, as well as thoughtful architectural solutions and relatively low prices. That is why the purchase of an apartment in the Yuzhny residential complex in Krasnogorsk is considered one of the most advantageous offers on the real estate market in the near Moscow region.

And no less important, the sale of apartments is carried out in accordance with the terms of 214 of the Federal Law.

Project

The project declaration posted on the official website of the complex indicates that the structure will be monolithic, six-section and with variable number of storeys. The facades are decorated with calm colors - beige and muted terracotta. The height of the sections is from 17 floors to 25. An individual project is developed in the shape of the letter "". For entrance groups and public areas, an improved layout is provided. The address of the Yuzhny residential complex in Krasnogorsk is 18 Zavodskaya Street.

  2. Also by personal transport, but along Volokolamsk highway. About 4 km, then turn left (Pochtovaya street), then go straight ahead 2 km, turn right into Zavodskaya street and after 300 m (on the right) - the complex.
