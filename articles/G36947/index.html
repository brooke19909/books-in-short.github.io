The best sanatoriums of Kislovodsk: rest and treatment

Almost 300 thousand people annually go to Kislovodsk, the best resorts of which provide effective treatment for various diseases.

After all, Kislovodsk, located in the Caucasus, is the country's leading climatic and balneological resort with world renown and 200 years of experience.





Kislovodsk - health resort city

Since the beginning of the 19th century, the glory of the narzan springs of Kislovodsk has spread , the best sanatoriums now use 4 types of local narzan waters. It is warm and cold dolomite and sulfate.

Staying in the best sanatoriums of Kislovodsk allows you to go through a comprehensive program of healing and treatment, as well as just relax and gain strength. The treatment here gives excellent results, for many people after the completed course the remission stage lasts for many years, and the disease does not progress.

In Kislovodsk, nature itself heals! Its temperate climate is suitable even for those who suffer from diseases of the respiratory system, because in winter there are no severe frosts, and in summer there is no heat. In the city, which is located at an altitude of 800-1000 m above sea level, surrounded by mountains, there are no strong winds, but the sun shines more than 150 days a year.

Unforgettable walks through the numerous paths of the magnificent city park, which is the largest in Europe. The air in the park is saturated with useful fumes of conifers growing at an altitude of more than 900 m above sea level.

Kislovodsk: sanatorium them. G.K. Ordzhonikidze

In Kislovodsk, the sanatorium Ordzhonikidze is recognized as one of the best, it occupies 24 hectares of parkland in the city center. It is located near the lower station of the cableway; shuttle taxis run from the health resort to pump rooms.





Accommodation in the sanatorium. G.K. Ordzhonikidze

The sanatorium consists of 4 buildings with different comforts:

  • 4-storey with rooms of the category "standard";
  • 4-storey with luxury rooms;
  • building in 2 floors, the so-called Cottage of astronauts;
  • palace building.

All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, there is a TV and a refrigerator, the following room categories are available:

  • Single and double with a balcony;
  • junior suite for 2 places;
  • suites, which consist of 2 rooms (bedroom and living room).

Those who visited this Kislovodsk sanatorium leave the best reviews about the quality of food. After all, the system of custom 3 meals a day allows you to choose only your favorite and healthy dishes.

Treatment in the sanatorium them. G.K. Ordzhnikidze

In the sanatorium them. Ordzhonikidze experienced medical staff and qualified doctors are involved in the treatment of diseases of the cardiovascular and nervous systems, respiratory and gastrointestinal tract, as well as the musculoskeletal system in adults and children.

In the sanatorium diagnostic center, the causes of diseases are quickly determined and treatment is started, in which mineral narzan waters and mud from the Tambukan lake are involved. All kinds of procedures are done with mineral water: lotions, inhalations, baths, irrigation. An excellent effect is given by staying in a salt room and an infrared sauna.

During a comfortable stay, guests of the sanatorium visit the pool, bar, library, go on excursions.

The health resort is facilitated by the extensive sports base of the sanatorium, which includes:
