Sozopol (Bulgaria) hotels: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Great places to stay in an unfamiliar village are hotels. Sozopol is a city in which a huge number of tourists gather every year. Sightseeing and the opportunity to have a good rest attract travelers more and more. Here are unique picturesque landscapes.

The city has been known for a long time. But as a tourist attraction, he gained popularity relatively recently. Despite this, there are many hotels and hotels. Each institution claims to be the best. You need to book rooms in advance, because you can stay completely without housing. The article discusses excellent accommodation options that have received many positive reviews from customers.





Villa bantantium

Interesting and attractive for tourists is the city of Sozopol. Hotels here are found both elite and middle class. They have all the services at the highest level: from cleanliness to service. This is also the hotel Villa Bizantium. It is located near the beach (three minutes walk). According to reviews of tourists, the coastline is constantly being cleaned. Guests say that you can always order an additional service to further improve your stay.

The rooms are air-conditioned. Internet access and a pool are available on site. For visitors with a car parking is provided, for which there is no additional charge. If we again return to the topic of reviews, here guests are given a welcome gift, which, of course, pleases everyone. Also, many note the beautiful decoration of rooms and a pleasant cozy home environment.



Art complex Anel

Which city to choose on the Black Sea coast if you come to a country like Bulgaria? Sozopol! Beachfront hotels are in demand. The Anel Art Complex has a restaurant, parking and internet. The last two services, by tradition, are free. All rooms are equipped with appliances, some have a seating area. Also, more expensive options have a terrace or balcony. The private bathroom has a hairdryer and cosmetic items.

You can register your entry or departure at any time of the day, as the reception is open all the time. Reviews of the complex make it clear that it is unusually beautiful, the rooms here are very comfortable. There were no complaints from guests about the living conditions. That is why there are always many tourists.





Diamanti

Hotels (Sozopol), reviews of which are left by satisfied tourists, are located mainly in the old part of the city. This area is popular for its well-developed infrastructure. Hotel Diamanti has access to the beach, and guests enjoy sea views from their windows. The restaurant has a terrace where you can have a tasty meal or just sit with a cup of tea. The kitchen focuses on international and local dishes.

All rooms have air conditioning. It has satellite TV and a fridge. If you believe the reviews, then the expensive apartments have a balcony. Cleanliness, service, smiling staff - all this is the hallmark of the hotel. Many come here every year. Due to increased demand, it is advisable to book rooms in advance.





Relax hotel

Relax Hotel is an upscale hotel with a wide range of services (Sozopol, Bulgaria). Reviews about the institution can only be found good. Tourists like the hotel is just a minute's walk from the beach. On the territory there is a swimming pool, the coastline is privately owned. The latter is always clean, and this is also noted by tourists in their reviews. It should be noted that there is free Wi-Fi.

All rooms have windows that offer wonderful sea views. There is air conditioning, TVs. More expensive options include a balcony. Since the building is multi-story, it is equipped with an elevator. The hotel has a restaurant. Guests say dining here is a pleasure. The terrace, where everything is furnished with comfort, only enhances the atmosphere of comfort and satisfaction.





Villa list

All hotels in Sozopol have a different number of stars. For example, Villa List is a four-star establishment. It is located 30 minutes from the beach. On the territory there are two types of pools - indoor and outdoor. Parking is free for all guests of the complex. The rooms have a balcony. In the lobby and in all common areas it catches free internet. All air-conditioned rooms have a TV and minibar. Private bathroom. You can enjoy sea view or city street view from the room. The reviews make it clear that you can have a great time at Villa List while living in comfortable conditions. If you have a chance to come here, do not miss it.



Selena

Going on vacation to the city of Sozopol, hotels will be the best place to stay. There are a lot of decent establishments. However, judging by the opinion of tourists, one of the best is Selena. The hotel is located a minute from the beach. There are all the amenities needed for a relaxing stay. The windows overlook the sea, which is good news. The establishment has a swimming pool, a restaurant with a terrace.

Each room has internet access. It has air conditioning, a balcony and a minibar. The restaurant offers a variety of Bulgarian dishes. There are also musicians who provide an excellent pastime.





Hotel Casa del Mare

As you can see, beautiful, modern and comfortable hotels are concentrated in Bulgaria, which Sozopol can be proud of. They are distinguished by a high level of service and a cozy atmosphere. I must say that Hotel Casa del Mare has been popular for quite some time. Reviews about him are mostly positive. All rooms comply with European standards. Guests stay in clean rooms. For comfort, the rooms are equipped with furniture and appliances. Internet and parking are free. You can register at any time at your convenience.





Sea sense

As can be seen from this article, all hotels (Sozopol) have a good reputation. Sea Sense is no exception. It is located very close to the local beach. There are always a lot of guests. It should be noted that all rooms are very clean, the bathrooms meet sanitary standards. On-site parking, a pool. The staff is ready almost instantly to fulfill any request of the guest. Reviews about the institution are constantly positive. This suggests that the administration of the complex takes care of maintaining the level of reputation.
