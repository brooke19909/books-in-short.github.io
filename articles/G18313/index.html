The recreation center "Tursib", Altai Mountains: description, rooms and interesting facts

The Tursib recreation center has been hosting guests for more than 10 years. They are able to hospitably welcome guests and make memories of their rest priceless. "Tursib" is a recreation center (Altai Mountains), which is today one of the most visited and expensive.

Base Description

The territory occupied by the base is huge. It houses houses divided by categories of accommodation, wellness and bath complexes. There is a tennis court, a restaurant, a summer cafe, a beach with a designated place for a volleyball court, a swimming pool, a frontal place and many gazebos in which vacationers can dine. The Tursib recreation center is located on the Katun River, in the Chemal region of the Altai Mountains.

The base equals Europe in its service: a smiling and attentive staff is ready to come to the aid at the first call.

The complex is built harmoniously, in integrity with nature: log cedar houses standing in the forest, the cold Katun River flowing nearby with a strong current and rapids.





A restaurant

The restaurant is decorated in the Russian style of the last century. Many items of antique household utensils. The banquet room is separated from the dining room by a real Russian stove: large and wide.

A variety of menus can satisfy even a gourmet. This is a Russian menu, complex, custom. The chef is able to prepare dishes from the children's or Lenten menu or to please guests with traditional Altai cuisine. A festive variety of buffet snacks and banquet dishes, a Christmas menu are also offered. The traditionally fulfilled desire of the guests is the preparation of dishes from local fish and game.

Summer cafe

It starts its work in the morning, at 11, and is open until the last visitor. Here you can meet with friends for cozy gatherings and work meetings or read books in solitude. The cafe stands on the Katun River, where, with pleasant music, they enjoy views of the beautiful beaches and the fast river with rifts. If desired, vacationers go down to the beach adjacent to the cafe to sit on a deck chair.

Standard

Standard rooms can be placed in detached log houses or in a six-room log cabin. These are one-room numbers consisting of a bedroom and a toilet room. In the bedroom there is a bed and a wardrobe. Townhouse

The log house houses 6 duplex apartments. The furnishings of each of them are standard and consist of a living room, a bedroom and a toilet room. This category of houses is intended for rental for a long time. The cost of living per day from one person is 7550 rubles, from two - 7900 rubles.

Children under 5 years old live at the base free of charge, but without a separate bed.

Extra beds cost 250 rubles per day from one guest.

In each of the rooms, in the sanitary-hygienic room, there are sets consisting of: towels, shower gel and body sponges, soap, tooth set, shower caps, razor set.

Each room has a minibar included in the price. In it: mineral water, herbal tea, wellness drinks, jam.

For guests, a daily visit to the water park is provided at certain hours: in the morning from 10 to 12.

A huge advantage of each house is its distance to a sufficient distance from each other, so guests sometimes feel like recluses. All houses are built of cedar, and inside it smells, but weak, unobtrusive.

Recreation center ‚ÄúTursib‚Äù, Russian Railways - what do they have in common?

The Tursib base is the brainchild of Russian Railways.

This recreation center was built for highway workers and their families. Russian Railways provides its employees with social guarantees, realizing that a good rest for every employee today is the key to his quality work tomorrow. There were times when the complex was closed to outsiders. But over time, everything has changed, and today the Tursib recreation center will welcome anyone.





Why Tursib?

There are recreation centers near Tursib, and not one. The nearest ones are Berel and Katun. The recreation center "Berel" has one-story summer houses and two-story alpine houses, a small lake in which vacationers bathe. Carry out many excursions of different directions.

The Katun camp site is an old and large recreation center. It has its many traditions and foundations: vacationers here in the 70s and 80s today bring their children here. And besides this, every year there is a festival of songwriters of performers from all over Russia.

The Tursib recreation center has positive reviews about its work. The only thing all the guests complain about is the price of a vacation. Guests give high marks for the service, because each of them is surrounded by attention and care.

The recreation center "Tursib" surprises with its size, design and thoughtfulness. In winter, cleaned tracks, sprinkled with sand, many convenient benches, swings.

The Tursib recreation center (Altai) organizes rafting on the Katun River, hiking to Lake Teletskoye, excursions to the Blue Lakes, Tavdyn Caves, and Korbu Falls. And to stand on the shore, breathing in a fresh woody smell, listening to the birds singing, the sound of pines and rivers - this is relaxation.

The organizers are fishing, but the fish are not always caught, according to the season. You can catch grayling and lenok. These are mobile and strong fish, adapted to the harsh conditions of a mountain river.





Evenings are held in different ways: these are simple gatherings with a guitar by the fire, and discos with animators.

For new arrivals, an obligatory tradition: in the evening they go to the frontal place, sit in a circle and get to know each other, briefly telling about themselves.
