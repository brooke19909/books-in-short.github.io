Types of Mastercard cards

All Mastercard payment cards can be divided into three segments:

  • entry-level plastic cards (Maestro);
  • mid-level cards (Standard);
  • premium cards (Gold, Platinum, World, Black Edition, World Elite).

The differences between the categories are the cost of service, security technologies, payment methods and additional services for travelers.

Maestro electronic card is the best choice for those who do not plan to regularly use the card and do not make card transactions abroad. A payment instrument has some limitations due to its class:

  • cards are mostly unembossed;
  • not all service points support payment through Maestro.

The MC Standard is great for everyday use as well as for traveling. The acceptable price of the service, pleasant bonus programs, the absence of restrictions on use both on the Internet and abroad - all this makes the "classic" card from Mastercard a convenient and practical means of payment.
