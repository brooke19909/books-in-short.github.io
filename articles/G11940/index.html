Banks of Crimea: briefly about reliable organizations

Crimea today attracts tourists and small and large businessmen. Of course, they are interested in the economic situation that has developed in recent years on the peninsula. And first of all, the answer to the question of which banks operating in the Crimea are the most reliable today. This article provides a summary of several of these organizations.

JSC "AB" Russia "

This is one of the largest banks in Crimea. Its main office is located in St. Petersburg. The Bank has a large network of branches, a significant part of which operates in Sevastopol and on the entire peninsula. JSC " AB" Russia "has earned a good reputation in part due to the established relations between shareholders and the President of the Russian Federation.

The main clients of the credit organization are large corporations, among which some divisions of Severstal and Gazprom.





Russian National Commercial Bank (Crimea)

At the end of 2015, Russia was able to take possession of 1.765 billion registered shares that belonged to RNKB - one of the largest banks, which also has a branch in Crimea. Before the organization became the property of the state, five people were its beneficiaries. Until 2014, the RNKB was under the leadership of one of the organizations in Moscow. After the Crimea entered the Russian Federation, the bank was sold to the largest local state-owned enterprise. At the moment, this institution is the property of the peninsula and is considered one of the largest credit organizations .





"Sevastopol Sea Bank"

The organization actively serves many customers on the peninsula. These include all kinds of enterprises and individuals. The main customers are transport organizations.

This Crimean bank functions as an institution that is interested in helping every client. For this, a personal approach is used, the purpose of which is to establish full-fledged partnerships. Bank employees are always happy to provide people with good deposit rates. Railroad and sea transportation of goods is actively developing in Crimea, these organizations are the main shareholders of this financial institution. The banking services provided, according to reviews, are carried out at a high level.
