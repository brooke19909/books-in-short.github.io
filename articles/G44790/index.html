Aqua Fun Club Hotel 3 * (Egypt, Hurghada): hotel description, reviews, prices

The hot sun begins to bake, the first tan appears on the faces, which means it's time to think about the place of vacation. Where do you want to be in your rightful piece of free time? Staying in a stuffy city? Move to the village? Or fly away to a distant and beautiful hotel?



If you like the latter, then look at the Aqua Fun Club Hotel. Budget enough, not so far and incredibly beautiful! Even the name itself is poetic: water, fun! Such associations arise among travelers, and so I want to confirm my fantasies sooner!

On vacation with a light soul

When you leave the house, you always experience a slight alarm. Are all appliances turned off? Will there be any annoying oversight? What if an uninvited guest appears? The unrest is exhausting and I want to spend at least a couple of weeks quietly and serene, worrying only about what swimsuit to wear on the beach. Therefore, it is recommended to choose a hotel on an all-inclusive basis, and it is better to do this in advance (2-3 months before the vacation) in order to be able to choose the best option.



Of course, ideally you want a 5-star hotel, but think seriously about whether this is important to you. In any case, you get a pig in a poke, because before arrival you do not know the exact condition of the rooms and the distance of the hotel from the beach. Sometimes resort hotels for 2-3 stars are much better. Look at the hotel information, ask the opinion of friends. Determine the most important moments for yourself: excursions, attractions, the comfort of the beach area. By pre-ordering, you save.

Why to Hurghada?

A trend has already been established, according to which the residents of Russia and friendly countries choose Turkey or Egypt as a place to relax. There are explanations for this fact. Firstly, these countries are relatively close and do not require a bunch of documents for entry. Secondly, there is a proven service, helpful staff who know Russian. And thirdly, there are relatively affordable prices. And besides, the tourist always has a choice, and he can find out everything about the resorts of Hurghada, hotels and even the work of animators. By the way, in almost all hotels contact information, telephone and fax are indicated.

So it does not take much effort to find out all the points of interest. What makes Aqua Fun Club Hotel attractive? It is worth noting that this is a club-hotel, and quite young. Geographically, it is only 5 km from Hurghada and boasts a private beach. He focuses on young soul and body, so that in it you can meet representatives of all age categories. Very convenient transportation to Hurghada: buses run every 30 minutes.

So, what attracts attention upon arrival? Aqua Fun Club Hotel is both luxurious and elegant. The spacious lounge opens onto a sun terrace overlooking the pool, around which small bars are crowded. For good health

Aqua Fun Club Hotel has a wellness center on site, including a gym, Turkish bath and spa. There are group classes for adults and children. This is a good boost of energy in the morning and relaxation in the evening. If the sun is too hot on the street, you can play croquet or mini-golf. By the way, there are two swimming pools at the customers' service. Do you like swimming? Then there are more interesting things than just lying on the beach or splashing around in the waves of the gentle sea! Go to the base for scuba diving and hunting, where you will be provided with equipment for surfing, scuba diving yachts and even boats with a transparent bottom.



Imagine only those sensations when you plunge under water, see with your own eyes all that amazing fauna, and maybe even get pearls! If you wish, you can visit the city, as there is transport for rent. But there is no particular need for this, because the hotel has a hairdresser, dry cleaning, and a shopping center.

We pass into the rooms

Aqua Fun Club 3 stars in no way inferior to competitors. This is a small but cozy and comfortable hotel. There are 100 double rooms with bath and shower. Each room has a telephone, music system and satellite TV. There is also a fridge, minibar, terrace, if the room is on the ground floor, and a balcony, in other cases. If desired, the client can turn the room into his little house for the period of vacation. Here you can cook, receive guests, enjoy the view of nature. The area is clean and well maintained, and the staff at the hotel are young and well-mannered. Many vacationers with children come here, so the rooms are equipped with everything necessary. Aqua Fun Club 3 * offers the opportunity to put valuables in a safe. For a small fee and subject to availability, you can move to a suite. By the way, this is often used by our compatriots.
