The pearl of Africa. Where is it better to relax in Tunisia?

Tunisia is the real gem of Africa. It is chosen by young people, families with small children and the elderly for leisure. But the needs of the young are radically different from the expectations of the older generation. Where to go on vacation in Tunisia to make the trip a pleasant one?

It is necessary to choose a place to your liking, especially since this African country has something to offer tourists.

Youth resorts of Tunisia

Sousse and Port El Kantaoui - these are two places where it is better to relax in Tunisia cheerful young party-goers. They are not far from each other and are recognized centers of nightlife.





There is everything for a bright noisy vacation: bars, discos, casinos, theatrical performances, dance shows.

The tourist infrastructure of this place is one of the best in the country. In Port El Kantaoui, you can play tennis and take horse riding lessons, and there are also many water activities: windsurfing, water skiing and so on. There is also the only water park on the coast and a huge botanical garden.

Respectable hotels for rest and treatment

The suburb of Tunisia Gammarth is built up with hotels and luxury villas with a high level of service. This is exactly the place where it is better to relax in Tunisia for people with wealth and discerning taste. There are beautiful clean beaches, and pine groves weave coniferous aroma into the smell of the sea. Many hotels have beautiful modern thalassotherapy centers.

Hammamet is the tourist capital of Tunisia. The city is more European than the rest; hotel staff speaks English and Russian fluently. Each hotel has balneotherapy and spa centers. There is no hustle and bustle, a lot of greenery, orange and olive groves. Of the attractions, you should definitely visit Medina, where the ancient eastern bazaar is located.





Where better to relax in Tunisia with children

Djerba island is the most popular place for families with children. The island is connected to the mainland by a Roman road and is covered with endless rows of date palms and olive groves. The beaches are strewn with fine clean sand, and the entrance to the warm sea is gentle and safe even for kids.

Children will love the beautiful pink flamingos, while adults will appreciate the seafood-rich cuisine and amazing local wines.

Mahdia is the southernmost resort of Tunisia. This is a quiet place with pearly well-groomed beaches and clear sea. The city has a beautiful old fortress and the ruins of an ancient city with preserved sea gates.

For lovers of outdoor activities

Tabarka is a picturesque place located near the Algerian border. It is called "African Switzerland" for the emerald hills covered with pine groves and oak forests and cliffs that intersect strips of snow-white beaches.





This is one of the places where it is better to dive lovers in Tunisia. The bottom is rocky, and the water is clear even at great depths. Fishing in this area is prohibited, and in the depths of the sea you can see a large number of diverse animals.

There are excellent golf courses and you can go hiking in the mountains. In the evenings, you can enjoy an excellent dinner in fish restaurants and watch performances in open amphitheaters.

What is the most beautiful and unusual place to relax in Tunisia? The reviews of those who visited Sidi Bou Said say that it is here that you want to stay forever, and this Andalusian settlement is so charming. All the old town houses are painted white and blue, and residents are not allowed to change their appearance.

For artists, this is a real paradise, many celebrities created their masterpieces here and historical films were shot. Every twentieth resident of the settlement considers himself a painter, they are so strongly inspired by the bright colors of their houses and gorgeous views of distant Carthage, the mountain and the bay with the port.

Art lovers can stay in one of the small hotels, drink mint tea with pine nuts and wander through the old curved streets in search of their muse.
