Bella Vista Apartments Stalis Hotel: description

The hotel complex has three stars, so it is available to most of our compatriots. The hotel is interesting in that it belongs to the category of apartments, that is, it provides everything necessary for guests to feel at home and not to change their usual way of life. First of all, this concerns food, because in each room there is a full kitchen with all necessary equipment.

The hotel complex Bella Vista Apartments Stalis receives mostly positive reviews, and this is primarily due to its incredibly picturesque architecture. The hotel is a cascade of Cretan bungalows scattered on a hillside. The roof of each house is the next terrace. From all sides the hotel is surrounded by a magnificent garden with an abundance of flowering plants.

Bella Vista Apartments Stalis has just thirty rooms in three categories. They will be comfortable for couples and families with children. The maximum number of guests who can be accommodated in one room is five adults.

Hotel location

Location Bella Vista Apartments Stalis - Crete. This island consists almost entirely of small villages, which over time have become real centers of resort life with a lot of entertainment venues.

As we already said, Bella Vista Apartments Stalis is located on a hillside above the village of Stalis. It, along with Hersonissos and Malia, is the main center of tourist life, where all vacationers from nearby resorts aspire.

From the hotel complex to Heraklion Airport is only 30 kilometers, which allows you to overcome this distance in just 20 minutes. Moreover, there is never a congestion on the roads of Crete.

A beautiful beach is 800 meters from Bella Vista Apartments Stalis 3 *. This distance can be covered in 8-10 minutes along a picturesque path winding along the side of a hill.

The hotel was built a long time ago, but the last update was completed only six years ago, which allows us to talk about this hotel complex as modern and very stylish.





Infrastructure

Many tourists who visited Bella Vista Apartments Stalis in their reviews must mention the unusual atmosphere that reigns on its territory. Usually in the evening, all guests gather at the barbecue area, where in a fun company they spend time with a glass of excellent local wine.

For those who prefer to always travel by car, a small parking lot is attached. It is free for hotel guests. If you are planning to rent a car, you can do it directly at the reception. Here is a rental point with a very quick paperwork.

Reception staff work around the clock, staff is fluent in English, German and Greek. If you speak at least a little spoken English, then solving the problems will be quite easy. The reception offers luggage storage and a safety deposit box.

The hotel has a very beautiful pool with views of the sea and the surrounding villages. Sunbeds and umbrellas are in sufficient quantity around the pool bowl on marble slabs.

On the ground floor of the hotel, which is considered the main one, guests can watch TV in the common living room or spend time in the cafe. Here you will be happy to prepare delicious cocktails or light Greek snacks from local products.

Entertainment

On the territory of Bella Vista Apartments Stalis 3 * you can spend an evening in a karaoke bar or behind a pool table. For lovers of gambling, there is a casino, but adherents of an active lifestyle will appreciate the opportunity to rent a bicycle. Such walks in the surroundings will be remembered by hotel guests for a long time.

The hotel complex has a small tour desk, from where you can go on a trip to the ancient monuments of Knossos and Faistos. Here you can also organize diving or fishing, as well as hiking along an unusual route.





Infrastructure and services for children

Bella Vista Apartments Stalis 3 * hotel is designed for children of any age, but cannot boast of interesting infrastructure for its young guests.

At the disposal of the kids - a playground and a special menu in the cafe, from which you can choose from a variety of delicious dishes suitable for sensitive children's stomachs.





Number of rooms

All rooms in the hotel are decorated in a magnificent style, so being in them is very comfortable and pleasant. They are represented by the following categories:

  • studio for two adults;
  • apartments for two adults;
  • Superior apartments for two, three, four or five adults.

Each room can accommodate an extra bed for children. The view from the room opens onto the sea, mountains and a magnificent beach. From all windows you can see the wonderful garden, which is carefully monitored by the hotel staff.

In the hotel complex there are several apartments designed for people with disabilities. They are equipped with special devices that make the lives of these people more comfortable.

Description of rooms

Almost all rooms have a bedroom and a spacious living room with a modern kitchen. All apartments have beautiful beds with bedside tables and a soft headboard with built-in lamps. Also in the room there are large wardrobes, tables and ottomans. During the day, you can spend time on a soft sofa or terrace, where there is always a coffee table and comfortable wicker chairs. Each terrace offers magnificent views of the surroundings.

The kitchen in the apartment is the real dream of the hostess. In kitchen cabinets there is everything necessary for cooking, and the room itself is equipped with a refrigerator, oven, electric kettle, microwave and coffee maker.

It also has an iron, satellite TV and telephone. Wi-Fi is free in all apartments.





Features of accommodation with children

Kids up to two years old can stay at the hotel absolutely free, an extra bed for children of any age is also provided. Only one extra bed can be accommodated in the room at no extra charge.

Check In Rules

If you plan to relax in the hotel we are describing, then keep in mind that check-in occurs from twelve in the afternoon. At this time, you will quickly draw up documents, and you will find yourself in a comfortable room.

Departure from the apartment is from half past eight in the morning to twelve in the afternoon. These rules can be found on the official website of the hotel complex.





Nutrition

Most tourists plan to cook their own meals when booking a room. But the hotel does have a meal in the bar, where guests can enjoy buffet breakfast or a separate menu.

Many hotel guests note that the hostess herself prepares food, with whom you can always discuss some dishes or ask them to cook them at a certain time. All vacationers were very pleased with the food from the cafe, as well as the bartender, who prepares the most delicious cocktails on the coast.

It's important to know!

Each hotel has a number of features that you need to know before you gather here on vacation.

Please note that smoking in the hotel complex is allowed only in special areas and some rooms. This fact must be clarified when booking the apartment.

Pets are not allowed in the hotel, so your pets must stay home and wait for your return.

The use of air conditioning is paid, you will have to spend five euros per day. The use of a safe is also paid, it will cost you fifteen euros in seven days or twenty-five euros in two weeks.

The hotel is more willing to accept payment by credit cards than cash. Therefore, take a card with you for payments on the territory of the hotel complex.

There is a bus stop 600 meters from the hotel, from where for little money you can reach any point on the island and save on transfer from the hotel.
