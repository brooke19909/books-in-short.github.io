DIY Origami Butterfly: Step-by-Step Guide

Origami butterfly can be a decoration on your desktop or part of any composition. This craft will surprise the child and may be the beginning for further hobbies. Consider in detail the step-by-step instructions for origami butterflies.

Necessary materials

What is attractive about this hobby? He does not need a lot of tools and materials. It‚Äôs enough to have scissors and paper on hand to create an origami butterfly from these simple objects. Paper can be any bright colors and shades. If you create a composition, select sheets in the same color scheme or suitable for each other. Take the size at your discretion, it all depends on what size you want to get a butterfly. The sheet should be square.

Execution technique

Fold the sheet in half so that side to side lies flat, and smooth the resulting bend so that it is clearly visible. Fold the paper to its original position and bend the other sides in the same way. When spreading the sheet, a smooth bend crosswise should be obtained. Next, fold the square with opposite corners to each other, expand and repeat with other corners. As a result, bends in the form of a snowflake are obtained on paper. Two side bends fold inward and, if you press the paper, you get a triangle. From the front side, we bend its corners one by one and turn the product over with the back side. Lay it with the wide line up and raise the bottom corner. Pull it, it should be above the wide line of the triangle. Wrap it on the upper side, thereby securing it, and smooth it carefully. Spread the butterfly by the central bend, squeezing it well. Flip the back side and you will see the formed origami butterfly.

Decoration

You can decorate anything with such original products. Follow your imagination. You can decorate butterfly wings with sparkles, rhinestones. It will look unusual if you collect a family of these insects from regular newspapers. You can even make them in black and decorate your office with them; it looks very modern. Or, on the contrary, decorate the walls of the children's room with colorful butterflies.

This will cheer you up and your children, especially if they take part in their creation. If you do not want to decorate the entire wall with butterflies, you can enclose them in a frame and hang them like a picture.

There are a lot of ideas, implement any decisions with the help of simple tools at hand, resulting in an inexpensive, but stylish design.
