Stone House 3 - holidays on the Mediterranean coast

Description: Stone House 3 * is an excellent Turkish hotel located in the city of Kemer (on the Mediterranean coast). Once this city was a small fishing village, but today it is a modern center of tourism. A huge number of cafes, bars, restaurants, countless souvenir shops are concentrated here. You can get here from the airport of Antalya, which is 50 kilometers from the hotel.



Stone House 3 consists of one four-story building, which was opened in 1996. Minor repairs are carried out annually, but the overhaul was in 2012. Of course, all vacationers will be delighted by the amazing nature and picturesque beaches. Just a few kilometers from the city lie the ruins of Fezelis and Olympos, whose buildings date back to the 3rd century BC. In the ancient city of Phaselis you can see the remains of an amphitheater, from which a paved road leads to the gates of Adrian.

Rooms: Stone House 3 offers 73 comfortable rooms, of which 3 are suites and 70 are standard. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a telephone, TV, safe, cable TV, among which there are Russian-speaking channels. The bathroom has a shower, hairdryer, bathrobe and necessary toiletries. The floor is covered with ceramic tiles. Each room has a balcony that faces the street or the pool.

Additional information: For the convenience of holidaymakers, Stone House 3 has an outdoor pool. Those wishing to explore the city are invited to rent a car or take a taxi. Laundry service, currency exchange, a doctor.

Among the sports activities there are table tennis, darts, billiards. Registration is carried out around the clock. There is a safe at the reception where vacationers can store their valuables during their stay at the hotel. A separate room is allocated for luggage. In the evening, a disco awaits guests.
