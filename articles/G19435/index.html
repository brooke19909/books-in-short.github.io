Club Calimera Sunshine Village Annex 4 hotel: overview, description, rooms and reviews

Club Calimera Sunshine Village Annex 4 * hotel includes one central building, as well as 6 additional buildings adjacent to it. A tunnel is located between this hotel and Club Calimera Sunshine Kreta. The guests of both complexes can use all the surrounding amenities, as well as visit institutions on the territory of each other. The beach is very close, you have to walk 400 meters. For the convenience of tourists there are special buses that will deliver to the desired destination.

Club Calimera Sunshine Village Annex is a 4-star hotel. Among tourists, it has a fairly high rating and occupies one of the leading places among other hotel complexes.





The hotel is located on the coast of Cyprus, in the beautiful and picturesque town of Kutsunari. This institution includes several complexes: Calimera Club 5 * (first line) and Annex 4 * (across the road). More recently, reconstruction has taken place here, the rooms and other facilities included in the complex have been updated.

This magnificent resort place is located on the coastline of the Libyan Sea, it is here that unforgettable landscapes open to the eye, and memories remain for a long time in the heart.

Accommodation

The Sunshine Club Calimera Kreta 5 * complex (Greece) is always glad to its guests. The hotel includes 219 rooms, most of which are family-type apartments. Rooms offer views of the sea, mountains or gardens that are planted around the complex.

In the complex Annex 4 * less rooms. Therefore, in order to settle here comfortably, you must pre-book your desired room. The total number of rooms is 107 apartments.

Wifi

On the territory there is round-the-clock access to the network. Everyone can connect their gadget to the Internet. The necessary passwords can be clarified at the reception. Internet access is absolutely free in the lobby. And if you want to use it in your room, you will have to pay extra for this service.

Apartment design

Each room has a set of all necessary furniture and other household items. The design of the rooms has a soft, pleasant style that does not bother, but, on the contrary, contributes to a comfortable rest. The room has a central air conditioning, the temperature of which you can set yourself. The bathroom is equipped with all necessary items, such as a hairdryer, a set of cosmetics and other toiletries. Satellite TV has a huge number of channels, here you can even find Russian channels and keep abreast of all the news. You can also use a telephone with direct connection.

In the refrigerator you can find all the necessary drinks, such as soda. The use of these products is paid at the time of eviction. Water without gas and sachets with tea and coffee are included in the room rate, there are also chocolates and dragees. The price includes the use of a safe, a key to it is issued upon check-in. Each room has a balcony or terrace, here you can enjoy beautiful views and breathe fresh air. Rooms are cleaned daily except Sunday. Bed linen is changed once every three days.

Nutrition



Club Calimera Sunshine Village Annex 4 * hotel (Crete) operates on an all-inclusive basis. Meals are included. It serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. In the restaurant you can try a variety of non-alcoholic cocktails, appreciate the taste of local beer and taste aromatic home-made wine. Bars are open around the clock, here you can enjoy drinks for every taste.

On the territory there is a Greek tavern. Chefs cook deliciously. Various salads are especially popular, you can also treat yourself to local fruits and soft ice cream.

Main meals take place at the central restaurant Dionissos. A buffet is served where everyone can choose what he likes.

Additional services

Medical staff are on duty around the clock at Club Calimera Sunshine Kreta Annex 4 *, so you can ask for help at any time. They will certainly help in an unpleasant situation, but all medical services are paid. There is a beauty salon on site that offers a wide range of diverse treatments. At the same time, it is important to consider that it is necessary to sign up for any procedure in advance. You can use the car rental service and choose any brand you like right at the hotel. There is also a small grocery store and a jewelry salon.

Entertainment

The Sunshine Calimera Club Kreta Annex 5 * hotel (Greece, Crete) offers a huge number of various entertainments. You can find something to your liking for any age and gender. The administration offers to occupy guests with various sports, such as volleyball, mountain biking and so on. You can also visit the sauna or jacuzzi. For those who follow their figure, there is a gym. On the territory there are several adult and children's pools. In the evenings, a special entertainment program with live music and animators is organized. The nice thing is that all this is included in the price and does not require additional fees.

Services for children

At the request of adults, for the convenience of children, cots or even an arena can be delivered to the room. The service is free, so parents can not worry about how their child will work. A mini club, playgrounds and several pools are available for younger guests. There is a separate buffet for children, they will take food in the company of professional animators. Staying at this hotel is very comfortable for kids of any age.

If the buffet is tired, then on the territory there are a large number of restaurants and themed bars. For example, in a disco-bar you can have a lot of fun.

If guests do not want to relax on the coastline, then all conditions for the pool are created for them. Adequate sun loungers, umbrellas and towels are provided.

Reviews about the hotel Sunshine Crete Village 4 * (Crete) are mostly positive.

Guest Comments

The main advantages of the hotel complex, according to tourists, are its cleanliness, comfort, comfort, well-groomed area. The hotel is ideal for families with children. About accommodation

The upper floors can be reached by elevator or on foot.

Rooms





Standard one-room rooms are incredibly comfortable. Nightlights and other attributes of comfort create a special atmosphere. The furniture has everything you need: a spacious wardrobe, a refrigerator, a comfortable bed, a large bathroom with all toiletries.

The rooms feature spacious balconies that you can even dance on. But it is better to book rooms starting from the second floor. The first is a little damp, and the windows do not receive light well.

The staff is very friendly, all problems are resolved in a matter of minutes.
