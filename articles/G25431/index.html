Hotel "Imeretinsky", Sochi: photos and reviews

The Imereti Lowland, located in a unique location on the Black Sea coast, has always been a popular resort. But the XXII Winter Olympics brought her special fame. By its opening, modern beauties of the building for athletes were erected here, they created a wonderful infrastructure. After the Olympics, all this became the property of tourists from all over Russia and abroad coming to rest in the Imereti district of Sochi. Hotels, hospitably opening the doors to all their guests, offer impeccable service, wonderful accommodation and leisure facilities, a wide range of services. The Imeretinsky Hotel has many good reviews and high ratings, on which we offer a virtual tour.

Location, how to get there

The Imeretinsky Hotel (Sochi) is located in the Imereti Bay, in the Adler district, at: Morskoy Boulevard (Imereti Lowland), the building number of the central building is 1. This place is located on the territory of the former Olympic Village, in the immediate vicinity of such famous objects, like the park "Southern Cultures", "Sochi Park", the station of the electric train "Swallow". To them from the hotel - about 5 minutes on foot. The sea coast is also very close, no more than 50 meters, however, opposite the hotel there is a port with yachts. The beach is a little further. The Imeretinsky Hotel (Sochi) is 6.5 km from the airport, 7 km from the railway station in Adler, 3 km from the center of Adler, and about 30 km from the center of Sochi. You can get there by train "Swallow". In addition to taxis and transfers, the hotel can also be reached by public transport. From the airport, this is bus number 135, which will take you to the New Century shopping center. There you will need to change to buses No. 124 or 124 and get off at the Southern Cultures stop. From the Adler railway station, you can get either on the "Swallow", or by bus number 125 to the park "Southern Culture".



Description

Hotel "Imeretinsky" (Sochi) is a hotel complex, which includes a four-star hotel "Imeretinsky" and a three-star apart-hotel. Upon entering the territory, which is carried out from Blue Street or from Morskoy Boulevard, guests get to a very spacious parking lot in front of the main building of the hotel. This is a 7-story stylish modern building made of glass and concrete. It has a reception, luggage storage, ATMs, restaurant, bar, leisure services. The hotel has two large free pools, the Pyaterochka store, a spa, a sports equipment rental service, including bicycles, a taxi call service, a tour desk, a well-equipped fitness room, a games room, and wireless internet (free of charge). For business events, the Imeretinsky Hotel (Sochi) provides 5 conference rooms.

The Flagship Hall is designed to accommodate up to 600 participants. It is designed so that it can easily transform into smaller rooms (for various groups or events of different directions).

The Frigate Hall has a beautiful design for holding business meetings with up to 12 people. It is equipped with a round table and all the necessary technical equipment for business events. The hall is decorated with large panoramic windows with sea views.

The Galleon, Brig and Battleship Halls are designed for 40 people each. They are also beautifully decorated in a business style, equipped with all necessary electronics, appliances and equipment, and can be converted into a common room for large groups of participants.

Beach

Very close to the coast is the "Imereti" hotel (Sochi). Reviews about beach vacations, however, are not entirely enthusiastic. The reason is that the coast opposite the hotel is a marina. This feature of the location does not bother tourists in Imeretinsky out of season, but tourists are not happy in the summer. They can walk 1.5 km to the nearest beach. The hotel organized a small free shuttle service to the beach, but it has only 15 seats, and it runs only once every half an hour.

The beach itself is also not suitable for everyone. Coverage here is large and medium pebbles. Entry into the water also with stones. Entrance to the sandy beach - 200 rubles each. All guests of the Imeretinsky complex are wearing bracelets upon arrival. For those who settle in the main building - white, for the rest - orange. On the beach, for owners of white bracelets, everything is free. The owners of orange bracelets for a sunbed pay 200 rubles. Those who do not have bracelets pay 400 rubles for a place under the Sochi sun. On the beach there is a lot of entertainment, shower, toilet, bar.

Additional Information

After the Olympics, the resort of Sochi, Imereti Bay , became unusually popular . The hotels here are all new, equipped and equipped with modern furniture, appliances and electrical equipment. Service and services in them are offered at the European level. Room prices are also not far behind European ones.
