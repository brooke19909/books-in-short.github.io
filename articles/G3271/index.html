Samaka Beach (Egypt, Hurghada): Holiday Reviews

Egypt is a country that is popular with tourists from all over the world. After all, here you can enjoy the beautiful sea and warm weather, regardless of the time of year. Naturally, many tourists choose the city of Hurghada as one of the stopping places - one of the largest and most popular resorts in the country. It is here that the small but cozy Hotel Samaka Beach is located.

Of course, during the vacation planning period, travelers try to collect the maximum amount of information about a particular place. So where exactly is the hotel complex located, are there conditions for a rich beach and sightseeing vacation? What living conditions await guests? Is it possible to relax with children? The answers to these questions are interesting to many readers.

Where is the hotel situated?





To begin with, it is worth saying that the El Samaka Beach hotel complex stands out for its favorable location. The hotel is located almost on the shore of the Red Sea, therefore, from the windows of some rooms, guests can enjoy the picturesque panorama. Hurghada International Airport is only two kilometers away, so do not worry about the long road after the flight: you will reach the reception desk of Samaka Beach 3 * in a few minutes. The distance to the Old Town with all its attractions, lively markets and cozy restaurants is only 6 kilometers. This makes the hotel popular among lovers of a rich sightseeing holiday.

Description of the territory and infrastructure of the complex

Hotel Complex Samaka Beach is a cozy and beautiful place for an unforgettable beach holiday. On a large, well-groomed territory, the main four-story building is located, as well as small bungalows built in a simple but elegant style. The whole complex is decorated with blooming flower beds, palm trees and other exotic plants. You can stroll along secluded alleys, relax on the terrace, enjoy the coolness in the shade of the trees or visit the sports ground.

By the way, El Samaka Beach Hotel was built and opened in 1984. In 2007, a complete reconstruction was carried out, so this place meets all international standards.

How many rooms are there in the hotel?

In fact, the Samaka Beach Hotel hotel complex can be considered relatively small: on its territory there are 163 rooms of various categories, including:

  • 90 small standard rooms, which are designed for one or two people (+ child);
  • 53 standard bungalows - these rooms are also designed for the simultaneous stay of two adult guests, but stand out with a larger area and more comfortable atmosphere;
  • 20 family rooms that will appeal to tourists traveling as a family or a large company; Suitable for 3-4 adults.

Of course, depending on your needs, the hotel administrator will help you find the right room.

Hotel complex Samaka Beach (Hurghada): photo and brief description of rooms





Of course, for many travelers, the issue of living conditions is extremely important, especially if they are going on vacation with their children. So what can the Samaka Beach Hotel offer? According to the descriptions of travel agents, as well as reviews of tourists, the hotel provides decent living conditions.

A door from the room leads to a spacious private balcony. Depending on the location of the room, you can enjoy views of the sea, the central pool or the picturesque garden.

The furnishings are elegant. You will find a soft, spacious bed, a table, chairs, sofas, a wardrobe, in a word, all the furniture necessary for comfort. Of course, there is also home appliances, without which the life of a modern traveler is difficult to imagine. For example, there is an air conditioner that can help you easily survive the Egyptian midday heat.

You can spend a free evening watching TV (there are standard satellite channels, including some Russian ones). Guests can use a small safe with a lock, which is perfect for storing documents and some other valuables. The room also has a minibar with a refrigerator, but its filling is not included in the price.

Naturally, each room comes with a spacious bathroom. There is a bathtub with a curtain and a shower head. There is also a washbasin, stationary hairdryer, dressing mirror. Each guest is given a set of towels, which are constantly changing.

Rooms at Samaka Beach Hotel are cleaned every day, so you are unlikely to have to complain about cleanliness. Bed linen is changed less often - 2-3 times a week.

What kind of food scheme can a tourist count on?





Of course, for many tourists, the question of nutrition is interesting. What can Samaka Beach Hotel have to offer? Egypt is a tourist country in which the all-inclusive system is widespread. This means that the price also includes full board meals. This is exactly the scheme this hotel offers.

Three times a day, guests are invited to the main buffet restaurant. Spacious, air-conditioned room with enough tables and chairs for 325 seats. The reviews of tourists indicate that the menu here is quite diverse, so every visitor will be able to compose for themselves a healthy and varied diet.

Another restaurant is located on the beach. You can also visit the bars in the lobby and poolside, where you can enjoy chilled Egyptian-made drinks, light snacks, and alcohol.

In addition, there are fish and Italian restaurants on site. This can only be accessed by prior reservation. And you need to add that dinner in these establishments is paid separately.

Where is the beach located?





Many travelers will agree that one of the best places for a beach holiday is Hurghada. Samaka Beach Hotel has its own private beach area, which is open only to guests. It is located just 100 meters from the hotel complex - you can get here in a few minutes.

The coast, as well as the entrance to the sea, is covered with soft sand. Nevertheless, just in case, it is better to get a pair of special rubber shoes. There is a large shallow area, making this area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe sea perfect for bathing children.

On the shore in sufficient quantities there are sun loungers with mattresses, as well as sun umbrellas - all this guests can use for free. Here, on the beach, you can get chilled drinks and light snacks.

Water activities for vacationers

Guests who prefer a more active holiday will not be bored. The beach has a pretty good volleyball court, where many vacationers organize games and even competitions. On the shore you will have the opportunity to ride a boat, water skiing, canoeing, jet skiing, boat, etc.

It's no secret that the Red Sea is famous for its coral reefs. For those who want to swim under water and enjoy the vibrant sea life, a diving center is organized at the hotel. Here guests can rent the necessary equipment and costumes, purchase a tour and even take a short diving course.

Are there any conditions for living with children?





Currently, people prefer to travel together, with the whole family. And in such cases, it is extremely important for parents to ask if there are conditions for staying with a child on the territory of a hotel. What can Samaka Beach offer in this regard? The hotel is adapted for children, so you can come here safely.

For starters, it's worth mentioning the amenities. Of course, an extra bed will be immediately delivered to your room upon request. In the restaurant you can always ask for a special high chair for feeding the baby. Unfortunately, the hotel does not have a special children's menu. On the other hand, the choice of dishes is quite diverse, so you can always pick up something healthy and tasty. And the kids just love oriental sweets and pastries.

Naturally, your child can have a good time on the territory of Samaka Beach 3 * hotel. In the courtyard there is a spacious but shallow pool where the guys have fun in the company of peers. And the local beach is ideal for bathing children, because there is a convenient, covered with soft sand stretch of the shallows, where the water is always warm.

A child can also have a good time on the playground with a swing. And with the children every day there is a team of animators who organize various training classes, theater performances, team games and evening discos.

Additional service

The owners of the hotel tried to create all conditions so that the rest of the guests was not overshadowed by domestic problems. Dry cleaning and laundry services are constantly available here, the services of which are affordable. Guests can use local parking free of charge, which is convenient for tourists traveling by rented or own transport.

The hotel also has a currency exchange office. You can also visit local shops selling interesting souvenirs, household items and essentials. Despite the fact that there is no medical office at the hotel, in case of problems, a doctor will be called to you immediately.

Entertainment for guests





Despite the fact that the hotel is designed for a relaxing holiday, guests are unlikely to be homesick. In the courtyard you will find a large pool with clean water, around which comfortable sun loungers and parasols are placed. Here you can relax, sunbathe and just chat with new friends. The pool also regularly hosts useful water aerobics classes and fun polo games.

Guests can regularly visit the local fitness center, which has a gym. Guests can also enjoy a game of billiards, squash and table tennis. There is a playground for volleyball and basketball. The hotel has fun animators to help you brighten up your leisure time with fun performances, contests and competitions.

What more can you expect from a Samaka Beach hotel? Egypt is a country with an ancient history, which is the best suited for sightseeing holidays. In the hotel or next to it you can buy a lot of sightseeing tours that will help you not only have fun, but also discover some secrets of ancient Egyptian civilizations.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

Samaka Beach is a hotel that hosts thousands of tourists every year. This place is perfect for a relaxing beach holiday, as there are no nightclubs and discos on its territory. Basically, families with children rest here, because the necessary conditions for a child to live are available. Nevertheless, young people also often visit the hotel: it is located near the center with all its nightly entertainment.

Hotel Complex Samaka Beach: Traveler Reviews

Undoubtedly, having studied the opinions of tourists who have already managed to visit a particular place, you can get a lot of valuable information. And for the most part, travelers accept this hotel favorably. The area here is really spacious and well-groomed - at any time you can relax by the pool or take a walk along the coast.





The hotel rooms are ordinary, but quite spacious and comfortable, and it is always a pleasure to spend time on the balcony. Some guests note the uniformity of the menu. On the other hand, the food is always fresh and served in sufficient quantities, so no one will have to starve. The animation at the hotel is decent: the guys create a mood for all residents in the daytime, but at the same time do not impose anything on anyone. By the way, those who wish can go with the animators to a disco or beach party in the city. The staff at the hotel are friendly and do an excellent job.

Of course, there are some flaws in the restaurant or in the design of the rooms. But you need to understand that this hotel offers a budget vacation: the prices here are much lower than at the prestigious five-star resorts.
