Tsumami Kansashi: a step-by-step workshop

Kanzashi tsumami is a special technique for decorating women's hair jewelry that appeared in distant Japan and conquered the whole world. If earlier flowers of silk adorned the hairpin of geishas, ‚Äã‚Äãnow you can see magnificent crafts made of fabric on paintings and caskets, on clothes and pendants on a bag. Masters create not only flowers, but also wonderful insects, bows and baskets, Easter eggs and even New Year's toys.

"Tsumami" from Japanese translates as "pinch." This characterizes the main way of performing each element by pinching. For these purposes, use tweezers and candle fire. Each petal or leaf is made separately, and then they are combined into a flower or other figure. Instead of the silk traditional for Japan, modern kanzashi tsumami is made from other fabrics. Most often, satin or crepe ribbons are taken to work, sometimes they use nylon or satin, lurex or organza. Connect the parts into a single unit either with hot glue, or sew together with threads.

In the article, we will consider a master class by tsumi kanzashi, we will figure out in detail how to fold and solder pieces of fabric correctly to get a beautiful petal. Step-by-step descriptions of the work will help you independently repeat the samples shown in the photographs. The tsumami kanzashi technique is a bit like oriental origami art, but instead of paper, masters use shreds of fabric.

Triple leaf

In order to make such a multi-layer leaf:

  1. It is necessary to prepare 3 identical pieces of a thin green satin ribbon, a candle, tweezers and a pin for temporary fastening.
  2. Fold the blanks in a flat pile and bend the entire bundle of fabric in half.
  3. Holding the tweezers, bring the fold line close to the candle and melt the edges of the fabric with fire.
  4. The photo below clearly shows how to straighten the ribbons to get an effective leaf.
  5. Connect the lower ends of each segment together and temporarily fix with a pin.
  6. It remains to melt with a candle and this detail of the craft.
  7. Put the finished leaf aside. It will be needed to join a bouquet of flowers.

Temporarily fasten the bundle with a pin.

Light flower of five petals

From the satin ribbon, cut the same five squares. Further work is carried out alternately on the manufacture of each petal. A square piece of fabric folds in half diagonally. It turns out a triangle, the extreme corners of which are also connected to each other. Turn the workpiece toward you with the long side, and grab the opposite with tweezers and scorch the candle along the entire length. You get a petal like in the photo below.

When all 5 elements are ready, you need to turn the blanks on the other side so that traces of the candle remain on the back of the petal. It will remain the threads selected in the tone of the fabric to sew all the details together to make a beautiful flower.

Christmas angels

Gorgeous snowflake

Christmas tree on a cone

A beautiful Christmas tree decoration will be the Christmas tree made in this technique of Japanese masters. You will need a plain satin or crepe ribbon. We took a white strip in our sample, you can make a green or red tree. You will also need a cardboard base for gluing small parts in the shape of a cone. They took bright golden, red and purple strips of fabric as a decor, decorating the top and bottom of the Christmas tree with interesting elements.
