Hotel "Cruise", Anapa: reviews, photos, description

Anapa is a resort loved by many Russian tourists. Everyone loves to visit him, from young to old. The fact is that in this city and its environs there is a lot of entertainment. In addition, there is a wonderful climate that contributes to a wonderful vacation. The sea coast of Anapa is very picturesque.

"Grand Cruise" is a hotel in Anapa, which is recommended by many tour operators for recreation. This is due to reasonable prices for accommodation in his rooms, as well as the excellent service of each guest. Once here, guests have full access to many important facilities and infrastructure, as well as entertainment.



general information

The Grand Cruise Hotel in Anapa, the photo of which is presented in the review, is located on the first coastline, almost on the beach itself - on Pioneer Avenue. It is very easy to get to the center from it - you will only need to cover 5 km, which can be easily done with any kind of public transport or taxi.

The hotel territory covers a fairly large area - 8000 square meters. m. There are several buildings and several areas for recreation and entertainment.

From the hotel you can get to the market, which is located in the village of Dzhemete - on it you can buy delicious and fresh vegetables and fruits, as well as household products. The market can be reached on foot - it is 900 meters from the Cruise Hotel (Anapa).

Rooms

The hotel is quite large - it includes 215 apartments, which are divided into several categories: economy, standard, luxury and apartments. All of them are located in four buildings, each of which extends up to five floors. The buildings have been well repaired; for the convenience of guests and staff, elevators are installed in them.

Economy

The rooms of the Cruise Hotel in Anapa economy class occupy small areas - 14-16 square meters. m each. Throughout the recreation area, there are 16 pieces. All of them are single and equipped with only a minimal set of furniture, which includes a single bed, a mirror, a safe, a telephone and a plasma TV. Each room of this type has a private bathroom, which has all the necessary bath accessories, as well as bathrobes and towels.

The cost of living in an economy room for one person, including the complex of all possible services and meals, will be approximately 1,500 rubles per day.



Standard

Standard rooms, of which there are 178 pieces in the Grand Cruise 3 * Hotel (Anapa), are designed to accommodate two guests. If necessary, the sofa, which is located in each room, can be laid out and will serve as an extra bed. Each room, whose area is about 22 square meters. m, there is a wardrobe for storing personal belongings, a mirror, as well as a TV with the ability to view satellite channels. They also have refrigerators, which is not in the "economy". In the shower room, everything is the same as in the version considered above, however there is a slight difference - the presence of a hairdryer for drying hair.

The cost of living in a standard class room is approximately 2800 rubles per day.

Suite

Deluxe rooms are famous for their high level of comfort. There are few of them at the Grand Cruise Hotel (Anapa) - only 16 pieces, and each area is about 48-50 square meters. m. Each room in this category is a two-room apartment, which is designed for two guests. As in the standard rooms, there is a sofa, which is laid out and can serve as an extra bed.

The suite is divided into living and sleeping areas. They are equipped with a set of furniture that allows you to provide the most comfortable living conditions. In particular, there are large double beds with orthopedic mattresses, cabinets for things, one mirror. The living room has a large plasma TV, sofa, dining table, chairs, as well as a small cooking area.

Guests of the deluxe rooms can relax on their private balconies, which offer beautiful views of the hotel and the sea. The balconies have several chairs and a small table. Like the rest of the rooms, the suite has a private bathroom.

The cost of living in a suite is from 4300 rubles per day.

For guests there are two rooms arranged in which there is a small living room with kitchen area, as well as a bedroom. These areas are separated by doors. The bedroom has a large double bed and a spacious wardrobe for things, in addition, there is a plasma TV with satellite channels.

In the living area there is a huge soft sofa, a TV and a table suitable for eating, around which there are chairs. There is also a small corner for cooking, equipped with household appliances. Conditions for children

"Grand Cruise" is a hotel in Anapa, which is ideal for relaxing with all the family members. This is due to the fact that on its territory everything is arranged in such a way as to be comfortable for representatives of all age categories. Particular attention is paid to the comfort of small guests. There is a separate pool for them, in which everything is done taking into account safety precautions. Children can frolic in separate areas, equipped for their outdoor activities. Here they can meet their peers and make new friends. In addition to all this, funny animation programs are regularly held here.

The local restaurant has special highchairs and a separate menu for small gourmets, which offers only the brightest and most delicious dishes.



Pool, sports and spa

Guests of the Grand Cruise Hotel (Anapa) in their reviews often turn their attention to the fact that all fans of a healthy lifestyle and sport will certainly find something to do on the territory of this vacation spot. The hotel has a huge gym equipped with a large number of simulators that work on different muscle groups. There is also a huge spa area, which includes a bath and a sauna. Hotel guests can visit the massage room in order to completely relax.

Water enthusiasts can visit the outdoor pool. Its dimensions are considerable - 18x15 meters. Around it is a large sun terrace, where guests have the opportunity to relax on comfortable sun loungers in the shade of beach umbrellas. There is also a small bar where tourists can enjoy light snacks and refreshments.



Beach

The hotel has its own sandy beach with a gentle slope into the water. It is located directly in the recreation area - literally 80 meters from the buildings of the buildings. It is always clean, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay for each guest: there are sun loungers, umbrellas, if necessary, air mattresses are provided, as well as beach towels. Guests can use showers, changing rooms, and toilets.

Reviews about Cruise Hotel in Anapa

Many vacationers share their impressions gained during their stay at the Grand Cruise. In their comments, they often mention the level of service, room interior and the general atmosphere that prevails here.

Guests often note a high level of service, and absolutely in all areas of the hotel. As many of them say, his rooms are always clean, his linen is fresh - this is what maids do, who know a lot about their work. The reception desk is run by administrators who are polite and can always give an answer to any question that concerns the vacationer related to the residence or location of tourist facilities.



The rooms, according to tourists, are very comfortable. Those who relaxed in an apartment or suite happily notice the beautiful view that opens from the balconies. Each room is always fresh, which is provided thanks to the climate control system, which is installed throughout the hotel. If we talk about the atmosphere in general, then it is laid-back, conducive to good rest and relaxation.

Some guests also note that tourists always have the opportunity to order a transfer service to the hotel, which is very convenient. It is usually paid separately, but some tour operators already include it in the price of the permit. In this case, the guests will be delivered as soon as possible and in pleasant conditions. The transfer fee from Anapa Airport is 500 rubles, and from the railway station - 300 rubles. There is the possibility of delivery from other points of the city, however, you need to find out all the nuances by the phone number that the hotel administration indicates.

Hotel Location

The address where you can find the Cruise Hotel is Anapa, Pionersky Prospekt 81. It is practically in the center of the city, next to the rest houses "Sea Lily" and "Grand Surf" 3 *.

Not far from it there is a large number of places for entertainment that can be visited by the whole family: amusement parks, restaurants, cafes, bars, night clubs.
