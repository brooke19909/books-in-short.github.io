Good hostels in Moscow: rating, description, addresses

Almost everyone who comes to a foreign city, for some time there is an urgent need to arrange their place of residence. Arriving in the capital of Russia, any tourist has the opportunity to settle in one of the hostels, which are many in the city. They belong to different categories of comfort and the price per night in them varies.

So what are the best hostels in Moscow? Where should a tourist stay during their stay in this wonderful city? Consider the top 10 in the rating "Best Hostels in Moscow".

10th place. "On Mayakovskaya"

A good hostel in Moscow, an inexpensive room in which anyone can rent, is called "On Mayakovskaya". It is so named because of its location near the metro station with the same name.

The attention of the guests here is presented by gender-separated rooms, each of which is designed for 8 people. Each room is serviced daily and bed linen is changed. Guests have the opportunity to use free internet access, and all this - from 400 rubles per day.

Hostel "On Mayakovskaya" has common amenities, including a kitchen equipped with necessary equipment, as well as a bathroom with a hairdryer, iron, ironing board and a washing machine.

You can find this hostel in Moscow, near the entrance to the Mayakovskaya metro station.



9th place. "Vysotsky"

Vysotsky is a small but good hostel in Moscow, located in the legendary district of the city - on Taganka. Here, every detail of the interior recalls the great performer of bard songs - Vladimir Vysotsky. That is why his fans often stop here. This place is also known under another name - "In the Library."

Hostel "Vysotsky" offers guests rooms designed for separate settlement of men and women. The men's rooms can accommodate 8 people, and the women's rooms - 4. You can also do this by contacting the administration in any other way that is indicated there. Booking is free of charge, and the cost of the cheapest room here is 600 rubles per day. You can stay here for a whole month - in this case, accommodation will cost from 8,000 rubles (for men) and from 9,000 rubles (for women).

Hostel "Vysotsky" ("In the Library") is located near the metro station "Taganskaya", at the address: Moscow, 2 Nikoloyamsky lane.



8th place. "Spring"

Hostel "Spring" is a place where any guest will feel very comfortable. It is located a five-minute walk from the metro stations Krasnye Vorota and Kurskaya, and also close to four major railway stations. Not far from its location are Red Square and Chistye Prudy.

There are very comfortable rooms, designed for a different number of guests (2-14 people), it also provides for the rental of a family room. In addition to the necessary furniture, each room has air conditioning and Wi-Fi access. Guests have the opportunity to use the equipped kitchen, showers and latrines. All guests are offered free tea, which guests consider a very welcoming gesture. The cost of rooms in this hostel varies from 400 to 2000 rubles.

Hostel "Spring" is located at the address: Moscow, Staraya Basmannaya, 5.



7th place. "Iris"

This is where you need to stay if the purpose of a visit to the city is to get acquainted with its main attractions: monasteries, architectural monuments, concert halls, theaters. Iris is the best hostel in Moscow in the city center.

The guests of the hostel live in a clean and comfortable atmosphere, there is good modern furniture and some appliances.

The hostel is located in a historic building, which was built back in 1913 and until 2006 functioned as a profitable institution. This establishment has a large and eventful history that can be found out by all the guests who stop to stay at the Iris Hostel. The cheapest room here costs 500 rubles per day.

Hostel "Iris" is located at: Moscow, 4-Tverskaya-Yamskaya, 5.



6th place. Admiral

Admiral is a good hostel in Moscow near the Belorusskaya metro station. It can stay both for a day and for a month. The rooms in it are designed for the number of guests from 4 to 12 people. Each room here has a certain number of beds in the form of 1.5-sleeping beds.

Each guest here receives free tea and coffee from the hostel. It also provides daily housekeeping and replacement of bedding with clean linen. Guests have the opportunity to use free Wi-Fi, as well as photocopy and printing services. All these amenities are provided to guests at a price of 600 rubles per night.

Hostel "Admiral" is located at: Moscow, Bolshoi Tishinsky Lane, 26/15.



5th place. "Aviator"

Aviator is a good hostel in Moscow, which is located near the square of three stations. This location is very beneficial for owners of the institution, as well as incredibly convenient for tourists who, getting off the train, already have the opportunity to stay in a room with good conditions.

The Aviator hostel has a prohibition on an ongoing basis, and for each guest there is free access to the Internet.

Guests have the opportunity to choose a room for any number of guests: from 2 to 8. Each room has a plasma TV, the corresponding number of comfortable new beds, as well as a well-equipped shared kitchen and a washing machine. The cost of the cheapest room in this hostel is 600 rubles per day.

Hostel Aviator is located at Moscow, Entuziastov Highway 26.



4th place. "Barberry"

This affordable hostel is located in the very center of the capital, near Paveletskaya Square, on the Garden Ring. Not far from this place there are metro stations Serpukhovskaya and Paveletskaya.

This hostel offers rooms designed for different numbers of guests (2-10 people). All are equipped with modern furniture and electrical appliances. It provides safes for storing personal belongings, as well as a washing machine, shoe dryers and a hairdryer. In addition to the main rooms for living, there are common amenities: a kitchen and restrooms. Hostel guests have the opportunity to use free internet access. The entire specified package is provided to people living here at a price of 700 rubles per day.

According to guests, Barberry is a good hostel in Moscow, where you can really relax from a grueling trip or hiking around the city. Here, every guest is very attentive, which creates an atmosphere of comfort and warmth.

Barberry is located at 29 Valovaya Street, Moscow.

3rd place. ABC Hostel

This place to sleep is in a very interesting place - in the historic "House on the Embankment." This place attracts many guests precisely because of its good location.

Tourists who are staying in ABC Hostel rooms have the opportunity to visit interesting places of the capital that are within walking distance, for example, the Central House of Artists, the exhibition hall "Lumiere Brothers Photography Center", and the "Information Space" center. Not far from this place are two large stations - "Kiev" and "Paveletsky", and directly opposite the building in which it is located - Cathedral of Christ the Savior. For closeness to such attractions and for the provided range of services, the guests of this hostel pay from 650 rubles per day.

Guests have the opportunity to choose any version of the room, designed from 2 to 12 people. In each of them are provided beds with the necessary number of beds, as well as a small set of furniture. Facilities at this hostel are shared.

ABC Hostel is located at: Moscow, Serafimovich Street, 2.



2nd place. "Academ"

The best hostel in Moscow calls itself Akadem, which is located near the Akademicheskaya metro station. In order to get from its location to the center of Moscow, a tourist will need only 15 minutes, which is quite good. Often his services are used by students of Moscow State University, DAS and RUDN University. The cost of beds in it is average - it starts at 700 rubles per day.

This hostel offers very good conditions to every guest. There is a shower, a large living room, a kitchen equipped with necessary equipment. Guests staying at the Akadem hostel often make new acquaintances in the living room - the atmosphere is at their disposal. The hostel has free access to the Internet, which allows you to always stay in touch on social networks, communicating with relatives and friends.

According to guest reviews, this is a really good hostel in Moscow, where there is a wonderful friendly staff who can always advise on any issues related to traveling around the capital of Russia.

The Academ is located near the Novye Cheryomushki park, the Cheryomushkinsky shopping center, and also near the Trinity Church, at 6 Shvernik Street, Building 1, Moscow.



1st place. "Apricot"

"Apricot" is the best hostel in Moscow, reviews of which are available on relevant sites, as well as on social networks. It is located in the very center of the capital of Russia, which provides its guests with walking access to the main attractions of the city. A 10-minute walk from this place are Red Square, Chistye Prudy, as well as guests of the hostel from here it is very convenient to get to restaurants, cafes, parks and other places for recreation.



There are very comfortable and cozy rooms of various categories (family, double (+ without windows, for 6 guests, for 8 guests). There is also a large kitchen in which there are the necessary accessories for cooking. In addition to the standard set of equipment, there is a dishwasher car Every guest who settles in the hostel "Apricot" is provided with an individual place to store luggage and personal belongings.

Everyone who was a guest at this hostel noted good service, comfortable atmosphere and relatively low prices. The cost of living here starts from the amount of 600 rubles, which is very little by the standards of the center of Moscow. The cost of a comfortable family room here is 2600 rubles per day.

Hostel "Apricot" is located at: Moscow, Luchnikova lane, 7/4.
