Which camcorder to choose: rating, review of the best models

Of course, smartphones can shoot video, but its quality will be very far from what a good camcorder can provide. Before making a purchase, you need to determine the characteristics and functionality of the image recording device. Which camcorder to choose for shooting adventurous rafting and family vacations? Is she a gift to a teenager in support of his dream of a movie career? Should she shoot in 4K? Or do you need to fit into a specific budget?

To help answer these questions, this article provides an overview of the best-rated cameras, which will suit both a novice filmmaker and an amateur.

However, do not go deep into the thicket of the market without clear guidelines. Every customer should know how to choose a good camcorder.

Cost

Which video camera is better to choose: an inexpensive model for 10 thousand rubles or a high-end device for 100 thousand? In this range, high-tech products from the rest of the market are usually separated by a mark of 40 thousand rubles. Although a quality camcorder can be found cheaper, most of them are expensive.

Life style

You should consider how you plan to use the camcorder. Will she always be with her so that she can start filming at any time, or will her use be limited only to special cases? Which camcorder to choose for immersion, which video blog for YouTube, and which to watch on an ultra-high definition TV? Does the future owner see himself as the next Steven Spielberg or does too many additional features make him bored?

Dimensions

Pocket camcorders such as the Cisco Flip are very popular today. They are very compact, extremely easy to use and the footage is easy to transfer to a computer and the Internet. They are inexpensive, usually cheaper than 12 thousand rubles. These advantages encourage many to choose pocket models, but this decision is associated with inevitable compromises.

The optics used in such devices are significantly worse than those installed in full-featured cameras. One way to distinguish them is to compare the optical magnification. If the lens has only digital zoom and lacks an image stabilization system, then this is most likely a pocket model. As a rule, such camcorders do poorly in low light.

Resolution

How to choose the right camcorder for video? Its quality is largely determined by the resolution of the image sensor. Like TVs, digital video cameras come in standard (480p), high (1080p) and ultra-high definition (2160r). Standard models are cheaper and provide video quality suitable for viewing on a computer or conventional TV. HD camcorders shoot widescreen video suitable for viewing on an HDTV. 4K resolution is 4 times better than HD, but requires a UHD TV, monitor, or projector to see.

Media format

The answer to the question of how to choose the right camcorder for shooting can be given by the type of data carrier. It affects size, weight, battery life, performance, and the overall user interface.

Standard definition and HD camcorders can record to hard drives, memory cards, and built-in flash memory. Older formats, such as mini DVDs and video tapes, are almost completely obsolete. Flash memory is lighter and smaller than hard drives, but does not provide that much data storage.

Key Features

After the user has decided on the goals of acquiring the camera, its resolution and format, you can focus on some additional basic functions. These include:

  • Optical zoom. Which camcorder to choose if you want to get as close to the subject as possible? You will need a model with a powerful zoom lens. Magnification is indicated as a scaling factor, so 10x optics will magnify an object 10 times. Some zoom lenses have 48x optical zoom or more.
  • Image stabilization. It is necessary to avoid blurry video caused by hand shake. This is an important feature for telephoto models.
  • Sensor size and lens aperture. How to choose a camcorder for shooting in low light? A model with a larger sensor transmits light better and provides a higher level of detail. Larger (faster aperture) lenses also contribute to higher image quality with a lack of light.
  • Photography. Like cameras, some models offer features such as scene mode selection, flash, burst, and exposure control.
  • Audio recording. Which camcorder is best for shooting home movies with sound? High quality models can record in Dolby Digital 5.1 format.
