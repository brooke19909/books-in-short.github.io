Knitted coats and cardigans with knitting needles: patterns for beginners

In modern knitwear fashion, outerwear models occupy a special place. Who among us has not even once looked at knitted women's coats and cardigans!

Pattern patterns, silhouettes and styles of these models today are so diverse that it is impossible not to applaud the art of owning such needlework. Meanwhile, you can learn how to knit such a knitwear, and this article is devoted to the basics of this ancient and at the same time modern craft.

Where to begin?

Before you start knitting, you need to purchase yarn and tools. Then select the appropriate model. And then build a full-size pattern and calculate the number of loops needed to get started.

Choose yarn and knitting needles

Since knitted models of outerwear are usually made of rather voluminous yarn, beginner craftswomen should not depart from established traditions and choose a thread of medium thickness with a footage of 200-250 m in a hundred-gram skein. The composition of the thread can be any, it all depends on the preferences of the knitter. This can be fully wool yarn mixed with various fibers - mohair, acrylic, nylon, silk or cotton. The variety of types of yarn currently does not limit the tastes and imagination of the knitter.



Knitting needles No. 3-4 are most suitable for threads of any composition and medium thickness. If the yarn is thicker (120-150 m / 100 g), then the size of the needles increases to No. 5-6. They are also selected from the material that the craftswoman prefers, but it is necessary to take circular knitting needles of different lengths: they may be required for the convenience of performing various parts. So, it is more convenient to knit the back on knitting needles with a fishing line length of 80-85 cm, and the sleeve on knitting needles with a shorter fishing line length.

How to make a pattern

It is desirable to use a pattern in the performance of a knitted model: it will turn out more beautiful, it will be better to sit on the figure. The inability to build it according to all the canons of sewing art in the case of knitted knitwear is absolutely not critical, since the plasticity of the knitted fabric is amazing: it hides many flaws. Loop test calculation

After the pattern is ready, you should correctly calculate the number of loops needed for the set. This is necessary for a good fit on the figure of any model of clothing, even such as knitted coats and knit cardigans. Pattern patterns are also important, so before you calculate the number of loops, you should choose a pattern.

In order to calculate the number of loops for the set, knit a sample and, steaming it, measure it. The number of loops in 1 cm of the canvas is multiplied by the number of cm in the part. Thus, the desired number of loops are found. In most cases, it has to be subsequently adjusted depending on the selected pattern and the number of loops in the rapport.

Knitted coats and cardigans with knitting needles: patterns for beginners

For needlewomen who have recently taken knitting needles in their hands, it is better to start with simple patterns. Cardigans made with a garter stitch look perfect, the basis of which is one loop - the front, that is, work is done both from the face and from the wrong side with the front loops.

Knitted fabric tied with a garter stitch - plastic, easy to perform and very effective. Knitted coats and cardigans with knitting needles, the schemes for which are so simple - the best option for beginners.

Structural patterns

Favorite drawings of beginners - structural. These include simple alternations of the front and back loops. For example, very often models of outerwear are performed with a ‚Äúrice‚Äù pattern, where the alternate knitting of these loops changes with each subsequent row, that is, the front loop is knitted over the wrong loop and vice versa. You can alternate not one, but two or three loops. You can increase the number of rows, performing 2-3 rows without changing the pattern, and from the 4th row to change the loop.

Such patterns are valuable in that you can independently conceive any alternation and translate them into the author's model.

Knitted patterns made from braids

Knitted coats, cardigans with knitting needles with patterns of braids, plaits, rhombs or aranas are always in demand and never go out of fashion.

Since the article is aimed at the audience of needlewomen striving for mastery, we will consider simple patterns from braids. The photo shows a pattern of narrow braids turning into rhombs.

The rapport of the pattern is 11 loops, i.e., for knitting parts, dial a number that is a multiple of 11 + 2 edge ones to knit the needles. They knit a braid along the back field, overlapping in each row 2 loops to the right, then to the left. Even rows (all) are performed according to the established pattern. Having knitted 24 rows, they begin to knit a rhombus, and from the 33rd row, the pattern repeat is repeated.

Braids can be small and large, voluminous and embossed. Knit can be tight or loose enough. The master, of course, chooses himself, relies on his own experience, preferences and fulfills the planned knitted coats and cardigans with knitting needles. The schemes whose photos are offered are simple, and it is almost impossible to make mistakes in them. In addition, you can perform the canvas, knitting one braid on the wrong field, placing it along the line of longitudinal tucks.
