GPS GSM trackers: full control

Modern technology makes it possible to accurately track moving objects in any area. For this purpose, special transmitters are used in GPS GSM trackers. As a rule, they are installed with high conductivity modules. Signal transmission is due to antennas.

They are mainly used in the built-in type and do not take up much space in trackers. The receiver includes a chipset. The specified element is capable of storing data on the movement of the object. Modern devices are actively used in cars. There are also watch models. To understand the issue in detail, it is more expedient to consider specific types of devices.





Compact beacons

The mini GSM tracker is mainly made with a built-in antenna. Adapters for them are selected, as a rule, of an expansion type. Chipsets in most cases are used at 35 Hz. They are able to work offline. Tracking device coordinates is carried out every second. All received data is sent to the server.

At the same time, it is quite simple to establish compatibility with smartphones. Many models boast a capture function. When choosing a modification, it is important to pay attention to the sensitivity of the receiver. This parameter must not exceed 3.3 mV. There is a high-quality compact modification in the range of 5-8 thousand rubles.
