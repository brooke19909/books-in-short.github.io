Directly about the hotel

The hotel consists of two buildings, built in 1983 and renovated in 2009. This hotel complex consists of a main building with an elevator and two additional buildings. The buildings - four-story snow-white buildings - are architecturally given the Art Nouveau style thanks to the semicircular arches above the loggias, the modernist arrangement of balconies. It is surrounded by greenery: stylish neat lawns, two types of palm trees and olive trees give the guests an atmosphere of peace and contribute to exercise. On the territory of the Oceanis 3 * hotel (Greece) there are two outdoor pools: a children's and an adult. Adult - large enough, suitable for swimming, and for playing polo. In the daytime, there is a bar next to it, where, in addition to drinks, you can have a meal with Greek dishes.

Hotel rooms

The hotel has 129 rooms. The design of the hotel complex involves three types of rooms. Standard is designed for two to three vacationers. It offers garden views. Excursions

Guests of Crete have the opportunity to choose for themselves numerous excursions. The central one is a visit to the remains of the Knossos Palace, built during the Minoan civilization in ancient times. It existed one and a half thousand years: from 2600 BC. e. 1100 BC e. Thus, the ruins of this castle are three thousand years old! Its territory stores places associated with Princess Ariadne, King Minos and the hero Theseus.



The ancient architecture of Heraklion is remarkable: Lviv Square, decorated with the Morozini Fountain, St. Titus Cathedral ... This amazing city has interesting architecture, it was founded on the territory of Greece, then it was conquered by Arabs, Byzantines, Venetians, Turks. And finally, in 1913 he was again returned to Greece. The architecture of all these countries left its mark on it.

Visiting the Lassi Plateau is also interesting for tourists; its picturesque landscapes with windmills are remembered . Here, in the midlands, is one of the most beautiful caves in the world - the cave of Zeus, its stalactites and stalagmites are impressive. According to legend, in a small pond located in a cave, God was bathed after birth. The stalactites hanging over this reservoir are called the mantles of Zeus.

Guests of the island should definitely see Palm Paradise, located at the town of Wai. The amazing nature of this corner is protected by a special European eco-program. The local palm grove and the white sand of the beaches are amazing!

An unforgettable boat trip to the ancient Venetian fortress of Spinalonga. This structure held the defense for another 50 years after the conquest of the rest of Crete by the Turks. On a small ship, tourists go to him along the bay of the Aegean Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãMirabello. On the way, tourists visit the legendary Lake Voulismeni, where, according to legend, the goddesses Artemis and Athena bathed, the classic folk fishing village of Eloundou.

Children will be interested in visiting the Water City water park, which is located between Heraklion and Hersonissos. In addition to all kinds of water slides, divided into three categories - for adults, for adolescents and for young children, fascinating attractions are also equipped here. Among them, the River for Lazybones, the pool with artificial waves, the Black Pipe, and Kamikaze are especially popular. Adults will enjoy the spa and hydromassage.

There is a wonderful route for lovers of outdoor activities. The largest gorge in Europe Samaria stretches for 17 kilometers. A 6-hour tour does not scare away hiking enthusiasts. By the way, this is the second most visited object of the island. From ancient times, people erected ritual structures in the gorge. The Greeks built an altar to Apollon here, the Byzantines - small churches, the early Christians arranged their burials.
