Sights of Latvia

The country can be conditionally divided into four regions, and each of them is unique. Vidzeme in the north-east of Latvia is famous for the medieval city called Sigulda. It is located in the Gauja National Park. Another attraction of the region is the Vidzeme Sea. A large number of country castles and palaces can boast of Latvia (Riga). To the south of it lies the Zemgale region. Among the picturesque landscapes there is a majestic Rundale castle. Kurzeme in western Latvia is famous for its natural attractions. A popular tourist destination is the valleys of the Venta and Abava rivers, as well as waterfalls. Latgale in the east of the country is notable for its monasteries. The most famous is the Aglona Abbey, located between the beautiful lakes of Egles and Tsirish.
