Sochi-Tuapse - what mode of transport is better to get to?

Before you make a trip along the route Sochi-Tuapse, you should consider your way. In principle, the distance is not very large, and there are many ways to get to the destination.





Trains and trains

The most budget option is the Sochi-Tuapse train, only ten rubles is worth a ride on it. This type of transport runs daily, and there are ten flights. Departure times can sometimes change, but in general, trains run from four in the morning until ten in the evening. Arriving train to the station "Tuapse Passenger". In general, the Sochi-Tuapse railway schedule includes 81 long-distance trains and ten suburban diesel engines. Regarding trains, it should be noted that the first one leaves at 00:02, and the last at 21:05. Time may vary. On average, the time it takes to travel on the Sochi-Tuapse road is a little over two hours. And the fastest train travels 1.43 hours. There is also the slowest one, it overcomes this path in more than three hours.

Bus

You can get to Tuapse by bus. There are several flights. The longest of those that run regularly is the direction of Sochi-Dzhubga. More than five hours will have to go to the final destination. This flight is valid on Wednesdays and Saturdays. Daily go faster, for example, Sochi-Astrakhan or to Nizhny Novgorod and Kislovodsk. Here the journey will take about three hours. The earliest bus leaves at eight in the morning, from the Sochi bus station (this is a flight to Astrakhan), runs daily.





Self trip

Many people decide not to buy a bus, train or train ticket, but to overcome the Sochi-Tuapse distance on their own. "How to get there faster?" - This is the first question tourists ask themselves. And this is a very important question, because you need to think through the route. Before you start planning an independent trip, you need to find out which settlements are found on the way along the Tuapse-Sochi road. The distance between cities is 118 kilometers, respectively, the journey by car does not take more than two hours. On the way to Tuapse, there are such settlements as Volkovka, Vardane, Golovinka, Volkonka, the village of Lazarevsky, Sovet-Kvadzhe and Shepsi. These are all cities and urban settlements located on the coastline. If you go without turning, then you can get to Tuapse as quickly as possible. After all, minibuses on the road from Sochi to the final destination do not go in a straight line, respectively, and the path lengthens. By the way, before you go on a trip, you should calculate the amount of gasoline that will go this way. If you know the distances, you can make this calculation, given that one liter takes 10 km.





sights

Both Sochi and Tuapse are very developed resort towns, which is why tens of thousands of tourists come there every year. In these cities, in addition to the sea, there are many attractions. These include, for example, the Tuapse Defense Museum or the Memorial Museum of Art. Kiseleva. And in the vicinity - dolmens of the Bronze Age and other architectural monuments. Behind the cape is the Kiseleva rock, which is popular among visitors. Sochi is known for its resort base: in the city there are more than four hundred institutions working in the field of tourism. And 120 of them have a "star" certification. And, of course, all the people who come to Sochi visit various sights, for example, the Olympic town.
