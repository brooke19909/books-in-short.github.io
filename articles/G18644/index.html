Qurna lake, Crete: description of how to get

Charming Crete washed by three seas is one of the places chosen by Russian tourists. Holidays for every taste and budget, well-developed infrastructure, unique attractions, amazing nature attract hundreds of thousands of guests to the island, kissed by the sun.

No matter which city travelers visit, everywhere they will find a lot of interesting corners, which expands the possibilities of those who get tired of basking on snow-white beaches. Fans of excursions in a paradise will definitely not be bored.

Natural miracle formed as a result of tectonic faults

One of the most curious natural monuments is the freshwater lake of Kurna, located in the western part of the island, in the Apokoronas area, near the village of the same name. Surrounded by mountain peaks, Kournas Lake, once known as Korisya in honor of the revered Athena of Koresia, is of volcanic origin. Many centuries ago there was a temple dedicated to the goddess, but after terrible earthquakes it was wiped off the face of the earth.



Mentioned in the VI century in the works of the philosopher Stefan of Byzantium, it is the largest in Crete among freshwater lakes of natural origin. True, its size is small - the total area is about 1.5 km 2 . The uniqueness of the reservoir lies in its depth (25 m with seasonal variations of two meters), which is below sea level. Since ancient times, local residents who looked at it from above believed that the natural miracle was bottomless.

An important part of the country's ecosystem is daily filled with waters draining from the Lefka Ori mountain range and the Mati underground karst spring. A stone bag at the bottom, formed as a result of tectonic faults, does not allow them to go to sea. In the north and west of the lake, drainage channels have been erected to allow water to flow into the fields that the inhabitants process.

Peaceful nature

The main attraction of Crete is amazing amazing scenery, which relaxes and pacifies. No matter what time of year you come, Lake Kurna is equally beautiful, and even in winter you can enjoy the wonderful views of the majestic mountains covered with snow, reflected in the clear waters.



There is a steep coast all around the perimeter, completely unsuitable for swimming. And only in the eastern part of the lake is a sandy beach, and in other places swimming is strictly prohibited.

Protected pond

Famous not only for its special beauty, but also for its rare fauna, Lake Kurna is under the protection of the European environmental program. The purest water that can be drunk is filtered through karst rocks. She likes freshwater turtles, living where people do not swim, and this place is fenced with buoys.

There are a lot of eels and other fish that make "peeling" of the feet. The service, which is popular in beauty salons, is available for free.

Qurna Lake (Crete): how to get there?

The natural miracle that is very popular with Russian tourists is located between the two resorts of the island - Rethymnon and Chania, and from the last path to the lake is much shorter.

You will have to go to a paradise in a rented car, since city transport does not stop nearby. How to get to Lake Kurna on your own? The easiest way to do this is for those tourists who rest in the west of the island. You will have to go east on the E75 highway, and after 40 kilometers a small town Georgioupoli will appear, next to which there is a tourist attraction. Coordinates of Lake Kournas for GPS - N 35 ¬∞ 19'936, E 24 ¬∞ 16'757.



There will be a crossroads, and after leaving the highway, you need to follow the signs on which Greek and English inscriptions are often duplicated in Russian. The main guideline is a green train. As soon as you see him, know that the attraction is already nearby. The car must be parked on the side of the road, and go down to the pond on foot.
