Steps for creating a drawing

Before answering the question of how to draw dolphins, you need to carefully understand the body structure of a sea pet. After all, this will allow you to more clearly portray important details. Scrolling through the photos of dolphins, we take up drawing.

Draw a line of motion

First of all, we need to get acquainted with the curve of the movement of animals, since it is precisely to be depicted. It is known that our sea friends have a very flexible body, so it is important to emphasize this detail in the drawing. So, in order to depict the dolphin's movement curve, it is enough to draw one smooth line. After that, you need to figure out the shape of the future animal. Only having coped with this task, you can proceed directly to the image of the voluminous parts that form the basis of the body of our hero.

Phased image of a dolphin's body





So, we have drawn the main line. We continue to understand how to draw dolphins. Let's start with the dolphin‚Äôs body - it consists of two curves, with the lower one curving upward. If we talk about the second, then it should be located higher and also curved up. Only in contrast to the bottom, the upper curve should be more convex. Now we know that in the question of how to draw dolphins, the basis of the drawing is important.

Now we begin to create the fins and tail of our beloved friend. Do not forget that the upper fin should be depicted near the center, but slightly closer to the head, and place the two lower ones immediately below it. As for the tail, it should be small in size and have a triangular shape. On the question of how to draw dolphins, it is important to keep proportions so that all the details look harmonious. Therefore, do not make the tail huge.
