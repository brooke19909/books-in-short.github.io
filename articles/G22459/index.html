Hotel Garni Hotel Koral 3 * (Montenegro, Budva Riviera): traveler reviews

Beach lovers will appreciate the opportunity to spend a vacation in Montenegro on the Adriatic coast. Visa-free regime, mild climate, Russian-speaking staff of hotels and most shops and cafes make Montenegro attractive for Russian tourists. Inexpensive hotel with the European level of service Garni Hotel Koral 3 *, located in the city of Budva, a six-minute walk from the sea, welcomes guests all year round.





Hotel location

Garni Hotel Koral 3 * is located in a quiet and comfortable place of Budva, one and a half kilometers from the city center and within walking distance to the sea coast. Despite the fact that the area in which the hotel is located is not considered central, it has everything that may interest travelers - shops, entertainment and shopping areas, a sports club, cafes and restaurants.

The city center and the famous Budva Mogren beach are just over two kilometers away.

From the Tivat airport to the hotel only 16 kilometers. The management of the Garni Hotel Koral provides an inexpensive comfortable transfer for its guests, which can be reached from one end to the other in less than half an hour.

This hotel is chosen for those tourists who avoid noisy companies and prefer a quiet and relaxing holiday, but not far from civilization.





Number of rooms

Many tourists staying at the Garni Hotel Koral 3 * (Montenegro, Budva) note that the rooms are small in size, but very clean and comfortable. Each room has a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, telephone, private bathroom, balcony, hairdryer, wardrobe, slippers and toiletries. But the guests are dissatisfied with the lack of a safe. Instead, the hotel offers a small drawer with a lock, which, if desired, any robber can simply remove from the room.

The hotel guests are also dissatisfied with the fact that instead of the promised sea view, they received only a small piece of the sea from the room, which could be seen from the side of the balcony.

Rooms and the entire hotel are cleaned daily, but some tourists complain that bed linen and towels were not changed throughout the rest.





Dining at the hotel

Hotel Garni Hotel Koral 3 * offers its guests only breakfast, but they leave only pleasant emotions with guests. Almost all visitors to the hotel praise a varied menu that will satisfy even the most picky gourmets. Everyone will find on the table something to their taste - omelets, scrambled eggs, salads, bacon, vegetables, ham, cheese, granola, hot and cold drinks, milk.

Hotel staff

Almost all hotel guests note the excellent work of the staff, especially the receptionists at the front desk. Everyone speaks Russian and tries to help guests if such a need arises. Problems and emerging issues are resolved immediately, which suggests that the staff is interested in making the rest of the hotel guests excellent.

Many tourists note that the administration often goes towards it if the check-in or check-out does not coincide with the time approved for this. Occupy guests immediately upon arrival, regardless of arrival time. There were times when the hotel management allowed tourists to stay in the room at no extra charge if the departure time of the plane was postponed for several hours.





Infrastructure near the hotel

Garni Hotel Koral 3 * (Montenegro, Budva) is located on a quiet street, but despite this, many tourists were satisfied with the number of shops and cafes that can be found nearby. Basically, they are all located on the way to the beach. At the reception, many guests found out where the best restaurants and cafes are located.

Near the hotel there is a fitness center, which is especially pleasing to those tourists who do not even plan to relax from sports exercises on vacation.

There is no public transport in Budva, so you have to move around the city either on foot or by taxi. You can also rent a car. Garni Hotel Koral 3 * has free parking. But most tourists prefer a taxi, which can be called at the reception.

As for the territory of the hotel itself, many guests were upset by the fact that it was simply not there. A small terrace for breakfast in the fresh air, parking and a small piece of land with trees do not count.





Room prices

Many who flew to Budva on vacation chose this hotel because of affordable prices for accommodation. For the same money, other hotels in the city offer less comfortable living conditions or a greater distance to the sea coast. The surcharge for breakfast is low. Almost everyone who stayed at this hotel during their holidays noted that the quality of service, food and rooms did not disappoint them. In fact, tourists were counting on a hotel of the worst level.

The price for a room with breakfast starts from about 2500 rubles per day.

Hotel Pros

Almost everyone who has ever visited the Garni Hotel Koral 3 * (Montenegro) leaves positive reviews. Particularly noted is the first-class work of the staff. This applies to administrators, cooks and maids. The hotel management does everything to ensure that customers are satisfied with their stay.

Many tourists note that the hotel‚Äôs location in a quiet and secluded place, while not devoid of civilization, makes it attractive for families. The proximity of the beach, located within walking distance, also pleases the hotel guests.

Many who rested at Garni Hotel Koral 3 * were surprised that the hotel offers a compliment to its guests upon check-in - a fruit plate. The presence of slippers in the rooms was also appreciated.

Everyone who was on holiday in Budva at the Garni Hotel Koral 3 *, appreciated the varied and delicious breakfasts. The products here are always exceptionally fresh. Food can be found for every taste. As many tourists note, young visitors also have something to try in a local restaurant.





Cons of the hotel

Garni Hotel Koral 3 * reviews get mostly positive, but there are some negative points. Tourists were unhappy that the five-story building does not have an elevator, and had to climb the upper floors through a narrow and uncomfortable staircase.

As visitors note, some rooms are too cramped, and the furniture in them does not allow you to place all the tourist‚Äôs belongings. This is especially inconvenient for those traveling in pairs or entire families. The more expensive the room, the less such inconvenience. Many guests came across rooms with creaky beds or without hangers in the closets, as well as with a TV with lost settings.

For those who like to sit in a room with a cup of tea or coffee, it seemed unacceptable the absence of teapots in the rooms. The only cooler is located at the reception on the ground floor. Tourists every time when there was a desire to drink a cup of hot drink, had to climb the uncomfortable stairs with boiling water in their hands.

Some tourists expressed their dissatisfaction with the fact that at least a few minutes walk to the beach, but the road passes through a dirty area, which has a lot of garbage. It really can spoil the mood of those who are used to order and cleanliness. And pebbles on the shore and bottom of the sea are not happy with all vacationers. In addition, in the afternoon the sea becomes dirty, which invariably causes discontent among vacationers.

Some guests also noted the lack of a consistently good Wi-Fi signal. In principle, it is in every room, but very often the signal disappears or becomes weak. But this is an insignificant minus for those who came to relax, and not to surf the Internet.
