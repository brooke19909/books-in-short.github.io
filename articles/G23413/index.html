Cone curling iron: a new way to make curls

While some women dream of straight hair, others resort to various means in order to make them curly or wavy. The most popular appliance used for this purpose is the curling iron. I want to introduce you to the latest novelty, which managed to gain popularity among numerous beauties. Meet the cone curling iron.

Appearance



The cone-shaped curling iron looks similar to the usual hair curling tongs , but the working surface itself is somewhat different. It is made in the form of a cone, while there is no clamp that is available in conventional forceps. Such design may seem uncomfortable to many, because it is not entirely clear what will fix the hair in order to give volume. Ordinary forceps secure them securely. But more on that later.

Most of the models of such plates have a titanium or tourmaline coating on the working surface. These materials are durable, strong, and most importantly, they are smooth, which eliminates the likelihood of curling hair. If we talk about the appearance, we also note the convenient handle on which the on / off button is located, in many models there are buttons to switch the temperature mode or select a special program. In general, evaluating the device in appearance, it can be noted that the cone curling iron looks dignified and stylish, lies comfortably in the hand, and the cord does not tangle.

Babyliss 2285BU

How to use

So, as mentioned above, beginners will not immediately be able to understand how to use such an interesting device for curling hair. It's all about the conical shape of the work surface. It is on her that the hair is wound. The cone curler has no clamps, so your hand will play a key role in creating curls. It is with it that you will twist the strand onto the cone and hold it so that beauty does not fall apart. For safety reasons, a special heat-resistant glove is supplied with the device to protect your hand from burns. The conical hair curler is good in that it allows you to easily remove the finished curl from the working surface. Remember how difficult it is to do with ordinary tongs.

Remington CI95AC

When using the appliance for the first time, do not be discouraged if you cannot make the hairstyle of your choice. You should also fill your hand here. A couple of workouts - and everything will work out.

How to choose

If you decide to buy a cone-shaped curling iron, consider a few points. First, pay attention to the working surface (smoothness, coating material). Secondly, choose the model that allows you to adjust the temperature of exposure to the hair in order to adjust to your type. Thirdly, look at the cord - it should be long and spin 360 degrees, otherwise during installation you will have to unravel it all the time, which is inconvenient. Well, of course, give preference to products of famous manufacturers: Babyliss, Remington, Rowenta, etc. The cone curling iron will become your faithful assistant in styling your hair, in giving it volume and the necessary shape. The most important thing is to learn how to use it, and the rest is your imagination and patience.
