Apartment-type hostel in Moscow: definition, layout, interior ideas

One or two young families without children, students and workers who come to Moscow often have to deal with financial problems. As a rule, they are primarily associated with expensive housing. Renting an apartment, buying your own square meters often become unbearable.





Hiding in a multi-bed dormitory or hostel for longer than one month, and even more so constantly, is impossible. We have to look for an alternative. And she is! There is a great option - an apartment-type hostel. In Moscow they can be found everywhere and with any type of acquisition:

  • room rental for short or long term;
  • buying a room on the secondary;
  • buying a room in a new building.

It is worth talking separately about each option.

Purchase in the secondary market and in a new building

In the capital there are a lot of houses that are officially called dormitories. They were originally built for workers. Unfortunately, in most of them the apartments have neither personal kitchen nor amenities. If such a minus is not fundamental, then you can find a room with a low cost. Moreover, such rooms have a very small area (about 15 square meters).

In modern new buildings, the rooms are more comfortable, and the layout allows you to turn it into a full-fledged apartment. What does this consist of? The fact is that they began to build houses more often, rooms in which have their own bathroom and kitchen. There are options with their own loggia. Of course, you can call such a living space a one-room apartment. The living room area can be more than 12 square meters. The cost of a new room depends on the area and conditions, geographical location. At a lower cost, but quality housing can be purchased either on the outskirts of the capital, or outside the Moscow Ring Road.





Of course, it is best for a young family to choose an apartment-type hostel in Moscow in a new building, where there are own conveniences and even a separate kitchen with a balcony / loggia. After all, you have to live on one living space for more than one year. In addition, this option is ideal for single people who want to retire, or for those who do not like to wait long for their turn, to share life with other people.

One-room apartment or dorm room

Among Muscovites and those who wish to stay in the capital forever, the question sometimes arises: an apartment or a room? Of course, a one-room apartment is better . But if the room has a bathroom and a partition with a kitchen, then why, due to a lack of funds for an apartment, not consider such square meters? They are in this case no worse. The only difference is that, most likely, there will be no hallway or balcony. Otherwise, everything is the same.

Room layout

The layout of the living space in an apartment-type hostel in Moscow may vary slightly. But most often it consists of:

  • rooms with a window in which a bed or sofa, table, wardrobe and other things are located;
  • a small kitchen with a spacious set and washbasin;
  • front door.

A huge plus, of course, will be a private balcony and toilet with shower. Usually the entrance to the bathroom is located either from the side of the room, or from the side of the kitchen. Ideally, if there is a place for a refrigerator in the catering unit.

Design your own room apartment

Suppose you bought a living space in Moscow - an apartment-type hostel. But you want to equip the house so that it resembles a real and full-fledged apartment. This is easy to do, just choose a design, clearly plan how the sleeping area and living room, as well as the place to work should be fenced.





Currently, it is possible to purchase cabinets, racks, which are designed to fulfill the function of a wall / partition. Often applied and more budgetary devices - screens.

In which areas of Moscow can I rent a room

If you plan to rent, rather than buy a room, for example, in the center, then you can familiarize yourself with the apartment-type hostel on Taganskaya. For guests are offered 2-, 4- and 6-bed rooms with daily rent. Of course, such apartments are suitable for shift workers, business travelers.

If the place of work is located in the north of the capital or outside the Moscow Ring Road, for example, you can choose an apartment-type hostel in Khimki or Mytishchi.
