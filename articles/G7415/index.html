PayLate payment system: reviews, features and interest

Today, many goods and services can be purchased without leaving home. To do this, it is enough to have a bank card and Internet access at hand. If there is no money available, you can issue a loan at least in the payment system, at least in a microfinance organization. If you do not want to get a loan, you can buy goods in installments online. Specially for this purpose, the PayLate service was developed. User reviews about this system, as well as the scheme of its functioning, we will consider further.

Briefly about the system

PayLate is a trust payment, online installment payment service. The client submits an application for the purchase / service, provides his contact details. After approval of the application, funds are credited to the seller‚Äôs account. A loan is issued to a client on an installment plan with monthly repayments in equal amounts on the PayLate.ru website. User reviews indicate that it is very convenient. It is not required to spend additional time visiting the bank branches. The contract is executed and signed online. Funds are provided for use for up to one year. The maximum you can get 150 thousand rubles. under 3.5%.

Customer requirements

Only citizens of the Russian Federation who have reached the age of majority but have not yet retired (from 18 to 65 years) can apply for an installment plan on PayLate. The list of mandatory requirements includes a permanent residence in the Russian Federation. It will also be necessary to confirm a permanent source of income. To do this, the client needs to provide work numbers of landlines. Like any loan, installment is drawn up for specific needs. Merchants who have entered into an agreement with PayLate immediately provide a link to the service on their website. So that buyers have no problems confirming the intended use of funds. The decision to issue a loan is made within 20 minutes according to the data from the questionnaire.

Loan terms

The service provides loans to everyone in the amount of 3 to 150 thousand rubles. A monthly fee of 3.5% is charged for using the funds. Where, then, by installments? You can use the loan for the first 15 days without interest. As much as possible you can get a loan for a period of 12 months on the PayLate website. User reviews confirm that the loan and interest calculation scheme is simple and straightforward.

It is worth noting that not all outlets are subject to the same conditions. The grace period can vary from 15 to 50 days. It depends on the conditions of cooperation with a particular store.

Payment by installments: reviews

Providing an online loan is not a new service in the lending market. However, neither microfinance organizations, nor payment systems previously provided loans for 12 months. Since all applications are processed through a credit bureau, after successful repayment of a loan, the credit history of the client improves. This is another advantage of the system.
