Hotel Virginia Family Suites 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): description and photos of rooms





When searching for a hotel, tourists, of course, pay attention to living conditions. The hotel has 30 spacious rooms. Each of them consists of a small entrance / living room and sleeping area. Some rooms have a spacious furnished balcony. The windows here, by the way, open, so you can enjoy not only landscapes, but also clean air.

Fun and comfort on the beach





The hotel complex Virginia Family Suites 4 * is located almost on the seashore - you can walk to it in a few minutes. The beach here is clean and covered with soft, golden sand and small pebbles. Of course, you can take a more active holiday, including boating, canoeing, boats, catamarans, water skiing. Travelers here are often engaged in windsurfing, and the underwater landscape is interesting, so you should try to snorkel or even dive.
