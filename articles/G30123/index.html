How to sew a cardigan with your own hands without a pattern quickly: instructions and tips for beginners

If you plan to sew a cardigan with your own hands without a pattern, the instructions and tips below will help you quickly make a stylish product. In work it is best to use knitwear. It does not crease, stretches perfectly and warms on cold evenings.

Tips on how to sew a cardigan with your own hands without a pattern quickly

There are many interesting and stylish styles of cardigans, however, novice masters are best to choose something simpler, which does not require the construction of a complex pattern. For this case, you can choose loose outerwear with a belt that is sewn from knitted fabric or a wide shawl. To begin with, you can try your hand at making outerwear, in which there are no shoulder seams. The main thing is to choose a suitable style and method of cutting. For sewing, it is recommended to use jacket draped fabrics. A knitted or knitted fabric will look great.

Features of tailoring a cardigan

Starting to sew a cardigan, you need to familiarize yourself with some features of this garment, they will greatly facilitate future work:

  • since the product is worn in a cold season, it is better to sew it looser so that it can easily be worn on a warm sweater;
  • most often, cardigans are sewn from knitted fabrics, products from denser fabrics intended for sewing a coat are much less common;
  • knitwear has the ability to stretch, so when sewing a product it is best to use a zigzag seam;
  • Before use, the fabric must be washed and ironed;
  • if for the manufacture of outerwear you choose knitwear with a larger knit, then you can quickly sew a cardigan with your own hands without a pattern without the help of a sewing machine.

How to sew a cardigan with your own hands without a pattern quickly? Description of the technique

If you choose a material whose edges do not crumble, then you can make a cardigan even faster. For size 42-46, you will need a piece of fabric, the width of which will be about 160 cm. With a long product, everyone will decide for himself, since here everything depends on the desired result.

A piece of fabric is folded in half. 15-16 cm are measured to the side from the top down, and 20 cm to the middle of the free edge. From the intersection of these lines, it is necessary to cut 15 cm down - these will be the armholes.

For the manufacture of sleeves, you will need two identical parts of a rectangular shape measuring 70 by 30 cm. They are sewn along the long edge and attached to the armhole.



DIY scarf cardigan

To make such a product, you will need a piece of fabric about 2 m long and about 70 cm wide. If you use a scarf with the right sizes when sewing, then outerwear of a similar plan can be worn on both sides. In this case, it is also better to use a fabric whose edges do not crumble.

Step-by-step instruction:

  • matter is stacked in 4 layers;
  • in the corner you need to draw and cut a neck (its width is about 10 cm);
  • Next, you should deploy one layer of the canvas, after which the front of the product is cut in half;
  • two shelves should turn out, the neck on them needs to be slightly lengthened;
  • the cut edge must be braided; for these purposes, skin or leatherette can be used;
  • after that, the product is lapped from the upper edge to the bottom with an overlap, departing from the upper edge about 25 cm (per armhole).
