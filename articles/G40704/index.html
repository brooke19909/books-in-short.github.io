Metropol 4 * (Costa Brava, Spain): hotel description, services, reviews

Spain is an amazing country that is perfect for a vacation. There is a warm sea, and an exciting excursion program. The original culture of Spain, combining both European and Arab traditions, will appeal to any tourist. For travelers who want to combine outdoor activities and low cost, Hotel Metropol 4 * offers its services, located in the north of Spain in the city of Lloret de Mar.

Hotel location and nearby attractions

The hotel is in the center of the spa town of Lloret de Mar. It is located near the main square, which regularly hosts recreational activities for locals and tourists. Not far from the Metropol Hotel 4 * (Spain) is the famous church of San Roma, which was built in the XVI century. The main attraction of this region, "Castle on the Beach", is about 7 km from the complex. Here, a bus regularly runs from the central square, which takes tourists to the water park for free. At the same time, the hotel is located very close to the public beach of the city. Walk to it less than 5 minutes.





Lloret de Mar is part of the famous Costa Brava resort , which is located in northern Spain, 100 km from the border with France and 75 km from Barcelona. It is famous for its combination of sandy beaches and rocky cliffs. And this place is considered the cheapest vacation in the country. brief information

Hotel Metropol was opened in 1962. Since that time, he has been hosting tourists from all over the world throughout the year. Most often Europeans stop here, among which there are many Russian travelers. The hotel has one seven-story complex, which houses 80 rooms. All rooms are equipped with special conveniences for wheelchair users. There are also non-smoking rooms. Hotel administrators do not speak Russian, but they understand English and Spanish well. But the restaurant has waitresses from Russia who will be happy to help you and answer your questions.





You can stay at the hotel at any time of the year, but the tourist season is traditionally considered the period from June to October. Unlike other resorts in Spain, the climate is milder, and in summer the air temperature does not rise above 28 degrees. The hotel provides accommodation for children of any age, as well as pets. But they should be notified in advance about their arrival. At the same time, the cost of the tour can be increased. Check-in starts at 13:00 and continues until midnight. You must leave the hotel after rest no later than 10:00.

Housing stock

The hotel has 80 small but comfortable rooms. Some of them have a full balcony, and the rest are complemented by the French version. It does not have its own area and is a small bars on the windows. The rooms are strictly non-smoking. The windows overlook the sea or the city. Due to the fact that the Metropol 4 * hotel (Spain) is located in the center of Lloret de Mar, it can be noisy both day and night. The rooms are classic renovated. The walls are painted in light colors and parquet floors. The furniture here has beds, a dining set, bedside tables, a desk. There are two types of rooms in the hotel:

  • Standard Room. Their area is 20 square meters. m. Designed to accommodate two adults and a child.
  • Rooms Balcony Sea View. Three adults can live here. The rooms have a balcony that overlooks the sea. Room sizes range from 19 to 21 square meters. m




Every day the rooms are cleaned by the hotel staff, and the maids hang clean towels in the bathroom. Bed linens are usually changed at least twice a week. The last complete renovation of all hotel rooms was completed in 2003.

Room amenities

Hotel Metropol 4 * (Costa Brava) takes care of the comfortable stay of its guests, so each of its rooms is equipped with all the necessary equipment that you may need during your vacation. All apartments have an individual bathroom, in which there is a bathroom, toilet, washbasin, towels, as well as cosmetics (soap, shampoo, toilet paper). Also, hotel guests can take a hairdryer at the reception free of charge. In addition, all rooms are equipped with the following amenities:

  • TV. Some rooms already have modern plasma models, but most rooms have old picture tubes. A set of cable channels is connected, among which there is one Russian-language.
  • Individual air conditioning. Works from June to October, when the city is hot weather. In the cold season, heating is turned on.
  • Small safe. In it, guests can store documents, telephones, money and other valuables. Usage fees are charged daily.
  • Direct telephone. From it, tourists can call the hotel administrator at any time or make calls to other countries.
  • Ironing facilities. Iron is available free of charge to guests at the reception upon request.

Some rooms also have facilities for disabled guests. For example, wide doorways, a convenient arrangement of sinks and towels, more spacious walkways by the bed. All the stairs of the hotel are equipped with ramps, and such tourists can climb into the room using the elevator.

Service and Facilities

Hotel "Metropol" offers tourists to take advantage of numerous services that will make hotel accommodation more comfortable. The following infrastructure facilities work for the guests of the complex:

  • Reseption. Works around the clock. There is always an administrator who will help solve problems, and also answer questions of interest.
  • The Internet. Works throughout the hotel for free. To connect it, you need to ask for a password at the reception.
  • Dry cleaning. Provides paid cleaning, drying and ironing services.
  • Public parking. Locates near the hotel. Here, tourists can leave their car for free. Advance booking is not required.
  • Luggage room. Hotel guests can leave large bags and suitcases here so that they do not take up space in the rooms.
  • Lobby. In this place, tourists can wait for their check-in or just relax. There is also a TV.




Dining at the hotel

There are several food concepts to choose from. So, guests of the Metropol 4 * hotel (Costa Brava) can only pay for breakfast, book half board or full board. All meals are held on the second floor of the hotel building, where the main restaurant is located. A rich buffet is served for tourists, where you can find dishes of European and Spanish cuisine. There is always fresh fruit, several types of meat, as well as fish and seafood. For breakfast, scrambled eggs and omelets are prepared, cereals, pastries, drinks (tea, coffee) are served.

Refreshments and spirits are available at the local bar. Alcohol is not included in the price. For lovers of local cuisine in the area there are many restaurants and bars offering lunch at a low price.





All about entertainment

Hotel Metropol 4 * (Costa Brava) does not offer numerous attractions for guests. Tourists come here for outdoor activities, so the hotel is used only as a place to sleep. Nevertheless, a gym equipped with modern equipment is open for guests. On the roof of the Metropol is a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols. This is where the entertainment options end. Therefore, coming here, you should think about the organization of your leisure time in advance.

Conditions for children

At the Metropol 4 * hotel (Costa Brava) children of any age are allowed, but special amenities are not provided for them here. For babies, a cradle can be delivered to the room upon request. The surcharge for accommodation in this case will be about 6 euros per night. In addition, only one cot can be ordered in one room. There are no more special conditions at the hotel. In addition, upon arrival here it is worth considering that the hotel is located in a noisy place. This can make it difficult for small children to relax.

Positive opinions of tourists

Tourists vacationing at the Metropol 4 * hotel (Costa Brava) leave good reviews. Most of them believe that the price of accommodation corresponds to the quality of service. They distinguish the following advantages of the hotel:

  • Very friendly and attentive staff who always smiles at guests. Administrators are trying to quickly resolve disputes. For tourists who do not speak English, they explain the information well with gestures.
  • Great location for those who like to stroll around the city. Close to the public beach, on which few people rest, so there are always free sunbeds.
  • A varied menu in the restaurant. In addition to standard dishes, there is always fresh fruit, vegetables, pastries and desserts.
  • The rooms are always tidy, and the plumbing in the bathroom is working properly.
  • Internet works well throughout the hotel, although there are times when the speed decreases significantly.
  • Near the hotel, entertainment events are often held, for example, an orchestra plays or dance lessons are held for everyone. You can watch them directly from the balcony.




Negative opinions of tourists

Hotel Metropol 4 * (Spain), according to tourists, has some disadvantages that can spoil the vacation. Guests note the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Noisy environment. At night, young people walk under the windows, the city hall clocks beat, and in the morning bells ring in the church nearby. Sleeping with open windows is impossible.
  • No double beds. They are replaced by two single spaced together, which are constantly moving apart.
  • Toiletries are not replenished. The bathroom is cleaned extremely poorly. Do not wash the bathroom and sink, but only take out the trash.
  • Drinks are not included in the price, so at dinner you will have to buy even ordinary mineral water.
  • Unfried fish and moldy pastries were found in the restaurant.
  • The rooms have high humidity. Even swimwear cannot dry in a day.




Draw conclusions

Thus, we can recommend this hotel to tourists who prefer an active holiday. It is perfect for those who want to travel on the Costa Brava (Costa-Brava) on their own, and considers the hotel only as a place to sleep. It will be a good option for young people who love vibrant nightlife, parties and discos. And couples with children and older people here, on the contrary, can be very noisy.
