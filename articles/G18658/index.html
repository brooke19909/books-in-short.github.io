What iPads are there?





Before you figure out how to find out which generation of iPad, you should briefly get acquainted with all versions of the device line. To date, Apple has released the following tablet models:

  • iPad
  • iPad 2;
  • iPad 3;
  • iPad Mini (in four versions);
  • iPad 4
  • iPad Air
  • iPad Air 2;
  • iPad Pro

Tablets of the Air version are distinguished by a thin body and light weight, as well as a faster processor compared to previous models. The iPad of the Pro line is as thin as the Air, but even more powerful. There is a standard 9.7-inch and an enlarged 12.9-inch version.

The iPad Mini is characterized by modest dimensions, making it look like a smartphone. The very first and second models are quite weighty, but they are no less reliable than newer gadgets, and they hold a charge for as long.

All iPads are alike. But an experienced user can easily determine what kind of device he is holding in his hands, focusing on its color, type of connector and other external differences.

External differences of the first, second and third iPads

Despite the strong similarities, iPads still differ externally. How to find out which version of the iPad, even without including it? The tips below will help you with this.

The first iPad is easy to recognize by the design, devoid of any streamline or smoothness, a wide front panel in black and, of course, the lack of a camera. If you see an iPad without a camera, this is definitely iPad 1. This model was produced in only one color and only with a black working panel. Another characteristic feature is the three speaker holes located at the bottom of the case.





The second iPad has smoother lines compared to its predecessor. It is equipped with two cameras. A hallmark of the second-generation tablet is the speaker grill, located on the back of the device.

The third iPad is outwardly indistinguishable from the second, it is only slightly heavier and larger. The difference becomes noticeable only when you turn it on: a display made using Retina technology pleases with image quality and color depth. If there is no older iPad model nearby, it is better to refrain from spotting by eye and use a more reliable identification method.

Differences between Air and Pro, how to find out what my iPad Mini is and how to recognize iPad 4

IPad mini cannot be confused with other models because of their size. Outwardly, they differ only in the quality of the picture on the display. The third iPad mini is highlighted with a Touch ID scanner and a golden color. The fourth compact version is slightly larger than the previous ones, and it also lacks the silent mode switch button.





The fourth iPad is not to be confused with the third - it, unlike the third-generation tablet, has a standard 30-pin connector replaced with a lightning version.

"Air" iPads justify their name: they are thinner, lighter and smaller than previous versions. However, the display remains the same size. This is clearly visible in the narrow fields of the working surface.





The iPad Pro is striking in its technical differences - you can easily get confused in its impressive features. Therefore, let's do it easier: it is easy to distinguish the Pro version by the Smart Connector connector on the side of the tablet.

Having familiarized yourself with the above methods, you will no longer torment yourself with the question "How do I know which iPad I have?", And you can accurately determine the tablet version.
