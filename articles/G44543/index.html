Simple spot painting. Equipment

Recently, such a kind of fine art as spot painting has become incredibly popular among the world's population. The basic principle of applying patterns lies in the name itself - a pattern is formed as a result of a combination of points. They can have different sizes and colors, and can also be applied with different paints. The main advantage of spot painting is its simplicity. Thus, even a person who in his life did not hold hands and has no idea how to draw correctly can create his masterpiece.



Spot painting becomes a worthy decoration of dishes in any home. Using various types of paints, you can paint both cutlery and decorative elements of dishes. Putting a pattern on the dishes that are used on the table, you should use only environmentally friendly paints that can be combined with food and at the same time will not cause adverse reactions of the body. If we are talking about decorating decorative dishes, then you can use any paints, but in the first and in the second case, they should be waterproof and not light sensitive.



An easy way to breathe new life into old accessories and souvenirs is spot painting. The technique of this type of painting will depend primarily on the shape of the product and, of course, on the type of material from which it consists. Paints should be carefully selected, otherwise they will not last long on things. Therefore, it is advisable in this matter to take the advice of professional artists or designers who have encountered this type of creativity.

Spot painting allows you to transform the most boring and unexpected objects, make them an element of decor in the interior. For example, using this technique, you can turn a standard glass bottle from under a drink into an original vase. By simply applying the pattern with individual dots on the glass surface, the bottle can be turned into a unique masterpiece. The main thing is to choose the right color scheme and texture, so that as a result, the accessory matches the appearance of the room and logically complements it.

With the help of special paints, spot painting can be embodied on home textiles and on your own things.

For such cases, there is spot painting MK. It describes in detail the technique of applying paints to the fabric, and the features of this process. Spot painting can easily combine in a single style all the textile elements in the room - a bedspread, pillows, curtains, towels, and so on, even though they will be made of various fabrics.

It is recommended that after completion of the work on the spot application of the pattern to varnish the surface (if it is, of course, solid). So, an ornament or pattern will be more securely fixed, therefore, it will last longer and retain its color qualities for a longer period. You can also decorate and complement dot painting with all kinds of beads, beads or rhinestones, depending on the tastes and characteristics of the interior.
