Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort 4 *: tourist reviews and photos

It's no secret that over the past few years, Egypt has become one of the most popular countries for travel. And one of the most famous cities in the country is Sharm El Sheikh. Here you will find luxurious resorts, an excellent climate, clean beaches, interesting excursions and many other attractions. Today, the Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort is in great demand among compatriots. What living conditions and service can you expect? Is it possible to come here with children? What do tourists who have used the resort say? The answers to these questions will be interesting to every traveler.

Hotel Location

The location of the resort Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort 4 * - Sharm El Sheikh, one of the most popular and luxurious resort cities in Egypt. The hotel is located in the popular Naama Bay area, next to the bay. The tourist center with all its attractions, nightclubs, shops and entertainment centers is only 50 meters away - you can easily and quickly walk here. And the Old Town is not far - only 5 km.

The seashore is located 500 meters from the hotel. In addition, the international airport is also relatively close - at a distance of 15 km.

What rooms await guests at Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort?

Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort 4 is a place renowned for its hospitality, comfort and modern surroundings. So what rooms are waiting for guests? Is there a wide choice? The hotel offers travelers 145 options in various categories:

  • 60 standard rooms are 30 square meters. They consist of a spacious bedroom and bathroom.
  • 30 Deluxe Naama Bay Rooms, which are also doubles.
  • 20 spacious family rooms, which consist of a small living room, a separate bedroom, a kitchenette and a bathroom. Area - 50 square meters. This room is designed for 3-4 people.
  • 35 Deluxe Family Suites measuring 65 square meters. There are two separate bedrooms, a small living room, kitchen, bathroom. This room can accommodate 5-6 guests and is perfect for relaxing a large company or the whole family.

At the hotel Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort 4 * there are rooms with special equipment for the disabled. There are non-smoking rooms.

Travel Scheme

What food can the Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort offer to foreign tourists? In fact, tourists are free to choose for themselves: a tour can include only breakfast, two meals a day or the all-inclusive system. And, of course, there are a lot of cafes, restaurants and shops near the hotel. If you refuse to eat at the hotel, you can easily find a suitable place for lunch or dinner.



Beach and water recreation for tourists

What rest can a guest at Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort expect? Egypt is a country that is famous not only for its ancient history and unique culture, but also for its wonderful beaches. Therefore, an interesting water holiday is guaranteed.

The sandy beach is located about 500 meters from the hotel building - it can be reached on foot, or you can use the free shuttle service (the bus runs four times a day). For guests Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort 4 * has its own stretch of coast. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient, and the coastal aground is perfect for children and those who can not swim. Hotel residents can use umbrellas and sunbeds at no extra charge.

Naturally, lovers of more active rest will not remain upset. The beach has a convenient volleyball court. If you wish, you can ride a catamaran, canoe, banana, water skiing. These places are also great for surfing and parasailing. There is also a diving center where you can take appropriate courses, rent equipment and make the first dive under the supervision of specialists - the underwater world is really exciting. Of course, all these services are paid separately.

For many tourists, the priority is the question of whether it is possible to have a good rest with a child at the Royal Oasis Naama Bay Hotel Resort 4 *. Reviews of tourists confirm that the hotel really has all the conditions for an exciting and eventful vacation with the whole family.
