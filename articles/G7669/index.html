How to smuggle a phone through a metal detector?

The use of arched and hand-held metal detectors is one of the most common search methods in any public place, from a regular store, a museum to representative reception rooms and airports. The article contains the most common myths, facts, and the exact options for cheating with a metal detector.

There are situations when, during the procedure of such a check, it is necessary to avoid the detection of certain objects. For example, for some people in such situations, the most pressing issue is how to carry the phone through a metal detector.

First of all, you need to understand how this unit works. In fact, a metal detector is a sensor capable of detecting metal objects of various types and chemical components. The principles of operation of the sensors differ depending on the purpose of the search. When examining citizens, pulsed and induction detectors are most often used. They are harmless to health and the most productive, since there is practically no modern simple way to deceive them. However, in emergency situations, you can try to use some tricks.

Option number 1. Hide carefully. Only works with a hand held metal detector

A hand-held metal detector is a portable device used by a security officer to check for unwanted items. In most cases, in such situations, the human factor works. The employee examines the areas most suitable for storing things, such as pockets, bags, in some cases sleeves, without attaching importance to less relevant areas.

A well-trained person using simple psychological methods of trust, namely: a relaxed relaxed state, even breathing and lack of interest in the search, can easily pass the test and achieve the goal of hiding an undesirable item. In the case where the task is how to carry the phone through a portable metal detector, socks and shoes become an ideal option. It is important only to securely fix the gadget on the body to avoid loss or damage to property.
