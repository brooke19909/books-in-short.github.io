Arkhyz, "Eagle's Nest": how to get, reviews

Arkhyz, a magnificent Russian resort in the highlands, which is becoming more and more popular every day, is perfect for those who love mountains and want to explore some new places. You can have a good time here both in summer and in winter. For those who have chosen Arkhyz as the place for their trip, the Eagle's Nest will be an excellent accommodation option. Such a beautiful name is the camp site located in this village.

Useful rest in Arkhyz

The first thing that comes to mind when we hear the name Arkhyz is, of course, mineral water. And indeed, it is bottled here, in the village of Arkhyz, which is located in the Karachay-Cherkess Republic. The Arkhyz locality begins in the plains and is called Lower Arkhyz. Then she rises to the mountains. These places are called the Upper Arkhyz. The nature here is indescribable. It is here that the village of Arkhyz itself is located.

"Eagle's Nest" is one of the guest houses of this place, which is represented by various cottages. It is worth noting that almost the whole of this village consists of wooden houses grazing here and there cows, rural shops and similar beauty of rural life.





At the same time, due to its climatic and natural indicators, Arkhyz is perfect for a resort place. Like Dombay and Teberda located in the same region, this village occupies a hollow surrounded by massive mountains with magnificent pine trees. Such walls do not allow cold winds here and maintain a very mild and pleasant climate here. In combination with the cleanest local air, all these circumstances make Arkhyz a great place for a relaxing holiday in the lap of nature.

There is also a gradually developing ski resort, which is located not very far from the village of Arkhyz. "Eagle's Nest" is a recreation center in which not only you can have a good time, but also with which it is convenient to get to the ski lifts.

Overview

On the vast green territory there is a great place to relax called "Eagle's Nest." It is also called a guest house, and even a hotel. However, most of all this place looks exactly like a tourist base.





After all, there is a whole complex of cute cottages that are very reminiscent of buildings made in the Alpine style. There is a common kitchen here and several barbecue facilities so that tourists can cook their own meals.

The territory occupied by the Eagle's Nest base (Arkhyz) also offers all kinds of sports grounds, gazebos and other outdoor activities that will make your vacation in this beautiful place unforgettable.

Location

At home, the Eagle's Nest camp site (Arkhyz) is located almost in the center of the mountain village. The exact address of its location is as follows: Republic of Karachay-Cherkessia, Zelenchuksky district, Arkhyz village, Bankovskaya street, house number 7.





Very close to the camp site is the very factory where the famous Arkhyz mineral water is bottled. Also nearby is a hotel called Bankovskaya Dacha, as well as a magnificent pine forest, which is very pleasant to walk at any time of the year.

How to get there

Arkhyz, famous for its mineral water, is located 200 kilometers from the airport of Mineralnye Vody. And just 20 kilometers from the village is a local small airport Zelenchuksky. A shuttle bus runs regularly from the nearest village called Zelenchukskaya to Arkhyz. Travel time is only about an hour. The village can also be reached by bus from Labinsk, Krasnodar, Nevinnomyssk and Cherkessk. The last two settlements can be reached by train.

Cost of living in the Eagle's Nest

Each cottage and room, which is offered by the Eagle's Nest camp site (Arkhyz) for guests, has a price.

For a house for a company of 10 people will have to pay 12 thousand rubles. Studio cottage will cost 7,000, luxury cottage - 5.5 thousand. Extra beds can be purchased for 700 rubles per adult and 500 for a child aged 5 to 12 years.





Standard double room will cost 2000 p. per day, and a place in the block number will be provided for 800 rubles per person.

If tourists came to rest with a child who is not yet 5 years old, then he is entitled to free accommodation without providing a separate place. Pets can also be accommodated for free, but prior authorization is required.

What the camp site offers its guests

The Eagle's Nest guest house has a covered guarded parking lot, which will surely appeal to those tourists who come here by personal transport. For lovers of active events there are sports and volleyball courts, billiard tables. Young guests of the camp site will have a great time at the playgrounds and in the recreation area, where the swing is installed.

You can get together with a large company in one of the gazebos or under a large canopy, and prepare a delicious dinner on the grill or in the shared kitchen. Fans of high temperatures can go to the Russian bath, which is also available on site.





And if there are fans of extreme adventures among tourists, then they will surely like alloys of various difficulties along the river flowing in this area called Bolshoy Zelenchuk.

Impressions of guests about the rest in the Eagle's Nest

Our compatriots, who managed to enjoy the hospitality of the Caucasus Mountains and who visited the territory of the tourist base "Eagle's Nest" (Arkhyz), leave mostly praise.

Without exception, everyone is delighted with the local nature and the territory on which the base is located. Based on the impressions of the guests, it can be concluded that all the cottages are clean and well maintained, there are plenty of barbecue facilities and gazebos for all residents, and they are located in the immediate vicinity of the houses.





Of the minuses, guests of the Eagle's Nest indicate the small size of the shared kitchen and its not very neat appearance. Guests also complain that the path from the base is not far to a store or outlet.
