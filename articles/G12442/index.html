Korostyshevsky quarry - Ukrainian Karelia

Korostyshevsky quarry is a place that thousands of Ukrainians have long chosen. Rest, fishing and just walking through the picturesque forest, especially in fine weather, have long been familiar to the people of Kiev. But not only residents of the capital love this natural attraction, which nevertheless has an artificial origin. People come to see her from all over the country. And even live nearby in a tent or in a neighboring village. Why is this quarry so remarkable? We will talk about this below.



Location, origin, description

Korostyshevsky quarry is located in the Zhytomyr region of Ukraine, near the town of Korostyshev. It belongs to the enterprise of the same name. Here, over millions of years, granite deposits have been formed. The valuable breed began to be mined and used in industry and commerce. Then the production was abandoned, and some of the quarries formed at the excavation sites were flooded with water coming from underground. And these lakes of amazing beauty were formed. There are three of them. They are surrounded by rocky shores, seven to twelve meters high. Therefore, the lakes are considered part of the so-called canyon. Granite mining is ongoing now, but in other places. No wonder Korostyshev is considered one of the largest enterprises for the production of monuments.



Korostyshevsky quarry: how to get there

To get here, you need to drive along the Zhytomyr highway, in the direction from the capital of Ukraine. Not far from the road enveloping the city of Korostyshev, there will be a pointer to a country road. It is she who will lead you to the desired career. You need to drive literally three hundred meters, and you will get almost to the water itself. You can also get to the farthest lake by car. To do this, turn off according to another sign - to the village of Novogorodetskoe. Then you need to drive a bit towards another village. It is called Urban. After it, turn left and you will see the last of the quarry lakes. You can get to these places by bus Kiev - Korostyshev. In this town, anyone will tell you how to get to your career. The nearest body of water is right on Krasny Partizan Street.



Beach vacation

Korostyshevsky quarry is known for its beautiful landscapes and the amazing purity of its waters. They are very transparent, because they enter the lakes from the ground through granite filters. And this breed is extremely ancient - one of the oldest on earth. Probably, the opportunity to swim in such water attracts the majority of ‚Äúsummer‚Äù tourists here. They chose Granite and Pashinsky lake. There are always many tents on the hillsides during the warm season. Trees grow along the shores of the quarry, so the shadow is always provided. The water is cool even in the heat, but comfortable for swimming. And granite platforms on carved rocks form natural diving boards for diving. The depth of the lakes is up to twenty meters. In addition, you can jump from a wooden bridge. And on one of the rocks there is a house, which local traditions call the "forester's dwelling". It also gives picturesqueness to the landscape. One of the most extreme types of water activities on a career is diving. The water is clear and the lake is deep. But one must be careful, because at the bottom there can be huge granite blocks.



Fishing

The quarry itself, as mentioned above, consists of three lakes. One of them - the cleanest and least visited is called the High Stone. He is very fond of fishermen. Of course, with a fishing rod you can sit on the other two lakes, but it is here that few people, the cleanest water and calm, which the Korostyshevsky quarry is famous for. The fishing here is great. There is a good catch in this man-made pond. There are many fish, and it is diverse. Well, if you suddenly forgot tackle, then near the Granite quarry there is an interesting institution called "Manor at the Mushroom". This is a good hotel and restaurant complex. There, right by the lake there are gazebos and all kinds of fishing accessories for rent. Right here, on the grill, you can also prepare prey.



Walking and other activities

Korostyshevsky quarry is not only recreation, swimming and fishing. People come here just for a walk, to admire the fabulous landscapes - because the trees grow, you can say, directly from the stone. Professional photographers love to be here. Pines, birches, dense grasses, mysterious paths in the forest - everything here breathes romance and speaks of nature, which has a soul and a tongue. Therefore, models and simply beautiful girls made this place a cult. Photoshoots are done here, and the pictures are posted on social networks. Mountain climbing is also very popular in the career. After all, after the development, interesting artificial mountain landscapes of different difficulty levels remained. Therefore, climbers like experienced and newcomers like to be on a career. For teens, there are rope crossings over lakes.

Reviews

Tourists love the Korostyshevsky quarry. Rest here is unforgettable. Landscapes on the quarry resemble Karelian. Water is an amazing turquoise hue. There is no sand or silt in it. Movies are often shot here. From each new ledge - a completely different look and angle. For tents nearby - a stream with drinking water. And if you want to live in comfort, then at your service are several roadside motels that will give you shelter and a table and two or three hundred hryvnias per day. In a word, visiting a quarry is a rest for the eyes and soul. But many travelers who have been here write that the main thing is for vacationers to keep order and remove garbage. Otherwise, these beautiful places can turn into a landfill.
