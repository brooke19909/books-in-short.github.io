Magnitogorsk: reviews of residents and tourists, infrastructure, economy and attractions of the city

Magnitogorsk is a city located in Russia, in one of its most picturesque corners - in the Urals. Many tourists tend to visit it, so there are some features that attract travelers not only from other regions of Russia, but also from different countries of the world.

So, we will consider in more detail some features of the city, its main attractions, and dwell on historical facts.

General information

The reviews of Magnitogorsk indicate that this settlement is very picturesque and attracts tourists with a huge number of interesting places. The city has some features. One of them is that it is located at the intersection of the borders of Europe and Asia, and the second is that Magnitogorsk is one of the few cities in Russia that were founded relatively recently.

However, according to local residents, this settlement began to exist in the distant 18th century, when a fortress was built on the site of its current location, and subsequently the Magnitnaya village.

Locals say that the first settlement was defeated on the left bank of the Ural River. Subsequently, its other coast was also populated. In reviews of Magnitogorsk, tourists note many picturesque places where you can make excellent photos on their background.

Geographic data

What is the main feature of the location of the village, it was said above. It is located at the crossroads of Asia and Europe, on the banks of the Ural River. In the place where the settlement in question is currently located, historical Bashkortostan was previously located. The city has a fairly large area - about 400 sq. Km. From north to south, the settlement stretches for 27 km, and from east to west - to 22. If we consider the territorial indicators of the city, then Magnitogorsk takes 25th place among Russian settlements.

Magnitogorsk is located at an impressive distance from the Russian capital - 2000 km. The regional capital of the region (Chelyabinsk), in which the city is located, is located at a relatively short distance - 308 km (if you move along the highway). The total population of the city is more than 410,000 people.

Economy

In reviews of the city of Magnitogorsk, it is said that the bulk of the economy of this settlement is in industry. The city has a number of large ferrous metallurgy plants, one of which was built right at the foot of Magnetic Mountain. Among the factories, the largest are the hardware-calibration and crane. A separate share of economic profit is accounted for by Prokatmontazh LLC, CJSC UralSpetsMash, as well as Magnitogorsk TPP. Magnitogorsk shoe factory is famous in many regions of Russia. These shoes are affordable and quality and quite popular among Russians.

As for the manufactured products, a large amount of ferrous metal, electrical equipment, automobiles and some other types of vehicles are produced in Magnitogorsk. In addition, city enterprises produce food products, tobacco products and some types of alcoholic beverages.

Key infrastructure

Residents and visitors of the city in their reviews of Magnitogorsk pay special attention to how well its infrastructure is developed. The transport system, which is represented by public types of vehicles and several taxi services operating within the city, functions perfectly here.

In reviews of a taxi in Magnitogorsk, it is said that the best local carriers are Yandex.Taxi, as well as Maxim and Luck.

According to tourists and residents of the city, the drivers of these companies always do their job clearly and behave very politely with passengers. Almost throughout the city there are free parking spaces, if desired, drivers can use paid parking lots.

It should be noted that in the city traffic congestion is often observed, in particular, this manifests itself in the evening, at rush hour, when after a hard day many hurry home.

According to reviews of the city of Magnitogorsk left by local residents, it can be said that the utilities here are working quite well, providing all the necessary services in a timely manner. Residents pay special attention to rather low tariffs set for utilities.

Among the negative reviews about the village, we can distinguish those that are left by young parents. According to them, the city does not have a sufficient number of places in kindergartens, and therefore there are certain difficulties with getting pre-school education.

Reviews of Magnitogorsk kindergartens for children say that far from all of them are equipped with the necessary materials for the normal education and development of the child. In this regard, many parents prefer independent learning of the child, and some of them practice hiring private nannies and teachers.

sights

Special attention of tourists deserve many of the attractions that are located in Magnitogorsk. The comments left by tourists who have been here often offer some recommendations regarding visiting certain interesting places.

In their reviews of Magnitogorsk, many travelers recommend seeing the historic monument to the steam locomotive located on Railway Station Square. Absolutely all tourists who arrived in the city by rail can enjoy it.

Fans of the monuments can also head to the object erected in honor of the first builders of the city - the First Tent, which is located in the Veterans Park. This object was erected in honor of the time when one of the first representatives of the city‚Äôs population had nowhere to live - most of them then simply stayed with their families in large open-air tents.

Attention of tourists is also attracted by the obelisk "Europe-Asia", installed on the border of the junction of two parts of the world. It is located near the entrance to the Central passage, which runs across the river. This place is located next to Nosov Square.

Art lovers, while in the city, should definitely visit such cultural monuments as the Magnitogorsk Drama Theater named after Pushkin, the Opera and Ballet Theater, as well as the Boris Ruchev Museum-Apartment, where you can find many interesting objects.

Special attention should also be paid to the Magnitogorsk Museum of History and Local Lore, which presents expositions that visually tell about the rich and very interesting history of the city.

Fans of walks in interesting places can afford a visit to Victory Park and the Alley of the Metallurgists, as well as the German Quarter, which is located near Uralskaya Street, along Lenin Avenue. For lovers of walks in the picturesque flowering areas and alleys in the village there are a number of gardens that present interesting species of plants, shrubs and trees.

On many photos of the sights of Magnitogorsk you can see the city Chimes. They are presented in the form of a high stella, which reaches a height of 29 m. The entire column, at the very top of which a large city clock is installed, is completely covered with marble. At night, this building is illuminated with the help of specially installed equipment for this. Often in this place lovers make appointments, often various events are held, photo shoots are arranged.
