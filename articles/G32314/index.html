Residential complex "Svetly" (Kazan) - a paradise, a place of silence

Svetly residential complex (Kazan) appeared due to the merits and efforts of Antei Development Corporation OJSC in 2007. It was this organization that was able to prepare and implement the investment and construction project of the complex.





Location

The address at which the Svetly residential complex is located: Kazan, Kuyuki. This is a small village just a few kilometers from the seething and vibrant city of a millionth city. About ten kilometers separate quiet life from the roar of Victory Avenue - one of the largest highways of Kazan. But at the same time, the Svetly residential complex (Kazan) cannot be called lost. It is located right in the village of Kuyuki and enjoys all its infrastructure.

The beauty of the building in this place is the excellent ecological situation. Directly behind the residential complex are forest thickets, clear lakes. Those who appreciate peace, unity with nature and comfortable rest after a busy day will appreciate all the advantages of living in this housing estate.





Design

The residential complex Svetly (Kazan) is made in the same style, which gives it even more charm. All houses are provided in the same composition. They are the same, lightweight and modern. Which only emphasizes the elegance of a new lifestyle.

All the houses provided for by the project are three-story, taking into account the last word in innovative technologies and engineering solutions. With the number of apartments from twelve to twenty in one house. The square footage of the apartments is also different, and anyone who wants to choose a house for themselves to their liking and wallet.

The technologies used in the construction of the complex are German-Canadian. They allow you to save heat and energy. All apartments have an individual heating system, gas, centralized sewage. They themselves are spacious, bright, supplied with clean drinking water.





The developer company provides its residents with apartments with pre-finished finish. Thus, the owners can realize all their dreams of comfort in the repair, which they themselves will plan.

Infrastructure

The project also provides for the construction of a school, a kindergarten and a shopping center. That is the main points of ensuring the life of the residents of the complex. Particularly carefully, the developer reacted to the design of the local area, pedestrian paths. All this so that young mothers with strollers and children can freely walk along houses and on playgrounds. And, going out on an evening exercise, one could enjoy the cleanest air.

The location chosen by the Svetly residential complex (Kazan) is no coincidence. Winds blow to the village in such a way that they prevent the entry of polluted air into its atmosphere. This once again confirms the statement of the developer about the environmentally friendly zone of the complex.

Flats

All houses are three-story. It is this number of storeys that makes the Svetly residential complex (Kazan) particularly elegant. Photos of already built houses only confirm this. People slowly walk along the equipped paths, enjoying the evening coolness and relaxing from the noise of a large city, spread out nearby.

When designing the complex, the developer took into account only the interests of small families, since the project provides only one-room and two-room apartments. True, the footage of the latter can reach almost eighty square meters. But all the same, families with two children may seem a bit crowded in Svetly.

But parents may not worry about the safety of their children. Yes, and for her too. Since the territory of the village is protected. There is a free park, which is very pleasing to car owners.





Currently, almost all apartments are sold. The Svetly residential complex (Kazan) is already almost populated. Reviews, however, about life in the village are different. From negative to positive. Of course, the developer has not yet fulfilled all his promises. There is no school yet. Which upsets parents who have schoolchildren. But life does not stand still, and soon foliage will appear on the construction site. Trees will grow, and the complex will be transformed, it will be echoed with different aromas. And it will seem to the residents that they live in a real paradise. And with all the amenities.
