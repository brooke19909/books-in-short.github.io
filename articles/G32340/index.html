Health resort of Loutraki (Greece): rest, treatment, excursions, reviews of tourists

What is sunny Greece famous for besides its magnificent beaches and rich cultural heritage? Of course, most tourists, without hesitation, will answer that medieval monuments and ancient attractions, which in this country are many. Do you know that on its territory there is a unique thermal resort town of Loutraki? Greece really is a multifaceted country full of miracles and magic.

Loutraki Health Resort - a truly amazing hydrotherapy place, surrounded by tropical greenery and surrounded by pine forests, it is one of the cleanest resorts in Europe. Hundreds of thousands of people from polluted countries visit the international balneological center to enjoy the tranquil silence, admire the beauties of the local nature and bow to the Christian shrines to the floor.



Resort Essentials

Loutraki (Greece) on the map is located on the coast of the picturesque Gulf of Corinth. Its population is approximately 11 thousand inhabitants. Previously, the ancient city was called Terme, but after several centuries the resort changed its name, but this did not become less popular. Today it is a well-known hydroclimatic zone with a highly developed tourist infrastructure. It is famous for its healing mineral waters, comfortable climatic conditions, unique nature and excellent service.

In 1936 it was transformed into the Station of warm springs, and since then it is the first center providing therapeutic services to people with kidney stones. On its fairly spacious territory, various entertainment complexes, shopping centers, luxury hotels and the promenade are concentrated. Guests will be able to simultaneously engage in their health and relax.



Treatment in Loutraki (Greece)

A huge hydrotherapy complex has been built for people, offering a variety of wellness treatments. Entrance to the center is by tickets, the cost of which does not exceed $ 10. According to practitioners, all the water in the springs is endowed with healing power. After completing a 30-day course of therapy, you can completely cleanse your body of harmful toxins and radicals.

With the help of hydrotherapy, patients get rid of cardiological, intestinal and vascular disorders. In addition, the nervous disorders and liver diseases will be eliminated by the health resort of Loutraki (Greece). Treatment (the resort receives enthusiastic reviews from tourists) is carried out according to an individually selected program and only after passing a complete diagnosis.

Entertainment and Attractions

Unforgettable emotions and a storm of vivid impressions will give Greece (Loutraki). Tours to the balneological health resort can be arranged at any travel agency. Travelers who set foot on Greek lands will find a florid maze in which the tunnels of the present and the past are harmoniously interwoven. Holidays in this resort will be remembered not only for healing waters, but also for sandy beaches, the warm sea and an abundance of excursion programs.

Comfortable conditions for active and calm tourism are created here. You will get a wave of adrenaline from surfing, jet skis and diving. Romantic boat trips will appeal to those who crave new discoveries. Travelers will have the opportunity to stroll through epic places such as the ancient Hydra Temple, to see firsthand the lighthouse on the Malagavi mountain range.





The former majesty of the columns of the temple is evidenced by the unique mosaic, preserved to the present, and marble slabs. Another place that deserves the attention of guests of the resort of Loutraki (Greece) is the lagoon near the Gulf of Corinth. Getting here, you are as if transported to the Bronze Age. It was here that archaeologists discovered the remains of a former civilization in the form of household items.

Near the city there are Delphi, Epidaurus, Acropolis, Olympia, Byzantine and ancient temples, monasteries. We recommend visiting the following excursion routes:

- a trip to Athens, from there you will be taken to the Archaeological Museum, the white marble stadium and the Acropolis;

- An amazing and interesting trip to Delphi with a visit to the sanctuary of Apollo and the "Navel of the Earth";

- it is worth seeing the monasteries of Meteora;

- cruise the ancient islands of Hydra, Poros and Aegina.

Excursions to Loutraki can be done on your own in a rented car. An interesting and informative journey through the ancient places of Greece will leave in your memory the atmosphere, impressions and spirit of the fertile land.
