There are currently many ways to make money online. But, unfortunately, it is impossible to write off scammers who do not miss the opportunity to deceive the gullible people and cash in on this. For example, investment social service "Webtransfer", reviews of which contradict each other. Some claim that they make good money on it, while others accuse developers of fraud.

What is Webtransfer?

Microfinance system "Webtransfer" reviews are not always flattering. But there are people who do not know what kind of service it is. Developers promise earnings on the issuance of loans without investment. Beginners register on the site, get a personal account. The system provides them with $ 50, which can be given at interest and earn first money. Then users can take loans and give loans at higher rates, as a result it turns out to make good money (27%), according to the developers. However, for this you need to deposit your own considerable funds into the account and also issue loans. Webtransfer (reviews confirm this) insures deposits only in words. In fact, few manage to get their money back, much less earn money.



How it works

To start investing, you need to register on the official website of Webtransfer Microcredit. The reviews confirm that this is easy to do, just have an account on one of the social networks or an email address. After that, the registration is confirmed via SMS message, which comes to the specified user number.

Upon completion of registration, the user automatically enters his personal account, in which you also need to specify some information about yourself in the appropriate fields. When all the information is indicated, you need to click on the "Confirm" link. Next, the system will send a number to confirm its data in the Webtransfer system. The overwhelming majority of user reviews do not recommend registering with this service, since you can lose time, money and effort in vain.





For credibility, the system provides each beginner with $ 50, which can be used to issue loans for several days. Then the service will automatically debit them from the account. That is what many ordinary people buy, dreaming of getting rich.

You need to replenish your account by at least $ 50 from your wallet, after which you can allegedly start issuing loans and making money. In this regard, there are doubts about the decency of the developers, because they unfoundedly promise income without investments.

Negative opinion
