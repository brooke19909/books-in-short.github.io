Hotel Istron Bay Hotel 5 * (Istro, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

The largest island of Greece is Crete. It is one of the most popular resorts in Europe. Ten thousand tourists from all over the world go here every year. Naturally, there are countless hotels and guest houses in Crete. One of those is Istron Bay Hotel 5 *. I would like to tell you about it.





Location

Istron Bay Hotel 5 * has been hosting guests for 35 years. This hotel is very popular at least because of its location. It was located not far from the capital of Nome Lasithi, which is Agios Nikolaos. The hotel is located in one of the most picturesque bays in all of Greece, surrounded by lemon, orange and olive trees. And a little less than a kilometer separates it from a village called Istron. The airport is far away - about 65 kilometers.

But otherwise the location is good. After all, you can reach the sea in a few minutes. Moreover, such beautiful views of the Istron Bay are opened here. Many people come here to enjoy the scenery and enjoy the beauty of nature.





Service and Facilities

In Istron Bay Hotel 5 * there is everything that can only make the stay of guests more comfortable. There is free Wi-Fi, private parking, a storage room, a 24-hour reception and a tour desk. The nanny also provides his services (for those who have taken children on vacation). There is a laundry, ironing, conference room, office with photocopy and fax. But that is not all.

A shuttle service to and from the airport is also provided. On the territory of the hotel you can find shops, car rental, a beauty salon with a hairdresser. Also organized the delivery of newspapers, drinks and food in the room. And if guests plan to go somewhere for the whole day, they will prepare packed lunches with them on the road. By the way, the hotel also has non-smoking rooms and VIP services.

It is also worth noting the fact that the hotel has multilingual staff. The hotel staff speaks not only Greek, but also English, French and Russian. So communication problems should not arise.





Entertainment

Being in Istron Bay Hotel 5 * (Crete), certainly no one will be bored. There is a sun terrace, a picturesque fragrant garden, an outdoor swimming pool. Guests are also provided with all the necessary equipment for water sports. Here you can enjoy windsurfing, diving, rent a canoe, try fishing or just snorkel with a snorkel.

Guests also often rent bicycles. Many people like to explore the surroundings on this particular vehicle.

Also in the hotel there is a tennis court, billiard room, massage room, volleyball court, and conditions for entertaining children. And in the evenings on the territory of Istron Bay Hotel 5 * (Greece) conduct entertainment shows. But the main way to brighten up leisure, being here is, of course, visiting the beach. Many come here for this.

Bars and Restaurants

In this hotel, as in any other hotel of the appropriate level, there are several establishments that you can eat and drink well. There is a restaurant - they serve a variety of Mediterranean and Cretan dishes. Guests can also enjoy seafood there.

There are two more bars. One with snacks and soft drinks (by the pool), and the other with a large assortment of alcohol and cocktails.

As people who came here say, the food in the restaurant is excellent. Everything is in large numbers: salads, several types of meat and fish, barbecue, grill. Breakfast includes rice porridge, granola, cereal, fried bacon and sausages, different types of eggs, beans in tomato and all kinds of fruits. In general, as elsewhere. For dinner - french fries, stewed pork, spaghetti, fried fish, seafood, soups. Nobody remains hungry for sure.





About accommodation

Now a few words about what apartments Istron Bay Hotel 5 * has. The photo above shows a 2-seater standard. This is the most budget option. The standards are located in the main building, they have their own access to the terrace or balcony, which offers amazing views of Istron Bay. In such apartments two people can live.

The comfortably furnished room has everything you need for a comfortable stay - a radio, telephone, satellite TV, safe, excellent air conditioning, as well as a plumbing unit with a toilet, bathtub and hairdryer. There is also a minibar in the apartment.

A week of stay in such a room will cost about 40 thousand rubles. for two people - and this is with the all-inclusive service! The price is quite modest. And, most interestingly, it can be even lower. It all depends on the tour operator, booking time and season.

Guest Comments

In the end - a few words about what leave about the rest in Istron Bay Hotel 5 * reviews. Most of them are positive. People assure: this is an ideal hotel for those who dream of relaxing in a quiet and peaceful place. The atmosphere here is really great, setting up relaxation. The kitchen at the hotel is good, so you can safely take all-inclusive. Rooms are cleaned every day, towels and linen are changed with the same regularity. The staff is polite and courteous, always ready to help guests.
