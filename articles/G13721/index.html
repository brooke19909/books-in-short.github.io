Hotel Suppamitr Villa Hotel 3 (Pattaya): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Pattaya is a picturesque Thai city that is located on the seashore. This place every year attracts more and more tourists from all over the world. It is here that the small but cozy budget hotel called Suppamitr Villa Hotel 3 * is located. Naturally, every traveler tries to get as much information as possible about this place. What is a hotel like?

Where is the hotel Suppamitr Villa Hotel 3 * (Pattaya)?





The center of Pattaya, the picturesque promenade and busy streets - it is here, in a fairly calm area, the hotel is located. By the way, it was built in 1992, but in 2014 a complete reconstruction was carried out here, so guests can count on a modern level of comfort. About 2 km from the hotel there is a large market and several shopping centers where you can not only do shopping, but also have fun. But the beach is located about 30 minutes walk.

What do hotel rooms look like?





Hotel Suppamitr Villa Hotel 3 * consists of 75 rooms. There are standard and improvement rooms with windows overlooking the pool or the city (depending on location). The rooms will be pleased with modern renovation, soft carpet and comfortable furniture. Of course, guests are offered a range of household appliances, without which it is difficult to imagine a truly comfortable stay.

On TV, tourists can watch satellite, including Russian, channels. There is a working air conditioning system, as well as a minibar. The spacious bathroom is equipped with modern fixtures - you can take a shower in a spacious booth. You are also entitled to a set of hygiene products and clean towels, which are replenished every day. Cleaning is carried out at the request of guests.

Can I eat at the hotel?





Like most hotels in Thailand, Suppamitr Villa Hotel 3 * offers its guests free breakfast, which can be obtained in the hotel restaurant. The standard menu is toasts, snacks and soups, pastries and hot drinks. In the same restaurant you can have lunch or dinner, but the rest of the day, dishes are prepared to order and are paid separately. Food rarely becomes a problem, since there are a lot of cafes, restaurants and other catering establishments in the city. Also nearby are several grocery stores where you can buy fruits, vegetables and even ready-made meals.

Beach and water recreation





The coast of Pattaya is very popular among travelers. Here you will find clean, sandy beaches, warm sun and calm sea. Suppamitr Villa Hotel 3 * hotel is located some distance from the coast - it will take you about 30-40 minutes to go to it. Many guests prefer to overcome this distance on foot, while admiring all the beauties of nature and the life of Thais. Others use taxi services, which, by the way, are quite affordable.

Since the beaches here are mostly public, renting sun loungers and umbrellas is paid separately. Naturally, in addition to swimming and sunbathing, there are other ways to have a good time. For example, for a small fee, vacationers can go boating, water skiing, go sailing, take a short trip along the coast.

Entertainment and additional service

The hotel is mostly used as an overnight stay, and therefore there is not so much entertainment here. Nevertheless, in the courtyard there is a small pool with very clean water, around which comfortable sun loungers with soft mattresses are placed. Valuables can be deposited in the administrator safe. Also, the hotel will help you choose an excursion, talk about the most interesting places in the vicinity.

Hotel Suppamitr Villa Hotel: reviews

The opinion of people who have already managed to spend time in one place or another is an invaluable source of useful information. So what kind of reviews do tourists leave about the hotel Suppamitr Villa Hotel 3 *? For the most part, travelers remain satisfied with their stay here. Naturally, the path to the sea takes a lot of time, but there are always enough taxis near the hotel, the cost of which is quite reasonable.

The rooms are quite spacious. Despite the fact that the furniture and appliances are not new, everything works fine. Breakfast is sometimes monotonous, but no one will have to starve. By the way, Russians rarely relax in this hotel, and therefore workers do not know the Russian language. On the other hand, you can communicate with them in English.
