Kata Sea Breeze Resort 4 *, Phuket: reviews, hotel description

If you chose the island of Phuket as a place to stay in Thailand , the Kata Sea Breeze Resort 4 * hotel may be a suitable place to stay if you are looking for a budget, but comfortable hotel near the sea.



Location, general information

Kata Sea Breeze Resort 4 * on the map you can find on the southwest coast of Phuket (Thailand). The hotel is in a peaceful location, twenty minutes from the city center. The nearest Kata Beach is 300 meters away. In the vicinity of the hotel there are cafes, bars, a market, supermarkets, souvenir shops, travel agencies, car rental offices, exchange offices and much more.

Housing fund "Kata Sea Breeze Resort" is represented by 330 comfortable rooms of standard, deluxe and deluxe categories. Some apartments have direct access to the pool.



Kata Sea Breeze Resort 4 *: reviews of Russian travelers

Perhaps, every experienced tourist will agree that it is advisable to begin preparations for any trip abroad in advance. This is especially true for the long-awaited vacation. After all, everyone wants their vacation to be perfect and not be overshadowed by negative emotions and negative impressions. Experienced travelers are advised to pay special attention to the choice of the hotel in which you plan to stay for several days or even weeks. In this process, many try to focus on the reviews of tourists who have already happened to stay in one place or another. This allows you to make a more complete picture of a particular hotel and make the right choice. To save your time, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the generalized comments of vacationers from Russia who stayed at the four-star Kata Sea Beach Resort during their holidays in Thailand. Just note that the vast majority of our compatriots liked this hotel, and they are ready to recommend it to their friends and relatives. But we learn more about everything.



Housing stock

Our compatriots were quite pleased with the rooms offered by him in the Kata Sea Breeze Resort 4 * hotel (Phuket, Kata Beach). Guests note that the rooms here are spacious, bright, pleasantly decorated, comfortable. Tourists liked the solid furniture, and especially the comfortable large beds on which the hotel guests had a good night's sleep. None of the vacationers complained about sound insulation. According to travelers, the rooms in this hotel have everything you might need, except for Wi-Fi. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby or at the bar. True, the connection speed seemed to the guests not very high. By the way, you can solve the problem with the Internet by purchasing a SIM card of a local mobile operator with an Internet package. For vacationers traveling with the help of a tour operator, as a rule, SIM cards are issued when meeting at the airport guides. You just have to replenish the balance.



Cleaning service

Our compatriots had no complaints about the work of the maids of Kata Sea Breeze Resort 4 * (Phuket, Kata Beach). According to them, they cleaned their rooms pretty well. Also, the maids did not forget to regularly change towels and bedding, add drinking water and replenish personal hygiene products in the bathroom.

Guests who have a holiday date during their stay at Kata Sea Breeze Resort (birthday, wedding anniversary, honeymoon) recall with pleasure that the hotel staff did not forget to congratulate them. So, as a compliment they were presented with a cake, a fruit basket and a bottle of wine. Also, attention from the hotel administration was appreciated by tourists.



Territory, infrastructure

Our compatriots were satisfied with the territory of the Kata Sea Breeze Hotel. According to them, it is medium in size. There are a lot of greenery, nice places for walking and relaxing. Everything is very well maintained and clean. Tourists liked the hotel has several pools. And one of them is equipped with a water heating system. Also in several pools there is a jacuzzi and children's sections. According to hotel guests, they are regularly cleaned. In addition, the fact that in a number of pools you can swim at any time of the day has become a big plus for vacationers. So, many tourists with pleasure freshen up in this way in the evening before going to bed.

There is also a spa on site. Our compatriots note that the services are provided very high quality, but it is difficult to call them low. Therefore, many vacationers preferred to go for massage to salons, of which there are many in the vicinity of the hotel.

Guests arriving on vacation with their children liked the fact that the hotel has a games room and a sandbox. So there will be something to do even for kids.

Also at the hotel there is a gym, shop, parking, billiard room. The reception is open 24 hours a day.



Nutrition

Kata Sea Breeze Resort rates include breakfast only. According to our compatriots, the choice of dishes for a morning meal is repeated day after day and is standard for hotels in Thailand. However, you can always find something to your taste. They cook here, according to the guests, very well. So, for breakfast, you can eat fried eggs or omelettes, fried directly at you, boiled eggs, sausage and cheese, meat or fish dishes with a side dish, soup, cereal, pastries, fresh vegetables and fruits, juices, tea, coffee, yogurt.

You can also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant, but at an additional cost. It serves a variety of Thai and international dishes. However, most of the guests at Kata Sea Breeze Resort preferred to eat outside the hotel. They visited both local cafes and bought ready-made food from street vendors and in mashinniks (mobile stalls).

The Kata Sea Breeze has several bars offering a wide selection of drinks. And one of the bars is located right in the pool. You can comfortably sit on a stool drowned in water and sip a cocktail without leaving the water.



Sea

As noted by tourists from Russia, the Kata Sea Breeze Resort 4 * hotel (Phuket, Thailand) is located near the Kata Beach. So, its guests can reach the sea in 8-10 minutes on foot. The road passes through city streets with lots of shops, shops, cafes, massage parlors, etc. The beaches here are in municipal ownership. You can sit right on the sand, laying a mat or rug brought with you. It is also recommended to purchase an umbrella from the sun, as you can burn here very quickly. Or try to look for a place in the shade of trees. Water on Kata Beach, according to travelers, is very clean, sometimes there are waves on the sea. Many of our compatriots claim that it is quite comfortable here to relax with children.
