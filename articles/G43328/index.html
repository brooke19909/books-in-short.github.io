Where to stay in Zhlobin: the best hotels in Zhlobin with a description

In the Gomel region on the banks of the Dnieper lies the beautiful modern city of Zhlobin. It is the third largest population in the region. The distance from Zhlobin to Gomel is 100 km. You can overcome this path by car. Also from Gomel to Zhlobin there is a railway connection. Travel time is only 1 hour.

Currently, Zhlobin is an industrial center, since about 15 industrial enterprises are located on its territory, including Zhlobin Metallurgical Plant OJSC, one of the largest ferrous metallurgy enterprises in the European Region.

In Zhlobin there are entertainments for every taste: a zoo, a cinema, a Transnistrian park, a swimming pool, a water park, a rowing base, an ice palace, a stadium, as well as many bars and restaurants. We have compiled a list of the best hotels in case you decide to visit this wonderful city.

"Dnieper pearl"

The Dnieper Pearl Hotel in Zhlobin is a modern multifunctional complex of glass and concrete. The property is located on Karl Marx Street, 2a.

The hotel is the largest among Zhlobin hotels and was loved not only by travelers, but also by residents of the city for the opportunity to have fun and usefully. The complex has a water park with a spa, a bar, bowling, billiards, a restaurant for celebrations, a pool and a hairdresser. It offers rooms with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a refrigerator, and a private bathroom with toiletries. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the Dnieper Pearl.

The price for a single room is 1 422 rubles. Double room will cost 1,761 rubles. The cost of the suite will be 3 387 rubles per night. Guests of the Dnieper Pearl believe that this complex has the best value for money from all Zhlobin hotels. They especially note the cleanliness of rooms, affordable prices, hospitable staff and the quality of food in the restaurant.

Hotel complex "Slavinasport"

One of the newest accommodation facilities among Zhlobin hotels is the 3-star Slavinasport Hotel, which opened its doors 2 years ago. The modern five-story building of gray color, resembling a sailboat, is located on Karl Marx Street, 5, on the banks of the Dnieper.

The hotel has 31 rooms, which can comfortably accommodate 76 guests. The rooms are divided into: single (2 617 rubles), double (2 644 rubles), suite (2 857 rubles) and suite (4 760 rubles). Each room has a private bathroom, satellite TV, air conditioning, hairdryer, refrigerator, safe and smoke detectors.

The price includes a continental breakfast, which was highly appreciated by the hotel guests.

Entertainment at the hotel "Slavinasport"

The hotel building also has a conference room, a business center and the Italian restaurant Limoncello, where you can have a good time. Near the hotel there is a sports and fitness complex where you can have fun with family or friends. On its territory you can work out in the gym, go ice skating on an ice rink, go to the water park and pool and take a steam bath in the sauna.

Amaks City Hotel

This hotel is located in the very center of Zhlobin on 44 Petrovsky Street. The hotel is just a 5-minute walk from the main car and train stations. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day.

The hotel rooms are equipped with everything you need: a private bathroom with amenities, a flat-screen TV, a kettle, a desk, a fridge and a minibar. The hotel consists of 51 rooms of various types: business single (1 400 rubles), business double (2 154 rubles), studio (1 710 rubles), suite (2 028 rubles), economy single (792 rubles) and economy double (1 204 rubles). The price includes breakfast. Moreover, the hotel provides a wide range of additional services, such as the possibility of paying for mobile communications through the terminal, organizing evening entertainment, a massage room, library, hairdresser, tour services, taxi, transfer, laundry, ordering train and air tickets, business center and a souvenir shop. Guests at the Amax Hotel appreciate the value for money, the cleanliness of the rooms, the friendliness of the staff, and the extremely convenient location in the city center.

"District 17 Apartments"

In addition to hotels, apartments are very popular in Zhlobin, Gomel region. These are very convenient options for accommodation, especially if you plan to stay in the city for a longer period and want to feel at home. "District 17 Apartments" are located at microdistrict 17, house 6. It offers guests: an elevator, flat-screen cable TV, ironing facilities and hi-fi equipment. The kitchen has everything you need for cooking.

The cost of living is 1,500 rubles per night. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi and bike rental. Free parking is available on site.

Apartments at: mk. 38 Libiyeevka near the South

This property is characterized by modern design and convenient location. The apartment consists of a bedroom, living room, kitchen and bathroom. The cost of living is 1350 rubles per night. The maximum number of guests who can comfortably stay in apartments is 4 people. For guests' convenience there is a flat-screen TV, iron, washing machine, clothes dryer, refrigerator, microwave, oven, stove, ironing facilities, bidet. The apartment is located on the ground floor and is great for people with disabilities. Pets are allowed by prior arrangement with the owner.
