Hotels in Berlin: overview, description of rooms, reviews

The capital of the Federal Republic of Germany is a metropolis that attracts both business people and tourists. Where to stay in Berlin?

The supply of housing in this city is huge. In addition to ordinary hotels, guests of the capital can book hostels, small guesthouses or private apartments. This housing can belong to a different price segment - from economy to luxury class. In this article we will present you with a brief overview of Berlin hotels.

In addition to the "star" of hotels, tourists are also interested in their location on the city map. After all, I want the place of residence to be close to the sights of the capital of Germany.

And what hotel would recommend experienced travelers for the first time arriving in Berlin? Which hotels have the best reviews?

Budget hotels (no stars)

The economy-class hotel in Berlin does not at all resemble the dubious backer shelter in third world countries. No, everything is just as respectable here as in three-star hotels.

Hostels are often located under one roof with a real hotel - in the basement or, on the contrary, in the attic. They consist of a common dormitorium (bedrooms for 6-8 people) and full rooms for 2-3 people. The last rooms can even be equipped with their own bathroom.

But most often amenities are on the floor. What is so attractive for tourists in hostels in Berlin? They have a common kitchen where you can cook your own food, and coffee, tea, sugar and other pleasant things are generally free there. You should not assume that hostels are located on the outskirts of the capital.

Some of them, such as Check In Hostel Berlin, are located in the city center. Incidentally, this hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a washing machine.

The shared kitchen of this hostel has a stove, microwave, several large refrigerators, a full set of dishes. There are also very original budget hotels. Die Fabrik - baxpax Hotel, for example, is located in a former factory, the workshops of which are converted into rooms.

Prices for budget hotels in Berlin are very affordable. They range from 500-800 rubles. A bed in the general dormitory will cost even less - 400-500 rubles.
