Chalet "Kashtan" (Saratov region, Ust-Karaman village): description, reviews

If you are looking for a comfortable recreation center in the Saratov region, we suggest you consider such an option as the Kashtan camp site. It is very popular as a venue for various celebrations, including weddings, and as a simple outdoor recreation.

Briefly about the recreation center

The Kashtan camp site (Saratov) is located on the banks of the Bolshoi Karamkan River in the village of Ust-Karaman, among the Volga Islands and backwaters. This is a great place for a rural holiday in comfortable houses with all amenities. Also, various activities are often held at the camp site: weddings, birthday parties, corporate parties, anniversaries and traditional holidays (New Year, Christmas and so on).

The Big Karaman River is just 300 meters from the houses. Guests are even allowed to stay with small dogs.

The territory of "Chestnut"





The territory of the Kashtan camp site is quite large, landscaped. On it, in addition to wooden cottages, there are various sports grounds, a swimming pool with sun loungers, a children's play complex, gazebos and barbecue facilities, a fireplace room for 50 people, a Turkish bath and a sauna, rental equipment for sports equipment and much more. In "Kashtan" you can not worry about the safety of the car, since the territory also has a guarded parking lot.

Since the Kashtan camp site is a place to relax and hold various celebrations, there is a dining room where you can order breakfast, lunch and / or dinner. There is also a full-fledged banquet room and two options for gazebos:

  • large - designed for 50 people;
  • small - by 10.

Organization of leisure in the camp site





For lovers of outdoor activities in the "Chestnut" there are various areas for sports:

  • Volleyball Court;
  • tennis court;
  • 18 hole mini golf;
  • bike lanes.

All year round, vacationers can visit the pond or river fishing, visit the billiard room, ride horses and ponies, rent a variety of sports equipment (from balls and rackets to bicycles, ATVs, jumpers, ski equipment and snowmobiles). In winter, guests can go ice-skating, zorb and karakat. The nearby forest allows you to go on fascinating walks along the mushrooms and berries.

In the warm season, the Kashtan recreation center has an outdoor pool (33 meters long and 15 meters wide, 700 cubic meters). There are sun loungers nearby.

For children there is an outdoor playground with various attractions, swings and slides. There are also adult swings and hammocks at the base.

And if guests want to improve their health, then you can do this in a Russian or Turkish bath. Each has a pool. The cost of visiting the bath as of 2017 is 750 rubles per hour. Sauna capacity is up to 15 people.

The location of the recreation center





The address of the Kashtan camp site: Saratov Region, s. Ust-Karaman. It is only 40 km from Saratov. The most convenient way to get from the city to the base is by car. To do this, you need to go along the highway Z226 towards Samara. Then you need to turn off the highway to Krasny Yar, drive through Podstepnoye, then continue to Ust-Karaman.
