The recreation center (Shelomentsevo) "South Coast" is a great place for a relaxing weekend

In search of a clean and pleasant place, many residents of the Chelyabinsk region go to Lake Peschanoe, which is also called Shelomentsevo. Here you can relax quietly and enjoy the beautiful scenery. The recreation center "South Coast" (Shelomentsevo) offers its services so that guests can comfortably stay and fully plunge into the long-awaited summer vacation.

Description of the lake

The reservoir is located in the Etkul region. The village of Shelomentsevo is located on the eastern shore, it was in honor of him that Peschanoe was given the second name. The lake has an almost regular, rounded shape. It is small in size, only one and a half kilometers in diameter. The maximum depth can reach five meters, but mostly offshore it is shallow. The bottom is mostly sandy, but silted in some places. About half of the coast is overgrown with reeds, which is very popular with fishermen. On the west and south coast you can see a beautiful forest.

The lake itself is surrounded by wild beaches, there is also not one recreation center here. Shelomentsevo sheltered swans, ducks and cranes in their waters and this makes him even more attractive. But many visitors to these shores are interested in the South Coast camp site.



Base Description

The camp site includes a clean, well-kept beach, pond and forest. The forest is divided into two parts by the road. On the one hand there is a place for tourists with tents, in the other part there are cozy, new houses for a more comfortable stay. But no matter in which part the guests stay, everyone likes this recreation center. Shelomintsevo lake also pleases with its cleanliness, since both the reservoir and the territory are regularly cleared.

Accommodation at the base

On the territory, two-story houses for several guests and one-story houses were built, in which two adults with a child (up to adolescence) can live. There are also clamshells. Vacationers can use refrigerators. Many people like to relax on the veranda, where there are chairs and a table.

If guests arrive by car, they are allowed to park at their houses.

Unfortunately, the recreation center (Shelomentsevo) does not allow pets to be brought into the territory.

You can also stay in a tent camp and enjoy the benefits of civilization, which offers a camp site.

Entertainment and service



The recreation center (Shelomentsevo) on the territory provided for a small shop. On sale is everything that may be required in nature. There is also a shower and a bathhouse, which guests can use the camp site.

Since many vacationers come with children, there is a playground for games. On it you can find swings, sandboxes and ladders. An additional children's area is also on the beach, where there is a water slide.

On weekends, discos are organized, and a huge bonfire is built on the beach, which illuminates, warms and gives exotic relaxation.

In addition, there are bonfire areas for guests at the base. In such special places you can make a kebab or cook a fish that you could catch in the lake.

Beach



The recreation center (Shelomentsevo) monitors the cleanliness of its beach. There are also designated places for volleyball and futsal. You can hide from the scorching sun under umbrellas. There are also sun loungers on the beach.

A catamaran and boat rental service is organized.

In the evening, lights are lit that illuminate the "South Coast". Often beach discos last until three at night.

As already mentioned, on the lake there is an opportunity to catch fish on your own. To do this, you can take a boat and go to the center of the reservoir. In the waters there are perch, crucian carp and even large carp. And if you like fishing, then you can come to the lake in winter and try to catch a ripus.
