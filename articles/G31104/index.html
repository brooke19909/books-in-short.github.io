Guest house "Amshensky Dvor", Lazarevskoye: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Sochi is one of the most famous domestic resorts. The sights of this resort can not be counted - such a native and wonderful Black Sea, exotic subtropical vegetation, picturesque nature, healing springs and mud, sea air saturated with coniferous phytoncides, unique architectural monuments and also many other factors that make your vacation full and unforgettable.

Lazarevskoye - the resort city of Greater Sochi

The variety of resorts in the so-called Greater Sochi is very large. One of the most popular and famous coastal cities of the Krasnodar Territory is the regional center Lazarevskoye. This village has a fairly developed resort infrastructure.

There are many attractions, cozy restaurants and cafes on the promenade, artists and sculptors exhibit their work, parties and discos are organized.





The sea in Lazarevsky is as pure as a tear. The water warms up and remains warm until October. Pebble beaches are considered one of the most spacious on the entire coast of Greater Sochi.

Exotic trees, curly palm trees, evergreen cypresses, spruce and eucalyptus trees, dates, lemons and oranges - a lot of southern vegetation in Lazarevsky.

They say that Sochi is an expensive resort. However, in the village of Lazarevskoye housing prices, so to speak, for any pocket. There are elite comfortable hotels and private mini-hotels. Vacation prices also depend on the location of the hotel, proximity to the sea and the city center.

Guest house "Amshensky Dvor"

Lazarevskoye is a picturesque village, the nature of which fascinates with its beauty. High above sea level, next to the mountain river Psezuapse, which flows into the sea, there is a cozy modern guest house "Amshensky Dvor".





The hotel is located on the foothills in a quiet, picturesque location. The surrounding nature is simply fabulous. Tourists sunbathe by the mountain river, grill kebabs and even bathe.

Amshensky Dvor Guest House (Lazarevskoye) is surrounded by green trees. The hotel is a six-story building of the cottage type, which houses the main number of rooms.

The guest house has a summer cafe with gazebos. The yard is landscaped, decorated with a waterfall, fountains, wooden fairy-tale characters.

Guest House "Amshensky Dvor" - 2 * hotel. Lazarevskoye is famous for its variety and accessibility of recreation, and this hotel is perfect for budget spending in comfortable conditions.





This place of rest is more preferable for tourists who prefer not only a relaxing holiday by the sea on the beach, but also active - hiking, sightseeing in Sochi, walking in the forest. At the same time, a quiet, shady courtyard and the murmur of a waterfall have a peaceful and peaceful rest away from city noise.

How to get there

In the event that you flew on vacation by plane to Adler, the hotel administration will be able to arrange a transfer, which will cost tourists about 2500 rubles. The distance from the airport to the guest house is about 100 kilometers, travel time is 2 hours. You can also take a minibus, which often runs from the airport to the resort villages.

There is a railway station in Lazarevsky, to which trains from many cities of Russia arrive. From the railway station to the "Amshensky Dvor" - about four kilometers. From the railway station you can take a taxi or minibus.

The address of the Amshensky Dvor guest house is Lazarevskoye (Big Sochi), 64/5 Kalarash Street.

Number of rooms

Comfortable housing at affordable prices away from the noise of trains and cars in a quiet place of the village of Lazarevskoye - "Amshensky Dvor" - a hotel that will provide its clients with a decent rest.

The hotel has 59 double and triple rooms of standard and luxury categories.

Each room has all the amenities: shower, toilet, washbasin, towels, toiletries, hairdryer. Standard rooms have the necessary set of furniture, TV, air conditioning.





Deluxe rooms - large bright rooms with more expensive and beautiful furniture, with designer renovation.

Each room has a balcony. In 2013, the hotel was completely renovated, the rooms were replaced with furniture, and high-quality repairs were made.
