Sanatorium "Ryabinushka": description, features and reviews

Sanatorium "Ryabinushka" is located on the landscaped territory, which is his property. This is a landscaped area, decorated with a fountain with gazebos, decorative waterfalls. On the territory of the sanatorium there is an outdoor pool with water park elements.

Where is it and how to get there

So, today we will talk about such a place of rest, as the sanatorium "Ryabinushka" (Anapa). Reviews about it will be presented below. In 2011, a new building was opened with comfortable euro numbers, where classes and seminars, conferences and presentations can be held.





The holiday home is located in the city of Anapa and begins its work from mid-spring to mid-autumn. To get to the Ryabinushka sanatorium, you must first get to Anapa by train or fly by plane, and then get to the bus station by minibus No. 110 (if moving from the railway station) or No. 113 (if driving from the airport). From the bus station you should transfer to a bus or minibus No. 114 or No. 128 and go to the Dynamo stop.

Where to live

Sanatorium "Ryabinushka" offers its guests to settle in one of the 160 rooms located in seven three-story buildings and one six-story, equipped with an elevator. Consider the number of rooms in more detail.

For one person

  • Single rooms located in the block include a bed, refrigerator, TV, shower, toilet, air conditioning and a balcony.
  • The single standard repeats the atmosphere of the previous room, but it becomes possible to install the folding bed as another place to sleep.

Double apartments

  • Standard room consists of a single bed, TV, refrigerator, air conditioning, shower, toilet and balcony.
  • The standard plus duplicates the atmosphere of the previous room, but the ability to install an extra bed (folding bed) is added.




  • Double 2-room superior room has a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, safe, 2 double bed, telephone, shower, toilet and balcony. You can install additional space (sofa).
  • Double 2-room with a kitchen is identical in decor to a superior room, but it also has a kitchen. There is also the possibility of installing additional space (sofa).

For three

This is a standard room with three single beds. It is equipped with a TV, fridge, air conditioning, shower, toilet and a balcony. No extra bed available here.

Quadruple rooms

  • 2-room room for a family of 4-5 people is equipped with 4 beds (single), air conditioning, TV, fridge, toilet, shower, balcony. There is the possibility of installing an extra bed (folding bed).
  • The 3-room suite with a small kitchen and the possibility of accommodating 4-6 people is equipped with a TV, safe, refrigerator, telephone, 2 double bed, 2 single beds. An extra bed is a sofa.

3-story building

  • Double Euro-standard with central air conditioning, TV, telephone, refrigerator, 2 beds (single), toilet, shower and balcony. An extra bed is offered for choice: a folding bed or a sofa.




  • Double studio with a bed (two single or one double), toilet, shower or bath, TV, telephone, safe, central air conditioning. Extra bed - sofa.
  • The three-room studio is identical in design to the two-room. An extra bed is offered a folding bed.

Junior Suite

The twin 2-room junior suite also has a studio-like setting, with only a 2-bed bed. An extra bed is a sofa.

The rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen is changed once every 5 days. Towels are changed at the request of guests. In suites, bed linen is changed once every 3 days.

What is fed

In spring and autumn, the Ryabinushka sanatorium, which works as a complex menu, feeds its guests 3 times a day. In the summer, meals also remain three meals a day, but are carried out according to the "buffet" system.





Entertainment Services

Here you will be offered free chess, table tennis, outdoor and indoor pools, a gym (only for those living in the 7th building), a library. Paid services include billiards, a lobby bar, Turkish and Finnish baths.

Treatment

Why go to the sanatorium "Ryabinushka" (Anapa)? Reviews say that here you can treat diseases of the musculoskeletal system, restore the cardiovascular and musculoskeletal systems, get rid of gynecological and urological diseases, revive the gastrointestinal tract and nervous system.

Therapies

  • Hydrotherapy - the use of fresh water externally, in the form of baths, dousing, rubdowns and showers.
  • Massage bathtubs - water jets directed under pressure in a pool, bathtub or shower.
  • Balneotherapy - treatment with mineral water in the form of a shower, drink, bowel lavage and inhalation.
  • Mud therapy - the use in the heated state of eruptions of mud volcanoes and peat deposits in the form of local applications and baths.




  • Thermotherapy - the moist heat of warming compresses, therapeutic muds, baths and baths, as well as the dry heat of paraffin, heating pad, electric light baths.
  • Inhalations - the introduction of drugs through the respiratory system.
  • Electrotherapy is a dosed effect on the patient's body of visible infrared or ultraviolet radiation.
  • SPA procedures - a wellness complex of procedures using mineral and fresh water, sea salt and algae, medicinal plants and healing mud.
  • Mineral water is used as a medicinal drink inside, in the form of baths or microclysters.

Children are taken for treatment when they reach 4 years of age.

Prices

What else is said about such a place as the sanatorium "Ryabinushka" (Anapa)? Accommodation prices pleasantly surprise vacationers. The most affordable room is a standard single in a 6-story building. Its price is 2320 rubles per day in the summer. This amount includes 3 meals a day, treatment prescribed by a doctor, swimming in an outdoor pool, visiting the equipped beach.
