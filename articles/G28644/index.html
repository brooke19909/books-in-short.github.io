"Southern water area". Residential complex "Southern water area" - reviews

St. Petersburg is one of the largest cities in Russia. Millions of square meters of housing are erected here annually. These are cozy cottages and spacious apartments overlooking the sights of the city. One of the tidbits is the houses included in the residential complex "Southern Water Area". This object will be discussed in the article.



Location

The site allocated for the construction of the residential complex "Southern Water Area" is located at the intersection of two avenues - Leninsky and Heroes. From here to the Ring Road - 9.2 km, and to the center of St. Petersburg - 16 km.



Description

"Southern water area" (St. Petersburg) includes 5 quarters located on the shores of the Gulf of Finland. Very soon there will appear houses in 7-25 floors. Designers have taken care of the comfort of not only residential buildings, but also the territories adjacent to them. Residents of houses will be able to walk through squares and parks. Pedestrian boulevards will be made especially for them.

The first floors are given for commercial blocks: shops, beauty salons, banks and so on. It is very comfortable. After all, residents of the "Southern Water Area" will not have to spend time traveling to other areas of the city. All that they need will be within walking distance.

"Southern water area" - These are houses built using the latest construction technologies. To strengthen the outer walls, mineral wool sheets are used . They will also provide warmth in homes during the winter. Decorative plaster will be applied over the reinforcing fiberglass mesh . And the facade glazing will complete the decoration of houses .



Flats

"South water area" is an excellent option for those who want to solve the housing problem or improve their living conditions. The new microdistrict is designed for 5,000 apartments with different types of layout. All of them are very convenient and comfortable.

The company-developer will rent apartments with decoration. This approach allows you to speed up the process of introducing people into the apartments they purchased.

In the corner sections will accommodate apartments with a non-standard layout. The rooms in them will turn out to be pentagonal. According to the designers, this will help to visually increase the space and realize the most daring design ideas.

Apartments located in the "Southern Water Area" have two distinctive features. Firstly, the ceiling height in them reaches 2.65 m. Secondly, the layout includes a spacious entrance hall.

It so happened that the one-room apartments are in the greatest demand. The developers of the complex "Southern Water Area" took this into account. They offer two variants of "odnushka" - with a separate and adjacent bathroom.

Now a few words about the finish. As soon as the houses are fully erected, specialists will begin their internal arrangement. The walls will be glued with wallpaper that meets the indicators of strength and environmental safety. The floors are covered with linoleum. Subsequently, apartment owners can replace it with another coating.

The bathroom is tiled. The apartments are sold together with the bathroom and toilet installed in it, as well as domestic plumbing and household meters. Sockets and switches, interior doors and metal double-glazed windows will also be in the apartments at the time of their delivery. All you need to do is bring furniture and textiles. And you can live in comfortable conditions.

Designers also thought about the safety of people. In every entrance and on each floor there will be CCTV cameras. They will also create a throughput system. Apartment owners will be offered to install video intercoms.



Infrastructure

When buying a home, you need to consider not only its size and functionality. Equally important is the infrastructure of the area in which the property is located.

The master plan of the "Southern Water Area" includes ground multi-tier parking lots, shops, pharmacies, beauty salons and bank branches (on the first floors of residential buildings).

Near the complex are located such objects as:

  • two kindergartens;
  • lyceum, gymnasium and school;
  • sports clubs;
  • South Seaside Park;
  • chain supermarket;
  • medical facilities (clinics, hospitals);
  • Juno market.

Near the "Southern water area" are the main highways of the Krasnoselsky district. Departure to the Ring Road and WHSD will provide affordable transport at any time of the year.



Offers for buyers

Currently, 1-, 2-, 3- and 4-room apartments are on sale. Most of the facilities will be commissioned in the IV quarter of this year. The cost of apartments varies from 2.9 to 7 million rubles. It all depends on the number of rooms, type of layout and footage.

There are several payment methods :

1. Interest-free installments. Down payment - 10%. The remaining amount is evenly distributed. An individual payment schedule is drawn up.

2. Mortgage. A loan for the purchase of housing can be obtained at VTB24, UralSib, or at one of 13 other banks.

3. In cash. At 100% payment, the buyer receives a good discount.

The reviews of those who have already purchased apartments there speak for themselves. Most people consider this a good investment. They are satisfied with the layout of the apartments and the infrastructure of the district.

The main advantages of the residential complex Petersburgers consider its transport accessibility, comfort and a well-thought-out security system. But this still needs to be verified.
