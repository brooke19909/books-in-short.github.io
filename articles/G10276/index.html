Silicon Computing Base Device

On the processor of 2013 of release of the MT6572 model of the MediaTek company, the Tele2 Mini smartphone is based. Specifications, reviews and its technical parameters indicate that this is the weakest link in the gadget. Alas, only two computing modules are part of this chip. Moreover, they are based on an energy-efficient architecture, codenamed "Cortex A7 ." But a high level of performance in this case is certainly impossible to obtain. The maximum frequency of this CPU is declared at 1.3 GHz. In this mode, it works when performing the most complex tasks. If the executable program code is not so complicated, then the frequency can automatically lower to 300 MHz. Another factor that can change the frequency of the CPU is its temperature. With its increase, the processor frequency automatically decreases regardless of the degree of complexity of the task. The technological process by which this chip is manufactured complies with 28 nm standards.

Graphics Accelerator

Also, an entry-level graphic accelerator is equipped with a Tele2 Mini smartphone (black). Reviews indicate that his performance is at a very, very modest level. His Mali model is 400 MP1. It is made by the same technological process as the central processor - 28 nm. Its clock frequency is fixed at 500 MHz. This accelerator is perfect for processing images in the format of 800x480 pixels, and its mere presence frees the central processor from processing graphic information.
