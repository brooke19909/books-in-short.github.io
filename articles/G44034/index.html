Sun Club 3 * (Djerba, Tunisia): hotel description, services, reviews

Tunisia is a magical resort with the azure sea, golden sand and palm trees, where an atmosphere of enchanting mystery and luxury is in the air. In this place there is a huge number of the latest hotel complexes, designed for every taste and budget. One of the worthy places for a comfortable stay is the Sun Club 3 * hotel (Djerba, Tunisia).

Description





This hotel is recognized as the best family vacation destination in Tunisia. A variety of entertainment here are at the highest level. There is diving, and surfing, and windsurfing, and all kinds of leisure activities for extreme lovers. Very close, a few steps from this wonderful complex, is the famous Tunisian water park, which is replete with various slides, fountains and a jacuzzi for both young children and adults. The hotel territory is reliably protected by a fence, and the hotel itself is located in a small but very cozy resort of Djerba. Nearby is a clean and well-groomed beach with comfortable sun loungers, a shallow beach and crystal-clear water. Attractions within walking distance include the Bardo National Museum, the old mosque and St. Vincent de Paul Cathedral.

The Sun Club 3 * hotel (Djerba, Tunisia) successfully practices visits to excursions to the most picturesque places of Tunisia. In the immediate vicinity of this tourist accommodation is the harbor where ships arrive.

This comfortable and very cozy hotel has 385 rooms of varying degrees of comfort.

Infrastructure




Around the hotel there is a beautiful garden with a variety of landscape design figures, shrubs and exotic flowers, as well as outlandish trees. A currency exchange office will allow you to recalculate the amount brought at the local rate. The complex also has a gym with a lot of fitness equipment, swimming pools, restaurants and cafes. On the territory of the Sun Club 3 * hotel, leisure activities are regularly organized, for which a bar is equipped. After active sports, any vacationer can easily take a shower in special cabins.

For lovers of Italian cuisine at the hotel, a pizzeria opens its doors wide, in which experienced culinary masters prepare this dish according to the most original recipes. Those who wish to watch their favorite show in a quiet environment are invited to visit a special room with comfortable furniture and a plasma wide-screen TV.





The number of rooms in the hotel complex Djerba Sun Club 3 * (Tunisia) includes 385 apartments, each of which has a telephone, a bathroom with all accessories and a steam shower, a washing machine, satellite TV, a desk and a dressing table, wireless connection Internet access is available at an additional cost of 5 euros per day. Children under the age of 2 years stay free of charge in the same bed as their parents. As for adolescents, accommodation here will cost about 70% of the price of a voucher for an adult vacationer. For children, there is a special playroom with an experienced nanny, as well as a club for older children.

Keeping pets at the hotel is strictly forbidden by the rules, therefore it is better to choose a different hotel if you want to take an animal on vacation. When booking rooms, breakfast is automatically included in the price, which is always prepared using only the freshest produce. In the restaurants of the Sun Club 3 * hotel (Djerba, Tunisia), each vacationer can order a dish of local or international cuisine, as well as enjoy a light drink, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic.

For lovers of free time, not tied to one particular place, the hotel offers to rent comfortable cars or prestigious high-speed bicycles.

On the contrary, those who prefer to stay permanently within the complex can choose aerobic gymnastics, water aerobics, golf, horseback riding, and dance at various outdoor parties near the pool. Gambling people can shoot archery, go in for darts or fitness, get a lot of fun by kayaking or banana.

The hotel is very convenient in terms of location, since the path to the train station takes only a few minutes, which can be reached by transfer. The best criterion for evaluating the hotel Djerba Sun Club 3 * - guest reviews.

What do vacationers say?




Judging by the reviews of guests, this hotel is quite respectable and can compete with other similar institutions, which are many in Djerba. The staff is pretty good in Russian, which greatly facilitates the process of communication. Check-in to hotel rooms is possible around the clock, luggage when making a small surcharge can be delivered directly to the room by the porter. They feed vacationers on special cards that are issued at registration, which is very convenient.

The rooms are very nice looking and comfortable. If something from the equipment breaks down or for some reason doesn‚Äôt work, the problem can be fixed right away by moving to another room. They repair equipment on request very quickly, you just have to warn the reception staff.

The condition of the rooms is relatively good, inside the furniture is at least already old, but brought into proper shape. The sewerage system in the room is not very well established, since the water after washing takes a long time to leave. There is no safe, which is a little upsetting. In the bathroom there is a bathroom and a washbasin, and in the room there is a bench, a pair of nightstands, a wardrobe, a bed and a table. There is no refrigerator in the rooms, there are few sockets for connecting equipment, if necessary, it is worth carrying a tee with you. The location of the outlets is inconvenient: everything is high.

Hot and cold water is served in the hotel Djerba Sun Club 3 * (Tunisia) uninterrupted, which pleases. Drinking water can be obtained for free by going to a bar opposite the office building. The hotel balconies offer wonderful views. The negative side is poor protection against noise and extraneous sounds.

The Hotel Sun Club 3 * (Djerba, Tunisia) is located in a clean and well-kept place. There are a lot of shrubs, which gives an indescribable flavor to each number. Garbage bins are located throughout the hotel, so there is absolutely no garbage in the vicinity.





All retail outlets and restaurants can be visited by going down to the ground floor. Sometimes local merchants bring a variety of pastries to the hotel to pamper guests. On the ground floor, they sell spices, jewelry, jewelry, toys and branded clothing. The downside is that the prices here are very high, when compared with retail outlets in the vicinity.

Service

Room service pleases with tact. If desired, and for a small reward, figures are twisted from towels, and the bed can be showered with flower petals. Towels at the hotel are old but clean. Lingerie changes regularly.

The staff treats guests very friendly if customers leave a small monetary gratitude. The restaurant feeds vacationers with fruit, which can be sold chilled for a small fee.

Contingent

The public of the hotel is international, mainly Arabs predominate, in second place the French, followed by residents of England and Germany. Travelers from Russia are also more than enough. The audience here is very noisy, Arabs and French constantly shout and laugh, preventing others from sleeping soundly.

the Internet

There are practically no problems with access to the World Wide Web. You can access the Internet both in the hotel lobby and in each room. There is enough speed to check mail or share impressions of the rest on social networks, posting posts and photos.

Nutrition





The food on site is just fine. Meals are issued on special cards, which are issued to the manager on the first visit to the restaurant, assigning a separate waiter to the client. Breakfast is prepared and selected by guests on their own, while for lunch and dinner, refreshments and delicious natural ice cream with fruit additives are served.

The restaurant is huge, as guests from the entire hotel go there to eat. The situation is quite clean, but there is always something to complain about. Alcohol and cocktails practically do not dilute, which pleases. The menu has all kinds of vegetable and hearty salads, coffee, hot chocolate and many main dishes of fish, meat and seafood. From fruits, there are always melons, watermelons and bananas.

Beach vacation

Despite the impressive size, the beach is often dirty, there is a lot of garbage and cigarette butts, and all the beds are busy and they can hear the cries of crazy foreign and Russian children. Jellyfish sting very often, but in the bar they give out something cold in case of burns.

As for the wellness bathing procedures, they few people delighted. Of course, for the first time this seems like a real paradise, but experienced tourists advise you to pass by, as everything is much poorer than described by travel agencies.

Pools





If desired, vacationers can splash in one of the three pools, each of which is heated and equipped with fountains. Near the water areas there is animation that will appeal to both adults and children. From animation, discos, a variety of outdoor activities, games and programs are held. The only minus of this zone is the abundance of bleach, which causes all allergic reactions.

General impressions

One of the most popular tourist destinations is Tunisia. Hotel Sun Club 3 * - a worthy place to stay in this resort. The hotel has several undeniable advantages. Separately, it is worth noting the excellent service in the restaurant and cuisine. Dishes almost never repeat. Everything is very tasty and satisfying. Tourists also note the excellent work of animators. These people are professional dancers and actors.

The hotel offers a decent vacation for younger guests. In the pool for them special slides are provided, there is a games room.

You can save a little by choosing the hotel Djerba Sun Club 3 *. A tour issued by the operator to this complex is much cheaper than independent booking.
