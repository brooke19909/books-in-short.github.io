Phone Admet B30: reviews of the owners. Instruction, review

More and more button devices are becoming owners of powerful batteries. This trend is not spared and B30, released in 2013. What else can surprise the brainchild of Admet?

Design





Although the appearance of the Admet B30 is not entirely ordinary, it is impossible to call it unique. The design is very similar to many phones designed for outdoor activities. Of course, there are significant differences. B30 is not capable of boasting additional protection.

The Admet B30 phone is completely made of plastic. The material is very high quality and, although the device does not have shockproof properties, most drops will do without noticeable damage. Such properties of the device allow you to use it during tourism.

The dimensions turned out pretty impressive, as for a conventional cellular. Dimensions are as many as 121 by 56 millimeters. Impressive was the weight of 170 grams. The culprit of such a weightiness of the device is the battery. Actually because of the battery, the device received 25 millimeters of thickness.

Significant dimensions and significant weight are compensated by bends on the sides of the device. Thus, holding the device has become much more convenient. In addition, the phone lies tightly in the hand and does not try to slip out.

The unusual device is noticeable in external elements. A small display, speaker, model number, and keyboard are located on the front. It should be noted that the buttons of the device are large and equipped with Braille. The back side has become a haven for a surprisingly large speaker and main camera.

The usb connector, for the docking station, is located on the left side, where is the place for the lace. The right side placed a usb socket on it, designed for charging. A curious feature was the huge flashlight at the top, and at the bottom - a microphone.

Design without any frills, since the phone is designed more for durability and convenience. The strong case, large buttons allow you to comfortably use the device in any conditions.

The screen size is only 1.77 inches, which is enough for the Admet B30. The angle overview, unfortunately, is not very high, because the display uses the old TFT matrix. The biggest drawback is 64 megabytes of memory installed in the Admet B30 phone. Owner reviews report the only solution, an increase in capacity due to the flash drive. The device supports cards up to 8 gigabytes.

The phone book is also not the largest. Only 250 numbers can be recorded in the device. The number of messages is also limited, the device fits 300 sms.

Communication





Cellular works exclusively in a GSM network at frequencies of 1800, 900, 1900 and 850. Support for GPRS Internet is also provided. But Wi-fi and navigation are missing. Your device only supports media such as usb and bluetooth.

The main advantage is the presence of two connectors for SIM cards in the Admet B30. Reviews boil down to the fact that this was the reason for choosing the phone. However, the B30, like most devices, has only one radio module. Thus, during a call, the second card is automatically disabled.

Autonomy

The battery capacity in Admet B30 5000maH, quite impressive volume. But such a battery is provided for a reason. Not only can the device last over a hundred hours of talk time, it can also charge other devices.

On the side, on the left, is a usb socket for connecting gadgets. Thus, without any adapters, and using only one cable, you can charge any phone or tablet.

The device will be an excellent choice if it is not possible to often recharge the phone. In passive mode and with minimal work, this miracle is able to live a whole month without an outlet.
