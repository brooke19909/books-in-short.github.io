Wow, I'll ride! or Pledge of an unforgettable vacation

In the park there is a huge amount of attractions that can interest both a child and an adult.

"Ferrari World" presents a variety of simulators used to train real modern riders. In addition, you can make an entertaining trip to the Ferrari factory either in the heart of the legendary car - the Ferrari 599 engine, a sightseeing flight over the surroundings of Italy, or visit the gallery dedicated exclusively to this car brand.

For strong men and women

The most popular attraction in the park is Formula Rossa. It represents the fastest speeding American race in the world with a length of 2.07 km. Steep bends and turns are guaranteed to everyone.

The cars made in the form of red Ferrari F1 cars gain speed up to 100 km / h in less than 2 seconds. Do not have time to blink an eye, as they say, and they are already rushing you at a speed of about 240 km / h. Dashing, what can I say!

Before the start of the race, safety glasses are given to each participant on the launch pad , which allow them to protect their eyes from powerful air currents generated during the movement. By the way, it should be noted that the cars start with a power of about 20,800 hp. and rise to a height of about 52 m in 4-5 seconds, and all thanks to the use at the start of a system of hydraulic winches that resemble those used to launch jet aircraft.





Feel like a real king of the track

An equally popular attraction is the Fiorano GT Challenge, where almost anyone can try their hand and fight with other "racers" rushing in parallel in cars reminiscent of a Ferrari F430 Spider to the finish line.

During sharp accelerations and sharp turns, competitors experience unforgettable sensations. In each race, of course, the winner is determined.





Dedicated to curious visitors

Anyone who for some reason does not intend to visit the attractions on this day can go to the Ferrari Gallery, located outside Maranello. The whole history of Ferrari is demonstrated here, starting from 1947 and ending today.

The gallery exhibited both modern models and a number of classic cars that were provided by ardent collectors who wanted to share their hobby with others.

Here you can almost always meet crowds of people with cameras and cameras. Why? Everything is explained very simply: what is seen around is so amazing that a burning desire arises to capture what is happening as a keepsake. Modern technology is an important component of the park

The multimedia screen provides an opportunity for gallery visitors to meet with racers, dreamers, creators and engineers who have made the Ferrari name recognizable all over the world and, one might say, a real icon of trademarks of the automotive industry.

Attractions for the little ones

The kids are most likely to be attracted by the interactive playground Junior Training Camp, whose name in translation into Russian sounds very symbolic: "Children's Training Camp."

Here they can try to wash the car without water on their own, feel like a designer or engineer, ride in their small copy of Ferrari, control cars using a remote control, and so on.





Prices and working hours

When entering the park, you should pay attention to important information about the cost of tickets. Agree that we are all used to the fact that in most cases prices are divided into children and adults. Here, it all depends on the growth of the visitor to the park: guests are divided into 2 categories - below and above 150 cm. And already on this basis, the possibility of visiting certain attractions (for "small" or for "large") is determined.

The cost of a regular excursion with the ability to inspect the territory and sites for a person below 1.5 m will cost about 45-46 US dollars, higher growth - 62 US dollars, and for a child whose growth is less than 1 meter, you do not need to pay.

For visitors, the park is open 6 days a week, from 12:00 to 22:00 local time. The day off is Monday.
