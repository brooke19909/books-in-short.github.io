Muong Thanh Mui Ne Hotel 4 * (Phan Thiet, Vietnam): hotel description, reviews

If you want to discover Asia and have chosen the resort of Phan Thiet (Vietnam) as a place for spending your vacation, Muong Thanh Mui Ne Hotel 4 * can become for you a suitable place for a comfortable stay on the beach.



Location

Hotel Muong Thanh Mui Ne on the map, you can easily find in the eastern part of Mui Ne Beach, near the city center. Phan Thiet is 16 kilometers away. You can get here by taxi or a rental car or scooter. In the vicinity of the hotel there are bars, cafes, restaurants, shops, souvenir shops. According to tourists, here prices are slightly lower than in the very center of Mui Ne, which is a definite plus of the hotel's location. Regarding proximity to the air harbors, Nha Trang Kamran Airport is 189 kilometers (about four hours drive), and Ho Chi Minh (Saigon) is 210 kilometers from Mui Ne (about five hours drive).

Hotel Traveler Reviews

Since for tourists the hotel becomes a second home during a foreign vacation, it is very important not to make a mistake with its choice. Therefore, in most cases, people planning a trip begin long before departure to study information about the hotels of the country they are going to visit. With special attention, experienced travelers are advised to read reviews of former guests about a particular place. Indeed, in their comments they describe the real pros and cons of a particular hotel, which helps to understand whether it is suitable for you personally or whether you should look for something else. Today we invite you to consider the generalized reviews of Russian tourists about the hotel Muong Thanh Mui Ne Hotel 4 * (Vietnam, Phan Thiet). It should be noted right away that this hotel has a very decent rating based on guest ratings. So, it is 4.3 points out of the maximum possible five. This suggests that travelers who have been here are mostly satisfied with the living conditions and quality of service.



Housing stock

Judging by the reviews of our compatriots, they really liked the apartments of the hotel in question. So, according to them, the rooms here are very spacious, stylishly decorated, bright and comfortable. The guests in them felt at home. The furniture in the apartment is new and comfortable, and the equipment is modern. Everything works without interruption. As for the location of the rooms, some travelers advise, if possible, to settle in those that have a sea view. And the point here, according to them, is not only the ability to enjoy the beautiful azure waters. So, the windows of other apartments overlook the street, the noise of which may slightly interfere with your vacation. Therefore, if you have small children, or you sleep very sensitively, it is best to move into apartments with sea views. For the rest, according to vacationers, they had no complaints about the rooms of Muong Thanh Mui Ne Hotel 4 *.

Cleaning service

Our compatriots who had a rest in it did not have any complaints about the work of the maids of the hotel we are considering. According to them, the cleaning of the rooms was carried out regularly and fairly high quality. Also, as necessary, the maids replenished supplies of personal hygiene products in the bathroom, the contents of the minibar and brought bottled drinking water. Sheets and towels were changed several times a week.

Other employees of Muong Thanh Mui Ne Hotel 4 * (Phan Thiet, Vietnam) also received positive reviews. So, the guests say that the hotel employees are always friendly, friendly, quick, attentive, glad to fulfill any request and are ready to answer any question and help in resolving any situation. Some employees here understand Russian, but mostly they will have to communicate with the staff in English.

Beach vacation

Hotel Muong Thanh Mui Ne 4 * did not disappoint its guests on this point. So, tourists in their reviews note that they were very pleased with the beach holiday here. From residential buildings to the sea, you can literally walk in a couple of minutes at a leisurely pace. You can sit here on comfortable sun loungers with umbrellas. The beach here is sandy, clean. Entering shallow water. However, some travelers warn of the need to be careful, as there is a risk of stepping on a sea urchin. Such cases do not occur often, but you should still be aware of this. As for the shortcomings, a number of guests, especially those who visited here during the New Year holidays, complained about the fact that there are not enough sun loungers on the beach. However, this does not happen because there are not enough sun loungers, but because tourists occupy them early in the morning (literally at 6 a.m.), and then they come to the sea at best for dinner. But when the hotel is not completely filled, such difficulties do not arise.
