Akrathos Hotel 4 * (Greece / Halkidiki): hotel description and reviews of tourists

The Halkidiki Peninsula is located in northern Greece and is washed by the waters of the Aegean Sea. Every year, this beautiful resort attracts thousands of tourists from around the world. The infrastructure on this peninsula is developed just fine. Guests of Halkidiki can fully enjoy the real European service in combination with the most ancient local traditions. Of course, in this resort, among other things, there are many convenient hotels. For example, Akrathos Hotel 4 * offers very good rooms to guests of Halkidiki .

Where is the complex located

This hotel is located in Halkidiki, two kilometers from the village of Uranopolis. The hotel is surrounded by lush olive groves on all sides. The distance to the sea from it is about 100 meters.





In most cases, domestic tourists fly to Halkidiki, of course, by plane. The nearest airport to the hotel is in Thessaloniki, 125 km away. Of course, it will be problematic for tourists to get to this hotel on their own. Therefore, booking rooms at the Akrathos Hotel 4 * hotel (Halkidiki, Athos), Russian vacationers, usually only through tour operators. Such companies, among other things, provide their customers with a transfer to the selected hotel.

Hotel guests can reach O Uranupoli very easily. Near the hotel there is a bus stop with buses to the village twice a day. A trip to Uranupoli is very inexpensive. Also, residents of the complex, if desired, can go on a cruise along the west coast of Athos and explore many ancient monasteries.
