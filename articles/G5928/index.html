Hotel Crystal Green Bay Resort Spa 5 * (Bodrum, Turkey): description and reviews

The first three words in the long title of Crystal Green Bay Resort Spa can be translated as "crystal green bay." The word "resort" on the signboard of the hotel means that it is a resort where a person can relax from the first to the last day of vacation, without feeling the need to go beyond it. This is a small town where there is everything your heart desires: restaurants, bars, pools, the sea, entertainment, sports. And the word spa in the name of the hotel indicates that spa services are being added to this set of services. The stardom at the hotel we are describing is the highest, 5 *. Where is the hotel located

Crystal Green Bay Resort Spa is located in the province of Mugla, in the vicinity of Bodrum, in the resort village of Guverginlik. It stands on the first line from the sea and has its own beach. The center of Bodrum is twenty-three kilometers from the hotel, and twelve to the Mughla International Airport. The hotel stands in a very picturesque area, among the mountains covered with pine forests. An amazingly clean bay with water of all shades of green approaches the hotel itself. Because of her, the Crystal network gave the hotel such a name. Guverdzhinlik village itself is very calm and safe. But there are places for shopping. You can go to Bodrum with a guided tour, but you can also do it yourself. Indeed, from the village to the city shuttle buses run. Due to the peculiar location surrounded by mountains and on the shore of the bay in Guvercinlik, a unique climate has developed. The Aegean Sea is famous for being colder compared to the Mediterranean. But the bay warms up in the summer months to +28 degrees! However, the fresh breeze makes the ringing heat simply imperceptible. Therefore, families of wealthy Turks like to come to Guverjinlik. And the hotel "Crystal Green Bay 5 *" positions itself as a place for families with children.

Beach, Pools

Crystal Green Bay Resort Spa (Bodrum) is located on the first line and has its own piece of coastline with a length of 250 meters. A large pier leads into the sea, as well as a sandy beach, lined with sunbeds and umbrellas. Sunset at sea is comfortable, gentle, without stones. All beach equipment, including towels, are free for hotel guests. The hotel is washed not by the open Aegean Sea, but by Kuyuchak Bay. The water in it warms up in the summer to +28 degrees. This is a great place for bathing children. The hotel has two outdoor pools. One of them is filled with sea water - a great alternative in case of a storm. On the beach, you can have fun on bananas, water scooters and the like. But they are paid. But on two slides of a mini-water park you can ride freely and unlimited times.
