Kavkaz Golden Dune 4 * in Bulgaria

Before proceeding with the description of the Kavkaz Golden Dune 4 * hotel, it is worth paying attention to the resort area where it is located. Sunny Beach is the most famous resort in Bulgaria. Its main feature is a sandy beach almost 40 meters wide.



Sunny Beach Resort in Bulgaria

Sunny Beach has no ancient history. Estimated date of foundation of the resort - 1957. For the comfort of holidaymakers, a large amount of tons of sand was brought then, many ornamental plants were planted, several dozen hotels and resorts were erected. During the first 30 years of the existence of this young resort, it was visited by more than 70 million vacationers. Most tourists are German citizens.

Now Sunny Beach is a modern respectable resort, where hundreds of hotels, cafes, restaurants, guest houses are located. Tourists use several tens of kilometers of sandy beaches and the azure expanse of the sea, where there are practically no waves.





Hotel location and climate

The convenient location of the Sunny Beach resort, in particular the Kavkaz Golden Dune 4 * hotel (reviews of tourists confirm this), makes it possible for vacationers not only to soak up the sun by the sea or pool, but also to make exciting trips in the form of excursions to various cities of Bulgaria. And weather conditions throughout the holiday season from May to September are pleasing with their constancy: air temperature from +26 to +30 degrees Celsius in the afternoon. Sunny Beach (Slynchev Bryag) - a place for a comfortable, cozy and inexpensive vacation by world standards.
