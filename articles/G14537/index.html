Location of the hotel complex





Of course, the location is an important point when choosing a stopping place. So where to look for the Coco Resort Morjim 2 * hotel? North Goa, Morjim, a cozy resort town - it is here, in a quiet area, very close to the picturesque beach and the hotel is located.

The nearest major tourist city is Candolim - the distance to it is about 14 km. Not so far is the city of Panji (18 km), which is rich in interesting sights. Reviews suggest that the rooms at Coco Resort Morjim 2 are quite comfortable. The decor here is simple, but the necessary amount of furniture is available, including large comfortable beds. Relax on the beach: entertainment and amenities on the beach





Of course, a vacation with many people is associated with a pleasant pastime on the beach. So what should a guest of Coco Resort Morjim 2 * hotel expect? India, Goa in particular, is famous for its picturesque coast. The popular Morjim beach among travelers is located just a few meters from the hotel grounds - you can reach it in a matter of minutes.

According to reviews, the seashore is clean, as well as the entrance to the sea. The beach strip is wide and covered with fine, white sand. By the way, this place is intended for public recreation, so from time to time you can observe a large influx of tourists. Nevertheless, at any time, you can easily rent a comfortable deck chair with a sun umbrella (for a fee). But large beach towels are given out at the hotel for free.

Tourists who wish to devote time to activity will also not be bored. On the shore there is a water sports center where you can rent non-motorized water transport (catamarans, boats). For a reasonable fee you can go water skiing, banana, boat, parasailing. Also, travelers are offered diving and windsurfing lessons - experienced instructors will deal with you, whose valuable tips will help you quickly learn the basics of these sports.
