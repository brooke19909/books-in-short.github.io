Beading, necklace: patterns for beginners

Incredible tenderness, charm and charm gives the female image a necklace. This unrivaled accessory will harmoniously complement the outfit, emphasize and effectively highlight the neckline area and will certainly draw attention to its owner. Not for nothing that in previous years only royal people and noble spouses of nobles wore necklaces, emphasizing their high position in society. Today, almost all women can afford it.

Origin history

This type of jewelry came to us from ancient times. Primitive people dressed up with acquired trophies (teeth of enemies, fangs of animals), beautiful flowers, unusual plants or amulets and amulets. Some of these decorations had a certain meaning, while others wore exclusively on rites and sacraments.

Since about the 18th century, precious metal necklaces have become fashionable. They spoke with their appearance of the status of an owner. Such an ornament was put on balls and social events to emphasize the image, to show its role in society. The choker inlaid with diamonds and precious stones looked especially chic.





With the advent of various materials, different types of this accessory began to be created. Beadwork did not lag behind fashion trends. Necklaces, patterns of which can be found in different thematic publications, you can create yourself in full accordance with your outfit and taste.

Materials for creating

In order to make a necklace, you need to choose a quality material. If the beads, then Czech or Japanese, if the beads, then the same size and without deformation.

Weave the product is necessary on a solid fishing line or monofilament. Metal inserts - chain links or wire also look very stylish and advantageous. You can also use memorial wire that holds its shape. For simplicity, a special thin needle should be used. This will greatly facilitate beadwork.

Necklaces, the schemes of which can be the simplest, can be varied by inserts from large stones, rhinestones, leather, polymer or fabric colors. They perfectly complement the outline of the product, giving it a unique and zest.





Work stages

Before starting work on a necklace, it is necessary to decide for what purposes it will be created. In type it is not an everyday decoration. It is worn only in very solemn occasions. The only exception is beadwork. Schemes for beginners necklaces are so simple that finished products can complement both the evening look and everyday.

At the beginning of the work, you should carefully select the material. It should be the same size of beads, which will not cause deformation of the finished product. The base on which it will be strung must be solid and reliable.

A necklace made of beads can be created even by a person who knows little about what beadwork is. The schemes of necklaces and bracelets are very similar to each other, so you can first train on a simpler product. As a result, you can get a good kit that attracts glances. If you create earrings from the remnants of the material, the image will shine completely unusual.
