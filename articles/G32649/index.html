Vacation spots

The architecture of Nha Trang is amazing. Many cathedrals, churches, beautiful green parks and just a wonderful atmosphere. For photography enthusiasts, this place is perfect.

The Cathedral is a place of peace of mind. The temple began to be built in the 30s of the twentieth century. The cathedral was built in the Gothic style. The building itself is made of concrete and cement slabs, which makes it reliable. Inside the cathedral, the rooms are decorated with fresh flowers. In the courtyard is a statue of the Virgin Mary, and next to it is Jesus.

In modern times, the cathedral houses the local bishop. It is worth noting that next to this cathedral was once a Catholic cemetery, but in 1988 the territory was cleared and the bodies exhumed. At the site of the cemetery is now a railway.

Another attraction of the city are the towers of Ponagar. They were erected at the end of the 18th century. Another cultural place of Nha Trang can be called a place called "Longshon Pagoda." At the top of the hill, behind the building, is a statue of Buddha.



The city is famous for massage rooms and beauty salons. At the end of the day, you can enjoy a massage in any salon. The city also has a diving center, the main diving site of which is Honmun. At the bottom near the island is full of all living creatures: fish, shellfish, corals.

There is a cable car in Nha Trang, which is the longest in the world. This road is located above the bay and is located at a high altitude (40-50 meters). The city also has an oceanarium and a huge lunapark.
