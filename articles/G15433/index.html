Omsk hotels (overview)

On the banks of the Irtysh River is one of the largest cities in Russia - Omsk. In Peter's times, the Omsk fortress protected the southern borders from raids by nomads. Over the 300-year history, the city has become the second largest in Siberia and a major transport hub. From south to north, the navigable river Irtysh flows through it , and from the west to the east the famous Transsib passes.

The active growth of science and industry in Omsk began after the evacuation of numerous enterprises from the European part of the country during the Great Patriotic War. To ensure their livelihoods, many higher and secondary educational institutions, research institutes and specialized design bureaus were required. Given the active political, scientific, educational and cultural life, the city required a large number of hotels of different classes.



Recommendations for guests of Omsk

Depending on the purpose of arrival, you must choose the right place of settlement. It is difficult to calculate how many hotels in Omsk, but according to official figures there are more than a hundred. Of these, the main ones are located in the city center on the right bank of the Irtysh. They are represented by large hotels, apartments, mini-hotels, hostels and are scattered throughout the city. Therefore, having carefully studied many offers, you can choose the optimal placement at an affordable price and level of comfort.



Omsk hotels on the left bank

A ten-minute drive from Tsentralny Airport is the Molodezhnaya Hotel (1 Pereleta St.). 117 rooms of various comforts are equipped with modern furniture, equipped with a minibar, Wi-Fi and satellite TV. Breakfast is included, there is a fitness center and a sauna.

Directly near the airport is the "AeroHotel" (25 Transsiberian St.). This is very convenient due to the round-the-clock arrival and departure of guests. 16 rooms provide a minimum package of services, including Wi-Fi.

To the south, on the river bank, guests will meet the large hotel Aurora (6 Povorotnikova St.). The hotel has 124 rooms from standard to deluxe, a conference room, and free Wi-Fi. The restaurant serves European and Russian dishes, and a breakfast buffet is served each morning.

On the street 112 Suvorovtsev is "Hotel 41", named for the number of rooms of different categories. Of the additional services - a conference room, a banquet room, a restaurant and a bar.

In the picturesque bird harbor park, the Nika complex is located (110 Suvorova St.). It has 86 rooms and a wide selection of wellness services: a SPA center with a wide variety of programs, a wellness center with a pool, a sauna, a children's pool and a jacuzzi. There are also two conference rooms (for 45 and 100 people), a restaurant with a banquet room (for 200 people), a bar and a cafe "Fresh", barbecue facilities.
