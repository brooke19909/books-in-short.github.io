How to tie a snood: will we make an elegant and bright accessory?

The main element of such a scarf is the "grandmother" square. The technology for knitting the product is simple: it is necessary to make a sufficient number of squares and assemble them into a single design. You can make both plain and multi-colored squares, while the choice of color scheme depends on the preferences of the wizard. Ethnic-style scarf can be made of muted, soft, terracotta yarn. Conversely, a snood can amaze with its brightness, richness and diversity, attracting attention to its owner. When assembling squares, you can alternate and combine colorful elements as you like. So, how to tie a snood, we will describe further. To work, you need a hook number 2.5 and yarn in several shades.

Knitting square motifs for a snow scarf



Performing this product, you can choose any technique of knitting squares. We offer you several similar schemes, and you have the right to choose any one - the one you like. All square motifs are beautiful in their own way. If you are just starting your acquaintance with a hook, we advise you to create a product in approximately such a simple pattern.
How to tie a snood: dial a chain of six air loops and connect it into a ring. Perform three VP lifts. Now knit like this: three air loops and three double crochets in a ring. Repeat this item two more times. Finishing the row, complete three VP and two CH. Finish knitting with a connecting post in the third loop of the ascent. You should get a little box. Start the second row with three VPs. Then do one double crochet in the first air loop of the previous row. Next, knit according to the scheme: one CH, three VP and another CH. Then double crochet. Next, knit one air loop. Repeat the pattern again, knitting the whole circle in this way. Complete the row with a connecting half-column.

Knit "grandmother" square

Start the third row with three VIs. They are necessary for lifting. Then perform one air loop and two double crochets in the first VP of the previous row. Next, knit according to the scheme: one CH - three VP - one CH in the second, and two CH in the third loop. Then perform one air loop and three double crochet in the VP of the previous row. Now knit one VP and two CH. Then the scheme "1 CH - three VP -1 CH" is repeated. After that, 2 double crochets are made into the third air loop of the second row. According to this scheme, the entire third row is knitted. At the end, two double crochets and a connecting loop in the third loop of the second row of lifting are made. Here is such a beautiful square you should get.

If desired, you can make it multicolored, starting the execution of each row with yarn of a different color.

How to assemble individual elements in a single design?

By analogy with the first openwork square, all the others are executed. After their manufacture, they begin assembling the snood scarf. A strip of the desired length is sewn from the squares and locked into a ring. And then any strapping is done, for example, with double crochet or single crochet, or with any other edge design technique. As a result, you get a gorgeous, soft and warm scarf. Now you know how to crochet a snood scarf. The manufacturing technique of the "grandmother" square is not complicated, so any needlewoman will be able to cope with this. Having mastered this element, you can make not only scarves, but also magnificent rugs, pillows and other interesting things.
