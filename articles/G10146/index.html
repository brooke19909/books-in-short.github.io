Sol Palmeras 4 *, Varadero, Cuba: photos, room selection, quality of service, additional services and reviews of tourists

For many tourists, Cuba has become the place where they want to return again and again. The island, where the smell of carelessness and freedom, rum, coffee and cigars is closely intertwined with the smell of naphthalene, Soviet thinking, some quiet sadness and simultaneous joy read in the eyes of local residents, is truly unique. There are many beautiful resorts, but the most beautiful, according to reviews, is Varadero, which lies on a sandy peninsula about twenty kilometers long.

general information

Varadero is one of the most popular resorts in Cuba. It has a developed tourist infrastructure. Its many kilometers of excellent beaches are included in the list of the cleanest in the world.

Originating in the north of Liberty Island in the middle of the sixteenth century, this city was for some time a rank and file settlement. And only at the end of the 19th century he received the status of a resort. For over a century, Varadero was gaining popularity. Today it is the most attractive holiday destination in Cuba. The resort of Varadero gained such popularity not only due to the excellent condition of its coastline and favorable conditions for diving, but also to excellent hotels. And the presence in the city of numerous restaurants, clubs and other entertainment venues, combined with proximity to Havana - the capital of Cuba - only increases the demand for tours in this direction. Tourists come here all year round. Some people choose the high season to bask on the beach under the warming rays of the sun or dive in the waves, while others choose the time when waves start on the ocean to go diving or windsurfing. Some people like romantic walks or incendiary dances until morning. At this resort, everyone finds the best option for spending time, something of their own.

Therefore, for those who want to relax on the sea, admire the richness of local nature and the extraordinary beauty of the reefs, the clear turquoise sea, sitting on the fine white sand of the cleanest beach, breathe the southern air, get acquainted with the rich historical heritage of Liberty Island and, of course, have fun, Varadero will real find. Many believe that you can only experience the new taste and aroma of the joys of life in Cuba.
