Main Attractions Tenerife

One of the most important points that attract an endless stream of tourists from all over the world to the islands of the Canary archipelago is their climate. The weather here is characterized by rare stability and you can relax on the beaches all year round with equal comfort, without remembering whether summer is now or winter. But besides a luxurious beach holiday, people are attracted by the thirst for new unusual experiences. In particular, the sights of Tenerife deserve to be looked at at least once. Acquaintance with them can be combined with a leisurely beach holiday. But he usually tires of his monotony in a few days. And I want something new.



Tenerife Attractions

Tenerife is the largest island of the Canary Archipelago, and there is something to look at.

The first thing you should pay attention to is the Teide volcano - the highest peak not only of the Canary Islands, but also in all of Spain, to which this archipelago belongs. Its height is 3718 meters above sea level, and not to notice this peak is quite difficult. Here, unlike other objects that are included in the category of "Tenerife attractions", there are no entertainment venues. This is a national park, that is, a carefully preserved corner of the wild. When climbing to the top, you will pass through several climatic zones, as is usually the case with mountain climbing. But to climb to the top of the volcano should only be in the presence of climbing experience and equipment.

Looking over other sights of Tenerife, it is impossible not to mention Siam Park. This is certainly one of the best water parks in the world, both in terms of the variety of water attractions and the landscape design of the water sports complex. Here you can spend the whole day, and time will fly by.

If you arrived on the island with children, be sure to visit such interesting sites as Monkey Park and Loro Park. The first of them is a monkey kingdom with a large set of close relatives of a person, and the second was planned as a collection of various parrots. But lately, there has become much more diverse fauna here. Both monkeys and parrots can communicate in any language and take pictures. You can hardly list all the attractions of Tenerife in a short note, there are many of them, from time to time something completely new is offered to the attention of the public. But, of course, attention should be paid to the ancient town of La Laguna - the former capital of the island. It hosts a wide variety of events, festivals and carnivals. Many deserve attention.
