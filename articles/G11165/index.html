Hotel Sonesta Beach 5 *, Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt: tourist reviews

For those who prefer to spend their holidays or summer vacations with their families in hot countries, a pearl located on the Red Sea coast - Sharm El Sheikh - is perfect. This village is famous for its chic hotels, amazing coral reefs, hospitable people and many attractions.

A stay here, as experienced, is not only a fashionable vacation, but also a healthy body. Clean air, picturesque expanses, a favorable climate and gentle rays of the sun - that will help you relax and immerse yourself in the atmosphere of paradise pleasure.

There are countless options for accommodation, however, the five-star Sonesta Beach Hotel, which has attracted tourists for many years in a row, stands out among all.

general characteristics

Experience and many years of practice improving services have led to an endless stream of tourists to this place. The first travelers to the Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 * hotel were settled in 1994. Since then, many interior transformations have been carried out and even the name has been changed. The latest global modernization is in 2009. The spacious territory, whose area reaches the figure of 172,000 square meters, includes 32 two-story buildings, a complex of swimming pools, as well as a garden full of greenery and fragrant flowers. The exterior of the buildings is represented by classical Arabic architecture. Buildings are snow-white, according to guests, they look airy and seem to soar in zero gravity.

Location

One of the main advantages of the hotel complex Sonesta Beach 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt) is the absence of long journeys after the flight. This is due to the good location in relation to the airport of international importance. The distance is only 10 km. Such a short transfer, as noted by new arrivals, is not tiring and easy. The hotel, according to international classification, is classified as "large" and is located on the Red Sea coast. The first coastline is for vacationers to their liking, which is noted in their many reviews.

Rules of residence

Check-in at the Sonesta Beach 5 * hotel is possible at any time of the day or night thanks to the 24-hour front desk. In accordance with internal regulations, check-in with pets is not allowed. There are no special apartments for people with disabilities, but wheelchairs are available free of charge upon request for people with impossibility of habitual movement. The staff speaks Russian and English. You can pay for services with cards of international payment systems.

Number of rooms

The great variability of the rooms allows you to choose those that would meet the preferences of different travelers. In total there are 505 comfortable rooms belonging to the categories Standard, Family, Luxury and Bellagio. Their difference lies in different dimensions and landscapes opening from the window. The smallest is 38 squares and the largest is sixty square units.

The interior is surprisingly interesting and unusual. Famous designers worked on it, investing part of their soul in every element of the decor. The color scheme is dominated by all shades of white in combination with dark decorating objects. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles.

Fresh flowers, wall paintings, carpet give a sense of comfort. As tourists note, being here is comfortable and pleasant. Curtains and upholstery are made of a single fabric. Numerous lamps and sconces illuminate the room, and a large number of windows allows the rays of the sun to penetrate the apartments and give their vacationers their warmth.

All rooms have a balcony or terrace. Clean maids clean up daily and change towels. Tourists in their many positive reviews about Sonesta Beach 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh) note perfect cleanliness and order. They claim that they feel cozy and comfortable here.

Arrangement

Among the items of furniture and equipment there is everything necessary for a carefree and comfortable pastime. The furniture set includes large soft beds, bedside tables with drawers, a coffee table and a sofa. You can place your suitcases on the luggage rack, and relax during the day on the couch. Spacious wardrobe, desk with chair, as well as other elements of furniture, made of wood.

The apartments are equipped with an individual air conditioning system, which allows you to create a favorable temperature at any time of the day. The TV is multi-channel, there are Russian-language and music channels. The telephone provides communication with the reception, whose staff in a matter of minutes resolve emerging issues.

Some rooms have tea sets, dishes and an electric kettle. The minibar is empty at check-in, filling is possible at an additional cost. Using a safe will allow you not to worry about the safety of important documents and fully enjoy a laid-back vacation. Iron and ironing board are available upon request.

The bathroom is decorated with ceramic tiles in brown tones. A rectangular illuminated mirror and a marble countertop under the sink look stylish and expensive. There is a hairdryer, a set of personal hygiene products, white towels and terry bathrobes.

Nutrition

In the hotel complex Sonesta Beach 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh) there is an all-inclusive concept. Under this program, meals are served in buffet style. Three meals a day are served at the Casablanca and Citadel restaurants. The interior is made primarily of marble. Ubiquitous columns form numerous arches. The furniture is represented by chairs with carved backs. Individual serving is made at the highest level, on the tables - bright flowers.

The variety of dishes served, according to the guests, is incredibly large. Baking, vegetables and dairy products, meat fireworks and sweets, as well as many other products can satisfy the gastronomic preferences of each guest. The children's menu will delight young guests with its nutritiousness and deliciousness, it includes two main dishes, salad and dessert.

There are 6 bars, where all drinks are served exclusively in glasses. Here you can not only quench your thirst, but also have a bite to eat with light snacks and flour products. Freshly squeezed juice, imported alcohol and bottled drinks require additional payment.

Beach

A feature of the coastal strip of these edges are golden sands, which delight the eye of swimmers and envelop the heat of sunbathers. Infrastructure includes locker rooms, showers, deckchairs with soft mattresses, umbrellas for lovers of shade and a plastic pontoon. The length of the beach is 130 meters. The towel dispenser is free of charge. Here you can visit the bar, the consumption of drinks of which are included in the all inclusive system.

In order to get here, vacationers need to overcome a distance of less than one hundred meters. The hotel is connected to the beach by a paved pedestrian road.

The main entertainment on the shores of the Red Sea is getting to know the amazing underwater world. Diving in the open sea near scuba coral reefs allows you to enjoy the purity and transparency of deep waters. Interesting and colorful inhabitants, widespread vegetation and many colorful fish, travelers claim, are breathtaking.

On the shore you can play volleyball or ride a scooter. Boating and banana rides are a popular activity for guests.

No less interesting is the team game of water polo. Guests are allowed to visit the Sonesta Club's mini-water park. Bright and exciting slopes will give a lot of joy and positive to each guest.

health and beauty

A visit to the spa will give pleasant emotions and a sense of renewal and freshness to each visitor. A number of wellness and medical procedures will rejuvenate the skin, normalize the work of the circulatory and musculoskeletal systems, and immerse vacationers in a state of relaxation and enjoyment. Quiet music, fragrances of essential oils and the hands of an experienced massage therapist, according to tourists, can give pleasure to every cell of the body.

Sauna, steam room and Jacuzzi are no less in demand. High temperatures and a friendly atmosphere allow you to get a lot of joyful emotions and a good mood.

Gym doors are open for all tourists. Professional trainers will help to distribute the load on the body in the best way, as well as conduct classes in fitness and aerobics. There are two concrete tennis courts. Racket rental and lighting are paid. Once a week the game of tennis is free. There is a field for indoor soccer and basketball.

Entertainment

Other activities include billiards and tennis. All those present at the evening show are directly involved in the process. Incendiary dances, interesting contests and performances by artists, as guests note, will not leave anyone indifferent. This show takes place in the amphitheater. In their free time, guests can try their luck at the local casino.

Toddler Leisure

The hotel complex is positioning itself as a great place for families. According to the guests in the reviews about Sonesta Beach 5 *, this is quite deserved and justified. The leisure of the children is thought out to the smallest detail. The presence of a children's mini-club allows kids to play board games with their peers, draw and participate in culinary master classes. Under the guidance of a professional educator, children play outdoor games and participate in games for the manifestation of ingenuity. A good mood, unforgettable impressions and unlimited happiness are guaranteed.

There is a playground in the fresh air. It consists of slides, transitions and carousels. There are children's tables with chairs for creative outdoor activities. The evening ends with a disco.

For parents who want to give their child under individual supervision, you can use the services of a nanny. A cot in the room is available upon request. Wheelchair rental is not available.

Additional services

The range of additional services is incredibly extensive in the institution Sonesta Beach Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh). A currency exchange office is located in the lobby. Laundry and dry cleaning services are paid upon. You can buy a memorable gift in the gift shop. Wi-Fi is free only in public areas. Doctor's services are paid. An Internet corner will allow you to go online and contact relatives at any time of the day.

Possible early check in and late check out. Experienced drivers will deliver guests to their destination in a matter of minutes and with safety for their health.

The hotel complex has a beauty salon and a hairdresser. Professional stylists will be happy to create for each visitor a new amazing look with the help of an original hairstyle or unusual makeup.
