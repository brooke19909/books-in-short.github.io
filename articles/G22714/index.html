Hotel Phuket Kata Resort 3 * (Phuket island, Thailand): description and photos

Do you like to relax in Thailand or do you want to visit this amazing hospitable country for the first time? Do not want to spend money on an expensive hotel, but would like to live in comfortable conditions? An excellent solution for you could be Phuket Kata Resort 3 * (Phuket). In what awaits the guests here, and we will understand further.



Location

This 3-star hotel is located in Phuket (Thailand), 500 meters from the popular Kata Beach. Judging by the reviews of tourists who have already been here, it is located in an area with well-developed infrastructure. So, in the neighborhood you will find many cafes, bars, shops, supermarkets, retail shops, companies offering excursions and car rental, pharmacies, markets, massage parlors, etc. Also, the hotel is located a short distance from the busy road, so guests noise will not bother. As for the distance from the nearest international airport, the distance to it is about 47 kilometers. So, upon arrival, tourists can get to Phuket Kata Resort 3 * (Thailand, Phuket, Kata Beach) in less than an hour.



General information, photo

The hotel in question began its work in 2002. Since then, partial renovation has been held here for several days. So today, tourists expect comfortable, fully equipped rooms with freshly renovated.

Phuket Kata Resort has a small, but very well-groomed and green area. It has several 1-, 2- and 3-story buildings, as well as three outdoor pools with a jacuzzi, bars, a conference room, sun terraces with comfortable sun loungers and parasols, a spa center. The hotel has a restaurant offering everyone who wants to taste excellently prepared dishes of both Thai and European cuisine. The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 158 spacious comfortable rooms decorated in a traditional Thai style.

In general, travelers who have left 3 * reviews about Phuket Kata Resort say that this hotel is an excellent combination of price and quality of the accommodation and services provided. This is also evidenced by the hotel rating, based on guest ratings. So, it is very high and is 4.6 points out of five possible. According to our compatriots, it will be comfortable here for all categories of tourists - both young people who crave entertainment, and family people with children and elderly vacationers.



Housing stock

As already mentioned, in total this hotel has 158 rooms. They are located in small one-, two- and three-story buildings, surrounded by greenery, flowers and palm trees. The apartments are presented in the following categories:

  • "Deluxe" (30 sq. M, the maximum number of guests - 2 + 1).
  • "Family Deluxe" (area 30 sq. M, the room is designed for a maximum of 2 + 2 people).
  • Family Suite with one bedroom (two rooms, king size bed, living room with kitchenette, equipped with stove, microwave and toaster, dining area). Rooms are 60 square meters. meters. It is designed for 3 + 2 guests.

In addition to the above, the rooms also include a TV, refrigerator, air conditioning, telephone, hairdryer, tea set, terrace or balcony. Wi-Fi access is also available throughout the hotel.



Service

Judging by the reviews of tourists, the work of the maids at Phuket Kata Resort 3 * they were quite satisfied. So, cleaning is carried out very high quality, every day. Guests note that the maids do not forget to wash the floors, which is not found in all hotels in this category. Towels will be updated every day too. Bed linen is changed a couple of times a week.

Also, guests can use the room service from 6:30 to 22:00.

Nutrition

When booking a room in this hotel, you can either choose the option that does not include meals, or order only breakfast. According to those who preferred the second option, they did not regret it at all. So, guests note that breakfasts here are always quite diverse. Dishes and products are always fresh and tasty. In the morning you will be offered omelet or scrambled eggs, boiled eggs, sausage, bacon, flavored pastries, cereals, a meat dish, noodles, juice, tea, coffee.

During the day, tourists can eat in the hotel restaurant for a fee, as well as in numerous cafes in the surrounding area.
