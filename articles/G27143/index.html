Profitable deposits at Rosselkhozbank: features and conditions for opening

In Russia today there are about 600 different banks. And, of course, the largest customer base is among those who occupy the top ranks of the rating. One of those is Rosselkhozbank. He is in sixth position. And, like any other self-respecting financial institution, it offers its customers deposits. In the "Agricultural Bank" people are offered good conditions and favorable rates. Here about them and it is worth telling in more detail.





Interest Forward

This is the name of the first contribution that I would like to touch upon. You can open it either for 395 or 548 days. The minimum investment should be 3,000 rubles (the maximum amount is not limited). Interest paid is very interesting. Once for the entire invested amount - the day after the opening of the deposit. They are transferred to the account of a payment card opened in the same place at the Rosselkhozbank. But, of course, replenishment, expenditure operations and automatic prolongation are not provided for at this rate.

This is a new proposal, it was launched in early November of the current 2016. It is joint-stock, so people can open such deposits at Rosselkhozbank before 02/28/17.

Rates are as follows: for 395 days - 7.2% per annum, and for 548 - 7.4%. If a person makes a contribution of more than 50,000 rubles, then the Amur Tiger card is also issued for free. With a yield of 7% per annum, by the way.





Amur Tiger - card for deposit

This tariff is worth a little more. This card is owned by many people who have opened deposits in Rosselkhozbank. It is a debit one, it is a universal payment tool accepted worldwide (there is a variant of Visa and MasterCard).

Performing any operations with it, a person takes part in helping Amur tigers. As part of the income (0.7%) received from the owners of this card, the bank transfers to the Center for the Study and Conservation of the population of these animals.

This is a convenient thing. She can pay for mobile communications, Internet, utilities, shopping in stores online. And besides, a person receives an additional income of 7% per annum on the balance of funds. Here's another explanation for why opening deposits at Rosselkhozbank is profitable.
