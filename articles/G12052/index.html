The complex "Cottages in Gagra" - the best place to relax

Soon, summer will come, and the soul will begin to beg at sea. Then everyone will think about where he would like to relax, because you want to get as comfortable as possible, so that the warm sea is as close as possible. As a good option for a resort city, you can consider Gagra in Abkhazia. In this city, the complex "Cottages in Gagra" (Abkhazia), which is located at 8 Avidzba Street, is especially popular.



Location

First of all, tourists are attracted by the proximity of the cottage complex to the sea (a little less than half a kilometer), because nothing can be worse than exhausting trips over long distances on a hot day. One kilometer from the complex "Cottages in Gagra" is a large city water park, where guests like to relax.

Not far from the cottages there is a large city market where you can buy souvenirs or tasty and fresh products: loose tea, honey from an apiary, unusual spices and spices. On every corner the famous churchkhela is for sale. Alcohol lovers are urged to buy a bottle of chacha or homemade tincture for themselves.

A railway station is located near the complex (a little less than two kilometers), and it takes about an hour to drive to the airport for a very short time (the nearest one is in Sochi, about 44 kilometers from the hotel).

Complex device

"Cottages in Gagra" is a hotel in which anyone can rent a room for themselves during the spa season, only it is advisable to book it in advance, as the flow of tourists is very large, and there are few rooms, only six, and all of them are located in one two-story building .

There are five small houses made of wood on the territory. They also have rooms for guests.

The hotel has a separate area for eating, located in the fresh air. There is also a separate grill, where guests can cook meat or vegetables for themselves. In the courtyard there are many places to relax: benches, deck chairs and armchairs.



Device numbers

The hotel rooms and individual houses do not differ from each other in the degree of comfort. Each of them has a good air conditioning, which regulates the temperature in the room, there are televisions broadcasting several channels. There is a personal shower room for each room, in which guests are provided with a standard set of bath accessories: towels, gel, soap, shampoo. The bathroom is also individual.

There is a separate kitchen in which there is a full set of dishes necessary for cooking. It also has a fridge for storing food and an electric kettle.

In general, everything is very well arranged, comfort is provided for those who decide to come to rest in "Cottages in Gagra". Reviews, due to attention to guests, the complex receives good.



Entertainment

On the territory of the complex itself, of course, there are no entertainment and show programs, but they can be found very close by, because the complex "Cottages in Gagra" is located within walking distance to all the entertainment places of the city.

One of the main favorite activities of tourists is excursions. In Gagra, there is the opportunity to book an unforgettable ride in jeeps - a very exciting trip that certainly will not seem standard and monotonous! You can also visit a trout farm, take a horse ride, go sea fishing or book a flight on an airplane.

The city has many restaurants and cafes, in particular, Mill, Gagripsh, Mojito, Platan, cocktail bar Kakadu.

Cultural lovers will appreciate visiting the Abaat city museum or a trip with an excursion to neighboring cities of Abkhazia, for example, to Sukhumi, Pitsunda or the village of Lykhny, where there are many attractions.

At night, guests can head to the Lime nightclub.

Check-in to the complex "Cottages in Gagra"

It is advisable to book a hotel room in advance. This can be done by phone or online by filling out an online form on the site. During the day, the administrator will call you back and, after all points have been agreed, you will be asked to make an advance payment for accommodation. If payment is not received within 24 hours, the reservation is canceled.



Shortly before the arrival of the guests, the service staff prepares a room, performs cleaning and lowers the room temperature to a comfortable level.
