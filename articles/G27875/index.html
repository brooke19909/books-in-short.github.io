Do you know how to stick a film on your phone?

We all want things that you have to buy to be as cheap as possible, but at the same time to be of high enough quality. Of course, it makes no sense to deny the rule that the price of a quality thing cannot be too low, but life experience shows that in many cases a compromise is still possible. So, many owners of mobile communication devices know very well that timely glued film for the phone screen not only reduces wear on the front side of the device, but also often protects against the need to purchase a new mobile phone.





If expensive models use a durable glass coating that is scratch-resistant, then the budget line cannot boast so much: the plastic of the screen during active use or not too careful handling is covered with scratches. If you immediately stick the protective film on the phone, then the load will fall on it. It remains only to periodically replace. The advantages of such a protective coating include the non-obvious fact that if the phone accidentally falls, the film will protect against the appearance of the infamous network of cracks.



Training

Although at first glance there is nothing complicated in sticking a tape onto a phone, in fact, everything is a little different. When glued without proper preparation, dust, fibers, air bubbles remain between the film and the plastic of the screen, which spoil the appearance of the device. Thus, you must first thoroughly clean the screen. This operation is recommended to be done with the help of special dry wipes, which usually come with a film (you can also use means for cleaning LCD monitors). People who do not even know how to stick a film on the phone should remember the rule that shreds of cloth are not suitable for cleaning (with rare exceptions), since they usually contain a significant amount of dust. So, the task is to remove all types of pollution. Sometimes it is recommended to stick a film in the bathroom, where there is less dust. However, this is at the discretion of the owner.



How to stick a film on the phone

After preparation, you can proceed to gluing. New film is always supplied based on. It is necessary to arrange it so that the adhesive side is transferred to the screen. To do this, attach the film to the upper or lower part of the display and pull a little at the angle marked on the basis (tongue). The transparent sticky layer adheres to the screen. It must be smoothed with a napkin to remove air bubbles. And so to the end: a little glued, smoothed, etc. The film should only be on the screen, without going into the plastic housing and institutions in between the surfaces - in these places it will begin to peel off over time. The main thing is that there are no specks of dust or nap on the display, since they cannot be removed without re-gluing. Air bubbles that cannot be driven out by smoothing will disappear after 2-3 days. Usually, when buying such a protective coating, instructions are attached on how to stick the film on the phone. It is necessary to familiarize yourself with it.
