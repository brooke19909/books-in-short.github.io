Let's talk about how to assemble a paper octahedron

In this article we will look at how to assemble a paper octahedron, what this figure represents and how it can be useful for each of us. Perhaps someone will find himself in this kind of creativity, or at last he will get the option of a homemade gift to a loved one. The instruction for making this stereometric figure is simple, and even a child of preschool age can handle it , so let's go.





We begin to collect the figure

Many, looking at the scan octahedron, are wondering how to correctly connect all its faces and sides to each other so that the figure acquires the correct appearance. Let's try to uncover this geometric secret and understand how to assemble a paper octahedron. First, we need the figure itself in the scan. It can be re-taken from the book or printed image that is attached to the article. We can say that this is a ready-made scheme. How to make an octahedron yourself? It is also easy. Take any value that you need as a basis. This will be the length of each face of the shape. Then draw six equilateral triangles as you see them in the picture, and draw two more to them.



Features of the installation of faces

Many, even after they build a scan of the figure, do not understand how to assemble the paper octahedron so that it turns out to be correct. To do this, cutting out the figure, leave allowances for gluing. They have beveled edges, and in one of the figures they are depicted. Now we apply glue to these allowances and collect a stereometric figure along the fold lines. It is important that you first make clear bends at the intersection of all the faces of the figure. So your craft will have a more beautiful and accurate, from a geometric point of view, appearance.
