Hotel "Zhoekvara" (Gagra, Abkhazia): description, services, reviews

"Zhoekvara" - a hotel (4 stars), which was built in the center of the famous city of Gagra in 2016. Due to its favorable location, it is suitable for traveling couples with children and young people, outdoor enthusiasts. The hotel is located on the seashore (20 meters from the water), which makes it very attractive for relaxation.

Hotel Location

The hotel was built "Zhoekvara" (Gagra) just 2 km from the airport of Adler. Lovers of antiquities will love the ancient Gagra, which offers views of the gorges, bay and majestic mountains. There is everything for a memorable vacation: the sea, ancient monuments, developed and modern infrastructure.





The nature and climate of the place where the hotel is located are considered to be unique. The climate is humid, subtropical. It is characteristic of the Black Sea coast. The weather in Gagra is the warmest in the European part of the countries of the former Union. The average annual temperature reaches 17 degrees. Here the warmth of the coast and the coolness of the mountains coexist. Due to the humid subtropical microclimate, the weather in Gagra is very pleasant for relaxation.

How to get to the hotel

To arrive at the hotel "Zhoekvara" (Gagra), you need to get to the city of Adler. You can use air or rail.

If you follow the airport, then by city bus number 173 or taxi you need to get to the Russian-Abkhaz border. Travel time takes about 50 minutes. At the Psou checkpoint, cross the border post (across the bridge). It is approximately 500 m. Further, already on the territory of the Republic of Abkhazia, you can drive to the hotel by taxi, city bus or minibus. Travel time takes about 1 hour 30 minutes.





There are several fixed-route taxis from the railway station to the Psou checkpoint: No. 117, No. 125, No. 57, No. 100. Travel time takes about an hour. Unfortunately, they stop a kilometer from the border. This distance will have to be walked. To avoid this, you can take a taxi from the station. Further from the checkpoint the route is the same as from the airport.

For guests who choose the hotel "Zhoekvara" (Gagra), it is possible to order a transfer from the border for an additional fee.

Sights of New Gagra near the hotel

New Gagra is the part of the city built during the Soviet era. All the guest houses and hotels of the resort are concentrated here. These places are suitable for young people who love outdoor activities. Cafes and restaurants of the New City are open until morning and welcome guests.

The pride of the city is a local water park. There are huge water slides for adults and children to enjoy, a freshwater pool and a cozy cafe with delicious pizza according to local recipes.

Guests of the town should definitely visit the market and buy souvenirs and souvenirs at attractive prices.
