Climate and coastal features

What can oppose Gangnam and Antibes Italy? The Ligurian coast is reliably sheltered from the cold northern winds of the Alps. Thanks to this, the tourist season in this region lasts from the beginning of May to the very end of October. The thermometer column stably holds at +22 ... +28 degrees Celsius. The temperature is more than comfortable, isn't it? There is no ringing heat, as in Tuscany, in Liguria, because the coolness of the summer brings sea winds. Administrative submission of resorts

The Italian province of Liguria stretches along the Gulf of Genoa for more than two hundred kilometers. Popular resorts stretch almost in a continuous series. In the middle of the Flower Riviera is the provincial capital - the brilliant white marble Genoa. The city divides the Ligurian coast of Italy, the map of which is presented here, into two halves. The western part is called the Riviera di Ponente. Riviera di Levante stretches east from Genoa to Tuscany. Both of these parts have their own characteristics. Riviera di Ponente is considered a place of elite relaxation. Alassio, Bordighera and Diano Marina are often visited by celebrities. And the Riviera di Levante attracts vacationers with enchanting landscapes, first-class beaches and bays with crystal clear water. The most famous resorts in this part of the Ligurian coast are Portofino, Santa Margherita and Rapallo.

Arenzano and Varazza

These cities of the Ligurian coast are located ten to thirty kilometers west of Genoa. In fact, Arenzano is the new suburb of the provincial capital. These are typical Riviera di Ponente resorts. Gorgeous seaside boulevards, an ideal climate with mild winters and not very sultry summers, proximity to Genoa make these cities excellent places for families. However, Varazze can boast of ancient antiquities. Here the remains of the fortifications and shipyards of the Roman sailing military fort. And since the Middle Ages, the companilla of the monastery church of St. Ambrose has risen here. But the main wealth of Varazze is a mile and a half sandy beach.

Spot, Finale Ligure and Pietra Ligure

In Pietra Ligure, the sand does not end, and there are also enough hotels that have their own beach.





Alassio

This town is the uncrowned capital of the Riviera di Ponente. He is known far beyond Italy for celebrities who regularly spend their holidays in this charming place with sandy beaches. This is the peculiar answer that Gangnam, Nice and Monaco can give Liguria. Alassio boasts an interesting attraction - the wall of Muretto. This is part of the city fortifications erected in the 16th century to protect against pirates. Covered with ceramic tiles, the wall served as a place for autographs of various celebrities. Ernest Hemingway, Winston Churchill, Louis Armstrong, Sophia Loren and others left their signatures on it. In addition to Muretto, in Alassio there is a Romanesque church of St. Ambrose, and other architectural monuments. Laiguelia and Diano Marina

We continue to study the Ligurian coast. The map shows that to the west of Alassio, almost merging with it, there is a small village of Laiguela. Her story is firmly connected with the name of Thor Heyerdahl, a Norwegian archaeologist and traveler. In 1958, he was captivated by the provincial spirit of this typical Ligurian village and built a house here, along with the restoration of old houses on a hillside. The attention of tourists is attracted by the majolica-covered domes of the baroque church of St. Matthew. A sandy beach surrounded by majestic mountains will be an added bonus for those who have chosen this exemplary resort for their holidays. Diano Marina is famous for having budget hotels here. San Remo and Bordighera

Every year in February there is a pop song festival that Italy is proud of. The Ligurian coast of the Riviera di Ponente cannot be described without mentioning San Remo. The capital of the Italian stage can please visitors to the city with a concert hall, the legendary Casino and the Orthodox Church, which was built by the Russian community in the 20s of the last century. And Bordighera, which is twelve kilometers from San Remo, has a completely different, sporting glory. It hosts the Grand Prix of Formula 1 racing. And Bordighera is known for its championship of fireworks. In addition to interesting events, the resort boasts a museum of cave paintings of primitive people.





Camogli

Now we are transported to the resorts of the Ligurian coast of the Riviera di Levante. The most famous of them is Ca-Moli. The name of the village is translated as "House of Women." In those days when tourists on the Ligurian coast still did not smell, all the men in the village went out to sea every morning. Their wives patiently awaited the return of the fishermen. Now the resort is famous for the bronze statue of Christ. Cristo degli Abyssi does not rise above the city, but rather, is deepened into the sea by 15 meters. In calm calm the statue is clearly visible - such transparent water off the coast of Camogli. In the same town, tourists can see nice little old houses, street-stairs, Benedictine abbey and the palace of the XIII century. Only by sea can you reach the village of San Fruttuoso. To attract early tourists, the Sagra del Pesche festival is held in Camogli in May, where all participants are treated to fish fried in huge pans.

Portofino

This former fishing village is also visited by world celebrities. And the French writer Guy de Maupassant began the pilgrimage. He loved to be here and wrote about Portofino, that "this is a realm of harmony, so contrasting with our world of vanity." The resort is located on a cape, so that the sand and pebble beaches of the Ligurian coast surround it on three sides. Many years have passed since the days of the classic of French literature, and in modern Portofino shops, restaurants, ATMs, hotels and other signs of our busy world have appeared. But nature still captivates tourists with its pristine cleanliness. Olives, pines, palm trees and cypress trees climb the hillside, at the top of which stands an old lighthouse.





Rapallo

This resort is located only forty kilometers east of Genoa. It is convenient for tourists who are planning an excursion vacation. If you are interested in Italy hotels with a private beach, pay attention to the Excelsior Palace Hotel. In this oasis of exquisite relaxation, there are rooms and suites that open onto terraces directly to the sea. There are also the most popular restaurants in the area - the panoramic Lord Byron buffet and Eden Rock on the beach, a la carte system. Rapallo has its own historical sights. This is a temple The 16th-century St. Francis, which houses several paintings by Bortsone, and the collegiate church with a bell tower of the 17th century.The face of the town is lovely, painted in bright colors old houses against the backdrop of lush Mediterranean nature. Quiet silence, tranquility and luxurious service.





Santa Margherita Ligure

The resort is convenient for tourists traveling by car. The main highways intersect here. You can easily get to Milan, Turin, Florence, Nice. Existing in the era of Ancient Rome, Santa Margherita Ligure bloomed during the Renaissance (as, indeed, the whole of Italy). The Ligurian coast at this place is called Tigullio Bay. Most of the postcards of Santa Margherita are the old port with a cascade of houses going down to it. Seaside Boulevard is decorated with an elegant Renaissance palace - Villa Durazzo, surrounded by gardens and fountains. The fact that Santa Margherita became a resort as far back as the century before last is evidenced by beautiful Art Nouveau mansions and palm-tree-lined boulevards. It often hosts flower fairs. At the same time, this is a town where young people like to relax: there are many discos and clubs.





Sisters Levante

This pearl of the Flower Riviera is located at the foot of a high rocky cape. Its peak is crowned by the church of San Nicollo, erected in honor of the patron of sailors in the Early Middle Ages. Since the town stands on a cape, it has at its disposal two bays at once - Favole (Fairy Tales) and Silencio (Silence), which are famous for their beauty on the entire Ligurian coast of Italy. Hotels here are on the first line and have their own beach. You can recommend Miramare 4 *: 75 rooms and suites are located in an old white-marble building. In addition to the beach services, which are free for guests, the hotel also provides spa pleasures, a variety of baths and procedures.
