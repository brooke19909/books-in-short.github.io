Car Alarms "Tomahawk" - high quality!



For more than ten years in a row, Tomahawk car alarms have been in great demand both among service center specialists and car owners. Distinctive features of equipment manufactured under this brand are high quality and ease of service. In addition, the Tomahawk alarm system, which costs an average of three thousand rubles, is affordable for almost any car owner. This anti-theft system is being manufactured in Taiwan, and today South America, Eastern and Western Europe, the Middle East, Southeast Asia and North America are considered the main export markets.

Benefits of Tomahawk Alarm

As the main competitive advantage of the equipment of this Taiwanese brand, experts call the convenience and ease of service and repair.





Car owners who opt for the Tomahawk security alarm are provided with technical support twenty-four hours a day. The company's specialists are ready at any time, if necessary, to help the owner of this anti-theft system with installation or configuration issues. In addition, this equipment is constantly being improved, as a result of which additional useful functions appear and the level of protection increases. It should also be noted that all Tomahawk anti-theft alarms are developed taking into account the requirements of car manufacturers using the most modern technologies with constant quality control at all stages of production, which is confirmed by various international certificates.

Key Features





If we talk about the functional capabilities of the anti-theft systems of the Tomahawk brand, it is first necessary to note the presence of the built-in auto-stop function. It will be indispensable when the car owner for some reason forgets to put the car on guard. In addition, it is worth mentioning the automatic closing of the siren output and power reset during an alarm, which will in no way affect the operability of the Tomahawk system. The alarm system, reviews of which are only the most positive, also has a built-in function to automatically start the car engine. In addition, different models of the anti-theft system are equipped with a large number of different programmable settings, allowing you to build the most appropriate security complex configuration for each specific car.

Purchase of Tomahawk security system

Signals of the Taiwanese Tomahawk trademark are currently available at most service centers. In addition, you can always contact the salons specializing in car sales. At the same time, at most points of sale of these anti-theft systems, the car owner will also be offered a professional installation.
