How it all began

The country of manufacture of the iPhone is America, everyone knows that. The world famous company Apple and the legendary Steve Jobs have released and developed this gadget. They conceived it and put it into practice. And what about mass production?

IPhone 6: manufacturer

The country producing the gadget of this model is Taiwan. The company that collects iPhone 6 is Foxconn (Taiwan). Its enterprises are located in China. And in addition to Apple products, she also produces cameras for Canon, popular game consoles, and mobile phones from a number of well-known brands. The company also manufactures motherboards with both its own logo and the illustrious Intel.

Foxconn, created back in 1974, began with the creation of small plastic parts for TVs, and after fourteen years it grew to the opening of its own factory in China. Now she has subsidiaries, she collaborates with large concerns for the production of electronics and components for her.

Why is an iPhone, the manufacturing country of which is geographically located in another hemisphere, assembled in China? It's all about scale. Foxconnt City is a whole town with a workforce of two hundred thirty thousand people, whose work schedule is twelve hours a day and six days a week. There is a whole system with certain rules and work schemes. And in the USA, there are simply not so many unoccupied engineers. According to studies, it will take about a year before it is possible to collect the required number of workers, in China it will take two weeks on the strength.

Small clarification

The iPhone (whose manufacturing country is actually Taiwan, but with production capacities in China) cannot be called completely Chinese. There are purely American components in it:

  1. Glass is manufactured at the Corning plant in Kentucky.
  2. The software is being developed by a data center (North Carolina).
  3. Semiconductors are manufactured by a factory in Texas.




How to identify the country of manufacture of the iPhone?

If you didn‚Äôt buy your smartphone from an authorized dealer and, after reading, you became interested in its origin, information about the device will help. On the packaging of the iPhone you need to find the serial number. If suddenly you did not find it there, refer to the phone menu. In the "Settings" section (the "Basic" tab) there is a sub-item "About the device", it also contains a number, usually consisting of numbers and four letters.

To check the country of origin, it is important for us to remember the last two letters. The Internet can easily find a list that decrypts all their combinations. For example, if the number is MC352LL, then LL indicates that the phone came from the United States. The Chinese copy will give the designation CH.

It was previously believed that the IMEI code helps calculate the country where the iPhone was manufactured. To do this, we used a special service, where a device code of fifteen characters was entered. As the results showed, the data on the region contained in IMEI differ from the information of the number of the iPhone. In this regard, Numberingplans will help determine only the make and model of the phone, it is useless to identify the country of origin.
