Hotel Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya): description, traveler reviews

In this article we will discuss in detail one hotel located in Thailand. If you really want to have a good rest and remember your vacation for life, then you need to go here. It is at Sea Breeze Hotel that you truly experience the essence of Thailand. Here they are waiting for you! Do not believe? We will prove now!

general information

Hotel Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * is located in the Jomtien area, which, in turn, is located in the southern part of the coast of Pattaya (Thailand). The hotel is a complex consisting of a main building and several adjacent buildings.



This hotel can be safely called universal, as it focuses on relaxation both for quiet couples and for large companies.

Hotel location

Hotel Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * is located just fifty meters from the sea, that is, visitors need only to go through the roadway to get to the luxurious beach with all amenities.

Jomtien District boasts its relative silence compared to downtown Pattaya. It is also worth mentioning that the beach is one of the best on the coast of Thailand. In 2015, the embankment was completely transformed by a professional construction company.

This city regularly holds competitions in beach and sometimes sea sports. Transport in the Jomien area runs regularly to various tourist destinations. In particular, direct buses run from the airport, which is located in Bangkok.

Hotel address and description

The hotel is located at 347/5 Jomtien Beach Road, Near Pattaya City. The institution works year-round, as the weather in Thailand contributes to this.



The size of the hotel is very large, as it has about 277 rooms of different categories. In the Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * (Jomtien Beach) there is also a small park for walking and a garden. The hotel has two shallow pools, in which both adult vacationers and children can spend time. Now let's discuss the institution from the inside out.

Rooms & Facilities

In Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * there are three main categories of rooms, namely: rooms for the middle class Superior, Deluxe and apartments for customers with children Junior Suites.

Superior room is suitable for the middle class and has a standard, but very modern and elegant look. These apartments have everything you need to relax: a TV, a minibar with cold drinks, modern and technological air conditioning, a telephone and, of course, a bathroom with everything you need.

Luxury rooms are equipped for the most comfortable stay.

Hotel infrastructure

The hotel has a games room in which parents-tourists can leave their children for a short excursion to local attractions, of which there are a lot in the area.



The largest room in the hotel is nothing more than a ballroom, which is designed for five hundred people. Various events, concerts and private parties can take place here.

Since the hotel is designed for a large number of tourists, it is not limited to the existence of only one ballroom. There are as many as three of them. They are equipped with all relevant equipment. By the way, in the hotel you will also find a conference room for up to 350 people.

Service staff

Every day, cleanliness and order are maintained by professionally trained staff. Airing rooms, replacing towels, wet cleaning - all this and much more are done daily by workers. Due to the high-quality cleaning with disinfectants, Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * does not even have a hint of insects.

At the reception are pleasant girls who know English well. Due to the fact that in Thailand there are a lot of Russian-speaking vacationers, you can contact the staff in Russian.

In any incomprehensible situation, residents turn to the staff at the reception, and all the flaws the hotel staff are trying to quickly eliminate. The hotel staff, whether masseurs or waiters, is absolutely adequate and reverent to the wishes of customers.

Service

One of the main services is free food, namely breakfasts. Do not forget about the quality protection of the hotel, currency exchange, which is produced without additional commission.



Pools and everything that is attached to them, namely: umbrellas, sunbeds, mattresses, towels are free, which will save a lot of money and spend money, for example, on additional excursions.

Paid services, of course, include much more services that Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * provides. First of all, visitors are concerned about the safety of their belongings, so the hotel provides safes that are located at the reception, as well as in rooms of certain categories.

Do not forget that when changing the climate, many vacationers may experience uncomfortable feelings, so the hotel has a special medical room with qualified staff. The hotel also has a pharmacy with all the necessary medicines.

For a more comfortable stay, the complex provides laundry and dry cleaning services. For events and rental of conference rooms, of course, you will need to pay.



There are special cots for children. In addition, each hotel client can call a qualified nanny.

Lovers of good relaxation will definitely enjoy modern and comfortable saunas and a jacuzzi. And customers who cannot live without sports for a day will be able to visit the fitness center with modern sports equipment.

Communication and Internet

For most vacationers during a trip and vacation, the tour operator issues a special SIM card with favorable conditions. But if you suddenly have a communication problem or something else, then there is always the opportunity to call from the hotel. In Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * (Pattaya) you will spend an unforgettable vacation. There is absolutely free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel complex, which you can use at any time of the day or night.

Visitors reviews

For all the time the Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * hotel received various reviews. The comments that customers leave online are highly controversial. Most vacationers have a pretty rest and recommend the hotel to their other compatriots. Some visitors did not like the high humidity of the rooms, but not all customers realize that high humidity is the norm in Thailand. Although for residents of Russia this is not always acceptable.





Many tourists liked the local Thai cuisine. Some customers note in their reviews that after a few weeks of staying at the hotel, cooked meals become very mundane and even sometimes begin to bother. Nevertheless, you can always find something new. Particularly well, cafe visitors celebrate a children's menu, from which kids come to an indescribable delight.

Some guests note in their reviews quality and responsible service. However, there are those who, on the contrary, did not like the attitude of the staff. It is worth noting that there are much fewer such reviews, and most of the comments relate to minor household problems, such as a towel left in the wrong place and so on.

Many tourists praise the comfortable and clean beaches, which are located near the Sea Breeze Jomtien Resort 3 * (Pattaya), because they are really luxurious. Both the coastline and the hotel itself will definitely appeal to most lovers of a relaxing and traditional holiday.
