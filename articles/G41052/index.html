Tablet Screen Protector and Installation



Tablet computers have become a novelty of the last two years and have firmly taken their place in the daily life of most residents of megalopolises and big cities. They combined the power and performance of laptops, a large display for easy viewing of information, as well as compact dimensions and low weight.

The daily use of a tablet computer for many has long replaced all other sources of information. Here you can read books, watch movies and programs, find out the latest news, stay in touch with friends and family.

The world of related accessories for tablets does not stand still and is developing along with the market for new technologies. The latest models, constantly changing sizes and dimensions of devices, as well as fresh fashion trends entail updating the range of accessories.

The most popular and functional additions to tablets are cases, bags and films. They allow devices to work longer and maintain a presentable appearance. Such additions protect them from mechanical damage, which will inevitably affect the surface of the gadget during daily use.



The protective film for the tablet allows you to save the most important thing on the tablet - the screen. It is this part of the device with which we read and perceive information that is the most fragile and fragile. Replacing the display glass is not a cheap procedure. Its cost often amounts to at least half of the total tablet price. Therefore, it is much easier to take measures that will help prevent damage. Moreover, sticking a protective film on the tablet is much cheaper than paying for expensive repairs. Special technologies help her protect the screen not only from scuffs, but also from scratches and chips.

The protective film for the tablet can be of different sizes and have additional advantages or features. The classic version is considered glossy, which completely covers the screen and protects it from external influences. But at the same time it is invisible. And besides, it fully preserves the brightness of colors and the susceptibility of the screen to control. The matte protective film for the tablet significantly reduces the amount of glare on the surface, but makes the colors more muted. The mirror surface is very popular among girls, which, when the display is off, turns the tablet into a full-fledged mirror. In addition, there are films with a reduced viewing angle, which will help keep the information displayed on the screen from the views of strangers. It is very useful in public transport or in a public place.



The protective film for the tablet (101) can be pasted in a special salon or directly upon purchase of the device. It is quite difficult to install the film on your own the first time. It is likely that several attempts are required. In this case, it is necessary to very carefully clean the display before installation and strictly follow the instructions on the package. After gluing, the film must be smoothed and the air bubbles formed. After that, the device will be ready for operation.
