Linevo Lake (Kurgan Region) - a great place for hunting and fishing

Linevo Lake is located in the Shumikhinsky district of the Kurgan region. To the north of its shores is the village of Travyanoe. This reservoir is one of the five largest reservoirs in the Shumikhinsky district.



Briefly about the lake

Lake Linevo (Kurgan region) is quite large. In length, it occupies 530 m, and the opposite banks are 320 m distant from each other. The depth can reach more than 10 m, however, the water is very clear, good visibility is preserved in places where the bottom is 2.5-3 away from the surface of the reservoir m

The bottom of the lake is covered with large sandstone, sometimes pebbles are found. On the coastline there are places that are subject to siltation. Geographically, the reservoir belongs to the Tobol River Basin.

Lake Linevo (Kurgan region, Shumikhinsky district) is fresh, drainless, but over time it gradually becomes brackish. It got its name thanks to the once large number of lineages of the cyprinid family living in it. Currently, this species is rare.



Fishing

Numerous fishing enthusiasts can be found on the lake year-round, as selective crucian carp lives in huge numbers here. The weight of some individuals reaches 1.2-1.3 kg, the average is 500-700 g. The populations in these waters of minnow are also numerous.

In winter, Lake Linevo (Kurgan Region), reviews and photo reports of fishermen confirm this information, does not lose its popularity. During this period, fishing is carried out from the ice away from the coast. And in the summer they fish mainly from boats, since the coastline is almost completely covered by reeds. On the surface of the water you can see water lilies and other equally beautiful plants.

Nature

Currently, Lake Linevo (Kurgan region) is located outside the modern river floodplain. The coastal terraces on the slope of the southern coast of the reservoir are rich in black alder forests with rare and endangered plant species. In 2001, the government of the Kurgan region decided to attribute this biocenosis to specially protected natural monuments. It prohibits any human activity that is not associated with sanitary and recreational activities, the care and protection of the forest from fires and pests. Agricultural land is adjacent to the lake from the west and north.



Animal world

In the forest near the lake there are many species of animals, such as elk, wild boar, capercaillie, black grouse and others. Various rodents are found, for example, field and forest mice, rats, as well as wolves, foxes and raccoon dogs. On the territory of the forest that surrounds Lake Linevo (Kurgan region), nesting birds are listed in the Red Book of the Kurgan region. This is a white-tailed eagle, as well as a black-headed laughter.

The popularity of the reservoir among fishermen and hunters

The lake constantly attracts a large number of people. Basically, these are fishermen, hunters and employees of research institutes. The latter are studying the area. In the autumn, a large number of hunters gather on the shore of the reservoir, ready to live in tents from a few days to several weeks in anticipation of the approach of northern birds migrating for the winter to the southern regions.
