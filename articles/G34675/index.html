Hotels in Volzhsky: overview, rating, addresses and photos of rooms

The town of Volzhsky is one of the main settlements of the Volgograd region, as well as a large industrial center of the Lower Volga region with a population of 314.4 thousand people.

The city is located on the coast of Akhtuba, 20 kilometers from Volgograd. According to the results of the All-Russian contest "The Most Equipped City of Russia", held in 2004, the town of Volzhsky was awarded the title of the greenest among settlements with a population of up to 500,000 inhabitants.

In Volzhsky there are many fascinating sights and monuments, most of which are dedicated to the first builders of the city. In the museum of local lore you can learn more about the history of the city, and in the old school (currently the art gallery) you can get acquainted with the work of local masters and architects.

For those who love outdoor activities, there are many ways to relax in Volzhsky: there are great opportunities for fishing on the Volga and Akhtuba. This article provides information about the best hotels in the Volga Volgograd region.
