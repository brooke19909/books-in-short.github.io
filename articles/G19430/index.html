What kind of food can a traveler count on?

Of course, good, tasty food is an important part of a truly comfortable stay. So what can the Konstantin Beach Hotel 3 * offer? Guest reviews suggest that you can really eat delicious food on site.

Even if you decide to refuse food at the hotel, there will be no problem. Numerous cafes and taverns are located nearby, where you can eat deliciously. On the menu you will find fish dishes, seafood and unsurpassed food prepared according to local recipes.
