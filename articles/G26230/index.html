Borjomi Palace Hotel: overview, features and reviews

In recent years, Russian tourists are increasingly choosing Georgia and its resorts for holidays. What attracts them primarily is the visa-free regime and the low cost of trips. In addition, the flight takes only a few hours, and the excursion program when visiting the country can always be combined with a wellness stay. The most famous resort city of Georgia is Borjomi, located in the south-west of the country. A lot of hotels are open here, including for wealthy tourists. If you want to improve your health and relax in the highest class, then pay attention to the Borjomi Palace sanatorium ("Borjomi Palace"). It is about this respectable hotel that we will describe in more detail in this article.

Hotel Details

First of all, Borjomi resort is famous for its mineral and mud springs, so tourists come here to improve their health and relax from the bustle of the city. Almost all first-class complexes here are simultaneously sanatoriums. The Borjomi Palace was no exception. It is located in the central part of the city in a picturesque area. A modern medical center has been opened on its territory.

The complex occupies a historic building, the construction of which was completed in the 70s of the XIX century. It is decorated in the classical Baroque style and its appearance is vaguely reminiscent of an ancient fortress towering on a hill. It is noteworthy that once members of the imperial family were resting in the building. The hotel has preserved the rooms in which they lived. They were completely restored, while maintaining their original historical appearance.

The hotel itself was opened in the building only in 2013, so do not think that it has an outdated and in need of repair rooms. In addition, on the territory there are two buildings built in 2014-2015. Borjomi Palace Hotel in Georgia offers its guests a total of 164 rooms, 42 of which are located in a historic building.

The staff in the sanatorium is fluent in not only English, but also Russian. The cost of accommodation, if desired, can include a set of medical services in the wellness center. The hotel accepts tourists with children of any age, but pets are not allowed here. As a rule, registration of guests starts from 14:00 local time.

Where is the sanatorium

In their reviews of the Borjomi Palace in Georgia, guests indicate that they liked the location of the complex. It is located in the central part of the city, located on a small hill, so some rooms offer amazing views of the surroundings. A lot of infrastructure facilities are open next to the hotel, including restaurants, cafes, bars and shops. 800 meters from the complex is the famous Mineral Water Park, where you can admire the natural beauty and enjoy the famous healing drinks that are produced in the city for free. The park is approximately 1 km from the hotel.

Borjomi is located in a mountain gorge, therefore, if desired, tourists can combine a healthy holiday with an active one. The nearest ski resort of Bakuriani can be reached in just half an hour.

There are no international airports near the resort, so tourists often fly to other large cities of Georgia, and then get to the Borjomi Palace Hotel on their own. The nearest of them is located near Kutaisi. The distance to it is more than 130 km. But it‚Äôs much easier to get to the resort from Tbilisi Airport. It is here that most of the low-cost flights from Moscow and other large cities of Russia arrive. It is almost 185 km from Borjomi. You can get from Tbilisi to the resort in a few hours by minibus, taxi or train. The train station in Borjomi is only 3.5 km away from the hotel.

Additional room facilities

Each room of the Borjomi Palace sanatorium in Georgia boasts not only its luxurious decoration, but also a modern set of amenities necessary for a comfortable stay. All apartments have large plasma televisions with satellite channels, an electric kettle, air conditioning, a wired telephone and a refrigerator where you can store food. Upon request, a minibar with soft and alcoholic drinks is also provided in the room. It is worth noting that they are not included in the cost of the rest and are paid separately. You can use the safe for free to store valuables, documents and money. All rooms have wireless high speed internet.

The bathroom is equipped with a large mirror so that guests can comfortably shave or apply makeup. Each guest is provided with an individual bathrobe and slippers, as well as a set of towels and cosmetics (shampoo, soap and shower gel). They are regularly replenished by the hotel staff. The bathroom also has a built-in hairdryer and clothes dryer. For a fee, tourists can order room service from the restaurant. At the same time, the apartment is serviced around the clock.

Infrastructure

Sanatorium Borjomi Palace can offer its guests a standard set of services. So, on its territory there is free parking, where any guest can leave their car. In addition, car rental can be arranged at the reception to, for example, explore Borjomi on your own. Tourists at the reception can exchange currency or leave valuables for the administrator in storage. The employee will put them in a separate safe where they will be protected. Around the clock professional guards work at the hotel.

For entrepreneurs and business people, the hotel has a modern conference room equipped with computer equipment. It accommodates up to 100 delegates. The hotel staff also organizes banquets and celebrations.

Catering

As a rule, the price of accommodation at the Borjomi Palace Hotel includes full daily breakfasts. However, if desired, guests can additionally pay full board, which also includes lunches and dinners. The main restaurant is located on the top floor of one of the buildings. Here, tourists can taste delicious dishes of European and Georgian cuisine. There is also a wine cellar on site. It serves homemade Georgian wines and other spirits. At the same time, you can not only try them, but also purchase several bottles to take home.

Medical center and other entertainment options

Hotel "Borjomi Palace" in Georgia is focused on recreation. It has a modern medical center and spa. They provide most of their services for a fee, but for long-term accommodation, a small set of wellness procedures is included in the price. In the medical center, tourists can make an appointment with a therapist, gastroenterologist, urologist, cardiologist and even a gynecologist. All doctors' offices are equipped with modern specialized equipment. The center has a 4D ultrasound machine for pregnant women. A full-fledged laboratory is also opened, where blood, urine and feces, bile and gastric juice are studied.

At the spa, guests can enjoy a course of wellness and beauty treatments that rejuvenate the face and body. During their conduct, specialists use local mineral water and mud, as well as infusions on medicinal herbs, honey and milk. The center houses electro-mud baths, a Finnish sauna and a Turkish bath.

In summer, tourists can relax and swim in the outdoor pool, filled with fresh water. Next to it is a small but cozy sun terrace with sun loungers. Tourists can play table tennis and hockey on site, and in the evening spend time in the billiards room.

Conditions for accommodating children

Tourists with young children are quite frequent guests at the Borjomi Palace Hotel, but a large number of additional amenities are not offered here. It is worth noting that there is no charge for all children under 6 years old. A baby cradle is also available in the room upon request, but their number is limited. The hotel has an outdoor playground and a children's room in one of the buildings. From time to time, evening activities for children are held in the evenings.

Positive feedback about the Borjomi Palace sanatorium

Tourists who have previously stayed at this hotel describe that they liked the quality of service. They believe that the cost of rest is fully justified by first-class service. Guests also indicate that when they visit Borjomi again, they will be happy to stay here again and will definitely recommend it to their relatives and friends.

According to guests, the hotel has a lot of advantages. We list the main ones:

  1. There is a restaurant with a terrace on the roof of one of the buildings, from where you can admire the panorama of the city.
  2. Tourists are offered a huge selection of breakfast food. They can try meat and fish dishes, fresh fruits and desserts, oxygen cocktails, coffee brewed in Turk.
  3. Often, guests who have paid breakfast only, the hotel administrator gives a free welcome dinner in the restaurant.
  4. The elevated windows overlook the nearby mountains and the Kourou River.
  5. Spacious rooms with new high-quality furniture and pleasant interiors. The staff regularly performs a thorough cleaning and changes of towels along with bed linen.
  6. Friendly receptionists working at reception. They will always help and give advice, in addition, all hotel staff are trying to quickly solve the problems of guests.

Negative reviews about the Borjomi Palace sanatorium in Georgia

Of course, like any other hotel, this complex has its drawbacks. Tourists do not always praise local services, sometimes they also point out its shortcomings. It is worth noting that most of them still seemed insignificant to the guests, so they did not spoil the impressions of the rest. But they believe that criticism will help hotel management improve service levels.

According to tourists who lived in the Borjomi Palace Hotel, the administration of the complex should eliminate the following disadvantages:

  1. The restaurant does not have a dietary and lean menu, and most of the dishes contain meat, fish and dairy products, so it is not suitable for eating vegetarians.
  2. Every day in the evenings, discos with loud music are held. Closer to night, they turn it off, but because of the noise, tourists will not be able to go to bed early.
  3. Some rooms have garbage in the drain, and noisy refrigerators are installed in the bedroom itself.
  4. Some of the apartments, according to tourists, need redecoration.
  5. The hotel has many smoking guests who smoke everywhere, and not in specially designated places.

This article provided a detailed overview of the Borjomi Palace Hotel in Georgia. Guest reviews were also reviewed above. Summarizing, we can name this complex - one of the best in the city. It has a prime location and is also housed in a historic building. Thanks to the modern medical center opened on its territory, you can combine visiting attractions with a wellness stay. The hotel can be recommended to families with children, the elderly and all other tourists looking for a place for a relaxing and respectable vacation.
