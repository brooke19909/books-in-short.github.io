Crimea, Peschanoe: reviews of tourists about the best pensions and leisure

Little Yalta - that's what the guests call this small resort town affectionately. It is very beautiful and crowded at the height of the season, which is why a bright association arises. Why is it worth discarding all other options and choosing Crimea (Sandy)? Reviews say that here is an ideal place for a person who is looking for peace, comfort and peace. Add to this the beauty of the Black Sea and Crimean steppe foothills.



Short description

Every Russian person should visit Crimea at least once in his life. Sandy reviews of tourists are often called the best spa resort. Here, nature itself heals. Around the resort town grow luxurious pine forests. The seascape is complemented by steep cliffs, especially beautiful in the early morning and evening, when the sun illuminates their slopes. In the evenings, the sea breeze brings the stunning smells of herbs and ripe fruits.

Accommodation for tourists

There are no problems with rental housing. A huge number of guest houses and small pensions makes it possible to choose the most suitable option for you. In recent years, interest in domestic resorts has faded a bit, more and more tourists prefer to travel abroad in the summer. We strongly recommend that you first visit the Crimea (Sandy). Reviews say that vacation on the Black Sea coast is no worse than in Turkey.

The owners of numerous boarding houses offer cozy cottages for living, a full board with an abundance of homemade cakes and fresh fruits, as well as an entertainment and sightseeing program, all for a reasonable price. We want to tell you about the best guest houses where you can stay when you arrive in Crimea (Peschanoe). Reviews from tourists will help you choose a truly worthy option.



Pension "Roman"

It is located almost in the center of the village and attracts tourists with wide beaches and comfortable rooms. The mini-boarding house is a three-story building of a modern building with rooms "economy" and "junior suite". The rooms provide everything you need for your relaxation. Each has modern furniture and a TV, air conditioning and a safe, a fridge and a bathroom. In the pension / complex there is a wonderful pool with waterfalls. Near them are sun loungers for relaxation.

First of all, Peschanoe (Crimea) attracts tourists with its pricing policy. Pensions, reviews of which are very good, improve the conditions every year and offer all new services. Economy class rooms in the Roman guest house with three meals a day cost 1,200 rubles per day for each tourist. Junior Suite is slightly more expensive - 1,500 rubles. And two-room suites - 1,600 rubles per day.



Hotel Laguna

This is a small but very comfortable family run hotel. If you have already been here, then surely your choice for the next year will still be the Laguna boarding house (Peschanoe, Crimea). Reviews say that this is an ideal place for a family vacation, especially if children are traveling with you. The hotel is located on the seashore. The most delicate sand on the beach, shallow warm sea - all this creates ideal conditions for a perfect vacation.

In total, 220 people can be accommodated here. New two-story buildings include rooms equipped with the latest plumbing and furniture, air conditioning. The cost of living in a standard room per day per person, including breakfast in the dining room, ranges from 600 to 1,200 rubles, depending on the season. The cost of individual rooms with sea views can reach 1,500 rubles.
