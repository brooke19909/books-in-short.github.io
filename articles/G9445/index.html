Smartphones Vertex: reviews, models, description and specifications

In 2010, VERTEX smartphones appeared on store shelves. Reviews of the new owners of such devices have focused on their advanced technical characteristics and high quality. At the same time, the cost of such devices is quite affordable. As a result, this brand dominates the Russian market among local cell phone manufacturers. It is his lineup that this review will be devoted to.

About Brand

As noted earlier, in 2010 sales of VERTEX smartphones began. Reviews primarily focused on their improved quality. The headquarters of this brand is located in St. Petersburg. But in the manufacture of devices, production facilities are used in China, and there is also a representative office there.

At the first stage, test samples are tested in the Middle Kingdom. VERTEX specialists check the devices for defects and give recommendations regarding changes in smartphones. Next, a second batch of test samples of mobile phones is manufactured, which is tested not only in China, but also at the main Vertex representative office in Russia. If no problems were identified, then the device becomes serial. Otherwise, the gadget is re-sent for revision and so on until a truly high-quality and reliable device is received.

Also, it should be noted separately that the moment that each digital device of this manufacturer has its own peculiarity. For example, the Impress Grip device has a degree of protection IP68 and can be immersed in water to a depth of 1.5 meters. But the Impress Saturn gadget, although it is a very low-cost device, is able to work with the most modern 4G cellular networks. It is this approach that allows this manufacturer to successfully compete with Chinese counterparts.

Device Impress Saturn

This is the most affordable VERTEX smartphone at the moment. His characteristics indicate that he has a low speed. This device is based on a quad-core chip with a nominal frequency of 1.3 GHz. In addition to this, the device is equipped with 1 GB of RAM and 8 GB of internal storage. The key feature of this mobile phone is the ability to work with 4G networks. If we add to this a very democratic cost of 3290 rubles, we get an affordable mobile device with high speed Internet access. At the same time, this model of a cell phone can work with any SIM card, and is not tied to any particular operator. It is these three advantages that its owners focus on:

  1. Low cost.
  2. High speed internet connection.
  3. Ability to connect to any cellular network.
