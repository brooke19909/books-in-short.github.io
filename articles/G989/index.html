The best hotels of Svetlogorsk: list, description, photo

What do you know about Svetlogorsk? If you are at a loss to answer this question, then this article is especially for you. At first glance, its location does not inspire much confidence, the cold Baltic Sea is not very welcoming. Of course, this is not Sochi, but it is Svetlogorsk that is distinguished by the longest warm days in the Baltic states. Every year a huge number of tourists (more than 120 thousand people) from Russia and nearby European countries have a rest here. The climate here is comfortable for treatment and relaxation. As a rule, the swimming season extends to July and August, although most often you can sunbathe and relax on the beach in spring and autumn. Svetlogorsk hotels never go without visitors, so today we want to tell you about the best of them, so you can book your seats in advance.

Rest in Svetlogorsk

We will deviate a little more from the main topic to briefly describe the resort itself, its features and advantages. As a resort, Svetlogorsk has been known for about 200 years. Over the years, excellent infrastructure has been created here. Tourists go not only to bask on the beach and improve their health, numerous attractions of this region attract visitors here. Svetlogorsk hotels provide excellent living conditions. Svetlogorsk is very interesting for its architecture; it has retained its German features. The city is very cozy and elegant, located along the sea coast. There is everything for a wonderful holiday, cafes and restaurants, discos and various attractions, there are water and sports centers. Of particular interest are excursions. Svetlogorsk hotels offer a large selection of different destinations in Kaliningrad and in the national park.

Pension "Wave"

Tourists come to this hospitable region not only to relax, but also in order to improve their health. Svetlogorsk Volna Hotel deserves special attention. This is a stunning complex that offers guests a huge number of options for a vacation. It is located on the seashore, only 35 km from Kaliningrad. It features a wellness center with a sauna and Turkish bath, with an equipped gym. Wellness programs have been developed here to cleanse the body and normalize weight, to treat the musculoskeletal system, as well as functional, urological problems. Hotel Rus

Based on reviews from tourists, this is the best hotel in Svetlogorsk. The European-style hotel is located in a picturesque forest corner of the city, 100 meters from the Baltic Sea. Despite the impressive size of the hotel, there is a homely atmosphere and comfort. Original interiors and excellent national cuisine, high professionalism of the staff - all this makes it possible to spend time pleasantly and without worries. Room rates - from 5700 to 8100 rubles per day.



Sanatorium "Amber Coast"

This is another wonderful hotel in Svetlogorsk (5 stars are assigned to it by tourists not for outward splendor and luxury, but for a wonderful vacation, and restoration of health). This is the largest recreation complex of the Kaliningrad seaside. It is located on the shores of the Baltic Sea, about 45 meters above its level. The resort is surrounded by a fabulous forest park. It is very convenient that the bedrooms are connected by warm passages with a hospital and a dining room, gymnastics and dance hall. Due to the unique climatic factors and the presence of a modern medical base, the resort receives guests for treatment all year round. Double two-room suite - 2620 rubles.

Hotel "Universal"

Very beautiful, cozy and comfortable hotel in Svetlogorsk on the beach. It is located in the center of the resort city. It was opened relatively recently, in 2001, in the green part of the city, just 100 meters from the sea. Guests are offered 33 cozy and homely comfortable rooms that are conducive to relaxation. Moreover, there are options for any, the most demanding tourist, ranging from small, one-room rooms for those who travel alone, and ending with two-story apartments. There is a fabulous restaurant and a charming patio where you can hide from the heat. "Universal" is built in the style of architecture of the old German city of Rauschen, its design does not leave anyone indifferent. The hotel meets all international standards.



Hotel Flamingo

Ideal for a quiet, family vacation. This hotel is located just 200 meters from the sandy shores of the Baltic Sea and a 5-minute walk from the train station. It offers free parking and a garden with all barbecue facilities. In the depths of the garden is the hotel itself, a small and very cozy building, made in delicate, creamy shades. The bright rooms are equipped with a TV and fridge, kettle and private bathrooms. Guests can enjoy Russian cuisine and enjoy the silence of sitting by the fireplace. And you can rent a boat and swim on the lake, which is located just 5 minutes from the hotel. Kaliningrad Airport is located 35 km from the hotel, you can order a transfer and not worry about delivery. The cost of living starts from 1600 rubles.



Hotel "Golden Bay"

Not all Svetlogorsk hotels in the Kaliningrad Region are expensive. You can find a beautiful hotel at affordable prices. For example, in this hotel the cost of living starts from 990 rubles.
