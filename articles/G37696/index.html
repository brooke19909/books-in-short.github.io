How to turn off your iPhone subscription: the best tips

Paid services on mobile devices are often troublesome. The user may not have been using them for a long time, and money will still be debited from the phone account. This is not the best phenomenon. Especially when it comes to the so-called subscription services. Some of them are free for the first time (a month or longer). And then they suddenly demand payment. Moreover - they independently write off funds for use. That is why many people think about how to disable the subscription to the "iPhone". What secrets will help bring the idea to life?



About shutdown methods

There are not many of them. And even a novice user of "apple" products should know about possible scenarios.

How to disable a paid subscription to the iPhone? You can do this:

  • Using iTunes on Windows
  • through MacOS;
  • by working directly with a mobile device.

In fact, everything is not so difficult. And if you follow some instructions, then even a person who does not understand anything on the iPhone will be able to refuse subscriptions.

Via iTunes

Let's start with the most common way. It's about working with IT. This application allows you to manage "apple" devices and work with their software.



How to disconnect subscription on "iPhone"? You can do this in approximately this way:

  1. Download and install iTunes on your computer. It is important that version 12.2.0 is installed.
  2. Launch the application.
  3. Connect to the internet.
  4. Log in with Apple ID.
  5. In the window that appears, scroll through the information almost to the very end. You need to stop at the "Settings" section.
  6. Select Manage next to Subscriptions.
  7. Click on the image of the desired subscription.
  8. Get acquainted with the parameters. Here you need to set the parameter "Off" opposite "Auto Renew". Or click on the "Unsubscribe" button.
  9. Click on Finish.

Just in the second case, it is possible that the latest version of iTunes will already be installed on your computer.

AppStore Subscriptions

Now a little about how to act if you have only a phone at hand. You can refuse paid subscriptions on iPhone without additional software. Simply having an internet connection is enough.



How to disable iPhone 6 subscriptions? For example, in the AppStore. Actions are reduced to the following steps:

  1. Launch a smartphone.
  2. Open the main menu and go to "Settings".
  3. Go to the "iTunes and AppStore" section.
  4. Click on your profile. If necessary, go through authorization in it.
  5. Connect to the internet. Better to do it in advance.
  6. Find and go to "Subscriptions".
  7. Click on the "Manage" button.
  8. Turn the switch to the "Off" position in the "Auto-renew" section.
  9. Save changes.

As a rule, this is how paid subscriptions in the AppStore are disabled. Choose a specific item, a person can refuse one or another paid service. But such an operation is best done using a computer.

Apple music

Often, owners of apple devices are interested in disabling Apple Music. This is a service that is somewhat reminiscent of Yandex.Music. You will have to pay for using a subscription.

How to disconnect subscriptions on "iPhone 5" or any other? Disabling Apple Music is best done through a mobile phone. This will require:

  1. Open the "Music" section in the gadget's main menu.
  2. Connect to Wi-Fi.
  3. In the upper left corner of the application, click on the image of your profile.
  4. Find and click on the line "View AppleID."
  5. Select "Subscriptions".
  6. Click on the "Manage" button.
  7. Mark the position of the pointer as "Off" in the "Auto-renewal" item.
  8. Click on the "Turn off" button.

Everything is extremely simple and clear. From now on, it‚Äôs clear how to disable the Yandex.Music subscription on the iPhone. And how can you refuse most other paid services too. But that is not all.



News opt-out

There is a paid service like Apple News. From him, too, sometimes I want to refuse. To do this, you have to act differently. How exactly? How do I disable an iPhone subscription called Apple News?

The action algorithm is proposed as follows:

  1. Go to the main menu of the device and click on "Settings".
  2. Visit the section "General", go to "Update content."
  3. Find the desired application.
  4. Set the slider to the "Off" position. In this case, a red light should light up nearby.

As a rule, nothing more is needed. These steps will help disable Apple News. If you wish, in the section "Updating content" you can stop the auto-update of some programs. But, as practice shows, usually this step is not needed.

Instagram and iPhone

Now it‚Äôs clear how to turn off the subscription to the iPhone in one case or another. The main thing is to have an Internet connection and AppleID. Without the latter, a person has no chance of using paid services.

Modern users quite often work with Instagram. How can I exclude many subscribers from the social network via iPhone? This is proposed using FollowMeter. To some extent, this task is also unsubscribing.

What to do? Necessary:

  1. Download and install FollowMeter for iPhone.
  2. Run the program.
  3. Log in using the username and password from the Instagram social network.
  4. Click on the Following button.
  5. Delete each unnecessary subscriber using the corresponding button.

Done! Just a few minutes - and the Instagram profile is cleared of unnecessary subscriptions. More precisely, from subscribers. There is nothing incomprehensible or difficult in this!
