What does HV-1 mean? Hotel category HV-1: What is Holiday Village?

Tourists, choosing a hotel, usually rely on three common variations of the separation of hotels. They are primarily interested in stardom. Travelers' attention is also attracted by the letter classification of hotels from A to D, characteristic of southern European countries. The stardom of a hotel affects its status, quality of service, price for services and composition of the public. The most comfortable atmosphere in 5 * hotels.

3 * hotels located in different states are very different. For example, apartments in Turkish and Egyptian hotels are quite spacious compared to rooms in European three-star establishments. In Asian countries, the hotel category is influenced by swimming pools and air conditioners, while in France such trifles as a bidet.

Club hotel - what is it?

With star hotels, everything is simple. The high cost and quality service sparkle hotels with the maximum number of stars. The information about Holiday Village hotels, designated in the tourist booklets as HV, has to be clarified. Institutions listed in the HV category are called club.





Under such hotel complexes there are closed park areas, including the main building and compact cottages for tourists. The infrastructure of the club hotels is built in such a way that they look like a tiny resort town with comfortable places for rest and living.

Tourist apartments are located in separate bungalows. The rooms of 1-2 storey houses are equipped with a separate exit to the street. The number of rooms individually in each cottage. Couples prefer to stay in double apartments with one extended room. Family tourists choose one-bedroom houses. Large companies are offered villas with many rooms.

The houses differ in prestige and cost. Standard rooms accommodate 2-4 people. They are equipped with a bathroom, air conditioning, TV and telephone. The suites have a balcony or terrace. The cottages produce daily cleaning with a mandatory change of bed linen.

Club Hotel Classification

Of the many ordinary hotels, they stand out with a special designation. There are three types of club complexes - HV, HV-1 and HV-2. By the level of service they are equated with European hotels 3 *, 4 * and 5 *, respectively.

Subtleties of HV Hotels

So what does HV 1 mean? Club hotels are assigned the category HV-1 (and vice versa, HV-1 hotels are classified as club). However, they have significant differences. Complexes having a vast territory adjacent to the sea coast are ranked as HV hotel clubs.

In the central zone of the towns are concentrated shops and restaurants, entertainment venues and establishments, places of recreation. The rest is the main building and many houses, which are apartments of various types.





Guests are offered water activities. Tourists gain vivid impressions, going down the water slides, riding catamarans, canoeing, surfing, swimming in the pools.

The hotel category HV 1 meets the same requirements. However, the prestige of the hotel-club HV is higher. In terms of quality of service, it is equivalent to a five-star hotel. The common thing among the hotel-clubs HV and HV 1 is to provide guests with free-standing cottages for their stay.

To support services at the proper level, to satisfy the interests of guests, such complexes contain a huge staff. The task - to ensure a comfortable stay - is attributed to cooks, animators, trainers, masseurs and other specialists. Therefore, rest in such towns costs a pretty penny.

Difference of HV-1 from HV-2

Hotel clubs have their own graduation. With the rise of digital designation, the quality of service decreases. Club-hotel status HV is assigned to tourist facilities with perfect after-sales service. Then category HV 1 - what is it? This includes complexes whose quality of service is not lower than in 3-4 * hotels.

HV-2 clubs are just like 2-3 star hotels. Their service is not perfect. And the beaches, although their own, do not adjoin the territory. They are a 5-10 minute walk from the town.





Club Hotel Services

Such complexes are equipped with everything that is interesting for tourists who prefer to relax by the sea - from multiple sports and entertainment centers to an endless series of shops and restaurants. Those services, the provision of which does not require the consumption of gasoline, the operation of expensive equipment (for example, for diving or parachuting), are provided to the guests of the club free of charge.

In all-inclusive restaurants, breakfast, lunch and dinner are served without requiring payment. But for excursions, drinks and snacks in bars, SPA-procedures and so on, you have to pay.

Club Hotel Benefits

Hotel HV 1 - what is it, what are its advantages? Guests find everything they need here. They provide a carefree vacation without going out of the resort town. Tourists can enjoy shopping, relaxing in bars and restaurants, leisure time at sports and playgrounds.
