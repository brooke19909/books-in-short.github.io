Sunny Days El Palacio in Egypt is a great place to relax

Sunny Days El Palacio is unique in Egypt. It has a shape reminiscent of the letter U. The hotel is located directly on the water, which provides panoramic views of the Red Sea and white sand from the windows of Sunny Days rooms. El Palacio in Egypt is the only hotel in Hurghada. on three sides surrounded by water.



Incredibly beautiful beaches will meet you with cleanliness and white sand. Service will please not only in the rooms. Each visitor is provided with a clean towel, umbrellas and sun loungers to visit the beach. And the longest coral reef in the region, located on the left side of Sunny Days El Palacio Hotel, is an incredibly convenient place for snorkeling. If you are a beginner, you can dive with an instructor in a group or in person. If not, then you just need to rent equipment.

It offers multilingual staff, including Russian. And although the hotel has only 4 stars, every month it successfully passes a safety and sanitary check.

The advantages of its location are not only that it is 99% located at sea, but also that it is only 3 km from the city center and 10 km from the airport.

Although you most likely will not have to leave Sunny Days El Palacio in Egypt (Hurghada). Near the hotel there is an alley with more than forty shops, a bank, a beauty salon, an Internet cafe, and a medical clinic. The following services can be obtained at the hotel: doctor's call, babysitter.

The comfortable rooms of the hotel have cable TV, air conditioning, international direct dial telephone, mini fridge, safe, bathroom, balcony or terrace with sea views, single or double beds.



The hotel has conference rooms, a diving center, Aqua Center, outdoor children's and adult pools, billiard tables, SPA center. There is a kids club at Sunny Days El Palacio in Egypt. A large number of events and competitions, gymnastics and DIY crafts - all this your children will be able to get from qualified specialists. Board games, darts, a visit to the pool and the construction of sand castles on the beach - this is what your child will be doing, and you can relax and devote time to yourself.





A pleasant surprise will be for hotel guests and a large selection of various dishes of Oriental and European cuisine. Restaurants and bars will not leave hungry even the most picky guest. Mirette Restaurant serves international cuisine, while Aldante Restaurant offers a wide selection of Italian dishes. Orca is a great seafood restaurant located on the seafront. The Lobby Bar, pool bar, beach bars and Terrace Bar offer you a well-selected variety of cold and hot drinks. There is a hotel and a service - room service.
