Fire warning system. What is a voice alert system?

The fire warning system is one of the components of the fire protection equipment. According to the fire safety standards of the Russian Federation, any building must be equipped with such a system.

Definition



Fire warning system - a set of organizational measures aimed at quickly alerting people about a possible danger, as well as the likely escape routes from the building. Such a system is installed along with a fire alarm. Most of them are equipped with devices to improve sound quality and emergency broadcasts. You can send the necessary notice using any of the internal telephones. To do this, just dial the special code number. Sometimes in large enterprises this system is used to search for a specific employee.

Types

There are five types of these systems, and their choice depends on the regulatory framework and, secondly, on the wishes of the customer.

  1. Sound alert. This is a tinted signal, an alarm siren, etc.

The sound warning system is the most reliable and convenient way to inform workers about a fire or other emergency, send special news or even congratulations.

Component warning systems

The simplest warning and evacuation control system includes: a microphone, a loudspeaker (one or several), an amplifier. In more complex ones, zone selection modules, mixing modules, and other blocks may additionally be included, based on the project and technical specifications.

Speaker Types

When choosing a specific loudspeaker, the warning and evacuation systems should be based on the conditions of its future work: in which place it will be used, on the street or indoors, only messages or also musical compositions will be broadcast, what acoustic characteristics the room where the installation is planned has.



Today, manufacturers are ready to offer potential buyers loudspeakers of various shapes, sizes and capacities. All this abundance needs to be sorted out somehow. The easiest way to divide them into main types:

  1. Horn-type loudspeakers. They are used in open areas and in production. These loudspeakers are characterized by high sound pressure. Used only for the transmission of announcements due to the narrow range of reproduced frequencies. They differ from ordinary loudspeakers in that they have a much smaller angle of emission of sound waves. Mounted on poles, towers, poles, building facades. They are used in production shops and other similar sites where the noise level is high.
  2. For installation in open areas. Dust-proof loudspeakers capable of operating in a wide temperature range are used at such sites. It is these parameters that should be given special attention when choosing this type. The degree of dust and moisture protection should be equal to IP-34. Temperature range -25 ¬∞ - + 40 ¬∞ . Such requirements are maintained by loudspeakers with cases made of heat-resistant plastic or metal. The mount allows you to change the orientation in one of two planes.
  3. Pendant loudspeakers. This type is widely used in rooms with high ceilings. Their design allows installation at the required
    height, which ensures uniform sound coverage of the premises of the most complex configuration. Such devices are used in trading floors with uneven or simply high ceilings. So that they organically fit into the design of the room, they are installed on the same level with lighting fixtures. This makes the speakers virtually invisible, but perfectly audible. They are attached to the elements of metal structures or beams.
  4. For installation in the wall. This type is not as popular as wall-mounted or suspended-ceiling speakers. This is due to difficulties in installation work. So, in a brick wall you need to prepare a niche, and not each of them has the necessary thickness so that this niche has sufficient depth. Installation in drywall is carried out using springs, which are located in a pre-prepared hole.
  5. For installation in a false ceiling. Widely used, suitable for rooms with any configuration. The main condition is the presence of a false ceiling. During the design process, particular attention should be paid to the overall dimensions of the speakers and their height, which should not exceed the distance between the main and suspended ceiling. Such devices are mounted using springs that secure them in the hole prepared in the ceiling.
  6. For wall mounting. Very popular, due to the ease of installation and compatibility with various types of interior. Mounted with dowels, which are attached to the walls.
  7. Special loudspeakers (intrinsically safe, explosion-proof, etc.). The voice warning system in the mines is equipped with this type. As well as in industrial premises and in open areas where explosive mixtures can or may form. This type has the highest security, but its cost is quite high.

Frequency range

The range of reproducible frequencies allows you to divide all the speakers into broadband and narrowband. The former have good sound quality and rather high cost. They are used where, in addition to voice messages, it is necessary to broadcast music programs. These are sports complexes, shopping and entertainment centers, supermarkets. The voice notification system, as a rule, is composed of devices of the second type. The main objective of such audio devices is to transmit only voice messages. These loudspeakers successfully combine sound of average quality and rather low cost.

Alert Certification

Both foreign manufacturers and domestic should certify their products of this type without fail for compliance with the fire safety standards of the Russian Federation. Verification of warning systems includes performance tests in various operating modes, electromagnetic compatibility, mean time between failures, resistance to ignition, etc. It certifies the Central Administration of Central Administration of Central Administrative Offices of the Central Administrative District of the Ministry of Internal Affairs (Certification Authority for Security Alarm Equipment of the Russian Federation).

Design



The general procedure for drafting warning systems is established by fire regulations. The project should provide for the possibility of integrating the civil defense warning system and this system. Both of them must be switched on at the command impulse that the fire alarm generates.

Dividing the building into zones, the designer should develop a special order of notification for employees who are at the enterprise. The sizes of such zones, the order of notification and the time of its start are determined using the conditions of safe evacuation of workers in a fire hazard situation.

Notification system. Mounting

Installation of voice annunciators in protected areas should be carried out in such a way as to exclude uneven sound and concentration of sound waves. These devices should not have volume controls, and they must be connected to the power grid without plug-in devices.



The fire warning system should work as long as people need to leave the danger zones or building.

Sound signals of the warning system in their tonality should differ from sounds of other purposes.

The fire warning system must be controlled from a control room (fire station) or other premises that meets fire safety standards and is approved in due order.

How to arrange emergency messages

For broadcasting such messages, it is better to use a pre-prepared recording, since the dispatcher's unstable speech can only increase the panic of people in a critical situation. In addition, psychologists recommend using a calm female voice, as its spectrum is in good agreement with the frequency range that is important for perception. The text of the message should sound neutral and suppress panic, contain information about the evacuation routes from the building. To attract attention, you can send a tone at the beginning and at the end of the message.
