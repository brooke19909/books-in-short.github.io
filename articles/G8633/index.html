Hemstitch

This is a very old and recently fashionable type of embroidery. It is performed on fabric carefully prepared in a special way. In the place where the embroidery will be located, cross threads are removed, the number of which depends on the planned width. After that, work is carried out by one of the types of patterns. And so it turns out. How to do it - we will consider further.

It is not difficult to carry out a trace, for it does not need any special devices and materials. But at the same time, this type of needlework requires utmost care and accuracy, otherwise the product with embroidery will look ugly.

This method is most often used when decorating products from cotton and linen fabrics. These are all kinds of tablecloths, blouses, bedding, kitchen towels and curtains. On any of these products, the hemlock will look very impressive. How to do it? Will learn.

  • Tassels. We extend the needle with the working thread through 3-5 main threads and tighten them with a loop. Similarly, we sew stitches to the end of the row.

  • Merezhka splinter. Consists of their brushes, made in a checkerboard pattern.

Skilled embroiderers add beads to the patterns, and several more variations are obtained. It is also possible to tape with ribbons. This pattern turns out to be voluminous, and if you choose the right color for the ribbon, the embroidery will look very impressive.

On a napkin

At the next stage of acquaintance with this type of embroidery, we will consider how to make a hem on a napkin.

Along the edge of the napkin, leaving room for a hem, pull out eight to ten threads from the fabric. Let‚Äôs go through all the edges in this way. Now do the hem. Next, you can begin to embroider. Sew always from left to right.

We fasten the thread by making several stitches. We collect from 4 to 8 threads on the needle (optional). The needle goes from right to left horizontally. Then we stick the needle from the wrong side and tighten the working thread to form a brush. Thus, go along the entire length of the napkin. It must be remembered that the brushes should be the same thickness, so you need to carefully consider the number of threads. Also pay attention to the passage of the corners so that they are neat.

Interesting hobby

You can decorate a tablecloth or bedding, a festive blouse or a ribbon on a hat with a cordon. In any case, using the basic techniques, you can experiment and apply different combinations of colors and patterns.

Slow painstaking work will allow you to relax, and the end result will leave a feeling of satisfaction in the soul.
