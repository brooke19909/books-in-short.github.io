Actual cape: pattern, recommendations, manufacturing tips

Fashion is changeable. Modern fashion trends do not stand still and fashion designers are on the alert, offering more and more new options for various elements of clothing - one is more beautiful than the other. For several autumn and winter seasons, various cloaks and vests have not left the catwalks.





Fashion trends

Fashion designers offer beautiful young girls, as well as ladies of a more serious age, in order not to chill into a dank autumn gloom, put on their shoulders, no, not an Orenburg down scarf, but a spectacular English-style cape-coat, the so-called cape.

This amazing version of demi-season outerwear combines lightness and functionality. Having such an element in the wardrobe, it is very easy to look amazing at any important moment. Cape goes well with strict trousers and a pencil skirt, as well as with ripped jeans. It can be supplemented with classic boats or super-current slip-ons or sneakers, uggs, high-heeled boots or flat shoes. In general, a miracle, not a coat!





Fashion Tips

How to keep up with new fashion trends and always look one hundred percent and still not be left with empty pockets? Of course, you can once again visit a fashion boutique and purchase a coveted new thing. But what if there was no suitable model or color (size) available? But what if there is simply no financial possibility, and you do not want to lag behind modern fashion trends?

There is one absolutely win-win option, to make a cape with your own hands. Patterns and recommendations for making the simplest and most spectacular models can be found in this article. This option is suitable not only for craftswomen who have skills in cutting and sewing.

You can entrust the tailoring of a fashionable little thing to the craftswomen from the studio, which today are enough in any self-respecting city. Those who know a lot about sewing tricks of a seamstress will also help with choosing the right fabric and the necessary accessories and advise which model to prefer, taking into account the peculiarities of the figure and other trifles. So, the decision was made, the studio was found, it remains to choose the appropriate cape pattern and go!

Actual Cape: Design Suggestions

For starters, it's worth being inspired by design decisions. These creations will help in choosing colors and proportions. In the article we offer only a few striking examples of these cozy capes, by the way, they can be insulated and even fur.





With appropriate skills, you can make cape patterns yourself or use the tips that are given in this article.

Cape Production Tips

So that the new thing does not look like a cloak of the Indians from South America, and does not dangle in a hoodie, but evokes romantic associations with foggy albion, brings back memories of the famous detective from Baker Street, we offer several recommendations for making a coat-cap with your own hands.

In its simplest design, a cape is just a rectangle of fabric stitched along the sides and with openings for the head and hands. A woolen fabric in a large geometric pattern, such as a large cage, will look very stylish.

If you choose the right pattern, the cape favorably emphasizes the dignity of any figure. Even ladies with curvaceous should not be afraid of him. Such a fair sex is suitable for a direct not too flared model. Slender girls should wear capes with a belt, this will highlight a narrow waist.

A floor-length cape cape is a truly stunning option that is indispensable for going out. Its owner will be the real star of the evening. For the everyday option, it is better to prefer shorter models. It is much more convenient and will allow you to move even on a bicycle.

Another cape can easily replace home clothes. A cloak made of terry cloth or soft knitwear will successfully replace oversized robes and pajamas.

Cape Coat Pattern





Another elementary, but very convenient little thing. Even a novice seamstress can make such a one. The pattern of the cape is so simple that even girls can make a new thing for their dolls, using it.

The sizes are universal, but they can be revised and slightly changed, since this will not be a big deal.

It will be quite simple if the fabric selected for such a coat does not require fringing. Otherwise, you need to overlay the edge or bend the fabric. It is also possible to do it yourself or you can turn to women workers for tailoring for help.





Such a cape-cape can be used with a belt that can be made from the same fabric or replaced with a finished belt made of leather or other material.

Using the proposed models, patterns and recommendations, no lady will definitely not go unnoticed this fall. After all, a fashionable cape coat this season will help create an exquisite look, and if it is made independently, then its originality is ensured.
