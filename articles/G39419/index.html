Cyprus hotels with a water park: where can I stay with children?

Many tourists believe that the water element should be an integral part of the holiday, so they usually choose Cyprus hotels with a water park. It is convenient in all aspects, especially for those who have not learned to swim (or swim badly) or are going to relax with their children. Kids should always be supervised by adults. However, is it possible to call parents staying at sea a vacation if they, like Cerberus, should not take their eyes off the peanuts to avoid an accident? Such a vacation is unlikely to please anyone.

Another thing is the water park. Shallow pools are usually located here, a lifeguard is constantly on duty near the descent from the hills, and there are many other vacationers nearby. Parents, leaving under the supervision of the baby, can go for a swim in the pool "Artificial Wave", "Lazy River" and other similar plan of entertainment attractions. So what are the Cyprus hotels with a water park? Further about the most popular establishments of the island.





Panthea holiday

This hotel is located in Ayia Napa. Located in one of the quietest areas of the city, in addition to its own beach, it also has water attractions. If we consider all the hotels in Cyprus with a water park for children, then this complex is the most popular in terms of popularity. And all because the use of the water park is completely free for those who book a room in this hotel. But outside tourists can stay here, who live in other hotels, only for them the entrance is paid.

In addition to a pool with an artificial wave and eight water slides, there is also a pool for children with slides. At each facility, a lifeguard is on duty, sometimes not one. Nearby is a tennis court and a bar. If you need to leave your child, paid babysitting services are provided.

Anastasia Hotel Apartments

When listing Cyprus hotels with a water park, it is worth mentioning the Anastasia Hotel Apartments, located in the resort of Protaras. The water park is located in the backyard of the establishment. Among the eight slides there are also designed specifically for children. In addition, there are two outdoor and as many indoor pools. Children's water bowl is comfortable and safe. In it, kids can splash around as much as they like. If parents want to spend this time on adult rides, the nanny will look after the child for an agreed time.

The park runs on schedule, which must be taken into account when planning your vacation. In addition, in bad weather, open rides close to avoid unpleasant events. If the water park does not work, you can go to the beach. There are two of them in the city - Sunrise and Konnos.





Aqua sol

The hotel is located in the western part of Paphos, which is considered the most popular and attractive among tourists. In the water park of the institution there is a pool with a lot of children's entertainment and 4 slides. Really like a vacation "Lazy River" and aqua aerobics. In addition, the hotel has done everything for a fun and unforgettable vacation for children: animators conduct entertaining shows that little guests are looking forward to. At a children's disco there is a fire show, Cypriot dances to live music. Only hotel guests can rest here, as the administration is trying to ensure a free and safe vacation for its customers.





Adams beach

Considering hotels in Cyprus with a water park and slides, one can not help but mention the five-star Adams Beach hotel from Ayia Napa. Of course, the water park here is not very large, but it has everything for a good rest for both kids and adult hotel guests. The complex has three pools, one of which is created in the form of a river with a calm course.

The only drawback is that the slides only work for a certain time. But this is not at all scary. After the park closes, you can go to the sea: the institution, like some other hotels in Cyprus with a water park, is located on Nissi Beach - the most famous holiday destination on the island. It is mentioned in all booklets about Cyprus, therefore it has excellent infrastructure.
