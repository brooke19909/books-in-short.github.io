The magnificent peninsula of Halkidiki: reviews and facts

Amazing in beauty, the Halkidiki peninsula , reviews of which are literally sparkled with admiration, fell in love with many tourists. Sandy shores, the smell of needles and tar, dense greenery and the bright blue sea captivate with its splendor. Greece has been attracting people from all over the world for unsurpassed beauty and indescribable landscapes. Halkidiki is perhaps one of the most famous and popular places in this country.

A bit of history



It is located on the coast of the Aegean Sea, and many note the similarities between the trident of Poseidon and the peninsula of Halkidiki. Reviews of some tourists are definitely imbued with the spirit of ancient Hellas after a trip to Greece. According to legend, two titans - Athos and Seaton - could not share the mermaid. And then the angry Poseidon resolved their argument by throwing a spear at the peninsula, thereby giving it an unusual shape. Initially, the name Halkidiki was used to designate its capital. Even during the reign of Philip III, the father of the great Alexander the Great , the Athenians subjugated 30 cities of this peninsula. This territory was very attractive for its deposits of metals and the richness of wood necessary for the construction of ships. During the Balkan Wars, this peninsula played a significant role in liberation from the Turkish yoke.

Popular places of Halkidiki



Reviews about this place will not let you lie: Halkidiki is one of the most popular and largest places in Greece. Beautiful and comfortable hotel complexes, amazing beaches and mirrored clear water of the Aegean Sea attract tourists from all over the world. The map of Halkidiki is quite small, but very diverse. The most popular resort is Kassandra. This cape is considered to be a paradise due to its magnificent bays and picturesque beaches. Not surprisingly, due to such landscapes, most hotels are located here. In addition to a wealth of images and a riot of colors, Kassandra has its own unique history. Perfectly combining antique places with modern urban surroundings, the resort becomes so attractive for tourists.

In addition to this town, many take a closer look at Sithonia - the middle peninsula of Halkidiki. Reviews of most tourists are full of delight and amazement from the green virgin forests and snow-white sandy beaches. Numerous harbors, bays and olive groves will not leave anyone indifferent. Well, speaking of the Halkidiki peninsula, one cannot but mention St. Athos. For the Orthodox of the whole world, this mountain is considered one of the main places on Earth. Since ancient times, this area was considered holy. Many temples and shrines were erected here. Athos is an independent monastic state in which women are forbidden to come. It is believed that they can lead monks into temptation.

In general, Greece is a wonderful place to relax. It is suitable for both romantic and family trips. And the famous Halkidiki peninsula should be visited at least once in a lifetime by everyone in order to fully imbue the spirit of Greek antiquity.
