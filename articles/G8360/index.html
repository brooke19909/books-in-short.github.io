Kemal Bay Hotel 5, Konakli, Turkey

Description: Eight kilometers from the Turkish city of Alanya, in the vicinity of the resort village called Konakli, there is a good tourist oasis. There, right on the Mediterranean coast, is the luxurious Kemal Bay Hotel 5. The photos of its two red buildings - the main six-story and the second, three-story - look very impressive after the renovation carried out in 2008. The buildings are surrounded by a huge garden - 80 thousand square meters. There is a bus stop right at the gate of the hotel , thanks to which you can reach the center of Alanya in seven minutes. To the air port of Antalya - about one hundred and twenty kilometers.





Kemal Bay Hotel 5 consists of: 255 rooms with carpeted floors, 5 of which can accommodate people who find it difficult to move around without assistance. The rooms are designed for two guests, equipped with a balcony. They have a TV with satellite channels (there are five in Russian among them), a minibar with free drinking water, a climate control system, a safe, and a private bathroom with a shower and a bathtub, a hairdryer and toiletries. Maids clean the premises every day, change clothes several times a week.

Meals: The hotel accepts vacationers according to the Super All Inclusive system. The Kemal Bay Hotel 5 restaurant is capable of hosting up to eight hundred visitors at a time. It has an open (on the terrace, under umbrellas) and covered sections. A variety of international and Turkish dishes are served daily . Lots of pastries, fruits and desserts. Five bars (including on the beach and poolside) offer a wide range of juices, non-alcoholic and alcoholic cocktails, beer, wine.

Beach: The coast here is sandy, without

Amney. The hotel is on the front page, so the distance to the sea from the buildings is only a few tens of meters. The bottom is sandy, without stones and a hollow sinks, which is very important for children. Well, one who loves to dive right into the depths can go down the steps from the pier. On the coast, platform and pools there are many sunbeds with mattresses under umbrellas. All this is free, but beach towels are secured. Two pools - indoor and outdoor - are filled with clean and fresh water. Garden area Kemal Bay Hotel 5 shares with the neighboring five-star hotel Green Hill Bay, which owns a water park with slides. Guests of both hotels have the right to ride them for free.

Additional information: This hotel is preferred by Russian tourists, which has become a kind of tradition. This situation is very convenient for those who do not speak foreign languages, because there is always a Russian-speaking staff at the customer service desk (which, by the way, works around the clock). Even the maids and waiters in the Kemal Bay Hotel 5 restaurant will quickly fulfill your every request. There is also an excellent Turkish textile store, a hairdresser, a laundry, a rental of equipment for water entertainment, and a diving school on the shore. Near the reception there is a room where you can leave luggage for storage at late check-out. The hotel cordially invites you to visit the SPA center (massage room, jacuzzi, hammam and sauna) and a gym with exercise machines. Tennis enthusiasts can exercise on the court. For children, in addition to the shallow pool

and playgrounds, a mini club.
