Features of the shopping center "Ocean Plaza"

The first thing that catches your eye when approaching the mall is its unusual architectural form. As if all the newest, most unusual ideas were subordinated to a single idea. She expressed herself in the name of the center and the decorative design of the interior.

The building has four floors. At the first, we will see the long shelves of the Auchan megastore, bank branches, a technology supermarket and much more. On the second and other floors - various shops of clothes, shoes, accessories. Everything you can imagine is in the center of Ocean Plaza! Kiev is going here to relax in the process of active shopping - all in the new traditions of the big city!





What you should definitely see

Over the weekend, the Ocean Plaza gathers many hundreds of visitors under its roof. Someone needs to be stingy, someone just came here with his family to relax. In addition to shopping, cafes and entertainment for children (3rd floor), there is one extraordinary installation in the Plaza. This is a giant, wonderfully equipped aquarium with exotic fish of different sizes floating in it.





The sight before the visitors of the center is bewitching. Many, which can be seen from the views, see for the first time live fish of this size. Napoleon fish, schools of small piranhas, four sharks (!) - this is far from all the most beautiful representatives of the water world represented here. The fish, swimming around their oversighters, go about their business, showing one side or the other of a fantastic color.

In addition to cafes, restaurants, cinema and dozens of stores, the Ocean Plaza shopping center (Kiev) is definitely worth a visit to this aquarium! Is there any need to say how delighted the children are with such a living corner?
