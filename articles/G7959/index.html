A little information



Those who come here for the first time should at least superficially learn the history of the resort. In ancient times, it was called differently - Fiskos. It was here that Sultan Suleiman the Magnificent moved when he was preparing for his campaign in Greek Rhodes. For some time the city was called by his name. Many sights are associated with this era in the history of Marmaris. Among the architectural monuments that have been preserved at the moment, it should be noted the Castle built in 1521, or Kervansaray.

In general, both in Marmaris itself and in its environs there are many amazing objects with a wonderful history. Most of them do not even know those who have come here several times to rest. The narrow cobbled streets of this city, the fortress built in the sixteenth century, the tomb of the famous Sarian, etc. - all this attracts those who are already tired of the beaches.

Marmaris today

Currently, this Turkish resort, affectionately washed by the Aegean and Mediterranean seas, the mountain slopes of which are covered with coniferous forests, is very popular among Russian tourists. People who choose an active and fun vacation to spend unforgettable time in Marmaris, a city where nightlife is in full swing, often come here. In addition, it is very convenient from here to make unique boat trips not only along the coast of Turkey, but also to Rhodes or the Turtle Islands.



Marmaris, whose coastal map shows that its sandy and pebble beaches stretch across the winding coast with bays and bays, is also the place where sailing competitions are held annually in the eastern Mediterranean.

general information



Choosing this hotel to stay, tourists should be clearly aware that they stay in an inexpensive and unpretentious place. Therefore, it is not worth waiting for the service that five-star hotels provide for an amount several times higher than the cost of a budget hotel. Intermar Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) is located at a distance of one hundred and fifty meters from the sea. It was built in 1990. And since then, in the high season, it is practically not empty. The last major overhaul was carried out three years ago.

The hotel area is two and a half thousand square meters. She is always clean and comfortable. The hotel staff communicates with clients in English and German, but a Russian-speaking administrator works for our compatriots. We accept Visa or Master Card.

The hotel has two elevators, laundry, TV room. Vacationers can use the laundry, ATMs, currency exchange, beauty salon. There is also a small conference room. At the always open check-in counter, you can arrange such a service as a transfer to the airport, the distance to which is about one hundred kilometers. Here you can also order a taxi, rent a vehicle, use a depository safe or a left-luggage office. For these services you need to pay extra.

Number of rooms

Intermar Hotel 3 * consists of one five-story building. It has ninety-eight standard rooms for a maximum of 3 adults at a time. Despite the fact that they are quite small in size, they have everything for living: a combined bathroom with shower, controlled air conditioning, satellite TV, comfortable furniture. There are also balconies or terraces. For an additional fee, hotel staff will show how to use the built-in safe, which fits a laptop, as well as connect to the Internet. Maids clean their rooms and change their bed linen regularly and unobtrusively: when residents leave. The rooms also have a paid minibar: upon arrival, a bottle of water is free. A hairdryer is also provided. There is non-slip tile on the floor.

Population



As in other hotels, Intermar Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) has a checkout time. As a rule, the settlement of visitors is carried out from two in the afternoon. On the day of departure, clients must leave rooms before noon. Those arriving at the hotel earlier than the set time, subject to availability of keys, are provided with keys immediately. Otherwise, customers will need to wait for the previous guests to leave the reserved room, which will immediately be brought into proper form. On the day of departure, you must pay not only for accommodation, but also for all additional services that were provided. You can pay in cash and plastic cards of payment systems.

Nutrition

In Intermar Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Marmaris) it is organized according to the all-inclusive system already known to tourists, when buffet tables are served three times a day in the main restaurant. Sea

Since Intermar Hotel 3 * is a small three-star hotel, it does not have its own beach. Vacationers can use the urban swimming area, which is one hundred and fifty meters from the hotel. The path to the beach passes through the motorway. For safety, pedestrians are encouraged to cross over to the sea through an underpass.
