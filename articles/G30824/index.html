National Park "Zavidovo" (Moscow and Tver regions): description, attractions

The national park includes 90 settlements, as well as a scientific and experimental farm. Just 150 kilometers from it is Moscow. Zavidovo is a village in the Tver region, but the park is also located in the Moscow region. In general, the complex occupies 5 administrative districts. But not in vain it is called Zavidovo. The Tver region occupies a large part of it - 54%, while the Moscow region - only 46%. Its plain territory includes several rivers, including the Yauza, Lama, Shosh and others.

Care for wildlife

This does not interfere with the main goal of creating the park - environmental protection. Its fauna and flora are protected. The local forests are home to a large number of valuable animals. This elk and hare, fox and wild boar, weasel, lynx, ermine, as well as a brown bear and roe deer. Beavers, a raccoon dog, deer and sika deer were specially introduced. There are many birds on the territory. Among them, capercaillie, partridge, hazel grouse, black grouse. The Upper Volga lowland, on which the Zavidovo National Park is located, has many ponds, wetlands, lakes, so there are waterfowl here : crested black, wide-nosed, crackler, googol, mallard and teal, as well as a featherworm and red-headed duck. Animals periodically move to neighboring forestries, and then come back.

The forests here are mostly pine, but also mixed. The northern part is a wasteland, swamps and fields. Those who wish to relax in an ecologically clean place can visit Zavidovo, a complex located near the village of Shoshi on the banks of the Volga. Relax in full

The complex has everything in order to have a great free time. Everyone will find something to their liking here. In summer you can sunbathe and swim on the beaches, in winter ride snowmobiles. Poet's house

The village of Zavidovo (Tver region) has many places worth visiting. For example, the house-museum of the poet Spiridon Drozhzhin. He lived in it from 1896 to 1930. The museum was opened in 1938. The exposition is a personal thing of the poet, manuscripts, books. The house-museum was damaged during the Great Patriotic War, but was restored, as well as the collection presented in it. The museum is visited by connoisseurs of creativity Drozhzhina and lovers of poetry in general. In the exhibition hall, meetings, screenings of educational and educational films, lectures for children are held.

Good cross

Another attraction is the Holy Cross Chapel, located in the center of the village. Inside it, in the 17th century, a cross was established, which possesses gracious power. The chapel was established later, and in the 20th century it was reconstructed. A two-meter oak cross stands on a stone foundation and attracts believers from all over the country. Therefore, the doors of the chapel are always open.
