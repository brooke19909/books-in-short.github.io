Rest in the Rostov region on the Sea of ​​Azov - tips for tourists

Rest in the Rostov region on the Sea of ​​Azov will be interesting and fascinating. The water in this place warms up in the morning with the appearance of the first sunlight. The depth does not exceed 15 meters, and that is why the sea is so warm. It beckons to itself with its blue color. Huge beach areas strewn with sand will appeal to every tourist. The air in these places is fresh, unpolluted.

Rostov, Sea of ​​Azov

The distance that residents of Rostov-on-Don must travel in order to get to the coast is approximately 100-300 km. The range depends on the selected location. The largest cities that are located on the seashore in this area are Taganrog and Azov. In the summer season, buses run regularly on these routes. You can also use the train service. And for those who like to travel in comfort, taxi drivers offer their services.

It is worth paying tourists' attention to one fact: it is advisable to book accommodation in advance, since holidays in the Rostov region on the Sea of ​​Azov are very popular. So where exactly can you spend your summer vacation?



Advantages of the Sea of ​​Azov

Water in this water area has healing power - it contains 92 elements from the well-known periodic table. That is why on the coast of the Sea of ​​Azov there are many sanatoriums, hotels, hotel complexes, recreation centers, camps and dispensaries. Such water significantly improves immunity and cures a considerable number of various diseases.

On very hot days, the sea temperature reaches +30 ° C. Due to the fact that the depth begins to increase only from about 20 meters from the beach, this place is considered a paradise for families with children. The only moment that may upset tourists who arrived in August is the flowering of water. At this time, the sea somewhat loses its appeal.

Taganrog Bay

If we calculate the distance from the center of the water area, then the route Rostov-on-Don - Sea of ​​Azov will be more than 250 km (directly), and if you get along the coast by road, this figure increases to 350 km. Residents of this area are most profitable to relax in the Taganrog Bay. It is located only 70 km from the city of Azov, and 100 km from Rostov itself. The main feature of the bay is depth: it is shallow on one side of the spit and deep on the other. The area is well developed infrastructure. There are recreation centers, children's camps. The level of services offered is quite high.

Taganrog Bay is the largest part of the Sea of ​​Azov. Its length is 140 km. The bay is located in the northeast of the water area. The swimming season opens in early June, and sometimes in May. In the middle of summer, the water warms up to +28 ° . Salinity in the bay is lower than in the sea itself. This is explained by many large rivers that flow into the water area. This is Don, Kalmius and others.



Taganrog

For those who appreciate peace and quiet, a vacation in the Rostov region on the Sea of ​​Azov is perfect. Everyone who arrives in Taganrog will be personally convinced of this.

The city has a lot of beaches. All of them are quite well equipped. For example, you can visit Primorsky, Central or Sunny. Sunbeds and umbrellas are provided for sunbathers on the beaches, and various attractions for children. On the territory there are small shops and cafes where you can buy food or have a snack.

Another important advantage of a vacation in Taganrog are adequate prices. When compared with the Black Sea, the costs will be less than about half.

Rest in Petrushino

For those who want to inexpensively, but fully spend a summer vacation, it is proposed to visit the village of Petrushino. There is a large sandy beach. It is equipped with changing cabins, a shower. Adults can rent a jet ski, or enjoy a banana ride. Children will not be bored either, because there is a slide on the beach. As a rule, it is crowded only on weekends and in the evening. Locals provide tourists with rental rooms and houses. In Taganrog every 30 minutes. the bus rides.

Family holiday

The Sea of ​​Azov is ideal for a family vacation. On the coast of the Taganrog Bay, you can relax in the following places.

  • Pavlo-Ochakov Spit. OK "Breeze" covers an area of ​​6.5 hectares. There is a boarding house and a children's camp. You can also relax with tents. The price category is average. On the territory there is a football field, playgrounds for sports. The dining room and cafe are constantly working. The beach is sandy, the water is clear. Fans of the underwater world will not be bored here.
  • Village Zaymo-Obryv. Base "Edem" offers tourists accommodation in equipped rooms of the hotel complex or cottages. Average prices - about 2,000 rubles. The territory is equipped according to modern requirements.
  • Farm Sedykh. Not far from the city of Azov is the camp site "Rainbow". It is perfect for those who want to save on accommodation. The territory is equipped with attractions, there is a playground for children. Guests are invited to stay in cozy wooden houses or rooms for 2-4 people.




Holidays in the Rostov region on the Sea of ​​Azov have always been popular among a significant number of people. Tourists come to these places from all regions of Russia. And in 2016, this flow almost doubled, since it is simply impossible to find a better ratio of price and conditions.
