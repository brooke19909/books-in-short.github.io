Cost of visit and work schedule

How much does it cost to relax in a water park in Pskov? The complex of water activities is focused on family vacations. You can visit it with children of any age. The smallest (0-4 years old) rest in the water park for free, under the supervision of their parents. The category "Junior" includes children aged 5-12 years. On weekdays, a three-hour bathing session in the water park for them costs 500 rubles, and for 600 rubles you can buy a subscription for the whole day. On a weekend, you can visit the water park in Pskov with a ticket of the Junior category for 600 rubles (3 hours) or 700 rubles (daily unlimited). Visitors over 13 years old are required to pay a visit to the water entertainment complex at adult rates. A three-hour session on weekdays costs 850 rubles, and a daily subscription costs 1050 rubles. On weekends, an unlimited visit for adults is 1250 rubles, and you can spend three hours in the water park for 1050 rubles. "Aquapolis" - a water park, the prices of which are affordable for everyone. The complex also has a flexible system of discounts for pensioners and other categories of beneficiaries, seasonal promotions. You can buy tickets cheaper with a group visit to the center, a large company or the whole family.
