Quik: setup, installation, step by step instructions, features

In trading, a very important role is played by the trading platform. All transactions are made on it. In trading on financial markets, there are various terminals that have their own characteristics and characteristics. This article will detail the Quick trading floor. The reader will receive information about the correct Quik settings for various trading assets and its installation.

Features of stock trading

Trading in financial markets takes place in various directions: stock and commodity exchanges, Forex, cryptocurrency, binary options and much more. It is very important for a speculator or even less an investor to have complete information in order to timely monitor any changes occurring in the market.

Quite often, many assets correlate with each other. This property allows us to assume the growth or fall of one instrument in relation to another. Since all assets are very different in their characteristics, it is almost always difficult to track their dynamics in one place.

Tool types

All trading assets can be divided into several groups, which depend on the direction of trade and the type of market.

Types of trading assets:

  1. Shares of companies, government and commercial bonds, shares, deposits, debt receipts and other securities.
  2. Futures, options contracts.
  3. Market indices.
  4. Currency pairs.
  5. Metals (gold, silver) and other tools.

All trading assets have their own specifics, volatility, features that a trader needs to know and be able to systematize, as well as analyze the data received.

Advantages of Quik Terminal

Above in the article, it was not in vain that information was provided on the diversity of assets and the characteristics of exchange trading. Modern trading is a very high volatility in almost all assets, which can not always be quickly and efficiently analyzed. Unfortunately, not every platform provides such opportunities for the trader, where you can fully engage in the analysis and forecasting of changes in quotations in one place. The MetaTrader platform is perfect for the forex market, but its use is simply impossible for the stock market, since there are no corresponding functions for futures and securities.

Therefore, for these purposes, the developers created the Quick platform. It allows you to visually see any changes in a variety of assets that can be tracked simultaneously. Setting up Quik for trading is not at all complicated, even a beginner can handle it. It is important to study the instructions and repeat the necessary steps.

Using Quik settings correctly, you can trade more efficiently. In addition, simultaneously monitor the movement of quotes of various assets, conduct market analysis, systematize the data obtained, create a portfolio for investment and much more.

Settings for stock trading

When trading stocks for clarity, it is advisable to always have two tabs open - for trading and market analysis. Determining the price range will allow more efficient and profitable opening and closing of transactions.

To create a new tab, you must select a function in the terminal menu "Add".

To be always aware of the release of important news, it is recommended to create a special window and call it "News". The next instrument for trading stocks is Current Trading. This is a table in which all the necessary information is displayed. For example, the name of a trading asset, percentage changes, market supply and demand, the number of transactions and much more. From the presented list, you can select the necessary Quik settings for stock trading.

Procedure:

  • open the "Menu";
  • "Current bidding";
  • "Creating a table of current trading";
  • go to the next window "Available tools", here you need to select the names of shares;
  • then "Available options."

In conclusion, you need to confirm all your actions by clicking the "Yes" button.

As soon as all points of the instruction are completed, the table will be reflected in the workspace of the terminal. One of the most popular tools for traders is the "Glass of prices" or "Glass of quotes."

To add it to the platform, in the settings of Quik stocks, in the "Current Bidding" window you need to select the name of the instrument and right-click on it.

Price Chart Settings

After these steps, you can go to the schedule settings. This is done according to the principle described above, that is, the name of the stock is selected and a mouse click is made in the "Menu"> "Price and Volume Chart" section. Once it appears, position it to the right of the Quotes Glass tool.

To connect these three tools, so that all information appears automatically, you need to connect using the anchor in the "Current Bid", "Quotation Glass" and "Chart" windows. To do this, click on the icon in the upper corner of the "Current Bidding" sign.

In addition, in the settings of the Quik terminal, you can sort stocks by turnover, that is, the maximum number of transactions. To do this, select "Turnover", "Sort". In the same tab, you can create tables for deals, orders and stop orders.
