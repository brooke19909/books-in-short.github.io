Beach "Cote d'Azur" (Yevpatoriya): description, photos, reviews of tourists

The sea, the sun, the beach - this is what people are going to the Crimea for. On long winter evenings, we dream of summer, of vacation, of plunging into the warm sea water and enjoying ourselves as much as possible, taking sunbaths on the picturesque shore. Beach holidays are most loved by tourists of all ages. And, as you know, the most gorgeous beaches of the Crimean peninsula are in Yevpatoria and its environs. Soft white sand, gently sloping bottom, clear sea - the beaches of these places are compared with the world famous Maldives.

Cote d'Azur Beach

Yevpatoriya is a resort that, as already mentioned, is known for its unique sandy beaches. Not without reason since Soviet times, this city is considered the best children's health resort.

Well, the best beaches of Yevpatoria are located in the area of Lake Moynak on the outskirts of the city. The Cote d'Azur Beach (Yevpatoriya) is just one of them. It differs in a very wide beach strip. The water in this place of the Kalamitsky Bay is perfectly clear, the sky is reflected in it and gives the sea a turquoise color. The sand is very small, soft and velvet to the touch. All these charms in combination with the warm Crimean sun and fresh air create ideal conditions for relaxation.

"Cote d'Azur" (Yevpatoria), the photo of which is presented below, is equipped and well-maintained beach. Yes, of course, it is not free, but for the entrance to the beach the price is symbolic, only 30 rubles per day.





On the beach there is a dining room, a cozy restaurant, a bar, various water attractions, slides, pools, a volleyball court, a yacht club and other entertainments.

Services

What is included in the price of a ticket to the Cote d'Azur beach (Yevpatoriya)? We are happy to answer this question.

Firstly, stay on the beach during the day. The beach has toilets, changing cabins, huge awnings for those who are afraid of the sun, there are places for smoking, urns. The use of these benefits is included in the ticket price. In addition, on the beach "Cote d'Azur" (Yevpatoria) there are constantly observing lifeguards, Wi-Fi service is provided.





For a more comfortable stay, you can take advantage of additional services. For example, for 150 rubles a day you can buy a comfortable snow-white lounger, and for 200 rubles a soft mattress will be provided. Hire an umbrella will cost 400 rubles per day.

In the event that you come by car, you should know that there is an equipped parking on the beach, the cost of which is 50 p. per day.

Fans of the VIP-vacation "Cote d'Azur" (Yevpatoria) offers to rent cozy bungalows that are located near the sea. Here you will be hidden from prying eyes.





Renting such a bungalow from 2000 to 2500 rubles per day depends on capacity. The bungalows feature mattresses with covers and towels.

Car camping "Cote d'Azur"

If you like to relax with a tent, then the Cote d'Azur (Yevpatoria) will provide such an opportunity, since the beach is equipped with an excellent camping site.

Car camping is guarded, electricity is provided, there are toilets, showers and even stoves are equipped.

You can come to this place with your tent and enjoy all the benefits provided, however, the cost of living per day per person will cost you 150 rubles. The exception is electricity, it will be provided for an additional fee of 150 rubles.

Car camping is provided with free Wi-Fi. An attractive point is the fact that residents of car camping are given a 10% discount on meals in the dining room, which is located on the beach.





On the campsite, vacationers can rent a cozy summer house made of cane, which is built like a tent. Instead of the floor, the houses have thick soft mattresses, all bedding is provided when renting.

Rent of such exotic housing costs from 1000 to 1500 rubles per day.

Beach Reviews

In the photo, the beach looks decent, beautiful and attractive. How does the Cote d'Azur really meet the guests of Yevpatoria?

Guest reviews about this beach made us happy. Almost all tourists call the "Cote d'Azur" the best beach in Crimea. Perfect cleanliness here. On the beach you can not find a cigarette butt, not even a pebble - everything is perfectly clean, and it is very pleasing.

The quality of showers and toilets is quite fine with everyone. In the dining room on site, the prices are adequate, the cuisine is delicious.

The prices of vacationers are more than satisfied. If you do not take anything superfluous, then for a minimal fee you can get coziness, cleanliness, convenience and comfort.
