Habtoor Grand Beach Resort Spa (Dubai UAE): hotel description and reviews

Holidays in the United Arab Emirates have long been very popular. By the quality of the services provided and the prices it is considered to be elite. The city of Dubai, located in the emirate of the same name, is a leader in the country in the development of the tourism business. Most hotels here have a category of "5 stars" and are modern stylish high-rises. One of them is the Habtoor Grand Beach Resort SPA Autograph Collection. This hotel, impressing with its grandeur and luxury, provides everything so that guests feel not just resting tourists, but at least kings and queens. Exclusive rooms, a wide network of restaurants that present the cuisines of dozens of countries, a spa, a huge ideally well-groomed area and always ready to help staff - this is what awaits every guest here.

Beach

It is very important to take into account the weather conditions for everyone buying tours in the Habtoor Grand Beach Resort SPA 5 *. The UAE is a country in the Middle East, where the climate is greatly affected by a vast desert area. The high months here are the winter months, when the weather is about the same as in the Moscow region in summer, namely, warm even can be said, hot days, cool nights, pleasant for swimming water temperature in the bay (about +22 degrees), plus almost full calm. From May to October in the UAE, the season is considered low, so all prices are also low. The reason is the intense heat. In July, the air temperature here reaches +45 degrees in the shade, there is no rain at all, but at the same time high humidity, which even healthy people can hardly tolerate. Rest on the beach at this time is impossible, because even the sea does not save, the water temperature in which reaches +35 degrees. At this time, Dubai is comfortable only in those rooms where air conditioning works, for example, in the ‚ÄúMall of the Emirates‚Äù, where you can even go skiing and play snowballs.

In the high season, lasting in the UAE from the first days of October to about mid-May, a beach holiday is perfect. Habtoor Grand Beach Resort SPA - a hotel built almost on the shore of the bay and having its own section of the beach. According to tourists, the sand is ideally clean and very soft, pleasant for feet, but you can walk on it barefoot only in the morning. In the afternoon, it heats up so much that you need to put beach shoes on your feet. The disadvantage of the beach is that sunbeds and umbrellas are located about 100 meters from the water's edge. But swimming here is very good, since the entry into the sea is smooth, there are no stones, jellyfish and algae, but the water is so salty that it plucks your eyes. After swimming, it must be washed off.

For the convenience of guests of the hotel on the beach there is a bar. Drinks and snacks in it are paid. Nearby there are other bars and several restaurants.
