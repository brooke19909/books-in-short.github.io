Tatarstan hotels: addresses, descriptions, reviews

Tatarstan is famous among tourists for its sights, architectural ensembles and beautiful landscapes, original culture and developed infrastructure. Due to a number of major sporting events, a huge number of tourists come here. But not everyone knows where to stay here for the night. In this article we will consider the most popular hotels of the Republic of Tatarstan.





Hotel "Tatarstan"

In the center of Kazan is the Tatarstan Hotel. Prices for accommodation in it are quite affordable - from 1950 rubles. A magnificent panorama opens from the windows onto the central street of the city - this is Bauman Street, which is also called the Kazan Arbat.

The thirteen-story hotel building allows you to move into any of the 211 rooms. Judging by the reviews, there are also superior rooms. Hotel "Tatarstan" (Kazan) offers guests to visit a restaurant with a bar, which accommodates 120 people, 2 banquet rooms, designed for 10 and 24 seats. In addition, there is a billiards room with service.

Cost - from 1950 rubles per day.

Gulf Stream

In the Soviet district of Kazan there is the Gulf Stream hotel complex, erected in 2007. You will need only 10 minutes and you will be taken to the historical center of the city. The hotel is located half an hour from the airport, close to the equestrian center, next to the Mega Kazan shopping center.





This Tatarstan hotel has different options for accommodating city guests. This is a business-class hotel, which is a full-fledged apartment of two and three rooms. The restaurant of the institution offers food with several types of cuisines, including national Tatar, European and Russian. There is also a conference room, bar, fitness center, saunas, pool and jacuzzi.

Cost - from 3150 rubles per day.

Hotel AMAKS Safar

Due to the high level of service, a wide range of capabilities and professionalism of the staff, the AMAKS Safar-hotel (Tatarstan, Kazan) deservedly occupies the main positions in the category "Best Hotels in the City". It has 191 rooms of several levels of comfort. It is worth noting that among the other hotels in Kazan, it compares favorably with the availability of the use of various complex services of a hotel, entertainment complex and business center.

Judging by the reviews, the complex has four conference rooms equipped with modern presentation equipment, in addition, a business center, which is equipped with office equipment. This hotel in Tatarstan has another advantage - it has banquet rooms, the total capacity of which is 350 people. They are perfect for corporate events, business lunches and wedding parties.

Cost - from 2100 rubles per day.

Open city

The new Open City Hotel is located in the center of Naberezhnye Chelny. Here you will find a combination of convenience, simplicity and modernity. Nearby are a variety of shops, large shopping centers, restaurants and cafes. The number of rooms is represented by 216 rooms of several categories.





All rooms have LCD TVs, telephones, comfortable furniture, a private bathroom and a safe. It offers guests an exquisite restaurant complex where you can enjoy various cuisines of several countries of the world, as well as a cozy lobby bar, karaoke room, billiard room, as evidenced by numerous reviews. For people who come to the city for work, there is a conference room with the necessary equipment. In addition, on site there is a guarded free parking.

Cost - from 3000 rubles per day.

Hotel "Kama"

"Kama" is a hotel (Naberezhnye Chelny), which has existed for a long time and is always glad to welcome its guests. You have the opportunity to book a room you are interested in at a convenient time for you, each guest will be able to find something to their liking. The hotel room stock consists of 19 rooms, made in an exquisite design. All of them are well equipped for a comfortable, relaxing stay, as evidenced by numerous reviews. The rooms have widescreen TVs, free internet, air conditioning and other amenities. It should also be noted that near the Kama Hotel there are main transport hubs and a huge number of leisure places and shops.

Cost - from 1800 rubles per day.

Hotel "Sakura"

Hotel Sakura is also located in Naberezhnye Chelny. A 2-minute drive takes you to the heart of the city. The area, despite its proximity to the center, is considered environmentally friendly. The hotel has an excellent geographical location. This makes it easy to get to the desired place in the city. Guests of this Tatarstan hotel can take advantage of additional services, including barbecue facilities, a garden. In addition, judging by the reviews, the hotel has a sauna and an indoor pool.





On site, guests can use the Internet, parking and a 24-hour reception desk for free. The rooms have everything you need for a relaxing stay: LCD TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, satellite TV, microwave, washing machine, kettle. Each room also has showers, a hairdryer and hygiene products. Near the hotel there are large grocery stores and shopping and entertainment centers.

Cost - from 2000 rubles per day.

Hotel "Tatarstan" (Naberezhnye Chelny)

This hotel is located in Naberezhnye Chelny near Begishevo Airport. It features a tour desk, a 24-hour front desk and free Wi-Fi.

Hotel Tatarstan (Naberezhnye Chelny) has spacious rooms equipped with a work desk, refrigerator, TV and air conditioning. Some have a seating area.

A rich breakfast is served here, which is prepared using local quality products. National dishes are served throughout the day.

Cost - from 2300 rubles per day.

Hotel "Absolute"

In Nizhnekamsk, on the eighth floor of a multi-storey building, is the Absolute Hotel. This is a cozy, spacious apartment, equipped with everything you need, which is suitable for people who love a homely atmosphere. It has modern appliances, soft sofas, wide beds, as well as a stylish kitchen. The guests of this hotel in Tatarstan, judging by the reviews, do not even notice that they have settled in the hotel, since they are surrounded by a truly homely atmosphere.





It is cleaned daily. Nearby are restaurants, cafes, stops, shopping centers and so on. Free internet is available on site. The hotel always has promotions and discounts.

Cost - from 3600 rubles per day.
