Belgorod-Rossosh: travel arrangements

The route from Belgorod to Rossosh may be useful for those people who want to get to resorts on the shores of the Black Sea and to cities in the North Caucasus. Recently, traveling from Belgorod to the south, for example, to the Crimea is difficult due to the situation in Ukraine, so it‚Äôs best to make a detour to the M-4 highway via Rossosh.

Bus ride

There are few buses from Belgorod to Rossosh, because the city is small, so there are no significant number of flights such as, for example, from Moscow to Tula or Tambov. Local bus number 552 leaves from the railway station as part of the Russian Railways multimodal transportation program. The flight leaves at 08:40 and arrives in Rossosh in 5 hours. The bus station in the city is located in the center, next to the churches, and the train station, on the contrary, is on the eastern outskirts.

In addition, transit buses go from Belgorod to Rossosh to Rostov-on-Don, Krasnodar and Yevpatoriya. It takes about 5 hours to travel, in summer there are more flights. Disembarkation of passengers can occur near the cafe-hotel "24 hours."

From Belgorod they leave at 09:20, 12:00 and at 15:30.

The price of a ticket between cities is from 570 to 640 rubles, and the distance from Belgorod to Rossosh is about 260 kilometers, therefore, the fare here is slightly more than 2 rubles per kilometer.

Rail ride

Due to the specific location of cities on the map, the railway connection between Belgorod and Rossosh is poorly developed. There is only a direct train that travels 13.5 hours. From Belgorod, he can leave at 05:35 and at 22:50, and runs not annually, but in the summer on certain days. The final station may be Anapa or Sukhumi.

The ticket price is slightly higher than the ticket price to Voronezh, where you can transfer to Rossosh. A reserved seat is approximately 900 rubles, and a coupe is twice as expensive.

There is also an option to get to Voronezh by train and make a bus transfer to Rossosh. Train No. 124 leaves at 09:10 and stops in Voronezh at 16:41, that is, you can really catch the evening bus to Rossosh. The ticket price is from 700 rubles in the reserved seat and from 1,400 in the compartment.

Buses depart from Voronezh to Rossosh from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m. from the central bus station or south-west bus station. A ticket costs 550 rubles. Travel time is 3.5 hours.

Travelling by car

By car, from Belgorod to Rossosh you can actually get there in 3-4 hours, it depends on the car, the load on the route and the weather. You need to leave to the northeast at 14 K-1. It leads through Novy Oskol and Alekseevka to the Voronezh region, where it continues as R-185. Only 260 kilometers. Rossosh will be the second settlement along the road after Olkhovatka.
