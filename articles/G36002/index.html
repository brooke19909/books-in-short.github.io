Modular mobile phone "Motorola" with a projector: review, reviews

One of the most original and unique phones in recent years has become the new Motorola model with a projector, speaker and camera, coupled with a tenfold optical zoom. Moto Z is the most unusual solution on the gadget market in recent years both in appearance and in technical specifications. All the nuances and uniqueness of the new smartphone-designer - in a review of the "Motorola" with a projector.

Contents of delivery

Despite the fact that the configuration of the smartphone for testing is very modest - only the gadget itself and the charger, users of the new Motorola with a projector come with a 3.5 mm adapter with USB Type-C, a special clip for removing the card tray and an additional panel.





Design

The new model of the smartphone was made in a very thin body, which allows us to draw an analogy with the Motorola V3 clamshell released in 2004. The company came up with an original solution, showing off the new Motorola with a projector, a bunch of accessories and a modular design with a unique design.

Despite the small thickness, the design of the phone is very durable and monolithic. The smartphone passed the standard test of strength without showing any creaks and backlashes. However, you should be careful about the complex and elegant design: an unsuccessful fall can lead to sad consequences.

The case of the new Motorola with an aluminum projector is made in three colors: black-gold, white-gold and black-silver.

The lower and upper parts of the rear panel are decorated with glass inserts, while the panel itself is trimmed with transverse stripes. Despite the fact that the aluminum panel is very easily soiled, it is resistant to scratches.

The Motorola camera with a projector is located on the rear panel and protrudes very strongly from the case due to the fact that the lens and dual flash are located on a separate platform, as a result of which the phone is lying unevenly on the surface of the table. However, the developers thought over this detail: the presence of an additional rear panel quickly eliminates it.

Elements of the contact pad are located below and allow you to connect additional modules to the smartphone.

A speaker enclosed in a metal frame is located at the top of the panel. A flash, sensors, and a front camera are hidden to his side.

The fingerprint scanner is located below the screen, next to it are microphones and motion sensors. The latter are made in black and are not particularly noticeable on a black smartphone, but stand out on a white model. During long-term operation of Motorola with a projector, such a detail does not attract attention, but when it is introduced it looks unusual and resembles a funny face.

The tray for SIM-cards and a memory card is located on the upper end.





Moto Mods

Moto Mods is a well-known line of additional accessories for the smartphone. The simplest element from it is a decorative back panel made of plastic. A wooden cover is supplied, but panels of other materials can be purchased if desired. The cost of one copy is about $ 20, they are attached to the smartphone with magnets and protect the back surface from damage. The sloping shape of the lid improves the ergonomics of the smartphone and allows you to hide the protruding camera.

A Motorola cell phone with a projector is equipped with a stereo speaker, created in active collaboration with the world famous company JBL, thanks to which the smartphone turns into a full-fledged music center. An additional module with a projector allows you to view photos and videos in large size. Each of them is equipped with its own battery and USB Type-C ports, so they do not plant a Moto Z battery and can be charged separately from it.

JBL speakers are connected via rear-panel connectors. The column is equipped with a stand, which allows you to conveniently place your smartphone and view video with high-quality sound. Power from the phone‚Äôs battery is activated after 10 hours of operation of the speaker from its own battery. The average cost of an audio module is $ 80.

Image output from the smartphone screen to any surface in a resolution of 854x480 pixels is carried out using a miniature Insta-Share projector. The module has a built-in 1100 mAh battery, which lasts for battery life for one hour. The cost of such a module is $ 300.

At a similar price, the Hasselblad photo module is provided, which allows you to turn Motorola with a projector into a full-fledged camera with a 12-megapixel sensor, tenfold zoom and good optics. Equipped with optical stabilization and xenon flash.

The cost of the 2200 mAh battery cover is $ 60. A similar panel with wireless charging function will cost $ 90.





Ergonomics

A distinctive feature of the Motorola novelty is the grace of the profile: such thin smartphones are hardly ever released today - with a weight of 136 grams, the case thickness is 5 mm. At the same time, the phone is equipped with a 5.5-inch screen.

The screen lock key and separate volume buttons are located on the right side of the case. They are located quite high, so without intercepting the smartphone, you can‚Äôt click on them. However, the phone screen is blocked by the scanner, so reaching for a separate key is not necessary. Accidental triggers are completely excluded, the keys are easy to finger, gently pressed and pleasant to the touch, the response is intelligible and clear.





Display

The novelty from Motorola is equipped with a 5.5-inch AMOLED screen with a resolution of 2560x1440, narrow frames and a density of 535 pixels per inch. The protective element is Gorilla Glass 4, curved at the edges, with a good anti-glare effect and an oleophobic coating: fingers leave no residue. The screen sensitivity is excellent, a ten-point multi-touch.

The display transmits a high-quality image with bright, saturated colors and excellent contrast. Depending on the lighting, the backlight level changes smoothly and accurately, so we can say that the sensor copes with its task.





Price

The main question of users is how much does Motorola cost with a projector? Given the wide functionality and a large number of additional accessories, it will cost a considerable amount - about $ 700.

Eventually

Optional accessories fuel interest in the Moto Z, and fans of custom gadgets will definitely appreciate it.
