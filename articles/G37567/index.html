How to choose a TV in the car: an overview of the best models and reviews

Due to constant traffic jams on the road, each driver finds for himself various ways of relaxation. Asking the question of how not to fall asleep in anticipation, not to become aggressive and angry, and how not to go crazy, a motorist must find what he likes. Someone is talking on the phone, sending SMS messages, listening to music, working, learning a foreign language or reading a book. Some people prefer to watch TV. It is easy to install the device in the car, so the main thing is to choose the most suitable one.

A few years ago, no one could imagine that, in addition to listening to music, you can watch movies, TV shows and your favorite TV channels from discs. Now there is a huge range of products that are intended for installation in a machine. Consider some of the nuances of installing equipment and the best models.



Types of TVs

Televisions that are installed in cars are conventionally divided into five types. Classification takes place according to the method of attachment and location.

Distinguish:

  • Standard TV in the car. Such a device is capable of working both from 12 W and from full 220 W. The device is connected to both special and conventional power adapters.
  • Built-in TV. As a rule, it is mounted in a headrest, a visor protecting from the sun, or in an armrest.
  • Ceiling TV in the car. Of course, it is installed on the top surface of the car. It has wide viewing angles, and consumes electricity thanks to a special ceiling.
  • Retractable TV. Such an apparatus is part of auto-stations.
  • Car monitor. He has no autotuner.

As is clear from the description, the first three options are the most favorite species among drivers.

Where to install the TV?

TV at home and in offices has long been an indispensable part. It remains to decide where his place in the car? It should be borne in mind that you should not install the device on the windshield, in the corner of the dashboard and in the center of the cabin. Ideally, the TV in the car should be near the passenger seat so as not to distract the driver. If a person is stuck in traffic, it will be enough to transfer to the place near which the device is installed.

Due to the expensive matrix, the device has a high resolution, good viewing angles. This allows you to watch TV, which is characterized by high-quality and contrasting picture.

All modules installed in the device work perfectly, they amaze every driver with their reliability. Like other built-in TVs in the car, this model has several connectors that allow you to connect to different power sources.

As already described above, headphones are included. If necessary, you can purchase others - more expensive, but of high quality. The TV is convenient in that its options have the function of automatic video playback.

The advantages of the device:

There are no minuses on the TV.

The approximate cost is $ 950.

It has good viewing angles. The image is clear, and the settings that allow you to improve it consist of a wide range of options. In order to adapt the picture for themselves, the user will have to study the menu in detail. By the way, it is in Russian.

Minus TV: poor sound level.

BBK LD1006TI

Phantom DTV 700B

The TV of this model has a 7-inch screen. The equipment is quite impressive, so it fully justifies the high cost ($ 100- $ 150). The TV works without charging for about 2 hours.

Reviews

It is important to note that the reviews about the devices described in the article are always positive. Even if you consider some of the disadvantages, they are either blocked by a low price or a list of advantages. Negative reviews are written very rarely. Often they are associated with problems that arise as a result of factory defects.

Total

The TV navigator in the car will be a great solution, if often the driver has to spend time in traffic jams. I must say that this device is a very convenient and useful thing. Especially such a gadget is useful on long trips.

Those who have a child will just need a TV. To occupy the baby, you can install the device in the headrest. Adult passengers will also be grateful for a good time. After all, of course, watching a movie or series is much more interesting than just watching the road.

The driver must remember to be careful when watching TV while on the go. We must remain vigilant and not be distracted from the driving process.
