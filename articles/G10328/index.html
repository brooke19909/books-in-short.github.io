"Oryol Park": description, services, reviews

Many people think that changing places is a great way to relax. Therefore, a great option for those who live in the capital of Russia is to visit a country hotel. Such an institution has all the conditions for visitors to feel as comfortable as possible, to be distracted from the routine and noise of the metropolis and enjoy the beautiful nature.

Oryol Park: general information about the hotel

This rural hotel complex is a popular recreation area among residents of the capital.

The infrastructure of this organization includes the following:

  1. Sixteen country houses for up to twenty four people.
  2. Bars and restaurants.
  3. Seventy-four guest rooms with original designs.
  4. Halls for banquets and weddings.
  5. Beaches.
  6. Bath complex.
  7. Playground for children.
  8. Zoo.
  9. Playgrounds for playing tennis and practicing winter and summer sports.
  10. The farm.

The Park Hotel Orlovsky is located at: Moscow Region, Leninsky District, Bogdanikha Village.

Accommodation options in the hotel complex

Guests coming to the "Oryol Park" can accommodate both in the room and in a separate house. Depending on the preferences of the clients, the following accommodation options are offered:

  1. Wooden houses with rooms of different sizes.
  2. Large cottages for guests.
  3. Seventy-four rooms in the main hotel building.

Rooms and houses in the hotel complex can be both standard and luxury. Some of them are located on the coast of the lake.

Varieties of rooms

Visitors coming to Oryol Park are provided with the following types of rooms:

  1. Standard rooms with 1 or 2 beds.
    These are comfortable rooms that have everything you need for guests. They have a modern design, in the decor of the rooms pleasant light shades prevail. The windows offer a beautiful view of the river bank.
  2. Superior rooms with a spa bath. These rooms are designed for two people. There is a bedroom with a large bed and a bathroom with a hydromassage. The premises include two rooms, also has a wide balcony.
  3. Non-family rooms (standard and superior). These rooms are decorated in a modern style and consist of two rooms. These rooms are considered to be a great option for a joint vacation for couples with children.
  4. Rooms "Alice in Wonderland" and "Picasso". The design of the first type of rooms is based on the motifs of a famous fairy tale. The interior of the rooms is bright and original. The design of the second type of rooms is based on the features of the paintings of the famous painter. Their decor is characterized by expressiveness, the presence of many colorful elements and rich colors.

Types of cottages and guest houses

In the "Oryol" park-hotel, guests have the opportunity to live in separate buildings. The following types of houses are offered to accommodate clients of the hotel complex:

  1. Standard cottages. These two-story buildings accommodate four people. Each of the cottages has a living room, attic and three beds.
  2. Superior Homes. Such premises have a fairly large area. Each one has a terrace.
  3. Guest houses. A rather large company (up to twenty-four people) can live in such premises. Private rooms are designed for two or four persons, there is also a hall and a large table. These rooms are perfect for corporate events and weddings. Guest houses can be either standard or luxury.

Wedding celebrations at the hotel

This holiday plays an important role in the life of any married couple. Of course, the newlyweds dream of holding it brightly and unforgettably. Today, many couples prefer not to order a banquet hall in a large restaurant, but to hold a celebration in nature. In the park-hotel "Orlovsky" a wedding is a great opportunity to relax outside the city and enjoy a bright holiday. In this hotel complex, you can organize a celebration in a tent or in a spacious banquet room with large windows overlooking the beautiful nature.

The organizers of the celebration think through everything to the smallest detail, and the newlyweds do not have to spend time and effort on solving important issues related to the celebration. Interior, light and music, table setting and choice of dishes - the hotel staff arranges everything taking into account the preferences and wishes of the bride and groom.

Leisure

The guests, vacationing in the "Oryol Park", certainly will not be bored. There are all the possibilities for outdoor activities. For children and adults are provided rides, computer games. Lovers of relaxation, both soul and body can spend time in the bath complex, where there is a pool, shower and TV.

On the territory of the park-hotel there is a beach with chairs and umbrellas, a water park where both children and adults can have fun. In the hotel complex you can go in for sports both in winter and in summer. There are areas for skating, skiing, bicycles, special places for playing tennis and football.

Guest opinions

The park-hotel "Orlovsky" reviews are mixed. Among the advantages of the institution are called developed infrastructure, various opportunities for recreation and entertainment, beautiful nature.

Many customers are satisfied with the quality of food in the restaurant, the living conditions in the cottages, the presence of a good beach in the hotel.

The disadvantages of the institution include inadequate cleaning of rooms, careless attitude of some employees to guests, inconvenience of the payment system through a special card.
