What will make us happy in Dubai?

As you know, one of the largest centers in the UAE is Dubai. A flower park, opened recently, is located in this city. At first, when you look at him, you do not believe your eyes. This is a mirage, and nothing else! Well, where in the desert so many flowers? But no. This is not a mirage, this is a real object - the Flower Park in Dubai, in which about 45 million different flower plants have found shelter.

Park Features

The development of this miracle was carried out by the Agriculture Company & Akar Landscaping. It was these companies that gave us a unique opportunity to admire the landscape design of the park, called Dubai Miracle Garden. Although the rumor has already managed to christen him in his own way. This is "part of a paradise", and "a miracle in the desert." All these names characterize the opened park absolutely precisely.

On an area of ‚Äã‚Äã6.7 hectares, about 45 million flowers are planted. Many European countries shared their seeds with the United Arab Emirates. For example, Germany, USA, Italy. Many plants in this climate are planted for the first time.





Park and modern technology

Dubai's flower park is not only striking in its scope. It also attracts with newfangled technologies that are used here to grow a huge number of plants. The creators took the path of saving natural resources. To care for flowers, subsystems of underground irrigation are used. They supply water and fertilizer to the very roots of plants. This saves up to 75% of electricity and water. Wastewater is used for irrigation .

Sights of the Arab miracle

The flower park in Dubai is attractive because the flowers here were not sown in a chaotic mess, but rather, each plant has its own place intended only for it. This is because any instance is considered part of the flower arrangement. What is worth seeing here?





  1. A portrait created from 1000 flowers. It depicts Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan. Arabs treat this person with love and gratitude, who has done a lot of useful and good deeds for their country. In memory of him, they composed a floral arrangement surrounded by seven hearts (according to the number of existing emirates).
  2. A pyramid of flowers that reaches a height of 10 meters.
  3. Huge unique floral clock.
  4. A unique wall of flowers, the dimensions of which are 800 meters in length and 3 meters in height.

Many amazing secrets and man-made masterpieces can bring to our attention the flower park in Dubai. The photo will not convey that bright kaleidoscope of shades (about 60), which is replete with a park located in the middle of the desert. It can only slightly open the veil of secrecy, and she herself will step towards you while visiting this miracle.





Park Schedule

When going to this paradise, remember that the flower park in Dubai does not work in the summer season. It is very hot in the Emirates at this time. But starting in September, it hosts guests daily from 9am to 9pm. On weekends, visits are allowed until 23 hours. Ticket price - 20 AED (approximately $ 5.5). Children under 3 years old can attend the park for free.

If you plan to visit the world's largest Flower Park (Dubai), how to get to it, you will be told in a travel agency or tour guides. On our own, we add that from any hotel to this new wonder of the world can be reached by taxi.
