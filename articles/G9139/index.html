Bluetooth speaker rating: review of the best models, specifications, reviews about manufacturers

Many inexperienced consumers can be confused by the deceptive simplicity of portable speakers using the bluetooth protocol. Despite its modest dimensions, each model hides a specific speaker with a special sound. Gadgets are also distinguished by a set of functions, design and some other operational features.

The main task of portable equipment of such a plan is to reproduce sound in those places and in such situations where familiar audio systems cannot be used. Bluetooth speakers will come in handy during a bike ride or a morning run, on a picnic in the forest.

Today's market offers a wide range of similar equipment. Critical Settings for Portable Audio

In order to answer the question, which bluetooth speaker is better than the other and why, it is necessary to weigh the key characteristics of such a technique. This will help determine the choice and filter out unnecessary options.

Number of channels and speakers

The sound is mono or stereo and is determined by channels. In the first case, one channel, and in the second - two. A mono speaker produces less surround sound. The speakers are responsible for the bands and frequencies - low, medium and high.

Experts strongly do not recommend buying a gadget where the number of speakers is less than the bands. To choose a bluetooth speaker with good sound, you must pay attention to the frequency range. The wider it is, the better the sound.

For low frequencies, the limit of coverage will be in the region of 20-500 Hz. The sound output will be much juicier if this indicator is minimal. If bass is extremely important to you, then it is best to look at models from the premium sector, because even the best low-cost bluetooth speakers from the budget sector, alas, cannot boast of decent reproduction of low frequencies.

The limits for high frequencies are in the region of 10 000-25 000 Hz. Raise such a threshold under the force of almost any column from the mid-price segment. Worthy options can also be found in the public sector. High frequencies are especially important for those who prefer tracks where instrumental parts predominate.

Power

This parameter does not affect the sound quality, but is only responsible for the maximum volume level. The simplest models from the budget sector produce about 1.5-2 watts per speaker. Gadgets of the average hand - about 16-20 watts.

The best bluetooth speakers, and at the same time the most powerful ones, rattle at 50, or even all 100 watts. This figure is comparable to multimedia acoustics. In addition, most serious models are additionally equipped with a subwoofer. The latter has its own power.

Interfaces

Well, when, in addition to the bluetooth protocol, the gadget has additional interfaces for connecting third-party peripherals. Some speakers have a USB output for recharging mobile devices. Also there are models with a micro-USB and AUX interface at 3.5 mm.

The latter allows you to listen to music through headphones. In addition, the gadget may have a micro-SD slot for working with flash drives. In this case, your favorite songs will always be at hand. Judging by the reviews and ratings, bluetooth speakers and a flash drive are very popular.

Autonomy

Features of portable equipment require their own power sources - batteries or built-in rechargeable batteries. Naturally, the higher the capacity indicator, the better and the longer the column will work.

Budget "kids" are equipped, as a rule, with batteries in the region of 1500 mAh. This is enough for about 8 hours of continuous operation at an average volume level. Devices that occupy first places in the top of the best bluetooth speakers in terms of autonomy can be equipped with batteries for 20 or more thousand mAh.

Such "monsters" can work day and night. But such devices have one significant minus - this is the weight. Capacious batteries have never been lightweight. So here we have a double-edged sword, where you have to choose - autonomy or mobility.

Protective qualities

Since we are dealing with portable equipment, which is supposed to be used in traveling conditions (dust, dirt and moisture), it is clearly not out of place to pay special attention to the protective characteristics of the device.

This indicator is usually marked with the IP index. For the portable speaker segment, there is a scale of one to ten. For example, a device with the IPX3 index is not afraid of dust and splashing water, while a device with an IPX7 protection level is not afraid of rain and bathing in the bathroom. Naturally, the models that occupy first places in the ratings of portable bluetooth speakers have acceptable protective characteristics.
