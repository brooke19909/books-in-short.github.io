Cottage towns, Almaty: description, photos and reviews

The ninth largest state occupied by the territory of the Republic of Eurasia is the Republic of Kazakhstan. Until 1997, its capital was the city of Almaty, the largest city in the region. Its population now almost reached the mark of two million people. Many of them need their own housing, but city property prices bite. Therefore, many prefer to buy houses in the cottage towns of Almaty, located if not within the city, then in the immediate vicinity.

Obvious choice

Almaty is a large, hectic city located in the hot climate zone of the mainland. Because of this, many prefer to choose housing in the best environmental conditions, where it is possible to breathe fresh air, rather than the polluted atmosphere of the metropolis. Therefore, they are eyeing the cottage towns of Almaty with ready-made townhouses, cottages, land plots in them with the ability to build a house on their own project. Housing prices in such towns are much lower than in the city itself, which makes purchasing affordable.

Infrastructure

This project, like many cottage towns in Almaty, provides for the improvement of the internal territory in the form of arrangement of parking lots, playgrounds. All the necessary objects of social infrastructure: school, kindergarten, pharmacy, shops, hairdressers, shopping centers - are within walking distance from the town. Transport accessibility of the town allows its residents to quickly get their own or public transport from the city center or any of its areas. Nearby is a major highway - North Ring Street.

Town "Zhana Kuat"

The cottage town "Zhana Kuat" in Almaty is located north of the city, next to the village of Pokrovka and the village of Otegen Batyra. You can get to it via the Ili or Kuldzhinsky tracts both on personal and public transport. The distance from the center of Almaty is eighteen kilometers, which will take about 25 minutes on the way.
