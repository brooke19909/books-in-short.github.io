Bryansk, hotel "Desna": address, description, reviews

Many tourists and people going on a business trip are looking for a comfortable accommodation option. Today we are interested in Bryansk. Hotel "Desna" is one of the best options that can be offered to guests of the city.





Brief description of the hotel

One of the best accommodation options in a city such as Bryansk is the Desna Hotel. Here you will be offered the optimal ratio of the cost of services and their level of quality. Here you will have everything you need - free Wi-Fi, a private parking area and much more.

A sufficiently attractive place for both tourists and businessmen is the city of Bryansk. Hotel "Desna" will provide you with a comfortable stay in comfortable rooms, the design of which is made in warm colors. This gives the apartment a homely atmosphere. In addition, you will be provided with a set of amenities necessary for a full stay and relaxation.

A huge number of cafes and restaurants boasts Bryansk. Hotel "Desna" has a large cafe, which occupies two floors. Here you will be available traditional Russian cuisine, as well as a European menu.

Hotel "Desna" (Bryansk): address

This establishment is located on Lenin Avenue, 39. It is about 4 km from the railway station and 1 km from the bus station. If we talk about the distance to the international airport, then it is almost 20 km. The hotel is located in the city center, and some rooms overlook the central square. Within walking distance is a huge shopping center, as well as the Drama Theater.





Superior rooms

Comfortable living conditions are offered to its guests by the Desna Hotel. Bryansk will bring you a lot of positive impressions if you stay in one of the rooms of the following categories:

  • Suite is a large two-room apartment. Here, the bedroom is combined with a spacious living room, and the kitchen has a separate area. Accommodation is made on a double bed, and there is the possibility of providing cots for additional guests.
  • Junior Suite is a spacious one-room apartment, which successfully combines the bedroom area, as well as a small living room with folding upholstered furniture. You can place all your things in the wardrobe, under which a small separate room is allocated.
  • Double standard rooms suggest a pair of one and a half beds or one large. Due to the successful layout, as well as a large window through which a lot of light penetrates, the room seems more spacious than it actually is.
  • For those who are used to traveling alone, standard single rooms are provided. Here, in addition to the bed, there is also a workplace and a dressing table.




Tourist Class Rooms

Many thrifty tourists and delegations come to Bryansk. The Desna hotel offers them budget accommodation options, namely:

  • Family room is a large room of 25 sq.m. There is a double bed, as well as a soft folding sofa, which can fit 2 more people. It has its own combined bathroom.
  • The standard economy class room is characterized by small size, as well as modest decoration. In addition to the bed, there is also a work desk and a nightstand with a TV. There is also an option with 2 single beds.
  • The Budget Single Room is a miniature room measuring just 9 square meters. m. There is all the necessary minimum amenities for a comfortable stay for 1 guest.
  • The rooms of the "Hostel" category imply the possibility of buying one bed in a quadruple room. At the same time, all amenities are on the floor.




The amenities provided in the apartment

In the rooms of this hotel you can use the following list of amenities:

  • Superior rooms include carpeted flooring so you can comfortably move around the room even without slippers;
  • depending on the category of apartments, you will have a spacious wardrobe or a separate room allocated for a wardrobe;
  • the presence of a desk will especially please business people who arrived in Bryansk on a business trip);
  • the combined bathroom is equipped with a shower cabin, and also provides a full range of cosmetic and hygiene items;
  • thanks to the TV, you can pass the time while in the room;
  • a refrigerator will keep your food fresh;
  • You will have access to free wireless internet.




Hotel "Desna": vacancies (Bryansk)

Hotel "Desna" is interested in the constant attraction of qualified personnel. To this end, special training courses are conducted in the following areas:

  • guides for organizing walks in the central part of the city;
  • organizers of master classes in various areas;
  • external agents to attract new customers;
  • animators to work in the kids club.

Positive reviews

The Desna Hotel (Bryansk) is considered a rather popular option. Reviews about this institution contain the following positive information:

  • very convenient is the location in the central part of the city (there are many attractions near it, as well as infrastructure facilities);
  • the staff is very polite and quickly responds to all wishes and requests;
  • excellent buffet breakfast for a relatively low price;
  • The hotel has a very quiet and relaxing atmosphere.




Negative reviews

The negative moments of staying in this hotel are the following:

  • the building is quite old and needs repair;
  • It would not hurt to replace the crushed mattresses from which the springs protrude;
  • old, peeled linoleum in the corridors, which you can easily trip over;
  • nearby is a taxi rank, which is incredibly noisy at night;
  • the double bed is too cramped;
  • in bathrooms poor-quality leaking plumbing;
  • the assortment of the hotel restaurant is very scarce.

General impression

Hotel Desna is a cozy establishment in the city center, where you can find apartments for every taste and financial capabilities. It has a friendly atmosphere supported by qualified hotel staff. As for the rooms, they are quite comfortable. If you wish, you can rent superior apartments, or you can stay at cheaper tourist rooms. You have the opportunity to have breakfast in the hotel cafe. The buffet offers a huge selection of dishes, while their cost is quite reasonable. The only drawback of the hotel can be considered its some dilapidation and attrition. The directorate of the establishment should think about carrying out cosmetic repairs.
