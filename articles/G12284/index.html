Tasia Maris Oasis (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): hotel description and reviews

Choosing a place for a long-awaited vacation, summer family vacation or a romantic weekend, many prefer the island of Cyprus. Favorable climatic conditions, hospitable residents and a lot of historical attractions attract tourists from all over the world.

The resort town of Ayia Napa is the busiest and most visited. It attracts with the possibility of excursion tourism and getting a chocolate tan. Here you can visit the Monastery, the Rock Tombs of Makronisos and the Museum of the Sea. Informative stories of guides and a variety of beauties seen will remain in the memory of tourists for a long time.

The 4-star Tasia Maris Oasis Hotel offers high-level service, a warm welcome and a wonderful pastime for every guest. Not only adults, but also children will find something to their liking in this institution.

general characteristics

For the first time, the doors of the hotel complex were thrown open to receive guests in 2013. The relatively young hotel consists of a main three-story building and five additional two-story buildings. Its territory is shrouded in spreading palm trees and a number of exotic plants. Each alley of the park zone is equipped with fragrant flower beds. The main building surprises with its snow-white and airiness. In their reviews, guests note the amazing modernity of each architectural element.

The nearest international airport is just over 35 km away. Overcoming this distance after the flight is not at all tiring for newly arrived tourists. Checking in with pets is not possible. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms. There are apartments equipped with everything necessary for people with disabilities. The staff speaks Greek and English. Accepted cards of international banks. Check-in is always at 14:00.

Number of rooms

Hotel Tasia Maris Oasis 4 * (Cyprus) offers 141 rooms of two categories for settlement: Twin ROH and Family. The smallest area is 20 square meters, large - 25 squares. All of them are one-room and have access to a balcony from which the picturesque expanses of the sea open. It is equipped with plastic furniture.

The interior of the rooms is modern. Staying here will allow you to relax and enjoy your vacation in full. From each element of decor and decoration as a whole it blows homely warmth and coziness. The selected color palette visually expands the space and fills the room with freshness. All shades of white successfully harmonize with designer curtains and furnishings. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles. Panoramic windows fill the rooms with light.

Daily clean up here, regarding the cleanliness of the hotel, all reviews are positive. Linen is changed twice a week. The wake-up service will allow fans to sleep awake at the appointed time.

Accommodations

Comfortable accommodation is ensured by high-quality and varied equipment of rooms. The furniture set consists of a bed, a table with a pouf, a luggage rack and a wardrobe. Above the countertop is a large mirror. There is air conditioning, a plasma TV with Russian channels, a telephone for communication with the reception and a safe. The rooms of the Tasia Maris Oasis 4 * hotel are equipped with electric kettles and mini-refrigerators. A tea set will delight guests with the opportunity to start the morning with aromatic coffee or hot tea without leaving the walls of the apartment.

Wi-Fi is available throughout and is included in the price. Iron and ironing board are available upon request.

The bathroom is equipped with a toilet, sink, shower and hairdryer. Her interior is extravagant. There are towels in the required quantity and a set of personal hygiene products: shampoo, soap and shower gel.

Beach

The cleanest and safest swimming beaches are located in Ayia Napa. In addition to the amazingly shining daytime waves, here you can enjoy the orange sunset and starry sky at night. Pool

On-site activities feature swimming. It is filled with fresh water and is not equipped with a heating system. The aquazone is surrounded by a sun terrace. Numerous sun loungers with soft mattresses are located here. For those who want to hide from the scorching rays of the sun, umbrellas are installed. In the evening, a delightful highlight lights up here, which pleases the eye of strollers.

For children there is a children's swimming pool, which is located next to an adult. This arrangement allows the kids to be under the constant supervision of their parents and enjoy the whole family vacation.

Children's leisure

Wonderful vacations that will be remembered by kids for many years can be realized at the Tasia Maris Oasis Hotel (Cyprus, Ayia Napa). There is an outdoor playground for children. It contains carousels and slides, houses and labyrinths. The coating in them is rubberized, which is safe for health in the event of a fall. Frolicking in the fresh air is not only fun, but also useful for the younger generation.

For parents who wish to give their child under individual supervision, babysitting services are available. A qualified teacher will entertain your child with exciting games and educational activities. Good mood, happiness and smiles are guaranteed for every crumb.
