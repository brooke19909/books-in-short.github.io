Hotel "SK Royal" in Yaroslavl: address, services, prices and reviews

Yaroslavl is the center and heart of the Golden Ring of Russia. In this regard, the city annually hosts a lot of both domestic and foreign tourists. Before going to this location, many people have a question about where to stay for the period of travel. One of the most popular tourist destinations is the SK Royal Hotel in Yaroslavl.

About city

Yaroslavl is one of the oldest cities in Russia. In 1010, Grand Duke Yaroslav the Wise laid this majestic city. There is hardly a person who has not seen this beautiful land on a banknote of 1000 Russian rubles.

During the existence of Ancient Russia, the Yaroslavl principality was one of the most powerful, financially and culturally rich territories. Throughout history, the city has had high rates of industrial development and all kinds of crafts, and during the Time of Troubles, even for a short time it became the capital of the state.

In the nineteenth century, Yaroslavl received the unofficial name "Russian Florence" for its beauty and grace. In the city you can see the architecture of 16-21 centuries.

Description of the hotel and internal functionality

Hotel "SK Royal" in Yaroslavl is quite young, it appeared on the territory of the city in 2012. And for such a short period of existence, she fell in love with both guests and residents of the glorious city on the Volga.

The presented hotel provides a range of both paid and free services. Conventionally, they can be divided into:

  1. Are common. This category includes parking, which is provided both for free and for a fee. Also, throughout the hotel "SK Royal" in Yaroslavl, guests enjoy free Wi-Fi.
  2. Food and drinks. There is a bar and restaurant on site. There is also a special function for delivering food and drinks directly to your room.
  3. Business center. Holding business events is a common practice in the business environment. In this regard, the SK Royal Hotel in Yaroslavl has a conference room for meetings, meetings and negotiations.
  4. Registration desk. It is open to all visitors and hotel guests around the clock. There you can clarify your questions. This category includes the functions of providing a safe and luggage storage, currency exchange, individual registration on the days of arrival and departure of guests.
  5. Pool and wellness facilities. For everyone, the hotel has an indoor heated pool, which operates year-round. The hotel also has a sauna, a fitness center, massages and a Turkish bath.
  6. Cleaning. All rooms are serviced daily. It is also possible to order individually dry cleaning, laundry, and shoe shine services.

A total of 153 rooms are located on the territory of the SK Royal Hotel in Yaroslavl. Ninety-three of them are standard, thirty are studio rooms, twenty nine are luxury rooms, and one is presidential. The restaurant has a capacity of one hundred seventy people, and up to fifteen people can be present in the conference room.

Guest reviews

Tourists who stayed at the hotel presented were pleased with their presence there. However, as in any hotels, guests find their advantages and disadvantages.

The positive points in the reviews about the SK Royal Hotel in Yaroslavl include clean and comfortable rooms, the availability of free Wi-Fi with a good range. Also, many appreciated the quality of work of the staff, who carried out polite and effective explanatory work with guests. The food and drinks presented at the hotel did not leave guests indifferent. Many appreciated the taste and quality of the prepared dishes and drinks on offer.

As the main disadvantage of the hotel "SK Royal" in Yaroslavl, the presence of an unpresentable view of the construction sites outside the window was noted. Also, many people were prevented by low noise insulation in the rooms. However, despite minor inconveniences, most of the guests were very pleased with the hotel accommodation and services.
