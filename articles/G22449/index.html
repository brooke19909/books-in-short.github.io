Unusual places of St. Petersburg: photos

St. Petersburg is one of the greatest cities in Russia. This is not only an important economic center of the Russian Federation, but also one of the largest cultural megacities. It is worth noting that the unusual places of St. Petersburg attract tourists from all over the world all year round. Let's talk with you about where you should definitely go if you come to St. Petersburg.

Many say that St. Petersburg is the most beautiful city in Russia. It is difficult to say whether this is true or not, but one thing can be stated with 100% certainty - there is something to see here, regardless of your financial capabilities and time of year. Here you will find huge drawbridges, imperial architecture, which has been perfectly preserved to this day, as well as a lot of museum streets, cathedrals and much more. You will also learn what the white nights of St. Petersburg are. However, for a tourist who does not know what and where is located, it will be very difficult to coordinate their actions. Mirror Maze

If you like extreme sports to some extent, then why not visit the mirror attraction on 61 Nevsky Prospekt. You need to be here if only because it is the world's largest mirror maze with a length of more than 500 meters. It is located underground. You will be a true treasure hunter. Your goal is to find a precious treasure. On your way there will be more than a hundred tips, but they are all hidden in antique cabinets, chests and chests of drawers. And who knows what dwells there: skeletons, ghosts or some other monsters. But you will not be alone in your journey. Heroes from a wide variety of fairy tales will come to the rescue, for example, Alice, Cheshire Cat, Red Queen and many others. Especially memorable will be the path with colored ribbons, which can be anything. At the end of the journey, every adventurer will receive a valuable prize, but most importantly, a bunch of positive emotions and many beautiful photos. If you go to look for unusual places in St. Petersburg, do not forget to go into the mirror labyrinth, where adventures await you.

Ghost Village in St. Petersburg

If you are looking for unusual sensations from your travels, then be sure to visit ‚ÄúRussian America‚Äù - this is the second name of this unusual place, which is located in the vicinity of the village of Nazia. The history of this village is amazing and exciting in its own way. In 1930, American specialists came here to establish peat extraction. During the war, work was stopped and many left this place. After some time, part of Nazia was restored, and the other half was left destroyed and empty. Already in the 1960s, production was resumed here and peat was mined again, but after 20 years, reserves were exhausted, and most of the population left. In 2012, only 240 people lived in Nazia. If you are looking for interesting places for photos (St. Petersburg), then you need to visit the ghost village. Here you will find abandoned soldiers‚Äô huts and towers, civilian houses, old factories, as well as storage facilities. In some places furniture is still lying, labels and newspapers are scattered.

Unusual places of St. Petersburg in 2014

One of the most amazing places, according to tourists, in 2014 was the planetarium of St. Petersburg. Every year, a lot of people come here with one goal - to look at the sky. "Why is this an amazing place?" - you ask? This is because this planetarium consists of 6 full-fledged halls, each of which is of great interest to almost everyone. There are also special laboratories for conducting experiments in optics and mechanics, in general, it will be interesting for both adults and children. There is also a star room. This is where most visitors come. Here you can watch comets, meteor showers, planetary motion or just enjoy the starry sky. In fact, you can make a full journey into the open spaces. There is an observatory in the planetarium, where several powerful telescopes are located, with which you can observe a variety of space objects. The most valuable are night observations, which are carried out in an astronomical tower. This place is of great interest among tourists, and local residents sometimes do not mind looking here, especially since the price of a ticket for an adult is only 350 rubles, and for children and senior citizens only 150.

We can say with confidence that it is almost impossible to visit all the unusual places in St. Petersburg, because there are hundreds of them. However, you need to start somewhere, and it is up to you to decide exactly what it will be.
