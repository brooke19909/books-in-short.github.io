Do-it-yourself rugs made of yarn: master class

You can simply and quickly decorate your home interior using different methods, one of the most practical is to make a fluffy do-it-yourself rug from yarn. To make it is simple and, most importantly, not at all costly.





Rug from pompons

The functionality of such a product is high. It can become a bright decoration of the living room, a convenient coating for slippery tiles in the bathroom or even a street rug for giving.

We will need:

  • yarn of any color (but not less than 10 skeins);
  • building mesh of the desired size (you can use a grid for the carpet);
  • ruler;
  • scissors.

Getting to work.

  1. We cut out the square of the size we need from the construction grid. You can choose another form, here everything is decided by imagination. Mats made of yarn made in the shape of animals with their own hands look very cute and unusual. We outline the head, paws and tail with a pencil and cut it out, and after making the rug, the remaining parts of the product can be tied with a hook.
  2. A pompom can be made in several ways. For a larger volume, two cardboard disks are needed (as pom-poms for caps do), a circle is cut out in the center and both disks are wrapped with threads. Next, the edges of the threads should be cut, and the middle tightly pulled and tied. The pompom is ready. The second way is simpler. We wind the thread around the fingers folded together, carefully remove and tie in the middle, cut the ends of the threads with scissors. It turns out something that resembles a bow in shape.
  3. We make the necessary number of pompons. Be sure to leave the middle thread, with it we will then fix them to the rug. We start to work from an angle and move strictly in a straight line. To keep pompons better, it is better to pass the ends of the threads through the net several times.
  4. Now the ombre effect is in fashion, for it you can pick up yarn of similar shades and first tie pompons of dark tones, gradually turning to light ones.




Thick mat

Such do-it-yourself rugs made of yarn are very practical. They can be made from the remains of threads accumulated at home. We also select the thickness of the product on our own, choosing the option that is most comfortable for ourselves. The functionality of such an interior item is unlimited, it can either be laid on the floor, or made into a soft seat for your favorite chair or even a comfortable bedding for your pet.

We will need:

  • yarn (any texture and density);
  • knitting hook 12 mm.

Getting to knitting our rug.

  1. The threads need to be taken from several balls at once (in the total number of them there can be up to ten pieces).
  2. Based on the thickness of the resulting yarn assembly, we select a hook. It should be so large that the knitting is dense, but in moderation.
  3. Such a rug has a classic rectangular shape, so the chain of dialed air loops should correspond to the length of our product.
  4. Each subsequent row fits a loop into a loop, clinging to both of their sides. We tie the mat to the width we need and proceed to tying it.
  5. Here you will need a hook with a lower number (8-9 mm) so that the edge of the mat is tight. The strapping thread can be selected in color or contrast. 3-5 rows will be enough to seal and form a beautiful edge of the product.
  6. The do-it-yourself rug is practically made of yarn, any decor can be crocheted on it - flowers, petals of fresh greenery, it all depends on the size of your imagination.




Round rug

Our grandmothers also wove such rugs of yarn with their own hands. But the technology can be greatly simplified by making a weaving version of this product. We will need:

  • many old knit t-shirts;
  • Hula Hup;
  • scissors.

It is most convenient to weave from T-shirts (t-shirts) and other knitted garments that can be cut into long rings of fabric. This will allow you to fasten the ends of the tapes together during weaving with the help of a regular loop and will make the work as simple and convenient as possible.





  1. We cut the rings of the same length and width and distribute them in a circle strictly perpendicular to each other, the distance between the strips of knitwear should be 10-15 cm, no more.
  2. Prepare strips of fabric in advance, they will need a lot. Weaving starts from the central part of the circle, alternately skipping the thread down, and covering the next one from above.
  3. Such a rug of knitted yarn with your own hands can be done in one to two hours. In the end, do not forget to fix the knitted threads, cutting them in half and neatly tied in knots.

Shred rug

A very budget option, which will require a bit more time, but it will save you from the old trash in the closet and will become a pleasant decoration for any interior.

We will need:

  • a lot of old unnecessary things;
  • large hook;
  • sharp scissors.

To make rugs of tape yarn with your own hands, you must first prepare the original thread. To do this, we lay out our thing, gently open it at the seams, and begin to cut out long ribbons of fabric from it (the longer they are, the fewer the knots on the finished product). To avoid tangling, we immediately rewind our homemade thread into a ball.





Important points

If the color of the threads (old clothes) is different, it is better to rewind them in different tangles and knit them alternately. In the process, when you need to add a new thread, bend the first one and form a loop from it, into which you pass the second, tie a neat little knot. For knitting, it is better to choose a larger hook so that the finished product is not too dense.

Rug making:

  • we start the process from the middle, as in ordinary crochet; first we make a small chain, close it and then continue to knit the rows in a circle;
  • we make sure that the rows are smooth, not tightened;
  • we knit the product to the size we need, fasten the thread with a knot - and our knitted rug from yarn is already made with our own hands.




Pompom workshop

Each self-respecting craftswoman tries to decorate her product after its manufacture. Knots and other defects in work are most easily masked with fluffy pompons, there are several ways to make them:

  • classic - the thread is wound on disks, cut along the edges and connected in the center, the advantages of this method are the pompom turns out to be voluminous, the minuses - to wind the thread in this way for a long time;
  • fork - it is used in the manufacture of a large number of pompons, here the yarn is wound around the teeth of a fork or a similar device made on its own, it is also cut from the side and connected with an additional thread in the middle;
  • Rectangular is the easiest and fastest way for which the toilet paper sleeve will fit perfectly. It must be folded in half, stretch one thread for fastening along, and wind the rest along the entire length of the sleeve.

Cons that are important to consider

An important role in the manufacture of the rug is played by the choice of the right material. Each type of thread in contact with water can behave differently. Therefore, when purchasing yarn for knitting in a store, it is worth consulting with the seller about this. A product made from an old rag that has survived more than one wash is not so prone to deformation.





The disadvantages include the loss of time, because it‚Äôs much easier to go and buy a ready-made rug in the store. But he will no longer have personality and will become a common item in the interior.

pros

Do-it-yourself rugs made of yarn are easy to make, and most importantly - it is very economical. So, for example, for a rug in a medium-sized bathroom, you need only 2-3 skeins of thread. Even if you compare the price of new yarn and a finished rug in the store, the difference will be significant.

Skilled in this matter, craftswomen create real wicker masterpieces. But even in the absence of dexterity, do-it-yourself rugs from the remnants of yarn can be done exactly following the pattern. Such a product will always be exclusively designer, since the choice of color scheme and material for its manufacture remains with the craftswoman.
