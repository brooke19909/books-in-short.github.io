Where to withdraw money from a card in Vladivostok: VTB-24 ATMs

The list of VTB-24 ATMs in Vladivostok is quite extensive. For the convenience of the bank's customers, the terminals are installed on the territory of many grocery supermarkets and the largest shopping malls of the city. Below is a list of ATMs located in the busiest places, addresses and opening hours.

Installed on the territory of a bank branch

In Vladivostok, the VTB-24 ATM, number 397816, is located at: Russkaya Street, 39b. It is installed on the territory of the bank office branch. Available services: receiving cash in rubles, dollars, euros, making cash and non-cash payments, withdrawing cash in rubles. Terminal working hours: on weekdays from 09:00 to 19:00 without a break and on Saturday from 10:00 to 17:00 without a break.

ATM number 387700 is open around the clock. Installed at the same address.
