Dividends are a way to improve your financial condition.

Every financier knows a lot about dividends, but for those whose work is not related to economics and finance, this concept is a mystery. It will not be out of place to sort it out, since dividends are an additional source of profit, however, with some nuances. Suppose there is a successful company. It directs part of the profit received this year to the development of the company, and distributes the remainder (dividends) to those who have the right to do so - to the holders of shares. The amount of this income depends on decisions made at shareholders meetings. In Russia, many companies pay small dividends. However, large organizations strive to raise them, in this they are even supported by state authorities.

Dividends - this is the share of profit that the company pays to its shareholders after it settles with all taxes. It should be noted that obtaining this income is often not the main purpose of acquiring shares. The main thing here is the ability to discern their growth potential.

How to receive dividends?

To become the owner of a well-earned income, it is not necessary to hold shares for a whole year, you can purchase them at the moment when the register closes. This date is approved at a meeting of the Board of Directors. Usually it falls on spring time, and in the summer meetings of shareholders are held. There are different ways in which dividends can be paid. It:

- payments to a special brokerage account ;

- transfers to current accounts in banks;

- cash payment;

- Money transfers.

The fact is that during this period the market value of shares decreases by an amount that is proportional to the premium paid on them.

Payout frequency

The size and procedure for paying dividends are established by the Meeting of shareholders. This can occur quarterly, every 6 or 12 months. For example, this summer, many large organizations distributed income from a portion of the profits earned in the past, 2012. Accordingly, the payment of dividends 2013 will be made next year.

New rules

In 2014, significant changes will occur. Here is some of them:

  1. Income from shares will be distributed according to the new rules. Dividends will have to be paid even when the company reduces its authorized capital.
  2. Previously, the company established the payment procedure on its own, now it will be allowed only with non-monetary forms of payment. Dividends under the new amendments will have to be transferred by mail or transferred to a bank account.

There are other changes, the purpose of which is to improve the relationship between companies and shareholders.



So, now you know that the amount of dividends depends on the profit that the organization receives. If you have a desire to acquire shares in any company in order to receive good income, first carefully study everything related to its activities. Pay attention to the prospects of its development, stability in the market.
