The most popular Iskitima hotels

Iskitim is a small town located in the lap of the picturesque nature of the Novosibirsk region. Among the local attractions, the most famous are the Church of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker, the Church in honor of the Icon of the Blessed Virgin Mary, the Church of Our Lady of Vladimir, and the Holy Spring. Guests of the city have different travel goals, but everyone has a desire to stay for the night in a comfortable place. It is worth to get acquainted with the best Iskitim hotels.

Hotel Crystal

It is located next to the square, where a monument to the Great Patriotic War, at the address: Communist street, 57. This is one of the most popular hotels with affordable rooms. The cost of living is from 900 rubles per day. This price includes breakfast served directly to the apartment.

Accommodation is possible in the following rooms:

  • double rooms;
  • economy class;
  • standard;
  • Suite

All rooms are decorated in a modern style, equipped with comfortable beds. In addition, there is a folding sofa, coffee table, TV, mini fridge. Each of them has a separate bathroom, shower with bath accessories.

In addition to the excellent housing stock, the Iskitima Crystal Hotel is famous for its wonderful minibar, where you can have lunch and dinner. There are two banquet rooms (one for thirty people, the other for ninety).

Additional hotel services:

  • taxi call;
  • transfer;
  • laundry;
  • free parking.

Hotel and restaurant complex "Poseidon"

Applies to business class hotels. The cost of accommodation is from 2500 rubles per day.

The number of rooms consists of 44 rooms (total 88 beds). The apartments are single and double. All rooms have a set of necessary hotel furniture, beds with orthopedic mattresses. Everywhere a split system is installed, bathrooms are equipped. All guests receive a set of towels and toiletries.

Other services of the hotel complex:

  • Internet throughout;
  • pool;
  • spa room;
  • massage room;
  • beauty saloon;
  • solarium;
  • billiard room;
  • gym;
  • laundry;
  • secure parking;
  • safe;
  • children's playroom;
  • transfer.

The restaurant offers dishes of Russian and European cuisine, business lunches, a children's menu, room service. Banquet room for 130 guests.

The hotel "Poseidon" is located in Iskitim at the address: Southern microdistrict, 55A, building 1.
