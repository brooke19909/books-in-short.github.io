Spain, Palma de Mallorca: resort description, attractions, hotels, photos

In Europe there are many attractive regions that are interesting as a beach-sea holiday. Spain is very popular in this regard. Palma de Mallorca is the country's best resort, according to experienced travelers. Every tourist dreams of visiting this city.

A bit about the resort

Mallorca is one of the most famous and attractive islands of Spain. Palma de Mallorca is the main port and city of the Balearic Islands, which is located in the bay of the island. He is better known to health resort visitors as a major tourist center. Mallorca has gained unprecedented popularity as a place that is ideal for rock climbing, beach holidays, windsurfing, diving, and just for relaxing in the lap of beautiful nature. The local climate is very favorable for recreation. An interesting fact is that the island was loved not only by simple travelers, but also by members of the royal family. They were the first to appreciate all the advantages of the region.

Mallorca can be safely called the heart of the Belear Islands in Spain. And Palma de Mallorca is a real city of dreams, located on its coast. Currently, the capital of the Balearic Islands is home to 500,000 inhabitants. The local population is engaged in tourism, which is quite logical, because the island's airport receives 25 million visitors a year. And this is not all tourists. Approximately two million arrives by ferry. Members of the royal family regularly come to enjoy the beauties of Palma (Spain). The city is no less popular among the stars of cinema and music of world scale.

The history of Palma de Mallorca began even before our era. It was founded by the Romans. Then the city was captured by vandals, and after it fell under the rule of Byzantium. Palma de Mallorca was periodically raided by African tribes. Later, the Moors of Cordoba ruled, after which the king of Aragon came. Subsequently, the island became part of Spain.

How to get to the city?

Many tourists who decide to travel, are wondering how to get to the most famous island of Spain. Palma de Mallorca has its own international airport, located in the southeast of the city. Flights from 50 European cities and from Russia regularly arrive on it. You can get to the island‚Äôs capital from Moscow on Es Seven flights. During the high season, flights of Ural Airlines are additionally introduced. Travelers are on the road for about five hours. In season, ticket prices increase. If you want to save, you can find cheap flights to Barcelona. And from it small-sized planes fly to Palma de Mallorca. The frequency of flights is comparable to a taxi.

Getting to the resort by bus and train is also possible, but such options are not relevant. They are uncomfortable and take a lot of time. Yes, and their value is not happy. Experienced travelers still recommend making their choice in favor of air travel.

As we have already mentioned, there are sea routes to the island of Spain. In Palma de Mallorca, there is one of the largest ports in the Mediterranean Sea, in which numerous ships and cruise ships arrive daily. You can get to the resort from many mainland ports. By the way, In between Palma (Spain) and other islands of the Belear archipelago excellent ferry service is established. Ferries can also reach Palma de Mallorca from Alicante, Barcelona and Valencia.

Climatic conditions

The city is one of the most attractive places in Spain. Palma de Mallorca is popular with travelers due to its unique climate. In the city, as on the whole island, the seasons change very smoothly. Here you will never see sudden changes in temperature. Therefore, the resort does not have such a thing as a low and high season, which is typical for many tourist areas around the world. The climatic conditions of the region can be characterized as typically Mediterranean. You can plan a vacation in Mallorca at any time of the year, without fear that the weather could go bad. The formally high resort in the resorts of Palma (Spain) begins in May, and ends in October. It is believed that at this time the weather is most favorable. The air temperature in the city is usually above +27 degrees. The water is so well warmed that you can not worry and swim in the sea without restrictions, especially for children.

The advantage of the local climate is that it is very warm, but there is no sweltering heat. Comfortable weather favors not only beach vacations, but also sightseeing. According to tourists, in Palma de Mallorca (Spain) there is an ideal ratio of humidity and air temperature. The hottest weather is inherent in July and August. The warmest water in the sea becomes in August and September. At this time, its temperature reaches +25 degrees. In November and October, precipitation falls at the resort, which is why this period is considered the wettest.

Aquarium

Every year, many tourists visit the resort, not only for the sake of the beaches, but also for viewing interesting sights of Palma (Spain).

One of the interesting places is the aquarium and marine park, which are considered one of the best in Europe. Here in 55 aquariums live representatives of 700 species of marine inhabitants. It is better to visit the institution during the tour, thanks to which you can learn a lot from the life of underwater inhabitants. The duration of the tour is four hours. During this time, guests manage to get around five thematic areas. In 24 aquariums the inhabitants of the Mediterranean are represented, in others you can admire the inhabitants of tropical latitudes.

Cathedral

Palma is a city in Spain that is interesting for its historical sights. The main architectural monument of the center is the Cathedral of St. Mary, which was erected 800 years ago. The building was laid in 1230.

But the decoration of the interior and facades was completed only in the twentieth century. The cathedral is made in the Gothic style. The special structure of the windows gives the room an unusual look. It seems that the temple is filled with light. The exterior lighting and the interiors of the building were designed by Antonio Gaudi himself.

Almudine Palace

Another interesting place of Palma (Spain), the photo of which is given below, is the Almudain Palace, which is the oldest in the country. During the reign of the Arabs on the island, the palazio was a real fortress that protected the whole city. The architecture of the building is very characteristic of the Middle Ages. The rectangular structure has two paved towers. One of them is called the "Tower of the Angel", on it is a statue of the archangel Gabriel, who is considered the patron saint of Palma.

Currently, the Almudine Palace is the official residence of the royal family. Here is the commandant of the Balearic Islands. In addition, the building houses the National Museum. On the territory of the palace you can see a small courtyard called the Royal, which houses a fourteenth-century chapel, made in the Gothic style. At its foot there is a garden.

La lonja

Another building of the city, made in the Gothic style, is La Lonja. It was built in 1456. In those distant times, it housed the Trade Exchange, which was considered the center of the economic life of the city. The building is often called the symbol of the prosperity of Spanish commerce.

In the fifteenth century, it lost its meaning, and therefore they kept grain in it. Now the building is considered one of the most luxurious in the city. Within its walls, exhibitions, meetings, receptions and other official events are held. On the facade of the building is a statue of an angel, and the interior is notable for unusual spiral columns that look like palm trees.

Belver Castle

Another fortification of the city is Belver Castle, located on a 140-meter hill in the western part of the city. Its feature is that it is the only round building in the world, made in the Gothic style. Three towers rise above the fortress walls of the complex, and another one is located outside the fortifications, it is connected to the castle by a covered gallery. The architectural structure was erected in the fourteenth century, and at the turn of the eighteenth and nineteenth centuries there was a prison.

Currently, the building houses the Palma Museum. It‚Äôs worth a visit to the castle even in order to enjoy the beautiful view from the observation deck. From here you can see the bay and the whole city. And in clear weather you can even see the outlines of a neighboring island.

Zoo

Another place that tourists will enjoy is the zoo. It was created in 1998. On its territory, adults and children can admire birds, primates, predators and other representatives of the animal world. But the pride of the institution is the lemurs. The zoo has playgrounds for children, and for adults - recreational facilities.

Aquapark

No beach resort can be imagined without a water park. In Palma for tourists operates "Aqualand", in which you can visit various attractions, among which there is a black hole. In the park you will find many attractions and slides. For a family visit it is difficult to find a more interesting place.

Resort beaches

What else attracts tourists to Palma (Spain)? Whatever the sights in the city, the beaches of the resort are of most interest. They have long been attracting thousands of tourists here annually. The coast of Palma de Mallorca is a soft sand and a gentle beach. The beaches of the resort are deservedly awarded with the Blue Flags. The coast of the city has a very developed infrastructure. There is always cleanliness and order, and garbage is cleaned daily. But a resort of such a high level is always crowded with tourists. The flow of travelers never runs dry, so the local beaches are always crowded. One of the least crowded places is Can Pere Anthony Beach.

Municipal beaches of the resort are very popular, which are equipped with toilets, showers and sun loungers. True, they need to pay 10 euros for renting a sunbed and an umbrella. But for 15 euros you can buy your own umbrella in the store. Tourists note that almost all the beaches of the resort are free. An exception can be considered small sections of the coast, which are considered club zones of some establishments. Such VIP beaches are more secluded. Additional services are available on their territory. There are only three of these beaches on the coast of Palma de Mallorca.

A few words must be said about Cala Major. This small beach is located in the very center of the city. Numerous tourists are attracted here by the clear water and sandy shore.

Hotels Palma Mallorca (Spain)

Any tourist will be interested in the question of where to stay in the city. There are a lot of hotels in Palma de Mallorca (Spain), since the region is fully tourism oriented. Among them, you can find not only expensive establishments, but also the cheapest hostels. The best hotels are located on the coast. Well, and more budgetary institutions - 10-15 minutes walk from the sea. The rooms, even in three-star hotels, are equipped with TVs and air conditioning. As for food, usually in such hotels a buffet is practiced. It serves fresh fruits, vegetables and seafood. Accommodation in such hotels per day is 30 euros.

Four-star complexes are characterized by more comfortable apartments. And the range of services offered in them is much wider. It has everything you need: from spas to laundries. Room rental in such an institution per day is at least 50 euros. The resort has many chic five-star complexes. But living in them is quite expensive. The minimum room rate is from 130 euros per day and above.

Experienced travelers recommend for families or companies to consider renting a villa. The city offers very different offers of such a plan: from the most modest to the chic. Tourists can choose for themselves an apartment for any wallet. On average, renting a villa for a week is possible for 520 euros.

Resort Attractions

At the resort you can not only spend time idly on the beaches, but also go surfing. The most favorable time for this is July and August. At any time of the year you can go diving. The underwater world off the coast of Palma de Mallorca is rich in coral reefs, caves and sunken ships. And there are a lot of marine life here. Fans of shopping can visit the numerous boutiques in the city center where you can buy things at the best price-quality ratio.
