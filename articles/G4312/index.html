Children's Health Camp "Star"

The unfavorable situation in the cities leads to a constant deterioration in the well-being of their inhabitants, and this is especially acute for children. Perhaps there are no such parents who would not want to protect their child from an unfavorable environment. Therefore, many go on vacation to various health resorts, move to suburban homes or send a child to the village, where for certain reasons his days are not much different from each other. However, not everyone has this opportunity, and for many, summer children's camps are the way out of this situation.





Traditional holiday

Thriving during the Soviet era, camps for children did not completely disappear after its collapse, which significantly makes life easier for parents. The traditions of such a holiday are slowly being revived. And, despite the meager funding, this is still a great opportunity to diversify the child's pastime, while strengthening his health. In addition, holidays in the camp have a lot of advantages.

Immunity increase

Vitamins, fresh air, the sun and the beneficial microclimate of the forest or the sea - all this helps to strengthen the health of the child. Moreover, the cost of such a vacation is much less in comparison with resorts.



Socialization

The constant environment of children instills in the child not only sociability, but also independence. He learns to make decisions, maybe not always right, but practice does its job. And the awareness of responsibility for committed acts teaches you to take any issue seriously.

Discipline

During the holidays, the child gets used to the daily routine, schedule, the follow-up of which is constantly monitored by counselors. In addition, educators do not give the child excessive indulgence, to cross certain boundaries. However, not all elders are the same as their methods, so first ask other parents for their opinions on the sanatorium team.

Benefits

Summer camps initially imply well-thought-out programs for health improvement, balanced nutrition, entertainment and accommodation. You do not have to worry about the possible use of fast food and other harmful products. Although there are exceptions to the rules, however, it depends directly on the chosen vacation spot. Many parents experience some fear and excitement when sending their child to a sanatorium, and sometimes even refuse to travel, preferring to take him to the village with their grandparents. But these experiences have no good reason, because the benefits of such vacations are undeniable. Yes, you yourself will see it when your child returns home matured, cheerful, rested and full of positive impressions.
