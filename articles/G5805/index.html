Yacht club "Rubin" (Tyumen) - all the pleasures in one place

On the shore of the stunningly beautiful and very large lake of the Tyumen region, St. Andrew's, there is a yacht club. The place ideally combines familiar comfort surrounded by unique nature, the necessary privacy after the noise of a big city and a large selection of entertainment.

Accommodation

The Rubin Yacht Club (Tyumen) offers several accommodation options.

Standard houses are designed for 8-10 guests. They have two bedrooms, a large living room and a toilet with a washbasin. The bedroom has a double bed, bedside tables and a wardrobe. The living room is equipped with upholstered furniture and satellite TV. In the cold season, the apartments are heated. A barbecue area is organized near each such house. This is a large table with comfortable benches and barbecue facilities.





The yacht club Rubin (Tyumen) offers guests cottages of superior comfort. Each of them can accept up to eight people. The house has several bedrooms, a living room with a seating area, a TV with satellite antenna and a music center. The dining room is equipped with modern appliances, including a large refrigerator. The bathroom consists of a toilet, shower and bath. In winter, the cottage is heated.

For VIP-clients in the yacht club there are separate log houses for two guests. It is possible to organize two additional places. Everything is provided for a comfortable stay: a roomy refrigerator, an electric kettle, a microwave, a TV with a satellite dish, a music center, a toilet with a bathtub and shower.



The Lesnaya Polyana house is designed for ten guests (two places are provided additionally). The apartments have a fridge, satellite TV, toilet and shower. In winter, "Forest Glade" is heated.

Prices range from 4,000 rubles to 13,000 rubles per day.

Baths at the Rubin Yacht Club

The most visited place in the yacht club is saunas. There are only three of them. One of them is famous for its hydromassage pool on the open veranda.

Each bath can comfortably accommodate up to 15 people. For your convenience, there is a cozy living room with a large dining table and a kitchen area. It is equipped with modern appliances. The cost of an hour is from 700 rubles.

A barbecue area with tables and benches is organized near each bathhouse.

Entertainment

The yacht club Rubin (Tyumen) offers many options for both summer and winter holidays. Each vacationer will be able to choose something to their liking.

On the land

Fans of "shooters" are invited to modern paintball. It is possible to organize corporate paintball, entertainment, sports and winter. Equipment, including masks, markers, balls, uniforms, etc., is issued by the organizers. Battle scenarios are developed taking into account the wishes of the customer.

Of all the entertainment, ATV riding is most loved by the club's guests. They can be rented at the club. All necessary equipment is issued.

For the most part, children, but their parents too, test their stamina, courage and vestibular apparatus in a rope park. The attraction has three difficulty levels. The first, children's, is a meter from the ground and has just over ten obstacles. The second level is family. The path passes at a height of four meters, there are as many obstacles, but they are an order of magnitude more complicated. And the third level - a height of 20 meters (above the standard five-story building) - is divided into several tiers. They say that only an active athlete can do all the obstacles, but the dashing trouble began ....

The club has several sites for football, basketball and volleyball. A children's holiday is also organized: a whole town of swings, carousels and other attractions.

On the water

Among the water activities that the yacht club Rubin provides (Tyumen), jet skiing is given reviews, their number is off the charts. Depending on the purpose of the trip, you can rent both a standing jetski and a sitting one. The former are suitable for high-speed riding, while the latter allow you to perform various tricks, including jumping of varying difficulty.



The yacht club Rubin (Tyumen), prices can be obtained from instructors, offers to try yourself in a new sport - kitesailing. It is very spectacular and extreme. The bottom line is that a kite (kite) is controlled by a person on a board. When the hands feel the power of the wind, which can be controlled, emotions go wild.

The beach area with a length of more than 700 meters is equipped with sun loungers, awnings and dressing areas. There are also bar areas on the beach.

It is possible to ride on a motor or rowing boat, catamaran, sailing yacht, boat, etc. Sports equipment rental - from 250 rubles per hour.

The yacht club Rubin (Tyumen) has its own pier and boathouse. As well as all the conditions (including a cafe with a large hall) for organizing and conducting trainings, rallies, banquets, festivals, presentations, etc.

In winter

For guests having a rest in the winter and in the off-season, a well-equipped gymnasium and several billiard rooms are open.



The club offers standard winter entertainment. This is all kinds of skiing, sleigh rides, ice skating on a frozen pond. Tubing and snowmobiling and snowboarding trips are also available.
