Scheme

How to get a loan for officially non-working persons? Even for risk categories of citizens, banks have special products. But the credit card additionally provides for a number of payments: from servicing plastic to cashing out funds. The process is simplified if the client has long been using the services of a bank and has a good credit history. Then you can simply ask for an increase in the limit of funds.





How to get a loan without official work on more attractive terms? You can provide collateral and receive a program with an individual interest rate. At low interest rates loans are provided for the purchase of durable goods.

Credit history

Before you get a loan without official work, you should make sure that the client fulfills the main requirement of the bank - it has a good credit history in the past. Deceive a financial institution does not work. Each bank checks the CI of its customers. The presence of delays in the payment of arrears in the past may be a reason for refusing cooperation.

CI is considered good if the client repaid the loans on time and in full. The category of the best also includes customers who have had one-time delays in paying debts for a period of not more than three days. According to statistics, the main reason for such delinquencies is that the client simply forgot to pay off the loan. Therefore, many banks send SMS reminders to their customers.





Non-disciplined are borrowers who repay a debt with a delay of 90 days.
