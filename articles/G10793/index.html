Rest on the recreation center "Aviator": how to get there and reviews

Every year, people go on vacation to make life more colorful and memorable. They are trying in advance to plan their vacation so that it goes as useful as possible. After all, time spent in the fresh air also brings health and well-being. Therefore, the rest at the Aviator recreation center is remembered by many guests for pleasant moments and beautiful nature. Everything has been created here for the maximum comfort of guests and excellent leisure. Families with young children also come to the base, as there is everything you need. Children under 6 years old can stay with their parents free of charge, subject to the use of existing beds.

basic information

Rest at the Aviator recreation center can be a great vacation or an opportunity to see new corners of the country. The institution is located in a beautiful place called the village of Tsarevshchina. Tourists will be able to admire not only the beautiful view of the Volga River, but also the Zhiguli Mountains. The place is characterized by clean air, so rest here will have a beneficial effect on health and general condition. For your stay, you will find comfortable rooms and excellent food. On the basis of properly selected staff who are responsible for a number of pressing tasks and issues.

The highlight of the base was the landing stage, in which there are 20 cabins with all the necessary conditions for living. It offers a gorgeous view of the river. In addition, you can spend your vacation at the Aviator recreation center in a summer house or guest house. All buildings meet the necessary requirements. In summer houses, amenities are not provided inside. You can also rent a cottage on two floors. It has all the necessary furniture, utensils and appliances.

How to get to the base

It will not be difficult for tourists to get to their destination. It is enough to know where the Aviator recreation center is located to get there by car or other means of transport. About 40 kilometers from Samara by car to the base.

On the way, you will need to cross the river called Juice and continue moving towards the village of Tsarevshchina. On the way you will meet several other bases and an experimental plant. You can focus on the recreation center "Island", because after it will be a race on the bridge. Then you need to turn left, a sign appears with the desired base.

Additional information can be obtained from the employees of the holiday home. On the official website for this, the phone number of the Aviator recreation center is indicated, by which you can contact. You can also get from Samara by minibuses under numbers 113, 392 and 389 or by buses 78, 79. Landing takes place in Samara in the Frunze Polyana district. By transport you should go to the station "Village Tsarevshchina", and from there take a taxi to the base.

Visitors reviews

For a long time, the Aviator recreation center has been collecting positive reviews. Most of the guests are very pleased with the vacation they spent here. They celebrate the beautiful nature and fresh air. Many tourists like living conditions and service. Therefore, many guests come here again to relax. Most often, the reviews indicate that the necessary attention was received from the staff. But there are cases that not all guests were satisfied with the service. More often, such an opinion is connected with rest in the winter. But about a summer vacation, almost all visitors respond positively, it goes just first-class. They indicate that vacation at the Volga is not inferior to sea vouchers.

Additional services

Rest at the Aviator recreation center can take place exactly as the guest himself wants. After all, absolutely all conditions have been created for this. Tourists can simply relax on the beach by the river or choose an active holiday. For leisure, a lot of options are offered. Fans of fishing will be able to appreciate this base, since here they have something to do at any time of the year. You can rent a boat, catamaran, boat, pedalo, or even ski. Sports enthusiasts also have plenty to choose from. They can visit the court, rent balls, as well as chess and checkers. Any evening will become even more comfortable if it passes near the barbecue. Therefore, it can also be rented for the rest. Many visitors like to compete by playing billiards or table tennis. For maximum comfort, guests are invited to visit the restaurant, coffee bar or bath. There is a conference room at the base, so you can hold meetings and important meetings here.
