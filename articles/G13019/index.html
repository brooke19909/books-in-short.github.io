Belorussky Train Station: the metro station closest to it, some history and interesting facts

Residents and guests of the capital know the Belorussky railway station, which is not only very beautiful, but also a strategically important object. To visit this place, you need to know not so much! So, to your attention the following information: Belorussky railway station, metro station, which is located next to it, ground routes and some interesting literary and historical facts.





How to get to the station by public transport?

Based on the fact that trains departing from the platforms of this station leave for the west, southwest and northeast of Russia, as well as to foreign countries, a lot of people come here. Not all of them have their own cars, and not everyone also drives a taxi. It was the same in the days of the Soviet Union. Therefore, next to the geographical object called Belorussky Station - Belorusskaya metro station. Note that there are two stations with the same name on the Zamoskvoretskaya metro line: Radial and Koltsevaya. It is very convenient that directly from Radial you can get into the station building. But the exit from Koltseva is convenient for those who want to be in the square at the station or for those who are heading for Gruzinsky Val Street.





It is also convenient that the Belorussky railway station, metro station and bus and trolley bus stops are very close to each other. Accordingly, buses with numbers 0 and 12, as well as trolley buses with numbers 12, 56 and 18, 1 and 78, are perfect for those who are uncomfortable to get on the subway. By the way, for those who are looking for the Belorussky station (metro station) for the first time - a card to help. It is not necessary to carry a paper tome everywhere - a smartphone will completely replace it.

Belorussky railway station (metro station): where can I go?

Going down the subway, you can quickly get to Vnukovo, Sheremetyevo and Domodedovo airports. True, no transfers will not do. But there are special aeroexpress trains that quickly reach the desired airport right from the station. Quite quickly from here you can get to other stations of Moscow.

A bit of history

Almost everyone knows that the metro on this site was built on the principle: under the Belorussky railway station - the Belorusskaya metro station. This station was opened in 1938. During World War II, defenders of the Motherland left for the front from here, and in 1945 a train arrived here, which delivered the soldiers who conquered Berlin.
