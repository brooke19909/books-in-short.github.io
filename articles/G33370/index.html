Pension "Volga" (Nizhny Novgorod Region): reviews, prices, photos

More recently, it has become fashionable to relax in Russia. Its boundless lands are scattered with many amazing and even outlandish regions with clean air, picturesque natural resources, winding rivers and crystal clear lakes. A huge advantage of spending holidays in our country is an acceptable pricing policy, lack of acclimatization, native language and culture.





Increasingly, urban residents tend to relax in country hotels, escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life and enjoy the surrounding silence. There is such an extraordinary place called the Volga boarding house (Nizhny Novgorod region). They come here as whole families with a bunch of children and spend the weekend fun, friendly and positive. Nearby there is an equipped beach (Volga River) with parking for water transport, an area for organizing picnics and various events. The sincere atmosphere, friendly staff and the presence of comfort set up for a favorable pastime. There will be no time to be bored and bored.

Food concept





Pension "Volga" (Nizhny Novgorod Region) offers guests to relax and have fun, and professional chefs will take care of the food. Country pension "Volga" (Nizhny Novgorod region) - health center





In 2014, the grand opening of a new innovative complex with a swimming pool, numerous cosmetology and treatment rooms took place. Now this is not just a recreation center, but a real sanatorium that provides professional medical assistance to improve the appearance. Qualified stylists and massage therapists work for you, ready to bring your physical and mental state into full order.





In the presence of a turbosolarium, Russian, Turkish and Finnish baths, a salt cave, a cedar barrel, anti-aging and anti-cellulite wrap, all types of cosmetology services. Not all boarding houses in the Nizhny Novgorod Region have such services. Prices for the procedures are quite reasonable, although they are not included in the total cost of living.

Corporate vacation





Business travelers often call this complex a business center, or rather, an ideal place for organizing psychological trainings, symposia, important negotiations and trainings.
