How to choose a good graphics tablet

Those who are not indifferent to art and painting enjoy spending time drawing, even if they are deprived of talent. For some, this is workdays, while for others it‚Äôs an activity for the soul, and sometimes both.





Professional artists can create anything, from simple sketches to complex graphic designs. Today's technology allows you to manipulate the brush as you like. One of the striking examples of modern trends are graphic tablets, or digitizers, which allow you to digitize drawings made by hand, and in real time. The main task of this device is to provide the user with a convenient and multi-functional tandem of traditional drawing and computer processing.

So, let's try to figure out which graphic tablet for drawing is better, consider the main characteristics of such devices and designate the most sensible models, taking into account the opinions of experts and reviews of ordinary users.

To begin with, we will determine the manufacturers of such devices and the market situation in this segment.

Manufacturers

Despite the abundant filling of the premium segment with apple and European devices, Asian models are considered the best graphics tablets. A good half of companies have long been working in the IT field and have an excellent base for the production of such gadgets. Others just came into the world, but with serious, and justified, claims to leadership in their segment.

If you ask any professional artist or designer which graphic tablet is better, then the models from the Wacom company will surely appear in the top three of his own list. The brand, as they say, ate a dog on such devices and offers its consumers a lot of options: from a simple gadget for amateurs to multifunctional and complex devices for specialists in their field.





In order to answer the question of which graphic tablet is better to choose, it is worth paying attention to the list of manufacturers that will help determine the purchase. All the participants were distinguished by the release of high-quality and price-balanced gadgets and the lowest percentage of negative reviews on certain models.

Manufacturers of the best graphics tablets:

  • Wacom.
  • Huion.
  • "Genius."
  • Ugee.
  • Hitachi
  • "Trust".

Choosing any manufacturer from this list, you will not be mistaken. All models from these companies are assembled with high quality, have a good set of functionality and a reasonable price for their characteristics.

Consider a few specific models from the above brands.

Wacom Intuos Pro L Large

In a professional environment, Intuos Pro L Large from Wacom is called the best graphics tablet. You can‚Äôt call him inexpensive, but he is worth the money spent and works out with interest.





The device was distinguished by an impressive working surface area - 300 by 190 mm and chic functionality, including multi-touch, gestures and pen drawing. Professionals especially value this model for absolute accuracy and easy control. The gadget is perfect for those who want to reveal themselves in design and art, as well as in photography.

Gadget features

The model has a proprietary and by far the best program for a graphic tablet - Grip Pen. This technology allows you to distinguish more than 2000 levels of stylus pressure, and taking into account the angle of inclination, which guarantees not only clarity of the picture, but also the naturalness of the picture.

Another distinguishing feature of the device is its controls. A competent functional ring and auxiliary keys will greatly facilitate the sculpting of masterpieces and save a lot of time. In short, the Intuos Pro L Large model is not just a good graphics tablet, but a pearl in its segment.





Advantages of the model:

  • light weight, as well as transportability;
  • large working area;
  • stylus without power (wireless);
  • Sync both via USB and Wi-Fi;
  • convenient and intuitive controls;
  • stand for the stylus.

Disadvantages;

  • the price is too high for a domestic consumer;
  • the stylus quickly grinds (it is better to have in reserve);
  • the surface is poorly protected from scratches (only careful handling).

Estimated cost - about 37,000 rubles.

Huion 1060 Plus

Another good graphics tablet from a relatively young company, Huion. The brand added a lot of headache to venerable regulars of this segment. Model 1060 Plus literally broke into the market and properly pushed competitors with its high-quality component and a very affordable price tag.





The device has a rather big working surface - 250 by 160 mm, and wireless stylus control. The matrix recognizes the accuracy of manipulations very well, and the response to the styles is barely perceptible. Among the other advantages of the model, one can note the capacious battery of the stylus, which allows working without problems up to 800 hours on a single charge, 12 additional controls, which can be programmed for almost any action and convenient touch soft keys.

Distinctive features of the model

As for the degree of sensitivity to pressing, everything is also good here: 2048 levels are more than enough. In addition, this good graphics tablet is equipped with a USB interface and a slot for external SD media (up to 64 GB). Users respond very warmly about the model. Especially she liked the beginner artists who are not yet ready to pay substantial money for a premium device, but want to maximize their creativity to the professional environment.

Pluses of model:

  • good looks;
  • lightweight stylus, despite the capacious battery;
  • multifunctionality;
  • perfect value for money.

Minuses:

  • the stylus is deprived of the eraser familiar to such devices;
  • the matrix does not distinguish between the angles of the pen.

Estimated price - about 10,000 rubles.

Genius EasyPen i405

Despite its more than affordable price tag, this good graphics tablet is enviable in both amateur and professional environments. Judging by the reviews of the owners, the model liked many of its ergonomics. The device will easily pull any drawing, drawing or editorial work, and the sculpting process itself will be most convenient for the artist.





The working surface of the digitizer does not differ in large area - only 140 by 102 mm, but this is quite enough for ordinary workpieces or in those cases when the creative impulse came suddenly, and it took too long to ‚Äúdisperse‚Äù some serious tool. Yes, and as a pocket easel, the model will fit perfectly if you are used to traveling in search of inspiration.

Tablet features

The matrix of the device provides 1024 levels of pressure sensitivity, and many soft keys (28 pieces) scattered around the entire perimeter of the working area will greatly simplify the creative process. The model feels great with all operating systems and is easily installed on both Windows and Macintosh. In addition, the manufacturer supplies its device with a detailed instruction manual not only in text but also in video format, as well as a host of templates for creating presentations, collages and simple drawings.

Advantages of the model:

  • affordable price tag;
  • nice looking appearance;
  • ergonomic design;
  • wireless pen;
  • rich scope of delivery (software and templates).

Disadvantages:

  • the stylus is powered only by AA elements (finger batteries);
  • not good synergy with venerable Photoshop.

Estimated cost - about 3,000 rubles.

Ugee M708 (Parblo)

The Parblo series is one of the most popular among other brand devices. This inexpensive gadget will be a great assistant for those who have just started to get acquainted with design and art drawings and decided to try their hand at computer drawing.





The M708 received a fairly spacious working area - 250 by 160 mm and 8 programmable controls. The gadget matrix easily recognizes 2048 levels of stylus pressure, and the pen itself is equipped with two additional function buttons.

The device connects to a PC via a USB cable, and the stylus itself is devoid of wires. In addition, complete with the device, the manufacturer put a lot of useful documentation and drivers for all occasions. So there will be no problems with any versions of Windows or Mac. It should also be noted that in the box, in addition to the usual set for the pen, there are eight removable nozzles, special tools for replacing them and convenient gloves for drawing.

Distinctive features of the device

Judging by the gadget by user reviews, the model is deprived of any critical flaws, and high-quality assembly, multifunctionality and a very affordable price tag only add credibility and popularity to it. Some owners complain that the stylus battery is running out too quickly, but the problem is solved by buying another pen (of the same brand) with a more capacious battery. Someone also noticed a slight delay in response in some graphic editors, since this does not apply to Photoshop.

Pluses of model:

  • affordable cost;
  • original appearance;
  • good ergonomic performance;
  • an attractive feature set for a budget gadget;
  • high sensitivity pen.

Minuses:

  • stylus battery runs out quickly;
  • a bit late response in some graphic editors (not in Photoshop).

Estimated price - about 5000 rubles.
