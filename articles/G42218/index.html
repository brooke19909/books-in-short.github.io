Home cinemas with wireless speakers: connection, setup, reviews

The audio equipment segment confirms that modern technologies change not only functionality, but also the meaning of many familiar concepts. So, more recently, home theaters have been associated with complexes in the form of a bundle of audio and video systems. Today, this concept often means multichannel acoustics, equipped with a modern option. The latest technological trend, which has been firmly entrenched in the market, has become home theaters with wireless speakers, which greatly simplify approaches to connecting audio systems. Such devices appeared several years ago, but only recently have wireless modules come closer to the traditional wired circuit in terms of sound transmission quality.





Wireless connection

In organizing the operation of a wireless multicomponent home theater complex, you can go one of two ways: use an integrated module in the form of a receiver or choose a specialized but separate signal transmission point. In the first case, Bluetooth is used, which can be equipped with a home theater system for wireless connection. Therefore, it is important to immediately provide for the inclusion of this module. With its help, acoustics are connected not only to the television panel, but also to game consoles, as well as other components of the audio system.

The second option provides for a more thorough and favorable connection in terms of signal translation quality. In this case, a special router is used that supports the wireless information transfer function. Some playback devices are equipped with a WLAN module (comes as an addition to wired channels). Sometimes there are even Wi-Fi receivers that provide key protection. One way or another, using a LAN or WLAN adapter, you can connect the network output of the Hi-Fi equipment to a transmitter that will be connected to the router. In this configuration, home theater systems with wireless acoustics provide the highest quality sound, which, in principle, is possible when a conventional cable is rejected.





Hardware Setup

And in the case of the router, and when using the integrated Bluetooth module, you must correctly calibrate the system so that it provides maximum capabilities. At the same time, you should not initially refuse to communicate via Bluetooth due to its loss to the adapters in terms of sound quality. Such a configuration can justify itself if the home theater setup is focused on connecting a smartphone. The transmission process must use the A2DP profile for audio distribution. Sound quality can be maintained using the unlicensed SBC audio codec. However, its capabilities with a bitrate of 128 kbps will not suit any music lover.

In the case of adapters, it remains only to choose the optimal communication channel, after making sure that all the components included in the system support a specific connection format. Such operation schemes may be inconvenient due to the inclusion of additional devices in the complex, but in terms of quality, this is the best wireless scheme by which a home theater can function. The review below will make the right choice in favor of equipment that supports cableless connectivity.





Sony model BDV-N9100W reviews

The set of the Japanese manufacturer includes traditional components represented by four speaker columns, a subwoofer, a center channel, a radio set-top box and a head unit. First of all, users note the high-quality implementation of the assembly - all components are made at a high level. As for the method of connecting via the radio channel, it does not cause any complaints, which, however, is explained by the combined method of communication. Still, the Sony brand home cinema system provides for the use of a wired connection of the front satellites. Nevertheless, the owners emphasize the full possibility of operating the system in conjunction with mobile devices using Wi-Fi.

Philips CSS7235Y Reviews

Philips has made a bet on Bluetooth with support for streaming music. Realizing that the module is not able to fully embody the advantages of high-quality wireless sound, the developers have introduced several technologies designed for a basic increase in acoustic properties. And users highly appreciated the result of the work done. In particular, many people praise the Philips home theater for using the Clear Sound crystal sound technology, which allows you to reproduce every note in its original quality with maximum accuracy.





The possibility of connecting a smartphone to the system is also noted, but the NFC system will be supported, which will facilitate the management process. That is, the mobile device in this case acts not only as a participant in the system broadcasting sound, but also as a means of control. True, the Philips home cinema in this configuration is still not able to fully reveal its acoustic potential at the proper level.

LG model LHB675 reviews

LG also implemented a fairly powerful component base, which caused a lot of positive feedback. In terms of wireless quality, a variety of features and capabilities are also noted. For example, home theater systems with wireless speakers from LG in the performance of LHB675 are equipped with advanced Bluetooth Standby technology. Its feature is the support of the standby mode. According to users, this development allows you to use the basic default settings, without the need for a connection for each session separately.





Conclusion

Acoustics in modern models involves working with large volumes of information, which allows it to provide high-quality clear sound. And the regularly increasing requirements for communication tools that provide interaction between different devices are quite understandable. In this regard, home cinemas with wireless acoustics seem something inappropriate, since the lack of high-quality cables will not allow you to reproduce sound in a clean and surround way. Nevertheless, manufacturers are increasing the quality level of the technical performance of modules transmitting an audio signal, which, of course, is reflected in the result. This is evidenced by bundles of adapters with routers designed specifically for audio signals, as well as Bluetooth modules that optimize wireless acoustics control processes.
