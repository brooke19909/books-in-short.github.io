Hotel Marabout Sousse Hotel 3 * (Tunisia, Sousse): photos, reviews

Goa, the United Arab Emirates, Turkey and Egypt are countries where most domestic tourists who prefer foreign resorts spend their vacations. But, of course, these are not the only southern states that offer Russians a decent vacation and a lot of pleasant experiences. For example, our compatriots can have a good time in exotic Tunisia. Trips here are usually inexpensive. Relax in Tunisia can be very good.

One of the three largest cities in this tropical country is Sousse. In addition to the magnificent southern landscapes and the warm gentle sea, this Tunisian resort is also notable for its many interesting sights. Hotels in Sousse, of course, built a lot. At the same time, most of the local hotels belong to the three-star class and are very well suited for not too demanding tourists traveling to the resort mainly to sunbathe on the beach, and not spend time in the room. In this regard, Marabout Sousse Hotel 3 * is no exception . Reviews about this hotel on the web are mixed. But the cost of living here is relatively inexpensive.





Location Features

This hotel is located directly in Sousse, near its center. This modern hotel is built on the seashore. The proximity to the beach is, according to many tourists, one of the main advantages of the Marabout Sousse Hotel 3 * (Tunisia, Sousse ) . Of course, this hotel is very well located in relation to the airport. The latter is located only 15 kilometers from it. That is, tourists can get to the hotel after arrival by transfer bus in just 10-15 minutes. At the same time, rattling airplanes never fly over this hotel.

The infrastructure of the city in the immediate vicinity of the hotel is well developed. In addition to shops and cafes, there are all kinds of entertainment venues in the area. To the capital of Tunisia from Sousse and the hotel is quite far. The distance to it is 140 kilometers.
