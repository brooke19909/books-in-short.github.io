How to make runes with your own hands: master class

If you want to learn how to make runes with your own hands, you need to have at least basic knowledge about them.

Magical science has existed among us since ancient times. Beginning as magic, it gradually flowed into the concept of "magic." The people who possessed it at the time of the Inquisition were, for the most part, burnt at the stake. But those who managed to survive transferred their knowledge to others and thus left them for centuries. Today, there are few people with magical abilities, but all of them perfectly possess their skills.

In general, magical practice is a certain position of the body and consciousness in space and time: it does not require any charmed objects or amulets. However, man is a living creature, subject to mood changes and under their influence. Especially so that this does not interfere with the qualitative manifestation of magic, special objects were created. Thanks to them, any magician, no matter how bad his mood may be, can tune in to the desired wave and enter the astral faster.

Runic magic is one of the varieties of magic. It uses a lot of enchanted items, but the most effective and often used are runes. Used in a set consisting of plates and a runic alphabet, they are able to give out much more information than other types of objects of magical property.

Where can I get runes?

There are three ways to get a runic kit for yourself:

  1. In souvenir shops or specialty stores. Sets are implemented as items for fortune telling, but they do not have sufficient magical power.
  2. Practicing magicians. They sell runes already consecrated.
  3. Make runes with your own hands from stones, clay or wood. However, to do this, everything must be done in strict accordance with the proposed instructions. But in return, you will get plates that are talking only on you.

In any of these cases, you can become the full owner of the runic set.

Varieties

Runes are made of a variety of materials, with each of them having its own magical meaning. It can be distinguished:

  1. Created from wood. Such runes are the most revered and most versatile. They can be applied daily.
  2. Carved in stone. If you have not decided yet what and how to make runes with your own hands, then you can choose a stone. It is ideal for all kinds of impacts.
  3. Made of leather. Ideal for those who want to improve their financial situation.
  4. Clay runes. They are most suited to fortune telling on earthly matters. Particularly good polymer base. It is believed that perfumes are most readily included in it, with which later it is possible to establish close contact.
  5. Black magicians make runes from the bones of animals or deceased people, human teeth or animals, from nails.

Correct storage

So, you cut out your set, also dealt with the question of how to make an amulet (runes) with your own hands. Now another, no less important question arises: how to store them correctly?

Here the most important condition is complete secrecy of the location of the runic set. This is due to such a nuance as the storage of your personal life force. With the correct execution of all stages in the runes, part of your strength must be preserved, which is necessary for successful and fruitful communication with spirits.

If you fall into the wrong hands (even relatives), your power can be used both against you and against another person. With the first option, you can die, with the second - you get seriously ill. To prevent this situation, no one should be allowed to see your kit.

All runes should be stored in a special bag. It can be from different materials. On it you can apply the image of Odin or any other Scandinavian god. This pattern will give extra protection to your records.

Where to get clay for making plates?

Before you make runes with your own hands in stages, especially from clay, you should correctly find places to search for material. You can find it in the following places:

  1. In the store - in the finished form. The easiest and most unreliable option. Clay will not receive the necessary charge, and the runes themselves will be deprived of their individuality and full strength.
  2. In the form of dry mixes. They are sold in hardware stores and diluted at home. However, such clay will also not be effective enough, as it will remain dead.
  3. Outdoors. It should be sought on slopes, in lowlands and other places saturated with moisture. Such clay, obtained by labor, will receive a maximum charge and will remain alive even after processing.




Clay preparation rules

After the clay has been brought home, it should be shaped into a future plate. Then the workpiece is wiped with a soft object and laid out on a lighted place or near the heater for drying. The process takes about a week. In this case, it is important to cover each plate with a bag with low air access for a couple of days. After solidification, the bag is removed.

At the end of drying, all products are fired. This procedure is performed as follows. Sand is laid on a solid cast-iron surface, on top - plates and a pot. Heating should be done slowly: from a low temperature to the highest. The heating process should last 6 hours.

Before you make the runes with your own hands from clay, the workpieces must be completely dried.
