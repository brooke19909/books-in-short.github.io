Senator Hotel 3 * Apartments (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): overview, rooms and reviews of tourists

Ayia Napa, located in the south-east of Cyprus, 41 km from Larnaca, is one of the most popular resorts in Cyprus. Luxurious beaches, picturesque bays with crystal clear water, a wonderful climate, fun, vibrant nightlife turn this corner into a favorite vacation spot not only for native Cypriots, but also for tourists from many countries.





It is believed that Ayia Napa is a holiday for gourmets, beach people and inveterate party-goers. In this ultra-modern city of contrasts, restless restaurant and club life mixed with the religious sights of hoary antiquity. But people who want a relaxed and serene pastime should also come. Here, in all its fullness, the joy of the long-awaited vacation will be felt both by young people and families with children, single travelers and elderly tourists. The main thing is to correctly develop a route along which you will get acquainted with this truly paradise, and also choose a suitable place to stay.





Ayia Napa is a resort famous for its high level of hotel services. Senator Hotel 3 * is one of the most popular holiday accommodation options.

About transport in Ayia Napa

Despite the small size of the resort, tourists have to resort to public transport, which is represented by buses and taxis. But they are usually used rather for trips to neighboring cities or for traveling around the area, vacationers share.

Buses depart from the central city square, which is located about 500 m from Senator Hotel 3 *. They stop at the main attractions and then follow to nearby towns and villages. The ticket price depends on the distance - up to 2-10 EUR (136.88-684.40 rubles). Taxi in Ayia Napa costs at least 5 EUR (342.20 rubles).

Scooters, bicycles, ATVs and buggies are very popular at this resort. Renting a bike costs 5-7 EUR per day (342.20-479.08 rubles), scooter rental - about 15 EUR (1025.60 rubles), buggy or ATV - from 50 EUR (3422 rubles).

The authors of the reviews do not advise resort guests to use a taxi or rent a car within the city, as traffic jams in Ayia Napa are usually unusually long, as are queues in parking lots.

Hotel reviews

The contingent of the hotel, according to guests, is represented mainly by foreigners of different ages, from youth to older tourists. Russians, as well as tourists with children, are usually few. The hotel is a checkpoint, without security, on the territory and in the hotel building, according to reviews, any passer-by from the street can enter. As vacationers were able to understand, this is the concept of all hotel apartments. At this resort you can often find the entrance to the room directly from the street. Despite the fact that the hotel is a checkpoint, vacationers do not report any incidents. Life here is very calm.

According to vacationers, the hotel is located in several buildings, 3 of which are located across the road.

Check in at the hotel is no problem. At the reception, the employees are very friendly, there is a Russian-speaking staff. All questions can be clarified through the hotel guide on the first day. Internet at the hotel is free to use, guests must purchase a coupon containing a username and password. The coupon is valid for 1 hour, but you can take them in unlimited quantities.

The photos of the hotel presented on the site, as tourists assure, are true, but the condition of the furniture in the rooms is somewhat worse than in the photographs. Wicker furniture, according to reviews, is already quite shabby.
