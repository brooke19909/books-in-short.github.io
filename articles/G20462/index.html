Borovoe, a resort in Kazakhstan: photos and reviews of tourists

Borovoe is a resort that is familiar to all fans of active and recreational recreation. This is a unique nature reserve in Kazakhstan, striking with a combination of various natural landscapes and their primeval preservation. The land of mountains and lakes conquers at first sight, and yet, in addition to this, there are its impenetrable forests, mountain rivers and water cascades.

Resort Overview

Borovoye village got its name in honor of the lake, in the immediate vicinity of which it is located. This settlement received the status of a climatic resort in 1910. This is an incredibly picturesque place with a special atmosphere, where you can not only enjoy the beauty of the surrounding nature, but also improve your health. The value of this place consists primarily in a special microclimate, and the combination of mountains, lakes and coniferous forests makes the air here special and truly healing. In addition, Borovoe is a resort where treatment with mineral waters and muds, as well as koumiss is used. Often this region is called the "pearl of Kazakhstan" or "Kazakhstan's Switzerland."





Rest in sanatoriums and dispensaries

Tourists who want to improve their health and are accustomed to relax with maximum comfort should get a ticket to a medical institution located in Borovoye or its environs. Pass a full examination in advance and get a referral from a doctor for treatment. Local sanatoriums effectively treat diseases of the respiratory, cardiovascular, nervous and genitourinary systems, as well as diseases of the musculoskeletal system and organs of the gastrointestinal tract. In addition to treatment with local gifts of nature, health centers offer healing baths, showers, and hardware. Borovoye resort (Kazakhstan) boasts an abundance of modern sanatoriums with developed infrastructure. Staying in them will not only improve your health, but also relax your body and soul - here you will find modern comfortable rooms, gyms, libraries, lounges and a variety of entertainment and health services. Often families come here on vacation, and not only guests from neighboring Russia, but also residents of European countries.





Alternative accommodation options

The tourist village of Borovoye is a resort that has been popular for more than a decade. In its vicinity there are many hotels and hotels of various levels of comfort. Also, various accommodation from private owners is rented here, and in the warm season it is not difficult to find an organized campsite or campground. Choosing an accommodation option should be based on your personal needs and your preferred holiday style. There is relevant information for tourists who love to travel "savages" with tents. In the summer season, these places are warm enough and you can swim, and the number of sunny days in Borovoye and its environs can be envied by many famous resorts of the Crimea and the Krasnodar Territory. However, you should stop at an organized campsite. Here you will find not only organized camp life, but also increased security conditions. Remember, wild animals and poisonous plants are still found in the surrounding forests and mountains, so it is recommended that you even take short walks accompanied by a guide.





Attractions and entertainment resort

The village of Borovoe is a resort designed for a relaxing wellness holiday. One of the main attractions of holidaymakers in the area is visiting local natural attractions. In total, there are 14 large lakes in the nature reserve. In many of them you can swim, on the banks of some of which are allowed fishing or boat trips. Do not forget that Borovoye resort offers year-round outdoor activities. In the winter months, well-maintained slopes for skiing and snowboarding are available. Numerous tourist centers in the area offer a variety of sports equipment rental and organized trips and trips. As for local attractions, in addition to the lakes, the most interesting are the mountains: Kokshetau (947 m high), Sinyukha, Bulektau, Okzhetpes. Resting in Borovoy, you should visit the stone beach.

Holiday Tips

The most popular question among tourists who are going to rest in these parts for the first time is how to get to the village. The most convenient option is by train from a city like Astana. Borovoye resort is a three-hour train ride from this village. We recommend that you use the services of local guides during your vacation. Without a guide in the vicinity of Borovoy, it is not at all difficult to get lost. If you are used to a comfortable rest or plan to combine rest with treatment and rehabilitation, book vouchers for sanatoriums or hotel rooms in advance. The resort is becoming more and more popular every year, at the height of the season it is not always easy to find an accommodation option or a place in a particular sanatorium.
