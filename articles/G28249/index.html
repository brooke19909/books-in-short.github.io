Hotel Alion Beach 5 (Ayia Napa): photos and reviews of tourists

Choosing a place for an annual vacation or children's summer vacation, often people stop in the settlements of Cyprus. Bright Ayia Napa is one of the most popular resorts, with sandy beaches and azure waters of the Mediterranean Sea, complemented by charming picturesque bays. A wide variety of entertainments, from sea sports to trips on ATVs, the largest Waterworld water park, a bustling nightlife with numerous clubs and bars, parties and discos, contribute to a favorable rest for both young people and families with children.

Features of climatic conditions allow you to relax here all year round: almost nine months a year - summer, winter - mild and warm. The population moves mainly with the help of bicycles, as cars are prohibited from entering the city center.

Hotel Alion Beach 5 * (Ayia Napa) provides its guests with an unforgettable vacation in Cyprus, gives a positive mood, favorable conditions and first-class service to each of its guests.





General characteristics of the hotel complex

A cozy and comfortable five-star hotel was erected in 1992. The last renovation was made in 2012. The main building is represented by a six-story building equipped with an elevator. Outwardly - a simple structure without pretentiousness and architectural delights. The prevailing colors of the interior decor are azure, emerald and many shades of white. Such a combination of the national color of Cyprus and modern European design created a special atmosphere within the walls of this complex, which guests like. Amazing park area, full of exotic plants and tropical greenery, creates a unique feeling of being on the island as if in a fairy tale.

The distance to the nearest airport is about 40 km, to the city center - 500 meters.

Number of rooms

The hotel complex has 100 beautiful and comfortable rooms, which belong to the Standard class and vary in views:

  • On the sea.
  • Park zone.
  • Panoramic view.

Park / Sea / Panoramic View rooms are 25 square meters. The floors are covered with laminate flooring or carpet. The interior is organically combined with the color palette of the beach: a tree of light tones and sandy shades of the walls are in perfect harmony with the skin color of the sea wave. French glazing, numerous sconces and floor lamps, designer curtains - are impressive from the first acquaintance. Flowers are an important attribute that creates comfort in the chambers in combination with wall paintings. Maximum capacity is 3 people.

All apartments have balconies with beautiful views of the local parkland or sea coast. The tables and wicker chairs located on them allow you to meet the dawn and see off the sunset without leaving the walls of the apartments.





Room facilities

The furniture is presented as a standard set: wardrobe, bed, armchairs, table and bedside tables. The presence of an air conditioner and heating system helps maintain a favorable air temperature. A multi-channel LCD TV, telephone, radio and hairdryer provide a comfortable living environment. The safe located on the top shelf in the closet ensures a relaxing holiday due to the lack of concern for important securities or jewelry, allowing you to enjoy your vacation to the fullest. There is a tea set, a set of dishes, an electric kettle or a coffee maker for self-preparation of hot drinks. A stocked mini-bar allows you to quench your thirst with soft drinks and juices.

Bathroom and Features

The bathroom is decorated with ceramic tiles in accordance with modern design trends. It is equipped with a bath or shower, a large mirror and is supplemented with all necessary personal hygiene products and a set of toiletries. The presence of snow-white bathrobes and disposable slippers makes vacationers feel at home.

All rooms have free internet access thanks to Wi-Fi. Cleanliness is maintained by daily cleaning by clean maids. Towels are changed daily; linen is changed three times a week.

Pets are not allowed in the Alion Beach 5 * hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus). An extra bed is available upon request and is an additional paid service, as well as international calls. Iron and ironing board are available at the request of the spa.





Hotel Food System

A healthy body and quality products are the fundamental provisions of the hotel's nutrition system. Foods rich in healthy ingredients and vitamins are always present in dishes offered to starving travelers.

Hotel Alion Beach 5 (Ayia Napa), the description of which is presented to your attention in the article, offers meals on different systems: only breakfast, half board and full board. The presentation is organized in the form of a buffet, where everyone can choose what his heart desires and put it on his own plate. It is possible to order a packing lunch in the room.

Among the institutions for satisfying hunger and thirst, the most favorite travelers are:

  • Kalimera Restaurant overlooking the endless abyss and park. Here you can only taste breakfast.
  • Symposium is a buffet restaurant with amazing ocean views. The main specialty is international cuisine. Themed dinners are the highlight of this establishment.
  • The deck a la carte - This restaurant specializes in seafood only - Mediterranean cuisine, the restaurant serves dinner only.
  • Pool Restaurant - This restaurant is near the pool. Specializes in the preparation of culinary masterpieces of international cuisine. This area is for lunch only. You can taste the full range, from appetizers and salads, grilled dishes and fish.




Hotel complex bars

It is possible to spend a pleasant and romantic evening in a quiet cozy atmosphere to the sounds of live music in the fresh air in the Amethystos bar. Favorite drinks or cooling cocktails, upcoming sunset and pleasant voices of performers, giving a unique feeling of relaxation and pleasure - all this is possible here.

No less surprising is the Muses Library Lounge, where you can enjoy a cup of tea or coffee while reading a fascinating book. There are also board games and an internet corner.

Spa and its benefits

Amazing SPA-procedures can be experienced in the center of beauty and health of Alion Beach 5 * hotel. The list of procedures for rejuvenating and healing the body includes seaweed wraps, salt peels, thermal baths and much more. Freed from toxins, the skin acquires an updated fresh look, metabolic processes in the body are activated and immunity is increased. A full range of refreshing treatments includes a visit to the jacuzzi, steam bath and sauna. An amazing massage from qualified staff will make the body relax and begin to relax.

Here you can also use the services of a beauty salon and a hairdresser, which will turn every representative of the fairer sex into a queen and give a genuine pleasure in the process.





Aquazone

The main outdoor pool with a gradual increase in depth is located in the flowering fragrant gardens of the Alion Beach Hotel 5 * (Ayia Napa) and is surrounded by shady trellises of flowers. A fenced children's pool allows adults and children to dabble in close proximity to each other. A small child is always under the supervision of parents. Umbrellas and sun loungers with mattresses provide a pleasant stay outdoors.

The third pool is located indoors and is heated in the winter. Its panoramic windows and fabulous ocean views - delight vacationers of all generations.

Toddler Leisure

In order to diversify the stay of young children, a playground with many swings, slides, carousels and mazes has been built on the street. It has a rubberized coating and is safe for games. The opportunity to swim in a shallow pool area for children is a great opportunity to spend the available energy for an evening sleep. At the reception you can order babysitting services. A qualified teacher will take the time of the baby, diversifying it with games, creative tasks and developing activities.

In restaurants, children are provided with highchairs and a special menu for children.

The location of the Alion Beach Hotel 5 * hotel (Ayia Napa) allows you to visit one of the main attractions of this area - a thematic country called the Water World water park. It is fully stylized in antiquity. The largest slides in Cyprus in length and height are located here. The whole family spent within this complex will forever be remembered for drive and excitement.





Beach

The beach is 40 meters from the hotel and is municipal. The beach area ends with a gentle descent into the water, which ensures safety when bathing babies. Free sun beds are provided for those who want to enjoy the sun bath and get an amazing tan color, as well as umbrellas for lovers of relaxation in the shade.

There is also a base of water activities, among which the most relevant and popular among travelers: diving, windsurfing, snorkeling, canoeing and others. A volleyball court will amuse fans of active games on the beach.

Leisure

On the territory of the hotel Alion Beach 5 (Ayia Napa), the photo of which you can see in the article, there are two large tennis courts, equipment and lighting are not included in the price and are paid extra. Table tennis and billiards are perfect for fans to compete for the championship in victory. A richly equipped gym will satisfy the requirements of every athlete and will please you with high-quality equipment.

There is the possibility of renting bicycles or cars, where you can make a sightseeing trip in the immediate vicinity. The tour desk provides a wide range of exciting excursions to local attractions at affordable prices.

Each evening ends with an exciting show program with the participation of professional dancers, acrobats, magicians and simply hospitable artists who are happy to give a good mood to all visitors to the show. A lot of games, contests and quizzes will amuse every holidaymaker.

Near the pool and on the beach, experienced instructors regularly conduct aerobics classes that contribute to the healing of the body and the formation of harmonious proportions of the body.





Wedding organization and other additional services

One of the areas in the services of this hotel is the organization of weddings. Many tourists, wanting to combine business with pleasure, prefer a wedding ceremony on the island to a standard wedding in a registry office. Such a place from year to year is Cyprus, and more specifically - it is the Alion Beach Hotel. The hotel administration is happy to organize such events, fully engaged in the design of the process itself and the festive dinner. Romantically decorated honeymoon room is individually decorated. A young couple will remember for the whole life an excellent ceremony of merging two hearts in this wonderful place.

Here you can order an amazing photo shoot among palm trees and azure shores. Ironing and laundry services are paid extra. For business meetings there is a fully equipped modern conference room.





Alion Beach 5 * (Ayia Napa): traveler reviews

Tourists who previously vacationed here are not stingy about leaving positive reviews. Amazing and good-natured staff, speaking four languages, according to the guests, is always ready to help and solve the problem as soon as possible. Incredibly tasty and nutritious menu, a lot of vegetables and fruits, meat dishes and fish - all this was noted by many holidaymakers in their reviews. The atmosphere of a park zone and hotel pools leads to unprecedented delight. Saturated with rest and fresh air, travelers return to the routine city life vigorous, sunbathed and rested. As tourists note, the Alion Beach Hotel 5 * hotel (Ayia Napa) is definitely worth considering as an accommodation option. Have a nice rest!
