Hotels in Armavir: review, rating, addresses and photos

Sometimes, in order to rest and relax, it is not necessary to leave the country, especially if it is Russia. Firstly, a trip to another state will be expensive. Secondly, our country is so huge and large that any traveler can find his favorite place here. By the way, you can travel to small cities, in most cases, they look very colorful. If you have a question, which settlement of Russia to look at this time, think over Armavir.

Armavir is a city in the Krasnodar Territory, located in the east of the district. A large number of peoples live here, for example, Russians, Tatars, Ukrainians, Circassians, Gypsies. Armavir is not the largest city in terms of territory, but it is an important transport and railway junction. It was founded not so long ago, in 1839. The climate here is very mild and warm, in winter the temperature does not drop below -1 degrees. In Armavir, more than 350 historical monuments, for example, the building of the Tatar mosque. In the vicinity of the city there are many paleontological and geological attractions. The main question is which hotel to stay at? It is about the Armavir hotels that we will talk about today. We will find the best options, read reviews and find out prices.

Hotel Inn Maiami in Armavir

The first hotel in Armavir on our list is called Inn Maiami. It is located near the city center, at 89 Khalturina Street. Guests of this mini-hotel can use the free internet.

Let's talk a bit about the types of rooms at this hotel in Armavir.

  1. Budget double room. This is a small and bright room with two single beds. There is a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a private bathroom, a wardrobe and a small table. The room rate per day is 1000 rubles.
  2. Double standard room. It is made in bright purple. It has a large double bed, sofa, refrigerator and dining table. The cost of this accommodation option is 1500 rubles per day.

In the reviews, guests say that the hotel has a good location. The rooms are clean and comfortable.
