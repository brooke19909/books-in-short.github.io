Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel: photos and reviews of visitors

Summer is a time of relaxation that many people prefer to spend on the seashore. One of the most popular places for tourists is the Greek island of Crete. This is a romantic place, shrouded in a veil of ancient legends and myths. In addition, white beaches and luxury hotels await travelers on the island. One of them is the Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel.

Of course, people planning their holidays are interested in any available information about the stopping place. What does the hotel look like and how close is it to the beach? What rooms are waiting for tourists? Can I bring a child with me? Is it worth it to come here? Answers to these questions will be useful to many tourists.

Hotel location description





Of course, the first question that tourists are interested in is the location of the Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel . Crete, Ierapetra, a picturesque resort village near one of the most beautiful bays in the southeast of the island. It is here, on a hill from which a breathtaking view of the sea opens, and the hotel was built, offering travelers shelter, relaxation and high-class service. About 1 km from the hotel is the colorful fishing village of Markigialos. Heraklion Airport is about 120 km away.

The hotel itself consists of the main building and several cozy bungalows. The territory here is large, generously decorated with greenery, and therefore spending time here is always a pleasure.

Hotel Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 *: photo and brief description of rooms





Living conditions are an important moment for every traveler. After all, in the evenings you want to relax on a soft bed in a comfortable room. 210 spacious rooms of different categories is what Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel offers its customers. Family Inland view ai, Standard rooms, Suites, Large Suites - each of these rooms (in some rooms even 2 or 3) is perfect for a comfortable stay.

In the room you will find compact, comfortable furniture, but also a set of household appliances. In particular, you can use the air conditioning system, without which you can not do in the hot months. There is also a minibar (available on request), a TV (you can watch satellite channels), a telephone, a small safe (for an additional fee), a set for making hot drinks (including tea bags and delicious coffee).

Guests can use the bathroom at any time. It has a spacious, modern shower (some rooms have a bathroom), a mirror, a toilet, a wash basin and a hairdryer. You can count on clean towels and the necessary hygiene items.

What kind of food do guests offer?





Guests at Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel can choose their own convenient food system. For example, you can pay only for breakfast, full meals, or completely refuse this service. In any case, you can always eat in the hotel restaurant (if you refuse, however, you will need to pay bills for each dinner or lunch).

Buffets are served at Laurel's main restaurant. Here you will find a modern setting, a relaxed atmosphere, excellent service and, of course, a large selection of Greek, Mediterranean and international dishes. According to reviews, the food here is really tasty and fresh.

Other catering facilities in the hotel

On the territory of the Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel there are other places where you can eat. For example, guests recommend to visit Lo Spuntino restaurant at least once. It works practically every day, but you need to reserve seats in advance. Here, in peace and quiet, you can enjoy the masterpieces of Mediterranean cuisine.

In the daytime there is also a tavern on the beach, which serves not only drinks but also snacks. There is also a poolside bar where guests can enjoy coffee, chilled drinks, juices and even delicious homemade ice cream. You can have a good time in the Blue Moon Lounge Bar.

Do not forget that in the village there are many cafes, bars and even shops where you can buy fresh fruits and other products. No one has problems with food on the island.

Where is the beach located?





The warm sea and beaches are just what many tourists dream of. What can the Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel complex offer in this regard? Crete is an island that is famous not only for its ancient history, but also for its many beaches. The hotel owns its own stretch of coast, which is located just 50 meters from the hotel grounds. You can get to the beach in a few minutes of leisurely walking.

The coast, as well as the entrance to the sea, is covered with soft sand. The beach is clean, because every day the hotel staff is cleaned here. There is a bar where you can get a cold drink or even dessert. Naturally, guests are offered to relax on a comfortable deck chair with a soft mattress. Here, tourists can get clean beach towels. Reviews say that the sea is calm, and the entrance to the sea is gradual, which is good for guests who want to relax with a child.

Are there any conditions for outdoor activities on the beach?

Naturally, more active, curious tourists will find something to do on the hotel beach. For a small fee, you can rent a boat or pedalo, canoe. Guests who are interested in more exciting entertainment can ride a boat, banana, get a dose of adrenaline, having tried parasailing. There are all necessary conditions for windsurfing and sailing. You can even visit a diving school, where in addition to training courses you can also get the necessary equipment and a suit. And travelers often buy boat trips around the island, which bring a lot of pleasure to people of different ages.

Hotel Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * (Greece, Crete, Ierapetra region): range of additional services

This hotel is considered one of the best on the coast. Naturally, its guests can count on a number of additional services. It has its own laundry (its services are not included in the price). Guests enjoy free hotel parking. By the way, here you can rent a transport, whether it be a car or a bicycle (this greatly simplifies the movement around the island).

Almost the entire territory has an Internet connection. A good store is equipped for guests, where you can buy not only souvenirs, but also various household trifles. There is a business center and a spacious conference room, which is used not only for business events, but also for organizing various celebrations.

How do guests spend their free time? Leisure and entertainment





Hotel guests do not have to be bored. In the courtyard there are three outdoor pools, one of which is equipped with a jacuzzi. Here guests have fun, relax and have a good time getting to know each other. There is also an indoor pool, which is open in the evenings and in cold weather.

Sports lovers will not have to be bored. An excellent gym is equipped for them, as well as basketball and five-a-side football courts, tennis courts. You can sign up for group aerobics classes. The spa offers a variety of wellness and beauty treatments, including professional massage. You can have fun playing darts, billiards, table tennis.

This is not all that the Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel can offer. O. Crete is a place with an amazing history. Be sure to use the services of a local tour desk. Here you will be told about the most interesting and beautiful places of the island that are worth visiting.

How do tourists respond to the hotel?





The opinion of tourists who have already managed to spend at least a few days at the hotel is a source of very valuable information. So what do travelers say about the Atlantica Mikri Poli Crete 5 * hotel? Reviews, and almost all without exception, are positive. Due to its favorable location, this hotel is perfect for almost any type of vacation, including family (there are plenty of entertainment for children).

The rooms are spacious and clean, the beds are comfortable, and the equipment is working properly.
