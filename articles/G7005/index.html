How to make clothes for a Monster High doll? Tips and Options

Monster High dolls were created by American designer Garrett Sander, who works at Mattel, which has been producing the famous Barbie for many years. The guy started with a simple packer, but it was the dolls of this series that glorified the author‚Äôs name all over the world. In sales and popularity among buyers, dolls from the Monster School exceeded all expectations, despite their high cost.

Which is better: buy or sew?

When buying a child one of the dolls in this series, be prepared for what she will have to either buy or sew outfits. Girls, playing with them, often like to take off their dresses and put them on, which gradually renders the finished suit unusable, and buying a new one can hit your pocket. In the article we will tell you how to make clothes for the Monster High doll with your own hands.

Denim dress

Since the girls from the Monster School are very fashionable, you need to sew modern clothes for them. For such a fashionable denim dress, you will need very little material. You can use your old mother‚Äôs skirt or jeans. Thin twine is also useful as a tie and a piece of black tulle. The dress is made by the standards of several parts. Two halves in front, back, wide skirt. After measuring the width of the back of the doll, transfer the size to a sheet of paper. From above draw a notch of a mouth and for hands. Before you make clothes for a Monster High doll, be sure to leave at least 0.5 cm to the seams.





The front part consists of two shelves. Cut them out, making recesses on the chest and abdomen. The main difficulty here is the small size of the doll's figure. The work ahead is pretty delicate. Both halves are laced in front. Its role can be performed by a dense thick thread. Its edges can be tied to knots.

The skirt is sewn in a circular pattern, as for a sun-flared skirt. On paper draw a circle equal to the circumference of the waist. Take a little more to make it comfortable for your child to put on a dress on the doll. Then you need to measure the length of the skirt and draw the same radius, then draw a larger circle with compasses. Since the front of the dress is shortened, they draw it on the pattern. Then, according to the template, the pattern is transferred to the fabric and cut out. Do not forget to leave 0.5 cm on each side to the seams. Before making clothes for a Monster High doll, you need to outline all the details and then sew on a sewing machine with an overlock or overlock foot. If the machine is not at home, you will have to go around all the seams with stitches over the edge so that the denim does not crumble.

At the back, under the skirt, a gathered piece of tulle is sewn so that the dress has a magnificent shape. It turns out an elegant and fashionable dress.

Hani swamp

How to make clothes for the Monster High doll using jewelry from satin ribbons, we will describe further. One of the most popular girls from the Monster School is the curly-haired beauty Hani Swamp. This is a swamp monster, so her body and hair have a swamp green color. This is a very romantic and feminine girl, so she loves outfits like what you can see in the photo below. This soft pink dress with a rose decoration. Before you make clothes for a Monster High doll, you also need to find out about her preferences. After all, everyone has a different character. Our heroine has the image of a girl very tender and sweet. No wonder she was called Hani, which translates from English as honey.





The dress is easy to sew, as it is quite simple. Two halves (back and front) are free, differ only in the size of the neckline. In front he is larger. But on the back you will have to make a cut and sew a hook so that the dress can be worn, because Hani's hair style is quite voluminous. The skirt is a rectangular pipe. When sketching it with a bodice, you need to make a couple of folds, evenly distributing the fabric along the waistline. Sew a strip of lace from the bottom. Separately, you can wind several roses from thin satin ribbons and sew them along the hem.

Knitted set

For knitters it will not be difficult to knit leggings and a raglan sweater. Before you make clothes for a Monster High doll from yarn, you need to find the thinnest knitting needles and threads. The sweater should have a large neckline, otherwise it can not be worn.





Such a kit can be connected in half an hour. And the child will have much more joy. Do not regret spending your precious time on sewing doll clothes. The joyful eyes of the child compensate you for all the time spent.
