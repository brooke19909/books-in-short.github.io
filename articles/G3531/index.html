Do-it-yourself dragon is one of the best gifts

The dragon is a mythical amazing creature with great strength, grip, sharp vigilance and cunning. No wonder the dragon is the emblem of the Romans' warriors and a symbol of supreme power not only in the ancient, but also in modern China. From many tales, we know that dragons live in caves and mountains, guarding their treasures from the encroachment of the enemy.

Today, many of us believe in superstitions and omens of life, one of them claims that if you keep the symbol or figure of the dragon at home, he will certainly preserve omnipotence, and if he is also appeased, he will bring prosperity and prosperity to the family. And you can appease the dragon by making it with your own hands, putting all your sincere love, hard work and desire into the work. It is easy to make it, only skillful hands, paper and a little time will be needed.





Perhaps the dragon made of paper will be not only a symbol of your wealth, but also just a decoration of the interior or a fun toy for the baby. There are many video tutorials, workshops on how to make a toy out of paper. After reviewing the information, it is clear that everything ingenious is simple. The invented dragon with his own hands is very cute and far from scary.

Manufacturing instruction

In order for paper dragons to be created without interruptions in search of missing parts, all materials necessary for this miracle should be prepared in advance: printer paper, corrugated paper, colored felt-tip pens or paints, wooden sticks and glue.

  1. Find on the Internet a coloring blank for the future dragon in the form of a head and tail.
  2. Print the picture on the printer.
  3. Show your imagination and brightly decorate it.
  4. Cut the dragon's body into long stripes of corrugated paper.
  5. Glue the opposite ends of the body to the head and tail.
  6. Glue one wooden stick to the head, and the other to the tail.

You can make a dragon with your own hands according to various schemes and drawings. Such needlework is called origami. Yes, it is harder to reproduce, but the emotions from the result will completely cover you, forcing you to admire your masterpiece.





Associations with the dragon are always vivid, exciting imagination, therefore, your invention should be impressive. So, for example, turning on your imagination, use various materials, such as glitter, beads, which will give your dragon a special charm.





Do-it-yourself paper dragon can turn into a living pet when using the effect of illusion. 3D dragons impress with their originality. It's all about optical illusion, which gives the illusion of movement, a real shake of the head. A DIY craft like a dragon with a 3D effect is easy to craft. It is enough to find a model of the circuit on the Internet, print it and connect along the marked lines. The result is not long in coming. You will be delighted!

Handmade dragon can serve as a symbolic present. We all know that the best gift is one that is made with our own hands. After all, so much love, diligence, warm feelings are invested in him that will not leave anyone indifferent.
