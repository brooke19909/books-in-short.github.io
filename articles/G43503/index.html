The best Belebey hotels: description and reviews

Belebey is a city in the Republic of Bashkortostan. This is the Southern Urals, the very center of Eurasia. The first mention of it dates back to 1757 (the village of Belebeevo, founded by the Chuvash settlers). Today it is inhabited with a developed industry, in its vicinity there are many historical monuments. If you plan to visit Belebey, the hotels of the city will provide you with comfortable accommodation and quality service.
