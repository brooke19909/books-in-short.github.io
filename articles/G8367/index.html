Skating rink "Mega" (Ufa): address and reviews

Most shopping centers allow their guests to have a great time. In addition to shopping, you can not only sit in a cafe, but also even go ice skating. So, the Mega skating rink in Ufa invites visitors to come for skating sessions that will bring a lot of positive emotions. The place is popular not only among the younger generation, but also people of middle and older age.

general information

The skating rink "Mega" in Ufa is open all year round, so you can even ride in hot summer weather. This is also a great option for those people who miss the winter and do not want to wait for the cold weather to get on their skates. The skating rink has been working for quite some time. Its opening was a rather significant event, so large lines were formed. Now there are fewer of them, but the ice rink is regularly visited by guests, therefore it does not happen empty.

For visitors, everything is thought out as conveniently as possible. Cafes are open next to the ice, so you can always relax by ordering delicious food. There is also a playground for the smallest. Therefore, adults can safely ride the Mega ice rink in Ufa while the child plays with other kids. If the children also want to ride, then special protective equipment is given out for them, among which there are helmets. If necessary, guests can rent skates. The size grid is quite large, so there will be no problems.

Entrance to the skating rink "Mega" (Ufa) costs 150 rubles for an adult visitor, for a child - 120 rubles. A skiing session lasts as much as an hour and a half. You can come from 9:30 a.m. to 9:30 p.m. In total, six skiing sessions are held per day. For visitors include music, so skating always takes place in a pleasant environment.

Ice rink address

Finding an institution is not difficult. The shopping center is very popular in the city, so many citizens come here. The Mega skating rink in Ufa is easy to find at the following address: Rubezhnaya Street, building No. 174. It is most convenient to get here by car or minibuses, which go quite often. Shuttle taxi numbers are suitable: 232, 277, 292, 298. Get off at a stop called "STC Mega".
