The best Anapa hotels with a private beach

Anapa is rightfully considered one of the best resort cities in Russia. It really has everything that should be in an ideal resort town: clean air, mild, temperate climate, healing springs of mineral mud, excellent sandy beaches and the Black Sea. The length of the beach strip of Anapa is about 40 kilometers, all along its length are all kinds of hotels, pensions, recreation centers and other health resorts. It is here that the most popular Anapa hotels with their own beach are concentrated, especially in the central beach area.





Beach season in Anapa

Thanks to the warm climate, the swimming season runs from May to October. At this time, the water in the sea is already warm enough for swimming, and the weather is conducive to relaxation and tanning. SPA-hotel "Deauville" - an unforgettable vacation

In the category "Best Anapa Hotels with Private Beach" this hotel may well occupy a leading place. The administration of the complex is very responsible for organizing leisure. It offers its customers the highest quality of service, first-class rooms and a rich leisure program. The hotel takes pride in its five-star rating and tries to justify this title.



Food, SPA services

It offers vacationers restaurants with exquisite diverse cuisine, bars, pizzerias. The SPA complex deserves special attention. This is a first-class medical center that provides a lot of wellness and relaxing treatments. This includes massage (classic, facial massage), sauna, salt treatment (salt cave), sea gommage, hydromassage and many others. It is worth noting that often when booking tickets through the hotel website a certain set of procedures is provided as a gift.

Leisure

If you are interested in Anapa hotels with their own all-inclusive beach, then the Deauville SPA hotel is definitely suitable for you. In addition, the hotel complex is positioned as an ideal place for families with children. Indeed, there are spacious family rooms, a leisure program and a club for children, babysitting services, a children's pool with slides and heating. The child will not be bored, even if the parents want to spend some time separately, they definitely will not have to.



Leisure for adults is organized at the proper level. Animation program, sports grounds, tennis court, entertainment programs and organization of excursions. There are several pools, internet access throughout. All this makes the rest rich and unforgettable.

Beach

Not all Anapa hotels with their own beach can boast such cleanliness and well-groomed as the beach of Deauville hotel. The distance to it is about 600 meters, if you wish, you can use the electric car, which runs back and forth with an interval of 20-30 minutes. On the beach there are umbrellas, sun loungers, a bar, as well as water equipment rental services and other entertainment.



Infrastructure and other services

Among the other services provided by the hotel are laundry and ironing rooms, dry cleaning, and maid service. An airport shuttle is available. Payment includes breakfast from the hotel. The choice of vacationers is 453 rooms, among which there are both budget options and chic luxury rooms with sea views. For business tourism, two conference rooms and a meeting room are available. And there are bathrobes and slippers in the rooms! Thus, the SPA-hotel "Deauville" is able to satisfy the needs of even demanding tourists, and its work is organized on the principle of serious foreign high-class hotels.

Grand Hotel Valentina 5 *

Continuing to consider Anapa hotels with their own private beach (5 stars), one cannot ignore the Grand Hotel Valentina. Its design is made in a classic style. Positioned complex for family and corporate leisure. The beach is located less than 200 meters, sandy, with a gentle slope into the sea. This is very good for vacationers with children and for those who do not like depth. The atmosphere of the hotel is calm and measured, which ensures a pleasant and relaxed holiday.



Rooms and facilities

Hotel Valentina offers guests a choice of several types of rooms, among which there are standard, family, diplomatic and presidential (suites). Daily cleaning, room service is provided. Bathrobes and slippers are provided, there is Internet, television, a telephone and a hairdryer. If necessary, laundry services are available.

The complex constantly has its own doctor. The territory also has mail, Internet access points, fax, telephone for long-distance calls. Those who can‚Äôt imagine life without exercise can visit the gym.

Health, entertainment

To ensure a pleasant and useful pastime, the complex has massage rooms, a sauna, a Turkish bath (hammam), a jacuzzi. Entertainment by the sea include equipment rental, boat trips, water rides. If necessary, the administration will ensure the organization of holidays, banquets and weddings, parties in the Freesia restaurant located on the ground floor.

If you choose Anapa hotels with a private beach for your holidays, you will go with your children or yourself, you should pay attention to the Grand Hotel Valentina. Here a calm and pleasant vacation will be provided.

Riviera Hotel 4 * - Mediterranean style

If we consider Anapa hotels with their own beach in terms of beauty and design style, then it is worth visiting the Riviera. Here you can not only have a good rest, but also get aesthetic pleasure from the appearance of the exterior and interior. The design of the hotel was specially designed in such a way as to repeat the charm of the refined resorts of the Mediterranean. In addition, the hotel complex can please with the presence of a full-fledged SPA center, where with the help of pleasant procedures you can improve your health and change your appearance.



Services and infrastructure

The rooms are pleasant and cozy atmosphere, the maids are constantly monitoring the cleanliness. The food at the hotel is varied and delicious. The atmosphere is diluted by funny animators, in the evenings discos are held. For children, a club, a games room, animation are organized, there is a children's pool. The hotel has private parking for customers' personal cars. Laundry facilities are available on site. If you do not want to go to the beach, you can sunbathe on a specially equipped terrace, and swim in the pool.

In addition to the SPA-center, you can visit the beauty parlor, hairdresser or jacuzzi. The hotel has a private beach, which is 500 meters away, which is considered the average distance from the sea. For the business community, vacationers have a conference room and a business center. Holidays in the Riviera will be pleasant and relaxed. This is facilitated by a harmonious surrounding view, polite and pleasant staff, as well as a high level of service.
