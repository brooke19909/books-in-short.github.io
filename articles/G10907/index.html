Yalta hotels with their own beach - a wonderful vacation by the sea

On the Black Sea coast, among the mountain peaks and green spaces, lies the beautiful city of Yalta. It is a favorite vacation spot of many tourists. Arriving here, you forget about all the hardships and problems, here you completely relax and transfer to an atmosphere of calm and harmony. Yalta is beautiful landscapes, the warm sea and the gentle sun. Modern cozy hotel complexes are spread along the coast; hotels of Yalta with their own beach attract thousands of tourists annually.

Hotel Oreanda

On the promenade, just twenty meters from the sea, is the cozy Oreanda Hotel. The hotel has 114 comfortable rooms with a modern design and all necessary amenities. On the territory there is a gym, outdoor and indoor pools, Turkish and Finnish saunas. In addition, there is a private beach equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as a bar, restaurant, diving center. Yalta hotels with their own beach, such as Oreanda, allow you to comfortably relax and enjoy all the delights of the sea.

Hotels in Big Yalta

From the southern cape of Sarych to the foot of Ayudag Mountain, along the entire Black Sea coast, stretches Big Yalta with its unique and picturesque villages, such as Nikita, Massandra, Livadia, Oreanda, Gurzuf, Gaspra, Miskhor, Alupka, Foros, etc.

These are hundreds of kilometers of sea coast with amazing cliffs and forests.
