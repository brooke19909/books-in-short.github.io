Enthusiastic tourist reviews: Sri Lanka

According to average tourist reviews, Sri Lanka is an amazing country that is not like any corner of the globe. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay: delicious food, vast expanse of water and thousands of attractions. But is it really so? Let's try to figure it out.

Section 1. Reviews of tourists. Sri Lanka: general description. Is it worth the ride?

Thinking about a vacation on the island of Sri Lanka? Reviews of tourists, prices - everything suits? Then start exploring this picturesque island right now. After all, it is here that you can relax from the hustle and bustle of the city, spend a lot of time outdoors, in the immediate vicinity of the sea. In this country, no one is in a hurry, the habit of enjoying every day of life affects. Along the coast are sandy beaches with coconut palms. Multi-colored fish swim in the sea, many coral reefs.

Local attractions and activities are mostly natural. Fans of noisy parties and discos have nowhere to turn around. Instead, there are barbecue or barbecue on the beach and fishing. Upset is the lack of direct flights from Russia and the CIS countries (statistics on Sri Lanka: tourist reviews 2013), but for the sake of relaxation you can close your eyes to this.

A hot tropical climate is an ideal environment for the spread of gastric infections. All tourists are advised to wash their hands before eating and not drink tap water. Going to a nature reserve or national park in the evening, do not forget about repellents. And you should not go on an excursion into the jungle without taking anti-malarial pills.

Section 2. Reviews of tourists. Sri Lanka: Local Attractions

The island has historical monuments of world scale. In the mountains of Sri Lanka, near Matale, there is an ancient temple consisting of thirteen caves - Aluvihara. A huge Buddha statue is located in the first cave, the walls and ceiling of which are decorated with drawings with motifs of lotus. Scary pictures are presented in the second cave, which depict scenes from hell, bloodthirsty demons and dead sinners very realistically. A little higher is a cave dedicated to the Indian scientist Buddhagosh. And if you climb to the top of the cliff, you can admire the panorama of the valleys.

If you find yourself in the southwestern part of the country, then be sure to visit the Sinharaja National Park. This island's only rainforest is a World Heritage Site. Some representatives of flora and fauna living among the massif are listed in the Red Book. Here you can find rare mammals, birds, butterflies and reptiles.

Section 3. Reviews of tourists. Sri Lanka: Useful Information

In Sri Lanka, it is better to relax from November to the end of April. In the remaining time of the year, it rains almost every day on the island. However, the air temperature is on average around 28 ¬∞.

Tea, Sri Lankan spices and exotic fruits are known throughout the world. In the country, meat, vegetables and rice are prepared according to a special recipe. But they prefer to replace meat dishes with fish and other seafood. This is due to the fact that cows on the island are considered sacred animals. On days of a lunar eclipse, a complete ban on drinking alcohol is introduced; the rule applies to all bars and hotels operating on the All Inclusive system.

There are local hotels right on the seashore. Each building is unique, built in the traditional style of the country. The hotels host theme nights for tourists, a moonlit barbecue and other spectacular entertainment.

You can rent a car on the island, but few do it. Unusual left-hand traffic, drivers and pedestrians who regularly violate the rules prevent tourists from traveling around the island in comfort. It is much more convenient to travel by bus or train.
