Urbanears headphones are sold in different colors. Reviews about the appearance are positive. In the market you can find different models - from black to purple. Why is that? Model design

The Zinken model is a DJ, so it has a rather strong headband. Hinges are made of metal. Thanks to them, the model can both rotate and fold. The angle of rotation of the cups is small, so many owners claim that the hinges are superfluous.

One of the cups has a 3.5 mm jack, the second 6.3 mm. This allows the use of cables with different plugs. Accordingly, there is no need to use an adapter. In the Plattan model, everything is a little different. These wireless headphones have a cable with a remote control and a microphone, but there are 3.5 mm connectors at both ends. The cord is extremely thin, has a fabric coating. Does not rustle and does not bring discomfort. Often the owners carry this cable with them as an alternative solution if the battery is exhausted. The headband has a cloth roller. It can be removed and washed if necessary. For the latter, water not hotter than 30 ¬∞ C is used.

Overhead type headphones, respectively, can put pressure on the ears. However, over time, the spring loosens. The charging port for the battery model is on the right side of the bowl. You can immediately find the button to turn on the device and search for the source of music. This is all the same key. To activate the search, you must hold it for six seconds. The same cup has a touch control panel on the upper side. Here you can answer incoming calls, change the playlist and increase / decrease the volume. The Plattan model has an indicator next to the panel. If it glows in red, then the battery should be recharged, if white - then the battery condition is 100%.

The drivers of both models have the same diameter - 40 mm. However, the designs are different. Zinken headphones have a double diaphragm, while Plattan only a single diaphragm. Both are made by hand.



Sound patterns

Despite the fact that the models have serious differences in design, their sound remains at the same level. In any case, if you connect the wireless model using a cable. The upper frequencies are noticeably lowered, the lower ones are excessively raised. This is striking when listening without the equalizer turned on. Especially if you use a computer as the sound source . With smartphones, these problems are almost invisible. Such a contrast between the frequencies was created in order to brighten up the weak power of the phones. When playing with stronger devices (some tablets, computers, musical instruments, etc.), the sound is often distorted. However, reviews about it, in connection with the price category, are not bad.
