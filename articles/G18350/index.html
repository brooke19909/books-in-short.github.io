Crimea, boarding house with food: selection criteria

Almost every vacationer, making their choice in favor of the Crimean peninsula, would like to find a boarding house with meals in their favorite resort. And this is not surprising - located in the picturesque points of the region, they are ideal for active tourists, fans of a relaxing beach holiday, and besides, people who can not imagine such a pastime without excursions.





Private inexpensive pensions in Crimea are in demand among family tourists, as well as among people who are not used to spending big money on hotel rooms. One of the main features of such establishments is their low prices, which in summer also remain at a very affordable level.

Arriving in Crimea, a boarding house with meals can be found in almost any part of this region. The most popular of them are near beautiful beaches, attractions and resorts. Unique healing climate, developed hotel and tourist infrastructure, health resorts and high-class centers are the main features that distinguish Crimea.

Pension "Bright Coast" (Crimea)

Pensions with meals ("buffet") are not difficult to find here. "Bright Beach" is an example of this. It consists of 5 cottages, located in the village of Uchkuevka, on the Black Sea coast, in the floodplain of the river. Belbek. This district was named after the village of Uch-Kuya, which was once the estate of F.F. Ushakov, or rather, part of it. The surrounding hills and the valley of the village are covered with overgrown vineyards.





The place is very well located - a good traffic intersection allows you to travel around the whole Crimea. The boarding house with meals saves the budget for going to restaurants. And just a 10-minute bus ride and boat trip separate you from Grafskaya Marina, located in the center of Sevastopol. At the same time, there is a ferry for motorists.

One of the selection criteria for this institution is that here all rooms have a separate entrance, a comfortable wide terrace with sea views and rattan furniture, are equipped with a split system, satellite TV, a refrigerator, a shower and a bathroom. In addition, they have the ability to accommodate another person.

It is worth noting that the closed territory of this tourist complex is constantly under guard.

Breakfast is included in the meal plan, and for a full fee, full board is available.
