I would like to note that in the Crimea there are as many as three villages with this name. In our article, we are talking about the Resort, located between Theodosia and Sudak. Both are a little over 25 km from there, and the famous city of Koktebel is only 13 km on the road. Enclosures

Arriving on vacation in the Crimea, in the village of Kurortnoye about the boarding house "Crimean Primorye" reviews leave different. This largely depends on the living conditions, which vary slightly in each building. In total, the boarding house has 4 buildings and several two-story wooden cottages. The buildings are old-built, so none of them have an elevator. The cottages are modern, have a good design and all amenities.

Building No. 1 is two-story, located almost on the seashore, hosts guests all year round. There are rooms "standard", designed for two people. Their equipment includes: furniture sets, including a chair-bed as an extra bed, refrigerator, air conditioning (not in all rooms), TV, hygiene room with shower and toilet.

Building number 2 is five-story, located 200 meters from the sea. It looks pretty modern. This building houses the administration and reception. There are rooms for 1 and 2 guests. Moreover, the balcony is only in double rooms. About the Crimean Primorye boarding house, reviews of tourists note that the second building is located next to the pool, so it is noisy in the afternoon. But this building is always very clean, and you can get an iron and an electric kettle from the concierge.

Building number 3 is four-story, located 30 meters from the sea. This building offers rooms of two categories:

- with amenities (bathroom, shower);

- with partial amenities (in the rooms there is only a wash basin, and a bathroom and showers on the floor).

Building number 5 is located in a park area, from the sea it is 100 meters. It offers economy class rooms with partial amenities, as well as superior standard rooms with air conditioning and all amenities.



Boardinghouse cottages are built surrounded by pine trees a stone's throw from the coast. Their area is from 60 sq. M. Layout in the cottages allows you to accommodate from 4 to 6 people. It has a spacious living room, two bedrooms and two hygiene rooms.

In addition to double rooms, the pension has several family rooms, designed to accommodate from 4 to 6 people.

Nutrition

The dining room, cafe and bar has the Crimean Primorye boarding house in its infrastructure. Reviews of tourists about catering are mixed. Vouchers here are sold according to the "full board" system, which involves breakfast, lunch and dinner for adults, and for children - also extra afternoon tea. Meals are served in the spacious and beautifully decorated dining room. The principle of distribution of food is a buffet. The breakfast menu includes dairy and regular cereals, egg dishes, stewed and fresh vegetables in salads, meatballs, chicken stock, tea. The lunch menu includes various side dishes, meat dishes, fried fish, fruits. For dinner, casseroles, meat or fish dishes with a side dish, vegetables, tea are served. At noon, children are given cookies or waffles. The cafe sells ice cream, sweets, juices every day.

All meals are strictly on schedule and last no more than an hour, which not all vacationers like. Also, not everyone is satisfied with the quality of dishes in which they put a lot of spices.

In the boarding house there is an opportunity to hold a family celebration, to celebrate an anniversary, birthday, just a meeting of good friends. For all such events, you can rent a banquet room, and the boarding house staff will help to decorate it and arrange guest services.

Leisure for children organized in a boarding house is pretty good. There is a children's and playroom for them, there is a specially equipped playground, and funny animators conduct various games and activities with them.

Leisure for adults

Many ways to have fun are provided by the boarding house ‚ÄúCrimean Primorye‚Äù near Feodosia. The reviews of vacationers here on the organization of leisure are unanimous and indicate that this health resort is most suitable for families with children and people who want to spend their holidays measuredly and calmly, with benefits for health. The pension has a large pool. The water in it is fresh, without heating. It can be used free of charge by guests staying in rooms with private facilities. The rest have to pay extra. In the boarding house there is an opportunity to get advice from a pediatrician, dentist and therapist. For the convenience of guests, there is a first-aid post and a small shop with related beach products on site.

In many reviews, tourists note a good massage room, where they are always happy to provide services to everyone, as well as a cosmetology room where they perform peeling, scrub cleansing masks and other procedures that help restore freshness and youth to the skin.

Leisure outside

Many interesting excursions can be visited by those who came to the boarding house ‚ÄúCrimean Primorye‚Äù. The Republic of Crimea reviews are only enthusiastic. A resort town is famous for its unique nature and reserve, located on the slopes of Kara-Dag. It organizes walking tours. A dolphinarium is located next to the reserve. Beach

It's no secret that for the sake of the wonderful sea, many people go on vacation to the Kurortnoye village, to the Crimean Primorye boarding house. Reviews of local beaches, however, are not very enthusiastic. The health resort is located almost on the coast, so you can quickly walk to the nearest beach. It is municipal, so there are always a lot of people. For guests of the boarding house loungers are given out free of charge, and this is perhaps the only advantage. Most of all complaints are caused by the beach cover, which is medium, and in some places large pebbles. Sunset at sea, although gentle, but also not very convenient, as it is lined with large and medium boulders covered with marine vegetation and because of this is rather slippery. But the water in the sea is excellent, very clean and warm. There are other beaches in the village, located in the direction of Fox Bay. They are not so crowded, but also pebble and practically not equipped.
