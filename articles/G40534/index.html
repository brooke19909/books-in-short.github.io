Hotel Alerya Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

The city of Kemer, located in Turkey, is among the top 10 most popular resorts in the world. Every year, thousands of people relax here looking for warmth, sun and fun. The city is located about 60 km from Antalya and the main attractions of the resort. Kemer's distinctive features are picturesque natural resources: there are stunning mountain elevations that protect the resort from strong winds and inclement weather, there are fragrant orange groves and coniferous forests.

It is worth noting that Kemer is characterized by an abundance of party places. The resort has many clubs, bars and restaurants, working around the clock. If you travel with children, then you should choose a hotel that is farthest from the central part of the city. The ideal option would be the Alerya Hotel 3 * hotel. Targeted by an audience with young children. Perfect for a romantic weekend, active, beach and sightseeing holidays.

general description





In 2000, the Alerya Hotel 3 * was founded. Kemer is a resort with rich infrastructure, numerous walking parks, historical monuments and long beaches. The airport is at most 55 km away. Entertainment centers for young guests are concentrated in the city, for this reason couples with children come here. The hotel has a small territory on which two three-story buildings are located.

Terms of accommodation

Alerya Hotel 3 * complex (Turkey) offers guests accommodation in comfortable rooms, the main part has balconies. The windows of the rooms offer stunning views of the mountains, the sea or the green area. The rooms are provided with regular cleaning - at least twice a week. Internet is available in all rooms, however, use will be paid.





Standard equipment includes a landline telephone for direct communication with the registration department. In the presence of air conditioning (cold / heat), cable TV with Russian channels is connected. A bathroom has been allocated, equipped with the necessary set of bath accessories. Paid services: electronic safe, alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks in the bar. A baby cot is available upon request.

Coastline

Alerya Hotel 3 * has no private beach. Municipal is located approximately 700-800 meters from the building. A minibus runs to the coast, if you wish, you can walk on foot in 20 minutes. The road to the coastline runs through a picturesque park with spreading palm trees, flowering plants, orange and pomegranate trees.





Entrance to the coast is paid. This is not crowded, equipped with beach equipment and water attractions (catamarans, jet skis, diving). If you feel sorry for the money, go to the free beach. The paid coast is equipped with bars, there is a cafe with Russian-speaking employees. Animators entertain guests, organize fun contests and foam parties. The sea is a separate issue: the water is clear, warm, without algae and large stones. A little further from the coast, all the flora and fauna appears.

What is the power system?

Hotel Alerya Hotel 3 * (Kemer) operates on an all-inclusive basis. Three meals a day in the restaurant, a variety of dishes. During the day, free drinks are offered: beer, wine, vodka, coffee / tea. According to tourists, the food is decent, properly balanced.





Morning meal consists of dairy products, oatmeal, cereal, fresh salads, sausages, cheese, sour-milk drinks. For lunch, meat, chicken in addition with side dishes and vegetables are served. Dinner will please you with an abundance of legumes, dishes of potatoes, eggplant, etc. Sweet tooths are pampered with jams, fresh fruits, and pastries. It's hard to stay hungry.

Also on the territory of the Alerya Hotel 3 * hotel there is a Pool bar with a rich wine list, elite varieties of cognac, whiskey, liquor. Coffee lovers will enjoy a real Turkish drink. Fruit drinks, freshly squeezed juices, milkshakes are prepared for children.
