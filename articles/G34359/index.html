Club Hotel Tess 4 * (Turkey / Alanya): tourist reviews, prices and photos

Millions of tourists visit Turkey every year. This country is supposedly created specifically for recreation: the gentle sea, the hot sun, clean beaches and one of the most developed hospitality infrastructures in the world. Turkey is located on the Mediterranean coast, where the climate is very mild. The state receives a good income from the tourism sector, so the country is so hospitable. Moreover, there is a convenient visa and customs regime and a well-developed transport network.

Antalya - one of the Turkish resorts

One of the most visited cities in Turkey is Antalya. This is a resort that is located right by the sea. The city has its own international airport, hotels, restaurants and shops, all kinds of entertainment for every taste and budget. Many tourists visit Antalya for the purpose of shopping, as here there are factories of leather products, jewelry, textile enterprises. Prices are much lower, and you can buy anything. The cuisine offered in the city is a local highlight. Juicy fruits and vegetables, fresh seafood and fish, Turkish sweets - all this is a must-try for every traveler. Among the most visited and beloved by the guests of the sights, one can single out Duden waterfalls, striking with their splendor, and the Olympus disco. Cultural attractions attract with original architectural structures; you can visit the most interesting archaeological museum. In the evening, they organize a Fountain Show - a sparkling event that will be remembered for a long time.

Club Hotel Tess 4 *





The hotel is located in Alanya - this is the area of the resort city of Antalya. It is located near the village of Konakly, 250 meters from its center. The airport is 110 km away. Club Hotel Tess, Club Tess Hotel 4 * - both of these names are true.

The distance from the hotel to the coast is 147 meters, this is the first coastline. The hotel building was built in 1990, the last reconstruction was carried out in 2010, major repairs - in 2013. Hotel Club Hotel Tess (Alanya) was formerly called Kaliopa Beach, then Lenna Beach and Sahara Beach. The area occupied by the hotel is 7500 m 2 . The building is a five-story building with a detached small villa, in which guests are also provided with rooms. Each room has a balcony. The territory is surrounded by greenery, which gives shade and coolness.





Rooms and service

The hotel consists of 157 rooms, it can be attributed to the category of small hotels of medium size. The total number of beds is 322. Each room has a balcony. 153 standard rooms are decorated in accordance with the standard of stardom, accepted around the world. Room service, daily maid service, linen and towels are a universally accepted service.
Each bathroom has towels, a hairdryer, a washbasin, a shower and a toilet. In standard rooms there is a TV with many channels (including Russian-language), a VCR, a safe for storing money and valuables, air conditioning and a telephone. The minibar in the room is paid, it is paid for when leaving the hotel, and it is replenished depending on the use of the guests. All rooms are comfortably furnished with new furniture. Upon request, an extra bed can be added to the room.

Food and entertainment





The system of the Club Tess Hotel hotel (Turkey) implies all-inclusive operation. On the territory there is a restaurant with national and European cuisine, 3 bars with alcoholic drinks and cocktails. The food is varied, there are a lot of fresh vegetables and fruits - guests do not stay hungry. Alcoholic drinks are offered free of charge from 10.00 to 23.00. The pizzeria is open. To entertain visitors, animators work day and night. An outdoor pool with water slides, umbrellas and sun loungers by the pool are provided for free. For guests with children, a children's pool is specially provided. The hotel's own sandy beach is 100 meters away. It is equipped with a separate bar, umbrellas and sunbeds are included in the price. For a fee, you can rent a mattress, order entertainment or enjoy water sports. At the request of the guest, you can call a doctor, use the laundry, visit the Internet cafe. There are several outlets on the territory. For an additional fee, you can use the conference room, which is very convenient for tourists doing business.





Paid and free service

The free service includes aqua aerobics, waterskiing, the use of a jacuzzi, left-luggage office, currency exchange, on-site parking, beach volleyball, an entertainment center, and a Turkish hammam.

For a fee you can visit the sauna, massage, solarium. Paid services also include 24-hour room service, car rental and dry cleaning. The hotel can book individual excursions in the surrounding area.

Vacation with children

It is worth noting that this hotel, like any four-star hotel, provides cots in the room for tourists with children for free. They can be requested upon check-in. The hotel restaurant provides a special seat for babies. In addition, the hotel has a children's pool and a playground - this will help to occupy the child and give him a lot of pleasant experiences. For children from 4 to 12 years old there is a special children's entertainment club.

Tourists reviews

Most guests of Club Hotel Tess note a small but well-groomed area, good food. Out of habit, the sharpness of the dishes is surprising, but this is typical of Turkish national cuisine.

Neighborhood

The hotel is located in Alanya, a resort area that is interesting for sightseeing. The town has several attractions that are definitely worth visiting for tourists. The main ones include the Red Tower - this is an architectural structure erected in the XIII century. The tower is built of red brick, this explains its name. It was designed to protect Alanya Bay, and the image of the tower is printed on the national flag.

The interest of tourists is the Pirate Fortress - a magnificent ancient building with more than 140 bastions. It consists of 83 towers, the walls are arranged in three rows and stretch from the coast itself at a decent distance - a magnificent sight!

Alanya Mosque is another man-made attraction. It covers a rather large area - 4500 meters. On the territory of the mosque there are kitchens, educational institutions, baths, there is its own observatory and library. A mosque in Alanya is a monumental structure designed to strengthen the Muslim faith. In her yard there is a mausoleum where Khan Suleiman, the famous Turkish ruler, and his wife rest. In addition to architectural monuments, the town is known for its unique natural caves.
