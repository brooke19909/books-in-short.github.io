Tilda Sheep (pattern): how to do it yourself

Do you like making interior dolls? Or have you never tried this popular activity? In any case, you will definitely like a cute accessory for home decoration - a tilde sheep. The pattern can be done both independently and according to the finished template. Choose ideas, embody them in beautiful souvenirs.



Interior dolls

Volumetric textile products in the form of figures of people and animals became a very popular decoration of the house. They come in many forms. A bunny, a mouse, a bear, a tilde sheep look very romantic . The pattern of such a product, as a rule, consists of a certain set of parts. If you master the construction of one species, for example, animals, you can easily cope with other souvenirs in the form of smart girls, princesses or angels. Even tilde-like animals are humanlike and stand on their hind legs. Such souvenirs are often hung up, but can sit on a sofa, a table and even stand. You may need to make a frame for this.

What you need to make

You will need the following to make a beautiful tilda sheep:



  • pattern;
  • the cloth;
  • a piece of chalk;
  • pins
  • scissors;
  • needle with thread;
  • sewing machine;
  • decor (optional).

If you do needlework, you most likely have all the tools. You can make an interior doll from all kinds of scraps left over from sewing large items. Multi-colored fabrics in small quantities are sold in stores by weight. Perhaps your city has such a department.

Do-it-yourself tilda sheep: patterns

Before preparing the fabric, it is worth deciding on the appearance of your souvenir. These dolls can be dressed up in dresses and other costumes or sewn from a material that imitates the texture of wool. Use additional applications and decorative stripes in the form of hearts, flowers.

So, you have found a suitable sample and decided what your tilde sheep will be like. With your own hands, patterns of body parts and clothes are easy to make, but for the first experiment it is easier to use a sample. You just need to print it from a computer on the right scale. A4 sheet will be enough. If you want to make a big doll, then the template can be divided into two landscape formats, and then glue the parts in the right parts.

Having mastered the work with samples, you can easily build everything yourself.

Tilda Sheep (pattern): master class

Such a wonderful animal doll is made of the following parts:

  • body;
  • ears
  • muzzles;
  • pens;
  • feet and hands.

The tilde doll "Sheep" (pattern) may consist of a different number of parts. In the picture below their minimum number. Legs and body are made as one common detail. In the previous example (photo above) they are sewn from two parts. Thanks to this, it is more convenient to give the character a sitting position. Although you can simply do an additional line in the right place. The muzzle with the head here is also combined on the pattern. It will be enough to simply cut off the detail of the face (it is separated by a thin line) and cut it out from the corresponding material.



Usually the base is made of light fabric, and the feet, palms and muzzle look better in beige color. All these dolls are as if humanized, the characters have a human body. As for the number of parts, it should be as follows:

  • base - 2 pieces;
  • ears - 4 (two multi-colored blanks for the inner and outer parts of each ear);
  • handles - 4 parts (one stitched from two parts);
  • feet and palms are made of a pair of halves represented on the pattern with one part (they need to be cut 8 pieces);
  • make additional decor (in this case hearts) as desired in the required quantity.

The image below shows how the parts are stitched together. For the first samples, it is better to choose templates with fewer parts, and accordingly, seams, then you can gradually complicate the workpiece.

The characters created using the above technology will always delight you and your home guests.

Different images according to one pattern





Based on any of the simple patterns described above or the next more complicated one, you can make a whole family of lambs. If you put them in different clothes, you get a whole collection of souvenirs.

Most importantly, you only need to make one template out of paper. All characters will look uniform and harmonious, but at the same time each will be unique. In the technique of manufacturing such toys, the scope for creativity is immense.

As for the last version of the template, it will turn out to be a more humanoid creature than in previous cases. Here the arms and legs are slightly different, and the muzzle is sewn not from two parts, but from a larger number. This gives it volume and naturalness.

So, you saw what a tilde sheep looks like. A pattern, taken from an article or built by yourself, will help you make the same cute interior doll yourself.
