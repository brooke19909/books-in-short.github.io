Crystal Flora Beach Resort 5 * (Turkey, Kemer, Beldibi): description of rooms, service, reviews

If you plan to spend an unforgettable vacation with family or friends, consider an option like Crystal Flora Resort. This is a modern institution that meets international standards. It will appeal to both those who love silence and tranquility, and those who love active pastime.

Hotel Crystal Flora: room description

Comfortable living conditions - this is one of the most important components of quality relaxation. In the Crystal Flora resort you will be offered the following options for accommodation:

  • Standard apartments are 19-26 square meters. m can accommodate from 2 to 4 guests. The room consists of a bedroom and a bathroom, as well as a private balcony or terrace. The room has a bed, sofa bed. Instead, there may be a bunk bed for children. 6 rooms of a standard category are specially equipped for guests with reduced mobility.
  • Family rooms are 37 square meters. m. They consist of two bedrooms and are designed to accommodate four guests. The adult bedroom has a large bed, a desk, a pair of soft armchairs and a balcony. Room for children. Equipped with twin beds or bunk.
  • King Suite is a 55 square meter luxury apartment. m. They include a spacious living room with a soft set and a bright comfortable bedroom with a king size bed. There is a private furnished terrace.

Catering establishments

Guests of the Crystal Flora Resort will enjoy quality drinks and delicious food throughout their stay. Meals are made in such institutions:

  • Aspendos is the main restaurant. Here, Crystal Flora guests receive a full four-course buffet meal. There is also a night buffet.
  • Barbecue restaurant Flora offers guests Crystal Flora 5 * to taste a wide range of dishes prepared on the grill. This is meat, and seafood, and vegetables. Meals are served in a relaxing setting on the outdoor terrace. For dishes you will be offered fresh beer or aromatic wine.
  • The Mexican-style La Fiesta restaurant serves traditional Latin American dishes. The range of establishments will appeal to lovers of spicy dishes. The atmosphere is supported by fun, traditional music.
  • Beach Club is one of Crystal Flora 's favorite destinations. The institution works in the mornings and offers guests a choice of several breakfast options, traditional for different countries of the world.
  • Starfish Fish Restaurant will delight you with a wide assortment of seafood caught in the Mediterranean. Quality wine complements the meal, while live music creates a romantic atmosphere.
  • Azur - this Mediterranean restaurant will delight you with delicious, hearty and at the same time healthy dishes. The main emphasis is on fish.
  • Ottoman is an institution specializing in traditional Ottoman cooking. This place is a must visit for those who want to fully understand the versatility of Turkey.
  • The lounge bar will delight guests with Crystal Flora 5 * pastries and aromatic hot drinks in the evening. Here at night you can eat sandwiches.
  • The snack bar will welcome visitors who want to eat in the afternoons. The institution specializes in salads and burgers.
  • The pool bar is a great place to satisfy your hunger between main meals. Right before your eyes, the chefs will prepare fresh cakes, pies and manti. And in the afternoon you will be treated to delicious Turkish ice cream.
  • The beach bar begins its work from noon. If you do not want to go to a restaurant for lunch, you can eat delicious pizza or salad right on the beach.
  • Cafe Turk specializes in aromatic invigorating coffee. There is also a wide range of other non-alcoholic drinks.
  • Five bars with alcoholic and soft drinks in different points of the hotel.

additional information

If you have chosen the Crystal Flora hotel for your holidays, do not be too lazy to find out more information regarding relaxing in this institution. Here are some information you should pay the closest attention to:

General impression

The first thing that catches your eye is the outdated interior (moreover, it is outdated not only physically, but also morally). Also, not everyone likes animation. Convenient location makes the hotel an excellent starting point for self-discovery of local attractions.
