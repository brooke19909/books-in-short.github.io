Kislovodsk Dolphinarium: description, schedule, prices

The resort town of Kislovodsk, located in the Stavropol Territory, is considered one of the most common holiday destinations throughout Russia. In this amazing and picturesque place, clean and healing air. Every year a huge number of tourists and travelers come to the resort city. Kislovodsk is also often visited by couples with young children, so the resort has a lot of all kinds of entertainment and places to relax.

Dolphinarium (Kislovodsk): description

One of the favorite establishments for all local residents and visitors is the dolphinarium. Children and adults enjoy watching marine animal performances.



The surrounding atmosphere allows visitors to plunge into the mysterious world of the underwater kingdom so that they can learn more about the inhabitants of the seas.

Species of animals that are in the dolphinarium

  • Walruses are able to surprise any person by demonstrating their skills acquired in training.
  • Northern fur seals will demonstrate their incredible abilities to all visitors to the dolphinarium. The dance performed by them will remain in the memory of every small spectator for a long time.

Most Popular Artists

Of course, as in any other dolphinarium, dolphins are the most popular and favorite animals for visitors. There are several varieties of them in the organization of Kislovodsk. All of them are active, very sociable and artistic. Therefore, children wish to return again and again to the dolphinarium (Kislovodsk). And what kinds of these mammals are in this institution?





  • Irrawaddy dolphins differ from other similar inhabitants with their convex head and flexible neck. The length of their body can reach one and a half meters.
  • Humpback dolphins most often have a milky white or grayish color. The length of their body can reach two and a half meters. These marine inhabitants got their name because of the elongated hump located on the back.
  • Beluga dolphins are endowed with a very cute appearance. Their plain color, which acquires a bluish-gray tint with age, makes them very unusual. Belugas can grow up to 6 meters in length. Therefore, their ideas are very interesting to watch.
  • Bottlenose dolphins are probably considered the most traditional inhabitants of any dolphinarium. You can recognize them by the dark gray body color and pronounced "beak".

All dolphins are trained by competent trainers and are the main participants in the performance. They are able to make five-meter jumps, which look very impressive, play with the ball and even draw pictures. Flip-flops, somersaults, swimming on the back, incredible tricks with trainers - all this includes the performance of dolphins. Naturally, these are not all the amazing numbers that these kind animals can fulfill.

Duration of show, shooting and video

The duration of the program is one hour. All this time, children and adults are told about all the animals that are present at the performance. Therefore, a trip to the dolphinarium (Kislovodsk) can be not only an entertainment event, but also a way to obtain useful information. For children, this form of presentation of unfamiliar facts about animals is the most optimal. Scientists have long proved that the child during the game process is able to absorb much more information.





During an exciting performance, it is allowed to photograph the most amazing moments, as well as shoot everything on a camcorder. Memories of dolphin stunts and experienced trainers who fearlessly dive, somersault and play with animals in water and on land will remain with visitors forever.

Swimming with a dolphin

Psychotherapists argue that close communication with sea animals can liberate any child, make him more open and less shy. Dolphinarium (Kislovodsk) provides an opportunity for everyone to swim in the pool with a dolphin.



There will be nothing better for a child than, having plunged into the water and grabbed the fin of a marine inhabitant, to swim a circle around the pool. Such an adventure will cause the most positive emotions even in an adult, to say nothing about children.

Dolphin therapy

It has long been known that communication with dolphins favorably affects children with various abnormalities. Many parents are ready to go anywhere to improve the perception of the outside world in a sick baby.

When the child is in close proximity to the dolphin, ultrasound signals from the marine life reach the brain. They can improve the brain activity of a child with a rare disease. Dolphins also favorably affect the vestibular apparatus, strengthen coordination of movements and much more. Therefore, dolphin therapy sessions are so in demand among children with diseases of the brain, childhood paralysis and others. Invites kids to swim with these good mammals and Kislovodsk.

Dolphinarium: work schedule and ticket price

The dolphinarium works all year round, so getting tickets to the performance will not be difficult. The only day off at this place is Monday.



On weekdays, the entertainment program with the participation of marine inhabitants begins at exactly 15:00. On weekends, due to the large flow of visitors, dolphins perform twice a day at 11:00 and 15:00.

The cost of one adult ticket is 400 rubles, a child ticket can be purchased for 300 rubles. Kids who are not yet three years old, admission is completely free.

Conclusion

Now you know how remarkable Kislovodsk is. Dolphinarium, the prices of which are quite reasonable, is waiting for guests almost daily.

An incredible, breathtaking performance can plunge all visitors into a real fairy tale. Therefore, being in the resort town of Kislovodsk, it is recommended to plan a trip to this amazing place.
