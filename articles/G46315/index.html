The main causes of the Russian economic crisis

There are at least five of them:

  1. The critical situation in Ukraine, which led to a decrease in a number of economic indicators, and growing corruption cause an accelerated outflow of capital (according to many experts, this will continue in the coming year 2015).
  2. Sanctions put forward by the Eurozone, Australia, the USA, Canada and other countries.
  3. The decline in Russia's competitiveness due to the outflow of foreign investment due to the above sanctions.
  4. Direct dependence on oil prices.
  5. The fall of the national currency and the inflation of interest rates of the Central Bank due to capital flight.

Equity Investing

This is the most popular investment tool to date. Its popularity is determined by a number of exceptional advantages, as a result of which it is leading among the rest of the investment tools. However, in order to ensure maximum efficiency from securities, you need to have a certain amount of knowledge regarding the investment process.

Stock trading is a fairly simple process because a substantial amount of data is stored electronically. If the required knowledge is not available, then it is worth entrusting the trade to a professional trader. He organizes tenders on favorable terms for commission fees.

Advantages of the investment instrument in question

Investing in stocks is beneficial for the following reasons:

  • annual return on shares is 100%;
  • the share price is set based on the profit of the company behind it; this profit is a derivative of inflation (directly, the art of company managers brings additional profit to the shareholder).

The disadvantages of this investment tool

Many people consider investing in stocks as an inexpedient solution, since:

  • they have a volatility property (under the influence of the market, the value of shares may drop sharply);
  • the shareholder will be able to make a profit in the last turn (first, payments are made to suppliers, employees, creditors, etc.).

Investment service PAMM-account

Literally translated as a module of the percentage separation of profits. As an investment service, this is a form of trust management, which allows the investor to profit at the Forex market, attracting professional traders for this.

Its principle of action: investors (any person who has invested in PAMM accounts) make contributions to the general account with which the manager (trader) of their choice carries out the appropriate manipulations. As a result, the profit (loss) received is distributed based on the investor's share in the total account.

Security is ensured by blocking the ability of investors to withdraw funds from their trader. The investor, in turn, can withdraw funds due to him at any time convenient for him.

A prerequisite for the manager is to deposit his own funds into the PAMM account. This is due to the sharing of risks (a trader, making transactions, risks his own means). For each profit earned, a fee is due to him, the amount of which is attributed in the agreement (20-50%).

In order to protect investment funds from their complete loss during trading on PAMM accounts, it is possible to set a loss limit to a certain level. It is also realistic to adhere to a zero risk strategy.
