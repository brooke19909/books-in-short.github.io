"Paradise island" (Hurghada) and its secrets

On our globe there are many heavenly corners, one of them is located in a fishing village on the Red Sea (Egypt). This is "Paradise Island" (Hurghada). It is for those tourists who like to combine leisure and entertainment. The island is completely surrounded by a reef, once you get there, you will marvel at the untouched nature, a fabulous beach, indescribable beauty of the sea and a variety of water activities and restaurants. But you can’t tell about all the charms. Let's imagine a little and imagine that we are going on a short trip. Destination - the country of Egypt, "Paradise Island" (Hurghada). But how to get there?

How to be in paradise

If you decide to spend your vacation on the coast of Hurghada, then we advise you to book an excursion to Paradise Island. You can get to the island by boat, yacht or boat, accompanied by a guide. During the tour you will spend a fantastic day on a desert island, enjoy its beauty, explore life under water and get an unforgettable experience. After about 45 minutes of sea voyage, you will find yourself in paradise.

Hurghada, "Paradise Island": photo, fauna and flora

So, you arrived at the place. Fresh sea air envelops you in a warm wave.

First of all, we will visit the beach and sea of ​​the “Paradise Island” or “Paradise” - this is what the locals call it. This name was given for stunning beaches and the purest Red Sea. The water in the sea is warm and striking in its purity and beauty. It has all shades of blue and green. A beach of the finest snow-white soft and literally airy sand. Umbrellas with sunbeds are waiting for tourists on a fabulous beach, under which you can relax and hide from the hot rays of the sun.
Umbrellas are covered with palm leaves. The beach has cozy bars serving chilled drinks and exotic dishes. When you arrive on Paradise Island (Hurghada), you will be amazed by the magnificent views of the rainforests and the amazing singing of exotic birds. The island is famous for its proximity to large multi-colored coral reefs.
Their height reaches 7-8 meters. Coral reefs are quite far from the sandy shore. The most interesting can be seen under water, where tens of thousands of different fish swim (parrot fish, Napoleon fish) and other inhabitants (squids, sea ​​urchins, stingrays, dolphins).


Paradise Island (Hurghada): Things to Do

Paradise will delight those tourists who prefer an active holiday. Here you can go diving and snorkeling. You can swim surrounded by tropical fish, dolphins, stingrays, and those who like to tickle their nerves can see different types of sharks. The island has canoe rental and the necessary equipment for immersion in water.



Paradise Island (Hurghada) is a colorful and amazing piece of the Earth where you can admire the underwater flora and fauna. This magical place fully justifies its name. In this place I want to stay for life. A fabulous beach, an amazing gentle sea, coral reefs with their inhabitants attract more and more new tourists, revealing to them the secrets of the Red Sea. Amazing memories of the island are a piece of paradise, which will remain with you forever!
