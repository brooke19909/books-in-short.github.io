Tikhaya Bay, Sakhalin: photos and reviews of tourists

Russia's largest island, Sakhalin, resembling a fish in shape, lies in the Sea of ​​Okhotsk and the Sea of ​​Japan. Large enough, it occupies a significant part of the Sakhalin Oblast, including the Kuril Islands. This is not just an island, but a rich source of natural resources that attracts scientists and travelers, naturalists and incorrigible romantics. It differs from many others in the variety of landscapes and a unique mixture of Russian and Japanese cultures.





Cozy corner

But today we want to draw your attention only to the Makarovsky district. It is here that Tikhaya Bay is located. Sakhalin is an object unique in nature. It is here that you understand very well how different Russia can be. It is cold and harsh, but there is an exception to this rule, and this is Tikhaya Bay . Sakhalin is perfect for recreation and fishing, you can have a wonderful vacation here.





general description

On many maps, a tiny bay is simply not marked. This is a real miracle of nature, which is worth your attention. If the spirit of explorer and traveler lives in you, then you will surely enjoy Tikhaya Bay. Sakhalin is a place where absolutely everything is bizarre: amazing cliffs and lush vegetation, huge stones, unpredictable sea and noisy gulls. Wherever you look, everywhere you see exactly what your imagination is capable of. A stone “elephant” or “turtle”, and above them a soft blue veil and an amazing pattern of white clouds. It is impossible to tell which Tikhaya Bay is. Sakhalin is an extraordinary island that you need to see with your own eyes.





History

Let us recall a little about those times when real navigators and travelers plowed the seas and plotted coastlines, bays and bays. Quiet bay (Sakhalin, Makarovsky district) is an extremely picturesque and amazing place, which is located in the bay of Terpeniya. She received this name from the Russian navigator Ivan Kruzenshtern. Rosehip and aralia, thermopsis and other vegetation are growing on the territory. By the way, sand in the bay is only in the coastal strip. The rest of the territory is covered by rocks and sharp stones.

Mariners surprisingly accurately gave names to objects. In the middle of the bay there is a rocky island “Noticeable”. It has a huge number of noisy gulls, so getting to it slowly is very difficult. Here are ideal conditions to enjoy nature or fishing, sailing in a quiet boat.



Winter adventure

If it seems to you that with the onset of cold weather this world plunges into dullness and despondency, then this is the biggest misconception. The biggest miracle of nature is Tikhaya Bay, chained in winter shackles. Sakhalin, whose photo can often be found in the reports of travelers, is not disclosed immediately. But the closer you know its essence, the better you can know its soul. In winter, numerous streams, streams and rivers turn into colorful icefalls.

This can not be described in words. Chilled waterfalls, from tiny and fragile, a meter high, to stunning giants that inspire awe and admiration at the same time. There are a lot of such icefalls; they adorn the rocks of the Zhdanko ridge along the entire coast. Here, in white silence, you understand best why the bay is called Quiet.





Coordinates

In fact, there is nothing difficult in getting to know one’s own natural wonder such as Tikhaya Bay (Sakhalin). How to get there, we'll talk now. The route is not at all difficult. Everyone can modify it a little. We will follow the path from Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, as an example. From the specified settlement, leave in the north direction and move in the given direction to the sign with the inscription c. Quiet. You will see him after passing the village of Vzmorye.





Most importantly, keep heading north and don't turn off. Feel free to pass the village of Ilyinsk, today it is not interesting to you. After the sign, turn right. Just a quarter of a kilometer - and you will see a railway crossing. To get to your destination, continue straight along the road to the fork. Here you turn left, drive through a small fishing village, and Tikhaya Bay (Sakhalin) is ahead. How to get there, you can additionally look at the map so that along the way you have a reliable reference point at hand.

Weather

There are not many places in Russia where the weather is just as moody. But perhaps this is precisely what attracts tourists to Tikhaya Bay (Sakhalin). Reviews emphasize that you can only appreciate the beauty of the majestic and cold nature by breathing in the air of these places. Therefore, get out of the house and venture on a real adventure.





The climate here is cool and humid, fogs often occur. The average air temperature is -24 degrees in the winter, and +19 ° C in the summer. That is, the melted ice in the middle of summer near the coast is a normal phenomenon. Winter lasts from October to May, heavy snowfalls are characteristic. In the mountains, snow cover can reach 5 meters. Due to the proximity of the Pacific, typhoons often occur. Therefore, winter travel to Sakhalin is an adventure for an amateur, it is better to choose the height of summer.

Island decoration

Rocks, caves with stalagmites, stone grottoes and lakes, streams and waterfalls - these are the harsh beauty of these places. Unusual, charming, she reverently keeps her secrets for someone who delves into this unique world. To explore this part of the island, it is recommended to move along the perimeter of the sea edge. The path is not the easiest, here you have to wade into a narrow rocky slit, then conquer the water surface. A grotto hangs from above, from which a real waterfall pours in places. Powerful streams destroy century-old rocks year after year, forming new faults and stones of bizarre shapes.





The largest waterfalls

There are a lot of them. If you are fascinated by the sight of falling water, then Tikhaya Bay (Sakhalin) is an ideal option for spending your holidays. Waterfalls are so common here that you definitely will not pass by. One of the largest and most beautiful is called Klokovsky. It is ideal for families with children, as the route is simple, and there is a small picnic area nearby.





There are two slightly smaller waterfalls on the Ugledarka River. The lower one is especially beautiful, but the path to it is rather complicated and in some places dangerous. Therefore, it is better to leave children and the elderly to wait for you in the parking lot. At a good pace, all the waterfalls of the Makarovsky district can be examined in one day and returned home in a good mood.

An amazing sight is represented by many waterfalls in winter. New and new are layered on the frozen jets of water, forming columns and arches. This creates stunning icefalls that are impossible to watch without admiration.

Tourists reviews

The nature of Sakhalin is amazing and harsh. This is noted by everyone who has been here once. The sun peeks out, and the water shines with blue azure, gently beckoning tourists to him. But do not flatter yourself, swimming here is not recommended even in the midst of summer. But you can sunbathe under a good combination of circumstances.





However, it’s worth a little to change the breeze, as the fog falls, it becomes damp and cold. It remains only to make a fire and bask. But for the sake of the surrounding beauty it is worth to endure the vagaries of nature, which reverently protects this region from uninvited guests. All those who have been here once say they will definitely be back.
