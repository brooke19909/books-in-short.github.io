What is Cyprus in May?

Cyprus is a fairy tale for vacationers, a paradise. In this place you forget about everyday problems and enjoy the magnificent scenery, the velvet sun, the clean and warm Mediterranean Sea. And what will be the rest in Cyprus in May? This topic is dedicated to this article.

Detailed information

Summer in this country starts in May. This month begins an active flow of tourists. The weather in Cyprus in May is already quite warm. Although short rains and slight gusts of wind are possible at the beginning of the month, the sun is already warming. Weather conditions are suitable for a pleasant holiday on the beach by the sea. Air temperature reaches 27-30 degrees. heat, water in Cyprus in May warms up to 21 0 . Although the daytime air has warmed enough, evenings and nights remain still cool.





In May, the water is still not very comfortable for swimming. But besides swimming, there is something to do. For example, you can go look at the sights, which, by the way, are plentiful here. At this time, you can visit the ancient fortress, which is located in part of Famagusta, the ruins and ruins of the old city, monasteries and fortresses. Cyprus in May will surprise with a riot of greenery. At this time, the organizers offer tourists excursions to the forests and walks in the mountains. You need to be careful here, as snakes can be found on forest trails.

The country is famous for its resorts.

So, Cyprus in May ... Where is warmer in this country? To make it clearer, we will consider some popular resorts.





Larnaca - one of the largest resorts in Cyprus, here is the main airport for international airlines. Larnaca is surrounded by sandy beaches, which must be reached through palm groves. There are bars, cafes, restaurants, spa hotels. In addition, you can relax on a yacht at sea. By the way, here, as in other resorts, there is a water park and a dolphinarium.

Ayia Napa, which is located near Larnaca, will also shock with a riot of colors and entertainment. It is believed that this is the most visited and warmest resort in Cyprus. The water here is crystal clear at any depth, you can observe the marine life, everything is perfectly visible. By the way, a park called "Dinosaur" was recently opened here . It has everything for children's recreation and entertainment. If you are a fan of golf, then for you in the resort of Ayia Napa has a separate field. Those who prefer diving can go on a trip with a separate group. If you love everything unknown, then you can visit the caves.

Limassol

The main port is located here, as well as the center for winemaking. Limassol can also be called the economic capital. The resort is interesting for its Kolossi castle, getting into which tourists unwittingly find themselves in the 18th century, when the Knights of the Knights Templar lived. You can also visit the excavations of the ancient town of Amathus. In addition, of course, conduct excursions to the wineries. Here, by the way, you can taste your favorite drink.





Paphos is located away from the sea. This resort has a very well developed infrastructure. Also here are the tombs of kings and emperors of the 3rd century BC. And most importantly - this is the "cliffs of Aphrodite", the most memorable in this marvelous place.

Policy

This is a small town that will appeal to lovers of peace and quiet. There are no noisy attractions and a large number of bars and restaurants. On narrow streets there are private pensions.

Another small resort is Kyrenia. Here, traditions of local cuisine from ancient times have been preserved. European dishes at this resort you can hardly taste.





A large number of festivals take place here. They are held in the city center. Oddly enough, May 1 celebrate the feast of Labor. In addition, there is an interesting celebration called Flower Festival, during which florists exhibit their works from plants. During this period, the whole street seems to come to life, it becomes incredibly beautiful and vibrant. Cyprus in May visits a large number of music groups, actors and artists. Therefore, if you come here at this time, you will not be bored.

Many tourists claim that May is the most suitable month for a vacation in this country, since the sun does not burn much, the tan is uniform with a chocolate tint, and the water is already good. Doctors advise exactly at this time to visit the resort for couples who take the kids to the sea. This period is the most favorable for rest and recovery of children.

Cyprus in May is especially beautiful. Be sure to take the time and see the sights of the country. If you visit many excursions, then you will probably want to have a satisfying meal. The cuisine here is diverse, you can find European, traditional Cypriot, Asian restaurants, in addition, there are oriental delicacies.





There is an interesting nuance - tip is not accepted, they are automatically included in the price of breakfast, lunch or dinner. In a taxi, you can leave a few dollars extra.

Price range

As for the prices of tours in May, the price is quite loyal compared to July-September. But tour operators advise booking hotels in hotels in advance. Since many people want to visit Cyprus on May holidays because of favorable weather conditions. A large number of clothes will not be needed, only the main thing, in hotels they will provide everything from towels to personal hygiene products. Prices for visiting night discos, excursions and other events are varied, you can choose options for any wallet.

Conclusion

Cyprus in May will leave a lot of positive impressions for many years. Therefore, if you want to relax mentally, then go to this country. Now you know what Cyprus is in May. Decide if this resort is right for you or not.
