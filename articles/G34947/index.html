How to watch TV on an iPhone using the application?

How can I watch TV on an iPhone? What are there for this application? This will be discussed in our article. Despite the fact that everyone in the house has a TV, many users of Apple devices are interested in how to watch TV on an iPhone. A step-by-step instruction on how to do this will be presented in the article. We will also consider different options for gaining access to channels on our smartphone.

There is no standard application for viewing. You have to download it. It is only necessary to decide which application to use for viewing.

Yunisov TV app: description

You will find this application in the AppStore as one of the best. IPhone users download this utility most often. It's all about the large number of channels provided for viewing. There are about 130 of them in Yunosov. However, it is worth noting that some of them require paid subscriptions. Another plus is the competent distribution of channels by category. There is a TV channel program.

Although this application looks decent, among the disadvantages it is worth noting the price of the application. It is not free and costs 429 rubles (as of 2018, so the current price may be outdated). However, this price is offset by the lack of ongoing advertising or offers to subscribe to some paid content or a separate channel. The application is recommended for use if the user of the iPhone has the opportunity to pay for it.

Crystal TV + app

To watch TV on your iPhone for free, you can download Crystal TV +. This is a free app. For this reason, Crystal TV + is particularly popular among iPhone users.

But in addition to the above advantages, the following can be attributed to the pluses:

  • the ability to increase the volume while viewing inside the application, as well as adjust the brightness of the broadcast image;
  • in parallel, you can watch the broadcast of one channel and view what is shown on others.

Cons of the Crysral TV + application:

  • a small number of channels for free;
  • the need for a subscription to view most of the proposed channels (169 rubles per month as of 2018);
  • In the free version, an advertisement often pops up and an offer to subscribe to the paid version of the application.

Despite a number of shortcomings, many users are quite happy with the free version of Crystal TV +.

Tviz service: description and features

This application provides the ability to view more than twenty channels for free. Paid downloads and monthly subscription fees include about 200 channels.

The peculiarity is the fact that the channels can only be watched live, as expected from the broadcast. That is, in this application there is no function of rewinding the air or pause during the broadcast.

The cost of subscribing to all provided TV channels is 229 rubles or 29 rubles per month (information may be outdated as of 2018).

The application itself can not but rejoice with its number of available channels on a paid basis. However, in the free version, ads and offers for a paid subscription systematically pop up, which makes it difficult to enjoy watching TV in full.

Lime HD TV app

Lime HD TV is a good app to watch TV on your iPhone. It includes more than 200 channels and 50 of them are free. The features of this application include the exclusivity of using the free version. It can be used for free only on mobile devices, including an iPhone.

Pluses of the application for iPhones:

  • wide range of channels;
  • on a free basis you can watch 50 channels;
  • You can make a subscription, after which 49 rubles per month will be debited, while the complete absence of advertising is guaranteed.

Lime HD TV application minus:

  • the ability to use the free version only on mobile devices;
  • You need a paid subscription to watch TV on devices other than mobile.

This application is very well suited for those who want to watch a minimum of channels, but without ads, when paying only 49 rubles per month.

Application SPB TV Russia

This application is a great option for those who want to systematically watch TV on their iPhone. It includes more than 100 channels, of which about 80 are provided for free.

A huge base of both TV channels and films in general allows SPB TV Russia to be brought to the forefront compared to other similar applications.

Those who want to watch TV on an iPhone should pay attention to this utility. Since it has a lot of advantages, namely:

  • a large number of free channels (about 80);
  • the ability to record broadcasts;
  • the ability to watch movies separately from TV broadcasts;
  • the ability to connect a file cabinet and disable ads at the same time for 99 rubles per month;
  • large file cabinet of films and series.

Cons of SPB TV Russia:

  • the free version has a huge amount of advertising,
  • the cost of connecting a file cabinet and turning off advertising (99 rubles per month).
  • the cost of full functionality (of all channels, a cinema hall and disabling of advertising) is 250 rubles per month.

This application is well suited for fans to see something interesting, but who do not know what to choose. A movie library will provide a good choice for every taste and color. Everyone here will find everything that their heart desires, for 250 rubles a month.

TV + application

TV + is a great app for those who want to watch TV via iPhone. It is intended for use only by the iOS system. That is, looking for this application in the Play Market is useless. It is exclusively in the AppStore. The application can be used on all Apple devices.

Advantages of the program:

  • free use of this application;
  • the ability to control the volume and brightness of the transmitted image inside the application.

Cons of TV +:

  • only Russian TV channels;
  • applies only to the territory of the Russian Federation.

For residents of the Russian Federation, this is a very profitable application to watch TV online on an iPhone.

Application "Yandex.Telemarket"

This application is used only in web-mode (via PC). If you use an iPhone, you can use the Yandex.Telemarket online service. This is not an application. You can use it through any browser installed on your iPhone.

Pros: the availability of all channels of this online resource. Cons of the application: you cannot use the application on an iPhone (only through a browser).

Conclusion

Now you know how to watch TV on an iPhone, what programs are useful for this. We examined the most famous applications for these purposes. As a result, we can safely say that you can watch TV with all the conveniences of an iPhone through a program only by purchasing their paid version. However, there is an exception - this is the TV + application. There you can watch all the content for free, but only within the territory of the Russian Federation.

In all other cases, it is necessary to purchase paid versions of the application so that you do not get constantly pop-up ads while watching your favorite show. There are applications that include movie theaters.
