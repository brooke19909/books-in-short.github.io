general description

The townspeople note that before they did not have the opportunity to combine a Sunday holiday, a walk with the children and shopping. Now it is no longer necessary to give preference to something. It is enough to go to the shopping center "North" in Orenburg. At first glance it is clear that this center deserves the closest attention.

This is a fashionable and modern entertainment complex, which is located at the intersection of the main transport arteries. This is Teatralnaya Street and Dzerzhinsky Avenue. From here you can get to almost anywhere in the city, as well as back. Spacious parking areas with 24-hour access are provided.



Opinion of the townspeople

Most reviews note that the appearance of the shopping center Sever in Orenburg was very pleased with its scale. The huge complex makes it possible to have a great time even for those who do not like to spend it on shopping trips. The interior of the four-story complex itself offers shopping. For convenience, there are escalators and passenger elevators. There is even one panoramic, which is very fond of families with children. Conditions allow you to spend all day shopping, with breaks for lunch and entertainment.



Main advantages

We will not deny that even a large shopping complex is not a novelty for a city like Orenburg. Shopping center "North" differs from them not only in its scale. Let's look at the main advantages:

  • First of all, drivers like driving here. No need to look for where to put the transport.
  • The second point is a wide range of products. There are almost 300 shops on the territory where each of you can choose what he likes.
  • It really has everything you need. If the husband needs to look into the bank, his wife goes shopping, and the children require entertainment, then here you will appreciate a universal solution. On the territory there is a bank branch, a family cafeteria, a grocery hypermarket, a cinema and a contact zoo.
  • Favorable conditions for tenants are created here. As a result, empty space never happens. This, in turn, is reflected in the value of the goods, because rent is also included in it.

What do we get as a result? A single space where you can find everything you want. At the same time, administrators ensure that the products sold are of appropriate quality and create conditions for the formation of competitive value. Finally, besides the shops, entertainment awaits you.



Working hours

Shops of the shopping center "North" in Orenburg are mostly open from 09:00 to 22:00. However, the shopping center itself is still operating, so some of them continue to operate until 23:00. The cinema ends after the last session. Usually at 22:00 the upper floors are empty, only spectators and visitors of the hypermarket, which occupies the first floor, remain.

Today, there is a huge, grocery hypermarket, where only fresh products, pastries and ready-made gastronomy are constantly presented. The second floor is mostly boutiques with clothes for men and women, as well as for children. There are quite a few boutiques with shoes, cell phones and accessories for them. The third and fourth floors - home goods, electronics, furs, gifts and souvenirs, and much more. Good reviews can be found about the Golden Empire jewelry salon. Shopping center "North" in Orenburg is not the only outlet in the city. In addition to her, there are two more who are also ready to offer you a rich assortment of jewelry, simple and exquisite, with and without precious stones.

Fun for kids

Going to the cinema is not all that you can offer your child. If you go shopping, then he can create the first masterpiece in the children's studio "Orange Elephant". Every day, from 10:00 to 20:30 you will find interesting master classes on the following topics:

  • Painting on ceramics.
  • Crafts from the mass for modeling.
  • Work with ball plasticine.
  • Unique pieces of stained glass paints.

Young talents are constantly found here and genius is revealed in a small visitor.

Igrodrom

This is a real children's town, with a dry pool of colored balls with interactive attractions. In the maze, the child can spend all day, make new friends and have fun. There are also slot machines and video games, where the participant will receive prizes for well-aimed shooting. And here you can celebrate the most fun birthdays. Invite your friends and the evening will be as wonderful as your baby deserves.

"Mini Piggy"

This is a small contact zoo. Piglets and chinchillas, rabbits and guinea pigs, squirrels and hedgehogs live here, as well as a large number of other animals. These are funny, furry creatures that are not at all against affection and goodies. All of them can be fed. Administrators guarantee the complete safety of your child. Cleanliness reigns here, animals are examined by a veterinarian on time and adapted to communicate with children. This makes it possible to learn about the environment, the development of new skills. Such lessons make children kinder and teach to love nature.
