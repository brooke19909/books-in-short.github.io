"Maryina Roscha" (LCD): address, reviews

Modern Muscovites have become more selective and demanding in choosing an apartment, they take into account the location, level of transport accessibility and infrastructure. They are equally demanding on the choice of a developer: they look at the projects he has completed and study customer reviews.

If you are looking for an apartment in the capital, not just within the city, but in the very center, close to major attractions, you will surely like our offer. "Maryina Roshcha" - LCD located in the center of the capital, not far from the metro station of the same name. All customers will find excellent architectural performance, author's plans, developed infrastructure and the opportunity to live in one of the best areas of the capital. Feedback from equity holders-buyers who managed to visit the facility and follow all the stages of construction will help to make the most objective review. Of course, the information collected is useful to those who plan to purchase real estate.





about the project

LCD "Maryina Roshcha" - a 15-story building, erected using monolithic technology with underground parking for 96 cars, which can be accessed using a high-speed silent elevator.

The developer is the Mangazeya group. This is a stable, dynamically developing company, which uses modern innovative technologies and unique materials in each project. There are not many projects on the account of this developer, but each of them is a real work of art. On a small area, the specialists of the Mangazeya group manage to arrange comfortable apartments with excellent planning solutions, ample parking, a landscaped yard and the necessary infrastructure.

Architecture LCD "Maryina Roshcha"

"Mangazeya Development" erects a residential complex according to the author's project. Exterior decoration of the house is made using modern technologies and high-quality building materials. The authors of the project, due to the advantageous combination of light and dark bricks, gave the building an attractive appearance that compares favorably with other residential objects.





Modern engineering equipment and a communications system give Maryina Roscha residential complex the status of comfort class housing. For the safety of each resident around the complex, as well as in the underground parking, a video surveillance system will be installed. The developer is considering the option of equipping the territory with a access system.

Location

Many are interested in the location of the LCD "Maryina Roshcha". New Moscow is currently attractive to developers and buyers. But it is unlikely that anyone will refuse an apartment located in the very center of the capital, in the region of the third transport ring. So where exactly is the Maryina Grove residential complex?

Object address: Moscow, 4th St. Maryina Roshcha, d. 3. From the house to the metro station of the same name, only 3 minutes walk, if necessary, 10 minutes you can walk to the metro station "Riga". Near the house there are stops of public land transport, from where you can get to anywhere in the capital.





Flats

The Mangazeya development company in the Maryina Roshcha residential complex offers 88 open-plan apartments to buyers , due to which each resident has the opportunity to arrange the space at his own discretion and implement even the most daring and extraordinary design project.

The developer focused on comfort and convenience, which is why all buyers can count on bright and spacious apartments with excellent views. Residential real estate begins on the second floor of the building, the first floors are reserved for commercial objects: shops, cafes, and a beauty salon.

Free layouts allow you to zone your space as you wish. Currently, many are designing apartments according to the European model, that is, they combine the kitchen and living room, while isolating the bedrooms and recreation areas. The design of apartments also allows you to do this. The windows offer stunning views of the sights of Moscow.





All those who managed to visit the facility note that construction is in full swing. The developer does not save on materials, qualified specialists are involved in the work, trying to provide each resident with a beautiful warm apartment with excellent sound insulation.

Price policy

LCD "Maryina Roshcha" - a project implemented not just in the capital, inside the Moscow Ring Road, but in the very center of the city. That is why it is stupid to count on the budget cost of apartments. Many of those who were interested in the issue of real estate acquisition, even the most modest, note that prices are just cosmic. Of course, not everyone can afford such a luxury. But if you choose an apartment in the center, then take a closer look at the project.





The cost of apartments starts from 11.9 million rubles. And this is under the condition that all apartments are rented with rough finish and require additional "infusion" of funds.

Reviews

The construction deadline is scheduled by the developer for the second quarter of 2018, so the house is not yet completed and not populated. Nevertheless, it is quite attractive for buyers of LCD Maryina Roshcha. Feedback from shareholders is not numerous, but there are those who visited the construction site, sales office and can share their experience.

Transport accessibility can really be attributed to the main and undeniable advantages. The complex is located in the very center of Moscow, so it will be comfortable to live here. Prices are very high. Many say that for such a price you can buy a similar apartment with decoration. But the project as a whole is aimed at wealthy people, for whom free planning and the absence of imposed design solutions is a plus. If a person buys an apartment for an average of 15 million rubles, he will definitely have funds for repairs and an author's design project.





But the infrastructure here is a bit tight. In the area there are only a few kindergartens, which are definitely not enough for the residents of the new house. That is why parents need to pre-arrange a place in the nearest kindergarten. The same situation with schools.
