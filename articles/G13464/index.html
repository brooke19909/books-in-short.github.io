By train

The railway station is one of the main attractions of the city, because it is an architectural monument. Trains depart from here to Novorossiysk, Anapa, and Abkhazia.

Of course, it is easy to get to Krasnodar from Moscow. A reserved seat ticket costs about 2,500 rubles.

There is another station in the city, but it serves exclusively suburban trains.

Trains to Krasnodar leave from the Kursk station. This is a rather tedious route. The road takes 30-36 hours.

By bus

Krasnodar station accepts buses from different cities of Russia. Including from Moscow. A ticket costs about 2,000 rubles. Buses depart four times a week from the Sadovod shopping center, located in the southeast of the capital. The road takes about 18 hours. You can also go to Krasnodar from the Krasnaya Vorota bus station. However, the trip will have to spend almost a day.
