Hotel Magic Life Imperial Resort: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

If you want to have a great rest in Sharm El Sheikh and are looking for a comfortable hotel with its own beach, then Magic Life Imperial Resort 5 * may be a suitable option for you.



General description, location, photo

This hotel is located on the shores of the Red Sea, in Nabq Bay. Naama Bay is 21 kilometers away, and the Old Town is 29 kilometers away. Sharm El Sheikh Airport is just ten kilometers away. So the road to the hotel will take you no more than half an hour.

Magic Life Sharm-El-Sheikh Imperial (Egypt) was built in 2002. In 2010, renovation was carried out here. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãits own territory of the hotel complex is 94.5 thousand square meters. m. The housing stock of the hotel includes 521 rooms. The apartments are located in a complex of 3-storey buildings. There are rooms of the following categories: standard (with garden or pool view), junior suite and suite. Some apartments are non-smoking. Regardless of the category, all rooms have the necessary furniture, air conditioning, TV (including two Russian-language channels), safe, hairdryer, minibar, bathroom with shower, balcony or terrace with a table and chairs. On the floor - ceramic tiles.

The hotel complex has several swimming pools (including for children), sun terraces, sports grounds, tennis courts, and a gym. The hotel also has a spa, beauty salon, several shops and souvenir shops, a diving center, tour desk, laundry service and much more. Guests are offered daytime and evening entertainment.

Housing stock

According to our compatriots, the rooms in this hotel are very decent. So, the apartments are quite spacious, pleasantly decorated, clean, bright. Guests loved the large furnished balconies and terraces (on the ground floors) where they could sit comfortably and relax. Also, guests note comfortable beds, where you can sleep well. There were no complaints about the operation of the equipment. According to vacationers, everything functions without interruption. But if nevertheless some kind of breakdown happens, then immediately inform the reception about it, and the malfunction will be eliminated as soon as possible.

Experienced travelers warn those who plan to stay at the Magic Life Imperial during their vacation that it is worth asking if you can settle in the buildings located closer to the main pool. From here you will be closer to the main restaurant and the beach. This is especially true for those who come to rest with young children, as well as for older people.



Cleaning

There were practically no complaints about the work of the maids in Magic Life Imperial, the reviews of tourists about which we are considering. So, according to our compatriots, they clean here quite well. Of course, if you start to look under the beds and wardrobes, you will probably find dust there, but most of the guests were satisfied with the quality of cleaning. Towels are changed every day, and bed linen is updated a couple of times a week. Also, as necessary, maids replenish supplies of bath accessories.

As you spend, you will also be reported drinks in the minibar. You will not find alcohol in it. So, in the minibar there are several small bottles of Coca-Cola, Fanta and drinking water. These drinks are included in the price of the hotel, so you do not have to pay extra for them.

Also, many travelers in their comments noted that the maids built funny figures from towels and flower petals and leaves on their beds. As our compatriots noted, they will be more willing to do this if you regularly tip them.



Additional service

A pleasant moment for guests who have a memorable date during their stay at the hotel are compliments from Magic Life Imperial. This applies to newlyweds, birthday people and couples celebrating their wedding anniversary. Please note that in the first and third cases, it is necessary to provide the reception administrator with a copy of your marriage certificate. As a compliment, guests are provided with in-room breakfast, a delicious cake, a bottle of good wine and a fruit basket. Tourists really enjoyed such an attentive attitude from the administration.

Population

The hotel Magic Life Imperial Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt) in accordance with internal rules, the checkout time is set. So, the settlement of newly arrived tourists in the rooms they booked should be carried out after two in the afternoon. However, according to our compatriots, in practice, everything happens a little differently. So, if you arrive at the hotel in the morning, then with a high degree of probability the keys to the room will be given to you quickly enough and will not be forced to wait until 14:00. This is a definite plus.

However, as some tourists note, you should be prepared for the fact that the room, which will be offered to you by default, will not satisfy you with its location. Therefore, if you want to immediately get great apartments, then experienced travelers are advised to thank the administrator at the reception with 10-20 dollars, and then you can not worry about your accommodation. By the way, you will not have to carry your heavy bags and suitcases on your own here. A hotel clerk will deliver your luggage directly to the door of your room.



Departure

On the day of departure, the occupied apartments should be vacated by 12 noon. If you plan to leave the hotel only in the evening, you can extend the room for several hours at an additional cost. It is advisable to inform your reception in advance about your intention. However, one can act in another way. So, many tourists leave the room, as it should be, before noon, leave things in the storage room at the reception and continue to use the hotel infrastructure in full.



the Internet

Since most modern people are used to always having the opportunity to be online, reviews about the Club Magic Life Sharm-El-Sheikh Imperial hotel regarding the Internet have received increased attention from travelers planning their holidays. So, judging by the comments of our compatriots, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel complex. However, guests say that in many rooms and in part of the territory, the connection speed is very low, and communication is often interrupted. But in general, wireless Internet can be used here. For those tourists who need to always be online, experienced travelers are advised to buy a SIM card of a local mobile operator with a prepaid Internet package immediately upon arrival in Egypt. This can be done right at the airport or in the city. Such a SIM card will cost you inexpensively (about 10-15 dollars), and you can use it throughout your vacation.

Sea

According to our compatriots, beach vacations in this hotel complex have their own characteristics. So, no doubt, a big plus is the fact that the hotel is located on the very shore of the Red Sea. Therefore, guests can reach their own beach in a leisurely step in just a couple of minutes. The beach here is quite large, clean, sandy, with a sufficient number of sunbeds and umbrellas for everyone. A bar serving drinks and snacks is nearby. However, not all tourists liked the entry into the water. According to them, as in many other Egyptian hotels, you can enter the sea here either directly from the coast or from the pier. But in the first case, you have to go long enough in shallow water. At the bottom there are dead corals, which somewhat interferes with movement. By the way, for the same reason, it is advisable to use special shoes so as not to injure your legs. The pier, on the other hand, seemed to be too long for some travelers. But you can descend from it by a convenient staircase, immediately falling to a depth near a coral reef. A big plus is the fact that a lifeguard is always on duty at the pontoon.

Experienced tourists warn that in the morning you are unlikely to swim near the reef, because at this time a strong wind blows and rescuers hang a red flag, which means that swimming is prohibited. As a rule, the sea becomes calm between two in the afternoon and six in the evening.
