Sunny costume for the matinee

Carnival costumes are very important in organizing various celebrations and New Year's holidays. In the spring days, a number of events are held in school or other children's institutions, dedicated to March 8, Easter and other special occasions. The Sun suit can be useful at any time of the year.

There are several ways to make a costume of the Sun for a girl with your own hands. Consider both simple and more complex options.

Option 1: application

Making your daughter look like a bright summer sun is easy and simple. It is worth digging into clothes and choosing a suitable bright yellow dress. If not, you can buy a cute robe in the store. On the bodice of the dress, sew the application of the sun. It is bought in special stores with sewing accessories or made independently. It looks like this:

  1. We find a suitable image and try to draw the sun, as shown in the figure.
  2. Carefully cut out.
  3. Sew on the bodice of the dress with yellow threads.
  4. You can put a plastic film between the sun and the bodice cloth, cover with linen cloth and support it under the pressure of a hot iron.

Dress with applique prepared for the event. We decorate the girl‚Äôs head with a yellow ribbon.

Option 2: Bezel and a bit of creativity

The second way to create a Sunny costume for a girl with your own hands is a little more complicated and interesting.

We will prepare materials that will help in creating the outfit:

  • any yellow dress or blouse and skirt made of yellow fabric;
  • rags or a piece of satin fabric yellow and orange;
  • cardboard;
  • bezel;
  • paper and a simple pencil;
  • PVA glue;
  • golden tinsel, rain;
  • Czechs or slippers according to the size of the legs;
  • needle, yellow and orange threads;
  • scissors.

Work algorithm:

  1. First, we‚Äôll deal with the outfit, and then with the paraphernalia. We make out a yellow dress. From pieces of orange fabric we cut out the cuffs with the edges of a triangular shape. Sew them to the sleeves, carefully tucking the edges.
  2. In the same way we decorate the collar. Cut a semicircle with spiky rays of orange fabric and sew to the neck. Lay a seam in the middle of each triangle.
  3. The bodice of the dress will become more elegant and bright, if the sun is drawn from sequins or tinsel.
  4. The edge of the dress is decorated with golden tinsel. Sew Czech shoes or slippers with a yellow cloth.
  5. We pass to the headdress. We cut out a kokoshnik on plain paper, adjusting the rim to the size. Kokoshnik draw in the form of a sun with rays. Cut the workpiece. We outline cardboard and fabric parts according to the template. Using PVA glue, glue parts of the headgear. We decorate the kokoshnik with tinsel, beads, sequins. We fix on the rim.

A bright and beautiful Sun costume for a girl, made with her own hands, will serve as an object of pride and admiration for the holiday.

Option 3: voluminous "Sun"

Making an original outfit for a matinee is a creative and interesting job. Every mother who wants to please her princess has the opportunity to create a unique costume for her daughter.

We will make the next suit of the Sun with the help of a headdress and a yellow skirt. For girls, consumables will need very little. We will cut out items of clothing from foam rubber.

Before starting work, we will prepare everything you need:

  • tape measure;
  • a piece of chalk;
  • scissors;
  • yellow and orange fabric dyes;
  • needle, yellow thread;
  • elastic;
  • hot glue.

Work algorithm:

  1. Children's costume "Sun" will consist of two parts: a headdress and a skirt.
  2. Take measurements: head circumference and waist circumference, measure the desired length of the skirt. We make two round patterns to cut out two parts in the shape of a sun-flare from foam rubber. We measure the radius of the circle according to the length of the skirt. Cut the circle in the middle according to the parameters taken from the waist circumference.
  3. We fold the second part in half, make a hole in the middle according to the head circumference.
  4. We fold the two blanks in half and make cuts in the shape of sunlight. We will face two large stars.
  5. According to the instructions, we dilute the paint in the prepared container and paint the foam rubber in yellow.
  6. After drying, sew the elastic to the waist and headgear.
  7. Pieces of foam rubber are painted in orange and glued on top of the yellow rays.

The costume made by the DIY for the girl Sun will create the image of a small bright luminary, pleasing everyone around.
