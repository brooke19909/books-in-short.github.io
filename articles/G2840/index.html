Hotel "Negresco" in Nice - facts from the history of creation

Everyone wants to spend time in a luxury hotel. And if he also has an interesting history, this becomes an additional factor in favor of the institution. A striking example is the 1913 Negresco Hotel. Nice acquired another attraction in the form of this hotel. This is one of the best examples of neoclassical architects, the construction of which at one time had spent more than 3 million francs.

The author of the architectural project was the famous Eiffel himself. According to his idea, the Negresco hotel is crowned with a unique pink dome. According to rumors, the architect was inspired by this decision with the bust of his own wife, but this is not known for certain. Also worth noting is the interior decoration, which boasts the Hotel Negresco (Nice). Photos of salons are simply amazing luxury. What is there a huge crystal chandelier 4 meters high and a unique carpet, which was created specifically for Maria Medici. Also, you can not pass by a unique fireplace, whose weight reaches 10 tons.

Given all of the above, it is not surprising that the hotel "Negresco" fell in love with noble people. Nevertheless, with the onset of the First World War, the building had to be converted to a military hospital. And after the end of hostilities, the owner had to sell it for debts. And only in 1957, when the hotel building became the property of the Ogier family, Negresco regained its former glory. The interior was replenished with numerous works of art. Now it is not just a hotel, but a monument of architecture and history.

It is worth noting that over the many years of existence, many famous personalities visited the hotel "Negresco" (Nice). The history of creation and work would be incomplete without mentioning such names as Ernest Hemingway, Coco Chanel, Marlene Dietrich, Albert Camus, Francoise Sagan, Jean Cocteau, Pablo Picasso, Michael Jackson, Salvador Dali, Yves Montand, Catherine Deneuve, Gina Lollobrigida and many others.
