What is the most logical explanation for such actions? Let's think it over. In fact, everything is terribly complicated at the same time, but also extremely simple. Production material

Color schemes





HTC always knew a lot about the design of its smartphones. The same goes for HTC Desire 500 Dual SIM, the characteristics of which are useful to study before buying. It is presented on the market in several colors. If you take the white variation, then it is divided into turquoise and red. At the same time, there are some practical reasons that increase the popularity of light devices. The fact is that on white panels fingerprints are not as noticeable as on black ones.

Front panel





It is made in the shape of a small arch. A similar solution was used in such smartphones of the company as HTC OneX, as well as HTC OneX +. The front panel is not protected against mechanical damage like scratches. This we can rightly call the first serious drawback. The fact is that if you put HTC Desire 500 Dual SIM, reviews of which will be given at the end of this article, on a dirty table with the screen down, then most likely there will be small scratches on the front panel. In addition to the display itself, you can find a front camera under the glass. In the lower part according to the standard we have control keys. These two elements are located on opposite sides.

Top end

Not all of the front part is covered with a protective glass. And here, where it ends, and ordinary plastic begins, the lattice of the main spoken speaker is located. At the top is a 3.5 mm jack, which is designed to connect a wired stereo headset to the phone. Nearby there is a key that allows you to lock the device. It is also designed to turn it on and off. The location of these elements, it should be noted, is a bit unusual. It is impossible to explain such a move with any logical arguments.

Right side

Owner reviews

In general, for the cost that the model has on the smartphone market, it can be called very good. Users have repeatedly noted the small amount of long-term memory that is available for storing data. In general, 4 GB is built into the device. But not everything is available, given that the operating system selects a good percentage. However, cloud storage is available to phone owners, which allows you to save 15 GB of data. In addition, you can always buy a memory card and use it successfully.

The battery to the device is optimally selected. This is a lithium-ion battery with a capacity of about 1800 mAh. It is removable, allowing the phone to work at a calm pace all day. But obviously it is not necessary to count on more. Custom software device is not burdened. There is a proprietary interface installed on top of the operating system.
