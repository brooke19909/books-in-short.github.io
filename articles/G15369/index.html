How to make a scorpion out of paper: two detailed diagrams

Origami is the folding of a square sheet of paper of various three-dimensional figures. Originating in the countries of the East, this type of creativity conquered the whole world. Such an exciting hobby does not require large investments of money. It is enough to purchase thin white or colored paper of A-4 format, from which a large square is cut. It is most convenient to make a blank for origami by bending one of the corners to the opposite side. It remains only to cut off the excess rectangular strip with scissors and the square is ready. You can start the work.

In the article, we will consider how to make a scorpion out of paper. This formidable creature can be used for games, composing compositions or for exhibiting at school. A voluminous craft is easy to perform according to origami schemes, acting step by step. Let's consider one of them in more detail.

Step-by-step scheme

The scorpion has a rather complicated structure, therefore, the paper workflow consists of 23 successive folds. You must act according to the serial number in the direction indicated by the arrows. A dashed line indicates the location of the fold. A thin black arrow indicates the direction of rotation of the paper, and a wide white arrow is used to push the inner crease.

So, in the figure above, the third step is to insert a finger between the paper layers and stretch the workpiece in the opposite direction. The broken arrow suggests folding the part with an accordion. So the scorpion tail is going. Where there is an image of scissors, you need to make cuts along the red line. Now you know how to make origami a scorpion from paper according to the scheme.

Option without hind legs

Let's look at another step-by-step diagram of assembling a scorpion from a square sheet of paper. As usual, all steps are performed alternately, acting according to the sequence numbers of the circuit.

Folds are sharp and smooth with fingers or a ruler. For work, it is most convenient to use thin paper, because you need to perform a lot of folds, and bending a stack of paper into several layers in the right direction is not easy.

Before finally ironing a bend, check its correctness and symmetry of one and the other half. This is the most difficult in paper origami, since it is hard to bend the paper at the same angles by eye, and asymmetry will make the work untidy and skewed.

Final result

If all the actions are performed correctly, then the scorpion will be perfect. The work according to the scheme is always painstaking and requires extraordinary care and accuracy in the work.
