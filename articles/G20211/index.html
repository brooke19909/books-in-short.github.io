Basic interpretation

A treasury is state property, including budget funds, which are not distributed between enterprises, organizations and institutions. It is used in phrases with the following words: state, military, monastic, municipal formation.

On the other hand, the treasury is just money and valuables. Then it is often used as an adjective. For example, a government account or property.

From the history of Russia

In the fifteenth to sixteenth century in the Russian state there was such a central executive body - the treasury. This body controlled the collection of most of the national direct taxes and customs fees. His duties included checking the fulfillment of certain duties (for example, Yamskoy), as well as how Pushkari, pishchiki and others serve.

This institution managed some areas of the state. In addition, it actively organized the embassy business. This executive body was the center of government administration. Under him, even the formation of the State Archive took place.

Among other things, the treasury was a reliable repository of treasures with the property of the princes. At the head of the governing body were two treasurers, assisted by printers.
