Legoland in Denmark - a fabulous vacation for curious children

Legoland in Denmark is the most beloved and unforgettable place for entertainment for kids, teens and their parents. There are only five similar parks in the world, but this one is considered the best of them. This is not surprising, because Denmark is the birthplace of the world-famous Lego designer. A toy park is by no means a toy size. When it first opened in 1968, it owned a territory of 38,000 m 2 , now it has increased three times and is not going to stop there.



Every year Legoland in Denmark receives more than a million visitors, and this despite the fact that it works only from March to October. All structures were assembled from parts of the designer of various sizes, in total about 46 million cubes were spent. Plastic houses and characters do not seem artificial and inanimate, playing music, compositions from living plants and even aromas help to make them more realistic. Legoland in Denmark is divided into 8 zones with different themes, this is done for greater convenience of visitors of different ages, since what suits a 3-year-old child will not be interesting for a teenager.

The world of miniatures is considered the heart of the park, about 20 million details were spent on the construction of miniature Japan, USA, Germany and Denmark. Toy houses, roads, vehicles are so realistic that they are not even striking in their small size. Absolutely all visitors will enjoy the world of imagination. The large 3D cinema gives an amazing viewing experience. Viewers not only enjoy high-quality images, but also smell smoke, gusts of wind, vibration from the train and many other special effects. The LEGO MINDSTORMS Center is also located here, where, using computer technology, children can design their own toy from the constructor.



Legoland Park in Denmark will welcome the youngest visitors with pleasure. Especially for them, the world "Duplo" was created, where all the structures are made of this constructor, and the zone is covered with a soft coating. A farm, a hospital and a driving school were built at different sites. Kids will be able to drive along a real street with traffic signs, a gas station and a road patrol, and at the end of the trip they will be given a driver‚Äôs license with a photo and a flag of their native country.

Legoland in Denmark has prepared for adventure lovers a park with pirate lands. Visitors have to go by ship to Roger's caves, they will not be able to avoid meeting with the robbers, so they will have to survive a brave battle and find hidden treasures. The city of Legoredo will bring children and their parents to the world of the American Wild West. Here you will need to swim along the river in a canoe, meet with the Leader of the Redskins, visit the gold mines and much more.



In the Middle Ages, a world of brave knights and beautiful princesses will also carry Legoland to Denmark. The photos of the heroes, as well as the huge Knight's Castle, amaze the imagination, let alone their close examination, where sounds, animation and even smoke join the beautiful picture. LEGO city is a miniature copy of a modern metropolis with various social services, factories, high-rise buildings. The world of adventure is designed for older children. There is a roller coaster, river rafting in the jungle, cable car, roller- coaster ride . At least one day should be allocated for a visit to Legoland, but the time spent will pay off in full with joyful emotions and warm memories.
