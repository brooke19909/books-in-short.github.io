Korablev Denis - the hero of the stories of Dragoon

Korablev Denis is the protagonist of the cycle of children's stories of the famous Soviet writer V. Dragunsky. This character is one of the most popular in the literature, as evidenced by the fact that he became the protagonist of several adaptations dedicated to these stories. These are "Fun Stories" (1962), and "Deniskins Stories" (1970), and short films shot on separate stories from the book of the same name in 1973, and "Secret to the World" (1976), and "Amazing Adventures" Denis Korableva "(1979). It is known that the prototype was the son of the author, for whom he wrote his works.

general characteristics

The events of the bulk of the stories take place in Moscow in the late 1950s - early 1960s. Korablev Denis in most works is a boy of preschool age. He lives with his parents, next to the circus, as mentioned in one of the works of this cycle. Subsequently, he had a younger sister. The narration is conducted on behalf of the protagonist, which is the charm of these works. The writer showed the world around him through the eyes of a child, many of whose judgments are striking in their truthfulness, prudence and directness.

In addition, the images of his parents play a large role in the stories, and his closest friend and comrade Mishka also plays a significant role. Secondary, episodic heroes periodically appear on the pages of stories, the presence of which, nevertheless, plays a big semantic load (for example, a school singing teacher).

Content

In all the stories, Korablev Denis tells of his adventures, funny stories and just episodes of his life. They are interesting in that everyone is very different from each other, and each event, as it were, opens the main character from a new perspective. Some of the works are funny, others, on the contrary, are very sad. Thus, the author shows the complex inner world of a child who is very keenly and vividly experiencing everything that happens around him.
