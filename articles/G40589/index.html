How to connect karaoke to a TV - step by step instructions and recommendations

Often, watching TV, people want to, like popular singers, be on stage, sing and swim in the rays of popularity. Of course, not everyone has such an opportunity, but you can show your vocal data at home. There is karaoke for this. And at the same time it is not necessary to go to a karaoke club, which costs decent money. You can create a concert hall at home.

Karaoke devices

Today, the home appliance market is full of all kinds of devices, the main function of which is to play phonograms. Customers who purchase this equipment may have a reasonable question: is it possible to connect karaoke to a TV? Naturally, it is possible, however, depending on the model and year of manufacture of the TV, the connection method will vary.

Moreover, karaoke can be configured on a personal computer, on a laptop and other devices. The only difference is that many PCs have the appropriate codecs for playing the necessary audio formats and for full-fledged singing you only need a disc with phonograms.

Setting up karaoke at home

In fact, figuring out how to connect karaoke to a TV is very easy even on your own.

Setup consists of the following steps:

  • connecting speakers and a subwoofer to the player to enhance sound;
  • connecting the cable from the DVD to the TV;
  • insert a disc with songs;
  • microphone connection.

The best option for doing what you love is to use special players (usually DVD-receivers, as well as so-called home theaters). They are equipped with stereo sound systems and the necessary connectors for connecting to many models of televisions and microphones. In addition, the new models, you can choose the playback methods - through a DVD-ROM or flash drives.

Using karaoke via a smart TV

And the latest technology achievement is TVs with built-in players. The leadership race has so far been won by LG and Samsung. And for example, even a child will be able to figure out how to connect karaoke to the Samsung Smart TV, since the set-up includes detailed instructions for setting it up.

Smart TVs made a real sensation among fans of singing their favorite songs in a relaxed home atmosphere. In addition, the new models are equipped with two microphone jacks, as well as quite powerful speakers. Loud, comfortable - and you can perform a duet.

There is one more caveat. When searching for solutions on how to connect karaoke to an LG Smart TV, remember that now the new technology has direct access to the Internet. And this means that songs can be played directly from the Web, without connecting additional devices.

Microphone setup

In fact, choosing a good microphone for your karaoke system is also not an easy task. Here you need to look at the manufacturer, the quality of the audio stream at the output, power and the presence of a suitable connector.

Therefore, before connecting a karaoke microphone to an LG or Samsung TV, you need to check the back or side of the TV (or the output connectors of the DVD player).

There are several varieties of microphones, ranging from professional to amateur:

  • wireless
  • USB microphone
  • standard plug-in microphone.

Wireless microphone is the best choice for karaoke

Of course, if you make a choice in favor of quality and comfort, then the solution is unambiguous. A radio microphone eliminates a whole bunch of wires in the room. There is also no need to purchase additional adapters for connection. Yes, and figure out how to connect a karaoke microphone to a Samsung TV, you do not need special skills.

In addition, such a microphone allows you to sing anywhere in the room, within the radio control.

Alternative microphones

Well, if acquiring a wireless microphone seems too wasteful, then you can use an alternative method. There are quite inexpensive devices with a standard connector and cord. Of course, you will have to look for the connector you need for your DVD player or Smart TV, but the result will still be a little cheaper. There are two types - with 6.3 mm and 3.5 mm connectors.

There are still USB microphones, which are universal, since almost all karaoke devices and TVs are equipped with these connectors. Unfortunately, to use such a microphone, you will need additional tuning, with which the average layman may not be able to cope. Then it is better to contact either specialists, or knowledgeable people who have similar techniques, or visit the necessary sites with instructions on the Internet.

How to set up karaoke software
