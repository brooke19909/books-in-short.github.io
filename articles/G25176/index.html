How to choose a music network player: market overview and manufacturer reviews

A network music player is a home electronic appliance. It is a connecting element between a conventional TV and the Internet. In order to control it, just pick up the remote control.

The player allows you to view any video, image or listen to music without using a laptop computer. The purchase of such a device will satisfy those who do not want to constantly configure programs on a laptop, but want to watch their favorite movie on the big screen without any problems. The network player has one main minus - playing the game or watching the news in text mode via the Internet will not work.





It is important to remember that the reputation of the manufacturer significantly affects the price of the device. Below we will review the models of different brands, consider their pros and cons.

Player functions

The device is connected to absolutely any TVs (CRT is no exception). In order to enjoy all the delights of such a technique, it is recommended to use Full HD screens. The player plays any formats that are related to HD. In fact, if we take into account precisely this nuance, we can say that the network player is an analogue of Blu-Ray. The significant difference between the two is that browsing is not via disks, but via the Internet.

In the market you can find models that support the option of storing files and sharing them. There is also the function of creating a personal library.
