Emirate of Fujairah: hotels on the Indian Ocean. Photos and reviews

Fujairah is the youngest, yet incredibly picturesque emirate of all. Its location is very favorable. Very close is the Indian Ocean, beautiful cliffs with rugged vegetation and clear white sand. The Emirate is surrounded by the mountains of Hajar, striking in its beauty and grandeur. In Fujairah, tourists who are fans of a quiet and measured rest are used to spending time. No large industrial enterprises have been built in the emirate, and the population is mainly engaged in the cultivation of gourds and strawberries. This resort is popular not only among visiting citizens, but also among the Arabs themselves, who want to relax and forget about all the problems under the bliss of gentle sunshine. In the resort, in addition to growing strawberries and melons, fishing is practiced - both by nets and ordinary fishing rods.

How to get there

The Emirate has its own airport, so the fastest way to reach it is by plane. The airport is located almost in the center of Fujairah. As a rule, all tour operators include the cost of transfer from the airport to the hotel. But there are no flights from the CIS countries directly to Fujairah. You have to fly through Dubai or Sharjah. You can also get here by car through the desert, renting a jeep with a driver. Buses run from Dubai and Sharjah to Fujairah.
