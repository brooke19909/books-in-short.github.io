When it comes to the most popular resorts in Vietnam, Nha Trang comes to mind first. This city boasts a developed infrastructure, modern hotel complexes, incredibly beautiful and vibrant beaches, as well as a host of historical and cultural attractions. Hotel Art Boutique Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): what do tourist rooms look like?





This is a city-style hotel, which offers its guests a choice of one of 60 rooms. There are both standard rooms and superior and deluxe rooms, which are mostly located on the upper floors. By the way, from the windows and balconies there is a wonderful view of the sea and the city, which looks especially beautiful at night.

The rooms, as former guests say, are quite spacious and bright. The repair here was made not so long ago, the furniture is new, and the appliances are modern. The floors are covered with either ceramic tiles or laminate. In order to make the vacation of travelers as pleasant as possible, the rooms are equipped with the necessary equipment, in particular an air conditioning system, which in summer heat simply saves. There is a telephone and a TV, as well as a refrigerator, which pleases guests. You can also use the Internet for free (good speed).

A snow-white bathroom gives you the opportunity to freshen up after an eventful day. A hairdryer, clean towels and hygiene products are provided (they are delivered to the room during cleaning daily).

What kind of food should I count on?





Of course, when planning a trip, questions about nutrition come to mind first. After all, resting on an empty stomach is unlikely to be tempting. What does the hotel offer Art Boutique Hotel 3 *? Traveler reviews suggest that food is unlikely to turn into a problem.

The hotel has a small cozy restaurant Rose, where tourists are offered mostly international cuisine, although you can enjoy some Vietnamese dishes. The hotel operates according to the bed & breakfast scheme, which means that in the morning you can go down to the restaurant room and have breakfast. The menu here is quite decent, guests are offered light snacks, salads, pastries, fruits and, of course, drinks. Guests are invited to the local karaoke bar, which is open in the afternoons and evenings, where travelers not only relax with a glass of strong drink, but also test their vocal abilities.

Of course, you can eat outside the walls of the Art Boutique Hotel 3 *. Vietnam, Nha Trang in particular, has a developed tourist infrastructure, and in large cities you will find cafes literally at every step. The prices in local restaurants, by the way, are pleasantly pleasing.

Holidays on the coast: comfort and convenience on the beach





Since many tourists prefer to relax on the seashore, the natural question is what the Art Boutique Hotel 3 * hotel complex can offer? Nha Trang is a city that became famous precisely because of its coastline. The distance to the beach is about 300 meters, it can be reached on the road in a few minutes. This is a luxurious and large public beach - its length is about 7 km. Of course, there are many tourists at any time of the year.

According to reviews, the coast here is very beautiful. It is covered with soft sand and cleaned here regularly - cleanliness is maintained, despite the abundance of vacationers. Of course, sun loungers and umbrellas are paid, but the prices are not too high.

Almost all types of activity are available on the beach for travelers, including boating, short boat trips, water skiing and parasailing. Since waves rise here from time to time, surfers have chosen the coast - here you can not only rent a board, but also take the help of experienced instructors. There are volleyball and football courts on the beach, as well as water sports centers.

Can I count on additional services?





Of course, having settled in the hotel complex Art Boutique Hotel 3 *, you can count on modern comfort and additional services. In particular, there is a spacious lounge, where guests spend their free time using access to high-speed Internet.

The reception, as well as room service, is open 24 hours a day. Here the staff will help you call a taxi, arrange a transfer, book tickets. There is a currency exchange office with a good exchange rate. And in the hotel you can rent a car or a moped (the most popular form of transport in Vietnam) without any problems, which will significantly speed up movement around the city and make travel around the neighborhood much more comfortable. By the way, next to the hotel there is parking.
