Novokuznetsk-Novosibirsk, features of a trip along the route

The distance from Novokuznetsk to Novosibirsk is about 310 kilometers in a straight line, and if you go by car, the distance between cities will be 370 kilometers. It can be traveled by car or bus, by rail, as well as fly by plane.

What to see in Novokuznetsk and Novosibirsk?

Each of the cities is interesting in its own way. They should be visited by lovers of Soviet architecture of the 1930-1950s, that is, the Stalinist Empire.

In Novokuznetsk it is worth visiting several museums on various topics:

  • Kuznetsk fortress. After all, the city was founded in the 17th century; it is 400 years old.
  • Dostoevsky Museum.
  • Geological.
  • Artistic and local history.

In addition, the city has a planetarium, a recreation park and a drama theater.

Novosibirsk in terms of attractions is much more interesting. The city has a metro, academic campus, a funny DNA monument, a crib shop, a children's railway, a museum of railway equipment and retro cars. Inspection of all interesting objects may take a week.
