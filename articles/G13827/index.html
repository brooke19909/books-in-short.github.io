Housing stock

Judging by the reviews left, the tourists were satisfied with the rooms offered by him at Natalie Resort. So, according to them, the apartments here are bright, spacious, stylishly decorated. The furniture in them is comfortable, the equipment is modern and in working condition. As our compatriots note, the TV in the room receives one channel with the Russian language of broadcasting. A plus is the fact that the apartment has a safe, hairdryer, and electric kettle. Also, guests liked the large furnished balconies. In addition, tourists were pleased that the rooms and the rest of the hotel have access to wireless Internet. Moreover, you can use Wi-Fi for free.

Service

As travelers noted in their comments, they were quite satisfied with the quality of cleaning in the apartments of Natalie Resort. According to them, they have no complaints about the work of the hotel maids. So, cleaning was carried out every day and was carried out quite carefully. Towels are also updated on a daily basis. The maids changed the bedding twice a week. Guests also note that both the towel and the linen are new, snow-white. Using them is very nice. Tourists also mention that daily maids replenished supplies of bottled drinking water and bath accessories.



Check in, departure

According to our compatriots, the arrival of new arrivals to the hotel Natalie Resort 3 * (Phuket) is always very fast. So even if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning, you will most likely be given room keys almost immediately, without condemning you to a long wait. However, keep in mind that you will be asked to make a deposit upon check-in. Its amount is $ 100 for the entire stay. On the day of departure, a hotel employee will inspect your room, and if everything is in order, the deposit will be returned to you. As for the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. If you plan to leave the hotel only in the evening, you can either extend your stay for several hours at an additional cost, or leave things for storage in the luggage room and continue to rest until the time of departure to the airport.

Many tourists considered the location of Natalie Resort quite successful. So, according to them, it is located very close to the city center, but at the same time in this area it is still not so noisy. Nutrition

As with most other hotels in Thailand, the rate at the Natalie Resort includes only a daily breakfast. He passes in the hotel restaurant from half past seven to ten in the morning. Our compatriots considered the choice of dishes and products to be standard - several types of rice, scrambled eggs, omelette, meat dish, sausage, sausages, vegetables, fresh fruits, pastries, sweets, juice, tea and coffee. Tourists who are here advise to arrive early for breakfast if possible. Since closer to 10:00 you run the risk of not finding all menu items available.

During the day, travelers can both have lunch and dinner in the Natalie Resort 3 * restaurant for a fee, and eat in cafes and restaurants located in the neighborhood. Most tourists preferred eating outside the hotel. According to them, here you can try a lot of different dishes. Moreover, the menu in some cafes is not limited to Thai dishes. So, you have the opportunity to taste both European and Russian cuisine.

Beach vacation

What do travelers say? Natalie Resort is located near the sea. So, the nearest Kata Beach can be reached in leisurely steps in 8-10 minutes. On the way you can buy freshly squeezed juice, fruits, drinks. The beach here is municipal, without deck chairs and umbrellas. Therefore, you can sit right on a snow-white sand, laying a mat or towel. The sea in this place, according to our compatriots, is quite calm compared to other beaches in Phuket. And the entry into the water is gentle, sandy. Therefore, Kata Beach will be comfortable enough to relax, even with young children.
