Room type low cost room - what does it mean?

When choosing a hotel, a traveler may come across different abbreviations and terms. For example, main building, de lux and many others. They are not always clear. But it is very important to correctly book a hotel room so that in the future there will be no problems.





Different abbreviations and abbreviations may surprise newcomers who first go on vacation abroad.

Especially often people are interested in the type of room low cost room - what is it? The answer will be given in the article.

Room Types

There are different types of rooms. What does low cost room mean? You need to deal with the main types in order to understand this term.

Each type of room has its own cost and various amenities. There are inexpensive rooms and those that are of great value. Everyone can choose exactly the option that suits him.

There are different classifications of hotel rooms. By size and composition of the room are divided into:

  • low cost room;
  • standart room;
  • suite;
  • apartments;
  • family room.

Also, the designations may be different. In the presented classification, the lowest number is the low cost room. It also has the least amenities. While suites and apartments are the most expensive, they have everything you need for a luxurious vacation.

Translation of the term

In order to understand what this type of number means and in general the term itself, you need to turn to the dictionary. With its help, you can accurately understand the meaning of any term.





What is a low cost room? Translation from English suggests that this is an inexpensive room. The word low cost itself means "cheap, inexpensive, low cost."

This phrase consists of three English words. If you look into the dictionary, you can see that low means "low, low, quiet." The word cost is "cost, price." The word room in translation into Russian means "room".

The term low cost itself can be seen in different meanings. Often it is used when it comes to low-cost, low-cost flights. But if we talk about the hotel sector, where low cost room is the type of room, what does this mean?

If you add all three words together, then literally they can be translated as "low cost of the room." That is, these are the most advantageous offers among all hotel rooms. If you make a free translation, then this phrase means a budget room in a hotel.

Who orders these numbers?

Room type low cost room - what is it? These are small in size rooms, but they have all the necessary amenities. For example, shower and toilet, bed and, possibly, TV.





Basically, people who want to save money order such a number. They can not afford chic luxury apartments, so they stop at this option.

More often these rooms are booked by single people. It can be students or young people who have an average financial situation. But anyone can order such a room. Increasingly, people try to spend as much time as possible on the street, and not in the room, so for many travelers the size of their room and its equipment is not fundamental.

Advantages and disadvantages

When it became clear that this is a low cost room type, you need to find out whether it is worth ordering them.





Low cost room have their pros and cons. Of course, the main advantage of such rooms is their low cost. Thanks to this, almost everyone can afford such housing for several days.

Also, the advantages of this type of room include all the necessary amenities. The room will have a bed, bedside table. It can also be a mirror, a TV or a small refrigerator.

If we talk about the shortcomings, it is worth noting that in these rooms there are no advanced amenities. They are simply furnished. They are also small in area, so this option will not be convenient for several people or families with children.
