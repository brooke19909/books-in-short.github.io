Hotel La Vella Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey): description, leisure and reviews of tourists

Such a resort region of Turkey, like Alanya, is very popular. Basically, tourists come here in search of a comfortable, fun and varied, but at the same time economical vacation. So, the main part of the hotel base here is represented by three- and four-star hotels with affordable pricing. At the same time, you can count on good service and comfortable accommodation for little money. Today we offer a closer look at one of the budget hotels in Alanya called La Vella Hotel 3 *. We will find out what travelers expect here, and also we will figure out what impression the stay at this hotel left with our compatriots.

Where is

This hotel has a very good location. So, the distance to the city center of Alanya is only two kilometers. You can overcome them both on foot and by public transport or taxi. Thus, all the entertainments of the capital of the resort region for guests of La Vella Hotel 3 * are within walking distance. In addition, you can take a fascinating walk to the main local attraction - Alanya Fortress. The distance to it is only three kilometers. Another famous attraction of the whole region - Manavgat Waterfall - is located 63 kilometers away. As for the beach, the distance to it from La Vella Hotel is only 300 meters. On the way you will need to cross the roadway (this can be done using the underpass). The hotel has its own section of the beach, where guests can use sun loungers and parasols for free. For an additional fee, you can also use equipment for water sports and entertainment.





How to get there

As for the airport, the nearest international air harbor is located in the city of Antalya, 120 kilometers from Alanya. Thus, the road to the hotel after landing the aircraft will take you about two hours. However, do not worry too much about this, because if the transfer is provided to you by a travel agency, you will get to the hotel by a comfortable bus, and along the way the guide will entertain you with an entertaining story about Turkey in general and the Alanya region in particular.

Alanya La Vella Hotel 3 *: description and photo

The hotel in question has been operating for quite some time. In 2013, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here, so today travelers have the opportunity to stay in updated and equipped with all necessary rooms. The hotel consists of one five-story building, which includes 80 rooms of a standard type. At the hotel, guests have the opportunity to swim in the pool, take sunbaths on the sun terrace, visit the restaurant and bar. On the whole, La Vella Hotel (Turkey, Alanya) is perfect for young people who prefer to spend most of their time on the beach, walking around the city, on excursions and visiting various entertainment venues. However, it will be comfortable for family tourists, as well as older people who want a budget holiday in the Turkish resort.





Posting Rules

As in other hotels, La Vella Hotel (Alanya) has a check out time. So, check-in of arriving guests is done after two in the afternoon. On the day of departure, release your room before noon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier than the specified time, then if you have free rooms you will be settled immediately. Otherwise, you will have to wait until the previous guests leave the occupied rooms, and the hotel staff will not prepare them properly for new tourists. As for the departure date, then, having vacated the room, you should hand over the keys to it at the reception, as well as pay for the entire stay (if you have not previously paid for the whole tour with your tour operator). You can pay here both in cash and with plastic cards of popular payment systems.

Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the three-star La Vella Hotel offers tourists to stay in one of the 80 rooms located in a five-story building equipped with an elevator. All rooms are standard. Despite the fact that they are small in size, there is everything for living: a private bathroom with a shower, air conditioning, satellite TV. There is also a balcony or terrace. For an additional fee, you can use the safe, as well as get wireless Internet access. Room cleaning and linen change are regular.





Nutrition

Dining at La Vella Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya) is organized by the all-inclusive system, which has long been loved by tourists from different countries. So, in the hotel restaurant three times a day a buffet is served . It presents both national Turkish dishes and international cuisine. In addition, guests can enjoy free local alcoholic drinks (wine, beer) in the hotel bars. In addition, during the day, tourists can "kill a worm" in the hotel with delicious snacks.

Sea and beach

Despite the fact that La Vella Hotel 3 * is a small hotel, it has its own beach. It is located 300 meters from the hotel. On the way to it you need to cross the road. An underground passage has been built for pedestrian safety. On the beach you can comfortably sit on the sun loungers. For an additional fee there is the possibility of using equipment for entertainment and water sports.





Entertainment

As for entertainment, since La Vella Hotel 3 * is small, it does not provide a special entertainment program for its guests. Therefore, if you want a team of animators to amuse you from morning until late at night, then it makes sense to find another hotel to stay. At La Vella, you can have a good time swimming in the pool and sunbathing on the sun terrace, equipped with comfortable sun loungers and parasols. You can also play ping pong and billiards at the hotel. On the beach you can use equipment for sports and fun on the water for a fee.

The bulk of entertainment is concentrated outside the hotel. So, at the reception you can book a tour you are interested in or rent a car and go to any corner of both Alanya and the whole of Turkey on your own. You can also rent a bike at the hotel and take a ride in the surroundings. Since the hotel is only a couple of kilometers from the center of Alanya, the entire entertainment infrastructure of the capital of the tourist region is within walking distance. Therefore, you can always visit local shops, restaurants, bars, clubs and other institutions.





Infrastructure

At La Vella Hotel, every effort is made to create conditions that maximize the conduciveness to a comfortable stay and an interesting vacation. For the convenience of guests, the reception is open 24 hours a day. So in case of a late arrival time at the hotel or an urgent issue arises, you will not have any problems. You can also contact the reception in order to make a currency exchange, book an excursion, rent a bicycle or car, and, if necessary, call a doctor. In addition, the hotel provides a shuttle service (should be reserved in advance). For the convenience of guests arriving on vacation in their own car or wanting to rent a car already in Alanya, there is also ample parking.

Accommodation cost

As for the prices for accommodation in this hotel, they are very attractive for tourists who want to economically relax in the popular resort of Turkey. So, the day of placement in a standard single room will cost you from 1900 rubles, in a standard double - from 2700 rubles, in a standard triple - from 3300 rubles.





La Vella Hotel 3 *: reviews of tourists from Russia

In order for travelers to have a more complete impression of this hotel, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the comments of our compatriots who have already stayed here during their vacation in Alanya.

First of all, tourists note the excellent location of the hotel. So, the distance to the sea is only about three hundred meters. Therefore, in just a few minutes you can walk to the beach. Despite the fact that you need to cross the road along the way, there will be no problems with this, since there is an underground passage. In addition, the plus is the fact that La Vella Hotel is not in the very center of Alanya. Therefore, the night here is quite quiet. If you crave noisy entertainment, then many restaurants, bars, clubs and discos are within walking distance.

As for the hotel rooms, in general, tourists found them acceptable. So, the rooms here are small, but clean and comfortable. They are equipped with only the most necessary.
