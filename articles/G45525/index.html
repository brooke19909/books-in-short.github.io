This option is included in the Mobile Bank package for free, both full and economical. It always acts by default, unless you specifically disable it. To do this, you can send a special SMS command or call the contact center of the bank. What is it for? Well, for example, if you leave the phone unattended and are afraid that someone might take it and send yourself money. Or are you afraid of viruses that steal money using SMS.

What does this service give us? Through it you can pay your own or any other phone number, transfer money between Sberbank's personal cards or from your own to someone else's. It is also possible to pay for services of some organizations.
