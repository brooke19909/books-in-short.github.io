Service

In Sanya Hawaii Hotel 4 * a number of services are offered, with the help of which the staff makes guests staying in its walls more comfortable and pleasant. Beach

The hotel does not have its own beach on the coastline. His guests can visit the resting place, which is located in the city. Here, according to them, there is a rather well-maintained territory where there is a clean and gentle beach - thanks to this, you can safely stay with the children near the sea.

On the beach there is the possibility of renting sun loungers and umbrellas, the payment for which is quite small and is calculated on the day of use. On the territory there are several public toilets, as well as changing rooms and a couple of showers.

Guests staying near the sea can be entertained by any means suggested. Here you can ride on airbags ("banana", "tablet", etc.), water skiing, parachuting, diving, as well as yacht and boat rental.
