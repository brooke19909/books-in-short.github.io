Sberbank deposits: a review of the best offers

In the article, we consider deposits for individuals at Sberbank.

Sberbank of Russia, a wide product line of which contains several deposit options for individuals, offers to profitably place funds using one of the programs. When investing savings of up to 1.4 million rubles. a full money back guarantee is provided, since the bank is included in the list of participants in the mandatory deposit insurance system.

This financial institution is today the leader in the number of deposits that are open to the population, largely due to trust, habits and support from the state. Many commercial banks offer higher interest rates. However, despite this, more and more customers trust Sberbank. Consider it the most reliable, offering interesting products.

Deposits with Sberbank come in several varieties and may differ in the term for which the agreement is concluded, the conditions for replenishing deposit accounts and closing accounts. In addition, for its regular pensioners, the organization regularly expands the number of offers - deposits are opened for them on favorable terms with more favorable interest rates.

Naturally, the main advantage is the presence of branches throughout the country, and all necessary financial transactions with the bank can be carried out in any settlement where there are no branches of many commercial banks.

What are Sberbank deposits for retirees intended?

Pension Plus contribution

This is a deposit for individuals to receive income from retirement benefits. It is intended for people who receive a pension, departments and ministries that provide pensions, as well as from non-state pension funds. It is possible to replenish the account and withdraw money without loss of interest; some preferential conditions are provided for early termination of the deposit agreement. The only minus of the Sberbank deposit for pensioners is a low interest rate, which is only 3.5% per annum. Such a contribution is opened for three years, interest is accrued quarterly. The capitalization of the deposit is provided, that is, interest is added to the main amount, increasing income in the following periods.

Who can open a deposit?

The conclusion of an agreement on opening a deposit account is a fairly simple procedure, and it does not take much time. All that is needed is a certain list of documents and a minimum amount of money.

Any person after 18 years can open a deposit at Sberbank of Russia. The opening procedure involves several steps:

  • notification of the employee about the intention to make a deposit;
  • The employee introduces the client with additional information;
  • entering customer data into the information database, listing of all necessary papers;
  • familiarization with the contract, signing it.

Documents required to open a deposit

The following documents are required:

  • passport;
  • TIN;
  • a military ID (if necessary) or a pension certificate (if the pensioner makes a deposit);
  • migration card (for those who are not citizens of Russia).

Opening a deposit does not take much time.
