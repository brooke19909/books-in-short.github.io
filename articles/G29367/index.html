Hotel First Bungalow Beach Resort 3 * (Koh Samui, Thailand): description, photos and reviews

Each year, Thailand is becoming more and more popular - tourism in the country is developing very quickly. Of course, when arriving here, many travelers wish to spend at least a few days on the seashore, enjoying the coastal beauty and mild climate.

Koh Samui is rightfully considered one of the most peaceful and picturesque places on the planet. Of course, almost all cities on the coast have long become resort centers and annually receive thousands of tourists. There are a huge number of hotels, and of different sizes and categories. One of the most popular holiday destinations is the First Bungalow Beach Resort.

Of course, when planning a trip, it is important to consider all the little things. Koh Samui, Thailand is a wonderful place that can pamper the traveler not only with natural attractions, but also with interesting history and original culture. But what living conditions can you expect? Is this hotel suitable for a beach family vacation? Do I have to worry about nutrition? Each tourist asks when planning a trip.

Where to look for a hotel complex?





As you know, when choosing a hotel, first of all, it is worth considering its location. First Bungalow Beach Resort 3 is located on the beautiful coast of Koh Samui, in the southern part of Chaweng Bay. This is a fairly lively, albeit calm area - there are shops and cafes nearby, so the guests do not feel divorced from life.

The distance to the center of Chaweng is only a kilometer - you can easily get there on foot or by taxi. By the way, the nearest international airport is about 12 km from the hotel building. Of course, you can count on a convenient transfer, but the road after the flight will take no more than 20 - 30 minutes, which is very important.

Hotel First Bungalow Beach Resort 3 * (Thailand, Koh Samui): information about the territory and infrastructure





The hotel complex has a fairly large territory. It itself consists of several small, cozy buildings (they are two or three-story), as well as individual small bungalows. The buildings are built in an elegant style and slightly reminiscent of country villas.

The territory is very well-groomed and beautiful - lawns, curly bushes, tall palm trees, bright exotic flowers, wonderful artificial grottoes, streams and, of course, Thai scenery are emerald green everywhere. Guests love the design of the hotel. By the way, in 2006 a complete reconstruction was carried out, and after it several cosmetic repairs. Today, the First Bungalow Beach Resort Hotel meets all international standards.

Can a tourist count on an additional service?





Of course, for a truly comfortable stay, tourists need some additional services. The hotel is trying to create a truly comfortable and relaxed atmosphere for each guest.

There is a laundry service, and ironing service is also offered. Travelers can easily exchange currency and get a safe at the reception desk for free.
