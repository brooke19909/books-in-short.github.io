Hostel "Apricot", Moscow: description, address, reviews

The conditions in the hostel "Apricot" are quite acceptable for a comfortable stay and a pleasant pastime. It has a homely atmosphere. The structure of the institution is as follows:

Rules of residence

The hostel "Apricot" in Moscow has certain rules governing accommodation. This is what it is all about:

  1. Check-in is after 14:00 and check-out is before noon. Subject to availability, early check-in or late check-out is possible at an additional cost.
  2. Upon check-in, the guest pays the full payment for the entire period of the planned stay (minus prepayment, if it was made earlier at the booking stage).
  3. Upon check-in, the guest must pay a deposit of 100 rubles for the key to the locker and 300 rubles for the magnetic key. The guest receives the money back when returning the key.
  4. Registration and check-in is carried out only with an identity document. Persons under 18 years of age are settled only in the presence of legal representatives.
  5. The administration is not responsible for valuables that have not been deposited with the inventory.
  6. The common areas are cleaned daily, bath towels are replaced every three days, and bed linen is replaced every five days.
  7. Pets are not allowed.
  8. It is forbidden to bring strangers to the hostel or give them keys.
  9. In case of damage or loss of property of the hostel, the guest is obliged to fully compensate for the damage.
  10. It is forbidden to smoke and drink alcohol. For violation of this rule, a fine of 1,000 rubles is provided.

Reservation Features

If you chose Apricot among the other hostels in Moscow, you should know that through the official website you can book a room or a place without prepayment. However, the reservation will not be guaranteed. The day before check-in until 13:00, you will need to contact the administration to check availability and confirm your arrival.

If you want to securely secure a place in a hostel in Moscow, you must make an advance payment in the amount of the daily cost of living during the day. Cancellation of the reservation with a refund of the prepayment is possible no later than three days before the expected date of arrival.
