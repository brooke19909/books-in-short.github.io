about the project

4 km of the Chusovsky tract was chosen for the construction of the cottage village "Palniks". This is a suburb of Yekaterinburg, an ecologically clean area. The Volchikhinsky reservoir and the Verkh-Isetsky pond are a barrier to the harmful industries of Pervouralsk. All residents of Yekaterinburg experienced and felt the negative impact of mining and processing industries, so the opportunity to purchase a house in an ecologically clean area is a great gift.





Clean fresh air, silence and tranquility, which violates only the singing of birds, and all this is not far from the city. That is how many remember the cottage village "Panniks" in Yekaterinburg. I want to come back here, I want to live here - is not this the highest mark?

About the developer

"Palniks Group" is a modern construction company engaged in the implementation of large-scale development projects for the construction of modern cottage villages, where all conditions for the most comfortable life are created. Today it includes six companies and represents a full cycle of construction services. The company is engaged in design, acts as a general contractor, deals with the issue of communications and design of each of its projects. The company appeared on the construction market of the Urals in 1992. Throughout the history of its existence, it has been able to implement a sufficient number of successful projects, the houses and townhouses of the developer impress with their quality, the level of comfort provided and are a role model.

Transport accessibility

Verkh-Isetsky district was not chosen by chance for the project. The proximity of major highways allows you to easily get to anywhere in the city, including the airport and train station. In the near future, it is planned to launch additional public transport routes, so even those who do not have a private car will be able to get to the village.

Infrastructure

The cottage village "Palniks" in Yekaterinburg boasts an excellent infrastructure. The territory of the village is fenced, is under round-the-clock protection, and is also equipped with a video surveillance system, so it will be comfortable and safe to be here. For children, an excellent playground with a special coating, slides, horizontal bars, houses, swings, sandboxes is equipped on the territory - a real expanse for the smallest residents.





Many worry about communications, but there is no reason for this. On the territory there is electricity, a gas main, water supply, sewage, all utilities, television and the Internet - in short, everything that a modern resident of a megalopolis needs.

All residents of the complex will be able to relax on a gorgeous lake, train strength and endurance in modern sports fields, celebrate holidays and important events in their life in an equipped recreation area. And all this is the cottage village "Palniks", located in the suburbs of Yekaterinburg.

Houses and apartments

On the territory of the cottage village in the Verkh-Isetsky district, you can buy a separate cottage, townhouse or apartment - it all depends on the preferences and financial capabilities of the buyer. All houses are for rent with a fine finish. All the necessary finishing work has been done: cold and hot water have been carried out, electrical wiring has been carried out, the walls and ceilings are aligned for the subsequent decorative decoration.

The developer offers a variety of planning solutions - most of them correspond to the European model. It is noteworthy that the village belongs to the city, so all its residents will receive a city registration.

Currently, almost the entire territory of the village is realized and built up. The attention of buyers presented excellent townhouses for two and four families, separate cottages.





Price policy

The commissioning of the facility is scheduled for late 2017 - early 2018. But there are already commissioned facilities. Today, even at the stage of construction of the village, an apartment, a townhouse or a separate cottage here can be purchased on favorable terms. At the moment, cottages of the third stage are on sale. The cost of townhouses in a village with its own plot of land and all necessary communications starts from 8 million rubles.
