"Royal Beach Club": hotel features and guest reviews

The ancient city of Evpatoria is located in the north-west of Crimea, in its steppe part. It stretches along the Kalamitsky Gulf and occupies fourteen kilometers of its shallow water. The climate of the city determines its location. Here it is mild, air humidity is moderate, in the summer, hot days refresh cool breezes.

Evpatoria has a lot of sun, so the weather here is almost always clear. In the coastal territories, the Kalimitsky Bay is shallow. The water here warms up well, the swimming season lasts from the beginning of summer to mid-autumn. This resort place attracts many tourists with its unique healing mud, brine, and healing mineral water. The local sun, air, beautiful beaches are suitable for families with children.

Sanatoriums and hotels of Yevpatoriya

In Yevpatoriya many unique resorts offer their unique services. Experienced specialists conduct treatment and prophylactic procedures using natural minerals and therapeutic mud. Many diseases are treated here:

  • skin
  • lungs;
  • heart and blood vessels;
  • blood diseases;
  • nervous system.

Numerous hotels offer their comfortable rooms for every taste and wealth. Each guest can choose a place to their liking. The hotel in Royal Beach Club 4 * is considered popular in Yevpatoriya. Tourists are offered decent conditions.



Location and general characteristics of the hotel Royal Beach Club, Yevpatoriya

The hotel is in a quiet location, ten kilometers from the center of the spa town and a hundred meters from the sea. Such an attractive location is a definite plus in choosing a stay at the Royal Beach Club hotel. The management is positioning their hotel as the only one in the Crimea that provides European accommodation with foreign management. The hotel began its work relatively recently - in 2013.

Royal Beach Club Living Conditions

Guests are offered rooms with a unique design, and each of them is individually decorated. There are 28 furnished rooms in the hotel. Here, at the Royal Beach Club, an indisputable advantage is a beautiful view of the sea, which opens from the balcony of the room. Very close to the beach. It is well equipped - comfortable sun loungers are available, umbrellas will hide from the scorching sun and the heat of tourists.



The beach is clean, sandy, its area is gentle and wide. Therefore, there is a fairly safe sea, in addition, the water warms up well. These advantages of the local beach are in demand for families with children. Therefore, the service at Royal Beach Club is designed more for family vacations.

Rooms are available for up to four people. Children under seven years old stay at the hotel for free if they are not provided with an extra bed. Guests are offered quality service and additional services:

  • regular room cleaning is done (you can call the maid during the day at any time);
  • the guests are provided with clean and ironed linens, fresh towels;
  • upon request, you can use a hairdryer and iron, a well-equipped laundry;
  • The rooms have WI-FI, its use does not require additional payment.

For a small price, guests are offered meals: only breakfast or full board. The management of the Royal Beach Club Hotel positions its cuisine as tasty and healthy. The recipes use only fresh and environmentally friendly Crimean products. Do not use preservatives, food additives, prepared sauces. Meals can be ordered in the room.

Transport service and organization of excursions

The hotel offers an airport shuttle service. Guests can park their cars at the Royal Beach Club.

Guest reviews

On the websites of travel agencies, many guests shared their impressions about the rest at the Royal Beach Club. Reviews are the most diverse and contradictory. Most guests like the convenient and quiet location of the hotel, proximity to the sea. Guests note that the beach is clean and comfortable, well equipped and comfortable for families with children. Guests like the rooms, their arrangement. The catering organization of vacationers causes many complaints. Not all guests agree that the food on offer is tasty and environmentally friendly. Many guests are not satisfied with the menu, and the scarcity of the diet is also noted.
