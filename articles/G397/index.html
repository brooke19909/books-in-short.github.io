Hotel President Resort 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet): description, services and reviews of tourists

The political situation in the world is such that the previously very popular resorts of Egypt and Turkey among Russian holidaymakers were not particularly accessible. Therefore, many domestic tourists began to consider alternative options for a beach holiday. Someone goes to India, someone - to Thailand, others prefer the UAE. Tunisia has recently become very popular with Russians. For a comfortable stay, this country fits perfectly. The tourist infrastructure in this resort is developed simply superbly. Of course, a huge number of comfortable inexpensive hotels is what, among other things, Tunisia can offer its guests. Hotels Hotel President Resort 3 *, for example, many choose for good service at reasonable prices. Reviews about this complex on the Web are mostly just good.

Location Features

Hotel President is located in Hammamet, in the immediate vicinity of the Mediterranean Sea. It is located on the second coastline - 400 meters from the beach. This, of course, is not too close. However, this hotel does not cause almost any complaints from tourists in terms of location in relation to the sea. A feature of the Hammamet resort is that living in hotels built on the first coastline is not very convenient here due to the noise of the trains.





Russian tourists who have booked rooms at the Hotel President Resort 3 * hotel usually fly to Tunisia, to Enfida Airport. To get from its territory to the hotel can be a maximum of an hour. Also, many vacationers come to Hamammet from the airport of Tunisia. In both cases, it is best to go to the place by bus. Public transport is located directly opposite the hotel. You can also hire a taxi or book a transfer at the hotel itself.

General description of the hotel

The Hotel President Resort 3 * is a hotel, reviews about which are really very good, a complex that includes the main five-story building and 15 two-story bungalows. Its entire territory is planted with beautiful tropical plants and looks like one large garden. The main hotel courtyard with pools is tiled with light tiles. Around it are mowed lawns with cafes and bars. In general, the hotel grounds look very impressive and prestigious. Many tourists also find the interiors of the main building and bungalows quite worthy.

Hotel President Resort by the standards of hotels in Tunisia is quite old. It was built in 1995. The last reconstruction in the hotel was also carried out quite a long time ago - in 2010. In 2013, this hotel became part of the Dessole Resorts & Hotels chain. The total area occupied by it is 3 hectares.

Number of rooms

If you wish, guests of Hamammet can rent one of 366 comfortable rooms at this hotel. All rooms rented at this hotel are equipped with televisions, safes and air conditioning. Unfortunately, there are no refrigerators in the Hotel Club President 3 * hotel (Tunisia, Hammamet). Of course, for a three-star hotel this is uncharacteristic and causes dissatisfaction with a certain part of the guests. But all the hotel rooms have balconies equipped with outdoor furniture. Here you can dry beach items, smoke or just admire the surroundings from here. The views from the balconies of this hotel really offer very good views - of the sea or the courtyard.

Bathrooms at the Hotel President Resort 3 * hotel (Tunisia) in each room are individual. In addition to the traditional set of household chemicals for bathing, a hairdryer is provided here for the convenience of guests. In addition to the usual rooms, this hotel also has rooms designed specifically for the disabled. They are equipped so that people with disabilities live in them as conveniently as possible.

Hotel infrastructure

The rooms in the Hotel President Resort 3 * hotel (Hammamet) are, of course, not as well equipped as in many other three-star hotels in Tunisia. However, in fact, the infrastructure itself in this hotel is very well developed. For example, there are only three pools in this large hotel. Guests have the opportunity to swim in the open (in the yard) or in the indoor. There is also a children's paddling pool on site.

In addition, hotel guests have the opportunity to use:

  • steam bath;

  • elevator;

  • free private parking;

  • a safe;

  • a left-luggage office.

This hotel is non-smoking. But at the same time, special places are specially equipped for adherents of such a bad habit at the hotel. This hotel also has a currency exchange office. Also, many tourists visit the President Resort:

  • volleyball and basketball courts;

  • tennis court;

  • massage room.





Reception at the Hotel President Resort 3 * hotel is open around the clock. If you wish, you can also order a wake-up call service at this hotel. There are several shops of different specializations, as well as souvenir shops, on the territory of the hotel. If desired, guests can rent a scooter or car directly at the hotel. True, this service is quite expensive here. Many tourists advise renting equipment not in the hotel, but in the city. Pets are not allowed in this hotel.

Business Services

Many consider the Hotel President Resort 3 * hotel (Hammamet) convenient not only for a beach holiday. Business people can also have a good time here. For their convenience, the hotel provides a large conference room and a business center.

Services for children

Many come to this hotel as a family. Living in it parents with children, according to many guests, quite convenient. In addition to the paddling pool, for younger guests this hotel provides:

  • playroom;
  • kids club.

If desired, parents can also rent a baby cot of the appropriate size right in the room.

Hotel infrastructure reviews

Living in the President Resort 3 * hotel (Hammamet), according to many tourists, is very comfortable. There are really many amenities for guests here. Of course, its guests consider swimming pools to be the main advantage of the hotel. Their hotel staff wash them often enough, and the water in them is always clean. Some guests consider the only drawback of the hotel pools to be that you can only start swimming in them from 9am. Meanwhile, many tourists, especially Russians, like to swim before breakfast.

The reception of the hotel, according to the guests, works very well. Queues are never here. Some complaints are caused only by the fact that the reception staff are not as welcoming to the guests as their colleagues do, for example, in Turkey or Egypt. In addition, sometimes guests who have booked rooms at this hotel have to wait too long to check in.

Among the minuses of the President Resort hotel, many guests include the fact that there are no kettles in the rooms. In hotels of other southern countries belonging to the three-star group, such equipment is always available in the rooms. In addition, the maids in such hotels daily bring special tea or coffee sets to their rooms. President Resort does not provide such a service. However, if you want to drink tea, its guests can still. To do this, they need to go down to the hall. An electric kettle is available.



Dining at the hotel

The Club President Hammamet Hotel 3 * (Hammamet) operates on an all-inclusive basis. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served to guests at this hotel in a designated area. Just excellent food - this is perhaps what Hotel President deserves the best reviews from its guests. Dishes served at the hotel are delicious, fresh and always warmed up. In addition, the menu at this hotel is very diverse. Especially many hotel guests like the fact that for lunches, breakfasts and dinners a lot of seafood is served here. In addition to the main dishes, hotel guests can order fruits and vegetables. Allowed to guests of this hotel and make a menu to your taste.

In addition to the dining area, this hotel also has a small restaurant. Here, if desired, you can order some delicious dish, expensive wine, etc. Also, the hotel has several cozy bars and cafes.





Entertainment at the hotel

There are no special entertainment events at this hotel. However, traditional animations are still held here from time to time. Also, the hotel often hosts children's discos. In general, the reviews of entertainment at the Hotel President Resort 3 * by most tourists are quite restrained. Many believe that this hotel is still better suited for senior citizens or family people than for youth.

Hotel beach

To the coastline from the territory of the Hotel President Resort 3 * hotel a quiet step can be reached in about 10 minutes. In order to get to the beach of the hotel, its guests need to go down the hill. The seashore is covered in front of the hotel with large light sand. And therefore, relaxing on the beach of the hotel is very convenient. The sea itself in this area is usually pretty clean. But, unfortunately, the beach is cleaned only once a week. Therefore, by the weekend, garbage and algae still appear in the sea. Beach towels at the hotel are provided to guests free of charge. But, unfortunately, you can only get one per number. That is, towels are not issued for every guest. It also raises complaints from some guests.

Swimming at the hotel's beach is convenient mainly for adults only. The fact is that the descent into the water here is quite steep. In the summer of strong unrest at sea in Hammamet is almost never observed. However, already in early September, the situation begins to change. At this time, swimming in the sea on the beach of the Hotel President Resort 3 * hotel is more careful.

Features of the infrastructure of the city near the hotel

Actually the city of Hammamet many tourists consider it very cozy. The streets in this town are clean. Hammamet area is very small. If desired, this settlement can be bypassed in just half a day entirely. The tourist infrastructure in the city of Hammamet is developed just fine. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many shops of various specializations, markets, pharmacies, various entertainment venues.

Experienced tourists in Hammamet are advised to buy fruits and all kinds of souvenirs only in large supermarkets - in residential areas of the city. In the tourist area in this resort, there are mainly shops selling all kinds of Chinese junk.

sights

Many praise the Club President Hammamet Hotel 3 * hotel, including the fact that its staff is happy to help guests plan their excursions and can tell you what interesting places in Hammamet or its environs are worth visiting. There are really a lot of attractions in this part of Tunisia. The most popular among tourists are:

  • International Cultural Center;

  • Phrygia park;

  • signal tower "Kasba".





The international cultural center in Hammamet is located in the villa of the Romanian millionaire G. Sebastian, built in the 20th century. The interior of this magnificent building is decorated with spectacular antique mirrors. In the courtyard of the villa there is a small pool surrounded by columns. The center's Roman Garden hosts international art festivals annually.

Phrygia Park is 34 km from Hammamet. Every day, from 11:00 to 16:00, in the pool located on its territory, performances are held with the participation of dolphins. Most tourists find these shows really interesting. At the end of each performance, guests of the park have the opportunity to swim in the pool with dolphins.

The signal tower "Kasba" is located in the old part of the city of Hammamet - in Medina. Its walls offer truly magnificent views of the city and the sea bay. This tower was built in the IX century. and very well preserved to this day.
