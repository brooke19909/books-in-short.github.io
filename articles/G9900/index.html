Holiday homes with beaches in the suburbs. The best beaches of Moscow region

On hot summer days, it is especially hard to bear the heat in a smoky, gassed, incredibly noisy large city. People without special need try not to go on the melting asphalt. Whenever possible, the majority of citizens seek to leave the city at this time.

Today we will talk about Moscow and Muscovites. Where can they spend hot days if there is no way to leave the metropolis far and for long? Probably, not everyone knows that the recreation area in the suburbs is well developed. You can easily choose a corner for a weekend or an entire vacation. Pensions, motels, holiday homes with beaches in the suburbs are no longer rare. Today, when the heat outside the windows torments the townspeople, we want to introduce you to places where you can feel easy and comfortable at that time.





Holiday homes with beaches in the suburbs

First of all, we suggest you pay attention to the comfortable rest house "Podmoskovye" located in Mytishchi district.

And what about the access to the water at this holiday home? There are no problems with the beaches in the Moscow Region. The complex is located on the Klyazma reservoir. The territory is well equipped - there are umbrellas, sunbeds. You will surely enjoy your vacation in the Moscow Region: your fond memories will be associated with a beach covered with golden sand, cool water and tall pines.

Three meals a day, ordered. The dining room is served by waiters. In addition, there are two bars, a phyto bar with karaoke, a disco, five saunas, a gym, a library, and sports equipment rental.
