Refrigerator "Don": customer reviews, specifications, new models

Refrigerators "Don" have established themselves as reliable and durable household appliances. The manufacturer uses high-quality imported parts and completes them at a domestic factory.

The production policy of these household refrigerators is aimed at regular updating of models, and this guarantees the quality of the goods. Today on the market you can see a large number of Don refrigerators, which in their characteristics are not inferior to more expensive imported goods.

General characteristics of refrigerators "Don"

Refrigerators of this brand are not lost among the products of foreign manufacturers. They are noticeable and recognizable. The product line is quite wide and is represented by models from 90 to 220 cm high. Refrigerators "Don" single-chamber are compact and easy to use.





Two-chamber models have separate chambers, they are more capacious and allow you to quickly freeze and cool foods.

Don refrigerators are available in 8 colors: white, ivory, mirror graphite, antique shagreen, jasmine, beech, mahogany, oak. For tinting, imported dyes based on powder from Greece, Italy are used.

The metal parts are coated with marble and wood. The creation of the top layer by sublimation makes it possible to use the Don refrigerator in various climatic conditions. This coating, in addition, creates additional protection against mechanical stress.

Internal chambers and accessories are coated with antibacterial composition.

The Don refrigerator is equipped with an Australian ACC compressor, which has high starting characteristics and an improved coefficient of specific cooling capacity. All models are resistant to a sharp voltage drop in the network.

The refrigerator has a drip evaporator, it creates a certain microclimate for storing food.

The camera has an automatic defrost function. Draining water during compressor operation evaporates from the vessel. Drop defrost allows you to turn off the refrigerator from the network for cleaning once every 6 months.

Non-explosive and environmentally friendly freon makes the refrigerator safe.

Energy saving at Don refrigerators corresponds to class A.

Management of refrigerators of any models is electromechanical.

The quality of Russian technology is not inferior to competing peers. And the affordable price allows you to buy it without much stress on the family budget.
