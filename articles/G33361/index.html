Primasol Hane Family Resort (Turkey, Side): description, reviews of tourists

Turkey is a favorite vacation spot of modern Russians. All vacationers and tourists are waiting for fashionable resorts with well-developed infrastructure, luxury hotels working on an all-inclusive system, as well as first-class service. By the way, the service is a visiting card of Turkey. Today, in no other country in the world you will not find such care for vacationers. If you are looking for a great place to relax with endless sandy beaches, crystal clear sea water and unspoiled nature, give your preference to Side. Orange groves, coniferous forests, stunning architecture - all this is a small but quite cozy Turkish town of Side. It is great for lovers of beach and outdoor activities. The hotels here are excellent, but their choice should be approached more responsibly. We suggest you pay attention to the excellent, judging by the description, option - the Primasol Hane Family Resort hotel. And what can reviews of real vacationers say about him?





About hotel

So, Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel is located in the resort town of Side, just 8 km from its historical center and 60 km from Antalya. The hotel is large with a huge surrounding area and its own sandy beach.

Rooms

All vacationers in Primasol Hane Family Resort 4 * will be able to stay in one of the standard, family rooms or suites. Whichever category you choose for yourself, you will find a fairly spacious and bright room with a private bathroom.





Each room has a TV with satellite channels, including Russian, a telephone, as well as air conditioning, which is responsible for maintaining the optimal microclimate in the room. A mini fridge is responsible for the freshness of food and the coolness of drinks in the room - its internal volume is enough to store dairy products, fruits and drinks. All valuables, including money and documents, can be put in a safe.

Well, of course, every vacationer will be able to meet the sunrises and see off the sunsets, sitting on the balcony with a glass of aromatic wine - what else is needed on vacation. The balcony offers stunning views of the city, the coast and the majestic mountains that rise above it.





Rooms are cleaned daily, towels and bed linen - at the first request of the client. The rooms are perfect cleanliness and freshness.

Hotel Territory

For hotels in Turkey, the presence of a huge adjacent territory is characteristic, the Primasol Hane Family Resort hotel was no exception to the rule. Its territory is literally immersed in greenery, due to which a pleasant shadow and coolness are created, saving from the scorching sultry sun.

All visitors to the hotel, vacationers and tourists can use the parking lot for free. Throughout the territory of Primasol Hane Family Resort 4 (Side, Turkey) free wireless Internet access works, vacationers will receive a password to it upon check-in. A currency exchange office is open on site, that is, vacationers do not have to look for it in the city. By the way, the course is quite profitable.





Primasol Hane Family Resort is a great place not only for relaxation, but also for conferences, parties and special occasions. For this, 3 huge conference rooms, several chic restaurants are intended. Vacationers can visit the gym and spa complex with a sauna, a bathhouse and a huge pool.

Additional services

For an additional fee, all vacationers can clean their belongings at the Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel through laundry services. At your service the friendly and hospitable staff who will answer all your questions will help make your stay at the hotel as comfortable as possible.

Entertainment on site

All vacationers can swim not only in the waters of the warm and gentle sea - on the territory of the hotel there are 10 huge pools, including a children's pool with slides and attractions. There are even several slides for adults and a heated indoor pool, ideal for the cold season, when the water in the sea becomes cool.

The hotel has enough sports fields and equipment: here you can play tennis, soccer, golf, and even shoot archery. It will be fun for children, and all thanks to excellent children's playgrounds and high-quality animation.





Nutrition

Primasol Hane Family Resort is an all-inclusive property. Vacationers can eat in two main restaurants, offering to get acquainted with dishes of national local, as well as European cuisine. At the bar you can enjoy freshly squeezed juice, a cocktail, a glass of wine or a stronger alcoholic drink. At the poolside bar, you can quench your thirst with refreshing drinks and a bite to eat. Be sure to visit the Turkish and Italian restaurant, as well as the local pastry shop, where you can treat yourself to delicious Turkish sweets.

Beach

Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel (Turkey) boasts a private beach: it is located just 300 meters from the main building. The beach is equipped with changing rooms, showers, as well as sun loungers and umbrellas. The coastline is quite wide and almost endless, there is enough space for all tourists and vacationers.

Here for an additional fee you can rent sports equipment, as well as water activities. If you want to master a water board, go diving, here you can get the necessary skills.

To summarize

The hotel is suitable for families with children, as well as for youth holidays. It has everything that a modern tourist needs: chic and comfortable rooms, a sandy beach with clear sea water, well-developed infrastructure, lots of entertainment for every taste. We hope that you can appreciate this four-star hotel.
