Hotel Centra Maris Resort Jomtien 4 * (Thailand, Pattaya): description and reviews

Centra Maris Resort Jomtien 4 * is a beautiful and modern hotel, located in a good location, surrounded by lush tropical vegetation. In Thailand, there are a large number of good hotel complexes, but this is worth telling in full detail.





Briefly about the hotel

The opening of the Centra Maris Resort Jomtien 4 * took place in December 2015. This stylish and modern resort hotel is located in the southern part of the town called Jomtien. It is located a few kilometers from Pattaya - one of the main resorts in Thailand. The center of this large city is a 30-minute drive away. U-Tapao International Airport is a 50-minute drive away. It takes about two hours to get to Bangkok.

In the vicinity of the hotel are various tourist attractions. The beach, according to many, is far away. But there the guests bring a bus. Although many walk - it takes about 15-20 minutes.

Although the hotel is new, but in the holiday season of 2016, it received a fairly large number of tourists. And, if you believe the reviews (they will be affected by attention a little later), then many would like to return here.

Service

The Centra Maris Resort Jomtien 4 * provides all the services and amenities that any good hotel should have. Free high-speed Internet (Wi-Fi) is available throughout the hotel, private parking is available nearby, and the reception is open 24 hours a day.

There is also a tour desk, a left-luggage office, a concierge (guests can use his services), a nanny (relevant for visitors with children), and a laundry service. Even a modern conference room is organized.

Shuttle services and food delivery services to the apartments are also available. The staff at Centra Maris Resort Jomtien 4 * is pleasant and welcoming, but does not speak Russian - only English and Thai.

Nuances of booking

Before buying tickets for a flight, you need to know that check-in at the hotel starts from 15:00 and lasts until 18:30. If the hotel room that guests booked is vacated earlier, then they can check in before three in the afternoon. Check out lasts until noon.

It is also worth knowing that the rooms do not provide extra beds. But when placing one child, under the age of 12 years, you will not need to pay for it.

By the way, when making a remote reservation, you will need to provide credit card details. An accommodation facility may block a certain amount on its account - but this is only until the guests arrive. These are the guarantees of their arrival on vacation.
