DIY soft toys: rooster pattern

The symbol of 2017 is the Rooster. In this article, we will talk about how to make several options for a soft talisman. There are options where you need a pattern of toys. You can also sew a rooster by drawing it yourself. Those who do not doubt their artistic abilities can do this right away on the fabric.





DIY felt cock (no pattern required)

To make the symbol of 2017 you will need:

  • Thin felt of a light shade for the body (optional).
  • The remains of hard felt in seven different colors.
  • Red hard felt.
  • A small piece of thin yellow felt.
  • Sharp scissors.
  • Threads in a suitable color.
  • Beads or pre-made doll eyes.
  • Cardboard for the pattern.
  • Chalk or remnant.
  • Silicone glue from the gun.

For this product we do not need a pattern. We will make a rooster out of 12 hearts. They can be drawn by hand. Such a rooster can be hung on a Christmas tree, presented as a toy to a child or loved one on February 14.

Draw 2 large hearts on paper, the size depends on how big the bird you want to sew. It can be a small cock-valentine or huge, like a sofa cushion.





We draw on paper two more hearts that are half the size of the body. Next, we depict 5 more elements for the tail of the cockerel and two small ones for the crest. Two very small hearts will be needed for the beak and beard. One is a little more, the second is less. The rooster pattern is ready. We cut out the elements from paper and lay them out on the fabric. We fix with pins for convenience. Now you can cut hearts from felt.

You can also just use patterns as a stencil and circle them. Better to do it with chalk. If you circle with a pencil or pen, an unaesthetic mark will remain on the cut. That is why many needlewomen prefer to pin a pattern to felt and cut blanks. About what is better to cut blanks, we will discuss below.

So, you cut out the elements of the future toy. Now you need to outline the eyes of a rooster. They will be on both sides. On a small product you can embroider them using the French knot technique or sew a bead. You can stick special purchased eyes for toys to a cockerel or sew on a button.

We pass to the wings. There are two options: stick them on or sew on. Take the blank, fold it in half and attach to the middle of the body in a way convenient for you.

Next we will make a gorgeous tail of our cockerel. To do this, take 7 pre-cooked items. They need to be fastened together. You can make this thread with a needle or glue. Place one heart in the middle of the second and fix.

We proceed to the assembly of the cockerel. Take two halves of the body and sew the edge with a buttonhole stitch. First, hide the knot. Stitch to the place where the tail should be. We insert it and flash it all together. Fill the cockerel with holofiber or synthetic winterizer. It should be a little, otherwise it will seem that cracks appeared on the felt.

Fold a small heart in half and insert the narrow side into the cockerel. This is a beard. Make a few more stitches and fasten the beak by making it from the smallest heart. It must be inserted between the two halves with the sharp side. Go to the place where the comb should be and insert two hearts. If you are making a Christmas tree toy, then insert a ribbon loop on top.

How to cut felt

If you are keen on making felt toys, then purchase separate sharp scissors for the fabric. Do not cut material with a tool designed for other purposes. Experienced needlewomen are advised to purchase separate scissors for different types of felt. To get the perfect edge, do not cut polyester fabric with scissors for woolen material. They are dull from this.

How to sew a rooster. Pattern of a fabulous bird

In this master class, a rooster pattern from Pushkin's fairy tale will be given. This toy is sure to appeal to children. The "Golden Cockerel" consists of parts of the body, head, wings, arrows. The toy can be sewn from felt or any variegated material. Cut out the parts and sew them with an inner seam. You can fill a cock with a synthetic winterizer, holofiber or cotton wool. Tip: when turning the arrow, it is convenient to use a needle, pencil or a Chinese wand.

Cockerel for good luck and prosperity

The simplest toy pattern will be presented here. We sew a rooster from dense natural fabric. Flax or cotton will do. Cut a 15 x 30 cm rectangle. Fold it in half with the front side inwards. Sew on both sides to make a pouch. You can make a line on a typewriter or sew stitches ‚Äúback needle‚Äù.





We use a little trick to get a voluminous rooster toy. With our own hands we will not build patterns from a large number of elements. To do this, the upper seam must be parted in different directions. The blank should resemble a triangular bag of kefir.

We will not sew up the last side, but will do it with Velcro. A toy can have several purposes: to amuse a child or to guard a house as a talisman. From its function will depend on how we fill it. It will be a soft filler: cotton wool, holofiber or synthetic winterizer. We will talk about the second filling option below.

Fold the free sides of the toy, take the Velcro and glue its parts along the inner edge of the product. Now we need to draw a comb and a goatee cockerel. You can do this right away on a piece of red or orange hard felt. Draw on the fabric with chalk, this will be the pattern. It is difficult to imagine a rooster without a chic beard and a comb; it is with them that he attracts the attention of hens.

Glue the comb with a heat gun between the upper seam. Stick a beard on your face. The cockerel is ready.





How to fill a cock so that it brings prosperity

If you want the bird to play the role of a talisman in the house, then it must be properly filled. Want to improve your financial situation in the family? Put seven coins in the cockerel, and preferably the largest possible real bill.

If there is a discord between the spouses, add lavender to the bag. If you want all family members to be healthy, put in a bag two cloves of garlic and five berries of dried mountain ash. Also, chamomile and St. John's wort carry health. Those who want to replenish the family will be helped by pumpkin seeds.

If there are children in the house who are afraid to fall asleep at night and see bad dreams, then put five branches of wormwood in a cockerel. Our ancestors believed that it protects the house from evil forces.

What does the cock symbolize

And finally, let's talk about where to put the toy at the end of the year. Do not dispose of the product. Hang it in the hallway at the entrance to the house. This bird among the Slavic peoples symbolizes protection from unclean forces. No wonder they say that they walk "to the first roosters." According to Feng Shui, this symbol in the bedroom protects against adultery.

Sew toys with love and in a good mood. Then all the talismans and charms will have power and bring good luck and prosperity to your home.
