Krasnodar Territory, Olginka: rest, private sector

The Krasnodar Territory is very popular not only among Russians, but also among tourists from other countries. The warm sea, the bright sun and the good-natured local population will not leave anyone indifferent. Well active young people and people of advanced age will be able to relax here. And families with young children are advised to choose the private sector for their holidays. Olginka is perfect for this!

Where is the village located?

Olginka is a small town in the Tuapse district of the Krasnodar Territory. Its population totals no more than 2000 people. Despite this, in the summer, a huge number of tourists come here. Local residents can live all winter on the money earned during the holiday season.





The Krasnodar Territory is famous for its beauties. Olginka is no exception. The village is located in the valley of the Tu River. The nature here is really bewitching. It is no coincidence that hotels and health centers were built here. The sanatoriums "Gamma" and "Orbit" have long been popular. To improve their health, people come here in summer and winter. Parents in the warm season try to take here often sick children. Clean sea air helps prevent respiratory diseases.

How to get to the village?

The Krasnodar Territory is famous for its good transport interchange. Olginka is a resort village. Therefore, you can get here in various ways. Faster and more comfortable you can get to the place on the plane. There is no airport in Olginka. But a very small distance separates the village from Dzhubga and Tuapse. When booking accommodation, you can agree with the owners of the apartments about the transfer service from the air harbor.

Perfectly accessible by train to the Krasnodar Territory. Olginka is closest to the Tuapse train station. Almost all of Russia direct trains come here. The road, of course, will take much more time than flying by plane. This option is perfect for relaxing economy class. After all, air tickets are not cheap pleasure.

Many tourists decide to drive their own car to the Krasnodar Territory. The village of Olginka will be easily found using the navigator. Such a journey makes it possible not to depend on the schedule of air travel and railway trains. In addition, at any time you can stop to have a bite to eat in a cafe and gain strength.

Private holiday

If Olginka (Krasnodar Territory) was chosen for recreation, the private sector is ideal for living ideally. The village has many comfortable mini-hotels and cottages. The hosts offer not only apartments, but also a number of additional services. In your free time from a beach holiday, you can play billiards or swim in the pool. It is also possible to rent a car.





Prices for accommodation in the summer are already in April. Krasnodar Territory, Olginka in particular, is very popular among tourists. Therefore, it is better to book a place for a vacation in advance in order to get the best prices and guaranteed reservation of accommodation. Many experienced travelers do this in March. If you contact the owner of the cottage in June, then you can go on vacation only at the end of the beach season.

For those who do not have great financial resources, the village of Olginka (Krasnodar Territory) is also ideal. The private sector has cottages, houses and apartments of different price categories. The most economical is the category "Tourist". In this type of housing, showers and toilets are usually located in a separate building, and the room has only a few beds and a refrigerator.

Beach vacation





The whole Krasnodar Territory is famous for its wonderful beaches. Olginka is no exception. It is here that the best pebble beach in the whole Tuapse district is located. The bottom here is very flat and comfortable for swimming. The entry into the water is gentle, which is another argument in favor of relaxing in Olginka with young children.

For lovers of marine entertainment on the beach there is a rental jet ski and scooter. There is also the opportunity to swim on a banana or yacht near the promenade. Visitors to the beach will never be hungry. So, locals offer everyone to feast on freshly cooked corn or sweet baklava. And those who are tired of the scorching sun can take refuge under the roof of a restaurant near the seashore.

Municipal beach - the pearl of Olginka

The central beach of the village is a real find for tourists. The water here remains clear even in high season. The width of the pebble strip is more than 50 meters. The length of the beach is also impressive - more than a kilometer. Despite this, in the summer it is quite difficult to find a free place for your deck chair. To soak up the gentle sun, you have to take a place on the beach in the early morning.





If you want to relax not just by the sea, but in an ecologically clean place, you should go to Olginka. Krasnodar Territory has many beaches that are characterized by perfect cleanliness. But the central municipal beach of Olginki can rightfully take one of the first places. Indeed, algae and mollusks (of which there are many) can be found in large numbers only in perfectly pure water.

Rivers in Olginka

In the village everyone has the opportunity to spend a vacation really high quality. After all, Olginka (Krasnodar Territory) offers relaxation not only on the coast, but also on mountain rivers. Water here for swimming, of course, is not suitable. But kayaking will not leave anyone indifferent.

The Tu River originates on the southern slope of the Lower Picket Mountain. In Olginka itself, it already flows into the sea. Hikes are organized from the village. The group rises to a hill and goes down the river to the sea.





The Kaban River is the largest tributary of the Tu. In its lower reaches there are a huge number of pensions and hotels. Clean air and crystal water of mountain rivers make it possible to cure various diseases and improve overall health.

Where else to go to Olginka?

An excursion to the Ashe River valley will give a lot of positive emotions. Initially, you will have to get to one of the Adyghe villages. The tour includes a tasting of honey and local wine. The products are provided by a hospitable Adyghe family. Next, tourists are invited to drive all-terrain vehicles to the waterfalls. This natural phenomenon is famous for Olginka (Krasnodar Territory). Photos of waterfalls fascinate with their beauty. And if you see this splendor live, vivid emotions will be provided for a long time.
