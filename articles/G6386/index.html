How to turn off voice control on an iPhone: tips, tricks, instructions

Mobile phones today are universal devices. With their help, you can not only talk, but also take pictures, download and listen to audio recordings and so on. Of course, these gadgets have several control methods. For example, by voice. This option allows you to activate certain functions of the mobile phone by speaking commands. Very comfortably! But today we have to understand how to turn off voice control on an iPhone . Sometimes this option is redundant. How can I refuse to use it?

Siri

It should be noted that the owners of "apple" phones can take advantage of a variety of voice dialing features. Each of them is disabled by separate operations. We have to get acquainted with all the options for the development of events.



How to disable voice control on an iPhone? The first step is to disable Siri. To do this, you have to:

  1. Download the gadget. Go to the main menu of the device.
  2. Select "Settings".
  3. Visit the section "Basic" -Siri.
  4. Turn the switch of this option to the off position. The red indicator should light up.

Voiceover

In some cases, owners of apple devices find that they have turned on the VoiceOver option. It also indirectly relates to voice control. This application calls the controls after clicking on them, and does not execute the requested command.



To opt out of VoiceOver, you must:

  1. Open Settings on your mobile phone.
  2. Go to the "Basic". Find the line "Universal Access" and click on it.
  3. Open VoiceOver.
  4. Turn the switch responsible for the application to the inactive position.

Now it‚Äôs clear how to turn off voice control on an iPhone. It is important to note that for successful rejection of VoiceOver, you need to click on all buttons 2 times. Otherwise, the operating system will simply call these or those controls.

How to turn off voice control on an iPhone, if we are talking about iOS7 or 8? To do this, you will have to use the previously proposed algorithm with some changes. Namely, turn on Siri. Voicecontrol

Often, the proposed action algorithm leads to the fact that VoiceControl is activated on the smartphone with a long hold of the Home button. How to disable voice control on an iPhone in this case? It should be noted that a similar scenario is found only on iOS 10 and newer software.



Deactivating VoiceControl is as follows:

  1. Turn on the mobile phone. Open its settings.
  2. Go to the menu item "General" - "Universal Access".
  3. Select "Home".
  4. Check the box next to "Turn off."

After that, voice control on an iPhone can be considered disabled 100%. This option to remove is not as difficult as it might seem initially.
