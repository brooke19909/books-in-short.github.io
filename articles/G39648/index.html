Hotel Acar Hotel 4 *, Turkey: description and reviews of tourists

Alanya is one of the most popular resort areas in Turkey. This region is visited every year by a large number of tourists who prefer relaxation in an exotic setting. The weather is always beautiful here, and throughout the holiday season the air warms up to a temperature of 35-40 degrees above zero. Tourists who relax here do not feel the intense heat, as the temperature softens due to the proximity of the region to the coastline washed by the waters of the Mediterranean Sea.

Hotel Acar Hotel 4 * is a very popular place to stay in this region. In it, several hundred tourists from different parts of Russia and Europe relax every summer. This is due to the fact that living here is not very expensive, and the service and conditions are simply excellent.



Location

Acar Hotel 4 * is located in the resort region of Turkey - Alanya, almost on the Mediterranean coast. The nearest airport is at a rather impressive distance from it - 130 km (Antalya).

The hotel area is surrounded by green plants and trees, as well as urban infrastructure: within walking distance from it there are several bars and restaurants, as well as nightclubs. Tourists who relax here can visit the shops nearby - they sell a considerable number of souvenirs that travelers buy for their friends and relatives.

Rooms

The entire complex of apartments, which is available in the Acar Hotel 4 * hotel (Alanya), is located in three large buildings. Each of them has 6 floors and a fairly modern look. All of them were built at the same time - in 2003. From this moment, regular repairs are carried out on the territory of each building and the hotel as a whole, which allow maintaining the foundation in good condition.

In total, Acar Hotel 4 * has 115 rooms, and they belong to two different categories: Standard and Family. Any apartment has all the conditions that are necessary for the comfort and safety of any guest.

All rooms have a private bathroom, where there is the necessary plumbing and furniture. Each guest is provided with a standard set of bath accessories, a bathrobe and a large terry towel. In addition, air conditioners are installed in the entire hotel building, thanks to which guests can adjust the air temperature in their room to the optimum.

All types of rooms are strictly non-smoking. It is also not allowed to stay with pets.

Entertainment

Adult tourists do not have to get bored during the holidays. For them, the hotel throughout the resort period hosts various outdoor events, and they are of a different nature: entertaining and sports. In particular, every morning here begins with sports relay races, which are held on sports fields and near the pool - vacationers actively participate in them.

In the evenings, parties are held with the participation of local DJs, who perform foreign hits, known throughout the world.

Nutrition

Tourists staying at the hotel on an all-inclusive basis, can eat at a local restaurant, which is striking in its beauty. It serves a buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner. You can visit this place as part of the "all inclusive" package from 10 a.m. to 11 p.m. Guests who have been here note the excellent taste of the food served, as well as the nutritional value of dishes.



In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has a small bar, which is located near the pool. He works in the same mode as the restaurant. The bar serves snacks and drinks, including both cold and hot.

Tourists in their comments very often note that the local restaurant also has conditions for younger guests - they feature special highchairs, as well as a special menu that includes only the brightest and most delicious dishes that will surely appeal to the kids.

Pool

The hotel has one large swimming pool, which is located in the open air. Its area is 100 square meters. m. Due to the fact that the maintenance staff performs regular cleaning in it, the water in the tank is always crystal clear, but without heating. At night, light bulbs light up on its walls, which give the whole structure a richer look.

Guests, who love water procedures, in between, can comfortably sit on the sun terrace, which is located around the pool. Its entire area is furnished with comfortable sun loungers, each of which has a large umbrella.

Services

In addition to the standard services that are included in the package, guests can also use the additional features that are in the Acar Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya / Obagol). Among them, laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as ironing, are very popular, for the use of which you have to pay at a separate tariff.

The hotel also rents vehicles, including cars and bicycles. On them you can go traveling in the immediate vicinity, while exploring the area and attractions. In case of renting a car, a parking space for it will be provided completely free of charge for the entire period.

In one of the buildings, on the ground floor, there is an exchange office where you can get the necessary currency - this service is provided free of charge. By the way, according to tourists, the staff who work in this hotel speaks excellent Russian, which greatly facilitates communication with them for guests from Russia and the CIS countries.

Beach

The beach line, located near the hotel, is covered with sand and pebbles. According to reviews of Acar Hotel 4 * (Alanya), tourists are very fond of visiting this place. They say that the territory of the recreation area is always clean, there is a very picturesque nature. Many of them prefer to sit on a wooden pier, which is located above the sea.

As for the comfort of the location, tourists are always given the opportunity to rent a personal sun lounger and umbrella - the cost of such will be about $ 1. For the same fee, inflatable swimming mattresses can be issued here.

The beach area is perfectly equipped for relaxation - it has several public toilets, showers and locker rooms. Along the coastline there is a small amount of entertainment in the form of trips on airbags, as well as water skiing - for the most extreme travelers. If you wish, you can play beach volleyball.



Prices

At Acar Hotel (Alanya), the prices are low, as the guests themselves note. Statistics show that in this hotel even the cost of paid services is slightly lower than in other places of rest, which are located in the same region of Turkey, next door.

As for the cost of trips to Acar Hotel 4 *, in the reviews for 2017, tourists often give specific figures, which are approximately equal to 55,000 rubles. This price is given for a couple of vacationers who wish to stay in apartments of the "standard" category for seven days. According to travelers, a package purchased for such a price includes not only room rental, but also a flight from Moscow (there and back), airport transfer, meals, apartment rental, as well as payment of the required insurance premium.

Tourists should remember that for a visit to Turkey, Russian citizens must obtain a visa, the cost of which is about $ 80.
