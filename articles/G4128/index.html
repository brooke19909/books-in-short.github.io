Hotel Citrus Waskaduwa 5 * (Kalutara, Sri Lanka): description, services, reviews

Have you wanted to visit Sri Lanka for a long time? Accustomed to comfort and excellent service? Want to be able to enjoy the ocean from your room? In this case, as an option for accommodation, be sure to consider the five-star Citrus Waskaduwa.



Location

This hotel is located on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka, in the resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãWaskaduwa. The nearest town of Kalutara is seven kilometers away. The distance to the airport is 78 kilometers. According to tourists, the transfer from the air harbor takes about two hours. Vaskaduva resort is famous for its beaches, warm ocean, rich underwater world and a lot of attractions.



Description

Citrus Waskaduwa 5 * was built in 2014. It is located just a couple of hundred meters from the ocean. Its area is 3.2 hectares. The hotel has 149 comfortable rooms. All apartments have luxurious ocean views.

The hotel offers meals in the following systems: only breakfast, breakfast and dinner, full board and all inclusive. Most tourists choose one of the first two options. The hotel restaurants are open buffet style. Here you can taste perfectly prepared dishes of both local and European cuisine.

The hotel has a fitness center, outdoor pools (children and adults), two bars, conference rooms, a reading room, a 24-hour reception, a tour desk, and laundry facilities. The hotel's spa offers guests a wide range of services. Tourists can also enjoy free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.



Citrus Waskaduwa 5 *: tourist reviews

Every experienced traveler knows how important it is to prepare in advance for any trip abroad. This applies not only to the choice of direction and purchase of tickets, but also to the search for the hotel in which you plan to stay for several days, or even weeks of vacation. This point becomes especially important for those tourists who are accustomed to comfortable hotels of 4- and 5-star categories. So how not to make a mistake with the choice? Experienced travelers are advised to focus not only on the official description and advice of travel agents, but also carefully study as many reviews of real people who have already stayed in the hotel you are interested in. In their comments, former guests try to share their own experience. Therefore, you can get a more complete and close to the real state of affairs idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhat will await you during your stay in a particular hotel. Today we suggest finding out whether our compatriots were satisfied with their stay at the Citrus Waskaduwa 5 * hotel in Sri Lanka.

Rating

Judging by the ratings put forth by the guests, this hotel fully met their expectations. So, the overall rating of the hotel is 4.4 points out of the maximum possible five. The highest ratings were given to infrastructure, room equipment and quality of service. Some tourists expressed dissatisfaction with the service for children. However, we suggest learning about everything in more detail.



Rooms

Despite the fact that Citrus Waskaduwa 5 * (Sri Lanka) is a new hotel, according to many tourists, the situation is partially in need of repair. As for the rooms, they are all spacious and stylishly decorated. True, in some places cracks are already visible on the walls. Some guests suggest that the fault is not very high quality materials used in the construction of the hotel. In fact, worry about this is not worth it. After all, such trifles do not completely spoil the overall impression of the housing stock. Almost all travelers in their reviews enthusiastically noted the magnificent ocean view opening from the windows of the rooms. It is very close, so here you will get a unique opportunity, getting up in the morning and opening the balcony door, let in the room the sound of waves and saturated sea air.

As for the situation, the rooms have everything you need: modern appliances, comfortable furniture, a safe. However, according to guests, hotel employees sometimes forgot to bring a bathrobe, slippers and an extra set of towels (if you came with a child). But it is only necessary to inform about it at the reception how the problem will be solved in the shortest possible time.



Food

Many of our compatriots who have visited the Citrus Waskaduwa 5 * hotel (Kalutara) advise you to pay when booking either the option of meals, which includes only breakfast, or half board. Indeed, in Sri Lanka there are many interesting places. Therefore, most tourists almost every day go somewhere on a tour. You can always eat during the day outside the hotel. Moreover, in its vicinity there are several excellent establishments with good cuisine and affordable prices.

As for food in the hotel itself, then, as guests note, here it is organized at a very good level. So, the menu is always diverse and includes all kinds of meat and seafood dishes, vegetables, exotic fruits, and delicious desserts. Breakfast at the hotel is European. True, tourists are advised to pay attention to the fact that most dishes are spicy. It is worth bearing in mind this fact, especially if you need to follow a certain diet for medical reasons or if you come to rest with children.



Service

On this item, the guests of Citrus Waskaduwa 5 * mainly express satisfaction. True, some hotel guests noted that room cleaning could have been more qualitative. So, according to them, maids rarely wipe the dust and almost never look into hard-to-reach places (under the bed, behind the nightstand, etc.). However, the guests were pleased that every day after cleaning, they find funny figurines of towels in their room. The maids also leave tangerines and sweets on the pillows of the guests. Children especially enjoyed such presentations. And if your birthday will be during your stay at the hotel, the administration will congratulate you with a cake and a bottle of wine. As for the hotel staff as a whole, our compatriots note its friendliness, quickness and willingness to always help in resolving all issues.



Entertainment

As many tourists say in their reviews, the Citrus Waskaduwa 5 * hotel is a great place for a relaxing holiday. There is no daily animation, as, for example, in hotels in Turkey and Egypt. Instead, guests can stroll through the large and well-groomed hotel grounds, watching the huge lizards and agile badgers, play billiards, relax in the spa, and visit the gym. The hotel also has an outdoor pool and a sun terrace. In addition, experienced tourists are advised to visit at least a few excursions. After all, Sri Lanka is an amazing country with an interesting culture and amazing nature. You can book a tour both at the hotel reception and at one of the local companies.

Beach

As guests note, from the hotel to the ocean you can literally walk in a couple of minutes with an unhurried step. The beach here is quite spacious, few people. Please note that you are unlikely to swim in the ocean in the truest sense of the word because of the constant waves. Therefore, to enter the water will turn out only waist-high. But tourists are happy to jump in the waves. Experienced travelers also warn that it is not worth leaving things unattended on the beach, because it is public. Therefore, to protect yourself from theft, it is advisable to constantly be on the alert.
