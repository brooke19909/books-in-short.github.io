Three Corners Palmyra Resort Hotel: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Sharm El Sheikh is one of the most popular cities visited by thousands upon thousands of tourists every year. This Egyptian resort can offer the traveler not only sandy beaches, a warm sea and luxurious coral reefs, but also upscale hotels and an excellent level of service. This is where the Three Corners Palmyra Resort Hotel is located. Many people during the trip planning period are looking for additional information about a particular hotel. For example, where exactly is it located, is there a beach, shops, recreation areas nearby? Is it possible to relax here with a child? Is the hotel recommended by tourists who have already managed to spend some time in it?

Location Description

The large hotel complex Three Corners Palmyra Resort is located in the picturesque resort town of Sharm El Sheikh, about the second coastline - the nearest beach is about 500 m away.





One of the advantages of the hotel is its proximity to the airport - you can get there in 5 minutes, so guests do not have to spend hours on the road. The city center with all its attractions, shopping and entertainment points can be reached by car or bus in about 20 minutes.

Hotel Three Corners Palmyra 4 * (Egypt): infrastructure and territory

The hotel complex is quite spacious - its area is 35 thousand square meters. The entire hotel area is clean, well-groomed, decorated with palm trees and exotic plants. The hotel consists of several two- and three-story buildings built in a beautiful oriental style. Patios are reserved for swimming pools, terraces, playgrounds for games and recreation.

By the way, the Three Corners Palmyra Resort hotel complex was built in 2002. In 2005, an almost complete reconstruction was carried out, and cosmetic repairs and improvements are organized here every year.

What do hotel rooms look like?

The hotel has 331 rooms:

  • 261 standard rooms for 2 people (+ child);
  • 68 spacious family apartments;
  • 2 suites with a high level of comfort;
  • there are also connected rooms that are suitable for a large family or company.




It is natural that many guests are interested in living conditions at the hotel. According to reviews, the rooms here are bright, spacious and clean. Almost every room has access to a small terrace or balcony. The room has a full range of furniture and household appliances. For example, there is a telephone with direct dialing function, a TV, for watching which you can pass the time, as well as a radio, an alarm clock and a small safe (it can be delivered to some rooms upon request). The minibar is filled at the request of guests, and the bill is billed separately.

The bathroom will please with cleanliness, convenience and constant availability of hot and cold water. You can use a spacious shower, washbasin, toilet mirror. Housekeeping is carried out every day, and reviews of tourists confirm that the premises are kept in almost perfect cleanliness.

Dining

Like almost all hotels in Egyptian resorts, Three Corners Palmyra Resort offers its guests full three meals a day. The main meals are held at the main restaurant. In a spacious room you can take a comfortable table and choose your own food according to your own taste. By the way, here guests are offered dishes of international, international, Egyptian cuisine. Seafood is served from time to time, and themed evenings are organized, for example, on Italian or Chinese cuisine.





Naturally, the hotel has several bars, for example, in the lobby and by the pool. Here you can get light snacks and refresh yourself with chilled drinks.

How far is the beach?

Most tourists visit Egypt precisely for the purpose of a pleasant holiday by the sea. What can the Three Corners Palmyra Amar el Zaman 4 * hotel offer in this regard? Immediately it is worth saying that the beach is located quite far away - the distance to the sea coast is about 9 kilometers. Nevertheless, the hotel tried to solve this problem by providing guests with a permanent free shuttle to the beach and back. Of course, you will have to spend about 20 minutes on the road, but buses run regularly.





The beach here is sandy and pebble. There is a convenient entrance to the sea, cleared of corals and stones, as well as a sandbank where children can swim. But you should not forget about the appropriate beach shoes. There are enough sun loungers and parasols on the shore. There is also a bar where guests can have lunch if they do not want to return to the hotel. Of course, a rescue team is constantly working on the beach.

By the way, there is a second beach, which is more suitable for families with children, as the coastal zone here is very shallow. And the distance to it is much smaller - about 1000 meters.

Water activities and beach vacations

It is natural that many tourists embark on a trip in order to pleasantly and actively spend their holidays. Is there such an opportunity for the guests of the Three Corners Palmyra 4 * hotel? Sharm el Sheikh is famous not only for its mild climate and warm sea, but also for its picturesque coral reefs. It is the rich underwater world that attracts scuba diving fans. By the way, there are several diving schools nearby, where for a reasonable price you can rent suits and equipment, as well as purchase an excursion to the most picturesque sections of the coast. Beginners have the opportunity to take a short training course.





Naturally, on the beach you can go boating, canoeing, jet skiing, water skiing, banana, boat, etc. In short, every vacationer will be able to find a fun exciting activity for themselves.

Conditions for families with a child

It is natural that many tourists today prefer to relax with the whole family, along with their children. And for such travelers, questions about the availability of certain amenities are extremely important. What does The Three Corners Palmyra offer?

Immediately it is worth reporting that children feel comfortable at the hotel. Extra beds in the room, chairs for feeding, a large selection of dishes for children of different ages - this is what this hotel can guarantee guests.

In terms of entertainment, this place also has some advantages. For example, a good playground with sandboxes and a swing is equipped for the guys. There is also a small, shallow pool, in which the water is constantly heated. Older children can use the water slides in the adult pool - this attraction will bring a lot of pleasant experiences to both parents and young tourists. In addition, a mini club is open every day, which accepts children from 4 to 11 years old. Here they are provided with a huge number of toys. Dancing and drawing classes are held regularly. And funny animators daily organize various team games, productions and performances that bring a lot of fun to children.





Additional service on site

Naturally, the hotel offers only the best conditions for its guests. Immediately it is worth noting that dry cleaning and laundry are constantly working here, the services of which are quite inexpensive. You can access the Internet at any convenient time. There is ample parking with constant security. By the way, renting a transport in the city is not at all difficult - you just need to have the necessary documents.

Here, at the hotel, you can use the currency exchange office (by the way, the rate here is pretty good). Guests are invited to visit local small shops where you can buy household items, essentials and, of course, unique souvenirs. In case of deterioration in health, you can always contact the medical center.

Hotel Three Corners Palmyra (Sharm El Sheikh): leisure travelers

What to do for a tourist who decided to stay at this hotel? In fact, hardly anyone will be bored here. In the courtyard there are seven pools of different sizes (some of them are heated). Here guests swim, organize various competitions and just relax on comfortable sun loungers.





There are two tennis courts, next to which you can rent balls and rackets. There are playgrounds for basketball and futsal. Tourists who want to always keep fit always and everywhere are offered to visit the gym, equipped with modern equipment. Everyone can sign up for group exercises in gymnastics, take a few dance lessons. There is an opportunity to do horseback riding, beginners often sign up for a couple of lessons. In the evenings, the hotel's entertainment team is entertained by an animation team.

What else can Three Corners Palmyra have to offer? Egypt is a country with incredibly interesting traditions, ancient history and rich culture. This is an ideal place for an excursion vacation. You can buy an interesting tour at the hotel or contact the city sightseeing centers for help.

Hotel Traveler Reviews

Three Corners Hotel Palmyra Amar el Zaman is a popular tourist destination from all over the world. Travelers leave mostly positive reviews, as the living conditions and service here are really at a high level. For example, a large well-groomed area is perfect for entertainment and quiet walks. All rooms are decorated in a bright oriental style, so you can feel like in a real fairy tale.

The food is also quite good - the dishes in the restaurant are always fresh, the menu is quite varied, so no one will remain hungry. The disadvantage of the hotel is its remoteness from the beach, although getting to it is not difficult. The staff here are polite and friendly, and they understand the Russian language quite well. Three Corners Palmyra Resort combines what modern travelers tirelessly seek , namely: quality service and low room prices.
