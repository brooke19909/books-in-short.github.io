Hotel Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos): photos and reviews of tourists

Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos) annually receives thousands of visitors from different countries. This 4-star hotel is very popular - there are practically no empty rooms. Such popularity is not surprising, because this hotel has everything that a tourist needs.





Location

Built in 2008, Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * is located in Paphos. This is an amazing and picturesque place. According to Greek mythology, not far from Paphos, on the beach of Petra tou Romiu, the goddess of beauty and love Aphrodite was born.

The hotel itself is located two kilometers from the city center. The airport is also quite close - only 13 kilometers away.

And of course, within walking distance there is a beach. It can be reached in just 1-2 minutes. And in the immediate vicinity are the archaeological complex, the city port and the Royal Tombs. And of course, there are a lot of different restaurants, bars, cafes and other entertainment facilities.

Bars and Restaurants

Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos) serves its guests according to several systems - depending on what kind of service the guests themselves paid. There are breakfasts, lunches and dinners. They are served in an elegantly decorated restaurant, which offers picturesque frontal sea views. It, like the entire facade of the hotel complex, is made of glass, so eating will be a pleasure, accompanied by a beautiful landscape. By the way, it serves mainly dishes of international and Mediterranean cuisine, which, of course, will appeal to gourmets.

Opposite the restaurant is the Lounge Bar, which offers a huge variety of soft and strong drinks. There is also a Pool Bar. It is also worth a look there.





Service

In Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos) everything is created for the convenience and comfort of guests. Here, round-the-clock service is organized, there is a car parking, an elevator and left-luggage office for storing things, as well as amenities for people with disabilities.

Also, if necessary, guests can use the services of laundry, dry cleaning or ironing. On the territory of the complex you can find various shops, a currency exchange office, a tour desk, car rental, a fax and photocopy office. There is even a small beauty salon. But this is not the main highlight of the complex. Much more interesting is that wedding ceremonies are held here. People who want to formalize their relationship should keep this in mind.

Other guests can be provided with a transfer service. And people with children can call a nanny - specialists of the corresponding profile in the hotel also work.

Recreation and entertainment

The Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos) has everything that will help brighten up leisure for tourists. There are several pools (located both under the open sky and indoors), a jacuzzi, a billiard room, a library, bicycle rental, a library.

Water sports are available, such as diving and snorkelling. You can rent a boat - both for walking and for fishing. Many, while in Greece, decide to go windsurfing - since there are windy places that create this opportunity.

And also on the territory of Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 *, the photo of which is presented above, there is a spa. In principle, this can be easily guessed. After all, the name of the complex clearly hints at this fact. So, you should definitely visit this Spa-center. There are aromatherapy treatments, a Turkish bath, a jacuzzi, a hydromassage, a steam room and a sauna. You can also go on a wellness massage.

If such entertainment is not to your liking, then it is better to go to the gym, play darts, tennis or golf, do aerobics. There are many options - everyone will find something to their liking, if you do not want to leave the hotel.





Budget accommodation options

In Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * (Paphos) there are rooms of various categories. The most inexpensive are standard. Their area is 23 square meters. m, and from their windows overlook the city or mountains. Inside, there is a 26-inch plasma TV with satellite channels and high-speed Wi-Fi.

There is a refrigerator, blackout curtains, a spacious bathroom with a combined bath and shower. A free hairdryer and hygiene products are also provided.

Other practical amenities include an iron, safe, ironing board, and telephone. If guests have such a need, they can provide them with a crib or cradle. Inside there is air conditioning. Sleeping guests in a spacious 2 double bed.

A week of living in such apartments for two will cost about 45 thousand rubles with breakfast. This is if without discounts. But usually the hotel administration holds various promotions. If you manage to get in at a good time, you can book a room 20-30% cheaper than the usual price.

About booking

Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * (Cyprus) receives mostly positive reviews . And many people, having read the comments pleasing to the eye, decide to experience all this on themselves. Therefore, the numbers "grab" very quickly, especially shortly before the start of the season. Therefore, it is better to reserve an apartment a couple of months before the trip.

Check-in at 15:00 and check-out until noon. The hotel can accommodate children of any age. If the child is not even two years old, then a separate cot is provided. And you do not need to pay extra for accommodation. Only in order to receive it, you must contact the property. And at least a day before arrival - so that the staff has time to prepare the crib and install it in the room.

It should also be noted that when booking, you will need to indicate not only your details, but also credit card information. The hotel warns that they can block the funds in the account before the arrival of the guests. This acts as a guarantee. Since many people reserve rooms in advance in order to leave them for themselves, and then cancel them because of broken plans or poorly planned trips. And this does not allow real guests to book apartments. So a guarantee is needed.

About staff

Now we can say a few words about what tourists leave about staying at Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 * reviews. They are mostly positive, and this can be understood.

Many guests pay special attention to the staff and the quality of service. Actually, this is an important nuance. Firstly, it is encouraging that the staff speaks six languages. These are Turkish, French, English, German, Greek and Russian. The latter is especially pleasing to people who are not polyglot.

As many claim, despite the fact that the check-in starts at 15:00, the administration can make concessions and check in guests earlier than this time. If, of course, there is such an opportunity (or, in other words, the room has already been prepared and put in order after the recently arrived guests). The rooms are cleaned carefully, towels are changed every other day.

By the way, guests recommend booking apartments with pool views. There, they claim, is quieter. But, in principle, you can always close the door to the balcony and, if it's hot, turn on the air conditioning.





What else is worth knowing

Many people leave reviews about Capital Coast Resort & Spa 4 *. The year 2016 was successful for Greece, especially in the summer. Many of our compatriots flew to her resorts. And many stayed at this hotel.

Tourists say that the pool is very helpful. There is access to the sea, you can literally walk in a couple of minutes, but in windy weather there are waves. And there are stones too. Although it scares only those people who are used to the sandy bottom and the beach. In fact, stones are not so bad. The sea is rarely polluted, and the inconvenient entrance can be endured. However, every 15-20 minutes from a nearby bus stop there is a flight to a sandy beach called Coral Bay. A ticket costs one and a half euros. You can buy a travel card. Daily, unlimited costs five euros. But a taxi for a short distance will cost 10-12 euros.

In general, people who visited here liked the rest. This conclusion can be made based on reviews. So, for example, many people say - if you order a cocktail at the bar, then you can give a second one for free, and then also shot or even a hookah. By the way, in the bar and restaurant there is a very wide variety of dishes and drinks, everyone will find something for themselves.

In general, this is an excellent hotel, corresponding to the level of four stars. If you want a vacation to be remembered for a long time, you should go here.
