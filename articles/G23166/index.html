Mediterranean Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus / Limassol): reviews, hotel ratings

Every year, more and more tourists choose Cyprus as their vacation destination. This island country has a lot to offer tourists: picturesque beaches and volcanic coasts, from the beauty of which are breathtaking, as well as a warm climate and quality service. And the visa policy here is very loyal, which allows tourists to travel around the island and enjoy a vacation without too much red tape with documents.

Naturally, on the coasts of Cyprus over the past few years, many hotels and pensions of various sizes and categories have been opened. One of the most popular tourist destinations is the Mediterranean Beach Hotel. There is nothing strange in the fact that every traveler tries to get as much valuable information as possible about the place of vacation. Where exactly is the hotel located? How far from it are the famous beaches of Limassol? Are there any conditions for a family holiday with a child? What kind of reviews do tourists who have already managed to spend several days leave here? The answers to these questions are sought by many tourists.

Description of the hotel and its location





Location can be an important advantage. So where exactly is the Mediterranean Beach Hotel located? Limassol (Limassol) - a picturesque resort on the young coast of Cyprus. It is here, on the shore of the bay of the same name, that the luxurious hotel complex is located. By the way, the airports are located at some distance, which is 70 km (Larnaca Airport) and 72 km (in the city of Paphos). You should not worry about this, as almost all travel companies, as well as the hotel itself, provide tourists with a convenient transfer.

The Mediterranian Hotel itself is a cozy place with a beautifully arranged area. Around the picturesque gardens and palm groves, where you can have a good time, enjoying the peace, exotic beauty and pleasant coolness. By the way, this hotel is the owner of a mass of prizes and awards, it is considered one of the best tourist destinations.

Hotel Mediterranian (Cyprus) what rooms does it offer?

This hotel is quite large - on its territory there are 292 rooms of various categories and capacities. By the way, 55 of them are adjacent, which means that, if desired, they can be used for cohabitation of a large family or company of friends.

The rooms are equipped with modern appliances, including air conditioning, TV, telephone, safe, work area. Also at guests' disposal is a minibar, which, however, is filled only with drinking water and soda. The remaining drinks are delivered to the room on request only. By the way, room service is available around the clock. The bathroom is equipped with modern fixtures. High-quality cleaning is provided daily. Reviews indicate that the Hotel Mediterranian (Cyprus) is an ideal place for a truly comfortable pastime.

Description and photos of rooms





The Mediterranean Beach Hotel hotel complex offers its guests a choice of several categories of rooms, each of which has its own advantages and features:

  • Deluxe Rooms - comfortable spacious rooms decorated in a calm Mediterranean style. They offer wonderful views of the courtyard or the sea. There is a spacious bathroom with a bathtub and hand shower, as well as a full range of modern household appliances. The room is designed for 2 or 3 people.
  • There are other options that the Mediterranean Beach Hotel can offer. Family Pool View Rooms - spacious rooms, divided into a sleeping and comfortable living area with soft folding sofas. The bedroom has one large double or two single beds. There is a terrace or balcony with views of the courtyard and pool. A tea and coffee set is also included.
  • Superior Rooms - spacious superior rooms designed for two people. There is also a bedroom and living area with upholstered furniture. In addition to the above technique, there is an ironing press. As a gift, guests receive free internet access, as well as soft bathrobes and slippers.
  • Luxury Studios are very large rooms, furnished with luxurious furniture, including a king size bed. There are two televisions (one in the living area), as well as a disc player, radio and a modern speaker system. There is wireless internet access. Guests are provided with bathrobes and slippers. There is a set for making aromatic coffee and tea.
  • Areti Suites - spacious rooms, consisting of a separate bedroom and living room. The bedroom has two large beds, as well as a private balcony and a spacious bathroom, decorated with granite. The living room is furnished with stylish modern furniture, including a large folding sofa. There is also a well-equipped dining area with a table and chairs. There is a small table for negotiations. By the way, you can connect a video conferencing system to the TV in the living room. Room can accommodate up to 6 guests. It is often used for business meetings in an informal setting, as well as for a secluded, unforgettable honeymoon.
  • Aura Suites - rooms that are simply stunning with their design. Here you will find luxurious furniture made from quality varieties of wood, original paintings hang on the walls, and all rooms are decorated with authentic antique products. There is not only a bedroom, but also a spacious living room. By the way, accommodation is also allowed here with children.

Hotel Mediterranean Beach (Limassol): meals for guests





For many tourists, the issue of nutrition is extremely important. After all, delicious food is an integral part of a truly high-quality and enjoyable stay. This hotel complex offers its guests a standard scheme according to which all-inclusive Limassol hotels operate. Naturally, if you wish, you can refuse and not pay for this service when booking a room. Nevertheless, most tourists choose exactly three meals a day at the hotel.

Reviews indicate that the food here is incredibly tasty and always fresh, and the atmosphere of local restaurants has a pleasant, relaxed pastime. And dinners here are often accompanied by interesting performances.

What kind of catering facilities are available on site?

What can the Mediterranean Beach Hotel offer? Cyprus is a country famous for its hospitality and interesting cuisine.

  • You should definitely visit the Celeste Restaurant, which offers a large selection of international dishes. In the summer, there is an outdoor pool terrace, and interesting themed evenings are often held here.
  • Aquaria Restaurant is a luxurious restaurant that is famous for its amazing design. The central composition here is a huge pool, as well as a ceiling decorated in the form of a starry sky. It offers garden and sea views.
  • Ristorante Bacco is a wonderful Italian restaurant where you can enjoy delicious pasta, as well as fish and seafood dishes.
  • Shumi Suchi Bar is a place that will appeal to fans of Japanese cuisine, as it serves the freshest and most popular oriental dishes.
  • Bistro Nautica is a small cafe located near the beach. Guests who have paid all-inclusive meals can dine here (buffet meals are served for them). In addition, you can order other dishes, including fresh salads, fish, pasta, Cypriot meze, etc.
  • Ouzeti tis Myrtos is a small, traditional Uzery cafe with a poolside terrace. Here guests are offered the most delicious Cypriot cuisine.
  • Lagoon Pool Bar - A bar located by the pool itself. On the one hand, there are graceful arbors with designer furniture, and on the other, high chairs standing right in the pool.
  • Cloud Lounge Bar - A bar that is characterized by a stylish atmosphere. Here you can drink aromatic morning coffee, relax with spirits and enjoy delicious desserts from local chefs.
  • Blu Bar is another interesting establishment, which, however, opens with sunset. Its interior is decorated in blue shades, and from the large panoramic windows you can admire the starry night sky and views of the pool.

Where is the beach located? Water leisure tourists





The beaches of Limassol around the world are famous for their beauty and purity. The hotel is located just a few minutes walk from the seashore. It is worth saying that this beach was awarded the prestigious Blue Flag Prize for its cleanliness and compliance with environmental standards. By the way, this is a place of public use, and therefore it is often quite crowded here. According to reviews, the entrance to the sea is cleared of stones, and the bottom is covered with sand. Nevertheless, it is worth getting beach shoes for greater safety.

For a small fee, you can rent a comfortable chaise longue with a soft mattress, as well as a sun umbrella. On the shore there are equipped volleyball courts, as well as several cafes where travelers are offered chilled drinks, snacks and even desserts. From the beach begins the promenade along the coast 12 km long. Tourists use it for walks and even morning jogs.

Fans of more active pastime can visit the sports center of the Mediterranean Beach Hotel 4 *. Here you can rent a boat or pedal boat , go water-skiing, banana, boat. There is also a good diving school where you can get a sea excursion to the most interesting places with a rich underwater world, rent equipment, and take a short, informative training course (relevant for beginners). Also, the coast is suitable for sailing, and the necessary equipment can be rented here, in the center of water sports. Due to the mild climate, Limassol is also suitable for water recreation in April.

Does the hotel offer additional services?

Naturally, the Mediterranean Beach Hotel provides its guests with everything necessary for a truly comfortable stay. To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel has dry cleaning and laundry services, and you can also iron clothes (not all rooms have a board, iron or trouser press). There are several shops, including a boutique where you can buy clothes, including one that is necessary to visit the beaches. There is a gift shop where customers are offered a large number of original trinkets. You can also visit the jewelry store, where they offer not only products from precious metals, but also high-quality jewelry.

On the territory of the hotel you can easily rent a bicycle, car and some other types of transport (naturally, you need to have the necessary package of documents). Also, guests are entitled to free use of the hotel parking.

Hotel Mediterranean Beach Hotel is focused not only on family, but also on business holidays. On its territory there are four spacious conference rooms of different capacities (from 30 to 450 people). If necessary, you can get video and audio equipment, as well as other equipment necessary for meetings. And these rooms can also be used for celebrations, including weddings. By the way, the hotel staff are excellent at organizing this or that event, including luxurious wedding ceremonies.

Leisure and entertainment: how do guests spend their free time?





Not only on the beach can you find a way to have fun. You can have fun on the territory of the hotel itself. For example, in the courtyard there is a large multi-level pool with beautiful small waterfalls and decorative caves. Around every day there are comfortable sunbeds with mattresses, as well as umbrellas that protect you from the scorching sun in the summer heat.

There is also a large tennis court, next to which you can rent balls, rackets. In the evening, the area is illuminated at an additional cost. You should definitely visit the Aquum Healt Spa Health Club, which offers a huge range of services. For example, there is a large gym with playgrounds and modern fitness equipment, which is suitable for people who want to always keep fit. Also, professional trainers regularly conduct various group exercises, for example, in Pilates or aerobics. There is also an indoor pool, in which the water is heated - this place is ideal for relaxing in the cold season.

Also, for a reasonable fee, guests can use the sauna, relax in the jacuzzi with a powerful hydromassage system. Massage rooms are constantly working, which offer both a general relaxing massage and various wellness treatments. Reviews indicate that the center employs real professionals whose services are worth using. In the health club you can sign up for other treatments, including some anti-aging and wellness.

A team of cheerful and talented animators is constantly working on the territory of the hotel. It is they who entertain the guests during the day. In addition to traditional competitions, guests can participate in team competitions, take a few dance lessons, in a word, have fun. And in the evenings, vivid performances and live music await you to dance to.

Do not forget that Cyprus is a country with an interesting history and culture. This makes it a great place for an intense excursion vacation. At the hotel you will surely be told about the most interesting places in the vicinity, and for a small fee they will help you plan an independent trip. Of course, tourists can purchase a ready-made group excursion tour and visit the most beautiful corners of the island.

Since the hotel is located in the resort town, you can count on an active life outside the hotel area. There are shops nearby, and in the center there are shopping and entertainment centers, night clubs, discos, etc. No one is bored here.

Are there any conditions for relaxing with a child?





Many tourists are sure to take their children with them on vacation. Are there any relevant conditions on the territory of the Mediterranean Beach Hotel 4 *? Cyprus is a wonderful place where you should definitely take your child. A high-class service at the hotel will make your stay here truly comfortable.

Of course, you can count on a comfortable folding bed, which will be delivered to your room. Food will also not be a problem, because the choice of dishes is wide. By the way, in the restaurant you are sure to get a high chair for the child, you just have to ask one of the waiters. There is a spacious but shallow section in the pool where the kids can splash fun. Your child can have a good time at one of the playgrounds with swings, sandboxes, slides and other attractions.

The weather in Limassol is always good, the sea is warm, and therefore children love to have fun on the beach. And every day there is a club, which accepts little travelers aged 4 to 12 years. By the way, various activities for children are constantly held at the hotel, including team games, developmental lessons, dances, theatrical performances, etc. Teachers work with each age group of children separately, and therefore children can really have fun and comfortably.
