Although according to the European classification this hotel has four stars on the sign, the Bulgarians themselves position it with a higher rank. Resort Primorsko

This is one of the most amazing places in the south of the country. It is located on a cape that protrudes far into the sea, and everything is covered with sand dunes. There is a unique nature here - clean air, many exotic plants, untouched places. Picturesque cliffs, ruins of mysterious civilizations and at the same time comfort and convenience - this is what a person who decides to rest in Primorsko will get.

For tourists, this area has opened relatively recently. After all, there were favorite places for holidays of the highest Bulgarian party bonzes in the communist era. But now there are many youth and children's camps, private hotels and such "monsters" as our "Grand". In the town there are many parks planted with oaks, beeches, hornbeams, and citrus fruits are also grown here.

Primorsko Hotels

Now in the territory of this resort there are many different hotels of various price categories. Their classification is from three to five stars. The city of Primorsko is also famous for boarding houses with treatment and sanatoriums. The level of service is almost the same everywhere. Many of them have a modern interior and fresh repairs. Typically, in such hotels - good furniture, interesting design, large pools, good animation and spacious rooms, polite and trained staff. Among the most popular hotels are Imperial, Stanopol Lux, Golden Dunes and others.



How to get, where is the territory

Since Primorsko is located quite far from the main airports of the country, the transfer here will be quite long. It takes about four hours to go from Varna, and about two from Burgas (the nearest hub). The area in which the Grand Hotel Primorsko is built is very quiet. It is quite far from the center, so you will not be awakened at night by cheerful young people and the noise of discos. Nevertheless, there are many cheap cafes nearby.

The hotel consists of a main seven-story multi-level building and four additional six-story buildings. The hotel has a very beautiful lounge; chic carpets hang everywhere, which gives the impression of a palace. Near the hotel there is an excellent water park, so that children will be happy. The hotel has swimming pools, two restaurants, three bars (on the beach, by the pool and in the lobby), free parking, shops. Nearby is a golf course, a large supermarket. One of the bars, which is located near the hotel - "Tequila" - is highly recommended for close companies. He is in the basement, there is fun and good music.

Where to sunbathe and swim

The beach near the Grand Hotel (Primorsko) is sandy. It is called Northern. Walk to him for about ten minutes, through the beautiful sand dunes. Depth starts smoothly, so the coast is suitable for kids. The water is clean. After all, the entire coast of Primorsko is awarded the Blue Flag. The beach is two kilometers long, the sand is shallow, pleasant.

The coast is municipal, but note: in this area there are also paid plots. For money, also sunbeds and umbrellas. But at the same time there are a lot of different cafes and bars, relaxing music sounds. There are many people in the season, but you can always find a place. But there are often waves, because North Beach is loved by surfers.

If you go a little further, you will fall into another swimming area. This is the south beach. He stretched for a kilometer. The sea is calm, the water is warmer. It is separated from the coast by the Devil's Lake, and you need to go there on a suspension bridge. The sand is smaller here, but more often there are jellyfish. In addition, the territory has outdoor and indoor pools, as well as a pond for children. Sunbathing and swimming here is also very comfortable. The outdoor pool is large and clean.



Rest in Primorsko: excursions

Very close to this resort is the famous Ropotamo Nature Reserve. This is the real jungle, which is often even called the "Bulgarian Amazon". These forests with rich and rare vegetation, unusual for Eastern Europe, are located along the Ropotamo River flowing into the sea. This place received the status of a reserve in pre-war Bulgaria. Since time immemorial, tourists have been transported here with special boats among the jungle and picturesque cliffs. You can book a voyage on a boat. And in that, and in another case, the impression that you are somewhere in Africa and about to hear the roar of a tiger and the sounds of tom-tom will not let you go. Boats make stops so that tourists can take pictures against the backdrop of bizarre rock formations such as the Lion's Head.

There are also many caves and sand dunes, just like in the Sahara. The tour usually ends at Cape Maslen Nose, where often there are not only dolphins, but even Black Sea seals. Here you can also see the ruins of a Thracian settlement and sanctuary. The city of Primorsko is located not far from yet another amazing natural attraction - the Arkutino Botanical Garden. And for longer trips, travelers recommend taking public transport to the nearest ancient city of Sozopol.
