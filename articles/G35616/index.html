Eilat

This resort is located in the region that is considered the driest in all of Israel. The sea, the sun, beaches and its climate suitable for sea holidays - this is what Eilat is known all over the world for. The weather here is excellent for sunbathing. There is practically no rain. Diving enthusiasts come here because Eilat has the only marine reserve in Israel with a huge variety of underwater inhabitants. It is the world's northernmost coral reef. Among the historical attractions, tourists recommend visiting the nearby Timna Valley, which is also called the ‚ÄúMines of King Solomon‚Äù. Indeed, there are ancient mines for the extraction of copper, which are almost ten thousand years old. In addition, ancient cave paintings can be seen in artificially hollowed caves, and in the evenings there are various concerts and shows.

How to get when it's better to come

To get to the C-Hotel Eilat 3 * hotel (Israel, Eilat), you need to buy a ticket, as a rule, on a charter flight. Airplanes come here from many cities of Russia. But there are regular flights - often with a change in Tel Aviv. Directly to Eilat fly not only from Moscow, but from Voronezh, Kazan, Irkutsk, Yekaterinburg. Flights to Israel depart from Domodedovo. The budget carrier is Israeli, and the most expensive is Aeroflot.

Tourists come to Eilat for holidays and excursions all year round. The weather in these places, of course, varies depending on the season. In the summer months there is a forty-degree heat. In autumn and spring, the temperature is more comfortable. But even in winter you can swim in Eilat. By the way, the warmest sea here in December.





Location

The hotel is located in the center of Eilat. At the same time, it takes about ten minutes to go from it to the coast of the Red Sea. Tourists noted that from any end of the city it is convenient to get to the C-Hotel Eilat 3 *. The location of the hotel is close to the promenade, and from the bus station, and even from the airport of local airlines. After all, the Eilat hub is located right in the center of the city, and guests can watch takeoffs and landings right from the window. But it is not noisy, and planes do not interfere with sleep. They fly only during the day. Three minutes walk to a large grocery supermarket with not too high prices. In other shops, those that are directly at the exit from the hotel, the set of goods is small, and its cost is much more. There are also many cafes and currency exchange points around the hotel. Nearby bus stop.

Territory

We already wrote that this is a city hotel - C-Hotel Eilat 3 *. The hotel infrastructure is appropriate. This is a seven-story building. Almost next to it is a shopping center. On the first and second floors there is a synagogue and rooms for staff. On the third - the lobby and administration, as well as the restaurant. The building has an elevator. At the reception there is a room where you can check in your luggage. The restaurant has an outdoor terrace. And his banquet room, if necessary, can be turned into a room for conferences or seminars. Since the hotel has almost no territory of its own, or rather, it is very small, sports and playgrounds, swimming pools and a spa center are located on different floors of the building or on the terrace. The hotel has a large parking lot, but if there is still not enough space for a car, you can leave it in the parking lot of the shopping center.





C-Hotel Eilat 3 *: room description

The hotel has one hundred and sixty rooms for guests. There are standard rooms for two or three people. The hotel offers special facilities for families. There are rooms for adults with one child, as well as two or three children. For large families, accommodation is offered in the studio. All rooms are large in size, air-conditioned, with mini-refrigerators, cable channels, wardrobes and a large number of clothes hangers. Soft mattresses, wonderful pillows. The studio has a terrace, a jacuzzi and a small kitchenette.

In other rooms of the C-Hotel Eilat 3 * hotel guests can use a kettle and cups. The bathrooms are tidy, towels are changed daily. Shampoos and shower gels are not given out, but there are dispensers that constantly fill up. The balconies are small, but the higher you live, the better the view. Those who settle on the upper floors can admire not only the airport, but also the Jordanian mountains and the sea. Towels and linen are snow-white. Drinking water can be drawn directly from the tap. A hairdryer is issued at the reception at your request for the entire stay. If you come in the cold season, you can take a fan heater.

Nutrition

The guests of the C-Hotel Eilat 3 * hotel certainly will not remain hungry. The restaurant and lobby bar are at their service for breakfast, lunch, dinner, as well as during snacks. You can just sit on the terrace with a cup of coffee or a cocktail. Most tourists book half-board at this hotel (mainly breakfasts and dinners). There is a lot of food, and it is great. All, of course, is kosher. Breakfast includes herring, cod, yogurts and cottage cheese, different types of hummus, eggplant and zucchini, scrambled eggs and omelette, pasta, salads, pizza, puff pastries, but no meat. For sweets - honey, halva, granola, canned peaches and pears. Here, delicious bread and good coffee. Orange and lemon juices - freshly squeezed, with pulp.





Dinners are plentiful, serving several types of hot dishes. Huge selection of meat, fish, chicken. Of the fresh fruits, there are always oranges; in season, tangerines and apples. Amazing cakes. If you are going on an excursion, huge lunch boxes are collected for you. If you arrive late, then dinner will also be served in your room. There is a children's menu. You can even take food with you from the restaurant. Israel is a rather expensive country, so travelers are mostly satisfied that the food is organized at the hotel. After all, if they ate in restaurants on their own, it would cost a lot more. And you should definitely stay for a Shabbat dinner to see all this national splendor of food!

Entertainment

It's not boring to spend evenings at the C-Hotel Eilat 3 * hotel. Entertainment here is quite diverse, despite the fact that the hotel has only three stars on the sign. Sea vacation

Many tourists, coming to rest in Eilat, choose this particular hotel - C-Hotel Eilat 3 *. The beach is municipal. It starts directly from the Shopping Center and goes into the distance, towards Jordan. Walk to him from the hotel for about ten minutes, first along the central park, and then after a few more streets. The beaches here are mostly strewn artificially. They are sand and pebble, equipped with sunbeds. All the best beaches belong to expensive first-line hotels.

Along the shore there is a promenade - a promenade with shops, attractions, souvenir stalls. When it ends, the so-called wild beach begins. In fact, it is also municipal and equipped, with large parasols. Just few people come here, so it‚Äôs spacious. This coast is not necessary to walk. You can get on bus number 15. The fare costs a little more than four shekels. In the opposite direction, the same bus runs already at number 16. If you drive to the end, you will fall almost under the Egyptian border. There is a decent beach of the already closed Princess Hotel. You can snorkel here because there are two coral reefs.

Two hundred meters from the aquarium is Migdalor Beach. It is the best for snorkeling, because the fish swim there already two meters from the shore. But swimming there is bad, everywhere there are sharp rocks and reefs, so it is better to have special shoes with you. Beach towels are taken at the reception. When you use them, you just need to drop them in a special box in the lobby. There is also a pool at the hotel. It is located on the fourth floor. Around him there are not only sunbeds, but also sofas and a relaxation zone. Towels are also given there. There is a toilet and an apparatus from which they collect drinking water. From December 1, the pool is usually heated.





Excursions

A travel agency works directly in the building of the C-Hotel Eilat 3 * hotel.
