Features of the architecture and landscape of the residential quarter

Residential buildings No. 1 and No. 2 - 10-storey buildings. With the layout of one and two bedroom apartments. Panel houses with technical basement and attic. The facade is painted with weatherproof paints in bright colors, creating an atmosphere of good mood.





The remaining four houses (No. 3,4,5,6) are 17-story skyscrapers. It also provides an underground and an attic. The layout is more diverse: one-, two-, three-room apartments.

All apartments of the residential complex "Crimean" in Novosibirsk have one consonant advantage - high-quality interior decoration. All rooms are bright, filled with sun. The walls are painted in warm pastel colors used for interior decoration of residential premises. It is important to choose non-aggressive shades, and the designers of the Crimean residential complex in Novosibirsk reasonably took this into account. Interior doors and flooring are made in a color that harmonizes with the other elements of the decoration, which emphasizes the interior of the room and makes it more expressive.

The lined walls are covered with textured wallpaper. The flooring is covered with linoleum / laminate. In rooms with high humidity - tiles. A good imported sanitary ware with silent drain is mounted in the bathroom and bathtub. In renting apartments, all metering devices for calculating utility indicators have already been installed.

The courtyard of the residential quarter is landscaped and landscaped. Here are mounted modern children's play complexes, outdoor sports simulators, playgrounds. Children, sports enthusiasts and those who like to spend time outdoors will find activities for themselves here.





Features of infrastructure and transport accessibility of LCD "Crimean"

The infrastructure of the Crimean residential complex in Novosibirsk is adapted for adults and children, taking into account the needs of everyone, because comfortable living is simply unthinkable without developed infrastructure. Within walking distance are shopping centers and entire chains of stores, entertainment complexes, sports facilities, including the Zvezdny Ice Sports Palace, the Zarya Football Arena. Near the complex there are educational and preschool institutions.

Thanks to its convenient location near the Trolley railway station, each of the tenants can easily reach any point in the city. The nearest metro station is 10 minutes away by public transport.

General arrangement of the complex

The structural scheme is based on a reinforced concrete prefabricated building, brick walls with an effective layer of insulation and a ventilated facade system.

Exterior decoration of houses, common areas, elevators and staircases is made of high quality materials using the latest sound and heat insulation technologies.

The introduction of modern technology

The houses are equipped with high-quality European-made elevators. Lifts are equipped with:

  • special sensors that determine the floor and ensure a smooth ride;
  • managed informational sensory tabloids;
  • ultra-reliable video system.

All this contributes to increased safety indoors. On the territory of the residential complex "Crimean" in Novosibirsk, a public house network with wireless high-speed Internet was carried out.

Video surveillance is installed not only in elevators, but also on the territory of the LCD, which provides a high level of security for residents.

The level of comfort of residents is not violated due to energy-saving LED lights, providing soft lighting of adjacent areas and parking lots.
