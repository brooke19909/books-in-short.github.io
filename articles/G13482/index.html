Egypt, Taba: hotels, views and local delights

The history of the Egyptian city of Taba is not simple. After all, it is located on the very border with Israel, on the shores of the Gulf of Eilat. In 1967, the Israelis occupied Taboo during the Six Day War . And when they were kicked out of Sinai in the 1980s , the city remained a disputed territory. The last excess occurred in 2004, when Israeli artillery bombarded border Egypt. Taba, the Hilton Resort Hotel, and other resort sites were then hit hard. But now the feud between the two countries is completely over. Moreover, for residents of Israeli Eilat and Egyptian Taba, the right of visa-free travel is valid. The first rush to the casino, which, incidentally, is located in the same long-suffering "Hilton", and the Egyptians can go on excursions and shopping in Eilat.

Turquoise warm sea, a gold placer of sandy beaches, flowery coral reefs with their exotic inhabitants - all this is famous for Egypt (Taba). The hotels, the photos of which seem to take you to the Arabian tale of the endless bliss, are mostly built in oriental style. It is good to relax with young children, because this resort is quiet, it lacks various nightclubs, foam discos, but on the contrary, a peaceful atmosphere reigns.



Despite the remoteness from the main resort places of the country - Hurghada and Sharm el-Sheikh, housing prices here are the same. But this corner can offer the vacationer something that the rest of Egypt cannot boast of. Taba, whose hotels stretched to the very border with Israel, provides an opportunity to see four countries at once. Because of the narrow Gulf of Eilat, the coast of Saudi Arabia is visible! But in neighboring Jordan and Israel it is quite possible to visit, spending on it ... no more than one day. To see the mysterious Petra, visit the Holy Land, on which Jesus Christ walked, and return in the evening to your hotel - this is beyond the power of tourists in Hurghada.

Resting at this resort, one should not forget about local attractions. These are coral reefs that are in no way inferior to those located in the famous Dahab and the popular Sharm El Sheikh; the island of Pharaoh, on which the fortress of the Sultan Saladdin of the XII century rises, as well as exotic fishing villages - now resorts - of the Red Sea Riviera. From this border point it is convenient to make pilgrimages to the Christian shrines that Egypt is famous for. Taba, whose hotels offer a variety of excursions, is located near Mount Moses and the monastery of St. Catherine. Not far from those places settled the first hermit monk Anthony. In a word, it is difficult to list the attractions that are worth visiting here.



What choice of housing does Egypt (Taba) provide in these parts? Hotels of the highest category are located on the front page and have their own beaches. The wealthy Israelis mostly rest in them (because in their native Eilat they are still more expensive). Among the luxury hotels, the notorious Taba Hilton Resort stands out - a beautiful ten-story building surrounded by two-story bungalows.
