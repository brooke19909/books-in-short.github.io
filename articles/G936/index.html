Rest in the Moscow region: boarding house "Petrovo-Dalnee"

If you are looking for a place to stay in the Moscow region, where you can not only walk around the surroundings, then we advise you to opt for the Petrovo-Dalnee health resort.

Description of the boarding house





Pension "Petrovo-Dalnee" is very close to Moscow, so you can come here to relax, even for a couple of days. This is a modern resort located on a large territory (about 60 hectares), which has a well-developed infrastructure. Each guest will find something to their liking. In the beautiful green area you can engage in active sports, swim in the pool, organize a barbecue, take a walk or horseback ride and much more. The pension also has everything for unforgettable family celebrations and corporate parties.

Number of health resorts

Most of the boarding houses in the Moscow Region are a large landscaped area with several buildings. Petrovo-Dalniy is no exception. Residential rooms are allocated the whole building. In total, 110 apartments are provided for guests, among which are:

Each room has a double bed, bedside tables, table and chairs, armchairs, TV, telephone. There is also an equipped kitchen with a small fridge and a minibar, and a bathroom with a bath. All rooms, except for one room, have an extra bed in the form of a one and a half sofa. Two-room apartments have a living room, and five-room apartments have an office. Each room has a balcony with a wonderful view of the surrounding nature.

The apartments have central heating and air conditioning, so it is comfortable here at any time of the year. Hot and cold water is supplied uninterrupted.

Services and services provided in the boarding house

Among the services provided by the health resort staff are:

  • 24-hour reception
  • Free private parking
  • free wireless internet (Wi-Fi);
  • shared lounge area and TV lounge;
  • ATM in the territory;
  • equipped smoking areas.

Pets are not allowed.





In the pension "Petrovo-Dalnee" (Krasnogorsk district), guests are offered:

  • outdoor activities (beach, terrace, garden);
  • various sporting events (tennis, football, pool and others);
  • entertainment for children;
  • arbors for a barbecue;
  • a conference room for training, meetings, negotiations;
  • banquet room for various celebrations (weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, corporate parties).

The resort has a restaurant and bar. The price includes breakfast. You can eat both in the restaurant complex and in the room. The restaurant is designed for 100 people, and the banquet room can accommodate up to 200 guests. Also on the territory of the boarding house there are three venues for banquets for 60-200 people: "Island of Love", "Island of Fortune", "Birch Grove".

Leisure activities

In the boarding house "Petrovo-Dalnee" everything is provided for a comfortable and eventful vacation. For lovers of active classes, there is an equestrian center, football grounds, mini-golf, a tennis court, a gym equipped with modern fitness equipment, as well as an indoor 25-meter pool with water slides and attractions. Even in the pension you can go fishing from the shore or a boat, play billiards, take a steam bath or a sauna, enjoy the treatments in the spa. Not far from the pension "Petrovo-Dalnee" on the banks of the Istra River, a sandy beach is equipped.





On the territory of the health resort there are playgrounds with slides and other entertainment, as well as a games room. In the daytime and in the evenings, various animated performances are provided for children.

Location

Pension "Petrovo-Dalnee" is located just 20 km from the Moscow Ring Road in the village of the same name. Using public transport to get to the health resort is quite problematic. First you need to take bus number 549, running from Tushinskaya metro station to the Mechnikov Club stop. Then you need to go to the boarding house on foot for about 1 km.





You can drive a car along Rublevo-Uspensky or Novorizhskoye shosse before turning to Ilyinskoye. Already move on along it to the village of Petrovo-Dalniy. The boarding house is located in the depths of the village, between the rivers Moscow and Istra. If you look at the map of the Krasnogorsk region, the health resort is located in the southwest.
