Useful tips on how to crochet a summer scarf for a girl

Recently, this version of the headgear is gaining more and more popularity. Very often, knitted tight scarves are used in the form of stoles, ladies throw them on their shoulders over a coat or raincoats. Thinner products replace lighter panamas on hot days. For little fashionistas, you can make several headwear for different types of clothes that are different in color and ornament. Crocheting summer kerchiefs for girls is very simple. In this article you will find a description of possible ways to make this wardrobe item. Having shown imagination and patience, it is possible to connect several beautiful and unique "things" for young beauties. The main advantage will be that the openwork gizmos will turn out in a single copy.

Crochet a summer scarf for a girl can be knitted in several ways.

1. From the bottom up (photo 1). Work begins with two or three air loops and gradually expands at an angle to the desired size. Increases are made on both sides of the canvas, knitted from the edge of two or three columns in the last loop.

2. From top to bottom (photo 2). You must immediately determine the desired width of the canvas. In the process, each row is not tied to several columns and smoothly descends according to the figure. So, the resulting scarf for the girl (crochet) gradually tapers to a minimum.





3. The expansion of the canvas "fan". The principle of obtaining a uniform and evenly expandable scarf is to add loops in the middle of the knit. As a result, the triangular canvas has a central lace strip. Such a crocheted knit scarf for a girl most closely matches the required proportions and shape.





4. Assembly of small lace ornaments (photo 3). This method requires certain skills, experience and mastery. Small pieces in the form of a square, circle or hexagon are knotted. Then a canvas is formed from them according to the principle of reducing the parts in each row: six ornaments, five, four, three, two, one. After that, they must be carefully sewn in places of contact with each other or knit, folding together the sides of the square blanks.

5. One-way expansion. The right edge is made straight, while the left looks like steps. Such a crocheted knit scarf for a girl expands only on one side at an angle of forty-five degrees. As a result, the lateral edge will be the base located on the forehead, and the teeth obtained during the addition process will be the original decoration. If desired, the entire product can be tied with a lace border in a circle. In order not to make a mistake with the size, it is better to pre-make a pattern. Having knitted the first two or three rows, you can calculate the required number of loops for the increase, so that the canvas turns out to be of the appropriate shape and without distortions.

What is necessary first of all for crocheting a kerchief for girls? Schemes of the picture and pattern of the future product must always be at hand. Performing work, you can also try on the workpiece directly for the child and adjust the size if necessary. After gaining experience when performing simple viscous headscarves, try also more complex models. Please little fashionistas with beautiful handmade products!
