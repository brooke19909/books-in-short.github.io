How to disconnect the Internet on "Android": instruction

Access to the Internet gives us a lot of opportunities, but in some cases you need to disable it on your mobile gadget. Here we are talking about traffic restrictions on the selected tariff plan, roaming, etc. How do I turn off the Internet on Android? What do I need to do?

If you turn off the Internet on your Android device, you can not only avoid unnecessary financial expenses, but also save the lion's share of the battery charge, thereby increasing the battery life of your smartphone or tablet. But some users have problems working with this functionality.

So, from this article you will learn how to disable the mobile Internet on Android, and make it as painless as possible for the user, and for a smartphone or tablet. Consider the main methods and steps of this seemingly simple procedure.

Pop-up menu

You can turn off the Internet on Android in the simplest way - using the top panel, or, as it is also called, curtains. This method is suitable for almost all firmware on platforms of version 4.2.x. and higher. On younger versions, this method, alas, does not work.

To disconnect the Internet on Android, you must do the following:

  1. Call the curtain by pulling the top of the screen down.
  2. Click on the Mobile Internet icon.
  3. In the list that appears, find the "SIM Cards and Mobile Networks" section.
  4. In the line "Mobile Internet", move the slider to the "Off" position.
