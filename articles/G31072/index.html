Rooms

To accommodate guests in Splashworld Venus Beach 4 * (Tunisia, Hammamet) there are two buildings with elevators - Beach and Garden, which have 340 rooms. Room categories are as follows:

Leisure

A lot of entertainment for all tastes is offered in Splashworld Venus Beach 4 *. Sea

The bulk of people for the sake of a chic coast and beach vacation travels to Splashworld Venus Beach 4 *. Tunisia / Hammamet have very good beaches, both owned by hotels and municipal ones - wide, sandy, with smooth entry into the water, well equipped. This is the property of the hotel in question. Walk to him from the buildings a couple of minutes through a beautiful shady area. On the beach there is a fenced area with sunbeds and umbrellas, which do not allow outsiders. That is, neither merchants nor barkers in salons and excursions interfere with rest there. Since Hammamet is located in the bay, the sea here is warm most of the year, and the waves are small. You can swim in open water from the end of April until the very last days of October, and you can sunbathe in sunny weather even in winter. The minimum air temperature here is +15 ¬∞ C during the day, but evenings in the winter are quite cool (+7 ... +8 ¬∞ C), so you need to bring woolen clothes with you.

Those who like to relax actively on the beach can go kiting, windsurfing, parasailing, ride a banana, kayak or catamaran, scuba dive to the depths and look at the underwater inhabitants.

The only minus of the beach is the Arabs, conducting along the sand of camels. They offer a ride on an exotic animal or take a picture with it. There is nothing wrong with that, but camels leave behind feces that no one removes.

Entertainment outside the hotel

Some tourists go to Hotel Splashworld Venus Beach 4 * (Hammamet) to make successful purchases and explore Africa. Tunisia is a very original and interesting country. Here the traditions of the ancestors are carefully cherished, ancient crafts are not forgotten. If we talk about excellent shopping, it can be done without leaving Hammamet. In the city, which is famous for linen fabrics, there are two markets (medins) - old and new, many boutiques and supermarkets. For unique pottery it is advisable to go to Nabeul, which is no more than 12 km away, and to buy exclusive silk it is better to go to Mahdia, although it is somewhat far away, as much as 107 km.



There are no attractions in Hammamet as such. You can see here only at the villa of a billionaire from Romania, built in the 20th century. But in the city there is a good amusement park, to which a taxi costs only 5 dinars. For an unforgettable experience, you can go on an excursion to the Sahara for 2 days. Animal lovers are happy to buy tours to the zoo, where many animals live outside their enclosures. You can feed them for money, as well as iron and arrange unique photo sessions with them. In addition to animals, in the zoo you can watch shows and performances of dancers, but they are held only at certain times.
