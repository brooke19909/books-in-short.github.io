Features of the all-inclusive system

It's time to talk about the nuances of "All inclusive", which distinguishes all the resorts of Tunisia. All inclusive in this country is more like a full board. Meals - there are four in the "five", in hotels with a lower status of three - are held according to a clearly established schedule. Soft drinks in restaurants and bars are offered free of charge, but for alcohol they may require money. In the "five" all the quirks of the client are included. But the rule does not make drunk drunk: time between orders (5-10 minutes). The bracelet has a certain bar code, which allows the bartender to navigate while serving customers. You can freely use sunbeds and umbrellas, participate in water aerobics classes and other entertainments of animators, take your child to a mini club.
