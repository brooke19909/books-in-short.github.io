The main components of the gaming process

To create your game, you need a clear understanding of what you need to be able to.





The game-building process can be divided into two large parts - technical and creative. They are quite different, but if your plans do not include the creation of a many-hour masterpiece with unique capabilities, then you will be able to cope on your own. So, the technical part of the computer game development process includes writing code, developing artificial intelligence, drawing graphics, designing three-dimensional models, and so on. This half of the process is suitable for those who have a mathematical mindset - knowledge of programming, mathematics and other exact sciences will come in handy. As for the second half of the process, it is no less needed if you want to create your own game on a computer and want it to turn out to be of high quality and unique. The creative process includes the creation of the entire substantive part of the game, that is, the game world, characters of characters, objects, locations, battle systems, development, finances, as well as the voice acting of characters and the creation of a soundtrack.

The easiest way to create games





There are various approaches to the development of games, but it is best to single out three main ones, among which, first of all, it is worth considering the simplest one. It should be used if you are thinking about how to create your own game, but you have absolutely no programming knowledge. To resort to this method, you will need to download a special game constructor - a program that already includes the basic functions of creating games. One of the most famous designers is Game Maker, which is quite easy to learn and has a decent set of functions. As mentioned above, the main advantage of this method of creating games is its simplicity - you only need to bring your ideas to life through the opportunities provided by the program. But the main minus here lies precisely in these very possibilities, or rather, in their limitations. In fact, using the designer program, you can create a template game by selecting functions for it from the general list. If you want to bring a little personality to the game, add a twist, your own special chip, then you will not succeed.

Create a unique game

But there is also a third method, which is the most difficult, but gives the most impressive result. It differs from the second only in that you do not have a ready-made engine, that is, the basics, the base of the game - you create this engine yourself. This means that in your game everything will happen and look exactly the way you programmed - to the smallest detail.

You will have to write the game literally from scratch, and in this case you definitely need a team of specialists so that everyone is responsible for their field: graphics, physics, programming, plot and so on.

A new industry branch - mobile games

If you want to try yourself with something simpler and less global, then an ideal option for you would be to try to create your own game for android, since mobile games have recently become no less popular than computer games, but at the same time require much less time and resources. You just need to know the java language and understand the principles of creating mobile applications.
