Tunisia, Thalassa Shalimar 4 *: photos, prices, description and reviews

There are many resorts on its coast, and among them is the respectable and slightly conservative Hammamet. However, this does not mean that there are no discos, clubs, bars and other entertainment venues. Just plenty, just the contingent of vacationers is distinguished by a certain stiffness. general description

Hotel Thalassa Shalimar 4 * was founded at the very end of the 20th century, in 1999. Since it did not correspond to today's concepts of comfort, it had to be closed for some time and subjected to complex reconstruction, and already in 2005 it reopened its hospitable doors before visitors and appeared in an updated and more modern form. Despite the fact that it is on the second coastline, it has its own 3,000-meter-long beach, where all amenities (sunbeds, umbrellas, mattresses) are provided to tourists free of charge, and this is a big plus for vacationers. Le Shalimar Ex Thalassa 4 * in its structure is a hotel complex, which consists of the main building - a three-story building with beautiful architectural solutions - and more than 50 one-story bungalows in the traditional Andalusian style. Naturally, the infrastructure includes restaurants, cafes, markets and kiosks, and much more. The complex has several pools at once - both for children and adults.

Location

As already noted, Shalimar Ex Thalassa Shalimar 4 * is located in the south of the Tunisian resort of Hammamet. From the international airport located in Infida, the complex can be reached in about half an hour, since there is a distance of 35 km between them. The airport that is more known to us, in the city of Monastir, is 90 km away, but from the center of Hammamet resort to the complex is only 9 km. Tourists from Russia, and from other countries, mainly fly to this same Infida airport, and from there they get by car to their destination. You can take a taxi, take a bus or minibus, you can even rent a car before arriving in Tunisia. Nevertheless, the best option is to pay for a transfer from the airport to the hotel, and then you will not need to delve into the road map in search of the desired address. This can be agreed both with the tour operator and with the hotel itself. Almost all hotels have this service. Near the complex there are spacious golf courses - Citrus and Yasmin, as well as a water park.

This hotel belongs to the prestigious Thalassa hotel chain in Tunisia, therefore, the interior design and service are consistent with the general concept of this brand. Infrastructure and service

Thalassa Shalimar 4 * (Hammamet / Tunisia) is suitable not only for relaxation, but also for conferences, seminars and symposiums. For these purposes, it has a fairly spacious, for 300 people, conference room, business center with the necessary equipment (printer, copier, scanner, several computers with free wireless Internet, etc.). The hotel also has one indoor and one outdoor spacious swimming pools for adults, as well as a children's pool with slides. Sun loungers with umbrellas located around the ponds are provided free of charge for guests, the same applies to towels, but air mattresses, which are very popular among sunbathers, swaying gently on the water, have a rental fee. The hotel complex Thalassa Shalimar 4 * (photo can be seen in the article) also has spa, fitness, thalasso, ayurvedic centers and a beauty salon, Turkish hammam, sauna, massage and medical rooms, currency exchange, tennis courts, laundry and ironing, lobby, car rental, restaurants, cafes, as well as a banquet hall, souvenir kiosk, etc. Guests can store their valuables in a safe at the reception.
