Each craftswoman involved in knitting, always drew attention to the airiness and weightlessness of clothing associated with openwork patterns. Using this technique, you can knit a beautiful cardigan or wrap, a fantastic shawl or stole. And you can choose a lace for a light summer dress or a thin top. In this article we will try to understand the secrets of the technique of openwork knitting and make the complex more understandable.

The principles of openwork knitting

Despite the apparent confusion of the patterns of openwork patterns with knitting needles, the knitting technique is not at all complicated. Of course, before starting work, you will need to understand the scheme, understand what the symbols mean, and try to make a control sample of the pattern you like. A control sample of the pattern is needed to correctly calculate the number of loops for the details of the product, because the density of knitting is different for each master.

In the technique of knitting openwork patterns with knitting needles, a pattern is created in the front rows of the canvas, the loops of the wrong rows are knitted by default in the pattern. The airy mesh structure of openwork patterns is manifested through the use of yarn. They increase the number of loops of the canvas, so their number is regulated by knitting two front loops together. Depending on the pattern, the faces are knitted together with a slope to the left or right (this will be indicated in the description of the pattern).

The simpler, the better.

For starters, you can try to master knitting simple lace patterns with knitting needles.

The setochka lace is considered to be the most uncomplicated one: its rapport is calculated only on two loops and is 4 rows in height.

The pattern is obtained through the alternation of two facial loops, knitted together and yarn. This combination of loops is repeated until the end of the first row. In the next front row, the order of the loops changes: the yarn is knitted over the front and vice versa. In the wrong rows, knit all the loops on the wrong side. Such a simple pattern looks good when knitting a long stole or shawl, you can use it for a light blouse.

Patterns of openwork patterns

Along with fairly simple, there are many complex lace patterns, the description of which would take several pages. Therefore, such patterns are deciphered in the form of knitting patterns. Each outline icon indicates a specific loop or loop action. These icons are universal, having learned to understand their meaning, it will be possible to decipher the scheme, the description of which is given in an unfamiliar language.

Knitting openwork patterns with knitting needles with a description given in the form of a pattern will require extreme attention and constant control of the number of loops. It may seem a little complicated for a beginner, but the result is worth it. Having mastered the technique of knitting openwork with knitting needles, you can create completely unique things!

The nuances of knitting openwork

When working with openwork patterns, it is better to use thin threads with a smooth texture. If you plan a warm, gentle thing, it is better to choose a thin mohair with a content of natural fibers of at least 80%. For thin summer models, you can use cotton or linen threads. It is better not to use all natural yarn for knitting things with an openwork pattern. She has the feature of being slightly stretched, and this can deform the finished product.

Please note that knitting openwork patterns is better to combine with small-diameter knitting needles, while the loops are more even and neat.
