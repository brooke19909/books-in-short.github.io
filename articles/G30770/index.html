Repairs

Despite the fact that the described device for self-defense looks very high quality, it may stop working over time. This happens from a quick discharge of the battery or from falls. How to disassemble a shocker flashlight and restore its former life? At first glance, it seems that solving this issue is not an easy task. After all, the case of the device looks cast. But disassembling a flashlight-shocker, according to users, is within the power of everyone. And you should start here by removing the tape protecting the mounting screws. Next we need a screwdriver. It is easy to unscrew the four mounting screws located on the housing. After that, we separate the back of the device, in which, in addition to the elements of the charger, a piece of foam is laid for greater density.

Next, the front part is removed. She, like the back, should be pulled to the side. In front of the flashlight-shocker are high-voltage conductors. According to them, electric current goes to the front arrester.

Dismantling the shocker flashlight allows you to determine the cause of its malfunction. After its elimination, the device will start working again.
