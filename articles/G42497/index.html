Adalin Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer, Kemer Center): hotel description and photos

Kemer is an amazing place. On land it is surrounded by impregnable mountains. On the other hand, it is surrounded by the azure waters of the Mediterranean Sea. The region includes the municipality of the same name, the regional centers of Kirish, Beldibi, Tekirova, Goynuk, Camyuva.





Tropical paradise

This part of the Turkish coast is especially popular with Russians. A huge number of premium hotels are concentrated here. Adalin Resort Hotel 4 * tops the list of the most popular hotels. It offers all-inclusive service. Many in Kemer speak Russian.

The main pedestrian and transport artery of the resort is Liman Street. It houses trade enterprises, pastry shops, restaurants and night clubs. Local shops sell souvenirs, clothing, swimming equipment and beach accessories. Here are the best discos "Aura", "Buddha", "Inferno".





Transport accessibility

Adalin Resort Hotel 4 * is 40 kilometers away from Antalya. The journey from the international airport, whose runways serve international and local flights, takes about fifty minutes. Usually a group transfer is included in a tour package. Otherwise, the hotel can be reached on their own.

A taxi ride will cost 650 rubles. Ferries run to Kemer from Antalya. They set sail from the city pier twice a day. Tariff - 160 rubles. Travel time is 45 minutes. You can get to Adalin Resort Hotel 4 * by a comfortable bus. A ticket costs about 150 rubles. Those who rented a car need to keep on the D400.

The cheapest way to get around the streets of Kemer is to take a minibus. The price is 30 rubles. Cars of official resort taxi parks are painted in bright yellow color. They expect their customers near major entertainment centers, close to stops and at special venues.
