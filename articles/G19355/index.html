Car alarm "Starline E90": installation and reviews

The alarm complex was released in 2013, to this day it does not lose relevance in terms of its technical, operational and functional qualities. The model relates to telematic security systems, which distinguishes it from most of the domestic developments. Moreover, even against the background of full-fledged car alarms with feedback, the Starline E90 kit looks more attractive thanks to the intelligent autostart function, CAN interface and Super Slave option.





General installation requirements

Installation of the alarm system is allowed only in vehicles with an on-board voltage of 12 V. Before installation, check the electrical equipment in the cabin, the quality of the wiring, the presence of insulating shells and the performance of the battery pack. If it is planned to introduce new electrical circuits, then it is recommended to do this with the maximum allowable distance from devices that could potentially become sources of electrical noise. The same applies to heating equipment. Thermal and electromagnetic radiation negatively affects the functions of the Starline E90 system. The instruction also indicates the risks of laying new networks in the areas of operation of the mechanical organs of the car. Wires should be laid so that they do not come into contact with the pedals, steering pad, and gear knob. In case of future integration of additional control relays, consider diode bypass.





Placement of Starline E90 components

The main functional elements of this car alarm package include a central unit, sensors, a signal receiver and a siren. The control unit is mounted in the instrument panel of the machine. It is necessary to determine in advance the place in which it will be possible to fix the box using profile metal clips. It should also be borne in mind that the correct operation of this component is possible only in the absence of strong vibrations and direct exposure to electromagnetic radiation. In order to minimize vibrations, it is advisable to perform the Starline E90 installation using damping material. For example, soft linings for mounting profiles will reduce the effects of vibrations during the movement of the car.

As for the sensors, they are located at security points - in the areas of windows and doors. The temperature sensor can be located anywhere, but so that it is not affected by the warm currents of the heating systems. Next, you can proceed to the placement of the Starline E90 antenna, which will be responsible for exchanging signals with the user. The optimal place to install the receiver is near the windshield, but as high as possible. For fixing the complete equipment is used. Alarm siren is placed in the engine compartment. To prevent its gulf water construction is located with a mouthpiece down.





Power Connection

The first thing to do is connect the black ground wire. It is fixed in the corresponding relay socket using the supplied nut. The instruction prohibits the use of self-tapping screws to fasten the wire to metal parts of the body. At the same time, there should be no plastic elements between the terminals and fasteners, otherwise there will be a risk of interruption of the contact. When placing the wire in the engine compartment, it should be further treated with anti-corrosion agents. After that, you can proceed to connecting ‚ÄúStarline E90‚Äù to the 12 V circuit. For this, red wires are used, one of which will be connected to the relay module. Non-standard wires can also be used - the main thing is that they have a cross section of at least 6 mm2. The connection is made directly to the battery.





CAN Bus Organization

The ability to use this interface may not be implemented in every car. Therefore, it is initially worth preparing for the use of alternative connection of the terminal outputs through the blue and green complete wires. In the future, the Starline E90 keychain will enable communication with the security nodes of the system through the main relay. As for the CAN module, it is introduced into the infrastructure with two or one cable - again, depending on the capabilities of a particular car. Connection is made to the central unit on the one hand, and on the other hand, limit switches from doors, hood, windows and other security points are connected to the bus through a relay.

Installation complete

After completing the installation operations and connecting the main functional blocks, you should immediately check the sound and light indication. An alarm must necessarily be accompanied by the operation of optics. Then, the operability of the Starline E90 security mode is checked. The instruction indicates that at basic settings, an alarm should occur if the trunk, hood and doors open. Depending on the settings of the limit switches, the alarm can be activated when the ignition is turned on, the brake is turned off and the pedals are depressed. In addition to checking the functions of the installed system, it is again necessary to revise the general condition of the on-board network with electrical equipment.





Positive feedback about the system

Although this complex at the given time cannot be called innovative, the combination of a wide range of modern functions is its main advantage, judging by the reviews of many owners. They note the autostart system, and the presence of effective sensors, as well as the ability to control via GSM channel and other telematic options. It would seem that with such a load, the system should become a serious burden for the battery. But users of Starline E90 car alarms include energy efficiency in the list of its advantages. In terms of mechanical reliability, the kit is assessed neutrally, but also does not cause tangible criticism.

Negative reviews

Nevertheless, the multifunctionality of the device did not allow developers to fully eliminate certain conflicts in the electrical filling. First of all, some alarm users note the instability of the autostart function, which is blocked by the immobilizer. To eliminate errors in the operation of this component, you have to install additional blocker crawlers. There are also reviews of Starline E90, which criticize the quality of signal reception. According to official data, radio reception should be carried out in radii of 800 and 2000 m, respectively, for the exchange of control signals and alerts. But in both cases, real ranges are limited to shorter distances. The modern design of the device and its compactness cannot be attributed to disadvantages.





Finally

This alarm package is the optimal representative of the middle class telematic security systems for the price and functional content. If you need a complex that has advanced technological tools for protecting a car, it is better to find this solution on the market is not easy. On the other hand, the Starline E90 car alarm requires additional adjustment. This increases the cost of the security system, can deliver certain troubles during operation. Therefore, they recommend this package only to users who are familiar with the nuances of the work of automotive electrical engineering, the use of telematics devices.
