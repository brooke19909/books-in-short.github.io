Jewel Sahara Boutique Resort 4 *, Hurghada, Egypt: hotel description, reviews

Jewel Sahara Boutique Resort 4 * is a chic boutique hotel, which is located in one of the most picturesque places of Hurghada.





Hotel Description

The Jewel Sahara Boutique Resort 4 * resort complex is distinguished by luxury not only in architecture and exterior decoration, but also in everything related to service. Guests are invited to stay in chic rooms, the level of comfort in which is comparable to home. An important point is the presence in the apartments of furnished cozy balconies with a unique view.

Jewel Sahara Boutique Resort 4 * has its own sandy beach, on which there are always a sufficient amount of comfortable sun loungers and parasols. Palm gardens are a special pride of the hotel. Also, you will certainly be surprised by the skill of the chefs working in hotel restaurants.

Also, the general atmosphere of carefree fun is supported by bars, where there is always a wide variety of drinks, exotic cocktails and light gourmet snacks.





Hotel Location

Amid the desert landscapes, the chic Jewels Sahara Boutique Resort is located. On the map of Hurghada, it occupies the first coastline relative to the Red Sea. It is located right on the sandy beach. Hurghada International Airport is just a 10-minute drive from here.





Service and facilities for guests

High quality service is distinguished by Jewels Sahara Boutique Resort. Tours to this hotel include free use of the following services:

  • all-inclusive meals, which include breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main restaurant, as well as snacks and drinks throughout the day;
  • maintenance of your rooms, which includes cleaning, replacement of bath accessories and bed linen, as well as solving problems and troubleshooting;
  • the use of sun loungers and umbrellas to protect from the sun both in the area near the pool and on the private hotel beach;
  • use of the service "alarm clock", which consists in the fact that the hotel staff will wake you at the right time.

The hotel also has a number of services that are available only at an additional cost:

  • use of car parking with round-the-clock security by prior arrangement;
  • renting a safe deposit box at the administration or at the reception;
  • room service;
  • filling the minibar;
  • currency exchange operations, as well as withdrawing money from a plastic card (commission fee is charged, as well as bank interest);
  • laundry and dry cleaning services;
  • tour desk services for organizing and conducting excursions;
  • use of the wireless Internet;
  • calling a doctor to the hotel or directly to the room.




Guest rooms

One of the most important moments of any holiday is a beautiful and comfortable room. So, the most numerous category of Jewels Sahara Boutique Resort apartments is standard. This room is a 26 square meter bedroom. m. There is a large bed for 2 people, as well as the possibility of placing an additional two cots. A must-have attribute is the bathroom, as well as a compact balcony for cozy gatherings and drying your belongings.

Superior apartments are approximately one and a half times larger than standard apartments. However, they are also one-room. There is more free space, as well as an order of magnitude more pieces of furniture, which helps to create an atmosphere of home coziness and comfort. Also a pleasant moment is the presence of a large balcony with wicker chairs.

Family rooms are ideal for those travelers who are used to relaxing with their loved ones, as well as for large and friendly companies. There is a bedroom for two people. Another 2-3 guests can stay in the living room, on a soft sofa and folding beds. The apartments have a bathroom and a balcony.

The interior of Jewel Sahara Boutique Resort Apartments is in a concise but at the same time refined style. Photos of tourists demonstrate the graceful and elegant decor of the rooms.





Room amenities and equipment

The apartments of the hotel complex Jewel Sahara Boutique Resort 4 * for your comfort include the following amenities:

  • the air conditioner operates in accordance with the general regime set in the hotel (this will protect you from unhealthy temperature changes);
  • the bathroom is equipped with a modern shower, in which, among other possibilities, a hydromassage is also provided;
  • a modern widescreen TV is connected to a satellite dish, which broadcasts several news and entertainment Russian-language channels;
  • the room has a radio, which is configured to broadcast not only local but also international stations;
  • the phone is designed for round-the-clock communication with the reception, as well as for using the international line;
  • the room has a minibar, which is filled for a fee;
  • thanks to the balcony or terrace, you can enjoy the wonderful Egyptian scenery without leaving your room.

Entertainment

Speaking about the entertainment in this hotel, the following features can be noted:

  • The sauna is in great demand, which is useful not only for health, but also allows you to completely relax;
  • On the basis of the hotel there is a modern fitness center, which contains the most modern fitness equipment, as well as aerobics groups;
  • in the courtyard of the hotel there is a large swimming pool with fresh water, the purity and sterility of which is ensured by chlorine and other similar reagents;
  • Be sure to use the services of local massage therapists, who own both quite traditional and exotic techniques;
  • animators regularly conduct master classes on belly dancing and other popular areas;
  • You can go diving, after passing through qualified training and instruction;
  • boat trips are organized several times a week for hotel guests, as well as the opportunity to go fishing on the high seas;
  • You can spend time at such popular sports games as basketball, beach volleyball, as well as table tennis, badminton.

Positive reviews

A lot of pleasant impressions and memories leaves the Jewels Sahara Boutique Resort 4 * hotel. Guest reviews reflect the whole gamut of positive impressions:

  • a very beautiful area, on which there is a lot of greenery, as well as all kinds of decorative elements;
  • good nutrition, since the menu contains dishes for absolutely every taste;
  • animators hold many contests with good prizes (discounts on services or even free accommodation);

  • the gym is very hot, because there is no air conditioning, and there is only an old fan.
