Topiary is a do-it-yourself tree of happiness

Topiary is called the skillfully trimmed tree crowns, which adorn gardens and parks. Such a decorative haircut is especially popular in Western Europe, and it came into fashion back in the 18th century. Slender rows of perfectly even shrubs, balls, squares, rhombs stretched along the alleys and created labyrinths in regular parks near palaces and castles. Currently, topiary is a decorative artificial tree, which is made from a wide variety of materials for decorating the interior. Moreover, the shapes, sizes and appearance of these works of floristic art are very diverse, it all depends on the skill and creative ideas of their creator.

Topiary is not an imitation of a living tree, not a small copy of it, it is a completely independent product in its concept, which sometimes has the most fantastic, bizarre shapes. To make its crown, natural materials are often used: dried fruits and large seeds, flowers, cones, leaves, shells, various spices that the housewives use in cooking. Artificial trees collected from fragrant plants are not only an ornament, but also aromatize the room, exuding a light pleasant smell. In addition to natural ones, artificial materials are often used in the manufacture of such a tree: flowers and twigs, glass balls and beads, twisted cord, satin ribbon, etc.

Topiary from leaves and flowers can be made from natural dried material (autumn leaves collected in the garden, immortelle flowers, which retain their shape and color for a long time). As the basis for the crown, take a round ball made of a floral sponge, foam rubber, nylon stocking stuffed with cotton wool, a children's toy is also suitable - a small plastic ball.

This base is planted on a stem, which is a tree branch, planed and painted in the desired color, fixed in a pot using plaster of water divorced in water . The crown base for the crown is pasted over with leaves and flowers, starting from the top, in a circle and up to the stem itself or (if it is made of a floral sponge or foam rubber) simply stick short short stalks of prepared dried plants into it. If you decide to work with glue, then attach the elements in a certain way. We smear only the lower part of the sheet with a sticky composition. Then, after gluing, its top will lag behind the surface of the base, and the crown will turn out more fluffy and natural. The leaves should be applied a little overlap, one on top of the other so that there are no gaps between them. If you just stick the petioles in a sponge, also do it tightly without leaving voids. After the crown-crown is completely covered with leaves and flowers, the composition is decorated, making it perfect, using satin ribbons, beads, etc. The surface of the pot with hardened gypsum is covered with colored pebbles, sisal threads, shells or dried moss. To topiary served as a decoration of the room for a long time, it is covered on top with a special Dried Flower Spray or regular hair spray.
