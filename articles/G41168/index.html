How to tie a scarf on your head is fashionable and beautiful?

Scarves - a favorite decoration of women for a long time. It was invented as a headdress several hundred years ago and was used for practical purposes - to protect it from the sun, wind and cold. Today, a scarf is the most favorite women's accessory. Having tied a beautiful piece of cloth on your head or neck, you can create an elegant and unique image.





Traditions of tying headscarves

Different countries have their own customs related to how to tie scarves on the head. As a rule, they start from religious or national traditions. For example, married women wore handkerchiefs in Russia, and at the entrance to the church they were women of any age. In Islam, girls who have reached puberty are required to wear a scarf. Legislators of world fashion, French, do not part with this charming accessory all year round. They are sure that even in the sultry summer, an elegant scarf is able to give the female look a special charm and elegance, and as a result - to attract the eyes of men. And indeed, today, when you can find a scarf of any shade and texture on sale, a woman who knows how to tie scarves on her head always looks the brightest and most attractive. Satin, silk, chiffon and woolen pieces of seductive material can transform the image of any fashionista in summer and winter.





How to tie a scarf on my head?

The classic version. It is very fond of being used by Hollywood beauties. The scarf should be folded diagonally. Throw the resulting triangle over the head so that the bangs remain visible. It remains to tie the ends of the scarf in a bundle under the chin - and you will look very elegant and cute.

Peasant option. The most practical way to tie scarves. It is very easy to become mysterious and elegant by folding a piece of multi-colored matter in the shape of a triangle, putting it on your head, covering your forehead, and tying the ends under your hair.

Pirate version. Great for boat trips, protecting your hair from the scorching sun and strong wind. To understand how to tie a scarf on your head this way, you should remember the famous film with Johnny Depp. As in the previous version, you need to fold a triangle of matter, put a scarf on your head, only tie a knot not under your hair, but over your head, in the manner of a regular bandana.

Fashionable option. One of the easiest and most beautiful ways to create a hippie look. The scarf must be folded diagonally, and then bent it several times, so that the result is a strip. If you know how to tie a scarf around your neck beautifully , then you can easily do this procedure. The bandage remains to be wrapped around the head, and its ends tied behind under the hair. A headscarf tied over the hairstyle will also look stylish.

Gypsy version. An original way to tie a scarf. To understand how to tie a scarf on your head, remember the pirated version. A piece of matter also folds into a triangle, is put on the head, covering the forehead, and the knot is tied over the scarf, only on the side. The ends are hidden under the material.
