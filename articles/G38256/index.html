It is noteworthy that some of our compatriots are so in love with Turkish resorts that they try to visit them twice, or even thrice, in the summer season. If you classify yourself as such travelers, you have probably heard of the Horus Paradise Luxury Resort & Club 5 * hotel. This luxurious complex can suit any category of tourists, therefore it is very popular among Russians and guests from Europe. Hotel Overview

Horus Paradise Luxury Resort & Club 5 * was built about nine years ago, but after the opening it had a different name and only after three years of operation changed it. Sometimes tourists can see the complex in advertising catalogs called Side Holiday Village, so do not be alarmed - this is the very luxurious hotel that is well known throughout the Turkish coast.

It is noteworthy that the hotel complex consists of several buildings. They were built at different times, which is noticeable in the design and deterioration of the number of rooms. The main building has seven floors and was commissioned the very first. It contains one hundred and thirty apartments. Renovation in this building was carried out three years ago.

A year ago, two five-story buildings were built. There are twelve new cottage-type buildings in the garden and by the sea. They have two and three floors, all apartments were updated about a year ago. Almost all hotel guests note that the number of rooms is in excellent condition and is fully consistent with the stated five stars.

Hotel Location

Located Horus Paradise Luxury Resort & Club 5 * in Side. This resort is well known to our compatriots and very loved by them. Guests write that the hotel is close to attractions, historical monuments and the main infrastructure of Side. Tourists can easily go to the ruins of the ancient city, the canyon or visit the Manavgat Falls. All this can be done even without travel agencies, which significantly saves travelers.

Nearby there are public transport and taxis. Buses run at intervals of no more than ten minutes, so any tourist, if desired, will be very quickly in the center of Side.

From Horus Paradise Luxury Resort & Club 5 * guests often go to noisy Turkish bazaars. They take place twice a week and attract a lot of attention.

The international airport is located far enough from the hotel. Upon arrival in Antalya, our compatriots have to get to the hotel complex for several hours, because they are separated by a distance of seventy kilometers.

Between Horus Paradise Luxury Resort & Club 5 * and the beach there is a promenade. To reach the sea, vacationers need to walk about two hundred meters.

Coast

The beach of Horus Paradise Luxury Resort & Club 5 * is quite beautiful and well equipped. However, keep in mind that it is rented, since almost the entire coast in the Side area is municipal. Guests can use the sunbeds and umbrellas for free, but all the beach infrastructure facilities are located on the territory of the hotel complex.

Take a shower, change clothes or refresh yourself with a delicious drink in the bar, you can only cross the road. This brings some vacationers inconvenience, especially families with small children who are not happy with such circumstances. They prefer to spend time at the pools.

The beach itself is a sandy strip that has a length of five hundred meters. In reviews of Horus Paradise Luxury Resort & Club 5 *, guests often write that the coast is not always clean. The biggest discomfort is brought by the numerous cigarette butts, which the kids constantly fish out of the sand. The entrance to the water is gentle, almost without stones. Only in some places are boulders and ravines found. They form mainly after a storm.

Beach towels at the hotel are secured at the hotel complex. You can change them once every three days.

Schedule of bars and restaurants

Dining at Horus Paradise Luxury Resort & Club 5 * is organized beyond praise. Guests constantly write enthusiastic reviews about him, and this is not surprising, because guests are subject to the ultra all-inclusive system. At the same time, it is simply impossible to remain indifferent to the abundance of goodies. It is noteworthy that this concept includes drinks not only local but also foreign production.

The main restaurant operates according to the following schedule:

  • breakfast lasts from seven to ten in the morning;
  • immediately after it begins the brunch, which ends at eleven in the morning;
  • you can come to dinner at half past one, at two in the afternoon the restaurant is already closing its doors;
  • dinner runs from seven to nine in the evening.

Keep in mind that there is a bistro by the pool. Late dinner is usually served here , you can come to it in the interval from eleven in the evening until one in the morning. If you need to leave early on a tour, then an early breakfast will be available for you. It lasts until six in the morning.

Dinner at these establishments begins at seven in the evening and lasts for two hours. You can visit all restaurants except Turkish and fish one time for free. Keep in mind that you can only get there by appointment.

Features of accommodation

The hotel has connecting rooms. They are chosen by large families who cannot accommodate in ordinary apartments.
