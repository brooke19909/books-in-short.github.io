Hotels in Irkutsk: overview, addresses, description

If you plan to visit Irkutsk in the near future, then you will certainly have a question where to settle in the city. It doesn't matter what the purpose of your trip is. The main thing is to choose the right hotel, which will be convenient for you in every sense. The choice of hotels in the city is quite large. In our article we want to present you a list of Irkutsk hotels, which, in our opinion, are worthy of the attention of guests. This will help you make the right choice.

"Merchant yard"

If you want something special, then in Irkutsk you will definitely find it. We suggest you start by choosing a hotel. Modern complexes have long become boring to avid travelers, so you should choose something unusual. For example, you can rent a room at the Kupechesky Dvor Hotel. The hotel is a unique reconstruction of a Siberian estate of a historical nature. Once this stunning home was a hotel for wealthy merchants who came to the city on business. Among the hotels of Irkutsk, it is the most interesting and unusual. The complex is located in the 130 quarter of the city. Hotel address: Sedova street, house 10. The advantage of the institution is that it recreates the view of the stunning Siberian wooden city of the era of the eighteenth to nineteenth centuries. We can say that the hotel is a kind of bridge between the past and the present.

According to guest reviews, the interior of the hotel corresponds to the decoration of a bygone era. But at the same time, comfortable rooms meet all standard requirements. The hotel administration offers its guests comfortable apartments, wireless Internet, delicious breakfasts, wide beds and comfortable bathrooms. Excellent service and unobtrusive service help to make the hotel feel at home.

The advantage of the hotel is that it is located in the historical center, which is also an entertainment and cultural center. Near the hotel you will find a large selection of cafes, restaurants, shops, souvenir shops and other institutions.

According to tourists, this hotel in Irkutsk is attractive in many ways. It is conveniently located and has a unique atmosphere. The hotel staff is polite, there are no problems. Positive reviews allow you to recommend the institution to guests.

"Europe"

If you are looking for a place where you will be comfortable living and working, the Hotel Europe in Irkutsk will be a great option for you. The institution is located in the cultural and historical center of the city at the address: Baikalskaya street, 69.

The institution offers its guests rooms of different levels of comfort: business, economy, standard, junior suite. The choice of suitable apartments is yours. Any room will allow you to relax and unwind in silence. Apartments of the Europe Hotel in Irkutsk are equipped with beautiful wooden furniture. The interior design is designed in the style of European classics. Each room has its own working area, which is useful to those guests who come to the city on business.

The hotel has a restaurant where you can not only have breakfast, but also order lunch, and also have a good evening.

According to tourists, among the hotels in Irkutsk, "Europe" is one of the leading places. Guests note that the hotel has friendly staff. The rooms are comfortable and convenient. Maroussia

If you are not a supporter of pathos and value comfort and coziness, you can choose the Marusya boutique hotel for your stay in Irkutsk. The institution is located in the city center at the address: Sedova Street, 12. The entire 130th quarter is decorated in the style of ancient Russian architecture of the eighteenth century. Forged parts, log facades, quiet side streets and a cozy atmosphere - all this is the historical center of the city.

The hotel takes guests to an old Russian hut. The whole building is eco-friendly. Linen curtains, lace napkins and bedspreads create a cozy homely atmosphere. The convenient location of the hotel allows you to reach the main attractions in a matter of minutes.

The hotel consists of only fourteen apartments, designed in a modern design. The rooms have televisions, hair dryers, bathtubs and toiletries. The hotel has a home-style cafe serving delicious food, as evidenced by guest reviews. If desired, tourists can visit the nearest restaurants and cafes, of which there are a lot. According to tourists, the hotel has a special charm. It is small but incredibly comfortable, which creates a homely atmosphere.

Irkutsk

Those who prefer to live in a modern building can recommend the Irkutsk Hotel. The nine-story complex is located directly on the embankment of the Angara River, in the historical center of the city. Hotel address: 44 Gagarina street. Walking distance to all the most significant sights of the city.

The hotel complex offers 163 comfortable rooms for its guests. All apartments, regardless of class, are equally comfortable and cozy. Hotel Irkutsk has an excellent infrastructure. The hotel has a restaurant serving European dishes, a cafe, a lobby bar. The hotel has everything you need to organize business events and significant events.

Positive reviews of tourists characterize the hotel from the best side. It is located in a quiet place, from the windows of the building offers panoramic views of the river and the surrounding area. The hotel has its own parking. If you are interested in inexpensive, but comfortable apartments, then the hotel will definitely suit you. In the restaurant, breakfast is served in buffet style.

"Ibis"

If you are interested in a hotel in the center of Irkutsk, Ibis may be one of the possible options. The hotel belongs to a well-known chain, in which there are more than two thousand complexes around the world. The hotel offers budget apartments with all amenities for a comfortable stay.

The hotel complex "Ibis Irkutsk Center" is located at: Polina Osipenko Street, 5. You can get to it from the airport in just fifteen minutes. According to tourists, the hotel is well worth the money spent on living in it. It fully corresponds to the three stars on the sign. Here you will find cable TV, soundproofed windows, a desk, ironing facilities, a fridge, a stove and a dishwasher.

Undoubtedly, the hotel has its advantages. It will appeal to thrifty tourists who value comfort and coziness. Attentive staff settles guests at any time of the day. Since the hotel is popular, it is recommended to book rooms in advance.
