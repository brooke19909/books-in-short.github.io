Guest house "Lavender" (Adler, ul. Izvestinskaya, 6): photos and reviews

The specificity of the southern coastal resorts is such that the hotels there often have the same name. The imagination of local hoteliers is not very rich. They call their hotels either “Wave”, “Sea”, “Sail”, or the name of some flower. And so it happened that there are a lot of hotels under the Lavender sign in Sochi. There is such a hotel on Alleynaya street, Uritsky, and Popov. And this is not Big Sochi, but only Adler! Guest house "Lavender" (ul. Izvestinskaya, 6) will be the topic of our article. This hotel has opened recently, and there are still few reviews. But the owners of “Lavender” used to keep the guest house “AiST” and won praise for their service and concern for the guests. This mini-hotel is working now. AiST is also located in the Kurortny Gorodok area, like Lavanda, only the other street is 74, Medovaya. You can go in and compare the services.



Adler, guest house "Lavender": where is it and how to get it

Resort Town is known in Sochi by all taxi drivers and private cab drivers. He stretched a long strip along the sea in Adler. From land, it is limited by Lenin Street. To get to the hotel we are interested in, it is not enough to tell the taxi driver “Kurortny Gorodok”, we need a clearer landmark. That is the sanatorium "Izvestia". It is located very close. Well, if you hire a taxi at Sochi airport, then it’s enough to tell the driver: “Adler, guest house“ Lavanda ”, address - Izvestinskaya street, six”. The location of the mini-hotel on the resort map is very advantageous. Sea - five hundred meters. It is enough to cross Lenin Street and go through the park area of ​​Kurortny Gorodok. Adler’s iconic sights such as the Aquarium and the Dolphinarium are within walking distance. The nearest grocery store is a hundred meters away, while a supermarket is two hundred. In the immediate vicinity of the mini-hotel there is a pharmacy and a budget dining room, cafe and restaurant. A bus stop is a hundred meters away. Reviews say that Izvestinskaya street is quiet. All noise-gum resort is located on the other side of the street. Lenin.



About the hotel: a brief description

Lavanda Guest House (Adler) was built in 2015. This is one building on five floors. Near the building there is a patio under constant observation, parking, pool. The territory of the guest house is small, but well equipped. Between the building and a high stone fence near the pool there is a row of sunbeds where you can sunbathe. There are also barbecue facilities and tables under umbrellas. The words "shared kitchen" cause nervous tremors in vacationers. But you should not imagine a queue for a single four-burner stove and a war for a free pan. Each room, even the simplest, has an electric kettle and a microwave. And on all floors of the building there is a common kitchen with a large set of kitchen utensils and a stove. So the rooms in the guest house "Lavender" can be described as apartments.



Room Categories

Both budget tourists and those who appreciate a comfortable stay can live here. The choice of tourists who come to Adler, the guest house "Lavender" (photo rooms correspond to reality) offers rooms of various categories. The lowest is a double standard with the possibility of accommodating a third guest. This room, with windows facing the patio, comes with a bathroom with shower and hairdryer. The bedroom has a small refrigerator, air conditioning, LCD TV, electric kettle. The bathroom is replenished daily with hygiene items. Triple standard has the same content, but it also adjoins a balcony. As we already mentioned, for cooking on each floor there is a kitchen. The rooms have Wi-Fi. Towels are cleaned and replaced every day. Clean linens are also made very often.



Apartments

For those who appreciate a very comfortable stay, the guest house "Lavender" (Adler) has prepared two-room luxury rooms. They are of two types. In the two-room suite, bathrobes, mosquito nets on the windows, a larger set of towels, and a balcony are attached to the usual room filling. Also, guests of the suites do not need to go to the floor to use the kitchen. Indeed, in their room there is a dishwasher, a cupboard with the necessary kitchen utensils and a set of tableware, as well as a microwave. There is also a duplex suite. The filling of the room is the same, but there is no balcony. All suites are designed for four, maximum five guests. Reviews of tourists claim that the photos of these apartments are fully true. The rooms are very stylishly decorated, staying in them immediately sets you up for a comfortable stay. The soundproofing in the rooms is good. There are no problems and interruptions with hot and cold water.



How can I eat

There is no organized food in the guest house. After all, the mini-hotel consists of apartments and provides guests with free access to the kitchen. Therefore, it is assumed that tourists will cook themselves. In the courtyard there is a barbecue, which can also be used for cooking barbecue and grilled dishes. If you are reluctant to stand behind the stove, go to one of the restaurants or cafes. There are many of them even on the street where the guest house "Lavender" (Adler, Izvestinskaya) stands. Reviews recommend Magnit. Buffet lovers will be satisfied if they drop by the Fir & Spruce Cafe. It works on the principle of "all food (except barbecue and soup) - sixty-five rubles per hundred grams." Many guests of the Lavanda guest house bought ready-made meals there, and then heated them in the microwave. In the courtyard there are tables under umbrellas, you can also bite in the fresh air.



Pool and sea

Everyone has heard about the pebble beaches of Adler , and we will not dwell on this point separately. Tourists looking at a map of the city may be confused by the fact that they have to cross the Lenin road. But to avoid an accident will help the pedestrian bridge over it. Reviews say that, unlike other resorts in Greater Sochi, the terrain in Adler is not very mountainous. A walk to the sea (ten minutes at a leisurely pace) will not be perceived as torment. If your stay on the beach seems too tiring or the sea is stormy, Lavanda Guest House (Adler) offers you free use of its convenient swimming pool. It adjoins a sunbathing terrace. The water in the pool, according to reviews, is clean, does not smell of bleach, the edges of the bathing area are finished with non-slip stone.



Services and Facilities

A distinctive feature of the rest of the 2016 season was that the guest house "Lavender" (Adler) provided a free shuttle service. No matter what time of day or night a tourist arrives, a car is waiting for him - both at the airport and at the station. Moreover, the guest was not only brought to a mini-hotel, but also escorted home. Another service that favorably distinguishes the guest house "Lavender" from other places of vacationers accommodation is permission to call in with pets. Registration of a visitor with the authorities is also included in the room rate. And for a fee you will be allowed to use the washing machine. Wi-Fi is available in every room. The receptionist will help you organize excursions and other leisure activities.

Guest house "Lavender" (Adler): reviews

First of all, tourists celebrate the hospitality of the owners. Literally every review expresses gratitude to this couple, who cares about the comfort of their guests. The rooms are very spacious and bright. They clean them "perfectly". Tourists also noted the cleanliness of the pool. The patio is cobbled, without greenery, but everything is very comfortable. A modern system for cleaning and filtering water in the pool makes swimming very enjoyable - no eye irritation, no smell of bleach! Many liked the beds. Orthopedic mattresses are not often found in guest houses, and even in the south. Tourists liked the location of the mini-hotel: it is close to everything, but it is very quiet around.
