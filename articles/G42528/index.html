Mogilev hotels "Tourist", "Provincial", "Signal": services and description of hotels

Mogilev is one of the largest cities in Belarus, it is located on the banks of the Dnieper, has a long history and many interesting sights. In Mogilev there is a railway and automobile communication with many cities of Russia, Ukraine, Poland and the Baltic countries.

Sights of Mogilev

Guests who have visited Mogilev advise future tourists to visit this wonderful city in spring or summer, during this period the city has a lot of greenery, beautiful flower beds and flowerbeds are decorated on the streets, there are many parks and squares.

Popular sights of the city are preserved historical objects. Most often, guests of the city visit St. Nicholas Convent, Cathedral of the Assumption of the Virgin Mary and St. Stanislav, the Church of the Three Saints, the city hall and the building of the Drama Theater. In Mogilev is the longest pedestrian street in the country - Leninskaya. Here guests of the city and its residents walk, visit numerous cafes and shops, cozy courtyards.



Hotels of Mogilev

In the city, every tourist can choose a hotel based on their needs, preferences and financial capabilities. Mogilev hotels are located in each of its districts, many of them occupy a convenient place near the railway stations of the city and in its center. In this city it is possible to find hotels of different stars, the rooms in them are represented by different categories from super economics to business class and luxury. Mogilev hotels ask for different room rates. In pricing, the proposed conditions, additional services, and general guest comfort are taken into account.

Many Mogilev hotels have differences both in the provision of services and in the conditions of guest accommodation. It is very difficult to determine which of them has the maximum comfort for living, since in the pursuit of attracting tourists all hotels strive to provide the best conditions for their customers.

Hotel "Tourist"

In walking distance from the city center, in a quiet park area on the banks of the Dnieper is the hotel complex "Tourist".

Hotel "Tourist" (Mogilev) is three-star, has 147 rooms: studios, luxury and standard single and double. In 2014, the hotel carried out a reconstruction, during which its number of rooms was renovated and refurbished, the rooms have all the necessary amenities and attributes of comfort, the atmosphere in them has become cozy and homely.

The hotel has a restaurant and a lobby bar. The menu is varied, represented by both Belarusian cuisine and European. Here you can prepare dietary and children's dishes.



Hotel "Tourist" (Mogilev) provides services for business meetings and conferences. There is a business center, a meeting room and two conference rooms for 50 and 100 participants.

Guests can use the wireless Internet in each room, book a transfer or various excursions, and use the laundry services.

Hotel "Signal" (Mogilev)

54 cozy rooms of the Signal Hotel are always ready to receive their guests. They can accommodate two guests. Here you can book suites, junior suites and standard rooms.
