Biography of Steve Jobs - a pioneer of the era of IT technology

Steve Jobs was born February 24, 1955 in San Francisco. His father, Abdulfatta Giandali, was a native of Syria, and his mother, Joan Schible, was a German from a Catholic family. Both of them studied at the University of Wisconsin. When father Joan found out that she was pregnant from a Muslim, he threatened her with inheritance. The girl went to give birth to a private obstetrician in San Francisco, and then abandoned the child, giving him for adoption to Paul Jobs and his wife, Clara Agobyan, an American of Armenian descent. So begins the biography of Steve Jobs - one of the most successful people of our time. So, a boy born out of wedlock and abandoned by his biological parents ended up in a foster family.
