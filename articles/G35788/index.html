Emerald (Hotel, Pattaya): reviews, photos

Every year, travelers become more and more popular with travelers. This is a country of incredibly beautiful nature, hospitality and sincere smiles. It is no secret that one of the most frequently visited beach resorts is the city of Pattaya. It is here, very close to the seashore, the Emerald Hotel is located.

This hotel is very small, but clean, comfortable, and most importantly, suitable for a budget holiday. So what living conditions can a traveler expect? Do they offer meals and additional services? How far do you have to go to the beach? The answers to these questions are sought by every tourist.

Description of the location and general information about the hotel

Location is an important moment for many travelers. Where to look for the Emerald Hotel? The hotel address is 144/1 Moo 12 Nongprue Banglamung Chonburi. In a nutshell, the resort is located in the southern part of Pattaya, just a few minutes walk from the picturesque, popular with tourists Jomtien beach. By the way, in the immediate vicinity are shopping centers and shops.





What does the Emerald Hotel look like? The hotel consists of a five-story building, built in a classic style, and a fairly spacious courtyard. By the way, the resort complex is relatively new, as it was opened in 2011. Every year it becomes more and more popular with tourists.

Comfort and the availability of modern amenities are what the vast majority of tourists require. What can the Emerald Hotel offer? The hotel is a great place to stay, because here the traveler is waiting for a spacious room with a modern renovation, calm design and comfortable furniture.





Naturally, guests can count on a set of household appliances, including an air conditioning system and a TV (you can watch most of the popular satellite channels). The room also has a small refrigerator. Every day it is filled with drinking water. Since access to the Internet is important for modern tourists, the hotel has free Wi-Fi access. Some rooms have a kitchenette with the necessary equipment (a small electric stove, microwave, electric kettle), as well as a set of kitchen and tableware.

The room comes with a private bathroom with toilet, shower and necessary toiletries.

Food for travelers

The price includes daily breakfast. In the morning you can visit the local restaurant, where guests can organize a buffet of delicious and light dishes. Here you can have a meal at any time of the day, however, already ordering dishes on the menu and paying the bill. Guests can enjoy masterpieces of Thai cuisine, as well as more traditional European dishes. The hotel also has a bar with a stylish atmosphere and a huge selection of delicious cocktails.





Where is the beach located?

What kind of entertainment can Emerald (hotel) offer? Pattaya is a city famous for its luxurious beaches. Just a five-minute walk away is the famous and popular Jomtien beach for travelers. This is a lively stretch of the coast where you can not only swim and sunbathe, but also engage in outdoor activities, try water sports (diving, parasailing, windsurfing) and, of course, make new friends. Since this is a public beach, you need to pay for the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas. But the towel can be obtained free of charge at the hotel (issued on bail).





How to have fun at the hotel? Leisure and additional services

Guests of the Emerald Hotel can count on additional service. To begin with, it is worth saying that the reception is open around the clock, as well as room service (by the way, even during the day you can order food to your room from the hotel restaurant). All doors are equipped with electronic locks, there is a video surveillance system and reliable security.

The hotel area is quite small, but there is a cozy, beautiful courtyard where you can relax, sunbathe and chat with friends. If necessary, employees can call a taxi or arrange a transfer to the airport. The hotel does not have a constantly working medical center, but in case of health problems, an experienced doctor is immediately called to the guest.

Do not forget that Pattaya is not only a beach city. There are many historical and architectural attractions, and in the vicinity there is something to see. The hotel staff themselves will be happy to tell you about all the charms of the city and help you plan the route for an exciting independent trip.

Emerald Hotel (Pattaya): reviews
