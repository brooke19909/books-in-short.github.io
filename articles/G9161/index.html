Credit expansion is an intensive expansion of credit transactions and bank operations with a view to profit

Credit expansion is one of the types of monetary credit policy whose essence is to increase profitability by expanding areas of influence and enhancing banking activities. The word itself means "expansion or distribution." Most often we are talking about the result of the policy of the state, which is trying in this way to act on its economy for profit.

Features

Credit expansion is a policy that can be implemented through real accumulation. In this case, it is aimed at providing loans, the service is quite widespread, while the range of products that are offered on the market is expanding.

The opposite of this process is the issuance of debt instruments. This type is called fiduciary. At the same time, the issued security turns out to be unsecured. In other words, lending is curtailed, and the expansion itself is carried out through borrowed funds.

When is credit expansion useful? This is a policy of expensive money, it limits emissions and constrains lending. It aims to stimulate the release of cash and increase profits through increased activity of the banking sector.

Methods

The main goal of credit expansion is the struggle for markets that bring maximum benefits, areas of capital investment and sources of raw materials. The following methods of its implementation are distinguished:

  • External expansion is carried out through investments of private and state capital abroad, the provision of export credit, export premiums, currency and commodity dumping, as well as insurance of export transactions of institutional private units against currency, credit, interest, and portfolio risk.
  • The internal method of pursuing a credit expansion policy is, in turn, aimed at regulating the economy and accelerating the rate of economic growth. It includes lowering official rates with expanding limits on pawnshop and accounting operations. In addition, it is possible to change the reserve standards of credit organizations or purchase securities in open markets, and at the same time expand purchases from commercial financial organizations of foreign currency and lower the interest rate on them. This process involves the abolition of quantitative restrictions on credit to stimulate economic activity.

Thus, credit expansion is an intensive way to expand credit transactions in order to make a profit.

How to get a loan at a low interest?

Interest on loans depends on many conditions. Basically from the requested amount and terms of return, as well as from the pledge or guarantors. If the latter are available, the rate will decrease, and from the first two points it will directly increase. Are there any recommendations on how the client can get the lowest interest rates on loans? Of course, they are well known:

  • It is necessary to choose only large state banking institutions with transparent tariff systems.
  • It is necessary to prepare documents from work in advance.
  • You can not get express credit in stores, their speed of issue is directly related to the highest interest.
  • You can not agree to personal compulsory insurance.

Following these simple tips, the client can always choose the best offer. The main thing here is, perhaps, preliminary preparation. Of course, a person will most likely have to spend his time collecting documentation, but it will certainly pay off in full.
