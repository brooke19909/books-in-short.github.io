What you need to pay attention to before buying an apartment in London

The first thing you need to know before buying a home is that in England there are several forms of ownership. For example, such a form of ownership as leasehold makes it possible to own housing only for a certain time and the minimum tenure for real estate is 1 year, and the maximum is 999 years. Upon the expiration of the term, the property owner loses ownership of the housing. This form of ownership can be compared with rental housing, only an English rental gives more rights to the temporary owner. Well, such a form of ownership as freehold makes it possible to fully redeem property.

You also need to know that the choice of housing in London is quite diverse and housing prices depend on in which area you are going to buy an apartment. For example, the most expensive areas of the city are: Mayfair, Knightsbridge and Belgravia.
