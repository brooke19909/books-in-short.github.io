Christabelle Hotel 3 * (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): overview and reviews

Ayia Napa is one of the most beautiful resorts in Cyprus, and the Christabelle Hotel 3 * hotel is a worthy accommodation option for all tourists. The combination of absolute comfort, high standards of service, luxurious design and a favorable location can satisfy the needs of the most demanding travelers.





Location

Although the Christabelle Hotel Resort is located in central Ayia Napa, it is a fairly quiet area, hidden from the noise of entertainment venues. The distances to the main infrastructure facilities and popular settlements are as follows:

  • central street (500 m, 5 minutes walk);
  • public transport stop (500 m, 5 minutes walk);
  • municipal beach (1.8 km, 20 minutes walk);
  • Nassi Beach (3.2 km, 30 minutes walk);
  • Protaras (10 km, 8 minutes drive);
  • Paralimni (13 km, 10 minutes drive);
  • Larnaca International Airport (45 km, 50 minutes drive);
  • Nicosia (85 km, 1 hour drive);
  • Limassol (100 km, 1 hour 20 minutes drive);
  • Paphos (180 km, 2 hours drive).

Number of rooms

Guests at Christabelle Hotel Apts can enjoy a homely atmosphere of warmth and comfort. Guests can choose the following options for accommodation:

  • Super Studios are comfortable open-plan rooms that have recently been completely refurbished. The apartments have new modern furniture. In addition to the sleeping and living areas, there is also a kitchenette. It has a private balcony. This room can accommodate three guests.
  • Standard rooms are located in an additional building. In their area and equipment, they completely repeat the super-studio. The difference is that these apartments were not renovated.
  • In terms of size and functionality, the indoor superior can compete with a full-fledged city apartment. The bedroom is equipped with two beds, which can be moved if necessary. There is also a combined bathroom and access to the balcony. The living room has a kitchenette, sofa and dining table.
  • Three-room superior is ideal for families. The main bedroom has a French bed, and the additional one has two separate beds. The living room, in addition to upholstered furniture, is also equipped with kitchen appliances and a dining table. There is a private balcony and two bathrooms.

Additional Information

It is worth to read more information about the Ayia Napa Christabelle Hotel Apts 3 * resort. It is worth paying attention to the following points:

  • Registration of newly arrived guests, as well as their check-in, is possible after 14:00 (for an additional fee you can get into the room earlier);
  • Departure is carried out before noon, but subject to an hourly payment, the stay in the room can be extended;
  • children under 2 years old in a special bed stay in the room for free;
  • upon registration, the guest is required to present an identity card with a photograph;
  • use of air conditioning in the room is possible at an additional cost;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel.

Negative reviews

On specialized resources, you can find a number of negative comments about such a resort institution as Christabelle Hotel Apts 3 *. It is worth noting the following disadvantages of this hotel:

  • the hotel is quite uncomfortable with respect to the beach;
  • Wireless Internet catches only by the pool;
  • the prices for alcohol in the bar are too high;
  • until late at night, music plays near the pool, which keeps us awake even with the windows closed;
  • if you do not have an adapter for an outlet, then the hotel will not give you a rental, but will sell it for 2 euros (usually hotel employees give them to guests free of charge);
  • the main contingent of the hotel is the noisy youth, who are almost always drunk;
  • the room does not have a remote control from the air conditioner, and if you need to switch the mode, you need to call a hotel employee (in addition, the use of this device is paid);
  • before the arrival of new guests, rooms are not aired, and therefore there may be a stale smell;
  • when entering the water there are a lot of stones that painfully pierce the legs;
  • air conditioning does not work well;
  • there is practically no infrastructure for relaxing with young children;
  • despite the fact that the hotel has a lot of signs, everything is so confused here that it is not difficult to get lost;
  • hotel staff are not very well oriented on the territory, and therefore you should not expect that with their help you will find your room faster;
  • during cleaning, maids almost never wash the floors;
  • Despite the presence of a TV and a telephone in the rooms, in some apartments these options are not available;
  • rare replacement of bed linen;
  • the restaurant has a fairly modest menu, and if you pay for complex meals, then it will be monotonous;
  • drinking water is not brought to the rooms;
  • the air conditioner is turned off not only with the balcony open, but even if you open the front or interior door;
  • the bed creaks strongly at the slightest movement.




General impression

Hotel Christabelle Hotel 3 * has a convenient location in relation to urban infrastructure, as well as cultural attractions. Nevertheless, if you are a fan of sea recreation, the institution will not suit you, because it is located quite far from the beach. Thus, this hotel is good for those who come to Ayia Napa for sightseeing purposes.

The main disadvantage of the institution is its focus on noisy youth. The music plays until late, and there are no restrictions on the sale of alcoholic beverages. Thus, in the afternoon you can meet a lot of drunken guests on the territory. This institution is absolutely not suitable for families with children.
