How to block Xiaomi bootloader and why is it needed?

A special bootloader, which is installed on Xiaomi smartphones, provides access to the operating system, allowing you to carry out various operations with it. Sometimes it can be convenient, and sometimes it can get in the way. Therefore, from this article you will learn several ways of how to block the Xiaomi bootloader, and you will understand why this is necessary.

Xiaomi bootloader

How to lock bootloader Xiaomi Note 3? First you need to decide what role the bootloader plays in the Xiaomi system. Bootloader is a built-in component on Xiaomi devices, which is an analogue of the BIOS. If you get the right to make changes to the system, you can directly influence various areas and programs. The standard OS is protected from outside interference in the settings, so to unlock the bootloader you need to make a certain amount of effort. In addition, the Xiaomi company blocks the bootloader in order to avoid introducing into the OS viruses that have recently begun to grow like mushrooms after rain. If you became the owner of a smartphone on which you can perform various operations directly, then most likely you bought a fake. Therefore, avoid entering personal data on such a phone.

Why block Xiaomi bootloader?

  • In order to protect yourself and your data. With the bootloader locked, malware will not interfere and forward bank card information to unauthorized persons.
  • The presence on the phone of an unlocked bootloader is complicated by the removal of the lock from a stolen or lost phone.
  • It is impossible to find a phone with a locked bootloader.
  • For weekly verification. When unlocking the bootloader, some programs can bypass this ritual, which means that you will not be able to restore the system in the future.

Xiaomi bootloader lock

Sometimes users need to get rid of the system to which access is open. Many owners of Chinese smartphones ask how to block the Xiaomi bootloader. There are several ways to do this. Flashing lock

There is another way to block the bootloader. It is more time-consuming and requires the mandatory storage of all data. How to block Xiaomi bootloader through flashing? The following must be done:

  1. Transfer all data to a computer. Unfortunately, during the flashing process, absolutely all information will be deleted. Therefore, it is important to copy everything: from photos to phone numbers and notes. Additional copying to an external hard drive or cloud storage is also worthwhile if the data volume is very large.
  2. Download the official MIUI firmware. You can find it on the Xiaomi company website. Make sure that you are not downloading a third-party application.
  3. Download MiFlash. With this boot program, a new OS will be installed on your phone. In this case, it is also important to use only official versions.
  4. Run the bootloader and specify the path to the downloaded operating system.
  5. Then enable Fastboot mode on your smartphone.
  6. Connect it to your computer.
  7. Click Refresh and wait for your device to appear in the list.
  8. Wait for the operation to complete.

As you can see, the answer to the question of how to block Xiaomi bootloader via the command line is quite simple. To do this, just install one program and download the latest version of the OS. Perhaps the most difficult part is the need to copy all the data.

Possible problems
