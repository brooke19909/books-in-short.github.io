The principle of knitting knitted patterns

Hand-made yarn items never went out of style. Moreover, from classic loops you can create original patterns and new ways of knitting. Modern craftswomen turn the mistake into amazing openwork weaves. Knitting patterns with flat loops are very popular today. They add mysterious translucency to the product.

Loop Opening Methods

In the classical knitting technique, a missed loop was considered a mistake that had to be corrected by raising the lost link with a special knitting tool. Now knitting knitted patterns with loops is considered fashionable and stylish. Amazing openwork is created from the passes, which are thought out in advance and prescribed in the schemes.

Openwork vertical line can be obtained by tying the canvas to the end, and then in the right place dropping the loop and loosening it to the bottom edge of the product. It turns out an openwork track. You can make several translucent lines in certain places.

In order to create a horizontal line, it is necessary to make several double crochets through the loop in the front row. The more crochets in one place, the wider will be a knitting pattern with lowered loops. On the reverse side they are not knit - they simply throw them off, and pull the adjacent link, aligning the canvas. Further, the loops can be crossed and knitted with the front or back.

Openwork cloth

Openwork knitting patterns with deflated loops can be created as a single element on the product, or symmetrically distributed throughout the part. To do this, you need to schematically arrange the patterns and in certain places make yarn.

After knitting several rows from the wrong side, additional loops lower and stretch the pattern. An additional loop can be obtained from the broach between the dialed links, then knit a few centimeters of the fabric and discard the dialed loop.

It is recommended that you knit the loops in a crossed way on both sides of the additional link. The pattern distributed throughout the product is called the rain. When knitting patterns with knitted needles, it is important to control the number of links and mark the places of increases. Otherwise, you can stray and accidentally throw off the adjacent loop, violating the symmetry of the pattern.

Description of the pattern with lowered loops

Spitsamm can tie the original openwork product. The patterns look interesting on light blouses, mohair scarves, summer cardigans from both plain and section yarn.

To knit an interesting pattern on knitting needles, dial as many loops so that the number is divided by 10. To maintain symmetry, add 6 more loops and 2 hem.

Openwork consists of 8 rows:

  • In the first strip, knit all the links in the front way.
  • Expand the work and after the edge, make every 10 loops in the following sequence: between the first 5 front loops, throw the thread 1 time, then 2 yarn, then 3 yarn, again 2 loops and 1 yarn.
  • Knit the next 5 stitches using the facial method.
  • In the 3rd row, additional links are thrown off, the rest are made by the front method.
  • Then two rows are knitted with a garter stitch.
  • In the 6th strip, rapports of 10 loops are knitted.
  • The edge is removed, the next 6 links are created by the front method, then through the front one to form the crochets, increasing and then reducing the number, as in the second row.
  • In the 7th row, repeat the actions as in the 3rd, and knit the last row of the pattern with facial loops.

It is recommended to make a training sample with the calculation of the yarn and determining the width of the canvas, since when the loops are dissolved, the part expands. Patterns adorn the product, make it a little messy, which is very much appreciated and promoted by modern fashion.
