Where is the hotel "Deauville" (Anapa) located

The most important thing in the location of the resort hotel is its remoteness from the sea. As for the Deauville hotel (Anapa), it is only 500 meters from the clean sandy beach. The city center is approximately 2.5 km away. But do not be afraid of this distance. You can go for a walk at any time, because a free shuttle service is provided daily for guests. As for the main transport hubs, the railway station is 5 kilometers away from the hotel, but the airport is 13 kilometers away.

Hotel Policies

When choosing the Deauville hotel (Anapa) for your vacation, you should definitely familiarize yourself with the rules that govern accommodation in it:

  • Check-in at the hotel is strictly after 14:00, but you must leave on the last day before noon;
  • no matter how long you stayed in the room, the fee is charged as for a full day;
  • if necessary, you can stay in your room after the checkout time, but from that moment on, hourly charges for overtime stays will be charged;
  • You have the opportunity to use non-cash payment options for accommodation and other services provided;
  • absolutely free, you can take advantage of such opportunities as calling an ambulance, providing a first-aid kit, delivering letters and press directly to your room, as well as the "alarm" service;
  • Checking into your room, you must fully pay for the entire time you plan to stay at the hotel;
  • in the period from 23:00 to 9:00 in the morning, it is recommended to remain silent and to refrain from noisy events;
  • if you intend to stay in the hotel for a long time, but do not want to pay the full amount at once, then you will have to make an intermediate payment every 5 days;
  • if you suspect that you have a dangerous infectious disease, the administration has the right to require an appropriate medical certificate (if it is not submitted, your contractual relationship will be broken);
  • You agree to fully compensate for the damage caused to you by the hotel as a result of damage to its property.
