Anna Hotel 2 * (Crete, Greece): hotel overview, description and reviews

Crete, most tourists rightly considers one of the most beautiful and unusual places on earth. This island is located at the junction of three parts of the world at once - Africa, Europe and Asia. Washed by two seas, Crete boasts not only picturesque nature, but also a huge number of different attractions. Of course, the tourist infrastructure in this resort is developed just fine. If desired, vacationers who choose to vacation in Crete can rent here, including convenient cheap accommodation. For example, very inexpensive rooms for tourists are offered by the budget two-star hotel Anna Hotel.

How to get to the complex

This comfortable hotel is located in the heart of the city of Hersonissos, well-known to all lovers of beach holidays and resort entertainment. The main administrative buildings of this village are located right next to the Anna Hotel 2 * hotel (Crete). Of course, all kinds of shops, as well as catering establishments, work near this complex. The distance from the sea to the hotel is about one hundred meters .





Of course, tourists who bought tours to Greece with accommodation in this center fly to Crete most often by plane. The nearest airport to Hersonissos is Heraklion, approximately 25 km away. It is easiest for holidaymakers to get to the hotel by operator‚Äôs transfer bus. Those tourists who want to check into the hotel as quickly as possible should hire a taxi at the airport or rent a car. Travelers who want to save on transfer usually get to the hotel by public transport. From Heraklion Airport to Hersonissos bus number 78 runs. The stop is located directly opposite the entrance to the airport building.

General description of the hotel

This hotel was built quite a long time ago - in the last century (1983) . In 2000, however, reconstruction was carried out here. The Anna Hotel 2 * hotel (Crete) is located in the very center of the town and looks, of course, quite presentable. The exterior of its buildings , for obvious reasons, is in harmony with the general style of architecture of Hersonissos. The decoration of the rooms in this hotel, in fact, is a continuation of the external concept and building. The total area occupied by the complex is 1,500 m 2 .

The owners of Anna Hotel 2 * (Greece, Crete) I am Greek Marius and his daughter Georgia. Judging by the reviews of tourists, they are respectful and courteous to their guests. The hotel also has its own small courtyard. Here is a swimming pool and palm trees grow.





Hotel Rooms

For tourists, the hotel Anna Hotel 2 * (Crete) is equipped with 44 comfortable rooms. The views from the windows of the hotel rooms open onto the pool, palm trees or a small town in the distance. The guests of the complex are offered both single and double, triple rooms. In the rooms themselves for the convenience of residents are provided:

  • refrigerators;

  • TV sets

  • air conditioners;

  • safes;

  • Phones

Unfortunately, tourists can use the refrigerator in the rooms of this center only for a fee. The television in the hotel is satellite. Of course, each room has a shower room. Towels and a hairdryer are provided here for the convenience of guests.

The guests of this hotel in the rooms can use the Internet absolutely free. Wi-Fi is available on site. Wired internet is also available in the rooms.





The opinion of the guests about the rooms

Reviews about the rooms rented at this hotel on the Internet are mostly discreet. Most of the guests of Crete consider the hotel rooms not luxurious, but they are quite suitable for an overnight stay. The furniture in the hotel rooms is quite old, but quite comfortable. Air conditioners in the rooms do not break down too often.

The majority of tourists include the pluses of the hotel rooms, first of all, that their maids clean quite carefully and often. The disadvantage of renting a hotel is considered to be primarily poor soundproofing of the apartments. Unfortunately there are no cabins in the shower rooms in the rooms of this center. Guests should use a simple watering can behind the curtain. This, of course, the majority of residents also refers to the minuses of the hotel.

Anna Hotel 2 * (Crete): sea reviews

On the websites of some operators it is stated that the beach at this hotel has its own. However, judging by the reviews of tourists, this is not entirely true. Opposite the hotel is still a public city beach. Guests of Anna Hotel 2 * (Crete) need to go to it no more than 10 minutes. There are always a lot of people on this beach. The fact is that not only the guests of nearby hotels have a rest on it, but also the residents of Hersonissos itself.

Those hotel guests who prefer to spend time by the sea in silence should visit another beach at this resort - Drepanos. It is located on the outskirts of the city, about a kilometer from the complex. You can get to it by public transport or by a rented car.

The beaches in Hersonissos are usually covered with fine sand. Therefore, you can sunbathe by the sea in this resort, including simply on mats. However, experienced tourists still recommend that beginners take paid sunbeds from the sea. The fact is that only those who rented a deck chair are usually allowed into the showers on the beaches in this town.
