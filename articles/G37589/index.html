Myra Hotel 3 * Marmaris: photos and reviews of tourists

Myra Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) is a hotel that has a double reputation among tourists. Europeans, as well as independent travelers from Russia, Ukraine and other countries of the former USSR, like it. Budget hotels in Marmaris

In this resort you can find accommodation that will suit both the capricious rich man and the picky backpacker. Outside the city are five-star palaces and club hotels with huge parks and gardens, as well as their own marinas and yachts. But the hotels closer to the center are mostly inexpensive. Either young people who have fun on Bar Street until the morning, or travelers who spend days on excursions and various walks often live there. As a rule, these are small hotels, with almost no territory. Most often they are located not on the shore, but two or three streets from the beach. Typical local "three rubles" include "Emre Beach", "Ochaktan", "Adler", "Siesta", "Egean Park". They offer a minimal set of services and are most often very loved by young people from different countries. Mira is included in the list of the best economical hotels in the resort. And the description of Myra Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) we will begin with its location.



How to get that nearby

Since our hotel is located almost in the center of the city, an independent traveler should get a Havash bus from Dalaman airport to Marmaris (or rather, to the bus station of this city), and then transfer to a local dolmush. Near the hotel there are two large supermarkets. These are Migros and Kipa. Therefore, guests of the Myra Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) do not even need to think about where to exchange currency. It is enough to buy something in such supermarkets, and you will be given change in lire, moreover, at the rate of the National Bank. And this is much more profitable than in exchangers. To the city center, Marina port and the square with the singing fountains - about half an hour leisurely step. Not far from the bazaar, where you can buy different things cheaper than in shops on the street.



Infrastructure and territory, service

Hotel Myra Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) is a four-story building with an attic and an elevator. There is a small green courtyard with a pool. The hotel catches pretty fast Wi-Fi - mainly in the lobby and near the restaurant, but even in some rooms. But if there are a lot of people, you will have to wait in line to connect at normal speed. You can play darts and table tennis. In the evenings, concerts and entertainment are organized near the restaurant. The staff is adequate, calm, unobtrusive and very positive. The maids are honest, nothing disappears, and it does not depend on the tip. At eleven in the evening every day they carry free to Bar Street. Fridays after dinner, dancing. This hotel is also notable for the fact that you can stay with a dog here, although not very large. If you have a late flight, then you are free to renew the all-inclusive service. But there are few children here, and practically nothing is provided for them.



Rooms

Typical guest rooms at the Myra Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) - standard. These are small rooms of 15-17 square meters. There are 70 of them in the hotel. The rooms have balconies with a table and chairs, they are air-conditioned, with bathrooms and showers. In the bathroom of toiletries only liquid soap. The rooms are bright. There is a flat-panel LCD TV with two Russian channels. Furniture is not new, but not "killed". There is always hot water with good pressure. They clean every day, well and thoroughly, wipe the dust on the shelves, bedside table and balcony table, change towels. Recently renovated. Safe is paid. Iron can be taken at the reception. The rooms have disposable slippers. From the windows and balconies you can see Marmaris. The hotel building is low, so you can not enjoy the panorama of the sea directly from the room. There is no refrigerator, which many tourists noted as a big minus. The shower is separated by a curtain from the bathroom. The most comfortable rooms are corner. There is a view from the balcony immediately in both directions.



Nutrition

Although the Myra Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) has only three stars on its facade, it feeds tourists according to different systems. Some only take breakfast, others practice half board, but many prefer the good old "all inclusive." Moreover, the food here is good, give fruits (melons, watermelons), a large number of various salads, snacks and Turkish sweets. For lunch and dinner, there is always meat, vegetables, greens, soups, at least two types of side dishes. European-style breakfasts include omelets, scrambled eggs, sausages, cheeses, tomatoes, olives, yoghurts, pastries ... Appetizers and salads are served in the form of a buffet, and the waiter brings the main dishes. There is a bar that operates on an all-inclusive basis from ten in the morning until half past ten in the evening. Beer, red and white wine, local strong drinks are all of good quality. Water, juices and coffee at the reception around the clock. On Fridays there is fish and grilled meat. Very fresh and soft bread. When the products are taken apart, they immediately bring everything new. There is no children's menu, but the restaurant has high chairs.

Excursions

Guests of the Myra Hotel 3 * (Marmaris, Turkey) are advised to definitely visit local attractions. Excursions are best bought at street travel companies, and it is best to get around a few of them and compare prices. The most popular trip is to visit the island of Cleopatra (Cedir) - a kind of open-air maritime museum. In the Gokova Bay, you can see the ruins of the ancient city of Kedrai, and according to legend, the sand on the beach was brought directly from Egypt. If you like the sea, then in the port you will find a huge selection of ships and walks in the Aegean islands with various programs, visits to caves, bays and bays. The program of such excursions necessarily includes lunch, fruit, as well as the opportunity to dive with a mask in clear water. Often, yachts are two-story, decorated original, in the form of pirate ships, and a group of animators work on board.
