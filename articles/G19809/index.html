Jacob's Natural Well (Texas, USA) and the Dangers He Preserves

The Texas attraction, rumored to have killed about a dozen divers trying to explore the pond, still attracts all fans of tickling nerves.

Dangerous abyss

Thrill seekers rush to a quiet pool, which, at first glance, looks very harmless. An artesian spring with surprisingly clear water spurting from the ground came to the surface in the city of Wimberley many centuries ago. Once upon a time a fountain hit the place of a standing reservoir, but over time it dried up, opening a curious reservoir filled with curious eyes, mesmerizing with mortal beauty and creating the illusion of a real abyss.





Jacob's Natural Well (Texas, USA), whose depth reaches about 10 meters, has become the subject of study by experienced scuba divers and amateur divers who risk their lives. The source, bearing the biblical name, still hides a lot of interesting and unknown. A dangerous abyss branches out into at least 4 caves and goes into a depth of about 40 meters, and what happens next cannot be verified. Nobody has been able to explore the entire network of underwater caves following one after another, and the most desperate remained at the bottom, entangled in mazes.

Well of Jacob (Texas, USA) and his secrets

Today it is known for sure that the first cave, 7 meters in diameter, is located at a depth accessible to divers. It is well lit and underwater beauties appear to divers in all their splendor. In order to get into the second cave, located at a depth of 25 meters, a very narrow gap has to be overcome, which took at least one human life. It is known about a panicked student stuck in a death trap, whose life was taken away by the well of Jacob - Texas, USA.





To penetrate into pitch darkness - without a single ray of light - the third grotto will have to swim through another narrow gallery. And the most dangerous and unexplored consists of ancient calcareous deposits, which in any movement so muddy the water that it is simply impossible to find a way out. In "Virgin", as they called the last cave, several daredevils got, but only one could get out. Perhaps there are a lot more grottoes, but nobody managed to overcome the fourth one in order to explore underwater secrets, and what is behind it is a big mystery.

Treasure of Wimberley

Despite the scary tales of claimed lives, Jacob's well supplying drinking water (Texas, USA) is a popular state attraction. Due to notoriety 35 years ago, the entrance to the pool was blocked by local authorities by installing grilles and concreting. However, this did not save the amazing place created by nature and recognized as a national treasure from the invasion of curious extreme people and ordinary tourists who dream of diving into the crystal clear water. After a while, all the barriers were removed to the delight of local and visiting lovers to cool off.





Ancient legend

But not everyone is limited to swimming. A local kid dives selflessly into a sinister hole that keeps secrets. The ancient stories of the old-timers, passed down through the generations, say that a jump into the well of Jacob (Texas, USA) will become a talisman of good luck for life for daredevils. But the main, and practically impracticable, point of the legend claims that after this one should never come back here. But how to refrain from the next visit to an unforgettable miracle of nature? Therefore, those who jump into the abyss are tempted to return and repeat them again.

Important tips for tourists

You will not be able to enjoy the bottomless abyss alone, because a deserted local attraction never happens. Plunge into the mysterious well of Jacob (Texas, USA), many travelers dream of planning their vacation for the summer - the best time to visit a cool spring. Arrivals in winter notice a mild natural climate, but they will not be able to jump into the underwater kingdom, but low temperatures and strong winds will not become an obstacle to contemplate the local landscape. The small town of Wimberley is located near the capital of Texas, you can get to the transparent pond on your own in a few minutes by taxi or sign up for a free tour starting every Saturday.





Without exception, all tourists who ventured into extreme sports enthusiastically recall jumping into the unique well of Jacob (Texas, USA). Photos taken from the cliffs convey an incredible feeling of flying into a transparent abyss, many even think that it was not without a photo montage, these frames look so creepy.
