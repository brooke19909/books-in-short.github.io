Rest in the guest house "Assol" (Gelendzhik): description, reviews of tourists

The Black Sea resorts of the Krasnodar Territory have always been very popular among Russians. Adopting foreign experience, they are becoming more beautiful every year, infrastructure is developing and service is improving. The most important advantage of domestic resorts is their proximity and accessibility. No need to draw up documents, passport, insurance, power of attorney. You can go on vacation at any time.

Rest in Gelendzhik

A vacation spent in Gelendzhik will bring tourists a lot of pleasure and leave an unforgettable experience. There is everything for a good rest. Sea air mixed with coniferous phytoncides is able to heal the human body and gives all vacationers a great mood.

If you come to rest with children, then you definitely need to visit the dolphinarium and the aquarium - get a lot of positive emotions. Also in the very center of the city on the waterfront there is a magnificent amusement park. Modern swings and carousels will please not only children, but also adults.





The pride of Gelendzhik is the unusual zoo Safari Park with many exotic animals and birds. It is located on a hill, and an excursion to the zoo begins with a cable car. During walks along the alleys of the park, picturesque views of the city and the sea open. Children and their parents will be able to spend unforgettable hours in the zoo and get acquainted with the wildlife.

And of course, adults and children need to visit the water park, especially since there are three of them in Gelendzhik.

Where to live?

Gelendzhik is a resort with a very developed infrastructure. Naturally, there are a lot of housing offers. Each tourist will be able to choose for himself a place where he will spend his vacation to his liking and afford. Luxury luxury European-class hotels offer their customers a VIP vacation with royal conditions. Numerous boathouses by the sea provide the opportunity to fall asleep and wake up to the sound of the surf. A large number of resorts operate on an all-inclusive system.





However, not all guests of Gelendzhik can afford such an elite vacation, and today housing in the private sector is most in demand. There are a lot of offers of private guest houses, mini-pensions and small hotels in Gelendzhik. The competition among the owners of private housing is very great, each owner tries to create the most comfortable living conditions, provide the best service, hospitality and hospitality.

Guest house "Assol"

Gelendzhik is located in a cozy quiet bay. In the very center of the city on the first line from the sea there is a cozy three-star private guest house "Assol". Rest in this mini-hotel is aimed at tourists who love comfort, appreciate beauty and amenities.

The hotel "Assol" (Gelendzhik) consists of two buildings. The first main one is four-story, this building houses high-class junior suites and suites. The second building has three floors. All rooms of this building are standard, they are smaller in area than the suites, but at the same time are equipped with all amenities.





Guest house "Assol" (Gelendzhik) is one of the ten best private hotels in the city. The highlight of the hotel is the chic swimming pool located on its territory, equipped according to modern standards.

On the territory of the guest house "Assol" (Gelendzhik) there is a cozy cafe where everyone can order a full three meals a day. It should be said that breakfast at the hotel is served to all vacationers and is included in the price.

Just two hundred meters from the hotel is the famous promenade of the resort Gelendzhik. Hotel Assol is generally very well located. The beach is nearby, shops, the market, the Magnit supermarket, many restaurants and cafes.
