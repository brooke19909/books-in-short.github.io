Black eyes: how to make them to enhance the photo or give the picture a mystical effect

The question of how to make black eyes in a photo interests people for various reasons. The first group wants to get rid of the red-eye effect. In this situation, only the pupils will have to be blackened. The second group of users wants to achieve demonic eyes that inspire fear in those who look at the photo.





First situation: eliminate red-eye

Usually this shade appears only on the pupil. Therefore, the task of how to make black eyes comes down to changing the color of this particular part. Therefore, in the Photoshop program, you need to open the selected photo and maximize the area you will need to work with.

Then you should take the Ellipse tool. They draw a circle on the pupil. It is necessary to capture a little more than the red circle, because a little unnecessary shade may remain around the pupil.

Now you need to choose any approach to how to make black eyes. There are several ways to get a black and white image. One of them is discoloration, which is located in the menu item "Correction". Simply apply it - and the pupil will turn black with white glare.





Second situation: completely black eyes

How to make sure there are no white areas? It is necessary to paint over the white of the eye with black, so that the look remains alive.

Using the Lasso tool and CTRL button, you need to select two eyes at once. And completely - from century to century. The selected area needs to be copied. Then create a new layer and paste on it what is on the clipboard.

The next step in how to make black eyes is to work with the Curves tool. You need to shift them so that the selected areas become completely black. If necessary, you should take a brush and tint the parts that remain light.

In order for the transition from black eyes to eyelids to be smooth, you will have to use the Drop Shadow tool. In its window there are five sliders that need to be moved until the desired result is obtained. It remains only to combine the two layers.

Of course not. Simple and free analogues of this program also have tools that allow you to understand how to make black eyes. Moreover, the tricks usually almost completely repeat those indicated for Photoshop. Sometimes tools are only found in other menu items.

For example, to eliminate the red-eye effect, the function "Filters - Improvement - Remove red-eye effect" is provided.
