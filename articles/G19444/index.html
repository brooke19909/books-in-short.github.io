Hotel Lilia 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands): photos and reviews of tourists

Holidays in Bulgaria and combines a high level of service and low cost tours. There are also luxurious five-star complexes that are suitable for tourists who prefer a first-class vacation. And this country is very loved for its clear sea, rich ancient heritage and health centers. Tourists who prefer a comfortable stay at affordable prices, choose the hotel "Lilia" (Bulgaria, Golden Sands).

Where is the hotel situated?

The hotel is built on the outskirts of the Golden Sands resort, which is the second largest in Bulgaria. It is famous for its vibrant nightlife, but the hotel is located far from the clubs of the city, so it is suitable for a relaxing holiday. At the same time, the resort center is only 600 meters, so tourists can easily reach large restaurants and bars. Public transport is also 20 meters away. The nearest 24-hour ATM is located 100 meters from the complex. Nearby you can also find souvenir shops, fruit and berry stalls, as well as restaurants and bars offering local specialties.





Hotel Lilia 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is built right on the sandy beach. The sea is only 30 meters away. You can get here from the airport of Varna, which is located 17 km from the resort. From there, buses run to the resort. You can also order a paid transfer or taxi. Arriving in Golden Sands, you should visit the local water park "Aquapolis", which has water slides for children and adults, and for lovers of extreme relaxation. Of the attractions, the Aladzha Monastery, built in the 12th century , stands out. It is 5 km from the complex.

basic information

The hotel was erected in 1957, during the mass Soviet development of the resort. Initially, the political elite of Bulgaria rested here. After the establishment of democracy, all hotels, including Lilia 4 *, were equipped for ordinary tourists. The hotel itself consists of three buildings: the main (9 floors) and two additional (5 floors). There are 230 rooms in the complex, most of which are suitable for a small family. For children under 12 years old the hotel offers good discounts. And it is strictly forbidden to call in animals. Apart from Bulgarian, hotel employees speak only English.





Check-in at the hotel starts at 14:00. But if you have free rooms, you can use the paid service that provides early check-in. Eviction begins in the morning, at 7:00, and continues until noon. You can pay for additional services with a credit card or cash. The hotel accepts Visa, MasterCard and Maestro cards. For cash payments, only the Bulgarian lev is accepted . A complete reconstruction of all the hotel buildings was carried out in 1999.

Learn more about in-room amenities.

"Lilia" is a hotel (Bulgaria), offering its guests comfortable accommodation throughout the rest. All rooms are carefully cleaned every day. Bed linen is changed twice a week, and maids change towels in the bathroom daily. Also, to create a cozy and homely atmosphere, all rooms are equipped with the following amenities:

  • Telephone. Used to contact the hotel administrator or to make international calls. They are paid separately.

All About Nutrition

For tourists staying at the Lilia 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands), several food concepts are offered. They can choose only breakfast, half board or all inclusive. The cost of the latter option includes all meals, as well as local spirits and soft drinks in the hotel bar. For guests of the complex there are various catering establishments. We list the main ones:

  • The main restaurant. Located on the first floor of the main building. Designed for 230 seats. Weather permitting, the outdoor terrace is open. Here, the buffet serves breakfast and dinner, which includes Bulgarian cuisine.
  • Beer restaurant. It offers tourists about 20 types of imported and local beer, as well as a variety of snacks for drinks.
  • Restaurant "BBQ". Specializes in grilled meat, fish and vegetables.
  • Lobby bar. Located near reception. Here you can have a bite to eat freshly baked pastries, fruit or dessert. Soft drinks including tea, coffee and milk are served.
  • Sky bar located on the roof of the hotel. It offers a wonderful view of the Black Sea coast.
  • Night club. It works in the evening and at night. Tourists can buy champagne, beer, Bulgarian wines, as well as refreshing cocktails and light snacks.




Hotel infrastructure

Another important factor affecting the rest is the hotel infrastructure. The Lilia 4 * complex (Golden Sands) cares about the comfort of its guests, therefore it offers them an extensive list of services that they can use when staying here. We list the main ones:

  • Shopping center. Invites tourists to purchase jewelry, souvenirs, clothes, perfumes, beauty products, as well as genuine leather products.
  • Secure parking. Leaving a car here is possible only after a preliminary reservation. One day fee is 9 euros.
  • Free internet in the hotel lobby.
  • Currency exchange point.
  • Safe box for storing valuables. Payment for it is charged daily.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning.
  • Therapist's office.
  • Business center. It has two conference rooms: Cassiopeia (capacity 150 people) and Galatea (up to 60 people). Also, tourists can use the services of fax, photocopy. All rooms are equipped with modern computer technology.
  • Beauty saloon. Male, female and children's hairdressers work.




Entertainment that offers "Lily"

For its guests, the Lilia 4 * complex (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) has prepared a varied entertainment program. All tourists can take advantage of the following leisure options:

  • Pool in the garden. There is a sun terrace, as well as umbrellas, sun loungers and beach towels.
  • Pool on the roof of the main building. Entrance fees apply.
  • Health Center. It offers tourists to attend massage courses, as well as anti-aging treatments for face and body. All sessions are supervised by experienced professionals. There is a sauna, solarium and steam room. Center services are not included in the price and are paid separately.
  • The night club regularly hosts discos and karaoke nights.
  • Water sports that can be enjoyed upon arrival at the beach.
  • Gym. The visit is paid.
  • Billiard room.
  • Table tennis. Public tennis courts are also nearby.




Vacation with children

Hotel Lilia (Golden Sands) is located far from the bustling city center, so it is perfect for families with children. For them, all the necessary conditions for living are created here. For babies, one cot is provided free of charge in the room. For convenience, all restaurants are equipped with high chairs. Tourists can even rent a stroller by making a small deposit. It also provides a paid nanny service, which can sit with the child while the parents are absent .

In the adult pool, located in the garden, there is an additional shallow compartment for children. On this entertainment for the guys ends. The hotel has no children's animation, no playground, no mini-club. Therefore, before traveling, parents should carefully consider their child‚Äôs leisure time in advance.

Positive opinions of tourists about the hotel

Reviews of tourists help to form a full-fledged image of the hotel, in which vacationers are going to stay. Lilia 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) mainly receives positive reviews. They note the following advantages of the hotel:

  • Great location. There is a small road separating the beach from the hotel, which you can also take a walk in the evening.
  • Small but comfortable room. The decor is neat and the furniture is in good condition. The rooms are cleaned carefully and every day.
  • Standard breakfasts. Hearty and quite diverse. The coffee machine makes delicious coffee.
  • After 23:00 the street is very quiet. Even near the pool there is already no one. You can sleep with the window open.
  • Friendly staff, very smiling girls work at the reception.




Negative opinions of tourists about the hotel

Given the opinions of other tourists, travelers can find out about the real problems that exist in the Lilia 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands). Hotel guests note the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Air conditioners in many rooms either do not work at all or cool very poorly. The same problem with refrigerators.
  • Low speed internet throughout the hotel.
  • Tasty food in the restaurant. Almost no fruit (sometimes apples or plums are laid out). Huge lines to the coffee machine.
  • The owner of the hotel is an unpleasant person who can be rude to guests.
  • In order for the staff to begin to properly clean the rooms, you need to leave a tip every day.
  • Shower gel and soap are given in very small quantities and do not replenish when they run out.

Conclusion

We can say that the Lilia 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands), the photo of which is presented above, will be a good option for a budget holiday. The hotel meets the standards of tourist complexes of the country. Having settled here, tourists will receive comfortable rooms and quality service. But you should not count on a luxurious reception. In general, "Lily" can be recommended for families with children, youth and business people.
