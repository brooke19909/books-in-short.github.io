Vityazevo All Inclusive hotels: overview, features and reviews of tourists

Summer is a vacation time. People who seek to spend this period as productively as possible usually leave for the sea coast. Hot sand, warm gentle sea, what else does a person who is tired of a year of continuous work need?

Some people prefer to spend their vacations abroad, but most average citizens choose closer places for relaxation, for example, the village of Vityazevo located on the Black Sea coast. All-inclusive hotels, available in sufficient quantities at this resort, will help to spend time with maximum comfort. During the rest you do not need to do anything, professionals will take care of the convenience of travelers.

We must also take into account the likelihood that the sea will be turbulent, and in order not to lose precious days of relaxation, it is best to stay in a hotel with its own pool. In the vicinity of Anapa Vityazevo , all-inclusive hotels for the most part offer guests this convenience.

Hotels

Many tourists who choose Vityazevo for their vacations consider the All Inclusive hotels to be the most preferred place to stay. And no wonder, because the benevolent staff and excellent conditions organized for the guests of the resort, combined with proximity to the sea, are the best suited to spend the long-awaited vacation in comfort.





In the village you can find hotels of various classes. The three-star National, perfect for both family and youth recreation, is ready to receive guests all year round. And the economy hotel "Pearl" provides rooms at budget prices, which will save money on accommodation and invest them in entertainment.

Complexes with a pool

Among tourists choosing a vacation destination, a village located near Anapa, Vityazevo, all-inclusive hotels with a swimming pool are becoming increasingly popular. The complexes that have placed such a water structure on their territory really have an advantage: their guests do not need to go to the sea if they suddenly wanted to refresh themselves on a hot afternoon. And if water slides are equipped in the pool, the popularity of the hotel complex among tourists who come to rest with children increases.





The most famous All Inclusive hotels in Vityazevo, with a pool located on site, include the following guest houses.

"Attica"

The modern complex, which has four floors, is built in the Greek style: graceful columns and large balconies give the building a unique look. Although the entire hotel occupies a relatively small area, its creators, however, found a place for a pool. It will pleasantly surprise visitors that the water in it is heated. There is also a children's section in the pool, which will especially please families who are staying here on vacation.

Getting to the hotel is not difficult. If you do not know these places, then by prior arrangement with the administrator making the reservation, Attica will arrange a transfer for you from the airport or from Anapa railway station, and this service does not require additional payment.

Green Plaza

The comfortable hotel, offering its guests chic rooms in which there are all amenities (Wi-Fi, refrigerator, satellite TV), also boasts an indoor pool. To plunge into the cool water on a sultry day, its visitors no longer need to go to the sea, just go out into the yard.

The location of the hotel is also extremely successful. All entertainment facilities are within walking distance. And to get to the central beach you need to overcome only 300 meters.

"Constantinople"

In the very center of the resort town there is another hotel, visitors of which can use the outdoor pool located on the territory. "Constantinople" will be happy to welcome guests who will be pleasantly surprised by the quality of service and the services offered.

Choosing to stay at any of the above All Inclusive hotels in Vityazevo, travelers will be able to enjoy not only excellent service, but also soak in the pool. This nice addition will greatly diversify your vacation.

Near the sea

Hotel complexes located in the coastal zone have always been especially popular with tourists: most people visit this resort city precisely for a beach holiday. Present near the sea in Vityazevo and all-inclusive hotels. Some of them have become particularly famous among holidaymakers.





"Patra"

Located in the first resort line, the guest house is located just 150 meters from the central beach. There are also entertainment infrastructure facilities nearby: a water park, an amusement park and the Paralia embankment, which is especially popular with residents and visitors of the city.

Visitors can stay in comfortable double rooms equipped with all amenities. In addition to furniture, they have all the necessary equipment - a refrigerator, TV and air conditioning. Guests are also accommodated in luxury rooms. The rooms have balconies with a gorgeous view of the coast and the resort town.

Located in Vityazevo on the seafront, All Inclusive Hotel has its own swimming pool with heating and hydromassage features. The water complex also includes a children's section, which will delight tourists who plan to bring a child on holiday.

There is a private dining room on the territory of the complex. With its help, three meals a day are organized for guests: a variety of dishes are offered, and homemade cakes and fruits make a pleasant variety on the menu.

"Poseidon on Peace"

The recently opened modern hotel has already managed to acquire its own regular customers seeking to spend their holidays in this place. The five-story building has both double and triple rooms, the decoration of which is a special pride of the owners of the complex: the design interior of the rooms is complemented by perfectly selected furniture. They are decorated in different color schemes, which allows visitors to choose a place to live in accordance with their taste.

Proximity to the sea - a sandy beach, which is located just a hundred meters from the complex, attracts a large number of guests. Nearby are sports facilities that are popular with visitors: bowling and a specially equipped complex for outdoor activities.

Ilios

If you want to settle by the sea in Vityazevo, the All Inclusive Hotel, located 350 meters from the beach area, is what you need for a wonderful holiday. Ilios has truly excellent living conditions. Excellent rooms with an original design, having all the necessary household items - shower, refrigerator and TV will delight visitors, and a vast landscaped area with summer tables will allow you to enjoy the sea air at any time.

In the courtyard of the hotel is equipped with a beautiful playground, as well as animators, which allows young guests of the complex to get new sensations from the trip.

Since people come here from different parts of the country who often do not focus on the locality, the hotel administration organized a free shuttle service from the station and Anapa Airport. For an additional fee, you can arrange a meeting at Art. Tunnel

Cost

Prices at All Inclusive hotels in Vityazevo will pleasantly surprise travelers. For the accommodation of one person in some guest houses you will have to pay a rather modest amount - only 1000 rubles per day. However, it is worth remembering that the cost depends on the quality of the rooms. For apartments in which there are all sorts of amenities, you will have to pay more. The price of luxury rooms is around 3000 thousand rubles per adult.





The cost of housing is affected by many other factors: proximity to the coastal zone and entertainment facilities, the month selected for the holidays. However, the hotels constantly hold all kinds of promotions that allow you to rent excellent rooms at an attractive price.

Guest reviews

All-inclusive hotels in Vityazevo have earned many positive reviews from vacationers. People note the particular comfort of the rooms offered and the friendliness of the staff.

When planning a vacation should take into account every little thing. Therefore, when booking rooms in one of the hotels, you should discuss all living conditions with the administrator of the selected hotel complex. It is from the hotel representative that you can find out the exact information about the services stated in it, the cost of the apartments and get answers to all questions.





To remember the vacation as the most wonderful time, it is necessary with special care to choose a place to live. All-inclusive hotels will be an excellent choice - all the conditions for a comfortable stay are organized on their territory.
