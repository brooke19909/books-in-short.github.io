general information

The hotel is only seven kilometers away from Alanya (Turkey). Club Hotel Anjelique 4 * by travel agencies is positioned as a place that is perfect for both youth and family vacations. Next to it is a bus stop. The hotel was built ten years ago. It fits perfectly into the overall architectural style of the region. The last major overhaul in it was carried out in 2013. The territory is thirty five thousand square meters. In the park area, planted with palm trees and pines, there are six buildings - a seven-story main and five residential blocks in four levels.

Infrastructure

Club Hotel Anjelique 4 * offers its customers a fairly diverse range of services. ATMs are installed in its main building, there is a point where you can exchange currency, a medical office, and concierge services. Laundry with dry cleaning - paid services. In addition, the hotel has a conference room, several small shops, including an inexpensive souvenir kiosk. There is no need to pay for secure parking.



The reception is open around the clock. Here you can duplicate documents and send a fax. Those who wish can also get a safe box. At the registration desk, tourists order a taxi or rent a vehicle.

Housing stock

In Club Hotel Anjelique 4 * four hundred rooms. Categories are standard and family. All are decorated in oriental style and furnished with white comfortable furniture. The rooms are individually controlled split systems, LCD TVs, paid mini-bar, a small safe in which you can store a laptop. Bathrooms combined with bathtub and flexible shower. A hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and other necessary accessories are also provided. On the floor - ceramic tiles, on the walls - panels.

The rooms are soundproofed. There are also non-smoking apartments located in a separate wing. Pets must be discussed with the property in advance. There are no conditions for people with wheelchairs.

Nutrition

Club Hotel Anjelique 4 (Alanya) offers full board meals. In a restaurant with snow-white tablecloths, armchairs and chairs in the same color, lamps that create a feeling of flying, a buffet is served three times a day. For lovers of Turkish and European cuisine, there are many dishes to choose from. In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has two more bars, where during the day you can get light snacks and drinks from local manufacturers for free. In addition to a wide selection of ordinary dishes, you can also get diet food. Tourists note the abundance of fruit on the table. Judging by the reviews of vacationers, desserts, tea, coffee will not leave anyone indifferent. There is a second restaurant on the menu, tables where you can reserve in advance at the registration desk.

For children



Club Hotel Anjelique 4 has a small swimming pool with small slides for its younger guests. Older children can spend time in the mini club. The restaurant has a special menu and high chairs for them. On the territory there is a children's animation.

Beach

To the seashore only two hundred meters. You can overcome this distance in just three minutes. Yellow sand, convenient entry into the water - all this is especially convenient for young children.

The beach is not very big. The coast of Alanya has a horseshoe-shaped shape, divided by rocky capes into small bays. In one of them is the beach of Club Hotel Anjelique 4 *. There is also a bar. Swimmers should not worry about anything: mattresses, umbrellas, towels - all this, according to the all-inclusive concept, is provided free of charge.



Entertainment

After swimming, it's time for entertainment. Table tennis, water rides on the beach, an excellent pool, darts - this is not the whole list of the proposed pastime. And after visiting the hammam and massage room, fatigue and stress will go far.

In the evening, guests are offered animations with entertaining shows. And if there is a desire to spend the night outside the hotel, then for ten dollars the shuttle will take tourists to elite night clubs and provide their protection.

Those who combine relaxation with sightseeing are recommended to visit the largest Turkish marine park, as well as the Byzantine Ich-Kale - a fortress located on a hill towering above the city.

A stay at Club Hotel Anjelique 4, reviews of which almost all are only positive, was remembered by many tourists. Quite a decent hotel - that is how the Russians characterize it. A lot of food, nobody has ever left the restaurant hungry. Clean pools, great sand on the beach. The staff is attentive and helpful. There is a Russian-speaking administrator.
