Chinese iPhone 6 on Android: reviews, descriptions, specifications

How do they do it?

Overview of one of the copies

In order to demonstrate how significant the difference between the copy and the original is, we will conduct a short review. Of course, there is more than one developer company, in the model line of which there is a Chinese iPhone 6 on Android. Customer reviews indicate at least a dozen manufacturers producing copies. It will not be possible to trace the differences between all due to the large amount of information that would need to be collected.





Therefore, we will take one of the most famous fakes - Lenovo S90. Of course, the company does not indicate that we have before us a Chinese copy of iPhone 6. However, you do not need to be an expert to not notice a very high level of similarity between phones in the general features of the smartphone, its body and size, color design.

Moreover, the device from Lenovo, most likely, will have more or less acceptable build quality. Indeed, in comparison with some noname models (and they are included once in a while), this product has official support conducted by Lenovo specialists.

Case Materials

So, for starters, consider the metal from which our duplicate is made, and compare it with what a real iPhone will demonstrate. Manufacturers from the Middle Kingdom claim that aluminum alloy is also used here (which is identical in characteristics to the material on the iPhone 6). However, the Chinese iPhone 6 on Android has not passed practical tests (reviews will confirm this). Scratching an Apple product is not so easy; whereas in the case of Lenovo, the situation is different - the top layer of paint on the case comes off very easily, resulting in a bare metal texture. This is impractical, especially if you wear a smartphone without a case or bumper.

The dimensions of the S90 are slightly larger than the original, but it‚Äôs not so easy to notice, without having an ‚Äúapple‚Äù smartphone on hand.

Screen





The displays of the models mentioned in the review also have a lot of differences. If the one installed on the original has not only the ability to transmit a wide range of colors at maximum brightness, but also a high level of protection, then the same cannot be said about Lenovo. The latter looks, however, not bad, but in practice the screen is easily scratched. And this was shown not only by our review describing the Chinese iPhone 6 on Android. The reviews of people who used the smartphone for a certain period of time confirmed this. The strength of the glass of the iPhone and its fakes simply can not be compared. It‚Äôs impossible to say for certain what the reason for this is the high technological coverage of the Apple display or, on the contrary, the oversight of Chinese manufacturers. Performance

Devices manufactured by Apple are known for their speed and virtually perfect response. Despite the fact that the iPhone 6 smartphone is not equipped with the most powerful processor available in the technological world today, together with a correctly optimized operating system, it can show impressive results, while maintaining a normal level of charge and not overheating.





A slightly different picture can be observed in the case of the Lenovo S90 (despite the fact that this is actually the best Chinese iPhone 6). The device does not slow down and does not freeze - for this, its Qualcomm 410 processor with 4 cores with a clock frequency of up to 1.3 GHz is too powerful. No, we are talking about how the smartphone as a whole shows itself: in the process of work, it can significantly heat up, because of which the battery level drops quickly. According to some reviews, the firmware of the Chinese iPhone 6 to a more recent version of Android in some cases can save the situation - but you should not really count on it.

Camera

Another key indicator of the quality of a smartphone is, of course, the pictures taken with its camera. In the case of the original iPhone, there can be no complaints - the device is known for its excellent photos and videos, which can be obtained even in low light conditions.

The camera on the Lenovo S90 cannot be called bad - it is a worthy example when it comes to Android smartphones. But alas, the Chinese iPhone 6 on Android (the reviews of which we will publish below) should have the best hardware capable of more. However, we did not notice this. In excellent conditions, high-quality photographs are obtained, but everything changes in a poorly lit room. Honestly, not even a flash saves.

Many of them praise the design of the device (‚Äúlicked‚Äù from the Apple product), the way it lies in the hand and behaves in practice. Price

Well, about the cost of devices you need to conduct a separate conversation. The purpose of buying a fake is to save. As a rule, people who purchase a copy do not want to pay for the original device.





The price for the Chinese iPhone 6 we are describing, however, is not so low - it is about 17 thousand rubles. For that kind of money, you can find the original, albeit second-hand, phone of an older version. The new iPhone 6 will cost 3 times more.

For those who are looking for an even more budget option, fakes are available for 10 and even for 7 thousand rubles. Finding them is not difficult - only they, of course, will demonstrate even lower quality work and even a big difference with the original than the described Lenovo model.

conclusions

Of course, this is a personal matter for everyone which smartphone to buy next. For some, it should basically be similar to Apple, while other buyers prefer just a high-quality and functional smartphone.





In our opinion, it is better to take a high-quality, inexpensive and with optimal characteristics device from a well-known manufacturer than to splurge with a copy of a top-class branded smartphone that is not able to demonstrate the same functionality. Yes, and you need to understand that the principle by which the Chinese iPhone 6 is made is cheap and cheerful.
