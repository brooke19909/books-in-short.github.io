Hotel EL Kantaoui Center 4 * (Tunisia, Sousse, Port El Kantaoui): photos and reviews

Wanting to spend an unforgettable vacation in exotic countries on other continents, many stay in Tunisia. This country attracts travelers with its rich history and the ability not only to soak in the waters of the Mediterranean Sea, but also to visit a number of outstanding sights - the mysteries of mankind. The small town of Port El Kantaoui is a compact, relatively young resort, which is famous for its unique Arabian architecture, golden beaches and clear sea waters. Rest here is amazingly versatile: you can spend time in a noisy nightclub or hide from the bustle of megacities in a cozy bay.

The hotel complex EL Kantaoui Center, according to the regulars of this institution, rightfully has four stars, offering guests a high-level service, comfortable rooms and a wide range of various entertainments. This place is perfect for families with children or friends. The mild subtropical climate led to a year-round influx of tourists to this institution. The presence of a short warm winter is not a reason to refuse a trip to these parts.

general characteristics

The hotel was opened at the beginning of the third millennium - 2002. It was at this time in the summer that the first vacationers arrived here for accommodation. Over the period of its existence, the hotel was subjected to a number of cosmetic repairs and upgrades, the last of which is in 2014. On a well-groomed territory of 32 thousand square meters is the main six-story building, equipped with elevators for the convenience of guests.

Once in the building, many say that they have the feeling that they crossed the threshold of an exquisite castle, decorated in a national style. Power columns, a small fountain, flowers in flowerpots and a luxurious crystal chandelier meet new arrivals. In the cozy courtyard there is an aquazone and a terrace for sunbathing. Those who find themselves here once will certainly come back here again.

Location

One of the significant advantages of this institution over other similar ones is its location. The hotel complex is located in Sousse, 20 km from the resort town of Monastir. The distance to the capital is 145 km, but the path to the nearest airport will not take more than an hour. The district has a well-developed infrastructure. El Kantaoui Golf Course is 1 km away. Not only children, but also adults can have fun in the water park and in the amusement park Hannibal Park, which are 400 meters away from the hotel. Elite alcoholic beverages can be purchased at the General store, which is located within walking distance.

The evening can be spent at the Golf Brau restaurant, which serves delicious beer, or visit one of the most driving discos in the Bora-Bora resort. To get to the beach area, you need to overcome a distance of 150 meters by crossing the roadway. Such closeness to the coast is to the liking of the guests, which is noted in their numerous reviews of the hotel EL Kantaoui Center 4 * (Tunisia).

Check In Rules

Accommodation with pets, according to the rules of the internal schedule, is unacceptable. It is strictly forbidden to smoke in the rooms. Check-in starts at 14:00. Possible accelerated check-in and late check-out. The staff is attentive and polite, greets each guest with a smile on his face and is ready to help at any moment. All employees are fluent in English, French and Spanish, which does not create barriers in communicating with tourists. When paying, it is possible to use cards of international banking systems.

Number of rooms

To check in at Hotel EL Kantaoui Center, 347 superior apartments are offered, which differ in views and area that can be opened from the window. The following room categories are distinguished:

  1. Standard Room.
  2. Studio
  3. Apartment.

The smallest of them - standard and studio - have an area of ​​up to 30 square meters. The maximum possible number of people to stay here is 3 people. The apartment class rooms can accommodate 4 people at once. Their area reaches 80 squares, and there are two rooms separated by an interior door.

Interior and service

The interior of the apartment is presented in a classic style. A modern furniture set, full technical equipment and cleanliness are what awaits vacationers who decide to spend their holidays at the EL Kantaoui Center hotel. Each element of decor successfully fits into the overall style of design. The walls are hung with paintings, on the tables and coffee tables the guests are delighted with fresh flowers. All this creates a feeling of home warmth and comfort and makes it possible to spend an unforgettable vacation. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles and polished to a shine. The room is filled with natural light due to the presence of panoramic windows. The color palette is dominated by soft colors, which contributes to relaxation and restful sleep.

There is access to a balcony or terrace equipped with plastic furniture. Here you can spend a romantic evening admiring the stars, or enjoy socializing in the company of friends.

Conscientious maids are in charge of maintaining order, maintaining cleanliness and updating towels. Linen is changed three times a week. For those who like to sleep, the “wake-up call” service is available, according to which responsible employees will wake guests at the appointed time. There is a service Room Service, which requires additional payments upon delivery. In general, as guests of the reviews of EL Kantaoui Center note, each employee makes every effort to ensure that their guests can feel at home and their vacation was not spoiled by minor troubles.

Arrangement

New furniture, represented by a comfortable bed, soft sofas, a spacious wardrobe and bedside tables will allow not only to fully relax after a busy day, but also to get aesthetic pleasure thanks to the unique design and environmentally friendly materials from which it is made. There is an individually controlled air conditioner that allows you to adjust the temperature regime for each individual. You can solve the issues with the reception staff, the communication with which is carried out by phone.

Some apartments have a kitchen area for preparing delicious meals of their own authorship. The minibar is replenished with free drinking water and other soft drinks. The presence of a TV will brighten up the evening and stay up to date with all the events due to its multi-channel. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

The bathroom is tiled. It is equipped with a sink, shower, large mirror and hairdryer. Here is a set of shower and wash products, white towels and bathrobes.

Supply system

The hotel has an all-inclusive system, according to which guests are offered four meals a day, organized in the form of a buffet. The atmosphere of the main restaurant is surprisingly comfortable and calm. Here you can enjoy your meal and chat with neighbors on the table to the sounds of live music. As guests note in their many positive reviews about EL Kantaoui Center (Tunisia), the abundance of products offered for consumption and their amazing taste characteristics will undoubtedly satisfy the hunger of even the most avid gourmet.

Beach

The hotel is located 150 meters from the Mediterranean coast. Vacationers note that, despite the significant narrowness of the coastline, the local infrastructure is incredibly developed. There are deck chairs with mattresses to get a dark tan. For those who want to hide from direct sunlight, places under shadow umbrellas are offered. The safety of the swimmers is tirelessly monitored by the rescue service. A large number of water attractions are available here for absolutely everyone. The most popular, according to experienced, are windsurfing, scooter and skiing, as well as parasailing.

Aquazone

There is a swimming pool in the courtyard of the hotel complex, the water of which is heated by the surrounding heat. There is also a children's aquazone where kids like to frolic throughout the day. For swimming in the winter months, there is an indoor pool with a jacuzzi, which is equipped with a water heating system.

Children's leisure

In the hotel you can spend not only a romantic weekend, but also come to rest with your children. There is everything necessary for this. Cots for babies are available on request. The restaurants have high chairs. In addition, the children are offered a children's menu, the dishes of which allow them to replenish their supply of strength and get enough of it. At the hotel, the doors of the children's room are open for all young guests. Here they can play with toys, watch cartoons or draw.

For parents who want their child to be individually looked after, babysitting services are available. A qualified teacher will take the kid's leisure with fun games, fun activities and cognitive tasks. A good mood and a sea of ​​smiles are guaranteed for every child.

Entertainment

Every traveler will spend an amazingly varied and rich holiday. Here you can check accuracy by playing darts or billiards. When using the services of the tour desk, acquaintance with local attractions and natural beauties will be organized at the highest level.

For those who wish to improve their health, you can head to the spa. Wellness procedures will replenish the reserve of lost strength, rejuvenate the body and give a good mood. A massage room is a paradise where you can experience a feeling of genuine relaxation and enjoyment. A gym equipped with new fitness equipment and dumbbells is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. There are daily aerobics classes.

The evening ends with a delightful show of the animation team and a disco, the entrance to which is free.

Additional services

In order to take care of guests, the EL Kantaoui Center hotel complex has created the maximum possible range of related services. For those arriving by plane, a paid shuttle service is provided. Drivers will deliver passengers to both sides of the airport quickly and comfortably. For those who arrived by car or rented it, it is proposed to leave the vehicle at a free guarded parking lot. An Internet cafe is open 24 hours a day and allows everyone to stay in touch.

There is a well-equipped conference room on site. Here you can hold a business event of any format at the European level. A cozy atmosphere, modern design and comfortable furniture are conducive to making important decisions.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are paid upon. A doctor is called upon request. A currency exchange and a tour desk are located next to the reception.

As you can see, EL Kantaoui Center 4 * (Tunisia) receives mostly positive reviews. Many promise to come here again and be sure to recommend the hotel complex to their friends and acquaintances.
