Home master class: how to sew without a dress pattern

Sewing without a pattern is quite possible. For this, of course, you need to be friends with a thread-needle, scissors, it is desirable to have a sewing machine and other tools of a tailor's set.

If you do not plan to make a mini and strictly close-fitting, then for a regular dress of medium length you need from 2.5 to 3 meters of matter. Plus, from one and a half to 2 meters of fittings for decoration, if any, it is planned (referring to different braid, color lacing, lace, etc.). Dress with a full skirt



Another interesting option for a home-made outfit is a dress with a full skirt. We sew a dress without a pattern as well as the first, only it consists of a bodice and a skirt. The bodice is also without shoulders. Take 3 meters of silk. Cut 1 meter, fold in half and sew the bodice from top to bottom. Do the same with the second cut, laid back on the skirt. Now measure one and a half centimeters at the top of the bodice, lay the material and hem (there you put in an elastic band so that it does not slip from the chest), and at the top of the skirt - also for elastic. Combine both fragments of the dress, retract the elastic bands and put on. Happened? Then iron well and enjoy your new outfit.
