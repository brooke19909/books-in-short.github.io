Types and conditions of deposits at Sberbank

Everyone chooses a contribution to suit their needs. One needs to accumulate, the other put and periodically use. Everyone pursues different goals. Therefore, banks offer a very wide selection of deposits.

Many trust their funds to Sberbank. He is the most famous among credit institutions. Anyone will like the terms of deposits at Sberbank, so we will talk about it further.

Types of deposits issued in the office

Conventionally, they can be divided according to their purpose. Let's consider each in more detail.

  • Deposit "Save."

Basically, it is drawn up to save funds for a while. From this, the term of the deposit is selected. The percentage depends on the term, the maximum - 5%. The smallest installment is from 1 thousand rubles. Do not close it ahead of schedule, as all accrued interest will be lost.

  • Deposit "Replenish".

A very attractive deposit in Sberbank, especially for the accumulation of funds, for example, for a major purchase or for a down payment on a mortgage. Here operations to replenish the deposit are allowed, but the ability to make a partial expense is limited. It is possible to open it in rubles and dollars. The difference is only in percentage, the maximum here is 4.55%. The minimum deposit is from 1 thousand rubles. You can issue for any period up to 3 years. With early closure or partial withdrawal of funds, all interest is also lost.

  • Deposit "Manage".

It is necessary if a person wants to freely manage their funds, but at the same time receive more interest. Possible income and expense. The smallest installment is slightly higher - from 30 thousand rubles. or 1 thousand dollars. Validity - from three months to three years. Depending on it, there will be a percentage, but a maximum of 4.25%.

  • Deposit "Social".

It receives periodic credits to orphans and war veterans. It does not open to everyone. Documents confirming the receipt of these payments are required. Conditions of deposit in Sberbank allow you to make a receipt and expenditure of funds. Percentage - 4.25%. The term is three years. The smallest installment is from one ruble.

  • Deposit "Replenish" on a minor child.

A great way to save money for a child's majority. Anyone, even grandparents, can deposit funds on this deposit. Partial spending is not possible here. Percentage - 4.55%. You can open an account in rubles and dollars.

Retirement deposits

Sberbank deposit conditions for pensioners are more attractive. They have the opportunity to issue both all of the above deposits and a pension contribution created specifically for this category.
