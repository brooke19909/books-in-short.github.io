Amusement parks in Dubai: description and reviews

Dubai is a resort city in the UAE, which every year is visited by a huge number of tourists from different parts of the world. It attracts travelers with a large number and variety of entertainments that are interesting to both adults and children. Tourists pay special attention to the amusement parks that are available in this metropolis. They can be visited by the whole family.

So, which amusement parks in Dubai are the most popular and interesting?

Motiongate

A huge number of tourists prefer to visit this particular place. This is the best amusement park in Dubai, which is fully decorated in the style of Hollywood films and famous cartoons: "Shrek", "The Hunger Games", "Ghostbusters", "Step Forward" and many others. If desired, his guests can be photographed with a huge number of animators who are dressed in costumes of famous characters.

Another feature of this park is the presence on its territory of a large film set on which scenes of famous films are filmed. Very often, tourists try to enter its territory and observe the process of creating a new cinema masterpiece.

In addition, the Motiongate Park has a huge number of attractions that will find their fans among both adults and children of all ages. Many of them, according to travelers, are truly breathtaking.



Bollywood parks dubai

Undoubtedly, real fans of Indian films will be happy to be here, as on the site of this amusement park in Dubai there are characters from the famous Bollywood films. Having visited this place, guests may encounter figures of famous artists who are famous in the vastness of Europe, as well as cricket stars.

Legoland dubai

The best attractions in Dubai, according to a large number of travelers, are in the huge Legoland Dubai amusement park. Here two separate worlds are harmoniously connected: one of them is intended for children, and the second for adults. Having visited this place, children can start building their own lego car, which can later be taken for a ride. Any child in Legoland Dubai has the opportunity to feel truly grown up, choosing a profession and completing a series of quests, depending on the chosen industry.



This amusement park is also famous for the fact that its designs are a real water park. In total, there are 25 of them, and a stream of water flows through each element of the park, carrying away entertaining people. Visitors to this place consider it rather unusual and beautiful, which is often shared in their reviews left on social networks under photos taken during the holiday. Many tourists also like the fact that children under the age of 4 can visit it for free.



Family Entertainment Center

Where is the best amusement park in Dubai? At Wonderland! This place is called Family Entertainment Center. The name of the park already says that it is intended for a family vacation, which pleases travelers who want to have fun with the whole family, while on vacation in the UAE.

On the first tier of the complex are the most exciting rides, and on its second floor you can find a huge hall in which video game machines are installed. Tourists often admit that, having visited such a place, neither adults nor children want to leave it. That is why it very often happens that travelers come here several times for the entire time of rest.

Hili fun city

This small in size amusement park in Dubai also attracts the attention of travelers from various cities of the world. It is located in one of the areas of the large metropolis of Al Ain, where a large number of tourists live.

On the territory of the place for entertainment, whose area is 22 hectares, there are 30 attractions, which are more likely suitable for teenage children. Travelers very often visit souvenir shops, as well as catering establishments, where you can restore your expended strength and energy in order to move to new adventures.

A bright train runs through the territory of Hili Fun City, with which you can easily get to the attraction of interest.



Sega republic

Often, in their comments, tourists note that visiting such a complex will be interesting to fans of the Sega game, but this is far from the case. Across the territory of the park, you can observe the attributes typical of famous games, which can attract not only fans of video games, but even those who do not know what it is.

In the Sega Republic park, you can meet the legendary Sonic, who has a considerable number of fans around the world.

All the attractions that are available here are created based on the most famous games, which allows guests of the park to feel like their heroes in real time. In Sega Republic there is a large laser maze, a large circuit. Let's Go Jungle - entertainment only for the most daring guests who are not afraid of monsters. Special attention of young tourists is drawn to the Halfpipe Canyon snowboard, long known to the world from Sega games.

Children's city

A city in which only children live - that's what the new Dubai amusement park is called. It is quite unique in nature, since here you can not only have fun having a ride on extreme installations, but also learn a lot about the world of dinosaurs. In addition, there is a small planetarium in the territory of Children's City, which can simultaneously be visited by up to hundreds of guests.



Parents who bring their kids to such a park are sure that it is here that they perfectly understand all the teachers and instructors who tell about interesting facts. All this is due to the fact that information is provided not only through words, but also through the interaction of the child with beautiful and functional exhibits. According to tourists, here a child can develop well, communicating with his peers, while having fun on fast carousels.
