Where is the hotel Sivila Hotel Afandou 3 * located (Rhodes)

At the northeastern tip of the Greek island there is a small resort village of Afandou. He is famous for two things. First: the longest beach of Rhodes and, as reviews add, the most spacious. Even in the midst of the holiday season, there is no feeling of crowding. And the second attraction of Afandou is the largest golf course on the island. Here in this village, not far from its center, on the third line from the sea, the Sivila Hotel 3 * is located.

Rhodes (the capital of the island where the international airport is located) is only twenty-two kilometers from the hotel. Another village with a beautiful beach - Kolymbia - is located two kilometers away. The nearest town, Faliraki, is just 5 km away. In the summer, a small free "train" goes to the famous Afandou beach, with a stop in the most key sights of the village. But four hundred meters can be easily overcome on foot in five to seven minutes. The hotel is located one and a half kilometers from the center of the village of Afandou and is surrounded by its tourist infrastructure.



Territory, buildings

All the reviews of tourists suggest that the territory of the three-star Hotel Civil is small. Nevertheless, there is room for parking, which is at the free disposal of guests, and a playground, and even a functioning Orthodox chapel. The hotel is a two-story building. In its basement there is a reception and a restaurant with a small outdoor terrace. Having passed a large bright, stylishly decorated hall, you find yourself on a platform from where a staircase and ramp lead to the pool. It has a sun terrace: umbrellas are sunny and sunny on one side, and a canopy on the other. There is also a small pool bar. A beautiful snow-white building with balconies was built in the ninety-fifth year of the last century. And the last reconstruction it took place in 2012.



Number of rooms

The two-story building of the Sivila Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) accommodates fifty-six guest rooms. They all have balconies - even those few rooms that are located in the basement level of the building. The hotel has two categories of premises. The most demanded are standards. These rooms are designed for comfortable accommodation of two guests. The standard area is twenty-five square meters. Guests can choose one twin bed or two single beds.

A more expensive room category is a studio. This room can accommodate even three guests, because the bedroom has a folding sofa. A characteristic feature of the studio, which tourists described as a virtue, is the kitchen corner. Those guests who paid for All Inclusive may not need it, but for those who had half board, the opportunity to cook their own meals was useful.

Excursions and independent trips from the hotel Sivila Hotel 3 * (Greece, Rhodes)

Afandou is a very ancient village. Previously, it was located near the sea, but the pirates, who attacked coastal settlements for the sake of the slave trade, forced residents to settle deep in the mainland. It is worth visiting the village church dedicated to the Mother of God.

But Kolymbia was founded in the thirties of the last century, when the Italian fascists captured Rhodes. The attraction of this village is the alley of eucalyptus trees that were planted in honor of the arrival of Mussolini.

A trip to Archangelos, a small village famous for carpets and handmade ceramics, will be interesting. For nature lovers, tourists recommend an excursion to Epta-Piges - Valley of Seven Springs. If you walk barefoot through the tunnel where these cold waters flow, you can throw off a dozen years at once. And the convenient location of Afandou between the capital of the island of Rhodes and the city of Lindos makes it convenient to travel independently to antiquities.
