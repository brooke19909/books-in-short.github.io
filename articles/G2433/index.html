In Russian, the following styles and types of speech are distinguished: journalistic, business, artistic, scientific and conversational. They all differ in the construction of sentences and the selection of words.

Most words are neutral in color and can be included in all types of speech. But some tend to give a special touch to what is said or written. Such words cannot use all types of speech, they are characteristic of only one style: either conversational (dominate, balamute, casual, cook, quickie), or artistic (greedy, landscape, subject).

The conversational style is used in oral communication between people in everyday life, in family and friendly relations, as well as in literary works in the form of direct speech. It is used to exchange thoughts and information with loved ones. It is carried out in the form of conversations, dialogues, notes, letters. The characteristic features of the colloquial type of speech: evaluative nature, emotionality, ease, sometimes lack of logic. This style is characterized by the use of colloquial, emotionally-evaluative words, phraseological units. Also, the colloquial type of speech is characterized by the construction of simple sentences (including exclamation, one-part and incomplete), the frequent use of calls, interjections.

The scope of the scientific style is technology, science, the educational process. It is used to communicate achievements in the field of latest technologies and discoveries. A scientific style is being implemented in the form of reports, speeches, textbooks, lectures, books, disputes. Its characteristic features: dry speech, logic, accuracy, abstractness and evidence. The vocabulary of this style is dominated by words with abstract meaning, nouns formed from verbs, and terms.

It is implemented in the form of contracts, business papers (statements, certificates, orders, reports, protocols, etc.). Characteristic features of a business type of speech: dryness, severity and formality.

The artistic style is used accordingly in fiction with the aim of influencing the reader by verbal depiction of life. It is realized in the form of novels, poems, short stories, short stories, essays, short stories and plays. Features inherent in the artistic type of speech: its evaluative nature, emotionality, concreteness and imagery.

The scope of the journalistic style is politics and society. It is used for the purpose of influencing the masses or providing information about important events and affairs for society. The journalistic style is realized in the form of brochures, disputes, lectures, articles, reports and speeches. Its typical features: passion, invocation, evaluative character.
