"Vasilsursky" rest house in the Nizhny Novgorod region: photos, reviews

The Vasilsursky holiday home is a life-giving corner on the banks of the famous Volga River. Geographically located in the Nizhny Novgorod region, Vorotynsky district, the village of Vasilsursk. Residential buildings are built on a hill with a panoramic view of the Cheboksary reservoir.

One of the main advantages of the camp site is the presence of mixed forest and flowering shrubs. Due to the abundance of greenery, the air is oversaturated with negative ions and volatile, beneficial to human health. Well-groomed walking alleys lie among the forest. This area is truly unique and has won the fame of an environmentally friendly village.





Significant remoteness from the metropolis, industrial plants and the highway contributes to the restoration of strength and spiritual harmony. On holidays and weekends, the Vasilsursky holiday home is crowded with city dwellers craving privacy with nature. The period of operation is year-round.

Number of rooms

Rest house "Vasilsursky" (Nizhny Novgorod region) invites people to spend a fabulous weekend. The housing stock is represented by comfortable rooms, which are concentrated in a two-story building. It offers two-room apartments equipped with modern furniture, household utensils and appliances. Hot and cold water is supplied regularly.





Also, cottages made of logs, upholstered in wood, with a capacity for up to four people, are built for living. All houses are connected to water and electricity. In winter, heating is provided. The interior is decorated in a simple laconic style. The houses have a TV, fridge, armchairs, beds. A bathroom with a small bath and a toilet is allocated.
