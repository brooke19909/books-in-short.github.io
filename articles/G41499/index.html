Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Oludeniz): description, service, reviews

Oludeniz in Turkey is one of the most picturesque areas. There is a large number of hotels designed for guests of various levels. One of the options for budget hotels located in this city is Marcan Resort Hotel 4 *. It is often recommended to many guests, in particular, those who wish to relax with the whole family.



general information

The hotel was built in 2012. Thanks to the recent construction, the building has very modern and beautiful buildings, they are technically well equipped. Here, various works are periodically carried out to maintain the general condition of not only the premises, but also the territory as a whole, whose area is about 9500 sq. m

According to the description, the hotel is perfect for families with children.

Location

Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * is located in the Fethiye area, which by its nature is very picturesque. A huge number of tropical plants grow here, which surprise Russian tourists with their unusual appearance. A couple of kilometers from the hotel is the nearest village, whose name is Hisaronu. The nearest Mediterranean coast is quite far from the hotel - 800 meters.

If we talk about transport routes, the nearest major air hub is 45 kilometers from Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * - this is where the journey of every tourist arriving from Russia begins, for which the hotel organizes a transfer at the client‚Äôs expense - its cost is included in the standard package. According to vacationers, the guest is transported by a comfortable car, which is equipped with air conditioning and an excellent cabin - so travel time is almost imperceptible.

Rooms

In total, the hotel has 131 rooms, each of which is designed for a comfortable stay of guests of different age groups. All of them, depending on the purpose, are divided into two groups: "standard" and "family".

All rooms have a private bathroom, where there is a full set of modern sanitary ware. There is also a small furniture set, represented by two cabinets that act as surfaces. They are waiting for guests neatly folded bathrobes, terry towels, as well as slippers. In addition, for all guests one set of bath accessories is provided, which includes items that are needed for a shower.

Each room has climate control equipment, which is regulated by central air conditioning. With it, tourists can enjoy the cool even on very hot days.



"Standard"

The apartments of the Standard group occupy the largest niche in the entire number of rooms of the Marcan Resort Hotel 4 *. Their description says that each room has a pretty nice, modern interior, which uses only modern furniture. They are located on a small area of ‚Äã‚Äã28 square meters. m and are designed for a couple of guests. Often these apartments are chosen by guests who come here to spend their honeymoon or just relax together.

The central place in each room is occupied by a large double bed with orthopedic mattress, which is covered with expensive and beautiful linen. A large elongated mirror is installed above it, which visually expands the space. The guests can put their personal belongings in small cabinets, which are located on both sides of the bed, as well as in a wardrobe, on the surface of which there is a large plasma TV. The apartments have satellite TV, which allows tourists from Russia to watch their favorite shows on Russian-language channels. Reviews of Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * often say that the interior of the rooms uses an original, but very harmonious combination of burgundy, beige and white colors, which set up an excellent vacation and a pleasant pastime. The dim light in the bedroom creates an atmosphere of intimacy and privacy from the whole world. Guests also enjoy wonderful sea views from the windows of the apartments.



"Family"

The comments left by residents in the rooms of the "family" group say that they are perfectly designed to accommodate guests with small children and adolescents. They are located on a larger territory - 35 square meters. m and are intended for four guests and one child, for which a bed is provided in the form of a bed type "playpen".

The territory of the room is presented in one room, where, in addition to a large berth in the form of a French bed, there is a folding sofa, also designed for two. In the furniture package there is also a pair of chairs with soft seats and a round table for relaxing - tourists say that it is very convenient to read books and conduct evening conversations.

Additional equipment for the room‚Äôs furniture includes an electronic safe, a telephone and a minibar, the range of which is regularly updated.



Conditions for children

Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * (Fethiye) is very popular among families who prefer to relax with the whole family, along with young children and adolescents. All this is directly related to the fact that on its territory and in the buildings there are all the conditions that are needed for a safer and more comfortable stay for kids.

First of all, in the hotel room stock there are several apartments that are designed for family living - in them comfortable beds can be installed on a free basis. As for amenities in the open area, they are represented by a small pool with automatic heating function, as well as with a special coating, thanks to which it is impossible to fall. Not far from it is a decent size, well-equipped area for active pastime of small guests.

In their reviews, tourists note that the Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * (Fethiye) is located in a very quiet area, away from the noisy city and entertainment infrastructure, which favorably affects children without annoying them.



Entertainment

The hotel has a small animation team, which includes only two people. However, despite such a small composition, they are quite able to maintain a general atmosphere among the guests. Russian tourists who rest in the Marcan Resort (Turkey / Oludeniz) note one inconvenience. It is due to the fact that animators speak only English, which some do not understand.

However, if you evaluate in general, the atmosphere in the hotel is quite fun. Here, every evening in a separate bar, parties are held at which famous hits by foreign performers are played. In the daytime, sports activities are held at the Marcan Resort Hotel 4 *, represented by relay races and small competitions.

Among the guests are very popular excursion tours organized by the hotel, which are conducted by professional guides. Travel destinations are usually the most interesting sights.

Nutrition

The hotel has one large restaurant where a buffet is served daily for guests who are staying in an all-inclusive concept. It offers an abundance of the best dishes prepared in the best traditions of Turkish cuisine. The restaurant has a specific work schedule - you can visit it from 8 am to midnight. In addition to it, there is also a bar located near the pool. His opening hours are from 10 am to midnight. The bar menu offers a wide cocktail menu, where there is an excellent selection of unusual cocktails and soft drinks. You can also try original snacks and desserts in it, the taste qualities of which are often praised by vacationers.

Guests of Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * often emphasize that the menu of catering facilities has a number of vegetarian dishes, which, from their point of view, is a positive point.



SPA

A spa center is essential in the lives of guests who prefer to attend relaxing treatments. According to them, real masters work here who know exactly how to immerse any client in a state of complete relaxation.

Here, an excellent thermal zone created from a sauna and a classic hammam is presented to guests. There is a team of parmeisters who know a lot about soaring. For a fee, you can visit the masseur, who works in a separate room.

Sport

Service Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * provides for a number of conditions for guests who lead a healthy lifestyle and prefer to follow their figure. For them, the hotel has an excellent gym, where several units are installed. You can visit this place only for a fee. Tourists can visit the table tennis room and the billiard complex for free.

If desired, guests can rent a bicycle in order to go sightseeing on their own. Some travelers recommend using this type of transport for trips to the sea.

Beach

Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * is located quite far from the beach line and does not have its own plot on it. Guests of this vacation spot often visit the city beach, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is covered with sand and small pebbles. If desired, tourists can get to it, using the services of a shuttle bus, which takes and picks up people for free - this is a big plus for vacationers.

Here, if desired, tourists have the opportunity to take sun loungers, air mattresses and umbrellas, paying a small rental fee for them. No one provides towels here, so tourists recommend taking them with you.

Pool

The Marcan Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey) has a very large aquazone, which perfectly compensates for the lack of a beach within walking distance of the hotel. It is represented by a pool filled with crystal clear water - its area is 1500 square meters. m. A small minus is that it does not have an automated water heating system, which is why in winter it does not warm up to the required level. However, there is an excellent cleaning system.

For active pastime of guests in the pool there are two slides for adults. They can be visited only at certain times (the schedule can be specified on the spot).



Around the aquazone there is an excellent sun terrace, which contains in its territory a large number of comfortable sun loungers, where you can sunbathe while relaxing. A folding umbrella is installed above each of them, which at the right time will protect the vacationer from direct sunlight.
