Stationary computers and laptops have entered our lives relatively recently, but along with mobile phones have firmly taken their place there. New development





More recently, namely in 2010, a new device appeared on the computer market - a tablet computer, or just a tablet. It very quickly became widespread among users due to its compact size and lower cost compared to laptops and desktop computers. On the tablet it is very convenient to watch videos, listen to music, read books in electronic form, communicate on social networks. But working on it, writing texts is difficult and inconvenient due to the lack of a keyboard and mouse. The advantage of the tablet is also lower energy consumption compared to a laptop. But still it has to be charged. There are no problems when there is a charger nearby, and if it is missing, then the question arises of how to charge the tablet without charging?





How to charge?

You can use a USB cable. Well, each user of various gadgets probably knows how to charge the tablet via USB. It is enough to have this cable with the appropriate connector and a desktop computer on hand. It should be noted that charging the tablet through USB is much slower than through the outlet, because the voltage and maximum charging current in the computer are much less. The tablet can be charged in this way from a laptop. Charging from a laptop follows the same principle.

Charging rules

It was previously believed that for high-quality operation of the device it is necessary to completely discharge the battery before the first charge. Currently, tablets are equipped with a new type of battery . And before using the tablet for the first time, you need to fully charge it. In order for the tablet‚Äôs battery to last longer, it needs to be recharged more often and not allowed to be completely discharged. To recharge the battery, the above-described method of how to charge the tablet from the computer is also suitable. But there are times when there is no USB cable either. And again, the problem arises of how to charge the tablet without charging. In this case, you can use a cigarette lighter. Here you will need a car charger for the tablet. On the one hand, in such a device the connector is adapted to the cigarette lighter of the car, on the other - USB. It can be bought at any electronics store. Before charging in this way, first of all, make sure that the current strength is sufficient . Then connect the cable to the tablet computer and to the car. This method is only suitable for recharging a seated battery.

But in this way it is better to use only in emergency cases. And in order to avoid such problems in the future at the most inopportune moment, it is better to purchase a spare battery and carry it with you.





A large number of options

There are chargers that allow you to charge your tablet computer from AA batteries (finger-type) using a USB cable. Radio amateurs can make such a device on their own, especially since everything you need can be purchased at a radio store.

How to extend the life of the tablet?

Some users believe that charging time depends on the operating system on which a particular tablet is running, but this is not at all. It all depends on the manufacturer of the gadget. So, for example, Chinese- made tablets are less capricious about charging conditions, and therefore there are less problems with them when using various charging methods.





To extend the life of the tablet‚Äôs battery, you must constantly monitor its charge level and prevent it from completely discharging. It is not recommended to leave the tablet turned on during the process.
