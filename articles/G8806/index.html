CASCO and car safety

If you want to insure your car, then you need to conclude a CASCO agreement. Unlike CTP, the company commits itself to cover the damage that third parties will cause to the car in cases specified in the contract.

AlfaStrakhovanie CASCO offers its customers three levels:

  • "50/50 +";
  • "business";
  • "all inclusive".

These levels differ in the set of risks included in the contract. The first program can be chosen by those who have a long driving experience and are confident that nothing serious will happen to his car.





The last level includes almost all the risks that could have been foreseen. If you want to feel financially secure in terms of a vehicle, then AlfaStrakhovanie CASCO All Inclusive has created just for you.

Such a distinction between CASCO levels helps to satisfy the demand of people with different material conditions. For clarity, the total cost of CASCO services, you can make a preliminary calculation on the website of AlfaStrakhovanie. Moscow is an expensive city, and insurance here also costs a lot. So, for a business class agreement with an average set of options, you will have to pay over 90,000 rubles.

This is not to say that it is cheap, but paying such an amount is better than constantly worrying that something could happen to the machine. The company also insures against theft. If something like this happens, the insurer is obliged to indemnify.
