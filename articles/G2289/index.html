Phuket, Patong. Map of Phuket (Patong) hotels

Phuket is a paradise island in southern Thailand. Its warm Andaman Sea attracts many tourists, who at the moment provide a flow of funds to Phuket. Patong is its largest beach, having won the fame of a party center. But this is not just a resort that can entertain people who are tired of the gray days of cold countries. It is also a place with a rich history.

Island history

Europeans became aware of Phuket in the Middle Ages. From the book of Claudius Ptolemy, they learned that sailors often have to go through a certain cape Chunk Silon, holding the path to the Malay Peninsula. This area has long been considered a transit point for merchants between China and India. Their hospitality and generosity provided an occasion to visit not only for geographical reasons. The main natural wealth of the island was tin. It was mined so much that by the 17th century a real war broke out between nations for the possibility of trade with Phuket. The first trading port was founded by the Dutch, and then the British and immigrants from Portugal. As a result, the influence of these countries spread so widely that the king of Ayutthaya, who ruled at that time, decided to alliance with France in order to gain hope for independence. By the end of the century, the French had gained the long-awaited monopoly on the extraction of tin, but this did not last forever, since the Siamese revolution took place. Looking at Patong, Phuket, the photo of which is presented in this article, it is difficult to imagine that it was not rest that was the primary concern of the islanders. The 18th century turned into a real nightmare for them, expressed in the Burmese invasion. The next century was marked for the island by a change in ethnic pattern: crowds of Chinese workers came to develop tin. All this time, Phuket was not considered a separate province, becoming such only at the beginning of the 20th century. Currently, the prosperity of the island is possible thanks to tourism and the production of rubber. Back in the 70s, a wonderful climate led to the formation of several resort areas on the island of Phuket: Patong, Kata and Karon.

Phuket Attractions

In addition to a beach holiday, it is worth highlighting this type of cultural entertainment, such as museums. The province is famous for its National Museum, an exhibition of sea shells and a mining department. Scuba diving takes an honorable second place in popularity after beach activities. The beauty of the deep sea and walks with their inhabitants do not leave tourists indifferent. Expeditions to the Similan Islands have long been considered the basis for diving enthusiasts.

Another great vacation on the island is golf. The territory of Phuket has completely changed by man to create some of the best golf courses. They meet the set standards so well that they even hold world competitions here. Some experts believe that soon Phuket Patong will share the level of popularity with golf. According to experts, every year this type of entertainment brings Thailand billions of dollars.

Patong Beach (Phuket ): Key Features of Thai Paradise

Patong is Phuket 's largest beach. This is a place full of life, fun and idleness. For a tourist who prefers peace and quiet, it will not work. This is a resort for active party-goers dreaming of plunging into the world of nightlife, parties and tropical cocktails. There are a lot of nightclubs, expensive restaurants, as well as hotels, which are famous throughout Thailand. Phuket, Patong and other beaches of the island can awaken the thirst for adventure for any visitor to the party. Dozens of different bars can be found on Bangla's main street. Non-alcoholic relaxation can be found in Patong massage parlors. The choice of the tourist will provide an erotic or therapeutic massage. And it can be done right on the seashore, on the beach itself. For fans of non-standard spectacles, cabarets with transvestites and strippers are always open.



Water sports

Water sports are one of the main advantages of places like Patong (Beach). Phuket - diver's paradise corner. Here you can always rent equipment and buy a card for swimming. Barracudas and turtles, sea snakes and unusual fish - all this is the underwater world of Thailand. A unique chance to live to see a whale shark or a rare manta ray is provided to Phuket fans. It is also possible to take a boat trip to other islands of Thailand. Jet skiing or parasailing are also popular.

Thailand. Phuket Patong Hotels

To choose a hotel in Phuket, you need to decide on its purpose. Since this is primarily a short-term accommodation (after all, most likely tourists will prefer to spend time on the beaches and other places of outdoor recreation), an inexpensive hotel may well be suitable. As mentioned above, hotels are what Phuket has recently been famous for. Patong provides 3 star hotels in an amount not dreamed of by other beach resorts. Despite such a number of stars, in any hotel in Patong, travelers will find a comfortable room, and even with air conditioning, without which life on the island would have seemed much more difficult.

For connoisseurs of good shopping, a significant number of hotels have been built near shopping centers. As soon as dusk falls on Patong, preceding the incendiary atmosphere of nightly dancing, it turns into a real market. Shopping street and all roads are lined with tents selling handicrafts. Such a souvenir will be the perfect gift for relatives.



Hotel Map

For better orientation, there is a map of Phuket hotels. Patong is depicted in great detail with all the major hotels. This card can be bought in bookstores, found in guidebooks, at the reception of the hotels themselves. As a rule, it depicts beach lines, hotel coordinates, main attractions and paths to other islands.

Hotels located in the third line are not necessarily far from the beach. So, many of them are located 15 minutes from it, and along the main street. Three star hotels can provide customers with transportation. Nearby, as a rule, gasoline is sold. This type of room has no frills, but all the basic amenities are provided: shower, beds, tables and even a small balcony. The room always has clean cups and tea. Sheets are regularly changed. Often there is also a small safe for storing especially valuable things. The cuisine is distinguished by the quality of fresh fruits, vegetables and fish. The helpfulness of the staff of economy class hotels amazes the townsfolk. The hotel always has a restaurant in which, as a rule, there is also live music.



The most comfortable hotels in Phuket

Studying the opinions of tourists who have visited Patong (Phuket), hotel reviews can be found very different. If a three-star hotel is enough for someone to plunge into the atmosphere of a country of parties and exotics, then some tourists will prefer expensive hotels that have an impeccable reputation, where everything is included. Such hotels can provide their residents with entertainment at one hundred percent. Without leaving the room, clients can appreciate the wonders of Thai massage, enjoy national cuisine, enjoy a delicious cocktail. The basement of five-star hotels has large pools, many of which are adjacent to aquariums, allowing you to appreciate the beauty of the local underwater world, without moving away from your place of residence. Prices in these hotels are much higher than in less "stellar" ones, but there are also much more services.

Large and luxury hotels are located in the center. Due to the number of night clubs around it can be very noisy. But if a tourist was looking for nightlife, such an arrangement would clearly play into his hands.

Unusual hotels of Patong

There is a boutique resort. This means that the hotel is in close proximity to the beach line (10-15 meters). In addition to their own pool, such establishments are also famous for their separate access to the room. The cost of a hotel of this level starts at 4,500 baht.



What else is Phuket famous for in the hotel business? Patong, whose hotels are located in a tropical garden, has become a true legend for tourists. In the territory, ranging in size from 10 to 12 acres of the most diverse vegetation, there is a luxurious multi-storey palace in which only those tourists who are willing to pay at least 5000 baht per day are allowed to live.



The most luxurious hotels

There are hotels in the north of Patong, which offer views and access to another famous beach - Kalim. Even in the rainy season, the price of a room in a hotel with such facilities does not fall below 6,000 baht. This is very prestigious for those vacationers who are important not only the comfort of their stay, but also the status of the selected hotel.



Studio-type apartments are another innovation for visitors to Phuket. Patong offers its guests villas or houses with such apartments for a price of 10,000 baht. The villa may have a separate garden, pool, balcony. The client will have both a jacuzzi and a shower. A transfer to various places of entertainment will certainly be included in the price.

Thus, every traveler can find exactly what he was looking for in Phuket. Hotels and hotels for every taste and wealth are a strong feature of relaxation in such a place.
