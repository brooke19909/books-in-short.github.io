Circus on the water in Krasnoyarsk: a fantastic world of mermaids and nymphs

When there is a circus in the city, how to spend leisure time with children is a matter of choosing the day and time for a trip to a bright and unforgettable performance. Children enjoy watching the performances of artists, whether people or animals, but the circus on the water is considered an even more exciting sight. In Krasnoyarsk, city dwellers and guests were lucky to see an enchanting show with the participation of "mermaids", "nymphs" and "unsinkable" clowns.

Water show

The circus on water (including in Krasnoyarsk) always causes curiosity among all age categories of spectators, and this is not surprising. Water is not the element in which a person feels himself the king of nature. He has to give primacy to the inhabitants of the seas and oceans.

The water show, which the circus group presented in Krasnoyarsk under the leadership of Andrey Shevchenko, is a fairy tale in which the spectators themselves are the heroes, traveling through the worlds with artists and getting into incredibly funny, colorful, and sometimes terrible adventures.

The circus on water in Krasnoyarsk has its own unique pool of almost 200 tons of water, a laser show, original music for each number and 60 talented artists who were loved by the audience of China, Israel, Italy, France, Hungary and Ukraine. So, what awaits the inhabitants of Krasnoyarsk in a circus on the water?

Presentation program

It is difficult to convey the impressions that this show evokes. The bright program involved:

  • Clowns and cute trained dogs.
  • Gymnasts performing tricks in the soap bubbles.
  • "Mermaids" performing a synchronous dance in the rays of laser illumination.
  • Chimpanzee, a kind of gentleman, showing an example of how to behave in a restaurant, boating and diving.
  • The highlight of the circus on the water in Krasnoyarsk was the California sea lions - talented animals who can not only show tricks, but also sing and dance.
  • Three pythons and a crocodile - the most "scary" circus performers, show that water is their element and there is nothing for a person to do there.

The show, which lasts 2 hours, brings together all the elements: water, air, fire and earth. The artists representing them were able to create a unique atmosphere from successive numbers that look at one go.
