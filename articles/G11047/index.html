"Silver Forest" (Omsk). Rest in Omsk. The recreation center "Pearl" (Omsk)

Life in the city, in addition to visible advantages, is characterized by futility, increased tension, which will certainly affect people's well-being. The best way to relieve fatigue, as you know, is fresh air, nature. It is this principle that should be guided by organizing your own vacation. In Omsk and its suburbs there are several objects, among which you can choose the right one that meets the diverse desires of visitors. Later in the article we will get to know them better.

Hotel complex "Silver Forest" (Omsk)





It is located in an unusually picturesque place, right inside the forest reserve. From the central part of the city you can get here in just 20 minutes by transport. Serebryany Bor (recreation center, Omsk) is a harmonious combination of natural beauty with civilization and comfort. The splendor of the scenery, the fresh breeze and the resinous aroma of coniferous forest will completely remove the accumulated daily fatigue. The possibility of outdoor activities will make this place a favorite for the whole family.

General information

The Serebryany Bor complex (Omsk, Podgorodka) includes a restaurant in which there are two halls serving 120 people at a time. This makes it possible to hold corporate events here, lavish banquets and weddings, after which guests can go home on the transport provided by the Hotel Complex. A conference room has been allocated for effective business meetings and negotiations, presentations in the Serebryany Bor complex (Omsk). There is also a hot Russian bath, a comfortable Finnish sauna, skiing, ice rink, slide, horseback riding.

Accommodation





For settlement in the Serebryany Bor complex (Omsk), only 51 rooms with different levels of comfort are offered:

  • 6 stylized rooms located in the main building: Asia, Greece, Africa, Europe-Modern, Marakesh, Europe-graphics;
  • 18 rooms with increased comfort of the "Standard" class on the 4th and 5th floors;
  • 15 single and double rooms of economy class located in sections;
  • one-room and two-room rooms (8 and 4, respectively), located in cottages, standing separately.

"Asia"

When decorating the interior, the nuances and centuries-old traditions characteristic of Asian countries were embodied: motifs in ornaments, wood carvings by skilled craftsmen. Perhaps here in your dreams Scheherazade will appear with his tales. There are 2 rooms (living room and bedroom), which means two people. For your comfort, air conditioning, TV, fridge, telephone, bathrobe with slippers, hairdryer, as well as coffee and tea tables are provided. The price of one day on holidays is 4200 rubles, on weekends (Fri-Sun) 3900 rubles, on weekdays (Mon-Thu) 3200 rubles.



"Africa"

The room is furnished in a characteristic style: a large ottoman made of solid wood, a double bed with leopard decoration, decorative panels and other design decisions create mystery and a sense of freedom. The living room and bedroom are designed for two guests who will find everything they need here: a telephone, a hairdryer, a refrigerator, a TV, air conditioning, a bathrobe and slippers and, in addition, a tea and coffee table. The cost of daily living on holidays is 4200 rubles, on weekends (Fri-Sun) 3900 rubles, on weekdays (Mon-Thu) 3200 rubles.

"Greece"

In the tourist complex "Pine Forest" (Omsk) there is a Greek corner where you can plunge into the atmosphere of ancient times with the pride of the walls of the acropolis and marble. All this is embodied in 1 room for 2 people. Comfort will be provided by the presence of a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, telephone, hairdryer, bathrobe with slippers, tea and coffee tables. The price of a weekly stay is 2900, weekend (Fri-Sun) - 3400, holiday - 3700 rubles.



"Europe Modern"

It combines the modern European level of comfort and bold color combinations. 1 room, designed for a couple of guests, is equipped with a TV, fridge, telephone, air conditioning, hairdryer. Extras include slippers with a bathrobe and a tea and coffee table. The cost of one weekday (Mon-Thu), weekend (Fri-Sun) and holiday - 2500, 3000 and 3200 rubles. respectively.

Economy rooms

1- and 2-bed rooms, located in the main building of the hotel complex "Sosnovy Bor" (Omsk), are furnished only with the most necessary items (in the second version there is a TV, either a double bed or 2 single beds - to choose from). Shower and toilet are designed for 2-3 rooms (section). The price is respectively 660 and 980 rubles. on weekdays (Mon-Thu), 800 and 1200 rubles. on weekends (Fri-Sun), 950 and 1450 rubles. on holidays.

Rooms in individual cottages

Other tourist complexes in the suburbs

The recreation center "Pearl" (Omsk) is located 36 km from the city, on the bank of the river. Omi with a picturesque landscape. It is an island of silence and harmony. There is not only relaxation, but also business meetings, weddings and corporate evenings. Guests have at their disposal cottages, over a dozen comfortable and cozy 2-bed rooms, a restaurant with varied cuisine, a banquet hall, a beloved Russian bathhouse for everyone, and there are barbecue facilities near the river. For lovers of relaxation, billiards, a summer pool, ski, roller, bicycle, skate rental, as well as sports grounds and a tennis court are actively provided. The children's area will delight babies and their mothers.

Base "Pearl" is known not only for the beauty of the landscape, but also famous for interior design. Here, European comfort is very clearly felt, the aroma of which comes from every detail.

Country complex "Fairy Tale"

Rest (Omsk, by the way, is known for its country villas throughout the country) will remain in the guests' memory for a long time. The complex is located on the territory of a relict pine forest 30 km from the city. The air of crystal purity, the magnificent landscapes of ancient pines, firs and birches and the whole life of nature will give a charge of positive emotions, bliss and a sense of peace. Here everyone can find a way to spend time with benefit both for the soul and for the body. For vivid impressions and a good rest, the suburban complex offers its visitors accommodation in hotel rooms or cottages, visits to the water park, the Morskaya Skazka aquarium, restaurant and banquet halls, billiards, a barbecue and children's area, sports ground and sauna complex. Due to the variety of services offered, the suburban complex will be appreciated by each family member.
