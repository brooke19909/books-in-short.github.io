Hotel Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Karon) - review, description and reviews of tourists

Due to the increased seasonal interest in holidays in Thailand, travel agencies began to actively offer Russians various tours to the resorts of this country. For our compatriots, winter vacations on sunny and hospitable Thai beaches have long become a good tradition. Indeed, in the midst of colds and cold, one wants so much for a week, but to forget about the snowy streets of his hometown and again plunge into a carefree holiday among palm trees to the sound of gentle waves. Phuket meets all the basic requirements of travelers. This island is famous for its magnificent beaches, an abundance of entertainment, a wide selection of sea excursions and, of course, its hotels. Most of the hotel complexes built here have a pleasant design and offer rooms at a low price. Our compatriots are very enthusiastic about how they had a great time in the "three rubles". Therefore, many tourists are considering for themselves such accommodation options. Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket / Karon) is very popular among Russians and most often it is here that they try to get a ticket. What impresses this hotel with our compatriots? Let's get it together.





Hotel Complex Overview

If this is your first trip to Phuket and, in principle, to Thailand, Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Karon) is an excellent option for you to get acquainted with the country and its cultural traditions. The hotel is built in traditional Thai style, which, as is known from numerous reviews of tourists, is very conducive to relaxation. Every detail here refers guests to the time when Thailand was not yet a world famous resort.

The complex cannot be attributed to new ones, it was built thirteen years ago. However, at first glance it is noticeable that the hotel staff carefully monitor its condition and regularly update the number of rooms. The last repair was carried out here three years ago, so most of the travelers were satisfied with their location in the apartments.

The Rattana Beach Hotel has a small territory, which is very typical for hotel complexes in Thailand. The total area is approximately two and a half thousand square meters. This is enough to accommodate a pool with a relaxation area and a compact garden. However, in the reviews of Rattana Beach Hotel 3 *, which tourists do, it is often noted that most of the time they spend outside the walls of the hotel. Guests almost every day relax at the sea, go on excursions and shopping, so the modest territory of the hotel does not bother them at all.

The hotel belongs to the category of urban and allows guests to explore on foot the most interesting places on the island.





Estimate the location

Many of our compatriots who are not familiar with the features of Phuket are trying to find the hotel, which is located on the beach. However, there are very few such complexes on the island and living in them is very expensive. Most hotels are located some distance from the coast, but you can almost always reach the sea in literally five to seven minutes.

The tourist catalogs indicate that the Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket / Karon) is located on the second coastline and at least five hundred meters will be separated from the beach for vacationers. According to tourists, the journey takes no more than five minutes. At the same time, along the way, you can always look into a cozy cafe or local market.

Our compatriots highly value the location of the hotel. Within walking distance from the hotel are shopping centers, cafes, restaurants and souvenir shops. In the evening you can walk along the streets of Phuket, which are always a lot of people walking. If you want to go to Patong Beach, considered the heart of the island, then in tuk-tuk you will get there in just five minutes.





Beach area

The nearest beach to Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Thailand / Phuket) - Karon Beach. This place can not be called the center of tourist life, but vacationers consider this beach area one of the cleanest on the island. Sun beds and umbrellas must be paid separately, but this is a well-known practice in Phuket, so tourists are not too embarrassed.

A brief description of the infrastructure of the complex

Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Karon) has a very modest infrastructure, but guests of the hotel have enough facilities. The center of attraction for holidaymakers is the pool. Its area is fifty-five square meters, and the depth varies within a meter and a half. He works from nine in the morning until six in the evening. Then it is closed for cleaning, and the employees of the complex do it honestly. Our compatriots note that the bowl of the pool is always clean, and the water in it pleases with transparency.

At the reception staff speaks Russian and English. The managing director of Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Karon) also speaks quite well in Russian, so you will not have a language barrier.

There are safes at the reception, for their rent you need to pay separately. However, this service is very popular, because in the rooms safes are not installed.

If necessary, guests can rent a car or motobike at the rental point, but please note that there is no private parking on site. Therefore, tourists will have to solve this problem on their own.

A conference room is available for vacationers at an additional cost, but it does not accommodate too many guests.





Services

It is difficult to imagine a good rest of a modern person without the ability to access the Internet. Wi-Fi catches excellent at the hotel, so all guests leave positive feedback about it. You can use the Internet throughout the hotel, including rooms.

If you do not want to get to the airport as part of a tourist group, then order an individual transfer. In a small minibus it is much more convenient to drive forty-five kilometers that separate the complex and the international airport.

The hotel has room service. Twenty-four hours a day, vacationers can order dishes from the restaurant and even some goods from nearby stores.

Accommodation in a hotel complex with children

Judging by the reviews of Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket / Karon), tourists with small children often come to the hotel. Despite the lack of a special infrastructure for them, the kids are quite fun in the hotel complex. After all, they can communicate with each other and give their parents time to relax.

Children under four years old have the right to stay at the hotel absolutely free. At the same time they will be placed in a separate berth. If you want to place a child from four to twelve years old in existing beds, please note that you will have to pay extra three hundred butts. Those who need an extra bed will have to pay one and a half thousand butts per day.

Usually, only one cot can be accommodated in an apartment, however, if necessary, hotel staff can go to meet you and consider the option of accommodating several children.

Infrastructure for the child

It is very difficult to find an extensive infrastructure for children in Thailand (Asia). Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket / Karon) also does not provide you with a choice of entertainment for the child. Of all the possible services, only a high chair in a restaurant and a berth can offer small guests. Therefore, keep in mind that a holiday in Phuket with a child implies the need to think through recreational activities for him.





Check-in conditions

Most often, tourists check into their room no earlier than two in the afternoon. This check-in time is typical of Thai hotels, but if necessary, you can get into the apartment in the morning. If the room intended for you is available, then the hotel staff will draw up all the documents before the due date.

Departure is carried out until twelve in the afternoon. Some tourists write that with a late departure, it is necessary to extend the use of the room in advance. Indeed, otherwise your apartment may already be booked by someone.

Pets are allowed at an extra charge. However, when making a reservation, it is necessary to indicate this moment in order not to encounter certain difficulties when checking in.

A few words about the number of rooms

The hotel complex consists of one residential building, which is equipped with forty-five apartments. They are divided into two categories:

  • Standard . There are thirty-eight such rooms in the hotel, each with an area not exceeding twenty-two square meters. The apartments are equipped with cable TV, air conditioning and a telephone with international access. A fridge and minibar are also included. The bathroom is stocked with toiletries, towels and a hairdryer. In addition to beds, the rooms are equipped with soft armchairs and a comfortable coffee table. Daily maids replenish bottled drinking water.
  • Deluxe Seven rooms of this category do not exceed the previous ones in area, the difference between them is only in improved equipment. For example, guests of deluxe suites have a bathrobe and slippers, and there is always a tea set at their disposal that allows you to spend time with a cup of coffee on the cozy balcony.

In general, Russians rate the number of rooms very high, they are satisfied with the accommodation and do not make complaints about cleaning the apartments.





Nutrition

There is one restaurant on site. It provides all guests with the necessary number of dishes for breakfast, if necessary, here guests can have lunch and dinner. Most often, tourists buy trips with breakfast included, and therefore reviews are mostly left about them. At Papa Restaurant, breakfast is served from seven to ten in the morning. It is a standard set of dishes suitable for children and adults.

On the table there are always egg dishes, vegetables, fruits, cold cuts and so on. Guests are never hungry.





Tour cost and reviews

Our compatriots planning a winter vacation are actively interested in the cost of trips to Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Karon). The price of a two-week holiday will be a little over one hundred thousand rubles. It includes a flight for two people from Moscow, accommodation in a standard room with breakfast included. A ten-day tour costs about seventy-five thousand rubles.

Reviews about the hotel Rattana Beach Hotel 3 * read very nice. Basically, they contain an abundance of information and gratitude to one or another staff of the complex. Of the minuses, tourists usually call the practice of settling on the first day in a nearby hotel. However, this topic must be discussed with your travel agent, because your vacation directly depends on it. Some Russians managed to make out on the territory of the hotel running rats. But this information has remained unconfirmed.

The advantages our compatriots include:

  • lack of deposit upon check-in;
  • high-quality cleaning;
  • Friendly staff, instantly responding to all requests;
  • a good selection of dishes for the "treshka";
  • magnificent beach with waves;
  • cleanliness on site.

Tourists recommend the Rattana Beach Hotel for a budget holiday in a paradise for people who prefer to spend most of their time outside the room.
