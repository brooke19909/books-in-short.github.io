A little bit about the resort ...

Choosing Hotel Astoria Park 4 * (Costa Brava) as a place for a vacation, tourists should understand what Lloret de Mar is like. The fact is that the city is not only the center of the tourist Costa Brava, but also the main party place on the Mediterranean coast, which is visited by young people. Therefore, if you are trying to find a place for a quiet and relaxing holiday, then perhaps you should choose a more relaxing resort. Lloret de Mar attracts with many discos, bars, nightclubs with entertaining dance programs and vibrant laser shows. But this is not all the sights of the city. It has more than a thousand-year history. From a small fishing village, Lloret de Mar has gradually turned into one of the most famous tourist resorts in Europe. And now it is considered one of the most beautiful cities on the Spanish coast.



Lloret da Mar is located in the south of the Costa Brava, just 75 kilometers from the famous Barcelona, ‚Äã‚Äã30 kilometers from Girona and 100 kilometers from France. This place has long gained popularity among tourists from all over the world. The climate of this resort is milder than in other cities on the Spanish coast. In summer, the air temperature is between + 25- + 28 degrees, and the beach season lasts from early May to October.

The city has the most famous beach on the coast - Lloret. It is used by guests of Hotel Astoria Park 4 * (Lloret de Mar). In addition, there is also the Fenals Beach, Water World water park. The local sandy coast is very wide and perfectly protected by cliffs and bays from the north winds, which makes it so attractive and popular. Numerous picturesque bays with green-blue water attract beach lovers from many countries.

Local Attractions

Resting in Hotel Astoria Park 4 * (reviews are given in the article), you should definitely take advantage of the opportunity and visit local attractions.

To learn about the region‚Äôs ancient history, you can visit the Verdaguer Cultural Center, which houses ancient relics of the Iberian kingdom dating back to 250 BC. In addition, the city has a maritime museum. Entrance to both establishments is inexpensive: 3-4 euros.

Walking along the seaside boulevard of Lloret de Mar, you can admire a copy of the rather famous Canale fountain, its original is in Barcelona. According to legend, the fountain is magical. It has an amazing property. Anyone who drinks water from it will either return to Barcelona or stay in it forever.

At the end of Lloret Beach, on a rock stands a bronze figure of Donna Mariner (the wife of a sailor), which is a symbol of the resort. She is the image of a woman looking into the blue distance, who meets and escorts her husband. This symbol of the coast of Spain was made in 1966 by the artist E. Maragalla. There is even a belief, according to which a person looking into the distance of the sea, holding the sculpture's foot, will certainly see a way to achieve his goals.



Another symbol of the city is the Castle on the beach. Once it was the private residence of Narsis Plach (industrialist from Girona). The castle began to be built in 1935, and it was only possible to finish it in the forties.

Leisure

Lloret de Mar is famous not only for its beaches, but also for its excellent infrastructure, which allows you to have fun on land and water. On the coast you can rent a catamaran for riding, rush off on a banana, go on a boat trip along the coast. There are quite a lot of diving centers in the city, you can also go snorkelling, play tennis, golf or bowling, and ride horses.

A free bus runs from the central square of the city to the water park. Children are enthralled by the Aqualeon mini-zoo and Marineland Park. In addition, it is interesting to simply stroll the streets of Lloret de Mar, which lead to the center, where there are a huge number of restaurants and cafes.



Even after sunset, the resort does not fall asleep. The city is changing, dressing in colorful lights. Discos and night clubs open, entertaining visitors until the morning. For lovers of parties and nightly entertainment, it is difficult to find a better place.
