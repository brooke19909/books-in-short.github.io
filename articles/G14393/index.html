Reviews

Currently, the project has been completed, all houses are populated, respectively, we can summarize the first results. So residents of Wellton Park residential complex celebrate the scale of the project. This is a great option for life: a good infrastructure, the territory has its own kindergarten, the work of a landscape designer deserves special attention.





Today, the main disadvantages are transport accessibility, or rather, its absence. Today, you can get to the complex only by private car, shuttle buses are at the planning stage, and at least 10 km to the nearest railway station, and trains run quite rarely. There is no school on the territory of the complex, and today it is not known whether it will appear here at all. Many note the insufficient number of infrastructure facilities; for others, peace and quiet became the main features of the project, its indisputable advantages.

In all other respects, the project is excellent: the place is beautiful, picturesque with clean air. And the infrastructure of the Solnechnogorsk district has just begun to develop, so in the near future all the missing infrastructure will appear here. In the meantime, you will have to suffer some inconvenience, offset by the democratic value of real estate.
