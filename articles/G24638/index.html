Golden Crown: Things to Remember When Sending Money

To complete the transfer, you should visit one of the nearest bank branches included in the list of organizations engaged in cooperation with this payment agent. You must have a basic document with you, which allows you to confirm your identity.





The client fills out an application stating the following data:

  • transfer destination (country and city);
  • exact data of the recipient (according to the passport);
  • Amount to send
  • contact numbers of the sender and recipient.

The amount required for the transfer is paid to the cashier. At the same time, a fee is sent for sending money.

The payment system assigns a nine-digit number to the executed transfer, which will be known only to the sender of the funds and to the person receiving the transfer. The recipient will be notified of the sending of money via SMS.
