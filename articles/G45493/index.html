Hotel Elihotel 4 * Italy, Sicily: overview, features and reviews

The island of Sicily is one of the most popular places for summer holidays. Here, a pleasant climate is combined with breathtaking landscapes and resorts for every taste and budget. Many travelers prefer to stay in Sant Alessio Siculo. And a popular place here is the hotel complex Elihotel 4 *.

Of course, before paying for your reservation, you should learn more about the selected hotel. For example, will you have to go far to the beach? What kind of living conditions do tourists provide? Will eating be a problem? What do former guests say about the hotel? The answers to these questions are important.

Where to look for a hotel? Location Information





When choosing a hotel, an experienced traveler pays attention to his location, because this is an important factor - someone wants to live in the busy center of the city, while other tourists prefer to enjoy privacy and silence.

Where is the hotel complex Elihotel 4 * located? Italy, about. Sicily, a colorful resort village of Sant Alessio Siculo - this is where many tourists prefer to enjoy all the delights of a measured vacation.

Nearby are shops, traditional Italian taverns and some island attractions. The nearest airport is in Catania, its distance is about 70 km. Palermo Airport is 260 km from the hotel. Upon request, staff will arrange a comfortable transfer upon arrival.

Hotel Elihotel Sant Alessio Siculo 4 * (Italy, Letojanni): territory and infrastructure





Reviews indicate that this is a calm and comfortable hotel with thoughtful infrastructure. The hotel complex Elihotel San Alessio Siculo 4 * consists of the main five-story building and two small two-story buildings. The hotel area is not too big, but very beautiful - the courtyard faces directly to the sea and is adjacent to the picturesque cliffs on which the ancient castle stands (it is definitely worth a visit).

By the way, guests have been welcomed here since 1975 - during this time the hotel managed to earn a lot of good reviews. In 2016, a complete reconstruction was carried out - repairs were made, furniture and household appliances were replaced.

Hotel Room Categories

Elihotel 4 * is a small hotel complex consisting of 69 rooms. Of course, you can choose a room of a suitable category.

  • Standard Rooms - these rooms are located on the first 2 floors, the windows overlook the surroundings, the area is 15-25 square meters.
  • Standard Rooms Sea View - the same standard rooms, but located in two-story buildings. The room comes with a balcony or porch, windows overlook the sea.
  • Superior Rooms - rooms are located in the main building. Their area is slightly larger (up to 30 square meters). The windows and balconies offer breathtaking views of the beach and cliffs.

Description and photos of rooms





As the guests themselves say, the rooms here are really comfortable - every visit was recently renovated, the furniture is new, including large comfortable beds with orthopedic mattresses. Large windows fill the room with light. Most rooms have balconies from which you can enjoy views of the sea and the rocky hills.

Each room has an Internet connection, and the speed is quite decent, but for many travelers this is a huge plus. You can pass the time while watching TV, however, there are no Russian-language channels here. Guests can use their own safe with a combination lock, which is also very convenient. It offers tourists a small refrigerator (aka a minibar).

The bathroom is quite spacious, and the plumbing was changed during the last repair. You can always use a shower cabin. There is a toilet, bidet, sink, vanity mirror. Every day, guests bring clean, snow-white towels. Most guests are completely satisfied with the living conditions at the hotel - here you can really relax with body and soul.

Nutrition information





Nutrition is one of the important issues, because it is impossible to imagine a really pleasant vacation without delicious food. Elihotel 4 * has its own spacious restaurant, which accepts guests three times a day. Breakfast is organized in the form of receptions, and for lunch and dinner guests order dishes from the menu (only snacks are taken out to the common tables). The menu here is not bad - they serve light salads, fresh fruits, delicious pastries (including sweet croissants), Italian pasta, pizza, meat and fish dishes, and seafood.

Of course, tourists themselves can choose the right food scheme. You can pay for full three meals a day or order only breakfast. In any case, there will be no problems with food, because there are grocery stores nearby, as well as taverns, where they feed very tasty.

Rest on the seashore: amenities and entertainment





The hotel complex is located almost on the seashore - the beach can be reached in a few minutes. The coast here is covered with small pebbles, as well as the entrance to the sea. That is why vacationers are advised to bring special shoes with them (by the way, they can also be purchased here).

According to reviews, the waters here are clean and calm, and the depth increases gradually. Sun loungers and umbrellas are arranged in rows on the shore. By the way, a pair of deckchairs is assigned to each room - you won‚Äôt have to take a seat in the morning.

During the high season, you can rent a boat or a catamaran on the beach, ride a boat and try different kinds of water sports. There is enough entertainment here. Alternatively, head to one of the public beaches where fun and noise are always over the edge.

Additional services





Of course, guests can count on some amenities. Despite the fact that the territory of the hotel is small, there is still a fully equipped and round-the-clock guarded parking. You can also rent a car or bike here. By the way, tourists themselves say that a bike ride in the vicinity can be a real adventure.

For an extra fee, guests can use the laundry facilities. There are luggage storage facilities. Throughout the territory, including facilities for recreation, there is access to high-speed Internet.

Leisure and entertainment at the hotel





Most tourists stay at the hotel, planning to spend all their time on the beach or in the city. The hotel provides a wonderful overnight stay, but there are no special entertainments here. The pool is absent, but in most cases it is not required, because the sea is very near. However, the courtyard has a terrace for relaxing and sunbathing.

There are several massage rooms on the territory, however, for such a pleasure you need to pay extra. On the other hand, good professionals work at the hotel, who offer not only relaxing, but also therapeutic procedures.

Animators on site work, but only in the summer months. They regularly organize some contests and performances, albeit in Italian.

Entertainment is worth looking outside the Elihotel 4 *. Italy is a country with an interesting history and inimitable atmosphere, which is worth enjoying in full. In the hotel or in the city you can buy an interesting sightseeing tour of the island - there are many places worth seeing.
