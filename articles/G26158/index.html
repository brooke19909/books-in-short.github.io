Hotel "Golden Lion" in Novosibirsk: description, address, photos and reviews

Novosibirsk is one of the largest megacities in Siberia, with a wide selection of opportunities for business trips and a pleasant vacation. Many modern hotels have been built to accommodate guests in the city, providing visitors with a traditional friendly welcome. The list of hotels in Novosibirsk includes establishments of various price categories - from inexpensive mini-hotels and hostels to luxury luxury hotels. To solve business issues, institutions in the center of Novosibirsk provide modern conference rooms equipped with the latest technology. Conditions for a comfortable and pleasant stay are provided in pensions and compact hostels. In any of the hotels in Novosibirsk, you can book a room of your choice online.





According to reviews, all the institutions of the city meet the declared category and the requirements of hotel services. In more expensive 5 * accommodation options, a high level of service, a wide range of services and a wide range of opportunities for entertainment and relaxation are provided. Economy mini-hotels and cheaper hotels in the city center have only a certain set of amenities without additional service. Therefore, the cost of living in economy hotels in Novosibirsk is significantly lower compared to premium establishments. Nevertheless, in the cheap hotels of the city, along with reasonable prices, they also offer comfortable and cozy rooms with all necessary amenities. One of the most popular accommodation options among visitors is the Golden Lion 3 * Hotel.

Acquaintance

Hotel "Golden Lion" in Novosibirsk is a cozy country house located in a pine forest. The hotel building is stylized as an old castle, inside which residents are provided with all modern conveniences. The abundance of clean air, walks among the pines, delicious quality food, the ability to ride a snowmobile in winter - all this provides an excellent pastime for guests and their good mood.

People come to the hotel to relax with friends or family, to hide from the hustle and bustle of the city, to celebrate an anniversary or to celebrate a wedding, to conduct corporate training among untouched nature.

Golden Lion, Novosibirsk: address

The facility is located in a pine forest. The address of the cultural and health club "Golden Lion": st. Lesnaya Polyana, 56 (not far from the metro station "Zaeltsovskaya"). According to the tourists, it takes about 20 minutes to get from the railway station to the hotel. The distance from the hotel to Tolmachevo Airport can be overcome in 40 minutes. You can get to the Golden Lion Hotel by shuttle taxi number 1055 or bus number 105 (stop "Sibirsky Bereg Plant").

Description

The institution "Golden Lion" (Novosibirsk) belongs to the category of suburban hotels. For more than seven years, people from different walks of life have come here. According to reviews, each of them provides everything necessary for a carefree holiday. The hotel building, located in a quiet pine forest, as guests note, is very similar to an old castle. Many people call the location of the hotel very convenient, because it allows you to quickly and easily get to the center of Novosibirsk, which is especially valuable for guests who come here for work. This arrangement is also popular with tourists who come to get acquainted with the local culture. Not far from the hotel are the main attractions of the city - the Botanical Garden and the zoo.

Guests also appreciated the cozy restaurant where you can treat yourself to dishes of Russian and European cuisine. The restaurant provides services for organizing various celebrations (weddings, anniversaries, corporate parties, etc.) At the request of guests, breakfast is delivered to the rooms.

Lovers of baths and saunas also do not stand aside. The hotel offers a Russian bath, as well as a Finnish sauna with pools (outdoor and indoor). In summer, residents can simply breathe fresh air in the summer area. And in winter you can go to a cozy fireplace room and sit here in a warm friendly company or play billiards. Judging by the reviews, hotel guests get a good rest at affordable prices.
