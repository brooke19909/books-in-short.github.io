What threads to choose for a shawl?



For crochet shawls, for beginners it does not turn into Chinese torture, you should not choose too thin yarn for the first things. If this is your first experience, then heed our advice. The threads that are used when crocheting a shawl for beginners, it is better to choose a medium thickness.
