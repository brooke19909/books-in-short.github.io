How to choose a property?

So, about what the price of a house in Spain on the seashore can be maximum, it has already been said. And now I would like to talk about how to choose real estate. If you believe the reviews, then, of course, you need to pay attention to infrastructure, remoteness from the sea, the orientation of the house in relation to the sun and the quality of the residential complex. They are called residences in this country. It was said above that in one house there can be six rooms, a sauna, a spa, a sports ground, and in another there is a modest pool and a neat front garden. It is logical that all this affects the so-called communidad - something like a rent. It can vary from twenty to 1500 euros per year! Water with electricity, by the way, is always paid separately.





Features of choice

Also, considering houses in Spain by the sea, it is worth paying more attention to options that are located in the green zone, near schools, playgrounds, shops. It is better if the windows of the house are placed perpendicular to the coastline. Choosing such a house, a person gets the opportunity to enjoy the fresh sea breeze, which is not only good refreshing, but also useful (because it will contain particles of iodine, minerals and salt). Reviews recommend buying a villa with a pool, because swimming in the sea in summer is too exhausting, as the water sometimes heats up to excessively high temperatures. But the pool can be filled with such water as you want: even ice, at least boiling water.

Clearance process

The first thing that is necessary for registration of real estate in Spain is a reliable agent, a specialist in this field, who will deal with the settlement of all issues arising in the process. The second is to fly to the country in advance, about a week before the planned purchase, in order to view the maximum number of options and choose the most suitable one.

Then - make an advance payment. 3-6 thousand euros are sent to the seller‚Äôs account, after which the person receives a villa reservation agreement. Then you need to open a bank account (required in Spanish) and put on it the amount that will be required to pay for the purchase. And then get the NIE. That is the tax number of a foreigner. This is a necessary document, since only if it is possible to activate a bank account. And then the date and time of the meeting is already set for signing the main contract. The completion of the registration process is considered the moment the buyer passes the bank check for the indicated amount in exchange for which he is given the keys to the cottage.





Documents

In order not to worry about whether something was forgotten or not, you must first check all the documents. The first, most important is a valid passport. The second is a certificate of employment. It should include data such as full name, salary, and also the position held. Still need a certificate of income. If a person is not officially organized, then you can show any other confirmation of his viability, for example, a contract for the sale of a car, garage, apartment: it does not matter what it will be, but the most important thing is to prove to the representatives of the Spanish bank that there is money to buy.

And in order to get the notorious NIE, you will need to fill out a questionnaire, present a passport, two color photographs (size 3 x 4), booking tickets for the return trip, bank statement and a preliminary reservation contract for the cottage. That's basically it. Not so difficult as it might seem at first glance.
