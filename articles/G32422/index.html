Turkey resorts with a sandy beach. Vacation with children

Among Russian tourists, Turkey is famous for its high quality service at a relatively inexpensive cost of vacation. There is a huge selection of entertainment: beach in the summer, ski in the winter. The resorts of Turkey with sandy beaches, as well as pebble and mixed ones, warmly and warmly welcome travelers. Divers will like to relax in these parts, because in Turkey there are many small islands with rich opportunities for spearfishing. Winter

the country is waiting for guests of ski holidays. In addition, there are medical spas with hot springs. Despite the prevailing stereotypes that there is nothing to do except bathing, in Turkey there is something to see. To see all the sights of the once great civilizations, not enough vacation.

The flight time to the resting place is 3-4 hours. After marriage, newlyweds, groups of young people and families with children go to Turkey to relax. Everyone will find their own activities here, you just need to choose the right city. Many vacationers choose this destination for several years.





For example, for a family vacation with children, Turkish resorts with a sandy beach are best suited. Such conditions are created in Alanya, Antalya, Belek and Side. The country is washed by two seas - the Aegean and the Mediterranean. On different coasts and the climate varies. The Mediterranean Sea is warm and affectionate in May-June, as well as in September, but in July-August the Russians endure with difficulty - it's too hot. But at this time, a vacation on the Aegean coast will go well . Resorts in Turkey with a sandy beach will leave an unforgettable experience with the whole family. Children can be left with animators in a group of the same resting comrades. Adults at this time can go sightseeing in Turkey. At the service of young travelers are numerous water parks and dolphinariums, sea excursions with the opportunity to see the inhabitants of the depths.

There are many wonderful places to relax with the whole family, among which you should choose the resorts of Turkey with a sandy beach. Antalya is very popular among tourists from Russia. This is a long resort, famous for its pristine beaches. There is a sandy beach in Lara, and there are platform ones.



Alanya is known for budget holidays. There is also a mass of golden sandy beaches and clear water. White sand is characteristic of the old city of Side, complementing the resorts of Turkey with a sandy beach. But this is only in the west of the resort, while its eastern part is represented by deserts, which is suitable for lovers of a quiet relaxing holiday.

Belek is not a cheap resort. Favorable conditions have been created for children: sand on the coast and coniferous with eucalyptus air from forests growing in the vicinity. Hotels in Belek are predominantly four- and five-star.

Whatever city is chosen as a holiday, staying in this wonderful hospitable country will leave the most favorable impression. The best resorts in Turkey are sure to bring joy, fill with new experiences and help restore strength.
