Tips for authenticating your iphone

Apple is a world-famous American company, opened in 1976, founded by Steve Jobs. The main office is located in California. For such a short time, this company was able to distribute products around the world that make us admire its reliability, uniqueness and excellent quality.





For almost 40 years, Apple has changed beyond recognition. As everyone knows, Tim Cook became the new head of the company in 2011, after the death of Steve Jobs. The founder of the company was a truly great man who achieved success in life without anyone else's help, only with his mind and zeal. The company's products have no boundaries in uniqueness and significance in world markets. At the moment, the most popular products are: iPhone, iPad and iPod, which already have many different models that differ in structure and functionality.

Due to the popularity of the company, its products begin to be faked and sold, posing as genuine. How to check iphone and not be mistaken with its choice? First of all, measure the diameter of the screen, it is 8.9 cm. Find a special slot for a SIM card, it is half the size of a regular one, and therefore the connector for it should be smaller and fit in size. Also, the coincidence of the three serial numbers located on the box, SIM card and phone menu is required. Authentication can also be done on the SNDeepInfo service.





There are several ways to verify the authenticity of the iphone 4s. One of the easiest is to check the correct Russian language on the phone. The presence of one SIM card, only the iOS 5 operating system and the SIRI system, suggests that the phone is real. You can also use the tips above to check your iphone. But still, the best and most reliable way is checking in iTunes, which should find the connected device, if this is not observed, then your purchase is just a Chinese fake.

Most recently, a completely new iPhone 5 phone was released, which is considered the receiver of the 4s series phone. Many have already bought a new product, and who has not had time to purchase it, he must know how to check iphone 5. First of all, you need to buy in large well-known stores that are not fooled. If this happens, then in such enterprises it is easier to solve the problem.





When buying, check the availability of all the components of the phone and its additional accessories, such as headphones and charging. Everything should be well-equipped and in excellent condition. Knowing   how to check iphone, you will never be mistaken in its choice.
