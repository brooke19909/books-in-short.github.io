Hotel Larissa Garden Hotel, Beldibi 4 *, Kemer: overview, features and reviews

Turkey is a country that attracts the attention of a huge number of tourists, especially during the holiday season. Many travelers come here every year who prefer to relax surrounded by exotic. One of the most visited resorts is located in the city of Kemer, which is famous for its beautiful beaches and the clean Mediterranean coast. It has a wonderful mild climate.

Larissa Garden Hotel Family 4 * (Kemer / Beldibi) - one of the most popular hotels, which is located in the border village, located on the most picturesque Mediterranean coast. Here, tourists can enjoy the wonderful surrounding view, and while staying at the hotel - enjoy excellent service.



general information

Hotel Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Beldibi) was built in 2000, and since then its general condition is maintained every year, during the decline in resort activity. The most large-scale reconstruction inside the building and on its territory was made in 2011.

This place for recreation occupies a relatively small area - 7500 square meters. m, however, there are many places for entertainment that will be interesting to both adults and children.

Tourists who purchase a tour to the hotel as part of their vouchers arrive at Antalya International Airport, from where they are delivered to their destination.

Rooms

The entire hotel is located in two modern buildings, each of which rises to 4 floors. The attention of the guests is presented to the apartments of two categories "Standard" and "Family". Each of them has been renovated in accordance with European standards, as well as modern furniture and some electronics.

Guests can use private bathrooms, which have a bathroom and shower, as well as a standard set of bath accessories, which consists of soap, shampoo and shower gel. Absolutely all rooms have a private balcony, from which you can explore the picturesque surroundings. As for the temperature of living conditions, they are regulated using personal air conditioners.

"Standard"

Standard rooms at Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Kemer / Beldibi) are designed to accommodate a couple of guests, which is why they are very popular among couples and young people who have come to celebrate their honeymoon. It has a large double bed with an orthopedic mattress, bedside tables, a wardrobe for personal items, as well as a table and a couple of chairs. Guests have the opportunity to use a telephone, an electronic safe, and a minibar for an additional fee, the assortment of which, on condition of use, is regularly updated.

"Family"

Family rooms are incredibly popular among tourists who prefer to relax with their children - for this there are all the necessary conditions, from an extra bed to a wider space, which is divided into two rooms, each of which has two beds.

As for the furniture set, it is exactly the same as in the "Standard" apartments: there is a writing table for work, a comfortable armchair, as well as several places in which you can store a small amount of clothes.



Living conditions for children

In family-type rooms of the Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Beldibi), for guests with small children an extra bed can be provided upon request, strollers are not available for rent. Younger guests can actively spend time on the playground and have fun in the small pool, which is equipped with all the safety standards.

Also, caring parents can send them to a special children's club in which kids will have the opportunity to communicate with their peers and will be able to develop in all directions under the supervision of animators and a nanny. However, it should be remembered that only children aged 2 to 12 years and at certain times can visit it: from 10 a.m. to noon and from 2 a.m. to 9:30 p.m.

Children's pots are not provided at the hotel, which many guests consider a big minus.

Entertainment for adults

Guests of the Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Kemer / Beldibi) will definitely not be bored during their holidays, as there is a huge amount of entertainment for them. In particular, here near the large pool entertaining morning aerobics classes and sports games are regularly held. From evening to late night, guests can visit the local disco, where you can dance to incendiary hits of DJs, in addition, theme nights and entertaining production shows are held.

In one of the areas on the territory of the Larissa Garden Hotel, there is an excellent amphitheater where guests can watch various films. Also, there are often arranged performances and presentations with the participation of leading.



Organization of local sightseeing tours

Groups of tourists traveling to the hotel can go on sightseeing tours organized by the hotel. Their cost is indicated in a separate price list and, as a rule, is not included in the price of a tourist permit. So, in the framework of such trips, guests have the opportunity to look at the famous Beldibi cave, which has a large number of cave paintings made in ancient times, as well as the picturesque Cape Gelidonia.

Accompanied by experienced instructors, everyone can arrange a climb to the mountain peaks, such as Takhtaly and Yanartash. In Kemer and its immediate vicinity there are many historical sites that are also open to the public. In particular, Rhodiapolis, the stone remains of the cities of Olympos and Phaselis, Aricanda, as well as the tower of Limir are especially popular among guests.

On the outskirts of Kemer, there is another interesting building that was created in ancient times - the Godelme Castle, near which there is also a beautiful cave.

Pool

At any time of the day, guests of the Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Kemer / Beldibi) can visit two outdoor pools of 200 and 120 square meters. m. In the largest of them there are two high slides for descending into the water, on which residents can frolic.

Around the pools there is a large sun terrace, on which comfortable sun loungers are installed - on them guests can relax and sunbathe under umbrellas. Also nearby is a small bar serving cold drinks.



Sports and SPA

Hotel guests who value relaxation in relaxation can always visit the local spa. Its structure includes a large sauna, Turkish bath (hammam), as well as a large indoor pool in which vacationers like to freshen up after hot procedures. If desired, you can visit the massage room, where professionals work.

Fans of a healthy lifestyle and sports will find their place in the gym, where there are many simulators for different muscle groups. The hotel has a section for water sports, where professional instructors can teach anyone to water ski or scuba diving, as well as tennis and volleyball courts.



Nutrition

Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Kemer / Beldibi) offers an all-inclusive package at the local restaurant, where a daily buffet is organized - it can be visited daily from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m. Here tourists can enjoy deliciously prepared dishes of European and traditional Turkish cuisine.

Guests also have the opportunity to visit the local bars, which offer light snacks, drinks, cocktails, tea and coffee. Sweet tooths have the opportunity to taste local desserts in them.



Beach

Along the coastline, literally 150 meters from the Larissa Garden Hotel Family 4 * (Beldibi) building, there is a personal sandy and pebble beach, where it is always very clean and beautiful. Its length is considerable - about 150 meters. The descent into the water is gentle, which ensures safety for young vacationers.

Favorable and comfortable conditions are arranged for guests here: sun loungers, umbrellas and towels are provided free of charge, if you wish, you can rent an air mattress. Visitors can use the local locker rooms, toilets and showers.

As for entertainment, there are a lot of them along the coastline - here you can go water skiing, rent a boat or a yacht, and also scuba dive under the guidance of a professional instructor.

Additional services provided at Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Beldibi)

All residents of the hotel have the opportunity to use some services that are not included in the price of the permit, but may well be provided for a fee. In particular, they include car rental, which is done without leaving the hotel, parking for cars will be completely free. Here you can also take your things dry cleaned or laundry. If necessary, for a fee you can use the services of a doctor who is on duty around the clock.

On the territory of Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Beldibi) payment by credit cards is possible, there is also an exchange office where you can get your desired currency.

Prices

The cost of a ticket to this hotel directly depends on which tourist operator it was purchased from and from which particular airport the plane will depart. On average, its price is about $ 500. The amount includes a weekly stay for two in a Standard room at Larissa Garden Hotel 4 * (Beldibi), round-trip flight, insurance, and airport transfer.

Very often, tourists are offered to purchase an additional package of excursions to local attractions, but it is better to organize such things on the spot, because, as practice shows, their cost in the hotel is much lower than that of Russian tour operators.
