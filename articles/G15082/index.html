General information about UniCredit Bank

Before exploring exactly where and in what quantity the UniCredit Bank ATMs in Moscow are represented, we will consider the basic information about the respective credit and financial organization.

UniCredit is an international brand that operates in the markets of about 50 countries, including Russia. About 9,300 offices of this bank are open in the world. In Russia, the financial institution, which is now called UniCredit, was opened in 1989 - it is, therefore, one of the first domestic commercial banks. Initially, this organization was called the International Moscow Bank. Its shareholders were other major banking institutions - VEB, Sberbank, and a number of foreign credit organizations. IMB is considered the first commercial bank to become a participant in trading operations on international markets.





Under the UniCredit brand, the bank has been operating in the Russian market since 2007. The institution provides a wide range of financial services to citizens and organizations. In particular, it provides servicing of bank cards issued by the organization. UniCredit Bank ATMs in Moscow and other Russian cities are represented by a very large-scale network of service devices. Development of UniCredit card servicing infrastructure: agreement with Raiffeisenbank

Among the most noteworthy facts that testify to the active work of UniCredit regarding the development of the infrastructure for servicing plastic cards is the conclusion of a partnership agreement with Raiffeisenbank in 2013. The essence of this contract is to unite the ATM networks of both major financial brands.





It is noteworthy that the infrastructure of Raiffeisenbank and the UniCredit Bank ATMs in Moscow and throughout Russia were combined in the regions where both credit and financial institutions operated. The total number of devices for servicing cards at Raiffeisenbank at that time was about 2 thousand units, at UniCredit - about 1 thousand.

In accordance with an agreement signed by banks, cardholders issued by financial institutions can use an integrated network of devices in such a way as to withdraw cash from their accounts without commission. For Raiffeisenbank, combining an ATM network with UniCredit infrastructure was not new: by that time, the financial institution had already entered into a similar agreement with Rosbank. As a result, his clients got the opportunity to withdraw cash without commission in more than 6 thousand devices for servicing cards throughout Russia.

The same can be said about UniCredit. The fact is that at the time of the conclusion of the contract with Raiffeisenbank, the relevant financial institution had signed a similar agreement with the IBC. As a result, customers got the opportunity to use not only UniCredit Bank ATMs in Moscow, but also devices for servicing cards from the ICD, which totaled about 3.7 thousand units.

Development of UniCredit card service infrastructure: agreement with Uralsib

Another well-known brand with which UniCredit has signed a partnership agreement in the framework of card services is Uralsib. A contract between financial institutions was also concluded in 2013.





In accordance with it, UniCredit customers got the opportunity to withdraw cash at Uralsib ATMs with certain benefits, in fact, on the same conditions as in the case of using the infrastructure of the brand that issued plastic cards. By that time, Uralsib had a network of ATMs, in which there were about 3 thousand devices. UniCredit customers have the opportunity to use them not only for cash withdrawals, but also to check the balance on their bank account.

Taking into account the concluded contracts of the considered brand of the banking market of the Russian Federation with other major players in the segment, UniCredit card holders have the opportunity to cash out cash on the terms described below.

Cash withdrawal from UniCredit cards: conditions

If a person who has issued a card at a branch of a financial institution in the Russian capital withdraws cash using, for example, UniCredit Bank ATMs in Moscow and the Moscow Region, he will not have to pay any commission for this transaction. Similar rules also exist for cash withdrawal through the infrastructure provided by:

- ICD;

- Raiffeisenbank;

- Uralsib.

If a person withdraws cash from the UniCredit card at ATMs of other credit and financial organizations, then the first 2 withdrawals can be made without commission, and then - subject to deduction of 1% of the amount of received funds, but not less than 300 rubles.

It may be noted that certain preferences have been set for clients of UniCredit Bank salary projects. The specific conditions for cash withdrawals in this case should be clarified in the offices of the relevant credit and financial organization.

Replenishment of funds: conditions

In turn, other conditions are defined for the operations of replenishment of the UniCredit plastic card. What are they?

If a person uses UniCredit Bank ATMs in Moscow and the Moscow Region to replenish a card issued by an appropriate credit and financial institution, he will not pay any commission. In turn, it is not always possible for UniCredit cardholders to use the infrastructure of partner organizations - in particular, those with which cash-out agreements have been concluded. This, firstly, requires even greater compatibility of the card service infrastructure, and secondly, the conclusion of additional agreements.

Note that between UniCredit and Raiffeisen so far no such contracts have been signed. Raiffeisenbank customers, in particular, can replenish their cards at ATMs of their credit and financial institution, as well as in payment devices from MKB, MDM and BINBANK. The use of UniCredit infrastructure in this case is not provided. Therefore, it is best to replenish the card to customers of the respective institution in ATMs belonging to the organization and included in its network.

Functions of UniCredit ATMs

As we noted above, devices for servicing plastic cards issued by the banking brand in question are capable of both issuing and accepting cash. But this, of course, is not all of their possibilities.

So, UniCredit ATMs are adapted to carry out a number of operations on the personal account of the card holder. Another noteworthy feature of these devices is the ability to quickly transfer funds to the credit account of a bank client. One of the specialties of UniCredit is the issuance of loans.

It can be noted that a large number of ATMs of the financial institution in question can issue cash not only in rubles, but also in dollars and euros.

Using UniCredit cards abroad

As we noted above, UniCredit is a European brand. This is due to the fact of the existence of special agreements between the Russian UniCredit Bank and foreign banks, which are part of the corresponding financial group with it.

In particular, under one of the agreements, holders of cards issued in the Russian Federation get the opportunity in some cases to withdraw cash abroad at Unicredit ATMs without a commission. Moreover, currency conversion - if it is different, is carried out in accordance with the internal rate of the financial institution. This opportunity gives very tangible benefits to Russian travelers who travel to Europe.

Of course, if a person pays for purchases in stores using a card, then a commission if payment is made using the international VISA or MasterCard system is not charged in any case.
