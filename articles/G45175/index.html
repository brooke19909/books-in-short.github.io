The most popular hotels in Makhachkala

Makhachkala is a southern sunny city located between the Caspian Sea and the foothills of the Caucasus, at the foot of Tarki-Tau. It is the capital of the Republic of Dagestan, as well as the third city in the region in terms of population.



In addition, at the moment it is one of the fastest growing cities in our country: industrial production is actively growing in this place, new enterprises and factories are appearing, while the city is constantly improving.

There is a Uytash airport in the city, which receives regular flights from St. Petersburg, Moscow, Yekaterinburg, Sochi, etc. A bus station is located 17 km from the nearest microdistrict, although tourists should be aware that there are no regular buses to Makhachkala. But to Kaspiysk, located nearby, there are regular minibuses. At this point, you can catch any bus that goes to the city.

Makhachkala hotels meet the necessary modern standards of service: city guests are given the opportunity to live in comfortable and clean rooms with modern appliances and furniture, internet and free breakfasts, a huge number of additional services. The best hotels for travelers are the 3-star President, Petrovsk and Lord complexes.

Hotel "Seaside"

Hotel "Primorskaya" (Makhachkala) has been hosting guests since 1986. A feature of the institution is the placement of guests by invitation, recommendations and letters to the administration.



Hotel "Primorsky" is located 100 meters from the coast of the sea and is able to accommodate guests in 67 rooms of various classes: luxury, apartments, standard, economy (each room has its own loggia with sea views). Separate 5-room apartments are provided for various officials. Moreover, each room has the necessary technical equipment (refrigerator, TV, air conditioning), as well as a set of accessories (iron, hairdryer, telephone).

Cost: from 2400 rubles / day.

Makhachkala, hotel "Leningrad"

Thanks to low prices and high quality service, the Leningrad Hotel is rightfully one of the most important places in the whole city.



Near it there are: a puppet theater, the Drama Russian Theater, museums, the National Library, various cafes and restaurants. Cozy rooms are suitable for a pleasant stay, as well as productive work.

Cost: from 1500 rubles / day.

Hotel "Petrovsk"

Hotel "Petrovsk" is one of the most comfortable and famous hotels in the city. Its building was built in 1994, and in 2007 it was completely renovated, each year the rooms are being renovated.



A variety of leisure services, business meetings or conferences, comfortable rooms, and the infinitely hospitable staff of this Makhachkala hotel will make your stay here unforgettable. It should be noted that the institution is located in an ecologically clean area, near Lake Ak-Gel and the Caspian Sea.

Cost: from 700 rubles / day.

Hotel "Argo"

Considering the hotels of Makhachkala, it is worth highlighting Argo, one of the most comfortable hotels in the city. He opened his doors in 2008, but is now very popular with locals and visitors alike.



The responsive and welcoming, attentive and polite hotel staff is happy to welcome their guests at any time.

Cost: from 1420 rubles / day.

Hotel Metropol

This Makhachkala hotel has a very advantageous location - it is located 10 minutes from the shore of the Caspian Sea and is suitable for travelers and tourists, as well as for businessmen or business travelers.



Affordable prices and a diverse number of rooms make it possible to choose a room to your liking. Each of them is equipped with modern appliances, comfortable furniture and plumbing.

Cost: from 3000 rubles / day.
