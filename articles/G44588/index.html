Sanatorium Profile

Any person will find help here. It is not for nothing that it was Green Grove that gained such popularity. Sanatorium (Ufa) - a diversified health resort. There are a number of specialized departments. The local hydropathic center is a real gem of these places. Current directions

The priority is the cardiology department. This includes the treatment of pathologies of the circulatory system, rehabilitation after a heart attack and heart surgery. A high percentage of return to normal life among patients with coronary artery bypass grafting can be noted.

Excellent results are shown by the treatment of the digestive system. Many factors contribute to this, including the favorable environmental situation, which is famous for the "Green Grove". The sanatorium (Ufa) carefully monitors the nutrition of its patients, cooks daily prepare about 400 dishes for all types of diets. Proper nutrition is the basis of treatment. Doctors additionally prescribe the necessary procedures, therapeutic baths and drug therapy.

Diseases of ENT organs are cured on average twice as fast, without the risk of relapse. If you or your children suffer from chronic bronchitis, be sure to come for treatment. In addition, doctors have extensive experience working with diseases of the nervous and endocrine systems, skin and metabolism, problems in the field of gynecology and urology. This is not a complete list of those who can be helped by Green Grove. Sanatorium (Ufa) is the best way to quickly get rid of the disease and stock up on energy for the whole year.

Number of rooms

Sanatorium "Green Grove" (Ufa) invites you to stay in one of four buildings. There are single and double rooms with a bathroom, a fridge and a TV. There are suites with an equipped living room. Also, guests are offered a VIP cottage, where each room has a meeting room, a billiard room, a sauna and a mini-pool. The attentive staff will fulfill all your requests.

Booking Policy

You can book a room in advance so that at the appointed time you do not have to hear a polite answer that all rooms are busy. To do this, you will need to make a 100% prepayment. If the plans have changed, it is possible 30 days before the scheduled arrival to cancel the reservation with a refund of the entire amount spent. If you refuse later, then the entire amount of the payment is withheld in the form of a fine. Children under 5 years old stay free of charge. If your child is from 5 to 12 years old, then the cost of 15% of the adult voucher is paid.
