Severodonetsk is a city of regional significance in Ukraine in the Luhansk region, it is one of the largest scientific, cultural and industrial centers of Donbass, a city of creators of computer technology, chemists, builders.

His biography and birth are constantly associated with the construction of a chemical plant in this place. The very first foundation stones of the city were laid back in the thirties. Together with the chemical plant, the workers' settlement Lishkhimstroy also grew. In 1950, it was renamed Severodonetsk, and received the status of a city in 1958. With its development, together this region has taken on an industrial, modern look.
