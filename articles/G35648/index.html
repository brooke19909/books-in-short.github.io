Hendershot Generator. Hendershot Generator Circuit

The creation of an energy generator that works completely autonomously has long been disturbing the minds of mankind. It is difficult to boast of particular success here, although, if you recall the Chizhevsky chandelier, this direction does not look hopeless at all. In this article, we will talk about the Hendershot fuel-free generator . The scheme of this device (probable) will allow everyone to check the truth of the information.

Performance history



The inventor of the device in question is the American physicist Lester Hendershot. For the first time, its generator was presented to the general public in 1981 in Toronto, where at that time a congress was held dedicated to the energy of the gravitational field. Then it was reported that it works due to the presence of a magnetic field near the Earth. It follows from this that its location and the orientation with respect to the south and north poles play an important role.

The inventor himself - Lestor Hendershot - did not live to see Congress. He died in 1961 (officially committed suicide).

General information on creation and related processes

The first mention of this device belongs to the range of 1927-1930. According to available records, Hendershot received a usable device, whose power was 200-300 watts. Then, for a short time, Leicester is honored by the American press as a national hero. But soon the praise in his direction is replaced by accusations of quackery and fraud.

At the same time, the inventor himself is injured by electric current and no longer speaks with his developments. According to his son, Mark Hendershot, his father received 25 thousand dollars for not disclosing information about the generator.

That's all. And let's go directly to the device. Are you interested in the Hendershot generator? Component exclusion scheme? But before dealing with such issues, let's find out what is needed directly for the device itself.

Necessary materials



So, you will need the following components:

  1. The coil of copper enameled wire is 0.95 millimeters in diameter and 50 meters long.
  2. Two parts of a copper insulated PVC wire, diameter - 1.5 millimeters, length - 18 meters. For the convenience of subsequent work, you can use wires with insulation of different colors.
  3. Two non-polarized capacitors. Each of them should have a capacity of 500 microfarads.
  4. 150 wooden rods, the diameter of which is 3 millimeters.
  5. Four non-polarized capacitors. The capacity of each of them should be 1000 microfarads.
  6. Two transformers with a factor of 1: 5, designed for operation with a voltage of 110-220 volts.
  7. One panel made of wood, plywood or particleboard, with sides 100/60 centimeters.
  8. Copper wire with PVC insulation, whose length is 10 meters, diameter - 1 millimeter.
  9. One rectangular steel bar, with parameters 10 / 0.5 / 2 centimeters.
  10. An external outlet rated for 110-220 volts.
  11. A sheet of cardboard, wood, plexiglass (but not metal) with sides of 10 by 10 centimeters.
  12. Two guide rails that are used in furniture fittings (but without wheels).
  13. Magnetic bar of rectangular or cylindrical shape. Diameter - 1.5 centimeters, length - 10 cm.
  14. Two cylindrical steel bars. Length - 8 centimeters, diameter - 2 cm.

Start assembly



We begin to produce a Hendershot generator. A do-it-yourself scheme can be implemented without problems, but for this you must adhere to the instructions. To get started, take the panel. Draw two circles on it, the diameter of which will be 100 millimeters. The distance between their centers should be 50 centimeters. If you do not understand how and what to implement, look at the diagram. In its upper right corner these parameters are indicated.

On circles, mark the points at equal intervals. Then they need to be drilled with a 3 mm drill. Insert wooden rods into them. They should rise by 70 millimeters. If there is an excess of this parameter, it must be cut off. Then gently straighten the wooden sticks after trimming.

Take a wire whose cross section is 1.5 millimeters by 2, and begin to lay it between them. It is necessary to make 12 turns for each coil. Then we take a wire of 2.5 millimeters by 2. It is already necessary to lay it, too, 12 turns per coil.

We have two types with different color designations. Each type of wire should be wound 6 times. Remember that the coils should have the same number of turns. Also, do not forget to leave 50-60 millimeters on top so that you can connect to them. During assembly, it is advisable to lightly press the turns with a wooden ruler so that they lie down as they should.

Making coils for controlling a magnetic resonator

To do this, wrap two cylindrical rods with a layer of wax paper. Then 40 turns of wire are superimposed on it, the cross-section of which is 1.5 millimeters. Using furniture fittings, plastic or cardboard, we produce a movable mechanism. We fix two previously made coils on it with epoxy glue . But they must move without significant effort and distortions. The length of the guide elements should not exceed 250 millimeters.

Place capacitors



Now we need devices for 500 microfarads. Glue a double-sided adhesive tape to their bottom. It is necessary to place the capacitors in the center of the coils made. After the devices are placed at 500 microfarads, the same should be done with devices for 1000 microfarads. Two capacitors are placed on the panel on the outside of the coils. The devices are non-electrolytic, therefore, have a significant size. Therefore, it will not be superfluous to think about their compact placement. Install the remaining components of the circuit. Transformers must be fixed to the panel.

Installation of construction

So we are approaching the end of creation, and soon it will be possible to check the Hendershot generator. Free energy, do-it-yourself instructions, a diagram - all this can be obtained and done by devoting several hours to the production process. All elements are connected thanks to soldering. Also pay particular attention to assembly accuracy. Try to adhere to the specified scheme as much as possible.

Also, be careful not to mix the end and the beginning of the winding when connecting the coils and capacitors. Also check for tight mounting. Then you can already connect the outlet. To do this, it must be installed on the panel in a convenient place for you. All open cores must be wrapped with insulating tape. A heat shrink tube can be used for this purpose. In this case, security must be put in the first place, because the price for breaking it is your health and even life. Therefore, do not spare insulating material.

Adjustable



The device itself is already assembled. Let's work with the magnetic resonator block. As a load, you can choose a lamp. We connect it to the device and begin to move the coils to the magnet in order to get maximum work efficiency. This can be judged by the strength of the lamp. Once the maximum effect is obtained, stop the adjustment. During this process, do not touch the iron rods on which the coils are wound. If you need to work with them, use dielectric material.
