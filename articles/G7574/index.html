Valley of Roses (Kislovodsk): history, description and location

Valley of Roses. In Kislovodsk, this is the name of a part of the spa park. The name itself evokes a feeling of something beautiful, sublime, and a dreamy mood envelops and makes you forget all the worries.

History

Speaking about the emergence of the Valley of Roses, it is impossible to separate it from the whole complex. So, in 1823 by By order of General Ermolov, a park was laid. From the origins of the famous Narzan along the Olkhovka River, planting of deciduous trees began.

Then there was no talk of romance, and he was simply called the boulevard or the official garden. But it could not be otherwise, because the breakdown of the park was carried out by the lower ranks of the military, who took part in protecting these territories from the highlanders. This was the first phase of construction.


Then they began to plant shrubs and flowers. And at the same time, the landscaped Tsar‚Äôs platform appeared, later renamed the Valley of Roses.

The second stage began in 1903. The park was expanded due to Cossack lands. And the main organizer was Albert Regel. Through the efforts of this then-famous gardener, the park became more picturesque, the list of planted trees was replenished with ordinary ash, English oak, Caucasian pine and others. And the Tsar‚Äôs platform was enriched with the composition "Russian round dance", consisting of European fir trees.

In the first half of the twentieth century, landscaping of mountain slopes continued.

When the Rose Valley blooms

It is naturally better to come to these places at a time when the Valley of Roses in Kislovodsk blooms. The peak falls in the middle of summer. Then it is especially interesting to walk on a square platform, planted with roses of different varieties.

Flowers of various types and colors grow in square sectors. These sections are separated by convenient tracks. You can approach, enjoy the look of flowers, their magnificence, variety, and breathe in aromas that also differ in their shades.


But even at another time, when roses are still gaining strength, walking in the park is very pleasant and beneficial for the body.

In addition to flower beds, tourists in the valley have other popular places that they must visit and take pictures there. For example, a huge stone crocodile with its mouth wide open, lurking under the trees, the observation deck itself and the recently appeared snow-white arbor, called the "Temple of the Air."

It is worth mentioning that you can look at the Valley of Roses from the height of the cable car.

Beneficial effect

For many years, the Valley of Roses has been attracting those wishing to improve their health. Kislovodsk is located in places where mountain air, a warm climate and healing springs are combined.

Walking around the resort park is not just a pleasant pastime. Staying here is an element of climatotherapy, aerophytotherapy and landscape therapy.


Here the technique of special health walks is practiced, developed back in the late 19th century and called "terrenkur".

Natural properties and human labor have created here one of the best places for treatment. Mountainous landscape and oxygenated air, healing water and trees, which are not just a design element, but a factor that improves well-being. All this is combined in a place glorified in classical literature.
