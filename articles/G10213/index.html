Lamp for plants: the main advantages

Growing any plant at home or in greenhouses can not do without additional artificial light. Today, such a fixture as an LED lamp for plants is very popular.

Such samples meet the energy efficiency requirements necessary today and do not present any danger and discomfort for the eyes. Gradually, incandescent lamps disappear from use, and in 2014 they will cease to be produced. This, of course, is a small loss, since everyone has long known that in order to save electricity, you need to buy LED lamps for plants (saving more than eighty percent is guaranteed). These lamps are characterized by a number of unique features. for example, they have five radiation spectra, four of which correspond to the most important physiological needs of plants during photosynthesis, and the fifth type of diode is white and gives the plants everything else necessary for growth. If you choose the right ratio of diodes, then you can achieve active plant growth. In addition, products of this type have other advantages that deserve attention.





Advantages of LED-lamps for plants

The main thing is safety when using. A LED plant lamp , by definition, cannot explode and is made of a very durable material. The service life is also quite impressive - it can work more than 50,000 hours.

The ability to control the intensity of the LED by changing the current that flows through the crystal is also an advantage of this type of product. If you decide to purchase new lamps for plants, then you should know that they are fully compatible with a standard base.

Currently, specially designed high-pressure sodium lamps are used for floriculture as illumination, but they consume many times more energy. But the usual LED lamp for plants provides the spectrum of the lamp optimal for photosynthesis, while the energy consumption indicators are quite acceptable.

It should be noted that the built-in ventilation system and the absence of constant thermal radiation make it possible to use lamps without additional devices.





Where are these products used and how can they be purchased?

The LED lamp for plants is used to grow flowers and vegetables that lack daylight (at home or in greenhouses). It is very convenient to operate, since such a lamp is designed for a standard voltage of 220 volts and, as already mentioned, can be used in traditional lighting devices. You can buy led-lamps for plants in any specialized store. LED lamps can also be used in large industrial enterprises, in hospitals, schools (and other educational institutions), shops. These lamps are universal and consume much less energy than their counterparts, as also mentioned above. All these factors determine the demand for this product.
