about the project

A rural village is a great idea applicable to modern life. Kemerovo, although it is not a large metropolis, nevertheless, its residents will not abandon their own home away from fuss, noise and dust. The building area will be 75,000 square meters. In accordance with the project documentation, several low-rise buildings, townhouses and necessary infrastructure will appear on them.

Developer

New buildings in Kemerovo are proposed by several major developers. So, SDS-Finance is a large construction company, on the account of which quite successfully implemented projects. Many residents of the city say that they can easily invest in the project of this developer, since all houses are rented out on time without any complaints. The company specializes in comfortable residential complexes with its own infrastructure, putting safety and convenience of residents at the head of each project.





Location

For the construction of the residential complex "European Provinces" (Kemerovo) a plot of land was selected just 700 meters from the city limits. This is an ecologically clean area: clean air, plenty of greenery, river. That is how many present the ideal place to live. The responses of the first buyers who visited the facility at the stage of laying the foundation of the houses focused on a good location. Residents are somewhat distant from the noise and dust of the city, but at the same time they can easily get to the city by private car or public transport in order to use all the necessary services and make purchases.





Construction Features

Many are interested in the sequence of construction work on the territory of the residential complex "European Provinces" (Kemerovo). When is the change at home? When will residents receive the keys? All interest holders are asking these questions. At this stage, the project is under active construction, but houses No. 11 and 12 have already been commissioned, and house No. 22 will be commissioned at the end of 2017.

All residents of the complex note the excellent soundproofing of the houses: even the noisiest neighbors will never disturb your peace.

Infrastructure

The country village "European Provinces" is provided with all the necessary infrastructure. The project does not provide for the construction of large shopping centers, hospitals and schools, but a kindergarten with 100 places will appear on the territory of the complex. Residents of the complex note that at the moment the kindergarten is quite enough to provide places for all the kids living in it. By the way, a kindergarten has already been built, began its activities. Qualified specialists, experienced educators work here. Moreover, the kindergarten is equipped with a video surveillance system that provides maximum comfort for babies and their parents.





The main decoration of the complex "European Provinces" (Kemerovo) will be a square with a fountain. According to the plan of the developer, it will unite all the inhabitants of the village, it is here that they will celebrate holidays, celebrations, it is here that they will relax and spend time with loved ones. The developer supported the modern concept of sports development, so there will be enough sports fields on the territory, there is a football field, and the construction of a tennis court is planned. Children will be able to frolic in excellent playgrounds. Due to the special coating, it was possible to reduce injuries during active and outdoor games. The first residents of the village note that the playgrounds are reliable and of high quality, all slides, horizontal bars are made in accordance with the basic requirements, so you can safely let the children play.

Of course, the residents of the complex would like to see more infrastructure, but in this case their absence is compensated by their proximity to the city. As a result, schools, hospitals, shops and shopping centers are very close.

Ecology

The developer has chosen an excellent place for the construction of the village "European Provinces" (Kemerovo). Customer reviews and residents are enthusiastic, it breathes differently. An apartment or a house in the village is an excellent investment in your future and the future of your children. Recreation areas will be equipped on the territory of the complex, right on the picturesque bank of the river: it is here that all residents will gather. Nearby there are no harmful industries, so you can not worry about the health of your near and dear ones.





Houses, apartments

Apartment buildings being built on the territory of the complex "European Provinces" (Kemerovo) are represented by 5 floors and 98 apartments. All houses are being built using precast monolithic technology. Here you can purchase one- and two-room apartments ranging in size from 32 to 57 square meters.

The co-investors of the complex and the first residents note that the one-room apartments are represented by spacious studios with a combined bathroom and wardrobe and full-fledged odnushki with an isolated kitchen and bedroom. Two-room apartments turned out to be quite compact, but practical and comfortable. The layout is represented by a small kitchen of 9-10 square meters, combined bathroom, wardrobe and isolated rooms. The apartments turned out to be not as spacious as we would like, but it was due to the competent distribution of even a small space area that managed to make the property so affordable. Townhouses are more spacious, all residents receive a garage and a small plot of land.





Price policy

At the moment, one-room apartment in the complex "European Provinces" (Kemerovo) can be purchased for just 1,170,000 rubles. Affordable cost for housing located in an ecologically clean area.

To summarize

LCD "European Provinces" (Kemerovo) - an excellent project in which it was possible to combine the delights of suburban life and urban comfort. Reviews of the first tenants are mostly positive, many even note that there is no such well-thought-out complex in Kemerovo. New buildings in Kemerovo - an excellent investment. That is why we strongly recommend that you pay attention to the village if you are choosing an apartment for yourself and be sure to visit the construction site in order to draw up your own idea.
