The most popular hotels of Severodvinsk

Severodvinsk is a small city located in the Arkhangelsk region. It is located at the confluence of the Northern Dvina into the famous White Sea. Having modest parameters, the city is the most important economic and military center of our country. It represents the main base of nuclear shipbuilding in Russia, so it is often visited with various business visits.

In this article we will consider the most popular hotels in Severodvinsk.

Hotel Volna

Hotel "Volna" (Severodvinsk) offers to accommodate one hundred rooms created in bright colors. Each of them has everything that is necessary for productive work and comfortable living - a TV, Internet access and a refrigerator.





There is a restaurant on site, where guests can order any dishes of European cuisine. The hotel also has a 24-hour reception desk, which simplifies departures and arrivals at night. Free parking is available on site.

Hotel Nikolsky Posad, Severodvinsk

The hotel is located in the very center of the city, on its main street. Nearby are: the main post office, the city administration building, banks, entertainment centers and restaurants, an ice rink, a swimming pool, gyms and shops.

The room stock of the institution includes 63 rooms of several categories. Among them are single and double, as well as studio and luxury rooms. Pets are not allowed. All rooms have comfortable furniture, a TV with sixty channels, an electric kettle, a refrigerator, a telephone, dishes, and a shower. Suites also have water heaters.





The hotel has a restaurant, cafe and bar. At this place, visitors are pleased to offer a wide selection of drinks and dishes. The business hotel has meeting rooms and provides free internet access. For an additional fee, guests are offered the services of ordering a taxi, train, air tickets, and laundry.

"Park 9"

Hotel "Parkovaya 9" is a great option for people who want to stay at an affordable price in Severodvinsk. It offers rooms with a fridge and microwave. Not far from here is a factory park where you can take a leisurely stroll.

The room fund of this Severodvinsk hotel is decorated in an extremely simple style. The institution has its own small cafe, where you can order delicious European dishes. In this case, you can get in some 15 minutes to the city center.
