Location, rooms and prices

The hotel is located in a square eight-story building with four facades. From the windows of one - a panoramic view of the sea, from the other two - a side view - on the right and left, the windows of the fourth facade look at the hotel grounds, which, in principle, is not inferior in color to the other three options. The hotel "Concrete Brut" rooms occupy the second to seventh floors. The first is occupied by the Buffet restaurant, a spa complex, a lobby bar and a reception. On the eighth, there is a restaurant with an open terrace, which offers magnificent views of the sea.

Of the one hundred and seventy-five rooms, sixty are Standart, twenty four are Superior, fifteen are Superior Mini Duplex, twenty six are Deluxe, eight are Deluxe Mini Duplex with a terrace and direct sea view, and six are similar with a side view.
