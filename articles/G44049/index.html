The recreation center "Oka", Ivanchino. Description. Reviews

At the modern ski resort Oka in Ivanchino (Ryazan Region), tourists can not only enjoy their favorite sports, but also enjoy the magnificent nature that inspired Sergei Yesenin. The village of Konstantinovo, in which the great Russian poet was born, is only a ten minute drive from here.

Description

From the high hill on which the Oka recreation center in Ivanchino is located, magnificent views of the river open. On the landscaped area there are hotel buildings and a cottage. Guests can use the services of the bath complex, the camp site has its own cafe. There is a play area for children. Equipped with a sports field for volleyball and badminton, there are tables for fans of table tennis.

In winter, tourists come here to ski, sled, snowboard, snowboard. For them, a comfortable lift works at the camp site. The necessary sports equipment, both for summer sports and for winter ones, you have the opportunity to rent. Guests can leave their vehicles in the car park. Only for vacationers health resorts by the river are equipped with a sandy beach. You can also go fishing on the shore, and there are barbecue facilities for picnic lovers at the camp site. Of course, the question arises of where is the village of Ivanchino and the recreation center "Oka", how to get to it.

  • From the M5 highway, turn off the sign "S.A. Yesenin Museum-Reserve".
  • Take the main road to the village of Ramenki (do not call into Rybnoe).
  • Where the road forks into the old and new, turn onto the old.
  • At the entrance to the village of Kuzminskoye, immediately turn right.
  • Having passed Kuzminskoe, and then Aksenovo, you will find yourself in Ivanchino.

Accommodation for vacationers

The recreation center "Oka" in Ivanchino offers several accommodation options in three hotel buildings or a summer house. The numbers are presented on any wallet - there are both economy and apartments. Breakfast is included. Each room is equipped with the following amenities:

  • one double or two single beds (can accommodate extra bed, as well as a crib);
  • a bathroom with a shower;
  • TV
  • a refrigerator;
  • internal telephone.

In the summer house, some rooms have air conditioning, and two are equipped with kitchen units. Some rooms have a balcony.

Additional services

At the modern ski hostel, guests are provided with many services to make their vacation more vivid and eventful:

  • There are slides for tubing, ski slopes at the Oka recreation center in Ivanchino, and instructor services are offered.
  • To get to the place of descent, you can use the modern ski lift.
  • In winter, guests are also given the opportunity to go ice skating.
  • In summer there are conditions for lovers of cycling.
  • Equipped sports grounds.
  • There is a rental service for sports equipment.
  • Meals are organized in a cafe.
  • Banquet facilities are available.
  • There is a private beach.
  • After an active day, guests can relax in the bathhouse.
  • Interesting excursions are organized for campers.
  • Car owners are provided with a parking space.
  • You can go fishing on the banks of the Oka.

The recreation center "Oka" (Ivanchino). Tourists reviews

Guests of the camp site share in their reviews both positive and negative impressions about the rest:

  • Tourists really like the location of the recreation center - in a picturesque place, clean air, a beautiful river.
  • Nearby there are attractions, you can visit the homeland of Yesenin, in the village of Konstantinovo, as well as in St. John the Theological Monastery, in the village of Poshchupovo.
  • Fans of winter sports really like tubing slides, however, they have to stand in lines for a long time.
  • Everyone notes that the cost in Oka is lower than at neighboring recreation centers.
  • Among the shortcomings, guests note small rooms with old furniture and poor soundproofing.
  • And there is a serious point - the left side of the ski slope is not equipped with a protective net.
  • And keep in mind that in the summer at the recreation center there are a lot of mosquitoes.
