The recreation center "Pleskov": address, contacts, description of rooms, infrastructure, photo

If you are tired of the bustle of a big city, go to the recreation center "Pleskov". Here, two characteristics are organically combined, which form the basis of quality relaxation - modern amenities and untouched nature. Also here you can hold a corporate event or an unforgettable celebration.

Location

The recreation center "Pleskov" is located in the village of Pechki, which is 23 km from Pskov. The institution is located on the shores of Lake Pskov. To get here by car, set the coordinates in the navigator 57.850607, 27.966835. Or follow this instruction:

  • Driving along the Kirov highway, you will see the memorial of the Red Army.
  • From it, turn onto Krestovsky highway, through which you will enter the city.
  • Moving along Oktyabrsky Avenue, get to the Pskov Kremlin.
  • In the area near the Kremlin, turn onto the bridge and follow the Riga highway to the village of Neyelovo.
  • Turn right towards Kirovsk.
  • After 15 km, you will reach the village of Pechki.
  • Immediately after the bus stop, turn right to Lake Pskov.
  • Having passed through the village, you will reach a pine forest, where the recreation center "Pleskov" is located.

Fitness Centre

On the territory of the recreation center "Pleskov" in the Pskov region there is an excellent two-story fitness center, which without exaggeration can be called the highlight of the institution. It is popular all year round for both guests and residents of Pskov. Here guests are available the following options:

  • 25 meter pool with separate section for children. Equipped with underwater lights, hydromassage and geysers. A water purification system is used that does not imply the use of chlorine.
  • Finnish saunas, Turkish baths, aromatic saunas and a jacuzzi are great options for relaxing and improving your well-being.
  • Gym with modern equipment and a full range of equipment. Pneumatic simulators allow people with varying degrees of physical fitness to train.

A visit is possible only by appointment, if you are not a guest at the Pleskov recreation center in Pskov. Phone for inquiries and records is on the institution's website.

Sports complex

Another pleasant moment for lovers of active pastime is the sports complex on the territory of the Pleskov hotel. It includes such features:

  • Gymnasium 19 by 42 meters with a safe rubber crumb flooring. The hall can be used for running or playing soccer, volleyball or even tennis. The presence of stands and a coaching room allows you to host amateur and professional sports competitions.
  • Table tennis, billiards and many other games for an interesting pastime.
  • Comfortable locker rooms, combined with shower, toilet and sauna.

If you are interested in the sports complex of the Pleskov Hotel, a telephone for information and rent is available on the recreation center website.

If you want to relax in the lap of nature, "Splash" will be the perfect option for you. Here are the possibilities the recreation center offers in this context:

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, this institution was not without flaws and shortcomings. Here are the points at which guests of the recreation center accentuate attention:

  • in multi-bed rooms there are not enough bathrooms (for example, in a house with 4 bedrooms only two bathrooms are provided);
  • weak pressure of water in the shower (especially in the mornings and evenings, when all guests are engaged in water procedures);
  • the quality of food is poor (the taste of fried foods seems especially dubious);
  • almost all services at the recreation center are paid (even upon entering the territory 300 rubles of deposit will be charged);
  • free access to the pool only in the morning from 07:00 to 09:00;
  • the territory is very crowded and noisy;
  • the road leading to the recreation center is very bumpy and broken;
  • Surprising is the lack of electric kettles in the rooms (even in suites);
  • in some rooms, a roof leaks during rain;
  • very thin walls in the guest rooms, which determines a strong audibility;
  • near the recreation center, someone constantly burns trash at night, and smoke spreads throughout the territory and enters the rooms;
  • there is no nanny or animator in the kids club (in fact, the children are left to their own devices and need constant monitoring by their parents);

  • poor service in the restaurant - the waiters work slowly and quite often get confused in orders;
  • the number of rooms has long been in need of repair and updating of equipment (plumbing is in especially poor condition);
  • in the rooms you can often see ants;
