How to make a new sweater from old sweaters: remaking woolen products

Oh, those beloved wool sweaters! Sometimes for years they have been lying on shelves unnecessarily, and the hostess does not know what to wear for work. Old sweaters, half-halves should not be rushed to throw away, because a lot of beautiful and useful things can be made from them. Real needlewomen manage to make a new sweater from old sweaters. And there are many ideas for remaking these warm products.



What to make from an old sweater

If a semi-large coat was lying in the wardrobe, then you can make a warm dress out of it. There are a lot of pattern options. In addition, it can be decorated with leather details on the collar, elbows, belt. You can also make an elegant dress out of your favorite sweater, you just have to sew a lace skirt on it, add lacework cuffs, a collar.

An ingenious and simple solution would be to make a warm winter hat out of a woolen product. To do this, you just need to spread the sweater, attach to it a sample of the style of the cap you need, so that the elastic is at the bottom. Then cut the two sides of the product and sew.

Sometimes recyclables of different shades accumulate in whole piles. This is another reason for making the original plaid. It is best if these sweaters first fall off a bit during long-term washing and high spin. After drying, square flaps are cut from the sweaters and stitched. You can alternate squares of different colors, you get a fun mosaic, or you can use calm tones.

All women love to choose bags for outfits, and so a homemade version of the old things is very suitable for everyday outfits. It is only necessary to cut a blank in the form of a bag from a sweater, sew it and turn it inside out.

What else can you do from old sweaters with your own hands? How do you like the idea to create a very fashionable accessory - a scarf snood? A knitwear is best for this occasion. The neck and sides, in this case, are cut off, and the sides are sewn together.

Alteration of the old sweater can serve as an occasion for the creation of cute woolen toys. Small animals are easily obtained from the sleeves of products stuffed with cotton or synthetic winterizer. It can be cubs, squirrels, bunnies and even octopuses.



New sweater from old sweaters

Such a fashionable alteration can be done by crossing a sweater with a blouse. The result is a stylish, extraordinary product. You can refresh any sweater by sewing it under an elastic band, lace or an insert of thin fabric.

Another option is to cross the details of the old sweater and shirt. For example, cut the sleeves and sides from a sweater, and take the front and back from the shirt. You can also sew cuffs from a shirt to the sleeves. It turns out a very nice little thing. Something like this is a new sweater from old sweaters.



Covers for pillows and tablets

The back and shelf of the sweater will serve to make woolen pillow covers that will decorate the bed or sofa. To do this, you need to find out the dimensions of the pillow, cut two identical squares or rectangles from them. Then they need to be sewn from the wrong side, leaving a secret hole. Then the cover needs to be turned out and put on a pillow. Such a warm pillowcase will especially please its owners in the winter.

Not only a pillow can be enclosed in an original case, a smartphone, tablet and glasses are also suitable for this. It is carried out in the same way as for a pillow, only it is sewn from three sides. Similarly, you can sew covers for vases and candlesticks.



Planter for indoor plants

Any woolen product will help turn a flower pot into a decorative thing. To do this, you need a piece of an old sweater, thread, needle and glue. Then the original envelope is sewn, in which crocuses, violets, hyacinths can flaunt.

An old sweater can be cut into strips, then knitted with knots. Here is the original hanging planter for the veranda!

Skirts, mittens, socks

In the fashion of recent seasons, warm knitted skirts. Fashionistas do not have to buy a new one, you can safely make a new thing from an old sweater. To do this, cut off the bottom of the sweater with an elastic band of the desired length. Then the cropped side is swept and an elastic band is inserted for fixing. Only half an hour takes such a product.

Mittens and mitts for frost are also not necessary to buy. Quite fashionable fingerless mitts will come out of the sleeves of the old sweater. And according to the pattern, you can very simply sew warm mittens or socks.



Beginner Tips for Knitting Sweaters

Knitting a sweater with knitting needles for beginners is not easy, you need to include patience and attention. It is possible to complete such a product in 5-6 evenings. What is suitable for beginner knitters? It is best for the first time to take half-woolen threads with acrylic, medium density. They will lie flat, not make the model heavier, and not fall over. On average, sweaters will need 600-700 g of yarn, depending on size.

The easiest way to learn is to knit a sweater with knitting needles for beginners from below with raglan. To begin with, 140 loops are typed on circular knitting needles (for size 44-46). Next, about 40 cm are knitted with the necessary pattern, the product is postponed. On separate hosiery needles, sleeves are individually knitted to the desired length. Further, the sleeves and the lower part are connected, and the raglan is tied from the bottom. Loop reduction is performed in four places, two loops through a row. Raglan should be approximately 20 cm. The remaining loops are knitted with a garter stitch, which tends to twist into a steering wheel. Get a kind of neck. This classic version will look quite impressive!
