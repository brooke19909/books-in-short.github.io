Koprino (recreation center). Koprino Bay, Rybinsk

Koprino is the first cottage village on the Yaroslavl coast. It is a natural park with coniferous forests and golden sandy beaches. The village is located in the Rybinsk district. Further in the article it will be described in more detail about the features of this tourist zone located in this area. We also learn about the services that are offered here and the conditions for accommodating visitors.



Features

In this place, all favorable conditions for yachting and for making exciting trips on the water are created. One of the main features that the Koprino Bay (Rybinsk) possesses is clean air. Here it is saturated with a mixture of the aroma of the river and pine needles. The cottage village perfectly combines a nature reserve with picturesque nature and a resort with modern infrastructure. The promenade and beaches are clean and tidy.

The yacht club harbor provides various services, including storage and refueling of boats. The recreation center located in the village of Koprino (Yaroslavl) is notable for its high level of service. Here you can find entertainment for every taste, and bars and restaurants offer their visitors delicious cuisine. For tourists, modern equipment is rented, a children's club is opened and animation programs are developed.

Koprino (recreation center) is located 40 km from the city, on the banks of the Volga, in a pine forest. This wonderful corner of nature will provide a great mood and give a charge of positive energy for a long time. Rest in "Koprino" (Rybinsk) is an excellent fishing trip for berries and mushrooms, clean air and magnificent nature. On the territory of the tourist zone, celebrations, anniversaries and important events can be noted. A banquet room with all amenities and attentive staff will ensure a pleasant pastime in Koprino. The recreation center is open to guests all year round and can accommodate up to 120 people.



Services and entertainment for vacationers

In the territory occupied by the recreation center "Koprino" (Rybinsk), there are two Russian baths for 4 and 10 places, a stable, thanks to which it is possible to ride and sled in the winter. Special places for barbecue and fishing were equipped at the Rybinsk Reservoir. At will, vacationers can visit billiards, table tennis, a solarium, a gym, a shop, and a cafe. In summer, visitors are provided with volleyball, badminton, soccer, darts. In winter, you can ride on a sled, ski, skate or ice rink. There is a playground for children.

Accommodation

Tourists can settle in a comfortable hotel with 18 beds. At Koprino's guests, the recreation center offers modern cottages. The hotel has double and triple rooms, each of which has a bathroom and shower. Different options are possible. The rooms can be fitted with single or double beds. The room has a TV, fridge, dishes, microwave, electric kettle. The cost of living - from 2100 rubles per place.

Cottages in Koprino

The recreation center offers houses of different capacities. In particular, tourists can choose:

  1. Double rooms. They are log houses consisting of a room with a single bed, a shower, a bathroom with hot and cold water, and a kitchen-hall. On the territory of the cottage is equipped with a place for barbecue, parking for cars.
  2. Double and triple. Two-room houses with a bedroom, a bathroom with a shower, a kitchen-dining room in which there is a sofa. There is a car park.
  3. Double house on the "chicken legs" with unique furniture made of pine. It has a small kitchen and a bathroom with shower. Equipped parking lot.
  4. Triple rooms. Log houses, consisting of two rooms (with single beds), a bathroom with cold and hot water, a kitchen-hall, shower. There is a barbecue and parking for cars.
  5. Quadruple. Wooden houses with two rooms, single beds, a bathroom with hot and cold water, a kitchen-hall, a shower. On the territory of the cottage is equipped with a place for cooking barbecue and parking.
  6. Five-seater. They are houses consisting of two halves, isolated from each other. Each of them has 2 rooms (one has a double bed, the other has three single beds), an open veranda with a table and bench, a large kitchen-dining room (with a TV, fridge, gas stove, sofa, armchair and a set of dishes). Each cottage has a shower cabin, a bathroom with hot and cold water. There is a separate courtyard with barbecue. Vacationers can park their cars in the parking lot.
  7. Six-seater. Chopped cottage, located separately and made of logs. It consists of two floors, on which there are bedrooms, a living room with a sofa bed, a kitchen-dining room, a bathroom, a shower room. Near the house there is a barbecue, equipped with a parking lot.




Important information

Check-in is made at 17.00, check-out is at 15.00. You need to have a passport, a birth certificate for the child, a ticket (accommodation is included in its price). It is possible both complex meals, and individual, which is ordered in a cafe.

Additional services

Koprino (recreation center) provides:

  • three meals a day;
  • extra bed in the form of a folding bed;
  • Russian bath for 4 and 10 places;
  • solarium, gym, billiards, table tennis;
  • hire of skates, skis, sledges;
  • a ride on a snowmobile with an instructor;
  • massage bed ;
  • horse ride.




Directions

From Moscow, along the Yaroslavl highway, you need to get to Yaroslavl, then to Rybinsk, and then turn left to get on the district highway. On the descent of the bridge, having passed a traffic light, you need to turn left again. Then you should go on the road to Glebov. After that you need to turn right and go along Larionov to the yacht club. After that, drive past the signs for Koprino Bay. So you get to the sign "houses for rent." In 200 m from it there will be Koprino - a recreation center where you will have a great time!
