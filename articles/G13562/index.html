Hotel "Kamenyuki"

The hotel complex "Kamenyuki", located on the territory of the "State Reserve Belovezhskaya Pushcha", has the category of three stars, includes four modern buildings. Here you can not only walk in the forest and see unique animals, but also get a lot of opportunities to actively spend your vacation. Bike hire is especially popular at the hotel, and in winter ski lovers come here. Horseback riding is also available.

In the cozy restaurant of the complex, designed for 250 seats, Belarusian cuisine is presented, here you will also be served specialties. There are several small cafes nearby.

The newly built on the territory of the Kamenyuki complex in the Bialowieza Hotel No. 4 (photo can be seen below) has conference rooms equipped with the most modern equipment. An indoor pool, a fitness center, a sauna, a tour desk, a souvenir shop, a parking space and Wi-Fi in public areas are available for free.

Children can spend time on the playground or in the children's pool. In buildings 1, 3 and 4, guests are offered accommodation in suites and junior suites:

  • the cheapest accommodation in a double junior suite - 3449 rubles per day;
  • most of all you will have to pay for accommodation in a deluxe room - 6502 rubles per day.

All rooms include breakfast. The Kamenyuki Hotel is located in Belovezhskaya Pushcha at the address: Kamenyuki Village, Kamenets District, Brest Region, Republic of Belarus.
