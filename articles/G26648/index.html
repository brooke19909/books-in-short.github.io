Sunny Egypt: Ali Baba Hotel - everyone can afford a vacation

Do you dream of spending time on a sandy beach, sunbathing under the rays of the gentle sun and at the same time living in a comfortable guesthouse? Then feel free to go to Egypt. Hotel "Ali Baba" first opened its doors in 2000. During this time, the complex has established itself on the good side. Thousands of foreigners come here every year. Rest at the hotel leaves customers only positive impressions and pleasant emotions. The contingent of vacationers here is of different ages: there are many married couples with small children, business people come here on vacation, and newlyweds come to spend their honeymoon. Everyone will find entertainment based on their interests, thanks to an extensive and multifaceted program.





For guests, excursion routes are organized in Hurghada, where they will not only tell you the history of the country, but also show funny performances in the palace of "A Thousand and One Nights." Mysterious pyramids, Sphinx and the city of the Dead will show you Egypt. Hotel Ali Baba is located right on the sandy coast of the Red Sea, surrounded by tropical greenery. In the same territory along with the complex there are two more hotels: Alladin and Jasmine Village. Internal infrastructure for all guests is common.

You can get to the tourist center from the airport of Hurghada (20 km), both by public transport and by private car-taxi. Upon request, you will be met by employees and delivered to the place. In total, the hotel has eight 2-and 3-storey buildings with spacious rooms. The guest house has an ice rink, many restaurants, its own pool and many other amenities. Near the building there is a water park "Titanic", shopping centers, souvenir shops.





The hotel has comfortable rooms of various categories. The room adjoins a balcony with panoramic views of the sea and the infrastructure of the complex. All rooms have individual amenities, climate control.

A safe and hairdryer are provided for free. To services - a large TV with Russian channels, a bar with a wide selection of drinks - on request (for a fee). A direct line telephone is also installed (local calls only). Daily maintenance and bed changes.





The complex operates on an all-inclusive basis. A personal menu has been created for children. Available diet foods. All tourists staying at this hotel can absolutely free of charge visit the facilities of neighboring hotels with special coupons.

On the territory there are 3 restaurants with oriental and Italian cuisine, a disco bar, a beer bar, a small bar counter near the outdoor pool and a cafe in the lobby. In the neighborhood there are excellent places where you can have a tasty and inexpensive meal, as well as dance and have fun.





In walking distance stretches the coast with a picturesque coral reef and pier. The beach is wide, roomy and clean, equipped with sunbeds, awnings and mattresses (free). The sea is calm, warm with a comfortable even descent (depth starts after 10 meters). There is a bar on the shore for customers, fun and exciting performances are held daily. A water entertainment center is also open: diving, surfing, water skiing, motorbikes, yachting and parachuting.

One of the most prestigious and best tourist resorts is Egypt. Hotel "Ali Baba" is a confirmation of this. The manual offers free classes in the gym, games on the tennis court. As well as futsal, golf, basketball, table tennis, billiards, bowling. During the day, guests will be able to actively participate in sports competitions. In the evenings, there will be no time to be bored either, since Russian-language animation, theater shows and a disco begin.





For passive relaxation, there are three pools, one of which is indoor with heated water, and the second is for children. For a fee, you can use the sauna, massage room, jacuzzi and hammam. The building has a conference room for 150 people, video games, an exchange office and an entertainment center. For children, a children's play area is allocated, a club is open.

On your own or rented car, you can visit children's entertainment facilities, recreation parks, water parks and water centers, as well as independently see all the sights of the country and directly tourist Egypt itself. Hurghada, the hotel "Ali Baba" is conveniently located in relation to all cultural, historical and entertainment facilities.
