Catherine Sloboda: location, features of rest, photo

"Catherine's Village" is a private family holiday home located on Seliger. They invite guests here all year round to spend time on the shore of one of the most beautiful lakes in Russia. Everyone will be able to enjoy the picturesque pine forest, the cleanest air filled with resinous aroma, amazing Central Russian landscapes.

About the holiday home

Rest House "Catherine's Village" consists of log cottages. Each has a playroom for children, a fireplace area for adults, comfortable and spacious bedrooms. The staff strives to do everything possible to provide a level of comfort familiar to all guests.

Rest House "Catherine's Settlement" is located in a surprisingly beautiful place between the lakes Seliger and Serminok. Here you are always ready to give a pleasant and carefree vacation, during which the smallest details will be thought out. A preventive and unobtrusive service will save you from any household problems, freeing up maximum time for entertainment.

Even if you arrive at Catherine‚Äôs Sloboda in Seliger for a few days, you still have time to feel amazing peace in the lap of beautiful nature, a serenity with which all problems and worries will be forgotten.

How to get there

The holiday home is located in the Tver region in the territory of Novosheshminsky district. To "Catherine's settlement" to get there is not difficult. For example, a car journey from Moscow will take only about five hours on a good and high-quality road.

In order not to get lost, you must remember the exact address of the Ekaterininskaya Sloboda holiday home: Novosheshminsky district, Sloboda village, Zeleny proezd, house 7.

Terms of Accommodation

There are three options for guests in the holiday home.

The most comfortable is a large cottage for six families. Here you will find a games room, high-speed Internet, lake views, a fireplace room, satellite TV.

The cottage is located in a pine forest on the shore of Lake Sermenok, 80 meters from Seliger. He is served by a personal administrator, cook, maid. It offers four triple 2-room rooms and three one-room 2-bed rooms. The cost of trips with meals and accommodation will be from 1,300 to 3,800 rubles per person per day, depending on the time of year.

This price includes, in addition to living in a cottage, three meals a day, services of guarded parking. You can also rent boats, bicycles, skis, fishing tackle, barbecue, smokehouse, sun loungers and sports grounds. There is everything that may be required for a good rest. Children under three years old are promised to be placed free of charge.

On the territory of the recreation center "Catherine's Village" there are three VIP-cottages, designed for one family each. These are small but cozy two-story houses. On the ground floor there is a dining room with a fireplace, which is heated by firewood. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are also served here. There is a glazed veranda with beautiful and picturesque views, a private bathroom.

On the second floor are two bedrooms. The price of a ticket with accommodation and meals is from 1,700 to 3,800 rubles per person per day.

Finally, you can stay in apartments with a separate entrance. They are located on the second floor, which offers a beautiful view of the lake. Each has a double bed, all necessary furniture, there is a toilet, shower, refrigerator. The cost starts from 2,200 rubles per person per day.

Nutrition

We note once again that all prices at the recreation center "Catherine's Village" are indicated with meals. This is one of the main features of accommodation in this place.

Guests are offered traditional Russian cuisine. Porridge (rice, semolina, oatmeal, buckwheat) is served for breakfast. And also any other dish: pancakes with sour cream, cheesecakes, pancakes, olivier salad, omelet, scrambled eggs with bacon, pizza.

For lunch, there is always a large selection of first courses - seliger ear, mushroom soup, Ukrainian borsch, okroshka, green cabbage soup, meatball soup, chicken broth. For the second you can order pork schnitzel, stuffed chicken leg, Seliger cutlet, cabbage rolls, chicken roll, fish under the marinade. All this relies on a side dish: buckwheat, rice, spaghetti, mashed potatoes, vegetable stew, fried or boiled potatoes.

For dinner, pilaf, homemade dumplings in a pot, goulash with a side dish, fried fish, breast with prunes, roast in a pot, meat under a fur coat.

Once a day, baked goods are served. They also offer vegetable salads, fruits, and soft drinks. Local cooks are ready to smoke for free the fish you catch.

Entertainment

At the recreation center you will definitely not get bored. There is everything you can wish for. And for a relatively small amount of money.

Two hours in a Russian bath will cost 2,000 rubles, renting an hour for a bicycle or boat - 1,000 rubles, an armful of firewood for a fireplace - 100 rubles.

Rackets and a ball for table tennis, a smokehouse, barbecue and deck chairs, basketball and football courts, fishing equipment are provided free of charge.

Guest Impressions

Reviews about the "Catherine's settlement" are very different. Guests regularly praise a surprisingly beautiful place with magnificent nature, a quick solution on the spot of all the questions that arise during the holidays, delicious and plentiful food.

For many, Seliger is becoming the place they regularly want to return to.

Among the shortcomings, visitors to the holiday home most often note unreasonably high prices. For many, the lack of a store within walking distance, as well as dead roads, becomes a drawback. In addition, getting here is not so easy, because in bad weather you can stumble on such tracks on which you can easily leave the suspension.

Also a disadvantage is not very comfortable bathrooms. Some people are confused by the strange arrangement of cottages in which many guests stay. On the ground floor there is a fireplace room, where they set the table, while there are absolutely no windows in the room.

Lake Seliger amazes everyone with its beauty, at the same time it terrifies the state of the coast. He is covered in cigarette butts and rubbish, a nearby rubbish bin filled. In the heat, a very unpleasant smell appears, which discourages all the desire to return here again and again.
