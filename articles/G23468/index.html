Krasnoyarsk "Big Ben" in figures and facts

In the rating of watch towers in Russia, the Krasnoyarsk tourist attraction takes an honorable second place. Only the Spasskaya Tower of the Moscow Kremlin is superior to the Russian Big Ben. The Krasnoyarsk clock tower boasts a dial with a diameter of 6.5 meters. The weight of the clockwork is 1.5 tons. Residents and guests of Krasnoyarsk can safely check the time on the main city clock. You can hear local chimes twice an hour when the minute hand reaches 30 minutes and 1 hour. At the end of 2017, Krasnoyarsk residents first heard the new Big Ben tune. In honor of the New Year holidays (from December 20, 2017 to January 14, 2018), instead of the usual fight, the clock tower pleased passers-by with a fragment from the ballet The Nutcracker by P. I. Tchaikovsky. Such musical accompaniment was chosen to create a festive mood among the population and is likely to become a new urban tradition.

How to get to the main hours of Krasnoyarsk?

Where is the Russian Big Ben? Krasnoyarsk, Weinbaum Street, 94 k - the exact address of the tower. Find this attraction is not difficult, because it is located in the immediate vicinity of the city administration building. Within walking distance are the local history museum and the opera and ballet theater. Thanks to this arrangement, it is very convenient for tourists to combine a visit to Big Ben and other interesting places in Krasnoyarsk. The nearest public transport stop is called the Opera and Ballet Theater.
