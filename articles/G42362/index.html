"Gagarinsky baths" - a bath complex for families

"Gagarinsky baths" in St. Petersburg is a bath complex for the whole family with the hotel. There are 3 types of steam rooms, a warm pool and a font, 2 lounges and comfortable rooms in the hotel. For an affordable price, you can organize a comfortable and useful vacation.

Where are

"Gagarinsky baths" are located in St. Petersburg on Yury Gagarin Ave., 1. The institution is located at the metro station "Victory Park."

The complex is open daily throughout the day. On weekends and holidays, the mode does not change. You can book your vacation by phone or online.

Near the institution there is a closed parking lot for visitors to the complex. She is guarded around the clock.

Relax with comfort

In "Gagarin baths" a number of quality services are provided:

  • Finnish sauna
  • Turkish steam room (hammam);
  • infrared sauna with salt panels;
  • shallow pool with hydromassage;
  • billiards;
  • bar, snacks;
  • hookah;
  • karaoke;
  • laser machine;
  • lounge room.

The entire building and the premises in it underwent major repairs in 2016, so it is very clean and beautiful here. Equipment regularly undergoes technical checks.

Mini Hotel

A small hotel is organized in the complex. It has several modern rooms. Two of them have economy class status. They have a large bed, a nightstand, a dressing table with a mirror. The first of the rooms is decorated in blue, and the other in dark. One room is of the standard class. The room is different from the previous area. It also has a large double bed.

There is additional furniture, flat-screen TV, telephone. The room is decorated in a pleasant lilac color. A bathroom and shower are equipped in the corridor for all rooms.

The Gagarinsky Baths Hotel provides for any time of booking. A thorough cleaning is done after each client. All rooms have free internet. The hotel welcomes guests 24 hours a day.

Possible hourly payment of rooms. "Economy" will cost guests 400 rubles. (day - 1700 rubles), and the "standard" - 500 rubles. (day 2000 rubles).

The advantages of the complex

"Gagarinsky baths" have a number of privileges that determine the popularity of the institution among the guests and residents of the city:

  1. The institution accepts any type of payment. For this purpose, a bank card, cash and even a telephone can be used.
  2. Modern interior. After the reconstruction of the building in 2016, a high-quality repair was carried out with a major replacement of all machinery and equipment. Experienced specialists worked on the design of the complex.
  3. The entertainment center operates without hidden fees and commissions.
  4. The best prices in the city operate here and various promotions are constantly held.
  5. The service in the complex remains at the highest level. The staff always listens to the wishes of customers.
  6. It is always maintained clean and tidy. In any room, after each client, high-quality cleaning is performed.
  7. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the complex.
  8. The center works round the clock without interruptions.
  9. The institution has a strict privacy policy.

All these items give customers the opportunity to relax after a hard day or week. The staff never interferes with the rest of the guests and pays attention only on demand.

"Gagarin baths": reviews

There are quite a lot of comments on the Internet about the operation of this entertainment complex. Basically, the responses regarding the bath department are positive. Guests note that the steam rooms and saunas are very clean. The pool regularly changes water, so every new company bathes in fresh.

According to vacationers, the service here is at a good level. Staff always listens to suggestions and comments. Some guests indicate that the pool is shallow, so it is very convenient to swim with children in it.

The bar serves quality drinks and delicious snacks. Some vacationers are unhappy that karaoke songs are ordered through the machine and each new melody costs a little, but still money.

There are double opinions about the work of the hotel. The rooms are clean and comfortable, but the sound insulation here is very poor. Therefore, to relax, when the fun happens in the bath, most likely, it will not work.

Customers note that the rooms also have enough water in the center due to the large amount of water in the center and they have to stay in the room for the most part with open windows. Guests indicate that it is most likely that the hotel is not very suitable for renting accommodation as tourists.

Here, most often, the guests of the bath house stay overnight after noisy fun, as well as tourists who need to relax for one night or even a few hours.

The overall impression of the guests of the complex can be rated at "4+". Vacationers are satisfied with the service and entertainment. Large companies often gather here to celebrate various celebrations.

Guests are pleased with the price of the service. And also they are pleased with the opportunity to come here with children (up to 10 years old - admission is free for them). On New Year's holidays, an interesting entertainment program is held here.
