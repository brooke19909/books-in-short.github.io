Hotel "Smolninskaya", St. Petersburg: description of rooms, services, reviews

Executive-class business hotel Smolninsky accepts not just business travelers and tourists. Together with the guests of the city, members of official delegations arrived here at the invitation of the Governor of St. Petersburg. Therefore, you can be sure that the design of rooms, meals and services in the hotel complex are at the highest level.

At the same time, the hotel has four stars on its sign, which allows counting on budget accommodation in the city on the Neva. In this article, we will conduct an in-depth analysis of the Smolninskaya Hotel. Where it is located, how close to it are the main attractions of St. Petersburg, what rooms are there and what kind of service - all this we learned by studying the reviews of travelers. The overall rating of the hotel is four and a half points out of five possible. It is recommended by 80 percent of travelers who left reviews.



Where is the Smolninskaya Hotel

The address of the hotel complex makes you flinch and recall the formidable years of dispossession and other achievements of the October Revolution. The hotel is located on Proletarian Dictatorship Street, house 6. Indeed, the hotel is located in the area that is the cradle of the October Revolution. The rooms offer views of the Smolny Cathedral. Nearby is also the Government House of St. Petersburg. From the proximity to the boarding house of noble maidens, which was the headquarters of the revolutionaries, the Smolninskaya hotel got its name.

How to get to other main sights of St. Petersburg? The hotel is located at an equal distance from two metro stations: Vosstaniya Square and Chernyshevskaya. Nevsky Prospekt, the Hermitage, the Summer Garden and the Russian Museum can be reached by bus number 22 in ten minutes. From Tulskaya Street, which is located near the hotel, trolleybuses of routes 5, 7 and 11 go to the center. It is seventeen kilometers to Pulkovo Airport.



What does the hotel complex look like?

Despite the formidable name of the street on which the hotel is located, it looks modern. And when you enter the luxurious lobby, make sure that capitalism ultimately triumphed. All the interiors of the modern six-story building are designed in the Empire style. The hotel has its own courtyard, where there is a free secure parking for cars of hotel guests. Many tourists recommend booking rooms that look exactly there, because there will not be noise from the street. Although the view of the openwork blue-white Smolninsky Cathedral is worth it to listen to the voice of Peter.

Check-in occurs from 13.00. This is perhaps the only inconvenience that reviews say. For early check-in, the hotel requires an additional charge of half the price of a room per day. The reception desk works without interruption. Hotel staff speak English.



Number of rooms

In the hotel complex "Smolninskaya" fifty-three rooms for guests. The modest charm of the bourgeoisie blows even from the standard category number. Stucco molding on the ceiling, paintings on the walls, new furniture. It offers a safe, interactive TV, minibar, refrigerator, air conditioning. Since businessmen and business people often stay at the Smolninskaya Hotel, each room has a convenient desk and Internet access. In the bathrooms, maids leave bath accessories daily. In addition to the standard (15 sq. M), there are other categories of rooms: Junior Suite (25 m 2 ), Suite (47 m 2 ) and one- or two-level apartments (100 m 2 ). The latter are a complete apartment with a bedroom, living room, dining room and kitchen. The bathroom includes a hairdryer, slippers and a bathrobe. The kitchen is equipped with all necessary utensils. The rooms are cleaned daily and very thorough.

Smolninskaya Hotel Services for Business Clients

Since this hotel belongs to the government of the city of St. Petersburg, all amenities are provided for members of official delegations who have arrived for business meetings. Each room has a well-lit work desk. Reviews say that free Wi-Fi in speed exceeded all expectations. It is everywhere, in the rooms farthest from the reception. The business hotel, of course, has its own modern conference room. It is equipped with all necessary appliances and furniture. There are also equipped meeting rooms for a small number of participants. To ensure that businessmen do not interrupt household chores, the hotel provides laundry and ironing. Receptionists provide services such as wake-up calls, taxi services. Room service is available around the clock.



Services of the Smolninskaya Hotel for tourists

After business meetings it would be nice to relax. To do this, the hotel has a sauna, solarium, massage room. Receptionists will call you a taxi or book an excursion. If you want to see the sights of St. Petersburg and its environs on their own, they will tell you how to get there.

Reception staff are fluent in English so that foreign guests can communicate with them. Girls will undertake all the work of ordering tickets for a plane or train. Hotel Smolninskaya (St. Petersburg) is located in an area where there are some city attractions. Nevsky Prospekt, the Winter Palace can be reached directly by metro. In the morning, you can get a boost of vigor in the fitness room, which has the latest fitness equipment. Recall that for those who came to the city on the Neva with their own vehicles, there is a guarded free parking in the hotel courtyard. And if you are traveling with your four-legged friend, he will be hospitably accommodated in your room.

Cost of living

Hotel Smolninskaya (St. Petersburg) belongs to the middle price segment. As in many hotels in the world, a loyalty policy to regular customers is encouraged here. If you order a number, then in your name there are points. With frequent visits to the hotel, they can provide you with a room absolutely free or for a reduced fee due to these virtual units. But for children, unfortunately, discounts are not provided. So what is the price of rooms in the Smolninskaya hotel?
