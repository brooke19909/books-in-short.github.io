Hotel Blue Sea Hotel Nha Trang 2 * (Vietnam): photos and reviews of tourists

Vietnam is a country that is becoming increasingly popular among tourists, because it can offer not only a mild climate and clean beaches, but also the pristine beauty of nature and genuine hospitality. Over the past few years, many hotels have appeared on the coast, one of which is the Blue Sea Hotel Nha Trang 2 *. What can a traveler staying at this hotel expect?

Short description and location of the hotel





A small hotel Blue Sea Hotel Nha Trang 2 * is located on the seafront, but in the northern part of Nya Chang. The city center with its busy streets is about 5 km away. You can get there by public bus or taxi. The distance to the airport can be covered in 45 minutes by bus or car. By the way, travel agencies provide their customers with a shuttle service, as well as the hotel itself (this service costs a little). The hotel itself is a small building with an adjacent territory, which pleases the eye with lush greenery.

Hotel Blue Sea Hotel Nha Trang 2 * (Vietnam): what do the rooms look like?





This hotel is quite small, as it consists of only 24 rooms. This is a standard double and triple apartment with a modern renovation, pleasant atmosphere and comfortable furniture. By the way, the hotel is equipped for a comfortable stay for the disabled.

In the room you can count on the necessary equipment, including telephone, TV, air conditioning (it is not new, but it works properly). There is also a bathroom with a comfortable shower, toilet, washbasin and modern fixtures. Cleaned up here on request. Tourists are provided with clean towels and necessary hygiene items. By the way, due to the small number of guests, the hotel is quiet and comfortable - nothing will disturb your peace.

Beach holiday

As already mentioned, the Blue Sea Nha Trang 2 * hotel is located in a quiet area in the northern part of the city. The seashore is very close - the distance to it is only 100 meters. The beach, of course, is public, and therefore rent of sun loungers and umbrellas is paid. By the way, the prices in this area, which is considered to be non-tourist, are much lower, this also applies to the rental of water transport, and other entertainment that a tourist on the beach can count on.

Food, additional services and leisure

Paying for accommodation at the Blue Sea Hotel Nha Trang 2 *, you also get the right to daily breakfasts, which are held in the hotel restaurant. The menu is standard, and therefore, for some travelers it seems a little monotonous. Nevertheless, you can count on toasts, cereals, snacks, soups, desserts and fragrant tea. In the same restaurant you can eat the rest of the day, however, for an additional fee. And tourists can eat at establishments belonging to other hotels.

Despite its relatively modest size, the hotel can offer an additional service that will greatly facilitate your stay here. In particular, there is parking, and guests can also rent bicycles and other means of transport. There is a well-equipped business center, as well as an excursion table. And you can relax after a busy day by visiting the spacious hot tub.

Hotel Blue Sea Hotel Nha Trang 2 *: reviews





For the most part, reviews about this place are positive. Naturally, you need to understand that this is a small hotel in Vietnam, and accommodation does not cost too much, and therefore you should not demand too much from the hotel. Most tourists use it only as an overnight stay. The rooms here are quite comfortable, despite the not too new furniture. Hearty breakfasts, there is always a choice of food. The staff are responsive and kind, although you need to communicate with them in English. And also, given the location, prices in stores here are much cheaper. In short, the hotel is suitable for travelers looking for a budget holiday by the sea.
