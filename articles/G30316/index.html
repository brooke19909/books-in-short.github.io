Essential Parameters

When choosing a device, its type of control is also important. It can be mechanical or electronic. In the first of them, the multicooker is controlled by rotary time and temperature controls, and in the second - using the LCD display and touch buttons. Mechanical control is the cheapest and easiest. Electronic - more automated. It is worth clicking on the button with the name of the dish, and you do not need to think about what time and temperature modes should be set.

With a teflon or ceramic coating, a multi-cooker can be purchased. Which is better? Expert reviews recommend ceramic coating. It is more expensive, but for those who care about their health, this option is recommended.

The devices should be selected according to the function of maintaining heat. It will allow after cooking to ensure the operation of the device in an economical mode. Ready-made treats will be warm for a long time.

Modern multicookers have a feature that can delay the start of cooking. The appliance will cook the dishes at the appointed time. When buying, pay attention to the timer. On some multi-cookers it can be installed for only two hours. There are models with a delayed start up to twenty-four hours.
