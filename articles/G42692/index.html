Hotel Amira Hotel 3 in the resort of Safaga (Egypt)

Egypt is a welcoming and hospitable country with a developed tourism industry. Millions of travelers are eager to get here in order to get acquainted with its ancient culture, to see first-hand the magnificent pyramids, the treasury of Tutankhamun and the mysterious Sphinx. Many amazing resorts are located on a large territory of Egypt.





The most popular and favorite is the city of Hurghada with alluring white beaches and powerful peaks. Modern hotels and pensions welcome guests with European comfort. Here you will find business-class hotels and budget complexes such as Amira Hotel 3. This is a small hotel, comfortably located in the quiet village of Safaga, about 60 km from the central part of the famous resort of Hurghada.

A quiet place attracts with its picturesque nature, remoteness from noisy restaurants and discos. It has been welcoming guests since 1999. The hotel has a welcoming, friendly atmosphere, which is an important factor. The complex is focused on a family weekend with children, fans of peaceful relaxation and diving enthusiasts. It's time to head to this fantastic corner!

Accommodations





Amira Safaga Hotel complex was built in the form of a 4-storey building decorated in a traditional Scandinavian style, which is characterized by aesthetics and simplicity of the interior. The building has an elevator and 95 rooms, five of which are of an improved layout. Rooms are cleaned daily.

From their windows, guests can enjoy the beauty of the local landscape. Some rooms have a loggia. It offers - air conditioning, cable TV with Russian channels, as well as a compact refrigerator with a minibar. For personal use, shower and toilet.

The daily diet contains fish, poultry, meat, several side dishes for different tastes. There are also cafeterias and bars with a wide selection of spirits and main dishes.

Coastline

A private beach, strewn with small pebbles, extends a 10-minute walk from the main building. If it is difficult for you to get to the coast at your own pace, you can take a minibus that runs several times a day to the sea. What about the coastline?





The beach area is closed, guarded by Amira Hotel 3 staff. It is always clean, there are sun loungers with awnings and towels. Special shoes are needed. A small bar with refreshments is open.

The sea near the shore is shallow, safe for children. There are picturesque corals, you can swim with a mask, watch the life of colorful fish and jellyfish. Active entertainment awaits you on the shore: catamarans, volleyball, boat trips, fishing.

Landscaping





The courtyard of the Amira Hotel 3 complex is small, despite the size, comfortable conditions for interesting and informative leisure are created here. There is a large open-air swimming pool with a bar (all drinks of local bottling are free of charge). High speed internet is available in all public areas.

There is a safe in the lobby at the reception (rent is free). You do not have to constantly carry valuables with you and worry about their safety. Guests do not need to go outside the hotel to exchange money, since there is a currency exchange on the territory. For a fee, you can use the laundry or rent a car. A conference room is equipped for the business public.

To keep fit, a modern fitness room with exercise machines is open. At the request of each guest, an experienced instructor will draw up an individual training program taking into account physical training. The massage room will delight guests. The real masters of their craft work in it.

After warming up, you can visit the steam room, cleanse your body and spirit with hot steam. Late in the evening, entertaining shows begin. Accompanied by musical accompaniment, professional animators will surprise the audience with interesting and fun contests.

Children's holiday

Not all Egyptian hotels are geared towards young guests. Amira Hotel is a great place for younger travelers. The hotel management offers an excellent infrastructure for children: a safe pool, a gentle beach, bicycles. Comfortable cots in the room, high chairs are provided for the little ones to make the child comfortable to eat, as well as a separate menu.

Young guests will feel the care and attention of the staff. Family boat trips with scuba diving (with children from 4 years old) are organized. Your kid will remember summer vacations for life and will leave vivid emotions from joint rest in his memory.
