Unique Cyprus: Water World Waterpark

On the island of Cyprus, you can relax in different ways: lie quietly and calmly on some beach, wander around the picturesque area or go on an excursion and get acquainted with local attractions. Well, extreme lovers can tickle their nerves by visiting an amusement park. For this, 4 water parks with a wide variety of attractions were built on the island, which will surely interest thrill-seekers. However, this type of relaxation will appeal not only to adults, but also to children. Therefore, family vacations can be exciting and unforgettable for those who visit such a water park. Cyprus, Ayia Napa - these names are probably known to many tourists. After all, it is at this resort that WaterWorld, or "Water World" is located, which is popular among tourists and among the indigenous inhabitants of the island.



The first thing that catches your eye here is the design: everything is designed in accordance with the history and mythology of Ancient Hellas. Many statues and columns, stone bridges and fountains create a magical, fabulous atmosphere, transporting visitors to the world of the ancient gods of Olympus. However, if you look around. then returning to reality will be quite easy: on the territory of this park there are water slides from which you can go down at a breakneck speed. Agree, it is unlikely that the Thunderer Zeus or the seductive Aphrodite had fun in this way. Here you can also dive into the pool with the legendary name Atlantis, drive through the Medusa tunnel or climb Mount Olympus. The WaterWorld water park also offers a newcomer to the guests of the island of Cyprus - the attraction "Aelos Rotating Ball". WaterWorld Waterpark will introduce you to ancient heroes, gods and goddesses, since everything here - every slide, marble statues, picturesque scenery, beautiful fountains - is dedicated to this topic. By the way, you can stay in the water park all day, paying only for admission. Well, not far from Limassol is the largest entertainment center of the island of Cyprus - the water park Fasouri Watermania. He began to receive first visitors in 1998. At first the park was small, but over time it gained impressive size. By the way, its considerable territory is decorated in the style of French Polynesia: tropical plants, canopies from palm leaves, wooden arbors surround the guests. In this water park there are entertainment for those who want to swim calmly, and for lovers of extreme relaxation. For example, the thrill can be experienced on the slides "Black Hole" and "Kamikaze." And you can admire the picturesque bridges, sparkling waterfalls and secluded grottoes while sailing along the "Lazy River". There is also a wide slide on which the whole family will fit; an unusual pool, the waters of which imitate 6 types of ocean waves, small slides for children and other attractions.
