Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma Maldives (Maldives, South Male Atoll): room description, service, reviews

What do you need for a wonderful holiday? Comfortable rooms, polite staff, excellent service and the ocean. The mood begins to roll over on the way to the resort: a 40-minute boat trip on a modern boat, the sun, the wind in her hair, splashes of water on her face. Wonderful! So many emotions without even reaching the Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma Maldives.

And what will happen next?! And ahead lies the first-class family-run holiday resort, Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma Maldives, on one of the islands of South Male Atoll. Yes, yes, you were not mistaken, we are talking about the Maldives. This paradise on earth is located on a small island of sushi called Kanduma Fushi, 35 kilometers from the capital of the Republic of Maldives.

Climatic conditions

The island where the Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma 4 is located has a subequatorial monsoon climate, with an average temperature of around + 24 ¬∞ C. This makes a year-round holiday possible. Thanks to the warm, humid climate, the island is surrounded by greenery all year round. Rains are typical for this area, but they are short-lived (usually in the evening or at night), short daylight hours.

Description of Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma

The hotel opened in 2009 and covers the entire island. Here, tourists are offered an extensive range of entertainment and wellness services. Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma Maldives offers 144 rooms. They are divided into several types, but each of them is a great place for an unforgettable vacation:

  • 44 beach villas;
  • 74 beach cottages;
  • 6 family beach cottages;
  • 19 villas on the water;
  • 1 surface pavilion.

The presence of Russian-speaking workers at the reception and a doctor makes life easier for Russian tourists in the hotel.

Each room at Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma Maldives has one or two bedrooms, a bathroom, an outdoor shower, a terrace, international telephone service, a safe, and Wi-Fi. Daily updated water supplies in the rooms, there is a paid minibar.

Residents of water villas have the opportunity to jump into the ocean right from the room. The houses are located around the perimeter, and in the heart of the island there is a green zone. The design of the rooms is dominated by the island style, the color scheme is close to natural. Panoramic windows create a sense of unity with nature.

The furnishings of the rooms can conquer people even with the most sophisticated taste.

To check into the water villa of the Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma Maldives 4 with a child under 12 years old, parents must sign a document stating that they take responsibility for the life and health of the child and will not make any claims to the administration in case of emergency.

Developed infrastructure

The range of additional services includes paid laundry, doctor's services, round-the-clock boat transfer. Tired of luxurious surroundings, Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma Maldives offers an excursion to a neighboring island. In this paradise, people not only relax from the worries of earthly life, but also organize seminars, meetings, conferences.

To do this, the hotel has created all the necessary conditions: the ability to accommodate large groups, comfortable conference rooms, the services of organizers who will prepare and conduct a business meeting at the highest level.

Those who wish to conduct the wedding ceremony will be helped by specially trained people.

Beaches

The hotel in the Maldives has three beaches, the rest of the coastal area is great for surfing. High waves make surfing on this island unforgettable. Many guests come here for the opportunity to work out this sport. Villas and cottages are located in the first row from the sea, and to get to the waters of the endless Indian Ocean, a tourist needs to take only a couple of steps. 20 meters of white sand is an ideal place for a lazy vacation by the ocean.

The entrance to the water is sandy. Near the coast - coral reefs, where it is possible to observe the inhabitants of the deep sea. The beach is the property of the resort and specially hired workers monitor its cleanliness. All beaches of the island are equipped with sun loungers. Having lounged around under the scorching sun and plunged into the clear turquoise ocean, tourists can go swimming with turtles.

Relaxation and sports area

In the center of the resort, in the green zone, the SPA-center is successfully located, where tourists are offered massage in the open air. For relaxation, visitors are invited to plunge into the pool with a hydrotherapeutic effect. In addition to the therapeutic and relaxing pool, the hotel has an open-air pool.

It is not surprising that the hotel is represented by numerous sea sports. The hotel offers rental equipment for surfing and catamaran riding.

Here you can find sports for every taste:

  • diving center;
  • surfing;
  • windsurfing;
  • catamaran rides;
  • water skiing;
  • kayak;
  • wakeboarding;
  • parasailing.

Inexperienced beginners get on the board and catch a wave will help experienced instructors.

The Kanduma Fushi coast is considered the most successful place for surfing and windsurfing.

The hotel is equipped with playgrounds for beach volleyball, football, basketball, tennis, badminton.

Supporters of static sports can choose yoga at sunrise and sunset. Rest is not a reason to stop taking care of your health. Moreover, the hotel has its own gym. If indoor activities are not for you, you can go for a run around the perimeter of the island and enjoy the impressive beauty of this amazing place.

Children's area

The hotel positions itself as a family resort. He created excellent conditions for children's recreation. The club for children offers a little vacationer a swimming pool, an interactive games room, a library, an area for creativity. If necessary, you can order the services of an animator in your room, babysitting for the period of the club. When giving a child to the club, you can be sure that professionals are looking after him and filling his leisure time. The club is divided into age zones.

Supply system

The island has 3 restaurants and 4 bars. Restaurants offer European and Asian cuisine. All restaurants offer either half board or full board. There are all-inclusive and full luxury systems. Each day of the week for dinner, a certain theme and cuisine is offered: Indian cuisine, European cuisine, barbecue, fish day.

The food is varied, very tasty and always fresh. Even the most inveterate foodie will be able to choose the right dish for himself. And the finished romantics can have a barbecue in their villa. Sunny sunset, delicious food, a glass of fine wine. What could be better for the perfect completion of another wonderful day on the island? The wine list is represented by European and American wines.

The hotel offers children a free children's menu.

In the bars you can try the classic drinks of the world, watch movies in the open air, cook pizza on wood, flavored coffee and the best tea in the world.

Once a week, a disco is organized where you can freely dance to the rhythmic music of a local DJ.

What tourists say

Tourists who have been here in reviews of Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma Maldives characterize it on a positive side. Everything has been created here so that the guests feel like in paradise. At the airport you will be met and escorted to the boat, which will deliver to the island. Tourists do not need to tinker with luggage, because they do not see it before they arrive in the room. Tastefully decorated rooms, rain shower in the open air, a terrace for relaxation, a surprisingly clean beach, as if Baunty had come down from advertising, a SPA center, restaurants, bars.

Of particular interest to tourists is the high level of service at the Holiday Inn Resort Kandooma, the good nature and care of the hotel staff. They are understanding, patient, responsive professionals.

Having been once in this wonderful resort, you will definitely want to come back here again.
