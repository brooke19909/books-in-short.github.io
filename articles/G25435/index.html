Turkey, Sunshine Hotel - The True Turkish Riviera!

Sunshine Hotel, located on the Mediterranean coast, is the real Turkish Riviera. The hotel complex is located near the small town of Kestel, 7 km away from Alanya and 135 km from the airfield. It was built in 1989, and was restored in 2004. The total area of ​​the hotel is about 12,000 sq.m. Very close to it is a banana grove. Any guest of the hotel, walking along the long coast, will go to the village where you can visit the local market.

Turkey, Sunshine Hotel. Accommodation



The hotel complex has 174 standard rooms (18 sq. M.), Each of which can accommodate up to three people. 14 rooms are family rooms with a separate bedroom, a bunk bed and a double bed. 5 people can stay here, each with a total area of ​​35 sq.m. All rooms have carpet floor, except those located on the fourth floor, where the flooring is ceramic tiles. Rooms are cleaned daily; bed linen is changed three times a week. For a small fee, the maids lay out stunning patterns on the beds. Absolutely all rooms fully or partially have a view of the sea or banana grove, or mountains. The rooms have a TV, air conditioning, telephone, filled mini-bar (for a fee).

Sunshine Hotel, Turkey. Reviews about nutrition



As tourists note, the main dishes here are vegetable, cooked in a variety of ways. The meat is prepared exclusively by one method, the menu is rich in soy dishes. Those who like spicy food will enjoy the local cuisine. The restaurant has a huge selection of desserts and pastries. There are not many fruits, but they can be purchased at the bazaar, which is often organized opposite the hotel. For dinner, both chicken and fish are served. Vacationers note that juices and alcoholic drinks are often bred.

Turkey, Sunshine Hotel. Animation

Guests here are entertained not only during the working hours of the animators. Adults will surely enjoy various contests, for example, “Sunshine's Best Couple in Love”, “Mr. and Mrs. Hotel”, exciting “Women Against Men” competitions, fire performances and fire shows. The hotel staff speaks many languages, including Russian. Some animators do yoga. Often organized excursions, trips to a disco in the city of Alanya, boat trips on a yacht.

Turkey, Sunshine Hotel. Territory



The hotel has a restaurant, 4 cafes, a doctor’s office, a television room, a hairdresser, a library, a conference room, and a laundry. Guests have the opportunity to rent a car, as well as stroll through the shops located on the territory. In addition, everyone is available tennis court, darts. The list of paid services includes: the services of a professional photographer and massage therapist, a sauna, a visit to an Internet cafe, slot machines, rental of rackets and balls for tennis, a game of billiards. The hotel has an excellent pool. You can relax and enjoy the soap massage in the Turkish bath.

Turkey, Sunshine Hotel. Children's leisure

According to tourists, the scope of entertainment for children here is not very well organized. Animators are often involved with children and in the daytime children can draw and make fake tattoos near the pool. However, there is no children's pool.

Have a pleasant and memorable vacation!
