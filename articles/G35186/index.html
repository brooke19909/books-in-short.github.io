Hotel Kotva 4 * (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach)

One of the most popular countries for the summer holidays of our compatriots for several decades is Bulgaria. In turn, one of the favorite resorts in Russia here has long been Sunny Beach. This is not at all surprising, because the local healthy climate and the cleanest sea are perfectly complemented by a host of hotels for every taste and budget, as well as an abundance of various entertainment. Today we would like to get to know one of the best hotels in this resort called Kotva 4 *. We will find out what this hotel complex offers to its guests, and also learn about what you can do when you come on vacation to the Bulgarian Sunny Beach.





Where is the Kotva 4 * hotel located?

This hotel is located in the southern part of the Bulgarian resort called Sunny Beach. Kotva 4 * is located near the Imperial 4 * hotel on the first coastline (the distance to the beach is 150 meters). The hotel complex is four kilometers from the resort center. Just 800 meters away is the historic city of Nessebar. The distance between Burgas Airport and Kotva hotel 4 * is less than 40 kilometers. Thus, upon arrival, you will arrive at the hotel in less than an hour. It is very convenient if you go on vacation with young children.

Rules of residence at the four-star hotel "Kotva"

In accordance with the rules of the Kotva 4 * hotel (Bulgaria), the arrival of newly arriving travelers in the rooms they booked is carried out after 14:00. If you arrived at the hotel a little earlier than this time, then you can leave your things in the luggage storage room and go for lunch or straight to the pool. In the event that at the time of your early arrival the room you booked had already been left by previous guests and the hotel staff had time to prepare it, you will be settled immediately.

On the day of departure from Kotva 4 *, the hotel must be discharged before twelve in the afternoon. Then you should pay for your accommodation and use of additional services. If you have already paid in advance your accommodation to a travel agency, then the hotel you will only have to pay for additional services. You can pay here both in cash and with plastic cards of the Visa, Mastercard and Maestro payment systems. At the same time, if you book a room via the Internet with a credit card, then keep in mind that the hotel may deduct a prepayment from you. In this case, upon discharge from Kotva 4 * you will only have to pay the remaining amount for accommodation and use of additional services.

Children

Since the hotel is designed mainly for family guests, children of any age are allowed here. When placing a baby up to two years old in your room in a crib, you will need to pay 2.5 euros for each day of stay. Please note that only one crib can be accommodated in each room. Extra beds are not available for older children or adults.





Pets

According to the internal rules of the Kotva 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria), guests traveling with their pets are allowed here. However, you must inform the property in advance if you plan to check into a hotel with a pet. In some cases, pets may be charged.

Description of the resort Sunny Beach

The Bulgarian resort called Sunny Beach occupies a seven-kilometer coastal strip 30 kilometers north of the city of Burgas. It is located away from railways and highways, so the air here is clean and healthy. Sunny Beach is the largest resort in the country. So, on its territory there are about 800 hotels of various categories.

Sunny Beach is ideal for families with children. After all, the sea near the coast is shallow, the entry into the water is shallow, most hotels have children's pools with slides and playgrounds. Also, just 10 kilometers from the resort there is a large water park where both adults and children can have fun. The big plus is that all the hotels in Sunny Beach are located along the coast and in order to reach the sea, there is no need to go down the mountain or stairs.

However, you should not assume that this resort is intended exclusively for respectable family people who prefer a calm and comfortable stay. So, the youth here will also find what to do with themselves. Indeed, during the tourist season there are a lot of nightclubs and discos.

The width of the beach strip of Sunny Beach is from 30 to 40 meters. The sand here is shallow and very pleasant. It has a golden color. In summer, the temperature here during the day ranges from 22 to 30 degrees Celsius, while the water temperature is from 20 to 26 degrees Celsius.

The resort has all the conditions for practicing various water sports. So, at your service there is equipment for windsurfing, water skiing and bicycles, parachutes, hang gliders, boats, yachts, banana riding, catamarans, etc. Also in the resort you can play volleyball, futsal, tennis, basketball, mini-golf, bowling, ride a bike, take a ride on horseback, shoot archery.
