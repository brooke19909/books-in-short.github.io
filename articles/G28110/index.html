Hotel Siva Sharm (ex Savita Resort) 5 *, Turkey / Alanya: photos and description

Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * hotel is located at a distance of 5 km from Sharm El Sheikh Airport, in the Red Sea Bay area of ‚Äã‚ÄãSharks Bay, at a distance of 12 km from Naama Bay. Newly arrived guests are brought by transfer to their place of residence in just 15 minutes.



The hotel complex offers its guests a five-star economy vacation at very reasonable prices. It is built in the first line (about 100 meters from the sea.) If you initially focus on staying for 7 nights in a double standard room in January - February 2016, then the price of the tour per person will be only $ 350!

The hotel building is extensive, it is a snow-white modern 8-storey architectural cascade, stylized under the classical Arab-Muslim style with numerous arches. Arcades adorned all levels of the hotel complex.

New arrivals service

Upon arrival at the hotel, tourists who have purchased a tour at the Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * are served in a very presentable reception area, located on the ground floor of the building. It is dominated by delicate pastel yellow colors (walls, artificial stone flooring) and white (internal, reinforcing column walls, decorative internal balconies, top of internal arches).

Guests at the reception immediately put on a pass bracelet in their hand. In case of payment by the client of the tour on the all-inclusive system, a blue bracelet is issued. However, in any case, settlement takes place only at 14-00. Arriving earlier have to wait.

But (since the bracelets have already been received) you can have breakfast in the hotel restaurant. It is spacious, triple ceiling height. If you wish, choosing what you like from the buffet, you can enjoy food on the terrace overlooking the sea, listening to the noise of the oncoming waves.

Hotel rooms

The hotel is big. It offers tourists - 542 rooms. Of these, the vast majority (510) are standard doubles. 20 family-type rooms (with two bedrooms and an expanded living room), 2 VIP suites, 10 double apartments.

The guest rooms of the hotel complex have all the conditions for the comfort of vacationers. Central air conditioning. There is a TV, a safe (free), a telephone, a hairdryer, and a minibar (for a fee). The hotel rooms are spacious. Guests use large bathrooms with showers or bathtubs (when checking in, it is advisable to choose a more suitable option). Each room has a balcony or terrace.

The rooms have tiled floors, pastel color walls, modern furniture.

Housekeeping

Cleaning of the hotel grounds and rooms is always done carefully. Bed linen and towels are replaced daily. According to unspoken practice, guests leave a tip to the staff every other day or two (a dollar every other day).



Hotel staff clean the rooms of Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * also every day.

When buying a tour - discount

According to the reviews of tourists visiting Egypt, Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * provides its services relatively cheaply. Discounts are provided by cunning managers due to the fact that the hotel is temporarily in a state prior to seasonal repairs. There will always be vacationers who want to save money.

By the way, the wear itself is uncritical, it will be eliminated by cosmetic repairs. After the repair, as expected, the overhead will be taken into account in the prices of the tours, and their cost will increase. In addition, special seasonal discounts are provided in Egypt in winter.

After all, as you know, the Red Sea is relatively cool at this time (the water temperature is 21 degrees Celsius, which, however, is acceptable for the inhabitants of the middle lane, since it is comparable to our velvet season). In addition, long winter excursions to ancient sights (to Cairo and Luxor) are complicated by the seasonal 50-day wind from the Sahara in winter. However, on the territory of the hotel and the hotel beach there is no such problem.

Hotel beach

Many tourists choose the hotel Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * because of the beach. Booking describes it as a three-tier, sandy, more than half a kilometer long. The beach is convenient and comfortable. A deliberate excess of sun loungers and umbrellas has been installed here. Visitors can easily find a convenient place for themselves here. Three buffets are open from noon.



Resorts enter the sea with small and comfortable pontoons. It‚Äôs more convenient, because the Red Sea is often called coral. Its distinctive feature is a gentle relief (in order to be at a comfortable bathing depth, you will have to walk several tens of meters along the bottom.) In addition, the coral bottom (except for the beauty of an unforgettable underwater world) has an unpleasant feature: without special slippers, you can hurt yourself on corals. The entrance to the water from the pontoons is balanced in depth of the sea and deprives bathers of such inconvenience.

Pools

The hotel offers 4 outdoor and one indoor pool. Their total area is 2645 square meters. The main pool is just huge. It is possible to dabble and swim, and even (which is regularly done) breaking into teams, play water polo. And you can do this at the same time: there is enough space for everything.

The hotel also has a heated pool (it is very popular in the winter season, because the water here is heated to 32 degrees.)

In the pool area there are a sufficient number of deck chairs, umbrellas. A buffet is open from noon. There is the possibility of a comfortable stay in the lounge style.

Meals: breakfast, lunch

The menu of the hotel complex is an abundance of main dishes of meat, chicken, liver, turkey, fish. Fruits are relatively few. Dates and oranges are present daily. Melon and watermelon are less commonly served. The pastries of the main restaurant are good.

In terms of food, the Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * hotel can satisfy any requests of its guests. From 7-00 to 10-00, vacationers have breakfast in the Sea Paradise (this is the main restaurant of the hotel complex. The food served on the morning buffet is light and nutritious: oatmeal, rice porridge, yoghurts, several types of omelettes, pancakes, pastries, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãfruits .

Lunch is traditionally more substantial. According to the schedule, you can take food during the day from 12-30 to 14-30. Presented European, Egyptian and Oriental cuisine. Hearty and nutritious main dishes, a variety of side dishes, salads. Tourists praise pasta and pizza seasoned with a variety of sauces. Traditionally, pastries are beyond praise.

Grab a bite to eat at poolside beach areas

Vacationers on the hotel beach Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * (photo shows us this wonderful place) have the opportunity to have a meal there in three bars. From noon, they treat beach-goers with pizza, meat and fish cooked on the grill; appetizers.

Drinks are served at the beach bar from 10-00 to 17-30.

Dinner

There is a bar in the pool area, it is called "Splash". There you can get ice cream from 11 to 16.

Guests of the hotel complex can dine from 18-00 to 21-30 in the Sea Paradise. Delicious minced meat sausages are prepared for dinner here.



Vacationing gourmets have the opportunity to order their favorite dishes for dinner in restaurants by appointment: Italian (Bella Vista), Chinese (Dragon), fish (Fish). The table at which the reservation of dishes is made is located in the main restaurant.

Great drinks are offered to vacationers in a Bedouin tent. Hookah, Internet cafe.

Hotel leisure facilities

The infrastructure of the Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * hotel is quite developed. Bucking sites vyingly praise the Garden Spa spa located here, a beauty salon, gym, diving club, massage parlor, and sports sections. By the way, the list is true. Experienced instructors and specialists are engaged with those who wish from vacationers.

The billiard room, gym, courts, golf course are very popular. Animators periodically organize beach volleyball and water polo competitions.

As we already mentioned, the hotel has its own diving center, on the Red Sea coast. The enterprise is successful and profitable: contemplation and photographing of the underwater coral world is in demand by vacationers. Many novice divers take a few paid lessons. In addition, the hotel has the opportunity to do water skiing, canoeing, kayaking, parasailing at leisure.

In the evenings, performances and shows are organized at the amphitheater. They often invite professional artists.

Excursions

Tourists can book excursions right at the reception. Those wishing to purchase 5 * tours at the Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort hotel are advised to think through their sightseeing routes in advance. Expecting acquaintance with the branded sights of Egypt travel to Cairo (near it, on the Giza plateau, the ancient famous pyramids and the Sphinx were erected) and Luxor with its majestic ancient funeral memorials.

Fans of outdoor activities prefer sea-level excursions, jeep trips to nomads. Diving enthusiasts will find an opportunity to immerse themselves in the bright and unique world of corals, as well as receive a worthy international certificate attesting to their diving skills.

From hotels in Egypt there is the opportunity to go on a one- or two-day excursion to Jerusalem.

However, according to the specifics of a five-star hotel, we recommend that you still plan your excursions more moderately than if you were staying in a three-star hotel. Be thrifty! After all, you paid for a five-star vacation. In addition, meals on the all-inclusive system on many excursions are not relevant.

Rest for children

The hotel has children's animators, parents can temporarily hire a nanny. For families with small children, a cot is provided free of charge.

The smallest holidaymakers here are given a lot of attention. There is a mini-club for children from 4 to 10 years old, both before and after dinner. For children there is a special playground, a cinema hall, a special pool for games and swimming.

Animators are attracted to children in sports games, relay races. They participate in productions of performances. Active recreation organically alternates with calm: children draw, Russian-speaking animators tell stories and fairy tales to them. In a word, the little holiday-makers resting in the hotel complex create the illusion of immersion in a special, bright and fairy-tale world. So some even get upset saying goodbye to the hotel.

At the Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 * restaurant there is a children's menu and a children's corner is equipped. There are chairs and tables suitable for growth for small holidaymakers. They are offered milk porridge, cottage cheese, fruits, delicious pastries.

Conclusion

The hotel complex considered by us provides five-star leisure services at economical prices. What can you recommend to tourists in order to ensure a more pleasant and complete vacation?

A certain part of tourists does just that. Perhaps that is why they come to rest again and again in Siva Sharm Ex Savita Resort 5 *.
