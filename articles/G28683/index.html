Holidays

Both children and adults are fascinated by the show. But that is not all. Near the dolphinarium there is a ferris wheel and a large number of attractions!

A lot of water activities in the largest water park of the Azov coast - the Amazon. Small bowl for children with cute animal figures and an adult pool.
