Lenovo A7600 Tablet: Device Overview

Expanding the range of medium-priced tablets is a pretty profitable solution for the manufacturer. Lenovo, producing inexpensive and functional devices, can rightfully be considered a leader in this niche.

Design





Chinese manufacturers have always paid a lot of attention to appearance. The Lenovo A7600 tablet was no exception . The device has got a large 10-inch screen and bright colors. The front side is made in the usual black color, but the back is made of blue plastic. This combination nicely distinguishes the Lenovo A7600 tablet against the general background. A similar decision by designers allowed the gadget to look a little more expensive.

On the device‚Äôs body there are two cameras, the front and the main, on the side there are controls for sound control, a USB socket, 3.5, and a place for a flash card. There are stereo speakers on both sides of the display. The logo of the company and the main camera were sheltered on the back.

The slightly slanted corners on the sides complement the design of the tablet, making it visually thinner than it is.

The tablet has a weight of 544 grams, respectively, holding the device with one hand will be very difficult. There are also disadvantages: fingerprints will be visible on the back cover of the device. The same situation is with the screen. After a few touches, already noticeable traces appear.

Screen





The display is one of the main advantages that the Lenovo A7600 tablet has. The characteristics of the screen, of course, fail a bit: considering the diagonal of 10 inches, the resolution of only 1280 by 800 looks rather mediocre. Small details are noticeably blurred, and the image is grainy. However, the viewing angle and excellent display behavior in the sun alleviate most of the shortcomings.

Battery

The device has got a battery with a volume of 6340 maH. The battery capacity allows the tablet to be in standby mode for approximately 50 hours without additional recharging. With active use, the Lenovo A7600 tablet is able to work for about 6 hours, which is very good for devices with a large screen and a lot of useful functions.

The most expensive feature in the device is 3G. Working in this mode, the device will require additional charging after 4 hours.

Camera





The Lenovo A7600 tablet has two cameras. 5 megapixels are installed on the rear panel, and only 2 megapixels in front. The quality of the shooting is poor. The pictures are very mediocre, although the camera resolution is not bad.

In addition to quality, the user will encounter a lack of autofocus and a rather bad flash. When taking a picture at night, you should not hope for much. After all, even in daylight, the camera does not always allow you to take a good photo.

The front camera also does not shine with advantages, but with its help it is quite possible to cope with such a task as making a video call from the Lenovo A7600 tablet .

Sound

The speakers located near the screen provide the Lenovo A7600 with good sound. Of course, the device does not have the best bass, and the location is chosen a little poorly. When working with the device, your fingers will interfere with the speakers, closing them. And it will affect the sound.

Undoubtedly, placing the speakers on the front side is an excellent solution, but the Lenovo company in this tablet model was not able to realize the full potential of what was intended.

Filling





The company equipped the device with a processor with as many as four cores with a capacity of 1.3 Hz. Accordingly, the tablet is able to work without braking and freezes. Naturally, not all modern games and programs will work perfectly, but much is not required from the budget representative.

The RAM, which is only 1 gigabyte in the tablet, will be less pleased. Such a filling can cope with video playback, even with HD quality. You can‚Äôt expect more from such a device. The tablet has 16 GB of internal memory, and in addition supports flash drives with a capacity of up to 32 GB.

The device is capable of working with 3G networks and, in addition, has all the necessary functions, such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.

System

The device runs on Android 4.4.2 with a proprietary shell from the company. Such a system is quite enough to install most games and necessary applications. If the device comes with an older system, it is advisable to replace "Android" with a more modern version.

Reviews





Stylish design and relatively good functionality - this is how the Lenovo A7600 tablet can be described . User reviews are more inclined to consider the disadvantages of the device, forgetting that this is just a budget tablet.

Undoubtedly, the device has a lot of minuses, but there are also positive aspects. A good screen, albeit with a low resolution, will not only allow you to comfortably view the video, but will also be convenient when working. A good filling and not the oldest version of Android allows the owner to maximize the tablet's potential in any necessary area.

Despite everything, it is also worth noting the reviews of the owners of the gadget about the tablet: most people say that it works without freezing and braking. This is also a significant advantage over the disadvantages that will be listed below.

Of course, not without negative opinions. Just a terrible camera really spoils the impression of the tablet. And due to the small resolution of the display, the impression spoils even more. The error in the arrangement of the speakers is also palpable. When working with the device, the fingers close it, and the sound deteriorates, and given the lack of bass, the sounding music becomes flat and lifeless.

Conclusion

For those wishing to purchase a Lenovo A7600 tablet, a review of the device will be very useful. A detailed study of all the advantages and disadvantages will help to make a choice. Understanding what to expect from the tablet, you can decide for what purpose it is most suitable. But still, the final opinion will have to be drawn up by the owner. As you can see, the tablet of this model is not suitable for everyone.
