Mini-hotel "Aphrodite", Divnomorskoe

Not far from Gelendzhik is a small resort town where you can relax, - Divnomorskoe. Aphrodite is a guest house in which your vacation will be even better. After all, the resort is important not only the surrounding atmosphere, but also housing conditions. In "Aphrodite" everything is provided not only for a comfortable sleep, but also for the diversity of your leisure: there is a swimming pool, a playground and a green area.

A bit about the hotel





Hotel "Aphrodite" (Divnomorskoe) is conveniently located ten minutes walk from the central promenade of the town. The hotel is small, it has only four floors. The building is new, built of brick.

The owners of the Aphrodite Hotel tried to create comfortable conditions for their guests to relax for a reasonable fee. Therefore, in the hotel you can often meet those who have been coming here on vacation for more than a year.

It is noteworthy that the mini-hotel "Aphrodite" fully operates all year round. Therefore, you can come here even in winter.

Hotel Rooms





The rooms in the mini-hotel "Aphrodite" are designed to accommodate from one to five people. All rooms are modernly renovated. The walls are bright, making the room seem spacious. For your comfortable stay there are comfortable single and double beds made of wood, bedside tables, a large wardrobe, a small table and a couple of chairs, a TV, a fridge. The rooms have air conditioning to establish a comfortable temperature. The bathroom is equipped with a modern shower or a full bath. It depends on the room category.

There are several suites - one and two rooms. They additionally have sofas. All rooms have a balcony.

The cost of living starts from 1000 rubles. But the hotel has various discount systems, especially for those who are not coming for the first year, with children or for a long period of time.

Services provided by hotel staff





The price includes the use of:

  • Wireless Internet (Wi-Fi)
  • parking at the hotel;
  • ironing room;
  • sewing accessories (needles and threads);
  • playground;
  • children's and outdoor pools;
  • sun terrace;
  • barbecue;
  • kitchen for self-cooking;
  • first-aid kit;
  • call the maids.

For an additional fee, the staff of the Aphrodite Hotel (Divnomorskoye) will provide you with:

  • transfer to or from the hotel;
  • organization of excursions.
