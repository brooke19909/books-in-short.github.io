Festa Via Pontica 5 (Bulgaria / Pomorie): photos and reviews of tourists

Everyone who loves traveling does not mind spending their holidays in a 5-star hotel somewhere on the seashore. It is advisable that it is inexpensive. Well, everything is real. You can go to Bulgaria and stay at Balneo Resort Festa Via Pontica 5 *. This hotel has reasonable prices, which can be even lower if you use early booking. However, first things first.





general information

Festa Via Pontica 5 * is located in the town of Pomorie (Burgas region). The location is very advantageous and convenient, since it is in Burgas that the nearest airport is located. At 100 meters from the hotel there is a bus stop with which you can get almost anywhere. And there are many attractions nearby. For example, Bourgas North Beach is 12 kilometers from the hotel. At about the same distance is the Skyscraper Project. And quite nearby (~ 2.5 kilometers) is the Salt Museum. At 300 meters from the hotel there is a water park. In 15 minutes, by the way, you can drive to the old Nessebar, a UNESCO heritage site.

The beach at Festa Via Pontica 5 * is private, which cannot but rejoice. He is literally at arm's length. And this is the most important thing for tourists.

Wellness Services

Festa Via Pontica 5 * is a truly unique hotel. At least because in its territory there is an excellent medical center. It is called Festa Medica. Here you can go through the diagnosis of diseases, comprehensive rehabilitation, physiotherapy, or simply prevention.

In the procedures, estuary mud is widely used - a valuable natural product obtained from the bottom of the Pomorie salt lake. It is rich in substances such as potassium, calcium, chloride, magnesia, hydrogen sulfide, etc. It is also proved that it not only stimulates the metabolism, but also has anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects. By the way, alkali is also used in the procedures.

The medical center of the Festa Via Pontica 5 * hotel (Bulgaria) treats diseases associated with the musculoskeletal system, central and peripheral nervous system and metabolism. But this, of course, is not all. They also deal with diseases that affect the respiratory, cardiovascular and reproductive systems, as well as the skin.





SPA

It is also worth noting that the Festa Via Pontica 5 * hotel has its own beauty center. What could be better than ending the evening with a visit to a steam bath or a warm sauna? Probably only a deep relaxing massage.

However, the list of services here is much wider. In the SPA-salon, as well as in the medical center, real masters of their craft with the appropriate education work. So the customers here are served perfectly.

Especially popular here are mud masks and body wraps, galvanic, salt and hydromassage baths, aromatic peeling and antioxidant care using mavruda. This is a Bulgarian red grape variety, which is one of the most valuable.

Many more are enrolled in so-called tightening therapy and chocolate wraps. But one of the most unusual procedures is a regenerating mask with silver water. However, everyone can get acquainted with the full list of services on an individual basis - upon arrival.
