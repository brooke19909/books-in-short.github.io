Cards: suit of clubs

Everyone knows what an ordinary playing deck looks like. Worms, crosses, tambourines and spades - these suits are familiar to the gambler. But few people know that each of them carries a certain energy. So, what is the meaning of the suit of clubs?

General interpretation

Ordinary playing cards do not have a serious esoteric meaning, like, for example, the Tarot, but this does not prevent them from being used for fortune telling: they represent a rather strong symbolic system. The whole deck of cards embodies some semblance of the Universe, life with its sorrows and joys, the opposition of good and evil, light and dark.

The black suit of clubs symbolizes the cold seasons and the dark time of the day: autumn and winter, evening and night. This is a male symbol. Clubs - work, luck, money, flame forces. The king is like Zeus, the lady is his wife Hera, and the jack is young Apollo. Almost any card of this suit that fell during the prediction means happiness. All this embodies the suit of clubs. Ace

It represents evening time, false gossip, success. If the card has its tip pointed up, a gift awaits a person (the card that lies closest shows who will be the donor). In any fortune telling you should focus on the cards. Laying them out on the table, carefully look: what color does it fall? Clubs can change their meaning in accordance with this.

King of the same suit. Means a happy resolution of your plans.

Ten clubs. A gentle, touching love awaits a person.

Nine peak. Point down - good news, up - the news of troubles.

Seven clubs. Big win, victory in the competition.

Six clubs. An unexpected conversation, a date. With sixes of other stripes - a long road along the railways.

King

This is a friend who will definitely come to your aid. In an alliance with other cards of this suit means the appearance of people who will allow you to become happier. If there is no king of clubs in fortune-telling, most likely, a person will fail in small matters.

Jack of clubs. Expect grief.

Nine clubs. Point down - an important person pays attention to you, up - he can become your enemy.

Eight clubs. Events on the water, long-distance sailing, ship wreck.





Lady

Imperious woman or child born out of wedlock. It can mean unfortunate circumstances, problems in personal life, the appearance of an opponent or rival.

Eight clubs. Soon you will be able to enlist the help of a close relative or girlfriend.

Jack

Man in uniform, military, comrade. If neither the king nor the ace is found in the situation, then in the near future great worries should be expected. If this card is the first to drop, the prediction will be correct.

The jack is located in the middle, between the two ladies - betrayal in marriage, an affair on the side. Between the two jacks is anxiety for the future.

Ace of tambourine. Unknown helps you to avoid troubles.

A dozen tambourines. Success in business awaits you.

Eight clubs. Unexpected events, a pleasant surprise.

Seven peak. Woe, problems due to the actions of ill-wishers.

Ten

If a dozen falls in fortune-telling (suit of clubs), the value of the card must be interpreted depending on the colors of the suit surrounding it. In itself, it symbolizes change, financial and moral poverty, hard work, various dangers. At tambourines - a meeting with a business man, at the tears - a joyful find, victory in the lottery.

Ace of clubs. A pleasant change is expected.

A dozen tambourines. You may soon receive a large amount of money. The same means the eight of clubs that has fallen in the layout.

A dozen worms. New acquaintances, flirt. You will find success in love affairs.

Nine clubs. Soon you will be very surprised. Establishing relationships with people you love.

Nine, eight and seven clubs. Successful combination. In life, everything will be just the best for you.

Seven of spades without any ace. You should expect betrayal, deception. Be careful with loved ones.





Nine

Easy money, doubts. When fortunetelling at a specific person means that this character will soon cease any communication with you. The edge up - sadness, longing, false conversations, bitter tears, down - a pleasant conversation. Together with tambourines, they mean the soon receipt of money that will be spent in vain. Fallen together with the nine hearts signal of mutual love.

King and Queen of Clubs. Great love, strong feelings, pleasant company of an influential person.

Ten rush. Troubles financially.

Ten, eight or seven clubs. Good luck in all your endeavors.

Eight

Sadness, sorrow, death of a family member or close friend.

Ace of clubs. Fabulous success in business, if the card lies with its tip up - disappointment.

Jack of Hearts. Long conversation, unexpected losses.

Ten clubs. In the near future you will acquire an apartment or a house, receive an inheritance and gain wealth.

Seven clubs. Time came happiness, surprise. In an alignment with an ace of worms, it signals an imminent marriage or inheritance.





Seven

If there is a seven (the suit of clubs) in the layout, you should expect a close road, good news, an unexpected inheritance. If the card has dropped its tip up, it means that the person who is being guessed will soon have to shed a lot of tears.

Eight peak. If the cards are laid out to a young man, then the beloved is deceiving him.

Jack of Hearts, Four Ladies and Seven. You will soon have a son.

A dozen worms, if there are ladies, jacks and other sevens. Soon you will learn about replenishment in the family. News of the pregnancy.

Six

Clubs are a suit that in many ways predicts a long road. Six signals the sea road, vain journey, a love meeting on the street or in the garden.

Ten clubs. Soon you will learn about departure.

Ace of Hearts. A pleasant morning date awaits you. If the ace of clubs falls in the layout, expect a date in the evening, ace of spades - you should prepare for a night meeting.





Any fortune-telling can tell you exactly what you should expect from life. Depending on your suit: clubs, spades, diamonds or worms, you can find out the most secret secrets of the future. It is only necessary to correctly determine the value of the cards and correctly interpret the layouts.
