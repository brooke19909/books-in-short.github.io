Hotel Nicolas Villa Agia Pelagia 3 * (Greece, Crete, Agia Pelagia): description, services, reviews

Tourist Greece is very popular among our compatriots. Hotels here are significantly inferior to complexes from Turkey or Egypt, but the very atmosphere of the country, its rich history and culture attract travelers from year to year. Often they choose a small but cozy hotel Nicolas Villas 3 * (Greece, Agia Pelagia), which offers relaxation at low prices. How does it attract tourists?

Where is the complex located? What sights to see?

Agia Pelagia is a former fishing village, which is now a vibrant Greek resort in Crete. The narrow cobblestone streets on which snow-white houses are located enchant her. The hotel is located on the outskirts of the city in a quiet location, which is well suited for a relaxing holiday. The seashore is located quite far - 700 meters from the complex. You can get to him on foot. In this case, the journey takes about 20 minutes. You can get to the beach by bus. The benefit of public transport is 50 meters from the Nicolas Villa Agia Pelagia 3 * hotel.

The international airport is located 22 km from the resort. If you go from there by taxi, then the road will take only half an hour. It can also be reached by bus. The cost of the trip will be much cheaper, but the travel time will almost double. The large city of Heraklion is 18 km from the complex . Shops, cafes and restaurants are located in the central part of Agia Pelagia, which is 2.5 km away from the hotel.
The main attractions of the island of Crete are located near Heraklion . Tourists who come to rest in Greece can visit the following of them:

  • Church of St. Pelagia;
  • Akhlada village ;
  • Savvafianon Monastery ;
  • El Greco House Museum;
  • Heraklion Archaeological Museum ;
  • Battle of Crete Museum.

Basic information about the hotel and the approximate cost of the tour

The hotel was built in 2006. It is located on a small hill with a great view of the nearby buildings. In total, the hotel has two three-story complexes that offer vacationers only 20 rooms. It is suitable for those who do not like large crowds of tourists. Most often, adults who travel on their own stay here, as well as older travelers and families with children. It is worth knowing that check-in starts strictly from 14:00 and lasts until 21:00. You cannot come here with pets. To pay for additional services, Visa, MasterCard and Maestro cards are accepted. The checkout time here is considered to be from 08:00 until noon. Tourists must leave their rooms at this time.





A tour to the Nicolas Villas 3 * hotel is usually chosen by people with limited means. The complex is considered a budget place, although travelers can find hotels cheaper if desired. The minimum cost for a standard double room here starts at 3,500 rubles. In July and August, prices may rise to 6,000 rubles. The price includes not only accommodation, but also all-inclusive meals. Thus, a week's rest here costs an average of 25,000-30,000 rubles for two.

A bit about the rooms

The hotel cannot boast spacious rooms. All apartments here have a small area. Their interior is also quite simple and made in a classic style. But each room is equipped with a small balcony with a beautiful view of the surroundings and the sea. All apartments are cleaned daily by maids. The Nicolas Villas 3 * hotel (Greece, Agia Pelagia) has an ecosystem. This means that towels and bed linen change as they become dirty. To inform staff that they are dirty, tourists should lay them on the floor. If this is not done, then the maids will change them every 3 days.





The complex has the following types of rooms:

  • Standard. Its area is only 15 square meters. The apartment has two single beds. An extra bed is available upon request. The room has a private bathroom and a small balcony.
  • Family Room. Two-room suite designed for four people. It consists of a bedroom, living room, bathroom, as well as a kitchenette. Dishes are not issued. There is a double bed and two folding sofas .

More about room equipment

The tourist complex does not offer rich equipment of rooms, but they have everything you need for a comfortable stay. So, the bathroom is equipped with a modern shower, toilet, washbasin and mirror, which can be used for shaving or applying makeup. Cosmetic accessories are not issued, so you should take them with you from home. A hairdryer can be requested from the administrator of the Nicolas Villa Agia Pelagia 3 * hotel, which is located around the clock at the reception. It is issued free of charge. A mini fridge can be used to store food and drinks . Family rooms also have a small stove. A small TV broadcasts cable channels, including several Russian ones.

If you are afraid for the safety of your belongings, you can ask the administrator for a safe. Its use is not included in the price and is paid daily. The safe is mechanical, that is, it is locked with a key. A large fine is foreseen for his loss. In hot weather, vacationers can order individual air conditioning in the room. It is also provided only for money. There is no internet connection in the apartment, even at an additional cost.

Conditions for relaxing with children

Hotel Nicolas Villas 3 * is a small complex, but here everything you need for a comfortable holiday for families with children. It is located in a very quiet place, and there are no nightclubs, restaurants and cafes in the area. Therefore, it is very quiet here at night and in the evening. The hotel has a non-fenced area where children of any age can play for free. There are several swings, a plastic slide. The platform is located on the grass. In high season, a mini club is open for children from 4 to 12 years old. Younger guests can swim in the small pool, the depth of which does not exceed one and a half meters.





For the convenience of parents, a cradle ( playpen ) can be delivered to the apartment . It is provided free of charge, but not more than one piece per room. The hotel restaurant has high chairs for feeding.

Features Greek cuisine, restaurants and bars

Greek cuisine is famous for its abundance of fresh vegetables, herbs, olive oil and dairy products. Meat dishes are most often prepared from lamb and low-fat chicken. Seafood and fish are also popular. Arriving at Nicolas Villa Agia Pelagia 3 *, you should definitely try a real Greek salad, local wines, grilled octopus, moussaka (stew from eggplant, tomatoes and minced meat).





The hotel itself offers its guests an all-inclusive system. This is quite convenient, because there are no restaurants and cafes nearby. All meals are served at the shared buffet. The hotel's restaurant menu includes recipes for Greek, Mediterranean and European cuisine. The price includes breakfast, lunch and dinner. In the daytime, local drinks are served: homemade wines, draft beer, mineral water, concentrated juices. The pool bar serves instant coffee, tea and refreshing cocktails. Crayfish are also served here. For dinner, tourists are offered liquors, wines, pastries and light snacks.

Read more about infrastructure and local services

Agia Pelagia and its hotels do not have a developed infrastructure. Only wired internet is available on site for a fee. It cannot be connected to mobile phones. The reception is open 24 hours a day. Here you can discuss your problems with the hotel administrator, as well as use the fax and photocopy services. If guests feel unwell, then a doctor can be called to the room. But it is worth remembering that his services are paid. The administrator can also send your things to the laundry.





For an additional amount, tourists are offered the services of a porter, who deliver heavy bags to the room. The hotel has a small mini market that sells essential goods. Tourists will be able to leave their car in private parking. You can do this at any time for free.

What entertainment options does the complex offer?

Nicolas Villas Hotel 3 * (Crete, Heraklion ) cannot offer a huge selection of entertainment. In summer, in fine weather, a freshwater pool is always open. You can sunbathe on the terrace nearby. It has free umbrellas and sun loungers. The hotel does not give towels. There are practically no options for active leisure in the complex. For a fee, tourists can rent table tennis equipment or play billiards. On the city beach is a water entertainment center.





Positive opinions about hotel vacations

To decide on a place to stay, tourists often read the opinions of previous guests. They help to find out the real situation in the hotel. Guests of the Nicolas Villas 3 * hotel leave reviews contradictory. Most often they highlight the following advantages:

  • The hotel area is beautiful and well maintained. The outer part of the cases looks very nice.
  • Good housekeeping. Every 3 days they wash the floors, plumbing in the bathroom, wipe the dust and take out the garbage.
  • The staff is responsive, quickly solves the problems.
  • The pool is very large and deep enough. Well suited for swimming adults. You can swim even at night.




Hotel criticism

Tourists vacationing in the complex Nicolas Villas 3 *, write reviews and negative. For some of them, vacation here was a real disappointment. They do not recommend staying here, arguing their point of view with the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Terrible food. The menu here consists mainly of fast food . Serve dry pasta, spicy rice. Unfinished pasta is mixed in salads.
  • Drinks in the bar are very diluted with water, they serve cheap tea in bags.
  • The rooms are in terrible condition. Some tourists say that a socket was torn out in their bathroom, and the lamp on the ceiling did not work in the bedroom.
  • A lot of insects. The hotel does not fight with them. In the evening, it is simply impossible to open the window in the room.
  • Long lines in the restaurant. You have to stand for half an hour to wait for a free table.
  • The beaches are very far away. Buses that go there are constantly packed with people, it is impossible to travel in the heat in them.

Hotel Conclusions

The hotel is suitable for a relaxing holiday. Pensioners, families with children and just adults love to stay here. Many of them use it only for an overnight stay.
