Margaret Studios 3 * (Greece / Rhodes): photos and reviews of tourists

Greece is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Europe, especially when it comes to beach and sightseeing vacations. And many travelers as the place of temporary stop choose the legendary island of Rhodes, which is famous not only for its beautiful beaches, but also for its ancient history and culture. Of course, there are many hotels on the island that are ready to receive tourists all year round. And the Margaret Studios 3 * hotel is considered a pretty good choice.

Of course, when planning a vacation, tourists try to get as much information as possible about the chosen hotel. So what does the hotel really look like and what kind of impressions does the tourist have in itself?

Can I eat at the hotel?





Hotel Margaret Studios 3 * offers its guests breakfast, which is held in a spacious restaurant hall. Reviews indicate that the food here, despite not too varied menus, is tasty and satisfying. There are light snacks and salads, toasts and pastries with jam, fresh fruits and hot drinks. In order to gain strength in the morning this place fits perfectly. The hotel also has a bar where you can relax and enjoy delicious drinks.

The rooms have well-equipped kitchenettes, and there are shops near the hotel, so many tourists from time to time cook on their own. And in this area there are lots of cafes and restaurants for every taste, where you can have a bite to eat, while paying not so much.

Beach: distance, amenities and leisure of tourists on the beach





Arriving here, tourists, of course, expect to relax on the beach. What should tourists expect at Margaret Studios 3 * hotel? Rhodes is an island that is famous for its spacious and incredibly beautiful beaches. The distance to the seashore from the hotel is no more than 300 meters.

Reviews say that the coast is beautiful. The beach itself, as well as the entrance to the sea, is covered with large pebbles. That is why travelers are advised to acquire suitable shoes. Immediately it is worth saying that the sea is not too calm here, most of the year there are quite large waves. It is this fact that makes the beach popular among surfers - there is even a windsurfing center nearby where you can rent boards, suits and other equipment, as well as take a short training course.

There are sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach, but their rent is paid. Nearby is a small bar where vacationers can enjoy a chilled cocktail or even have a tasty meal. Of course, here tourists can go boating and water skiing, engage in various types of water sports and relax actively.

Additional amenities for guests





Despite the relatively modest size of the Margaret Studios 3 * hotel, its guests can count on some additional amenities. In almost all public areas, including the lobby, there is Internet access, and its speed is decent.

Free parking is located near the hotel, and renting a transport, whether it be a car or a bicycle, is not difficult. For a small fee, you can rent a safe at the reception desk, which, by the way, works around the clock.
