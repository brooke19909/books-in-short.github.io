Hotel Hotel Rivijera 4 * (Petrovac, Montenegro): overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

In one of the most beautiful resort corners, the Hotel Rivijera 4 * hotel is located . Petrovac (Montenegro) attracts tourists from all over the world with its magnificent nature, as well as high-quality service and affordable prices.





Short hotel description

Since 1963, the Rivijera 4 * hotel (Petrovac) has been hosting guests from all over the world. Beach, blue pools, green gardens, clean air, comfortable rooms, delicious food - what else is needed for a good rest? After the restoration of 2004, the hotel was brought into line with international standards. In addition, confirmation of the high level of the institution is its belonging to the well-known hotel chain Crystal Riviera Resort. On the territory of 22,000 square meters. m there are 2 residential buildings with 91 guest rooms, as well as numerous infrastructure facilities.

Location

In a picturesque seaside town is a resort hotel Rivijera 4 *. Petrovac, Montenegro, Obala bb, 85310 - the address of this institution. This place is located between the cities of Bar and St. Stephen. If you want to walk around Budva, then you have to overcome a little less than 20 km. Dubrovnik Airport is 20 km away, while Tivat is 40 km away. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a green park area with cypresses and pines, as well as the main promenade with shops, restaurants and entertainment venues.

The hotel Rivijera 4 * hotel can be considered an ideal place for a sea holiday. Petrovac is a small place that is loved by tourists from all over the world due to the clear sea and the picturesque coastal zone. You can visit the following best beaches:

Negative reviews

There were some negative moments in the Rivijera 4 * hotel (Petrovac, Montenegro). Reviews say the following:

  • the restaurant has a fairly uniform food (therefore, it is better to limit yourself to breakfast, and get acquainted with local cuisine in neighboring establishments);
  • the rooms are quite cramped;
  • the restaurant flies a lot of wasps, and the staff does not struggle with this phenomenon;
  • even taking into account the fact that sunbeds on the beach are paid, they are not enough for all, and therefore you need to take places literally from 6 in the morning;
  • although the hotel is smoke-free, the restaurant and bar smelled of tobacco smoke;
  • beaches open only in June, and therefore in the spring neither toilets nor other infrastructure work on them;
  • the air conditioner cools the air slightly enough, which is especially noticeable in the summer heat;
  • there are few walking paths in the territory;
  • the hotel does not have its own beach (only municipal);
  • almost no animation (this is offset by a convenient location next to a variety of entertainment venues).
