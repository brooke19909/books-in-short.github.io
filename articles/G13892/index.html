Acacias Hotel 4 * (Bodrum, Turkey): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

For more than twenty years, Turkey has been a favorite vacation spot among Russians. At the same time, one of the most popular in this country is the resort of Bodrum, which offers a combination of magnificent beaches, a mild and healthy climate and lots of interesting sights. In this amazing place, the weather is excellent for six to seven months.

Here you can have fun spending time surfing or sailing, strolling along the beautiful streets of the city, contemplating local attractions, enjoying fascinating sightseeing tours.



Bodrum Hotels

Picturesque nature, golden shores with dunes, majestic mountain ranges, shrouded in relict vegetation, a turquoise calm sea, heated by dazzling sunbeams - all this makes this resort attractive for tourists coming here.

Acacia Hotel 4 * (Bodrum): reviews

In general, this hotel meets the expectations of Russian travelers. Perhaps the only minus of its vacationers called some remoteness from local attractions.

Reviews are almost all positive. And this is not surprising: a great location, the beach is very clean, albeit small, but with comfortable sun loungers and an excellent bar.



As for the number of rooms, although everything in the rooms is neat and tidy, in many reviews the need for cosmetic repairs is noted, although it is emphasized that the plumbing works properly, without causing any hassle.
