Why is it recommended to refinance cards in other financial institutions?

Sberbank credit card refinancing is used for certain purposes, for example:

  • Lower monthly payment.
  • Interest rate cuts.
  • Combining several loans at once.

Most modern financial institutions offer customers cards with grace periods. This means that if the debt is repaid quickly within a short period of time, interest will not be charged. However, in the event that the borrower does not have time to return the money on time, they will take a rather high interest rate on the loan. Moreover, in most situations, interest is accrued from the moment the funds are withdrawn. In this case, refinancing cards helps to optimize the burden on budget expenditures.

Such a procedure can be useful even when the card has been issued for a long time. In this situation, most likely, the borrower will be forced to pay huge interest. Considering that in most examples banks do not set monthly payments, many holders of such credit cards deposit a small amount as part of paying the loan. Subsequently, they wonder why the main debt is not reduced. In fact, the funds contributed are barely enough to pay interest.

Regardless of the purpose for which refinancing is planned, it is important to calculate any costs of the procedure with the benefits of it. Only after comparing the results of the calculations can we say with certainty whether it is profitable to apply the proposal in question. Next, find out which financial institution should refinance a Sberbank credit card.

Refinancing offer from Alfa Bank

Alfa-Bank proposes to refinance credit cards and other loans. In this case, you can combine up to five contracts, provided that their total amount does not exceed three million rubles. If necessary, you can get some of the funds in cash and spend on personal needs.

The rate starts, as a rule, from 12% per annum, which is significantly lower compared to many credit cards in other structures. At the same time, the size of the insurance policy does not affect its size. Absolutely all salary clients can count on the lowest possible rate.

The procedure with the design is simple. It is enough to leave a request on the official website and, after waiting for a preliminary decision, go to the bank with the original documents. After receiving the loan will be converted into payment of refinanced funds. When they are closed, you need to get a certificate of debt repayment.

Where else can I refinance a Sberbank credit card?

Offer from Raiffeisenbank

Up to four credit cards can be refinanced at Raiffeisenbank. In this case, the rate starts at 12% per annum, and the maximum maturity can reach 60 months. Customers are given the opportunity to receive part of the cash. In this case, the amount of the issued loan should not be more than two million rubles. A distinctive feature of the program are not only favorable conditions, but also the complete absence of the need to obtain permission from an existing creditor for the possibility of refinancing.

It is important to keep in mind that this financial structure will require confirmation of the repayment of loans that have been submitted for re-lending. During the quarter, information on the complete closure of the loan must necessarily appear in the credit bureau, or the borrower can bring the appropriate certificate. In the event that this is not done, the rate will increase by as much as 8 points.

Refinancing at Tinkoff Bank or 120 days without interest

The balance transfer service is a type of refinancing developed by Tinkoff Bank. Debts on any credit cards, including Sberbank, can be transferred to this organization. At the same time, the refinancing amount is limited by the limit set by the client on the Tinkoff card. For those who do not have a card, it is enough to leave a request online and get the necessary consent on it.

Reverse refinancing of the Tinkoff credit card is also carried out in Sberbank.

If the program is used, the client can return the debt to this bank without any interest within 120 days. The only condition is to make a monthly payment of at least 6% of the amount owed. This approach to the refinancing procedure allows not only to reduce the overpayment, but also completely get rid of interest in the first four months.
