Kempinski Hotel Aqaba Red Sea 5 * (Jordan / Aqaba): traveler reviews

If you want to warm yourself in the hot sun in the autumn or winter and swim in the gentle waters of the southern seas, and you do not want to fly to Egypt or Tunisia, then go to Jordan. In addition, it will be possible to combine a beach holiday with an informative one; visit the historical sites that this amazing country is rich in; and even on the shores of the Red and Dead Seas, you can improve your health; have fun and eat well. The best restaurants are located on the territory of luxury hotels, for example, in Kempinski Aqaba Red Sea.



Holidays in Jordan

This country has recently become a popular tourist destination. There is a rich excursion program, the magnificent coast of two seas at once - the Dead and the Red, excellent diving and snorkeling, the golden sands of the desert, etc. Tourists with children especially like it here, the country will seem a little boring to young people in terms of entertainment and nightlife. However, rest here can not be called cheap. There are very few budget hotels, and staying in luxury hotels, for example in Kempinski Hotel Aqaba Red Sea 5 *, is expensive - about 250 euros per night. However, the service provided by these hotels is well worth it. Mostly a special category of pilgrim tourists come to Jordan, who at all costs dream of going to the city of Petra, as well as visiting other ancient monuments of architecture that are distinguished by their uniqueness. Many are also interested in the country's major cities - Aqaba, and of course, Amman.



How to get to Jordan?

Regular flights are operated from Moscow to the capital of Jordan by Royal Jordanian, as well as Siberian Airlines (S7 Airlines). The flight duration is about 4 hours. There is no need to pre-apply for a visa. You can receive it upon arrival in the country. If you fly to this Arab country to relax on the Red Sea at the Kempinski Hotel Aqaba 5 * hotel (Jordan), then, having arrived in Amman, you can take the only domestic flight to Aqaba, where this hotel is located. You can also go there by the comfortable JETT bus or private Trust. The road also takes 4 hours.

There is another great option - rent a car. To do this, you will need international rights and a credit card, on which the amount from 150 to 500 US dollars will be blocked as a security. If you rent a car through a travel agency - it will cost you less. By the way, the movement in this country is right-handed. Climate

Despite the fact that Jordan is washed by the waters of the Dead and Red Seas, the climate here is subtropical Mediterranean, but dry and sharply continental. Even in January, the temperature does not drop below 10 degrees. The maximum air temperature is + 35-40 degrees. It is best to visit Jordan in the fall and spring months. It is quite rainy here in winter. The exception is the same Aqaba, where the aforementioned Kempinski Hotel Aqaba 5 * hotel (Jordan) is located. The photos that are posted in the article are gorgeous, but they cannot fully convey all the charm that reigns in this paradise. So, in this resort city all year round you can indulge in a beach holiday. Water even in the coldest months warms up to 21 degrees.

Aqaba Resort

It is located on the shores of the bay of the same name. This is a fairly young resort, so the hotels here are relatively modern. There are about 340 kilometers from Amman to it. As already noted - 4 hours by bus, and only a few minutes if you fly by plane. The water in the bay is just crystal. If the country was in the EU, the beaches would be awarded the Blue Flag. The town is surrounded by rocky mountains, which provide a comfortable microclimate. It is often hot in the summer, so if you cannot stand the heat, then avoid coming here in late July and early August. By the way, in the off-season prices will be noticeably reduced here, and there will not be an influx of tourists. Further in the article we will tell you about the Kempinski Hotel Aqaba 5 * hotel . As you know, it belongs to the world-famous luxury hotel chain. And this means that everything here must correspond to the general concept of the brand, and therefore - to be at the highest level.



Kempinski Hotel Aqaba 5 * (Jordan): hotel description

The hotel is just a few minutes walk from the business center of Aqaba, where, in addition to the offices of large corporations, banks, etc., there are shopping centers and branded stores. By the way, Aqaba is one of the largest cities in Jordan, and Kempinski Hotel Aqaba is one of the best five-star business hotels, and only then - a seaside resort designed for a beach holiday. Nevertheless, even the businessmen who arrived at the conference or symposium, having seen the beauty of the sea landscapes, dream of finishing all tasks as soon as possible, so that they can spend longer time with azure and crystal clear water and devote all their free time to swimming, diving and sunbathing.

As for the appearance of the hotel, the exterior is made in the Arabic style. However, the water zone, which descends in semicircles to the beach, attracts particular attention. Some pool compartments are hot tubs, while others are pool bars where the seats are under water. The hotel has a magnificent spa with access to a sandy beach where guests can enjoy thalassotherapy. The best rooms at Kempinski Hotel Aqaba are suites located on the top floor. Their most important advantage is a beautiful, breathtaking landscape that opens from windows and from balconies.



Location

The Kempinski Hotel Aqaba 5 * hotel, as already described, is located directly on the shores of the Gulf of Aqaba, which belongs to the Red Sea. He has his own sandy beach 10-20 meters from the building. From Aqaba Airport, which connects the city with many countries of the world, the hotel can be reached in just 10 minutes. And the journey from the main "air gate" of Jordan - Amman Airport, which is called Queen Alia, is about three hours. The city center, with its many supermarkets and shopping centers, is just a 10-minute walk away.

Hotel building and grounds

The Kempinski Hotel Aqaba 5 * was built in 2011. As already mentioned, it belongs to the international hotel chain Kempinski International. The beautiful crescent-shaped building has 10 floors. The first two are public facilities. That is, it is a spa, restaurants, bars, etc.

Room conditions

Despite the fact that it is almost never cold here, all rooms at Kempinski Hotel Aqaba are equipped with a heating system, and, of course, air conditioning, which maintains a normal microclimate in the rooms even in the hottest weather. All rooms, without exception, have either balconies or spacious terraces with summer furniture for a pleasant stay. The beds in all bedrooms are simply gigantic, and Executive rooms have non-standard shapes and sizes. The suites have kitchenettes with tea and coffee making facilities. In the recreation area (living room) there is a plasma TV with satellite channels. Internet in the hotel is wireless and free; telephone - with direct connection. All rooms have a safe and minibar. Bathrooms, depending on the category, have different sizes. However, without exception, all have a shower, bidet, bathrobes, slippers, hairdryer and all the necessary toiletries. As already noted, only superior rooms have a jacuzzi. As for the room service, food is delivered around the clock. Printed products can also be ordered. Linen change and cleaning are daily. Pets are not allowed in the rooms.
