Holiday Village "Orlinka" on Seliger: description and reviews

If you are considering for a relaxing holiday surrounded by the coniferous nature of the recreation center on Seliger, Orlinka is exactly what you need.

general information





On the recreation center "Orlinka" it is good to relax both alone and in a large company or family with children. Everyone will find something to their liking. A distinctive feature of the base is the ability to relax all year round. In summer, you can sunbathe on a sandy beach and enjoy the local berries, in the autumn you can pick mushrooms, in winter winter types of entertainment (skiing, hockey) become available.

Number of rooms





The recreation center "Orlinka" offers its guests the following accommodation options:

  • Two-story townhouse. On the ground floor there are eight double rooms, consisting of a small living room and bedroom. The apartment has a double bed, a wardrobe, bedside tables, a table and chairs, a sofa bed (as an extra bed), a TV and a refrigerator. On the second floor there are four four-bed rooms with two extra beds. The apartment has an entrance hall and two rooms, one of which has a balcony. The set of furniture in them is the same as in the rooms on the ground floor. Shower and toilet are provided in each room.
  • Double cottage. One-room apartments have a double bed, an additional chair-bed, a TV, a fridge, an electric kettle and a set of dishes. The bathroom is equipped with a shower.
  • Quadruple two-story cottage. On the ground floor there is a living room with a TV, a fridge, an electric kettle and a set of dishes, an entrance hall, a shower and a toilet. Two bedrooms and an attic are equipped on the second floor.
  • Hotel. One mini-hotel has 6 rooms, and the other has 8. There are single and double apartments. Each room has the necessary set of furniture, as well as a TV, an electric kettle, a refrigerator, dishes. The bathroom is equipped with a shower.
  • Summer houses. You can stay in one-room double rooms from June to August. All apartments have the necessary furniture for a comfortable stay, a TV and a bathroom with a shower. The rooms have access to the veranda.

Facilities and service





The list of services provided at the recreation center "Orlinka":

  • car parking;
  • cable and satellite television;
  • gym;
  • various leisure options;
  • medical office;
  • Cafe;
  • children's playground with swings and slides;
  • beach.

Among the available entertainment are:

  • summer cinema;
  • table tennis;
  • billiard room;
  • volleyball and basketball courts;
  • sauna with a swimming pool;
  • Russian sauna;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • fishing;
  • boat rental;
  • excursions by boat or bus.

You can rent a sun lounger or umbrella on the sandy beach.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are available in the dining room. It serves Russian cuisine and a variety of hot and cold drinks. Meals are included in the price. If you wish, you can visit the cafe located in the recreation center.

Reviews about the recreation center "Orlinka"





The recreation center has been welcoming guests since 1976. During this time, restoration work was carried out several times, the number of rooms was updated, new leisure options were offered. Vacationers differently assessed the efforts of employees of the recreation center "Orlinka", but from all the reviews left, you can highlight the following points:

  • The recreation center has a great location. Around amazing nature: lakes, forest, various vegetation.
  • You can take a walk in the vicinity, if the weather is good, then you can pick up mushrooms and berries.
  • The rooms are clean. Even the summer houses, which are considered an economy category, are tidy and comfortable.
  • In the dining room, the dishes are not very diverse, but everything is fresh and tasty.
  • The excursion program is very eventful.
  • Administrators are polite, friendly.
  • For children, they hire an animator who constantly comes up with new exciting activities for kids.
  • The beach is clean, the entrance is gentle. The only drawback is that there are no changing cabins.
  • Pleases a lot of entertainment.
  • In the summer, almost every day they organize discos for adults.

Among the shortcomings of the Orlinka recreation center, guests note the lack of wireless Internet and the poor road. Also, with a large number of vacationers, if it is too late to come to breakfast in the dining room, the buffet will already be empty.

Location

The recreation center "Orlinka" (Seliger) is located just 4 km from the village of Peno in the Tver region. It is separated from Moscow by 410 km. You need to drive your own car along the Leningradskoye Shosse to Torzhok. Then take the road to Ostashkov. Then you just follow the signs that lead directly to the recreation center.

Also in "Orlinka" can be reached by public transport. To do this, take the train number 604, the next from the Leningrad station on the route "Moscow-Ostashkov." Next, you can get to the base by taxi or private bus, which runs on reservation.
