Atlantis and the Tale

If your whole family has gathered for a summer vacation, then you should definitely visit the Atlantis water park. Yalta will meet you on hot sunny days and give you an unforgettable experience. The water park is known for its slides and attractions, where you can tickle your nerves, take pictures for memory and, no doubt, get an adrenaline rush. There you can enjoy the feeling of weightlessness.

For lovers of more relaxed types of relaxation, the Waterfall attraction is provided in the water park, as well as a swimming pool with waves, which will give no less than pleasant impressions. High quality service and reasonable prices - this is what will please those who visit the Atlantis water park.

Crocodile aquarium

But not only in the zoo you can see animals. Lovers of wildlife will certainly be interested in visiting the Yalta Crocodile Aquarium. This is an amazing exposition of reptiles, which is located in the heart of the city. Surely not only the kids will enjoy such an excursion, but also adults will not without interest look at the crocodiles, pythons, iguanas, monitor lizards, river and sea turtles living there.

"Glade of fairy tales"

Another place where the whole family will undoubtedly enjoy it is Glade of Fairy Tales. Yalta boasts such a landmark as the open-air fairy tale museum. It is located in a picturesque place - at the foot of the Stavri-Kaya rock, surrounded by forest and mountains. Although the museum is designed for children, it will be interesting for adults to meet with the heroes of childhood fairy tales. The program of the traditional excursion includes a performance with the participation of Baba Yaga. And in the summer - a theater where clowns, pirates, mermaids perform every hour. Children are sure to enjoy the puppet show, which the Glade of Tales prepared for its young guests. Yalta does not limit its attractions. Visiting it, you will not regret it if you visit other picturesque places.

Massandra Palace

Fans of historical architectural monuments should visit the Massandra Palace in the village of Massandra, near Yalta. This complex began its history since 1783. It was originally designed by the French architect Bouchard in the Neo-Baroque and Neo-Renaissance styles on the orders of Count Vorontsov. But after the death of the count (before the end of construction), the palace was acquired by Alexander III. And it was already finished according to the project of Messmacher, which made significant changes to the decor of the building. Now, the Massandra Palace has turned into a museum, the first and second floors of which were occupied by the exposition of the palace interior. Halls of the third floor are given for exhibitions.

Botanical Garden





One of the "cult" places in Yalta, which is also known outside our country, is the Nikitsky Botanical Garden. Here you will see more than a thousand species of flowers and trees, some of them are truly rare. And in the science museum you can see the numerous (almost 170,000 copies) herbarium, be sure to look into the cactus greenhouse. Florists will also like this place because, in addition to souvenirs, you can buy seeds and seedlings.
