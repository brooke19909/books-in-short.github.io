Kingston upon thames

Kingston-on-Thames is a suburb of London where the famous Royal Palace settled in the past. This place is rightly called the most picturesque in the whole capital of England and its environs. The name of this suburb of London translates as "the city of kings." This is due to its history, because it was here that the English monarchs were crowned. The area is located near Charing Cross Station and is the most well-groomed place in London.

The Middle Market Square and ancient streets remind of the Middle Ages here. All this ancient surroundings is wonderfully combined with elements of a modern metropolis - cars, skyscrapers, shops. Kingston attracts many investors and tourists from around the world.

Near this picturesque area there are three wonderful flowering parks where you can enjoy the peace and abundance of greenery. You can also arrange an excursion to the sights and gardens of Kingston, as well as visit the amusement park called "Chessington World."

At the Chessington Zoo, you can admire a wide variety of flora and fauna, from leopard babies to the incredible species of fish that are abundant in the Sea Animal Center.

Richmond upon thames

Houses in a suburb of London called Richmond will amaze you with their luxury. Not surprising, because this area is considered the most fashionable. There are a lot of manicured parks and beautiful gardens. The locals set themselves the goal of moving to Richmond, as this is a certain indicator of success. Tourists come here to enjoy the pristine natural beauty and well-groomed.

Richmond, having the full name of Richmond-on-Thames, is one of the suburbs of London. Moreover, the capital is located thirteen kilometers in the northeast. Richmond was founded in the 16th century during the foundation of the palace of the same name. Now about 22 thousand people live in the city.

Natural beauty is one of the main trump cards in this area. The city is divided into several parts:

  • Richmond Riverside, whose main square is located near the River Thames.
  • Richmond Green with the ancient Lane Palace.
  • Richmond Hill. Here is the park, which is the largest national park in England.

In this part of the capital there are a lot of different cinemas, theaters and other cultural entertainments. What else is London and its suburbs famous for? Well, of course, pubs and restaurants! If you love horseback riding, you can spend time in Richmond riding horses.

Brent

This suburb of London, located in the north-west of the capital, is known for the fact that it is here that the Wembley Stadium is located. It is mainly used for football matches, but concerts and charity events were held there at different times. Wembley is also the official venue for international football matches for the English teams and the FA Cup final, as well as the UEFA Champions League competitions for several years.

This huge stadium is the most expensive of such structures in the world, and accommodates about 100 thousand people.
