Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * (Hersonissos, Greece): Tourist reviews

Fans of exotic vacations abroad often choose Greece as the most suitable option. Many tour operators recommend visiting the city of Hersonissos, which is famous for its bustling nightlife and an abundance of entertainment places. This settlement will certainly become a godsend for young tourists who adore having fun.

One of the best hotels in Hersonissos is Thalia Hotel Crete 3 *, accommodation in which is relatively inexpensive, but for the money paid, guests receive maximum comfort and pleasure from the time spent.



Location

The hotel is located in Greece, on the island of Crete, in one of its largest cities - Hersonissos. Not far from it is the central part of the village, where there are a huge number of interesting places to visit. Near the hotel there are large transport hubs - the seaport (about 28 kilometers) and the international airport of Heraklion (25 km.).

Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * (Hersonissos) is located within walking distance from a major public transport stop - it is from here that you can get to anywhere in the city and see all the interesting sights, of which there are many.

Rooms

The hotel has a wide variety of apartments, which are divided into several categories depending on the level of comfort of living in them: "standard", "family", "junior suite" and "luxury".

In each of them, everything is decorated in accordance with design ideas and comfortable modern furniture is installed, which was updated during the overhaul in the building, which was produced in 2002.

Each room has its own private bathroom, which has a shower, sink and toilet. Some apartments (mainly "junior suite" and "deluxe") have individual air conditioning, with which guests can independently set their own climatic conditions. For a fee, guests at the Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * can use the phones.



The furniture here has the necessary number of beds in the form of beds with orthopedic mattresses, bedside tables, a wardrobe for a small amount of personal items, as well as a desk for work and a chair. All apartments have a small fridge and a plasma TV with satellite channels, including in Russian.

All apartments have their own private furnished balcony, on which there is a table and several comfortable seating areas. The windows of the suites and junior suites overlook the sea, while the rest overlook the interior of the hotel, and some overlook the mountains.



Entertainment and sports

Guests of this vacation spot never get bored, there is always something to do. In particular, if desired, tourists vacationing at Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * can always go in for sports in the large gym - all comfortable conditions are created for this, it also has shower cabins.

In the special massage room, guests can visit a professional massage therapist who is able to offer excellent relaxing treatments. Here you can also make a wrap. Several other cosmetic procedures are also offered.

In their free time, each guest on a free basis has the opportunity to play billiards, for which a special room with several tables has been created.



Pool

Guests of the Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * hotel (Greece / Hersonissos) can always swim in the local pool, which is located in its internal territory. Its capacity is always filled with crystal clear cool water, and at night the backlight works here. Around the pool there is a small sun terrace, where comfortable sun loungers, beach umbrellas are installed, if necessary, you can use air mattresses and beach towels for use.

Guests always have the opportunity to visit the bar, which is located near the pool - you can taste real Greek snacks in it, as well as drink cold drinks or bright unusual cocktails.



Nutrition

All tourists who rest in Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * (Greece) on a package tour, have the opportunity to eat in a local restaurant. A daily buffet is organized in its large hall with a huge assortment of dishes traditional for Greek and European cuisine. It serves a lot of desserts, pastries and snacks. There are also several dishes for vegetarians, in the preparation of which products of animal origin were not used.

There is a lobby bar for guests, which can be found on the ground floor of the building. It has free access to the Internet, which can be used by all residents of the hotel. Here you can order traditional Greek desserts, home-made ice cream, fruits, as well as various drinks, including alcoholic ones.

Beach

The coastal recreation area is literally 50 meters from the main building of the Thalia Hotel Crete 3 *. The coast is completely covered with sand and has an attractive picturesque view. From here you can admire not only the sea surface, but also the dense green vegetation that surrounds the beach and the hotel.

On the territory of this recreation area there are a large number of comfortable sun loungers on which tourists can stay for a fee, and guests are provided with umbrellas that protect against direct sunlight. There are several toilets, showers, and locker rooms on the beach square.

All tourists who are on the beach near the Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * hotel can take advantage of several entertainment options: go water-skiing or air-cushioning, rent a yacht for a while, where you can surf the sea, or master diving skills under the guidance of professional instructors and windsurfing.

Services

Residents of the Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * hotel (Greece / Hersonissos) can use a number of services that are provided by its staff and administration. In particular, for guests on the ground floor of the main building there is a 24-hour reception desk - reception, behind which there are several administrators. They can always give answers to various questions of guests related to living conditions or the provision of specific services.

If necessary, tourists can take their belongings to a laundry or dry cleaning service - this is done for a fee. Here you can also rent a safe for valuables. For a fee, babysitting services for a small child can be provided.

On the ground floor of the building there is a small shop where you can buy souvenirs for your near and dear ones.

As for the service, which is included in the price, daily cleaning is done in each room, towels are changed to fresh twice a week, and bed linen is changed three times within seven days.



Prices

The cost of living at the hotel is low, given the level of all the conditions provided. On average, a weekly stay for two - including meals, flights from Moscow to Greece and vice versa, as well as all the necessary insurance - costs about $ 400 for tourists. Children under the age of 8 stay in the rooms completely free of charge, and a cot for them is also provided at no charge.



Tourists reviews

Hotel guests often share their impressions of their stay at the Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * in reviews that they post on their pages on social networks or on thematic sites. Often they are accompanied by colorful photographs taken at the hotel, near the pool, on the sea coast or in other interesting places where excursions were made.

In their comments, guests often mention that each guest at Thalia Hotel Crete 3 * is surrounded by attention and care. Tourists also mention the magnificent interior, made in the best traditions of European hotels. The staff who work here are always friendly and do their job clearly.

Tourists often bring down the taste of dishes prepared in the local restaurant and lobby bar, calling them unusual. They also often say that in neighboring institutions you can taste a lot of delicious food and have a lot of fun.
