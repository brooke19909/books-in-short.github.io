Cordless landline phones with a SIM card: an overview of the best models and reviews

A cell phone in the traditional sense is a device that easily fits in your pocket and is suitable for moving over long distances while fully maintaining functionality in standalone mode. But there are so-called stationary cell phones. In appearance, they are more likely similar to the classic wired models, as well as in the main controls. They may be in demand, for example, by older people, for whom a cell phone in a modern format is unusual. Or, as an option, in the office, which for some reason is not connected to land lines. What are the main features of landline cell phones? What models of the corresponding type of devices are popular in the Russian market?

But if we talk about the most important characteristics of the device, then such modern experts single out the following list:

  • number of supported GSM frequencies;
  • sensitivity of the standard antenna;
  • the presence of an external SMA antenna;
  • display type, as well as its basic characteristics;
  • Battery capacity;
  • operating temperature range.

GSM support

As for the supported GSM frequencies, most of the stationary GSM phones available in the Russian market, one way or another, can work in the GSM 900/1800 standard. Other frequencies are rarely used by leading mobile providers. Therefore, with regard to this characteristic, almost any landline telephone with a SIM card sold in the Russian Federation will be practically guaranteed to meet the compatibility criteria with Russian GSM standards. But if the device is bought abroad, for example, in an online store or at an auction, then it makes sense to pay attention to what frequencies it supports.

Antenna Specifications

Regarding the sensitivity parameters of the receiving module, it is desirable that they be at least 100 dBm. It can be noted that in most cases, those models of devices that are common on the Russian market also meet this criterion. The next parameter is the availability of an external SMA antenna in the telephone set. Display and battery

The next significant aspect of choosing a stationary phone with a SIM card is the type of display. In most cases, to meet the basic needs of the user of the device of this type, an LCD display with a resolution of 128 x 64 or higher is sufficient. The optimal battery capacity of a stationary phone with a SIM card is 800 mAh. Of course, higher rates are always welcome.

Temperature range

Regarding the temperature range at which the manufacturer guarantees the stable operation of the device, landline phones with a SIM card of those models that are most popular in the Russian Federation are able, as a rule, to function in an environment from minus 10 to 55 degrees. However, again, if the device is bought outside the Russian Federation, it will be useful to double-check its corresponding characteristic - it is quite possible that the device will not be designed for use, for example, at sub-zero temperatures.
