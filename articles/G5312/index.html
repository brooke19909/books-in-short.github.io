Kemer hotels: rating, photos and reviews

Kemer is one of the most popular resorts on the Mediterranean coast. This beautiful corner is located southwest of Antalya, at the very foot of the Taurus Mountains. An incredible combination of greenery, sea and mountains annually attracts a huge number of tourists.
