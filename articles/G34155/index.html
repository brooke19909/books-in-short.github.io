Hotel Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 *: description, rooms and reviews of tourists

Spain is an ideal tourist destination. There are sandy beaches, and the picturesque sea, and amazing culture, and rich nightlife, and extremely cheap shopping. Thanks to the Schengen visa, tourists resting here can easily visit, for example, France. Rest here is a little more expensive than in Turkey or Bulgaria, popular with Russian tourists. If you want to save on vacation, then you should pay attention to the hotel Garbi Park ("Garby Park") 3 *. But is it worth it to relax here?

general description

For tourists, the hotel opened its doors in 1970. On its territory there is a five-story building, which housed 250 rooms. Since the Garbi Park Lloret Hotel is a three-star complex, you should not count on a luxurious reception. The rooms here are modest but comfortable and recently renovated. Their last restoration was carried out in 2013. The hotel staff does not speak Russian, but it understands English and French well. The complex provides accommodation for children of all ages, including infants. Unfortunately, pets are not allowed here even at an additional cost.





Check-in starts no earlier than noon. Tourists who arrived at the hotel in the morning can leave their belongings at the reception and go on vacation. You will have to leave the hotel at the end of your vacation between 7:00 and 10:00. However, subject to availability, guests can extend their stay. The minimum cost of living in a standard double room is about 2500 rubles per night. We accept Visa, Maestro and MasterCard credit cards.

Housing stock

Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 * hotel offers tourists to choose one of 248 rooms. Some of them are designed for single use, there are also family apartments. But most of the rooms are double. Settlement of the third guest is carried out only on condition that he is a child who is not more than 12 years old. All rooms are equipped with a small balcony on which there is a plastic dining set (table and two chairs). The windows of the hotel mainly overlook the streets of the city, but some of them have a view of the pool.





The design of rooms in the Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 * hotel (Spain) is simple, without frills and extra luxury. We can say that it is made in a minimalist style. The walls are painted in soothing colors (white, pale pink) and the floor is tiled. All rooms have a standard set of furniture: several beds, bedside tables, wardrobes, a desk and a mirror. The rooms are decorated with small paintings and elegant wall lights. The apartments have a small bathroom. It has a shower, washbasin, mirror and towels.

Room Amenities

In addition to the standard set of furniture, each room is equipped with a host of amenities that greatly simplify relaxation. The employees of the Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 * daily clean up the rooms, take out the trash and make beds. Sheets and towels are regularly changed. For a comfortable stay, all apartments have the following equipment:

  • Safe for storing valuables, money and documents. It also comes with instructions for use. Payment for use is charged daily.
  • A small tv. The model is old, non-flat. Connected to satellite TV, there is one Russian channel.
  • Radio. Only Spanish radio stations work.
  • Individual air conditioning. You can use it from June to September. Guests can adjust the cooling level themselves. Connects for an additional fee.
  • Central heating. It is included during frosts, as well as in autumn and winter.
  • Telephone for communication with the reception. Other calls will have to be paid separately.
  • Wi-Fi. Paid by the day. A hairdryer is available upon request. For using it they will have to pay a deposit (10 euros).

Service for guests

Being a three-star hotel, the complex Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 * (Costa Brava) has a slightly developed network of infrastructure facilities. But there is everything you need for a comfortable stay for guests. Staying here, tourists can use the following services:

  • Private parking where travelers can park their car. Located near the hotel. It is under constant guard. Price per day is 9 euros. There is also public parking. Here you can park your car for free.
  • Tour agency. Guides will help you book sightseeing tours in the vicinity of Lloret de Mar or arrange a trip to Barcelona and other major cities in Spain.
  • Reception, at which the administrator is present around the clock. He will help with solving problems, as well as answer all your questions.
  • Car rental Tourists over 21 years old can rent a car for independent travel around the country. Mandatory international driver's license. Gas is paid separately.
  • Currency exchange point.
  • Free wifi in public areas. For an additional fee, you can rent a modem.
  • Wheelchair ramps. They are equipped with all the stairs in the hotel. There is also an elevator in the main building.




All about entertainment

The entertainment that Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 * offers is more suitable for outdoor activities. There are few of them, because most tourists prefer to look for entertainment in the city. There are many nightclubs and sports grounds in the area. A simple walk around the city is a great way to spend time. Picturesque streets will delight travelers with numerous architectural monuments. For guests who want to have fun directly on the hotel territory, the following leisure options are presented:

  • Outdoor swimming pool with fresh water. All sun loungers, umbrellas, towels and mattresses are provided for a fee.
  • Indoor heated pool where tourists can swim in bad weather.
  • Equipment for playing table tennis.
  • Billiard tables. Their use is not included in the price and is paid separately.
  • Own solarium. Aerobics and water gymnastics lessons.
  • Water sports: scooter, canoe, banana, yacht charter. They can be enjoyed visiting the public beach.
  • In the evenings, an entertainment program is held by the pool, which tells tourists about Spain. Here you can listen to live music, dance or take flamenco lessons.

Children's holiday

Arriving on vacation with children at the Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 * hotel, it is worth considering that it is in a noisy place. There are many nightclubs everywhere, from which loud music and cries of youth can be heard. Nevertheless, quite a few families with small children stay at the hotel. For the youngest guests, the hotel is ready to make a good discount on accommodation. And for babies under the age of two years and do not have to pay. In addition, a free baby cot is available on request.

Entertainment for children is not so much. For them there is no separate pool, so you have to bathe them under the supervision of an adult or not to let the kids go to the water. But hotel guests can visit the water park, which is located in one of the neighboring hotels. Guests of the complex receive a good discount on visits. There is also a mini club at the hotel. There is no playground, no animation, so parents will have to independently organize their children's leisure.

Opinions of tourists: advantages

All tourists before issuing vouchers prefer to get acquainted with the opinions of other vacationers. Hotel Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 * gets different reviews, but most of them are still positive. They note the following advantages of the complex:

  • Good location. Nearby is a bus station, to the sea to go less than 10 minutes.
  • Very clean rooms that are well tidy. The bathroom is equipped with a new, perfectly white bath.
  • Friendly staff who, however, do not speak Russian at all.
  • For dinner, a very large selection of various dishes is offered.
  • You do not need to hand over keys at the reception every time you leave the hotel.
  • You can bring food and drinks purchased in the store to your room.




Opinions of tourists: disadvantages

But the guests who rested at Garbi Park Lloret Hotel 3 *, leave negative reviews. In their opinion, the most significant disadvantages are:

  • Poor soundproofing in the rooms. You can even hear the usual conversations of neighbors.
  • The noise from the street. It is worth taking a room overlooking the pool, it is usually much quieter there.
  • Poor and monotonous breakfasts.
  • The hotel has a lot of young people who make noise day and night. And since the soundproofing in the hotel is terrible, it is impossible to fall asleep from their screams.
  • There are a lot of people on the beach, among whom you can meet nudists.

Draw conclusions

After reading the description of the Garbi Park 3 * hotel and reviews about it, we can conclude that this is a good complex for the price it offers. Particular attention should be paid to young people and people who love outdoor activities. Families with children and older couples should look for another place, otherwise the vacation can be spoiled by noisy neighbors or drunk teenagers who walk at night on the streets of the city.
