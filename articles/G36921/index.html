To make such a rose, prepare the following materials and tools:

  • Viscose yarn of 2 similar shades - dark and light.
  • Thread with a needle.

After the knitting of the strip is completed, it will need to be rolled up with a spiral and fix the flower with a hook or thread with a needle. Rose is ready.

Knit slippers

And you can crochet bright warm and soft slippers. The description given in the article is suitable for size 37, buttons are used for decoration.

The sole begins with a chain of twenty air loops and goes in a circle. We knit 9 rows with single crochet, making 5 - 6 loops on the ends of the increase.

In the tenth and eleventh rows we also knit single crochet without adding.

From the 12th to the 16th row, we knit the main fabric with single crochet, reducing four loops in the middle of the sock.

For the jumper, we will make a chain of fifteen air loops, on it we knit several rows with columns without a crochet.

It remains to sew it to the slipper and decorate it with buttons. Well, comfortable crocheted slippers.

Crocheting a washcloth

Let's try to make another accessory useful in everyday life. Crocheting for the home includes not only the manufacture of interior items, but also such an important trifle as bath and shower accessories - washcloths.

It will take quite a bit of sisal twine. It is perfect for making washcloths for peeling. These are used by professionals in beauty salons, but they are not cheap. A hook for making such a washcloth will need a thick one, about 11 to 12.

To get started, dial 65 air loops and close the ring with a half-column. Next, knit to the desired length with columns without crochets in a circle. If you need a more ribbed and roughened washcloth, then alternate single crochet with double crochet. To make handles on both sides, tie chains of seventy air loops and tie them with one row of double crochets. Your hard peeling washcloth is ready.
