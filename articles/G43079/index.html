Here between the groups are placed "stars" for ease of study. In contracts, forms, receipts and payments, the sequence is recorded without intermediate characters. Specify:

NNN - cells 1, 2, 3; balance account number I;

MM - characters 4, 5; balance account number II;

XXXXX - characters from 16 to 20; front, internal banking reference.

It does not matter in which bank the payee or payer opened a bank account: there are always twenty digits.

Internal set

The reader now knows how many digits are in the current account (twenty). It remains to consider the last seven digits of the set.

SS - ZZZZZ is the personal number inside the bank.

What is a personal account in the appendix to the decoding of the studied concept? This is a seven-digit group in categories 14 - 20. It is used to account for mutual payment transactions with citizens and institutions. It reflects credit and cash transactions with a specific client.

Two SS characters encrypt the type of personal account: on demand - ruble, currency, accumulative.

ZZZZZ is the defining sequence. The combination does not carry characterizing information. This is an intra-system serial number.

In 2017, the Central Bank of the Russian Federation issued Regulation No. 579-P such that the personal account differs in numbering for budgetary, transit, reporting financial results and all other financial products.

In the structure of the budget bank account, the symbol of budget reporting takes cells from 14 to 16. The index consists of four characters in cells from the seventeenth to the twentieth.

The transit account is marked with the number ‚Äú1‚Äù in box fourteen; characters from 15 to 20 - serial number.

Symbols about OFR occupy categories 14 - 18, and for local accounting cells 19 - 20 are provided.

Single picking rule

A unified approach to identification of a personal number does not exist. Each KFU has its own layout for the SS and ZZZZ groups.

The current account in Sberbank contains twenty digits, and digits 13 and 14 for the ruble object are assigned the value 00.

Conclusion 4. In example 13 and 14 given at the beginning of the article, the cells have the value 00, which means that the account is in rubles on demand, not deposit and not accumulative.

The current account at VTB consists of twenty characters. It is formed according to the same rules and instructions as other Russian banks. The only difference is the front number, digits from 14 to 20. Each KFU tries to protect the information and comes up with its own encoding scheme.
