Ski base "Nikolaevskaya Sopka" in Krasnoyarsk

The ski resort "Nikolaevskaya Sopka" in Krasnoyarsk is very popular among residents and visitors of the city in the winter season. Many outdoor enthusiasts prefer this place. Indeed, its advantages include a convenient location, equipped slopes and excellent material and technical base. All this causes interest in skiing in both children and adults.

About base

The recreation center "Nikolaevskaya Sopka" is located in the Oktyabrsky district of Krasnoyarsk. This place is popular at any time of the year. In summer, you can go mountain biking or just go on a picnic. On the territory you can rent a barbecue and other equipment for outdoor recreation. In winter, the ski resort begins to operate. During this period, especially many people flock here who want to go skiing, snowboarding or buns. For hungry holidaymakers, there is always the Highlander Cafe, where you can have a tasty snack in the fresh air and warm yourself with hot tea or coffee.

In the winter season, three ski slopes open on the basis of Nikolaevskaya Sopka in Krasnoyarsk. Both beginners and professionals can ride here. Experienced instructors work in the territory, ready to teach both children and adults all the subtleties of skiing. Also in winter there is a children's ski school. Teenagers learn downhill skiing, snowboarding and ski jumping.

Residents and guests of the city can come here in the evening, the tracks are always lit. For those who want to spend the night on the highway, there is a hotel with cozy double rooms and amenities on the floor. Guests of the city can stay in other pensions and hotels with different levels of prices and service.

Infrastructure

For the convenience of visitors, the Nikolaev infrastructure has all the necessary infrastructure for a good rest:

  • equipped ski slopes;
  • lifts;
  • Cafe;
  • first-aid post;
  • hotel;
  • equipment rental center;
  • training center;
  • parking for cars.

Trails and lifts

Three tracks operate on the territory of the base: the longest - 800 meters, and two 400 meters each. The height difference is 250 m. A separate slope is equipped for riding on the "buns", there are tracks for the youngest visitors.

You can get to the very top on a cable lift, short tracks are served by a tow-type lift.

Location and opening hours

Residents and guests of the city can find the ski resort "Nikolaevskaya Sopka" in Krasnoyarsk at 85a Svobodny Avenue. You can get here by city buses: No. 12 and No. 42.

On weekdays, the territory is open from 18.30 to 22.00, on weekends and holidays from 12.00 to 22.00.

The benefits of skiing

Like most sports, skiing brings many benefits to the body. In winter, this is one of the most affordable types of outdoor activities. In addition to a good mood, riding heals the body:

  • immunity is strengthened;
  • the cardiovascular system improves;
  • the body is saturated with oxygen;
  • has a beneficial effect on the nervous system;
  • pressure comes to the right standards;
  • muscle tone is trained;
  • there is a surge of strength and energy;
  • vitality increases;
  • mood improves.

How to dress for a ski trip

For skiing, a not very thick jacket and a warm sweater are best suited, you need to wear thermal underwear under it. Due to the large amount of clothing you can sweat and immediately catch a cold. This is worth considering. It is best to wear a sports hat on your head, and warm socks and comfortable shoes on your feet. It is important that the shoes are not too big or too tight.

"Nikolaev hill" in Krasnoyarsk is glad to visitors at any time of the year. Outdoor activities - the key to health and good mood.
