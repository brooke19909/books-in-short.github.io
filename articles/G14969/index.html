Crochet hat: schemes, description, patterns, master classes

Crocheting a hat has several important advantages.

At any stage of work, the product is easy to try on and correct defects. The calculation of loops and rows is quite simple.

The round knitting technique allows you to do a thing without a seam and with a smooth crown. So the hat looks neat.

You can make a knitted fabric of any density. It is almost not deformed, comfortable to wear and care.

There are many patterns and techniques that allow you to create fantasy models for any season and for every taste. Funny children's hats and elegant women's hats are crocheted. Therefore, crocheted hats are always popular.

Here is a selection of models for beginners and experienced craftswomen: children's and women's hats for all seasons, a detailed description and fresh ideas.

Round hat for beginners

Such a simple model is a real find for beginners. Crocheting a hat of an adjacent, round shape begins with the crown. To make the top of the cap smooth, you need to dial six air loops, lock them into a ring and knit with double crochets, evenly adding twelve loops in each row. Get a circle of twelve wedges. For crochet with crochet, six loops in each row must be added. The circle will be divided into six parts.

The diameter of the crown is calculated simply: head circumference / 3.14 (this is the number of Pi). For simplicity, you can divide exactly three, the error is small. The main thing to understand is that this is the optimal width of the product. If you incorrectly calculate the diameter of the head, the cap will either not be worn or will slide into your eyes. In this model, it is important that the thing neatly rests on the head. When the crown is ready, you need to continue knitting without adding until the crown reaches the desired depth. In the process, the product is tried on several times so as not to make a mistake with the size. You can decorate a crocheted hat to your liking with any suitable accessories.

How to knit a hat without a seam

The junction of the rows with circular knitting forms a strip of large holes, with a noticeable slope to the right. It is especially clearly visible on a smooth canvas, connected by columns with a crochet, as in the previous example. Even in the tightest knit, the lifting air loops form a noticeable seam, giving the product an untidy look. You can make the seam more accurate as follows. At the beginning of each row, an additional column is tied from the base of the lifting air loops. At the end of the row, the connecting air loop is tied into this additional column, and not into the lifting loops. The seam will be smooth and without large holes.

To ensure that there is no seam at all, use the method of "spiral knitting". From the initial ring, the product is knitted in one continuous row, without lifting loops. Additions are made according to the drawing. It is very important not to lose count and not lose the beginning of the series. Each turn should be marked with a pin or a special convenient lock, moving the marker from row to row. To align the edge of the product, the last columns are knitted in decreasing order: with a crochet, a half-column and without a crochet. As a result, on the back of the head, the crocheted hat will look as neat as in the front.

Cap for boy

Work begins with a round, flat crown of twelve wedges connected by double crochets. When you get a circle of the right size, in the back of the cap, the addition is stopped. The front three wedges continue to add evenly. Three to four rows can be knitted depending on the length of the visor. In the next four rows they also uniformly make a reduction in the front wedges. So the rim of the workpiece again will correspond to the girth of the head.

The visor can be made soft or put inside the liner to give shape. The part can be knitted separately or continued directly on the rim. The number of loops corresponding to the front wedges is reduced to 30 in four rows. They add it again so that the double part is symmetrical. The visor is stitched at the base and sides. With a hidden seam, it is attached to the tulle so that the cap does not tear apart during active games. The rest of the rim is tied with two rows of single crochet for greater rigidity.

If you knit this model of thin cotton threads, you get a great summer accessory.

Helmet for boy

Crochet children's hats for boys can be decorated no less spectacularly than for girls. A simple adjacent model with a round top turns into an original design thing, if you add a couple of embossed details to it.

The crest on the crown is a straight double rectangle. To keep the part in the desired position, they begin to knit it directly from the cap. To do this, from the forehead to the back of the head, make a mark with a contrasting thread. Double crochet on both sides of it. It is enough to knit three rows. At the top, both sides of the ridge are connected. If you want to make the comb taller, you can complement it with a plume of bright lush yarn.

For a visor, a rhombus is tied. Its short diagonal is equal to the distance from the nose to the chin. The long diagonal is equal to the distance from the earlobe of one ear to the earlobe of the other. Slots for buttons are tied at the sharp ends, with which the visor is attached to the temples. The slots should be sized so that the part moves freely on the forehead and back.

There are two ways to decorate a visor with black stripes of slots. Immediately knitting the fabric with alternating gray and black rows, or embroidering with black thread on the finished part.
