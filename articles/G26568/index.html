Hotel Kleopatra Fatih Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists

Alanya is one of the most popular resort cities in Turkey. It is here that every year a huge number of tourists come from all over the world. After all, the coast of Antalya is picturesque beaches, amazing nature, a lot of historical and cultural attractions, as well as hundreds of hotels, including the hotel complex Kleopatra Fatih Hotel.

Naturally, when planning, the traveler is interested in any additional information, because the quality of the vacation largely depends on the right choice of hotel. It is important, for example, the location of the hotel and the distance to the sea, because it will have to be overcome on a daily basis. In addition, the quality of accommodation, the food plan, the availability of amenities on the territory and many other factors should be taken into account. So what is the hotel Kleopatra Fatih Hotel 3 *? What impressions do tourists who have already managed to visit here share?

Location Features





Location is an important point for almost every traveler. It is worth saying right away that the location of the Kleopatra Fatih Hotel is really convenient - the distance to the beach is only 100 meters (you just have to cross the road).

The busy center of the big city of Alanya is located just one kilometer from the hotel - this distance is easy to overcome on foot. Nearby there are many shops, cafes and entertainment venues. Most tourists arrive at Antalya International Airport - the distance to it is quite large and is 150 km. Travel agencies, of course, provide their customers with a shuttle service, but you should prepare for a long trip.

Hotel Kleopatra Fatih Hotel: description of the hotel and its infrastructure

This is a medium-sized hotel complex located in a quiet resort area. It consists of a large seven-story building, built in a modern style. By the way, the internal design is also pleasant here, the elevator system is constantly working.

The territory is small (the area is 2 thousand square meters), but very well-groomed and beautiful. There are palm trees, flowering bushes, bright flower beds, secluded grottoes, as well as a pool and terraces for relaxation. By the way, the hotel was opened in 1993, and the last thorough restoration was carried out in 2011.

What categories of rooms does the hotel have?

The hotel complex Kleopatra Fatih Hotel has at its disposal 70 rooms. For the most part, these are standard double rooms with a private balcony. There are several rooms with a small area - they are designed for one person.

It also offers more spacious family rooms, where there is a well-equipped living room with upholstered furniture and a sofa bed. When booking a room, a consultant will certainly help you find the right room.

Hotel Kleopatra Fatih Hotel: photo and general description of rooms





Of course, potential guests are interested in the living conditions that the hotel offers. According to reviews, the rooms here are quite spacious, and the view from the window opens beautiful. Guests can count on a selection of furniture, including large, comfortable beds that are literally designed for a relaxing break and a good night's sleep.

Of course, the rooms are equipped with household appliances. You can watch something interesting on TV at any time (there are even several Russian-language channels). There is also access to the Internet, which is important for almost every modern traveler.

A powerful air conditioning system with a control panel will help to create pleasant temperature conditions in the room, which is especially important during the summer heat. There is a telephone from which you can make calls directly. There is a minibar, it is equipped with a cooling system, but it is filled only with drinking water - all other drinks are brought at an additional cost. Also, guests can use the safe for storing valuables and documents - it can be rented for a small price.

There is a bathroom with a spacious shower and modern fixtures. Each guest can count on clean towels, hygiene products, as well as a hair dryer (stationary).

Reviews confirm that the cleaning is really carried out every day, and the staff work well. Bed linen is changed three times a week, so you can sleep on clean, fresh sheets.

Meals: scheme for tourists





Good food is an integral part of a good vacation. So what should the Kleopatra Fatih Hotel 3 count on? Reviews say that the cuisine here is really good. By the way, when paying for your stay, you yourself can choose a convenient meal plan - it can be either "all inclusive" or "half board" (breakfast + dinner).

The main meals take place in the main restaurant in the form of a buffet. There are a sufficient number of tables and chairs, and the waiters are polite and courteous. The menu is quite varied - there are always fresh fruits and vegetables on the table, as well as several types of side dishes, meat dishes (sometimes fish), as well as light snacks and desserts. In addition, guests are offered a wide selection of drinks, including aromatic Turkish coffee.

There is also a bar with a large selection of drinks, including strong drinks, though locally produced (beer, several wines, crayfish, etc.). Naturally, you can have a snack on the territory of the city - there are a huge number of cafes, pizzerias and restaurants for every taste and budget.

Convenience and entertainment by the sea





Is it possible to count on a beach holiday for a guest at the Kleopatra Fatih Hotel? Turkey, in particular Alanya, is famous for its incredible beaches. One of them is located very close to the hotel, across the road - the distance is about 100 m.

It is worth saying right away that this is a public stretch of the coast, which means that rental of umbrellas and deck chairs is paid separately. Tourists say that the coast is cleaned regularly. The entrance to the sea is convenient, there is a shallow area.

Naturally, fans of outdoor activities will also find something to do. On the beach you can play volleyball. Travelers love to snorkel. You will have the opportunity to ride a boat and water ski. They also engage in sailing and windsurfing. There are several diving schools where beginners can take an express training course and make the first dive.

Description of additional service





The Kleopatra Fatih Hotel has all the conditions for guests to relax easily and naturally, taking advantage of all the benefits of civilization. For example, a store operates here, where you can purchase various household trifles. There is also a laundry room where you can rent clothes for cleaning.

At the reception you can rent a safe - here your things will be completely safe. Also, throughout the territory there is constant access to the Internet, and, according to reviews, the connection speed here is not bad. The hotel has a medical center where every traveler can get expert help in case of health problems.

There is also ample parking, where guests can leave their own or rented transport (by the way, renting a car in the city will not be difficult if you have documents). The hotel has a spacious conference room designed for various business events and celebrations.

How to have fun at the hotel?





Most tourists arriving in the city rely on a fun beach holiday. On the other hand, you can have a good time at the hotel. There is a spacious pool with a terrace, on which there are new sun loungers and wide umbrellas. By the way, in the daytime guests can have fun on the water slides - there are several of them. In cold weather, you can swim in the indoor pool with a heating system.

You can spend time with friends playing a game of table tennis. There is also a billiard room. You can improve your health and just relax in the bathhouse. By the way, there are excellent massage therapists who offer wellness treatments. It features a spacious jacuzzi. And for tourists who prefer to keep fit everywhere and always, a gym is equipped with all the necessary equipment and several talented instructors.

Are there any conditions for a vacation with a child?

Comfort is what parents who are traveling with their child are primarily interested in. So what can the Kleopatra Fatih Hotel offer? Reviews of tourists say that the hotel is suitable for a family vacation.

Naturally, you can count on some amenities. For example, a folding bed is delivered to the room, and in the restaurant you can choose tasty and healthy food for the baby. By the way, the hotel has a system of discounts for children.

A huge amount of entertainment for a child of any age can be found in the city. But the kid can have a good time at the hotel. For example, there is a special children's pool with a shallow depth and warm water. Older children have a great time on a water slide with their parents. And you can have fun on a large playground with slides, swings, sandboxes and other attractions.

What do travelers say about the hotel?





How do tourists actually spend time at the Kleopatra Fatih Hotel (Alanya)? Reviews say that here is a good place to spend a vacation on the shores of the warm sea. The hotel territory is well-groomed and clean, and the rooms are quite comfortable - it is clean, the furniture is comfortable, the appliances are in good condition, and there is hot water around the clock.

As for food, for some guests it seems a little monotonous. On the other hand, there is always a choice, the food is fresh and tasty, so hardly anyone will remain hungry. By the way, the staff here are very nice and helpful. Despite the fact that many workers do not understand the Russian language, they are always ready to help resolve certain problems.

Travelers recommend the Kleopatra Fatih Hotel hotel complex for a relaxing, enjoyable and affordable vacation by the sea.
