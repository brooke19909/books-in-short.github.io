Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): overview, description and reviews

Thailand is a popular tourist destination. Hundreds of thousands of Russians relax here every year. The secret of popularity is extremely simple - Thailand is great for a beach holiday. The season here lasts from November to April, respectively, those who did not have time to relax in the summer, can always splash in the warm gentle sea and get a beautiful even tan in the middle of winter. A good excursion program is organized here: tourists have to go on a real exotic trip, ride elephants, meet other representatives of the fauna, visit the paradise islands and enjoy the views of the landscape. Phuket Island - a paradise, washed by the waters of the Andaman Sea. Stunning nature, excellent cuisine, ample opportunities for leisure activities attract many Russians. Quite often here you can meet those tourists who have always rested in Pattaya, but are tired of the hustle and bustle and decided to enjoy the tranquility, expect a high level of service. But, going to Thailand, it is necessary to clearly understand that not all hotels correspond to their description, which is why even at the stage of selection you should take a closer look at the reviews, be sure to get acquainted with the reviews of real vacationers. Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * managed to gain a good rating on tourist portals. What living conditions can he offer to modern tourists? Is he worthy of the attention of potential vacationers? This is what we will talk in more detail about.





Location

So, Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * is located in the resort center, in the Patong Beach area. It is surrounded by infrastructure: numerous bars and restaurants, large shopping centers and souvenir shops. Bangla Road night entertainment street is just a 10-15 minute walk at a steady pace.

The hotel is quite calm, the noise from the street does not interfere at night. That is why so often Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * hotel is chosen by couples for their holidays. But this resort area can hardly be called budgetary: the prices in the cafe are just cosmic, and entertainment on the beach here is far from budgetary. All those who have already rested in Pattaya and decided to spend their holidays in Phuket should understand that the cost of rest here is 15-20% higher.





Population

When checking into the hotel room Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * (Thailand, Phuket, Patong) all vacationers will have to leave a deposit of 2,000 baht. You can exchange money at the nearest point located across the road. Having a deposit is a common condition for Thailand, so the hotel administration wants to protect itself from not the most accurate guests. That is why it is so important to check the number when checking in, especially the serviceability of equipment and plumbing. Otherwise, the hotel may present you with an invoice for damage caused upon eviction.

Some tourists note a confusion with the rooms: guests are provided with rooms without a balcony, overlooking the courtyard. So, if within the framework of your tour you purchased apartments with a balcony and a view of the coast, and you were given the keys to the room without a balcony, you must immediately go down to the front desk and fix the problem within 5 minutes.

For access to the room they issue a plastic card - just one. If you need a second card, you will have to pay 300 baht for it. Check-out time is 12:00, but even if tourists arrive a little earlier than the specified time, if there is a free room they will be settled within 5-10 minutes. In the worst case, you can always leave things at the reception and go to the sea.

Room service

Rooms at the Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * hotel are cleaned daily. Cleaning includes garbage disposal, changing bath towels, mopping and cleaning plumbing, replenishing shampoo and shower gel in the bathroom. Cleaning takes place during the absence of tourists. Upon returning to your room you can enjoy the atmosphere of cleanliness and freshness. Every day, tourists are given 0.25 liters of water.

Nutrition

Like most other hotels in Thailand, all guests are offered breakfast at Tuana Patong Holiday 3 *. Reviews of tourists note that the menu is very monotonous, already on the third day of rest I want something new. Most often, breakfast includes fried noodles and rice, toasts with jam, boiled eggs, soup, sausages, drinks of your choice, fresh vegetables and fruits. Tourists who have already spent their holidays at Tuana Patong Holiday Hotel 3 * complain about the lack of fresh tropical fruits. Moreover, it is advisable to arrive at breakfast early, at about 8 in the morning, to catch dishes warm.





Entertainment on site

Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * (Thailand, Phuket, Patong) offers all vacationers a nice pool. But even in the evening, almost no one is resting here. The territory is very tiny, because the hotel itself is an urban type. It is intended solely for sleeping, resting and receiving hygiene procedures. Do not rely on animation, the presence of an entertainment program for tourists.

At the information desk you can get acquainted with the excursion program of the resort, purchase the most interesting direction for yourself.

Service

The reception is open around the clock, due to which vacationers at any time can contact the hotel for all matters of interest to them. Here you can use the safe for storing documents and valuables. Beach

Patong Beach is most often visited by guests of the Tuana Patong Holiday Hotel 3 * hotel. Reviews of tourists note that this is the busiest and rave place of the resort and, unfortunately, far from the cleanest. This is the most popular beach in Phuket, located within walking distance from all infrastructure facilities. During the day there are quite a lot of holidaymakers, equipment rental for sports and water activities is open. In general, during the day the beach is not much different from the others, with the exception of garbage, but in the evening and at night it becomes a platform for discos and exciting show programs.

The beach stretches for 3 km, the entire coast is lined with sun loungers and umbrellas, the daily rental of which is in the region of 300 baht. Due to the huge accumulation of budget hotels of categories 3 and 4 stars along this coast, there are always a lot of vacationers, and therefore quite dirty. But Patong Beach is uniquely cleaner and more comfortable than any Pattaya beach.

If you are looking for a calmer place for your vacation, you can go to the paradise islands - from the island of Phuket it is much easier to do than from the mainland. You can stroll to Freedom beach with crystal clear sea water and white sand.





To summarize

Hotel Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * (Phuket) is a mid-range hotel, not without some drawbacks. If you travel to Phuket in order to get acquainted with nature, enjoy the taste and aroma of tropical fruits, immerse yourself in an exotic atmosphere, you are not going to spend all your free time at the hotel without hesitation to book a room here.

The hotel is suitable for a relaxing family vacation, it is notable for a good location relative to infrastructure facilities, and therefore attracts tourists from different parts of the globe. If you are looking for a budget hotel for your holidays in Thailand, check out this option.
