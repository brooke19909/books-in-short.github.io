Begonville Hotel (Turkey / Marmaris / Marmaris Center): hotel description and reviews

For almost a couple of decades, Begonville Beach Hotel has been offering tourists from around the world excellent accommodation conditions that will appeal to many. Tours to it can be purchased at many agencies, and their cost, as a rule, pleasantly surprises many.

general information

Begonville Hotel was built in 1998, since then its owners continue to maintain its original appearance through regular repairs and refurbishment. One of the last was carried out in 2012. At this time, the interior of the apartment was updated, some furniture was replaced and a more modern climate system was installed in each room.

Today the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is almost 4000 square meters. m. There are two small four-story buildings, as well as recreation areas, provided for guests of different age categories.



Location

Begonville Hotel is located in a very beautiful picturesque place, covered with greenery, blooming tropical colors. It is located almost in the central part of the city of Marmaris, which belongs to the largest resort region of Turkey. Not far from it there is a considerable number of entertainment places and sites designed for sports.

The nearest airport is Dalaman, a hundred kilometers from the Begonville Hotel. It is in connection with this range of location that each ticket to the hotel contains in its package the included cost of the transfer service. As tourists say in their reviews of Begonville Hotel, this service is provided by convenient machines that are equipped with air conditioning - this allows you to get from the point of arrival to the destination as soon as possible and at the optimum temperature in the cabin.

Rooms

The entire fund of apartments, which is in the hotel, is located in its two buildings. Their total number is 104, they are divided into three different categories: standard, family and suite. In each room, regardless of the level of comfort, there is air conditioning, with the help of which optimal climatic conditions for living are created.

Also, all apartments are equipped with a separate bathroom, combined with a bathroom. They have a shower or bathtub, as well as a washbasin. At the entrance, guests receive a set of bath soap supplies for maintaining hygiene, bathrobes, towels and slippers.



Standard

The Begonville Hotel has 76 standard rooms. Each of them is located on an area of ‚Äã‚Äã32 square meters. m and consists of one room, which is designed for a maximum of three guests.

The room has one large double bed, which in some apartments is replaced by a pair of single beds. If necessary, an additional resting place in the form of a folding bed of a European type can be installed here. Near the beds there are side tables on which guests can store small items. A wardrobe is provided for clothes.

Near the large window in the room there is a relaxation area in the form of a pair of soft chairs and a round table with a low leg. While staying in standard apartments, guests can watch their favorite TV shows.

Family

A small number of rooms (11) are arranged in such a way that it is very comfortable for more guests to live in them - up to four people. Tourists themselves in their comments note that they are ideal for both relaxing with friends and for a trip with the whole family. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãsuch apartments is about 44 square meters. m, and it is divided into three small rooms, two of which are bedrooms, in the third a small kitchen is organized.

The bedrooms have a standard furniture set, similar to what is standard in the rooms. The balconies that are available in this type of room offer excellent views of the city and the sea.

The presence of this type of kitchen in the rooms, according to most guests, is an excellent solution. Everyone who is relaxing at the Begonville Hotel (Marmaris) with children will certainly stay in such apartments, since in the kitchen you can prepare tasty, healthy food for your child, and, most importantly, it will be fresh. This section of the apartment is equipped with countertops, as well as a small amount of household appliances. There is an electric kettle, stove, as well as a small set of dishes for up to 4 people.



Suite

The rooms of the suites, of which there are only 17 in the Begonville Beach Hotel (Marmaris), are provided with four-person superior conditions. Such apartments have personal access to a large open balcony, where a small amount of furniture is installed: a pair of armchairs and a table for tea drinking. Guests relaxing in these apartments recommend sunbathing, reading their favorite literature, and admiring the beautiful surroundings.

Inside, the room is divided into two separate bedrooms, as well as one kitchen. For the installation of such apartments used the latest furniture. The beds in the bedrooms are covered with orthopedic mattresses; they have quite expensive linens that have hypoallergenic properties. Here in each bedroom there is a plasma TV connected to the satellite broadcasting system.

Nutrition

All tourists staying at the Begonville Apart Hotel have the opportunity to eat in his restaurant as part of the all-inclusive concept. A daily buffet is served here, serving the most exquisite dishes. According to gourmets, they are quite nutritious and tasty. As a rule, they are all prepared in the tradition of Turkish cuisine.

In addition to a large restaurant, there is another large catering establishment on site, which can offer Mediterranean dishes. Guests of the Begonville Hotel (Marmaris) in their reviews recommend visiting it without fail, since here you can taste the most delicious seafood prepared according to company recipes.

In addition to two restaurants, the hotel has two bars, which can offer not only a wide range of alcohol, but also soft drinks. If desired, guests can order desserts that are distinguished by their originality, and snacks.



Sports and spa

Reviews of Begonville Hotel (Marmaris) often mention wonderful vacation destinations that are incredibly popular with guests. One of them is a thermal zone, the second is a massage room and spa, and the third is a fitness room, where all fans of an active lifestyle gather. By the way, visiting the hall, unlike other places, is completely free.

According to vacationers, true professionals work in the spa center, after visiting the procedures of which clients will certainly feel the improvement of their skin and the body as a whole. If desired, guests can choose a set of procedures that are suitable for individual needs. The most popular procedures here are body wraps, masking and peeling of individual parts of the body.

Also, the guests address a lot of positive comments towards the work of massage therapists who carry out their activities in a separate room. Vacationers say that local craftsmen can completely immerse the client in a state of complete relaxation. The atmosphere and interior reigning in the hall also contribute to this.



Aquazone

The hotel has a beautiful aquazone, which is located in the open. It consists of a large sun terrace and two swimming pools, one of which has original decor - a small waterfall. Around this resting place are green gardens, which are periodically covered with bright flowering - this undoubtedly attracts the attention of tourists. It also has a small round pool designed for bathing children. It is designed to meet all safety requirements.

On the sunny terrace there is a considerable number of sun loungers on which vacationers can sit for free. Many admit that during their stay in this place, under a folding umbrella, they read books or simply drank the cocktail purchased at the bar.



Beach

The hotel is not located on the first coastline, but 200 meters from it. He does not have his own territory on the beach, so guests are forced to visit the municipal territory for recreation, which, they say, is well arranged for a pleasant pastime.

Here you can always rent sun loungers and umbrellas; tourists recommend bringing towels from the hotel, as they are not rented. Staying in the sandy territory, you can have fun in various ways - there are conditions for this. If you wish, it is possible to rent a yacht - on it many recommend going with your loved one on a short trip. Also, near the shore, you can go water skiing or dive to scuba diving, but for this you need to hire an instructor.



Hotel Facilities

All tourists who are in the territory of Begonville Hotel, can use a number of services which are provided in addition. In particular, these include those that are dry cleaned and laundry. Here you can rent vehicles, including cars and bicycles, which travelers recommend going to explore the surroundings. Many guests admit that in this way they go to seek entertainment in the central part of Marmaris. Here they recommend visiting nightclubs, karaoke and restaurants.

All guests who arrived at the hotel as part of a business visit can use the conference room rental service. It has a small area and is well equipped for meetings with business partners, presentations and other business events.

Throughout the entire area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel, tourists can enjoy free access to the Internet, to which most vacationers have a positive attitude.
