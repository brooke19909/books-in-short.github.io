How to make an origami paper boat for children: step by step instructions

How to make origami boat out of paper? For children, as well as their parents, there is a detailed step-by-step instruction. Familiar to everyone since childhood and probably the simplest origami "paper boat" can be run in the bathroom, puddle, lake, as well as arrange boat races with friends.





Paper origami for children: boat

The paper boat layout is quite simple, even a child can handle it.

  1. To get started, take a sheet of A-4 paper and fold it in half, then unfold.
  2. Fold the leaf again, but so that the second fold is perpendicular to the first. Thus, the center point of the sheet will be found.
  3. Bring the upper corners to the center so that you get a triangle, under which there is a rectangle.
  4. Fold the part of the rectangle under the triangle up, then flip the design over and do the same on the other side.
  5. Now the future origami paper boat for children resembles a triangular hat. Fold the sheet so that the center points at the bottom of the triangle diverge at different poles.
  6. You will get a diamond-like shape.
  7. Now bend the bottom corner of the rhombus up, the same thing needs to be done on the other side.
  8. Now you have a triangle in your hands, and it‚Äôs multi-layered.
  9. Next, you should repeat step No. 5, that is, to separate the center points of the bottom of the triangle on different sides.
  10. It should again turn out a rhombus, but not so big.
  11. Now you need to slowly pull the diverging ends of the triangles. And now the origami paper boat for children is ready!
