Ideal Pearl 4 - Hotel for a relaxing holiday

Marmaris, located in the southwest of the Turkish coast, is a resort generously endowed with nature. Here, a mild climate with a clear sea, picturesque bays with the surrounding mountains, coniferous forests with sandy beaches are surprisingly harmoniously combined ...

Marmaris itself is small. It has one embankment street, along which there are restaurants and cafes, from which you can see the harbor, port and hotels in three rows. One of them - Ideal Pearl Hotel 4 * - is located two hundred meters from the sea. We offer to get to know this hotel closer.





general information

The hotel, built in the nineties of the last century, was completely renovated two years ago. Today, Ideal Pearl 4 * is a beautiful five-story snow-white building on the second line. The distance to the center of Marmaris is three kilometers, to the nearest airport - ninety kilometers.

From the hotel, at the gates of which there is a stop, every fifteen minutes, minibuses leave for the waterfront. The hotel has a small area of ​​4,500 square meters. meters. There is a stall selling essentials, a conference room for eighty people, a laundry, an ATM. In the building on the ground floor there is also a currency exchange and dry cleaning.

Infrastructure





The registration desk is open around the clock. For settlement you need to come here. Nearby there is a paid medical office, which works continuously. In addition, the reception has a left-luggage office and a paid safe. Here you can arrange a transfer, which includes wires to the airport. You can also rent a car, send a fax or use a photocopy machine.

There is also a tour desk on the ground floor of the building. Specialists will help organize tours to the attractions of the resort of Marmaris and its environs. Those Russians who do not speak English can use the services of a Russian-speaking guide.

With Ideal Pearl 4 *, a small but guarded convenient parking lot is equipped. There is no need to book it.

Housing stock

Ideal Pearl 4 * (Marmaris) is one five-story building. A quarter of all rooms have sea view windows.



The hotel is considered small. It has 114 standard rooms with an area of ​​eighteen square meters. In each of them, up to three people can live simultaneously (2 + 1). All rooms have furnished balconies.

Flooring - ceramic tiles. The rooms are decorated in a modern style in soothing colors, no frills. Curtains hang in calm tones on the windows, there is also an individually adjustable split system, a TV with one Russian channel. There is an electronic safe, the use of which is possible at an additional cost.

The combined bathroom has a shower and washbasin. A large mirror hangs on the wall. A hairdryer and beauty products are also provided, which are updated daily.

Two rooms are specially equipped for guests with disabilities. They are located on the ground floor and have everything necessary for the convenience of disabled people.

Nutrition

Ideal Pearl Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) works on the half-board and full-board systems . The all-inclusive concept is valid until eleven o'clock in the evening. Three meals a day and evening snacks are offered at the bar. From two to four in the afternoon you can enjoy pizza and sandwiches, and from five to six, guests can drink tea or coffee, and try delicious oriental pastries.

All drinks from foreign manufacturers, freshly squeezed juices, and ice cream are paid.





A restaurant and poolside bar are on site.

Beach

The distance from the hotel to the sea is two hundred meters. The beach here is public, urban. Umbrellas, trestle beds and mattresses must be paid separately. Beach towels are not provided.

Entertainment

On the territory of Ideal Pearl 4 * there is a gym, there is the opportunity to play table tennis. Three times a week, animation and evening shows are offered. Those who do not want to swim in the sea can sunbathe by the pool. There are many sun loungers and umbrellas, a bar works.



The hotel has a sauna and hammam, as well as a massage room. The city beach has a lot of water paid entertainment. For children in Ideal Pearl 4 * there is a playground and a shallow compartment in the pool.

Reviews

The hotel is positioned as a calm family. Judging by the reviews, young people in Ideal Pearl 4 * are a little boring, but they can always go to the center of Marmaris, where there are many nightclubs and discos.

The Russians who rested in this hotel are not very many. But those who have been here are mostly satisfied with the price / quality combination. Some complain about the small area of ​​the rooms, although others say that they spent very little time in the hotel itself, as they relaxed on the sea and went on many excursions.

Regarding nutrition, the reviews agree on one thing: it is very good. Many pay tribute to the cooks of Ideal Pearl 4 *. According to the guests, all the food here is very tasty, high-quality and fresh. There is always meat, fish and poultry, a variety of side dishes, salads for every taste, as well as fresh fruits.
