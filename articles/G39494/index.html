"Dzhanhot" hotel: rest in a quiet zone of the Black Sea

Krasnodar farm "Dzhanhot" near the city of Gelendzhik - a corner of unspoiled nature on the Black Sea coast. The mountainous terrain is immersed in the greenery of Pitsunda pines. Sea water is clean and clear. Maximum sunny days per year.

One of the few resort sites "Dzhanhot" hotel is open for those who are tired of the daily bustle and want to regain strength in silence, unity with nature.



Housing stock

Despite the remoteness from civilization, the Janhot hotel provides vacationers with more than comfortable living conditions in a multi-storey building with a penthouse and VIP cottages.

Standard 2-room rooms with junior suites are close to the European level: warm interiors, Italian furniture and plumbing, air conditioning, TVs.

The suites, also with living room and bedroom, overlook the sea. They are decorated with sophisticated simplicity, complemented by a minibar, small fridge, safe, satellite TV, tea accessories. For each guest, a bathrobe, slippers, towels, a hairdryer, and hygiene products are prepared in the bathroom.



The stylish atmosphere of the 2-room apartment, a jacuzzi with bath accessories surround guests with a comfortable atmosphere. From the sea there is a balcony.

Interiors with stretch ceilings, imported furniture create premium conditions. Sitting in a comfortable chair on the balcony, you can enjoy the panorama of the Black Sea and mountains.

The rental price includes:

  • breakfast;
  • use of the sports ground, parking, beach equipment;
  • emergency medical care.

Family holidays and meals

The hotel welcomes families with children. Up to 3 years, bed and breakfast is free. This room is complemented by a cot.

The Janhot hotel has its own restaurant. A varied buffet menu is served in the breakfast room. If desired, food can be packaged if an excursion or a long walk is planned. Vacationers order lunch and dinner themselves.

The restaurant has fun meetings, banquets, children's parties.



Territory

The courtyard adjacent to the hotel is filled with flower beds, lawns, and southern greenery. It is pleasant to sit here on a bench or in a sun lounger, walk along the cobblestone paths under the pines, and gather together in a gazebo.

A large area is reserved for:

  • tennis court, table tennis;
  • sports and playgrounds;
  • B-B-Q;
  • terraces;
  • peacock
  • parking lots.

The spacious pool is filled with artesian water. There is a convenient descent, a small shower-waterfall. A multi-level bowl separates the shallow area for the kids.

Without leaving the water, you can order bar drinks, cocktails on an all-inclusive basis. Nearby is an aquarium with sun loungers. In the evening, the pool is highlighted and gives coolness to visitors to the bar.

Entertainment

"Janhot" hotel provides an active recreation program:

  • horseback riding;
  • riding bicycles;
  • bus excursions;
  • hiking;
  • boat trips.

Tired of the impressions of travelers, a sauna and professional relaxing massage sessions await.



Holidays by the sea

Down to a small bay is a white staircase, as if descended from paintings of the XIX century.

Pebble strip and rocky seabed are a continuation of the rocky terrain. Extremely clean water softens this feature.

The beach is shared, with free access. The boarding house equipped the shore with sunbeds for its vacationers. In hot summer weather, you need to take care of a beach umbrella or a light tent.

Hotel "Janhot", reviews

Judging by the responses of tourists, everyone is fascinated by the local beauties, especially the air, drunk with sea and pine trees.

Many liked the "Janhot" hotel, the prices for accommodation in it are considered acceptable: from 1000 to 4500 rubles per day for one person.

Breakfast levels are declining, although the restaurant is still up to par. At the height of the season, the beach becomes crowded, but there are plenty of sunbeds for everyone. Parents are concerned that there is no animator for children. Other inconveniences - lack of Internet, pharmacy, boring in the evenings.

Additional Information

"Dzhanhot" hotel is located at: Chernomorskaya street, 1, "Dzhanhot", Gelendzhik district of Kranodar Territory, Russia.

Those who know how to appreciate not only comfort, but also the uniqueness, healing power of nature, not polished by civilization, will return here again.
