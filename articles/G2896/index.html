Sea Drift Hotel (Sri Lanka, Negombo): description, service, reviews

The Negombo resort area is located on the west coast of Sri Lanka. This city is the fourth largest in the country. Its popularity among tourists is explained not only by the presence of convenient clean beaches with golden sand, but also by the vibrant nightlife. The tourist infrastructure in Negombo is developed simply superbly. There are at least a huge number of hotels here. For example, some domestic vacationers rent rooms in Negombo at the Sea Drift Hotel.

Where is the hotel situated?

The location of this hotel is considered by most domestic vacationers to be quite convenient. S ea Drift Hotel is located on the first coastline. The distance from it to the sea is only a few tens of meters.





Tourists who book rooms in this hotel arrive, usually in Sri Lanka at the airport of Colombo. The distance from the Bandaranaike to the hotel is only about 20 km. Therefore, tourists renting rooms here can quickly reach the place both on the tour operator's transfer bus and taxi. In the latter case, the trip in terms of Russian money will cost only about 350-400 rubles.

You can also get to Nagombo from the capital's airport by tuk-tuk. In this case, the road will take a little longer than when calling a taxi - about 1 hour. But tourists, of course, will have to pay less. A trip to Nagombo in a tuk-tuk is only about 200 rubles.

Some tourists travel to the hotel from the airport and by bus. In this case, the road costs the vacationers about 50 rubles. In the city itself, the hotel is just a short walk from the stop.

Sea Drift Hotel: general description

This hotel took its first guests a long time ago - in 1980. That is, this hotel can be considered quite old. Most of the claims made by her guests of the administration are connected with her venerable age.

Despite the fact that Sea Drift Hotel is a very small hotel (there are less than 10 rooms for rent), it has its own territory. Externally, this mini-hotel, according to tourists, looks very attractive. The hotel courtyard is completely surrounded by tropical greenery, and its low-rise building looks exotic in the south.

Check in the rooms of guests in this hotel begin at one o'clock in the afternoon. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.

On the websites of tour operators, the Sea Drift 2 * r hotel is classified as a two-star hotel. And the service provided on its territory, judging by the reviews, corresponds to this particular category. According to tourists, this hotel is well suited mainly only for unassuming young vacationers or couples who are not too picky in terms of comfort. With children in this hotel, according to vacationers, it is better not to settle. Negative reviews available about her on the Web are often associated, including with some unsanitary conditions prevailing on its territory.





There are usually quite a lot of Russian tourists in this hotel - about a third of the total contingent.

Hotel Rooms

In total, the Sea Drift Hotel (Sri Lanka) offers six rooms for guests of the resort. Most of the rooms rented out are air-conditioned. Some rooms also have spacious balconies where guests can enjoy beach amenities.

In addition to beds, the hotel for the convenience of guests in the rooms provides desks with chairs, mirrors and bedside tables. The hotel rooms also have small wardrobes with safes. Some apartments also have wicker shelves. Beds at the hotel guests at night can cover with canopies.

From household appliances, in addition to air conditioning, kettles are provided in the hotel rooms. Tourists, of course, also attribute their presence to the advantages of the complex.

Despite the fact that the Hotel Sea Drift 2 * is just a two-star hotel , the bathrooms in its rooms are equipped with individual ones. Here, in addition to the toilet bowl and sink, there are watering cans for bathing. Those rooms that do not have air conditioning have fans. Also, guests at this hotel can use the phones.

What do vacationers think about hotel rooms rented out?

The equipped rooms of the Sea Drift Guesthouse are , of course, quite simple. This fact is also confirmed by those tourists who have ever already lived in this hotel. But after all, the cost of renting rooms in this two-star hotel is inexpensive.

The main advantage of the rooms rented out in the complex is that tourists consider them quite large. Regarding the cleanliness of the rooms, as well as the bed linen in this hotel, unfortunately, there are not very good reviews on the Web. And the decoration of the rooms of this hotel, according to tourists, leaves much to be desired. In the shower rooms of the hotel, as well as in its rooms themselves, it can be damp. Therefore, tourists have to ventilate the rooms from time to time. Some rooms at this hotel, unfortunately, do not even have a ceiling. The roof directly protects such rooms in the complex (in which, in addition to everything else, there are also gaps).





Unfortunately, the isolation in the rooms in this hotel also leaves much to be desired. However, the guests of the complex do not consider this a special problem. After all, the hotel is actually located in a very quiet place. It was built on a village long street, next to it there are no noisy establishments. Do not drive past this hotel and transport.

In addition to the large size, the guests also include the pluses that the rooms are handed over in the complex that each room has a separate entrance from the courtyard. Thanks to this, as well as the small size of the hotel, tourists feel here, according to them, "like my grandmother in the village." The atmosphere in this hotel is in any case very friendly and friendly.

Microclimate in the rooms

The lack of air conditioning in some rooms is not considered a special disadvantage of the hotel by most tourists. The climate in Sri Lanka itself is very comfortable. Such sweltering heat, as, for example, in India or Thailand, is not here. The weather in Sri Lanka in November-April is simply magnificent - warm and comfortable. But if you wish, you can come to this country in the summer. The heat at this time is also not special. Rains in such a period in the country really go often. But at the same time they are not as protracted as, for example, in the same Thailand.

The staff of this complex tourists in their reviews on the sites of tour operators are usually praised. Hotel Infrastructure

Hotel Sea Drift Hotel belongs to the class of only two-star complexes. But the pool on its territory is still available. The hotel guests respond well about it. The water in the hotel pool is warm and does not smell too much bleach. Of course, tourists can use deck chairs and umbrellas in the courtyard of the complex for free.





The hotel infrastructure is developed, so for a two-star hotel is pretty good. In addition to the pool, this hotel also has parking. Moreover, guests can use it absolutely free.

Service at Sea Drift Hotel has earned relatively good reviews from tourists. It turns out to guests at this hotel, including a service such as a transfer from or to the airport. The only thing - to pay for such convenience to the residents of Sea Drift, of course, you need to pay separately. The hotel offers free WiFi throughout the property. This tourists, of course, are also attributed to the advantages of the complex.

Hotel Feature

Perhaps the rooms at the Sea Drift Hotel are not particularly comfortable. However, this hotel has one interesting feature that makes it quite attractive in the eyes of romantically inclined travelers. The fact is that this complex was built almost on the territory of the jungle. From the windows of the hotel rooms offer views of either the blue sea or the picturesque forest. Gibbons live in the thickets near the hotel, exotic birds fly from branch to branch, chipmunks run and even lizards go. In fact, all this living creature lives almost on the territory of this hotel itself.

Many tourists admit in their reviews that, going to Sri Lanka at one of the cheapest Negombo hotels, they did not expect to see all these unusual tropical wonders at all.

About hotel catering

You can rent rooms at Sea Drift Hotel (Negombo, Sri Lanka) , including with breakfast. Unfortunately, lunches and dinners at this hotel are not offered to tourists. The dining room in this complex is equipped with a small one, but judging by the reviews of vacationers, it is quite comfortable. As for the breakfasts offered at the hotel on the web, there are basically only good reviews. The menu at this hotel is not very varied, but the food here is quite tasty. At the same time, the dishes themselves are always fresh and not cooled.

Cafe in the city

Hotel residents can have lunch or dinner in the city. There are many good cafes, including those located near the hotel. You can also buy raw foods and ask the hotel owners to cook them. The owners of the complex, living right on its territory, usually do not refuse this to their residents. The wife of the innkeeper is cooking, judging by the reviews, very well.





In the city, hotel guests have the opportunity to purchase fresh fish, vegetables, meat, seafood, chicken, etc. Experienced tourists recommend shrimps to go to Galle for shrimps.

What is the beach of the complex?

To get to the sea, guests at Sea Drift Hotel should just go out the gate, turn right and walk a few tens of meters in a straight line. This hotel is located opposite the center of the local beach. Judging by the reviews of tourists, swimming in this place is quite convenient.

Tourists have a good opinion of the hotel’s beach, including because the water in the sea is always calm. Whatever the weather in Sri Lanka in November-April, and stood (or even in the rainy season), there are almost never big waves in this place. The fact is that the hotel's beach is reliably protected by a reef. The water in the sea at this place, judging by the reviews of vacationers, is simply unrealistically beautiful in color. At the same time, there are usually not too many people on the beach located opposite the hotel. Tourists can relax here in almost complete silence.

The beach is covered with soft sand. Therefore, you can also sunbathe here with comfort. Unfortunately, beach towels are not issued to guests at Sea Drift Hotel . But the owners are allowed to take to the sea those that are designed for showers. In the subsequent towel you can simply change.





Excursions

Sea Drift Hotel is usually chosen by those tourists who like to sunbathe on a sand and swim in the sea, and to visit various interesting places in the resort. Sri Lanka has really a lot of attractions.

For example, guests of the Sea Drift Hotel often visit Sigiriya in this country . In another way, this rock is also called the Lion Fortress. Once, one of the local rulers ordered to make an impregnable palace out of this natural site. And even today, tourists climbing a rock can see the remains of artificially constructed lion paws. On the way to Sigiriya from Negombo, guides bring tourists to several more interesting places both culturally and historically.

The two-star Sea Drift Hotel, of course, does not have its own tour desk . But experienced tourists with regard to mini-trips around the country are advised to consult with their owners. Hotel owners give their residents usually very practical advice in this regard.





Instead of a conclusion

Thus, in spite of some simplicity of the room decor and not particularly intrusive service, time to spend tourists at the Sea Drift Hotel is very good. In any case, vacation in Sri Lanka and in this hotel will certainly be remembered by vacationers for a long time and on the positive side. The atmosphere in this complex really reigns very emotional. The nature around it is actually luxurious. But, of course, for those tourists who are on vacation in Sri Lanka, looking for comfort, it’s worthwhile to choose a hotel for yourself more expensive and comfortable.
