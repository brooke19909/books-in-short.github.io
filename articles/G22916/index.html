What is a flower from SDM and how to make it?

Perhaps no festive event can be imagined without balloons. They decorate the interiors of premises for celebrations or presented as a cheerful gift. Balls are not just inflated; various designs are made from them. The most popular element of the presentation is the flower from the SDM. From this article you will learn how to make this jewelry with your own hands, as well as collect an elegant bouquet.





What is SDM?

Surely you have a question: what do these three letters mean? ShDM are specially made balls for modeling. They differ from ordinary rubber balls in that they are made of a more flexible modern material, which allows the created structure to hold its shape for a long time, not to deflate and not burst. Of course, there are losses in any case. But after mastering this technique, they will be minimal. To weave a flower from SDM and other elements, long products are used in the form of sausages, as well as oval and round balls of small size and patterns in the shape of hearts. The material for making jewelry can be matte, transparent, shiny. Balloons are inflated with air using a special small pump with a conical tip for ease of operation. In order for the structural elements to soar under the ceiling, they are filled with helium, since the balls are quite dense and heavy.





Modeling Rules

Before you start creating a flower from SDM, you need to practice. To do this, you need to purchase several additional balls of a long and narrow shape. During inflation, the product should not be completely filled, but partially, leaving a small empty tail. This is necessary in order to avoid breaking the ball. When the material is twisted, the air will move, filling the model completely. If there are many nodes in the created element, then initially you need to leave more empty space. During the simulation process, one ball can be twisted in several places, as well as several balls with each other at nodal points. After training and with minimal loss of material, you can try to create several flowers from the SDM to arrange them in a bouquet.





How to weave a camomile

One of the simple elements of modeling is a flower in the form of chamomile. To create it, you need a colored ball of elongated shape. The part for the flower must be inflated, leaving 3-4 cm empty. In the place of the hole, make a knot by placing the opposite end of the ball in its center. You should form a closed ring that needs to be folded and twisted at the bend. Then divide the resulting design visually into three equal parts, twist in two marked places. Fold the model in the form of the letter Z, squeeze the nodes with the thumb and forefinger. The links filled with air will bend to form petals. Twist the workpiece in the middle, fixing the position of the modules. The flower from ShDM is almost ready. It remains to add a twig and form a center.

How to make a stalk

To make the flower legs you will need an elongated ball of green. Fill it with air, leaving the end free. Tie a knot at the base and twist the ball, backing 10 - 12 cm from the edge. Align the tail of the part with the site of the knot and twist, the middle of the flower is formed. Insert a twig with the folded part between the chamomile petals. Having retreated a few cm, bend the leg up, and after 20 cm down. Then twist the resulting zigzag shape in the middle, and twist each link together. You should get stalk leaves.





Following a detailed master class for flowers from SDM, you can collect an original bouquet and give it to any celebration. It will bring joy and cheer up others.
