Hotels in Elektrostal: a list with addresses, photos and descriptions, guest reviews

Arriving in a foreign city, first of all, a person should take care of a place where he can relax and spend the night, especially if the trip is delayed for several days. In Elektrostal, hotels provide their rooms for guests on different conditions, but everyone will find an optimal place for themselves for temporary residence. Where can I stay for a day or two, what conditions await guests?

Hostel Nest

The hotel in Elektrostal, where you can spend the night for a low price, is the Nest Hostel. We offer places in 10-, 4-, 2-bed rooms, it is possible to accommodate with your pet, if you rent the entire room.

Free parking is provided, and laundry and drying are available for a fee.

A train station is located one kilometer from the hostel, and Moscow's Paveletsky station is 2.5 hours away by car.

Hotel "Orange"

The recognized leader among the hotel complexes of the city is the hotel "Orange", which began its work in 2012, but already has a constant and large customer base.

Rooms of various comforts (deluxe, junior suites, comfort, standard), private restaurant (with capacity for up to 100 people), providing buffet meals, honeymoon room, conference room - all this makes each guest's stay comfortable.

In addition, you can use the following services:

  • mail delivery to the room;
  • safe at the reception;
  • meals right in the room;
  • clothing care much more.

Hotel Anchor

Another budget option for those arriving in the city is the Anchor Hotel, housed in a former dormitory building. Despite its past, the hotel fully satisfies the needs of guests who share their positive reviews about the hours spent there.

Clean rooms, located in sections with a shared kitchen, a bathroom and a vestibule, a lounge on the floor with open Internet access. In the immediate vicinity are various supermarkets and fast food cafes.

Hostel "Eden"

There are hotels in Elektrostal that provide inexpensive and reliable services to their visitors. According to customer reviews, the Eden hostel is one of those that provides a sleeping place and comfortable conditions for a temporary stay for those who do not have a large budget.

Location in the city only 125 meters from the railway station.

Total number of rooms: 42, of which there are double rooms, as well as 4, 6, 8-bed rooms, in addition, there are common rooms, all have free access to Wi-Fi.

Hotel "Elemash"

A comfortable hotel located near the OJSC "Machine-Building Plant" - hotel "Elemash".

At the service of each guest: television, in all rooms a toilet, shower, disposable cosmetics.

Types of rooms: standard rooms (28 rooms), comfort (4 rooms), luxury (4 rooms). Accommodation is single, but for a fee it is possible to check in the 2nd person in the room, as well as a sauna, steam room, laptop rental and copying of documents.

Hotel "Jazz"

When you go to rest with children, the question always remains open regarding the conditions of their placement. Hotel "Jazz" meets the parents, and in one room you can move in with a child (or children) of any age. Regardless of the number of children under 6 years of age, they are accommodated in extra beds for free, and older children - for 400 rubles per day.

Depending on the price of the room, it is possible to stay in a room with a TV and a private bathroom. The rooms can accommodate from one to 4 people.

Hotel "Calm"

Hotel "Calm" in Elektrostal is located in the city center. The organization considers itself to be an economy class institution - a bed can be rented for 150 rubles, there are more expensive conditions.

All amenities are shared, namely shower, bathrooms, kitchen, toilets. The hotel enjoys particular popularity among long-term caretakers and arrivals.

Total number of rooms: 50 rooms, the city center is only 2.5 km away, and Moscow is about an hour away by car.

You can call in and book a room around the clock.

Hotel Elektrostal

The complex "Elektrostal" is in great demand and respect among regular customers for comfortable rooms, welcoming staff and a variety of infrastructure.

Conference, banquet, romantic evening - "Elektrostal" will help in the implementation of the activities of its visitors and ordinary residents.

19 rooms with all amenities (including a refrigerator and free Wi-Fi access), a cafe, a secluded location in the city center, modern design of rooms and the building itself, affordable prices - that is why I want to come back here again and again.

Mini-Hotel Banifatsiy

For guests who want to not just spend the night, but also relax on a grand scale, the Banifitsiy hotel will be an excellent option: luxury rooms, two-story apartments, billiards, a sauna, stylized rooms - all this is provided to those who love the all-inclusive service.

There is a bridal room with the ability to arrange a romantic evening with petals, flowers, champagne, designer rooms "2 * 2", "Monroe" equipped with Finnish steam rooms.

Possible delivery of meals directly to the room, breakfast is included in the price.

Customer service is carried out at the highest level - this is evidenced by the numerous enthusiastic reviews of visitors to the institution.

Hotel "Southern Tribune"

"South Tribune" - Elektrostal Hotel, which is popular among athletes and fans.

In total, the institution has 12 rooms of various capacities (from 1 to 6 people).

Check-in is from 8 a.m. to 20 p.m.

For a fee, you can park your car in a guarded parking lot, as well as visit the Finnish sauna.
