Hotels in Sukhum, Abkhazia: overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

The resort city of Sukhum is the capital of sunny Abkhazia. As in any other tourist town, many hotel complexes and hotels are ready to provide their services here. In this article, based on reviews from numerous tourists, the most worthy hotels of Sukhum will be given.

Hotel Inter-Sukhum



This beautiful hotel with a very high level of customer service is located in the very center of Sukhumi, thus beating other places. A few meters from the hotel there are numerous infrastructure facilities, including the Sukhumi market, which is always open for tourists who want to get souvenirs or fresh products. In the same market, you can buy various exotic spices and seasonings for cooking all kinds of dishes. In addition to the market, next to the hotel there is a magnificent promenade with a gorgeous view of the local beauty.

This hotel has 165 comfortable rooms, furnished according to all the laws of the tourist business. Each room has a TV with satellite broadcasting, air conditioning, a refrigerator, a boiler, all the necessary furniture and utensils for making tea or coffee. There is always hot water.

The hotel has a bar "Sakura", where you can watch football matches or just warm up and have fun at evening discos. For those who arrived by car, there is a guarded parking lot and video surveillance. The lobby bar, restaurant and cafe are also actively meeting guests. There is a dining room.
Wi-Fi is available on site for a fee. There is a hall for conferences and negotiations, a wellness center with services from the sphere of beauty and sports (Pilates, yoga, a fitness room, a territory for dancing).
There is a children's development center for visitors from 3 years old, where qualified teachers work. For guests at the hotel people are given free tours on comfortable vehicles. If necessary, a shuttle service is available to guests.

"Inter-Sukhumi" hotel is located 200 meters from the pebble beach, where there are sun loungers, inflatable mattresses for relaxation, equipment for water activities.

Room Categories

This hotel offers accommodation in rooms of the following categories. The first is a standard for 2 people with two single or one double bed, a refrigerator, a TV, a bathroom with a shower and a bathtub, as well as with all the necessary accessories. The room has a balcony or loggia, wireless internet.

Triple standard room has 3 single beds, TV, internet, refrigerator, bedside tables, a bathroom with a set of hygiene items and towels, a balcony.

The PC number for 2 seats includes: 1 double or 2 single beds, 2 bedside tables, a work desk for working with a laptop or eating, a set of towels, a bathroom with a shower, air conditioning and heating, a TV. The loggia or balcony overlooks the street, while offering magnificent views of the surrounding landscape.

A single studio category room has all the necessary furniture, a safe for storing valuables, a TV with satellite TV, a refrigerator, 2 bathrooms (main and guest), beds.

The suite for 2 people has a TV, fridge, 2 air conditioners, 2 balconies, internet, shower with all the necessary details. This type of room is divided by a partition into 2 separate rooms. The shower has a special booth.

Nutrition

The hotel provides buffet meals, and there is a lunch and dinner in the dining room, which are available for a fee. Delivery of the ordered menu is possible directly to the room.

As for catering in the hotel, in the evening there is an opportunity to order and taste a variety of crown dishes of the peoples inhabiting the territory of the Black Sea coast.

At the Sakura bar, you can have a measured chat while enjoying a portion of aromatic natural coffee or freshly brewed green tea. The menu of the bar is no different from the list of dishes available in the hotel restaurant.

The Inter-Sukhum hotel deserves numerous positive reviews. Comfort and care for vacationers is the basis of this complex.

The rooms have private bathrooms, air conditioning, plasma TVs, loggias with mountain or sea depths. Hotels in Sukhumi are not only places to sleep, but also establishments where you can enjoy local dishes.
