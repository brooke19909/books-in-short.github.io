Sights of Mari El: photos and description

The Republic of Mari El is located near Chuvashia and Tatarstan. It is famous for its pristine nature, virgin forests and pristine ponds. For example, the Sea Eye Lake in its beauty and purity is in no way inferior to the famous lakes of Switzerland.

Legend has it that in immemorial times there was a high hill on which the village is located. One of the peasants decided to marry his own sister. In the midst of the feast, the earth beneath the sinners opened, the abyss swallowed the village. In its place a lake formed. Those who are not sinners can still hear the noise of fun that comes from a wicked wedding. The lake, as it were, lies in a huge bowl, the banks of which reach a height of 90 meters. The shape of the pond resembles the eye. The water in it is emerald. This lake is not suitable for swimming for everyone, since even in the warm season, the water warms up to no more than twenty degrees above zero. Another wonderful lake of these places is Yalchik, on its shores there are several sanatoriums and tourist centers.

Big Kokshaga Nature Reserve and Stone Mountain Nature Reserve

Tourists are delighted with the Big Kokshaga Nature Reserve. These are the richest coniferous-deciduous places rich in various types of flora and fauna, along which several interesting tourist routes have been developed. It should be noted that the reserve is usually overcast, the weather here does not indulge in sunny days. If travelers want to enjoy the contemplation of nature under the sun, then it is better to do it in July.

On the territory of the republic there is a landscape that is compared with the Alps in miniature. This is Kamennaya Gora Nature Reserve . Among this wild and extraordinary nature, the skillful creation of human hands looks very harmonious - a small gazebo near a small elegant Orthodox chapel, and next to it is a modestly equipped natural spring.

Mari Chodra National Park and caves

In the village of Gornyak there are picturesque caves. They are rich in natural ornaments - stalactites and stalagmites, and the walls are covered with hoarfrost. The caves are from permafrost, so the temperature here does not rise above six degrees of heat all year round.

Sheremetyev Castle

If you are interested in the sights of Mari El, you will like the description of the next palace. In the village of Yurino there is a magnificent Sheremetev castle.





It is an ornate fairytale palace, with dark red walls and a green roof. Windows and doors in a snow-white frame. Graceful turrets in the old Russian style and battlements in the Gothic style. The palace is difficult to attribute to any one architectural direction. There is a successful combination of several. Next, seven columns support the glass dome of the winter garden. Museums

Continuing to describe the sights of Mari El, the photos of which are presented in the article, one cannot but tell about the museum of the city of Yoshkar-Ola. Those wishing to learn about the history of the republic can visit it. The traditions of the indigenous people can be found in the Museum of Applied Arts. It is located in a modest one-story building with an attic. Also in the city there is a museum of peasant labor and life. The "highlight" of it is a rich collection of bells and bells.





More recently, the Tsarevokokshaysky Kremlin was built in the center of the capital. The impressive old Russian fortress made of red brick, the walls and towers are covered with a green roof, resembles the scenery for a historical film. This complex contains a church, an archaeological museum and a monument to the founder of the city, Fyodor Ioannovich.

Churches and cathedrals

The monument of Russian church architecture is the Church of the Holy Trinity. With the advent of Soviet power, it suffered the same fate as many churches in the country. It was closed and almost destroyed. The restored temple impresses with its splendor and marvelous architecture.

What other interesting sights of Mari El? For example, the Orthodox shrine of the capital of the republic is the Cathedral of the Resurrection of Christ. It is a building with sky-high towers, with reddish walls with white stripes that visually increase the height of the building. Green roofs and golden domes attract the eye of a traveler struck by this beauty for a long time.



In 2007, the Annunciation Tower was built in the central part of the city. The construction is 55 meters high and resembles a fragment of the Kremlin. Equipped with the most accurate clock, which are regulated by satellite.
