Market Overview

To understand how to use Metatrader 4, you need to familiarize yourself with the functionality of the terminal. After downloading to a computer and installing the trading platform, the user will immediately be allocated three zones:

  1. Working area or quotation chart.
  2. Navigation.
  3. Statistics and additional features.

At the very top:

  • "file";
  • "view";
  • "insert";
  • "charts";
  • "service";
  • "window";
  • "reference".

These are the main parameters of the platform. A little lower in the terminal will be displayed the necessary functions: selection of technical indicators, timeframes, graphical instruments, opening orders, connecting auto trading, a crosshair, creating a new chart and more.
