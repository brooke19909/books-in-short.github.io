Food processor Philips: reviews

The Philips food processor can become the most useful thing in the house. Each housewife spends a lot of time preparing dishes. The processes of cutting, chopping, whipping, kneading are the most lengthy and time-consuming. Just imagine that the time spent can be reduced by almost half. How to do it? Purchase a food processor. The price of such a device is not low, but given that it can simultaneously replace a blender, mixer, coffee grinder and other useful household appliances, then this cost is fully justified. For the simplest model (for example, Philips HR7605) you will have to pay about 4000 rubles. And there are those that cost around 20,000 (Philips HR7958 / 00).





The Philips brand itself appeared in 1891. The company began operations in the Netherlands. Now it is one of the most popular and popular brands. Its lineup consists of many copies of various household appliances - washing machines, refrigerators, blenders, food processors, irons, hair dryers, audio equipment and much more. If the buyer decided to choose a device specifically of the Philips brand, the company guarantees a high level of build quality, the latest technology, a long service life.

So, let's start the review of popular models of food processors.

Philips HR7778

The Philips HR7778 food processor can be purchased for approximately 18-19 thousand rubles. It is equipped with high power - 1000 watts. The harvester performs the work of a blender, mixer, there is a nozzle for squeezing juice and a press for citrus fruits. Suitable for those who process a large number of products every day.





Stationary construction. Speeds for work only 20. The kit includes 12 nozzles. The manufacturer took care of the maximum convenience of consumers during the operation of the device: a box for storing knives, suction cups, a brush for washing, and the main bowl is large enough.

Reviews on the model Philips HR7778

For those who are going to purchase a Philips food processor, the reviews will help you make the right choice. As you know, even among premium technology, you can find flaws in certain models. Let's find out an opinion on the Philips HR7778. Most consumers called this food processor quite practical and versatile. However, they also highlighted several negative aspects. During operation, the device makes a loud sound, but it, in principle, is the same as in combines of other brands. Some buyers drew attention to a weak blender, the lack of a nozzle for dicing. The distance between the disc and the pusher is large, so there is always an uncracked piece.

Philips HR7761

Domestic buyer can now choose any food processor. The price of the Philips HR7761 model starts from 5 thousand rubles. For this money you can buy an apparatus with a shredder, grater, mixer, the possibility of kneading dough, a mill.





It's hard to believe that so many functions can be assembled in such a small combine. Only 10 tips included. The device is equipped with two speeds. Power consumption - 750 watts. There is a pulse mode.

Philips HR7761 Reviews

The Philips HR7761 food processor was loved by many. This model is rated very highly. The advantages include compactness, ease of maintenance, low noise during operation, a large number of functions, magnetic legs, suction cups. Unfortunately, buyers found some disadvantages in this model. This is a small bowl of a blender, deeply recessed knives, loose closure of the lid. The disadvantages voiced by customers can be safely called insignificant, since they practically do not reduce the efficiency of work.

Philips HR7605

The Philips 7605 food processor can be bought at any store for 3-4 thousand rubles. It is a multifunctional device that can quickly beat, chop, chop, grate any products. During operation, it consumes 350 watts of power.





The size of the device itself is small, the volume of the bowl (2.1 liters) allows you to use the harvester for harvesting for the winter. Stationary construction. There is only one speed. Included are 5 nozzles, one of which is emulsion. There are three shredding discs and a chopping knife. The length of the power cord is one and a half meters, in the case there is a special compartment for storing it.

Philips HR7762

The Philips 7762 food processor is the most compact and inexpensive machine in this model line. The capacity of the blender is 1.7 liters, the flask is made of plastic, there is a lid with rubberized edges. With it, you can grind meat, grind soups, chop vegetables, cook mashed potatoes and more.





The chopper nozzle copes with grinding nuts, coffee, herbs, peppers or cereals. She will even cope with a chunk of ice. There are five different discs in the set, thanks to which you can cook mayonnaise, sauces, cut french fries, there is also a large and small shredder / grater.

Philips HR7762 Reviews
