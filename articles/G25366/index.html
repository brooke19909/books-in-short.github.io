Samara River Shipping Company

Samara was laid as a fortress on the banks of two rivers at once. Initially, her whole life was connected with water, and for many years river transport was the main one when transporting goods and people. With the destruction of the Soviet Union, watermen suffered greatly. Only at the beginning of this century is the Samara River Passenger Enterprise revived again.
