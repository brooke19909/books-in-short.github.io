How to sew a dress on the floor with your own hands



Surely, each of us had situations where we came across a girl in exactly the same dress as ours. And that unpleasant feeling that arises at this moment cannot be confused with anything. Of course, we all understand that this is a normal situation, but for some reason I want to fall through the ground, throw out a dress and never wear it again. To prevent such situations from happening again, you can start making clothes yourself, for example, sew a dress on the floor for the summer with your own hands. Calling all your imagination and creativity to help, you can create a unique product in which you will feel great and original.

We sew on our own: preliminary rules



In order to properly sew a dress on the floor with your own hands, you must perform actions in a certain sequence, not forgetting once again to measure and remake something. So, select the model. Having thought over the dress of your dreams in your head, we create a sketch - how this dress will look in reality. Then we take measurements and we do a pattern. Then comes the process of sewing the dress directly and bringing it to the final with the help of additional details. The most important process is the preparatory one: a mistake made at this stage can ruin the whole undertaking. Therefore, it is better to double-check everything.

Take measurements and make a pattern

In order to sew a long summer dress on the floor, you should take these measurements:

- the length of the entire product;

- the length of the back from the base of the neck to the waist;

- half the circumference of the waist and hips;

- half of the circumference of the chest and above the chest;

- length from neck to shoulder;

- half the circumference of the neck.

Patterning a dress on the floor is a rather complicated matter, so you should proceed step by step. It is better for beginners to sew a simple dress from inexpensive fabrics first. You can use the base pattern for a long dress, supplementing it later with the necessary changes.

Choose a style and fabric

The style should be selected based on the characteristics of your figure. Choosing a model that seems to be suitable at first glance, you can measure these things in the store and decide that they are right for you. If you first decided to make a dress on the floor with your own hands, then choose simple styles that do not require special sewing skills. After that, you can go to the store for a cloth. Since we sew a summer dress, it is better to choose a natural material, such as cotton or silk, chintz. In such a product it will be comfortable even on the hottest day.

Sewing process

Now take measurements, create a pattern on paper and transfer it to the fabric. This is done using a special crayon or dry soap and ruler. We cut out the pattern and sweep the details. Remember to process the edges before final stitching. The edge of the fabric is simply tucked and stitched.

We connect the details - and the dress is almost ready! Add all the additional details, decorate with lace, appliques, sequins. So we sewed the dress on the floor with our own hands, while spending a minimum of effort. Now there is no doubt - no one really has such a dress. Using one pattern, you can sew several dresses from different materials and with different details and get a diverse wardrobe for the whole summer!
