Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, service, reviews

Kemer is a resort town in Turkey, which has long been recognized as an entertainment center. As a rule, hotels in this area host young people and those who are a fan of nightlife. One of the most popular hotels in Kemer is Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 *.

Despite the fact that this place to stay is located in a fairly noisy area, it is often chosen by those who want to relax with the whole family.





Location and General Information

The Ambassador Hotel is located almost on the Mediterranean coast, in the very center of the resort town. From its main building to the beach is literally a couple of minutes walk, as it is located 50 meters from it.

Five kilometers away is another entertainment center - the city of Kirish, where many hotel guests often go to seek entertainment - there are a large number of restaurants, bars, karaoke, nightclubs, and a casino. As for the airport, the nearest one is in Antalya, at a distance of 40 km from Kemer.

Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 * was built in 1990. Currently, thanks to regular reconstructions, its buildings have a very modern look, they are comfortably furnished and equipped with electronics. Rooms

As for the apartments, there are 200 of them at the Ambassador Hotel. All of them are located in five blocks, two of which are 4 floors high, and there are also three three-story buildings. All rooms are divided into categories such as standard and promotional.

All apartments are equipped with excellent European furniture, as well as an air conditioning system that allows guests to feel as comfortable as possible in any weather outside. The apartments also have shower rooms with bathtubs, sinks, and cabinets for storing bathrobes, slippers and bath accessories.



Standard

In total, the hotel has 195 apartments of this category. Most of the windows overlook the flowering garden, which is located on site, from the rest you can watch the view of the aquazone.

Rooms of this category are small in size - each of them is 25 square meters. m, they consist of one room. It has a large berth, designed for a couple of guests, as well as the possibility of installing an additional single bed of a European type. Guests staying in the room can place their personal items in the cabinets. A desk for work and a comfortable soft chair are also presented to their attention. The apartments have a TV connected to satellite channels, as well as W-Fi, which provides guests with family and friends, even during leisure.

One of the rooms of this group, which is located on the ground floor, is equipped with everything necessary for people with disabilities to live in it. There is appropriate furniture, as well as plumbing, made taking into account all the characteristics of people with disabilities.



Promo

This type of room is often chosen by guests of the Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 *, who come to rest with their whole family. Tourists themselves note in their reviews that it is very convenient to stay with a small child who requires special conditions for living - the hotel administration does everything possible to provide them to the maximum.

Among the furniture there are beds designed for three guests. If necessary, a special cot is installed in the apartment - there is no additional charge for it, however, you should book it in advance. As for furniture, its set is standard. If desired, guests can use the electronic safe and minibar, payment for which is charged daily. There is also a telephone.





Conditions for children

Tourists who are on vacation at the Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 *, in their reviews, often emphasize that it is very convenient to live here with children, even the youngest ones. This is due to the presence of a certain number of conditions.

First of all, for kids there is a large playground for sports and just for active pastime. Also for this category of guests provides a small pool, which is a niche with crystal clear water, which is automatically heated. The walls of the pool are not slippery, which ensures the safety of swimmers.

Parents note in their comments that, if you wish, you can use the paid babysitting services in case you need to leave the child for a while. On the negative side, many are considering the lack of the possibility of renting strollers at the hotel.

Sport

Adult guests who prefer an active lifestyle can visit the gym at any time of the day or night. Here you can hire a personal trainer who will advise on any issues related to sports. Access to the gym at the Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 * is paid and is charged hourly.

In addition to the gym, the hotel has a section for playing table tennis, as well as daily rental bicycles.

Spa

Tourists who are fans of relaxing treatments can visit the massage room, which is a small part of the spa area. Here professional massage therapists can surprise the client with their work, as well as offer a fairly wide range of additional cosmetic procedures.

The spa area also has a thermal complex, which consists of a bath and a sauna, and the bath is a classic Turkish hammam, which will certainly be appreciated by tourists from Russia and the CIS countries. All bath accessories necessary for visiting are provided absolutely free of charge, but spent in pair time is paid at the established rate.

Not far from the thermal part there is a swimming pool in which there is an automatic water heating system that provides comfortable bathing at any time of the day.



Nutrition

The Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 * has one large restaurant, which is very richly decorated inside. Guests who relax in the hotel assure that the atmosphere in it offers a pleasant pastime, accompanied by high service and excellent quality of prepared dishes. A daily buffet is served at this establishment, designed for guests who relax on an all-inclusive basis. According to tourists, this restaurant serves dishes prepared according to traditional recipes of Mediterranean, Turkish and European cuisines.



If desired, guests of the hotel can visit four bars, three of which are located on the territory of Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 *, and one more is on the beach. Behind the bar there are masters of cocktail art who are able to truly surprise guests with their skill. Here you can also order various snacks and desserts.

Beach

Despite the fact that the hotel is located on the second coastline, it has its own beach, which is well equipped for relaxation and covered with pebbles. The official description of Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 * states that the hotel and this recreation area are separated by an existing highway.

On the beach there is everything you need for a comfortable pastime. Here, all visitors can take free sun loungers and use large umbrellas for free. For the convenience of guests, there are several public toilets, showers, and locker rooms on site. There is also a point for the delivery of air mattresses and large towels - they are provided for a rent equal to $ 1.

Services

According to guest reviews, the service at Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 * is at the highest level and fully corresponds to the category assigned to the hotel. It employs extremely polite staff who are very responsive to guests. Particular attention is paid to young children.

On a paid basis, guests can take advantage of additional services that are charged according to a separate price list - the exact cost of each of them can be checked with the administrator.

While staying at the hotel, tourists can take advantage of laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as ironing. On the ground floor of the main building, you can exchange currencies. Next to this point is a vehicle rental bureau, in which it seems possible to rent a car for temporary use, which many tourists, judging by their comments, do in order to go and see the sights of the region on their own. For such cars, a place in an open, but guarded parking lot is provided for free.

For everyone who wants to keep themselves in good shape in the main building, there is a beauty salon, where there are hairdressers, cosmetologists and manicurists. The Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 * (Turkey) also has a doctor on duty around the clock, who can be called if necessary.

Accommodation prices

A ticket to this hotel in all regions of Russia costs differently - it depends on how far you need to carry out the flight. As statistics show, a package for Muscovites costs an average of about 60,000 rubles. In this price, the tour operator includes a two-way flight (round trip), a standard weekly stay in the apartment, as well as all the necessary insurance. Food in this package is also taken into account.

Tourists reviews

All vacationers who live in this hotel, as a rule, leave it with a satisfied rest. Often, tourists share their impressions in the reviews that can be found on the Internet: on the websites of agencies, as well as on personal pages on social networks.

In the reviews, guests evaluate the level of service, as well as the internal atmosphere that prevails in the apartments. Guests note that each room is always comfortable, regardless of the weather on the street. Assessing the interior, tourists often pay attention to its simplicity and lordship. The view from the windows also leaves many in awe.

As for the level of services provided, guests like literally everything from a room service that is provided three times a week to cooking in a restaurant. Often, travelers focus on the successful embodiment of the imagination of cooks in seafood dishes - according to them, every fish or shellfish served here is a unique work of art.

Fans of nightlife say that there are none at the hotel, there is also no animation team. However, they recommend that if you want to visit entertainment places go to nightlife establishments, which are located almost in the vicinity of Ambassador Hotel Kemer 4 *. According to them, in such places you can meet a considerable number of Russian-speaking tourists and guests from Europe.

Most hotel guests in their reviews say that they do not mind returning here to relax again, recommending that their relatives visit this place. Almost all positive comments are accompanied by vivid photographs taken in the hotel rooms, on its territory, as well as on the beach.
