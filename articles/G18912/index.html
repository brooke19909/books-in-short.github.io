Country Hotel "River of Seagulls" in the Nizhny Novgorod Region: living conditions, entertainment, reviews

If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation surrounded by picturesque untouched nature and breathe plenty of fresh forest air, go to the country hotel "River of Seagulls". Here you will be provided with comfortable living conditions, quality service and plenty of opportunities for entertainment.

Location

The country hotel "River of Seagulls" in the Nizhny Novgorod Region is located in a cozy corner of untouched nature 1 km south of the village of Podrysie. If you travel by your own car, you need to follow this route:

  • From Nizhny Novgorod on the highway leading to Kirov, you need to drive 140 km.
  • Next, drive past the turn to Krasnye Baki, and near the Lukoil No. 90 gas station, turn right from the Kirov highway.
  • For the next 9 km you need to follow the hotel's brand signs.

If you travel by rail, you need to get to Vetluzhskaya station. From there you will be met by a hotel car.

Excursions

Relaxing in the country hotel "River of Seagulls", you should definitely get acquainted with local attractions. The hotel offers tours in the following areas:

  • The Krasnobakov History Museum is a cultural institution that has been operating since 1968. For 12 years, local historians, students and just volunteers have collected unique exhibits, among which are tools, photographs, books, letters, bones of ancient animals and much more. The museum has 13 stands with several hundred exhibits.
  • Svetloyar Lake is a unique natural monument with a water mirror area of ‚Äã‚Äãalmost 12 hectares. Scientists still have no consensus on the origin of this reservoir. It is interesting that the water from the lake can be stored in any dishes for years without losing transparency and taste.
  • The Church of the Life-Giving Trinity is an ancient wooden structure, erected in 1713 on the steep bank of the Vetluga River. In the Nizhny Novgorod region, this is one of the few such structures that has survived to this day.
  • Hot air ballooning is a unique opportunity to see the surrounding beauty from a bird's eye view.
