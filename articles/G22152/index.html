How to find out the Beeline tariff using service numbers

The modern market of mobile technologies does not stand still and from year to year pleases users with an increasing number of new offers. Russian mobile operators also do not stand aside, expanding the range of entertainment and information services.



One of the representatives of the "Big Three" of the largest domestic cellular communication companies is Beeline - we will devote this article to its services. She will talk about how to find out her Beeline tariff, and also, if you want to change it, will help you choose the one you need.

Oddly enough, some subscribers who have been using the services of the same mobile operator for many years do not even know the name of their tariff plan and the conditions for its provision.

You can find out your Beeline tariff:

1. In the service and account management menu, from your phone, dial the combination * 110 * 05 # and press the call button. The information you are interested in will come in a reply SMS message.

2. Through the automatic voice service. To do this, dial 067405 and wait for a response from the system.

Also in this system you can listen to the description of tariff plans (TP).

Recently there is a new way to find out the Beeline tariff:

3. Using mobile internet. To use it, you must first create a personal account on the operator‚Äôs official website. To do this, dial * 110 * 9 # from your mobile, in the SMS message you will receive the login and password necessary for the first login. If you are not satisfied with the password combination, then it can be changed in the system settings.

This service is also convenient because if you encounter any difficulties, you can contact the support service, whose staff will promptly answer your questions, for example, explain in detail where you can view information about new promotions, how to find out the Beeline tariff, how to order drilldown details and much, much more. This method is undoubtedly the most convenient, because all the necessary information is at hand, in a short time you can see

Check the prices for mobile services provided in your TP. As mentioned above, if you wish, you can change it to one that is more suitable for your style of communication. This can be done in the same way as to find out the tariff. Beeline offers its subscribers a wide range of tariff plans for any type of communication: for calls in the home region, for calls to Beeline numbers throughout the country, as well as in the CIS and abroad, a line of unlimited tariffs "All inclusive".

As mentioned above, in order to switch to a new TP, you can use the services described above: your personal account, a call to the operator (dial 0611, after the system answers, press 0 and wait for an answer) or the menu * 111 #. Using this service, you can perform actions such as: find out the Beeline tariff and the ability to communicate with a zero balance, disable or enable services. You can also switch to another tariff plan at the nearest cellular company office in your city.
