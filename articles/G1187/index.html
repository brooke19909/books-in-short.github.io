Dessole Sea Lion Beach Resort Mui Ne 4 *: reviews, photos

If you plan to spend a vacation in Mui Ne (Vietnam) and are looking for a large hotel with developed infrastructure on the first line, then Dessole Sea Lion Beach Resort Mui Ne 4 * can be an excellent option for you.



Location, general information

The hotel in question is located on the beach, 10 kilometers from Phan Thiet. The nearest airport is Kamran, 220 kilometers away.

The hotel's housing stock is represented by 269 rooms of the categories Standard, Superior, Deluxe and Family. The apartments are located in several buildings. Some rooms have magnificent panoramic sea views.

On the territory of Sea Lion Beach there are several swimming pools (two adults and two children), sun terraces, a mini club, a stage for performances, sports grounds, bars, a restaurant, a snack bar, shops, a tour desk and much more. Guests here are offered both a daytime and evening animation program. The hotel has its own fine sandy beach with comfortable wooden sunbeds.
