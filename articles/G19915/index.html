Meat grinders "Kenwood" - little helpers in the kitchen





It is known that among all kitchen appliances, the most popular is a meat grinder. It is no coincidence that in the assortment of almost every manufacturer of kitchen equipment, this unit takes its place of honor. Kenwood meat grinders were no exception. The manufacturer took care not only of the high quality of the products offered, but also of a wide range. Consider this device in more detail.

Metal case

Meat grinders "Kenwood" are represented by various models. High reliability and durability are provided by the stainless steel case. Meat grinders of this type (individual parts) can be washed in a conventional dishwasher. KENWOOD appliances have very high productivity, for example, the MG 700 can easily process up to three kilograms of meat in just a minute. But not only the Kenwood firm took care of this. The meat grinder 510, like many of its ‚Äúsisters,‚Äù for example MG 720 and the MG 520 model, has a reverse function that allows you to clean the auger from the veins without disassembling the entire structure. All models of this product line are equipped with a set of special disks with different sizes of holes. More sophisticated models are equipped with additional nozzles for slicing products, for cooking all kinds of kebabs and home-made sausages. The Kenwood meat grinder kit may include a special grater for rubbing cheese and vegetables.



Plastic case

The metal case is indisputably practical. But many note the rather high cost of such a Kenwood meat grinder. Reviews of regular customers of this brand suggest that such a unit can be bought much cheaper. It is worth paying attention to meat grinders, the case of which is made of high-strength high-quality plastic, for example, KENWOOD MG 450 or MG 470. Such devices are half the price of their metal counterparts.

The plastic case, however, can withstand significant loads. However, it is highly undesirable to apply serious physical impact to such units, for example, throw them on the floor. With proper, careful handling, such Kenwood meat grinders will serve their owners for many years. It should be remembered that too frequent and prolonged operation of such a unit is also not recommended.

Security

Kenwood specialists pay great attention to observing all the necessary safety standards and guarantee the possibility of long-term operation of the mechanism. The electric part of the meat grinder is reliably protected from moisture, which avoids electric shock, even if you touch the device with wet hands. A special system protects the engine from overloads. In order to avoid various injuries to the limbs, all Kenwood meat grinders are equipped with a plastic pusher (for some models, for example for MG 510, the pusher is made of metal). It allows you to push stuck products.

To summarize, we conclude that the KENWOOD meat grinder is a necessary device in the kitchen. It will greatly simplify the work and help you easily and quickly cook all your favorite dishes of meat or fish.
