Paper asters: different manufacturing techniques

Fresh flowers are always great. And in the winter, as an alternative, you can use homemade. Paper -made asters made with their own hands look especially good .

Bouquet

Autumn flowers, which include asters, have a particularly bright color. But after their flowering, nature begins to fade. In order to keep warm and remember longer sunny days with a variety of autumn colors, you can make a bouquet of beautiful asters from paper.

To make it, you will need:

  • double-sided colored paper (pink, purple, orange, yellow, red and green);
  • scissors;
  • glue;
  • wire.


Instruction:

  1. Fold a sheet of paper four times.
  2. Cut into small strips without cutting to the end of a centimeter and a half.
  3. Glue all four strips together.
  4. Bend the petals with scissors.
  5. From yellow paper cut a strip 1.5 cm wide and also cut a fringe. These are stamens.
  6. Twist a narrow strip and glue it. On it, wrap, gluing, a wide strip.
  7. From two green ribbons on the same principle to make sepals. Screw them into wide strips, gluing.

One paper aster is ready. And so do the rest. Put them on the stems and put in a vase. The bouquet can be admired all winter.

Colored paper astra

For manufacturing you will need:

  • green corrugated paper;
  • wire;
  • scissors;
  • pencil;
  • ruler;
  • compass;
  • colored paper;
  • glue.


Step by Step Instructions:

  1. Prepare pieces of wire for aster stalks.
  2. Cut green leaves.
  3. Make strips of green corrugated paper 2 cm wide.
  4. Wrap the wire with ribbons of paper, inserting leaflets into it.
  5. Cut a strip of yellow paper 3 cm wide.
  6. Bend it in half and make cuts with a Christmas tree.
  7. Collapse in a circle, you get stamens.
  8. Draw two concentric circles with a common center and radii of 4 and 1.5 cm.
  9. Divide the small circle into 16 equal parts, continue the lines until it intersects with the large circle and on each ray put off equal segments. The resulting points are connected with the middle of 1/16 of the large circle.
  10. Cut the resulting triangles, cut the petals along the rays to a small circle and bend the petals in half. There are 6 pieces of such blanks.
  11. From green paper to make two of the same parts a little smaller, it will be a sepal.
  12. Glue all parts of the aster, starting with green ones. To do this, coat the central circle with glue and press the workpiece tightly.
  13. Pierce the stem and secure it.
  14. Glue the stamens from above, and the paper aster is ready.

Corrugated Paper Astra

There are several ways to make a beautiful flower called aster. For the particular case under consideration, you will need:

  • corrugated paper (green, yellow and purple);
  • wire 20 cm;
  • scissors;
  • glue.


Step-by-step instructions for making paper asters:

  1. Cut 2 strips of lilac paper 4 cm wide and one yellow.
  2. Fold them several times and make deep cuts: yellow - often, and purple - less often.
  3. Cut green leaves.
  4. Wrap the wire with a thin strip of green paper, while winding the leaves. Lubricate with glue for strength. Got a stalk.
  5. A yellow strip - stamens tightly wrap on the end of the stalk, smearing the edge with glue.
  6. Twist the tips of the petals of the purple stripes inward using scissors or a pencil.
  7. Glue the edge of the tape to the stamens 1 cm above the base with a bend inward. Do the same with the second lilac strip, gluing its edge to the first.
  8. Spread the middle of the aster to see yellow stamens.
  9. Cut sepals from green paper in the form of petals and stick on the bottom of the flower.

Conclusion

Astra is an amazing flower with a huge range of colors. Making them a composition, it is easy to choose it for any interior. In this article, three ways to make an aster out of paper are covered. Everyone who wants to decorate the house with an autumn bouquet of flowers can use the tips. Such a bouquet will delight you with its colors until next fall.
