Memory, RAM, Chipset and Performance

The Samsung Galaxy Note 8 version for America is equipped with 6 GB of RAM, while for China, as much as 8 GB is provided. Built-in storage has 64 GB, and external drives up to 256 GB are supported. Battery

A review of Samsung Note 8 makes it clear that the phone is equipped with a 3300 mAh battery, it is a lithium- ion type. If we compare it with the S8 + model, the figure is slightly lower, but it copes with many tasks perfectly. If a person uses the technique quite actively, then the phone can last about a day. With a skillful approach and low brightness, this figure can be increased to 2-3 days. It is advisable that for the entire period the screen work no more than 8 hours.

Note 8 is considered a long-lived gadget. There are wireless and fast charging, power saving mode.
