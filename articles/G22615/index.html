How to choose a music receiver: an overview of the best models and reviews

A music receiver is an essential part of any modern audio system or even a home theater. This is the main component in the whole system. Any receiver concentrates several important devices at once - it is a signal preamp, tuner and a signal switching station in one person. All components of audio‚Äì, video systems are connected to a single center - the receiver. It receives signals from all devices and redirects them to others. There are a huge number of receivers, and all of them have not only different characteristics, but also different purposes. A receiver designed for car speakers is simply not able to work in a more powerful home system.





Manufacturers

There are quite a lot of manufacturers of electronic devices in the world, and it is no secret that half of them are Chinese firms. It is not customary to save on receivers. Buying a Chinese replica of a branded device is not worth it, although it is much cheaper. Otherwise, there is a serious danger of being disappointed and underestimating music receivers. Before buying, you should look at the following companies:

  • Yamaha
  • Pioneer;
  • Onkyo;
  • Denon

It is worth choosing brands that are well-known in the domestic Chinese market only if there is no way to purchase a receiver from a well-known company. Replicas and remanufactured devices are best avoided.

Varieties of Receivers

All receivers are different, and not only by characteristics, but also by purpose. That is why, choosing a receiver, it is imperative to determine in advance its purpose. As a rule, the first stage of selection is simple.

  • Mobile receiver A great option for a music receiver in the car. It is compact and can even fit in a pocket. It is connected via the standard AUX connector, which is provided in many cars. Unfortunately, this is where the advantages of such a device end. Due to its size and specific purpose, it does not produce such a desirable high-quality sound.
  • Stationary receiver. This is a whole group of devices with a huge variation in product cost and quality. An option for home audio or video systems, or even cinema systems. The stationary music receiver is a rarity. Typically, AV receivers are used.

It is equally important to understand exactly how the system will connect to the receiver. There are not many options, but they are constantly expanding. You can connect the receiver using:

  • Cable.
  • Wi-fi connection.
  • Via bluetooth. Not all companies provide bluetooth. The music receiver works without it, as some manufacturers decided and suffered losses. Such a connection in the modern world is necessary.

Good receiver models are not so demanding to connect, because they contain a really large number of different connectors. There are not only modern ports, but also obsolete ones. When buying a receiver, one should not forget about decoders. There are several options:

  • Dolby Digital Plus. A modern decoder for reproducing high-quality sound and video. First of all, it is necessary for Blue-Ray discs.
  • Dolby Digital. This decoder is required if the audio system involves playing content from a CD or DVD disc.
  • Dolby Pro-Logic. This obsolete decoder will provide high-quality work with obsolete film cassettes, however it does not work with optical discs.

The music receiver always needs calibration. There are no universal parameters. The configuration of the connected devices and the size of the room are different. Of course, self-calibration is a science, but automatic comes to the rescue. Modules for automatic calibration are usually supplied with the device itself, but they may not be available when it comes to professional equipment.





Receiver design

Receivers vary in design. Each of the designs has its advantages and disadvantages.

  • Semiconductor receiver . The most popular and frequently used solution. Such receivers are quite reliable and can even survive significant voltage drops.
  • Receiver on the lamp. This is a very expensive and impractical solution. As a rule, this option is typical for professional models. The music receiver on the lamps gives a more lively sound. You can not buy cheap models of such receivers. They are very short-lived and will be much worse than similar-priced semiconductor receivers.
  • Mixed receivers . An extremely rare solution for receivers. It is found exclusively in highly specialized models.

Music AV Receiver

This versatile receiver is the most popular option on the market. Receivers intended only for music systems are very expensive professional tools that are not available to ordinary people. The AV receiver does an excellent job of any kind. It is not a highly specialized device and gives excellent sound, excellent synchronization and high-quality image on the TV screen. It can be used in home theaters, separately in audio systems and even combine all this.





Ample features of AV receivers

The capabilities of such receivers will satisfy not only an inexperienced music lover, but also a picky specialist. This device combines a number of features:

  • Gain. The primary signal, as a rule, is rather weak, and for its high-quality reproduction on good equipment it is necessary to amplify the signal. The receiver eliminates the need to buy a separate device and independently amplifies the signal, thereby making the sound more saturated.
  • Commutation. This is a necessary property for each receiver. The essence of switching is to combine multiple signals into one.
  • Decoding audio tracks. On an optical disc or any other medium, the audio file is recorded in an encoded video track. The receiver decodes the track and plays. The better and more expensive the receiver, the more formats it can decode. If the format of the material cannot be decoded, it must be converted to an acceptable format, and this is far from always possible without loss of recording quality.




Each receiver for a music center or home theater has all these features. In compact car receivers, amplification may be completely absent.

The best receiver for a car

Not all cars are equipped with modern audio systems. It often happens that the radio does not have a USB connector or even AUX. This in itself is a pretty tangible problem. Car receivers are always small in size. They have a number of disadvantages, but with their help you can listen to music without radio interference and CD-ROMs. A bluetooth music receiver is the only way to listen to high-quality sound in an old car. Managing a playlist using your phone is very convenient, but quite dangerous in motion. Do not also forget that bluetooth receivers, especially inexpensive models, will not give high-quality sound.

Everyone, buying any product, wants to get the best quality at a lower price. Such an opportunity is not always available, but fortunately, electronics manufacturers understand that not everyone can afford expensive devices. The market is full of cheap Chinese fakes and the so-called younger models of branded devices. Choosing something really good is hard. Each of these receivers allows you to get great sound at minimal cost. Of course, the sound will not be perfect. However, an ordinary music lover will not hear any flaws in the sound of these models.
