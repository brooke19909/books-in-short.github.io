Baby ninja turtles costumes

Do you want to make your child an inexpensive and comfortable outfit in which he can show off not only at the holiday, but even play with friends? Then take note of this option:

For beautiful fans





But such a costume for ninja turtles for a girl can be made in two hours:

You will need:

  • Fatin different shades of green.
  • Elastic.
  • Scissors.
  • Needle with thread.
  • Form for cooking.
  • White sports top and knee-highs.
  • Green paint in a spray can for fabric.
  • Watercolor.
  • Black lace.
  • Old t-shirt.

Production Order:

  1. First, paint the top and socks in green.
  2. Then we make a skirt: we cut off the elastic band at the waist circumference (keep in mind that it stretches). Sew it in a circle, cut tulle into thin strips. Fold it in half and fasten it with a loop on an elastic band. So repeat until you get a fluffy skirt.
  3. We paint the aluminum shape in green, thread the laces to wear it like a backpack.
  4. From an old T-shirt of a suitable color, cut strips on the head, elbows, knees.

The result is a great suit. The self-made ninja turtle is ready. It remains only to paint the abdomen and back with a green watercolor , and make the cubes on the press a lighter color.
