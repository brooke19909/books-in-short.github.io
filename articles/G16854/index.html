Guzun Igor Georgievich - actor, teacher and artist

If a person is destined to become an actor, nothing can stop it. Cinema lovers are familiar with the surname Guzun. Igor Georgievich was a teacher of drawing at the Moldavian school. He starred in several dozen films, gained the experience of a screenwriter and producer.



Biography

Astrologers claim that Gemini is a creative and energetic sign. It is difficult to disagree with them when it comes to Igor Georgievich. He was born on May 30, 1960. There was a time of great events: the first satellite was launched, a man was preparing to go into space. People believed that they had a happy future. In this environment, Igor Guzun grew up. From childhood he was attracted to art.

After graduating from high school, he entered a pedagogical institute, after which he became a drawing teacher. A few years later he completed acting courses in Romania. After completing the courses, Igor Georgievich was invited to work at the Moldovan film studio.

The Bucharest Academy of Motion Picture Arts can deservedly be proud of its graduate.

Creative way

Guzun Igor got his first role shortly before the collapse of the Soviet Union - in 1989. His character became a gangster-Bandera in the film "Codri." The talent and excellent performance of the actor was appreciated not only by the audience, but also by the directors. Roles followed one after another. There was not a single year when Igor Georgievich would not act in films.

Many actors were known and in demand in certain historical periods. This is not about him: Guzun enjoyed popularity in the last years of the USSR, when he was just starting his career, and in the dashing 90s, and in more calm subsequent years.

Already in 2006, Guzun Igor tried himself as a screenwriter in the film "Alias" Albanian ".

Due to the high level of knowledge of foreign languages, Igor Georgievich collaborates with American film companies. He is fluent in English, fluent in Romanian and Italian.

Films and roles

Igor Georgievich played more than 30 images. Among them:

  • military;
  • bandits;
  • security guard;
  • Minister
  • doctor;
  • supply manager;
  • Joseph Stalin.




Igor Guzun, whose filmography includes dozens of works, starred in the role of Stalin 6 times! And once depicted his double. He plays the military perfectly, regardless of which side they are fighting on. And the FSB agent, and the French officer, and even Bandera - all these are his characters.

The appearance of Igor Georgievich corresponds to these images: high growth - 180 cm, strong physique. Thanks to the natural dark color of his hair and brown eyes, he naturally looks, playing not only Europeans, but also southerners, whom he repeatedly depicted on the screen.

Guzun Igor is a modern and talented actor.
