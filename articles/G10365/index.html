Gresso phone is the pride of the rich

At all times, wealthy people sought to own the appropriate accessories, emphasizing their high social status and level of wealth. But if earlier an indicator of prestige was, for example, expensive watches, rings, rings, chains and other similar elements, today the trend has changed a bit, and gadgets come to the fore. One of these is the Gresso mobile phone, which will be discussed in this article. We will be able to get acquainted in detail with all its characteristics.

In fact, the name of an innovative product fully shows the consumer the concept of this brand, which consists in creating a product that fully complies with all modern trends and combines various exclusive materials, thoughtful design and high-precision engineering.

Gresso phone is an expensive thing that several years ago broke into the world market and appealed to many wealthy people.





Production Features

The products of the Swiss company combine advanced modern technology and manual work, which uniquely guarantees the proper quality of the received communication equipment. A distinctive feature of each collection is the presence of an engraved individual number on the case, so each Gresso phone is unique.

The case of the mobile device is made on the basis of titanium. Finishing, which is a directed grinding, is carried out exclusively by hand. About eight hours are spent on the manufacture of each case, and new generation equipment is used for this.

By the way, titanium used in production has a 5th grade (it is often used in the aerospace industry). In addition to it, gold of the 750th test in the amount of 18 carats is used. Almost all models use the Smart Lock magnetic lock for reliable and rigid fixation of the battery compartment during the operation of the phone. The keyboard is made on the basis of precision turning technology, which allows to achieve the highest form accuracy.

The letters and numbers on the product are laser-cut and have a very clear outline. The color scheme of the display has hundreds of thousands of colors. The keyboard plate has an ultrahard titanium coating that guarantees the working surface an appropriate level of resistance to a wide variety of mechanical damage.

Limited edition

In 2014, the Gresso phone was developed and first appeared in the form of a dual-SIM model. Her name is Gresso Azimuth. It is noteworthy that this exclusive item was produced in extremely small quantities in the amount of 999 pieces. On the back of each such phone is indicated by its serial number.



The device has a mineral glass front panel that has a very dark color. All buttons of the accessory are polished exclusively by hand. The front panel also has a gold plate with an engraved corporate logo.

Dimensions of the phone: 118x49x14.5 mm. Weight is 160 grams. The amount of internal memory is only 2 GB. Battery capacity - 1110 mAh. In standby mode, the phone can last 680 hours without recharging, and in the case of a continuous conversation, the user has the option not to use the charger for 20 hours.

Luxury addition

Another Gresso phone, reviews of which are exclusively positive, is able to satisfy the needs of wealthy people. It's about the Regal Black Edition.

Its body is finished in white or yellow gold. The case thickness is very small and is only 8.8 mm.

Manufacturers did not stint on technical specifications. So, in particular, the phone has:

  • five-inch FullHD display;
  • quad-core processor (1.5 GHz);
  • RAM - 2 GB;
  • flash memory - 32 GB;
  • the main camera - 13 megapixels;
  • front camera - 5 megapixels;
  • operating system - Android.

The only "bad" moment can be considered only the high price of the product - about 150 thousand rubles.





Gresso regal titanium

Another model, which is also focused on the attention of people with wealth, is Gresso Regal Titanium. The body weight of this phone is only 130 grams, and it is not afraid of moisture, bright sunlight or corrosion. Also, this device is perfectly protected from falls.

This Gresso phone has a circulation of 333 copies. The unit price is four and a half thousand dollars.





The most expensive models

Like any exclusive product, luxury Gresso phones initially have a high cost. However, even in their nomenclature there are models that are the undisputed leaders in terms of price. So, Grand Premiere can be put on the first line in terms of high cost, for which a potential buyer will need to have $ 50,000 in his pocket.

And on the second line in cost is the Gresso Avantgarde model. By the way, this particular phone in 2007, respected by many analytical magazine Forbes, was ranked among the ten most expensive phones on the planet.
