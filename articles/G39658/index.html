Ribbon monkey: how to create

In this article, we will look at how a wicker monkey is created. It‚Äôs not difficult to make a template for it, and then we will tell you how. A handmade souvenir will become a decoration in the New Year's interior. The creation of such crafts will bring a lot of joy to children and mothers. Ribbon monkey is a great gift for family and friends. When creating this baby, invest positive emotions, and let her bring a lot of joy to the house.

Materials



So, as a result, we should get a monkey. A template cannot be created without special materials. To make a monkey you will need: satin ribbon, felt, you can take one that is available, its color is not important, scissors, glue, ruler.

Instruction manual



We turn to the practical part of solving the question of how to create a monkey from satin ribbons. Let's create separately blanks for each part of the monkey‚Äôs body, it should turn out: two tummies of brown and pink color, tail, head, 2 arms, 2 hands, 2 legs, 2 feet, 2 ears, 3 parts of the muzzle. Thanks to these blanks, a ribbon monkey will soon be created.

Go to the next step. To create legs and pens, you need to prepare a brown tape. Its dimensions: width - 6 cm, length - 35 cm. We assemble the tape into an accordion and tighten it to a size of 4 cm. You will get one leg or one handle, there are three more such pieces of the same size. The bottom of the pink color is attached to the resulting blanks. For its manufacture, a tape is taken - 6 cm wide, 13 cm long. We tighten it with an accordion to get a workpiece of 1.5 cm in size.

Union



Our ribbon monkey should be whole. We combine the brown blanks for pens and legs with pink. We fulfill the muzzle of the monkey in two parts. These two elements should take the form of round petals. In order for the part to get the correct shape, just the one that is necessary for the formation of the head, you need to cut the small end from the brown petal and part the tips in different directions. We singe the cropped part so that it becomes flat and wide. We cut the pink part a little more, the finished product should be 5 cm in size. We make the ears of brown ribbon, in finished form their size is 4x4 cm. Our ribbon monkey has a felt base. We create it. All tape elements will be attached to it. Dimensions for the base: head diameter - 3 cm; the height of the pear-shaped body is 4 cm, the width of the lower semicircular part is 3 cm. The length of the legs and arms is 4 cm. The size of the tail is up to you.

Algorithm



All details are prepared, we are assembling the New Year's souvenir. You must have two identical felt blanks. On the first part we fix the legs and tail, after attaching the finished tapes to them. We apply a thin adhesive material on their base and fasten the brown ribbons, and pink ones for the foot. Then we connect exactly two bases, apply carefully so that their edges coincide. On the side that will serve as the front, we fix ready-made handles. They can be attached in various ways, for example, so that they hang down below or stick out to the sides, calling for a hug. Glue round ears to the felt head. For the tummy, we twist the accordion tape of a pink color with a spiral, apply glue to the felt in the center of the body and apply the pink part. Around the resulting pink area we fasten the brown ribbon.

The execution of the muzzle must be taken responsibly, because this is a face, it should turn out to be pretty. We attach the pink part of the muzzle in the center of the head and attach only the upper part to the felt, and leave the lower part free. Around the pink petal we fix the brown accordion. To the free side of the pink muzzle we attach a double red petal and fix it so that we get a mouth. From the outside of the red petal we apply and gently glue the brown petal. Mouth ready. New Year's monkey left to mark the eyes. It is necessary to outline the place where you need to stick your eyes.
