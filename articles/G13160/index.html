General impression

The main advantage of the Rossiya Hotel in Veliky Novgorod is its advantageous location in the central historical district. It is located on the embankment of the Volkhov River, near the main attractions - the Kremlin and Hagia Sophia. This makes this place especially attractive for tourists. Business people will also be pleased to enjoy the beautiful scenery after a busy day. Especially guests are pleased with the presence of a spacious furnished balcony, from which the city of Veliky Novgorod opens in all its glory.

The situation in the hotel is generally comfortable. However, there are some problems with the quality of cleaning. At first glance, everything is removed. But if you look under the bed or behind the nightstand, you will find there a layer of dust and a lot of garbage. The price includes a delicious breakfast. But if you want to make an order on the menu, then you will find disappointment in the form of a meager assortment and too high prices. But the friendly and polite attitude of qualified staff smooths out many of the shortcomings of this hotel.
