Hotel complex Ibis Phuket Kata 3 (Thailand, Phuket, Kata - Beach): description of the location





Naturally, the location of the hotel can be both a significant advantage and a disadvantage. Where is the Ibis Phuket Kata 3 hotel complex located? Thailand, Phuket, Kata-Beach - a district that is popular among tourists. This place seems to have been created for a relaxing, but at the same time interesting rest.

The nearest airport is 45 km away. The hotel, as well as travel agencies, provide travelers with a convenient shuttle service, so there is no need to worry about the distance. The area is calm, and next to the hotel complex there are many shops, cafes, restaurants, entertainment and health facilities. And the picturesque Kata Beach is located just 600 meters, which, of course, is an advantage.

What does the hotel area look like?





Ibis Phuket Kata 3 Hotel consists of a large five-story building, which resembles the letter "G" in shape. The first floor is reserved for reception, bars, restaurants and lounges, while the upper floors have residential rooms. There are working elevators, which is important for many tourists, especially travelers with disabilities.

The hotel territory is fenced and closed, only guests can spend time here. Its area is about 9.5 thousand square meters. In the courtyard there is a swimming pool, terraces for relaxation, playgrounds. The territory itself is well-groomed, beautiful and well-designed. You can relax in the shade of palm trees and enjoy the beauty of exotic flowers. By the way, the hotel was opened not so long ago - in 2010. Since then, several modernizations have been carried out here, today this place meets international standards.

Description and photos of rooms





The hotel guarantees its guests accommodation in spacious rooms. Reviews indicate that the rooms are kept clean and tidy, and they are equipped at home. The design is simple and straightforward, but at the same time pleasant. There is a necessary set of furniture, including large, comfortable beds with orthopedic mattresses, bedside tables, spacious wardrobes, tables and soft sofas. Some rooms have private balconies. They are small but secluded, here you can enjoy views of the garden, city and courtyard.

Of course, you can count on a set of household appliances, without which it is simply impossible to imagine the comfort of a modern traveler. For example, you can pass the evening while watching a flat-screen TV: for the most part, cable channels are shown here, but there is the opportunity to watch satellite TV. There is a modern air conditioning system, which, by the way, works almost silently.

There is a minibar in the room, but it is filled with drinks only upon request. Nonetheless, a daily supply of chilled drinking water is provided to guests free of charge. There is an electric kettle, as well as a set for making hot drinks, which includes tea, sugar, coffee and, of course, the necessary utensils. Guests can use a small safe with an electronic combination lock for free. There is also a telephone with the ability to dial directly (long-distance calls are paid separately). You can use the Internet, but this is a paid service.

The room comes with a small but fully equipped bathroom with a toilet, washbasin, shower, wall mirror and, of course, a set of clean towels, which are changed almost daily. Hot and cold water is supplied to all rooms around the clock.

Rooms are cleaned every day at a specific time or at the request of guests. Bed linen is changed several times a week. Room service, as well as the reception desk are open around the clock: you can get information or order something in the room. High quality service and comfortable rooms are what Ibis Phuket Kata 3 offers. Phuket is a really good place for a pleasant holiday by the sea.

Meals for guests





Living in comfortable rooms and delicious breakfasts is what Ibis Phuket Kata Hotel 3 offers its guests. Traveler reviews say that they feed well on the hotel. Every morning, tourists are invited to the restaurant, which pleasantly impresses with its elegant atmosphere and coziness.

The dishes on the menu are simple but satisfying. Fresh pastries, delicious sausages, Thai noodles, beans and potatoes to garnish, fresh vegetable salads, a large selection of exotic fruits, as well as desserts, cold and hot drinks. There is always a choice, and the food is freshly prepared and tasty. There is no children's menu, but you can always choose something for the child. By the way, in the local bar you can also order light snacks, but they, of course, are paid separately.

From time to time, theme nights are held at the hotel restaurant, serving dishes from different Asian and European countries. Such events are not organized every day, but guests say that they are worth a visit.

Food in any case will not be a problem, since next to the Ibis Phuket Kata 3 hotel complex there are many cafes and restaurants for every taste. Travelers can enjoy traditional Thai dishes or find a place where they serve more familiar European cuisine. By the way, the fish market is not so far away, which is definitely worth a visit to lovers of fresh seafood: here you can enjoy the seafood prepared right before your eyes. And the prices are good.

Where is the beach located?





The Ibis Phuket Kata Hotel 3 hotel complex is located on the second coastline, the sea is about 600 m away. A convenient walking path leads to the beach. Tourists can enjoy an unhurried walk while watching the leisurely life of local residents and holiday travelers. Near the coast, children of all ages are constantly swimming and having fun. Of course, a rescue team works on the beach. There are cafes and bars where you can eat for a small fee. Renting sun loungers and umbrellas is quite inexpensive, and a beach towel can be obtained free of charge at the hotel.

Water recreation: what awaits the traveler?

Sunbathing, swimming in the warm waters of the sea - what could be better in order to relax, rest and improve health? But what to do for those tourists who prefer more active types of recreation? A portion of vivid impressions can be obtained by skiing or banana, doing parasailing. Here, tourists can choose boat excursions: for a small fee you can visit the most picturesque and secluded sections of the coast, it is pleasant to relax in the company of other travelers and local guides. According to reviews, you can have a great time very close to Ibis Phuket Kata 3. Phuket is a wonderful place for all kinds of tourism. Ibis Phuket Kata 3 Hotel (Thailand, Kata, Phuket): Description of Additional Services





Of course, this is a small hotel complex, which for the most part is used as a place to sleep. Nevertheless, guests can take advantage of some additional services to ensure maximum comfort.

To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel has a small, but convenient parking. There is also a car rental point where you can rent a car, motorcycle, scooter or bicycle. It is worth saying that most tourists travel around the city on rented transport, as this is the fastest and relatively cheap way to quickly and fully explore not only Phuket, but also the sights of the surrounding area.

Internet is available at the hotel, but this is a paid service. By the way, the prices at the hotel are relatively affordable: you will have round-the-clock access to the network, which, of course, is important for almost every modern traveler. If you do not have devices with you, you can visit the local Internet cafe. The business center in the hotel is small, but guests have the opportunity to send a fax, use certain equipment, pay for postal services, etc. Small conference rooms host various meetings, seminars, business meetings, and special occasions. By the way, employees for a small fee can undertake the organization of the holiday. Sometimes even weddings are held here.

There is no medical office in the hotel. In case of health problems, you should contact the staff at the reception, they will quickly call you a professional, experienced doctor, whose services, of course, are paid separately. By the way, here you can call a taxi or even arrange a transfer to the airport.

Entertainment of tourists on site

Do not forget about sightseeing holidays. Thailand is an amazing country with a hospitable population, an interesting history and a unique culture. Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?

Many parents travel with their children. Of course, in such cases, the important question becomes whether it is worth taking a child with you to Ibis Phuket Kata 3. Phuket is a city that is the best suited for families.
