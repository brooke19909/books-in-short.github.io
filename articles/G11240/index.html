Alanya. Hotel "Ozkaymak Inkekum". Accommodation in rooms

The hotel complex has 350 rooms, of which:

  • 310 - standard (23 sq. M), which can accommodate a maximum of 3 people;

All rooms at the hotel are carpeted. Each room is equipped with a TV, minibar, air conditioning and a balcony. It offers views of the mountains, pool or sea. The bathrooms include a hairdryer and toiletries. An extra rollaway bed is available upon request. The rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen is changed - three times a week.

The main restaurant operates on a buffet system and is designed for 400 people. The assortment includes both dietary and vegetarian dishes.
