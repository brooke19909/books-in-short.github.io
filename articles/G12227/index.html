Trust Bank: customer reviews

This institution began its existence in 1995 as a subsidiary of Menatep. Since March 2005, it has been an independent institution under a new name: National Trust Bank OJSC.



The main shareholder is UK Trust CJSC (over 98%). One of the main advantages of this enterprise is the widely developed geography of representative offices and branches throughout Russia. Trust Bank receives feedback from customers in 170 cities, and throughout Russia has established itself as a reliable partner. A powerful network is actively servicing 200 points of sale. More than 28 thousand different organizations have used the services of the bank.

The main activities

For the main activity, the bank chose such areas as the development of small and medium-sized businesses. The reviews about Trust Bank show that it is the main partner for small entrepreneurs. Another well-developed area in which the company operates is the investment services market. In this area, the bank has won one of the strongest positions in Russia. For its clients, Trust will be able to offer a full range of services in this area. This institution is also the largest producer of international plastic cards in the Russian Federation. For individuals, it is part of the state deposit insurance system .
