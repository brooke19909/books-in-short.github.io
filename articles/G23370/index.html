Navigator "Garmin eTrex 10": reviews, instructions

An ideal tourist navigator called "Garmin eTrex 10" started its own work back in 2012 and since then has gathered enough real fans. Of the entire series, it was this model that exceeded not only the previous options, but also many other manufacturers.



"Garmin eTrex 10"

In general terms, a lot can be said about the device, but we will highlight only its main functions and capabilities. The unique navigator "Garmin eTrex 10" has an excellent set of functions and a fairly robust design, and you should not forget about the availability and up to 25 hours of operation. In the new model, significant improvements were noticeable in the interface, adding a world map, geocaching without any paper notes.

There is also a processor with a fairly high sensitivity and universal technology. The navigator itself is able to determine the location of the owner instantly and fairly accurately. It communicates with specific satellites, so even in the wilderness of forests, ravines or near high structures, the signal will be determined with good accuracy.

Usability

The new "Garmin eTrex 10" reviews have excellent thanks to the ease of use and the simple interface available to everyone. The overall dimensions of the device are quite compact, so you can carry it anywhere in the pocket with you. Also, the housing design itself has certain recesses and bulges that help create comfort at the time when a person holds it in his hand.

The device is equipped with a new display model that displays an image that is visually pleasing to the reader. Absolutely any lighting conditions do not interfere with the brightness and clarity of the image.



Accidental placement in water for half an hour or a little more will not worsen its work, and for the entire time spent in the liquid, the system will not stop. And durability, which is the highlight of the manufacturer itself, reliably protects against the penetration of dust, dirt and moisture to internal parts.

Geocaching

A very good plus in Garmin eTrex 10 is that it has support for any GPX geocaching files. This makes it possible to download various caches in any places, as well as their descriptions directly to the device.

Special sensors built into the navigator save and quickly display on the screen all the basic information of interest to the user. This display includes all the hints, descriptions, difficulty, and the exact location. All this will help tourists who like to travel to unusual places, not knowing the exact path.

Due to the presence of geocaching, the user consciously refuses to write on plain paper, thereby preserving nature. At the same time, the efficiency of geocaching is significantly increased, which allows to improve the operation of the device.

Traveling around the world

The portable navigator of the new model called "Garmin eTrex 10" is designed for general consumption. It is capable of simultaneously receiving signals from both GPS satellites and Glonass (the system of the Russian Federation). The new Russian system finds the desired object in time 20% faster than the standard world system.

When using two systems, the signals received together increase to 24, unlike the only GPS system.



Total information

The excellent model of the device has a black and white display with 2.2 inches, as well as excellent resolution. Navigator "Garmin eTrex 10" is controlled with a single joystick and several buttons located on the side.

The memory contains about 1000 waypoints, set both manually and automatically. In addition, the body of the device has strong protection against moisture, as well as rubberized buttons and plugs for ports.

The monochrome type of display and high-sensitivity receiver provide excellent work without errors and any interference. Power is supplied using a pair of AA batteries.
