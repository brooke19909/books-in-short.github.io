Sound Amplifier DLS MA41

Very often from motorists you can hear the following question: "Which sound amplifier is better to install in a car?" This article is about the DLS MA41 four-channel amplifier . These devices are produced by the famous Swedish company DLS. They have long won the respect of motorists and professional experts. Last year, the amplifiers of the most popular DLS MA41 series underwent restyling. On the shelves of the stores you can see updated devices with a spectacular design and great sound.



Amplifier DLS MA41 is an ideal solution for increasing the sound power of the speaker system of any modern car. Such a device with a load of 4/2 Ohm is capable of delivering 4x75 / 125 watts. But it is worthwhile to understand that the great value of the rated power is not the only advantage of the DLS MA41 amplifier. A major advantage of this speaker system is a high level of sound quality. This model belongs to class AB amplifiers, which indicates its excellent quality. And the proof of this statement is the high value of the signal-to-noise ratio - 100dB.

It should be understood that the interior of the car is a very difficult acoustic environment, therefore, installation and proper tuning of the sound system in such conditions is a very delicate matter. If you succeed, the result will exceed all expectations. Indeed, the DLS MA41 amplifiers are equipped with various types of filters (high-frequency 50-5000Hz and low-frequency 15-5000Hz), as well as a special system designed to enhance bass - "GrandBass". The DLS speaker system can easily distribute frequencies on each channel individually. For example, you can weaken the range of speech for the subwoofer, and for speakers to strengthen it. The crossover built into the amplifier gives great opportunities for individual settings, as well as various acoustic experiments that reflect the user's preferences.





The amplifier housing DLS MA41 is made of silver aluminum alloy without a decorative coating. The company logo is shaded with red enamel. Thick-walled slabs are milled with diamond tools, a powerful body perfectly removes excess heat from the output stages of the device. However, the main circuit and technical characteristics and solutions, switching, energy and functional capabilities of the device remained almost unchanged.

DLS amplifiers have a very compact design that provides greater system flexibility during installation. It can be placed both under the car seat and in the luggage compartment. Ease of installation is another positive feature of the DLS MA41 amplifier.





Audio enthusiasts' comments about this device note that the system has a very reliable electronic circuit for protecting against overload of output channels and short circuits, convenient and intuitive remote control, and an interesting implementation of automatic remote on or off when a signal is sent to a high-level input without additional connection of individual controllers wires.
