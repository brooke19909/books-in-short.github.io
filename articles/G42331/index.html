Crochet sweatshirts: general recommendations

Before you begin, you need to learn some very important nuances, without which you can make a lot of mistakes. So, you need to understand the rules for selecting a hook to the threads. The hook should be two orders of magnitude thicker than the thread. Here is a simple rule. Compliance with it will help to create a beautiful canvas, with loosely arranged loops. When crocheting sweatshirts, you need to take care of the amount of yarn. A small sample is made for calculation. Usually they knit a cloth measuring 10 by 10 cm. What should be crocheted sweatshirts for women



A product made by yourself, first of all, must be beautiful and original. To do this, you need to choose a yarn that will look good in the selected product. For example, cotton is suitable for a summer blouse ; linen will also be a good option. For a winter product, it is better to take wool. Openwork jacket will be beautiful from mohair. Spring product can be made of acrylic. This thread is well worn, does not require complicated care. It is recommended for all beginners to create the first product from acrylic thread.
