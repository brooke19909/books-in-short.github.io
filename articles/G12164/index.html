Smartphone Lenovo S850: reviews, specs and a review of the model

The famous Lenovo brand often has interesting and noteworthy products, and this applies not only to the laptop segment, but also to the mobile market, where the Chinese company demonstrates respect for its client and stable growth in demand.





Even despite the high competition in this area, Lenovo finds the opportunity to create truly high-quality and noteworthy models, which include 850-series smartphones.

So, the hero of today's review is the Lenovo S850: specifications and photos, design and ergonomics, as well as expert opinions, together with the reviews of ordinary gadget owners, are presented in the article.

Positioning

The series can be attributed to the number of typical middle-class devices, where the original approach is clearly visible and the standard (which does not mean at all that is bad) filling for its level. Most gadgets in this category have the same technical specifications, and the only difference is their price, which can vary depending on the positioning of the model. Therefore, it is especially nice to get a pretty smartphone for yourself, which also has the original differences, which are emphasized in the Lenovo S850.





User reviews positively note the presence of such chips as a glass back surface, analogues of which can be found among dear brothers from Sony and Huawei, and the original highlight of the company logo. You can also see other distinctive features of the gadget.

Design

Even in spite of its origin, which many smartphone owners associate with something not very high quality and cheap, the model makes a pretty good impression. This applies not only to the design of the Lenovo S850. Owner reviews are full of positive aspects about the appearance and ergonomics of the smartphone, and judging by the number of such statements, the brand copes well with improving the image by promoting its devices not only due to the low price, but also due to modernization, taking into account the opinions of ordinary users.





The smartphone at first glance does not seem too large or vice versa small, although it has a not-so-standard 5-inch size. It can be compared with the same ‚ÄúNexus‚Äù of the fifth version, but in our case the model turned out to be a little higher and a little thicker.

The gadget is presented on the market in three main colors: bright red, blue and white, and the first option is clearly to face Lenovo S850. Reviews on this subject are all different, but most users still prefer white for the sake of modesty than a stylish, but defiant red tone.

Screen

With a 5-inch screen, the smartphone has a very good resolution of 1280 by 720 pixels. And all this works on a high-quality IPS matrix designed specifically for the Lenovo S850 (photo and angle overview at an appropriate level). The image does not fade when the phone is rotated, and the photo image is not distorted.

There is a solid margin of brightness with a good density of dots. In addition, the smartphone is equipped with anti-reflective coating, so on a sunny day the gadget feels great. Oleophobic protection is also provided, which after work allows you to clean the screen of Lenovo S850. Reviews of protective coatings are mostly positive: the gadget does not collect dust, small scratches and other dirt from a pocket or purse.

Camera

The device is equipped with a decent 12-megapixel camera, which is complemented by autofocus and flash. The front part can take photos with a maximum resolution of 5 megapixels, which selfie lovers will surely appreciate.





The quality of the shooting itself can be called average, but the company promised to fix this item with firmware in subsequent models, so do not rush to mark this part as a minus.

Media

Musical characteristics are also close to the average in this segment. For the money you spend on a smartphone, there are more interesting sound solutions among competitors, but the gadgets themselves will lose on other points that are no less important than music.

There is a functional radio as a pleasant addition, as well as a rich gallery and video section with an interesting design and a more understandable interface compared to previous lines. Nevertheless, many users in their reviews complain about poor codecs, so in order to avoid unpleasant situations, it is better to use third-party software from the same "Google Play", since there are plenty of various player options there.

Summarizing

Against the background of a large number of similar gadgets in the segment, Lenovo gave its fans a breath of fresh air in the face of Lenovo S850. Where to buy a novelty? The most proven option, coupled with a wide selection / selection of the right model from the series, is Yandex.Market. You can wander on the shelves of "Aliexpress", but there will be obvious problems with the translation.

The smartphone has an ordinary stuffing that can be found in almost any representative of this segment. Some users note as a minus a non-removable battery, while others like it on the contrary. In any case, the smartphone justifies its investment and is a well-balanced gadget for 10 thousand rubles.
