Hotel Barcelona Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam): photos and reviews

Hotel Barcelona Hotel 3 (Nha Trang) is located in the European central quarter of Vietnam's most beautiful city. From the height of a multi-storey building, amazing views of the sea and the city. The hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable and unforgettable vacation.

Nha trang

The small resort town of Nha Trang has recently become very popular among Russian tourists. It attracts vacationers with its snow-white beaches, famous mud baths, and a wide variety of day and night entertainment.





Nha Trang is a unique place that will appeal to both lovers of a relaxing holiday, and those who prefer active entertainment. Here you can completely plunge into the exotic Asian atmosphere, get acquainted with the cultural, historical, architectural and religious attractions of Vietnam.

Excursions

There are quite a lot of Russian travel agencies in Nha Trang. Here, guests of the city are offered a variety of trips and excursions not only to Nha Trang, but also to Laos and Cambodia. The following group excursions are very popular:

  • Sightseeing tour program includes visits to the main attractions of the city.
  • A trip to the northern islands will give vacationers the opportunity to get acquainted with the exotic flora and fauna of Vietnam.
  • A day trip to the southern islands is entertaining. Fans of beach sports, diving and snorkeling choose it.
  • Visit the village of Young Bay, where there is an eco-park with waterfalls, thermal springs and a crocodile farm.
  • An excursion to the town of Dalat - this is waterfalls, extreme in the form of a cable car, a walk along the "Valley of Love" and shopping.




The above tours are recommended to be visited as a group. The rest of the sights are better to see on their own.

sights

In Nha Trang, there are simply a huge number of architectural, religious, historical, modern, natural and other attractions. What to see first? List of the most interesting and visited attractions of Nha Trang:

  • Tourist complex and museum of Villa Bao Dai.
  • Mud clinic Thap Ba.
  • Oceanographic Institute.
  • Cathedral of St. Mary.
  • Hong Lao Monkey Island
  • Buddhist temple Pagoda Long Sean.
  • Tourist complex Winperl (amusement park).
  • Po Nagar Towers (Mount Ku Lao).




In addition, you can visit one of the many museums in Nha Trang. Acquaintance with the sights of the city is a great opportunity to immerse yourself in the Asian atmosphere of Vietnam, to touch its history and religion a little.

Hotel Barcelona Hotel 3

Hotel Barcelona Hotel 3 (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is located in the city center on the third coastline. The hotel is a 14-story building with an equipped rooftop pool. The sandy city beach is a 5-7 minute walk from the hotel. The beach is very clean and well maintained. The equipment on it is paid. Hotel Barcelona Hotel 3 (Nha Trang) offers its guests a cozy and enjoyable stay. A wide range of services has been created for a comfortable pastime. Within walking distance from the hotel there are prestigious shops, markets, cozy restaurants and cafes, diving centers, etc.

Number of rooms

Guests can place in one of fifty rooms of Barcelona Hotel 3 (Nha Trang). Description of room categories:

  • Premium Suite (50 sq. M.).
  • Deluxe (28 sq. M.). Sea view room.
  • Superior room (28 sq. M.).
  • Deluxe (28 sq. M.). City view room.




All apartments are equipped with a fully equipped minibar, wardrobe, kettle, hairdryer, TV (there are Russian channels) and a safe. Guests have access to free Wi-Fi. Rooms are cleaned daily. The modern interior design is in all apartments of Barcelona Hotel 3 (Nha Trang). Photos show a stylish combination of colors. The undoubted advantage of all rooms is the presence of a large window, which makes the room bright and comfortable. If desired, the window can be closed with special dimming plain curtains, which are also in every room.

Infrastructure

Hotel Barcelona Hotel 3 (Nha Trang) has a developed infrastructure. The complex offers free parking and car or bicycle rental. Transfer to / from the airport is possible.

For tourists who stay at Barcelona Hotel 3 (Nha Trang), tours include free buffet meals (breakfast). Room service or room service can be ordered for a fee. The restaurant is located on the ground floor of the hotel, the menu is varied and includes dishes not only of local cuisine, but also from different countries of the world.





Barcelona Hotel 3 has its own conference room. It is located on the 10th floor. Guests can use the laundry facilities, visit the spa and fitness centers, take a steam bath in the sauna and swim in the hotel's rooftop outdoor pool. For babysitting there are professional babysitters. The hotel has a tour desk, currency exchange and gift shop.

Reviews

Tourists who rested at the Barcelona Hotel 3 (Nha Trang) leave positive and negative reviews .

Positive information:

  • The hotel is in the center, near many cafes, restaurants and shops.
  • The beach is located very close, despite the fact that Barcelona Hotel 3 (Nha Trang) is located on the 3rd coastline. Walking to it in an unhurried step takes no more than 10 minutes.
  • The room has everything you need for a comfortable stay: a refrigerator and a kettle, air conditioning, a TV with a Russian channel, a hairdryer, cups, even a toothbrush and toothpaste can be provided upon request.
  • Very friendly staff. All arising issues are resolved promptly, settlement is very fast.
  • There is a pool on the roof, although it is small but clean. There are sun loungers nearby, you can swim and sunbathe.
  • Bed linen and towels are changed regularly, cleaned every day.
  • The windows of the upper floors offer scenic views of the sea or the city.

Negative reviews are left mainly by hotel guests who choose to stay in one-room apartments on the lower floors. These rooms are very small and lack windows. Also, many were confused by the mirror wall, which in some rooms divides the room and the toilet.





On the food in the hotel Barcelona Hotel 3 reviews are contradictory. Not everyone speaks positively of the national Vietnamese cuisine, and to someone the quantity of food offered for breakfast seemed insufficient. But there are reviews in which, on the contrary, vacationers talk about the diversity in nutrition and its excellent preparation.

In general, rest in Barcelona Hotel 3 leaves more positive impressions. And the inconvenience from small shortcomings can be fully compensated by beautiful walks in Nha Trang and delightful entertainment. Almost all tourists in their reviews write about the desire to re-visit this city, which gives guests an unforgettable and vivid experience.
