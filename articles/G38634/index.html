Arab Emirates, Dubai. Holidays in Dubai

The United Arab Emirates (Dubai) in the modern world is an exciting combination of contrasts, where the old and the new, East and West, the modern rhythm of life and ancient traditions are closely intertwined. All this attracts a huge number of tourists.



Long before oil was found, this "paradise" gained a reputation as a "city of merchants", always welcoming travelers and merchants who arrived in the United Arab Emirates. Hotels (Dubai) delight with their comfort and diversity, and the streets are clean and safe. This city has received several times the award "the safest place on the planet."

The United Arab Emirates is a dynamically growing global commercial center. This is the place where international exhibitions and conferences are held. Its role is difficult to overestimate. Here every year the Dubai Trade Festival is held with great success. More than 2 million visitors come to it. Also, the festival "Summer Surprises" is organized here, where tourists from all over the world are waiting for exhibitions of the latest fashion goods, unimaginable sales, all kinds of cultural and entertainment shows for children and adults, opportunities for individual, group and family vacations. Therefore, tours to Dubai are in great demand. More on this later.



The main attractions of Dubai

Here everyone can find a lot of interesting things for themselves. Without a doubt, this place impresses tourists with a huge variety of attractions.



  • The central territory of modern Dubai is divided into two areas - these are Bur (southern) and Deira (northern). In general, each part of this city has a lot of interesting things. In the old district of Bur, you should see Bastakia. Here you can see many Arab traditional dwellings with courtyards, wind towers, once replacing air conditioners.
  • It is impossible to visit this city and not look into the Dubai Historical Museum, the insignificant fort Al Fahidi, who managed to visit the palace, the garrison and even the prison. There are extensive collections of objects of everyday life as the Bedouins of ancient times, and modern residents of Dubai. The marine and underground parts of this exposition also operate.
  • The pearl of Dubai is the palace of Sheikh Zayed, who was the first ruler of the city. So that he could observe the ships, his palace, surrounded by wind towers, was built on the seashore.
  • Another attraction is the Jumeirah Mosque. She is unusually beautiful in the light of special evening illumination. This building is a striking example of Islamic architecture.
  • Another recognizable antiquity is Burj Nahar - a watchtower guarding the old city. This building is worth it in the picturesque gardens of Deira. She is a favorite subject for photographing numerous tourists.
  • Undoubtedly, the Dubai World Trade Center can be called a miracle of modern architecture. Hundreds of tourists visit the observation deck of this 39-story building every day.
  • Beautiful parks and gardens of the city of Dubai (UAE). The most significant are Jumeirah Beach Park, Dubai Crixide Park, Mushrif Park, Raz Al Mamzar Park and Safa Park. The local zoo is rightfully considered the best in the Middle East.
  • Rest in Dubai can be endlessly diversified. One of the most famous water parks in Asia is the Wonderland amusement park. Feel free to explore any of the thirty attractions - from a fun carousel for your children to a roller coaster for yourself - and enjoy a carefree pastime.
  • The Wonderland Amusement Park includes Splashland Water Park, Spray Land, which has a common ticket. It allows you to use any rides without restrictions.
  • Holidays in Dubai include visits to the ethnographic museums of the emirate. Near the bay is the "Heritage Village". In it you can observe the work of craftsmen. Fresh mountain air, clear cool lakes, the ancient picturesque fortress of Hatta Fort, Juma Mosque, the oldest in Arabia - this picturesque place will undoubtedly attract the attention of every tourist.

  • The most famous are the City Center, Al-Huber (Deira). They operate about 300 stores.
  • No less popular is the Gold Souk ("Gold Market"). It is also visited by many tourists. Many consider it a global center for retailing gold products and precious stones.

The mode of operation of shopping centers and methods of payment for goods

It is important to know. Large shopping centers are generally open until ten in the evening, sometimes around the clock. Department stores, markets, boutiques, and certain stores operate on Fridays - on the official day off in the UAE. All large and most small trading points accept credit cards. It is also possible to pay in US dollars. But we must remember that the United Arab Emirates is an eastern country, the largest discounts can be obtained by paying in cash in local currency.

Dubai shopping festival

Vacationers with experience purchase tours to Dubai in the spring, namely in March-April. At this time, the Trade Festival is held there, lasting a whole month. Sales provide unimaginable opportunities for the purchase of all categories of goods.

A lot of entertainment programs - shows, lotteries, horse races, as well as bullfighting will not let you get bored in between shopping trips. At numerous sales, discounts on goods can often reach seventy percent, and on hotel stays - forty; air carriers often increase their baggage allowance.

Restaurants

There is no person who, having arrived in the United Arab Emirates - Dubai, would remain indifferent to the local cuisine, to its magnificent variety.

In the UAE, it is not difficult to choose a restaurant - there are a huge number of them for any, most demanding, taste - from chic to simple. Most of all, of course, are Arab. Although you can find both Russian and Italian restaurants, there are also Irish pubs. The quality of products in these institutions is strictly monitored by the Sanitary and Epidemiological Service. Arabic cuisine has rich traditions, and the number of delicious dishes cannot be counted and is worthy of a separate detailed story. Everyone here can pick up something of their own free will. Seafood from the Arabian Gulf and the Indian Ocean is constantly popular among tourists. They are in great demand. You will always be served the freshest seafood - perfectly cooked crab, lobster, shrimp, tuna. Impossible to resist local desserts. Making coffee is an art, and you should try it in one of the many Arabian cafes. Prices, as elsewhere, are very different - from affordable $ 8-10 in a hotel to $ 50 or more in a five-star hotel for a regular lunch. In numerous street restaurants and cafes, this stands, of course, below. This is a characteristic of the United Arab Emirates (Dubai). Here you can safely drink tap water, it is completely safe. Nevertheless, most hotels and restaurants offer visitors mineral water from local producers.

Alcohol

This product has certain restrictions. The UAE is a Muslim country, therefore, alcohol can be bought almost freely only in hotels, bars and restaurants. Alcohol is prohibited on open sale. This is especially true for the United Arab Emirates. Dubai in this regard is the most democratic city. For those who do not drink alcohol, there is always a huge selection of very fresh cocktails made from a variety of fruits. It is allowed to import into the country no more than two liters of alcoholic beverages. It is important to remember that drinking alcohol in public places, on the streets, beaches, while driving is prohibited. Violation of this rule can lead to a very large fine, and sometimes to imprisonment.

clothing

In Dubai, local people appreciate modesty in this regard, which is especially true of the fair half. In the city, tourists should abandon too frank clothes: short skirts, shorts and deeply low-cut dresses; topless is not allowed to sunbathe, wearing a scarf on the head is welcome.

Behavior

Visitors during Ramadan eat and drink in special restaurants for foreigners or in a hotel. Also, tourists should remember that in public places it is not supposed to smoke and chew gum. Noisy entertainment is also prohibited in Ramadan. In the UAE, they are very clean: for the garbage that the tourist imprudently throws on the street, a fine of 500 dirhams may be imposed.

Dubai (United Arab Emirates): photos

Strictness is present in this regard, as some rules must be observed. In this country, it is forbidden to photograph the palaces of the sheikhs, any state institutions and, of course, military facilities. A serious violation is an attempt to film local women.



Tip

If you have chosen the United Arab Emirates (Dubai) for your vacation, then you need to remember the indicated features of this peculiar country. It is best to plan this trip from October to April, when there is still no sweltering heat. In this case, you can completely do with summer clothes.

However, it must be remembered that in the evenings in winter the temperature drops sharply. Then in this case, warm clothes can be very useful.

Conclusion

After reviewing the above, almost everyone will have a great desire to visit such a "paradise" as Dubai (UAE).

Prices for tours in this direction are quite acceptable, that is, from 14 to 45 thousand rubles, depending on the company providing these services, the number of vacationers, etc.
