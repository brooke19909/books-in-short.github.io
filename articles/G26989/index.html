Guest house "Surf" (Divnomorskoe): description, reviews, photos

The desire for a comfortable stay is inherent in each of us. I would like to spend the long-awaited vacation in an exciting and interesting way away from the annoying fuss. So that the rest does not turn into a complete disappointment, many carefully plan their departure, analyze the quality of hotel complexes based on the feedback of people who have visited a particular corner of the world.

You can often find rave reviews about the village of Divnomorsky, located near the city of Gelendzhik. The resort got such a romantic name thanks to the amazingly clean and warm sea. Year-round sanatoriums and pensions are crowded with visitors who visit them to restore strength and health.





In addition to a full-fledged treatment, there are many entertaining, leisure places for a young audience. Natural beauty attracts ecotourism lovers. For maximum comfort, choose proven guest houses or hotels. For example, the guest house “Priboy” (Divnomorskoe), recommended by many tourists, is located in the center of the village, next to a rich infrastructure.

Brief location information

The complex is comfortably located in a busy place at a minimum distance from the main objects of the village. Within walking distance are shopping galleries, supermarkets, souvenir and grocery stores. Nearby is a bus stop and coastline. Hotel "Surf" (Divnomorskoe) has a fenced landscaped area, which is guarded around the clock.





The hotel administration provides its guests with the opportunity to spend their holidays in complete harmony and friendly atmosphere. The main concept of the complex is a combination of home comfort, friendly service and a modern interior. It is recommended for a romantic, family, friendly weekend.

Accommodations





Guest house "Surf" (Divnomorskoe) offers accommodation in neat renovated rooms, which are concentrated in a three-story building. The rooms are divided according to the level of comfort. All rooms are decorated in pleasant colors and equipped with necessary furniture. Regardless of the choice of their category, tourists will be surprised by the cleanliness and order.

Maids clean the rooms daily; towels and bedding are changed on a schedule. Available double rooms with a toilet and a refrigerator on the floor at the best price. The pride of the hotel is five-bed apartment with two rooms, a large balcony, an entrance hall, a bedroom and a living room. This room features climate control, a fridge and kettle.

Catering

A spacious dining room with self-service and a wide range of dishes is open in the building of the Priboy hotel (Divnomorskoye). Reviews about the diet tourists leave in a positive way. Visitors were satisfied with the quality of the products, friendly attitude. The food is always fresh, varied, prepared by professional chefs. The menu includes children's and meatless dishes. There are cafes and restaurants 50 meters away, where hotel guests can taste delicious and inexpensive national treats.

Coastline





Due to the small size of the village, all hotel complexes are close to the Black Sea. Guest house Priboy (Divnomorskoe) is 70 meters from the public beach. The beach is strewn with small pebbles, equipped with sunbeds, showers, and a toilet. The entrance to the water is gentle, convenient and safe for children.

There are food stalls all over the beach, bars, cafes and restaurants. There is a large water park, aeration, a rental point for the rental of catamarans, jet skis, beach equipment. Boat trips by boat, banana are organized daily. Lovers of tickling nerves can scuba dive to the bottom and watch the life of aquatic creatures.

Inland equipment





Hotel "Surf" (Divnomorskoe) is equipped with high-speed Internet. There is an underground parking equipped with a security system. In the middle of the courtyard there is a swimming pool with sun loungers. It features a tennis court. Everyone can freely visit the beauty salon to undergo cosmetic procedures. It is located in a nearby hotel called Dream.

Children's leisure





Excellent service for young tourists. This is probably why couples with their children are constantly resting here. A safe shallow pool with water slides of different sizes has been built for the guys. All entertaining elements are decorated in bright, colorful colors to cheer you up. A fountain in the form of a dolphin is installed in the center of the pool.

In addition to water activities, the guest house “Priboy” (Divnomorskoe) offers children to visit the playground with horizontal bars, swings, slides, sandboxes and other elements for active activities. There is also a closed complex for kids up to seven years old with a labyrinth and cubes.

Opinions of tourists about the rest

Guest house "Surf" (Divnomorskoe) regularly receives enthusiastic reviews from guests. Those who were lucky enough to visit this wonderful corner of the Krasnodar Territory were impressed. Impeccable service, friendly and friendly atmosphere in the hotel set in a favorable mood. All staff are attentive, courteous to their guests. There are no complaints about cleanliness, internal order, food and the services provided.
