Hotels in Tomsk in the city center: overview, addresses, reviews

Tomsk was formed in 1604, is the oldest city in Siberia. The rich history, interesting monuments of stone and wooden architecture, the beauty of the surrounding nature attract many tourists here annually. And hotels in Tomsk in the city center will delight you with their comfort and hospitality.

The hotel is a ten minute walk from the city center. Within walking distance from the hotel are supermarkets, cafes and restaurants. Free private parking is available for car owners. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. The hotel has a small shop and a vending machine with drinks. Ironing facilities are provided if necessary. For a fee, guests can relax in the sauna. The rooms are well soundproofed. The following options are offered to accommodate guests:

Each room at Extra Hotel is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a work desk, an electric kettle, and free toiletries. The hotel is located in Tomsk in the city center at the address: 21 Gorshkovsky Lane.

Hotel "Mandarin"

The beach on the Tom River from the hotel is only 15 minutes walk. Nearby are supermarkets, a market, cafes and restaurants. Guests can relax outdoors on the terrace. The hotel has a bar. Guests can leave their car in a free private guarded parking lot, which provides parking spaces for people with disabilities. The Mandarin Hotel has a currency exchange office, a mini market, vending machines for selling food and drinks, and ticket services. Laundry, ironing and shoe shine services are available at an extra charge. An airport shuttle is available upon request. For accommodation, several categories of rooms are offered:

  • single bed in a dormitory room - 900 rubles per day;
  • single - 1500 rubles per day;
  • double economy - 1800 rubles per day;
  • Standard Studio - 2400 rubles a day for two, and 2100 rubles a day for one person;
  • Superior Studio - 2600 rubles and 2300 rubles per day, respectively;
  • double with two double beds - 2700 rubles per day for two, and 3400 rubles per day for four people;
  • Studio with a large double bed (with breakfast) - 3300 rubles a day for two, and 2900 rubles a day for one person;
  • family room - 4900 rubles per day for three, and 5700 rubles per day for six people.

All rooms, except the studio with a large double bed, include accommodation only. At the request of guests, you can order a room taking into account the continental breakfast. Mandarin can be considered a good option if you are interested in inexpensive hotels in the center of Tomsk.
