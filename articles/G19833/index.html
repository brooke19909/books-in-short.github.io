How to make perfume at home - three simple recipes

Inhaling the aromas of blossoming linden flowers, a lavender field or a bouquet of lilacs, I want to keep this smell for a long time. Of course, you can buy essential oil. But it is much more pleasant if your favorite flair will be sealed with your own hands. And therefore the question arises: "How to make perfume at home?"

Source Composition





A bouquet presented by a loved one is easily transformed into the best perfumes in the world. How? Of course, with the help of perfume technology. To do this, you need fresh petals of your favorite flowers, citrus peel, freshly picked herbs. It‚Äôs best to start your experiments with monocomposites. Choosing a fragrance, you should choose the basis for it. The best solution is olive oil.

In order to create better perfumes, dark glassware is needed. Selected components are lowered into it and poured with oil until they are completely covered. If petals are used, then they are pre-crushed. After that, the container is covered with natural cloth and placed in a cool dark place for a day.

After the specified time, according to the recipe for how to make perfume at home, you should strain the suspension and leave to insist on the day again. At the end of the second day, the process of direct perfume production begins. To do this, vodka and the resulting aromatic oil are poured into dark glass bottles. Proportions depend on what they want to receive: for perfumes it is three to one, for perfumed water - four to one.

Composition of oils



The above method is suitable for creating your own flavor. A different situation arises if fashionable perfumes are reproduced, especially when it comes to their vintage designs.

For example, "Shalimar" from Guerlain, 1925. To create them, you will need several types of essential oils, such as bergamot (top note), rose, jasmine and iris (like heart notes), as well as vanilla, tonka bean and opoponax as base notes .

How to make perfume at home based on the famous composition? Of course, through experimentation. So, in a bottle of dark glass is poured 25 ml of alcohol or vodka. After they are added successively flavors of the indicated notes in the amount of 25 drops of each oil. Once the composition is completed, it should be removed in a dark place for two days, and then check the resulting aroma. If the notes are not heard, add the missed oils and put the bottle back in a dark place.

After the aroma is composed and aged, 2 teaspoons of water and 5 drops of glycerin are added to it to enhance the durability of perfumes.

Solid perfume



These perfumes are ideal for those who prefer mono-aromas, as well as exclusively natural ingredients in perfumes.

How to make perfume at home on a solid basis? In fact, the process of their manufacture is quite simple. They will need wax, an oil base (for example, vitamin E or jojoba oil, almond oil ) and, of course, any essential oil or composition.

In a steam bath in a glass dish, a mixture of finely grated wax and an oil base is gently melted. Use one tablespoon of each ingredient. While the wax is being heated, a perfume composition is being prepared. You can use one flavor or make an attractive combination. The claimed amount of wax will require 15 drops of perfume.

As soon as the mixture becomes homogeneous, the composed aromatic composition is added to it and mixed thoroughly. Without allowing the perfume to freeze, they are poured into metal or glass containers, in which perfumes will be stored in the future.

The presented methods of creating perfume are basic and can be changed as a result of work.
