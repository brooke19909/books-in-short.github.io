Hotel Club Big Blue Suite Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey): photos, reviews

Welcome to museums and attractions, monuments and natural masterpieces offers its guests welcoming Turkey with a large number of resorts, one of which is Alanya. A historic city with many ruins allows travelers to learn a lot about the place visited and about history in general.

The four-star Club Big Blue Suite Hotel is a unique symbiosis of comfortable living and rich pastime within the walls of the establishment. As noted by numerous guests in the reviews, the friendly, helpful and friendly staff from the first minutes of meeting sets up a wonderful, easy and carefree upcoming vacation.

Beach

In order to get to the beach, you need to make a path of 500 meters by crossing the roadway. A paid shuttle runs in both directions. However, according to the reviews of vacationers, a short walk is only a pleasure. Golden sands, a light sea breeze, a bronze tan - what is available to everyone here. The infrastructure includes sun loungers with umbrellas, towels, but these services are paid.

Here you can take a shower at the exit and use the changing cabins. Multiple attractions - riding in the ring, canoe, water scooters and banana - diversify the coastal leisure of vacationers: both children and adults. Beach volleyball is for activists and those who are used to alternating a relaxing holiday on deckchairs with an active pastime.

Children's leisure

Incredibly fun and provocatively, the kids will spend time in the walls of the Club Big Blue Suite Hotel 4 * (Alanya). An outdoor playground will give unforgettable emotions to every child. Slides, tolokars, swings and labyrinths are places where children forget about time.
