Bukovel hotels: overview, description, photos and reviews

Bukovel is a popular ski resort in Ukraine. It is located near the village of Polyanitsa. Its slopes are located at an altitude of more than 900 meters above sea level. The nearest large settlements are Ivano-Frankivsk and Yarechma. The famous peaks of the resort are the mountains of Black Cleva, Bukovel, Bulchineha.

Key information

Regular buses run from Lviv to these places. Travel time is about three hours. The skiing season in Bukovel begins relatively early, in mid-November. A stable crust in the offseason is provided by powerful snow cannons, which work almost the end of May.
