Nubian Village 5 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Sharm El Sheikh is one of the most popular tourist cities in Egypt. And the hotel complex called Nubian Village 5 is in great demand. Therefore, today, Russian tourists are increasingly interested in information about this place. Where is the hotel located? Who should rest on its territory? What services are offered to foreign tourists?

Where is the hotel situated?





The hotel is located in a picturesque bay called Nabq Bay, surrounded by palm trees, exotic plants, very close to the golden beaches. The nearest international airport is just five kilometers from the hotel complex, in the famous resort town of Sharm el-Sheikh. The distance to the less popular place among tourists, Naama Bay, is only 20 kilometers - if desired, you can easily get here by public transport or by rented car.

In fact, the location is considered one of the advantages of the Nubian Village 5. Sharm El Sheikh is a great place for various types of recreation, as it can offer travelers not only luxurious beaches, but also entertainment centers, high-class service and, of course, interesting historical sights worth seeing.

Short description of the hotel complex

A large well-groomed area with a huge amount of greenery is one of the many characteristics that the Nubian Village 5 hotel is famous for. Reviews of tourists confirm that the infrastructure of the complex is really convenient. In the center is a large main building, and around it are three-story residential buildings and small secluded bungalows. All of them are built in the traditional Nubian style, which gives the hotel a unique touch. And between the buildings there are narrow stucco-lined streets, secluded arbors and spacious courtyards with fountains.

By the way, the resort complex was opened in 1999. The last major overhaul was carried out in 2004. Nevertheless, every year the hotel is improving - here you will find not only a homely atmosphere and genuine hospitality, but also modern equipment and interesting design. There is another hotel located next to Nubian Village, Nubian Island 5. In fact, these two hotels represent a single large resort complex, since, regardless of the place of residence, travelers can use the territory of both hotels.

What do the rooms on the hotel look like?





Nubian Village Hotel 5 provides its guests with decent living conditions. On its territory there are 292 rooms of the following categories:

  • 174 Standard rooms.
  • 104 bungalows.
  • 12 Junior suites.
  • 2 King suites.

Regardless of the category, all rooms are furnished with the necessary furniture, including large beds with soft mattresses, a table, bedside tables, chairs and a wardrobe. The rooms are decorated in a restrained classic style. From each room there is access to a balcony or a private terrace, from where you can enjoy sea views, watch hotel life by the pool or enjoy the beauty of a flowering garden.

As for household appliances, here, of course, there is a complete set. To begin with, it is worth mentioning climate control - a modern air conditioning system with a convenient control panel will help create the most comfortable temperature conditions in the room. You can spend your free time watching TV - some Russian satellite channels are shown here. The room also has a small safe where guests in most cases keep documents and valuables. There is also a minibar, but its filling is carried out exclusively at the request of guests and is paid separately. You can make international calls by phone, but again, this service is paid.

Surely you will like the spacious bathroom. Here you can always relax in the foam bath or take a quick tonic shower - cold and hot water is served around the clock. There is also a wall mirror, lighting system, washbasin. Each hotel client receives a set of clean towels and a set of branded cosmetics, including soap and shampoo.

This is exactly what the rooms look like on the territory of the five-star Nubian Village 5. Hotel reviews of tourists confirm that the rooms are in excellent condition - the furniture and appliances are in good condition, high-quality cleaning is carried out daily. In addition, hotel employees regularly change both towels and bed linen.

Meal plan for guests

Many travelers believe that this hotel has gathered all the best that Egypt can offer. Nubian Village 5 offers its guests a system known as all-inclusive. Accommodation in the hotel complex includes three meals a day, as well as local drinks and light snacks throughout the day.





Guests can enjoy buffet meals at the main restaurant. Here you will find a huge variety of dishes of international and national cuisine - these are cold snacks, and several types of meat, and seafood, as well as fresh fruits, light salads, delicious desserts and, of course, home-made ice cream. There is also a special menu for children, which is also important. And you will be served by polite and attentive waiters.

In addition, there are several more catering facilities at the hotel. In particular, you can visit the Corallo eatery, which offers mainly Italian cuisine, including freshly baked pizza, salads, spaghetti and several varieties of pasta. And on the seashore is the El Nour fish restaurant, which operates in the evening - here travelers are offered fish and seafood dishes.

On Tuesdays and Fridays, El Dawar Restaurant, created in a traditional Egyptian style, opens its doors for tourists. Here you can not only enjoy the exquisite delicacies of oriental cuisine, but also enjoy exotic African performances. By the way, you need to reserve tables in advance, and ordered dishes are paid separately.

Naturally, there are several bars at the hotel. For example, two of them are located near the pool - here guests are offered juices, chilled drinks, ice cream and light snacks. A lobby bar is perfect for gatherings with friends - here, in a relaxed atmosphere, you can chat while enjoying the taste of coffee, strong drinks and interesting exotic cocktails.

In the evening, near the beach you can see a colorful Bedouin awning, under which guests are offered delicious teas, real oriental coffee and hookah.

Beach and water recreation

Soft sandy beaches and clear sea waters are what give the resorts a special charm. Nubian Village 5 owns its own, albeit small, section of the coast. The distance to the beach is not more than one hundred meters.

It is worth noting that there is a picturesque lagoon of natural origin with a gentle, safe descent into the sea - this place is suitable for swimming for children and travelers who do not know how to swim. On the other hand, there is a coral reef near the shore, so you need suitable shoes for swimming. By the way, if you did not bring it with you, then you can go to local shops - you will definitely find what you need.





There are protective umbrellas and sun loungers with comfortable mattresses on the shore - guests of the Nubian Village 5 can use them for free. And since the beach is private and fenced, outside visitors will not be bored.

Of course, the hotel complex can offer other types of water activities, in addition to swimming and sunbathing. For example, the hotel has a diving center. For beginners, there is a special training program that includes learning the basic rules and the first dive in the pool. Those tourists who do not need additional lessons can rent a mask, flippers, as well as the necessary equipment for diving. It is worth noting that the coral reef looks amazingly beautiful.

Naturally, for an additional fee you can engage in other types of recreation, including boating, catamarans, jet skis and skis, etc. In short, no one will be bored here.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?

Of course, parents traveling with children primarily prefer those hotels that are suitable for families. And the Nubian Village 5 hotel is a great place for a bright, unforgettable children's vacation. Of course, the owners of the hotel first of all took care of the comfort of their parents. For example, upon request, a baby bed will be delivered to the room immediately - this service is free.

The restaurant has a huge selection of dishes that are sure to suit your child, especially when it comes to desserts. And for a little tourist, polite waiters will certainly offer a comfortable high chair. If for some reason you need to leave for a while, you can safely leave the child with a nanny - only experienced and professional teachers work at the hotel. This service is paid separately, but it is not so expensive.





And do not worry about the child's leisure, as Nubian Village 5 offers decent travelers a decent environment for relaxation. To begin with, it is worth saying that the natural shallow lagoon on the beach is perfect for swimming children. In addition, children can play in the hotel pool. In cold weather or in winter, children have fun in a separate section of the indoor pool, equipped with a heating system - by the way, there are regularly held sports competitions that they like so much.

Moreover, the water park with interesting rides and water slides is open almost all day - here children can have fun with their parents or accompanied by teachers.

Of course, there is a large playground with trampolines, swings, labyrinths and slides, which the little tourist likes so much. The hotel also has a kids club. By the way, there are also Russian-speaking animators, educators, so your child is unlikely to have communication problems. Here, children are helped to get to know each other, offer interesting games, organize fun contests and other events, including some educational programs.

The hotel also has a sports field for indoor soccer. In addition, children can visit the game room, where there is always the opportunity to spend time with new friends for a particular interesting game.

What additional services does the Nubian Village 5 (Sharm-el- Sheikh) hotel offer?

Of course, the availability of additional services can significantly simplify the stay for foreign travelers. So what services does the Nubian Village 5 hotel offer? SharmelSheikh is a famous resort town that has been engaged in tourism for many years. And local hotels always know how to make a perfect vacation.

To begin with, it is worth noting that the reception is open around the clock. There is also a currency exchange, which is also very convenient. If desired, you can rent a car or other type of transport at the hotel - it will be more convenient to move around the city and explore the surroundings. Leisure and entertainment for travelers

If you enjoy a fun beach holiday spiced with fun and educational activities, then Egypt would certainly be a good option. Hotel Nubian Village 5 made sure that its customers had everything they needed for entertainment. Here you can not only sunbathe and swim, but also go in for sports, enjoy fine cuisine and, of course, explore the most famous historical places of the ancient state.





So what does the hotel complex Nubian Village 5 offer? The photo shows that most of the courtyard is occupied by a large pool, which employees call Swim-up. This place is intended for a relaxing holiday. By the way, for this purpose, comfortable loungers with soft mattresses and protective umbrellas are placed around, under which it is so nice to relax. Beach towels are issued free of charge, which can also be considered one of the advantages.

But various sporting events are held on the territory of the Corallo large indoor pool. By the way, in cold weather here include heating, so the water is always a pleasant temperature. It is in this pool that water aerobics classes are held , as well as fun competitions in water polo. And the hotel owns its own small water park.

Of course, such a large hotel complex offers some wellness treatments. For example, guests always have the opportunity to visit the spa. His services are paid separately. Nevertheless, here you will be offered a lot of healing procedures, including anti-aging wraps, healing baths, etc. The hotel also has a so-called wellness center where clients are offered to visit a sauna or a steam room, relax in the jacuzzi or book an excellent massage.

Fans of sports activities will not be disappointed either, as there are a lot of opportunities for them. For example, you can always visit the gym, equipped with modern equipment. In addition, aerobics and yoga classes are regularly held for those who wish. The hotel also offers guests to visit the billiard room, where it is always a pleasure to spend an evening with friends for an interesting game. There are also table tennis tables.





The hotel has equipped volleyball, basketball and five-a-side football courts. Those who wish can always spend time on one of the tennis courts - in the evening the area is illuminated, but for a fee.

And, of course, do not forget about the team of cheerful, groovy animators who really know their craft very well. In the daytime, children entertain guests by the pool. And in the evenings, guests are gathered in an amphitheater. Here you can watch fire shows, enjoy the grace of oriental dances, see the performances of acrobats, etc. Live Italian music is played in the lobby every evening.

There is a disco at night - admission is free. By the way, once a week on the beach grandiose foam parties are established, which end with an exciting fire show.

Of course, this is not all that Egypt can offer its guests. Nubian Village 5 owns its own tour desk. Here you will be helped to choose the most interesting places for excursions, as well as make a travel route . For example, nearby is the Nabq National Reserve, which is definitely worth a visit - here you can enjoy the unique beauty of Egyptian nature. Tourists are also often quite often offered ATV rides through the mysterious Echo Valley.

Quite a popular place is the Colored Canyon, which is located about 150 kilometers from the hotel complex. Here you will find an exciting tour and the opportunity to enjoy the outlandish natural colors of the rocks. And in the south of the Sinai Peninsula there is the famous Ras Mohamed Reserve, which is famous for its large salt lake, mangroves and exotic nature - in most cases, divers come here, since the underwater world is incredibly rich here.

Next to Sharm el-Sheikh there is another interesting place that everyone probably knows about - this is the legendary Mount Sinai, where Moses received the commandments many thousands of years ago. At the top of the mountain today, a mosque and the Greek temple of the Holy Trinity are built.
