Hotels in Dombay: list, reviews and photos

"Better than mountains can only be mountains that have not been before." Who does not know these words from the song of Vladimir Vysotsky? There are people who are so in love with the mountains that they travel at every opportunity, sometimes thousands of kilometers away, to be among the majestic beauty of the snow-capped peaks. One of the favorite places for tourists has always been and remains the Dombai ski resort in Karachay-Cherkessia. So that you do not feel divorced from civilization, the most modern and comfortable conditions will provide you with Dombay hotels.

Hotel complex "Grand Hotel"

The hotel complex "Grand Hotel" welcomes guests all year round. In three residential 5-storey rooms, guests are offered accommodation in luxurious rooms with expensive elite furniture. The restaurant serves Caucasian and European cuisine. The sports bar serves vitamin drinks. In the evening, lovers of mountain relaxation can relax in the Russian bath, Finnish sauna or hammam, as well as swim in the indoor pool. To maintain good physical shape, a gym is working for you. The cable car is just 50 meters away. In the vicinity of the complex there are all conditions for lovers of skiing, hiking and horse riding. The distance to a single chair lift No. 1 is 600 meters, and to a double chair lift - 1.1 kilometers. The staff can help guests organize excursions. The number of rooms of the Grand Hotel in Dombay is 76 rooms of several categories. For motorists free parking is equipped. There is access to free high-speed wireless Internet.

Hotel "Top"

Hotel "Vershina" in Dombay is a complex with apartments equipped with everything necessary for outdoor activities in the mountains. A separate place in such an ‚Äúapartment‚Äù is reserved for the storage and drying of winter equipment. Each room has a balcony from which you can admire the magnificent mountain scenery. The floors in the apartments are insulated, there is a kitchen, bathroom and necessary furniture. The price includes breakfast, which is served in a cozy cafe. In the open air there is a bathhouse (it works only in summer). Beginners have the opportunity to study at a ski school for an additional fee. Ski equipment can be rented. Vacationers leave their vehicles in the underground parking lot. Apart-hotel "Vershina" is located in the center of the village, next to the gondola cable car (50 meters), a market, a cafe, a bowling alley. Guests are offered accommodation in one-, two- and three-room apartments. Many consider them a good alternative to hotels.

Hotel Crystal

Hotel "Crystal" in Dombay is a hotel complex, which includes three buildings, designed to accommodate up to 130 people. The hotel is located on the banks of the picturesque Amanauz river. Ski resorts are within walking distance, this is an ideal place for both winter and summer holidays. Tourists can take advantage of sports equipment rental, ski storage and a dryer, as well as a car park. Guests are invited to relax in the Russian bath, and in the hotel's own cafe you will be offered dishes of both national Karachai cuisine and European. Nearby is the supermarket "Mill" and the Dombay market. For accommodation, rooms of several categories are offered. Each offers a wonderful view of either the mountains or the river. The price includes breakfast.

Hotel Andersen

Hotel Andersen in Dombay is a three-minute walk from the village center. A single chairlift number 1 is located at a distance of 800 meters from it, a double chairlift number 3 is at a distance of 1.2 kilometers. Car owners enjoy free parking. There is a great restaurant on site. Tourists with small children have the opportunity to use the services of a nanny. For a fee, guests can visit the Turkish bath. Hotel guests have access to free Wi-Fi. There is an elevator. For accommodation are offered single, double, triple, and quadruple rooms. Breakfast is included.

Hotel "Snezhinka"

Surrounded by dense coniferous forest, the three-star Snezhinka Hotel in Dombay is located. The hotel has a closed guarded territory, is a complex of three-story and five-story buildings. Each building has an elevator. Within walking distance from the hotel are the pendulum cable car, gondola lift, ski lift (leading to the beginner trail) and chairlift. The intricacies of winter sports will be taught by experienced instructors, and in summer they will help organize and conduct tourist routes. In summer, an outdoor pool and a volleyball court are also open. For those who wish, the hotel organizes helicopter tours. Vacationers can relax in a Russian bath or in a Finnish sauna. Guests enjoy free parking and a first-aid post. There is a restaurant with two rooms. You can rent snowboards, skis, ski suits. In the evening, you can visit the disco in the night club. The hotel has 65 rooms of various categories. Unlike other Dombai hotels, in Snezhinka you will be offered both economy class and superior comfort living conditions for any wallet. A bathroom is available in each room. Buffet breakfast is included in the price.

At a distance of 80 meters from the hotel there is a pendulum cableway, 280 meters - a chairlift, at a distance of 50 meters - a new gondola. Experienced instructors will give you skiing and snowboarding lessons. Ski equipment can be rented. Guests can enjoy national cuisine at the cafe. There is a dining room. For lovers of picnics there is a barbecue. Upon request, they organize excursions and hiking in the mountains. In the evening, there is a disco for those who wish. The hotel has parking for vehicles. In single, double and triple rooms of tourist class from amenities there is a shower, a toilet and a balcony. The balconies are spacious wooden. There are also deluxe and junior suites.
