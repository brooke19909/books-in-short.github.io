Finland. Lapland and the Northern Lights

One of the places that cannot be imagined without winter, snow and harsh weather is Finland. Lapland, which is considered the home of Santa Claus in many countries, is located in the northern part of the country. The weather here is particularly severe. In winter, the thermometer can drop to -50 degrees. On a polar night, which occurs here in mid-autumn and lasts until spring, the sun can not be seen for several days. Summer temperatures in this region range from 10 to 20 degrees Celsius.

Finland (including Lapland) has good transport accessibility. You can get here both by plane, and by train and bus. Roads in the country, even in the cold winter, are maintained in working condition. When traveling by car, you must comply with all safety requirements for yourself and your passengers.





During the Christmas period and New Year's holidays, for many tourists it is Lapland that becomes the most attractive. Finland, tours to which are already in demand, receives many guests at this time. Thousands of people want to see how and where Santa Claus lives.

Finland (Lapland and other northern regions) is famous for its ski resorts, where not only adults, but also children can have fun and usefully. Trails prepared by qualified instructors and a carefully thought-out safety system will help people with any level of training begin to ride.

The beauty and grandeur of the nature of the Far North leave few indifferent. Finland is very indicative and interesting in this sense. Lapland, whose territory lies beyond the Arctic Circle, is full of surprises in the form of breathtaking landscapes and unique phenomena. There are 7 national parks, the safety of which is protected by the state and which can become a place for great travel.



The mountainous terrain, combined with a large number of lakes and rivers, is surrounded by coniferous forests. All this beauty is covered with a thick layer of snow, which will serve as a blanket until spring. Magnificent landscapes become real scenery for the life of local peoples, most of which still adhere to an established lifestyle, not agreeing to change it to a modern rhythm.

Photos of this natural phenomenon are amazing, but what will happen when this spectacle appears right before your eyes? This is all the northern lights - a unique phenomenon that can be observed on the territory of Lapland. These places are considered one of the best for hunting this rare natural wonder.

Traveling to Lapland will be unforgettable and interesting for all categories of travelers. Beautiful and unique nature will forever leave a mark in the soul of everyone who saw it.
