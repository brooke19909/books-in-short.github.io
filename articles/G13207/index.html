Introduction

If a car or other vehicle has an owner in the form of a legal or natural person, and is also registered in the country, then it must be taxed. The size of the tax and the procedure for its calculation are established both by federal laws and regional.

Within the boundaries established by the Tax Code of the Russian Federation, state entities independently determine how much, how and when to levy a motor tax. They also have the right to provide various categories of citizens with benefits on payment of fees to the treasury.

For example, the authorities of a subject of the Federation have the right to increase the transport tax, but its amount should not exceed ten times the amount established by the federal authorities initially.

Will the transport tax be abolished? It is unlikely, but local authorities have the right to completely cancel the transport log (for low-power vehicles), and at present, the governing bodies of some regions of Russia are ready to nullify the transport tax for hybrid and electric cars.

Terminology

Legal practice shows that the term ‚Äútransport tax‚Äù, despite its definition by the legislation of the Russian Federation, can be interpreted in three different ways. The standard interpretation states that the transport tax is the payment established by the regional authorities in respect of vehicles with an engine, or shipping units with a sail, for individuals and legal entities.

The second interpretation of the concept describes the term as a mandatory tax charge that applies to all categories of vehicles without exception, which are officially registered in the territory of a particular region of the Russian Federation, depending on the power of the vehicle engine.

The final interpretation of the term represents transport tax as the amount that citizens annually pay in favor of the state for the vehicles they own.

Tax calculation

The legislation of the Russian Federation has fixed a general scheme according to which the tax on transport is calculated in various regions of the country. At the same time, the authorities of the region of the Russian Federation, again, within the framework of federal legislation, themselves establish the procedure for calculating the payment of tax for vehicles owned by someone else. Moreover, the frequency of tax collection is strictly fixed - 1 time per year.

Once again, it is worth recalling that the regions have the right to differentiate the amount of tax on cars 150 liters. from. depending on the area where it is charged, and on the social situation in the subject of the Federation.

When calculating, such indicators as:

  • engine power;
  • total car capacity;
  • engine thrust;
  • register ton of the car.

Since the calculation also takes into account the lifetime of the vehicle, it is worth mentioning that only full months are taken into account. Days and weeks are not counted. This is done to simplify all bureaucratic procedures associated with the calculation of automobile tax.

  • DS - the share of the owner in the ownership of the vehicle. If the car has one owner, then this parameter is indicated as 1/1. If there are two owners, then 1/2.

Pay the received tax of 150 l. from. in various ways. Everything is done for the convenience of citizens. Payment can be made using bank details, which are indicated in the payment receipt sent to the address of the owner.

Common mistake in calculating tax amount

One of the most common mistakes that both individuals and legal entities make when trying to figure out what tax on a car is 150 liters. s., is an incorrect determination of the period during which the vehicle is owned.
