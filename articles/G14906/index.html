Catering establishments

Tasty and high-quality food is offered to its guests by the Grand Prestige Hotel resort complex (Side, Turkey). Description of catering establishments is as follows:

  • "Uranus", "Prestige" - these are the main restaurants of the institution, where guests are provided with full meals according to the buffet system. Each of them is designed for 300 seats.
  • Aqua is the third main restaurant of the resort complex. Its difference lies in the fact that it is located on the outdoor terrace overlooking the pool.
  • The Sultan's Meal is an authentic Turkish establishment. Due to the fact that the institution works on the principle of an open kitchen, you can watch how the chefs prepare local dishes for you.
  • "Panorama" is an institution specializing in fresh seafood. The restaurant is located on the outdoor terrace.
  • The Italian restaurant attracts guests with a stylish interior and cozy atmosphere. The aroma of pizza and pasta is emphasized by quality imported wines.
  • Taurus is a pastry shop with a warm and cozy atmosphere. A huge amount of traditional Turkish and European sweets is complemented by aromas of quality coffee and tea.




Hotel Bars

You can enjoy quality drinks, hearty snacks and a relaxed atmosphere in the bars of the Grand Prestige Hotel & Spa 5 * resort complex in Side (Turkey). We are talking about such institutions:

  • The beach bar specializes in soft drinks, local beer and fruit smoothies. In the snack area you can enjoy Turkish cakes and pizza.
  • Dozens of cocktails are available at the pool bar. The atmosphere of relaxation is complemented by groovy music and fiery animation.
  • Niagara is a disco bar that will appeal to lovers of nightly entertainment. Disco and DJ performances are held every evening.

  • instead of large double beds, guests are offered to sit on shifted single beds;
  • uncomfortable and, sometimes, faulty folding beds are offered as an extra bed;
