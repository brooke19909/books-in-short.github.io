Emerald Isle of Thassos, Greece

The Greek island of Thassos is a unique place, which is located in the waters of the Strait of the same name in the Aegean Sea. It is part of the possessions of Greece, therefore, culture, history and architecture in this place are simply saturated with the atmosphere of antiquity and lightness. By right, this island is considered one of the most environmentally friendly and landscaped resorts, which attracts tourists from all over the world. Also, the island of Thassos is the personification of peace and quiet. He has a certain ancient mysterious aura, therefore most often the guests of these lands are history buffs, lovers of silence and romance, as well as nature connoisseurs.



There are no direct flights to this wonderful place, so all tourists come to Thessaloniki, and then, following local routes, most often they get to the desired point by ferry. You can also get to these lands on a rented car, the main thing is to know the area and not get lost. To do this, you need a map of the island of Thassos, it is sold in neighboring cities located on the mainland. You can also drive around these provincial green lands by car, however, the roads there are not wide and not designed for driving too fast.



It is believed that on this northern island more than 3 thousand years ago, the ancient Greeks founded the city, which was called Thassos. Today, among the forests and rocks you can see its ruins: ancient temples, palaces and colonnades. Since then, these lands got their name - the island of Thassos, the nearby strait and the capital of this province were also named.

Amaze all guests of the island and the natural heritage, thanks to which these lands began to be called the emerald paradise. Almost everything here is green: endless forests, shrubs and flower beds, pine plantations and countless rows of olives. Zelentsoy gives and the sea itself, which washes the island of Thassos. Many local bays are shallow and have a gentle entrance to the sea, because this place is considered ideal for spending holidays with kids. White sand sands stand out against the background of all these natural emeralds, which stretch in wide stripes along the strait.
