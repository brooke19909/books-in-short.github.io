What to choose: grain device or capsule?

Firstly, there is more free space in the selection of coffee, as well as the resulting drinks, a much better taste and higher performance. Secondly, using such a Philips coffee machine will cost several times cheaper than a capsule one. At first it may seem that this is not so. After all, the second option costs a lot less.
