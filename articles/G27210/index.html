Hotel Atlantica Akti Zeus 4 * (Crete, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

The Cretan hotel Atlantica Akti Zeus 4 * is part of the famous hotel chain. It is modern, popular and visited. And why, it is worth telling in more detail.





general information

Atlantica Akti Zeus 4 * is located in Crete, as already mentioned. To be more precise, in the resort of Amoudara. This is one of the most popular places on the island. Amudara became famous thanks to its clean, long sandy beach, in the immediate vicinity of which the hotel complex is located.

The airport is also located nearby - only 11 kilometers away. Heraklion, the administrative center of Crete, can be reached in about seven minutes. Some go on foot, others go by taxi. But the most comfortable and budget option is a bus ride. The bus stop is located next to the hotel, which is very convenient.

About service

In Atlantica Akti Zeus 4 *, like in all other hotels of this chain, there is everything you need for a comfortable stay and relaxation. Naturally, this is parking, free Wi-Fi, a 24-hour front desk and other standard services.

There is also a medical office where you can go to resolve unforeseen health-related situations. By the way, just a 10-minute walk from the hotel there is a pharmacy. It is also important to know that the hotel is adapted for guests with disabilities.

And also in Atlantica Akti Zeus 4 * there is a currency exchange and car rental. There are no shops in the hotel, as in other hotels of this chain. But they are literally at hand, and the prices there are also low.

Modern tourists perceive each hotel not only as a place for temporary residence, but also as an entertainment complex. Well, Atlantica Akti Zeus 4 * is perfect for this definition. Here, definitely, people who value active pastime will like it.

Restaurants

Atlantica Akti Zeus 4 * (Crete, Amoudara) has several excellent establishments that serve delicious dishes prepared according to original recipes.

The main restaurant is called Minos. Breakfast in it starts at 7:00 and ends at 10:30. Lunch is from 12:00 to 15:00, and dinner is from 18:00 to 21:30.

Faidra Greek Restaurant serves local Cretan cuisine. The establishment is popular, because if you want to have dinner there, you need to book a table in advance. It is open from 19:00 to 21:30. It is important to follow the dress code.

And the institution is open from 19:00 to 22:30.





Bars

In Atlantica Akti Zeus 4 * (Amoudara, Fodele) there are several more establishments with a wide selection of drinks - both strong and non-alcoholic.

The Evropi lobby, open from 10:00 to 01:30, has everything from coffee to exotic cocktails. But you need to know that according to the all-inclusive system, you can treat yourself to it until 00:00, and then everything is paid.

Sentia Bar is open from 19:00 to 23:00. It is located in an Italian restaurant. To enjoy the exquisite atmosphere and the relaxing atmosphere, you need to take money with you and dress according to the dress code.

Dionisos Kafeneion is a Greek-style cafe. This is where you can try real Cretan coffee and traditional sweets. This establishment, like Sentia, is not included in the all-inclusive service.

But lovers of noble alcohol will like Semeli Wine Cellar. This wine bar is open from 19:00 to 23:00. There you can not only enjoy fine branded drinks, but also sign up for a tasting.

There is also Ariadne Entertainment Bar. It works from 19:00 to 23:30, and all sorts of animated shows and performances are held there.

There is also a poolside bar called Oceanus and a Pasiphai snack bar with snacks. Both establishments are open from 10:00 to 18:00.





Standards
