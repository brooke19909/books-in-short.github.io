Diamond Hill Resort 5 * (Turkey, Alanya): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

One of the favorite vacation spots for most Russian tourists is sunny Turkey. The holiday season here lasts long enough, the sun is hot and the sea is warm. What else do you need for an unforgettable vacation? Choosing for this resort area Alanya, you will not regret it. However, you should think about where you will live in advance. The ideal place for this could be the Diamond Hill Resort 5 * hotel . It is perfect for newlyweds, couples with children and tourists traveling alone.





Location

Diamond Hill Resort 5 * is located in Tosmur. Alanya city center is just four kilometers away. The excellent location of the hotel will allow you to get to many interesting places in the shortest possible time. Large shops of clothes, souvenirs and all that attracts shopping lovers are waiting for you almost around the clock. The vibrant nightlife, clubs, bars, trendy restaurants are just a ten minute walk from the hotel. In addition, there is a promenade nearby where you can take a walk before going to bed and enjoy the clean sea air.

Near Diamond Hill Resort 5 * flows the mountain river Dim. Its picturesque surroundings are definitely worth a look. In such places, time seems to stop, and habitual life will seem unrealistic.
