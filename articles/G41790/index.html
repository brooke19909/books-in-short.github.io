Promsvyazbank: transfer from card to card. Rules, features, commission

Since 2014, Promsvyazbank PJSC canceled the fee for transfers between accounts. Clients of a credit institution can pay for goods in online stores, utilities, send money to relatives without a fee. Read more about how to make transfers from card to card through Promsvyazbank CJSC, read on.





Briefly about the service

For the transfer of funds between accounts, credit institutions charge a fee of 10 rubles or more. up to 2%. Small savings are possible if small amounts are sent, and the transaction is carried out through the Internet bank. Some institutions provide this service with a minimum commission. For example, Sberbank of Russia charges 30 rubles for transferring funds between cards of different cities. Of great importance is also the type of plastic. Debit card transactions are cheaper than credit cards. There are also separate services that provide the service of converting funds between bank cards of all countries of the world. Commission ranges from 1-2%. If the payment amount is small, then you can choose any intermediary. But if it comes to the transfer of 10 thousand rubles. and more, it is worth exploring the tariffs.

Service Details

Promsvyazbank CJSC transfers from card to card of its customers for free. If the recipient has provided the credit details of another institution, the sender must pay a commission of 1% (at least 50 rubles) of the transaction amount. The commission for transactions on debit cards of other institutions is 1.5% (minimum 30 rubles). The speed of the transaction depends on the institution. Promsvyazbank CJSC transfers money from card to card instantly. If funds go to other lending institutions, the process can take 2-4 days. The transaction is carried out only in Russian rubles.





Limitations

Promsvyazbank CJSC transfers from card to card via the Internet bank in the amount of up to 70 thousand rubles. per day and not more than 600 thousand rubles. per month. From one account to another, clients can transfer up to 100 thousand rubles. On the day you can send 4 payments - only within the Russian Federation. Confirmation of the transfer is carried out through 3D-Secure (enter a one-time password).

Cash transfers
