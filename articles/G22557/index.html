Zhukovsky city: how to get from Moscow to it in the best way

Everyone can have their favorite ways to solve transport problems. But one of the most common answers to the question of how to get to Zhukovsky is to take an electric train at the Kazan station and follow it to the platform "Rest". It is located in the city itself, and in the immediate vicinity there are still Illyinskaya and Kratovo stops. They may well be located closer to the place where you intend to get. Such a system of transport links with the capital has been formed over the past decades. But recently, other transport options have become increasingly popular. Many of those who are interested in how to get to Zhukovsky prefer to get to the Vykhino metro station first. When leaving it, you should cross the square and take the bus 478 or 474 at the bus stop. But the latter does not suit everyone, because it passes through Zhukovsky and most of its route passes outside the city. Here, on the square near the Vykhino metro station, you can also take a minibus. They go more often than buses, and the fare is quite acceptable.
