Travel to Saona Island - Dream Beach

Despite some carelessness, the inhabitants of the Dominican Republic love their own nature and cherish it, therefore the island was declared a National Park, and it is strictly forbidden to open hotels here. At the same time, tourists come here every day in an organized manner by the sea to appreciate all the charm and beauty of wildlife in natural conditions.
