Villa Elenite, Bulgaria. Elenite Resort (Bulgaria) - Travel Reviews

Hospitable, flooded with sun and smiles of local residents, Bulgaria has long been a favorite vacation spot for Russians. Back in Soviet times, our compatriots enjoyed visiting this country.



A bit of history

Until 1985, the current territory of the complex was not built up. In the forests, near the mountain of Stara Planina, deer were found. Hence the name of the resort. The resort complex Elenite (Bulgaria) was built by specialists from Finland. The work was completed in the eighties, but until the nineteen ninety-third year the complex was closed. The Elenite project is in many ways similar to the famous Golden Sands resort, but at the same time it has significant differences in architecture. The park territory of this resort is built up with low-rise villas. This allowed to preserve the nature of these places in their original form. Today it includes three- and four-star hotels. They are located on the seashore. All of them have their own well-groomed sandy beaches. Their length is eight hundred meters.



Location

Today the topic of our conversation is the Elenite resort. Bulgaria is known for many recreation areas, but this is perhaps the cutest and most romantic corner in the south of the country. It is located near Burgas and Sunny Beach, near the Stara Planina Mountain. The village is well protected from cold winds. The climate here is mild and warm, which makes the stay here very comfortable for a relaxing and measured vacation with children and loved ones.

Climate

Many Russians have long appreciated the resort of Elenite. Bulgaria, reviews about holidays in which it has long crossed the borders of the country, is striving to develop tourism more and more actively every year. If you have chosen Elenite for your vacation, you should know that the climate in this place is temperate, with little Mediterranean influence. It features a long swimming season - from May to October. The average summer temperature is + 28-30 degrees. Water heats up to +26 degrees. The resort is located in a picturesque bay, in which northern winds are not terrible - it is reliably protected by the Stara Planina Mountain from the north. A stunning combination of the purest sea air and luxurious coniferous forests magically affects the human body. Holidays in Elenite (Bulgaria) do not wear out the sweltering heat. This is what attracts many here.



Bulgaria: Villa Elenite

On the green hills between the mountains and the sea, cascading magnificent villas are located. Comfortable conditions for vacationers are the main advantage that distinguishes sunny Bulgaria from other countries. Villa Elenite (Villas Elenite) - one of the most popular complexes, which consists of individual villas, conveniently located in a large park area in the heart of the resort.

They are one- and two-story buildings, connected by paths and stairs. It offers rooms with satellite TV, air conditioning, an outdoor pool, and free Wi-Fi. You will be accommodated in a villa under different conditions, including the All Inclusive option . In 2006, the rooms were reconstructed.

The resort complex is very conveniently located - just 100-300 meters from the beach. In total there are 247 villas, outdoor pools for children and adults, a disco for children, a night bar, a fitness center, minigolf, a Turkish bath and a jacuzzi, a sauna and spa, shops, an Internet club. The rooms are equipped with satellite TV, telephone, refrigerator, hairdryer.



Which hotels to choose?

Many hotels of different levels are located in Elenite. Bulgaria and its residents are always happy to see guests and try to do everything so that tourists' relaxation, even in a modest 2 * hotel, is as comfortable as possible. What can we say about the magnificent 5 * villas!

In 2010, the truly luxurious Royal Castle 5 * hotel began to receive guests. Its building is impressive in scale and the rooms are luxurious. The hotel is the most remote from the sea, located behind the water park.

Andalusia 4 * and Atrium 4 * are hotels of about the same level. They are located on the first line, close to each other, have almost the same rooms and prices for accommodation. Each hotel has its own restaurant.

Royal Park 4 * is on the first line. It is separated from the beach by a spacious pool. All rooms in the hotel are originally furnished in a marine style.

Royal Bay 4 * is a little away. It is located on the opposite side of the beach. A distinctive feature - unusually bright and sunny rooms.



The largest territory is occupied by Holiday Village 4 * villas. Comfortable houses are arranged in tiers, you should climb the stairs to them. When pre-booking a room, you can specify only its type, you cannot order any particular house. The rooms are spacious, it is possible to stay with two children.

Transport

Every day every hour from Elenite there is a bus to Nessebar, Sunny Beach. Twice a day from the beach to Nessebar walks a walking boat.

Restaurants

The two largest of them are located in the very center of Elenite. Bulgaria has always been famous for its exquisite cuisine. Restaurant "Sozopol" is located on two levels. The luxurious rooms offer original dishes of national cuisine. You will appreciate the magnificent panoramic view of the sea. There is also a nightclub, cocktail bar and patisserie on the terrace.

Right on the beach, near the Royal Park Hotel, there is another very popular restaurant - Talana. Its interior is stylized as an old ship. The restaurant seats 320 people. Here you can enjoy delicious fish and seafood dishes prepared on the barbecue and grill.
