Italian beach Togliatti: how to find relaxation in a big city

The city of motorists of Togliatti stands "on the water", and on the banks of the Volga there are wonderful places worthy of the attention of citizens and visitors.

The Italian beach of Togliatti in the summer is filled with vacationers. It is very beautiful here - an unforgettable view of the Volga and the Zhiguli opens, and motels and pensions are located nearby.





Do not miss the long-awaited summer relaxation on the Volga

June, the first month of summer, gives warm sunny days and invites you to enjoy your vacation wherever there are ponds and a sandy beach. The long cold winter is behind, so you should not sit at home, come swim, sunbathe and relax in nature.

Every year the Italian beach is becoming cleaner and is constantly being improved. This is a great option for those who are just bored with swimming and would like an active holiday. An urgent question for those who seek to get to the Italian beach in Togliatti: "How to get here and on what?"

How to get there

From the Komsomolsky district of the city, you can get to the Italian beach directly on the No. 1 trolleybus from the Transformer plant to the Tatishchev monument, located on the shore of the reservoir, not far from the very city founded by him, now flooded by the Zhigulevsk Sea.





Trolleybuses of route No. 1 do not run on Saturday and Sunday - they are replaced by No. 7: an extended seventh flight was launched on weekends to the recreation area. This new schedule is convenient for vacationers and employees of sanatoriums located in the green zone. From the stop you need to go down the stone stairs, admiring at the same time the monument to Tatishchev.





In total, more than 9 cars leave on route number 7 at the weekend with an interval of movement of up to 20 minutes. Going by public transport is cheaper than getting by minibus.

Car enthusiasts take advantage of a personal car and can get from the city center to the recreation area in 30 minutes, and by ordering a taxi, you can get to the Italian Tolyatti beach the fastest: its address is simple - Lesoparkovoe highway, green zone.





How to find a beach

Almost every resident of the city can answer the question of where the Italian beach is in Tolyatti - this place is so popular. It is located just 4 kilometers from the center at an equal distance from three urban areas.

Geographically, it is located in the Central region, which in the west borders with Avtozavodsky, and in the southeast - with Komsomolsky, and is separated from them by a green forest zone. In the south of the Central region, a coniferous massif extends, which further extends to the Volga.

The green zone is removed from the plants. Nearby, in the so-called Old Town, a large number of places of recreation, resorts, hotels and monuments are concentrated.

In total, at the beginning of the summer of 2017, 3 beaches were officially open and operating in Tolyatti, one of them is Italian in the Central District. Garbage is being collected, and the SES checks the status of each of the officially authorized operating bathing places.

Characteristic Italian beach

The Italian beach is an officially authorized place for relaxation and swimming, which has received a positive conclusion from the SES. The bathing area is marked by buoys, there is an Emergencies Ministry post, a first-aid post is working.

On this beach in the summer there is a lot of sun, silky sand and the quiet rustle of coastal waters. When the residents of the city have a question about where to go so as not to waste time, not to be disappointed and have a good rest, most choose the Italian beach of Tolyatti. Here, the waters themselves beckon to plunge and bask in the sand.

It is surrounded by coniferous forest, has a gently sloping sandy bottom, is fenced, there is parking. Volleyball is played here, and there are cafes around the perimeter where you can have a cheap meal.

Shuttle taxis: fast and reliable

Minibuses go to the Italian beach in Tolyatti from early morning to late evening, bringing workers, youth and tourists to rest by the water. Minibuses have the advantage, because there are more than trolleybus lines.

To get to the municipal Italian beach, you need to take the minibus number 91, drive through the green zone and get off at the Nadezhda Sanatorium stop. This stop follows the stop "Sanatorium" Russian Bor "". Route 91 is convenient for workers at the enterprises of JSC Fosfor, Zavod SK, KuibyshevAzot, Volgocemmash, as well as visitors from the bus station.





You can get to the beach area by minibuses that go to the street. Revolutionary is more than 4 flights.

A great idea is to get to the beach by bike. Bike rides are becoming increasingly popular in a growing city, where a city-forming enterprise is a car factory. And this can only be welcomed - the green zone will be cleaner, and the benefits for vacationers, most of whom are young people under 35, are enormous.

The shore of the Zhiguli reservoir and the Italian beach of Togliatti await vacationers, guaranteeing comfort in the fresh air. Be sure to follow safety rules: do not lie in the sun for a long time, do not drink alcohol, so as not to overshadow the long-awaited summer relaxation.
