Hotel Pineta Club 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey) - photos, prices and reviews

The hotel complex Pineta Club 4 * is a great place for a carefree and fun vacation. Every year, thousands of tourists visit this hotel. After all, here every traveler is offered excellent living conditions, quality service against the backdrop of traditional oriental hospitality.

And today, many fellow citizens are interested in information about this place. Where is it located? What conditions for entertainment and relaxation does it offer? Can I come here with a child? These answers will be of interest to many potential customers.

Where is the hotel complex located?





The large Pineta Club Hotel 4 hotel complex is located very close to the Mediterranean coast, in a small picturesque village called Cityler. Some travelers prefer to overcome this distance on foot, admiring the scenic nature and vibrant sights along the road.

The nearest international airport is approximately 93 kilometers from the hotel complex. Transfer to the hotel is usually organized by a travel company. In any case, you should prepare for the road.

Brief infrastructure description

Beautiful beaches, national colors and luxury hotels - this is what the city of Marmaris is famous for. Pineta Club 4 is a large resort complex, whose area is about eighteen thousand square meters. Around the large pool are residential buildings - one five-story and two six-story buildings.

The hotel first opened its doors to tourists in 1988. But the last major reconstruction was carried out not so long ago, in 2008. Therefore, today the Pineta Club 4 hotel complex stands out not only with its original design, but also with modern equipment.

Many tourists note the well-thought-out organization of hotel services and convenient infrastructure. And of course, all conditions are created here for the rest to be aesthetic: bright flower beds, exotic ornamental plants and large palm trees, in the shade of which it is always so pleasant to relax. The hotel looks especially impressive in the evening, when the lights turn on.

Description of rooms at the hotel





Hotel Pineta Club 4 can be considered a fairly large resort complex. On its territory there are 501 rooms, including:

  • 480 standard rooms of different capacities (from 1 to 3 people);
  • 21 suite rooms consist of two rooms and are designed for the simultaneous residence of four people;

Of course, the rooms are quite spacious and furnished with all necessary furniture. A soft comfortable bed will always be waiting for you here. And the door from the room leads to a private balcony, which overlooks the sea or the hotel courtyard, depending on the location of the room - in any case, you can spend time alone with yourself, enjoying the fresh air and the surrounding landscape.

Of course, the rooms are equipped with household appliances, without which comfortable living is almost impossible. An air conditioner with an individual control system will help to create the most comfortable air temperature. There is also a TV on which you can watch satellite channels (there are even several Russian ones). Guests will certainly appreciate the presence of a small refrigerator, which in most cases is used to cool drinks. There is a small but reliable safe - you need to pay extra for its use, but the price is not too high.

The room has an attached toilet and a bathroom, which is equipped with either a spacious shower or a bathtub, in which it is so pleasant to relax after an eventful day. Here you will find a hair dryer for drying hair, a set of clean towels and a set of branded hotel products for personal hygiene.

This is exactly what the rooms on the territory of the Pineta Club 4 hotel complex look like. Reviews from tourists indicate that the rooms are really cleaned every day. There will be no problems with towels, as their stocks are updated as they are used. Bed linen is changed three times a week. By the way, there are several specially equipped rooms for a comfortable stay for people with disabilities.

Food scheme for tourists

On the territory of the Pineta Club 4 hotel complex, all conditions have been created so that guests do not need anything. And the cost of living includes full three meals a day. The large main restaurant serves generous tables for guests. Chefs here every day delight their customers with delicious dishes of Turkish, Mediterranean and international cuisine. You are waiting for dishes of meat, fish and seafood, as well as a large selection of salads, an abundance of fresh fruits, real Turkish sweets, pastries and local ice cream. From time to time, theme nights are organized at the hotel: you will have the opportunity to taste the masterpieces of Italian, Greek or even Chinese cuisine. By the way, the waiters are fast and always polite.





In addition, during the day, guests are offered light snacks and desserts. There are four bars on the territory of the hotel, where you will be treated not only with delicious food, but also with excellent drinks. Drinks of foreign manufacture are not included in the price - you have to pay for them separately.

On the other hand, do not forget that Marmaris is a large resort city. Here you will find a lot of catering establishments, including pizzerias, children's cafes and luxurious restaurants. And in the market you can always buy fresh fruits and local delicacies.

Beach vacation

Long beaches with fine sand and warm sea waves - this is exactly what the city of Marmaris is famous for. Pineta Club 4, unfortunately, cannot boast of having its own stretch of sea coast. However, a public beach is located just 300 meters from the hotel complex and can be reached in five minutes.





Despite the fact that the beach is open to all residents and guests of the city, cleanliness and order are observed here. On the shore you will find soft clean sand. Descent to the sea is smooth and safe. The coastal zone is very shallow, which makes it an ideal place for bathing children. And if we are talking about security, then there is a rescue tower on the shore, where there is always a working staff ready to come to the rescue at any time.

Here, for visitors, there are sun loungers with soft mattresses and umbrellas to protect against heat. You have to pay for the use of sun loungers, but this service is quite inexpensive.

Naturally, on the seashore you can engage in more active types of recreation. To begin with, it is worth noting that there are volleyball courts where you can always have fun and make new friends. For a small fee, you can ride a water bike, boat, banana or canoe. There is an opportunity to engage in motorized water sports. Tourists love to go boating, water skiing, jet skiing and parachuting. There is also a diving center, where they are ready to offer assistance to both beginners and experienced divers.

Is it possible for children to relax on site?

Of course, for a family traveling with children, the conditions for a comfortable pastime are priority. So what does the Pineta Club Hotel 4 offer young travelers ? The hotel, of course, provides some additional services.





To begin with, it is worth noting that folding extra beds and high chairs in the restaurant are taken for granted. Here you only need to ask the administrator or any other employee for this. Do not worry about nutrition. In the local restaurant you are sure to find healthy and tasty food that will appeal to your child.

The little tourist is also provided with plenty of opportunities to have fun. Of course, your child will enjoy relaxing on the beach. And at the hotel, a small and not too deep pool is specially equipped for children - here the guys can play and splash around under the supervision of the hotel staff.

Children are also invited to attend classes at the kids club. Here, children are educators, educators and animators who know how to organize leisure activities. In the club, children have the opportunity to engage in various types of creativity, participate in quizzes and competitions.

You can also have a great time on the playground with swings, a sandbox and interesting rides. The children are also entertained by animators, although for the most part they speak English. If you need to leave for a while, you can safely leave the children with one of the nannies - only professional staff work at the hotel, and their services are very inexpensive.

This is exactly the kind of children's vacation the Pineta Club 4 hotel offers. Marmaris is a big city, a walk along which will also bring your child a lot of positive emotions. After all, there are entertainment centers with interesting attractions and a host of other attractions.

Hotel Pineta Club Hotel 4 (Marmaris): additional services

An additional service is extremely important, because it helps to improve living conditions and save travelers from the mass of small problems, which, however, are quite capable of spoiling the vacation. So what services does the Pineta Club 4 hotel offer? Reviews of tourists indicate that the hotel has everything you need for a carefree vacation.

To begin with, it is worth noting that the hotel provides dry cleaning and laundry services. In addition, a currency exchange office is constantly operating in the lobby, and bank cards are accepted for payment - this eliminates all possible cash problems. Since the hotel provides relaxation for the disabled, you can rent a chair right here.

If you are traveling by car, there will be no parking problems, as the hotel has its own large parking lot, where residents can leave vehicles at no extra charge. And in the hotel complex there are three stores where you can buy essentials, as well as souvenirs and gifts.

The hotel offers its customers to use the services of a business center. There is a computer room with Internet access. And if necessary, you can send a fax from here, make an important call, make copies of documents, etc. There are two spacious conference rooms with a capacity of 200 and 600 people, respectively. The hotel often becomes the venue for scientific conferences, seminars, business meetings , etc. - employees are always ready to help with the organization of this or that event.

Fun and Sports for Travelers





Many potential customers are primarily interested in questions about what kind of entertainment the Pineta Club Hotel 4 hotel complex can offer. Reviews from tourists indicate that here everyone can find something to their liking.

The courtyard is reserved for a large pool. Water is cleaned regularly, and the temperature is always comfortable for swimming. Comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas are nearby. Guests love to relax by the pool. By the way, in the evening or in cold weather, there is an indoor pool with heated water. Here you can not only relax, but also enjoy the view of the night sky through the glass roof.

Of course, this is not all that Turkey can offer tourists. Pineta Club Hotel 4 has its own internet cafe. You can visit the games room. Travelers who wish to keep fit will also not be left out: the hotel offers free gym services. There are also paved tennis courts, and balls and rackets can be rented nearby for a small fee. There are also excellent volleyball and soccer courts. By the way, often tenants arrange fun competitions here. There are also billiard tables, but you need to pay extra for their use.

You should definitely visit the local Turkish bath. Guests can make an appointment for a massage. Women will be pleased with the presence of a beauty salon, where experienced stylists and cosmetologists will offer their services. The hotel has its own solarium. You can relax perfectly by visiting the jacuzzi or sauna. And, of course, in case of health problems, an experienced doctor will be called to your room.





The hotel complex has a team of animators. In the afternoon they entertain guests by the pool, organizing competitions, competitions, water aerobics classes. And in the evening, guests will find vivid shows, catchy performances, oriental dances and live music.

Excursion holidays in the city

Hotels, beaches and shopping centers - this is not all that Turkey can offer the traveler. Hotel Pineta Club 4 offers its guests to use the services of a tour desk, as there really is something to see in the city, as well as in the surrounding area.

For example, in the northern part of Marmaris on the top of the hill is the large archaeological center of Ashartepe, where a huge collection of artifacts from all eras is collected. You will also have the opportunity to visit the monumental castle of Marmaris, which, according to sources, was erected in the third millennium BC. Today, a museum is organized here with seven large exhibition halls.

There are also a lot of natural attractions. You will probably be advised first of all to visit the beautiful Managavt Waterfall. By the way, there is a restaurant on the terrace - you can have lunch, enjoying the breathtaking view. In addition, you will have the opportunity to visit the legendary island of Cleopatra, which, according to tradition, was presented by Mark Anthony to his beloved. At one time, this paradise was the cradle of true love.

How much does a hotel stay?

For every traveler, the issue of cost of living is extremely important. And the hotel complex Pineta Club Hotel 4 can please tourists with relatively low prices. On average, a day at the hotel will cost about 2-3 thousand rubles. Of course, it all depends on many factors. For example, it is worth considering the date of arrival, pre-booking, the selected room category, etc.

And do not forget that some hotel services are paid separately. Yes, and you probably want to take a walk around the city or go on an excursion - all this must be taken into account when calculating the budget.

To begin with, it is worth noting that in the hotel complex mainly residents of Europe rest. Nevertheless, today many Russian tourists choose the Pineta Club 4 hotel. Reviews about this place are mostly positive.

If you need a bright, eventful vacation that would combine sea baths, excursions, fun entertainment and shopping, then the city of Marmaris will be a great option . Pineta Club 4 in terms of price and quality is rightfully considered one of the best hotels on the coast.
