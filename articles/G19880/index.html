Kaliningrad equipment Telefunken: reviews, an overview of the best models

The products of the Kaliningrad representative office of Telefunken, the reviews of which are given below, were previously produced by the German manufacturer of television and radio equipment. The year the company was founded in 1903. This happened through the merger of part of the specialists from the representative offices of Siemens and AEG. In 1941, Siemens' share was sold, and the AEG partner became the sole owner of the brand. Due to financial difficulties, the new association broke up in 1972, and after 24 years it ceased to exist. However, the further course of history allowed to renew this brand, albeit in a rather unusual way.
