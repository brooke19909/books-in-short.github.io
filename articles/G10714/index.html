Weather and climate

Egyptian weather is very changeable in March. This is the time when the cool season is replacing the cool season. The big advantage is that there is no sweltering heat. Excursion novelty

Tourists, tired of temples and pyramids, in Egypt awaits a novelty that has fallen on an unspoken list of attractions. This is the largest camel market in the country. It is located in a picturesque place of Bourkash in the Nile Valley. Here you can see different breeds of camels, observe the emotional trading. Of course, tourists do not buy desert ships, but they can get a blanket from the wool of this camel, try healthy camel milk. The excitement in the market happens on the eve of the holidays. The most brisk camel trade is conducted before the festival of sacrifice - Eid al-Adha.





Egypt Holidays in March

When planning a vacation in Egypt in March, you can become a witness and participant in the festivities. In some years, the birthday of the Prophet Muhammad falls on March. For example, on the 1st of the day, Athletes Day is celebrated in the country, and Mother's Day is celebrated on March 21.
