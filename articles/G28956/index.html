A little bit about the country

The UAE is located in the Middle East, between the Gulf of Oman and the Persian Gulf. 40 years ago there was an uninhabited desert on this territory, which in a short period of time turned into one of the most popular tourist destinations. 7 emirates formed the country of the UAE and managed to combine simplicity and luxury, traditions and modernity, entertainment and business on their lands.

The most popular among tourists are cities such as Dubai, Sharjah, Abu Dhabi, Fujairah, Umm al-Qaiwain, Ajman, Ras al-Khaimah. Each of them can offer the vacationer a decent service, comfortable accommodation and interesting entertainment. Wealthy tourists will prefer ultra-modern Dubai, lovers of silence - Abu Dhabi, active - Fujairah, health-conscious - Ras al-Khaimah, economical - Ajman.

Things to do?

Arriving in the UAE, a tourist will find many fascinating activities to their liking, for example:

  • beach vacation. Each hotel has a comfortable furnished beach, which offers a wide range of water activities.
  • safari. In the vast deserts of the UAE, you can ride a camel, quads or jeeps.
  • water parks. It is in this country that the world's coolest and most interesting water parks are located.
  • fishing. One of the unforgettable entertainment is fishing and cooking it on board the yacht.
  • shopping. In the UAE, you can buy everything - from oriental trinkets to clothing from global brands.
  • excursions. The young state is able to conquer tourists with modern buildings, palaces, mosques, as well as interesting museums.
