Metro "Kantemirovskoe" ... Have you been? Not? Be sure to visit!

Kantemirovskaya metro station on the metro map is viewed perfectly. And to find her does not present any problems either for Muscovites themselves or for city guests. It should be noted that this station has a number of characteristic features that make it unique and not similar to any other in the capital of the Russian Federation.

Metro "Kantemirovskoe". general description





What is this station like? What is the difference? The fact is that the Kantemirovskoye metro is located on the territory of Tsaritsyno in the administrative district of Moscow, Yuzhny. It is part of the Zamoskvoretskaya line and is considered an intermediate station in the Moscow Metro .

Today, the lining of the track walls is made of brown marble with small ornamental liners of historical and military themes, the floor of the station hall is covered with granite slabs of red, gray and black. In the center of the hall on the arch are lamps.

With its design, the station extols the defenders of the Fatherland - the soldiers of the 4th Guards Kantemirovskaya Tank Red Banner Order of the Lenin Division named after Yu. Andropova, who was very distinguished in the Great Patriotic War. These warriors are represented on the bas-reliefs created by sculptors A. Kibalnikov, G. Vybon and V. Protkov. All the walls of the station are covered with light marble, and the platform is immediately illuminated and decorated with marble floor lamps, which are surrounded by benches for passengers.

Metro "Kantemirovskoe". Milestones in History





The project of this station was developed by architects R. Pogrebnoy and V. Phillipov, with the participation of I. Plyukhin. According to their idea, this single-vault station is only 8 meters deep, built in an open way and has no columns at all, and the design of the station's arch was originally made of integral concrete.

Few people know that on the Kashirskaya-Orekhovo section, the station opened twice. This happened for the first time in 1984 - December 30, but that night there was flooding due to a violation of the waterproofing. The station was closed. And only a month later, it was possible to eliminate all the shortcomings and open the station for the second time in February 1985.

Metro "Kantemirovskoe". Station features





Near the Kantemirovskaya station there is the Tsaritsyno Palace Complex, where you can have a good time at any time of the year. This is a favorable, environmentally friendly area with beautiful parks, lots of trees, ponds. Also in this area is well developed social infrastructure. Near the station there are three hospitals (including a nursery), pharmacies, 2 universities, 26 schools (including art and music), 5 kindergartens. In addition, 8 cinemas, 13 shopping centers, cafes and restaurants with various cuisines - Russian, European, Caucasian and Japanese; pool, sauna, hairdressers and beauty salons.

"Kantemirovskaya" is a metro station that does not have ground pavilions, and you must climb stairs to the underground vestibules from the station platform. From the lobby you can exit into the ground crossings, one of which will lead to Proletarsky Prospekt and Kavkazsky Boulevard, and the second to Kantemirovskaya Street.
