The recreation center "Beretskaya Polyana"





The recreation center is located on the banks of the Mana River and consists of three block houses, cottages and small luxury houses. Three block houses have ten rooms with one or two beds. The blocks can accommodate up to sixty guests at a time. The rooms are divided into two functional areas (dressing room, kitchen and residential) and are equipped with upholstered furniture, tables, cabinets. In the bathroom with a shower, guests are offered a set of towels and hygiene items. The recreation center "Beret" (Krasnoyarsk) can offer its guests conditions of increased comfort.

Accommodations



Two luxury cottages have four, eight, nine and ten-bed rooms. They are divided into kitchen and living areas. The kitchen is equipped with a stove, refrigerator. It has dishes and a kettle.
