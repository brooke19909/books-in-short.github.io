Work as a home operator in Tinkoff

One of the most sought after vacancies at Tinkoff is teleworking at home. There are several directions for potential employees:

  • sales

  • transaction support;

  • counseling;

  • customer retention.

The most popular channel is active sales of the products of the organization under study. According to job reviews, work at home at Tinkoff Bank, especially sales, is well paid. The average salary of sales managers who work without leaving home is 46 thousand rubles.

No dress code, meetings and travel costs: the manager himself chooses a convenient schedule and the number of hours of work per day. Future specialists will find out about this even before employment.

Contributions to the FIU and tax are deductible, but the employee cannot count on paid sick leave and leave, including maternity leave.

  2. Meetings online only. Often, novice employees want to receive recommendations and advice personally, but consultations after the device are possible only via Skype.

Many people are mistrustful of the GPC agreement. This is easy to explain: according to the Tinkoff company‚Äôs terms, the contract is renegotiated once a month. This means that the result must be shown regularly, without long downtimes and failures.

Couriers: features of work

Many customers are aware that Tinkoff Bank provides services only online and does not have branches. Therefore, the delivery of banking products, namely credit cards and debit cards, is carried out by the Tinkoff courier.

What is the responsibility of the Tinkoff courier:

  • receive a package for a customer;

  • inform the recipient of the visit and make an appointment;

  • to deliver the card without delay and only to its owner;

  • take the documents of receipt.

According to reviews, working as a courier at Tinkoff Bank is one of the easiest directions. It does not require sales skills. Payment for couriers is therefore low. On average, Tinkoff employees receive from 15 to 25 thousand rubles. They are compensated for travel, discounts on bank products.

However, the main focus of work in the banking business is associated with the profit of a commercial organization. Therefore, lately couriers who only deliver correspondence are not required by the bank. Jobs couriers on the Web almost never occur, unlike the other, a popular type of earnings - a sales representative of Tinkoff Bank.

How to get a job?

Even those who do not have any work experience or sales skills can become a representative of Tinkoff Bank. Applicants are subject to the minimum requirements:

Gas expenses are reimbursed at the end of the month. But even without a car, the applicant has every chance of becoming a representative.

Most reviews of work at Tinkoff Bank come from Moscow and the surrounding regions.
