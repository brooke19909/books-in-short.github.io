How to make crocheted flower? Scheme

Want to decorate a hat or blouse? Need to update some thing from the wardrobe? Make a crocheted flower . The execution scheme of such a decor is simple. Look at different options, choose your favorite sample. When you understand the principle of creating these objects, you can come up with your own models.



Nice needlework

In order to learn how to crochet flowers, it does not take a lot of time and effort. Each product is small, so it is done quickly. When you understand the principle of operation and speed appears, you can easily perform several dozen elements in one go.

It is worth mastering the different options and ways to create colors. Some products are knitted as a single unit in a circle, others - first in the form of an openwork strip, which is later assembled in a spiral and fixed with a thread. Complex volumetric decorations are made from individual pieces that are stitched together. Mastering all the techniques will significantly diversify the range of what you can do.



Crocheted openwork flowers (patterns)

If you are still inexperienced in this type of needlework, it is worth starting with simple options. Better to use samples. You can train on the threads of the same color, see the density of knitting. When the first samples are ready, proceed to more complex templates.



How to make crocheted flower? A schema usually has standard conventions. Remembering them is easy. In the form of an oval, an air loop is depicted. A stick is a single crochet, crossed out once a stick is a double crochet, two transverse dashes indicate a double crochet, a cross represents a half- column or a lifting loop. This is the basic thing that will be required to make the flowers. Any knitting in a circle begins with a set of air loops, and then the work goes in accordance with the figure.

What can be decorated with such elements?

Love updating things? Looking for new ideas? To decorate various items of clothing, accessories, use a crocheted flower. The scheme of work will be as follows:

  1. Choose a yarn suitable for your item in a shade (contrasting or matching).
  2. According to any sample, make a flat or three-dimensional product in the required number of copies.
  3. Take what you will decorate and sew elements.
  4. As a complement, use beads, buttons, knitted leaves, laces.




Crocheted flowers are used to decorate the following things:

  • scarves;
  • hats;
  • sweaters;
  • dresses;
  • children's clothes, booties, socks, shoes, panamos;
  • bags;
  • interior dolls and soft toys;
  • bedspreads and covers for furniture.




You can use these colorful decorations for curtains and curtain magnets. And also for original wall clocks, tied caskets, containers, gift wraps, photo frames and mirrors. You can find the most diverse application.

What accessories can be made from knitted flowers?

In addition to decorating ready-made things and updating existing ones, from the associated colors you can come up with many ideas for beautiful accessories and souvenirs for the interior and even make garments.

If you liked crocheting flowers and want to try all the patterns and options, and in different color combinations, do them not only in pleasure, but also with benefit. From several colors or a large number of them, you can do the following:

  • scarf;
  • belt;
  • neck pendant, brooch, bracelet and even earrings;




  • hoop and hair clips;
  • vest;
  • top or blouse;
  • napkin, tablecloth;
  • volumetric decoration in an interior vase;




  • mural on the wall.

Everything is limited by your imagination and patience. For large things (such as a blouse), it is better to select such color options that easily connect to the canvas in the form of a picture. For small accessories, use any combination.
