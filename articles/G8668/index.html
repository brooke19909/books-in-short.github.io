Making Bean Crafts

Everywhere we are surrounded by natural materials that can be used in the joint work of children and parents. For example, you can make bean crafts. It is stored in the kitchen of many mothers. Using it in his work, the baby, of course, together with his parents will develop fine motor skills. What will mothers and kids make? What products? You can make different crafts from beans with your own hands, such as paintings, three-dimensional figures, applications and much more.

Making beauty fast and easy

The simplest thing you can create is a beautiful interior detail. How? To do this, you need a bottle (of course, empty) and beans of different colors. Alternating, fill the beans into the container. After you fill it all, you will get an original, bright and beautiful thing.

Handmade applique

Talking about how to create bean crafts, I would like to dwell on the application. Now we will tell you how to make a chicken. To work, you will need:

• eight seeds;

• color cardboard (green, red);

• a simple pencil;

• scissors;

• black plasticine;

• 1 grain of corn;

• white beans;

• red plasticine;

• glue.

The process of making bean crafts

1. Draw a chicken silhouette on the cardboard (red), then cut it out.

2. Then stick it on the green paper.

3. Now take the red plasticine, pinch off a small piece and glue it to one bean. It must be attached to the chicken. Seal the whole bird with beans in the same way. Just do not forget to leave a place for the beak and eyes.

4. Next, take the plasticine in black, roll a small ball. Stick it on the chicken, it will be the eye.

5. Then take the corn kernel, spread it with plasticine (red) and attach to the chicken.

6. Now you need seeds, as well as plasticine in black. Now we will make the chicken legs. First glue one at a time, and at the very bottom three seeds. That's all, our wonderful application called “chicken” is ready.

Pattern of black and white beans - cute cats





You can do not only crafts from beans, but also paintings. Such products will become a decoration of any room. For work you will need:

• black paint;

• wooden cloth;

• black and white beans;

• glue;

• White paint;

• pencil.





Making beans

1. First draw a sketch of the picture with a pencil.

2. Then paint it with paints.

3. Then you need beans in color: white - white, black - black. That's all, the picture is ready.

Making a beautiful bean tree

In the end, I would like to tell you how to make a bonsai tree. To create such a miracle, you will need:

• threads;

• brush;

• flower pot;

• Red beans;

• silicate glue;

• balloon;

• varnish for wood (choose a dark color);

• a branch for a tree trunk.





Product manufacturing process:

1. The process of making this bean craft will begin with the preparation of a balloon. It must be inflated. Then, dipping the thread into the glue, wrap the ball with it.

2. Then wait until the product dries and lower the ball.

3. Next, take one bean and grease them with glue, glue on a ball of thread. It is advisable to take threads of the same tone as the legumes.

4. Then paint the tree branch with paint (brown).

5. Now install it in the pot and secure with pebbles.

Little conclusion

Now you know what crafts can be made for children from beans. Such products can be made together with your child. You just need to be careful so that the baby does not get beans in the mouth. It is advisable to engage in such creativity with children older than three years.
