Mobile dolphinarium in Ufa

In the life of a modern person, there is often a lack of communication and interaction with animals. However, citizens have the opportunity to visit zoos and dolphinariums. Such leisure for the whole family will certainly bring a lot of positive emotions. First of all, it will be useful for children. After all, it is they who most openly and joyfully react to all living beings. With regard to the development and expansion of horizons, this will also be extremely useful.



Program of performances

Dolphinarium in Ufa is a unique opportunity to get acquainted with marine mammals live. Residents and visitors of the city should definitely take this opportunity. Tours of the Moscow mobile dolphinarium began in December 2012. Since that time, not far from the Arena Expo shopping center, their tent was located. The Dolphinarium in Ufa-2013 is a great opportunity to introduce children to marine life and relax in a relaxed atmosphere.

For the tour, the artists prepared special performances. The dolphinarium in Ufa presents the audience an amazingly beautiful and fun dolphin show. Positive mammals will show their skills. But that is far from all. The program also offers numbers of fur seals that will demonstrate their mastery of dance. The white whale, a favorite of the public and the most unusual artist of the troupe, will perform before the audience.



The organizers of the tour claim that the dolphinarium in Ufa is installed in accordance with all the rules and complies with safety standards. Inside, the most comfortable conditions for animals were created, including the temperature of water and air in the room. You can not worry about animals, especially when you watch them move around the stage - they do it with such pleasure and activity. The artists work time is 45 minutes. During this time, they all have time to show what they are capable of, and get their portion of refreshments.

In addition to watching the show, a list of additional services is provided for guests. For example, after the performance, children and adults can come up and chat with mammals - stroking, talking, smiling. In addition, you can make a memorable photo, the cost of which must be clarified on the spot.

Schedule and ticket prices

So, the performances of marine artists are certainly worth a visit to the dolphinarium in Ufa. The work schedule depends on the day of the week. On Wednesday, Thursday and Friday, performances are held once a day - at 18:00. On weekends, the number of sessions increases to three. The time of performances is 12:00, 15:00, 18:00.

The cost of a ticket to the show depends on the number, place and age of the visitor. For adults, it ranges from 400 to 800 rubles. For children under 3 years of age, free admission to the event is provided. The availability of tickets must be consulted in advance by calling the institution, since due to increased demand they can be bought up in advance.



A trip to the dolphinarium in Ufa will leave an unforgettable experience. It will be interesting for both adults and children. It is here that you can laugh heartily and have fun with the whole family.
