Pamukkale

This amazing natural phenomenon is visible to tourists from afar. It‚Äôs hard not to notice in advance from the bus a pearly rock complex of travertine, decorated with centuries-old salt deposits of mineral thermal springs. Healing geysers from underground cured even the ancient Romans. The resulting cascade of small and large pools with water level differences is very picturesque. The most famous pond of Pamukkale is the Cleopatra pool, heated by a geyser. There tourists are allowed to swim.

Cappadocia

The unique creations of nature are also the rocks of Cappadocia, drained for thousands and thousands of years from rocks to a uniform harmonious oblique mushroom-shaped with conical "caps". However, in addition to natural creations, the land of Cappadocia is decorated with creations of human hands. The locals here are almost connected with nature, literally "leaving the rocks." We are talking about underground churches of the early Christians cut down in the rocks, and about dwellings - apartments, also equipped in the rocks.

Grotto Dalmatash

An unforgettable experience for tourists can be an excursion to one of the most magnificent and famous grottoes in the world. He is called Dalmatash. It was opened by accident. The mining company produced industrial explosions. The laid dynamite opened an underground passage leading to a rocky cavity.



However, cavers who arrived at the call found that the stalactite and stalagmitic cave, located below sea level, at different times served as a shelter. At first, corsairs hid from the persecution of the sultans here.

During World War II, a special German intelligence unit was hiding here.

Now the grotto is a popular tourist attraction. Majestic stalactites and stalagmites are specially highlighted. Classical music constantly sounds. Here, all year round, the temperature is +22 0 and humidity is 98%. Such a microclimate also has a therapeutic effect. Therefore, some tourists visit this cave not only for an aesthetic impression, but also with health benefits.

In addition, the park has a large terrarium, an aquarium with exotic fish.
