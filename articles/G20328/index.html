Smartphone Samsung J3 (2017): reviews, specs

The brand positions the J line as an initial level, even though the cost of the upper models can reach the mark of 20 thousand rubles. The price spread could not but affect the technical characteristics of the devices. The most prominent representatives of the series are the gadgets J3, J5 and J7.

The company presents new models as an alternative to the more noble A-series. That is, J-devices are designed for those who need advanced, as well as expensive functionality, without need, but want to have a gadget from a well-known brand in their pocket, and not some kind of no-name device from China with an unclear guarantee and incomprehensible service.

Nevertheless, the abundance of more than competitive "Chinese" makes the younger J-series (J3 / J5) not so attractive. So here we are talking more about the purchase of a venerable brand, rather than functionality and some notable features. Consider the most attractive option for domestic consumers - the J3 model, the youngest in the series.

So, the subject of today's review is the Samsung Galaxy J3 (2017) smartphone. Characteristics, owner reviews, advantages and disadvantages of the gadget, as well as the appropriateness of the purchase will be discussed in our article.

Here we are talking about the AMOLED-screen, which is equipped with older models in the series. In fact, the gadget received even a good, but still TFT-matrix. On the flip side there is a very modest specification and manufacturer markers. At the ends you can see the accompanying labels and stickers of distributors. Appearance

The manufacturer did not become wise with color schemes and released the J-series in the usual classic colors. In stores you can find blue gadgets, golden, pink and black. Judging by the reviews on the Samsung Galaxy J3 (2017) Black smartphone, buyers are fed up with black, and a good half of users prefer blue or gold colors. Pink devices are extremely rare, but due to the specificity of the shade, it is almost not in demand, so this point is difficult to record in cons.

The dimensions of the device can be called acceptable for its diagonal - 143 x 70 x 8 mm. With a weight of 142 grams, the model fits well in the hand and there is no feeling of discomfort even with prolonged use. Reviews about the smartphone Samsung Galaxy J3 (2017) in terms of ergonomics are completely positive: the device is really convenient and fits perfectly in the palm of your hand.

The case of the device received a metal, but with extensive inserts in the upper and lower parts. Comparing older models with J3, you can see that the brand saved on design: visually the device looks like a budget employee, and only an expensive case can hide it. The next thing the manufacturer saved on are the sensors. The smartphone does not have a fingerprint module, as well as an automatic light sensor. The latter is worth a penny, and such savings are not entirely clear. Users in their reviews of the Samsung Galaxy J3 (2017) have repeatedly blamed the brand for such a solution. Even frankly cheap Chinese models have a light sensor, and some have a fingerprint scanner. So here the manufacturer clearly went too far with cutting out the excess.

Interfaces

The usual Samsung physical button is located on the front panel, and two touch keys are arranged on the sides. They work perfectly and respond well, only they are completely deprived of backlighting - again, savings.

In the upper front part there is a peephole of the front camera with a flash. On the left side is the volume rocker, and on the right is the power key. The speaker is also located at the end just above the power button. This decision caused a mixed reaction in reviews of the Samsung Galaxy J3 (2017) smartphone.

On the one hand, the speaker does not cause any problems and even looks original. But on the other, his voice was noticeably reduced. And if other gadgets from Samsung differed not only in high-quality sound, but also in a good supply of volume level, then here we have only a solid middling.

The bottom end is reserved for a standard 3.5 mm mini-jack and a micro-USB interface for synchronization with a computer, peripherals and recharging. The slots for SIM and SD cards are located on the left side: one for the nano format, the other for an external drive or the same SIM card.

Assembly

As for the build quality, there are no complaints. Users in their reviews of the smartphone Samsung J330F Galaxy J3 (2017) did not note any creaks, backlashes, gaps and other shortcomings that are characteristic of gadgets of the budget segment.

All parts are tightly fitted to each other and the device looks monolithic. So here we have a solid five: the affiliation of the gadget to a noble brand is still felt.

Display

There is no automatic adjustment of brightness and contrast, but there are several presets for different operational scenarios. For example, the included "Outdoor" mode twists the brightness to its maximum value and the screen becomes easy to read even in direct sunlight.

The screen has no specific settings. In general, users in their reviews of the Samsung J3 (2017) are perplexed about this. If the internal drive is not enough, then there is the opportunity to increase the volume with the help of external SD-cards, up to 256 GB.

Judging by the reviews of the Samsung Galaxy J3 (2017) SM J330F, users are generally satisfied with the performance of the gadget. In fact, for what paid, then received. Communications

All the same savings for the sake of the manufacturer equipped the gadget with a single-band Wi-Fi module. Users in their reviews of the Samsung J3 (2017), especially city dwellers, have repeatedly complained about this decision. In large cities, the air is packed to the maximum, and with one range of normal operation it will not work.

In addition, the device lacks ANT +; it does not even have the necessary NFC module in it today. There are no complaints about the Bluetooth wireless protocol: version 4.2 works as it should and transfers data quite quickly. The GPS module, it can be said, functions as standard, and there are no questions about it either. Platform

The smartphone runs on the Android operating system version 7.0.1. In addition to the standard set of applications, the manufacturer finally included an intelligent FM radio in the firmware. Previous versions of the latter were so buggy that they caused a storm of negative emotions among users. In the latest versions of the platform, the holes patched up, and now everything works as it should.

We should also mention the KNOX function, where the user has the opportunity to install any application in two copies at once. This is very convenient if you actively use instant messengers like WhatsApp on your working and home SIM cards.

Standalone time

The gadget received a 2400 mAh lithium-ion battery. For the voracious "android" brother, this is clearly not enough. The tacit minimum in the budget segment ranges from 3000 mAh, but here we have a very modest battery.

If you use a smartphone specifically as a phone or messenger and, with rare exceptions, allow yourself to play games or watch high-quality videos, then the charge will last for two, or even three days, with a margin.

Fans of games and other multimedia entertainment will be sorely lacking in battery life. The device in this mode is barely enough for daylight hours, and already closer to dinner, it begins to "ask" for the outlet. So autonomy is one of the weakest features of the model.

Wireless charging, like the older generation, is not here, but for most users this is not critical. But what I wanted to get was a quick charge. Owners in their reviews have repeatedly complained about the manufacturer because of the lack thereof. With such a modest indicator of autonomy, such a necessary thing as instant recharging is simply necessary. In normal mode, the battery is recharged in 2.5-3 hours.

Summarizing

As for the phone, there are no complaints against our respondent: the connection is good, the vibrating alert is palpable, the caller is heard very well, as well as you. The speaker volume is average, but you can‚Äôt call the device quiet.

The smartphone is suitable for those who need a telephone gadget. For those who often surf the Internet and like to play games or watch movies, J3 is far from the best option. Here we have a mediocre TFT-matrix and the same performance. All this is perfectly optimized for standard tools, but something really serious he, alas, will not pull. Yes, even if it pulls, it will last no more than an hour with such a modest battery.

J3 is the youngest model in its series and costs about 12 thousand rubles. The next generation (J5 / J7) has more serious components and costs respectively - 15 and 18 thousand rubles. If you are looking for a normal gadget in the mid-price segment, then the J5 and J7 models are very good options, which cannot be said about J3. It is difficult to attribute it to the budget category because of the price, but it does not reach the average in its characteristics.

If you are not an ardent fan of the Samsung brand, and you need an intelligent device up to 10 thousand rubles, then it is better to pay attention to the solid "Chinese" from "Meizu", "Huawei" and "Xiaomi". They prevail in the budget segment and offer really high-quality and inexpensive gadgets.
