Ferry Stockholm - Tallinn: description, reviews

Traveling by ferry is one of the most romantic. The Big House, floating on the waves, equipped with all amenities and filled with various entertainments: restaurants and shops, will not leave indifferent any passenger.

What is a ferry?

A ferry is a watercraft designed to transport goods, cars, passengers and even railway cars. As a rule, they run along certain water routes, from one stop to another. Able to walk on virtually any body of water: lake, river, bays and even seas.

Stockholm Tallinn ferry description

Communication on the ferry line along the route Stockholm - Tallinn is carried out by Tallinn. Two ferries run between these cities at once: Victoria I and Romance. If the trip is planned with a return to the departure point, then you can go to Tallinn on one, and return to Stockholm on another ship.



On board there is a large and varied selection of restaurants and bars. Here you can have a tasty and leisurely dinner in a restaurant, have a light meal in a fast food cafe or sit in a pleasant company in a pub or bar. For those who cannot sleep at night, the Stockholm - Tallinn ferry offers an extensive entertainment program in the show bar, and there is a night disco for everyone. For gambling people on board there is a casino and slot machines.

Children will also have something to do, because a large game room works for them, where you can play, draw or watch cartoons.

For lovers of shopping, there are 3 shops operating on the ferry, including a tax-free system. Here you can buy a variety of sweets, jewelry, perfumes, small accessories and, of course, souvenirs.

Stockholm - Tallinn ferry is equipped with a spa, a conference room. The information desk is open around the clock.

The cabin on the ferry you can choose from 5 options: luxury, deluxe, A-class, B-class and for people with disabilities.

The size of the luxury cabin is 30 square meters. m, it is designed for a family of 4 people.



Facilities: fridge, TV, shower and toilet. A la carte restaurant, soft drinks and fruits are included in the cabin price.

Deluxe class cabin size 14 square meters. m. It also has a refrigerator, TV and shower with toilet. The price includes 2 visits to the restaurant, as well as soft drinks and fruits.

A-class cabins are 2-seater and 4-seater. All are equipped with air conditioning, shower and toilet.

B-class cabins measuring 8 square meters. m are designed for up to 4 people. A distinctive feature is the lack of windows. There is air conditioning, shower and toilet.

For people with disabilities, there are cabins for 2 people on board the ferry. And cabins for people suffering from allergies can accommodate up to 4 people.

There are also several cabins for people traveling with pets.

The Tallinn - Stockholm ferry, reviews of which are exceptionally positive in color, runs between the two capitals year-round and daily. Therefore, you can go on a trip at any time.

Ferry St. Petersburg - Helsinki - Stockholm - Tallinn: description

The ferry that runs on this line is called Princess Anastasia. You can go on a cruise on it all year round. This route is operated by St. PeterLine, an international ferry operator in St. Petersburg.



On board the ferry, each passenger will find suitable entertainment for themselves.

You can relax and unwind in the spa, where there is a sauna, swimming pools for adults and children, massages, beauty and wellness treatments. There is also a cinema with sessions for children and adults, a casino, a children's play room and slot machines.

There is a Duty Free system store and several restaurants and bars with a varied cuisine and drinks, as well as a night disco. In summer, for lovers of parties, there is an open-air disco.

The route is constructed in such a way that, in addition to entertainment on board, you can spend a full day on land - the Peter - Helsinki - Stockholm - Tallinn ferry and its schedule allows you to visit the Scandinavian capital cities.

Helsinki - Stockholm - Tallinn Ferry: description





Ferry companies do not offer cruise offers on this route, but it can be combined. For example, Viking Line offers regular flights from Helsinki to Stockholm with the Mariella and Gabriella ferries. And from Stockholm you can take advantage of the Tallinn company offer, buy a ticket for the Stockholm - Tallinn ferry to visit the capital of Estonia.

The Mariella and Gabriella ferries have similar characteristics to those described above in terms of quality and quantity of amenities and entertainment on board. Cabins are also of a similar class, including internal equipment.

What time of year is better to swim

Ferry lines operate year-round, and most ferries leave the route daily.

Reviews of vacationers call spring, summer and autumn the best time to travel by ferry. The upper decks are already open, on which you can climb and admire the surrounding beauties, it is no longer cold and the sun is shining, under the spring and autumn rays of which it is so good to warm up, and under the summer - to sunbathe.

In addition, with the advent of summer days, various outdoor activities are available on the open decks of the ferries.

Booking and prices: reviews

The higher the tourist season, the higher ferry prices. But the cost depends not only on the season, but also on the class of the chosen cabin and the included services. So, when buying a full package, including, for example, accommodation in a cabin, breakfast, dinner, transfer, the price will be the most favorable.

If the trip takes place lightly, and its emphasis is on visiting cities, it is quite possible to save by booking an economy-class cabin. It is worth noting that buying food on board costs a little more than if you purchase it in advance.

Also, to save money, it is best to book a ferry in advance, as a rule, carriers make very good discounts on early bookings. Closer to departure dates, prices are on the price list, and such a wide selection of cabins and meals may simply not exist.

If the trip takes place with a car, the ferry companies often offer interesting packages, including a cabin, meals and car seats on board at very competitive prices.
