How to tie a belt beautifully?

Along with a bag, wallet and stylish shoes, the belt has become an integral part of the fashion wardrobe of every modern girl. It used to be a belt or belt used exclusively for the utilitarian purpose of supporting trousers or a skirt so as not to fall off. Now it is a stylish accessory without which the image cannot be considered complete. And there are an incredible amount of ways to tie a belt beautifully.



Now belts are not only worn with trousers or skirts. They adorn dresses, outerwear, blouses and jackets. How to tie a belt, for example, on a blouse? If the belt is thin, you can simply limit yourself to a beautiful knot on top of the buckle. Another option on how to tie a belt on a blouse: try to fix it not on the waist, but under the chest. This is a win-win combination for a contrast belt and a white shirt or blouse with skinny pants or jeans. In this case, you can also play on the contrast of textures - combine a thin blouse made of chiffon with a coarse belt for jeans, best of worn, artificially aged leather.



Another way to tie a belt in style is to use several belts at once. Keep them in the same color scheme or style. A combination of two narrow multi-colored belts worn one above the other will also look good. The ends of the belts behind the back should be crossed. Then in front you get two multi-colored domino belts. Another way to wear several narrow belts is suitable for military-style outerwear. Put one such belt on your waist, as it should be worn, and cross two of the same belts in the manner of a sword belt on your chest, throw it over your shoulder, and tie it around the main belt. Such an image will attract a lot of attention of others.

How to tie a belt on a dress? Remember that in addition to narrow belts, corset-type belts are also popular. They will make any dress feminine, accentuate the waist and highlight the hips. Not bad on almost any type of figure will look ethnic belts on the hips. Decorated with decorative elements, tassels, pompons, embroidery or beads, they will also be able to revive some models of dresses and tunics that do not differ in shape.



Remember also that besides branded belts, the most unexpected things can act as this accessory: satin ribbons of the right shades, lace stripes, neckerchiefs, scarves, leather and velvet laces. Even wide motley laces for skate shoes can turn into a stylish belt if they are combined with clothes. Also, a stylish belt can be sewn independently and trimmed with beads, glass beads, beads, artificial flowers, knitted elements and other accessories. And one more tip. How to tie a belt to create an ultra-stylish image? Tie a long chain to an unusual knot or use a long string of beads. Such an accessory will look especially advantageous with jeans or a romantic dress. Just do not wear jewelry around your neck, so as not to look like a magpie. Such a small-looking belt or belt opens up the scope of imagination and helps to make the same outfit completely different.
