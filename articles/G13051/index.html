Turkey: nice beach. Belek, Turkey, beaches. Holidays in Turkey

Tourists from all over the world are famous for Turkey as the best country for vacation. About 15 million tourists come there every year. What attracts them? A high level of service, a clean warm sea, the beauty of nature and an abundance of historical and architectural monuments are what Turkey is famous for. It is quite easy to find a good beach there, because it is for this that most tourists come to this country.

Turkey beaches

This state is considered the best for families, so the greatest interest is not the availability of developed infrastructure, but sandy clean beaches. Especially important for most tourists, a gentle descent into the water and a shallow depth near the shore. Recently, all Turkish beaches have become publicly available, even for a sunbed and an umbrella, you do not have to pay everywhere.



A lot of wonderful places of rest are located far from the resorts, and they are known only to local residents. Tourists most often visit city beaches or those owned by hotels and restaurants. Most of them need to pay for umbrellas and sunbeds, and only in expensive hotels is this included in the price. Therefore, when buying a ticket you need to choose not only a resort, but also a hotel, because often even two beaches are different nearby. Turkey is a country with a rocky topography, so many of them are rocky and pebbled. The most popular tourist destinations are the coasts of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas. The mild climate and developed resort infrastructure attract people here. Sandy beaches of Turkey are located mainly along the shores of the Mediterranean Sea.

Beach Behavior

There are several rules that all vacationers should know:

  • Many hotels often prohibit outsiders from entering their territory, but most beaches can be accessed for free. But for the sunbed and umbrella have to pay.
  • On the beaches owned by restaurants, you can relax only if you order something.
  • After sunset , it is forbidden to swim on city beaches, because the current in the sea is very strong and can drag a person far into the open sea.
  • Shared beaches are rarely cleaned and cleaned, only hotels maintain cleanliness. Especially where the surface is covered with small pebbles, garbage is noticeable: cigarette butts, food debris.
  • More than 100 beaches in Turkey are awarded the Blue Flag sign, which means that there is very clean water and sand, so littering is prohibited there.

Sandy beaches in the Mediterranean

Magnificent landscapes, a clean warm sea and mild climate attract tourists on the Mediterranean coast. The most popular resorts are located east of Antalya. This is a large modern city with a developed infrastructure and an abundance of entertainment for tourists. The holiday season here lasts from April to October, and the sea warms up to 27 degrees. It is best to relax in the suburbs. One of the most popular is Lara, located 12 kilometers from Antalya. Many hotels, a long strip of sandy beaches and proximity to a large city attract tourists with children here. The sand here is shallow and soft, and the descent into the water is gentle. In addition to Antalya, on the Mediterranean coast there are many resorts with sandy beaches, for example, Belek, Side and other less well-known. The largest of them is Alanya. The beaches there are covered with dense yellow sand and are mainly located in the bay, so there are no big waves on them. The coastline stretches in a narrow strip for 20 kilometers. Very interesting is a two-kilometer stretch of coast, which is called Cleopatra's beach.

Rest on pebble beaches

But not only because of this, Turkey is famous. A good beach is not necessarily sand. Many pebble beaches are also very popular. They are located in the central and western parts of the coast. Their peculiarity is that water in such places

usually more transparent. But mainly young people choose such beaches, since when entering the water there is a sharp decrease, often they make descent from the pontoons or on the stairs. In those places where large smooth cobbles, such as in Belbidi, you can slip at the entrance to the water. On some beaches, pebbles are mixed with coarse sand. Such places are difficult to clean, so they are usually quite dirty. Cigarette butts and food debris are often visible. But the prices there are much more affordable.

Kemer beaches

Turkey in recent decades has become the most popular resort country. Its convenient location between the four seas in the mild, warm climate attracts more and more tourists every year. A lot of interesting things there
places with beautiful landscapes. One of them is the resort area of ​​Kemer. It includes many villages and detached large hotels located between the Mediterranean Sea and the mountains. Wide sandy and pebble beaches stretch for many kilometers along the coast. Where the surface is covered with coarse pebbles, the cleanest water in the world. The best sandy beach is Yoryuk, and the most popular among young people is Moonlight. Although it is sandy and pebble and therefore a bit dirty, but there are many water activities and there is a park.

Resort Belek, Turkey





The beaches here stretch along the coast for 20 kilometers. This resort is awarded the blue flag - a symbol of the purity of the sea and sand. The fact that Belek has the cleanest sandy beaches is known not only in Turkey. This is a favorite vacation spot for many tourists from around the world. Protected by rocky mountains, Belek offers vacationers not only a clean beach and sea, but also calm windless weather and the smell of pine needles from a nearby pine forest. Mostly young people travel to pebble beaches, as there are a lot of sports activities and a water park. And all-inclusive meals allow you to choose what you like best.

Side Resort





It is located 65 kilometers east of Antalya. This is another place that Turkey is famous for. The beaches of Side are snow-white clean sand, a gentle slope to the sea and green areas of numerous hotels. This resort is one of the oldest in the country. The water in the sea is clean, and the inhabited territories of hotels delight the eye with lush greenery. The city itself is crowded and noisy, and recreational areas are mainly located in the suburbs. The beach is located in a cozy bay, and there is a pine forest with clean air nearby. A distinctive feature of the resort of Side is the presence of archaeological sites - you can visit the ruins of the term and the historical museum.

Beaches on the coast of the Aegean

There are many resorts for which Turkey is famous. A good beach for a relaxing family vacation, however, is not easy to find here, because the coast is completely pebble. Sand is very rare, and it is mostly bulk. But for lovers of outdoor activities and antiquities - this is an ideal place. What are the most popular Aegean resorts?

  1. Marmaris is located in a bay on the border between the Aegean and the Mediterranean Sea. There is very clear water, but the beach is narrow with large golden brown sand.
  2. Calis is located 5 kilometers from Fethiye. The surface of the beach is white sand mixed with fine pebbles. This is a nature reserve, as sea turtles of a rare species come here.
  3. Oludeniz is located behind a sand spit, mountains surround it on all sides, so there is no storm at all. Paragliding is popular here.




Ancient resorts of the Aegean

  1. Bodrum is located between two seas on a small peninsula. This is a city with a rich history, in ancient times it was called Helicarnassus, so there are many ruins. Now it is the capital of nightlife and outdoor activities. Water sports and diving are very developed here. Bodrum is also attractive because the air here is dry, so there is no sweltering heat. The beaches here are pebble or covered with concrete slabs. Often the descent into the water is so steep that steps or even elevators lead to the sea.
  2. Pamuchak will also appeal to history buffs. Nearby is the ancient city ​​of Ephesus, where you can see the house of the Virgin Mary and the Temple of Artemis. The Meander River flows right along the beach. There are no shops or hotels in the resort itself.
  3. Olympos is a quiet ancient town with no signs of civilization. It is attractive with large pine forests with many marble ruins. Here you can also look at the "chimera" - the release of natural gas to the surface and its ignition.

Sandy beaches of Turkey for children

The best place for a family holiday is, of course, Turkey. It is easy to find a good beach for young children, but it is better to take care of this when buying a ticket. Of course, warm water, clean sand and many attractions can be found in almost all popular resorts. But there are those that are specifically focused on families with children.



  1. First of all, it is Belek. Everything is provided for a comfortable stay of mother with baby, even special meals.
  2. Lara and Side are characterized by fine sand and a very gentle descent into the water. These are great places to teach children swimming.
  3. Patara is considered the most beautiful beach in Turkey. Snow-white sand and calm environment also attract tourists with children. Here you can see architectural monuments. According to legend, St. Nicholas was born in Patara, who became the prototype of Santa Claus.
  4. Iztuzu is also called the "turtle beach" because once a year sea turtles lay their eggs there. The water there is clean, and the shore is shallow, which is ideal for families with children.
  5. For older children, Icmeler is a good fit. This place is also called "Lost Paradise." Pure water and healing springs, an abundance of interesting flora and fauna and picturesque mountains have a good rest.




So, what is interesting for Turkey? The beach - sand and pebbles, water sports and clear water, magnificent landscapes, mild climate and many modern attractions attract tourists from many countries. Everyone will find a place here to their liking, because there are secluded and quiet resorts, and there are modern ones with an active nightlife. Therefore, vacation in Turkey is the best way to spend your vacation.
