Your helpers are electronic steelyards

Sometimes in the markets there is a possibility of weighting. How to prevent this? How to accurately weigh the purchased goods? Electronic steelyards help to cope with this task. What are these devices? How accurate are their measurements, can they be trusted?

Description

The portable electronic steelyard consists of a plastic case on which there is an LCD display with backlight. It is on this screen that the weight of the object being weighed is displayed. It can be not only food. This appliance can weigh and zero the tare if necessary. Steelyards electric are automatically set to zero. They have the opportunity to choose a unit of weight. Many of these devices are capable of not only weighing, but also additionally measuring air temperature. If the steelyard is not used for some time, it will automatically turn off, which saves battery power.





Scope of application

Electronic steelyards have proven themselves as a new generation tool that allows accurate weighing under various conditions. These devices are used not only in trade, but also in everyday activities in the family, for example, when walking in nature to weigh the harvest of berries or mushrooms. Steelyards turn out to be a good accessory on hunting or fishing, helping to accurately determine the weight of prey, etc. Efficiency, ergonomic design, ease of use - this is an incomplete list of the advantages of this electronic device.

Measuring range

Electronic steelyards are able to determine the weight of the load with an accuracy of 10 g. The maximum permissible load is 40 kg (depending on model). It is not recommended to load your steelyard with too much weight.

Electrical parameters

Electric steelyards are able to operate on two AAA batteries. When the backlight is on, current consumption doubles. The manufacturers recommended operating temperature of this device is 10-40 degrees.
