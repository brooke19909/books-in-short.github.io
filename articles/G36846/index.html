Several options for making a hedgehog from natural materials

A huge number of various crafts can be made from natural materials. In this article you will learn how to make a hedgehog. This interesting work can be done together with the children, this will develop their imagination, as well as fine motor skills.

How to make a hedgehog with your own hands from cones?

It is quite simple. For work, you will need a bump, dry pine needles, a bit of plasticine and black peppercorns . First of all, you need to make a hedgehog needles. Variant of seeds and clay or plasticine

How to make a hedgehog from natural materials, such as seeds and clay (plasticine)? Everything is very simple! This lesson is suitable for the development of motor skills, attention and patience of the baby. It is necessary to mold the body of a hedgehog from clay or clay. In it, with a sharp end you need to stick the seeds. The nose and eyes can be made from black pepper with peas or plasticine. It turns out a very nice hedgehog.

Buckwheat and nutshell

Further We will tell you how to make a hedgehog from natural materials using buckwheat and nutshells. Such a hedgehog is made even simpler than from clay and seeds. In order to make it, you need half a nutshell, buckwheat, glue, black pepper peas and a black marker for drawing eyes. Glue the nose on the sharp edge of the shell. Draw the eyes. Apply glue to the surface of the shell and sprinkle with buckwheat. And if you choose shells of different sizes, you can make a whole family of hedgehogs.



And a few more options

You will learn more about how to make a hedgehog from natural materials using stones, rice, chestnuts, clay, autumn leaves, acorns and twigs from our article. To make pretty cute hedgehogs from stones and rice, pick up stones of the right size and shape while walking. Paint the stone to make it look like a hedgehog. When it dries, you can apply rice grains on its surface in the same way as buckwheat in the previous version.



In autumn, you can make hedgehogs from chestnuts. To do this, use both the chestnut itself and the peel from it. You can still make a hedgehog from the acorn and small twigs, but this is already more painstaking work. Although it will be useful for children to develop fine motor skills and perseverance. We attach the twigs, like needles, to the stomach we make the nose and eyes from plasticine. You can also mold paws from clay. Clay may also be used in this embodiment.

And finally

Even in autumn, wonderful hedgehogs from fall foliage are obtained. Returning with the children after a walk, at least once, but you brought home a bouquet of leaves. Do not rush to throw them, you can make a beautiful hedgehog from them. Make a hedgehog body from clay or plasticine and fix the leaves on it so that they stick out like thorns. Eyes and nose can be made from black pepper with peas or from small balls of plasticine.

From our article, you learned how to make a hedgehog from natural materials. This information is sure to come in handy if you have kids. Good luck in making crafts.
