A country with white beaches - choose the best

The fast pace of life, which does not give a second of rest, the accumulated mental and physical fatigue, poor mood and well-being - all this can be solved by changing the environment and going to rest on the coast of the sea or ocean. Any country with white beaches will allow you to break out of the grayness of working days and return energy and energy. There are many resorts where you can relax, and this vacation will leave an indelible imprint on life. Below are the states most suitable for a beach holiday.





China

A country with white beaches is China, in which rest is possible throughout the year due to the presence of more than three hundred sunny days a year. This state is famous for its long beaches, first-class hotel complexes, Asian features and incredibly low prices. Tourists will appreciate local excursions with immersion in the national flavor. Hainan is a popular beach tourism destination in China, expanding every year, due to new hotels and developed infrastructure.

Vietnam

Also very popular is an exotic country with white beaches - Vietnam, which is rapidly gaining recognition of tourists. This is a beautiful state, representing the most ancient history and culture, picturesque landscapes, beautiful beaches and a favorable climate. In addition to a luxurious beach holiday, you can visit interesting excursion programs, try healthy and tasty food, go in for the cheapest diving in the world and experience the healing and relaxing properties of spa treatments.





Thailand

You can relax and enjoy Thai massage in Thailand. This country with white beaches thanks to its coral reefs, luxurious flowers, exotic fruits and incense leaves only the best memories. The largest and best resort here is the huge island of Phuket, with its beautiful nature, bays, bays, palm beaches with fine white sand and sparkling sea dotted with islands. These places are amazing with excellent hotels, seafood, architecture, lush tropical landscape and the opportunity to play sports.

Tunisia

You can also highlight Tunisia - a small, but hospitable and surprisingly interesting country, which is considered the most Europeanized Arab state. The state has at its disposal five international airports, all types of beach and outdoor activities, noisy oriental bazaars and interesting excursions. This country with white beaches attracts with its Mediterranean Sea, olive groves, deserts, picturesque cork woods, good infrastructure and large hotel areas. The most popular resort regions can be called Monastir, Sousse, Hammamet with a variety of excursion programs, surfing, golf and modern thalassotherapy centers.



Conclusion

You can endlessly continue the list of countries that differ in beautiful coastal and beach areas, luxury hotels for every taste and affluence and interesting excursion programs. What is the best country with white beaches? The answer to this question is impossible to find, since each resort has its own charm and attractiveness, attracting and attracting each individual tourist.
