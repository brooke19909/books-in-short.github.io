Theodosian military sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation: description, services, location and reviews

Rest in Feodosia itself is beautiful. The unique climate of Crimea, beautiful landscapes, the sea - this is all that you can only dream about on vacation. In addition to rest, it is important for every military man to keep fit and healthy. The sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation in Feodosia was created both for recreation and for treatment. You can relax here year-round.

Feodosia - a city on the Black Sea

Theodosius is a historical monument of the Crimea. The city was founded by the Greeks in the 6th century BC. It still keeps monuments of ancient history. The Genoese fortress has been preserved to this day, and every tourist will be interested in visiting it.

In Feodosia, a large number of parks and squares. In one of them is the Feodosia Military Sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation. The health resort itself is buried in the greenery of the garden and is hidden from the eyes of passers-by. It is always quiet and calm, which creates excellent conditions for a good rest.

The climate here is very warm, while the fusion of mountain and steppe air eliminates stuffiness, the air masses here freely intersect with each other. Winters are mild, snow in Feodosia is a rare occurrence, but rains prevail in the winter months.





The territory of the sanatorium

Theodosia military sanatorium is located on the territory of a large park, given specifically for this institution. He first opened his doors in the summer of 1944. The park is home to Bronevsky, indirectly associated with the poet A.S. Pushkin. Here he stayed on the road to Gurzuf. Vacationers in the sanatorium will also be able to see the Pushkin Grotto.

Special attention deserves the beach health resort. It is isolated, equipped with shower, deck chairs, water rides. Also on the beach there is a first-aid post, buffet. This complex can serve up to 650 people on its territory at a time.





Treatment in a sanatorium

Of course, the main purpose of the complex is treatment. First of all, citizens are interested in the proposed health course, on which the description of the institution is based. Theodosia Central Military Clinical Sanatorium offers medical treatment for its guests:

  • diseases of the gastrointestinal tract;
  • urogenital system ailments in women;
  • diseases of the musculoskeletal system;
  • endocrine system diseases;
  • climatotherapy;
  • mud therapy.

Also here you can do physical therapy, raise immunity, start eating right. Theodosia Military Sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation, reviews of which can be read below, has an excellent base for treating teeth and gums.





Nutrition and mineral water

The Feodosia military sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation promises good food. There are four halls, each of which can serve up to 250 people at a time. Nutrition is agreed with dietitians and is based on ten different diets. This allows citizens with various pathologies to fully eat, including gastrointestinal diseases.

For those who do not need treatment of the digestive system and can sometimes afford barbecue and a glass of wine, there are a large number of cafes and restaurants outside the sanatorium, as well as discos that deserve good reviews.

Theodosia military sanatorium MO (Russia) is a health resort with natural mineral water. Her quality is known all over the world, for the first time she deserved the award back in 1916. Mineral water is prescribed for treatment to those who need it.





Accommodation in a sanatorium

Theodosia Military Sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation offers its guests a huge number of rooms of various categories, depending on the cost of the permit. Numbers are provided from the following fund:

  • dormitory building in 12 floors;
  • cottages;
  • dormitory building on 4 floors.

Numbers can include both single occupancy, and rest with family and children. The rooms are offered as follows:

  • one-room for one or two persons;
  • two-room apartment for two persons;
  • three-room for two or more persons.




The rooms of the class are divided into standard and superior. For guests of the health resort there is: shower, toilet, balcony or loggia, TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, electric kettle, a full set of furniture and other accessories, the list of which depends on the type of accommodation when buying a ticket to the Feodosia military sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation. Services are also agreed upon in advance or on site after being examined by a doctor.

The health resort officially informs that the checkout time starts at 00:00.

Voucher purchase

The sanatorium on the basis of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation provides vouchers on the following conditions:

  • preferential;
  • commercial.

The vouchers are sold directly by the Feodosia Military Sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation, as well as partners of the institution. To receive treatment, the following documents are required:

  • application for a permit;
  • identity document ;
  • certificate No. 070 / U.

Instructions for completing the certificate can be found on the official website of the sanatorium. When placing children with their parents, it is important to remember that they cannot receive treatment. For each child, an additional certificate from a dermatologist, a certificate of form No. 079 / o, a copy of the birth certificate is additionally provided.





Reviews about spa treatment

The health resort itself has experienced quite a few problems throughout its existence. Today, out of a large number of sleeping high-rise buildings, only two can work and receive guests. The rest require major repairs, which will take time and money.

Today the sanatorium has opened its doors under the Russian flag and is in great demand. As of the beginning of February 2016, all summer vacation packages have been fully implemented.

Judging by the reviews on the Internet pages, there is very good food and a medical base. All staff are responsive and love their work.

Negative reviews relate to a temporary lack of water. This is due to the establishment of the Crimea's water supply and brings significant inconvenience to guests, especially in the summer months. Due to this problem, the water pressure may be low. The authorities of the region undertake to completely solve this problem within the next two years. The health resort infrastructure has been preserved since the Soviet period and will soon be updated.
