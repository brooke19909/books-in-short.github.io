Essentuki: hotels. Essentuki: rest, treatment, entertainment

Every day, hundreds of tourists, businessmen, business travelers and just wishing to visit the famous health resorts, motels and hospitals come to Essentuki. One of the most developed areas here is the business of accommodating city guests. All kinds of hotels in Essentuki offer their guests daily.

Hotel Rus

Hotel Rus is located in the very center of the city. It offers guests free internet and a 24-hour front desk. The cafe serves traditional European and Russian cuisine. A ten minute walk from here is a concert hall named after Chaliapin.



Judging by the reviews, all rooms with interesting interiors are decorated in a unique style and equipped with air conditioning. Each has a fridge, TV and a private bathroom.

Hotel "Rus" offers dishes of European and Russian cuisine, which are served in a cafe, created in the classical style. It also has a games room for young guests. It is worth noting that for a fee, breakfast is served directly in the room.

The city center with restaurants and shops is a ten minute walk from here. Also nearby is a spa park.

Essentuki Train Station is a 15-minute leisurely walk, while Mineralnye Vody Airport is a forty-minute drive from here. Free parking is available.

Hotel Volna

Hotel "Volna" is located in the business center of Essentuki. Within walking distance there is all the necessary infrastructure: a park with fountains, a railway station, a concert hall, a post office, a bus stop, shops.



Reading the reviews, you can find out that on the territory of the guests' services - a sauna, a steam bath, a swimming pool, a Wi-Fi zone, a cafe, a paid guarded parking lot.

Volna Hotel offers 12 rooms of various categories with an unusual interior to accommodate guests.

Hotel Santa FAC

Hotel KVS (Essentuki) is located 10 minutes from the central scenic park. It offers guests - 39 rooms of different price categories. The hotel has a cafe of European and Russian cuisine, billiard tables and a traditional bathhouse. Breakfast is served here in the mornings. It can also be delivered directly to your room upon request. Room service is available.



Each one is furnished in a unique minimalist style. They are equipped with a TV, fridge and telephone. Each has a pair of slippers. Judging by the reviews, guests can relax in the cafe, read a free newspaper in it, visit the shops located on the hotel premises and play ping pong. The 24-hour front desk has a safe.

Hotel PLAZA Essentuki

Hotel "Essentuki", reviews of which you can find only the most admired, is located in the very center of the city, on Fountain Square and Theater Square, in the Medical Park, next to the Ministry of Affairs of the North-Kazakhstan region. It takes five minutes to walk to the Essentuki-17 and Essentuki-4 wells.

Judging by the reviews, among the amenities of the rooms are air conditioning, TV, minibar, desk, safe and refrigerator. The bathroom includes a hairdryer, slippers, free toiletries and bathrobes. Free internet is available on site.

In the family cafe, guests can order lunch, here they organize all kinds of programs for kids. In addition, the Opera karaoke bar, the Hmel brasserie and the Shater cafe are open in this place. Orangery (restaurant) was created in the style of Provence. She specializes in Mediterranean cuisine. Guests can also order a cup of aromatic coffee at the Abazhur establishment.

The hotel has a wellness center and spa, where you can take mineral baths, in addition, book massages, mud and balneological treatments. Beauticians work at Sakura (beauty salon). Banquet facilities and meeting facilities are also available.

Hotel Crocus

Crocus is located in the city center. The guests of this hotel (Essentuki are known in Russia and abroad as a large balneological resort) have a 24-hour reception desk and a garden. Two minutes walk is one of the most popular mineral springs.



The modern rooms at the Crocus Hotel are uniquely decorated; A TV is installed in the living area. The bathroom includes free bathrobes. The rooms have internet access.

After reading the reviews about this establishment, you will understand that the hotel's cafe serves a hot breakfast. Nearby, a five-minute leisurely walk, several restaurants are open for visitors.

You can make an appointment at the front desk for guided city tours. There is a beauty salon next to the hotel. The city park is a 10-minute walk away.

Hotel Espero

This hotel (Essentuki offers many hotels to stay, and this is one of the best) has a very good location: it is fifty meters from the mud baths named after Semashko and the source "Essentuki-4". It offers hotel guests a spa, free internet and a cafe.

The latter serves European and Caucasian cuisine. Guests can also enjoy snacks and drinks at the bar.

Judging by the reviews, all rooms are equipped with TV, air conditioning, refrigerator and safe. The bathroom comes with free toiletries, bathrobes and slippers.

The spa has an indoor pool and sauna. Guests can order all kinds of body and face treatments, massage.

Hotel Pontos Plaza

This hotel is 5 minutes from the train station. A spa center and free internet are at the guests' disposal. Spring No. 4 is a 5-minute walk away.





Judging by the reviews, each room of this hotel is decorated in an original style and is equipped with a TV, air conditioning and a minibar. Bathrobes and slippers are provided in the bathrooms.

The charmingly decorated restaurant serves traditional European cuisine as well as chef specialties. There is also a bar and cafe on site, where you can order drinks and snacks.

Guests at the hotel can relax in the cozy spa, equipped with a swimming pool and sauna. The beauty salon hosts many daily beauty treatments. You can also play billiards here.

Hotel Estet

Considering mini-hotels (such establishments can offer Essentuki), it is worth highlighting the Estet Hotel, which is located in the very center of the city, five minutes from the Medical Park. It is worth noting that it offers guests free Wi-Fi, as well as air-conditioned rooms. Theater Square is just 400 meters away.

By reading the reviews for this hotel, you can find out that its classic rooms have a thin-screen TV, a desk and a fridge. The bathroom has complimentary toiletries.

Various cafes, restaurants and bars are within walking distance.

Hotel SunHotel

SunHotel is a hotel located near the center of Essentuki. It offers guests - huge rooms with a refrigerator, TV and air conditioning, Russian cuisine, free parking and a 24-hour reception.



Judging by the reviews, the hotel has an elegant bright facade and provides elegant rooms created in neutral colors and equipped with dark wood furniture. The bathroom comes with free slippers and toiletries.

Breakfast can be ordered in the room. At the same time, the restaurant serves local specialties and European dishes.

Free internet is available in the lobby and comfortable leather chairs are set surrounded by deciduous ornamental plants.
