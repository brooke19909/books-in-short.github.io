Rest in the Hot Key of the Krasnodar Territory

If you move south from Krasnodar by the federal highway connecting the regional center with the city of Dzhubgo, then after about 60 kilometers you will come across a resort town with the interesting name Goryachiy Klyuch. It is located in a picturesque corner on the left bank of the Psekus River at the foot of the Caucasus Mountains, on their northern side. You will always remember your vacation in the Hot Key, but do not forget to visit the most picturesque places.





What is located next door

The neighbors of the Goryacheklyuchevsky district are Seversky, Tuapse, Absheron, Belorechensky districts and the Republic of Adygea.

Upon closer acquaintance with the sights, it seems that you are in the foothills of the Himalayan mountains - in the distance you can see mountains drowning in green trees (occupy 70 percent of the territory), and small cozy houses decorated with carvings. It is not surprising that the mountainous terrain and the forest have preserved the pristine purity of the air; it is truly healing here. Plus, to the Black Sea, as they say, is just a stone's throw - just some 60 kilometers. This explains the saturation of air in the resort area with useful ions. That is why recreation centers in the Hot Key are everywhere.

Hot Key Features

At one time, found many sources of mineral alkaline, sodium, hydrogen sulfide waters. Probably, the presence of thermal chloride-bicarbonate waters, the temperature of which ranges from + 36 to +60 degrees Celsius, contributed to the emergence of the name - Goryachiy Klyuch. At the moment, six types of mineral waters with different composition and healing properties are already known. They are extracted from 17 wells. If we take in relation to the total volume of mineral water in the Krasnodar Territory, this is almost 80 percent. This is a clear reason to spend a vacation in the Hot Key for the May holidays.





In sanatoria, depending on the type of disease, various methods of treatment with mineral water are practiced. They can be divided into two main groups:

  • drinking mineral water;
  • various kinds of balneological procedures (bathing or showering, bathing in pools, inhalations, irrigation, washing, etc.).

Famous sanatoriums

In the 70s of the century before last, mineral water healed wounds and restored the health of officers of the Russian army. Currently, rest in the Hot Key of the Krasnodar Territory attracts all the inhabitants of our country.

According to dry statistics, each year the Hot Key receives about 80 thousand guests, which can be divided into two categories. Some travel with the goal of improving their health or getting rid of various chronic ailments in the sanatoriums "Mountain", "Rus", "Emerald", "Foothills of the Caucasus", "Hot Key". Using a complex of medical procedures, the digestive organs, nervous and cardiovascular systems, musculoskeletal system and gynecological diseases are successfully treated. All actively used treatment programs developed by medical workers of local health resorts are considered the most effective not only in Russia, but also at the international level. Another group of visitors chose this place to restore strength and admire nature of the picturesque corner of the Kuban. It is always a pleasure to spend your holidays in the Hot Key in the forest.

It offers comfortable rooms in hotels and mini-hotels. In the sanatoriums after medical procedures, vacationers have the opportunity to take their leisure time, taking part in various game, entertainment, educational or dance programs. For children there is a children's playroom and educational activities under the guidance of an experienced teacher.

Climate, weather

Goryachy Klyuch is located in the temperate continental climate zone. Warm summer days here usually come in May, and cold days in October. Slightly moist, with the smell of ozone, the air warms up to +28 degrees during this period. The temperature does not fall below +18 degrees. The microclimate of the Hot Key resembles Anapa - as many sunny clear days throughout the year.





In winter, which is short in this area, the average air temperature ranges from -2 degrees to +4 degrees. Moreover, the amount of precipitation in the warm period is moderate, and in winter they are even less. Snow in the Hot Key is generally rare - over the whole winter it can fall 3 or 4 times, and even then it does not stay for long.

The summer heat and piercing wind do not penetrate the Goryachiy Klyuch due to the fact that the Caucasus Mountains cover it with a reliable high wall . Be sure to spend your vacation in the Hot Key, having gone there for the weekend.

Chapel and Healing Park

Alley of a thousand pines attracts attention. It is completely flat, exactly elongated along a straight line, the length of which is 2 km. Along the entire avenue, thousands of trees are represented by two species: Kokha pine and Pitsunda pine. In fact, the Healing Park and the resort area begins with the alley, and it ends with the famous Drinking Gallery. The staircase of life is a historical monument located between the rock of Salvation and the Zvonka cave, on the slope of the Abadzekh mountain. A staircase hides behind thick trees. According to legend, thanks to a stone staircase, criminals managed to escape the death penalty to which the ancient Circassians were sentenced.





The Iversky chapel was cut down in a rock by the Cossacks near the Iversky spring of mineral water. Due to the movement of mountain layers in the 20s of the last century, the source ceased to exist. As a reminder of him, the Iberian chapel remained. A bowl shaped like a sea shell confirms the fact that there used to be water. The icon, like the source, has wonderful healing properties.

Psekup dolmens

These are 20 stone slab structures. Therefore, the term "dolmen" is translated from the Celtic language as a "stone table". It is assumed that they appeared in the Psekups valley around the period of 3-2 centuries BC. Scientists who have explored some of these ancient man-made piles are wondering how blocks from 3 to 40 tons each could be delivered and built. How to get to the Hot Key

You can get to the city of Goryachiy Klyuch by means of land and air transport. There is a road and rail (train) connection to the resort. Goryachy Klyuch from the capital of the Russian Federation is 1,400 km away. Since there is no airport in the resort town, you can fly to one of the largest settlements - Gelendzhik or Krasnodar by plane.

Goryacheklyuchevskaya railway station is a kind of connecting link between Krasnodar and the Black Sea coast, in particular Tuapse.

For those who prefer personal transport, you should choose a route that is connected to the M4-Don highway.
