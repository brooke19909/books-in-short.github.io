So, where to start buying an apartment?

The first stage is the preparatory stage. You already have a certain amount in your hands, for which you want to purchase housing that is convenient for you. If you are confident in your abilities and confident that you will do without the help of a lawyer or a realtor, then, first of all, you need to study the documents when buying an apartment that the seller represents, make sure that he is the owner of the apartment, compare the data from the property registration certificate with as indicated in the title document. If redevelopment is done, be sure to ask permission of the relevant authorities.

The second stage is the registration of the purchase of an apartment or apartment building. Current legislation requires the purchase of an apartment by signing an agreement with a notary, and then this transaction must be registered.

For this, in addition to confirming the seller‚Äôs ownership, the following documents are needed when buying an apartment, presented in a notary's office: passports of both parties, written consent of the spouses to alienate and purchase this housing, if there are children, permission of the guardianship council (for the seller), a certificate transferring 1% of the amount to the Pension Fund (for the buyer), certificate of absence of utility debts (for the seller).

Experienced lawyers recommend signing an Acceptance and Transfer Act for an apartment or house when concluding a transaction regarding the sale. This document is an integral part of the contract, an appendix to it. However, it does not mean that ownership has already passed to the buyer, because in accordance with the law, a buyer who has already completed all the documents when buying an apartment, signed an agreement and received his notarial certificate, it is necessary to carry out state registration of property rights in the BTI.
