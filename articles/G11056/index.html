Negative reviews

Hotel Yadis Djerba Golf Thalasso (5 stars, Tunisia) has a number of disadvantages that are reflected in the traveler reviews. The following points can be considered the most significant:

  • huge claims arise for cleanliness (this applies to rooms, and public areas, restaurants);
  • many Bedouins with camels and horses walk on the beach, intrusively offering their services;
  • instead of cleaning algae carried ashore, beach workers push them back into the sea;
  • in a restaurant, at a distribution, there are always long lines, as well as a mess created by children whom no one is following;
  • in the restaurant there is an acute shortage of spoons (and there are no teaspoons at all);
  • the air conditioner buzzes loudly, which is why you have to turn it off at night (as a result, the room becomes very stuffy);
  • all the staff, with the exception of one administrator, is not that Russian, but even English and French practically do not understand;
  • rooms do not provide drinking water;
  • the minibar is not replenished, even if you are willing to pay extra for this service.

Travelers note that the five-star status of this hotel is somewhat exaggerated. Of course, the list of services is very wide, but their quality leaves much to be desired. The biggest complaints arise regarding the cleanliness, as well as the technical equipment of the rooms. As for nutrition, this is one of the most pleasant moments of relaxation.
