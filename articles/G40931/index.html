Recreation facilities in the Leningrad region with a pool: photos and reviews. Rest in the Leningrad region

Often, city dwellers do not want or are not able to go on holiday to the sea or abroad. For them, recreation centers were built in the Leningrad Region with a pool. They are equipped with all kinds of entertainment and places for barbecue.

Recreation complex "New Lakes"

The base was built in 2016. Here, guests are offered to rent modern cottages equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The houses are built using duplex technology. This design is very convenient for relaxing from 4 to 16 people.





Gatchina district has beautiful places that are surrounded by forests and ponds. There is clean fresh air and silence. In one of these places is the New Lakes complex. It is located 55 km from St. Petersburg in the KP Rybitsa-1.

Comfort in cottages

Each house has its own terrace. It is quite large and offers a beautiful view of the surroundings. The area near the cottage is equipped with a barbecue area. Here is a modern barbecue and the necessary accessories.

Inside the house is a modern renovation with an original design. Cottages are equipped with:

  • satellite tv;
  • a refrigerator;
  • Microwave;
  • kitchen appliances.

The bedrooms are equipped with comfortable furniture. Each house has a bathroom with all amenities. In the cold season, the cottages are heated, and guests feel comfortable. Gatchinsky district is easily accessible both by private car and by public transport. Shuttles and electric trains come here from St. Petersburg.

Additional services

The complex has two reservoirs. In one, guests can swim, while the other is designed for fishing:

  • catfish;
  • carp;
  • sturgeon;
  • silver carp.

A neat Russian bathhouse was built near the ponds. There are several lounges for guests who decided to stay here for the night. There is a large font on the street, in which water is heated year-round.





The property has a vibrant playground where young guests can have fun. In winter, the children enjoy sledging from the equipped slide in the complex.

In "New Lakes" you can celebrate any celebration. Here is a cozy banquet hall for 30 people. Experienced professionals will serve the banquet at the highest level. There is also a cafe on the base equipped with modern light music and a varied and tasty menu.

In the reception area there is a children's corner where children can play with high-quality and colorful toys. On the territory there are platforms for outdoor games:

  • badminton;
  • darts;
  • table tennis;
  • football.

The administration often holds promotions for customers. For example, when renting a cottage for 8 people for 3 nights, guests can use the Finnish sauna for free.

Yucca (Leningrad Oblast): vacation in winter and summer

This area is 7 km from St. Petersburg. Holidays in the Leningrad region for many are associated with this resort. It began to develop back in the 60s. last century. A modern ski resort has been built here, on which many country champions-skiers have grown and studied.





Not far from the resort are Finnish shrines and a source of healing water. These places are surrounded by a forest strip and 400 meters from the base there is a beautiful lake with clear water. You can diversify your stay with excursions to interesting places in the area.

There are 3 tracks for skiing and snowboarding. Young athletes can train their skills on a small gentle slope. Experienced athletes are offered a long track with extreme descents and turns.

What else to do at Yucca Park

On the territory there is a bathhouse and a sauna. Many city residents are looking for recreation in the Leningrad region with a pool. In the "Yucca Park" in the cold season there is an indoor bathing room. In summer, guests will find an equipped outdoor pool.

Here is a mini-hotel. It has a billiards room and a children's room. On weekends, an experienced nanny is engaged there with young visitors. The resort has several cafes and a restaurant with a banquet hall. In it you can order a corporate party or celebrate your birthday.

A selection of holiday cottages at Yucca

Tourism is developing very fast in this area. The best recreation centers in the Leningrad Region with a pool are spread throughout the area. They are located near the Yucca Park and await guests throughout the year.

Base "Dranishniki" has on its territory 4 cottages and an outdoor pool. Here you can take a steam bath and eat in an excellent cafe with delicious dishes from the chef. In the spacious banquet hall, you can celebrate any celebration.

"Lake Cottage" is built in an aristocratic style and has three levels. Here, up to 40 people can relax at the same time. All rooms have an original design and are served by experienced hotel workers.





The cottage has a modern billiard room and a sauna. On the ground floor there is a large banquet room with a fireplace. There is also a spacious terrace that can accommodate up to 90 people. Conferences are often held here.

"Yucca cottages" on its territory have 7 houses with different levels of comfort. Small one-story cottages can accommodate a family of 3-5 people. There is a kitchen and a bathroom. The rooms are clean and warm.

Large houses can accommodate from 20 to 40 people at a time. They have banquet facilities and billiards tables. Several cottages have indoor pools and saunas. In "Yucca" (Leningrad Region) you can find a comfortable place to stay for any budget and with the right conditions for a fun and active holiday.

A country vacation will help you enjoy nature, clean air and silence. Here you can relax with your family, friendly company and relax from the bustle of the city in solitude.





Recreation facilities in the Leningrad region with a pool are very popular among tourists. "Timeout" has an indoor bathing room with hydromassage and a bath. In summer, you can sunbathe on the equipped beach and go fishing.
