Hotel Memories Varadero Beach Resort 4 * (Cuba, Varadero): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Holidays on the Caribbean - this is what many people dream of. Today, Cuba is a very popular destination. Its coast is very convenient - it is characterized by deep bays and numerous bays. Modern statistics show that on average 2 million vacationers come here annually. But every tourist needs somewhere to live during the holidays. Well, there are many different hotels. And with special attention I would like to note Memories Varadero Beach Resort 4 *.





general information

This is a relatively new hotel since it was opened less than ten years ago. Moreover, not so long ago, in 2012, he went through a major overhaul.

To the sea from the hotel - a stone's throw. The complex is located in Varadero, a resort town in the province of Matanzas, where there are some of the most beautiful beaches in the whole state. And it is not just words. In 1992, Varadero Beach received the status of one of the cleanest beaches in the world, and this title was awarded to it by UNESCO. The peninsula on which Varadero is located reaches 20 kilometers in length and only 1,200 meters in width.

The hotel's location is good, as you can quickly reach the Juan Gualberto Gome Airport from here. It is 15 kilometers from Varadero.

Service

Memories Varadero Beach Resort 4 * has all the amenities that visitors to Cuba might need. The reception here works around the clock, as well as a currency exchange, offers babysitting services (convenient for those who come with children) and a concierge, car rental, and a laundry with dry cleaning. The hotel even has a conference room that can comfortably accommodate 250 people. It is equipped with modern audiovisual equipment necessary for meetings and other events of an appropriate nature.

Also on the territory of the complex there is a medical center, several shops (including a souvenir), an Internet room and even a hairdresser. By the way, the hotel has its own photographer. If you need his services, you can contact the reception.





The perfect place for a wedding

Memories Varadero Beach Resort 4 * hotel is extremely popular among honeymooners. And to be more precise, among those who are just going to become them. The hotel organizes weddings - the specialists working here do everything at the highest level. Experts carefully study all the conditions set by the heroes of the occasion, reverently treat any trifles, realizing that they matter.

Specialists are immediately ready to offer several options. Take, for example, the Joy Package. In addition to accommodation and meals at the hotel, this package includes a welcome cocktail, professional coordinator services, delivery of sparkling wine to the room on request, a wedding ceremony (where customers wish), decoration and decoration of the venue. After the event, a traditional rite. The package also includes a bouquet for the bride and a boutonniere for the groom, two cakes with sparkling wine, musical accompaniment, preparing the room for the wedding night, dinner in one of the restaurants and the Honeymoon Package as a gift for the newlyweds.

In addition to the above, future newlyweds can order a host of other services. A photographer, for example, a professional printout of pictures, bouquets for girlfriends of the hero of the occasion, shooting a movie from the scene, a wedding hairstyle and makeup for the bride, and much more. Memories Varadero Beach Resort 4 * has a very large range of services of a similar nature. You can find out more about everything by contacting the administration directly.





Leisure

In Memories Varadero Beach Resort 4 * (Cuba, Varadero) you can not only have a great rest, but also have fun. Here you can play golf on a professional field, do motorized water sports or tennis (there are three courts on the territory). There are also four outdoor swimming pools, of which two are for adults, and the same is for children. In the evenings, the hotel organizes entertainment shows and a disco. By the way, kids will not be bored. Here, a full-fledged mini-club and a children's playground are organized for them.

Adults can play beach volleyball or work out on the sports ground, ride a catamaran or pedalo, go windsurfing or diving. Even a fitness center with fitness equipment is available here. By the way, diving is definitely worth the time. In Varadero, 23 excellent centers are organized that teach this sport and organize underwater excursions. The guests of the resort at sea enjoy a stunning sight - coral reefs, tropical fish (including a parrot, needle, sword), white-headed sharks, bastards, barracudas, dolphins, stingrays, turtles - this is just a small list of creatures you can meet with by diving.

For body and soul

Memories Varadero Beach Resort 4 * (Varadero) has an excellent SPA center with a beauty salon, where highly qualified specialists with a medical education work. Thalassotherapy is actively practiced here - wellness procedures that use warm sea water, mud and algae. There are also several massage rooms, a jacuzzi with hydromassage and a bath.

Deluxe rooms

Now you can talk about the apartments that are offered to guests as a temporary residence. There are more than a thousand rooms in the complex. But there are only two categories. The first is deluxe rooms. These apartments have one king-size bed (or two double beds - guests can choose any option), a balcony or terrace, powerful air conditioning, a plasma TV with CD and USB player, and a minibar replenished every day. It also has a telephone, a safety deposit box, ironing facilities, a hairdryer and a bathroom. These apartments are designed for three adults. Or two, but with 2 children.

The daily average cost of living in a deluxe room is about $ 150 (with the all-inclusive service). If, for example, you reserve an apartment for a week in the fall, a month before arrival, you will have to pay about 65 tons. for two. Which is a pretty modest price.
