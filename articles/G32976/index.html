Detailing SMS ("Megaphone") with text: how to do it?

Mobile users sometimes need to get detailed information about what actions were performed on the number. For example, to check why money was debited from the balance sheet, or to find out what numbers the child called, etc. For these and similar purposes, Megafon's Detailing service can be used (calls and SMS, as well as connections with internet). With its help, you can easily satisfy your curiosity and answer any questions related to communication services on a specific number. In this article we will talk in detail about what kind of information this service allows to receive, as well as how the details of SMS (‚ÄúMegaphone‚Äù) can be formed.





General description of the service

Detailing (you can also hear the "decryption") of the account is the provision of data on what actions were performed on the room, with detailed information about them (for example, if it is an Internet connection, you can find out the amount of traffic spent per session). It is important to understand that the information is available only about those actions that have been performed (a connection has been established with another subscriber, a text message has been sent, etc.). Often, by this service, subscribers mean SMS detailing. Megafon, like other domestic mobile operators, does not decrypt messages. Thus, if you read about such an opportunity on some resource on the Internet, be sure that this is fraud.

Types of detailing Megafon

You can find out what actions were performed on the number and how much was charged for them in several ways. Depending on the period for which you need to obtain information, and the option to obtain it, the choice of one of them will depend.

  1. Details of the latest paid actions (information on the last ten paid operations on the number).
  2. One-time detailing (one-time request for data for the permissible period).
  3. Detailing in 30 days (receiving information about all paid actions for a month).
  4. Monthly invoice (delivery of decryption by email every month).




Information Options

As mentioned earlier, in the decryption there will be calls, SMS for the specified period. "Detailing" ("Megaphone") can be obtained in several ways:

  • at the office of a cellular operator (only the owner of the number can obtain such information by applying for an identity card; information can be provided for a period of up to three years);
  • via the Internet (through the "My Account"; information is sent by e-mail);
  • via SMS (in this way you can only get data on the ten last paid actions on the number);
  • through a courier (this option of obtaining information is paid additionally; the cost can be clarified by calling the customer service of your region).

Getting information about the latest paid actions on the number

This type of detailing can be successfully used when it is necessary to clarify information about which operations have been carried out recently. At the same time, a request for such data can be sent from the Megafon mobile number. Detailing via SMS, which will be received in response to a USSD request * 512 #, will contain information about the type of service (call, message, Internet), its duration / volume and time of completion. The service is provided free of charge.

One-time detailing

This type of decryption by number is done via the Internet or directly in the office of the company. The option of courier delivery is provided (the cost of such a service should be clarified in the customer service of your region). The cost of the order in one day is 3 rubles. The period for which information is available is determined by the method of ordering details. If you use the "Personal Account", you can view data for the last six months. If you want to receive a printout in the office of the mobile operator, a document will be issued to the client containing the data for the selected period. When ordering through the Internet, the details will be sent by e-mail specified by the client. Detail per month

When ordering details for a month, information will be provided for the selected 30 days. You can get it, as in the previous case, via the Internet, courier or in the office. The cost of the service will be 65 rubles. The principle of obtaining a document is similar to one-time detailing.

Monthly bill

Another option for decryption is a monthly bill from Megafon. Detailing SMS with text is also not performed in this way. The document will contain all the same data as in the first two cases, except that the sending will be carried out automatically by the subscriber once a month (approximately on the 10th day). By connecting this service, the client will receive a document with decryption by e-mail until it is disconnected. The cost of this option for obtaining information is 100 p. per month.





What information is contained in the details

When ordering details via the Internet, information will be received in the form of a document of a certain format by e-mail. It will contain the following data:

  • date (when the operation was completed);
  • the number of the subscriber or service department where the call or message was sent;
  • volume (how long the conversation lasted, the number of megabytes spent);
  • direction (this parameter applies to calls and SMS messages, displays in which direction the call was made, SMS was sent - in the home region, long-distance, to another country);
  • region of location (region where the subscriber was at the time of the transaction: in his region, in intranet or international roaming);
  • type of call (message, call, Internet);
  • cost.
