What cash loans does Sberbank give to individuals?

Credit programs of the country's main bank can be divided into three large groups according to the type of collateral requested:





  1. Sberbank loans to individuals without any collateral. Even insurance is not required, but you just need to document your stable financial position and employment. Amount - up to one and a half million rubles. The term for the return of requested funds can be extended to 5 years. The rate will vary between 17-21.5% per annum.
  2. Credit with a guarantee. Any solvent relative or acquaintance can act as a guarantor. The presence of a second actor increases the maximum loan amount to 3 million rubles, and the term will remain the same - up to five years. Interest can reach 16.5-20.5% per year.
  3. Loans with a pledge of property, where the pledge can only be real estate, allow you to get up to 90% of the value of the subject of the pledge (but not more than 10-15 million rubles) for up to seven years at a rate of 13.5-14.5% per year.

However, the shares until mid-February, you can get borrowed funds without collateral or against a guarantee at the same rate as with the pledge - from 14.5% per annum. Consideration of applications for participation in this promotion is carried out individually, so until you submit an application, you will not know your chances of a profitable loan.
