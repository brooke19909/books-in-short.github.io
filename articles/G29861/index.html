Hotel The Grand Hotel 4 *, Hurghada (Hurghada): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Grand Hotel 4 *, Hurghada is a well-known Egyptian hotel, which corresponds to many features that are traditional for the hotels of this resort. Inexpensive is first. On the seashore - secondly. And finally, it offers a lot of different services. And there are many more criteria that this complex meets. And it is necessary to tell about them.





basic information

First of all, telling about the hotel Grand Hotel 4 *, Hurghada, I would like to note that one of its main advantages is its favorable location. In addition to the close proximity to the sea, it is also interesting that the airport is nearby. Less than twenty kilometers distance between it and the hotel. And you can get even in less than 20 minutes.

Interestingly, the Grand Hotel 4 * (Hurghada) is part of a large hotel chain Red Sea Hotels. And it includes the guest complexes of Hurghada, Sharm El Sheikh and Makadi. A distinctive feature of all these hotels are their own beaches with well-groomed territory, which are located figured pools and beautiful gardens.

Plus, the Grand Plaza Hotel (Hurghada) 4 * is located in the best tourist area (known as the Promenade). Here is the best natural beach in the entire resort. And along the sea lies a very picturesque garden, where there are walking paths and benches. Many guests spend time in the evening.
