Cottage Village "First", Kostroma: customer reviews, layout and features

On the Volga River there are many cities of the state. One of them is Kostroma, with a population of just over 300 thousand people. Many residents change their usual city apartments for country houses. The cottage village "First" for permanent residence offers just such an option.



The location of the village

The village is successfully located within the city of Kostroma, which automatically gives its residents the opportunity to obtain state registration. In addition, this zone is called promising for further development.

You can get to the village both by personal and by public transport. From the city center you need to go along Rabochy Prospekt, which goes into River. The latter, in turn, will lead directly to the village. About 2 years ago, from the village of "First" to the village of "Volzhsky" with a 15-minute interval, a bus began to run.



Description of the village

The territory of the village is designed for the construction of fifty-five residential buildings with floors from one to three floors. Also, the developer provides for many land plots sold for individual residential development. Why is it recommended to use the First Village (Kostroma) for the purchase of country houses? Customer reviews - those who have already become the proud owner of an apartment or a plot - are mostly positive. In total 1300 apartments are planned for sale: from one-room to three-room.



In addition to residential buildings and land, the village provides for the availability of necessary social infrastructure. This is a gym, a pharmacy, a bank and a post office, a store. It is planned to build a kindergarten and a school. To control the safety of the inhabitants of the village, access control, round-the-clock security, video surveillance are installed.

Ecological situation

As for the environmental situation, we can only say that of all the new buildings in Kostroma, the village of "First" is located in the most favorable place. It is located at a sufficient distance from the city center and all industrial enterprises. On the way to the cottage village, the road leads through the forest, which allows residents and guests to enjoy clean, fresh air.



Opposite the village is the Kostroma River, in the waters of which you can swim in the hot summer, relax on the beach, fry the kebab, and heartily chat with family and friends.
