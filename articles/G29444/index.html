Mickey Mouse Costume: Step-by-Step Guide

Mickey Mouse is the most famous cartoon character in the world, which was created in 1928 by Walt Disney. This cheerful little mouse won the hearts of the audience, and to this day, his adventures are watched with pleasure not only by young spectators, but also by their parents. That is why the Mickey Mouse costume is always popular.





Materials

Making such a fancy dress does not require serious expenses from you. You can even use materials of other colors. But the main constant element is always the ears of the hero. We will need:

  1. Fabric and threads of different colors for tailcoat.

  2. Headband for ear fixation.

  3. Cardboard.

  4. Big white buttons.

  5. Scissors.

Mickey Mouse Ears

There are several options for making ears for a Mickey Mouse costume. It is better to use a hoop, as the ears will hold better and not be deformed. You will also need glue, black cloth or felt, thick foam or corrugated cardboard, pattern paper.

We draw 2 circles of the required diameter on paper, transfer the pattern to black fabric and cut four circles from the fabric. From foam rubber or cardboard we cut two circles with a diameter of 1.5-2 cm less. We insert the filler between the two circles of fabric and gently stitch it, leaving a small hole below.

We plan on the hoop where the ears will be placed, and glue two small erasers on these places. After the glue has completely dried, we put on the ears on the erasers with an unwired piece and sew them from below under the hoop. We cut off the excess fabric and we trim the whole hoop with black material.

Ears can be decorated with sequins, sequins. For a girl, you can make an elegant red bow, and for a boy, you can make a small cylinder made of cardboard or felt.
