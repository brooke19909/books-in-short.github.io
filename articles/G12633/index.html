Manufacturing technology

You will be able to create metal products with your own hands. Photo drawings and stages of the process will help you with this. Performing artwork involves the steps of molding, processing and decoration. The manufacture of things, as has already been said, occurs in various ways.

  • By casting is meant casting a mold with a liquid alloy.
  • Forging is a plastic processing of material with a hammer, a press, as well as bending, twisting, cutting.
  • Bending - a simple view of the previous version, performed by riveting or welding parts of the product.
  • Difovka is a method of processing a thin, not more than 2 mm, sheet of metal by hammer blows, thanks to which it acquires the necessary configuration.
  • By stamping and cutting, individual elements are prepared, holes are made.
  • Filigree is made of twisted or smooth, sometimes flattened copper, silver, rarely gold wire. The curved parts of the patterns are soldered.
  • When minting, a relief pattern is obtained using stamps or special tools that are placed between the hammer and the product.

Drawings of metal products can be done manually or in special programs on a computer. Three-dimensional modeling allows you to make not only a regular drawing, but also to build a three-dimensional model of the future product. It makes it possible to see the appearance of a thing, including in the interior, long before its actual appearance. In this case, it is possible to adjust the parameters of the product without material costs for its re-manufacturing.





How to make an aluminum can box

The most elementary thing made of metal can be performed even by a schoolboy. The main thing is that it will take almost nothing except time, hard work, imagination and the desire to create. You can make a beautiful box from an ordinary can of soda.

Materials and equipment

The raw materials for production can be said to be lying under your feet on the street. You will also need the following items:

  • sand paper (sandpaper);
  • sharp scissors;
  • ruler;
  • pencil;
  • ball pen;
  • solid base with a format not exceeding A4 (depending on the size of the box);
  • sheet of paper for the scheme.

Sequencing

At the first stage, a layer of paint is removed from the can using sandpaper. It is better to do this with unopened containers, since its walls remain elastic under the influence of the contents. The process will go faster and you will not spoil the metal. If using empty, do everything carefully so as not to crush the jar.





At the next step, from the workpiece you need to get a thin sheet of aluminum the size of a scan of a future box.





For this bank is cut.

Sharp and uneven edges are removed.





The next step is the marking of the ribs and sides of the product.





A thin metal sheet is laid on a solid base.





The box diagram can be drawn directly on a metal sheet.





And you can circle a sketch made in advance on paper.





When the reamer box is ready, proceed directly to the drawing. This is done with a ballpoint pen or sharpened pencil. It should be noted that you will have to circle the patterns several times on both sides. This will give a greater decorative and convex pattern.





Next, the workpiece is collected in the product. It turns out simply and effectively.

How to sell your crafts online

If you decide to opt for online trading, then take into account that the principle of operation of all stores is the same:

  1. You register as a craftsman who sells items.
  2. Fill out the profile.
  3. Expose your work.
  4. Agree with the customer.
  5. He pays for the goods.
  6. You are delivering.

There you can put ready-made items or take orders. It is very important to choose the right category of product, come up with its description, choose a high-quality image. Be sure to make a signature to the photo and indicate that you are doing metal products with your own hands. Exclusive items are accepted for sale, which can easily be sent by courier or by mail to the customer. Things should not contain very thin or fragile elements that could break during transport. If such parts are available, you must provide for quality packaging so that the product reaches the customer in its original form. After all, every mistake can significantly affect your image. Try to perform all decorative metal products efficiently and on time. Good customer reviews play an important role in this industry. So you turn your favorite activity into a source of income.
