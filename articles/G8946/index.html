The history of the construction of a luxury palace

At the end of the XVII century, after an attack by Turkish troops, the building was seriously damaged and required restoration. It was decided to build a new palace, more luxurious than the previous one. The invited architect took as a basis for a large-scale project the magnificent residence of the "sun king" - Versailles.

The spouse of the empress proposed to build greenhouses in which rare plants and exotic flowers were delivered from different parts of the globe.
