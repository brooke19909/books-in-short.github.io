Hotel HSM Atlantic Park 4 * (Mallorca, Spain): photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel HSM Atlantic Park 4 * (Mallorca, Magaluf) was previously called the shorter name Atlantic Park. This is a fairly old hotel, which has won the hearts of many tourists. It is notable for its convenient location, relatively affordable prices, comfortable leisure conditions and high service.

Hotel Description

Hotel HSM Atlantic Park 4 * (Mallorca) is located on the third coastline. Nevertheless, the distance from the edge of the sea is only 400 meters. The hotel was built in 1972. In 2006-2007, a complete reconstruction was carried out, after which the hotel reopened its doors for guests. During this period, not only repair and updating of the hotel design was made, but also all furniture, equipment, and textiles were replaced.





The building has nine floors. Therefore, for the comfortable movement of hotel guests, an elevator is provided. The hotel's fa√ßade is surrounded by a balcony-terrace with views of the hotel courtyard and outdoor pool.

The hotel does not work year-round, but only from May to October.

Number of rooms at the Atlantic Park Hotel

Hotel Atlantic Park (Mallorca) is quite large. The number of rooms consists of 242 apartments. Each room provides all the necessary set of furniture and equipment for a comfortable rest and sleep: single and double beds, bedside tables, a large wardrobe, chairs and a table, desk, minibar, LCD TV. Provide an acceptable temperature regime will help air conditioning.





The bathroom has either a modern shower or a bathtub. There is also a built-in hairdryer. Each room has access to a balcony-terrace.

Room reservation and room rates

The hotel has three categories of rooms: standard Single, standard Double, Suite, Junior Suite. Prices start at 1,200 euros for two (as of May 2017). The price includes three meals a day according to the buffet system, the use of the pool, children's entertainment and many other types of services.

When booking with tour operators, you are provided with a letter. Payment by credit cards or cash is possible.

Catering at the Atlantic Park Hotel

Atlantic Park Hotel (Mallorca) has a Swedish line with a variety of international dishes. Among the gastronomic diversity you can find vegetarian and diet food. Also once a week there are themed buffets.





Breakfast in the restaurant is from 8 am to 10:30, lunches - from 13 to 14:30, dinners - from 18:30 to 21:30. In the morning, teas, coffee, juices and champagne are offered, and for dinner, beer and homemade wine. Light snacks are also served three times in the snack bar: from 11:30 to 12:30, from 15 to 17, from 21 to 22:30.

When you check in, you can choose the following food modes: all inclusive, half board, full board, breakfast.

Additionally, the hotel has a small cafe, and near the pool there is a snack bar where you can enjoy cold drinks and a little snack.

Organization of leisure at the hotel and beyond

The hotel HSM Atlantic Park 4 * (Mallorca) has a large outdoor pool. Umbrellas and sun loungers for guests are provided free of charge, while towels are provided on deposit. In the pool you can not only swim, but also enjoy water aerobics and water polo. Also at the territory of the Atlantic Park Hotel there are areas for sports: darts, archery, petanque, futsal, golf (field within three kilometers), table tennis, tennis (there is a court), volleyball. All this is completely free. For fans of card games there is a special room.





You can play billiards or enjoy a massage at an additional cost. For those who want to tan faster, there is a solarium. Travel lovers should book an excursion to local attractions.

In the evenings, the hotel hosts various show programs - entertaining and professional, there is animation. Separately, it is worth mentioning entertainment for children. Here they have a separate pool and a playground with swings, slides, game horizontal bars and so on. The restaurant for children has a children's menu, and the administrator can ask for a crib. The hotel also organizes children's clubs depending on the age of the child: from 4 to 7 years old, from 8 to 12 years old and from 13 to 17 years old.

A few minutes walk from the HSM Atlantic Park 4 * hotel (Spain, Mallorca) is Playa de Magalluf beach. Sun loungers and umbrellas are charged here. Aqua Land Water Park is 600 meters away. Also around the hotel there are a lot of different cafes and restaurants, shops, souvenir shops and so on.
