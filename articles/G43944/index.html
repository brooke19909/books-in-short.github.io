Aqua Fantasy Aquapark Hotel & Spa 5 * in Kusadasi (Turkey): description, leisure and reviews of tourists

Which hotel to choose for a family holiday in Turkey? Surely this question worries many parents planning a vacation in advance. We will try to tell in detail about one of the most comfortable hotels, which deserves rave reviews from vacationers.

Neighborhood

The suburban Aqua Fantasy Hotel Spa 5 * hotel is located near large population centers: it is 8 km to the resort town of Kusadasi, and 9 km to the Selcuk region. The nearest airports are in Izmir and Bodrum, the distance to them is 56 and 120 km, respectively. However, you can get to the hotel by bus, which expects tourists right at the exit from the airport.



A 10-minute drive from the hotel is a unique architectural and historical monument - the ruins of the ancient city ‚Äã‚Äãof Ephesus, which is visited daily by many tourists.

Excellent weather conditions are considered a particular advantage. Optimum humidity and a comfortable temperature of water and air on the coast of the Aegean Sea are the main reason that they like to come here with young children, who, by the way, quickly adapt to the local climate. The resort is favorable for the elderly, of course, if they like entertainment and a lot of vacationers.

Hotel Territory: Description

In Kusadasi (translated from Turkish as ‚Äúbird island‚Äù, abbreviated ku adas) Aqua Fantasy considered one of the most comfortable holiday destinations. This is a modern hotel, built in 2003 (the last reconstruction was carried out in 2013). A magnificent spacious territory is surrounded on one side by a pine forest, and on the other it is surrounded by the sandy Pamuzhak beach.





For the settlement of tourists, nine 3-storey and nine 2-storey buildings are built together. The number of rooms of 3-storey buildings of the World type includes:

  • 130 World Double rooms;
  • 40 Superoir rooms;
  • 266 World Family rooms.

In 2-storey Club-class buildings, room classification is as follows:

  • 85 rooms Club Double;
  • 27 Superior rooms;
  • 72 Club Family rooms (one-room);
  • 156 Club Family Suite rooms (one-room).

The buildings are built along the coast in such a way that the windows of half of the rooms are facing the sea, while the rest overlook the garden. The rooms have the necessary furniture, TV, floor or ceramic tiles, telephone. Guests can independently make tea and coffee in the room, for which there are special sets.





In the World buildings there is a central air conditioning system, individual air conditioners are installed in Club-level rooms. Some apartments have a jacuzzi and DVD. Cleaning is carried out daily, linen is changed every 2-3 days.

Nutrition

Upon prior request at the Aqua Fantasy Hotel They also organize diet menus and prepare meals for vegetarians. The main restaurants - Oasis and Tropicano - offer all-inclusive meals.





Since there are many gourmets among holidaymakers, an additional 5 more restaurants work for them on the territory : Mario's - Italian cuisine; Mandarine Gourmet - Chinese and Oriental treats; Prime Steakhouse - Mexican dishes and steaks; La Meze - Turkish dishes; Marine Restaurant offers amazing seafood. Tables need to be reserved in advance. Vacationers can visit one of the restaurants for free (except for a steakhouse).





Entertainment on site

Aqua Fantasy Aquapark Hotel Spa is an ideal place for family holidays. During their stay, tourists staying at the hotel can visit the local water park, which consists of 12 children's slides and 19 attractions for adults, free of charge. All drinks and products here are paid separately.

For swimming, the hotel has 6 pools (4 open and 2 indoor), inventory next to which is free (sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas, towels can be taken on bail). Outdoor terrace for sunbathing. Dry cleaning and laundry services are available, there is a library, an Internet cafe and a TV room. Business meetings or negotiations can be held in conference rooms for 350 and 200 people.

Any high-level hotel has a spa and beauty salon. There is a hairdresser, a cosmetologist offers their services, they conduct thalassotherapy sessions. The hotel also has a doctor. There is a Turkish bath, sauna and jacuzzi. Massage services are paid. Free car parking is available for guests.





Vacation with children

What to do with kids at Aqua Fantasy Aquapark? Amusement mass. There is a children's playground and a club for the youngest visitors. Toddlers will surely enjoy the mini-disco where animators work. The restaurants offer a high chair and dishes from a special menu. For walks, parents of babies often order a stroller (for a fee), and an extra bed can be put in the room. Nanny services must be paid separately.

Leisure

Many tourists can not imagine their vacation without playing sports. If you are one of them, be sure to take the opportunity to visit the gym, aerobics classes, play basketball, volleyball or table tennis at the Aqua Fantasy Hotel for free. It offers guests a room for archery, darts, futsal.

Paid activities include bowling and billiards, tennis court rental and classes with a personal trainer. You can rent bicycles or ride a horse. A great way to diversify your vacation is to do water sports (canoeing, jet ski, water skiing, parasailing, bananasailing), which are booked on the beach. Extreme sports include diving and windsurfing.

Beach vacation

Nearby the Aqua Fantasy There is a private sandy beach. Equipment is available free of charge. Sea water is very clean, and the entrance is gentle and shallow. The sand is small, dark with golden tides. The beach is quite spacious, so there is enough space for everyone.
