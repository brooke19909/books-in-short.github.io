San Panteleimon Beach Hotel 3 *

Neos Panteleimon, where the hotel is located, is an hour's drive from Thessaloniki Airport, where regular flights from various airlines from around the world take place.

The hotel is located at the foot of Olympus, just a hundred meters from the coastline.

Seventy-two rooms of the hotel were renovated and updated in 2013. The hotel has its own decorated park area. San Panteleimon Beach Hotel 3 * perfect for visitors who want to combine a relaxing holiday at sea and an abundance of excursion programs at an affordable price.

Photos and room descriptions

The hotel rooms are designed for more than two hundred visitors, there are non-smoking rooms.

  1. The Double Room category is suitable for families and groups of up to four people. The room has a double bed and two single beds.
  2. Double Bungalow garden View rooms are located in two-story buildings. The rooms have a kitchen area equipped with equipment, double and single beds.
  3. Double Executive rooms updated. Possible accommodation options: three adults or two adults and two children under the age of 12 years.
  4. The Junior Suite category is suitable for three adults or a family of two adults and two children. It has a seating area with a sofa, a double and a single bed.

Services and infrastructure at the hotel

The price for a double standard room is 4600 rubles per two people per day. At a moderate cost, the hotel has a fairly developed infrastructure:

  • Fresh water pool with free umbrellas and sunbeds.
  • Salon for watching television broadcasts.
  • Programs with live music and Greek evening - twice a week.

The hotel San Panteleimon Beach Hotel 3 * provides business services: a conference room with the possibility of accommodating up to 100 people, wireless Internet Wi-Fi. For convenience, Visa, MasterCard cards are accepted for payment.

There is a playground for children, and a cot is available in the room upon request.

Dining at the hotel is organized in three ways:

  • Breakfast (BB) is included in the price.
  • Half Board (HB).
  • All Inclusive (AL).

Meals are organized in the hotel restaurant, pizzeria and two bars: in the lobby and by the pool.

Beach and Fun

Sandy and pebble beach, located less than a hundred meters from the hotel, with convenient access to the sea, sunbeds and umbrellas, for a fee, will make it possible to enjoy thalassotherapy to the maximum.

On the coast you can use the services of outdoor activities on the water. The tour desk of the hotel San Panteleimon Beach Hotel 3 * (Pieria) offers to book excursions:

  • Sightseeing and shopping in Thessaloniki.
  • A trip to the fur factory of Kastoria (fur coat tour).
  • Sea cruise with a visit to St. Athos.
  • Excursion to the Meteora.
  • Sightseeing tour of Pieria.


If you want to get more excursions, you can study the historical sites of Pieria in advance and compile an individual route map. At each sightseeing object, information can be obtained on site by joining a group of tourists.

Another option is also possible: San Panteleimon Beach Hotel 3 * Exclusive offers individual tourist services. The price of individual excursions is on average three times higher than the group price. An hour of service when renting a luxury car and with the services of a certified guide will cost 100 or more euros. This is a more expensive way to organize excursion services, but judging by the reviews of grateful travelers, the level of preparedness of guides for these programs fully justifies the investment.

Tourists reviews

According to visitors to the San Panteleimon Beach Hotel 3 *, whose reviews have formed a consistently high rating for the hotel (3.92 on a five-point system), you can definitely recommend it for traveling to Greece.



The advantages of the hotel include:

  • Good service.
  • The friendliness of the staff.

  • The quality of food.
