What is the thinnest phone in the world? Model Overview

Mobile phone manufacturers are constantly in fierce competition. Each of them is trying to create a device that will be unique in its kind. Currently, much attention is paid to the thickness of smartphones. Thick models have long been a thing of the past. Users would like to have the thinnest mobile phone. Who released this model?

The category of ultra-thin gadgets includes all devices that have a thickness of less than 7 mm. In this article, four models that meet this criterion will be considered. The reader will be able to familiarize themselves with their characteristics.

Vivo X5 Max - the thinnest phone in 2014

This device has attracted the attention of the buyer with its dimensions. Its thickness is only 4.8 mm. The model was released in 2014. It is based on the Quad-core Cortex-A53 processor. During operation, the computing modules accelerate to 1,700 megahertz. The screen diagonal of 5.5 inches allows you to classify this phone model as a phablet. The device is equipped with a battery of 2,000 milliamps per hour. Based on Android 4.4.4. The screen is manufactured in compliance with Super AMOLED technology. Its resolution is 1080x1920 pixels. The main camera has a 13 megapixel module. The maximum resolution of the image is 4128x3096 px. Even considering that it is the thinnest phone in the world, it did not affect the level of performance in any way. It is rated by users rather high. The fact that the amount of RAM is 2 GB, says a lot. The gadget copes with all modern applications. To save them, there is a storage of integrated memory, whose capacity is 16 GB.
