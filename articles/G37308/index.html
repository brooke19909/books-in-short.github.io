Vacuum cleaner LG V-K99161NAU: review, specifications and owner reviews

A vacuum cleaner is an integral attribute of modern cleaning. It is he who makes it possible to get rid of dust and debris in such a way that he does not stay on all horizontal surfaces of the house or apartment, as happens with the usual sweeping. And the time with this unit is spent on cleaning much less. In order to filter out the air passing through it as much as possible, manufacturers have developed water filters. One of the models equipped with such a filter is the LG V-K99161NAU. Let's take a look at its main characteristics, as well as pay attention to user reviews to find out how good this model is and whether to buy it for your home.

What is a water filter?

A water filter, or an aquafilter, is a special cyclone installation that is used instead of the usual garbage bags or containers. Its advantage is that particles in the air passed by the vacuum cleaner, under the force of inertia, fall into the water, absorb it into themselves and settle on the bottom of the tank.

As the test results show, this approach allows you to more efficiently clean the air and filter more fine dust, which usually does not stay in other types of filters. Yes, and the maintenance of an aquafilter is easier - it is enough to rinse it after use with water, dry it - and it is ready to fulfill its functional purpose again.

Conclusion
