Todorovic Villa 2 * (Budva, Montenegro): description, service, reviews

Budva is a rather large resort of Montenegro, located in the central zone of the Adriatic coast. This city is considered the center of the country's tourist life. Thousands of vacationers visit it annually. This resort is popular with both beach lovers and those who are looking for nightly entertainment. Of course, the tourist infrastructure in Budva is very well developed. Not only a large number of cafes, restaurants and night clubs have been built here. There are, of course, many hotels. For example, guests of Budva have the opportunity to rent comfortable rooms at the Todorovic Villa 2 * hotel .

Location Features

Hotel Todorovic 2 * is located in one of the quietest areas of Budva, about 350-400 m from the coastline. The Old Town with its attractions is about 1 km from the hotel.





The nearest airport is approximately 24 km from the hotel - in Tavita. There is also a large air hub in Podgorica - about 6 4 km from the hotel. Tourists who rented rooms here can reach the hotel both by tour operator bus and public transport.

There are many cafes, restaurants and bakeries next to this hotel. There are no various entertainment facilities in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. So tourists who choose this complex to stay in Budva have the opportunity to relax in peace and quiet both day and night. Entertainment lovers staying at the hotel can find them in the city, in more lively areas.

Todorovic Villa: hotel description

Budva welcomes this hotel in one multi-storey building of the original building. The hotel courtyard is covered with paving tiles. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a small parking lot. The hotel yard is well landscaped and looks, judging by the reviews, very beautiful. On the territory of the complex, kiwi trees, citrus fruits and various spectacular tropical shrubs are planted.

Owning the complex, judging by the reviews of tourists, a very friendly and hospitable Montenegrin family. The reception at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. Which is certainly very convenient for its guests. Travelers in the hotel in the rooms at 14:00. After the end of the paid period of stay, guests must leave the rooms at 10:00. On the ground floor of this hotel there are utility rooms. On the second, third and fourth - the actual rooms for residents are equipped.






Housing for rent

If desired, guests of Budva can rent in the Todorovic Villa 2 * hotel rooms of the category:

  • studios for 2-4 people;

  • apartments.

In all rooms rented at the hotel, tourists have the opportunity to use:

  • air conditioners;

  • refrigerators;

  • TVs.

Each room in this hotel also includes a well-equipped terrace. The views from it, depending on the rented room, can open both on the hotel courtyard and on the picturesque mountains in the vicinity of Budva. One of the features of the Todorovic Villa (Budva) hotels is that, if desired, its residents can use free wireless internet.

Television at the hotel provides satellite. If desired, guests can even watch one of the Russian channels. However, as some tourists note, it is transmitted, unfortunately, only in the recording. That is, you can watch here mainly only the old issues of various kinds of domestic programs.





Reviews of tourists about rooms rented at the hotel

The majority of vacationers consider the accommodation offered to vacationers at the Todorovic Villa 2 * hotel to be quite convenient and comfortable. In particular, good reviews about the rooms of this complex are available on the Web due to the presence of comfortable spacious terraces. If desired, vacationers can not only dry clothes here, but also have breakfast, lunch or dinner. On the terraces for the convenience of tenants there are comfortable tables and chairs.

Also, the advantages of this hotel tourists include the presence in the rooms of dummies. Such an accessory is provided, unfortunately, by no means in all 3 * Budva hotels. Reviews about the complex are good on the web, including and therefore.

Tourists consider not too thorough cleaning as some drawback of the rooms of this recreation center. The maids, unfortunately, also do not change the bed at the hotel very often. The same goes for towels. New residents are brought no more than once every 2-3 days.

Showers in the Todorovic Villa Cat B 2 * hotel, according to its guests, are too small. Unfortunately, taking water procedures in them is not particularly convenient. However, in this case, interruptions with hot water in this complex are never observed. In the hotel showers, unfortunately, windows are also provided for some reason. Before taking water procedures, experienced tourists are advised to close them. The fact is that through them the rooms of showers are well visible from the windows of neighboring hotels and villas.

To the pluses of the bathrooms of this complex, guests include the presence of hair dryers in them. With a wet head, for example, the residents of the hotel do not have to go to bed.





Complex infrastructure

Unfortunately, a large number of amenities are not offered to tourists at this affordable hotel. But the minimum set of services for the comfort of vacationers living here, of course, is provided. As already mentioned, if necessary, for example, guests can use parking at this hotel. True, she is not too big in the hotel, open and not guarded.

Unfortunately, there is no pool in this hotel. However, the hotel is relatively close to the sea. Therefore, guests do not consider the absence of a pool to be a special drawback of the hotel.

Todorovic Villa 2 * (Budva): service reviews

Most tourists consider the hotel owners to be very polite and helpful. The owner of the hotel tries as quickly as possible to fulfill any requests of his guests. This applies to all sorts of additional services, and, for example, breakdowns of household appliances. All the shortcomings that tourists complain about are resolved very quickly. For this, including deserving good this hotel reviews from guests.





You can find the owner of the hotel at almost any time on the ground floor, in one of the rooms. Here, the owner of the complex lives with his family.

The owner of Villa Todorovic Cat B 2 * understands quite well, including the Russian language. The rest of the hotel staff speak mainly local. The latter, however, is very similar to Ukrainian. Therefore, in principle, what is at stake, it will not be impossible for the Russian tourist to understand.

Hotel Food Reviews

Unfortunately, there is no private dining room at this hotel. But at the same time, as in many mini-hotels of the Black Sea in Russia, this complex has a common kitchen. Here, guests can prepare breakfast, lunch and dinner on their own. Also, if necessary, rooms at the Todorovic Villa 2 * hotel can be rented with meals. In this case, breakfast is offered to guests in a cafe located next to the hotel. Reviews this place from tourists deserved relatively good.





Guests of the Todorovic Villa Hotel (Montenegro) can also buy food at bakeries and butchers located nearby. Here, according to most of the guests of the complex, the food is also sold very tasty and fresh. In addition, it is relatively inexpensive.

Beach Hotel Reviews

Hotel guests need to get to the sea for about 10 minutes. This complex is located, as already mentioned, not on the first coastline. The closest to the hotel is Slavic beach. Actually, the coastline itself in this place, judging by the reviews of tourists, is pretty clean. It is covered with a mixture of sand and pebbles. Therefore, sunbathing is relatively convenient here.

If desired, hotel guests can also visit the beaches located in the Old Town. It will take you about half an hour to get here. But the water in this place in the sea is much cleaner than on the Slavic beach. And the infrastructure here, as many tourists say, is better developed.





Also very good reviews from vacationers in Budva earned the picturesque Morgan beach . If desired, hotel guests can come to sunbathe and swim here. Another popular destination for guests of this resort is the island of St. Nicholas. Very comfortable, clean and beautiful beaches are also located here. You can get to this island by boat. Such a trip will cost about 3 euros.
