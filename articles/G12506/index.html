Comfortable hotels. Chita is looking forward

Problems in the field of foreign tourism business forced many to pay close attention to Russian cities, a journey through which can give many new unusual impressions.

For example, in Chita there are many interesting things for a real traveler.

sights

In such a historical center of the Transbaikal Territory as Chita, a traveler can discover many interesting monuments and remarkable places.
The founding date of this city is considered to be 1653, when Russian pioneers only mastered Siberian open spaces.

Many traces of these events have survived to our time.
The oldest Trans-Baikal Regional Museum of Local Lore, which has existed for more than 120 years, has over 170 thousand different exhibits.

A lot of interesting works of art and decorative and applied arts of the peoples of the North can be found in the Chita Museum of Art.

A lot of interesting things can be learned when visiting:

  • geological museum;
  • picture gallery;
  • museums dedicated to the Decembrists, local railway workers, war heroes;
  • the majestic orthodox Kazan Cathedral;
  • Shumovsky Palace;
  • catholic church;
  • Buddhist monastery;
  • Transfiguration Church.




The city has a sufficient number of theaters and other cultural institutions.
The abundance of natural attractions scattered around the city leads tourists to indescribable delight at any time of the year.

In order to enjoy all these beauties, the corresponding tourist infrastructure has been created: transport hubs, restaurants, cafes, hotels. Chita has moved far enough in this direction. Many travelers have appreciated this from their own experience. A lot of positive reviews about this can be found on forums on the Internet.

Chita Hotels

It is worth telling in more detail about some hotels, Chita deserves it. Their comfort is quite high, a sufficient selection of rooms of different class is provided.

"Arcadia"

Hotel "Arcadia" (Chita) is a classic design and quite comfortable conditions for staying couples.

The location of the hotel is very convenient: in the city center next to the Drama Theater. The train station is literally three hundred meters away. There are shops and restaurants nearby.

In the hotel rooms there is a TV, tea and coffee set, refrigerator, safe, hairdryer, there is wireless Internet access.



Suite is provided for newlyweds. Tourist-budgetary may be limited to a single economy class room.

For customers, the hotel "Arcadia" (Chita) can offer car parking, a sauna, Wi-Fi. Pets allowed. The total number of rooms is 140.

Mont Blanc

In the stylish eleven-story building there are more than 170 rooms of various comfort classes. They are equipped with a convenient desk, telephone, TV, air conditioning.



Guests can visit the pool, sauna, massage room, billiard room.
For a bite to eat, guests can check out a cozy cafe with live music called Khutorok. Here you can taste not only Russian, but also European, as well as Japanese cuisine, order a festive banquet.

Hotel "Mont Blanc" (Chita) has a comfortable conference room with 45 seats, where you can hold a coffee break, business lunch. The room is decorated with pleasant brown and beige tones. It is possible to rent the necessary video and audio equipment.

"Transbaikalia" - hotel (Chita)

This seven-story hotel, built on Leningradskaya Street using the St. Petersburg project back in 1965, is rightly referred to as urban symbols. Its comfort is significantly enhanced by modernization and reconstruction activities.

The windows of the building overlook the central city blocks. Within a few minutes you can reach the regional drama theater, central square, regional and city administrations, shopping centers, restaurants and cinemas.
In total there are 112 rooms of different categories.



There is a telephone, a minibar, a refrigerator and a TV, typical of first-class rooms. Air-conditioned air enters suites and junior suites, which pleases hotel guests; Chita has always been distinguished by summer heat.

In the restaurant with the same name, it is interesting to listen to live music, compare European and Mediterranean cuisine. Corporate evenings and banquets are conveniently held in the VIP room.

In your free time in the entertainment center you can work out on sports simulators, play billiards, take a dip in the pool of the SPA center.

"Transbaikalia" is a hotel (Chita), which has a children's playroom, where experienced teachers will entertain children in the absence of guests.

Guests can use the wireless Internet, guarded parking, ATM, laundry.
