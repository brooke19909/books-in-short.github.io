Blacklist: Beeline offers a useful service

Has it ever happened that people on your cell phone called with whom you would not want to talk? For example, a former life partner, too sociable and annoying person or some kind of ill-wisher. Telephone conversations with such interlocutors can take a lot of your time, cause unpleasant memories, ruin your mood or even lead to more serious consequences. Some change the SIM card to protect themselves from calls and messages of people unpleasant for them. Others have to turn off the phone for a while. Naturally, in such cases, there is a simple way to abandon the conversation - click on the ‚ÄúEnd‚Äù button. But there is also a more rational method of how to protect yourself from unpleasant conversation, which does not require changing the number or disconnecting the phone.



Beeline Blacklist may be the best way to solve such a problem. To protect yourself from an unwanted interlocutor, now you do not need to repeatedly drop a call or ignore its SMS messages. It is enough to add this subscriber to a special list. Beeline gives the right to all its subscribers to use this opportunity. In such a "black list" you can enter a maximum of forty subscribers. If the person you don‚Äôt want to talk to dials your number, you will hear an answering machine message asking you to call back later. No matter how many times he tries to contact, the result will be the same. The Beeline Black List service is activated for free. But if the operator of the Support Center connects it, it will cost fifteen rubles, and for post-paid subscribers, 30 rubles. Adding a new number to the Beeline Black List costs 3 rubles. Removing from it is free.



The Beeline Black List service gives you the opportunity to find out how many times and when exactly an unwanted subscriber called you. To do this, type a specific command, after which you will receive SMS. It will indicate both the time of the call and the number of calls from the blacklisted subscriber for the last day. This request will cost five rubles.

For users of a prepaid settlement system, the service will cost 1 ruble per day. In all other cases, it will cost 30 rubles at the same time. Deactivation of the Beeline Black List service is free of charge at the request of the client.

Now this idea was adopted by Beeline. The black list can be replenished with any numbers - from stationary to international. Naturally, for the operator, a complete refusal of the client to accept calls from certain persons is disadvantageous. Therefore, for a subscriber, such a service is not entirely free. Agree that one ruble a day is not too much money, especially when it comes to peace of mind or even your safety.
