Planckoe Slit village - fresh air and unique nature

Seversky district of the Krasnodar Territory is located thirty kilometers from the capital of the Kuban. Having passed from it only a hundred and a few kilometers, you can find yourself on the Black Sea coast.





Unique area

This is a unique place. More than eighty different nationalities live side by side in the Seversky district. The most numerous of them are the Russian, Ukrainian, Armenian, Greek, Georgian and Belarusian communities.

The main attraction of the region is its unique landscape and, of course, wonderful places where you can relax. Due to its favorable geographical position, the Seversky district has become extremely attractive for investments in the development of the resort tourism business.

The hallmark of this territorial unit is the famous Mount Sober-Oashkh, which is seven hundred and thirty-six meters in height. On its slopes beat the purest springs. That is why the locals for a long time considered this mountain sacred.

Many natural monuments of the region, for example, the gorge of the Red Stream, Pshad and Dariny waterfalls, Planic Slit, the peaks of Mithridates, Fortress, Papai, Lake Upper Afipskoe, which is also called "Lohness", are shrouded in secrets and legends. That is why they have long been popular places where people come to rest. The parking of primitive man brought world fame to the Seversky district. This monument of the Middle Paleolithic has a history that began one hundred and thirty thousand years ago.





Slit

Krasnodar Territory provides a wide range of tourist services, ranging from medical and recreational to extreme - hang gliding, rock climbing, etc. Not only those who need to improve their health come here, but also extreme lovers. Seversky district is very favorable for recreation. The tourist season in the region lasts nine months. Many visitors for a vacation choose the picturesque village Planicheskaya Shchel. It is located in the Seversky district on the banks of the small river Afips.

It has only four streets: st. Zarechnaya, Forest, Peaceful and River. This is a very small town. During the Great Patriotic War, Planck Slit was the place where the partisan detachment named after the Ignatov Brothers was based. But today, the village is better known among tourists located in the immediate vicinity of the same name rocks. It is here that people come armed with climbing equipment.

Planicheskaya Shchel - a village that is lost in a forested lowlands. It is located on the Afips River at an altitude of one hundred and seven meters above sea level. The nearest settlements are the village of Mirny and the village of Krepostnaya.





Popular rock routes

The village of Planicheskaya Shche annually receives more than one thousand tourists. This small village in the Seversky region of the Krasnodar Territory, located on the Afips River, is currently gaining popularity primarily with its rocks. They are also called Planic. They laid excellent rocky routes with various categories of difficulty. Moreover, they are, in fact, the closest to the city of Krasnodar. Perhaps this explains their outstanding popularity both among climbers and ordinary tourists. On weekends, there are always a lot of people on Planic Rocks or on the Planes, as they are popularly called. They are stretched almost three hundred meters. Composed of armored sandstone, these cliffs reach a height of twenty-two meters.

In the vicinity of the village there are two objects well known to tourists. These are Planic rocks and Reds. The first locals call Golden Stones, while the second Naumovsky, in memory of the famous climber V.P. Naumenko.

Red and Planic rocks are very close to each other. They became especially famous in the country in the seventies of the last century, when a tourist boom began in the Kuban.

Then Planic rocks turned into a training ground for climbers and climbers preparing for more serious peaks. They perform this function to this day.





Just relax

People come to the Planar Slit just to be distracted. The townspeople are attracted by the fresh air here, the noisy forest that sprawls around the village, mountain streams and just pleasant sensations.

The Planar Slit from Krasnodar is only sixty kilometers away. The road from the city takes only an hour and a half. And only the last ten minutes of the journey have to go on an unpaved primer, which, however, is in a fairly good condition.

Recreation center

Those who prefer to rest "savages", pitch tents on the banks of the river Afips, enjoy barbecue and relax after picking mushrooms. But those who prefer comfort can stay at the Plancheskaya recreation center , located in the village itself. The complex consists of cozy wooden houses, always ready to accept both noisy companies and vacationers with children.

You can come here for a few days or just for a picnic. There are gazebos equipped with barbecue facilities, wooden tables and benches in the shade of trees, which can be rented for several hours. Depending on the number of vacationers, you can rent four-, eight-, ten and sixteen-seater houses.
