Pattern of Tatyana Konne dolls with a one-piece head

In this master class she is not sewn, but cuts out simultaneously with the body. The Konne dolls pattern option has a small nose on the face and neat feet.





For manufacturing, you will need material for the body and a foam ball with a diameter of 7.5 cm. You will also need a wide satin ribbon to make hair.

We place the patterns on the fabric and pin them with pins. Cut out. Now we will sew. Let's get started with the legs. Sew the item twice in one line. Leave a hole in the thigh area. Through it will be stuffing.

Head and torso

We turn to the creation of the head. Foam ball wrap with synthetic winterizer. We form a nose: we take a pin and twist its tip into a synthetic winterizer. We fix it on the face. We put the ball in a pre-cut head. We collect the crown on a thread with a needle. We are tightening. We also stuff the neck with holofiber. We fill the trunk from the bottom and sew it up.

Hand blanks are also sewed for the fortress twice. Do not forget to leave a space for stuffing. Fill them with holofiber. Sew.

Legs

In order for the big head to be stable, one trick is applicable: we will insert plastic into the foot. Stuff your legs tight. Before attaching them to the body, you need to make tights. Pick matter that stretches well. Cut the workpiece to fit your feet. Sweep and put on.

We fix the legs with buttons. This mount is considered the most reliable. Sew and put on the tights completely.

Fill the upper limbs with holofiber and attach using buttons. Next we will need to make a t-shirt for a big head. It is simple, sewn from a rectangle (without sleeves).

Ribbon hair

Think about how long hair you want to make. This parameter determines how much tape is needed. Cut it into 6 pieces, each equal to two times the length.

Sew it in the middle on a sewing machine. The most painstaking part of the job: unfold the tape so that the thin threads do not get tangled. So the wig is ready.

Pin the hair to the head and tie the ends into the ponytails. So the hair will not be confused and it will be easier to sew on. Walk a few times with a needle and thread along the parting. Make a fashionable haircut or trim your bangs. The artist makes hair for her girls from ribbons, threads, artificial or natural hair.
