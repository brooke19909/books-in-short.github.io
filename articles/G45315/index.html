Hotel The Pride Sun Village Resort & Spa 4 * (India / Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

India is a country of contrasts. Different cultures and religions meet here, therefore travelers from all corners of the world seek here. In addition to sightseeing holidays, the country can offer holidays on the picturesque coast.

The main center of beach entertainment is Goa. It is here that tourism infrastructure develops. Travelers will find beautiful beaches, a mild climate, a variety of entertainments and, of course, hotels and resorts. One of the popular stopping places is considered the hotel The Pride Sub Village Resort & Spa 4 *.

Of course, at the planning stage, a person needs to learn as much as possible about the place chosen for the vacation. Is the hotel location convenient? Can a traveler count on comfort and good nutrition? How do other tourists respond about this hotel?

Where to look for a hotel? Location





Where exactly is the hotel complex located? After all, as you know, a location can be both an advantage and a disadvantage. Hotel Pride Sun Village Resort & Spa 4 * is located in a quiet resort area on the north coast of Goa. This is a beautiful place near the seashore - by the way, the distance to it is 1.5 km.

The nearest international airport is located in the city of Dabolim, about 45 km from the hotel complex. The road to the hotel after arrival will not take too much time. Many travelers believe that the location of the hotel is quite convenient.

How many rooms are there?

What living conditions can a guest of The Pride Sun Village Resort & Spa 4 * hotel expect? Reviews say that the rooms here are really comfortable and beautiful. By the way, the hotel has 135 rooms, namely 85 standard and 50 rooms with a high degree of comfort.

It is worth saying right away that all rooms are very spacious, with balconies and large windows. The rooms are divided into sleeping and living areas. Superior rooms are larger and have some additional appliances and furniture.

Photo and description of hotel rooms





What do the rooms at The Pride Sun Village Resort & Spa 4 * look like? The photos show that the rooms are well-equipped, and the design is pleasant, it creates an atmosphere of comfort. Of course, guests can count on the availability of working appliances.

You can skip the evening by watching local and satellite channels on a large-screen TV. It offers guests a telephone, a safe with an electronic lock, as well as an air conditioning system that saves you in the summer heat. Tourists can use the refrigerator. It also has everything you need for making hot drinks, including dishes and an electric kettle. Upon request, employees deliver an ironing board to the room with an iron, which, of course, is convenient. There is a minibar in the rooms, but it is filled at an additional cost and only upon request.

A small bathroom will delight you with a comfortable design and modern fixtures. There are large shower cabins, as well as a toilet and a sink. Guests can count on a stationary hair dryer. Of course, guests are provided with clean towels, which are changed regularly. According to the hotel schedule, rooms are cleaned daily, but linen is changed twice a week.

Food scheme for tourists





Of course, a good rest must include food. Staying in such an exotic country, I always want to please myself with delicious delicacies. The territory of the hotel complex The Pride Sun Village Resort & Spa 4 * has a spacious, roomy restaurant, as well as a bar.

When paying for a room, the guests themselves choose a convenient meal plan, whether it is only breakfast or full three meals a day. Everything is organized in the form of a buffet - you can choose dishes to your liking. By the way, the menu contains both traditional Indian and more familiar international food. Reviews say that the menu here is quite varied and necessarily includes fresh fruits, vegetable dishes, homemade cakes.

At the local bar, you can enjoy a chilled drink or a stronger drink at any time of the day. If for one reason or another it is not possible to eat in the hotel restaurant, there are a sufficient number of different cafes and other establishments nearby.

Distance to the beach, description of amenities





Of course, they come to Goa in the first place in order to have a good time on the beach. The hotel complex The Pride Sun Village Resort & Spa 4 * is located at some distance from the beach - the distance is 1500 meters. Many tourists prefer to overcome this distance on foot, because sometimes a walk can bring a lot of pleasure. Nevertheless, a shuttle service runs from the hotel to the coast and back several times a day.

Guest reviews confirm that the shores here are very beautiful, and the soft waves are great for beginners in windsurfing. The entrance to the sea is cleared, and the depth increases gradually - both adult tourists and their children have a great time here.

Water recreation: entertainment for tourists

Bathing and sunbathing is always a pleasure. Nevertheless, many travelers prefer more active types of recreation. There will be no problems with fun and entertainment on this beach, because a large sports center is organized here. Those who wish can rent a boat or catamarans. You can also rent a swimming mask or a windsurf board. Beginners for a small fee can work out for several hours with instructors.

Tourists also have the opportunity to go diving - in the center you can not only rent equipment and purchase an excursion, but also take the necessary training courses, having received the appropriate certificate. And you can ride a boat, water skiing, banana, jet ski and other motorized vehicles.

What conditions can guests of the hotel complex The Pride Sun Village Resort & Spa 4 * expect? Services provided by guests provide maximum comfort. How to spend free time at the hotel?





Of course, Goa is an interesting place where every traveler will find entertainment to his liking. Nevertheless, you can have a good time on site. For example, there are two pools where guests like to spend time. Around are new, comfortable sun loungers with umbrellas, there is a bar with drinks, as well as a point for issuing clean towels. You can swim, sunbathe and meet new neighbors.

A beauty salon is constantly working, where you can consult with a stylist. The presence of a full-fledged gym will delight tourists who always prefer to keep fit. Ayurveda Center is at your service, where you can learn more about a healthy lifestyle and Indian traditions. And guests can use the sauna and relax in the large jacuzzi.

Do not forget about the possibilities of sightseeing holidays, because there are many interesting places nearby that you can visit for little money. Also nearby are the famous Goa markets, which are definitely worth a visit for a unique flavor and, of course, interesting purchases.

Are there any conditions for hanging out with a child?
