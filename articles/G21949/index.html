Holidays in Side - The Best Combination of Beach Resort and Excursion Programs



Holidays in Side are popular among tourists at any time of the year. This town, located near Antalya, is one of the best examples of tourist cities of forever sunny and green Turkey. Local landscapes will bring aesthetic pleasure to connoisseurs of unity with nature and lovers of contemplation of the beautiful. The resort is located on a very beautiful natural cape in the Mediterranean Sea.

Side Resort

Side was founded in the 7th century BC. Over time, it turned into the main port of the historical region of Pamphylia. In the 2nd century AD, the most famous monuments were erected here, which today attract tourists so much. These are the Amphitheater, Agora, Necropolis, aqueducts and Roman baths. Therefore, holiday in Side is often chosen by lovers of ancient history.

The beaches here are located in the eastern and western parts of the city. Western beaches are more crowded, so if you like silence, choose hotels in the east.

Side resort consists of several areas included in it: Kumkey, Titreyengel, Monavgat, Cholakli and Sorgun.

The city has a well-developed tourist infrastructure. There are many restaurants, including those specializing exclusively in fish menus. In the evening and at night, you can always find a place for an interesting rest, for example, in a bar with live music or in a disco.

During the day, you can wander for good to the souvenir shops and shops, shopping in which is not tiring at all, but very, very pleasant.

It is also good to come here with children, because excellent conditions are also thought out for their relaxation.

Tours in Side

Travel packages in Side are bought up by travelers all year round. They can be purchased in advance or signed up for last-minute tours in Side. Compared to many other Turkish resorts, Side is not so expensive and practically inferior in no way to more fashionable and popular cities. In addition, if you compare the city with other Turkish tourist mecca, then this one is worth choosing, because you do not have to spend extra time and money on long trips to places of historical and cultural value. After all, Side is an open-air museum.

Therefore, ordinary or last minute packages in Side are so in demand among all groups of vacationers. You can choose tours of different durations and different living conditions.

Excursions

  The main attraction of the city is the Main Gate. They are located between two towers, giving them a particularly majestic look. The Nimpazum fountain, which is located nearby, is very picturesque. Agora is a unique center of trade and craft destination. Here today you can go shopping, visit the colorful local market.

You can also go to the structural arch of the Roman Empire, which is located on the main street of the city, visit the city theater, monasteries and the archaeological museum, which today have become Roman baths.

You can go to neighboring Antalya, which is located not so far, less than 70 km. To do this, it is best to purchase a bus tour. Holidays in Side can be very active.

Side Hotels

Hotels here are located throughout the resort area. The service in any of them is truly first-class, which is typical for all Turkish hotels. The staff greets guests in a friendly and helpful manner.

The hotels have their own well-kept beaches, and Turkish dishes successfully complement the rest with local culinary cultural traditions. Choosing a vacation in Side, you get comfort and excellent service.

On the streets of the Old Town you can walk to the sea, walk along the picturesque harbors. Numerous shops offer an amazing abundance of goods from jewelry to handmade napkins. In the city center, you can find small family-run hotels, as well as wonderful villas surrounded by green gardens and flowers.
