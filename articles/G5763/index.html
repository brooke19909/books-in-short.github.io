Priozersk - hotels of the city

A successful choice of hotels in Priozersk is one of the factors that create a good impression for every visitor. And while the cost of rest plays an important role. The best options in this case would be 2 * -3 * hotels. In them, the service provided and the cost are proportionate.



In Priozersk, hotels with high cost are 5- and 4-star hotel complexes. Here you will find luxurious furniture, a unique design, modern appliances. The trained staff will help you solve any problems. Such hotels have excellent infrastructure, and in addition to accommodation, guests are offered excellent service. Expensive? Of course. In such hotels, prices are always high.

1 * hotels, hostels, apartments and guest houses are budget options. These are those Priozersk hotels, the prices of which even students can afford.

Chalet "Squirrel"

Offering a barbecue and garden, Belichie Chalet is located in Belichye Village. Free private parking is available here.



This camp site has a playground for children. The bar has the opportunity to order a variety of drinks.

Guests can rent a bicycle and enjoy various water sports. Other facilities include billiards and table tennis. The area is popular for hiking, fishing and cycling.

Reading reviews of this database, it becomes clear that it is best suited for people seeking a relaxing holiday.

Cost: from 2800 rubles per day.

Base "Pink Cottage"

Considering inexpensive hotels in Priozersk of the Leningrad Region, it is worth highlighting the Pink Dacha base, which is located in the village of Kutuzovskoye. Guests can swim in the pool, and nearby and on site you can go fishing, cycling, skiing and other outdoor activities.



All units include a kitchen equipped with a microwave and stovetop. A fridge is also featured. There is a private bathroom with free toiletries in every unit.

Guests can rent ski equipment at the property and use the games room. Free parking is available on site.

Judging by the reviews of this place, you can find out that most of the guests like the presence of a swimming pool and opportunities for outdoor activities.

Cost: from 6500 rubles per day.

Kexholm Hotel

Keksholm is located in Priozersk with a sauna and a terrace. It offers free parking and Wi-Fi.



The rooms are equipped with a hairdryer and a modern TV, although this is different for many hotels of Priozersk, the reviews of which are presented in this article. Some of them have a balcony.

Reading reviews about the establishment, you can find out that there is a bar and a restaurant on site. Room service and packed lunches are available upon request.

Cost: from 4200 rubles per day.

Hotel Korela

As mentioned above, in Priozersk hotels are presented to the taste and budget of each. Korela is located in the central square of the city. It offers free Wi-Fi.



The rooms have a private bathroom with underfloor heating and free toiletries, a TV and a fridge. Some rooms offer stunning views of the main square of the city.

Guests can visit the cafe. There is free parking for thirty cars on site.

Reviews about the establishment suggest that the marina is located nearby - from here boat excursions go to the island of Valaam. The distance from the establishment to the train station is 2 kilometers. You can get to St. Petersburg in 3 hours by train.

Cost: from 2600 rubles per day.

Hotel "Captain Morgan"

This hotel is located in the village of Motor, near the town of Priozersk. Hotels here are ready to receive guests year-round.



There is a garden next to this hotel. The accommodation is equipped with a seating and dining area. There is also a kitchen, equipped with a microwave and stovetop. There is a private bathroom with a shower, kettle and fridge. Bed linen is included.

Captain Morgan Hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a restaurant.

Hotel guests say that you can use a private beach, play tennis, rent a bike, go fishing and go cycling / hiking.

Cost: from 3500 rubles per day.
