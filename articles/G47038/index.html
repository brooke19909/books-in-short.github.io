Briefly about China

China is a powerful country, the largest in Asia, with a huge population, government problems, etc. All these difficulties do not prevent it from developing at a pace that will lead it to first place among all the economies of the world, moving the indestructible leader - the United States .

The history of China dates back thousands of years, rooted in the times of the origin of civilizations on the planet. Numerous monuments of history, architecture, customs and much more are still preserved here, which undoubtedly attracts tourists from all over the world.

Road to Pearl River Garden Hotel on Hainan Island

Communication with the island exists from almost any city in Russia, even the Far East is no exception. The most convenient option is to travel by bus or train, especially if it is interesting to see the mainland of China. There is no direct flight connection with Hainan, not taking into account the possibility of ordering a charter from Moscow. The road consists of several stages. First you should fly to the Chinese village of Guangzhou. Then transfer to a bus or train, if desired. After that, either a bus or a train will take you to the capital city of Fr. Hainan - Haikou. Hotel Location Overview

Pearl River Garden Hotel or Pearl River Garden Resort 4 * is located very well, right in the center of the resort south coast of the island. The hotel is adjoined by a hospital of Chinese traditional medicine according to ancient traditions - ‚ÄúGarden of Longevity‚Äù. So you can combine a pleasant stay with useful wellness treatments.

Hotel Description

The Pearl River Garden Hotel 4 (Hainan, Dadonghai) began operating 11 years ago, in 1996. The construction is a single building with a curved shape and stepped levels, 8 floors high. The hotel has 240 cozy rooms of different levels of comfort with the possibility of sea views (Suite and Deluxe rooms). The hotel does not allow pets. There are valid Visa and Union Pay credit card terminals. The hotel has its own beach area 50 meters wide with sun loungers and sun umbrellas. The hotel has a large swimming pool with a children's area. The total area occupied by the hotel is 8240 m 2 .

Since this is one of the most beloved hotels for leisure among Russians, they are warmly welcomed at the hotel, the staff speaks Russian, the TV channel is set in their native language, even the restaurant can offer a menu in Russian. In general, all so that domestic tourists feel comfortable and come back here again and again.

A special feature of the Pearl River Garden Hotel 4 (Dadonghai) is the miracle escalator located on the third floor, which takes everyone to the beach and takes it back.

Room filling

Apartments are cleaned daily. Each room has a safe, iron, hairdryer, slippers, interchangeable eco-linen, bathtub, a set of towels, desk, carpet on the floor, TV with one Russian channel, air conditioning, products for making tea or coffee, bathrobe, wi-fi minibar, from which only water can be taken for free.

Dining at the hotel

The hotel provides different types of food to choose from vacationers. The first option is to book a room without meals (RO). There is the option of providing a breakfast room (BB). Often it looks like a buffet. Half Board (HB) is available, which includes breakfast and dinner. Drinks on this system are offered for free. Alcohol is paid. The last option for this hotel is FB type, which means full board with breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Infrastructure on site

While staying on vacation at the hotel, on its territory you can use the services of a parking lot, currency exchange, dry cleaning, laundry, go to a medical center and, without leaving the hotel, make purchases in stores. Hotel beach

The Pearl River Garden Hotel 4 (China, Hainan) has a 50 m wide beach reserved for its visitors. The sand on the beach is white, shallow, clean, gently sloping into the sea. Free sunbeds and umbrellas are provided for guests. In addition, you can hide from the sun in the shade of palm trees. Sanya Pearl River Garden Hotel 4 (Dadonghai) is a 15-minute walk from the beach. Vacation with children

The hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable stay with children from eight months and older. This is a safe beach, a children's menu, the provision of a crib, paid babysitting services, entertainment in the pool area for children and the kids club.

Leisure for adults

It offers vacationers free access to the gym at any convenient time. Paid activities include a sauna, massage rooms, all kinds of water activities and a table tennis club.





Pearl River Garden Hotel 4: traveler reviews

Based on the ratings from various travel sites, Pearl River Garden Hotel can be rated 8 out of 10.

About Pearl River Garden Hotel 4 (Hainan); traveler reviews vary widely. There are people who liked everything, but there are those who have nothing. Trying to summarize and highlight the main thing, we can say that the most important best moments during the rest were the location of the hotel, a clean beach, which is also close, a warm gentle sea, convenient entry into it, a beautifully planned and equipped territory of the entire complex, amazing view from the window to the sea and mountains, an impressive hall. The main disadvantages include the poor condition of both the walls and the furniture in the rooms, they look very scruffy, the paint peels off, the colors are not very pleasant, etc. The high cost was the second negative point. According to many tourists, prices for additional hotel services, excursions, meals in local cafes were overpriced.
