The main attractions of Kalyazin

In the vast forests of the Tver region there is a small town Kalyazin, which is visited by tourists who travel along the Golden Ring of Russia. The village is located on a picturesque shore, at the confluence of the river Zhabnya in the deep Volga. The ancient architecture of the city and the main attractions of Kalyazin are attractive for tourists and are located only 170 kilometers from Moscow.





History

A small town by the river was founded in the 12th century. Unfortunately, the central part of the city with ancient merchant houses and cobbled streets was mainly flooded during the construction of dams and a reservoir. sights

The bell tower of St. Nicholas Cathedral is very famous and attracts many tourists to the city of Kalyazin. Attractions often pay special attention during boat trips. It is popularly known as the "floating bell tower", which has been strengthened more than once at the base to protect it from water. This is the only part of the monastery that survived after the construction of the Uglich Reservoir, during which the main part of the city of Kalyazin was flooded. Sights are given a special place, and for many the city is associated with it. Among other places, you should definitely see the Church of the Ascension of the Lord and the Church of the Epiphany, built behind the Zhabni. Currently, the city has a local history museum. It contains many exhibits and things that tell about the life of the local population over the years. The museum is located in the Church of the Epiphany.

Today the city is a major cultural center of the region. But tourists and visitors are attracted not only by ancient architecture, ancient buildings but also other sights of Kalyazin. The city is a great place for yachting enthusiasts. Fans of water walks actively relax on the Volga, enjoying the magnificent nature. Motorcycle enthusiasts also love and actively visit the city. The sights of Kalyazin are complemented by the biker club of one of the most famous teams called Night Wolves. Therefore, today you can go to Kalyazin not only to touch the ancient culture and enjoy the beautiful nature, but also to have an active rest on the water and in the city.





It is definitely worth noting that the city of Kalyazin is the birthplace of many prominent people in Russia, one of which was the famous writer Saltykov-Shchedrin and the famous artist Krylov. Here you can see the sights of Kalyazin, visit memorable places and feel the atmosphere in which these wonderful and talented people grew up.
