J2 Prime: specs and review

Samsung needs no introduction. In the market of smartphones with the Android operating system, Samsung has long been confidently holding the palm. Not to mention the constant battle for a client with an equally well-known competitor Apple.

The article will discuss the company's budget representative - the Samsung Galaxy J2 Prime smartphone , the characteristics of which are hardly outstanding. Is it worth the money that the famous manufacturer is asking for it, or is the end user still overpaying for the brand? Let's try to figure it out.

General information and technical stuffing

What are the characteristics of the J2 Prime? This smartphone is an entry-level budget device announced by the general public at the end of 2016. Of the features, we can note only the number of processor cores (it has 4 of them), support for 4G networks (LTE), the presence of an LED flash in the front camera and some software solutions.





Consider the main technical specifications of the Samsung J2 Prime. The smartphone uses a 4-core Mediatek MT6737T processor. As a graphics accelerator acts Mali-T720. RAM with a capacity of 1.5 GB is not impressive, but for basic tasks it will be enough. Built-in memory has a capacity of 8 GB (the user has about 4 GB, the rest is occupied by the system and applications).

MicroSD cards supported. The display has a diagonal of 5 inches and a dull resolution of 960 x 540 pixels. The front camera of the device has a resolution of 5 megapixels and its own flash. A standard camera with a resolution of 8 megapixels acts as the main optical module. The J2 Prime operating system is Android 6. The system works in symbiosis with the Samsung TouchWiz II proprietary interface.

Two SIM-cards of micro form factor can be inserted into the device, the device can work in 4G networks. There is a standard set of wireless modules (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and GPS), there is an FM receiver. The battery capacity is 2600 mAh. As you can see, the smartphone Samsung Galaxy J2 Prime in terms of specifications is not better than most budget devices from other manufacturers.

There were no headphones, even the cheapest ones, in the box.

As already mentioned above, the smartphone has on board a 4-core Mediatek MT6737T processor. The chipset is made using 28 nm technology. And what does it mean? Let's try to figure it out. The older brother of J2 Prime, the J5 smartphone, was built on the basis of the Shapdragon 410 processor. When comparing two smartphones, an interesting picture develops in the tests. In terms of performance, the J2 processor is practically not inferior to the J5. There you go!

Communication and wireless modules

The phone supports the use of two SIM-cards micro format. Communication works flawlessly, voice quality is up to par. It is worth noting that much more depends on the operator and the location of the caller. A nice feature of the smartphone is the ability to connect to 4G networks. For a budget smartphone, this is very good.

Also, the device has Bluetooth 4.2, Wi-Fi 802.11n, GPS and GLONASS modules. There are no complaints about their work. Everything is functioning at an excellent level.

Cameras are a little better than "nothing special"

Many are interested in the Samsung Galaxy smartphone . J2 Prime specifications are generally pretty good. And yet, its optical modules are not particularly distinguished from those of competitors.





The pluses include the resolution of the front camera of 5 megapixels and the presence of a flash. Selfies are obtained in very good quality, the properties of the video image when talking via Skype are also not satisfactory.

The main camera with a resolution of 8 megapixels allows you to get good quality pictures. Photos taken in the dark with a flash, of course, cannot be called ideal, but, in fairness, it is worth noting that their quality factor is at a very decent level, given the budget class of the smartphone and the characteristics of the camera. Both cameras can shoot decent quality videos in FullHD resolution.

The screen is sad but understandable

And now about the scoop of tar. The screen has a resolution of 960 x 540 pixels, which with other characteristics of the smartphone looks, to put it mildly, strange. Despite the low resolution, the image is of quite acceptable quality. The display has an oleophobic coating, but this does not prevent it from collecting fingerprints. On the gadgets of some competitors, you can find high resolution screens for the same money.

Then the company decided to use the magic power of its brand, considering that the coveted Samsung name on the box will force the buyer to close their eyes to the low resolution of the screen. Of course, only large companies can afford to play such games with users, thanks to the reputation earned over many years.





In fact, this is what happens: a person, despite the worst characteristics of J2 Prime, buys it because of a well-known brand, and not a fancy gadget of a less well-developed company.

Battery: like everyone else

The smartphone battery capacity is 2600 mAh. With an average load, the device may well live for a day. If you play a serious game, you can seat the device in 4-5 hours. These are quite common indicators for the batteries of most modern devices. The pluses include the fact that the battery on the Samsung Galaxy Prime is removable. Many modern smartphones have a battery, for the sake of a thin body, built-in, and cannot be replaced, in which case.

Summary of dating

What else is this Samsung famous for? The performance of the J2 Prime is a bit confusing. On the one hand, a powerful processor, good cameras, wonderful proprietary software, on the other hand - a screen with archaic resolution and a small amount of internal memory. Another Samsung experiment.





However, despite conflicting specifications, the J2 Prime will in any case find its buyer. Many will turn a blind eye to screen resolution for the sake of gaming performance and the feeling of owning a branded gadget.
