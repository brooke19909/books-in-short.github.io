A bit about the resort

Capo Peloro Club Resort is located on the island of Sicily, which is one of the unique and mysterious places in Italy, surrounded by a halo of rumors, myths and mysteries. And now it is a good resort in the south of the country, striking the imagination with beautiful beaches and numerous natural and architectural attractions. The island has the country's largest active volcano Etna. I must say that in Sicily not only different eras, but also cultures were interwoven: here you can see many temples, medieval churches, magnificent palaces.

Capo Peloro Resort 4 * (Sicily) is located near the city of Messina, which is one of the largest settlements in Sicily and a picturesque resort. The city is a port with active trade; it stretches thirty kilometers along the coast. Messina is located five minutes from the mainland, only the strait separates it from it.



The city has not only the historical part, but also modern areas. There are many historical attractions that will be interesting to tourists. Therefore, interesting leisure is provided to vacationers. Messina as a resort is good because, in addition to the most beautiful beaches, it has many interesting places that can be studied by guests of the island.

Tourists, who first visited this town, are certainly amazed at the abundance of fountains in it, which gives it some airiness, lightness and fabulousness. I would like to emphasize that in Messina not only lovers of the sea coast will like to relax, but also lovers of historical sights. Since the city is always crowded with vacationers, its tourist infrastructure is well developed. There are many cafes and restaurants offering the best dishes of local cuisine, hotels, shops and entertainment centers.

Local Attractions

The historical sights of Messina are very interesting. Unfortunately, not all of them have survived to the present. Many of them were destroyed during the war, which caused significant damage to the town.

Nevertheless, tourists will certainly be interested in a sightseeing tour of Messina, during which you can look into the regional museum and admire the famous works of Caravaggio and Antanello de Messina, as well as visit the museum of folk culture and music.

It will be no less interesting for vacationers to see the Cathedral of Messina, the fountain of Orion, the church of Santa Maria Annuciata dei Catalani of the 12th century, the Cathedral of Santa Maria delli Alemanni.



In addition to historical places, guests can see the most beautiful fountains: Senatorial, Neptune, etc. Local guides can tell the most interesting history of the city to tourists.

The mild Mediterranean climate in Sicily and in Messina makes it possible to rest comfortably throughout the year, the active tourist season in the region lasts from early April to late October. Although the rest of the months of the year are warm enough, the sun almost always shines.

Messina is rich not only in attractions, but in entertainment for active people. On the beaches for tourists - a variety of water activities, and local hotels vying to offer the best pools and spas.

Interesting places in Sicily

Relaxing in the Capo Peloro Resort 4 * (Messina), you can not only limit yourself to excursions in Messina, but also see the most interesting places on the island. Almost every town in Sicily has its own unique attractions. For example, in the famous Palermo you can see churches, monasteries, castles of various eras. Of particular interest to tourists is always the most important building of the city - the Palazzo Normanni (the former residence of the kings).

On the island, near the town of Agrigento, is the Valley of the Temples. Here among the ancient ruins you can see the Temple of Concord, built in honor of the goddess Juno, and the temple of Olympian Zeus. In Sicily there are many places where archaeological excavations are still being carried out.

Shopping enthusiasts will be surprised, but you can make wonderful purchases on the island. There are excellent shopping centers that are in no way inferior to Milan, as well as good outlets. In Palermo, you can visit the most popular and old markets - Il Capo and Vucciiria.





Palermo has some pretty well-known shopping malls like Forum Palermo. In addition, tourists can visit the main shopping streets - Liberta, Viitorio Emanuele, Ruggiero Settimo. Fans of fashionable things will find shops from eminent fashion designers here. But all kinds of souvenirs can be bought in numerous shops.

Summing up the conversation about the hotel, I would like to discuss the reviews of tourists who managed to relax in it in order to have a more complete picture of this place and to understand whether it is worth paying attention to it.
