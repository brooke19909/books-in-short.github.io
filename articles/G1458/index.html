The best entertainment in Astrakhan: list, addresses

Astrakhan is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, the main cultural center of the Lower Volga region. Excellent weather conditions and many opportunities for entertainment make it a favorite vacation spot of our compatriots. Especially in this wonderful city couples with small children like to come. They know for sure that here they will have a place to go with their baby so that they can remember their rest for a long time. The article will present options for the most popular children's entertainment in Astrakhan. Study them carefully and go for pleasant emotions.

Rest in Astrakhan

Going on vacation with a small child, we advise you to think over a plan of cultural events in advance. After all, the overall impression of a family vacation depends on how you have fun and usefully. There are many entertainments for children in Astrakhan. The main thing is to remember that children are very energetic and they like more active entertainment. Also try to make the rest as diverse as possible. Otherwise, the kids will quickly become bored and instead of the vivid impressions that they would like to receive, they will be disappointed.

Zoo and circus

So, a trip to the contact zoo "Baba Frosya" will be a good option for a family vacation. This is a great place where a truly homely, kind atmosphere reigns. Visitors seem to be visiting their beloved grandmother (hence the unusual name of the zoo). Kids will be able to see here more than 100 different animals and birds, some can be fed and stroked. Small visitors are also given small souvenirs as a keepsake. Such entertainment in Astrakhan will long remain in the memory of the child.

On weekends, be sure to go to the circus show. The city circus has a large selection of entertainment programs. Coming here, as if you get into the world of magic and fairy tales. Clever gymnasts and acrobats, fearless trainers of lions and tigers, as well as funny clowns will show their best numbers to you.

Toy museum

A unique collection of old toys preserved from the time of Tsarist Russia can be seen in the Astrakhan Toy Museum. Despite the fact that it opened only four years ago, it has already managed to win love and popularity among residents and visitors of the city. The initiator of the opening of the museum was the professor, candidate of pedagogical sciences Lyudmila Bareeva. Together with his brother, they collected unusual toys from all over Russia for twenty years. In the museum, children and adults can see the old railway, a large number of dolls, cars, helicopters and much more than our grandparents once played. Guides will tell you many interesting facts from the history of the appearance of the first toys and their development. Great entertainment in Astrakhan! Both adults and children will be satisfied. Amusement park "Planet"

What child doesn‚Äôt like to ride rides and jump on trampolines? Your kid will be delighted after visiting the amusement park "Planet". It occupies a vast territory, on which there is everything you need for an amazing vacation. There are flower beds with beautiful flowers everywhere, there are benches on which you can sit and dream or read an interesting book. Planetarium

The mysterious and mysterious world of distant stars and planets awaits all visitors to this entertainment in Astrakhan. On each floor of the planetarium there are interesting exhibits that will be interesting to both children and adults. At the first you can see the spacesuits and food of the astronauts, thanks to the vacuum packaging it does not deteriorate and retains its original taste. Also, kids will be delighted with a computer application with which you can create the illusion of holding a planet in your palm. On the second floor you will be told a lot of interesting facts about comets, the solar eclipse, various constellations and a lot of interesting things.

The planetarium is located at Admiralteyskaya, 1/8. Entrance ticket price is 250 rubles.
