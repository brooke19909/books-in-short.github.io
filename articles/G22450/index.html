Sydney. Sights that have conquered millions of tourists

Sydney ... The sights of this city, as a rule, are to their liking and delight even experienced tourists, whom there are so many that, perhaps, no one is surprised to hear French, Spanish, Portuguese or, quite possibly, Russian speech on the street ! Holidays on numerous beaches will be interesting to those who are fond of beach volleyball, loves surfing and swimming. More relaxed travelers are happy to go on a tour of local monuments or on a ferry excursion. Great conditions have been created for all this.

Section 1. Sydney. Sights of the blossoming metropolis

According to tourists, the atmosphere in this city is quite friendly, calm and even a little relaxed, despite the fact that this settlement is one of the world financial centers. There are many wonderful parks that create an amazing atmosphere for both locals and numerous tourists. A special pride of Australians is the Royal Botanic Garden, where you can have a wonderful time with friends and family.





There are few ancient monuments here, but you should pay attention to the Victorian quarters and places of entertainment. Tourists are interested in visiting buildings that date back to the 19th century: St. James's Church, the Mint, the barracks located in Hyde Park. Blue Mountains with beautiful eucalyptus air enjoy constant popularity - all this is Sydney ... Sights, photos of which will certainly become a worthy decoration of a family album, will live in your memory for a very long time ...

By the way, this city is a real paradise for lovers of delicious food. You can try traditional Australian wine and cheese in the Hunter Valley.

But in the port of Stephens you can get a lot of impressions by seeing dolphins and whales with your own eyes.

Of course, no matter how much you are in this city, there is still not enough time to get to know him completely, and you will definitely want to visit him again and again. What to pay attention to first of all? Is there anything really unusual in Sydney? Of course! We will talk about two such places today.

Section 2. Sydney. Attractions: Aquarium





In Sydney, you should definitely visit the famous large aquarium, the entrance to which is open to all comers. A variety of Australian fauna and flora appears before the eyes of visitors.

In general, the opening of this aquarium was dedicated to the 200th anniversary of Australia. And today it is one of the most important attractions of Sydney.

Particular attention is paid to marine ecosystems. In addition to the main hall, there are open aquariums, which are inhabited by sharks weighing up to 300 kg.

It should be noted that a careful attitude to the fragile nature of various aquatic environments - this is where the attention of visitors to the aquarium is focused.

Section 3. Sydney. Sightseeing: Observatory

For tourists who are interested in monuments of scientific history, the Sydney Observatory (built in 1858) is of great interest. Here you can attend entertaining lectures, as well as observe celestial objects, not only at night, but also in the daytime.





The real historical value of the Sydney Observatory is its unique telescope (manufactured in 1874). 3D-theater allows for a virtual journey in outer space.

The sun today can be observed using the latest alpha-hydrogen telescope. Observations of the starry sky in a planetarium, lectures on astronomy, meteorology and the history of scientific research - here those interested in astronomy can learn a lot of interesting things.

In the store, which is located on the territory of the observatory, you can buy souvenirs for memory.

So, you are convinced that the sights of Sydney (Australia) are not just a set of buildings, monuments and parks. This is something more. The fact that according to many travelers will attract, attract and with each visit more and more fascinate.
