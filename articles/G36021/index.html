Where better to relax on the Black Sea in summer

Every year, thousands of Russians travel to the sea, and everyone wonders where to relax on the Black Sea. Mostly choose the Krasnodar Territory, Crimea and Abkhazia.



The most famous holiday destination is the Krasnodar Territory. However, the demand for key resorts: Adler, Sochi - has declined somewhat in recent years. At the same time, the attractiveness of small resort towns and villages is growing, where beaches are freer and the air is cleaner. Accordingly, housing prices and food during the holiday period will be lower. Great reviews about holidays in Lazarevsky: the sea is cleaner than in Sochi, and there are fewer people on the beaches. Well-developed infrastructure, there is a dolphinarium, water parks, many cafes and other tourist activities. But the surroundings are especially attractive. Developed many routes to dolmens, waterfalls.

Choosing where it is better to relax on the Black Sea, do not forget that Abkhazia is once again gaining popularity. The tourism business is steadily and confidently gaining momentum. The ideal choice for families with children is the legendary Pitsunda beaches. Gagra is an option for outdoor enthusiasts, where you can visit the sights of the Soviet era. The capital of Abkhazia, Sukhum, also offers a huge number of fascinating excursions, comfortable pensions, clean beaches, a cafe with national cuisine.



Many Russians, planning a summer vacation at sea, choose Crimea. It attracts vacationers with unique natural conditions and unique landscapes. Here the blue sea and majestic mountains are combined with a huge number of architectural and historical monuments and attractions. Famous from childhood, the castle Swallow's Nest, Bakhchisaray, Ai-Petri, the famous wines of the Crimea - all this simply can not leave anyone indifferent. Rest here is interesting for both adults and children.

Where better to relax on the Black Sea in the Crimea? Come to Yalta - the pearl of the Crimean peninsula, where you will find a bright, active and eventful vacation. You will visit the Nikitsky Botanical Garden, and the children, of course, will be delighted with the Glade of Tales. The magnificent Massandra Palace will leave a lasting impression. And Yalta itself is an abundance of flowers, greenery and magnificent architecture.



However, there are also downsides to the rest in Yalta: these are crowded beaches, not very clear sea and higher prices. Therefore, dreaming of a comfortable and measured vacation with children, free beaches and clear sea water, it is better to settle outside the city. Therefore, Russian tourists, wondering where it is better to relax on the Black Sea, choose small towns that stretch along the southern coast of the Crimean peninsula. The most famous among them are Miskhor, Foros, Katsivel. There are many beautiful beaches, cozy modern hotels and restaurants, affordable prices and gorgeous nature.
