Rusfinance Bank: customer reviews, services, loans, deposits, interest and terms of payment

It is important to hear the feedback of Rusfinance Bank customers to those who are just about to begin cooperation with this financial institution. This company is considered a very large financial organization, which provides all types of popular and popular services.

About the bank

The feedback from Rusfinance Bank customers who have already encountered the work of this company will help everyone else to make the right impression about it. This financial institution is a subsidiary of PJSC Rosbank.

It specializes in issuing consumer loans through an extensive network in which about 25 thousand partners across the country. In particular, these are car dealers and retail chains. Also, Rusfinance Bank has its own representative offices in 74 regions of the Russian Federation.

The company calls itself one of the leaders in this market, offering services in the field of consumer loans. In particular, this is the provision of loans at points of sale, car loans, the provision of cash, the issuance of bank cards.

According to experts, in the field of car loans the company is one of the three leading banks in our country. It is worth noting that it is part of a large international financial group that uses universal banking models in its work. They allow us to effectively combine sustainable development and financial stability.

Car loan

Judging by the customer reviews of Rusfinance Bank, its activities are primarily focused on three areas. These are car loans, consumer loans and cash loans. We will tell about each of them in the most detail.

The company is a partner of a large number of car dealerships and car dealers throughout the country, it is actively developing a car loan program. Here you can get a loan to purchase a new car of any make and any model, as well as a used one, even if you make a purchase from a private person.

In each of these cases, a loan can be provided in an amount equal to 90% of the value of the car. In this case, a minimum set of documents is required, the loan terms are flexible, a preliminary decision is made within half an hour. It is also worth noting that there are special loan products developed in conjunction with automakers.

Additionally, you can take out life and health insurance, CASCO insurance, a special comprehensive insurance program "Protection", which is designed to protect you from any financial risks. It includes immediately the life and health insurance of the borrower, as well as CASCO. When you include it in the loan amount, the interest rate is reduced.

Consumer loan

One of the most popular banking products is consumer credit. It can be issued for the purchase of goods in more than 23 thousand stores throughout the Russian Federation. As a rule, it is used in the purchase of furniture, household appliances, household goods, computers, fur and jewelry, and much more.

In this case, the amount of a consumer loan is from 2,250 to 500,000 rubles. Loan terms - from three months to two years. A minimum set of documents is required, joint advertising campaigns with distribution networks are carried out, special tariff programs for regular partners are provided.

Consumer credit is a great opportunity to purchase the goods you need today and pay for them for a long time.

In order to get a loan for goods it is not necessary to come to the bank branch. Everything can be done right in the store.

Cash loan

In any branch of the bank you can get a cash loan for any purpose. Amount - from 5 to 50 thousand rubles. Moreover, for loyal customers, the loan amount can be increased to 450 thousand rubles. It is worth noting that the amount of the monthly repayment is fixed immediately, and then does not change throughout the repayment term.

To obtain a loan, you must fill out the appropriate application at the bank office, submit the necessary documents. Requirements vary by customer category.

A loan can be approved for new visitors if they are citizens of the Russian Federation (men from 23 to 63 years old, and women from 23 to 68 years old). Permanent registration is required, as well as that the actual place of work and residence are in the same region.

After making a positive decision, the bank transfers the funds to the account of the borrower.

Cards

In this financial institution it is possible to get a credit card. The credit limit for such a card is from 40 to 150 thousand rubles. It is worth paying attention to users that a fee is charged for servicing and providing a card. This amount can be up to 500 rubles.

The interest rate on the debt of excess limits is from 31 to 35%. Moreover, there is a revolving credit limit on the card up to 150 thousand rubles, as well as a grace period of 50 days. A Cash-back comes to the card in the amount of 1% of the amount of purchases, which will immediately be returned to your card. You can monitor the status of your account using free SMS notifications about all transactions.

Deposits

At the same time, customers are also offered to make deposits in Rusfinance Bank, several programs have been developed for this. True, in fact, they are not placed on the accounts of Rusfinance Bank itself, but of Rosbank.

The basic contribution is "150 years of reliability." Its amount should be from 15 thousand rubles, and the minimum period is three months. Interest rate from 5 to 5.7% (if you decide to withdraw money after three years). Interest is accrued at the end of the term, and then they are paid.

The ATM deposit can be opened with 3 thousand rubles in the account. The minimum term is three months. Its main feature is that it is opened directly at the ATM. Interest is calculated on a monthly basis with capitalization. There is a possibility of replenishment. The rate is 5% per annum.
