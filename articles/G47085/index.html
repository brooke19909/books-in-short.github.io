Dmitry Krikun - a popular photographer and blogger

Dmitry Krikun is a popular photographer and blogger who lives and works in the city of Moscow. In his work, he uses professional photo equipment.

Dmitry Krikun puts his work on all of his pages on social networks.

Dmitry's customers can only be people who have reached the age of 14 years, not younger. Currently, the photographer operates only in Moscow; he does not go to other cities to work.

Dmitry gives the finished result within two weeks, according to the signed contract with the customer, if there are no other special conditions.



There are a lot of positive reviews about Dmitry Krikun. He is considered a young and gifted photographer, whose work is very popular. He has many admirers among the younger generation, although he himself prefers to work with older people.

Dmitry Krikun. Biography

The photographer is only 22 years old, and he already has thousands of subscribers and fans throughout Russia. He has a funny nickname Oleg. Why is Oleg? This is his dark and extraordinary side. Dmitry Krikun shoots a large number of videos and places them on his channel. They are based on absurd humor, which is understandable only to some people, but they are not so few.



Dmitry is very jealous of his work and does not give any advice and comments about the use of photographic equipment. And also does not conduct master classes and various trainings.

Contacts

Dmitry has a lot of different pages in all popular social networks, such as Instagram, VKontakte, YouTube, Periscope, Twitter, etc.

You can always write Dmitry an email to discuss your ideas.

The cost of work

To order a photograph from Dmitry, you can contact him by e-mail. In the letter you can describe in detail the forthcoming work. Dmitry Krikun is a photographer who takes on all the interesting shots, and even the most difficult ones. For him there are no obstacles and barriers.



The cost of each survey is discussed separately. Everything will depend on the complexity of what the customer will come up with. But any final cost arrangement will also include:

  • two-hour work of the photographer;
  • Processing the top 10 photos.

The average cost of a photo walk is about 5,000 rubles.

Dmitry Krikun, by agreement, can issue all the source material in jpg format, but subject to its non-publication.

Each processing of an additional photograph (in addition to 10) will be 350 rubles.

Make-up artist (work cost from 3000 rubles) and stylist (work cost from 2500 rubles) can work with Dmitry on a team.

Dmitry works only on condition of making an advance payment on the card of Sberbank. This is necessary to guarantee your visit to the shooting. It is possible to postpone or cancel the shooting only at least two days before the approved date. Otherwise, the advance payment goes to the compensation of the photographer. Dmitry took such a step, based on his negative experience in working with some customers. He considers prepayment under the contract as a guarantee for both parties to the transaction.
