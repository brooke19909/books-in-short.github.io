Hotel Four Seasons (St. Petersburg, Voznesensky prospekt, 1): photo, description, reviews and how to get there

St. Petersburg is the second largest city in Russia after Moscow and the largest tourist center. People come here in order to see its many sights, on business trips, for treatment, etc. And of course, many comfortable hotels have been built in this city. People who are not too financially constrained can stay, for example, in St. Petersburg at the Four Seasons 5 * hotel.

What is a hotel

Four Seasons are rooms in one of the oldest buildings in St. Petersburg. This hotel is equipped in the famous St. Petersburg "house with lions" - a palace built in the 19th century by the French Montferrand. Before the revolution, this mansion belonged to Prince Lobanov-Rostovsky. Facades, the main entrance, sculptures of lions, the vaults of the first and ground floors, as well as the main staircase in this building are historical objects protected by the state.

This hotel is decorated in a luxurious Napoleonic Empire style. A feature of the rooms of this hotel are the differences not only in the area, but also in the height of the ceilings, the size of the windows, and the layout. Each apartment rented here is individual and unique.

For various business events, residents and guests of St. Petersburg at this hotel can rent a former ballroom. All the necessary equipment here, a projector, a screen, lighting, etc., is hidden in this luxurious room in the ceiling and is lowered if necessary.

Location Features

The Four Seasons Hotel is located in St. Petersburg almost in the center - on the corner of Voznesensky and Admiralteysky Prospekt. The distance from it to the Palace Square is only 500 m. You can literally walk to the Hermitage in 3 minutes, and just 400 m from the lively Nevsky Prospekt.

The distance to the Moscow station from this complex is approximately 3 km. The nearest Pulkovo Airport is 15 km away.

How to get to the hotel

The Four Seasons complex is located in St. Petersburg at Voznesensky Prospekt 1, surrounded by, as already mentioned, many attractions and significant places of the city. The bank of the Neva from this hotel is just a few hundred meters.

If desired, this complex can be reached, for example, by metro. Perhaps this method will be quite convenient for some travelers. In this case, you need to go to the metro station Admiralteyskaya (St. Petersburg).

When leaving the subway, tourists who want to rent rooms in this hotel will need to go to the place no more than 5 minutes. It will only be a couple of blocks away.
