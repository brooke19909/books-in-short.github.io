We learn how to crochet two beautiful variants of the Christmas tree pattern. Interesting motives in a piggy bank of ideas

A hook is a great tool for creating patterns of incredible beauty. If you want to learn how to knit non-trivial, interesting motives with your own hands - this article will be useful to you.

In it, we will look at how to make two original Christmas tree patterns crocheted. The schemes and descriptions of the work process presented in the article will be understandable even for beginners in working with a hook. Take on these amazing motives and create!

Pattern number 1: "Herringbone" for beginner needlewomen. We knit quickly and just an incredibly beautiful motive.

Creating interesting patterns does not always require a needlewoman a lot of time, effort and a high level of skill. We present to your attention one of the easiest ways to crochet the Christmas tree pattern. For its implementation, the master will need only knowledge of the technique for performing the basic elements - air loops, double crochet and without.

Despite the apparent simplicity, the canvas with a pattern looks very impressive and beautiful. Be sure to remember this motif and knit luxurious shawls, scarves, stoles, rugs and any other accessories.

A step-by-step description of the Christmas tree motif

To knit a test piece, take a small amount of yarn and a suitable size hook. Dial 34 air loops (VP). Make 1 double crochet (abbreviated C1H) in the fourth loop from the hook. Next, use this pattern: 4 VP - 1 single crochet (RLS) - 4 VP - 3 C1H (all three in one loop). Complete 2 C1H and rotate the sample. The initial row is ready.

In row number 1, make 2 VPs, in the next loop, perform 2 C1H. After use the scheme: 3 VP - 1 SBN - 3 VP - 2 1 (to the top of the first column of the bottom row) - 1 1 (to the top of the second) - 2 1 (to the top of the third column). Row complete 1 C1H and rotate the product.

In row number 2, run 2 VP. In the second loop, knit 1 1, and in the third - 2 1. To the end of the row, use the pattern: 2 VP - 1 1 - 2 VP - 2 1 (to the top of the first column), 1 1 - 1 1 - 1 1 - 2 1 (to the top of the fifth column of the previous row). Complete 1 C1H in the last loop. Turn the pattern.

Row number 3 begins with 2 VP. In the second and third loops, knit 1 1, in the fourth - 2 1. Now use the scheme: 1 VP - 2 1 (to the top of the column of the lower row), 5 1 (one in the following loops), 2 1 (the last vertex of the column). Row complete 1 C1H. Knit all subsequent rows by analogy to obtain the required height of the sample.

Pattern number 2. Beautiful motif with Christmas trees for creating dense products

If it is necessary to create a dense canvas, for example, for winter wardrobe items, rugs or bedspreads, needlewomen successfully use the following scheme of a spectacular Christmas tree pattern.

We recommend that you familiarize yourself with this beautiful crochet pattern and use it in your work! Products made in this technique have an interesting texture and look original and unusually stylish.

Crochet herringbone pattern. Schemes and description of the work process

To knit a test pattern, we collect 24 VP.

In row number 1, we retreat three loops and, starting from the fourth, we knit 1 1 in each. We turn our blank.

In row No. 2, we form 3 VP and 3 C1H. In the sixth loop we make * 1 a concave column, then we perform 1 VP and another 1 concave column - in the next *. Next, we knit 7 C1H, again repeat once * - *. Close row 4 C1H. We turn the workpiece.

In row number 3 we create 3 VP and 3 C1N. Make * 1 a convex double crochet. In the arch of 1 VP, we perform 1 1 - 1 VP - 1 1. In the next chain we knit 1 convex column *. Next, perform 6 C1H, repeat the pattern * - * and complete row 4 C1H. We turn the sample.
