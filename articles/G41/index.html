LG G2 Mini: reviews. Specifications, instructions, prices, photos

LG is one of those companies that fight for the title of the best smartphone maker on the planet. Specifications

A manufacturer from South Korea tried to do everything to make this smartphone the best, at least among the ‚Äúsmall copies‚Äù of the flagships. Therefore, the developers equipped the LG d618 G2 mini with very strong features. The processor consists of four cores, each of which has a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz. As a RAM, it was decided to put 1 gigabyte, and as internal internal memory - 8 gigabytes. If this amount is small, then this smartphone supports MicroSD flash cards up to 32 gigabytes. The device itself has a screen diagonal of 4.7 inches, but its resolution is not very large, only 540 x 960. In addition, the latest version of Android is used as the operating system - 4.4.

This smartphone has several names. One of them is the LG Optimus G2 mini, as a tribute to the previous line of corporation smartphones - Optimus. The 8-megapixel camera of this smartphone decorates its back side, while the quality of the pictures remains at a fairly good level.

Ergonomics





Many people liked the design of the LG G2 mini. The appearance characteristics of the smartphone are very attractive. The whole phone is made of plastic. At first glance, everything is done at a fairly high level. The material itself is very high quality, in the hands of the phone is very convenient. On the front of the smartphone are touch control buttons, as well as a speaker and a front camera. Turning the device over, you can find a rather interesting phone socket along with the main camera. The panel attracts special attention. The fact is that this is not just an ordinary piece of plastic, but slightly corrugated. This was probably done in order to avoid the phone‚Äôs excess markiness and to improve the grip of the LG d618 G2 mini white smartphone with a hand. If you look at the bottom end of the smartphone, you can find several holes. Two of them are speakers for conversation, and one is a connector designed both for charging the phone and for connecting a smartphone to a personal computer. In the upper end of the device is a headset jack. It is also worth noting that the phone‚Äôs power button (as well as volume controls) are located in an innovative way, namely on the rear panel, right under the camera. Display

LG G2 mini (technical specifications of the display and its size) surprised everyone even when it was announced, because the screen size is not standard 4 or 4.3 inches, like other "younger brothers" flagships, but 4.7 at once. However, despite such a large size, the developers were stingy with its resolution of 1280 x 960. Instead, we get unacceptable 960 x 540. The coating of the glass on the screen itself is very tactile and not too scratched, which is a definite plus. The screen brightness is at a very good level, and the colors displayed on the smartphone display look very realistic. The problem occurs in bright sunlight. In this case, the image becomes faded, and the detail is reduced. But this is a problem for many smartphones, so this fact should not be considered a special minus.

The latest version of Android, namely Android 4.4, is installed on the LG G2 mini phone , reviews of which are good enough both from buyers and from reputable experts. In addition, the company has developed several interesting programs that will facilitate not only the use of a smartphone, but also the use of various household and electronic devices that are turned on using the remote control. LG G2 mini, specifications: battery and runtime

That's where the real plus and advantage of this model is in its battery. The battery capacity of the device is large enough for those smartphones that exist on the market today. It is 2440 mAh. This suggests that you can use the smartphone for various purposes for almost the entire working day, and it will not require a momentary charge. If you use your smartphone adequately, then the battery charge can easily last for two business days.



Audio & Video

The sound quality of this unit is good, but still inferior to the clear leaders in these characteristics, which are the NTS and Samsung. As for the video, thanks to a sufficiently strong processor and a gigabyte of RAM, the videos play quickly and without hangs. But there are problems associated with screen resolution. Due to the fact that it is only 960 x 540, sometimes there is a slight ripple in the frames.

Camera and photo quality

LG G2 mini, the technical characteristics of which do not cause any questions, has two cameras: the main and front. The main camera has eight million pixels, which is quite normal for a small copy of the flagship. The quality of the photos taken by this camera is kept at a fairly high level. It should be noted that the detail and clarity of the image when shooting with a smartphone from LG is better than similar smartphones of other companies. This can not but rejoice the first owners of this device, especially if for them the camera and the quality of the photos in the smartphone is the most important factor.

Communication and Internet





Let's look at such an important factor for the phone as communication, with the smartphone LG G2 mini. Reviews of many owners say that this is one of the best devices in order to go online and chat with friends. And perhaps they are right. The fact is that when you connect to the Internet, the connection occurs almost immediately, and the initial connection speed is always at a very high level. As for the Wi-Fi network, the situation is similar. When you find the necessary access point, the connection occurs instantly.

As for conversational communication, which is the main function of any phone, there are also no problems. The interlocutor is heard perfectly even at a speaker volume level of fifty percent. In case you are on a busy street, an 80 percent volume level is enough to hear a friend located on the other side of the connection.

Options and prices for this gadget

After purchasing a smartphone from a South Korean company, you will receive a box with all the necessary accessories for the LG G2 mini. The paper-based manual is the first thing you see when you open the box. It can be useful for those people who first encountered the issue of using a smartphone. In this manual you can find all the necessary information regarding the device, its functions and capabilities. In addition to the documentation, you will also receive a headset in the form of headphones. Moreover, this accessory is of very good quality. Of course, if you are a music lover, it is preferable to buy something better, but for an ordinary listener these headphones are a pretty decent option. Another component in the box is a USB adapter, designed to connect a smartphone to a computer. Well, the final item in the box is the charger. Case for LG G2 mini is not included, but you can always buy it almost anywhere.

As for the prices for this product, they are set by different stores independently. In some cases, there may be a different price for this phone, depending on the additional flash memory.
