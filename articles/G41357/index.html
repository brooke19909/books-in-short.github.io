The best professional synthesizers: a review of models and reviews

Thanks to playing a musical instrument, you can easily relax and delight people around. A synthesizer is a great solution for small apartments. It will not take up much space and will allow you to escape from pressing problems at any time. Parents often acquire such an instrument so that their child in parallel with the main school also studies in music, and it is also perfect for self-development. Synthesizers differ in the number of keys, sound, and additional options. The article will describe the model of professional synthesizers.

Which company to prefer?

If you do not want to devote much time to selecting a company, then you can pay attention to the top five. We are talking about Casio, Akai, Korg, Yamaha, Medeli. Models of these manufacturers differ in sound, as well as their additional functions, which is why before buying a certain one you need to not only pay attention to the characteristics, but also read reviews.

Synthesizer operation

Thanks to a professional synthesizer, you can play at home, in a cafe or at any events without much difficulty. More serious models are most often acquired either by specialists or those who already have some skills and want to work hard.

The dimensions of this device make it easy to carry and transport the device. It fits quietly in the back seat. Can be placed not only on a small stage, but also in a small room. There is an audio output that allows you to connect to the sound amplifier, making the volume comfortable for the audience.

Synthesizers differ in sound. You can play the keyboard, but the sound will be like a guitar or drums. The equipment will allow you to mix notes, turn on background sounds, and so on. Due to this, anyone can learn complex compositions in the shortest possible time. On an ordinary piano, such results can be achieved only after a few months (sometimes years) of training.

Devices work incredibly simple. When a key is pressed, a short circuit occurs, a signal is sent. The latter processes the central processor, which will allow you to get a sound wave at the output. If you change the characteristics, you can get a different tone.

The benefits of professional devices

Like any other device, professional synthesizers have their advantages. They are used at performances. They allow you to play a large number of sounds, save the tunes you just played and work with them.

Some models have a color display. Keys from 61 to 88 pieces. There is an active, passive and hammer type device. You can record a voice and process it. These devices have about 700 different tones. As for polyphony, then - up to 200 votes. The effects are saturated.

Disadvantages of devices

Professional synthesizers have their drawbacks. There are few of them. Users note a high price category, which is justified by a large number of characteristics. With prolonged use, the devices heat up. At multi-instrumental concerts, digital synthesizers do not stand out, they sound modest.

Medeli M17

Cost: about 10 thousand rubles. Makes China.

This synthesizer is inexpensive. It is possible to connect pedals to it. The keys are full-sized, sensitive enough. Polyphony is supported. Up to 64 notes can be played simultaneously. There are 390 tones. With regards to the learning function, it has been expanded to 110 melodies. That is why this professional tool is great for beginners and amateurs.

The model belongs to the budget segment. There is auto accompaniment. Works about 100 styles. Built-in display. The reviews note the real sound, quiet key travel, the information content of the display, as well as the separation of the keyboard. Of the bonuses, the buyer gets a double sound and the ability to record a song.

Casio CTK-7200

The cost of the device: about 30 thousand rubles. It is made in Japan.

The Casio CTK-7200 professional synthesizer has 61 buttons and 820 tones. The keys, although full-sized, are compact. If desired, you can connect headphones, a microphone, pedals and use the controller to change the tone. There is a display. The synthesizer supports working with memory cards, so you can play tunes from the media. Polyphony is excellent. The instrument can play up to 64 notes at a time. Powered by batteries or batteries.

With regards to auto accompaniment, up to 260 styles are presented here. You can connect a player, a guitar, a microphone to the model, and record the composition to a memory card. Therefore, it is often used in an instrumental ensemble. The model is also used to create the arrangement. Due to the presence of special sliders, you can experiment with sound. The sound is good, but not suitable for professionals. More designed for beginners, amateurs.

Casio CTK-6200

Japan produces some of the best professional synthesizers. The described model can be purchased for 20 thousand rubles. Like the previous one, this one is made in Japan.

This device has good functionality. The price fully justifies the filling of the device. This synthesizer is unlikely to be suitable for beginners, but for those who are ready to develop, but no longer want to work with "light" devices - completely. In addition, the device is inexpensive. Of the pluses, it should be noted a powerful amplifier (6 W), the ability to work with memory cards, the presence of a linear input and an arpeggiator. Polyphony at the average level - 48 sounds. Tones - 700, which is a good indicator.

Buyers emphasize that they purchase a device for working in a cafe or other medium-sized sites. It is on them that the device sounds most profitable.

Yamaha PSR-R200

A professional Yamaha synthesizer can be bought for 15 thousand rubles. Production established in Japan.

The device is famous for its good build and its characteristics. This device, although professional, is suitable for both beginners and the music guru. The functionality is simple, but allows you to create complete compositions. There is a training mode, about 9 sound effects, an output and MIDI type input, it is possible to work on battery power. In addition, the synthesizer is very small and weighs no more than 4.5 kg. These points are noted by buyers. Carry the device as conveniently as possible.

Yamaha PSR-S975

Previously, Japan was famous for its professional Yamaha PSR-2700 synthesizer, but now this model is considered obsolete. You can buy it only by hand. Model S975 is sold for 138 thousand rubles.

This option is for advanced musicians. The device can do almost everything, being almost an arrangement station. This option can also be used at home.

There is a 128-note polyphony, 61 dynamic keys. As for the tone library, more than 1 thousand options are presented. The device can be connected to a USB flash drive or computer, playing music. The screen has a diagonal of 7 inches. With regards to the functionality, the Yamaha S975 professional synthesizer has an equalizer, compressor, noise reduction and other options. There is a large amount of internal memory, which allows you to work with the device without external media.

Reviews about the device are good. In most cases, it is used to work on a large stage. Buyers note that when working with an orchestra, sound is not lost and sounds great.
