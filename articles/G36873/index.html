Fun in Anapa for the whole family: Golden Beach water park

This place occupies a vast territory and is located near the Central Beach. There are several pools, 11 slides, among which it is worth noting the "Yellow River" with a length of 110 meters. The main attraction of the water park is the artificial sea, where waves of 3-4 points high are raging. There is also an attraction "Storm Hill" with heavily boiling water. The infrastructure is also at the proper level, rescuers work in the territory. Ticket price varies within 1000 rubles.
