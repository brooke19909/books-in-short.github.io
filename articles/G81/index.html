Apartments "Manor Burger" - description, history and interesting facts

Vyborg is an ancient city in the north of Russia, a unique architectural monument. It is not even necessary to look for some sights here, because the whole city is an open-air museum. Almost every building in the historical center is some kind of medieval building. And to stay in this corner of the Middle Ages is also best in some charming mini-hotel, stylized as an old house.





History of the Manor of the Burger Hotel

In the very center of Vyborg, at the intersection of Progonnaya Street and the Water Outpost, there is a complex of ancient buildings, which is a unique architectural monument. These buildings are called Burger Manor. Thick walls made of large stones attract the attention of tourists. These buildings have long been owned by wealthy merchants and wealthy citizens. And very close to this medieval building is a small hotel with the same name "Manor Burger". The apartments are just one room with beautiful views of the old part of the city. And the famous Vyborg Castle is only a few minutes on foot from here.

Accommodations

The guests who have chosen Apartments "Manor Burger" (Vyborg) have at their disposal their own kitchen (equipped with an oven), a private bathroom (towels are provided), a bedroom (bed linen is included in the price), a spacious terrace.

Room under the so-called "family" category is designed for four people. Additional relocation is also possible. Children under 12 years old can stay in the room for free. No beds are available. An extra bed is also available.





Check in time and cost

Check-in at the Burger Manor apartment: from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m.

Guests will be asked to leave from 7 to 11:30.

The cost varies from 4 to 7 thousand rubles per room. So, on weekdays and off-season, the price is the same. For the number per day you will give four thousand rubles. On holidays, weekends and days of festivals, the cost increases to seven thousand rubles. Book better in advance. This is especially true for the July days, when the city hosts a knightly festival. Then you should book your accommodation in advance, otherwise there is a risk of settling somewhere on the outskirts of the city, if not at all in Zelenogorsk or St. Petersburg.

It is also worth taking care of early booking if you want to celebrate New Year's holidays in this ancient unique city. The flow of tourists is increasing significantly. In these apartments, you can arrange your New Year's dinner yourself, sitting on a spacious terrace overlooking the center. And after listening to the chiming clock (there is a TV in the room), you should go for a walk around the city. Castle Island is only a 10-12 minute walk. This is really a great accommodation option in Vyborg.

As for discounts, when booking a few days the price will be slightly lower. So, for example, four days on weekdays will cost 19 thousand rubles. This is instead of 20, if you count on 4,000 per day.

In any case, you are in the very center of the city. Everything is within walking distance and everything is at hand. This is very convenient, because in the evening there is no need to take a taxi to get to your hotel. You can always walk.

Cons of apartment accommodation

Despite the fact that the "Burger Manor" has won numerous thanks from tourists, here, as in any other place, there are some flaws. To some they may seem insignificant, but someone will think about it.

For example, pets are not allowed here. It will not work to come to Vyborg with your beloved dog, or you will have to look for another accommodation option.





Also, for someone, a serious drawback may be that in the apartment payment by card is not allowed. In the modern world, we are used to paying many things by bank transfer, including hotel accommodation. However, on the other hand, money for payment can be withdrawn at any ATM nearby. The apartments are located in the heart of the city, there is where to withdraw cash.

So, having considered this placement option, we can draw the following conclusions. Apartments "Manor Burger" (Russia) is perfect for the whole family. The hotel is located in a quiet location, moreover in a private building. No annoying or noisy neighbors - just soothing silence and a great view of ancient medieval buildings.
