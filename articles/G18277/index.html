The recreation center "Kama Dali" in Udmurtia: description, photo

Udmurtia is rightfully considered one of the most beautiful regions of Russia. Local camp sites are usually located in luxurious mixed forests on the banks of picturesque lakes or rivers. There are a lot of such complexes built in Udmurtia. And one of the largest and most famous is the Kama Dali Center . Reviews about this camp site are different.

Location Features

There is a convenient complex "Kama Dali" near the village of Galevo on the banks of a large reservoir. The regional center of Udmurtia, the city of Izhevsk, is located about 80 km from it. The largest settlement closest to the base is Votkinsk. It is 20 km from the base. In winter, a ski complex operates near the center.

The road to this settlement on the highway takes about 2 hours. Next, travelers need to turn to the village of Galevo and drive about 17 km. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the road from Izhevsk to the base is, unfortunately, not in very good condition. However, you can still get to your destination quickly enough.

On a minibus, tourists usually travel from the bus station on a flight, also following to Votkinsk. Then, those who wish to relax at the Kama Dali base make a transfer to the bus going towards Galevo. In any case, booking rooms at this base is in advance - by phone. Tourists who arrived without warning may not be provided because of its absence. This base in Udmurtia is really very popular.

What is the proposed housing

If desired, tourists can reserve a base at the Kama Dali base ( photos are offered to the attention of the reader on this page) in the Cat House Hotel located on its territory or in a room in one of the panel or chopped houses. Most vacationers prefer to choose the latter option. Living in log cabins on this base is really convenient. It‚Äôs cool in the heat in these buildings, and warm in the cold. The panel houses are less comfortable, but the rooms in them are a bit cheaper than in the chopped ones.

The Koshkin Dom Hotel is a small three-story building. It is located next to the main square of the base. The rooms here are for rent in different categories.

All rooms offered by the base guests are provided with such modern amenities as:

  • refrigerators;
  • TV sets
  • armchairs;
  • electric kettles;
  • tableware.

The rooms located on the second and third floors have balconies. Also, all rooms of the Kama Dali complex have well-equipped showers.





Base infrastructure

Quite a good service is offered to tourists on the territory of the complex. " Kama Dali" - a recreation center, guests of which can use :

  • free parking with a canopy;
  • rental point for catamarans, boats and jet skis ;
  • by the pool;
  • Russian bath.

Also on the territory of the base there are football and basketball fields, a billiard room, a children's playground. Near the houses are equipped with places designed for frying barbecue and barbecue (paid).

Nutrition

Most tourists come to this complex, of course, not for long - for 2-3 days. Such vacationers most often bring food with them. Tourists who come to the base for a longer period can visit a small cafe on its territory. Meals , unfortunately, are not included in the cost of living at the Kama Dali base . Tourists must pay for breakfast, lunch and dinner separately. There is food in a cafe base relatively inexpensive - about 500-600 rubles for lunch.





Beach

As already mentioned, the Kama Dali base (Udmurtia) is located on the banks of the large Votkinsk reservoir. Therefore, if desired, its guests in the summertime have the opportunity, including swimming and sunbathing. Unfortunately, there is no private beach at the complex. This, of course, is a big drawback of the base. But tourists can always rent a catamaran and visit one of the many wild beaches located in the immediate vicinity of the camp site. Swimming in such places is comfortable. However, tourists who decide to sunbathe on the wild beach of the reservoir should take measures to prevent tick bites in advance.

Opinion of the guests about the rooms of the complex

Reviews about this camp site on the network are both good and not very good. Most of the holidaymakers consider the rooms rented in the complex to be quite comfortable. This is especially true for hotel rooms and chopped houses. Former guests of panel houses, there are sometimes various kinds of complaints regarding the convenience of rooms. For example, in such rooms the doors may not close or sockets may not work.





But in any case, the administration of this base is trying to solve all the problems arising from the residents promptly. The maids in the complex are also responsible for their duties. And if the cleaning of the rooms may also not be carried out regularly enough, then the bed linen here is always very clean and fresh.

The advantages of this base, many tourists include the presence in the showers of a free set of toiletries. In such complexes in Udmurtia, and in other regions of Russia, such a service is usually not provided to guests.

Base infrastructure reviews

The rooms in this complex, therefore, for rent, according to tourists, are quite decent. The administration of the center does everything possible to ensure its guests a comfortable stay. ‚ÄúKama Dali‚Äù in this regard is considered a relatively convenient base. However, the infrastructure of this complex, unfortunately, has earned mixed reviews from guests. The advantages of the service provided by the hotel tourists include the presence of a pool. The water in it is usually clean. In addition, guests of the base can use sun loungers for free. Also, the advantage of this center is the presence of a playground.

The disadvantages of the complex tourists include not too well-thought-out location of infrastructure. For example, residents of the Cat House hotel only need to walk about 5 minutes to the pool. Which, of course, is far. A catamaran rental point in this center is located under a rather steep hill. Getting down from it, for example, will not be very convenient for older people.





Also, many consider the lack of a base to be the absence of ATMs on its territory. For all additional services, guests have to pay in cash. The nearest ATM to the base is located far away - in Votkinsk.

Tourists and parking in this complex do not praise too much. Most vacationers come to the base, of course, by car. Parking spaces for the center are usually enough for all guests. However, unfortunately, far from all tourists manage to install the car under a canopy.





Dining Room Reviews

There are also mixed reviews about the food in the cafe at the Kama Dali base . The dishes served at this establishment are considered by most tourists to be quite tasty. They are prepared from natural products and are never cold or stale. However, some guests still have certain complaints about the staff of the cafe. Sometimes waiters and kitchen workers, unfortunately, may not be particularly polite with customers.
