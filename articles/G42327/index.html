Kingdom of Thailand

This is an Asian country that has not experienced the influence of European colonial regimes. Perhaps due to this fact, she was able to maintain her identity and ethnicity. Almost 80% of the population are Thais and Lao.

The country honors its history. Plots from the ethnic group became the basis of colorful colorful shows that travelers enjoy watching, taking souvenirs with them and leaving the country significant financial resources, which each year make up the largest part of the country's income.

The king in Thailand is not a mythical figure. This is a revered ruler who defends the interests of his people. The tradition of respect for the king deserves absolute reverence, and you should be very careful in showing your attitude to the head of state in order to avoid problems.

Thailand, tours which is in great demand among Russian tourists, actively develops not only leisure infrastructure. This country turns its history and ethnic traditions into elements of world culture, introducing into it the nature of harmony and oriental wisdom. In this country of Southeast Asia, you can choose to rest on the mainland, islands, or you can combine everything together.

What a good vacation in Phuket is

There are some of the most popular tourist destinations in Thailand. This is a rest on the mainland (Pattaya), rest with a direct flight to Phuket or on other islands (with an additional transfer), very picturesque and colorful. Koh Samui, Koh Chang are the most popular of them.

Hotel Description

Andaman Beach Suites 4 * Hotel is located 150 meters from Patong Beach. Arriving at the international airport in Phuket, you can reach the hotel by car in less than an hour.

The 22-story high-rise building offers panoramic views of the Adaman Sea in the Indian Ocean. The hotel's rooftop pool allows you to combine the contemplation of magnificent views with pleasant thalasso treatments and sunbathing.

Conveniently located for shopping and entertainment in Patong. Jungceylon Shopping Center is a 15-minute walk. In walking distance - the lively Bangla Road promenade, which has a lot of clubs, snack bars, night bars and shopping places. You can visit the not very famous, but very curious Tin Mining Museum, which is also located near the hotel. The road to it can be clarified at the reception of the hotel.

The hotel was built in 1993, and in 2013 the rooms were renovated.

Useful Tips

For a pleasant trip to the Kingdom of Thailand, you should consider the features and ‚Äúusefulness‚Äù that will help to leave only positive memories of the trip:

  • When preparing for your trip, print and bring along a tourist dictionary. Your attempt to communicate in Thai will solve any question that arises in the best way.
  • Examine your place of residence in advance and make a map of independent travels so as not to waste precious time relaxing in exploring an unfamiliar area.
  • You should not take valuables with you. Not every hotel offers a safe. A hotel of only a high level, from 3 * + and above, guarantees the safety of valuables in personal cells.
  • You should not take expensive clothes. Everything you need to relax is best purchased on site. This will save money and leave new things in mind.
  • Take small souvenirs with you - this will be the best form of appreciation for the quality services in Thailand.
  • This country has very high humidity. In the case of abscesses on the skin, or infection of the wounds, on-site healing will be prolonged. You should take antiseptics with you in this case.




For travel it is better to take out extended insurance, which includes additional risks (risk of sunburn, dental services, sports). Otherwise, the need to use medical services of any plan will be an expensive pleasure for foreigners.
