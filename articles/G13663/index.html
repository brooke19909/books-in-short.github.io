Camp "Kupalinka" in Radoshkovichi

Caring parents always want their son or daughter to have a good and fun vacation. A great idea is to send your child to the Kupalinka camp in Radoshkovichi (Belarus). Here, the child will be able to recover and relax to the fullest. Professional staff does not let kids get bored for a single minute.

What is good about Kupalinka Camp?

The first thing you should pay attention to is the location of the Kupalink DOL. The institution is located in a beautiful corner of the Minsk region, surrounded by a picturesque and fragrant pine forest. Near the camp flows the river Udranka. This all suggests that the Kupalinka camp will give the kids an excellent microclimate. And also boys and girls will receive a lot of positive emotions in this place, because wherever you look, beautiful landscapes and enchanting nature are everywhere.



Children from 6 to 18 years old can be sent to the camp. This allows both young and adults to work with peers and older or younger children. Boys and girls are divided into groups by age. Each squad is a separate state in which its preparations for the holidays and events for the competitions take place.

Living conditions in the children's camp "Kupalinka"

Buildings for living in DOL are brick. Each structure consists of 2 or 3 floors. Each room is designed to accommodate four people. And amenities (shower, toilet) are located on the floors.

Also in the center of each floor there is a games room where children can watch TV, play games or just relax.

Food in the camp "Kupalinka" five times a day. The menu is thought out to the smallest detail, so children get all the necessary vitamins and minerals for health.

Children's leisure in the Kupalinka recreation center

Vacationers in the institution will surely find entertainment and activities to their liking. Indeed, on the territory of the camp "Kupalinka" there is everything that is necessary:

  • Places for sports (playgrounds, football, basketball and volleyball fields).
  • There is also a cinema hall where children are offered fascinating and informative cartoons or movies.




  • On the territory there are pavilions in which circles of various subjects on interests operate. Here you can learn how to make origami, sew soft toys. Many other interesting activities are also held.
  • Festivals are regularly held, the main characters of which are the children themselves.
  • For boys and girls, professional actors show theatrical performances.
  • In the evenings, there are discos for all groups.

As you can see, there are a lot of entertainment and leisure options. Therefore, sociable and shy, funny and serious children will surely find something for themselves to their liking, in which they will be able to fully reveal their talents and skills.

What do you need to prepare for admission to the camp?

When sending a child to the Kupalinka camp in Radoshkovichi, you need to collect the following things for him:

  • personal hygiene items;
  • a set of sportswear;
  • several sets of interchangeable clothes;
  • two pairs of shoes for everyday use;
  • sports shoes;
  • underwear - several sets;
  • elegant suit or dress for evening events and discos;
  • personal items that a child may need.

Do not give boys and girls with them expensive mobile phones, jewelry made of precious metals, large sums of money.



Professional and qualified staff will find an approach to any child. Children open up and find new talents under the watchful eye of counselors and leading holidays. And most importantly, after staying in such a climate, boys and girls acquire strong immunity and become healthier. Therefore, it is worth sending your favorite child to the Kupalinka camp, there are many new acquaintances, fun events and unforgettable trips around the world of childhood.
