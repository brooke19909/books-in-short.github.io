Divnomorskoe, hotels by the sea: a description of the best

On the shore of the Black Sea bay, in a picturesque valley is a small resort village Divnomorskoe. The air here is always clean and fresh, because from here the famous pine forest begins and stretches along the sea, in which Pitsunda pine grows . Today, the village of Divnomorskoye, which is often called Divnomorskoye, has become a real developed resort, which is visited annually by thousands of tourists.

Climatic conditions

The climate in these places is warm and mild. Summer heat is tolerated quite easily thanks to the sea breezes. In July, the air warms up to an average of +25 ¬∞ C, and water - up to +24 ¬∞ C. The beach season is long - from late May to mid-October. In September, the velvet season begins in these parts, when the water in the sea is still very warm and the heat subsides. Therefore, at this time there are always many vacationers in Divnomorskoye.



Where to stay?

Fortunately, there were times left when tourists were looking for at least some corner in this resort village, not even dreaming of an apartment, to spend several days of their long-awaited vacation here.

Today, the village of Divnomorskoe has radically changed and prettier . Hotels on the seashore are presented here for every taste and financial wealth. In this article we will present you with a small overview of the most popular and, according to vacationers, the most comfortable hotels in the resort.

Divnomorskoe: hotels with a pool by the sea

Hotel Nevsky (Primorskaya St., 20) is located at a distance of only 290 meters from the sea. The hotel staff meets their guests at the station and takes them to the place of rest for free.

The rooms are double and triple rooms standard, deluxe, junior suite. They have all the amenities, furnished with stylish furniture and necessary appliances. On the territory there is a dining room, a playground, a pool, a games room, its own comfortable beach, on which there are no problems with umbrellas and sunbeds.

Here you can rent scooters, pedal boats, ride a banana. Little vacationers will have a great time in the children's water park, and for adults, a boat trip to the open sea will certainly be interesting.

House Hotel (Golubodalskaya, 27)

It should be noted that today quite high demands are made by tourists choosing hotels on the seashore in the village of Divnomorskoe: their own beach, spacious and modern rooms, a swimming pool, proximity to the sea, etc.



All these requirements are met by the House Hotel, located just a few minutes walk from the beach. On its territory there is a large outdoor pool and a magnificent garden. Twenty rooms of all categories are offered for living: from "economy" to "luxury". All are equipped with air conditioning, LCD TVs and satellite TV. Bathrooms have all the necessary toiletries.

"Athena" (Lenin St., 12)

Hotels (Divnomorskoe) on the seafront with food are always in high demand, because few people want to spend the long-awaited vacation time on cooking. "Athena" refers to those institutions whose owners took into account this desire of vacationers. The friendly hotel staff guarantees its guests excellent service. The cafe located on the territory of the complex offers delicious and varied dishes, and if you want to cook your own barbecue in the fresh air, then you will have a barbecue area.





The hotel is located in a beautiful green area, just 300 meters from the sea. At the same time, shops, cafes, and public transport are located nearby. The number of rooms is:

  • double standard and comfort;
  • triple comfort and deluxe.

All rooms feature functional, modern furniture, air conditioning, satellite TV and internet access.

"Flamingo" (st. O. Koshevy, 19)

Not only beach lovers come to Divnomorskoe. Hotels by the sea often offer preventative and wellness treatments for a number of diseases. An example of this is the Flamingo boarding house, located in a picturesque corner of the village almost on the seashore.

It treats many ailments - from diseases of the endocrine system to problems with the gastrointestinal tract and respiratory system. In the pension you can stay in rooms of different categories: standard, deluxe and junior suite. All of them are beautifully furnished and equipped with necessary appliances: air conditioning, televisions, refrigerators.

The administration of the pension pays great attention to the leisure of fans of outdoor activities. A beautiful sports complex with modern gyms has been built on site. In addition, everyone can enjoy tennis or horse riding. Every day, the hotel hosts cultural events: dancing, showing films, animation programs.



"Prometheus" (st. Kirova, 10)

Someone may be surprised, but today some domestic hotels (Divnomorskoe) can compete with foreign analogues. There are many hotels on the seashore. Prometheus is somewhat removed from the coast (1.7 km). But this is more than compensated by the excellent service, luxurious decoration of rooms and well-groomed territory. The hotel is pet friendly.



Verona (Chernomorskaya St., 8B)

If you are choosing hotels on the seafront in the village of Divnomorskoe, we recommend that you pay attention to the Verona Hotel. It is located very close to the pebble beach. The hotel welcomes friendly staff at the 24-hour front desk. Here, vacationers can get all the necessary information about the village, the opening hours of shops and entertainment venues.



The hotel offers thirteen comfort and junior suites. They are decorated in a classic style. For their design, natural materials in pastel colors were used. The rooms are equipped with LCD TVs, refrigerators. The comfort rooms have a bathroom with a shower, the junior suites have a bathroom. Numerous cafes and cozy restaurants are located very close to the hotel.

Sparta (Lenin St., 10/2)

In the village of Divnomorskoye, hotels by the sea can be chosen for every taste: large and lively or small and homely. These include the Sparta complex, located in the very center of the village, one hundred and fifty meters from the promenade and the central beach. The hotel has three types of rooms: double standard plus, double standard, triple room with extra bed. In 2011, all rooms were renovated and furnished with new furniture.



The hotel has a cafe, where guests can enjoy set meals or custom-made meals.

Tourists reviews

Judging by the reviews, today many Russians already spent their holidays in the village of Divnomorskoye. Hotels by the sea are well-known among vacationers. As a rule, these are new, modern, not too large buildings. Many guests are attracted to them by the lack of fuss and noise, from which they so want to relax at least during their holidays.

The indisputable advantage of the village travelers call the opportunity to choose a hotel in accordance with their requirements. I am glad that in many hotels much attention is paid to young guests, for whom playgrounds and games rooms are created.
