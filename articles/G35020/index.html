Contrast and viewing angles

Another important feature of concave screens is the increased viewing angle and contrast. Reflections and Optical Distortion





The non-standard surface of the screen with curvature changes the angle of reflection of light rays, which reduces glare. Consequently, the concavity forms a focal point at a distance of half the radius of curvature from the center of the screen. Reflections will blur if the viewer sits farther than that focal point. In addition, Samsung TV with a curved screen is able to reduce optical distortion. But in this case, the opinions of experts are not so clear. The minimization of distortion is explained by the fact that the human eye does not have a flat surface, but when interacting with the same screen, harmonious perception is ensured.
