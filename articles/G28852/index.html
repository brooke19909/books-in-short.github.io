Barracuda Beach and other Adler coasts

Every summer, people wonder where to go on vacation. Yes, so that inexpensive, beautiful and good. Everyone begins to sort out the options: Turkey, Miami, Hawaiian Islands. But there are domestic health resorts that are no less attractive and original. For example, Sochi and its resort areas.

Rest in Adler

Today Adler is a wonderful resort on the Black Sea. There are many hotels and resorts with various entertainments. That is why the tourists who arrived there are satisfied.

As the time for the Sochi Olympics was approaching, Adler received additional funding. The city was improved, new buildings were erected, and the amount of entertainment was added.

The area has a very good climate. Tourists who come to Adler spend their free time on the beach, but it is important to know what time of year they do.





The beach season there begins from the end of spring and lasts until mid-autumn. This city is not in vain called the Black Sea resort, but all because there are many beaches. But the most attractive and popular is Barracuda Beach. People of any age will have a good rest here and will always remember this wonderful place.

Central beach

The central beach of Adler is a small coastal area near the lighthouse. The coast itself is pebbled, and the bottom has a smooth decline. There are a lot of pluses: the railway is far away, but it is easy to get to the beach. Available rental deck chairs, umbrellas and so on. Barracuda Beach has the same advantage. In the summer, trampolines are installed for children on a certain territory of the central coast. But the free places are hard to find, because there is a lot of traffic, and many consider this a minus.





Beaches of Adler

"Gull"

Adler has the longest beach. It is called the "Seagull." It has a width of only 40 meters, but its length reaches almost a kilometer. The shore is semi-sandy and semi-pebble, well-groomed. "The Seagull" has its own peculiarity: sellers of freshly brewed coffee are located along the coast. This can only be found on this beach. Rescuers are on duty there regularly. In the water there is a site that is fenced specifically for children, circles can be rented. Also available is an amusement park.

"Twinkle"

The length of the beach "Spark" is in second place. Its width is 40 meters and its length is 800. Sochi Airport is located a few kilometers away . And on the other side of the beach there is a culture park and children's attractions. It is very reminiscent of "The Seagull", so the government wants to bring them together, and everyone is interested in what it will be called.





Lego

Tourists who come to Adler often look for less crowded beaches to enjoy the beauty of nature. Then this option will become a find. The beach got this name thanks to the constructor Lego due to its specific design. Next to it is a botanical garden. They mainly walk there, hiding from the hot sun.

Mandarin Beach

The Mandarin beach (Adler) does not belong to the resort, but the Mandarin entertainment center, and is considered the most expensive in the whole city. And in terms of purity, it is compared with Barracuda. But "Mandarin" also has a drawback: the water there is much colder than on other coasts of Adler. And when it rains, the Mzymta River brings in dirt (often this happens closer to spring, but not in summer).

Going to the city of Adler, you need to visit at least a few of these beaches. Because, having got there, you find yourself in a fairy tale, which you would definitely like to repeat.
