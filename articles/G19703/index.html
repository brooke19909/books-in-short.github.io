Maldives: Male Airport

The Republic of Maldives can be compared to a scattering of pearls in the Indian Ocean. Every year, tourists from all over the world come to this beautiful country to enjoy its beauties. The most vivid images come before your eyes with only one word - the Maldives. Male Airport, in turn, is the only "heavenly harbor" in the country that has international status.

Description

The airport is located on the small island of Hulule, at an altitude of only 2 meters above sea level. Not far from here (on another attole) is the capital of the Republic of Maldives - the city of Male. History

The history of the airport begins in 1960, when the first runway was laid. She was paved with nothing more than steel sheets. But after 4 years, the local government decided to replace the coating with asphalt. In 1966, a new strip of the island state of the Maldives was ready for operation. The airport received international status only after 15 years.



Flights

Today, 25 airlines operate scheduled or charter flights to Male Airport. The Maldives, as many know, is a welcome country for many tourists. Airplanes arrive here from 27 major cities, such as: Moscow, Berlin, London, Rome, Colombo, Amsterdam, Vienna, Delhi, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Bangkok. The largest foreign carrier to the Maldives is Sri Lanka Airlines. She makes more than 30 flights a week. Aeroflot Airlines from Sheremetyevo can deliver Russian tourists to Hulule Island. Transaero from Domodedovo and Vnukovo also offers seasonal routes.

Services

Male International Airport Terminal is a modern building. Here you can find duty free shops, mail, wireless Internet, exchange offices, children's playgrounds and even a free shower.

How to get from the airport to other islands

A country that has a huge number of small islands is, of course, the Maldives. The airport in this case is no exception, it is also like an island state. Hulule Island, where the airport is located, is small. Here is just one single hotel. Many tourists, after landing at Male Airport, continue on their way to another island. There are many options for getting to a particular resort:

  • Speedboats will quickly take you to the nearest islands.
  • Maldivian Air Taxis are small VIP-class seaplanes.
  • Flights to other airports in the country by Trans Maldivian Airways. There are two more airports, by the way, in the Republic, besides Male.
  • Traveling by ferry is the most economical option.

You can arrange a trip using one of these methods at the airport itself. For everyone who wants to visit the Maldives, Male Airport is always open!
