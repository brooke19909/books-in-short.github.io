Standard rooms

Andron (Hotel, Moscow) offers you a wide selection of diverse rooms. Let's start with the budget option. Double "standard" is characterized by the presence of a double bed, that is, perfect for couples. Cost - 1770 rubles per day per room. Breakfast will cost you 350 rubles. Interior decoration is restrained, but light shades visually give volume to the room and emphasize its perfect cleanliness, so it is quite comfortable here.
