Beira Mar Resort - Hotel in North Goa

Goa is more than one hundred kilometers of the beach area on the shores of the Indian Ocean. This smallest Indian state has a very developed tourist infrastructure: many bars, restaurants, night discos, diving and Ayurveda centers, and, of course, a wide selection of hotels of various levels.



All about Goa

This resort is divided into two parts: Northern and Southern. In the latter are relatively expensive hotels. Its exact opposite is North Goa. There are many cheap hotels in which advanced young people who come to the resort from Europe and America prefer to settle. Here tourists can relax with any income and various preferences.

The high season in Goa begins in October. It was at this time that the monsoons subsided and there were fewer waves on the ocean. In the period from October to April, vacationers rush here.

Goa is famous for its abundance of restaurants where you can taste excellent fish and fresh seafood. The resort is very popular beach parties to the special music - "techno". This type of entertainment here is called the "Goan trance."

The heat in the high season, unlike the low, is not so fierce and the climate is not so humid. From October to April Goa becomes a real paradise.
Many Russians come here to celebrate Christmas. After all, such an exotic beginning of the year gives exercise for all the next twelve months. It should be borne in mind that this period - from December 20th to January 15th - is considered the most expensive for a vacation in Goa. Prices are literally rising. However, at this time, Goa is crowded.



At least once in a lifetime, each person dreams of moving away from problems and fuss, to breathe in the sweet spirit of Indian exoticism, recharged with the powerful energy of the East. You can fulfill your desire by staying at the Beira Mar Resort Hotel (2 *). Goa is famous for its beaches. Among the most popular is Calangute (Calangute), located in the city of the same name.

Description

Beira Mar Resort is located in Bagi. This is a suburb of Calangute. Until July 2013, it was called differently - Beiramar Alfran Resort. Changing the owner, the hotel changed its name. Rhythmic music, disco dancing on the beach until morning, massage rooms offering a wide variety of services, a huge number of shops selling souvenirs and Indian spices that drive their aromas crazy ... This is the picture that surrounds Beira Mar Resort.

Fans of a beach holiday here will find everything you need for an unforgettable pastime. The coast of Calangute has an active nightlife. The resort center is a kilometer away. The nearest airport is forty minutes away.

Travel Tips

The Russians who are going to relax in Goa in the "economy" class are perfect for the Beira Mar Resort. The reviews of those who have already been here vary. Those who did not hope for exclusive living conditions liked the hotel. Although some complained about the slowness of the staff and some shortcomings in the room.
