Teply Stan, Solnechny pool: description, prices, reviews

Nowadays, people have increasingly begun to adhere to a healthy lifestyle and play sports. And as you know, one of its best types, which helps to strengthen health, has no contraindications and is suitable for people of all ages, is considered swimming. For this reason, in many cities of the country began to build various sports complexes and fitness clubs with pools.

This article will focus on the health complex, which is located in the Moscow region Teply Stan. The Solnechny pool, located on the territory of the complex, is a large bowl with five paths, where a variety of activities on the water are held, which help strengthen the immune system and carry a lot of benefits.

The Solnechny pool on the Teply Stan was commissioned about eight years ago as part of the Moscow government‚Äôs program for the construction of sports facilities. The building is located in the courtyards, and this fact allows citizens living in the district to go swimming in close proximity to the house.

Organization Description

Under the guidance of professional trainers in the pool conduct classes for children and adults on training in swimming, water aerobics, recreational swimming.

Competitions are regularly held in Solnechny, swimming lessons for the disabled are organized, groups are recruited into various sections.

The organization is popular among residents of the South-Western Administrative Okrug. The doors of the swimming pool are open to all fans of sports and outdoor activities.

Description of the pool in the Teply Stan area

The Solnechny pool has dimensions of 25 by 14 meters, depth - up to 1.8 meters. It has five swimming lanes. Water in the bowl is disinfected by the modern method (ozonation). It provides bactericidal and hygienic safety of water and saturates it with oxygen. Due to this, water procedures benefit and improve the general condition.





Services of the Solnechny complex (Teply Stan)

The Solnechny pool offers visitors several sections and programs:

  • swimming lessons for children from the age of six and adults;
  • water aerobics;
  • independent swimming;
  • water polo;
  • swimming for the disabled and schoolchildren from large families.

Additional services:

  • volleyball;
  • fitness exercises for people of retirement age;
  • gym;
  • group classes (yoga, aerobics, stretching);
  • dance directions;
  • martial arts;
  • beauty saloon.
