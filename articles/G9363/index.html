Klin hotels: services and facilities

Wedge - one of the most beautiful corners of the Moscow region. This small old city with a rich history invariably attracts tourists, because there is something to see: many old buildings that are adjacent to modern buildings, a number of interesting places and museums. Residents of the capital and large cities rush here to relax in soul and body in the lap of nature, in this region of hills, rivers and dense forests.





In order to get only pleasant impressions from your vacation, you need to thoroughly approach the issue of choosing a hotel. What can this wonderful city offer travelers? Klin hotels provide their guests with only comfortable conditions.

Hotel "Inspiration"





The hotel with the poetic name "Inspiration" welcomes its guests warmly . This is a great option for temporary residence for both tourists and businessmen. It is located in the village of Selinskoye, Klinsky district, in a beautiful place near the lake and forest, where you can fish and pick mushrooms. There are rooms for every taste, the most demanding: from standard to luxury, and prices are quite affordable. Each has its own bathroom, bathroom, as well as TV, air conditioning, refrigerator.

Living conditions and services

You can stay in one of the following rooms:

  • No. 1, No. 5 "Standard" (with two separate beds).
  • No. 2 "Standard Plus" (with one double bed).
  • No. 3 "Two-room Suite" (a bedroom with a king-size double bed, a living room with a sofa and a dining table with chairs).
  • No. 4 "Junior Suite" (with one double bed and sofa).
  • No. 6 "One-room Suite" (with a double bed, sofa, dining table with chairs).
  • No. 7 Quadruple (with four single beds, a dining corner).
  • No. 8 "One-room triple suite" (with one double bed, one single bed, sofa, dining table with chairs).

The hotel "Inspiration" (Klin) offers its guests to relax in a wonderful bath and sauna. The healing effect of water procedures is enhanced by jadeite, a stone with many healing properties. Swimmers will appreciate the man-made pool. You can relax and unwind after the bath in the relaxation room, which has a TV, an electric kettle, a microwave oven, and a refrigerator.

Hotel "Spectrum"





Hotels in Klin are represented by another wonderful place to stay. If you want to stay directly in the city, the Spektr Hotel is suitable. It offers comfortable rooms with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and an en suite bathroom.

All rooms have free internet. Also, guests can use the services of a 24-hour reception and free parking at the hotel. Hotel "Spectrum" Klin has its own restaurant with excellent service.

Hotel Aquamarine




Hotel "Aquamarine" in the city of Klin - a great combination of low prices and high quality service. Guests are offered a European accommodation system - accommodation in rooms designed for several people. The number of rooms includes 1-, 2-, 3- and 4-bed rooms, equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV. Depending on the type of room, it can be a 1-bedroom or 2-double bed. Luxurious rooms with 2 rooms and a jacuzzi will satisfy the highest demands of guests.

On the floor there is a dining room and laundry room, as well as showers and a bathroom. You can register at any time of the day or night. Free car parking is available on site for hotel guests.

Hotel "Aquamarine" (Klin) is an ideal choice for those who stay in the city for the purpose of a business visit, business trip or just to relax.

Hotel ViP - an ideal offer for a business trip





Small Klin hotels with a convenient location are perfect for a business trip. In the center of Klin, not far from the Ice Palace, is located the hotel "ViP". It has a favorable location: within walking distance from it are shops, a park and the banks of the Sestra River, from here it is not far from the bus and train station.

There are all conditions for successful business and an unforgettable vacation: comfortable rooms, consistently high quality service, attentive staff, proximity to the main objects of urban infrastructure. Not all Klin hotels (the Moscow Region is large, and there are many tourist establishments in it) can please their guests with such a convenient location.

It offers modern rooms of the Standard and Suite categories, each of which must have air conditioning, a TV and a refrigerator. The price includes free internet access.

Cheap holidays with children

Hotel ViP is a good choice for families with children. With them you can take a walk to a nearby amusement park, as well as to the river bank, which has a boat station.

Hotel ViP is ready to offer all this at reasonable prices - one of the lowest in the city. Klin hotels are not only comfortable, but also cheap.

Hotel "Clover"





The comfortable Clover Spa Hotel in Klin offers its guests a wealth of diverse leisure and relaxation opportunities. All rooms include a TV and free Wi-Fi. Bathrobes and slippers are provided. Rooms are cleaned every day.

Some Klin hotels offer their guests a full range of services for a good rest. One such place is the Clover Spa. There are many different procedures to maintain health and beauty: a hot tub, jacuzzi, massage, Turkish bath.

Services

The spa has a garden and terrace where you can enjoy nature.
Other activities available include horse riding in the surrounding area, and walking around the city. In the evening, a specially organized program will not let you get bored. The hotel also provides tour services. There is a kids club for children at the hotel, animators work. Baby sitting services are available . Pets are allowed without payment, but by prior arrangement.

For guests' services - a 24-hour front desk, laundry, as well as free parking (subject to reservation). Free Wi-Fi internet access is available anywhere in the hotel.

The hotel has restaurants serving a varied menu, as well as a breakfast buffet. In addition, a dietary menu is available upon request. You can have a good time and a snack in the bar located on the hotel premises.

For the convenience of holidaymakers, express check-out is available here, concierge services, ticket sales, and car rentals are offered. An airport shuttle is available at an extra charge. The hotel can host both business events and weddings and banquets. There is a room for honeymooners.

Beautiful rooms with modern amenities, impeccable service, delicious restaurant cuisine, the charm of a quiet city - all this will appeal to both those who wish to have a good rest, as well as those who find themselves here on business matters.
