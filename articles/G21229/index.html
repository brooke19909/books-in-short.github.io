Summer garden. How to get and how to find it in St. Petersburg

St. Petersburg is a tourist city. At any time of the year you can meet thousands of people from different parts of the world. All of them come here to feel the indescribable atmosphere, to see the famous treasures of the architecture and culture of this city. His story is interesting and unusual. Erected in the middle of a swamp at the behest of one person, the city throughout its entire existence is famous throughout the world and is one of the most visited places among tourists from all over the world.

Excursions and independent walks

Undoubtedly, every resident and guest of the city will surely fall into the Summer Garden. How to get to this popular place? Tourists and residents can take one of the sightseeing tours, which cover all the main attractions. It is very convenient and informative. During the trip, an experienced guide will tell about the history and interesting facts about the places that can be seen outside the windows of the bus.



However, not only from the windows of the bus is it worth visiting the Summer Garden. How to get there during the tour, we examined. For independent travelers, there are other options. The Summer Garden is in close proximity to the Nevsky Prospekt and Gostiny Dvor metro stations. Walking is a good way to get into the Summer Garden. How to get to the place from the metro? The easiest way is from Nevsky Prospekt to go up towards the Neva, along the Fontanka embankment or the Griboedov Canal. In the first case, the Summer Garden is impossible to miss - it will begin on the left side of the river. In the second option, you should go to Manezhnaya Square and turn right - on the Field of Mars. After it begins the Summer Garden.



Walking is not the only way to visit the Summer Garden. How to get to it from the Neva? Already from the shores of Hare Island you can see the openwork lattice and lush greenery on the opposite side. You can go there along the Trinity Bridge, while admiring a beautiful view of the Neva Delta.

What is the Summer Garden so famous for? This green oasis in the very center of the city has always been attractive to tourists and locals. The romantic mood of shady alleys, stunning sculptures made of white marble - all this can not fail to amaze at the first meeting. Many famous paintings, postcards and literary works contain references to the Summer Garden.



On his territory he collected a large number of sculptural compositions, beautiful fountains, premises of the Bird's yard, a natural reservoir in which white swans swim.

Opening hours of the Summer Garden

Visitors of all ages enjoy free admission to the Summer Garden. The official site helps to get acquainted with official events and events held on its territory. Summer is undoubtedly the best place to visit the Summer Garden. Opening hours (2013) depend on the season. From May 1 to September 30, visitors can walk around the territory from 10 am to 10 pm. In the autumn-winter period from October 1 to March 31 - from 10 am to 8 pm. In April of each year, the Summer Garden is closed for drying. Keep this in mind when planning your trips.
