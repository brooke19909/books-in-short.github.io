Washing machine Indesit IWSC 5105: description, specifications, reviews

Modern washing machines are quite relevant. As a rule, every housewife tries to acquire such a miracle technique to facilitate her work. Thanks to this device, you can allocate time for rest or for other worries, and there are always plenty of them.

It is worth thinking about budget options, which have a very impressive number of functions and options, and in quality they are in no way inferior to more expensive options. One of such models can be called Indesit IWSC 5105 Cis. A lot of people like her. In their reviews, they note many advantages that completely cover its shortcomings.

The machine belongs to class A, which indicates its efficient washing, low power consumption, high safety and reliability, as well as excellent performance of additional functions. She deserves special attention, which is why it is worthwhile to look at her nevertheless.



general characteristics

As mentioned above, the Indesit IWSC 5105 model is cheap, therefore it is available even to low-income families, especially if you take it on credit. It is equipped with good and powerful options that will appeal to even the most capricious customers. If you believe the claimed characteristics from the manufacturer, the washing class is the most effective. The linen will sparkle with its cleanliness and smell pleasantly of powder, which, by the way, is completely rinsed out in one wash cycle.

Now you should consider the standard features. The machine allows you to load up to 5 kg of dry laundry. The maximum spin speed is 1 thousand revolutions per minute. Unlike many other budgetary washing machines, Indesit IWSC 5105 has the option to select the washing temperature and the required spin. If we talk about the modes, we can highlight the fact that many necessary options are available. There is also an express wash. In this mode, the washing machine copes with slightly contaminated things in just 15 minutes. As in other devices, in this technique there is not the slightest information about programs near the selection knob. Only numbers and small images are printed there. More detailed information can be found in the instructions or on the tray where detergents are poured and poured. The control panel has small knobs that allow you to set the desired temperature and spin speed.

If we consider the main advantages of Indesit IWSC 5105, then it is necessary to note the increased level of water used during the rinse. This advantage will give a good mood to allergy sufferers and young mothers who will not suffer from the powder left in things. The number of minuses is minimal. In principle, customers often complain about high noise levels. But this drawback is fully compensated by the pros and price category.

Indesit IWSC 5105 will be the best washing machine for a family of 3-4 people with a small budget.



Specifications

This model of the washing machine has a front load of laundry. The drying function is not provided by the manufacturer. The device can be installed in any room, since it is a freestanding type. Office electronic view. If the washing class Indesit IWSC 5105, reviews of which constantly talk about it, is the most effective (that is, A), then the spin cycle is not very (C). Leakage protection is not fully installed, so be careful. The model is available only in white. The dimensions of the washing machine are small, namely 600x450x850 mm, which allows it to be attributed to narrow appliances. For more convenient installation there is a removable cover. In an hour , the Indesit IWSC 5105 washing machine consumes up to 1 kW, water - 43 liters. It is worth taking a look at this model at least for its small power consumption.



Special washing programs

"Express" (number 10) - a mode that allows you to wash slightly soiled things. It will take only 15 minutes. When loading, you need to check the weight of dry laundry, it should not exceed 1.5 kg. You can wash clothes from any type of fabric, with the exception of delicate ones.

The Sport program (number 11) is designed for uniforms, shoes, etc. You can load no more than 2.5 kg per cycle. Washing lasts no more than 80 minutes, subject to all proportions. You can read more about them in the instructions. The washing machine (narrow) Indesit IWSC 5105 in the kit also has a booklet on operation, where the main points are described in detail, and most importantly, affordable.

Intensive mode for sports items (number 12) is suitable for washing laundry that is slightly dirty. The manufacturer recommends loading no more than 2.5 kg for a more efficient washing. The whole cycle lasts about 70 minutes.

The mode "Sports shoes" (number 13) carries out the entire washing process in 50 minutes. You can load no more than 2 pairs.



Advantages

Many people like a washing machine, which is very easy to learn to use. For some reason, some consumers did not expect to find the washing function of sports shoes, so they were pleasantly surprised. At the same time, after carrying out a full cycle at the output, this garment is incredibly clean, you will not achieve such a result with a manual process. Among the advantages, people also note the capacity of the Indesit IWSC 5105. The instructions are understandable, and it is easy to figure out how to work with the device. Some consumers talk about its usefulness at night. You can confidently leave it to be washed while the owners are sleeping. Even the fact that it is a bit noisy can not break the dream anyway.



disadvantages

The main negative aspects are:

  • lack of drying;
  • lack of display;
  • during the spin cycle, the noise level rises, but you can get used to it;
  • the powder cake must be washed periodically, but this is inconvenient;
  • during the spin cycle, in addition to noise, you can hear a small whistle, which is inherent in almost every Indesit washing machine;
  • spin in some programs is too powerful;
  • sometimes there are problems with heating the water to the required temperature - some consumers complain that the temperature does not reach the desired level.

Installation process

During installation of the washing machine, it must be unpacked and properly connected. This process is described in more detail in the instructions, but here you can read small installation recommendations.

After the shipping box is removed, it is necessary to check for defects, and then remove the screws, plugs with gaskets. You can find these items on the back of the device. The manufacturer recommends that you save all the details so that you can use them later for the necessary transportation (which will happen anyway).

The washing machine should only be installed on a flat floor, where it will certainly not stagger and move when washing. If the floor is covered with a carpet, it is necessary to leave a place for ventilation, without which the device will break.



Indicators

The Indesit IWSC 5105 Cis washing machine has several indicators. They are necessary so that the user is aware of some important events. For example, one of the indicators will light if the owner (hostess) has activated the delay timer. After the required amount of time has passed, the light will go out and the wash will begin.

There is also a built-in indicator, the operation of which indicates the blocking of the loading hatch. When the door is open, the light goes out. This will take about 3 minutes.

The manufacturer took care of the indicator, which reports that the execution of a certain function when you turn on any mode is impossible.

The main indicators are bulbs that light up in accordance with the selected washing mode. You can learn more about them by reading the instructions.

Personal settings

It is possible to change the washing temperature (from hot to cold). In this case, the user will not be able to set the degree more than that provided for in the program of washing itself.

In order to adjust the spin, you must use a certain knob. You can also turn it off completely if you wish. As in the temperature mode, the spin cannot be set more powerful than it is provided for in the washing program. In the silk mode, for example, this function is not available, as it is not provided by the software itself.



Additional functions

Among the additional features include:

  • Eco Time. This option should be used when washing cotton and cotton products. It will help to wash clothes with less load, electricity and water consumption.
  • Whitening. To implement this mode, you must additionally pour detergent into a special compartment. It will help remove even stubborn stains.
  • Extra rinse. This mode guarantees complete cleaning of powder or other detergent.
  • Snooze timer
