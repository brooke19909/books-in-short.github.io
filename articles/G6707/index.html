Hotel Coral Island Resort 3 *, Phuket: reviews, photos

In Thailand, many tourists go for exotic. And they are absolutely right. Here you can stroll through the real tropical jungle, personally gaze at the animals and birds that are in Russia only in the zoo, soak up the white sand under the shade of palm trees. Thanks to all this, tours to Phuket, to the Coral Island Resort 3 * in particular, are now in great demand. This hotel is notable for being on a small exotic island, and in the singular, which is very attractive in itself. Add to its advantages the friendliness and helpfulness of the staff, unusually tasty food, rooms in modern comfortable bungalows, but under reed roofs. Many tourists who have been here call this place paradise. But, unfortunately, he also has drawbacks that everyone who decides to buy a tour here needs to know about. This is described in detail in our article.

Location

In the western part of Thailand, just 410 meters from the mainland lies a beautiful green island and at the same time the province of Phuket. Coral Island Resort 3 * - a hotel located on another island called Koh He, or in Russian Coral. Administratively, it enters the aforementioned province, and even from the largest island of the country it is separated by a strip of water only 9 km wide. Therefore, many tour operators offer permits to Thailand, to the Coral Island Resort 3 * in Phuket. At the same time, they omit the name Ko Hye. Briefly tell about this wonderful corner, so that tourists have a concept where they have to relax.





This piece of land formed by coral reefs (hence the name) is very small. Theoretically, it can be bypassed in a day. However, in practice this is difficult to do, since most of the island is covered with impenetrable tropical jungle, in which not the most harmless fauna lives. In some places, the forest approaches directly to the water, but along the coast there are picturesque wide strips with fantastic white sand, on one of which our hotel was built. Thanks to its location, its guests are waiting for an unforgettable beach holiday and almost complete privacy.

How to get there

In relation to the Coral Island Resort 3 * hotel in Phuket (meaning the province), it is difficult to say how conveniently it is located. Theoretically, getting to Coral Island is easy. From Russia you need to fly by plane to the airport of Phuket. It takes 7.5 hours to fly from Moscow. At the airport, tourists are waiting for transfers of travel agencies where tickets were purchased. Buses go to Phuket Port, located 32 km from the airport. According to tourists, this distance is covered in 45-50 minutes. In the port you need to catch a boat, which takes a quarter of an hour to the pier near the hotel. Those traveling here bypassing travel agencies need to follow the same route - from the airport by taxi to the seaport, and then by boat to the Coral Island.

However, in practice, things can look more complicated. The fact is that boats leave for Koh He strictly by the hour, namely at 9-00 in the morning and at 15-00 in the afternoon. If the transfer by this time did not manage to deliver its passengers to the port, they will have to spend the night in Phuket for an additional fee.

Description of the hotel and its territory

Coral Island Resort 3 * was built and opened in 1990, but in 2014 there was a total reconstruction. So now the hotel is shining with novelty, and everything is functioning perfectly. Its territory covers 24 thousand square meters. It has six rows of 64 exotic-looking bungalows, a swimming pool, a restaurant with a very spacious open veranda and a roof, which are crowned palm trees, a massage parlor, a small shop with a variety of beach products and volleyball courts. The first row of bungalows lined up almost at the water's edge. Here is the most expensive accommodation, but tourists vacationing at Coral Island Resort 3 * do not leave the most flattering reviews about accommodation on the first line. The disadvantages are the constant noise from people on the beach and the flooding of houses during strong tides.



On the second line, located just a few meters from the first, there are no such surprises. On the third, living is also very comfortable, and from the windows of the rooms you can admire the endless sea. From the bungalow on the remaining lines, only the territory and green spaces are visible, and the sixth line is located almost at the jungle border. It is noteworthy that in the hotel, despite being so close to the forest, mini-gardens are equipped with the same tropical palm trees and oleanders. For the eyes it is very nice.

The reception is located in the main building of the hotel. Its employees speak their native Thai and English, only a few people speak Russian here. At the reception you can use a safe, a left-luggage office, hand over personal items to laundry, make an appointment for a massage and pay for it, and solve current issues. The hotel lobby is decorated in Thai style, everything is very beautiful and dignified. Wi-Fi is available in the lobby around the clock; a code is issued for free.

Services for children

Coral Island Resort 3 * in Phuket is happy for its young guests, but there is no wide service for them here. Parents can book a crib in the room. In the restaurant, among the abundance of products for babies, there is always something to choose. On the territory of the hotel in the pool there is a children's department with a depth of 50 cm. Here, perhaps, are all the services provided by the hotel to the kids. The important thing is that there is no doctor on the island. If something happens to the child, you will have to sail to Phuket. Therefore, parents traveling with their children to the Coral Island Resort should take medicines from Russia with them, which, in their opinion, may be needed.

Sea and climate

The island of Ko Hye lies in the Andaman Sea in a tropical zone with a predominance of seasonal monsoons. It is always summer here, always warm and humid, but heavy rains fall only from the second half of May to October. In November, the high season begins in Phuket, because sunny and almost cloudless weather sets in, although high humidity remains. The temperature of sea water in this region is always high - up to 26-28 degrees heat. Therefore, you can swim in any season.

As noted above, on the coast itself, you can say, right on the beach are the bungalows of the Coral Island Resort 3 * hotel in Phuket. The photo captures only a small piece of this fabulous corner. According to the reviews of absolutely all guests, the beach here is magnificent. Its coating is large, very light, almost white sand. Entering the water is ideal for children - shallow, the depth is far away, there are no stones or sharp corals. In the water, the place where it is allowed to swim is surrounded by buoys, and in order to avoid any unpleasant situations, lifeguards are on duty at the beach.





Here you can simply sunbathe, enjoying the whisper of the waves and the rustle of palm twigs, or you can spend your leisure time more actively, for example, ride a ‚Äúbanana‚Äù or a catamaran, dive with a mask or scuba diving to watch the inhabitants of the coral reefs, play volleyball. Additional Information

Not all categories of vacationers fit Coral Island Resort 3 * in Phuket. Thailand is an unusually original and interesting country, having arrived in which, I want to see so many things. However, from the Coral Island, although it is located very close to the coast, it is difficult, and in bad weather it is impossible to get to the mainland. Therefore, excursions from here are difficult to get. In addition, in order to avoid misunderstandings, hotel guests are offered to leave it the day before their flight by plane, cross to Phuket and spend the night in another hotel there. Money in a room in such cases is not taken, but still for many tourists this becomes an unpleasant surprise.

The second feature of relaxation on the Coral Island is also its isolation. It affects the assortment and prices of goods in stores and restaurants. According to tourists, everything here is twice as expensive as in Phuket. Despite these nuances, many people prefer to stay in this hotel, and more than once. Regular customers are given a bottle of champagne and fruit on their arrival day. Compliments are also provided for newlyweds and birthday people.
