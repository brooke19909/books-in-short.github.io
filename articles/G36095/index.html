Where is Syktyvkar: information about the city

Where Syktyvkar is located, I want to know everyone who first hears this unusual and intriguing name. Translated from the Komi language, the name means "city by the Sysola River."

Vedomosti about the city

Finding out where Syktyvkar is located, we really see that a river flows nearby. The city itself has been standing here since the year 1586. However, the new name began to be used only in 1930. Before that, one could only hear about Ust-Sysolsk.





The forest surrounds Syktyvkar from all sides. Where this place is located, it will become clear if on the map you find a settlement between the Vycherga and Sysola rivers. It is the capital of Komi. To understand where Syktyvkar is located, it is worth looking at the north-west of the Russian Federation. From Moscow you need to drive 1300 km. The area where Syktyvkar is located is 152 square kilometers. The largest part falls to land, 5% of the territory has passed to ponds and wetlands.

Resource base

Studying the question of where the city of Syktyvkar is located, we see that forests prevail around, due to which the wood industry is very well developed. 72% of urban land is covered with dense tree thickets. Here you can find many minerals, including mineral and ore. This is peat, and valuable iron ore, and useful oil shales, and stone materials. If you go down the city, you can find sources of fresh as well as healing mineral water.

A lot of valuable resources are located where Syktyvkar is located . What area is still able to satisfy the need for valuable minerals for other Russian lands, if not the Komi Republic?

The prevalence of temperate continental climate is noticeable here, since this place is located in the north-west of the country. It is not hard to live here, since winters are very severe, and summer, unfortunately, is short. In the area where Syktyvkar is located, winter temperatures can sometimes drop to minus 40 degrees. Summer also does not pamper the inhabitants with heat. The maximum is only 17 degrees of heat.





Fauna

It features a large number of species of wildlife Syktyvkar. Where this city is located, you can understand if you pay attention to the dense forests of Komi. 7 units of 40 species can be met by you during a walk. The smallest of them is a tiny shrew. The weight of the animal is no more than 2 grams. At the same time, the largest beast reaches a body weight of 350 kg - this is an elk.

In addition, you can meet a brown bear, a fanged wild boar, a dangerous wolverine and a gray wolf. There are also a lot of hares, cute squirrels, nimble foxes, agile hens and other residents of the local surroundings. The burs are full of deer. Also, work is underway to create good conditions for the reproduction and life of beavers, which were started in 1938.





Demographic situation

Of course, not only animals inhabit this land, but also people of 235 thousand in number. About 60% of them are Russians. There are also representatives of the Komi people, whose share is 30%. In addition, Ukrainians who came from Belarus and Germany found a home for themselves here. In total, approximately 70 nationalities can be counted.

People belonging to a particular nationality unite in their societies. As for demography, it can be characterized as having good trends. The population has been growing in recent years.

Since this is a large industrial center of the north of Russia, wood, timber, and pulp and paper products are actively processed here. 40 enterprises are engaged in the creation of non-woven material. There is a combine, a distillery, an airline company. Flights are carried out between those areas of Komi, where it is difficult to get, as well as some enterprises.

Wealth of the area

There are ten areas of industrial development. Most (about 60%) is forestry, woodworking, and pulp and paper products. Agriculture is not a strong point of the region and its share is 2%. The work of two state farms and seven farms engaged in farming activities.





Local people travel by public transport. For the most part, these are buses and minibuses. You can order a taxi. Some areas are planned to be improved by the presence of trolleybus lines, but so far this is only in the project.

If we talk about external transport, then most of them are railways. From the city station you can reach the Mikun station. You can also use the services of the Chovyu node, as well as the Ezhva and Koity stations, which are located outside the city.

Air transport delivers from the local airport. By direct flight you can fly to different parts of the country, as well as Bulgaria, Ukraine, Spain and Belarus. Nearby there is the town of Sokolovka, in which a new airport is being built.

Infrastructure

By car, you can also drive very comfortably. There are three roads of republican significance, and a bus station from where you can get to Kirov, Cheboksary, Perm and other cities. A full life can be provided by a developed infrastructure, which includes 94 kindergartens for children, 47 school institutions, 4 higher education institutions and 10 middle levels. Active pastime is also well developed, thanks to the Central Stadium of republican significance and the swimming pool, which is the largest in Komi. There are 11 sports schools for children and adolescents.





This is a well-developed and comfortable city for life, bringing many benefits to other areas of the state.
