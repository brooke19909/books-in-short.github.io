The best hostel in Almaty

In the article we will consider the best hostels in Almaty. Choosing the right hotel for yourself will not be difficult. The main thing is to consider in advance all the desired options, as well as pay attention to your budget. The article describes those institutions that can be considered the best. They ideally manifest themselves in a combination of price and quality, in addition, they are comfortable and clean.

Asia

The average room rate is 600 rubles. Address: 44 Dostyk Avenue.

Asia Hostel in Almaty is located just 15 km from the sports complex. Nearby are also: ski resort, National Academy of Sciences. The administrator of the institution can provide any tourist information. On-site parking is available, it is free for guests. The rooms have safes and bed linen.

There is a shared kitchen. Pets are not allowed in the hostel in Almaty. Internet is free for guests. Rooms are cleaned every day. There is a shared lounge with a TV. Laundry open. The reception desk is open all the time. There are special lockers for storing things. No smoking on site. There are rooms for families. All rooms have air conditioning.

Reviews about this hotel are rather neutral. The main advantage is that the rooms are cleaned and the hostel is conveniently located. However, many notice that the administration can be rude. If a person makes a reservation through a special website, it is better to phone the hotel additionally. Otherwise, there is a risk that you will have to wait until any number is released. It is noisy.

Amigo

The average cost of a room per day is 450 rubles. Address: Manas Street 9.

The hostel has free internet. Free parking is available for motorists. A shuttle service is available at a surcharge.

There is a shared kitchen. Amigo Hostel (Almaty) has heating; additionally installed air conditioners. Vending machines with food are installed. Luggage storage facilities are available. Smoking is not allowed on site. For this, special places are equipped. There is a laundry room. Rooms are cleaned every day. There is a shared lounge with a TV. A shop is located on site. The staff speaks Russian and English.

The hotel reviews report that the rooms have everything you need, appliances and internet are working.

Om

Room rates per day start at 200 rubles. Many people like this hostel in Almaty. Address: Ormanova street, 15a.

The hotel has air conditioning. Equipped parking. From the windows you can see a beautiful garden and mountains. Each room has a private bathroom. Depending on the cost, it will either have a bathtub or a shower. The entire hostel has internet access. The rooms have a TV. There is a shared kitchen for cooking.

For fans of reading, a library is open. There is a playground, but it is located on the street. For those who smoke, a specially equipped area. Rooms are cleaned every day. There is a laundry room. Administration at any time of the day can resolve the issues.

In the reviews, many notice that the price / quality ratio is optimal. The hostel has a convenient location, it is well served, and all rooms are clean.

Baza

The cost of living starts from 350 rubles. Address: Kaldayakova street, house 17.

Many consider this hostel the best in Almaty. It has a convenient parking lot where you can park for free, as well as fast Internet. Bike rental is available. For an additional fee, staff will meet or accompany guests to the airport. The hostel has common rooms, which are divided into female and male. If you want to live separately, you can rent a tent with a single bed. There are no restaurants near the hostel, but there is a shared kitchen. You can cook your own food on it. If you do not want to, you can order a breakfast or lunch in your room, and coffee is also served.

Barbecue facilities are available for rent. Near the hotel there is a ski resort. Here you can take all the necessary equipment for a small fee. The hotel can store skis. In addition, a ski pass is for sale. The hotel has heating, safes are installed. There is a gift shop. The bar is open. There is karaoke and a library. For children, a playroom is equipped, as well as cartoons, books. Nearby is a night club; You can play board games or darts.

In the reviews, guests describe the fact that in front of the hostel there is a market where you can buy well. The kitchen is fully equipped, there is everything necessary for cooking. The common rooms are comfortable. The hostel itself is spacious and bright.

Freedom

The minimum cost for a room in this hostel in Almaty is 250 rubles. Address: Begalina street, 126.

This hostel can also be called one of the best in the city. There is a picnic area, from the windows you can see pleasant landscapes, there is free internet, parking. You can also come with pets. The rooms are comfortable. The atmosphere is great. The rooms are made in an interesting style. There is a seating area where you can relax in a large company. There is no restaurant, but the administration took care of this. Equipped shared kitchen. There are all accessories and surfaces. Products can be purchased independently or ordered in advance from the administrator. This hostel in Almaty is located next to the mountain, which pleases landscape lovers. There are other attractions nearby. Also relatively nearby is the airport.

The hotel has a tour desk, there are rooms for families, as well as those who do not smoke. For fans of the smoke made a special zone. Nearby there is a place where you can change the currency to local. The administrator works around the clock. A children's room is equipped for children. All rooms have heating. If you wish, you can wash your things in the laundry room. There is an evening program.

The reviews write that some rooms are cold. The rest of the guests have a pleasant experience with the hostel. The cost is low, the administration is friendly, the rooms have comfortable furniture.

Dos

Another good hostel in Almaty. Cost - from 270 rubles per day. Address: 195 Yassaui Street.

The hotel has all the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay. There is internet access and parking. Reception is open 24 hours a day. There are dormitory rooms as well as private rooms with a private bathroom. All rooms are made in one common style. There are bright details that allow you to make the overall atmosphere in the hotel as pleasant as possible. There is no restaurant in the hostel, but there is a shared kitchen. She also matches the overall style.

In the reviews about the hostel they write that the staff is pleasant, pays attention to each client. The rooms have everything you need from technology to hygiene products.
