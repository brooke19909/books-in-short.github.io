How to stick a film on a tablet. Advice

Probably each of us, having acquired a brand new tablet, would have thought about how to more reliably protect it from scratches, moisture and shock. After all, you will constantly use it, take it with you to work, study, etc. During the operation, it can significantly fray. Of course, a mandatory attribute will be a cover and a protective film. With the case, everything is clear - select the size, and then put it on your gadget and that's it! But with how to stick the film on the tablet, many may have difficulties. It must be sized, carefully cut, etc. Most do not know how to do this or simply do not want to! But you can learn everything. That is why we want to tell you about how to stick the film on the tablet yourself.





How to choose

On the shelves of electronic equipment stores you can see a large number of protective films. They will differ in price, size and quality. The coating can be different in structure - glossy or matte. Both are good in their protective qualities, but we would prefer a matte film, because there are no fingerprints on it. But the gloss will periodically need to be wiped, because after work it will leave traces of fat on it.

How to stick a film on a tablet

  1. Wash your hands.
  2. Open the packaging where the protective film is located . Along with it, a special microfiber cloth is usually included. So, wipe the tablet screen thoroughly with this napkin.
  3. If the protective film is not in size, but slightly larger, cut off the excess with a clerical knife or blade.
  4. Now you need to get rid of the transport layer. To do this, pull the tab with the number "1" and immediately the side of the film that is released, lean against the top of the screen and slowly paste it. So, gradually tearing off the transport layer, we reach the end.
  5. Smooth the film thoroughly and use a plastic card to expel all the bubbles, if any.
  6. Remove the upper transport layer by pulling on the tab with the number "2".

Well, now you know how to stick the film on the tablet.

Useful Tips

  • It is better to stick the protective film in the bathroom, because there is increased humidity, and, accordingly, dust has no way to get onto the tablet.
  • If you do not have a good napkin that does not leave fibers on the surface of the screen - do not worry! All that remains of it can be removed with adhesive tape. He will quickly collect all the villi from the surface of the display.
  • If the dust accidentally gets on the film, then you can get rid of it with ordinary running water. To do this, rinse it under a tap and then blow dry. Do not worry! The sticky surface will not be affected.

Conclusion

As you can see, if you follow all the rules of how to stick a film on a tablet, everything can be done quickly and efficiently. But if you miss at least something, then all efforts will be in vain - you can spend a lot of time, nerves and money! Therefore, if you are not confident in your abilities, ask your friends where to stick the film on the tablet. Usually this service is provided in almost all stores where such equipment is on sale, but keep in mind that you will have to pay for the service.
