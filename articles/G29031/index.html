Why do I need to make a book with my own hands?

This method of recording their thoughts, feelings has been used by people since ancient times. In the lines of the diary you can easily pour out all your emotions and desires, without experiencing discomfort, condemnation, envy, which can occur during a normal conversation with a friend or acquaintance. And over time, you can easily recall events from the past by re-reading them in your notes. Also, many use this type of presentation of thoughts to assess a particular life situation, its analysis. In order for the diary to be pleasant to use, and it always caused you only a positive mood, you need to know how to make a personal diary with your own hands. Therefore, it will be more valuable to the owner.



How to make a diary with your own hands

First you need to purchase a general notebook or diary, as you wish. If you do not want your notebook to have the usual size of a notebook, you can cut it along the contour from three or two sides to shorten it in length and width. After that, you definitely need to take up the cover, as this is practically the most difficult question on how to make a diary with your own hands. You can make a separate cover of the same size or attach an existing material to an existing one. This can be a variety of clippings from magazines, just beautiful pictures, rags of cloth, such as linen or wool, with sewn beads or glued figures made of felt.

A great idea is to cover the cover with small and thin wood fibers. It is important that the appearance of your diary be individual. It is with such a book that you will be pleased to share your thoughts. The outside of the diary is your inner world.

Page Design

How to make a diary with your own hands, already figured out, now you can do the decoration of its internal pages. You can use various beautiful stickers, sparkles. Small photos or inserts from magazine pages will perfectly complement your entries. If you like clarity, planning, you can put down dates for each day, decorating them with an unusual ornament, mark important events, such as birthdays, with small notes on the margins of certain days or at the bottom of the pages as notes.



Use whatever you find

Do not be afraid to experiment, because you are creating something for yourself. Use everything you have at hand: ribbons, beads, pins, colored skin. Create your own unique world in a small diary, then it will be a pleasure to use it.
