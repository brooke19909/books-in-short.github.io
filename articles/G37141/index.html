New in retro style. Mirrorless Samsung NX3000

The Samsung NX3000 mirrorless camera was presented to the public in the summer of 2014 and became the oldest in the line of half-frame mirrorless Samsung NX. If you compare this camera with the previous version of the NX210, then the developers have added a new processor, sensor and other not so significant changes. What is this novelty capable of? Let's consider in more detail in our article.

Samsung NX3000 Review

The shape of the case is traditional for mirrorless cameras. This is a standard box, but on the right there is a small tide for convenience. The camera is sturdy and light. The device itself is made of polycarbonate, glued with leatherette and made in retro style. The following colors are available: white, silver, black and brown. The small size (122x63x40 mm) and weight of about 290 grams make the Samsung NX3000 an indispensable camera. Reviews about her can only be heard positive.





There is no built-in flash on the camera, but an external flash is included. For her (or for the GPS module) there is a hot shoe mount. There are USB and HDMI connectors, it is possible to mount a tripod. If we compare this model with its predecessor Samsung NX210, then the buttons responsible for control have slightly changed - some shifted to the right, others to the left. In general, management is convenient, although there is one drawback. If the photographer has big hands and holds the camera firmly, then it is likely that he can press several buttons with his thumb.

The main thing that has been added is a large sliding display. There is an opportunity to turn it up and down, he stands very little for the dimensions of the camera. A reliable metal mount has been created that protects it from damage. Such a display is indispensable when the photographer wants to take a top view, that is, high. Or, conversely, low, for example, a creeping ant.

There is the possibility of a 180 ¬∞ turn and self-portrait shooting. And if you turned the display and winked at him, then after 2 seconds your picture is ready! The dimensions of the display are 3.3 inches diagonal, the resolution is 20.3 megapixels (720 by 640 pixels), according to technology - this is AMOLED touch. The touch screen is certainly a very convenient thing. It is possible to quickly configure the device, the browser even has an on-screen keyboard. But there are also disadvantages. In bright sunlight, nothing can be seen.





High resolution CMOS sensor , excellent detail, stunning color reproduction, low noise level allows you to take pictures with high quality. The wide ISO sensitivity range from 100 to 25600 makes it possible to shoot in low light and with minimal blur both on the street and indoors.

The camera has a high shutter speed (1/4000 second), so every photographer can easily take pictures of different moments when you need to respond quickly and you can‚Äôt hesitate. The shooting speed - 5 frames per second - certainly deserves praise. Even novice photographers who pick up the Samsung NX3000 can take great photos.

Optics and video

Samsung has released for its NX3000 Kit with a dozen lenses from 12 to 200 mm. Most of them are equipped with an OIS image stabilizer .





Beautiful Full HD video at 30 frames per second with a stereo microphone shoots the Samsung NX3000 Kit. User reviews indicate that the video is obtained with an excellent study of small details. And the H.264 format will help record clips in MPEG4 format.

Additional functions

Those who actively use the smartphone will like the functions of Home Monitor +, Remote Viewfinder Pro, Tag & Go, which allow you to transfer the captured photo material to compatible devices. It is possible to instruct the NX3000 camera to monitor children and send messages to the parents' phone if the baby woke up or cried.

Through NFC and Wi-Fi, you can share your favorite pictures. MobileLink helps send entire albums.

Equipment





When buying a Samsung NX3000, you get a lens with an external flash, a charger, a strap, all the necessary documents, a battery, a microUSB cable, as well as a disc with a licensed program for the latest version of Adobe Lightroom.

A feature of the built-in portable PZ-lens 20-50 mm with the i-Function system is that it is possible to adjust white balance, shutter speed, aperture, photosensitivity, exposure with a single button. You can charge the camera via the USB port both from a computer and from the network.

The Samsung NX3000 camera is a beautiful style, excellent features, low cost, high-quality photos and videos. In a word, all that is necessary for the modern user.
