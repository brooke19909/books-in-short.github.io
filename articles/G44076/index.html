Podbornoe lake: a brief description

In the Uvelsky district near the village of Khomutinino there is a lake unique in every respect.

The pond is small in size, with a total area of ‚Äã‚Äã1.25 square meters. km The greatest depth is 3 meters. Visibility is poor, not more than 1 m.

Lake Podbornoe, Chelyabinsk region, is classified as a hydrological monument. The pond is located in a forest dominated by pine and birch.

It is believed that the lake got its name because of the nearest huge forests, that is, it is located under the forest. There is also a theory that the lake and other nearby natural bodies of water are remnants of a dried up ancient sea or ocean that disappeared from the face of the earth several million years ago. And in its place there were only small lakes, but with a unique chemical composition and healing properties.

The water supply is due to underground sources and rainfall. There are no islands on the lake. Sandy shores with an admixture of clay.

The water in the lake is salty, and the banks are gentle, so you can come here calmly with children and those who can not swim.

Podbornoe Lake in the Chelyabinsk region is not of interest to fishermen, in order to fish, you will have to drive to a nearby reservoir called Krugloye.

Rest and treatment

There are many recreation centers on the shore of Lake Podbornoe, there is even a sanatorium. You can come here and settle in a tent. This is an option for those who imagine a vacation without comfortable living conditions. The tent camp is located directly in the coastal zone, but you will have to take care of food yourself. But in the beach area there is infrastructure, dry closets and garbage cans are installed. Entrance to the beach area is paid.

Sanatoriums, boarding houses and treatment

It is on the Podbornoe lake of the Chelyabinsk region that the Ural resort is located. The famous health resort hosting residents of the region and other areas. Nearby there is a children's recreation camp and two boarding houses: UralZlak and Forest Lake. All these institutions are actively using the medical infrastructure of the sanatorium.

Patients with the following problems come here:

  • in the presence of diseases associated with the musculoskeletal system;
  • with problems with the skin;
  • if necessary, treatment of the respiratory system;
  • with problems of the genitourinary system;
  • with diseases of the gastrointestinal tract.

In addition to taking mud and sun baths, in the Uvelsky district there is a museum of local lore, the House of Folk Art. You can visit the zoo at Baranovo Farm. And of course, there are magnificent pine forests and birch groves in the area. Here you can walk endlessly and even pick mushrooms.

Sanatorium "Ural" is designed for 412 visitors. You can stay in 1 and 2 bed rooms. The building is 7-storey, combined with a dining room, leisure and medical buildings, warm transitions.

In the dormitory building there is an elevator, in the rooms there is a loggia, refrigerator, TV, sanitary unit. You can eat in the dining room or cafe, everywhere there is the opportunity to arrange custom meals. It is not necessary to wait for the warm season, you can take mud baths in the walls of the sanatorium in any season.
