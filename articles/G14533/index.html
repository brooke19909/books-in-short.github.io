The village of Golubitskaya is a place where you can relax as your soul desires: noisy, incendiary or quiet, calm, with family or friends.





Uniqueness of relaxation

The village stretches along the Azov coast along the isthmus between the Akhtanizovsky estuary and Temryuk bay. It is conditionally divided into 2 parts: upper and lower. Amazing landscapes open in the upper part of the village, this is the place of family pensions, silence, filled with the smells of the steppe and the sea. Rest in hotels of the Golubitskaya village can be both active and passive.

The Azov coast near the village is shallow, therefore already in May the water warms up enough to open the swimming season. In the summer, the temperature of sea water warms up to + 26-28 0 . The air in the village is full of useful aromas of steppe herbs and marine fumes. Unforgettable impressions get vacationers when visiting a stanitsa mud volcano. Sensations while swimming are indescribable. Due to the high density of mud, it is impossible to drown in a pond, the depth of which is more than 300 m. Bathing in the mud is a fun and also extremely useful wellness procedure.

How to get there

If a hotel in Golubitskaya by the sea has already been booked, it remains only to get to the place of rest. This can be done as follows:

  1. Air traffic. There is a taxi from the airport in Anapa to the village of Golubitskaya (cost about 1,500 rubles) or a bus to Temryuk, where you need to change to a bus to Golubitskaya. From the Krasnodar airport you can get on any bus that goes to the Crimea (it makes a stop in Golubitskaya) or to Temryuk with a transfer.
  2. A train. The railway station is located in the village of Dzhemet near Anapa. Buses go to Temryuk or Crimea.
  3. Car. From Krasnodar through Slavyansk-on-Kuban to Golubitskaya, the R-251 highway leads.
