Features leisure and attractions. Nessebar (Bulgaria).

Thousands of Russian tourists come to rest in friendly Bulgaria every year. They are attracted by the clear sea, beautiful beaches, beautiful nature and an extensive excursion program. The city of Nessebar (Bulgaria) is considered the pearl of this Balkan country. Here you can not only have a great time, but also see the masterpieces of ancient architecture. In this article, we will tell you about the features of relaxation in the resort town, as well as about its main attractions. Nessebar (Bulgaria) is officially declared a museum city, and since the end of the last century has been under the auspices of UNESCO. It is divided into two parts, each of which has its own characteristics.



Old Nessebar (Bulgaria)

The ancient part of the city is located on a small peninsula, which is connected to the mainland by a narrow isthmus. Arriving here, you can immerse yourself in a unique atmosphere, inspect the remains of the churches, the ancient port and the walls that were destroyed by time. Walking along the cobbled streets, you can admire the buildings of the 19th century and ancient buildings with wooden bay windows. Here you will find the Archaeological and Ethnographic museums, in which everyone has the opportunity to get acquainted with the amazing history of this part of the country. You will not be indifferent when you see in the first museum an impressive collection of icons, figured ceramics of the Middle Ages and Antiquity, as well as Thracian stone anchors. And in the second you can get acquainted with the costumes of the Bulgarians and their traditional jewelry.



Churches of Nessebar

According to most tourists, all the most important sights are concentrated in the old city . Nessebar (Bulgaria) is known for the fact that many temples and chapels have been built on its territory. The main part of the buildings is perfectly preserved, and the other is represented in the form of ruins, which are still admired by the guests of this wonderful country.

The Church of Christ Pantocrator (Almighty) is considered one of the most beautiful in all of Bulgaria. It was built in the 12th century, but even today it is able to impress tourists with the richness of decoration and the bizarre form. The fifth-century Metropolitan Church is the oldest building in the city. Legend has it that the builders erected it on the ruins of an ancient temple dedicated to Apollo. In the Church of St. Stephen, which was built by Christians in the ninth century, you can see the well-preserved murals of the 16th century, the iconostasis, the pulpit and the throne of the bishop.



New town

The modern part of the city lies on the mainland. There are many comfortable hotels, cottages, cafes, restaurants, entertainment venues and shops. Tourists appreciate the resort of Nessebar for the fact that you can relax here, while saving considerable money. A large selection of two and three star hotels allows families with different incomes to spend several weeks by the sea, without denying themselves anything. The fashionable five-star complexes of the new city accept those who are not shy in their means and want to spend a vacation with all possible comfort. Those who do not like to settle in crowded places can find shelter in ancient private houses built in the past and the century before last. The hosts provide guests with all the modern conveniences, including access to the Internet and watching television programs.

Bulgaria. Resort Nessebar

Many vacationers consider the beaches of the city and its environs the most beautiful in the country. Environmentalists also agree with this opinion, having awarded the Blue Flag international award to a wide strip of golden sand. Locals and tourists come here, tired of visiting many attractions. Nessebar (Bulgaria) does everything so that its guests are not bored. On the beach of the resort town, you can rent a yacht, go windsurfing and diving under the guidance of an instructor, play volleyball and hide from the sun in the nearest cafe or restaurant. From here you can go on an excursion or get to Sunny Beach, Burgas and St. Vlas.

Aquapark

Aqua Paradise is located just four kilometers from the city center. It can be easily reached by a free bus from Sveti Vlas, Sunny Beach and Ravda. At the height of the holiday season, the water park operates seven days a week and receives visitors from morning to evening. However, most of the slides, attractions and pools are in the open. Therefore, in case of bad weather, it is closed completely or partially.



Trips around

If you are active enough, then after some time, study the main local attractions. Nessebar (Bulgaria) is a large museum that is very difficult to fully explore. However, do not forget about other interesting places that you can visit on your own or with the help of a travel company. You may be interested to see a performance that reproduces the life of a Bulgarian village. Locals dressed in national costumes will show the guests a wedding ceremony, entertain with songs and dances, and also introduce you to the features of everyday life. In the evening, you can taste the dishes of Bulgarian cuisine and watch how the artists dance on hot coals. Lovers of outdoor activities will enjoy a jeep ride in the surroundings and a pirate party organized on board the yacht. Do not forget that the most beautiful cities are adjacent to Nessebar. Be sure to take a trip to Balchik and spend an unforgettable day in the local Botanical Garden. Here you will see an impressive collection of cacti, go for a wine tasting, take a walk among the pink flower beds and admire the residence of Queen Mary.
