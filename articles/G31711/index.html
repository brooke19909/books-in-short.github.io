Restaurants

Delicious, high-quality and varied dishes will delight its guests with the resort complex Port Side Resort Hotel. Dining options are provided by the following restaurants:

The facility has a dining room decorated with natural wood, as well as a spacious outdoor terrace opposite the pool.
  • The Ottoman restaurant will introduce you to local culinary traditions. The dining room is decorated in accordance with oriental interior traditions. You can also enjoy Turkish music here.

Negative reviews

The hot climate and warm weather in Side contribute to a full and pleasant rest. Turkey is one of the most popular tourist destinations, which is famous for its quality service. But unfortunately, each hotel has its pitfalls, flaws and shortcomings. The Port Side Resort was no exception. The hotel received such negative reviews:

  • paid use of the safe, which, as a rule, does not happen in five-star establishments (they ask for one and a half euros for this option);
  • the cosmetic kit does not include hair conditioner, which girls especially do not like;
  • during off-season, the hotel is very boring because there is no animation, and half of the infrastructure facilities do not function;
  • due to the fact that the main contingent of tourists is tourists from Germany, almost none of the staff speak Russian (learn some basic phrases in English in advance to be able to communicate with employees);
  • the maids do not have a clear schedule for cleaning the rooms (they come in the morning or after lunch, absolutely not adjusting to the vacationers);
  • the playground is very small and modest in terms of equipment (in addition, it is under the scorching sun, and therefore it is problematic for children to play on it during the day);
  • the animation is sluggish and uninteresting (and given that it is conducted only in English and German, it is doubly uninteresting as well);
  • inflated prices in retail outlets located on the territory of the resort complex;
  • there are not enough changing cabins on the beach (besides they are very small).
