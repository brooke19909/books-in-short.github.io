Pho Bien Hotel (Vietnam / Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

Pho Bien Hotel is a unique resort hotel that will appeal to even the most demanding and demanding traveler. Here you will find comfort and real Vietnamese exotic. The staff of this hotel will gladly fulfill all your wishes and whims.





Resort Feature

Pho Bien Hotel is one of the best resort hotels you can find in Nha Trang. Here they are very kind to the guests, and also strive in every way to please them. For this purpose, the reception is open for you around the clock, so at any time you can solve the problems associated with accommodation, as well as ask questions that are of interest to you. One of the main features of this resort hotel is a bar located directly on the roof, which offers very picturesque views.

The Pho Bien Hotel is quite modern and has a sleek, stylish design. This can be seen not only in the architecture and decoration of the lobby, but also in the interior design of the rooms. All apartments have a high level of comfort and a good range of services. One of the main features of the rooms of this hotel is the huge windows, which not only allow a lot of light into the room, but also provide an opportunity to admire the scenery.

Pho Bien Hotel offers a host of services that give you plenty of opportunities for a relaxing holiday or a vibrant entertainment. For your convenience, car parking, laundry, and a room for ironing are provided. You can go on an exciting sightseeing trip. You can not worry about calling a taxi to the airport, because the hotel provides such a service as a transfer.





Resort Location

Pho Bien Hotel (Nha Trang) is located in the city center. This opens up great opportunities for tourists, because you have access to many infrastructure facilities, as well as good transport interchange. You will have to walk no more than 30 meters to the public beach. But the distance to the airport is about 30 km. If you want to use the railway, then there is a train station at your service, which is 3 km from the hotel.





Resort Guest Rooms

Resort Pho Bien Hotel (Vietnam) can offer you a choice of one of the following comfortable rooms:

  • The largest and most affordable category of rooms is the standard. There are all the minimum necessary amenities to make guests feel cozy and comfortable. On a small area - two single beds or one large.
  • Superior room has a slightly larger area, and, accordingly, is more spacious. Since only double beds are offered here, this option is often offered to newlyweds.
  • Deluxe rooms can be designed for both double and triple occupancy. Some of these apartments have windows overlooking the sea. Here, in addition to the bedroom, there is also a small seating area with cozy upholstered furniture.
  • If you want to pay the lowest possible cost for your accommodation and intend to spend the minimum amount of time in the apartment, then a dormitory room will do. One room has several bunk beds. In the same room are both men and women.




Helpful information

It will be useful for tourists to familiarize themselves with the following information regarding the conditions and rules of accommodation at the Pho Bien Hotel 3 * resort hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang) :

  • no matter what time you arrived at the hotel, you can register and check into your room no earlier than after two in the afternoon;
  • on the day of departure, guests are required to return the keys to the reception before noon, otherwise penalties will be charged;
  • children under 6 years old stay in apartments for free if they do not need an extra bed;
  • children from 7 to 12 years old under the same conditions live in rooms for $ 4 per day;
  • the hotel does not provide extra beds in the rooms;
  • You have the opportunity to pay for the services received by credit card;
  • within 5 days after you reserved the room, you must make a money transfer to the hotel account as a deposit (otherwise the reservation will be considered invalid).




Positive hotel reviews

Many tourists prefer the Pho Bien Hotel resort for their holidays in Vietnam. Reviews about this institution contain a number of positive comments:

  • the hotel is very conveniently located, and the public beach is just across the road;
  • the bathroom always has hot water, even on the upper floors (although in other hotels there are some interruptions in this regard);
  • cleaning is quite high quality, and towels are changed daily;
  • an undoubted advantage of this hotel is the presence of balconies in the rooms.
