Guest house "Solnechny" in Kabardinka: description, reviews

The popular tourist village of Kabardinka is located just 9 km from the city of Gelendzhik. On a minibus from the railway station of this city, tourists can get here in just about 20 minutes. A distinctive feature of Kabardinka is the low prices for services and a very well-developed tourist infrastructure. In any case, there are many convenient mini-hotels in this village. For example, some tourists come here to the Solnechny Hotel. In Kabardinka, among vacationers, this comfortable hotel enjoys well-deserved popularity.

Where exactly is

This mini-hotel was once built in the very center of the village. That is, its guests can take full advantage of the infrastructure of Kabardinka. In the immediate vicinity of the complex there are many various shops, cafes, restaurants, etc. The exact address of this recreation center is as follows: Kabardinka, 44 Doobskaya, Solnechny Guest House.





Very quickly, hotel guests can reach the promenade. This main street in Kabardinka is a center of entertainment and relaxation for tourists of any age. Located next to the guest house "Solnechny" (Kabardinka) and several other comfortable complexes. The closest one is Hope.

Description of the complex

They accept guests at this hotel in one small three-story building. The hotel building itself has a rather original finish and architecture and, according to tourists, looks quite presentable. Of course, the Solnechny complex has its own small courtyard. The area in front of the hotel building, as well as all the paths in its courtyard, are paved with paving tiles. Everywhere in the hotel there are flower beds and trees planted.

Tourists staying at this hotel are not required to comply with any special rules. The only thing - it is not allowed to check into the rooms of this hotel with pets, as well as smoking in the rented rooms. For lovers of cigarettes in the complex are equipped, among other things, special places.





Hotel Residential Fund

In total, in the Solnechny complex 8 comfortable rooms are rented out for tourists. The hotel rooms are designed for 2, 3 or 4 people. Tourists can rent deluxe, standard or studio rooms in this hotel. The apartments at the hotel consist of:

  • hallway;

  • kitchens;

  • bedrooms;

  • shower room.

In the most expensive rooms of this hotel, instead of microwave ovens in the kitchens, double-stove gas stoves are installed. In the bathrooms of the hotel rooms, for the convenience of guests, washbasins, showers and sinks are provided.

All rooms have a TV, kettle and air conditioning. Also in the rooms of the hotel, its guests can use free Wi-Fi.

Guest House Solnechny (Kabardinka): hotel infrastructure reviews

Of course, guests of the Solnechny complex can use some conveniences directly on its territory. The hotel infrastructure, for example, includes such elements as:

  • massage room;

  • free parking;

  • children playground.

This hotel also has a small pool. The fact that they need parking, vacationers must warn the hotel owners in advance - during the booking of rooms.

In addition to a massage room, a playground and parking for cars, there is a laundry room in the Solnechny guest house (Gelendzhik, Kabardinka village) . Also, for a fee, guests are provided with a service such as ironing. This hotel also has its own mini business center. Here, hotel guests can, for example, make a photocopy of any documents.





Reviews of meals in the complex

Own dining room at the hotel "Solnechny" (Kabardinka) no . But the residents of this hotel have the opportunity to cook their own meals in the summer kitchen located on its territory. Also, the owners of the hotel provide their guests with the opportunity to purchase food in the restaurant of the boarding house located next to it. Also, the guests of the Solnechny Hotel, of course, have the opportunity to have breakfast, lunch and dinner in the cafe next to it or in the cafeterias in the city.

Sea

Of course, there is no own territory on the coastline at the Solnechniy mini-hotel. The guests of this complex visit the public beach belonging to the village. This hotel is located, unfortunately, quite far from the sea. In order to get to the beach, its residents need to overcome about a kilometer. It takes the road to the sea from the hotel buildings for about 30 minutes for tourists.





Located opposite the Solnechny Guest House (Kabardinka, 44 Doobskaya St.), the beach is equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas. But for their rental guests of this hotel have to pay separately. There are various kinds of water activities on this beach. Hotel guests, if desired, can ride a "banana", a jet ski, a catamaran, etc.
