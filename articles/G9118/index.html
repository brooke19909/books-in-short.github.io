Refrigerator Indesit IB 160 R: description, features and reviews

No family can do without a refrigerator. But when it comes time to choose a particular model, many people begin to get lost in a wide range. Indeed, he is great. In stores, expensive units are presented and not very. If the budget is limited to 15-16 thousand rubles, then you should take a closer look at the Russian-made model Indesit IB 160 R. Reviews and a brief description of the characteristics read below.

A few words about the refrigerator

This model is presented in a classic design. Ideal for any style of cuisine. It belongs to the compact category, as it has dimensions: height - 1670 mm, depth - 670 mm, width - 600 mm. The metal case is painted white, there are plastic inserts. They have recesses for easy door opening.

Indesit IB 160 R - model with two cameras. Below is a freezer with three drawers. This allows you to observe the commodity neighborhood. They are quite roomy. They easily fit meat, fish and other products.

There are glass shelves in the refrigerator. They are adjustable in height, so putting a large pot, jar or bottle will not be a problem. Special containers are provided for storing greens, vegetables and fruits.

Indesit IB 160 R: Specifications

Before you finally decide on a purchase, you need to consider in detail the characteristics of this model. According to the manufacturer, the total volume of the refrigerator is 278 liters, of which 83 liters are reserved for the freezer. Implemented an electromechanical control system. According to the owners, there are no problems with choosing a certain temperature, just turn the knob.

Indesit IB 160 R is class B in terms of energy consumption. It consumes 445 kWh per year. The compressor is one. During its operation, a sound is produced equal to a value of up to 39 dB. For defrosting, a drip system is provided. Climatic class - N. There are two doors in the refrigerator. If necessary, they can be outweighed on the other side. This is important for those who have a rather small or furniture-laden kitchen.

Owner reviews

Unfortunately, the Indesit IB 160 R unit receives not only positive reviews. Some owners have comments on noise levels. If the device is installed in the corridor or studio apartment, then during operation the sound made by the engine interferes with sleep.

The compact body size is an advantage for many. But the dimensions of the cameras depend on them. Some owners drew attention to the fact that the ratio of the refrigerator and freezer compartments is distributed irrationally. It is important to understand that this model is not suitable for large families, but for students, to the country is the best option.

The indisputable benefits of the Indesit IB 160 R include simple maintenance. The drip system allows you to forget about the thick layers of ice that can form in simple refrigerators. The device defrosts manually, that is, it is disconnected from the power supply. The advantage is not in the method, but in the time period. Defrosting is rarely required, since frost on the walls cannot form thicker than 5 mm. Outside, the case and doors are covered with high-quality enamel, so any pollution is washed off effortlessly.
