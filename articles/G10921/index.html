Meliton Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) - budget vacation in one of the most popular hotels of Theologos

One of the most popular Greek mid-range hotels is Meliton Hotel 3 *. Rhodes is a real Mediterranean jewel, so it is not surprising that tens of thousands of tourists come here every year. But everyone needs to stay somewhere, and the most popular option is hotels.





Location

Meliton Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) is located in a small but very picturesque village called Theologos. By the way, this is one of the best places for a real rest - here you can soak up the warm rays of the sun, swim in the sea and enjoy the stunning scenery. The infrastructure is well developed here, and every tourist will find something to his liking. Night clubs, restaurants, shops, excursions - this resort really has everything that your heart desires. That is why both elderly couples and noisy youth companies come here.

Meliton Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) is located right on the first coastline - in the immediate vicinity of the beach. In addition, there are several attractions near the hotel that are worth a look - these are Filermios, Seven Springs, Butterfly Valley, as well as Ancient Kamiros. And this, it should be noted, is only a small list of the amazing places that are here.

By the way, the hotel is only five kilometers from the airport. This, one might say, is an ideal distance - and the noise will not interfere with the guests, and get quickly.

Rooms

Now we should talk about what conditions the Meliton Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) offers its guests. In all rooms of the hotel there are necessary conditions for living and a good rest. The most ordinary apartments have a balcony with a picturesque view of the surroundings, satellite TV, telephone, air conditioning and comfortable comfortable furniture. Also in the rooms there are such necessary "little things in life" as ironing facilities, hairdryer, refrigerator, wardrobe. And, of course, a bathroom with a shower and personal hygiene products.





All apartments are decorated in an elegant style - everything looks very nice, in these rooms it is really nice to relax. By the way, guests booking double rooms are offered a choice - either they will be given the option with a large double bed, or with two single beds.

Cost

As already mentioned, Meliton Hotel 3 * (Greece) is a middle class hotel. Booking prices here are really loyal. So, for example, if you rent a comfortable double room for a week, you will need to pay only 18,000 rubles for this. And this is with the included breakfast! If you take the option with the all-inclusive service, then it will come out more expensive - 30,000 rubles.

A triple room with all necessary amenities will cost 33 thousand - and this is the same as in the previous case, "all inclusive". It turns out only eleven thousand for each person - and this is for a luxurious weekly stay in a three-star hotel in Greece!





Four-bed rooms are also offered to potential guests, and the cost of booking such apartments is even less - about 40,000 rubles for four, also "all inclusive." In general, as you can see, this is a really good budget option. Not surprisingly, the rooms at this hotel are snapping around like hotcakes. Facilities and amenities

This hotel is not only a good option to stay there for a while. It is also a good place to relax. On its territory there is everything necessary for guests to relax. For example, the pool bar serves a wide variety of drinks (both strong and non-alcoholic), as well as excellent coffee. There is also a snack bar where you can enjoy a tasty meal.

In the pool you can not only swim, but also enjoy the hot tub. Fans of more active pastimes are invited to spend time in the billiard room or play tennis. If you want to relax in silence, you can sit in the picturesque garden or sunbathe on the terrace specially designed for this. The hotel also has a massage room, library, karaoke and even bicycle rental. And, of course, there is free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.

Reviews, comments and recommendations

Much has been said about the Meliton Hotel 3 * (Rhodes). Testimonials are other things to talk about. Many people pay attention to them when they decide to book a room in a particular hotel. And this is correct, since the description of the hotel is one thing, but the reviews of people who have already stayed there are completely different.





Guests comment on their stay at Meliton Hotel 3 * with pleasure. Good service, attentive staff, tasty treats, stylish and comfortable rooms - there is really everything inside that will make your vacation unforgettable. A small but comfortable hotel - this is how you can describe the Meliton Hotel 3 * (Rhodes). The photo of the hotel, by the way, is confirmed once again - looking at this small hotel, so harmoniously fitting into the landscape surrounding it, you involuntarily begin to feel the desire to see all this with your own eyes. Well, this is a worthy option, only you need to remember that the hotel is open from April to the end of October.
