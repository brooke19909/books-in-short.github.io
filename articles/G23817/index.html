Hotel El Mouradi Club Selima 3 * (Tunisia / Sousse): photos, reviews

Tunisia has a huge variety of hotels with the highest level of customer service, most of which are concentrated in Sousse. The beaches of the hotel complexes are always clean and well maintained, and the rooms are spacious and at the same time very comfortable.

One of such chic hotel complexes is El Mouradi Club Selima 3 * (Tunisia). Reviews about this hotel are mostly positive.

general information





The complex is very favorably located on the territory of Port al Kantaoui. This hotel is considered one of the best among those that have a level of three stars. On the territory there are two restaurants, one of which is a la carte, two swimming pools (one for children, the other for adults), five bars with alcoholic and non-alcoholic refreshing cocktails and drinks, seven tennis venues, a comfortable and modern lobby bar, beauty salon.

Location





At a distance of 2 kilometers from the hotel there is a modern golf course, and a little further, 2.8 kilometers away, is the famous water park and many other significant infrastructure.

The distance to the most famous local liquor store is about 3 kilometers, and to the interesting Ice Cream Palace - about 7.5 km. A bar for fans to taste a mug of foamy drink is located at a distance of about 2.5 kilometers.

The central part of Sousse is 9 km from the hotel complex, and the city's airport is 36 km away.
