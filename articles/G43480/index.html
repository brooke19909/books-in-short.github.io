Smartphone Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb: reviews, description

The hero of our review - the smartphone Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb - received different reviews. "Why?" - you ask. The reason for this was the strengths and weaknesses of this gadget, which we still have to figure out.

This model was a continuation of the Honor line. The manufacturer has released two devices with pretty sophisticated features. Design

The first criterion by which the buyer chooses a new smartphone is design. This confirms a large number of reviews. Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb Blue looks amazing. In addition to the blue version, the line also has black, gold and white. Each user can choose for himself a favorite color. However, in these moments it is necessary to pay attention to the soiled surfaces. Why is this question so relevant for many users? The reason is the lack of oleophobic coating. Fingerprints and greasy spots are best seen on the Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb Black. Reviews of the owners of such an instance suggest that the front and back panels must be constantly wiped with a soft cloth or carry a smartphone in a case. However, in defense of the black specimen, let's say that it looks very stylish and majestic. Bright options in this matter have a greater advantage, since fingerprints are almost invisible on their surface.

In the comments, users often talk about the quality of the materials that the manufacturer used for the case. Compared to other models of this line, glass located on the front and back panels can be confused with plastic by touch. Unfortunately, even the rim passing along the ends of the body is not made of metal. Users worry that when used, severe scuffs will appear. In order to prevent abrasion of surfaces, you can use special bumper covers. Speaking about the body material, the owners noticed another drawback - the surface of the phone is very slippery. Based on this, users are advised to hold the phone in their hands rather tightly.

But according to the assembly criterion, Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb Gold received only praise reviews. There are no creaks, backlashes, large gaps on the case. Details fitted as accurately as possible. Also commendable is the thickness of the gadget, which is only 7.6 mm. Users also include the mass of the device (147 g).

The ergonomics of the device are thought out pretty well, thanks to the rounded corners of the case and the side edges of the smartphone in the hand is quite comfortable.

On the front panel, as usual above the screen, there are notifications indicator, earpiece, camera and sensors. Large touch screen occupies 70% of the area. Under it flaunts the name of the ruler. Touch navigation keys are displayed on the screen. As for the quality of the speakers, in the comments, users talk about loud clear sound. During a conversation with a subscriber, the speech sounds clearly and clearly. In this matter, the manufacturer worked wonderfully.

The location of the connectors and controls has not been changed. At the bottom of the case are the speaker holes, a microphone and a USB port. At the opposite end, the manufacturer placed an additional microphone and a headset jack. The side faces are occupied on the right by the volume and lock keys, and on the left is the tray with combined slots. Although not all users are thrilled, but a memory card is installed instead of the second SIM card.

The glossy surface of the back panel of the Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb (photo below) is a significant drawback. If you approach only from the aesthetic side, then it looks quite impressive, but completely impractical. One has only to pick up the phone, as soon as a large number of spots and fingerprints remain on it. This moment really does not please owners who do not use covers. On the back cover at the bottom is the name of the Honor line. At the top, the manufacturer installed a camera lens and a flash. Immediately below them is a fingerprint scanner. The indisputable advantage is that the lens and flash are made flush with the body. Thanks to this, there is no need to worry about the fact that they will be constantly scratched.



Screen

In the review of Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb RAM 4Gb, it is necessary to include a description of the screen, since users on the forums are actively discussing it. In this model, the manufacturer has implemented an excellent display with high resolution. Smartphone owners can enjoy Full HD quality. A screen diagonal of 5.2 inches opens up great opportunities for the user. Using your phone, it‚Äôs very convenient to watch movies, chat on social networks, play games. An indisputable advantage, according to users, is the lack of familiar thick frames.

Often in the comments, the owners talk about useful features that are implemented in this model. The most interesting ones are vision protection and automatic color correction.

Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb: performance characteristics

This model runs on a processor of its own production. It is worth noting that the company installs it on all inexpensive gadgets. HiSilicon Kirin 655 is based on 8 cores. The system row is 64 bits. Cortex-A53 Compute Modules are capable of operating at maximum load with a clock frequency of 2100 MHz. The production of the chipset corresponds to the 16 nm manufacturing process. This indicates an energy-efficient platform, which is also provided by FinFET + technology.

The characteristics of the graphics accelerator fully satisfy the requirements of a medium category of devices. In this model, manufacturers installed the Mali-T830 MP2. The video card is based on two computing modules. He copes with the main tasks quite adequately.

Users in their comments note the following features of the gadget:

Battery

In reviews of the Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb RAM 4Gb, you can also find out about the characteristics of the battery. In this model, a fixed lithium-ion battery is installed. It has a capacity of three thousand milliamps per hour. In their comments, users claim that with an average load, the device will work for about a day. During this time, you can connect to 4G, go online via Wi-Fi-module, make calls on 3G networks.

However, you need to understand: if you constantly keep LTE on, the battery life will be reduced to about 20 hours. For players, this model is not very suitable, since in this mode at maximum brightness the device can last no more than 4-5 hours on a single charge. When watching a video, you can count on 9 hours. Surfing the Internet will put the battery in 5-6 hours.

Negative reviews

As previously mentioned, Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb receives negative reviews. Unfortunately, at present, even in the middle price category, it is difficult to find a perfect gadget. This model is no exception. A high level of marbling of the glass caused a flurry of complaints. At first glance, the case made of glass, it immediately becomes clear that you can not do without a cover. For those who absolutely do not like to use it, this model can be a big problem.

There are owners who have comments on the gyroscope. Errors occur when watching a 360-degree video. Well, if you find fault with a lot of things, then the disadvantages include problems with the color rendering of the screen that occur at different levels of brightness.



Finally

So, the review of Huawei Honor 8 Lite 32Gb is completed. Reviews both negative and positive were considered in full. Based on them, we can conclude that by and large the phone justifies its price.
