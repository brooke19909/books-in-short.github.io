The main radio stations of Voronezh

This material will feature Voronezh FM radio stations. In addition to those described below, in this city you can hear “World”, “Europe Plus”, “Guberniya”, Energy, “7 on seven hills”, “Dorozhnoe”, “Chanson”, “Autoradio”, Love, DFM, “Russian” , “Retro FM”, “Lighthouse”, Maximum, “Melody”, “Borneo”, “Cottage”.

Entertainment channel



We will begin to describe Voronezh radio stations with Humor FM. The format is based on entertainment programs. The radio station is part of the Prof-Media holding. It began broadcasting in 2005. This is the first fully humorous radio station in the country. The final choice of the name was made through a sociological survey.

Lead FM

This is an informational radio station. It is part of the VGTRK holding. It began broadcasting in 2008. In total, it can be heard in 60 settlements of Russia. In 2014, there was an update in the design of the broadcast. The audience is mostly men. Income per family member is above average. The project was awarded the Radio Mania Prize.

Other channels



There are Voronezh radio stations for a young audience, in particular, the channel "Children's". The project was created by the founder of the holding Gazprom-Media. This is the first and only radio station in Russia focused on a children's audience. The channel takes into account the daily routine and age characteristics of the audience. Preschoolers addressed the morning block.

Some Voronezh radio stations were created specifically for men, including Sport FM. The project is part of the European Media Group. The radio station tells about all kinds of sports, from golf to football. On the air, you can follow the live broadcasts of the most important competitions. Part of the broadcast is given to sports news.

We should also mention the political radio stations of Voronezh, in particular, Komsomolskaya Pravda. This channel informs listeners about what is happening in the country as well as in the world around the clock. On the air there are heated discussions with experts on relevant topics. Often the channel manages to be among the first to cover events.

In Voronezh you can hear Relax FM. The format of the station involves the broadcast of melodic compositions aimed at emotional relaxation of the audience. The project was founded in 2006. The studio is located in Moscow. The weekly audience exceeds 700,000 listeners. The main audience is married women and men.

Radio of Russia is a state radio station. The project is part of the All-Russian State Television and Radio Broadcasting Company. Broadcasting began in 1990. There are broadcasts of our own production on the air. The channel broadcasts in the FM band in 65 regions. The official resource of the project says that Radio of Russia is the only federal station of a general format that produces all kinds of programs.
