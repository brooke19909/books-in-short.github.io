Perestroika money: where do they give loans for apartment renovation?

If you decide to speed up the process of transforming your living space, then targeted credit programs with financing for repairs come to the rescue. Their distinguishing feature is the requirement to attract professionals - paying in cash with a team of guest workers at the expense of borrowed funds will not work. In which bank is it better to get a loan for apartment renovation , and where are the most suitable conditions for transforming your family nest?

This bank is ready to provide an amount of up to 500,000 rubles for 1-5 years to improve the quality of life - apartment or house repair. Orient Express will contribute to the renovation of your home for a fee of 23.5% per annum if you are a regular customer, and from 26% per year if you came to the bank for the first time. A loan for the repair of apartments is issued without the involvement of guarantors or registration of a pledge.

The decision to issue the said financial institution takes 5 minutes, and for registration you need a civil passport and one of the documents for real estate:

  • certificate of ownership;
  • privatization agreement;
  • contract of sale;
  • will;
  • the court's decision;
  • office rental agreement .

This consumer loan is available for apartment repairs to citizens of the Russian Federation from the age of 21, and women can take money from the age of 20 when paying 10% of expenses from their own pockets.
