Netanya specifics

This resort is the exact opposite of ancient and pious Jerusalem. Netanya is full of ultra-modern buildings, wide promenades, and shopping centers. But the main distinguishing feature of the resort is the feeling of endless celebration. Discos, bars, clubs, cafes on the beach - this is what attracts the youth of Netanya. Hotels in the city for the most part have two to three stars. Reasonable prices for housing and food make the resort even more popular. It is chosen not only by tourists from abroad, but also by Israeli youth in the capital. After all, from Tel Aviv to Netanya, only an hour and a half by car. Eleven kilometers of the coast is divided into eight beautifully equipped beaches: from Blue Bay to Poleg. They have everything that your heart desires. Shower, toilets, fountains with drinking water, paths for people with disabilities, changing rooms, sports grounds and much more. Enough in Netanya and attractions. Hotels in Netanya (Israel): reviews

Tourists say that accommodation at this resort can be found on any wallet and credit card. There are fabulously luxurious hotels and modest hotels without any stars. But the level of service in them will still be very high. In order to find suitable housing for a low price, you do not need to go to the outskirts of the city. Almost the entire hotel base is concentrated in a few minutes of leisurely walking from the central square of Ha Atzmaut. You can go along the trading artery of Netanya - Rehov Ha-Meleh David - to the north or go down the big streets to the south - you will find a lot of hotels. Moreover, due to the elongation of the resort along the coastal strip, it will be within easy reach of the sea. Reviews say that in Netanya, you can rent apartments from private individuals.
