Golubitskaya: beaches, hotels and entertainment

The village of Golubitskaya is one of the most popular holiday destinations on the Sea of ​​Azov. It is located in the Krasnodar Territory on the Taman Peninsula. This place is especially loved by families and children, because Golubitskaya has beautiful sandy beaches, as well as a shallow sea. Water warms up because of the shallow water very quickly, so it is warmer than in the Black Sea. And on the wide sandy beaches there is enough space for everyone. It remains only to choose a suitable place and type of vacation.

Description of the village of Golubitskaya

The village of Golubitskaya is one of the oldest settlements in this region. In the past, this is a small fishing village for only 5 thousand people, and in modern times it is one of the most popular resort places. Tourists from all over the country come to rest in Golubitskaya. The sea, the beach and the friendly sun - there is everything for a good vacation.

The village is located on the Taman Peninsula, 10 km from the city of Temryuk. It is surrounded on all sides by water: the sea and estuaries, mud pools. It has two parts: the hill, where the gardens and vineyards are located, which are famous for the region, and the coastal part, where there are residential guest houses and, of course, wide sandy beaches flooded with sun.





The sea near the village of Golubitskaya

The sea near the coast of the village is shallow, so it quickly warms up. In May, the water temperature already reaches twenty degrees. The seabed on the coast is sandy. As for the quality of sand at the village of Golubitskaya, the beach here is made of small shell rock. Walking along such a coast is comfortable. It is not quite soft snow-white sand, like anywhere else in tropical countries, but also very pleasant for the feet. Acupressure foot massage provided!

The sea is calm during the hot months, but from autumn to spring the northeast periodically blows here, therefore Golubitskaya chooses surfers for herself. The waves sometimes reach two meters, the wind sometimes, in the cold season, blows at a speed of more than 10 m / s. All this dispels the myth that the Sea of ​​Azov is a shallow muddy “puddle”.





Golubitskaya: beaches and coastline

The coastline in the village extends for 10 kilometers and has an average width of 50 meters. On these wide beaches, thousands of tourists relax every year. The infrastructure is well developed here: you can rent a deck chair, an umbrella, sports equipment, visit one of the many cafes on the coast. Along the coastline lined 15 sandy beaches. Choose any to your taste.

And if you visit the central beach in Golubitskaya, then not far from it you will find a completely unique place - a mud estuary. This lake is a storehouse of useful chemical elements. It contains hydrogen sulfide and bromine. In a healing mud pond come to swim from Anapa and Temryuk itself. Magic mud has beneficial effects on the muscle system and bones.



What to do in Golubitskaya

There are a lot of entertainment options in this seemingly small village. Firstly, you can go on foot to Liman and enjoy plenty of healing mud.

If you are tired of the beach and the sea in the village of Golubitskaya, you can visit the dolphinarium or crocodile farm. It is interesting to observe how these ancient reptiles live and eat. There are also shows with crocodiles. There are also snakes and other reptiles.

For lovers of water entertainment in the village there is an Amazon water park. On the vast modernized territory there are several slides, trampolines, various restaurants and cafes. An entire day passes unnoticed here.

You can just go to the wild coast in Golubitskaya and relax in peace and quiet. There are many wild beaches here.





Where to stay in the village of Golubitskaya

There are many accommodation options in the village. Those who arrive in their car can choose auto campsites (for example, “Rest”, which is next to the water park and dolphinarium, or “Worker of the sea”, “Atlantic”, “Primorye”). The advantage of such a vacation will be that you are not dependent on transport and can travel in your car wherever you want.

There are boarding houses and sanatoriums in Golubitskaya: “Severyanka”, which provides for a variety of wellness treatments; in the boarding house "Nadezhda" your vacation with children will be wonderful. The cases are located on the first and second coastlines, so you can choose to relax on any wallet. Pension "Cypress" has a gym. And pets are allowed there. You can take your favorite dog or cat with you to the sea!

A major tourist complex in the area is Golden Beach. Golubitskaya really has various accommodation options even for discerning tourists. The main hotel of the Golden Beach shopping complex is stylized as an old castle. It is interesting to find yourself in a corner of the Middle Ages on the shores of the Sea of ​​Azov. The complex has everything for a comfortable stay. There is even a water park.

There are also many guest houses and apartments in the private sector in Golubitskaya. In the off-season, a night in a village can cost from 300-500 rubles!





How to get to the village

If you are traveling to the Sea of ​​Azov from the central or northern regions of Russia, or perhaps from far away, then the fastest way is to fly by plane. From Anapa Airport to Golubitskaya is only 55 kilometers away, which can be reached by taxi (about 1,500 rubles) or by bus. Cars depart from Anapa from the bus station at Krasnoarmeyskaya street, 11. The price of a bus ticket to Temryuk is about 200 rubles. Then, an ordinary minibus will take you to Golubitskaya. Minibuses leave from the bus station on Uritsky Street, 55, every 15 minutes.

Railways do not lead to the village, the nearest station in Dzhemete near Anapa. From there, you will also have to take a bus or taxi.
