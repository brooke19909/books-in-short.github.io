DIY Balloons Flowers

Balloons always give a holiday and a good mood to both children and adults. In addition, if you make various figures of the balls with your own hands, you can originally decorate the interior and make the atmosphere more solemn. And flowers from balls will be an excellent alternative to a living bouquet. Such a gift will be original and financially beneficial. Making flowers from balloons with your own hands is not so difficult.





The art of decorating the interior with balloons is called aero design, and the technique of making such ball ornaments is called modeling. With the help of this article you will learn how to make wonderful flowers from balloons with your own hands and whole bouquets to create a festive decoration.

Green ball stalk

If you want to make flowers from balloons with your own hands, you first need to prepare the stem.

  1. A balloon with a free tail of about 10 cm is inflated. This tail will be filled with air during the twisting process. Having inflated the ball, it is necessary to release a little air so that it becomes softer. Then we make a twist in the form of a tulip. She looks as follows. The tip of the index finger should pierce the neck assembly into the ball. Then, with the other hand, grab the knot through the ball to gently pull your finger out. After this, twisting is done so that the node is located below it. The bud is ready.
  2. A ten-centimeter bubble is twisted, which begins from the twist of a tulip. Then you need to make two small bubbles and twist them with a lock. This is the first leaf on the stalk. In the same way, you can make a second leaf. The stalk is ready.

Flower

To make flowers from balloons with your own hands, you need to fully inflate the balloon, then release some air to make the balloon soft. Otherwise, the ball may burst during twisting. After that, a knot is tied and simulation begins. The end of the ball must be compressed to make a small ponytail. After that, both ends should be tied together.

At the point opposite the node, the ball is twisted so that it turns out to be divided into two equal parts. The resulting parts are folded together in a fan. The joints should form a straight line. After that, it is necessary to squeeze the ball at the joints and twist them with a lock all together. After these simple manipulations, you should get a flower with six petals.

The connection of the flower with the stem





To connect the figure, you need to take the stem by the tulip and stick it between the joints in the center of the flower. Similarly, you can make other shapes, for example, a heart of balloons, a dog, a giraffe, and so on. Show your imagination and a real holiday will come to your home.
