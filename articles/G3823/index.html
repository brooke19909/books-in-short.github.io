Thin smartphones with 2 SIM cards: review, specifications, reviews

We are used to working with compact, attractive design devices capable of solving the maximum of our tasks. In fact, all the devices that are currently on the market have such characteristics. However, some of them differ from others in their dimensions, design, build quality, capabilities.

In this article we will talk about those gadgets that can boast of a slim body. After all, manufacturers seek to minimize the thickness of the device, make it more elegant and sophisticated, even if sacrificing technological capabilities.





Benefits

The positive qualities that thin smartphones possess are obvious. Firstly, it is a decorative meaning. Yes, the refined case gives the phone some charm, makes the device more attractive in the eyes of the buyer. No wonder today, when creating an advertising video about a particular model, manufacturers often mention something like ‚Äúthe thinnest body in the world‚Äù or ‚Äúthe thickness of the case has decreased by 4 mm.‚Äù Such phrases are aimed at causing the user to associate, which makes it clear: the thinner the model, the more valuable it is.

Secondly, reviews note that such devices are more convenient. In fact, holding a model with a large body is not so convenient. This is especially true for girls with a relatively small pen. Therefore, a thin smartphone gives them the opportunity to hold the model more firmly in their hands, for example, during a conversation.

Thirdly, a sophisticated gadget is more practical. Think for yourself: how can you put a large device in, say, a pocket in a handbag? Or, say, to put a smartphone in a pocket of tight trousers, it is necessary that its dimensions allow it to be done, that is, be as small as possible.

For these and other reasons, users love thin smartphones. Therefore, if you choose your device next time, we recommend that you contact the owners of such a case.

disadvantages

Of course, serious problems are hidden behind a small thickness. The first and one of the most significant is the "fragile" character. We all probably remember the story when the iPhone 5 and 5S that came out in the first installments suffered a lot from bending in the back pocket of their trousers. This was caused, again, by the desire of the development company to reduce the size of the smartphone. These thin smartphones did not have a sufficiently strong case that could withstand the pressure in your pocket. As a result, thousands of reports that the device is bent from around the world.





The second important point, which can be called negative, is the lack of sufficient space to accommodate any technological modules and parts. For example, with a larger case, the user can count on a capacious battery and a powerful ‚Äústuffing‚Äù, which can not be said about thin smartphones. This is normal, because any equipment, being more developed, will occupy, accordingly, more space. In some cases (judging by the reviews, again), one of the components will have to be sacrificed in order to ensure the success of the other. If you want a thin smartphone, remember that a large battery will not be provided in it.
