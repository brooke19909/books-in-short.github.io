Hotel Sheraton Sanya Resort (Sanya, China): reviews

Recently, an increasing number of tourists decide to go on vacation to China. The tropical resort of Sanya is especially popular. There are countless hotels in this city, but many visitors stay in the Sheraton Sanya Resort (Sanya) five-star suite. Their choice is due to many reasons. The nearby beach, the high standard of service, luxurious restaurants, stylish rooms ... this hotel has many advantages. And I would like to talk about all of them.





Location

Sheraton Sanya Resort (Sanya) is in a very tourist-friendly location. The international airport is 25 kilometers away and the train station is 8 km away. This place is with well-developed infrastructure and transport links. Interesting sights are nearby. Pools

Sheraton Sanya Resort (Sanya) has several pools. The largest is decorated in the form of a lagoon, which seems to be buried among the dense thickets of tropical trees that seated the whole picturesque territory of the complex. And this is not just a pool. It has slides and waterfalls, which makes it an ideal place to relax for both children and adults.

There is also an oasis type swimming pool. Bathing in it, vacationers can enjoy panoramic views of the South China Sea. In the oasis there is not only slides, but also a compartment for kids and a bar removed from it with a canopy for adults.

In addition, the hotel has a separate children's pool. And the picturesque Lotus Garden. This is a very special heated pool located in the lotus garden, next to the SPA center of the complex. There is also a large jacuzzi section and a bar nearby.





For body and soul

Special attention would be noted hotel SPA-salon, which is called Mandara. Vacationers coming there find themselves in an atmosphere filled with the spirit of cultural and historical traditions, as well as picturesque views. Antique furniture, lush gardens, stunning views of the South China Sea, natural wood bridges, babbling waterfalls ... you can spend a whole day at Mandara SPA and not notice how time flies.

SPA center services

Here you can enjoy body treatments using aloe, healing salts and algae, order a massage with a tropical exfoliating scrub or try a vitamin peeling enriched with macronutrients.

But this is not the whole list of procedures. They also make an excellent massage of any kind: relaxing, tonic, traditional Chinese, Ayurvedic, Thai, stone therapy. And any can be ordered in the room.

And at the SPA-center of the Sheraton Sanya Resort Sanya 5 * hotel there is an elite beauty salon in which they do everything from haircuts and styling to manicure and pedicure.

However, this is only a small part of what you can try when you come to Mandara SPA. The list of services is very wide, and everyone can get acquainted with it individually or after checking into a hotel.





Sports and Entertainment

It is worth noting that in Sheraton Sanya Resort 5 * there is a modern gym with everything necessary for a full physical load equipment that works around the clock. He has power and cardio machines, treadmills, barbells and dumbbells, table tennis, a yoga room and much more.

In addition, while in the complex, you can enjoy water sports, rent a bike, play billiards, visit karaoke, tennis court or golf course.

For the smallest, the hotel organized a children's club with a playroom, TV-lounge, library, drawing corner, etc. Guests who want to relax can bring their child there and not be afraid that he will be bored. Teachers look after the children in the club, and animators constantly arrange an entertainment program in the fresh air, including competitions, contests and sports.

Restaurants

Many people staying at the Sheraton Sanya Resort 5 * (Hainan) pay special attention to establishments of this format. There are as many restaurants as there are four bars.

The first is called Lotus, located in a tropical garden with waterfalls. They serve traditional dishes of South Chinese cuisine (there is a children's menu).

At Baiyun Restaurant you can enjoy delicacies from different provinces of the country. This place is especially fond of gourmets. After visiting it, they get the opportunity to go on a culinary tour across the country. And there is also a tea lounge where a huge variety of this drink is presented.

The third restaurant is called Spice Garden. This establishment practices Pan-Asian cuisine. Lovers of culinary diversity adore it, because you can try anything in it - starting with classic sushi, ending with green curry with chicken, sea bass with sweet and sour sauce and crab meat.

Of course, you can not ignore the Samba Night Club. This is an ideal place, according to vacationers, for a fun pastime in which you can play billiards, sing karaoke, dance and just make your night enjoyable and varied.

What do the guests say?

People staying at the Sheraton Sanya Resort 5 * hotel, reviews leave it mostly positive. And in the first place, they always pay attention to its well-groomed territory. The hotel is literally immersed in greenery, and this is emphasized by every person who visited it.

A private beach with white sand, which is constantly monitored for cleanliness, is nearby, and towels are provided there.
