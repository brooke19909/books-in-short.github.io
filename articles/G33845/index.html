How to make a homemade do-it-yourselfer? Master class: do-it-yourself housekeeper

Even grandmothers told us that each house is guarded by a small creature from the other world, which people have called brownies. The weight knows that this resident needs to be pampered from time to time: put a saucer with milk and put sweets next to it. But you can also make yourself a small copy of the brownie and place it in front of the front door. So this guard will protect the house from evil forces and bad people who will cross the threshold of their homeland.

If the hostess wants to figure out how to make a home- made do-it- yourselfer, then you need to clarify that the toys can be completely different. So, you need to stock up on burlap (this will be the basis of grandfather), thread on burlap (to create handles), tow for hair, synthetic winterizer (for stuffing), as well as all sorts of little things: eyes, nose, cereals, dummies of berries, vegetables and fruits for decoration .

Getting started: preparing the basics

So, where to start, if you need to understand how to make a home- made do-it- yourself? First of all, you need to make a bag of burlap, which will be the body of a home guard. Size can be chosen at your discretion, depending on desire. You need to take a piece of material twice as long as necessary (it is also important to remember that you need allowances for seams), fold it in half and stitch it on the sides from the inside, and leave the top open for filling. Also, first you need to weave handles from burlap threads to the home. This is easy to do: weaving can even be in the form of an ordinary pigtail. It is important not to forget to fix such a braid around the edges so that it does not roll and does not burst. The product is threaded into the bag in the middle (just pushing its threads on the sides, it is quite easy to do it in the burlap), holes are made on both sides next to the seam. These will be the pens of the grandfather guard. Next, the product is stuffed with padding polyester and a basting seam is sewn along the top. The foundation is ready.

Make hair

We go further, figuring out how to make a house-block with your own hands. Now you need to prepare grandfather's hair (as well as a mustache). It is necessary to take a tow of the desired length (by hair level) and calculate the width. For this, it is only necessary to measure the diameter of the finished base, because the hair will be attached around the crown. If the tow is ready, it must be stitched so that the hair does not fall apart. This can be done both manually and on a sewing machine. To do this, under the hair, where the line will go, attach a narrow braid and sew with a wide step. It remains only to highlight the bangs of the brownie, cutting it a little. The product is sewn around the crown on the base. To make a mustache, you just need to take a tow of the desired length and bandage it in the center with a thin thread. Mustache is ready!

Brownie legs

We understand further how to make a do-it-yourself house. Now you need to create the legs of the guard. To do this, stock up on small bast shoes (they can be tied on their own or bought). Next, you need to make two bags according to the size of the shoe (on the basis of the principle), which are also stuffed with synthetic winterizer and sewn on top. That's all, the legs are ready.

Assembly parts

Now you need to collect all the details in a heap and make a brownie. To do this, you need to glue the nose and eyes on the base, sew on a mustache, put on a hat (make it easy from the same burlap in the form of a cap). You can also create three or four small bags that are filled with cereals (brownie for wealth). It is easy to make them, as well as the base, however, you do not need to tie or sew on top, they will be open and fit behind the brownie. You also need to sew bast shoes to the base. Now you can decorate your grandfather at your discretion with vegetables and fruits. The product is ready!





Thread Brownie

You can create a simple little house from threads with your own hands. To do this, you need multi-colored yarn and nothing more. The toy will be quite simple and straightforward. To do this, you need to take the threads of the required length (two times longer than the desired product), bend them in half - this will be the basis of the doll. First of all, the head is created: you need to tie the finished base with a thread, stepping back a little from above, then the body stands out according to the same principle. To make legs, you need to divide the remaining base in half and tie each individual leg with thread on the bottom. Hands are created from yarn separately: you need to take the length immediately into two hands (given that they will pass in the middle of the body) and tie it around the edges with threads. Now, to make the toy look like a brownie, you can attach a mustache or a beard from the threads, as well as insert a bag into your hands. Mustache and beard are made according to the same principle: the thread is simply tied in the center, the product is inserted into the face to the desired height, skipping just one thread in advance. Making a sack is just as easy, you need to bend the threads in half and tie them on top, leaving a small tail. And that‚Äôs all - a home made of threads, created with your own hands, is ready!
