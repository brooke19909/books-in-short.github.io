Hotel Rose Bay Resort 3 (Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists, description

The popularity of the Kingdom of Thailand as a tourist destination is impressive in its scale - in 2013 the number of tourists exceeded 26 million!



And the number of Russian tourists is second only to China and Malaysia. The compatriots' love for Thailand is understandable: a wonderful beach holiday, year-round season, exotic nature, democracy and cheapness make this country uncompetitive for old Europe ...

Pattaya: many benefits in one place

Pattaya is chosen for affordable prices and a variety of options for recreation. This resort area is endowed with wide possibilities: beach vacations, nightly entertainment, shopping and excursions, a combination of continental opportunities and island relaxation - all can be found here.

Especially on vacation, there is no time to sleep, so the choice of a hotel is often dictated by the convenience of its placement in relation to areas of tourist interest and the price of services.

One of the three-star hotels, Rose Bay Resort 3 * (Pattaya), which will be discussed, is perfect for youth recreation. Small, very cozy, it is located in the central part of Pattaya, near many shopping centers and night clubs.

Location

Hotel Rose Bay Resort 3 * is quite new. Opened in 2012. This is important to consider when choosing a placement. Thailand has a high level of humidity, old hotels often become the habitat of unwanted neighbors - insects. New hotels are often sold at a low price to attract tourists. Moreover, the quality of service in these institutions is at a very high level.

A transfer from Bangkok International Airport Suvanapum will deliver guests to their accommodation within an hour, the hotel is 119 kilometers away. If the flight is carried out to the local U-Tapao airport, then half an hour is enough to overcome thirty kilometers to the hotel.

Pattaya: a world of entertainment

From the hotel Rose Bay Resort 3 * to the most popular street Walking Street can be reached on foot in half an hour or reached by tuk-tuk in 10-15 minutes. A three-minute walk is the 3D Art Gallery. According to hotel guests, it should definitely be visited. What else can you do at this resort?

  • Beach vacation. The opinion of not too clear water on the beaches of Pattaya has developed because the bottom is sandy, and red sand makes the water not transparent enough. But you can always go from the pier to the islands and relax on the immaculate beach. The coast of Pattaya is facing west, which makes it possible to admire the picturesque sunsets.
  • Sights. Tropical garden Nong Nooch, which is listed in the Guinness Book of Records as the largest collection of plants. Temple of Truth, Mount Khao Chi Chan with the largest Buddha image.
  • Entertainment. Jungle cable car, show of pink dolphins, jeep safaris, snorkeling, diving, sea fishing and cruises.

For shopping, you should visit the famous Festival shopping center, which is located between Second and Beach Street, a 15-minute walk from the hotel Rose Bay Resort 3 *. For small purchases, a visit to the 24-hour Family Mart and 7-Eeven supermarket is suitable. Big C and Tesco Lotus are large chain stores and you can buy more solid items from them.

Excursion programs

Pattaya has its own originality in everything, including excursion service. There are no frozen museum exhibits, everything is filled with life, rituals, sacraments, in which tourists are given the opportunity to personally participate. Hotel tour desk Rose Bay Resort 3 * (Thailand) offers the following programs:

If you wish, you can then individually visit those places that have attracted interest. The excursion program includes: visiting the temple complex of Wat Yan, the Chinese temple of Viharn Sien, Mount Khao Chi Chan, the village of the Karen tribe (female giraffes), a snake farm, and an oceanarium.

Vacation Reviews

Hotel guests appreciate the quality of service at Rose Bay Resort 3 *. Reviews of tourists speak of quality service, convenient location of the hotel in relation to busy places. Good to know for travel

Travelers generously share their travel experiences in the Kingdom of Thailand. They advise:

  • When buying excursions, contact your local bureau, as this can significantly save money. It is only necessary to take into account the risk of non-payment of insurance if it is included in the price of a package tour. The operator is not responsible for the tourist in case of purchasing services from third parties.
  • Be sure to visit massage centers in Thailand. The quality here far exceeds the cost of the service.
  • The purchase of natural cosmetics will be a good gift for loved ones. In Thailand, there is severe punishment for a fake, so its quality does not change over time.
  • It is worth visiting the local souvenir shops where you can buy unique products for a small price: coconut, coral, leather.

Interesting facts about Thailand

  • The reckoning in the Kingdom of Thailand is carried out according to the Buddhist calendar. The difference with Gregorian is 543 years. If you want to meet the new year 2558, you should think about a trip to this mysterious country.
  • There is a monkey college in the city of Surat Thani. This once again confirms the proximity of these highly developed primates to humans.
  • Thailand is one of the ten most fun states on the planet. Here is the largest number of holidays and weekends. This is probably why Russian tourists love the country so much.
