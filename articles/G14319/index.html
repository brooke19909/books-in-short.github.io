Description of the recreation center "Birch cosiness"

The Birch Coziness complex is not just a recreation center. Here visitors can improve their health, visit medical procedures, relax with the whole family and even go fishing. The base is 50 kilometers from Kemerovo. You can get here by car, as well as by bus to the station "Village Kalinkino", and then transfer to your destination.

general information

The recreation center "Birch Coziness" (Kalinkino) offers its visitors maximum comfort and excellent conditions. Comfortable rooms equipped according to all the rules are designed for guests to stay. There is the necessary equipment, as well as flat-screen TVs. Visitors can use satellite TV and free internet.

Food on the basis of "Birch comfort" - in the form of a buffet. It offers modern and sought-after dishes made with fresh products.

The complex has a sauna, swimming pool, gym and phytocenter.

Wellness Services

The complex "Birch comfort" is well known to those people who value their health. Here you can try many useful procedures.

A branch of a phytocenter called Praskovya has been opened on the territory of the base. In it, specialists conduct treatment according to various author's techniques. Patients are advised to first go to a consultation to discuss with the doctor the presence of contraindications. If they are not, then visitors can sign up for manual therapy, lymphatic drainage, dry hydromassage, speleocamera, various types of baths and massages, stone therapy and much more. The center also provides treatment using essential oils and infusions of medicinal plants.

Additional features

Base "Birch cosiness" offers its guests to relax in full. Fishermen will also like it here. After all, they can fish at any time of the year.

On the territory of the base there is a banquet hall where you can arrange a wedding or a banquet. The complex also has special facilities (which are often used) for seminars. Sports enthusiasts can use the gym. There are football and volleyball courts on site. And for active games, there is a room with billiards.

For the beautiful half of humanity there is a SPA complex in which specialists offer skin and body care services. Children at the base also have something to do. A waiting room, playgrounds and other entertainment await them. For families, you can rent a bicycle or rent a gazebo with barbecue facilities.
