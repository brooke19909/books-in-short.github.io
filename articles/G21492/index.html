Pavlodar hotels: overview, rating, rooms, location and reviews of tourists

Pavlodar is one of the most beautiful and interesting cities located in the north-eastern part of Kazakhstan. Only 335 thousand people live here, but this does not prevent new hotel complexes from appearing. By the way, there are more than 20 hotels in Pavlodar, however it is extremely difficult to choose one of them that is really worthy, because the administrations of all the facilities claim that their establishment is one of the first to visit.

Today we will discuss in detail the best and most interesting hotels in Pavlodar, of which there are not many. We will also find out their exact addresses, approximate prices for room rental and other useful information that will definitely come in handy. So, we will begin to discuss the best hotels of Pavlodar!

"Dvin"

This tourist complex is one of the best in this city. The institution has only seven rooms, where there is everything for an excellent pastime. Wireless internet is available throughout the hotel, and the hotel itself is just 3 kilometers from the famous Irtysh River. At the same time, 1 km to the railway station of this village. It is also worth noting that a parking zone is equipped on the territory of the hotel complex, where any guest has the opportunity to leave his car for free.





Each room has a flat-screen TV and fridge. In addition, all rooms have private bathrooms, where for each client cosmetic products are offered. This hotel has many advantages, among which you should definitely highlight the possibility of ordering drinks and food directly to your room. At the same time, other hotels in Pavlodar cannot provide their guests with such an opportunity, and this is already a minus!

On the territory of the hotel complex there is an excellent restaurant "Dvin", which includes a small summer cafe. Anyone can enjoy the best culinary masterpieces of European and Armenian orientations there.

Reviews

Comments on RuNet, left by guests of this hotel complex, indicate perfect service. In addition, quite often hotel customers note the possibility of visiting 2 saunas and several indoor pools located on the territory of the Dvin project. The reception here is open around the clock, so the client will be able to clarify any information or ask questions to representatives of the mini-hotel at any time convenient for him.





Also, visitors to this hotel consider its location quite successful, because it is only 17 kilometers to go to the local airport.

Number of rooms

In this case, the tourist complex is represented by only 2 categories of rooms. Any visitor to the hotel can stay in a double room with one bed for 2 thousand 374 rubles. alone or for 2 thousand 499 rubles with his soul mate.





In addition, the same room is also available for rent, but with two twin beds. The cost of daily living is no different.

Be sure to note that the price of hotel accommodation includes breakfast in a local restaurant. By the way, this hotel is located in Zeleny Lane (40th house).

"Friendship"

If you are looking for inexpensive Pavlodar hotels, then this complex should suit you. This institution is one of the most unpopular in the city and does not have an official site, and the prices for accommodation here are quite low.





The Druzhba Hotel (Pavlodar) on Kutuzova Street (21st house, 1st building) includes a small restaurant, laundry room and a parking zone. Reviews of this hotel indicate an acceptable level of service and very low prices. Not all visitors are happy with this establishment, but it is still ideal for those who want to save money.

"North"

This tourist complex is also an interesting enough institution where you can stay in rooms of different categories. In this case, the hotel has only 63 rooms, each of which is equipped with everything necessary for living. In almost all rooms you can use a cable TV, telephone, hairdryer, minibar and shower. All rooms have air conditioning.





The Sever Hotel (Pavlodar) is open around the clock and is located only 2 kilometers from the city center at the following address: Mira Street, 39th Building. In addition, reviews about this complex are quite positive. People are satisfied with the good level of service and rental prices. In general, Sever is an interesting hotel with its own atmosphere!

Altyn Adam

This tourist destination is a mini-hotel where anyone can stay in one of the 6 rooms available for rent. In this case, excellent Wi-Fi works throughout the hotel, and for customers there is a special parking zone for cars.





Each of the hotel rooms has a modern interior and is equipped with all amenities for living: air conditioning, plasma TV and so on. Some rooms also have chic city or river views. It is also worth noting that all rooms have private bathrooms with a shower or bath. Each guest is individually provided with slippers and a bathrobe.

The registration desk works in the hotel around the clock, which means that you can check with the administration at any time. By the way, the distance to the local airport is quite small - only 13 kilometers.

Reviews and number of rooms

Such hotels in Pavlodar have low rental prices, otherwise there will be very few customers. You can stay at the Altyn Adam hotel in a double room with two single beds for only 2677 rubles.

At the same time, the cost of renting a spacious single room is exactly the same. By the way, please note that in both cases the price includes breakfast.





Comments by Runet users who have ever visited the Altyn Adam hotel indicate an excellent level of service. Clients of the hotel leave positive feedback, which refers to the fact that there is a modern interior and a fairly reasonable price policy. In general, the complex is excellent!

Finally, we note that this institution is located on Yestaya street (30th house).

Pavlodar hotels: rating

So, our article is already drawing to a close, so let's make a short rating of the best tourist resorts in this city, which are worth paying attention to those who plan to have a great time in this city:

  1. "Dvin."
  2. "Altyn Adam."
  3. Respect (Toraigyrova Street, 79th Building, 1st Building).
  4. "Sary Arka" (Toraigyrova street, 1).
  5. "North".
  6. "Friendship".

Have a nice rest and good mood!
