Eastiny Place Hotel 3 *, Pattaya: tourists photos and reviews

Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * is a rather modest, but very cozy, and most importantly quiet, calm and comfortable hotel on the very shore of the Gulf of Thailand, in the central part of Pattaya, in the most popular and famous resort of Thailand.

Introduction

The hotel is always ready to meet and receive its guests, its doors are open 24 hours a day. The staff of the Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) - surprisingly cute, friendly and smiling people. Here you are sure to enjoy it!



The grand opening of the Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) took place in 2004, and since then for many years in a row light has not gone out in its windows, it receives guests at any time and delights them with its coziness, comfort and unforgettable hospitality.

Do you dream of a calm and quiet family vacation? Or vice versa, love nightlife, but prefer to sleep off after an active and fun night in the bustling center of the resort in silence, without constantly disturbing extraneous noise? In any case, Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * is just the perfect option for you and your friends! Now let's talk about this institution in more detail.

Rooms

Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) is quite small by the standards of Thailand. It has a total of 87 rooms of various categories.

The rooms are quite spacious and bright, in a traditionally elegant European style with notes of the East. Here you will find 53 Standard rooms (one room area is 30 m 2 ), 32 Superior rooms with the same area as standard rooms, but with an improved layout (these rooms are renovated and furnished with updated furniture) and 2 rooms of the Junior Suite category , its area is 42 m2.

Number of rooms

All categories of apartments can comfortably accommodate a maximum of 3 people. All rooms, without exception, have private balconies.



Each room has the necessary set of quality furniture made of wood. All are equipped with bathrooms (with bath or shower), with all necessary toiletries and a hairdryer.

Each room has a TV with one channel in Russian, high-speed Wi-Fi Internet (on request for an additional fee), telephone, mini fridge or minibar (empty, filled for an additional fee on request), air conditioning.

Rooms are not equipped with individual safes. If necessary, you can rent a safe at the reception.

Additional services

The price of your stay at Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) includes good continental breakfasts. Very hearty and tasty. Guests can have breakfast in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere in a cozy restaurant overlooking the hotel grounds. The restaurant is open daily from 7.00 to 23.00, which is certainly very convenient. The institution is designed for only 60 people, that is, your breakfast will be held in a calm and cozy atmosphere.



And if during the day you want to taste a deliciously delicious non-alcoholic or alcoholic cocktail or enjoy a cup of fragrant cappuccino, then you will always be welcome in the pool bar. Opening hours - daily, from 8.00 to 19.00.

Territory

Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * (Pattay) has a rather small compact area. Nevertheless, this does not prevent her from being pretty and sweet.

But it is important to remember that, having flown to rest in a country of smiles, amazing in every sense of the word, it makes no sense to spend all your magical and long-awaited vacation without leaving the territory of the hotel.

Go to the pristine beaches of the islands, attend interesting excursions, walk and have fun, dance and light the nights on Walking Street. And come back to the hotel to take a shower, change clothes and spend the night. But if you suddenly get tired of activity and drive and decide to take a break and spend one day without leaving the hotel, then you definitely will not be bored.

The hotel has a fairly developed infrastructure: an outdoor pool, near which tourists have a great time sunbathing, reading a fascinating book or chatting with friends. Umbrellas and sun loungers are provided to hotel guests free of charge.

The restaurant and pool bar will delight you with a variety of alcoholic drinks of local and foreign production, as well as a large selection of soft drinks, alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails.



Have a desire to relax and relax? Entrust yourself to the strong and gentle professional hands of Thai masseuses at the Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) spa. Here, in addition to the world-famous Thai massage, you can get a full range of spa treatments.

Excursions

At the reception with the hotel administrator you can get full and professional advice on guided tours around the country and at the resort, as well as immediately order your favorite.

Or maybe you want to ride a bike around the resort? No problem, here, at the reception, you can rent a scooter at any time. They will tell you everything in great detail, prompt and help. Remember: Thailand is a country of smiles, sincere, joyful and open.

By the way, the hotel has a small shop where you can buy everything you need.

Benefits

Another important and undeniable advantage of the Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * hotel (Thailand, Pattaya) is, of course, its very convenient location. The hotel is approximately 150 km from the airport. For example, if you arrived at the resort on a previously purchased tour package, then you will be met by a group or individual transfer at the airport (usually these are large comfortable buses or no less comfortable minibuses).

All the way to the hotel you will be accompanied by an employee of the host company in Thailand, who during the movement will share with you important and necessary, and most importantly, very interesting information about the country, the resort and Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * (Pattaya).



Independent travelers arriving in Thailand is not the first time, most often upon arrival or pre-book a taxi that will take them to the hotel. To do this, it is enough to tell the driver the exact address of the hotel, in this case this: 327/2 Moo 10, Eastiny Place Hotel 3 *, Pattaya Beach Road, Soi 10, Pattaya City.

Beaches

The distance from the hotel to the famous Pattaya Beach in Thailand is only 250-300 meters. Municipal beach, the use of umbrellas and sunbeds - for an additional fee. Most tourists go to island beaches. They are clean, beautiful and with well-developed infrastructure. The islands can be easily reached by boat, scheduled to depart from the pier. You will need to walk a bit from the hotel. But it is not very far. Just go to Beach Road, turn left and go straight all the time. As soon as Beach Road ends and Walking Street begins, you will immediately see the marinas from which the boats depart.

Walk from Eastiny Place Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) to the piers and to the beginning of Walking Street for about 15-20 minutes with a slow walking step.

Walkin Street is Thailand's most famous street. It is never quiet here. There is noise and fun around the clock, discos and various entertainments take place. Come here to hang out, have fun and have fun young people and just fun and active people from around the world.
