Tools and materials

To create such a gift you will not need something special and expensive. Perhaps you have everything you need at home, you just don‚Äôt know how it can be used for decorative purposes.

Look at the photo. Flowers from cotton pads (calla lilies, roses) look so natural that they can become a worthy decoration of the room. To make them, you need the following:

  • cotton pads and sticks;
  • paints, brushes;
  • scissors;
  • glue;
  • starch;
  • plasticine;
  • threads
  • wire;

Work principles

You can make one flower or a whole composition of cotton pads with your own hands: an applique or a voluminous bouquet. The source material is used white or stained in any color, drawing veins on the petals and making transitions of several shades.



The middle of the flowers and the stalks are made of cotton buds dipped in paint. Also use beads and wire frame. To give the green shade to the stalk, wrapping in colored stripes, applying plasticine are used. Leaves can be cut from cotton pads, fabric, satin ribbons, made from salt dough, polymer clay, or taken ready-made from artificial flowers.

Using cotton pads is quite simple. They can be bent, folded, cut the desired shape. Before starting the process, the material can be processed in starch. To connect the parts of the flower, its elements, use threads, wire, thermal gun or PVA glue.

Cotton pad application : flowers
(children's creativity)

This option is suitable as an activity for making a gift for mom. Guys can perform daisies, snowdrops or just abstract flowers.

The work will consist of the following:

  1. Take the base (color cardboard, paper, plastic). It is better to draw the contours of the future bouquet on it.
  2. Make leaves and stems from plasticine.
  3. To make snowdrop petals, it is better to make a stencil from cardboard and cut out the required number of colors from cotton pads. So, they will all be the same and neat. Chamomile is even easier to perform. Enough cuts in a circle of cotton pad.
  4. The middle can be made of a cotton swab, the head of which is painted with gouache, and the base is coated with plasticine. The same can be done with beads and wire.
  5. In a cotton pad of petals, make a slot for a cotton swab and insert it there.
  6. Collect all the elements based on. As a decor, create a pot or vase in the form of an application of beans, beads, sequins, plasticine.

Calla

This cotton bud flower is made as follows:

  1. Color the cotton bud in yellow.
  2. Collapse a white circle in the shape of a flower. You can pre-cut it by sharpening.
  3. Connect the stick to the workpiece from the disk. With the help of threads or wire fasten them together.
  4. Wrap the stalk with a green ribbon or coat with plasticine.
  5. Leaves and decor do as you wish.

Roses

Craft "cotton bud flowers" can look very natural. The main thing is to try to imitate the shape of the petals of the selected plant.

Naturalness gives a corresponding shade. For roses, unpainted cotton pads are also suitable. It will take more time, patience and the blanks themselves. In this case, it is better to connect them with a thermogun. Sepals can be made from fabric, thin decorative paper or satin ribbons.

Pansies

To make such a flower from cotton pads, you will first need to color them in the appropriate colors. Take samples with realistic images as samples. To create a form, you will have to trim the material. This is best done after joining the petals into the workpiece.

Daffodils

This cotton pad flower looks very beautiful and natural. To make it, you will need several white circles, a sample of a photorealistic image, long strips of green paper to create leaves. Plasticine in this case is not suitable. It will be difficult to make a lanceolate elongated three-dimensional form of small thickness from it.
