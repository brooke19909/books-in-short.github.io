The best navigator for a car

So many electronic gadgets are so firmly entrenched in our reality that we cannot imagine our existence without them. Some of them are just funny toys, but there are some that allow us to expand our capabilities, make our life more convenient and comfortable.



These gadgets include a GPS navigator. This is a device that receives and processes a signal from GPS satellites, which determines its location and, accordingly, the location of the car. This device only receives but does not send signals. The accuracy of the readings will always depend on the number of satellites available to it. After determining the location, he makes a route to the destination of the movement. GPS-navigators allow you to download only those maps that are compatible with their programs.

Today, many manufacturers offer a wide selection of portable on-board devices. We can say that their whole sea. It remains to be seen how to really catch the best navigator in this sea. Because they are so similar in appearance and function that sometimes it‚Äôs very difficult to understand.

Start choosing the best navigator from the display. The optimal size is five inches. A device with a larger display is worth buying only when you plan to use it as a video player. This option is relevant for people spending a lot of time behind the wheel. Display resolutions for continuous use are enough 480x427 pixels, but for some connoisseurs there is an option of 800x480 pixels.



The best GPS navigator should have a good and detailed map. This choice can only be made by the buyer himself, as some users vote for Progorod cards, another for Navitel, and Garmin navigators and other devices of large manufacturers have their own cards.

In addition to the main technological features, there are many more additional features. In the navigator can be integrated: magnetic barometer, compass, GSM-module, multimedia functionality and a DVR.

How to figure out if it should all contain the best navigator? The presence of Bluetooth, GPRS or Wi-Fi will help to obtain information about the presence of traffic jams, and also allow you to use the device as a tablet. If the car already has an audio system that can support these functions, then you should not overpay.

The best navigator should be stuffed with the most advanced hardware platform, something like SirfAtlas V or from MTK. It is desirable that there is 128 MB of RAM and 16 GB of memory card. This is necessary if you intend to use the device as a multimedia device.

It is not possible to give preference to any particular manufacturer, since basically the quality of navigators and their technical characteristics are close to each other. Here the choice is left to the buyer and his preferences.
