Pattaya Attractions

Crown Pattaya Beach Hotel is located in Pattaya. What interesting places can a traveler visit? Pattaya has relatively few attractions that can be described as belonging to the traditional culture and history of Thailand. Travelers often come to this city in order to find entertainment in modern formats. If the tourist's goal is Crown Pattaya Beach 3 (Thailand, Pattaya), his reviews will most likely be related to how he had fun at the bar or at the disco. But in the spectrum of Pattaya's attractions there are wonderful places, the visit of which can give pleasure to both lovers of noisy parties and travelers who prefer a measured, relaxing holiday.

A great place to hang out is Nong Nooch Garden . It is considered one of the largest and most beautiful in all of Southeast Asia. You can stroll through this garden for a long time and slowly, admiring the surrounding beauties. Here orchids, palm trees and a huge number of varieties of other tropical plants grow, butterflies fly. Also, an unusual spectacle is organized for visitors to the garden - an elephant show.

For tourists, the Temple on the Buddha Hill is open. Travelers from all over the world are eager to visit here. On the Hill is the largest Buddha statue in Pattaya. It offers a beautiful view of the city itself, as well as its magnificent bay.

Another famous attraction of Pattaya is the temple "Sanctuary of Truth." It is located in the northern part of the city. The temple is unusual in that it is built of wood. Its height is impressive - 105 m. Its entire surface is covered with interesting carved sculptures.

The most beautiful temple, Wat Yang, is open for the traveler. It has an unusual and at the same time elegant appearance. The temple was built relatively recently, in 1976. Therefore, in its appearance there are elements characteristic of modern architectural concepts.

The traveler will like the Mini Siam Park, which is famous for having copies of world-famous architectural structures on display for the public. Mini Siam also has a beautiful garden. Russian architecture is represented in the park by St. Basil‚Äôs Cathedral, as well as a monument on Mamaev Kurgan, which was erected in honor of the defenders of Stalingrad.

A great place to spend time is the Underwater World Aquarium. It contains the inhabitants of the Gulf of Thailand. The aquarium is a 105 meter tunnel. Rare and very beautiful species of fish and turtles live here. There are fish from the Amazon River.

Pattaya's most popular attractions include Elephant Village. Here, majestic animals learn the most diverse human skills: playing football, riding a car, mastering musical instruments. There is an opportunity to ride an elephant through the jungle.

Pattaya has unique floating markets. In their modern form, they are more likely an attraction for travelers than a place for active trade. But here a person interested in Thai culture can always buy an interesting souvenir and try a national dish.

Among the recently appeared sights of the city is a museum of 3D paintings. It presents more than 150 exhibits. They are called upon to create in man the illusion that in front of him is precisely the object that is depicted in the picture. Museum exhibits can be photographed.
