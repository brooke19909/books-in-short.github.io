Cheap hotels of Ulyanovsk: description, location, reviews

Ulyanovsk is a beautiful city located on the banks of the mighty Volga River. It was here that V.I. Ulyanov-Lenin was born, who was destined to turn world history around, creating the world's first socialist state. But not only this is famous for the metropolis. Beautiful nature, favorable climate, unforgettable river cruises and numerous attractions attract travelers. In addition, the city is a large industrial and business center. Thousands of tourists and businessmen come here daily for whom the doors of cozy but inexpensive hotels in Ulyanovsk are open.

The city is located in the heart of the Volga region, on both banks of the vast Kuibyshev reservoir. It is not surprising that the infrastructure for recreation is well developed here. It is not for nothing that the river is called the Volga Mother. You can go fishing here at any time of the year and sunbathe on the vast sandy beaches in summer.
