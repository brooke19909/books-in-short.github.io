Hotel Aquarius 3 * (Budva, Montenegro, Budva Riviera): description, reviews

Montenegro, once a part of Yugoslavia, has few seaside resorts, because the length of its coastline is only 300 km. Of these, one-fourth of the equipped beaches account for 73 km. But despite this, the rest here is wonderful. We invite you to look into the small family-run hotel Aquarius 3 *. Budva is the city where it is located, and at the same time the best and most fun resort in the country. There are many places for entertainment, many narrow old streets, which are so interesting to walk along, many gorgeous beaches and even more attractions that give the city a special highlight. The owners of our hotel are pleased to all guests and try to make their stay in the country pleasant and comfortable.

Location

Without exaggeration, in each review of tourists it is reported about the convenient location of Aquarius 3 *. Budva is located approximately in the middle of the coastline. This is one of the smallest regions of Montenegro. On the one hand, it is washed by the Adriatic Sea, on the other three, the resort towns of Kotor and Bar adjoin it, as well as the second capital of the country, Cetinje, which does not have access to the coast. Hotel guests can easily visit all these amazingly beautiful places, since the distances here are small. So, to Kotor - only 22 km, and to Bar - about 35 km. The bus station is located from Hotel Aquarius ten minutes away. Also very close are the market, the Old Town and the coast.

Airplanes from Russia fly to Podgorica only in season, and the distance from there to our hotel is about 52 km. But the path lies through the famous Skadar Lake.

There are more flights to Tivat from Russia, moreover, they exist not only from Moscow, but also from Samara, Yekaterinburg, Nizhny Novgorod, and St. Petersburg. Description

Hotel Aquarius is not just urban, but located on one of the streets near the center of Budva. The hotel occupies a corner position, so roads run from both sides - one with more busy traffic, and the other a narrow and very quiet one. There is not much parking space in front of the main entrance to the Aquarius, but the hotel staff does not look at the guests' cars. The territory in "Aquarius" is as compact as possible. In fact, it occupies the entire hotel building, surrounded by flowers and a small garden with evergreen trees. Under their canopy, a recreation area is equipped. There is no pool at this hotel.

Infrastructure

Hotel "Aquarius" is very small. In the lobby of his only four-story building, on the second floor, there is a reception. The hostess of this institution sometimes speaks good Russian. Basically, at the front desk there is staff who understands only their mother tongue and English. At the reception, guests can use the safe, take an iron, call a taxi, check bus routes, buy a tour, invite a doctor. The lobby has a wonderful corner with sofas and armchairs. It is convenient to just relax or sit on the Internet using free Wi-Fi.



General characteristics of the rooms

In Aquarius 3 * (Budva) there are two buildings - A and B. According to tourists, the second is somewhat more comfortable than the first. There are only 19 rooms in the hotel, floors are 4. There is no elevator, you can get into your room only through intricate staircases, which are an integral part of the building design, and you can get to the first floor only by first going up to the reception and then going down again , but already on another, small staircase. Every single room in the hotel is different both in area and in design. Some are very spacious, others are too compact, some have bathtubs, others have showers, and others have only a shower with a tray and a curtain. Another difference is the balconies. In some rooms they are not at all, and in some there are very spacious terraces. All this must be clarified when booking. But the views from the windows of all rooms are approximately the same. They can be on the road, neighboring hotels and city buildings. They clean the hotel very high quality, linen and towels are changed every other day, hygiene products provided on the day of arrival are regularly replenished. The hotel rooms are so different that we decided to dwell on their description in more detail.

Additional Information

Hotel "Aquarius" is designed for active guests of any age who come to Montenegro, to Budva, to get acquainted with the beauty of this land, for those who do not plan to spend much time in the room, for those who are not spoiled by the impeccable service of "fives". For children in the hotel in question there is absolutely nothing. Parents must take care of the comfort of their little ones on their own. For young people, there is nothing in Aquarius itself, but there are many bars and discos nearby, on the neighboring streets. In addition, music festivals and theatrical performances are often held in Budva.

Tourists who want to get acquainted with the country can take dozens of excursions both in the hotel and in city travel agencies. The most popular is a trip to the island of St. Nicholas. But you can go there yourself, paying only 3 euros for a ticket on a ferry. The beaches here are pebble, sunbeds and umbrellas are expensive, from 15 euros per set.
