Screen

The Prestigio Wize G3 display has a diagonal of 5 inches, which, in principle, is already a certain standard. But the resolution let us down. Instead of the expected 1280 x 720, here you can find 854 x 480 with a pixel density of 198 ppi per inch. The matrix here is also used not IPS, but cheap TN with all that it implies.

To put it mildly, the display on a smartphone is not the strongest place. Viewing angles are limited, the color rendition is so-so. The balance is more biased towards cold shades. There is no manual correction. Plus, under different slopes, the image slightly changes color, and with a fairly strong deviation, the picture starts to ‚Äú fade ‚Äù. In general, all diseases of the TN matrix are present. The oleophobic coating, by the way, is not bad, the finger glides well.





If we talk about the margin of brightness, then at home or in cloudy weather the information on the screen will be visible well, but in bright sunshine you will have to look carefully.

Multitouch support is present. At the same time, the smartphone can recognize up to three clicks - this is better than nothing.

Android version and shell

The Prestigio smartphone is delivered with the Android version 6. 0 on board. Optimization is a little lame, in some places slowdowns are visible. This is especially noticeable when scrolling through the application screen, contact list, long text and opening the dialer .

As for the shell, Prestigio does not have its launcher or UI, so Android has a standard or, as it is also called, ‚Äú naked ‚Äù look.

The device also has no third-party or ‚Äú custom ‚Äù firmware, and, judging by everything, it will not. At w3bsit3-dns.com , Prestigio Wize G3 in the general branch of the company's devices is not at all, and the chance that it will appear there is extremely small.

Camera

The main camera module of the Prestigio Wize G3 has a resolution of 5 megapixels and is complemented by a small LED flash. Shooting modes are standard here, you should not look for any innovations . Among the settings, only the change in white balance, exposure and metering is available . There is no autofocus, which only makes it difficult to get blurry photos.





The very quality of the final pictures leaves much to be desired (this can be seen in the photo above and below). In good light or in bright, clear weather, photos turn out to be bright with good color rendering, but the lack of sharpness turns everything into a solid soap. Also quite noticeable is the work of noise reduction, especially in shaded places.

Surprisingly, the smartphone has a function for shooting in HDR mode, but, given the capabilities of the camera, this is more like a mockery.





The front camera, as already mentioned, has a resolution of 0.3 megapixels, and its ability is limited only by communication via Skype or other instant messengers with video call support.

Performance

The processor in the Prestigio Wize G3 is installed by Spreadtrum, model SC 7731 . This is a fairly cheap analogue of the MediaTek MT 6735 P. In terms of performance, it is slightly worse, but it will do for a budget device. SC 7731 operates at a clock frequency of 1, 2 GHz . Considering not very good optimization and not too fast processor, which is supplemented with only 1 GB of RAM, you can sometimes observe small slowdowns and slightly long transitions.





Graphic video acceleration in Wize G3 installed Mali 400-MP 2 . There is nothing special to say about him. Budget, copes with light toys, including simple 3D-graphics.

In Antutu, the smartphone was able to score 11,200 points, which is quite good for a state employee. In the Epic Citadel benchmark, the final average FPS was 36 frames / sec., Which, in principle, allows you to play some games at high settings. However, during the benchmark itself, very strong drawdowns were observed, up to 20 fps.





The bottom line is that for some simple arcades and timekillers the smartphone is suitable, but it will be extremely difficult to play comfortably in Need For Speed, WoT or Asphalt even at low settings.

Battery life

The battery of the Prestigio Wize G3 has a capacity of 2000 mA / h. Not a particularly record figure, but if you look from the other side, then this volume will be more than enough. The fact is that you won‚Äôt especially play games on this device, since performance is not so hot. What is left? Surfing on the Internet, social networks, instant messengers, watching online videos, well, calls + SMS. You can also listen to music or the radio.





So, for all these tasks, a 2000mAh battery is enough. Of course, with an intense load, the phone is unlikely to survive until the evening, but with a moderate average it will last for a day.

Reviews

Finally, one of the main parts of the review is the Prestigio Wize G3 reviews. Among the pluses, users note a good design, bright colors, assembly, the quality of the speaker, energy-saving mode and fast charging.

Cons are quite predictable: weak cameras, slowdowns , small viewing angles at the screen. Also, for some users, firmware fails. There are freezes that can be corrected only by juggling the battery, as well as self-resetting several times a day. Some in this situation helped update the firmware.

Conclusion

What can be said about the new product from Prestigio ? The phone turned out to be a solid C grade. On the one hand, low cost, "Android 6.0", a 5-inch screen, holds a good battery, good reception and communication quality. Also pleased with the design, assembly and mediocre performance.





But if you look at the reviews about the Prestigio Wize G3, it immediately becomes clear what the modern user lacks: a better screen, HD resolution, a camera with autofocus and at least 8 megapixels, a good front-end, a little more productive hardware and a stable version of the firmware. A metal case would also be a plus, but this is nitpicking.

Prestigio Wize G3 is a pretty good option that will work out 100% of its cost and functionality .
