The Lenovo company is strictly segmenting its products in the mobile device market. Series A are budget smartphones with modest settings and a very affordable price. The devices of the S series are positioned as high-performance and expensive gadgets. Well, the P line has an increased battery capacity and a very long battery life. It is to the last group of mobile devices that Lenovo P90 Pro belongs. Reviews in addition to the increased battery capacity highlight another number of important advantages of this particular phone model. This is an improved hardware platform with an increased level of speed, and a large number of both RAM, and the capacity of the built-in storage of programs and data, and a very high-quality camera. Well, given the value declared by the manufacturer, the gadget has every chance to become a bestseller in the niche of mid-range smartphones.

CPU

A very productive semiconductor chip is the basis of such a device as the Lenovo P90 Pro smartphone. The reviews of experts and owners are primarily allocated by the manufacturer of this silicon solution - Intel. In the server and desktop segment, it has no equal. But in the composition of mobile phones, its products are not so often found. But this does not mean that it is bad. The Android OS has long been successfully optimized for x86 processors. So there is nothing to worry about. The specific CPU model in this case is the Atom Z3560. Its frequency potential is limited to 1.86 GHz. It consists of 4 x86 cores at once, and it can process exactly the same amount of program threads. The chip itself is produced according to a manufacturing process with tolerances of 22 nm, and its level 3 cache is 2 MB. The maximum temperature that is acceptable for a CPU semiconductor crystal is 90 0 . From all the above characteristics, we can conclude: this processor will be difficult to compete with the most efficient processors based on AWP architecture (for example, Snapdragon 8xx series), but as a part of average gadgets level it will just look great. Its computing capabilities in this case will be more than enough for comfortable work and solving most typical tasks.

The graphics component of the central processor

There is also a graphics accelerator in the Lenovo P90 Pro. Reviews, a description of its technical specifications indicate that this is a mid-range solution. His model is Power VR6. The clock frequencies of this semiconductor solution range from 457 to 533 MHz. This product is able to cope with the tasks of the middle level. But to complete the most demanding three-dimensional toys, it will not be enough.

Integrated Memory Subsystem Parameters

One of the key advantages compared to competitors is the amount of RAM in the Lenovo P90 Pro - 4Gb RAM. The reviews, however, indicate that after the launch of the system software, the majority (about 3 GB) remains free and the owner of the mobile device can use it at his discretion. Type of RAM - LP DDR3 with an operating frequency of 1600 MHz. Of course, against the background of modern PCs already equipped with DDR4, this characteristic looks rather modest, but most mobile gadgets are still based on this outdated form of RAM, and it still continues to be relevant in this niche.

The capacity of the built-in drive for storing information and installing application software is 64 GB (this can be found out easily and simply from the name of the smartphone model). Some of them are initially filled with pre-installed software, but most of them are allotted to the needs of the gadget owner. There is simply no slot for an additional external memory card. As a result, you have to be content with exactly what is already there. In extreme cases, you can resort to cloud services and store the most valuable files there.
