So, to make the assassin's blade with your own hands, we need the following:



  • Lace.
  • Paper.
  • Scotch.
  • Scissors.
  • Threads.
  • Toothpick.

1. Turn the paper into a tube and fix it with tape. Do not make it too narrow.

2. We crush or flatten on both sides, so that we get a box.

3. We attach the blank to the hand and measure out the required amount. The length should be approximately equal to half the distance from the hand to the elbow. Cut off the necessary part, and put off the rest.

4. Now we begin the main part of considering how to make an assassin's blade with your own hands. We strengthen our box with tape. In the middle of the sides we make cuts, which we also fix.

5. We take more paper and twist it into a tube of a smaller diameter. We fix as in paragraph 1.

6. Flatten as well as a large workpiece, then divide into several small parts, more precisely, into two.

7. One of the blanks is sealed on one side and cut out on the other in the shape of a concave arc. Stuff some paper inside.

8. The second workpiece, the end of which is cut in the shape of a dagger, is inserted into the first. We glue.





9. We proceed to the next part of the description of how to make an assassin's blade with your own hands. We take a toothpick, with glue we fix it at the base of our paper knife.

10. Connect our hidden blade to the box. Insert the ends of the toothpicks into the slots. We eliminate defects by sticking paper into the box on both sides of the blade (top and bottom).
