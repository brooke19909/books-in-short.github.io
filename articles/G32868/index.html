Microwave convection: what is it? Convection and Grill Microwave

If you decide to change your old microwave oven to a new one, then the variety of models today will pleasantly surprise you. The most widely used microwave ovens are convection (reviews about them are only positive). But before acquiring such a model, let's first look: convection in a microwave oven, what is it all about.

Principle of operation

The very concept of "convection" means the transfer of heat through circular movements of the heated air. This method of transferring thermal energy is used not only in household appliances, this method is very relevant in large enterprises, in industry.

Convection microwaves not only allow you to heat or defrost foods. In such ovens you can cook delicious pastries, bake fish or meat.

Many microwave models allow you to use the device in several modes: standard (in which you can only heat or defrost food) or combined (in this mode, you can also fully cook or bake products in the microwave).

There is a combined microwave with convection and grill. A fairly functional household appliance. With this oven you can even cook juicy grilled ribs.

The main differences

For the correct and optimal choice of microwave oven model, it is important to know what are the main differences between this device and similar devices. It is worth noting that conventional microwave ovens heat food using infrared radiation. New generation microwave ovens not only heat food using infrared rays, they, using convection, help to cook it deliciously.

In addition, convection microwave ovens (customer reviews confirm this) have large dimensions and higher power consumption. They are equipped with an additional fan and a special element that heats the air. These components are located on the back wall or at the top of the microwave.

Because of this, the furnace has significant dimensions and greater weight than its counterparts with a simpler device. But then it has additional functions that allow you to cook and bake in it.



Thanks to the convection method of using heat, all products are well fried and baked, and during the preparation of the bird a delicate crisp is obtained. If we talk about the time of cooking, then it will have to spend much less, when compared with the time that goes into cooking meat in the oven. Thanks to the convection function, a conventional microwave oven turns into a full-fledged oven.

Therefore, first of all, attention should be paid specifically to the power indicator of the device. Security

So, now you can answer the question: "Convection in the microwave: what is it?" - and, accordingly, decide whether you need it. If the answer is yes, then make sure that it is correctly installed.

Carefully read the passport that came with the device. Be sure to consider the possibilities of your wiring. When using powerful appliances in private homes, wiring is sometimes quite weak and may simply not withstand such a large load. Therefore, there is a risk that the cork in the house will be constantly "cut down".

Grounding

Typically, manufacturers indicate recommendations for installing the device in the instrument passport. The need for grounding when connecting a convection oven is one of them. This is because such powerful devices must be earthed to avoid accidents. It is up to you to decide, but it is advisable not to ignore the instructions of the manufacturer and once again protect yourself.

Installation

Before you go to the store to buy a microwave oven with convection, think about where you will put it. Remember that such devices have rather large dimensions, and there should be enough free space around the oven.

Convection microwave oven must have air access from all sides. Installing the device close to the wall is also prohibited. You can not put any objects on top of the device, since during cooking it can become very hot. Ignoring such simple requirements can lead to the fact that your device will fail prematurely.

The lack of free space may cause the device to simply overheat and burn. Items left at the top of the oven can ignite at high temperatures, and this is a direct path to the fire.

Exploitation

The microwave convection mode, what is it, you know, so try to operate the device correctly and follow the basic rules. After each cooking, the oven must be cleaned or wiped with a damp sponge. It will be easier for you to get rid of dirt and soot in the device immediately after cooking. If the dirt is too strong, use only special cleaning products that do not scratch the surface. Do not use drugs that contain alkali.

Try to follow the instructions recommended by the manufacturer. Use special utensils and do not experiment. As practice shows, experiments with such devices lead only to their quick exit of their system.

When choosing, pay attention to the display of the device. This can be a fully digital panel or manual adjustment of cooking modes. There are models that have built-in recipes for cooking certain dishes. All that is required of you is to enter the weight, type of product and set a timer.
