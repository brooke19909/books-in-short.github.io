Hotel Starlet Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): reviews, description, photo

Starlet Hotel 3 * in Vietnamese Nha Trang is a fairly new elegant establishment where every tourist will feel comfortable and cozy. Here you will discover all the delights of an exotic holiday.

Brief description of the resort hotel

Taking into account the relatively low cost of living, it is worth noting that the resort Starlet Hotel 3 * offers its guests quite decent living conditions. Her rooms are an example of style, cleanliness and comfort. There is a huge range of amenities without which a modern tourist simply cannot imagine his life. The apartments are thoroughly cleaned daily and bed linen and towels are replaced.



At Starlet Hotel 3 * you will be provided with many services, as well as opportunities for recreation and entertainment. So, one of the pleasant moments can be considered free access to wireless Internet. Moreover, the signal extends not only to the public areas of the building, but also to all available rooms. In addition, you will have the opportunity to book a fascinating tour, get advice from reception staff and many other services.

If you want to plunge into the Vietnamese culture as deep as possible, you certainly need to get acquainted with the cuisine of this original country. The Starlet Hotel 3 * restaurant, which offers buffet breakfasts, as well as a huge selection of dishes on the menu, will help you in this. If local culinary traditions seem too exotic to you, you will always have the opportunity to order your usual European dishes. In the event that you do not want to go down to the restaurant, the waiters will be happy to deliver your food directly to your room.

Where is the resort located

Perhaps the advantageous location can be considered one of the main advantages of the resort hotel Starlet Hotel 3 *. Vietnam is famous for its beaches. Nha Trang - one of the most famous - is just a 5-minute leisurely walk from the hotel. It is worth noting that the hotel is located in the central part of the city, which is a huge plus for those who like to relax in crowded places. There are many shops, markets, restaurants, bars, cafes and entertainment centers.

Useful information about hotel accommodation

Any resort hotel has a set of rules to which both employees and hotel guests must comply. Starlet Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) is no exception. Reviews of tourists indicate the following features:

  • Registration of newly arrived guests is carried out from two in the afternoon until midnight, but on the day of departure you must have time to solve all organizational issues in the period from 11:30 to noon;
  • pets are strictly prohibited in the hotel, regardless of their size, weight, or breed;
  • Children under the age of 6 years stay in the room free of charge - provided that they do not need an extra bed, and children under two years old are provided with cribs;
  • extra bed will cost $ 20 per night;
  • you can pay for accommodation and additional services in cashless form using a plastic card;
  • upon check-in, a security deposit will be collected from you, which can be paid both in cash and non-cash;
  • during registration, you must provide a photo document proving your identity.

Negative reviews

Despite the general positive picture outlined by the guests of the Starlet Hotel 3 * hotel, its reviews also contain some negative comments:

  • some kind of sluggish staff in terms of response to complaints and requests from guests;
  • the furnishings, furniture, appliances and everything else are incredibly old and shabby, and therefore the hotel urgently needs repairs if it wants to keep customers;
  • no matter how many guests are accommodated in the room, they give out only one key card (as a result, if one person needs to leave, the second needs to wait for him in the room or sit without light);
  • the balconies in the rooms are absolutely not adapted to spend time on them (there are no tables or armchairs), and there is also no dryer or at least an elementary clothesline;
  • the gym is very crowded, there is no ventilation system, because of which there simply is nothing to breathe;
  • the lobby area is incredibly small - there is practically none;
  • maids do not consider it necessary to do wet cleaning (at best, it will be done once a week);
  • despite the fact that the hotel has two elevators, quite often there was such a situation that only one works, which is why large queues were created;
  • water is periodically turned off (both hot and cold), but in most cases the problem is solved within a couple of hours;
  • carefully monitor how much is withdrawn from your card when paying for services (it is not uncommon that, counting on the carelessness of guests, employees write off more than necessary);
  • the air conditioning in the room is so "ancient" and weak that it practically does not cool the air due to its clogging;
  • the television shows very poorly - it snows and fonit, and the channels are almost all local (only in some rooms the Russian Channel One catches).

conclusions

When choosing a hotel for yourself, it is best to rely on the reviews of tourists who have visited many places. So, a quite popular place to stay is the resort Starlet Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang). Reviews indicate that the main reasons for this choice are a favorable location and affordable price. The hotel is just a couple of minutes walk from the beach in the city center. But the low price is due to the dilapidation of the hotel.

Nevertheless, there are many positive aspects. One of them is breakfast, which is incredibly varied and satisfying, compared to other similar institutions in Vietnam. In addition, the hotel guests are provided with all the necessary list of related services: a gym, a swimming pool, rental bicycles, and organization of excursions and more. Separately, it is worth noting the gorgeous views from the window, which slightly smooth out the negative from outdated room decoration.

This hotel can be recommended to those tourists who go for vivid impressions, and not for luxury. For the minimum cost, you will get quite decent living conditions, saving a certain amount for entertainment and excursions.
