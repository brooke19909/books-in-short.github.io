Crafts from plastic bottles - palm and other products

Each family in large quantities acquires mineral water or lemonade. Especially in the hot season. Such drinks are traditionally bottled in plastic bottles, since in this case the cost of the goods sold is much lower. The used packaging is then simply thrown away, although the material used in its manufacture can harm the environment, as it does not decompose in the ground.



If desired, using such waste, you can create crafts from plastic bottles. A palm is the most common option for their use. Such an exhibition will certainly become one of the main decorations of a personal plot or courtyard near a multi-storey building. Considering that household waste is actually used to implement the plan, the costs in this case will be minimal.

If a palm tree is chosen as a craft from plastic bottles, then in order for it to keep its shape well, it is also necessary to take care of the reinforcing material. If the finished product is small, then enough wire will be enough. If you plan to make a tree about two meters high, you will need either reinforcement or a log. The reinforcing element is placed inside, and around it are plastic bottles. They can be worn on a wire or simply attached to a wooden trunk.



If a palm tree is chosen as a craft from plastic bottles, then in the process of its manufacture it is also necessary to carefully consider the way foliage is formed. It can be obtained in various ways. Someone uses bottoms for this purpose. Others prefer the side walls. To do this, they are cut into identical rectangles, from which then the leaves of the required shape are obtained. With any chosen method of making crafts from plastic bottles, the palm tree turns out to be quite beautiful and original. The main thing is to approach the process of its creation creatively and do all the work very carefully.

If it will be difficult for you to place a tall palm tree somewhere, then if you wish, you can make other crafts from plastic bottles. Plastic pigs look especially interesting, which can also serve as a flower pot if one prudently cuts out part of the wall and puts soil inside. It is also worthwhile to purchase suitable plants so that the finished exposure looks the best.





Crafts from plastic bottles, made together with children, deserve special attention. These can be the most diverse animals - the same pigs, for example, or inhabitants of the deep sea. Such joint creativity will allow the child to develop certain skills and innovative thinking, which will best affect his learning and knowledge of the world.

Hand-made fakes will certainly become the favorite toy for the baby. Indeed, in the process of its creation, he put so much effort that, most likely, he could not part with it for a long time.

Thus, this material opens up great opportunities for creativity. The main thing is to show a little imagination and make a little effort.
