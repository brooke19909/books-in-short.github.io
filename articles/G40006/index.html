Holidays in Israel - interesting places and hotels in Jerusalem.

The holy city of Jerusalem has a rich history and is revered by Jews, Muslims and Christians around the world. It is divided into several parts. In the Old Town, tourists can see almost all local attractions. It also has Christian, Armenian, Muslim and Jewish quarters. East Jerusalem is the center of Arab culture, and West Jerusalem is one of the fastest growing areas. More than half a million people live in this city. Holidays in Jerusalem are popular all over the world. This is a great place for cultural and historical tourism. In addition, millions of people make pilgrimages here every year. In this city there are many churches, monasteries, ancient buildings, luxurious mansions, museums and art galleries. Guests of Jerusalem can go to the theater, attend the international film festival, which takes place here and engage in exciting shopping.

A variety of Jerusalem hotels will delight visitors with modern conveniences and the level of prices for any wallet. Of course, if you are not constrained by the means, then problems with the placement will not arise in any case. As a rule, even people with incomes below the average can find a decent place to stay and enjoy the magical atmosphere of this ancient city.

So, where can you stay and find the lowest prices for quality service? Hotel Alcazar is located very close to the Old Town. It has 38 comfortable rooms and beautiful views of the holy places. Here you will be offered fascinating excursions to the Dead Sea, to Bethlehem, Kurman, Jericho, Nazareth and other interesting places.

There are also such hotels in Jerusalem where business people are good to stay. Beit Ben Yehuda has a beautiful meeting room that is equipped with a video and spotlight. You can also use the wonderfully equipped kitchen and the large library. Nearby there is everything a tourist needs: bus stops, shops and restaurants.

The family-run Mount of Olive Hotel is located near the holy sites mentioned in the Bible. Guests will enjoy comfortable and clean rooms with a private bathroom. Here, those who come as pilgrims often stop here.
