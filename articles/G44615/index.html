Ten day tour

All round the world, no matter how many days they take, begin in Simferopol. Guests are welcomed at the airport and at any station. Here, excursion tours end.

Where does it all begin

Tour organizers meet guests in Simferopol (any station or airport). Then transfer to Sevastopol. Here everyone is accommodated in a comfortable hotel or, depending on the conditions of the tour, in a guest house on the coast. This day is considered exploratory and free. You can spend time in the beach doing nothing, doing your favorite water sports and other entertainment. In the evening, the organizers gather everyone for a gala dinner. The day of the "Crimean round the world" in 6 days is exactly the same.

Second day

Dedicated to Sevastopol. Tourists get acquainted with the past of Inkerman: they examine the medieval fortress, visit the men's monastery (the oldest cave monastery in these parts).

Chersonesus is not forgotten either. Following the excursionists move to the Vladimir Cathedral, famous for being erected at the site of the baptism of Vladimir. Further, a few more iconic places of Sevastopol, with a mandatory walk along the bays, in one of which is our pride - warships of the Black Sea Fleet of the Russian Federation. The third day

This is Balaclava. The path to it lies on the water. During this time, you can enjoy the interesting stories of local residents about the past of these places and swim in the open sea. A tour of the Balaclava includes a mandatory visit to the ancient temple of the Twelve Apostles, which has no analogues on any continent, and the underground naval complex. In your free time, you can grab a bite to eat at one of the many fish restaurants, enjoy a meal at Inkerman's signature vintage wines, and just wander along the beautiful promenade.

Further, the whole group moves to Cape Fiolent. It is interesting for its volcanic origin and the St. George Monastery. By evening, everyone returns to the hotel for dinner.

Fourth day

"Crimean circumnavigation" introduces the guests of the peninsula with the treasures of the South Bank. The beginning is a tour of the temple complex of the Resurrection of Christ in Foros, followed by the stunning beauty of Alupka Park and the enchanting Vorontsov Palace.

Incidentally, it is recognized as a unique architectural monument of the early nineteenth century. Closer to dinner, everyone arrives in the capital of these places - Yalta. The day continues with a boat trip with a sunset on Aurora rock, with which you can clearly see the smallest castle in our country - the Swallow's Nest. Further, the Massandra Park and the palace, and, naturally, the Massandra winery with a tasting of world famous products. By evening, everyone will again have dinner at the hotel and rest.

Fifth day

Free time. The organizers offer to enjoy the sun on the beach, wander around Sevastopol and optionally visit the unique corners of Crimea on their own.

Sixth day

This day of the Crimean Round-the-World tour is dedicated to mountains and caves. Morning begins from Mount Demergey. Seventh day

"Crimean circumnavigation" continues the mysterious Bakhchisarai. The day begins with a guided tour in the Maria Gorge (Maryam-Dere beam) to the most ancient monastery of these places - Holy Assumption. It was founded around the beginning of the 9th century and was very revered by Muslims. Then begins the journey through the city of Chufut-Kale. This is a unique cave city with more than one hundred rooms. The first mention of it dates back to 1299. By the beginning of the 15th century, the city became the main city of the Crimean Khanate. And in the XVI century they are assigned to the newly built Bakhchisaray. Lunch is held in one of the best cafes of the city with Crimean Tatar cuisine. The day ends with the main decoration of Bakhchisaray - the Khan's palace.

Eighth day

Or the second, if the ‚ÄúCrimean round-the-world‚Äù is 5 days in total - it is Sudak and the beautiful village of New World. The day starts from the Sudak fortress. This is a universally recognized fortification monument of the Middle Ages. The tour is quite fascinating, especially around the Consular Castle. Paradisio (from French - "paradise") or the New World today is a beautiful place.

It is located between the mountains (Sokol and Khoba-Kaya), which do not let cold winds in here, and rocky capes crashing into the sea waters form cozy picturesque bays. They got their name by the color of the water: Green, Blue and Blue. This place is truly a paradise for fans of beach do-nothing and selfies. From Zelenaya Bay, tourists will have to climb a path cut out in rocky ledges, called the Golitsinsky. It leads to a grotto with an interesting story. It is said that Chaliapin sang here, Prince Golitsyn kept the collection wine and organized concert programs, for which a variety was made. Today, absolutely definitely, music festivals with invited stars are held here. Then the tourists are escorted to Cape Kapchik at the Green Bay, and from it there is a direct road to the juniper grove. Then free time begins. And he already has something to spend here. You can rent almost anything, including scooters, deck chairs, windsurfing and snorkeling gear.

Day nine

"Crimean circumnavigation" invites you to Simeiz. The day begins with climbing Mount Cat. On its top, interesting remains of the fortification of the medieval period - Limena-Kale were discovered. It protected the village from one northern side. From the rest - rocky cliffs of Mount Cat. Going down, tourists find themselves on the stunningly beautiful and cozy promenade of Simeiz. The guide offers a walk, admire the country houses of an old building, which are considered an architectural delicacy. Then everyone moves to the Resort Park. Junipers, hornbeams and ancient oaks enrich the air with useful substances. In general, the walk is healing. Toward evening, personal time begins. Here you can spend it on the beach at the cliff of Diva or on its top from the observation deck to enjoy unforgettable views. Those who wish can visit the "Blue Bay" - a local small water park.

Tenth day

The final day of the excursion tour "Crimean circumnavigation." After collecting things, the organizers suggest holding a small picnic in the village of Zalesnoye on the Miracle Oslik farm.

This is the only place in the Crimea where donkeys of Somali and Nubian breeds are professionally bred. In addition to them, there are Vietnamese pigs, Cameroonian goats, horses, ostriches, peacocks, hens of the rarest breeds, Hungarian wolves, etc. All animals are tame: they eat from their hands, allow themselves to be stroked and photographed. The farm itself is located in a huge cherry garden, through which here and there you can meet the mysterious mystical rocks-sphinxes.
