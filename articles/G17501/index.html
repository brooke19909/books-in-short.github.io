Tourist base "White Swan" in Anapa

If you are planning an inexpensive, but high-quality vacation in Anapa, we suggest that you consider the White Swan tourist base. It has been open for more than a year, but constantly continues to develop and delight vacationers with various innovations.

General information about the White Swan





The hotel building, residential houses and other buildings of the recreation center were lost in the numerous greenery. On the territory of the "White Swan", whose total area is 10 hectares, bright flower beds and alleys of Pitsunda pine are broken. There are also several types of active entertainment and places for families with children are provided.

It is noteworthy that the recreation center "White Swan" is suitable for both those who want to spend their holidays in nature, and those who do not want to think about cooking. Guests of the White Swan camp site in Anapa are offered a choice of two housing options: a four-story dormitory building or summer wooden houses. The hotel building was commissioned only five years ago.

Number of recreation facilities





The hotel has 40 standard, family and deluxe rooms. There are double and quadruple apartments. All rooms have the necessary furniture (modern single and double beds, bedside tables, wardrobes, tables and chairs, Euro folding beds as an extra bed), a refrigerator and a TV, a split system is installed. Each room is equipped with a bathroom with shower. Exit to the balcony is not everywhere.

Summer two-room houses are designed for four guests. The total number of mini-cottages is 40. Each house has a veranda that combines two rooms. There are also two-story mini-cottages with an attic. Each room has one and a half beds, as well as other furniture necessary for a comfortable stay. A refrigerator and a table for eating are on the veranda. Some houses also have a gas stove. To establish an extra bed in the rooms is not possible. There is no bathroom in the houses. A toilet, washbasin and shower are located at the base.

Prices for accommodation start at 400 rubles per day per person (as of 2017).

Services and services available at the recreation center





At the recreation center "White Swan" in Anapa, the following types of services are available to guests:

  • 24-hour reception
  • free parking at the base;
  • elevator in a residential building;
  • safe and luggage storage at the administrator;
  • Ironing room
  • daily housekeeping;
  • linen change every five days;
  • medical Center;
  • amenities on site;
  • three shared kitchens with the necessary set of dishes and utensils;
  • Cafe;
  • a relaxation room where you can arrange an evening movie show;
  • various entertainments;
  • sports and beach equipment rental;
  • children's playground with slides and swings;
  • possibility of accommodation with pets (by prior arrangement);
  • organization of excursions (the hostel has a private bus).

On the territory of the recreation center "White Swan" (Anapa) there is a cafe serving delicious homemade dishes. Breakfast is included in the price of the guests of the dormitory building. But vacationers order lunch and dinner on their own. Since the camp site does not work all year round, the cafe is open only from June 1 to September 15. If you wish, you can cook in person. To do this, there are three kitchens with all the necessary equipment and supplies, as well as two-burner gas stoves in some houses.

Organization of leisure in the base and beyond





On the territory of the White Swan, guests are offered the following leisure options:

  • billiards (additional charge is charged);
  • a sports ground for playing soccer, volleyball, badminton and so on (all the necessary equipment can be rented from the administrator of the recreation center);
  • Sauna and Jacuzzi
  • equipped places for rest in the park (arbors and benches).

The city sandy beach is only 50 meters from the recreation center (about fifty minutes walk). There are sun loungers, umbrellas, changing cabins, toilets and showers. For children, slides are installed on the beach, and adults enjoy various water activities (scooters, catamarans, jet skis, water skiing, and so on). You can also rent a boat or boat for a boat trip. Around there are a large number of small cafes, restaurants, outlets with food, souvenirs and other trifles. Also from the recreation center close to the main places of entertainment Anapa: parks, promenade, dolphinarium and water park.

Location of the tourist base

Hotel "White Swan" (Anapa) is located at: Dzhemete district, Pionersky Prospekt, 233. The base is located just 2 km from the city railway station and 10 km from the local airport. You can get to the White Swan both by car and on your own. To do this, you must take any shuttle bus that goes towards the bus station. You need to get out at the stop "Sanatorium" Ryabinushka ". From it to the recreation center is only two minutes on foot.
