Ski resorts in the Murmansk region. Rest in the winter

Numerous ski resorts in the Murmansk region give this territory special popularity among tourists. For example, in the city of Apatity, on Vorobinaya Gora, there is a well-equipped slope that attracts a large number of skiing enthusiasts and professionals annually.





A similar resort of Spolokhi is located on the Cross Mountain. Magnificent ski centers are located in other cities of the region (Kandalaksha, Monchegorsk and in Murmansk itself). This article will focus on some of the most remarkable ski resorts located in the vicinity of the cities of Kirovsk and Apatity.

Kirovsk Ski Resorts

In order to get a huge dose of adrenaline, thousands of tourists annually come to Kirovsk (Murmansk region). The ski resort located on Mount Kukisvumchorr is one of the most popular. The route starts from the highest point of the mountain. Its length is up to two and a half kilometers. Four ski lifts deliver skiers.

Ski slope "Big Woodyavr"

On Olimpiyskaya Street, on Mount Aikuayvenchorr, there is a thirty-meter ski run, ideal for freestyle, as well as for ski jumping. Skiers lift eight lifts on the mountain. Ski resorts in the Murmansk region accept not only experienced athletes. On the southern slope is the Big Woodyavr complex with a track length of up to three kilometers and two ski lifts. This resort is recommended for beginners.





For lovers of extreme descent, a helicopter flight is provided from one mountain to another, followed by skiing (freeride). Since it is possible to fall under an avalanche, such skiing is necessary under the guidance of an experienced instructor.

Due to the availability of tracks of varying difficulty, it is possible to choose the most suitable. All tracks are lit. Therefore, you can ride even in polar night conditions. Ski resorts are available at all resorts; snowmobiling and paragliding are provided. After descending from the mountain, you can relax in the sauna and visit the restaurant.

Ski complex "Khibiny Snow Park"

In the center of Kirovsk there is another popular ski resort (Murmansk region) - "Khibiny Snow Park", the route of which is only two hundred meters. It is perfect for initial ski downhill training . The track is perfectly flat with a slight difference in elevation. On the basis of the ski complex there is a children's school of the Olympic reserve.





Thanks to the excellent lighting, you can ride even in the evening. Climbing the mountain is carried out by two lifts. Of the additional entertainment available riding cheesecakes. Those wishing to relax with benefit should definitely visit Kirovsk (Murmansk region). Khibiny Snow Park ski resort offers the best conditions for children and adults.

Sparrow Mountain: ski slope





The ski resort is located in the town of Apatity, in the village of Belorechensky. It is located in a forest belt, which makes it possible to hide from the wind. The length of the route is 550 meters. The elevation reaches 80 meters. Skiers are delivered to the descent point on two lifts. The track is well lit. A good mood of skiers is provided by a musical broadcast. In order to teach your children skiing, you can go to Apatity (Murmansk region). The ski resort "Sparrow Mountain" has its own children's sports school. On weekdays, the resort is open until 18 hours. On weekends - until 17:00.

Track for experienced

Nearby is another mountain - Vudyavrchorra, the snow cover on which lies until the end of May. Descent from the mountain is associated with a great risk of falling under an avalanche. Accordingly, this is recommended only for experienced skiers under the guidance of an instructor.

Ski resorts in the Murmansk region offer their guests more than just skiing. Helicopter flying around the mountain peaks is very popular. In an hour, you can fly up to five mountains and descend snow virgin lands from them.

How to get there

You can get to the city of Apatity from Murmansk or St. Petersburg, using the railway transport. Travel time by train from St. Petersburg is about a day. You can also get to the city by plane from Moscow, a flight from Arkhangelsk Airlines, from Sheremetyevo-1 Airport and from Pulkovo Airport from St. Petersburg. The flight time will be about one and a half hours.





The best option for any outdoor enthusiast is a ski resort in the Murmansk region. Prices in each complex may vary slightly. On average, the cost of one run is about 50 rubles, provided that up to 10 runs are made. The more slopes declared, the lower their cost.

Murmansk region is one of the centers of domestic skiing. The availability of slopes of various difficulty categories makes it possible to choose the most suitable descent, depending on the level of skier training. All ski resorts are equipped with modern reliable lifts. The safety of people while skiing from the mountains is ensured by good illumination of the tracks, the presence of medical attention. Competent instructors are always ready to assist in the development of downhill skiing skills .

Ski resorts in the Murmansk region are full-fledged complexes offering their guests a full range of services. Tourists and athletes, skiing, can take a steam bath in the sauna or Russian bath, as well as visit a cafe or restaurant.

When choosing a place for skiing, one should take into account the level of training, the length of the ski slope, and elevations. Without the appropriate skiing skills, it is better to train on the slopes of small difficulty, for example, in the Khibiny Snow Park complex.

Helicopter flying around the mountain peaks and their subsequent descent, despite the external attractiveness and high level of extreme sports, is suitable exclusively for experienced skiers and should be carried out under the supervision of an instructor. Given these recommendations, you can make your vacation in a ski resort as pleasant and safe as possible.
