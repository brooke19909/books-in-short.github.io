Ramada Beach Hotel ("Ramada Beach Hotel"), United Arab Emirates, Ajman: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Ajman - the so-called smallest emirate, which is part of the UAE. Its area is minuscule in comparison with other cities - only 0.3% of the entire state! However, this fact did not prevent to become this place one of the most remarkable and visited resorts of the Emirates. Of course, as in all developed cities, a tourist base is well developed in this emirate. Thousands of guests from all over the world come to Ajman all year round. And not only an exotic state with its attractions attracts them. Genuine interest is caused by the quality of service and the highest service of tourist establishments. This will be discussed.





Holidays in Ajman

So, before proceeding directly to the topic of what Ajman hotels with their own beach exist in this resort, it is worth noting a few interesting facts about the city itself. The emirate is famous for its pearl mining, fishing, delicious dates, shipbuilding and, of course, perfumery and furniture. This city has its own medical institute and a separate university. Interestingly, due to the size of the emirate, it is not just an airport that is missing - even there are no bus services to other large regions. So you need to get to Ajman by taxi from the nearby emirates. But problems should not arise, since the nearest point is Dubai Airport, which is 30 minutes by car from Ajman. In the emirate, there is something to see, in addition to the warm sea and the gentle sun. For example, an ancient fort, which is one of the main attractions. With pleasure, visitors visit the National History Museum. And, of course, it is impossible not to mention natural attractions: it is a limitless sea, flowering agricultural territories, picturesque mountains. In general, Ajman has everything you need for your vacation. And after potential tourists are convinced of this, they begin to search for a temporary residence.
