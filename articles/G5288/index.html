Customer note

The current legislation of the Russian Federation taxes all currency deposits with income tax. It concerns deposits with an interest rate of more than nine percent per annum.





Income tax for residents of the Russian Federation is 35%, non-residents - 30%. It will be calculated from profits in excess of the nine percent annual rate.

A tax is charged at the time of payment of the calculated interest on the deposit. Any bank of the Russian Federation is a tax agent and is obliged to transfer taxes on profits from foreign currency deposits, therefore, the interest earned on the placed currency funds will be received by the client, net of taxes paid.
