A few words on how to get maternity capital

State policy aimed at increasing the birth rate and providing the family with everything necessary for this solves many problems. Considerable funds are allocated from the budget, but certain conditions are necessary to take advantage of them. The fact is that these funds are earmarked, and the state is carefully monitoring what they are being spent on. Many families are wondering how to get maternity capital. First of all, the applicant must comply with the conditions under which it is possible to apply for a certificate.



Regardless of where you live, on the territory of the Russian Federation or in another country, if you are a citizen of Russia, then you can submit documents and expect a positive decision. A prerequisite is the birth or adoption of a second child after 2007. Also, one of the conditions is that the certificate is not issued a second time. This means that when you give birth to a third child again, you will not receive this help. If twins were born in your family, then you can get help from the state. Family capital can also be obtained by the father of the children if he is solely engaged in their upbringing. How to get maternity capital for parents whose children were born before 2007? Submit documents at the birth of the next child, and they will certainly help you.
