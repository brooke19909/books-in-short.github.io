Safa 3 * (Tunisia / Hammamet) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Safa 3 (Tunisia) is a beachfront hotel in the Hammamet resort area called Yasmin. It is a solid "three rubles", corresponding to its stardom. Therefore, tourists who come here should understand that they are resting in a budget hotel, and not in a five-star palace. But at the same time, the hotel is very good for everyone and provides excellent services. Well-fed, comfortable standard rooms, reasonable internet speed. Here the pleasant and cheerful staff, good specialists, a professional doctor in the spa. There is a thalassotherapy room. The hotel is popular with both locals who like to relax here and Western Europeans. She is in a calm, safe place, there is where to walk and have fun. The hotel is good for those who come to swim in the Mediterranean Sea, watch Tunisia and walk around Hammamet.



Location

The Enfida Airport, where most tourists come from, is about thirty-five kilometers from Hotel Safa 3 (Tunisia). The center of Hammamet - Medina is ten kilometers away. But it is not so far to go to the shopping streets of Yasmin, about ten minutes. You can get there, of course, by taxi, or by shuttle bus. And at fifteen minutes on foot - the port of Marina, where you can go have fun. To get from the airport by transfer - about forty minutes. In the vicinity of the hotel are five-star luxury hotels. And behind the hotel there is a supermarket where you can buy everything you miss on vacation. Immediately after the neighboring Hotel Medina is an entertainment center where you can walk in the evening.

Hammamet

This resort itself has long been a favorite place for swimming. Wherever you look, for many kilometers stretches of pure white sand washed by a warm and gentle sea. The tan in these places is smooth and beautiful, even with a minimum use of sunscreens.

This resort is very good for children. The entrance to the water is shallow and safe, and they simply do not want to crawl out of the sea.

But not only tourists come to the beach to Safa 3. Tunisia, whose photos of sights are very attractive, is a country of great civilizations, succeeding each other. They left their mark on modern life.

Tunisia is a completely atypical Africa, and it is best seen in Hammamet. If you want to get to know this country well, it is better to travel on it not in tourist buses, but on public transport, go to shops, watch the locals. And then you will see that they are not much different from the inhabitants of the European Mediterranean.



Territory

Hotel Safa 3 (Tunisia) is huge, beautiful. And this is evident in his photographs. It does not have a large park. But here it is nice and well maintained. As is customary in decent Tunisian hotels, it has its own spa and beauty salon. There is a business center, the services of which can be used if you can not go out of business even during your holidays. Kids love the pools and the water park. They just do not get out of there, because of which adults often do not have the opportunity to go on excursions. There are sports fields, courts, for the services of vacationers - an Internet corner. Nice playground. The nanny on call works. There are several equipment rental points on the beach.



Number of rooms

The Safa 3 hotel (Tunisia) has four hundred and fifty rooms. The vast majority of them are standard, modest, but quite suitable for recreation. They are equipped very well for a beach treshka. There is a TV with a Russian channel, a small refrigerator. You can put a crib for a child. The rooms have air conditioning, all the plumbing is working. They often clean, various figures are wound from sheets, and beds are sometimes sprinkled with flower petals. Towels are changed daily, they smell delicious, everything is clean and decent. There are chairs and a table on the balcony, you can relax in the evening. The rooms are decorated in Arabic blue and white style, the interior is comfortable and decent. There is a dressing table, ottoman. Tourists recommend taking rooms with sea views - then you can sleep without air conditioning, with the door open, and inhale the salty breeze.

Services

In the lobby of the hotel Safa 3 (Tunisia) catches a network of free "Wi-Fi." Here you can also put things and jewelry in a safe. But this service is among the paid. Staff

Now let's talk about the employees of the Safa 3 hotel (Tunisia). Reviews from tourists tell us that the staff here are friendly. When settling problems practically does not arise, everything is solved quickly. No extra charges for rooms with sea view. True, administrators communicate mainly in English or French. Entertainment and excursions

If you want to take a walk, it is best to first pay attention to neighboring hotels. For example, to the right of Hotel Safa 3 (Tunisia) is one of the best local hookahs. They serve not only tobacco, but also mint tea and oriental sweets. Singers sing beautiful Arabic songs.

In walking distance - shopping streets and the medieval quarter of Medina, where you can not only see the sights, but also buy souvenirs. Mostly they buy dates, olive oil, Turkish delight. Opposite the hotel, along the beach and to the very center, there is a promenade along which people walk in the evenings. The old Medina and souvenir market can also be reached by bus number one hundred and fifteen. In the center of Hammamet you can still transfer to a minibus that goes to Nabeul. In the evening, in the medina of the Yasmin district, you can watch colorful shows, or even break away in the morning at some local Disneyland. Of the good discos recommend Latina. Prices

Those who need budget trips and an excellent vacation at sea, choose Hotel Safa 3 (Tunisia). Eight days will cost a couple of tourists thirty-three thousand rubles for two. Prices in the center of thalassotherapy are much cheaper than in neighboring hotels. Procedures cost about five hundred rubles. One and a half liters of bottled water in the lobby - one dinar. But you can buy cheaper at the supermarket. To smoke a good hookah costs sixteen dinars. Public transport is cheap, but taxis are not expensive either. A trip will cost an average of nine dinars. Moreover, the drivers work without cheating, you pay exactly on the counter. Towels from the rooms are not allowed to be taken out, therefore beach ones should be taken with you or bought for five dinars.
