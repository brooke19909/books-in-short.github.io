Territory

Hotel Giftun Azur (Hurghada) has a very beautiful territory that does not allow to forget that this is Africa. Around a lot of palm trees. Even the playground has found a place between these exotics. Infrastructure

Giftun Azur (Hurghada) received the first holidaymakers in 1984. Despite such a solid period for hotels, everything here functions and has a neat appearance. In the main building there is a reception where tourists are served around the clock. There you can hand over things to the locker for free, and valuables - in an individual safe, call a doctor, order a nanny for the child, buy a tour, hand over clothes for laundry and dry cleaning, exchange currency, use a photocopy machine, fax machine, long-distance telephone, rent car.





The lobby has soft sofas, armchairs, comfortable coffee tables, a lobby bar and free Wi-Fi. For tourists who come to the sea, this is not so important, but for those who organize business meetings in Egypt, it will not be out of place to know that the Hotel Gift Azur (Hurghada) has a fully equipped and equipped conference room. To ensure the best relaxation, there is a beauty salon, massage room, hairdresser, sauna, Turkish bath, and for tanning there is not only space around the pool, but also an outdoor terrace.

Accommodation

Giftun Azur (Hurghada) offers several accommodation options. On its territory the main building in 2 floors, 17 bungalows, another two-story and one four-story building harmoniously fit into the overall design. They have a total of 522 rooms. Despite the fact that the last major reconstruction in the hotel took place in 1998, everything is soundly in the rooms, it works without problems. Categories:

  • Standard with a view of the hotel (garden) . Area - up to 32 squares. The room is double, but it is possible to install 1 folding bed. Equipment - a necessary set of furniture, a modern TV (1 channel in Russian), air conditioning, a telephone, a minibar (regularly updated), an electric kettle, dishes and accessories for tea and coffee, mosquito repellents, a hygiene room in which there is a toilet bowl, hairdryer, mirror, washbasin, shower, and some rooms have a bathtub with shower. Personal hygiene products are represented by toilet paper, soap, shampoo, shower gel.




  • Standard overlooking the Red Sea . Area - up to 40 squares. The room is double, but extra bed is possible. The equipment is the same as in the standards with garden views. The difference, except for the area, is that there is a welcome drink (non-alcoholic) and fruit upon check-in.
  • Junior Suite with Red Sea View Area - up to 70 squares. The room has a living area with upholstered furniture and a balcony. Equipment - a set of bedroom furniture, upholstered sofa, table, chairs, wardrobe, TV, minibar, telephone, air conditioning, bathroom, which provides not only personal hygiene products, but also bathrobes, slippers. When you check in to the deluxe rooms, you will find a fruit basket and a welcome drink (non-alcoholic) upon arrival.

The furniture in all rooms is not new, but high-quality, there is no carpet on the floor. Cleaning is done every day, linen and towels are changed according to the schedule.

Beach

Giftun Azur Resort Hotel (Hurghada) is located a couple of steps from the coastline. The beach here is well equipped. Sun beds and umbrellas made of cane are always available. The hype happens only when they are occupied in the very first row from the sea. The beach cover is sand that is cleaned every day by the hotel staff. Entering shallow water, the depth is not immediate. The sea is unusually clean, without corals, therefore, you will not see beautiful exotic animals either. When it begins to get dark (differently every month), swimming is no longer allowed.





Of the entertainment on the beach - volleyball, water skiing, catamarans (free). Here is the hotel bar, where cold and hot sandwiches are served throughout the day, waffles with various fillings and buns are baked. From drinks you can take beer, coffee, tea, cocoa, cappuccino. Two to three minutes from the Giftun Azur beach is the beach of the CUBA hotel, on which a sand spit crashes into the sea. It is in that place that you can admire the beautiful marine inhabitants.

Entertainment

In Giftun Azur Resort 4 * all entertainment ends around 24:00. This hotel is more suitable for relaxing measured, calm, family than for active youth. Evening discos are held here once a week. But in the amphitheater and by the pool every day they organize shows, competitions, water and usual comic gymnastics, there are theater performances and concerts. There is animation for children. The only caveat - all this is organized in German.

For sports enthusiasts, the hotel has tennis courts and tennis tables, billiards (free of charge), squash courts, a golf course, sports fields, a gym with exercise machines. Guests can also relax in the sauna and hammam. The hotel has a very large swimming pool for adults with always clean and warm water. The depth in it is different, from an average of about 1.5 meters to 2 meters. Nearby is a children's pool up to 50 cm deep. Sun beds and umbrellas are always available by the pool, towels are handed out and changed every day. There is a bar nearby.
