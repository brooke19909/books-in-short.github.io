One of the recognizable images of Russia, defining the bright meanings of its past, has long been rightfully considered a noble estate. In the minds of present generations, the noble estate was preserved not only as a myth. "Raiki": a brief history

Selco Raiki once belonged to the Davydovs, then the Kondrashovs, and later to Ivan Nekrasov. It is known that the famous poet Boris Pasternak spent several years on this estate.

Over the entire period of its existence, the estate "Raiki" has seen many prominent personalities, musical works and paintings were written within the walls of the estate. Currently, its territory is used by the boarding house "Youth" and the sanatorium. A.M. Gorky. In the 70-80s, modern residential buildings were built here for the needs of these institutions. Unfortunately, many magnificent buildings of the 19th century were destroyed by time, but something has survived. Even now, visitors to the "Raiki" estate can admire the remains of the "hanging" gardens in the former stepped park, preserved by an ancient pond with islands, as well as trees that eternally adorned the legendary estate.

It is known that since 1811 the masters of this earthly paradise were Messrs. Davydovs, in 1852 the estate passed into the ownership of the landowner Aghey Abaz, from 1890 the estate passed into the possession of the silk manufacturers Kondrashovs, and then I.I. Nekrasov, a famous gold miner. It is with him that historians associate all the most significant events in the life of Raikov. The last estate was owned by the manufacturer S.I. Chetverikov, after the revolution, emigrated with the whole family to Europe.

Architecture

At the turn of the XIX-XX centuries, designed by architect L.N. Kekusheva is building a number of interesting buildings in the Art Nouveau style. Unfortunately, in the 1996 fire, the pearl of the architectural complex burned down - the main house with an octagonal turret, designed by the famous architect. Among the beautiful creations of L.N. Kekusheva also includes a residential building in the park, the so-called "American house" in the style of half-timbered houses and other buildings, each of which has its own unique style.

Dutch (American) house, arch. L.N. Kekushev (1901)

According to experts, a symmetrical and compact American (or Dutch) house with two floors and an attic deserves special attention. In each room the details are thought out to the smallest detail, and the design of the external decor inspired a lot of pleasant romantic associations. The facades of the building were finished in marble, and the roof was laid with Marseille tiles. It is known that in 1907-1909. it was here that the family of the famous poet Boris Pasternak spent her summer vacation.

Also in 1911, the family of the famous engineer and inventor Bari stayed here for the summer. Ten years earlier, in 1901, the famous Russian artist Surikov worked on his painting ‚ÄúStepan Razin‚Äù. Artists K. Bedrosov, V. Denisov, P. Konchalovsky and many others also created and rested on the estate. In different sources, this building is called differently: it is also called the Fachwerk cottage, and the Dutch, and the American house ...

In the photo you can see that the facades of the building with a wooden frame are visually divided into rectangular fields. The building simplistically reproduces common Victorian style trends. Experts say that the general silhouette of this building refers more to the style characteristic of the times of Queen Anne, and the wall decoration refers the visitor to the times of Tudor and Elizabeth. Unfortunately, today on the facade you will not see dark stripes characteristic of fachwerk - they were painted over with light paint.

The building, built in just one year, the architectural community recognized as an example of a new style. The house has become a recognized standard of comfortable suburban housing. It is known that light, water, gas were installed in the building. In this comfortable cottage, the wife of the owner of the estate Nekrasova, Anna Timofeevna, who was seriously interested in painting, loved to live for a long time. In Soviet times, V.M., who was in disgrace, sometimes stayed here. Molotov.

From antique furniture, a bookcase has survived, which today stands in the library, and a dressing table. In the house itself, as well as in the modern extension, there are several suites in which visitors can live.

About the boarding house "Youth"

The boarding house is located on Shchelkovo highway 23 km from Moscow. The pension is surrounded by an old landscape park with paths laid along it. The boarding house has three separate residential buildings, a restaurant complex, a wellness center with a sauna and a swimming pool, an entertainment center and its own ski lift. The mode of operation of the boarding house: round the clock.

Vacationers are invited to take advantage of a wide range of services: access to the sauna, pool, massage, beauty salon, salt room. Guests can play table tennis or billiards here. Green forests and picturesque ponds create a unique atmosphere that inspires you to enjoy pleasant walks.

Vacationers are offered a wide range of accommodation options: from a 1-bed standard to an exquisite suite with an empire-style decor. All rooms have a TV, a comfortable bed and a private bathroom.

Meal of guests (three meals a day, included in the price) is organized in the dining room of the boarding house. In addition, the Imperial restaurant is available for vacationers, in which mass banquet events are held.

The distance from the boarding house to the Moscow Ring Road is 23 km, so it is not difficult to get to it. The Belorussky Train Station is 38 km from here, and Vnukovo Airport is 63 km away. Boarding house address: pos. Youth, Shchelkovo highway., Those who are interested in how to get to the estate, experts recommend using the coordinates: n. 55.91790100, east 38.12895300.

"Paradise-Semenovskoe"

Returning to the conversation about "paradise on earth" (see the beginning of the article), it should be noted that, in addition to the "Raikes", there are other ancient Russian estates, which, with their beauty and tranquility, provoke thoughts of paradise among visitors. Association with paradise, one way or another, was reflected in their names. One of these places is the estate "Paradise-Semenovskoe" in the Serpukhov district. For many decades, the village of Semenovskoye belonged to the Ordin-Nashchokin boyars. At the turn of the XVIII-XIX centuries, the owner of the estate was the corps marshal of Paul I A.P. Nashchokin, who created a magnificent architectural ensemble in the estate and proudly called it ‚ÄúParadise‚Äù. In 1803, the owner opened a hydropathic establishment in the estate, which later became widely known. To this day, resort buildings, unfortunately, have not been preserved. After the revolution, "Paradise-Semenovskoe" gradually fell into desolation.

The estate "Raek" (Tver region)

From a bird's eye view, the complex resembles a precious necklace decorated with large diamonds, with a fastener - where the gates are located. History

The history of the Znamenskoye-Raek estate dates back to 1746, when its construction was commissioned by order of General-General Fedor Glebov. The manor complex was built according to the project of Nikolai Lvov. It was assumed that the monarchs would stop at the estate in Tver along the road from St. Petersburg to Moscow. The construction here was carried out with a truly royal scale.

A brief history of the estate "Raek" says that the owner F.I. Glebov did not have a chance to live to see the finish of the estate. His widow hardly visited the estate. Heir, hussar officer P.F. Glebov-Streshnev, died in the battle of Borodino. After his death, the estate was sold, after which it gradually began to decline. After the revolution of 1917, park buildings gradually disappeared. Much of what HA created by Lvov has not survived to this day. The last loss of the "Raek" estate in the Tver region was the beautiful rotunda collapsed in 1991.

Homestead today

It is not yet possible to live in the estate, but if you wish, you can still explore the territory (the duration of the tour is 1 hour).
