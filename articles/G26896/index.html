Greek island of Samos: photos, attractions and reviews

One of the largest islands of the Aegean Sea is still an exotic tourist destination. The homeland of Pythagoras and Epicurus deserves the attention of those who want to relax at sea, and all lovers of the ancient culture. The rich architectural heritage and many beaches will pleasantly surprise foreign travelers who prefer privacy.

A bit of history

Samos, the ninth most populated island, is separated from Turkey by Eptastadio, a canal about 1600 meters wide. The lands of the paradise, part of the Eastern Sporades archipelago, are considered the most fertile in Greece, and the wonderful wine with a mild taste ‚ÄúWafi‚Äù has long been known outside the country.

Before the ice age, Samos was part of Asia Minor, and due to its favorable geographical position, the Greeks quickly populate it. In ancient times, it was one of the richest colonies of Hellas, and later the island becomes an economically developed metropolis of Ancient Greece.



In the Byzantine period, it receives the status of autonomy, and in the 15th century its inhabitants leave the fertile place due to the constant raids of pirates. And only ten years later, the uninhabited island of Samos is inhabited by Orthodox migrants who were looking for a secluded corner across the ocean. In 1830 it was annexed to the Ottoman Empire, and during this period the center of the Ionian culture literally flourishes.

At the beginning of the 20th century, Samos became part of Greece. During World War II, it was occupied by Italian and German troops, and bombing destroys many of the sights of the island. And only with the development of tourism a unique example of the history and nature of Greece returns its lost position.

Climate Legends of the Island

The mild Mediterranean climate, characterized by sunny summers, short and warm winters, will appeal to adults and children. Beneficial to the respiratory system, it contributes to a pleasant pastime throughout the year. Summer heat (up to 35 degrees) is easily tolerated thanks to a refreshing sea breeze. The swimming season begins in May and ends at the end of October.

A place for relaxed and active relaxation

The Greek island of Samos, whose capital is the city of the same name, is a wonderful place to relax. Equipped beaches, warm sea, picturesque landscapes, developed hotel infrastructure have recently attracted an increasing number of tourists. Many attractions allow guests to spend their holidays to good use.

Temple of Hera

In the south of the resort is its historical center, named after Pythagoras. Here are the ruins of one of the most important monuments of ancient civilization - the temple dedicated to Hera.

Even the historian Herodotus in his works singled out several wonders of the world, among which was the majestic sanctuary of the wife of Zeus, located on the territory of a blossoming corner of paradise. According to legends, Hera from the island of Samos became the wife of the formidable son of Kronos, who knows the whole world. The followers of the cult of the goddess who patronized the marriage set out to erect a structure in her honor, and in 720 BC a temple appeared surrounded by a large number of columns.



It was a whole religious complex with a length of about 109 meters. The high temple served as a model for other structures, but after a strong earthquake it was destroyed. Now visitors to the archaeological site can observe one column, which has been preserved since time immemorial.

Underground Aqueduct Tunnel

In Pythagorio, the second important attraction is located - the aqueduct, which is an underground tunnel. Hollowed out in the rock, he provided the inhabitants of the island with clean water. A unique building, which is a masterpiece of engineering at that time, Herodotus also included in the list of the main wonders of the world.



Monument to the philosopher and a cave named after him

The name of the great Pythagoras is associated with several more interesting places that tourists who come to the picturesque island of Samos must visit. Attractions are of genuine interest to guests of the resort. On the seashore stands a monument dedicated to the ancient Greek philosopher, and at the foot of Mount Kersis there is a cave named after him. It is believed that a mathematician spent about 10 years in it, hiding from the persecution of a tyrant ruler.

List of the most popular beaches of Samos

Colorful photos of Samos cause an irresistible desire to visit him and soak up in the warm turquoise waters. The picturesque resort is famous for a large number of pebble beaches, ideal for families. Clima is considered the best, surrounded by inexpensive bars and taverns offering national cuisine. Tourists with children have a rest on a shallow beach located not far from Poseidonio, and even loud noise and bustle will not prevent you from enjoying the wonderful beauty of the island.



Near the city of Wafi is Agia Markella - an ideal place with clear water. The wild beach, named after the patron saint of the island, is very popular.

Psili Amos, located in the vicinity of Samos, will appeal to lovers of picnics and active entertainment. It is a sandy beach, and a gentle entry into the water makes it incredibly convenient for guests.

Kerveli is chosen by connoisseurs of secluded relaxation. This is a not crowded beach where you can spend the whole day relaxing and enjoying nature.

Malagari is chosen not only by lovers of water activities, but also true connoisseurs of good wines, as there is a winery near the beach where you can taste sparkling drinks.

The resort producing the best muscat in the world

When it comes to wine, it is impossible not to mention that the island of Samos is the birthplace of sweet muscat. The Vatican once held its own wine factory here, but now the Catholic Church has given permission to make a drink for Holy Communion.

Samos Muscat, which received all kinds of international awards, has been a major export product since ancient times. In the first ten days of August, a fabulous corner is filled with tourists from different countries who have arrived to taste the energizing drink. Purchased one glass of wine with an exquisite fruit bouquet is allowed to be filled as many times as guests of the blessed island wish.



Nobody leaves here without a bottle of sparkling drink and freshly squeezed olive oil.

What else to bring from the island?

The main souvenir of Samos is considered the "circle of justice" invented by Pythagoras. At that time, water was worth its weight in gold, and the ancient Greek mathematician and philosopher were instructed to come up with a special vessel that would allow working slaves to fairly distribute life-giving moisture among themselves.



So an amazing mug appeared: you can pour water only up to a certain point, otherwise the liquid will spill out.

Samos Island is famous for craftsmen making leather and pottery, so tourists get beautiful belts, wallets, handmade dishes. And those who have visited the village of Pyrgos, the inhabitants of which for several centuries have been known throughout the world for their beekeeping traditions, acquire fragrant honey.

Kitchen and dishes

Tourists admit that local dishes are one of the main reasons to visit the resort. Hippocrates wrote about the quality of Samos food, and all travelers consider the national cuisine the most delicious.

The inhabitants of the island produce fresh yogurts, salted cheeses, and local restaurants offer a variety of dishes from eggplant, tomatoes, seafood. You can not ignore the fragrant fruits grown under the Greek sun.

Samos Island (Greece): reviews

Tourists with enthusiasm recall about the rest spent on the island. The luxurious resort is loved by couples with children and travelers who crave loneliness. Guests of Samos admit that there is a feeling of harmony and unity with nature.

Everyone notes the excellent service and hospitality of the locals, who are happy to talk about national traditions. Archaeological sites cause genuine interest among tourists, and the beaches with clear water pleasantly surprise even the discerning vacationer.



The exotic island of Samos (Greece) is one of the most attractive corners of the world where you can hide from all problems and worries.
