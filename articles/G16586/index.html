Hong Kong: beaches and beach vacations. Photos and reviews

Hong Kong is a beautiful star city in the Asia-Pacific region, which is located in the southern part of China at the mouth of the Dongjiang River on the Chinese coast of the Indian Ocean. This is a highly developed and dynamic crossroads in Asia, which is also the gateway to mainland China.

Hong Kong amazes everyone who arrives at first sight with an abundance of colors, the number of modern skyscrapers, luminous hieroglyphics on signs, an endless stream of cars, a huge number of people living on a rather modest plot of land.



Location

Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, the largest financial center in Asia and the world, is located in southeastern China and borders the Guangdong Province, whose capital is Guangzhou. Hong Kong is divided into three zones: the Kowloon Peninsula, Hong Kong Island, as well as the New Territories, which include rural areas on the mainland north of Kowloon and south of the border with mainland China. In addition, 260 small islands are included in Hong Kong.

Holidays in Hong Kong

The mixture of Asian and European cultures gives this amazing city a special appeal. The modern and classical antiquity of buildings, the unhurriedness of oriental ceremonies and the crazy rhythm of the modern metropolis, the fun in countless nightclubs and the highest spirituality of temples - all this makes this area unique.

The Chinese joke that Hong Kong is chasing two birds with one stone, and admit that it manages to catch both. As a rule, tourists who arrived here for the first time love to walk along noisy streets, looking at skyscrapers, the tops of which are lost in the clouds. You can admire the panorama of the city from the observation deck, take part in a tea ceremony, climb the mountains by tram, visit local fortune tellers and find out your fate, lie on the beach - Hong Kong offers various leisure options.

sights

There are so many interesting and memorable places on this earth that it is impossible to even quickly examine them in one trip. Below we will present you only some of them, the most popular.

Big buddha

A giant of 34 meters in height is closely watching the Chinese people. You can approach the bronze giant by breaking 268 steps. Nearby is the Po Lin Monastery with its magnificent garden, which is filled with the fragrance of flowers and birdsong.



Golden Bauhinia Square

This is a symbol of Hong Kong, a reunion monument, a gift to the city from the Central Government. In the evening, this "Hong Kong orchid" is very competently highlighted. Nearby you can see a 20-meter stele, collected from 206 stone slabs, which symbolize the country's historical dates and future years. Every day at 8 am the state flag flies on the square to the accompaniment of the national anthem.

Symphony of Lights

This amazing show is listed in the Guinness Book of Records. Searchlights and laser beams against the background of the night sky create an amazing, fabulous performance. For Christmas or New Year the show is accompanied by fireworks.

Beaches in Hong Kong

Hong Kong's coastline is indented with numerous bays and bays. She stretched for several kilometers. The beaches of Hong Kong (photos you can see in this article) are mostly reliably protected by the mountains from the wind. There are no very large waves, so they are so loved by guests with children.



The best beaches in Hong Kong are municipal. This means that everyone can sit for free in any place that they liked. Today, on the island of Hong Kong, as well as in the southern part of the Coluon Peninsula, there are twelve municipal beaches, and in the New Territories another thirty-three. Of course, there are private beaches in Hong Kong. They can be visited by paying an admission ticket.

Hong Kong: Lo So Shing and Hung Shue Yeh beaches

These places of rest, despite their remoteness, are very popular. They are located on the island of Lamma. The question arises: "How to get to the beach in Hong Kong?" To do this, you need to use the ferry, which departs from the city center. The pier is located near the skyscraper, which houses the international financial center. The trip is paid in cash or by card.

A pleasant boat trip takes only thirty minutes. Then from the pier you need to walk (following the signs) to the beach of Hung Shue Yeh. There are no cars or buses on Lamma Island. Locals move on foot or by bicycle.

The beaches of Hong Kong (reviews of tourists confirm this) amaze with the purity of the water. This fully applies to Hung Shue Yeh. It is surrounded by picturesque hills and dense vegetation. The bathing area is indicated by buoys, two rescuers are constantly working - one is always at sea on a catamaran, the second on a rescue tower.



If you prefer a more secluded vacation, then go through the beach of Hung Shue Yeh, then walk through the jungle (there are signs). Thirty minutes later you will find yourself on the nearby Lo So Shing beach. This is the most secluded place on the island of Lamma. The white sand and warm sea are simply amazing. The beach is well maintained and well equipped. There are several bars on the shore that offer light snacks according to national recipes. Rescuers and protective nets from sharks will help you feel completely safe.

Silvermine bay beach

This wonderful place is located on the island of Lantau, in the bay of Silvermine. All guests arriving in Hong Kong try to come here. Beaches of this level attract vacationers with their incredible cleanliness, luxurious landscapes, calm atmosphere. In addition, a gentle approach to the sea is convenient for families with children.

Turtle cove

This beautiful beach is located in the southeast of Hong Kong. He was awarded the first class for serving vacationers. It is very small, but very clean and well maintained. It has all the necessary infrastructure.

Repulse bay

One of the most famous and popular beaches of Hong Kong. It is located in the beautiful bay of the city, in its expensive and prestigious area. Repulse Bay beach is quite long. The water surface is covered with numerous small islands that shelter the bay from the winds. Hotels

We must immediately make a reservation that Hong Kong hotels with a beach are not worth looking for. The hotels of the city do not have their own private beaches, although many of them are located on the seashore. Below we will present you hotels in Hong Kong near the beach.

Auberge Discovery Bay 5 *

The magnificent modern hotel is located in Discovery Bay - one of the most picturesque and prestigious areas on the island of Lantau. It is located among the sea and mountains, away from the bustling city and very close to Disneyland.

The luxurious rooms have panoramic windows with stunning sea views. All of them (regardless of category) are equipped with the necessary equipment, modern comfortable furniture. The hotel has a swimming pool, spa, gym.



Silvermine Beach Resort 4 *

This hotel is also located on the island of Lantau. In close proximity is Paolin Monastery, National Park. The hotel offers 128 air-conditioned rooms with cable TV, a bar. There are two restaurants, a cafe and a bar on the territory . For business meetings there is a business center.

Warwick Hotel 3 *

The hotel is a thirty minute ferry ride from the city center. Near the hotel is a magnificent beach. This hotel is a great vacation spot for both tourists and businessmen. At the reception, which is open around the clock, you can get tips and help organizing excursions and trips around the city.

The hotel offers standard double rooms with stunning views of the beach and natural beauty. All rooms are furnished with new furniture and necessary appliances.



Tourists reviews
