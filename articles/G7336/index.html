Synchronization

There are no interface ports in the Garmin Vivofit Bundle, so sharing it with other equipment is possible in one of the following ways:

There is an opportunity to buy Garmin Vivofit complete with a monitor where the heart rate is displayed. Thus, another function will appear - the heart rate monitor.
