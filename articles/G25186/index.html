Sanatorium, Divnomorskoe. Sanatorium "Divnomorskoe" Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation

Divnomorskoe is located on the Black Sea. It is a small village. Divnomorskoye is located at the mouth of the mountain river Mezib. From the village to the city of Gelendzhik - twelve kilometers.



General information

This place was called "fake Gelendzhik" until 1964. Such a name was received by Divnomorskoye in the last century. This was due to the similarity of certain features of the coast in this place with the entrance to the bay of Gelendzhik. Because of this phenomenon, a lot of confusion arose. In particular, the sailors mistakenly tried to land in the wrong place. The low mountains of the Markhot ridge, covered with mixed forests, surround the village and the coastal valley on three sides.

Weather

A mild maritime climate prevails here. Many factors have a beneficial effect on the mood and health of vacationers. A huge number of sunny days can be attributed to them - there are more than one hundred and fifty of them. In addition, there is picturesque nature and the gentle sea. Divnomorskoe annually receives a large number of tourists. The infrastructure is widely developed here. One of the popular health resorts in the village is considered to be a Navy sanatorium. Divnomorskoye has all the conditions for recreation and recreation.



Historical information

The military sanatorium "Divnomorskoe" began to be created back in 1923. This happened largely thanks to the efforts of Belorezov Alexei Gavrilovich. At that time, he served as chairman of ChOSK. Aleksey Gavrilovich, using the cash desk and his personal savings, was engaged in the reconstruction of farm buildings and former landowners' dachas. Later, the opening of the first trade union rest house in this area. A small children's sanatorium was also organized. Divnomorskoe was an ideal place for the formation of a health resort. The staff consisted of 57 employees. In just a few years, the military sanatorium "Divnomorskoe" was able to assemble a large medical and diagnostic base. Later, a small hydropathic center, dental and physiotherapy rooms began to function here. Mud procedures became one of the main ones that the sanatorium offered. Divnomorskoe is a place with unique natural conditions. All of them contributed to the effective recovery of visitors. Widely used in a boarding house and x-ray studies. According to 1926, about six thousand mud procedures were carried out here.





In 1953, the health resort turned into a military sanatorium. Divnomorskoye became the place where employees went for treatment. A new young team was formed in the holiday home. He made every effort to create the necessary conditions for the recovery of sailors and their families. The health resort staff independently repaired service and residential buildings, as well as ennobled the territory. In 1976, the staff of the rest house totaled about four hundred employees. Later, Colonel Kochetov VM became its leader. Construction of residential premises for personnel was in full swing. In the fall of 1980, the holiday home became a climate sanatorium. Divnomorskoye has become one of the few places where a health resort is located for army personnel. In the future, it actively developed and expanded. Huge work has been done to organize the healing process and provide services for holidaymakers.
