New Year's deer from felt: pattern and instructions

If you have been doing needlework and creating Christmas souvenirs for a long time , and your new idea is a decorative deer made of felt, a pattern of a soft toy that you will see below, you will definitely like it. For beginners, there are simpler options, consisting of two identical parts or even performed without threads, and in the application technique. In a word, everyone will find a suitable idea.





Souvenir Options

New Year's deer from felt can be both flat and voluminous, like soft toys.

Funny figures can be used as the following New Year's souvenirs:

  • interior decorations;
  • Christmas tree toys;
  • a magnet;
  • key fob;
  • gift wrapping;
  • ordinary children's soft toys;
  • element of any decor.

As you can see, there may be several options. Simple felt products, such as magnets or deer-shaped charms, can even be made by children.





How to sew a deer

In order for you to get a beautiful and neat felt deer, the pattern must be chosen correctly. The template should be selected in accordance with the experience and level of training of the needlewoman. For beginners, options consisting of a minimum number of parts and, accordingly, seams are suitable. When you master simple souvenirs, you can move on to more complex products, voluminous soft toys that are sewn from more details.

The overall sequence of work will be as follows:

  1. Cut out a paper template that you can print from the Internet or draw yourself.
  2. Place the details on the flaps of felt, pin with pins, circle the contours with chalk. If necessary, add allowances and cut elements.
  3. Sew or immediately sew carefully all the necessary parts manually or on a typewriter. Felt is good because it does not require processing of the slices, as it does not crumble, so you can stitch the elements even on the front side.
  4. Turn out the sewn parts if you were sewing from the inside out. If you worked on the front side, skip this step.
  5. Fill the finished product or individual blanks (depending on the complexity of the object) with holofiber or other prepared material.
  6. Sew the holes that were left for packing.
  7. Combine all the details into one object.
  8. Sew or glue small decor (ears, eyes).

All is ready.





Materials and Tools

You will need the following to get a beautiful New Year's deer:

  • pattern;
  • pins
  • a piece of chalk;
  • felt;
  • scissors;
  • threads
  • needle;
  • sewing machine;
  • filler (holofiber, cotton wool, synthetic winterizer);
  • glue;
  • any additional decor (beads, ribbons, bows, sequins).

If you will make magnets, you will need a base (magnetic strip), for a key fob - a chain, braid or ribbon.
