Via Mobile Bank

One of the most convenient solutions to the problem of how to send money from card to card (Sberbank) is the Mobile Bank service. In order to make a transfer, you only need a mobile phone and a phone number of the payee. Of course, both participants in the procedure must have Sberbank of Russia cards, the Mobile Bank service is activated, and the phone number that is used to transfer money must be registered in the bank's system.

Then everything is simple. Send SMS number 900 with the following contents: TRANSFER NNNNNNNNNN SSS, where N..N is the telephone number of the payee in ten-digit format (without 8), SSS is the payment amount (in rubles). The disadvantage of this method is the restriction on the amount of transfer. It is eight thousand rubles a day.

Still how to send an SMS with money to a Sberbank card? In the previously described command, instead of the phone number, indicate the last four digits of your credit card number and the last four digits of the recipient's credit card number. For example, 9999 8888 500, where 500 is the payment amount. The limit on the amount of transfer is five thousand rubles per day. Agree, convenient?
