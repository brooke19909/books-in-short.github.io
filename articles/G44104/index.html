Hotel "Bristol" (Pyatigorsk): address, description of rooms, infrastructure, photos and reviews

Since 2011, the Bristol Spa Hotel in Pyatigorsk has been operating. The author's design of the institution, friendly staff, a favorable location, opportunities for healing - all this and much more makes this hotel one of the favorite accommodation options among city guests.

Location

Hotel Bristol is 3 km from the train station and 24 km from Mineralnye Vody Airport. Address - Pyatigorsk, Soborna street, 19. This is the very center of the city near the resort park "Flower Garden".
