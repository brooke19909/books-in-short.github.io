How to send money from a phone to a card? Rules of Procedure

Every day many different financial transactions are made. One of them is the transfer of funds from the phone to the card. For many, this procedure has become familiar, but some still do not know about it. How to send money from a phone to a card? This procedure is simple. Each operator has a slightly different one.

Why do you need a translation?

If a large amount is on the balance of the phone, then it can be transferred. Many accidentally replenish the account from the card, and therefore it is required to make a reverse transfer of funds. In any case, the money in the account can be transferred to the card. Now such services are provided by mobile operators. Popular ones include Beeline, Megafon and MTS. Each company has its own characteristics regarding operations. There are also some limitations.

In addition to transferring money, subscribers of any operator can perform other operations in their personal account. There you can pay for Internet, utilities and fines. Also, the system allows you to pay loans. Operators are constantly expanding the range of services, which is becoming very convenient for subscribers.

What is needed for this?

Translations require a computer, laptop or tablet. The main thing is that you can access the Internet from your device. Also required are the details of the card for which funds will be transferred. Usually you need to know the number, expiration date of its validity. You must register in your account on the operator‚Äôs website. From it it will be possible to carry out various actions, including translations.





Also with mobile operators it will turn out to send money to the phone from the card of Sberbank. It is enough to go to Sberbank online, find the desired form, enter the number and amount. After that, you need to confirm the operation. Money is credited instantly.

Beeline

How to send money from a phone to a card if the Beeline operator? There are two ways from which you can choose the most acceptable. The first option is to visit the official website of the company. You must select the section of money transfers to the card. In the window that appears, fill in the data. Need a card number, your phone, amount. At the end, the operation must be confirmed using SMS. Then a receipt will appear that can be printed.

There is another way to transfer funds. To do this, you will need a phone and card details. The message must write: "VISA card number amount." SMS is sent to 7878, there is no charge for this. Then it will be necessary to confirm the operation.

There are limits on the amount. The minimum is 50 rubles, and the maximum is 14,000 rubles. For each operation, 5.95% + 10 rubles are charged. On the balance sheet after sending money should be at least 50 rubles.

"Megaphone"

How to send money to a card via phone, if the operator "Megaphone"? Like Beeline, there are 2 ways to do this. First you need to visit the company website, select the translation section. In the field that appears, you need to log in. Then you should enter the card number, its validity and amount. Then a message will be received on the telephone, to which a confirmation must be sent.





To use the second option, you do not need a computer, you need a phone. The message must write: "card, card number, expiration date of the card, amount." SMS is sent to number 3116. The operator does not take money for it. You can send 1-15 thousand rubles to a Sberbank card. If you transfer up to 5 thousand, then the commission will be equal to 7.35% + 95 rubles. If the transfer amount is more, then 7.35% + 259 rubles.

MTS

How to send money from MTS from phone to card? In your personal account you must visit the section "Easy payment". There will be transfers to a bank card. Enter the required information in the field that appears. Then a message will arrive. In response, you need to confirm the operation. Only then will the funds be credited.

Send money to the card by phone number will be within the limited amount - 50-15 000 rubles. The number of transfers is five. The commission is 4%, but not less than 60 rubles for 1 operation.

These simple instructions let you know how to send money from your phone to a card. Such services are very convenient, because the procedures are performed without leaving home. All you need is a computer or phone.

Uniform conditions

The period of receipt of funds can be up to 3 days. This is determined by the work of the banking processing center. If there is no payment on the card, then you need to contact a specialist on the hotline. To do this, it is advisable to have a receipt with you, since the payment is verified according to its data. This will clarify the circumstances of the transaction.
