Spain, Costa Dorada: Hotel of Dreams

Costa Dorada in Spanish means "golden coast". This coast stretches south from the town of Cunit to the resort of Alcanar, where the Valencia district already begins. Where does the name come from the coast? The beaches here have a lot of mica, so if you stir the sand in the water, it rises and then falls off with a lot of bright scales, sparkling like golden splashes. This is what Spain, Costa Dorada is famous for. A hotel or private apartments here can be found for every taste, since there is plenty to choose from. After all, the coast stretches along the Mediterranean Sea for more than a dozen kilometers.



The capital of the Costa Dorada is the second largest city in Catalonia - Tarragona. And the most significant resorts that everyone is hearing about are Salou with its famous Port Aventura park, Cambrils, L'Ametilla de Mar, Monroch, Perello and La Cava at the mouth of the Ebro River . And in each of these listed towns, as well as in the mass not yet named here, you can find suitable housing for a good rest. Places in the sun on wide beaches will be enough for everyone.
