Exchange rating: review, reliability

Cryptocurrency trading is a common thing for many Internet users who have been mining for years or investing in ICOs. However, this issue may lead to newcomers to a dead end. After all, you need to be not only aware of the real real value of a particular cryptocurrency, but also choose the right trading platform. Only a stock exchange verified over the years, carrying out thousands of financial transactions per day, can ensure the security of the transaction, as well as guarantee full payment to the account of a user who has entrusted their funds to the trading platform. Based on the reviews and comments left on the forums dedicated to the topic of cryptocurrency trading, we made our own rating of cryptocurrency exchanges that you can trust.

Bittrex

Bittrex exchange (bittrex.com) leads our rating. This trading platform is a contender for the title of the largest online platform that allows you to exchange cryptocurrency for fiat money. The exchange is founded and registered in the United States of America. Pride Bittrex - an ultramodern server that allows you to quickly and without any ‚Äúhangs‚Äù to process millions of operations per minute.

For users of the exchange who are accustomed to making money on fluctuations in the rate of cryptocurrencies, there are a huge number of trading pairs of altcoins. This platform offers current courses, constantly monitoring the global cryptocurrency markets.

The main advantages of the Bittrex platform:

  • is one of the most popular in the world, which indicates the absolute trust of users;
  • in addition to the main cryptocurrencies, it offers a large number of altcoins for exchange;
  • has an intuitive interface for both desktop and mobile browsers;
  • Efficiency of technical support reaction to users ‚Äôrequests.

The obvious cons of the Bittrex platform:

  • registration is temporarily closed for new users;
  • there is a restriction on the withdrawal of money - the maximum amount should not exceed 0.4 MTC per day;
  • wallets are periodically turned off, as a rule, at the time of a sharp jump in the rate of major cryptocurrencies.

Despite its shortcomings, Bittrex tops the list of the best exchanges. By entrusting your funds to this trading platform, you can be sure of the safety of funds, the security of transactions. And this is one of the main conditions of any trader.

Poloniex

In the ranking of the best cryptocurrency exchanges, the Poloniex platform (poloniex.com) is in second place. It is very similar to Bittrex. It is also convenient and offers a huge number of major altcoins for exchange. Registration here is open to new users. However, in order to withdraw money, it is necessary to verify. The administration approaches the confirmation of identity quite seriously. To create a verified account, you will need to go through several stages and provide a fairly solid package of documents, which will take a lot of time.

The main advantages that allowed to take second place in the ranking of cryptocurrency exchanges include:

  • good technical support, instantly responding to user requests;
  • the presence of its own "cold" storage, which allows the most reliable storage of user deposits offline;
  • the most beautiful and intuitive interface designed for both desktop versions of the browser and mobile;
  • It has large volumes of popular altcoins, including Ether.

Like any other platform included in the rating of exchanges, Poloniex has its drawbacks:

  • there is no way to replenish your wallet with fiat money;
  • a withdrawal limit is set - a maximum of $ 2,000 per day.

Users working with Poloniex for many years will remember that the exchange had serious security issues. Several years ago, hackers managed to successfully attack the trading floor, steal a large amount of altcoins and fiat. But at the moment, according to the exchange administration, there are no problems.

Coinbase

In third place in the ranking of cryptocurrency exchanges for Russia is the Coinbase platform (coinbase.com), founded and registered in the United States of America. This online platform that allows you to exchange cryptocurrency for fiat is one of the oldest, and its investment estimate has long exceeded the mark of 1 billion dollars. Unlike most trading platforms that represent the rating of exchanges, Coinbase is more like an electronic wallet that allows you to exchange crypto for fiat.

The main advantages of this exchange can be considered:

  • ease of use;
  • Attractive and intuitive interface
  • the presence of mobile applications;
  • the ability to input / output fiat;
  • no withdrawal restrictions;
  • quick response of technical support to user requests;
  • maximum degree of protection for customers' online wallets;
  • absolute user trust (Coinbase already has more than 5 million regular customers).

Despite the fact that Coinbase is among the first three leading the rating of exchanges, the trading platform has its drawbacks:

  • Lack of anonymity. In order to become a verified Coinbase user, the client must disclose quite a lot of personal information that will be sent to the tax office. Without verification, the withdrawal of funds is impossible.

For residents of Russia and Ukraine, it is important to know that they will not have full access to all the tools of the exchange. For example, you cannot enter fiat from a debit card.

SEX

In fourth place in the ranking of cryptocurrency exchanges is the SEX platform (cex.io). This platform was created as a service for cloud mining, but since its foundation (in 2013), SEX has been repeatedly upgraded to become a multifunctional cryptocurrency exchange.

Despite the fact that this platform is in fourth place in our ranking of exchanges and its popularity is much lower than that of its predecessors, SEX has an undeniable advantage. The online platform allows you to not only change cryptocurrency to fiat, but also instantly withdraw real money to a plastic card. SEX offers cross-platform trading through the REST API, Web, WebSocket and mobile application.

In the ranking of cryptocurrency exchanges for Russia, cex.io will confidently enter the top three leaders. Why? SEX accepts for exchange all the world's leading currencies, including rubles. Thanks to this platform, cryptocurrencies such as BTC, ETN, ZCash, Dash can be quickly and safely exchanged for fiat. Popular altcoins are also featured here.

Positive aspects of SEX:

  • the ability to carry out financial transactions with debit and credit cards;
  • intuitive interface that allows even an inexperienced user to understand all the tools;
  • lack of hidden fees;
  • Russified technical support.

Negative sides:

  • rather rare update of trading pairs.

Binance

Binance (binance.com) is a relatively young currency exchange whose rating is constantly growing. Due to the fallen popularity, the administration suspended the registration of new users. The forced measures are explained by the fact that for the stable operation of the service, the site infrastructure must be changed. Most likely, the exchange, whose server is capable of processing up to 1.5 million orders per second, turned out to be unprepared for the rapid growth of new users.

The first auction on Binance was held 07/15/2017. From this moment, both beginners and experienced traders began to show interest in the exchange. Most users were attracted by customer focus, a convenient and intuitive interface, and the ability to maintain anonymity. However, the site became truly popular only when it offered its users the option of PoS mining. For storing the NEO cryptocurrency, which was popular at the end of 2017, a fee of 0.1% of the total deposit is charged.

Strengths of Binance:

  • quite a wide selection of trading pairs;
  • minimum commission;
  • large set of tools;
  • the ability to carry out financial transactions without verification and registration;
  • maximum withdrawal amount (for an unregistered user) - 2 MTC per day;
  • PoS mining.

Weaknesses:

  • Quite often the main page of the site freezes.
