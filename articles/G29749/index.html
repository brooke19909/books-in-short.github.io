Rest in Crimea with children: vacationers reviews only positive

As summer vacations draw near, the question always arises of where to relax and where to go. Of course, now there are a huge number of offers on tourist trips to different countries of the world. This is undoubtedly a wonderful vacation. But if the family plans to spend a vacation with children, especially small ones, then it is not always worth the risk and go on such a long journey. In this case, local climatic conditions will be most suitable. You can spend your holidays in Crimea with children. Reviews about this resort are the most positive.

Big Yalta

Starting from the majestic Ayudag Mountain, to the very southern cape Sarych stretched Big Yalta. The unique climate, clean air, warm sea created ideal conditions to get a wonderful vacation in Crimea with children. Reviews about the resorts of Big Yalta are only good, because here, in picturesque places, among the mountains there are the best hotels.

The sanatorium complexes of Yalta, Alupka, Simeiz, Foros offer excellent healing and treatment of many diseases. In addition, interesting and informative sightseeing tours will allow you to have a good time. Children will be interested in visiting the wonderful Yalta Zoo, "Glade of Fairy Tales", have fun in the water park. Crimea has healing air and a unique climate. Vacations with children are collected only by the most wonderful reviews, because here you have the opportunity to heal your child and take interesting entertainments.


Rest in Evpatoria

Those who want a relaxing holiday on the shores of the warm Black Sea with sandy beaches should go to Evpatoria. This is a true paradise for children, where dozens of unique health resorts and resorts are concentrated. If you choose Crimea, an inexpensive vacation with children will turn out in Yevpatoriya. Prices here are much lower than on the south coast, and allow you to spend a good and quality vacation with the whole family. In Yevpatoriya, all conditions for children's recreation are created, there are many different attractions and entertainments, and most importantly, the warm and shallow sea. In local sanatoriums, diseases such as bone tuberculosis, bronchial asthma, and various nervous disorders are successfully treated.



Resort Sudak

Many good places are intended to spend holidays in Crimea with children. Reviews speak of their popularity and availability. The city of Sudak is considered one of the best resorts , it is called the real pearl of the Crimean peninsula. It is located in its southeastern part. It has a unique climate that is close to the Mediterranean, mild and warm, without any extreme temperature changes and with a large number of sunny days per year. In Sudak, beautiful wide beaches that stretch for almost two kilometers, with dark gray sand, rich in silicon, the sea is shallow with a gentle entrance. This is a great place to travel with young children, it is here that you can have a good rest in harmony with nature, restore strength, improve your body and enjoy the picturesque scenery. A wonderful pastime will be a vacation in the Crimea with children. Reviews of such tours indicate the popularity and attractiveness of the resort.
