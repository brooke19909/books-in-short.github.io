Pleshcheyevo Lake, Pereslavl-Zalessky: how to get there, rest, fishing, recreation centers

In the south of the Yaroslavl region, in the Upper Volga basin, is located the Pleshcheyevo Lake National Park (Pereslavl-Zalessky). On this territory several architectural, historical and natural monuments are combined. About 20 species of animals listed in the Red Book live here. Also in this area there are many rare representatives of the flora that are under protection. This park includes a lake. It was formed more than 30,000 years ago. It is currently a popular tourist attraction. In summer you can go windsurfing and kite surfing. During the winter season, activities such as snowkiting are also offered. Tourists can settle in recreation centers or in hotels located on the shore. Rest in Pereslavl-Zalessky, on Lake Pleshcheevo, will give a lot of pleasant experiences.



Description

The lake is located in the southwest of the Yaroslavl region, in the Pereslavl region. Freshwater pond, formed after the retreat of the glaciers. Has more than 10 titles. However, Pleshcheyevo Lake is considered official. Pereslavl-Zalessky - a city located on the shore of a reservoir. He served the emergence of one of the names - Pereslavl. However, it is used only in the everyday life of local residents. According to historians, the correct name is associated with the disappeared old Russian city of Kleshchin. It was he who in the XI-XII centuries was located on the territory of modern Pereslavl-Zalessky. And according to popular legends, the lake got its name due to its unique property - to throw away all the things that are thrown into it.

The reservoir is home to more than 15 species of fish. This is burbot, buster, pike and so on. One can observe how a white-tailed eagle, a heron and seagulls soar in the air above the water surface. Waterfowl also live here: swan, duck and others.

The lake is used in fishing. As a rule, on an industrial scale, fish species such as ruff, perch, etc. are caught. It is also worth noting that numerous attractions are located on its shores.

Climatic features

Before you go on vacation to this area, you need to learn more about the weather here. Pleshcheyevo Lake (Pereslavl-Zalessky) is located in a zone with pronounced seasonality. The climate is humid, temperate continental. The coldest month is January. The temperature here drops to -12 ¬∞ C. This season can be described as moderately cold. The lake freezes in November. The maximum heat peak occurs in July. This month the average temperature is around + 18 ... + 20 ¬∞ . There is no particular stuffiness, the summer is moderately warm. As for precipitation, most of it falls in the winter. On average, about 500-600 mm fall out per year.

Relaxation

Just 150 km from Moscow is the city of Pereslavl-Zalessky. Lake Pleshcheyevo (camping is popular here) is one of the interesting places of the Golden Ring. Muscovites with great pleasure come to these places to spend their holidays or weekends. Numerous hotels and tourist centers have been built on the shores of the reservoir, but there is also a place for lovers of "wild" relaxation. Camping sites are allowed on the lake. As a rule, they are erected near the beaches. Here you can sunbathe, wander around the local surroundings, visit the sights and go fishing.

Beaches

This information will be useful to those who decided to spend their holidays in a city such as Pereslavl-Zalessky. The beach of Lake Pleshcheyev will surprise many who choose paid places. Everything is covered with clean sand. There are cafes, equipment rental. There are rides on the shore. The only drawback, although for some this is an advantage, is the shallow depth. The water level at a distance of 50 m from the beach does not rise above the belt. That is why a paid beach is ideal for families with small children. The administration offers to go fishing. Its cost is purely symbolic - 300 rubles. They give out all the necessary equipment. The advantage of a paid beach is that the entire territory is protected and constantly cleaned.

There are also free places on the lake. However, according to the reviews of vacationers, swimming there will not be very pleasant. Mostly they are heavily overgrown with grass and reeds. The bottom is usually muddy, a lot of garbage. Most of all, these places are suitable for fishermen to launch the boat.



Recreation facilities on the lake

Tourists who do not want to relax in tents, but at the same time try to save on accommodation, are invited to stay at recreation centers. The infrastructure of the lake is currently very well developed. Each vacationer will be able to find for himself the most suitable option. Entertainment is organized at any time of the year. It is important that everyone will be able to fully spend time visiting Lake Pleshcheyevo (Pereslavl-Zalessky). Recreation facilities that are equipped on the shore are in high demand. They offer comfortable houses for living, a clean equipped area, secure parking and clean beaches. As a rule, cafes and small restaurants work at the bases, there are playgrounds and attractions. Let's look at some of them.



"Blue Stone"

Not far from the reservoir, just 200 m, there is a beautiful recreation center "Blue Stone". Everyone who comes here appreciates the fragrant air, coniferous forest, birdsong and clear lake water. It is located very close to a city such as Pereslavl-Zalessky. Plescheeva Lake Beach is a 5-minute walk away. Vacationers are invited to visit the Russian bathhouse, a small restaurant that serves delicious dishes. For celebrations or other events, you can rent a gazebo or a terrace with a barbecue. Due to the fact that it is not allowed to light open fire on the territory of the lake, everyone who wants to grill kebabs in the open air will need to use the barbecue area.

On the base you can rent fishing equipment. The administration also offers various excursion programs. They include visits to ancient temples, architectural and historical monuments, museums. Everyone who wants to spend time actively can do kitesurfing, ride bicycles or just train at equipped sports fields. Wooden cottages are provided for living: two-, four- and eight-seater. For one day you will need to pay from 2500 to 8000 rubles. for the house. In order to get to this recreation center, you must go along the Yaroslavl highway. Having passed through the whole city, at the sign "Nikitsky Monastery" you should turn left. Then you need to continue driving along a dirt straight road to the reservoir itself. Near the lake there will be a right turn, after which it is still necessary to drive about 3 km to the fence from the stockade. This will be the recreation center "Blue Stone".

Not far away is a local attraction, famous throughout the region for its miraculous power. This is a blue stone. On the territory of the base there is a pond equipped with sturgeon and trout. If desired, you can organize fishing. For corporate events, it is proposed to rent a gazebo. There are also small barbecue areas equipped with benches and a table. The recreation center is quiet, so you can come here for families with children.

Fishing

Fans of turning gray with a fishing rod also enjoy visiting the city of Pereslavl-Zalessky. Lake Pleshcheyevo (fishing here can always please with a good catch) is saturated with a rich underwater world. More than 10 species of fish live here. Best peck pike, roach, crucian carp and breeding. There are several restrictions on fishing in this region. First of all, you cannot use networks. From mid-November to early January, even fishing is strictly prohibited, as it is during this period that spawning occurs. If you break the rules, you will be fined. In winter, fish bites best on bloodworms, bread or worms. If there is a desire to catch a burbot, it must be borne in mind that it lives at a depth of not more than 14 m. The most unique species that the pond is famous for is vendace. It is recommended to catch it only on spinners. Roach can peck at a depth of 4 m.

In summer, fishermen come here for bream, roach, pike. The latter is recommended to catch near the undergrowth. During fishing, you need to use a boat, as the largest individuals are found at great depths. Professionals advise not to choose a thick fishing line, since the water in the lake is very clear. The cost of fishing is about 250 rubles per day.

Local attraction

Pleshcheyevo Lake (Pereslavl-Zalessky) impresses with its unique sights. Blue stone is one of them. It got its name due to the fact that when wet, a pronounced gray-blue hue appears on it. Its origin dates back to the times of the ice age. Currently, it returns only 30 cm above the water. However, earlier it was seen more than 1.5 m. According to some reports, the huge size of this formation is hidden under the water. And what is visible above the earth is just a small part. The approximate weight of the stone is 12 tons. This place is unique not only as a natural monument. According to legend, the stone has healing properties. If you touch it or sit on it, you can be healed of many diseases.

Breakfast is included in the price, it is possible to order lunch and dinner in the room, but for a fee. The Komsomolskaya Hotel belongs to the economy class. Rest of one guest costs about 1000 rubles. But a family of two will need to pay only 1200 rubles.



Pereslavl-Zalessky, Lake Plescheevo: how to get there?

There are two ways to get to the lake: by private car or public transport. If tourists are satisfied with the latter option, then in case of a trip from Moscow they need to use intercity buses. They depart from the Central Bus Station, the route to Pereslavl-Zalessky. The ticket price is about 150 rubles.

In order to get to the lake by private car, it is necessary to overcome a distance of 120-150 km. In time it will take no more than 2 hours. From Moscow it is necessary to leave on the highway E115 / M8. There are enough signs on it, so it's almost impossible to get lost.
