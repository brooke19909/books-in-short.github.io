Sanatorium "Salute" (Sochi): description, photos, reviews of vacationers

In a picturesque and convenient corner of the Black Sea coast is the sanatorium "Salute" (Sochi). Photos of this comfortable health resort you will see below. She is subordinate to the Main Directorate of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Russian Federation. On the territory of the sanatorium there are two buildings for guests: a 16-story building, designed for 224 places, and a three-story building for 226 people. The health resort is located at: Sochi, st. Sanatorium, 49. There is a private beach at the disposal. It is equipped with sun loungers, changing cabins and showers.

Sanatorium Park

In 1938, near the building of the sanatorium, the leadership set up a park of subtropical cultures. It is located on the slope of the Grape Mountain, which offers beautiful views of the coast.

The history of the sanatorium

The health resort is located in one of the most beautiful corners of the Black Sea coast. This is the oldest sanatorium in Sochi. "Salute" first opened its doors to guests in 1926. At that time, he could take 45 people. Before the Great Patriotic War, another building was completed, and the health resort could accommodate 270 guests. A year later, the following appeared on the territory:

  • summer cinema;
  • electric base;
  • a park.

War years

During the war years, the health resort turned into evacuation hospital No. 2133, in which the medical staff raised wounded soldiers to their feet. The medical institution was glorified by Vladimir Dmitrievich Bantov, the only bone surgery doctor in the city of Sochi. At that difficult time, he did operations in the morning, afternoon and evening.

Doctors-assistants Bantova:

  • Friedman
  • Nailing;
  • Gorodetskaya.




In the summer of 1942, when the German invaders came very close to Tuapse, the military leadership decided to evacuate the hospital in Transcaucasia. From October 1, 1942 to July 15, 1943, the health resort was evacuated in the city of Tbilisi. On August 1, 1943, when the threat passed, the hospital was returned to its primary location.

The medical staff was on duty near the soldiers for days. Around the sanatorium grew healing moss. Everyone was engaged in its collection for the manufacture of medicines. After treatment at the hospital, 80% of defenders returned to duty. Bantov developed new techniques, thanks to which soldiers recovered several times faster. Sanatorium "Salute" (Sochi) survived the most terrible war years, but the building still survived, and the staff continued their work. The heyday of the health resort came in the 70s. Then greatly expanded the range of medical services. Doctors began to apply climatotherapy, physiotherapy exercises, and physiotherapy. On the basis of the health resort, new equipment has appeared.
