A little about St. Petersburg

This is the former Leningrad (Petrograd) - a city founded by Peter I and a symbol of the Russian state, imperial power and military glory. What the city and its inhabitants did not survive during their existence: several revolutions, a long-term blockade, the deaths of residents. But he did not give up, he became even more beautiful. Only sixty-seven kilometers are separated by St. Petersburg and Sea Terraces. The village is located at around 25 km along the Primorsky highway. There is the village of Sands in the Vyborg district. Cottage village: description and device





How to reliably describe the "Sea Terraces"? This cottage village is designed for year-round living in high comfort. Such conditions are possible, since the developer has provided for the full laying of engineering infrastructure and roads rolled into asphalt. Security is the responsibility of the security service. And all communications are serviced by an operational service.

The territory of the village stretches along the coast for six hundred and fifty meters and occupies sixty-five hectares of land. In total, two hundred and fifty-seven land plots are provided according to the documentation. Each of them on average takes fifteen hundred parts. But there are lots suitable for the construction of almost estates. Their area reaches seventy hundredths.

In addition, almost seven hectares of land will be occupied by public and administrative-technical zones. Four hectares are also reserved for a walking park area with a lake, a stream, children's and sports grounds. And all this along the coast of the Gulf of Finland, where you can make a leisurely promenade.





Ecological situation

Probably, no other cottage village of St. Petersburg and the Leningrad Region will be able to offer its owners and residents a more favorable ecological environment, as the Sea Terraces offer. The cottage village, the photo of which can be seen in the article, actually meets all the requirements for living in the fresh air.

No other cottage village can boast of a location right on the shores of the Gulf of Finland. Yes, equipped walking areas, where you can walk and listen to the sound of the surf. Only the "Sea Terraces", so named according to their location, provide such comfort.

Internal infrastructure

The developer does not just sell land. He equipped the whole cottage village and adapted it for a comfortable life. Asphalted all roads that lead to the "Sea Terraces", the village. Which area boasts similar? In addition, all roads are lit, as well as driveways with pedestrian zones.





The security of the residents of the village is ensured by three checkpoints with an organized round-the-clock duty service. Also in the village is the administrative and children's centers, playgrounds and sports grounds. For owners of their own cars and guests arriving by car, guest parking has been built. The developer even designed an artificial lake in the center of the Sea Terraces village, so that its residents had an additional place to relax.

If we talk about engineering communications, then they are available. This is electricity up to ten kilowatts per house, and main gas, and central water supply.

Land

All plots of land represented in the cottage village are diverse in their shape and area. There is a strict rectangular shape. There are irregular, cone-shaped. Also, the sites differ in their location: off the coast of the Gulf of Finland or closer to the Primorsky highway. There are also land plots in a pine forest. For those who want to immediately take up construction, flat plots are offered. In the center of the village you can buy plots near an artificial lake.

Land after their acquisition is transferred to the property. And today they are almost all already implemented. All engineering communications are included in the price of the plots.
