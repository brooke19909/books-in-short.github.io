Reliability concept

Before raising the question of which is the most reliable bank in Russia, you should familiarize yourself with the direct definition of reliability. Reliability is understood to mean the ability of an organization to fulfill obligations in relation to its customers in a timely manner. These factors include a wide variety of criteria - from the necessary documentation to the rating of a particular organization. Reliability can be indicated by payments on time, for example, funds on deposits. In addition, providing suitable conditions for customers is an essential criterion for the stability of a banking organization.





It should be noted that if the history of a bank in Russia has a long period, then such an indicator is rightfully considered the most important evidence of its reliability.

Thus, given all the criteria, the organization can be confidently called the most reliable bank in Russia.

Reliability criteria

As mentioned earlier, reliability criteria may include several indicators. In particular, licenses for the right to carry out activities in this area are one of the important factors of importance, but not the only one.

When forming the reliability rating, other equally important criteria are taken into account, for example, such as: liquidity indicator, sufficient capital, suitable refinancing rate, risk level in assets, profitability and others.

Who does the rating





Of course, not all organizations have the opportunity to draw up an appropriate classification of banks. This work is carried out, as a rule, by independent institutions,
specializing in this field: mass media, various associations (for example, journalists), informal entities (which have the right to evaluate for a specific category), special rating agencies and others.

Later, after carrying out all the evaluation activities, the relevant results of the work done are publicly shown in the form of an assessment or sorting of the participants (1st place, 2nd place, etc.).
