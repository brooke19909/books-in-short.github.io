Paradise Valley Hotel in Dzhubga: description, reviews

The resort village of Dzhubga for tourists traveling on vacation to the Krasnodar Territory on the Black Sea, is the first to meet. Trains going to Adler, unfortunately, do not enter this settlement. Most vacation in Dzhubga is popular with motorists who come to the sea by their own cars.

There are a lot of good hotels in this village. For example, the Paradise Valley complex offers good numbers to guests of this resort. Reviews from tourists, this recreation center has earned pretty good.





Where is the complex located

This comfortable hotel is located in the village of Dzhubga. The distance from it to the sea is approximately 800 meters. Thus, guests of this hotel can reach the nearest beach in 10-15 minutes.

Tourists who decide to get to the Paradise Valley Hotel in Dzhubga by train usually leave at Gelendzhik station. From here you can get to the village, for example, by bus. Public transport is approximately 50 meters from the hotel. Gelendzhik also has its own airport. In this case, tourists will first need to get from this transport hub on a minibus to the city, and here already transfer to a flight next to Dzhubga. If desired, vacationers who have rented rooms at the Paradise Valley Hotel can order a transfer from the administration. This service will cost tourists not particularly expensive. But future hotel guests need to agree on a transfer, of course, in advance.

The nearest cafes and restaurants are located on the waterfront of the village. The guests of the complex can reach them in about 7 minutes.
