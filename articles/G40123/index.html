Hotel Kleopatra Sahara Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey): description, leisure and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resort regions of Turkey among CIS citizens has long been Alanya. There are many hotels for every taste and budget. However, economy-class hotels are in special demand in Alanya, so the largest percentage of tourists is represented by young people. In addition to a magnificent beach holiday, you will find a stormy excursion program and the presence of a mass of shops, restaurants, bars, nightclubs and other entertainments for every taste. If you want to have all the infrastructure of the center of this resort region within walking distance, but at the same time you want to be able to relax in a calm and fairly quiet place, then the Kleopatra Sahara Hotel 3 * will be a good option for a budget stay. Today we will find out what is awaiting guests at this hotel, and also try to figure out what impressions our compatriots had on their holidays here.





Location

This nice little hotel has a very good location. So, it is located almost on the seashore, just 1.5 km from the center of the capital of one of the largest resort regions of Turkey - the city of Alanya. Just an hour's drive from the hotel is located one of the most interesting sights of the country - Manavgat Waterfall. The ancient Alanya Fortress is just five kilometers from the hotel. So you can take a pleasant walk to this famous tourist attraction in Turkey. As for the air harbor, the nearest of them is 125 kilometers from the hotel, in the city of Antalya. After landing at the airport, the trip to Kleopatra Sahara Hotel 3 * (Alanya) will take about two hours. But do not worry about this, as the travel time will fly by unnoticed while you enjoy the views from the windows of a bus or a car and listen to the entertaining stories of the guide about Turkey in general and the Alanya region in particular. Since one of the most important points that people pay attention to when choosing a hotel on the coast of a country is the proximity of the beach, we also cannot help but touch upon this issue. As for the hotel we are considering, it does not have its own beach. However, it is located almost on the coast. The distance from the main hotel building to the nearest municipal beach is only 100 meters. At the same time, along the way, you will have to cross the roadway and cross the promenade stretching along the coast.





How to get to the hotel

If you buy a tour to Kleopatra Sahara Hotel 3 * through a travel agency, then most likely you will be taken from the airport to the hotel by bus accompanied by a guide. If you plan to travel to Alanya on your own, then you will have to worry about transport to the hotel yourself. By the way, most hotels offer their own transfer services from the air harbor. However, often they can not be called cheap. Therefore, we propose to consider more economical options. So, if you arrive at Antalya Airport, you can get to Alanya by bus. They go regularly, and one way travel will cost you about five euros. Travel time will be about two hours. In Alanya, an intercity bus will take you to the station, located three kilometers from the city center. From here, the hotel can be reached either by city bus or taxi. If you plan to travel to other cities of Turkey before arriving in Alanya, you can choose a flight to Gazipasa Airport. It is only half an hour from Alanya and serves only domestic flights.

In addition, you can get to Alanya by sea. So, ferries from Kyrenia, located in Northern Cyprus, regularly arrive at the port of this city. The cost of such a trip is 32 euros one way.

Hotel check-in rules

In accordance with the internal rules accepted at the Cleopatra Sahara Hotel, check-in of guests is carried out from 12 noon. However, this does not mean that, having arrived at the hotel earlier, you will certainly have to expect noon. If Kleopatra Sahara has free rooms, you will be checked in immediately upon arrival. However, in the midst of the tourist season, the hotel is one hundred percent full, so if you arrive early, you will have to wait until the previous guests have vacated the rooms and the maids have prepared them properly. You can shorten the waiting time by going to a restaurant, to the pool or straight to the beach. As for the departure date, you need to free your room before 11 a.m. At the same time, when handing over the keys to the reception, you should pay for the entire period of stay at the hotel. We accept cash and credit cards Visa and Mastercard.





Accommodation for tourists with children

Since the Kleopatra Sahara 3 * hotel is positioning itself, among other things, as a family institution, you will be only happy here if you come to rest with children of any age. At the same time, if you place a child up to three years old on existing bedding or a baby up to two years old in a crib, then you will not have to pay extra for this. If you are staying in your room for an older child or an adult in an extra bed, this will cost you 10 euros per day. Moreover, in each room only one extra adult bed or playpen can be installed .

Accommodation with pets

This item will be very important for those tourists who do not want to part with their four-legged pets, even during the holidays. So, unfortunately, the domestic rules of the Kleopatra Sahara Hotel prohibit pets from traveling on site. Thus, you just have to either look for another hotel belonging to the Pets Friends category or leave your pet at home for the duration of the trip.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, Kleopatra Sahara Beach Hotel 3 * consists of one building, which includes 33 standard double and triple rooms. All apartments are comfortable and simply decorated. Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer, air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone and a balcony or terrace. On the floor - ceramic coating. The hotel provides housekeeping on a regular basis, as well as the replacement of towels and bed linen. In addition, guests have the opportunity to order a room service.

Sea and beach

As already mentioned, Kleopatra Sahara Hotel does not have its own beach. The nearest beach is a hundred meters from the hotel and is municipal. Here for an additional fee you can use sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas, as well as a variety of equipment for entertainment and sports on the water.

Entertainment and infrastructure

Since Kleopatra Sahara Hotel 3 * is a small hotel, there is no such wide selection of entertainment as in large hotel complexes. However, in Cleopatra Sahara guests can have a good time. So, at their service there is an outdoor pool with a sun terrace, equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas, a volleyball court, a pool for children. The nearby beach has everything you need for relaxation and sports on the water.

With regard to infrastructure, the three-star Cleopatra Sahara Hotel (Alanya, Turkey) has all the conditions for a comfortable stay for guests. So, the rooms here are equipped with everything necessary, as well as a restaurant, bar and parking. By contacting the reception, you can exchange currency, call a doctor, rent a car, book a tour and get answers to all your questions. You can also store things in the safe at the reception. Wi-Fi access is available in public areas of the hotel.





Cost of living

Since the Cleopatra Sahara hotel (Alanya, Turkey) is a three-star hotel, it can be classified as budget in the price category. So, a seven-day stay here at the height of the tourist season (July-August) will cost you from 23,000 rubles (for single occupancy), from 27,250 rubles (for a double room) and 37,600 rubles (for a triple room). The indicated prices include all inclusive meals.
