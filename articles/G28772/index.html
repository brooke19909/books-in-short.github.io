What to do with the old TV? Purchase and disposal of TVs

An obligatory attribute of spending leisure time for many people in our country is television. But unfortunately, this type of technology often breaks down. In addition, more and more advanced models appear annually, which are even more practical and of high quality. Sometimes they decide to change this type of equipment because of a breakdown or because a new device has appeared on the market. And what about the old TV? What to do with it so as not to harm the environment? In fact, there are plenty of places to put on an old TV, which, in addition to eliminating unnecessary trash, will also bring a small monetary reward.

Do not dispose of electrical appliances and household appliances in a landfill! It is prohibited by law.

The main rule is that it is strictly forbidden to throw old equipment into the trash bin. It is very harmful to the environment. The fact is that the details of the TV contain various heavy metals, and plastic emits toxic substances when burned. Simply put, such garbage will lie in a landfill for a long time and not decompose under the influence of external factors. Old TV is not subject to dumping in a general landfill and under the law. Since it has a third class of environmental hazard.





Among other things, throwing the old TV in a landfill, you can get a considerable fine for breaking the law. Nevertheless, it is necessary to get used to take care of the ecological state of the world and the area in which we live. Therefore, do not consider any garbage containers, landfills or simply landfills for household waste as a place for the disposal of equipment.

Reception of old TVs

Where to put the TV, which has become unusable? The first option is a special bureau. You can just pass the equipment into it. In such bureaus, old TVs are received for recycling. These companies are engaged in the purchase of devices with a view to their further disassembly into components. Specialists use some of the parts for repair, while others start processing. By the way, buying up old TVs and other equipment is not only large companies, but also small workshops. Skilled specialists can take out many details from the devices and use them in their work.

other methods

What to do with the old TV? The very old equipment, which was inherited from the parents, can be turned over to a museum or to a company for creating real quests, where they make interiors for games.





Another option is to contact the owners of the slot machines. They will happily adopt a similar technique. It only needs to be equipped with a sufficiently large screen. Also, owners of small cafes will accept an outdated TV to place it in the hall. But remember that this must be a flat screen model.

Disposal of devices and equipment

Disposal of TVs takes place in several stages. First you bring the master your technique. Then the specialist removes all the valuable details. Then the remaining elements are disassembled into parts. Then they are sent for processing. All glass parts, as well as metal, are crushed. After which they will be further used in the processing industry. All plastic components, including the housing from under the TV, go for remelting. That's exactly how TVs are disposed of.

Where to hand over the device with a kinescope in the case? Such TVs are extremely rare in recent years. But they are still in demand. They are also taken for recycling by a recycling company. And sometimes such a TV brings more profit than usual. That's because there are more parts in such household appliances that are made of precious metals. Silver and gold from the parts goes to the manufacture of new parts for computers, televisions and other household appliances. Products made of ferrous metal are simply remelted. Liquid crystal screens handed over for recycling are used for the manufacture of gadgets and new modern technology.

Buying up TVs is a fairly common type of business. Therefore, even in a small town, you can find a place where to send the old device.





Too many companies that are engaged in the disposal of equipment place their advertisements in the local area. By calling the indicated number, you can find out the conditions for the disposal of old equipment. After which you can order a master who will come to you at a convenient time. Representatives of the company will take out your equipment yourself with the help of workers. At the same time, they will also independently remove all garbage. This method of disposal is much better than getting rid of the equipment yourself.

Sales Announcements

What to do with an old TV with a kinescope that is in working condition? It can be sold. For example, putting it on one of the trading floors on the Internet. You can also advertise in the newspaper. Believe me, there will definitely be those people who want to buy your home appliances for the home or the cottage. Often, old TVs at a low cost are bought either by young families or students. Also, such a technique is acquired by companies of people who live together on the same living space. Usually they have a small income. Therefore, they, unfortunately, have no opportunity to purchase a new TV.

Give to children or the elderly

And what about the old TV of a newer model, which can still work for a long time? A good option is a nursing home or an orphanage.

In such places, household appliances are usually not enough. For this reason, residents of such houses will gladly accept useful things.





Just remember that when giving household appliances to a municipal institution, you must be completely sure of its integrity and working condition. After all, everyone who gives a thing to such places is responsible for it. Of course, in this case, the benefits are not worth talking about. But, despite the fact that you will not receive money, you can please those in need, which means you will do a good deed.

Exchange of old equipment for new

Where to rent an old TV in exchange for a new one? Many hardware stores hold similar promotions. You can learn about them from advertising. She is usually shown on TV. By the way, many stores still help with the delivery of old equipment to the place. Therefore, such an exchange can be considered a very advantageous offer.





An old TV is, most likely, a rather cumbersome thing that cannot be simply removed from the occupied space on the balcony or the top shelf of the cabinet. In order to get rid of it, it is worth considering several types of offers and choosing the most cost-effective.





Although you can just give such a technique to a low-income family. Thus, you will get rid of technology and do a good deed for those around you.

Little conclusion

An old TV is a thing that, if necessary, can be both profitable for sale and easy to dispose of. After consulting with households, you can choose the appropriate way to get rid of the device as soon as possible. Moreover, it does not matter what condition your equipment is in.

Note that buying up TVs is a fairly popular and sought-after service. There are many such firms in each city. Every year there are more and more of them. Therefore, you can quickly get rid of this technique.
