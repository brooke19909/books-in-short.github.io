Holidays in Crete in September: weather and other features.

Holidays in Crete in September are one of the most unique opportunities to visit this part of the world in the velvet season. During this period, the island's air temperature remains warm in summer, however, the scorching rays of the sun become much more gentle and pleasant. As for the sea, in the first autumn month the water is warm enough and clean, which is favorable for a friendly holiday, and for a tourist trip with children.

Local september weather

Weather conditions that develop immediately after the summer heat on this island are very favorable. Your vacation in Crete in September will be held at 27-30 degrees Celsius, and the sea will be warmed up to about 28. It is believed that such indicators are ideal for a beach holiday, and for constant excursions, travels and trips to local attractions . As for evening time, Crete cools slightly in September. On quiet and calm days, the air temperature here is 18-20 degrees, but if the wind rises (which, by the way, can cause a small storm), then you have to warm yourself with sweaters and sweaters.



Features of autumn Crete

Despite the fact that there are no more than a few rainy days in this region per season, there are often winds on the island. In this regard, holidays in Crete in September (reviews of tourists who have been there, say exactly that) are characterized by frequent changes in temperature and windiness. Short-term cloud cover can gather here, which will alternate with clear sunny and very warm days. It is noteworthy that the feeling of the new autumn has come in this Greek province is not at all the same as in our cold region. Many tourists from Europe believe that vacation in Crete in September is the best way to spend the velvet season and enjoy all the beauties of nature.



What can be seen in Crete

This Greek province is considered unique throughout the world. Natural landmarks are concentrated on it, stunning beauty, which originate from the hills and plains and end far beyond the sea horizon. It is important to note that the island has very lush tropical and green vegetation. In some regions, thickets approach directly to the coastline, which creates some oases. It is also noteworthy that the tours to Crete in September allow you to enjoy the deserted wild beaches located in the bays and bays. If in the summer there is also a small number of tourists, then in the first days of autumn it is almost impossible to meet them here.



Historical attractions of the region

The ideal place and time for exploring the most significant sights of history is Crete in September. The reviews of travelers who have been there say that mild, slightly windy weather allows you to go around all the monuments of art and architecture with the greatest comfort. As a rule, all tours here begin with a visit to the Lassithi Plateau, where many old mills are located. Then each tourist goes to the gorge of Zeus, which is considered one of the oldest on the planet. In the neighborhood are the Kurtaliot Gorge and the Church of St. Nicholas, one of the oldest saints in the world of Christianity. In addition to all this, Crete is fraught with a whole host of monuments of both ancient and medieval culture, but the city of Heraklion deserves the greatest applause. On its expanses the most beautiful Knossos Palace was erected, a significant and very valuable architectural monument.

September Travel Rates

A unique, affectionate and inimitable vacation in Crete in September is also good because it will cost much less than in any month of summer. Compared with August, the budget for the tour is reduced by 10-15%, and compared with July, the price drops by as much as 25 percent. Note that if you have the opportunity to purchase a last-minute tour, then the savings will be much greater. If we compare the cost of the trip in early September and at the end of this month, then the second will cost much cheaper. October tours, however, cost very little, but during this period the sea already becomes very cold, air and sand cools.

Conclusion

Crete is a unique island located in the heart of the Mediterranean Sea, between Europe and Asia. On it you can find a synthesis of various cultures, and the unity of the ancient heritage. And, of course, the warm sea, hot sand and wild beaches that settled in the bays will give you a lot of impressions, good mood and unforgettable moments that can be stored in memory and on photographs.
