Belkon Hotel - comfortable stay

Description

Belkon Hotel, located on the second sea line, was built in the original Nubian style. Opened in the 2005th year, it offers an increased level of service.

The territory on which the hotel buildings are located is more than 12 thousand square meters. meters. Fully landscaped and landscaped, it is convenient for walking and relaxing away from the busy city. The distance to the center of the small resort town of Belek is 30 kilometers. The infrastructure of Belkon Hotel includes: a medical office, a currency exchange office, TV corner, a conference room with 100 seats, a small shopping center, a fruit market, boutiques, a souvenir shop, a specialized photo studio, concierge services, laundry with dry cleaning, and a beauty salon. Vehicle rental is available at the 24-hour reception desk, here you can arrange a transfer and use the safe. The distance to the nearest airport is 27 kilometers.

Rooms

Belkon Hotel 4 Foundation, whose reviews are only positive, is located in one six-story building with three elevators.

In total, the hotel has 186 rooms of standard and family categories. Furniture and plumbing are new. In bathrooms, besides shower and bidet, there is also a hair dryer, slippers and bathrobes. On the floor - carpet. The hotel rooms are air-conditioned. All public areas have free Wi-Fi. Internet connection, like a minibar, is a paid service. If desired, in the room you can get an ironing board, extra bedding. The hotel provides wheelchair access and non-smoking rooms.

Nutrition

Belkon Hotel organizes meals for guests. Those who wish can eat on the outdoor terrace. This four-star hotel also has three bars, including a poolside bar. On the ground floor there is an Internet cafe. The menu has a lot of meat dishes, fruits, salads.



Beach

The distance from the Belkon Hotel to the sea is almost two kilometers. A free shuttle service is available to guests at the beach. Buses depart every forty minutes. The length of its own beach strip is 50 meters. Coverage is sandy, the entrance to the sea is convenient. Umbrellas, sunbeds and trestle beds are issued free of charge only to the hotel guests.

Additional Information

For children, Belkon Hotel offers a small outdoor pool, a playground, a club, special chairs in the restaurant and babysitting services. Adults can play chess, darts and indoor soccer at the Belkon Hotel. The beach offers a wide variety of water sports: surfing, skiing, diving, scooter, parachute. In the evenings, discos are held next to one of the three outdoor pools, and musicians often come. The hotel has a sauna, hammam, massage room, Spa and Wellness centers. There are two adults and one children's water slide in the pools. Bike rental is available.

Guest reviews

Belkon Hotel, reviews of which almost all are positive, is convenient for a respectable relaxing family vacation. The staff is very polite, Russians work in the pool bar. The hotel has a very interesting animation. The situation in the rooms corresponds to the declared hotel category. The disadvantages noted by the residents of the hotel are the monotonous menu and the constantly smelling barbecue throughout.
