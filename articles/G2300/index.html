Dinopark in St. Petersburg - acquaintance with prehistoric animals

In the northern capital there are enough places where you can not only see small and huge dinosaurs, but also have a great time on the rides. Experienced guides will tell fascinating stories that will interest both adults and children.

Museum "Dinosaur Planet"

The trip of the whole family to this amazing museum will not leave indifferent even adults. Here you can get in touch with ancient history and significantly broaden your horizons. The museum does not look like a regular exhibition; the exhibition includes moving models of dinosaurs that completely recreate the real appearance of animals. Also, a living environment is realistically created here, where children can wander around and imagine what the Jurassic period looked like. Here you can always capture yourself or your child against the background of any dinosaur or in the thickets of the oldest green massif. Young children can run around the prehistoric forest - the developers also realistically created a supposed habitat.
