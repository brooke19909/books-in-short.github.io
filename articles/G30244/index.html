Smartphone Tele2 Mini: reviews, specifications

An ultra-budget class smartphone with a modest cost and good technical specifications for this class of devices is the Tele2 Mini. The reviews of most owners indicate that this is an excellent entry-level device. It is about him that we will discuss later.





The reason for the appearance of this device

Most mobile operators do not focus only on the provision of communication and data services and significantly expand the list of services provided, which include the distribution of devices linked to a specific mobile network. The pioneers in this market segment were the big three operators: MTS, Megafon and Beeline. Tele2 came along the same path .

Everyone benefits from this approach. The subscriber receives at a very modest cost a mobile device. And the operator, in turn, binds the client to himself and, due to this, also receives profit in the future. Today, in the official Tele2 store on the Internet, a fairly large number of devices are presented, including a smartphone of the budget level Tele2 Mini. W3bsit3-dns.com - the forum, in turn, indicates that in this case, you can, if necessary, update the software and remove the binding of the gadget to a specific mobile network.

Who is this smartphone aimed at?

Two important points immediately indicate the positioning of the Tele2 Mini. Its price is very low and, as a result, it is a very affordable, budget device. And the second important point is the firmware, which is focused on installing SIM cards of only the same name operator. True, this restriction only applies to installation slot No. 1, but it does not fundamentally change the situation. Therefore, the niche of this device is the existing and potential Tele2 subscribers who need an inexpensive, but at the same time quite functional device. It is for them that this mobile device has been released.

Typical, as for an entry-level smartphone, the equipment of the hero of this review. But there are certain differences in this mobile device against the background of its direct Chinese competitors. First of all, all the documentation that is presented with a warranty card and a brief user manual, in this case in Russian. It should also be noted that finding an official service center for repairing devices of this model is quite simple, and this will greatly simplify the process of solving problems in case of a breakdown of the gadget.

In addition to a cell phone, the list also includes wired headphones with an economy-class microphone, an interface cord, a charger and a 1500 mAh battery. Definitely in the list above, there is definitely not enough protective film for the front panel and case. But if you can do without the first accessory for the first time (use a factory protective film instead), then without a second accessory it will be difficult.

Another important component, without which it will not be possible to fully reveal the potential of this mobile device, is a memory card. But not only in the configuration of entry-level smartphones this accessory is missing, but in the middle and even premium segments it is not so often seen now.

CPU. Its characteristics

The central processor of the MT6572 model of the Taiwanese MediaTech company is the cornerstone of the Tele2 Mini. The reviews indicate that it was released back in the year 2013 and by the standards of the electronic world is outdated both physically and mentally. But nevertheless, the computing capabilities of this chip are quite enough to solve the simplest and least demanding tasks.

It is the launch of such software that Tele2 Mini is aimed at . The characteristics of the CPU indicate that it includes 2 A7 computing units with support for 32-bit computing, the frequency of which can vary in the range from 300 MHz to 1.3 GHz. Moreover, the value of the latter is determined immediately by 2 factors. One of them is the level of complexity of the program code of the problem being solved, and the second is the degree of heating of the silicon crystal. The chip itself is produced using technology with 28 nm tolerances. The computational capabilities of this semiconductor solution are rated by the manufacturer at 4.5 GFlops.

Graphic accelerator

The Tele2 Mini is equipped with an entry-level graphics accelerator. His model is the Mali-400MP1. It includes only one video information processing module, which operates at a fixed frequency of 500 MHz.

Technologically, this semiconductor crystal is manufactured according to the standards of 28 nm. Its computing resources are quite sufficient for displaying images in 800x480 format, and the presence of this component in the smartphone significantly increases its computing capabilities due to the fact that the processing of graphic information falls on his shoulders, and the processor only processes the program code.

RAM The built-in drive. Their characteristics

Only 512MB of DDR3 standard memory in Tele2 Mini. The characteristics of this phone indicate that after the loading of the operating system the user can count on 100-150 MB, and the rest of the RAM will be occupied by system processes. This volume is enough to solve the simplest and most common tasks: social networks, navigation using Google Map, reading books, web surfing and so on.

The Tele2 Mini is equipped with 4GB internal storage. At the same time, about 2.7 GB is initially occupied by preinstalled system software. Well, the remaining 1.3 GB for comfortable work on this gadget certainly will not be enough. As a result, an additional memory card is indispensable. Its maximum size with which this device can work is 32 GB.





Cameras

One of the weaknesses is the cameras in the Tele2 Mini. A review of their technical specifications indicates that one cannot expect even acceptable photo or video quality from them. The main camera has a sensor element of only 2 megapixels, there is a single backlight based on one LED.

About 5 years ago, such a specification was relevant, but now the quality of photographs with such a sensitive element, even in excellent lighting, will be disgusting.

Even worse in this case is the situation with video, which can be recorded in 640x480 format. In the front camera, the situation with video is similar and its format is similar. The sensor has only 0.3 megapixels and this is absolutely not enough for a more or less high-quality photo. The only thing this camera is capable of is making video calls. It is certainly impossible to get more from her.

Battery

The capacity of the complete battery is 1500 mAh in Tele2 Mini. The reviews , in turn, indicate that this device has good autonomy. A small screen and even a modest resolution and a very efficient processor are not all the factors that indicate the phenomenal autonomy of this device. In the maximum load mode, one battery charge is definitely enough for 2 days of battery life. Well, if you minimize its use, then it is quite possible to count on even 5 days of mobile device functioning on one charge .





List of interfaces

Smartphone Tele2 Mini boasts support for such ways of exchanging information with the outside world:

  • Cellular networks in 2 G and 3G format . They allow not only making calls, performing various operations with SMS and MMS, but also transmitting data at speeds up to 500 kbit / s (2 G) and up to 42 Mbit / s (3G).

  • Another important interface for this mobile device is Wi-Fi. With it, you can download data from the global web at speeds up to 150 Mbps.

  • A list of previously mentioned wireless interfaces complements Bluetooth. It allows you to synchronize with similar gadgets or even with a wireless headset.

  • The 3.5 mm audio jack, in turn, allows you to output an audio signal to a wired headset.

  • MicroUSB is excellent for synchronizing with computers and for charging the battery.
