Sochi hotels: prices, reviews. Cheap hotels in Sochi

The Black Sea coast is the dream of many Russians and residents of the near abroad. Everyone is attracted by the opportunity to lie on a golden beach and enjoy the tender sea. The most difficult question usually becomes the choice of hotel. Sochi offers a huge selection of luxury hotels, small hotels and private pensions. All of them are very different in cost and level of service, so you need to choose carefully enough so that the rest meets your expectations.

Most Sochi hotels are economy class hotels. There are luxury options, as well as luxury villas in closed areas, but most often tourists are interested in a decent vacation at reasonable prices, so let's focus on the selection of just such options. Believe me, the choice is truly huge. It is better to start choosing a hotel, boarding house or hotel long before the holidays, then the chance to book a room exactly where you want will be much higher.

Pride of Sochi: Krasnaya Polyana

This hotel is located in the center of the most modern ski resort. Krasnaya Polyana (Sochi) is a 428-room hotel where guests will find home comfort and excellent service. The hotel infrastructure includes 2 restaurants, a spa and a night club. The windows offer beautiful views of the Caucasus Mountains, snow-white ridges and passes. All rooms, both standard and deluxe, have an elegant interior and modern equipment. You will not have to deny yourself a beach holiday if you decide to visit Krasnaya Polyana in spring or autumn.
