Victory Park in Yekaterinburg: rest with benefit

Each city has a quiet and green oasis in which people can relax and enjoy the fresh air. So, Victory Park in Yekaterinburg is very popular with both citizens and tourists. After all, it is here that you can spend your free time perfectly for the benefit of the body and soul. Many people just come here for a walk, while others are actively involved in various sports.

general information

Victory Park in Yekaterinburg will always be reminiscent of the events of World War II. It can be considered a real monument, which perpetuated the feat of millions of people. The park has its own story, which even appears on the tank road. Guests can see the date plates, thanks to which you can learn more about the events that took place in the city back in the last century. Nowadays, visitors are increasingly coming to see a real tank, which is not only a symbol of victory, but also the pride of the park.

Already in 1975, its territory began to be landscaped for a comfortable stay for guests. The first pedestrian paths appeared in Victory Park in Yekaterinburg, along which residents and visitors of the city still walk. The place of rest is also valuable in that, in addition to monuments, it has Lake Shuvakish. In this green park you can see pine and birch trees, giving fresh and clean air. Visitors often observe squirrels, who sometimes run out to get to know the vacationers better.

How to get to the park

Getting to the destination is possible in several ways, for this it is enough to know the address of Victory Park in Yekaterinburg. It is located at the end of Kirovograd street (number 101).

The park can be reached at:

  • Tram numbers 8, 22, 24 and 17, get off at the Kirovogradskaya stop.
  • Minibus at number 33 (final stop).
  • Take the metro to the Uralmash station, and you can also get off at the Cosmonauts Avenue stop.
  • By rail to the Komsomolskaya station.

Visitors reviews

Victory Park in Yekaterinburg is becoming more and more visited and popular. Many guests celebrate their birthdays here and write in reviews that the place is ideal for this. Visitors also do not forget to praise the beautiful and memorable nature, which allows you to forget about the bustle of the city. Some of the minus guests say that something could still be improved. So, they write that parking has become paid, and now it will be more difficult to leave the car. Most of the guests like the popular arbors, although some people have not the most positive impressions of them. However, beautiful views and a large lake still can not leave visitors indifferent.

Fun in the park

The place of rest guarantees visitors a lot of interesting options. Moms with children like to come here, as they have great attractions and a playground. In addition, the park enjoys great popularity among those guests who love an active lifestyle. Here they can jog, ride bicycles, and engage in other sports. The volleyball court is rarely empty. Companies often play here or hold competitions. Among other attractions, you can rent a jet ski or electric car to explore the park as best as possible.

Pavilions in Victory Park (Yekaterinburg) are very popular for picnics and various events. Many of them are even suitable for winter, as they have a stove and everything necessary for a good rest. There are excellent offers for recreation at a price of 2000-3000 rubles, in which everything is already included. The park has become a favorite place not only for couples in love, but also for newlyweds. It is in it that often conduct exit registration of marriage and beautiful photo shoots against the backdrop of nature. For guests at the park there is a cafe called "Fairy Tale", where you can always relax and eat.
