Where to go for a weekend by car from Moscow?

Moscow is a bustling metropolis. There are, of course, many places where you can go and have a great rest. Only sometimes you want to get out of the capital and visit other interesting places and cities in the suburbs. And when, finally, the working week ends, the question arises of where you can go on a weekend by car from Moscow. This is what our article will tell.

Husky Land

Where can I go on a weekend trip by car in search of new experiences? In "Husky Land" it will be interesting to learn about the traditions and life of the peoples of the Far North. The keeper will tell about legends, shamanistic rites, crafts and folklore. Then everyone can ride a dog sled. There is also a beaver farm, which will be interesting to see not only for adults, but also for children. For those who are not afraid of heights, balloon flights are organized. There is also an alternative to hotel accommodation - rent a yaranga or a plague. Husky Land is located in the village of Sosny, Moscow Region. When visiting on weekdays, an appointment is required.

Retro style

Where to go for a weekend on a car from Moscow to lovers of retro technology? Such people can be advised to visit the museum of Vadim Zadorozhny. It presents one of the largest collections (over a thousand exhibits) of weapons, air transport, motorcycles, automobiles and military equipment. A museum is located in the Moscow region, in the village of Arkhangelsk.



Famine Pyramid

The Hunger Pyramid is 44 meters high and is made of fiberglass structures with night illumination. It is believed that this place has healing properties. A pyramid is located next to the Novorizhskoe highway.

Sparrow Bird Park

In the unusual bird park "Sparrows", located near Moscow, collected more than 2000 species of birds from around the world. In addition to them, you can see rare species of reptiles, mammals and animals. Despite the name of the park, the basis of the exposition is not sparrows, but parrots and exotic species of birds collected from all over the planet. Park "Sparrows" is located in the Kaluga region, in the Zhukovsky district.

Ball near Dubna

Finally, workdays end, and so I want to relax. So where to go for the weekend by car from Moscow with children? Shar near Dubna is an art object the size of a five-story building. It has a diameter of 18 meters and is located in the Tver region near the village of Ignatovo, near Dubna. He is famous for his acoustics. It is a stable large fiberglass sphere. Any sound made inside the ball is reflected from the walls, causing multiple echoes. There is a hypothesis that the sphere has mystical and magical properties. This ball is very popular among tourists.



Farm "Russian Ostrich"

At the Russian Ostrich Farm you can see these huge birds. They not only enjoy contact with visitors, but also love to pose for photographs. There are special excursions in which the guide talks about these birds and their care. Near the farm there is a cafe where you can enjoy barbecue and ostrich meat cradles, scrambled eggs and exotic dishes. "Russian Ostrich" is located in the Serpukhov district, in the village of Stary Kuzmenki.

Cave temple

If you have your own transport, then the question of where to go on a weekend by car, you can answer that there are a lot of such places. For example, in the Belgorod region there are many caves. Partially restored, but there are also abandoned ones.

Where to go on a weekend by car, if interested monasteries? There is a working monastery. It is located 15 kilometers from Novy Oskol, near the village of Holki. For a long time, the caves in which it is located were closed to the public. They were discovered only in 1990. Most of the monastery is occupied by numerous corridors and cells, in which there are only beds, carved from the walls of the caves. Inside there is a small church around which a large tunnel has been dug for the procession. The monastery is located just 620 kilometers from Moscow.

Veliky Ustyug - on a visit to Santa Claus

Many people wonder where to go for a weekend in a car from Moscow in winter, especially with children. Of course, to visit Santa Claus, the Great Ustyug. In winter, it is most interesting there. In local workshops, guests are introduced to the weaving of the famous Vologda lace, to carvings on birch bark and to other northern highlights. The shop sells fabulous souvenirs. Those who wish can visit the fabulous hall of Santa Claus and sit on his throne.

And in a pine forest 15 km from the city is its magical residence, which is considered the main attraction of Veliky Ustyug. What you can visit there, what kind of entertainment:

There is even a mini zoo in which there are many northern animals and birds. You can stay at the hotel for the weekend.
