Corfu, Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 * - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Holidays on the Greek island of Corfu are a beautiful journey to the blue lagoons and green hills, to the past of Greece and a real European civilization. Vacation is approaching, and it's time to choose a travel route.



Take a closer look at Benitses - an ancient city left in the inheritance of Greece from Roman rule. It is located twelve kilometers from the city of Corfu.

In the past it is a small fishing village, and in the present it is a rather lively tourist center of Corfu. There is something to do here. The location in the center of the island provides a good opportunity to deeply explore this historical place, but this will require more than one visit, so great is its history and so rich are opportunities for relaxation and learning the pages of Greek culture and history ...

Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 *

In the center of the city of Benetsis there is a cozy hotel, which has gained popularity with good service and reasonable prices for the pleasure of spending a vacation here. This is all the more important since most of the hotels on the island are deluxe. And this means that relaxation becomes quite an expensive pleasure.

The four-story building of Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 (Corfu) is located seventy meters from the sea, in a beautiful and well-kept garden.

A quiet and comfortable place, a large number of taverns, shops and shops located near the hotel, give a special charm and comfort to rest.

Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 * exceeds the declared star rating. This is evidenced by reviews of grateful tourists. Hotel rating is 4.7 on a five-point rating system. And this means that, by purchasing a ticket to a three-star hotel, you will provide yourself with the level of relaxation typical of a five-star! And you can buy it at a very affordable price - in the range of three thousand rubles per room per day (price for two people).

Hotel facilities

The Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 * has its own disco club, games room, leisure clubs. Facilities for disabled guests are provided.

Every Friday, a Greek evening is organized for hotel guests, concert and entertainment programs are offered.

Round-the-clock service for vacationers, umbrellas and sun loungers by the pool, morning waking up, daily room cleaning and linen change, use of a children's and adult pool with sunbeds and umbrellas - all these services at Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 * offered for free. Extra charge You can use the following services:

  • Parking for a personal car.
  • Car rental.
  • Excursion service.
  • Call a doctor.

Nutrition

The hotel offers continental breakfasts, the cost of which is included in the price. The restaurant has a wide range of Greek and international dishes. The outdoor terrace will enhance the effect of lunch and dinner.

Two bars - at the pool and in the lobby of the Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 * - offer guests a wide selection of drinks, snacks, juices and cocktails.

Greek cuisine is a separate topic of conversation. For the chef, the praise of the guests is the highest recognition, so you should thank the author for the dish you marked. They willingly share the secrets of its preparation and unique recipes that cannot be found in any directory of the world.

You should not be limited to meals on site. Nearby is a large number of cafes and eateries. You can diversify your menu without much damage to your wallet, tasting Greek cuisine and wine. Believe that after completing the journey, the taste of these dishes will not give you peace for a long time.

Accommodation

In accordance with European standards, settlement takes place at 14 hours. Subject to availability and early check-in, the room is provided before the deadline.

In hotel Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 * one hundred thirty numbers, in single, double and triple accommodation options. Each of them is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of tourists.

The rooms have balconies with picturesque views of the sea and the hotel grounds, telephone, air conditioning, refrigerator, satellite TV.

The bathroom comes with a set of toiletries.

The concept of the hotel allows you to relax with the content of four-legged pets. When booking a room, it is only necessary to coordinate accommodation with animals in advance.

Entertainment

Accommodation in 3 * Potamaki Beach Hotel (Benitses) is a great bonus for vacationers: the beach is seventy meters from the hotel, sand and gravel, comfortable for entering the sea.

Once every three days, entertainment events are organized on site, with live music and concert programs.

The tour desk provides a great choice for exploring the historic sites of the island. Excursion programs:

  • Sightseeing tours of the island of Corfu and the city of Benitses.
  • Sea cruise to the islands of Paxis and Antipaxis.
  • Excursion to Corfu, with a visit to the church of St. Spyridon, the most beautiful Greek square Spianad.
  • Visiting Vlacherna Monastery in Kanoni.

Fun and relaxation at Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 *:

  • Drive to Water Village.
  • Training at the Dive Center.
  • Horseback riding.

Reviews

A stay on the island of Corfu is different from a vacation on other Greek islands such as Rhodes, Kos, Santorini. There is no unbearable heat, and vacationers do not always use air conditioning.

But precisely because the greenery is not scorched by the sun, he retained his amazing beauty.

Therefore, you can capture the scenes of Greek life, your relaxation and picturesque lagoons, bays and embankments of this island - this is one of the attributes of a trip to the shores of Greek culture.

Holidays with a good level of service and impeccable behavior of the staff, which with sincere participation and care relate to all the needs of vacationers, are characteristic of the Potamaki Beach Hotel 3 *. Reviews visitors resting here are confirmed by the high rating of the hotel.

Guests celebrate:

  • Excellent service (80%).
  • Comfortable accommodation conditions (75%).
  • The friendliness of the staff (85%).

Eight out of ten vacationers plan to visit this hotel again. This is not surprising. For a short period of rest, it is almost impossible to see all the beauty and sightseeing wealth of the island, with about eight hundred historical sites. And given that the hotel provides an opportunity to save money on visiting excursions without losing quality of service, it is an excellent option for planning your next meeting with Greek culture.
