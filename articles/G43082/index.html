Hotel "Volga Riviera" (Uglich): photos and reviews of tourists

Both Russians and foreign tourists travel the Golden Ring every winter and summer. The ancient, unusually beautiful, although small, town of Uglich also belongs to it. Here are ancient monasteries, exclusive museums, wonderful parks. The new chic four-star Volzhskaya Riviera hotel harmoniously blended into the unique look of the city. Uglich can rightfully be proud of this object of the tourism business. The hotel building seems to admire itself, reflected in the mirror of the calm waters of the great Volga. The exterior of the Volga Riviera and the interior decoration can delight and satisfy the most demanding taste. Let's take a short walk around this wonderful hotel and see what conditions of rest are here.

Location

The Volzhskaya Riviera hotel was built in a very convenient location. Uglich is a nice, cozy town, built on the banks of the Volga, 220 km from Moscow and just over 100 km from Yaroslavl. There is a city from the V century and for a long time was the center of trade, spiritual and political life. To this day, interesting historical monuments for tourists have been preserved here. The Volga Riviera was built ten meters from the great Volga River and about one kilometer from the center of Uglich, where many museums, parks, monuments and other interesting objects are concentrated. It is noteworthy that not on the road itself, but somewhat on the side is the beauty of the Volga Riviera (Uglich). The hotel address is as follows: Uspenskaya street, building number 8. Nearby is the Uglich Legends Museum and the Church of the Icon of the Mother of God.

Well, you can comfortably get to the hotel on a transfer booked in advance. He can meet his guests at the airport, at the bus station and in the river port.

Description

The building of the Volzhskaya Riviera Hotel (Uglich) looks like a real palace of ancient times. The photo captures the majestic tall columns, a marble staircase leading to the front door. Here, like formidable guards, the figures of lions froze around which all tourists like to arrange photo shoots. The building is surrounded by a wonderful park with neatly trimmed shrubs and trees. Free parking is available on site. The hotel stands almost on the bank of the river. A beautiful staircase also leads to the water, a playground equipped with white railings is equipped. On it newlyweds like to be photographed. The interior of the hotel impresses with its luxury and pomp. The hall is decorated with columns and candelabra, furnished with soft sofas decorated with gilded brocade fabric. A spiral marble staircase leads to the upper floors , and an elevator is located nearby. At the reception, which is open around the clock, friendly staff will quickly resolve any issue. Here you can hand over valuables to a safe, order a taxi, use the services of mail, hand over personal items for ironing or washing.



Infrastructure

In the wonderful Volzhskaya Riviera hotel (Uglich), everything was done so that the guests feel the maximum comfort. In the lobby there is a hairdresser and two exclusive shops. In one you can buy jewelry with precious stones, and in the other - gift options for knives, various souvenirs and some household items. Near the reception there is a lobby bar, the interior of which is fully consistent with the surroundings. In the Volga Riviera you can not only relax, but also work fruitfully. For business people, two conference rooms are equipped here, Catherine II and Elizabeth, each 47 square meters in area. Both rooms are decorated in the same style as the entire hotel. In addition, the business center has 2 meeting rooms for 8 people each. They are perfect for indoor events. Lunches and receptions are available at the hotel's restaurant for business events.

Volzhskaya Riviera Hotel (Uglich): reviews on leisure activities

The hotel has a modern spa equipped with the latest technology. Here, for extra money, each guest can visit the sauna, bathhouse, pool, jacuzzi. Sports enthusiasts staying at the Volga Riviera can have a great time fishing summer and winter, or engage in shooting at moving targets. During the period when navigation is open, each guest can take a memorable tour of the Volga on the ship. In the evening, guests of the hotel will enjoy live music in the restaurant, and movie buffs can visit the Rossiya cinema every day, located just 300 meters from the hotel. But the main thing that awaits almost everyone staying at the Volga Riviera is fascinating walks along the streets of Uglich, admiring the Kremlin, beautiful monasteries, visiting museums, among which are such exclusive as the Museum of Prison Art and Russian Vodka, the Museum of the Worker and the Rooster.



Services for children

For young guests there are not many amenities that the Volzhskaya Riviera hotel can offer. Uglich is an old city, but there are many modern entertainment centers. So, in several shopping centers there are playrooms for babies, where all kinds of swings are installed, competitions and interesting classes are held. In addition, the city has puppet and musical theaters, a planetarium and a zoo. The Volga Riviera Hotel itself has cots for children, and payment is not taken until the child reaches the age of 2 years. Also, free of charge, but without a seat, children aged 5 years or less are accepted. In the restaurant, parents have the opportunity to order children's dishes. Among the activities at the hotel for children, walks on a children's train are arranged.
