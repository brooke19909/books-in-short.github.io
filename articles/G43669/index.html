Reasons for the loss of sound on iPhone 4S





It should be clearly understood that two logical ladders can lead to this kind of problem. Your device may not sound, either due to physical damage or due to the presence of a software malfunction. Accordingly, before you ask yourself why sound is lost on the iPhone 4S, you need to clearly determine what exactly happened in your case. Try to remember if you could have physically damaged the device during use. Maybe somewhere you dropped your phone? Although this was an accident, but still such situations often cause breakdowns. If the fact of causing mechanical injuries has occurred, then most likely the user will need to seek qualified help from a qualified service center. If the problem lies directly in a software failure, then you can deal with it in proud solitude, without resorting to the help of strangers.

IPhone 4S Setup: Volume Return





The easiest step towards solving the problem is to simply check the volume level. Try using the keys to increase it. It is possible that you just accidentally turned off the sound on your device. To return to normal level, the volume slider will help. For speakers and headphones, it is configured separately, this should be noted. If a wired stereo headset is connected to the device, you will see the corresponding indicator. If it does not appear, then you might think that not everything is okay with the headphone jack.

Second step
