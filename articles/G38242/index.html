Hotel Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa 5 * (Greece / Crete): photo and reviews of tourists

Are you planning to spend a full vivid experience vacation in hospitable Greece? Are you considering Crete as a vacation spot? Looking for a comfortable beachfront hotel? In this case, be sure to pay attention to the five-star Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa. Perhaps this hotel is exactly what you need.



Location

Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa is located on the Greek island of Crete in the village of Stalida. The resort towns of Malia and Hersonissos are nearby. So there will be no shortage of objects of tourist infrastructure and entertainment here. Heraklion Airport is 30 kilometers away. The hotel is located on the seafront, just 200 meters from its own sandy beach.



Description and photo of the hotel

Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa 5 * (Greece, Crete) is a modern hotel offering guests a relaxed and relaxing holiday, combined with comfort and excellent service. The housing stock of the hotel consists of 220 stylishly decorated and equipped with all necessary rooms. The apartments here are represented by the categories "standard" and "family". Half of the rooms have amazing sea views.

All apartments have modern appliances and comfortable quality furniture. So, in the apartments you will find air conditioning, LCD TV (satellite TV and radio), telephone, safe (a laptop can easily fit in it), minibar, hairdryer, bathroom, tea set. Some rooms also have a jacuzzi. Guests have free access to a safe and wireless internet. Also upon arrival in your room you will find a bathrobe, slippers, bath and beach towels, hygiene products. Apartments are cleaned and towels are changed every day. For an additional fee, you can order room service.

With regard to infrastructure, the hotel has several pools, including a large outdoor, equipped with a jacuzzi. Guests also have access to a spa, gym, beauty salon, internet cafe. The hotel offers daytime and evening animation. In terms of catering, Aktia Lounge offers two systems - half board and ol inclusive. The hotel has a restaurant, several bars and a snack bar.

The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Therefore, you can always contact the duty administrator with a request or question. The hotel staff mainly speaks English only. However, even having basic language skills from the school curriculum, you can communicate without problems. Business people can, if necessary, rent one of the three state-of-the-art conference rooms.

The hotel also offers laundry and dry cleaning services, car rental, transfer, concierge.

Rating

It should be noted right away that the overwhelming majority of Aktia Lounge guests were completely satisfied with their choice. This is indicated by a hotel rating based on guest ratings. He is 4.3 points out of the maximum possible five. Thus, the highest ratings were awarded to the location of the hotel and the equipment of the rooms. Some of the complaints of the guests concerned the infrastructure for children. But we suggest you learn more about everything.

Location

According to tourists, despite the fact that Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa 5 * (Stalida) is located in a small village, there are no problems with infrastructure. So, in the vicinity of the hotel you will find several good cafes and restaurants, a hookah bar, bars, tour agencies, car rental offices and much more. As for the distance from the airport, it is also not far. So, our compatriots, who got to the hotel on a bus provided by a travel agency, spent about 50 minutes on the road (including arrivals to other hotels). If you order a personal transfer or use the services of a taxi, then you can reach the hotel in 20-30 minutes. Tourists also note as a plus the fact that the hotel is located next to the highway. Thus, if you rent a car, you will not have to stray along the narrow city streets.



Hotel itself

Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa 5 * (Greece, Stalis), as noted by travelers, cannot boast of having a large territory. Moreover, she is practically not here. Since almost the entire territory of the hotel is occupied by a swimming pool and a sun terrace. However, the pool here is very large, so you can have a great time here. In addition, stunning views of the sea. Many vacationers preferred to sit on sun loungers near the pool. They went swimming in the sea, which can be reached in just a minute.

A big plus of Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa 5 * (Crete, Greece), tourists also consider the availability of wireless Internet. Staff

Travelers also praised the work of the hotel staff. So, according to them, everything here, from maids to administrators to the reception, perfectly copes with the duties assigned to them. In addition, they are always friendly, friendly and happy to help in resolving any issue. As for, for example, housekeeping, it was always carried out at a time when guests were not in the apartment, so as not to disturb them. Moreover, tourists spoke very well about its quality.

In addition, according to our compatriots, at Aktiya Lounge they did not notice any bias on the part of the staff, as is the case in some other hotels.

Population

In accordance with the internal rules of the Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel (Greece), the photo of which will surely force you to include it in the list of places to visit, check-in of new guests is carried out after two in the afternoon. However, in reality, in most cases, everything happens differently. As our compatriots note, even if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning, you will most likely be given the room keys almost immediately. And immediately upon arrival you will be offered a cup of coffee or a refreshing glass of freshly squeezed juice. After registration, the porter will deliver your things and show you your number, as well as tell you about the internal rules and how to use certain items. Such attention to the guests, undoubtedly, pleased the guests.



Nutrition

When booking accommodation in Aktia Lounge, you can choose one of the catering options - all inclusive or half board. The first option is suitable for those tourists who plan to spend most of the time on site. Half board also makes sense to choose those travelers who prefer an active vacation - trips on excursions, long walks, etc. In any case, according to our compatriots who have been here, you will be satisfied. So, in the hotel restaurant there are excellent cooks who will always delight you with delicious dishes made from the freshest products. However, do not forget that Aktia Lounge is a budget five-star hotel, so you should not expect delicacies here. In general, most of the guests appreciated the work of the restaurant and the quality of the dishes offered. By the way, in addition to the three main meals, vacationers also have the opportunity to eat refreshments in the bar during the day.

If you want to change the atmosphere, you can always go for lunch or dinner in one of the restaurants in the vicinity of the hotel. They cook here, according to tourists, everywhere is very tasty. So you are unlikely to be disappointed.

Sea

Of course, most tourists who choose Greece for a summer vacation, and in particular the island of Crete, count on a great beach holiday. In this regard, according to our compatriots, the Aktia Lounge Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel will not disappoint you. So, it is 200 meters from the sea. In addition, Aktia Lounge boasts a private beach. This feature, admittedly, is rare for hotels in Greece. Moreover, the beach here, according to former guests, is simply excellent! So, he is freed from large boulders that lie on the sidelines. The beach itself is sandy, and the entry into the water is gentle, without stones. A lifeguard is always on duty at the shore. The beach is well equipped. So, there are a sufficient number of comfortable sun loungers and parasols, and there is also a bar where you can enjoy a drink or a snack.

Entertainment

As our compatriots note, in Greece you will almost never encounter animations like the one offered in Turkish hotels. As for the Aktia Lounge, there is still an entertainment program. So, guests will be offered to shoot from a rifle, do Pilates, water aerobics, play bocca or darts.

As for other entertainments, it offers vacationers a large pool with clean water and a jacuzzi, spa, gym. You can play volleyball at the beach.

Experienced tourists also advise all vacationers in Crete to go for at least a couple of excursions. Moreover, this can be done both independently, by renting a car, or as part of an organized group by contacting a travel agency. Many travelers advise the first option, as in this case you will get complete freedom of movement and will not be limited in time.
