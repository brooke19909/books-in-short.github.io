Complex "Golden City", Tula region: photo, address, reviews

The old manor, from the visit of which there is a subtle, subtle sensation of the transience of all that is happening, is the Golden City complex in the Tula Region. Wonderful antiquity, which takes visitors and vacationers to the past with only one view of its architectural ensemble. A rare case when in one place all the signs of a traditional Russian estate are combined and there are elements of East Chinese buildings. In the very heart of Russia you seem to find yourself in a mysterious Middle Kingdom with all its lush exotic.





Complex "Golden City" (Tula region)

There is something unusual here that is very attractive. Those who discovered this place must return here, and more than once. Moreover, not only Tulchans come, there are many vacationers from Moscow and from other cities. Fortunately, get close. How to get to the "Golden City" of the Tula region?

From Moscow you just need to get to Tula, and there every taxi driver knows the way from the Tula region to the "Golden City". If you want to save money, then a regular shuttle service is at your service. There is a bus from Tula that takes passengers to Lenin Square near the Tula Kremlin. In Novomoskovsk, you can catch a bus to the complex at the bus station - where minibuses leave for Moscow. Settlement with the driver on the spot, with children under 6 years of age no fee.

If you drive your own car, it is better to use the tips of the navigator. He will tell you how to get to the "Golden City" in the Tula region, if you correctly set the coordinates of the place or make the request. GPS coordinates: 54.122753, 38.023943. From Moscow you need to go along the M-4 Don highway up to 199 km, where to look for the exit to the right. Further the road leads directly to the place.

Oriental Tea Ceremony - Meditation to Soul

Today, many are keen on the east - did not think why? Why, for example, do Japanese and Chinese attach so much importance to following certain rituals? Eastern wisdom teaches that this brings some order to our life, adds meaning to it and eliminates unnecessary throwing and doubt. The ritual itself is not important, its strict observance is important, methodical and calm. This is a peculiar desire to achieve perfection in every action, no matter what it leads to.

Orientalists call Chinese tea drinking one of such simple and very important rituals. It gives inner harmony and allows you to leave the hustle and bustle beyond. This is a very special atmosphere that reigns between well-knowing each other or unfamiliar people. In the Middle Kingdom they believe that during tea drinking time changes its course, clinging to space, state and leaving only fire, water and a tea leaf in the spotlight. Anyone who has never attended a Chinese tea ceremony might think that everything said is too exaggerated. But people who have already participated in it do not need to say anything - they already felt the full value of this slow and enchanting action on themselves.
