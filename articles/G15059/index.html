Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * (UAE, Sharjah): photos and reviews of tourists

Experienced tourists know that a well-spent vacation is a charge of vivacity and positive for the whole year. That is why they prefer the hotel Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 *, located in the Persian Gulf. Everything contributes to a wonderful pastime: a magnificent beach, warm water, wonderful climate and excellent living conditions.

Hotel location

Many tourists have already managed to visit the very popular Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * hotel, located on the Persian Gulf coast. This 4-star de luxe is 25 km from Dubai International Airport. Sharjah city center is 18 km away.

The hotel is in a very quiet area, which allows tourists to spend their vacations here calmly. It is located directly on the coastline, and the distance to the sea is only 50 m.

Hotel Description

The six-story building combines urban modern and Asian styles that harmoniously complement each other. A beautifully decorated entrance with monumental columns looks great against the background of strict geometric lines of the structure.



The interior of the Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * hotel impresses with its beauty and luxury. The hall of this institution looks like a palace chamber. For the convenience of arriving guests in the lobby there are many upholstered furniture and tables, near which you can pass the waiting time for the settlement. Since the last reconstruction was carried out in 2014, everything here looks fresh and beautiful.

Description of the Territory

Not all UAE hotels can boast of luxurious territory. Hotel Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * is not among them. Many tourists consider magnificent gardens the main attraction of this place. The climatic conditions of this region do not contribute to the growth of rampant greens, but thanks to the efforts of the hotel staff, the territory can be called a real paradise.

The guests love cages with a variety of parrots. In addition, there are tables on the paths, near which it is very nice to sit in the shade of huge palm trees. Everywhere there are a lot of flower beds with a variety of colors that you can endlessly admire.



Near the hotel there are two swimming pools: for adults and children. The children's pool has a shaded area, which allows kids to frolic for a long time in the water without burning under the rays of the scorching sun. Near the pools you can always find free sun loungers shaded by umbrellas. According to tourists, there are always a lot of guests at the pools, despite the fact that the beach is located just a few minutes walk from the hotel.

Infrastructure

According to tourists, the hotel Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * offers excellent conditions for a vacation. Everything is provided for each guest to feel comfortable and spend their vacations without unnecessary worries. You can contact the reception at any time of the day, because it works 24 hours. Here you will always find Russian-speaking employees who are ready to listen to any request and solve all arising issues.



In the hotel Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * you can use the following services:

  • currency exchange office;
  • a camera or a safe for storing valuables or bulky luggage;
  • concierge services;
  • tour desk;
  • free parking;
  • Internet connection
  • transfer to Dubai.

At the hotel you can also use the services for which you have to pay:

  • car rental;
  • rent a banquet hall;
  • a business center or conference room, including all the necessary equipment for business meetings;
  • laundry or dry cleaning services;
  • medical services;
  • Internet cafe.

The hotel has a trading floor and shops where you can buy various souvenirs or favorite things.

Rooms

Most of the tourists who rested at the Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * hotel (Sharjah) liked the rooms offered here. They are very comfortable and cozy. The hotel has 156 rooms. All of them are in the main building. The only thing that guests do not really like is the lack of balconies or terraces. You can contemplate the opening view only from the window. In addition, it opens only upon request. It is not possible to ventilate the room yourself, although staff does this during the cleaning.



The rooms have everything that is provided for a comfortable stay. There is satellite TV, but you can only watch one Russian channel. Air conditioning in the rooms works well, but the system itself is quite noisy, as vacationers have repeatedly mentioned in their reviews.

Each room has a safe, coffee maker, fridge and minibar. In the bathroom there are always bath accessories, you can use a shower or bath. There is also a hairdryer. Guests do not complain about the operation of electrical appliances, since everything is in good condition. The inconvenience is the lack of space for drying clothes. In this case, the hotel offers to use a specially equipped dryer located on the third floor.

The quality of cleaning the rooms is good. Cleaners try to cope with their duties when guests are absent. Linen and towels are changed daily.

Power Features

Tourists who are going to visit the Coral Beach Resort Hotel (Sharjah) can be offered the following food options:

  • breakfast only;
  • half board;
  • "all inclusive".

The menu in the main restaurant abounds with variety. The quality of the dishes is also very popular with the guests. The only thing that disappoints vacationers is the monotony of food. A la carte restaurants , where you can enjoy Italian or fish dishes, save the situation. In addition, you can always choose snacks, desserts or drinks in the hotel bars, which are located near the pool and in the hotel lobby.

Beach vacation

The Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * hotel has a very beautiful beach, the photo of which allows you to see the charm of this amazing place. Many guests are delighted with walks on the soft white sand. The beach is kept clean, although at times this is not easy. As the hotel guests note, in windy weather, various debris is taken ashore, which is immediately removed by the workers.

Tourists do not really like the quality of water. As many note, it is quite muddy, has a not very attractive color due to plankton. The smell near the sea is also not very popular with guests, because it resembles a swamp. But hotel guests say that you can get used to all these moments and enjoy bathing and relaxing on the beach.



Towels, mattresses, sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach are always enough. Vacationers can at any time find a suitable place and sit comfortably on it.

Entertainment at the hotel

You will not have to be bored at the hotel, as there are various options for an exciting pastime. Sports fans can have fun with all kinds of games:

  • volleyball;
  • futsal;
  • aerobics;
  • table tennis.

You can play real tennis or book lessons with an instructor at an additional cost. Also, a game of billiards will cost guests a certain amount. Exercising in the fitness center is free. From the windows of the hall offers a beautiful view of the sea.

Water sports are available at the hotel only for a fee. In this case, surfing or water skiing will be possible only with a certificate. The scuba diving school invites guests to take lessons and learn the subtleties of diving.



For people who monitor appearance, a beauty salon offers its services. Here you can take a massage course, order a variety of body treatments.

Conditions for young hotel guests

Going to the Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * hotel (Sharjah), parents can be sure that their children will have a great time. On arrival, a baby cot can be ordered free of charge. Parents will also not have to worry about the nutrition of their babies, as there is a children's menu.

Kids can have fun during their stay at the hotel. There are many different devices, ladders, houses, which allows the guys to come up with all kinds of entertainment. Animators also spend time with the kids, entertaining them with fun shows. An extensive program is offered to children by a mini-club, but you need to pay extra for its visit.

Entertainment outside the hotel

Each guest of the Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 * hotel will not be able to resist the opportunity to admire Dubai. The tour can be booked at the bureau located in the hotel lobby. Experienced guides will indicate all places worthy of attention. Dubai and the city of Sharjah have very beautiful aquariums with many marine inhabitants. Many tourists mark the Ferrari World Park as the most interesting place. It will take more than one day to visit all the attractions available here.

Hotel Coral Beach Resort Sharjah 4 *: reviews

According to tourists who visited the hotel, excellent living conditions are created here. According to vacationers, the 5-star Coral Beach Resort Sharjah hotel deserves for the quality of service, attentiveness and courtesy of the staff, well-groomed and cleanliness in the rooms and on the territory. The high price of accommodation (71 573 rubles per week) is a little frustrating, but this is fully compensated by the magnificent and comfortable conditions.

Tourists are advised to consider when choosing a vacation time that the month of ramadan in the hotel can be boring, as in nearby cities. At this time, many entertainment venues almost do not work and music is almost inaudible anywhere. But if there is a desire to spend the holidays calmly and just admire the local attractions, then this will not become an obstacle.
