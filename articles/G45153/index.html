Scarlet Sails in St. Petersburg - the attraction of the Northern capital

In youth, many live in the expectation that the world will be bright and welcoming. That dreams come true. Let not immediately, but the obstacles are easily overcome. The Scarlet Sails holiday in St. Petersburg is dedicated to this bright, pure and unique - one of a kind, the only such celebration of graduates in the whole world.

history of the holiday

The first colorful celebration of the end of the school year was proposed in the Palace of Pioneers and Schoolchildren of Leningrad in 1968. Own forces for a couple of months, enthusiasts built a stage, designed. The idea was the fact that all graduates of schools on the day of the last call, which is celebrated throughout the country, walk along the beautiful embankments of the city on the Neva, admire beautiful white nights. Just a little bit added color and light to dreams. They helped at least for a few hours to believe that miracles happen, as it was in the book "Scarlet Sails" by Alexander Green.





But it was in Leningrad in 1923 that the author created the images of this most beautiful story of love and faith in a beautiful dream.

Unfortunately, in 1979, fearing the consequences of a large gathering of young people during the celebration of "Scarlet Sails", the head of the CPSU Regional Committee of Leningrad Grigory Romanov ordered to stop the celebration. But when did the bans stop? It's just that the celebration has become unofficial.
