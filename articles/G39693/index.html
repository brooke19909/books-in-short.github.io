Side Iona Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea is one of the most popular tourist destinations for Russians. Every year, tens or even hundreds of thousands of tourists from different cities of our country buy tours to the hotels of the legendary Antalya or other equally beautiful resorts of the Turkish Riviera, for example, Alanya, Kemer or Side. Hotels of various categories are scattered across the coastline - from 2 to 5 stars. By the way, for those tourists who want to relax normally and at the same time save money, it is recommended to choose three-star options. For example, Side Iona Hotel 3 * and other hotels of the same category. The service is quite acceptable in them, and the prices are quite low. For example, a weekly tour at Side Iona Hotel for two will cost 40,000 rubles.



Side - one of the best resorts in Turkey

From Antalya, where the international airport is located, to Side is only 75 kilometers away. This means that there, after arriving in the country by air, you can get there by car or dolmushe (minibus) in 40-60 minutes. Unlike other areas of the Turkish Riviera, the relief of these territories is more flat. There is no lush vegetation. However, these places can hardly be called deserted. And still, the owners of hotel complexes are doing everything possible to green their territories. So, the hotel Side Iona Hotel today literally immersed in greenery. Here, with the light hand of gardeners serving at the hotel, many magnificent shrubs, trees, vines and flowers bloom magnificently. But the biggest pride of this resort is the beach. Thanks to its parameters, it has repeatedly awarded the EU blue flag.





Features of rest in Side

There are more than enough cultural attractions here, so it is perfect for those tourists who prefer not only to relax on the beach, but also to enrich themselves spiritually and see a lot of new and unknown. The ruins of the ancient city, the colonnade in the Hellenistic style, the ruins of the ancient Greek amphitheater and much more ... All this will tell you about the former glory of these places. In addition, Side is a great place to relax with children. In Turkey, the younger generation has a special warm attitude. Children here are welcome guests. Turkey is always glad to them. Side Iona Hotels and others, even the most inexpensive, are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay for young tourists: a cot, high chair, special menu, private pool, etc. In addition, the entrance to the sea on most beaches is gentle, which means safe for babies. Naturally, in any hotel there is a team of animators who will not let guests get bored.



Side is a city for lovers

Everyone knows about the beautiful love story of the Egyptian queen Cleopatra and the Roman commander Mark Anthony . So, one of their most romantic dates in the history of mankind took place here in Side. This is told by an ancient legend. That's why this resort is preferred by many honeymooners for their honeymoon. So, if you want to spend the first joint month of your married life interestingly, but you don‚Äôt have the money to travel to Venice or Paris, go to Side. Side Iona Hotel, and all other resort hotels, will welcome you with open arms, a bottle of champagne and a basket of fruit. The choice of hotels in Side is very large: there are for every taste and budget. Here there are multi-storey modern hotels, and hotel complexes, consisting of many buildings and bungalows, and apart-hotels, etc.

Recreation areas

Side is divided into several main resort areas: Kumkoy, Cholakli, Titreengel and, in fact, Manavgat. It is in this area that Side Iona Hotel 3 * is located. Turkey, unlike other Mediterranean countries, provides tourists with holidays at relatively low prices. Moreover, the level of service is no worse, and sometimes even better than in many of them. This resort is just a few kilometers from the center of Side. By the way, there is a magnificent oriental bazaar. So, if you are a shopping fan, then you have a direct way here.

Iona Hotel Side Hotel - the best option for an economical vacation

This hotel was built in 1998. In those days it was called differently - Side Life Hotel. Later, after reconstruction in 2008, it was renamed the Side Iona Hotel. This is a three star hotel. It consists of five three-story buildings located next to each other and united by a common pool and territory, the size of which is 4200 square meters. Number of rooms

The Side Iona Hotel has a total of 119 rooms of various categories. Among them there are non-smoking rooms, as well as rooms for pets. Of the total number of rooms, 36 are standard rooms, and 83 are family rooms. Besides the fact that standard rooms are 10 square meters. meters less than family, the second have separate bedrooms and living rooms.

Room arrangement and service

All rooms have a balcony or terrace, depending on the floor and the location of the room in the building, as well as a well-equipped bathroom, where you can find all the necessary toiletries and a hairdryer. For the convenience of guests, there is a split system, a safe and a minibar for a surcharge, satellite channels, a direct dial telephone, etc. The rooms are carefully cleaned every day, and linen is changed 2-3 times a week. Towels can be changed daily.



Dining at Side Iona Hotel 3 *

Like most hotels in Turkey with a status of 3-5 stars, this hotel serves tourists on the principle of All Inclusive (OI). The hotel has a buffet restaurant. From the reviews of the guests it becomes known that the food here is pretty decent: tasty and varied, plus everything, baked goods and fruits are served daily. In addition to the restaurant, there is a bar, lobby, pool bar.

Infrastructure and Services

The hotel has a parking lot, a currency exchange office, a medical office, a left-luggage office, car and bicycle rental, laundry and dry cleaning, ironing services, etc. For the kids there is a mini club, a children's room, a playground with a sandbox and swings, and also a children's pool.

Entertainment

Exotic lovers can visit the hammam (Turkish bath), while more conservative tourists can relax in the Finnish sauna or take a course of relaxing massage. In the fitness club you can work out aerobics or just build muscle in the gym. And before a romantic dinner, you can look into the beauty salon, where you will be provided with hairdressing services, manicures and pedicures. Sports enthusiasts are invited to ride a bicycle, play tennis, darts, billiards, etc. And for fans of excursions, various trips to attractions are organized. The most interesting are visits to the ancient city of Side, an inspection of the ruins of old buildings, which are of historical value.

Beach

A mile and a half from the hotel is a private beach. A bus leaves there in the morning, and takes tourists back in the evening. Sun loungers, umbrellas, mattresses and towels are free of charge for hotel guests. However, many tourists prefer to visit the municipal beach, which is located just a 5-minute walk. Here, the inventory is provided for vacationers for a fee, however, the prices are not too high. For $ 5 you can rent an umbrella with a couple of sunbeds for the whole day. Almost all beaches are equipped with showers, toilets and changing rooms. Vacationers are given the opportunity to engage in water sports, such as water skiing, surfing and diving, parachuting above the sea, snorkeling and yachting, and a catamaran ride.





Tourists reviews

The comments of the guests about this hotel are quite positive. At the same time, as often happens, vacationers point to both the pros and cons. For example, a big plus is the fact that you can stay at the hotel with your pet. And this is very rare. In most hotels in Turkey this is prohibited. Tourists also point to excellent food conditions. The menu is quite varied and includes a large selection of international dishes. It is very important that during all meals tourists are served fresh fruits and vegetables. The level of service is also at a high level. There are no complaints about sanitary conditions. This is all right here. But the minus, perhaps, can be called not very fresh linen and towels. Some guests point out that bedding is quite washed out, although spotlessly clean. In a word, the hotel provides good conditions for relaxation, of course, if we consider such a statement from the point of view of not too picky people. And on the other hand, we must remember that this is not a five-star hotel, but a three-ruble note.
