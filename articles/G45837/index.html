Hotel Donau Marina Centro 3 * (Italy, Rimini and Ravenna): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Almost any sophisticated holidaymaker who has visited ten or more hotels in Turkey, Egypt and even flew to Thailand or Vietnam, when choosing another hotel comes the moment of truth. The essence of the latter: the desire to visit, after all, a classic European resort. What to do such a tourist? Indeed, on the one hand, hunting, as they say, is worse than bondage, and on the other, European resort prices will be higher than, say, in Goa.





However, in any situation there is a way out. In particular, you can visit a fairly democratic resort, while choosing a small and inexpensive three-star economy hotel.

Hotel among attractions

As an option (one of many), you can recommend a tour with accommodation in Donau 3 * (Italy). Hotel Donau (Rimini) is located extremely well: in the center of the famous Adriatic resort, just in the center of Rimini, Marina Centro quarter, and even on the first line. Magnificent European Blue Flag beaches are spread out over a wide sand strip of 15 kilometers along the coast. In addition, Rimini is considered one of the world famous centers of budget fashion. Italians themselves come here to relax.

Not far from the hotel we are considering, lies Piazza Cavour (square), the famous Rimini Conference Center, and the Fiabilandia theme park. From the hotel you can walk to the train station in a quarter of an hour, and Miramare Airport in a few minutes.

However, the Italians themselves will describe the location of the hotel in a slightly different way: Donau Marina Centro 3 * (Italy, Emilia Romagna). After all, Rimini for them is one of the eight provincial centers of the richest, successful and dynamic region of Italy, which is called Emilia Romagna. He is known in the world as the patrimony of the Ferrari, Mozeratti, Lamborghini automobile concerns. This land is also famous in the world of art thanks to such names as Pavarotti, Toscanini, Verdi.

About hotel

In a compact five-story building at Viale Vittorio Alfieri, 12 47921 Rimini is a small private hotel Donau Marina Centro 3 *. Reviews about him emphasize his niche in the resort business: a budget but decent vacation. The architecture of the building and the design of the rooms can be characterized by a single word: minimalism. However, tourists get good basic services here and enjoy obvious bonuses from the advantageous location of the hotel.





Newly arrived tourists settle in Donau Marina Centro 3 * from 12:00, and departing must leave their room at 10:00. Such is the order in the hotel complex. Friendly and welcoming hotel owner Antonio and his wife Svetlana. The hostess and another employee speaks Russian at the hotel.

Rooms

Only 27 rooms are offered to tourists by the city hotel Donau Marina Centro 3 *. Guest reviews indicate that there are practically no empty guest rooms. The rooms are very compact, but the bathroom is quite comfortable, plumbing and water supply are at their best. The rooms are double with a small balcony. Of the technology in the guest rooms have a TV and air conditioning. A separate bonus is the in-room safe.





For Wi-Fi you need to pay $ 5. Free access is available in the reception area. However, resourceful and advanced guests of Donau Marina unofficially use the wireless network of a neighboring hotel (password is its name).

Donau Marina Centro 3 * rooms are cleaned daily, towels are replaced with laundered every other day, and bed linen is replaced twice a week. Nutrition

The food offered by the Donau Marina Centro 3 * hotel to its guests can be called typical of small hotels. It is provided by a small hotel restaurant. As you would expect from an institution with 20-30 rooms, the range of dishes is not too wide, but only a little more diverse than what is prepared at home. The emphasis is on freshness and quality.

Since most tourists are on the beach during the day, on excursions or shopping, then, accordingly, the type of food offered to them is bivariate:

  • breakfast + dinner.

Breakfast, judging by customer reviews, is quite easy: scrambled eggs, ham, cheese, granola, croissants, several coffee options (the restaurant has a good coffee machine). All this is prepared by a really tasty hotel owner himself, with unique good nature and the skill of a good chef.

Shopping

Some of the guests of Donau Marina Centro 3 * traditionally go to Rimini precisely because of shopping. Therefore, we highlight this aspect in more detail. After all, this city is considered in Italy the informal capital of budget fashion, a place where relatively inexpensive, but spectacular purchases are made. It is here that cunning "shuttles" entrepreneurs fill their trunks.





Where to begin? First, accept it as an axiom: experienced buyers purchase light industry products in Italy not in markets and stores, but directly in the factories that manufacture them. At the same time, for example, buying a pair of shoes in a warehouse can save up to 150 ‚Ç¨ compared to purchasing it in a company store.

What do hotel guests write in their testimonials? Let's start by buying fur coats. There are two factories in Rimini: Unifur (which produces more affordable products) and Brashi. Accordingly, it is more profitable to buy in the warehouses of these companies.
