Receiver "Tricolor TV" - all you need to watch many channels!

Receiver "Tricolor TV" - This is the main device in the set of satellite equipment. As practice shows, it is he who has the highest cost. When choosing a receiver, most of our compatriots make a decision based on their financial capabilities. But it is not always justified. The ability to view many additional channels and the quality of the picture on the TV screen depends on its functionality. The interface is also worth considering.

connectivity. In cheap devices - a limited set of connectors and it is not always possible to connect to a TV. All this must be remembered at the stage of choosing a device such as the Tricolor TV receiver.

Entry level models

Conventionally, satellite signal receivers can be divided into groups:

  1. Working with a signal in MPEG-2 format
  2. Working with a signal in MPEG-2 / MPEG-4 format

The first type of device is slowly going into oblivion, but its low cost contributes to a stable level of sales. It makes sense to buy such receivers only if the diagonal of the TV is small and there is no plan to replace the television receiver in the foreseeable future. Also, any Tricolor TV receiver must be equipped with a card reader or equipped with a DRE CAM module. Without this, there will be no possibility of physical viewing. The most inexpensive tuners - from $ 40. Before purchasing, you should also pay attention to the presence of the necessary connectors for connecting to a TV. To do this, look through the documentation for it and determine the possible connectors for connection - SCART or RCA (tulip). The most favorable option is when the Tricolor TV receiver is equipped with both. A COM port for connecting to a PC via RS-232 should also be output (this will significantly simplify the process of updating the device software). The most popular models in this segment are the DIGIRAUM DRE 4000 and DIGIRAUM DRE 7300, which meet all the previously stated requirements.





Advanced systems

All of the above must be included in the more expensive receivers costing from $ 65. They are also equipped with a USB port and an HDMI interface. The first of them is sometimes intended purely for updating software (for example, a device from the General Satellite GS-8307B trademark). In some cases, it can also be used to play videos, watch photos, and listen to music (the same JEFERSON X-008). The HDMI interface allows you to get the highest quality image on the latest TV models with a diagonal of 32 inches or more. In such a situation, the Tricolor HD receiver is complemented by a high-resolution TV, and the picture with sound is simply excellent. This is the optimal combination, which in the next few years will be exactly relevant,

since until the appearance of new standards is not expected, and their implementation will take some time. And such systems have already been successfully operated and managed to prove themselves from the best side.
