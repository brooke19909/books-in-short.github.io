4G LTE - what is it? Yota 4G LTE Modem

Internet technologies do not stand still - this is a fact. Surely many of us have heard of such a mobile communication format as 3G. Maybe one of us now has a card of an operator providing third-generation communication services on his phone or tablet. So, imagine, 3G technology is out of date. In many ways, it is inferior to the new connection, which is several times faster - 4G LTE. What is it, and why the transition to a new and improved communication format will not happen as fast as we would like, we will tell further.

4G connection - the best internet in the world





So, let's start with the fact that today communication using fourth-generation technology (or LTE-Internet, as it is also called) is the most advanced format for remote data transmission in a mobile network. It can be called the best in comparison with those formats that existed earlier (2G and 3G in particular). The reason for this is simple - the high transmission speed of 4G LTE. What this means in practice is evident from the simplest example: if we can download a movie in a third-generation network in 40-50 minutes, then, working in LTE, downloading the same amount of data can be achieved in some 7-8 minutes. Really impressive?

Why are we still on 3G?





However, do not rejoice at such a high speed of information transfer prematurely. In fact, operators are experiencing a large number of problems associated with building a 4G network. First of all, it is the high cost of equipment, which must be launched additionally in order to block the existing mobile network. Roughly speaking, to provide the Internet in 4G LTE format, MTS (like any other operator) is forced to rebuild a huge number of towers. All this is a big operational and financial cost.

How to find a device to work in LTE?

In addition to the problem associated with the construction of a network for the transmission of data of the fourth generation, there is another. It lies in the device with which subscribers are connected.





Let's just say: in order to catch 4G Internet, you must have one of the most advanced gadgets (since only the "top" models, as a rule, are able to work with this format). Therefore, a logical question arises as to whether it is worth making such a quick transition to a 4G LTE network, for which a modem is not so easy to find. The operators obviously proceed from the fact that it is not so expedient to do it very quickly. Therefore, we continue to use mainly Internet services in the 3G network.

4G disadvantages in Russia

Of course, in Russia, the culture of using high-speed mobile Internet has not yet reached the scale that can be observed in the West. In the USA, for example, many operators are trying to transfer subscribers to LTE format as soon as possible, offering smartphones that support this network and more favorable conditions for those who thought and switched to such a tariff.

As for Russia, here everything is sadder in terms of technology. Despite the huge number of tablets and smartphones, most of them belong to the budget class, and therefore work in LTE is not supported. Operators see low activity on the high-speed Internet network, and therefore are not in a hurry to increase coverage. In turn, users complain about failures in signal reception and lack of coverage in most regions in general. It turns out a kind of vicious circle, get out of which will be possible, given the economic situation, soon.

However, do not despair. Residents of the capital, at least now, can use the services of operators, instantly downloading pages, downloading music in a couple of seconds and downloading the full version of their favorite movie in minutes.
