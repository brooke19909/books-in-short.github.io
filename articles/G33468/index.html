Samara Sights of the city and region

Samara is an interesting and versatile city. Until now, he does not have a single special tourist brand that could be safely associated with this village. Travelers and tourists, getting to Samara, whose attractions are scattered throughout the city, begin their inspection from the main streets, from walks along the long embankment of the Volga River and tastings of real Zhigulevsky beer, which is produced here.

From the history

So, let's start with a little historical background. The first settlements in this territory date back to the time of the Paleolithic era. Subsequently, events in the territory of the region developed under the influence of Batu Khan and the era of the Golden Horde.



The history of Samara is also inextricably linked with the Cossacks. In the 16th century, free settlements began to settle here, which subsequently long resisted joining the main territories of the country. However, in 1586 the fortress of Samara was founded, which in 1689 became a city.

Great people

Samara, whose sights are closely connected with the life events of many famous people, played a large role in many destinies. It was here that Maxim Gorky began his career and spent the first years of his life writer Alexei Tolstoy. It was in this city that the Ulyanov family lived for a long time.



Signed Places

All travelers should definitely visit Samara Square and Glory Square. From this point, a stunning view. To look around the city, spread out on both sides of the mighty Volga, and enjoy its majestic view - this is something that must be done! In the square, tourists stop at the Monument of Glory. It is a statue of a worker in whose hands are wings. This symbolizes the great contribution of Samara workers to the raising and development of aviation activities in the country. Here eternal flame burns .

Samara, whose attractions include monuments not only of the Soviet period, but also of more ancient times, is filled with temples and cathedrals. Not far from the main square is the Cathedral of St. George the Victorious. On Stepan Razin Street is one of the most magnificent buildings in the city - the Ascension Cathedral. For all lovers of ancient architecture, it will also be interesting to visit the Intercession Cathedral. He once suffered during a fire that destroyed his frescoes and murals, but the building itself is beautiful and pleases citizens and tourists today.

For many who come here for the first time it becomes a real discovery of Samara. Sights, entertainment, monuments, temples, Volga - all this makes an impression. A monument to Vysotsky was erected in the city , the Zhiguli Brewery is operating, Samara Embankment, the Voronezh Lakes Park and the Drama Theater are also attractive. Samara sights with addresses can be found on the city map. They are handed out free of charge in tourist centers.



All lovers of active time will surely enjoy Samara. The sights of this city are not limited to architecture - the nature here is really beautiful! On the wide sandy beaches of the river, you can master kite surfing and try to catch the capricious river wind.
