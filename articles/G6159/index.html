Hotel Telatiye Resort 5 *, Turkey, Alanya: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Alanya - a sunny resort in Turkey, located 120 km from Antalya - attracts a developed tourism industry and a rich infrastructure. This is not as popular a place as, for example, Kemer, but it is still among the popular resorts such as Marmaris, Bodrum and Side. Long sandy beaches, an abundance of interesting sightseeing tours, the azure Mediterranean Sea, many elite boutiques for shopping are the main advantages of Alanya, which make the resort attractive to Russian and foreign citizens.





In addition, in the city you will find hotel complexes for every taste and prosperity - luxury apartments and budget rooms. Do you dream of living near the coast in a modern complex? Then book a room at the Telatiye Resort 5 * hotel (the old name is ex. Seal of Star). The hotel is built in the national style, and it is located in the small village of Konakli, where there are many green walking alleys, shopping centers, entertainment areas and a huge bazaar with interesting souvenirs, decorations, exotic treats.

There is a dolphinarium, an amusement park with attractions, a golf club. The hotel area is not large. It housed a multi-storey building consisting of seven floors. Employees speak several foreign languages, including Russian, which greatly facilitates communication. For guests who arrive by personal transport, a parking space is allocated.

Accommodation

Telatiye Resort (ex. Seal of Star) can accommodate about 400 guests at a time. For accommodation, 176 comfortable and renovated rooms are provided. All rooms have loggias overlooking the park area or the Mediterranean Sea. Spacious apartments are available for couples.





Non-smoking rooms are available, as well as a room for people with disabilities. All rooms are connected to cable TV, equipped with air conditioning and a private bathroom. The rooms are equipped with a landline telephone with direct dialing, soft beds, a desk, wardrobes. A safe and a drink bar are available for a fee.

If you need ironing equipment, then it will be delivered to the room upon request. As for cleaning, it is carried out according to the schedule, at a time convenient for guests. Towels and bedding are changed daily.

Food concept

The payment includes full three meals a day. The method of serving dishes is carried out according to the European buffet system in the main restaurant of the Telatiye Resort 5 * hotel. Beautiful table setting and a variety of delicious treats will delight any gourmet. Several times a week, chefs pamper visitors with oriental dishes, which are distinguished by their spiciness and riot of spicy spices.





On the tables there are always exotic fruits, sweet desserts, pastries, as well as drinks of different strengths. For guests there are 4 restaurants and 5 bars with delicious cuisine and a wide selection of elite alcohol.

Coastline

One of the advantages of Primasol Telatiye Resort, according to most people, is the sand and pebble coast, fenced off from prying eyes. Due to the presence of fine pebbles, the water in the sea is clean and transparent. True, you will need special shoes so as not to injure your legs. You can get to the coast in 5 minutes.





Terry towels are provided at the reception with a cash deposit, which is then returned. In free use - sun loungers with umbrellas. There are 2 bars on the beach with free snacks and drinks. Guests can enjoy water transport (jet ski, banana, ski, yacht). Works children's karting, trampoline. In the late afternoon, enchanting performances are organized for the adult audience on the coast.

For young guests





If adults go to the Telatiye Resort 5 * hotel in order to escape from everyday life and routine, then children need new knowledge and vivid impressions. All this is organized for them by employees of the complex. In addition to stormy fun at sea, building sand castles, swimming on water slides, animation programs await children. On the territory adjacent to the territory there is a play area where children can be creative (draw, sculpt, cut). For active children - a playground equipped with modern swings.

Leisure activities for adults





Those who are too lazy to get to the beach can always enjoy water treatments in the outdoor pool. Sports tourists will love the gym with an experienced instructor. Telatiye Resort 5 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya, Konakli) has soft tennis courts, spacious volleyball, football and basketball courts.

Aerobics is carried out for those who wish, guests are taught oriental dances. The building has a billiard room, a television room for watching your favorite films. At your leisure, read an interesting literary work taken in the library.





Women will appreciate the spa with beauty treatments, a sauna and a jacuzzi. Every day at the Telatiye Resort 5 * hotel there are fascinating shows with dancing, live music, and performance by artists. For the business public - conference rooms of small and large areas (for 40-250 people).

Digest

In a positive vein, guests are talking about the Telatiye Resort Hotel 5 * hotel. Guest reviews indicate quality food. The beautifully decorated restaurant captivates with a cozy atmosphere, delicious treats, full of fresh fruits. Skillful cooks prepare stunning dishes of meat, seafood.





There are treats for small children. For the concept of food guests put a huge plus. Tidy and clean rooms with new furniture and plumbing equipment received good marks. An unthinkable amount of praise is available for animation, sports games.

It is not at all necessary to attend local discos, since dances, karaoke and incendiary programs take place on the territory of the complex. A vacation spent in the resort of Alanya will charm everyone with its picturesque nature, hospitality and attractions. Do not forget to bring along a great mood and a camera to capture positive moments!
