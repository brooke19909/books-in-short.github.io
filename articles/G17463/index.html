Rest in Adler: reviews and photos

Adler is the southernmost housing estate in Greater Sochi. The city stretches along the pebble coast of the Black Sea. The resort is the starting point for excursions to Krasnaya Polyana. Adler is seventeen kilometers long. The Mzymta riverbed divides the city into two parts.

Historical reference

Those who are going to spend their holidays in Adler will find it useful to know that the first settlement in this place appeared at the beginning of the XIX century. In the era of the USSR, the microdistrict received the status of a health resort. Local sanatoriums still use healing mud extracted from the bottom of the Black Sea. They are diluted with mineral water from the balneological sources of Matsesta.

Sludge baths have an effective therapeutic effect on patients suffering from diseases of the musculoskeletal system, digestive disorders, pathologies of the cardiovascular system. Adler managed to become a resort of all-Russian scale after the Olympic Games in 2014.

The presence of modern sports facilities and an amusement park attract tens of thousands of Russians on vacation in Adler. After the Olympics, the city administration was able to completely modernize the local infrastructure. The resort has new motorways, hotels and the promenade.

How to get there

Most residents of the northern regions of the country get to Adler by plane. The international airport is a short drive from the resort. Every day in the high season, it serves more than six dozen flights arriving from Moscow, St. Petersburg, Kazan, Perm, Samara, Rostov-on-Don, Novosibirsk, Krasnoyarsk, Ufa and other settlements of the country.

Those who come to rest in Adler are usually greeted by the owners of the guest houses. Taxi drivers offer their services. On average, a trip with a breeze costs three hundred rubles. Municipal buses run. The resort's renewed railway station accepts dozens of trains coming from the center of Russia and other regions of the country.

Suburban trains "Swallow" stopping on its platforms from Tuapse, Sochi, Krasnaya Polyana and Lazarevsky. In the summer, Russian Railways launches an electric train to Abkhazia. A wide highway runs to the city. It starts in Sochi. It was commissioned on the eve of the Olympic Games. This road is the main and only artery accessible to those who arrive on vacation in Adler by private car.

Almost all parts of Adler have hotels of various levels, long pebble beaches, cafes and restaurants for every taste. There is no access to water in Chaysovkhoz, Moldovan, Blue Dahl and Oryol-Emerald.

At the very sea

Fun is a few kilometers from the coast. Adler holiday reviews say the owners offer a free shuttle service. Comfortable buses and cars ply the beach. Imereti resort is chosen by those who are interested in objects of the Olympic Park. The beach in this part of the microdistrict is narrow. Large boulders are found on it.

The length of the promenade is almost five kilometers. It rests against the bed of the Psou River, along which lies the Russian-Abkhaz border. In this part of the village there is a large selection of housing for those who dream of relaxing on the beach. In Adler, there are fewer such options.

Spa life

Along the promenade are concentrated stalls, souvenir shops, cafes and restaurants, rental bicycles and scooters. On the first line are large chain hotels. Their territory is surrounded by tropical greenery. Many have outdoor pools around which deck chairs and colorful umbrellas are placed.

Accommodation at the recreation centers of Adler is chosen by the young and active. Such options are available in the central part of the district and along the road to Sochi. Often they are campsites and consist of compact wooden houses located a few meters from the Black Sea. The beach in the city is usually crowded.

Next to it is the McDonald's. Immediately stretched the ownership of the farmer's market. This part of the resort is recommended for families with children. In Adler, no one will be bored. The city is replete with shops and shopping galleries. There are water parks and an aquarium. The business card of the microdistrict is Sochi Park. In the evening, numerous discos and bars open their doors. Restaurants are open until the last visitor.

Recreation area

Guest houses, recreation centers, the private sector in Adler are concentrated along the Black Sea coast. The swimming season in this part of Greater Sochi begins in May, and finally ends in October. Local beaches are covered with pebbles, interspersed with sand. The size of the stones is small, rarely exceeding ten centimeters.

Entrance to the water in the swimming areas is smooth and even, without boulders and sharp ledges. Almost everywhere there are breakwaters. They are not in the region of the Rossiya state farm, therefore it can be dangerous to be on the shore in a storm. Holidays by the sea in Adler attracts with its accessibility. At the disposal of tourists municipal and private beaches. Entrance to the territory of sanatorium zones may be paid.

Kudepsta's beaches are famous for the abundance of tropical greenery that approaches the very edge of the Black Sea. In the early morning you can meet dolphins in the water area. The best views of the Caucasus Mountains open from the beach, located near the mouth of the Mzymta River. True, this place has long been chosen by nudists.

Sports and diving

Holidays on the beach in Adler has an active pastime. There are several schools in the city that teach beginners deep-sea diving. They offer an international certificate. The introductory course will cost 2,000 rubles. Comprehensive training costs 20,000. Instructors organize night baths in the open waters of the Black Sea.

Professionals can enjoy extreme diving in underwater gorges, diving in lakes located high in the mountains.

Where to eat

On the territory of Adler, more than five hundred cafes and restaurants are registered. The functioning of many is seasonal, but there are those that do not close even in winter. Inexpensive and tasty dinners are offered by Trattoria Fettuccine, Triconi tavern, Hops and Suneli, Olivier, Sail, La Luna, GastroCub, and the Art Bar.
