Where to withdraw cash from VTB card? List of ATMs VTB-24 in Lipetsk

VTB Bank today is one of the largest and most reliable in Russia, which is why many residents prefer to use debit and credit cards of this financial institution. For the convenience of customers, a large number of ATMs have been opened in the city. The list of addresses is presented below.

Located in bank branches

The VTB-24 ATM in Lipetsk was also installed in the branch of Bank 3652 Lipetsk at 33 Gagarina Street. It operates 24/7. Bank customers are able to withdraw and deposit cash even at night, pay a loan or utilities, and replenish the balance of their mobile phone.

The ATM with serial number 386213 is installed in the branch of Zvezdny Bank at the following address: Valentina Tereshkova Street, 13, Building B. Terminal working hours: 24/7 without days off. Of the available services: cash in foreign currencies and rubles, cash in rubles, various payments.

The ATM with serial number 377514 is installed in the additional office of the bank at Cosmonauts. Exact address: Kosmonavtov street, 102.

Installed in institutions and organizations

ATM VTB-24 in Lipetsk can also be found on the territory of the railway station. The exact address: 106 Gagarina Street. Clients can either deposit cash in dollars, rubles and euros, or withdraw their money from the card account. It is worth noting that this terminal also operates around the clock.

The ATM with the serial number 382979 is installed on the territory of OOO "Friend-L" at the address: Universalniy passage, house 11. It functions according to the operating mode of the LLC. Of the available functions: cashless payments, cash withdrawals.

The ATM with serial number 395147 is installed on the territory of Lipetsk-Lada OJSC. Exact address: 36 Moskovskaya Street. Cash deposit is not available, withdrawal only.

Installed in the shopping center

The address of VTB-24 ATM in Lipetsk, installed in the shopping and entertainment center "Moscow": German Titov Street, 10. Of the available services: cashless payments, cash withdrawals. The operating mode of the terminal coincides with the operating mode of the mall.

The ATM with serial number 382812 is installed on the 1st floor of the Auchan shopping center. The exact address of the shopping center: st. Hero of Russia Eduard Belan, 26. The terminal is available according to the opening hours of the store. At the same time, the work schedule may change during the holidays.

Also, the bank terminal can be found on the territory of the Lipka store (IE Chebotareva Elena Aleksandrovna). Exact address: st. Them. General Merkulov, building 14.
