Creating the foundation for the Christmas tree

As a basis, you can use a purchased foam cone, a rolled part of the bottle and a paper sheet. Here we will look at how to make a paper cone for a Christmas tree, which will be the basis for almost all masterpieces.

To do this, you will need thick cardboard, adhesive tape or glue, scissors and compasses. Measure the height of the future beauty on a compass and draw a quarter circle on the prepared cardboard. For convenience of fastening, leave a small allowance. Now connect the two straight lines and fix with glue or tape. This will become the basis. How to make a Christmas tree from paper. The photo below will tell you where to start.





As a leg for a tree, you can use one or more glued wooden skewers. Now you can start decorating the cone. The easiest way is to wrap it with tinsel with a slight tilt and fix it on glue.

Herringbone of toilet paper

Today, few people are surprised that the tree is made of paper. How to do such unique things, few still know. Imagine the surprise of the guests when they see the beauty of toilet paper!

Instructions for creating:

  1. Cut a piece of toilet paper.
  2. Cut four semicircles so that the radius of the next is 0.5 centimeters smaller than the previous one.
  3. Form from them four cones.
  4. To betray the spruce trees, cut the bottom of each of them with a fringe.
  5. Place one on top of the other, starting with the largest.
  6. Place them on a suitable foot.

A simple and affordable way to create an original and funny decoration that will surprise guests.

Christmas tree with paws in the form of cones

Draw a circle with a compass and divide it into 12 identical sectors. Cut them along the marked lines so that they can easily form cones. Stick them on a drop of glue or on a small piece of thin tape. Such details will need at least 10 pieces for the smallest Christmas tree. For large beauties, they need much more. Start your work with a small copy, and only then swipe at the masterpieces of the "global scale". Each next circle should be slightly smaller than the previous one. Keep decreasing until work becomes impossible.





Use a thick wire or skewer to hold them together. The big advantage of the wire is that it can be used to create a small base that will give the tree stability. Many are interested in how to make Christmas tree toys from paper, because it is not able to withstand massive decorations. Consider gluing rhinestones and sparkles, in some cases it may be plastic buttons. The surest option, suitable for any beauty, is decoration with paper toys or artificial snow, which can be purchased at shops made with hand made goods.





Pointed Christmas Tree

To create such a design version, you will need thick colored paper. It is necessary to cut out even circles from it, which will differ by a radius of about 0.5-1 cm. After that, each of them fold in half. The workpiece must be folded in half as long as possible. Iron all folds carefully with the tips of your nails or scissors. It is important to achieve rigidity and understand the principle of how to make a Christmas tree from colored paper. Now carefully straighten the tier, without straightening the resulting accordion. As a result, you should get a cone that looks like a wavy skirt. Do this with all the circles.





You can collect the tiers obtained on a skewer installed in a cork and fixed there with a solution of alabaster or ordinary plasticine. Attach beads, rain or other light decor to your feet. As a star, you can use a large shiny bead that will hide the tip of the wire or skewer.

Christmas tree from the magazine

Surely everyone has somewhere hidden a stack of magazines that you are unlikely to use for their intended purpose. Fold all the pages in the form of triangles, then turn out the magazine and glue the two extreme pages. A big plus in such a Christmas tree is that it does not need any decor. Aesthetic appeal is achieved by the beautiful drawings that were on the pages.





The second option is also made from an old magazine. Instead of folding pages, cut them according to the template with the same pattern, for example, in the form of several traditional triangles.
