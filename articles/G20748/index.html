Vestel refrigerator: reviews, instructions, country of origin

The modern market for household appliances is rich not only in a variety of models, but also in manufacturers. A huge selection of refrigerators is available to consumers, depending on their own needs, product quality, technical specifications, and price criteria. Often, buyers are primarily interested in the manufacturer. Vestel refrigeration equipment has recently attracted attention. This is mainly due to the current economic situation in the country, which increases the demand for economy class models.

Vestel refrigerators: manufacturer

The registered company has a head office and production in Turkey. Many are sure that the country of origin of the Vestel refrigerator is Turkey. It is worth noting here that the equipment that is supplied to the Russian market is assembled in our country, in the city of Alexandrov, according to the technology of the Turks and under their control. The Vestel refrigerator, according to customer reviews, meets all the criteria that apply to modern technology. Therefore, this Turkish manufacturer is considered one of the most popular companies producing low-cost and high-quality refrigerators. This is due to the fact that for a very reasonable price you can buy a technical device equipped with additional functions, which greatly simplifies everyday life. Today it is a huge holding, which includes 19 companies. Each of them is engaged in technical development and production of electronic devices.





The company was founded in the 80s of the last century and began to develop so quickly that in a few years it had many representative offices in Europe - in France, Germany, Italy and a number of Scandinavian countries.

General instruction manual

Each model produced by the manufacturer has distinctive features and advanced features. Therefore, there is no single instruction manual for Vestel refrigerators. However, it is worth paying special attention to safety measures, since key aspects are suitable for the device in general, regardless of their novelty.

The developer strongly does not recommend installing refrigeration equipment in a place where direct sunlight falls. This is due to the fact that in sunny weather the refrigerator will begin to cope worse with its duties and become very hot. The same goes for the tight installation of stoves and other heating elements next to the refrigerator. If it is possible to make a permutation, then it is better to carry it out, or think in advance of the installation location of the new device.

Disadvantages of refrigerators

However, Vestel refrigerators have their drawbacks:

  1. After some time of active use, the required temperature to maintain the quality of the products ceases to be maintained, gradually increasing the indicator until the function is completely lost. As a rule, in order to eliminate this breakdown, you have to change the fan.
  2. Loud noise during operation of the Vestel refrigerator. The instruction manual obliges consumers to correctly install the device. If this rule is not observed, the operation of the unit may be incorrect. Most of the models of modern lines of the manufacturer belong to the A class and have a comfortable range of noise levels. Regarding this problem of the Vestel refrigerator, the reviews of the masters are almost the same - improper installation of the device in terms of level.
  3. Depressurization. This is the most serious drawback, because in this case all the products begin to deteriorate quickly, because the cold does not remain in the chamber. Most often, to solve the problem, it is necessary to replace the sealing gum on the refrigerator door.
