The best Valdai hotels: overview

Valdai is the cradle of the rivers of Russia. These are not just words: here the great rivers originate - the Dnieper, Volga, Western Dvina. In addition, an uncountable number of their tributaries - small rivers and rivers, thousands of springs, of which dozens are of great value as sources of mineral healing water.

The rivers are crystal clear and full of water. Some of them are swift and ringing, others are quiet and unhurried, but each determines the well-being of a large territory of the entire European part of the country. Valdai Upland, in fact, is the main source of fresh water in Russia.

Today we will consider the most popular Valdai hotels among tourists.

Hotel Valdai Dawns

Among tourists, the city of Valdai is quite popular. Most of the hotels here are ready to receive vacationers around the clock.



Hotel "Valdai Dawns" is located in one of the most beautiful places of the reserve, on the shores of Lake Valdai. A huge number of rooms makes it possible to choose a room even for the most demanding customer. The restaurant with amazing cuisine located here will help you spend time with friends, and in summer there is a beach where you can soak under the caressing rays of the sun.

Cost: from 2500 rubles per day.

Rest house "Valdai"

In Valdai, hotels are mostly located on the banks of rivers. Rest house "Valdai" is located on the shores of Lake Dinner. It features a spa, sauna and pool. In public areas there is free Wi-Fi, free wired internet in the rooms. The comfortable rooms are traditionally furnished and equipped with a fridge, air conditioning, an electric kettle and a TV.



Bathrooms feature complimentary toiletries. In addition, on the territory of this complex there is a phyto-bar, cafe and restaurant. For active guests, a tennis court and a fitness center are provided. After a hard day, guests can play billiards or table tennis. The property provides a shuttle service to / from the bus and train station.

Cost: from 5900 rubles per day.

Mini-hotel "Jupiter"

This mini-hotel is located on the shores of Lake Valdai and offers stunning views of the famous Trinity Cathedral. The hotel is located near the city center of Valdai (hotels here, as already mentioned, mainly delight guests with a convenient location and excellent views). Number of rooms: only 5 rooms with a total capacity of 22 people. At the same time, there are 2 superior rooms.



The rooms have a double ottoman, a wardrobe, a sofa, a bathroom, satellite TV.

Cost: from 2050 rubles per day.

Mini-hotel "Maracanas"

"Maracans" is located near the very center of the city, near the Valdai Lake. The elegant and stylish rooms of this Valdai hotel will delight everyone with their comfort.



The highest level of service, homeliness and caring staff is the visiting card of this mini-hotel.

Cost: from 1500 rubles per day.

Hotel "Russian Village"

This Valdai hotel offers 8 rooms of different price categories. There is a DVD player, satellite TV, 3 warm bathrooms (one on the second floor and two on the first), as well as refrigerators.



Cost: from 1800 rubles per day.

Mini-hotel "Flagman"

This Valdai hotel offers 4 rooms of different price categories for guests.



In each of them there is a TV, a bed, a coffee table, bedside tables.

Hotel Valdai

The hotels of this city are never empty. Especially if we talk about the Valdai Hotel. It is located in the city center, next to the Museum of Bells. For guests there are 55 rooms equipped with comfortable furniture and created in the spirit of functional conciseness. One of the main advantages of the institution is its very favorable location. The hospitable and friendly staff will make sure that guests feel comfortable.



The rooms are equipped with a TV and air conditioning. Private bathrooms come with complimentary toiletries. Extras include a fridge and a balcony. Guests are offered meals in a buffet style. They can also enjoy snacks and drinks at the cafe. On the territory of this holiday home you can use all the necessary barbecue facilities. There are also 2 phyto bars where guests can order tasty and healthy oxygen cocktails.

There is a tennis court and a fitness center. After a difficult day, guests can play billiards with friends and enjoy a relaxing massage. Staff at the tour desk located here organize excursions to nearby attractions. A boat station and a souvenir shop are located on the small pebbled coast of the lake.

Cost: from 4100 rubles per day.
