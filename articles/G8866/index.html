DIY aircraft modeling: how to make a plane out of wood

Every boy in his childhood dreamed of being at the controls of an airplane. The sky, clouds, travels always entailed adventurers and brave ones. But in order to feel closer to aviation, it is not necessary to buy a plane ticket or enter a flight school. After reading this article, you will learn how to make a plane out of wood with your own hands.



Necessary tools and materials

Before you search for a suitable model of the aircraft and its drawing, you must provide yourself with the necessary materials. The best option is aviation plywood. In its manufacture, three layers of birch veneer are glued using phenolic glue.

In this case, the thickness of the material is important, it should be about 1 mm, but thicker plywood sheets may be needed. If you do not know how to make a flying plane out of wood, then pay attention to this parameter. Moreover, this material is very light, flexible and durable.

When choosing plywood suitable for work, you should pay attention to its appearance. Over the entire area, its thickness should be the same. All kinds of defects, delaminations, chips or cracks should be absent. The material must be dry, so it is easier to handle.

How to make a plane out of wood

  1. To date, there are various sets of wood for modeling. You can assemble anything: ships, planes, helicopters , etc. In this case, all the details are selected precisely in size, and they are easily attached to each other.
  2. You can go the more difficult way and cut your favorite model from a wooden bar. The figure will be in the form of a single design. As a result, it will need to be varnished.
  3. If you do not know how to make a plane out of wood yourself from scratch, then you can build a structure of metal and plywood. A suitable drawing and the accompanying instructions can be found on the Internet or in special publications. If you have a desire to do aviation modeling, you can join the club of aircraft enthusiasts. It is there that everyone can be taught the wisdom of such art.

Useful Tips

Now that you have mastered the information on how to make an airplane out of wood in various ways, you can choose any of the options and modify it as you wish.

The design can be designed independently, but for this you will need to study publications on the design of modern aircraft. Various virtual design programs may be helpful.

If you are unable to find the dry material, plywood sheets can be prepared by yourself, having stood two or three weeks in a room with low humidity and a constant temperature.

If you have never done and don‚Äôt know exactly how to make a plane out of wood, then take up work only if you know what the future model will be, or when its aerodynamic properties are not important (making a children's toy).

In the manufacture of a decorative model or toy, you should get rid of sharp corners by removing the chamfer.



If you are working on a model designed for flying, then special attention should be paid to the exact positioning of the tail and wings. After all, aerodynamic properties depend on them.

Conclusion

Models of aircraft of various levels of complexity are made by masters around the world. In this case, the most complex controlled models can perform aerobatics.

Having picked up a suitable drawing, you can easily figure out how to make a plane out of wood. However, such work will be simple only in the manufacture of the simplest models. More labor-intensive tasks for beginning builders will be beyond their power, they should learn the simplest technologies and schemes, as well as understand the process in more detail.
