Knitted owl. Master class on creating an original toy

Great experience in creating such a toy is not needed. Enough basic knowledge. How to crochet an owl and not ruin the product? Take advantage of our workshop. The basis of the product is made up of two colorful circles sewn together. Simple columns, half- columns with a crochet and whole columns with one crochet, as well as a connecting loop will participate in the work. A knitted owl can be just an interior toy hanging on a cabinet handle or curtain rod.

Take yarn of different colors, but of the same thickness, and the hook corresponding to the threads. Work begins with 4 air loops connected in a ring. Then lift the next row, knitting 3 air loops, and make 13 double crochets into the ring. Connect a row in a circle. Since our knitted owl will be colored and bright, cut the thread and take a yarn of a different color. Next, attach the thread between the columns of the last row and knit a chain of 3 air loops. In the same place, tie the crochet column. Repeat 13 more times - make an increase in each space between the columns. At the end of the row you should get 28 loops. After fixing a new thread of a different color, knit three double crochet between the columns of the previous circle. After making 42 loops, connect the row and cut the thread.





Head Knotting and Assembly

A knitted owl should be dense, so similarly create the second side of the toy, using threads of different colors. The order of the shades can be changed at will. In the next row we will shape the head. Enter the yarn between the columns, fix the connecting loop and knit 2 more airy, here is a double crochet. Muzzle

Knit eyes from white thread. Connect 3 air loops and knit into the resulting ring 11 st.s. Pull the thread coming out of the middle, tightening the eye. Close a row with a connecting loop and cut a thread. Tie the second eye in the same way. Sew the circles to one side of the body. Embroider a beak between the eyes. Sew pupils from black beads or beads. Sew the two halves while filling the toy with a padding polyester. Owl ears can be shaggy. To do this, take the thread, fold it several times and thread it with a hook into the corners of the body above the eyes, pull the ends through the loop formed and tighten more tightly. Do the same on the other side. It remains to trim the ends, leaving small tails. From the master class, we learned how to crochet an owl.
