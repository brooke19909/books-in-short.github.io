How to make a rainbow on your face? It's complicated?

Catch a rainbow or draw?

To begin with, let's take a look at the elementary: you can get the rainbow effect on a photo in two ways - using reflectors and using photo editors. The first method may seem rather laborious and complicated, which is only partially true, while the second, in turn, will seem easy for those who are at least a little versed in Photoshop and photo editors. The first method is partially doomed to failure if you do not have enough patience or hands (which really can be an obstacle), because the first time you will not necessarily get exactly the effect that you expect. To implement the idea, you will need an assistant who will direct the rainbow to the place you need and take pictures immediately. The second method is less risky and less expensive in resources - both human and material, but for it you will need to try to figure out the photo editors yourself.





Water and mirror

How to make a rainbow on your face with water and a mirror? There are two ways to get the effect. For the first, we need a dark room with light walls. It is necessary to put a mirror in a bowl of water so that one half is in the water, and the other is outside. Then you need to take a flashlight and direct its rays along the mirror so that in the end you see a rainbow as a reflection on the surface of the face. For the second method, you need sunny weather, a light background, all the same bowl of water and a mirror. The mirror must again be placed in a bowl of water so that only one half is under water, and the second is outside. Place this entire structure near the window so that the sun's rays fall directly on it. Next, with the help of a white sheet that acts as a reflector, first catch a rainbow on it, and then, getting used to it, already on the face of the model.

Glass

How to make a rainbow on your face with a glass of water? First, we need an old Soviet faceted glass or its modern stylization (or, for example, a crystal vase from the same era with faces), which must be filled with water and placed in front of the window on a sunny day. Next, you need to take a light background and stand in front of a glass of water. After that, it is necessary to moisten the window with hot water and adjust the glass with water until you see a rainbow.





Prisms are used!

If you accidentally ended up with prisms that are usually used in physics classes, you are absolutely lucky! Such prisms can be in the form of a triangle, a sphere, a cube, and other shapes, and by shooting through them, you can achieve an interesting effect of broken or inverted photographs that appear to be in a haze. If, with the help of such a prism, you try to reflect a sunbeam onto your face, you will easily and without much effort get the rainbow effect.





Using cd

How to make a rainbow on your face using a CD? First, find the disc without scratches and scuffs on the mirror side, carefully wipe it so that it is not dusty. There are two ways to get a rainbow with it - in a dark and bright room. In order to get a rainbow in a gloomy room, you need to shine a flashlight on the disk and reflect it on your face. In order to get the rainbow effect in a bright room, it is enough to catch the sun's rays on the disk and use them to reflect them on the face. In order to get a rainbow in motion, if you shoot a video, this method is most suitable. After all, the disk can be gently twisted in different directions and watch how the colors move on your face.





We draw!

How to make a rainbow effect on your face without using improvised materials? For advanced users of social networks Instagram and Snapchat, this is no longer a secret. Indeed, in Stories mode, you can find a rainbow filter and take a photo or video. The same function is also in the PicsArt photo editor, where you can find the Rainbow Dreams filter, with which you can draw a rainbow in the photo without resorting to Photoshop. In general, there are many ways.

If you still have not decided how to make a rainbow on your face, Photoshop will be the best assistant in this. Knowing how to work with layers in this program, you can easily achieve the desired effect. To do this, create a new layer and use the Gradient tool, select the appropriate gradient, the location of the future rainbow, its brightness and saturation.
