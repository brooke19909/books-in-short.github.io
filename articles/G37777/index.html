Hotel Carelta Beach Resort & Spa 4 * (Kemer, Turkey): description, photo, reviews

If you are one of the tourists who prefer to spend their holidays in Turkey and who choose budget, but comfortable hotels working in the ol inclusive format, then Karelta Beach Resort & Spa (Kemer) may be a suitable option.



general information

Hotel Carelta Beach Resort & Spa 4 * is located in the resort village of Beldibi on the beach. Kemer is 17 kilometers away, and Antalya Airport is 45 kilometers away.

This hotel was built in 1999. It consists of one five-story building, accommodating 205 standard rooms. The apartments have a TV with cable channels, a telephone, a minibar (for a fee), a safe (for a fee), air conditioning, and a bathroom. Internet is available in the hotel lobby.





On the territory of Karelta Beach there are two restaurants, three bars (one of which offers snacks during the day), three swimming pools (outdoor, indoor and children's), water slides, sports grounds, a gym, a hammam, a kids club and much more.

Carelta Beach Resort & Spa 4 *: reviews of Russian tourists

Since hotel selection is a very important part of vacation planning, many travelers today pay much attention to this issue. So, they try not only to get acquainted with the official information and tips of travel agents, but also read as many reviews as possible from those who have already had occasion to stay at a particular hotel. Indeed, thanks to this, you can be aware of not only advantages, but also some disadvantages. Therefore, we bring to your attention the generalized comments of travelers from Russia who recently stayed at the Karelta Beach Hotel (Kemer, Turkey). Running a little ahead, we note that the vast majority of our compatriots did not regret their choice at all. On the contrary, they assure that this hotel corresponds to the declared category and cost. But we will understand everything in more detail.



Number of rooms

Judging by the comments of tourists provided to them at Carelta Beach Resort & Spa 4 * (Kemer) by the rooms, they were mostly satisfied. However, as the guests note, the hotel is already quite old, and reconstruction was not carried out in it. Therefore, you will not find modern design and new furniture and appliances in the apartments. However, in general, everything is quite high quality and is in working condition. If some kind of breakdown happens, then after contacting the reception the problem will be fixed as soon as possible. In their reviews, our compatriots also mention that Wi-Fi in the rooms practically does not catch. In most cases, you can use it only at the reception.



Check-in, cleaning

Some hotel guests have complained about the work of the maids. So, according to them, the cleaning was not always carried out efficiently. In addition, sometimes maids forgot to exchange dirty towels for clean ones, as well as replenish supplies, for example, toilet paper. However, after reporting this at the reception, the situation improved.

As for the settlement, then, as our compatriots note in their reviews, upon arrival at the hotel you will most likely have to wait two hours in the afternoon. The fact is that due to its rather low price this hotel is very popular, therefore, in the midst of the holiday season, it is almost always full, as they say, to the eyeballs. However, you do not have to wait for the keys to the room at the reception. So, you can go for breakfast or lunch, swim in the pool or go straight to the beach.

Nutrition

In general, on this item the opinions of guests of the Carelta Beach Resort & Spa 4 *, the photo of which can be seen in this article, agree. So, tourists believe that the food in this hotel is organized at a very decent level. Of course, one does not have to rely on any culinary delights and delicacies. But a varied menu of deliciously prepared dishes will be provided to you in the hotel restaurant every day. In addition, guests can always have a bite to eat pizza, hamburger or sandwich in the snack bar and treat themselves to delicious cakes that will be baked specially for you. Hotel guests were satisfied with the quality of the drinks offered in the bars.
