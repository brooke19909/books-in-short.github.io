Spa Salon

The name of the hotel clearly indicates its availability. The local spa is a real paradise for people who value personal care most. Here are some features worth noting first:

  • Ayurvedic treatments are practiced at the spa.
  • There is a beauty salon where you can do anything from a hardware pedicure to a haircut.
  • There are medical pavilions in which sessions aimed at improving health are held.
  • There are treatment rooms for couples and spa services for two.
  • There is a Russian bath, a jacuzzi, a sauna, hydrotherapy pools, a rain shower, open flower baths.
  • The following types of massage are offered: Swedish, Hawaiian (Lomi-Lomi), Balinese, Shiatsu, aromatherapy, Thai, as well as hot stones.
  • You can try original scrubs based on coffee beans, seaweed and coconut.

And this is not even half of what is available in the Welness-center of the Olhuveli Beach & SPA Resort 4 * hotel. However, everyone will be able to familiarize themselves with the full spa menu individually.





Restaurants

They can not be noted attention. The restaurants on the territory of Olhuveli Beach & SPA Resort 4 * five:

1. Sunset Restaurant. The main institution. Open daily for breakfast and dinner. The main specialization is local and international cuisine. The signature dish is juicy king prawns with spices in wine sauce.

2. Lagoon Restaurant. Located on the beach, so the dress code can not be respected. An ideal place for lunch or dinner. The institution is open from 12:00 to 22:30. Specialization - Mediterranean cuisine.

3. The Four Spices. The restaurant is located on the pier, directly above the water. It serves breakfast (7: 30-10: 00) and dinner (19: 00-21: 00), serving a buffet. It is also possible to order from the menu. The restaurant serves Japanese, Indian, Thai and Maldivian dishes.

4. Island Pizza. This restaurant, the highlight of which is a huge selection of pizzas, is located next to the pool. But he does not specialize in Italian cuisine. On the contrary, here you can try Mediterranean, Western, Asian and sea delicacies. Or even order fast food - something like a sandwich or a hamburger. All in all, the perfect restaurant for a bite to eat. Open from 11:30 to 18:00.

5. The Plankton Grill. Fish restaurant with open kitchen. Seafood lovers will appreciate it, because there is everything here - from sea bass and crabs, ending with lobsters and lobsters. Open from 19:00 to 22:00.

Well, the food at Olhuveli Beach & SPA Resort 4 * is at the highest level - this conclusion can only be drawn from the description. By the way, the last restaurant of the listed ones has an evening dress code, and a preliminary reservation is also required.





Bars

There are fewer of them in Olhuveli Beach & SPA Resort 4 * than restaurants, but they also deserve attention. So, these places:

  • Sunrise Bar. A bar located in a busy place - near the main pool. A huge assortment of drinks is offered.
  • Dhoni Bar. This establishment is located right on the beach. Guests can sit on comfortable sun loungers and enjoy a cocktail, enjoy the Indian Ocean and colorful sunsets. In addition to drinks, snacks are also served here, as well as Western and Eastern dishes. It is open from 12:00 to 22:30.
  • Lagoon Bar. A place with the most relaxing atmosphere. Instead of tables - wide comfortable swing on the pier. The bar, by the way, works all night and until the morning.

Leisure

The main entertainment at Olhuveli Beach & SPA Resort 4 * is diving. Everyone knows that in the waters that surround the Maldives archipelago, thousands of species of amazing creatures live. Is this not a good reason to go diving while on vacation?

It will turn out to see a giant moray eel, stone fish, octopuses, lionfish, reef sharks, turtles, as well as many other amazing inhabitants of the underwater world. The hotel has a professional diving center, so everyone has the opportunity to try diving with equipment in the Indian Ocean, under the strict guidance of experienced instructors.

Interested in something more active? Then you can do water sports! Canoe or catamaran, water ski, try air / water surfing or go on a boat with a guide to uninhabited islets. In general, there is something to do here - vivid impressions are provided for everyone.
