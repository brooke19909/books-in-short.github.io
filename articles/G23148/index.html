Mitino Hotel in Moscow: reviews, ratings, photos

If you need a comfortable accommodation option in Moscow, then pay attention to the Mitino Hotel. Here you will be provided with all the necessary services that will ensure you a good rest.

Brief description of the hotel

Hotel "Mitino" offers you not only comfortable accommodation, but also many related services for a good rest. So, here you have your own beauty salon, where you can prepare for a responsible event. The hotel also has a sauna and spa. You will also be provided with such a necessary service as free wireless internet.
As for the rooms, the Mitino Hotel offers you comfortable conditions for living and recreation. All rooms are equipped with a full range of amenities that a modern tourist may need. It is also worth paying attention to a cozy home interior.

Special attention deserves the restaurant, which has a hotel "Mitino". Due to the fact that it works around the clock, you can satisfy your hunger at any time. Also, be sure to visit the bar, which offers a huge assortment of drinks and gourmet snacks.





Hotel "Mitino": address

The hotel is located at: Pyatnitskoye Shosse, 18. This is the northwestern district of Moscow. Next to a good traffic intersection is the hotel "Mitino". Hotel staff will tell you how to get there if you contact them upon arrival in the city. You can walk about 10 minutes from the Volokomskaya metro station. If you are traveling by car, then you need to move along the MKAD to turn onto Mitino. Sheremetyevo International Airport and a huge exhibition center are located very close.

Hotel Rooms

The Mitino Hotel in Moscow offers travelers to stay in one of the following rooms:

  • Luxury apartments offer comfortable accommodation in two spacious rooms. The bedroom is equipped with a huge bed, a spacious wardrobe, and a cosmetic table. The living room has a folding soft sofa, which acts as an extra bed.
  • Junior Suite is a large one-room suite. It combines a bedroom and a relaxation area with soft armchairs and a coffee table. Luxurious decoration in a laconic classic style deserves special attention.
  • Superior double room includes a large bed in a compact room. There is also a dressing table, which can be used as a workplace.
  • The standard room is equipped with two single beds and individual bedside tables. there is a set of all necessary amenities for a comfortable stay.




The amenities provided in the apartment

The Mitino Hotel offers its guests comfortable apartments, where the following list of necessary amenities is located:

  • TV connected to a large package of satellite channels;
  • in order for you to watch video from external media, a player is provided;
  • a landline phone will allow you not only to contact the administration, but also call to another city or country;
  • all rooms have wireless Internet access, the use of which is included in the price;
  • thanks to the presence of an individual air conditioner, your apartment will always have a comfortable air temperature;
  • the bathroom has an electric hairdryer so you can quickly do volumetric styling or simply quickly dry your hair.




Hotel facilities

The following infrastructure has been developed for guests of this institution:

  • vending machines for drinks, sweets and snacks;
  • there is a terminal in the lobby that is used to pay for mobile communications and other services;
  • for business events, a conference room with modern office equipment is provided;
  • The price includes breakfast in the buffet format (you can also order food in the room);
  • in the courtyard of the hotel there is a private car parking with 24-hour security and video surveillance;
  • on each floor there are coolers with both cold and hot water;
  • if necessary, you can order a transfer to the railway station or international airport;
  • at the reception there is a paid safe (guests can rent a cell for the required period);
  • You will not need to look for a hairdresser for a long time, because it works directly in the hotel;
  • hotel staff will help you get tickets for cultural events that take place in the capital;
  • the tourist office organizes interesting study tours for you;
  • You can do your figure in the gym equipped with the most modern equipment;
  • in the lobby bar you can enjoy your favorite drinks in a relaxed atmosphere, as well as listen to music and satisfy your hunger.




Hotel "Mitino": rating

Based on the reviews of guests, the following rating of hotel services can be determined (on a ten-point scale):

  • The hotel infrastructure is rated quite high (7 points) due to the presence of a 24-hour restaurant, as well as its own hairdresser. The rating is reduced due to an inconvenient access to the parking zone.
  • The quality of service is rated at 7.5 points. In general, employees work harmoniously and professionally, but the quality of cleaning is not the best.
  • The hotel rooms deserve a rating of 8. The only significant drawback is the poor soundproofing.
  • Children's infrastructure is rated at 4, since it is practically absent. There are no children's animators, as well as a special menu.
  • For location, you can put a rating of 6. Remoteness from the central part of the capital is offset by good transport interchange.




Positive reviews

Quite a lot of tourists choose for their accommodation such an option as the Mitino Hotel (Moscow). Reviews about this place contain the following positive comments:

  • For breakfast, you will find an excellent buffet with a huge selection of dishes;
  • the hotel has a good restaurant where you can eat for little money;
  • convenient location relative to the metro station;
  • the staff quickly responds to all requests and wishes of guests;
  • a huge market is located nearby (although some guests consider this a drawback).




Negative reviews

Accommodation in this hotel is characterized by a number of negative aspects, the most significant of which are:

  • the hotel practically does not pay attention to cleaning - it is dirty both in the corridors and in the rooms;
  • it is rather problematic for foreign citizens to connect to wireless Internet (the activation code comes only to the numbers of Russian operators);
  • TVs are not working in some rooms, but the cost of living is not reduced;
  • the beds are old and squeezed, and therefore have long been replaced;
  • weak pressure of water in the shower;
  • Shampoo of very low quality is provided in the bathroom (bring it with you or buy at the nearest store);
  • due to poor ventilation in the corridors there is a very unpleasant smell;
  • there are holes on the front door that are simply sealed with electrical tape;
  • the music is too loud in the restaurant, and the repertoire leaves much to be desired;
  • too high cost of living for rooms with such a worn interior.




General impression

Mitino is a typical hotel on the outskirts of Moscow, which is not distinguished by cleanliness and a high level of comfort. At the same time, accommodation prices are quite high (most likely, this is due to the proximity to the metro station and good transport interchange). But the quality of repair and cleaning in the rooms leaves much to be desired. A positive moment of living in this institution is the opportunity to have a tasty and relatively inexpensive breakfast. Also a good impression makes staff. The employees are very polite to the guests and quickly enough respond to all requests and comments.
