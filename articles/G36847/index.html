"Altan" - recreation center in the Altai Mountains

The picturesque, amazing beauty of the nature of our country annually attracts thousands of tourists to rest. Modern, equipped with everything necessary for an exciting pastime complexes await guests all year round. We will make a short review of the Altan base of the same name, each of which provides all the conditions for an unforgettable outdoor recreation.





"Altan" is a recreation center. Mountain Altai

Gorno-Altai recreation center "Altan" is located 450 km from the city of Novosibirsk. It is located on the left bank of the Katun River, in a pine forest, only 700 meters from the most beautiful lake Aya. People who want to take a break from the noisy city and merge with nature strive here. The calm atmosphere is closely combined here with modern tourist infrastructure. Convenient living conditions, various entertainments, including organized excursions - all this will be provided by Altan (recreation center).

Infrastructure base "Altan"

You can stay here comfortably in any room that you like. You can choose from a standard category room (rooms are located in two-story houses made of timber) or a summer version - economy rooms.

"Altan" - recreation center on Baikal





Not far from Khuzhir-Nuga Bay on the Small Sea, which is part of Baikal, there is a cozy base called "Altan". This place is perfect for both family vacations and friendly company. The staff of the base find an individual approach to each guest. Comfortable rooms, convenient infrastructure, a unique location, the proximity of sacred zones make the rest in "Altan" attractive at any time of the year. "Altan" (recreation center) is only 350 meters from the coast.

In recent years, the tourist complex has significantly increased the level of its service. More recently, on the Small Sea there were only camp sites with wooden houses, outdoor amenities, a common dining room. Today the Altan boarding house meets the highest requirements.

Infrastructure base "Altan"

At the same time, the complex can accommodate 120 people. Can accommodate:

  • in separate houses with a veranda (2, 3, 4 persons), but shower rooms with cold and hot water are located on the territory;
  • in two-story cottages (2-bed rooms), each room has a balcony, the cottage itself has a common living room, satellite TV, equipped kitchen, shower, bathroom;
  • in a common building (2-bed rooms), where each room has all the amenities, shower, bathroom, satellite TV.

In winter, houses, buildings, cottages are heated, living in them is very comfortable. All rooms offer wonderful views of Lake Baikal.

The houses of the "economy" class are in great demand, and the point is not only in saving money. Many just dream to relax in an environment close to nature, to forget about urban conditions. "Altan" (recreation center) does not leave anyone indifferent. Tourists leave the warmest reviews about their stay here and dream of returning here again.

At the guests' disposal are central power supply, a swimming pool, a beach (just 400 meters away), a bathhouse (steam room, fireplace room), billiards, shower rooms, sports grounds, a shop, a guarded parking lot, gazebos for relaxing, a bonfire, a barbecue gazebo.





The dining room has a spacious summer terrace with a wonderful view of Lake Baikal. Kitchen specialties are dishes made from fresh fish. For each client an individual approach. For children there is a special menu. There is a cozy bar.

Leisure and entertainment offered on the basis of "Altan"

All year round "Altan" (recreation center) offers its tourists a wide selection of entertainment programs. The area where the complex is located is easily accessible by car. Excursions to Ogoy and Olkhon, to sacred places, to the Sarma gorge, to healing springs, caves are very popular in these places. Favorite place to relax is the Small Sea. For fishermen, it's just a paradise. It organizes both summer and winter fishing. Base "Altan" provides fishing equipment for hire. Special fishing tours are organized. An experienced instructor will help beginners. It offers vacationers - boat trips.

Altan Hada in Tutai Bay

The recreation center ‚ÄúAltan Hada‚Äù is no less attractive for tourists. It is located in the Tutai Bay of the Small Sea, near the village of MRS. Accommodation, meals, services on the basis of "Altan Hada"

You can stay at the base in cozy wooden two-story houses for 10 people. You will be offered a common lounge on the floor, 2-3-bed rooms. Facilities are located on the street (toilet, shower). Complex meals are organized, the Buryat yurt is used as a dining room, this creates an atmosphere of unusual comfort and coziness. The menu is varied, natural products are used for cooking. Fresh milk is even offered . Additional paid services include access to the steam room. You can also order exciting excursions by car, by boat across the expanses of Lake Baikal.





Fishing on the Small Sea

Many are eager to get to these places to go on a real fishing trip, she is waiting for lovers in January. Transparent ice, good weather, excellent bite give fishermen great pleasure. Due to the shallow water, the Tutai Bay warms up very quickly, and in March active fishing begins. Organized tours are collected by dozens of lovers. An experienced instructor will tell you about all the subtleties of fishing. By evening, you will return to the base with a good catch, you can cook a fragrant Baikal fish soup or a national sagudai dish.
