Sanatorium Anapa "Blue Wave": a good rest and good treatment

Sanatorium Anapa “Blue Wave” is a place where whole families come to relax and improve their health. After all, the city of Anapa is considered mainly a family resort, which is famous for the quality of service. Thanks to the microclimate and favorable geographical location, this city becomes the ideal place for spending holidays.





History

If we talk about a place like the sanatorium of Anapa “Blue Wave”, then we can not help but tell about the history of its occurrence. It was founded in 1984. The territory itself, on which the pension is located, has an area of ​​3.2 hectares. Lawns with flowers, relict trees, everything around is green, blooms and smells - the picturesque views are pleasing to the eye. By the way, one can not fail to note the advantageous location of the boarding house - in the city itself. 310 people can easily accommodate in the sanatorium - that is how many places he has. Nobody will be deprived of housing, on the territory there are a medical-diagnostic building and residential complexes.





Features of the boarding house

What attracts people who come to the Anapa resort “Blue Wave”, especially those who dream of enjoying the warmth of the sun and sea baths? As you could already guess, of course, the sea. The boarding house has its own beach, which is an undoubted priority, which is taken into account by all potential tourists of the Krasnodar Territory. Just one hundred meters is what separates the Blue Wave children's sanatorium from the sea. Anapa is a sunny city that is famous for its hospitality, so tourists are offered absolutely any rental service.





Boarding house services

The sanatorium in Anapa “Blue Wave” is famous for its high quality service. This complex has everything you need for a good rest - for every taste and color. If a person likes to read, then a library is provided for his attention. After all, it should be noted that educational work is something that is closely related to ongoing medical and preventive measures, as well as compliance with the regime established in the sanatorium. And, of course, lovers of an active lifestyle are given the opportunity, even on vacation, to keep fit. A basketball and volleyball court, a children's sauna and a tennis court are on site. Cinema connoisseurs are invited to the cinema hall, which constantly hosts a variety of cultural and public events.

Accommodations

In order for the treatment to benefit, comfortable living conditions are needed, and especially for children. As it was said, families with children come to the Blue Wave boarding house, and for children comfort is especially important. In the sanatorium, in the rooms, there is everything necessary for a good life. There are five- and six-bed rooms with shared facilities and rooms for living with parents. For them, even a separate building was erected, where double and single rooms are provided. Nutrition is also an extremely important aspect that affects proper rest and fruitful treatment. The administration of the boarding house with full responsibility approaches this issue. In the dining room, which is located on the territory of the sanatorium, they provide balanced dietary meals five times a day. This is enough to support strength and energy throughout the day.





Treatment

Not only for the sake of entertainment and outdoor activities people come to this guesthouse in Anapa. The Blue Wave Sanatorium, which all grateful guests leave reviews, is a specialized medical institution that treats children who have developed bronchopulmonary pathology, respiratory diseases that are non-tuberculous in nature. The sanatorium complex has everything you need to conduct highly professional, full-fledged treatment. Cabinets of aerophytotherapy, physiotherapy, thermal therapy, inhalation. And, of course, massage and physiotherapy exercises are provided. But that is not all. Functional diagnostics of acupuncture, a biochemical laboratory - in the complex, they responsibly approach the treatment of city guests. No wonder the sanatorium Anapa “Blue Wave” is famous throughout Russia and abroad. The complex has a seven-bath bathroom, where the guests of the boarding house take useful preventive coniferous-sea, iodine-bromine and, of course, sea baths. Thus, we can conclude that the Anapa sanatorium “Blue Wave” is a hospitable place where families come to enjoy the freshness of the Krasnodar Territory and improve their health. Combining business with pleasure, tourists leave home refreshed and take with them a lot of pleasant experiences.

Welcome to the sanatorium! Only pleasant moments await you!
