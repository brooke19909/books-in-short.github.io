Serena Residence 3 * (Bulgaria, Sozopol): hotel description, services, reviews

Serena Residence 3 * (Bulgaria, Sozopol) is a beautiful hotel, which is widely known for its advantageous location and quality service.





Briefly about the Serena Residence Hotel

The hotel was built in 2009. The last major overhaul was made just a year ago - in 2015. The Serena Residence 3 * building has a modern appearance, but at the same time blends harmoniously into the surrounding nature. And the orange color of the facade emphasizes the lush forest surrounding the hotel. The hotel territory is 3920 square meters.

Serena Residence 3 * hotel is suitable for both relaxing with friends and family vacations. The rear has six floors, so for the convenience of living there is an elevator.





Hotel Rooms

Serena Residence Hotel offers 75 Apartments and 17 Studios. Each hotel room is made in calm beige shades and equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay and a good night's sleep. The furniture set consists of wooden single and double beds with orthopedic mattresses and hypoallergenic pillows, bedside tables, a dining table and chairs, a dressing table or a chest of drawers, a wardrobe. There is also a flat-screen TV with cable and satellite channels, a small refrigerator, a telephone for communication with the administrator, city and long-distance calls.

The optimum temperature is maintained by the heating system and air conditioning. The bathroom includes a shower and hairdryer. Each room has access to a terrace with a magnificent view of the surrounding nature.





Cost of living at the Serena Residence Hotel

Prices as of 2016 for accommodation at the Serena Residents Hotel are as follows:

  • in the low season (first half of June and from the second week of September) you will have to pay from 54 leva (approximately $ 20);
  • in the middle season (second half of June and the end of August ‚Äì first half of September) - the room charge starts from 83 leva (about $ 30);
  • at the height of the season (July and almost the whole of August) accommodation will cost from 99 leva (approximately $ 35).

Children under two years stay free of charge, as does a child under 12 years old, occupying only one extra bed. For the placement of babies from two to twelve in a second extra bed, you will have to pay 50% of the total cost of one adult.

For early bookings, guests receive a discount on accommodation in the amount of ten percent of the room rate.

Catering at Serena Residence

On the territory of the Serena Residence 3 * hotel there is a restaurant, a bar and a lobby bar. There is also a great panoramic barbecue restaurant.

The restaurant has a wide selection of dishes from European and local cuisine. Breakfast is served from 7:30 to 10 am, lunch - from 12:30 to 14:30, dinners - from 18:30 to 21 pm. Snacks are also provided - from 16: to 16:30. Breakfast usually serves a variety of drinks (coffee, tea, juice, milk), yogurt, cheese, cold cuts and cold meats, granola and various cereals, various jams, honey, sausages and sausages, eggs, seasonal fruits and vegetables. The following dishes are offered for lunch and dinner: cold appetizers and soups, hot soups, meat and fish dishes, pasta, vegetables, desserts, seasonal fruits. Pizza, sweets and crackers, fruits, ice cream, sandwiches. Meals are included in the price - full board. When ordering all inclusive, the price will include drinks: wines, beer and others.

For self-catering and light snacks, each room is equipped with a kitchen corner with the necessary utensils. The hotel has several grocery stores and vending machines with various snacks (chocolate bars, drinks, crackers, and so on).





Organization of leisure time on the territory of the hotel and beyond

On the territory of the hotel "Serena Residence" provides a fairly large number of entertainment. For lovers of silence there are nooks where you can sit and enjoy the surrounding nature. And if you want to warm up, the hotel has a gym with fitness equipment, a tennis table, darts, billiards and so on.

The hotel is particularly in demand in the outdoor pool. There is always clean water and a sufficient number of sun loungers. In case of emergency, a lifeguard is on duty nearby. It is noteworthy that while swimming in the pool, you can observe the surrounding nature and enjoy not only fresh air, but also silence.

In addition, it is impossible not to mention that a couple of steps from the hotel is the main attraction of the area - the most beautiful Bulgarian Black Sea coast Kavazite. The road from the hotel to the beach is asphalted. The coast itself has a length of about one kilometer and a width of up to 30 meters. The beach is sandy, the entrance to the sea is convenient.

Other activities include several excursions around the surrounding towns. You can order them from the hotel administrator. There is no additional entertainment, because the hotel is located in a secluded place. In order to go shopping, visit various souvenir shops, restaurants and bars, as well as museums, you will have to go to Sozopol. Fortunately, it is only three kilometers from the hotel.

Negative comments of guests

In addition to the good points, which are many, the Serena Residence Hotel has several negative ones. Here is an example of what especially hooked the hotel guests:

  • The hotel rests quite a lot of Russian-speaking citizens. At the same time, only the administrator and one maid can speak Russian from the staff.
  • The menu in the restaurant is quite large, but does not differ in variety. Therefore, with a long stay, it quickly bothers.
  • There is no entertainment program. From entertainment only a gym, restaurant and walks in the surrounding nature. If you find a company, you can play billiards, tennis, chess or darts. If you want to visit various places, you will have to go to Sozopol.
  • Room cleaning is not always daily and of high quality. Quite often, maids need to be specially called.
  • The Wi-Fi signal catches well only near the pool or on the restaurant's outdoor terrace. In rooms, the signal is either weak or disappears altogether.

As for the moments that are not entirely in the power of the hotel staff, the travelers did not like the situation on the beach. There is a lot of garbage there (for example, cigarette butts, plastic bottles, and so on).

It is also worth noting that Serena Residence is not suitable for people who love the bustle of the city and want to spend their vacation somewhere where there are many places for entertainment. After all, the hotel stands apart, and around it is only nature.

Hotel location

The exact address of the Serena Residence 3 * hotel: Bulgaria, Sozopol, Mapi area. The hotel stands three kilometers from the south of the city. The main guideline is the nearby Kawashi Camping.

Distance from the sea - about five to ten minutes. To the city of Sozopol, a taxi ride will take about ten minutes. You can also take a steam train. They walk every half hour until nine in the evening. There is no other public transport nearby, so only a taxi remains.
