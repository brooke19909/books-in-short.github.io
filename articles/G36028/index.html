General from infantry - who is it?

To understand what a general is from infantry, the dignity of this rank, it is necessary to explain what infantry itself is. This word, coming from a foreign language, means infantry or infantry army. The Russian army was made up of artillery, infantry and cavalry. Until 1796, the rank of general from fortification and the general of artillery was in the army.

High military rank





General from infantry - this phrase meant in pre-revolutionary Russia the highest rank in the infantry. In the Table of Ranks, he ranks between Field Marshal and Lieutenant General. The rite was introduced in 1699 and lasted the entire remaining imperial period. From 1763 to 1796 it was canceled, then reintroduced by Emperor Paul I. This military rank, which corresponds to the civilian "Privy Councilor", refers to the ranks, canceled by history. Kornilov L.G. , Yudenich N.N., Wrangel A.E. - the last White Guard officers, who wore the title "General of Infantry."
