Hotel "Prichal" (Adler, Russia): address, description, reviews

Want to relax on the Black Sea coast and are thinking about which hotel to stay in? A good option for a vacation is the Hotel Prichal (Adler). Why choose this place, read below.

Location





Hotel "Prichal" is located in the heart of the resort town of Adler and right on the promenade, ten meters from the sea. The train and bus stations are five kilometers away, and the international airport is a fifteen-minute drive away.

Nearby is the Mandarin Park, Bestuzhev Square, numerous restaurants, shops and cafes on the waterfront, nightclubs, an amusement park, an aqua park, a dolphinarium, Russia's largest oceanarium, shopping centers and much more. Such a convenient location determines the great demand among tourists for accommodation in this hotel.

Beach

Hotel "Prichal" (Adler) is located directly on the promenade, so that his guests, barely leaving the hotel, find themselves on a well-maintained urban sand and pebble beach. For a fee, you can rent umbrellas and sunbeds, for the convenience of vacationers there are locker rooms, toilets and showers, the beach is equipped with a medical center and a rescue department.





In addition, for outdoor enthusiasts, there is a rental of catamarans, ATVs, scooters, bananas, pedal boats and other water attractions. Therefore, every traveler will find something to their liking here and will not be able to get bored.

Number of rooms of the hotel - choose according to taste and wallet

Hotel "Prichal" (Adler) offers its guests only twenty-one rooms - such a small size of the hotel creates a special homely and cozy atmosphere that will allow you to truly relax on vacation and relax. The hotel can accommodate in double rooms of the following categories: one-room "Standard", "Breeze" and "DeLux Romantic" (for newlyweds), two-room and three-room "Suite". The size of the rooms is from twenty-five to forty square meters. m





Each guest room has all the amenities: a bed, a table, a sofa, a bedside table, a mirror, air conditioning, a satellite TV, a minibar, a safe, a telephone, a wardrobe, a bathroom with a bath or shower, a hairdryer, hygiene and cosmetics . Bathrobes and slippers are provided. The rooms of the "Standard" category can be with a view of the sea or the surroundings, the rest of the varieties will please only the sea landscape. Smoking is not allowed in all rooms.





The interior of the rooms is made in original color and style solutions. The furniture is carefully and tastefully selected, thanks to which the furnishings will satisfy even the most demanding guest. Rooms are cleaned every day; towels and linen are changed every few days.

For children

Hotel "Prichal" (Adler) also invites young guests - there are necessary conditions for accommodating children. They are accepted from any age, and accommodation with kids up to three years is free. A crib is also provided for babies, and a special children's menu is available at the cafe. In the evenings, animation programs are provided for younger guests.

If you want to organize coffee breaks or dinners, you need to coordinate these issues with the administration in advance. Terms of accommodation

Check-in is from 13:00, check-out must be no later than 12:00. Pets are not allowed.

Please note that Adler is a resort town where you can relax at any time, which is why the hotel is open all year.

An extra bed can accommodate only one person. If one person is going to stay in a double room, the cost of living is reduced by the price of a second breakfast.
