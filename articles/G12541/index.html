LCD "Aviator" (Khimki) - an ideal solution for moving

LCD "Aviator" (Khimki) is presented in the form of four multi-story buildings. Its location near the Moscow Ring Road allows you to enjoy all the benefits of the capital.





Location

Only six kilometers separates the Aviator LCD (Khimki) from the Moscow Ring Road and, accordingly, from the capital. In order to get into it, it is necessary to move along the Leningradskoye and Novoshodnenskoye highways. The address of the complex: Planernaya microdistrict, houses 3, 5, 6, 7. This is the city of Khimki.





Description

For the construction of a residential complex allocated an area of ​​eleven hectares. All this is necessary for the construction of four residential buildings and shopping centers. Objects are being built according to the latest technology in the form of monolithic buildings lined with bricks. Thus, it turns out that the wall frames are built in one piece, and the outer walls are made of two layers of aerated concrete blocks. Outside, facing brick gives a beautiful view of buildings. All construction technologies are aimed at reducing noise penetration, i.e. soundproofing. The exterior design will be completed by painting the facades in warm colors.

LCD "Aviator" (Khimki) will be spread over one hundred and fifty-five thousand square meters. These are four residential buildings and two shopping centers. All planned buildings will be completed in one storey - seventeen floors. And in all, the project provides for the placement of underground parking lots for the total number of parking spaces - four hundred seventy-two, which cannot but please future owners-motorists.





Three buildings of the complex will consist of four hundred and forty-eight apartments in each. And one more - out of two hundred thirty-nine.

Flats

Various offers LCD "Aviator" (Khimki) planning solutions. Each family - whether it is one person or with three children - will find housing in the complex according to its needs and budget. There are also one-room apartments, small in area (from thirty-six square meters), and three-room apartments - large and spacious, reaching ninety-two meters.

Modern apartment designers take into account the needs of residents in the need to put their things somewhere. Therefore, dressing rooms and pantries are immediately provided. And in apartments that have more than one room, two bathrooms are also provided, since by default it is calculated that these apartments will be purchased by families with a large number of people. Therefore, in order not to provoke inconvenience and conflict, the designers plan ahead in such apartments a comfortable number of bathrooms.





Apartment renovation

The developer offers for sale an apartment with a rough finish. She suggests:

  • plastering walls;
  • alignment of them and ceilings;
  • floors are made with rough screed;
  • entrance doors and windows are installed;
  • glazed loggias;
  • electrical wiring divorced for apartments;
  • sewerage risers and water pipes were connected, but stoves and sinks are not connected;
  • heating radiators installed.

In many cases, future owners choose to purchase apartments in new buildings with a rough finish. This option, firstly, is significantly lower in cost, since it assumes that the owner will carry out all repairs or hire a construction contract for these purposes. The latter can be provided by the developer himself in order to save time and money of the owner of the apartment.

Also, for the convenience of residents, all buildings included in the Aviator residential complex (Khimki) were built with their own boiler houses. This allows people to not know the worries and problems with planned outages of hot water. And individual temperature regulators available in each apartment provide comfortable heat, suitable for everyone according to his desire.

Infrastructure

The microdistrict, which the developer chose to build the Aviator LCD (Khimki), has only positive reviews. It is part of the city line of Khimki. A great advantage for residents is the proximity of the Planernaya electric train station. Literally four hundred meters from the complex, you can catch an electric train to Moscow. This, in turn, allows you to quickly get to work, bypassing multi-kilometer traffic jams on the Novoshodnenskoye highway. Indeed, there are no traffic jams on railway tracks.

Given the location of the complex, we can conclude that it was built in an area with a healthy environmental situation. Directly behind the complex, a forest begins with the Skhodnaya River flowing in it . This allows residents of the complex to enjoy clean air, in contrast to the gas-polluted metropolis.





Infrastructure is also developed. Since the complex is located in the city of Khimki, it uses all its infrastructure. LCD Aviator (Khimki), whose photo is posted in the article, has access to all the benefits of civilization, in particular, a school and a kindergarten are located nearby. There is also a large shopping center with such anchor networkers as Auchan, Ikea, OBI. For those who are sick, a medical center and a clinic are provided.

Construction problems

The buildings of the complex are being built in the order according to which Aviator LCD (Khimki) was designed. The contacts of its original developer, AleutStroy, which suspended construction in 2015, are on the official website. And this residential complex is not the only unfinished developer. To date, the administration of Khimki has not been able to find a new investor-developer. Therefore, the complex remains unfinished, which violates the rights of equity holders. Until now, not all buildings have been erected in the Aviator residential complex (Khimki): photos show this. And when it will be fully completed - it is not known, since there is no documentary evidence of the investor’s consent to continue the work.
