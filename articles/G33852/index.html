"Bathhouse Manor on Elk Island": description, reviews

It is known that baths are not just a place intended for washing the body. Many believe that the soul truly rests in the steam room, the soul is cleansed and cured. Having been in a Russian bath, a person not only gets rid of the accumulated physical fatigue, but is also freed from the blues and sadness. To satisfy the most demanding client, giving him the opportunity to relax, improve his health and enjoy the silence of the surrounding forest, the service complex “Bathhouse Manor on Elk Island” is called in, where you can not only take a steam bath, but also get acquainted with a unique culture of relaxation based on ancient bathing traditions.

Briefly about the history of the company

Establishment date of the Bannaya Manor company is 2000. Initially, the organizers planned to create a modern base in the capital's metropolis for recreation and health promotion based on the best traditions of the world bathing culture, and especially Russian. Today, a network of brand complexes covers a number of cultural and recreational centers on the streets of Lodochnaya, Vishneva, Panfilov, Voikovskaya, on the October Field (north-west of Moscow); estates on Elk Island (northeast) and on the street. Ugreshskaya (south). In addition, the company owns a manor in Nizhny Novgorod - the only one outside the capital.

All estates have real log cabins with Russian “black” and “white” baths, as well as Finnish saunas, Turkish baths, Japanese ofuros and Roman baths.

“Bathhouse Manor on Elk Island”: Acquaintance

This elite complex uniquely combines comfort, convenient location and true Russian style. In the reviews, “Bannaya Manor on Elk Island” is called a truly paradise, where you can plunge into the times of ancient Russia, with its original traditions and customs preserved in their original form. According to the authors of the reviews, upon arriving here, the visitor finds himself in an old village, where twelve chopped 2-story towers are located.

Terema

Each of the log houses of the Bannaya Manor on Elk Island (8 Yauzskaya Alley St.) is distinguished by its style and interior decoration. The names of the towers are very eloquent. Few would refuse to find themselves in the stylized atmosphere of “Tsarsky”, “Starorussky”, “Hussarsky”, “Cozy” towers, as well as log cabins “Duck Hunt”, “Gzhel”, “Birch Grove”, “Khokhloma”, “Mill”, “The meeting place cannot be changed”, “Cottage” and “Tale”! All the towers in the “Bathhouse Manor on Elk Island” were built from noble wood species, due to which, as experts assure, even the walls in them heal the health of visitors.

Each house has a spacious refectory with traditional Russian stoves. Comfortable furniture and large oak tables are perfectly combined with modern appliances (karaoke, video and audio equipment). The interior decoration repeats the historical views and the traditional filling of the baths with the observance of national characteristics. The design of each of the towers corresponds to the name of the log house. According to reviews, staying in them does not leave visitors indifferent. So, hunters with pleasure have a rest in "Duck Hunt", romantics prefer to spend leisure time in the "Hussar" log house. Fans of Russian culture are pleased to visit the Khokhloma or the Gzhel, which is so unlike it.

Helpful information. Guest Impressions

  1. The rules of the complex prohibit drinks and food to bring with you.

  2. There are currently no open vacancies at Bannaya Manor on Elk Island.

“Bannuya Manor on Elk Island”, which has been operating for more than 15 years, is well known in the capital. Many visitors call her services truly unique. According to reviews, the complex is a whole village in the forest. Rest of the guests here is not disturbed by anything. All log cabins have a courtyard, closed from prying eyes, surrounded by a strong high fence, where virtually no extraneous sounds penetrate. Many guests here like spacious pools, lined with beautiful tiles, with clear blue water. In the interior, visitors note the presence of wooden tables and benches, in the territory - barrels of water (cold), as well as a beautiful barbecue area.

In good weather, many people like to organize parties in the open air, and in bad weather they move to a house where, according to the authors of the reviews, everything is also arranged in the best way and thought out to the last detail. The guest here can comfortably sit on a soft leather sofa and look at the fire, stirring the coals in the fireplace and breathing in the smells of dried herbs and natural wood. You can take a steam bath in a Russian steam room with a birch or juniper broom. Either play billiards or drink tea from a samovar - with sweets and jam. And then the guest can climb to the 2nd floor and relax in silence, spread out on a wide soft bed. At the same time, many recall with pleasantness and sadness childhood, time spent in the village and regret the traditional life and old ways that were lost in the modern bustle. Visitors call the bath complex a place in which harmony settles in the soul, the body gains vivacity and health, the mind is completely disconnected from problems, and a person can safely sail in the waves of truly unearthly pleasures.
