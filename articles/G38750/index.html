Blackberry phones BlackBerry. Reviews speak for themselves

BlackBerry in translation from English means "blackberry". Quite an unusual name for the world famous company that produces phones. What does the favorite berry have to do with such a corporation? It is difficult to answer this question. Although you should not forget that, to some extent, fruits and berries have become a kind of prototype for communications made in the United States. There is a striking example. This, of course, is about Apple and their "apple" name and brand. Despite the fact that the apple is not related to high technology, Apple has managed to become a global giant. Let's see what is so interesting and worthwhile in the "blackberry" products.





BlackBerry is the brainchild of Canadian giant Research In Motion, originally specialized in wireless data devices. The first mobile phone they released in 2000 with the model name 957 Proton. In just a couple of years, the brand name became authoritative among business people. Reviews of BlackBerry began to spread at the speed of light, which, in fact, led them to such a level of recognition and sales.

The main reason for this success was the fact that all devices of this brand have the function of data encryption. That is, it is impossible to listen to and intercept the mail arriving at them, as well as other data. About the quality of work BlackBerry reviews have always received the best. This is not surprising, since initially all of the company's products have established themselves not as advertising slogans, but as serviceability and reliability.

Despite the fact that initially the export of these products from the United States and Canada was banned, the company occupies a leading position in the world among such giants as Nokia, Sony, Samsung. The first millionth copy was sold in 2004. On the BlackBerry phones reviews rained from all sides. They confirmed their position in the market, the guaranteed quality of communication and the services provided.





Just 10 months later, the company repeated its triumph - the second millionaire sold! Reviews about this BlackBerry model were as delightful as about the previous one. Triumphal success has been with the brand for almost ten years now. By the new 2007, five million people have become happy owners of BlackBerry products, and after another three and a half years - thirteen million lucky ones.

Here it is, the bird of happiness BlackBerry. Reviews about the millionth models have done their job. The company began to develop with the dynamics of a horse gaining speed for a gallop. And right now she is at the top of her fame.

The latest models produced by the corporation have become unrecognizable in comparison with the first-born. Due to the growing demand for smartphones with widescreen displays and a touch screen, BlackBerry did not lag behind and released the Z30. This five-inch flagship can only be recognized by the brand name.

This device does not have any features that strongly distinguish it from rivals in class. Nevertheless, it has become very popular and unusual for BlackBerry products. Reviews about this model are the best. They only once again confirm the quality.





What do you think of BlackBerry products? Reviews help developers correct inaccuracies in the operation of systems and programs. The opinion of users is always important for manufacturers, because they are the buyers.
