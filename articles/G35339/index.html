Mobile air conditioners without duct: reviews of owners, sellers and doubters



We have become gentle. One hundred years ago, no one grumbled over the weather, they enjoyed what they had: in winter - cold, in summer - hot. Progress pampered us to an incredible degree. We will not tolerate either cold or heat, give us a comfortable temperature all year round. And here we have at our disposal convectors and infrareds, split systems and mobile air conditioners. Lucky for us!

The essence of the news

In this article, we will consider a progressive miracle, more precisely, an interpretation on the topic of a well-established floor-mounted air conditioner. The essence of the novelty is that, unlike the previous variation, the unit is completely mobile. This is what the emphasis is on when introducing mobile air conditioners without an air duct. Reviews for this miracle of technology are still mixed. The reason, in fact, is one - doubt.

The fact is that its prototype, like any other unit that has a powerful engine, generates heat, despite the fact that the goal is cooling. For this, an air duct was provided at the floor air conditioner, which made the device less efficient and certainly not practical.

Cooling is generated by cooling the air passing through the wet filter. These devices do not have freon, as, for example, in a refrigerator or split system.

Doubt

Many consumers who are well versed in physics are very confused by the statements of manufacturers. In their opinion, mobile air conditioners without an air duct (reviews are collected from the forums) are nothing more than a commonplace humidifier, just at a higher price. To which those who have already purchased and use such a device respond categorically with a denial, assuring that the device is coping with its tasks.

Leaders and Pros

Of the models on the market, Electrolux mobile air conditioning is most popular . Buyers consider it the most successful combination of price / quality / design. Moreover, the indisputable advantages include not only low energy consumption, but also mobility in the truest sense of the word. To the above, you can safely add ease of use and lack of installation.



Floor air conditioners mobile without an air duct will be a great help in the summer at the cottage. Such an air conditioner can be moved from room to room and cooled only to the part where necessary, which will undoubtedly lead to energy savings.

Minuses

The operation of the device is still not so careless. First of all, it is necessary to remember that mobile air conditioners without an air duct (customer reviews are evidence of this) are best used with a good influx of fresh air, that is, it is necessary to keep the window ajar. The device is not recommended for use in damp rooms (basements, cellars). In a special hole, it is necessary to systematically pour clean water, which is subsequently wetted by the filter of the device.

The choice is entirely yours. If all of the above indicators suit you, then, of course, this is your option for salvation in the heat.
