Nizhny Novgorod is one of the largest cities in the country, which does not stop growing and developing. That is why at a certain point in time the existing residential areas and houses became sorely lacking, the need for real estate increased at times. Modern construction companies to replace the cramped and small-sized Khrushchevs have offered spacious and most comfortable apartments in areas with their own infrastructure. One of these projects was the Zenit residential complex in Nizhny Novgorod, located on Krasnozvezdnaya Street near Gagarin Avenue.

Apartments, layouts

Many of us are tired of cramped and completely inappropriate for family life apartments. That is why the residents of the city had great hopes for the Zenit residential complex in Nizhny Novgorod. Thoughtful ergonomic layouts presented in a variety of species - this is what they got.

The complex offers one-, two- and three-room options from 38 to 92 square meters. A one-room apartment is a great option for a young family, because in the concept of the project it is much more than the usual standard "odnushka". Her footage allowed to break the space into a spacious kitchen-dining room, which can be used for lunch, dinner, as well as reception, and a full isolated bedroom. At the same time, there was a place for a spacious wardrobe and a separate bathroom.

When designing two-room apartments, they adhered to the principle of rational use of the area: a spacious kitchen-living room, an additional bathroom, a wardrobe, rooms facing opposite sides. All this provides maximum comfort for residents. And finally, two-bedroom apartments are a great option for a large family. Their layout is classic, but functional. Large kitchen, spacious rooms, facing different directions, and in the hallway there is a place for additional storage.

Finish

All apartments in the LCD are for rent with rough finish, which provides for the preparation of surfaces for future decor, screed on the floor, electrical wiring, plumbing. Each apartment has high-quality entrance doors and double-glazed windows of European production.

Issue price

Apartments LCD "Zenith" in Nizhny Novgorod - affordable luxury. And proof of this are real estate prices. So, you will be able to purchase a one-room apartment with rough finish for only 2.4 million rubles, a spacious three-ruble will cost you 5.5.

Opinion of residents

Many buildings have already been commissioned and settled, their residents do not cease to leave feedback. Most of them are positive. People are delighted with the scale of the project, the impressive territory that they managed to organize and equip correctly. All the necessary infrastructure is really at hand, they can be reached on foot in 5-7 minutes. The apartments turned out to be spacious, bright and quite warm.

Residents focus on the good work of the management company: the yards are clean and well-groomed, garbage is removed in a timely manner, the elevators are monitored. By the way, all the entrances are equipped with noiseless elevators, so nothing violates the peace of the inhabitants. Perhaps the only drawback is the lack of free parking spaces. Now there are so many cars that some residents are forced to park them right in the yard, hampering the movement of pedestrians and closing the passage for special equipment. The developer really did not calculate the possible number of cars. Residents can only hope that by the time the construction is completed, a multi-level parking will be erected, which will definitely solve the problem.
