Hotel "Marins Park Hotel", Novosibirsk: description, rating, reviews of tourists

Hotel "Marins Park Hotel" (Novosibirsk) is one of the largest hotels in the whole city, which is distinguished by high-quality service and a favorable location.

Hotel "Marins Park Hotel" (Novosibirsk) has a longstanding tradition and impeccable reputation. All employees and services of this institution are working to maintain a high status and make the best possible impression on tourists.

Hotel Location

Where is the hotel "Marins Park Hotel" (Novosibirsk). In Novosibirsk, finding her is easy. It is located in the very center of the city, the landmark is the Garina-Mikhailovsky Square metro station, which is located literally a few steps from the establishment. If you will take a taxi from Tolmachevo International Airport, you will need to indicate the address: Station Railway, 1.





Standard rooms

Hotel "Marins Park Hotel-Novosibirsk" (Novosibirsk) offers its guests three options for comfortable accommodation, the most popular of which is "Standard". The cost of living in such a room may vary depending on which food system you prefer (from 2500 rubles). The apartment is designed for two adult guests, it is also possible to stay with a child. So, for babies under 4 years old a cot is provided for free, and a cot for children under 16 years old will cost 400 rubles per night.





Superior Apartment

But not only standard rooms are offered by the Marins Park Hotel (Novosibirsk). 4 stars - the level of service at which visitors have the right to count on superior rooms. They are one room with a wide range of amenities. Unlike standard apartments, there is a safe and a hairdryer. In addition, a cot or cot is available to children under 16 at no extra charge. The minimum cost of living in such apartments will be 3,500 rubles, and the price increase can be made at the expense of food.

Executive Suite

What else can please the Marins Park Hotel (Novosibirsk)? Novosibirsk offers guests a sufficient level of comfort. You can stay in the rooms of the category "Suite", the cost of which starts at 8,000 rubles. At the same time, there is no charge for an extra bed for children. Guests of these apartments have access to a minibar, which is filled every day absolutely free of charge. Also included in the price is a transfer from the airport (subject to prior reservation). Laundry facilities for Executive Suite guests are also included.
