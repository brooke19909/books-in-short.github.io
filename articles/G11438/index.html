Botanic Hotel Turkey - Oasis for tourists

Hotel Botanik 5 * in Turkey is located in the Karaburun district, 30 kilometers from Alanya and 90 kilometers from Antalya Airport. If you want to spend your whole vacation in the Botanical Garden, then you simply must choose this hotel. The territory of this garden occupies 75,000 square meters. meters and it presents more than 200 species of flora and fauna of the subtropical Mediterranean.

Botanic Hotel Turkey is popular with many tourists who visit it, the only one who is better not to go to relax is those who get tired of the noise of children. Children there are everywhere and always, from early morning until late at night. The Palma, Orchid and Tropic buildings are well suited for living. There is also a Mimosa building that overlooks a neighboring hotel with one wall and in the evenings it is quite noisy due to the fun animation in a neighboring hotel. Reviews of the Botanic Hotel in Turkey say that the Park building is the most unsuccessful in its location, all its windows face the restaurant, and this view does not at all offer a cozy and relaxing vacation.

Separately, the Rosa building should be noted, this is a pretty good and comfortable building, but just recently, a ferris wheel, an amusement park and a children's town were made not far from it, as well as an amphitheater for evening animation. Therefore, vacationers in it sometimes complain that it is difficult for children to fall asleep. Arrival and check-in at the hotel does not bring any inconvenience, there is almost always a Russian-speaking employee at the reception. The entire process of settlement is well-established, no need to stand in line and wait. It is enough to fill out the questionnaire and relax in a soft chair, and at this time all new guests are treated to wine, champagne or cocktails at will, give Turkish delight and fruit.

The beach in the Botanic Hotel Turkey is not sandy but pebble, so it is better to have special shoes for swimming. But if there are no such shoes, then without problems you can dive into the sea from the pier, it is very convenient, as it is equipped with special stairs. However, the pebble beach gives such an undoubted advantage that sea water is not muddied by sand, the sea is very clean and swimming in it is just a pleasure. The beach is large, so there is enough space on the beach for all vacationers. In addition, large mattresses lie on the pier and many prefer to sunbathe directly on them. Beach towels that are issued by hotel cards can be changed without any restrictions.

The attendants at the Botanic Hotel Turkey are very friendly and are well disposed towards Russian tourists. From other nationalities tourists are Germans and Dutch, as well as residents of the Baltic states and Poles. The staff is quite restrained and does not show their emotions, even if you did something wrong. The rooms in all cases are standard, the furniture is new and pretty good. On the first day of check-in, guests usually receive wine and fruits as a gift from the hotel. The room has a safe and a minibar with drinks and nuts. Clean, cleaning is done regularly, bed linen pleases with its whiteness.

Many tourists like the Botanic Hotel Turkey for its landscaped and very green area. Pergolas and tents, shady cafes allow you to hide from the scorching sun. A lot of free-walking birds, similar to a mixture of chicken and partridge. A large number of the most diverse plants, in the summer season you can see the flowering of bananas and cacti. Location The hotel's nerd in Turkey on the beach in a cozy green area creates an unforgettable feeling of relaxation in the shady more often during the hot summer season. As for food and drinks, here the hotel receives only positive ratings, from 7 a.m. to 24 p.m. you can get what your soul wants almost without interruption. In addition to regular three meals a day during breaks, a variety of snacks in bars. You can enjoy Turkish tea and coffee throughout the property. From 10 a.m. in the vitamin bar freshly squeezed juices and fruit shakes. There is a disco bar open until 2 a.m. Particular attention is paid to the range of baby food.
