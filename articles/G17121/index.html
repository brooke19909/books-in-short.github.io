Ramada Prague City Center Prague (Prague / Czech Republic): photos and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular European travel destinations is Prague - one of the most beautiful and most romantic cities in Europe. It was located on both sides of the Vltava River and occupied the territory of nine large hills. Due to this, in common people he is called "Northern Rome".

The cultural pearl has a number of attractions, the presence of which is due to its rich history. Here you can find architectural Gothic buildings and buildings, the exterior of which is decorated in the Baroque or Renaissance style.

The amazing simplicity and friendliness of the indigenous residents of Prague surprise from the first moment of acquaintance with the village. Beautiful natural landscapes in combination with architectural monuments delight guests of the city and are the subject of pride of local residents.

The hotel complex Ramada Prague City Center, which, in accordance with international standards, is assigned 4 stars, is glad to meet with representatives of all corners of the planet. A warm welcome, comfortable accommodation and positive emotions are guaranteed to every tourist. Description of the hotel Ramada Prague City Center (Prague) read on in the article.





Location

Hotel Ramada Prague City Center (Prague) is located on Wenceslas Square, which is the center of the Czech capital. The distance to the central railway station is 300 m, which allows travelers to easily reach the property on foot. The distance to the metro station is 100 m, which is convenient when implementing independent excursions. It takes about half an hour to get to the international airport. Florenz Bus Station is 2.5 km away.

general characteristics

Ramada Prague City Center EA Hotel is part of the Ramada Worldwide hotel chain. For the first time, its doors for receiving vacationers were opened in 2002. The latest renovation entailed a change of name that happened in 2011. This place is perfect for overnight stays during excursion vacations and business trips. The building is represented by a multi-storey building, which is equipped with passenger and freight elevators. Walking along the street on which this building is located, it is simply impossible not to notice it: a large and bright sign catches everyone's eye.

On the ground floor, newly arrived guests are greeted with a warm smile by the reception desk staff, who has a semicircular shape and many spotlights above it. The atmosphere of the lobby is incredibly cozy and creates the first incorruptible impression of a future stay. An original chandelier, huge works of painters, green plants in flowerpots, soft chairs - what awaits travelers on the doorstep at the Ramada Prague City Center hotel.

Accommodations

The furniture is made of high quality wood in dark shades and is represented by soft beds with bedside tables, a coffee table surrounded by two chairs, a writing desk under the TV, a luggage rack and a spacious wardrobe. Individual air conditioning allows you to adjust the temperature in the room for each guest independently. Entertainment

The main purpose of the trip, if no business meetings are planned, is to sightseeing in Prague. For this, at the reception you can order any excursion to the memorable places of the city. Within walking distance there is a gym where you can exercise regularly.

Ramada Prague City Center (Prague) often receives positive reviews for additional services provided. Tourists claim that they are at a fairly high level.
