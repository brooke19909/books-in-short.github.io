Porto Iliessa 4 * - an unforgettable vacation in an upscale hotel in Greece

Porto Iliessa 4 * is an excellent hotel located on one of the picturesque islands of Greece called Zakynthos. Many people come here from different parts of the world to enjoy local attractions, archaeological sites and, of course, stunning scenery and warm gentle sea.





Great location

All tourists, even those who go on a trip for the first time and do not navigate at all in the city where they decided to stay, are well aware that the hotel must be chosen not only by the number of stars and quality of service, but also by the place in which it is located. Porto Iliessa 4 * is located very favorably - on the first coastline. This can be said to be ideal for visitors, especially for those who came here for a beach holiday, because they will be less than 200 meters from the sea.

basic information

Porto Iliessa 4 * can be seen from afar - this is a tall unusual building, which instantly catches your eye. It is difficult not to notice the hotel complex, located right on the seashore.

The hotel, by the way, has been popular for a long time since it was built in 1998. True, several years ago, a restoration was carried out, after which the eyes of the visitors appeared an updated modern hotel complex. The hotel consists of five buildings, one of which is the main one.

But the main advantage of the hotel complex is not modern, well furnished, rooms and an excellent level of service, but two gorgeous beaches - sand and pebble, on which there are sun loungers and sun umbrellas. And what else does a tourist need for a good rest away from a noisy and dusty city?

Accommodation options

The Porto Iliessa 4 * hotel (Zakynthos) has only fifty rooms. This room type has a bedroom and a living room, plus a fully equipped kitchen. And, of course, a bathroom with a spacious balcony.
