Crochet technology may seem complicated and too confusing, but this is only at first glance. Crocheting the air loops is the main work, so you need to master this technique thoroughly so that everything works out quickly and simply, as if by itself. To do this, you need to train a lot and be patient. Knitting can be for you not only a means to create beautiful things, but also calm your nerves after a long day.



The first thing you should think about where to buy threads, yarn for knitting. These materials can be found in any sewing and knitting store. If you accidentally buy a too large hook, then the loops in the knitted fabric will be too large, which means it will turn out to be very loose and in holes, in addition, you can lose the whole pattern. If the hook is small for yarn, then the thread will completely slip out, which will cause a lot of inconvenience.

Crochet technique for beginners involves knitting air loops and crochets. In order to crochet the air loops for the first time, you may need a lot of time, because it may be difficult for a beginner to even hold the hook correctly.

First make the first main loop. To do this, throw one thread on another so that a loop is formed, holding two threads at the place of interweaving. Pass the hook into this loop and tighten the thread around the hook. Now try to hook the working thread and pull it through this loop. As a result, one loop should remain on the hook, which is called the main loop. To crochet the air loops, you need to grab the working thread again and stretch it through the loop that you made before. One loop should remain again. Crocheting the air loops You will notice that a pigtail is formed, into which further knit columns are crocheted or crocheted.
