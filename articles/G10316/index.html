Hotel "Mayak", Listvyanka: description and reviews of tourists

Tourists from all over the world dream of seeing Lake Baikal. One of the best accommodation options near this natural wonder is the Mayak Hotel. Listvyanka is a great place not only for travelers, but also for residents of nearby settlements who want to have a good time away from city noise.





Rooms and prices

Comfortable conditions for living and recreation are offered by the Mayak Hotel. Listvyanka will seem to you an even more attractive place if you stay in comfortable and beautifully decorated rooms. The options for accommodation are as follows:

  • Single standard is a cozy room decorated in a modern European style. high-quality textiles and soft carpeting immerse you in an atmosphere of home warmth and comfort. This room has a single bed and a desk. The cost of living is 4600 rubles per day.
  • The double standard is decorated in pleasant pastel colors. The room may have a double or a pair of one and a half beds. There is a desk and a wardrobe. The combined bathroom is equipped with a bath. It is possible to install an extra bed. The cost of living is 5400 rubles per day.
  • Studio is a spacious room with a classic interior design and Italian wooden furniture. The room combines the functions of a bedroom and a living room. In addition to a large bed, there is a large soft sofa and a coffee table. The cost of living is 8500 rubles.
  • Penthouse - a spacious two-room suite. The bedroom has a large bed and a soft folding sofa. The living room is equipped with leather upholstered furniture. The bathroom has a modern shower with hydromassage. The daily cost of living is 12,100 rubles.
  • Apartments are a luxurious room that will make you feel like a VIP person. The living room has upholstered furniture, a large dining table. A separate study is provided. The bedroom has a king size bed. The interior is made in a classic palace style using natural wood furniture. The cost of living in the apartment is 14 500 rubles per day.

Catering establishments

If you want to have a good meal, visit the restaurant of the Mayak Hotel. Listvyanka will delight you with authentic tastes of Siberian cuisine. Special attention should be paid to fish specialties caught right in Baikal. Omul and grayling will delight even the most demanding gourmets. The hotel has an outdoor terrace on the second floor, offering amazing views of the lake. Also in the institution you can celebrate any special occasion.

On the seventh floor of the hotel there is a panoramic grill bar, which is popular not only among tourists, but also among the local population. This is a kind of visiting card of this hotel. Here you can enjoy the views not only with your own eyes, but even through a telescope. The holiday picture is complemented by delicious vegetables, fish and meat cooked on charcoal.

Negative reviews

Despite all the positive moments of relaxation in such an institution as the Mayak Hotel (Russia, Listvyanka), not everything is perfect here. Based on the reviews, it is worth highlighting such negative points:

  • food in a restaurant is expensive, and the quality and quantity do not quite match the price;
  • to have a normal breakfast, you need to come to the opening of the dining room, and closer to 10:00 there is practically nothing left at the distribution;
  • when you check in, you can find a lack of cosmetics in the bathroom;
  • poor sound insulation in the rooms, every word of the neighbors is heard through the wall;
  • queues are constantly gathering near the elevator;
  • there are no refrigerators in the rooms;
  • the rooms have poor lighting (although, if you apply for a reception, they can provide an additional table lamp or floor lamp);
  • when banquets are held in the restaurant, the noise spreads throughout the hotel, it is impossible to relax in the room);
  • the rooms do not have crockery and cutlery (only one glass);
  • few free outlets in the room;
  • the room area is very small even for one person, not to mention two guests or families with children;
  • the registration and check-in procedure takes at least half an hour, which is very tiring after the trip;
  • hotel staff almost never take into account the special wishes of guests, which are indicated when booking a room;
  • in the cold season, rooms are poorly heated, and even heaters do not save;
  • the sauna has a very slippery floor, and also does not catch mobile communications;
  • there are no floor mats at the entrance to the rooms, and therefore, if there is slush on the street, all the water and dirt from the soles gets into the room;
