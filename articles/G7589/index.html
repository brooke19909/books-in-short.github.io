Marriott Tsaghkadzor Hotel: overview, description and reviews of tourists

"Marriott Tsaghkadzor 5 *" is a luxury hotel, which is located near the famous ski resort in Armenia. The peak of its popularity falls on the winter period, when people begin to think about where to spend the New Year holidays. Often their attention falls on this particular place to relax, since, according to them, a real fairy tale reigns here in the winter.

general information

Marriott Tsaghkadzor Hotel has been hosting tourists since 2012. It was during this period that it was discovered. Despite such a recent discovery, reconstruction works were carried out on its territory and in buildings, which were carried out in 2017. At this time, the authors of the project made some changes to the technical equipment of the hotel.

Location

The Marriott Tsaghkadzor Hotel is located in Armenia, in the so-called Valley of Flowers, not far from the ski resort, in whose honor it was named. Not far from the hotel is the central part of the city, where there is a small amount of entertainment and there are several attractions. In particular, travelers vacationing here are very fond of visiting the ancient fortress of Bjni, which was built back in the 10th century. Not far from here is the Kecharis Church, which is visible from some of the rooms.

Rooms

The hotel offers comfortable accommodation in apartments with a high level of comfort. Some of them are located in the main building ("Standard", "Deluxe" and "Suite"), and the rest are in cottages, and are luxurious apartments, most likely intended for living with whole families.

Many tourists note that each room has a built-in climate system in the form of a powerful air conditioner, with which you can adjust the temperature conditions in the apartments. Each type of room also has a bathroom with modern fixtures. Here, without fail, there is a sink for washing and a place for bathing. The bathroom is connected to the bathroom.



"Standard"

These rooms are located in the main building, they are small in size (22 sq.m.) and are represented by just one room, decorated in a classical style with the use of modern solutions in the interior. There is one large double bed, on which there is silk bedding, on both sides of it are equipped with small cupboards for small accessories.

The room has a small sitting area, presented in the form of a pair of armchairs and a coffee table - here, according to the guests, it is very convenient to drink freshly prepared coffee while admiring the view of the beautiful nature that opens from large windows.

The room must have a large plasma TV, as well as a safe. Guests can enjoy access to the Internet. For a fee, you can install a minibar here.

Accommodation in cottages

Often, guests recommend that families who plan to have a rest with young children in the Marriott Tsaghkadzor settle in cottages that offer luxurious conditions for a comfortable and relaxing holiday. These apartments are designed to accommodate a couple of guests with the possibility of installing an extra bed for a child. If you want to get a cot for the baby, you must make a preliminary request about it. For the third adult, these rooms have a rollaway bed.

According to vacationers, apartments located in cottages have absolutely all the conditions not only for a good night's sleep, but also for conducting business affairs. There is a working area with a desk in which you can solve business issues.



Nutrition

Fans of delicious food very often show off with positive comments the taste of the dishes offered in the Marriott Tsakhkadzor 5 * restaurant. They can offer dishes prepared in the best traditions of the Caucasian and Armenian cuisines, which are able to win the hearts of even the most sophisticated gourmets. If desired, guests can request a diet and vegetarian menu.

The hotel also has a bar located in the main building. Its menu offers a huge assortment of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as the most delicious sweets prepared according to old recipes.
