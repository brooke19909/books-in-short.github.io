Kaprun (Austria) - a secret that attracts again and again

Shaking ourselves from hibernation and carrying my thoughts somewhere far away, I want to escape from the gray everyday life and, forgetting about everything, go somewhere to rest ... for example, in the Alps! If you really want to, then dreams, as you know, tend to come true. And now the suitcase is packed, and the resort is selected.





general description

A very interesting ski resort Kaprun (Austria) has gained particular popularity due to its almost year-round work. Geographically, it belongs to the district of Salzburg, by the number in it there are hardly 3,000 people. Kaprun is one of two ski resorts that make up the European Sports Region (ECP).

The resort of Kaprun, Austria. Geographic features

A 20-minute drive from the town is Mount Kitzsteinhorn (3203 m), or the Kaprun Glacier, as tourists call it because of the unpronounceability. Snow cover does not leave the mountain either in winter or in summer, which makes it an ideal place for ski slopes. Nearby is the picturesque Lake Celsk.





Ski slopes

Two riding areas. The first is Mount Maiskogel (1675 m), it is its slopes that are more convenient for beginners, family skating and practicing equipment. There are 2 blue and 1 red tracks - wide and comfortable, accessible by lifts: for adults - a 15-minute drive from the town, and children‚Äôs can be reached on foot. Very close is a family amusement park. The ski season is from December to April. Another zone is on the glacier itself. Starting from a height of 3029 m, there are more serious tracks here: blue and conditionally black. At the level of 2452 m there is a superpipe and a fan park, in which special tracks for snowboarders are laid. A very pleasant moment: in the area located on the glacier, all tracks are illuminated until 22.00.

sights

The medieval castle of the 12th century, where knightly tournaments, weddings, festivals, a museum of old cars and a historical one are held, is far from all the outstanding places of the town of Kaprun. Austria has preserved very few monuments of Gothic architecture, so tourists are strongly recommended to visit the modest parish church of St. Margarita, designed in this style. Another interesting attraction is the camp, laid out at an altitude of 2500 m of the Kaprun glacier. It consists of 11 ice huts, and in one of them, in the middle of the wonderful world of snow and ice, it is possible to spend the night if there is not enough thrill.





Schools and courses

A ski kindergarten from 2.5 years old, 4 schools for snowboarders, ski courses located on a separate territory and having 2 lifts, 10 ski schools - this is an incomplete list of places where you can acquire and hone the necessary sports skills.

Kaprun, Austria. Entertainment Reviews

Spends time merrily and profitably here every vacationer. Tourists are offered squash courts and tennis courts, 4 outdoor ice rinks, curling, winter golf, a wellness center. For a change, vacationers go around the lake on a tram or go on an excursion to the mine. A very popular disco at the Baum Bar. It is only one, but, according to the reviews of those who have been there, it is beyond praise.

Kitchen

Local culinary traditions are unshakably honored in the town of Kaprun. Austria, as you know, is famous for the simplicity of the national cuisine, and the local restaurants mainly offer unpretentious, but very tasty dishes.

In the memories of a tourist who visited this picturesque resort, there will remain an amazing atmosphere of the holiday, lasting all year round. Healing air, unique landscapes, outdoor activities combined with developed infrastructure will make you recall the town of Kaprun more than once. Austria with its beauties and secrets will beckon again and again.
