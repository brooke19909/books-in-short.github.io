Hotel Grand Hotel Faros 4 * (Turkey, Marmaris): description, photo, reviews of tourists

A huge number of people relax in Turkish resorts annually. Among Russians, Turkey is also considered one of the most popular tourist destinations. It has a wonderful climate, clean and fresh air, rich infrastructure and three whole seas for every taste: Black, Aegean and Mediterranean. As practice shows, cities located on the Mediterranean Sea (Kemer, Belek, Side) are more popular among tourists from Russia. But many argue that the Aegean coast also should not be discounted. It has a very beautiful nature, many attractions and amazingly beautiful underwater world. Among the most popular resorts of the Aegean coast, Marmaris, Bodrum and Kusadasi can be noted, where many budget-friendly hotels and hotels 2,3 and 4 * are concentrated.





In this review, we will present you a description of a modern and elegant four-star hotel called Grand Hotel Faros. What is he like? The hotel is part of the young Faros group and is located in the Turkish city of Marmaris. We will discuss its infrastructure, number of rooms, food system, and also find out that vacationers are talking about this tourist complex.

Entertainment and infrastructure

What else? For entertaining holidaymakers, the Grand Hotel Faros 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey) provides: a large swimming pool (sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas - free of charge), a gym, a sauna, for a fee - a hammam and massage services. The hotel has a tour desk that offers various tours and boat excursions. For fans of active games there is the opportunity to play darts, table tennis, billiards (for a fee), rent a bike and explore the surroundings of the city on your own. Three times a week a team of animators works at the hotel, and exciting evening entertainment programs are held.





For the convenience of tourists, the hotel has a currency exchange, a doctor (on request), dry cleaning, laundry, beauty salon, free parking, wireless Internet in public areas (free of charge), Internet cafe (for a fee).

For children

There are few vacationers with kids at the Grand Hotel Faros, since the complex is not focused on the relaxation of young travelers. There is no children's animation, mini-club or a separate children's menu. There is only a children's pool and a small playground. Also, the road to the beach on the bus may not be completely pleasant for children, and the hours of departure of the transfer may not coincide with the kids' daytime sleep. Therefore, before you go to this hotel with a child, you should think about whether you will be comfortable there.

Catering

On the territory of the Grand Hotel Faros (Turkey, Marmaris) there is one bar and restaurant. The hotel serves a daily buffet, which offers a varied and tasty menu of international and Turkish dishes. Dinner is served at the salad bar. Drinks are available at the poolside bar. Local alcohol - free in unlimited quantities, imported drinks - for a fee.





Beach area

An important point: the Grand Hotel Faros has its own sandy beach, located 2.5 kilometers from the complex. A bus runs every two hours to the beach and back. Mattresses, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided free of charge, towels - for a fee.

Things to do for people who booked a tour to this Marmaris hotel

To begin with, the city in which Faros is located is located in a picturesque bay and is surrounded on all sides by mountains and hills with dense vegetation. Here you can arrange endless walks along the 13 km long embankment and admire the beauties of nature throughout the rest.

Due to the fact that the city is located in a bay, the sea here is always calm, without waves. The beaches are sandy, which makes relaxing with young children especially enjoyable. Although, in comparison with the Mediterranean Sea, the water here is colder, in July and August you can calmly make long swims, enjoy the warm weather and bask in the bright rays of the sun.

Marmaris is also considered the most party city in the country. In addition to the longest promenade, where there are many bars and clubs with free admission, there is also the famous street - Bar Street, where the fun lasts until the morning. From Grand Faros to Bar Street can be easily reached by public transport in 15 minutes.





There is no open sea in Marmaris, and because of this feature, excursions on yachts in the bays are common in the city. At the hotel you can buy such a tour at a very reasonable price. Also, Faros guests can independently visit the famous ancient fortress of Marmaris (entrance costs 5 lire), take a walk along the narrow streets of the old city, take beautiful pictures in the square with a clock tower and enjoy the show ‚ÄúSinging Fountains‚Äù (beginning at 9 a.m. daily). Near the square is the archaeological park "Rocks of Wellbeing", the entrance to which is completely free. Here, all the rocks are planted with plants and cacti that grew in the country in the ancient period. Also in the park you can see the finds found in the city in the IV century BC. Two water parks are open for fans of water entertainment in the city: the small Atlantis complex and the larger Aqua Dream.

Judging by the reviews, from Grand Hotel Faros 4 * (Marmaris) you can go on many excursions that are almost inaccessible to tourists spending their holidays off the shores of the Mediterranean Sea.
