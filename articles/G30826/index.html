Camera "Canon 70 D". "Canon 70D" (Canon EOS 70D): description, specifications and reviews

The moment of appearance of the model fell at the peak of the technological peak of the concept of SLR cameras. The leaders of the formed segment had only to look for new directions and ways to attract customers. As a result, Sony optimized its models by abandoning the pentaprism, Pentax relied on a stabilization system, and Nikon surprised everyone with the exception of the low-pass filter from the optional list. In turn, the creators of "Canon 70 D Body" continued the development of videographers, successfully implementing autofocus. However, not only this distinguished this DSLR.

The device is not much different from previous modifications in terms of housing design. The knowing eye will find the same chopped shape, completely subordinate to ergonomics and the control system. The model is based on a magnesium base, the lining is characterized by a low tendency to soiling and resistance to mechanical stress. Actually, this combination surprises users with a combination of modest dimensions and a rich internal "filling." However, for those who are not familiar with the lineup, this device may seem massive and weighty. Compared to the class rivals, the model really seems a bit heavy, but if you compare it in your own family, the relief in weight will become obvious. Also, in comparison with its predecessors, a tendency to change optics has already taken shape. The complete camera lens, with all its advantages, imposes a limitation on the capabilities of the device, which forces sophisticated photographers to perfect such a crucial part of the camera.

To access various functions, you should refer to the right part, there is also a small information screen. Only a focus area is knocked out of the previous concept. The back side remained unchanged, although a careful analysis reveals the desire of designers to anatomically position the 70 D controls. ‚ÄúCanon‚Äù managed to bring the lines closer to the optimal structure in terms of interaction with the palm and fingers. Functionality





The model received a new sensor, which largely determined the increase in the quality level of the family. So, if in previous versions the sensor was used at 18 megapixels, then this modification acquired the APS-C module, whose resolution is 20.2 megapixels. As a result, the camera received a two-pixel focusing system. Any camera lens from the EF and EF-S families suitable for its technical device can adjust autofocus for itself.

The CMOS system provides focus on more than 80% of the width and height of the screen frame. To implement the phase determination on the image plane, the right and left photodiodes are read separately, as a result of which the finished offset images are used to calculate phase mismatches. This principle of operation is also confirmed by the practice of operating ‚Äú70 D‚Äù. ‚ÄúCanon‚Äù has most successfully implemented the concept in the Live View system, which provides effective tracking focus for videographers.





Video recording

The screen in this model is rotary and touch, which allows you to literally mark objects on it with a finger for fixing autofocus. Actually, the videography of the device is one of the strengths. Even when compared with good representatives of Sony's SLR devices in the spirit of the SLT line, the advantages of the same focus will be obvious. Otherwise, the characteristics are familiar to fans of the family. The model provides FullHD recording at a standard frequency of 30 frames per second, but an increase in frequency is also possible. Offers frame-by-frame compression, as well as proprietary stereo microphone "70 D". Canon offers a recording time interval of 30 minutes as a standard. Of course, even these camera capabilities are not enough to consider it as a professional device for video recording, but by the standards of the abilities of other representatives of the segment, this, of course, is a technological breakthrough.

Positive feedback about the model

First of all, professionals point to the optimal design, which is soundly assembled and easy to handle. The anatomical implementation of the design is entirely commendable. Many owners of the device also emphasize the redesigned focus, which demonstrates effective operation in Live View. Modern lenses for the "Canon 70 D" from the EF families allow you to maximize the advantages of fast, clear and tenacious stabilization. The model also causes positive reviews in terms of working in video mode. In this part, you can highlight the confident capture of the frame, as well as the convenience of the provided controls.





Negative reviews

The connoisseurs of natural shots were disappointed by the fact that the matrix of the device conducts the frame through the correction of the integrated editor before the final fixation. As a result, the photo not only loses in naturalness, but also loses in overall quality, this is noticeable even with low sensitivity. There are also questions to the unsuccessful execution of the functional "Canon 70 D". Reviews indicate that when the Wi-Fi module is on, the video mode does not work. Also, pictures in the HDR system can be taken in JPG, but not in RAW.

Conclusion





The model is one of the most successful representatives of the class of SLR cameras. The developers have complied with the optimal standard for providing basic technical and operational characteristics, as well as mastered several new developments that have specifically improved the functionality of the EOS 70D camera. The price of the model, however, is also considerable and varies in the domestic market in the range of 60-70 thousand rubles. If you add a set of accessories that is mandatory for a modern photographer to the basic configuration, then the amount will rise to 100 thousand. This investment is not justified in all cases. For example, for a professional in the niche of photography, new video options may be unnecessary, which nevertheless noticeably increased the overall attractiveness of the model. But for fans of mobile shooting on video, this option is optimal. True, only for the amateur level, which is due to both the restriction in recording frequency and recording formats in comparison with professional equipment.
