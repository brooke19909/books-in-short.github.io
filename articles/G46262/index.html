Hot springs, Krasnodar Territory - always warm. Unique resorts in the region

The great lands of the Krasnodar Territory are known far beyond the borders of the Russian Federation. This largest and most hospitable tourist region, washed by two seas - Black and Azov, is famous for its unique resorts. These include Bolshoi Sochi, Goryachy Klyuch, Tuapse, Anapa, Gelendzhik, Yeysk and Krasnaya Polyana (ski resort center in the subtropics).





Almost all hot springs are rich in magnificent natural landscapes, exotic plains, magnificent peaks, healing mineral waters. Krasnodar Territory is now also the venue for the 2014 Olympics

What is unique about the southern district?

Nature itself rewarded the region with favorable geographic and climatic conditions. The plentiful gentle sun, the azure sea, kilometers-long clean beaches, numerous boarding houses, health resorts, hotels with open arms welcome guests from different parts of the globe. Frantic pleasure and indescribable emotions annually give their guests hot springs. Krasnodar Territory has always been and remains the most popular and beloved tourist center.

All resorts in the south have great healing potential. The health resorts of this sunny region have become famous all over the world for their high-class medical services, balneological, climatic and mud bases. Due to the abundance of sanatoriums and pensions, the hot springs of the Krasnodar Territory (prices in hotels start at 500 rubles per person) have secured the title of a favorite of first-class recreation.





The region has a developed tourism industry and infrastructure, enriched with protected areas and natural monuments. Tourists can combine exciting trips with beach vacations, while not forgetting to visit theaters and concert halls. The choice of entertainment services is so diverse and large that it is sometimes difficult to navigate.

The most popular hot springs in the Krasnodar Territory: Mostovskoy District

Mostovskaya village is known for geothermal healing springs. People with various diseases come here to restore health. Mostovskoy district is a natural complex that attracts tourists not only with thermal waters, but also with clean ecology, historical and archaeological structures and picturesque nature.





Comfortable living conditions and a whole range of wellness treatments are offered by the hot springs of the Krasnodar Territory. Mostovskaya is a place where you can truly relax, strengthen your immunity, shaken nervous system and recharge with positive energy. Mountain air, thermal water, a favorable climate and the blue sea contribute to this.

A lot of recreation centers have been built on the territory of the resort village, the most popular are Ginseng and Khutorok. Pensions offer customers comfortable conditions at affordable prices. Here skin diseases, gynecological pathologies and even malignant diseases are treated. Women come here to get rid of infertility. The chemical composition of water contains minerals and minerals necessary for the human body.

The resort city of Sochi is the only mountain climatic hot spring





The Krasnodar Territory is a great asset of Russia, on the territory of which there is an amazing place called Sochi. It is both a beach center and a ski resort. Subtropical vegetation, hospitable surroundings, an abundance of entertainment and a mild climate make the Black Sea coast even more visited.

Over the past few years, the resort city has been completely updated, has become the Olympic capital and the center of the tourism industry. Here are concentrated more than 600 medical centers, boarding houses, hotels of international level and private hotels. Sanatorium-resort treatment combines specific healing procedures, such as: climatotherapy, mud therapy, balneological therapy, SPA-procedures, psychotherapy and physiotherapy exercises.

Gelendzhik - natural "bath"





The hot springs of the Krasnodar Territory cannot but boast such a resort town as Gelendzhik. This is a favorite place for middle-income Russians. The atmosphere of celebration and goodwill reigns throughout the resort. Plane alleys, slender cypress groves and green parks adorn the territory.
