How to get from Milan to Genoa. Route Options

Milan is not only a wonderful city, but also a major transport hub. Many hiking trails start from here. The distance from Milan to Genoa is 143 kilometers. By car, you can drive in two hours. Read more about how to get from Milan to Genoa in this article.

One of the most economical options is a trip by rail. At least, talking about how to get from Milan to Genoa, tourists who have been to Italy more than once recommend this method first of all. But there are, of course, cheaper options.

By bus

How to get from Milan to Genoa? Buy a ticket for one of the buses running between these cities. It is desirable from the company "Fixbus". These are convenient, comfortable buses. Each one has Wi-Fi. Most importantly, tickets for Fixbus buses are relatively inexpensive.

Flights are operated daily from eight in the morning until eight in the evening. Departure interval is two hours. If you are interested in how to get from Milan to Genoa as soon as possible, then you should buy a ticket for the Fixbus bus. Such a journey lasts less than a journey by rail. The ticket price in translation into Russian currency is approximately 600 rubles. Most buses leave from Lampugnano Station.

Baltour is another major transport company. Buses from this company depart more often than Fixbus buses. However, tickets are a bit expensive. For ItalyPass cardholders, the transport company makes discounts.

By train

By rail, traveling from the capital to the homeland of Columbus and Paganini is about twenty minutes longer than by bus. And a train ticket, as already mentioned, is more expensive. The most profitable option is Intercity trains. But for those who want to get from Milan to Genoa as quickly as possible, he will not do.

Frecciabianca is the only high-speed train that runs between these Italian cities. It leaves daily at 1:15 from the Main Station. Arriving in Genoa at 14:55. You need to buy a ticket for a high-speed train in advance. Intercity Intercity is even faster. Just an hour and a half. Ticket price - 800 rubles.

The Intercity intercity train leaves every hour from Milan. The final station is Piazza Principe. The first departure at six in the morning. The last - at eight in the evening.

A regional train leaves from the Italian capital every two hours. Like intercity, arrives at Piazza Principe station. It is worth remembering that the last regional train leaves at half past six in the evening.

It is better to buy train tickets in advance on the websites of transport companies. Milan - Genoa is a popular destination. There may be problems with tickets.

By car

Fans of Italian attractions recommend renting a car. Traveling by car will allow you to visit not only Milan and Genoa, but also the settlements located between them. The road along the highway without stops will take an hour and a half.

By plane

There are no direct flights between these cities. However, at the airport in Milan you can buy tickets with transfers in cities such as Rome, Munich, Naples. The flight will take from three to eight hours.

Alitalia's flight is the most affordable option. The flight will take three and a half hours. Docking takes place in Rome, at the Fiumicino airport. Aircraft of this company make about 10 flights per day. Ticket price - seven to eight thousand rubles.

A flight by Meridiana is the most economically viable option. However, the stopping time at the Naples airport is at least ten hours. This is a morning flight. The plane lands in Genoa at 22:00. The cost of the flight is seven thousand rubles.

The price of tickets for aircraft of other companies is about three times higher. All depart from Linate, the closest to the center of Milan. The airport can be reached by bus number 173.

Sights of Milan

The Italian capital is a trendsetter of European fashion, a city with an interesting and eventful history. Among the attractions are the cathedrals founded in the early Middle Ages. Many tourists are interested not only in architectural monuments, but also in boutiques, which are here at every step.

Nevertheless, before shopping, you should visit such sights as the Cathedral of Milan, La Scala Opera House, the Basilica of St. Ambrose, the Pinacoteca Brera, the Ambrosian Pinacoteca.

Attractions of Genoa

The city, which is a large northern port, is a great place for excursions and beach vacations.

The Ligurian coast is famous for its mild climate and an abundance of architectural monuments. Sights of Genoa: Piazza Ferrari, Old Port, La Lanterna Lighthouse. Bakkadass, which is located along the promenade of Corso, is also popular among tourists.
