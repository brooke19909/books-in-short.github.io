Hotel Novi 3 (Montenegro) - photos, prices and reviews

Igalo is a well-known medical and health resort both in Montenegro and beyond. He gained such fame thanks to the Simo Milosevic Institute, founded here more than half a century ago, in which he successfully treats the most diverse diseases: from rheumatism to skin problems. However, Igalo is also attracted by ordinary tourists who want to spend their holidays in a calm place with a healthy climate.

In addition to the Igalo Institute, there are many hotels of various star categories: from the simplest and cheapest to luxury hotels. If you decide to spend your vacation on the Adriatic coast in the town of Igalo and at the same time you are looking for a comfortable, but budget accommodation option, then pay attention to the three-star Novi Hotel. Today we will introduce you to this hotel more closely, as well as tell you what impressions our compatriots had on their stay here.





Where is

Hotel Novi 3 * (Montenegro) is located in the famous resort of Igalo on the Adriatic coast. Just a few kilometers away is a large city - Herceg Novi. Its historic center can be reached in half an hour. Also, from here it is literally within easy reach of the border with Croatia. So, the distance to Dubrovnik is only 30 kilometers. Getting to Igalo is convenient both from Tivat airport (about 40 minutes) and from Dubrovnik's air harbor (it will take about the same time, but you will need a national Croatian or Schengen visa). You can travel from Igalo to other cities of Montenegro by bus, taxi or rented car. As for the beach, the Novi Hotel does not have it. The nearest beach is only one and a half hundred meters and is famous for its healing mud.





Novi 3 * (Montenegro): photo and description

Novi is a modern hotel that first opened its doors in 2010. Here guests can stay in 80 comfortable rooms and apartments. The hotel offers quality food ("all inclusive" or "breakfast only"), excellent service and a pleasant atmosphere, setting up for a calm, measured vacation on the beach. By the way, this hotel is very popular with our compatriots. According to them, this is a great place for a family vacation away from the bustle of the city. If you decide to settle in Novi 3 * (Montenegro), you can book through one of the online booking sites or directly through a travel agency.





Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of this three-star hotel, the settlement of tourists who are on vacation is carried out after 13:00. However, if you arrive at the hotel earlier (for example, due to an early flight), then in case of availability of rooms you will be placed immediately. Otherwise, you will be asked to leave things at the reception and take a walk around the neighborhood, have a snack or go to the beach, in a word, start to relax. On the day of departure, the occupied room in Novi Igalo 3 * (Montenegro) should be vacated before 11 a.m. In this case, you will need to hand over the key to the reception and pay for accommodation. We accept both cash and Visa and Mastercard payment cards .

Special conditions

The Novi Hotel (Igalo, Montenegro) will always welcome guests arriving on vacation with their entire families, with children of all ages. For your children, extra beds can be requested. It is recommended that you contact the hotel in advance about the possibility of such a service for your room category and its cost. As for guests who do not want to part with their pets even on vacation, unfortunately, the policy of the hotel in question does not provide for the placement of animals on its territory.





Number of rooms

Hotel Novi 3 * (Montenegro) has 80 rooms and apartments located in two buildings. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, a telephone and a balcony. Apartments also have a kitchenette. Cleaning is done daily, and bed linen and towels are changed twice a week.

Nutrition

Hotel guests are offered a choice of two types of food: breakfast only or all inclusive. You can have breakfast, lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant, where chefs serve a buffet.

Beach vacation

The nearest beach to the Novi Hotel is just 150 meters away. It is sandy, with a shallow entry into the water and is famous for its healing mud.





Infrastructure

The Novi 3 * hotel (Montenegro) has everything you need for a comfortable stay for guests. So, the reception is open around the clock, there is a restaurant, a bar and parking, a safe at the reception. In addition, the hotel has all the conditions for disabled people.

Accommodation cost

The price for accommodation at the Novi Hotel (Igalo) is consistent with its three-star category. So, a seven-day placement per person will cost in the amount of seven thousand rubles.





Hotel Novi 3 * (Montenegro): reviews of tourists from Russia

In order for your impression of the Novi Hotel located in Igalo (Montenegro) to be more complete and reliable, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the opinions of our compatriots who have already had a chance to relax here this year. Looking ahead a bit, we note that most of the reviews from tourists are positive. However, there are also a few negative ones (however, as is the case with other hotels). But we offer to understand everything in order.

As for the hotel itself, it is quite new and built in a modern style. The rooms and apartments of our compatriots seemed cozy and fairly spacious. In addition, they had everything they needed. By the way, furniture and appliances are modern, which many tourists did not expect from a three-star hotel, which is far from the richest European country.

As for the cleaning of the rooms Novi 3 * (Montenegro), then the guests in general were satisfied with its quality. True, sometimes the maids forgot to replace bed linen or replenish supplies of toiletries on time, but contacting the reception quickly solved all the problems.





Since we touched on the topic of maids, we suggest finding out the opinion of travelers about the hotel staff as a whole. So, many tourists note that the hotel staff are very working and friendly. True, sometimes due to the large influx of guests they do not have time to do everything on time, but our compatriots do not blame it on the staff, but on the hotel owners, who did not take care of hiring a sufficient number of employees. As for knowledge of the Russian language, only a few speak it. But you can always explain in the Serbian language, as it is much like Russian. In addition, the hotel almost always has a Russian-speaking guide, which you can contact on any issue.

As for the territory of the Novi 3 * hotel (Montenegro), traveler reviews describe it as small. There is no pool and special entertainment infrastructure. However, according to tourists, this is quite normal for small European hotels. Pleased guests with the location of the hotel. So, you can reach the sea in just a couple of minutes. Also in the vicinity of the hotel there are restaurants, supermarkets and small shops.

Fairly controversial reviews received food at the hotel. So, according to many guests, the food here was tasty and of high quality. The choice of dishes, of course, cannot be compared with four- or five-star hotels in Turkey and Egypt, but it was impossible to stay hungry. Every day, guests were offered meat and fish dishes. Some tourists were dissatisfied with the choice of dishes and argue that the hotel should pay more attention to the issue of food.

In general, experienced travelers recommend that people planning to stay in Novi 3 * (Montenegro) do not pay for all-inclusive meals by choosing the option ‚Äúbreakfast only‚Äù. In this case, you will always have the opportunity to go for lunch and dinner to the surrounding taverns, where for more than an affordable price you can try a variety of dishes of the national Montenegrin cuisine.

As for the beach, you can reach it in just a couple of minutes. It is sandy here and has a gentle entry into the water. The local beach is famous for its healing mud. Therefore, the water here, according to tourists, is unclear. However, lovers of mud smeared with healing mud, as well as families with children who are happy to play sand on the beach, will surely like it here. If you want to relax on the beach with cleaner water, then you only need to take a little walk along the coast.

As for entertainment, the Novi Hotel, like most Montenegro hotels, does not offer guests an animation program. Mostly people come here in search of a calm, measured beach holiday away from the bustle of the city. Therefore, this hotel is perfect for family tourists with children of different ages, as well as older people.

Young people, accustomed to a stormy vacation, will be bored here, according to experienced tourists. For this category of travelers it is recommended to choose hotels in Herceg Novi or Budva. In addition, seasoned tourists strongly recommend that anyone who spends their holidays in Montenegro should definitely go on excursions. You can do this both on a rented car, and as part of an excursion group. Moreover, Montenegro can offer a lot of interesting places to visit not only on the Adriatic coast, but also inland (there are the famous Skadar Lake, the Durmitor National Park, the modern and historical capital of the state (Podgorica and Cetinje) and many other attractions).

In general, summing up, we can say that the Novi 3 * hotel (Montenegro, Hercegnovsk Riviera), according to tourists from Russia, is a good place for a measured family holiday on the Adriatic Sea, offering comfortable accommodation and good service.
