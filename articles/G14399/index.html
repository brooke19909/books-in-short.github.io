Surprise for lovely ladies, or How to make tulips out of paper

Men should give flowers to women. And not only on holidays. But what if the man is so small that he does not have money for an expensive bouquet? Or are the stores already closed, but would you like to please your beloved ladies right now? The only answer is to arm yourself with improvised means and think about how to make tulips out of paper.



There are a lot of ways to perform such an crafts. Answers to the question: "How to make tulips out of paper?" - a great variety, flowers can be made in the form of applications, from corrugated cardboard and even from egg cells. But without a doubt, the origami style is most popular. Indeed, with sufficient dexterity (and if you make a whole bouquet, it is acquired quickly), this method allows you to get beautiful voluminous flowers. They can be put in a vase so that they bring joy more than one day.

Origami tulip manufacturing steps

First, let's figure out what it takes to make tulips out of paper:

- colored paper sheet (for stems and leaves - green, for buds - any);

- scissors;

- accessories for decoration.

The hardest thing to do is make a flower bud out of paper. To do this, take red, pink, yellow or any other sheet and perform the following actions with it:

  1. We fold the sheet diagonally, cut off the part that protrudes beyond the triangle. As a result, we should get a square.
  2. We turn the square along a different diagonal, carefully fold all the bends with a ring of scissors.
  3. Now we turn the square in half in the direction opposite to the diagonal folds, expand and bend in half along the other side. The result should be a workpiece with distinct folds along the diagonals and in the middle parallel to each side.
  4. In order for the next step not to have problems with how to make tulips out of paper, it is important to ensure that all folds are carefully ironed. We connect the two sides of the square, while simultaneously folding inward one of the center lines, as shown in the figure.
  5. We put the resulting triangle in an angle opposite the base to ourselves. We bend the upper free corners, connecting in the center. After that, we turn the triangle over and repeat the procedure. The result should be a rhombus.
  6. We insert the left corner into the pocket of the right, iron it. We do the same on the opposite side.
  7. Holding the corner with three fingers, blow into the hole opposite. Filled with air, the craft will resemble a pyramid.

It is unlikely that those who are just learning how to make tulips from paper, the resulting

the design will already resemble a flower. To turn the pyramid into a bud, you should put it on the base and bend the free corners so that the petals turn out. Now we make a stem with leaves from a sheet of green paper, put a bud on it - the tulip is ready.

By the way, the best gift for March 8, which a child can give to mom or grandmother, is a paper craft. A handmade tulip will not only please loved ones and will support the holiday atmosphere, but will also help develop baby's perseverance, accuracy, and fine motor skills.
