How to choose a coffee machine

If it‚Äôs hard for you to imagine a morning without a cup of freshly brewed coffee, sooner or later a coffee machine will appear in your home. But did you know that the taste of the drink and its aroma directly depend on the right choice of device? That is why the purchase should be made carefully, having studied in detail the types of coffee machines on the market.

Filter Coffee Maker

It heats the water to 85-95 degrees, after which the liquid moves drop by drop into a special filter where ground coffee is located. Slowly flowing down the filter, the water manages to absorb the aroma of the grains as much as possible. By the way, the strength of the drink directly depends on the speed of water runoff. The advantages of such a coffee maker from the brand kitchen aide lies in ease of use and attractive cost. Its only drawback is that this technique does not provide for the preparation of a drink with whipped cream.

Espresso coffee makers

Using this technique, you can make not just a standard coffee from ground beans, but your favorite coffee drinks, such as mocha, cappuccino, ristretto. Cooking in this case is carried out due to steam pressure. If you decide an espresso machine from Kitchenaid buy , you need to know that its main advantages are affordable price and the ability to make coffee not only from beans, but also from portioned capsules. The main thing is to be careful during operation and follow the instructions to avoid the risk of burns. In addition, how correctly you tamp the horns in the device will affect the taste of the drink.





Capsule Coffee Machines

Such machines are similar in principle to espresso coffee makers. The difference is only in the source material, because for the preparation of a fragrant drink you will not need ground grains, but special capsules. This is an airtight container filled with a mixture of grains that underwent a roasting process that does not lose its aroma and taste for several months. But despite the amazing taste of the drink, the cost of such capsules is extremely high. In addition, from one such container you can get only one cup of coffee.

Geyser coffee machines

The principle of their work is as follows. Steam enters the vessel with ground grains, which condenses and enters a special compartment. The taste of coffee made in a geyser coffee machine is an order of magnitude higher than in other types. But at the same time, you are always forced to prepare such a portion of coffee that meets the full volume of the coffee pot.
