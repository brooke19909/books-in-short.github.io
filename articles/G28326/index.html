How to distinguish an original from a fake

  • Pay attention to the packaging. The one that Apple does has no images. Just a white box with the inscription of the model. Fakes are stacked in iPhone boxes with a picture of the phone, a series and other inscriptions.

  • Equipment. All refurbished phones are equipped with new headphones, a charger and a cable. In fakes, too, all this is, but without the corporate badge "apple".

  • Appearance. It is very difficult for an ordinary person in appearance to determine where the fake is and where the original is. This can only be done by a professional store or service center employee.
