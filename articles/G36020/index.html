What attracts the country of tourists?

Indonesia is an island nation located between two oceans. Tourism in the country began to develop not so long ago, so travelers who are busy looking for something new and unusual try to come here. A mysterious and unknown country beckons with exotics, rich fauna and flora, a pristine sea coast, and a stunningly beautiful underwater world. No less attractive is a very unusual architecture. Nearby are Buddhist and Hindu temples, beautiful oriental palaces and magnificent mosques.

According to tourists, a vacation in Indonesia attracts with its exotic and novelty. Spoiled travelers come to the country, who have seen a lot of things and are eager for new sensations. Everything is interesting for beginners here. And, of course, a nice addition is a beach holiday. Indonesia in this regard can compete with the famous resorts of the world.

A little bit about the country

If this is your first time going on a vacation to Indonesia, reviews from tourists will help you understand some of the nuances. After all, each country has its own characteristics. Surely you know that Indonesia is the largest island nation in the world. Its territory spread out on both sides of the equator, therefore it is washed by two oceans - the Pacific and the Indian. The islands are located along the equator line between Asia and Australia. According to statistics, the archipelago consists of 18 thousand islands. The largest of them are Borneo, Java, Sumatra, Sulawesi, New Guinea. The capital of the country is the city of Jakarta, located on the island of Java.

All islands are incredibly beautiful. Most of them are covered by plateaus and mountain ranges. Many islands have volcanoes. The archipelago is generally located in a seismically active zone. Imagine that out of 400 volcanoes in Indonesia, 150 are active. Most of the population lives in the hollows between the volcanoes.

Climatic conditions

The equatorial and subequatorial climate reigns on the territory of the archipelago. There is never a change of winter and summer, all year round the weather is warm. On some islands, the existence of a dry and wet season can only be noted. But this is far from characteristic of the whole country. Uniform climatic conditions are provided by warm waters, which make up most of the archipelago. Throughout the year, in coastal areas, air temperature never drops below +28 degrees in the daytime. In the mountains, the indicators are slightly lower - +23 ¬∞ . Indonesia is characterized by a high level of humidity. In the dry period, humidity is 70-80%, and in the wet - 90%.

According to reviews, it is better to plan a vacation in Indonesia for the period from May to September. At this time, dry and sunny weather prevails. During rainy times, the air temperature remains unchanged, but it can rain at night for several hours.

If you are planning your vacation for a certain time, then you should see the approximate forecast in the selected resort in advance. On different islands, weather conditions may vary due to the local microclimate. This is a feature of the archipelago.

If you believe the reviews, a vacation in Indonesia in May is quite comfortable. Daytime air temperature is +33 degrees, night temperature is slightly lower: +24 ¬∞ . In summer, the air temperature in the archipelago decreases. Ocean water is also getting cooler. Air temperature is +30 degrees, and water +29 ¬∞ . According to reviews, for a vacation in Indonesia in June, you should choose Bali. In July and August, the air temperature is within +32 degrees, and water - 29 heat.

A very good time to relax in Indonesia is September. Most of the archipelago has dry and hot weather. Daytime temperature reaches +30 degrees, at night the thermometer is held at +25 ¬∞ C. Water off the coast is very comfortable for swimming. It warms up to +28 degrees. Rainy weather in September reigns only in Sumatra.

When is the best time to go on holiday to Indonesia? The answer depends on the resort you choose. But on average, you should focus on May-September.

The main resort of the country

If you believe the reviews, a vacation in Indonesia is something amazing. The main advantage is a unique opportunity to combine swimming at sea and active entertainment. Local resorts offer interesting hikes in the mountains, boat rides, natural and architectural sightseeing. The unique nature of Indonesia with its unique landscapes is worth your attention. It has many national parks and reserves.

Each island of the archipelago is unique in its own way. Almost everyone has lush tropical forests, mountain ranges and even smoking volcanoes, as well as sandy beaches.

So, which resort to choose for a vacation in Indonesia? The best place many consider the island of Bali. On its territory are the most famous resorts of the country. Many believe that Bali is a real paradise. Here you can escape from your problems and enjoy all the charms. The peculiarity of the island is in close proximity to tropical thickets with sandy beaches. The most famous resorts of Bali, tourists consider Seminyak and Kutu.

The latter, by the way, is considered the largest resort of the island. Kuta is of interest to young people and those people who want to have fun. Tourists note that the beaches of Kuta are very comfortable. But not all places are suitable for swimming. The coastal waters are very turbulent, so there are often waves due to strong currents. For this reason, the resort was chosen by surfers. But shopping lovers are not bored here. Local stores regularly conduct sales.

If you want to find an all-inclusive vacation in Indonesia, you should pay attention to Seminyak. Respectable tourists come here. The resort has a lot of entertainment, as in Kut, but the prices are higher and the conditions are much better.

It is worth recalling another resort. Sanur is another famous holiday destination. The resort's beach is large. Its length reaches five kilometers. On the coast, you can safely swim with children, because it is protected by reefs.

The most fashionable resort of the island is Nusa Dua, located on the Bukit Peninsula. Everything is created for a luxurious vacation - chic restaurants, tropical gardens, pools and shopping centers. There are no noisy merchants on the local beaches, and the sea is clean and warm. But you can swim in it only in the morning and in the evening, because in the afternoon the water leaves.

Reviews about Bali

According to tourists, among the islands for holidays in Indonesia, most often travelers choose Bali. Its resorts are beautifully appointed and offer plenty of entertainment. In addition, here you can find not only fashionable establishments, but also more budget places. Tourists are invariably delighted by the beautiful sandy beaches and lush vegetation. It is not for nothing that Bali was called the paradise island.

The best hotels on the island are located on the coast. Such establishments have their own beaches. For fans of surfing, there are camps. By the way, young people most often visit Kutu because of its discos, bars and parties. Older people in Bali are attracted by stunning excursions to volcanoes, parks, temples, and the botanical garden. After all, rest is not only the beach and the sea. According to tourists, Bali is ideal for outdoor activities.

Other resorts of the country

Sumatra is another popular island of Indonesia. Rest on it can be combined with an excursion program. There are many temples and palaces. Experienced tourists recommend visiting Lake Toba, a waterfall, as well as nature parks. Active travelers can go diving, climbing and trekking here.

Komodo Island, which is often called the island of dragons, is known for the eponymous national park. People come to him to admire the Komodo lizards that look like dragons. There are no hotels or shops here. You can get to the island only as part of the tour.

Many people know the island of Borneo. But it is not very favorable for visiting due to the unstable situation. Previously, it was of interest to lovers of ancient monuments.

Java is probably the most visited after Bali. It is cultural. the political and administrative center of Indonesia. On its territory there are two active volcanoes, temple complexes and a national reserve.

According to tourists, the most famous resort of the island is Bogor. The city attracts with its natural landscapes and cultural attractions. No less famous is the mountain resort of Bandug with its hot springs and tea plantations.

Hotels

According to reviews, a vacation in Indonesia is very comfortable. Not the last role in this is played by the excellent hotels of the country. Tourists note that local hotels are very comfortable.

They fully meet the stated stardom. At local resorts you will find 3 *, 4 * and 5 * hotels. By the way, many fives have a deluxe level. In all hotels you will appreciate the cleanliness and comfort. It is not customary to drink tap water. Bottled water is provided for guests. There are hotels in the country's resorts that offer all-inclusive meals. These include the following institutions: Alila Villas 5 *, Samabe Bali Suites & Vilas 5 *, Rimba 5 *, Grand Hyatt 5 * and others.

Local resorts offer not only prestigious hotels, but also more modest options. In Jakarta, for example, you can find accommodation for every taste: mini-hotels, hostels, guest houses. Among the hotel complexes there are establishments belonging to the famous international chains. At local resorts, villas, cottages and bungalows are in demand. By the way, in Bali you can even stay at the campsite. In general, the choice of housing depends on your preferences and financial capabilities.

Entertainment

Indonesia is a world of exoticism and entertainment. At local resorts, tourists can find a variety of entertainment. In Bali, the main centers of nightlife are concentrated. The most famous resort in this regard is Kuta. Bars, clubs and restaurants are open until morning. Kuta is considered a youth recreation center.

People fly to Indonesia primarily for a beach holiday. Stunning beaches with white and black sand, tropical greenery and reefs attract thousands of tourists. Resorts for divers are no less attractive. After all, there is an amazing opportunity to admire the picturesque corals of the Indian and Pacific Ocean. Very beautiful underwater gardens. Divers usually seek to visit the island of Sulawesi, which has a marine reserve. Experienced tourists also recommend visiting Jakarta, the island of Sanur, Lombok, Padang Bay. These places have long been chosen by divers.

Indonesia resorts are good for surfers, as there are big waves on the islands throughout the archipelago. Most often, for surfing, choose the coast of the islands of Java, Sumatra and Bali. For real professionals, there are very interesting places where not everyone will be able to curb the wave.

According to tourists, the most interesting for guests of the resorts are numerous attractions. There are a lot of them on every island. Tourists have a choice: visit national parks or focus on architecture. The culture and nature of Indonesia is an inexhaustible source of positive emotions.
