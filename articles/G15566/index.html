Hotel "Khutorok" (Domodedovo): description, photo

Everyone who wants to actively relax in the suburbs should go to the outskirts of the capital, in the famous district of Domodedovo. The Khutorok Hotel is located in a calm and ecologically clean place, not far from the international airport. To ensure the comfort of guests there is a bathhouse, billiards, restaurant, pool. For the preparation of barbecue, a specially equipped area in the courtyard.





Khutorok Hotel (Domodedovo): Acquaintance

The hotel is located in the Domodedovo district by the address: Kashirskoye Shosse, 44th km, ow. 1. Having traveled some 15 km from Moscow, the traveler finds himself in a real fairy-tale world. The Khutorok Hotel (Domodedovo) was built from environmentally friendly materials. A dense pine forest and a light birch grove lie nearby. The hotel has created a large number of interesting landscape and architectural compositions. From early spring to late autumn, the Khutorok Hotel (Domodedovo) delights visitors with the lush flowering of more than 700 species of ornamental shrubs and various flowers.

Accommodation, services, reviews

The Khutorok Hotel (Domodedovo) offers comfortable rooms of various categories to accommodate visitors, from standard to junior suites. Clean, comfortable, cozy rooms are equipped with showers, there is audio and video equipment, Internet is not provided in the rooms.

According to visitors, here you can have a wonderful rest. Everyone who wants to improve their health, enjoy tasty and healthy food, live surrounded by a lush green forest, the authors of numerous reviews recommend visiting the Khutorok Hotel (Domodedovo).
