Thermal springs, Krasnodar Territory. Recreation at the thermal springs

Among the many attractions of the Krasnodar Territory, it is worth paying attention to the mineral and thermal springs. Heated water emerging from the very bowels of the earth, saturated with various salts, has a positive effect on the whole organism. Origin

In places of active volcanic activity or in areas of accumulation of certain rocks, deep heating of groundwater occurs. Through faults in the underground crust, water flows rush out. Open sources with hot water are called geothermal. These include keys heated over twenty degrees. Three types of sources are distinguished depending on the water temperature:

  • 37-50 ¬∞ C - hot;

The healing properties of hot keys

The fact that groundwater at the exit endowed with healing power was known in ancient times. Plunging into them, people were cured of various ailments, strengthened immunity. Mineral water has an incredible effect on the skin and hair. After several visits to thermal springs, the skin becomes smooth, supple and refreshed. And hair gets extraordinary softness and healthy shine.

Today, rest on thermal springs continues to be in demand and popular. It includes not only therapeutic activities, but also air baths, hiking, sports, visiting local attractions and active pastimes.

Thermal waters - an effective treatment

Underground mineral keys are rich in dissolved salts, which help in the complex treatment of many diseases. Thermal springs (Krasnodar Territory) should be regularly visited by those people who have:

  • nervous exhaustion of the body or often get into stressful situations;
  • deviations in the cardiovascular system;
  • diseases of the genitourinary sphere;
  • problems with the musculoskeletal system;
  • metabolic disease;
  • respiratory diseases.

Many minerals dissolved in hot water contain in their composition the active forms of such chemical elements as iodine, bromine, calcium, sodium. Due to the richness and variety of salts, thermal water has a positive effect on the overall well-being of the body.

Thermal springs of Stavropol

The village of Suvorovskaya, named after the great commander, is located near the famous resort cities of the Caucasus. In the middle of the last century, a source of healing thermal water was discovered here. The well reaches a depth of over 1200 meters.

Suvorov thermal springs are absolutely unique in water composition. The rich mineral composition of healing moisture allows you to heal patients with a wide variety of diseases and problems. Thermal treatments for skin diseases (burns, ulcers, eczema, psoriasis, dermatosis, scars) give a particularly good effect.

The treatment process was called "Suvorov baths." They differ from traditional washing in that they pass without a steam room and brooms. Hot alkaline water is supplied from sources to ordinary baths, which patients take.

Suvorov thermal waters are also prescribed for oral administration. They perfectly cure diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, kidneys, gall bladder, metabolic disorders.

A high percentage of recovery and quick recovery of the body provides Suvorov sources with steady popularity among residents of not only Russia but also the countries of the former CIS.

Keys of Transcarpathia

Thermal springs (Krasnodar Territory) have water properties very similar to the mineral springs of Western Ukraine. In Transcarpathia, there are three spa complexes where you can go through a full course of therapeutic procedures with healing waters. They are located in the village of Veliatino in the Khust region and in the cities of Kosino and Beregovo. Thermal springs in each of them have some differences. The temperature level of Transcarpathian waters ranges from 30 to 80 degrees.

In Beregovo there is a pool filled with a geyser. The source well has been drilled to a depth of over one kilometer. Here, the water temperature is always within 50 ¬∞ C.

Mineral-rich water contains substances that actively inhibit the activity of pathogenic microbes. No filter systems are used in the pool, so the water looks cloudy and not very attractive. But it has such a strong effect on the body that stay in it is recommended to limit it to one or two hours a day.

Thermal resorts of Russia

Hot spring vacations are popular all over the world. The most attractive winter immersion in a hot spring. In Russia, thermal keys are located in various parts of the country.

Everyone knows the famous Kamchatka geysers, which allow you to plunge into their waters under the open sky at any time of the year. Among them there are sources, arranged and ennobled, and there are also untouched, in its original form.

Swimming in the hot springs of Transbaikalia will be no less extreme in the winter. The combination of therapeutic procedures with skiing gives a charge of vivacity and good mood for a long time.

Altai Territory is pleased to offer everyone a radon water coming from many underground sources. Mineral mud and blue clay are used here as additional treatment methods.
