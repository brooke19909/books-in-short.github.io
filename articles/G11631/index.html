Dinler Hotel 5 * (Kargicak, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Turkey is an exotic country that attracts tourists with its vibrant and unusual nature. Every year a huge number of tourists from Russia and the CIS countries come here to spend time with family, friends or with their soulmate. It is not surprising that several very popular resort areas have formed in the state, one of which is Alanya.

In this region there are a huge number of hotels. Among them, Dinler Hotel 5 * boasts special popularity. Reviews about it can be found on various sites, and they are usually good, and in some cases even enthusiastic.

Location

This hotel is located a short distance from one of the most important entertainment centers in Turkey - the city of Alanya, in the coastal town of Kargicak. With regard to proximity to transport hubs, the airport closest to Dinler Hotel 5 * (Kargicak) is in Antalya, which is 140 km away.

The hotel harmoniously combines with the beauty of the surrounding nature, represented by sand, exotic vegetation, rocky rocks and endless expanses of the sea. It stands almost on the coast of the clean and warm Mediterranean Sea, which is very popular with guests.

As for the climatic situation in Turkey, it undoubtedly contributes to the development of tourism in different regions of the country and in Alanya as well. It is warm all year round, but the height of the holiday season begins in the period from May to October - at this time, tourists can enjoy the hot temperature, which is mitigated by the Mediterranean air currents, allowing travelers not to languish from the heat.



general information

In the description of Dinler Hotel 5 * (Kargicak) on various sites it is said that this resting place was built in 1997. Since then, on its territory and in the buildings, various types of repair work are regularly carried out, with the help of which the hotel maintains its beautiful appearance, and with each reconstruction it acquires an increasingly modern look. One of the last major repairs was made in 2015, at that time some adjustments were also made to the rules for servicing tourists - it was improved.

The recreation area is highly rated by international standards: the Dinler Hotel rating is 5 *. This means that the hotel has met all the conditions that are necessary for a comfortable stay of people. The entire territory of the resting place occupies 15,000 square meters. m, which is enough for the location of a large building, as well as some entertainment in the open.

Rooms

The Dinler Hotel 5 * fund (Turkey) has a total of 215 apartments, each of which offers its guests a wonderful view of the endless Mediterranean Sea. All of them are divided into two categories: comfort and family suite. All of them are located in a large modern six-story building.

Each apartment has a private bathroom, which has absolutely all amenities: a bath with shower, sink, as well as a bathroom. There are also cabinets for storing bath accessories and linen. Guests have the opportunity to use a hairdryer to dry their hair.

The hotel also has three rooms that are designed for disabled guests. They differ from ordinary apartments in that in their decor everything is done taking into account the limited physical capabilities of the guests who live in them. The furniture installed here is made to order, according to European standards.

Comfort

Dinler Hotel 5 * (Kargicak) has 200 comfort rooms, each of which is located on a small area - 21 square meters. m. Each of them has a private balcony on which you can relax, sunbathe and enjoy the beautiful view of the sea and the surrounding nature.

This class of apartments has modern furniture in a harmonious combination of white and brown. There is a large double bed with orthopedic mattress, bedside tables, as well as a long wardrobe for storing personal belongings, the length of the entire wall. Above it is a plasma TV, on which you can watch interesting programs broadcast via satellite TV. If necessary, guests can use the safe, telephone and minibar, but these services are provided for a fee.

Many rooms of this level have an extra bed in the form of a small bed for one person.

Conditions for children

Tourists who relax in the Dinler Hotel 5 * (Kargicak) often note that this hotel is well-oriented for family vacations. In his room fund there are one and a half dozen apartments, which are arranged in the best way so that family members can relax together. On the territory of the resting place, everything is done taking into account the safety requirements of the child.

Among other things, kids here always have something to do. First of all, a playground has been built for them, on which you can actively spend your time. Children also have the opportunity to carry out water procedures in the pools, one of which is built on the street, and the second is in the main building.

Parents can send their child to a well-equipped kids club, where it is always possible to watch cartoons, games, and chat. Here they will be looked after by professional animators, which, by the way. speak Russian. This club in Dinler Hotel 5 * (Kargicak) is open every day from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. (from noon to 3 p.m. there is a break here).

Entertainment for adults

Adult guests of the hotel never have to get bored, as for them entertainment events are regularly held here. In particular, every evening in an open area near the pool, a disco begins, which continues until late at night. At this time, guests can dance to incendiary hits performed by local DJs. Dinler Hotel 5 * (Turkey) has a well-coordinated team of professional animators who can entertain guests of different ages - it consists of 7 people.

As for morning entertainment, they are also available. In particular, tourists can take part in sports and entertainment events, which are also held near the pool.

Nutrition

As for meals at the Dinler Hotel 5 * (Kargicak), for tourists who relax on an all-inclusive basis, it is completely free. A large and beautiful Turkish restaurant serves a daily buffet for them, where you can have a delicious and hearty lunch, breakfast or dinner. In order to visit this institution, you need to pre-reserve the table for yourself and your companions.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has five bars, which are located in the building, near the pool and on the beach. In these places, tourists are offered light hot and cold snacks, desserts, as well as soft drinks and ice cream.



Pool

Tourists relaxing in the hotel are very fond of doing water procedures, and then sunbathing in the sun. For such purposes, there is a well-equipped summer terrace, where there is a large pool of 160 square meters. m. It is filled with clean fresh water, it is daily cleaned. There is no heating in this pool.



There is a sun terrace around the outdoor pool. It has a large number of comfortable sun loungers, as well as beach umbrellas, which save vacationers from heat and sunstroke. There is also a small-sized bar where you can relax and enjoy a cocktail.

Sport

The infrastructure of the Dinler Hotel 5 * hotel allows vacationers who love a sporting lifestyle to exercise their health and figure, even during vacation. For such purposes, there is an excellent gym with an excellent set of training equipment for different muscle groups. In addition, on the territory of this recreation area there are several specialized venues arranged for specific team games: football, basketball, volleyball, there is even a beautiful tennis court. Inventory for any game can be taken at the hotel free of charge.

Guests can visit the billiards and table tennis halls, which are located in the main building, if desired.

For business

Hotel guests, whose life is directly connected with doing business, can do business right during their holidays. For such purposes, there are three wonderful conference rooms, each of which is ideal for negotiating, presenting and conducting business meetings with business partners.

Each hall is equipped with all necessary sound and lighting equipment, which is modern and high-quality. It also has quality furniture: lacquered tables made of dark wood and leather chairs. From technology there are also various players, projectors, laptops and microphones. In the arsenal there are also office supplies.

Beach

Hotel guests love to be on the local beach, which is located directly on the territory of the Dinler Hotel 5 *. This beach is completely covered with soft fine sand and small pebbles. The descent into the water here is gentle, and the depth of the sea grows gradually - this is great for young children, since such conditions provide them with the most safe rest.

The sand and pebble area for recreation is well-maintained: there are several changing cabins, showers, and also public toilets. The whole area is furnished with comfortable white chaise lounges, as well as umbrellas - they can be used absolutely free. If desired, tourists can sit on a wooden pier, which is also available here. It also has a considerable number of sun loungers.

As for beach activities at Dinler Hotel 5 *, they are represented by the possibility of extreme parachuting, water skiing, and scuba diving. If you wish, you can rent a luxury yacht or boat and make a short trip along the waves of the Mediterranean Sea. If desired, in a special paragraph, you can take an air mattress and just lie on the water.



Additional services

Service at Dinler Hotel 5 * (Kargicak) provides the ability to provide a wide range of additional services that are not included in the price of the trip. One of the most popular are dry cleaning and laundry services, along with which there is also the possibility of ironing. In addition, tourists can take the opportunity to call a doctor, which is done on a fee basis.

If you wish, guests can rent a car - you can go on it to explore local attractions. In this case, a parking space for such a car will be provided free of charge.

For those who wish to carry out certain cosmetic procedures or use the services of a hairdresser, they can go to the beauty salon, which is on the ground floor of the building. Not far from it is a currency exchange, several ATMs and shops.



Prices

As for the prices for tickets to this hotel, they vary depending on the region of Russia from which the flight will be made. So, for residents of Moscow a weekly stay alone in apartments of the comfort category will cost about 60,000 rubles. Most tour operators guarantee the inclusion in this amount of a flight from Moscow to Antalya and vice versa, airport transfers, room rental, meals, as well as the necessary insurance premiums. As tourists themselves note, such a cost is more than acceptable for the conditions provided.
