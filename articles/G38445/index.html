The Queen of Montenegro Hotel 4 * (Montenegro, Budva Riviera, Becici): description, service, reviews

Montenegro is a beautiful country, just perfect for spending your summer vacation. And its most important resort is considered to be Budva. Mostly those tourists who prefer a perky, active vacation go to this city. Vacationers can stay in Budva with great comfort. There are many hotels offering comfortable rooms. For example, many four-star hotel The Queen of Montenegro Hotel 4 * is highly praised.

Where is the hotel?

This comfortable hotel was built in the suburb of Budva - in the village of Becici. It is located almost at the very top of a picturesque hill, from which a magnificent view of the local four-kilometer beach opens. Budva is just 4 km from the hotel. So, guests of the complex can reach this city by public transport in just a few minutes. Buses to Budva stop nearby every 10 minutes.





Some guests of The Queen of Montenegro Hotel 4 * even go to this city and just walk. With a calm step, such a walk usually takes no more than 50 minutes.

Quite close to this hotel is the well-known island of St. Stephen, where celebrities and rich people like to relax.

Bechichi is only about 25 km from the nearest Tivat airport . That is, guests of The Queen of Montenegro Hotel 4 * can get from here quickly enough. The easiest way to get to the hotel for tourists arriving by plane is by taxi. Unfortunately, public transport does not go to Tivat airport. If necessary, you can also order a transfer at the hotel.

The Queen of Montenegro Hotel: hotel description

This hotel was built in Becici in 2006. Later, cosmetic repairs were carried out here. This complex was originally called Queen Of Montenegro Hotel Becici. Residents here are taken in a large multi-story building of a modern building. The territory of this hotel is quite small, but judging by the reviews of travelers, it is very beautiful and neat.





Reception at this hotel is open around the clock. Smoking and pets are not allowed in the hotel. In addition to cash, tourists can pay with cards for various additional services at this hotel.

Despite the fact that Budva is mostly a youth resort, the Queen of Montenegro complex itself is suitable, according to many tourists, best for families with children or senior citizens. But of course, young people can feel quite comfortable here .

Hotel Rooms

Hotel T he Queen of Montenegro Hotel 4 * - quite large. In total, a little less than 250 rooms of different price categories are equipped here. The rooms at the hotel include the following amenities:

  • air conditioning;
  • safe;
  • TV;
  • wired Internet;
  • desk.

Of course, in every room of the hotel there are also shower rooms. Also, all rooms have a balcony or terrace. It costs accommodation in a hotel of about 50,000 p. per week per person. Most of the rooms in this hotel are standard rooms. There are also suites and family rooms.

Road to the sea

On the sites of most tour operators there is information that the Queen of Montenegro Hotel 4 * is located on the first coastline. However, this is not quite true. To get to the beach, guests of this hotel must first cross the road across the bridge. Next you should go down the stairs. After all, the hotel is located, as already mentioned, on a hill. When choosing this method, the guests of the complex can reach the beach in about 5 minutes.

Tourists with young children and strollers will hardly be able to go down the stairs or climb it. But there is also a detour to the beach from the hotel. There are no stairs here. Walking along this road is also quite convenient, but the path to the sea when it is selected will take about 7 minutes.

sights

The Queen of Montenegro Hotel (Montenegro) , among other things, has a tour desk. However, experienced vacationers do not advise taking all sorts of trips to the hotel. It will be much cheaper to buy a tour just on the street. There are not too many attractions in Budva. But the nature in Montenegro is very beautiful. Experienced tourists are advised to beginners to travel around the outskirts of Budva.

The most interesting places of this city are:

  • the old city of Budva;
  • Citadel;
  • sculpture "Dancer from Budva";
  • Cathedral of John the Baptist;
  • Church of the Holy Trinity.

Tourists can also visit the Podmaine Monastery in Budva. This Orthodox monastery was founded in the XI-XII century. It is from here, according to legend, that Savva Serbian went on a pilgrimage to Palestine. Among other things, hotel guests should definitely visit the Budva City Museum and the Mogren Fortress.

St. Stephen's Island

Tourists can reach this attraction from The Queen of Montenegro, either by public transport or on foot. True, in the latter case, the walk will take quite a lot of time. If you stand facing the sea, then you will need to go to the island to the left. On the way, tourists can enjoy the truly magnificent views of the Budva Riviera . Also, if desired, you can visit the beaches for the rich. But the entrance here is quite expensive. You will need to pay at least $ 50 to visit such a beach .
