Best hotels in Mozyr (Belarus): names and addresses, reviews of tourists

Mozyr is the second largest city in the Gomel region. It has several city-forming enterprises, the largest of which is an oil refinery. Many business travelers come here, so the question of their accommodation is relevant. Mozyr Hotels are pleased to offer all guests comfortable living conditions.

Hotel complex "Pripyat"

The hotel is located in the central part of the city, on the right bank of the Pripyat River. There are two supermarkets within walking distance. City attractions, the Monastery Castle and the Cathedral of the Archangel Michael are only 400 meters away. You can relax and take a walk in your free time in the nearby Victory Culture and Recreation Park. The hotel's own restaurant serves Russian and national cuisine. Wireless internet is free. For guests with their own car, a private guarded parking space is available upon reservation for a fee. If you are interested in Mozyr hotels with facilities for people with disabilities, then the Pripyat hotel is a good option. For a fee, guests can use dry cleaning and laundry services.

The hotel offers several categories of rooms:

  • single economy - 735 rubles;
  • single, suitable for disabled people - 991 rubles per day;
  • single standard - 1387 rubles per day;
  • double economy - 1630 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 2365 rubles per day;
  • double standard Suite - 3612 rubles per day.

Price is for accommodation only. Upon request, you can pay for the room including breakfast. All of their windows offer beautiful views of the river or of the sights. An elevator is available to access the upper floors. The Pripyat Hotel in Mozyr is located at Proletarskaya Street, 8. The reception is open 24 hours a day.
