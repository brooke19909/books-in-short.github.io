Hotel Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 * (Kemer, Turkey): description, photos and reviews of tourists

A luxury option for a sea holiday - Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 *. Here, guests can experience the Turkish hospitality, as well as high standards of service. Thanks to the beautiful design, spacious grounds, numerous services and vibrant entertainment, your vacation will be the best.





Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 *: description

The hotel has a huge area, which is almost 100,000 square meters. m. Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 * is surrounded by densely growing pine trees that exude a pleasant coniferous aroma and create a life-giving shade. It is worth noting the fact that two years ago the hotel underwent a complete reconstruction, as a result of which the number of rooms and the adjacent territory were completely updated.

It is worth noting that the Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 * hotel consists of many separate bungalows, each of which is equipped with several guest rooms. In addition, the territory is combined with a neighboring hotel. Thus, you will have many places for walking and even more opportunities for entertainment and relaxation.





Where is the hotel

It is worth noting the convenient location of the resort hotel Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 *. Turkey, Kemer, Goynuk district, Ahu Unal Aisal street, 07994 - the exact address of this institution. This is the first line relative to the sea (about 150 meters). The distance from Antalya International Airport is just over 50 km.

Types of Guest Apartments

In accordance with the most modern standards, the Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 * hotel operates. You will be provided with the most comfortable accommodation in guest rooms of the following categories:

  • The two-story bungalows feature comfortable garden room apartments. These are standard rooms with windows overlooking the garden (as it becomes clear from the name of the category). This is a fairly spacious and bright bedroom, which is designed to accommodate two adult guests. It is also possible to accommodate one additional guest. Thanks to the restoration carried out in 2015, the rooms look modern and fresh.
  • Kimeros room - these are comfortable rooms with sea views located in a three-story building, renovated in early 2016. The rooms are large and designed as a studio. In addition to the bedroom, the room also has a living room and a small dining area.
  • Family rooms are the same in all buildings of the resort complex. They consist of two bedrooms, one of which is for adults, and the second for children. The rooms are combined with a door and have a common bathroom. The rooms overlook the blooming garden and mountains.

It is worth noting that the Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 * hotel is in rather great demand. Room reservation is carried out for several months in advance. You can reserve apartments through the official website or specialized resources.

Catering establishments

Many catering establishments operate on the territory of Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 *. Photos of restaurants and bars show a rather cozy atmosphere, elegant design and elegant decorative elements. Nevertheless, all these characteristics fade before the quality of food and drinks that are served here. To get acquainted with Turkish culinary traditions and just have a good time, it is recommended to visit the following places:

  • In the 24-hour pool bar, you will always have access to a huge assortment of drinks: from green tea to whiskey and aromatic liquors. The bartender at the counter, located in the water, prepares delicious tropical cocktails. And of course, light snacks are served.
  • One of the hotel's favorite establishments is a cozy snack bar. Here you can have a meal during the day with your favorite fast food. These are hamburgers, and fried potatoes, and pizza, and ice cream, and much more. Also, do not forget about the wide range of refreshing drinks.
  • The disco bar is open-air and opens at night. Clockwork music plays here, which makes guests go to the dance floor and dance until you drop. Here, guests can try a huge number of cocktails and original snacks.
  • There is a cozy cafe on the beach where guests can always have a drink and eat. For those who enjoy spending days at sea, delicious grilled dishes are served here.
  • The vitamin bar will certainly appeal to those guests who lead an active lifestyle and are accustomed to proper nutrition. Here you can always find clean drinking water, fresh fruit juices and healthy foods.
  • The restaurant serves buffet meals. Between the main meals, as well as in the evening, guests can order from the menu.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, not everything is as smooth in resort hotels as guests would like. Given the huge flow of tourists, the flaws that may arise in the Kimeros Park Holiday Village 5 * hotel look quite natural. Reviews in 2016, in addition to positive ones, also have negative ones. They are as follows:

  • due to a poor-quality mixer, it is quite difficult to regulate not only the temperature, but also the pressure of the water;
  • bed linens are rarely changed;
  • if you live in a room on the ground floor, then be prepared for the fact that your windows will be at the level of human growth, which constantly makes the impression that someone is spying on you;
  • the hotel hosts a disco almost all night, and the sound of loud music makes it difficult to sleep even if your windows are closed;
  • gardeners constantly spray lawns and green spaces with poison from insects, which is why many guests have allergic reactions;
  • in the rooms you can often find ants;
  • pebbles are very large on the beach, and the entry into the sea is too steep;
  • if you arrived at the hotel in the morning, then you most likely will not be able to take advantage of the opportunity of early registration, because all rooms are busy at this time;
  • surely hide all your valuables, money, documents and expensive equipment in a safe, as there are cases of theft in the hotel (even if you complain to the administrator, then most likely this business will not be solved);
  • the maids either deliberately or deliberately bring linen, towels and cosmetics to the room only for the main guests, and you have to constantly remind about the additional guest;
  • the restaurant has a lot of people, besides there are not enough tables;
  • very bad smell in the sauna;
  • You can normally use wireless Internet only at night, when most of the guests are sleeping (or you need to look for less crowded places);
  • there is not enough food for children in the restaurant, because everything is very fatty and spicy;
  • often in the restaurant for dinner they serve those products that guests did not eat at dinner.

General impression of the hotel

This hotel can be called quite worthy, and the cost of the services provided is consistent with their quality. Perhaps the most pleasant moment can be considered friendly service. Russian-speaking administrators are very polite with guests and try to solve all problems that arise as soon as possible. Nevertheless, if we are talking about such uncomfortable moments as, for example, theft in rooms, then they will all let go of the brakes.

The hotel has recently undergone a complete renovation, and therefore everything is new and very beautiful in the rooms and on the territory. The garden and the pine grove deserve special attention. Perhaps the only disappointment is the beach. It is strewn with large pebbles, and the entrance to the sea is too steep, which makes it difficult for children, as well as those guests who do not swim very well.
