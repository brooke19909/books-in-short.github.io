Hotel "Falcon" in Suzdal

Elegant hotel "Sokol" is located in a historic building with architecture of the XIX century, in a former luxurious merchant estate. The hotel is in the heart of glorious Suzdal. It is adjacent to the walls of the famous Suzdal Kremlin, a magnificent city square and the Gostiny Dvor shopping center. From the upper floors of the hotel, you can clearly see Suzdal. Sokol Hotel is within walking distance of local attractions.

The hotel, which underwent a major renovation in 2013, is served by friendly staff. The equipment of spacious apartments fully meets the requirements of the present. They are equipped with refrigerators, air conditioning, TVs and private bathrooms. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.





The restaurant serves breakfast buffet for guests. Lunches and dinners are held on the principle of A La Carte - guests choose dishes from the proposed menu. Tables are served in two spacious rooms and on the summer terrace (in season). The chef pampers guests with mouth-watering dishes from European and Russian cuisines.

Monasteries, temple complexes and churches abound in Suzdal. Hotel "Sokol" helps to organize wedding ceremonies, themed celebrations and significant festivities. It often hosts corporate events.

The staff organizes excursions to natural and cultural-historical monuments. The budget hotel in Suzdal "Sokol" offers to visit the museums of the city, go for a ride in horse-drawn carriage, go horse riding, ride a river tram along Kamenka, relax in a Russian bath.

Apartments at the Sokol Hotel

A cozy hotel in Suzdal "Falcon" is equipped with double superior rooms and luxury apartments. Double rooms are formed by a room with a bedroom and a living area, a bathroom. The room is furnished with two beds, a desk, armchairs with a coffee table, wardrobe, TV. There is a kettle and a telephone in it. In the bathroom combined with the toilet, guests will find a hairdryer, a set of towels and hygiene products.

Luxury apartments are furnished with double beds, tables: dressing, work and coffee, wardrobe. They are equipped with a TV, telephone and kettle. They are equipped with two bathrooms with a hairdryer, towels and toiletries - in the guest room there is a toilet room, the main one is equipped with a bathroom with a toilet.
