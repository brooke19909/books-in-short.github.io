Hotel Vrissiana Beach Hotel 4 * in Protaras: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Protaras is a beautiful city on the island of Cyprus, which is famous for its extraordinary culture, architecture and natural sites. Many tourists, especially those who want to learn new facts during the trip, come here for a good vacation, and often with their families.

Travel agencies always have several hotel options that can be offered to tourists for a vacation, one of them is Vrissiana Beach Hotel, which differs from all others in its unusual hull - it is made in the form of a large boat. This appearance attracts the attention of tourists, which they often write about in their comments.



general information

The date of construction of the hotel is considered to be 1985, however, despite this, at the moment it has a fairly modern appearance, as well as technical equipment inside. All this was achieved thanks to regular repair and restoration work aimed at improving the living conditions of all guests. One of the last major renovations was completed in 2008, after which he was assigned a new category - "4 stars".

Location

Vrissiana Beach Hotel is located in Protaras in the very center of it. There are many places for entertainment that can be visited both in the daytime and at night. On the one hand, the hotel borders on urban infrastructure, harmoniously combined with green tropical plants, and on the other, it opens up to a warm and clear sea.

Tourists who arrive in Cyprus begin their journey from the airport of Larnaca, which is located at a distance of 35 km from the hotel. That is why in any tour package purchased by a traveler, a transfer service is mandatory included - the hotel provides it in a small car with air conditioning. Guests respond to this decision very positively, since in this case you can get to the place of rest as quickly as possible, and the travel time passes almost imperceptibly.

Rooms

The room fund offers guests 138 apartments, each of which offer superior comfort conditions. Each room has a private bathroom, which is equipped with a full set of plumbing, which includes a bath or shower, sink and bathroom. All the plumbing presented here is made of high-quality ceramic and looks very expensive. Guests also note in their reviews of Vrissiana Beach Hotel that the lavatory has modern white wardrobes, where you can find a set of bathrobes and towels, as well as bath and shower facilities. All guests are also provided with an individual pair of slippers.



Since the last renovation, each room has a separate air conditioning, which, regardless of the weather conditions that are established on the street, can maintain the air temperature in the room at an optimal level.

Interior Description

Hotel guests often in their reviews mention the beauty of the interior decoration of the rooms. In them, they indicate that the style of the interior was chosen very well - the classic gives the overall picture a certain luxury. The overall color scheme is represented by a harmonious combination of white, beige and blue shades, which set up a romantic atmosphere, allowing you to relax and have a good time.



In the rooms everything is designed for two guests, which can be clearly seen in the furniture set, which is installed here. Central to the interior is a large double bed, which is covered with expensive hypoallergenic linen and covered with an orthopedic mattress. Near it there are a pair of pedestals and a landline telephone that is connected to an international line - you must pay for its use at the established rate. The apartments for brightening up leisure time are equipped with a small plasma TV, which is connected to a satellite broadcasting system. There is also a dressing table with a mirror and a chair.

Each room has a private terrace, where tourists are very fond of sunbathing and admiring beautiful views of the Mediterranean Sea and natural landscapes. The terrace is furnished - it has a small table and a pair of soft armchairs, in which, as guests note, it is very convenient to read books or drink hot drinks prepared using the tea substation installed in the room.

Some apartments have a jacuzzi on the terrace. These rooms are located on the top floor.



Conditions for children

Vrissiana Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus) is often called a family-type hotel. Such reviews suggest that tourists often relax in it with their families, which include small children. Representatives of this category of guests often respond positively about spending time here, which is due to the presence of a huge number of conditions for children and their parents.

If necessary, a crib is provided for free in any apartment, which is designed for young tourists under the age of 12 years. Another convenient circumstance noted by parents is the availability of professional babysitting services, which are paid and provided around the clock.

In the open area for kids there is a small pool in which everything is arranged taking into account safety precautions: it is shallow and there is a coating that prevents the child from slipping.

Nutrition

In a huge number of comments about Vrissiana Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus, Protaras), great emphasis is placed on issues related to the nutrition of guests. In them, tourists often say that only professional chefs work in the hotel, who are ready to surprise even the most sophisticated gourmets with their talent.

Vrissiana Beach Hotel has one large restaurant with all-inclusive service. It is here that a daily buffet is served, which can be visited three times a day. According to the guests, they serve fish dishes that are unique in their taste qualities - many recommend tasting them as soon as possible. The menu also offers dishes prepared in the best traditions of the Mediterranean and Europe.

In addition to the restaurant, there are also four bars, which offer huge cocktail and bar cards, as well as a considerable selection of appetizers, salads and original desserts.



Sport

The main building has a large, well-equipped gym, which guests tend to visit in order to maintain their health and body in shape. A visit to it is free, which is especially pleasing to tourists. They also note that, if desired, competitions can be arranged here for team games, which are easily organized in open areas. Tourists in their reviews note that the hotel has a beautiful tennis court, the rental of which is paid on an hourly basis.

Services

In their reviews, travelers note that Vrissiana Beach Hotel (Cyprus) offers a first-class service that helps to increase the comfort of guests of different ages. That is why they are provided with a wide range of additional features. Among them, the possibility of renting cars and bicycles is in particular demand. Guests recommend using it to everyone, since it is on your own transport that you can go to any place in the city and explore it.

Often guests use dry cleaning and laundry services, which, according to their marks, have a very reasonable price. The hotel also has a medical office where the doctor is on duty around the clock - his services are paid, you can call a specialist on request.

If necessary, guests whose visit is business can use the services of a business center, which provides not only the equipment needed for work, but also the opportunity to conduct business meetings, presentations and negotiations. It should be noted that this service is also incredibly popular in Vrissiana Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras), as it is often visited by those people whose life is connected with doing business.

Completely free of charge, guests can get advice on any matters of interest related to accommodation and the provision of services. They are carried out by administrators, which can be found at the front desk at any time of the day or night. Here you can ask to call a taxi or order the service "Alarm", which is in demand at Vrissiana Beach Hotel.



Beach

A fan of relaxation by the sea will not leave indifferent the sunny sandy shore of the Mediterranean Sea, which is located near the hotel. Guests of Vrissiana Beach Hotel (Cyprus, Protaras) very often notice that the coastline is perfectly landscaped for relaxation, public showers, toilets, and also changing cabins are evenly placed on it.

If desired, guests who visit it can rent sun loungers, beach umbrellas, as well as towels. There are also many types of water entertainments along the coast, most of which are extreme: diving, water skiing or jet skiing, parachuting. Tourists really like them and are in great demand among hotel guests. For such sports, the rental of equipment or the services of an instructor is mandatory.



Prices

The cost of a trip to Vrissiana Beach Hotel (Protaras), according to travelers, is on average about 80,000 rubles, provided that the guests will relax together for a week. Without fail, the tour operator includes the cost of a two-way flight for two in this package, as well as a transfer. For an additional fee, guests can add to these conditions the desired number of excursions to interesting places, but those who have already visited the Vrissiana Beach Hotel in Cyprus (Protaras) do not recommend this, justifying their opinion that the price of the tour will be much lower than the operator. In all other respects, prices are recognized as quite acceptable and reasonable.
