Recreation center "Kirochnoe Compound": overview, description, features and reviews

The cottage complex "Kirochnoye Compound" is surrounded by bewitching nature. Everyone who is tired of the daily routine of worries, a dusty city and the noise of endless cars is here.

Where is

To get to this quiet place, guests do not have to travel a few hours. The recreation center is located in the Semiozerye district on Srednevyborgskoye Shosse, 19 km from the Leningrad Region. From Zelenogorsk the club is only 22 kilometers away, almost near the village of Polyana.

About the recreation center

The Kirochnoye Compound complex is a young facility that was built in 2010. He began to receive guests on May 1, 2011. All buildings on the territory, from cottages to restaurants, are stylized as serf monasteries. The recreation center is surrounded by nature. Just a hundred meters away are blue lakes, the water in them is very clean and clear. There is a good beach on the lake. Long, you need to walk only 300 meters to it. You can also sit comfortably by the pool, which belongs to the club "Kirochnoe Compound".



The recreation center offers a lot of interesting things for active people, as well as for those who come just to relax. Here you can walk around the forest paths, picking berries or mushrooms. Guests are also invited to go fishing, take a dip in the refreshing lake and take part in sporting events. Another option to entertain yourself is to go boating, make aromatic barbecue on the grill surrounded by greenery, visit the sauna and take part in competitions at events that are regularly organized here.

Summer fun

Kirochnaya Compound offers good venues for fans of volleyball, basketball or football. If you like paintball, it will be organized, because there is everything you need for this game. Here, guests can enjoy billiards on quality tables or tennis.

During the warm season, you can spend time by the outdoor pool. Its length is 11 meters. Near it there is a restaurant where it is possible to choose a drink to your taste. Another option for spending time near the water are lakes. It is good to fish on them, because fish lives in the water. Also, many people like relaxing on the beach.

Interesting leisure is also provided for children. The local playground is equipped with two outdoor children's pools. Also there are several slides and sandboxes.

Winter Fun

The Kirochnoye Compound welcomes vacationers even in winter and promises that guests will not be bored during this period. What is prepared for them? First of all, it is skiing, the famous Finnish sleigh and funny cheesecakes.

As soon as the temperature drops to the desired level, a smooth ice rink is poured here. Immediately appear guests skating. Equipment can be obtained at your local rental point. There is also an excellent wooden slide, which is also filled with water and turns into an ice slope.



After a cold rest, you can warm up in the sauna. In a local bathhouse you can not just spend time, but take a steam bath with brooms made of linden, birch or oak to avoid a cold.

Food in the Compound

All guests staying here are provided with breakfast in the morning. Kirochnoe Compound owns a chic restaurant located on two floors of the main building. Here, visitors are always offered fresh and the best dishes of three different cuisines. No less delicious are drinks that are prepared according to traditional Russian recipes. These include fruit drinks and lemonade. There is a barbecue area.

Hosting an event

Kirochnaya Compound is a great place for celebrations. If it is necessary to organize a buffet, wedding, children's party or other event, the club administration will take it upon itself.



You can spend an important day in one of the two banquet rooms, which can accommodate 70 and 150 guests. On the territory there is a summer covered veranda, suitable for lush celebrations or corporate events. It can comfortably accommodate 350 people. There is also a grill area.
