IPhone Parental Controls: Program Overview

Programs for organizing parental controls on an iPhone help dad and mom protect their child from the dangers of the digital world. Such software prohibits a child from accessing adult content or communicating with strangers.

In addition, if you set parental control on an iPhone, you can monitor the time spent by the child on the World Wide Web. It is very difficult for children armed with tablets, smartphones, and at the same time messengers with the ubiquitous "YouTube" to stop in time and postpone writing a post or watching a video until tomorrow.

Staffing Functionality

The company has fairly well developed the ability to restrict children from accessing the Web. To put parental control on an iPhone, you need to go to "Settings" -> "Basic" -> "Limitations". Here you need to create a unique password. Be sure to write it down, because otherwise you will have to configure the phone from scratch. It will also be needed to disable parental controls on the iPhone if necessary

In the "Limitations" section, you can block access to a variety of applications, utilities, as well as to the camera. In addition, it is possible to prohibit the installation of any software and its removal. You can disable the work with built-in purchases in applications and set up verbal censor: movies or songs with obscene words will be excluded from the search.
