Replacement of glass on "iPad 2": features of work

Apple products are known for their quality build and excellent device performance. But for various reasons, the equipment may require repairs. Often this is due to a sloppy attitude of a person. Replacing glass on the iPad 2 is required due to damage or due to other factors. This is described in the article.

Causes of glass breakage

The display usually cracks when the device falls. In addition to mechanical stress, glass bursts for the following reasons:





  1. Factory shortcomings. Prolonged use of the device leads to an increase in temperature. If the display is poor-quality and has air in it, then it bursts with heating.
  2. Use in the repair of Chinese components. Cheap parts are different from the originals, so they quickly go bad due to various factors.
  3. Bloating battery. Deformation of the battery leads to an increase in pressure on the glass, because of which it bursts.
  4. Deformation of the body. Even if the device remains in order with a fall, glass deterioration occurs later. The display may break due to violation of the shape of the case.

When is it necessary to change the screen?

Replacing glass on the iPad 2 is performed not only because it crashed, but also due to some problems with the touchscreen:

  1. There is no reaction to pressing.
  2. Incorrect touch processing.
  3. Part of the touchscreen does not work.
  4. Cracks are present.

With such problems, device repair is required. After a professional display replacement, the iPad 2 will work again properly.

Diagnostics

Before replacing the glass on the iPad 2, diagnostics are required. The procedure allows you to determine what is broken, and also because of what it happened. Moreover, there are not always external symptoms of a malfunction.





Often, the touchscreen module and the screen matrix break down with the display. Then not only the replacement of the iPad 2 screen is needed. In modern versions, it is usually required to change the entire screen module. You should not conduct diagnostics on your own, it is better to entrust this work to professionals.

The difficulty of repair

Replacing glass is considered a complex procedure, which requires skills. When repairing, there may be the following problems:

  1. Torn loops. This is due to the improper removal of broken glass, especially when this work is done by the users themselves.
  2. Unsuitable glue. Glass should not be attached to super-glue, as it will break even with minor bumps. And in the workshops, double-sided tape is used.
  3. Glass cleaning. The old glue must be removed completely, only then it will turn out to be able to qualitatively install a new screen.

There may be wrinkled corners on the device. Due to additional defects, the work of the master is complicated, therefore, the cost of repair will be more expensive.

Why do I need to contact a service center?

Repair "iPad" must perform the master. He will make sure that no errors are made during operation. When working inside the device should not get dirt, dust. Otherwise, you must again clean, disassemble the tablet. When ordering a job from a professional, a person receives the following benefits:

  1. Quality service.
  2. Use of original spare parts.
  3. Work on professional technology.
  4. Short repair time.
  5. Warranty.




The iPad 2 device is designed so that repairs are easier than other equipment. But there are a lot of screws in the tablet, so for disassembly and assembly it is necessary to have experience and knowledge, special tools, equipment. All this can be provided only in a professional center.

Cost

Masters begin repairing the iPad with a diagnosis. After this procedure, you will be able to set a price, because it all depends on the complexity of the work. How much does a glass replacement on the iPad 2 cost? The minimum price is 1500-2500 rubles. It also depends on the speed of work. If correction of other defects is necessary, the price increases.

The duration of the repair is usually 30-60 minutes. When replacing, the old glass is removed, the body is cleaned of glue, glass, and then the body is fixed. Then, with a constant flow of air, a new display is installed. For fastening a special double-sided tape is used. If everything is done correctly, the new screen will be fixed perfectly.

How to increase glass life?

What you need to do to make the touch glass work for a long time. Although the device has a scratch-resistant display, it can still crack, break. To extend the life of the iPad 2, you need to be careful about it, that is, protect it from falls, and also avoid excessive loads. Devices are carried in briefcases, in cases.





It is important to use a protective film that protects the screen from many adverse factors. Thanks to it, the display will not deteriorate from dirt, scratches. Some films have an anti-glare effect, due to which the tablet can be used in sunlight.

Often the screen deteriorates from falling at an angle. In the iPad 2, the case is made of soft aluminum, so the impact leads to a uniform breakdown of the entire area. To minimize the negative consequences, you need to use covers that cover the case. The device must be protected from falling into the water, since the liquid causes the failure of many systems.

Thus, the screen repair on the iPad 2 is best done in a professional workshop, otherwise independent activity may degrade the operation of the equipment. And then you still have to pay for the restoration of the device in a specialized center.
