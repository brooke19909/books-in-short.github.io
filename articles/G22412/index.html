Openwork cardigans with knitting needles. Where to begin? Scheme and job description

Like all the forgotten old, knitted openwork cardigans in a new reincarnation return to us. Do-it-yourself models are popular with modern fashionistas. Craftswomen also prefer to knit clothes for themselves, using patterns and a step-by-step description.





Model Options

Knitted openwork cardigan looks stylish and attractive. A properly connected model not only retains heat, but also emphasizes the elegance of the figure, creating a light and airy image. Cardigans can be short or elongated. A popular item is a product that is tied no lower than the mid-thigh line. This model hides the imperfections of shapes and emphasizes the length of the legs.

Knitted openwork cardigans can be fastened with buttons, tied with a belt or soft lace, and have freely hanging shelves. The variety of knitting methods and the number of openwork patterns is impressive. The lineup offers cardigans in vintage or oversized style. The classic shape of the style is not forgotten. Fantasy openwork patterns can have vertical or horizontal lines, can fill the entire canvas of the product or partially knit. Also, the color scheme allows you to combine models with any clothes. Knitted openwork cardigans can have both long and short sleeves, as well as straps of various lengths with a back.





Where to start knitting?

Before you begin, you need to determine which model of openwork cardigan with knitting needles you will knit and for what season. The choice of yarn depends on this: either it will be woolen or made of cotton. Then, from the prepared yarn, you need to knit the sample with facial loops to determine the density and calculate the required number of loops for knitting the model on your figure. It is worth noting that openwork patterns tend to stretch, so it‚Äôs worth linking the pattern with the pattern.

Craftswomen recommend taking needles 1.5 to 2 times thicker than yarn. This method emphasizes and highlights the openwork pattern, giving it airiness. An important parameter when knitting openwork cardigans with knitting needles is the structure and length of the thread, the thickness of the yarn and the color scheme. The thread can be either natural or mixed. To make a warm cardigan, mohair, angora or alpaca yarn is used. A percentage combination of several varieties of wool is allowed. Cardigan for the warm season can be knitted from cotton and viscose.

The classic model has five parts that are knitted in the following order:

  • back;
  • two shelves (left and right in mirror image);
  • sleeves with your chosen length;
  • armholes at the base;
  • sleeves around.

Cardigans do not include collars. They can optionally be imposed additionally.
