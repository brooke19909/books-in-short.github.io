Where is the Paveletsky direction of the train

Paveletsky station is far from the busiest in Moscow. But he has been carrying out his service regularly for the second hundred years. And the Paveletsky direction of the train reliably connects the center of the capital with the southern regions of the near and far suburbs. The railway is not in the least dependent on traffic jams. And this fact is an important competitive advantage over other modes of transport.



How did the Paveletsky direction of the train

The relatively low congestion of this railway line is not surprising. That was originally conceived. The Paveletsky direction of the train provides passenger transportation on a road that was never planned as a trunk. It appeared to some extent by accident, when at the end of the nineteenth century the idea arose to bring to the capital the Ural-Ryazan railway that had been functioning successfully by then. She had no access to Moscow, and this annoying omission was corrected. So on the Garden Ring of the capital appeared Paveletsky Station. It was a kind of gateway to the Russian province, that part of it that is located south of Moscow.



The movement of trains Paveletsky direction

Apparently, this is the quietest of all Moscow stations. From here you can go to the south of Russia and to a number of Black Sea resorts. But the main function of the Paveletsky station is to provide suburban passenger traffic. The railway here for more than a hundred years now reliably allows you to get to small towns and holiday villages of the southern suburbs. And with the increase in the number of traffic jams on all the main highways of the capital, its significance only increases. In addition, the Paveletsky direction of the train provides round-the-clock delivery of passengers to the largest Russian airport, Domodedovo. Most Muscovites and guests of the capital prefer aeroexpress. This type of transport allows you to save time, money and nerves. The movement of electric trains in the Paveletsky direction is characterized by stability and adherence to a schedule. Nobody has yet succeeded in being late for the flight, for this you will need to not be in time for the departure of the Aeroexpress train in the direction of Domodedovo. The only negative here is that they go less frequently at night.



Schedule of trains Paveletsky direction 2013

The movement schedule of suburban electric trains does not substantially change in this direction from year to year. Is that the interval of departure of express to Domodedovo airport has decreased, and Aeroexpress began to go more often. And there is no particular reason to suppose changes in the schedule in the near future. And such a healthy conservatism is welcome. Already for several generations of Muscovites, the way to Kashira, Barybino, Mikhnevo, Stupino, Uzunovo and Ozherelye stations near Moscow runs through the passenger terminal of the Paveletsky station. And here there is always confidence that the trains will arrive at their destination in full accordance with the schedule.
