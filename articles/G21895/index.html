Section 3. Attractions and ground infrastructure



As previously reported, the Tsaritsyno metro station (the map shows it perfectly: one of the stops on the green line) is very popular.

All this is observed thanks to architectural monuments located on the surface. One of the attractions, which, incidentally, the station owes its present name, is the Tsaritsyno palace complex, which was laid down even under Empress Catherine II. However, its construction was completed only at the beginning of the XIX century.

In general, it should be noted that the park of the complex is located between Tsaritsyno and Orekhovo metro stations, but still the main, or, as it is called here, central, entrance is located just near the first.

Muscovites and guests of the capital like to visit the famous arbor, which is called the "Golden Sheaf" or "Temple of Ceres." By the way, in the revolutionary years it was half destroyed and restored only after a large-scale restoration.

The alleys also had to be reconstructed. After completing all the repairs, it was decided to install several sculptures in the park.

The Tsaritsyno station is an unusual metro. It can introduce everyone to the remnants of the distant past. Although the ruins of the tower are the only partially preserved structures, they can be considered as quite original examples of park architecture of the complex.

Behind the grape gates is a building that is decorated with double-headed imperial eagles. This is the middle palace, still called the opera house. By the way, in our days concerts are often held in it.

Section 4. Useful information and interesting facts.



The Moscow Metro is rightfully considered one of the most convenient and technically equipped in the world. The metro station Tsaritsyno is no exception. Moscow, it seems, is doing everything possible and impossible so that the passenger gets to his destination with maximum comfort.

For visitors, the building opens at 5:30 in the morning. The work ends at 1 am.

The station has mobile communications. Operators MTS, Beeline and Megafon are supported.

The entire Tsaritsyno area, in which the station of the same name is located, is located in the geopathogenic anomaly zone . For unexplained reasons, it is precisely in this area that malfunctions are most often associated with power supply and flooding.

According to some researchers, this is a geopathic zone of natural origin, and all the so-called mysterious events occur due to the movement of water flows through zones of discontinuous disturbances and once buried channels. Employees of the complex of gardens and parks note that during working hours, being on the territory, they feel as if in another world.
