"National"

Hotel National (Kobuleti) is located just a minute from the beach. The complex has a swimming pool. The hotel provides free private parking and internet access for free.

The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, kettle. The balcony offers sea views. The bathroom has slippers, bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Meals are provided on site. Dishes of Ukrainian, Russian, Georgian and Italian cuisine are served. The restaurant serves a buffet breakfast. After eating, you can go to the bar for a drink or relax on the terrace.

"National" is located 30 km from the railway station and 40 km from Batumi airport. A shuttle service is provided, but for a fee.

Like other Kobuleti hotels, Chveni Ezo is 2 minutes from the coastline. It is 90 m from the Black Sea, 5 km from the center. The front desk is open 24 hours a day, internet is free, and small parties are allowed on the terrace.

This institution is included in the conditional list, which could be called "The most popular hotels on the coast of Kobuleti."
