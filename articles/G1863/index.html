Travel to Astrakhan - rest at sea

Few people do not know that the Caspian Sea, in fact, is a lake that has a coast of a marine type. It has no access to the ocean, which prevents the full use of this definition. Holidays on the coast is famous for clean warm water without jellyfish, the opportunity to go fishing. Thanks to this, Astrakhan is often visited. The sea is located immediately in three climatic zones: temperate, subtropical and continental. In winter, the reservoir in the northern part is covered with ice, in summer it warms up to 27 ¬∞ C. There are no particularly luxurious hotels, but tourists are most attracted to a large number of fish that live in the Volga and Caspian deltas.

Beckon people Dagestan and Astrakhan. Holidays at sea in these places travelers will not forget soon.



Fishing

No self-respecting fisherman will give up fishing in the Caspian Sea. Here you can find a huge number of catfish, pike, bream, common carp, crucian carp and others. Sometimes come across sterlet and beluga.

There is an officially recorded record - a pike weighing 14 kg, catfish - 115 kg, pike perch - 5 kg were caught in a pond. If you get the appropriate permission, you can hunt for wild boar or birds, which often fly to this area.

Where to go?

Recreation centers in Astrakhan and Dagestan are open for travelers. None of them will cause distrust, because their service and comfort comply with European standards.

However, it should be remembered that in Dagestan sometimes a dangerous situation may arise due to the frequent terrorist acts. In the Astrakhan region, such problems will not arise.



Rest in Astrakhan

Many people like visiting the Caspian in a city like Astrakhan. Holidays at sea on the basis of the "Dardanelles" - a great option for couples and small companies. It is easy to remember the location, since it is located directly in the Volga delta. The building has comfortable rooms with air conditioning, refrigerators and amenities. There is also a deluxe room in which there is a jacuzzi. The recreation center has its own bath and billiards, organizes excursions. Of course, there is a dining room where you can get a very decent breakfast and dinner. Staff can also prepare a personal catch for the fisherman.

The prices are reasonable. Room for 3 people with meals will cost 7, for four - 8, and luxury apartments - 10 thousand rubles a day. The base is an hour drive from the city of Astrakhan. Holidays at sea promise to be unforgettable.

Base "Tortuga" is specially founded for fishermen. The price category is almost the same as that described above. The complex is located in Liman. Equipment necessary for fishing can be rented. Children come here to rest, because the rooms have bathrooms, household appliances, a refrigerator, dishes and other things that are needed for a comfortable pastime.

The city of Astrakhan itself (everyone enjoys a seaside vacation in this area) has about 18 recreation centers, so in any case there will be places for tourists to stay.



Dagestan

In order to ensure a terrific vacation in Dagestan, you need to visit the base "Dagokhotrybolovschestvo". The administration offers wonderful places for catching animals. It is allowed to hunt rounds, bears, wild boars, raccoons, roe deer, squirrels, etc. On the territory of the base you can find a place for cooking and the necessary accessories. The issue of permission to shoot is resolved in the "Hunting and Fishing Society". However, this base is not suitable for those who came to rest with their family, because the conditions here are Spartan.

For parents with children a tourist base "Surf" is suitable. Although the building looks unattractive, the rooms have all the necessary amenities: air conditioning, refrigerator, dishes. From the windows you can see a beautiful clean beach with sun loungers.
