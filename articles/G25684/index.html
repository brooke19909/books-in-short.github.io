Hotel Marlita Beach Hotel Apts 4 * (Cyprus, Protaras): traveler reviews

Cyprus is a unique island, characterized by a warm climate, the presence of beautiful mountain landscapes, as well as a large number of archaeological sites. Tourists who are tired of the popular resorts of Greece, Turkey or Egypt often come here. Hotels on the island are almost not like huge hotel complexes offering a huge number of services on their territory. Most often, apartments are rented here. A good option for this accommodation would be the Marlita Beach Hotel Apts 4 * hotel in Protaras. We will tell you more about it in our article, and also talk about the reviews of tourists who have already had to rest here.

Details for the location of the hotel Marlita Beach Hotel Apts 4 *

Protaras is a small and relatively young resort town with a well-developed infrastructure. It is known for its restaurants, bars and nightclubs, but there are areas for a relaxing holiday. It is in one of them that the Marlita Beach Hotel Apts 4 * hotel is located (Cyprus). It is built right on the coast of the Marlita Bay, the landscapes of which are considered one of the most beautiful on the island. On foot in 15 minutes, tourists can reach the central part of Protaras. The hotel is located right on the beach, so the beach can be reached in just a few minutes.

The large tourist city of Ayia Napa is a 10-minute drive away. This can be done both by public transport and taxi. The nearest international airport is near Larnaca. The distance to it is approximately 47 km, so it will take about 1-2 hours to get to the hotel. Another advantage of the location of this hotel is its large water park, which can be reached in 5 minutes. Several restaurants are open next to the complex, there are shopping centers that are definitely worth visiting for shopping lovers.

General information and settlement rules

Hotel Marlita Beach Hotel Apts 4 * opened its doors to guests in the distant 1990. But do not be afraid of a dilapidated number of rooms, because a complete overhaul was carried out here in the winter of 2017. Tourists who come here on vacation are accommodated in rooms with new furniture - there are 147 of them in the hotel. Most of them are designed for two adult guests, but there are apartments for large families. In total, about 350 guests can simultaneously stay in the hotel.

The complex has several residential buildings, a swimming pool, a sun terrace, and catering facilities. The hotel is decorated in a Mediterranean style - the exterior facade is painted white and is notable for its simplicity.

Few hotels in Cyprus have Russian-speaking staff on their staff, but Marlita Beach Hotel Apts 4 * is not one of them. There is always an employee at the front desk who speaks not only good English, but also good Russian. He will help tourists fill out all the necessary documents for arrival. It starts here in accordance with international standards - from 14:00. To register, you will need an identification card and a credit card. Moreover, its data must coincide with the information specified in the passport. After the vacation, you need to free your rooms before noon. But if there are free rooms for a fee, tourists can extend their stay.

Description of rooms

As mentioned above, the hotel has 147 rooms. Most of them are apartments resembling a small studio apartment. Therefore, they are fully suitable for a full stay, and not for a banal overnight. The living room is divided into a bedroom, living room and kitchen area, where tourists can prepare their own breakfast, lunch or dinner. Each room also has a private bathroom and a balcony. The apartment on the ground floor is replaced by an open terrace. Depending on the category of the room, its windows can open onto nearby buildings, the hotel grounds or the sea. Some apartments also have only side views of the coast.

All rooms are decorated in a modern style: the walls are painted in pastel shades, and the furniture, on the contrary, is decorated with bright colors. Their area may vary. Beach and other water activities

Hotel Marlita Beach Hotel Apts 4 * in Protaras (Protaras) is located on a public stretch of the coast, which is fully equipped for a beach holiday.
