The recreation center "Pearl" (Kabardinka): description, reviews, photos

The recreation center "Pearl" (Kabardinka) is one of the closest places to rest to the beach. Only 50-60 steps to the sea. Do vacationers like living conditions? How to get to the hotel? Read about all this below.

Description of the area

Kabardinka is a village that is part of the resort city of Gelendzhik. The base is located in a picturesque area covered with dense greenery. Nearby are cafes, discos, shops and markets. On the resort promenade you can find entertainment for adults and children in the form of attractions. Gelendzhik can be reached by taxi in half an hour.

Kabardinka has developed infrastructure. There are attractions, organization of excursions, a wide range of bars and cafes. "Old Park" impresses with its architecture, design and vegetation. The creator of the cultural object tried to combine the architecture of different times and countries in one place. Here are the pyramids from Egypt, and Chinese buildings and structures from the time of ancient Greece. Well-kept promenade, decorated with flower beds and fountains. The beach with smooth and coarse pebbles, equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. Near the place you can find shops and trade stalls. You can easily get to the recreation center located at Kabardinka, Mira Street 20. It is necessary to get here by public transport from the airport of Anapa, then take a taxi to the hotel. If you get by train, then from the station in Novorossiysk.





"Pearl": description of rooms

Many tourists like to relax in the area where the recreation center "Pearl" is located. Kabardinka is a beautiful village full of greenery. The air here is clean and healing. The base itself has a vast area. On its territory are located:

  • two single-story buildings;
  • one two-story;
  • one three-story.

The base is guarded round the clock, there is video surveillance. Economy rooms can be occupied in buildings with one floor. Double and triple wooden houses are equipped with the necessary furniture: single or double beds, bedside table, wardrobe. Present and household appliances: TV, refrigerator, air conditioning. Each room has a separate shower and toilet. The recreation center "Pearl", Kabardinka which can be proud of, offers all guests free access to the Internet.

For vacationers who want additional comfort, there are rooms in a three-story and two-story house. In addition to the fact that they have an individual entrance, in the room you can find air conditioning, a table with chairs, a sofa, a set of dishes. The rest of the furniture is the same as in the "economy". The area is well maintained and large enough for walking. The recreation center “Pearl” (Kabardinka) is literally drowning in greenery. The photo below demonstrates this. In addition, there is a sauna, swimming pool, playgrounds.



Extra paid service

Three meals a day are organized upon request. Also, the recreation center "Pearl" - Kabardinka is its location - has on its territory a dining room-cafe with 100 seats with barbecue. You can pay for meals right away when you purchase a ticket. Paid parking is located 150 meters from the place. The bathhouse runs on wood. Holds up to 5-7 people. Cost for 3 hours - 6 thousand rubles. At the beach you can rent umbrellas and sunbeds for a moderate fee.





Guest reviews

Thousands of guests each season describe their impressions of the rest that Kabardinka gave them. The recreation center "Pearl" reviews collected both positive and sharply negative. Let's start with the negative ones. Service, and this is an important component of any holiday, is lame and reminds many of the Soviet past. Tourists complain about the sluggish staff and rude cleaners. Oil is added to the fire by the fact that some people receive tickets from the state.

Of the inconveniences, vacationers note the following:

  • steep steps for descent to the sea;
  • a public beach, where you have to take places in the early morning;
  • On-demand cleaning is not always carried out at the desired speed.

But, despite everything written above, there are much more positive reviews than negative ones. People like the quiet place, the design of the area and the unobtrusive attendants. Wooden houses set up for rest and relaxation. Food in the cafe is tasty and inexpensive.
