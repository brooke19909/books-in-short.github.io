Crafts from car tires for flower beds



Today, every gardener can find flower beds made of tires. It is attractive that this material is not afraid of rains and frosts, products from it will last you a long time. Someone uses the usual way: they simply fill the tire with earth and plant plants in it. Well, it can still be decorated with different colors. But it‚Äôs better to show a little imagination and patience to get original flower beds. Armed with a grinder and a sharp knife, along the contour of the tire you cut out the cloves, then turn it inside out. The teeth can be of different sizes (from 10 to 40 cm), it all depends on the size of the wheel itself. Now you need to turn your product over, fix it from below for stability, and fill it with earth from above. But before that you, of course, can decorate your crafts from car tires. Then they will only have to arrange in their area and plant flowers in them. Especially such flower beds are relevant in yards where everything is concreted and there are no ordinary flower beds.


Products from car tires for the garden

For the garden, you can come up with a lot of crafts that will be not only beautiful, but also useful. The most common are high ridges, for example, for strawberries. And the place will take a little, and the berries will always be clean. Today, most people prefer a healthy lifestyle and like to ride bicycles. Take a few wheels and dig them in a row vertically into the ground. You will get an original bicycle parking. You can still make an interesting wash basin. Just put a decorated tire under the tap, or several pieces one on one, and in them mount an old sink or an unnecessary basin. Arrange a drain, give the surface an interesting texture, and a useful craft for the garden is ready.





In addition, you can arrange in the garden all kinds of figures from tires. Naturally, the most common are swans, which can be used as flower beds, and placed at a makeshift pond. For children in the garden, you can build a sandbox from a huge wheel and insert a large beach umbrella into it . Car tires can also be used as roofing material for some shed where garden equipment is stored . You can also make garden furniture. Just put the small wheels on top of each other, and make a soft seat in the middle. And from large wheels, build a leg for the table, on which fix the tabletop from any available material. As you can see, there can be a great many ideas, it remains only to fantasize and apply a little effort.
