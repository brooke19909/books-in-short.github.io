How to make butterfly applique

Today we will tell you how the butterfly applique is made of colored paper. These creatures of nature can be admired endlessly. To cut out the details of the applique, we need scissors.

Training



As a result, we should get a butterfly. Paper applications require compliance with a number of conditions. We will need scissors with rounded ends. Tools for creating applications should be stored in a designated place. Scissors should be placed with sharp, closed ends away from you. The tool must be passed with closed blades, rings forward. You can‚Äôt cut on the go. When working with the tool, you need to monitor the position and movement of the blades. Do not work with blunt scissors or with a tool that has loosened hinges. No need to hold the blades up. We select colored paper for future work. Work will also require patterns.

Procedure



To make our butterfly applique, first we take a blue cardboard. Getting to cutting grass. Take the green paper. Fold it in half. Apply a template to it. Butterfly application does not require special skills, so even a child can perform it. At the next stage, circle the pattern. Cut and glue on the bottom of the cardboard. We select colored paper. Using the template, we cut out three flowers. Glue them on the grass. Next, we need a middle. Glue it. The clearing is ready. We turn to the manufacture of butterflies. First cut out the wings. Glue them on cardboard. Cut another pair of openwork wings. Glue them to the butterfly. We select the color of the paper. Next, cut out the small wings. Also glue them to the butterfly. At the next stage, cut out the body of the butterfly. Glue it.

Finishing touches



Butterfly applique is almost ready. It remains to cut and glue the eyes of the insect. Our butterfly should become bright and beautiful. You can use different colors. If children work on the composition, the choice of shades will help develop their imagination. Now you know how to make a butterfly applique. The master class is suitable not only for children, but also for adults who like to engage in creativity, which is based on colored paper. The above instruction may also be useful for students in labor classes or for children who attend classes. Theoretically, even the crumbs visiting the garden can cope with the tasks described in the material. However, before setting them a similar task, they should give them pre-cut details of the composition. Children will be able to develop the ability to perform interesting crafts from colored paper. A person who follows this instruction will be able to learn how to choose the right color for materials for application. In addition, the child will learn safety when using scissors. Such work contributes to the development of imagination and fantasy. Also, the manufacture of applications fosters accuracy and perseverance. For work, we need: velvet colored paper, pencil, glue, scissors, cardboard, colored paper.
