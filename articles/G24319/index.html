Ways to transfer money from Life to Life

Mobile operator "Life" provides its services in two countries - Ukraine and Belarus. And if you want to know how to transfer money from "Life" to "Life" in these countries, then read the article to the end. The fact is that their methods differ. The one that works in Ukraine will not work in Belarus. In addition, there are several ways to transfer funds from one balance to another.

Translation in Ukraine: the first way





Immediately consider how to transfer money from Life to Life in Ukraine. There are only two ways: using a USSD request and using an SMS message. First of all, consider the first method.

So, in order to transfer funds, you need to enter the following USSD request: * 111 * recipient's mobile phone number * transfer amount # and press the call key. As a result, you will receive a message that describes what needs to be done to confirm the operation.

If you do not want to remember long requests, then there is another option how to transfer money from Life to Life using a USSD request. This time, enter * 124 #. After that a menu will appear. In it, select "Perevod balansa". As a result, you only need to indicate the recipient number and the transfer amount itself. Roughly speaking, this is the same as the first time, only divided into stages.

Translation in Ukraine: the second method





I said above that there are two ways to transfer money from Life to Life. We have already disassembled the first one, now let's move on to the second - using an SMS message.

In general, everything is simple. Start creating a new message. We will send it to number 124, but in the text input field you will need to enter the following: PEREVOD number of the recipient_transfer sum. Then send a message. After sending you will also receive a message to confirm the operation.

If this explanation baffled you, then let's give an example. Consider the situation when you want to send 50 hryvnias to 380631111111. Based on this, the text in the message should be as follows: PEREVOD 380631111111 50 and you will need to send it to 124.

This is the end of the analysis of how to transfer money to Life in Ukraine, we will end, we will go directly to how to do it in Belarus.

Translation in Belarus: the first way

So, in order to transfer money to Belarus with Life, you can also use two methods. Now we will begin to consider the first - using the USSD request. This method will be quite similar to the one given before, but there are still some differences that we will talk about right now.

It is worth saying right away that the USSD request looks like this: * 120 * 1 # and the call key. Further, as in the previous example, you must follow the instructions in order to make the translation. Now let's talk about the nuances. If you are a subscriber of the tariff plan called "Super life :)", then you will not be able to use this service "Balance Transfer", you can only accept transfers. This is a tariff restriction and cannot be changed in any way. There are also restrictions on the maximum amount transferred. For a week you can send 15 thousand rubles.

Translation in Belarus: the second way





In Belarus, you can send money through the special Life website. This is just the second method of translation. To do this, you need to get to the "My Page" section. You will see a form for entering personal data. You need to specify your number as login and password.

If you do not know what your password is, then you can create it by sending a USSD request * 120 #. As a result, you will be shown the desired code. If you do not have the opportunity to use your phone to get a password, then you can specify the PUK1 code of your package in its quality. These were all ways to transfer money to Life both in Ukraine and Belarus.
