Hotel Villa Disney 3 * (Montenegro, Budva): photos and reviews of tourists

The decision to travel to Eastern Europe raises a logical question for many tourists: "How to relax inexpensively and comfortably?" Changes in exchange rates played a key role in the preference of most vacationers to find cheaper housing. 3 * hotels are gaining more and more popularity among Russians. They have basic conditions of stay and, as a rule, pretty decent food. These places include Villa Disney 3 * (Montenegro, Budva). All the advantages and disadvantages of this hotel are considered in detail below.

What is interesting about the resort

Budva is, without exaggeration, the most popular Montenegrin resort. Lovers of a relaxing beach holiday, adherents of an active nightlife and even fans of historical places come here. The variety and color of this city amazes the imagination of even the most experienced travelers. In such a small country like Montenegro, it is surprising to find something similar, but it is a reality. It is in such a pleasant place that Villa Disney 3 * (Montenegro, Budva) is located.

Here you will find the sunny coast, gambling evenings in the casino, fascinating tours of the ancient fortresses of the 15th century, and vibrant music festivals. The streets of Budva at the height of the season are filled with tourists, so you can retire with the sea only 4 km from the city - on the island of St. Nicholas, it is also called the local "Hawaii". For those who prefer lying on the beach to noisy parties and outdoor activities, this is a paradise.





Hotel Location

The hotel with the fabulous name Villa Disney 3 * (Montenegro, Budva) is located on the second line from the sea. A walk to the beach takes approximately 10 minutes. From the hotel to the coast you have to walk 400 m.

Upon arrival, the distance from Tivat Airport to the hotel will be about 25 km, and from Podgorica Airport - about 60 km. You can hire a taxi or take a public transport. Villa Disney 3 * (Montenegro, Budva): room description

This three-star hotel contains 12 comfortable rooms. The capacity is designed for a small group of vacationers or a single tourist - from 1 to 4 people can be accommodated in one of the rooms. You will be offered several types of accommodation:

  • standard rooms with a French bed or twin beds;
  • two-room apartment for 2 or 3 people with a French bed and sofa;
  • four-bed apartment with two separate bedrooms, one with a French bed and the other with separate beds.

You can also choose rooms with a spacious terrace and a balcony. The price from this factor may slightly change for the better.





Each of the rooms is equipped with air conditioning, a minibar for drinks, a TV with connected local cable TV, a private bathroom, a hairdryer and all bed and bath accessories. Free Wi-Fi is also available throughout the hotel. In the superior rooms, you can cook in your own kitchen, using all the necessary utensils: pots, plates, cutlery. In standard rooms such an opportunity is not provided.

Services and infrastructure

If you have pets, then you can easily stay with them. There is a safe at the reception where you can leave valuables and documents for the entire rest. At the hotel you can leave your own car. The villa staff will clean the rooms daily, towels will be changed every other day, and bed linen is changed every fourth day of stay. There is also its own cafe-pizzeria with the name of the animation company - Disney.

As for food, the client is free to choose: you can take a tour with a full meal at the hotel or dine in various places at your own discretion. In the vicinity of the hotel there are many entertainment venues, bars and restaurants, grocery stores and all the services necessary for tourists: tour agencies, souvenir shops and places for outdoor activities.





Beach vacation

For a relaxing beach holiday, Villa Disney 3 * (Montenegro, Budva) is the best suited. A photo of this place does not convey all the beauty of the surrounding sea landscape. The sea here is surprisingly clean, and the beach is strewn with small pebbles. You can rent sun loungers and umbrellas to protect Montenegro from the scorching sun. Private beaches also have to pay. Unlike municipal ones, there will not be such a large number of vacationers, and it will be possible to settle down freely for your pleasure.





Villa Disney 3 * (Montenegro, Budva): reviews

Tourists' impressions of this place range from partially negative to the most enthusiastic. The reason for this contrast lies in the expectations of the vacationer and his previous experience of traveling abroad. But all the opinions of people agree on one thing - the hotel is fully consistent with the declared level of comfort, rated at 3 stars.

Among the undoubted advantages of staying at the hotel, tourists call tasty and hearty food. Experienced travelers are advised to take only breakfast and dinner. The diet will always be varied and consist only of freshly prepared dishes. In Montenegro, it is not customary to heat food in public catering establishments, so among the negative aspects you can find dissatisfaction with the speed of service. Additionally, food can be inexpensively purchased at nearby large chain stores and cooked in room.

They also note that the rooms are spacious, have a complete set of everything necessary for a comfortable stay. For a modest budget, this hotel is just a godsend. Many remember the friendliness of the owners of the villa, their respectful attitude towards customers and the ability to delicately resolve problems. Conveniently, excursions can be organized independently, simply by renting a car from the owners. This is another additional service that is provided at the hotel.

So briefly describe the rest in Villa Disney 3 * (Montenegro, Budva). The review is useful to everyone who wants to visit Europe, visit sunny Montenegro. This is a completely different type of vacation, which will especially impress beginners with an average budget.
