How to get to the Emerald quarry of Chelyabinsk and what to do there

The relic Chelyabinsk urban forest is located in the west of the city and stretches widely from north to northeast of the region. The emerald quarry of Chelyabinsk is one of the largest reservoirs in the forested area. It has long been a favorite vacation spot for locals in the summer heat and during the winter holidays.

Geography and Ecology

Located in the southern part of the city, surrounded by a forest with a unique microclimate and clean air, the Emerald quarry in Chelyabinsk is far from large industrial enterprises.

There is clear and clean water, as the bottom is rocky. Pine forest, steep banks form beautiful landscapes at any time of the year, however, as elsewhere in the Southern Urals.

The appearance of mines

The region is rich in natural resources, and mining has long been a profitable business. One of the mining items was granite and marble, which were in abundance in the nearest modern suburb of Chelyabinsk.
