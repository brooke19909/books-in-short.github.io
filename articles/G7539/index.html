Where does the name come from

The hotel got its name from the street of the same name located in Berlin - Alexanderplatz, which was named in honor of Alexander I, the Russian emperor and autocrat.

Number of rooms





The hotel has two categories of rooms: standard and luxury. In each of them it is pleasant to spend time. The rooms are beautifully furnished, the furniture is new and has a nice view. The hotel guarantees the cleanliness and neatness of each room.

In a standard double you can choose either one double bed or two single beds. The best and most qualified designers of St. Petersburg worked on the interior of each room, and the view from its windows to the quiet charming streets will give everyone a tremendous aesthetic pleasure. The price will include breakfast in a cozy restaurant with delicious European and Russian cuisine. Such a room will be an ideal option for accommodating guests of St. Petersburg who have visited it on a business trip, or those who want to enjoy the Northern capital with their soulmate. Alexander Platz is the best suited to help you find yourself in an incredibly romantic and homely atmosphere. The cost of such an atmosphere is not too high - 3000 rubles per night.

Luxury rooms are characterized by increased comfort. Here you can relax on large and comfortable beds and sofas from the bustle of the St. Petersburg center, and most importantly - at any time, guests of deluxe rooms can visit the beautiful and relaxing Finnish sauna, located right in their room! All rooms are equipped with refrigerators, air conditioning and heaters. This is a wonderful choice for those traveling in St. Petersburg with family or friends. It is important to note that for all the advantages of this hotel, the cost of living in one of its chic rooms will not hit your wallet. Children under the age of twelve stay at the hotel for free.
