Hotel Magic Sun 4 * Turkey, Kemer: traveler reviews

The Mediterranean coast of Turkey is the most popular tourist destination for Russian tourists. Every year, hundreds of thousands of our fellow citizens prefer to spend their holidays in Antalya, Marmaris, Belek or Kemer. The Magic Sun Hotel 4 *, which will be discussed in this article, is located in the village of Beldibi, located between Antalya and Kemer. Tourists are attracted by the high-class service that they meet at this hotel, the hospitality and friendliness of the staff, as well as their attentive attitude towards families with children. Here, young guests feel themselves in the spotlight, excellent animation is organized for them, there are many opportunities for interesting and eventful leisure.

What can Kemer offer tourists

This resort consists of the city of the same name and several villages - Tekirova, Goynuk, Kirish, Camyuva, Belbek and Beldibi. Across the coast are beautiful buildings of gigantic hotel complexes. Their architecture is striking in its scale and originality. Magic Sun 4 * is also located in a very beautiful building, which at first glance attracts tourists.

The vast majority of Kemer hotels provide all-inclusive services. This is what attracts tourists from many countries here. The Russians feel quite comfortable here, as the attendants speak Russian. Kemer's climate is milder than on the rest of the Turkish coast. A 2000-meter-high wall of Taurus Mountains protects the resort from the winds. Therefore, here the beach season lasts longer, until the end of October.

Beldibi Resort

As already noted, the Magic Sun 4 * hotel is located in the village of Beldibi. It is built on the historical land of Lycia. Hotel complexes here are lined up in several rows. The aforementioned hotel is on the second line, but has its own private beach, which can be reached in just 10-15 minutes.

On Ataturk Street, tourists can find an incredible amount of shops and shops, as well as restaurants, cafes and other entertainment facilities. For fans of extreme recreation near the village there is the opportunity to go rafting on a mountain river, and diving enthusiasts can visit the underwater cave, which is located in close proximity to the beach.

The biggest advantage of this resort is its climate, thanks to which there is an extensive green strip, mainly consisting of coniferous forests, filling the air with freshness and aroma. Fans of landscape design can enjoy their holidays in the beautiful coastal parks. In addition, all tourists who visit the Magic Sun 4 * Hotel, Kemer provides an excellent opportunity for educational tours. Near the village there are ruins of the ancient city of Phaselis, founded by the Rhodians in the 7th century BC. e. According to legend, it was here that Alexander the Great was buried.

How to get to Beldibi

If you bought a tour to Turkey, in Magic Sun 4 *, then, of course, you are interested in how you will get to it. The only international airport that accepts flights from all over the world on the Turkish Mediterranean coast is Antalya. Therefore, no matter which resort you choose, you will still need to fly to Antalya, and from there you will have to get to the village and hotel by ground. Beldibi, in which the Magic Sun 4 * hotel is located, is 40 km from Antalya, so you can get there in half an hour. In the village there are only a few parallel streets that stretch along the coast. For its guests, the hotel provides a transfer from the airport to the entrance to the complex. As we have already noted, Magic Sun is located on the second line, a 10-minute walk from the beach.

About hotel

This hotel complex was built in 2006, and the last restoration was made in 2011. The total area of ​​the hotel is 6300 sq.m. Magic Sun 4 * is a five-story, very beautiful building with an elevator, which is surrounded by a garden, with coniferous trees, tropical flowers and palm trees. According to the reviews of tourists, the hotel is very comfortable to stay, has an incredibly high-quality service. Here, families with children and young people like to relax.

Number of rooms

The hotel rooms are 181 units. All of them are comfortable and cozy. Of these, 157 are classified as “Standard” (they have an area of ​​26 sq / m and are designed for 2 + 1 people); 30 - “Economy” (they are the smallest in size in this hotel (24 sq / m) and can accommodate no more than two guests); 3 rooms with an area of ​​43 sq / m are family rooms and are designed for 4 + 1 guests. There is also 1 comfortable “Suite”, which consists of two rooms - a living room and a bedroom. Some of the adjacent standard rooms may turn into “connected”. As a rule, families are placed in them. The 44 rooms are non-smoking.

Description

Each of the rooms of the Magic Sun 4 * hotel (regardless of its category) has an individual air conditioning, a bathroom with a toilet, where you can find a hairdryer on the wall, and towels of different sizes, toiletries and cosmetics on the shelves. If you have valuable things or cash with you, then when leaving your personal space you can leave them in a safe. The room also has a telephone with direct international dialing, a TV with satellite channels, including Russian, there is a minibar in which drinking water is added daily.

Rooms are cleaned every day, even if this is not necessary. The same goes for changing towels. However, bed linen is changed every other day. Room service is available around the clock. You must pay extra for this service. You can also ask the administrator about the “alarm” service.

Hotel infrastructure

The hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable stay for guests. This is evidenced by reviews of Magic Sun 4 *, which are left by guests. For the convenience of guests there is a laundry, dry cleaning, mini-beauty salon, which provides hairdresser, manicure and pedicure services. On the territory there is parking, a left-luggage office, a currency exchange office, several shops and souvenir shops, a first-aid post. Doctor services for a fee.

Entertainment

Wi-Fi is free throughout. In addition, there is an Internet cafe where you can use computers and other electronic equipment (for a fee). The hotel has an outdoor pool with sun loungers and parasols around it. Sunbathing mattresses are provided free of charge, and towels are chargeable. The hotel has a sauna, as well as a real Turkish hammam with soaping tourists. Foam parties are organized here. As for the services of a massage therapist, they are paid.

For fans of productive leisure there is a fitness room with exercise machines. Animators conduct aerobics classes here. There are also tables for billiards and table tennis. In the evenings, a disco is organized on site and admission is free. Once a week, the Turkish Night show program is held. In reviews of Magic Sun 4 * (Kemer / Beldibi), tourists talk about how interesting these events are, with belly dancing and live music. This allows guests to feel the charm of the Turkish night.

Children's entertainment

For younger guests, the hotel also hosts a disco, but it lasts until 9-10 pm. This program ends before the start of activities for adults. For children, the hotel has a children's pool with slides, a playground for games and a mini club where educators and animators work with them. For the convenience of the smallest guests, you can ask for delivery of a crib to your room.

Nutrition

Like most Kemer hotels, Magic Sun Hotel 4 * operates on the “All Inclusive” power system, that is, “All Inclusive”. At the same time, guests are simply delighted with the cuisine and service of the main restaurant, where a buffet is served 3-4 times a day, consisting of dishes from both Turkish and international cuisine.

According to the reviews of tourists, each of the meals turns into a real feast, because the guests here are fed unusually tasty and varied. The menu is always full of fruits, fresh pastries, various canned sweets (jams, jams and jams). All products are top quality. Tourists are served dishes of poultry, beef and lamb, as well as various types of fish. Tasty drinks, cocktails along with snacks can be enjoyed in one of the three bars, including the lobby.

Beach

Magic Sun has its own private beach, where sun loungers and umbrellas are free for hotel guests. Here you can calmly sit in the sun or under an umbrella, sunbathe, enjoy coffee or tea, which are kindly served by the staff of the bar located on the beach. Lovers of active time can go diving or snorkeling, as well as ride a "banana", jet ski, parachute.

Magic Sun Hotel 4 *: guest reviews and ratings

If you start looking for any negative reviews about the Magic Sun Hotel, you are unlikely to find it. Guests are very pleased with both the hotel service and the food provided. This item causes a lot of positive emotions. The only thing that tourists note as the “minuses” of the hotel is its location on the second line and the fact that you have to go to the beach for about 10 minutes. But this is their personal choice. It is because of the remoteness from the coastline that the prices here are much lower than in hotels with similar service on the first line. Among the obvious “advantages” are beautiful animation, chic evening entertainment - “Turkish Night” and “Foam Party”.
