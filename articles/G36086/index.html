It attracts many tourists with its developed tourist infrastructure, magnificent beaches, the presence of a variety of hotels for every taste and budget. Where is

Hotel Mari Beach 3 * (Crete) on the map, you can easily find on the coast eastward from the resort town of Georgioupolis. The hotel is separated by a distance of five kilometers. The tourist village of Kavros is just 300 meters away. So all of its infrastructure for the guests of Marie Beach is within walking distance.

How to get there

If you are on your way to the Marie Beach Hotel, it is best to choose Chania as your arrival airport. There is another air harbor in Crete. It is located in the city of Heraklion, but drive further to the hotel from here. The distance of Mari Beach 3 * (Crete) - Chania is only 37 kilometers. So after arrival, you can reach the hotel in about 45 minutes. It is very convenient, especially for tourists traveling with children.

Photo and description of the hotel

Marie Beach is a small three-star hotel that first opened its doors to guests in 1988. The last repair was carried out in 2007. It consists of six small buildings, offering everyone to stay in comfortable rooms. The hotel also has a picturesque green area with a swimming pool, restaurant and bar. Just a couple of tens of meters from the pool is a sandy beach. Mari Beach 3 * (Crete), reviews of tourists confirm that, is a great place for a relaxing comfortable holiday by the sea. It will surely appeal to both family travelers with children and youth.

Hotel Policies

Check in at the hotel according to the rules is made after two in the afternoon. But in fact, Marie Beach staff always tries to accommodate arriving guests immediately after arrival. On the day of departure, the room must be vacated before noon.





Number of rooms

Hotel Mari Beach 3 * (Crete) has at its disposal 61 comfortable rooms. They are located in six small buildings. The housing stock of the hotel is represented by standard as well as family rooms. Each of them has its own bathroom with bath or shower, TV, telephone, Wi-Fi. A safe and minibar are available at an extra charge. Most rooms overlook the sea. Rooms are cleaned every day, and linen and towels are updated several times a week.

Nutrition

Mari Beach Hotel 3 * (Crete) offers its guests all-inclusive meals. Three times a day, a buffet awaits you at the restaurant. In addition, during breaks between main meals, you can have a bite to eat delicious pastries in the hotel cafeteria. There is a bar in Marie Beach, where an experienced bartender will treat you to a drink for every taste.





Beach vacation

Just 20 meters from the hotel there is a magnificent beach. It is municipal, so you will have to pay for the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas. The beach here is sandy, in the water near the shore there is a narrow strip of pebbles, and then again sand. Thanks to the gentle approach, it is very convenient to relax here with young children.

Accommodation cost

The price for accommodation in Mari Beach 3 * (Crete) is consistent with the category of hotel and the services offered in it. So, a seven-day accommodation for two in the summer will cost you in the amount of 35 thousand rubles.
