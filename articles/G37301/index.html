Holiday Village "Upkan": description, services and route

The nature of Bashkortostan is rich and diverse. About twelve thousand rivers flow in this country. The republic is called the lake pearl of Russia. Ponds of Bashkiria from a height look like mirrors of various shapes, decorated in a magnificent green baguette of the forests. Hot summers and cold winters make the rest active and diverse throughout the year. Beautiful nature, rich wildlife, plenty of berries, excellent fishing - all this attracts vacationers throughout the year.





One of the most beautiful corners of the republic of Bashkortostan is located near the capital, in the Nurimanov district. It is here, near the village of Nimislyarovo, that there is the purest Lake Upkan, and the recreation center "Upkan" is located nearby.

A bit about the lake

In the “Komsomolki” rating, Lake “Upkan” received the title “The Nut Lake”. The reservoir has the shape of a horseshoe and is the old woman of the Ufimka River. It is here that a unique watermelon Chillim grows (more precisely, just two of its species). Floating on the surface, at the end of summer this plant drops ripe nuts into the water, which are valued no less than ginseng. The lake has a total length of 2.5 km. It is framed by deciduous forests. Since 1965, the lake has been recognized as a hydrological natural monument.

How it all started

And it's just a paradise for fishermen. Here you can catch pike, perch, whitefish, roach, burbot, crucian gold and silver, rudd. For this reason, in the sixties of the last century, the flagship of the Russian motor industry - OJSC “Ufa Engine-Building Production Association” - built a holiday complex.





After the overhaul, the Upkan recreation center was given to the UMPO youth union. And now, after working days, there is gaining strength, competing, conducting festivals a young shift. In this place motorists hold informal congresses, competitions - fishing and hunting enthusiasts, the military-patriotic camp "Yuldash" is functioning, and young people are simply resting actively. UMPO youth festivals are held here, seminars are regularly held for young activists of the workshops and departments of the association, and in the summer one race is given to children.

What does Upkan offer?

Consider the living conditions. After a major overhaul, Upkan Recreation Center hosts guests all year. For a good time, it is preferable to book rooms in advance. On the territory of the base there are four large comfortable buildings that can accommodate up to 47 people at a time in the warm season. Bright, comfortable rooms are designed for two, three and six beds. There is everything that you may need on vacation:

  • A large living room seats up to 60 people.
  • TV and music center.
  • The large kitchen is open all year.
  • For children there is a special equipped playground.

The lovers of outdoor activities in the summer, the administration of the base offers to drive the ball on the football field, go on a real fishing trip, arrange competitions in beach and classic volleyball, romance on a boat trip or on a catamaran. A large sandy beach will welcome lovers of a relaxing holiday. In the summer, you can use the kitchen in a separate area where there is a place for barbecue facilities, a roomy table for a large warm company, hot and cold water.





In the winter season, the recreation center "Upkan", the photo of which is located above, provides a wonderful skating rink, skiing, snowmobiling. In bad weather, you can relax in the games room with tennis tables and tabletop American football. A large bathhouse is open all year, from which you can go down a straight path directly to the lake.

"Upkan" - recreation center: how to get from Ufa

The easiest way is to go from Ufa in the direction of Iglino, and then move along the road to the Pavlovsk reservoir to the village of Nimislyarovo. You need to drive through the village right to Upkankul Lake. From Ufa to the recreation center about 60 km.

Conclusion

The recreation center "Upkan" is waiting for those who:

  • Appreciates real relaxation;
  • prefers a bath;
  • loves nature, fishing and hunting;
  • happy to spend time in the company of boring friends.
