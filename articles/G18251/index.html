DEXP TVs: customer reviews

The overview below will describe a range of versatile multimedia devices such as DEXP televisions. Feedback from their owners, technical specifications and the cost of such solutions will be further considered in stages. A brief summary of this company will also be provided.

Company Brief

The DEXP brand appeared in 1998. This is a Russian company, and initially its headquarters was located in Vladivostok. Its experimental laboratories, which develop electronic equipment, are located in the Russian Federation. But the company itself is forced to carry out the production process in China by concluding appropriate assembly contracts for the products.

Initially, personal computers were manufactured under the DEXP brand. But then the range expanded significantly. In addition to PCs, the company began to produce tablets, smartphones, laptops.

Separately, it is necessary to note the multimedia centers of this company. They have a fairly high quality, reliability, reasonable price and good characteristics. This is how DEXP TVs characterize reviews. Who produces them? In each case, it is necessary to study the documentation. Most often is China. But you can also meet both the Russian Federation and Belarus.

Mid-level multimedia system : options
