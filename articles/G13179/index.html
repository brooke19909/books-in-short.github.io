Starry sky as a gift

Planetarium Star Hall thought out to the smallest detail so that a small visitor can fall in love with this world at a glance. The hemispherical dome with the help of six projectors creates the effect of the starry sky.

Sitting in chairs that recline 45 degrees, you can get the effect of a space flight to uncharted worlds. Visiting the observatory on the second floor, with the help of telescopes and the latest equipment, you will get a chance to observe not only the stars, but also to see a beautiful panorama of the city itself with its bridges, squares and parks. Theatrical performance can greatly expand the child's knowledge of the world, as well as captivate him with the secrets of the cosmos. Beast park and pleasure rides

Novosibirsk Zoo is a real decoration of the city. Thanks to Maxim Dmitrievich Zverev, the founder of the zoo, and his followers, this masterpiece of human love and responsible attitude to nature has become a symbol of a civilized connection of the animal and human world.

He was appreciated throughout the country, and in 2008 the Novosibirsk Zoo became the finalist of the "Seven Wonders of Russia" contest. In it, a huge area is reserved for natural enclosures, in which about ten thousand representatives of the animal world feel at ease in an approximate natural habitat. That is why every year you can admire the offspring of even those species of animals that rarely breed in unnatural environments. The white teddy bear Shilka, a favorite of the Novosibirsk children and the star of the zoo, was born here. The zoo holds children's contests and holidays. One of the results of this competition was an exhibition of birdhouses, the unique and inimitable beauty of bird houses, which welcomes visitors to the zoo.

Sculptures of giant ancient animals recall the history of the animal world. With them, the townspeople and guests love to be photographed for memory.

If the question arises, where to go with a child in Novosibirsk, the answer is obvious - to the zoo! And an amusement park on its territory will be a good bonus of pleasure for children.

Theater - for children

The cozy small hall of the Regional Puppet Theater seats just 180 spectators. This is a very popular theater for children, famous for its repertoire far beyond Siberia. Novosibirsk is a big city, so you need to buy tickets for children's tales and performances in advance, three or more months before the show.

For ease of viewing, the chairs are adjusted to the growth of small spectators. Performances for children in Novosibirsk can be watched by spectators aged two to twelve years. Parks and attractions

There are a sufficient number of parks in Novosibirsk. In the summer, children are offered amusement rides for different ages, and in winter they are snowy towns with slides and winter activities - sleigh rides, hot cakes in cafes and barbecue skewers.

The largest amusement park is Central. Located virtually in the city center, it is also a concert venue for famous music groups and artists.

Pervomaisky Square is a good and interesting place for walks of many lovers of joint rest with children. Park "On the Embankment" is located on the banks of the Ob. In summer, cafes and attractions are warmly welcomed here, recreation programs on river modes of transport begin and end. In winter, the most picturesque Winter City is being built on the waterfront.

City for children

An excellent way to free and usefully organize free time with children is to visit the Car Park. It is built recently. The goal of this park is to turn children into attentive road users. In winter, you can use your own or rented bike. In summer, all types of transport are available: scooters, bicycles, children's vehicles. An interesting game to use - a great form of relaxation.

Where to go with a child in Novosibirsk? Of course, to the circus!

The State Circus in Novosibirsk is located in the very center of the city. The program changes several times a year, so the variety allows you to visit the circus quite often.

The circus has always been and remains a favorite place for children. Where can I see magic tricks, bears on scooters, clowns, take pictures with trained dogs? Of course, in the circus.

He opens not only the central doors, but also the backstage. At intermission, you will find artists and animals who allow you to learn the secrets of everyday life. You can ride a horse on the arena and buy delicious popcorn, ice cream and a circus props for memory!
