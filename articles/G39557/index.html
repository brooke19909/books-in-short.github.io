How old are tax deductions for children? Article 218 of the Tax Code of the Russian Federation. Standard tax deductions

Today we will be interested in tax deductions for children. How old are they paid? And how to arrange them? In what sizes are these provided? The answers to these questions are recommended to be clarified to each citizen of the Russian Federation. They are especially important for those who will soon have children. Everyone wants to take the official opportunity to reduce personal income tax. Only not everyone has such a chance. Next, we will try to study the deductions for children in great detail.

Description

What to deal with? And before what age can tax deductions for children be formalized?

This type of refund is an official way to reduce the tax base when calculating personal income tax for a fixed amount. Thus, a citizen will be able to pay ultimately less income tax on salaries. True, not everyone has the right to use this state bonus.

Basic conditions

Who can apply for a deduction for a child? To implement the task it is necessary to meet certain criteria. They are established by the state and are uniform for all regions of the Russian Federation.

To be eligible for a deduction for children, you must:

  • be a citizen of the Russian Federation;
  • have an official place of employment;
  • receive salary taxed by personal income tax;
  • bring up children (including adopted).

As a rule, both adoptive parents or parents are entitled to a tax deduction. One of them may waive such powers in favor of the spouse. Then the citizen will receive an increased return. Below we consider the process of implementing the law being studied.

Salary Limitations

It is worth paying attention to another very important point. The thing is that a tax deduction for children is possible until a citizen receives a salary up to a certain annual level. What does it mean?

To date, the studied return can be claimed by someone who meets the criteria set above, provided that he receives no more than 350 thousand rubles a year. As soon as annual earnings become higher, the right to a tax deduction for a child is abolished. Of course, it will be restored if income falls to the specified value.

Who are you putting on

According to Article 218 of the Tax Code of the Russian Federation, parents, guardians or adoptive parents can apply for the bonus under study. The main thing is that adoption is official, and kinship is proven.

The legislation of the Russian Federation allows for a deduction for all children, including persons with disabilities. True, up to a certain age. But we will talk about this later.

It is worth noting that even after adulthood, the law allows in some cases to draw up a tax deduction. True, selectively.

Important: deductions are issued for each child. That is, their sums add up in the end.

Basic age restrictions

Want to request a tax deduction for a child? To what age is it provided without special difficulties and unique conditions?

By law, a parent, guardian, or adoptive parent can demand a reduction in the tax base when calculating personal income tax from their salary until the child is 18 years old. The termination of the relevant bonus from the state is carried out before the end of the year in which the minor became fully capable.

However, if at the same time the child is employed or gets married / gets married, the right to return funds from his parents is abolished immediately. That is, from the month in which the relevant events occurred.

After coming of age

The tax deduction for a child under 18 years of age is drawn up in general cases. Nevertheless, Russian legislation is very flexible. It allows you to receive a variety of benefits, bonuses and other unique opportunities for children, even after they reach adulthood. And a decrease in the tax base when calculating personal income tax on salaries is no exception!

After 18 years, the tax deduction for a child, as already noted, is drawn up selectively. Usually parents of full-time students can apply for it, provided that the students are not married, and also do not have an official place of work.

In this case, a reduction in personal income tax is required before the child reaches 24 years of age, or before graduation, but provided that these age limits are not reached.

Important: the reduction in the taxable amount of the parents' salaries is carried out before the end of the year in which the education ceased or the child is 24 years old.

About sizes

The amount of tax deductions per child is also of interest to many citizens. Not all state bonuses are profitable. Some of them provide significant paperwork, but the output is very small.

Fortunately, in the case of tax deductions, things are not so bad. Paperwork is minimal, and the sense of exercising the corresponding powers really helps citizens to get a small increase in salaries actually paid on their hands.

Currently, the tax deduction for 1 child is 1,400 rubles. The same amount can be claimed for two children. If there are three or more minor / full-time students in the family, the amount of return will be 3,000 rubles. Similar amounts are due not only to parents, but also to guardians / adoptive parents.

The situation is somewhat different with children with disabilities. For each "special" child, the parent can apply for a deduction of 12,000 rubles, and the remaining legal representatives - for 6,000. These amounts are relevant for minors and students up to 24 years old.

Thirdly, if the child for whom the deduction relies is disabled, you can get a slightly increased decrease in the tax base. For example, for one disabled child you can get 13,400 rubles in the form of a deduction, for one disabled person and one healthy baby - 14,800 rubles and so on.

  • passport;
