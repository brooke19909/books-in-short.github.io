Characteristics of this profession

A specialist in ichthyology studies the evolutionary development of fish, their structure and life forms, and the characteristics of reproduction. Based on the obtained data, he explores various fishing methods, determines which of them are the most rational. In addition, an ichthyologist is a scientist who studies fish farming and is engaged in fish conservation activities.



It should be noted that this profession involves not only acquaintance with the anatomical features of aquatic inhabitants, but also with their industrial species. Only an ichthyologist, whose profession is rather complicated but interesting, can indicate how to breed fish properly, keep them and grow them.

I must say that work in this direction has certain features. Let's consider them in more detail.

The specifics of the professional activity of ichthyologists

These specialists conduct research related to the construction of dams, hydroelectric power plants or any enterprises whose activities are associated with the use and discharge of water into lakes, rivers and seas (natural bodies of water).

What do ichthyologist professions do? They can work in research institutes, at industrial and floating fish canneries, apply their knowledge while working in enterprises that specialize in fishing, and also engage in scientific work.

In addition, an ichthyologist is an employee who can occupy certain positions in zoos or oceanariums, conduct monitoring in reserves and at sites that belong to nature protection zones.

It should be noted that for successful professional activities such people need to be well-versed not only in ichthyology, but also have a sufficient level of knowledge in the field of geography, hydrology and botany, as well as paleozoology and climatology.

What is necessary for work

As a rule, special equipment is not necessary for the ichthyologist's research work - it is enough to have containers with formalin and alcohol. For catching fish, a trawl net is predominantly taken. The researcher gets on board the vessel and sent to the studied areas of the reservoir, after which the catch of aquatic inhabitants begins. Due to the specific shape of the trawl net, which looks like a net, the fish has no chance to get out of it.

After there is enough catch, the net is taken aboard. The fish that is caught is laid out on a tarp and studied. How to become an ichthyologist?

The profession of ichthyologist is a rather complicated field, therefore, appropriate education is compulsory. A sufficient level of knowledge in order to become a good specialist can be obtained at the Lomonosov Moscow State University by graduating from the Faculty of Biology. Here, the training of ichthyologists is carried out, which in the future can independently conduct various scientific studies.

If you graduate from Moscow University of Technology and Management, you can get the education of an ichthyologist, who later has the right to work in zoos, zoos and other institutions where fish are kept. You can also graduate from Dmitrov Fishery College.

It should be noted that ichthyologists should be well versed in other related sciences and be able to use the latest achievements of chemistry and physics, as well as special equipment for underwater research and surveys.

Ichthyologist in fish farming

This specialist monitors the hydrobiological condition of the reservoir, develops the necessary tasks to optimize the operation of the site, which specializes in fish farming, and also develops a work plan and schedules in accordance with the stages of development of aquatic inhabitants.



What does an ichthyologist do? He also controls fish stocks and studies the influence of various factors on them, analyzes the species, age and weight composition of the catch, conducts biological research. In addition, the ichthyologist develops proposals regarding the more rational use of water bodies, conservation and growth of fish stocks, and establishes permits or prohibitions on fishing, takes part in the development and implementation of the latest technologies for the cultivation of commercial fish species.

His responsibilities include monitoring the proper use of apparatus and equipment, as well as the state of the water protection strip along the banks of water bodies. He also monitors certain preventive maintenance activities (for example, the layout of pond beds).

What an ichthyologist should know for maximum fish stocks in ponds

This employee must have perfect knowledge in the field of fish farming and the operation of hydraulic structures, understand pond and lake fish farming, know the features and the principle of operation of various equipment and mechanisms that can be used. He must also determine the level of acclimatization of fish and their diseases, carry out the necessary veterinary, sanitary, preventive or therapeutic measures, monitor the quality of fish products and establish industrial and economic relations with other enterprises for its profitable marketing.



The ichthyologist should also know the regulatory documentation for fish farming, the rules and regulations regarding labor protection and fire safety, be able to record and report in the prescribed manner.

For these specialists put up the relevant qualification requirements. So, in order to be a fish farmer of the first category, you need to have a higher ichthyological or zootechnical education, as well as work experience as a fish farmer of the second category for more than three years.
