In the historical center of the city is the hotel "Baccarat". Yaroslavl, Corporate Street, 9 - this is the address of this institution. It is only 6 km from the train station. Tunoshna International Airport is 2 km away. It is attractive that just 200 meters away is the promenade, which is so nice to walk after a busy day.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, no hotel has absolutely perfect living conditions. Speaking about this institution, it is worth noting such shortcomings:

  • the bathroom has very poor lighting (if you close the curtain while bathing, it becomes completely dark);
  • there are no slippers in the rooms, and the floor in the bathroom is very cold;
  • upon arrival, the minibar is empty (drinks must be ordered additionally);
  • there are no sockets in the bathroom;
  • there are no maids at night;
  • breakfast lacks fresh pastries;
  • the smoking area is located right at the entrance to the hotel, and there is always a "smoke screen";
  • there is very little space on free parking; only a few cars fit;
  • plumbing needs to be replaced;
  • there are no outlets near the bed;
  • maids do not make beds in rooms;
  • for accommodation of small children in a room you have to pay extra if they are on the same bed with parents;
  • the set of breakfast dishes is repeated day after day (but this is only important for those guests who have been staying at the hotel for a long time);
  • Of the toiletries in the bathroom, only liquid soap in the dispenser;
  • one cooler with drinking water for the whole hotel - on the ground floor;
  • buffet breakfast is ugly served during breakfast;
  • due to poor ventilation, the smell of food from the restaurant is always felt on the ground floor;
  • there is no air freshener in the bathroom;
  • the drain does not work well, the smell of sewage is often present;
  • the rooms have little furniture (you have to sit on the bed);
  • moldy bread is often served for breakfast (this is perhaps the only drawback of the dining room);
  • weak air conditioners do not cool rooms well;
  • the number of towels in the room does not match the number of guests;
  • cleaning is carried out absolutely haphazardly (maids can come both early in the morning and late in the evening);
  • when a wedding banquet is held in the hotel restaurant, the noise spreads throughout the establishment, none of the guests can relax normally;
  • an old-fashioned interior that does not correspond to the four-star status of the hotel.

Conclusion

Hotel "Baccarat" is a good place for those who want to get to know the city and conduct tours of Yaroslavl. Nearly all attractions are within walking distance. Living conditions and prices are very reasonable. The only thing that upsets is the condition of the street on which the hotel is located. It features terrible unrepaired roads, as well as ugly gray buildings.
