PJ Patong Resortel 3 * (Thailand, Patong Beach): room description, service, reviews

PJ Patong Resortel is a hotel in Thailand that invites all tourists who want to spend time together to spend a vacation within their walls. Travel agencies often recommend it to those travelers who wish to relax at the lowest cost and in the most favorable conditions.

general information

The year of construction of the hotel is considered to be 2008. At this time, a five-story building was erected in a relatively small area, in which all rooms were placed. Since then, its appearance has been supported by various renovations, the largest of which was made in 2010 - it was at that moment that the technical equipment of the apartments was improved.

This place is perfect for families who come to rest without children, because, according to the guests, loud music that plays during discos and shows will certainly interfere with children's sleep. This category of tourists reviews recommend looking for a quieter place.



Location

PJ Patong Resortel is located on the island of Phuket, which is under the jurisdiction of Thailand. It is located on Patong Beach, but at a fairly large distance from the sea coast. A huge number of tourists who live in it, in their reviews note that they like to visit local shopping centers located near the hotel. One of the most famous among them is Bangla Road, which was built a 10-minute walk from the main building. The area also has a huge number of places for night and daytime entertainment, which are surely visited by guests.

If we talk about transport links, the nearest international airport is 25 minutes away by car from the PJ Patong Resortel 3 * building.

Rooms

The hotel has a small number of apartments - only 90. All of them are located in one building and are made in the same category - "standard". Each of them has been modernized, and some technical equipment has been created that allows guests to feel more comfortable staying here.

All apartments have tiled floors and offer beautiful views of the city surroundings. The official descriptions of the rooms of PJ Patong Resortel say that each of them has a built-in air conditioner, which works from the central control panel according to a certain schedule. Thanks to this, tourists staying here do not languish from the heat in the street, as the air temperature in the whole room is cooled thanks to this device.

Each room has a private bathroom, where there is everything to maintain hygiene: from ordinary bath accessories to modern sanitary ware.



Room Interior

Guests of PJ Patong Resortel in their reviews often talk about the beautiful stylish interior in which the apartments of the hotel are made. According to them, a real classic reigns here, emphasized by the presence of a large number of dense textiles on windows and walls, as well as a large number of wood details.

The central place in the rooms is occupied by a large double bed, opposite which there is a large TV connected to the satellite channel system, many of which, as guests mark, are Russian-speaking. The rooms have excellent lighting, which can be adjusted from more muted to the brightest. There is also a small work area, represented by a large desk made of lacquered wood, as well as a chair with a soft seat.

If desired, guests can request the installation of a minibar, safe and telephone with the ability to dial international numbers. If desired, guests can use the tea substation, which is available in each apartment, on a free basis - with its help and from the provided ingredients, you can prepare your favorite hot drinks.



Services

On the territory of PJ Patong Resortel 3 * staff provides a range of services that are aimed at making the conditions for tourists more comfortable. The most popular among them are those that are provided by the laundry and dry cleaning (for washing clothes and linen). At the entrance to the hotel, your luggage can be handed over to the camera, which is rented for a certain period of time - you need to inform the administrator about the need to provide it at the time of arrival.

On the ground floor of the complex, near the main entrance, there is a currency exchange, as well as an ATM, where you can get cash. There is also a tourist office where you can organize a collective or individual tour of the local attractions, which are many in Phuket.

On a free basis, any hotel guest can get advice on staying on its territory or providing services. They are provided by the administrator, who is located at the reception around the clock. Among the services of PJ Patong Resortel there is also the possibility of calling a taxi and ordering a wake-up call.



For business

Often, guests of a hotel are people whose vacation is related to doing business. While on a business trip at PJ Patong Resortel, many have the need to meet with business partners, hold negotiations or presentations. To achieve such goals, the body has a small conference room, which is equipped with office equipment, sound and video equipment, which without fail will make any event more vivid and eventful. All guests invited to the meeting can sit in comfortable chairs, at small tables.

The hall can be rented hourly, the rates for this service, guests are advised to find out at the hotel at the time of arrival, as they tend to change.

Pool

A huge number of hotel guests in their reviews about the rest in it tells that there is a beautiful aquazone, which is arranged in compliance with all safety standards for those who bathe in them. This whole territory is not slippery at all, there are places where you can relax - they are presented in the form of large deckchairs that are placed under folding umbrellas, which carefully protect tourists from the harmful effects of sunlight.

The central place in the aquazone is occupied by a large pool, which is presented in the form of a niche of a rounded shape with crystal clear water inside. Vacationers note that there is no automatic heating system in it, as a result of which the optimum water temperature is achieved solely under the influence of the sun, which is not very favorable in winter. In the evening, the pool walls glow with lights - it looks very beautiful from the side.

Children's entertainment at PJ Patong Resortel mainly comes down to swimming in a small pool, which is separately equipped for them.



Nutrition

The hotel has two catering establishments, which offer delicious dishes prepared in the national traditions of Thailand. Tourists who relax here also note that the menu of the main restaurant presents a number of European dishes. The food at PJ Patong Resortel, in their opinion, is simply excellent, it provides for a visit to the buffet in the main institution, which updates the list of dishes on offer three times a day.

Tourists pay special attention in their comments to the presence of a bar in this place for relaxation. It serves excellent drinks that have an original taste, as well as suitable snacks. Often there are reviews in which the group of desserts presented in the list of dishes is presented with positive ratings.



Also, many tourists who rested here recommend visiting catering establishments located next door to the hotel. They, as noted, also offer a luxurious selection of cuisines from around the world.

Relaxation

A large number of PJ Patong Resortel guests talk about the beach in passing, without particularly touching on the details. All this is due to the fact that many of them do not visit it while staying in a hotel, but are limited to the territory of the aquazone. This is because the hotel is located on a sandy territory, but it is quite far from the coastline - in order to reach it, you need to overcome a distance of 800 meters, which takes about 15 minutes. According to tourists, in hot weather this is not the best solution.

The nearest beach is urban, it is completely covered with small pebbles. On its territory there are free public toilets, showers, and locker rooms. If desired, guests can rent a sun lounger, umbrella or air mattress. Experienced tourists recommend bringing towels with them, as their rental service is not available here.



A huge number of positive comments tourists address towards the massage room, which operates in the hotel. According to them, real professionals work here, who know exactly how to give the client maximum pleasure and immerse him in a state of relaxation.

where to go

Small tours of local attractions can always be arranged in a special bureau, which is located on the ground floor of the building. It often offers a trip to places such as Freedom Beach, Wat Chalong, and many recommend being here, without fail, visit the Phuket Simon cabaret.

Literally a kilometer from the hotel is the central part of Phuket, where nightlife is actively boiling. There are many cabarets, bars, restaurants, night clubs and other places where spending time will be very interesting. A huge number of tourists recommend buying some souvenirs for themselves and their relatives and friends before leaving, which are sold in the market and in shopping centers located near the PJ Patong Resortel 3 * (Phuket) building.

Prices

The cost of a ticket to this hotel is very low, which makes it popular among tourists. According to their reviews, the average tour price in PJ Patong Resortel is about 120-150 thousand rubles, which is quite acceptable for hotels in Thailand. Any tourist operator includes a flight for two on an airplane, a weekly rental of apartments, as well as a transfer of guests from Phuket Airport in a modern car with air conditioning. If desired, guests can include a number of other services in the additional cost, however, tourists who often relax in Thailand recommend booking and paying on the spot, since in this case their cost varies greatly, and in favor of the client. This is especially true of prices for excursions.
