Knitting technique

Each needlewoman will be able to crochet amigurumi. The schemes for such products are primitive and uncomplicated. First you need to wrap the selected yarn around the finger in two turns. We knit six columns into the ring, gently tighten the ring. Knitting takes place in a circle. By adding new loops and removing old ones, the desired shape is given to the details of the character.





Only through experiment will you understand in which place it is worth making the addition, and where it is better not to change the shape.

Green monster: description

Now we will tell you how to crochet amigurumi dolls. We will not need schemes for this monster, since the process is quite simple.





It is better to start knitting from the head; it is twice as large as the body. To do this, we collect a chain of air loops. Their number depends on the dimensions of the future creature. In this case, a chain of five loops was drawn. They close in a circle. Each row is crocheted with columns. Do not forget to add loops so that the monster's head expands. To do this, you need to knit two double crochet in one loop . As soon as you get to the middle, the workpiece should be reduced with each row. The reduction is due to the fact that two double crochet columns are made , which will have a common vertex. Thus, we move to the very edge. Having tied a knot, cut the thread.

The body fits in the same way. Only the diameter of the workpiece will be smaller. As you can see, amigurumi crochet toys are very easy to create. Schemes are not even required. Next, we knit legs and handles. The upper limbs are slightly thinner than the lower ones. Keep this in mind when creating your character. Remember how many air loops you collect, otherwise one hand will be thicker than the other.

From black yarn it is still necessary to tie the ears to our cosmic being. They can be connected by the rectangle method. Tie a square and sew the edges. Eyes should not cause complications either. The main thing is to observe symmetry. It is better to decorate the character with beads or additional lines of thread. Here's a cute crochet amigurumi. The schemes were not even useful.
