Krasnodar Territory, Nebug. Nebug: hotels. Nebug village: reviews of vacationers

A few years ago, the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory were considered a budget option for spending summer vacations with appropriate comfort prices. Today, Russian cities on the Black Sea are not much inferior to foreign holiday destinations. If you decide to visit the Krasnodar Territory, Nebug is an excellent village for living. It has a well-developed infrastructure, and prices are slightly lower than in the more publicized resort areas.

Location and Features





Nebug is located near the famous city of Tuapse. The distance between settlements is only about 15 km. The village received its name in honor of the river of the same name. The private residential sector stretches in this area in the direction from the beach to the mountains. This area is notable for its natural beauties. On the outskirts of Nebug begins a pine forest, the trees of which, according to some sources, are at least a hundred years old. Today it is a developed resort center due to the large number of entertainment facilities. During the day, you can meet a huge number of tourists on the streets, but a relatively small number of them live in the village during the holidays. Most of them come for day trips from neighboring cities. The village of Nebug is a magical place to relax, not without reason even its name is unusual - literally translated from the Adyghe language it means "wide eyes."

Beach vacation





The gentle sea and a lot of sun ... That is what attracts the Krasnodar Territory. Nebug, like many coastal villages, has both pebble and sandy bulk beaches. In the immediate vicinity of the village there are many sanatoriums and rest houses, some of which also boast their own access to the sea. These beaches are usually better landscaped than urban. If you wish, vacationers who do not live in a sanatorium or hotel can get on them, but you will have to pay for the entrance. Free access to the well-maintained main beach of the village, there are also "wild" places for swimming. Those who wish to swim in a secluded place should be extremely careful. Hotels

In Nebug there are a large number of mini-hotels and private holiday homes. Several large hotels stand out against their background: Lightning, Sea Club and Aqua Vita. Any tourist will find an accommodation option that suits him in terms of comfort and cost. Too many vacationers come here in the tourist season, it is recommended to book accommodation in advance. The village of Nebug has various hotels, the minimum cost of living in them is from 200 rubles per day per person or from 1000 rubles per room. The cost of housing increases in proportion to its comfort and location relative to the sea. Some hotels have their own beaches, while others will have to spend at least 10-15 minutes on the road to the promenade. Not all hotels in the village are family oriented. If you are planning to go on vacation with your children, it is worth clarifying a number of parameters. As practice shows, the most important is the presence of children's furniture or special rooms for children and parents to live together, playgrounds and pools in the hotel, a special menu in the dining room or cafe.

Entertainment and leisure





Disco until the morning, inexpensive cafes and respectable restaurants, modern amusement parks - all this is the Krasnodar Territory. Nebug is no exception, the village has its own dolphinarium and water park. These centers will appeal to children and adults. In dolphinariums you can watch performances with the participation of marine animals, touch them and take pictures for memory. Waterpark is a great place where you can spend an exciting day at an affordable price. Among the attractions are children's for kids of different ages, calm family and extreme. An entrance ticket is paid, after which each visitor can ride on all the attractions any number of times. Free use of sun loungers and parasols. For some attractions, sliding mats and inflatable rafts for descents are issued. Bright nightlife is another reason to visit the Krasnodar Territory. Nebug vacation offers the most different in the evening hours. You can find restaurants with gourmet cuisine and live music service, night clubs or bars on the promenade are no less popular. There is a private diving center in the village, and there are a lot of water attractions on the beaches.
