Back loop in knitting

Knitting with knitting needles is an old and already widespread type of needlework. DIY products have always been relevant. Using knitting needles, you can knit products for children's, women's and men's wardrobe. Such clothes have a unique and original appearance. Now, knitting, as never before, has become relevant and fashionable again. Not only grandmothers and mature women are engaged in it, but also very young people who will only learn what knitting technology is and how to master it.





You can learn to knit even on your own with the help of special literature, which is actually available in a regular bookstore. The basis of knitting is the front loop and the wrong loop, with which it becomes possible to create even the most intricate and complex patterns. Show maximum patience and perseverance, and then you can master all the intricacies of hand knitting.

Traditional purl loop

Known to all knitters, the performance of the traditional purl loop makes sense in the following actions. To get started, remove the edge loop and place the main thread of work in front of the left knitting needle on top of your index finger. Enter the right one into the very first loop by moving from right to left. Grab the main working thread, wrap the thread and pull it into the loop. Your wrong loop is ready. The most spectacular patterns that can be obtained when knitting with purl loops are a pearl pattern, a scarf pattern, a purl. You will have to use the technique of knitting the purl stitches all the time. Almost no pattern can do without them, because they are present in the simplest and most complex patterns at the same time.

Wrong crossed loop



Another kind of loop is the wrong crossed loop. She also needs to learn how to knit to master this technique of needlework. We take and start the thread on the left knitting needle. We introduce the right knitting needle by smooth movement on ourselves from left to right. We try to firmly grasp the working thread with a gentle movement in the direction away from us and pull out a new loop. If we knit the wrong crossed loops, in this case all the front ones also need to be knitted with crossed loops.
