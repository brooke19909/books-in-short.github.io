An interesting and useful device - a mini-air conditioner

Despite its modest dimensions, mini-air conditioning can compete even with large devices.

Multi-zone air conditioner

Such a unit is designed for cooling, heating and filtering air inside a number of residential, commercial or industrial premises: cottages, apartments, shops, restaurants, small offices, etc. The peculiarity of a multi-zone mini-air conditioner is that it is able to maintain comfortable conditions simultaneously in several rooms. Such devices are usually classified as equipment with a split refrigeration circuit. The system can include up to 9 units - outdoor and indoor units. The first are oriented to installation outside the building. The second ones suggest that they will be placed inside - directly in an air-conditioned room or in rooms located nearby. Between themselves, indoor and outdoor units are combined through freon and electrical communications. Such devices have great potential in terms of the organization of air conditioning systems. The control of these units can be done manually or by connecting to a computer.



It is worth mentioning about such a model as a mini-air conditioning in the car. This option may be of interest to those who do not have an air conditioning system in the car . It is conveniently integrated, connected to the car wiring, which allows you to create comfortable conditions inside the vehicle.
