A visit to the Yaroslavl Dolphinarium - an explosion of joy and positive emotions!

Every day we are faced with negative emotions and stress. This is extremely detrimental to well-being and health. A visit to the Yaroslavl Dolphinarium is one of the ways to improve your mood and emotional state, as well as experience a storm of indescribable sensations. Time spent with dolphins will help you forget about your problems and enjoy the warmth and affection of these unusual mammals. Initially, such establishments were designed as entertainment centers, which make it possible to visit in the immediate vicinity of the animal world. These days, these complexes not only surprise with incredible show programs, but are also active participants in various social events, helping to educate, support and even treat young viewers.

Location and a bit of history

The Yaroslavl Dolphinarium located in the village of Dubki is a unique complex in Yaroslavl and the region itself. It was opened in 2009 on the basis of the former Sever sports complex with the participation of businessman Andrei Torbin. The first animals brought here from Cape Chkalov were a fur seal and three belugas. They were completely unprepared for performances, because initially the entertainment program was a visual training. What is included in the complex?





The Yaroslavl Dolphinarium complex accommodates a maximum of 300 spectators. It contains two swimming pools, a small hotel with seven comfortable rooms and apartments, a wellness center, a lobby bar, a cafe with wonderful views, a restaurant, a summer terrace, a photo studio, a playroom and a summer playground for children, as well as a living room with tropical butterflies.

The pool of the complex has a size of 25 meters by 11 meters and has two depth levels: up to 1.5 meters - for swimming with animals, and 4.5 meters - for animals, their performances and recreation.

Actors

If you have planned a visit to the Yaroslavl Dolphinarium, then it will be interesting for you to get acquainted with the main cast. Now we will tell who pleases the eye of the audience. These are beluga whale Misha, Archie's Black Sea bottlenose dolphin and the northern fur seals Utah, Mary and Yara.

Yaroslavl Dolphinarium. timetable

Since the number of seats is limited, it is worth worrying about purchasing tickets to the dolphinarium in advance. This place is open from Monday to Saturday from 09.00 to 18.00 without a lunch break, closed on Sunday.

Additional services

In 2011, a health center was opened on the basis of the complex, which conducts courses of dolphin correction and dolphin therapy. These programs are designed for children who have problems with physical, mental or emotional health. Communicating with kind animals helps children cope with stress and stress, strengthen immunity, and restore emotional and physical balance. Each session of the program involves competently calculated swimming with a dolphin, as well as the use of game components.

Procedures are held in the presence of highly skilled workers. This is a psychologist, teacher and dolphin trainer. In the process of these sessions, the child and the dolphin interact at a tactile level, which is also of great importance for recovery. The standard course of dolphin therapy consists of 10 procedures.

Show programs





Dolphinarium show programs are constantly changing and improving. Ticket price
