Which resort in Turkey to choose in October?

If you are wondering where to go to Turkey in October, then you should pay attention to the Mediterranean coast. As an option, you can offer such resorts as Alanya, Antalya, Belek, Kemer and Side, as well as all adjacent smaller towns and villages.



In general, it is worth noting that if you want to swim not only in the pool, but also in the open sea, it is better to go on vacation until mid-October. Rest at such a time has several advantages, firstly, there is already no influx of tourists, and secondly, the cost of tours is significantly reduced. But there are also disadvantages. Very often in hotels at the end of the season they start saving on food, the number of animators significantly reduces or removes them altogether. Therefore, thinking over the question of where to go to Turkey in October, you need to be prepared for such not very pleasant trifles.

In the first half of October, summer heat still reigns on the coast of resorts, although there is no more heat. Daytime temperature ranges from + 25-27 degrees. But at night it‚Äôs already getting cool: + 16-18 degrees. Large water masses are so warmed up over the summer that they cool down quite slowly. The average sea temperature in October is + 22-24 degrees.But near the end of the month, cold rains begin, which later lead to a cooling of the sea. But even at this time you can find a lot of entertainment. Firstly, you can see interesting places, and secondly, go shopping, because at this time in the country begins the season of discounts and sales.

In addition, in October you can still visit amusement parks and water parks. Very often hotel staff at the end of the season is very tired, which affects the level of service. Rest in the offseason has not only positive, but also negative aspects.



In the middle of autumn, the period of holidays begins in the country. The most important of them is Kurban Bayram. It is celebrated for four whole days. At this time, you can see numerous shows, fireworks, demonstrations and festivities. And at the end of the month, the celebration of the Muslim New Year begins. If you are interested in festive events, then you can choose a tour that will coincide in time with the holidays. In addition, while relaxing in Turkey in October, you can pay attention to shopping. At this time, the weather allows you to visit the local colorful markets and shopping centers where you can buy gold jewelry and fur and leather products. A small number of tourists during this period forces traders to be more compliant and make good discounts.
