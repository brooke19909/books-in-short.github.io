How to call to Kazakhstan? Very simple

The Republic of Kazakhstan is located in the very center of Eurasia and is a neighbor of Russia. In the recent past, both republics were part of the Soviet Union and made up its bulk. The raising of the virgin lands of the Kazakh steppes, the construction of new plants and factories - all this contributed to the fact that there were a lot of Russians in the republic. Arriving to work on new buildings, people gradually settled down, started families and stayed to live in this sunny Asian republic.

Today, about 30% of the country's population is Russian. Many Russians here have relatives, friends and acquaintances with whom they maintain relations. And since in our time communication is carried out by telephone, the question of how to call Kazakhstan is of great interest to many.





We comply with international regulations

The Republic of Kazakhstan is a different country, and therefore phone calls there must be made in compliance with international rules. The country has its own code, but before that it is necessary to enter the international line. You can call Kazakhstan not only from your home phone, but also from public telephones installed in public places, as well as from a mobile phone or using the Internet, social networks, Skype.

In order to figure out how to call to Kazakhstan, we will consider all the actions in order of execution. If you have a desire to make a call from a public payphone, then this can be done using a telephone card. It can be bought at every post office or in newspaper stalls.





Making a call from your home phone, we dial 8 first, wait for the beep, continue to dial 10 - this is the code that provides access to the international line. Then follows the number 7 - the international telephone code of the country. Then we dial the code of the settlement and the number of the subscriber to whom we are trying to get through.

Before calling to Kazakhstan from Russia in the above way, you can try another one. The fact is that both states have the same communication code - 7. Therefore, the subscribers of these countries can call up without dialing 10. The current sequence of actions can be this: first dial 8, and then, after a beep, immediately dial the settlement code and the desired number .

Calling to Kazakhstan from a mobile number to a landline is even easier. After dialing +7, immediately follows the area code and subscriber number. It happens that mobile operators instead of +7 require dialing the number 8.





The easiest way to call from Russia to Kazakhstan is from mobile to mobile. In this case, you do not need to dial any codes at all, just one subscriber number is enough.

For those who want to know how to call Kazakhstan almost for free, we inform you that the cheapest calls are connected to the Internet and Skype. Such social networks as Odnoklassniki and Vkontakte offer to make calls to any country in the world, including to the countries of Central Asia. Moreover, when the call comes from computer to computer, then the fee is not charged.

As you can see, calling to Kazakhstan is easy.
