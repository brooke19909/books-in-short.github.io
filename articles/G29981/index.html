Ice Palace "Star" (Orenburg): description, prices

Modern sports complexes allow people to have a great time. They offer a wide range of services, so each visitor will be able to find for themselves an interesting option for leisure. The Zvezdny Ice Sports Palace is suitable for family holidays, meetings with friends or just for a pleasant pastime. The skating rink in the center is in great demand, and people from different parts of the city come to see hockey.

general information

The sports complex has been operating since 2007. Its construction took less than two years, and as a result, the townspeople received an excellent center for outdoor activities. The complex has an ice rink, and there is also an ice hockey arena. It accommodates about 2500 spectators. A hockey club called White Tigers regularly trains here. Under the roof of the palace there are competitions and tournaments that bring together lovers of active sports. In total, three blocks are open in the complex. This is a training area, an ice arena with stands and a public area. The complex is always full of visitors.

The Zvezdny Ice Palace in Orenburg is well known for its ice rink. Mass rides are held regularly and collect on ice people of all ages. The duration of the session is 45 minutes. On weekends, evening skating is often held, which starts after 22.00. At this time, you can sometimes ride longer. If the guests do not have their own skates, then they can rent them for 70 rubles.

Entrance prices at the Zvezdny Ice Palace (Orenburg) are as follows: for children under 10 years old - 60 rubles, for adults - 130 rubles.

Separate entertainment on ice are "Pokatushki". This is not just ice skating, but real theme parties. Guests can ride for two hours. The entrance ticket to the event is sold for 250 rubles, skate rental - another 50 rubles. "Pokatushki" give guests the opportunity to have fun, as they look like a real show. Enjoy fun and contests. Often on the occasion of the holidays you can see a masquerade or an interesting performance. Children under the age of sixteen may only come with adults.

Address

Ice Palace "Star" in Orenburg is regularly visited by many people. But still not all citizens managed to get to the popular sports center. It is located on Komsomolskaya Street, building - 197, building - 3.

How to get there

The sports complex is located next to the garden of the October Revolution. You can focus on it when searching. There are several ways to get to the Zvezdny Ice Palace (Orenburg). By trolleybus number 6 you can get to the stop called "Hospital". The complex is quite close to it. You can also go to the stop "October Street". Buses 33, 39, 67 and minibus 30 stop at it.

Additional features

Ice Palace "Star" (Orenburg) impresses with a wide selection of services for its visitors. It even has a hotel where guests can stay. Meals are organized in the cafe-bar, where there is a pretty good selection of dishes and drinks. It is also often visited by guests after the rink or hockey.

In the sports complex, you can actively engage in your figure and lose weight. There is a good gym with enough exercise equipment. If necessary, it is always available to seek help from a trainer. He will give advice on compiling the program personally to each visitor.

In the sports complex you can sign up for a massage, which is carried out by specialists. In addition to the usual, other types are also available. There is an anti-cellulite massage, as well as procedures for rehabilitation after injuries.
