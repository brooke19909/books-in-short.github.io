Traditional treatment

Zhirovik usually grows very slowly, without causing any inconvenience, so people can not go to doctors for years. But the probability of its rapid growth for no apparent reason is quite high. Therefore, doctors recommend in most cases to remove the tumor surgically. Moreover, one cannot exclude the possibility of its degeneration into a malignant one.

Removing such a tumor is a process that usually takes several minutes and is performed under local anesthesia. Healing occurs within a few days. Methods of removal depend on the size of the tumor and on the place where it is located. This may be liquid nitrogen, a laser, electrocoagulation, or conventional surgery.
