Hotel Atrium (Halkidiki, Greece): overview, features and reviews

To date, many tourists prefer tours to Greece. If you are among them, then you should think about where to stay. It is necessary to approach this issue as responsibly as possible so that the vacation is not hopelessly spoiled by poor living conditions. Alternatively, consider Atrium Hotel (Halkidiki).

General characteristics of the hotel

Cozy, comfortable and very popular among travelers, the hotel in Greece called Atrium is located in the wonderful, quiet and picturesque village of Pefkohori (Halkidiki), on its outskirts. It was built in 1999 and immediately won the love and popularity of tourists from both Russia and guests of other countries. An interesting architectural building conveys real Greek flavor. From the large and spacious windows of the wonderful Atrium 3 * hotel (Halkidiki, Kassandra), a magnificent sea panorama of the famous Toroneos Gulf extends, and there are windows overlooking the green and well-groomed territory, which is carefully monitored here day after day.

The hotel was awarded four stars, as it really offers a lot of services and excellent service for guests from any country. It has one hundred and twenty comfortable rooms for two, three people, as well as cozy suites. Each room has a chic balcony, a bathroom with all the necessary trifles, as well as towels, a spacious refrigerator and air conditioning, beautiful and stylish furniture and high-quality textiles. There is a telephone, satellite TV, and many channels in different languages, including Russian, in the rooms. All rooms are decorated in an individual, modern and interesting style.

The hotel building is very beautiful and magnificent, surrounded by greenery and trees growing in the country. The territory is well-groomed, neat and comfortable. You can find shade in exotic palm trees and pines on a hot day, or just wander around the territory, admiring the vegetation and magnificent flowers. In the evening, the illumination of the pool and other facilities gives Atrium Kassandra 3 * (Greece, Halkidiki) a charming fabulousness. According to tourists reviews, this hotel is ideal for families.





Hotel and facilities available

Any tourist who chooses Atrium Hotel 4 * (Halkidiki) for a vacation will always quickly find the answer to his question and solve any problem thanks to the 24-hour front desk and friendly staff. Nearby there is a small mini market where you can buy everything you need at any time of the day. There is a safe for storing documents, valuables and cash. In addition, right at the hotel, as well as nearby, you can make a currency exchange at any time convenient for tourists.

Internet is available in every room and on site. According to tourists, it works well, and you can use it throughout the territory. Those who wish can rent a car or bicycle for the required period during the holiday. The rooms are cleaned regularly and very carefully; hotel guests are usually very satisfied. The staff is very polite, friendly and friendly. At the reception there is a Russian-speaking guide, which is very convenient for guests from Russia.

At Atrium Hotel (Halkidiki, Kassandra), everyone will find plenty of entertainment for an interesting pastime to their taste, and no one will be bored on vacation. On the territory there is a huge and luxurious pool with clear transparent water and convenient slopes. Plunge into the cool water during a hot and sultry day - what could be better? This is a favorite place for vacationers, and most of the time guests spend it near the pool. There are two shallow depth compartments for children. In the adult pond there is a different depth, which is also very convenient. Around the pool there are sun loungers and umbrellas, which will not allow vacationers to sunbathe in the heat. Sea and beach near the hotel

The distance from the Atrium 4 * hotel (Halkidiki, Kassandra) to the sandy beach is about eight hundred meters. For many tourists it is inconvenient if they go on vacation with young children. But this is not a problem, since a special bus constantly runs from the hotel to the beach and back, and this service is absolutely free. The beach has numerous sun loungers and sun loungers, as well as parasols, which are available at an additional cost. The sea here is very calm and clean, the beach is sandy, the entrance is gentle, which is very convenient for families with small children.
