Anapa resort on the Black Sea coast. Once having been there, you are unlikely to be able to forget your vacation in this nice and cozy town. Pebble beaches and quartz golden sands, enchanting sunsets and the sound of the surf, are very popular. Arriving in Anapa, you find yourself in a fairy tale!

Settles in the resort town, it will not be any work. The hospitable hotels of Anapa will open their doors in front of you. Anapa hotels can offer vacationers rooms for every taste and price level. If you wish, you can choose an area on the coast or in the center, near entertainment centers or in a quiet corner.

The only five-star hotel in Anapa is the Valenitna Grand Hotel. Favorably distinguished by the individual design of all rooms, the interior and decoration of the guest areas. True connoisseurs of comfort will appreciate this hotel and enjoy its elegance of comfort.

When listing hotels in Anapa, one cannot but mention the Captain hotel, located on the central promenade of the city, almost at the very water. Thanks to such an original architectural solution, you can enjoy from the room how the hot sun descends into the sea. And also contemplate snow-white yachts peacefully swaying on the waves. Leaving the hotel, you immediately find yourself on the quay of Anapa.

Not many Anapa hotels have the status of three stars. These include the Eurasia Hotel, located in the first resort area. Almost at its entrance is a yacht club, and reach the water's edge by hand. After walking just a few minutes along the promenade, you find yourself in a whirlpool of vibrant resort life: restaurants, cafes, night clubs, water parks and attractions and much more will be at your disposal. But back to the hotel. Tastefully decorated rooms are equipped with all necessary appliances, quality furniture and household items. Each room, without exception, has its own balcony, which offers excellent views of the sea or the resort town.

And another hotel that you just can't help but mention is De La Mapa. Location - city center. The main advantage of this hotel is its location, as all cultural and entertainment centers are literally within walking distance. This theater square "Rodina", which hosts all kinds of holidays and festivals, and an amusement park and the best water park in the city. And of course, the Anapa embankment is also located not far, about three hundred meters from the hotel. If you wish, you can easily reach her and have fun in the cafes, of which there are a great many.

But all without exception, Anapa hotels have one advantage - they are part of Anapa, this unique resort city, which all numerous tourists can appreciate. You will surely be delighted with the sandy coast or forever remember the unique sunsets against the backdrop of the vast sea. And gourmets will appreciate the delicious Anapa wines. But the main value of the city is its inhabitants. It is possible that he will not agree that the foundation of any city is its enterprising and energetic people, who partly make the city what it is. Without exception, all the guests of the resort city of Anapa can have a great time and appreciate the hospitality of the inhabitants of the town.
