Turkey hotels for families with children: a description of the various options

Summer has come, and with it the long-awaited summer family vacation? Then feel free to go to a travel agency and buy a ticket to the hospitable sunny Turkey, which will delight you with an abundance of children's resorts. There you will receive excellent hotels in Turkey for families with children. The undoubted leaders of recreation and tourism are the cities of Alanya, Belek and Kemer.

Alanya is a city emerald from greenery with magnificent golden sandy beaches and a warm climate. It is considered the most important and best place for families.

The five-star Delphin Botanik Hotel is undoubtedly the best option you can find when considering all the hotels in Turkey for families with children. Three types of outdoor pools, one of which is specialized for children. Water slides, a cinema, an amphitheater, an amusement park and a variety of food outlets will cause you a wave of delight and a desire to spend your holidays here again and again. Depending on the age of your children, various types of leisure are provided for them, from a mini-disco and mini-clubs to animated performances and just playgrounds. Your memories of this hotel will be unforgettable.

If your financial means are modest, then the economical, but no less attractive option of the hotel Justiniano Club five stars will be most suitable for you. The hotel offers spacious rooms. And you and your children can have a good time enjoying the pools, water slides, horse riding, archery, canoeing, banaseiling. Children's mini-clubs and groovy animation will appeal to any child.

Choosing hotels in Turkey for families with children, do not miss the chance to visit the five-star hotel complex Pegasos Planet Royal and Pegasos Resort, in which you will find a holiday just a magical fairy tale from childhood. You will be impressed by the six pools, two of which are indoor. Animation for children, a children's mini-club, a playground and a mini-disco, an amazing amphitheater - this is all for your pleasure. The best pool called "Captain Nemo Castle" with water slides will plunge you into the world of Jules Verne‚Äôs novels.

Kemer is a city with bewitching mountain landscapes, a pine smell of air, long sand beaches and a transparent caressing sea. This is the city where upscale hotels in Turkey are located for families with children.

If you and your child are fond of the romanticism of the era of pirates and amazing adventures on sea waters, then the Pirates Beach and Pirates Club hotel chains will plunge you into this fascinating world, giving you the opportunity to enjoy the Crazy River-style wave pools, mini zoo, water park, mini-club, animation contests. The pirate leader with a real talking parrot will happily take a picture for your family photo album.

The five- star Comfort Flora Beach Hotel is designed for a quiet, measured and relaxing holiday. Children who are keen on sports will enjoy darts, table tennis, indoor soccer, catamarans and canoeing. Quiet alleys, pools, playgrounds, special highchairs and strollers surround you with coziness and comfort.

Belek is a center of holidays, fun, joy and entertainment with a crystal clear sea, sandy beaches and first-class hotels. The city where Turkey hotels are located for families with young children.

The well-thought-out infrastructure of the five-star Adora Golf Resort Hotel includes an amphitheater, water slides, two large outdoor pools, a chain of diet restaurants, a mini club for children, Turkish baths, SPA procedures - these are a wide range of services offered by the hotel. You can also use the services of a professional nanny for your child.

You will not regret choosing hotels in Turkey for families with children, and your child will write an excellent essay at school about how he spent the summer.
