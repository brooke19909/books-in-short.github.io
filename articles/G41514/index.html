Ryazan: a little about the city

The city of Ryazan is located approximately 200 km from Moscow.

It is a beautiful city in which modernity is closely intertwined with history. Attractions and excellent infrastructure attract tourists and visitors.

Ryazan is a marina, as it is located at the intersection of the Oka River and the Trubezh River. This is a city with an ancient history. It is decorated with monuments, monuments, museums, temples and monasteries. The Ryazan Kremlin is the main symbol of the regional center.

In recent years, thanks to the program for the restoration of the Kremlin, tourism has been actively developing here: new hotels in Ryazan and hotels appear, recreation areas are equipped, yacht clubs are opening.

Ryazan region is fraught with a huge number of monuments of nature and architecture.
