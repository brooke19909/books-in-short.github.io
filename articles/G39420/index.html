Drawn dream, or how to make a wish collage

In achieving your dreams and goals, all means are good (only those, of course, that do not harm yourself and others). A useful thing in this matter will also be a collage of desires, which is designed to accelerate the process of implementation of everything conceived. How to make a collage of desires so that the dream quickly and, most importantly, precisely, comes true? There are some nuances in this matter that are considered the rules of compilation. It is better to observe them, then to calmly expect what you have in mind.

How to make a wish collage : key points

So, a wish collage (or a wish card) is such a ‚Äúlayout‚Äù of the main goal or dream, made with the help of pictures. They should reflect and transmit what a person wants to receive (achieve). For example, a young guy dreams of a Mercedes or Toyota. He needs to find the images of these machines in the print edition, cut and paste on a thick sheet of paper.

Then it remains to hang it in a prominent place or put it in a purse to nourish the collage with your energy and desire. Work with the collage should be accompanied by high spirits, the belief that everything will come true, and we will focus on very specific practical actions. What does it mean? The fact that this car, as in our example, will not fall from the sky, but we need to take real steps towards making the dream a reality.

How to make a wish collage : practical tips

A collage can be created for one dream (as in the previous case), and can be made for several spheres of life. It is a map, conditionally divided into 9 zones. Each is responsible for such areas: first row (from left to right) - wealth, glory, love; second row - family, health (is the central sector in the collage), creativity; third row - knowledge, career, helpers. How to make a wish collage? If you want everything to come true, then prepare it before the new year, but according to the Chinese calendar.

This collage will be considered a kind of illustrated plan of what you want in the coming year. Now arm yourself with scissors, good glue and magazines (pictures can still be printed from a computer). Most importantly, do not forget your photo - it should be of excellent quality and you should like it. Attach it to the center of the Whatman paper (or album sheet) and start filling in the sectors with corresponding images. Make sure that the clippings are beautiful, shiny, clean, without scuffs, bends. Glue the pictures so that there are no white spots on the sheet.

How to make a wish collage: amplification tools

During work, it is important to maintain a positive attitude and believe that everything will come true. The collage is still good in that it allows you to determine your specific goal in the process.

After all, it often happens that a person is tormented by vague doubts, and such work allows him to see the main thing - his real goal and aspirations. And to strengthen the finished collage, meditation can, in which it is necessary to clearly imagine how everything has already been realized in reality. You can also decorate the collage with various thematic symbols or images of saints in whom you believe. It is believed that they will certainly help. Be sure to look at your creation in the morning when you wake up, and in the evening, before going to bed. So you will fill it with the energy of your desire. It is believed that everything should come true in 1-3 years.

This is how a wish collage is created. How to make it? Simple enough! The main thing in this matter is a strong desire and faith in a miracle, backed by real actions!
