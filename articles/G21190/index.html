Beach

Royal Albatros Moderna has a private beach. It is sandy, equipped with a pontoon, changing cabins, shower. Umbrellas and sunbeds are issued free of charge, as well as towels (the latter, however, after the presentation of a special card). The entrance to the sea is gentle, safe. Infrastructure, services

The hotel has its own parking and car rental. For business people who want to combine work and leisure, there is a conference room, perfectly equipped with modern technology (for 260 people). Royal Albatros Moderna has three adult pools, one of which is heated. It has its own beauty salon, baths, laundry, and its own Internet cafe, an ATM, many different shops, six restaurants, twelve bars and a lobby bar.
