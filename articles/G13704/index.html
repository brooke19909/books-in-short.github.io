Hotel Delphin Botanik Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya, Okurcalar): traveler reviews

The Turkish village of Okurcalar has become a resort relatively recently. And therefore, the prices for various services for tourists here are not very high. The resort is actually inexpensive. But this does not mean at all that the service for lovers of beach vacations here is of poor quality. You can rest in Okurdajalar no worse than, for example, in the same Antalya or Kemer.

There are not too many kinds of entertainment at this resort. Therefore, Okurcalar is preferred mainly by lovers of a relaxing beach holiday. Such tourists may very well approach in this village, for example, the Delphin Botanik Hotel 5 *. The hotel is actually comfortable, and many vacationers speak well of it.





Where is the hotel situated

This hotel is located on the first coastline. That is, in order to get to the beach, tourists living here do not need to cross any roads, railway crossings, etc. Of course, the majority of guests consider the proximity to the sea to be the undoubted advantage of this hotel.

From the airport, the Delphin Botanik Hotel 5 * hotel is located quite far away - almost 100 km. That is, tourists need to go to it about 2-3 hours. But in any case, the trip to the hotel is unlikely to be too tiring. Most of the tour operators working with this hotel provide tourists who rented rooms here with quite comfortable buses.

Major popular resorts in Turkey are also quite far from Okurcalar and the hotel. The nearest of them, Side, is 30 km away. From Alanya to Okurdzhalar - 32 km.

General description of the hotel

In fact, the Delphin Botanik Hotel 5 * represents two combined independent complexes - Botanical Platinum and Dolphin Botanical. They take guests in this large recreation center in several buildings of different floors, each of which has its own name. In total, this hotel occupies about 55 thousand square meters.

The territory at this hotel, according to most tourists, is very beautiful. All kinds of exotic plants are planted in the courtyards of the hotel buildings, which are carefully looked after by specially hired employees. In total, more than 1800 species of various southern decorative cultures grow on the territory of the complex.





The hotel Delphin Botanik Platinum Hotel 5 * operates on an all-inclusive system. Reviews from tourists she deserved just excellent. Many vacationers consider this hotel one of the best in Turkey. Even in Europe, complexes of this level, according to some tourists, are few. Of course, after the crisis associated with well-known political events, the service at this hotel worsened a little. However, most tourists visiting this hotel from year to year believe that soon everything will be fine and the complex will become as comfortable as it was before.

The last reconstruction at the Delphin Botanik Hotel (Turkey) was carried out just a few years ago - in 2014. Therefore, the hotel buildings and its interiors look more than worthy. Wearing branded bracelets at this hotel is a must. Tourists who have visited the complex more than 10 times are given such accessories in black. Such guests, among other things, the hotel relies on additional bonuses. For example, upon arrival they are presented with a T-shirt and a bag with hotel symbols.

Entertainment on site

Good guest reviews Delphin Botanik Hotel Resort 5 * deserved, among other things, because there is not one, but three pools at once. In one of them, 8 water slides were installed. That is, this pool is even a small water park. Riding a roller coaster in a hotel, according to many tourists, is safe and fun.

The relaxation pool at this hotel is only open to tourists from 15 years old. Mostly elderly holidaymakers come here. After all, here they can safely swim without the screams and splashes of adolescents. Such an interesting service is offered to tourists by no means in all hotels in Turkey, even five-star ones. In the Delphin Botanik, the relaxation pool has also recently appeared. And, of course, its convenience was immediately appreciated by many hotel guests, which positively affected its ratings.

Animations at this hotel are very common. And not only in the evening, but also in the afternoon. So, the all-inclusive system includes, for example, gymnastics, water aerobics, and step classes. The shows held at this hotel can have a variety of topics. Hotel guests have, of course, the opportunity to dance and sing karaoke.

Services for children

Of course, amenities are provided at the Delphin Botanik Hotel 5 *, not only for adults. According to most tourists, this hotel is just perfect for couples with children. For small guests, literally everything is provided here. On the territory of the hotel there is even a mini Luna Park with a Ferris wheel, slides, sandboxes and so on. Guests with children can visit it according to a certain schedule. But in any case, the Luna-Park of the hotel is very pleasant to its young guests and the reviews earned are just excellent.

Of course, a kids club also works in this hotel. Guests also respond very well about it. The hotel club nannies are responsible for their work. As a result, children attend classes with great pleasure. Hotel residents from 4 to 12 years old can come here.

Parents can rent strollers at this hotel for free. The only thing - they will need to leave a small deposit. Of course, there are also children's pools in this hotel. They are cleaned quite often. And parents speak well of the hotel's paddles.

The cleanliness of the territory in this hotel staff generally monitor quite carefully. Perhaps, thanks to this, among other things, cases of the illness of children, for example, Koksaki, were not observed at this hotel.

Beach Hotel Reviews

The Delphin Botanik Hotel Resort 5 * is located, as already mentioned, on the seashore. The road to the beach from its buildings takes tourists no more than 3-4 minutes. The hotel beach itself has earned pretty good reviews from its guests. The coastline at this place is covered with a large jackdaw. But there are no big stones and boulders on the hotel beach. Walking along the seashore next to the hotel is barefoot. Experienced tourists recommend that beginners must purchase special slippers in coastal shops.

Entering the sea on the beach of the hotel, judging by the reviews, is quite convenient. But those tourists who are still disturbed by large pebbles can jump into the water right from the pier. There are no dangerous animals in the sea in this place. The hotel guests respond well about the work of the beach staff.
