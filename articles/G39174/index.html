Belpoint Beach Hotel. Kemer (Turkey), hotels: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Kemer is one of the most popular resort cities in the Republic of Turkey. It features beautiful landscapes, a clear sea and the presence of many modern hotels. Hotels in Kemer vary in price level, in their territory and the number of services offered. Belpoint Beach Hotel 3 * (Beldibi) refers to budget hotels, but provides all the necessary range of services for a good rest.

Rest in Beldibi, Kemer

Kemer stretched along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea for several kilometers. The district is divided into the following villages:

  • Beldibi;
  • Goynuk;
  • Kirish;
  • Camyuva;
  • Tekirova.

Beldibi is the first resort village on the way from Antalya. It is considered a very cozy Turkish area due to the fact that it is located next to the mighty Taurus Mountains, and pine forests grow around it. In any place you can enjoy the splendor of the surrounding nature and breathe in the fresh sea air.

The entrance to the sea is pebbled on almost the entire coastline, but some hotels fill their beaches with sand or equip the descent into the sea with special stairs and pontoons.

general information

In the village of Beldibi, 15 km from the center of Kemer and 23 km from the city of Antalya, in 1995 a hotel was built, which was originally called Poseidon, and later was renamed to Belpoint Beach Hotel 3 *. In 2012, repairs and restoration were carried out in it, furniture and appliances were replaced. Over the years, the hotel has gained its loyal customers and a good reputation due to the optimal ratio of price and quality.





The distance from the Antalya International Airport to the hotel is about 40 km. The road here even with young children is not too tiring. You can take a taxi from the airport to the hotel. And you can use the transfer - group or individual, ordered when buying a tour.

The hotel mainly tourists from Russia, Ukraine and Belarus. In addition, among the guests there are Germans, British and local residents. These are mainly couples or families with children.

Hotel concept

Turkey is the best choice for a family, relaxing or active holiday. Hotels Belpoint Beach Hotel, Venus Hotel, Tal Hotel, Marinem Diana and many others are located in the first part of the village of Beldibi and are reasonably priced.

The hotel is located on the second coastline. It is separated from the sea by a roadway and a pedestrian road. Do not worry about the difficulties or dangers of a daily trip to the sea. A safe underground passage leads to the beach area. The coast is 150 meters from the hotel, shops are 50 meters away, and a disco is nearby.

For guests, the hotel is open from April until the end of November. It offers an all inclusive system for guests.

To check into the hotel, tourists must present a voucher, passport and fill out a registration card. If necessary, at the reception you can make currency exchange, rent a safe, order a taxi or rent sports equipment.

Infrastructure

Belpoint Beach Hotel 3 * (Kemer) over the many years of its existence has several times changed the system of its work, added and removed additional services.

The hotel has a main restaurant, a lobby bar, a poolside bar. The pool has its own mini-water park, consisting of two slides. For guests working out gym, Turkish bath and sauna, Internet cafe. There is parking in front of the hotel.





There is a doctor here, but do not forget that tourist medical insurance implies service in specific medical institutions, so the services of a hotel doctor may be paid.

The concept of the hotel has a number of paid and free services. The rooms have booklets with information about them. In addition, representatives of travel agencies and staff at the reception are ready to help resolve difficult situations.

Number of rooms

The area and maximum capacity of the rooms are as follows:

  1. Standard room, consisting of one bedroom and bathroom (23 square meters, 3 people).
  2. Family room of two bedrooms and one bathroom (25 square meters, 4 people).
  3. Combined room, consisting of two bedrooms and one bathroom (23 square meters, 3 people).

Each of them has a beautiful interior, a balcony, air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone, empty refrigerator.

The bathrooms in the Belpoint Beach Hotel 3 * can have a bath or shower, they are equipped with a hairdryer and sockets for connecting electric shavers. There are towels for arms, legs and body. Toiletries are also provided.





Towels are changed when cleaning rooms, bed linen - once every four days. Room service is chargeable.

Supply system

Half board, full board, all inclusive, ultra all inclusive or breakfast only are the most common types of food in the Kemer region hotels . Belpoint Beach Hotel is an all-inclusive property. She was especially fond of guests from Russia, because after paying for the tour at the travel agency‚Äôs office, there would be no other expenses for food during the vacation.

Service in bars and restaurants is available from 7 am to 9 pm. The main restaurant serves breakfast, brunch, lunch and dinner. The menu includes traditional Turkish as well as international cuisine. A wide assortment of salads, vegetables, soups, meat dishes, desserts and fruits. Guests choose their own treats and lay on their plates. Waiters clean tables and deliver drinks. For children in restaurants there are highchairs. The bars offer drinks of Turkish origin. From alcohol, you can choose beer, wine, local crayfish vodka and gin.





Service

The hotel staff is friendly on the day of arrival at the reception. Peddlers help deliver luggage to rooms. Maids clean the rooms, take out the trash and change bath towels. According to Turkish traditions, it is customary to leave a tip for the service. Sometimes this affects the quality of service. And the maids leave a drawing or a figure from a towel on the bed.

The hotel staff speaks Russian, so there should be no problems communicating with guests. Any breakdowns, malfunctions and wishes can be reported to the Belpoint Beach Hotel reception staff. They can order a taxi, exchange dollars for local currency, and learn about popular places nearby.

Room service is available upon request. You can order drinks or food, but this service is paid. Upon eviction from the hotel, you should ask for the bill and pay it. It accepts cash and bank cards.

Beach, sea and pool

Amazingly clean and beautiful sea with a pebble beach in Kemer. Gentle waves and warm sun will give the most unforgettable emotions to vacationers in this area. Belpoint Beach Hotel has its own beach with free sun loungers and parasols. The beach is 150 meters away. An underground tunnel leads to it. The hotel's beach is clean, there is daily cleaning. Occupy sunbeds in the morning is not required. Their number corresponds to the number of vacationers, and the hotel staff quickly helps to find free space. There is a freshwater shower on the beach. The hotel does not have a private bar on the beach. At sea, you can ride on water rides daily. This service is paid.

Beach towels are issued and changed free of charge. There is a special counter where at certain hours they are exchanged for clean ones. At the same time, guests are issued plastic cards for towels, and on the day of departure from the hotel they are returned to the reception. Towel loss will incur a fine.

Entertainment

Belpoint Beach Hotel has a pool with slides, which hosts water games and water aerobics. There is a swimming area for kids, as well as a children's mini club.

For men, there is a gym that can only be used with sports shoes.

Guests at the hotel can use a basketball or volleyball court, play table tennis or darts. These entertainment services are free. Billiards is available at an additional cost.

A Turkish bath at the hotel is open daily. It includes a sauna, a bathhouse and a hammam, which are provided free of charge. Every vacationer can enter, sit and bask. Massage, peeling and any other procedures are not included in the price of the Turkish bath.

Daily in the lobby of the hotel representatives of travel agencies organize the sale of sightseeing tours. You can choose a trip to historical places, to ancient cities. You can go on a sea cruise on a yacht to Antalya and watch the waterfall falling directly into the sea. You can test the adrenaline on the descent along the mountain river in kayaks or go to the dance concert "Lights of Anatolia".

Even for the smallest vacationers, there are special excursion programs, including horseback riding, visiting the zoo or dolphin shows.

Young people in the evening can get to the most famous discos in Kemer - "Aura" and "Inferno". Every week, famous Russian singers, singers and show men perform there.

For shopping, it is better to go to Antalya, there are many shopping centers, parks and entertainment venues. Traditionally, it is customary in Turkey to purchase jewelry, leather jackets, fur coats, sheepskin coats and textile products. It is better to make purchases in the velvet season when the maximum discounts are set in stores.

Vacation with children

Almost all resorts in Turkey are suitable for spending holidays with children, and Kemer is no exception. Belpoint Beach Hotel is family oriented. The restaurants have highchairs for babies, in the rooms for an extra fee you can put a cot. The menu in the restaurant includes a variety of dishes. Therefore, it will not be difficult to choose the right food for the baby.





Older children can stay at the kids club without parental care. At this time, mothers and fathers can visit the massage procedure or just relax without children. In the pool there is a zone with a shallow depth especially for small guests.

In addition, the sea air itself and a pleasant climate contribute to the restoration of health and strengthening the child‚Äôs immune system. But when buying a tour, you must choose the right dates for the trip. It can be too hot in the middle of summer, so you should consult your doctor before your trip.

Traveler Reviews

For those who want to save a little on the hotel and spend money on shopping or sightseeing, it‚Äôs suitable Belpoint Beach Hotel. Reviews about him are both positive and negative.

The hotel staff is very friendly, especially praised by the hammam. Tourists recommend the hotel to those who are going to spend time on excursions and at sea, while staying in rooms at a minimum. Guests note that in high season, when the hotel is full, a lot of people gather in the restaurant, it is difficult to find a free table.





The rooms are small, comfortable, but the furniture and plumbing are the simplest. Towels are changed regularly, but the cleaning of the hotel leaves much to be desired.

Location, number of services, prices and other factors affect travelers when they choose a hotel. Kemer, a 3-star hotel similar to Belpoint Beach, attracts tourists who are vacationing in Turkey for the first time, or those who prefer economy options.

Holiday Prices

The price of hotel accommodation depends on the season, demand and occupancy of the hotel. At the very beginning of the season, when there are still a few people, the cost is set to the lowest. But on the May holidays, when many tourists from Russia rush to go to Turkey to bask in the sun, prices are rising. So, the cost of a standard double room at the beginning of the season can be about 24,230 rubles. But in May days it increases to 29 120 rubles. While in August or July a ticket may cost 36,400 rubles, but on a last-minute tour at the hotel you can relax together for 24,750 rubles.

People who have visited the Belpoint Beach Hotel are advised to bring rubber slippers for entry into the sea or buy on-site. You should not count on a strong and stable Wi-Fi signal, it is better to solve all working issues before or after a vacation. In general, you should not expect anything supernatural from this hotel. This is a good but simple hotel for a budget holiday.
