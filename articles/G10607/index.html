Casco insurance rules. How to "put" a car in a "helmet"?

The rapidly growing flow of vehicles has spawned the phenomenon of auto insurance. A great way to protect your car, provided that you can fall into the hands of bona fide and honest insurers. Casco insurance has become very popular in Russia. It will be discussed further.

What it is?

Casco is a type of car insurance, the purpose of which is to compensate for damage in case of damage or theft of the car, and if there is a special item in the conditions, you can protect the health of the driver. Such insurance is purchased on a voluntary prompting by the owner of the vehicle.

In what cases is payment made?

Each insurance company establishes a list of insured events. Roughly it looks like this:

  • Accidents, failures in the ground and under the ice, fires, explosions, thefts, thefts, robberies and more.
  • Harm and damage to victims, if it arose due to the fault of the driver, as a result of violation of the rules and obligations by him.
  • Loss of legal capacity by the insured if this happened during an accident.
  • Unforeseen expenses for the car owner.

Is it worth it to draw up?

In this matter, the choice always remains with the owner of the car. If the vehicle is purchased on credit, then until the full repayment of the amount, it will be the property of the bank, and this organization will take care of the insurance at the expense of the debtor.

When a car is bought on its own, the decision on insurance is also free. If the driver is confident in the need for the services of insurers, then you should responsibly approach the choice of the company and conditions that are convenient for yourself.

What affects the cost of hull insurance?

Buying Casco is an expensive pleasure. In order not to suffer unnecessary losses, you need to take into account the details and nuances of the programs and tariffs offered by SK.

When studying this issue, you need to focus on the following points:

  • Sum insured determined by the insurance contract. Within its limits, SCs also pay compensation to victims. For example, according to Casco insurance rules in VSK, the safety reserve can be either diminished or not diminished, while other organizations can be limited to only one type. In the first case, the insurance company makes payments out of the total amount, gradually bringing it to zero, in the second case, the reserve always remains unchanged and will be the same for all insurance cases. It is clear that the cost of the latter will be higher.
  • The degree of protection against threats. The owner has the right to choose the full or partial level. In the first case, insurance can be claimed for damage and for theft, in the second - for one of the incidents. In the case when the car is hidden in the garage, the risk of theft is reduced, which means that you can save and purchase a partial policy.
  • With or without depreciation, payments are made. Casco "Rosgosstrakh" under the rules of insurance provides only one option. While many companies give the customer a choice.
  • Application deadlines in insurance cases. The longer this period, the better. For comparison, 10 days are accepted in RESO, according to the rules of comprehensive insurance in Ingosstrakh, this period is 7 days.
  • Place of car repair. Whether this moment is determined by the owner freely or, at the option of SK, the company repairs cars in partner workshops. It is not difficult to guess that this option is more convenient and cheaper for the insurer. The amount of the hull itself will also fall below.
  • Availability of additional services. It should be understood that their presence will significantly increase the cost of insurance.

"Underwater rocks"

Nobody wants to overpay. Insurers are no exception. Companies seek to minimize losses. In this regard, the organization establishes clearly defined points for which insurance payments are not relied upon.

Such lists are compiled separately by each company and can differ dramatically. However, it is possible to perceive a certain standard; insurance cases will certainly not be damage due to:

  • Radiation exposure.
  • Mass unrest.
  • Military action.
  • Nuclear explosions.
  • Seizure of a car by state authorities, if there are legal grounds for that.

Also, insurance does not cover lost profits and non-pecuniary damage.

Careful consideration of these circumstances by the car owner will help to avoid unnecessary disagreements and financial waste in the future.
