How to wash the microwave inside - tips and tricks

Fat is the worst enemy of our beloved kitchen utensils, and the microwave is no exception. Every housewife often wonders how to wash greasy drops and stubborn stains inside the microwave ?!

There are a lot of ways to clean the microwave from pollution, but not every one of them is convenient or effective. We all know that a multifunctional microwave oven is quite useful in everyday life, because you can cook many different dishes in it: from toasts to grand cakes.





But we will not go far from the topic, we will consider several options for how to wash the microwave inside.

On the shelves of specialized stores, we offer a variety of cleaning products, such as sprays, powders and various liquid types of soap. However, to make sure the effectiveness of a particular tool, you need to buy and try it. Having spent money on a large amount of funds, often many buyers are disappointed in them, not getting the desired result. And again they are looking for ways to wash the microwave inside.

Self- cleaning microwaves are available. For such microwave ovens, you just need to pour water into a special hole and press the desired button. The microwave will melt the oily drops with hot steam, and you just have to wipe it inside with a napkin. It is worth noting that such a microwave oven is quite an expensive pleasure. Each user needs to decide for himself which microwave oven is best for him.

1 way

Soda and water. Pour water into a deep cup, put a spoonful of soda in it and put in the microwave for 15 minutes. Then with a napkin, carefully remove the ingrained fat from the walls of the microwave. Soda can be replaced with citric acid or slices of the lemon itself.

2 way

Orange zest. Before washing the microwave inside, prepare an orange, or rather, crusts from it. Then place the crusts in a cup of water and do the same as described in method 1. In the same way, you can get rid of unwanted odors from the microwave. You can choose aromatic oil to your taste, add to a cup of water and boil a little. The more drops you add, the stronger the aroma will be from your microwave.

3 way

Laundry soap. Everyone knows that laundry soap copes with difficult contaminations very well, but in order to wash the microwave inside, you first need to foam it well. Then apply evenly on the walls and leave for 30 minutes, then rinse thoroughly with warm water using a sponge.

4 way. Most effective

The simplest and most effective way is, of course, to purchase a special cover that prevents droplets of fat from spraying on the walls of the furnace.

How to wash a microwave - naturally, the housewife chooses. All microwave ovens are different, each needs its own cleanser, and what it will be is up to you.
