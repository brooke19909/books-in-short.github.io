Copacabana Lloret 3 * (Spain, Costa Brava): hotel description, reviews and photos

Spain is a European country that attracts our tourists no less than the more exotic Turkey and Tunisia. In our article we want to talk about a three-star hotel with the beautiful name Copacabana Lloret 3 *.

A bit about the hotel ...

Copacabana Lloret 3 * is located in the center of Lloret de Mar (Costa Brava resort). Nearby there are many clubs, cafes and restaurants. The hotel is 400 meters from the coast, and there is a bus stop near the complex .



The complex is easy to reach for visiting tourists, since the nearest airport in Girona is located only 22 kilometers away.

The hotel first started operating in 1968 and since then has been successfully hosting guests annually. The last renovation of the complex was carried out in 2010. In total, the hotel has 165 rooms.

Number of rooms

Copacabana Lloret 3 * has at its disposal apartments of the following categories: family, double, triple and single. All are equipped with a balcony, bath, telephone, air conditioning, safe, Wi-Fi, TV and cable channels.

Dining at the hotel

Copacabana Lloret 3 * offers its guests to take full advantage of the buffet. The chef of the restaurant prepares the best European and international dishes. The food in the hotel is quite varied with a good selection of meat dishes, sweets, seafood and ice cream. The main restaurant is very clean and beautiful, the waiters are fast and agile.

Complex infrastructure

The Copacabana Lloret 3 * hotel (Spain) has a quite acceptable infrastructure, thanks to which there is always everything you need on vacation at hand. Reception is open 24 hours a day, there is a swimming pool with an equipped recreation area. The complex also has a bar. And the lounge bar has live entertainment in the evenings.

Sports and Entertainment

Copacabana Lloret 3 * (Spain) has a sun terrace. The hotel staff is multilingual (employees speak German, Dutch, Polish, Russian, French Italian Spanish Catalan, Portuguese and English). The hotel is non-smoking. Also, pets are not accepted here. There is a private pool for children.



Vacationers can visit the gym, court, play table tennis, squash, billiards. And on the coast, tourists expect numerous traditional water activities. In the evenings, there is a disco bar, guests are entertained with live music in the restaurant.

Beach

Copacabana Lloret 3 * (Lloret de Mar) is 400 meters from the coast. The hotel, like any European complex, does not have its own beach. It offers - the municipal coast. There are several ways to get to the beach. The shortest road takes no more than seven minutes. Local beaches are stunningly beautiful and clean. There are many shops and cafes on the waterfront.

A little bit about the resort ...

As we have already mentioned, Copacabana Lloret 3 * (Lloret de Mar) is located in the resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãLloret de Mar, which is the center of tourism throughout the Costa Brava. It is worth noting that Lloret de Mar is the main place for parties on the entire Mediterranean coast. Young people from all over Europe come here to relax. And because there is an incredible number of nightclubs, bars, discos, restaurants. Local clubs surprise spoiled tourists with a wide variety of programs, including a laser show.



Lloret de Mar has a long history (more than a thousand years). Over the years, a small fishing village has become one of the most popular tourist resorts in Europe.

How to get to the hotel?

Hotel Copacabana Lloret 3 * has a fairly convenient location. The guests of the complex can order a transfer, or they can independently reach their destination, thanks to the developed transport infrastructure. The hotel is 75 kilometers from Barcelona and 30 kilometers from Girona. As a rule, Russian tourists use flights to Barcelona and Girona. And then getting to the hotel is quite simple. You can take the train, bus, rent a car or call a taxi.

Local beaches

Lloret de Mar is known for its stunning beaches. In the very center of the resort is the famous Lloret, and to the south - Fenals Beach. In general, in the entire area there are many other good beaches. Their undeniable advantage is the reliable protection from the north winds by cliffs and bays, which makes them the best places to relax in the entire Costa Brava. Picturesque landscapes with numerous bays and with green-blue sea open spaces attract thousands of beach holiday lovers here.

Entertainment and Attractions

Lloret de Mar has a long history, as evidenced by the relics of the Iberian region that have survived to this day (they date back to 250 BC). They are stored and on display at the Verdaguer Cultural Center. In addition, the city has a maritime museum. In Looret de Mar, on the sea boulevard, you can admire the exact copy of the famous Canale Fountain. And the original is located on Las Ramblas, in the famous Barcelona.





In 1968, the authorities of the capital presented the resort with this copy.

One of the best attractions of the city are the Gardens of St. Clotilde. It is a park that is easy to find. It is located in the exclusive area of ‚Äã‚ÄãFenals. The park offers stunning sea views. In 1995, the park was awarded the highest title of national treasure of Catalonia. It was founded in 1919 and is made in the Renaissance style.

Children will be incredibly interested in visiting the Dwarf Park, which is located in the suburbs of Lloret de Mar. The main characters are, of course, the gnomes who hide in the forest. There are many attractions for children. And older people can try their hand at adventure routes with many obstacles that must be overcome.

In the city center there is a cat museum, which was opened in 2002 by Russian emigrants. This place will appeal to tourists of all ages. The three-story mansion has more than three thousand exhibits. It is unlikely that somewhere else you can see so many cats. There are figures made of wood, ceramics, porcelain, paintings, silverware, even on the walls depicted "mustachioed-striped."

The symbol of the city is a bronze statue of Donna Mariner (wife of a sailor). It rises on a cliff at the very end of Lloret Beach. The image symbolizes a woman waiting for a loved one.

The city has all the conditions for outdoor activities on water and land. On the beaches you can ride catamarans, bananas, go on a boat trip on a boat. A large number of diving centers operate along the coast, providing lessons for diving enthusiasts of different levels.

City guests can visit the Water Park, the Marineland Waterpark and the mini zoo. In the daytime, it is pleasant to stroll through the local streets leading to the center, along which there are many nice restaurants and shops.





The city does not fall asleep even after sunset. Lloret de Mar transforms, dressing in colorful lights. Night clubs, discos, bars open their doors for lovers of night parties.

Copacabana Lloret 3 *: reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I would like to refer to the reviews of tourists who managed to visit this place. Copacabana Lloret 3 * (Costa Brava) is a good three-star economy class hotel on the Costa Brava. The hotel has a good location, being almost in the center of the resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãLloret de Mar. From here you can easily reach any point of the town. But it is also necessary to mention that there is a casino next to the complex, and therefore, rooms facing the main street are sometimes very noisy from cars and late visitors to nightlife venues. As for apartments facing the other side, there is much quieter. Although the overall soundproofing of the walls of the hotel is very weak.

The hotel has spacious rooms with far from new furniture. All apartments have nice balconies with furniture and clothes dryers. The rooms are cleaned daily and of high quality. Towels and bed linen are scheduled to be changed. The rooms, although not new, need repair, but are kept clean. So it fully corresponds to the status of the hotel.



Special attention deserves food in Copacabana Lloret 3 *. Breakfast, dinner and lunch at the hotel are quite varied and nutritious. Some tourists even believe that not all four star hotels are so well fed. At breakfast, there is a regular set of eggs, cheeses, sausages, yoghurts, juices and coffee. For lunch and dinner, guests are offered a large selection of meat, fish dishes, sweets, fruits, ice cream and pastries. Staying hungry here is simply impossible. At the same time, there is always enough food at the buffet. There is no need to rush to the beginning of the meal to catch all the dishes. In general, vacationers praise the local chef, whose dishes are always tasty and varied. Food is organized at a decent level.

At Copacabana 3 * (Lloret de Mar), the staff is multilingual, so there is never a problem with explaining at the reception. Almost all the hotel staff are pleasant and good-natured people. In rooms, internet is paid. But in the lobby you can use free Wi-Fi. For air conditioning and a safe, you need to pay extra, but they are not too needed. In the evenings, evening entertainment is available at the poolside bar. In general, animation is at an average level. The pool at the hotel is small but clean. It is cleaned and poured daily with disinfectant mixtures.

Hotel Copacabana 3 * is located near the beach. Walking steps to the coast can be reached in five to ten minutes. The municipal beach is very beautiful and well maintained with a sand cover. Many streets lead from the hotel to the coast, you can safely go along any of them, you will still go ashore. Sunset at sea is gentle and comfortable, and the sea is always clean and welcoming.



The hotel has a local travel guide offering all kinds of trips. However, many tourists prefer independent trips to Barcelona and other cities. There is a transport stop near the hotel, so there is no problem getting to the nearest cities. The capital of Spain is definitely worth a visit and see the famous fountain in action. This is truly an amazing sight.

Instead of an afterword

Hotel Copacabana 3 * - an excellent hotel for a budget holiday. It is perfect for unpretentious tourists or vacationers who do not consider it necessary to overpay extra money for additional benefits. In general, the complex provides a full range of services that are necessary for a full beach holiday. The main advantages of the hotel are: clean rooms, good food and a great location.
