Which bank's plastic pension card is best?

Today, banks offer a large selection of social cards. They can store scholarships, allowances, pensions, transfer interest on deposits. It is only necessary to make out in the proposed conditions in order to choose the best bank for service.

Social card

This banking product was originally intended to receive and store various social benefits: scholarships, pensions, benefits. But today, the functions of this payment instrument go beyond the standard. Pension bank card replaces its holder with a compulsory medical insurance policy, travel document and payment instrument. Placing photos on the back of the plastic suggests that in the near future it will replace the pension certificate of citizens.





Decor

A pension card is issued upon a written application from a client who has provided a passport and a pension certificate. Typically, such programs use cheap cards - Electron, Cirrus, Maestro. Release and maintenance of plastic is free. The card is valid for 3 years. You can get a pension card immediately at the department after the conclusion of the contract. This is not a registered plastic, so you won‚Äôt have to wait long for its release. SMS-informing and Internet banking services are connected for free. Through online banking, any payments are made: from replenishing the phone to paying fines and utilities.

In some cases, a pension bank card is used as the account to which credit funds are transferred. But not all banks provide overdrafts on such payment instruments. Social card services are usually entrusted to large financial institutions. Their income in this sector may amount to several percent of revenue. But the main reason why banks strive to service social projects is that this area brings a small but stable income, increases the confidence of regulators in a financial institution.





No fees are charged for withdrawing funds from "native" ATMs. But there are internal limits on credit institutions. For example, in Sberbank the daily limit is 50 thousand rubles. For withdrawing a larger amount from the account, you will have to pay 0.5%.

Pension cards of banks unload employees, reduce the cost of customer service, issue passbooks and increase the turnover on accounts by servicing the social "plastic".

How to choose a bank?

The funds are credited to the account without commission. But this rule applies only to social benefits. For the transfer of funds between individuals a fee will already be charged. These conditions are the same in all banks. Therefore, when choosing a financial intermediary should focus on other criteria.

Firstly, a card must be opened to the account to which the social payment is transferred. Otherwise, to receive a pension, you will have to stand in line at a bank branch. The same problem is faced by people who receive payments through the Russian Post.

Secondly, the ability to issue additional cards to the account is of great importance. If the pension card is used as a ‚Äúcommon boiler‚Äù, then all family members must have access to funds. In this case, one card will not be enough.





Thirdly, you should ask in advance about the number of ATMs in the city. It makes no sense to draw up a payment instrument in a large bank if there are no ATMs of this credit institution or its partners in your city. Non-cash payment of goods in any case will be free. But for the withdrawal of funds will have to pay a commission.

If the social payment is not immediately spent, but accumulated in the account, then it makes sense to inquire about the size of the interest rate on the balance of funds. Most banks provide this service. But subject to fixing a certain amount on the account for at least a month.
