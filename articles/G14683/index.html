Small refrigerator: advantages, disadvantages and scope

In a small kitchen it can be difficult to place all the necessary equipment. A small refrigerator, despite its modest dimensions, can be quite roomy and partially solve the problem of lack of space. In order to choose the best option and not miscalculate, you should determine the desired size, functionality and study possible modifications. Design also plays an important role: a stylish refrigerator can become a real decoration of the kitchen.

Features and Benefits

A small refrigerator is a compact device with a height of not more than 130 cm. Such equipment is indispensable in small-sized apartments, office buildings, as well as in cottages. It allows you to comfortably and creatively equip your living space and does not require too much free space. The main advantage of such models is their compact size, thanks to which the unit can easily be placed under the kitchen worktop, built into a cupboard or taken with you to the cottage. The built-in small refrigerator is ideal for fans of stylish interiors. The engine of the unit also has a modest size, so it makes almost no noise. The device can even be put in the bedroom and not worry about loud sounds.

Thanks to the compact engine and other internal mechanisms, the small refrigerator has good capacity, for example, an apparatus with a height of only 85 cm has a useful volume of 115 liters. Another important advantage is the affordable price, since a smaller size also means lower cost. The features of a miniature refrigerator include an almost full-fledged functionality: the ability to hang the door to the other side, a built-in voltage stabilizer, a freshness chamber, a freezer, an audible alert when the door is open.

Varieties

There are several types of small refrigerators, each of which has its own purpose and features. First of all, it is necessary to distinguish between floor and table models. The former have a larger size and functionality, while the latter are extremely compact and small in capacity. There are also built-in models that blend harmoniously with the kitchen set and are completely invisible in the interior.

Small refrigerators are single-chamber and two-chamber. Single chamber have one cell, sometimes divided by shelves. Such a model can be equipped with a compact freezer or available without it. A small refrigerator without a freezer is usually used in offices, so that employees can keep their lunches brought with them. Two-compartment refrigerators are larger, as a rule, one chamber is a refrigerator, and the second is a freezer. Such models are suitable for a small apartment and a small family of two to three people.

Manufacturers of household appliances have also provided special devices for a narrow audience. Portable refrigerators are compact portable units equipped with an autonomous power source. Bar serves for cooling drinks and desserts. Automobiles work from the cigarette lighter. There are also refrigerators for wine, in such models the door is usually made of glass. Of particular interest are miniature devices with a USB cable that can be powered from a computer or laptop. Although the internal volume is relatively small, in such a micro box you can cool several bottles of water, a portion of ice cream or yogurt. A small refrigerator for the office is an ideal solution for employees in the summer.

Equipment

When choosing a refrigerator, you need to pay attention to its configuration. The presence of a freezer, an autonomous power source, a handle or a carrying bag can be key.

Dimensions

The maximum dimensions of a small refrigerator are from 130 cm in height, about 50 cm in width and 60 cm in depth. Compact models have smaller dimensions. A device lower than 85 cm can be installed under the countertop.

How to choose

To choose the best model, you need to determine the purpose of such a purchase and the place of the intended operation: you need a small refrigerator for the office, home or cottage. If the refrigerator will be at home, it is best to choose a two-chamber model of small height, and the portable version is more suitable for giving. The following parameters should also be taken into account:

  • internal usable volume of the refrigerator and freezer - make sure that this size suits you;
  • the dimensions should correspond to the furniture set and fit into the situation;
  • a high class of energy efficiency will save energy;
  • high-quality plastic for internal equipment does not absorb odors and lasts a long time;
  • zoning of temperature conditions will allow you to safely store different products;
  • the built-in small refrigerator fits harmoniously into the interior, but you need to order a special cabinet for it.

Manufacturers

The most popular brands are such giants as Samsung, Ariston, Indesit, Electrolux, Zanussi. Refrigerators manufactured by Nord and Beco are also in stable demand. In the line of each brand there are several models of small refrigerators with different functionality and appearance. Smeg company specializes in the manufacture of designer household appliances. Such devices will delight connoisseurs of retro style. With a vintage look, these refrigerators are equipped with the latest high-tech engines and the highest quality fittings.

The compact fridge is the perfect solution for a small room. A variety of models, designs and possible functions will allow you to choose the best option and satisfy almost any individual needs.
