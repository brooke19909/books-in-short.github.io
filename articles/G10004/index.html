Acer Aspire 5100: review of the budget laptop

The Acer Aspire 5100 is the solution for users who want a powerful compact machine. The model became the discoverer of the company, having received Turion 64 X2 on 2 cores. A distinctive feature of the Acer Aspire 5100 was the cost, which classifies the laptop in the initial class. "Stuffing" allows you to work with demanding programs, and a high-quality display - watch movies. The Acer Aspire 5100 also has a good case. The characteristics of this laptop are really attractive for purchase.

Appearance

The device is made in the familiar form factor of previous models. The developers focused on usability and durability of materials. The case is made of plastic. Its quality can be called average. And to see more expensive material in a budget device would be surprising. However, you can‚Äôt call him bad. It is pleasant to the touch and scratch resistant. Assembled Acer Aspire 5100 soundly. Squeaks appear only with strong compression of parts. Moreover, they cannot be avoided, given the presence of plastic. The display, surrounded by a frame, is mounted on two hinges. Here you can highlight the dignity of the model. Hinges allow you to turn the lid at a very wide angle, which many competitors cannot stand out from. The fasteners are moderately tight, there are no accidental discoveries. In addition, two locks are used to hold the lid, preventing foreign objects from entering between the screen and the keyboard.

In general, the appearance is quite pleasant and does not repel the user. The build quality was affected by the low cost, as a result of which the owner of the device may encounter squeaks and punching in some places.

Keyboard

The keyboard of the Acer Aspire 5100 is made typical of a laptop unit. The keys are separated from each other, located on the site. This design allows quick replacement in the event of a breakdown. Like the brothers, the Acer Aspire 5100 has a touchpad. It is a small block made in black. The touchpad is sunk into the body, which makes it easy to grope it. There are three buttons for control. Two of them are responsible for the function of the mouse buttons, and the third - for scrolling pages.

Screen

A liquid crystal matrix with a diagonal of 15.6 inches is installed, resolution - 1280 x 800 pixels. The screen received a good contrast and rich colors. The device cannot boast of wide viewing angles. Things are bad with work on sunny days - direct rays cause glare. This display is similar to those installed on competitors' laptops.

Performance

The laptop is positioned by the manufacturer as a model for solving everyday tasks. The chip is installed from AMD, in order to save on hardware. The 2-core Turion 64 X2 operates at a clock frequency of 2 GHz. Today it can already be called obsolete. The machine copes well with office programs, but problems can arise with more demanding ones. The resulting laptop is not very energy-intensive, which is important for mobile devices.
