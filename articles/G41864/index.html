Atlantis Hotel, Dubai: description and reviews

The fabulous atmosphere of luxury and carelessness reigns in the Atlantis hotel. Dubai is an amazing resort that combines the features of traditional Arab culture and modern western metropolis. This amazing symbiosis can be fully felt when staying in the hotel in question. Rest spent here will be unforgettable.

Luxury resorts are famous for Dubai. Atlantis Palm Hotel is one of them. The institution consists of two buildings connected by a bridge, and includes more than one and a half thousand rooms. The complex, opened in 2008, was built on the model of one of the Bahamian hotels. By the way, the opening was held with great fanfare. In 10 minutes, 100,000 fireworks were released into the sky.

Hotel Atlantis (Dubai) is very popular among travelers. Description is worth starting with the location of the institution. It is located in the area of Palm Jumeirah - a unique man-made island. This is a convenient location for the sea coast, urban infrastructure and attractions.

How to get there

The property in question is 28 km from Dubai International Airport. How to get to the Atlantis Hotel in Dubai? The easiest way is to book a transfer at the hotel or take a taxi. If you want to get on your own, keep in mind that direct transport does not go to this area. You will have to go through a difficult path, which consists of the following steps:

  • From the airport, take the metro to Dubai Internet City Station.
  • Then walk along the coastline towards Palm Jumeirah. Please note that there are no pointers.
  • At the road junction there is a monorail station.
  • Rise to the second level and get a ticket (cost - 30 dirhams).
  • Climb to the third level, where trains leave.
  • In 15 minutes you will be near the hotel.
