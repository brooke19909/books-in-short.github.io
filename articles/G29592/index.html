Where to go in Kaluga to relax and have fun?

The small city of Kaluga, located on both banks of the Oka River, is famous for its amazing landscapes and attractions. Since its founding in 1371, he managed to find his own history, which a huge number of tourists come to learn about. In addition to historical values, there are many entertainment venues in Kaluga where people of any age will find employment.

Planetarium

For example, where to go with a child in Kaluga? To the planetarium. The city can boast of its incredible system, combining optical-mechanical and digital projection. Thanks to this, young viewers get into a real fairy tale, which tells about distant planets and stars.



The planetarium organizers have developed several courses aimed at expanding knowledge of space. At the same time, excellent projection quality, transmitting even shades, contributes to a better assimilation of information.

Aquapark

Where can I go with a child in Kaluga? Of course, in the water park. All visitors to this place are given the opportunity to play water polo, ride from incredible slides, swim in the pool or play in the children's town. Probably not to find a child who would not have such emotions evoked positive emotions. In the water park you can spend at least all day. After all, right on its territory there is a cozy express cafe where you can have a bite and relax from outdoor activities.

Sparrow Bird Park

What could be more surprising than communicating with wildlife? In the park "Sparrows" contains about 2 thousand species of birds and animals. Such an adventure will be not only interesting for the child, but also useful for its further development.



In addition, the organizers offer everyone to stay in houses above the river, visit a farm where ostriches are raised, ride a real carriage or take pictures with birds.

At this place, pleasant leisure awaits not only the children, but also their parents. The entertainment program is designed in such a way that not a single visitor would be bored. In addition to all kinds of attractions, the complex has a small cinema with a 4D system.
