Hotel Vera Seagate Resort 5 * (Turkey, Belek): reviews, description, photo

If you plan to spend an excellent vacation in Turkey and prefer a high-quality, but not unbearable vacation, then the five-star Vera Seagate Resort hotel can be a suitable option for your stay.



Location

The hotel complex "Vera Seagate Resort" was built directly on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. It is located seven kilometers from the center of the spa town of Belek and thirteen kilometers from the city of Serik. Antalya, the tourist capital of Turkey, is 45 kilometers away. The airport is 30 kilometers away from the hotel. The way to the hotel from the air harbor usually takes about an hour, since on the way the bus takes tourists to other places.



Belek, Vera Seagate Resort 5 *: description and photo

This hotel was built in 2005. Until November 2013, it was called the Diad Hotels Belek Resort. After that, the Vera Hotels & Resorts hotel chain bought it, and it got its current name.

The housing stock includes 235 rooms of a standard type. There are also apartments specially equipped for the disabled. Each room has a minibar, an LCD TV with cable channels (including those with Russian language broadcasting), a telephone, a hairdryer, air conditioning, a safe, a bathroom and a balcony. Laminate flooring.

Dining at the hotel is carried out on an all-inclusive basis. In addition to the main restaurant, functioning in the buffet format, there are also two √† la carte restaurants (with the possibility of ordering dishes from the menu) - fish and Italian. The hotel also has several bars with drinks and a snack bar.

The hotel has its own sand and pebble beach, equipped with sun loungers and parasols. The hotel has several pools, including for children, water slides, sun terraces, spa, gym, sports grounds. Animators work all day, and in the evenings entertaining shows and shows are held.

According to most tourists who have had a vacation here, this hotel is perfect for family guests with children and older people. Young people and travelers who are accustomed to outdoor activities can be a little boring here.



Vera Seagate Resort 5 *: reviews of Russian travelers

Looking ahead, we note that the vast majority of tourists were not disappointed in their choice and are ready to recommend this hotel to their friends and relatives. But we learn about everything in more detail.



Hotel itself

In general, our compatriots, for the most part, were satisfied with the rooms offered to them in the hotel in question. So, according to them, the apartments here, although not large, are quite comfortable, bright and clean. There is enough space for everyone. The furniture and appliances in the rooms are not new, but everything is in good and working condition. If something fails, then contact the reception, and all problems will be eliminated as soon as possible. In their reviews, a number of tourists indicate the fact that when checking in, it is best to choose apartments whose windows overlook the access road to the hotel. The fact is that here you will not be disturbed by extraneous noise. But some of the guests, whose windows overlooked the neighboring hotel, complained that loud music was very disturbing to them.

As for the hotel, it is quite compact. However, most tourists liked this, since there is no need to spend a lot of time and effort moving between infrastructure facilities. In addition, everything is planned very competently, there are nice places for walks and pastime. The territory itself, according to our compatriots, is very clean, beautiful and well-groomed.



Check-in, cleaning

According to our compatriots, the administrators of the Vera Seagate Resort, which we are now reviewing, in most cases adhere to the hotel policy regarding the check-in of newly arrived guests in their rooms after two in the afternoon. However, some tourists managed to speed up this process by paying a certain amount. But in any case, even if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning, you will not have to spend several hours in the lobby with your suitcases waiting for check-in. So, upon arrival, they will immediately put on your bracelets, and you can use the entire infrastructure of the hotel along with other guests, leaving things at the reception.

As for the cleaning of the rooms, in general, our compatriots were satisfied with its quality. True, according to them, the maids sometimes came at a not very convenient time or forgot to leave clean towels, but these problems were solved after a call to the reception. Cleaning is carried out every day. Towels and bed linen are updated several times a week. By the way, many guests note that both towels and linen are new and snow-white. So using these items is a pleasure.



Nutrition

Most of the guests of Vera Seagate Resort (Belek) had no complaints about the quality of food. So, according to them, the choice of dishes here is quite worthy for the budget "five". There are always perfectly cooked poultry and fish on the menu; meat is served several times a week. There are not so many seafood here, but you can taste them from time to time. In addition to the main dishes, the buffet always has a large selection of salads, side dishes, snacks, pastries and sweets. Also, tourists who arrived on vacation with their children, note the presence in the main restaurant of a children's corner. There are cereals, mashed potatoes and other dishes for babies. There is also a microwave and blender. So if you plan to come to Vera Seagate Resort with a small child, then there is no need to bring food with him.

In addition to the main restaurant, during the day you can eat on the beach. Here you will be offered a variety of fast food - pizza, hamburgers, nuggets, etc. In addition, the hotel has several bars where you can enjoy an alcoholic or soft drink to your taste.

Sea

Many of the guests of this hotel in their comments praise the local beach. According to them, it is very clean and large. Sun beds and parasols are available in sufficient quantities. So there is no need to run ashore early in the morning to take a deck chair. According to our compatriots, at any time of the day you can find free sunbeds here. Of course, this is unlikely to be the first line, but nevertheless, there will be no problems with accommodation.

The beach itself is sandy. But when entering the sea at the bottom of pebbles. Therefore, some tourists recommend taking special shoes with them.



Entertainment at the hotel

You can have a great time on the territory of Vera Seagate Resort 5 * (Belek). So, there are several pools with sun terraces and bars. Also in one of the pools equipped with water slides. During the day, tourists are entertained by animators. With everyone they want to do yoga, aqua aerobics, play water polo, darts, volleyball, etc. In the evenings, entertaining shows are held with the participation of invited artists - Turkish night, magic night, acrobat shows, etc. These events are often with pleasure visited not only by adults, but also by children.

Lovers of relaxation can visit the local hammam. The tourists who visited it greatly praise the work of massage therapists.

In their reviews, many tourists especially note the work of the hotel staff.
