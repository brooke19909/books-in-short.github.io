Laxmi Palace Resort 2 *: hotel description and reviews

Laxmi Palace Resort 2 * is located in India (North Goa, Morjim village). This hotel is a combination of simplicity and grace, a colorful interior and exterior with exotic greenery, bright and juicy. The place is perfect for a budget tourist who wants to relax for a relatively low price.

Description of hotel and rooms

The four-story building Laxmi Palace Resort 2 * was built in 2008, and 5 years later it was restored. The green painted exterior walls blend wonderfully with the surrounding vegetation, which can be admired from the balcony in almost every room.

Only 12 rooms of three categories:

  • Studio - 25 square meters. m
  • One bedroom apartment - 36 sq. M. m
  • Two-bedroom apartment - 70 sq. M. m

All have a refrigerator, a kettle with mugs, sugar, tea and coffee, a bathroom with a shower, a TV, internet access, air conditioning, a minibar with free water and access to a balcony or terrace.





Nutrition

The cost of a trip to Laxmi Palace Resort 2 * includes breakfast only. The on-site Caf√© the Fresh Restaurant serves a variety of dishes. Near the hotel you can find several cafes with delicious food.

Entertainment and the beach

An outdoor pool is on site. In the evening, a disco is held for guests. The garden adjacent to the hotel allows for day and night walks. There are no other entertainments at the hotel, so lovers of spending time here actively will not like it.

Two kilometers away you can find shopping centers with a wide variety of products. They have catering facilities, as well as grocery and souvenir shops. You can take a walk in the city center, which is 10 km away.

The beach is public, located 1500 m from the hotel. There are sunbeds and umbrellas, but you will need to pay for them.

Near the hotel there are attractions. The nearest of them is almost 1.5 km. This is a fort in the town of Chapora, built by the Portuguese in the XVII century.





Services

Laxmi Palace Resort 2 * offers free parking for guests. If necessary, it is possible to exchange currency at the hotel, wash and iron clothes for an additional fee.

Reviews

The opinions of tourists from all over the world about Laxmi Palace Resort 2 * (India) come down to the fact that the hotel is not bad, but it could be better. The main complaints are put forward by the guests to the level of service. Some came to the hotel and were forced to check into another room, since the reserved room was not ready. Other tourists complain of dirt that is everywhere, including textiles. But many guests left positive reviews and believe that this is an ideal option to relax for such a small amount.
