Short description of the city

Khabarovsk is a city, the center of the Far Eastern Federal District and the Khabarovsk Territory. This is the largest political, cultural and economic center of the region. The city is located on the right bank of the Amur River, near China. The climate here is very contrasting, because winters are snowy and cold, the temperature can drop to -25 degrees, and summers are wet and hot, the temperature can reach + 25-30 degrees. Khabarovsk is full of cultural attractions, for example, here you can visit the Transfiguration Cathedral, the Amur Bridge Museum, the Assumption Cathedral, the White Theater, a temple named after Seraphim of Sarov.
