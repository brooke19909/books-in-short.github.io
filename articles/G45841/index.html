AKS Porto Heli Hotel 4 * (Greece / Porto Heli): reviews, photos

The Peloponnese is considered the southernmost region of mainland Greece, but is not popular with domestic tourists. And completely in vain, because there are clean sandy beaches, picturesque nature and an abundance of historical attractions. There are fewer hotels here than in other resorts, so the coast is not crowded with tourists. The resort has both small hotels and large complexes. For a budget holiday, the popular resort hotel Aks Porto Heli Hotel 4 * is suitable. We will tell you more about it in this article.

Hotel location and nearby attractions

The hotel is located near the public port of Emionides, which is 900 meters away. Opposite the complex is a public beach, which can be reached in 2-3 minutes. For this, tourists will need to cross the pool and the sunbathing area. The hotel is located at a distance from the central part of Porto Heli, so you will not find many cafes, restaurants and shops nearby. But at night it is quite quiet, which attracts many tourists with children and elderly people to the hotel. You can get to the resort center by taxi or public transport. The distance to it is approximately 3.5 km. In addition, a small marina is located at the pier next to the hotel. From here, guests can take sea taxis to the island of Spetses, famous for its beautiful natural landscapes. The ancient monastery of Agion Anargiron is 12 km from Porto Heli, and the Katafiki Gorge is 18 km away.





Typically, tourists come to the hotel Porto Heli Hotel 4 * (Peloponnese, Argolis) from Athens International Airport. The distance to it is about 180 km. You can get to the resort by car or bus. The whole journey will take about 2-3 hours. Upon request, the hotel can also arrange a paid transfer for tourists round-trip.

Important hotel information

Four-star hotel Porto Heli Hotel 4 * is a five-story building, built in a modern Mediterranean style. Next to it is a spacious swimming pool with fresh water and a sunbathing area. They offer views of the immediate surroundings, and some have direct sea views. The hotel was completed in 1975, but it can not be called obsolete and dilapidated. In 2000, a full repair was carried out here. Cosmetic renovations are also held annually in the rooms. In total, more than 200 apartments have been prepared for guests, intended both for single occupancy and for living with the whole family. The resort staff speaks English, French and Greek.

Settlement of new guests begins at 14:00. Express check-in is available for a fee. Upon arrival, the administrator will require you to pay a deposit by credit card or cash. To register, you also need a document proving your identity. When leaving the hotel, the deposit is usually returned. Tourists must leave their rooms no later than noon, otherwise they will have to pay another day of their stay.

Number of rooms

For guests of the Porto Heli Hotel 4 * in Peloponnese prepared 200 comfortable rooms. Most of them are intended for two adults, to which you can additionally add a child. Guests can choose between double and single beds. For family holidays, triple rooms are additionally equipped with a sofa. The living quarters also have an open balcony and a combined bathroom.
Also, hotel guests can take advantage of the following amenities:

  • Plasma TV with satellite TV;
  • music player and radio;
  • a refrigerator for storing food and a minibar for drinks;
  • individual air conditioning;
  • a safe provided for an additional fee;
  • direct dial telephone;
  • a set of cosmetics in the bathroom;
  • built-in hairdryer and bath towels;
  • daily cleaning of living rooms;
  • room service in the morning, afternoon and evening.




Hotel infrastructure

On the territory of the complex AKS Porto Heli Hotel 4 * in Peloponnese there are many infrastructure facilities necessary for a comfortable stay. Guests can rate the quality of local services using the following amenities:

  • a spacious lobby, equipped with soft sofas and armchairs, a TV and air conditioning;
  • 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, ticket sales and safe deposit boxes;
  • a modern conference room equipped with computers and projectors (it is available for a fee);
  • a garden with benches and a rooftop sun terrace;
  • a mini-shop where you can buy snacks and drinks, souvenirs and beachwear;
  • jewelry store where jewelry is sold at discounted prices;
  • laundry, where you can hand over dirty clothes and shoes for an extra fee;
  • Private parking with free parking for hotel guests and car rental;
  • paid hairdresser.




Beach, pool and other leisure facilities

Porto Heli Hotel 4 * (Greece, Peloponnese, Argolida) is located on the bay, so the beach can be reached from here in 2-3 minutes. The hotel has its own stretch of coast, equipped with free sunbeds and umbrellas for guests. The bay also has a water entertainment center where tourists can enjoy boating, parasailing, surfing and diving. Water slides are available for children. Towels are provided on bail. Guests can play beach volleyball for free.

Next to the hotel is a rectangular outdoor pool filled with fresh water. It does not heat up. Its area is about 400 square meters. Here tourists can also use free sun loungers and sun umbrellas. For small children there is a separate pool.





The hotel is focused on a relaxing holiday with your family, so there are no nightclubs. In their free time, tourists can play water polo or table tennis. A paid fitness club is open daily. A mini club is open for children in the morning and afternoon. During high season, the hotel hosts entertainment programs, including a movie theater. Outdoors, you can play on the playground with a swing and a sandbox. The hotel also has a games room and a children's library.

Catering

Arriving to relax in the Porto Heli Hotel 4 * complex, tourists can be sure that the hotel will take care of organizing their meals. The price of the stay usually includes the All Inclusive concept. The main restaurant serves a common buffet in the morning, afternoon and evening. You can also grab a bite to eat at a local buffet or order room service. The menu of the main restaurant consists of dishes of intercontinental and Greek cuisine. Local soft drinks and spirits such as beer and red wine are also free. For a fee, tourists can visit the rooftop restaurant. It serves Mediterranean cuisine and seafood. Exotic cocktails can be enjoyed at the poolside bar.





Positive feedback about the AKS Porto Heli Hotel 4 *

When choosing a hotel for vacation, tourists often prefer to first read reviews of other travelers. The Porto Heli Hotel 4 * complex has a good reputation among vacationers. They believe that the cost of living here is consistent with the quality of service provided. So, tourists list the following advantages of the hotel:

  • modern air conditioning - virtually no noise, and the level of cooling is conveniently controlled using the remote control;
  • friendly and friendly hotel staff helping tourists to get comfortable in the resort;
  • a spacious and always clean pool, and there are always free sunbeds on the terrace;
  • the rooms are always tidied, and on the balcony there is a clothes dryer;
  • a free bus runs to a good section of the beach every half hour;
  • entertainment program for children - mass games, watching movies in the fresh air are held.




Negative reviews about Porto Heli Hotel 4 *

However, some tourists were still unhappy with the rest in this place. They warn other travelers and do not recommend checking in here. In their reviews, they indicate the following disadvantages of the complex:

  • old furniture in the rooms, worn-out plumbing in the bathroom;
  • the beach area near the hotel is small, and the sea is dirty and shallow, so tourists are forced to relax away from the hotel;
  • the restaurant serves a small buffet, so if you come for breakfast later, you can stay without food;
  • some guests are accommodated in rooms located in the basement, where there are no windows;
  • increased humidity and unpleasant odor in some rooms;
  • Some of the guests complained about the presence of cockroaches and other insects in the room.

Should I come to this hotel on vacation?

The resort complex Porto Heli Hotel 4 * is a good option for tourists looking for a relaxing holiday in a quiet place. It is suitable for older travelers and families with children. Its advantages include a good location, friendly staff and low cost of living. However, the hotel needs to be renovated, so if the internal state of the room is an important criterion for you, it is better to choose a different hotel.
