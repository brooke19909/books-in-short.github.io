Compressor failure

What should I do if the Bosch two-chamber refrigerator (Know Frost) stops freezing? In most cases, the problem is compressor failure. When this happens, repairs are very expensive. In order to independently inspect the device, you must remove the back cover of the refrigerator. The pipe can not be touched. First of all, it is important to twist the capacitor. It is mounted near the roller. The next step is to remove the bottom filter.

After that, it will be possible to see the compressor of the Bosch refrigerator. When it breaks, it burns very much. In this case, the contacts appear blackened. Malfunctions with a temperature regulator

The temperature controller is installed at the rear panel, which has a Bosch refrigerator (two-chamber, Nou Frost). Failures with this part are rare. To repair the device you have to disconnect the base. It is mounted in all models with screws. Under the base is a small gasket. Behind it is a dehumidifier: you can not touch it. In order to inspect the thermostat, you have to twist the protective cover. In many models, it is fixed on the stops. In this case, the edge of the plate you just need to pry a sharp object.

After that, the temperature controller can be carefully removed. During inspection, the wiring connection is checked first. If the connecting contacts are broken, you can try to fix the situation with a soldering iron. If the problem is a breakdown of the regulator specifically, you must contact the workshop.

Terminal box repair

She very rarely fails. This happens, as a rule, due to overvoltage in the network. Repair of Bosch refrigerators in this case should be started by disconnecting the motor. It is located next to the pipe, however, it is forbidden to touch it. The capacitor must be removed during repair. The terminal box is located at the bottom of the refrigerator. It is located on a small stand, which was specially built for it by the manufacturer. To repair the part, the base does not need to be unscrewed.

To repair the terminal box, first clean the contacts. After that, inspect the pipe. If it has moisture, it should be removed. This can be done with an ordinary rag. If the above steps do not help, then you have to take the refrigerator to a service center.

Spare parts for refrigerators can be ordered through service centers.
