The recreation center "Friendship", Arkhipo-Osipovka. Description, reviews

If you want to see the most unusual landscapes around you, the Friendship recreation center in Arkhipo-Osipovka invites you. It will surprise you with a truly unique location. One of its sides is bordered by mountains, a river flows nearby, and, most importantly, within walking distance is a sand and pebble beach on the Black Sea.

The recreation center "Friendship", Arkhipo-Osipovka. Description

On the territory of the recreation center, many flowers and trees are planted, in the shade of which you can hide from the summer heat. Sports lovers can work out on the equipped sports ground. The main beach of the resort village of Arkhipo-Osipovka is just 100 meters away. The developed infrastructure is presented on the beach for tourists: bars, restaurants, souvenir shops, all kinds of attractions, including water ones.

Near the health resort, the picturesque Vulan River flows into the sea. Meals are available in the private large dining room at an additional cost. Tourists can leave their transport in a free parking lot.

For young guests there is a playground.

As for amenities, they are located separately (this applies to summer houses). Hot water in the shower is served around the clock. In the guest houses of the Druzhba recreation center in Arkhipo-Osipovka, amenities are provided in the rooms.

The central promenade can be reached on foot, and if you want faster, it is possible for an additional fee by boat. The hostel welcomes guests from June to October inclusive. You can get to Arkhipo-Osipovka by regular bus from Krasnodar. If a group calls in, you can pre-order a bus for its delivery.

Guest Accommodation

The Druzhba recreation center located in the village of Arkhipo-Osipovka offers its guests accommodation in comfortable two-story buildings (eight rooms each) and in summer houses. There are several types of rooms in cottages:

  • double and triple - equipped with furniture, TV, fridge, air conditioning, private bathroom;
  • quadruple two-level - with furniture, TV, refrigerator, air conditioning, private bathroom, there is also a sofa and a mirror.

In the summer building, guests can stay in standard double or quadruple rooms with single beds and a refrigerator. The toilet and hot shower can be used in a separate sanitary unit, located 50 meters from it.

Additional services

In order for your stay at the recreation center "Friendship" in Arkhipo-Osipovka to be more diverse and memorable, various additional amenities are provided on its territory.

  • In the spacious private dining room for guests, three meals a day are provided. It is paid separately.
  • Picnic lovers can use barbecue grills to prepare barbecue in the fresh air.
  • Car owners are provided with a parking space.
  • Under the shady trees there are comfortable benches.
  • Children can spend their time on the playground and sports grounds.
  • For lovers of outdoor activities at the base there is a volleyball court and a table for playing table tennis.
  • If your room does not have a TV, then you can watch TV shows in a dedicated separate housing.

Arkhipo-Osipovka. The recreation center "Friendship". Reviews

Tourists who rested at the recreation center "Friendship" leave their objective reviews for future guests. There are many good points.

  • Firstly, everyone notes a large number of flowers on the territory. Especially a lot of roses. The area is very well maintained.
  • The base has its own parking lot.
  • The rooms have everything you need, bed linen and towels are very clean, there are blankets, also clean.
  • There is a convenient volleyball court, on which, when the heat subsides, there are people who want to stretch and compete.
  • The TV works well, though there are few channels, but on vacation this is enough.
  • There is always hot water in the shower.
  • You can put the brazier directly at your house and use it while you are relaxing at the camp site.
  • There are small tables near the house, so you can have lunch outside, in the shade of trees.
  • The beach is very close, the water in the sea is clean.

There are a number of comments regarding the stay at the recreation center Druzhba in Arkhipo-Osipovka.

  • Car owners note an inconvenient entry into the base.
  • The rooms have very poor soundproofing.
  • Some vacationers complain of very hard sofas in the rooms. However, extra mattresses are provided here.
  • There are no wardrobes in the rooms, so you have to store things in suitcases or bags.
  • There are no curtains in the bedroom on the second floor.
  • Many tourists consider it a drawback that the rooms are not cleaned. Apparently, it is not included in the price.
