Crafts from the "Kinder Surprise" with children. What can be made of plastic eggs from Kinder Surprise?

A treat such as Kinder Surprise is enjoyed by all children. And this is not surprising, because the child immediately receives both sweetness and a souvenir, such a 2-in-1 gift. Small figures of fairy tale and cartoon characters that come across in a chocolate egg become favorite toys. But where to put the plastic containers from under them? Sometimes they are going to a lot. Do not rush to throw them away. In this article we will tell all moms and dads how to make interesting products from such boxes. Making crafts from Kinder Surprise is very exciting. Attract the kids to this lesson, they will really like to "turn" with their own hands an ordinary trinket into an interesting toy. What can be done from Kinder Surprise? We learn this from the following sections of the article.





Caterpillar

If you have about a dozen containers of chocolate eggs, use them to make a toy in the form of a caterpillar or a snake. It can serve not only as an attribute for entertainment, but also as a decorative element decorating a large pot with a houseplant. To perform such crafts from eggs from Kinder Surprise, we need plastic boxes (at least 5 pieces), a narrow ribbon or cord (1 meter), wooden or acrylic beads and awls.

Getting to the manufacture of toys. With an awl in the containers we pierce holes on the end sides. Remember that in two boxes that will serve as the head and tail of the caterpillar, you need to make only one hole. Next, we collect all the individual details of the crafts from "Kinder Surprise" in a single design. We stretch the ribbon into the first container (head), tie a knot, and close the box. Next, we string a bead on the tape. Then we fix one more plastic part. Thus, we catch all the containers, placing beads between them. We put the last box (tail) on the ribbon and tie a knot inside, close it. Now you need to draw a caterpillar face. Cut out eyes and mouth from colored paper, glue the elements with transparent tape. You can also decorate the body with an application (draw spots or stripes). The craftwork turns out well bending, it can be twisted and bent. The kid will really enjoy playing it.





Puppet show

Thinking about what to do from Kinder Surprise? Why not use sweet egg boxes to make puppet theater heroes? Such toys will always be relevant. You can play whole theatrical performances with your whole family. Let's look at examples of making such Halloween-themed toys. For the creative process, we prepare the following materials: chocolate egg containers, wooden skewers, awls, acrylic paints or markers, plasticine, pieces of braid, ribbons, laces.

Pumpkin - Halloween Theme Toy

To make crafts from Kinder Surprise in the form of pumpkins, we select a container of yellow or orange chocolate eggs. We pierce a hole in its lower part and insert a wooden stick into it. Inside, on its tip we string a piece of plasticine. It will serve as a holder and will not allow the skewer to fall out. If you want to make the mount more durable, use hot melt adhesive. Close the container. We draw the muzzle of the pumpkin. We mold a hat from plasticine and glue it on the top of the plastic container. In the place where the skewer comes out of the box, we tie a bow from a ribbon or braid. The craft is ready.





Spider

We continue to study ideas on the topic "Crafts from eggs from Kinder Surprise. From the following description we learn how to make a spider. It can also serve as a hero of a puppet theater, be a decorative decoration of pots with indoor flowers or the walls of the aquarium.

The plastic egg is completely stained black with a permanent marker. Next, with a hot awl, we pierce a hole in its lower part, approximately in the middle (on the long side). Insert the skewer into the hole and fix it with hot glue or plasticine. Now we begin to make paws. To do this, use an awl to pierce parallel holes on both sides in the lower part of the product. There should be six (three from each edge). Through these holes we stretch cuts of laces. Their length should be 7-8 centimeters. We tie knots at the ends of the laces . On the front side of the container we make a muzzle a spider. Acrylic paints or corrector draw eyes and mouth.

On the same principle, you can do other crafts from the "Kinder Surprise" for the puppet theater.





New Year's toys

Chocolate egg containers are an excellent material for making Christmas tree decorations. In our master class, we will tell you how to make your own toy pendant in the form of a fish. For work, you need such materials:

  • plastic container for a sweet egg;
  • thin decorative ribbon for decorating gifts;
  • golden or yellow beads;
  • accessories "eyes" or two small buttons;
  • golden braid with sequins - 50 centimeters;
  • thermal gun or glue "Dragon", "Titanium";
  • thin fishing line;
  • needle;
  • awl.

We make a fish (decoration for the New Year tree) according to the following instructions. To make the tail, we cut the decorative ribbon into pieces of 8 centimeters. We put these blanks on top of each other. With a needle with a fishing line stretched into it, we pierce a stack of ribbons from one edge. On the one end side of the container with an awl, we make two holes, placing them close to one another. In them we stretch the ends of the fishing line and tie it on a knot from the inside. So that the mount is reliable, drip glue on it. Close the box. Next, glue the entire surface of the plastic container with tape with sequins, laying it in a spiral. On the back of the beads we form a fin, having previously stretched a piece of fishing line through them, which will serve as a loop. Glue the eyes in the right place. We tie the fishing line sticking out of the fin onto a knot. New Year's toy fish is ready. Similarly, you can perform other crafts from "Kinder Surprise." With your own hands you can make a whole set of toys for decorating the Christmas tree.





Original crafts from Kinder Surprise toys: box

What to make from empty chocolate egg containers, you already know. But where to determine the toys themselves, if the child is tired of them and just wallow in a box? They can be used to make caskets. How? We are telling.

For work, we prepare: a wide plastic cream container, a Kinder toy, Secunda or Moment glue, silver or golden spray paint. We attach a toy to the middle of the lid. Let the glue dry. We decorate the box with spray paint. That's all. Handmade casket is ready! The sweet egg figurine in it serves both as a pen and as an ornament.
