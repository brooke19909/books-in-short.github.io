Wonderful do-it-yourself sock hares

DIY toys attract all the little ones. Such little things are soft, tender, cute and funny. To make them from the material available to any mom is very simple! If you invite children to work, you get a wonderful joint lesson. A fun toy - a handmade hare made of socks - you can present to friends or relatives.

A simple bunny from a sock without a pair

Very often, socks lose their pair. This is a very common situation, and in our chest of drawers there is one almost new sock, which for some reason is a pity to throw away. It is from him that you can create a wonderful bunny having paws and ears.

For work you need: sock, needle and thread. You will also need small buttons or beads - for the eyes, pink threads - for the mouth and nose, and a narrow ribbon. Any filler - synthetic winterizer, holofiber or cotton wool.

So, we make a hare of socks with our own hands:

  1. Turn the sock out and straighten the heel, laying it down.
  2. Gently cut the cuff and spill it. This part is useful for sewing the tail and forelegs.
  3. Fill the sock with the filler, reaching the ankle. Firmly tighten the thread on top. Under the heel zone, also tie a thread - this is the neck of a hare.
  4. The heel itself will play the role of the face, we will issue it later.
  5. In the lower, toe part, on both sides, select two leg hooks and also tighten them with thread.
  6. Above the top constriction we have left a long part of the sock. It must be cut lengthwise along the entire length and stitched along the edges with a seam ‚Äúover the edge‚Äù. These are the long ears!
  7. Cut the cuffs into three parts, put a small lump of filler in each and pull it with a thread. Sew the pens and tail in the right places.
  8. It remains to attach the bead eyes and embroider the nose.

Tie a ribbon around your neck and you're done! You can play.

Baby Bunny

Every kid loves baby dolls. After all, they are so tender and sweet that they can only cause emotion and love.

These hares are made of thick kapron.

Prepare the following materials and tools for manufacturing:

  • White toe capron.
  • Pink sock.
  • Threads of white and black colors.
  • A piece of felt fabric - white or light pink.
  • Hollofiber or cotton wool.
  • Needle, scissors.

Let's get started!

  1. We cut off the cuffs of the white sock. In the remaining "bag" we put the filler and sew on top.
  2. In the neck area, we will drag with a white thread and tie it, hiding the tips inside the "body".
  3. In the same way we will make the lower paws.
  4. We embroider eyes with black thread - select the color according to your desire.
  5. Next, take the pink sock and remove the cuffs, as well as the lower part along with the heel.
  6. This is a sweater - let's put it on a bunny. From the bottom we also cut a piece - panties - we put on and sew between the legs.
  7. From a piece of felt, cut out the ears and sew to the head.
  8. The rest of the capron will go to the handles and tail.
  9. We tie bows of pink nylon to the ears and make a scarf.

Our cute baby doll is ready.

Do-it-yourself "anti-stress" hare from a sock: step-by-step instruction

Making a soothing baby toy is not at all difficult. To do this, you will need:

  • Smooth, tight toe.
  • Felt matter.
  • Thread or elastic band.
  • The pompom is small.
  • Beads - 2 black and 1 pink.
  • Glue crystal.
  • Stuffing - buckwheat or rice groats is ideal, one glass is enough.
  • Narrow satin ribbons.

Step 1. Pour the cereal into the sock and tie the thread on top. Tighten the neck area, but not very tight.

Step 2. Cut the free part of the sock lengthwise to the hauling thread. These are ears - sew them around the edge with any seam convenient for you.

Step 3. On the face, glue the eyes and nose - beads.

Step 4. Attach the pompom ponytail and tie a beautiful bow - on the neck for the boy, on the ears - for the girl.

Fluffy hare

All that is needed to create this positive bunny is 2 long terry socks and a sewing needle with thread, a synthetic winterizer, buttons, a pink bead.

In order to make a hare of socks with your own hands, you need to cut socks.

  1. One in the foot is cut lengthwise, not reaching the heel of 2 cm - so we will prepare the ears for work.
  2. We cut the same sock on the other hand in the same way as in the first case - legs.
  3. From the second toe of the foot, it is cut to the calcaneal region and cut into two parts along. Got blanks for pens.
  4. Cut, it remains to flash all the seams.
  5. On the first toe, we sew in the ears - we make tight stitches so that the fabric does not "go" after stuffing.
  6. The legs also need to be sewn up, not forgetting to leave a hole for the synthetic winterizer.
  7. Do the same with the handles - sew the side seams and fill with filler.
  8. Sew them to the right places and decorate the face of the bunny with buttons and beads for the nose.

For kids it is better to make embroidered eyes so that they do not accidentally tear off and swallow buttons.

This is how fluffy terry hares are made from socks with your own hands. There is practically no difficulty, but great joy.
