How to dry a rose correctly? Proven New and Old Ways





A composition of dried roses, carnations, chrysanthemums and other flowers can become as worthy a decoration for a home as a bouquet of living plants. The main thing is that the material from which the product is made looks aesthetically pleasing and attractive. And for this you need to know how to properly dry roses and other flowering plants at home. The most reliable and proven methods are described in this article.

How to dry a rose: method number 1

Your attention is the easiest way to dry flowers. In a dry and darkened room (pantry, attic, barn), pull the rope. Hang each rose head down on it. Flowers should not touch each other. Make sure that the air in the room circulates all the time. Direct sunlight and humidity can spoil the appearance of a flower: the petals fade, twist and crumble. After 8-10 days, the flower will dry completely.

Method number 2

Do you think how to dry the rose bud so that it remains saturated color and the original volumetric shape? Use our hint. Wash and dry river sand. Then sift it through a fine sieve. If you do not have natural sand, then quartz, which can be purchased at pet stores, is quite suitable for these purposes. It is designed for swimming chinchillas.





Remember how to dry a rose in the sand. In a deep cardboard or plywood box you need to pour a layer of sand (5 cm). Insert flowers in it with the buds up. Then, with careful movements, fill the plant all the way down to the very heads. Make sure that the roses do not lean, but stand exactly upright. Cover the box and leave it in a dry place for two to three weeks. After this period, make small holes at the bottom of the box and let the sand slowly spill out through them. Inside the container you will see beautiful dry flowers.

Method number 3, or How to dry a rose using professional tools

In the floristic craft, there is a drug - silica gel. It is a bulk material that quickly absorbs moisture from plants. This is where roses can be dried. But when working with it, carefully read the instructions for use. Violation of technology can lead to the fact that the flowers burn and crumble.





Dry roses in an old proven way

If you need flowers flat or even in the form of separate petals, then you can dry them between the pages of books. How to dry a rose in this way, we know from childhood. Put the buds in a thick book. Put the load on top. Do not open the book within 10-15 days. As a result, you get a dry flower of a flat shape. For drying individual rose petals in this way, it will be enough 5-6 days.





How to dry a rose quality? Useful Tips

  • To dry, cut the flowers in dry weather so that they do not have drops of rain and dew.
  • If the bud has changed color, it can be tinted with nail polish.
  • Treat already dried flowers with hairspray. This will make them stronger and protect against moisture.

Using these simple methods, you can dry roses while preserving a bouquet dear to your heart and pleasant memories of the occasion when it was presented to you.
