The village of Kadnikovo, "White Horse" (equestrian club, recreation center). How to get there?

In the Sverdlovsk region there is a unique equestrian club "White Horse". This place has a rare infrastructure that creates optimal conditions for riding lessons. In addition, the construction of the club was carried out in accordance with modern European standards. This makes it possible not only for professional athletes to conduct training here, but also for lovers to learn the basics of equestrian sports. Further in the article, we will take a closer look at the infrastructure and services of KSK "White Horse" (Kadnikovo).



The main wealth of the club

Located in the village of Kadnikovo, "White Horse" has on its territory a number of stables that accommodate more than 130 animals. Each of them is equipped with automatic watering machines, sinks and solariums. In addition, the equestrian complex has an indoor training arena and field. Typically, each arena is equipped with a geotextile coating that protects against injuries. In addition, the recreation center "White Horse" (Kadnikovo) accommodates four arenas 20x60 meters in size, each of which is equipped with a bar, free Wi-Fi and changing rooms with showers. Classes in the club are conducted by professional trainers with higher specialized education. For information, they are winners and winners of not only Russian, but also international equestrian competitions.

With the youngest age group (from 7 years old) classes are held on ponies and Vyatka horse breeds. These animals are short and soft. It is worth noting that every child can learn the basic riding skills. Parents of young riders can safely entrust their children to experienced trainers, as they conduct classes in compliance with all safety requirements.

All children under 14 years of age are taught the basics of riding in an indoor arena. The duration of one lesson is 45 minutes. However, it should be noted that for the first lesson the child (or his parents) must bring the insurance with the mark "equestrian sport". This document can be obtained from any insurance company.

Adults can study in both indoor and outdoor playpens. Those who are first trying their hand at this sport will certainly be provided with a well-circled and calm horse. Mentors will explain how to control the animal, stay in the saddle, move from a step to a trot or gallop. Experienced riders arriving in Kadnikovo, "White Horse" offers dressage lessons or show jumping. Please note that adult riders must also have insurance with the appropriate mark.





Romantic walks

KSK "White Horse" (the village of Kadnikovo) provides everyone with a great opportunity to relax from the bustle of the city, riding a horse in the forest. Just imagine how many pleasant and warm minutes this romantic evening will give to lovers! In addition to secluded communication, couples can enjoy the beauty of the Ural forest. And cheerful groups of like-minded people not only together will enjoy the joy of peaceful communication with nature, but will also receive a lot of indelible impressions. Horse walks take place near Yekaterinburg in a forested area. It is worth noting that this event has a clearly planned route lasting 45 minutes. Before the trip, an experienced mentor individually selects the most suitable temperament animal for the rider. Before going for a walk, the coach will make sure that all participants in the event have insurance with the appropriate mark.



Additional services

In addition to carrying out the above entertainment events, the club offers a ride in the picturesque places surrounding the club in a carriage. In the winter, everyone can take a sleigh ride. A chance to be photographed with horses. For this purpose, the animals are dressed in elegant ammunition. Visitors can choose to shoot at the arena, in the stable or in the open field with a beautiful view of the river and forests.

Children's camp

In the village of Kadnikovo, "White Horse" gives all children the opportunity to feel in a new role, learn an unfamiliar exciting game or do what they love. For this purpose, a children's camp has been created on the basis of the club. In it, qualified teachers arrange theatrical performances, conduct intellectual games, and also organize fights in various sports. In "Talented White Horse", children can reveal themselves in show programs and concerts, and just spend time actively, studying with the most affectionate animals in the world - horses. The camp program is designed for children from 6 to 14 years. The distribution of children in the units is carried out according to the age principle. The daily regimen is formed in accordance with the psychophysiological characteristics of children. In addition, they seek to intelligently alternate rest with active phases of activity.





Parents note that the daily routine does not leave children the opportunity to spend time idly, because they have every minute scheduled. For accommodation, small guests are provided with two-story houses, which accommodate no more than 5 people. Five meals a day will provide the growing body with the amount of calories that is necessary for development and active pastime. Children's diet has a variety of dishes. It serves fish and meat dishes, fresh pastries, dairy products, fruits and vegetables.

"The White Horse" (Kadnikovo). How to get to the club?

From Yekaterinburg we are moving towards the Chelyabinsk tract. On this road we go to the traffic police post, which is located at the turn to the city of Sysert. Before reaching the police cordon, you need to turn right, and then immediately left (under the bridge). Now the path will run through Kadnikovo. Immediately after the village, turn left. The green roofs of the equestrian complex will be visible from here. Of course, this path is not the closest, but as simple as possible.
