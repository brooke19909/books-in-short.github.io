Basic parameters of bank deposits

When choosing the optimal product, as well as the bank itself, the client should take into account the following conditions for the deposit:

  • deposit rate and term;
  • deposit currency;
  • the possibility of depositing additional funds to the account;
  • early withdrawal of money and closing of the deposit;
  • availability of interest capitalization;
  • way of registration.

The interest rate indicates the income of the client and is expressed as a percentage for the year. As a rule, banks prefer to make deposits for long periods, so the interest rate increases depending on the term of the deposit. However, if the depositor for some reason closes the deposit ahead of schedule, then in such a situation the rate under the contract changes to the base one, which is several times less than the originally established one.

The deposit period is of two types: unlimited and urgent. An unlimited deposit provides for the possibility of replenishment of funds, and in most cases it is allowed to withdraw money from the account without reducing the interest rate, which, as a rule, is lower than for term deposits. A fixed-term deposit involves investing money for a specific period at a higher interest rate.

The deposit can be made in rubles, dollars, euros and other currencies.

If it is possible to regularly replenish the deposit, you should choose a product for which the addition of funds to the account is permissible.

If the financial position of the depositor is sometimes unstable and depends on external factors, then perhaps you should choose a product that provides for early withdrawal of money.

Capitalization is the addition of interest accrued over a specific period to the deposit amount for a subsequent increase and is an important parameter of any deposit.

Making a deposit is possible with a personal visit to any of the bank branches with the necessary documents or remotely. An example of remote registration is the VTB Seasonal Deposit 24. Customer reviews indicate that there are no problems with remote deposit processing via the Internet.
