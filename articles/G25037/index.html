How to make a bouquet with your own hands from toys?

A bouquet of soft toys ... It sounds strange, but, nevertheless, this direction in the field of souvenirs and gifts is very popular today. Why not? It would be very pleasant for every girl, girl, woman to receive, with or without him, a beautiful bouquet of soft toys. Such a present can be done independently. In this article we will tell you about how to make a bouquet with your own hands from toys. Watch, read, learn!



Making bouquets of soft toys requires prior preparation. You need to decide what kind of plush characters will be the basis of the product, and prepare them. Soft toys-keychains are very suitable. They are small in size, therefore, you can put a lot of them in a bouquet. You can also use homemade figures of hares, bears, and dolls as the main characters of the composition. They can be knitted, sewn or knitted from wool. The bouquet may consist of 2-3 toys of standard size (12-15 cm tall), which are sold in each children's store. Note that the composition can include both exactly the same figures, and different.

To make a bouquet of toys from your own hands, you will need such materials: a piece of thick foam, a tube for making a pen (you can use a cardboard base from food foil or film), floral wire, wrapping paper or a flap of a beautiful fabric, corrugated paper, decorative ornaments.

DIY bouquet of toys: the basics

A cone must be cut from the foam, the diameter of the lower part of which should correspond to the circumference of the cardboard tube. Spread this end with a hot gun or glue "Dragon", "Titan". Attach to the handle tube. When this workpiece dries, cover it with cloth or brown paper, securing it with a construction stapler. Next, pierce each toy in the region of the legs with a piece of floristic wire, the ends of which twist together and stick into the foam base. Alternatively, to make a bouquet with your own hands from toys, you can use long bamboo skewers instead of wire.



Decoration of the composition

The next stage in compiling a plush bouquet is its decorative design. The space between the toys can be filled with flowers. Moreover, they can be natural or artificial, made from sweets or sausage balloons. The pen and the whole bouquet must be wrapped with corrugated paper or decorative mesh. DIY bouquet made of toys. He is ready to please himself the hero of the occasion.

How to make original bouquets from toys? Some more useful information

Instead of a cardboard tube, you can use a basket, a cardboard box or a flower pot as the basis for a bouquet. Decorate them with cloth, paper, lace, insert polystyrene inside, and then fasten the toys as described above.

To make the whole composition look like a single whole, select toys and decorative elements in matching tones and proportions. For example, toys of different sizes do not look very good in one composition. It is better to take different gizmos, but about the same size.



Each toy can be worn with a kind of skirt made of wrapping paper. This will give strength and additional volume to the product.

Original handmade bouquets of toys will be wonderful gifts for your children and friends. Let your needlework bring joy to others!
