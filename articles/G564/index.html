Hotel Hidden Paradise 3 * (North Goa, Ashvem): photos and reviews of tourists

Recently, Goa has become one of the most popular tourist destinations for our compatriots. Every year, thousands of fans of beach vacations, amazing diving and incredible exotics literally travel to distant seas to spend their holidays on this Indian island.

The tourist infrastructure here is well developed, especially on the North Coast. At the same time, you can find a variety of accommodation options, from cheap hostels and guesthouses to smart five-star hotels. However, the best option are three-star hotels, for example, Hidden Paradise 3 * (India, North Goa, Ashvem). Here, the prices are affordable and the conditions are quite comfortable. The main thing is very clean, there are no insects in the rooms, everything is just sterile, which cannot but please the eye.



What is the difference between North and South Goa?

All those tourists for whom rest is peace and quiet, a deck chair, an umbrella, headphones in their ears and an interesting book, without a moment's hesitation, can go to the south coast of the island. Here you will find a respectable vacation, breathtaking scenery, clean beaches and the sea of ‚Äã‚Äãunprecedented beauty. It is here that the most expensive Goa hotels are concentrated , many of which belong to global hotel chains.

Tourists who are not very demanding on comfort prefer to save on housing, but are willing to spend a lot of money on entertainment, a hospitable north coast awaits. It is here that the Hidden Paradise 3 * hotel is located. To everyone who falls on this coast, one of the old-timers does not forget to report that the famous Beatles once performed here on the coast.



Description

The name of the island of Goa comes from the word Govarashtra, which is translated from Sanskrit as "shepherd‚Äôs country." Here on the beaches you can meet peacefully grazing cows. This is the calling card of the island.

As you understand, we are talking about South Goa, but in the northern part - incredible energy, the spirit of freedom, the opportunity to find and understand yourself. Of course, at first glance, unsanitary conditions, licentious morals and poverty of the local population reign here, however, even staying in 2- or 3-star hotels, for example in Hidden Paradise Mandrem 3 *, you will not feel this at all.

Both the internal structure and the quality of services are fully consistent with international standards. The administrative center of the northern part of Goa is the resort Mapusa (Mapusa).



How to get to the hotel Hidden Paradise 3 * (North Goa, Ashvem)?

It is most convenient to arrive on the island by air. To the delight of our tourists from Moscow, the Northern capital and other major cities of the Russian Federation, both direct flights and connecting flights as well as charters are carried out here. Tickets cost about $ 400 (round trip), flight time about 7 hours.

By the way, between Moscow and Goa the time difference is about two and a half hours. Rossiya Airlines operates a direct flight from Sheremetyevo Airport. You can use Qatar Airlines connecting flights, as well as Air India, Emirates, etc. The cost of tickets for these flights does not vary much, but a little cheaper, starts at $ 315. However, twice as much time is spent on the road - 10-19 hours. So if you bought a tour to the Hidden Paradise 3 * hotel (India, North Goa), then you can choose different flight options.

All flights land at Dabolim Airport, the only air hub on the island. And from here to the desired resort, in this case, Ashvem, where the above-mentioned hotel is located, can be reached either by taxi (this will cost from 800 to 1200 INR) or by bus, which is several times cheaper. You can also buy a transfer, or rather include it in the price of the tour, which most tourists do. This is the best option.



The beach on which the Hidden Paradise Hotel is located

Ashvem is one of the quietest places on the island of Goa. It is located on the north coast. There are very few visitors here. Rare couples stroll along the sandy beaches, smooth and comfortable, from where a beautiful panorama of the turquoise sea opens. There are absolutely no cliffs that can be seen in other areas of the island. The sand on the beach is not white, but grayish, because it is of volcanic origin.

Sand boulders look beautiful. The sunset that opens to the eye from the shore is considered the most beautiful. Tourists who relax in the Hidden Paradise 3 * hotel have the opportunity to watch this beautiful picture painted by nature every evening. You need to go down to the sea on a gentle slope. Water in the sea is clean, swimming in it is a pleasure. Nearby are the beaches of Arambol and Morjim. Vacationers from the Hiddem Paradise Hotel can always go there and soak up the sand.

Weather

Tourists who are going to visit Goa and stay at the Hidden Paradise 3 * hotel, naturally, are interested in what the weather is like on the island. Since it is located in a subtropical climate, here the average temperature all year round varies between 30 and 36 degrees in the daytime, and about 26 degrees at night. Despite this, some months of the year for recreation are not quite suitable. This summer. So in this period it is better to go on vacation to some other resort, but Goa is better left for dessert: for the velvet season or winter. In the winter months, you need to especially take care of the skin and use sun protection products.

Hotel location

Hidden Paradise 3 * is located 50 km from the airport and 24 kilometers from the Thivim railway station. The closest is the bus station - 13 km from the hotel. The nearest beach, as noted, is Ashvem. It is 450 meters away, meaning you can walk there in 7-15 minutes. Near the hotel are shops and a market.



Hotel Description

The Hidden Paradise was built in 2013. The opening year is 2016. The hotel is located on the territory of 3,000 square meters. meters. It consists of one two-story building - the Main Building and eighteen one-story cottages. The hotel is separated from the beach by the road. You need to go to it for about 10 minutes, and if through the yards of the houses of fishermen, then 3-5 minutes.

Number of rooms

In total, the hotel Hidden Paradise 3 * 29 rooms. Of these, 9 are deluxe. They are located in the main body. These rooms are 23 square meters. They can accommodate no more than 3 people. The remaining 20 are wooden cottages. In each of them is placed a single 22 m 2 room. They can also accommodate up to 3 guests. By the way, what is called an extra bed is just a mattress, which is available on request. As you can see, there are no standard numbers here. It offers views of either the pool or the garden. Both landscapes are beautiful.

Description of rooms

The rooms in the main building have either a balcony or a terrace. Cottages also have access to either the garden or the balcony. They have a set for making tea and coffee. There is a canopy bed (s), which is very romantic, a desk, bedside tables, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

There is also air conditioning, the possibility of wireless Internet, a minibar (for a fee), a refrigerator, a safe, also for a fee, a mirror on the entire wall, an iron (if desired, an ironing board can be ordered in the room).

Bathrooms are well equipped. They have a bath and shower, toilet and washbasin, several towels, toiletries (soap and paper).

As for the service in the rooms, linen, towels, and also cleaning are done 1-2 times a week. This is the charter of the Hidden Paradise 3 * hotel. Reviews of tourists regarding the service, it can be said, are pulled by the C grade. However, one should not forget, this is just a budget hotel, which has not 5, but 3 stars, and you should not demand from it a luxury service.



Nutrition

Those who cannot imagine their life without meat dishes, of course, have nothing to do at the Hidden Park Hotel, as its restaurant is designed exclusively for vegetarians. In addition to the restaurant, there is one bar serving delicious cocktails and drinks. When making a tour, tourists can choose two types of food - BB (breakfast only) and HB (half board, that is, breakfast and dinner). Breakfast is continental: sandwiches, jam, cheese, toast, sometimes oatmeal, and dinner - dishes of local and European cuisine.

Infrastructure

The hotel has a rather large swimming pool with sunbeds and umbrellas, a spacious sun terrace, a spa and Ayurveda massage parlor, and there are also doctor services. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available. If you rented a car, you can leave the car in the parking lot in front of the hotel. The reception is open around the clock, next to it there is a left-luggage office. However, check-in time is 12.00 and departure time is 10.00. For tourists, excursion routes are organized.



Beach

As already noted, from the hotel to the beach about 400 meters. This is a public area, entrance to which is free for all, without exception, but umbrellas, deck chairs and other beach equipment are provided for a fee. Here you can rent a scooter, sign up for diving courses and take an underwater trip. Play beach volleyball.
