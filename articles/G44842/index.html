Kata Noi Resort 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): description of the hotel, rooms, infrastructure, photos and reviews

The hotel complex Kata Noi Resort is conveniently located, to the delight of tourists, in one of the best places in fabulous Phuket, 47 kilometers from the airport. Just a few minutes walk separates it from the amazing Kata Noi beach. Convenient transport links will allow you to quickly get to your destination from the airport and back, move around the island during your vacation.

A quiet and picturesque area with an abundance of bright greenery and the beauty of nature will allow vacationers to plunge into the local flavor with an affordable pricing policy of the hotel. Own swimming pool, bar and restaurant facilities give a feeling of comfort and convenience throughout the entire stay in the country. As a result, for a tour lasting ten days, lovers of a thoughtful and enjoyable vacation will have to pay no more than 200 thousand rubles.

In the kitchen, guests can cook their own meals or take advantage of existing offers. For those who like to soak up or have fun, there is a swimming pool that is open during daylight hours. In a small but clean artificial pond, you can splash around not only for adults, but also for children.

If you want to get to know the local beauties and history closer, you can trust professional guides who will guide you about the places of interest to you.

Kata Noi Beach

Kata Noi is one of the most convenient and cozy beaches of Phuket, which will allow you to have a wonderful time without additional expenses. Its duration reaches 800 meters, and the strip width is more than 50 meters. Compared to other recreation areas, it is considered small, but one of the most comfortable.

The small size of the bay did not allow shipping to develop in this region, so all guests will be able to enjoy the cleanliness of the territory and silence. The beach is separated from the busy highway by a chain of buildings, so the noise will not disturb tourists.

Numerous comfortable trestle beds with umbrellas are available for everyone, and towels are provided at the hotel. The beach area consists of pure white sand without an admixture of stones, so even young vacationers from the Kata Noi Resort can safely move barefoot.

The entrance to the water is gentle and unsharp, so you will have to walk 5-7 meters, until the depth reaches 1.5 meters. The bottom is sandy and clean, it can be seen through the turquoise water of crystal clearness and coolness. With the help of constant swims, you can perfectly escape from the Thai heat.

A nice feature of this recreation area is the distance from numerous entertainment complexes, so nothing will disturb the silence and the existing peace. On the reefs you can find marine life that delight with its colors and exotic look. The price of rest at the hotel is also pleasing, as tourists then rest on the beach at the expensive Katathani hotel, through which you need to go on the way to the ocean.

Nutrition

The hotel does not provide for the mandatory availability of food, so tourists can cook their own meals or use the services of local cafes, which are located in the immediate vicinity of the hotel and allow guests to choose the best option for themselves. Trips

Kata Noi can be easily reached by taxi, which takes no more than 10 minutes and is inexpensive. All local experts are well aware of this area, so getting lost is unrealistic even with a great desire, but it is better to remember the name of the Katathani hotel nearby for orientation.
