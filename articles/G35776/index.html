Sberbank branches in Kirov: addresses, opening hours

The addresses of the branches and the operating hours of Sberbank in Kirov are presented later in the article.   • granting a loan for education;

Branch offices serving privileged customers

Sberbank Premier is a special service package that offers a number of privileges to bank customers. For example, an increased interest on deposits, favorable interest rates on loans, favorable exchange rates, free servicing of premium debit cards, insurance, and medical advice from leading world experts. Clients of the Sberbank Premier service package can be serviced in any bank branches, however, a personal manager is attached to each client, ready to make an appointment at a convenient time for the client and answer all your questions. Personal managers serve only in the dedicated Sberbank Premier zone, which operates in only a few branches in this city.
