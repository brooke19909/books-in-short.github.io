The recreation center "Irtysh" in Omsk

50 km from the city of Omsk on the banks of the Irtysh River is the village of Chernoluchye. There is a recreation center "Irtysh", where everyone is offered a comfortable rest and the organization of special events in nature.

brief information





The recreation center "Irtysh", the photo of which is available in the article, is located in a picturesque and environmentally safe place right on the banks of the Irtysh river. Around the forest stretches where you can collect delicious berries and mushrooms. The territory of the recreation center is large and well-maintained. There is a main building, residential houses, sports and dance floors, a cafe, a bathhouse, a sandy beach and a large number of various entertainments. Also, various types of events are often held at the recreation center: weddings, graduations, sports competitions, birthdays and so on.

You can relax here at any time of the year. True, some houses are only available for living in the summer. But then the rooms are heated, so they are comfortable even in severe frosts.

  • beach vacation;

Leisure activities





The following leisure options are available for holidaymakers at the Irtyshye recreation center:

  • bike rides;
  • games of volleyball, table tennis;
  • billiard room;
  • discos held every weekend and public holidays;
  • board games (for example, chess, checkers, backgammon);
  • sandy beach of the Irtysh river.

In winter, guests are offered such forms of entertainment as downhill skiing, ski slopes for beginners and professionals, and an ice rink.

Steam room lovers will love the opportunity to take a steam bath in a Russian wood-fired sauna using their favorite essential oils and traditional birch brooms.

For children on the territory of the recreation center "Irtysh" there is a children's room where the kids are supervised by the teacher, as well as a playground with slides, swings and other entertainments. Also in the cinema for younger guests are constantly showing interesting cartoons.

Location

The address of the tourist base "Irtysh": the village of Chernoluchye, Resort Street, 12. It is 50 km from the city of Omsk. You can get there by personal or public transport.

By car, drive along the Krasnoyarsk tract to the village of Chernoluchye. Next, you need to get along Kurortnaya street to the sign "Recreation center" Irtysh. " Buses leave here from the Omsk bus station daily.
