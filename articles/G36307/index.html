Coffee houses in Kazan, where to go for a coffee break?

Kazan is one of the most visited cities in Russia. Crowds of tourists are attracted by the rich history and true Tatar flavor. The city is also famous for its gastronomic base, occupying one of the leading positions in the opinion of critics and travelers.

Important detail - institution concept

During a vacation or in the middle of a working day, you often want to take a break by going to a cozy establishment. Kazan coffee houses have all the conditions for this: freshly brewed coffee, mouth-watering desserts, a relaxed atmosphere and a scent in the air. What else is needed for relaxation and a pleasant conversation?

Coffee shop "Korzh-coffee" - everything for rest and leisure

In the heart of the city, on the pedestrian Bauman Street, the town of Korzh-Kofe is open. The interesting thing is that there used to be a cinema "Tatarstan" here. In the place of his foyer is the current cafe hall. A large room was equipped at home: soft sofas, comfortable chairs, unobtrusive lighting. Incredibly soulful atmosphere! Of the many coffee shops in the center of Kazan - a win-win choice.

In the assortment, as befits such a kind of institution, all types of coffee from several varieties. In addition to the traditional drink, modern options are offered - for example, orange raff coffee, original cocktails and homemade soft drinks, freshly baked cakes and rolls. And one of the main features is board games. You can get together with friends or family to have fun.



The average bill is 300 rubles, address: st. Bauman 58B.

Wake up with the Lark

If the question arises about breakfast, the Lark coffee shop in Kazan will leave a vivid impression of such an ordinary phenomenon. The institution promotes the culture of first meal and specializes in traditional morning dishes: various cereals with a huge selection of toppings and additives, fresh pastries and traditional cookies, curds and yoghurts. The interior is bright and welcoming, the most comfortable setting to start the day.

All this revolves next to the main symbol of a peppy morning - coffee. Several varieties to choose from, all classic types and several non-standard. Rough coffee, coffee-based cocktails. Professional barista is working on coffee machines, as well as preparing an air press. The cafe is suitable for friendly meetings, sweet conversations or simply saturation with delicious food.



The average bill is 250 rubles (very democratic!), Address: ul. Professor Nuzhin 7.

A chain of coffee shops in the center of Kazan

One of the first institutions of this concept is Chicolat. Since 2003, a whole network has developed that maintains status in the center of a major tourist city. Convenient location also plays a role - large shopping centers, where soft chairs, calm music and pleasant aroma stand out among the hustle and bustle. At the moment, this is a whole brand that is associated with real brewed coffee and snacks.

The menu includes breakfast, salads and sandwiches for those who like a hearty meal. Coffeemans will enjoy classic espresso and americano. On their basis, no less tasty cocktails are made, as well as milky necks and designer tea bouquets. Macaroons and strudel of own production perfectly harmonize with drinks. For especially active citizens and tourists there is a take-away system. A place that set a standard for Kazan coffee houses for the future.



The average bill is 300 rubles, addresses: ul. Yamasheva 97, st. Petersburg 1, st. Moskovskaya 2, st. Spartak 6.
