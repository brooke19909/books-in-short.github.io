Tropics

The three-star Tropiks Hotel (Saint Vlas, Bulgaria) with 52 rooms is located just 70 meters from the beach and a 15-minute walk from the resort center. Despite the fact that it is located in the city, it is calm here. The four-story building of the hotel complex is quite presentable. Its facade, decorated with intricate glazing, faces the city street, and a pool area is equipped behind its back.





The hotel has spacious double rooms, new ergonomic furniture, air conditioning, comfortable furnished loggia. The TV is tuned to Russian channels. The small refrigerator is serviceable and roomy enough. In the reception area, Wi-Fi is pretty decent in speed. The hotel staff is Russian-speaking.

The hotel has a children's playground and a children's pool. The territory of the hotel complex is quite compact, there is little vegetation on it.

Beach vacation

Rested in St. Vlas, tourists recommend this resort in their reviews. They unanimously celebrate the local particularly clean and pleasant air. Mountains, like a natural barrier, do not allow wet air masses. The air in St. Vlas is a cocktail of mountain freshness and sea ions, it is relatively dry. The average temperature is 27 degrees. The holiday season lasts from May to September.

The sandy beaches of St. Vlas "Marina" and "Central" are quite small, the water area near them is limited by compact bays. Connoisseurs of sunbathing and swimming, noisy youth parties, water sports prefer to visit the wide beaches of Sunny Beach stretching along the coast through transport. They can be reached by bus in 15 minutes.
