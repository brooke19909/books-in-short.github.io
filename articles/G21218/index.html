State farm "Russia": rest, photo, reviews

The successful location of the village distinguishes it from other coastal towns. It is characterized by picturesque vegetation and a mountain landscape in a quiet bay.

Geographical position

On the coast of the Black Sea, ten kilometers from the city of Adler, there is a quiet and clean place to relax - the state farm "Russia". This resort picturesque village, located in the Adler region of Sochi, is only one kilometer from the famous Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay and not far from the Abkhaz border. Recently, a new promenade was built in it with objects in the same style and with bicycle paths.

Infrastructure, entertainment, beautiful landscapes

The village has many catering outlets with a varied cuisine, among which it is easy to choose the right restaurant for every wealth and taste. In the daytime you can have a meal in the dining rooms and private eateries, and in the evening sit in cafes or bars. Inexpensive amusement park with attractions is perfect for children of all ages. In the house of culture weekly children's performances are held. On the territory of the village there are shops, a Magnit supermarket, pharmacies, Sberbank of Russia (there is no ATM) and mail. Along the new beach there is a bike path. There is a bike rental point.

State Farm "Russia" has many picturesque places with views of the sea and mountains. Here is a rich flora. For family walks, a cypress avenue equipped with comfortable benches has been planted. For vacationers who love to see the sights and interesting places, there is the opportunity to go on excursions to Adler or Sochi, which are in the immediate vicinity.

Beach

A large number of vacationers, unfortunately, adversely affects the cleanliness of beaches and water off the coast. Such problems arise in large resorts, for example, Adler, Sochi. The state farm of Russia is famous for its free beaches, clear water off the coast.

Small, sand-like pebbles cover the entire coast. Due to this, the water is transparent. In some places you can find huge boulders. The beach is flat and wide, without breakwaters. On its territory there are comfortable locker rooms, rental of catamarans and sun loungers. Fans of water entertainment can ride on a "bun", scooter or banana. On the beach, massage therapists offer their services for therapeutic massage. Any beach area is free.

Housing

There are many options for living in the village: from cheap and modest rooms to comfortable rooms in mini-hotels for a very reasonable price. The most common type of housing in the village is the private sector. There are a lot of offers in it. You can find an option for any wallet. A lot of private hotels with various rooms are offered to their guests by the state farm Russia. Photos of tourists will capture not only the beauty of local landscapes, but also comfortable comfortable rooms in mini-hotels. They are clean, with good repair and everything necessary for a comfortable stay.

There are no sanatoriums on the territory of the village, but there are recreation centers and camp sites known since the times of the USSR. The main advantage of housing at the Rossiya state farm is its affordable price compared to similar offers in other areas of the coast. A room with the same living conditions in Sochi or Adler will cost the tourist much more. And the Rossiya state farm also has an additional advantage - the cleanest sea and fresh air. This, unfortunately, cannot be said about the more famous resort cities. You can rent housing on a state farm directly with the owners, saving on the percentage of intermediaries. When choosing a village to relax in the autumn, you need to remember about seasonality. Many, but not all, residential properties receive visitors all year round.

Popular with vacationers Tsimlyanskaya street

An ideal place for a relaxing holiday on the clean Black Sea coast in Russia is the Rossiya state farm. Tsimlyanskaya, Staroshkolnaya, Uritsky and Staroklubnaya are the names of the four streets on which almost all the infrastructure and residential facilities of the village are located. Its advantage is that any accommodation is located near the beach. Uritsky Street is central in the village, a 5-minute walk to the sea. Cypress trees grow on it. The most popular among vacationers is Tsimlyanskaya Street, since it is the closest to the sea. All houses located on it have an equipped descent to the sea. Not all housing has a sea view on Tsimlyanskaya Street. The coast can be observed from the windows of a private hotel (house number 1-a) or residential buildings after number 38, which are also removed from the center with its vibrant nightlife, which guarantees peace. The remaining houses of Tsimlyanskaya Street, although they do not have a view of the coast, are located away from the highway with minibuses and a patch with night cafes. The bus route runs through the streets of Uritsky and Nizhne-Imeretinskaya. The convenience of housing on Tsimlyanskaya is that the beach is close by, walking close to entertainment is about 7 minutes, and a quiet sleep is provided.

In addition, many houses have excellent living conditions. Their advantage in comparison with the hotel is the presence of a cozy courtyard, surrounded by greenery. In the shade of the trees you can dine or just sit in the fresh air. Vacationers understood all the advantages of Tsimlyanskaya street, so many who come again or on the advice of friends choose housing on it.

Reviews

The cleanest sea, refreshing air, a spacious beach, a beautiful new promenade are the huge advantages of the resort, according to the unanimous opinion of vacationers who visited the Rossiya state farm. Guest reviews of the village are usually enthusiastic. Some tourists who love outdoor activities, note that the amount of entertainment is small. But still, all other advantages compensate for this small drawback. Vacationers are surprised at the hospitality and hospitality of the inhabitants of the village. Most of all, the guests of the farm like transparent sea water and a comfortable beach of small pebbles. Some even saw diving dolphins, indicating the cleanliness of the sea off this coast.

Well, of course, they cannot but please the tourist with affordable housing prices with good conditions. Most vacationers are confident that they will return again to this beautiful clean place.

conclusions

Rest at the farm "Russia" is perfect for families with children, senior citizens, calm couples who love peace and purity. Of course, the number of objects can not be compared with the city of Adler, but it has all the necessary infrastructure for entertainment. The sea is clean, the beach area is not crowded, there are no crowds. People do not feel like "herrings in a barrel." Around the colorful landscapes. Housing prices are relatively low. State Farm "Russia" is one of the best recreation areas among the Black Sea sunny resorts of the Russian Federation.
