How to get there

For those who want to get to the resort village of Kudepsta in order to personally enjoy the natural beauties and visit the FSUE pension Avtomobilist (Sochi), there are several options for travel to the final destination:

  • Firstly, bus number 105 K. goes from the airport to the city. It will take you directly to the village. There is also the opportunity to get to Kudepsta from the Sochi railway station: buses No. 125 and 122 run. But after that you will have to take a ten-minute walk to the boarding house.
  • Secondly, an aeroexpress train leaves from the airport to the neighboring settlement of Khost (Sochi). Next, you need to transfer to bus number 134, going straight to the village.
  • It is possible to get to the resort and from Adler. From there buses No. 134, 118, 60 A and other routes leave.
