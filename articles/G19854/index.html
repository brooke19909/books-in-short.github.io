MG White Lilyum Hotel 5 *, Kemer, Turkey: reviews and photos of tourists

Preparing for an annual vacation, often people prefer a hot and colorful Turkey, which is famous for its mild climate, clear waters of the Mediterranean, fresh air and a friendly population. Here you can combine business with pleasure: having satiated with bathing and entertaining excursions, go on shopping, having bought many new clothes and gifts at attractive prices.

Resort Summary

Camyuva resort, located a little south of the legendary Kemer, is a tiny village rich in attractions and beautiful mountain scenery. Staying here, you should definitely visit the unique antique city of Phaselis and the incredible beauty of Paradise Bay. The five-star MG White Lilyum Hotel is the place to stay for travelers. Smiling staff, comfortable rooms for living and an abundance of dishes to satisfy hunger are to the liking of every hotel guest.

Beach

The local coastal pebble-sand zone is divided among the hotels. 35 meters away is the MG White Lilyum Hotel. In order to reach the coast, it is necessary to overcome a distance of 150 meters by crossing the roadway.

Crystal clear water, through which the bottom and underwater inhabitants of the shallows are visible, is interesting when swimming with a mask for both the older and younger generations. After lengthy bathing and diving, the opportunity to lie on a deck chair under the scorching sun is given, getting a brown tan. A soft mattress, an amazing cocktail from the beach bar, endless sea distances and closely spaced mountains create a sense of paradise. For those who love shadow, umbrellas are provided.

The infrastructure of the coastal zone includes a guard service and rescue workers who tirelessly monitor the order in this territory. Essential components are showers, changing rooms and a toweling point on bail.

Here you can play volleyball, having plenty of fun and having made new acquaintances. Team spirit, enthusiasm and drive are characteristic for everyone who is a participant in these competitions. Numerous water attractions, riding a banana, a circle, scooters, a kayak, a canoe and a catamaran will bring bright colors and unique sensations to a beach holiday.
