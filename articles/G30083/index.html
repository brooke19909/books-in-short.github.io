Gelendzhik: pensions and resorts. Rest in Gelendzhik

A resort town popular in our country and beloved by many Russians is located in the Krasnodar Territory, in the south of Russia. Gelendzhik is a relatively small city. Its population is 72,030 people. It is located in the Gelendzhik Bay. Every year, tens of thousands of vacationers come here for recreation and treatment, and Gelendzhik is popular not only in the summer. Pensions and sanatoriums of the city for the most part operate year-round.



But mainly in the summer, tourists come to Gelendzhik. Pensions and resorts of the city attract guests with excellent service, comfortable living conditions, the ability to combine a pleasant vacation on the Black Sea coast with wellness. We invite you to get acquainted with some of them. Perhaps this will help you choose a great place to relax.

"Blue distance"

In the village of Divnomorskoye (in the vicinity of Gelendzhik) is the sanatorium "Blue Distance". Here, fans of a quiet measured rest, as well as lovers of active pastime, can have a great rest. Guests are offered to stay in comfortable rooms in one of three buildings. Their total capacity is 540 seats.



Guests will appreciate the well-maintained beach with a length of 200 meters, a modern medical base. The shady park of the sanatorium, which occupies 15 hectares of territory, is attractive. Here you can always find a corner to read, dream, relax in the shade of sprawling trees.

Many vacationers enjoy spending time at the decorative outdoor pools surrounded by magnificent rose gardens. In the park you can see rare species of plants: magnolias, Lebanese cedar, weigella, calendula, daylilies and many others. The park smoothly turns into a relic grove in which Pitsunda pine grows - the main attraction of the flora of these places. Here, these rare trees occupy the largest territory in the world - more than 800 hectares.

Rooms

The sanatorium "Blue distance" has rooms of European class category 3 *. Vacationers can settle in one of three buildings surrounded by greenery. They include:

  • 237 standard rooms;
  • 6 junior suites;
  • 12 standard rooms without a balcony;
  • 16 deluxe rooms (two rooms).

All of them are designed for two people, but additional places are also provided (folding beds or folding chairs). Regardless of the category, the rooms are equipped with split systems, televisions, refrigerators. The junior suites have a bathroom with shower. From the windows you can admire amazing views of Divnomosky Bay.

Nutrition

It should be noted that the organization of proper nutrition in the sanatorium is given great attention. The dining room here consists of two halls - "Blue" and "Red", which in total accommodate 520 guests. For guests staying in junior suites, there is another room - "Yellow Acacia".

Guests are offered a varied three meals a day. The menu includes dishes of Russian cuisine, a lot of vegetables and fruits. There is a special diet menu.



Treatment

The sanatorium is focused on the treatment and prevention of the following diseases:

  • respiratory organs;
  • circulatory system;
  • disturbances in the work of the nervous system;
  • metabolic disorders;
  • musculoskeletal system.
