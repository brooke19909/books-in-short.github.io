How to make your daughter a doll house with your own hands?

Each daughter at least once in her life asked her parents: "I want a house for dolls." Do-it-yourself DIY is much easier to do than to buy a finished product in a store. This solution has a number of advantages. The main one - doll housing will fully meet the requirements of your child. In the process, you will definitely consult with your daughter and take into account all her wishes.





Sketch

The first thing every home made for dolls begins with is a sketch. We take a set of pencils and draw its appearance. If imagination is not enough, then we use ready-made ideas. It is important to consult with the child and take into account his ‚ÄúWishlist‚Äù. The end result of this stage is getting finished housing for dolls on paper.





Markup

At the next stage, you need to decide on the material from which the house for dolls with your own hands will be made. Most often, cardboard or wood is used for these purposes. From the first of them it is much easier to make a house, but it will not differ in a high degree of reliability. When overloaded, it can simply collapse. Therefore, it is better to choose a tree. For example, plywood. It is simple to make from it, while the resulting design will be strong and reliable. Then we analyze the sketch of the house. You need to make a list of details that will be needed in the process. Next, we draw each of them with a pencil and a ruler, taking into account the dimensions on the cardboard, and cut it out. You should get a set of blanks. The next step is to verify their correctness. To do this, using adhesive tape, we glue them and assemble the house. If the assembled model corresponds to your plan, then we apply each of them to the surface of the plywood and draw it. Otherwise, you need to look for the error and fix it. We do this until we achieve the desired result.

Cutting, assembly and processing





After marking the surface of the plywood, it must be carefully cut with a jigsaw. The edges of the workpieces are filed. Next, do-it-yourself doll house from the received details. You can use glue, screws and bolts to fasten them. From the point of view of appearance, it is better to use the first of them, but the latter provide the greatest reliability of the resulting design. At the final stage of how to make a house for dolls on your own, you need to properly arrange it. To do this, it can be glued with colored paper or wallpaper. Then, the furniture is made from cardboard, which is placed in it (this can be done by the child himself). After that, the house is ready. We place it in the children's room for games.

Conclusion

In the framework of this article, an algorithm was described on how to build a house for dolls on your own. There is nothing complicated about it. So we sit down and do it. At the same time, we take into account the wishes of the child and enjoy the end result. After all, there is nothing better than a gift that was made with one's own hand. And such housing for dolls for such purposes is ideal.
