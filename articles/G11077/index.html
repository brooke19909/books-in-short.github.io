Diamond Sunny Beach Hotel 4 *: Bulgaria is waiting for you

In the heart of the Black Sea resort Sunny Beach (Slynchev Bryag), which is located in Bulgaria, the magnificent Diamond Sunny Beach 4 * hotel is located. Life is boiling and bubbling around, because the hotel is located at the intersection of the main streets of the resort.





Sunny Beach

Some of the hotel rooms overlook the magnificent panorama: an endless series of Black Sea sandy beaches. And around the bustling bustle: colorful shop windows and boutiques, fun companies in bars, gyms, nightlife of clubs and restaurants, loud laughter of kids in playgrounds and water parks. Near the hotel complex Diamond Hotel Sunny Beach 4 * there is an entertaining water water park "Action". This is a fascinating world of entertainment for vacationers of all ages. It is impossible to pass by, entertainment and actions capture and attract with their freedom and endless fun.

Hotel location





Near the hotel there is an intercity bus stop, which is a convenient starting point for independent travel in different directions: to Varna, Nessebar and Burgas. There is something to see, where to go and what to see. These cities are the guardians of the centuries-old history of the country, its cultural centers. To spend a vacation in Bulgaria and not visit the nearest attractions is simply unreasonable, as many tourists think, and they are right.

Description

This is the oldest hotel in the resort. It was built in 1967. The location was then chosen by chance. Once this hotel was the resting place of the party elite. However, now it in no way resembles a socialist holiday home. In 2004, the hotel complex was reconstructed. Diamond Sunny Beach 4 * has turned into a chic modern hotel for youth and family comfortable relaxation.





Rooms

The hotel complex offers 301 rooms of various categories:

  • 284 standard rooms with an area of ​​22-25 square meters. meters designed to accommodate no more than 3 people;
  • 14 family studio type rooms, up to 32 square meters. meters, for accommodation in a room no more than 4 guests;
  • 3 suites, with an area of ​​32 square meters. meters, apartments with elements of increased comfort.




All rooms are decorated in soft colors with hints of caramel and greens. Created a feeling of home comfort. In each room is available:

  • air conditioning;
  • balcony;
  • refrigerator;
  • hair dryer;
  • wireless Internet;
  • combined bathroom;
  • toiletries;
  • telephone;
  • TV with several Russian-language channels;
  • beds, sofas, armchairs and other furniture;
  • towels and bed linen.

The room is regularly cleaned, linen and towels are changed.





For hotel guests

On the territory of the Diamond Sunny Beach 4 * hotel there is a huge pool for adult guests and a small one for children, with a mini-fountain in the form of a fungus. Around the pools are sun loungers and umbrellas.

The hotel complex has developed its own medical program, which is based on the use of a set of procedures:

  • thalassotherapy;
  • mud therapy;
  • balneotherapy;
  • Massage
  • cosmetic procedures.

For lovers of outdoor activities on site there is a gym. The hotel offers bicycle rental. Using this service, you can conduct a familiarization cycling trip in the surroundings. According to vacationers who took this opportunity, we can conclude that they were very pleased with such a walk. You can also rent a boat or boat to conquer the sea. According to the observations of holidaymakers themselves, who were secluded away from the coast, this way you can go fishing and get a good catch. Caught fish (for an additional fee) they then cooked in a hotel restaurant.

Nutrition

Restaurant Diamond Hotel Sunny Beach 4 * - this is the main catering point at the hotel complex. Dishes served to hotel guests are a true masterpiece of gastronomy. The assortment of dishes represents many traditional dishes of Bulgarian and international cuisines.





From time to time, restaurant chefs hold master classes and theme nights, dedicating Diamond Sunny Beach 4 * guests to the mysteries of preparing delicious dishes. In the lobby bar and cafe, guests are offered a variety of drinks, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. There are various light snacks, ice cream and a variety of desserts.





The hotel works on various types of service concepts: BB, HB, AI. It all depends on the type of ticket or tour. Typically, guests of Diamond Hotel Sunny Beach 4 * prefer on vacation all-inclusive service.

Service

Little guests of the hotel are not deprived of attention. For them there is a special playground for games, a children's pool and highchairs for lunch in the restaurant. On request (at an additional cost), an extra cot can be ordered in the room, if necessary.





The sandy city beach is located near the Diamond Hotel Sunny Beach 4 *, at a distance of about 300 meters. Entrance to the beach is free, but for everything else: sun loungers, deck chairs, umbrellas, a bathroom and shower - you have to pay separately if it is not included in the subscription.

Entertainment

The hotel has a small number of recreational activities. Periodically, a disco is held on site. You can have fun playing tennis, volleyball, billiards, visiting spa treatments, a hamam, a sauna.

The resort of Sunny Beach has a large number of places where you can have fun. One of them is the famous Christie winery. Reviews of tourists who visited this place are very, very positive.

Fans of shopping can enjoy shopping at the Mladost, Zephyr supermarkets. The resort has restaurants and pubs.

For business people there is a small conference room, an Internet club, a fax, wireless Internet, a currency exchange, and a safe.

The hotel has its own parking lot.





A string of bright colors, new acquaintances, a lot of impressions, a charge of vivacity, a feeling of eternal youth - all this will give you Diamond Sunny Beach 4 * (Bulgaria). Reviews of grateful tourists left at the hotel confirm this. Those who once relaxed here are returning to Bulgaria again and again.
