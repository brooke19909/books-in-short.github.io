Phillips smartphones: reviews, models, specifications, review

Philips is traditionally considered a supplier of inexpensive and at the same time functional smartphones. These can be presented, at the same time, in different price categories, and thus differ in terms of performance and performance. But how pronounced can such differences be when comparing the parameters of devices in relation to the difference in their cost? What are the main price segments for smartphones from Philips?





Phillips smartphones: price categories

Phillips smartphones, reviews of which due to the popularity of this brand are found on a large number of thematic online portals, mainly relate to the budget price segment. That is, the cost of most models of the corresponding devices does not exceed 10 thousand rubles. However, there are premium Philips smartphones - their prices exceed 20 thousand rubles. Of course, a lot depends on the marketing strategy of a particular retailer. It happens that firms offer their customers electronic devices at significant discounts.

Premium segment

If we talk about the most expensive models of mobile devices, that is, those that belong to the premium level, then among them we can distinguish:

- Philips X818.

- Philips V787.

- Philips S616.

Middle segment

To the middle segment of devices include devices:

- Philips V526.

- Philips S396.

- Philips V377.

Lower price segment

There is a lower price segment in the line of devices of the brand in question. If we consider the inexpensive Philips smartphone, then one can be found among such solutions as:

- Philips S337.

- Philips S309.

- Philips S307.

Consider the main characteristics of the devices in each of the marked price categories, as well as user reviews of the relevant solutions.

- auto focus function;

- support for serial, panoramic, HDR-shooting;

- digital zoom;

- sensory focus;

- face recognition function;

- Interfaces for setting white balance, ISO;

- automatic shutter release;

- choice of shooting scene;

- GPS module ;

- compass;

- built-in flash memory in the amount of 32 GB;

- RAM modules of 3 GB;

- battery with a capacity of 3.9 thousand mAh;

- dual LED flash.

Indeed, these characteristics against the background of what other Philips smartphones have (user and expert reviews can confirm this) look impressive, and allow, indeed, to attribute the device to the premium segment. In terms of quality of work and ease of use, the device is rated very highly by the owners and experts.
