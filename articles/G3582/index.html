How to check the balance on "Tele2" (Kazakhstan): 3 easy ways

"Tele2" came to the Kazakhstani mobile market in 2010. The key principles on which the company operates are good communication quality and affordable prices. Tele2 was one of the first to launch an expanded LTE advanced (4G +) network in Kazakhstan, a new wireless technology that provides data downloads over the Internet at speeds of up to 225 Mbps.

Convenience of service, competitive tariffs and wide coverage of communications attract more and more people to use the services of "Tele2". Especially for them, this article is devoted, from which you can learn about how to check the balance on Tele2 in Kazakhstan.

How to find out via the USSD command?

This method is the fastest: you do not need to listen to the voice message of the answering machine until the end or wait for the web page to load. By entering a digital command, you can check both the balance on Tele2 in Kazakhstan and other data on the subscription package of services.

To obtain data on the status of the personal account, you need to dial * 111 # from your mobile number. To find out information about the rest of the traffic and bonuses according to the tariff plan, you need to enter the command * 123 * 1 * 3 #. A list of other useful USSD codes can be found on the official Tele2 website.
