Dominican Republic, Boca Chica: description, photos and attractions

On earth there is an ideal place with beautiful beaches with soft and clean sand. Undoubtedly, this is the resort of Boca Chica in the Dominican Republic, located in the south of the country near the capital of Santo Domingo and washed by the waters of the Caribbean Sea.

This Dominican town is considered a favorite destination for both tourists and locals. Here you can have a wonderful vacation with the whole family, have a good rest with friends or retire in this paradise with your soulmate. Any tourist who comes here is guaranteed to be able to find a suitable activity for themselves.

Description

The first hotels in the resort of Boca Chica (Dominican Republic) were built in the twenties of the last century. They looked like residences intended for the summer holidays of the local elite. Thanks to the clear waters, the gentle sand on the white beaches and the close proximity to the capital, this town has turned into one of the most visited resorts in this state.



The main advantage that the Dominican Republic possesses (Boca Chica) is its natural coral barrier, which has a length of about five hundred meters. Today, the Dominican Republic (Boca Chica) is a busy place with many hotels. The convenient location and proximity to Las Americas International Airport make this resort very attractive for tourist trips.

Beach vacation

The Dominican Republic (Boca Chica) has a coastal strip stretching for three kilometers and consisting of several beaches, the feature of which is fine and granular sand, reminiscent of flour. The following beach areas are most popular with tourists: Playa Andres, La Matica, Los Pinos and the central beach.

Some vacationers prefer to receive sunbathing on a separate small island. In order to get to it, you just need to go through the shallow water.

The beaches here are the real pride of this Dominican resort. There are no deep waters, and the coast is clean and comfortable. Therefore, the Caribbean lagoon is very popular among families with children. Its maximum depth barely reaches one and a half meters.





Hotel and hotel complexes

The Dominican Republic (Boca Chica) hotels are mainly located on the coast, and many of them have their own pier. These are three-star and four-star hotels with excellent service and wonderful living conditions. They almost all work on an all-inclusive basis. Some complexes are located far from the coast, but, given the small territory of the resort, it cannot be said that these are some serious distances.

One hotel is even built near the water. Tourists resting in it can enter the water directly from its territory and dine in the evenings to the sounds of waves that create a romantic atmosphere. The name of the hotel Don Juan Beach Resort is fully consistent with the atmosphere prevailing there.

What can I do?

Dominican Republic (Boca Chica) can please its tourists with a variety of outdoor activities. Fans of scuba diving can explore the underwater world of this resort, where there are many coral reefs. Their inhabitants are various exotic representatives of marine fauna that amaze even sophisticated divers with their beauty.

Despite the fact that the shallow lagoon is famous for its peaceful and relaxing holiday, here, undoubtedly, fans of windsurfing and lovers of sailing will find entertainment for themselves . You can also play tennis or ride horses.

Tourists who come here in April can become participants in the annual fishing competitions, organized by the founders of the local water club.

Relaxing in the resort of Boca Chica, it is simply impossible not to try the culinary masterpieces from local chefs. There are a huge number of restaurants, cafes and bars that want to treat tourists with unsurpassed dishes of their national cuisine. Especially amazing is the taste of food prepared from the inhabitants of local waters. Those who prefer spicy dishes should try pineapple with black pepper, rum and chili.





Where to go on an excursion?

This resort is famous for its boat trips on yachts or boats. In the immediate vicinity of its beaches are two small islands - Los Pinos and La Matica. The first is interesting for tourists with its beaches. It became completely uninhabited after a hurricane fell on it and destroyed all the enterprises located here. When going there for an excursion, it is better to stock up on comfortable shoes with thick soles and mosquito repellents so that you can explore the dense thickets of this island.

On the territory of La Matica grows a magnificent mango tree, which gave the name to this island. Tourists who decide to visit this paradise will get acquainted with many exotic birds, and the most amazing thing is that no vehicle is needed for an excursion to this island. You can get there on foot along the shallow lagoon.

Reviews

If we talk about paradise on earth, then, of course, this is the Dominican Republic (Boca Chica). Reviews from tourists who were fortunate enough to visit the beaches of this resort are extremely positive. Those who rested in the hotels of this island were simply delighted with the excellent service and the green territory surrounded by the hotel complexes.



Of course, for an ideal, calm and relaxing holiday, you should go to the Caribbean coast, to the resort with the name Dominican Republic (Boca Chica). Photos taken in his lagoon amaze the imagination with its colorfulness.
