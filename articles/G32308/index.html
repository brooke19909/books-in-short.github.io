Antibes, France: photos and descriptions, attractions, recreation, reviews of tourists

In France, Antibes is one of the most popular cities located on the Cote d'Azur. It is famous for its luxurious parties and yachts, and it also has very interesting sights and places for entertainment for every taste: both for lovers of parties and for families.

Short description of the city

In France, Antibes is the second largest city on the Cote d'Azur, which is only inferior to Nice. It is located between Nice and Cannes. At the height of the holiday season, the population of this city increases several times. But tourists visit it at any time of the year.

Antibes is located in that part of the Cote d'Azur, which has the most favorable climate in all of France. The presence of the sea and mountains creates all suitable conditions for sailing and relaxing on the beach. The prevailing weather in this city is dry and warm, which is especially conducive to relaxation.

Walks in the old district of the city

A leisurely walk is a great opportunity to get to know all the sights as best as possible. An important feature of the cities of France is the presence of special ancient areas that are distinguished by their special architecture. In Antibes, such an area is located in a very beautiful place, known as the Bay of Angels.

Cobbled, winding streets are perfect for leisurely walks, allowing you to feel the special French atmosphere. You can diversify your route by visiting boutiques, small restaurants with local delicacies located on these streets. In France, Antibes has a typical atmosphere and a view of an old Mediterranean town.

In addition to the opportunity to enjoy the magnificent architecture, you have the chance to purchase fresh fruits and vegetables and other products traditional for Provence. Also, getting by bus to this part of the city, you can admire the exceptional panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Beaches Cap d'Antibes and Juan-les-Pins

Of course, all vacationers like to enjoy the sunshine while lying in a deck chair. In France, the beaches of Antibes are considered one of the most popular. On the coast of Golf Juan you can see a beautiful cape. It is a kind of border between Antibes and Cannes. Beach lovers can enjoy the view of pine groves.

Tourists can easily rent a room in one of the coastal hotels. In total, there are 13 beaches in the Antibes area. Their distinguishing feature is that the pebble part goes into the sand. Most of them are equipped with showers and toilets for the convenience of visitors. It is not surprising that beaches are one of the most popular tourist destinations in Antibes.

Picasso Museum

One of the sights of France (Antibes in particular) is the museum of the famous artist Picasso. It is located in the stone castle of the Chateau Grimaldi. In the 13th and 14th centuries the castle was inhabited and served as a defensive tower. The history of this museum is interesting.

While in France, in Antibes, Pablo Picasso was looking for a workshop that would be spacious and bright. And he was kindly invited to organize it in the castle of Grimaldi. It was there that the famous masterpiece of the artist "Night fishing in Antibes" appeared. This picture was the starting point in the creation of the collection, which was gradually replenished with the works of other artists. Now the Picasso Museum, located in Antibes, is one of the largest and most famous cultural centers on the Mediterranean coast.

Fort Carre

Another attraction of Antibes (France) is Fort Carr√©. It is also called the symbol of the city. These are ancient fortifications built in the form of a four-pointed star. The fort is located on the Saint-Roche Peninsula at an altitude of 26 meters above sea level.

It was built by order of King Henry the Second in the second half of the 16th century. Around Fort Carre there are parklands with flora and fauna typical of the Mediterranean coast. Since the fort is the current military base of France, fortifications can only be admired from the outside.

Cape Antibes

It is the main attraction of the Mediterranean landscape. On the cape is the lighthouse Group and a beautiful old chapel, which serves as a museum. It presents very interesting relics of Russian origin that have been brought there since the Crimean War. Also, vacationers need to take a walk in the Thuret garden.

The year of foundation of the garden is 1857, its creator is the famous French botanist and researcher Gustav Thuret. There you can find a large number of plants - more than 3000 species. In addition to the listed attractions, vacationers can enjoy the picturesque scenery.

Bastion of Saint Andre and the chapel of San Bernardini

Attraction of Antibes is another fortification - the bastion of Saint-Andre, built in 1698. In 1928, the bastion was converted into an archaeological museum. Its first exposition represented a small number of finds that belonged to the Halo-Roman era. The museum was officially opened in 1963, and now visitors will be able to admire exhibits whose age is more than 2.5 thousand years.

Many cities in France have ancient chapels. And Antibes is no exception: the San Bernardini chapel, built in the 16th century, is one of the most popular attractions in this city. It is built in neo-Gothic style and is one of the most beautiful French chapels. Inside, it is decorated with paintings, stucco molding and stained-glass windows.

Family Vacation Options

Not only lovers of fun parties and luxury yachts come to this city. Many go with their families. In France, a vacation in Antibes is a great opportunity to enjoy the wonderful Mediterranean climate. In this city there are many wonderful places where you can go with the whole family.

One of the most popular places is Marineland Park. Here you can find a large aquarium with exotic marine inhabitants. In addition to acquaintance with the inhabitants of the underwater world, you can go to a pet farm, which has attractions for children of any age.

If you enjoy water activities, in this park you will find a water park with pools and slides. There is also a golf club where everyone can learn this game. In this park, there are show programs in which dolphins and seals perform. And little children can swim in the same pool with dolphins.

If you are a lover of beautiful landscapes, you should definitely visit Parc Exflora. Here you will find a beautiful landscape, charming glades with a green lawn, large flower beds and an artificial lake. A great advantage for families is the specially equipped picnic area.

Fans of outdoor activities should visit Soccer Park - a sports complex dedicated to one of the most popular games in the world - football. Fans of this game will be able to find a sports bar and fully equipped areas for a football game there. Due to the fact that there are open and closed areas, the complex is open to visitors all year round.

In Antibes, you can have a great time in the amusement park Lockout - Live Escape Game. It focuses on holidays with older children. Visitors are offered a wide selection of quest rooms for every taste. Antibes Land is a park with classic attractions. Visitors are waiting for roller coasters, carousels and pools.

Children will be interested in visiting an unusual puppet theater. It is unusual in that domestic animals perform there. These performances will be interesting to children of any age.

Antibes, France: tourist reviews

Vacationers note that this city is one of the best on the Cote d'Azur. In this part, the most favorable Mediterranean climate. Tourists say that in Antibes there is an opportunity to find entertainment for every taste and for any age.

But in the midst of the holiday season, one should be prepared for the fact that there are a large number of vacationers in the city, which means that reservations should be made in advance. You can stay in luxury hotels as well as in budget hotels.

In Antibes you can find expensive restaurants with delicacies or affordable eateries. In addition to the beaches, this city is famous for nightclubs - this is where wealthy young people come. Antibes is loyal to tourists, which is the advantage of this resort. Maybe he is not so popular worldwide as Nice or Cannes, but there are no less options for a pleasant stay. A favorable climate, beautiful nature, attractions and amusement parks are all important components of a wonderful holiday.
