Phone Explay Power Bank

At the beginning of last year, Explay introduced its new creation to the world - the Power Bank mobile phone. This device is the prototype of the popular communicator Philips Xenium X130, which is known for its ability to charge other devices, such as tablets or e-books.

Explay Power Bank phone is distinguished by its strict design, good functionality and endurance. Such a communicator is suitable for lovers of lengthy conversations on a mobile device, since the battery can withstand up to 13 hours of talk time and up to 620 on hold without recharging. Support for two SIM cards allows you to combine the tariffs of services of mobile operators to use the most profitable ones, provides the ability to separate work calls from personal ones, and also opens up broad prospects for business people.

Supply

The Explay Power Bank mobile phone is packed in a light brown cardboard box, which is made from recycled materials. It has the manufacturer‚Äôs logo, the name of the phone, its technical characteristics, a graphic image of the device and the location of the company.

Opening the packaging box, you will see the Explay Power Bank mobile phone and its accompanying original equipment. It includes a charger, a stereo headset, a USB cable and documentation.

Explay Power Bank: screen quality reviews

Mobile phone manufacturers offer two colors - black and silver. An opaque film is pasted on the screen with information that the mobile device supports two SIM cards. Before starting work, this film must be peeled off, because it completely obscures the display.



Under protection is a fairly high-quality display with good color reproduction, optimal resolution and viewing angles. The image is a little distorted when you tilt the phone towards you, but this is a minor drawback.

The case of the Explay Power Bank mobile phone is made of matte plastic, it is much more practical than a glossy coating, fingerprints and invisible dirt are not visible on it. The device is framed by a silver frame located around the entire perimeter of the front part.

Compared with previous models of push-button telephones, the screen has slightly decreased, but thanks to this, the communicator is very convenient to hold in your hand. The buttons of the device are large enough, with their help it is comfortable to type messages and numbers. All keys are evenly lit by blue LEDs. The microphone hole is located on the left side under key 1.

Control

The interface of the Explay Power Bank mobile phone is more like a smartphone model. To unlock, hold the center button for a few seconds until a green bar appears on the screen. The left side of the center key allows you to quickly get to the settings for volume, display brightness, bluetooth, background image, alarm clock, camera and radio. Functional tables move in a circle, which means that after the last table you will return back to the first. You can create your own list of files for quick access to them.

Opinion

Speaking generally about Explay Power Bank, user reviews indicate that the main advantages of the mobile phone are its convenient keyboard, security mode, 3 desktops, mobile charging and a good battery.



When buying a communicator, pay attention to the complete set of the device, the adapter is especially important for charging and connecting to a computer. As for the Explay company itself, it was created in 2005 and since then has been working in the direction of sales, as well as warranty service of electronic devices. The brand produces mobile phones, tablet computers, portable speakers, flash drives, stereo headphones, voice recorders, video cameras, digital photo frames, e-books, MP3 players, GPS navigators.
