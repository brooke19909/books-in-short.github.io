Djerba, Cedrian. Hotel Cedriana 3 * (Tunisia / Djerba Island): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is one of the leaders in the tourism market of North Africa. Pleasant climate, warm Mediterranean Sea, large flat beaches, rich spa - these are good reasons to want to spend a vacation in Tunisia. In addition, there is such a diverse cultural heritage, before which even Egypt and many European countries take off their hats. The fact is that a piece of the Earth, which is now trampled upon by the inhabitants of Tunisia, has always been tried by anyone to conquer, and many even succeeded. So, in ancient centuries, the city of Carthage flourished, which was founded by the Phoenicians. Then it was destroyed by the Romans. Tunisia has long been under French rule - until then, when the Arabs did not take up public administration. Naturally, each conquest left a significant imprint on the culture of the country. And in our time, almost every “imprint” has become a popular tourist attraction. The most notorious tourist attraction of Tunisia is the ruins of the ancient city of Carthage, and in El Jem there is an ancient amphitheater, which in size and grandeur is not inferior to the Roman Colosseum. Other interesting objects, such as the Saint-Vincent-de-Paul Cathedral, the Hammamet History Museum, the Gazi Mustafa Fortress, La Goulet, etc., also annually receive their portion of international tourists.

For Russian tourists, vacation in Tunisia is not overshadowed by almost any formalities. Citizens of the Russian Federation do not need to go through the visa pre-registration procedure to enter Tunisia, and even upon arrival at the airport a visa stamp is put on for free.





Tunisia is a wonderful resort, but in a classic beach holiday it can hardly compete with industry leaders - Egypt and Turkey. But there is one thing in Tunisia that makes it especially attractive for tourists, or rather, for tourists - thalassotherapy. This is a set of procedures for the treatment of various diseases and cosmetic defects. The main component and the main active ingredient of thalassotherapy is sea mud. In qualified clinics, there are programs for the successful treatment of serious illnesses, the consequences of injuries and operations with sea mud, but tourists mainly try thalasso to improve the appearance, tightening or expelling of hated cellulite.

Tunis Hotels

Although Tunisia is focused on the European tourist audience, the standards of service in this country are far from ideal. The approach to serving tourists in many institutions is quite archaic, and the hotel sector is developing randomly. On the one hand, several first-class prestigious hotels have already been rebuilt in the country, and even for economical tourists several worthy options have appeared, but most hotels in Tunisia are still quite old and dull.

When choosing a hotel in Tunisia, you should pay attention to the region, listen to the opinion of the manager in a travel agency, if he had to go on an advertising tour, and to the reviews of tourists. But the star classification of the hotel sector in Tunisia is a controversial criterion. Sometimes a three-star hotel may not be inferior in all respects to a five-star hotel.





Club Cedriana Djerba 3 * (Tunisia, Djerba)

The hotel is located on the north-east coast of the island of Djerba, near the city of Midoun. Djerba - one of the most southern resorts in Tunisia, is especially famous for first-class thalassotherapy. From the island it is convenient to go on excursions in the southern and central regions, especially on a trip to the Sahara or to Tatauin Park - the place of ancient settlements where many scenes from Star Wars were filmed.

The hotel has 175 rooms. The complex consists of a heap of white buildings in a traditional style. The hotel has been operating since 1992; major repairs were made in 2015.





The official check-in time is 2:00 p.m. On the day of eviction, the number must be handed in before 12:00.

Pets are not allowed.

The hotel does not have rooms for disabled guests.

Until recently, the hotel was called Caribbean World 3 * +.

In Tunisia, many hotels operate all year, including the Cedrian Djerba. Accommodation prices, depending on the season, differ quite dramatically. According to the spring-summer 2017 concept, the price for a double room is $ 260 / day for two, including ALL. In low periods, the hotel’s food system is simplified, pool bars and a beach bar do not work. Guests mainly travel to Tunisia, visit the indoor pool and, to the full, use the various services of the spa complex and wellness center.

Hotel location

Hotel "Djerba Sedriana" 3 * is located in 300. from its own large sandy beach, in the north-east of the island.

Djerba Zarzis Regional Airport is just 21 km away. This airport accepts European and Russian charters, as well as regional flights.

The nearest town, Midoun, is 5 km away. This administrative center is a rather colorful city with a market square, many oriental shops and souvenir shops.





To the capital of the island - this is the city of Houmt Souk - only 23 km. In the capital, a popular attraction is the fortress of Bordj el Kebir, a majestic fort of the 16th century, built for defense against the Spaniards. Also, tourists willingly visit the beautiful mosques of Jama'a-el-Gorba and Ash Sheikh. From this hotel (Tunisia, Hotel "Cedriana Djerba" 3 *) you can go on a sightseeing tour, bought from a tour operator, or on your own, using public transport. There is a bus stop 400 meters from the hotel. Also, the hotel itself has a car rental office and free car parking for the convenience of especially dynamic tourists.

Hotel Club Cedriana Djerba 3 * (Tunisia, Djerba) is a very multifunctional complex. Each tourist can feel comfortable and appropriate in it. Entertainment and sports

Sports games, animations are popular mainly among Europeans and youth, and, of course, children. Among other categories of travelers, sporting events in hotels are not at all in favor.

However, for those who are not enthusiastic about visiting gyms and group aerobics classes, the Cedriana Djerba has the opportunity to combine entertainment and outdoor games: minigolf, tennis court, beach volleyball, and, of course, everyone's favorite water slides.





Among other free sports activities at the hotel there is a gym, water aerobics, basketball. For an additional fee, you can rent a bicycle, catamaran, canoe, as well as diving, archery, bowling and billiards.

Beach

The hotel "Djerba Sedriana" 3 * has its own sandy beach 300 meters from the hotel. There is a road between the hotel and the beach. A shuttle service to the beach is not provided.

The beach at the hotel is simply huge, very well equipped. The beach is cleaned regularly. Stationary umbrellas and sun loungers are available to hotel guests for free. Beach towels are issued free of charge, but on deposit. There is a beach bar. For guests with all inclusive bracelets, local soft drinks and alcoholic drinks, as well as snacks and snacks in the bar, are absolutely free.





The entrance to the sea is gentle, the sea is calm, well suited for bathing children.

Tunisia offers many water sports. Hotel "Cedriana Djerba" 3 * in this regard is very successful. Many types are absolutely free: windsurfing, rental of kayaks and surfboards, beach volleyball.
