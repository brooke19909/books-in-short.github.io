The core of the book

First you need to prepare the inner sheets of paper. This will help the printer. In Microsoft Word, set new page options. The first thing you need is to change the page margins for the correct formation and readability of the text. Fields must be:

  • top 1.5 cm;
  • lower 1 cm;
  • inside 2.5 cm;
  • outside 1.5 cm.

The page type needs to be set to ‚Äúbook‚Äù. Paper size A5. After the text, broken into book pages, you need to number. Go to the "Insert" menu, select the "page numbers" field (at the bottom of the page). Only when you make the correct arrangement, you can begin to typeset. This is necessary so that the text does not change from changing the position of the page. Lines may have jumped up - incorrect transfer of a sentence to another line. It is important to put everything in order so that reading is comfortable. Printing can be done by helping the program navigate the text. We bend ready-made sheets and compose brochures. Further, everything is worth stitching to connect the heart of the book together.

The way to smooth the surface

To get a flat surface, the stitched sheets are placed under the press. For this, you can use something heavy. When pressed with a press, the butt is not touched. We cut the end of the book with a hacksaw. Six vertical incisions along the entire end. The depth should be such that it affects all sheets of paper. After the end you need to cover with PVA glue. On a fresh coating with glue we attach the fabric so that the edges protrude by 5 cm. Calico fabric is the best option. The core must be allowed to dry, after which we sew each sheet to the fabric with threads. The firmware goes through the cuts. We cover everything again with glue on top of the threads, preferably not liquid.

Cover

To create a cover you need to find a very thick cardboard. Two rectangles are cut out of the cardboard. Their dimensions should exceed the page above and below by 4 millimeters. The third rectangle is created from a thinner cardboard. In length it should be the same as two large rectangles, and in width - as the thickness of the end of the pages.

Then we cut out two pieces of fabric. The first piece should be the same as for gluing the sheets of a book. The second is as tall as a narrow rectangle - and wide - 6 cm larger than it. We put the cover so that two large rectangles are on the sides, and a small part is in the middle. The main thing is that there should be a gap between the parts. We glue the fabric on a small part, so that it evenly captures the gaps and edges of large parts. On the other hand, glue the second piece of fabric so that the tails remain. We spread these tails in the middle and attach to the wrong side. Then comes the freedom of creativity. Front cover decoration is to your liking. It can be painted with paints or pasted over with a colored wrapper. The bends of the wrapper should be on the inside about 2 cm from all sides. And with the last touch we glue the cover to the core.

How to make a felt book

Children's books are sewn in the same pattern. The first thing you need is to attach a gasket to the front rag. Cut out all the details of the book and its elements. On the page of felt, the parts are fixed with pins in their location. They sew it with a sewing machine or sew it on their own with a needle. The two sides of the cover must be sewn together, and the tails should be hidden in the inside. You can use items with Velcro, but in such cases, so as not to spoil the material of the book, the soft part of the Velcro needs to be sewn to the base of the book. There are three types of seams that can be used to create such a book.

Types of seams:

  • ‚ÄúForward with a needle‚Äù - this is how the details that can be removed are stitched.
  • ‚ÄúBehind the needle‚Äù - parts that are not detached are sewn.
  • ‚ÄúLooped‚Äù - in this way all pages are flashed on the sides. This design very harmoniously decorates the book.

A binding for a book made of felt can be chosen in a variety of ways: loops, on the grommet, tape or pieces of felt. You can use the "curly" scissors, the work done by them will be neat and beautiful. For the softness of the book, you can lay a synthetic winterizer between the pages, this method will be advantageous to use for very young children. You can also lay canvas fabric between the pages. In this case, the book will be much tougher than the syntepon.

Velcro can be sewn onto the sealing fabric for convenience:

  1. You need to put a dense cloth on the back of the book.
  2. Sew Velcro to the back.
  3. Be sure to trim the excess fabric for accuracy.
  4. Sew on the details.
