LCD "Manor" on Lansky: description, reviews and photos

"Manor" on Lansky is a prestigious multi-family mansion in the green zone of St. Petersburg. Architects tried to combine innovative building technologies with the Stalinist Empire. This is an urban infrastructure balanced by water surface and surrounding parks.





Location

Lanskaya Manor has a unique location: a residential complex is located at the intersection of Lansky highway and Engels. This area is surrounded by parks, gardens, pedestrian areas. Nearby is the historical center, the WHSD and the Ring Road. To the subway (stations "Lesnaya", "Pionerskaya", "Black River", "Specific") on foot - 20-27 minutes.

The infrastructure is extremely developed. There are no problems with schools, kindergartens, sports facilities, medical facilities. Within walking distance of commercial, entertainment, shopping facilities. From the complex there is a road to Lanskoy Garden.

Earlier here the estates of the nobility really were located. After the revolution, a bakery was built, the dilapidated building of which was recently demolished. Part of the LCD looks like galleries, remotely resembling a destroyed bakery.





Architectural features

Two blocks of different storeys for 207 apartments - this is how the "Manor" looks like on Lansky. The photo of the complex impresses with its fashionable decoration and design, embodying the traditions of the 50s in a modern interpretation. The buildings are united by an underground floor, equipped with utility rooms and a parking lot.

The house will be divided into five 7-11-story sections. The first floors will be occupied by commercial structures. The external walls are monolithic, the material of the partitions varies from brick to aerated concrete. The main building from the road will be blocked by a three-story building, which will house life support systems (transformer substation), studios and offices. Between the buildings equip a cozy courtyard, near the park - a playground, recreation areas.

The facade in the Stalinist Empire style is planned to be partially finished with granite (up to the second floor). The plaster is decorated with rusty: grooves imitating the masonry joints will be applied. The architectural and artistic illumination of the walls is designed to revive the LCD at dusk, to set a good mood on long winter evenings. Public areas (foyer, corridors, flights of stairs) are designed according to an individual design project.





Security

"Manor" on Lansky is a safe area controlled by security systems. Engineering infrastructure is maximally thought out for a comfortable stay. They build their own gas boiler room, and the heating will be laid in the floors. Air conditioning is centralized.

The enclosed courtyard is protected from strangers. Video surveillance is provided for both the territory itself and the parking lot. Concierges will work in every front room.

Planning decisions

LCD "Lanskaya estate" - an example of a convenient mansion of the XXI century. Future residents are offered one-, two-, three-, five-room studio apartments of various layouts ranging in size from 32.4 m 2 to 187.6 m 2 . Ceiling height - from 2.73 to 3.03 meters. For sale are apartments of both classic layouts - with separate kitchen areas, and European layouts - with separate private and kitchen-guest areas.

A small number of apartments on the floor (from 2 to 5) does not create a sensation of an anthill. Most of the rooms offer beautiful views of the parks. It is possible to arrange fireplaces on the upper two floors.





Comfort

"Manor" on Lansky is designed for comfortable living. A centralized air conditioning system is created on the basis of ABHM equipment (chiller-fan coil) with sound attenuation and the ability to control the temperature in each apartment. Ventilation valves in the window blocks allow you to regulate the natural flow of air.

Among other pleasant moments:

  • Comfortable elevators of KONE firm.
  • Cozy courtyard without cars with equipped areas for adults and children.
  • Modern fire warning and smoke removal systems.
  • Barrier-free environment.
  • Quality soundproofing.
  • Wheelchairs in each front door.

Window systems

Eco-friendly windows with silencing ventilation valves in the 7-11-storey part of the complex will provide pristine silence. Warm stained glass glazing with an aluminum profile will allow the use of balconies year-round. A number of apartments have high panoramic windows on two walls.

Heating system

Own gas roof boiler room will provide uninterrupted heat supply and hot water supply. Heating is performed according to the collector-perimeter scheme with the piping in the floor. Meters are placed in collector cabinets, which will allow you to pay only for the heat consumed. The heating devices are equipped with temperature regulators, which makes it possible to control the heat by the apartment. A pleasant trifle: heated towel rails will be from stainless steel.

Communications

The Manor on Lansky will be equipped with a computer network with a dedicated Internet line, a digital television system with a package of television channels, and integrated telephony with a separate number. An interphone cable is held for each apartment and an intercom is installed . Tenants can optionally equip a color video intercom.

Underground parking and storage rooms

Spacious warm guarded parking is designed for 82 cars. In the basement for residents there are 60 pantries from 3.68 to 9.84 m 2 for storing bicycles, homework and other things. Access to the pantries and parking is by elevator.
