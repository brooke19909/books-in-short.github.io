Resort Description

Transport access of Marmaris is provided by fixed-route taxis and buses. They regularly run between hotels, shopping and entertainment centers of the village. The city is covered by a network of sea passenger traffic. The coastline of the resort is dotted with moorings, to which motor ships and pleasure boats arrive hourly.





The region has excellent opportunities for deep sea diving. On its territory there are 50 certified diving centers. They are located near the capes of Sary-Mehmet, Kutyuk, Haitly and in the bays of Aksu, Abdi Reis. The most popular place among Russians is the Basa Cave. Its bowels represent an intricate labyrinth, generously decorated with sea elements.

General information about Marmaris Resort Hotel

Consider one of the most popular hotels. Marmaris Resort Hotel occupies the first coastline. It is only two hundred meters from the sea.
