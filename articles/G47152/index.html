Hotel Blue Aegean 4 * (Greece, Crete): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Blue Aegean 4 * is a hotel consisting of eight buildings, each of which has the name of one of the Aegean islands. The hotel is quite popular. Largely due to its rather modest prices. But in general, she has many advantages and interesting features. They should be talked about in a little more detail.





Location

So, there is Blue Aegean 4 * in the resort called Gouves. It is approximately 18 kilometers from Heraklion. That is, in the north-eastern part of Crete. The airport is not far, as is one of the most interesting cities in Greece, so you can say that the location is good. But most importantly, from the hotel to the sea - a stone's throw.

Gouves itself is a rather interesting town. This is where the Institute of Marine Biology, the Mediterranean Institute of Agronomy, as well as many other fairly well-known universities are located. But Gouves is focused not only on science, but also on tourists. Germans, British, Irish, Poles and, of course, our compatriots often come here to rest. After all, it is in Gouves that you can immerse yourself in the traditional atmosphere of Crete. This town has retained its historical identity, which is indicated by cozy narrow streets, ivy entwined with houses and beautiful temples.

In Blue Aegean 4 * guests are provided with services and amenities of various kinds. The hotel is non-smoking, but there are some places where guests can enjoy a cigarette. There are also soundproofed rooms and bridal suites. Facilities for disabled guests are also provided.

Also on the territory there is a conference room with a business center, at which there is an office where you can receive or send a fax, and also copy the documents.
