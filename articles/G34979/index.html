Hotel Filerimos Village 4 * (Greece / Rhodes): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Rhodes is considered one of the most beautiful islands of Greece. He captivates, forcing him to come back here again and again. At the same time, a comfortable climate is far from the only advantage of Rhodes. Its delightful coasts with small coves, the mesmerizing contrast of calm valleys with mountainous terrain have made this place popular.

Rhodes holidaymakers are provided with not only a high level of tourist service, but also a huge variety of opportunities for active and informative recreation. The island is particularly attractive as a place to spend a vacation and the unique landscape with the peculiarity of each of the three seas washing it. The level of services offered is consistently high. In addition, in Rhodes, relatively low, compared with the rest of Greece, prices.

Filerimos Village 4 *, Rhodos, Ialyssos



Built in 1978, this hotel is located nine kilometers from the center of Rhodes at the foot of Mount Filerimos, from which it got its name. It is positioned as a place where families with young children and the elderly can have a good rest. The last major overhaul in Filerimos Village 4 * was carried out in 2010. Furniture and plumbing have been completely updated. The hotel is open from April until the end of October.

Location

Filerimos Village 4 * (Rhodos) is located in the northwest of the island. Its enclosure is surrounded by a stunning orchard. Oranges and other fruits spread an enticing aroma in the air. The hotel is located between the small resort villages of Kremasti and Ialissos, right at the foot of the famous shrine of the mountain. Ten minutes drive to the city center. The proximity of the international airport, which is half an hour away, is another advantage of the hotel.



Near the hotel there is a bus stop, where for two and a half euros you can get to the center of Rhodes. Shuttles depart every fifteen minutes, travel time is a quarter of an hour. The address at which the Hotel Filerimos Village 4 * is located: Rhodes, Ialysos (Trianda).

For several years, Filerimos Village 4 * has been participating in the Fortuna accommodation system. In this case, the ticket price is one and a half times lower.

Territory

Four hectares, on which Filerimos Village 4 * (Rhodes, Greece) is located, are completely surrounded by greenery. Lawns are everywhere, many flowering shrubs. The hotel territory is conceived as a beautiful park, where in between swimming in the sea and excursions you can relax under the shade of olive trees and luxurious palm trees. Tall pines grow everywhere, among which cicadas sing in the evenings, creating an atmosphere of romance.



Guests are offered convenient parking for cars and tourist buses, which must be reserved in advance.

For the convenience of guests throughout the hotel, signs are placed with the numbers of buildings and rooms.

Infrastructure

Filerimos Village 4 * offers its guests concierge services, a lobby, a left-luggage office and a depository safe. The hotel infrastructure also includes an Internet cafe, a games room, a laundry, a currency exchange, an entertainment center, an amphitheater, and dry cleaning. A souvenir kiosk, several small shops, including a jewelry one, are located on the territory.



At the round-the-clock reception you can arrange a transfer to the city or airport terminal. There is also a tour desk and a car, motorbike and bicycle rental service, and you can order a taxi.

Number of rooms

In total, Filerimos Village 4 * has two hundred and three rooms. They are equipped in two- and three-story snow-white cottages with coquettishly bright terracotta roofs. Vacationers are offered rooms in four categories.

Twin Standard, a twenty-square-meter double room, can accommodate two adults and one child. This category is convenient for a small family, which will have a refrigerator and a TV, individually controlled air conditioning.

But lovers of romantic privacy should book a family Bungalow with a bedroom and an open living room. In addition to the standard set of furniture, it has a couch. In front of the bungalow on the green lawn there are sun loungers and a table. All interiors in rooms of this category are designed in calm colors: in beige and light brown tones. The decoration of upholstered furniture enlivens its discreet design with its bright textiles.

Each room has a balcony or terrace with plastic chairs and a coffee table. Sitting outdoors, you can spend hours admiring the beauty of the surrounding landscapes - the Aegean Sea or the mountain slopes.

The combined bathrooms have a shower or bathtub, bidet, wash basin, dryer. Some rooms include a hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers.

Tourists should consider that for the use of air conditioning in Filerimos Village 4 * (Ialyssos / Ixia) you need to pay separately. Everywhere on the floor is ceramic tile.

There are rooms for honeymooners, where free fruit and wine are served daily. Wheelchair users do not have the appropriate amenities.

Service

In all rooms of the Hotel Filerimos Village 4 * (Rhodos, Ialyssos), reviews of which guests mostly leave positive, you can get paid Wi-Fi service, which costs four euros per hour, as well as a hair dryer. An iron and an ironing board can be requested from the receptionist.



When staying in a room with a child under the age of ten, an extra bed is provided free of charge.

The Hotel Filerimos Village 4 * (reviews of those who came here confirm this) pets are not allowed, even for an additional fee.

Hotel staff clean six times a week. Soaps, shampoos and other cosmetic accessories are replaced at the same frequency. The hotel is positioned as non-smoking. For violation is subject to a fine. This restriction also applies to the territory.

Nutrition

Hotel Filerimos Village 4 * (Rhodos, Ialyssos) offers the following food concepts: continental breakfast, half board and all inclusive. Type of serving - buffet in the main restaurant. The menu has many dishes from European and Mediterranean cuisines, since the main contingent coming to this hotel are Germans, Austrians and Spaniards. Those who choose the half-board option receive drinks only during breakfast.



The main restaurant operates according to the following schedule: breakfast from seven in the morning until half past nine, lunch from twelve to two. Dinner is served at eighteen thirty, and ends at nine in the evening. The restaurant has a capacity of three hundred and eighty seats.

According to the all-inclusive concept, until eleven o'clock in the evening you can get soft or alcoholic drinks only from a local producer: coffee, tea, beer, wine. At each approach to the bar, only two types are issued. Lunch box must be ordered in advance, about a day, until seven in the evening.

The hotel has three bars: the main one, which is open until one in the morning, where there is always a large selection of wines, lemonade and freshly squeezed juices, as well as on the beach and pool.

Sea and beach



The hotel is located on the shores of the Aegean Sea. Six hundred meters from the main building there is a public pebble beach. There are never many people on it. All that is needed for a comfortable bathing and tanning: sunbeds, umbrellas, trestle beds and mattresses - are issued for a fee. The beach has toilets and a shower with fresh water.

Due to the fact that the entrance to the water is pebble, the presence of special shoes is simply necessary.

Those who prefer exclusively sandy beaches, it is better to go to the east coast for swimming.

Conditions for children

Since the Filerimos Village 4 * hotel is positioned as convenient for families with children, you can often hear childish voices here. Upon request, parents receive a cot in the room or a high chair for the baby in the restaurant. There is an opportunity to order a children's menu.

There is a shallow pool for the kids. There is also animation and a playground.

The main advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the airport, which is especially true for children who have poor road tolerance. In just twenty minutes by shuttle you can reach the hotel.

Entertainment and sports

You can play futsal and volleyball at the beach. Convenient tennis courts and a basketball court can be used directly on the territory of the Filerimos Village 4 * hotel (Rhodes), reviews of which are mostly positive. By renting a bike, you can tour all the surroundings. On the beach, water sports are well represented: diving, windsurfing, etc.

In order to swim or just refresh yourself in the cool crystal clear water, it is not at all necessary to go to the shore of the Aegean Sea. On the territory of Filerimos Village there are two spacious and clean pools for adults and one more - specially equipped for younger guests.

Near the Hotel Filerimos Village 4 * (Rhodos, Ialyssos), reviews of which indicate that you can relax, there are two famous resorts - Ixia and Ialissos. They are famous for excellent surfing conditions. Here are the best waves and wind on the whole island.

Excursions

The hotel is positioned by many as a convenient launching pad for exploring the sights of this large island. The capital of Rhodes is only eight kilometers away. At the same time, a rich excursion program can be ordered either at the hotel itself or independently. Many come to Filerimos Village 4 * (Rhodos, Ialyssos), reviews of which are mostly positive, to combine the study of local attractions with recreation on the Aegean Sea.

In just fifteen minutes, you can take a bus to the island's capital, a city founded in the fifth century BC, to stroll along its cobbled streets and see the Palace of the Grand Master, as well as the ruins of the ancient Acropolis.

Many do not miss the opportunity to visit the Valley of the Butterflies, and archeology lovers seek to see the famous "Pompeii of Rhodes" and the ancient Ialysos with its Byzantine monasteries. Here, near the hotel, is the temple of the goddess Athena. And after the tour you can have a great lunch in one of the traditional taverns.
