Top brands of TVs: an overview

What could be better than relaxing after a hard day in a comfortable armchair with a cup of tea or coffee in front of the TV? However, is it possible to achieve maximum effect without having high-quality equipment? Many will answer this question in the negative. Color reproduction, stereo sound, screen size, remote control - all this affects the feeling while watching your favorite television channels. Given that this kind of equipment costs a lot of money, everyone wants to buy a device that will work without breakdowns for a long time. And what acts as a guarantor? Of course, a brand. It's no secret that if you choose the right brand of TV, it will serve faithfully for many years.

Some reputable publications annually compile brand popularity ratings. It takes into account the conformity of price and quality, the level of reliability, the duration of operation, functionality and other criteria. It is worth noting that there are already well-established leaders. Among them are brands such as Samsung, Sony, Philips, Panasonic and others.

Let's look at the best brands of TVs. The article will provide information on their advantages, disadvantages. In it you can familiarize yourself with the technologies used by the manufacturer, new products, as well as find out which brands are trusted by the domestic buyer and why. The rating is based on reviews and consumer demand.

What brand of TV to choose?

Currently, the range of household appliances in Russia is truly huge. The stores sell goods of both foreign and domestic brands. However, such diversity is both an advantage and a disadvantage, since it is easy for an unknowing person to get confused. All modern TV models are designed for different consumers, among them there are both budget devices and those that belong to the premium class. The main difference between them is the price. It should be noted right away that its range is quite large. But not only this is the difference. Service life, innovative technologies, reliability, signal quality, functionality - and this is only a small part of the criteria that are worth paying attention to. In order to simplify your choice, you can simply study the reputation of the brand (manufacturer). Thanks to this information, it will be possible to see a real picture of the quality of the devices: the number of factory defects, breakdowns, etc. It is also worth paying attention to the availability of service centers and spare parts, since many have problems with the repair of equipment of certain brands. Note that world-famous brands are trying to take into account such nuances. That is why their products are in high demand.

So, what brands of TVs are popular in Russia? These are Samsung, Sony, LG, Philips, Panasonic, Toshiba.



Samsung

The Korean manufacturer has long been a leader in the global market. Six years ago, Samsung launched production in Russia. This decision allowed the sale of digital technology for relatively little money. What does this brand offer the buyer? Thin LCD TV with a wide range of functions. For example, the Samsung UE32J5500AW model . It is equipped with LED backlight. The screen size is 32 ".

Benefits:

  • Beautiful design;
  • good level of brightness and contrast;
  • multifunctionality;
  • convenient Smart TV interface;
  • the availability of service centers in almost every Russian city.

As for the shortcomings, most buyers did not find them at all. The only thing that could overshadow the viewing of your favorite television channels is too bright colors in some models. However, at the present moment, such a flaw has been practically eliminated.



Sony

Japanese brands of TVs have always been famous for quality. Sony is no exception. This company has been in high demand for a long time. Now the manufacturer offers two new products: the Bravia TV (its own development equipped with the Sony Entertainment Network interface) and the NSZ-GS7 set-top box (they used the Google TV platform to create them). The devices demonstrate not only broadcast in online mode, but also pre-recorded programs. Network memory is large enough.

Modern models have quick access to applications such as Skype, Youtube, VK, Facebook.

Benefits:

  • build quality;
  • original design;
  • wide range of functions.

The disadvantages include only the high cost. It is she who largely affects consumer demand.



LG

The Korean brand of LG TV is a great option for those who are not ready to pay a lot of money. Its products are sold at an affordable price, but they are practically not inferior in equipment and quality to more expensive brands. In Russia, LG TVs are sold in a large assortment and are popular. It was this South Korean company that introduced the domestic buyer to all the possibilities of innovative Smart TV technology.

Benefits:

  • interesting design;
  • Direct LED-backlight;
  • excellent image quality in 3D;
  • affordable price;
  • modern content;
  • the ability to display pictures from different sources;
  • Smart Share application;
  • a large number of service centers.

No flaws were found, however, factory defects can occur.



Philips

If you consider inexpensive brands of TVs, then you definitely need to talk about Philips products. It has been sold on the Russian market for a very long time. Thanks to the good equipment, TVs of this brand are selling out quickly. Interesting and useful services, for example, the Miracast Wi-Fi application, are especially popular. Using it, you can transmit the image wirelessly.

Benefits:

  • high image quality;
  • a large number of useful features;
  • low price;
  • direct data transfer (without downloading) from any medium;
  • Ambilight backlight.

If you study the reviews, you can see negative comments. They mainly concern the operation of the menu. Many have noticed that it slows down.



Panasonic

No less interesting brand of digital technology is Panasonic. The Japanese company is once again proving a high level of quality. Televisions have unique equipment called Viera Connect. Thanks to him, a grid of nine frames is displayed on the screen. In the center, the online broadcast mode is displayed, and at the edges is an image from other sources. Setting up the application is very easy. You can also use the online store, which offers a variety of videos, games and applications.

Benefits:

  • reliability;
  • modern design;
  • equipment;
  • average price;
  • excellent image quality.

No obvious flaws were noticed by the consumer.



Toshiba

Continuing to study the best brands of TVs, we will return to Japanese quality again. This time, it is proposed to consider products sold under the Toshiba brand. Devices stood out with modern equipment. The company develops the technologies used independently. Of particular note is the Toshiba Places smart system. It allows you to create multiple accounts, thanks to which you can restrict access to certain functions. This application is ideal for monitoring children, since you can put a ban on some content. The devices are also equipped with quick access to social networks. You can set the necessary settings in your account.

To summarize

Which brand of TV is better, everyone can decide only individually. Of course, there are certain criteria that it is recommended to pay special attention to, but buyers can designate their importance on their own, based on personal preferences. Indeed, at the time of purchase, as a rule, a lot of decides the ratio of price and quality, since not every domestic consumer is able to pay a fortune for a TV, even if it is equipped with the latest technologies. And, as you know, world-famous brands are slightly overpricing their prices.
