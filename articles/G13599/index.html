Dzhubga, Krasnodar Territory. Dzhubga on the map of the Krasnodar Territory

In the summer, many vacationers go to the Black Sea - in the Krasnodar Territory. Rest (Dzhubga - one of the most popular resorts) has recently been organized very well. Small quiet towns of the Black Sea coast are becoming more and more popular with tourists. The village of Dzhubga is located 108 km from Tuapse and is attractive mainly for motorists. The fact is that this is the first settlement by the sea on the highway Krasnodar - Sochi. Many come here just for the weekend. Others do not want to overcome the steep Caucasian mountain serpentines, which begin behind the village, and stop there.

The story of Dzhubga

This small city was founded in 1864 as one of the villages of the Shapsug battalion. Translated from the Adyghe word "dzhubga" means "windy place." Indeed, a refreshing cool breeze often blows in the village. The fact is that the Dzhubgi river valley goes far into the mountains and represents a convenient "path" for air masses.

There is a more romantic version of the emergence of such a name. According to legend, in this place once lived a beauty named Dzhubga, drowned in the river because of unhappy love.





Sea and entertainment

The village of Dzhubga (Krasnodar Territory) is located in a small bay between two mountains. Its beach has a length of 800 km. Sandy sections of the coastline alternate with small pebbles. There are rocky, not too comfortable for swimming places. However, there are not many of them. Descent into the sea on the beach is gentle, there are no pits. Large boulders are also missing.

The entertainment complex Dzhubgi beach offers its guests a standard. You can rent a pedalo or a motorbike, jump with a parachute, etc. The coastal zone is equipped with changing rooms. You can rent a deck chair and an umbrella. The average water temperature in the sea near the village in the summer season is +23 degrees, air - +26 ... +28 degrees.





Private Sector Prices

For a budget holiday p. Dzhubga (Krasnodar Territory) - the place is very good. The private sector is not too expensive. The price of a room in a mini-hotel can range from 300 to 500 rubles, depending on the month and the number of places. Service in the private sector, judging by the reviews of tourists, is at a very good level. The owners of most hotels are polite and helpful. The rooms are comfortable and clean. In the courtyards for guests' relaxation there are benches, gazebos and playgrounds. A car can be parked for free near almost every mini-hotel. Lunches and breakfasts, of course, have to cook yourself. Each private bed & breakfast has a shared kitchen.





Excursions

The village of Dzhubga is interesting not only for its convenient beach and warm sea. In its vicinity there are a lot of attractions worthy of attention. Very popular with tourists are the bays of Golubaya and Inal. Here you can not only have a good rest, but also heal with healing blue mud.

In the vicinity of the village itself there are many waterfalls. Three quarters of the territory adjacent to Dzhubga is occupied by forest. Therefore, the air in the village is very clean. If you wish, you can go to the Gaum Gorge or the Dry Beam. Very popular with vacationers and hiking on Mount Hedgehog. Although most tourists who have already visited Dzhubga are not advised to go here alone. On the mountains in the summer in the vicinity of the village there are a lot of snakes. And they behave aggressively.





Water Park Dzhubgi

This is the main attraction of the village, which you should definitely visit. The park was built in 2009 and is one of the largest in the Tuapse district. You can have a good time here both alone and with friends or family. For a not too big fee in the water park of Dzhubga, tourists will find a huge amount of a variety of water activities. Slides, pools with warm sea water, inflatable boats - all this will allow you to spend time fun and interesting.

Right next to the water park there is a parking for cars for 500 cars. At the bar you can buy cool beer or soda. There are also cafes and pizzerias in the park. The water park is open from ten in the morning until seven in the evening. An adult ticket costs about 1300 rubles, a child - 700 (for the whole day).

City infrastructure

The village of Dzhubga (Krasnodar Territory) accommodates 4 health centers operating year-round, and 126 seasonal camp sites. The first holiday home in this town was opened in 1935. At the same time, the central park was defeated. This is currently a favorite vacation spot for residents of Dzhubga and its guests. Here, by the way, you can see another attraction of the town - the ancient dolmen.

In the village, among other things, there is an ambulance, pharmacy, post office, restaurants, cafes. Vacationers and the local market greatly praise. The structure of the Dzhubginsky district includes the village itself, as well as the adjacent villages of Gorskoye, Moldavanka, Bzhid, Defanovka and the colonel's farm. The airport closest to the town is in Anapa, and the train station is in Tuapse. The distance from Dzhubga to Tuapse is 135 km. Krasnodar is 125 km away. The first seaside village, as already mentioned, on the E115 highway will be Dzhubga. On the map of the Krasnodar Territory (its coastal part), this is clearly visible.





On weekdays, the village is more or less quiet and calm. The weekend is usually very noisy. A huge number of motorists from all over the Krasnodar Territory come to the village to relax after work by the sea on Friday. Like the entire Black Sea coast, the holiday season in Dzhubga lasts from May to October.

How to get to the village

So, the Black Sea resorts that Russia is famous for became your choice for a vacation. Krasnodar Territory (Dzhubga, as well as other towns and villages of the coastal zone) are always welcome guests. Here you will find a warm welcome and a lot of entertainment. But how to get to the same Dzhubga? Let's look into this in more detail.

By train, tourists usually travel to Tuapse Station. Next, you can buy a bus ticket to Dzhubga itself. Having bought a ticket to a boarding house with the same name, you need to go out on the street. Cooperative. If desired, a bus can also be reached from Krasnodar. Motorists usually travel along the same highway.

Of course, since Dzhubga (Krasnodar Territory) is a noisy village, traveling by train, most of the country's tourists still travel further towards Sochi. Mostly motorists still prefer to visit this particular town. Their reviews of Dzhubga are mostly good. Of the negative aspects, only not too clean streets of the town and its beach are noted.

Also, tourists who have already visited Dzhubgu are not advised to order something in the coastal cafes. The dishes here are very expensive and also prepared from not very high-quality products. But, perhaps, in the holiday season of 2015, the administration of the town will nevertheless allocate time and energy to improve the conditions of stay of tourists in this regard. In the rest, the village of Dzhubga (Krasnodar Territory) - a place for a vacation or a weekend is just perfect.
