Slide projector "Firefly": reviews, cartridges, manufacturer

Almost every adult warmly recalls his childhood with slide projectors and filmstrips of famous Soviet cartoons. This magical device has a rich history, so any person can plunge into the world of fairy tales for one evening. The device will immerse the younger and older generation in an atmosphere of magic.

The "Firefly" slide projector can be attributed to a model of engineering art, since advanced technologies were used in its development. The recognized creator of new trends in the book market is Amphora Publishing House. Modern trends in this area have led to the formation of a separate unit. The manufacturer of the Firefly slide projector is Amphora Media, which has concentrated its efforts on the production of children's products. These devices were the first major project of this company.

A filmstrip is a kind of analogue of an e-book, because along with the picture, text is also displayed on the screen. Thanks to this device, the child will quickly learn to read and will be imbued with love for this occupation. The device also develops speech skills in the youngest children. The device fully meets the modern requirements of the market, and also has unique properties, which will be discussed in this article.

How to use?

The device is quite simple to operate, which is confirmed by the positive reviews on the "Firefly" overhead projector. The cartridge must be installed in the projector and the book inserted in the remote control. Then it is enough to direct the slide projector to any surface. Users can independently select the operating mode of the device. Before viewing, you must turn off the light in the room.

Features and Benefits

Children's slide projector "Firefly" not only shows images, but also voices pictures. The device is conveniently controlled using the remote control. The presence of a special lamp allows you to read fairy tales in complete darkness. Users can choose the most convenient mode of operation. The device does not work from an outlet, but from ordinary batteries. The picture is projected onto almost any surface, which facilitates the use of this toy. The projector is absolutely safe for children. The product has appropriate quality certificates and related documentation, therefore it is safe for children.

A filmstrip is a collection of books and cartridges that hold up to 34 slides. Long one fairy tale is no more than 26 minutes. The book includes illustrations and the text itself. All filmstrips for children use illustrations by professional and famous artists, and some tales are voiced by the writers themselves. Some works are voiced by television and radio hosts, as well as honored artists. Filmstrips are recorded at a professional recording studio. All cartridges for the Firefly slide projector contain melodies and songs familiar to everyone since childhood.

Device functionality

The filmoscope is designed to demonstrate illustrations from a standard 35 mm film. If parents still have fairy tales from their childhood, you can also see them. In order for the device to last as long as possible, it is not recommended to use it for more than one hour.

What can an audio slide projector do? The device plays sound cartridges in several modes:

  • independent reading - the projector produces an image, and the text is voiced by parents;
  • voice option - simultaneous playback of music and text with self-switching slides;
  • automatic playback - the projector independently reproduces the sound and displays the image.

On the body there are control buttons and a sound control lever. It is important to know that when you turn on the device, music turns on at maximum volume. The user can direct the lens to the desired location as the body moves in an arc, up and down. Some reviews of the Firefly slide projector indicate that the design moves quite hard, and sometimes even seizes.

If in one place the picture becomes clear, then in another it becomes cloudy. However, children with great pleasure watch cartoons and listen to fairy tales, report user reviews.

The nuances of using
