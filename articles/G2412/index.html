Yalta Beach: photos and reviews. Massandra beach

If you decide to choose Crimea as a vacation spot, then Yalta is exactly what you need. The prices and services here will pleasantly surprise you, and the Yalta beach will leave an unforgettable impression. The unique climate of the city, clean sea air and well-equipped coastal areas will create an excellent mood for the whole vacation.

Yalta Beach

People call this place the city of the sun. And this is not surprising. Most of the days in the year the sun gives its guests its warmth.

The city has many coastal holiday destinations that attract a huge number of tourists. What is famous for Yalta? "Massandra" beach is one of the most popular places on the Black Sea coast. It is rightly called the sea paradise. A big plus of this beach is its division into sectors according to the level of comfort. That is why it is unusually attractive for tourists of different social status. Relaxation is provided by comfortable sun loungers, comfortable places under thatched roofs and special bungalows. Having visited cafes and restaurants on the Embankment, you will taste delicious dishes and relax with pleasant music. That's how Yalta meets vacationers! The Massandra Beach is the best indicator of its hospitality.





On the outskirts of the Embankment lies the "Seaside" beach. Its attractiveness lies in its convenient location and the availability of a huge selection of attractions. The kitchen of cozy eateries will pleasantly surprise you with the prices and quality of dishes, and VIP-zones will provide a relaxing holiday. The entrance to the Primorsky beach is located near the Oreanda Hotel.

Locals most value Dolphin Beach because of its remote location from the city. The beach rests in the village of Livadia. It takes about 15-20 minutes to get here, but the beach infrastructure makes up for this shortcoming. Entrance to the recreation area and car parking are completely free. The schedule of the beach from 8:00 to 20:00. For those who want to eat, there is a cheburechny bar and a dining room. The beach is equipped with trestle beds both by the sea and under a canopy. There are enough changing rooms and a shower room. Near the beach, the Livadia Hotel was built, which provides excellent SPA services. Crimea, Yalta, beaches - all this provides comfort and good mood for vacationers.

The opinion of "experienced"

If you rely on the opinions of local residents and experienced visitors, it is worth visiting the "Dolphin", "Seaside" and "Livadia" beaches of Yalta. Reviews about these recreation areas are the most positive. Tourists explain the delight with the convenience of the location of the beaches. Each of them can be reached by minibus. But, like every city, Yalta has its own network of "secret" paths, which are known by local residents and are happy to share this information with bewildered tourists.
