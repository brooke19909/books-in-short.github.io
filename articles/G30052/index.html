How much electricity does the refrigerator consume? Economical refrigerators

Large electricity bills are forcing people to look for economical household appliances. As for refrigerators, there are many manufacturers who present interesting options. On average, a two-chamber model consumes about 230 kW per year. If we consider refrigerators without a freezer, then this parameter decreases to 150 kW per year.

The power consumption of the compressor, the volume of the chamber, as well as the type of control unit affect the power consumption. It is also important to consider the parameters of the start-up relay and capacitor. Some models have two evaporators installed, and this is also displayed on the energy consumption indicator. In order to understand this issue in more detail, you should separately consider single-chamber devices and refrigerators with freezers.





Single chamber devices

Single chamber economical refrigerators are always produced with one compressor. Its average power is 5 kW. The control units are of a mechanical type. Devices with digital displays are rare on the market. Evaporators are used with one filter.

It is also important to note that capacitors are field-type. However, some manufacturers offer single-contact models. In any case, power consumption remains low. If you select a model for the home, it is important to pay attention to the climatic classes of the device. The temperature regulator for setting the temperature must have at least four divisions.

Two-chamber modifications

Two-chamber models are in great demand. Their power reaches an average of 15 kW. There are many models with digital power supplies on the market. The volume of the chamber is on average 320 liters. With a power of freezing of 6 kg / day. about 240 kW are consumed per year. Some models are made with two evaporators.

If we talk about 20 kW devices, then it is worth noting that electricity consumption is significantly increased. When choosing a refrigerator, only models with one compressor should be preferred. You must also pay attention to the control units and thermostats. Display devices are considered more expensive, and energy bills are serious.
