In memory of Zhukov

The uniqueness of the LCD "Marshal" in Kaluga lies in the fact that the project was originally developed not only as a developer, but also as a socio-historical one. The fact is that the museum of the Marshal of the Soviet Union Georgy Konstantinovich Zhukov will be located on the ground floor of the new complex, in which they plan to carefully preserve the memory of the commander in chief and his great victories.

Externally, the Marshal LCD in Kaluga is a 12-story monolithic building, which is developed by outstanding designers and architects on an individual project.
