Hotel InterContinental Nha Trang (Vietnam, Nha Trang): description with photos, service, reviews of tourists

Thailand is a favorite place for tourists from Russia, because accommodation in local hotels is extremely cheap, and the sea here is warm almost all year round. It is also a very exotic country with a unique culture, so you can come here not only for a beach holiday. In Thailand, you can sample local specialties, look at ancient Buddhist temples and visit local shopping centers. Hotels in Thailand are for every taste and budget. If you want to stay comfortably, then pay attention to the five-star complex InterContinental Nha Trang Hotel. We will tell about what it is in this article.

Location Details

No wonder they say that a hotel with a favorable location is the key to a good rest. This modern complex was built in the heart of the vibrant Nha Trang resort, which is very popular among Russians. InterContinental Nha Trang 5 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is located on the 1st line of the sea, which means that tourists can walk to the beach in just a few minutes. The distance to the coast is only 30 meters. Conveniently, there are many infrastructure facilities near the complex. Not far from it there is a pizzeria and a supermarket, and after a few blocks - a large shopping center. The hotel itself is located on the busy tourist street of Tran Phu. A market is 500 meters away. It is worth noting that this is a fairly lively area, which means that it can be noisy on the street day and night.

The international airport accepting flights from Moscow and other major cities of Russia is 40 km from the hotel. This means that you can get to it after arrival in about 1 hour. When paying for the transfer, the hotel‚Äôs own air-conditioned bus will pick you up from the airport. Also on your own, you can always get by taxi or other public transport.

Basic hotel information

The modern city-wide InterContinental Nha Trang Hotel in Nha Trang was recently opened in 2014. It was built by the hotel chain of the same name, which is considered one of the largest in the world. Its hotels are open in almost all resort cities and megacities. The complex itself consists of one 19-story residential building, which houses 262 comfortable rooms. The hotel can hardly be called a budget, as it is aimed at wealthy tourists. It is noteworthy that in 2015 he was named the best hotel in all of Asia. The hotel accepts tourists with children of all ages. Pets are strictly forbidden, even for a fee. The complex employs qualified staff who speaks excellent English and understands the Russian language.

Upon check-in, a deposit will be charged from all guests. Its amount will depend on the selected apartments and time of stay. Money is returned to the account of tourists after the end of the holiday, if they have not ruined the equipment of their room. Registration of guests starts at 15:00. Tourists must leave their rooms after the end of their stay no later than noon local time.

Number of rooms

The beach complex InterContinental Nha Trang 5 * in Vietnam offers its guests spacious rooms, the interior of which is decorated in a modern style. There are 262 of them in the hotel. When paying for your stay, you can choose one of the following categories:

  • Deluxe Room - the most common option for accommodation. This is a one-room apartment with a bath and an open balcony. Area - 39 m 2 . The living room has a double bed and a sofa bed. You can choose rooms with partial or full sea views. The second option, as a rule, costs a little more.
  • Club Intercontinental - a similar room with the same area, but it has additional equipment. The room has a minibar, and its guests are given bathrobes and slippers. They also have exclusive access to Club Lounge Caf√© and discounts on laundry services.
  • Club Ocean View room - spacious apartment of 43 m2 with an open balcony with amazing sea views.

Apartment equipment

All rooms of the InterContinental Nha Trang 5 * hotel are equipped with new furniture and appliances. It is for its safety that the owners of the complex are worried, taking a deposit during their stay. Of course, all living rooms are equipped with beds: single or double. The rooms have sofas, armchairs, wardrobes and cabinets, coffee tables. Wicker chairs are located on the open balcony.

From technology, each room has a plasma TV with a large diagonal, as well as a telephone, hairdryer and a safe for storing money and documents. The cooling at the hotel is central, which means that its strength is controlled not by the guests themselves, but by the employees of the complex. All rooms have free Wi-Fi. Towels, bathrobes and slippers are provided upon arrival.

Housekeeping is performed daily by qualified maids. They also take out the trash and change towels with bedding. Every day, the hotel employees bring 2 bottles of drinking water to the rooms.

Infrastructure Details

Since the InterContinental Nha Trang hotel is located in the central part of the city, it does not have its own huge territory. All infrastructure is concentrated on the first floors of a high-rise residential building. The reception is located in the main hall, where several administrators work around the clock. They not only settle and evict guests, solving their urgent problems, but also exchange currency, and also help to buy a tour or arrange car rental.

On the ground floor there is a gift shop and an ATM, as well as special smoking areas. At the front desk, you can leave your luggage in the room designed to store it. A laundry service, hairdresser and internet cafe are also on site. Car parking is available near the residential building. Upon request, the administrator can call you a taxi that takes you to the center of Nha Trang.

For business people and businessmen, the hotel has a conference room equipped with modern computers and projectors.

Catering

When paying for their stay at InterContinental Nha Trang, all guests are invited to choose one of three food concepts. Only breakfast will be the cheapest. You can also pay half or full board. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are usually served in the main restaurant. Morning meals are served in buffet style. Day and evening, all visitors are served on a fixed menu, which mainly includes Thai and international dishes. A special vegetarian menu is provided free of charge.

If you want to have a drink and a snack during the day, you can visit the Cookbook Cafe, which prepares seafood and grilled meat. Refreshing drinks are served in the hotel bars. They are located in the lobby by the pool.

Entertainment

The main orientation of the InterContinental Nha Trang hotel is a beach holiday, which is why it was built right on the seashore. The complex also has an outdoor freshwater pool. The beach is about 60 meters away. It is public, but admission is free for all guests, and they can also use sun loungers and sun umbrellas for free. It is separated from the residential building by a road.

At the hotel you can go to the gym, spa or visit the library. But most of the books in it are presented in English. At the spa, most services are available for a fee. For example, the famous Thai massage is not included in the price.

Accommodation with small children

It has already been said that the hotel accepts guests with kids of any age. For infants under 2 years, a cradle is available on request. High chairs are always at the main restaurant. In addition, for a fee you can call a nanny in your room around the clock. But her services will not be included in the price.

There is no children's animation at the hotel, but a mini club is open daily. For the safe swimming of the kids, the complex has a separate shallow pool.

Positive feedback on InterContinental Nha Trang

This hotel traditionally occupies high places in the ranking of the best hotels in Nha Trang. It provides first-class service to its guests. Therefore, they, as a rule, are happy with the rest at the hotel. His guests actively praise him in their reviews, calling them an excellent option for accommodation in the central part of Nha Trang.

What did guests like the most? In their opinion, this hotel has the following advantages:

  • Stylish hotel rooms that the maids really care about. They are carefully cleaned every day.
  • Beautiful views, so tourists recommend taking those rooms that directly overlook the ocean.
  • Friendly staff who are fluent in Russian and English. Employees always meet the guests and help them solve their problems.
  • And although the beach near the hotel is urban, the staff carefully monitors its cleanliness. In addition, there is always a concierge by the road, which helps guests cross through it.
  • A varied diet that includes a lot of exotic Bulls Thai food and fresh fruit.

Criticism of the complex

But sometimes reviews about InterContinental Nha Trang 5 * are negative. Not all tourists liked this complex, although often they find only minor flaws that do not affect the quality of rest. What are the disadvantages found by guests during their stay at this hotel? We list the main ones:

  • Busy traffic near the hotel, so even at night the rooms can be noisy.
  • Not always the hotel has Russian-speaking staff, which complicates the rest of those tourists who do not speak English.
  • Some rooms have stained furniture.
  • The hotel has many Chinese people who make a lot of noise and litter everywhere.

Despite the shortcomings, it can be said that the InterContinental Nha Trang complex is a good place to stay in Nha Trang. The hotel has a favorable location, new rooms and offers high quality service. It can be recommended to all categories of tourists, including people with young children and newlyweds.
