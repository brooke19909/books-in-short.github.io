Knitted napkins: how to decorate a house with your own hands

Do you want to decorate your house somehow original? Knitted napkins can come to the rescue. Making them yourself is a snap. The main thing is to master a few basic elements and find schemes that will please and fit.

Needlework was in fashion before when it was difficult to get beautiful finished products, however, even now hand-made jewelry can amaze guests, because they are absolutely unique! Crocheted napkins, patterns for which are abundantly presented on the net and can even be invented independently, will definitely decorate your home and give it comfort.





No special training is required to learn how to make knitted napkins. Of course, it is better to learn from an experienced person who knows how to knit well, but this skill can be mastered independently, and without any financial costs.

The first thing to do is choose the right yarn and hook. They should be the same thickness, for napkins, it is better to take threads not too thick and fluffy, but durable. Cotton yarn of type "Iris" is well suited.

The second thing to do is learn to read knitting patterns.
At first, this can frighten, but in fact, a small transcript is attached to each scheme with an explanation of how to knit the first few rows. An important note - as a rule, knitting starts from the center of the product and then goes in a circle. Having understood the principle by which knitting patterns are made, you can
