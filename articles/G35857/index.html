Lviv hostels: a brief overview

Lviv is one of the most beautiful cities in Ukraine with incredible architecture, rich culture and interesting people. Therefore, every year thousands of tourists come here from all over the world. To ensure a comfortable, and most importantly - inexpensive, rest in the city, there are many hostels for every taste and budget. The hostels of Lviv are especially popular, which are located in the city center, within walking distance from all the main attractions and interesting places.

Hollywood home hostel





Almost every city has a main square. Lviv is no exception, here it is called Market Square. And just 20 meters away is the Hollywood Home Hostel. But close proximity to the city center is not its main plus. In addition to ordinary rooms for 6 people, there are also double apartments, and they will cost travelers less than a similar hotel room.

Like other Lviv hostels, this one offers free Wi-Fi, but there is something that not every hotel can provide its guests with bike rental. Lviv is one of the few cities in Ukraine where there are bicycle paths. Therefore, traveling to places of interest is very convenient. And in your free time you can spend your leisure time in the library.

In addition to this, the hostel offers standard services such as laundry, safe, tour desk. For the convenience of their visitors, the staff speaks several languages, including Russian. Hostel prices: from 223 p. per person up to 1900 p. for the whole room.

Hostel Just Lviv It





Lviv hostels are striking in their diversity. Fans of vibrant modern design should pay attention to Hostel Just Lviv it. This bright hostel primarily attracts tourists with its fresh and unusual interior, but they also do not forget about the convenience of the guests and offer services such as laundry, ironing, bike rental, tour desk, luggage storage, Internet.

Many tourists are looking for hostels in the center of Lviv, as this provides an opportunity to save on transport. This option just applies to them, because it is located just a few minutes walk from the Market Square. And having walked a little further, in 10 minutes you can be near the Lviv Opera House. Hostel room prices: from 248 p. per person and before 1985 for the room.

Hostel OK

Those who are looking for cheap Lviv hostels with all the necessary services should pay attention to Hostel OK. In addition to the very affordable prices here, they offer a wide variety of services, including ironing and laundry, food delivery, left-luggage office, TV, and an alarm clock service. In addition, guests will be pleased with the design of the hostel, which is made in a modern style.





The hostel staff speaks Russian, so there will be no problems with registration and accommodation. What is nice, animals are allowed to be settled here. All services in this hostel are thought out to the smallest detail, this makes the guests rest as comfortable as possible. For those who travel by car, there is private parking near the hostel, but it is paid. Cost of placement: from 223 p. for one bed and up to 744 p. for a double room.

"Mr. Hostel"

The hostels of Lviv are especially popular, which can offer their guests not only common rooms for 6 or more people, but also double rooms. This greatly saves the budget of travelers, and such rooms differ from apartments in hotels only by the lack of a bathroom. By booking a double room at this hostel, guests have a private bathroom.

Here, guests are offered different types of accommodations at affordable prices. There are laundry facilities, luggage storage. The hostel has a TV, free internet. Those tourists who travel with their families will probably choose this place, because it is allowed to live with children here, and the place is provided to them absolutely free of charge. In addition, you will not have to pay for parking in the area adjacent to the hostel. Price range: from 992 p. per bed and up to 2680 p. for a double room.





Hostel Sun

Various standard services are offered by almost all Lviv hostels, the prices of rooms in which almost everyone can afford. But in order to enjoy a beautiful view of the city, play tennis with friends, fry meat on the terrace, you should pay attention to the Sun hostel. There is also a disco, rental of ski equipment and bicycles, a sauna, a hot tub, a games room and many other activities that will not make guests bored.

In addition, most standard services are provided here, such as: food delivery, free internet, laundry, luggage storage, packed lunches, vending machines, free parking and a conference room. Therefore, those who want to relax as comfortably as possible while not overpaying for apartments should definitely pay attention to this hostel. The cost of accommodation starts at 600 p. per person.





Here are just a few of the many hostels in Lviv. In fact, there are many more, and they all have their own advantages. Therefore, those tourists who plan their trip to Lviv will not have problems finding cheap housing. Here everyone can enjoy the beautiful architecture of the city, aromatic coffee, delicious chocolate, visit amazing places of interest and meet hospitable residents.
