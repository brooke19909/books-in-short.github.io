Screen

The model is equipped with a 5.5-inch screen, working on a completely sensible IPS-matrix. Resolution should be enough even for the most demanding owners - 1920 by 1080 pixels with a density of 403 ppi. This, of course, is not a "retina" from the same Apple, but more than an acceptable option for the budget "Huawei Honor 5X".





Owner reviews note very attractive screen characteristics: viewing angles are practically maximum for this type of matrix, there is no distortion when turning, a good margin of brightness and contrast, as well as the presence of an intelligent oleophobic coating. Camera

The main camera of the smartphone has five lenses, a phase focusing system, a good resolution of 13 megapixels, as well as a good two-tone LED flash. The frontal eye has, of course, a more modest matrix performance (5 megapixels), but for a more or less high-quality selfie it is quite suitable.

Managing the camera is very simple: the interface is familiar, simple and intuitive. The main functionality is somewhat reminiscent of the management of "apple" gadgets. The user has the opportunity to choose from a fairly extensive list of favorite subjects, as well as add some effects while working with cameras "Honor Honor 5X".





Owner reviews about the characteristics of the cameras are written in a positive way. In the daytime, the gadget completely copes with the shooting, and the pictures are quite decent. The only negative is work at night: noises appear, contrast, brightness decreases, and detail is also noticeably reduced. Nevertheless, this moment cannot be called a critical drawback, because the smartphone fulfills its budget category by almost 100%, and you do not need to wait for the capabilities of the NTS or Sony from it.
