Pension "South" (Tuapse): description and reviews

In the Tuapse district of the Krasnodar Territory, on the Black Sea coast, the Yuzhny boarding house is comfortably located. Tuapse is a twenty-minute train ride from the guesthouse. But if you want to relax in silence from the bustle of the city and enjoy the clean sea air, come to the village of Yuzhny, where the health resort is located.

Description

Pension Yuzhny (Tuapse) is located 150 meters from the coast, has its own beach, sprinkled with small pebbles. The descent into the sea is gentle, the water is clean, it warms up quickly. This is a great place to relax with children. Water activities await you at the beach:

  • you have the opportunity to see the city from a bird's eye view, if you decide to ride in tow with a parachute;
  • You can ride on the "banana" - the most popular high-speed attraction;
  • You will also have the opportunity to ride a puck and a jet ski.

The area around the boarding house has its own microclimate due to its unique location in the gorge. Demonstrates a top view of the gorge, in which is located the boarding house "South" (Tuapse), photo below. If you want to get to the neighboring village of Dederkoy, you can take a walk along the wild beach along the sea. The boarding house is located in a large park zone with a length of one kilometer. On the territory of the health resort for guests are equipped with sports grounds for playing volleyball and basketball, own tennis court. At your service are solariums and a summer cafe. Guests can enjoy traditional Russian dishes in the dining room. Not far from the pension is the train station.





Accommodation

To accommodate guests, the Yuzhny boarding house (Tuapse) offers rooms of several categories:

  • A budget with three single beds and amenities on the floor, the cost is from 1000 to 1400 rubles per day, depending on the season.
  • Economy (2nd and 3rd-2nd floors) with three single beds in a two-room block, amenities are in the block, the price for a room is from 1200 to 1800 rubles per day.
  • Economy (5th floor) - the cost ranges from 1300 to 2100 per day, depending on the season.
  • Standard (3rd and 4th floors) for two people in a two-room unit, amenities are in the unit, the cost is from 1300 to 2100 rubles per day.
  • Junior suite (3rd and 4th floors) for two people with a TV and a fridge, a bathroom in the room, the price ranges from 1600 to 2500 rubles per day.
  • Suite (from the 2nd to the 5th floor) for two people with a bedroom, a living room and an entrance hall, the cost is from 3500 to 4000 rubles.

The cost is based on per person. Children under three years of age without a separate seat are free of charge.





Services

For vacationers, the Yuzhny boarding house (Tuapse) provides a number of additional services:

  • Airport shuttle is available for a fee.
  • Guests can arrange tour services.
  • Free Wi-Fi is available.
  • Luggage storage is available.
  • A banquet and conference room is provided.
  • Vacationers are offered a ticket service.
  • Tourists' services include a tennis court and sports grounds.
  • Organization of hiking is possible.
  • Reception is open 24 hours a day.




Tuapse. Pension "South". Positive reviews

Guests of the health resort leave a lot of positive reviews about their holidays:

  • Very beautiful nature around, wonderful clear sea and dense jungle.
  • The reception is friendly staff.
  • The beach is not very busy, you can always find free space.
  • The rooms are in good repair, the rooms are very bright.
  • Each floor has a water cooler.
  • The food in the dining room as a whole is not bad, they are prepared from good products.
  • In the pension there is a vegetarian cafe, they cook very tasty there, and the choice of dishes is good.
  • Nearby are two grocery stores.
  • There is a billiard room.
  • In the evenings, stunning sunsets and views of the city lights.
  • The balconies have comfortable tables.
  • In Tuapse, there is an electric train twice a day, very convenient.
  • There is air conditioning in the lobby.




Negative reviews

Unfortunately, the rest in Tuapse (the Yuzhny boarding house, in particular) caused a number of complaints among some vacationers:

  • There were complaints from guests that at the entrance to the sea there are a lot of sharp stones, it is inconvenient to enter the water.
  • There were complaints of poor Wi-Fi performance.
  • True, it is worth noting that guests who did not like the conditions for living in a boarding house and who found other accommodation for themselves are grateful to the director for the timely return of money.
  • Maids sometimes neglected their duties and did not clean the room every day.
  • For some guests during the 12 days of rest they were never offered to change their linen.
  • Dining room with a very poor interior, the staff is unfriendly, the menu has very few fruits.
  • After storms, the sea is dirty.
  • Many people think that the prices in the pension are overpriced.
