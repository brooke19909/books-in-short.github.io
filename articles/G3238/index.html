Display and communication with the progenitor

A screen with a diagonal of 10.9 cm (which equals 4.3 inches) and a resolution of 540 x 960 pixels - approximately 256 ppi - is equipped with the Samsung Galaxy S4 mini. Under the battery are slots for a SIM card (depending on the model, there may be two) and a memory card.
