DIY glowing trees

The desire to decorate their home or apartment is familiar to all happy owners of real estate, as well as those who have to live in a rented room. Modern manufacturers of various decorative devices for racing are in a hurry to satisfy growing demand, offering novelty after novelty and creating the most unimaginable combinations of styles and technologies.

Some of them perform an exclusively aesthetic function, others combine the attractiveness of appearance with functionality. Luminous trees made of polymer materials and equipped with a large number of small LED bulbs can also be attributed to such useful decorations . This decor is able to perfectly illuminate part of the room.



Naturally, the brightness of such a lamp cannot compete with full-fledged lighting; rather, it is intended to replace lighted candles or a night lamp.

What is LED lighting?

The rapidly growing production of LED lighting devices is steadily displacing traditional incandescent lamps, conquering the market with the help of many advantages:

  • Exceptional durability.
  • High quality lighting (brightness).
  • Security.
  • Economical power consumption.
  • Environmental friendliness.
  • Resistance to humidity and temperature changes.

The essence of the LED (Light Emitting Diode) of luminaires is the emission of light by a semiconductor material under the influence of electron motion. The manufacture of such devices does not require phosphorus, mercury or other hazardous substances. And during operation, burns and electric shocks are excluded , since the lamps do not heat up and consume a negligible amount of electricity (5-9 watts).

How do LED trees look?

The lion's share of the popularity of LED lights can be attributed to their attractive appearance.

Luminous trees, whose branches are dotted with numerous sparkling lights, are decorated with flowers, berries or other decor, evoke only the most pleasant associations with fabulous landscapes and films of the fantasy genre. In addition, the owners of some devices have the opportunity to change the color of the shining lights, turn on the blinking mode, or get a design whose lamps play in several colors.



Depending on the purpose of the lamp and the style of the room or landscape, there are trees with absolutely no decor. Their lamps are not hidden by floral nozzles, and the barrel is made with a minimum of unnecessary elements, which perfectly fits into the interior in hi-tech style.

Types of LED Trees

Thinking about how to decorate your house or territory with a LED-lamp in the form of a tree, it is worth collecting information on devices of this kind in advance.

LED trees can be of various sizes and types:

  • Small ones are designed to be placed on tables and tables.
  • Medium and large are placed on the floor in spacious rooms (large living rooms, offices, trading floors).
  • Street lights are mounted on the site or mounted on living growing trees.

Some of these fixtures can be purchased fully finished, others need to be assembled and connected. For lovers of decorating a house with their own hands, there is an opportunity, in addition to buying a factory product, to make a similar device on their own.

Creating a LED tree at home

With even minimal knowledge in the field of electronics, you can quickly make a luminous tree with your own hands. Although you will have to make some efforts choosing, buying and connecting components, even the most demanding hand-makers will be satisfied with the result.

There are two ways to create such a decoration:

  • Collect luminous trees from prepared elements (a stable base, frame, tape with diodes and decorative flowers).
  • Solder your own electronic design using separate LED lights, wires and insulation.

The first method is much simpler and faster. It does not require the use of skills to work with a soldering iron and electrical circuits, since you just need to connect the prepared nodes of the device in a certain order. Here, creativity is manifested in the choice of colors for the future tree and in a unique combination of ready-made elements.



Choosing the second method and completely creating a luminous tree with your own hands, the master sequentially performs the following steps:

  • Assembles a frame (pipes of different diameters, wire, wooden bases are suitable for this).
  • Solders resistors to the wires of LED lamps and provides isolation of the joints.
  • Attaches LEDs to branches.
  • Leads the wires to the power supply and fastens them using solder method (using phosphoric acid and tin).
  • It fixes the frame of the tree with lamps on the branches on a stable horizontal basis.

Performing such work, you must carefully monitor the observance of the correct polarity (+/-).
