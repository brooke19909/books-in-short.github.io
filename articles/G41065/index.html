Relaxation. Divnomorsk: recreation centers with a description

The urban village Divnomorsk is located near Gelendzhik and is a popular resort center. The attractiveness of the village as a place for perfect relaxation is facilitated by a favorable climate, picturesque nature and the presence of small-pebble beaches in conjunction with a sandy and flat bottom.





Along the coast there are many hotels, pensions and recreation centers. The best place for those who prefer a relaxing family vacation is Divnomorsk. Recreation facilities are located along the coast. Some of them will be discussed in the article.

"Divnoe" - a cozy recreation center in Divnomorsk



The recreation center is located in a vast guarded area, which is under continuous video surveillance. For vacationers built nine two-story brick houses. The number of rooms consists of forty double, triple and quadruple rooms. All have terraces.

Meals are provided in the dining room. The tables are located both indoors and on the outdoor terrace, which offers a magnificent view of the surrounding landscape. Guests are offered three meals a day, which is included in the price. For breakfast, the menu includes delicious and nutritious goat milk and homemade cheeses.

Rest among the picturesque nature

Fans of bright colors of nature should definitely go on vacation to Divnomorsk. The recreation center "Divnoe" literally immersed in the greenery of relic plants. Vacationers have the opportunity to play sports, watch movies on the big screen. There is a billiard room for them. On the territory there is a barbecue for the preparation of barbecue. Wireless Internet access is provided.

Recreation facilities in Divnomorsk - the best option for families with children. On the basis of “Divnoye” a playground is equipped for kids, a special menu is provided in the dining room. Children under the age of three stay free. Travelers at the bottom of the plot will enjoy free parking.

Holiday Village "Vityaz"



Recreation centers in Divnomorsk are entire resort villages. A striking example is the Vityaz complex. The recreation center is located ten kilometers from Gelendzhik. The climate of these places is ideal for children. Designed for the simultaneous residence of three hundred guests, the base is immersed in the greenery of Pitsunda pine. The territory is adjacent to the sea coast, located just ten meters. This is a great option for travelers choosing the city of Divnomorsk for a vacation.

The recreation center "Vityaz" accommodates guests in one-story two-, three- and four-seater houses built of stone. They have the necessary furniture (beds, chairs, wardrobe), TVs and refrigerators. The toilet, shower and washbasin are located in a separate building. Hot water for showering is supplied every other day according to the schedule. Cleaning in the houses is carried out by the vacationers themselves.

Meals for guests - in the dining room. Children under the age of four stay free. For children under fourteen years of age discounts are provided. In the administrative building there is a safe for storing valuables and household equipment provided to vacationers.

Health base "Rainbow"



For youth recreation, the student recreation center "Rainbow", owned by the Don State Technical University, is perfect. A favorite vacation spot for many students and schoolchildren who know that Divnomorsk provides a good vacation. Recreation facilities for this population are also available here. About 8 thousand students, graduate students and university professors from all over Russia visit the Rainbow complex every year.

Accommodation at the base

There are four buildings on the territory (three four-story buildings, one building on seven floors). The number of rooms in four-story buildings includes two-, three- and five-seater, as well as double rooms of superior comfort. All are equipped with shower and toilet. The seven-story building has double, triple and superior rooms with beautiful sea views and balconies.

Separately, we should talk about the four-story building "Daisy". It includes double and triple rooms with balconies. It offers a wonderful view of the sea. Room cleaning in all buildings is carried out daily. In the central hall of each building is a TV. Incredibly picturesque city of Divnomorsk. The recreation center "Rainbow" is simply buried in green spaces. There are playgrounds. For outdoor activities there is a place for sports, equipped with modern equipment, table tennis, a beach volleyball court and a small football field.

For those who care about having a quality vacation, Divnomorsk, recreation centers in this village are ideal.



Three meals a day are organized in a modern, well-equipped dining room, located one hundred and fifty meters from the beach and can accommodate up to eight hundred and fifty people. Menus are diverse. For celebrations, the dining room has a banquet hall and two VIP-halls. In addition to the dining room, there is a snack bar in the seven-story building. There is also a sauna with a beautiful pool and a computer room.

Beach holidays and other activities

The complex has its own cozy pebble beach, equipped with awnings, showers, toilets. The safety of relaxing on the beach is provided by rescuers on duty and medical staff.

Vacationers in the evenings can have fun on the dance floor or watching movies. The list of entertainment also includes a visit to the computer room and the opportunity to play modern video games, billiards and karaoke.

The safe living and relaxation of guests is facilitated by the availability of round-the-clock security and medical staff. There is a fence around the entire perimeter of the territory and 24-hour video surveillance.

The village of Divnomorsk is a wonderful vacation spot for various categories of the population (children, youth, middle-aged people). Developed tourist infrastructure provides for this a variety of opportunities. The best option is to truly understand what provides a wonderful vacation Divnomorsk. Recreation facilities here are some of the best on the coast.
