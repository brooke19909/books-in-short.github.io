"Garden" district. Cheboksary are becoming more beautiful

The master plan for the development of the site called "Sadovy District" (Cheboksary) involves a highly developed metropolis with a wide avenue and the lights of numerous supermarkets. But this is in the future. At present, a kindergarten and residential buildings have been built. Another kindergarten is in the plans of the developer.





Territory

Between 2013 and 2018 in the Leninsky district of the city will grow a new "Garden" district (Cheboksary). Its address is between Gennady Aigi Avenue and Bogdan Khmelnitsky Street. It is planned that in 2019 approximately thirty thousand citizens will live in this wonderful corner of the city.

To date, the construction of two neighborhoods has been completed, each of which has several multi-storey buildings. Before the end of the time allotted for construction, it is planned to construct many more residential buildings. Each of which will accommodate comfortable apartments.





The "Sadovy" district (Cheboksary) is more suitable for newlyweds or small families, as the housing fund provides for mainly one-room and two-room apartments. A three-room apartment is a very limited number.

Such a high-speed construction is due to the fact that houses are built only from panels, which allows you to complete the project on this scale in just a few years.

Planned infrastructure

In addition to residential buildings, the Sadovy district (Cheboksary) will have all the necessary infrastructure for urban residents: a kindergarten, a school, a clinic, shops and accessible transport links. A kindergarten has already been built, and all other facilities are planned to begin to be built after the completion of the construction of residential buildings. A sufficient number of consumer services enterprises are also provided. All that is designed to provide a comfortable life.





A lot of living space will be transferred to state bodies to meet municipal needs, that is, in order to implement programs to provide housing for orphans, large families and other needy categories of citizens. It was on this condition that land was allocated for the construction of such a large project.

In addition, for the convenience of future residents of the "Sadovoy" district (Cheboksary), the adjoining avenue was rebuilt. For this, the administration developed design and estimate documentation and carried out the necessary work.

At the beginning of this year, perestroika will also affect Khmelnitsky Street. There, construction of a school for 1,500 study places will be carried out there. Construction will be completed by 2018, and the school will accept its first students.

Construction

Thus, the developer intends to put into operation annually one hundred thousand square meters of housing built. At the same time, erecting infrastructure facilities. The promised school, and the much-needed school, should also be built by 2018.





Residents of the city differently evaluate the "Sadovy" district (Cheboksary). Their reviews are also different. Someone likes life in the new microdistrict more than in their previous place of residence. And someone is dissatisfied with the incompletely constructed object. But all the negative phenomena are temporary. After all, the company-developer does not stop there and continues to fulfill its obligations and is building up the territory.

Appearance

Everything becomes more beautiful and more beautiful "Garden District" (Cheboksary). A photo of this area is a confirmation of this. You can watch how high-rise buildings grow, how cars and people around them appear.

The fully built microdistrict promises to be perhaps the most significant in the city, because in terms of scale and number of living space it is many times greater than the already constructed ones.
