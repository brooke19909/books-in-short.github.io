HTC Beats Audio Phone Features

Numerous admirers of various gadgets with enthusiasm and awe rejoice in the emergence of new electronic "smart toys". Added and improved functions, a simplified interface, a full set of necessary applications - all these parameters are decisive in the matter of product selection. Such devices are already indispensable for the lifestyle of a modern person. To simplify their existence, many people are looking forward to new, more advanced appliances.

You are probably familiar with Taiwanese HTC. The company is one of the leading manufacturers of the most popular smartphones in the cellular market . She launched the HTC Sensation Beats Audio. This event took place in 2011. It is about this model of cell phone that will be discussed in our article.

General information and differences





To simplify the process of perceiving information, we will reduce the name of the model of a cellular device to HTC Beats Audio. The reason is this: this smartphone is almost a complete copy of the flagship device of the company called Sensation. To let the reader understand what kind of gadget in question, we will discuss some points.

Although HTC Beats Audio is almost a complete counterpart to the famous HTC Sensation, it has a number of distinctive features. First of all, this, of course, appearance. The case of the new device has brighter, even aggressive tones. However, this in no way repels the potential buyer, because in the new version the smartphone looks more interesting and attractive.

The second hallmark of HTC Beats Audio is its deeper musical focus. No wonder the Taiwanese corporation joined forces with the American company Monster, headed by the famous Dr. Dre. Technologies called Beats Audio and the development of Asian engineers have given customers a great opportunity to enjoy the amazing quality of music using their phone.

Inside the box



What is included with this device? Unlike many other similar products of the company, in the box of the HTC Beats Audio smartphone, in addition to the usual charger, operating manual and USB cable, there are Beats Audios branded headphones from the American manufacturer Monster. In all previous bundles, the headset was standard. In variants of the new lineup, the user receives excellent headphones worth more than a hundred US dollars. For such a cool headset, the manufacturer additionally puts a case in the box. It is noteworthy that the headphones are made in a similar color to the smartphone. It is worth noting that, depending on the country of the supplier of the device, a memory card may also be included.
