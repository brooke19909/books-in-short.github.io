"Polyana 1389" - hotel & Spa 4 * (Russia, Krasnaya Polyana). Overview, description and reviews of tourists

Resorts of the Krasnodar Territory are suitable for both summer and winter holidays, and Polyana 1389 will provide a warm welcome to tourists. The hotel operates to the highest standards of service.

Location

Fans of mountain recreation should pay attention to the hotel "1389". Krasnaya Polyana, Esto-Sadok, Achipsinskaya street, 8/11 - address of the institution. It is one and a half kilometers from the train station and 45 kilometers from the international airport. Also, vacationers will be able to visit the following tourist sites:

  • ski lift (1 km);
  • cable car (1 km);
  • sledding center (2 km);
  • biathlon competition complex (3 km).




brief information

In one of the most beautiful places of the Krasnodar Territory is located the hotel "Polyana 1389". The hotel got its name, given the fact that it is located at an altitude of 1389 meters above sea level. For the comfortable accommodation of guests, 151 rooms, 70 full-fledged apartments and 28 cottages are provided. One of the main advantages of the hotel is the use of environmentally friendly materials in the interior.

The resort complex "Polyana 1389" (hotel-spa) gained wide popularity. Prices for accommodation are quite justified. In addition, it is worth noting such advantages of this institution:

  • location on one of the highest mountain points, which provides a picturesque panoramic view;

  • the opportunity to visit the wellness center with a wide range of procedures for beauty and well-being;
  • banquet halls and meeting rooms offer great opportunities for events of various kinds;
