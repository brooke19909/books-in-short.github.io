Description of the recreation center "Zelenaya Polyana", Belgorod

As soon as the summer time comes, many people wonder where to spend their holidays. Just look at the photo of the recreation center "Zelenaya Polyana" in Belgorod, and the question will disappear by itself. Here you can not only have a good weekend with a fun company, but also enjoy a vacation with your family. The base was built on the shore of the North Donets. There is a beach, a forest. Everyone will find entertainment to their liking and will be recharged with vitality for the whole year.

Conditions for a long rest

At the recreation center "Zelenaya Polyana" (Belgorod), comfortable luxury houses are offered for extended stays. They are designed for 2, 4 and 6 people. In the latter version, conditions are provided for a guest stay of a company of 15 people. The houses are equipped with everything necessary for relaxation. They have beds, tables, chairs, TV. Large rooms include a sofa. Shower and toilet are located directly in the houses. There is a stove on which vacationers can cook full breakfasts, lunches and dinners. Do not worry that the purchased products will disappear in the heat, as the rooms have a refrigerator. In the event of a drop in outdoor temperature, each room is equipped with radiators that are used for heating.

One day rest

Very often, the recreation center "Zelenaya Polyana" (Belgorod) is visited for celebrations. As a rule, such companies rarely stay overnight, therefore comfortable arbors are offered for them. They are designed for a different number of people: from 2 to 15. Each guest gazebo is equipped with comfortable benches. In the center is a table. If the company wants to cook meat at the stake, then there is a barbecue nearby. By renting such gazebos for the weekend, there is no doubt that the rest will be fruitful. For more solemn events, such as weddings, a banquet hall is intended. It is cozy and beautiful. There is all the necessary furniture and even a fireplace.

Territory arrangement

At the recreation center "Zelenaya Polyana" (Belgorod) there will be no time to be bored. For active recreation sports grounds are provided here. Those who wish to play volleyball or soccer are offered all the conditions. The ball can be rented at the base.

Kids will also find something to do. For them on the territory are equipped with slides, swings, there is a sandbox. Teenagers can play table tennis. But their parents will not only stroll, enjoying the beautiful nature, but can go play a game of billiards.

How to get there

It is quite easy to get to the recreation center "Zelenaya Polyana" from Belgorod. If you have a personal car, then you can use it. You need to move along Dalnaya Street. At the intersection with st. Watchtower turn left and follow to the base. The distance of this path is 2.6 km. You can overcome it in 8-10 minutes. If there is no personal transport, then you can use public transport. By bus, the journey takes about 30 minutes.
