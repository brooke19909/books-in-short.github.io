"Voroniy Khutor" (recreation center): photos and reviews of tourists

Every resident of a big city, especially such a metropolis as St. Petersburg, knows how the constant bustle and rush of modern life exhausts. There is absolutely no time left to fully relax. And to do this in a big city is difficult: noise, gas and monotony of the stone jungle. But for the residents of St. Petersburg there is an opportunity to take a break from this, you just need to come to the Raven Farm. The recreation center is located in a picturesque place, away from roads and large cities, just 90 kilometers from the Northern capital.



Picturesque nature

In the cleanest pine forest on the shores of Lake Borisov is the Raven Farm. The recreation center of St. Petersburg is proud of the unique picturesque nature of the Karelian Isthmus and provides a relaxing holiday. Swimming in a clean lake, fishing, boating and catamarans, walking in the forest, picking mushrooms and berries - all this is included in the recreation program. Nearby is Priozersk, where you can visit a unique natural monument - Losevsky rapids. The recreation center "Raven Farm" is surrounded by pine forests and pristine lakes. The picturesque Vuoksa River flows nearby.

What is the "Raven Farm"

The recreation center is unique. It is a cottage village where you can retire and comfortably relax alone or with your family, as well as celebrate any holiday with a big company. A picturesque place in the Priozersky district of the Leningrad region allows you to relax comfortably. Just 90 km from St. Petersburg is the recreation center "Raven Farm". Address: pos. Borisovo, Leningrad region.

There are all conditions for outdoor activities and entertainment at any time of the year. 10 km away is the popular ski resort, where extreme sports enthusiasts like to come in winter. Nearby is also a sports and entertainment complex in Korobitsino. But the recreation center "Raven Farm" itself provides on its territory all the conditions for a comfortable stay. Accommodations

A room or a whole cottage is provided at the Voroniy Khutor base for 2 days. People come here most often for the weekend, as it is easy to get from the city. This is the best place to spend a weekend. The price for a weekend stay varies from 5200 for a double room, up to 12-40 thousand for a cottage. On weekdays, the cost is halved. The price of the ticket includes, in addition to accommodation, bedding and the use of barbecue. Meals and all other services are paid separately.

What living conditions does the Raven Farm provide to vacationers? The recreation center is modern, therefore all superior rooms have a bathroom and shower, a refrigerator and a TV. Each room has hot and cold water. The rooms are located in two-story cottages with a modern layout. They have a separate entrance and a full-wall window.

Separate cottages have a kitchen with a stove and kitchenware. The complex has 4, 6, 12, 14 and 16-seater houses. They are made of modern technologies from timber and trimmed with wood inside. They all have adjustable heating, heated floors, many cottages have a living room with fireplace and a washing machine. An extra sofa bed is available for a fee.



Food at the recreation center

Each cottage in the kitchen has a cooler with drinking water, which is provided to vacationers for free. But food is only for a fee. The recreation center has a large cafe on the lake. In it you can eat or order food directly in your room. The cafe has two large halls with 40 seats, one of which is open and goes directly to the lake.

In addition, you can pay for food immediately when buying a ticket, so it will be a little cheaper. Breakfast will cost 150 rubles, lunch 300 rubles, and dinner 250 rubles. By prior order, fish is baked or national dishes are prepared, for example, Uzbek pilaf.



Additional services

The recreation center "Raven Farm" in the Leningrad Region has all the conditions for a comfortable stay. This complex is created to modern standards and provides vacationers with numerous additional services. Free car parking is available at each cottage. Barbecue facilities can be used daily. Other services for a fee. On the territory of the complex there are three Russian baths (5-6 local), an hour of visiting which costs 2 thousand rubles. They are located quite secluded and have separate approaches to the water.

On the lake there is a sandy beach, a volleyball court. You can rent a boat or catamaran. You can also ride bicycles or ATVs. On the territory of the recreation center there is a beautiful wooden children's town. In winter, a large skating rink is poured, on the slope they arrange a track for riding on "cheesecakes", skiing and sledding. For an additional fee, you can also rent:

Year-round guided tours of the surrounding attractions are provided, and you can also travel around the surroundings on your own. It is especially interesting to see the Oreshek fortress, Lutheran churches, the Konevsky monastery and other places.



Entertainment provided at the recreation center

Here you can relax in comfort, just secluded, visiting the lake or walking in the picturesque surroundings. And what kind of entertainment does Raven Farm provide to vacationers? This recreation center provides an opportunity to find an interesting activity for both children and young people who love outdoor activities, and couples and elderly people.

Children's club "Voronenok" is open daily from 12 to 19 hours. He invites children from 3 years. For the smallest, a special program is held with dancing and games. They are taught to draw, sculpt. Older children can participate in creative workshops or theme parties hosted by animators. An hour at the club costs 150 rubles.

Horse walks in the woods cost from 500 rubles. They can be independent or with an experienced trainer who will conduct initial riding lessons.

On the territory of the recreation center there is a fitness club, volleyball and basketball courts, paintball.

You can rent sports equipment, ATVs, catamarans or board games.



Voroniy Khutor (recreation center): reviews

People vacationing in this cottage complex leave only positive reviews. Most like privacy and silence, as the houses are located far from each other. Vacationers also note the comfort of rooms and delicious food in the cafe. And even the price is not the cause of negative feedback, since it is not higher than at other recreation centers. But the quality of service, the convenience of living, as well as the developed infrastructure make the Voroniy Khutor one of the most favorite vacation spots for residents of the Leningrad Region.
