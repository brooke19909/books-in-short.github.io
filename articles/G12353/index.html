King Tut Waterpark Hotel, Hurghada: description, features and reviews

Did you choose Egypt as your vacation destination? King Tut Waterpark (Hurghada) can be a great option for your stay if you prefer inexpensive and comfortable hotels.



Location

This hotel complex is located almost in the center of the resort city of Hurghada. So the guests have access to all objects of the tourist infrastructure - bars, restaurants, shops, a tour desk, etc. The distance to the airport is only five kilometers. So, upon arrival, you can reach the hotel in just a quarter of an hour.

Egypt, King Tut Waterpark Hotel (Hurghada): general information

This hotel was opened in 2004. Partial renovation was carried out in 2011. The housing stock consists of 135 rooms located in the main six-story building and several bungalows. Apartments in the hotel are presented in the following categories - standard, family, suite. Each room has everything you need - a TV with cable channels, comfortable furniture, a telephone, air conditioning, a bathroom, a hairdryer, an electric kettle, a safe, a balcony or terrace.





Food is organized on an all-inclusive basis. There are also several bars on site offering both a variety of drinks and snacks.

Nutrition

As many tourists who have visited Egypt at least once know, the local cuisine is distinguished by a large number of spices used, which are unusual for our compatriots. Therefore, quite often travelers from Russia and other CIS countries complain about the fact that the dishes offered in hotel restaurants do not suit their taste and even sometimes cause digestion problems. As for the hotel we are considering, most of the tourists with the level of organization of food here were very pleased. According to them, the choice of dishes in the main restaurant of the hotel is very wide. So, here you can taste meat, and poultry, and fish, and seafood, and a variety of snacks, salads, fresh vegetables and fruits, magnificent fragrant pastries and delicious sweets. Once a day, guests are treated to ice cream.



Beverages

The King Tut Waterpark Hotel (Hurghada) has several bars on its territory. Tourists were quite satisfied with their work. As they say, bartenders do their job well, and the choice and quality of drinks are on top. In addition, being at almost any point in the hotel, you can ask a waiter passing by to bring you this or that drink, which will save you from having to go to a bar.

Sea

Judging by the comments of travelers living in the hotel complex under consideration, their beach holiday was a success. So, according to them, you can reach the sea literally in a couple of minutes. The beach at the hotel is quite large, clean, sandy. There are many sun loungers and umbrellas, there is a beach bar where you can not only get a drink, but also have a snack during the day. You can enter the sea like a sand, which is very convenient for parents with small children, and go down from the pier immediately to the depths. Near the coast is a large and beautiful coral reef. So the guests enjoyed snorkeling with pleasure, admiring the various bright and amazing inhabitants of the Red Sea.



Entertainment

King Tut Waterpark Hotel (Hurghada), according to our compatriots, offers enough options for leisure activities. So, in the daytime you can have a great rest near the pools on the specially equipped sun terraces. Bars with a wide selection of soft and alcoholic drinks work right there. In one of the pools several water slides are equipped, which both children and adults enjoyed with pleasure. The only negative for some tourists was the fact that the water does not heat up.

From morning to evening, a team of animators works in the hotel complex. They offer guests to take part in water aerobics, gymnastics, yoga, play water polo, darts, boccia, beach volleyball , etc. In the evening, entertaining performances are held here, including with the participation of invited artists.





The youngest tourists did not ignore the hotel. So, for them, a mini club works throughout the day. Here, kids are offered various fun and educational activities in accordance with their age. In the evenings, a mini disco is held for children.

Staff

The staff of the King Tut Hotel left a most favorable impression on travelers. So, our compatriots note that the staff here are always very friendly, polite and willing to help. Many of them understand Russian. So no difficulties in communication should arise here.
