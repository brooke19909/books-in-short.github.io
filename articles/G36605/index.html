Payroll - posting. Payroll postings

Salary is a monetary amount due to an individual for work performed. Its amount is the main source of human existence and fulfillment of his needs. The level of wages depends on many factors, such as experience, qualifications, the number of hours worked or the direct result of work. The accrual of amounts consists of several stages. Consider the main types of wages and the order of its reflection in accounting.

Salary Forms

In determining the amount due to the employee for the performance of work, there should be a basis on which the accountant will rely when drawing up the calculation. In the Russian Federation, one of two forms of wages is applied : time or piecework. The value of the first is determined on the basis of the time actually spent at the workplace and its quality. In this case, the employer may apply the tariff rate of hourly or weekly wages or set the size of the monthly official salary. When calculating the payment for each hour or week, the total amount of wages in each month may vary slightly. This happens due to the different number of working days. The amount of official salary remains unchanged.





The direct piecework form of remuneration depends on the result of the employee‚Äôs work efficiency. At the same time, payments for the established unit of calculation remain unchanged even with a positive development dynamics. The chord system is of a similar nature, only payments are made in full of the output / organized goods turnover. These methods are mainly used to motivate employees, on whom the financial activities of the company directly depend. Regardless of the way the employer calculates payroll, the posting is the same.

Salary and allowances: accrual, payment, postings, characteristics

Each of the forms of remuneration, according to the contract, may provide for a bonus system when fulfilling or overfulfilling the plan set for the employee. At each separate enterprise, other allowances may be provided for:

  • seniority gained in the company;
  • harmful conditions;
  • the complexity of the tasks;
  • stimulating;
  • overtime, business trips;
  • other surcharges.

The amount of allowances is included in payroll. Posting is made up one or more, if the surcharge is made later. When accruing , the payroll account is credited, and when paid, it is debited, which completely repeats the procedure for issuing an official salary to an employee.

Payroll process

The formation of amounts due to employees occurs in several stages. First, the accountant, using data on the characteristics of the performance of labor duties, calculates the total amount of nominal wages. After that, tax deductions are made, and the employee receives funds in a timely manner. Values ‚Äã‚Äãof nominal and real wages are indicated in the payroll.





The time to receive the amounts due depends on the internal schedule of the company. Usually these are the first weeks of a new month. Often at the end of the period, the organization provides an opportunity to receive an advance.

Units of synthetic and analytical accounting of remuneration

To collect information on payments of earned funds, the standard chart of accounts provides for account 70. In relation to the balance sheet, it has a passive structure and accruals are made on a loan, which means the company owes to employees for wages.





Upon payment of funds or deductions, the account is debited. Each employee has a separate analytical account for which payroll is made. Posting in the general case looks like this: Dt 70 subsch. "Ivanov I.I." Ct 50/51.

Real wage formation: tax deductions

The first mandatory payment to the budget is the income tax of an individual. For its calculation, the amount of nominal wages is used, from which the amount of due tax deductions is taken. In accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation, they can be counted on:

  • disabled people from childhood;
  • parents of children under 18 years old, when studying at universities under 24 years old (provided for each child);
  • participants of the Chernobyl accident;
  • parents to care for children with disabilities of I and II groups;
  • employees in other circumstances provided for by the Law.




If the employee is not supposed to reduce the tax base, then the calculations begin immediately after the formation of the nominal wage by multiplying it by the tariff rate. Suppose an employee is engaged in primary production. Operations are in the following order:

  2. A deduction was made on personal income tax: Dt 70 Ct 68 (subch. "Income tax").

Tax is paid monthly from all accrued amounts subject to taxation. Detailed information on the amount of deductions, tax rate and types of income included in the database is contained in the Tax Code.

Payroll postings with social contributions

In addition to tax payments, there are mandatory payments to social funds, which include:

  • PF of the Russian Federation at a rate of 22%;
  • Mandatory Medical Fund insurance in the amount of 5.9%;
  • Social Insurance Fund at a rate of 2.9%;
  • Accident insurance fund from 0.2 to 8.5%.

Payments of a different nature

On account 70 is not only the write-off or payroll. A posting with its use can also be made with payments:

  • social nature, for example, benefits (with correspondence 69.1);
  • vacation and business trip;
  • for compensation of physical harm received in the performance of official duties;
  • compensation for used personal transport and other costs.

In addition, the company has the right to deduct from the salary the amount of material damage caused by the employee, or receivables for accountable funds.
