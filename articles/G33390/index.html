What is Tunis in August? Prices, entertainment, leisure features

Tunisia blooms in August. At the end of summer, a period of long and very warm nights begins, accompanied by discos and other entertainment. The sea is warmed to the maximum, and throughout the day there is clear sunny weather, and there is not a single cloud. In addition, it was during this period that local chefs very generously treat all tourists with various local seafood dishes. In a word, visiting Tunisia in August is a must if you want to enjoy this country in full.





Weather

All weather forecasts for this time can be characterized very briefly and concisely - the heat. The weather in Tunisia in August is a typical Mediterranean summer, which is famous for sultry days and very warm nights. During the day, the air temperature warms up to +40 degrees and only in the evening drops to 35. At night, the thermometer shows us 25-27 degrees above zero. The complete absence of wind, any coolness and precipitation. Perhaps you should not say that the sea also resembles fresh milk. Its temperature reaches 30 degrees, and you can swim in such a hot giant bath both day and night.

Beach Holiday Rules

Tunisia in August is just a resort paradise, where people come only to swim in the incredibly warm sea and sunbathe under the bright tropical sun. However, with each of these entertainments it is worth being extremely careful. Solar activity here rolls over, as the terrain is essentially deserted. A complete lack of moisture in the air can cause dehydration in people whose health is not very strong. Therefore, a cream with a high level of protection is your first weapon against the local sun. You need to relax on the beach in the shade, but it is best to come here either early in the morning, or after five in the evening. Visiting excursions this month is not recommended, it is better to simply enjoy the sea views and eucalyptus plantations.





Hotel Features

Due to the fact that Tunisia in August is not able to raise large amounts of money from the tourism business due to excursions, numerous SPA salons of various categories were opened in the hotels. Both men and women regularly come here at the end of summer to enjoy the virtuoso work of the local masseurs and cosmetologists who work wonders. The main feature of Tunisia is considered to be thalassotherapy. The essence of the procedure is that you not only rejuvenate and keep your body in order due to algae and other seafood, but you can also get rid of many diseases in this way. We also note that for relatively low prices (in comparison with Moscow) you can whiten your teeth, undergo a course of anti-cellulite massage, lymphatic drainage, facial cleansing, and also carry out a whole host of cosmetic procedures.





Local kitchen

Despite the fact that Tunisia is a Muslim state located in Africa, restaurants here can be compared with Spanish or Italian. Cozy, small, often hidden in the basements of houses or on the second floors. They are ideal for romantic dates, warm evenings in the company of close friends and family dinners. As for the local cuisine, it abounds with hot spices and spices. In August, a huge number of seafood dishes are served here , among which there are roast, various fish snacks and soups. All dishes are very seasoned with various spicy additives, therefore, they can only be eaten with bread. Alcohol is rarely served here, although there are already adapted menus for tourists. The Tunisian mint tea, in which pine nuts are placed , is especially popular. In principle, such a drink can be eaten.





Price equivalents

To spend a minimum of money and at the same time have a good rest - this means visiting Tunis in August. Prices for trips here are much lower than on the exotic and popular islands of Bali, Thailand, Canary Islands, Hawaii and so on. Nevertheless, for this country, August is considered one of the most expensive months for tourists. If in spring or winter you can fly here for $ 1000, then in summer such a price can only be for a burning tour. On average, you can relax here together for 1300-1500 dollars (taking into account the flight, insurance and a good hotel). We also note that in August prices for the same thalassotherapy and other services that are provided at the resorts also rise.

Through the eyes of travelers: Tunisia in August

Reviews of tourists who visited this small cozy country at the end of summer can be described as follows. Pricing policy, although considered overpriced at this time, is quite acceptable for a resident of Russia. The services that can be obtained for such a minimal fee are quite acceptable. The result of any procedures or entertainments that are held here, everyone is quite satisfied.

The weather in August is generally a fairy tale. Despite the unbearable heat for us, the atmosphere is very lively and truly summer. You can not get out of the sea for days, get an excellent tan (just do not overdo it) and improve your health well thanks to eucalyptus and fir plantations.
