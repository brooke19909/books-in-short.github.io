Hotel Ayaz Aqua Hotel 4 * Turkey, Bodrum: reviews

When planning a trip abroad to realize a long-awaited vacation, many opt for Turkey with its many cities near the waters of the Mediterranean. However, resorts of the Aegean Sea, including Bodrum, are no less popular. He earned the title of “party” settlement due to the high density of bars and restaurants. However, a trip to these parts will be interesting for lovers of active and informative vacations: the developed infrastructure and rich history will allow you to have fun and entertaining leisure time.

The Ayaz Aqua Hotel, a four-star hotel complex, offers guests comfortable accommodation, quality service, new emotions and an unforgettable experience.





general characteristics

Ayaz Aqua Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Bodrum) has a considerable history and has gained great experience over the years of its existence. The institution was opened in 2002. The last update and repair was made in 2014. On a territory of 4790 square meters there are two three-story buildings and a spacious aquazone. The buildings are not equipped with elevators and have a distinctive exterior that resembles huge huts more than cozy chambers. Vines of fragrant bright flowers fall on balconies from the third to the first floor.

The nearest airport is 35 km away. The absence of heavy and tiring travels after the flight favorably affects the mood and general well-being of newly arrived tourists. The distance to the coast is 250 meters.

Travelers are incredibly satisfied with the hotel's park area, which is not large, but is saturated with greenery, shrubs and tropical palm trees.

Pets cannot be accommodated here. Smoking in the apartment is prohibited. There are no specially equipped rooms for disabled guests. The staff speaks many languages, which makes it easy to understand and satisfy the wishes of customers.





Accommodation

The room fund of Ayaz Aqua Hotel 4 * is represented by the chambers of two categories, including Standard and Economy. They differ among themselves with landscapes opening from the windows, and spaciousness. The total number of rooms is 75 units. Each has its own terrace or balcony, where you can spend a romantic evening, admiring the sunset, or meet the first rays of the sun, drinking a cup of coffee, sitting in a wicker chair in the fresh air.

The interior is classic, made in accordance with all modern trends. The predominance of light colors in the design visually increases the dimensions of the spaces and adds elegance and gloss to them. The walls are hung with paintings, cosiness is created thanks to vases with fresh flowers. Lighting is sufficient, provided by floor lamps, sconces and exclusive lighting. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles or laminate.

Ayaz Aqua Hotel 4 * receives a positive plan for cleanliness and comfort. The guests note that the maids clean up daily and change bed linen twice a week. You can order food in the room or the service "wake-up call". An extra bed is represented by a sofa, which is zoned and represents a mini-living room.





Arrangement

The chambers are equipped with everything necessary for a carefree holiday. There is an individual split system that regulates the temperature of the internal microclimate. The TV has Russian-language channels, which allows you to stay up to date with all the latest news and events. Phone - paid, in order to contact relatives, additional payment is required. Wi-Fi is free throughout. There is a refrigerator. Modern fire safety systems are present in every apartment. The safe is located at the reception, its use is paid.

The decoration of the bathroom is represented by ceramic tiles. It is equipped with a shower cabin, a large sink, a large-scale mirror and a hairdryer. A set of towels is available, the number of which complies with international standards. A set of personal care products is represented by soap and shampoo.





Nutrition

The concept followed by Ayaz Aqua Hotel provides for the organization of breakfast, lunch and dinner. They are held according to the buffet system. The variety of dishes on offer impresses every guest, as noted in their numerous reviews of Ayaz Aqua Hotel 4 * (Bodrum) . Meat, fish, cereals, dairy products, desserts, snacks, fruits and much more - this is what the spa people satisfy the hunger. The facility has an outdoor terrace.

There is a cafe, where meals are paid after the fact, and a pool bar and lobby. Tea time from 17:00 to 18:00. A selection of desserts and sweets are served along with hot drinks. All local drinks are included in the All Inclusive program.





Aquazone

Many guests in their reviews of the Ayaz Aqua Hotel speak of a large-scale swimming area, which is never crowded. The main pool is filled with fresh water and does not have a heating system. Its area is 650 square meters. Nearby there is a children's pool, sunbeds and umbrellas. An integral part of staying in this zone is training in water aerobics, conducted by professional trainers.

Beach

The proximity to its own beach allows vacationers not to spend much time on the road. The infrastructure of this territory is quite developed. It contains sun loungers with umbrellas for sunbathing and air bathing. The towel dispenser operates on a deposit basis.

The beach is sandy, the entrance to the water is gentle. Gradually, an increase in depth and the presence of shallow water allows the kids to frolic, play and dive aground. There is a lifeguard service that tirelessly watches over the order and safety of vacationers.





Water activities

In order for the time spent here not to be monotonous, you can have fun playing volleyball. New acquaintances and easy communication will allow you to find new friends, and possibly friends. No less popular are water rides and diving. Immerse yourself in the bottom of the Aegean Sea, explore the underwater inhabitants and get acquainted with the relief of deep-sea lands. There are only age restrictions.





Children's leisure

Ayaz Aqua Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Bodrum) receives positive reviews from tourists who come to rest with their children. In addition to the open-air swimming pool, children can frolic in the children's playground, which is equipped with carousels, slides, labyrinths and ladders. It is safe, has a rubberized coating and is made of high quality plastic. Time spent with peers is full of new emotions, excellent communication and brisk running. This has a beneficial effect on the well-being of children, their body heals, and their mood is always joyful.

Highchairs in the restaurant are freely available. Playpens in the room are provided free of charge upon request.





Free time activities

The list of entertainment and ways to spend active leisure include a game of billiards, table tennis, mini-golf. Guests are invited to visit the massage room, where they will experience unprecedented pleasure and relaxation with every cell of their body. Once a week, a Turkish night is held, which every guest comes to visit. Interesting customs, national cuisine and dancing are what are in the program of this event.

Additional services

Ayaz Aqua Hotel 4 * reviews the positive plan very often receives for the provided additional services. Here they are, as guests note, at a fairly high level. In order to quickly respond to requests from guests and instantly solve problems, the reception is open around the clock. Here you can book a tour of local attractions. An interesting storytelling guide will tell the secrets and traditions of these colorful lands. For those who like to relax on their own, it is proposed to rent a comfortable car and go on their own to get acquainted with the surrounding natural beauties.

The lobby has an internet corner equipped with computers and a seating area with a TV. Parking spaces are provided free of charge. Currency exchange and laundry services are available at the reception. Doctor services on request. A grocery mini market is located in the hotel complex. Professional photographers offer their services, who, using their skills, capture unique moments of travelers' relaxation. Those who rested here once, in the reviews report a desire to return here again.
