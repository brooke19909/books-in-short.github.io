How to implement a request for details on the number of the Megafon operator

In order to inform customers about the costs of mobile communications, Megafon, along with other telecom operators, added bill details to the list of services offered. At the request of the subscriber, information about paid actions performed from his number for a specific period of time can be provided to him. Detailing: general information

The service "Detailing" can mean two concepts. As a rule, the more common is the full decryption of the paid actions performed on the customer‚Äôs number (numbers of other subscribers with whom they interact, the volume of services, the time it takes to complete operations, debiting for ordered content, etc.).

Another type of detail is general information about the costs of a room without specification. Megafon subscribers have the opportunity to receive both versions of the report. Moreover, the provision of summary information by number (without details, in the format of the service - cost) is not charged.

Megafon: request for bill details - overview of available options

You can view information about what operations were performed from the number, as well as how much and how much money was written off for them, using any of the options below:





  • One-time details - implies a request for the current month (to view earlier events regarding the number, you should choose another option for the service).
  • Decryption for a month - receiving data for two months (the subscriber can choose which calendar month the data is required for).
  • Regular distribution of a brief overview on monthly expenses - sending notifications by e-mail, by courier; through the office staff you can also get data when contacting the person to whom the SIM card is registered.
  • Request for the cost of the last ten actions (express detail) - the request is sent via a mobile device; in response, the subscriber receives a brief overview of the last day (namely, the last ten operations performed on the number, without specifying details).

Megafon: how to order detailing yourself

Each of the previously mentioned methods of obtaining data implies that the client sends the request for data sampling. He can do this in several ways:





  • Visit your personal account: all types of details are available here, except for express.
  • Use USSD functionality - this command is used only to get a brief summary of the last ten actions per day.
  • Contacting a communication salon is the most expensive option for updating cost data. The cost of each type of detail will be discussed in more detail below.
