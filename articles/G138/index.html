Mysterious Mexico: a review of tourists about large resorts and the country as a whole

Mexico as a tourist destination has just appeared in the field of view of our tour operators, although Americans and travelers from Western Europe have been resting there for many decades. We have an idea about this fabulously beautiful country on the basis of soap operas "The Rich Also Cry," and various similar to this non-life series. But the world opening behind the walls of the cinematic "hacienda" is much more incredible and exotic than any film. What is she - this mysterious Mexico? The recall of tourists who have already been there will allow us to figure this out.



The ancient Mayan and Aztec pyramids framed by the lush greenery of the jungle, colonial palaces among palm trees, Catholic cathedrals, formidable volcanoes, snow-white beaches, paradises of unspoiled nature, huge megacities - all this is Mexico. The recall of tourists tells us that the country is full of contrasts, and with the splendor of the well-protected rich neighborhoods, the poverty of slums adjoins.
