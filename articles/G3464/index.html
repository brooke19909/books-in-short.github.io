Sewing principle

Wash and iron the cuffs and collar. Starch if necessary. The older generation remembers well how to sew cuffs on school-style sleeves so that they are easy to take off. They steamed decorative elements on Friday night and stretched them. On weekends, they were engaged in starching and sewing.

Little tricks

In order not to constantly engage in sewing, mothers came up with several tricks. They attach very small white buttons to the cuffs and make loops. Thus, the cuffs are easy to change even every day. Buttons can be replaced with buttons.

If you sew school dresses for your daughter to order or on your own, then think over the design so that there are gaps on the sleeves and around the throat for stretching the ribbons on which these cute accessories will be held. This is the most convenient way to fasten, and you will not need to constantly think about how to sew the cuffs on the school uniform.





Some simple ideas

An elegant removable collar will decorate any outfit in a few minutes. It will add along with sophistication and chic. In addition, everyday blouses and cardigans will look different every day. Now, to look fashionable and elegant, you do not need to spend a lot of money on the wardrobe.

Flat turn-down collar and cuffs

A great material for making a collar is faux leather. It does not require special care and washing - wiped the product, and it is like new. For decor, prepare a brooch flower or bow from a velor ribbon.

In a similar way we will make cuffs. Let the daughter put on the thing that you plan to decorate. Attach a piece of unnecessary fabric to the sleeve and outline the cuff. Their edges may be rounded or sharp. Cut the buttonhole stitches in the cuff or sew the buttons.

To make a pattern, put a piece of unnecessary fabric on the shoulders of the model. Chalk directly on the material to draw a silhouette of the collar. Cut it out. Attach the blank to the skin, circle it and cut it out. Fix the edges with a brooch or sew on them ribbons that can be tied to an elegant bow.

In a similar way, make a decor for the sleeves. The accessory, which is attached to the tape, is very convenient, you do not need to constantly think about when and how to sew the cuffs to the school uniform.

Fabric and braid collar

To make, take a wide pleated braid. Take only one measure - neck circumference. Cut a strip equal to the measurement and add 4 cm. Sew on the edge of the lace. In the middle we sew velvet braid of a contrasting shade. Tie a bow - and the stand-up collar is ready!
