Hotel Green Garden Apart Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Fans of travel and exotic holidays abroad often choose the Green Garden Apart Hotel 4 * as a place to spend their vacations, which offers some of the best living conditions on the entire Mediterranean coast.

Tour operators often emphasize that rest in this place is relatively inexpensive - the average cost of this ticket is about 50 000 rubles, which usually includes not only room rental, but also all round-trip transportation costs.

general information

Green Garden Apart Hotel 4 * was built a long time ago - in 1998. However, its owners have managed to preserve the beautiful modern look of the buildings and the well-groomed surrounding area. Vacationers staying here can enjoy the excellent technical equipment of each of the five buildings, as well as living in beautiful rooms, in which, during one of the last renovations, the interior was renovated and updated.

The total area on which this resting place is presented is just over 28 thousand square meters. m. The creators of the project managed to place five large buildings on them, in which the entire number of rooms, a small set of bungalows, as well as a huge aquazone, several sports fields and a beautiful garden that pleases the guests with their flowering throughout the holiday season are located.

Green Garden Apart Hotel 4 * (Alanya) has a rather impressive room stock, which includes 366 apartments. Each of them is arranged in the best way, they have modern furniture and fresh repairs. Staying here, tourists note the presence of light colors in the interior, as well as excellent technical equipment of the rooms. In addition, each guest in the rooms of Green Garden Apart Hotel 4 * is waiting for an individual bath set, which includes a towel, bathrobe, as well as accessories necessary for bathing.
