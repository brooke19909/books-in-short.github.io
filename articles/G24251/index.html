Atlantica Marmari Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece): hotel infrastructure, room description, service, reviews

When purchasing tours to Greece with a vacation at sea, you need to decide on the location. After all, all travelers so want the hotel to meet expectations, so that no minor troubles spoil the vacation. Greece pampers its guests with many good hotels: chic and luxurious five-star, restrained but decent 4 * hotels, budget and comfortable three-star hotels. Among this diversity, many experienced tourists distinguish Atlantica Marmari Beach. Let's look at the features of this hotel to see if it is right for you.





Location

Hotel "Atlantic Marmari Beach" is located in Greece, on the island of Kos, in the town of Marmari. Any journey begins at the airport and it is always nice when you do not need to spend a lot of time on the way to the hotel. The Hippocrates International Airport is 20 minutes by car from the hotel. The cost of a one-way taxi ride is 30 euros. You don‚Äôt have to look for a taxi for a long time - you can take it immediately near the exit from the airport.

You can reach Kos in 25 minutes by car, taxi or local bus. By the way, the bus stop is right next to the hotel, as is the car rental point, which is very convenient. Excellent Greek roads will allow you to go around the entire island and make your own travel itinerary. It is enough to take international rights with you.

Near this Greek hotel, Atlantic Marmari Beach, there are several shops with very reasonable prices. Both souvenirs and products are on sale. Nearby are cafes, restaurants and even a disco. The disco, however, does not interfere with hotel guests' sleep at all.

The resort of Marmari is quiet and calm, it is ideal for families, especially with children. Atlantica Marmari Beach is famous for its gorgeous beach, which will be discussed later.





Sea and beach

The finest white sand, a smooth entry into the sea with gradually increasing depth - what more could you want from a Greek beach? At Atlantica Marmari Beach Hotel 4 *, the beach is the best on the island, it is very large and clean. The hotel is located on the beach and provides guests with free umbrellas and sun loungers. The cleanliness of the coast is monitored by a special employee.

For those who are not used to spending all their time on the beach, there is also entertainment - surfing stations nearby and a beach volleyball court. The weather in Greece rarely brings tourists. On a hot day, you can relax in the shade of umbrellas and sun loungers on the beach. Frequent storms do not bother. When it is windy in Marmari, waves appear on the sea to the delight of children and surfers. And you can swim with a mask and see green turtles near the shore. By the way, the weather in Greece varies depending on the season. Accordingly, when planning a vacation, do not forget to ask about this feature. It is best, of course, to go to this wonderful country in May or September. In May it is not so hot, and in September the velvet season begins. In the summer heat is in full swing. In the afternoon, on average, +30 - +32 ¬∞ C. Water in the sea warms up to 26 ¬∞ C.

Dining at the hotel

What kind of vacation will do without exploring the local cuisine, especially if you are in Greece? Atlantica Marmari Beach provides its guests with every opportunity to love Greek food.

Dining at the Atlantic Marmari Beach Hotel is all-inclusive. Your day begins with breakfast, it is served in the main restaurant from 7 in the morning. For breakfast you can try yogurt, granola, pancakes with various syrups and toppings, national dishes and delicious desserts. And on Sunday you will start your day with champagne!

Lunch and dinner are offered on a buffet basis - guests themselves choose what they want to try. The food is varied, every day the chef offers something new and interesting. An abundance of fruits and vegetables, seafood and meat dishes. There is a special children's menu for kids. Themed evenings are organized several times a week, where dishes are prepared in accordance with the culinary traditions of different countries.

Each pool of the Atlantica Marmari Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece) is equipped with its own bar, there are three in total. Ice cream, beer, wine, cocktails and other alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are waiting for guests at the poolside bars. Guests can enjoy a drink in the water or relax with a glass on the sun lounger.

Even if you went to the beach, nothing will stop you from cooling off with a tasty, refreshing drink or a snack. The beach bar serves a variety of delicious cocktails, beer and wine. Hot and cold snacks are available to guests from 11 a.m. to half past five in the evening.

And what is Greece without a traditional tavern by the sea? Guests have the opportunity to visit the tavern at the hotel once for free once per stay. You need to book a visit in advance. Want to taste pizza or pasta? No problem at all. At Atlantica Marmari Beach Hotel 4 *, an Italian restaurant with a competent chef is always at your service. Dishes in the institution are selected from the menu. A visit to the restaurant is paid.

As you can see, no one will be hungry at this hotel. Fresh food is served at any time of the day. Kids will love the kids menu, sweets and ice cream. And adults will appreciate expertly prepared second courses.

Territory and infrastructure of the hotel

It should be especially noted that the hotel belongs to the Atlantica Hotels & Resorts chain. All hotels in the chain meet the highest standards of European hospitality. Atlantica Marmari Beach Hotel 4 *: tourist reviews

Making a choice of an object for living on vacation, I want to know what impressions other tourists had. After analyzing the ratings of the guests, we can distinguish what I liked most and what I would like to improve.

The most appreciated was the beach at the hotel - 4.7 out of 5 points. Excellent value. Tourists especially pay attention to the convenient entrance to the sea and the cleanliness of the beach. The number of vacationers on the beach is comfortable, sunbeds are always available.

The hotel infrastructure has also earned many accolades. Guests enjoy a sense of security, landscaped grounds and convenient parking. Room rating is also at a high level. Tourists like quality cleaning, good air conditioning. But not everyone liked the quality of furniture and sanitary ware - guests mention the outdated equipment of some rooms.

Guests of Atlantica Marmari Beach Hotel 4 * reviews are almost enthusiastic for their high quality service and excellent service. The staff is polite and competent, many like the unobtrusive and pleasant Russian-speaking guide at the hotel. But the feedback on the work of the animation team was divided. Some guests are delighted with the original performances and competitions, however there are those who do not share this opinion.

Almost every review contains an extremely positive assessment of the food in the hotel. Varied, chic, delicious - it's all about the cuisine at Atlantica Marmari Beach. According to reviews, the restaurant offers meat and seafood dishes, fresh vegetables and fruits every day. A lot of enthusiasm is caused by local desserts and ice cream.

According to most vacationers, the hotel is most suitable for a leisurely family vacation with children. You can go to this hotel with friends or a couple, but only for a measured rest, since finding nearby places for an active nightlife will be problematic.

In general, based on reviews, the Atlantic Marmari Beach Hotel has a stable positive rating of more than 4 points, which has not changed much over the past 2 years. Given the number of guests and the variety of tastes, this figure can be considered very worthy.

If you are planning a relaxing holiday with your family, you will certainly enjoy Atlantica Marmari Beach. It is so nice to relax on the perfect sandy beach to the measured sound of the sea, to taste the still unfamiliar, but incredibly delicious Greek dishes. And to see how both adults and children jump in the waves of fun and carelessly, succumbing to the feeling of happiness and enthusiasm that there is no need to rush anywhere, that you can simply enjoy the days in beautiful Greece. But this is what I want on vacation.
