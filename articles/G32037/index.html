Hilton Sharjah 5 * (Sharjah): room description, service, reviews

In the very center of the emirate, the fashionable resort complex Hilton Sharjah 5 * is located. Sharjah gives you a warm climate, a welcoming atmosphere, as well as a host of attractions. This place is definitely worth a visit.





Location

In the heart of the emirate is a resort complex "Hilton". Sharjah, Al-Mahaz, 3 - address of the institution. It is only 15 km from Dubai International Airport, which is equivalent to approximately 20 minutes by car. You can also get here by taxi, which will cost you about 1000 rubles. It is possible to order a transfer or limousine directly at the hotel. Please note that there is no public transport from the airport to the hotel.

What to visit

Rich leisure activities can be arranged by travelers outside the Hilton Hotel. Sharjah will delight you with such features:

  • Shara Center is the largest shopping center in the United Arab Emirates. The number of outlets in it is hundreds. Both global brands and exclusive local products are presented here. In addition, dozens of restaurants and entertainment venues operate in this shopping center.
  • Al Montazah is the largest amusement park with a water park, which is designed for families. In addition to water attractions, theatrical performances, sporting events and numerous catering establishments will attract your attention.
  • The Sharjah Aquarium Museum will present you with over 250 species of inhabitants of the deep sea. There are even sharks. You can relax while admiring the artificial ponds. There are several dozen cafes and souvenir stalls on the territory.
  • Malik Ibn Aws is a small two-story mosque that contrasts interestingly with the modern skyscrapers surrounding it. This is not just an architectural landmark, but also the spiritual center of the emirate.
  • Al-Buheira is a picturesque promenade with a length of about three kilometers. From any of its points a beautiful view of the sea opens. Dozens of fountains work here in the daytime, and at night all objects are beautifully illuminated.
  • Al-Qasba is a beautiful mosque, which is included in the list of the main attractions of the emirate. The architectural solution combines oriental style with modern trends. The building is especially beautiful at night when the lights turn on.
  • The footbridge across the al-Qasbah Canal is one of the resort’s most beautiful structures. It was built in just two months. Its cladding is made in a traditional oriental style. Walking along the bridge, you can enjoy panoramic views of the city.




Hotel Hilton Sharjah 5 *: room description

Comfortable living conditions are one of the most important factors of relaxation. This moment is taken into account in the resort Hilton Sharjah 5 * . You will remember Sharjah as a hospitable resort, and this institution will provide accommodation in 259 rooms of the following categories:

  • The standard rooms are spacious and have a discreet, classic interior. Guests will be invited to sit on a large double bed or a pair of twin beds, opposite which a modern plasma panel is installed. At the panoramic window, which overlooks the lagoon, there is a relaxation area, presented by soft armchairs and a coffee table.
  • Suites are large, bright studio apartments. The bedroom area is represented by a king size bed, and the living room - leather upholstered furniture. The panoramic window overlooks the lagoon. The classic interior features stylish and elaborate decor.
  • Executive rooms are the most luxurious accommodation option in this hotel. All of them consist of two rooms - a bedroom with a bed, a working area and a soft sofa, as well as a full living room. A distinctive feature of rooms of this category is that they are all located on the upper floors of the Hilton Sharjah 5 * . Beach, urban surroundings and attractions - all this will be visible from your panoramic window.

Catering establishments

Delicious and quality food is one of the advantages of Hilton Sharjah 5 * . Meals are provided in such establishments:

  • Al Dallah Lounge is a cozy place for relaxation, business meetings and friendly gatherings. The classic interior is decorated in light pastel colors. It serves fresh pastries and a wide range of pastries. An excellent addition will be high-quality strong coffee or fresh fruit juices.
  • Citrus Lobby Lounge is a cozy light bar, open from 08:00 to 02:00 for guests of the Hilton Sharjah Hotel . It serves delicious lunches and business lunches. A wide range of pastries and pastries is also available. The highlight of the design is the huge number of living plants in the dining room.
  • Mojo is an international restaurant. The menu offers dishes made from quality natural products that you can enjoy during breakfast, lunch or dinner. The institution operates on a buffet system. The decoration of the hall is in chocolate-ultramarine color.
  • Shiraz is a traditional Iranian restaurant. The interior is made in accordance with oriental motifs, and the windows of the establishment offer a bewitching view of the Khalid lagoon. The restaurant invites visitors to lunch and dinner.




Halls for your events

Four luxurious halls for events presented to your attention in the Hilton Sharjah 5 *. Sharjah is an attractive place not only for tourism, but also for business meetings, conferences and special occasions. Here are the rooms presented to your attention:

  • The ballroom can accommodate up to 500 guests. Here, as a rule, presentations or celebrations are held. It is worth noting the presence of a luxurious lounge area.
  • Meeting Room No. 1 is a spacious room decorated in a classic style. Equipped with a round table and a computer projector. Maximum capacity is 14 guests.
  • Conference Room No. 2 has a strict atmosphere, thanks to the predominance of dark colors in the design. There is a long rectangular table for 20 guests.
  • Meeting Room 3 has an island layout. This means that guests are accommodated at small freestanding round tables. Maximum capacity is 20 people.

House Rules

If you plan to relax in the Hilton Sharjah 5 * (Sharjah) , check out the rules of accommodation established in this institution in advance. Particular attention should be paid to such significant points:

  • Check-in of newly arrived guests in their rooms is possible after 14:00, and you need to leave the apartment on the day of departure before noon;
  • No pets are allowed at the hotel except guide dogs;
  • guests can smoke only in rooms located from the fourth to the seventh floor (smoking is strictly prohibited in any other apartments and premises);
  • children under 12 years old using existing beds will cost guests 800 rubles. per day;
  • placing an additional guest on a folding bed will cost 2300 rubles. per day;
  • for the services provided, guests can pay with credit cards of international payment systems.

Negative reviews

Not a single tourist is safe from negative moments during their vacation. This is mainly due to flaws and shortcomings of the resort. Hilton Sharjah has received such negative reviews:

  • extremely inconvenient shower design (due to the lack of a flexible hose, it is impossible to adjust the direction of water flow);
  • the quality of cleaning is not the best (dust on furniture and poorly cleaned plumbing are especially striking);
  • the cosmetic kit provided in the bathroom does not include a toothbrush or toothpaste;
  • expensive use of the Internet (although some persistent guests managed to get a wifi password for free);
  • overpriced food prices absolutely do not correspond to either taste, quality or assortment (it’s good that there are many cafes and restaurants nearby where you can eat tasty and inexpensively);
  • due to the fact that breakfasts are organized in the lobby, and not in the restaurant, it is always very crowded and crowded, and queues gather at the distribution;
  • sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach are very old and in some cases faulty (besides, they are not enough for everyone);
  • The highlight of the rooms is the large panoramic windows, but for some reason they are not washed well enough, which spoils the impression of a picturesque view;
  • if you check in with small children, be prepared for the fact that they are not provided with separate towels;
  • the rooms have long been in need of redecoration, and the plumbing needs to be completely replaced;
  • there are practically no plants on the territory, and therefore it looks a little depressing (especially at least some greenery near the pool is not enough);
  • after eviction from the hotel, the deposit must be returned to the card for several weeks;
  • rental rates for vehicles are almost double the rates for similar services in agencies located near the hotel;
  • if you make a cash deposit in dollars, they will refund it in dirhams at a very unfavorable rate;
  • very upset by the lack of balconies in the rooms (there is no way to fully enjoy the views and just breathe in fresh air);
  • the beach is very small and crowded, but because the rest on it can hardly be called comfortable and enjoyable;
  • inconvenient and unregulated cleaning schedule gives guests a lot of inconvenience (often maids come in the afternoon, when guests relax);
  • the hotel is located inconveniently relative to the sea (only the availability of a free transfer saves);
  • the hotel is located in an area with heavy traffic, and therefore guests traveling by car constantly get stuck in traffic when entering and leaving.
