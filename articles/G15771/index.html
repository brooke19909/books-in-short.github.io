How to sew a backpack from jeans with your own hands: patterns, recommendations

The topic that we will touch upon in this article is old jeans and original backpacks from them. Everyone has jeans that have been very fashionable for some length of time, and possibly just comfortable. But over time, they wear out. Such jeans could simply be thrown away by buying new ones, but you can give them a new life, which is what designers who offer new and original solutions do. These are bags, shorts, breeches, backpacks. A small master class on how to sew a backpack with your own hands will probably help some of you to create such an original little thing for yourself.

To do this, choose which style you will sew. The pattern of each backpack is different. It can be in the form of a bag with an extended cord, tightening the top. In such a backpack, the bottom is not done. Or sew a roomy product for hiking. How to sew a backpack more spacious? It should have a volume that creates the bottom and sides. The shape can be oval and rectangular, and to maintain the shape, you need to insert a sealant between the lining and the denim.

There is no need to buy a new fabric for a backpack, if among the things in the wardrobe there are old jeans that are very suitable for creating an exclusive product. Before you start sewing, you must prepare the material: remove all seams, if necessary; wash the prepared material and iron.

If you plan to decorate the backpack with embroidery or applique, all this must be prepared in advance. It is worth considering that you might want to combine your product with another fabric or leather. In this case, you should choose the appropriate color and texture of the partner's material.

We make a pattern

The first thing to do before you start making a backpack out of jeans is a pattern. The product can be very simple, without any exact calculations, but the pattern greatly facilitates the workflow.

According to the chosen model, the paper size of the future backpack must indicate the desired size. This is the height of the back and the width of the front and rear parts at three points (bottom, top and middle).

If it is a volume backpack, then the dimensions of the sides of the backpack and the bottom are necessary. The sidewalls have a height equal to the height of the main product, and the lower part - the bottom - should have dimensions equal to the width of the main product and the width of the sidewalls. Sewing a backpack with your own hands on a pattern will be much easier.

Valves, pockets, belts must be modeled, taking into account the dimensions of the product. When the pattern is ready, it is cut according to the seam allowances. Denim products are dense, so you need sharp scissors and thicker threads. As accessories, you can use carabiners, clips, leather belts, buttons and rivets.

How to sew a backpack from old jeans quickly and without patterns? This can be done by sewing it from the top of the trousers.

From the jeans prepared for the alteration, we cut off the legs. If the upper part in depth is not enough for the backpack, and in your opinion it will seem small, we make the necessary lengthening from the legs. This is approximately 15-20 centimeters in height, and the width is taken along the width of the top of the backpack. The straps are cut out, sewn and sewn to its back, and a cord is pulled into the harness of the belt, which tightens the top of the product.

Backpack for boy

Now consider how to sew a backpack for a boy. The first requirement is that he should look more strictly than a girl. On it, patch pockets on the back of jeans trousers or welt pockets with a zipper will be actual.

For sewing such an accessory it is better to take not the trouser legs, but the upper part of the trousers. A backpack for a boy made of jeans and decorating fabric placed on top will look beautiful. It should be in tone with the color of jeans or have discreet patterns, for example, in the form of stars. There is no bottom for such a backpack, just stitched panels of the front and back of the backpack. At the bottom, harnesses are sewn from the cut belt into which the cord is threaded. The best option would be blue or black. As you can see, in order to sew a backpack from jeans, a pattern in this case is not needed.

Patchwork Style Accessory

It is not necessary to have such old jeans that make up a full-fledged backpack from trousers or the upper part. You can make an original model from pieces of denim cut from pants. It can be strips 30 cm long, 5 cm wide. These strips are sewn with a zigzag seam to the fabric, which serves as the lining of the backpack.

You can arrange the stripes vertically and horizontally, as you like. In our photo they are located horizontally.

This backpack can be sewn in the form of a bag with strap fastenings in the lower part and in the middle of the upper. The straps can be made removable on small carbines. It is possible to provide decorative pockets torn from the back of old jeans trousers.
