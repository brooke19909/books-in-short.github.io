Recreation centers in Tobolsk: description

In Siberia there is the wonderful city of Tobolsk, one of the most picturesque and impressive. It is enough to visit it once to fall in love with this stunning nature, perfect for relaxing, walking and conducting various outdoor activities. Centuries-old coniferous forests give coolness and tranquility. In them you can walk for hours without thinking about anything. Art lovers and anyone who wants to get to know the history of the city more closely will appreciate the magnificent architectural structures and cultural monuments that reflect the life of the city since its inception.

Tobolsk is also famous for recreation centers in which everyone can find something to their liking or just relax from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Tourist complex "Abalak"

The wooden fortress located on the high bank of the Irtysh River, built on the site of the battle between the troops of Khan Kuchum and the Yermak squad, still attracts attention from afar. Here is the recreation center "Abalak", which is popular not only among Tobolsk residents, but also among residents of all of Russia.

The infrastructure is very diverse and stylized to one theme. Hotel rooms have names that refer us to Russian legends and epics: Voivodship chambers, Streletsky, Cossack. For newlyweds, there are Chasozvon Tower apartments - Wedding. All numbers reflect the main specifics of the decoration of Ancient Russia: they have Russian stoves, antique clocks, carpets sewn from pieces of fabric.

In the White Owl tavern, everything is done in such a way that when you go inside you feel like you are in a real Russian hut: there is a huge stove, wooden furniture, hunting trophies. And, of course, food, including Siberian delicacies: dumplings with lingonberries, dumplings from beef or hare, dishes from raw fish and meat, venison and much more.

The recreation center located near Tobolsk operates year-round. In summer, boat and catamaran rental, hunting, fishing, horseback riding are organized for guests. A picnic area with barbecue facilities and gazebos is equipped. In winter, visitors can rent skis, snowmobiles and sledges. A ski slope with a length of 350 meters and a height of about 50 functions on the territory of the complex. The track is equipped according to all safety rules, so anyone can try their hand at it.

Every year, the base hosts the international festival of historical reconstruction "Abalak Field". The program is quite saturated: combat fights of warriors, interactive platforms with various programs. A camp has been set up on the territory, including medieval pottery workshops and forges. All visitors who wish to be in the medieval era can take part.

Holiday Village "Fairy Tale"

Tobolsk is known not only for the tourist complex "Abalak". An alternative to it may be another recreation center located in a pine forest in the Stroitel 5 microdistrict. It offers guests the following services:

  • two cottages;
  • barbecue area;
  • sauna with steam room;
  • Jacuzzi and pool;
  • equipped children's corner;
  • Russian billiards, tennis and much more.

Whatever recreation center in Tobolsk you choose to organize your leisure time, the time spent there will definitely be remembered by you for a long time.
