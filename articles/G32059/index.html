Violet Hotel: hotel description and reviews

Nha Trang is a city located in Vietnam. The ancient resort, whose name means "reed river", has earned wide popularity among aristocrats and connoisseurs of measured relaxation. The cozy atmosphere of the town is complemented by fantastic natural resources, picturesque bays, surrounded by a chain of majestic mountain archipelagos and tropical islands.

The beauty of the local landscape, combined with long beaches and oriental hospitality attracts millions of tourists. Hotel complexes of Nha Trang are distinguished by high service and versatile leisure. The price range in hotels is quite wide and allows every tourist to feel comfortable. To save your budget, we recommend booking rooms at the three-star Violet Hotel.

Among the palm thickets and tropical plantings, a high-rise building of seven floors, consisting of 72 rooms, is comfortably located. The following room categories are available for guests:

  • Senior Deluxe.
  • Deluxe
  • Superior




Each room has an attached balcony, which opens an amazing panorama of the azure sea, mountains or park area. For a comfortable stay in the rooms installed air conditioning. In the individual use of the bathroom with a bath or shower - you do not have to share the toilet with strangers.

The room fund of the Violet Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) is equipped with cable TV, sleeping furniture, a large wardrobe, a desk / coffee table. In the presence of a safe, refrigerator with a bar, direct dial telephone, hairdryer. Room service is available 24 hours a day.

Catering

The payment includes a morning meal. Depending on the workload of the hotel, tables can be served on the buffet system or a custom menu is available. Meals at Violet Hotel are always fresh, full of pastries, seasonal fruits and vegetables.





Guests can visit the local restaurant and bar, which serves national and international cuisine. Not far from the residential building are concentrated numerous cafeterias with different culinary orientations. The menu has a wide selection of fish delicacies, rice cakes stuffed with interesting products, and other delicacies.

Coastline

A 10-minute walk from Violet Hotel (Vietnam) is the municipal coast, surrounded by tropical plants and coconut trees. The beach is scattered with many bars and restaurants offering delicious national food. Those who wish can rent beach equipment and enjoy a stay at the sea all day.





On the shore there are entertainment for every taste and budget. The most popular activity is fishing. Many hire a boat or yacht and go fishing for scallops, tuna and other living creatures found in the sea. In addition to fishing, you will be offered surfing, beach volleyball, and water-skiing. Scuba diving is organized.

Business services

Hotel Violet Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) is an ideal place for a business traveler. The administration is pleased to offer a full range of business services for productive work: unhindered access to Wi-Fi, multimedia technologies, delivery of fresh correspondence, a workplace. The individual requests of each guest are taken into account.

If you need to hold an important meeting, organize a seminar, corporate event of any orientation or present a product, then for these purposes a conference room for 80 people with a wide screen, a large table, air conditioning, a microphone, and video equipment is provided. Available - photocopier , fax, telephone, printer.

Children's leisure

The tourist complex of Nha Trang resort has created favorable conditions for young children. The employees tried to do everything possible so that the children's holidays were carefree and joyful. In the courtyard of the hotel is equipped with a playground with modern equipment: swings, slides, horizontal bars, sandbox. Parents can use the services of a professional nanny who will look after the children in their absence.
