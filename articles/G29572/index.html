Krasnodar Territory, Inal Bay. Holidays on the Black Sea

There are many beautiful places in the Krasnodar Territory , one of which is Inal Bay. Rest here can be compared with heavenly enjoyment. This bay is a popular tourist attraction among residents of Russia. In order to come from the city of Krasnodar to the bay, it is necessary to overcome the distance of 130 km, and from Tuapse - 60 km. Every year, tens of thousands of tourists come to this area. For their resettlement in the bay built numerous recreation centers. There are about 100 of them here. They are located mainly on the road, so finding the necessary object of labor will not be.

Why is Inal Bay attractive ? Rest here is not limited to swimming in the sea and sunbathing on the beach. Thanks to the unique climate and blue clay, which is a lot in the area, you can also get better. There are various spas in the bay that offer healing treatments.



Excursion programs

Due to its unique nature and favorable climate, Inal Bay enjoys increasing popularity. Holidays here are ideal for people of all ages. What kind of entertainment are offered to tourists? Unfortunately, there are no attractions in the bay itself. But this does not mean that people will have nothing to see. Given the well-developed infrastructure and service, interesting excursions are often organized here. The program includes trips to Tuapse and walks along the Black Sea coast. During excursions, it is proposed to visit local history and art museums, theaters, historical and architectural monuments, a dolphinarium and other entertainment venues. You can also go for a walk in Kadosh Park or in the mountains of Chessy. In the Tuapse district there are many mysterious caves, canyons and rocks. A very interesting excursion to the volcano Indyskho. Those who wish are even invited to visit Krasnodar. Such a rich vacation is to the liking of many tourists. That is why they return from year to year to Inal Bay.

On vacation to Inal Bay

Excellent weather conditions, clean air, plenty of vegetation, warm sea - all this awaits tourists in Inal Bay. Rest in these places can not overshadow. Due to the fact that there is not a single industrial facility nearby, this area is recognized as environmentally friendly. There are deposits of blue clay right at the bottom of the sea, this makes it possible for vacationers to take mud baths. The most convenient place is the lake, located at the foot of the mountain. Scientists have proven that blue clay has not only healing properties, but also a rejuvenating effect. In order to enhance its effect, it is necessary at the end of the procedure to plunge into sea water.

The most visited places are the central beach and the promenade. The latter was built recently. It is very cozy and comfortable. Private sector

All tourists who want to save on accommodation are advised to pay attention to the private sector. Locals are happy to rent cozy houses and rooms for rent. It is worth noting that if you choose this accommodation in Inal Bay, rest from this will not become less comfortable. The accommodation is equipped with a TV, air conditioning, necessary furniture, as well as a telephone. Some residents rent equipped cottages. On their territory there is a guarded parking lot, saunas, pools and gazebos with barbecue facilities. As a rule, such cottages are located near the beach. These are the conditions provided by locals coming to rest in Inal Bay. Those who have already visited these places leave only positive reviews.

The recreation center "Inal"
