Phuket Kata Resort Hotel Reviews

Phuket Kata Resort is a great option for those who plan to spend their holidays in a quiet and secluded place.





Brief description of the hotel

Phuket Kata Resort is a beautiful resort hotel, the territory of which is a dense and green tropical garden. This creates a special atmosphere of tranquility, relaxation and unity with nature. It is worth noting the design of this hotel. This is a successful combination of modern architectural and interior trends, as well as Thai traditions and decorative elements. Three clean pools fit perfectly into this picture, in which both adults and children will enjoy spending time.

At Phuket Kata Resort, great attention is paid to the organization of leisure guests. For this purpose, an excellent tourist infrastructure has been developed. The pools have water slides, as well as a gym and a tour desk, where you can help you choose an interesting and eventful route. After the entertainment, you can comfortably relax in a spacious and bright room, where you will find a beautiful interior and many diverse amenities.

Particular attention in the resort and hotel complex Phuket Kata Resort is given to food. For this purpose, there are as many as three restaurants on its territory that specialize in cuisines of different directions. You can also eat and drink your favorite drinks at the bars. At the hotel you can always listen to live music and enjoy a cozy and at the same time romantic atmosphere.





Resort Location

Hotel Phuket Kata Resort is located in Phuket, or rather, in the village of Kata. It is approximately 45 minutes drive from the international airport. The hotel is located quite secluded, which will provide you with peace and quiet during your vacation. If you want noisy entertainment, then, after driving 15 minutes, you can be in Patong.





List of guest apartments

Phuket Kata Resort (Phuket) offers travelers to stay in one of the 39 rooms, which are divided into several categories, namely:

- The standard room can be equipped with one or two beds. This is a fairly compact apartment, which is a very comfortable bedroom with a bright design. There is also the possibility of placing an extra bed.

- Deluxe apartments are reminiscent of standard rooms in terms of equipment and interior. Nevertheless, their area is much larger, which gives a feeling of space and comfort.

On the ground floor of the hotel there is a separate class of apartments that have their own access to the pool through the veranda. The terrace also has plastic furniture (table and chairs), a special clothes dryer.

In the depths of the garden, as well as near the beach are small villas. They can accommodate up to four guests, taking into account additional beds. Each of the villas has a terrace. The cost of living may vary depending on the location relative to the beach.

Guest Room Features

At Phuket Kata Resort Hotel, guests will be invited to take advantage of the following apartment amenities:

  • to provide an interesting and exciting pastime in the room will be able to modern TV, which is connected to a large package of satellite channels with many entertainment and music programs;
  • at any time of the day your favorite drinks will be available to you due to the presence of a minibar in the apartment;
  • You can store the products you bought in a small compact refrigerator;
  • if you need to make an international call or ask a question to the reception staff, you can use a landline phone;
  • the bathroom has a shower, built-in electric hair dryer, as well as a full range of bath accessories and disposable branded cosmetics;
  • individual air conditioning allows you to create your own comfortable climate in your room.




Positive reviews

Tourists have long been fond of the Phuket Kata Resort. Reviews about this institution are mostly positive, which gives the right to judge the high level of service at the hotel. It is worth noting the following comments of guests:

  • the hotel is quite well located - it is located in a secluded corner and far from the road (nevertheless, there is a market very close by where you can buy food and souvenirs at an affordable price as a souvenir);
  • on the way to the sea (which takes no more than 10 minutes) you will meet small shops and cafes, as well as a pharmacy and a salon where they make a wonderful massage);
  • the apartments are spacious and bright, with an original bright design, as well as all the necessary amenities;
  • the rooms are thoroughly cleaned daily, and towels and bed linen are changed;
  • the hotel has a huge parking lot with enough space, even if each of the guests wants to leave a rented car or bike on it;
  • there are three swimming pools in the resort complex, one of which is equipped with hydromassage, and the children's one has water slides.

This hotel can be considered a real oasis for travelers. The main plus is its location in a quiet remote area, which guarantees you a relaxing stay. Nevertheless, there is a market, shops, and cafes, which will allow you to enjoy all the benefits of urban civilization. The hotel is beautiful both outside and inside. Beautiful architecture, a green garden and the original interior of the rooms - all this creates a holistic picture that cannot be admired.
