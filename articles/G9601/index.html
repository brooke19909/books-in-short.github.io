Park Hotel Volzhskaya Riviera, Saratov: review, description and reviews

Sometimes it seems that the traditional and only true meaning of the word "Riviera" (Italian-French coast of the Ligurian Sea) has long been forgotten. Travel agencies are proud to offer you a huge number of options with the same name, and not only in our country. The riviera, in fact, has become the personification of the coast, luxurious relaxation and high service. If there is a large river and a warm climate, then that is where it is to be. In Russia, everyone has heard the Volga Riviera: Saratov, Kazan, Uglich, Astrakhan, etc. In short, this is the Volga region, surrounding one of the largest rivers in the world - the Volga. When planning a trip, one cannot help but think about where to stay.

The article will focus on one of the popular hotels in the city of Saratov - the Volga Riviera.

Location





Comfortable and popular among tourists, the hotel is located in an ecologically clean area, away from noise and city bustle. The name of the suburban health-improving complex where the Riviera is located is Volzhsky Dali. Saratov with all its sights, a cultural and entertainment life is located just fifteen kilometers from the place. The locality in which the hotel is directly located is the Pristannoye settlement. In order to get to the Volga coast and the beach, you need to walk only 200-300 meters.

Volzhskaya Riviera Hotel (Saratov): description

The hotel at the level of cost of one night stay belongs to the middle price segment. It is a five-story building with comfortable rooms, a wide range of services, responsive and competent staff, and quality service. The year of construction (last reconstruction) of the building is 2009. The hotel has everything you need, an elevator, and free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

Number of rooms

The hotel has in its arsenal of 59 comfortable rooms of various levels and prices. The price of accommodation at the Volzhskaya Riviera Hotel (Saratov) always includes breakfast for two people and the use of wireless Internet. A pleasant bonus for owners of four-legged pets will be the fact that pets are allowed. However, you must inform the administration in advance, even at the stage of booking.

Beach near the hotel

For its guests, the Volzhskaya Riviera Hotel (Saratov) offers certain recreational activities. The name of the hotel itself indicates that the rest is associated primarily with the beach. Saratov, located along the Volga, has a large number of city beaches. Not all of them are well equipped and comfortable. Hotel Volga Riviera is located in an ecologically clean place, surrounded by nature and near the river.

The hotel's beach is gentle, equipped with special awnings that protect from the scorching sun in the summer. Various water attractions and entertainments are organized on the shore.

Trophy fishing on the Volga





The hotel is often perceived as a camp site. The Volga Riviera (Saratov) offers its guests a unique and exclusive entertainment - fishing on the Volga. The river is a real fishing paradise, replete with a large number of species, a good catch is guaranteed to absolutely everyone regardless of skill and experience. Not far from the hotel is a small island measuring 5 by 10 kilometers. At different times of the year, in its vicinity you can catch pike perch, pike, asp, catfish, perch, head, rudd, bream and other types of fish.

A boat is provided for hire, including with a motor, fishing tackle, as well as necessary accessories for processing and preparing the catch: barbecue and firewood. Not only avid lovers can go fishing, but also the whole family. There is a playground for children, water rides.

For the most demanding guests, the staff of the Volzhskaya Riviera Hotel (Saratov) organize amazing trophy fishing. An experienced team will take you to the best waters of the Volga, because, as you know, fish is looking for where deeper. Volga Riviera (Saratov): reviews

Saratov hotel has a good reputation. Its rating, in comparison with other accommodation options in this region, is above average and corresponds to an assessment of four points out of five possible.
