1. Microloan

There are many companies with names like Fast Money and Borrow Before. Points where you can urgently borrow money are located at almost every step in any city.

A microloan is a small loan. Usually up to 30 or up to 50 thousand rubles for a short period of time - a month or two. The interest rate depends on the amount and term. Each company has a different one, therefore this information should be clarified directly with a loan officer.

This is a good option, giving an answer to the question of where to get the money urgently on your passport. It is only necessary to choose wisely a company that offers fully understandable and transparent conditions before taking such a step, because one unsuccessful attempt can drag a person into a debt hole.

The benefits are as follows:

  • It is issued very quickly and there is no need to provide any documents additionally. Only a form is required.
  • Guarantors or co-borrowers are not needed.
  • The purpose of obtaining a small loan is not of interest to the company.
  • There are offices that issue microloans over the Internet. It is enough to indicate the details of the bank card on the site. Refunds are also made by electronic payments. No need to go anywhere and wait long in line.
  • If the above method is difficult, the nearest point of issuance of small loans will cope with this too quickly.

Important:

  • You need to take only the necessary amount of funds - a penny in a penny, without rounding. The surplus will be wasted, but it will have to be given in the end. Of course, the company's employees will persuade a potential client to take such a step, but you should be aware of what all this can turn out for the loan recipient.
  • It is necessary to repay the debt on time. Earlier it is possible, later it is impossible. Delay threatens with large fines and an increase in interest, which ultimately leads to overpayments and poor credit history. Where to get the money later if everyone will eventually refuse such an unreliable borrower?
  • If the payment delay was allowed, you should immediately contact a company representative and discuss the situation in order to avoid undesirable consequences and uncomfortable negotiations. According to the law No. 230- dated 07/03/2017, the borrower's risks are reduced today. Companies no longer need to literally ‚Äúfill up‚Äù the debtor with fines, exacerbating the situation. According to statistics, only 30% of such overdue loans were eventually paid. Therefore, the above law regulated the size of the penalty for delay. But it should be understood that the further the customer delays the deadline for resolving the payment incident, the less chance of refinancing.

2. Bank lending

Another place where to get money with a further return is the bank. Consumer credit is issued for a large amount and for a long time: months, or even years. Accordingly, the percentage is lower than on a microloan.

Consumer loans are usually resorted to starting a personal business or acquiring a car. The method is not for those who think where to get money on credit without failure, because the bank checks the client for compliance with the following points:

  • The presence of a constant and stable income. If the borrower has not worked anywhere for six months and, accordingly, will not be able to provide a certificate of income, then it is likely that the bank will refuse to issue.
  • Credit history without violations. Means that previously taken loans were always paid on time and without delay.
  • The bank is entitled to refuse without explanation. To increase the likelihood of approval, you should stock up on a surety. This is a person who also puts his signature in the contract and agrees to repay the loan in case of financial insolvency of the borrower. It should only be taken into account that not everyone will agree to this. It should be either a very good friend, ready to go for it, or a relative. You need to know that a unfamiliar person who asked to vouch for him when drawing up a loan agreement may turn out to be a fraudster and disappear with money, and all payment obligations will be transferred to the ‚Äúvictim‚Äù.

The bank may require anything in pledge as a guarantee that the borrower will be able to repay everything. In case of termination of payments, the mortgaged property may be withdrawn from the owner. Most often, a car or an apartment is left as collateral, and the bank resorts to this measure in the case of mortgage lending.

This is also not very simple and does not suit those who are looking for where to get money without failure. Getting a mortgage is even more difficult, but it is also an option to purchase a large amount to buy a home or car. Therefore, when a question arises about where you can borrow money, you should think about whether it will be possible to redeem a thing from a pawnshop. Otherwise, it is better to choose a different method.
