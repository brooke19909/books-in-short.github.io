Valueology is a science of health. Valueology as a science: its goals and content

From ancient times, man understood that the most important thing in the life of every intelligent creature is health. Without it, neither wealth, nor honor, nor glory will be needed. That is why the medical profession is one of the oldest, most respected and sought after in the history of human civilization.

What is valeology?

So what kind of science is this - valeology? To answer this question, we turn to the etymology of the name. The term is formed from two components of Latin origin: "valeo" or "valere" - to be healthy, and the well-known "logos" - doctrine, study.

So it turns out that valeology is the science of the art of being healthy, leading a healthy, proper, active lifestyle, and accustoming to it from childhood.

From the side it may seem that this science does not carry anything complicated in itself. However, it is not. Indeed, valeology considers health comprehensively, holistically, including all its sides and aspects, and studying from all sides and options (mental, social, mental, moral, physical). Due to the global nature of the concept, valeology turns out to be connected with a whole complex of other sciences.

Subject and object of study

What does the science of valeology study? The main subject and object of its study are:

  • physical and genetic health mechanisms inherent in every organism;
  • a person is integrated, including all its components, which affect the state of any kind and type of health.

If we talk about valeology as an object, then the main object of its study will be pedagogical foundations aimed at forming an independent desire in children and adults to achieve a healthy lifestyle.





That is, to make sure that each person himself understands the importance of his condition and strives to maintain his normal position and strengthen and maintain the body's natural immunoprotective properties without the use of drugs. Roughly speaking, carry out the prevention of diseases, conscious and focused.

Goals of this science

There is a definite goal pursued by valueology as a science. Its objectives and content are as follows:

  1. Educate people on a healthy lifestyle (HLS).
  2. To instill the basics of the rules of strengthening and restoring the health of the body without the use of medications.
  3. To teach the construction of the correct life schedule: nutrition, sports, giving up bad habits, observing basic hygiene rules, timely access to medical care.

These goals automatically entail the solution of the following problems of valeology.

The tasks of valeology

Valeology as a science of health solves the following tasks:

  • study of the inner potential of each person‚Äôs health;
  • health assessment for all generally accepted medical indicators;
  • the desire for the formation of an internal installation of healthy lifestyle for people;
  • health promotion and disease prevention through the application of healthy lifestyles.




If science succeeds in solving all of the above problems, then humanity will move to a whole new level in terms of maintaining health. The number of diseases of any plan and nature will decrease, the nationwide level of brain and physical activity will increase, the number of pathologies at the birth of offspring will decrease, the number of disabled and incurable patients will decrease.

Methods of Valeology

Valueology is a science of health, which means that it uses a certain set of methods for research. What are these methods?

  1. Methods of research and assessment of the physical and mental state of a person using computer diagnostics.
  2. Methodology of pedagogical observation of subjects during the educational process. The goal is to accumulate a theoretical basis for deeper analysis and research.
  3. An experiment (most often pedagogical). It is carried out together with subjects for visual confirmation of a particular postulate of valeology and proof of the importance of its value.
  4. Sociomethodology. These methods include questionnaires, opinion polls and other types of social interactions in order to identify average statistics on various issues of human health and to accumulate a theoretical basis for study and analysis.
  5. Mathematical methods. They are necessary for processing the data obtained in the previous paragraph.
  6. Comprehensive analysis of pedagogical ideas. It is necessary to form conclusions on issues related to children (their upbringing, learning healthy lifestyles, and so on).




The combination and complex application of all methods of valeology research allows you to get very good results with positive development dynamics. And not only among children, but also among adults. So, for example, this led to the fact that the 21st century was marked by a "fashion for health." That is, now to maintain a healthy lifestyle - it is relevant, fashionable, youth and healthy. It is foolish to deny in this a deliberate long-term impact on children's consciousness, namely, valeology and related sciences.

Where and when is valeology studied?

Valeology is studied mainly in the course (school) or as a student discipline. After all, it is clear that the foundation of healthy lifestyle should be laid precisely at these ages. In the school course, this subject most often takes the form of semi-game, semi-cognitive lessons called "Hello, the science of valeology!"

At the same time, it is important to make sure that classes in valeology are not of a heavy forced nature, but are perceived as something light, exciting. Then the children will be happy to absorb all the knowledge that this science wants to convey.

To do this, it is better to choose non-standard forms of conducting lessons, and select topics so that there is always the possibility of practical research, a little laboratory work. Quizzes, puzzles, competitions should be arranged more often, and information and communication technologies should be used during classes.





With students, everything is a little simpler and more serious. These are almost adults, many of whom, unfortunately, have already been or are exposed to the antagonists of valeology: smoking, alcohol, drug addiction, daily consumption of fast food, physical inactivity and so on.

Therefore, a whole serious course is needed here, which includes a number of theoretical and practical exercises, which will cover all the issues of valeology as a whole and will show its significance.

It is best to play in contrast and give an idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe difference between those who maintain a healthy lifestyle, and between those who refuse it, to describe the benefits of such a lifestyle. Valueology is the science of human health, and therefore students should understand how important it is, this is health itself and its timely preservation.

The role of this science in school

Even from school, it is very important to make it clear to the child how important it is to be healthy. After all, children have slightly different values, and they begin to understand the full significance of health only after they become ill with something. Therefore, you need to try to explain to them that valeology is the science of health, its preservation, disease prevention and strengthening the general physical condition of people.

Best of all, children will understand this if we give them an example of specific cases of people's lives before they thought about healthy lifestyles, and after they began to actively conduct it. Show what hereditary diseases and human tragedies can be avoided if you turn in time and listen to your body.





It is also important to teach children to independently compose for themselves a competent and correct dietary diet in terms of healthy lifestyle, daily routines and do not forget to pay attention to physical exercises.

With the proper organization of such classes, valeology will certainly leave a true imprint on the consciousness of children. And this, in turn, will entail only positive dynamics in their future lifestyle.
