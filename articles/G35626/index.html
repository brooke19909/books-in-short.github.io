Hotel Hanoi Golden Hotel 3 *, Nha Trang, Vietnam: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Hanoi Golden Hotel 3 * hotel offers its guests an unforgettable vacation that meets high standards of service. The ratio of the cost of living and quality will pleasantly surprise you.





Short hotel description

Hanoi Golden Hotel 3 * hotel offers its guests a fairly budget, but quite comfortable stay. The equipment and decoration of the rooms is given special attention. All apartments are very spacious, bright and bright, as well as equipped with all necessary amenities so that tourists can feel as comfortable here as they do at home.

Hotel Hanoi Golden Hotel 3 * offers tourists many services that will make their stay at the hotel pleasant and convenient. Here you can get both transport or excursion services, as well as those related to banking operations or the work of postal services. You will also have the opportunity to rent a car, and for tourists more modest - a bicycle or a scooter.

The cuisine of Vietnam is quite specific, both in terms of taste, and in terms of products and the cooking process. In the restaurant of the Hanoi Golden Hotel 3 * you can taste many national dishes, as well as enjoy the usual European cuisine. Also nearby there are many cafes and other catering establishments where you can make many gastronomic discoveries for yourself.





Hotel location

Hotel Hanoi Golden 4 Hotel 3 * is characterized by a rather favorable location. Very close to the hotel there are many shops, cafes and other objects of the urban resort infrastructure. Just a 5-minute drive away, or a 15-minute walk, there is a huge local market where you can buy ethnic clothes and jewelry, souvenirs from Vietnam, and local fruits and other foodstuffs quite inexpensively. A little further is the famous Ponagar Temple, which should definitely be visited by any tourist.

How is the Hanoi Golden Hotel located on the coast? 1 line. Hotel 3 * is located very close to the beach and the picturesque Changfu promenade. From the center of Nha Trang, where this hotel is located, to the airport by car or by bus about 45 minutes drive.

Useful hotel information

The Hanoi Golden Hotel 3 * (Nha) has a number of internal rules that are required to comply with both staff and hotel guests. These include the following items:

  • check-in and check-out is carried out at 2 pm and noon, respectively;
  • subject to the availability of a sufficient number of free apartments in the hotel, guests can use the services of early check-in or late check-out for an additional fee;
  • Children under the age of 5 stay at the hotel for free (provided that they do not require the installation of an extra bed);

  • the hotel has a cashless payment system;
  • The hotel has the right to block a certain amount of funds on your credit card until the date of your arrival.
