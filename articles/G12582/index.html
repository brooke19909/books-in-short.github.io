Prague, Hotel Ambassador: address, description, photos and reviews of tourists

Prague is considered to be one of the most beautiful cities in Europe. If you want to immerse yourself in its unique atmosphere, then the hotel should be appropriate. One of the favorite accommodations for guests of Prague is the Ambassador Hotel.

Name history

The full name of the popular hotel in Prague is Ambassador Zlata Gusa. Of interest is precisely the second part of the name of the institution. Once the Ambassador Hotel occupied only part of the building on Wenceslas Square. The second belonged to a wealthy widow. She had a daughter-in-law. The girl was so ugly that, despite the fabulously rich dowry, no one took her as a wife. Locals nicknamed the girl "Golden Goose." When both hotels were combined, it was decided to keep this story and give the institution a double name.

  • Junior Suite (40 sq. M.) - a spacious studio room in which the bedroom is combined with the living space. It can be equipped with large or twin beds, as well as a daybed for additional accommodation. There is also upholstered furniture, a workplace and a small dining table with chairs. Price - 103 euros / 7777 rubles.
  • Family apartments (50 sq. M.) - a spacious two-room suite. One of the rooms is a small bedroom, and the second is a bedroom combined with a living space. Two couches can be provided for extra seating. Price - 118 euros / 8900 rubles.
