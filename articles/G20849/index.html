Origami fish for the smallest

The origami technique is popular today. This type of creative development is carried out by both children and their parents. To make a masterpiece from a piece of paper is real fantasy. Origami fish can be the first product that, together with a child, can be made for a game or an interesting picture. Such a figure may be of varying degrees of difficulty. Of course, for the first time you need to choose something easier, for example, a whale or a scalar. These fish are the most uncomplicated in manufacturing techniques, they are easy to make even the smallest kids.

Necessary materials for work

Before you make origami fish, you need to stock up with the necessary stationery and tools. You will need:

  • Colored paper in various shapes and sizes.
  • Ruler for drawing the necessary elements on paper.
  • Scissors.
  • Felt-tip pens.
  • Various beads, shiny ribbons for the decor of the finished paper origami. After all, the fish can be additionally decorated

Step by step instructions for making an origami whale

To make origami fish beautiful and voluminous, it is best to follow the instructions in the manufacturing process. The steps for creating a whale are as follows:





  • First you need to prepare two square sheets of paper.
  • They need to be placed one on the second so that the bottom sheet has a small ledge from under the top.
  • Bend the right side so that it occupies exactly half the sheet.
  • The same thing needs to be done with the left side.
  • Lay the tail remaining on top exactly in the center of the upper part of the product, bending it.
  • Then take both sheets from the bottom and lift up, this will be the tail.
  • After that, you can do the decoration of the product by drawing or gluing eyes, scales.

Step-by-step instructions for fish scalars

Such an origami fish will turn out even for children from six years of age and older. The implementation process is quite simple and affordable. To do this, follow these steps:





  • Take a square sheet of paper at least 12x12 centimeters in size and fold it into a triangle.
  • Then it is necessary to fold the other side as well so that cross-shaped bends form on the sheet.
  • After this, you need to fold the sheet with protruding bends into a triangle shape.
  • We bend the triangular ends on the sides of the line to get a frog.
  • The fish is ready, you just need to cut out the shape of the tail and fins with scissors.
  • It remains only to draw the eyes, scales and mouth for the final completion of the work.

Origami fish ready for swimming. You can glue it on a sheet of paper previously painted under the seabed, or just play with a fish, like an ordinary toy. Using the origami technique, you can create your own hands with a variety of complexity products. This type of creativity helps to develop fine motor skills, perseverance and attentiveness in children of different ages. Therefore, you should pay attention to this type of creativity in order to fully focus on the talents of the baby and create interesting products from plain paper with your own hands. Create, and your efforts will be appreciated by your beloved child.
