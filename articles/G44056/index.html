Hotel "Princess Adriana" (Rhodes / Greece): holidays, photos and reviews

Choosing a vacation spot in Greece, many prefer the sunny island of Rhodes, which is washed by the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas. Most resorts are focused on a quiet and secluded pastime, especially those located in the eastern part. This is also Kiotari - a picturesque corner, buried in cypress and pine forests, olive groves and coniferous vegetation.





This is a relatively young tourist resort in Rhodes with well-developed infrastructure, long beaches and luxury hotels. It was here that in 2011 the modern and comfortable Princess Adriana complex was erected. Rhodes is a colorful place where time suspends its course. Arriving at the Princess Andriana hotel you will get the maximum satisfaction from your vacation, see firsthand the ancient architecture of the resort, visit the great sights and enjoy the local hospitality.

What conditions do you have to live in?





The five-star complex "Princess Adriana Resort" (Rhodes) is located on a beautiful landscaped area, surrounded on all sides by tropical trees, tall palm trees and flowering shrubs. Landscaping impresses with well-groomed and magnificent. The hotel consists of three-story cottages and the main building, which is located across the road.

In total, the complex has 380 rooms. Available chic Superior Junior Suite apartments located in snow-white buildings with a pool and relaxation area. Family rooms are offered for couples with stylish designs, home appliances and the Internet.





It should be noted that all categories of rooms are decorated in pastel colors, furnished with new modern furniture, have a separate bathroom and air conditioning. There are rooms for people with physical disabilities (disabled). For this category of guests there is an elevator and ramps.

Coastline

The golden beaches, warm azure sea and the rich natural beauty of the famous island of Rhodes. Hotel Princess Adriana is located about 300 meters from the coast. The beach belongs to the complex, it is regularly cleaned of debris and large stones. You can get to it through the underpass.





All beach facilities, such as a sun lounger and sunshade, are available for free use by hotel guests. There are entertainments for lovers of calm and relaxed leisure, as well as for active travelers who are thirsty for thrills. It offers diving, pedal boats, a parachute, jet skis and other water sports.

Food concept





The hotel complex "Princess Adriana" (Rhodes) operates on an all-inclusive program. Meals are served in buffet style at Medallion Restaurant. The menu offers exquisite Mediterranean, national and European cuisine. Meat delicacies abound, many fish dishes and desserts to the delight of the sweet tooth.

Themed parties are regularly held here , where guests can completely appreciate the dishes of different cuisines and taste drinks. Several other noteworthy establishments are open on the territory where you will be deliciously and heartily fed: Fresh, All Day and Royal bars, Moonlight and Coral restaurants.

Entertainment and leisure activities at the Princess Adriana Hotel (Rhodes / Greece)





The complex is famous for its own SPA center, which houses a gym with a sauna, Turkish bath, solarium, jacuzzi and massage room. For guests there is a hairdresser, manicure and pedicure salons. If you wish, you will be individually selected for physical exercises under the guidance of a qualified instructor.

The main highlight, so to speak, of this hotel is the swimming pool with a multi-colored musical fountain. In the evening, an amazing fairy tale spectacle begins: everything around shines with lights, shimmers with different colors to melodic music. The magic extravaganza simply enchants and hypnotizes those around you.





Some guests come to this island of Rhodes for this exciting sight. The Princess Adriana hotel is in great demand among discerning tourists. A real festive atmosphere reigns here, and carelessness is felt, which is so lacking in everyday life. It offers grandiose animation programs, numerous sports games (tennis, billiards, soccer, darts) and a disco.

Additional service

A conference room with technical equipment is provided for the comfortable holding of various events and meetings. You can use photocopy and fax if necessary. The internal infrastructure of the Princess Adriana Hotel (Rhodes) is aimed at making the stay of guests secluded and carefree.

On its territory there is an exchange office, travel agency and dry cleaning with laundry. Private parking is available to guests, as well as transport rental and a chain of retail stores.

Rest for a children's audience





At a high level, leisure activities are organized for young guests. For children interesting programs are constantly held with animators, sports competitions for teenagers and fun contests. Even the smallest tourists will not get bored: there is a separate pool, a development club, a playground. Parents can use the services of a nanny, which will occupy the child during the absence of adults.

Five-star complex "Princess Adriana" (Rhodes): reviews of tourists

Vivid emotions and a storm of positive impressions overwhelm people who have visited this complex. Without exception, everyone liked the magnificent design of the hotel. The luxury of the surrounding interior and the hospitality of the staff help to truly relax and enjoy.

An unforgettable vacation will give a warm and friendly Greece (Rhodes). Princess Adriana Hotel is a paradise, attracting with excellent service and affordable prices.
