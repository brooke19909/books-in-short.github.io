How to remove bad odor from a washing machine

Freshly washed linen, from which a musty spirit emanates, even a Buddhist lama can get out of a state of nirvana. You should not test your own nervous system if you have such a problem. You just need to remove the unpleasant odor from the washing machine. How to do it? Read valuable recommendations and use the advice that is most appropriate for your case.



The smell of a washing machine: types and causes

  1. If the smell of molten plastic comes from the new washing machine, it is a manufacturing defect.
  2. Sewer odor from the washing machine may indicate that the water drain from the appliance is not installed correctly.
  3. When heated, some types of washing powders tend to exude an unpleasant aroma.
  4. Persistent stench of mold occurs due to improper maintenance of the equipment. It is possible that the hatch door of the turned off washing machine is constantly closed, or the laundry that has been washed is not removed for a long time from the mechanism, or dirty things are not stored in the basket intended for this purpose, but in the drum of the machine.




How to remove the smell of a washing machine?

  1. Call the master or simply return the washing machine to the seller if you just purchased it.
  2. A plumber summoned to the house will easily eliminate the cause of the stench.
  3. Change the detergent in consultation with the seller.
  4. Do not close the door after washing, let residual moisture evaporate naturally. Things should not be in the washing machine while waiting for the next wash.

Remedies

  • When changing the washing powder, pour half a glass of new detergent into the drum, close the hatch, and turn on the washing machine to the highest possible temperature. Turn off the spin and rinse systems. Let the machine idle.
  • The unpleasant odor from the washing machine may disappear when changing the filter.
  • To remove mold, moisten a sponge with a thick solution of regular drinking soda and carefully remove unwanted formations. After that, wipe the treated area with table vinegar diluted with water 1: 1. If mold again appears on the rubber parts of the washing machine, replace them with new ones.
  • Pour 100 g of citric acid into the drum and idle the machine at the highest possible temperature. The dirt adhered to ten over time will disappear.
  • Liquid bleach eliminates unpleasant odors. Pour 2 cups into the washing machine without any laundry and turn on the 90-95-degree operation without rinsing and spinning.
