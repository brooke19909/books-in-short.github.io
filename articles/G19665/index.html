Healing factors





Mountain resort Arshan (Irkutsk region) is famous for sulfate-magnesium waters, which are shown for many pathologies. They have practically the same beneficial properties with the Kislovodsk narzan. They are used both inside and in the form of bathtubs. According to doctors, hydrocarbon sources help to cure gastrointestinal and cardiovascular diseases, metabolic disorders, diseases of the nervous system, gall bladder and liver.

In addition to mineral waters, silt mud is used for therapeutic purposes, which treat the pathological processes of the circulatory system, diseases of the endocrine and urinary systems. In the village of Arshan (Irkutsk region) come for rehabilitation after surgery.





Accommodations

The resort has two large sanatoriums - "Arshan" and "Sayan". As well as many small hotels and private hotels. You will be offered a lot of options for inexpensive accommodation near mineral springs. Pensions provide professional treatment. They also specialize in fasting, which is important for patients with gastrointestinal diseases. The course of therapy includes mineral water intake, mud therapy, balneotherapy, inhalation, physiotherapy, gymnastics, massage, speleotherapy and phytoaromatherapy.

Leisure in health resorts

In boarding houses of the town of Arshan (Irkutsk Oblast), entertainment and sports programs are well-organized. Both resorts have a swimming pool, a gym, a sauna, a library, a billiard room, a disco club and a concert hall. There is a tour agency and a point for issuing sports equipment. You can spend your holidays productively, fun and at ease in the balneological centers of the village of Arshan (Irkutsk Region). The map will tell you the right direction if you are driving your own car. Pleasantly surprised by the price of accommodation and the level of service.
