How to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar - all ways

Kyivstar is one of the leading mobile operators in Ukraine. One of the services that are provided to customers is the transfer of funds. Many users are interested in the question: how to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar? We will consider this topic in this article.

Why is the ability to transfer money from one account to another important?

Mobile operators have introduced a money transfer service not so long ago. Earlier, when mobile communication in our country just appeared, subscribers did not have such an opportunity. If a person ran out of money in the account, he had to solve the problem on his own.





From time to time, such moments happen when a person can replenish the balance only after some time. For example, the subscriber is in a village where there are no shops, or on the road in a train. And to call and solve business issues urgently. It is in such cases that the opportunity now offered to transfer money will come in handy. Kyivstar sends them to another subscriber without any problems. Believe me, this convenient service can very much help out in difficult times.

How to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar?

Knowing about the ability to transfer money from one number to another is one thing, and being able to use it is another. After all, it is necessary to master the procedure for transferring funds.





For example, you cannot send less than 5 hryvnias. The operator also set an upper limit of 1000. The subscriber who sends the money should also have at least 5 hryvnias left in the account after the transfer. This is done so that the sender of the transfer can also use the services of Kyivstar for a certain time without the need to urgently replenish the account. The cost of the operation is 50 kopecks.

To order a transfer of funds to the account of another subscriber, you need to dial the following combination on your phone: * 124 * the amount of money that the person wants to transfer, * recipient's phone number # call key.

Consider an example. We need to replenish the number 098 983 75 72 by 10 hryvnias. We dial the following combination on the phone: * 124 * 10 * 0989837572 # call key. Then you need to send the confirmation code that came in the SMS reply to number 125. We dial * 125 * code # call. After that comes a notification about the successful transfer of funds.

Is it possible to transfer from account to account via the Internet?

Technology is constantly moving forward. Therefore, probably, no one will be surprised that such an operation can be done through the World Wide Web.





How to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar via the Internet? Visit the company website. At the bottom of the main page there is a section "Services and entertainment". In it, select the option "Mobile money". You must go to this section. You will see three possible options for continued work. You must select "Transfer funds." By the way, only subscribers who use prepayment can send money.

So, then we select "Mobile operators". In this option, click on Kyivstar. A window will open in front of you, in which you need to fill in several fields:

  • phone number to which the money should be transferred;
  • replenishment amount;
  • Your phone number.

So we learned how to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar in this way. We hope that you now understand how to send funds from one mobile number to another. Using this service, you can help your friends or relatives at the moment when they need help. They, in turn, will always help you if necessary and replenish your account when you ask.
