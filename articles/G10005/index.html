The recreation center "Oasis" (Novosibirsk): photos, address and reviews of tourists

Russia has always been famous for its marvelous nature, clean, full-flowing rivers and mesmerizing mountain peaks. In these places you breathe differently, all hardships are forgotten and go by the wayside. Any person sometimes wants detachment, peace, solitude and peace in order to plunge into the bliss of silence.

Such a place is in the Novosibirsk region, in the Horde district, in a small but very picturesque village with the interesting name Abrashino. Here, among the relict plantations, on the banks of the crystal clear Karakan River, a suburban recreation center "Oasis" was built. Novosibirsk is rightfully proud of this wonderful place, whose frequent guests are eminent artists from show business.

Many people come here for the weekend to go fishing on the spring lake, admire the surroundings and, of course, enjoy amazing home-cooked dishes cooked at the stake. Accommodation options

The doors of the suburban complex under the intriguing name "Oasis" are open all year round. Recreation center (Abrashino) - a corner of freshness near the dusty and noisy metropolis. For a peaceful stay and relaxation, you will be offered several accommodation options.





First of all, a guest three-story cottage overlooking the spring lake. On the ground floor there is a spacious dining room with a kitchen area, an open veranda and a gazebo with barbecue facilities. The second floor is occupied by standard double rooms with soft beds and the necessary arsenal of furniture. Facilities in the hallway.

On the top third floor there are magnificent apartments with their own fireplace, two bedrooms, a kitchen and a bathroom. Expensive pleasure for discerning customers who are used to not denying anything to themselves.





All guests living in the guest house can use the billiard room, play table tennis, dominoes and watch films in the lobby on the second floor. There is a children's room with educational toys.

In addition, to stay in the warm season, the recreation center "Oasis" (Novosibirsk) offers summer "Campings" right on the lake with partial amenities. Everyone can camp in a tent and enjoy a romantic weekend.

Guests staying at the guest house can count on three meals a day. Cafes are open for other tourists, there are barbecue facilities and gazebos, where you can personally prepare dishes. If you do not want to cook food and visit establishments on your own, you can always pay an additional set menu.

Internal infrastructure





Arriving at the complex, as if you are in a paradise, where you are cared for, cared for and cherished. What entertainment is not here - for every taste!
