Hotel-Park Atlas (Domodedovo): reviews

The modern rhythm of life requires a lot of tension from any resident of a large city, especially when it comes to such a metropolis as Moscow. That is why places for recreation of citizens should meet a number of conditions, such as comfort, environmental friendliness, affordability, comprehensiveness, a combination of price and quality, relaxation and recreation.

The Atlas park - hotel (Domodedovo) corresponds to all these conditions, which is conveniently located in relation to the airport and at the same time is in the accessibility zone for business and private trips.

Location

In the Moscow region, on Kashirskoye Shosse, Atlas Park (Hotel) is located. Domodedovo, a famous international airport, is located 15 kilometers from it. Therefore, the hotel is convenient for accommodating guests of the city. It is more than a hotel, and more than a park. This is a whole complex for recreation, recreation, organization of mass and corporate events.



This is a variant of a country hotel in which prices are much more attractive and lower than in the capital, and the conditions are more comfortable and offer a wide variety of choices.
