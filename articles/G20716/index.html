Royal Grand Suite Hotel 4 * (United Arab Emirates, Sharjah): photos and reviews of tourists

Vacationers who decide to relax by the sea can stay in this town in one of the many local hotels. For example, quite often Russian tour operators sell permits to Sharjah with accommodation at the Royal Grand Suite Hotel 4 * . Reviews of vacationers, this hotel has earned quite good.

What you should know about

Accommodation in the Royal Grand Suite Hotel 4 * hotel (Sharjah) , therefore, judging by the reviews, it seems convenient. So says most of the former guests of this hotel. But, unfortunately, this complex has one rather serious drawback. When leaving the hotel, the administration can easily and completely without reason accuse its former guests of stealing small items from the room (mugs, towels, etc.). Unfortunately, many tourists report similar unpleasant incidents in this hotel on the websites of tour operators in the "Reviews" section.

Beach

The Royal Grand Suite Hotel 4 * is located in Sharjah in the UAE , as already mentioned, far from the sea. But for the convenience of guests, the hotel offers a free shuttle to the beach.
