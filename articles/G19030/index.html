How to make a cap from a newspaper? Step-by-step instruction

In hot weather, a cap is a necessary accessory. But it is not always at hand. Creating origami, you can give the newspaper a second life and get the necessary thing. This article will step by step describe how to make a cap from a newspaper.

There are several possible options for the manufacture of hats from newsprint. So, let's look at some examples. For production, you will need a standard sheet of newspaper or other paper, the size of which is at least 53 X 53 cm. A cap from a newspaper will not protect from rain, but it will be an excellent assistant when you are under the scorching rays of the sun. Its use is especially relevant in the garden or during outdoor recreation.

Classic newspaper cap with a visor

1. A square is made from a sheet of newspaper. To do this, one of the corners is laid diagonally. The lower part of the newspaper is superimposed on the resulting triangle and cut off.

2. The sheet is left folded at an angle of 45 degrees or folded. If everything is done correctly, it turns out a triangle.



3. Paper flips the wide side of the triangle down.

4. The upper corner is bent to the center of the lower part of the previously formed figure.



5. The folded angle rises, and the lateral lower parts of the workpiece are laid on top of each other. In appearance, the resulting figure resembles a trapezoid.



6. One of the parts (located on top) of the elevated angle is lowered down and tucked under the folded layers. It is necessary to press it harder for a more secure hold.



7. The workpiece must be turned over to the other side.



8. Then the rest of the triangle is laid down.



9. The model is flipped 180 degrees. After that, the lower part is opened so that the inside of the future cap is visible.

10. Next, you need to click on the sides to give shape.

11. The upper parts are laid so that the inside view exactly matches the picture. Corners bend towards the center and look at each other.



12. The visor is formed at the last stage. First you need to lay it up, then bend it back, leaving 1-2 cm from the fold line.

Done! That's the whole instruction how to make a cap from a newspaper.

A simple option without a visor

This is an easy option for making a cap yourself.



1. We take a sheet of newspaper.

2. Fold in half to form a rectangle.



3. The upper corners are bent toward the middle of the sheet.



4. They snuggle well.

5. One of the lower parts bends up.



6. The workpiece is turned over, with the second part, similar actions are done.



7. The lower part opens, the headdress is shaped.

Here is such a simple instruction that answers the question of how to make a cap from a newspaper with your own hands. The task is not difficult, even a child can cope with it.

Square cap

  1. It is necessary to take a sheet of newspaper.
  2. One of the corners bends to the opposite side, forming a square.
  3. We repeat this procedure on the other hand, while the second diagonal is indicated.
  4. The workpiece is bent diagonally and turned upside down.
  5. The corners are laid with the bottom side to the wide part of the large triangle.
  6. The model is turned over, the previously performed actions are repeated.
  7. If everything is done correctly, then a visor is formed, which must be bent up.
  8. To fix the form, it remains only to bend the corners.

Conclusion

There are a lot of ways to create origami caps, such products are original, not like each other. They can be both with or without a visor, square and round.

The article described only a few options for how to make a newspaper cap without using scissors and glue. These methods are available to both adults and children. For kids, the creation of paper hats is a fascinating and developing activity, and at the same time, you get the right thing.
