How to make a bead bracelet with your own hands

Beadwork is one of the most creative and interesting types of needlework. Many women plunge headlong into the creation of real masterpieces from miniature beads, you just need to learn how to make a bracelet or other accessory from beads. Hand-woven ornaments look not only

fashionable and luxurious, but also relevant, so you can without hesitation combine them even with an evening outfit. Bracelets are especially popular, so the question of how to make a bead bracelet is one of the most frequently asked questions today.

Bracelets made in machine or manual technology are very popular. There are a fairly large number of weaving patterns, so everyone can choose the most suitable for themselves. Of course, you won‚Äôt surprise anyone with an ordinary bauble bracelet. This jewelry is more suitable for everyday wear, it lacks volume and elegance. Many are interested in how to make a bead bracelet voluminous.

In order to weave a voluminous bracelet, we need improvised items. An ordinary plastic bracelet can serve as a basis. Choosing a foundation

pay attention to some nuances. Firstly, due to the fact that the braided bead bracelet is always slightly translucent, select a base of the color that the beads from which you are going to weave the jewelry. Secondly, the thickness of the base should not be too large, because the wider the plastic bracelet, the more difficult it will be to fit it with a bead cloth. And finally: the size should be chosen taking into account the fact that when braiding the product will become slightly smaller.

So, if you are interested in how to make bracelets from beads, and want to weave a jewelry with your own hands, then the first thing you need to choose the basis. After all preparations are completed, you can proceed to the next step.

We measure with a centimeter tape or ordinary thread the width of the future bracelet, its length both on the outer and inner circles. Approximate parameters will be: width 1-1.5 cm, length (external) - 24-25 cm, inner circumference - 20-22 cm. Thus, in order to braid the bracelet with beads, we need a canvas

1.5 to 25 cm in size. Already directly in the process of fixing the beads on the bracelet, it will be necessary to reduce as much as the measurement data requires from the inside. In our case, by 3 cm, that is, up to 22 cm.

How to make a bead bracelet correctly and quickly? Dial the beads on the needle to understand how many beads will be needed per 1.5 cm of the bracelet. If you use Czech beads 10/0 in size, you get 15 pieces per 1.5 cm. Next, you need to choose a bead weaving scheme. The main thing is to calculate your strengths, so start with simple patterns.

After the bead strip is ready, you can sew it into a ring, and then try on a bracelet. If the dimensions are all right, feel free to start fastening the bead web on a plastic base. To do this, you need to take the fishing line and fix it on any extreme bead. Then we collect the required amount of beads and fix on the other side of the bracelet. In this way, we act along the entire inner side. Ultimately, the inside is completely braided.

Everyone can create a masterpiece, since making a bead bracelet is not at all difficult. This requires desire and imagination, and then you can realize even the most unusual ideas and be fashionable and inimitable.
