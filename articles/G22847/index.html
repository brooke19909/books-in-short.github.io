Rooms

The hotel has 110 apartments, which are arranged in the most modern way. In reviews of Marti Beach Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi), one can often hear comments that in the rooms reserved for relaxation, everything is done in a very beautiful style: a combination of white and brown.

All available rooms are divided into two groups: standard and family, in which conditions are organized to meet the different needs of travelers.

Standard

Standard rooms in the Marti Beach Hotel 4 * hotel are designed for a romantic vacation together with a loved one. They are usually chosen as apartments for a honeymoon.

On a small area (18 m 2 ) there is a large bed designed for two. If necessary, additional folding sleeping equipment can be installed in the room. For clothes and small accessories of guests in the interior there is a large pedestal and a pair of shelves located on both sides of the bed.

There is a mirror in the room as a decoration, as well as several original lamps. If desired, vacationers can watch TV - they show satellite channels on it, a small part of which are Russian-speaking.

Great attention from tourists is paid to the beautiful view that opens from the balcony. They talk about the fact that here you can watch sunsets and sunrises, which are distinguished by their beauty.

Services

The hotel offers a small range of services, including medical. In the Marti Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Kusadasi) on the ground floor of the building there is a small cabinet-medical center, where a doctor is on duty around the clock. Everyone can contact him, but all services are paid.
