Turkey Hotel Dolphin for children and for outdoor activities

Hotel Dolphin de Luxe is one of the most fashionable and expensive hotels in the Turkish resort of Alanya. His building was built in the form of a capital X. When entering the hotel building, it is impossible to walk past a table with refreshments. In hospitable Turkish hotels, you probably will not find a richer table. There are various soft drinks, red and white wines, as well as fruits and Turkish sweets.

Turkey Hotel Dolphin de Luxe has quite spacious rooms, which in principle are designed to accommodate three to four people. There is a large double bed, a single bed and a small sofa. Maximum number of people staying in the room: three adults with one child or two adults with two children. All rooms have a comfortable loggia, thanks to the convenient structure of the building, many of them have a good view of the sea. Each room has a central air conditioning system, satellite TV with a Russian channel, a bathtub equipped with a hairdryer, a safe and carpeted floors. Housekeeping and linen change are provided daily.

The location of the Dolphin Hotel in Turkey is 90 kilometers from Antalya International Airport and 30 kilometers from Alanya. Meals for tourists are offered on an all-inclusive basis. All main meals are made in the large main restaurant with outdoor terrace. At other restaurants located on the territory, you can try dishes of various cuisines: Chinese, Turkish, Italian and European cuisine, as well as fish and diet restaurants. All of these restaurants offer a wide selection of wines. Nine bars offer refreshments, snacks, alcoholic drinks and cocktails. The hotel's Turkish cafe serves authentic Turkish coffee.

Turkey Hotel Dolphin de Lux provides a fairly convenient and comfortable stay for tourists with children. There are a lot of kids, preschool children and older children, all of them are interested and it seems that everyone likes it. At the very moment when the registration of visitors begins, there is a division into children and adults: bracelets of different colors are put on both hands. Try not to confuse the bracelet for your child, since in bars alcohol is dispensed only with adult bracelets. Turkey Hotel Dolphin de Lux provides a variety of children's entertainment and programs. There is an outdoor children's pool with sea sand at the bottom, an active children's vacation, show programs at the playground or in the mini club. Babysitting services are available for a fee.

Turkey Hotel Dolphin de Lux has its own well-kept beach, where free sun beds and mattresses, umbrellas and beach towels are provided . The amount of entertainment will appeal to everyone who wants to spend their holidays at the highest level. Throughout the day, guests are entertained by animators who in the evening show a large show program on stage. You can use the gym, sauna and Turkish bath, jacuzzi and steam bath for free. In the evenings, an open-air disco and an indoor disco are held. A large number of opportunities for those who want to play sports. Volleyball on the beach, tennis on two tennis courts, there are darts, table tennis and this is not a complete list of all the hotel's facilities. Reviews of the Dolphin hotel in Turkey suggest that tourists have almost no complaints about the level of service provided. Many vacationers really like the opportunities for outdoor activities and water sports on the beach. You can ride a canoe or a pedalo. Activities such as water skiing, parachuting, banana and jet ski are available at an additional cost .
