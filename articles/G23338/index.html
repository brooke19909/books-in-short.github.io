The most popular hotels in Ramenskoye

The city of Ramenskoye is located 30 km from the capital of Russia. About 109,000 people live there. The city used to have a well-developed textile industry, and at the moment there are electrotechnical, mechanical, instrument-making and perlite plants. In addition, there are dairies, meat processing plants and food processing plants.

The city has a museum of local lore, the Borisoglebskaya and Trinity Church. Ramenskoye is famous for its beautiful houses, decorated very unusual for our country. In the heart of the city there are sculptures and fountains created based on the characters of various Soviet cartoons. The city has several hotels where you can spend the night. Reservation is best done in advance to be sure of the available accommodation.

Among the various sports facilities, the most famous is the Ramensky racecourse - this is a famous venue for competitions, exhibitions, auctions. It is worth noting that the equestrian club pupils have become many times the winners of various competitions. Just 50 years ago, the city was much smaller and was actively expanding. At the same time, the construction of neighborhoods took place in such a way that in the vicinity of Ramensky not a single village was built up with multi-storey buildings. In addition, the amazing Moscow region nature - meadows, forests, river valleys - has been preserved here, which even today attracts visitors and residents of the city throughout the year.

Hotel Calypso

Hotel Calypso (Ramenskoye) is located 38 km from the Moscow Ring Road. For guests, 45 rooms of several price categories are provided for services. At the same time, the foundation of this hotel was laid in 1850, when the Malyutins (brothers-manufacturers) built the first public largest bathhouse in the city for workers at a local textile factory. She occupied one wing of the building. In 2003, this building was reconstructed, after which it was built on another 3 floors.



To date, the legendary Ramensky baths are considered part of the Calypso Hotel with a developed infrastructure and unique interior solutions that provide guests with a range of high-class services. Among them - a sushi bar, a restaurant, a sports bar, a hotel, an Internet cafe, baths (Finnish sauna, Russian wood-burning, Turkish hammam), fitness club, SPA salon, beauty salon, billiard club.
