Driving route

As already agreed, in order to quickly get to the neighboring regional center, you need to go from the Moscow Ring Road or the Leningradskoye Highway to the E-105 (M10 "Russia") highway and, knowing how many kilometers are from Moscow to Tver, go on a journey.
