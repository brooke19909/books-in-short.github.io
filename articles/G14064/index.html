Sol Palmanova Mallorca 4 * (Spain, Mallorca): room description, service, reviews

The most famous Balearic island is Spanish Mallorca. Every year its territory is visited by tens, hundreds of thousands of tourists who travel here for vivid impressions and unforgettable holidays. The hotel is located in a city called Palma Nova, which is considered the most visited resort of the Balearic Islands. The center is about 14 kilometers away, and the airport is about 20 minutes. The beach, in turn, is a three-minute walk from the hotel.

It is also worth noting that in the immediate vicinity (5-10 minutes on foot) are various interesting places. This Katmandu amusement park, Nikki Beach, three water parks, Santa Ponsa Golf Club, Port Adriano, Jungle Park, Mallorca Casino, as well as the port and Palma Yacht Club.

Entertainment

The main reason why the vast majority goes here is a beach holiday. The weather in Spain, on the island of Mallorca, is warm from May to October, which significantly prolongs the high season. Air temperature in this period varies on average from +25 to +22. Maximum reigns in August and July. In these summer months, even the average rarely drops below +29. What about water? In May, it starts to warm up from +21, then keeps in the summer at around +25, and by October drops to +19. However, many even bathe here in January - +13 for some it is quite an acceptable temperature.

This is about the weather in Spain. But if people have no purpose to spend all their time on the beach or if the water seems cool, they can stay on site and have fun there. You can sunbathe on the terrace, swim in the pool, visit the solarium. Water sports are highly recommended! For example, diving, water and air surfing, boating or water skiing.

Even lovers of entertainment will love the classes of water aerobics, Pilates and water polo, table tennis tournaments and evening show programs.

Many are poisoned in Sol Palmanova Mallorca 4 * (Spain, Mallorca) with their children. This is the right decision, as this hotel is aimed specifically at family vacations. And even if there are not so many establishments in the hotel, the 4 * reviews left about Sol Palmanova Mallorca allow us to make sure that the main thing is quality, not quantity.
