Digital camera Fujifilm X30: reviews, instructions, photo examples

A large assortment of digital cameras on the market, which have an external similarity, the same cost and differ only in brand logos, is pretty tired of potential buyers. After all, there is nothing to choose from. Enthusiasts looking for a compact device want something special. Apparently, the Fujifilm technologists came to the same thought, who introduced the world to the X30 digital camera, made in retro style. For a long time there was nothing like this on the market, so the novelty immediately interested enthusiasts.





The focus of this article is the compact Fujifilm X30, presented in the segment of advanced digital cameras for leisure and creativity. Specifications, reviews, instructions, sample photos and a functional review will allow the reader to get to know the new product closer.

First meeting

Taking the camera for the first time in hand, the user will find 100% similarity of the gadget with the film technique - almost the same weight and size. The weightiness of the device gives the magnesium case, made in retro style, which forces the buyer to pay attention to the Fujifilm X30 digital camera. Reviews of the owners, however, on the body contain negative notes. Users noticed one drawback - the technique has no protection against moisture and dust.





From the side, the gadget resembles a small copy of a SLR camera - the same finish, the location of controls and interfaces. The manufacturer tried to convey in retro style and color the digital device. There are two modifications on the market: black and steel. In the steel version, the model is more similar to a FED film camera than to high-tech equipment.

Creative camera

It is clear that any review of the device should begin with a study of the technical specifications, but the Fujifilm X30 digital camera is an exception. After all, he, unlike competitor counterparts, is equipped with a semi-professional lens that has not been used in compact cameras until now. With a range of focal lengths of 28-112 mm (equivalent to 35 mm), the camera provides a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãopportunities for creativity.





A fast lens is the dream of any novice photographer, because with a maximum aperture of 2.0-2.8, you can shoot in a room with poor lighting without increasing the sensitivity of the matrix (ISO). Naturally, you can simply forget about the noise in the final image.

It only bothers the diameter of the thread on the lens for installing filters. The size is not quite standard for optics; accordingly, finding the right component in the domestic market will not be so simple.

Work with the object

The exposure can be selected in two ways - using a digital viewfinder, as well as using a Fujifilm X30 LCD. Examples of photos immediately before shooting are available in the preview mode, which can be activated by any adjustable button on the body of the digital device.





As for the screen in a compact camera, not all users may like it. The fact is that the 3-inch display is not touch-sensitive. This is a serious manufacturer flaw. The second drawback, which is reported by all owners in their reviews, is the ability to rotate the screen. It is implemented at a low level - the display moves only in the horizontal plane.

Specifications

This is not to say that a 2/3 '' CMOS sensor is the best solution for a Fujifilm X30 camera. Yes, compared to the ‚Äúsoap dishes‚Äù the size is impressive, but for creativity you need the APS-C matrix. All potential buyers will agree with this. As for the speed of work, here the digital device is in full order - the powerful EXR Processor II processor demonstrates high performance. The owner will not notice any inhibition even in the process of serial shooting with saving photos in RAW format.

But the ISO range at first causes doubts among potential buyers (100-3200 with software expansion up to 12800 units). It looks somehow poor even against the background of compact cameras. However, during operation, the owner will understand that there will be no lack of lighting - a high-aperture lens can stretch any exposure.

Remote Control

Judging by user reviews, the convenience of controlling the Fujifilm X30 camera also deserves praise. The instruction manual that comes with the gadget describes in detail not only the functionality of the camera, but also gives many examples of settings that the user can immediately add to the camera. Management is really convenient and there are no questions to it. Even the lack of touch input went unnoticed by many users.





As in SLR devices, the camera is equipped with a swivel wheel that allows you to choose the shooting mode. Everything is simple here: aperture priority, shutter speed, manual mode or automatic, and there is also the possibility of creating your own settings and applying effects. The video control button is located separately and is located near the shutter release. At first, such a decision by the manufacturer seems absurd, but during operation the negative disappears, as accidental button presses are excluded.

Feedback

Reviews about the Fujifilm X30 camera are more positive, but they could not do without the negative. Users did not like the implementation of the built-in flash in the camera. Its size and effectiveness leave much to be desired. Fortunately, the manufacturer equipped the digital device with a shoe for connecting an external device, otherwise the camera would not have had any chances to stay on the market.

The presence of a wireless Wi-Fi module was initially met with skepticism, however, having understood the settings, users were delighted. Fast transfer of pictures to a smartphone and remote control of the camera from a mobile device (Android or iOS) is the best that the manufacturer could do in the Fujifilm X30 camera.





But the camera has video problems. Yes, the video is recorded in FullHD format at a speed of 60 frames per second, however, manual settings are not available to the user, and the auto focus constantly misses.

Finally

The compact Fujifilm X30 camera will definitely interest users who want to get a decent device with great functionality at an affordable price. After all, the main features of this camera are compactness, convenience and quality of shooting. To the symbiosis of the mirror device and the ‚Äúsoap box‚Äù, it is worth adding a retro style, which is able to attract the attention of others to the camera.

But for beginners who want to buy a camera for creativity, this camera is not suitable. The problem is that it focuses more on manual control, requiring knowledge in the field of photography. It is possible to use automatic mode, but this is not the camera that is interested in shooting everything in the "Auto" mode.
