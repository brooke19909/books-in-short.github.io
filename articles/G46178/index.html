How to crochet a backpack: ideas, description, choice of yarn

How to crochet a backpack to make it original? You need to start with the selection of yarn and style. You can crochet not only a children's, but also an adult accessory.

Yarn selection

A backpack is a convenient and practical accessory. It can be made of any materials. A crocheted backpack has become a fashionable trend. How to choose the right yarn for him:

  • Acrylic has a wide range of colors, easy to knit.
  • Cotton is ideal for making a summer accessory, it is practical in the heat.
  • Knitted yarn allows you to create an interesting texture for a backpack, washable.




It is important that the thread is strong enough and not thin. So the accessory will keep in shape.

Simple workshop

Crocheting a backpack from knitted yarn can be fast enough. First you need to prepare the materials:

  • yarn;
  • suitable hook.

Knitting process:

  • We start by creating a bottom. We collect 3 air loops, close in a ringlet and knit 6 SC in a circle. Then in each row we distribute 6 increases evenly. We tie the bottom to 25 cm in diameter.
  • We begin to make the walls of the backpack. We knit in a circle without increments with columns without a crochet of at least 30 cm in height.
  • Next, we make holes for ties: * 5 single crochet, 5 air stitches * - repeated until the end of the row.
  • The last row is tied with a "crustacean step".
  • A tie is a chain of air loops of the required length.




Even a beginner can crochet a knitted backpack. The accessory can be decorated with decorative elements or buttons.

Kids backpack

Bright and beautiful accessory will appeal to any girl. How to create a children's backpack crocheted?

  • To start, yarn of several shades and a hook are selected.
  • Then knit the bottom with columns without a crochet. It can be round or oval.
  • The walls of the backpack are made by the selected pattern of at least 25 cm in height.
  • The top of the bag is tied with crochets.
  • In the middle of one side create a valve. To do this, attach the thread and knit 20 rows in rotary rows.
  • Separately made straps. To do this, gain 8 single crochet stitches and knit in 30 cm long swivel rows. Sew to a backpack.




Children's backpack decorated with flowers. You can also embroider the face of any animal.

Knitted backpack

When choosing yarn for bags, the main thing is that it keeps its shape. How to crochet a backpack?

  • First, the bottom is formed. To do this, make 2 air loops, in the second, knit 6 SC, and close in a circle. Make increases in each row. The bottom should be at least 20 cm in diameter.
  • To go to the walls of the backpack, one row is knitted without increases for the rear loops. Then move up 25 cm.
  • Next, you need to bind the valve. To do this, they collect 6 SC in a ring and pull it together. The semicircle is knitted in rotary rows, increments are made in each row. Then the valve is sewn to the backpack. A large button is sewn, a loop is made.
  • The straps are made of the required width and length with single crochet.

The backpack, knitted from knitted yarn, holds its shape well, it can be washed even in a washing machine.

5 interesting ideas

The needlewomen in search of new ideas are inspired by the work of other masters. To understand how to crochet a backpack, it is enough to know the basic principles of creating things from yarn.





% of ideas that will help you choose not only the color, but also the appearance of the accessory:

  • A backpack in the form of an animal is perfect for a child. First, the bag itself is knitted, and then a cute muzzle is made out. The most popular are: bunny, teddy bear, kitty and dog.
  • For the summer, you can make a rainbow backpack. Having picked up all seven shades, knit alternately in rows. Decorate with a flower or a beautiful button.
  • Ladybug will not only attract attention, but will also cheer you up. Such a backpack will appeal not only to children, but also to adolescents.
  • Incredibly beautiful and cute will be an accessory made of floral motifs. It can be connected in any color scheme. For lovely girls, pink shades are suitable, for courageous and active people - bright colors.
  • Backpack "zebra". Such an accessory will suit any outfit, as it will be knitted from threads of two classic colors - white and black.

A backpack is such a thing that almost every person has. It can become not only a roomy bag, but also a nice accessory that complements the style of clothing.
