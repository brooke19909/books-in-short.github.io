The basic rules of differentiation used in mathematics

To begin with, it is worth remembering what a differential is and what mathematical meaning it carries.

The differential of a function is the product of the derivative of the function from the argument to the differential of the argument itself. Mathematically, this concept can be written as an expression: dy = y '* dx.

The differential of a function is the product of the derivative of the function by the differential of the argument.
