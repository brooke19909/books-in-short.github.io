Amwaj Blue Beach Resort

Description: Amazing Amwaj Blue Beach Resort gives travelers the opportunity to spend their vacation days as brightly as possible. The staff at this hotel exclusively cares about their customers on the Red Sea. It should be noted the convenient location of the hotel. Amwaj Blue Beach Resort is located fifty kilometers from the city of Hurghada in Egypt. The international airport can be reached in thirty minutes.





Rooms: Hotel has for its customers a four-story building in which they will be offered various types of rooms: 150 garden view rooms, 124 sea view rooms, 100 deluxe rooms, 25 family rooms, 3 junior suites and 3 royal suites. The rooms have all the amenities required for a memorable stay: wide soft beds, satellite TV with Russian TV channels, telephone, air conditioning, hairdryer, balcony or terrace, bathroom, coffee maker, as well as a minibar in which travelers will find soft drinks.





Meals: The hotel has an all-inclusive system. At the Amwaj Blue Beach Resort you will find a considerable number of restaurants. In them, caring staff offers its customers to try dishes of international, oriental and Italian cuisines, as well as many delicious snacks prepared from the highest quality products. Bistro, Bridge, Refresh, Relax and Fusion bars delight guests with a variety of first-class drinks.

Beach: A free and clean sandy beach is located on the territory of the hotel. Any tourist can use mattresses, towels, sun loungers and umbrellas there.





Information for vacationers: Everyone living in the Amwaj Blue Beach Resort has the opportunity to visit three pools (one of them is heated), a jacuzzi, a spa, a gym, a sauna, a steam room, a beauty salon, and a disco. At the hotel you can freely play billiards, volleyball, darts, tennis or table tennis, as well as get quick access to the Internet, for which you will have to pay a fee. At will, any client is offered services: a doctor, a masseur, a nanny, laundry and dry cleaning. Amwaj Blue Beach Resort 5 * urges its guests not to be shy to contact the staff for more information without any obligation.

Reservations can be made either through the main site, where tourists leave applications, or by phone call to the hotel staff. International credit cards are accepted to pay for accommodation at the hotel, so paying for a room upon arrival will not be a problem for guests.

Reviews: Tourists leave only positive reviews about Amwaj Blue Beach and advise their friends and acquaintances who plan to spend their holidays in an exotic place to stay at this hotel. Travelers note the cozy atmosphere prevailing in spacious and tidy rooms, convenient location and reasonable prices. Also, the hotel does not leave anyone indifferent thanks to the multilingual staff, always ready to help in any situation. All customers speak with enthusiasm about a variety of dishes prepared exclusively from fresh products and giving a lot of energy for outdoor activities.
