Peach Hill Resort (Thailand / Phuket): hotel description and reviews

Among the many Thai resorts, the island of Phuket is in great demand among our compatriots. In this fabulous place, newlyweds love to spend time, couples with children, as well as everyone who is eager for adventure, which more than can provide various excursion bureaus of the island. Hundreds of thousands of Russians visit Phuket regularly, and several tens of thousands more think about traveling here, studying the lists of the most suitable hotels for accommodation in terms of price and quality. If you are also looking for the best hotel complex for your future Thai voyage, then take a look at the Peach Hill Resort. It is great for those who are careful about their finances and prefer to live in some distance from the noisy streets. In this article we will tell you in detail about the hotel itself, its features and the assessment that tourists give it.





Peach Hill Resort 4 *: general description of the hotel complex

Phuket has beautiful hotels scattered over the hills with stunning views of the jungle and the sea. Most of them are a complex of villas and bungalows with a small private garden. This arrangement allows tourists to feel in seclusion, even in a completely filled hotel. Peach Hill Resort also looks similar, with a picturesque view of the island's surroundings.

The hotel complex, which will be discussed today, is far from new - it opened in 1997, but has since been constantly refurbished and refurbished. At the moment it consists of three parts: two buildings and a complex of villas. The last few years were rebuilt from a bungalow, and now they are luxurious Thai-style jacuzzi houses decorated with precious woods. The first building - Peach Wing - was commissioned after repair in 2001, the second - White Peach Wing - in 2009.

The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is fifteen thousand square meters, which houses residential buildings, restaurants, swimming pools and beautiful gardens, where numerous animals and insects live. Guests at the Peach Hill Hotel Resort can accommodate in two hundred and sixty-two rooms of various categories.

Hotel location

The hotel complex Peach Hill Hotel Resort 4 * is located on the slope of a rather high hill. On the one hand, this provides guests with an excellent view of the rooms and gives the hotel some special charm. But on the other, it makes you climb fairly steep stairs several times a day, which is quite difficult for people aged and young children to do. And it‚Äôs hard for ordinary people to quickly adapt to such a load.

The hotel is located on the second line from the beach, which is quite natural for Phuket. Most of the hotel complexes on the island do not have their own beach area, so you have to get to the sea on foot or in a special shuttle. Peach Hill Hotel Resort is located about two hundred meters from the beach, which is easily overcome in five to ten minutes. Interestingly, the hotel is built at the junction of the two best beaches in Phuket. Therefore, tourists can relax on each of them in turn. If you prefer the noisy and youthful Patong beach with an abundance of nightlife, you can reach it by breaking fifteen kilometers on a rented motorbike or tuk-tuk.

Please note that approximately fifty-six kilometers are required from the airport to the Peach Hill Resort. For Phuket, this is not much, because the airport is located at some distance from the main tourist centers. In addition, most hotels provide a shuttle service for a fee, which eliminates the need for an urgent matter after arrival to look for a taxi or get to the city by public transport.





Infrastructure

For those who like hotels with a vast territory where you can always find something interesting, Peach Hill Hotel Resort 4 * (Phuket, Kata) is just perfect. Here, for example, among the trees and flowers are three restaurants. In the largest of them, designed for the simultaneous presence of one hundred and twenty people, breakfasts are held. In the rest you can treat yourself to a delicious lunch or dinner consisting of dishes of European and Thai cuisine. Judging by the reviews of hotel guests, the chefs at Peach Hill Resort 4 * never repeat and simply prepare culinary masterpieces that will appeal to even the most capricious and choosy gourmets.

If you want to sit on a deck chair with an exquisite and aromatic cocktail, then there will be two bars at your service. They are very conveniently located on the hotel grounds, so they never stand empty. People come here by the pool or those who just want to relax, enjoying magnificent views of the mountains.

Peach Hill Resort has three pools, each of which is located near its body. You do not need to leave the room for a long time wandering around the hotel in search of a place where you can freshen up and sunbathe. However, if you want to swim in the pool near another building, then no one will forbid you from doing this. Hotel guests have access to all facilities on its territory. The largest pool is located at the exit of the main building, there are always a lot of people and quite noisy. Couples prefer to swim by the second pool, where there are artificial stones and waterfalls. It is the most colorful and exotic. Keep in mind that all pools are open from half past six in the morning until seven in the evening. At a later time, the hotel strongly discourages water treatments.

Peach Hill Hotel Resort 4 * offers free parking for guests where you can leave cars and motorbikes. For business people who even control their business on vacation, a conference room and the ability to use the installed equipment to receive a fax or copy the necessary documents are suitable. This allows you to make the right decisions, even being hundreds of thousands of kilometers from your home.

The hotel has a small gym. His guests celebrate with special warmth, because thanks to the administration of the hotel complex, the room has everything you need for a full workout, which is very important for people who are seriously involved in sports or simply responsibly approach their physical form. For guests of the female Peach Hill Resort (Phuket), an excellent spa and massage parlor will be a pleasant surprise. Of course, the procedures here are not included in the price, but everyone who used the services of the specialists of this center responded with the best words about them.

The hotel complex also offers laundry and dry cleaning services. For a small fee, your clothes will be put in order, and you will not carry with you a whole suitcase of dirty things that you will have to wash at home for several days.

The reception in the main building is open around the clock, so with any questions you may have, please contact the Peach Hill Resort staff directly. Reviews about them in their bulk are positive. In addition, more than half of the staff speaks excellent Russian, another part of the hotel staff understands English well. Therefore, despite your level of knowledge of foreign languages, there will be no problems with expressing your requests and wishes to the staff of Peach Hill Resort (Phuket).

Luggage storage, currency exchange and a tour desk are also available at the reception. Some tourists are very worried about buying excursions from street vendors (although this is quite common in Phuket), so you can safely buy tours to the most popular destinations at the hotel for a reasonable price.





Service at the hotel complex

Most vacationers primarily note the excellent speed of Wi-Fi, which is free and catches throughout the hotel. Upon check-in, guests are given a username and password, they must be entered every time you enter the Internet. Many tourists noted that every three hours you are thrown out of the system, so you must re-enter by entering the login.

Peach Hill Resort (Kata) has developed a flexible compliment system from the hotel. They go to the following categories of guests:

  • newlyweds;
  • birthday people;
  • those celebrating the wedding anniversary;
  • regular visitors to the hotel complex.

In addition, each guest receives a welcome drink and a bouquet of flowers before checking in, which is very pleasant after a hard flight and a long journey.

Most vacationers at Peach Hill Resort 4 * (Phuket) note that the staff at the hotel is quite helpful, and the maids clean the rooms very high quality. Tea and coffee making facilities and a toilet set are replenished daily. At each cleaning, the bed is changed and towels are changed. Some tourists write in their reviews that they noticed dirt on the floor after cleaning the room, but it turned out that the maids had to be warned in advance about the need to clean the floor. Otherwise, they will limit themselves to wiping out dust, arranging things, making the bed and sweeping the floor.

If you relax by the pool, then sunbeds and umbrellas will be free for you. If you wish, you can take a beach towel, take it with you to the sea or lay on a lounger by the pool.

When booking a room, you must indicate the need for a transfer to and from the hotel. This service is paid extra, but greatly facilitates the movement of hotel guests.

Those who prefer to spend their holidays in solitude, alone with each other, can order food in their room. The staff at Peach Hill Resort Phuket 4 * will bring you the ordered dishes and drinks, in the same way you can order cocktails from the bar.

It is interesting that in advertising catalogs the hotel has a different star. In some, it goes like a "treshka", in others it is assigned four stars. If we rely on the reviews of vacationers in this matter, we can say that the hotel complex is still a four-star hotel in which the service is delivered to a very high level.





Hotel Rooms

The rooms at Peach Hill Hotel Resort 4 * (Phuket) have a very good design and excellent equipment. Hotel guests noted the high-quality equipment, good new furniture and excellent views. Also, many were pleased with the silent air conditioning and refrigerator, because it allowed you to sleep well after a busy day.

In the hotel complex you can choose from the following room categories:

  • Deluxe

To make it easier for you to navigate among them, we will talk about each category in detail.

Superior

These rooms are located mainly in the main building and have a view of the city. Superior features cable TV, a fridge, a safe, air conditioning and a minibar. Each room has a small balcony where you can dry things and enjoy the evening lights of the city. Superior also always has a tea set, and every day the staff gives out one bottle of water per guest. Toiletries in the shower include shampoos, soap, hair conditioner and body lotions. Please note that these rooms do not have a bathtub; guests will only have to shower.

Check In Rules

Check-in takes place at two in the afternoon, check-out should occur before twelve in the afternoon. Please note that pets are not allowed in the hotel. At registration, tourists need to leave a deposit of one hundred dollars and indicate the credit card number, the name of the owner of which coincides with the name of one of the guests.

In special cases, when a guest arrives before the check-in time, he can visit breakfast and spend time by the pool until a room is prepared for him. If the room is already ready, then check-in will occur at the time of arrival.





Hotel stay with children

Every tourist can come to Peach Hill Resort (Phuket, Kata) with a child of any age. Here you will be received with great pleasure. If your baby is not yet three years old, then you can place it on your bed and not pay extra for accommodation.

Older children are accommodated in an extra bed. Please note that only one bed can be accommodated in a room. The cost of such a service is equal to one thousand baht per night.

In the restaurant, your child can be provided with a special children's menu and a chair for very young guests. If you need to leave your baby for a while in your room and go on business, the hotel will call a nanny for you. Naturally, this service is paid separately.

On the territory of the hotel complex there is a small children's pool suitable for swimming, and there is a special club where specially trained staff will entertain the kids.

Peach Hill Resort 4: reviews

If you want to know whether to go to the hotel described by us, then you should carefully study the reviews of those who have already rested here. On the Internet, such comments are posted in large numbers, so we tried to analyze most of them in order to form an opinion on the hotel complex, as close as possible to reality.

Of course, not without negative reviews. After all, people have different addictions and habits, which is why they evaluate the environment completely differently. Some guests find the food quite monotonous, and accommodation on the mountain is very uncomfortable. Others do not like the abundance of living creatures in the hotel (and this is natural, if you remember that the hotel is practically in the jungle). But the third people remember with an unkind word the staff who did not always respond to their requests with due speed and quickness.

Mosquitoes are particularly displeasing for guests. During the day, they hide in dense greenery, but at night they haunt absolutely none of the tourists. Most of them are near the villas, because they are located farthest from the city streets. This annoying nuisance does not allow spending evenings on the balcony with a cup of aromatic coffee or tea. However, especially savvy guests quickly find a way out of this situation - they buy a fumigator and insect plates, which are installed on the balcony. In this case, it becomes possible to calmly enjoy the night Phuket.

We cannot say that all of the above is fiction and nit-picking. Nevertheless, positive reviews about staying at the hotel are much more than negative.

Many guests noted how well the staff monitors the hotel. Everything is repaired, clean, and even water samples are taken from the pools. This is repeated regularly and inspires confidence that you are in a great place where everyone cares about your well-being and health.

More than once in the reviews, tourists talked about how they were absolutely free to change their booked rooms to rooms of a class above. Moreover, this happened even without any requests.

Upon arrival, guests are transported in a small shuttle throughout the hotel and shown the most beautiful places. Thus, you can navigate the location of the pools and restaurants.

The hotel guests also considered the transfer to the beach, which has an interval of one hour, to be very convenient. Indeed, many can reach the sea on their own, but far from every vacationer can reach the steep hill.

The location of the hotel is very convenient for those who like to wander through the streets of the city on foot. In close proximity are small shops with a mass of goods necessary for life, shopping centers and restaurants. Most of the hotel guests emphasized that they had dinner at various cafes almost every day and were always pleased with the food and service.

Many appreciated the beach bags, umbrellas and mats that are in each room. Thanks to this, it is convenient for tourists to pack on the beach and leave the hotel if there is a sudden tropical rainfall outside the window (and this happens in Phuket very often).

A pleasant surprise for our compatriots was the ability of the staff to communicate in Russian. For Thai hotels this is a big luxury, so for a certain category of travelers this fact may be decisive when choosing a hotel.

Of course, Peach Hill Resort can not claim to be a five-star hotel, but it copes pretty well, being the "four". Here you can relax, plunging into the indescribable atmosphere of Phuket. In addition, a pleasant advantage of such a vacation would be its low cost. If these are important points for you, then feel free to book a hotel for the coming dates and go on your Thai voyage towards adventures.
