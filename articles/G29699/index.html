Regency Lodge Hotel 3 * (Egypt): photos and reviews of tourists

The hotels of one of the best Egyptian resorts, Sharm El Sheikh, are always crowded with tourists. Twelve months of the year guests come here from all over the world. This is facilitated by a surprisingly favorable climate, as well as reasonably reasonable prices, combined with the high level of service inherent in local resorts.

Sharm El Sheikh Hotels





The most popular hotel complexes are built directly on the Red Sea. Almost all of them have their own beach. In general, choosing a vacation in this resort city, tourists should consider that there are not many sandy areas for swimming, more often the bottom of the sea is covered with stones and corals.

Many hotels are known for their presentability and a wide variety of services. Vacationers are offered hotel water parks and discos, water sports centers and health clubs. If the complex is aimed specifically at family guests, then its infrastructure necessarily includes a mini-club, and there is also a playground for children.

Meals are most often offered on an all-inclusive basis. Three meals a day are served buffet style, and local drinks are available free of charge in the bars.

Many good hotels in Sharm El Sheikh with four or five stars are located in the Sharm El Maya area, where there is a single sandy beach. Hotel complexes here almost completely occupy all three lines from the coast. All of them have different stardom and prices.

Many of those who are going to relax in Sharm el-Sheikh in the economy class choose three-star options when they come to Egypt. 3 * Regency Lodge Hotel in this matter suits many Russians not only with its affordable prices, but also with a good location.

General Hotel Information

The hotel was built in 2001. In 2013, the Regency Lodge Hotel underwent a major renovation, which included not only a complete repair of the room stock, but also the replacement of plumbing.

It is located in the Hadabat area on the second line from the sea. Shuttle buses run twice a day from the hotel to the famous Naama Bay and to Shattel Beach and back. The old town is a ten minute walk. The hotel is next to El Mercato Street, where there are many shops and boutiques of various European brands, as well as cafes and restaurants. Nearby is an entertainment complex in the oriental style of "1000 and 1 night." The Old Town, where you can visit the bazaar, is one kilometer away.

The territory of Regency Lodge Hotel 3 * is almost ten thousand square meters. It is completely planted with lawns, many palm trees that reach the balconies of the second floor of residential buildings.

The nearest airport is half an hour away. Apparently, this is the reason that those who come to Egypt with young children choose this hotel: a short transfer is one of the many advantages of Regency Lodge Hotel 3 *.

For children

Tour operators 3 * Regency Lodge Hotel, reviews of which the majority of Russians leave positive, are positioned as family. Therefore, the administration for small customers provided the following free services and entertainment: a high chair for feeding in a restaurant, an extra bed on request, and a special menu, the need for which must be stated immediately upon arrival, at the receptionist.

The hotel has a playground for children, there is also a shallow compartment in the pool, where young guests can safely frolic under the supervision of their parents.

Beach

Residents of the Regency Lodge Hotel can use two beaches at once: the sandy Elkheima beach, located across the road a ten minute walk and Fanara Beach, two kilometers distant, with a coral bottom, so rubber shoes are necessary, especially for children. Sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas and beach towels are available for a fee.

Additional Information





The hotel has a massage room, there is also a sauna, a wellness center and a hairdresser. For an extra fee, you can rent racquets and a table tennis ball, as well as play darts and billiards.

Regency Lodge Hotel also has a freshwater pool . Those who, for whatever reason, do not want to go to the beach, can have a great time swimming and sunbathing on the sun loungers around it or just relaxing in the bar while drinking drinks. In the mornings, there are classes in water aerobics.

Sports and Entertainment

The hotel provides animation; in the evenings, discos or theme nights are organized in the amphitheater. Many different water sports activities are presented on the beaches. Vacationers can canoe, rent a boat or a boat, engage in bananasailing or windsurfing.

There is a diving school on the beach. Under the supervision of licensed instructors, anyone can go scuba diving. An experienced Russian-speaking specialist conducts classes at sea. A shuttle departs twice a day from Shattel Beach, bringing tourists directly to the Regency Lodge Hotel.

Reviews

Egypt is considered a country in which to judge a hotel by its stardom is not worth it. And this is especially true for the Regency Lodge Hotel, which, despite its low star rating, has recently become popular among Russians.





It is designed, according to reviews, for tourists without any complaints. Located in the center of Sharm, it has a rather convenient position: within walking distance of the Old Town and the famous entertainment complex, the hotel overlooks El Mercato - the central street of the district. Here are the McDonald's, Starbucks and KFC, as well as the office of the local mobile operator, Fares fish restaurant, banks.

Clients do not need to go anywhere on purpose, because everything you need is literally within walking distance.

The hotel area is always clean. The buildings form two cozy patios. The rooms, although modest, without any frills, are quite comfortable for living.

Reviews of Russians about food are somewhat divergent. Some consider it monotonous, others were satisfied with food, especially desserts.

Summarizing, we can say that the Regency Lodge Hotel is suitable for those who clearly understand that they are not going to "five", but to "three stars". And for the price difference, you can pick up and buy those services that, in their opinion, are needed on vacation.
