Furnishing and bathroom

The furniture set is presented as a standard set: a double bed, a table, an armchair, a luggage rack and a dressing room. Furniture arrangement is practical and expedient.

The bathroom is finished with ceramic tiles of good quality. Equipped with countertop with sink, shower or bath, hairdryer. The number of white towels complies with international standards. There are all the necessary toiletries and personal hygiene products.

Meal

According to the all-inclusive concept, three meals a day are organized according to the buffet principle in the main restaurant of the institution. Nutritional snacks, ice cream and pastries are available throughout the day. The presence of two bars allows you to quench your thirst by the pool and on the beach. Freshly squeezed juices, Turkish coffee and imported alcohol are paid extra.





Beach

A private beach is 50 meters from the hotel. The length is 60 meters. On this territory there are soft sun loungers with umbrellas, a volleyball court, changing cabins and showers. A towel dispenser is available for a fee.

The entrance to the sea is stone. The safety of the swimmers is constantly being watched by the rescue service. Among the most popular entertainment are: riding a "banana", jet skis, windsurfing and diving.

The presence of a pier with a length of 30 meters allows you to dive into the deep waters of the Mediterranean, which brings a lot of pleasure to swimmers.

Aquazone

The main pool is open air. Its area reaches the mark of 230 square meters. It is without a heating system, has a different depth. Classes on water aerobics are regularly held here, in which everyone is interested, regardless of age and gender. It is surrounded by numerous sun loungers with umbrellas. Nearby is a children's pool of 8 "squares". Close proximity allows kids to be under the constant supervision of their parents.
