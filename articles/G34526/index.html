Ladybug made of plastic bottles - a beauty that can be created with your own hands!

A growing number of domestic masters are thinking about how a ladybug from plastic bottles can be made on their own. Most

A simple way to make it is from the lid. He is even capable of a five-year-old child. But the next two options are more complicated, require more thorough preparation, and at least a teenager can complete them.

From the lid

Ladybugs made of plastic bottles are made most easily from the lid. It is immediately necessary to prepare red, black and white paints. We take the lid from such containers of any size (starting from 0.5 and up to 5 liters - everything will do). We cover it with red and give it the opportunity to dry. In the next step, make a black center line, face and dots

black paint. After it dries, decorate the eyes with a brush and white paint. When all this is dry, the ladybug from the plastic bottles is ready. Now it can be used both as a toy and as a home decoration, in general, at your discretion.

5 liter capacity

To create such a figurine you need a large container with a capacity of 5 liters. We lay it on one of the side faces. On the lower side of the neck, draw a line with a marker parallel to the lower edge along the entire perimeter. That is, starting from the neck, then - along the cone, side edge, bottom, next side, on the other side of the cone and return to the starting point. We cut the resulting workpiece along this line. This product is made out similarly to the previously described method, with the only difference being that the ladybug made of plastic bottles was small, and here there will be a large figurine that will become a worthy decoration of your collection of plastic crafts. In this case, the cap and neck are used as the head.

Large flower bed

For such a flower bed, we need a set of red, white and black containers. They can be made by coating with paint the corresponding color of any container. Preliminary it is better to make calculations and determine the approximate

the amount of raw materials. It is recommended that they be procured 10 percent more so that there is a certain supply. Further, in the place of the future flowerbed, a round hill is made, the height of which gradually increases from the edge to the center. Around the perimeter of the circle, black containers are buried with the lids down and the bottom up. Then (strictly in the middle) you need to make a straight line of the same color in a similar way. With the help of buried white bottles, we also make eyes. The rest of the muzzle is filled with containers with black color. We draw up the rest with red paint, without forgetting about the spots. They are made from single black bottles. Then the ladybug from the bottles done. Such an article will be an excellent addition to any personal plot.

Conclusion

Not such a rarity such crafts from plastic bottles for the garden. Photos that are often found confirm this. In the first case, everything is very simple, and such a modest work can be done even by a preschooler. But the last two crafts already require more thorough preparation. Such figures are recommended to be made by experienced masters who have gained a hand in this matter.
