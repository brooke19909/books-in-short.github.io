Where is the best place to relax in Spain? We decide together

Nowadays, you will not surprise anyone with traveling abroad. Many managed to visit another country at least once and understand how wonderful it is to relax abroad. Firstly, this is a change not only of the place of residence, but also of culture, customs, traditions, language, people. This, as a rule, benefits everyone. However, there are still many places that you have not visited, although you really want to. In this article, we will talk about where to relax in Spain. After reading the article, you will understand that rest in this country is beautiful, which tour operators are not unreasonably trying to convince travelers.





Spain can be divided into island and mainland. Although this is one country, the atmosphere in different parts is different. Basically, a Mediterranean climate is observed throughout the territory, in which the sea heats up and the air does not heat up. Those who do not want to dramatically change the climate will be happy to relax in Spain, as you can swim there until October.

If we talk about Spanish cuisine, then it is considered one of the best in the world and is famous for its diversity. Therefore, thinking about where to relax in Spain, you can also rely on what they cook there and what you would like to try. Each of the regions of Spain has its own characteristics in the preparation of various dishes. So, Valencia is famous for its wonderful paella (a dish of seafood, vegetables and rice), in Catalonia they prepare extremely tasty sauces. Well, in the center, various types of roast are most in demand. Also in Spain there is an opportunity to try dishes of Chinese, Italian, French cuisines. If you are interested in the price of lunch for one person (main course, salad and dessert), then it is from about 5 to 8 euros. You can also try delicious wine and beer, the cost of which ranges from 1 to 4 euros. Many cafes are open around the clock, but from about 13.00 to 16.00 all of them are closed for siesta.





So where is the best place to relax in Spain? The most famous resorts are Mallorca, Ibiza and Costa Brava.

Mallorca is the largest island of the Belear archipelago. Here you can see the capital of the island - the city of Palma de Mallorca, which comfortably combines old and modern. The island also has a marine animal park, Galatzo Nature Park, giant Arta Caves and Spanish Disneyland, which is the largest amusement park in Esrop.

The Costa Brava resort is located in northern Spain. It is famous for its beautiful coastal landscapes, as well as spacious beaches, alternating with cliffs that are covered with evergreen trees. Are you interested in where it is better to relax youth in Spain? You can visit Ibiza. This is the best youth resort. It is also called Spanish Hollywood. There are no restrictions on clothing or behavior on this island, only fun and unlimited freedom. Here you can visit the best discos in the world, the hippy market, as well as historical sites.
