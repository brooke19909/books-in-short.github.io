Kempinski Grand Hotel, Gelendzhik: rooms, reviews, prices and hotel description

Gelendzhik is one of the most popular resorts in the south of Russia, perhaps second only to the city of Sochi. Well-developed infrastructure, comfortable and clean beaches, many hotels and guest houses for tourists with any budget, extraordinary beauty - there are many reasons to come here for a vacation. Kempinski Grand Hotel is not just a resort hotel, but also a premium establishment located on the Cape of Tolstoy. Surrounded by its amazingly beautiful relict park with pine trees. The hotel is located on the first line. It offers its guests comfortable rooms of several categories. This hotel has everything for a pleasant pastime, whether a person came here on vacation or at work.





Kempinski Grand Hotel: Description

The hotel has a great location. And not only because it is located on the first beach line and is surrounded by its relict park. It's all about the landscape. As a premium hotel, Kempinski can be proud of its well-developed infrastructure. There are conditions for a relaxing and active holiday, you can organize some important event. For example, an anniversary, a wedding anniversary or just another birthday. Kempinski Grand Hotel is a modern multi-story building with stylish rooms. He works year-round. In summer, you can take a dip in the pool, ride a water slide, cycle through the area, play tennis and go to the beach.
