How to make a dog out of paper with your own hands

Various figures made of paper are called origami. To engage in this type of needlework is very exciting and interesting. A special place here is the creation of various species of animals. An interesting craft is a dog. This article will discuss how to make a dog out of paper with your own hands.

Where to begin

Before embarking on an original craft, you must prepare the following materials:

  • one-sided colored paper of the color that the dog is planning;
  • scissors;
  • pencil.

Phased production

  • In the first step, the head is made. From a sheet of paper you need to cut a figure in the form of a square.
  • Then it is necessary to fold the workpiece diagonally and iron the fold well.
  • The resulting figure must be positioned so that the vertex is facing up. First, you need to bend the corner located on the right to the upper part, and then the one on the left.
  • Now you should straighten the triangles and bend them to the fold that has just appeared.
  • At this stage, you need to straighten the corners and bend both to the first fold.
  • Then the workpiece must be turned over.
  • Small triangles need to be bent along the fold line.
  • The corners remaining below must be bent to the workpiece.
  • At this stage, they are straightened and bent inwards along the existing fold line.
  • The upper part of the corner must be bent down.
  • The workpiece is turned over again, and the next step will be to bend up the corner at the bottom of the craft. The sheet of paper was one-sided, which means that the face of the dog will turn out white.

This part of the article talked about how to make a dog out of paper in stages, namely how to create a head for crafts.

How to make a torso

First you need to cut another square out of paper of the same color, which went to the manufacture of the muzzle. And then you need to adhere to the following instructions:

  • Add a triangle shape from an existing square.
  • Turn the workpiece in such a way that the bend is vertical and bend the corner located in the upper right to the vertical line.
  • You also need to do with the angle on the left to get the same triangles.
  • Now you need to bend the remaining corners below in the same way.
  • The resulting triangle bends to the central part, so that a few centimeters remain to the top of the craft.
  • Now you need to bend diagonally part of the triangle.

So, it‚Äôs almost clear how to make a volume dog out of paper. Thus, the craft appeared tail, which will serve as a support. At the last stage, you need to draw a muzzle and connect the torso with the head, it is simply worn over the body.

What else is the option of crafts

How to make a bite dog out of paper? Here you will need the following materials:

  • one-sided sheet of colored paper, it should be square;
  • scissors;
  • pencil.

Step by step execution

  • Fold the right and left sides of the sheet in such a way as to get a triangle.
  • Then fold the workpiece in half.
  • Bend the left side to obtain a vertical triangle, with one side coinciding with the middle.
  • Then straighten the fold and iron the upper part, thus forming the future head.
  • To make ears, you need to bend both sides of the craft on the sides. The lower corner is bent up to make a nose.
  • Now you need to turn the craft.
  • To the body was uniform, it is necessary to make a longitudinal bend.
  • Then the workpiece is turned over to make the dog a tail.
  • A more original craft will be if the tail will consist of several folds.
  • At the last stage, eyes are drawn to the dog.

The third way to make a dog

Having considered two methods of crafts, you can move on to the third. How to make a dog out of paper? The technique, which will be described below, is ideal for working with children. Before you start origami, you need to have at hand the following materials:

  • square sheets in the amount of two pieces (any color is selected);
  • a small leaf of pink color;
  • felt-tip pen or black pencil;
  • scissors;
  • glue.

Step-by-step manufacturing

  • The square-shaped paper sheet is folded so as to obtain a triangular shape. The workpiece is folded diagonally.
  • The lower corner of the craft bends a little, and the workpiece is laid aside for a while.
  • To make the dog‚Äôs head, you will need a second sheet of paper, which must be folded in the manner described above.
  • The triangle that turned out needs to be folded again to get additional lines, they should be ironed carefully.
  • The left and right edges of the triangle are bent to the central part, but do not reach slightly to the main bend line. It will be the ears of the dog.
  • Now the craft turns over to the other side, the corner located in the center needs to be bent and the bend should be ironed properly.
  • It remains to connect the head with the body with glue.
  • At the last stage, you need to draw a nose, paws and eyes, as well as cut out the tongue for the future pet from pink paper and also attach it with glue in the right place.

So, now it should be clear how to make an origami dog out of paper.

A bit of history

If we turn to Japanese culture, from where, in fact, the unusual art of origami came to us, then this word can be translated as ‚Äúfolded paper‚Äù. Interestingly, such needlework originally originated in China, where paper was invented. Gradually, unusual crafts gained worldwide recognition, but most of all they became interested in Japan.

This piece of art for a long time was available exclusively to people belonging to the aristocracy. Here, the ability to add origami belonged to one of the signs of good taste. The secrets of paper masterpieces were passed down from generation to generation and even created whole books, which, if necessary, corresponded.

Origami can be considered the most unusual and original form of art. The main rule here is not to make changes or remove parts, even if they seem redundant. It is this approach to business that the Japanese prefer, creating real masterpieces from solid sheets of paper. Initially, this technology was widely used for many ceremonies, both secular and religious. For example, during the marriage, butterflies in the origami style were made to decorate the glasses of people present at the celebration, and they did not forget about the figures symbolizing the young couple.

It is interesting that origami could serve as a kind of amulet for samurai, for this paper was additionally decorated with dried meat or shark skin. Such amulets were supposed to keep the soldiers and help them win victories. As you can see from the article, origami is a whole art, which will be interesting not only for adults, but also for children. If you master simple works first, then gradually you can move on to real masterpieces.
