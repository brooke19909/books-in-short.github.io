Hotels in Moscow: Cosmos. Cosmos Hotel (Moscow): photos and reviews of tourists

Moscow is the cultural center of Russia. On the territory of the metropolis with a population of more than 12 million inhabitants there are more than 100 theaters, 60 museums, a large number of art galleries. This is a city with a rich history and its own special urban subculture.

The capital opens up great opportunities for business communications. Therefore, a visit to this large city is always a trip with a rich sequence of events. In order to understand the spirit of Moscow and be at the center of the cultural and business environment, it is advisable to consider a hotel with rich infrastructure, conveniently located in relation to the historical center of the city. The legendary Cosmos Hotel fully complies with all these characteristics. The history of its creation begins with the pre-Olympic 1979. It was created to accommodate guests of the 1980 Olympics.

Hotels in Moscow: Cosmos

This is one of the most famous hotels in the capital, rich in its history, a list of distinguished guests, the number and level of events organized on its territory.



In the immediate vicinity of the Exhibition Complex VDNH, on one of the main thoroughfares - Prospekt Mira - there is a four-star Cosmos Hotel. Hotel address: Prospect Mira, 150, Moscow, 129366.

Description of the hotel complex

The 25-story high-rise building of the hotel offers panoramic views of Moscow. It is especially convenient to enjoy them from the restaurant of the Planet Cosmos hotel, combining the process with tasting dishes and leisurely conversation in a business or family atmosphere.

Choosing the Cosmos Hotel (Moscow) for accommodation in the capital, guests get the opportunity to get to the city center in 20 minutes, take a walk in the Losiny Ostrov National Park located nearby. A walk will lead to the Ostankino Tower, to the Cosmonautics Museum. Two hundred meters from the hotel is the VDNH metro station, from where you can get to any corner of the capital without traffic jams and traffic problems.

Services

The hotel has a very rich and developed infrastructure for recreation. Muscovites and guests of the capital are famous for the Big Concert Hall of the Cosmos Hotel. It is designed for 1000 seats, equipped in accordance with modern standards and serves as an excellent platform for organizing concerts, shows, television programs.

A whole range of services for guests: a beauty salon, a fitness center with a gym, baths, pools, currency exchange, souvenir shops, parking, laundry, medical diagnostic center - all these services are offered by the Cosmos Hotel. Prices for services, according to guest reviews, correspond to their quality. The hotel offers a motorhome rental.

This service is becoming more and more popular. Those who wish receive training and driving experience on specially equipped technical equipment.

Events for business

Moscow is a center of business and business communications. The hotel has excellent opportunities for organizing business events of any level. You can hold a meeting in the meeting room, organize a conference, seminar, exchange delegations. All the equipment that is necessary in these cases is offered by the Cosmos hotel: a hall, technical equipment, communication, copying services, organization of receptions and banquets.

It is possible to order air and train tickets for groups and individual visitors, apply for a visa, order a transfer.

Entertainment

For vacationers in Moscow there is a huge selection of options for entertainment. Going to theaters, concerts and shows, visiting night clubs, walking along the Moscow River.

For holidaymakers, Cosmos Hotel (Moscow) offers to visit its own Solaris nightclub.

This is one of the first and most famous strip club in Eastern Europe. Spectacular theme programs are offered, you can play billiards, there are hookahs.

Excursions

Moscow has a large selection of excursion programs. You can purchase tickets at any tour desk, contact the reception of the Cosmos Hotel, and go to the Excursions counter. The following programs are offered here:

  • Visit to the Trinity-Sergius Lavra. Its main temple preserves the shrine - the relics of St. Sergey of Radonezh.
  • Sightseeing tour of Moscow. Several options for city tours with a choice of museums and themes are given: Tsvetaeva, Yesenin, Vysotsky.
  • Visiting iconic places in Moscow in the evening.
  • Excursion to the Ostankino tower.

Hotel guests in their reviews often note how well the place where the Cosmos hotel is located: they can easily reach VDNH without transport. This is an interesting Moscow expo complex for its history and present.

Vacation Reviews

Rest is often judged by the number of impressions. But guests of the capital pay no less attention to living conditions: how much the price corresponds to the quality, what they liked or what is unacceptable in the hotel service. The Cosmos Hotel, whose address is often reduced to the location of the station at VDNH, is liked by guests who visited it for its location. The hotel's infrastructure and its rich opportunities for recreation and entertainment receive a large number of ratings.

Cosmos Hotel, guest reviews of which give it a high rating and are ranked among the rating hotels in the capital, also has a number of comments in its luggage. In particular, there are often complaints about the quality of furniture for standard rooms. There are comments on junior suites - guests say that the rooms are smoked, the air in them is stale.

As a remedy, smoking is currently only permitted in designated areas. Pets are also not allowed.

The Cosmos Hotel has retained its popularity for over 30 years. This is a great option for convenient acquaintance with the possibilities of the capital.
