Mexicana Resort Sharm 4 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): photos, reviews

Surely every tourist knows that Sharm El Sheikh is an Egyptian city that is in incredible demand among tourists. Here, travelers can be offered comfortable accommodation, relaxation on a sandy beach and, of course, rich leisure. Naturally, in the city there are many hotel complexes of various sizes and categories. One of them is the Mexicana Resort Sharm. Many tourists today are looking for any additional information about this place. So what does the hotel look like and is it worth coming here?

Location Description





Where is the hotel complex Mexicana Sharm Resort 4 *? By the way, the distance to the Old Town with its picturesque streets and indescribable atmosphere is only 3 kilometers, so you can have fun there at any convenient time.

It is also worth saying that the hotel is located on the second coastline - the distance to the beach is 1.7 km. About 6 km away is the world-famous Naama Bay. The nearest international airport will have to cover a distance of about 18 km. Nevertheless, you should not worry about this, as the hotel provides its customers with a shuttle service.

What does the hotel area look like?

The Mexicana Resort Sharm hotel complex was built in 2000. This is a cozy place with a small but very beautiful area. Here, literally everything is buried in greenery, even residential two-story buildings are decorated with flowering, winding shrubs. You can chill in the shade of palm trees or stroll along the convenient alleys, enjoying the fresh air and the incredible beauty of nature. And here you will find swimming pools, comfortable terraces for relaxation, sports grounds. In a word, everything here is created for a truly comfortable stay. By the way, the last thorough reconstruction was carried out in 2007, although cosmetic repairs are constantly organized here.

Hotel complex Mexicana Sharm Resort: photo of rooms and description





The hotel has 194 rooms in the following categories:

  • 106 standard rooms;
  • 76 spacious family rooms that are great for a large family;
  • 12 suites.

From the room, of course, there is access to a private balcony or terrace, where you can relax (there is comfortable garden furniture) and observe the garden and hotel grounds. Naturally, in each room there is a sufficient amount of comfortable furniture and a set of household appliances. In particular, there is a TV on which some Russian and music channels are shown, as well as a telephone, air conditioning with an individual control panel and a safe, which is suitable for storing small things and documents. Filling the minibar is carried out only at the request of guests and is not included in the price.

Of course, each room has an individual toilet and a bathroom where you can take a shower. There is also a washbasin, toilet mirror, hairdryer, a set of towels, soap, shampoos and other hygiene items.

Nutrition: what can you count on?





For many travelers, the issue of nutrition is important. What does the Mexicana Resort Sharm offer? When you pay for your stay, you can choose the right food scheme yourself or refuse it at all. By the way, the most popular is the all-inclusive scheme, as it provides the possibility of full three meals a day, and also gives the right to receive drinks and light snacks during the day for free.

Main meals are served at Sombrero's main restaurant. The reviews indicate that the food is at a good level - there is a wide selection of international dishes, often fish and seafood are served at the table, and, of course, the cook delights guests with sweet oriental pastries every day.

In addition, there are other places where you can have a bite to eat at Mexicana Resort Sharm. Tourists recommend visiting the Alfredo restaurant, which prepares delicious pasta and offers a large selection of classic Italian dishes. At Pizza Corner you will always find fresh pizza topped with to your taste. Amigo Bar offers delicious cocktails, desserts and light snacks in a relaxed, almost homely atmosphere.

Where is the beach located? Rest of guests





What beach vacation can the Mexicana Sharm Resort offer? Sharm-el-Sheikh is a city famous for its beaches, mild climate and warm sea. As already mentioned, the beach is approximately 1.7 km from the hotel. Of course, most tourists do not like the prospect of covering such a distance every day. Therefore, the hotel provides its guests with a permanent transfer to the beach and back. The bus runs constantly throughout the day.

On the beach, which, by the way, belongs to another hotel, you can count on a fun holiday. The beach here is sandy, but due to the proximity to the coral reef it is worth getting the appropriate shoes. There is a backwater where children feel comfortable. On the shore there are sun loungers and umbrellas that you can use.

Tourists have the opportunity to spend more time. For example, rent a boat, ride a boat, water skiing, banana, jet skis and bicycles, do parasailing. Of course, one of the most popular activities among travelers is scuba diving. There is a lively reef near the shore - you can enjoy the beauty and diversity of the underwater world, even swimming with a mask. You will also have the opportunity to attend a diving school. Professionals can buy a tour to the most interesting places in the reef, as well as rent the necessary equipment. It is better for beginners to sign up for short training courses.

Is it possible to relax here with a child?





What can the child offer Mexicana Sharm Resort 4 * hotel? Traveler reviews indicate that this place is perfect for children. Kids can splash around in a special shallow pool. There is a spacious playground with a swing, where your child will also have a good time. And little travelers like to visit the mini club, where experienced animators and professional educators play with them.

Additional service for tourists





Hotel owners have tried to create all conditions for a comfortable stay of tourists. For example, laundry and dry cleaning will help to avoid a lot of small household problems. A currency exchange office is also constantly operating. Directly at the hotel you can rent a car or other type of transport for a reasonable price. Guests enjoy free local parking. There is no medical center as such, but in case of problems a good doctor will be called to you.

Are there any conditions for entertainment? Leisure of guests

Mostly beach lovers come to this hotel. Nevertheless, there is something to do on its territory. For example, in the courtyard there is a large freshwater pool where you can have a good time swimming or playing water polo. Comfortable sun loungers with mattresses are placed around - here guests relax, sunbathe and meet new neighbors.

Lovers of an active lifestyle can have a good time on the basketball court. There is also a tennis court, next to which you can rent balls and rackets. You can pass the time for a game of billiards or table tennis. The hotel offers bath services and experienced massage therapists. Local animators are entertained throughout the day.





Of course, this is far from all possible activities, because you arrived in Egypt. Hotel Mexicana Sharm Resort 4 * has its own excursion center, where they will tell you about the most interesting sights of the surrounding area and help you plan your travel itinerary. You can also get a ready-made tour, during which you will learn more about local history, culture and ancient traditions. This country is ideal for excursions.

Hotel Mexicana Sharm Resort 4 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): reviews

A lot of useful information can be obtained by asking the opinion of tourists who have already visited one place or another. So how about the Mexicana Resort Sharm? First of all, guests note that the territory is really big, beautiful and green - it is always nice to walk and relax here. The rooms are also quite spacious. In some rooms, furniture and appliances are not new, although everything is in working order. The disadvantage, perhaps, is the remoteness of the beach, although buses do run constantly. The hotel staff understands the Russian language, so you should not worry about the language barrier. And of course, living here is relatively inexpensive, which makes the hotel so popular among tourists looking for budget options for relaxing on the shores of the picturesque and warm Red Sea.
