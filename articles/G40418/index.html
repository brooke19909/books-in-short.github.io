Best hotels in Costa Brava (Spain)

Costa Brava is a famous resort in the northeast of Catalonia. It is the most beautiful region in the whole Mediterranean. Unapproachable cliffs and cliffs overgrown with coniferous trees, alternating with bays and bays with sandy and pebble beaches - all this is beautiful Costa Brava. The local coast was noted by people back in the days of ancient civilizations. Monuments of the past are still preserved here - the ruins of castles and ancient buildings, ancient monasteries and buildings. If you are planning a vacation on the Mediterranean coast, then you will probably be interested in information about the Costa Brava hotels. It is about them that will be discussed in our article.

Resort areas

In order to choose a Costa Brava hotel for relaxation, you need to understand the areas of the resort. The fact is that the coast stretched 240 kilometers from Blanes to Portbow itself. Blanes is the southernmost and closest resort to Barcelona. The area has excellent sandy beaches, a zoo and a water park. As for the sights, here is the botanical garden and the castle of St. John.

The next resort area is Lloret de Mar. This region is considered the most popular tourist destination. The area has an incredibly developed infrastructure. There are many tourists at any time of the year.

But the most picturesque area is considered to be Tossa de Mar. The resort attracts vacationers not only with its chic beaches, but also with the old center, where the medieval fortress is located. There is also an old casino, which was first opened in the nineteenth century.

But the resort town of Sagaro became known only in the twenties of the last century. Celebrities such as Humphrey Bogart and Elizabeth Taylor preferred to spend time here. Playa de Aro was once a small fishing village. And now the resort attracts guests with a large two-kilometer beach and interesting sights.

Seafood and fishing enthusiasts come to Palmos. But Figares is interesting to fans of surrealism, because here is the theater museum of Salvador Dali.

Empuriaravu is an amazing place. The resort area is dotted with a network of canals. Here you can moor your yacht right next to your apartment.
