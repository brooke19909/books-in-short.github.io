Sydney (Australia) - the main port of the Green Continent

Many tourists consider Australia a unique state, which, in addition to its high level of development, has various attractions. The largest city in the country, of course, is Sydney. It was founded back in 1788, but, unfortunately, did not become the capital of Australia, although to this day it is considered the visiting card of the state. This is due to the fact that both architectural monuments and business centers are located in the city.



Sydney (Australia) is primarily famous for its opera house, which in the modern world is considered one of the wonders of the world. This is the most outstanding building that was built in 1973. Since then, all tourists dream of visiting a famous institution in order to be imbued with its beauty and majesty. The creator of the unusual structure decided to build it in the form of many sails. The roof of the theater was lined with millions of tiles. The construction of the building lasted fourteen years. Many people know that Sydney (Australia), whose sights are striking in its diversity and originality, is considered a city of opportunities. This is due to the fact that wages are very high, and there are always a lot of jobs. It is also worth noting that in Sydney the mass of the population of different nationalities is concentrated. In this regard, you can meet people who speak Chinese, Indonesian, Greek and Russian.



In addition , Sydney (Australia) surprises with a variety of landscapes. In the city you can see beautiful bridges (in particular Harbor Bridge), visit the Royal Botanic Gardens, the Mint, the town hall, the TV tower, numerous parks and museums. Each tourist will also be able to relax on one of the Australian beaches, the most popular of which is Bondai. Its length is one kilometer. Upon arrival in Sydney (Australia), many tourists are invited to visit the world famous Tarong Zoo. It opened in 1916, today the territory is divided into eight zones. A total of 340 species of animals live in the zoo. All lovers of the underwater world should definitely visit the Sydney Aquarium. More than 650 species of various fish and other inhabitants of the depths are concentrated here. Visitors will be able to see marine animals through underwater tunnels. In this case, a shark and other fish can swim over the audience.



For many, Australia (including the city of Sydney) is a country of wonders and unique sights. Tourists will be interested in visiting the Cathedral of the Virgin Mary, which was recognized as a national shrine, the Museum of Justice and Police, the pier of Wollumulu Bay (whose length is 410 meters) and Elizabeth's farm. Many are attracted by the Sydney TV Tower, whose height is 309 m. The building was built over six years, and as a result, $ 36 million was spent on its construction. Visitors have the opportunity to visit the observation deck and see the city, as if in the palm of your hand. Sydney (Australia) is full of mysteries and interesting places, it is here that every tourist can find entertainment of any kind. And leaving him, everyone will hope for a speedy return to the most important city in the world.
