DIY decoration and painting of wedding glasses

A wedding is a solemn moment in the life of every person, and everyone wants to make it memorable and bright. In advance newlyweds think over all the main points. One of the main accessories of the wedding ceremony are glasses. It is after a sip of champagne from these holiday vessels that the lovers kiss.

In the article we will look at how you can beautifully paint your wedding glasses with your own hands, we will tell you all the necessary details and nuances. After such tips, it will be easy to make decoration. This work is fascinating, even a novice master can do it.

Pattern - Feathers

Let's start with a simple pattern. First you need to buy simple glass glasses without a picture and prepare the necessary materials:

  1. Paint simple acrylic or contour gold color.
  2. Surface degreasing agent - alcohol or nail polish remover (vinegar is also suitable).
  3. Cotton sponges.
  4. Feather pattern.
  5. Scotch.
  6. Thin brush.




DIY wedding wine glass painting: step-by-step technique

First you need to choose a template for the future drawing. If you yourself do not know how to draw beautifully, then this does not matter. In various stores for needlework, many options are presented for future designs.





You can choose the feather served above, which has a heart in its inner part. For a wedding, this option is perfect.

On the inside of the glass you need to glue the cut out template onto the tape. On the outside, before painting, it is recommended to wipe thoroughly the entire surface with alcohol. Only then can you start painting your wedding glasses with your own hands.

Then you need to put them to dry. It is advisable to leave them no less than a day. At the end, you can still cover the surface with acrylic varnish. Then the drawing will remain for a long time.

Contrast drawing

To make such beautiful glasses you need only two-color paints that will be contrasting. Do-it-yourself workshop for painting wedding glasses with your own hands read on.

Then the acrylic varnish is opened on top of the blue layer. When the product is completely dry, you can continue to paint your wedding glasses with your own hands.





For curly stains, get contour paint. Such splashes can be performed without a template, by eye. This is not difficult. You need to finish the pattern smoothly, moving to individual points. After drying, again varnish and put on the day.

The transition between contrasting colors must also be made out so that the junction is not visible. To do this, you need rhinestones small gold and larger silver. We glue the leg first with two rows of small pebbles. We‚Äôll also have large ones nearby.

Using Modeling Elements

Glasses that are decorated with plastic parts look very nice. It is not difficult to mold from such a material; molding methods can be tried on clay or dough. If you get flowers or any other elements, then you can get to work. It is better to buy imported plastic, in the reviews of needlewomen praise Belgian products. Separately sculpt all the details and stick to the glass goblets.





If the details are small and do not grasp the bends of the glasses, then they can be dried separately. If the details are large, for example, a swan, then they dry it in the oven with a glass. Do not be afraid, the glass will not crack. You need to put the blanks in a cold oven, then it, gradually warming up, will dry the elements that will take the form of a round surface. Drying takes place within 10-15 minutes, not more. Then you need to carefully remove the stucco molding from the glass and, degreasing the surface of the glass, glue the molding to the Moment Crystal glue or using an adhesive gun. You can also try PVA glue.

Leaf decoration

If the glasses are decorated with plastic over the entire surface, including the leg, then the stucco molding cannot be removed after baking. But when covering the entire glass, the plastic hardens, hardens and will not budge. So you can leave the product after baking in the same form, without gluing.
