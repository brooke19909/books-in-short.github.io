History

The building of the European Hotel in St. Petersburg was built at the end of the 17th century. A.I. Postnikov, P.I. Gaberzettel demolished the old structures and built new stone houses. After the design of the Russian architect K.I. Rossi, the building was rebuilt again. Already in 1875, according to the project of Ludwig Frantsevich Fontan, a hotel in eclectic forms was built.

At the beginning of the last century , the Europe restaurant was created in the hotel . The hotel was rebuilt many times, the style inside the hotel changed 4 times, a system of identical rooms was also introduced, an attic floor with the restaurant "Roof" was built.

During the war, the hotel was a hospital, when it ended, it again began to function as before. Soon, another restructuring was carried out under the leadership of I. G. Kaptsyug and S. V. Panov-Gunich.

In 2005-2008, the last general reconstruction was carried out, it was spent $ 2 million, the rooms were decorated by the French designer Michel Joan. In 2009, the hotel was awarded the "Best Hotel in Europe and the World".

Location

Hotel "European" is located in the former capital of Russia, in the city of St. Petersburg. It was built in 1703 on the coast of the Gulf of Finland, at the mouth of the Neva River. The city ranks second in terms of population, has federal significance for the country. This is the main cultural and historical center of Russia, the city symbolizes the beginning of the existence of the Russian Empire and its status as a European superpower.

Address of the European Hotel in St. Petersburg: 1/7 Mikhailovskaya Street, Central District. The hotel occupies the entire left side of the street, with its windows offering stunning views of Nevsky Prospekt.
