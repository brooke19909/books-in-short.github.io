"Beeline Smart 2": reviews on the smartphone, description, price

The mobile operator Beeline introduced its second brainchild on the domestic market - a budget smartphone for 490 rubles. The previous model was supplied by stock, which contained seven months of using communication services, which is a considerable period. The current version of Smart-2 can also be purchased for 490 rubles, however, the period of use of the package of services from Beeline is reduced to four months.

Also, manufacturers equipped the smartphone with mobile TV and made some improvements to the technical characteristics of the device. In Western countries, mobile operators have long begun to sponsor the release of mobile devices for their SIM cards. In the United States, you can buy a modern iPhone, which will cost the buyer several times cheaper than its cost, but the user must enter into a two-year contract with a particular company.





According to the laws of the Russian Federation, the user can change the mobile operator at any time. For suppliers of communications there is no sense and benefit to conclude contracts for a long time. Russian operators began to look for a way out and come up with various solutions to the situation. One of these options was the Beeline Smart 2 communicator, the user reviews of which we will now consider. So, everything in order.

Beeline Smart 2: reviews, specifications, features

The smartphone supports the operation of only one proprietary SIM card, if desired, you can unlock it for another operator. The mobile device has a dual-core processor with a capacity of 1.2 GHz. With regard to the power of Beeline Smart 2, the reviews of the owners indicate that it is enough to perform simple tasks: working with applications, surfing the Internet and watching videos. For most games with high graphics or large applications, the smartphone is not suitable. In addition, the device has the following characteristics:

  • Android 4.2.2 operating system
  • 4 GB of internal memory;
  • RAM 512 MB;
  • screen size 4 inches, like most budget models;
  • The battery contains 1200 mAh.

Despite the availability of the Beeline Smart 2 smartphone, user reviews often emphasize that the display can please with good color reproduction and viewing angles. It supports 16 million colors, with a resolution of 400x840 pixels. The functions have a brightness adjustment key, which allows you to achieve optimal performance. There are proximity sensors and lighting. Photo and video

Let us consider in more detail what other Beeline Smart 2 smartphone reviews are. Photos and videos in this device, unfortunately, are not at the highest level. There is no front camera in the mobile phone, but the main one is only 3.2 megapixels. However, it is possible to configure various shooting parameters, this allows you to take photos that are quite acceptable in quality, despite the fact that the phone is budget-friendly. In conditions of good lighting, the pictures are quite good, they lack blurriness and noise. After taking the photo, you can immediately upload it to social networks.





As for the weaknesses of the Beeline Smart 2 camera, the reviews of the owners name the absence of a flash among the shortcomings. The smartphone supports wireless communications and navigation. What are you getting

The package consists of the smartphone itself, a charger, a Beeline SIM card, a connecting cable, a warranty card and a user manual. There is no memory card, just like a headset. Manufacturers, apparently, did not consider it necessary to invest in 2 or 4 gigabytes of media. However, this is not so important for those who lack 1.7 GB of internal memory. Inserting a large card in volume and because of this increase the cost of a mobile device does not make sense. Some users may well do without additional memory.





Discussing the Beeline Smart 2 smartphone (reviews confirm this), users were much more sensitive to the lack of a headset in the kit, as this leads to the inability to use radio and mobile television. Manufacturers should have taken this fact into account and included at least cheap headphones. Now it is recommended to purchase a headset with a smartphone so that all the functionality of the device is available.
