Recreation center, Shchurovo. Base “Forget-me-not”, “Alpha”, “Dewdrop”, “Pine”, “Ozerki”

Today we’ll talk about recreation facilities in Shchurovo. This is a cozy resort corner in the Krasnolimansky district of Donetsk region. Numerous recreation centers are located on the Seversky Donets River near the Blue Lakes. There are also various sanatoriums and dispensaries in the area. Numerous enterprises of the city send their employees here for treatment and recovery.





We will talk about the most famous recreation centers so that you can make your choice and go on a vacation next summer.

The youngest recreation center Shchurovo - base "Forget-me-not"

Quite small, but very comfortable, it will gladly open the doors for its guests. It works all year round, giving people the opportunity to appreciate the beauty of each season. Pine forest, fresh air and ringing silence - isn't that why we are trying to get out of the city? They place tourists in cozy white-stone cottages. Double or quadruple rooms are at your service. For a friendly company there are large six-bed apartments. And if you want privacy, then we advise you to rent a separate house, which is designed for four people.

On the territory there is an amazing Russian bathhouse, billiards, a dining room and a wonderful children's town. As already mentioned, this is a new recreation center Shchurovo. The Forget-Me-Not base was built with love for people and nature. Located between centuries-old pine trees, it organically blended into the life of the forest.

Entertainment for guests

While the children are busy on the territory (and there are many opportunities here - bicycles and rollers, chess and tennis, cartoons and pools), you can go camping. Today, the administration of the base offers an amazing weekend tour - rafting on the river. It has nothing to do with extreme rafting. This is a family event during which you can admire the beauty of the river bed.





Bonus will be a night in tents, songs by the fire and porridge with smoke. The next day you return to Shchurovo. The recreation center "Forget-me-not" again welcomes dear guests.

Hotel complex "Alpha"

This is a stunningly cozy and beautiful complex. Right on the territory of the base there is a huge pool around which there are cottages for guests. This is not just a place for a pleasant pastime, of which there are a lot in Shchurovo. The recreation center "Alpha" is the best option for a vacation or vacation. A magnificent park area, silence and the stunning smell of coniferous forest, calm atmosphere - all this makes it possible to have a great time. A well-equipped playground will not let your children get bored, and open gazebos with barbecue facilities will become a favorite place for leisurely conversations over a glass of tea or a glass of wine. Animators work at the base, who will try to brighten up your leisure time and engage in the entertainment of children. There are sports fields and rental of various equipment.





For accommodation of guests double rooms in one-story houses are provided. Each of them has a separate entrance, refrigerator, shower with hot and cold water. There are also two-story cottages of superior comfort. These rooms are additionally equipped with a kitchen. This is the best that Shchurovo recreation center can offer you. Base "Alpha" has been operating for a long time and is known for excellent conditions for tourists.

Base "Dewdrop"

Tired of a noisy city? Then Shchurovo (recreation center) is waiting for you. "Dewdrop" is waiting for you constantly, regardless of the time of year or the weather outside. Here, every tourist will find a lot of entertainment to their liking. Inexpensive and very cozy houses are equipped with everything necessary. There are apartments for two, three and four people. All houses are equipped with beds and wardrobes, armchairs and refrigerators. For each person a set of necessary utensils is issued. All houses are heated and receive guests all year round. Each of them has an equipped kitchen. Very small in area is the recreation center (Shchurovo). The base includes only 7 houses, including one two-story building for 32 people, that is, a large and friendly company.

Rest and leisure

The Seversky Donets River is famous for its fishing. If you belong to lovers of sitting on the shore with a fishing rod, then you will definitely like it here. Awesome pike and crucian carp, common carp and tench are caught in this place. The territory of the base provides a huge number of options for a great pastime. There are summer showers and gazebos, a playground and shops, a cafe, a parking lot and billiards. Near the base there is a disco, cafe, boat and catamaran rental.





Be sure to visit the cafe "Dewdrop" (Schurovo). Recreation facilities, the photos of which allure to gather on the road, offer not only a great time in nature, but also sit at a table in a cozy cafe, as well as try one of several dozen dishes prepared by professional chefs.

The recreation center "Pine"

This is one of the most comfortable and modern bases in the area. It is very different from the rest, where small wooden houses organically fit into the surrounding forest landscape. Here you will find a real country hotel - three three-story buildings, equipped with everything necessary. Each room has a refrigerator and TV, upholstered furniture and household appliances. Not everyone likes traveling from the city to settle in a large white-stone hotel with all amenities. Someone will enjoy the sun-heated wooden house or tent under the stars.





This is a very welcoming, green and amazingly cozy place where you are expected at any time of the year. The local beach is very happy - the cleanest and most beautiful in the whole territory of Shchurovo (recreation center). "Pine" most often becomes the choice of beach lovers. It has everything you need - a large number of umbrellas and deck chairs, tents and mushrooms, fine sand and a smooth descent to the water. This is especially important for those who are going to relax with their children. The beach here is quite small, but there are not too many people here. Based on a total of 87 rooms, each is designed for two or three people.

Would you like to visit the Forest House?

This is another wonderful recreation center, which allows you to spend your holidays in peace and quiet with pleasure. This is a special place that is created for unity with nature at any time of the year. Here you will find a large two-story cottage, made entirely of wood. In addition, small and cozy wooden log cabins are scattered throughout the base, under the arches of which it is so wonderful to listen to how autumn rain is knocking, or to watch how snow is falling. And in the summer you can go to the site in front of the house and sit in a cozy gazebo.





Almost under every tree there is a hammock stretched here, which anyone can use to read a book. And when you get tired, you can play table tennis, basketball or badminton. Here you can rent any sports equipment. A large town has been built for children, where you can jump on trampolines, swing on a swing and play in the sandbox. And for adults, the most wonderful vacation is a walk through a fabulous forest rich in mushrooms and berries. And the main decoration of the base is a living corner, where beautiful squirrels and magnificent pheasants live. Animals can be fed directly from the hands.

The recreation center "Ozerki" (Shchurovo)

Another modern recreation center that invites you to spend your vacation in a stunningly beautiful place, in a coniferous forest, on the banks of the river. It is located just one kilometer from the blue lakes, which is why it got such a poetic name. This base is a four-story cottage, which is located in an area surrounded by greenery. Guests are accommodated in double or triple rooms with all amenities. There are cafes and several cozy shops. And if you rent a boat, you can ride on the Black Lake, famous for its wonderful water lilies.





Any of the listed recreation centers can be a great option for a summer vacation. Some work only in the summer, others await their guests all year round. Be sure to come here with the whole family, and you will be provided with pleasant memories.
