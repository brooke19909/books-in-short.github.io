Pension "Olshaniki" (Leningrad Oblast, Vyborgsky District)

Residents of the metropolis strive to spend their weekend away from the gassed air, stay closer to nature and enjoy the peace and quiet. To truly relax and gain strength invites suburban pension "Olshaniki." It harmoniously combines comfort, privacy and picturesque landscapes.

Introductory Information

This place is located near the center of St. Petersburg, between Lake Ladoga and the Gulf of Finland (the territory of the Karelian Isthmus), in the Vyborg district, in the small village of Olshaniki. Near the complex is a clean lake under the romantic name Chernyavskoye, surrounded by mighty relic pines and conifers.





The amazing boarding house "Olshaniki" (Leningrad Region) is a place of multifaceted leisure, vivid impressions and a memorable vacation. For guests, profile treatment is offered in the wellness center. The resort has its own agricultural production (meat and dairy farm, greenhouse and vegetable garden for growing vegetables).

At the tables, guests get only ecologically clean and healthy food. Both children and adults are invited to rest at any time of the year. For everyone there are interests and activities to their liking. Fans of a relaxing holiday will be able to walk for berries and mushrooms, as well as fish and explore local attractions.

For lovers of thrills, various ecotours, active games, and in winter skiing and ice skating are offered. Leisure options are unthinkable. Thanks to the hospitality and hospitality of the administration, high service and security, the resort has gained an excellent reputation among nonresident guests and local residents.

What is included in the payment of a spa vacation voucher?





Wellness boarding house "Olshaniki" for an acceptable payment offers an interesting entertainment and treatment program. By purchasing a ticket, you can freely visit the sauna, pool and reading room. The payment includes a variety of four meals a day, accommodation in comfortable apartments and a parking space.

In addition, you will be provided with high-quality and highly professional medical care. On certain days, a cultural and entertainment program is organized. There is also a rental point for renting sports equipment: sledges, boats, skis, skates, bicycles and more.

Terms of Accommodation





The number of rooms is represented by comfortable three-story cottages with rooms of different categories. To accommodate the boarding house "Olshaniki" (Leningrad region) offers standard rooms and suites with a spacious loggia (view of the park area). All rooms have a private bathroom and bath. The complex can accommodate up to 140 people at a time.

The standard room for two visitors has a small corridor, a color TV, a compact refrigerator, bedside tables and a wardrobe.

The apartments are divided into two zones: a bedroom and a living room. There are two beds in the bedroom, in the living room there is a couch with a wall. There is the necessary appliances. A fold-out sofa chair is provided.

Supply system





This is one of the few farming complexes. All natural food from your garden, without harmful impurities and chemical additives. Serving is carried out according to a certain schedule - 4 times a day.

A large room for 160 people was allocated for this. Pension "Olshaniki" takes care of its guests and offers only fresh products, so to speak, in the heat of the heat. The menu has a huge selection of diet foods. The client independently chooses the food he likes.

Medical services

Almost all recreation centers in the Leningrad region of the Vyborg region are equipped with treatment centers. The "Olshaniki" complex is not an exception. A wellness building has been built on the territory where you can improve your health. The main profile of the sanatorium is diseases of the cardiovascular and musculoskeletal systems.

For proper treatment, the tourist needs to provide a personal card to the therapist who prescribes certain tests (blood, urine, ultrasound, X-ray, ECG) and only after receiving the result determines the therapy. The treatment and diagnostic base is expanded, includes physiotherapy and massage rooms.

All equipment is modern, and specialists are professional and experienced. Here you can go through heat therapy, electrotherapy, hydrotherapy, phototherapy. Gymnastics and classes are held with patients right in the pool. If necessary, inhalations and injections are prescribed.

Contraindications to therapy

There are some limitations in treatment. Absolutely all tourists, getting into the Olshanyki complex (Leningrad region) on a package trip, undergo mandatory diagnostics. If during the examination the doctor reveals malignant or benign formations, then therapy will be refused. Contraindications also include infectious diseases and exacerbated pathologies.

Internal infrastructure





In order for young tourists to have something to do on vacation, they equipped a spacious cinema hall where children can watch cartoons. Not all recreation centers in the Leningrad Region are equipped with such entertainment. There is also a separate safe pool for babies and a playground. For swimming life rings and vests are issued.

Adult tourists will not be deprived either. In a separate building there is a sports hall with a rental point. Here, depending on the season, you can rent sledges, bicycles, fishing equipment, skis, skates, tennis balls and rackets. There is a swimming pool and three saunas.





Spacious playgrounds were built for active games: football, basketball, volleyball. There is a billiard room and a tennis table. In the summer months, the beach season opens. Sun loungers and mattresses are provided.

You can take a boat and go along the expanse of the lake to admire the local beauties and fish. In the evening, entertainment shows and a disco await you. For tourists, they organize interesting and funny performances, hold draws and competitions. If you wish, they will organize a tour of the area for you, show and tell about the local attractions.

Pension "Olshaniki": how to get to the place?





There are several ways to get to the tourist village:

  • By public transport (by bus): according to the schedule, routes No. 675, 678 and 858 leave from Ozerki station daily. You will reach them to Pervomaisky, then you will have to follow your own path or take a taxi to the sanatorium.
  • Convenient and faster to drive your own car. The road to the boarding house "Olshaniki" will take about an hour. You need to go along the Scandinavian highway, turn towards Pervomaisky and go straight to Olshanka.
  • The third method - by train from the Finland railway station to the station. Roshchino. From there, bus number 124 runs (rarely).

The complex organizes a shuttle once a week - on Mondays. Departure time of the bus from st. Lakes are checked with the administrator. You should find out detailed information in advance by calling the Olshaniki boarding house.
