Zurich hotels: overview, selection, reviews

Zurich is Switzerland's largest city by area and population. Since Zurich is the central hub of the Swiss railway network and also operates the largest and busiest airport in the country, it is usually the first place tourists come to. Due to the proximity of the city to the tourist resorts in the Swiss Alps, it is called the ‚Äúportal to the Alps‚Äù. Zurich has more than 50 museums, more than 100 galleries, about 60 movie theaters, and most of the old sights are in the old city - along the river and on the waterfront.

To visit Zurich, you need a Schengen visa to Switzerland, according to which you can visit other countries of the Schengen agreement. To obtain a visa, you need a valid passport with two blank pages.

Hotel on the shopping street

Luxurious elegance

Baur au Lac is a real attraction of the city. The luxury hotel is located in a park overlooking the lake. Designer shops are located nearby. The international airport is a 20-minute drive away. Since its opening in 1884, it has been a family hotel.

It offers 119 rooms ranging from 25 to 100 square meters. The interiors of the rooms are decorated in various styles, all furniture is custom-made. Marble bathrooms are equipped with modern fixtures.

The hotel has a Pavillon restaurant, in which the chef is awarded a Michelin star. Rive Gauche Terrace offers light snacks, refreshing drinks and homemade ice creams.

Hotel Baur au Lac is an exclusive and quite cozy place in the very center of the city.

Zurich hotels: traveler reviews

It is quite difficult to find a cheap hotel in Zurich. Cheap (by local standards) rooms can be rented only in not the most pleasant areas. In the center, even the simplest hostel room will cost more than a three-star hotel in another country.

Hotels in the old town always meet all modern requirements: breakfast, free Internet, excellent service, comfortable rooms.

Getting a visa to Switzerland is not difficult, so you should visit this small country to get acquainted with Zurich and its attractions.
