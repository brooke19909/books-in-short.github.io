General Rokhlin: life and death



Not only throughout his short life, but also after death, General Rokhlin attracted the close attention of the people. He went his life's path in the pursuit and struggle aimed at improving the quality of life of the whole country. A strong army, a developed science, a stable economy are all for the good of mankind.
