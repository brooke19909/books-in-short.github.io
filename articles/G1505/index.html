Hotel Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort 5 *: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

When choosing Egypt as a country for spending holidays alone or with the whole family, the hotel you are planning to stay in is of paramount importance. Only by evaluating comprehensively the information received from the tour operator and from real tourists who spent their holidays in it, you can get an approximate idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe place.

There are plenty of hotels on the Red Sea coast, and it can be difficult to make a choice. We will tell you about one of them, which has a good rating and positive reviews, is the Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort 5 *. He declares his status and stardom, which means that there is reason to safely count on comfort and an interesting vacation in it.

Location

The location of the hotel plays an almost paramount role, because many go on vacation solely to see the sea, swim in it and enjoy the clean beaches. Someone wants to be in the center and in the thick of events, others - in silence and complete tranquility, away from the city bustle. It is the second option that this hotel offers.





It should be borne in mind that the Renaissance Golden View Beach Hotel (Paris 3, 4, 5) has its own network around the world, including in France, Germany, and the USA. They differ in price category and level of comfort.

The indicated hotel in Egypt is located just 350 meters from the sandy beach and the sea (first coastline). The road from and to the airport will also not take you too much time, because it is only 18 km away. Nearby is the famous Naama Bay, and three kilometers away is the Old Town and many local attractions that can be reached on foot.

Hotel Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort 5 *: description

The buildings of the complex, and accordingly the entire infrastructure, are quite new. They were built in 2003, the last restoration (partial) was carried out in 2011. The hotel includes a one-story building - the place of registration of guests, as well as several two-story - rooms for vacationers (total - 384).

Proximity to the sea and a large area for walking and entertainment make it a very convenient place to relax. You do not have to leave the complex in order to get to the beach (length - about 350 meters), everything is located within the reach of pedestrians. Sun loungers, umbrellas and large towels are provided free of charge. A significant area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is 95 thousand square meters. m

Room Categories

Almost four hundred comfortable rooms of Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort 5 * are divided into two large groups: premium and deluxe. All of them differ depending on the area, view from the window and spaciousness.

  • Deluxe Room is located on 36 m 2 , has a bathtub combined with a shower, and a balcony or terrace with a picturesque view of the garden.
  • Deluxe Room with a similar area overlooking the sea coast of Egypt.
  • Premium Sea View Room - 36 m 2 , view of the Red Sea.
  • Premium Suite - windows overlook the garden or the coast, area - 90 m 2 , bedroom and living room.




If a need suddenly arises, an extra bed can be provided to hotel guests for free, but only one. There are rooms for those with reduced mobility and non-smokers. Pets are not allowed. The selected food system (breakfast, full or half board, all inclusive) can be paid at the time of booking or on the spot.

Restaurants and bars on site

The most important institution is the Coast open-air restaurant, which offers visitors a choice of international and regional dishes. A comfortable and pleasant place for a romantic dinner or breakfast, because tables are served right on the open-air terrace.

In addition, the resort Renaissance Golden View 5 * has its own Italian (Il Palio) and Mexican (Acapulco Joe's) restaurants.

Drinks and oriental cocktails can be enjoyed in the Aqua Bar, La Rotunda, Lobby Bar and Zinzina bars, which serve the most delicious and aromatic coffee, as well as wine.





The hotel offers a romantic dinner during golden sunset on the beach as an additional service. This is the perfect place for a secluded evening together in the most hidden corner of your own beach.

Pool and beach

As already mentioned, the hotel has its own private, 350 m long sandy beach. It has a pontoon with a length of 65 meters. Leisure facilities include free sun loungers, towels, umbrellas and mattresses. The hotel recommends that you use special shoes on the beach so as not to damage your legs on the coral. The clean coast and the hot sun around the Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort 5 * will give you true moments of pleasure, if you wish, you can dive with a mask and get acquainted with the inhabitants of the Red Sea, some of which are visible to the naked eye through the water column.

The complex has four outdoor swimming pools, two of which are heated in the winter. In the mornings, near them or on the beach, you can enjoy conducting gymnastics classes in the company of professional trainers. Near the pools there is a relaxation area with sun loungers and parasols. Wi-Fi catches only near the lobby bar, the service is paid.





Wellness and Health

Swim in the sea or pool and sunbathe on the beach - this is the minimum range of hotel services and the most modest. It has an excellent SPA center that will satisfy the needs of even the most demanding customers. Pleasure is not free and is not included in the basic packages, but it costs additional costs. Various types of massage, a sauna and a steam room, a jacuzzi, wellness treatments aimed at body and face care - all this is ready to be provided by the Renaissance Sharm el Sheikh Golden View Beach Resort 5 *. Everyone can work out in the spacious and comfortable gym (for a fee).





Entertainment and sports

Beach holidays and the proximity of the sea determine a very standard set of entertainment, but at the same time, necessary for every tourist. An indisputable advantage is the fact that the hotel offers its guests free water sports (motor), as well as beach volleyball and darts. For an additional fee, at Renaissance Sharm el Sheikh Golden View Beach you can play table tennis or regular table tennis on your own small court. In addition, if you wish, you can go through basic training in a diving center and try to dive to the bottom of the Red Sea under the supervision of professional and experienced instructors.





Every day animators work with all the guests, organizing entertainment programs and evening shows, and in the evening there is live music. The hotel provides a shuttle service to the Old Town and the Naama Bay. It‚Äôs free for the dolphin show, and there‚Äôs a discount on entry fees.

Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort 5 *: location reviews

The total hotel rating for all reviews (about 1300) is 7.8 on a ten-point scale. From their experience, many tourists are convinced that, preferring the All Inclusive system, you need to choose the hotel very meticulously, because in it, most likely, you will spend most of the time. In terms of location, this complex is only pleasing to everyone. The place is cozy, away from the hustle and bustle, has a large landscaped area and you can enjoy walking and enjoying nature. Cleanliness reigns everywhere, the pools are crystal clear.

Due to the fact that the Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort 5 * is located on a hill, the whole territory is replete with winding paths and stairs, descents and ascents. The buildings and reception area are located in different buildings. According to our compatriots, for people with disabilities and problems with the musculoskeletal system, this can become a problem.

Rooms and service

Judging by the reviews left by tourists who have already visited the Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort 5 * hotel, everyone was satisfied with the rooms. They are such as stated in the photo and in the advertisement: spacious, furnished, not old furniture, all communications and plumbing are in perfect order.

The staff is generally friendly and answers all questions. Cleaning is done daily, however, not always with high quality and sometimes not at all quickly. Some vacationers note the rudeness of the staff and his desire to constantly evade work.

Nutrition

In this matter, the opinions of vacationers from different countries, including Russia, were divided. Tourists note the average quality of alcoholic beverages and the limited "all inclusive" time. In general, restaurants, bars, as well as drinks and food in them, visitors rate the solid four. This, in principle, does not correspond to the declared level of the hotel. To some, the food seemed rather monotonous, but nevertheless tasty and of high quality.

Sea and beach

Clear water and a wonderful reef with colorful fish were remembered by many guests and it is not surprising that this is what Egypt is famous for. 5 * Renaissance Golden View Beach Resort, however, according to them, was cunning when he announced the open beach. Sunset at sea is possible only from the pontoon. From the shore it is very shallow, the water level reaches the knees, and then the coral reef begins, and all the rescuers stop. But the sea most often pleases with calm and the absence of strong drafts and wind is noted.





Opinion was also divided on the organization of the work of animators. Some noted the absolute primitiveness and boredom in games and entertainment, while others, on the contrary, thank the company of lovely girls for their quality work and memorable leisure time at sea. Many appreciated the lesson in aerobics (regular and water) and yoga.

Summing up, we can say that the Renaissance Sharm el Sheikh Golden View Beach Resort hotel reviews are mostly positive, but with an indication of some shortcomings in organizational matters. It is hoped that the administration of the complex will take all the wishes into account and take note of the comments to make this place even more pleasant and interesting.

Recommendations

The hotel is located in a quiet area. Tourists who have been there recommend to get out of it and be sure to visit two restaurants. The first - Italian - "La Luna", luxurious, expensive and sophisticated. It is located in another five-star hotel, on the second floor, and the windows overlook the coast, which allows you to enjoy the pleasant breeze and starry sky over Egypt. Dishes of Italian cuisine are prepared from high-quality and freshest products, tables need to be reserved in advance.

The second restaurant is Souk, it is a whole gastronomic complex, one of a kind in this resort. The design and menu harmoniously combine the elegance and sophistication of Southern Europe and the sophistication of the mysterious East.

Tired of leisure, you can always get to the heart of the Old Town, which is located only three kilometers from the hotel. It is like immersion in a completely different, unfamiliar world. Oriental bazaars and souvenir shops with handmade products, the smell of oriental spices and sweets, the culture of Ancient Egypt, museums and architectural monuments. And, of course, traditional cuisine. The streets are filled with the aromas of delicious street food, you just can not help but try it. Tempting cafes and bars will surely treat their visitors with delicious coffee, kebabs, ice cream and fruit, grilled meat and various sweets.
