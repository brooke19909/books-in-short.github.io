Openwork knitting: patterns, patterns, products

Openwork knitting allows you to create unusually beautiful things for yourself and your loved ones. Basically, thin yarn is chosen for such products. These products are very lightweight. They are often worn in the summer. Girls in such outfits look very attractive. You can independently knit summer sundresses, stoles, shawls, sweaters and even swimwear.





Openwork - the beauty of products

Openwork knitting requires utmost care. After all, it is necessary in each row to monitor the number of knitted loops and make reductions or crochets in time in accordance with the scheme. First you should choose a model of the future product and decide on the pattern. After all, there are many schemes with descriptions that help create original, attractive products.

Among the wide variety of elegant clothes, lace sweaters stand out especially. These gentle, attractive and at the same time practical products have many advantages.

Each needlewoman, having decided to master openwork knitting, will be able to create a delicate and attractive image. A sweater with a through ornament transforms a woman, adds charm and freshness to the image. That's why many people want to have at least one such thing in their wardrobe.





Fashionistas of all ages use such clothes, thereby emphasizing their own unique style and originality.

Openwork sweater

A jumper is a product that resembles a sweater, is worn over the head and has no gate. This is a functional, convenient and comfortable clothing. Openwork knitting allows you to create a festive outfit that adds a twist to the image of a fashionable woman. A distinctive feature of such a product is lightness, bulkiness, texture.

Women's openwork sweater is a great option for walking, and for important meetings, and for festive events.

Modern designers offer many models of lace products. They differ not only in shape, length, but also in the way of decoration. The age of the model should also be considered. For young girls, it is better to choose shortened products with a deep neckline and short sleeves. For older women, products with a V-neck and a length below the conditional line of the hips are suitable. Sweaters with lace inserts are suitable for absolutely everyone.

Palatines, shawls

These unusually beautiful products are loved by many women, regardless of age. Modern openwork stoles have many fashionistas in their wardrobe. Often, mohair is used to make them . This yarn is not only warm, tender, but also unusually fluffy.

To make such a cozy and beautiful product yourself, you need to choose a suitable and simple openwork knitting pattern. The description of one of them is later in the article.





Experienced needlewomen recommend that beginners choose simple patterns, knit a pattern several times, and only after that start work on a lace product.

hint

Stoles and shawls are knitted from thin threads, but with large knitting needles. For example, the stole is knitted on knitting needles No. 4.5-5.0 mm from 100 g / 550 m yarn. The choice of knitting needles directly depends on the size of future holes on the openwork product.

Such stoles and shawls protect well from the cold and wind. They will be a great gift for family and friends.

Conclusion

As you can see, openwork knitting is quite popular. With the description you can associate a light, delicate element of clothing that emphasizes your originality and individuality. The main thing is to carefully monitor the sequence of loops in the circuit. And before starting work, practice a little and knit the desired pattern several times.

A self-knitted sweater or stole of thin, fluffy threads will delight you every day. Warmth, tenderness, grace - these are synonyms of the word openwork .
