Halloween ghost costume for adults and children

At the end of October, in all English-speaking countries, All Saints Day is celebrated. Traditionally, Halloween is accompanied by costumed parties, trips to friends and neighbors, begging for sweets and sweets. An interesting tradition has come to our country, but more often it is celebrated by adults in restaurants, cafes and other entertainment venues.

Costumes should be intimidating in order to frighten evil spirits who can walk free on this day. In the windows of houses put a terrible face cut out of a pumpkin and illuminated by a candle or a flashlight. One of the most popular outfits is a ghost costume. In the article we will consider several options for such products for adults and children.

Easiest option

Such an outfit is sewn for both children and adults. You will need black guipure, white fabric, colored trimmings for patches, black marker, needle and thread.

White fabric is folded in half and sewn on the sides with an internal seam. A few centimeters are left unstitched on the sides for the hands. Outline the middle. Where the eyes will be located, you need to cut two large ovals. In the middle a little lower is a circle for the nose. On the inside, the circles are lined with black guipure. The character will look and breathe through the transparent fabric, and only black elements will be visible from the outside.





From below, the fabric is incised with sharp corners or made a fringe at the edges. Black marker draw eyebrows, mouth. Sew in the center a pair of patches. These bright details are arranged at will. A character in a ghost costume can be given a bag of sweets made in the shape of a pumpkin.

Option for a boy

It is better to make a ghost costume for children so that the head is not completely closed, since the child in the previous version will feel uncomfortable. The Halloween costume outfit below consists of two elements: hats and drape. They are made of warm felt, so that at the end of October a child can easily walk in the streets in it, wearing a warm sweater underneath.

In order not to mess with sewing hats, you can buy it in advance and add some detail at home. The drape is sewn simply. The material is folded in half, the neck is cut out in the center. By measuring the distance from the neck to the end of the arm extended toward the side, note the width of the product. Having measured the distance from the neck to the palm on one side and the other, we cut off the excess tissue. The bottom can be made even or rounded, as in the photo in the article.





The edges of the felt do not split, so that the bottom of the ghost costume can not be bent. You can decorate the edge of the outfit as follows: cut the hem into equal strips, each tie a knot on top. It will turn out a beautiful edging. It remains only to make the application of black felt - the eyes and the grin of the mouth. They can be sewn or glued to PVA.

Outfit for the girl

From a daughter to make a small and cute ghost even easier. You need to pick up white leggings, a T-shirt and sew a fluffy bundle of white guipure. For this, several layers of matter are taken, gathered on top of the gum. It remains only to decorate the t-shirt with black felt applique.





Cut out the nose, eyes with cilia and a big wide smile. On the child's head, you can wear a white magnificent bow with an elastic band. The ghost costume is ready!

Pattern Suit

This outfit can be sewn on the pattern below. It will then be enough to decorate it in different ways. Consider carefully what it is. Take a white cloth folded in half, you can use the old duvet cover. The middle is marked and a semicircle of the neck is cut out. You can try on a future ghost costume for a child and check whether the head goes into the hole or not.





Further, the circumference of the chest is measured and its diameter is noted in the center. To the left and right of the marks, using a long ruler, draw sleeves and a hem that expand closer to the edge. Fringe is cut from below on the hem. It remains only to sew the edges of the pattern and the basis for the Halloween ghost costume is ready. It remains only to decorate it with small details. Let's see how this can be done with the following example.

Awesome Ghost

On the resulting workpiece, you can attach a lot of cut strips of fabric or guipure. They are directly sewn onto the fabric or put on top of it a drape made entirely of scraps and thin strips. They can be from the main material or from white guipure. So the ghost will look more airy and transparent, as befits this creature. A bunch of the same stripes is also sewn on top of a white hat.





You can wear white gloves. An additional element will be a sewn plastic black chain. You can make up a girl by drawing black circles around her eyes and covering her face with white powder.

The article describes several options for tailoring a ghost costume with your own hands. Choose any, surprise relatives and friends. As you can see, it is not difficult to sew such an outfit, and there remains a place for the implementation of creative ideas.
