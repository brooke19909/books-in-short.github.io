Average, minimum and maximum living standards in Germany

The average salary of citizens of the Federal Republic is 2,800 euros (as of 2018). This applies to general indicators, excluding tax deductions and other obligatory payments. It is important to consider that German taxes are extremely delicate. The percentage of income tax depends on the size of income, social status, marital status and some other points. For this reason, many qualified professionals complain that taxes often eat up half of their earnings. Needless to say, this greatly affects the family budget.

On average, Germans pay the state about 20-25% of their monthly income. It is clear that not everyone is forced to pay large taxes. For example, the average salary of a hairdresser is a little more than 1,000 euros. Accordingly, he pays less tax.

Salaries exceeding 6 thousand euros are considered high and prestigious. People with such earnings are equated with the elite. Since 2018, a single minimum wage in the amount of 1,500 euros has been operating in the state.
