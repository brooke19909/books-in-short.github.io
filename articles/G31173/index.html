Board game "Jenga": rules of the game

The rules of the game "Jenga" are so simple that they can be explained to anyone in a minute. The set includes wooden blocks with a rectangular section, each of which differs slightly from the other in size. All of them are made of natural hypoallergenic materials, therefore they are safe for children and people prone to allergic reactions. Before starting the game, you need to assemble a tower of these parts, laying them perpendicular to each other in three pieces. The task of the players is to take out one bar at a time from any floor of the tower and move it upstairs.
