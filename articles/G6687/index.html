Which is better - Lenovo or Samsung? What to choose?

When choosing a mobile device, the question arises: which company to choose? In this situation, a detailed comparison of all parameters will help.

Price

One of the main parameters for comparison, of course, will be the cost of the device. Value for money is the main parameter to choose.

Choosing the best price for Lenovo or Samsung, the first option most often wins. Devices of the Chinese manufacturer are much cheaper than their Korean competitors. The difference in value can reach as many as hundreds of dollars.



Of course, such a difference is present when comparing the flagships of these companies. By purchasing a budget type device, this difference is leveled. In the budget category, it will be much more difficult to determine which is better - Lenovo or Samsung.

Filling

Comparing smartphones from the category of flagships, it is very difficult to give preference to any particular one. Since the fillings of the devices of the companies are very similar.

Each device has its own zest, and in some parameters outweighs its competitor. But when considering the big picture, we get shaky equality.

Taking the Lenovo Vibe Shot as an example and comparing it with the Galaxy S5, we can come to the conclusion about the advantages and disadvantages of each device. Samsung is the owner of a processor with four cores, each of which is 2.5 GHz. At this time, Lenovo has as many as 8 cores, but the frequency is less - 1.7 and 1 GHz. A similar trend is present in almost all device parameters.

But in the case of the display, it is no longer so difficult to conclude that it is better to Lenovo or Samsung Galaxy. Undoubtedly, Lenovo screens are significantly inferior to their competitors.

Huge Samsung screens with rich colors and the use of modern technology are the best on the market. Without a doubt, the displays of Lenovo devices are also quite good, but in many respects they do not reach the leader.
