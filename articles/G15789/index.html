Yekaterinburg: Cuba Beach Club is the best place to relax

An excellent vacation of a European level can be offered by the entertainment complex in the city of Yekaterinburg, Cuba Beach Club, which is a combination of a restaurant, a night club and a good bath.

This truly heavenly place is located on the banks of the Verkh-Isetsky Pond and works all year round.





A restaurant

Many people know that, having arrived in Yekaterinburg, the Cuba Beach Club is worth a visit and you should certainly try the kitchen of the restaurant of this complex - Fidel ("Fidel").

This venue is quite spacious - up to 200 guests can be accommodated in the restaurant hall, which is very convenient for banquets and celebrations. Using the picturesqueness of its location, the institution has long specialized in this.

Fidel Hall has a good venue for guest artists to perform. For good music sounding, high-quality sound equipment is installed, and modern lighting equipment will always help maintain the required level of illumination.

The restaurant can offer a very extensive list of dishes of European and Russian cuisine: they can prepare juicy pizza, seafood or meat on the grill. The bar menu is also rich, which has a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãcocktails for every taste.

Fidel Restaurant also offers the option of ordering custom cakes for your ceremonies.





Bathhouse

Who in Yekaterinburg does not know a lot about a good Russian bath? Everyone knows him. Therefore, the baths of the Cuba Beach Club complex (Yekaterinburg), reviews of which are mostly positive, constantly receive visitors.

There are three types of steam rooms for guests to choose from: in the Russian style, in the oriental, as well as a banquet bath. Inside, they are decorated in the best traditions of Russia and the East, which allows you to fully plunge into the atmosphere of these regions. The nature that surrounds the complex will delight the eye of any guest: the park and the pond will appeal to every lover of nature.

In the baths there are real oak brooms, as well as traditional fonts with ice water, which you can plunge into when leaving the steam room.

Fans of smoky smoke should definitely rent an oriental-style bathhouse - they hook up a hookah, and if you want to celebrate, a banquet bathhouse is specially designed for such cases, in which there are all conditions for this.

When renting any bathhouse of the Cuba Beach Club complex (Yekaterinburg), the prices will please the wallet, as they are very affordable.





Beach

Fans of outdoor activities will also find their place here. A separate attraction of the Cuba Beach Club is the beach complex, which is located on the sandy shore of the Verkh-Isetsky Pond.

There is also a separate large pool with a good water purification system and its heating to maintain optimal temperature. Near it there are a large number of sun loungers and several gazebos for a small number of people. This pool is known throughout Yekaterinburg.

Cuba Beach Club can offer residents of the city the opportunity to hold wedding events on the banks of the reservoir: on the pier there is a specially equipped venue where it is convenient to hold wedding ceremonies. It is designed for a small number of invited guests - up to 60 people.

For children

The Cuba Beach Club entertainment complex can be safely called family, as here adults and children will certainly find something to do. Especially for young guests in the complex there is a team of animators who will occupy your kids with exciting productions, educational games. While you are resting, your children will develop and have fun.





Every weekend, at night, Cuba Beach Club hosts noisy and incendiary Varadero parties, which are held in the open air: the best music plays, hot cocktails flow and fragrant hookah smoke soars. Welcome!
