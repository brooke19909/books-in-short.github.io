There are many ways to replenish a card account. The first thing you can do is go to the bank branch in person and deposit cash into your card account. The transfer procedure is quite simple: contact the bank's cash desk with a plastic card, passport and cash. Bank employees will explain how to recharge a Sberbank card and make a transfer to you. Leaving the bank building, you can safely use a plastic card. This method has its advantages, for example, if you are planning a long trip. You can take a small amount of cash and a plastic card with you.

You can also use ATMs that are equipped with a cash-in system. To do this, a Sberbank plastic card is inserted into the ATM. Next, we follow the instructions that appear on the terminal of the ATM, it explains in detail how to recharge a Sberbank card. Deposit cash through the cash-in device and wait for the operation to finish.



You can replenish a card account by wire transfer from your bank account. To do this, simply contact the bank and get the necessary advice. The transfer is carried out through a cash desk at Sberbank. The documents in this case are still the same - a passport and a plastic card.

The same operation can be performed if you have a deposit with a bank. It is advisable that your deposit be issued on conditions under which a partial or full withdrawal of funds is possible. It is important not to lose the accrued interest. Clarify this point, it should be indicated in the contract with the bank. Next, contact your deposit manager and find out how to recharge a Sberbank card through a deposit. From the documents you will need a contract with the bank to open a deposit and a passport.

You can also replenish your Sberbank card using online banking. To do this, you must be a registered user of this system and have access to the Internet. The mobile banking system has the same capabilities.



Transfer using payment systems (Yandex Money, WebMoney, Qiwi). If you have registration in these systems and an electronic wallet, you will be asked to get a personal certificate. It allows you to use all the features of the payment system. To make transfers in the WebMoney system, it is enough to obtain a formal certificate, simply by specifying the passport data. After a while, you will receive a letter in the mail about the assignment of a formal certificate to you. Make a transfer from one of the payment systems indicating the amount of transfer. Funds to the card account arrive after some time, which the bank will need to verify. You will be charged a small fee for this operation.

It should also be noted that Sberbank does not accept payments to the card account from terminals of another bank. Choose a cash deposit ATM owned by Sberbank.
