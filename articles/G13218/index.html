LCD "Ilyinsky" description, reviews

Own apartment in the center of the capital is a dream that is quite difficult to fulfill. Thinking about buying an apartment, many are horrified. The high cost of real estate in Moscow forces you to take a mortgage, get into debt. That is why the apartment must meet all the requirements. New buildings in the near Moscow region have become a budget alternative to expensive metropolitan complexes. Ilyinsky Residential Complex is a vivid example of comfortable housing located just 22 km from the Moscow Ring Road. In the framework of this material, based on the feedback from the first shareholders, we will evaluate the residential complex from all sides.





about the project

Located in the Ramensky district, the Ilyinsky Residential Complex is an elite housing project at an affordable price. Two buildings on the 9th and 13th floors with spacious apartments, a guarded territory, and their own infrastructure have already attracted the attention of many. The first inhabitants were truly delighted with the chosen place. They never saw so much greenery on the territory of metropolitan complexes.

About the developer

The developer is AT-Development, an experienced company that, even in times of crisis, did not stop pleasing Muscovites with excellent residential complexes that meet all the requirements. The use of modern materials and patented building technologies, meeting deadlines and the desire to please every tenant - this is the basic concept of the company. At the office, qualified employees will answer all your questions and talk about the conditions for purchasing apartments.





Location

For the construction of the residential complex "Ilyinsky", the town of the same name was chosen . This decision was made not by accident. The place is picturesque, clean, free from the accumulation of harmful industries and only 22 km separate it from the Moscow Ring Road. By metropolitan standards, this distance is negligible. The first residents easily get to work, spend their weekends in the capital. New settlers have at their disposal all the infrastructure facilities of the Zhukovsky science city.

Transport accessibility

Many refuse to consider the complex remote from the city because of the lack of personal transport. But in this case, the developer has provided all the nuances. A 5-minute walk from the complex is the railway station of the same name, from where in just 20 minutes you can get to the Vykhino metro station, and in 40 minutes to the Kazan station. Buses and taxis running on several routes will also deliver, if necessary, to almost anywhere in the capital.





Construction Features

Recently, the monolithic technology of building houses has been actively used, which endows the building with the necessary strength, durability and endurance. The Ilyinsky residential complex in Ramensky district was no exception. The responses of the first residents glorify their own boiler room, which allows you to control the temperature in each apartment, maintain the most comfortable microclimate and save on costs. The use of modern two-chamber double-glazed windows protects the new settlers from noise coming from the roadway.

Infrastructure

The project provides for the construction of only a multi-level parking and shopping center in the complex. Is this all that can offer its residents LCD "Ilyinsky"? Reviews of interest holders focus on the proximity of all necessary infrastructure: a kindergarten, school, clinic - all within walking distance. The area is ideal for the creative and sports development of the younger generation. Not far from the new buildings there is an excellent stadium, FOK, a school of music and creativity. Residents certainly do not suffer from a lack of stores, there are plenty of them.





Beautification has already been completed on the territory of the complex: flower beds and flowerbeds, walking paths, recreation areas have appeared. Living space can be fully used by people with disabilities. For this, the developer expanded the sidewalks and equipped them with comfortable exits.

Apartments, layouts

What can Ilyinsky LCD boast of? Comfort class apartments in an ecologically clean area have attracted the attention of many Muscovites. The developer offers a choice of spacious individually planned apartments from 1 to 5 rooms, as well as duplex penthouses with a luxurious terrace. Each apartment and regardless of the occupied area has a glazed balcony or loggia. The first new settlers emphasize that this is not the tiny corner that we are used to seeing in typical houses. The developer offers an independent space, which, if desired, can be converted into a full room or a winter garden.





Issue price

Luxury can be affordable - this is the concept that the developer adheres to. Currently, the cost per square meter in the residential complex "Ilyinsky" starts from 70 thousand rubles.
