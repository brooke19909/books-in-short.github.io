Santa Marina Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria / Sozopol) - photos, prices and reviews

Sunny Bulgaria and Russia are connected by rather long tourist relations. The country is very hospitable, it offers both beach and ski holidays. Also, the Balkan people have a pretty good excursion program. And this is not surprising, because the country has a very rich history.

As regards accommodation, many hotel complexes have undergone major repairs, the hotel base has significantly improved and, of course, the service for providing various services has grown.

The best comfort at affordable prices.





The fairly new Santa Marina Hotel 4 * is located in a beautiful green area on the shores of a fabulous bay, not far from the small resort village of Sozopol.

On the highway you can easily reach the city of Burgas. By car, it will be only about 40 minutes. Naturally, shuttles and comfortable buses go to the hotel.

The hotel consists of small villas, which are built almost on the shore of the bay. In the construction of houses were used building materials that are ideal for local conditions.

The construction of the buildings consists of reinforced concrete, and the partitions and external walls are made of red brick. Buildings have excellent thermal insulation.





The hotel was built in 2006 and provides excellent accommodation. There is direct access to the highway. Also nearby are various restaurants and bars.

Hotel Santa Marina has its own beach, which is undoubtedly very convenient for tourists. The sandy coastline is simply mesmerizing with its cleanliness and fine sand. You can lie on it without any deckchairs and thoughtlessly admire the water surface of the water.

By the way, in the town you can meet on vacation almost the entire Bulgarian bohemia: famous artists, artists and musicians. They, like tourists, are attracted by the unique atmosphere that prevails in this place.

Hotel rooms

For a magical holiday at the resort, you can settle in one of the 29 buildings of such a structure as this hotel. Santa Marina Hotel 4 offers modern apartments with constant comfort, luxury and tranquility. Most rooms have a panoramic view of the sea.





Spacious rooms have comfortable bedrooms and open attics. Also has its own kitchen, equipped with the latest technology.

For greater convenience, the rooms have air conditioning, telephone, television with various channels, refrigerator and minibar.

Great vacation in Crete

Ammoudara is a small village in the prefecture of Heraklion (Greece). The village is located on a beautiful sandy beach. Along the main street there are numerous restaurants, souvenir shops and, of course, hotels, and one of them is Santa Marina Hotel 4 (Crete).





The hotel is only 20 minutes from the airport. The building is in a very convenient location. From it it is almost a stone's throw to the various interesting sights of Crete.

Also from the complex close to the sandy beach (approximately 70 meters). This is the cleanest coastline that has won the Blue Flag several times. This award is awarded to facilities for cleanliness, grooming and well-being.

Entering the water is very convenient, it is suitable even for the smallest children.

Room features

The room base of Santa Marina Hotel 4 consists of more than 200 rooms. Most of them are located in the main hotel building. There are also four spacious bungalows in the complex.

So that guests do not need anything, the rooms are equipped with everything necessary. This will allow you to enjoy a relaxing and enjoyable holiday. The standard room can accommodate up to three adults.

It is nice that the rooms are cleaned every day, and bed linen is changed twice a week.

Rooms also have a private bathroom. You can use air conditioning in your room to set your climate. Naturally, the room does not do without cable television, a telephone. There is a fridge and a small bar.

In addition, Santa Marina Hotel 4 (Greece) offers special rooms for people exposed to various allergies. Their design meets all the necessary standards, and the environmental quality of the materials is quite high.

Fun and relaxation

The complex has a fairly large territory. It has one swimming pool with clean water, and there is also a reservoir for children. The adjacent area is equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas.

Those who are accustomed to keep fit, Santa Marina Hotel 4 invites you to visit the well-equipped fitness center with professional instructors. The complex also has a team of animators. Almost on the beach (some 100 meters from the hotel) there is a station for water sports.

The hotel has multilingual staff at the reception, so there should not be any problems communicating with it.
