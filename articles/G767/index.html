Digma company

Digma company was established in 2009 in Hong Kong. The main areas of production are small computer peripherals, tablets, e-books and navigators. The company's products have become very popular in the Russian market mainly due to low prices for gadgets. E-books and tablets were in great demand. Especially after the last 3G-modems were introduced. Navigators are also very popular. They added GLONASS support, and domestic buyers began to acquire them in large quantities. And now the company has focused on the release of laptops. But is a Digma laptop good? The reviews suggest that consumers have different views on this matter.
