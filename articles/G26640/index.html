The recreation center (Cheboksary) "Sunflower": a description of the houses and services

The recreation center (Cheboksary) "Sunflower" welcomes guests from different parts of Russia, as well as the CIS countries. The base staff is a team of professionals who love their job. The employees are ready to do everything so that the guests of the recreation center feel as comfortable as possible. The Sunflower Recreation Center offers both active and dynamic relaxation, as well as fascinating relaxation in nature. The recreation center is located at: Lesnaya street, house 21, Mikshi-Enzey, Cheboksary, Russia.





Facilities

Visitors can rent modernly equipped and comfortable houses for a pleasant pastime. The recreation center (Cheboksary) "Sunflower" has:

  • 2 large houses (8 beds + 4 extra beds);
  • 2 small houses (4 berths + 2 extra beds).

The large guest house can accommodate up to 15 guests, and the small one can accommodate up to 10 visitors. All types of houses can be rented for a day or every hour, both with and without a bath. The cost of renting houses is recommended to check with the administrator over the phone, as the pricing policy may vary depending on seasonality and holidays. The recreation center (Cheboksary) "Sunflower" is a great place not only for relaxation, but also for family events, various celebrations, friendly gatherings, corporate parties, visiting ceremonies, student equators and other bright holidays.





Home Information

The recreation center (Cheboksary) "Sunflower" in the rental price in both a small and a large guest house includes:

  • TV
  • Towels and slippers.
  • Crockery and kitchen appliances.
  • Basic and additional beds.
  • Barbecue and grill for barbecue.
  • Outdoor pool.

When renting a large guest house, visitors additionally get the opportunity to use karaoke, high-quality acoustic and lighting equipment for parties and discos, and can also enjoy playing various board games. Each guest house has its own exit to the open veranda, where there is wooden furniture for evening gatherings. All houses are made of logs in the classic Russian style. In the rooms you can find modern upholstered furniture, tables and a bar, a kitchen corner and much more. Each house is fenced and has a swing and a swimming pool on its territory.





Services

Visitors to the recreation center can play volleyball, table tennis, darts or badminton in their free time (there is all the necessary equipment for games), and the kids will have a great time in the modernly equipped playground. The recreation center "Sunflower" (Cheboksary district) provides for an additional payment oak and birch bath brooms, bath sheets and towels, skewers, a boiler and barbecue grills. For clients of the recreation complex in the Cheboksary district there is a discount system of discounts and an individual approach is guaranteed.

"Sunflower" (Cheboksary, recreation center) is a great place to relax, where you can forget about the bustle of the city, workdays and just enjoy the clean air, romantic orders and sunrises. The complex is built taking into account all the requirements of a modern traveler, captivating guests with its cleanliness, hospitality and a wide range of services.
