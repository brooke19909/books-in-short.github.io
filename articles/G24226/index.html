Hotel Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt): description, reviews of tourists

Are you going to relax in Egypt in the popular resort of Sharm el Sheikh and would like to spend reasonable funds on this, and get a good result? Then, quite possibly, the Coral Beach Rotana Resort Tiran 4 * hotel will suit you . He has established himself well and will please a lowly tourist.

About the hotel and its location





Getting to it will be easy, and most importantly, quickly. The hotel is located just five kilometers from the international airport in the Sharks Bay area. Small and comfortable, it will delight guests with its green territory and proximity to the Red Sea coast. Oriental hospitality and an upscale European level of service, a large number of entertainments for different ages and tastes make this place the most suitable place for a family vacation.

The hotel complex is quite new and recently built. It consists of several buildings: the administrative, where registration is carried out, and a pair of two-story buildings with well-appointed and comfortable rooms of various categories for vacationers. There are 367 of them, including non-smokers.

Beach

The hotel is located in a picturesque location on the first coastline, and therefore is the happy owner of its own sandy beach, which is located just 100 meters from the complex. It is coral, so the administration strongly recommends that you use special shoes when bathing. Entry into the water is possible only through the pontoons; for convenience they were made by two. Free umbrellas, beach towels and sun loungers are provided.





Number of rooms

Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 * hotel offers everyone a choice of three categories of rooms: standard (1-, 2-, 3-seater), spacious family rooms, Suites and comfortable, presentable apartments. All of them are characterized by increased comfort and coziness. The design is made in a modern European style, no frills, extremely concise, but tasteful. Only a few interior details will remind you that you are in the East: traditional bright inserts from material in white and blue stripes on bedspreads and curtains, carpets with Arabic patterns.

Room features

Regardless of the type of accommodation, you will have a small balcony or terrace with a picturesque view of the garden, sea or the territory of the Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 * hotel. The rooms are fully furnished, with one large or two twin beds. It is allowed to additionally organize another berth as needed.

The bathroom is combined and includes a bathroom with shower, toilet, washbasin, hairdryer, towels and toiletries for each guest. Cleaning is carried out daily, bed linen is provided, service is provided at an additional cost.





Each room has a table and comfortable armchairs that can be easily taken out to the balcony, safe, satellite TV with several channels broadcasting in Russian. The air conditioning is individual, and everyone can easily customize it for their own needs, a minibar and internet in the rooms are paid, the phone has a direct long-distance line.

How to choose food?

Resorts, as you know, are different. In some hotels you should limit yourself to breakfast, and spend the day and evening getting to know the local traditions and cuisine, while others come to one place, which is called, without departing from the cash register, to get everything at once. The latter are just the ones that are loved by domestic tourists, Turkey and Egypt. Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 * is ready to offer its guests two types of food: breakfast and all-inclusive. As shown by numerous experience and practice, the second option in this case is better and more profitable. Most tourists go outside the hotel for a whole number of times during their holidays, and most of them go on excursions. It does not make sense to look for new restaurants or cafes daily, as the hotel has quite good infrastructure. Having paid everything in advance, in reality you will only have to relax and enjoy everything that happens.

Restaurants on site





The bulk of holidaymakers and the service themselves are taken by a restaurant with a romantic atmosphere and the equally attractive name Mermaid. It opens in the early morning to feed everyone breakfast buffet, and ends its work after a late dinner, at ten in the evening.

In addition, in the territory of the comfortable and cozy hotel Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 * (Sharm el-Sheikh) you can visit the Mediterranean-style restaurant Shark's Bay, specializing in the virtuoso preparation of dishes from the freshest and highest-quality seafood. It opens in the afternoon and is open until eleven in the evening.

Hotel and beach bars

Having a sufficiently rich number of rooms and able to accommodate a sufficient number of vacationers, the hotel is simply obliged to provide them with a place for leisure. To avoid queues and delays, the administration offers as many as five bars and one cafe. The latter is located on the territory and, with all its atmosphere and atmosphere, has a relaxing holiday in the company of friends or family. It is open from morning until late in the evening. The menu contains various drinks and light snacks, but it is best to try here delicious and aromatic coffee with pastries.

Right next to the pools on the territory of Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 *, the photo of which is presented in the article, there are two bars: "Aquarius" (next to the shady garden) and "Bodyline". All vacationers can enjoy delicious alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, cocktails, fresh juices without leaving the water, but at the same time briefly hiding from the scorching, hot desert sun in the shade.





Another bar is nearby and allows, among other things, to admire the picturesque sea views at sunset. On the private beach of the hotel, everyone is welcomed by the Palma establishment. It offers not only drinks, but also a good menu with food.

Well, in the evening, when dusk falls on the resort, the Blue Moon bar opens its doors in the hotel lobby (floor 1) Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 *. It works from 11 in the evening until one in the morning. The original design of the room, comfortable furniture and a unique atmosphere will make your evening interesting and memorable, in addition, discos are organized there.

Entertainment and sports

The hotel has two spacious pools (one of them is heated in the winter season). Near them neatly located sunbeds and umbrellas with towels prepared for tourists. Every day, under the guidance of animators or trainers, special gymnastics is performed in the water, which has a beneficial effect on the whole body. Everyone can play water polo with enthusiasm or do snorkeling, that is, diving with a mask.

Other water activities are a paid service. It is noteworthy that at the Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 * hotel there is one of the best diving schools. Experienced instructors will teach you the basics of diving and introduce you to the underwater world of the Red Sea.

For beauty and health

Rest will be inferior if you do not include procedures for relaxing and restoring the body. Without exception, the hotel provides an opportunity to visit saunas and steam rooms, wellness and fitness centers, a jacuzzi and massage treatments. Some of the listed services are provided for a fee, the cost and price list must be clarified at the hotel during registration or at the staff during the holidays.

Service for children

Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 * hotel positions itself as a family, for a calm and measured rest, where everyone will find something to do and have fun to their taste. In fact, children are not bored here. To start at least with a special club, it works every day for two hours before lunch and after. Adults can fully devote this time to themselves. Various entertainments and a leisure room will surely please the baby, and experienced animators will deal with them. There is also a playground on the territory, a special disco is held every evening, popular and favorite cartoons are broadcast on the big screen, and in the pools there are special departments where it is not deep.

If necessary, the hotel is always ready to provide a cot and chairs in restaurants for free, as well as babysitting services for an additional hourly fee.

About restaurants and food

Opinions on this issue are not unanimous, but the vast majority note a mismatch between the level declared by the hotel and the actual service in restaurants and bars. The staff is not too polite, the menu is monotonous, the food is not very tasty. Many note the presence of a queue for second courses, which, in principle, is unacceptable in a hotel of this level.

This place is not for those who go for delights and delicacies, gastronomic masterpieces. Everything is simple and affordable here, people who are unpretentious in food, the service is quite satisfied, as well as the menu. In general, the price and quality level are quite comparable, which attracts a large number of tourists to the hotel, including from Arab countries.

Former guests and residents recommend visiting other restaurants nearby, especially those specializing in seafood.
