Rumyantsevo Business Park: how to get to the oasis of business life

In our century, when business life has become a priority, a special corner is just needed where you can solve all problems at the same time. Muscovites are lucky, they have such a place. His name is Rumyantsevo Business Park.

Probably anyone already knows how to get there. It is located near a traffic intersection. Therefore, it is convenient to come there not only to residents of the south-west of Moscow, but also to guests of the capital from the Moscow region. Vnukovo Airport is also nearby, so it will not be difficult to get there for residents of other regions of Russia.

History tour

The construction of this huge complex, which combines all the possibilities for a quiet business and outdoor activities, began in 2004. In 2008, he already hospitably received the first visitors. At the moment, its area is 180 thousand square meters. This is what an amazing miracle Moscow has. Rumyantsevo Business Park has become a favorite office and shopping center for many residents of the southwestern areas of the capital.

Rumyantsevo Business Park: how to get there

The shopping complex has gained its popularity also because of its convenient location.

It is located near the metro station "South-West". You can get there by bus (for example, No. 272, 707, 844) or by minibus (No. 590, 343).

If you get to go by your own transport, it is better to get off the MKAD, get out onto Leninsky Prospekt and drive a few kilometers towards the Vnukovo airport.
