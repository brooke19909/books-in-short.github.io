Hotel Palace Royal Garden 5 * (Djerba, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is incredibly popular with our compatriots. Moreover, the country's resorts offer vacations at reasonable prices at a time when it‚Äôs already cold for a long time. In our article we want to talk about one of the Djerba hotels - Palace Royal Garden 5 *.

A bit about the hotel ...

The island of Djerba is washed by the waters of the Bay of Gabes. Locals grow dates, olives, figs, and drinking water is taken from artesian wells. Palace Royal Garden 5 * spreads a crescent on the seashore. The complex is a group of bright buildings located along the coastal strip, surrounded by a luxurious park of 20 hectares. It is located six kilometers from Midoun and eighteen kilometers from the capital of the island of Houmt Souk. The hotel was built in 1194. The last room repair was carried out in 2012.



The hotel is very beautiful and impresses its guests from the very first minute. In the main building, tourists are greeted by a huge hall, made in the style of Arabian fairy tales, decorated with numerous Moorish carved panels, patterns and ceiling decorations.

Number of rooms

The rooms of the Palace Royal Garden 5 * hotel are visually divided into a living area and a bedroom. Their interiors are more restrained than those of public places in the hotel, but nevertheless they differ in elegance and modern design. All apartments are of decent size and equipped with everything that you might need for a comfortable stay: TVs, air conditioning, satellite channels, electronic safes, mini-refrigerators, private bathrooms, Wi-Fi.

Palace Royal Garden 5 * has 288 rooms. In the arsenal of the hotel apartments: Standard Suite, President Suite, Villa. The hotel has non-smoking rooms.

Dining at the hotel

The Royal Garden Palace 5 * (Djerba) operates the All Inclusive system , which gives an excellent opportunity to taste a large number of dishes. Tunisian cuisine is incredibly diverse, as it mixes the traditions of Persians, Andalusians, Berbers, Turks and Egyptians. The hotel restaurant is open buffet style. In addition, there are theme institutions with Italian cuisine - Don Chichio, Tunisian - El Mayda, Mediterranean dishes - Pecheur. The hotel also offers a vegetarian menu. In order to fully experience the local flavor, you should definitely visit the restaurants and cafes of the island, where you can taste national food and drinks.

Wellness center

Royal Garden Palace 5 * (Tunisia) has its own wellness center called Centro Palace. The objective of the institution is that tourists can not only improve their health, but also achieve complete relaxation. Here you can undergo balneological procedures (treatment with mineral water), visit massage sessions, a bath, a sauna. All this will help restore good health and recharge your body with energy for a long time. But dancing classes and exercises in the gym will help maintain excellent physical fitness even on vacation.

Lovers of outdoor activities can play tennis on the hotel's outdoor courts. In addition, a volleyball court and bicycle rental are open for tourists. From water entertainments to guests are available: canoe, catamarans, windsurfing. Near the complex is an excellent golf club. Fans of this game can visit it, in addition, the hotel offers the possibility of free delivery to it.



Fans of horse riding and horse riding will enjoy the presence of stables near the hotel. All sorts of programs have been prepared at the riding club, among which there is also a children's one.

Hotel infrastructure

Royal Garden Palace 5 * (Djerba) has a huge pool surrounded by comfortable sun loungers, where you can relax. Nearby is a spacious sun terrace. In addition, the complex has an indoor pool with heated water and a jacuzzi.

The hotel has a fairly developed infrastructure, on its territory there is everything that will make your stay comfortable and interesting. There is a beauty salon, wellness center, gym, shops, a sauna and a bath.

For children, the hotel has a mini club, a playground and a private pool.

Royal Garden Palace 5 * (Tunisia): reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to refer to the reviews of vacationers who visited this hotel. The complex is the most pleasant experience. The first thing that strikes is the chic and luxury of the hall. All public areas have marble floors. The hotel is beautiful both outside and inside.

When you check in, registration takes place very quickly. It is necessary to fill out a questionnaire, and they immediately bring you the room keys and a card to receive towels. The entire hotel staff understands Russian, so communicating is very simple.



What is the nutritional situation at Royal Garden Palace 5 * (Djerba)? Reviews of tourists regarding food are only positive. Each vacationer can choose a dish to their liking, the choice of food is simply amazing. Men are not allowed to wear shorts for dinner, so you should take appropriate clothing with you on vacation.

The hotel has great food and quality drinks. Fresh juice is always present on the tables (at least 4 types). Breakfast includes: delicious fresh croissants, milk porridges, cereals, dried fruits, yogurts, mashed potatoes, sliced. For lunch and dinner on the tables there is always a delicious fish (fillet), cooked in various ways.

The work of the restaurant is always watched by the administrator, from whose eyes flaws do not escape. On the day of arrival, each tourist is assigned to a certain table. During dinner, meals are served in two shifts, so you must immediately decide when you want to come to the restaurant - at 18.00 or 20.00.

The hotel also has √† la carte restaurants. All Inclusive allows you to go to each of them once, but only by appointment at the reception of the main institution. Italian and Tunisian restaurants are small spaces where you can enjoy a great dinner in a relaxed atmosphere. There is no usual noise and fuss, as in the main institution.

The restaurant has a microwave, which can be useful for tourists with small children. There is no separate menu for kids, but among the dishes you can always choose the right food for the kids. For example, on the tables there is always: fish, fillet, rice, chicken, mashed potatoes. Sometimes even semolina is served for breakfast.



I would like to pay special attention to drinks. The All Inclusive program allows you to take them in any quantity. From nine in the morning, all alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are provided completely free of charge. The hotel serves delicious coffee and tea with an unusual taste for us. Cappuccino and coffee can be taken not only in the main restaurant, but also in the pool bar.

Room Reviews

The hotel boasts beautiful rooms in a modern style. They have a TV with Russian channels, a bed with bedside tables, a table, an armchair, a wardrobe with hangers. The apartments are spacious and bright. In the mini-fridge there are four bottles of water (with gas and without), a sprite and a fantasy. All this is included in the price. The contents of the refrigerator are replenished daily.

In the rooms of tourists awaiting excellent new bathrobes and two towels per person. The bathroom has a hairdryer, soap in the dispenser and shower caps.



Not all apartments have sea views. In order to guarantee an apartment with a sea view, you must purchase the appropriate ticket. The rooms have a free safe with simple and convenient instructions.

General impression of the hotel

In the Royal Garden Palace 5 *, the reviews of which are given in the article, you can exchange money if you have a passport directly at the reception, but you can‚Äôt transfer dinars into dollars. But this is not a problem, a similar operation can be performed in the airport exchangers with a receipt. Also at the reception you can withdraw money from an ATM.



The hotel has a very beautiful, well-groomed and at the same time very extensive territory, which you can always walk around. There are also tennis courts. The pools of the complex are open from early morning until 19.00. They are simply huge in size. The main body of water is equipped with three gentle entrances from different sides. Around the pool there are sun loungers with umbrellas and a bar where you can take any drinks, as well as a snack. The hotel also has indoor pools, the temperature of which is +25 degrees. They are in great demand in rainy weather.



Beach towels can be obtained for free near the reception, they can be changed at any time and in any quantity. Sun loungers by the pools and on the beach are free.

The hotel has three shops where you can buy: mattresses, souvenirs, swimwear, creams. There is everything that tourists may need, and the prices of goods are quite acceptable, and sometimes even lower than in neighboring markets.

Experienced tourists are advised to shop at the nearest Aljazira store. In it you can buy dates, spices, alcohol, olive oil, sweets, figs, souvenirs and gifts. All products here are much cheaper than in the markets.

The hotel has an animation team. In the afternoon, they conduct water aerobics sessions, learn to play darts and petanque. In the evenings, entertainment shows and a children's disco await guests. A detailed entertainment schedule can be seen at the reception.

Children's animation is not very interesting, because the kids are provided with foreign cartoons without translation, and there is absolutely no modern Russian music in the disco. According to vacationers, excellent entertainment events are held in season (from May to August).

Tourists are very pleased with the service and attitude of the staff. All the hotel staff are incredibly positive and sociable people who are always happy to help you.

The sea in the season is very warm and beautiful, but in the autumn period the weather can bring surprises in the form of algae driven by the winds. But the water always remains warm. The hotel's beach is just a 7-minute walk from the complex in the park. He is incredibly beautiful and clean. Kids can be carried in wheelchairs. There are many umbrellas on the coast, each of them is equipped with an urn.

Based on the wonderful positive reviews of tourists, you can safely recommend Royal Garden Palace 5 * (Tunisia, Djerba), the reviews of which are given in the article, for a comfortable family holiday on the coast.
