Decorative lamps - an exquisite decoration of the interior

Decorative lamps create a large and diverse world of light. Their color, shape and materials are true works of design art. The bulk of the existing fixtures, in addition to performing a direct functional action, is entrusted with a decorative purpose, which is the decoration of the interior of the room. But it is worth remembering that lighting is still considered a priority. The decor is a pleasant addition.





However, in the world of light there is a separate category of chandeliers, sconces and table lamps. These are, in fact, decorative lamps, the main task of which is interior decoration. The special light of these devices is designed to create certain notes of mood - festive or soothing and relaxing. Often the rays emanating from these sources are in continuous motion, shimmer and change. The bewitching play of light makes a person disconnect from the daily hustle and bustle, brings a sense of peace and tranquility.

A special mood is created by devices imitating a live fire - fireplaces, candles and bowls. Such decorative lamps give cosiness, comfort and warmth to your home. Of course, artificial flame can not be compared with the present. However, such devices do not need firewood and matches. They suddenly do not go out and are not able to set fire to the tablecloth. When arranging a romantic evening for two, it is best to put real candles on the table, but cheerful gatherings of friends can be decorated with devices that simulate a flame of fire.





Decorative LED lights were a real revolutionary breakthrough in the design world. They are great for decorating the interior, while possessing a number of advantages:

- profitability;

- long term of operation;

- the constant quality and brightness of the light beam;

- fire and environmental safety;

- the absence of ultraviolet and infrared radiation, which negatively affects the eyes.

There are decorative lamps that attract the eye with unusual effects. These are plasma or lava devices. A vessel of such a source, in the center of which is a round electrode, is filled with a special gas. Neon rays of the lamp create unusual visual effects that arise as a result of the movement of plasma discharges.





Decorative table lamps can be placed on any piece of furniture. They can serve as a source of directional or scattered light. It all depends on the type of lampshade. Models of such lamps have a static design. Their leg and head are not adjustable and do not rotate. All elements of such lamps are made of beautiful decorative materials - textiles, porcelain, glass, wood or stone.

The bulk of the table lamps, designed to decorate the interior, has the shape of a cone. In this case, one part of the rays falls down, which allows you to illuminate the space for homework and reading, the second is reflected from the ceiling and walls, rushing up, and the third, passing through the material of the lampshade, creates a diffused and soft stream of light.
