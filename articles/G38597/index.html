DLS R6A speaker system: specifications, description, advantages and disadvantages

The DLS R6A speaker system is one of the most popular systems of the Swedish company DLS. These devices are characterized by sound execution, the use of special components designed to ensure high-quality sound (a tweeter with a silk diffuser and a midwoofer with paper).



Technical characteristics of the DLS R6A speaker system :

- type - two-component acoustics;

- rated power - 80 W;

- maximum power - 120 W;

- sensitivity - 94 dB;

- reproducible frequency range - 35-20000 Hz;

- crossover: low-pass filter, high-pass filter;

- nominal resistance - 4 ohms;

- installation depth - 76 mm;

- size - 16.5 cm.



The speaker diffuser is made of unpressed cellulose, which in addition is reinforced with carbon fiber, and the suspension is made of butyl rubber. Thanks to a powerful magnet, a large reference system, as well as an improved voice coil, stability and flexibility of reproduction is ensured for any signal power at the output of the system and any musical styles. DLS R6A loudspeakers have a carefully muffled basket of stamped thick steel sheet. The speaker magnets are protected by rubber rings against mechanical damage. The crossover case is made of durable plastic with a transparent cover. The inductors that are used in the circuit are wrapped in a thick copper wire, the lowering of the tweeter signal is four-stage. The RF emitter, housed in a plastic case, belongs to the Ultimate Reference series. The frequency response of the speaker system has a decline at the upper end of the range, but down plays exactly up to 50 Hz. The crossover separation frequency is exactly 3000 Hz.

The advantages of the DLS R6A bass system include natural and soft sound, powerful bass, good workmanship. Despite all the advantages, this equipment has its drawbacks, as it has a large installation depth and an oversized midwoofer.

When listening to this speaker system, high information content is noted, as well as the accuracy of recording transfer. As for the nature of the sound, it can be called detailed and open. The upper middle of the range has been sufficiently developed in it, which is explained by a correctly made diffuser from cellulose. The average bass is very deep and quite collected, has a good terrain, but it does not reach the lower range.



Opinions of amateurs about the DLS R6A speaker system are very different. Some argue that the device is uncertainly reproducing medium frequencies, while other consumers, on the contrary, claim that the system is an exemplary representative of the middle class. However, both of them nevertheless agree that this is a very reliable and high-quality equipment. According to independent tests conducted with the DLS R6A acoustic device, the feedback from the audio enthusiasts is absolutely accurate: the system is well-designed, has excellent musical sound and, in addition, provides unique bass capabilities, like for 16-cm speakers. Fans of good quality sound will be satisfied with this speaker system.
