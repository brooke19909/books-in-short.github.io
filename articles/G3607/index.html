Sony M2 Xperia: review, specifications, reviews

Flagship smartphone models have always attracted the attention of a large number of people. But because of their high cost, few can afford such a luxury. But even with the usual comparison of company sales profits from such gadgets and from simple budget options, it becomes clear that the latter benefit significantly. Sony M2 Xperia is just such a smartphone. It is inexpensive, can support two SIM cards, and at the same time it looks a little different from the flagship models. Let's look at how this device differs from its own kind and, based on reviews, we will draw certain conclusions.

Appearance and ergonomics

The smartphone comes in three different colors. These are white, black and purple colors. In appearance, it is completely similar to all gadgets from Sony.

Despite its solid dimensions, Sony M2 lies in the palm of your hand very well. You can even control it with just one hand. The button used to unlock it is located exactly where it should be - under the thumb.

The keys are conveniently located and have a good power reserve. The assembly itself is very high quality. Nowhere does not play and creaks. In general, the device gives the impression of solidity. The only drawback, judging by the user reviews, is the slippery back panel. If the hand is wet, then there is a high probability that the smartphone will simply slip out.





Display

Smartphone Sony M2 received an average quality screen with a diagonal of 4.8 inches. The sensor used here is good and responds to the slightest touch with high accuracy.

Resolution guarantees a dot density of 230 units per inch. This is pretty good when you consider that this model is a budget option. On closer inspection, the points are noticeable, but they do not greatly spoil the overall picture.

Sony M2 Dual showed poor contrast performance. So, if it is used on average brightness parameters, the picture is poorly recognized. Unfortunately, for a low cost you need to sacrifice something.

A little depressing is the lack of oleophobic coating, or it is, but of very poor quality. Fingerprints remain, and very quickly the screen becomes "flabby."

OS and software

The smartphone has a preinstalled OS Android version 4.3. But after connecting it is immediately updated to 4.4.2. The shell has a corporate look and is very similar to all those used in the gadgets of this manufacturer.

The OS itself is fast and clear. The smartphone Sony M2 has all the important software, which is enough to start fully using it.

User reviews

So, we draw conclusions. Focusing on user reviews, we can highlight the main pros and cons of a smartphone. Among the positive advantages:

  • excellent assembly and a great design solution;
  • quite high performance;
  • good main camera;
  • the ability to support two SIM;

Cons can be considered:
