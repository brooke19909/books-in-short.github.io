Location

The closest town to the resort is La Romana. This is the southern coast of the island of Hispaniola, and it is washed by the Caribbean Sea, not the ocean. Therefore, the water here is warmer, and there are almost no waves. The nearest settlement to Viva Wyndham Dominicus Palace 4 * - Bayahibe (Dominican Republic). This is a small fishing village with several shops, including a large MIA supermarket, pharmacy, ATM, fruit and fish markets.

But there is everything necessary for a quality vacation in the hotel complex. Both hotels grabbed most of Dominicus Beach - one of the most beautiful on the Caribbean coast. There are three international airports in this island country. The hotel is just 12 minutes from La Romana Air Harbor. From Punta Cana and Santa Domingo - from one and a half to two hours.

Territory

If you stand with your back to the sea, then Viva Wyndham Dominicus Palace 4 * will be on the right (at the pier), and Viva Dominicus Beach on the left. The complex has a single territory, so it is impossible to find the border between the hotels. The difference between the Palace and the Beach is only in the fact that the last hotel has few buildings, and vacationers are mainly settled in bungalows. There is also a small cottage village called "Viva Village". But it is located away from the sea.

The Viva Dominicus Palace described here consists of several three-story buildings built in the colonial style. The last large-scale repair was carried out in 2015, so that everything on the territory shines with novelty. The garden between the buildings is simply mesmerizing. Tourists in the reviews say that at first it seems like he was in Eden. Walking along the alleys among the marvelously smelling flowers, now and then you come across iguanas, turtles, chipmunks. Special attention deserves a large pond with pink flamingos. Tourists recall that they loved to feed ducks and goldfish. Upon check-in, guests are given a map of the huge complex. And in the territory, so that people do not get lost, there are road signs everywhere.

Room Categories

Viva Wyndham Dominicus Palace 4 * is proud of its name "environmentally friendly hotel." In the construction of three-story buildings, only natural materials such as stone and wood were used, and bungalows were built from corals. Landscape designers have tried to ensure that these buildings blend harmoniously with the landscape. All rooms have either balconies or terraces isolated from each other with direct access to the green lawn or garden.

The rooms on the ground floor are ideal for older people and families with children. The hotel has two rooms, fully adapted for people with disabilities, namely wheelchairs. Most guest rooms are categorized as superior rooms. They overlook the garden or pool. But there are buildings, from the numbers of which you can see, albeit not frontally, the sea.

Photos of rooms of all categories show pleasant light interiors with stylish decoration. Consider what is available in the rooms of the cheapest category, superior room. Beach and Pools

Viva Wyndham Dominicus Palace 4 * (Bayahibe) stands on the first line from the sea. Dominicus Beach is one of the best on the Caribbean coast of the island. Its characteristic feature is the growth of palm trees right by the water. Therefore, there are no umbrellas on the beach - sunbeds are set right in the shade of the trees. The sea here is gentle, warm. Sand carefully cleaned.

On the beach there is a bar, diving center and free rental of non-motorized equipment for water sports (kayaks, masks, snorkels, catamarans, surfboards). The coast here is suitable for bathing children. Tourists say that, despite the presence of the pier, the sea is very clean, because boats rarely moor here. The complex has several freshwater pools. Children's areas adjoin them. Beach towels are provided.

Conditions for children

Viva Wyndham Dominicus Palace has two mini-clubs. One accepts children from 4 to 12 years old, and the second - teenagers under 15. Parents say that they themselves and their children were satisfied with the animation. With children they are really engaged, and do not allow them to watch cartoons day and night.

The club building for the smallest has its own restaurant and a sleeping room with cots. Experienced teachers can captivate with competitions and games even obstinate teens. If desired, for a fee, you can hire a personal nanny or baby sitter for your child.

Entertainment for adults

In the reviews of Viva Wyndham Dominicus Palace 4 *, you can often read that the hotel has schools: Caribbean dances, cooking classes, makeup, yoga and meditation, diving, sailing. All lessons are absolutely free. Daytime animation is represented by water aerobics, beach volleyball or darts.

The complex has 4 floodlit tennis courts, a billiard table, a gym, a sauna and a jacuzzi. For a fee, guests can use the services of professional masseurs, hairdressers. Every evening there is a show with artists and DJs, and later a disco.
