How to sew do-it-yourself sofa covers

It is easy and simple to bring a feeling of comfort to your home environment. Sometimes, in order to create a certain atmosphere, emphasize interior design, it is enough to learn how to sew covers on a sofa with your own hands. In addition, this fashionable detail helps protect furniture. There is always the opportunity to change the cover if circumstances so require (for example, it needs to be cleaned or you have new ideas for decorating the room).

What material do we take?

First you need to decide: what fabric will the cover be from? To practice, you can take something inexpensive, old, for example, a cotton sheet.

After we learned, take another fabric. In order to figure out how to sew covers on the sofa, so that they turn out beautiful and durable, you need a material that meets the following requirements. It should not slip, after washing do not sit down, do not stretch, with a simple but beautiful pattern. If your household (especially children) like to dine at the TV, then pick up a material of such a color that it does not show drops left over from food. If you have pets, you will need a fabric on which the wool does not stick.

Getting to work



Of course, sewing covers on sofas begins with the creation of a pattern. It may seem that this is a difficult matter. After all, the subject has a complex shape. But this is not scary. The cover can be spacious and just slip over the sofa. The scheme should take into account all the proportions of furniture. To do this, the sofa should be well measured, writing down its length and width. On the pattern, it is necessary to take into account the allowance, and it should be quite large. If, for example, it turns out that the back of the sofa is already 10 cm wide, you need to cut a rectangle with an increase to a larger size. Enough 15-20 centimeters. This will protect you from errors.



To implement your idea and get acquainted with the ways to sew covers on a sofa, we will consider this process in more detail. You can draw a pattern or use newspapers for it. They should be glued so that they mimic the shape of the furniture. For the seat, they need to be selected exactly in size. On the fabric, add 20 centimeters to the result. The width of the armrest is measured near the seat, as well as at the floor, below. For a pattern of fabric, you need to choose a large width and take into account the allowance (10 centimeters). To determine the length of a piece of fabric, you need to measure the entire armrest and the distance from the seat to the floor. On the fabric add five centimeters to each edge.

For armrests, you need to cut two identical parts and fasten them with pins. Then the planned seams should be stitched on a typewriter. To fasten the bottom and side edges of the cover, use tape, ribbons, cords. Fringe is suitable as a decoration.

Unlike a rectangular one, stitching a cover on a corner sofa is a little more difficult. Due to its shape, each part must be manufactured separately. But in general, the process is very similar to that described above. On a sewing machine, all the details are joined together. Now you can be proud of your own work!



There are many options for how to sew covers on a sofa. Here you can be guided not only by fashion, style, but also by your own taste and imagination.
