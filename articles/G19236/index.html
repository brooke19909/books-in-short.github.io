Phone "Philips E160": reviews

Philips is known as a provider of low-cost, yet functional and reliable mobile devices. Among them are the classic monoblock phones E160. These devices support the most popular communication functions and have a huge battery life. What are the main features of E160 phones? How do users evaluate the quality of their work?





Device Overview

The Philips E160 phone, reviews of which due to the rather high popularity of this device are often found on thematic online portals, belongs to the category of classic, monoblock models. It is designed for operation with 2 SIM-cards (on each of which separate contact groups can be formed).

Among the most remarkable features of the Philips phone E160 (reviews of it can confirm this) - a capacious battery that provides autonomy of the device, if we consider the descriptions from the device manufacturer, talk time up to 8 hours, and standby time - up to 35.





The device is equipped with a technologically advanced camera, with which you can take pictures with a resolution of 640 by 480 pixels. Files can also be sent to friends via MMS. The phone is equipped with remarkable multimedia solutions - FM-receiver, MP3-player. You can connect a headset to your phone. You can transfer files from the device via online channels, and if their resources are not enough - via a USB cable or Bluetooth. If necessary, you can use the built-in flashlight.

Main characteristics

Let us consider in more detail the main features that the Philips Xenium E160 mobile phone has. The characteristics of this device, which you can pay attention to in the first place, are as follows:

- dimensions - 120.5 x 51 x 14.5 mm;

- weight - 93.9 g;

- antenna - built-in type;

- display - TFT with a resolution of 240 by 320 pixels, a diagonal of 2.4 inches, capable of displaying up to 262 thousand colors;

- support for communication technologies - GSM, WAP 2.0;

- battery - with a capacity of 1.6 thousand mAh;

- support for 2 SIM cards;

- the presence of a camera.





Key device features

The phone supports the following functions:

- Installation as a ringtone of MP3 files, polyphonic ringtones;

- playback of files in MP3, AMR, MIDI, as well as AAC;

- the ability to connect external memory cards in Micro SD format up to 32 GB;

- data transfer via Bluetooth.
