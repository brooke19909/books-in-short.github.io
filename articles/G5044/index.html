Costa Brava Blanes 2 * (Spain / Costa Brava): photos and reviews of tourists

Costa Brava is a popular Spanish resort for tourists, for many it is a real synonym for a great summer vacation at low cost. Both lovers of active and beach family holidays can have a good time here. Until recently, the Costa Brava was an impregnable wild rocky shore. Today, this part of Spain offers its guests a simply well-developed tourist infrastructure and many various amenities. In any case, excellent accommodation on the Costa Brava can be rented without any problems. For example, a very good hotel offers tourists a cozy hotel Costa Brava Blanes 2 *.

Features of the location of the complex and how to get to it

This two-star hotel is located on the south coast of the Costa Brava, in the village of Blanes. About 70 km from Costa Brava Blanes 2 * is the largest seaport in Spain - the city of Barcelona. This hotel was built in the center of the village, about 500 m from the sea.

Hotel Beach Reviews

Most often, guests of the Costa Brava Blanes 2 * hotel go to the public beach of Blanes. Reviews about him are mostly good. The coastline at this point is covered with soft sand. Hotel guests need to get to the beach not too long. In total, the road takes about 10 minutes. Tourists need to go along the direct shopping streets of the city. The infrastructure on the beach of the hotel, according to most vacationers, is developed quite well. If desired, tourists can dine, for example, in one of the cafes located near the sea. According to many tourists, most of all the beach, located next to the Costa Brava Blanes 2 * hotel, is suitable for families with children, as well as for those who are not too good at swimming. For fans of the same windsurfing, it is, of course, unlikely to be too convenient.
