Best recreation centers (Dakhovskaya): description, prices

The village of Dakhovskaya in winter and summer is popular among tourists. It is located in the Maikop district of the Republic of Adygea. In the summer season, people relax on the banks of a beautiful mountain river called Belaya. As a rule, they engage in extreme sports. For example, visitors can offer hiking in the mountains, rafting on a turbulent water stream and much more. The weather is favorable this season. Given that the village is located in a mountain pit, the air temperature is kept at +25 ¬∞ C. From morning to evening, the sun pampers with its bright rays, the wind almost never happens. It is worth noting that in the air, even on the hottest days, mountain freshness is felt.

In winter, tourists will not be bored either. First of all, visitors will be amazed by the beautiful mountain scenery. It is possible to engage in skiing, snowboarding, sledding. However, it is worth considering the fact that there is no official ski slope on this page. But this does not bother extremals at all. For visitors, there are numerous recreation centers. Dakhovskaya is a village that you can visit to enjoy unique natural attractions. After all, it is here that one of the most legendary Una Koz massifs passes.

"Mountain Village"

On the banks of the turbulent Belaya River at the foot of the Trezubets Mountain there is a large tourist complex called "Mountain Village". From here you can observe the most beautiful snow-capped peaks of mountain ranges. Extreme sportsmen are happy to stroll along the narrow road around the gorge. It is from this place that the picturesque views open. Like many other recreation centers (Dakhovskaya), "Mountain Village" is located in the valley of the White River. This site is called Granite Canyon. For accommodation, tourists are provided with one and two-room rooms. They are designed for 2-4 guests. The minimum cost of a double room is from 2100 rubles. Here tourists can use a shower with hot and cold water, a bathroom and all the necessary furniture. There is a cafe, a sauna and an outdoor pool on site. Tourists can rent gazebos with barbecue facilities and play table tennis. The administration of the complex organizes river rafting and horseback riding along mountain trails.



"Mountain mood"

Recreation facilities (Dakhovskaya) are large tourist complexes with a wide range of services. "Mountain mood" is no exception. The base is located southwest of the village. It was built in a meadow near the ridge of Azish-Tau. Tourists, relaxing in this complex, can enjoy the clean mountain air and beautiful picturesque landscapes. A restaurant serving delicious dishes is on site. For accommodation, rooms are double and triple. Minimum cost of living - from 2000 rubles. It is possible to rent housing with partial amenities as well as superior comfort. As an entertainment, guests of the complex are offered fast riding on powerful snowmobiles, hiking and horseback riding, interesting excursions to local attractions.



"Dakhovskaya Sloboda"

This tourist complex offers a wide selection of economy, family, superior and standard rooms. The minimum cost of accommodation is 2200 rubles. The rooms can accommodate from 2 to 4 guests. The entertainment programs of this recreation center (Dakhovskaya) are designed for people with different interests and needs. Here you can visit the billiard room, bathhouse, pool. ATVs and bicycles are available for rent in the summer, and snowmobiles in the winter. Also in this complex you can go in for extreme sports, such as horseback riding in the mountains, river rafting, jeeping.

"Guest estate"





The recreation center is located exactly in the place where the two rivers join their waters - Dah and Belaya. Wooden houses were built to stay on site. All of them are equipped with necessary furniture, equipped with household appliances. The territory is equipped with barbecue facilities, gazebos, there are sports fields and playgrounds for children. The houses are designed for 5-8 guests. The minimum cost per person will be 1,500 rubles. There is the possibility of installing an extra bed, an additional charge for this will be 300 rubles. Guests of this recreation center should be aware that the cost of the weekend increases. It provides free parking, rental of sports equipment. Tourists can take excursion programs.

Golden Deer





Those who want to get closer to pristine nature are recommended to choose this tourist complex. All vacationers will receive the maximum charge of vivacity, will be able to fully restore the lost strength. During your stay here you can arrange walks along mountain ranges, rafting on rivers, visit waterfalls and forests. The village of Dakhovskaya is located only 10 kilometers away. There are two buildings for living. The rooms are designed for 3 people. The minimum cost is from 800 rubles. There is the possibility of installing additional space, the cost is 400 rubles. All rooms are equipped with satellite TV, good modern furniture. The building has a large dining room, bathroom, a spacious lounge or fireplace room.
