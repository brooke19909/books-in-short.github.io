Summer Dream 3 * (Rhodes, Greece): hotel description, services, reviews

Today, Greece is one of the most popular resorts in the world. Golden beaches, gentle sunshine, national hospitality and quality service - that's what attracts millions of tourists. Dozens of hotels here open their doors annually and offer relaxation with a different set of attractions and price options. The Summer Dream 3 * hotel is no exception.

Rhodes is a Greek island that is often called the "pearl of the Mediterranean." This is where the hotel is located. We will get acquainted with the description, services and reviews of tourists about this resort.





Location

Always green, with bizarre reliefs and small coves, the Greek island of Rhodes offers breathtaking views of the endless sea. On its northwestern coast in the center of the village of Theologos in 1992 Summer Dream Hotel 3 * was founded. It is most convenient to get to it from the international airport of Rhodes, which is located just 4 km from the resort. To do this, you can use the bus or order a taxi.

Description

Externally, the hotel is a two-story complex in several buildings. The surrounding area is quite extensive, well-groomed, with orchard. Crossings through landscaped areas are paved with neat paths. An outdoor pool is located in front of the hotel. The interior is quite restrained, but it is comfortable and neat. All this splendor in aggregate resembles a piece of paradise, which is not accidentally called the Summer Dream Hotel 3 *. Greece, about. Rhodes in particular only reinforces this status. And the locals are doing their best to use the natural resources and rich history of the country to create entertainment and educational trips and excursions.





Rooms

Summer Dream 3 * hotel includes 67 rooms of five types: single (standard), double, triple and family rooms (studios and apartments). Sharing guests is not allowed, but you can place a cot (for a fee).

All rooms are furnished with comfortable modern furniture. The studios and apartments have a kitchen and all the necessary equipment, utensils for self-cooking and heating food. Each room has a balcony overlooking the picturesque sea and island landscape. For a comfortable stay, the hotel also has air conditioning, satellite TV, a refrigerator, a bathroom with toiletries and a direct dial telephone. Room cleaning according to the rules of the hotel takes place daily, and linen and towels are changed at the request of guests.

In addition, in the reception area you can use free Wi-Fi, a safe for storing documents and valuables, and luggage storage.





Restaurants, bars

When choosing a Greek resort, the tourist does not have a question about food, as cafes and restaurants are open there in abundance, and the prices are very reasonable. There is also a restaurant at the Summer Dream 3 * hotel. Rhodes serves its guests delicious national dishes and has a menu of international cuisine. Hotel guests can arrange meals included in the price of the permit. Breakfast and dinner are served buffet style. You can also visit any other restaurant outside the hotel at a convenient time for yourself. Guests can have a tasty snack by the pool. There is a cafe and bar with an extensive menu of drinks.

Services

As already mentioned, Summer Dream Hotel 3 * on its territory has an outdoor pool, which is conditionally divided into two zones: a nursery and an adult. The water in it changes daily and is being purified. Sun beds and sun loungers are provided free of charge. For business people there is a conference room, fax, photocopy and internet cafe. In addition, the hotel has billiards and a cinema. For children there is a kids club, a playground and a nanny call. Other services include medical assistance, a car park and laundry facilities.





Entertainment

The sea and the beach are perhaps the most important entertainment for which tourists seek Summer Dream 3 *. Rhodes has an amazing nature and climate. The holiday season lasts from June to October. Average air temperatures are 25-27 degrees, and water - 22 degrees. Sandy beaches stretch for hundreds of kilometers. The hotel is located on the third beach line, and the distance to the coast is approximately 600 meters. The road runs through village shops and cafes. The beach is municipal. It has water attractions (scooters, boats, windsurfing). Loungers and deckchairs at an affordable price can be rented.

Minuses
