The best Sestroretsk hotels: an overview

Sestroretsk is a city within the Kurortny District of St. Petersburg, located on the shores of the Gulf of Finland. This place is famous for mineral waters and healing mud. In the city there are several interesting historical areas. If you plan to improve your health and get to know Sestroretsk better, the hotels of the city will provide you with a decent welcome.

Mini-Hotel "Black and White"

If you want a quiet and relaxing holiday, go to the mini-hotel Black and White in Sestroretsk. It is located at: Primorskoye Shosse, 238. Here guests can take advantage of the following advantages:

  • banquet rooms for 30 and 40 people;
  • wood-fired bathhouse;
  • summer playground;
  • organization of events;
  • accessible areas for the disabled.

To accommodate guests provided 7 comfortable rooms. Cost - from 2990 rubles per day.
