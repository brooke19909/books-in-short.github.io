Inertialess coil: features of choice

Almost every fisherman has a gear in his collection on which a non-inertia reel is mounted. This device must meet all the requirements, because otherwise you will not see successful fishing. Such devices are worth a lot, so they are often purchased in a single copy and used for several years.

Thus, you must carefully consider the choice of coil.

Inertialess devices should have the following characteristics. The quality of the device depends on the gear ratio and power. The smaller the first parameter, the more power the non-inertial coil has. The gear ratio represents the ratio of the turns of the logger to the number of revolutions of the handle.

This ratio is different for different devices. Therefore, when determining the power of the coil, one should look at the gear ratio, which, for example, can be designated 4: 1. This suggests that for one turn of the handle the logger will perform five rotations.

It turns out that the more painful the diameter and length of the spool, the further you can cast the bait. Also, the inertialess coil is considered to be of better quality if there are more bearings in its design.



The reason lies in the fact that in this case, the load falls on the rotating components of the device more evenly. However, you must be careful, because there are coils not the highest level, with many bearings made of low-quality material. It is better to pay attention to well-known companies.

The performance of the device is affected by the friction brake, which ensures the rotation of the spool when exerted on the fishing line. With this function, you can prevent the breakage of the line with the resistance of the fish. A high-quality friction brake lowers the line without jerking, smoothly. Adjusting the mechanisms of the coil makes it possible to strengthen or loosen the clamp of the spool, which will allow working with the use of scaffolds of different diameters on one device.



When choosing a coil, you need to see where the friction brake is located. It can be located both rear and front. In this case, the choice depends only on the personal preference of the fisherman. Most people buy non-inertia coils with rear clutch, despite the fact that devices with a front brake are considered more reliable.

An instant stopper for reversing is useful when bottom gear is used to catch fish . With this coil element, you can fine tune the nod. It should be noted that the smaller the backlash, the better the device.

The reliable inertia-free coil has in its design a conical roller of the logger, which, as a rule, has a hard coating on the bearing. This element helps prevent twisting of the fishing line.
