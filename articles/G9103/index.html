Lake Beloe (Altai Territory): the recreation center "Skala", "Dali-ViDali", "Priozernaya" and the Tigievs rest house

Altai Territory is famous for its resorts and is known throughout the world. This is where people can gain strength and just have a good vacation. Lake Beloe (Altai Territory) is very popular . Recreation facilities, although there are not numerous, but this is enough to comfortably spend time on a pond.

Tigiev Holiday House

A nice feature of the Tigievs holiday home is that it is located in close proximity to the lake. The base itself consists of one two-story manor, in which 20 rooms. Such a vacation is suitable for friends who dream of spending it in nature. Rooms of different capacities. There are one-, two-, three-, four-, five- and even six-bed rooms. However, all amenities are on the street, shower is paid. A big advantage is the presence of a TV and washbasin. But for nature lovers this is not a hindrance, especially if you take into account the beautiful surroundings and the healing air, which Lake Beloye is famous for (Altai Territory). The recreation centers, although equipped with the most comfortable conditions, however, spending time in them should not resemble stuffy apartments in noisy cities.



The dining room and kitchen are shared, but it is quite comfortable here. Every day there is a new menu, and you can also order something at your own discretion or even add it yourself. For an extra fee, staff at the holiday home can provide fresh vegetables and fruits, only picked in the garden. It is also worth noting the presence of parking on the territory, but it is located in the open.

The recreation center "Priozernaya"



Mount Sinyuha is an attraction of this region, within the limits of which Lake Beloe (Altai Territory) is located. Recreation facilities built in the vicinity advertise healing mountain air. Not far from the top of the mountain stands the Priozernaya tourist complex. It is in high demand among vacationers. The base works both in summer and in winter. On its territory there is a playground where parents can safely leave their child without fear that something will happen to him. The base itself consists of several houses for up to 4 people. There is a kitchen-dining room. And although you have to cook yourself, rest from this will not get worse. For lovers of barbecue there is a specially designated place with barbecue. Guided tours are organized for children, volleyball, chess and checkers competitions are held. You can go swimming on Lake Beloe (Altai Territory). Recreation centers, or rather, their staff, especially for these purposes, cleaned the beaches and equipped them with sun loungers and parasols.

The recreation center "Skala"



One of the new tourist centers is the recreation center "Skala", located on the shores of Lake Beloe. The complex has several summer houses for up to 4 people and a hotel center. Also nearby is a tent camp for everyone. People come here who really like to relax in nature, because this complex will give an unforgettable experience of the virgin forests that surround the pond. The recreation center "Skala" (White Lake) offers a specific list of services. Of the amenities there is a summer kitchen, as well as a bathroom, but unfortunately shared. The complex has a bathhouse. This is very good, because not at all bases in the Altai Territory you can steam up from the heart. The recreation center on the White Lake "Dali-ViDali"



On this lake there is another recreation center, no less popular than the above. This is the Dali-ViDali complex. In the summer season, up to 40 people can be accommodated here. The base works all year round. There is a playground, gazebos, specially designated places for people who like to enjoy barbecue. The unique mountain air contributes to relaxation. The recreation center on the White Lake "Dali-ViDali" organizes various excursions and trips. For those who like to relax at night, there are discos. Cost per place - 500 p. per day. For an additional fee, meals are offered. There is also a cafe, billiards, gym, tennis court and even a library. However, the main reason for the arrival of tourists in these places remains recovery.
