JW Dubai Marquis Dubai: short description

JW Dubai Marquis Dubai 5 * is a fashionable hotel, which consists of two tall buildings and offers guests 1608 rooms with different levels of comfort. This hotel is a 10-minute drive from Dubai Mall. Dubai Marina Promenade is a 20-minute drive away.

Hotel Facilities

Vacationers are met by the helpful hotel staff; individual check-in and check-out are offered. The reception is open 24/7 - here you can leave things in the storage room, order a car, exchange currency for a local one, use the help in organizing excursions.

The hotel has a laundry, ATM. Babysitting services are available for babies. For business meetings, there is a well-equipped conference room. If desired, guests can book a transfer to and from the airport.

Room service

The 5-star JW Dubai Marquis Dubai Hotel offers three types of rooms.

  1. Standard. Room size reaches 44 square meters. m. Guests can choose accommodation with a double or two single beds. In addition, it is possible to settle in a room with a view of the city or the beach.
  2. Executive. As an extra service, guests are offered a complimentary breakfast and lounge access.
  3. Suite These luxurious rooms are 88 square meters. m. They have a bedroom and a spacious living room. In addition, access to the lounge and complimentary breakfast.

Regardless of the level of comfort, the rooms are equipped with comfortable furniture, carpeted floors. The bathtub and toilet are tiled and equipped with a shower. In addition, each room is equipped with a minibar, however, an additional charge is required for its use.

Food and entertainment system

On the territory of the JW Marriott Marquis Hotel Dubai 5 * there are 14 restaurants that offer their visitors dishes of national and European cuisine. The main catering facilities are open buffet, but some are a la carte restaurants. They have a la carte service, and you need to book a table in advance to visit.

You can spend your free time visiting the sauna, spa or wellness center. For those who prefer to monitor their figure, the hotel offers to go to workout in a well-equipped fitness center.

The day can be spent with a glass of cocktail near the pool, where the animation team conducts an entertainment program. In the evening and at night, the hotel hosts a disco in a nightclub.

To get to the coast, to the well-equipped beach of the city, it will take about 15-20 minutes by car.
