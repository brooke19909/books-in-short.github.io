Asel Resort Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey): description, service, reviews

The small hotel Asel Resort Hotel 4 * has long attracted the attention of tourists from around the world. Many vacationers often recommend visiting it to their friends, acquaintances and subscribers on social networks, showing vivid photos taken during the holidays, which perfectly reflect the reigning atmosphere.

general information

Asel Resort Hotel 4 * is a small place to relax, located near the Turkish capital, in the town of Goynuk. This area belongs to the resort region of Kemer, which is famous for having a variety of entertainment in its cities. The nearest beach is 300 meters from the hotel, often vacationers get to it on foot, which takes about 5-10 minutes.

The nearest air routes are located in two large Turkish cities, at a rather impressive distance - 45 km (Antalya) and 18 km (Kemer). The hotel offers tourists a paid shuttle service, but does not necessarily include it in the package. Tourists, if desired, can use a taxi or public transport to get to the hotel.

The furniture of the rooms is rather modest. Here there is one large double bed (in some rooms it is replaced by two single beds), as well as a wardrobe, where vacationers can store their personal belongings. As a decor, there is a small mirror. Opposite the bed there is a TV with satellite channels. Among them, according to tourists, there are several Russian speakers.

If desired, guests can use Wi-Fi access, as well as a safe, which can be installed for a small surcharge. Travelers often note the presence of air conditioning in the room, which softens the tropical temperature conditions inherent in Turkey.

All apartments have a shower room, which has a bathing cabin, as well as a bathroom. The hotel provides towels and bathrobes.



Nutrition

A lot of attention from tourists is paid to the food that is provided at Asel Resort Hotel 4 *. The reviews constantly say that, if desired, any hotel guest has the right to visit a local restaurant located in the building of the building. Here, every day, within the framework of the all-inclusive concept, a buffet is served, which offers dishes prepared in compliance with the best Turkish traditions of creating culinary masterpieces. Some types of alcohol are offered completely free of charge, but you need to pay for juice, coffee, beer and hookah.

Near the water zone in Asel Resort Hotel 4 * there is a bar where there is a separate menu, different from what is offered in the restaurant. It contains a huge assortment of soft drinks, soft drinks, as well as tea and coffee. If desired, guests can order exotic fruits and light snacks.



Infrastructure

While staying at the hotel, guests can use Internet access in all corners of Asel Resort Hotel 4 *. The description of this vacation spot also states that laundry and dry cleaning services are provided here - they must be paid separately.

All apartments are regularly cleaned once every three days. If you have any questions related to the provision of services, living conditions, as well as the location of certain objects, guests can always contact the administrator, who is located at the reception desk.



Aquazone

The guests spend a large part of their time within a small aquazone located in the open territory of Asel Resort Hotel 4 *. It consists of a sun terrace, fully furnished with sun loungers and huge folding beach umbrellas, here vacationers can comfortably sit after water procedures.

In the very center of the terrace is a swimming pool filled with clean water - it is updated every day, which ensures constant freshness. There is no automatic water heating system in the niche; this process is carried out under the influence of sunlight. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe pool is 70 sq.m.



Beach

The coastline is located at a distance of about 300 meters from the building of the main building of Asel Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey). In order to get to it, you must cross the road, which many recommend doing through the underpass.

This beach is not badly landscaped, it offers a picturesque view not only of the vast expanses of the Mediterranean Sea, but also of the gardens, which are especially pleasing with their flowering in the summer. The entire coastline is covered with fine and very soft sand, the descent into the water is gentle, which is quite safe even for children.

Staying on the beach, you can rent umbrellas and sun loungers, which are issued at a special point. It is completely free to use the public toilet and locker room. If desired, tourists can have fun with water skiing, parachuting, as well as scuba diving. It also offers the service of renting a yacht or a small boat, which is especially popular among couples who are on a romantic trip.

Prices

Tour operators in different countries of the world offer different prices for trips to this place. In the reviews of travelers from the Moscow region, its average cost is given, which is about 40-45 thousand rubles for two. This amount includes not only a weekly stay, but also travel expenses associated with the flight, insurance, meals.

Sharing their impressions obtained during the holiday, tourists talk about the excellent service at Asel Resort Hotel 4 *, the beautiful interior in the rooms, as well as about the sights that you can visit while staying here. People also recommend purchasing sightseeing tours on-site, right at the hotel or at the nearest office, since in this case its cost will be much lower than what Moscow agents can offer, for example.
