What are Nomi capable of (telephones)? Reviews and specifications

Studying devices of Chinese manufacturers, you can stumble upon a little-known company Nomi. Although the company did not receive due popularity in the CIS, it has something to offer the buyer. What is a Nomi phone?

Range

The buyer will definitely be interested in the variety of Nomi devices. An extensive assortment includes not only Android devices, but also the most common cellular ones.

Among the devices you can find simple budget employees and functional devices of the middle category. Do not ignore cell phones that support two SIM cards, with tangible autonomy.

Other companies were also copied. Surprisingly, the manufacturer does not even try to hide this flaw. Such thoughtless imitation creates an unpleasant sensation.

Using a ready-made design, the company is trying to save on materials. All devices are made exclusively of plastic. In addition, some devices have a glossy case. One of these was the Nomi I500 phone. The case of the device becomes a magnet for dust, fingerprints and scratches.

Even mid-range phones have a similar problem. It is noticeable that the manufacturer did not think about the protective coating or simply refused it. Therefore, the user will be forced to independently solve this problem.

Camera





In Nomi phones, the manufacturer installed matrices acceptable for state employees. The cheapest smartphones got a 5 megapixel camera, more expensive models received 8 and 13 MP. No complaints should arise here, because the price-quality ratio is very good.

In expensive models, such as the I505, you can find a front camera with 5 megapixels. Other phones have 2 or 0.3 MP each, which is also good.

The screens installed by the company on their devices leave much to be desired. First of all, the resolution is lame. So, the five-inch I500 display received 960 by 540 pixels. This is not enough to get rid of cubes and grain.

TFT matrices are also common. This technology is installed on the I503, and the resolution of the 5-inch display is 800 by 480.

There are models that have worked much better. The I505 received not only an IPS matrix, but also a decent resolution of 1280 by 720 pixels.

Hardware

Things are better with the "stuffing" of devices. The cheap MTK processor allows the devices of the company to obtain the performance necessary for state employees at a low cost.

Almost all series I devices received a gigabyte of RAM. The amount of memory is quite acceptable for a state employee. RAM is enough not only for working with the Internet and several applications, but also for entertainment. You will have to forget about advanced games, but the phone will cope with the rest.

Nomi mobile phones and their native memory are not deprived. Such devices as I400 and I401 have four gigabytes, the rest have 8 and 16 GB.

Autonomy

Owners will encounter a very unpleasant feature of the Nomi. Absolutely all I Series smartphones have weak batteries. The most powerful battery is installed in the I550 and is 3000 maH. On average, the battery capacity ranges from 1500 to 2100 maH. Although the Nomi are state employees, this is definitely not enough for good battery life.

The company's devices in passive mode last a little less than a day. Active use reduces the time to 3-5 hours, depending on the hardware and battery.

Among similar devices from the Middle Kingdom, the cost of the company's products looks much more democratic. The cheapest, of course, are the Nomi cell phones. The prices for push-button devices range from one to one and a half thousand rubles.

Much more expensive are devices running on the Android system. Such Nomi phones have prices in the region of 3-9 thousand rubles. One of the most expensive is the 505.
