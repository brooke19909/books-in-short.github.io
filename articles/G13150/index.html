LCD "Matryoshka" (Tolyatti): description, review

Modern new buildings in Togliatti are a real opportunity to get your own apartment in an area with developed infrastructure. LCD "Matryoshka" (Tolyatti) offers its residents comfortable housing that meets all modern requirements of residents. How true is the description? Are customer expectations met? In this we will try to understand within the framework of this material.





about the project

The modern housing format is what exactly fits the description of the Matryoshka residential complex (Togliatti). Reviews of the first customers were just inspired by the uniqueness manifested in architecture, layouts. If you are tired of the same type of residential complexes, gray and boring facades, you should consider the LCD "Matryoshka".





Two three-entrance houses on 14 and 18 floors with bright facades will definitely attract attention. But they still create excellent living conditions for modern residents.

Location

For the construction of the complex, the intersection of Frunze Street and Moskovsky Prospekt was chosen. Currently, the district is actively being built up and developing, due to which it creates favorable conditions for life. Address LCD "Matryoshka": Tolyatti, Moskovsky pr. / St. Frunze.

The area is free from hazardous industries and is characterized by favorable environmental conditions. As it grows and develops further, squares and parks with green spaces will appear here, which will become a favorite place for walks and recreation for local residents.

Technology

A monolithic foundation provides reliability and strength, so necessary for high-rise buildings. Facades are represented by expanded clay concrete blocks and a modern ventilation system. This technology allows you to insulate the walls, make homes ergonomic, reduce heating costs in the cold season.





All balconies and loggias are glazed and insulated, which allows their internal space to be used as a full room, study or conservatory. The entrances are equipped with an intercom system and systems: fire and warning residents in case of emergency.

Infrastructure

Given the fact that the complex was built in a relatively young residential neighborhood, many are concerned about the availability of necessary infrastructure. Customer reviews LCD "Matryoshka" (Togliatti) just focus on the comfort provided to all residents, including families with small children. Just a few minutes walk from the new buildings is an excellent kindergarten and secondary school. The first buyers have already managed to visit these institutions and see the quality of the educational services provided. Children do not have to travel a significant distance to the nearest kindergartens and schools in the early morning.





Convenient parking for 220 cars will be located on the territory of the complex and will provide all residents and their guests with parking spaces for cars of any class and size.
