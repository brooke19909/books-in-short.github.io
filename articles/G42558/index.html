Hotel Eftalia Aytur Hotel (Turkey / Alanya / Alanya Center): description and reviews

According to many tour operators, the Eftalia Aytur Hotel is a great option if you need to organize youth vacations. Often, young couples and noisy groups of friends choose him to travel to Turkey. Tourists are also pleased with a rather pleasant pricing policy. According to many vacationers, the cost of a trip to such a place for two is approximately 35,000 rubles, which is quite inexpensive for a decent level of service and good living conditions that the hotel provides.

general information

Eftalia Aytur Hotel was built in 2013. Due to its novelty, the hotel offers modern territory and fresh, high-quality repair in the apartments. It is often visited by Russian tourists who note in their reviews that the hotel staff has a considerable number of Russian-speaking staff - this greatly facilitates living here.

In the description of Eftalia Aytur Hotel, it is stated that its total area is 35,000 square meters. m. On this territory there are several buildings in which all the rooms represented in the fund are located: one of them, six-story, is the main, and the other six are additional. All of these buildings also have a fairly decent number of storeys - they extend up to 4 and 5 tiers.



Location

Eftalia Aytur Hotel is located in one of the largest resort centers in Turkey - Alanya. This region is famous for the large number of places for entertainment that guests of the hotel so often want to visit. Not far from this place of rest is the populated town of Konakli (5 km) - here you can see a huge number of attractions that visitors like to do.

The coastline is within walking distance of the hotel - in order to get to it, just cross the road, which can be done through the underpass. Tourists who rest here consider this solution to be very correct for security reasons.

The hotel is located at a fairly impressive distance from international airports. One of them is located in Alanya (50 km), and the second - in the large resort city of Antalya (110 km). And in one and the other, future hotel guests arrive, from where they are picked up by the car provided as part of the transfer service.



Rooms

Eftalia Aytur Hotel belongs to the category of large hotels, as its room stock contains in its structure 462 apartments. All of them are divided among themselves into several categories, depending on the provided conditions of comfort: Standard, Bunkbed, Large and Apart.

Each room has an individual air conditioning, which is able to regulate the air temperature in the room at a certain level.

In the apartments of the hotel, regardless of the comfort of the conditions, a private bathroom is provided, where there is all the necessary plumbing for living, as well as bath accessories and bathrobes.

Bunkbed

In these rooms, many guests of the Eftalia Aytur Hotel recommend staying for tourists who are going to relax with children or teenagers. This is primarily due to the fact that for their placement there is one bunk bed. For a couple of adults, a large double bed is provided, presented in the form of a European-style bed. In all other respects, the furniture set present here is fully consistent with that available in Standard rooms.

This type of apartment has a large balcony, which is open and partially furnished - it has a table and a pair of armchairs where you can comfortably sit.



Large

Large type rooms have only one room of 25 square meters. m, in which there is a visual division of space into two zones: for relaxation and for sleep. This type of apartment is designed to accommodate three guests, for which there are two beds: one of them is single, and the second is double. In the recreation area there is an armchair and a small sofa. Many tourists recommend reading books and drinking hot drinks here.

Large room also has a balcony with excellent views of the sea, mountains and beautiful gardens, which bloom abundantly in the midst of the spa season.

The relaxation area also has a pair of armchairs and a coffee table. There is also a hanging TV. If desired, you can order a safe and a minibar in your room.

The room is equipped with a small kitchen where there is everything necessary for cooking. Its structure includes a sink for washing dishes, countertops, cabinets, microwave, stove and other accessories.



Entertainment

Eftalia Aytur Hotel (Alanya) is a hotel that is ideal for youth recreation. First of all, this is due to the fact that it has a huge amount of entertainment. Here every day there is a team of animators, which consists of 15 people. According to vacationers, many of them are fluent in Russian, which greatly facilitates communication with them for tourists from Russia.

Every day, starting in the morning, guests can take part in recreational activities. The earliest of them are sports, they are held mainly near the aquazone. Tourists often create teams and compete with each other in different sports games for which there are playgrounds in the open.

In the evening, there are all kinds of entertainment programs in the form of parties. They play incendiary hits, known throughout the world, as well as shows and competitions.



Nutrition

Gourmets at the hotel do not miss the opportunity to tell in their reviews about Eftalia Aytur Hotel what food is provided here. They talk about two excellent restaurants, one of which serves deliciously prepared dishes of Turkish cuisine, and the other - fish. A buffet is served in them, where, as part of the all-inclusive concept of accommodation, tourists who are vacationing on a tour are invited. To visit these establishments, preliminary reservation of tables in them is required.

Despite the level of service in such establishments, bars are more popular, of which there are only five in the Eftalia Aytur Hotel. Each of them offers a unique list of snacks, desserts, as well as a huge assortment of drinks and cocktails that will appeal to vacationers.

Eating options include a diet menu or a vegetarian menu.



Services

The hotel has a wide range of services that are aimed at improving the living conditions for guests belonging to different age categories. One of the most popular among them is the rental of cars and bicycles, where experienced travelers recommend going to explore local sights or admire the picturesque scenery, which are many in the vicinity of Kemer.

Eftalia Aytur Hotel often presents luxurious conference rooms, of which there are only two. They are ideally arranged to hold various business events, as well as meetings and presentations. As noted by those who rented such halls, they are fully equipped with everything necessary for the event to be bright and successful.

In addition to all of the above, guests can use the paid services of a doctor, dry cleaning, laundry, masters in the beauty salon and contact the tour desk for free to select both individual and group tours to local attractions.



Beach

Special attention of tourists is paid to the coastline, where the hotel has its own plot, intended for the relaxation of guests. As they note in their reviews of the Eftalia Aytur Hotel, everything is very well arranged, there are places to relax in the form of sun loungers covered with large umbrellas - you can borrow them for free, but for towels and mattresses you will need to pay a rent.

If desired, vacationers can take part in water activities that are available along the coastline.

Relaxation

The Eftalia Aytur Hotel features several options for guests to relax in. One of them is the aquazone, which is represented by three pools with slides that operate without heating, but also with a water purification system. There is also a large spa center consisting of a massage room and a thermal section.

Fans of an active lifestyle can visit sports and gymnasiums, open playgrounds for games, as well as engage in water sports, most of which are extreme and can be practiced only if the instructor is paid. Many travelers admit that it was here that they mastered surfing and diving skills. They also talk about highly qualified instructors.
