Patong hotels: overview, description, rating

Patong is the heart of Phuket. The success and popularity of the resort revolves around two things: an amazing beach and a carefree wild nightlife.

Patong Beach is a famous beach stretching for 2.5 kilometers. This is a very popular and noisy place, but on both sides of it there are more quiet and shallow beaches. In the middle of Patong Beach, it is difficult to park not only a car, but also a bicycle. The northern part of the beach is much calmer: fewer people, convenient parking and many small shops.

Patong has many busy markets and a large shopping center. Prices at the resort are much higher than in other areas of Phuket. This is because many tourists come here from all over the world.

Thanks to the popular beach and a huge selection of entertainment, the resort is fully built up with hotels. Patong hotels on the 1st line are a rarity for the resort, there are not very many of them. But there are many such hotels that are located just a few tens of meters from the beach.

Tourists can choose both simple boarding houses and luxury hotels of Patong 5 stars. In Thailand, you can relax as a VIP person, or you can book a budget hotel.

Impiana Resort Patong 5 *

Overlooking the Andaman Sea, this unique boutique hotel is located directly on the sandy beach in Patong. The beach pool is located a few meters from the water's edge, and many restaurants, bars, shops and nightclubs of the famous Bangla Street are within walking distance of the hotel.

The Impiana Resort Patong Hotel provides 71 standard rooms with beautiful views of the sea or the well-kept garden. For an elite holiday, there is a royal villa with a luxurious interior, modern electronics and a jacuzzi. All rooms and suites come with free Wi-Fi, a fridge and minibar, and satellite TV.

The following restaurants and bars are open on site:

  • Sala Bua Restaurant serves a Mediterranean menu. The chef prepares specialties, while dinner on the terrace overlooking the sunset creates a unique atmosphere.
  • Three Spices Restaurant is one of Patong's modern places combining authentic cuisine with healthy eating. The menu consists of dishes from Malaysia, the Philippines, Thailand, China and India.
  • Beach bar - perfect for lovers of refreshments and signature cocktails.

Hotel Services:

  • Car rental.
  • Airport transfer services.
  • Organization of excursions.
  • Currency exchange.
  • Car parking.

The cost of living in a boutique hotel starts at 20 thousand rubles per night.

Phuket Marriott Resort & Spa, Merlin Beach 5 *

The five-star Merlin Beach Hotel invites couples with children, business travelers and youth companies to relax. Noisy Patong is very close, but the hotel's excellent location on a secluded beach makes it peaceful and quiet.

The elegant rooms and suites feature luxurious bedding, modern appliances and a private balcony with scenic views. The resort has a fitness center and three outdoor pools. The hotel offers a wide range of entertainment for children, as well as many excursions.

The hotel provides for guests only suites and junior suites. There are apartments with a private pool and a terrace.

All rooms are renovated and decorated with chic decorative elements. The terracotta tiled floor goes well with natural wood in the interior. All rooms have a private balcony with pool, beach or garden views.

Patong Resort 4 *

This superb resort is one of the best places in Phuket. Well-equipped rooms, excellent amenities, hospitable staff and a large area make Patong Resort the ideal place to relax. The hotel is located just minutes from Patong Beach, as well as nearby shopping outlets, clubs and restaurants.

Patong Resort Hotel consists of 325 rooms of various categories. All apartments are non-smoking and feature modern amenities.

There are several restaurants on site. Pavarotti serves Italian cuisine in the garden. The Japanese bar offers a wide selection of dishes: sushi, sashimi. In the lounge, you can enjoy a refreshing drink or choose a wine from the extensive wine list. Patong Resort is suitable for hosting various events. To do this, there is a large conference room and meeting rooms.

A hotel room will cost from 4 thousand rubles per day.

Wyndham Sea Pearl Resort Phuket 4 *

The resort consists of villas, whose windows offer unforgettable views of Patong Bay and the Andaman Sea. It is located in a lush tropical garden near the beach. All rooms have a jacuzzi and a balcony with pool or sea views.

The resort area is designed as comfortably as possible: numerous pools, a spa with various services and many other luxurious amenities.

The restaurant serves Thai dishes using the freshest seafood and organic products.

A night in a hotel costs from 7 thousand rubles.

Best Western Patong Beach 3 *

Best Western Patong Beach Hotel is located in the city center and offers affordable accommodation surrounded by major attractions. The gorgeous Patong Beach is a 10-minute walk from the hotel, while Jungceylon Shopping Center and the colorful nightlife on Bangla Road are even closer.

The clean and modern rooms are elegantly furnished and feature flat-screen TVs and private balconies. Guests can relax by the pool or by the bar, or dine in the trendy Mana Restaurant, which overlooks the street, which serves international cuisine.

Hemingways Silk Hotel 3 *

This is a new hotel, which is located in the very center of the resort. Restaurants and a shopping center are just a two-minute walk away. It features a swimming pool, a rooftop bar and a large sun terrace. The beach is 7 minutes from the hotel. Hotel Silk offers well-equipped superior rooms with or without a balcony. The hotel has a rooftop restaurant. Breakfast is served on the ground floor and includes: sausages, bacon, eggs, cereal toasts and croissants, seasonal fruits, as well as some Asian dishes. Coffee and tea are available at the buffet.

Royal Phawadee Village 3 *

Only a three-minute walk from Patong Beach, this romantic boutique hotel offers warm Thai hospitality. The hotel is built in the style of a traditional Thai village and is decorated with handmade sculptures.

Guests can enjoy spending time by the pool and enjoy delicious Italian and Japanese cuisine at the Tuscany Restaurant.

What is a vacation in Patong hotels? This is an exciting nightlife, great shopping, a huge selection of restaurants, beautiful snow-white beaches, transparent blue pools and the famous Thai hospitality.
