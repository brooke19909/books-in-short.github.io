Children's hotels in Turkey: relax with the whole family

The most diverse categories of citizens go to Turkish resorts: people of different social status, marital status and age. Here you can have a great time, plenty of sunbathing in the sun and swimming in the gentle waters of the sea. When choosing a hotel, pay attention to what category of tourists it is oriented to. Young people should choose a place of residence located closer to nightclubs and discos. Couples and elderly people should give preference to quiet and comfortable complexes, where it is pleasant to relax, enjoying a serene tranquility. On the territory of such hotels you can find beauty salons, spa, massage rooms and saunas.





Separately, it is worth highlighting children's hotels in Turkey. This category of establishments is equipped in such a way as to ensure a comfortable stay not only for adults, but also for their children. The rooms in these hotels have cots. The restaurant will offer you high chairs with high legs, but the children will experience special joy when they find themselves in the territory adjacent to the hotel. In addition to the adult pool, there will definitely be a "paddling pool" in which young tourists can play enough in the water. On the street there are also children's play complexes, slides, sandboxes and swings. Very rarely, but it still happens that the weather deteriorates, then the child will be able to frolic inside the hotel, in a special children's playroom.

Prices for hotels in Turkey 2013-2014





The cost of holidays in Turkey increased on average, compared to last year, by 10-15%, depending on the region and the hotel complex. The average price per person will be 40,000 rubles for 6 days and 5 nights. For a good rest, it is necessary to choose four- and five-star children's hotels. In Turkey, the choice of places of residence is huge, so you can find options both cheaper and more expensive, but you should carefully study the infrastructure of the hotel. It is also worth making sure that the hotel is located near the beach, and the beach itself is sandy and gentle. The services of a nanny, as well as the availability of organized children's leisure, are no less important - they allow parents to allocate time for themselves and get vivid impressions from the vacation.
