Hotel LEXX, Koktebel: where is it located? Reviews

The LEXX hotel (Koktebel) is remarkable to everyone , even in comparison with many other upscale club-type hotels. During the construction and arrangement of the territory, its creators made sure to adhere to the eco-concept in everything, and now this is reflected both in the quality of the services offered and in each segment of its infrastructure. Made of purely natural materials (even wooden decoration predominates in decoration), the LEXX Hotel (Koktebel) fits perfectly into the natural landscape.





Territory

The very vast territory of the hotel-club is carefully ennobled, but so that it does not look like a foreign interspersing in the pristine Crimean landscape. A special pride of the creators is the large and well-groomed trees that were exported from Italy by small seedlings. Ecotourism is the main component of leisure offered by the LEXX hotel (Koktebel). This is because a more suitable place for this purpose is unlikely to be found - only the Crimea and only Koktebel with its cleanest peninsula, including beaches and the sea. The landscapes here are amazing! Service and comfort are at an exceptionally high level. Hotel LEXX (Koktebel) will be able to accept and occupy all comers, regardless of age, gender and preferences.

Kara-Dag

Excursions to the reserve are exclusive, only for hotel guests with their own guide, for other vacationers this part of the Crimea is closed. There are many reasons for this: the flora and fauna of this picturesque place on the peninsula are unique, in the Red Book there are 125 endangered species of animals left only in Kara-Dag. Here visitors can meet wild boars, European roe deer, stone martens, black vultures and many other rare fauna species in wildlife. The excursion to the research biostation, which is also provided only by the LEXX spa hotel (Russia, Koktebel), is extremely interesting and informative.

But the local flora looks especially jubilant: feather grasses (eight species!) Sway in the hills, among which flowers of a fabulous beauty erupt. Only here you can touch real relic trees, and the only volcano in the world, preserved from the time of the Jurassic period, will leave impressions truly unforgettable. The walk will not be easy, but it is worth the effort! Here, hotel guests love to do yoga, and the "enlightened" claim that Kara-Dag is a place of power. Hiking enthusiasts come here. Club-hotel LEXX in Koktebel, reviews of which are presented in the article, does not leave anyone indifferent, everyone knows about the incredibly beautiful nature of this famous corner of Crimea. The magnificent bay has been described many times, the mountainsides twined with grapes and centuries-old pine trees tending to the sky.
