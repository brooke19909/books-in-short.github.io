Powerbank, portable charger: description, specifications, instructions

As the productivity of mobile devices increases, so do the demands of their power supply elements. A standard battery for a phone or smartphone can support the gadget's performance for 2-3 days under medium load conditions. Obviously, this will not be enough if you plan a week-long trip to nature or a trip in which it may not be possible to recharge. For such cases, special devices called powerbank are provided - a portable charger that has a large capacity to power the same smartphones, tablets, cameras, etc.





What is a powerbank?

Outwardly, such devices resemble the same handsets of mobile phones. The standard form factor is a small cylinder or rectangle in which the working filling is placed. As a shell, plastic or metal is used. The second option for finishing the case increases strength, and the first - facilitates the design of the powerbank. A portable charger of this type only externally differs from conventional mobile batteries, but it is manufactured according to the same principles. As a rule, the filling is a Li-ion element, which is small in size and has a sufficiently high capacity.

However, there are different versions of such drives. The standard version is the one that allows you to charge tablets and smartphones at home via the USB interface. But there are special modifications, which include the model D-Lex Powerbank - a portable launcher and charger intended for use in the car. This drive is powered by a 12V car network and, thanks to two USB outputs, allows you to charge mobile gadgets. This model also features a flashlight and shockproof plastic in the case. If we talk about functionality, then with the help of D-Lex the driver can start the engine.





Main characteristics

When choosing storage batteries, two operational indicators should be taken into account - directly the capacity and charging current. To begin with, it is worth considering the capacity, which averages from 10,000 to 20,000 thousand mAh. For example, one of the best representatives of the segment, the Xiaomi Power Bank device of the Mi modification, provides the user with 16,000 mAh. But here it must be borne in mind that dividing the capacitive potential by the volume of the target battery does not at all give an idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe complete recharge cycles of the battery. To understand the actual possibility of servicing a mobile device, for example, it is necessary to cut about 30-40% of the received capacity.

As for the current strength, on average it is 0.5-3 A. The lower level is optimal for modest consumers in the form of low-power phones, smartphones and flashlights. A tablet or a productive camera may also require 3 A. It is advisable to focus on the recommendations of the device manufacturer and select a powerbank for this value. A portable charger with inappropriate amperage can also perform its task, but the energy efficiency of this process will be reduced.

Xiaomi models





This manufacturer is a leader in the segment, successfully competing with market giants in the form of Apple and Samsung. Although the Chinese developers do not offer anything fundamentally new, releasing completely traditional and already standardized solutions. The simplest Xiaomi product from this family can be called a modification of 5000 mAh. This drive has a modest dimensions of 12.5x5.9x1 cm and a small weight of 156 grams. That is, the device is smaller than many of today's smartphones. More importantly, the Xiaomi Power Bank at 5000 mAh has flat batteries provided with modern surge protection. User interaction with the device is facilitated by the LED indicator of the charge level. If we talk about the shortcomings, then they can safely be attributed to only one single USB-port.

GP Powerbank Series





A rather unusual line, whose representatives can not be called full-fledged powerbank devices. GP models are more likely to be in an intermediate niche between rechargeable drives and chargers. Such devices are designed to recharge the batteries and some versions of batteries separately from the phone. At the same time, the GP Powerbank device itself must be connected to a 220 V outlet during maintenance. The fundamental difference from full-fledged drives is the lack of the possibility of an autonomous charge via USB. Nevertheless, such devices find application. In particular, the GP PB420 modification allows you to simultaneously charge 4 AA and AAA nickel-metal hydride batteries.

User's manual

To start working with the drive, you need to connect the target device to the USB interface. The energy filling process will start automatically. The current status of the serviced battery will be indicated by an indicator on the drive‚Äôs display. Again, do not forget that the socket for this procedure is not required in this case, since powerbank is a portable charger. How to charge multiple batteries with different amperage? As a rule, models in which several USB outputs are provided provide current of different strengths to each circuit. A common example of a three-channel device is a configuration with 1, 2, and 3 A ports.

Conclusion





Despite the obvious benefits of portable drives of this type, using them as universal assistants is not always convenient. After all, this means that along with the same smartphone you will have to wear an auxiliary unit for charging. Especially in this regard, a powerbank with a solar battery is impractical, which, thanks to the accumulation panels, has rather large dimensions, and also requires being outdoors. Nevertheless, there are cases when an additional and independent source of energy is indispensable. This can be not only long trips or trips out of town, but also excessive loads on the smartphone on weekdays, when the charge runs out within a few hours.
