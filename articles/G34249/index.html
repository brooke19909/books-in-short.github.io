Hotel Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Side / Side Center): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Side, being one of the popular Turkish resorts on the Mediterranean coast, annually receives hundreds of thousands of tourists from around the world, including our compatriots. It has a very rich history. Initially, Side settled Greeks-immigrants, then the city was conquered by Alexander of Macedon, and then he passed into the possession of Ancient Rome. It was during this period that he reached his peak. Unfortunately, after the conquest of this territory by the Arabs, almost all cultural monuments were destroyed. Today's Side is a modern seaside resort with a well-developed tourist infrastructure.



general information

The nature of this region is not only the majestic Toros Mountains, completely covered with forests, but also golden sandy beaches. The gentle sun, mild climate and warm Mediterranean Sea - this is exactly what Side attracts tourists like that. In high season, the coast of the resort is literally crowded with vacationers. The main beaches of the city are awarded the Blue Flags. The entrance to the water here is convenient and fairly gentle. On the beaches of the resort, tourists are offered a huge number of different water activities. Those who wish can go sailing, diving, go water skiing, buy excursions by boat or boat. On the territory of Side, many fairly large hotel complexes have been built, there are mini-boutiques, villas and apartments. Everyone can choose a hotel for rest at this resort. Side tour operators are positioning as a fairly affordable direction in terms of price. The cost of hotel accommodation ranges from the lowest to very high. It depends on the category of apartments, their location and specific dates of tours. The price peak in Side hotels falls on July and August. The most popular are considered two- and three-star hotels. One of them is Sayanora Park Hotel 3 *. Reviews about staying in it, infrastructure, location and the list of services provided in this hotel are presented in this article.



Description

"Sayanora Park Hotel" has long been chosen by Russian tourists. They in it are primarily attracted by the low price and proximity to the airport, i.e., a short transfer. Sayanora Park Hotel 3 *, the photo of which is presented below, is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea on the Antalya Riviera. It consists of a complex of two- and three-story buildings and several cottages. The hotel area is a park area.

The hotel was built in 2005. Since then it has been reconstructed several times. The latest update was in 2015. Its location is considered by many Russians to be very good. Within walking distance - not only the beach and many attractions of Side, but also the famous Turkish shops where you can spend great shopping. The distance to the airport is seventy kilometers.

Housing stock

In total, Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * (Side) has fifty-four standard and family rooms. They overlook the park, pool and backyard. Room filling is standard for all Turkish hotels. The rooms have comfortable beds with orthopedic mattresses, a fairly spacious wardrobe, trunk, bedside tables. Of the equipment here, a TV, telephone and split air conditioning are installed. The use of the built-in safe must be paid separately.

The rooms are bright. Before them there are quite spacious balconies with plastic chairs and a table, there is also a dryer for bathing suits and towels. On the windows - blackout curtains, selected to match the bedspreads. On the floor - tiles.

Bathrooms at Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * combined. Towels and toiletries are updated daily. The staff carries out the cleaning carefully at the most convenient time for the residents of the rooms.



Nutrition

Like almost all Turkish hotels, Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * also offers customers tours of the all-inclusive concept, so beloved by Russians. This choice is really justified by the fact that tourists on vacation take the opportunity to disconnect from everyday problems as much as possible. Three times a day, guests at the Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * guests can receive a full buffet-style meal. For this, a spacious restaurant is provided, which can simultaneously accommodate up to two hundred people. The menu is mainly international dishes, a lot of vegetables and fruits, as well as pastries. According to the all-inclusive concept, guests are entitled to receive free drinks at the bar, including alcoholic drinks, but only of local production. This is a common norm in Turkey. For the rest of the booze - imported or freshly squeezed fresh - you need to pay separately.

Prices

In their reviews, many Russians who have already had time to relax at the Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * write that they were satisfied with the ratio of the cost of the purchased tour and the quality of the services offered to them. For example, in the low season for next year, prices start at about twenty-seven thousand for two with departure from Moscow and staying in a double room of the standard category according to the all-inclusive concept for seven nights. The cost of tours in the high season is much higher. It can range from eight hundred dollars and above under the same conditions.



Beach

Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * (Turkey) is five minutes from the beach. Judging by the reviews, the sea in this part of the Turkish coast is clean and warm. On the beach, sunbeds and umbrellas are issued for payment. Towels are not provided at the hotel. The beach has toilets, showers and bars.

Services for children

Judging by the reviews, Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * is great for relaxing with your family. Children on site have something to do. A mini club and a playground are equipped for them. Upon request, parents bring a cot to the room, and in the main restaurant you can freely get a high chair for easy feeding. For children, a so-called "paddling pool".



Additional Information

Since the Sayanora Park Hotel is 3-star, there is a fairly modest range of entertainment on its territory. The animation is presented very sluggishly, there is only table tennis. But on the beach you can get such activities as diving, riding a banana, boat excursions, etc. Those who wish can go to the sauna and hammam for free, get a massage course for a fee. The main entertainment on the territory of the hotel is the swimming pool around which there are sun loungers and umbrellas.

Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * (Side): reviews

I must say that most Russians liked this hotel. It has a convenient location in order to explore local attractions on its own, to conduct a good shopping without spending a taxi, and, of course, it is located near the beach. Judging by the reviews, the hotel grounds are magnificent - pretty cottages, lots of greenery, well-groomed paths everywhere. It seemed to some Russians that the rooms were not very spacious. However, no one has any complaints about their service: the staff works harmoniously, everything is always done on time, they always bring clean towels. And the occupancy of the rooms is good, there is everything you need for a normal stay.



The pool, too, according to our compatriots, is not bad: it is always clean, however, some believe that the number of beds is insufficient. Regarding nutrition, the vast majority of Russians left positive reviews: tasty and varied. True, some believe that the waiters are very slow, but this is apparently due to the workload of the restaurant. As for entertainment, then in this matter Sayanora Park Hotel 3 * is somewhat behind.

Summing up the reviews, we can say that this hotel is quite suitable for a calm and measured vacation with the family. Many Russians believe that you can return here.
