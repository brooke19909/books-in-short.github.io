Smartphone Rating 2013

Small phones with buttons go farther into the past: today smartphones shine everywhere with their touch screens . However, it is not difficult to understand their owners: given the need to use the Internet, phones are becoming almost indispensable.

Smartphone Evaluation Criteria

To rank smartphones,

you must select the criteria by which the devices will be evaluated. Most people, choosing a phone, look primarily at the appearance: here it is necessary to take into account not only the design, but also the materials used - how durable they are and keep their appearance. Next, the display is evaluated: capacitive or resistive, its size and resolution, type of matrix. The question that the buyer has after examining the display is about the sound characteristics of the device - how loud are the speakers, is the sound clear, how does the music in the headphones sound? Of course, what kind of smartphone is deprived of the camera - therefore, the quality of the received photos and videos is of interest. The following generalized criterion is the functionality of the phone as a whole: the interface, useful gadgets, and ease of use. And, of course, the battery power is of interest. Also important is the operating system on which the smartphone is running.

Comparing smartphones

By compiling a list of evaluation criteria,

You can create a rating of smartphones.
