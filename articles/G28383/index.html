How to make ripped jeans at home?

Fashion is constantly changing, so everyone is trying to adapt to it. And today it can be noted that ripped jeans again became popular among fashionistas. Young people buy them in stores, spending a lot of money on it. And it is known that all this is done simply. How to make ripped jeans? If you have everything at hand and follow the instructions, there are no problems.

What do you need?

Torn jeans are easy to make, but you need to pay attention to some nuances:

  • Material. To manage to create a classically fashionable thing, you need to take fabric of medium density. Do not make a choice in the direction of the stretch material, because it will not work out to create a fringe around the holes. Arrows may also appear. They spoil the whole look and do not give the desired effect.
  • Which cut? To create unique ripped jeans, the latter must be of a certain cut. And it will depend on the figure of the fashionista. Not too tight, but better than a little bigger than the available size. But too wide models will simply hide the holes, and the whole view will deteriorate.
  • Style direction. It is believed that it is not so important which model a girl or boy prefers. If everything is done correctly, it will be beautiful. Landing and color scheme also go by the wayside. To make the process easy, it is better to give preference to natural fabrics.

What are the cuts?

Here you need to take the size of the legs as a basis. If these are large hips, then gaps are made in the vertical direction, bypassing problem areas. To visually look beautiful, you should not do too much in the lower part of the slots and close them to each other. If everything is done randomly, then the legs visually become longer.

If there are no problem areas, cuts can appear in any part of the jeans. You can choose a variety of colors, even one that will stand out from the crowd and emphasize individuality. This is what every person strives for, creating such clothes for himself.

How to make ripped jeans?

It is clear that, in general, this is not difficult. It will be necessary to create parallel and perpendicular cuts on things. So, how to make ripped jeans from jeans? The first question that every person faces is the number of slots. It is believed that there is no basic rule, but the total part of the gaps should not exceed half of the leg.

Such pants will look sloppy. This is what every mod seeks. To achieve the desired appearance, you will have to make cuts different.

You need to know some simple tips and rules. For the process to be successful, you must have at hand:

  • Any dense surface. But it is better to use a board. This is necessary so that it is convenient to work on jeans. The size of the board must be at least half a meter.
  • Knife. In this situation, a suitable clerical option.
  • Small plywood.
  • Scissors.
  • A needle to make torn places more unique. It should not be too thick and thin (better - more authentic).
  • Sandpaper.
  • Pencil, chalk or soap - any that will be visible on the selected fabric.

Preparation process

How to make ripped jeans at home? Step-by-step instructions will be presented below. But remember: for the whole procedure to have the desired result, you will have to prepare for it. The first is to choose jeans and try on them. This is required in order to understand how to make ripped jeans. In the process, places where gaps appear will be outlined. Do not forget that the size must be taken into account immediately.

Outlines are made, namely the lines of the desired length and width. For this, a pencil, soap or chalk is taken. In the process, jeans are put on, removed and all visibility is evaluated in order to understand in time how everything will look. Execution technique

How to make ripped jeans? Walkthrough - below. In order to avoid oversights, you need to do everything in stages.

  1. First you need to arrange the jeans so that they do not appear wrinkles. After you need a small plywood. She is pushed under the leg, which will be processed. This is a prerequisite so as not to damage the back of the trousers.
  2. Then you will need to use a large board. Jeans are located on it in full length, after which the legs are tucked so that they are sandwiched between two pieces of wood.
  3. Next, it is necessary to precisely note the beginning and end of future gaps. No need to make more than nine slots on one pair of legs. There is also one more point: do not make holes too close to each other. It will be interesting to look if they turn out at the same distance. But this condition is not required.
  4. Once all the sketches are completed, you can proceed to the main actions - cutting. To do this, take a knife. But he will not cut specifically along the line. We'll have to do everything diligently, and then a small amount of thread is pulled. Remember that after washing the design will change a little.
  5. Since the entire denim is made of a pair of threads - dark and light, then you need to pull out the first. It is convenient to get them out of the fabric with the help of tongs. You can complement and create irresistibility by making incisions in the back pockets. And the emery sheet will help to make tears more disheveled and give the effect of wear.

In addition, by turning on fantasy, you can decorate each hole with rhinestones or something similar. It turns out that creating ripped jeans at home is more profitable, since everything turns out individually and inimitably.

What should not be forgotten?

How to make jeans torn at home, it is already clear to us. But there are some more recommendations:

  • Do not forget that now under such trouser legs visible legs. So, depilation is required without fail.
  • If you need to transform the wide leg of jeans, then you need to make cuts at a great distance from each other. So they look prettier.
  • If the girl has slim and long legs, then you can place a lot of holes. From this pants will only be prettier.
  • Some ladies add a twist - they spray a little chlorine-containing substance onto the thing, and then they wash it in a machine. As a result, unique bright spots appear. It looks very unusual.
  • How to make ripped jeans? The main thing is not to overdo it so that one big hole does not form.
  • Another way to stand out is to sew a beautiful guipure under each hole. There will be no difficulty, because the guipure is neatly fixed on the other side. If there is no experience in using a needle and a seamstress talent, then you can simply use adhesive tape and fix it.
  • If there is imagination, then on the holes you can place rhinestones, beads, etc. Both pockets and the bottom of the legs can fall into the decoration zone.
  • If something went wrong, do not throw away and destroy the jeans. With the wrong approach, you can save the top and make shorts. But for this you need to start making slots from the bottom up.

What can you wear such things with?

For a unique look, you can wear a bright red blouse and pumps in the same color with ripped jeans. Let the shoes be patent. You can add a handbag in color or, conversely, contrary to the general view.

If such pants are used for everyday wear, then a top or light-colored T-shirt and ballet flats (sneakers can be added) are added to them. Sneakers are good in this combination, and on top is a gray cardigan.

Even in the warmer months, jeans will be appropriate. To do this, create a torn shirt and put on sandals. But do not get too naked, otherwise the image will get a touch of vulgarity. It is easy to make ripped jeans at home, but in the process, do not forget about fitting. This will minimize damage to things. It will work better with a dense fabric and complement everything well with something unusual - sequins, rhinestones, beads, etc. Such a thing will definitely become unique.
