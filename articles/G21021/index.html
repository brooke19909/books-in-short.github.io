How a satin ribbon flower is born

One of the most famous materials for decoration are satin ribbons. It was with this fabric that royal outfits and costumes of ordinary people were once equipped, at weddings they were woven into horses' manes. Nowadays, needlewomen also willingly use ribbons for the manufacture of various jewelry. A flower from a satin ribbon is very popular today . This accessory is easily made at home, and then serves as an excellent complement to any look.



Handmade technique has been enjoying considerable popularity for many years. That is why the answer to the question of how to make a flower from a satin ribbon will be simple - making it only requires imagination and basic material.

A simple but at the same time original accessory can be made by folding the tape with an envelope. To do this, you must first take the white material and bend at a right angle in the middle so that the upper edge is perpendicular to the lower edge. The end of the tape, lying horizontally, we lower down, while stretching it under the main part of the product, and put it near the second end. At this stage, our satin ribbon flower has an angular structure. Holding this corner lightly with your finger, turn the left edge of the material to the right, and you get an envelope. Sew it along the edge, leaving the thread with the needle in the fabric. Slices slightly tinted so as not to bloom. Then we tighten the thread so that the ribbon gathered in a bud. The resulting petals are straightened. From the pink ribbon we make the exact same bud, put it on white and fasten it in the center. In the middle we sew a bead or beads.



Green satin leaves are made. To do this, we roll the tape with a cone, cut off the excess fabric, we scorch the cut. Sew together or connect the edges of the tape with glue. In the same way, we make two more leaves, put the glued edges under the flower and attach to it. The result was a miniature satin ribbon flower.

Another variety of such crafts are artificial carnations. To make the bud voluminous, take a wide ribbon a little more than a meter long. Iron it and singe the ends. We lay out stitch stitches along one edge of the material. We collect it on a string of medium frequency, while forming folds, and fix the result. We wrap the tape inside from one edge and continue to turn it into a roll. Each turn needs to be fixed with a thread along the edge where the stitches were laid. DIY satin ribbon flower is ready! In the same way, we make a small clove from a narrow strip, only the thread is tightened and the assembly is thicker.



Having mastered the easy ways of making flowers from various materials, it will be possible to proceed to more complex techniques. The scope of the colors from the satin is so great that for those who have the desire to create jewelry with their own hands, there is great scope for choice. You can invent a single exclusive flower from a satin ribbon, or you can make up for whole floral arrangements. Accessories from improvised materials can be very diverse in terms of color and style.
