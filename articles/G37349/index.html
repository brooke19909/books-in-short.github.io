Smartphone Alcatel One Touch Pop C2 4032D: review, specifications, reviews

Affordable smartphone for all occasions - Alcatel One Touch Pop C2. Its technical and software components allow you to solve most of the most common problems, and the price makes it one of the most affordable solutions in this segment of devices.





Gadget Niche

The price of 2240 rubles clearly and unequivocally relates Alcatel One Touch Pop C2 to entry-level solutions. This is also indicated by the used model in it of the central processor. This is one of the most affordable smartphones of this manufacturer. It implements support for basic functions. For the rest, this is a typical gadget of the entry-level solutions segment.

Equipment

Smartphone Alcatel One Touch Pop C2 4032D, as noted earlier - a budget solution. Hence, the equipment is very modest. In addition to the mobile device itself and the battery, it includes the following:

  • Charger.
  • Typical interface cord.
  • Smart phone user guide and warranty card.

A stereo headset, a protective film and a case must be purchased separately for the owner of this smartphone.





Design

The Alcatel One Touch Pop C2 4032D is a typical all-in-one smartphone with touch input capabilities. On the front panel it displays a 4-inch screen. Under it there are the usual three control buttons. Well, on top is a conversational speaker and an LED event indicator. On the right edge of the device, volume controls are displayed, and on top is an audio port and a lock button. At the bottom, only the MicroUSB port is displayed. A loud speaker and its only camera are displayed behind.

Screen

Camera

The Alcatel One Touch Pop C2 is equipped with just one main camera. Its characteristics are really not impressive. The sensor element underlying it can boast of a 3 megapixel matrix. In reality, this value is much less. Well, the previously mentioned 3 megapixels are obtained by software using interpolation technology. The maximum resolution of digital photos obtained can be 2102 x 1500. There is no additional LED backlight in this gadget. The situation is similar with the autofocus and image stabilization system. As a result, only at a normal level of illumination can normal photos still be obtained. With video, the situation is even worse. The resolution of the recorded clips is 640 x 480. Accordingly, on modern screens with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 or more, it is simply impossible to watch such a video. There is a camera in this device, but its capabilities are really modest. But you certainly do not have to expect more from a budget-level smartphone.





And what is inside of us?

Alcatel One Touch Pop C2 is built on the basis of a very modest today central processing unit - MT6572. This is a development of the Chinese company MediaTech. It consists of 2 computing modules, which are based on the Cortex A7 architecture, which, if necessary, can accelerate to 1.3 GHz. The manufacturing technology of this semiconductor device is based on a 28 nm process. It should be noted right away that this chip only supports 32-bit computing. Therefore, the latest 64-bit applications will not be launched on it. Another problem point for him is the most demanding applications of the latest generation. The computing capabilities of this chip are not enough to run them and, as a result, they will not run on it. The capabilities of the central computing device are harmoniously complemented by the Mali-400MP2 graphics accelerator. Its performance level corresponds to the CPU. Its main task is to free the processor from processing graphic information and it copes with these very well. The amount of RAM in this device is 512 MB. At the same time, the operating system occupies about 300 MB. Therefore, the user can count on 200 MB at best. The capacity of the integrated drive in this case is equal to a "modest" 4 GB. At the same time, 2 GB is initially occupied by system software. There is a slot for an external flash drive, the capacity of which can be equal to 32 GB and without it it will be quite difficult to fully work on this gadget.

Autonomy

The device is equipped with a removable 1300 mAh battery. Given the diagonal of the gadget 4 inches, this is enough for 2 days of using a "smart" phone. It will certainly not be possible to achieve more on it. Software

The software foundation for this gadget is Android. At the moment, version 4.2 is installed on it. This is enough to run most major applications. But the owner of this smartphone must be prepared for the fact that in the foreseeable future there may be problems with the launch of the latest software due to software incompatibility.





Gadget Reviews

The subject of our review has many obvious flaws - Alcatel One Touch Pop C2. Reviews highlight such of them:

  • Weak central processor. But for now, this is enough to solve most problems.
  • Low amount of memory. Therefore, the gadget owner immediately needs to purchase an additional flash drive and without fail to install additional software to optimize the operation of the device. However, you can find another way out - to remove half of the unnecessary pre-installed "garbage", and the memory will be significantly added.
  • A modest camera, which, however, can function normally with a good level of illumination.
  • The display is not the best quality.
  • Weak autonomy of the device.

But the advantages of this device, those who have already purchased and use the gadget, noted the following:

  • low price;
  • high-quality assembly;
  • excellent work on the Internet;
  • all applications and games work without hangs;
  • excellent communication quality, sound;
  • sensitive sensor.




Summary

If you need an urgently available and reasonably functional economy-class device, then the Alcatel One Touch Pop C2 can be a great solution. In other cases, you can wait and finish on the best mobile device. In fact, this smartphone has a significant advantage - low price and the resulting accessibility. According to the reviews of its owners, this is really a budget purchase that allows you to successfully solve most problems.
