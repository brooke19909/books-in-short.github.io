Villa Maria Revas 5 * (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach): tourist reviews

It's no secret that Bulgaria has long been one of the favorite vacation spots of our compatriots. Sunny Beach is the largest resort in the east of the country, it is known for its beautiful ten-kilometer beach. In our article we want to talk about one of the hotels of this wonderful place called Maria Revas 5 *.

Location

The hotel complex is located in the south-west of Sunny Beach (highway Varna - Nessebar - Burgas). Getting to Villa Maria Revas 5 * is not at all difficult. The road from Burgas International Airport will take no more than twenty minutes. Hotel address: Sunny Beach, Bulgaria, Sunny Beach 8240.

The indisputable advantage of Maria Revas 5 * is its convenient location. From the hotel to the sea - only one hundred and fifty meters, which is important for a seaside resort. In the immediate vicinity (fifty meters) is a bus stop, a currency exchange. The distance to the center is no more than two kilometers.

Number of rooms

Villa Maria Revas 5 * is a boutique hotel with unique architecture and unusual interior. The complex operates year-round, and therefore it is glad to guests at any time. The hotel is connected to central heating, so even in autumn and winter, vacationers will feel comfortable within its walls.



The highlight of the complex is exotic furniture made from rattan, a jacuzzi and large terraces with sea views.

The hotel has a total of twenty-seven rooms in the following categories:

  1. Studio (there are five rooms of this level). The apartments are equipped with air conditioning, telephone, minibar, TV, bathroom (bath or shower) and hairdryer. These rooms are designed for two adults with a child (or three adults).
  2. One bedroom apartments are designed for two adults with two children (or three adults). The room has air conditioning, minibar, terrace, TV, telephone, bathroom, hairdryer.
  3. Two-bedroom apartments are designed for three adults and two children (or four adult vacationers). Room has air conditioning, TV, terrace, minibar, hairdryer. A feature of such apartments is the presence of a kitchenette with all necessary equipment. In addition, the room has two bedrooms, each of which is equipped with its own bathroom. The living room is made in a modern style and furnished with new furniture made of natural materials.
  4. The penthouse apartments have a balcony, air conditioning and all kitchen utensils. Room size is fifty square meters.

Hotel facilities

Maria Revas 5 * (Sunny Beach) has its own free parking, which can be used by all vacationers. The complex has a business center with a conference room with a capacity of fifty people with all the necessary equipment (flipchart, multimedia system, audio system, projector, Wi-Fi, fax, photocopy). The room is suitable for exhibitions, seminars, presentations and business meetings. The lounge has a bar where coffee is served during events.



Rooms are cleaned and changed several times a week.

As for adult and children's animation, it is not there. Such a service is not provided at the hotel. But for young guests there is a games room and a children's pool.

Vacationers can use the fitness room and play billiards. For a fee, the hotel can arrange shuttle service, laundry and dry cleaning services.

Nutrition

By purchasing a vacation tour in Maria Revas 5 * (Sunny Beach), you can choose the option with a continental breakfast. The hotel has a restaurant with a capacity of fifty people with a VIP-hall (20 people). The chef offers visitors specialties of European cuisine.

In addition, vacationers can use the services of the lobby bar, where you can sit with friends and have a cup of coffee. The staff offers a good selection of soft drinks, alcoholic drinks and cocktails.



Guests can always enjoy refreshing drinks at the poolside bar.

Hotel pool

Hotel Maria Revas 5 * (Sunny Beach) has a deep pool for adults with an equipped sun terrace (umbrellas and sunbeds). Guests can also enjoy the spa and massage services.

Beach

Sunny Beach resort is a place where thousands of vacationers flock annually for the sake of beautiful sandy beaches and the sea. Hotel Maria Revas 5 * in this regard has several advantages that guests can take advantage of. The main advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the sea, it is located on the first line, surrounded by a park area. For those tourists who do not like pebble beaches, Sunny Beach will be a real discovery. Kilometers of pure fine sand along the sea will win the hearts of all tourists.

Things to Do in Sunny Beach

Sunny Beach is famous for its sand dunes. A ten-kilometer beach with a width of about 30-40 meters will impress even the most sophisticated tourists. The golden strip of fine sand along the blue strip of the sea is the pride of the local resort. Vacationers on the coast can not just bask in the sun, but also engage in water sports: windsurfing, water skiing. In addition, you can ride a yacht, a "banana", a water parachute, hang glider. Fans of land sports will be pleased with numerous tennis courts, volleyball and basketball courts, equestrian base of thoroughbred horses, water park.

Night life

As for nightlife, here it is in full swing. More than 250 restaurants in Sunny Beach are happy to receive guests every night. In addition, vacationers will be interested to visit the invention of Bulgarian tourism - restaurants and attractions "Khan's tent", "Caligula". Sightseeing Tours

Hotel Maria Revas 5 * has its own branch of the tour desk. The local guide will be able to recommend interesting sightseeing trips, because Bulgaria is not only sand, sea and sun ... Here there are sights and interesting places that tourists should definitely see.



An obligatory point in the excursion program should be the ancient town of Nessebar, which is full of ancient buildings and cobbled streets. It is interesting for its architecture. There are a lot of great places. However, tourists chose the town not only because of its attractions, they fell in love with numerous local shops and cafes, which reigned in a special atmosphere. You can come to Nessebar again and again.

In addition, lovers of water activities will certainly enjoy the water park. If you want a surge of emotions and adrenaline - you definitely need to visit this attraction. For families with children, a trip to the amusement park and karting can be an interesting entertainment.

There are places that, while relaxing in Bulgaria, you should definitely visit. The pearl of the country is the Rila Monastery. It is able to surprise even sophisticated tourists. Of interest is primarily its beautiful architecture.

Continuing the conversation about local beauties, it is worth recalling the town of Shumen. It will delight guests simply with a huge number of historical sights and the measured, calm life of a small city.



Almost all the sights of Bulgaria can be reached independently by bus. How to choose the right route, a guide will tell you in the hotel Villa Maria Revas 5 * (Bulgaria). In addition, the resort has a tourist office, which will prompt you the necessary information.

Maria Revas 5 * (Bulgaria): reviews

To form an objective opinion about the hotel, I would like to discuss the reviews of tourists who visited it. It should be emphasized that Maria Revas 5 * Sunny Beach is a wonderful family hotel. Since the villa is a very quiet and peaceful place, it is possible that lovers of noisy parties may not like it very much. The hotel has practically no animation, but for many visitors this moment is absolutely not important, because you can always visit one of the local famous discos if you wish.

An advantage of the Maria Revas 5 * Sunny Beach complex is the availability of rooms with kitchenettes equipped with all necessary utensils. Such apartments can be a great option for those families who do not want to feed their children for some reason in a public place. Products can be purchased at local supermarkets (for example, "Janet"). The ability to cook food for many is the main argument in favor of the hotel.



Does the hotel restaurant Maria Revas 5 *? Reviews of tourists regarding food are a bit controversial. Not everyone is satisfied with the local cuisine, considering that breakfast, for example, is very scarce and monotonous. Also, tourists note high prices in a local restaurant. Food problems can be avoided by living in apartments with a kitchen. In addition, there are a lot of bars and restaurants around, the services of which can be used, because they serve good dishes at reasonable prices.

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with good, quality furniture, new linens, no stains. Apartments are cleaned a couple of times a week, but garbage is thrown away every day. Beach towels and linen are replaced every other day. Unfortunately, not all rooms have a good view, some of them overlook the backyard.

Reviews of tourists about the coast

The pool at the hotel is very good and clean, people always relax near it, but there is no crowding, everyone has enough space.

The beach deserves special attention. It is located next to the Maria Revas 5 * hotel (Bulgaria), at a slow pace the road to it will not be more than five to seven minutes. The sea coast is clean. Beautiful fine sand and the hot sun just attract sunbathing on the beach. The warm, clean sea in the region of Sunny Beach is rather shallow. Entry into the water (about one hundred meters) is gentle, which is very convenient for kids. There is plenty of room for their swimming. Therefore, as a children's resort, it is difficult to find a better place than Sunny Beach.

The only drawback may seem to be the fact that you need to pay for an umbrella and sunbeds on the beach. Many vacationers prefer to use their umbrellas, taking them with them to the beach (they are sold in local stores). But sun loungers, in principle, are not very necessary, because lounging on a fine hot sand is a pleasure.



Relaxing in Bulgaria, you definitely need to see local attractions, many of which are within walking distance of the Villa Maria Revas 5 * hotel. Reviews of tourists about excursions and walks to local interesting places are full of enthusiasm and experience. I would like to note that the complex is located not in the center of the resort, but at the same time there is everything necessary for relaxation nearby. Tourists love to go for a walk in Nessebar, since it is very close. The old town is full of interesting places, a special atmosphere reigns on its streets, and local cafes attract guests to their tables.

The youngest guests of the complex will be interested in visiting the water park and amusement park.

Holidays in Bulgaria for our compatriots are good because they are not associated with language barriers. There is absolutely no need for knowledge of the English language. Oddly enough, but everyone understands each other, a lot of people who know at least a little Russian. Perhaps, for this reason, vacationers feel comfortable.

Instead of an afterword

Hotel Maria Revas 5 * can be recommended as a good place for a family holiday on the sea. Perhaps spoiled tourists will not find the chic inherent in some five-star complexes here, but here you can have a great time surrounded by sun, sea, sand and local attractions.
