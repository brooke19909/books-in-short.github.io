Types of payments

The Russian Pension Fund funds are used to pay pensions that serve as compensation for salaries to people who cannot work. In our country, there are several options for such payments.

The role of pension reform

Today in Russia there is a funded type pension system. With its help, it will be possible to regulate the accumulated money, which will be issued to people in old age. The amount of payments is determined by the time the person worked, whether the employer transferred payments. Thanks to this, the expense of the organization is reduced.

Funds transferred by the employer do not currently receive pension benefits. Money accumulates, and when a pensioner does not have the opportunity to work, he will be paid a allowance in the prescribed amount.

The storage system is more convenient than the distribution system. The reason for this is that pension funds are recognized as property of a person, not the state. This principle increases system reliability. Thanks to the funded principle, the economy is normal, which is an important task for the FIU.
