Beautiful swans made of cones and plasticine

Often during walks in kindergarten, children collect leaves, twigs, acorns, cones. Then all the material can be used to make beautiful crafts.

Swans

For example, beautiful swans are made from cones and plasticine. Grace these birds will give the neck and beaks. Together with children, you first need to fashion a head with a beak and a neck. But before you begin to work, you need to find a beautiful picture with the image of this proud and beautiful bird. The child will distribute plasticine, and this will be the main material according to the colors that he sees in the prepared image. The swan's beak is molded from red plasticine, and the neck and head will be white. Now it remains to attach the neck with the head to the found cone, make eyes from plasticine in black.

From a pine cone





Quite different are the swans from cones and plasticine, if you use a pine cone as the body of a bird. Kids are happy to collect them and develop their imagination when completing the tasks of their educators. The bump should be slightly open so that its scales look like the swan's expanding wings. Useful for crafts cotton and plasticine. The body of the bird still needs to be completed, one cone is not enough. Between the scales there is an empty space, it needs to be filled with small lumps of cotton wool. Take white plasticine and form a curved sausage from it for the neck and head of a swan. Looking at the photo of the bird, you can see that the white swans at the base of their beak have a black frame around it. Therefore, first, a small rhombus is molded from black plasticine, which is attached to the place on the head where the bird's beak is supposed to be made.

Next stage





Then you need to take a bright red material for modeling and make a beak. The neck and body are joined together. Then you need to flatten a small piece of plasticine to the color of water. A swan from a cone sits on it, a photo of which is presented in the article. This will give the bird stability. On the resulting lake, you can "plant" reeds made of ordinary toothpicks, on which brown plasticine was screwed on top.

Thus, a wonderful piece of cones came out. The swan will adequately represent the skill of the child at any exhibition in kindergarten.

Do not limit the imagination of the child. Invite him to make other figures of birds and animals from materials already familiar. Mom can advise you to turn to famous fairy tales and cartoons. The child will have new do-it-yourself friends toys.

Each child can do such crafts; the simplest materials can be used. This creative work will help the baby to develop imagination, will contribute to the ability to create simple and complex compositions, develop mental abilities, since in the manufacture of a swan from cones and plasticine, work with molding material will be needed, which helps to improve the fine motor skills of fingers. On their pads are important receptors that transmit signals to the brain.
