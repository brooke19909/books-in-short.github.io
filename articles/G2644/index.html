Going to Adler. Hotels with a pool near the sea in Adler: overview, list, description and reviews of tourists

Adler is located on the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory at the foot of the Caucasus. Every year it receives thousands of tourists. Lovers of exotic, gentle sun, surf come to Adler. Hotels with a pool near the sea are very popular among vacationers, but to book a room, you need to take care of this in advance. Many hotels except the pool offer a wide range of services. Let's consider in more detail what the coast is famous for and where it is better to stay in Adler.





Rest in Adler

Adler is one of the areas of Greater Sochi. It has always been popular with tourists, but after the Olympics here, love and interest in this city have grown several times. Hotels, hotels, pensions and resorts Adler welcome their guests and wait for them all year round. This region of Sochi is flat, the longest in length, which facilitates movement along it. The center where the beach is located is not separated from the city by rail, which is typical for the rest of Sochi. The seashore is covered with large and small pebbles. The beach is equipped with sunbeds, aeraria zone is located separately. There are many water attractions for vacationers: riding a scooter, a catamaran, a banana, a turntable, a cheesecake, paragliding, boat trips and much more. Those who come to rest are simply delighted and respond with warmth about the days spent in Adler.

A good vacation here is not only in high season. On velvet days, and this is September-October, the sea warms up to maximum temperature, the sun is gentle, not hot. For those who like to relax without fuss, this is the most golden time.

Everyone who came to Adler must visit a trout farm. It is very proud of its own breed of trout.

The richest in the number of hotels, mini-hotels, campsites, recreation facilities on the coast is the city of Adler. Hotels with a pool near the sea are most popular. They are located on the first or second line, the beach can be reached in a few minutes. There are many cozy restaurants, bars, cafes nearby. There are excursion bureaus everywhere, so getting out on an excursion in any direction is not difficult.





Weather

Adler is a one-stop resort. You can relax here all year round. The swimming season begins in May and lasts until October, the average temperature is +25 degrees. Water mainly holds a temperature of +22 degrees. Summer is hot, winter is warm. Not everyone will dare to swim in the sea at +9, but many can sunbathe. Winter is a little cooler than in Central Sochi, the humidity is slightly higher than in Khost. Adler belongs to the northern subtropics, and its climate can be compared with the mountain resorts of France, Italy, Switzerland. The healing climate and mountain air attract many vacationers to these places.

sights

If you arrived to rest in Adler, hotels with a pool near the sea should not be your only place where you will spend all your time. Be sure to go sightseeing. Believe the reviews of tourists who have been here - in this area there is something to look at. Since 1999, the main highlight of the resort is the Utrish Dolphinarium. The Akhshtyrskaya cave is located 15 km from the city , where archaeologists discovered the remains of ancient settlements. If you go to the New Athos cave, you can get acquainted with the amazingly beautiful stalactites and stalagmites, see the underground lake. Mount Akhun in Krasnaya Polyana and Lake Ritsa in Abkhazia are simply a must. What else is worth seeing? Monkey nursery, trout farming, waterfalls on the Agura River, a monastery in New Athos.

"Azimuth"





For those who choose hotels with a swimming pool in Adler, one of the best options would be the Azimut Hotel. It is located just a few minutes from the Olympic Park. This hotel deservedly received a three-star service class. It is perfect not only for relaxation, but also for productive work.

Azimut has a huge number of rooms. Vacationers are provided with a choice of any of the 288 rooms. Windows overlook the mountains, the sea or the fragrant garden. The hotel provides an opportunity to check in for people with disabilities.
