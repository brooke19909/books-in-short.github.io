History of Adygea

Adygea is a small republic that is part of the Russian Federation. She has her own history of development, her own traditions. The population of Adygea - Circassians or Circassians, who are proud of their history, were once able to seize power in ancient Egypt, being there slaves. It was they who founded the Circassian dynasty, which ruled for a long period Syria and Egypt. It was the Adyghe princess who married Ivan the Terrible. Then the territory where this nationality lived was annexed to Russia.

The modern capital of the Republic of Adygea is the city of Maykop, founded as a strategic object by General Kozlovsky in the conquest of the North Caucasus, which became a Russian military fortification in 1857. Only in 1870 the city turned into a county. The open rich oil fields had a great influence on the development of the economy of the city and the whole of Adygea. Adygea is a territory in the foothills of the Caucasus, rich in minerals, with traditional flora and fauna corresponding to this region, with its own climatic features.
