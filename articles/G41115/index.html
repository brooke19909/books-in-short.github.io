Addresses of Avangard Bank in Arkhangelsk

Branches of Avangard Bank in Arkhangelsk are located on the territory of shopping centers. Most of them serve customers on weekends. The city has a large number of round-the-clock ATMs.

The central office of Avangard Bank in Arkhangelsk is located at ul. Vyucheyskogo, 25. The express office is open on weekdays from 09:30 to 17:30. A round-the-clock customer support service works, which can be contacted by phone published on the bank‚Äôs official website.

Service for individuals

Bank Avangard address in Arkhangelsk: st. Nagornaya, house 1, on the territory of the Hippo shopping center. It is important to note that the express office is open from Tuesday to Saturday from 11:00 to 19:45, a break from 15:00 to 15:40. The office is closed on Monday.

Express office of Avangard Bank in Arkhangelsk at ul. Timme, d. 4/3 is located on the territory of the Polyus shopping center. The bank branch is open daily from 9:30 to 21:00.

Corporate customer service

A branch of Avangard Bank in Arkhangelsk at the address: Leningradsky Prospekt, building 255 is located on the territory of the shopping center. Service for individuals every weekday, except Mondays, from 11:00 to 19:40. The break is from 15:00 to 15:40.

The customer support phone numbers indicated on the official website are valid for all bank branches in the city of Arkhangelsk.

ATMs "Vanguard"

For operations with bank cards, it is not always necessary to contact a bank branch. A round-the-clock ATM of Avangard Bank is located at 33 Nikolsky Avenue, located on the territory of the Admiralteysky shopping center. In the same mode, an ATM is installed at the following address: Raidovaya street, 14.

The ATM installed in the terminal of the city airport is also working around the clock. It is possible to deposit cash into bank card accounts, select banknotes, transfers from account to account, from card to card and many other services.

Every day from 7:00 to 23:00 an ATM operates on the territory of the Petromost shopping center at Leningradsky Prospekt, 255. Cash deposit / withdrawal and other banking services are available here.
