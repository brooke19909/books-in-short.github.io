"Berendeev Forest": a description of the recreation center

Most people prefer to relax outside the city. This allows you to escape from the hustle and bustle, as well as enjoy the fresh air and the beauty of nature. Country Club "Berendeev Forest" offers its guests excellent conditions for living. It is located on the northern shore of Lake Akkul. People come from different cities to relax at the base, as the place is famous for its beauty and excellent service.

General information

The territory of the club resembles a real Russian village and has its own unique flavor. Here you can combine outdoor recreation with comfort. In the country club "Berendeyev Les" cottages of various types are offered for living. The premium-class house involves a bath, a fireplace room, aroma rooms with hayloft. In the cottage you can see many elements of wood that are striking in their beauty. Guests can have a barbecue in the special area. There is a separate gazebo for a meal. A stay here will cost from 23,000 rubles a day.

You can also stay at the Green Tower. It is designed for 8 people. It has all the necessary conditions for living, as well as an open veranda. It costs from 8,000 rubles per day. On the territory of the recreation center "Berendeev Forest" there are cottages under the names "Summer Hut" and "Sunny House". Each of them is designed for two persons. The price per day starts from 1500 rubles. All necessary furniture and appliances are also prepared for guests. All cottages except the Summer Hut have kitchens with kitchenware. If necessary, in an extra bed you can pay 300 rubles and it will be provided.

Where is

A popular recreation center is located in a picturesque place. The nearest train station is Tatysh. You can also get here by car. The country club is located 13 kilometers from Kyshtym, 80 kilometers from Chelyabinsk and 150 kilometers from Yekaterinburg.

Exact address: Berendeev Forest recreation center, Akakul, building 26-B.

Additional features

It offers not only beautiful nature, but also many interesting activities. Regular visitors know that the country club has great saunas. In the "Palace of Tsar Berendey" waiting for a real Russian bath.

For lovers of SPA-procedures there is a "Merchant Bath". It involves not only a steam room, but also aromatherapy, and a pool with a font. Also, the Berendeev Forest Club offers the All Inclusive complex. For guests prepared: steam room, pool, relaxation room, tea, brooms and much more.

In the winter season, visitors can ride a skating rink or from an ice slide, take walks in the winter forest. Fishing is very popular. In the warm season, guests are actively collecting various berries and mushrooms. Available to take equipment to ride a bike or catamaran. Base "Berendeev Forest" organizes tours and excursions that allow you to see new places. Visitors are taken to the mountains, to the Sugokam cave, an ecopark. Young guests will not have to be sad on vacation, as a special children's town with entertainment is designed for them. The beach is not far from the base. It is only a hundred meters away. Most of the guests remained quite their rest, so they recommend this place.
