Hotel Continental Calella 2 * (Spain / Costa del Maresme): reviews, tours, photos

Costa del Meresme is one of the most inexpensive resorts in Spain. The vast majority of hotels here offer low prices for accommodation. Tours to the small hotel Continental Calella 2 * ( Calella ) are popular with travelers from Russia and the CIS countries. What attracts them to this hotel?

General information and location

The hotel opened in 1969. It is located at the crossroads of two small streets, which often pass cars. The Continental Calella 2 * hotel does not have its own territory. The beach is approximately 200 meters away. You can walk to the coast from here in about 10 minutes. All beaches here are public, so tourists are forced to buy sunbeds and mattresses on their own. The complex is located 100 meters from the central part of the city. Almost all the attractions of this resort are within walking distance. Barcelona Airport is approximately 75 km away. It takes tourists about an hour to get to the hotel.





Complex Continental Calella 2 * (Spain, Calella) consists of one five-story building, which houses 125 standard rooms. As in the whole country, registration of guests starts here at noon. At the same time, tourists must leave their rooms at the end of the holiday or to extend their stay for another day. The hotel allows check-in with pets whose weight does not exceed 5 kilograms. Making a tour to this hotel, it is worth knowing that upon arrival, tourists must pay the service fee, which is charged at all resorts in Catalonia. The cost of a ticket to this hotel for a week will cost about 20,000 rubles per person.

Number of rooms

All rooms at this hotel are simple in size and small in size. They consist of a small living room and a bathroom, which is equipped with a shower. Some rooms have a miniature balcony with no furniture. The windows of the Continental Calella 2 * hotel overlook the city street. The upper floors offer partial sea views. All residential buildings were last renovated in 2004. The staff of the complex daily performs cleaning of all rooms, and also updates towels and bed linen once a week. The housing stock is represented by standard rooms, which are designed for 4 adults. They are available in double or single beds to choose from. A baby cradle can also be delivered to the room.





The apartments have basic equipment corresponding to this category of hotel. We list the basic equipment that guests can use in their rooms:

  • Tube TV. Pretty old model that broadcasts a small number of satellite TV channels.
  • Mechanical safe. Lockable with a key. Using it is not included in the price and is charged separately.
  • Telephone. For an additional fee, you can make calls to other cities and countries.

Infrastructure and entertainment

Hotel Continental Calella 2 * is a complex designed for tourists who prefer to spend their time outside its territory. Therefore, there is no wide variety of services or entertainment. However, unpretentious tourists will find here everything they need to relax. We list the main infrastructure facilities that are located on the territory of this hotel:

  • Outdoor pool, which is located on the roof of the hotel. It is filled with fresh water. It works only in fine weather. Nearby is a sun terrace with a small number of sunbeds and umbrellas.
  • 24 hour reception. An administrator works here who will try to quickly solve the problems that have arisen. Unfortunately, he speaks only English and Spanish. A guide will always help you find interesting excursions or book tickets.
  • Shared lounge area where tourists can relax and watch TV.
  • Billiard room.
  • A conference room that is perfect for organizing and conducting small seminars, negotiations and other business meetings.
  • A hall with non-hazardous slot machines.




Suggested meals

The cost of the tour at the Continental Calella 2 * hotel, as a rule, includes food. The hotel offers guests to pay for breakfast, half board or full board. When checking into the room, vacationers are given a special card on which the exact schedule of all meals is indicated. They take place in the form of a general buffet in the main restaurant of the hotel. The menu includes not only international, but also traditional Spanish dishes. It also offers refreshments and hot drinks, freshly baked pastries and sandwiches, light snacks, fruits and desserts.





In addition to the main restaurant, the hotel has two bars. One of them is located by the pool. It specializes in refreshing drinks, cocktails and ice cream. A second bar is located in the lobby. Here you can not only drink, but also have a snack with snacks or cookies.

Positive reviews

What reviews are left by guests who have already rested in the Continental Calella 2 * complex? Most often, tourists are satisfied with the quality of services, realizing that the class of the hotel does not provide for luxurious service. In their reviews, they talk about the following advantages:

  • Great dinners. The restaurant serves delicious chicken, baked fish and seafood salads. There are many fruits to choose from.
  • High-quality and thorough cleaning of rooms.
  • Clean pool and the area near it.
  • Great location. The beach is a 5-minute walk. The same way takes the road to the center.
  • The hotel has almost no youth, so in the evenings it is quite quiet.




Negative reviews

People who stayed at the Continental Calella 2 * hotel sometimes leave negative reviews. In their comments, they ask the administration to pay attention to the following shortcomings:

  • Monotonous breakfasts. Sometimes stale, slightly dried pastries came across.
  • The hotel staff is not friendly. The administrator speaks badly not only in Russian, but also in English.
  • There is no air conditioning in the room, so during the day you have to close the curtains tightly and sleep at night with the windows open.
  • Lack of free internet.

Thus, we can conclude that this hotel is well suited for a relaxing budget holiday. It is recommended that tourists stay with children, adults, or the elderly. Most often, travelers from Russia, Germany and Spain come here.
