Resorts of the Caucasus at all times attracted many people. The cleanest mountain rivers, mild climate, fresh air - all this helps to enjoy a wonderful vacation and improve health.



City Mineralnye Vody

This small, cozy city is located in the Stavropol Territory and is the administrative center of the KMV district. There is an international airport, a federal highway passes and there is a large railway junction.

Mineralnye Vody has no healing springs, all of them are located in neighboring cities, such as Kislovodsk, Pyatigorsk, Essentuki.

How to get there

Due to the presence of the airport and a major junction station, you can easily reach the city of Mineralnye Vody. Hotels cities have a fairly wide price range: from budget pensions to expensive hotels.

From Moscow and St. Petersburg it is most convenient to fly by plane. This will save a lot of time. You can also get to Mineralnye Vody by bus. The bus station accepts flights from many cities of Russia.

Despite the fact that the selection of cafes and restaurants of Mineralnye Vody is not particularly diverse, there are a number of places where you can eat tasty and inexpensively. Entertainment and Attractions

Architectural monuments, unique natural and historical sights - all this all year round attracts tourists to the city of Mineralnye Vody. Hotels conveniently located in the most attractive and comfortable parts of the city will make your stay complete and high-quality.

The railway station is a beautiful historical building of the city. Built in the fifties of the last century.

An interesting sculpture is an eagle with a snake in its paws. There is a legend that this majestic bird managed to get healing from a bite of a poisonous snake thanks to water from the healing springs of the local region.

A unique natural attraction is Mount "Snake", which is of volcanic origin. On its slopes there are many adits, monuments and holy sources.
