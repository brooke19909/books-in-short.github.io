Meals are organized on an all-inclusive basis. The buffet offers guests a choice of refreshments, locally produced alcohol (wine, gin, beer and vodka). Bars

The beach bar is open from 12-00 to 16-00. Here you can order light snacks, from 16-00 to 17-00 - time for tea, soft drinks and beer. Disco pool bar is paid separately.

Entertainment

In addition to several bars and a restaurant, the Aperion Beach Hotel offers tourists free animation services, aerobics and gymnastics, futsal, darts, tennis, volleyball and chess. For a fee, you can play billiards, enjoy water skiing, motorbike or canoeing, and enjoy massages and peeling.

Beach



Like many hotels in Side, Aperion Beach has a separate sand and pebble beach. From the hotel to the sea there is a footpath. The entry into the water is conveniently equipped; there are no large stones at the bottom. Beach umbrellas, sun loungers and mattresses are issued free of charge, but towels will need to be paid separately.

For children

The hotel has a children's pool and a playground for games. At the request of holidaymakers, a cot can be put in the room, while the restaurant will offer the use of high children's chairs. Babysitting services are available at an additional cost.

In case of need, the hotel has a first-aid post, a left-luggage office, a mini-market, a currency exchange office, a laundry, car rental and parking, a sauna, a Turkish bath, and photographers and hairdressers.
