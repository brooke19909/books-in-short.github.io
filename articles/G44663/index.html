Phuket Island View Hotel 3: description, services, rating and reviews

Phuket Island View Hotel 3 * is a resort hotel located in Thailand, on one of the longest beaches in Phuket, which reaches a length of about 3350 meters.

Thousands of tourists stop here every year. Why are they opting for this hotel? What is so special about it? The question is relevant, and therefore now we should pay a little more attention to this topic.

Location

As you could already understand, Phuket Island View Hotel 3 * is located within walking distance from the beach. But this is not the only advantage of the location occupied by the hotel.

In the immediate vicinity are other interesting sights. These include SC Plaza Kata shopping center, the famous Karon night market, Suwan Khiri Ket Temple, Big Buddha.

It also provides quick access to the Kata Karon Observation Deck, Chalong Pier and Phuket Simon Cabaret.

By the way, Phuket International Airport is not so far away. To go about 30 kilometers. Krabi International Airport is located further at a distance of 81 km.

Leisure

Many are interested to know how developed the entertainment infrastructure is in the territory of Phuket Island View Hotel 3 *. Reviews from tourists allow you to make sure that there is something to do, without even leaving the hotel.

On the territory there are three pools - a deep, combined (with a bar and hydromassage) and a children's. There is also a sun terrace and garden where you can spend your free time relaxing. In addition, there is the opportunity to play table tennis and enjoy a massage in the spa, which is also located on site.

There is no animation at the hotel, and for many this is a significant plus, since most tourists who come here are set up for a calm, peaceful vacation.

And lovers of fun can find it outside the hotel. In the immediate vicinity is Bangla Road - one of the most noisy streets of the island, where clubs, cafes, bars and other entertainment venues are in maximum concentration. It can be quickly reached by cheap taxi or tuk-tuk.
