Hotel Asel Hotel 3 *, Turkey: description and reviews of tourists

The resort town of Beldibi in Turkey is a great place to relax, although not as popular as, for example, Antalya. However, this undoubtedly has its advantages, because the local beaches are free from a large influx of tourists, which means they are quieter and calmer. In addition, the cost of a ticket to Beldibi is more affordable compared to tours to other Mediterranean resorts. Small hotels are especially attractive in this regard, for example, Asel Hotel 3 *.

Description

This guest house is located in Beldibi. On the territory of 1000 square meters. m. here is a compact 4-story hotel building and a well-equipped area with a swimming pool and green spaces.

The building was built in 2004, and several years ago was renovated. Now tourists are welcomed by small, but clean and bright rooms. The design of the hotel is modest, but tasteful - nothing more.





Distances:

  • Antalya Airport is 40 km away.
  • City center - at a distance of 700 m (the road will take 10-15 minutes).
  • The city beach is 400 m.

Service

The list of services provided in the hotel is quite consistent with the rank of the hotel (3 *). Everything you need for a comfortable stay is offered:

  • Reception The registration desk works without days off and breaks, that is, 24/7.
  • Luggage storage. This service is available at reception.
  • Transfer. Meeting guests at the airport and delivering to the hotel allows you to save time and energy in search of transport.
  • The Internet. Wi-Fi in the lobby is available for a fee.
  • Room cleaning. It is carried out every day.
  • Change of towels. It is provided 3 times a week.
  • Washing clothes. Dry cleaning, laundry and ironing services are available at an additional cost.
  • Buying excursions. You can buy excursions and tickets to local attractions directly at the hotel.

As for the design of the rooms, it is made in a minimalist style with bright finishes. The flooring is carpeted, which makes the atmosphere warmer and more comfortable.

Room Facilities

Half of the rooms feature a small balcony with a table and chair. It can be a great place for an afternoon break and admire the surrounding scenery.

All rooms have a spacious tiled bathroom. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, toilet, washbasin, mirror. In addition, the room has:





  • Air conditioning system. Air conditioning is available in all rooms. You can use them for free.
  • TV. Hotel guests can have fun at any time thanks to the TV and satellite television. There is also a Russian channel.
  • Safe. Here you can place all the most valuable things of tourists. The service is available for a fee.
  • Telephone. You can use it for free.
  • Kettle. Hot tea or coffee is available at any time of the day.
  • Mini bar. To be filled on the day of arrival. There is bottled water, soft drinks. It is paid separately.

In the restaurant, guests are invited daily to 3 meals a day, which serves international cuisine. Buffet-style meals include hot meats, salads, fruits, pastries and desserts.

Entertainment in Asel Hotel 3 *

The hotel has a small freshwater pool. Cleaning and cleaning is carried out regularly, so the water in the pool is always fresh. Bathing will bring only positive emotions.





The platform in front of the pool is equipped with sunbeds. In other words, the hotel will have something to do between trips to the beach and sightseeing programs.

Beach

The Kemer Asel Hotel 3 * does not have its own beach, however, at a distance of 400 meters (2 coastline), there is a city sand and pebble beach. The entrance to the water is gentle and safe, so you can safely come here with children.

The beach is equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds, anyone can use this beach equipment for free. Nearby you can use paid entertainment:

  • windsurfing;
  • boating and yachting;
  • Beach volleyball.

What to do in Beldibi

The clear sea and sandy beaches are not the only attraction of this resort. Tourists staying at the Asel Hotel 3 * hotel should definitely visit some especially significant corners of the city.

  1. Dino Park Kemer. This attraction is located in the city of Kemer, however, the journey there will take no more than 15-20 minutes. Dino Park occupies a fairly large territory and is famous for a large number of exhibits - dinosaurs that make sounds and move.
  2. Mount Takhtaly in the village of Kirish. According to legend, this mountain was the habitat of the gods. The cable car will help you get to the very top, and from the observation platform, the surroundings are visible in full view.

Asel Hotel 3 *: reviews

Before buying a tour to Asel Hotel, you should definitely familiarize yourself with the reviews that tourists who have visited here have managed to leave.

I must say that among the Russian speakers there are quite a lot of negative reviews, but there are also many positive features.





  1. Distance from the airport. The hotel is located 40 km. from the airport, so the trip will take about 30-40 minutes. In this case, it is better to use the transfer from the hotel, as finding a bus or taxi can be problematic.
  2. Lack of entertainment. On the territory of the hotel from entertainment there is only a pool (and that one is small). Animation, billiards and other entertainment infrastructure is not provided. However, those tourists who go to the beach and plan a busy schedule of excursions will not notice this.
  3. Nutrition. Most guests agree that the food in the hotel is somewhat monotonous and does not meet the stated descriptions. Partially solve this issue will help small cafes and bars, located in large numbers in the town itself. The food is excellent here, and the prices are very, very low.
  4. Sea. This is exactly what it is worth coming to Beldibi for. The absence of a large number of tourists (including the Turks themselves) allows you to enjoy the beach in silence and cleanliness.
  5. Prices for accommodation. This is another reason why vacationers choose this hotel, despite the negative reviews about the Asel Hotel 3 * Kemer. The cost of accommodation here is one of the most attractive in the area.

Who can safely recommend the Asel Hotel 3 * hotel? To all those who appreciate the solitude and beauty of nature, to all who are looking for a clean coast for a beach holiday, lovers of excursions. In other words, everyone who expects from the hotel no more than a place for an overnight stay and a hearty breakfast will be completely satisfied.
