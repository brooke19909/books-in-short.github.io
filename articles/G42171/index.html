Sights of Moscow for the child. Where to go with children in Moscow?

When planning to spend school holidays in the capital of Russia, an excursion and entertainment program should be drawn up, first of all, taking into account the interests of children, and only then choose suitable places for adults.

Leisure in Moscow with children can be done in different ways. To get new knowledge, you should go on a sightseeing tour, to a museum, to an exhibition. Fans of outdoor activities are best to spend time on attractions, skating rinks, rollerdromes. You can arrange walks through the city streets, parks and gardens. Some ingenious parents can arrange entertainment for their children even from a trip on a city tram or metro.





Prepare for the trip should be in advance. All the attractions of Moscow for a child must be included in one list for consideration by family members. Having chosen the most interesting ones, you should start collecting information about them.

Where to lead the child in Moscow?

After the family has decided on the selection of the program, it is very important to draw up the correct route. If you have to travel around the city using public transport, you need to collect as much information as how long the trip can take, at which stops you should go, which way to go. Realizing which way to go, travelers will save themselves from confusion, unnecessary movement and the loss of precious time.

Travelers will need special training. In an unfamiliar city, you can easily get confused, and drive the extra kilometers in search of the right turn. First, you should lay a route using an Internet map, then check it with the atlas of Moscow roads. A good assistant on the way can be a high-quality electronic navigator.

If the family intends to live in the city for several days, it is better to spend the first days on the sights of Moscow for the child, and the subsequent days on the program for adults.



Visiting museums, exhibitions, opening days, you should definitely use the services of information support. An experienced guide will help to make a visit to the center of culture systematized and more meaningful. By submitting fascinating information about what he saw, the guide is able to open a new and amazing world of exhibits for the child. And then the sights of Moscow for the child will become a memorable event.

For those who do not like group visits, you can use the convenient service called audio tour. Visitors receive a temporary use of a CD player, with which they travel through the museum alone. The audio guide through the headphones interestingly talks about the surrounding objects, and guides the movement of the excursionist in the right direction. Unfortunately, such a guide is unable to answer questions.

The duration of a museum tour usually takes no more than one and a half hours and covers far from the entire collection of exhibits. If time permits, then you can spend an interesting day there all day. You should read historical references, watch documentaries.

To choose the most successful route for an exciting trip, it is proposed to consider a list that presents the main attractions of Moscow for a child and other family members.

Moscow Kremlin



Arriving in the Russian capital for the first time, you should definitely show the children the Kremlin and Red Square. These two objects, which are historical and cultural symbols of Russia, are known throughout the world. Here are the most famous sights of Moscow for children and their parents. The Moscow Kremlin today is one of the largest museums in the world. The residence of the Russian president is located on its territory, so the place should be defined as the political center of the country.

Walking along Red Square, you can see St. Basil's Cathedral, Lenin's Mausoleum. On the territory of the Kremlin are the Assumption, Arkhangelsk and Annunciation Cathedrals, the Faceted Chamber. Inspection of architectural monuments can be combined with walks in Cathedral Square and the Alexander Garden.

Zoological Museum named after Lomonosov

You can go with a child to the Museum of Zoology in Moscow, if, of course, he is fond of the animal kingdom. The zoo museum fascinates and delights with unique expositions dedicated to the inhabitants of the deep sea and ocean.





The magnificent collection of insects, reptiles and rare birds is impressive. In addition to a fascinating tour, you can also watch interesting informative films about animals.

Ice museum

A visit to the exhibition of ice sculptures will not leave indifferent not only children, but also adults. The unusual crystal labyrinth is filled with many transparent sculptures of fairy-tale characters and cartoon characters. Highlighting from multi-colored lights greatly enhances the effect of what he saw.

Around the museum there is a beautiful park where you can take a walk, feed squirrels and visit the ice cafe. The peculiarity of this establishment is that all the food here is served in ice dishes. For relaxation, you can sit on a bench and just look at the local landscapes.

Cosmonautics Museum at VDNH

An unusual museum located in one of the huge pavilions tells the story of the mysterious and mysterious world of space. Here you can see models of rockets and artificial satellites.





The guide‚Äôs story about the brave dogs Squirrel and Strelka, about the flight of the first man into space will captivate young guests. With their own eyes, children will see a space suit and will be able to find out what astronauts eat in zero gravity.

Butterfly House

In another pavilion of VDNH there is a cozy house inhabited by amazingly beautiful creatures. What impresses visitors is the fact that numerous butterflies fly completely freely in a vast space planted with many tropical plants.





While in the pavilion inevitably there is a feeling of a real stay in the rainforest. This is achieved due to a certain humidity, optimal temperature and a myriad of exotic insects fluttering around.

Zoo

A visit to the Moscow Zoo will be a real holiday for the whole family. This is the oldest animal park in Russia. On a vast territory is a real fauna, consisting of eight thousand pets. All of them are ready to endlessly surprise and delight visitors.





At certain times, guests are allowed to participate in the feeding of animals, which cannot but please the little visitors. For lovers of marine animals in the dolphinarium, located at the zoo, regularly held performances. Water tricks and pirouettes of handsome dolphins invariably cause an explosion of emotions and a storm of delight in the audience.

Planetarium

A planetarium is the best gift Moscow can make for children, where you can go with your parents and friends. In this place you can see real miracles. The large star hall offers visitors a demonstration of stars on a huge screen. In the room called Sky Park, all kinds of time meters are collected. Here you can see a variety of sundials and calendars. Lunarium will cause the greatest emotions of children, because it is allowed to touch the exhibits with your hands, try on the image of an astronaut. The Museum of Urania presents many different measuring instruments: globes, telescopes, projectors.

Soyuzmultfilm Film Studio

Of course, a visit to Moscow will not be complete unless the famous animated film factory is shown to the children.





The tour will introduce you to the most famous children's heroes - the crocodile Gena and his friend Cheburashka. Here they will tell and show how they draw animated characters, make dolls and decorations. Young guests will be invited to take part in a master class in animation.





Each participant, under the guidance of a professional animator, will be able to independently portray the hero of his future cartoon. At the end of the program, guests will be shown the most famous cartoons.

Conclusion

Now you know what are the sights of Moscow for children, photos of which you see in our article. As you understand, the capital of Russia offers a great variety of entertainment for children. Adults can only choose the most suitable to arrange a real holiday for their children.
