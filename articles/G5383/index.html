Dana Beach Hotel, Hurghada

The five-star hotel consists of the main building (the reception is located in it) and several 2, 3 and 4-storey buildings. The complex is located in the Sahl Hasheesh area, 28 km from Hurghada. It takes 15 minutes to get to the international airport.

"Dana Beach", Hurghada. Rooms



The hotel offers upscale accommodation in rooms:

  • single standard;
  • double and triple;
  • family;
  • Superior
  • Junior Suites;
  • Suites.

All rooms are well furnished in European style, well-lit, equipped with comfortable and modern furniture. All have a balcony or terrace, TV, bathroom with shower or bathtub, hairdryer and toiletries, minibar with refrigerator (extra charge), air conditioning. Housekeeping and linen change are carried out every day.

"Dana Beach", Hurghada. Infrastructure



The hotel offers vacationers a wonderful time in:

  • 4 restaurants;
  • 4 bars;
  • 3 pools (2 of which are winter heated).

Restaurant "La Clef de L Orient" operates on a buffet system - the same as "Castello". Taste magical drinks, original light snacks, the Le Rendez Vous bar and the very cozy Ceasar invite you. For a fee you can use the conference room, which seats 120 people.

"Dana Beach", Hurghada. Sport and leisure

The hotel offers to relax and have a great time in the sauna or Turkish bath, fitness center or gym. You can ride a catamaran, go diving, windsurfing. For sports companies, a basketball and volleyball court, a tennis court, a football field, table tennis tables, a place for playing handball, bocce, darts and billiards are allocated. Also in "Dana Beach" (Hurghada) you can ride a horse and learn the skill of riding.

Sea and beach





The hotel has its own sandy lagoon beach and pier. Vacationers have the opportunity to take umbrellas, wicker fences from the wind and sunbeds for free. The entrance to the sea is gentle, the beach is sandy, so you can not carry special beach shoes with you. Children can spend time on the playground equipped for them, in the gazebo for animation, play bocce, volleyball or darts. Working hours of the beach bar: from 10 a.m. to 18 p.m. Drinks in the bar are the same as in the rest of the hotel, you can have a snack from 12.30 to 14.30. During this time, staff offers snacks and hot dishes.

Animation

Evening programs are organized by animators, sometimes with the participation of famous artists. Often held a mini disco. After 11pm, you can relax at the foam party or take part in the evening show at the Havana nightclub, which is located across the street from the hotel. Admission is free for hotel guests, with the exception of performances by celebrities and foam parties.

"Dana Beach", Hurghada. Prices and surroundings

Near the hotel there are shopping arcades with souvenirs, leather, textiles. The goods here are expensive, you need to bargain and boldly bring down the price by 70-80%. On foot you can reach the shops with fixed prices - Cleopatra and SENZO Mall. Hurghada can be reached by hotel shuttle, departing from the hotel at certain times. In general, the hotel has the best value for money and is perfect for families.
