Spain in September. Spain: beach holidays in September

Spain is one of the most hospitable, vibrant and colorful countries in Europe. Many tourists believe that you can come here only in the summer on a beach holiday, but this is not so. Spain is open for guests at any time of the year, you just need to know when it is better to go to the sea, and when - to ski resorts, sightseeing tours, shopping, etc. You also need to familiarize yourself with the weather conditions, because the air temperature, quantity precipitation on the mainland and islands vary significantly.



What is the appeal of the velvet season?

In most southern resorts, high temperatures remain until mid-October. Spain in September is attractive for people who are accustomed to a calm measured rest, pacifying atmosphere. In the fall, there is no longer such an influx of tourists as in the summer, restless students and schoolchildren leave to study. Of course, there is no certainty in the weather, rains are not excluded, but this is a wonderful time for sightseeing tours. The sea is still warm, on the beach you can get a great tan, but there is no suffocating heat, from which neither hide nor hide. That is why many travelers decide to relax in Spain in September. In the velvet season, housing prices are slightly lower than in the summer. Tourists are attracted by the cultural program, the high level of service and, of course, nature enchanting with its beauty.

Weather in Spain in September

Until mid-autumn, Spanish resorts are great for a beach holiday. The second half of September is usually cloudy and rainy, there may be storms that prevent walking near the coast.

National cuisine

Spain cannot boast of certain culinary traditions. Each region prepares different dishes. Spaniards use Moorish, Roman, African, French ideas, many recipes are taken from Mediterranean cuisine. Light snacks are served for breakfast, lunch and dinner. It can be sandwiches, dried squid, cod, scrambled eggs, small pieces of meat or fish, clams in batter. Variations are very different, it all depends on the imagination of the chef.



The first dish is always soup, it can be creamy or mashed. Such dishes are flavored with tomato, grated tomatoes. They are cooked with clams, fish, ham, beef, spices, stewed vegetables, croutons, garlic bread, etc. Spaniards are very fond of meat, fish, seafood. You can try a variety of combinations with vegetables, fruits, spices. Peppers, onions, eggplant, potatoes and much more are mixed with seafood and stewed in sauce.

Entertainment

All people have fun in different ways. Some associate Spain with flamenco, others with bullfighting, and others with water parks. This bright and cheerful country will not let anyone get bored; absolutely everyone can have a good time here, regardless of wallet size and age. Holidays in Spain in September are interesting because at that time there are no more crowds in aquariums, parks, zoos, shopping and entertainment centers. Therefore, you can relax and slowly explore all the local attractions.



In Spain there are a lot of beautiful parks where you can relax, relax at your pleasure, enjoy the delightful nature. Barcelona has a huge zoo, which has a large number of animals brought from different parts of the world. Both adults and children like amusement parks, the main thing is not to keep emotions in yourself, but to laugh and squeal with pleasure while riding the rides!

Also, do not forget about sightseeing tours of the cultural, architectural and historical sights of the country. Holidays in Spain in September just have to leisurely inspection of all interesting places, familiarity with the culture and traditions of the people. In autumn, in many stores the discount season begins, so you can set aside a day for shopping to get high-quality, stylish and not very expensive things. In Spain, at any time of the year there is no time to be bored; there is always something to do here.

Beach vacation

For many years, Spain has positioned itself as a beach resort. Hundreds of thousands of tourists come here every year to enjoy the warm transparent sea, clean beaches, and amazing landscapes. Spain in September, especially at the beginning of the month, is very suitable for hanging out near the water. There are more than 1700 equipped beaches in the country, organized both in mainland and in island resorts. Tourists can sit on the sandy or pebble, Atlantic or Mediterranean coast. In Spain, there is snow-white, golden and anthracite-black sand, each traveler can choose the most acceptable shade of the beach. The country is famous for its numerous resorts that hospitably welcome tourists at any time of the year. A high level of service and developed infrastructure attract guests from all over the world not only in Europe.



The best resorts of the country

Costa Brava, Costa Dorada, Costa Blanca, Costa Tropical and Costa del Sol are the most popular cities that are famous for beautiful weather, pearl beaches, clear sea. Spain in September pleases with sunny days, the water temperature does not fall below +23 ¬∞ . The Costa Blanca has snow-white beaches; active tourists who love water activities should go to the Costa Brava. Nature lovers will also like it here. The scenery at the resort is truly amazing, the imagination is struck by cozy bays, rocky shores, clean beaches.

Costa Dorada is the most popular resort, suitable for all age groups. The sea in Spain is still not cooling in September, so travelers have time to enjoy all the delights of a beach holiday. Costa Tropical and Costa del Sol are southern resorts, you can swim and sunbathe for six months. The sun shines here 300 days a year.



Holidays on the islands

Absolutely all tourists want to spend an unforgettable vacation, to fully enjoy the trip, but, unfortunately, often the weather cancels all plans. Fortunately, island Spain is more stable in terms of weather than mainland, so travelers who do not like surprises are advised to go to the Balearic or Canary Islands. Ibiza, Mallorca - these are the best resorts for restless youth. Spain at the end of September can upset torrential rains, but this only applies to the mainland, almost summer weather reigns on the islands until mid-October. In Ibiza, tourists are struck by the color of the sea, clean and well-equipped beaches. Here around the clock life is in full swing, the coast is full of entertainment venues. In Mallorca, the crystal clear blue sea, not only young people, but also representatives of the older generation like to relax here.





The Canary Islands are ready to receive guests at any time of the year. Many influential people, stars, politicians decide to spend their holidays in Spain in September in Gran Canaria, La Palma, and Tenerife. The Atlantic Ocean, snow-white coasts, laurel groves, parks drowning in flowers, cypress alleys - all this beauty cannot be described in words, you just need to come and explore this splendor on your own.
