Where to sunbathe and swim?

The Camelot Hotel Pattaya has its own beach with sun loungers and parasols. True, swimming here, like almost everywhere in the center of Pattaya, is impossible. Sitting by the sea, enjoying the sunset, even eating is another matter. But in order to swim, you have to travel to the islands, since the pier is a stone's throw away. The shortest way to it is through the territory of the Elephant Hotel. Everyone who wants to lie down on white sugar and splash in the turquoise sea goes on ferries. At the pier you can buy tickets to the islands at different prices - from sixty to one hundred and fifty baht. It depends on where you go - to a shared beach, where there will be a lot of people, or to a more secluded place. If you do not want to travel to the islands, you can use tuk-tuk. For thirty butts you will be taken to the distant beaches of Pattaya. They are clean, the water is clear, and there are few people there. But refreshment near the hotel is not a problem. After all, the hotel has an outdoor pool with a children's section. Around it - equipped terrace for sunbathing. The pool itself is great. You can swim there not only during the day, but also in the evenings or even at night if you wish. Filtration in the pool - according to the latest technologies, the water is always clean and warm. So you can spend time here if you were unable to leave for the islands. By the way, the hotel gives out beach towels for painting .
