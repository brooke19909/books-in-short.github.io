"LCD Marshal", October Field: reviews

If you have been looking for an excellent residential complex for living in the capital for a long time, we suggest paying attention to the Marshal residential complex (October Field). The reviews of those who have already managed to purchase an apartment for themselves here will help us to make the most objective review and evaluate all the conditions that the complex can offer to the first tenants.





about the project

Multifunctional residential complex "Marshal" (October Field) is an excellent option for those who are looking for an apartment in Moscow and prefer exclusively developed, yet environmentally friendly areas. The construction is based on the concept of "city in a city", which allows the construction of self-sufficient residential quarters and entire microdistricts with their own infrastructure.

Architecture

LCD "Marshal" (October Field) was built according to a unique project of world-famous architect Mikhail Filippov. The facades are decorated with classical-style columns, graceful arches and buttresses from the ends create a powerful architectural image of a reliable and even monumental building.





The majestic complex attracts attention and enthusiastic looks, not only during the day, but also at night. Residents note that in the evening the facades are highlighted, which adds to their nobility and luxury. From the upper floors of new buildings, a stunning view of the sights of the capital opens up - it is already breathtaking. Location

For the construction of a new residential complex , one of the oldest districts of Moscow, Schukino, was chosen. Geographically, it belongs to the northwestern administrative district. This is a beautiful and well-maintained area with developed infrastructure, guaranteeing comfort to every resident. In walking distance - the Moscow metro station "October Field", which allows you to get to anywhere in the city without traffic jams.

Address LCD "Marshal" (October Field): Moscow, st. Marshal Rybalko, 2, building 1-9

Transport accessibility

Transport accessibility is another advantage of the Marshall residential complex (October Field). New buildings are within walking distance from the metro station. In addition, next to the complex there are enough stops of ground public transport, with which you can get to anywhere in the capital.





Ecology

Responses of residents of the residential complex "Marshal" (October Field) paid attention to the ecology of the construction site. No matter how strange it may sound, even within the MKAD you can really find an area with excellent ecology. In the vicinity of it there are enough parks and squares that fill the air with oxygen. The absence of harmful industries with chemical emissions also favorably affects the environmental situation.

Infrastructure

Residents of the neighborhood focus on its developed infrastructure. In walking distance from new buildings there is everything that is necessary for a comfortable life: shops and supermarkets, kindergartens and secondary schools, including those with in-depth study of foreign languages, art schools, stadiums, and sports sections. The first floors of residential buildings will be reserved for commercial infrastructure and services: there are enough bank branches, pharmacies, dry cleaning, cafe bars and restaurants where you can celebrate a grandiose and important event in your life.

The builder took care of the car owners, laying the underground two-level parking in the project. Residents of the complex emphasize that there is enough space for everyone. Moreover, the underground parking has become an excellent modern alternative to garages, protects cars from bad weather and hooligans.

On the territory of the residential complex "Marshal" (October Field) there was a place for the construction of a full-fledged business center with offices, a conference room and all necessary communications. Thus, you can rent a room for your business and work without leaving the complex.

Apartments, layouts

In the design process, great attention was paid to the preferences and requirements of modern residents. As a result, managed to create a luxury apartment, ideal for living. A choice of buyers offered apartments ranging from 38 to 27 square meters. The free layout of apartments allows you to translate any design ideas into reality. Panoramic glazing contributes to the penetration of a sufficient amount of light, and which of the panoramic windows offers a view!





If you want to buy luxurious apartments, follow the advice of the first residents and purchase an apartment on the top floors of the buildings: they are reserved for two-level apartments.
