Where is the hotel Armeria 3 *

This hotel complex is located in the heart of Kemer. Armeria 3 * is removed from the nearest Antalya airport at a distance of 55 kilometers. Thus, upon arrival, you can reach the hotel within an hour. The hotel is located on the second coastline and does not have a private beach. The nearest city beach is 400 meters away.

Turkey, Kemer: photos, description of the resort

One of the most popular resort areas - Kemer - is located in the southern part of the Turkish Republic. From the nearest airport, located in Antalya, you can get here in about 1 hour. The resort zone stretching for 70 kilometers includes several settlements: the city of Kemer, as well as a number of villages - Kirish, Beldibi, Goynuk, Tekirova and Chemyuva. On the one hand, this resort is surrounded by a blue sea surface, and on the other, picturesque mountains. In Kemer, a large area is occupied by pine forests, which makes the local climate very healthy.

As for the beaches of Kemer, most of them are pebble. However, some hotels brought sand to their beaches. Nevertheless, at the entrance to the sea, pebbles are still found almost everywhere. The sea here is very clean. So, the bottom can be seen even at a depth of eight meters.

Kemer Resort is a great place for a relaxing family vacation with children, and for a fun and noisy pastime for a company of youth. So, tourists traveling to Turkey with children, most often opt for the villages of Camyuva and Kirish. Here the beaches are small-pebble, which is important for a comfortable rest for kids. In addition, local hotels have all the necessary infrastructure for an excellent pastime for families with children.

If you are a fan of a calm and respectable vacation, then buy a ticket to the city of Kemer or to villages located to the south of it. There are a sufficient number of luxury five-star hotels that can satisfy the needs of the most demanding tourists.

Young people are mainly attracted by both Kemer itself and villages located north of the city. There is always a vibrant nightlife with lots of bars, clubs and discos for every taste.





Kemer Attractions

Arriving for a vacation in Kemer, do not hesitate to visit the famous Tekirova coast, located just 20 kilometers from the city. The main local attraction is the magnificent beach, considered one of the best in Turkey.

If you want to feel the spirit of the ancient history of these places, then be sure to go on an excursion to the ancient city of Phaselis, where you can see for yourself the remains of the amphitheater, basilica, as well as ancient sarcophagi and tombstones decorated with beautiful bas-reliefs.





The benefits of a vacation in Kemer

- A lot of hotels for every taste and budget: from small two- and three-star hotels in the city center, designed mainly for fun young people, to comfortable four- and five-star hotels with a large territory and their own beach, designed for an excellent family vacation with children.

- Healthy climate and beautiful nature.

- Kemer is located near medieval and ancient historical monuments, excursions to which will make your vacation in Turkey more interesting and diverse.

- Clear sea with clear water, creating excellent conditions for diving and snorkeling.

- A relatively short distance to the airport (it takes about an hour to get there). So, for example, tourists going on vacation to Alanya are forced to spend more than two hours on the road from the air harbor.

- The presence of a wide variety of entertainment for every taste: from nightclubs, restaurants, shops to hammams, spas and sports.

Hotel selection and cost of living

When choosing a hotel in Kemer, be sure to pay attention to the fact that in the city itself most hotels have a very small territory of their own. However, there is a large number of various shops, souvenir shops, bars, restaurants, night clubs and other entertainments. Hotels located in resort villages, as a rule, have a large territory of their own, on which various entertainments are concentrated. Hotel prices in Kemer vary greatly. So, you can always find here both a budget option of housing, and choose a luxury hotel with the appropriate cost. For example, a three-day stay in a two-star hotel "Ersa" (only breakfast included) will cost $ 170, in a three-star hotel "Ilimira" (all-inclusive food) - $ 185, and in a four-star hotel "Magic" - $ 200 .
