Vladimir Mortgage Fund: people's hope for new housing

The desire to live in your own apartment is often fraught with certain difficulties. For example, the lack of money for the purchase of housing in the primary or secondary market. The Vladimir Mortgage Fund will help solve the financial issue.

Fund Basic Information

The most important factor guaranteeing the reliability of a fund is that it is state owned. It was created by the Vladimir city administration. The purpose of the organization is to solve the problems of residents of the city of Vladimir with housing. The fund has been operating for several years.





The office of the fund is located in Vladimir at the address: Lenin Avenue, 21A. Clients can get a consultation at the office from Monday to Thursday from 9:00 to 19:00; on Friday from 9:00 to 18:30. The Vladimir Mortgage Fund also operates on Saturday from 10:00 to 15:00. A lunch break is not provided for in the fund’s work schedule, that is, clients can seek help throughout the working day. The fund’s specialists will be happy to offer everyone who wishes the most suitable option for him.

Mortgages

Today, many families are interested in mortgages. The Vladimir Mortgage Fund offers several suitable home loan programs. Let's consider some of them.





For example, "Housing for a Russian family." People can participate in the program whose current housing area does not exceed the maximum permissible norms. In addition, we are talking about families with two or more children, war veterans, as well as families living in rooms recognized as emergency. To participate in the program, a person submits documents to the local administration on the basis of which he is included in the list of program participants. After that, you can contact the Vladimir Mortgage Fund for a loan. Also, a person must conclude an agreement with the developer on the share participation in the construction or the contract of sale of the apartment.





Recently, a very profitable scheme has begun to operate - a mortgage at 9%. The participants in this project receive very favorable credit conditions, namely:

  • preferential interest rate (discount up to 5% per annum);
  • savings for the entire lending period will be up to 250,000 rubles;
  • instant decision on the issuance of credit funds.

Absolutely everyone can participate in the program.

For the military and medical, the Vladimir Mortgage Fund has also prepared special offers. Preferential conditions for loans for the purchase of housing can be obtained by health workers under the age of 41 who have higher or secondary special education; employees of medical institutions in whose family two or more people serve a medical business will also receive a discount. Similar discounts are provided for the military.

Vladimir Mortgage Fund: reviews

People talk about the fund, are interested in its work. And this is no wonder, because very few can now buy an apartment by paying in cash to the seller. Turning to the Vladimir Mortgage Fund, the client receives reliable support in solving their property problems. Many people like special preferential programs that the fund developed for certain categories of citizens, for example, for doctors, military, etc.
