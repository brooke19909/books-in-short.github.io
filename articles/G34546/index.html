Waterpark in Kstovo: opening, prices, promotions and customer reviews

In the late 90s and early 2000s, such large-scale places of mass entertainment as water parks began to open in large cities of Russia. Our consumer is used to watching such a luxury only on the screens, so the excitement was grandiose. Similar structures began to be erected in resort areas and rich regions. Until recently, residents of central Russia could only afford water activities in Moscow or St. Petersburg, but in 2015 the long-awaited opening of the water park in Kstovo in the Nizhny Novgorod Region took place.

History of creation

Information about the construction of a new facility appeared back in 2013, when the ceremonial laying of the capsule in the future foundation of the building took place. The initial budget was about 800 million rubles, but, as always in our country, the amount was constantly increasing and supplemented. Many people no longer hoped for a happy completion of the water park project in Kstovo.





The construction site was not chosen by chance. In Nizhny Novgorod itself there are no water parks, as the city has few free sites for such a large-scale construction. 30 km from the regional center there is a small town of Kstovo with a population of 70 thousand people, and it is not difficult to get to it. In addition, this construction was a good incentive for the development of local infrastructure.

Initially, the date of the opening of the water park in Kstovo was called the end of 2014, but internal work was somewhat delayed, so the first visitors appeared here closer to the spring of 2015. The water park received the sonorous name "Atoll". According to the designers, the equipment and comfort are fully consistent with European standards, so the park can compete with Kazan or some Moscow water entertainment centers.

Opening

The first photos from the opening of the water park in Kstovo appeared in February 2015. The organizers and builders of the project noted that the new facility is the first link in a series of large-scale construction of an entertainment zone in the Nizhny Novgorod region. Its appearance is connected, first of all, with the upcoming 2018 World Cup.





The long-awaited opening took place on February 7. Outside, frost and blizzards, but here tropical heat, water and all kinds of attractions. But the first day did not live up to the expectations of the organizers - only about 200 people came to the water park. The people were outraged by exorbitant prices, especially during the tough crisis in Russia. So, an adult ticket cost 3 thousand rubles, more was only in water parks in Moscow. The leaders soon realized their mistake, and prices fell slightly, as a result of which the flow of visitors increased markedly. The photo from the opening of the water park in Kstovo, which visitors posted on their pages and in the official group, also contributed to raising interest.

During the construction and equipping of the water park, the authors of the project tried to meet international standards, since it is planned that foreign guests will also visit it. At the opening of the water park in Kstovo, the management promised that the current state of the park is not the limit, and in the future it is planned to put additional attractions and recreation areas.

Prices

All information on the work schedule, cost of visiting and other questions are on the official website of Atoll, as well as in the Vkontakte, Odnoklassniki and Instagram groups. As noted above, the initial prices greatly angered the public, people in groups even created petitions, but the situation very soon changed in the opposite direction, and today a visit is available for the wallet of any family. In addition, the management constantly holds all kinds of promotions, discount cards are practiced.





Since the opening, the Atoll water park in Kstovo has been operating from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m., it is located at pl. Lenina, d. 5. The following prices are set for the summer season:

  • On weekdays, an unlimited ticket will cost 800 rubles for an adult, 550 for a child under 13 years of age. Three-hour visit for an adult - 650 rubles, for a child - 450.
  • On weekends and holidays, unlimited for an adult will be 1100 rubles, for a child - 700, three hours - 850 and 550 rubles. respectively.
  • There is a supplement from the 15th minute for each subsequent 30 minutes at 150 rubles.

A ticket can be purchased by phone or ordered online. The price includes access to all attractions, pools and bath complexes, SPA-salon and cafe services are paid separately.

Stocks

The opening of the Atoll water park in Kstovo attracted residents not only from the Nizhny Novgorod region, but also from the neighboring ones, Ivanovo and Kostroma. Despite a decent distance, people come here as families to relax. It turns out much cheaper than a trip to Moscow or Kazan. And the management of the water park in every possible way supports the interest in their project: a convenient system of discounts and other incentives for visitors is practiced.

The list of stocks is constantly updated on the official website. So, at the beginning of summer in the "Atoll" the following events are planned:

  • The action dedicated to June 12 - Day of Russia. On this day, all visitors dressed in tricolor colors receive a 20% discount.
  • In June, discounts for students and retirees continue.
  • The latest innovation is swimming for babies.
  • Anyone can purchase gift cards or certificates of attendance.




In addition, every weekend in the water park there is an animator for children. Before visiting, it is recommended that you familiarize yourself with the rules of conduct on the premises.

Visitors reviews

The opening of the water park in Kstovo was looking forward to several years. In the harsh conditions of the Russian climate, the appearance of such a place is a real gift for both adults and children. The first reviews were controversial. Those who have never been to such institutions wrote about their enthusiasm and appreciation to the organizers. More sophisticated people noted the low cost of attractions, the banality of the park. Of course, the water park in Kstovo does not even reach the average American similar projects, which have a developed infrastructure and entertainment industry. But for the average layman, a visit to the Atoll is a wonderful opportunity to relax, especially on frosty winter days.
