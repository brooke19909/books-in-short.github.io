DIY canary and horse costume

About half a year ago, the Internet was blown up by two photos: a canary and a centaur costume. Not too talented parents made carnival dresses for their children, from which they want to cry and laugh at the same time. In fact, you can sew beautiful costumes for these heroes for children. Remember, every time you came to the next morning party, you saw at least five princesses and six birds with one stone. If you make a canary costume, you will completely avoid repetition, and your child will feel unique.

All that is needed is a yellow dress with wings and a mask. Making it at home is easy. Take a piece of yellow fabric, preferably satin or any other "light". Cut a large circle with a hole in the middle. It should be a kind of skirt-sun. But this hole is not for the belt, but for the neck. Sew the sleeves so that when the child raises his hands up, wings are obtained. That's all!

You can make the canary costume much more elegant by decorating it with sequins and sequins. A bird mask can be bought at the store. If you did not find a yellow mask, then you can buy any other and paint it at home or paste over with yellow paper.

Another advantage of this costume is that the canary can easily turn into a firebird, which all princes will fight for, if the holiday script requires it.



Horse costume

It is unlikely that you will ever need to make a centaur costume, but if suddenly some teacher decides to arrange a children's matinee in the ancient Greek style, then borrow the idea from the photo below. We will tell you how to make an alternative horse costume.

In specialized stores, suits-overalls are sold today. They are very soft and look like plush toys. In this outfit, your child will look very cute. However, if you do not want to spend money on a costume for one evening, then you can do it yourself.

All you need is a jacket, preferably brown, with a hood, pants, fringe, feather boa, gloves or mittens. Sew the ears onto the hood, and between them stretch the boa (this will be the mane).
