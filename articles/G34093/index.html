Basic requirements for radar detector





The main function of a car radar detector is to notify the driver of the risk of deviation from the rules. As a rule, the device detects the presence of speed control systems on the road and gives the corresponding signal to the owner. Accordingly, a good radar detector should not only track DPS points, but also quickly warn about them, and with a minimum percentage of false positives. This function is provided by various means, the most popular of which is the antenna.

The evaluation criterion for a high-quality radar detector is reduced to the sensitivity of this antenna. In the middle price segment, devices are presented that can notify the driver several hundred meters to the tracking point. But increasingly, a good radar detector with a DVR is associated among specialists with wireless technologies based on GPS receivers. Features of such models should be considered separately.

Advantages of devices with a GPS module





If the antenna allows you to fix the radar signal locally, in close proximity to the observation points, then the GPS model works directly with maps of the coordinates of the location of the cameras. This gives a huge advantage, since traditional detectors are not able to pick up the signal from modern surveillance systems. For example, even a good radar detector with a highly sensitive antenna will not allow you to detect the aforementioned Avtodoria complex. The fact is that the latter does not have radar and microwave radiation at all.
