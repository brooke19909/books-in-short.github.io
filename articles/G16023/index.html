How to get from Orenburg to Ufa?

Why do people travel from Orenburg to Ufa? The first reason is related to recovery. It is in this city that there are three famous salt lakes that have a beneficial effect on the human body. Also in Ufa there are several attractions and beautiful nature (lush forests, rivers, picturesque hills). Sometimes it is absolutely necessary to overcome this distance in order to see a relative, chat with friends or make money. Whatever the reason for the trip, you should know in advance about all possible modes of transport in order to choose the option that suits you best.





Use of a personal vehicle

The distance from Ufa to Orenburg is only 374 kilometers. You can overcome it in just 5 hours and 30 minutes. Therefore, it is most reasonable to use a personal car. The main benefit is that you have to spend only on fuel, there will be no need to buy tickets for public transport.





Throughout the path, the road is quite wide and not bumpy. An amazing feature is that there are no forks that could cause difficulties. Plates with direction are placed along the perimeter of the route. This means that you can easily get from one point to another without using a navigator. The advantage is also the new asphalt and bright markings.



People who are well aware of the Ufa-Orenburg highway recommend that they approach the Sterlitamak Shikhan along the way. The height of these mountain peaks reaches up to 200 meters. Nearby, their view is simply mesmerizing.

By plane

Despite the fact that the distance from Ufa to Orenburg is small, three times a week, Orenburg Airlines operates a direct flight from one point to another. This only happens on weekdays - Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays. It is preliminary recommended to call the organization's hotline and consult. Indeed, flights are often canceled due to bad weather conditions.





The only flight leaves strictly according to the schedule at 06:10, and at 07:25 there is a landing in another city. Thus, it is easy to calculate that the entire road will take a little more than one hour. An amazing feature is that the airport in Orenburg is located right in the city. This means that you don‚Äôt need to take a long taxi ride to it. In Ufa, the airport is located far from the center. But difficulties with overcoming the path will also not arise. After all, buses (No. 110 and No. 101) go there very often.

As for the purchase of tickets, this can be done at the airport ticket office or pre-booked on the official website. The cost per person is 3350 rubles.

By train

What type of transport do many travelers prefer? That's right, it's a train. Its big plus is convenience. The total travel time is 8 hours 30 minutes. Usually the trip is carried out at night. Therefore, a person can sleep, wash, have breakfast, and then, gaining strength, go to explore the beauty of the new locality. From Orenburg to Ufa, the route runs under number 381 daily. The departure point is the railway station of the city of Orenburg. It is located on Privokzalnaya street.

Arrives transport to the railway station of the city of Ufa, which is located on the station square. Before you go, you should carefully study the schedule. After all, it can vary depending on the season. In summer, departure takes place at 9 pm, and arrival at 6:30 in the morning. Tickets can be bought directly at the box office. Their minimum cost is 1950 rubles.





By bus

A bus also runs daily from Orenburg to Ufa. However, not everyone will decide on such a step. The total travel time is 7 hours and 30 minutes. Sitting so long in one position is quite difficult. If the trip is carried out on imported vehicles, then this is a completely different matter. In the cabin of such a bus, the seats are perfectly regulated, there is air conditioning and a TV.

If you wish, you can arrange with the driver to make a stop in a particular village, to go to the toilet, buy food on the road or just breathe in fresh air. The first flight leaves from the Orenburg bus station at 7 a.m. You can buy a ticket directly at the box office. Its approximate price is 810 rubles.





Finally

Which of the four main transport is better to get from Orenburg to Ufa? The answer to this question depends only on the individual characteristics of a person. If he needs to overcome this distance in the shortest possible time, then he should give preference to the aircraft.

Pregnant women, the elderly and passengers with children should not skimp and buy a train ticket. In this case, the trip for them will be as comfortable as possible. The most economical option is a bus. But it is not suitable for everyone. If you have a personal car, you can find several fellow travelers and together, having bought the necessary amount of fuel, hit the road. Such a trip promises to be profitable, fun and eventful.
