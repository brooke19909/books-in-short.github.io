What feats did Dobrynya Nikitich accomplish? Russian hero Dobrynya Nikitich

One of the popular trinity of Russian heroes was Dobrynya Nikitich. This character of the Russian folk epos was portrayed as a hero who serves under Prince Vladimir. The wife of Dobryni was the beautiful Nastasya, who was the daughter of Mikula Selyaninovich.

The prototype of Dobrynia Nikitich

An interesting moment can be traced in the epics. The fact is that it is the Dobrynya prince who usually gives various kinds of instructions: either to help out a niece, then to collect tribute and bring her to the court, he himself is also called to fulfill orders that other heroes refused. Thus, the proximity to the princely relatives of Dobrynia, who is sometimes called the prince himself, the nephew of Vladimir the Red Sun himself, becomes apparent . In historical terms, the prototype of a hero can be safely called governor Dobrynya, who was the uncle and governor of Prince Vladimir, brother of mother Malusha. Dobrynya carries out personal instructions, using not only strength, courage, but also diplomatic abilities, which speaks of his education and intelligence. What kind of Dobrynya Nikitich was, we learn from epics, they often talk about his versatility, he is adroit, swims perfectly, shoots, and also owns musical instruments. Often he plays the harp, sings, plays tawley.

Characteristics of Dobrynia

The description of Dobrynia Nikitich in epics is definite and clear. He has a courageous character and is endowed with power unprecedented by ordinary people (only Ilya Muromets is stronger). A distinctive feature of Dobrynya from other heroes is its "veterancy", that is, diplomacy and tact.
