Service activation

Even 7 years ago, many subscribers before the trip wondered how to connect international roaming. MegaFon once and for all solved this problem, being the first of the cellular trio to include this service in the list of default ones. However, before traveling, it is advisable to make sure that everything is in order. If the client of the company at the time of connection did not have permanent registration in Russia, roaming services are not provided to him. In addition, it can be turned off at will.



For those who have access to international roaming, MegaFon gives you the opportunity to use your phone in more than 180 countries, as well as during a flight or sea cruise. True, in some countries there are small features of this service. So, in the USA, cell phones operate at a different frequency, and therefore the device used in Russia may not register. Although the SIM card itself will work in another mobile phone. In Japan, however, it will not be possible to charge your phone from a conventional wall outlet; their power supply operates at a different frequency. All nuances to the subscriber will be reported to the consultant in the information or office of the company.

Rates and additional discounts

More and more subscribers are traveling, which means that more and more they need international roaming. Understanding this, MegaFon is constantly expanding the number of operators with which it cooperates in this field. As a result, over the past few years, it has been possible to significantly reduce roaming tariffs for many popular destinations among Russian citizens. Today, prices in Europe, the CIS and some other countries are already much lower than those of the closest competitors.



But this is sometimes not enough. Therefore, subscribers of the company can additionally activate tariff options. The services "Preferential Roaming", "Communicate Without Borders", "Worldwide" and "Vacation Online" allow you to reduce the cost of calls, SMS and Internet in different countries. True, they will be charged an additional connection fee or in the form of a daily payment. And therefore, if international roaming itself is not needed in the near future, MegaFon recommends disabling these options as well. And when you need them again, you just need to reconnect them.
