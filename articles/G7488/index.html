Transport tax in Bashkiria. 2014 tax rate

Today we will be interested in transport tax in Bashkiria. This payment is in every republic and region. Bashkiria is no exception. There is a number of rules regarding payment. And its own features of the calculations. Nevertheless, similar moments exist in each subject of the Russian Federation. And so now we have to get acquainted with the transport tax in principle. This knowledge will help to avoid the mass of the most common problems. What is the transport tax in Bashkiria? What is this all about?



Transport tax is ...

Before you pay for something, you need to know what kind of payment you received. Indeed, often citizens simply do not understand what kind of fees they pay to the state treasury. Has a payment document arrived? So you have to pay it. This is the wrong position, especially with regard to transport tax. Practice shows that it is very often sent to "everyone in a row."

Transport tax is an annual mandatory payment for vehicle owners. An important point here is that your vehicle must have a motor. So, for example, a bicycle does not require tax. And a skateboard too.

Transport tax is levied in certain amounts and is regional. Settlement rules in each district are established individually. What is the transport tax for (in Bashkiria and not only)? For registering a car on the road. This payment allows you to travel to cities and countries. You cannot drive without him.

Whether it is necessary?

Do I need to pay the transport tax (Moscow and other areas)? Many citizens are puzzled by this question. Answer here definitely will not work. And all this is because each subject of the Russian Federation has its own exceptions. And more precisely, the categories of citizens who can take advantage of the discount or not pay the payment document at all.

However, if you are not a beneficiary, you can say one thing - you must pay. Without fail. But only when you own a car or any other vehicle. For driving thereof by proxy, you will not be charged this tax.



Preferential categories

Transport tax 2014 (Bashkiria and not only) has several categories of beneficiaries. They, as already mentioned, are either completely exempted from payment, or only partially. Who can hope to "evade" from paying legally?

Firstly, they are disabled. Citizens with a disability group of 1 or 2 are fully exempt from transport tax. By the way, the organizations that serve them (transport, and so on) - too.

Secondly, these are veterans and heroes of the USSR. This also includes military personnel who have ever taken part in hostilities. In other words, they were at war.

Thirdly, firms and companies involved in the creation and restoration of roads may not pay transport tax in Bashkiria. In fact, there are very few such beneficiaries.

Fourth, large families. They are also exempted in full from the payment of transport tax. Please note: there must be more than 3 minors in the family. Otherwise, this unit of society will not be considered large.



Old men

What about older people? Pensioners are eternal beneficiaries. But, as practice shows, when it comes to property payments, some of their privileges simply disappear. Transport tax (Moscow and not only) is no exception.

The thing is that people who have gone on a well-deserved rest must pay this contribution to the state treasury. With the exception of parallel entry into the category of beneficiaries exempted from payment. For example, if a person is disabled or a veteran.

Otherwise, the payment will have to be made. But not fully. And only 50% of the amount indicated in the payment document. It turns out that pensioners are entitled to a discount. If you own several vehicles, this privilege is granted only for one of them. You yourself determine which one. To receive a discount, you will have to contact the tax office. Without this, you will have to pay your bills in full.

True, this method is not too accurate. He has errors.

The transport tax calculator (2014-2016) will not give you such accuracy. Therefore, many choose precisely personal counting. Now we have to figure out how to conduct it.

Components

First you need to find out what is required for the calculations. Transport tax (Moscow region and not only) has at least 4 formulas that will help you deal with the task. Honestly, they will not cause difficulties. Especially if you have all the necessary data.

The first and most important component is the transport tax rate. 2014 or for any other take it? This tax rate is assigned annually new. So, you must recognize it for the period that you need. The tax rate (for transport) is the cost of one horsepower.

The second component is engine power. Just it is expressed in the form of horsepower. The more power a car has, the higher the payment.

The third component is the number of months of ownership of the vehicle. This value is only useful when you own a car for less than a year. Otherwise, it is irrelevant.



The last thing that can only come in handy is none other than the so-called up-factor. It applies only to cars that are luxury. Of course, directly related is only to cars.

Formulas

Well, now you can find out exactly how all the calculations are made. The transport tax in Bashkiria and other areas has the same formulas for the implementation of the task. The difference is only in tax rates. They are different in each region.

The standard formula is when the horsepower in the engine is multiplied by the vehicle tax rate. It turns out the exact amount that is due for payment. Nothing difficult, right? If you have a luxury car, then the proposed formula should be multiplied additionally by the specified increase factor.

What to do with vehicles that are owned less than a year? Divide the number of months of ownership by 12. And now multiply this indicator by the engine power of the car and the tax rate. In the case of luxury and expensive cars, you also need to multiply the amount received with a raising factor. Luxury cars cost more than 3 million rubles. And for the exact calculation of transport tax in this case, it is better to use a special online calculator.

For benefits

So, what to do if you are entitled to benefits? The transport tax (Moscow and not only) requires, as already mentioned, the prior application of a citizen with a statement of his rights. This is not so difficult to do. The main thing is to collect a certain list of documents. Which one? This includes:

  • civil passport;
  • a statement indicating the type of exemption;
  • ownership of the car;
  • certificates confirming benefits;
  • pension certificate and military ID (if any);
  • SNILS and TIN.




If you additionally paid for payments that you should not have, a refund is issued. How exactly? The above list of documents must be accompanied by receipts of payment (not more than 3 years), as well as bank details for crediting funds.

Please also pay attention - individuals pay transport tax until December 1, organizations - until March 1. And the advance payment is usually paid until April 30 (at the end of the reporting tax period).
