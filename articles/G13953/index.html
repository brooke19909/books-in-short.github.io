Hotel Xanadu Resort 5 * (Turkey, Belek): description and reviews of tourists

Hotel Xanadu Resort has been enjoying a reputation of a very high level of service for many years. It is located in the first line, among the pine forests of Belek.

Hotel Location

Xanadu Resort Hotel 5 * in the resort town of Belek is part of a group of companies called OTI Holding and with great pride offers guests from all over the world a holiday organized at the highest level. The complex is quite conveniently located in the center of Belek, on the Mediterranean coast. Hotel Xanadu Resort is a five-star hotel offering an all-inclusive High Class vacation. It is surrounded by beautiful pine trees. The hotel area is very large, more than a thousand pine trees grow here. Among the pools, columns and amphitheater, you will feel as if you were magically transported to Ancient Rome.



Hotel Xanadu Resort impresses guests with its ancient architecture. From the complex to the center of Antalya - only fifty kilometers, and to the central part of Belek itself - five kilometers, to the airport - forty kilometers.

Xanadu Resort Hotel 5 * is located at: Turkey, Belek 07500, Acisu Mevcii, Belek Tourism Center.
