The best hotels of North Goa: hotel rating

Today, North Goa is a tourist-developed resort corner of the world, its snow-white beaches attract tourists with its beauty, pristine nature delights and attracts eyes, a comfortable climate has a relaxing and measured rest. This is a real exotic paradise, where the air is saturated with floral aroma. Hotels in North Goa are relatively inexpensive, designed for unassuming tourists.





All hotels, as a rule, are located next to each other, most of them offer high-quality and affordable food. Hotels in North Goa are suitable for people looking for an economical vacation option. It is not necessary to have a lot of money to spend your holidays in the northern part of India in a fun and comfortable way. The best tourist complexes, according to vacationers, are concentrated in the city of Panaji.

Most guest houses offer scenic views of local surroundings and attractions. Many hotels have original pools located directly on the roof of the building. On average, the cost of living will cost 70 euros per day. Each resort village has many nightclubs, bars and restaurants, because it is not without reason that young people gather here. Colorful and fiery trans parties are held on the beaches, so no one will be bored.





We give a rating of hotels of North Goa 4 stars. Most hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis.

The village square

Quiet and peaceful complex, located in the small village of Zorin, just 45 km from the airport. The hotel is 4 years old, but during this time he established himself on the good side. Frequent visitors are newlyweds and adults who want to retire and enjoy nature.

The cozy rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a quality pastime. It features a wellness center, several pools and game rooms. The distance from the beach line is 2 km, but a regular bus delivers guests to the coast.





Resort Terra Paraiso

The magnificent guesthouse, located 200 meters from the city beach, will give you unforgettable pleasure and a lot of positive emotions. On a fairly spacious territory there are 7 two-story cottages, some of them have a jacuzzi. The atmosphere of the holiday reigns in the complex.

Here you will find a beauty salon, a medical center, an exchange center and a restaurant chain. There is a safe pool for the kids. Active people will be able to visit the fitness club and massage room. For business travelers there is a conference room with appliances.
