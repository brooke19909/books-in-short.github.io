Huawei 4G modems: review, specifications, models and reviews

Currently, most companies produce equipment that provides access to the World Wide Web for everyone who wants to. 4G modems appeared in their assortment.

Huawei is one of the best companies that is popular with domestic buyers. Developers from the Middle Kingdom implement only innovative technologies in devices. In the lineup there are models of different price categories, which makes them available to the public. Let's look at a few modems that have received a large number of positive reviews.

Huawei 4G modems: user reviews

After examining the forums, you can see that Huawei products are rated quite highly by users. Positive feedback has been noted by many models. For example, E392, E3372, E8372. What can be attributed to their advantages? The owners drew attention to the technical characteristics, in particular, to the transmission speed, which reaches 50-150 Mbit / s. In this case, the devices will work with only one provider.

We found users and shortcomings in the modems of this brand. The most significant are the sizes. It is the dimensions of the device that cause some inconvenience during operation. Huawei E3372

The requirements of the modern user are no longer able to fully satisfy wired routers, so they were replaced by new generation devices. Their advantages are undeniable - access to the Internet can be obtained from anywhere. These are the features that the Huawei E3372 4G modem provides. It is considered one of the best routers currently on the market. It features a high connection speed. It is sold with universal firmware, so it can work with any operator. In places with a weak signal, it is recommended to install an antenna, for which the developers have provided a special port. Supports 3G, 4G standards. The only thing that the user should consider is that in areas with poor coverage in the gadgets to which the modem is connected, the battery life will be significantly reduced. The reason is that a weak signal consumes more energy.

Consider the characteristics of this model:

  1. The modem type is USB, LTE.
  2. Dimensions: height - 91 mm, width - 29 mm, thickness - 11 mm.
  3. Mass - 31 g.
  4. The signal reception speed is up to 100 Mbit per second, transmission - up to 50 Mbit / s.
  5. External antenna connector: type - CRC9, quantity - 2.
  6. Network standard: 2G / 3G / LTE.
  7. Compatibility with OS: Linux, Windows (all versions), Mac, Win Blue.

The owners of this model during the operation did not find any flaws. The reviews say mainly about the advantages of the modem. They include: universality (work with any operators), the presence of microSD flash drives, small sizes, high-speed connection, the ability to install an external antenna. The average cost of Huawei E3372 varies within 2550 rubles.





Huawei E392

According to most users, the E392 is ideal for a laptop. In the reviews she is called the best and fastest. You can purchase the device in MegaFon stores. The developers have provided a slot for installing an external microSD format drive. Also, the E392 modem can be used as a USB reader.
