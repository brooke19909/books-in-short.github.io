What to do if the phone does not see the SIM card?

The phone has become an integral accessory and assistant in our lives. But sometimes some breakdowns upset us. Often the phone does not see the SIM card. With such a malfunction, a message appears on the display of the cellular device about the need to insert it. Offline inclusion may also occur if there is support for working without a SIM card. How to be in this situation? First you need to figure out why this happened.

What are the problems with the sim card





There can be many reasons. The main thing is the malfunction of the SIM card. This happens when she wears out or her contacts get damaged. A particularly great risk of this is the frequent replacement of SIM cards, reinstalling it in different phones and devices. In this situation, you need to contact your mobile operator, he will replace your card.

It also happens that the phone does not see the SIM card, as it is locked to a specific operator. That is, it does not support numbers of other companies. This also happens when numbers are often changed, if the slot or the device itself is damaged. It is necessary to carry out diagnostics and replace the part in the service center.

- Damage to the SIM connector (this is the card holder), which happens when you carefully remove or insert a SIM card, when improvised devices are used that can harm.
