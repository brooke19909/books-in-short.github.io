Which camera to buy for a novice photographer, or the path of a professional

The camera is now not a means of luxury, not a privilege of masters. Moreover, at the moment, the photo business is strictly divided into photos and photographs. But still there are people who strive for the great. Each of these people asks one question: "What kind of camera should a beginner buy?" One of the famous photographers said a very interesting phrase: "The most important detail of a good picture is behind the camera." Naturally, he was referring to the photographer. Many novice photographers "lower" the cameras for $ 80, for 100, for 200, and so on.



There is a preconceived notion in the world that money predominates in the question of which camera to buy for a novice photographer, and this is a high-quality and expensive equipment. But it is worth removing pink filters from the eyes. The key to any high-quality photograph is not a car, but a person. In life, there are a thousand confirmations of this statement. It is enough to take any average owner of a professional camera for 2000 - 4000 thousand dollars. So, 90% of such people never translate the shooting mode of their camera beyond the machine and video. And imagine what miracles such a camera could do in the hands of a master.

Expensive doesn't mean good

In response to a question about which camera to buy for a novice photographer, many expensive shoppers or half-educators with a 20-megapixel matrix and 12,500 ISO are trying to buy many customers in stores. After all, the buyer does not know that the larger the physical size of the matrix, the more noise it emits and the more disgusting the pictures are. There was a case when a buyer asked a consultant a question about which camera to buy for a beginner photographer, and the consultant offered him a model for $ 2,500. The model had both video and a 21 megapixel sensor, etc., etc. At the same time, the buyer asked for a simple model for $ 800 and began to take a series of pictures from both cameras with a sensitivity of 400 ISO. And in the end it turned out that a cheap camera 2 times surpassed an expensive one. And that is a fact. This phenomenon is very common.
