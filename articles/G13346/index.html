Simple and original takes

For knitting you will need cotton yarn (in 50 grams 240 meters), two hooks - the first and fourth numbers. Changing the number of hooks, you get a dense or loose pattern. Knit openwork summer crochet, starting from the center of the circle.





  1. Lock six air loops into the ring.
  2. Each row begins with three lifting loops. Knit nineteen double crochet (CCH).
  3. Next, alternate 2CCN and three air loops. You should get ten pairs of ‚Äúroll‚Äù columns.
  4. The next row is knitted in the same way, only between two cape columns you get five loops.
  5. Now you knit paired columns (referring to 2CCN) over similar elements of the previous row, and on the arch of five loops form the following pattern: * 2 loops, 1CCN * - repeat twice, 2 loops.
  6. You knit paired columns without changes, and in the arched pattern you collect three air loops, 3CCN, three loops.
  7. The next row is knitted almost the same as the previous one, but instead of 3 CCHs, knit a slingshot (2CCH in one base), 1CCH, a slingshot.

The end of knitting a hat

Finish knitting a crochet summer openwork beret. Scheme and description from the 20th to the 30th row:
