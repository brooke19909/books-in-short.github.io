How to make a boat out of paper? The instructions will help you do the job simply and quickly.

Do you want to master the origami technique? You can start, for example, with the proposed description of how to make a boat out of paper. The instructions in this article will help you make this simplest product very quickly. Schemes clearly demonstrate the whole process of work.





The first stage is folding the product

The following describes in detail how to make a boat out of paper. The instruction is proposed in the form of step-by-step numbered steps, each of which corresponds to the next picture presented in the general diagram.

1. Place a sheet of A4 paper in front of you so that the short sides of the rectangle are on each side. Fold it in half along the indicated dashed line and scroll the workpiece 90 ¬∞ to the left.

2. To form a small and dense product, you can repeat the previous paragraph, acting in the same way. The result is a paper envelope consisting of four layers.

3. Expand the workpiece so that the fold is in the upper horizontal line. Take the top two corners and bend them to the middle dotted line. Smooth the lines with a ruler, sweeping the place where the sheet is folded to form clear contours of the future boat.

4. Divide the remaining space from the bottom of the workpiece in half horizontally with a dashed line. Wrap one of the parts on the other in the direction of the arrow - up.





How to make a boat out of paper: instructions for the final formation of the product

5. Repeat the fourth paragraph, forming two tube-shaped folds that will overlap the main product.

6. Lift the middle part toward you and at the same time bring the sides of the boat together.

7. Wrap the workpiece 90 ¬∞ around its axis. It will look like a rhombus with two solid sides at the top and two loose at the bottom.

8. Dot out the lines for the hem.

9. Turn up the bottom corners and repeat the sixth step by turning the product.

10. Fold the free lower space upward, aligning with the side triangular parts.

11. Gently pull the paper folds located at the edges, away from each other.

Isn't it described in detail how to make a boat out of paper? The instruction will help to easily complete the work even for a younger student. Using the material with the desired ratio of parameters, you can make products of different sizes - from the smallest to the largest.

How to fold a boat out of paper and then decorate it: an improved version

On the basis of the proposed description, you can come up with countless others. There are step-by-step schemes, thanks to which various mini-vessels are obtained. One of the most popular models is a paper boat with a sail. But if you have not become an ace in the origami technique, you can improve the product obtained by the above instructions. To do this, cut the sail from the finishing material and fix it on a long wooden stick, piercing in two places. This "pole" with a paper sheet will be an improvised decoration of the boat. Place the stick on the middle protruding part, piercing the very middle. To make the structure more stable, pull dense threads from the bow to the stern through the edge of the sail. You can also fix the stick with tape on the back, turning the boat upside down. And, of course, do not forget about the bright flag that you need to attach to the very top. Spring children's boat is ready to sail!
