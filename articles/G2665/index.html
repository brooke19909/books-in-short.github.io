Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort 3 * (Hurghada, Egypt): guest reviews and photos, location, description

Today it is very difficult to find a person who has not visited Egypt at least once. Many, having fallen in love with this beautiful country with constantly warm weather, rich history, a colorful underwater world and a comfortable dry climate that helps to cure and stop the development of many chronic diseases, are ready to return there several times a year.



Due to its short flight and affordable prices, Egypt has become one of the most popular destinations in the tourism industry. Relaxing in Egyptian resorts is now much cheaper than in sanatoriums and boarding houses of the Crimea and Krasnodar Territory, and in terms of living conditions and quality of service, the country of the pharaohs has long surpassed domestic health resorts.

Arriving in Egypt, you can see the mysterious sphinx and magnificent pyramids, ride a boat on the Nile, visit the Valley of the Kings and see the mummies in the Cairo museum, ride through the desert on a deadpan camel, visit the Bedouin village and arrange an ATV race on sand dunes. The main wealth of Egypt, of course, is the Red Sea, which attracts us with the inexhaustible wealth of its depths.

Many tourists for frequent visits to Egypt choose economical hotels in Hurghada. If you decide to combine a quality vacation away from the hustle and bustle of the city center and enjoy a good beach vacation and a rich excursion program, there is no need to overpay for a pretentious five-star hotel.

A competent choice in this case could be Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort 3 Hurghada, whose rating, according to visitors, is four out of five points. The best reviews received: the territory of the hotel, its beach and number of rooms, as well as food. As a drawback, distance from the beach is noted. Knowing the Egyptian traditions sometimes assign stars to hotels at random, many tourists are wondering if the hotel deserves its three stars. Let's try to figure it out.

Hotel location

The 3-star Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort is located in the northern part of Hurghada, on the road to El Gouna. The distance from the airport is seven kilometers, to the old center of Down Town is only a kilometer, to the new center of Sakkala will have to travel three kilometers. The fare on the minibus is a couple of Egyptian pounds, by taxi - about ten. There is a two-way road in front of the hotel.



The Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort complex was opened in 1996, in 2006 it was reconstructed and in 2008 - redecorated.

Hotels nearby

Near and across the road are the 3-star Mirett and Palma de Mirett 4 hotels, a little further - the five-star Sunny Dais El Palazzio. The complex has many shops and several cafes.

Hotel Territory

The 3-star hotel complex Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort, whose territory is mainly designed in oriental style, is immersed in lush green vegetation, similar to a tropical garden. In the center is a large unheated pool and a shallow one for children. Sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas are provided.

The hotel complex includes a one-story building with a reception, ten two-story villas and five main buildings with 4-5 storeys. On the territory there are several shops with souvenirs, jewelry, oils and perfumes, a currency exchange, laundry and dry cleaning (for an additional fee). There is an Internet cafe, there is Wi-Fi (paid).



There is a doctor who can provide emergency medical care. However, it must be remembered that if an insured event occurs, you must call the telephone number indicated in your insurance document.

If your departure is only in the evening and you need to clear the number earlier, there is a luggage room for your luggage in the administrative building.

Number of rooms

For guests of the Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort 3 stars, there are standard rooms for 2-3 people, apartments with a living room, a kitchen with kitchenware and a bedroom.

Spacious villas can accommodate a family or a group of six and are equipped with two bedrooms, a dining room, a large living room and a terrace.

Non-smoking and disabled rooms are available. Newspapers and services are available in the room (surcharge).



The standard room has a balcony, a bathroom with the necessary toiletries and a hairdryer, a TV with several Russian channels, a refrigerator, air conditioning, a telephone, a safe and a minibar, the use of which is paid.

Cleaning is done daily; bed linen is changed twice a week.

Food concept

Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort offers HB (half board with two meals and drinks at breakfast) and ALL (all inclusive with three meals and local drinks, except freshly squeezed juices and express coffee).

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the Zahabia restaurant according to the buffet system. Preparing dishes of national and European cuisine. Food for a three-star hotel is quite diverse and includes a good selection of fruits, vegetables, pastries, cold dishes, meat and fish.

In the morning you will be offered: cereals and cereals, several types of cheese and sausages, fresh and pickled salads, fried eggs, scrambled eggs and boiled eggs, beans, mashed potatoes, fresh and stewed vegetables.

Children really like pancakes with different types of jam or chocolate and all kinds of pastries. From drinks are offered water, juices, tea, brewed coffee and milk.

For dinner, two types of soup are usually served , rice and potatoes in different types, pasta with various fillings, two or three types of fish and meat dishes, fruits and vegetables fresh and stewed. Amazing selection of bakery products and sweets.

From drinks you can choose beer, red and white dry wines, local strong alcoholic drinks, tea, coffee and juices.

For dinner, the choice is almost the same as for lunch, sometimes grilled dishes are added.

If you are going on an excursion with an early departure, you will be prepared, upon reservation, a box with a dry breakfast.

The hotel bars offer alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks and light snacks. Fresh seasonal fruit juices are included in the special menu.

Beach

Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort is three hundred meters from the hotel, across the road. You can walk to him in 10-20 minutes on foot, or you can ride in a wagon drawn by a peaceful, quiet horse, or on a bus.

The hotel has two beaches: sandy with a convenient gentle entrance to the sea and coral, one of the best in this part of the city. You can enjoy colorful fish and underwater vegetation all day long; quite often tourists can see moray eels and huge stingrays.



On the beach there is a cafe, diving center, rental masks and fins.

The territory is landscaped and equipped with sunbeds, umbrellas and awnings. You can surf, ride a catamaran and a boat, go fishing, learn scuba diving.

On the beach, you can order a temporary henna tattoo with a pattern of your choice. The photographer and artist offer their services. You can order a photo session in memory of the rest in the most picturesque places of the hotel or your portrait.

Sports and entertainment for adults

The hotel has a large international animation team. Every evening, the main restaurant hosts entertainment shows and theme nights. Each race you can see a belly dance, a fakir show and a performance with cobras.

On the beach and near the pool, exercises, yoga, water aerobics, teaching oriental and Latin dances are carried out. You can play football, volleyball, basketball and boccia.

At night, the hotel often hosts discos, offers trips to Hurghada to visit famous clubs and discos, such as: Hard Rock Kafe, Little Budda, Papas Beach, Gavana and others.

Perhaps a free visit to the company store from the factory "El Baracca", where you will be offered to purchase the famous Egyptian medicinal and cosmetic oils and perfumes.

Paid services include: tennis, billiards, beauty services, sauna, Turkish bath and massage services, where experienced specialists will offer you several types of relaxing and therapeutic massage.



The Cleopatra wellness program, which includes a session in a sauna, a jacuzzi, a visit to a Turkish bath with full body peeling with coconut flakes and subsequent massage using aromatic oils, is very popular with hotel guests.

Conditions for children

For children there is a branch in the pool, a playground with swings, there is a kids club. There is a children's animator who specializes in games with toddlers, in the evenings a mini-disco is organized where children are taught to dance.

Horse and camel rides are available at an extra charge.

The restaurant has a children's menu and high chairs for children, and an extra bed can be provided in the room.

Accommodation with animals

Hotel policies prohibit check-in with pets.

Holiday Category

Zahabia Hotel Beach Resort Hurghada is recommended for economical holidays for families with children, youth and people of retirement age. As a rule, young people fall asleep in the morning and come to the beach for dinner, middle-aged and older people get up and go to bed early, so there is always enough space on the beach and hotel grounds.

The hotel is an excellent option for a beach, relaxing holiday or for an excursion program.
