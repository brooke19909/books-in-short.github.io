Janhot Village: Guesthouses "Janhot" and "Greenwood"

Among the resorts of the Black Sea coast, unique corners have been preserved, where there is an opportunity to relax in silence, enjoy the magnificent nature and the sea, and breathe in the healing air. One of these places is the small village of Janhot.



Why choose janhot?

Every year an increasing number of guests come to rest in Janhot. Such popularity is due to the fact that nature is unique. On the shores of the clean and warm sea there are pebbly furnished beaches. The wild beaches around the resort are very picturesque. Relic Pitsunda pines growing on the slopes of the gorge and on the seashore fill the local sea air with a special aroma and make it healing. The mountains, the Hocetai River, at the mouth of which a bay is formed, delight tourists. City dwellers, tired of noise and smog, come here for peace and silence, relax from the daily bustle.

The proximity of the village to Gelendzhik and convenient transport links with it allow vacationers to visit popular places in the resort town.

Guest houses (Janhot)

Guests in the village receive numerous hotels, apartments, there is a boarding house and children's health camps. Cozy accommodation is offered by guest houses. Dzhanhot - this is the place in which in recent years this type of hotel services has been rapidly developing. They are handled by small private firms and families. Great competition in the hotel industry of the resort village obliges their owners to invest in business development and increase the level of comfort. Therefore, guest houses (Janhot offers them in sufficient quantities) future guests begin to book long before the start of their vacation. At the same time, accommodation prices here are quite affordable.

The opportunity for tourists to live while relaxing on the seashore, to admire it from the window is a pleasant and convenient feature of such a resort as Dzhanhot. Guest houses by the sea are the hallmark of this place. This determines the popularity of the resort among many visitors.
