LCD "Bitsevsky hills": prices, reviews

Bitsevsky Hills is a residential complex under construction near Moscow, in the city of Vidnoe. A series of 5-17-storey buildings is being built in a privileged area, near the park area. A variety of studio apartments for design and footage allows you to choose an acceptable option at attractive rates.

The city of Vidnoye (Moscow region) is located close to the capital and is practically a part of it. The administrative boundary here is rather a convention. The historical center immersed in greenery, represented by old cottages, is located 3 kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road.

Near low-rise buildings are being built. Thanks to modern standards, the introduction of innovative building technologies, the level of comfort in them is higher than secondary housing. Green Zone

According to the architectural concept, the project received the status of a cultural and residential complex. It is made cultural by its proximity to a historical monument - the Timokhovo estate in the town of Vidnoe (Moscow region).

Restoration work involves the improvement of part of the territory of the prince's park and, in fact, the estate. An updated view of the ensemble will allow the ravines and hills that sheltered the park to be an important city attraction. The attractiveness of the Bitsevsky Hills for residents and investors naturally increases, so investments in landscaping the green zone are justified.

Landscape

The natural environment is an important attribute of the attractiveness of housing. The houses are being built close enough to the transport arteries of Vidnoye and the Don highway to visit Moscow without difficulty. At the same time, a wide forest belt lies between the highway and the complex.

Transport

The Bitsevsky Hills are conveniently located at the nearest point of entry from the capital into the historic center of Vidnoye. This location is ideal for using both personal transport and public transport. The road by car to the Moscow Ring Road and the Don highway will take 5 minutes. To the railway station "Rastorguevo" walk 10 minutes. To the ring metro (station "Paveletskaya") - half an hour by train, to "Tsaritsyno" - 12 km.

The main advantage of buyers consider attractive the cost of housing for such a complex and location. A number of apartments differ from the declared area (often up) by 2-3 m 2 . But buyers were warned in advance of this possibility. On average, reviews on the strong "four".
