How to use Sportmaster bonuses? Accumulative club cards in Sportmaster: bonuses for each purchase

The Sportmaster chain of stores has a unique club program. Each customer of the store has every chance of receiving a specialized Club card, with the help of which bonuses are accumulated. To get a card, it is enough to make a purchase in a network of sports stores in the amount of up to 15 thousand rubles.

Types of cards





Before using Sportmaster bonuses, you need to get one of the cards of the store chain on hand. Depending on the amount by which the purchase is made, the customer may receive a regular card, silver and gold.

  • Standard card - purchase amount up to 15 thousand rubles.
  • Silver card - the purchase amount should be from 15 thousand rubles to 150.
  • Gold card - purchase amount over 150 thousand rubles.

When making purchases in a chain of stores, cards will be credited with bonuses. Each bonus is equal to 1 ruble. Sportmaster stores offer bonuses on the map to pay for further purchases. In accordance with the discount program with the help of accrued bonuses, you can pay up to 30% of the value of the goods. The promotion applies to all stores in the network, with the exception of representative offices called Sportmaster Discount.

Before using Sportmaster bonuses, they must first be accumulated. The latter are accrued in parallel with each thousand rubles spent. The amount of points awarded will depend on the type of card.

Every year on March 1, the store conducts a complete reset of bonus accounts. This operation does not apply to cards that were issued in January and February of the current year.
