Golden Bay Beach Hotel 5 * (Cyprus, Larnaca): photos and reviews of tourists

Golden Bay Beach Hotel is a great option for relaxing on the sea coast. It offers comfortable rooms and excellent service.





Hotel Location

Resort Golden Tulip Golden Bay Beach Hotel 5 * is located a few meters from the beach. After a couple of hours by taxi or bus, you can visit Nicosia - the capital of Cyprus. The hotel is about a quarter hour drive from the center of Larnaca. This will provide you with distance from city noise, and at the same time, you can quickly access all the benefits of civilization.





Hotel Rooms

The resort Golden Bay Beach Hotel 5 * offers you accommodation in apartments of the following categories:

  • Standard rooms include double occupancy. They are a bedroom with en-suite bathroom. also has access to a private balcony. If necessary, you can book connecting rooms. You can choose an option with a view of the pool or the sea coast.
  • Mini- suites are luxurious apartments decorated with handmade wooden decor and designer furniture made from natural materials. The floors are white marble. The bedroom is equipped with a large French bed, as well as a full working area. The room has access to a private terrace with a small garden and a jacuzzi.
  • Grand Suite is a spacious two-room apartment. In addition to the bed, the bedroom has a working area. The living room has upholstered furniture and a dining table. The spacious furnished balcony offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.
  • The presidential suite offers you comfortable accommodation. The living room has upholstered furniture for relaxing, as well as a dining table. The bedroom has access to its own terrace, equipped with a jacuzzi, sun loungers and parasols.




Room Amenities

In the rooms of Golden Bay Beach Hotel guests have the opportunity to take advantage of the following list of amenities:

  • An individual air conditioning system will allow you to create a cool oasis in your room, even if there is heat outside the window;
  • there is a radio, thanks to which you can enjoy listening to local stations;
  • the combined bathroom is equipped with a shower, hairdryer, and is also equipped with bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • a safe with an electronic combination lock will ensure the safety of your valuables and documents;
  • the phone will provide you with communication with the administration and the restaurant, and also allow you to make international calls;
  • a TV connected to a satellite dish will fill your leisure with music and entertainment programs;
  • the minibar always provides drinking water, as well as alcoholic and carbonated drinks;
  • thanks to the presence of a coffee maker in the room, you can always treat yourself to a fragrant invigorating drink;
  • presidential apartments have game consoles.




Resort Infrastructure

Choosing a Golden Bay Beach Hotel 5 * resort for your relaxation (Cyprus, Larnaca), you can take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • at the hairdresser you can do a hairstyle, manicure, as well as take a course of cosmetic procedures;
  • if you want to have a good time, relax and improve your health, visit the steam sauna;
  • an ATM operates at the hotel to withdraw cash from plastic cards;
  • in the lobby of the hotel operates a currency exchange office;
  • luggage storage is provided;
  • On the territory there is a large garden with palm trees, flowers and other exotic plants;
  • the hotel has its own parking with 24-hour security;
  • shops with souvenirs, groceries and clothes are open.




Entertainment opportunities

Entertainment is an integral part of any holiday. In Golden Bay Beach Hotel 5 * you will be given the following options:

  • a large outdoor pool with water slides, a bar, as well as specially equipped sunbathing areas and a children's area;
  • Indoor heated pool opens for visitors in the cold season;
  • in the hotel building there are game rooms for both children and adults;
  • in the shade of the garden there is a playground with swings, horizontal bars and a sandbox;
  • evening entertainment shows are held every night in a pirate night club;
  • on the beach you can enjoy a lot of marine activities, as well as instructors in water sports;
  • in the daytime and in the evening, a team of professional animators will entertain you and your children.




For sports lovers

If you are a fan of sports and outdoor activities, then at Golden Tulip Golden Bay Beach Hotel you can use the following services:

  • the gym is equipped with all necessary simulators for full-fledged training (it is also possible to get an instructor consultation);
  • group aerobics classes are held outdoors;
  • there are several ping-pong tables on site;
  • you can learn archery and even participate in competitions;
  • two modern tennis courts equipped with lights for playing at night;
  • Bicycle rental is available.
  • golf enthusiasts can arrange trips to a nearby golf course.

Service for guests

A resort such as Larnaca is very popular with tourists. Golden Bay Beach Hotel provides guests with the following list of services:

  • in the business center, guests have access to office equipment;
  • Guests enjoy free sun beds, umbrellas and towels to relax by the pool or beach;
  • Guests can use free wireless Internet throughout the hotel;
  • hotel staff will assist you in organizing and conducting celebrations;
  • during your absence at the hotel, a qualified nanny will look after your children;
  • in the laundry you can be provided with laundry and ironing services;
  • if necessary, guests are provided with a transfer from the airport and back;
  • Hotel guests can rent a car to explore the island on their own.

Catering establishments

The following establishments are responsible for the full nutrition of the guests of the Golden Bay Beach Hotel resort complex:

  • Les Etoiles Restaurant serves a buffet breakfast with a huge selection of international dishes. All the rest of the time you can order dishes from the French menu. An extensive wine list is also presented to your attention.
  • Throughout the day, you can enjoy light snacks and a variety of salads in the Yacht Club restaurant. It often hosts parties in the Cypriot style.
  • A huge selection of dishes for lunch and dinner is available at Thalassa Restaurant. It is worth noting the presence of a special vegetarian menu.
  • If you want to take a closer look at Greek cuisine, then visit the open-air restaurant Ouzeri. Special attention deserves the interior of the institution, made in the Byzantine style.
  • Sports enthusiasts will have a great time at the Shakespear Pub, where you can watch football and have a glass of beer.
  • The pool bar will allow you to enjoy your favorite drinks without leaving the water;
  • In the cocktail bar you can taste the most original and delicious drinks. Live piano music always sounds here.




Loyalty program

Golden Bay Beach Hotel has developed a loyalty program for the following categories of guests:

  • The hotel pays special attention to the newlyweds. Upon arrival, they receive a bottle of champagne, as well as a basket of fruits and sweets. If the couple stays at the hotel for a long time, then she is given a free romantic dinner in one of the restaurants.
  • Regular guests, as well as guests staying in a representative apartment, receive fruits and local sweets as a gift.
  • If your birthday falls during your stay at the hotel, you will receive a birthday cake as a compliment.

Golden Bay Beach Hotel 5 * (Cyprus): positive reviews

This resort hotel is very popular among tourists, because it is characterized by a lot of positive aspects. So, from the reviews of tourists you can get the following information:

  • a very convenient location (on the one hand, the distance from the noisy city, and on the other, you can quickly get to the resort center);
  • large bright rooms with a comfortable layout;
  • on the beach, the entrance to the sea is very convenient - without sharp stones and sharp drops in depth;
  • You can reserve a sun lounger for the entire stay, which saves you from having to take a seat early in the morning;
  • the tour desk organizes very interesting tours of the holy places;
  • Friendly staff who are fluent in Russian;
  • the food is tasty and varied;
  • very beautiful seascape from the windows of the rooms; direct access to the beach from the hotel;
  • get close to the airport, which is very important after a tiring flight;
  • good signal and wireless internet speed;
  • at the slightest cooling in the pool, heating is turned on;
  • a good cosmetic kit in the bathroom;
  • a lot of entertainment for children;
  • every day a bottle of drinking water is provided free of charge;
  • upon arrival, guests receive a pleasant gift in the form of a bottle of local wine;
  • The SPA center provides excellent facial skin care treatments.

Golden Bay Beach Hotel (Cyprus): negative reviews

Based on the reviews of tourists, the following negative moments of relaxation in this hotel can be distinguished:

  • if you like a relaxing holiday, then this option is not for you, since the hotel usually has a lot of families with children;
  • not the best quality of cleaning the rooms;
  • too spicy food in a restaurant (not suitable for those who are used to diet food);
  • the hotel area is too small;
  • the price includes full board without the possibility of excluding dinner;
  • the city center cannot be reached on foot;
  • the magnetic card from the number does not work well;
  • there is very strong audibility between the rooms, and noise from the corridor also interferes with sleep;
  • too little entertainment for young and active tourists;
  • rather "shabby" repair in the rooms;
  • the hotel atmosphere does not match the declared five stars;
  • in the evenings, music plays late in this and a neighboring hotel, and therefore you will not be able to fall asleep early;
  • there are no employees on the beach to help you open an umbrella or deal with a sun lounger;
  • bed linen and towels are quite old and shabby;
  • the mattresses on the beds are very old and hard (in addition, everything is stained);
  • in the minibar - expired drinks;
  • nearby are farms from which a characteristic odor is periodically heard;
  • during lunch, waiters rush to pick up plates, even if guests have not finished eating;
  • during eviction, an extra bill may become an unpleasant surprise (it is worthwhile to warn about paid services even during registration).

General impression

A quite popular accommodation option is Golden Bay Beach Hotel 5 * (Larnaca). This is one of the best hotels in this resort. Nevertheless, he does not always justify his high status. The five-star hotel was awarded, most likely, for the number of services provided, but not for the quality. Therefore, if you expect a luxurious atmosphere in the rooms and delicacies for breakfast, then this hotel is not suitable for you. Nevertheless, if you do not pay attention to small flaws, but want to enjoy a clean and warm sea, as well as friendly service, then safely book a tour.

It is worth noting that for active youth in the Hotel Golden Bay Beach (Cyprus, Larnaca) there is not so much entertainment. In addition, it is located quite far from the center of Larnaca. This option is most often chosen by families with children, since it is for young tourists that a lot of opportunities are provided here. Also, this is a great option for people who want to relax from the bustle of the city and admire the beautiful sea views, and just lovers of a calm and measured pastime.
