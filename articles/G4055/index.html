LCD "Mitino Park", "PIK": apartments, address, reviews

Moscow is a multi-million city, a city of temptations and unique opportunities, attracting people from different parts of the country. Thousands of people come here every year in order to improve their living conditions, to achieve the set heights in their careers and to arrange their own destiny. That is why the housing problem for Muscovites and visiting residents of the city is most acute. It would seem that construction does not stop here, the city is rapidly rebuilding and expanding, but not all new buildings can satisfy the demanding taste of modern buyers. LCD Mitino Park (PIK), reviews of which will form the basis of this review, is the project that we want to present to all potential buyers. If you are in the process of choosing an apartment, most likely this review will attract your special attention.





about the project

The concept that is so popular these days is the harmonious combination of the comforts of metropolitan life and the charms of living outside the city that formed the basis of the Mitino Park complex (PIK). Six monolithic buildings, its own kindergarten and school are what are in the project. It will become the largest in the Krasnogorsk region and will provide a huge number of Muscovites with separate apartments.

Location

For the construction of the Mitino Park residential complex, PIK GC chose the Krasnogorsk district of the Moscow region, a piece of land that is only a 10-minute walk from the Mitino metro station. In the north-west, a new residential neighborhood is bordered by Moscow, namely the Mitino district. Thanks to the reconstruction of the Pyatnitsky highway, the emergence of new road junctions, the road from the complex to the center of the capital will take no more than 30 minutes.





Technology

Economy class buildings are being built using monolithic technology, which gives them the necessary quality and reliability. For cladding of facades multi-colored hinged panels are used. In addition to water, gas, electricity, a telephone and Internet are connected to the house, which is included in the list of requirements of modern buyers.

Construction is carried out using high-quality materials and innovative technologies: two-chamber double-glazed windows of European manufacture are installed in the apartments, balconies and loggias are glazed, the entrances are equipped with high-speed passenger and freight elevators.

Infrastructure

The complex is being built in an area with developed infrastructure. In walking distance from the residential complex "Mitino Park" ("PIK") there is a kindergarten and a comprehensive school where residents of the micro-district can easily arrange their children. By the way, this feature has largely affected the popularity of the Moscow region complex. Judging by the reviews, he is preferred, including families with young children. Kids will no longer have to travel a huge distance, get on public transport - they will be able to get the necessary knowledge not far from home.





Only 10-15 minutes away are the largest shopping and entertainment complexes of the area: Mega-Khimki, Your House, Crocus City Mall - where you can not only make the necessary purchases, but also have a great time with your family and friends . Do you prefer outdoor activities? Interested in winter varieties? At your service are the slopes of the Snezhkom ski resort.

Ecology

For modern residents of the metropolis, the question of the ecological state of the area chosen for life, comes to the fore. That is why many acquire cottages away from the capital in order to spend at least two days a week in the fresh air. Residential complex Mitino Park (PIK), whose address is Krasnogorsk, Dubravnaya Street, is an excellent opportunity, without leaving the complex, to enjoy the peace, tranquility and regularity of life.





The Mitino landscape park, which is just a 10-minute walk from the complex, is its main decoration. Many buyers who are skeptical of new buildings outside the MKAD, having visited a chic forest park, did not want to leave here, which is why they preferred this particular complex.

The territory of the park is characterized by a variety of relief: high hills are replaced by spacious plains and valleys. The forest park has already become a favorite place for the first residents of the complex for walks, outdoor recreation. In addition to walking shady alleys, the park has excellent playgrounds and sports grounds, as well as a full-fledged boat station where you can rent a boat and go for a walk through the ponds.

Beautification

On the territory of the Mitino Park complex (PIK) large-scale works will be carried out to improve the adjacent territory: there will be playgrounds and sports grounds, walking paths and full-fledged recreation areas with gazebos and benches. An impressive part of the territory of the new complex will be reserved for parking - each car owner will find a free place for himself when he returns home.

Apartments, layouts

Excellent planning solutions are offered by the Mitino Park (PIK) residential complex. The apartments turned out to be bright and spacious. A choice of apartments ranging from 29 to 101 square meters, mainly represented by one- and two-bedroom options. Three and four bedroom apartments are designed for large and friendly families, which, thanks to proper space planning, will provide comfort to every household.





Finish

Apartments in "Mitino Park" ("PIC"), the photos of which clearly show the compliance of the plans with the preferences of customers, are rented out without finishing. But if desired, the company's specialists will prepare an individual design project and perform turnkey finishing using innovative materials. All those who took advantage of this proposal emphasize that the cost of finishing work is much lower than that offered by contractors and brigades.

The cost of apartments

At the moment, the complex is under construction, its final commissioning is scheduled for 2019. That is why everyone has the opportunity to purchase an apartment in a house under construction with significant benefits. The price per square meter of the complex starts at 87,000 rubles. You can buy an apartment not only for cash, but also using a profitable mortgage program.





Is it worth buying?

The apartments in the Mitino Park residential complex really turned out to be bright, spacious, they fully satisfy all the requirements of modern buyers. Through the use of advanced technologies and innovations, it is possible to build high-quality, reliable houses, provide the necessary heat and sound insulation properties, reducing the cost of further house maintenance and the amount of monthly payments. Today it is one of the few complexes that combine impeccable quality, developed infrastructure with affordable cost. Yes, and the complex is located just 3 km from the Moscow Ring Road, within walking distance from the metro station, so its residents will definitely not experience any inconvenience.

Excellent project of large-scale residential development, awarded the highest ratings. Be sure to take a closer look at it and at least once visit the construction site.
