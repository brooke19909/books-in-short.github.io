Card classification by credit schemes

Credit cards from VTB 24 can be classified according to a number of criteria. There are cards under which the client gets the opportunity to repay the loan on preferential terms. Who can get a credit card?

If, say, some US banks are ready to provide banking services related to loans to non-residents of the country, then a VTB 24 credit card can only be issued to residents of the Russian Federation. The minimum age of a client for processing bank products related to loans is 21 years. Borrower solvency is the most important criterion for VTB 24. A credit card, the terms of issuance of which are prescribed by the bank fairly transparently, is issued to those borrowers who have a regular income, preferably due to work executed according to the Labor Code.





Credit history is also an important criterion for making a positive decision on issuing cards.
