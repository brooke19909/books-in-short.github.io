How to get to the hotel

From Kamran International Airport, you first need to get to Nha Trang. Then you need to cross to Bamboo Island. This can be done by two modes of transport: in the cabin of the world's longest cableway (3320 meters) or on a high-speed boat. Unfortunately, both the cable car station and the pier are located on the outskirts of Nha Trang. Therefore, you will need to take a taxi from the city center. But for tourists who check into the Vinpearl Resort Nha Trang 5 * hotel, travel by water and by air will be free. The registration procedure is already taking place on the continent. You make a deposit (you need to pick it up at the reception on the island), after which they take your luggage and issue an electronic key to the room. The boat dock on the island is located at the Winpearl Nha Trang Bay Resort Villas Hotel, and the cable car station is located directly in the amusement park. Pools and the sea

Vinpearl Resort Nha Trang 5 * hotel is located on the front page of its own beach. Sun beds, umbrellas, beach towels - all this is free for hotel guests. The sandy coast is carefully cleaned from possible shells, corals and algae. Lifeguards are on duty at the beach from dawn to dusk. Sunset at sea gentle.
