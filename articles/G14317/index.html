Vardana. Reviews of tourists about holidays on the Black Sea coast

Many of us strive to relax abroad, believing that there the sea is cleaner and more comfortable. But we have many places where you can relax no worse. The Black Sea coast is a whole string of magnificent resorts, among which is the small cozy village of Vardane. The reviews of those who have already been here say that this is a modern resort offering excellent conditions for a magnificent and unforgettable vacation. Vardan is part of Greater Sochi, the distance to which is 30 kilometers.



The main advantage of the resort is its location and the climate zone of humid subtropics. The village is located directly at the mouth of the Buu River . This is a great place to relax from the noise of the city. At the same time, it is not a village forgotten by God called Vardane. Reviews of tourists show that there is a fairly developed infrastructure. There are many kinds of shops, a school, a clinic, greenhouses, a livestock farm, several recreation centers and much more.

It is impossible not to note the nature that surrounds Vardana. Reviews about her vacationers are extremely positive. Local landscapes are magnificent and amaze with a riot of greenery. It combines evergreen mixed forests that adorn the mountains and alpine meadows, saturated with aromas of herbs and flowers. Therefore, rest in the village is very good for health. Thanks to the mountains that surround Vardana, cold air does not penetrate here. The outdoor season opens in May and closes only in October.





But this is not all that is attractive for a vacation in Vardan. Reviews of tourists confirm that a clean pebble beach with a length of 500 meters is well equipped here. Vacationers on the beach expect excellent cafes and restaurants with a wide selection of dishes. For children and adults, all kinds of attractions work both on land and on water. A place has been set up where shopping malls are installed where souvenirs can be purchased. Those who love extreme sports can go kayaking on mountain rivers, take part in a rally on SUVs or go diving.

Choosing a place to stay in Vardan on vacation, each tourist usually considers several accommodation options. This allows you to most fully satisfy all your wishes. In most cases, the accommodation preferred by the guests of Vardane is hotels, mostly small ones. In addition, guest houses located in close proximity to the sea are also popular. It offers excellent service and many additional services at affordable prices. Those who prefer a traditional holiday can choose one of the local guest houses.





A huge number of vacationers choose the private sector for accommodation in Vardan, since the cost of living in it is much lower. The real estate market in this village is developed quite well, in this regard, the choice of accommodation options is huge. In addition, there are sites where advertisements for the surrender of the private sector are posted in Vardan with a description of the living conditions. Photos can also be attached here, which allows you to make the choice that most suits your criteria and wishes.
