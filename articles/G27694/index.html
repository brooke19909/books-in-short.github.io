Hotel Armas Belek Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Belek): description and reviews

Traveling is the dream of many people who will sooner or later realize their plans. When choosing a direction, an analysis of exactly how one wants to spend time is needed: to get new knowledge from beautifully narrated guides, visit art galleries or fascinating museums, soak up the sun, swim in the sea, or get in touch with wildlife away from civilization. The choice is not easy, however, deciding to combine a comfortable stay with the warm waters of the Mediterranean, Turkey, which is full of a variety of resorts, is often preferred. One of them is Belek.

They managed to gain a reputation as the most expensive holiday destination thanks to the presence of extra-class golf courses. They determine the significant influx of holidaymakers in the winter months. The main season favorable for swimming is the period from May to October. The gentle rays of the hot sun, a light sea breeze, pine and eucalyptus forests allow the body to renew, recharge with positive energy and increase its immunity.





The five-star Armas Belek Hotel 5 * hotel, the photo of which can be found in the article, according to the reviews of tourists, combines high-quality service, a number of interesting entertainments, comfortable living conditions and an amazing variety of food.

general characteristics

Hotel Armas Belek Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Belek), the photo of which you have the opportunity to see in the article, was commissioned in 2006, the last renovation was made in 2014. The total area is 40,000 square meters. The territory is well-groomed, alleys, flower beds, urns - inalienable attributes of numerous squares. The nearest airport is Antalya International Airport - 35 km.

The main building of the Armas Beach Hotel 5 * (Belek Soho Beach - former name) has three floors, and numerous cottages for living are represented by two-story buildings surrounded by green lawns and deciduous trees.





Pets are not allowed. Smoking is strictly prohibited in apartments and public indoor facilities. It is possible to add an extra bed to the rooms, but prior coordination with the administration is required. Check-in takes place no earlier than 14:00, check-out no later than 12:00. The staff speaks four languages: Russian, English, German and Turkish.

A distinctive feature is the care of people with disabilities. Comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary are at their disposal, where the stay will be calm, comfortable and cozy.

Guest Accommodation

Number of rooms Hotel Armas Belek Hotel 5 * (Belek) is represented by 367 apartments, among which the following categories are highlighted:

  • Standard Room;
  • Eco Garden Room;
  • Family Room.

The interior has a classic focus. The color scheme is mainly represented by light shades of white and beige colors. The floors are covered with laminate or ceramic tiles. There is access to a balcony equipped with chairs for tea and air baths. The ground floors have a private terrace. Good illumination is achieved through wall sconces and floor lamps. French glazing also plays an important role, which together with a large mirror visually increase the room space. Possible views: garden, pools and sea.





Towels are changed and maids are cleaned daily in the morning. Bed linen is changed twice a week.

Housing Features

The furniture is represented by a standard set made of quality wood, and includes: beds, a table, a spacious wardrobe, an armchair, a stool and bedside tables. The presence of individual air conditioning, LCD TV with Russian-language channels, telephone, electric kettle and hairdryer contributes to a comfortable stay in the apartment. There is a tea set consisting of tea bags, sugar and coffee - updated once a week. Upon arrival, guests will find a minibar filled with drinking mineral water and soft drinks. The safe provides a carefree stay, and its use is included in the price.

The bathroom is decorated in accordance with modern trends, equipped with a shower or bath. All necessary hygiene products, such as shampoo, soap, shower gel, toilet paper, are located on the sink and must be updated when cleaning rooms.





Nutrition

Hotel Armas Belek Hotel 5 * received positive reviews in 2017 for catering. The all-inclusive concept, which the hotel adheres to, implies serving a buffet five times a day according to a predetermined schedule. The main restaurant has a modest design, the walls and columns are decorated with interesting works of local painters. The chairs are simple but soft. Meals are available both indoors and on the outdoor terrace. According to the dress code of the institution, visits to swimming trunks, swimsuits and pareos are prohibited.

Two additional restaurants specializing in the preparation of fish and national dishes are open for visits in the evening, free of charge, require a preliminary reservation of the table.

You can quench your thirst in the 24-hour lobby bar. The bars by the pool, beach and disco have a clear schedule. All drinks of local production, alcoholic and non-alcoholic, ice cream, snacks, pastries and other goodies are included in the All Inclusive system. Only alcohol in glass bottles, imported drinks and freshly squeezed juices require additional payment.

The main restaurant has a buffet for children, available for lunch and dinner. Baby Corner contains a microwave, blender and bottle warmer. Highchairs are provided.

Beach

Armas Hotel Belek is located 100 meters from the Mediterranean Sea on the first coastline. A wide beach with velvet-golden sand, transparent crystal clear waters with a convenient entrance contribute to a pleasant time spent under the rays of the warming sun. Sand is cleaned and leveled daily in the evening using specialized equipment.

An integral component of the beach infrastructure is the lifeguard service, which tirelessly keep watch over the order and safety of swimmers. The presence of chaise lounges with soft mattresses, awnings and umbrellas, showers and locker rooms make the rest favorable not only for sun lovers, but also for those who prefer shade. There is a desk with beach towels, which are provided free of charge.
