Hotels with pool in Anapa: description and reviews of tourists

One of the most favorite vacation spots on the Black Sea is the resort city of Anapa. The warm gentle sea, the southern sun and the hospitality of comfortable hotels will make your stay here bright and unforgettable. And if you do not go to the beach on a hot afternoon, hotels with a pool are at your service in Anapa.

Hotel "Cote d'Azur"

The three-star hotel "Cote d'Azur" has a landscaped protected area. Its own sandy beach, equipped with awnings, sun loungers, rescue and medical posts, is only 50 meters from the hotel. Guests can use the services of an outdoor pool, Finnish bath, gym. You can rent bicycles, scooters, strollers. For playing sports on the territory there are sports fields, there are tennis tables. Young guests can spend their free time in the playground or in the games room. Three meals a day are served in the on-site restaurant. Vacationers leave their vehicles in free parking. Within walking distance is a supermarket, market, many cafes. The Cote d'Azur is a great option for those who are interested in Anapa all-inclusive hotels with a pool. Guests are offered accommodation in the following rooms:

  • double economy class (ground floor);
  • double standard (there is no balcony and with a balcony);
  • single standard (without balcony);
  • four-room two-room with a balcony;
  • in a deluxe room;
  • in deluxe room;
  • in the apartment.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, there is free WI-FI. The hotel "Cote d'Azur" is located in Anapa at the address: Pionersky Prospekt, 234.

Hotel "Golden Dunes"

In a hotel built in 1991, tourists will find accommodation in two- and three-story multi-colored buildings. If you are considering hotels in Anapa with a swimming pool on the first line, Golden Dunes is just what you need. Its own sandy protected beach is equipped with umbrellas, sun loungers, there are playgrounds for beach volleyball and beach soccer. The hotel has a swimming pool, gym. Private parking is available for a fee. The hotel is all-inclusive. The restaurant serves a buffet. Young tourists will find entertainment to their liking in the kids club. There is a playground on the street for them. Tour services are available. Your holiday will be decorated with funny animators. Within walking distance there are shops, markets and cafes. The following numbers are offered for accommodation:

  • standard;
  • family apartments;
  • suites.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. The hotel "Golden Dunes" is located in Anapa at the address: Pionersky Prospekt, 223.

Hotels in Anapa with a pool. Hotel Nostalgie

Hotel Nostalgie is located in a new modern four-story building. A large heated pool with a sun terrace has sun loungers, two showers and a bathroom. The beach is a short walk away. Within walking distance are several shops, cafes, canteens, as well as a market. Hotel Olympus

If you are interested in hotels with an all-inclusive system in Anapa with a swimming pool, the Olymp Hotel welcomes you. For guests here is offered three meals a day, which is served on a buffet system. There is a special menu for children. Intermediate meals are also organized, which allows vacationers to enjoy confectionery, drinks and various pastries between main meals. Club Hotel "White Beach"

Are you looking for a beachfront hotel in Anapa with a pool? At your service club-hotel "White Beach". In a modern complex consisting of detached two-story houses, tourists will find everything necessary for a good rest. The restaurant serves traditional Russian cuisine, and there is a bar by the outdoor pool. In the children's pool, the water is heated. Guests can use the services of a sauna, massage services, free secure parking. On its own sandy beach there are sun loungers, deck chairs, umbrellas, changing cabins, a toilet, a volleyball court.
