Not everyone has their own apartments, renting a house in such a large city as St. Petersburg is quite expensive, which is why sooner or later the question arises of buying your own apartment. Currently, new buildings are becoming increasingly popular. LCD "Colors of Summer", being built in the northern part of the city, a vivid representative of modern complexes. As part of this material, we will evaluate the project from all sides.





about the project

LCD "Colors of Summer" (St. Petersburg) - a large-scale development project of the Vsevolozhsk district of the northern capital. The first phase of the complex is represented by 19 buildings of various shapes. A variety of planning solutions, a unique concept, well-developed infrastructure and affordable cost of apartments are what the first real estate buyers appreciated. Most of the apartments are currently sold, but you still have a chance to become the happy owner of a great apartment.

LCD "Colors of Summer" fully lives up to its name, the facades of houses will be decorated in bright colors.
