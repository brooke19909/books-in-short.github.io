For children and adults

Exciting creativity to drown. The scheme of work in it is a certain sequence of tensioning the threads on a solid base with the help of holes or studs. The whole image is based on the sequential filling of the contours of the details of the picture by pulling colored or plain threads over pre-arranged hooks - holes or studs.
