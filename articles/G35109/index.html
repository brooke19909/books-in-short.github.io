Pagination is ... Definition, features, requirements and walkthrough

How many terms are there that are unknown to people? With the development of technology and everything connected with the Internet, there are more and more concepts every day. Even previously familiar terms acquire a new meaning and are used by other specialists in a completely different field. Pagination is just the concept that has migrated from publishing to Internet marketing and has become an integral part of it.

General

Immediately you need to determine the general pagination in publishing and pagination in Internet marketing. In both cases, the term comes from the Latin word "page". We can conditionally say that the initial concept practically did not change its meaning. Pagination switched from paper to electronic format and in some cases somewhat changed its specialization.

Initial definition

So, in this case, it is believed that pagination is a multi-valued term that somehow relates to pages.



But still, initially the world learned about this concept from publishing. There he describes the sequential pagination, which is implemented through the columns. They can be placed at the top, bottom and even on the sides.

In simple terms, in publishing, pagination is the usual marginal numbers in books, documents, magazines, etc.

Automatic

Continuing the topic of publishing, you can find out that there are several types of such numbering. Automatic pagination of text with illustrations is formed according to complex rules and according to a special algorithm that independently determines where it is necessary to form a page break.

In this case, the rules should not forget about other norms, for example, on hanging lines. This is a fairly strict rule in the publishing house, from which many editors have recently begun to depart. Moreover, it is not clear for what reason: either from ignorance, or from laziness, or in protest.

A dangling line is a term that describes the end line of a paragraph that hits the beginning of a line or column. Also, the hanging line can be called the first line of the paragraph, which turned out to be ‚Äúlost‚Äù and left on the previous page.

As a result, to prevent this from happening, automatic pagination algorithms monitor this. Thus, the semantic constituent parts of the content are together, without separation from each other.

Separate

Such pagination is a bibliographic term approved by GOST. It is a classic manifestation of this definition. Designates a separate page numbering for each article, part of the collection, chapters of the book.



Together with this term, the concept of "foliation" appears. It is interpreted as numbering, but not of each page, but of each sheet. Hence the similar terminology (translated from Latin folio, this is ‚Äúpage‚Äù).

Development

Further, our term appears in the basics of web programming. Pagination is used by designers who interpret it differently. For them, this term means showing a certain amount of information on one web page. For example, in the search results show 10 pages. The forum can be used 20-30 threads, etc.

In this case, pagination serves as a separator of large amounts of information and is implemented through the navigation block, which helps you navigate to other pages.

Problems

So now that you know SEO, understanding what pagination is will be easier. As mentioned earlier, this term in web programming means almost the same as in publishing. Sites also have their own serial numbering, which can be up or down. Often we meet her on the pages of an online store with goods, when by filtering a particular product we are given a whole list of models.

It so happened that pagination for internal optimization is an important point. With her, questions and two main problems can often arise:

  • Limited work of the search robot.
  • Duplicates

Limitations

So, if the correct pagination is configured on your site, but without taking into account possible problems, then you will encounter limited work of robots.

When they visit your site to scan new pages and add them to the index, they do it in different modes. Depending on the site, its depth, the number of elements, such work may take them more or less time. If you have a young resource on which content rarely appears, then robots will come to you less and less, and scanning will become superficial.

If you have pagination pages, then, most likely, robots will not go through each of them. Accordingly, not all products will be indexed, will fall into the search results. All this will lead to low sales figures, because a potential buyer will not even know that there is such a toy in your online store.



Duplicates

The next problem is with duplicates. Of course, it all depends on the structure of your resource and how well you thought out its content. But often it happens that pagination pages have similar or identical content.

For example, on sale there is a machine of a certain model and manufacturer, but in several colors. Agree, it happens that the client is looking for a specific product, indicating all the details about it, right down to the color. Therefore, having separate pages for products that differ only in color is good, but dangerous.

All this leads to duplicates. You begin to find identical meta tags. Duplicate content may be misinterpreted by the search engine. It‚Äôs difficult for her to determine how important this or that page is to you and which one is more relevant.

Manually

So, the first option to solve these problems is to get rid of pagination by pagination by removing it from the index. To do this, use the noindex tag.



Most often, this option is advised to use, because it is quickly and easily implemented. Thanks to this method, you can easily exclude from the search results all pagination elements, except the main one.

To apply this option, you need to add a special meta tag to every page except the first. This command must be placed in a block.

This tag will help us remove all pagination pages from the index, except the main page. But the indexation of each product will still be carried out.

Using this option, you need to remember a couple of points. Firstly, if you have a description of the main page of the catalog, then it should only be on the main page. Secondly, you need to monitor the duplication of "url".

This solution has both pros and cons. It is believed that this option is suitable for the Yandex search engine, and it is also quite simple to implement. But among the shortcomings, you can find that so you can "throw out" additional content from the index.

conclusions

So interesting was the term being monitored. All you need to know for an ordinary user who hears this concept for the first time: pagination is an ordinal numbering.
