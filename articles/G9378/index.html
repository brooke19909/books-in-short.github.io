Holiday Village "Green Glade", Belgorod: description and reviews

On the banks of the Seversky Donets, which is a tributary of the Don, is the glorious city of Belgorod. This is a large industrial city with many historical and architectural monuments, an outpost of Russia. Belgorod is a city of military valor and glory. Rich, beautiful nature, which is densely populated by various animals, there are many oak forests, meadows and springs with mineral water with a unique composition. Belgorod Oblast and its capital are known for their clean air and temperate continental climate, which is suitable for recreation and recreation. In winter, strong winds blow from the Arctic seas, and summers can be arid, with thunderstorms and rain showers. The abundance of forests has a positive effect on the health of those who do not tolerate the scorching sun of hot countries. There are many camp sites and recreation centers in Belgorod where you can enjoy the tranquil beauty of lakes and meadow grasses.

Rest on the shore of the Seversky Donets

Summer is just around the corner, and with it comes the long-awaited vacation. How to spend it? You can wave to the sea, but there is a place in Belgorod where you can have a wonderful time not only on holidays, but also on weekends. P. "Zelenaya Polyana" in Belgorod (a recreation center, it is also called a cottage village) invites everyone who wants to relax in a quiet comfortable place for health and enjoy the beauty of the south of the Russian plain, its rivers, streams, to breathe the fresh air of Belgorod oak forests. On the bank of the Seversky Donets River, in the clean, green zone of Belgorod, this base stands, and you can come here as a family or even as a team.

The base is easily accessible not only by car, but also by public transport. There is a bus from Belgorod to Zelenaya Polyana. For a personal car there is a spacious guarded parking lot. Reviews testify: nothing will happen to the car. One way or another, arriving at this base, you will receive a charge of vivacity, a wonderful opportunity to relax from the bustle of the city and the monotony of weekdays. Clean air and rich nature will restore strength and health.

One day rest in gazebos

On the territory of the base are a dozen cozy houses suitable for living and spending the night for several days. There are several accommodation options. But even a one-day vacation can be beneficial. For this you only need to order a guest gazebo, which is located at some distance from the houses themselves. Pavilions are different in capacity and are designed for a company from 2 to 15 people.

They have benches, a long table and, of course, a barbecue. Having secured such an arbor, you can be sure: a fruitful holiday on a Saturday or Sunday is guaranteed. A wedding or corporate event can be easily celebrated in a house with a banquet hall. It has everything you need for such an event, even a fireplace. And in the rooms for six people there is a stove for cooking.

Full rest in houses

A full two-week vacation needs to rent a house. They have a luxury class and can be one-, two-, three-room for families with children or groups of friends from two to eight people. A corporate party or a birthday requires more space, and a four-room suite will work best. In all rooms there are beds, tables, chairs, there are even rocking chairs.



All houses are equipped with radiators, and if clouds appear in the sky and it rains, you can turn on additional heating. In the room you can see a refrigerator, TV, there is a bathroom with a toilet. In the summer heat, going to the beach is a fabulous pleasure, a sandy beach on the banks of the Seversky Donets is waiting for guests to swim and sunbathe.

Outdoor activities

Do you like outdoor activities? Excellent! At your service is a volleyball and soccer field where you can drive the ball with your whole family or company, divided into two teams. If you forgot the volleyball at home, then it is not a problem to rent at the base for 100 rubles.

Little children will be delighted with the playground with a slide, a sandbox and a swing. Teenagers will love table tennis, it is also available at the box office. And venerable vacationers will pay attention to Russian billiards. The reviews say that around the Zelenaya Polyana base near Belgorod there are beautiful places and many paths, go jogging or Nordic walking, and there are no extra pounds. And the charge of vigor from such a walk will remain for a long time.
