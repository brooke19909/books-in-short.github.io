How to make plastic earrings and always be in the spotlight

Each girl has a secret chest in which her favorite trinkets are stored. Among these trinkets, for sure, there are earrings, and rings, bracelets and beads, which are dressed for different occasions as bright accessories.

Often jewelry should look simple, without too much sparkle and sparkling rhinestones. In such cases, plastic or plastic clay comes to the rescue, with which you can create unique jewelry for everyday wear.

Today, numerous pages are full of articles on how to make earrings with your own hands, and this is no accident. After all, creating jewelry that meets the taste is very simple. You just need to stock up on the necessary tools and materials.

How to make earrings from plastic, if it is not at hand? Further, so as not to raise the question of how to make earrings, it is worth stocking up with cloves or other fasteners to create earrings on which finished products are easily attached. As well as all the necessary tools for rolling and cutting clay, patterns and varnish for coating, sequins and rhinestones, feathers and rags of fabric. All this can help in creating unique, beautiful and catchy earrings that can not be found in anyone in the city.

How to make earrings from plastic? To do this, select the form that they will have. This issue will be raised later, while the technique of working with plastic clay will be described. It is necessary to take the required piece and roll it to the desired thickness, depending on the type of earrings and the thickness can vary. When rolling clay, you can use one color or mix two or more to create iridescent shades and a blurred mass that will look spectacular on the ears.

After the clay has become soft and plastic, you can sculpt the desired shape from it. What form to choose? Let the imagination tell you. It can be a variety of flowers, bows, animals and birds, food, utensils. All that you want to hang on your ears, even if it will be even noodles, can be fashioned from clay and create charming earrings. When clay, under the influence of fingers, has taken the desired shape, it needs to be connected to the fastener and placed in the oven.

After future earrings have been heat treated, they can be removed and cooled. What to do next? If you want to leave the earrings matte, then you can safely put them in your ears and go to your girlfriends. If you want to add shine and gloss, you will need to cover them with varnish. you can sprinkle with glitters or add another accessory that begs for the received earrings.

How to make earrings from plastic quickly and beautifully, there is no need to tell. This is only a matter of technology, as well as imagination. Someone likes to experiment, someone adheres to the classics, but it is easy to look beautiful with plastic.

Today, every girl can master this technique and surprise herself and loved ones with her abilities. After all, beauty is an abstract category, and everyone creates it around him. So let the flowers be on the ears, the bows are on the neckline, and the fingers are ringed with variegated butterflies.

With the help of plastic clay, you can easily add colors to everyday life, as well as fill your innermost chest with outlandish little things only by figuring out how to make plastic earrings. The main thing is that the imagination does not leave in the process of creating jewelry, and a good mood always inspires new ideas and achievements. And plastic clay will always come to the rescue and make the creation process simple, quick, diverse and exciting.
