Grand Astor Hotel 4 - one of the pearls of the Mediterranean coast of Turkey

Description : On the Mediterranean coast, in the Turkish resort center of Alanya, the Grand Astor Hotel 4 was built in 1990. After 20 years, the hotel had planned repairs. The hotel is 121 km from the international airport and 1.1 km from the new urban area of ​​Tosmur.





Alanya is a developed city with a wide network of services, entertainment and infrastructure. To this day, historical monuments of architecture have been preserved in it: watchtowers, fortresses, many churches and temples. For lovers of city trips, a stop is 52 meters from the hotel.

Grand Astor Hotel 4 covers an area of ​​5000 m2. It is a pink one-block building with burgundy balconies and a rooftop terrace. It is noteworthy that these colors and their derivatives are present in the rooms, lobby and restaurant. From the upper floors there is a panorama of the surrounding high-rise buildings of a modern building. The hotel is suitable for leisure adults, business and elderly people, youth companies, families with children.

Rooms : Grand Astor Hotel, Turkey has 92 guestrooms. Of these, 88 are standard-type apartments and 4 family-type two-room apartments.

The capacity of both types is two tri-people. Each room opens onto a small balcony and is equipped with a small safe, air conditioning, split system, minibar, telephone with city line, satellite TV. The rooms held wireless Internet. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and shower. Large terry towels are included with the bedding set. Room cleaning is done on a daily schedule.

Beach : Private beach of sand and pebbles is located at a distance of one hundred meters from the hotel Grand Astor. The road to it goes through the underpass. The beach strip along the sea stretches for 50 meters. It offers free mattresses, sun loungers and beach umbrellas. For an additional fee - rides. In addition to the beach, the hotel in its fenced area near the building has 2 swimming pools - wide for adults and shallow small for children. Around it are set umbrellas and sunbeds. From entertainments - a water slide and a bar.

Meals : In its restaurant Grand Astor Hotel 4 for guests provides a varied menu for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The food system is a buffet. In three bars you can taste any appetizer or carbonated drink, freshly squeezed juice, low alcohol cocktail, and sweet dessert.





Additional services : Separately paid services, saunas, massage, laundry, doctor and beauty salon. Grand Astor Hotel 4 can organize business meetings, meetings, and presentations. For these events, the hotel provides a conference room for 140 people, equipped with video equipment. In the evenings, an entertainment program is organized by the animation team. Sports activities include darts, aerobics, table tennis and chess.

Reviews : Vacationers at the hotel respond differently to this place. Most visitors appreciated the friendliness of the staff, the delicious food, the excellent work of the animators, the comfortable rooms, the daily cleaning, the cleanliness of the area and the Mediterranean climate. A great advantage of the hotel is the knowledge of the Russian language of the hotel workers.
