Hotel Ban Me Dakruco Hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang): overview, description, reviews

Vietnam is an exotic country that is becoming more and more popular with tourists from year to year. Travelers from all over the world come here to enjoy the unique nature, white beaches and interesting local color. Naturally, many hotels of different sizes and categories have been built on the coast. One of them is the Ban Me Dakruco Hotel. Of course, every traveler will try to get more information about the place where he is going to spend his vacation. So what are the advantages and disadvantages of the hotel?

Description of the territory and location of the hotel





The small and cozy Ban Me Dakruco Hotel is located almost in the center of the popular resort town of Nha Trang, next to a picturesque bay and sandy beaches. Near the hotel there are many shops, catering establishments, spa centers, massage parlors and other places where you can have fun and usefully spend time. By the way, you can get to Kamrani International Airport in an hour's drive. The hotel and travel agencies provide tourists with convenient buses, so your transfer will be comfortable.

The hotel itself is a five-story building of medium size. The total area here is small, but generously decorated with greenery, flowers and traditional Vietnamese sculptures.

Hotel Ban Me Dakruco Hotel (Vietnam): photo and description of rooms





The hotel is small - on its territory there are thirty rooms of various categories, including:

  • Superior rooms - spacious rooms with a pleasant atmosphere, two single or one king size bed.
  • Deluxe rooms - rooms with a high level of comfort.
  • Family rooms - spacious rooms that are perfect for a family of 4-5 people.
  • Dormitory rooms - large rooms with five single beds, well suited for joint relaxation of large companies.

All rooms stand out for their luxurious but elegant decor. At your disposal will be a small private balcony, where you can enjoy views of the surroundings and fresh sea air. Guests at Ban Me Dakruco Hotel can count on comfortable furniture, clean linens and a basic set of household appliances, without which you can hardly imagine a pleasant stay.

For example, the climate control system is constantly working, which will help to make the temperature in the room as comfortable as possible. There is a telephone, TV (you can watch cable and satellite channels). The minibar is filled with water, but you need to pay extra for the rest of the drinks. You can also use the safe.

There is a bathroom and a toilet, water is supplied constantly, and residents can rely on clean towels and high-quality hygiene products every day.

How far is the beach? Water leisure travelers

What in terms of beach holidays can the hotel offer Ban Me Dakruco Hotel 2 *? Vietnam is famous all over the world for its luxurious beaches. And one of them is located just 400 m from the hotel complex. A convenient path leads to it, so you can get there in a few minutes.





This beach is public, the hotel does not belong. Nevertheless, it is not too crowded, there are enough sun loungers and umbrellas, the rental of which is not so expensive. Sunset at sea is convenient, there is a quiet backwater where children can play and splash around. Of course, there are conditions for more active pastime, including boats, boats, water-skiing, catamarans. On the territory of the city there are diving, windsurfing and sailing schools.

Entertainment and additional service

The Ban Me Dakruco Hotel is small, but nevertheless offers its guests some additional services. For example, here you can exchange currency at a rather favorable rate at any time. There is also an ATM. A laundry service is available for a reasonable fee.

Parking is available near the building, which you can use for free. By the way, in the city, as well as in a hotel, you can rent a bicycle, car or scooter. This service is very popular among vacationers, as it makes it possible to quickly explore interesting places in the vicinity.





And here there is a good business center where you can use a computer, fax and some other equipment. A small but comfortable conference room is equipped where employees organize a meeting, seminar or celebration for you.

Do not forget that next to the hotel there are beauty salons, massage rooms, shops and the largest market in the vicinity, where you can buy food, clothes, household items, jewelry and original souvenirs. At the hotel you can use the services of a tour desk, because Vietnam is a really interesting country.
