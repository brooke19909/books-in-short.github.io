How to sew a sundress with your own hands for a girl?

Sundress - a type of women's clothing, invented in Ancient Russia. This thing does not have a lot of details and darts. Usually, a sundress is sewn from one or two panels and straps that hold the product on their shoulders.

First model

To make such a thing yourself will not be difficult even for those who have never held a needle in their hands. The simplest model is sewn without using a pattern. The process will take literally an hour. To do this, you only need to measure the chest circumference and increase it by 2-3 centimeters. On fabric folded in half, set aside the required length of the future product, with allowances for hem. Mark the length from two perpendicular sides and connect with an arcuate line. Cut in an arc. Then measure the upper part half the length of the circumference and cut off along this line. Sew the fabric on the back, bend the fabric on the top and bottom of the product, stitch with double stitch.





Straps for such a sundress can be used from a finished satin ribbon. Another option is to sew on your own. To do this, fold the fabric, measure the necessary width and length. Stitch on the wrong side, leaving one edge open. Then it is worth turning to the front side. After you need to iron and sew to the main part. Sundress is ready.

Sew on a T-shirt

Most often, sundresses are everyday clothing for girls of different ages. After all, such clothing is light, practical and does not impede movement. How to sew a sundress with your own hands for a girl? You can, of course, use the first sewing method described above. And you can sew it using one of the baby's shirts as a sample. To do this, you need to fold the prepared fabric twice with the wrong side up.





Then put the shirt and circle it along the contour. Then you should postpone the required length. The second part of the dress will correspond to the back of the shirt. Then both parts are cut with allowance for seams. It remains only to stitch along the side sections and upper sections on the shoulders. After you need to process the side armholes on the sleeves, hem the bottom of the product. Here's how to sew a sundress with your own hands for a girl without drawing up a pattern. As you can see, everything is done quite simply.

We sew a dress from an old thing

How to sew a sundress with your own hands for a girl from an old skirt or dress? To do this, cut the bottom of the dress. This is usually the largest part with a lot of fabric. Then you can attach a baby T-shirt to the material and sew a sundress in the second way. Another option - you can sew a sun skirt, with a length equal to the desired length of the sundress, insert an elastic band into the waist part along the chest volume. Complete with ribbons for shoulder straps. Everything, the outfit is ready.

We sew with a pattern

How can I sew a sundress for a girl whose patterns are already available? They need only be transferred to a plastic film and then transferred to a cloth. And you can build a pattern yourself. It is only necessary to measure the circumference of the chest, the circumference of the waist, hips and length of the product.





Sundress pattern is based on the trapezoid. At the upper base, half the girth of the chest is laid, then the length from the chest to the waist is measured, at this intersection, half the girth of the waist is laid. After the perpendicular is held equal to the length from the waist to the middle of the thigh. At this point, half the hips are laid. Further, the pattern continues to the required length. The base of the dress should be 10 centimeters more than the circumference of the hips. Pattern for sundress is ready. Now it is simply transferred to the fabric, after which the thing is made on it.

How to sew a sundress for a girl whose patterns are made for large volumes? It is only necessary to recount all the data regarding the size of the model. Then you should rebuild the pattern, based on this model.

Denim model

How to sew a sundress with your own hands for a girl from denim? Jeans are quite dense matter. With a little sewing experience, you can spoil both the fabric and the future product. It is best to use the services of masters. However, if the sewing machine copes with denim and the hostess has sufficient sewing skills, then you can make everything yourself.





Pattern jeans denim should not be too complicated. This product is usually made on the basis of a T-shirt dress, has a fastener in front of the product and two side pockets. There should be no additional tucks or creases on the jeans outfit.

Advice

In order for the sundress to sit on the figure beautifully and add elegance, it must be remembered that only regular, even seams should be on the product. All measurements must be carried out accurately and put them on the fabric centimeter to centimeter. The length of the front and rear panels should be the same. That's all the tips.

Conclusion

Now you know how to sew a sundress with your own hands, some patterns are presented in the article. Useful tips are also provided. Do not be afraid to make such a light model. After all, a sundress is always in fashion and always useful for wearing both at home and for walking along the beach or city. Each girl of any age will be glad to have a couple of different models in her wardrobe.
