Villa Botany Kata Beach 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): description of the hotel, services, reviews

Every year, more and more travelers choose Thailand as their vacation destination. This is an amazing country that has a lot to offer tourists: a mild climate (and even in winter), snow-white beaches, incredible natural attractions and, of course, a unique culture and traditions.

If we are talking about beach vacations, then you should visit the picturesque island of Phuket. Of course, there are many hotels of different sizes and categories. And quite often, travelers prefer to spend time at the Villa Botany Kata Beach 3 * hotel.

Naturally, when planning a vacation and booking a hotel, you need to familiarize yourself with additional information. Where is the hotel located and will you have to go far to the beach? What to do with food? Can I bring a child with me? What do former guests think about this hotel? The answers to these questions are very important for a person who is going to spend time on the island.

Where to look for a hotel?





Of course, the location is an important point when choosing a hotel. So where to look for the hotel Villa Botany Kata Beach 3 *? Phuket, Kata - a picturesque resort town by the sea. It is here, in a quiet area, surrounded by a tropical garden, that the hotel complex is located. Within walking distance are several well-known and popular tourist beaches, including Patong Beach and Kata Beach.

The city center with all its attractions and cafes can be reached in 15 minutes. About a kilometer from the hotel grounds there is a large shopping center where you can not only do shopping, but just have fun. The airport is about 30 km away. Do not worry about the move, as the travel agency and the hotel itself provide tourists with a shuttle service. Nevertheless, the road does not take much time, which, of course, is convenient.

Hotel Villa Botany Kata Beach 3 * (Phuket, Kata Beach): what does the area look like?





The hotel complex is small but very cozy. Almost all of its territory resembles a picturesque tropical garden where you can spend time enjoying the beauty of nature, silence and fresh sea air. Hotel Villa Botany Kata Beach 3 * consists of a small main building (lounges, a dining room and a reception are located here), as well as fifteen separate bungalows, where travelers settle down. Of course, there are terraces for relaxation, gazebos and sports fields.

By the way, the hotel was opened in 2004. And already in 2014, a radical reconstruction was carried out. At the moment, the hotel is considered one of the best on about. Phuket

Description and photos of living rooms





Hotel Villa Botany Kata Beach 3 * is not considered large, since there are 15 rooms on its territory. Each of them is a separate bungalow in the middle of a tropical garden. You will find a two-room suite, consisting of a bedroom, a living room and a bath. There is also a small terrace where you can relax while enjoying the sea air.

According to reviews, the rooms have all the necessary furniture, including a spacious wardrobe, comfortable beds, tables, chairs and a soft sofa. As for household appliances, you can count on a standard set. Air conditioning and a fan will help to create a comfortable temperature in the room. Cable and satellite channels can be watched on TV. And each room has the ability to connect to the Internet. There is a small refrigerator.

The bathroom is also quite spacious - there is a shower, washbasin, toilet. A hairdryer is provided in the room upon request. You can count on the availability of clean towels and various hygiene products, including soap, shampoos, shower caps, etc. Housekeepers visit for cleaning every day - the rooms are kept in order and clean.

Do guests offer food?





Of course, going on vacation, the tourist also considers the issue of nutrition, because during your vacation you really want to treat yourself to delicious dishes. Paying for accommodation in the hotel complex Villa Botany Kata Beach Dome Resort 3 *, guests also get the opportunity to have breakfast here. Everything is held in the usual form of the buffet. Guests have access to a spacious and well-furnished dining room.

Reviews of tourists indicate that the food is good here. The menu is quite varied for breakfast: guests are offered toasts, pastries, cereals, light snacks, exotic fruits and some traditional Thai dishes. You can count on drinks, including tea and aromatic coffee. There is also a small bar where you can relax while enjoying a cocktail or a glass of strong booze.

Unfortunately, the rest of the day, the hotel does not offer food. But this is unlikely to be a problem, as there are cafes, restaurants and shops in the immediate vicinity.

Where is the beach located? Facilities on the beach

Water activities

Kata Beach is a lively place where every traveler is sure to find something to his liking. A spacious volleyball court is equipped on the beach - from time to time, tourists even arrange competitions here.

Fans of more active recreation will not have to be bored either. There is a sports center on the shore where you can rent a jet ski, boat or catamaran. Also, vacationers love to water ski, do parasailing, make small boat trips on a boat. Windsurfing and sailing facilities are available. By the way, for a small fee, you can be instructed by professional trainers.

In the evenings, the beaches often organize various shows, competitions and parties where you can have a good time and learn more about the local culture.

Additional service: is it possible to count on some amenities?





Despite the relatively modest number of rooms, the hotel can offer several additional services that make tourists more comfortable. For example, on-site parking is provided, and in the hotel you can rent a bicycle, motobike, car, and for a fairly modest price.

Laundry facilities are located on site. Staff provides ironing services. Here, guests can exchange currency at a favorable rate. By the way, almost throughout the territory there is wireless Internet access, which is very important for most modern travelers. Hotel staff are happy to take care of booking tickets or arranging transfers.

How do you spend time at the hotel? Leisure and entertainment





Of course, most tourists spend time on the beach or in the city with all its attractions. But on the territory of the hotel, guests, as a rule, relax after bright daytime events, preparing to plunge into the resort fun again tomorrow.

There is a spacious pool in the courtyard. The water here is clean, and employees are cleaned daily. On the terrace there are comfortable sun loungers with sun umbrellas - here guests relax, sunbathe and spend time in pleasant communication. There is also a spacious jacuzzi with a powerful hydromassage system. By the way, you should definitely try Thai massage, which is offered by real professionals.

Do not forget about sightseeing holidays, because Thailand, and Phuket itself, is a place where you can plunge into a unique culture, admire the architectural sights, get acquainted with an interesting history. On the territory of the hotel you will be sure to be told about local traditions and attractions, they will help you choose an interesting sightseeing tour.

What kind of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

Tourists recommend this hotel to those who want to relax on the beach. Here, guests have the opportunity to enjoy all the charms of a beach holiday, while spending nights in peace and quiet. Despite the lack of special conditions for living with a child, many travelers still stay here with children, because the guys on the hotel are really comfortable. Often, youth companies, as well as older people, stop here. The hotel complex is perfect for any type of vacation.
