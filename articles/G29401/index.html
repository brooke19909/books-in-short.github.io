"City on White Mountain"

Local historians argue about the date of its foundation. According to some reports, the Belgorod fortress was laid in the middle of the XVI century, but on the basis of other historical and documentary data, the municipal authorities celebrated the 1000th anniversary of the city back in 1995. Belgorod in different centuries belonged to the territory of Ukraine and even was its temporary capital during the formation of Soviet power. Since 1922, it is part of the USSR, and then the Russian Federation.

During the Great Patriotic War (1941-1945), the city was twice occupied by the Nazis, bloody battles were fought in its vicinity. Belgorod and its population have suffered greatly over the years. The destruction was almost total, the historical part of the city disappeared from the face of the earth. After heroic efforts, people after the war carried out restoration work.





Today it is a large cultural, industrial and scientific-educational center, which is located on the right and left banks of the Seversky Donets River. It is famous throughout the country for its cleanliness, ennobled streets, green gardens and parks.

Who will be interested in the city?

The nature of the region, rich in rivers, lakes and other beautiful ponds, picturesque hills and hills, oak groves and nature reserves, stimulates the development of ecological, including rural, amateur, beach tourism. There is a demand for outdoor activities, sports trips, water activities. The presence in the bowels of the edge of sodium chloride mineral springs has allowed us to develop a small network of balneotherapy centers and sanatoriums.

The city blended picturesquely between the slopes of three mountains: Kharkov, Melova or Belaya and Zapadnaya. A small but beautiful river Weselka flows into Seversky Donets . In addition, the Belgorod reservoir is of recreational importance for tourism.

The temperate continental climate in the region is characterized by cool winters with insignificant temperature differences, hot and dry summers. The off-season is not pronounced, so the weather here is quite comfortable and mild in spring and autumn.

People come here most often for educational purposes. Of great interest are architectural structures of different eras, temples and cathedrals, underground monasteries and ancient settlements. Interesting festivals, exhibitions, and shows are regularly held in Belgorod. Fans of skiing on the good slopes of the ski resorts have a rest here in winter.
