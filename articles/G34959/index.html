Unusual New Year's toy from plastic cups. How to make a snowman out of plastic cups

A fabulous and amazing New Year holiday is loved by both adults and children. At this time, everyone is waiting for something amazing and magical. It is impossible to imagine a New Year without an elegant Christmas tree and fragrant tangerines, without Santa Claus, the Snow Maiden and, of course, the Snowman. On the eve of the holiday, many begin to craft all kinds of interesting crafts, so that they can decorate their own home or office. In this article, you will learn how to make a snowman out of plastic cups quickly and easily.

What will be required for work?

For the manufacture of New Year's crafts, the following materials and equipment will be required:

  • about four hundred disposable white cups;
  • a few blue and red cups;
  • stapler;
  • hot glue gun;
  • colored paper;
  • cardboard;
  • baby bucket;
  • Christmas tinsel.

You may need to stock up on spray cans of paint. Of course, they will be needed only if you suddenly could not buy disposable glasses of white, blue and red colors. And how to make a snowman out of plastic cups in such a situation? In this case, you can purchase a transparent plastic dish and paint it in the right shade using an aerosol can.

How to make a snowman out of plastic cups: step-by-step instructions





  1. Start work with the formation of the body of a snowman. Depending on what size you want to get the craft in the end, take the required number of white cups and connect them in a circle with a stapler or glue gun. Make several rows this way. Remember that in each subsequent row you need to reduce one glass, otherwise you will not get the perfect shape of the ball.
  2. Immediately arrange the buttons. How to make? We make a snowman out of plastic cups in the same style. In the fourth row, exactly in the middle, you need to attach one blue glass, then do it through the row until the ball is formed.
  3. Make a belt to the snowman. To do this, take the red cups and connect them to each other.
  4. In a similar technique, craft two balls. Of course, the second should be slightly smaller in diameter than the first. Connect the balls to each other by placing cardboard inside. So the design will become more stable.
  5. Make the third ball. It will be the head of a snowman. Remember to make eyes, mouth and nose from colored cups and colored paper. Here, a fantasy will tell you how best to do it.

Making a snowman is a snap





It seemed to you that making a snowman is a time-consuming process? It's not like that at all. For a successful enterprise, it is enough to stock up with the right amount of material at hand and the desire to make an interesting toy that will delight both children and adults with its amusing look. Know that the financial costs of creating such a craft are extremely small. To make a cheerful snowman of even a very large size, not so many funds will be required.

Advice

The craft will look more interesting and advantageous if you place a Christmas garland or an LED cord inside it. Then install the snowman near the outlet. It is forbidden to place light bulbs inside the toy, as they become very hot during operation and can melt plastic cups.





The quality of the source material also plays an important role, because even quite minor defects or differences can negate all your efforts. That is why it is important to purchase plastic cups from one batch and at one point of sale. Do not buy disposable tableware with rims, as it will be difficult for you to avoid large joints between the rows. And this means that the spherical shape of the elements is unlikely to be achieved. Do not be afraid to stock up on a few more cups than was originally planned. After all, some of them may be defective.

How to make a snowman out of plastic cups more stable? To keep the snowman firmly on the floor or other surface, glue it with double-sided tape.
