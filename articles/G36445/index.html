Vileika reservoir: description, features, fishing

The Vileika reservoir (map below), which is part of the Vileika-Minsk water system, is located in Belarus. It is the largest of the artificially created within this state. The construction of this attraction took place in 1968, and it was only filled in 1975. The project was financially beneficial, the goal was to supply Minsk with water.



It is a known fact that during the construction a number of villages were flooded. Locals claim that by putting an ear to ice in winter, the traveler will definitely hear the ringing of bells.



Short description of the reservoir

The volume of the reservoir reaches 0.26 cubic meters. m, length - 27 km, width - 3 km, area is 73.6 square meters. km Relatively shallow depth stopped at around 13 meters, the coast is artificially fixed.

Vileika reservoir includes 5 hydraulic pumping stations, there are 10 islands. The water level is unstable and constantly reaches different values. 1/4 of the part with technical labeling is mainly spent on the supply of enterprises and factories, the rest is considered drinking water. Do not be afraid of diseases. Water undergoes thorough treatment with liquid chlorine, phytoplankton is removed, and filtration is applied.



Vileika reservoir: fishing

In winter, the reservoir attracts inveterate fishermen who organize competitions among themselves. Many have already known each other for several years and do not miss the opportunity to return again. For example, in 2002, spinning competitions were held in these parts, which revealed the best fishermen.

The place is quite unique and allows you to enjoy a large catch, rare species of fish. Especially a lot of good reviews about biting in the village of Sosenka, where the depths of the animal world are filled with water depths.

The Vileika reservoir is an ideal place in Belarus for fishing. Here they successfully catch even from the coast, without using boats. The main thing is to choose the right bait: seeds, crackers, and in some cases bread.

The fauna of the reservoir is quite rich in its representatives: roach, bleak, crucian carp, pike, zander, gudgeon, tench. This list can be continued indefinitely, it is important to note that cyprinids predominate, many species are subject to fishing. The state is taking a number of measures to avoid poaching, which contributes to the rapid breeding of fish.



Controversial issues

Often there are misunderstandings between amateurs who are used to fishing with a motor boat and those who like to engage in fishing in the usual way. Excessive noise scares away the fish and reduces the likelihood of a large catch. Especially sensitively captures the noise vibrations of bream. You can sail, but in no case use internal combustion engines. They are banned, and rarely anyone knows about it. However, disputes about this do not subside, everyone has different opinions. Environmentalists notice that there is some danger, and urge to be more careful about the flora and fauna of the reservoir, as well as the health of people around. Environmental raids are regularly conducted to prevent emergencies.

Vileika reservoir: rest

In addition to fishing, hunting, water skiing and sailing are especially popular . In June, the water surface is covered with blue-green algae, covering the surface. On the one hand, this is a beautiful phenomenon, and on the other, a decrease in the beneficial qualities of water.

The Vileika reservoir is surrounded by a fairly vast territory, which allows you to easily find your favorite place to relax and admire nature in the summer months. A special clean air will help to improve your health and gain positive emotions. But in winter you can admire the bewitching northern lights.

Local landscapes attract not only local residents and guests of neighboring regions, but also foreign tourists, who are located in comfortable conditions of recreation centers.

Visitors note the availability of hotels and restaurants with good service, boat rental, gazebos. Outdoor sports will also appeal to not only adults, but also children. Statistics show that every day the reservoir hospitably welcomes thousands of people on its banks.



Animal and plant world

The animal world is rich in muskrats and beavers. Of the birds there are hawks, woodpeckers, capercaillie and snipe. Forests hide wild boars, elks, goats and raccoon dogs in their bowels.

Those who are not keen on fishing will enjoy spending time on the shore. Camping is not uncommon here, especially since there is a pine forest nearby. Also, a variety of elms and ash are introduced into the plant world. Of the herbs, bluegrass, buttercup, thyme, and forget-me-not are more common.

The Vileika reservoir is a picturesque corner of nature that will not leave anyone indifferent and will give pleasant moments.
