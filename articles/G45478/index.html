South Ozereyevka: guest reviews

A village with trees and shrubs typical of the south of Russia is spread in a valley between the mountains. The climate in these places is Mediterranean: little rainfall, moderate humidity and the absence of strong winds attract tourists here.

Central beach

Between the mountains stretched the main beach of Ozereyevka (just over two kilometers). The rocky shores beyond the central beach were chosen by tourists, vacationing "savages." A big plus to stay in the village of Yuzhnaya Ozereyevka, reviews confirm this, is a small number of tourists, even at the peak of the season. Therefore, the coastal beach is free and comfortable, and the sea is clean.





A lot of water activities are organized for guests on the shore. There are rental points for catamarans and jet skis.

The percentage of guests coming from other regions is still small here, but is growing year by year.

Cozy province

Holidays in Yuzhnaya Ozereyevka, reviews of tourists say the best about it, is an ideal option for those tired of megacities, bustle and noise. In the village there are several guest houses, mini-hotels and recreation centers.

There are many leisure options. A diving center is open in Ozereyevka. It hosts lessons teaching the basics of diving and finding wrecks. Guests also enjoy visiting the horse-racing show "Rome" and a horse riding club. The excursion program of these places is diverse: Gelendzhik, Novorossiysk, trips to Abrau-Durso and other memorable, historical and simply beautiful places of the Krasnodar Territory.





Residence

Most of the hotels in the village are located on two central streets: Peace and Freedom. Guest houses

Vacationers in the village of Yuzhnaya Ozereyevka leave good reviews about several guest houses. One of them is Asteria. Opened only three years ago. For guests prepared apartments superior: two-room, double and triple "standard". Each of them has a TV, split, refrigerator and toilet with shower. Free Wi-Fi is available on site, and a playground is provided for children. The fee includes accommodation in rooms, use of a shared kitchen equipped with modern appliances, barbecue areas and a parking lot.





By prior arrangement, a transfer is possible, which is paid separately.

No less interesting proposal - hotel-cottage "Sedovil" in the village of South Ozereyevka. Reviews of vacationers, interrupting each other, praise this place. It is located on the main street - a stone's throw from the sea. The complex consists of two buildings: wooden and brick. There are two rooms with an independent entrance in the log house: Green and White. Two more rooms have been prepared for guests in the capital building: Red and Blue. All apartments are equipped with a split system, refrigerator, TV, toilet with shower and kitchenette. It is equipped with an electric stove, sink, electric kettle and a small table. Accessories for cooking and eating are included. There are no interruptions in water, including hot.





On the territory of the complex in a shaded place there is a large capital gazebo. It is equipped with a barbecue area, grill and barbecue. There are tables, comfortable sofas and benches.

Excellent conditions for guests of the village of Yuzhnaya Ozereyevka, reviews of their customers confirm this, in the guest houses "Diving", "By the Sea" and others.

Holiday near the water: camping

On the left side of the central beach right on the shore there is a motor camp "South Ozereyevka". It can simultaneously accept up to 80 cars. The campground has a good infrastructure: a cafe, barbecue and kitchen areas, a dining room and dry closets. It is allowed to set up tents on site, but you can stay in small wooden summer houses.

Guests of the village of Yuzhnaya Ozereyevka (camping) sometimes leave unflattering reviews due to the lack of protection and drinking water. There is only a well supplying industrial water. But basically, vacationers are happy: there are several steps to the sea, there‚Äôs nothing at all, there is a lot of vegetation, there is where to hide from the heat. The rental fee is minimal; children under ten years of age have a free vacation.

Other options

In the private sector, there are many successful offers of economy housing. On various sites, you can book one bed or a small room for several people with a minimum of conditions. Some have a fan, some have a TV, fridge and shower. But in the majority - the toilet, showers and kitchen are in the courtyard.





For wealthy tourists, there are several offers for renting apartments of the so-called apartment type. In them - from two to four rooms. Necessarily in the room there is a toilet, shower (and almost always a bath) and a kitchen. The apartments are equipped with modern appliances: TV, refrigerator, split system, kitchen equipment and cooking and eating facilities. Some of the apartments in the village of Yuzhnaya Ozereyevka (reviews give reason to think so) have built-in safes.
