Hotel Tia Maria 3 * Hotel (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): overview, description and reviews of tourists

In Southeast Europe, the state of Bulgaria is located. The climatic conditions of this region make it incredibly popular among tourists who prefer a beach holiday. Bulgaria is called the "Pearl of the Balkans". One of the best resorts in the country is Sunny Beach. The name of this place comes from its natural warmth and almost always reigning sunny weather on the Black Sea. Those who want to spend an active family or youth holiday will find everything you need for this, there are entertainment and sports events. All of them are more concentrated on the coast of Sunny Beach.

For a short period of time, the resort really liked the tourists, and every year there are more and more people wishing to visit it. There is a huge number of hotels, campsites, restaurants, bars and nightlife. The peculiarity of this place is pale golden sand, which is strewn with an 8 km long beach in the shape of an arc. The coastal zone is washed by the translucent water of a warm gulf. Protected by natural undulating dunes, the beach area is awarded the Blue Flag.

Everyone can find rest for the soul and afford. It has clean beaches, friendly locals and vibrant clubs. I am glad that housing at the resort is available even to budget tourists. Hotels are located directly on the Black Sea. Entertainment at reasonable prices, a variety of cocktails and drinks, the staff of the institutions is very pleasant and professional.

Beach vacation

As already noted, the beach stretches as much as 8 km. The local sand is very nice to walk and play with the children. Even the most sensitive person will be comfortable in contact with soft sand dunes, on which, by the way, you can have fun with children, playing hide and seek. Parents can not worry about their child, near the shore there is a shallow bottom, which will allow kids to play. For those who want to spend time comfortably on the beach there are sun loungers on which you can relax and enjoy the sunshine. Those who prefer to sunbathe on a sandy beach can bring their own rugs and sit under specially equipped umbrellas.

In Bulgaria there are a lot of various hotel complexes of various levels. One of them - the hotel, which is rightfully considered one of the best in the resort of Sunny Beach, Sunny Beach Tia Maria 3 * - will be presented in this article.

Description

Amazing three-star hotel Tia Maria invites tourists to stay in comfortable rooms in the center of Sunny Beach. The hotel officially began to receive guests since 2012.





Within a radius of 1.5 km there are such famous places as Old Nessebar, Aqua Paradise and Cacao Beach. And while walking you can visit the Kuban, the GRAND club and the Djanny restaurant.

Supermarkets and grocery stores are conveniently located for making independent purchases. Hotel Tia Maria 3 * is located close to Burgas Airport, which can easily be reached by taxi in 25 minutes.

Rooms

The hotel has about 165 rooms, each of which has air conditioning, a TV, a sitting area, a kitchen and 24-hour Internet. There are private bathrooms with showers or large family bathtubs and all necessary utensils.

Nutrition Reviews

Hotel Tia Maria 3 * (Sunny Beach) offers various food options. Breakfast is usually included in the price. According to many tourists, the food is not very diverse. Several egg dishes, fresh vegetables, olives, cheese, sausages, breakfast cereals with milk, cereals, fruits and hot drinks are offered. The latter are not of very good quality, so if possible it is better to purchase instant tea or coffee. Of the fruits, there are often watermelons, melons. The sausages are tasteless, but other dishes can satisfy your hunger. During the day, you can eat in local restaurants and cafes for little money. You can also purchase products in supermarkets. There is an opportunity to pay extra for dinners at a local restaurant, but this is often unprofitable, since vacationers do not like to be attached to a place on vacation.

Staff Opinions

Hotel service reviews called neutral. Here, no one is showing their emotions too much. The work is done efficiently.
