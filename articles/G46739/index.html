Women's knitted sweater with a hood of blue yarn

It is necessary:

  • 550/600/700 g of blue yarn (100% natural wool, 220 m) or acrylic and wool.
  • Knitting needles 3 mm.

Back

Dial 90/110/120 loops. Knit 4 rows with handkerchief, 10 rows with front stitch, 20 rows with transverse stitch, 10 rows with front stitch. Go for textured knitting. Knit to the armhole 40 cm, the armhole is done according to the pattern. Finish knitting.

Before

For each half, we collect 50/60/70 loops. Knit patterns in the same sequence as the back.

Sleeve

Dial 30/40/45 loops. Pattern knit both front and back.





Hood

Get 100 loops. Perform 4 rows with handkerchief knitting, 6 rows with front stitch, 45 cm with textured knitting. Close 33 loops on both sides, continue to knit 34 remaining loops with textured knitting. Close all loops. Sew side seams. Sew the hood to the neck.

Assembly

  • Sew all the finished parts of the sweatshirt. Sew sleeves to the armhole.
  • Gather loops around the edge, tie the strap fastener. Sew on buttons.
