IXUS 132 Canon Digital Digital Camera: Review, Specifications and Reviews

In recent years, manufacturers of photographic equipment have achieved high results in the quality of their premium models. Users note achievements in a variety of directions - from the elementary nuances of ergonomics of the case to the development of new principles in the implementation of autofocus and the viewfinder. But the lower budget segment, which is also dominated by large companies, does not go unnoticed. A vivid confirmation of this is the modification of the Canon Digital IXUS 132, in which the matrix base has been optimized and the function management system has been improved. However, belonging to the range of affordable cameras also makes itself felt, so it could not have done without flaws.





Model Overview

The model demonstrates an example of the already classic concept of a light, almost miniature and at the same time functional digital camera. It must be said right away that modification does not indulge working indicators that are too high for its class, but it attracts with the quality of their performance. It can be said that each parameter declared by the manufacturer was conscientiously implemented in IXUS 132. Canon did not try to surprise with optional features, but the model nevertheless received some pleasant additions like an intelligent stabilizer.

The form factor of the case is typical, if not template. However, you will not especially turn around in this segment. On the one hand, it is necessary to maintain one of the main advantages of such cameras - ergonomic compactness, and on the other hand, do not forget about the ease of physical control. Indeed, inexperienced beginners and amateurs make up the main consumer audience of the IXUS 132. Canon has been working with compact digital cameras for years, and in this development, of course, the company sought to realize at least in the optimized form the maximum of those shooting opportunities that are successful.





Specifications

It is clear that we are not talking about a comparison with the premium class in terms of the quality of the internal filling. But the situation is such that in its niche the apparatus does not claim to be a leader. This is primarily confirmed by the characteristics that the Canon IXUS 132 has:

  • Matrix - 16 million pixel CCD type.
  • Zoom - 8x module.
  • Stabilizer - present.
  • Screen - 2.7 inches display.
  • Resolution - up to 4608 x 3456.
  • The height of the chamber is 52.4 mm.
  • Width - 93 mm.
  • Thickness - 21.6 mm.
  • Mass - 133 g.

Characteristics by modern standards look modest, even taking into account the budget segment. But for the sake of justice, it should be noted that the price tag seems to be a record for this category - 2.5-3 thousand rubles.





Functionality

In terms of shooting, you can highlight the presence of automatic exposure. This mode is popular in cameras that allow you to take high-quality landscape pictures, but for compact devices it is still something outlandish. But the other modes are more than traditional and will not surprise the user with anything special. But this can not be said about the IXUS 132. communication tools. Canon provided the model with a Wi-Fi sensor, which allows you to send materials to the network without using wires. The option is also not new, but in a budget class its presence is not so common. Positive reviews

Using the example of this model, one can partly dispel the myth that nominal performance is a determining factor in obtaining high-quality images. According to many users, the photo and video materials of this camera turn out to be better than those of competitive devices with an exceeding number of pixels. Saturation in the color gamut, sharpness, detail and decent photosensitivity for this level are noted. And these are only factors that relate to the direct shooting function of the Canon IXUS 132. Reviews regarding the ease of operation are also mostly positive. Owners point to a thoughtful and ergonomic arrangement of the main control buttons, an optimized and friendly menu, and also to the high speed of the overall interface, which is rare in cheap models.





Negative reviews

Given the price tag, it‚Äôs rather difficult to talk about the shortcomings of a generally successful model, nevertheless they are also present. First of all, they relate to working qualities. In automatic mode, the ISO sensitivity settings may be lowered - this applies primarily to photo shoots in darkened rooms. In general, many note that the automatic modes of this camera are most effective precisely in the summer, when shooting in the sun. In low light conditions, noise accents often remain. There are also problems of a different nature with the Canon IXUS 132: the battery does not charge, the lid is poorly fastened, and there are backlashes at the joints. If the first malfunction is typical of a malfunction due to damage to the charge socket or power controller, then the remaining defects are common factory ones, and, unfortunately, it is impossible to fix them. True, in the process of operation, they quickly fade into the background.





Conclusion

Since the segment of compact inexpensive digital cameras is densely occupied by different companies offering mostly the same products, this model may seem to be nothing more than an interpretation of the product for the masses. But with a detailed examination, the advantages of the IXUS 132 will become apparent. Canon in this case is an order of magnitude superior in quality characteristics to the bulk of the competitive product. Actually, we can talk about full-fledged competition only in comparison with similar models from Pentax and Fuji-level brands. And with such an analysis, it becomes clear that the representative of the IXUS series is almost a piece product, although it has become fixed in the group of state employees. It is noteworthy that, despite the difference in quality, this model is literally in the same price segment as the Chinese little-known digital cameras of a similar form factor.
