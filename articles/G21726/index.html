Nizhny Novgorod, residential complex "Ankudinovsky Park": description

Nizhny Novgorod - one of the largest cities in the country, the industrial center of the Volga region. The number of inhabitants every year only increases, accordingly, the need for additional square meters increases. Modern newlyweds dream of their own housing, where they can live separately from their parents, which is to say about families with small children. Those residential areas that were built during the Soviet years in the city are sorely lacking. That is why the construction industry is currently on the rise: there are modern residential complexes with their own infrastructure. If you are attracted to life by Nizhny Novgorod, the Ankudinovsky Park residential complex will be a good option. Want to know everything about the location of the complex, the technology of its construction and the comfort that every resident can count on? Continue reading our material.





About the complex

The new residential complex "KM Ankudinovsky Park" offers excellent living conditions. 27 houses of varying number of storeys (19-35 floors) are built using monolithic technology using innovations. A completely new format of quality and affordable housing for all residents of Nizhny Novgorod.

Location

For the new residential complex "Ankudinovsky Park" in Nizhny Novgorod, the Soviet district was chosen. He spared industrial production, is considered one of the most favorable for life. The new microdistrict is surrounded on all sides by forest, which only improves the ecological picture. All residents will be able to enjoy clean fresh air, as well as stroll through the forest, botanical garden whenever they want. The location of the complex is called by many one of the main advantages.





The remoteness from the central part of the city in this case is compensated by the proximity to Ankudinovskoye Shosse, by which it will be possible to reach the city in a matter of moments using a personal car or public transport. Moreover, the developer promises that public transport stops will appear directly on the territory of the complex, and additional public transport routes will be launched in its direction.





Beautification

A conceptual new approach to the construction of residential real estate, care for all residents - this is what the developer of the Ankudinovsky Park residential complex emphasized. Reviews about the project, the construction of which is still ongoing, are few, but you can already understand from them that the project is worthy of special attention and detailed study. The territory of the microdistrict is fenced, is under round-the-clock surveillance. Yards are isolated from the through traffic of cars; it is planned to build modern playgrounds and sports grounds, walking areas, bicycle paths.

Infrastructure

There is nothing strange and surprising that modern residents need only an ecologically clean area to live in - they do not want to lose access to developed urban infrastructure. That is why the developer tried to compensate for the shortage of socially significant objects by laying their construction in the general plan. 4 huge kindergartens, a large school, a sports complex, playgrounds, a medical center, bank branches, pharmacies and shops - all this is an incomplete list of those infrastructure objects that every resident will have access to.





Flats

A diverse set of modern planning solutions helps everyone find the option of housing that meets all preferences and requirements. On the territory of the Ankudinovsky Park residential complex in Nizhny Novgorod there are small but cozy studios, full-fledged one-room apartments, two-bedroom apartments for families with children and spacious three-room apartments for large and friendly families. The developer even tried to place a spacious kitchen, a separate bathroom, isolate the bedrooms and allocate a little space for the wardrobe even in a small area.

All apartments are rented turnkey. The Ankudinovsky Park residential complex in Nizhny Novgorod is a great solution for those who want to move right after receiving the keys. Finishing work is carried out using modern materials with the involvement of qualified specialists. Thus, it is enough just to get furniture.

Prices

If you decide to consider the option of acquiring an apartment in the Ankudinovsky Park residential complex, the price per square meter of area is probably your main selection criterion. The cost of a studio apartment with decoration starts from 1.7 million rubles.
