Club "Pepelats" in Izhevsk: what is famous for and where is it located?

The huge entertainment club "Pepelats" in Izhevsk was built in the form of a spaceship, where in different departments there is a leisure option for each guest. No one will feel alien and sad within the walls of this hospitable establishment.

Holidays for every taste and budget

In practice, the space is divided into four sectors - by interests.

  • The most basic and beloved by visitors is a bowling hall. Everything is equipped with the latest technology. Particularly strong coating of luminous game tracks, in addition to monitors, flat LCD screens are installed that reflect the real score in the game.

A little higher in the same hall are guest places where you can watch bowling fights.

  • Karaoke bar. Here you can have a great time both alone and in a big company, singing your favorite songs.
  • Irish Pub. A traditional Irish beer house where you can not only taste your favorite beer drink, but also have a tasty snack with pleasant soft music. In the warm season in Izhevsk in the club "Pepelats" you can have a great time on the open veranda.
  • Dance floor. The dance disco club "Ashes" is characterized by driveability even in design. Modern lighting and music equipment. Here you can not only actively spend time on the dance floor. But also have a good time with friends. For this, comfortable sofas are located about the hall. And to add a taste to the evening, you can order both food and drinks to taste.

How does it work and where is it located?

The entertainment complex is waiting for its guests every day.

You can just run into a business lunch. Chefs cook here excellently. Or order lunch with delivery to the office.

Standby time and guest service - from 12:00 to 05:00.

Remember the address of the club "Pepelats" in Izhevsk: Industrial District, st. Basic, 19. In the evening it is better to get by taxi, because it is not known how the party will end. See you!
