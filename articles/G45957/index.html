Interested in how not to pay loans legally? As a general rule, the limitation period is three years. At the same time, the beginning of its expiration will be considered the moment when you last contacted with bank employees on the issue of loan repayment. Are there any other options for resolving the issue of how not to pay loans legally? Of course. Try to negotiate with the bank so that it enters your position and relaxes its financial claims. Adoption of the Law on Bankruptcy of Individuals

Very soon, loans can not be paid due to the adoption of the law "On Bankruptcy of Individuals". In this case, we are talking about situations where, with a debt of 50,000 rubles, the borrower does not make payments within 3 months. After that, in court, a person is declared bankrupt, they are given a five-year installment plan for paying a loan, and in parallel with this, a restructuring plan is developed.
