Rest in Genichesk - recreation centers "Era", "Express", "Kolobok", "Coral"

During the summer vacation season, many people think about where to relax. Most tourists prefer the sea for this. For residents of Ukraine, and not only, it is proposed to organize a vacation in Genichesk. Recreation centers in these parts are designed for any wallet. Let us review a few of them.

Holiday Village "Era"

Residents of large cities especially need a good rest. It can be obtained in full on the coast. 50 meters from the refreshing salt water is the recreation center "Era" in Genichesk. The territory of the whole boarding house is quite well landscaped. Vacationers are accepted from May to September.

Era offers rooms located in 4-storey block buildings (one block consists of two rooms). Available are two-, three- and four-bed apartments for every taste and budget - from standard to suites. Each room has a refrigerator.

Estimated prices (daily payment):

  • improved on 2 and 3 places - from 128 to 395 UAH. per room;
  • standard - from one person from 55 to 125 UAH .;
  • triple room of two rooms - from 225 to 510 UAH.

In the rooms of the standard class fans will help to cope with the heat, the rest are equipped with air conditioning. Superior apartments and suites will delight guests with satellite TVs. It is worth noting that all the equipment is modern and in working condition.

In the pension there is an internet club, as well as WI-FI. In the cafe near the "Era" you can order food - breakfast, lunch and dinner. It will cost tourists about 100 UAH. per day. The guesthouse is guarded, on-site parking is available.

Rest house "Kolobok"

Looking for home comfort with an active pastime? Then all you need is a vacation in Genichesk. Recreation facilities, and in particular "Kolobok", will satisfy any needs of tourists. Convenient location on one of the main streets, within walking distance of the sea (distance of about 300 meters). Vacationers can stay in different rooms - from 2 to 4 beds. Two-story buildings were built on the territory.

Housing prices (daily payment):

  • triple suite - 400-550 UAH. per room;
  • Suite for two - 280-380 UAH .;
  • standard rooms for a place - 110-140 UAH.

On the territory there is an outdoor pool, gazebos, a playground. It is possible to organize a comprehensive three meals a day in the dining room - 140 hryvnias per person. The territory has WI-FI coverage, parking and a small library.
