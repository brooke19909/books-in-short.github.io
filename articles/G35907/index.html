Chania: Greek Resort Attractions

Chania is considered the real gem of the Greek island of Crete. Sights of the town attract many tourists who come here from all over the world. Travelers will remember for a long time cozy, neat houses built in the Venetian style, narrow, clean streets, architectural masterpieces erected many centuries ago, historical monuments and amazingly beautiful landscapes. In the greenest part of the island, 140 km from Heraklion, is Chania. It is best to see the sights here in spring and autumn, because in the summer it is very hot (the temperature rises above + 30 ¬∞ C), and in winter it is not very cold, but it often rains.



Most tourists go to Crete to have fun, plenty of fun in the warm, azure sea. Chania's main pride is its many kilometers of golden beaches, which are great for families with children. The approach to the sea is gentle, the waves are practically absent, and the sand is very fine. The city offers a wide selection of housing, so for a very reasonable fee you can rent apartments near the beach.

Fans of architectural structures and antiquities should definitely go to the Old Town, which is considered one of the beautiful places not only in Chania, but also in Crete. During World War II, the historical part of the city managed to survive. In the quarter of Topanas, narrow streets and houses are transferred to the era when Chania was still under the control of the Venetians. The sights of this area belong to different periods of history and were built by different peoples. Firkas Fortress offers amazing views of the old port. In the Jewish quarter, you can see the ruins of the Schiavo bastion and the ramparts. Also in the Old Town is the Janissary Mosque.



Without a noisy market rich in a wide variety of goods, Chania cannot be imagined. Of course, you need to see the sights of historical significance, but you cannot return home without souvenirs. The Agora city market was built in 1911, its shopping arcades are directed to all four cardinal directions and have the shape of a cross. Here you can buy absolutely everything: seafood, fabrics, jewelry, souvenirs, books, vegetables.

Chania is also proud of the new part of the city. Sights (the map will help you focus and visit all the interesting places) are not as interesting as in the old quarters, but you should still look at the Kipos garden, the house of Manusos Kunduros, the residence of Bishop Despotiko. To get to know the island of Crete, you should go to the outskirts of the city. Here you can visit forests untouched by a human hand, the largest European canyon, beautiful gorges, Lefko Ori mountain range.



Traveling in a rented car through the villages, you can see the real picturesque beauty of the local nature, get acquainted with the traditions and culture of the people. Everyone who wants to have a good rest, get a boost of vivacity and optimism for the whole year, return home with pleasant impressions and memories, needs to go on vacation to the address: Greece, Crete, Chania. This resort will provide everyone with a lot of positive and fun.
