Hotel Club Azur 4. Egypt, Hurghada. Booking, Prices, Photos.

Description: The resort complex Club Azur 4, which is part of the Swiss Inn network, offers tourists comfortable conditions, close to international standards. The hotel was founded in 2000, 32 km from the village of Hurghada and 39 km from the airport. In 2005, overhaul was carried out in the hotel buildings.

Today, the guesthouse is one of the most visited places in Egypt, thousands of guests come here every year from all over the world. On the territory of the complex, seven two- and three-story villas are built, in which apartments of various categories are located.



Hotel Club Azur 4 (Hurghada) is located away from transport hubs and close to the coast. Thanks to this, guests will be able to spend their holidays calmly and bask in the azure and clear waters of the Red Sea.

Types of rooms: Consists of 238 rooms with sea or garden views. Each room has a spacious terrace. All rooms are not less than 25 m2, furnished with high-quality furniture and modern appliances. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower, toilet, large sink and hygiene products.

There is an air conditioning system, a plasma TV with satellite TV, a long-distance telephone, a hairdryer, a minibar with free water (daily replenished). An electric kettle and ironing equipment are delivered upon request.



Every day, pleasant maids carry out wet cleaning at a time convenient for you. The bed changes several times every seven days.

Meals (All Inclusive): The all-inclusive concept of food includes non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks of local bottling, as well as sweets. Table setting is carried out in the main restaurant ("buffet") - according to the schedule. On the menu: several types of fresh fruits, vegetables, sausages, cheeses, side dishes, meat and fish dishes, snacks and desserts.

Club Azur 4 has bars and restaurants offering a wide range of dishes and drinks. In the evenings, the disco bar hosts interesting programs and contests.

Beach (own): A private beach area with an artificial lagoon extends 150 m from Club Azur 4. The reviews from vacationers are just enthusiastic: the lagoons were especially pleased - comfortable for kids. All beach amenities are offered free of charge (sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas). The coast is spacious - there is enough space for everyone.

On the shore there is a bar, shower and a water entertainment center where you can rent a catamaran or a motorcycle and go on a journey through the water. Fans of diving will be able to snorkel and watch the colorful marine life and the underwater world. Kids will love the aqua center with water slides.

Additional Information: Club Azur 4 has a spacious conference room with 125 seats, equipped with the latest technology. For sports and leisure, there is a gym, a golf course, soccer, a tennis court, ping pong, riding school, darts, aerobics classes are built.



In your free time you can play giant chess, billiards, ride a bike, shoot archery or go to the desert on camels (with a guide). For children and adults - two outdoor pools (one heated). For kids there is a play area and a small club.

Internal infrastructure includes an exchange office, a medical office (doctor's call), a trading shop, and a laundry / dry cleaning department. In addition, there is a rental car, a massage room, a sauna and a bath.

Digest: The chic Club Azur 4 complex, close to a five-star hotel, is ideal for a relaxing, measured and romantic getaway. If you do not want to overpay and want to spend your holidays fun and interesting, then welcome to Hurghada! This hotel has professional and polite staff who will do everything for your well-being.

Half of the contingent consists of French and Germans, there are very few Russian-speaking tourists, which is why there is no noise and rudeness. Relax and enjoy the beauty of the local nature, the warm sea and the sights of Egypt!
