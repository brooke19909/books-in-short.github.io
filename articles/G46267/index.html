Hotel Royal Century Pattaya Hotel 3 * (Thailand / Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

If you are looking for an inexpensive but comfortable hotel with a good location for a vacation in Pattaya (Thailand), then be sure to pay attention to the three-star Royal Century Pattaya Hotel.



Description, location

This hotel is located in the heart of Pattaya, within walking distance of Walking Street and the beach. Bangkok Airport is 150 kilometers away.

Royal Century Pattaya Hotel 3 * was built in 1991. It is an eight-story building, accommodating 250 comfortable rooms of the standard categories, Century Suite and Royal Suite. All apartments are equipped with necessary furniture, as well as a TV (there are Russian-language channels), a safe, air conditioning, a refrigerator with a minibar, a telephone, a bathroom, a balcony. Cleaning is done every day. Towels are refreshed daily and bed linen once a week.

The hotel has a large swimming pool with a sun terrace, a bar, a restaurant, a parking lot, and a car rental service. Reception at the hotel is open 24 hours a day. For a fee, guests can order room service.

Population

As tourists note, almost always the accommodation of newly arrived guests in this hotel occurs immediately after their arrival. Nutrition

The cost of living in Royal Century Pattaya Hotel 3 * includes breakfast only. They are held in the hotel restaurant. Depending on the number of guests currently at the hotel, breakfast can be held either in buffet style, or you will be served by a waiter. The choice of dishes and products, according to tourists, cannot be called particularly diverse, but you will always find it easy to eat. So, in the morning you will be offered scrambled eggs or scrambled eggs, sausages, Thai soup, a side dish, fresh fruits, pastries, as well as juice, tea and coffee. During the day, most of the guests preferred to eat in the surrounding cafes and restaurants. Prices are pretty reasonable everywhere. Also, experienced tourists are advised to visit the grocery market, where you can buy fresh fruits and seafood, which for a small fee you will cook here. In addition, do not bypass the mobile food stalls (in Thailand they are called shingles). Here you can have a tasty and inexpensive meal.



Beach vacation

This point, of course, excites most tourists arriving on vacation in Thailand. As for the Royal Century Pattaya Hotel 3 * we are considering, the distance from it to the nearest beach is about 800 meters. You can reach the sea in 10-15 minutes. However, guests rarely go to the nearest beach, as it does not differ in cleanliness. Seasoned travelers are advised to choose other places to stay. You can get to them by tuk-tuk or by renting a motorbike. Experienced tourists are also advised to visit the islands at least a couple of times. Here you can swim in the clear water and relax on the beautiful beaches. You can get to them by ferry, which regularly departs from the pier of Pattaya.
