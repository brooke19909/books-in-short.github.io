Krutitsy airfield (Ryazan): description and activities

What is an airfield Krutitsy (Ryazan)? What is he famous for? You will find answers to these and other questions in the article. With great love, the pilots, businessmen and other people who are not indifferent to the flight built up the Krutitsa air hub in a clean field. In 2010, they came to the former site of aircraft chemical operations with a crooked hangar and the remains of the destroyed strip. The result was a magnificent air harbor.

Description

Krutitsy airfield (Ryazan) wants to visit everyone. Part of its technology park is hidden by the hangar in which the BDRM-2 is located. This armored car can drive where there have never been roads. Here it is used only for entertainment purposes, like other equipment that is in store for those who want to recharge with adrenaline.

At the airfield there are self-balancing electric scooters, buggies and ATVs, a red Mi-2 helicopter, a yellow An-2 board, light aircraft SM-92T, SportCruiser, Cessna-172 Skyhawk.





Here, those who wish can take a parachute jump both independently and in tandem with an instructor. At the airport Krutitsy (Ryazan) there are hotel houses. There is also a cafe where visitors are deliciously fed. This air hub is privately owned, as is the entire fleet. Soon it is planned to build a new runway here and increase the number of aircraft. Perhaps in this air harbor interethnic competitions will soon be held.

Intelligence

Krutitsa airfield (Ryazan) is located at the following address: Ryazan region, Shilovsky district, 284 km, M5-Ural - Krutitsa air hub. The air harbor is open daily from 9:00 until sunset. The flight shift is open from Wednesday to Sunday (Monday and Tuesday are days off).

The site is registered in the Territorial Interregional Administration of Air Transport of the central regions. She has such distinctive features:

  • frequency - 119.250;
  • callsign - "Zanzibar";
  • KTA position - 54.16 ”29” ”p. w. 040.49 ”02” ”c. d.

How to get by car?

The distance from Ryazan to Krutitsa airfield is 80 km. The course of travel is the M5 highway (Ural federal road) towards Chelyabinsk. You need to go along the Kuibyshev highway. At 284 km on the right side you will see the air hub (brown houses, white hangar). Also, a good reference are the bright yellow hangars and the aerodrome billboard.





The necessary turn is placed to the yellow hangars to the right. You need to turn, drive through the yellow hangars and turn right again. You will see a concrete road and an entrance to the air gate. Cars can be left in the parking lot. From Ryazan to the airport you can reach in 3 hours 53 minutes by car.

On the minibus

How to get to the airport Krutitsy (Ryazan) by minibus? Minibus No. 585 departs from the central bus station on schedule. You can also take any passing bus through Shilovo to 284 km of the M5 highway. It is necessary to ask the driver to make a stop before turning on Shilovo - opposite the air hub (yellow hangars). You can also get to the air gate by train from the Ryazan-1 stop (leaves at 7 hours 14 minutes) to the stop about. p. 306.

Documents

So, you already know the address of the Krutitsa airfield (Ryazan). And what documents do you need to have for those who want to take a parachute jump? To perform such jumps at the airport we are considering, you need to have the following papers:

  • The passport for the construction with notes on the laying of the spare dome (no later than six months), the implementation of the regulations on the device and inspection.
  • Parachute book with notes on admission to the execution of specific types of jumps, license.




Beginners performing the first jump need to complete a test with an instructor. Jumps are performed from the An-2, SM-92T, and Turbo Finist boards. The control jump must be made by those who have less than a hundred jumps or have never jumped from 4000 m. The conformity of the jump task and the canopy used to the level of the subject is determined by the instructor.

Night jumps

Those who are trained and have at least 30 dome jumps are allowed to night jumps. The first jump is performed at dusk (after sunset), the second - at night. When performing such jumps, the wind speed should not exceed 4 m / s, and when performing jumps from the PS of the Wing model - 7 m / s. In this case, the landing area is indicated by light signals, and skydivers must have a flashlight. Wind direction is determined by illuminated smoke or illuminated cone. The training place for paratroopers is illuminated by car headlights or spotlights.

Aerodynamic tunnel

Do you know that there is an aerodynamic tunnel at the Krutitsa airfield? This is an attraction for kids and a perfect simulator for skydivers.





Flying in a wind tunnel is identical to parachuting from a height of 4000 m, but a person does not put himself at risk at all. Here you can enjoy the feeling of free fall.

Flight by plane

The airport also offers private tours by aircraft. Such a flight will be interesting to those who want to visit interesting places.

Training

At the Krutitsa airfield, you can train paratroopers at the AFF course. Having attended this training, any person will master the free fall technique, control the “wing” type dome, learn to navigate in space and control his body in the air. The course lasts only a couple of days. Aerodrome instructors also suggest people take RW courses. Here, beginners are taught basic confident GA movements, proper run-up and tail-lining.
