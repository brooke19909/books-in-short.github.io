Sights of Varna. Overview

Bulgaria is not only a country with wonderful Black Sea beaches, gentle sunshine and excellent service. It is also of great interest both for connoisseurs of the historical past, and for ordinary tourists who prefer an exciting vacation.

The most popular tourist resort in Bulgaria is Varna. Black Sea embankments, mild climate and a highly developed entertainment industry attract thousands of travelers every year.



Undoubtedly, the passive type of recreation, which consists in constant spending time on the beach, quickly exhausts. Therefore, the sights of Varna diversify the rest. It is recommended to visit all kinds of famous places, which are abundant here. This resort town has a rich historical heritage. Where exactly to go and what sights of Varna to visit first of all?   For tourists, this is a very problematic question, but only from the variety of choices. Any traveler will be able to find something attractive and memorable for himself here.

Varna Attractions

A special place is given to the dolphinarium. Thousands of admiring tourists visit it annually.

Of course, half of them are children and adolescents. In addition to established cascades, tricks with balls, hoops and other shells, visitors can provide the opportunity to swim with dolphins.

No less popular is the aquarium, the opening of which took place in 1932. Externally, the building is similar to a huge oyster. The collection includes a lot of Black Sea fish species and other ocean inhabitants.

Of course, it is impossible to imagine the sights of Varna without creations of architecture, among which the colossal Assumption Cathedral stands out. Built in 1886, it is famous for its extensive collection of icons and frescoes, mostly painted after the Second World War.



The seaside park "Evsinograd", which opened about two centuries ago, is currently a unique combination of rare plants, colorful alleys, leafy trees and intricate shrubs.

The sights of Varna are numerous museums. So, in the Museum of the Sea you can see old boats and various types of equipment, household items of sailors and personal belongings of military commanders.

Of great importance is the Museum of Natural History with a collection of the entire biological environment of the state. Exposure is divided into sections: geology, flora and fauna, prehistoric minerals and rare modern species.

The Museum of Archeology with the most ancient exhibits should not be left without attention. Some of them are of the highest value, for example, gold jewelry that is seven to eight thousand years old. In total, the collection has more than fifty thousand rare items.

Varna, Bulgaria, attractions - these words, without a doubt, are synonymous with an exciting pastime in this country.

If you rent a car, you can diversify your stay and visit interesting places near the resort. Among them are the rock monastery Aladzha, Cape Kiliakra, "Stone Forest".
