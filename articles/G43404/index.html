Rybinsk hotels: addresses, reviews

The city of Rybinsk has a very favorable location - it is 800 km from Yaroslavl and 300 km from Moscow. There are 3 fairly large rivers, as well as many streams and small tributaries. Rybinsk itself is located on 2 banks of the river. Volga.

Here the terrain is mostly flat, sometimes swampy, because the conditional border of taiga and mixed forests goes through the city.

Going here, first find out which hotels of Rybinsk will open their doors to you.

Hotel "Rybinsk"

This hotel in its city is well-known; it was open even during the USSR and has been providing guests and guests with comfort and peace for many years. This place has acquired a unique chic over the years, the traditions of domestic hospitality are carefully preserved here. The institution has affordable prices, the quality of service in this case corresponds to a high level.



Guests are provided with a huge range of free services, such as water coolers, the Internet, the use of office equipment, ironing rooms, etc. A taxi will be called for guests if necessary. Judging by the reviews, security of living here is guaranteed: the work of the security service, a video surveillance system, as well as a fire alarm system.

Address: st. Cross, 120.

Visit Hotel

Considering inexpensive hotels in the city (Rybinsk), it is worth noting the Visit Hotel. There are 4, 6, 8 and 12-bed rooms for accommodation. All rooms are equipped with lockable bedside tables and wardrobes. In the kitchen, equipped with everything necessary, everyone can cook their favorite dishes.



Reading reviews, you can find out that restaurants, clubs and other entertainment are just a few steps away.

Address: st. Chkalova, 34B.

Hotel Volga

Not far from the famous reservoir is a cozy beautiful hotel "Volga" (Rybinsk). The institution, named after the main Rybinsk River, meets the best expectations of Russian hospitality and a warm, sincere atmosphere. The hotel is in a good location - close to the countryside and the center at the same time.



Due to the fact that the city is small enough, it is easy to get to any point from here. A stunning view from the hotel overlooking the river will bring great pleasure to all its guests. Judging by the reviews, the main advantage of the establishment is its comfortable clean rooms - it will be nice to spend even a long time away from your home.

Address: Ave. 50 years of October, 32A.

Hotel "On Kazan"

Considering the hotels of Rybinsk, it is worth noting this institution. It is located in the historic city center, near the Volga embankment. It offers free internet, a bar and parking. The cozy rooms are traditionally furnished and include a fridge, kettle and TV.

Free toiletries and a hairdryer are provided in the bathroom. Reviews of the guests of the restaurant indicate that there are various shops, restaurants and cafes nearby. In the same accessibility, in addition, there are busy streets with a drama theater and an amusement park.

Address: st. Malaya Kazanskaya, 7.

Hotel Koprino Bay

We continue to consider the hotels of Rybinsk. The Koprino Bay Hotel opened its doors 6 years ago. It successfully combines European traditions of service and Russian comfort. To accommodate guests there are 42 cozy cottages with wood-burning fireplaces, as well as 24 rooms that have everything you need for a complete comfortable stay.

There are two restaurants here - Kalita and Ark, in addition, there is a tavern "Navigator". Guests here can enjoy traditional Russian and European cuisine. On the territory there are spacious and comfortable meeting rooms and conference rooms with all the equipment necessary for holding various events. Reviews about the institution suggest that it is located on the banks of the Volga in a pine forest. At the same time, its territory includes a beach with the necessary infrastructure and golden sand.

Address: Pogorelsky s / o, 103.

Guest House Burlak

In the historical part of the city there is a small cozy hotel "Burlak" (Rybinsk). Staying here will bring real pleasure to all lovers of comfort. In this place there are only three categories of rooms, while each of them has ergonomic modern furniture, chic decoration and high-quality equipment.



Judging by the numerous reviews, the facade of the hotel is very attractive - this is an old building that goes well with the rest of the center. Not far from here are the main attractions of the city - the park, the embankment of the Volga River, museums. In addition, from the center to get to various remote areas of the city will be very easy.

Address: st. Pushkin, 1.

Hotel "Brigantine", Rybinsk

This hotel is located 50 meters from the reservoir. It offers 38 comfortable rooms of several categories. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay (household appliances and modern furniture). Judging by the reviews, the friendly staff will be able to find an approach to all guests.



The facility is fully consistent with international standards of quality, comfort and safety. The Brigantine is a great place to relax with the whole family, as well as for business, ceremonial and corporate events.

Address: st. Shipbuilding, 1B.
