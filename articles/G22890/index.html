Healing properties

Kazminsky thermal spring does not in vain attract people with health problems. Penetrating through the skin, iodine compounds help strengthen the walls of blood vessels and heart muscle. Place to relax

Kazminsky thermal spring is visited not only in order to improve their physical condition. It is also a favorite vacation spot of many quite healthy people.



The Hot Spring base was opened in 1987 and is located almost in the center of the village. It features a large outdoor pool (50 x 18 m) with backlight, aero-massage and hydromassage installations. The health staff of the complex will certainly be interested in the health status of each visitor: it will not be out of place to make sure that bathing will bring only benefits.

Many are attracted by the opportunity to splash in 45-degree water when it is freezing outside, and fluffy white snowdrifts surround the pool.
