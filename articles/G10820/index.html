Tourist complex "Eagle's Nest", Altai: description

Rest in Altai can not be compared, perhaps, with nothing. Highlands, air saturated with pine needles, lakes ... The most beautiful places of the Altai Territory attract tourists from various parts of Russia and abroad. The places here are fabulous, breathtaking and the sight of a tourist, leaving an indelible impression of their visit. Snow-white peaks of mountains, cliffs and steppes, mysterious wilds of the taiga, caves and passes, beautiful waterfalls open in front of the viewers of the natural panorama. One of the most famous holiday destinations is the Eagle's Nest. Altai is a place where you want to return.

Holiday Options

For those who love outdoor activities in Altai in the summer, various route options have been developed. If you want to merge with nature, fill it with life-giving energy - choose the horse route. Amazing landscapes, unity with the animal world - all this will provide an opportunity to improve your health and get great benefits from relaxation. Horse route

During the route, on horseback, tourists go to mountainous places where there is no housing, so overnight is carried out in tents. Many (but not all parking lots) are equipped with a canopy and a garbage pit. The toilet is usually primitive. Everyone who buys tickets to Altai should find out from the tour operator all the conditions in which the route will go. Usually, special bags are used for carrying luggage, which are thrown over the horse in a hinged manner. It is desirable that the weight of the cargo together with the tourist does not exceed 100 kg for one horse. Bags for shipping are called archemaki. If the routes are long, then the choice of things is very rational. The rest of the luggage is stored in the left-luggage office of the base from which the tour begins. Naturally, warm clothes should be with you without fail.

Vacation at a tourist base

If you don‚Äôt want to travel around Altai, but just want to enjoy your vacation in a picturesque place in the fresh air, then you can come, for example, to the Eagle's Nest camp site. Tourism complex infrastructure

The base has a playground on the territory, a restaurant with 60 seats and a fireplace room. The restaurant has European and, of course, Russian cuisine. There are all the necessary benefits of civilization. However, the location of the base involves a quiet holiday with family or close friends, and possibly even alone. At the same time, the base has its own summer cafe with a dance floor. So lovers of dancing have the opportunity to relax and get a lot of positive emotions. For parents who want to spend time in solitude, the opportunity is provided to place the child in a nursery with an animator. The base has at its disposal well-maintained cottages and alpine houses. The base is popular, therefore it is better to contact the administration in advance and discuss the conditions of rest and the possibility of arrival.
