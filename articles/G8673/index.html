Number of rooms

Park Hotel Golden Sturgeon provides guests in cozy cottages, surrounded by a mixed pine-deciduous forest. Here are the options available for placement:

  • The standard is designed to accommodate two guests. It consists of a bedroom and a bathroom. Each cottage is equipped with two rooms, some of which are adjacent. The buildings are located in close proximity to the administrative building and restaurant.
  • Junior Suite is a spacious bedroom with a large French bed. The walls are decorated with natural wood. In addition to the berth, there is a small sofa for relaxation. It is possible to provide a clamshell to accommodate an additional guest.
  • Suite - a spacious two-room suite, which can accommodate up to 4 guests, not counting extra beds. The bedroom has a French bed and a lounge chair. The living room is represented by a folding soft sofa.
  • Suite with a sauna differs from the previous accommodation option by the presence of a kitchen area, as well as its own steam room. It is worth noting the original interior with bright purple accents.
