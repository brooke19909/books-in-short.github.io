Kleopatra Beach Hotel 4 *: tourist reviews

Throughout the world, Turkey is famous for its many resorts. Alanya is one of the most popular among them. In this town there are some of the best hotels, and the Kleopatra Beach Hotel 4 * (Alanya) chain is a great confirmation of this.

general information

"Cleopatra" - a network of modern comfortable hotels that are specially created for a comfortable unforgettable vacation for tourists from around the world. It consists of seven beautiful hotels located on one large, amazingly beautiful beach, the name of which is known throughout the city of Alanya - "Cleopatra". In reviews of Kleopatra Beach Hotel 4 * it is often written that everything is correctly planned on its territory and absolutely everything is provided for so that every vacationer can feel like a special and welcome guest. It is on this beach that you can find a lot of different entertainments for vacationers of all ages.



Structure

The first hotel of this structure was built in 1993, and it functions to this day. In 2014, major repairs were carried out. The name of this wonderful place to relax is Kleopatra Dreams Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews in 2017 note a significant improvement in the hotel's interior and increased comfort. This is the place where you want to return again and again. The warm sea, friendly staff, excellent food and a large selection of entertainment leave pleasant memories for the whole year.

In addition to this, this group includes such hotels as: Ada Beach 4 *, Ada Hotel 4 *, Atlas Hotel 4 *, Royal Palm 4 *, Premier Hotel & Spa 4 *, as well as Kleopatra Beach Hotel 4 *. The territory of the latter is located on the border with the popular beach, which is the reason for this name.

So, the Cleopatra hotel group includes seven hotels, and each of them is in great demand among vacationers from around the world. They are also recommended by operators of various travel agencies. So what is each of them famous for? What kind of reviews do tourists leave on social networks and on the open spaces of tourist-related sites about their holidays in these hotels? All this in more detail below.

Kleopatra Dreams Beach Hotel 4 *

This hotel was built the very first of the entire chain in 1993. Repair works are regularly carried out on its territory and in buildings in order to give it a perfect look and improve the living conditions of tourists. A hotel of this type is ideal to relax in it with all the family members, as well as individually.

As for the number of rooms, the attention of the guests is presented to 180 comfortable apartments of the Standard category. They have a basic set of furniture, which is necessary for a certain number of people. As for electronics, each room has a TV with the ability to view satellite channels, among which are Russian-language. Tourists can also use a telephone and an electronic safe at any time, but only if this service has been paid in advance.

In their reviews of this place to stay, tourists very often tell that the staff is very attentive to guests who relax with children - they create special conditions for increased comfort.

As for additional features, on the territory of Dreams Beach 4 *, as guests note, they do not let anyone get bored. In your free time you can enjoy various water sports, play volleyball or, for example, table tennis. Vacationers have a large outdoor pool, where it is nice to swim in the summer heat and refresh themselves with refreshments in the nearby bar, or have a bite if you wish.

Another great hotel of the chain is Premier Hotel & Spa Kleopatra Beach 4 *. It is located near the beach and is attractive for tourists from different countries. Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel 4 *

This vacation spot captivates all holidaymakers at a glance. Tourists are greeted by a beautiful modern five-story building in white, on the territory of which there is a beautiful garden and a large pool. The number of rooms is represented by 188 apartments of the Standard and Family categories, of which two rooms are designed for people with disabilities. All apartments have a private bathroom with a full set of all necessary accessories, as well as a French balcony from which residents can enjoy the surrounding beauties. As for the equipment, all apartments have the necessary number of beds in the form of beds with orthopedic mattresses, wardrobe cabinets, bedside tables, as well as mirrors. Tourists can use telephones, electronic safes and a minibar, but only for a fee. If necessary, an extra bed can be installed in the room in the form of a clamshell of the European type - this service is designed more for those tourists who come to rest with the whole family. As for electronics, the rooms have a plasma TV with satellite channels, as well as an electric kettle.



Tourists note in their comments that there are excellent conditions for people whose life is connected with doing business - for this category of guests there are two well-equipped conference rooms.

For the entertainment of adults and children, swimming pools with crystal clear water are equipped. Also, everyone can go play on the volleyball court or table tennis. The building has a paid gym with fitness equipment. Many tourists note the professional work of the coaching staff of the hotel. If desired, you can draw up a competent training program and a balanced diet.

Cleopatra Beach

Known in the entire resort city of Alanya, the Cleopatra beach is within walking distance of these hotels. Tourists of each of them have free access to the coastal line. According to them, everything is arranged here for a comfortable stay for both adults and children, but many are negative about the presence of a carriageway between hotel buildings and the beach.

The beach area is covered with fine and very soft sand, the descent into the water is shallow, and the depth grows gradually - this allows you to bring children here and have complete confidence in their safety.



For guests on the beach there are several locker rooms, toilets and showers. All of them are distributed evenly throughout the territory so that vacationers have easy access. If you wish, you can rent comfortable sun loungers, umbrellas, towels and air mattresses.

As for entertainment, there are a lot of them on the coastline. First of all, tourists are attracted by the opportunity to do water sports, for example, such as surfing, water skiing or skydiving. Fans of studying the underwater world can go down to the depths with scuba diving, but only under the guidance of a professional instructor. For the less daring, there is the opportunity to ride on air cushions or on a yacht that you can rent. The arsenal also has small boats and catamarans.

The safety of vacationers is regularly monitored by professional rescuers, who, if necessary, can use motor boats to go to the depths and provide emergency assistance.
