Hotel "History", Tula: address, opening hours, reception, selection of rooms and services

The small cozy hotel "History" in Tula offers its guests a European level of comfort and service. The hotel is suitable for both business and leisure travelers, given its convenient location. At the end of a busy day, you can fully relax, getting aesthetic pleasure from the interior design of the institution.

The address of the hotel "History" in Tula

The convenient location of the hotel plays a role for both business people and tourists.

The address of the Istoriya Hotel in Tula: 24. Shukhov Street, 24. It is a 10-minute drive from the city center and 15-20 minutes from the railway station. You can get here as follows:

  1. From the Moscow railway station - by minibuses No. 12, 15, 60, 61 to the stop "Shukhov Street".
  2. From the central bus station - by bus No. 28 to the stop "Ulitsa Shukhova" or minibus No. 33 to the stop "DK Kombaynostroiteley".
  3. From the RTI stop - by minibus No. 40K to the stop "Shukhov Street".
  4. From Zarechensky station - minibus No. 63 to the stop "Street Shukhov."
  5. From Ryazhsky railway station - minibus No. 63 to the stop "Street Shukhov."

Hotel "History" in Tula: rooms

Comfortable living conditions are the most important condition for a successful visit to any city. The number of rooms of the hotel "History" in Tula is represented by 29 comfortable guest rooms. Here are the available accommodation options:

  1. Suite is a luxurious two-room suite. The bedroom has a comfortable wide bed and a chest of drawers for personal items. The living room is equipped with a seating area for relaxation and additional accommodation. The cost of living is 4500 rubles per day.
  2. Standard - a cozy room of 20 square meters. m. There is everything to relax after a tiring road or a busy day. The room is equipped with a pair of twin beds (or one double), as well as a desk. The cost of living is 3000 rubles per day.
  3. Standard Plus differs from the previous category in an increased room area (25 m 2 ). There are also classic decor elements that make the room more comfortable. The cost of living is 3500 rubles per day.
