Today is Mother's Day: Mother's Day Applique

Applications can be made from almost everything that comes to mind and comes to hand. An interesting application for Mother's Day can be made of paper, paper napkins and candy wrappers.

Workflow preparation

In order to make such an application, you will need:

  • colored paper;
  • album sheet or cardboard sheet;
  • white paper napkins;
  • candy wrappers;
  • hole puncher;
  • paints;
  • tassels;
  • glue;
  • scissors;
  • clear nail polish.

If there is no colored paper, you can do with white sheets and with the help of paints make paper of the desired colors from them. An album or cardboard sheet is needed as a substrate. If there is no thick paper, you can glue together two simple sheets.





It is important to decide what will be depicted in the craft. The basis of future crafts

First of all, the foundation is being prepared. The background of the substrate should preferably be light, but this is a matter of taste. On the substrate, they draw thin lines with the outline of what they are about to portray.

The necessary details are cut out of colored paper - it can be a stalk and flower petals, butterfly wings or other elements of the applique. Now everything needs to be glued to the substrate.

Paper napkins

Napkins need to be torn into pieces with a diameter of about 1 cm and crumple them. But crushing to dense balls is not necessary. To paint the resulting little loose lumps with different bright colors. Or, if convenient, you can color the paper before tearing it.

It's okay that the paper gets wet through, you just need to let it dry (so as not to lose time while the wipes dry, you can do candy wrappers).





Now we paste the dried-up pieces of multi-colored napkins into the necessary places on the applications. They can be placed on the specks of butterfly wings, used for flowers. You can design them as a background of flying salutes. Or even put the whole flower out of them.

For example, a very beautiful application for mother's day will turn out if the whole branch of lilac is decorated with such lumps of napkins painted in lilac color. One lilac lump is one flower, and lilacs have a lot of them. Accordingly, they need to be glued in large quantities, close to each other.
