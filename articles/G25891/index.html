Entertainment for every taste

In Rimini there are many attractions of an entertaining nature that will not leave anyone indifferent. "Italy in Miniature" is a unique park where all the masterpieces of world art are represented. The park occupies 85 thousand square meters. About 300 miniatures are collected here. The scale of the work is from 1:50 to 1:25.

Another attraction of Rimini is Italy's largest dolphinarium, very popular in Europe. Colorful theatrical shows featuring dolphins regularly take place there. Here you can visit the dolphinarium museum, get acquainted with the development history and life of this wonderful and intelligent marine mammal.
