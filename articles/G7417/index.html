Holiday Village "Smile", Stary Oskol: reviews

Amazing nature, the breath of Russian antiquity, unique architecture annually attract thousands of tourists to these places. Chalet "Smile" (Stary Oskol) is one of the best places for a comfortable stay.

City Stary Oskol


Stary Oskol is a small ancient city on the banks of the Oskol River, located in the Belgorod Region, an important railway, industrial hub. Currently, this city can pleasantly surprise tourists with its hospitality, cleanliness and friendly residents. The Belgorod region is favorable in terms of climate, and from an environmental point of view, this area is considered clean. Stary Oskol is a promising city in terms of economic development. Over 40% of the industry of the region is concentrated here. The city is developing rapidly, its population is growing.

sights

Stary Oskol pleasantly impresses with its well-being, provincial comfort, neat lines of houses, an abundance of white-stone churches and a peculiar style. Architecture is the hallmark of this city. It is important that the historical identity and unique style have been preserved for posterity. In the center of Stary Oskol, you can find merchant wooden houses dating back to the 19th century. Even today it is divided into territorial units, which are called not districts, but settlements. This all reinforces the feeling that you were not in a modern, but in an old city, having traveled several centuries ago. Guests of Stary Oskol should definitely visit the Yamskaya Steppe nature reserve, the Old Russian settlement of 11-12 centuries, the Kholkovsky underground monastery in caves.

It is impossible not to admire in the center of Stary Oskol the structures of the former women's gymnasium, the Museum of Local Lore, the impressive Palace of Solemn Events, the mansion of the merchant Kobzev, the administration building. Here they remember and respect history, are proud of it. Local residents honor memorial complexes, monuments to the liberators of the city from the conquerors. The souvenir industry is also flourishing. Since the 18th century the city has been famous for adobe toys, which at all times were produced and sold here in abundance. The Art Museum has a souvenir shop, which is definitely worth a visit. Purchased toys and colorful trinkets will be a wonderful gift for friends.

Rest in these parts

Belgorod region is perfect for holidays due to its picturesque nature. Here you can enjoy your vacation in coniferous forests, on the banks of a reservoir or river. Locals call the reservoir the sea and love to come to its shores to relax and go fishing. In these parts you can expect a truly rich catch of carp, pike and perch. And in the fall there is a wonderful "quiet hunt" - picking berries and mushrooms. Fans of tent rest have long chosen these places due to the abundance of firewood, silence, and the beauty of nature.



Chalet "Smile" (Stary Oskol)

It is located in an ecologically clean place outside the city on the Starooskolsky reservoir, surrounded by forests, seven kilometers from Stary Oskol, one hundred kilometers from Voronezh and six hundred kilometers from Moscow. Initially, the camp site was built for the recreation of metallurgists from a local plant, but today it is open to all comers - both for the city residents and its guests.

A convenient road leads to the territory of the base; safe parking is provided for motorists. The recreation center "Smile" (Stary Oskol) offers various categories of rooms with a modern renovation and design for every taste and for different possibilities, from standard to luxury. In 87 rooms the camp site has everything you need for a long comfortable stay. Television, telephone, Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a hairdryer, shampoos and shower gels - all this is ready to provide guests with a recreation center "Smile" (Stary Oskol).
