Hotel Casa De Royal 3 * (India, Goa): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Anjuna is one of Goa's busiest resorts. Not only lovers of a relaxing beach holiday come here, but also those who are looking for all kinds of entertainment. Of course, the tourist infrastructure in this village is developed simply superbly. In any case, you can rent inexpensive, good housing in Anjuna without any problems. For example, very good rooms, according to many tourists, are for rent in the Casa De Royal 3 * hotel .

Where is the complex located

This hotel center is located directly in the village of Anjuna. The distance to the famous beaches of Chopar and Vagator is only 1.5 km. Russian tourists arrive in Goa most often, of course, by plane. Dabolim Airport is approximately 50 km from Anjuna. Tourists can get to the place by taxi or by the shuttle bus provided by the tour operator. In the first case, you will have to pay about 1 thousand rupees for the trip. Some vacationers travel to the hotel and simply take local bus services. The road at the same time costs, of course, much cheaper. However, getting to the hotel in this case will take about 4 hours. Which, of course, is not particularly convenient.





From Dabolim Airport, you can also simply take a train to Mapusa, and then take a taxi or take a shuttle bus. The distance from this city to Anjuna is only about 8 km.

General description of the hotel

Hosted by Anjuna, Casa De Royale Resort is housed in several colonial-style buildings. The courtyard of the hotel is well equipped and landscaped. The reception at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. All rooms in the complex are designed for non-smoking guests. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.

In total, the Hotel Casa De Royale 3 * (Casa de Royal) rents 16 comfortable rooms. If you wish, you can rent rooms of the class Standart, Superior, Deluxe Room or Standart Ac Room. All rooms have private showers. Also, residents of the center can use:

All rooms at the hotel include private balconies or patios. Some rooms are also air-conditioned. Fans at this hotel are available in all rooms. And they work very well. Projection panels are not installed in every room rented out at the hotel. Some rooms have only old tube TVs.

Guest opinion on hotel infrastructure

Good reviews are available not only about the rooms of this cozy hotel, but also about the service provided in it. According to most of the former residents of the Casa De Royal 3 * center , the staff here are quite polite and helpful. If tenants do not like the reserved room, they can be relocated to another without any problems. Sometimes for this you do not even need to contact the reception. Changing a room if guests are dissatisfied can even be just the hotel employee responsible for checking in.

The pool in this complex is like most of the guests. Reviews about him are only positive. Its depth is sufficient for comfortable swimming. The staff changes the water in the pool once every 10 days. At the same time, employees do not use too much bleach to disinfect it. Hotel workers immediately catch all trash falling into the water with a net. Swimming in the hotel pool is possible not only for adults, but also for children. Especially for them, a small shallow area is fenced off.

Casa De Royal 3 *: dining reviews

If necessary, tourists have the opportunity to rent rooms in this hotel including breakfast. The guests of the hotel are fed in the dining room from 7 am to 11 pm. Reviews about food in the center on the network are mostly good. For breakfast, residents have the opportunity to take two large toasts with butter, jam, juice, milk, coffee, tea. For an additional fee, you can take other dishes at the hotel, for example, scrambled eggs with bacon, fruit salad, etc.

Some tourists prefer not only breakfast at the hotel, but also dinner and lunch. Dishes are served quite tasty, fresh and always warmed up. Moreover, the cost of food in a hotel is no more expensive than in necks working on the coastline.

What you should know about

India is a poor country, and one of its problems is, unfortunately, theft. The fact that the Hotel Casa De Royal (3 stars) are trying things, none of its former guests, fortunately, never complained. However, everything of value in India is still worth carrying or deposited in a safe. This will help to avoid unnecessary problems. Experienced vacationers also recommend that beginners close the balcony door in their room just in case for the night.





There are more men in India than women. Apparently, because of this, local gentlemen always show an increased interest in tourists, including Russian women. Therefore, in too open dresses in the evenings alone, women are not recommended to walk in the streets of Anjuna. Some of our compatriots even talk about attacks on tourists on the net.
