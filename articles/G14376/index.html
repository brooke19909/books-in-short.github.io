The best skating rinks in Almaty

If you are looking for an ice rink in Almaty, this material will help you. Mass rides in the city begin when it is covered with snow. The skating rink is a place that can be visited by the whole family. Next, a description will be presented of the most popular objects that will allow you to relax with your loved ones.

Highlands

"Medeu" - an ice rink in Almaty. It is located at an altitude of 1691 meters above sea level in the mountains. You can visit this facility from morning until late in the evening, on any day except Monday. Address: Gornaya street 465, VSK "Medeu".

The skating rink keeps up to date and is regularly updated. The mountain valley was named Medeu, the name of the nomad who lived in the last century. He broke his village in the picturesque places where the rink is located today. The thickness of the ice cover here is 2.3 meters. Reinforced concrete plates are built into its surface.

170 kilometers of cooling pipes are distributed throughout the canvas. Separately, it is worth mentioning the system of artificial freezing of ice. It has no analogues in the world, and makes it possible to maintain the ice surface of the ice rink for eight months a year. Ice skating has become a good tradition for residents and visitors alike, especially on weekends.

The anti-mudflow dam is located at an altitude of 1733 m above sea level, 25 kilometers from Almaty. It was created to save the city from the mudflow, which is formed in the Western Tien Shan.

Mega ice

There is also an indoor ice rink in Almaty. It is located in the center of Mega Almaty. This place invites adults and children. The center is located at: Rozybakiev street 247a, Mega Center Almaty center. It works from 10 to 22 hours daily. For 30 minutes you will have to pay 1,100 tenge, with skate rental. Without it, the cost will drop to 800 tenge.

Mass skating

There is an ice rink in Gorky Park (Almaty), it is located in the Medeu district. The object was created specifically for mass skating. If you are interested in free skating rinks in Almaty, you should also visit it. The city leadership took up the creation of this facility in order to popularize an active lifestyle among both children and adults.

Other vacation spots

You can visit the skating rink in the Almaty Arena. The largest Ice Palace in the country opens mass skating every weekend. The guests of this institution have the opportunity to ride on a huge rink, as well as visit significant cultural and sports events. Address: Algabas microdistrict - 1, 7, Almaty Arena complex. From 11 to 13 hours an adult ticket costs 500 tenge.

There is an ice rink in Almaty and in the Halyk Arena ice palace. This complex has 3,000 beds and invites to mass rides at weekends. Here visitors will find: new skates for hire, good music, perfect ice coverage. Address: Kulzhinsky tract 2, Halyk Arena. From 11 to 13 hours the price for children is 300 tenge, for students and adults - 500.

You can also visit the Baluan Sholak Sports Palace. Here every Sunday they offer a pleasant and comfortable leisure time. Visitors will have the opportunity to eat and warm themselves with hot drinks, friendly staff, parking. Among the advantages of this rink can be called a convenient location. Address: 44 Abay Avenue, Baluan Sholak Sports Palace.

The skating rink also operates in the park of the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan. More precisely, not far from it. This complex is free. The ice platform is designed for mass skating. She works in the winter season. If weather conditions allow this, this period may be extended until mid-March. The initiator of this project is the Akimat of Almaty. It is located on Dulati Street.

There is also an ice rink near the Mart Shopping Center. It is free and designed for mass skating. This object is open to all residents and visitors. Its total area is 722 square meters. Here are areas for rental skates and fast food. Address: Richard Sorge street, 18. The visit is free, however, skate rental here will cost 600 tenge per hour of use.

The Abylay Khan ice rink should also be visited. It also operates for free and invites everyone who loves mass skating. It is also equipped with food, skate rental areas and nicely decorated sides. The facility is located at the intersection of Abylay Khan and Zhibek Zholy streets. Skate rental here will cost 600 tenge per hour of use.
