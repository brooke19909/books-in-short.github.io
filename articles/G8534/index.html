Lake Bannoe, Magnitogorsk: recreation centers, motels, ski resort

Nothing pleases after a long hard work, like a good rest. Lake Bannoe (Magnitogorsk) is one of the best places for this. Translation of the name - "bright water". The lake has always been attractive to vacationers, hunters and fishermen.





Bath lake has a good location. Nearby is the ski resort "Metallurg-Magnitogorsk." Therefore, not only fishermen and hunters are frequent guests in these parts. Fans of outdoor activities also appreciate the rest on Lake Bannoe. Magnitogorsk is famous for other picturesque places, but all of them can not be compared with the nature that surrounds the reservoir.

Rest on the lake

Around the reservoir there are many bases and holiday homes. In almost all, you can enjoy spa treatments. Connoisseurs of personal space can settle in bungalows and detached cottages located near the lake. Such housing is ideal for spending holidays or weekends in a large company. Here you can improve your health in one of the local sanatoriums. A lot of impressions will bring rest on Lake Bannoe. Magnitogorsk is visited most often in order to restore their strength and how to relax.

The recreation center "Birch"

One of the most visited places on the lake is the Berezki holiday home. Skiers and snowboarders are frequent guests of this base. It is located directly on the shores of Lake Bannoe (Magnitogorsk), not far from the ski resort.





The base has several residential buildings with different levels of comfort. The infrastructure is quite developed. Tourists can visit the spa complex, use the pool, dine in the cafe and rent sports equipment. In addition to the ski resort, a sports equestrian base is relatively close.

Getting to her is not difficult. You need to get to the city of Magnitogorsk in a way convenient for tourists. From there, direct taxis run to the base. If you go by your own transport, it is better to get to the city of Beloretsk, then go along the central highway and turn left at the Tashbulatovo-Yakty-Kul sign, and then go straight all the time. The distance from Magnitogorsk to Lake Bannoe is 50 km. It takes an hour and a half by car.

Sanatorium "Jubilee"

Near the Ural Mountains in the midst of beautiful nature is a cozy recreation complex "Yubileiny". The territory of the sanatorium captures Bashkiria and the Chelyabinsk region. Nearby is the city of Magnitogorsk. The proximity of ski resorts allows guests of the resort to combine treatment with active pastime. Throughout the season, a free shuttle service is provided from the sanatorium to the Metallurg ski resort.

There are also small buildings in the form of cottages. You can have a tasty lunch in the designated dining rooms or order an individual diet menu. Relax and have fun

In the evening, it is time for shows and dances. Vacationers can attend all kinds of entertainment programs or dance at a disco. Young people are invited to go to a club party or watch a movie. Sanatorium "Yakty-Kul"

If the bustle of the city begins to cause concern, then you can go to Lake Bannoe (Magnitogorsk). Recreation centers and sanatoriums here are always welcome new visitors. The health complex "Yakty-Kul" is located in the southeastern part of the Trans-Urals among picturesque forests. The resort specializes in mud therapy.

The main direction of the health resort is the use of silt-sapropel mud, which is in large quantities on the bottom of Bezymyannoye Lake. The resort also offers unique radon therapy. Also, the health resort practices the treatment of infertility. The cost of the tour includes the passage of seven procedures.





A few kilometers is the ski resort "Metallurg". The resort has its own equipped beach with a boat station. You can visit the indoor pool, bath, sauna, gym. Ski and bike rental is available. You can quench your thirst for gambling in the billiards room or on slot machines. There is a playroom for children. You can also use the services of a beautician. A convenience store and tour desk are on site.

From the sanatorium there is an opportunity to get to the ski complex for free. A library, hairdresser, laundry service, cafe and conference room are available for visiting. For housing, bedroom buildings with rooms of various levels of comfort are provided.
