Knitting coat for girls: schemes and description, photo

For many knitters, autumn becomes a truly magical time of the year, because during this period you can finally wear knitted pullovers, sweaters and cardigans. Especially rejoicing are those mother-craftswomen who managed to knit a coat for the girl with knitting needles. Schemes and description, photos of models and a drawing of a universal pattern - all this can be found in this article.



What is the specificity of a knitted coat

Externally, a coat and a cardigan can be very similar, but not in vain they have completely different names. Unlike making a long sweater or cardigan, knitters use yarn much thicker and try to get as tight a knit as possible when making a coat with knitting needles for a girl. Schemes and descriptions of the most common patterns ("rice", shawl, hosiery) are given in the following paragraphs.



As a rule, coats are worn on top of other clothes: a sweater, T-shirt, dress. Therefore, such a wardrobe item should be slightly larger and freer than a cardigan. Here it is necessary to provide not only wider details of the back and shelves, but also deep armholes and neck. If the armholes are small, the child will be very uncomfortable moving, especially raising their hands.

Do I need a lining

For many knitters, the question of the need to hem the lining fabric is extremely acute.

Opinions were divided almost equally, which gives each of us the right to independently make this decision. Of course, the lining is designed to make a knitted coat with knitting needles for a girl warmer and denser.
Schemes and a description of even the most continuous patterns will not help to get a knit of such a density.


Especially cool is a children's product with a lining on a synthetic winterizer. You can walk in such a coat even on winter days (of course, if there is no snow and the sun is shining). However, if the craftswoman does not cope with cutting and sewing, she may well leave the product as it is: a simple knitted coat with knitting needles for the girl. Schemes and descriptions of the most popular patterns consist of front and back loops, so that all work can be completed in just a few days.

It is worth adding that an excellent lining for knitted items is obtained from fleece, it, like knitwear, is a little springy and has a fleecy structure. Cut out details can even be sewn by hand, this will not be visible.



Hand knitting. Coats for girls with knitting needles: scheme, description

The photo below shows a universal model of a knitted coat. The pattern can hardly be called original, but the plus is that it is quite simple to make. The product will suit many things and accessories, and will also appeal to both girls and boys.



It will take from 450 to 750 grams of yarn to knit a coat for a girl with knitting needles. Schemes and description (4-5 years, 5-6 years, 7-8 years, 9-10 years, 11-12 years, 13-14 years) provide the opportunity to make a product of any size.



Knitting needles can be used straight or circular. Their number should strictly correspond to the yarn used, you can even take the tools a little thinner than indicated on the material label. This technique allows you to get a tighter knit, but this is not suitable for those who tend to tighten the loops.

Patterns should be drawn in full size and cut out so that details can be applied to them.

The preparatory stage should also include knitting a control sample. This is necessary in order to get the correct calculations and to know the number of loops forming the first rows of the back, shelves and sleeves.

Production of basic parts

Focusing on the control sample and on the pattern, you need to dial loops for knitting the back. The first 3-5 rows, it is desirable to perform a garter stitch (all loops in all rows are made facial), so that the fabric does not curl. Then you need to go to hosiery (there is a front and wrong side).

The drawing shows a trapezoidal coat with knitting needles for a girl. The diagrams and description can be easily simplified and connected to the product evenly.

When the fabric is connected to the level of the armholes, it is required to reduce 3-7 loops on both sides (depending on size). Then, on each side in each front row, cut 3 times in 1 loop.

Next, they knit exactly until you need to make the neck:

  • The middle 7-12 loops are transferred to a thread or knitting pin.
  • Further, each shoulder is knitted separately. In each front row, they cut 3 times in one loop (from the neck).
  • Lead the canvas smoothly.
  • Collect shoulder loops on the thread and leave until the shelves are ready. Combining the loops of their shoulder parts, the craftswoman sews the parts with a knitted seam.
  • The second shoulder is done similarly.

Shelves are knitted almost the same way as a half back:

  1. Loops are gathered taking into account the width of the strap (5-10 loops) and several rows of garter stitches are performed.
  2. They switch to hosiery, but they knit the strap with a garter. When working on the left shelf, you need to remember about the holes for the buttons.
  3. Tie up to the armholes and form rounded cuts.
  4. Make a neck.

Knitting sleeves and hood

The cuff of the sleeve is made exactly with a garter stitch, then they switch to the hosiery and raise the fabric to the level of the armhole. At the same time, 3-5 loops on each side are added uniformly (approximately after 5-8 cm).

The knit is knitted as follows:

  • In each front row, 3-5 loops are reduced by 1 loop.
  • Sew open loops to the armhole of the front and back with a knitted seam.

Using this method of joining parts is the only way to avoid the appearance of coarse and ugly seams.

Loops previously set aside are transferred to circular knitting needles and the remaining loops get along the neckline. This is the first row of the hood. He is knitted with hosiery, but the bar continues to be woven.

At a height of 20-25 cm, the canvas is folded in half and open stitches are sewn together.

Then they knit pockets, decorate them with tassels and sew in the right places.

Alternatives

If desired, you can use other patterns to knit a coat for a girl with knitting needles.

Schemes and description of the chessboard ornament:

  1. The number of loops must be a multiple of six.
  2. Four rows of knit hosiery.
  3. 6 facial loops alternate with 6 wrong loops.
  4. Repeat item number 2.
  5. 6 reverse loops with 6 front loops alternate.

To form a further pattern, repeat the sequence from point 2. The finished coat can be decorated as you like: here applications, embroidery, labels and stripes will be appropriate.
