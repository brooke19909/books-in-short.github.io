"Enkhaluk" (Baikal): rest on the base, photos, reviews of tourists

Baikal is an amazing lake in the central part of the Asian continent. Locals affectionately call it the holy sea. The unique combination of cool winds, burning sun, crystal clear water, refreshing air and absolute silence make this place favorable for relaxation. Baikal is beautiful in any season. There are many picturesque corners.





One of such properties of Baikal is the village of Enkhaluk with mixed forests, wide sandy beaches, wild and rich nature. In this wonderful place the recreation center "Enkhaluk" was built. Baikal attracts tourists with pristine beauty, serenity and a welcoming atmosphere. Thousands of people come here annually, not only from Russia, but also from far abroad. A comfortable climate, transport accessibility and a high level of comfort are what attract travelers.

Location

The Enkhaluk camp site is located on Lake Baikal directly near the deep-sea lake, 6 km from Cape Proval. The complex really lives up to its name, which means grace. In this place, directly surrounded by a golden beach and forest, you begin to understand the meaning of life and the insignificance of worldly problems. You will plunge headlong into the "pool" of pleasure and peace.





The boarding house is quite large, designed for 100 people. Scientific conferences, presentations and seminars are often held here. The hotel has created excellent conditions for solving business problems. Courteous staff will always come to the aid of corporate clients - organize a feast in nature or a restaurant. Not for nothing that the hotel enjoys enormous popularity both among the local population and nonresident people.

Residence





There will be no problems with accommodation even in winter. The Enkhaluk base on Lake Baikal offers accommodation in comfortable two-story cottages for 29 people. The building has standard rooms and apartments. The rooms were heated, new furniture was installed, the necessary equipment and a bathroom were allocated. Each visitor is given bedding and hygiene products.

In addition to cottages, real national Mongolian yurts for summer holidays are built on the territory. They stand a little further from the main building and are small villas decorated in a national style. Each yurt has beds, electricity, a stove-stove and satellite TV. Facilities (WC / shower) are located in the courtyard. In addition to yurts for accommodation there are wooden campsites (triple).

In warm time, such housing is very popular among tourists at the Enkhaluk boarding house (Baikal). Guest reviews indicate that living in such villas helps reunite with nature. Also, guests were pleasantly surprised by the affordable rates for yurts and interior decoration.

Catering





For a complex diet will have to pay about 750 rubles. It includes a full breakfast, a balanced lunch and a delicious dinner. Meals are organized in a restaurant with 80 seats. The menu contains not only Buryat national dishes, but also Russian and European dishes. For those who want to diversify their holiday on Lake Baikal, Enkhaluk offers to eat in a yurt restaurant with an open area.





You will be surprised by the gourmet cuisine in the institution, professional service and a festive interior that sets in a positive mood. To the sounds of "live" music you can dance and have fun. The restaurant accepts orders for banquets, corporate gatherings and anniversaries. If desired, guests can independently prepare on the grill various dishes.

Coastline





A gently sloping shore with a sandy coating and thickets of taiga forest is concentrated within walking distance from the residential building Enkhaluk (Baikal). The beach is equipped with sports fields for active games, changing cabins and a toilet. It offers vacationers a rental point for renting catamarans, boats, air mattresses and deck chairs. There is a marina for water transport. There is a summer bar - selling sweets and soft drinks for guests. In the evenings, entertainment programs are organized right on the beach with a disco.

Active leisure





People come not only for beach vacations on Lake Baikal. "Enkhaluk" (photo presented in the article) - this is also informative and fun entertainment in the bosom of untouched nature. The complex has created ideal conditions for playing sports. A tennis court and even a small ice rink are on site.

Volleyball and football fields are at the disposal of tourists. Golf enthusiasts will appreciate the mini-hole course. It features a billiard room with a large table. You will receive unbelievable pleasure from visiting a Russian bath.

It is located in a separate wooden structure. What a sauna without a cool pool and relaxation room? First-class amenities you will experience. In addition, a gym is open with horizontal bars, horizontal bars, exercise machines and other sports equipment.

Sightseeing tours at the hotel





Works in the boarding house "Enkhaluk" (Baikal) its own travel agency, which offers informative walking, helicopter and water routes in the suburbs. A trip to the hot springs in the village "Zagza tract" is organized together with a guide. Guests can take a dip in the healing waters and improve their health. You will be taken to the village of Sukhaya, where the water in the lake has healing qualities.

An indelible impression will be made by a trip to the Omulevsky Baikal Maritime Museum with a rich collection of exhibits: masts, mock-up of the longboat, compass, flashlight. A pleasant tour will be made by a tour to Bird Harbor - here you will observe the life of gulls, geese, herons and other outlandish inhabitants. Baikal has absorbed many amazing objects.

Discount system

Good discounts are offered by the Enkhaluk (Baikal) boarding house for its guests. For children under the age of five, bedding with parents is completely free. A 50 percent discount is given to children under 12 years old - on a couch or a folding bed (meals are paid separately). For vacationers who come to rest in large groups (over 10 people) - 5% discount on accommodation. For further information, please contact the hotel administrator.

Chalet "Enkhaluk" (Baikal): how to get to the place?

You can get to the village both by personal transport and by bus from Irkutsk (6 hours), Ulan-Ude - 3 hours will take the journey, Selenginsk - in 1.5 hours. The section of the route passing at the distillation of Nikolsk Enkhaluk is a gravel road more difficult in contrast to the M55.

They built a bridge, which is closed in winter. Motorists need to refuel in the village of Baikal-Kudara, since there are no more gas stations further. In the village of Oymur should be extremely careful because of the poor road. Before check-in, we recommend that you check in advance the availability of seats in the boarding house "Enkhaluk" (Baikal).
