Pension "Turgoyak": description, reviews, how to get

For more than 95 years, the boarding house on Lake Turgoyak has been hosting visitors on its territory who wish to not only have a good rest, but also get a little healthier. Its conditions are ideal for relaxing with the whole family.

general information

Pension "Turgoyak" is located in a picturesque place where clean and fresh air reigns. 30 meters from it is an incredibly beautiful reservoir - a lake of the same name, which attracts the attention of tourists.

As for the geography of the location of this recreation area, the city of Miass (17 km away) is located not far from it, and the nearest large and densely populated point is Chelyabinsk (89 km).

You can come to rest in this place at any time of the year, because it is beautiful and there is something to do in any season: tourists can sunbathe on the lake, play sports, swim or just sit in a friendly company around a fire.



Rooms

The room fund of the Turgoyak boarding house (Miass) is located in two buildings. Also on its territory there are two cottages that can also be rented - they are made of a natural wooden log house and are very comfortable arranged inside. All numbers presented are divided into two categories: "Drummer" and "Patron". In the photo of the Turgoyak pension, presented in the article, you can see what is offered to all vacationers in the apartments.

In summer, tourists can stay on the lake - there is a zone for a tent city. For a fee, travelers have the opportunity to set up their own tent here and relax in it for a certain time.



"Drummer"

Class rooms "Drummer" offer guests a very comfortable environment. They are designed to accommodate 2-5 people in one space.

Among the amenities here is comfortable modern furniture, an individual bathroom and a shower cabin, to which a standard set of bath accessories is attached. From the furniture there is the right amount of sleeping places in the form of single beds, cabinets for things, a table. Three rooms of this type have a refrigerator, which is very convenient for residents. The cost of "Drummer" costs guests 700 rubles per person per day; if a refrigerator is installed in it, then the cost rises to 800 rubles. Some rooms of this category have a private balcony, the presence of which will increase the amount of payment up to 900 rubles.

"Maecenas"

The rooms of the Turgoyak boarding house of the Patron class are offered to be placed on its territory of 14 sq.m. 3-4 people.

Of furniture there is also the necessary number of beds, beds and cabinets for things. The room has a small amount of electronics - a refrigerator, stove and kettle, as well as a small kitchenette. In addition to all this, all apartments are equipped with individual bathrooms and showers.

The cost of such apartments is 1000 rubles per day.

Camping

Tourists who are interested in camping can pay the owners of the recreation area for a separate place on its territory and camp with their own tent. The cost of such a vacation is 500 rubles per day.

Mangal zone

Many tourists come to the boarding house "Turgoyak" in order to have a good rest on the lake, to fish and grill kebabs. For such purposes, a separate zone is equipped here, equipped with barbecue facilities and relaxation areas - gazebos. It is designed for 20 people, which allows you to organize a fun picnic on its territory. There is always a sale of charcoal, nets for frying meat, as well as firewood and disposable tableware. For younger guests in the brazier zone there is a swimming pool, air mattresses and slides.

Throughout the territory in order to maintain the safety of tourists, anti-mite treatment is regularly carried out.

The cost of renting such a territory is small and amounts to only 200 rubles per hour, and if you need a gazebo, it increases to 500 rubles.

Prices for services and entertainment in the boarding house "Turgoyak"

The description of this recreation area says that here you can not only relax well, looking at the lake, but also engage in active sports. In particular, for all guests of the boarding house, a shooting platform is equipped - a shooting range, an area with tables for table tennis, air hockey, a sports trampoline.

In addition to all this, vacationers of the Turgoyak boarding house have the opportunity to rent cars, scooters and bicycles. All this is done at an additional cost, separate from the cost of living.

For business

On the territory of the boarding house there are some conditions that may contribute to the conduct of business. In one of the buildings of the sanatorium there is a carefully equipped conference room with a stage, which is ideal for holding business meetings, conferences, business negotiations and other purposes. It is designed for 90 comfortable places, in front of which a huge projector with good sound equipment is installed. The hall has modern speakers and a microphone.

The cost of renting a conference room is 500 rubles per hour.

Nutrition

Vacationers have the opportunity to eat on their own, using the improvised means that are available in their rooms. However, on the territory of the sanatorium there is a public dining room, which serves delicious homemade dishes. The cost of a comprehensive three meals a day in it is 600 rubles per day per person.

In addition to the dining room, there is a summer cafe in Turgoyak. All guests can purchase the necessary products in the store, which is a 15-minute walk from the pension.

Guest reviews

All guests of the boarding house share their impressions received during the holiday. Most of them are satisfied with the provided conditions and atmosphere. They leave their reviews about the Turgoyak boarding house on its official website, on social networks, and also on thematic forums.

Often, vacationers celebrate the magnificent nature, among which is the resort area. It is she who sometimes attracts such a large flow of vacationers. Trees grow around the boarding house, it is located on the lake, clean air is everywhere - what could be better? Acceptable is the pricing policy that operates in Turgoyak - many guests consider it quite reasonable and do not spare the money paid. Most of them also prefer to use the services of a public dining room - many note the excellent taste of the food cooked in it.

Vacationers appreciate the conditions that are provided by the leadership of the base for children - everything is provided for their comfortable pastime, there are not only toys, but also areas for outdoor activities.

However, against the background of positive comments, sometimes those that are more likely to be classified as negative are also distinguished. In particular, some guests advise, before arriving at the sanatorium, once again to make sure that the desired room is exactly booked, as sometimes, according to them, there are misunderstandings in the form of lack of reservation for the apartment.

Boarding house address and how to get

The sanatorium is located in the Turgoyak forestry of the Miass forestry, on the shore of a local picturesque lake.

How to get to the boarding house "Turgoyak"? First of all, you need to decide which side the access will be from - from Yekaterinburg or Zlatoust.

In the first case, everything is very simple: from the city center of Miass you need to follow in accordance with the road signs, moving along the highway of Bogdan Khmelnitsky. If the road lies through Zlatoust, you should immediately turn left after the Bresel Brauhaus restaurant - this road will lead to the village, which is called Turgoyak. On the main highway you need to move to the fork, and upon reaching it go straight ahead - this road will lead straight to the boarding house.
