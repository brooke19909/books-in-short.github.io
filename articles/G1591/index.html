Samantha Smith Who is that girl?

Born in June 1972 in an ordinary American family, Samantha Smith was a smart and inquisitive American girl from the state of Maine (USA). Like every child, she asked various questions about the world and the events that took place. Probably, a certain natural courage of Samantha prompted her to write a letter to the leader of the USSR, which turned her whole life upside down.

Samantha Smith Letter

The story of how a little girl decided on such an unexpected act for a child of 10 years old seems normal for our time, but for the eighties of the twentieth century it was a rather extraordinary step. The period of the Cold War and the Iron Curtain left its mark on the views of people and their actions. The people behind the curtain seemed different, vicious and full of treachery. The ideological machines on both sides worked to blacken each other and justify the buildup of military power.

Once, when she saw in The Magazine a photograph of the new leader of the USSR, Yuri Andropov, with material about his danger to the United States and the whole world, Samantha wondered why he wanted to destroy her country. The girl then wrote a letter to Yury Andropov, who was only elected to the post of General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, with a simple but at the same time complex question. She asked why Andropov wanted to take over the world and whether he would start a nuclear war against the United States. If it were not for the will of fate, then perhaps this letter would have remained dusty in the endless archives of the KGB. But some far-sighted and wise official found him. This man came up with how to use the message of an American schoolgirl in the ideologically correct direction.

There was a cold war, and both sides tried by all means to debunk and convict each other, each chance to prove the erroneous actions of the opponent was important. To achieve this goal, it was decided to use the letter of Samantha Smith. It was published on the pages of the all-Union newspaper "Pravda" and showed all the duplicity of the United States.

Most likely, this persistence forced Yuri Andropov to write an answer and invite Samantha to visit the USSR in order to verify the good intentions of the Country of Soviets.
