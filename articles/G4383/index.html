Ramada Hotel Suites Sharjah 4 * (United Arab Emirates, Sharjah): photos and reviews of tourists

The United Arab Emirates is a country that attracts wanderers from all over the world thanks to a unique combination of ancient culture and modern technology. And many tourists give their preference to the large, beautiful city of Sharjah. This is a large resort, on the territory of which there is everything from beaches and secluded cafes, to colorful markets and giant ultramodern shopping centers.

Of course, in the city of Sharjah there are a large number of hotels of different categories and sizes. One of the most popular places is the hotel complex Ramada Hotel Suites Sharjah 4 *. This place is famous for its service and good living conditions. But what do the tourists themselves say? Do travelers like accommodation at the Ramada Hotel Suites Sharjah 4 *? Reviews, room descriptions, food conditions, remoteness of the beach and city attractions - all this interests many people planning a vacation in the UAE.

Location Features





Location is extremely important, therefore, every experienced traveler pays attention to this factor. Where is the hotel complex Ramada Hotel Suites Sharjah 4 *? UAE, a busy area in the resort city of Sharjah - this is where many tourists like to spend time.

It is worth noting that this is a city-style hotel - there are other hotels and commercial high-rise buildings nearby. The distance to the beach is slightly less than 8 km, although this is unlikely to be a problem, because the hotel provides guests with a comfortable and, importantly, free shuttle service.

The international airport is just 7 km away, which is also convenient. By the way, for an additional fee, the hotel staff will be happy to find transport for you. Just a two-minute walk is the huge Sahara shopping center, which is sure to appeal to shoppers. The hotel provides a free shuttle service to and to one of the most famous places in the UAE - the Deira shopping center.

What does a hotel complex look like?

Should I stay at this hotel? What do tourists say about Ramada Hotel Suites Sharjah 4 *? The hotel rating is quite high, and the price of accommodation is not too high.

Room categories and their characteristics

Ramada Hotel Suites Sharjah 4 * - a large hotel complex, which consists of 245 rooms. Tourists are offered a choice of several categories of rooms. For example, you can stay in spacious luxury rooms - there is one large king size bed or two single beds.

Many travelers like hotel apartments, which consist of a large bedroom with a door, as well as a kitchenette, a cozy dining room and a relaxation room with soft sofas. By the way, there are rooms with two bedrooms, which are ideal for a large family or, for example, a group of friends.

Is the hotel suitable for a beach holiday?





It is difficult for many tourists to imagine a truly exciting vacation without walking on the seashore. So, is the Ramada Hotel Suites Sharjah 4 * hotel complex suitable for a beach vacation? Sharjah Beach, a scenic coastline, is approximately 7-8 km from the hotel building. Of course, the distance is large, and travelers are unlikely to like it every day on their own. That is why a convenient shuttle service goes directly to the beach from the hotel - by the way, you can use it for free, which, in fact, is done by guests of the Ramada Hotel Suites Sharjah 4 *.

Sharjah Beach is a beautiful place that tourists from all over the world have already chosen to love. Here you will find clear sea waters, soft sand, gentle sun and, of course, a lot of ways to have fun.

You can visit public beaches, which are often crowded. But for a small fee, you can rent a comfortable lounger for the whole day or visit the colorful bar. There is an opportunity to engage in other, more active and interesting types of recreation.

For example, diving is a popular attraction among travelers - on the shore you can easily rent equipment or take quick training courses (followed by a certificate), as well as get an excursion to the most picturesque places. According to reviews, such a swim can bring a lot of pleasant impressions, because the underwater scenery is simply breathtaking.

Of course, here you can ride a boat, water skiing, boats and other types of water transport. Travelers love sailing and windsurfing. And on the beach, various contests, funny entertaining events, sports competitions and parties are constantly held - no one is bored here.
