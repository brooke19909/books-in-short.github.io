Children's camps in England: overview, features and reviews

A camp in England is a place where many children and teenagers would like to go. Friends from other countries, unforgettable impressions, learning new languages, playing sports - all this awaits the children who come to the UK for holidays. Choosing a good camp that meets the preferences of the child can be difficult, and this article will help you.

Benefits of British Camps

There are many reasons why you should send your child to a camp in England at least once.

  • Children and adolescents are immersed in the classical language environment, which helps to improve such skills as speaking and listening, which are necessary for communicating in English,
  • British education is recognized as one of the best in the world, so the experience of studying in England will benefit the child.
  • In foreign camps, children get the opportunity not only to learn the language through daily communication, but also to engage in creativity, sports and art. Some British camps have programs for teaching children to ride, swim, and team games.
  • A child will fall into an international environment, because children from all over the world come to Britain.
  • English camps organize cultural activities, so children will have the opportunity to see with their own eyes the most famous sights of the United Kingdom.

Accommodation Features

A camp in England can be located in the center of a large city or beyond. Often, such establishments can be found on the maps of London, Brighton and Winchester. Living here, the guys feel like residents of noisy cities. If the choice fell on a camp surrounded by picturesque nature, then its inhabitants will be able to spend their vacations as tourists staying in a fashionable hotel.

Children can accommodate themselves directly on campus campuses, school residences and English families. Living in a residence implies that the child will live in a 1-4-bed room in full board. Educators will be nearby. Host families usually have older teens and students who wish to immerse themselves in the English lifestyle. This will allow the children to study the traditions and life of the British from the inside. Host families are thoroughly screened by the British Board of Education. The hotel will cost the most. It is suitable for those people who came to England for a short time.

Types of vacation programs

As mentioned earlier, British education is considered one of the best in the world. Pupils and students have the opportunity to choose the programs for which they will be engaged during the training period. The guys who arrived on vacation can do the same.

  • The language camp in England for teens allows you to study in the classical linguistic program and have a great rest. The course is designed for those guys who want to tighten up their language skills, but do not spend all their free time reading books.
  • An intensive language program is designed for those who wish to improve their knowledge in the shortest possible time. Each week, more than 30 hours of foreign language classes are allocated. Evening relaxation and weekend entertainment are provided by the program.
  • English and sports is another program that allows young athletes to improve their language skills without interruption from the training process. Camps in England are very popular, in which swimming, football, sports dancing and equestrian sports are practiced.
  • English and in-depth study of additional subjects is a program focused on preparing for university entrance examinations. You can study several disciplines to choose from, for example, physics and mathematics or biology and chemistry.
  • Family vacations is a program whereby not only a child, but also his parents can come to Britain. English classes are held in different groups, but accommodation and excursions are joint.

Brighton College Summer Camp

Brighton College was founded in 1845, since then it has confidently gained popularity among students from many countries. Currently, it is considered one of the most prestigious educational institutions in England. Located in the city of the same name, it is within walking distance of beaches with piers. The territory is under constant protection, as noted in their reviews by parents who sent their children here.

Children aged 9 to 17 years can take a program aimed at improving language skills, as well as take a break from school. The cost of living is about 880 euros per week (64,500 rubles). Students are accommodated in 1-3-bed rooms. Bathroom and shower rooms are shared. The guys are kept in full board.

Teenagers who have visited this language camp in England are delighted with classes with highly qualified teachers. Learning English can be combined with courses in acting, drama, physics, chemistry, biology and mathematics, the basics of world religions. You can attend training in volleyball, golf, rugby, tennis, football. If desired, children can start learning other foreign languages, even the dead: Spanish, Italian, Russian, French, Greek and Latin.

In their free time, a large number of recreational and sports events take place, excursions to London and Portsmouth are organized, as well as cruises on the Thames. Students can use the gym, tennis courts and indoor heated pool. The children note that the holidays at Brighton College are very productive, upon arrival at home, adolescents improve their language, communication skills and physical fitness.

Tottenham Football Camp

This international language camp in England allows children not only to study English, but also to learn how to play football. Boys aged 10-17 can come here on vacation. The camp is located at the base of Royal Holloway University, in a suburb of London. Foreign language classes are held with highly qualified teachers, football training - with professional athletes. In addition to English classes and training, for the children they organize parties, discos, excursions to London and Wembley Stadium.

Tottenham is a camp in England that lets kids know how it feels to live on campus. Teenagers are accommodated in double rooms. The disadvantage of the campus that the boys celebrate is that all the amenities are on the floor. The price includes three meals a day. A stay in the camp for 2 weeks will cost almost 2 thousand euros (147 000 rubles).

The campus houses educational buildings and sports facilities. There is a library and a computer room with internet access. Football fields, a sports hall, playgrounds - all this is freely available for boys, which is very pleasing to young football players.

Academic Summer Camp (Abbey DLD London)

Children aged 13-18 years can visit a camp in England for teenagers. Located in London, it can accept 200 students at a time. Training programs are aimed at preparing for international exams in English. Students can take an advanced course of elective academic subjects or attend medical training classes. Weekly adolescents expect 30 hours of classes, after which the guys can have some fun, for example, visit a disco, take part in a bowling tournament or a talent show, play intellectual quizzes and watch interesting films with friends.

In addition to studying academic English, the camp in England allows students to prepare for exams such as Oxbridge, IELTS, SAT. The cost of the voucher directly depends on the chosen course. Training in the above programs will cost 1300-1700 euros per week (95-125 thousand rubles). The course of medical training lasts 2 weeks, its cost exceeds 3 thousand euros (220 000 rubles).

Students can choose one of two forms of education: full-time or boarding. The second option is preferable for foreigners. In the residence, the guys are accommodated in bedrooms with private bathrooms and toilets. Food occurs three times a day. The food here is very satisfying and delicious, as noted by adolescents who have visited the camp. Teachers and educators are around the clock in the residence, so parents can not worry about the safety of the child.

The huge advantage of this camp in England with the study of the English language, which many students note in their reviews, is a large number of field trips. Weekly guys have the opportunity to see London, visit Oxford and Cambridge, the Windsor Royal Residence and other sights of England. Trips to Brighton, Winchester, Canterbury are organized.

Concord College Summer Camp

One of the best boarding schools that implement summer vacation programs is Consord College, located near Shrewsbury. Founded in 1949, the school specializes in preparing students for international exams and entrance examinations at British universities. Education takes place outside the city, in the picturesque countryside, so the guys can not only improve their language skills, but also take a break from the bustle of the city.

In the camp you can choose one of the destinations. In addition to learning English, children will be able to learn horse riding, play tennis or play golf. The cost of living in a residence for 3 weeks is about 2900-3100 euros (approximately 212-227 thousand rubles).

Children aged 9-17 are placed in 2-3-bed rooms. Facilities on the floor. On the territory of the residence there are educational buildings, computer classes, a library, playgrounds, stables, playpen and all the necessary equipment for horse riding.

In the evenings and on weekends, children and adolescents can take part in recreational activities and sports competitions. Students note that excursions to London, Chester, Cardiff, Birmingham, Oxford, Liverpool and Shrewsbury are organized in an English camp in England.
