Resorts of Indonesia: description, reviews, photos

On the border of two oceans are thirteen thousand islands of Indonesia. Here you can spend hours alone on the morning beaches, enjoy the magnificent nature, admire the waves that roll on the white sand, and taste fresh and juicy fruits that grow in abundance on this generous land. Resorts in Indonesia are well known to our compatriots. In recent years, thousands of tourists from Russia have spent unforgettable holidays on the islands of Lombok, Bali, Bintan and others. Today we will tell you about these heavenly places.

Choose a resort

Each of us, when planning his vacation, sets priorities when choosing a resort. What is more important for you - an exotic country or a short flight, a comfortable hotel room or economical accommodation, the opportunity to have fun and actively spend time or the desire to be alone and take a break from the bustle of the city?



Choosing the resorts of Indonesia, you should know that the path to these paradise places is not close. Twelve-hour flights are difficult for small children and elderly people to tolerate. This inconvenience is practically not noticed by middle-aged people and young people, for whom the resorts of Indonesia with white sand do not seem so far away, for them there is no significant obstacle to the relaxation they dreamed about, the time difference.

It should be noted that exotic is not to everyone's taste, but the resorts of Indonesia are equipped with comfortable hotels, where everything is organized at a European level. In cozy restaurants you can always find dishes from different cuisines of the world, including Russian.

Bali

This most popular and most visited island is located in Southeast Asia and is part of the Indonesian archipelago. Its highest point is Gurung Agung (active volcano, 3,142 m).

The relief of the island is mountainous in the north and terraced in the south. The total area is 5633 square meters. km It stretches 150 km from east to west, and 80 km from north to south. The population is about three million people. Denpasar is the capital of Bali.



Among tourists, the island is famous for its delightful resorts. Kuta, Nusa Dua and Sanur, located in the south of the island, are the most popular, according to tourists, resorts in Indonesia. They are dominated by yellow fine sand, and on the northern coast (Amed, Lovina) - black volcanic.

What to see on the island

Bali is often called the island of 1000 temples, although in reality there are more than 2000. Local travel companies organize interesting excursions to the mountains. A park of reptiles and birds will not leave anyone indifferent. Elephant safari, rafting, one day tour to the neighboring island of Lombok - everyone will be able to choose a vacation to their liking.

Bintan

The resorts of Indonesia, which tour operators immediately offer to see, win the hearts of travelers instantly.

Bintan Island is located at the equator, so there is a tropical even climate. Air temperature ranges from +21 to +32 degrees. Humidity - from 61 to 96%. Usually the island is sunny all year, from October to March it rains more often and the wind intensifies. All hotels on the island are open all year.



Bintan resorts are pristine beaches with snow-white fine sand, bordered by smoothly swaying palm trees, crystal clear sea and lush vegetation. All this leads to relaxation and bliss, which are necessary for a good rest.

sights

In modern Bintan, the past and the present are closely intertwined. In the southern part of the island are fishing villages, characterized by a leisurely rhythm of life. Those who wish can visit the mausoleums, the royal mosque. Here you will be shown the ruins of the royal palace, which are a reminder of the monarchical past of the island.

Tourists are offered to learn the traditional fishing methods or take a boat ride to the mangroves to find out the nature of the tropics. If you want to learn more about the culture and everyday life of the local population, visit pineapple plantations, watch women who weave various souvenirs and bags from pandan leaves.

After a busy day filled with excursions and new experiences, you can treat yourself to a relaxing massage. A huge number of SPA centers work here.

Lombok

The resorts of Indonesia, photos of which you can see in our article, are all very different, but they are united by an unusually beautiful nature. Lombok is part of the southern chain of islands overlooking the Indian Ocean, which are located east of Bali.

The name of the island comes from the word lombok, which in translation from the Indonesian language means "hot pepper". The fact is that the island resembles pepper in its shape. It is the highest volcano in Indonesia - Gunung Rinjani.



Lombok Island attracts lovers of a relaxing holiday. Crowded beaches, the absence of noisy and annoying tourists will allow you to completely relax. Here you can go in for exciting diving, explore ancient temples and magnificent pagodas. Locals of the island are friendly and welcoming. Those who wish can familiarize themselves with the skills of weavers and potters.

The climate of Lombok Island is drier and softer than in Bali, so it is ideal for relaxing in any season.

Java

The islands of Indonesia attract tourists from all over the world because they can have a great rest at any time of the year. To do this, it is not necessary to wait for the beach season.

Indonesia is not in vain called the country of the eternal sun. It is always comfortable here. Even if it sometimes starts to rain, then it ends very soon, without even leaving small puddles. It resembles, rather, a refreshing shower. Resorts in Indonesia in January also have a good rest, as in July. The air temperature in this winter month rises to +30, and water - to +29.



Things to do

Java is famous not only for beach holidays. For tourists it is of great interest to get acquainted with the capital of Indonesia, Jakarta; the ancient city of Yogyakarta - the cultural capital of the country; the famous volcano Krakatau, known throughout the world; the oldest national reserve Ujung-Kulon, in which Javan rhinos feel very comfortable; stunning temple complexes Prambanan and Borobudur.

In the center of Jakarta on Freedom Square, you will see the Monas National Monument, which is a marble obelisk. Its height is 132 meters, but its main feature is in the golden flames, which took thirty-five kilograms of gold to make. You can take the elevator to the observation deck, which is built on top of the monument, and see the magnificent panorama of Jakarta. An hour's drive from the capital is Bogor, a popular resort famous for its botanical garden.

Sumatra

Resorts of Indonesia are located on the most western island - Sumatra. Here, tourists will be offered to visit Mount Lauser National Park, where rare and endangered species of animals live.



Lake Toba is the largest in southeast Asia. It is located in Sumatra and is famous for its unusually picturesque views. Guests of the island are offered to engage in a variety of water sports - water skiing, surfing, enjoy boat trips.

Resorts Indonesia: reviews

Everyone who was lucky enough to relax in the resorts of Indonesia unanimously agree that the luxurious nature of these places deserves to be seen at least once.



Divers are happy with the rest in these places - the underwater world of Indonesia is unusually beautiful. In addition, this type of recreation is well developed here - you can rent the necessary equipment, get advice and advice from experienced instructors.

Many travelers note the opportunity to choose the hotel according to their own taste and financial capabilities. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and the work of the staff of hotels, hotels, restaurants is beyond praise.
