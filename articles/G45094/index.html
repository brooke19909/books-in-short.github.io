Change in Russia

Previously, it was not possible to change the carrier, retaining the phone number. The changes took effect only in December 2013. And today, this is the right of every user. However, each operator sets its own conditions for such a transition. It does not bother to sort them out and find out what's what.

Let's start with Beeline

So, how to change the operator, keeping the number? The Beeline company will be happy to meet everyone who has decided to switch to it from another operator. However, you will have to pay 100 rubles one-time. These funds will be debited from your account during the first three days of using the network. And now about how to do this:

  1. Are you going to change the operator? You can leave the same number as follows: you contact the Beeline office, where you write an application to transfer the number and connect to the new network. Take your passport with you if the number is issued for you. Otherwise, you need another notarized power of attorney from its owner.
  2. Choose a tariff plan and get a set of documents with a SIM card. The price of a set depends on the chosen tariff.
  3. Next, you need to wait until your number is activated in the Beeline network. You can determine the date yourself, but usually at least nine days have passed from the day the application was written.
  4. While you will be with the previous operator, you will have two SIM cards, a new work number will be temporarily set on the new one . You will receive text notifications on this number about how the process of your transition to the new network is going.
  5. As soon as a new SIM card is activated, the old one stops working. This is very convenient for the customer in the first place. He will not be left without a connection for a single day. So you can change the operator without changing the number.

If you are refused

There are times when the operator is forced to refuse his new customer, although for him it is unprofitable. This can happen if at least one condition is not met. For example, if there is an account debt or your data does not match. In all these cases, the problem is solved simply. It is only necessary to eliminate the reason why the new operator refuses you. Pay off the debt, visit the office of the previous operator to unblock your number, submit documents with your new data. After that, you will need to re-contact the office of the new operator.
