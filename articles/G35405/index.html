Physiognomy of the face and character

Agree that looking at people's faces is a very interesting activity. After all, they are all very different from each other. Faces are very simple. But sometimes on the path of life there is a person from whom it is difficult to take his eyes off.

When examining young people, you can see traces of a certain naivety and childish enthusiasm on them. Representatives of the middle generation show signs of self-confidence or, on the contrary, the prints of a failed life. But what can be seen on older persons? They reflect the traces of past defeats and victories, as well as a whole series of long-term human emotions.

Unusual science

Looking at faces, you can say a lot about people. After all, their features are always available and an extensive source for obtaining information. Humanity has even created a separate science about the face - physiognomy. People who own her skills can tell a lot about a person. In principle, it is not so difficult. You just need to compare the information obtained by looking at the person with the existing knowledge. For someone who has successfully mastered this science, many of those character traits that will appear in the interlocutor during the dialogue will not come as a surprise. After all, most often his face will tell about it in advance.

What is physiognomy? The science of the face, which allows one to determine the qualities and properties of a person by his features, as well as by body shapes. Translated from the Greek word "physio" means "natural inclinations" or "nature". The second part of the concept is ‚Äúgnome‚Äù - ‚Äúinsightful‚Äù or ‚Äúknowledgeable‚Äù. With the help of this art, facial features recognize not only the character of a person, but also his fate.

Depending on the profession, physiognomy of the face must be known to some specialists. This will help them competently fulfill their duties and achieve the highest levels of skill in their business. Such professions include doctors and lawyers, researchers and lawyers, writers, psychologists, artists, as well as managers engaged in the selection of personnel.

A bit of history

The origins of the physiognomy of the face as a science are rooted in the distant past. The knowledge gained, similar to the healer secrets, was passed from generation to generation, passing from father to son, from teacher to student. At the same time, they were preserved in legends and traditions.

A little later, similar art arose in the East. So the Chinese physiognomy of the face appeared. The healers of ancient civilization classified some concepts of this direction and gave it a systematic look, which gave the right to call it a doctrine. Moreover, the physiognomy of the face included not only knowledge about its main features, which is taking place at the present time. She also considered the figure of a man, his gestures and facial expressions.

Nevertheless, Western knowledge was different from the Chinese physiognomy of the face. Over time, the healers of the Middle Kingdom further developed this art. They have created special treatises that have spread throughout the countries of the East. They were also brought to Japan. Of course, it is impossible to say that all the inhabitants of the Land of the Rising Sun are fond of physiognomy of the face. After all, this is far from the case. However, the influence of this science is clearly visible in Japanese painting and in the theater, as well as in the production of masks. It is used in various fields of communication.

Japanese technique

Are physiognomy of the face and character of a person connected? Japanese researchers are convinced that yes. And before starting to study facial features, they suggest conditionally dividing it with two horizontal lines. This will form three zones. Physiognomy of the face pays considerable attention to the upper of them - the frontal part. According to it, the researcher can determine the path of life that a person has traveled. The upper zone serves to obtain information about a person for a period from 15 to 30 years. If the forehead is perfect, that is, it has the correct shape and healthy color of the skin, then it can be argued about the excellent state of mind and body of the interlocutor.

The middle zone starts from the eyebrows and ends with the tip of the nose. On it you can get information about the life period from 35 to 50 years. If this site is harmoniously balanced, then it can be argued about the ordering of the psyche of the interlocutor.

The lower zone covers the upper lip and jaw, mouth, and chin. On it you can get information about that period of life, which is in the interval from 51-77 years, as well as about advanced age. With the correct forms of this zone, experts talk about the balance of character.

Eyes

How can a person‚Äôs character and state of health be determined using physiognomy of a person? Anyone who tries to give a classification of the eyes on the move, comes to mind their size. And she really has a special meaning. Nose

This part of the face is also a vivid indicator of the nature of a person. The nose in the physiognomy of the face, like the eyes, can tell a lot about its owner who has reached the age of 40.

Its shape is considered ideal with a rounded tip and decorated wings. Too big nostrils speak about personality defects of a person.

The science of physiognomy does not at all believe that a long nose indicates the curiosity of its owner. As a rule, it is peculiar to conservatives. Too long a nose talks about the capricious nature of a genius. That is, the length of this part of the face most of all indicates a person‚Äôs attitude to life.

The owner of a short nose has an open and soft character. At the same time, he is sociable.

What can be said about the owner of a bony nose with a high inflection or with a small hump? This man is proud, stubborn, but at the same time decisive and even aggressive. But in general, in physiognomy, the hump is considered a sign of melancholy, frowning, and sadness. The more it is expressed, the more clearly these qualities are manifested. At the same time, such people can always find something positive and funny even where, it would seem, there is no reason for fun.

A narrow nose usually indicates a workaholic. These are people who are used to achieving their goals through hard and long work.

By the way, in physiognomy, the shape of the nose has different meanings, depending on whether it belongs to a man or a woman. So, among the representatives of the weak half of humanity, its small size indicates the presence of morbid jealousy. Small noses in men indicate their narrow circle of interests and that they prefer to take care only of themselves.

It is recommended that you take a closer look at those people whose nose wings balance its large and flat back. One should not follow their flattery and multiple promises. After all, these are natures cold and prudent.

A nose ending in a round tip indicates good character traits of its owner. It is believed that such people are lucky in life. However, they are not waiting for her to come lying on the couch. Similar characteristics are possessed by those people whose tip of the nose is large and convex. It testifies to their readiness for self-sacrifice, warmth and kindness.

Face shape

She is the first to catch the eye when looking at any person.

The face shape in physiognomy is divided into several types. Let's consider them in more detail:

  1. Oblong. This face resembles a rectangle in shape. The width of his forehead and chin is approximately the same. This type of person is considered aristocratic. One can say about such a person that he is smart, prudent, has great prudence and sensitivity. In addition, the holder of an elongated face is purposeful. He, without any doubt, has the talent of a wonderful organizer.
  2. Triangular. The owner of such a face has a wide and high forehead, a thin nose, protruding cheekbones, a small chin and deep-set eyes. This man is not caring and cunning.   3. Trapezoidal. In its configuration, this type of face is similar to triangular. It is characterized by a narrowed chin and a wide forehead. According to physiognomists, such people are artistic, sensitive and intelligent. Women are overwhelmed with optimism. They live long, happy, able to create comfort.
