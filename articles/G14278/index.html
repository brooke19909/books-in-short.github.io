DIY vase from a tire: free, creative, fun

Each summer resident or owner of his own courtyard thinks about how to decorate his plot, while spending a minimum of money.

Crafts from tires with their own hands (photo), for example, a fence made of old rubber or a staircase made of tires, a sandbox, original tables and chairs, a swing, sports equipment, figures of fairy-tale characters, flowerbeds, sculptures will decorate a yard or a house and help the owner to show individuality. Such crafts cost nothing at all, you can work on them with the whole family. And still old rubber will easily replace expensive flowerpots for flowers and beds. Here are a few ideas of original and not very complex vases that can be used for planting fresh flowers, bouquets or compositions from natural material.

Do-it-yourself vase from a tire from a large car



To make it, you will need several old tires from large trucks. The result is not a vase, but rather a flowerpot that can be installed on a garden plot and plant flowers in it. The easiest way is to mark the wheel with chalk and cut out the pattern: petals, waves, any other intricate shapes. It is not necessary to remove the rubber from the rim. With a jigsaw or a very sharp knife, the slope is cut according to the applied drawing, turned out and painted with any waterproof paints. After that, the ramp is filled with earth, and the auto-flowerbed is ready.

If you take several tires, set one into one according to the principle of a nesting doll, then you can make a wonderful flowerbed consisting of several tiers or a vase from the tires with your own hands.

DIY vase from a tire for decorating a room

For such a product, you can take a smaller ramp. They work with him in much the same way as in the previous case: the rubber is cut and twisted. Then the rubber is carefully covered with white or any light paint, on top of which a color in the Gzhel, Khokhloma style, etc. is applied with colored paints. It will turn out a beautiful big vase. With your own hands from the tire, you can also build small vessels for flowers. You can take the rubber from a bicycle ramp or a motorcycle, insert a suitable plastic bottle into it.
The rubber, which should be twice as long as the bottle, is cut into strips that are folded in the shape of petals, woven into patterns, wrapping them in a bottle. If you do not want to mess with homemade products at all, you can make a simple flower vessel from a section of an unnecessary bicycle chamber or tire. You just need to cut the desired length, solder the lower end and with the help of another piece of rubber and two nails attach the elongated pot to the wall. It can be painted, pasted over with river stones, beads, pieces of glass, even painted beans. Since water is difficult to pour into a flowerpot attached to the wall, this vase with your own hands from a bicycle tire is more suitable for dry compositions. Especially good rubber vases look complete with tables and chairs, also made of tires.
