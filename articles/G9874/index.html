How to find out the balance of maternity capital through the Internet - a step-by-step description

Maternity capital is timely social assistance to young families with minor children. Parents whose family members have two or more children have the right to count on it. The very right to receive such state assistance is recorded in a special certificate. It is known that parents can use this benefit at their own discretion for the benefit of their own children. However, the program has a validity period. According to preliminary data, it is valid until the end of December 2016. Therefore, many parents today are interested in the question of how to find out the balance of maternity capital through the Internet. And is it really possible to do this remotely?





How to get information about the balance of the uterus: online calculator

Suppose you and your family are happy owners of a family certificate. One of your children is already three years old, and you have already spent part of the money allocated by the state. Next, you had plans to use the remaining part of the funds, but you do not know the exact amount in the account.

How to find out the balance of maternity capital in this case? The easiest option is to use the service on which there is an online calculator for such calculations. As a rule, such calculators have a very simple menu. It has 5-6 items, scheduled by year. In order to carry out the calculation, it is necessary to indicate in the necessary column the exact amount that you spent in a particular year. For example, in 2013 you spent only 20,000 rubles.

Why did these amounts increase?

Do not assume how to find out the balance of maternity capital? In the calculations, did you get an increased amount of the balance? The main reason for this anomaly is the indexation of the amount of mother capital. That is, the law provides for annual indexation of money until 2017. In particular, we are talking about those families that at a certain point in time have not yet managed to use all the money from family capital, but only a small part of it has been used up.

In such cases, each year the amount of the remaining capital will be indexed and, accordingly, increase.

How to get information about the balance of uterine capital by mail?

In addition to the Internet, you can receive information about the balance in your account by mail. The State Pension Fund fulfills this task. Moreover, the procedure for sending such information occurs no later than the first days of September of each year. In this case, letters with the amount of the balance are sent to the address indicated in the application for the issue of maternity capital or at the place of registration.

Who can receive information about the amount of the balance? The following citizens are entitled to receive data on the balance of the capital:

  • have not yet used at least part of the state aid;
  • who have already managed to use part of the funds and have been entered in the federal register of persons;
  • in which the amount of the remainder of the capital is more than 30 rubles.




How to get data on the balance of capital in the Pension Fund?

If you want to find out the balance of the maternity capital before the deadline set by the state, then you can always address this matter to the GPF at your place of residence. To submit such a request, you must personally come to the state agency with a passport and the original certificate and write a statement to provide information on the account balance. Or the aforementioned application with attached copies of the certificate and all the meaningful pages of the passport can be sent by mail.

The answer to your request can be obtained in the form of a statement-extract, which is provided within three days according to the passport.

Can I go to the website of the Pension Fund and get information about the balance?

Despite the large amount of useful information, many parents do not lose hope of watching the balance of maternity capital through the Internet. Can this be done? At the moment, you cannot enter the Pension Fund website and find information about the balance of the capital there. This is due to the law on the storage of personal data of users.





Can a spouse or family member take a statement of the balance of funds?

The certificate on the account status of the capital can only be obtained by the parent who previously wrote an application for the issuance of such a document. He, too, can receive a certificate only by passport. A spouse or any relative of this person will be able to get a certificate only if there is a notarized power of attorney. Otherwise, no account balance information is provided.

Can I get a certificate of the balance in another city?

Many mothers are interested in the question of how to see the balance of maternity capital in another city. For example, if the certificate was issued in Moscow, and you currently live in Volgograd. In this case, you can get such information. However, before this, it makes sense to contact the Pension Fund branch at the place of residence and write a statement about the request for your case to that branch of the fund, in which documents for issuing the certificate were issued earlier. Further, the Pension Fund will send a request to another body of the FIU.

After receiving your case from Moscow, you will be able to write an application for a certificate of the amount of the balance. According to preliminary information, the electronic transfer of your case will take about 1-2 weeks.

Does the expiration date have a certificate of the requested amount of the balance of the capital?

In addition to the question of how to check the balance on maternity capital, many parents are also interested in whether the certificate with such information has an expiration date.

Based on the rules, there is no expiration date on the certificate requested by parents about the balance of funds in the account. By and large, it is relevant only at the time of treatment. Suppose, after receiving it, the owners of the capital decided to spend another part of the money. Consequently, the amount indicated in the certificate will no longer correspond to real data.

Do I need to attend the Pension Fund twice?

Many people think that before applying for the use of family capital on credit (for example, as a first installment of a mortgage), you need to come to the Pension Fund twice: first - to write a statement about the balance of the funds of the capital, and then - to apply for use funds as a down payment on a loan.
