DIY fairy costume in a few hours

Not only children, but also adults love to dress up in unusual outfits, especially for carnival holidays and costume parties. The selection of the necessary image is often long and thoughtful, and sometimes it is necessary to create a costume in a hurry, without having the necessary materials in advance.

Is it possible to create a fairy costume in a few hours or is it unrealistic? In this article you will find the answer.

Fairy is a favorite character of children's fairy tales and cartoons. Good sorceresses are always liked by girls, and angry and envious cause dislike. Gentle, quivering kind fairy-tale creatures constantly become objects to follow, especially in clothes.

How can I make a fairy costume for adults? There are two options: either sew in advance, as expected, using a pattern and all the necessary materials, or make quickly from improvised materials: an old dress, a light organza scarf, a fluffy short skirt. To get started, let's consider the option of preparing the suit in advance.



Actually, a fairy costume is an airy dress, decorative wings, a magic wand. The image is complemented by a high hairstyle and ribbon in the collected hair. In order to sew a light dress for the fairies, you need to take a satin, organza and many decorative ribbons. Sew the dress of the desired length from the atlas according to the standard pattern with a flared skirt. It is desirable that the finished outfit has an empire line (the waist line was moved under the chest). Sew organza over the finished dress, decorate the finished fairy carnival costume with ribbons. You can equip the outfit with sleeves, if there is such a desire.

One of the main attributes of such a fabulous character is transparent wings. You can also make them yourself or buy them. If you decide to create the whole fairy costume manually, you also need to make wings yourself. To do this, you will need a stiff wire, lace or organza to match the dress, sequins, sequins, beads. Cut the base for future wings onto cardboard or paper. Transfer it to the fabric (organza, lace), cut it out.





Make a wireframe according to the sketch of the wings. Sew the fabric carefully to the frame so that the seams are not visible. Decorate the finished wings with beads, sequins, sparkles. The resulting attribute can either be worn on elastic bands over the shoulders or sewn onto the fairy dress.

If a few hours are left before the party, and you really want to appear in the image of a fairy-tale hero, you can make a fairy costume - an express version. Most likely, you have an airy summer dress in natural shades. You will take it as a basis. Add a shiny belt matching the shoes to the dress . You can also sew the wings yourself - now you already know how. If you don‚Äôt have organza or lace at hand, you can use your favorite scarf that goes with the dress. Do not forget the magic wand! It can be made from any large pen by covering it with a beautiful cloth.

Any girl can create a fairy costume. The main thing is desire, imagination and a creative approach to the process. Use any suitable things and accessories, create an image that will be remembered for a long time!
