Link to social networks

On the page of your personal account in the upper right corner, among others, there is a link "Link to social networks", where you can link your account to accounts in the social networks "Classmates", Facebook and "VKontakte". To do this, you will need to enter the password and login from your personal account and click on the "Next" button. After you confirm the linking of the social network to your account, a message will appear stating that the operation was completed successfully. For each network (if you have several), the binding procedure will have to be done separately.

You can also bind to social networks on the authorization page of your personal account, where there are three buttons at the bottom (VKontakte, Facebook and Odnoklassniki). Choose a network and just follow the prompts.





Creating a link will allow you to directly view the balance on the accounts of all your services connected to your account directly on the social network, and you can use the password and login of the social network to enter your personal account.
