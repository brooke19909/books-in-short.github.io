Choose an account

To work on trading floors, Sberbank brokers offer their customers two categories of accounts:

  1. Main. It includes simple brokerage accounts.
  2. Special. It includes investment accounts and individual ones.

A bidder has the right to open two accounts simultaneously. The accounts of the first category when working on the site reflect all ongoing transactions with securities, show the movement of financial assets in real time and the results of transactions (including for a certain period).

In the reviews of brokers of Sberbank of Russia, many indicate that an individual account has some tax benefits. In addition, there are restrictions on the amount of transfer: it should not exceed 400,000 rubles.





Open an account

Opening an account allows Sberbank to become an intermediary for a client on a trading exchange. To do this, you must come in person to the department of the financial institution and write a statement in which it is necessary to indicate the phone number and current electronic mail box. The bank will send confirmations for transactions and financial reports to it. In addition, a bank specialist will need a passport, a plastic card of a future broker and his identification number.

After opening an account with the help of a bank manager or on your own, you need to get account details in the terminal. The whole procedure takes no more than half an hour.

Judging by the reviews, Sberbank brokers do not limit the size of the lower limit of the brokerage account. It depends on the desire and solvency of the client.

Addresses and phone numbers of branches and branches of Sberbank in which they work with broker accounts can be found on the official portal of the credit institution.





The account is open. Now what?

Upon completion of account registration, the bank is obliged to issue to the client:

  • conditions of tariff plans for depository services ;
  • declaration of possible risks in the securities market;
  • completed investor profile;
  • a list of remuneration to the bank for brokerage;
  • a card with an investor code table and a copy of its acceptance certificate;
  • copy of the application written by the investor for brokerage services.

Now, legally, you can attract a bank as a broker and start studying the basics of investing, having previously installed the QUIK system software.
