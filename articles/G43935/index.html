Where is the best place to relax in Greece? The choice is yours!

When the time comes for vacation, each person probably thinks about which country to visit. And if the choice fell on the land of Hellas, the question arises: "Where better to relax in Greece?"

As you know, Greece is a country of warm azure sea and golden sand. It is famous for a huge variety of resorts. There are all conditions for a comfortable stay: clean beaches, Mediterranean climate, as well as a large selection of entertainment for every taste. You can visit this fabulously beautiful country at any time of the year, but still, the high season falls here at the end of summer. Greece has many islands, so if you are a lover of sea air, we offer you those islands of Greece where it is better to relax. The most famous of them are Corfu, Rhodes, Crete and Mykonos.





Corfu

This famous resort will be remembered for you by its pristine beaches, rich cultural and historical heritage and beautiful landscapes. The island is divided into 3 parts, so here every tourist will find what he is looking for. If you want peace and quiet, then the Nissaki area is suitable for you, Dassya is suitable for youth or family vacations. But if you are a fan of entertainment and parties, you need a part of the island called Ipsos. It so happened that Corfu combines many different cultures, such as Turkish, French, Byzantine, Roman and others, which is reflected in local traditions and architecture. Here you can see the ruins of the temple of Zeus - a monument of ancient culture. If you think where it is better to relax in Greece, then this island will surprise you.





Rhodes

It is the most visited and famous island of Greece. Here you will find medieval architecture preserved to our time. Rhodes is also famous for its unique ceramics, fine wines and delicious cuisine. One of the seven wonders of the world - the statue of the Ear of Rhodes - glorified the island throughout the world. Rhodes can also be divided into 2 parts: a medieval city surrounded by the walls of an ancient fortress, and a modern resort city with wide streets, shops and vibrant evening life. It is also one of those places where it is good to relax in Greece.

Crete

It is washed by the Libyan, Aegean and Ionian seas. This is a unique place to relax, one of those where it is better to relax in Greece. According to mythology, he is the birthplace of the gods. The nature on the island is extremely picturesque; moreover, here you can find small mountain villages, clean beaches and the azure sea, which, of course, attracts tourists from all over the world.

Also among other attractions there is a beach for lovers of water skiing and nudists called Paradise. On Psaru Beach you will find a scuba diving center and excellent surfing conditions. BC, which depicts the Trojan horse, the Aegean Museum, the Ethnographic Museum, as well as the Church of the Virgin Paraportiani.





Greece is a country of unlimited possibilities for tourists, so you only need to choose where it is better to relax in Greece, and your vacation will be unforgettable!
