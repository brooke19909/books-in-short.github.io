Rest on the Volga: recreation facilities. Fishing on the Volga

Each person has a hobby: someone collects stamps, others collect coins, and someone cannot imagine their life without fishing. And it is no secret that such a fiery hobby can occupy the lion's share of free time. Sometimes close people suffer from this addiction, because often, instead of spending time with the family, an avid fisherman spends it on the banks of a river or lake. And if you are in such a situation, then try to at least sometimes all go fishing together. This will make your family even stronger and happier. With children, of course, it is better to do this in the summer, because not everyone will appreciate all the charms of winter fishing. And while the father of the family with enthusiasm will fish out the river inhabitants, the rest of the family members will be able to buy enough and sunbathe. And if we are already talking about fishing, then, of course, it must be arranged on the Volga. If you plan to leave for a few days, then it is better to take care of in advance what will be the rest on the Volga. Recreation facilities will help you organize unforgettable leisure. At your service will be not only fishing, but also many other kinds of various entertainments.



The Volga River Flows

Volga is the central waterway of Russia. And it is also one of the deepest rivers in the world. The majestic lowland river is striking in its power and beauty. Isn't that why so many songs are composed about her? This river is divided into three zones: Upper Volga, Middle and Lower. From the source to Nizhny Novgorod, the river (upper part) flows through the forest zone, the middle - along the forest-steppe, and the lower - along the steppe. Therefore, each person will be able to find a place for himself. The length of the river is 3350 kilometers. The water temperature in the summer reaches 20-25 degrees, because you can sunbathe and swim up from the heart.



Unforgettable fishing

Thousands of fishing enthusiasts rush to this water paradise. And it is no coincidence that the ichthyofauna of this river is so diverse that even the most sophisticated fishermen will be delighted with the catch. The local waters are inhabited by carp, bream, zander, sterlet, grayling, pike, beluga. All inhabitants are simply impossible to list. Everyone will like fishing on the Volga - recreation facilities located on the shore will help arrange an unforgettable fishing trip even for an inexperienced beginner. And if your loved ones do not share your passion for fishing, then they will also have something to do: walks on the river with water skiing or a catamaran will certainly appeal to you. If you decide to arrange a rest on the Volga for yourself and your loved ones - recreation centers are always at your service. Below are some of them.



Chalet "Berezhok"

If you want to spend your vacation in such a fabulous place, where there will be a river on one side and a forest on the other, then go to the Upper Volga, to Kazan. Here you can successfully combine fishing with a quiet hunt. Recreation facilities on the Volga (Kazan) are always welcome guests. Be sure to visit the camp "Berezhok". There is everything for an unforgettable vacation: beautiful nature and a gorgeous panorama of the water surface, clean beaches, sun loungers overlooking the vast water and forest. For your stay you will be offered separate houses, for especially demanding guests there are deluxe rooms. And what fishing is here! You can easily surprise your loved ones with a trophy in the form of a pike, catfish or grayling. If you suddenly want to extreme, then riding a banana, water skiing is at your service. On the territory of the complex there is a bathhouse, a sauna for guests, it is possible to organize a party with the preparation of barbecue, fish soup or pilaf. The base staff can arrange full board for you, but if you want, you can cook it yourself. The animators working on the territory of the complex will do everything possible to make your children enjoy the rest on the Volga - the recreation centers are equipped with everything necessary for this.



Chalet "Wonderful"

This tourist base is fully consistent with its name. You really have a beautiful view of the river and the forest. Cozy wooden houses will be provided at your service. Also allocated territory under the tent camp. There is everything in order to arrange a wonderful vacation on the Volga. Recreation facilities are the best option for those who prefer not only active pastime, but also like to go fishing. At the camp site you can rent all the necessary gear.



Chalet "Scarlet Sails"

If you go downstream, then the traveler will be in the middle of the river, where the sixth most populated city (in Russia) is Samara. Let's look at some recreation centers on the Volga. Samara can offer many such that will be interesting to tourists. It is worth paying attention to the Scarlet Sails camp site. This recreation center is located in a mixed forest on the banks of the Volga. There is everything in order to combine business with pleasure. A clean beach will allow you to sunbathe no worse than on the seashore, basketball and tennis courts will appeal to fans of sports games. Those who wish to go fishing will be given fishing equipment for rent. The staff of the complex will place you in luxurious cottages with all amenities. The complex has gazebos and barbecue facilities.

Chalet "Volga Dawns"

Other recreation centers on the Volga (Samara) are also eagerly awaiting guests. Volga Dawns is a unique tourist base. It is located in the Zhiguli Forest, on the banks of the Volga River, in the territory of the Samarskaya Luka National Park and the Saprygin Nature Reserve, just a few kilometers from the village of Shiryaev, which has a large number of historical and cultural monuments. The recreation center "Volga Dawns" is a very cozy place with its own sandy beach. For accommodation, you can be offered both rooms in a comfortable hotel, and individual houses. There are even cottages of increased comfort. All in order to please the guests. At your discretion, you can choose both full board and self-catering. The complex has a summer cafe. Recreation facilities on the banks of the Volga are open to guests not only in summer but also in winter.



Chadym Oak Forest Camp

In the middle of the river is the city of Saratov. Recreation facilities on the Volga, located in this region, have always attracted many tourists. And if you also decided to spend your vacation on the banks of this wonderful river, then welcome to the Saratov region. The tourist base "Chadym Dubrava" is located on a small island connected to the "big" land by a road bridge. Fishing lovers are definitely worth a visit here. Thanks to such a secluded location, the bite in these places is simply excellent. Everything is found here: asp, catfish, pike, zander, perch, bersh. For your stay, you will be given a choice of one of seven comfortable cottages. The complex has a playground, a bathhouse, a sauna, a pool and billiards. So if you are interested in unforgettable fishing on the Volga, recreation centers will provide you with such an opportunity.

Chalet "Lagoon"

This recreation center is located in the most beautiful part of the river, between the hills of the right bank. Here you will find everything: picturesque nature and clean air. A high level of service will complement the experience. Here you can rent both beach equipment and sports equipment. Guests of the camp site can enjoy hiking and cycling in the surroundings, arrange picnics, and even in the cozy cafe in the evenings. Be sure to come to the Volga, and the recreation centers will take care of everything so that your vacation is at the highest level!
