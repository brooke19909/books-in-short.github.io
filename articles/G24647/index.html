Kemer - the greenest resort in Turkey

There are well-known resorts on the Antalya coast : Alanya, Antalya, Belek, Kemer, Side, Fethiye. Each of them has its own merits. Antalya is suitable for connoisseurs of proximity to urban centers. In Alanya, reasonable prices. Belek offers wide sandy beaches and expensive service. Side is a young democratic resort. Fethiye is the most remote of all, respectability is combined with the lack of fuss and crowded, you can even find wild beaches with a natural landscape.

Kemer is chosen because of its picturesque nature and the combination of a mild Mediterranean climate and the richness of coniferous forests, which creates a special climatic therapeutic effect that has a beneficial effect on human health.

Kemer is a resort center on the Mediterranean coast. Nearby are villages, in the past - ordinary fishing villages. Each of them has its own characteristic features. Recommendations for holidays in Turkey

If you want to get a good impression of the rest, try to follow the simple rules and standards of etiquette of the country:

  • Before the trip, get acquainted with the traditions and cultural customs of Turkey.
  • Particular attention should be paid to standards of behavior and a dress code when visiting Muslim mosques.
  • It does not hurt to include sports risks, water and extreme types of recreation in the insurance for the period of the trip. Extended insurance includes dental services, assistance with sunburn and burns from jellyfish.
  • Learn some of the most necessary words in Turkish that will help to establish communication and understanding with local residents: greeting, gratitude, appeal to men (women).
  • When checking into a room, you should carefully check its functional suitability: taps, air conditioning, refrigerator, safe. If the proposed option for some reason did not suit you, do not rush to make a complaint. Quietly explain the reason for your disagreement. As a rule, motivated requests are accepted, and the relocation takes place without any surcharges.
  • In case of health problems and the decision to be treated independently, notify the insurer about the problem. The situation will be taken under control.
  • In the first days of your stay at the resort, exercise caution and try to protect yourself and especially children from exposure to sunlight to avoid getting burns. Be sure to use sunscreen (cream, glasses), especially during sea voyages and with prolonged exposure to water.

Compliance with these simple recommendations will make your stay enjoyable and prevent potential problems.
