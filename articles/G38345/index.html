Grand Panorama Family Suites 4 * (Marmaris): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Almost every average Russian traveled to Turkey. Someone was lucky with the hotel, but someone was not completely satisfied. To avoid problems upon arrival at the place of long-awaited vacation, you need to study the reviews of tourists about the selected hotel in advance.

In this article, we will virtually visit the Club Seray Forest hotel complex. Today its name has somewhat changed and turned into a more respectable - Grand Panorama Family Suites 4 * (Marmaris - its location).

The total number of rooms is 85. All of them belong to the category of suite rooms: the room has a bedroom and a living room, separated by an internal door. A maximum of 5 people can live here.

Nutrition

The hotel restaurant is pleased to offer its guests delicious regional dishes. Meals are organized as a buffet three times a day. Also throughout the day, guests will be offered numerous local drinks. On certain hours, tourists can enjoy snacks and afternoon tea.





Ice cream and freshly squeezed juices are served at an extra charge. Newlyweds can count on wine and a basket of fruit.

The hotel restaurant can accommodate 160 people, it works on the principle of a la carte. An air-conditioned covered area and a spacious summer terrace await guests. The pool has an excellent bar: agree, the rest is more pleasant with a cocktail or soft drink.

The route "Turkey-Marmaris-Grand Panorama Family Suites 4 *" - known and popular. As some tourists note, the British often come here.

It should be noted that the restaurant serves kosher and halal food, that is, dishes prepared according to religious canons.

Entertainment and sports

The main entertainment in the Grand Panorama Family Suites 4 * (Turkey) is a beach with golden fine sand and small pebbles. Its length is 30 m. Accessories (umbrellas, mattresses and deck chairs) are issued completely free of charge. Unfortunately, towels on the beach are not issued.

Between the hotel and the beach there is a roadway and a walking path. At 10:00 a bus takes tourists to the coast. They get back on their own by taxi.
