What does the interest rate consist of

When a client applies to the bank for a specific purpose - to obtain a consumer loan in cash with a low interest rate, he compares his material capabilities and considers offers. To some extent, the client himself, his reputation and much more can affect the size of the percentage, but basically it consists of many other factors:

  • inflation growth;
  • costs of servicing deposits from the population;
  • economic situation in the country;
  • the cost of maintaining the office of the bank and its employees.

All of these factors are in no way connected with the borrower; accordingly, the client will not be able to influence them in any way. But there are a number of circumstances that will allow you to get a consumer loan on more favorable terms.

A customer with an ideal credit history can get consumer loans with a low interest rate at the bank. That is, at the time of issuing a cash loan, the client must have loans closed without delay. Lack of credit history cannot affect the rate reduction.

The last option is to get a secured loan, that is, leave a deposit or attract a guarantor.
