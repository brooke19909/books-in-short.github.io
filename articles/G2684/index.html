Slow cooker Redmond: reviews and features

In domestic supermarkets several years ago crock-pots appeared. These devices are advertised as being able to cook absolutely everything. Is this really so, Redmond multicooker will help to find out . Reviews about her are more often positive than negative.



What the Redmond multicooker is capable of and how well it copes with its duties, we will consider the example of the popular RMC-M4502 model.

The developers claim that Redmond is a slow cooker that saves space in the kitchen, because it successfully copes with the functions of the stove and can replace a double boiler, air grill, oven, deep fryer, a bread baking oven and a device for making yogurt. What else can replace the Redmond multicooker? Reviews indicate that with it there is no need to use even such familiar things as a pot, cauldron and frying pan. To make sure these statements are true, let's take a look at the completeness and features of the RMC-M4502.



The main capacity of the multicooker is a five-liter removable bowl with a coating that does not allow food to burn. These properties are guaranteed by Du Pont, which once developed Teflon. This non-stick coating was insured by Redmond designers, who installed a special sensor that responds to a critical decrease in liquid level. If in the roasting mode there is a risk of burning, the sensor will work, the slow cooker will stop cooking and will notify you of this.

In a bowl with a useful volume of three liters, you can cook, fry, stew and bake. Moreover, all these operations do not require mixing of the components - you just lower them into the container, close the lid and start one of the programs on the control panel. There are only 16 programs and they are designed for cooking soups, cereals, pilaf, meat, fish, poultry, side dishes, pasta. There are programs for deep-frying, cooking fondue, baking, yoghurts, jams, jams, desserts and compotes. The completion of each program is supported by a sound signal.

What else can a Redmond multicooker? Reviews indicate that, thanks to the presence of the basket in the kit, you can quickly steam vegetables, fish or meat. At the same time, food stores more vitamins and nutrients than when frying, cooking and cooks faster. For example, Redmond steams fish in just 10 minutes.



One of the interesting and useful qualities of Redmond can also be called the ability to "get up early" and cook breakfast. The delayed start function allows you to program the multicooker from evening to the beginning of work in the morning. If breakfast is ready, and you have not woken up yet, the slow cooker will patiently wait for the owner, using automatic heating.

The recognition of the multicooker as a hostess is indicated by the honorable places that the Redmond multicooker occupies in culinary forums. The feedback received from users, the developers take into account when creating new models. This was confirmed by the new Multi-Cook program, which allows cooks to independently change the temperature and time of cooking.

Regardless of the model and year of manufacture, any Redmond multicooker deserves to take pride of place in any kitchen.
