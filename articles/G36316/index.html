"Marrakesh Gold", 3 * hotel in Anapa: reviews of tourists

Anapa is chosen by many Russians as a venue for summer vacations. This city is very beautiful; it provides its guests with many amenities. Of course, there are comfortable and inexpensive hotels in Anapa. One of them is Marrakech Gold. Hotel (3 * it is quite consistent ) this hotel offers tourists well-equipped rooms and good service at relatively low prices.

Location Features

This comfortable hotel is located not in Anapa itself, but in its suburb - the village of Vityazevo. In relation to the sea, it is located quite conveniently. Built "Marrakech Gold", a 3 * hotel, on the second coastline. The distance from it to the beach is approximately 500 meters. That is, guests of this hotel can reach the sea in a maximum of 8-10 minutes.





The distance from the airport of Anapa to the hotel is only 2.5 km. Vacationers who arrived by plane can either get to the place either by minibus or taxi. In the first case, you should first get to the bus station by mini-bus. Then you need to transfer to minibus number 128 and follow the stop to the Vityaz Sports Complex.

Of course, there is in Anapa and its own railway station. From it to the hotel you must first take a minibus number 100 to the bus station. Next, you should also transfer to minibus number 128 and get off at the Vityaz Sports Complex stop. It is not difficult to get to the hotel from here even with suitcases.

General description of the hotel

This hotel is a seven-story impressive building of modern architecture. Marrakesh Gold, a 3 * hotel (Anapa) was built , by hotel standards it was quite a long time ago - in 2012. However, since a large-scale reconstruction was carried out in the hotel in 2015, the decoration in its rooms is completely new. The same goes for furniture and equipment.

Judging by the reviews of tourists, both outside and inside the Marrakesh Gold, the 3 * hotel looks very good. Finishing materials in the corridors, halls and rooms of this hotel are used expensive and prestigious. On most sites of the corresponding specialization, descriptions and photos of the hotel are provided not only advertising, but also quite true.

Most tourists who have ever stayed at this hotel praise its rooms. Cleaned in hotel rooms daily. At the same time, maids take out the trash and always pour a disinfectant into the toilet. The bed in the hotel is changed, unfortunately, not with every cleaning.

The hotel rooms are decorated, according to many guests, it is really very beautiful. Moreover, their layout is well thought out. In general, almost all vacationers like accommodation in the rooms of the Marrakech Hotel.

There are practically no room for room in this hotel. The only thing, many tourists note the fact that double and triple rooms here have exactly the same area. At the same time, they surrender, of course, at a different price.

Dining at the hotel

Of course, this three-star hotel has its own dining room. It is located directly in the housing. Rooms at the Marrakesh Gold Hotel are rented either without meals, or with breakfast, or with a guesthouse. Meals in the dining room are served buffet style.

Reviews about the food at the Marrakech Hotel are both good and not very good. Some tourists believe that the food in this hotel is pretty good. Marrakesh Gold, 3 * hotel (Anapa): reviews of tourists about the beach

Quite a great distance from the sea is considered some of the drawbacks of this hotel. Tourists with children or older people getting to the shore from the hotel in the heat may not be too convenient. Most often, guests of the Marrakesh Gold Hotel visit the common beach of Vityazevo. According to most tourists, relaxing on the coastline of this resort is quite comfortable.

The beach of the village is not pebble, as is the case in most cases on the Black Sea, but sandy. Its width reaches 120 meters. Unfortunately, there are always quite a lot of people on this beach. But the infrastructure is also developed here just fine. On the beach of the village there is a whole network of various cafes and restaurants. There are a lot of bistro-style catering establishments by the sea in Vityazevo. Such mini-cafes are most popular among holidaymakers.

Of course, the village provides for a full range of all kinds of water activities on the beach. If desired, tourists can, for example, ride a "banana", "tablet" or a catamaran. Such water rides on the beach of the village are not too expensive.
