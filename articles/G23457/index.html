LCD "Green Alleys": reviews, developer, layout, infrastructure

Moscow is the capital of our vast country, the city that beckons the inhabitants of remote corners. But indeed, many come here in the hope of a better life, its high level, self-realization. It's no secret that the cost of renting even the most modest housing in the capital is quite high, which is why sooner or later everyone begins to think about buying their own apartment. If you had the opportunity to evaluate the Moscow real estate market, you probably were impressed by the cost of square meters.

But now there is a more affordable alternative - new buildings outside the Moscow Ring Road. Moreover, it is absolutely not necessary to move hundreds of kilometers from the capital, to endure the mass of inconvenience. It is quite possible to buy an apartment within 10 km of the MKAD at an attractive price and with fairly comfortable living conditions. Take, for example, LCD "Green Alleys". Reviews about the complex help to evaluate the project, revealing all its advantages and even the most hidden drawbacks of the developer. It was this project that fell into our field of vision.

about the project

Before you start considering reviews of the LCD "Green Alleys" (Vidnoe), you should get acquainted with what the developer offers. So, comfortable housing just 3.5 km from MKAD is the main concept of the project, which attracts the attention of all Muscovites interested in purchasing an apartment. A full-fledged urban environment, all the necessary infrastructure, surrounded by a forested area - the best combination, which some time ago it was impossible to dream of. The project provides for the construction of 14 monolithic buildings that meet all the requirements of reliability, durability and safety.

Developer

In many respects, the prestige of the company leading the facility affects the quality of construction and the level of comfort offered. So, the developer of the Green Alleys residential complex in this case is the MIC. For more than 20 years, the company has been constructing residential buildings and entire complexes with its own infrastructure and has managed to acquire an army of fans. Moreover, it is among the industry leaders. The construction of the LCD "Green Alleys" is far from the first in the history of the company. I must admit that she managed to realize many residential complexes in Moscow and the region.

Attention to detail, high professionalism, the use of high-quality materials and innovative technologies are what apartment buyers managed to note. Moreover, the developer attaches great importance to planning, trying to take into account all the existing needs: spacious and bright apartments with isolated rooms, an additional bathroom and a wardrobe.

Location

Residents of the capital, rather vain, gassed, like no one else needs clean and fresh air, the opportunity to be alone with themselves and their thoughts. And this can only be done in nature. So what can Green Alley LCD offer? Customer reviews focus on the excellent location chosen for the facility. New buildings are being erected in the town of Vidnoe near Moscow, which is located just 3.5 km from the Moscow Ring Road. On the one hand, all residents receive comfort and benefits urban life, and on the other - suburban delights.

On all sides, the complex is surrounded by a forested area, which is a barrier to metropolitan noise, dust, and dirt. You can rest assured: here you can fully enjoy the peace, tranquility, relax in nature with your family and friends. In addition, the project provides for the creation of a pedestrian zone with boulevards and gazebos.

Transport accessibility

You can get to any point of the capital not only by personal transport, but also by public transport. There are several bus stops on the territory of the complex, the routes are quite enough in order not to feel discomfort.

And this is not all the advantages of the LCD "Green Alleys". The responses of the first buyers regarding transport accessibility are quite encouraging: already in 2020 a metro station should appear here, which will make the complex even more attractive for living.

Floor plans

Our parents could not pay attention to the layout of the apartments, because in most cases they received square meters from the state. Nowadays, when we invest our own funds, it is necessary to more demanding approach to the choice.

So, buyers are offered 1,2 and 3-room apartments of improved layout. They are distinguished by spaciousness and thoughtfulness: each square meter of area carries its own functional load. Apartments in Vidnoye (Green Alleys residential complex) are really represented by a variety of layouts, so everyone will find the option that will satisfy his requirements and financial capabilities. Finish

Most of the apartments in the LCD are for rent without finishing, but the developer decided to take into account the wishes of modern buyers, offering their own repair options. This is a real salvation for those families who do not have the time and finances, who want to call in their family nest right after receiving the keys

In the standard version, the apartments are rented without finishing, the interior floors are made "in one block". The first buyers note that they inherited from the developer two-chamber double-glazed windows of European manufacture, extremely high-quality and reliable entrance doors, heating radiators, and also electrical wiring. Moreover, all the loggias are glazed.

But if you focus on the quality of work and materials used, this complex will meet your expectations.The developer does not really save on materials, but attracts real professionals to work.
