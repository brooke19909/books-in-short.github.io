Holiday Village "Kasargi" in the Chelyabinsk region - photos and reviews

The edge of 3500 lakes

Although the country is large, not everyone knows that in the Chelyabinsk region there are more than 3,500 lakes. One of them, Kasargi, is located in the Sosnovsky district, in the immediate vicinity of the city of Chelyabinsk (only 27 km). This means that for residents of the city, the lake is a permanent place for summer recreation and fishing.

For convenience, there is a parking lot for cars nearby. The lake has an elongated shape, its length is about 6 kilometers, its width is 2. It is small, but it is difficult to call it small, the maximum depth is 6-7 meters. The lake is formed due to tectonic faults. The nearby village is also called Kasargi, from the ancient Turkic language it is translated as a masculine name. The water in the lake is slightly salty, it is considered mineralized. Flowing, replenished with underground springs, so it is clean and perfect for swimming - the bottom in the lake is mostly sandy, pleasant and smooth. For convenience, the beach is equipped with sunbeds and cabanas, there is a store. Other lakes are also nearby.

Comfortable holiday among birches

A wonderful birch grove surrounds the lake. Just imagine: slender, thin birches, a clear blue sky, peace and quiet. No city bustle, no incessant rumble of cars. You can completely relax and completely surrender to a wonderful vacation.



Conveniently located recreation centers on Kasargi (Chelyabinsk), kindly providing all clients with comfortable accommodation in an ecologically clean place. It is very convenient, you can come and settle in a comfortable house or rent a cozy cottage. Guests who have already come to this wonderful place assure that the conditions here are at the highest level.

Many people prefer to relax with the whole family and take their children with them. It is for a good family vacation that the recreation center (Kasargi Lake) is intended. A comfortable settlement is also provided in cozy houses. For guests' convenience, plastic windows are installed in the rooms. The rooms are heated - this means that your vacation can be winter, but at the same time comfortable. In the houses there is a kitchen corner with a stove and a refrigerator, so, if necessary, there is the opportunity to cook your own meals. This is especially true if you are relaxing with children. Self-cooking allows you to save the familiar menu, which, in turn, eliminates unpleasant consequences after eating unfamiliar food.

Fishing

What could be more relaxing and pacifying than a quiet pastime alone with yourself or in the pleasant company of a loved one? The base of rest "Kasargi" is ready to organize this. For those who like to sit in silence with a fishing rod, the lake is just the perfect place. There are a huge number of different fish: carp, ripus, peled, common carp, crucian carp, perch, chebak, pike perch, burbot, pike, omul and whitefish. This is a true paradise for lovers of fishing.



For fishing, you can come with your equipment or rent. All that is needed is ready to provide recreation "Kasargi." You can fish from both the shore and the boat. Moreover, they fish all year round on the lake, and ice fishing is also popular here. In order to come here in the winter, there are also no restrictions. The recreation center "Kasargi" is heated, which guarantees a comfortable and cozy stay and complete relaxation.

Fishing is a wonderful vacation: sitting quietly and calmly and watching a barely moving float is akin to meditation. It relaxes and soothes. Well, the presence of a variety of fish is a guarantee of a good catch.

Russian sauna

Leisure with health benefits - what could be better? Holiday Village "Kasargi" offers this kind of pastime. For all lovers of steam bath built on the shore. Where else can one have a good rest both in body and soul? Nice warm steam, fragrant broom, warm log cabin. A visit to the bath has a positive effect on health, although this type of relaxation has contraindications. But if your condition allows, then there is no reason to miss the opportunity to take a steam bath from the heart. For all lovers of the Russian bath, as well as for all those who want to be healthy, the presence of a bath near the lake is another reason to visit this place.
