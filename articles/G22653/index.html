Lighting Osram Fluora: purpose, application, specifications

Lighting Osram Fluora is a special light bulb designed to illuminate aquariums and artificially grow plants. Such products are characterized by an increased proportion of red and blue radiation, therefore, they are excellent for stimulating photobiological processes in representatives of the flora. Thanks to Osram Fluora, their growth is significantly accelerated. These lighting devices are used as additional illumination in places such as decorative islands in shopping centers, hotels, offices, residential buildings. Or in greenhouses, shop windows, on windows where flowers are grown.



Lamp Specifications Osram Fluora

Lighting devices "Flora" are represented by one type W / 77, however, they may differ in power, luminous flux and size. Let's consider them in more detail. The diameter of such lamps is 26 mm, the type of cap is G13 (this option is the most common cartridge among tubular lamps). These two parameters are the same for all Flora products. Depending on the power, Osram lamps Fluora They are divided into five types: 15, 18, 30, 36 and 58 watts. Accordingly, the luminous flux will be 400, 550, 1000, 1400 and 2250 Lumens; product lengths - 438, 590, 895, 1200 and 1500 mm. The service life is 13000 hours. The most popular are lighting devices with a power of 18 and 36 watts. Luminescent phytolamps practically have no parasitic heating, respectively, power is not consumed, so a value of 30 W is quite enough for such devices.



Description

The Flora lamp is a fluorescent lighting device with intense radiation in the range of 440 and 670 nm. It is these areas that are maximally used by vegetation for growth and vegetation. Osram Fluora helps keep the bush healthy. To create a balance of the light effect, as well as to obtain a powerful light flux, you can optionally install conventional fluorescent lighting devices with improved color reproduction. Such a tandem will give an excellent effect in the artificial cultivation of flowers and ornamental plants. In fact, phytolamps are portable light sources. They are massively used to organize lighting in greenhouse complexes. They are suitable for aquarium flora and fauna, as well as for lighting terrariums, but provided that plants grow in them.

Conclusion



Lighting devices "Flora", in addition to perfectly stimulating the growth of flowers, have an aesthetic appearance and an interesting glow. For example, the pink light in the bedroom does not irritate the eyes and can create a romantic atmosphere and replace a nightlight. However, do not forget that a person is not recommended to be under prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation. Phytolamps consume a small amount of electricity, do not flicker and do not heat up. Experiment lovers can try the Osram Natura lamp in action: it has a natural white glow and is ideal for flowers. Such devices are used to highlight products in store windows. Despite the white light, such phytolamps also have powerful red-blue radiation, and in addition also a green region of the spectrum.
