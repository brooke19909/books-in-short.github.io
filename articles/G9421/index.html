Hotel Vincci el Mansour 4 * (Tunisia / Mahdia): photos and reviews of tourists

Every year, Vincci el Mansour 4 * receives thousands of visiting tourists. Most of them visit Tunisia to devote all their time to a beach holiday, others for the sake of attractions. However, no matter what goals the tourist sets for himself, the hotel will make his stay even better thanks to excellent service and a large list of services.





general information

The hotel complex was built in Mahdia - a resort city located in the southern part of Tunisia. This place is widely known for its snow-white sandy beaches. For their sake, in fact, people are going to Mahdia. And what is especially pleasing is that Vincci el Mansour 4 * has a private area on the beach, in the immediate vicinity of which the hotel is located.

The complex itself consists of four main buildings and several snow-white bungalows built in a traditional style. In terms of design, this hotel is very good. Throughout its interior, the Moorish and Andalusian styles can be traced. This situation adds even more color to the place.

The nearest airport is Monastir. To go about 40 minutes. But close are various attractions. For example, the Great Mosque built by Ubaidallah, or the fortress of Bordj el-Kebir, located on the edge of the cape. You can also pay attention to the medieval tower of Borj al-Ras and the gates of Skif al-Kala. Connoisseurs of interesting places will definitely appreciate it.

Service and Facilities

Vincci el Mansour 4 * has everything from concierge services and a left-luggage office to a 24-hour reception and currency exchange.

There is a laundry with dry cleaning, where they also iron clothes and clean guests' shoes, there is a minimarket and several shops, a shared lounge. A shuttle service and VIP services are provided. The staff, by the way, speaks not only Arabic and English, but also the staff of the complex speak German, Spanish, French, Italian and Russian.

Entertainment

Vincci el Mansour 4 * is not only a hotel, but also a good entertainment complex. There is an outdoor swimming pool, a sun terrace, a large garden for walks, as well as animation staff, who daily organizes various shows and interesting events for guests.

You can also play tennis or minigolf at the hotel, and visit the fitness center with modern fitness equipment. The hotel has a billiard room, a sports ground and conditions for volleyball. Many, being here on vacation, decide to go in for water sports. Diving is especially popular among tourists.

By the way, if guests took their children with them on holiday, they may not worry that the kids will have to be bored. Indeed, in the territory of Vincci el Mansour (ex. Ri 4 *) there is a mini club. Children from 4 to 12 years old are accepted there. There is also a separate pool for children.

For body and soul

In Tunisia, almost every second hotel has its own SPA center. Vincci el Mansour (ex. Ri 4 *) is no exception. The SPA center of this hotel is called L'Olympe. Thalassotherapy procedures are especially popular in it, involving the use of sea water, algae, therapeutic mud, etc.

There is a sauna, massage rooms, a beauty salon and even onsen (hot springs). Guests are given on-site brochures with a full range of services. Wraps, hammam, masks, hydromassage, scrubs, Ayurvedic treatments, reflexology - this is just a small list of what you can try at the hotel.





Restaurants

Vincci el Mansour 4 * (Tunisia, Mahdia) has several excellent establishments where guests are offered delicious and varied dishes.

The first restaurant is called El Fatimi. It serves traditional Tunisian cuisine. Fans of pizza, spaghetti and European dishes will love the Italian restaurant. The Bereber Cafe serves excellent hookah and tea. You can quench your thirst with soft drinks in the lounge or at the poolside bar. They also make an excellent grill and barbecue.

As the reviews left about rest in Vincci el Mansour 4 * show, food here deserves all five stars. And the service, by the way, too. For the period of stay for each guest reserve a certain table, fixing it for this or that number. And the dinner is divided into two parts, so there are no lines in the restaurants. And the waiters serve incredibly fast, barely manages to empty the plate of food.

The choice of dishes is varied. There is everything from delicious fresh pastries to shrimps, grills and freshly squeezed juices. Accommodation options

In Vincci el Mansour 4 * (Mahdia) more than 300 rooms. And to start talking about the options available is a description of a single room. The cost of living in it is about 52 euros per day. Price may vary, depending on the season. But this is not very much, given that the hotel serves all-inclusive guests.

The apartments are decorated in a simple style without excess, but with marble flooring. Inside there is a large bed, a safe, telephone, TV, good air conditioning and access to a private balcony. There is also a work desk and a bathroom with a bath, shower, hairdryer, toilet, towels and free toiletries. There is also a dressing room - everything you need for personal comfort. By the way, all 1-bed standards are located on the ground floor, so they do not have balconies, but terraces.

Other apartments

Also in Vincci el Mansour Thalassa (ex. Riu 4 *) there are 2-bed rooms and bungalows. These apartments are designed for two people. In terms of conditions, everything is the same, only the price of a daily stay is about 75 euros. But still, living together is more profitable than one.

Apartment for three will cost 120 euros per day. They are distinguished by a larger area and the presence of an extra bed. The same can be said of family rooms for 2 adults and 2 children. The price of daily living is 170 euros.

By the way, there are also presidential suites, but, in fact, they differ from family ones only in name.

The guests staying at Vincci el Mansour Thalassa 4 * assure that the rooms are good, well-groomed. And what is especially surprising and unusual for hotel apartments is a separate bathroom. The main thing is not to book connecting rooms if guests are not sent by the company (for which such an option is intended). Because in this case you will have to suffer from good audibility. Since, in fact, the connecting room is a single apartment, divided by an interior door into two rooms. And if you want maximum silence, you need to reserve a room on the top floor and not overlooking the pool, since there are constantly entertaining noisy events.

Guests on vacation

Most tourists had a pleasant experience with the Vincci el Mansour. 4 stars is really his level. But the guests pay special attention not only to the service, food, and comfort of the rooms, but to the sea and the beach, to be more precise.

It is incredibly clean here, even in the midst of the holiday season. His condition is monitored by janitors, so it is very difficult to see the garbage here. Tunisia cannot find such sand anywhere else: white, crumbly, as if semolina. The sea is also clear, sometimes there are waves. And a separate balm for the soul is that the beach is private, and only hotel guests can visit it.

By the way, here you can not only swim and sunbathe, but also have fun. Parasailing costs 50-60 dinars (about 1700 rubles). You can still ride on the "bananas" or rent a jet ski.





About staff

In Vincci el Mansour 4 * (Tunisia) an excellent attitude to guests. The staff is polite and welcoming. In this case, no one begs for a tip - you can completely do without them. Quality cleaning and daily, with the replacement of linen and towels. People arriving late in the rooms are sure to have a surprise - a bottle of wine and a fruit basket. And the alcohol in the bars, by the way, is undiluted. And his choice is diverse. Bottles are often opened right in front of customers. Bartenders know a lot of cocktail recipes, so you can feel free to ask them to cook something.

What else is worth knowing?

As mentioned earlier, the hotel is popular not only among lovers of beach holidays. Many come here for the sights. Well, tourists who have been here assure that it is better to book tours through the hotel operator, who is here every day. There are very interesting and advantageous offers, for example, a 2-day sightseeing tour in the Sahara with an overnight stay in a 5-star complex. Many people like sand dunes, the Eden Museum with a tasting of honey and palm juice, jeep and camel rides and much more. In which case, the operator himself will tell everything and advise the most interesting options.

It is also worth knowing that in Tunisia everything is bought for dinars. This is logical, but many think that here, as in many other countries, you can pay the euro. Currency can be exchanged at the hotel, the rate there is no less favorable than on the streets. Just do not need to exchange large amounts at once. Since back to dollars or euros, you can change everything only at the airport, where there is an unprofitable rate and huge lines.





General impression

In general, the hotel receives only positive recommendations and reviews. Which is not surprising, because everything is good in it, from helpful staff to food. And the prices are quite affordable.

It is best to come here in winter. Firstly, the weather in the supposedly cold season is milder than in summer. The sun is not scorching, water and air are quite warmed up. Secondly, prices are half as much, including accommodation, since there are not so many tourists in Tunisia in the winter - after all, most have summer vacations. And, by the way, for the same reason it is more spacious here both in a hotel and in a resort. Lovers of silence are also advised to come in winter. Since here at this time families with children who interfere with a peaceful rest do not rest.

Inveterate travelers and amateurs will definitely like it here. Numerous reviews are a direct confirmation of this. As well as the fact that many people who have been here come back to rest again in the same place.
