"Lights of Yekaterinburg" (residential complex): description

Yekaterinburg is one of the largest industrial cities in Russia, located in the Middle Urals. Its population currently exceeds 1.5 million people. From the point of view of developers, this city is a very promising place. Yekaterinburg Lights is a residential complex that is the most successful acquisition for buyers. He will be discussed in this article.





Ekaterinburg

This city is the largest administrative, scientific, cultural center of the Ural region. In Russia, it takes fourth place in the number of people living in it (the first three are Moscow, St. Petersburg and Novosibirsk).

Yekaterinburg is an important economic center of the country, producing 60% of global GDP. In addition, thanks to the international airport, the Trans-Siberian Railway and six federal highways, the city is the largest transport and logistics hub of the state. Here are concentrated many industrial enterprises in the field of optical-mechanical industry, instrument making, metallurgy, light and food industries.





Location of the residential complex

Yekaterinburg Lights is a residential complex located in the very center of the city. At the intersection of Bolshakov and Belinsky streets. This arrangement provides residents with the most convenient transport accessibility, allowing them to quickly get to their homes from anywhere in the city. This can be done both on your own car and on public transport. Shuttle taxis go to the residential complex. A few meters away is a tram line.

Description of the complex

LCD "Fires of Yekaterinburg" is a 23-storey residential building, erected on a monolithic reinforced concrete technology. The complex is equipped with its own gas boiler room, which allows its residents not to experience any inconvenience associated with the supply of heat and hot water to the apartments. Installed air conditioning and fire.





In order to manage the complex more professionally and comfortably for residents, the reins are given to their own management company. It attracts experts and specialists in the field of professional organization of residential and commercial real estate. For those who purchase apartments in Yekaterinburg in order to invest money, buying a property in this complex will be a bargain.





Complex completion date

Yekaterinburg Lights (residential complex) was commissioned on time - in 2015. Construction began in 2013 and, in accordance with the terms stipulated by the construction declaration, it was completed exactly two years later, and the keys to the apartments were handed over to the apartment owners. The developer of the complex is the Atomstroykompleks company, founded in 1995 and today is one of the leading construction companies in the Ural region. The company's reputation and project portfolio speaks for itself: a quarter of all the new buildings in Yekaterinburg were built by it.

Security in the complex

The Yekaterinburg Lights complex, reviews of which are mostly positive, can offer its residents everything they need for a comfortable life. As already mentioned, the heat is supplied to the apartments by the own gas boiler house.

For owners of their own vehicles, a three-level underground parking is provided, which is under 24-hour security, which can be accessed directly from the elevator. For arriving guests of the residents of the complex there is a ground guest parking. In addition to parking, the entire territory is also under the supervision of a round-the-clock monitoring service, equipped with a modern video surveillance system consisting of 189 cameras.
