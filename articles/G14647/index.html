Azur Royal Resort 5 *, Makadi Bay, Egypt: hotel description, photos and reviews of tourists

Holidays in the Land of the Pharaohs, of course, has its own characteristics. Vacationers in Egypt will first have to (during an air flight) see an unforgettable landscape: a majestic desert covered with roads, like veins, the boundless turquoise expanse of the Red Sea.

In order for the vacation to succeed, it is important for the tourist to choose a solid hotel that provides quality resort services. Those vacationers who purchase a tour to the five-star Royal Azur Resort 5 * hotel (Hurghada, Makadi Bay) will not be disappointed.



This hotel complex was built in 1998 in the first line of the Makadi Bay Bay of Hurghada. From the airport to it is only twenty-five kilometers. The advantage of this hotel is to ensure a good rest from the city rush and crowds. The local animation is unobtrusive. This is a solid family-run hotel complex that offers a comfortable economy class vacation. It is quite large, with 480 rooms. The main highlight of the hotel is one of the best beaches in Hurghada.

Low prices with excellent service

It is nice to realize that a wonderful vacation in Egypt is possible at very attractive prices. Especially in winter. We provide relevant information. In January 2016, for those who wish to relax, significant discounts in the region of 43% for the purchase of tours at Royal Azur Resort 5 * apply. The price of a room for 6 nights (respectively, 7 days) in January 2016 is actually $ 483 against $ 826 of the average annual cost.

Such a significant discount is due to the climatic features of this month in Egypt. January and February are considered the coldest months, but not for residents of the middle zone! Judge for yourself: a 5-hour flight, and the Red Sea with a water temperature of 21 degrees Celsius is at the service of tourists.

At this time, in Egypt, the hamsin wind (translated into Russian - ‚Äúfifty‚Äù) blows from the desert, making it difficult for tourists to relax. Every year he carries with him the air of the Sahara for exactly fifty days. Vacationers in Egypt in the winter because of this are practically unable to travel long distances. The hot sandy wind, often turning into storms, turns motor transport trips into hell. The finest sand penetrates not only the interior of the car, but also (inexplicably) through the double-glazed windows. So, the January or February desire of a tourist to examine the pyramids of Giza or the burial place of Luxor becomes practically irrelevant.

However, the resort where the Royal Azur Resort 5 * hotel is located , Hurghada (Makadi), is protected from the wind by the mountains. This is the best place in Egypt in January - February, and also very worthy at other times of the year. If vacationers are going to enjoy a sea holiday and get acquainted with the unique underwater world of the Red Sea, then the choice of hotel will not disappoint them.

Makadi Bay Village

The village itself is purely resort, it is composed exclusively of hotel infrastructures. Formally, it belongs to Hurghada, but in fact it has its own resort system.

Here (Egypt, Makadi) there are no Egyptian apartment buildings nearby. In addition to tourists - only the staff of hotel complexes. The largest of these associations is called Madinat Makadi. It consists of chain hotels: French Iberotel, Italian Sol Y Mar and German Jaz.





In terms of relaxation, the choice of a quiet and peaceful hotel Royal Azur Resort 5 * in this village is preferable because of the beach, which we will discuss later. Makadi village is located in a unique ecological zone adjacent to the sea, where sea turtles and dolphins are not uncommon.

By the way, the resort itself (Egypt, Makadi) is only half an hour from Hurghada. Transport (minibus) travels regularly. Round trip costs $ 8. It is very convenient in terms of shopping.

Makadi is world famous for its chic coral reefs - a favorite destination for professional divers. And at the Royal Azur Resort Makadi, scuba diving enthusiasts are frequent guests.

Territory

Newcomers to the hotel in the resort village of Makadi under consideration are striking in their excellent landscape design. It is implemented really harmoniously.

The territory is small, about 100 to 100 meters. She is professionally landscaped and abundantly planted with flowers. The terraces are artistically decorated. An atmosphere of chamberness, calmness, and pacification has been created here. The pools of the hotel complex are artistically arranged in the form of a cascade.

The hotel staff is attentive to guests and helpful. Comments made by vacationers on duty at the reception are eliminated almost immediately. Here, at the reception, you can rent a car, sign up for an interesting tour. Among the employees of the hotel complex Azur Royal Resort there are Russian-speaking.

In the neighborhood there are such compact comfortable landscaped areas of the Stela and Club Azur hotels. But their beaches are inferior to Royal Resort in terms of convenience.

Hotel buildings

At the Azur Royal Resort hotel complex there are three two- / three-story residential buildings. They are located in such a way that they resemble the letter U, turned by the open part to the sea lagoon. For new arrivals, it is preferable to choose a newer building with the numbers "2 + 1" (it is most remote from the sea). In two other buildings, located closer to the beach, smaller rooms: double and single.



However, not only buildings are the place of residence of guests.

In 9 stylish chalets live lovers of an interior stylized as rural. Such apartments are in demand by tourists of the Royal Azur Resort (photos of them are often visible on the advertising of the hotel complex). The internal surroundings of these buildings are traditional: timber walls, low ceiling, original lighting. The local chalets repeat the details of a traditional house of the same name in the Alps - stylish wooden furniture, rugs, various accessories from cashmere and leather.

Room furnishings

The rooms of the hotel complex are presentable and well-groomed, high-quality wooden furniture is updated on time when it reaches a noticeable degree of wear. They have a balcony specially equipped for relaxation (terrace).

The room has a TV with four channels, air conditioning, a free safe and a minibar. A positive aspect of the service is the daily update of drinks in the minibar and linen in the room. The minibar is replenished daily with two bottles of drinking water and one - mineral.

The room also has small appliances: electric kettle and hairdryer.

For Russian-speaking guests in the TV program 4 channels.

The bathroom of the hotel room is equipped with a shower.

There is Wi-Fi in the reception area enough to receive and send mail.

The hotel infrastructure is carefully thought out and maintained in good condition. Judging by the reviews of regular guests of the hotel, its material base is developing dynamically due to investment.

The hotel has a modern conference room equipped with the latest office equipment for video conferencing.

There is paid Wi-Fi, which allows you to dynamically work with online programs. For lovers of comfortable gaming Internet, the hotel has an Internet cafe. Information services in the hotel complex are paid.

Vacationers at Royal Azur Resort 5 * have the opportunity to engage in outdoor activities. For tennis lovers, two courts are equipped here, there is a volleyball court and a five-a-side football field.

Trips to Hurghada

In order to diversify the vacation, vacationers of the resort (Egypt, Makadi) periodically visit the 90-thousandth Hurghada, which is the center of the Red Sea province.

Its main attractions are located in the old part of the city, which is called Dahar. Among the most interesting places are the Coptic church, the mosque of Abdulhasan Elshazi, the eastern market. Here, closer to the port, is located the entertainment center "New Marina". The favorite place for vacationers and townspeople is the promenade, dotted with cafes, restaurants, attractions (among the most popular - "Vertical Take-Off").

If you want to relax in a more lively place than calm Makadi, you can recommend the hotel of the same Royal Azur Resort 4 chain (Hurghada). For beginners who love water sports, it also makes sense to periodically visit Hurghada, because it is the international center of this branch of outdoor activities. Here you can go through a serious school of snorkeling (snorkelling). The undoubted benefits of such classes are testimonial. Royal Azur Resort Hotel itself is located near picturesque reefs. Therefore, vacationers who are knowledgeable in scrolling and diving, get more positive impressions than lovers of a quiet passive rest.

Here for vacationers who want to get the initial qualification of a diver, there are numerous diving courses of various degrees of difficulty. Trainees are issued international certificates suggesting the possibility of diving of a certain complexity.

Also in Hurghada, it is proposed to do windsurfing, kiting.

Trips to El Gouna

Fans of comfortable outdoor activities are advised to visit El Gouna, it is still romantically called Venice in the sands. From the hotel Royal Azur Royal Resort 5 * (Hurghada, Makadi Bay), it is only fifty kilometers away. This is a unique place, the likes of which no longer exists on Earth.

Sami Saveris, an Egyptian billionaire, owns a hotel and leisure island infrastructure with picturesque bridges and canals. It was designed by the ten most famous world architects.
