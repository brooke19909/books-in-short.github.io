Ares (Mahmutlar, Alanya): description and reviews of tourists

If you want to spend an excellent vacation in Turkey, so beloved by many of our compatriots, and at the same time want to stay in a comfortable hotel, the price of accommodation in which, however, does not hit much on the family budget, then a four-star Ares (Mahmutlar, Alanya) will be a good option. Read what our guests expect here, as well as what opinions are left from staying at the hotel with tourists, read in our article.



Location

The four-star Ares Hotel is located in the resort village of Mahmutlar. It is 125 kilometers away from the tourist capital of Turkey - the city of Antalya. Since the nearest international airport is located here, it will take about two hours to arrive on the way to the hotel. The hotel is located on the first coastline and has its own sand and pebble beach. The distance to it is several tens of meters.

Housing stock

Ares (Mahmutlar, Alanya) has 106 rooms located in a five-story building with an elevator. All rooms are standard and are designed for a maximum of 3 + 1 guests. There are several apartments specially equipped for disabled guests. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. Each room has a bathroom with a hairdryer, air conditioning, necessary furniture, a TV and a balcony. There is also a minibar and a safe, but they can only be used at an additional cost. In addition, it is possible to connect to Wi-Fi (paid service).



Ares (Mahmutlar, Alanya) Travel Reviews

In order for you to have a more complete picture of this hotel, we give a generalized impression of our compatriots who stayed here. In general, most travelers characterize this hotel as very suitable for unassuming people who want to save on accommodation during their holidays.

The rooms, according to our compatriots, are cozy, spacious and clean. Furniture and appliances are modern, all in working condition. If something fails, then after contacting the reception the problem will be solved as soon as possible. Rooms are cleaned every day, towels are changed twice, and linen three times a week. In general, it is noted that the staff in Ares (Mahmutlar, Alanya) 4 * is very friendly, helpful and quick.

As for food, here, as with most hotels, the opinions of the guests were divided. Someone found the proposed menu quite diverse, and the quality of the food is good. Someone did not like the food, and they assure that they had to eat in cafes and restaurants.

According to our compatriots, there is no special animation at the hotel. So, sometimes animators offer those who wish to play games by the pool, and in the evening they take everyone out to a nightclub. Mini-discos for children are also held.

Tourists liked the location of the hotel. So, the beach can be reached in just a couple of minutes. The beach belongs to Ares (Mahmutlar, Alanya), so there is no need to pay for renting sun loungers and umbrellas. Also near the hotel there is a large supermarket, kiosks selling fast food, cafes, shops with a variety of goods and souvenirs, tour agencies.
