Prestigio tablets: user reviews and description of models

Currently, there are a huge number of different tablet computers that differ from each other in terms of parameters and price. That is why it is quite difficult for a modern consumer to decide on the choice of a device. However, there are brands that in this category deserve special attention, because they combine increased reliability, quality and relatively low price with maximum functionality. These include Prestigio tablets. Reviews of these devices have made them famous as the most inexpensive and affordable. At the same time, even the most demanding customers can find the most functional models that are not inferior to famous brands.



First of all, it is worth noting such Prestigio tablets, reviews of which indicate their availability. These include the model "Prestigio MultiPad PMP3270B". Such devices are equipped with processors with a frequency of 1000 MHz budget model. They have a RAM of no more than 512 MB and connect to the Internet only via WI-FI. At the same time, such a device does not have additional functions such as GPS or Bluetooth, but at a low price ($ 70), such disadvantages are almost invisible. It is worth noting that even the Prestigio tablet, the price of which falls under the budget category, is equipped with a multi-touch capacitive screen, which makes it very prestigious compared to other brands of the same price category.



If you need a model with 3G capability and an 8-inch screen, you should pay attention to more expensive devices from this manufacturer. These include the Prestigio MultiPad 2 PMP 7280 C model, which best represents the Prestigio functional tablets. Reviews about them are twofold, because you can find devices of other manufacturers for the same money ($ 150), but they will not have such parameters and additional features. However, this group also has disadvantages. They consist in a short battery life , which makes such a Prestigio tablet less mobile. The manual says that it can work for about 120 hours in standby mode, but with a battery capacity of 2500 mAh, watching video files will put the device in five hours. Although for many this is quite enough, because where WI-FI is used, you can always find access to the electrical network.



Some of the most expensive models from this manufacturer cost about $ 300. However, these models have pretty good parameters and slight differences from less expensive ones. This is what characterizes all Prestigio tablets. Reviews about this line of these devices are very concise, because they say that for the same money you can buy devices better. In fact, this statement is close to the truth. All models of this price category differ only in large screen and processor type. Moreover, the RAM and additional functions remain unchanged. That is why they do not have much demand in the mobile device market, although many users assure them of their promise and reliability.
