Hotel Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Thousands of Russian tourists choose Egypt for their holidays each year. After all, this country offers not only relaxation by the warm sea. Here you will find luxurious hotels, high quality service and genuine Oriental hospitality. It is no secret that one of the most popular Egyptian resorts is the city of Sharm El Sheikh. It is here that the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * hotel complex is located.

Of course, during the trip planning period, every traveler tries to get as much information as possible about a particular place. So where exactly is the hotel located and how far is the beach? What conditions of accommodation and meals are offered to guests? Can I come here with a child? Are there any opportunities to have fun? Each tourist asks these questions.

Where is the hotel complex located?

The distance to the colorful Old Town is only three kilometers - it is easy to overcome it even on foot. Thus, guests have the opportunity at any convenient time to explore all the attractions of the resort and study the history of the ancient country.

The famous Naama Bay is about 10 km away. You can get to it by public transport or call a taxi. The distance to the international airport is about 20 kilometers. In any case, the hotel will provide you with a transfer - 30 minutes after leaving the airport you will arrive at the hotel.

What does the hotel look like? Brief description of the territory





Many tourists are interested in the infrastructure of the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 *. The photo shows that the hotel area is really large, its area is about 35 thousand square meters. The hotel consists of a main building and several small residential buildings built in a traditional oriental style. Most of the courtyard is reserved for pools and terraces for relaxation. There are also sports fields. And almost the entire territory is decorated with bright exotic plants and beautiful palm trees, among which it is always a pleasure to relax.

By the way, this hotel is part of a hotel chain called Shores Golden. Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * was opened in 2005. The last overhaul was carried out in 2015, so today this place meets all the requirements of tourists and international standards.

Hotel Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 *: photo and description of rooms





The hotel has 250 rooms. Basically, these are standard rooms designed for shared accommodation of 2, 3 or 4 people. On the first floors of residential buildings there are several rooms, specially equipped for the stay of tourists with disabilities. There are also connected rooms that are suitable for large families and vacationers.

Almost every room has a private terrace or balcony with views of the pool or the flowering garden. Naturally, the rooms are equipped with the necessary furniture and household appliances. In particular, there is a split air conditioner that will help to create the most comfortable temperature conditions. In the room you can watch satellite (including some Russian) channels on TV. Guests can use the telephone and safe. A minibar is available, but its filling is paid separately.

You will appreciate the spacious bathroom where you can take a bath or shower. Here you will find sets of clean towels, sets of hygiene and cosmetics, as well as a hairdryer. Tourists rarely complain about cleanliness, because cleaning is carried out every day.

What kind of food is offered to guests?





Almost all resort complexes in Egypt operate according to the convenient all-inclusive scheme. Hotel 4 * Otium Hotel Golden Sharm (ex Shores) is no exception. Each guest, paying for accommodation, gets the right to a full three meals a day. Three meals a day are served in the spacious restaurant of the main restaurant. There is always enough food here, and the choice is quite large, and they offer international and Egyptian dishes, so every tourist will find something to their taste. Almost every week, the restaurant hosts themed dinners dedicated to the traditions and cuisine of a particular country.

Additional hotel catering facilities





In addition to the main restaurant, on the territory of the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * there are other places where you can eat. For example, travelers recommend visiting the Altxandrina Restaurant, which serves brunch, light snacks and various types of pizza throughout the day, and offers delicious oriental cuisine for dinner.

There are also several bars near the pools, in the lobby and on the beach. Here guests can get snacks and sweets, chilled drinks, cocktails and spirits. It is worth saying only that imported products (including alcoholic ones) are paid separately.

On the other hand, next to the hotel, as well as in the city, there are many catering establishments for every taste, including luxurious restaurants, cozy coffee houses and lively cafes, so every traveler can find something to his liking.

Where is the beach located?

Since most tourists come to Egypt in search of a fun holiday on the seashore, the interesting question is what the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * can offer in this regard. The beach is located about 500 meters from the hotel territory - a convenient pedestrian path leads to it. A private 450-meter stretch of the seashore awaits you, shared by two more neighboring hotels of the same Shores chain.

The beach is covered with soft sand and is cleaned every day. The entrance to the sea is comfortable, the bottom is sandy. Nevertheless, there is a coral reef nearby, so tourists are advised to get special shoes. There is a shallow area where children like to swim. For lovers of swimming at the depths, a convenient pontoon is equipped.

Comfortable deck chairs with mattresses and sun umbrellas are in sufficient quantity on the shore. Here you can get clean beach towels for free. There is a small bar on the beach where you can get chilled drinks and light snacks.

Are there any conditions for water recreation?





Will it be interesting to fans of more active leisure at the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 *? Traveler reviews indicate that here everyone will find a pleasant way to have fun. For example, a beach volleyball court is equipped on the shore, where fun competitions are often held.

Here you can rent a boat, pedal boats and canoes. For a small fee, tourists have fun water skiing, banana, parasailing. And on a boat you can take a short trip. By the way, this coast is popular among windsurfing and sailing enthusiasts.

Since the Red Sea is famous for its luxurious coral reefs, a diving school is organized at the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 *. Here, tourists with a certificate can rent the necessary equipment and purchase a tour to the most interesting places for diving. Beginners have the opportunity to take special preparatory courses and enjoy the delights of the underwater world in the company of professionals.

Conditions for staying with children





Today, many parents are going on a trip with their children. What amenities and entertainment can Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * offer to young tourists? In fact, the main category of vacationers here are couples with children of different ages.

As for amenities, if necessary, an extra comfortable cot is delivered to the room. The restaurant attentive waiters will certainly offer a high chair for the youngest guests. Unfortunately, there is no special children's menu here. Nevertheless, among a large number of dishes it will be easy to choose something suitable for your baby.

As for the entertainment program, a mini-club works every day for young tourists, where children can play, draw, take part in various productions and team games. There is also a special section of the pool designed for bathing children - the water here is constantly heated.

Additional service in the complex

Naturally, the hotel has everything you need to make the stay of tourists as convenient as possible. There is free parking at the hotel, and car rental is fairly easy in the city. Naturally, dry cleaning and laundry are constantly working, the services of which are quite affordable.

In case of health problems, a qualified doctor is called for tourists. Internet access is available in the lobby, but this service is charged separately. If desired, you can visit the local Internet cafe. Guests can rent a safe at the reception free of charge. By the way, in the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * there are several cozy shops where you can purchase various household trifles, as well as interesting oriental souvenirs.

Entertainment and leisure





Naturally, the hotel has something to do. For example, there is a large pool with one water slide, where it is always a pleasure to spend time. Nearby, on the terrace, there are sun loungers where you can sunbathe, relax or just chat. The second pool is equipped with a heating system and works in cold weather.

The hotel has several sports fields. You should definitely visit the massage room, where only the best specialists work. At the spa, you can enjoy a bath or sauna, and book a variety of wellness treatments. The fitness center has a gym. In addition, guests are constantly held sports group classes. You can also sign up for dance classes (including belly dancing).

In the evenings, guests can enjoy exciting shows, which include performances by local groups, dance and acrobatic performances, etc. Live music is played that can be danced or just relaxed. Naturally, this is not all that the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * hotel offers. Egypt is a country with a unique ancient history. Therefore, here you can easily sign up for an interesting excursion, where they will tell you more about local traditions and culture.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

As already mentioned, this hotel complex is primarily focused on family vacations - most often families with children stop here. This place is also suitable for lovers of evening entertainment and night clubs, because the city center can be reached very easily. Often, elderly people spend their vacations here, who also find interesting activities for themselves. And of course, travelers who prefer to spend time on various excursions will also not be disappointed, because the city has a huge number of travel agencies, and Sharm el Sheikh itself is rich in interesting sights.

Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 *: tourist reviews

Many people, going on a trip, are interested in the opinions of those who have already managed to visit a particular place. What do tourists say about the Otium Hotel Golden Sharm 4 * hotel? Reviews are mostly positive. It is really very beautiful and clean here. The rooms are spacious, neatly cleaned. Household appliances in good condition. As for food, some tourists sometimes complain about some uniformity. Nevertheless, there is a choice of dishes, so no one will remain hungry.

A small minus is the remoteness of the beach. On the other hand, it can be reached in a few minutes, it is closed to outsiders, and there are plenty of opportunities to have fun. Special thanks to the guests telling the animators who really love their work and make their stay at the hotel pleasant and fun.
