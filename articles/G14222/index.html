Picturesque and unique bay of Balos

On the territory of the island of Crete there is a unique and incredibly beautiful bay of Balos, which for many decades has attracted tourists from all over the world. Often it is called the confluence of the three seas, since it is there, according to geographers, that the waters of the Cretan, Ionian and Libyan basins intersect.

Balos Bay is famous for the beauty of its seas, as well as for its extraordinary nature, vegetation and wildlife. Children come here to rest in a noisy company, and often the newlyweds prefer to spend their honeymoon in this corner of the world.



Basically, Balos Bay is formed by the adjacent Tigani Peninsula, which also forms a good sandbank in the area. On most beaches, the depth of the water does not exceed seventy to eighty centimeters, as a result of which the sea warms up to very high temperatures. Also, thanks to the rich underwater world, water plays with all its colors. Often, young artists create their masterpieces on the shores of this picturesque harbor, trying to convey the beauty and color of these unique waters.



Balos Bay is surrounded by wide and spacious beaches that are covered with snow-white sands. In some places, the sand acquires even a slightly pinkish tint, which gives special magic and originality to this resort. It is also worth noting that the vast majority of the beaches in the harbor are not equipped with modern sun loungers, awnings, as well as various restaurants, discotheques and other entertainment complexes. You can rent a sunbed at your hotel, although there is no particular need for it, because there is nothing more beautiful than basking in the snow-white southern sand.



The historical state that has always ruled here - Greece, also gives its charm to this region. Balos Bay is the purest example of the confluence of nature and monuments of the ancient world. Progress has not captured this region too much, therefore nature is the hostess here. And the main thing that you will see in this place is landscapes of unearthly beauty, the colored sea, pink sunsets, long sandy valleys and a riot of greenery. This place is ideal for a measured and relaxing holiday, for solitude or for relaxation after a busy city and bustle.

It is best to get to this wonderful place by car, although sightseeing buses and local minibuses go there. You need to navigate to the village of Cavigliani. If you get lost on the road, the locals will always tell you the right path. It is also advisable to focus on the northwest of the island, because it is in this region that Balos Bay is located.

Photos of this miracle of nature are presented in the article, and looking at them you can try to feel the atmosphere that prevails in this place. However, in order to fully enjoy it, it is advisable to visit Crete. After all, it is there that the picturesque and unique Balos Bay opens its natural doors in front of you and gives you many unforgettable moments. Have a nice rest!
