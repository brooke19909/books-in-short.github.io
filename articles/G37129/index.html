Sberbank cards: how to find the code word

Sberbank customers who use a bank card sometimes find themselves in situations where they urgently need to call support. Most often this is due to the need to block the plastic product in case of loss or theft. Card operations when calling the Contact Center are made only after the verification information has been reported. If the client does not remember what he indicated in the application for opening an account, he should keep in mind how to find out the code word in Sberbank.

Control information: what is it?

The control word in "Sberbank" is an alphabetic and / or digital combination of 4 or more characters, which the client indicates when issuing a plastic card.

The Bank does not impose restrictions on the entered data, with the exception of information related to:

  • Name of owner.
  • Date of birth.
  • Passport data of the client.

Information is confidential and confidential: bank employees do not have access to a control word. If the client has forgotten the code word of the Sberbank card, how to find out, the employee of the organization will tell him, but the operator cannot name the previous combination. Such information will also not be provided in the support service: Contact Center specialists are not authorized to disclose confidential customer data.

Why change the code word?

Change of control information, as well as its restoration, can only be initiated by the owner of the plastic card. Without the knowledge of the client, Sberbank is not entitled to change the data.

The need to use the control information is revealed when you call the Contact Center of a financial organization. In this case, if the user does not remember the data, he should check with the operator how to find out the code word of the Sberbank card.

In this case, the call center specialist does not have the right to disclose previously entered data, therefore, in case of loss of the application, it is recommended that the cardholder change the control information.

How to find the code word in "Sberbank"?

If you need to use the control information, it is recommended to have an application for processing a debit (or credit) card on hand, which indicates the code word. If the user of the bank card has lost the certificate, it is recommended:

  1. Contact the bank to recover the lost document.
  2. Change the code word.
  3. Try to recall the information in the document.

It is not possible to find out the code word in Sberbank through an ATM or Internet banking.

Recovery of lost data with the help of Sberbank employees

Another way to find out the code word in Sberbank is to request information in the archive. It is about restoring a copy of the statement lost by the client.

When a bank card is issued, 2 copies of the document are printed: the first is issued personally to the account holder, the second is sent to the archive of the financial organization. After registration of the appeal, it is sent to processing. Archive service specialists review the user's application and decide whether to send a copy or refuse to provide a certificate. The review period takes up to 30 days.

In case of satisfying customer requirements, the card holder receives an SMS notification of the bank or a call from a specialist. The client can receive a copy of the application at the office in which he left the appeal.

How to change control information?

The fastest and most popular way to find out the code word in Sberbank is to replace the previously specified data. It is allowed if the owner of the credit card indicates the same control information that was entered when opening the bank account. This may be because managers do not see characters previously entered by customers and cannot inform the owner of the code word indicated at the opening.

Change of control information can be made at any office of the bank. To complete the operation, the client must provide a passport. The code word is a unique identifier for all plastic cards: it is enough to indicate a combination of characters only once.

When replacing a word, previously entered characters become irrelevant. A feature of the code word is that it is used for identification on all bank cards of the client. The owner of credit cards does not need to indicate several options, it is enough to replace the information on all accounts once.

Fraud Protection

When changing or initially entering control information, a Sberbank credit card user should keep in mind that these data can be used by cybercriminals to obtain financial and other benefits. Therefore, when changing control information, it is not recommended:

  • Communicate the word to anyone, even from near or dear ones. Moreover, it is forbidden to disclose data to third parties that may be submitted by bank employees.
  • Keep copies and originals of the application for a card in easily accessible places (on bedside tables, shelves, near window and doorways).

If the control information has been lost or forgotten, the account holder should immediately contact the bank for its change. The term of the service takes no more than 24 hours.
