Niche

Price, parameters and specifications are among the mid-range solutions of Lenovo K3 Note. The recall of any owner of such a smart phone indicates that this device is in no way inferior even to flagship solutions. Design and usability

Most of the front black panel is occupied by a screen (its diagonal is a solid 5.5 inches), protected by an oleophobic coating in Lenovo K3 Note. Feedback almost any smartphone owner is distinguished by its high quality. Under the display is displayed, as expected, a control panel of 3 buttons with backlight. Above the screen are sensors, a speaker and a front camera. Mechanical control buttons are correctly grouped on the left edge of the smartphone. An audio port is displayed on its upper side. At the bottom is the MicroUSB. Next to him is a conversational microphone. The back cover, unlike the front panel, can be made in various color options: white, black, blue, yellow and green. It displays a loud speaker, the main camera and its dual LED backlight.

Reviews and price

The Lenovo K3 Note is almost perfect. The reviews and the price of the gadget indicate that its competitors simply do not have chances. A powerful processor and graphics accelerator, a large amount of memory, an excellent screen, high-quality main and front cameras, a fresh version of system software are far from a complete list of the advantages of this smartphone model. The minus, however, is essentially one - this is a small battery capacity of 3000 mAh. You can solve this problem in one of the following two ways. One of them is reducing the load on the device and saving battery life. And the second one is connecting an external battery (you will have to buy it separately).
