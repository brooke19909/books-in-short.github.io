Paphiessa Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos): hotel description, services, reviews

Cyprus is an amazing island from which antiquity blows. There is a special atmosphere of antiquity, a complex historical past and, of course, incredible hospitality.

Cyprus is full of fascinating places, rooted in the distant past.

Paphos is a small resort on the island. Its streets themselves are a local attraction. Choosing this place to relax, you can learn a lot about the history of the island, its cultural heritage and local traditions. Among other things, there are many places for leisure. It is in Paphos that one of the best water parks in Europe is located. But most of all tourists are attracted by wonderful beaches. There are a huge number of them. By renting a car, you can go around everything and choose the place under the sun that really suits you.

Ideal option is accommodation in one of the local hotels. Experienced travelers are advised not to spend a lot of money on accommodation. In Cyprus, it is important to spend the night and eat. The rest of the time is best spent on beaches or excursions.

It is worth paying attention to the three-star complex Paphiessa Hotel 3.

Hotel Description



A small and welcoming hotel designed for family and quiet relaxation. There are no frills and fanciful riches; in return, the tourist is offered a cozy and homely rest, a relaxing atmosphere, conducive to a pleasant pastime.

Great location

Paphiessa Hotel 3 is located in Paphos. The complex includes 6 buildings. Accommodation is convenient in that the guests of the establishment have access to all the bars, restaurants and shops, and most importantly, all the attractions are nearby. The good news is that the staff speaks Russian, so finding a common language, as well as learning all the necessary information, will be easy.

Beach

Some discomfort may result from the fact that there is no beach near the hotel. Therefore, getting to the nearest will have about 1 kilometer. This path can be overcome on foot or take the bus, next to the hotel there is a bus stop. The city beach is sandy, it is constantly cleaned, so there is no garbage there. Sun loungers, umbrellas and towels are available.

Infrastructure





If you do not want to get to the beach, then a good alternative is swimming in the pool. There are several of them at the Paphiessa Hotel and Apt, they are filled with fresh water. There is a sunbathing area next to the pool. There are also several bars, a cafe, a restaurant. The cuisine here is European, and local traditional dishes are also offered. For the convenience of guests, a tour desk is located on site, the staff of which will be happy to pick up interesting leisure activities and tell about all the attractions that you can visit. For those who want to explore the city by private car, you can rent a car directly at the hotel, it will take a little time, and most importantly, save precious minutes on vacation. There is also a beauty salon on the territory, where everyone can refresh their style or radically change their hairstyle.

Children's holiday

The hotel also takes care of its young guests. Therefore, at the reception you can use the services of a nanny. This employee speaks Russian, so it will not be difficult for her to find contact with the child, which means that your child will be looked after for a while. The restaurant has a special children's menu, all the dishes are very beautifully served, and the child is interested in eating.

Entertainment

For relaxation, Paphiessa Hotel (Paphos) offers massages, a bathhouse, a sauna and various beauty treatments. A gym and a tennis court are provided for more active relaxation. From evening entertainment, guests are offered karaoke, themed parties (for a fee), a disco, as well as evenings with live music.

Description of rooms





As already mentioned, the Paphiessa Hotel and Apt consists of 6 buildings with a total of 110 rooms. The apartments are available in the following classes:

- Standart room;
- Studio number;
- Onebedroom apartments.

Each room has a small balcony, apartments of a more expensive category have their own terrace. Paphiessa Hotel Apartments is a middle class hotel, it cannot afford new equipment, so all the furniture is a bit old, but the apartments are systematically cleaned and try to maintain cleanliness. Each room has its own bathroom, hairdryers and towels. There is also air conditioning, satellite TV with many channels, a small refrigerator and a safe (for a fee). This room may have pool views (rooms at Paphiessa Hotel and Hotel Apts 3 standard room) or mountains. The superior apartments have a small, private kitchen, so you can buy groceries in the store and save on meals in a restaurant or cafe.

The rooms are non-smoking, but the hotel has designated smoking areas. Internet access is available only in the hotel lobby, there is no Network in the rooms.

Paphiessa Hotel Apartments (Cyprus, Paphos) is a great option for a budget holiday, tourists will definitely like it.

Location Reviews

Check-in at the hotel is strictly on schedule. Whenever you arrive in the morning, you will be settled at two in the afternoon. You can pass the waiting time by exploring local attractions.

Even if the room is not booked in advance, the hotel is trying to provide its guests with only the best apartments available.

A special coloring is already felt in the corridor, where there are carpets on the floor, which today can be seen with grandmothers on the walls. But this does not spoil the atmosphere. On the walls of the floors you can see plain paintings with strange plots. Some of them even open, but inside there is a void.

About rooms

Paphiessa Hotel Apartments is designed for a budget holiday, so the quality of the rooms here is appropriate. Nevertheless, the rooms are spacious, many have balconies. The rooms are in need of repair, but the decor creates a pleasant atmosphere of coziness. A special role in the interior is created by magnificent wooden furniture combined with antique curtains.

It is better to warn about your preferences regarding sleeping places, otherwise you may get a room where two single beds will be moved together.

Of the bath accessories, only soap is available, the rest must be taken with you.

Get ready for cats in the room and in the dining room. There are a lot of them in the district, but they are harmless. There are practically no insects.

The air conditioner is old, sometimes out of order, but free. Alternatively, you can use a fan, but again, it is not in all rooms. A safe is available for a fee.

The rooms have a small old TV, which even shows the Russian channel. But in Cyprus, watching television is a crime when there are so many interesting things around.

Room Service





Towels and bed linen are available on request. Do not expect maids to do this without reminder. The closet has extra pillows and warm blankets. There are enough hangers for clothes. Some items of comfort no longer work, for example, lamps, although beautiful, but do not shine. There is light on the balcony.

All rooms have a built-in radio that plays popular Cyprus music.

Entertainment

For the sake of the sea, sun and fun, travelers go to Cyprus. Paphiessa Hotel offers its guests an interesting leisure time every evening. The bar has an entertainment program with karaoke, guest artists and other entertainment events.





Guests who prefer a relaxing holiday can take evening walks along the Paphos promenade with many shops and cafes. The hotel infrastructure is well thought out. The noise from the bar to the rooms is practically not heard.

The hotel has a nice pool. Opposite the complex is a wonderful sports center with a large water area. Classes cost 5 euros.

Beach vacation

The road to the beach takes about 20 minutes walking through the busy quarter. You should not spend too much money to live on the first line. In Cyprus, this is simply pointless. All beaches are municipal, equipped equally well with sunbeds and umbrellas.

The closest to Paphiessa Hotel 3 is the beach, adjacent to the Alexander Hotel Complex. The coastal zone is clean, the entrance to the water is gentle. The sea is sometimes cooler than elsewhere.

The staff is a separate article in the hotel comments. The Paphiessa Hotel complex is no exception.

Service Reviews





Hotel staff for the most part try to please the guests as they can. But you can also encounter a biased attitude towards tourists who do not speak English. This is expressed in looks, gestures, intonation.

The owners of the hotel are nice people, but if they notice arrogance on the part of the guests, then the latter will have to endure their entire rude attitude to themselves. Perhaps this is justified, as some tourists behave obscenely.

If you remain human to the end, then your attitude towards you will be human.

  2. The cafe has huge portions. Side dishes, bread and sauces are also free. One serving is enough to fill two adults. Prices in almost all establishments are the same. So you can significantly save on excursions, the cost of which is on average 30 - 40 euros per person. There are tours for 350 euros, but all this can be visited almost for free, but on your own.

Cyprus is an unusually fabulous island, which is accessible even for tourists with a small income. The hotels here are only for overnight, it is better to spend your time not on burning money in local bars, but on interesting trips to amazing places. Therefore, the Paphiessa Hotel 3 is a great option for those who like to save money. Nevertheless, there are good living conditions, varied food and a wide range of additional services. This hotel complex is ideal for those who prefer a relaxing holiday with family and close friends. Youth here may be bored.
