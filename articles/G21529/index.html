Mobile phone teXet X-driver Quad TM-4082R: reviews, specifications

This smartphone promises a lot of extremals, active athletes and autotourists, that is, all those who wanted to distance themselves from the benefits of civilization, but did not dare to abandon the familiar and universal Android gadget.

A bit of educational program

Such devices have existed in the mobile device market for quite some time. If we do not focus on various military and industrial equipment, such as geodetic instruments, walkie-talkies and other heavy-duty terminals, we will see that long before the first mobile phone appeared, shockproof and waterproof watches were offered to the mass consumer - first mechanics, and a little later, electronics.

Every year, cellular communication develops and multiplies its consumer - moisture-proof and reinforced designs of mobile gadgets begin to appear. At first, the devices were protected only from splashing water or rain. The pioneers in this segment were the brands Siemens and Nokia, which released in the early 2000s. shockproof and moisture resistant Nokia 5210/5100/5140 and Siemens models ME45 / M65 / M75. Gadgets turned out to be quite decent, but times have changed a lot, and this class in today's realities has gone under the wing of the Chinese.

The design recipe for such devices is almost the same for all Chinese brands. They take the usual and unremarkable filling of some budget gadget, put a simple platform with a mediocre screen and camera, and then all this puts on a solid “armor”. Some companies that take special care of their customers and their reputation equip devices with high-quality and high-quality batteries, and sometimes add something interesting like a walkie-talkie radio.

Further, the resulting device is sold under some big name - “Jeep”, “Land Rover” and others like it, or offered to foreign distributors, that is, OEM brands who have the right to stick any label on this gadget and pack it in their company box.

Texet

Any sensible OEM-brand, which St. Petersburg "Texet", one must think, is, is struggling with Chinese suppliers not only for the price, but also for the quality of mobile products, which, as a rule, varies greatly among Oriental companies, and in everything - materials, assembly, electronic components, and even in software.

Despite all the factors and nuances, we will consider the teXet smartphone as an independent product that has passed through bureaucratic and very picky Russian certification, which means that it has been burdened with some kind of warranty and service.

But here the charger for the car, alas, is not in the box with the teXet X-driver Quad TM- 4082R. First impressions

For a person who is used to working with ordinary smartphones, the gadget visually looks like “Wow!” In terms of tactile sensations, the device offers a completely different operational experience than a regular smartphone.

The design turned out to be quite heavy and thick - 261 grams with a thickness of 18.3 mm. The case is rubberized with massive sidewalls and fits firmly in the palm of your hand. It seems that in the hands is some kind of serious industrial device like a construction or geodetic tool.

Design teXet X-driver Quad TM-4082R

If you look at modern mobile devices that have captured the entire market, which cannot be called a flat “spatula”, the class of shock-resistant and moisture-proof gadgets remains the only segment where the design idea can at least somehow unfold (just like in the era of button devices). It’s quite another matter that venerable A-brands do not want to take any part in the development of the class, and all the stylistic notes are set by Chinese designers with their specific methods and taste preferences, which could not but affect the teXet X-driver Quad TM-4082R . Owner reviews note the presence of a long-forgotten classic style in the gadget, which also adds to the device of fans of retro style. Screen

As for the characteristics of the screen, here it is a matter of habit and pretentiousness. Naturally, if you had to hold a flagship smartphone with Super Amoled and Retin in the openings earlier, our “hero” has a 800 by 480 pixel screen that looks like a dull dinosaur. By today's standards, in principle, the way it is. But for gadgets of such a plan and characteristics, such screens are the norm, competitors have more modest devices for the same price, so you don’t have to turn your face much.





In defense of such displays, we can say that it loads the system much less, significantly increasing battery life, which is very important for extreme phones far from 220 V. The glass for the Texet smartphone is designed quite competently, so there are no problems with visualization. The only thing worth noting is that the picture is clearly not enough brightness, and in the afternoon and even in the late afternoon, you have to unscrew the indicator by 100%. In direct sunlight, the screen fades (the glass for the smartphone reflects all the rays), and to read the information, the gadget needs to be covered with your hand or searched for a shadow.

Communication

The flagship smartphone “Texet” is equipped with dual-SIM functionality with one radio module, that is, the alternate operation of SIM cards. You can call in standby mode to any of the cards, however, when a subscriber makes a call or transmits any other data using one SIM card, the other is automatically blocked. By their functionality, SIM cards are also divided: the first works in 2G and 3G networks, and the second only with 2G.

There are no critical comments on the quality of communication: the speaker, although not outstanding, but twisted to the maximum, allows you to hear the interlocutor on a noisy street. The only negative, and the teXet X-driver Quad TM-4082R case is probably to blame, the speaker cuts both high and low frequencies, so the voice on that end sounds somehow unnatural. In the reviews of the owners this point has been discussed more than once, but users do not consider this nuance to be a serious problem.

The same thing is observed with the microphone - the interlocutor hears you quite distinctly, but unnaturally and with “dry” notes. The manufacturer sins for sealing, responding that protection against water damps high frequencies. The call signal is quite loud, so you can hear it even from a backpack behind your back. Due to the massiveness of the body, the vibration alert is very weak, which was quite expected.

Work in browsers is comfortable and does not cause serious complaints - the speed of 2G and 3G is similar to competing devices. The owners in their reviews noticed one glitch: if the connection from one card disappears and reappears, then the gadget does not find it, so you have to reboot the device to solve this problem. Apparently, the dust and water tight smartphone does not hold a weak signal as well as we would like.

Camera

On the official website of the company, the specifications of the cameras are listed in the specification: front - 1.3 MP, and the main - 8 MP. Either the manufacturer messed up something, or there are some kind of variable models, but on the main Internet trading platforms, gadgets with slightly different characteristics flaunt just like on the store shelves: the main camera is 5 megapixels (2880x1720 px) and the front camera is 0.3 MP (640x480 px).





St. Petersburg “Alcatelists” do not give any comments, so it is not clear whether individual batches are so equipped, or such indiscreet interpolation was made. In any case, if you should adhere properly, you can get the necessary parts for the teXet X-driver Quad TM-4082R (preferably branded) and replace the cameras with more sensible ones. It is better to do this in the service center, there may also be all the components necessary for this procedure.

By modern standards, the pictures are obtained not so bad, but some faceless: the module is clearly budget and you should not expect anything special from it. In the classic mode (pointed and shot), the result can turn out to be the most unpredictable: sometimes even in poor lighting the pictures are more or less normal, and in perfect light - some kind of blurriness and fading.

Very often sharpness is blurred in the frame and blurred areas are visible almost everywhere. Also, the camera suffers from incorrect gamma calibration (lying colors). In general, there are enough opportunities for Instagram, and to show your friends “how we went camping” will do.

Regarding the front camera, there is nothing special to say - lovers of beautiful selfies will have to relax in the background of scenic spots. Of course, if you “take a breath” of your portrait as a souvenir, you can see the face, but this camera is unfortunately not suitable for anything serious.

Video

The device can record video in a resolution of 1920 by 1088 pixels, more or less acceptable by modern standards (precisely 1088, not 1080), which also indicates the presence of interpolation (stretching the frame programmatically).

The quality of the video is approximately the same as the pictures - the same budget and dull. The disadvantages of the camera are especially noticeable in low light conditions: sharpness drops, detail and fog appears. In thickening twilight or drizzling weather, recording some more or less sensible video will not work.

The gadget can be used as a car DVR by downloading the necessary software from the same “Market Play”, which, without exaggeration, is a dime a dozen. We talked about the picture (everything is bad), but what the Texet dust and water tight smartphone can boast of is the reading range of car numbers. The capabilities of the gadget are slightly superior to similar profile devices that write in HD-resolution, although they do not reach the Full HD-development. The capture angle, of course, is less than that of ordinary navigators, but we still have a smartphone in our hands, and not a full-fledged car gadget.

Offline work

The smartphone is equipped with a very good 3100 mAh battery. In the most active mode, the device will last about 4.5 hours. Web surfing, calls, SMS will put the battery in 6.5 hours. If you use a headset and minimize brightness, then the phone is enough for 12-13 hours of video viewing. You can use the gadget directly as a phone for several days.





There is no point in installing all kinds of “optimizers”, “accelerators” and “battery doctors” so actively advertised by Texet itself. All these applications will hang in the device’s memory and also “eat” the battery.

Protection

The protection class declared by Petersburgers according to the unified European classification is IP68. And this means the ability to work in a submerged state (number 8) and complete isolation from dirt and dust (number 6). There are also its own nuances: the classification does not specify the time or depth of immersion with the device, leaving this moment to the conscience of the manufacturer.

The official resource “Texet” and the detailed manul for the gadget are silent about this, and in the instructions sometimes there are light puns like: “despite the shock resistance, the smartphone case cannot be called shockproof”. On the one hand, this seems to be correct, because ordinary gadgets can also have IP68 protection. The same Sony devices have one, but the manufacturer does not recommend dropping them. The standard that allows you to "drop" the device on any hard surface is MIL-STD-810G (military), but in our case it is not claimed.

Nevertheless, the impact resistance of the gadget, both on the site and in the description, is explicitly emphasized. Specifically, it is said that the case was reinforced with metal plates on the back wall and under the screen, and in addition, the display is additionally equipped with a special Asahi glass, which, according to the text, is “three times thicker than ordinary glasses for smartphones”.

Summarizing

If you look at the cost of the teXet X-driver Quad TM-4082R smartphone (the price is about 10,000 rubles), its main characteristics and other secondary indicators, then in comparison with direct competitors the gadget looks very advantageous and even dumps somewhere. And this despite the fact that a venerable device from some “Sony” or the same “Yabloko” passes over the mark of 60 thousand rubles and differs, as a rule, only in fast filling (well, why, in fact, for an extreme tourist, a game device )

In general, the model leaves a pleasant impression of operation. The smartphone is really tenacious, and, having passed “fire and water”, it continues to work without any glitches and other failures. It seems that if you do not intentionally drown the apparatus at depth and do not kill against stones, then you will live happily ever after. Anyway, buying this kind of gadgets, do not flatter yourself that they can withstand an atomic strike or a global flood. By and large, such a device has a good image function for a good 50%, to some extent harmoniously complementing beautiful outfits.

As a permanent mobile help, “Texet” can be recommended only to those who, by all means, want to be unique among friends and colleagues, lovers of an extreme tourist style and financially limited patriots.
