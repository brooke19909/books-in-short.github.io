Nalchik hotels: description, reviews, addresses

Nalchik - the capital of Kabardino-Balkaria, is located at the foot of the most famous Caucasus Range. This is one of the most beautiful places on the world map. Tourists constantly flock here, because rest here is an unforgettable impression of the virgin nature and beauty of the majestic mountains. Where to stay in the city, which Nalchik hotels are considered the best?

Chalet "Falcon"

One of the most popular hotels in Nalchik is the Sokol tourist base.
This institution is located in a pine forest near the Baksan River, in the village of Elbrus.

You can get to the base by public transport or in your own car. There is a free car park here.



Similar hotels in Nalchik, the addresses of which are indicated in this article, are designed for a large number of guests, however, only 25 visitors will fit here during the winter. Accommodation is offered in 9 standard junior suites and suites.

At the base there is a large trout pond. The hotel area is very large, so it is possible to accommodate groups of tourists with campgrounds. Often in the surrounding area various sporting events and competitions are held. On the lake, of course, there are always many fishing enthusiasts. In winter, a skating rink is poured on the lake.

The average cost of rooms is 450 rubles per day.

Meals are included in the price separately.

Hotel address: Kabardino-Balkaria, Elbrus district, Elbrus village.

Hotel Alpina

Rest in Elbrus region is an unforgettable experience. The resort is located in the Baksan Valley at the foot of Elbrus itself - the highest peak in Russia and Europe.

Just at the foot of the mountain the Alpina Hotel is located. It is located in a non-avalanche zone (there is a ski lift just 150 meters away).

Alpina is a seven-story hotel with an original European interior that immerses guests in a fabulous atmosphere, and the branches of the huge pine trees that are visible directly from the windows of the rooms fill the atmosphere with the grandeur and beauty of Caucasian nature.

Alpine has 46 rooms ranging from economy options to luxury rooms. All rooms are equipped with television, free internet, international and long distance communications.



Showers, sinks, bidets, hair dryers, complimentary toiletries, bathrooms are all located in the bathrooms.

The cost of living is from 800 rubles per day. The price includes breakfast, parking, and laundry services.

Address of the Alpina Hotel: Kabardino-Balkaria, Elbrus District, Azau Village.

In 2008, the Women's World Chess Championship was held at the hotel. The impressions expressed about this event were extremely positive both from the side of the participants and from the reporters who covered this event.

Then, in 2008, the equipment of a new building for medical and recreational procedures was completed. The hotel has been renamed the Spa Hotel. Today it is a hotel with a unique medical and health program, upscale cuisine and an unforgettable favorable atmosphere. The treatment programs carried out by professionals at this hotel help to improve the general condition of guests with cardiovascular diseases, as well as digestive problems, nervous system diseases, gynecological problems, etc.
