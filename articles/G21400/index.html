Turkey, Side: 5 star hotels. Photos, reviews

Side is a fairly common resort among travelers, known for its beautiful landscape, an abundance of historical and cultural memorials of various times. Choosing Side as their vacation destination, travelers get one of the best options for spending time in Turkey, 5-star Side hotels (All Inclusive) are famous for their beauty and comfort. Typically, 4 * or 5 * hotels are built on the seafront, surrounded by beautiful cliffs and orange trees. For vacationers there are resorts in Beldibi, Konakli, Manavgat, as well as in other places near Side. And directly in the city itself there are also excellent hotels. In most cases, the beaches are sandy.

5 star hotels in Side, Turkey (1 line) are ideal places for tourists on the sea and bays (Manavgat hotels are located in the delta of the Manavgat River). Hotel complexes have not only the residential buildings themselves, but also bungalows that are located in the vicinity of beautiful terraces near the seashore. Almost all five-star hotels have discos, as well as services for using a minibar and spa treatments.

An overview of the most popular Side hotels in Turkey (5 stars), a photo of them and a description can be found in this article. Using this information, you can choose a hotel for a unique holiday.
