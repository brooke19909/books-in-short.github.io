Fishing on the river Bird

This river flows from the slopes of the Salair Ridge in the Altai Territory, and is a right tributary of the Ob. They call it the pearl of the Salair Ridge. Throughout the territory of Altai, the length of the river is small, about thirty kilometers. Flowing, meandering between mountain ranges covered with fir-pine forests, aspen-birch groves. Having burst into the plain, the river falls into pine ribbon forests. The Berd River flows through the territory of the Maslyaninsky and Iskitim Districts in the Novosibirsk Region. Bird flows into the Novosibirsk reservoir. Quite steep and steep banks of Birdie, but low. Bird River flows, coils, there are long stretches, rifts. There are terraces and even rocky ledges.

Fishing on Birdie, a beautiful Siberian picturesque river, of course, is unforgettable. The river connects the village Maslyanino, Iskitim and the small town of Berdsk. Near the forest, rocks, wonderful nature. Fishing on the river Bird will be just wonderful. Pike, perch, crucian carp, grayling, bream are found in the river, and large pike perch at the mouth. On Birdie in the Iskitim region, toothy pike are well caught in the reaches.

Pikes in the river are not picky, they are caught on the simplest lures: coulibia, turntables, twisters, vibro-tails. Perch in Birdie is caught on turntables, you can use cicadas, poppers, wobblers. The largest catches of halibut and zander occur by the end of summer. Fish at this time gets into large flocks. Such a fish takes maggot, a worm from the bait. A special bait among local fishermen is considered lupine. It is cooked for a very long time. Boiled grains hold well on the hook. To keep the fish in place for at least half an hour, it is enough to throw about a liter of lupine into the water.

From Iskitim to the confluence with the Ob, the Berdsky Gulf stretched for forty kilometers. It is a resort area. There are many sandy beaches. The water in the Bird River is warm in summer. Here you can sunbathe, swim.

Features of fishing on Birdie

This place is very beautiful. The Berd River in the Novosibirsk Region is popular with fishing enthusiasts. Fishermen both in summer and in winter rest for their favorite pastime. Caught here are burbot, pike perch, ide, roach. Also found are sterlet, whitefish, sturgeon.

The best time for fishing

In Birdie there are many fish thermophilic and cold-loving, so fishing will be successful year-round. Spring in these places begins in April, when the ice has completely melted. Tackle

On the River Bird, fish of different breeds, this allows anglers to try a variety of fishing gear. Suitable for fishing on birdie spinning. It can be fished all year round. You can catch perch, pike, burbot on it.

Tackle with floats, it is desirable to have fishing rods made of bamboo or birch. Nozzles can be worms, corn, bread. If the angler wants to catch predatory fish, then double or triple hooks must be used instead of standard hooks. As bait, you can use live bait. In winter, you can catch mormyshka.

Each fisherman has his own secrets of successful fishing. For example, signs:

  • The first sign of a fisherman - nibble will be more active when early spring and late fall.
  • In spring and autumn, the fish bite well during the day.
  • In summer, on the contrary, during the day the bite is bad, but at dawn it is good, and in the evening it is not very good.
  • When the fish spawns, the bite intensifies.
  • When water decreases, nibble is good.
  • Wind and cold contribute to good nibble, especially for fishing.
  • When water arrives, the bite gets worse.
