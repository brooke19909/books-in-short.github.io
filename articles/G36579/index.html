Hotel "Berega" (Ryazan): description with photos, infrastructure, reviews

If you want to spend a weekend in an atmosphere of comfort and hospitality, pay attention to the Park-Hotel Berega in Ryazan. The three-star complex offers guests a lot of opportunities for a comfortable and vibrant vacation. Natural factors are organically combined with quality service, coupled with the cordiality of the staff.

Location

The institution in question is located in the village of Polyany on Zhemchuchnaya Street 1. Fun for kids

Considering the photo of the hotel "Berega", a bright family vacation comes to mind. Going here with your loved ones, you can be sure that no one will be bored. Here are some activities for the youngest guests:

  • Animated adventures - conducted throughout the entertainment complex, accompanied by animators.

  • Children's club in the restaurant - while adults enjoy delicious dishes and pleasant communication, children can take part in culinary master classes.
