Zavidovo, Radisson Resort: how to get there? Reviews and photos

An ideal place for a relaxing countryside holiday is Zavidovo. Radisson Resort is a luxury resort hotel. Belonging to the world famous hotel chain is a guarantee of quality service.





Why you should choose Zavidovo, Radisson Resort

The quality of rest is determined not only by the place, but also by the hotel that was chosen to stay. So, a great place for a weekend weekend is the village of Zavidovo. Radisson Resort is worth choosing for the following reasons:

  • from the windows of their rooms, guests can enjoy picturesque forest or river landscapes;
  • here you can not only relax, but also improve your health, thanks to the work of a luxurious SPA center;
  • huge opportunities for lovers of outdoor activities and sports;
  • several restaurants will satisfy the needs of even the most demanding gourmets;
  • guests will have access to 900 meters of a well-groomed river beach;
  • many places for walking in the fresh air.

By the way, according to UNESCO, this area is considered one of the most environmentally friendly in the world.





Resort Location

In a quiet picturesque location is the hotel of the village of Zavidovo "Radisson Resort". The address of this resort hotel is Tverskaya Boulevard, 2. The complex is located 120 km from Moscow. The M10 motorway leads to the village, which is about 6 kilometers from the hotel. You can also get to Zavidovo by rail, and then take a taxi to the hotel.

Hotel Restaurants and Bars

The Radisson Resort SPA is very popular among tourists. Zavidovo boasts clean forest air, which, like nothing else, catches up an appetite. You can satisfy your hunger in the following hotel establishments:

  • Restaurant "Beach" is the main establishment of this hotel. Here, guests can enjoy a sumptuous buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner. On the menu you will find both traditional dishes and real masterpieces of international cuisine.
  • "Lake" is a grill restaurant where mainly fish and meat dishes are presented to your attention. A great addition to your meal will be collection wines.
  • The mooring cocktail bar invites you to try original drinks, as well as light salads and other snacks.
  • The Shore nightclub always has a huge selection of spirits and soft drinks. Here you can dance or sing karaoke.
  • The summer cafe is located on an open area with a view of the river. Here you can treat yourself to fresh juices, delicious pastries and a huge number of specialties from the menu.

For thrill-seekers, a winter hunting session will be organized. It will be held on the territory of the Bezborodovsky hunting farm, which is the oldest in the region. Positive reviews

It is popular among tourists Zavidovo, Radisson Resort. Reviews about this hotel contain a lot of positive comments:

  • the hotel is surrounded by incredibly beautiful nature;
  • the opportunity to do water sports on the beach;
  • from the windows of the rooms beautiful forest landscapes open;
  • very beautifully decorated apartments;
  • there are bike paths in the territory;
  • there is a night club.




Negative reviews

Some travelers remain unhappy with their vacation in Zavidovo. Radisson Resort (hotel) was marked with the following negative reviews:

  • restaurants have a fairly small selection of dishes, and there are no other establishments nearby;
  • on the beach there is no shower, toilet and locker rooms;
  • the hotel is too crowded due to conferences or corporate events;
  • lack of sunbeds on the beach;
  • high prices for accommodation and meals;
  • restaurants close very early;
  • apparently, pillows and blankets were not taken for dry cleaning for a long time, since they smell very bad;
  • bathrobes and slippers are not in the room initially, and therefore you have to ask the administrator;
  • single beds are very narrow and uncomfortable.

General impression

Radisson Resort in Zavidovo is a luxury hotel. Nevertheless, on the corporate identity and interior, as well as high prices, it ends. The cleaning is not enough quality, the beach is not landscaped, the restaurants offer a very meager assortment of dishes, and the staff could be more polite with the guests. However, beautiful nature and clean air compensate for many of the shortcomings. In addition, guests are provided with a lot of opportunities for outdoor activities and sports. If you are not afraid of the lack of infrastructure around, then you will have a great time.
