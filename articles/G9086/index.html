Kurganinsk: overnight hotels

The Krasnodar Territory is beautiful due to nature, but people have made additions to its natural charm by rebuilding cities and building transport routes. Now this region is visited by many businessmen and tourists.

Arriving in a small Kurganinsk, which is located on the banks of the Laba River, visitors to the city see well-groomed streets, new roads, friendly residents. The city knows how to welcome guests, and the proof of this is good hotels, although there are not so many of them as in large cities.

The rooms are offered a variety of, including family and for newlyweds superior.

Modern design, thoughtful convenience of each room - these are not all the advantages of a small hotel with 14 rooms. The issue with food has been resolved: there is a cafe, restaurant, banquet hall. If you need to discuss working moments, you can use the conference room, make photocopies or send / receive a fax. And at leisure, go to the sauna or bath.

You can order to wash or iron clothes, use the washing machine.

The working day is round-the-clock, because the doors of the hotel are always open wide.

Small cozy corner
