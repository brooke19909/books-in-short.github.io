Where did the network name come from

The hotel chain received this name not by chance. The thing is that not far from the famous resort center of Turkey lie the ruins of the ancient city of Perge. Their "home" is the north-eastern part of the Aksu region, which is part of the territorial composition of Antalya. According to legend, this ancient city was founded when the Trojan War ended. The heroes Mopsos and Kalkhas arrived in these places from Argos.

In 1953, excavations were carried out at the place where the main Hellenic gate of Perge was located. Then, inscriptions dated 120-121 were found. BC, which indicate the colonization by the Greeks of these places at the end of the distant 2000 BC. Statues were also discovered telling about the glorious heroes who participated in the founding of the city: Mopsos, Kalkhas, Rixos, Labos, Machaon, Leontus and Minyasas. In honor of one of these brave men, Fettah Taminji named his hotel company.

Rixos Beldibi Hotel 5 *

Located in the famous resort-oriented village of Beldibi (Turkey, Kemer), the Rixos Beldibi hotel was erected in 2002, and in 2014 it underwent a major restoration. To get to it from Antalya Airport, you need to overcome only 50 km - this is about an hour away by car or public transport.
