Hotel Dolphin Marina 4 * (Bulgaria): overview, description, rooms and reviews

When traveling lovers have a question about where it is best to spend the upcoming vacation, everyone thinks and starts looking for different options. And one of the best (in terms of combination of price and quality) is Bulgaria. This is a fairly popular country for tourism, and there are many hotels. I would like to talk more about one of them, which is known as Dolphin Marina 4 *.

Briefly about the hotel

This hotel complex is located in the resort of Saints Constantine and Helena. It is not far from Varna - a major port city in Bulgaria. The nearest airport is also located there.

The resort got its interesting name thanks to the eponymous monastery, erected here back in the 18th century. This is a very popular place among tourists, since there is a huge number of mineral springs concentrated here. And here is the purest mountain air and sea in the immediate vicinity with excellent beaches. Everything here is conducive to relaxation and recovery.





A bus stop from the hotel is approximately 300 meters away. You can get anywhere, but there are plenty of attractions in these places. 6 km away is Aladzha Monastery, Varna Zoo, Golden Sands Aquapolis, Kabakum Beach and much more. By the way, the beach is within easy reach - it takes about a minute to go.

Infrastructure

Dolphin Marina 4 * has everything that a guest may need. There is a modern conference room equipped with new equipment, a rental car and, accordingly, private parking. There is also a currency exchange, individual safes for storing valuables and a luggage room. There is also a laundry with dry cleaning, a medical center, various shops and an internet corner. The reception, of course, works around the clock.

It is worth noting that Dolphin Marina 4 * provides transfer services from / to airports. Hotel representatives can pick up guests from Sofia, Varna or Burgas. Rates are different. The most expensive transfer from Sofia airport. A car for 1-3 persons costs 200 euros. A trip for the same number of people from the airports of Burgas and Varna will be 19 and 97 y. e. respectively. Car services are also provided for a larger number of people (even for 49 people you can order a bus). Prices must be specified individually.

Sports and recreation

Already no one is surprised by the fact that each hotel is considered not only as a temporary residence, but also as an entertainment complex. Dolphin Marina 4 * may well refer to those.





On its territory there is an outdoor pool with a children's section. In it, for safety reasons, a minimum depth is made. There are also outdoor pools with sea and hydrogen sulfide water. There are sun loungers and umbrellas right there, and towels are also provided.

Squash games are also held daily (from 10:00 to 18:00). You can also play table tennis, do water aerobics and gymnastics. Sports enthusiasts will especially appreciate the fitness center. And people who like a noisy pastime will definitely love the entertainment show programs that take place here daily.

Restaurants

Dolphin Marina 4 * (Bulgaria) works according to a system that is loved by all tourists. This, of course, is all inclusive. The price of this service includes breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main restaurant of the hotel complex, which serves guests according to the buffet. Three times a week in this institution organize themed dinners.

Grand Hotel Varna offers a nightly buffet. This hotel and Dolphin Marina 4 * (Bulgaria) are part of one large resort complex, so in this case guests receive the double advantage of using the services of both the first and second hotels.





Guests also have the right to attend themed dinners in four restaurants throughout the resort. The only caveat - you need to reserve tables in advance. The same goes for dinners and lunches at an Italian restaurant called Dolce Vita at the Grand Hot Barbeque, as well as at Susam (Asian cuisine) and South Beach (serving Mediterranean delights).

Bars

Hotel Dolphin Marina 4 * also has institutions of a different nature. There is a great bar with hot dogs, for example. And there is also a separate establishment with a huge selection of fruits. The day bar serves sandwiches, crackers, ice cream and sweets throughout the day, as well as all kinds of drinks - from tea and coffee to exotic cocktails and spirits. Also, "all inclusive" applies to bars located near the sea. And there is a restaurant (also a buffet), a bar with burgers Grand Burger, and also South Beach, where you can try a variety of drinks.

It should also be noted that the Dolphin Marina 4 * hotel takes care of all the guests equally. Including here they think about children. For them, each restaurant has high chairs and a separate menu. Babysitting services are also provided. And also there is organized a small water park for the smallest, a mini club and attractions. Everything is free. By the way, the all-inclusive system comes into effect from lunch on the day of arrival, and ends with breakfast - before guests leave the hotel.
