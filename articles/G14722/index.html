Transformer ultrabook - the dilemma of choice is solved

What wonders of technology in the field of computers you will not meet today. Modern people no longer want to buy a simple laptop or netbook. When planning to purchase a small laptop computer, many are lost in the choice of buying an ultrabook or tablet. Stores are full of offers, manufacturers offer many functions with small sizes - how not to get lost! Relatively recently, a wonderful hybrid appeared on the market - an ultrabook transformer.

The ultrabook transformer can also be used as a tablet, while the transformation mechanisms of all manufacturers are different.

Hybrids are mainly based on the operating system Windows 8. This solution is caused by great convenience when working on the touchpad and touch screen. Without a sensor, it is not very convenient to work with this operating system, but manufacturers are trying to provide video navigation with gesture recognition through a webcam built into the tablet lid.





Consider the examples of several models, the main advantages and disadvantages of such gadgets.

Ultrabook-transformer Lenovo IdeaPad Yoga 13 - a model that has become a sales leader and has surprised many users. The ability of the cover to be converted to a tablet by turning 360 degrees allows you to flip the touch screen to the back of the device. Thus, to work in the state of the tablet, the keyboard becomes the basis of the transformer. The manufacturer thought over this nuance: as soon as the screen rotates, the keyboard functions are blocked (by the way, this option is easy to disable).
