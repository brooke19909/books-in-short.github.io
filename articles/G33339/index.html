Hotel 3 * Baan Karon Resort (Thailand / Phuket): reviews, photos

Hotel 3 * Baan Karon Resort offers its guests an unforgettable and comfortable stay on the Thai island.





Brief description of the hotel

The resort complex 3 * Baan Karon Resort is an incredibly cozy and beautiful hotel, which offers guests comfortable conditions for living and recreation. Here you will be met by friendly staff who are ready to fulfill your every whim and always come to your aid. Do not forget about comfortable rooms, where you will be very pleased to relax after a busy day, full of discoveries and vivid impressions.

Resort 3 * Baan Karon Resort provides many services, without which it is impossible to imagine the rest of a modern traveler. Special attention deserves a beautiful clean pool, decorated with decorative fountains, as well as a huge number of sunbeds around it. There is also a pool in the spa, where you can enjoy hydromassage and incense. The hotel also provides free parking and free Wi-Fi access.

One of the main features of Thailand is its cuisine. In the restaurant of the 3 * Baan Karon Resort hotel you can taste both the most exotic and quite traditional dishes for home cooking. European cuisine is also presented. If necessary, the restaurant waiters will deliver your order directly to your room.





Resort Location

Baan Karon Resort 3 * hotel is located no more than 700 meters from the famous and popular Karon beach among tourists. Another quite famous sea coast of Kata is a kilometer from the hotel. You will have to drive about 7 km to the center of Phuket.

Resort Resort Guest Apartments

If you plan to visit Phuket, Baan Karon Resort 3 * hotel will be able to offer you comfortable accommodation conditions. You can choose from several categories of apartments:

  • Standard apartment in the amount of 46 rooms. These are quite compact rooms that are designed for two adult guests. It can accommodate in large or single beds. A foldaway bed is also provided as an extra bed for a child or adult.
  • Superior apartment (9 rooms) is distinguished by its spacious area, as well as refined decoration. The large room through decorative zoning is divided into a bedroom, a living room and a small entrance hall. Folding furniture can be used as an extra bed.
  • Three rooms from the entire number of rooms have family status. They are large in size. These are two adjacent bedrooms, each of which has an entrance to its own bathroom. Each room is equipped with double beds. The possibility of providing cots is also provided.




Room Amenities

We can talk about the high level of comfort in the rooms of the Baan Karon Resort 3 * hotel. Photos of the apartments demonstrate a concise and rather sophisticated design. But the most important thing is a wide range of amenities that guests can use:

  • one of the main points is the availability of an individual air conditioner, which allows you to enjoy your vacation in a comfortable temperature, regardless of the weather outside;
  • if you want your favorite drinks always in your room, then after paying for the corresponding service the minibar will be filled with them;
  • since many tourists worry about the safety of their money and valuables during their absence in the room, electronic safes are installed in all guest rooms;
  • the bathroom has a shower, hairdryer, towels and other bath accessories, as well as disposable cosmetics.




Positive reviews

The Baan Karon Resort 3 * resort is very popular among travelers. Guest reviews give the right to judge that the rest in this hotel is accompanied by many positive aspects:

  • the atmosphere in the hotel is very comfortable;
  • reception staff greet guests politely and warmly;
  • due to the fact that the hotel is not located on the first line relative to the beach, products at retail outlets here are much cheaper;
  • right next to the hotel there is a large parking of tuk-tuk;
  • breakfast in the restaurant, although it repeats every day, but all the dishes are quite tasty and hearty;
  • near the hotel there is a large and relatively inexpensive supermarket;
  • there is fast free wireless Internet, the signal of which is excellent not only near the pool, but also in the rooms;
  • cleaning is carried out every day (while guests at breakfast), the rooms are very clean;
  • the apartments are very good - they have a decent set of furniture, as well as modern working appliances;
  • the pool is very clean and incredibly beautifully designed, and the smell of bleach drowns out the aroma of trees blooming in the garden;
  • the gym is small, but it has all the necessary simulators for full classes.




Negative reviews

Speaking of negative reviews, it is worth noting the following comments of travelers regarding this hotel:

  • near the pool there are not enough sunbeds, which are occupied from the very early morning by enterprising guests;
  • the beach is located far enough from the hotel;
  • there is practically no sidewalk along the road to the sea, and cars rush along the highway at a frantic speed;
  • in the rooms, the windows of which look out onto the street, there is noise and buzz from the road around the clock
  • since no partitions are provided between the balconies of the rooms, sitting there is not very comfortable, and it is also scary to leave the window open for the night;
  • some rooms do not have hot water;
  • in the corridors and hotel rooms you can find not only ants and cockroaches, but even large rats;
  • during cleaning cosmetic accessories are not reported - you have to buy everything yourself;
  • for each guest only one towel is given;
  • the hotel restaurant serves delicious food, but the dishes are very expensive, and the room is incredibly hot and stuffy.

This hotel can be considered one of the best of its kind. Nevertheless, there are some unpleasant moments that would not hurt to eliminate. So, if you solve the problem with insects, the hotel will seem even more comfortable and welcoming.
