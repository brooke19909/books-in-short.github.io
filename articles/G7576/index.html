The best apartments in Tallinn, Estonia: description, reviews and photos

What to choose - a hotel or a rented apartment when you arrive in another city? Living in a separate apartment has several advantages. Firstly, there is more space, secondly, almost everywhere there are equipped kitchens, thirdly, a feeling of home comfort is created. Of course, living in apartments is not suitable for everyone, because everyone has different travel goals and different desires.

Near Town Hall Square

Tallinn City Apartments Old Town is a self-catering accommodation located in the heart of the Old Town. These modern and very spacious apartments are located in a restored historic building. It offers free Wi-Fi, cable TV. Each apartment has a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher. The bathroom has a shower or bath. Central location is a great choice for travelers who are interested in the history of the city.

Various options are available for living: with one, two and three bedrooms. Some apartments have a sauna, and some have a separate entrance from the street. The cost for two guests for one day starts from 90 euros (6500 rubles).

Towels, bed linen, a hairdryer and an iron are included in the rent. The apartment is located at Toom-Kooli 7.

Tourists note the excellent location of the apartments and believe that these apartments in Tallinn are quite inexpensive.

Luxury Residences

OldHouse Apartments - 29 apartments located in the medieval part of Tallinn. OldHouse has three residences: Town Hall Square Residence, Rataskaevu Residence and Vene Residence. Living in apartments is a great alternative to a hotel, because there is more privacy, personal space and independence.

Town Hall Square Residence consists of eight apartments with beautiful views of the Town Hall Square. The residence accommodates a total of 32 people.

Rataskaevu Residence is located a hundred meters from the Town Hall Square and has seven luxury apartments for 22 people. Apartments are equipped with saunas, a jacuzzi, balconies and fireplaces.

Vene Residence presents four standard apartments for 14 people.

OldHouse Apartments are ideally located, close to attractions and shops, and are suitable for both tourists and large families.

Types of apartments offered:

  • Two bedroom apartment with balcony and bath.
  • Deluxe with a sauna.
  • Family Two-Bedroom Apartment.
  • Deluxe with sauna and fireplace.
  • Romantic apartment with a balcony and fireplace.
  • Three bedroom deluxe apartment with sauna or balcony.

The cost of living in OldHouse starts from 130 euros (9400 rubles) per day.

Cheap accommodation

Tallinn City Apartments - Town Hall Square is a complex of apartments a few meters from the Town Hall.

Three-bedroom apartment in Tallinn on Rataskaevu Street is located in a historic building. The apartment can accommodate from 10 to 16 people and is equipped with two toilets, a bathroom with shower, a kitchen and free internet access. There is a 24-hour mini market in the same building.

Five-bedroom apartments are ideal for large groups. The apartment is equipped with a fridge, kettle, toaster, hairdryer and dishwasher. The cost of living starts from 190 euros (13 800 rubles) per night.

Studio apartment on Vienna street is suitable for three guests. This is a clean, comfortable studio apartment with a sauna, fireplace, cable TV. Accommodation in the studio starts from 50 euros (3600 rubles) per night.

Deluxe apartments are located in a quiet old courtyard on Rataskaevu Street, close to all tourist attractions, museums, restaurants and the Town Hall Square.

The apartment can accommodate from 4 to 6 people, it is ideal for those guests who love a modern style, mixed with antiquity. The cost of living is from 80 euros (5800 rubles).

The apartment complex in Tallinn is suitable for hiking enthusiasts. Guests love these apartments for the perfect combination of peace, tranquility and vibrant Old Town life.

"Delta" in Tallinn

  • A flat-screen TV with cable channels.
  • Seating area.
  • Fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher and microwave.
  • Private bathroom comes with free toiletries.
  • For an extra fee, guests can have lunch or dinner at the nearby Meriton Old Town Garden Caf√©.

Delta Apartments is 350 meters from Town Hall Square. Tallinn Train Station is 750 meters from the hotel, Lennart Meri Airport is 5 kilometers away, and the bus station is two kilometers away.

These rooms offer the best value for money that can only be found in Tallinn. The apartment costs from 150 euros (10 900 rubles) per night.

Guests note the cleanliness, comfort, facilities and friendliness of Delta Apartments staff.

Budget saving

Aarde Apartments are 100 meters from Niguliste Concert Hall and 300 meters from Toompea Castle. The apartments have a flat-screen TV, a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher, an oven, a coffee maker, and towels.

There is no private parking in the apartment, public parking is located on the adjacent territory and is available at a price of 20 euros (1400 rubles) per day. According to reviews, the apartments do not have extra beds, and the building has a steep staircase, which can be dangerous for small children.

Accommodation in apartments starts from 90 euros (6500 rubles).

House Rapunzel

Rapunzel Tower Apartment is a unique place with fantastic views of Tallinn Harbor and the Old Town. The apartment is on the fourth floor, which is the top of the tower. As in almost all old houses, the tower has only a staircase (no elevator).

Rapunzel Tower is one of the few apartments in one of the city defense towers of Old Tallinn.

Near the Rapunzel Tower, parking is expensive enough to find cheap parking, you need to drive away from the apartment.

There is a good restaurant next to the apartment, and within 500 meters there are pubs, cafes and clubs in Old Tallinn.

Cost of living: 130 euros (9400 rubles) per day or 750 euros (54,500 rubles) per week, additionally you need to pay 45 euros (3200 rubles) for a one-time cleaning and make a deposit of 350 (25,400 rubles) euros.

The price includes: bed linen, towels, toilet paper, shampoo, coffee and tea.

Guests go to this apartment due to its special exclusivity. After all, you can spend the evening in the tower near the lit fireplace and enjoy the beautiful view from the window only in the Rapunzel tower, the guests say.

Great place to stay

Another great place in Tallinn is Alika Apartments. Allika Apartment are located within 10 minutes from the city center. All rooms are equipped with free Internet access, a computer, a seating area, a small kitchen. The bathroom includes a hairdryer, towels and bathrobes.

Alika is one of the few apartments where you can stay with your pets. Children under 6 years stay free of charge when using free beds. Tourists think this is a great place to stay to explore the Old Town.

The address of Alika Apartments in Tallinn is Allika Street 12. Room rates start at 150 euros (10,900 rubles).

To rent an apartment in Tallinn inexpensively is not a myth. You just need to expand the search range and pay attention to apartments located further away from the Old Town.
