Lavender Hotel Sharjah 4 (UAE / Sharjah): Travel Reviews

Our article will interest those tourists who are planning to visit the UAE in the near future. We want to talk about Lavender Hotel Sharjah 4 *, located in Shariah. A beautiful, cozy hotel that looks like a ship, is perfect not only for business people, but also for families, as it offers a huge range of different entertainment and services for children and adults.

Location

Lavender Hotel Sharjah 4 * is located in the very center of Shariah. Not far from the complex stretches beautiful beaches with fine sand, for which many tourists flock to the OEA. A short walk from the hotel there is a marine aquarium called Sharjah, a twenty-five minute drive from Dubai Airport, and the Shariah Expo exhibition complex is very close.

About the hotel ...

Lavender Hotel Sharjah 4 * was built in 2008 and is a three-story building. The hotel is located at: Shariah, Al Khan Beach Road. Getting to it is quite easy, the public transport stop is only a hundred meters from the complex.

Catering

Lavender Hotel Sharjah 4 * (United Arab Emirates, Sharjah) provides its guests with a very diverse and delicious meal. Here you can taste the best international dishes. The hotel has a restaurant and banquet facilities.

Which food option to choose, tourists decide: two or three meals a day. The food at the hotel is very tasty and satisfying. Lavender Hotel Sharjah 4 * (photos are given in the article) is designed to receive not only business people and youth, but also couples with children. Beach

As for the beach of Lavender Hotel Sharjah 4 *, reviews of tourists confirm its absence at the complex. More precisely, the hotel does not have its own beach, but there is a public beach nearby. You can always go for it, but at the same time you need to be prepared for a large crowd of people, including locals. There are no umbrellas and sunbeds on the beach. But this problem can be solved, since there are beaches of other hotels nearby on the coast. Among them there are paid and free, in principle, you can choose an acceptable option.

They say a lot about holidays in Lavender Hotel Sharjah 4 * reviews of tourists. It always starts from the sea, which causes a lot of positive emotions. It is always very warm, some even emphasize that it is too warm. This is not surprising. In the hot season, under the scorching rays of the sun, it simply can not be otherwise. Therefore, in the Emirates it is good to relax with young children, for whom the water temperature is the most important indicator. And not all adults love swimming in cool water. So, everyone who loves the warm sea and the beach should definitely visit the UAE.



The hotel has a very good principle of daily replacement of beach towels. In many complexes, problems arise with this. Lavender Hotel Sharijah 4 * (Sharjah) takes care of its guests and offers them daily clean white towels, which is very important, because during the day they get dirty on the beach.

In addition to travel, vacationers can use the services of the fitness center, visit the sauna or enjoy a massage. Darts and table tennis are available on site.



The hotel is in a good location, it is very easy to get to the center from it, which allows tourists to see Dubai on their own, you can walk on it endlessly. The hotel has a service such as delivery twice a day to one of the malls. If you want to go to another place, then you can use the buses, metro. As for shopping, tourists are not advised to rely on the acquisition of cheap goods. As a rule, prices in malls are higher than Moscow. Of more interest is the infrastructure of entertainment centers. But be prepared to pay for everything.
