Hotel Barcelo Punta Cana (Punta Cana / Dominican Republic)

Description: 32 km from Punta Cana Airport on the Caribbean coast built the majestic tourist complex Barcelo Punta Cana. These are snow-white buildings with bright red roofs surrounded by a tropical park with landscaping. Recently reconstructed hotel (in 2008) is included in the chain of the best Barcelo complexes. Holidays in this paradise of the Dominican Republic will turn into a fairy tale, thanks to the rich infrastructure, an abundance of entertainment and the gentle warm sea.



Barcelo Punta Cana Resort is ideal for fans of surfing, and divers will be delighted with the colorful and diverse underwater world. Families with children, newlyweds, lovers of excursion and beach holidays will also like it here. Businessmen who come to work will be able to do business and conduct various kinds of presentations in a pleasant and relaxed atmosphere.

Rooms: Hotel Barcelo Punta Cana 4 consists of 798 rooms with views of the flowering garden or the sea. The rooms are divided into categories: Double, Double Ocean View, Superior, Superior Ocean View, Suite / Club Premium (two rooms, a dining room and a jacuzzi), two Presidential Suites / Club Premium. On the day of arrival, the bride and groom are given symbolic gifts and flowers.



All rooms are decorated in bright rich colors and equipped with modern furniture and appliances. The rooms have balconies, a private bathroom, air conditioning, a direct dial telephone, a bar with free water and juices. In addition, plasma TVs that broadcast satellite channels are attached to the walls.

Guests can use a CD player, hairdryer, safe, tea / coffee making facilities, ironing facilities. Cleaning is carried out daily, bed changes - according to the schedule. Cots are available upon request.

Meals: Accommodation at the Barcelo Punta Cana hotel in the "All-Inclusive" type. In addition to three meals a day, light snacks, low-alcohol drinks and soft drinks are offered for free in all cafe-bars of the hotel. Guests can dine at the buffet or in restaurants with a reservation. Dishes at the restaurant serving French and Mexican cuisines, as well as grilled meat, fish and vegetables.



It offers as many as six restaurants with Mediterranean, international, Mexican, Japanese, Chinese and Italian cuisines. Some establishments require a "Dress-Code" and reservation. Also on the green territory there are five bars open.

Beach: Literally 100 meters from the Punta Cana Dominican Hotel , there is a coastline with white sand and all the beach amenities. For outdoor activities water sports are provided: windsurfing, catamarans, skiing, diving.



Additional information: Among the spreading palm trees and tropical plants, several outdoor pools are built, one of which is intended for children. Little fidgets will be able to engage in educational games in the mini-club or frolic on the court. Also, funny contests and dance programs are organized for the children, where each child can prove himself.

Adult tourists can not only sunbathe on the beach, but also improve their health by visiting the SPA center. Highly qualified employees with certificates work for you. So, for example, among the abundance of procedures, you will find massage, cosmetic rooms. Thalassotherapy, yoga, meditation and mud therapy services are provided. In the same center there is a steam room, sauna and fitness room.



The hotel building has a casino, a disco, a rental car, a chain of stores, a left-luggage office, and parking. For fans of sports: rock climbing, roller skating rink, tennis courts, billiards, squash, golf, volleyball.

Digest: The Barcelo Punta Cana resort complex includes not only white beaches, gentle sun and excellent food, but also immaculate rooms and high service. If you really want your holidays to be full of bright colors and positive emotions, then do not hesitate to go here - this is the best option for a family, romantic and business cruise.

Thousands of tourists from all over the world annually relax in this hotel, enjoy the unique nature, explore outstanding sights and take with them a storm of impressions, a mountain of souvenirs and a sunny mood. Indeed, this guesthouse is a place where dreams come true. The complex was awarded the best praise and ratings, as evidenced by numerous reviews of tourists.
