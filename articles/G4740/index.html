Hotel "Anacopia Club", Abkhazia: photos and reviews of tourists

New Athos. The city is close to the Russian border, on the Black Sea coast. This resort is popular with tourists, because here you can spend your vacation for little money, but with service by European standards. The city offers thermal sulphide springs and mud baths. Such services are also provided by some hotels in Abkhazia. Anacopia Club is one of them. Its closed territory allows you not to worry about security, but to relax with the whole family or a noisy company. The complex itself is surrounded by beautiful tropical, exotic, picturesque plantations, palm trees and eucalyptus trees. It is an ideal place for a comfortable stay. If you have not yet decided where to go on vacation, Abkhazia welcomes you.

Hotel Anacopia Club. Description




This complex is located on the Black Sea coast, in the beautiful and wonderful city of New Athos (Abkhazia), near the Russian Federation. Ancient history attracts tourists and travelers. The new hotel building consists of three floors. You can get to the hotel without difficulty. There is a direct connection to Abkhazia from Moscow, and after arrival, tourists need to get off at the New Athos station. There is also a hotel. But if there is no way to go this way, then you can arrive in Adler and there will be a transfer. There will be a transfer at the train station, which will comfortably and quickly get to the hotel.

Room Categories



The number of rooms of the Anakopia Club Hotel (Abkhazia) includes 30 rooms, different in their comfort and equipment:

- Comfortable rooms for two are located in the building. They are equipped with one double or two single beds.

- There are also triple rooms equipped with three single beds.

- Families can stay in a triple apartment with a living room and bedroom. These rooms consist of a hall with a double bed and another room with a single bed.

- A room for a family of 4 consists of a bedroom with a double bed and another room with two single beds.

- Apartments located in a detached cottage with two rooms. They are equipped with a double bed in a common room.

- Room for 2 people PC SV also occupies a separate cottage.

- A room for 3 people is located in a two-story cottage (total of 4 rooms). There are also apartments for three PCs.

- Rooms for one or two people studios occupy a separate cottage.

- Single Studio SV rooms are located in a separate two-story cottage (total of 2 rooms).

- Economy rooms with the ability to accommodate 3 people occupy a three-story building.

The apartments have everything for a comfortable stay and a carefree stay at the Anakopia Club 3 * hotel (Abkhazia). There is a refrigerator, air conditioning, a bathroom with a shower and a toilet, a TV with satellite TV, for those who wish - wireless Internet and a telephone. A hairdryer can be requested from hotel staff. Extra bed is not provided in the rooms. To accommodate children under 14 years old, they offer a cot in the European class.

Room service: the rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen is changed at will or on a schedule 2 times a week.

Food for vacationers



The hotel complex serves a restaurant and a local summer cafe located on the territory of the hotel. On the menu, cooks offer hot soups and meat and fish dishes, snacks with fresh herbs, fruits and vegetables, and, of course, desserts and sweets. Tea, coffee and compote in unlimited quantities. But this is only breakfast, lunch and dinner - at an additional cost.

Upon request, guests can purchase home-made food products: hard cheese and home-made preparations, spirits, wine and chacha, also popular Abkhaz honey and local fruits.

Guest Comments

You can safely go here with the whole family. Not only children will be satisfied, but also the elderly. Sunny Abkhazia always welcomes its guests. "Anacopia Club" - a hotel by the sea - one of the best places to stay in the resort. The proximity of the coast is a huge advantage. Just strolling through the hotel, drowning in thickets of eucalyptus, breathing in the aroma of the sea breeze, you can improve your nervous system in just a few days.

About beach vacation

The beach is equipped with free sunbeds that can be moved wherever you need. It is even possible to install them in wooden arbors located on the waterfront. Pebbles on the beach are warming up quickly. A trip to the sea is equivalent to a full foot massage. Those who are embarrassed can purchase special shoes.

The protected area is fenced. It is noticeable that staff with special awe cares about green spaces and cleanliness.

The security system is top notch. You will be watched not only on the territory, but also on the floors. Therefore, do not worry about your valuables or children.

Everything necessary for this is available. Of course, children's leisure will not be complete without swings and trampolines. Fortunately, all of this is here.

Adults can play various active games (volleyball or soccer). On neatly paved paths, children can ride bicycles or scooters. Adults can also use bicycles.

The hotel staff is always ready to help in solving various issues. Very friendly people.
