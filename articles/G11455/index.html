Temporary telephone. Free virtual phone number for receiving SMS

Those who live an active life on the network notice that sites with different themes and purposes often request a phone number for registration, newsletters, sending order confirmation, etc. But what if there is no mobile at hand, there are communication problems, do you want have a second account, or just do not want the site to bother you with periodic SMS mailings? There is a way out - and this is a temporary phone number. About what features and advantages it has, as well as where you can get it, we will tell in this material.

What is a temporary phone and how is it useful

A temporary or virtual phone number is a service for receiving incoming SMS messages (and sometimes voice calls) to a "fake" phone number, from where this information is forwarded to real recipient numbers. For non-commercial purposes, a considerable range of resources provides a temporary telephone for free, but if you get it for business, you will have to pay for the service.

What are virtual numbers used for? Consider the most common cases:

  • registration of the second profile in social networks;
  • confirmation of registration on some thematic sites;
  • indication of the phone number for the promotion (so that in the future the addressee does not bother with annoying mailings);
  • when registering a mailbox;
  • to receive instant confirmation when ordering in online markets;
  • to improve anonymity, eliminate the possibility of surveillance of Internet scammers.

But be careful - a service that provides a temporary phone number for receiving SMS should also not be trusted with personal data. If the code, login or password should come to you in the message, then the message should not contain information about the sending site. Otherwise, after registration, you need to urgently change the "login-password" pair.

Along with a temporary telephone for SMS, a number of services provide one-time mailboxes. By sending an email in this way, you are guaranteed to hide your IP, location and other information that can be used against you from the recipient.

This concludes the introductory part and proceeds to the practical one - consider a number of reliable services where you can get a temporary phone for free.
