Holiday Island Resort Spa (Maldives / Ari Atoll): tourists photos and reviews

Holiday Island Resort & Spa is a true paradise in the Maldives. For more than twenty years in a row, many people have come here to relax and unwind in a secluded relaxing environment.





Briefly about the hotel

Holiday Island Resort & Spa is located on an atoll called Ari. It is located about a hundred kilometers from Male International Airport. It should be noted that Ari is one of the largest atolls of the archipelago, which stretches for 33 kilometers from west to east and 96 from north to south.

To be more precise, the hotel is located on a small island called Diffushi. This is the southern part of Ari. Very cozy little island. Its dimensions are 700 meters long and 140 meters wide. The beach here is within easy reach. But the airport is far, but it is fearless. Indeed, from there to the island can be reached in 35 minutes by seaplane. Or spend 2.5 hours going to the hotel on a boat.

Holiday Island Resort & Spa is popular for many reasons. Largely precisely because the hotel is a complex of small comfortable bungalows, which have everything you need for a secluded holiday. However, there is still much more.

Service

Holiday Island Resort & Spa provides a variety of services. Except the internet. He is here, but in public areas. And besides it is expensive - $ 1 per minute. However, few people need the Internet here.

A paid transfer from the airport and back is provided, there is a photocopy and fax machine, a small gift shop at the hotel, and a currency exchange. There is a laundry service, and guests are also offered room service. The hotel also has its own doctor and can be called, but at an additional cost.

In the open air, by the way, there is a barbecue and terrace.





Entertainment

Staying at Holiday Island Resort & Spa 4 * (Maldives), you can not do water sports. Moreover, there are all the necessary features for this. You can rent a catamaran or water ski, ride a "banana", canoe, jet ski and scooter. It is definitely worth taking a mask with a snorkel for snorkeling. In the Indian Ocean, tens of hundreds of different types of marine creatures live - dolphins, crabs, turtles, crayfish, fish, and all are very beautiful and vibrant.

Here you can also do parasailing - parachute flying, the cable of which is fixed on a boat racing along the ocean surface. Water snowboarding (wakeboarding) is also possible here, as well as surfing.

And of course, such entertainment as billiards, tennis, volleyball, golf and basketball are available here. If you wish, guests can go to the fitness center. And in the evenings, colorful Maldivian shows are held here. So you will not have to be bored while staying at Holiday Island Resort & Spa 4 *. Everyone will find something to their liking.





SPA center

The name of the hotel clearly hints that it has its own spa complex. And there is. In the SPA-center of the hotel you can enjoy not only a sauna, a bath and a hammam. Here they make sand massage with coconut oil, bathing in flower baths, body wraps, scrubs, cosmetic procedures for face and body care. You can also take time for aroma and hydrotherapy. The so-called Ayurvedic procedures are also carried out. For example, scalp massage with hot oil. Or stone therapy. Which is commonly known as hot stone massage.

Clients of the SPA-salon of the hotel can be any guest over 12 years old, but all procedures, without exception, are available only to adults. And children under 12 years old are not allowed in the SPA center without adult supervision.

By the way, the salon also has separate treatment rooms for couples in love, where they can enjoy massages and other services together.

Bungalow

As mentioned earlier, the hotel consists of numerous villas. They are small - 26 square meters. m, but very comfortable. Inside there is one large double bed. Although there is a bungalow with two separate, standard. All villas are located along the beach, so that all windows offer amazing views of the azure Indian Ocean.

Each bungalow has a patio. The rooms include satellite TV, a safe, powerful air conditioning, a separate seating area, and a spacious bathroom. It has a shower, free toiletries and a toilet. There is also a bathtub.

And of course, the rooms have a refrigerator, minibar, hairdryer and fan. In general, everything you need for life in these cosily decorated modern bungalows is available.
