LCD "Green Park", Krasnodar: address, layouts, developer, completion date

When buying apartments, more and more Russians pay attention to real estate in new buildings. There is a logical explanation for this. With a small difference in price, the buyer gets a lot of advantages. One of them is new engineering networks, walls and floors. This eliminates the need for repairs in the coming decades. In addition, it is in new buildings that you can choose apartments with an improved layout and well-designed internal infrastructure.

Among the new buildings in Krasnodar, one cannot ignore such a large project as the Green Park residential complex (Green Park). It is notable for its convenient transport interchange, the level of housing comfort and quite reasonable prices. Important is the fact that many apartments in this complex have already been purchased, which means that there is a demand for them.

general information

The residential complex Green Park in Krasnodar is located in the Karasun district, where the active construction of residential complexes is underway. The address of the new building: Krasnodar, Dmitry Blagoev street, 29 (lit. 1-3).



According to the approved project, Green Park consists of 3 houses, each of which has 18 floors. The total number of apartments is 900. The developer is the company Capital-Invest (CapitalStroy). According to many experts in the field of real estate, the developer managed to combine the aesthetics of the houses, the availability of housing and the most convenient location in the project.

The deadline for commissioning Green Park residential complex is scheduled for the 3rd quarter of 2017.

Construction Features

Design and construction work was carried out taking into account all the necessary norms and requirements, which guarantees high quality construction site. The materials used during the work are the products of well-known companies. At the same time, the project has some distinctive characteristics. They are very important to consider, since they directly affect the operational quality of housing.



  • Application in the construction of monolithic frame technology.
  • The walls of the houses are made of bricks.
  • Exterior decoration of houses involves the installation of a ventilated facade with porcelain stoneware. For this, an aluminum subsystem is mounted. Such a decision is today considered one of the most rational. The ventilated facade gives the building an attractive appearance and, thanks to the aeration of the panels, protects the walls from moisture. The walls of such a house will last much longer, and mold will not appear on them either.
  • Ceiling height reaches 2.8 meters. This can be considered as an advantage, because even today many developers offer low ceilings (2.5 m).

The internal infrastructure of the residential complex "Green Park"

The internal infrastructure of the residential complex was carefully thought out at the planning stage. The first and second floors of the houses will be occupied by offices of various service companies. Among them are provided:

  • grocery store;
  • pharmacy;
  • beauty saloon;
  • Kindergarten.

There is a concierge room on the ground floor.

The territory of the residential complex is equipped with underground parking with a sufficient number of parking spaces for residents - only 383 places. There is also a guest parking area.

Yards of houses are a convenient landscaped area with green spaces, benches for relaxation, sports and playgrounds. In other words, the territory is equipped to the smallest detail.

Apartment renovation

The developer of the LCD "Green Park" offers the most convenient and profitable option - pre-finishing. It includes the following works:

  • high-quality double-glazed windows are installed on the windows;
  • loggias are glazed;
  • fully wired;
  • communication systems laid (central water supply, sewage);
  • installed meters for heat supply, water and electricity;
  • floors and walls are aligned;
  • durable high-quality entrance doors are installed.

The pre-finishing allows to reduce the repair time (compared with the rough finish) and allows new owners to move in as quickly as possible. At the same time, there is a significant saving in price compared to fine finishing.

The cost of apartments

Speaking about the cost of apartments in the Green Park residential complex in Krasnodar, it should be noted that it belongs to the middle segment and is similar to prices in some other new buildings in the city. On the other hand, for apartments belonging to the comfort class, they can be called quite attractive from a financial point of view.



One-room apartment in the LCD "Green Park" in Krasnodar (at the address: Dmitry Blagoev street, 29) can be purchased at a price of 1 840 000 to 1 990 000 rubles. In the calculations, it turns out that the cost of 1 square. m. of housing is approximately 44 500-45 000 rubles.

The price of one-bedroom apartments varies from 2,930,000 to 3,172,000 rubles, depending on size. A square meter of housing in this case has about the same price - 44,500 rubles.

Payment Methods

When buying an apartment in the Green Park residential complex in Krasnodar, you can choose the most convenient payment method. The developer company offers several options:

  • Making a mortgage. Buyers can arrange housing in a mortgage at a rate of 8.49%. The project is accredited by Sberbank.
  • Installment plan. The main requirement for such a purchase is an initial contribution of 50% of the price of the apartment. The remaining amount can be paid within 5-20 years.
  • Military mortgage. Citizens who can take advantage of a military mortgage receive more loyal conditions.
  • Maternal capital. An initial mortgage payment can be made on the basis of a certificate.

In other words, the needs of almost all categories of buyers were taken into account, which positively characterizes both the developer company and the project itself.
