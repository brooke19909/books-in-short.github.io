Myshkin hotels: names, addresses, reviews

The city of Myshkin (Yaroslavl region) has about six thousand people. Its area is only five square kilometers. This picturesque town attracts at least one hundred thousand visitors a year due to its magnificent nature and many attractions. In this article we will consider accommodation options for guests of such a wonderful city as Myshkin: hotels, accommodation prices and guest reviews, as well as many other information will be presented in the article.

"Cat house"

City hotel with 29 rooms, located near the central square and the Assumption Cathedral. Yes, and it can be safely called a landmark of the city: thanks to its design, it looks like a real Russian hut. The interior successfully combines the old Russian spirit and modern comfort. In addition, each room has its own color scheme.

The guest house is equipped with internet access. The hotel is equally suitable for those who want to relax in silence, as well as a large company. The house has a bedroom, a relaxation room and a bathhouse.

A breakfast buffet awaits guests in the morning. The hotel has a traditional Russian restaurant "Mousetrap", decorated with stained-glass windows and a stone fireplace. The restaurant employees are dressed in national costumes, kitchen utensils are styled in antique style. In one of the halls there is a genuine Russian stove.

Among the indisputable advantages of this hotel, guests noted the responsiveness, hospitality of the staff and good cuisine. Many visitors believe that this hotel is a real gift for children. Among the few shortcomings you can answer is not very good sound insulation and lack of entertainment.

Price level: from 2050 rub.

Address: Myshkin city (Yaroslavl region), K. Liebknecht street, house number 27.

"Myshkino Compound", hotel (Yuhot)

This country hotel, surrounded by a picturesque forest, offers all the conditions for an ideal holiday with children or a large company. In its architectural solution, elements of Russian wooden architecture are clearly traced. Guests are accommodated in cottages, and each room has its own entrance and terrace. Room heating can be adjusted as desired.

Visitors praised the beauty of nature and opportunities for outdoor activities. The disadvantages of the hotel include the high price and poor soundproofing in the rooms.



Price level: from 2550 rub.

Address: Myshkinsky district, Yukhot village.

You can get to the hotel along the Yaroslavl highway. You need to turn left, to Sergiev Posad. After 400 meters, after the McDonalds restaurant, turn left again - to Kalyazin. For two kilometers to Kalyazin turn right - to Uglich. Further straight, to the Central Square, then again to the right. Follow the main road to the sign "Hotel" Myshkino Podvorye ".

"Summit"

The Summit Hotel (Myshkin) is a two-story building with a basement, which is located literally on the banks of the Volga. The rooms offer scenic views. The summer cafe is decorated with an old oak tree, with which, according to legend, the emergence of the city itself is associated. Most of all, the complex is suitable for families.



According to guest reviews, the biggest advantages of this hotel are its location and cleanliness. Of the additional services, the pool is the most popular.

Price level: from 900 rubles.

Address: Uglichskaya street, house number 7.

Pension "Forest Fairy Tale"

This sanatorium is a two-story building in the middle of a magnificent pine forest. This guesthouse, like other Myshkin hotels, is open all year and offers all the conditions for an ideal family holiday with children. It offers guests a sauna, pool, gym with exercise equipment. Here you can relax wonderfully in nature and enjoy wellness treatments.



According to vacationers' reviews, clean air and friendly service are the undoubted advantages of a boarding house. Of the minuses - modest food in the restaurant at the sanatorium.

Price level: from 1750 rub.

Address: K. Liebknecht street, house number 149.

Guest house "Volga"

The neat one-story hotel building is located near a pine forest, on the outskirts of Myshkin. The picturesque area is fenced and equipped with gazebos and barbecue facilities. The landscape is complemented by a flower garden and a small pool-fountain. Having overcome the distance of only three hundred meters, you can reach the natural beach.

The hotel has 14 rooms. Guests have access to a kitchenette (in the presence of a microwave, kettle and stove) on the ground floor.

Hotel guests praise pleasant in communication, helpful staff, room cleanliness and value for money. An indisputable advantage is the proximity to the Volga. Among the shortcomings note too simple furnishings in the rooms.

Price level: 1000-1800 rub.

Address: K. Liebknecht (Rybinskaya) street, house number 147.

You can get there by train to the Volga railway station, then by bus to Myshkin.

Comfort, guest house (Myshkin)

The small two-story Comfort Hotel with 9 rooms, opened in 2010, is located on the outskirts of the city. A kitchenette is available for guests.

Guests note comfortable and clean rooms. Some visitors did not like the appearance of the building.



Price level: 1000-2000 rub.

The Comfort Hotel is located at Uglichskaya Street, house number 47.

You can get there by train to the Volga station, then on the route taxi to Myshkin. By own car - from Rybinsk and Yaroslavl - by ferry in the summer, via Uglich - in the winter.

Guest House "On the Forest"

This is a truly quiet forest area off the coast of the Volga. The maximum number of guests is only 40 people. Facilities include a home kitchen with a fridge and kitchenware.

According to reviews, the hotel has very friendly staff and a great sauna. Of the minuses - the lack of air conditioning and access to the Internet.

Price level: from 600 rubles.

Address: Lesnaya street, house number 56.

Guest House "Creative Cottage"

The complex consists of two hotel houses, in the interior of which elements of the historical philistine Myshkin are traced. Guests are offered a bathhouse, a place for barbecue, an apple orchard. The hotel is recommended for recreation and recreation with children!

Most vacationers noted the hotel's good location, warm and homely atmosphere.

Hotel address: Mira street, house number 2-4.

Hotel Okhotino

The hotel is located six kilometers from Myshkin, in a pine forest. The hotel consists of 25 summer houses. It offers guests a park area, natural beach, sports ground, laundry and cafe.



According to visitors' reviews, the houses are quite old, they have not been repaired for a long time.

Address: Myshkinsky district, Okhotino settlement.

First you need to get to Rybinsk by train, then by Rybinsk-Myshkin bus to Okhotino.

The recreation center "Forest Island"

This is a wonderful base in a conservation area. Designed for holidays of both families and individual travelers. The interior of the cottages combines naturalness and modern comfort. Here you will be presented with excellent conditions for hunting and fishing. You will find a parking place at each cottage.

Many visitors note the magnificent air and a wonderful bath in the base. Of the minuses, we can only note that there are very few dishes, and cleaning in the cottages is extremely rare.

Price level: from 500 rubles.

Address: Myshkinsky district, village of Yeremeytsevo.

Of course, Myshkin's hotels are not limited to this list, but it will help you find your way and choose the best option for everyone who wants to stay in this beautiful city.
