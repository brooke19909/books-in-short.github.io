Hotel Belek Soho Beach Hotel 5 * (Hotel Belek Soho Beach Hotel), Belek, Turkey. Booking, Prices, Photos.

Description: Belek is one of the cleanest resorts in Turkey. No wonder the Belek coast received a special award - the Blue Flag, which is issued for observing and maintaining the environment. It is here that the comfortable Belek Soho Beach Hotel is located. The hotel itself consists of several two-story buildings, surrounded by pine trees and exotic palm trees. The hotel has always paid great attention to the comfort of each tourist. The last large-scale reconstruction in 2013 was aimed at the modernization of rooms, additional landscaping, as well as the re-equipment of the water park, playground and the construction of an amphitheater.





The airport is 45 minutes away by bus (30 km), and the nearest cities Antalya and Side are 40 km away.

Rooms: Belek Soho Beach Hotel offers 348 comfortable rooms ranging in size from 22 to 50 square meters with balconies or terraces. Accommodation is mainly double or triple.

The rooms have a bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer, a TV with cable channels in Russian, Internet, a minibar, a telephone, air conditioning, and a safe. Extra beds are available for younger travelers.





Beach: Private, sandy with free umbrellas, sunbeds, mattresses and towels. Belek Soho Beach Hotel is 600 meters long.

Meals: According to the world-famous All Inclusive system, where breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as additional snacks are provided for free. In addition, all locally made drinks, including alcohol, are free.

Information for holidaymakers: Room service is provided daily, and bed linen is changed every three days. Room service is provided. There is an Internet cafe and a laundry service, a currency exchange office, small shops, spa treatments, a sauna, massage, and the famous Turkish bath.





Belek Soho Beach Hotel's main restaurant has a large selection of varied hot and cold snacks, soups, fruits and desserts. There is a children's menu and a menu for diet food. The preparation of many dishes takes place directly in front of the guests - this is a real culinary show. For those who like to sleep longer, a late breakfast is provided, and for departures a lunch package is provided. Belek Soho Beach Hotel 5 hv has several bars where you can enjoy a drink or a snack while waiting for dinner.

  • Pool bar
  • Lobbi bar
  • Beach bar

The poolside bar serves fresh pastries in the afternoons, and fresh fruit is offered at the beach bar.

There are two restaurants ala-map Sultan ottoman and Marine. A table in them can be booked in advance at the reception.

All staff are friendly and professional. Many speak Russian.

Belek Soho Beach Hotel has two outdoor pools with a variety of slides and animations. Of the sports, classes are held in water aerobics and aerobics. You can play soccer or table tennis.

There is a children's pool and playground, a mini club, children's animation. Babysitting services are available at an additional cost.

Reviews: All tourists note a high level of service. Some note the perfect match for price and quality, as well as a clean well-groomed area. Others like the opportunity to relax with their children. Gourmet dishes and barbecue at the Belek Soho Beach Hotel restaurant were especially appreciated by many. Reviews of vacationers are positive. Often this hotel is chosen for a vacation not for the first time.
