Hotel Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Spa 5 * (Dominican Republic): photos and reviews of tourists

Dominican Republic is a fabulous country that amazes the traveler with incredibly beautiful nature and interesting culture. And many tourists, planning to spend some time here, choose a luxurious hotel complex called Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Spa 5 * as a stopping place. Of course, before the trip, people try to collect as much information as possible about this hotel.

Description of the territory and location





Naturally, first of all, tourists are interested in questions about where exactly the Sirenis Hotel is located. This beautiful hotel complex is located on the west coast of the Dominican Republic, just 400 meters from the beautiful beach, in a quiet tourist area of ​​Punta Cana. The international airport is about 40 km away. About 45 km away is the famous tourist town of Bavaro. By the way, there is a public transport stop near the hotel, so you can easily move around.

Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Spa 5 * hotel is a very large complex, which consists of more than a dozen two- and three-story bungalows. The territory itself resembles a real tropical garden. By the way, the hotel was opened in 2002, and the last thorough reconstruction was carried out in 2010.

Dominican Republic, Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Spa 5: photo of rooms and their description





As already mentioned, this hotel is very large - on its territory there are 810 rooms of different sizes, categories and capacities. And since the rooms are located in small comfortable bungalows, residents can enjoy peace and quiet.

Here you can count on large, spacious rooms with a private balcony or terrace. Naturally, you will find the necessary furniture, including comfortable beds, sofas and even desks. There is a full range of household appliances, including a large-screen TV, fan, air conditioning, telephone, safe (for an additional fee), a small refrigerator, a kettle and a set for making hot drinks.

The spacious bathroom will also delight you, where you can lie in the hot tub or quickly refresh yourself in the shower. By the way, some rooms have a private jacuzzi. Guests are provided with clean towels (change every day), a set of shampoos, soaps and other cosmetics. By the way, cleaning is carried out every day, and linen is changed 2-3 times a week.

Meals for guests





What kind of food does the hotel complex Sirenis offer guests? Dominican Republic is a wonderful country, which is famous for its hospitality, generosity and excellent cuisine. Most travelers choose an all-inclusive meal plan. Reviews confirm that guests here are fed with tasty, fresh and, most importantly, healthy food.

In addition, the hotel has several a la carte restaurants, including Italian, Oriental, Mediterranean and some other establishments. Each guest can visit each of the restaurants free of charge twice a week, but subject to prior reservation. By the way, there is even a special vegetarian restaurant that operates at night, as well as seven cozy bars where you can delight yourself with exotic cocktails and local drinks.

Where is the beach located? Water fun for travelers

The hotel complex Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Spa 5 * is located just 600 m from the picturesque beach. On the seashore, you can count on comfortable sun loungers, parasols, and clean towels. The entrance to the sea is convenient, the tides are practically not felt. The underwater world is rich here, so tourists like to snorkel and go diving. There is also the opportunity to do different types of water sports.

Vacation with children: are there any conditions?





For parents who are going on a trip with a child, the issue of the availability of certain amenities becomes important. Indeed, the Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Spa 5 * hotel can offer young tourists a quality vacation. Naturally, parents can count on such amenities as a cot for their child, high chair and even a children's menu. In turn, children can have fun on the playground with swings, swim in a special shallow pool or have fun in the mini club.

Additional services and activities at the hotel





The hotel complex Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Spa 5 * offers its guests a full range of services. Your stay here will not be overshadowed by domestic problems. For example, there is open parking, and in the city it is very easy to rent vehicles at a reasonable price. In addition, guests can use the local dry cleaning and laundry services. There is an ATM, as well as a point where you can exchange currency at a good rate. There is also a fairly well-equipped business center where you can contact a post office employee, use computer equipment and fax. There is a spacious conference room where they organize business meetings, seminars, as well as various holidays. By the way, the hotel employees are organizing weddings, because, you see, a wedding in the Dominican Republic, on the seashore, will become an unforgettable event.

In terms of entertainment, the hotel can also offer a lot. For example, there are several outdoor pools where you can refresh yourself. By the way, the pool system resembles a river. On the terrace around there are enough deck chairs with soft mattresses, as well as parasols. The pool has two large bars. Towels can be rented here. For a small fee, guests can spend time in a local water park - water slides will appeal to both adult tourists and their children.

There is a gym here, and on the territory of the hotel various sports classes are held throughout the day. You can have a good time at the tennis courts. By the way, beginners can also take some lessons from professionals. The hotel also offers several sports fields, ping-pong tables and billiards. There is even a place for archery.

You should definitely visit the spa, which offers massages and various beauty treatments. The hotel also has a night club, casino and games room, so tourists can have fun. And the animators are constantly organizing various competitions, contests, team games and evening show programs.

Hotel Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Spa 5 *: reviews





Immediately it is worth saying that tourists leave exclusively positive reviews about this hotel. It is really very beautiful here - the vast territory resembles an ever-blooming garden, and you can have a good rest near the pools. The rooms are also comfortable, furnished with modern furniture. The food is very good, and the cooks are real geniuses.

Children are also having fun here, especially often they spend time at the water park and mini club. The advantage is the proximity to the beach. Yes, and in the hotel there is something to have fun. By the way, the hotel staff is very polite, always ready to help cope with any difficulties.
