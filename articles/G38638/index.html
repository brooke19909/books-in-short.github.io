Introduction





Nokia 7230 was created in order to compete with phones that were manufactured by Samsung. It should be immediately noted that in the slider segment, the South Korean manufacturer of mobile phones has largely entrenched, and the struggle was not short-lived. In general, the task of the Finnish company was as follows. She was required to create an apparatus that would become of interest to a sufficiently large audience. Moreover, among the voiced conditions were low cost and related manufacturing materials. In fact, that's all. This was the only basis for the creation of Nokia 7230, the price of which is seven thousand rubles.

And if it was possible to somehow cope with the cost and bring it to the desired level, then the engineers obviously messed up something with the production materials. At first, their manipulations were completely incomprehensible. As a result, the developers, as it seems, decided not to bother with this and simply simply used the first material that came across, without regard to its characteristics and parameters. What, in principle, one could still expect from the Finnish manufacturer, no offense to Nokia will be said. Although the company knows a lot about smartphones, it sometimes makes decisions that go against the tradition (and, it should be noted, to the detriment).

Conclusion on the reasons for the creation





So, creating a Nokia 7230 phone, firmware for which is available on the official website of the company, the developers relied on the brand. According to their plan, the name itself was already to displace the sliders of the South Korean manufacturer from the market. Additional advantages of the model include a fairly high-quality screen and, by the way, good technical characteristics.

Production material





This, of course, is ordinary plastic. The corpus consists of it for all 80 percent. Nevertheless, plastic can be said to be harmless to the user, his environment, and indeed to the environment, it can be recycled after disposal. Yes, about what, but the Finnish manufacturer this time took care of the environment properly.

Side faces





The sides of the phone are edged with colored plastic, which has a chrome texture. The experience of the creation prompted Nokia experts that this material of manufacture begins to largely wipe and fly around after it has been deeply scratched. But engineers and designers missed this remark by their ears, and again we all see the same plastic. Absolutely nothing has changed.

Rear panel

It is made in relief. You can still find fault with execution, but this will be discussed a little later. So, the structure of the back panel is corrugated. This has its practical reasons. Firstly, fingerprints are noticeable several times worse. Secondly, such a panel will not receive so much (and less noticeable) mechanical damage. Thirdly, this gives the device a more marketable appearance than with a monolithic, even face. And finally, the tactile perception of such a cover is much nicer. Now let's talk about the shortcomings of such a performance.

It so happened that the back cover is very, very thin. Due to the use of low-quality plastic grades, scratches and other mechanical defects are noticeable, even though the panel is made in relief form.

Latches and mechanisms

Completed the first, to put it mildly, very strange. You can immediately note the inconvenience. When the user opens and closes the back panel a sufficient number of times, it will begin to emit a constant creak. So here - you use the device, and it begins to speak in a language understandable only to him. A terrible dream of people who hate backlash. At the same time, it does not take a lot of time to ‚Äúkill‚Äù the back cover.

We can say that the Finnish manufacturer was chasing, first of all, a beautiful appearance, and in the backlashes and creaks it was this pursuit that played a fatal role. However, nothing can be done. The South Korean company Samsung in this direction has acted more reasonably. What did the Koreans do? They missed the move, without adding ostensibly chrome elements. Why? Yes, because chromed plastic is a favorite way of attracting attention from Chinese manufacturers. So Nokia was in a trap, which, in theory, wanted to catch its competitor (well, or even in some way a colleague).

Dimensions

In all three planes, the dimensions of the phone are as follows: 98 by 48 by 14.75 millimeters. The mass of the device is only one hundred grams. I would like to say that this is such a brick. However, the mass does not allow this to be done. If she was at least fifty grams more, then another thing. The rest can be said that the dimensions of the device are average, standard. At the same time, the phone lies securely and conveniently in the hand. There should not be any complaints in this regard.

Top end

There is a connector for connecting a wired stereo headset standard 3.5 mm. Also nearby is a charger cable slot. The port for the MicroUSB wire is covered with a special plug.

Right side

There is a key with which you can control the camera of the device. She herself is on the back of the phone. The camera resolution is 3.2 megapixels.

Color schemes

The device entered the smartphone market in two color variations. It is pink and graphite. Both options, in principle, look pretty pretty. But the first, as you might have guessed, was for women, and the second for men.

Conclusion

The owners of the phone have repeatedly recorded in the advantages of the device a good quality of voice transmission. And distinguished in a good sense of the word not only the microphone, but also the speaker. The volume is good there, the interlocutor on the other end of the wire is heard just fine. Not spared and voice call. The ringer volume is at the level, the same applies to vibration mode. Skipping an incoming call even in a noisy environment will be difficult.

The disadvantages this time include the material of manufacture of the housing. He really spoils the opinion of such a good device. Backlashes immediately appear, the device begins to creak, which, by the way, can annoy the owner of the phone.
