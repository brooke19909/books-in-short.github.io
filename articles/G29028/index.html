Rooms

The presence of air conditioning is perhaps the main criterion for evaluating hotels when it comes to hot countries. So, Welcome Jomtien Beach Place 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) provides comfort and coolness in the rooms due to modern climatic equipment. The rooms are small but bright and comfortable. Each is equipped with a private bathroom with necessary bath accessories, there is a TV with satellite channels, including Russian, a telephone, a kettle with dishes, and a balcony.





The furniture in the room has a huge bed, two armchairs, a table, a wardrobe, a chest of drawers with a mirror - everything is stylish and modern. The rooms are decorated in calm pastel colors.

Infrastructure

Own territory for hotels in Thailand is a rarity, because most of them are urban-type hotels. Welcome Jomtien Beach Place 3 * is an exception to the rule. On its territory there are several restaurants with European and Asian cuisine, a bar with alcoholic drinks, an outdoor pool with sunbeds and umbrellas, several chic conference rooms, parking for cars and motorbikes popular here, as well as its own laundry. On the territory of all vacationers can use the currency exchange and at a favorable rate to make money exchange. Car and motorbike rental services are also available here.

The pool is small, there is a jacuzzi. The gradually increasing depth of the pool allows water procedures to be taken by vacationers of different ages. Near it there are very few sun loungers, but it is almost always free, since most tourists prefer the sea.





Fun for kids

Recently, more and more often in Pattaya you can see vacationers with children, so the issue of having entertainment for them worries many parents. So the hotel has a good playground, a children's pool, for an additional fee, you can use the services of a nanny.

Service

The hotel rooms are cleaned daily: towels are changed, plumbing is cleaned, the floor is cleaned - in general, the room is quite clean. The reception is open around the clock, so at any time vacationers can ask the staff with their own question. Here you can use the safe for storing documents and valuables. The hotel staff understands the Russian language well, so difficulties with the explanation should not arise.

At the reception, all vacationers are given beach towels for free. The only thing is that they must be returned on the same day until 11 p.m.

Nutrition

All vacationers can count on breakfast in the hotel restaurant. Breakfast includes omelet and scrambled eggs, sausages, boiled rice, light soups, spaghetti, cold cuts of meat and cheese, toast, jam, fresh tropical fruits, tea, coffee, juices. There are no queues even at the height of the season, the food on the tables is constantly replenished, therefore, having barely opened eyes to run for breakfast, no matter what time you come, you will always be offered a good assortment of dishes.





Almost all tourists who come to Pattaya purchase excursions here, most often the trip is scheduled for early morning, and here vacationers will certainly encounter a hotel drawback - the lack of lunch boxes. Beach and sea

Welcome Jomtien Beach Place 3 * (Thailand) does not have its own beach, like other hotels in the city, so tourists will have to spend their holidays on a city sandy beach located across the street from the hotel. For an additional fee on the beach you can rent sun loungers, umbrellas. Many sincerely believe that in the city it is simply impossible to take sun baths and swim due to dirt, but this is completely not so.

Jomtien Beach is clean enough, daily cleaning. Yes, the sand here is not as snow-white as on the islands, and the water is not of such an azure hue as in the photo from the Internet, but to relax and enjoy the warm and gentle sea is quite comfortable and pleasant. Tourists note a good beach infrastructure: here you can rent sports equipment, engage in active water activities, as well as buy soft drinks, fresh fruits and enjoy seafood.
