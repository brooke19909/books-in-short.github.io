Spasskaya Hotel, Tyumen: address, service, reviews. "Best Western Plus Spasskaya"

Tyumen is an ancient city in which there are many historical sites that attract the attention of tourists. That is why guests come here every year from different cities and from neighboring countries who are interested in historical events and the features of infrastructure.

The Spasskaya Hotel in Tyumen is a wonderful place that is incredibly popular among visiting business people and, of course, tourists. It is designed in such a way that it was very comfortable and pleasant to be in it. Her guest book lists the names of many famous people who have stayed here.

general information

The hotel is a place for relaxation, which perfectly combines world-class service and excellent amenities. It was built in 2012 according to a unique project. Immediately after its construction, the hotel received international status, which remains to this day. This is due not only to high-level living conditions, but also to the fact that the staff is mostly multilingual, that is, each employee knows at least one foreign language, which helps in communicating with tourists from abroad.

Location

One of the main features of the Spasskaya Hotel in Tyumen is that it is located in the historic city center. Not far from it is the famous Bridge of Lovers, on which couples traditionally walk. Being here, you can visit the nearby local history museum "Duma", the center "Geologist", as well as the house of Masharov, which is currently a historical monument - a museum. Adherents of religion very often stay in this hotel in order to be within walking distance from the Church of the Archangel Michael, as well as the Ascension-George Church. Not far from here is the Holy Trinity Monastery, which can be visited after covering a short distance.

A couple of minutes from the hotel is the famous promenade, where you can take a walk with the whole family or together with your loved one.

The exact address of the hotel: Tyumen, st. Lenin, 2a.



Rooms

Hotel guests have the opportunity to choose a suitable place to stay from 82 comfortable apartments. All of them are divided into several categories: "standard", "comfort", "twin", "business" and "presidential". Disabled accessible apartments are also available.

Each apartment, regardless of category, has a separate air conditioning, which can be used to create optimal temperature conditions in any season of the year. There are also bathrooms equipped with a full set of sanitary ware and various bathing accessories, which include soap, shower gel and shampoo. Bathrobes, towels and slippers are also provided. If desired, everyone can use the hairdryer in the bathroom.
