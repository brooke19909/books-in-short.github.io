Hotel Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

The popularity of Bulgarian resorts is due to the low cost of living and the good quality of the services provided. Sunny Beach is the most vibrant and active tourist center in the country. All infrastructure here is designed for visiting travelers. Along the coast are many budget and first-class hotels. If you consider yourself in the latter category, but still want to save money, then you should pay attention to the Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * complex (Bulgaria). Tours here are in great demand among tourists from Russia and the CIS countries.

General information and location

This resort complex opened its doors to tourists in 2002. It is located on the first beach line, so the distance to the coast is about 100 meters. The city beach is separated from the hotel by the pedestrian promenade, which stretches along the entire coast of Sunny Beach. Vacationers like to stroll here in the evenings when the temperature drops slightly. The central part of the resort is also within walking distance. It is removed only 200 meters. Nearby are a hospital and a police station. A large shopping center can be found just 300 meters from the hotel. You can also visit the local water park. It is only 600 meters away.





Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * (Sunny Beach) has its own vast territory, whose area is 25,000 square meters. m. It is located not only the main nine-story building, but an outdoor pool and garden. Tourists with children often stop here. Babies can request a cot in the room upon request. Restaurants are equipped with high chairs for feeding. It is worth knowing that the settlement with animals is impossible. The hotel only accepts tourists between May and September. In winter, it carries out repair and restoration work.

Number of rooms

Tourists who decide to stay at the Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * (Sunny Beach) will appreciate the number of rooms presented here. The last renovation of residential premises was made in 2007. All apartments have a private bathroom and an open balcony. The rooms overlook the pool and garden. Employees clean rooms every day while tourists relax on the beach. They also change towels, complement toilet paper and cosmetics. Sheets are usually updated three times a week.

The housing stock of the hotel Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * is represented by the following types of apartments:

  • Standart room. It consists of a living room and a combined bathroom. The bedroom has two single beds and a sofa bed. At the same time, no more than three adults can live here. Its area is approximately 30 square meters. m
  • Apartments. Two-room suite, which consists of a living room and a bedroom. There is a kitchen area where you can cook your own meals. Suitable for a family of two adults and two children. They are accommodated in a double bed and a pull-out sofa. Between the rooms there is a full door. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe apartment is 75 square meters. m
  • Suite It differs from the apartments in more expensive and modern furniture.




Room amenities

Comfortable accommodation of guests largely depends on the equipment of the rooms. The hotel has all the necessary amenities for a normal stay. For example, in the evening, tourists can relax watching TV. Several cable channels are connected to it, among which there are Russian ones. In the Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * (Bulgaria), the photo of which can be viewed in our article, in hot weather there is a central air conditioner that maintains a comfortable temperature for sleeping. You can store food and drinks in the mini-fridge , which is located in the kitchen area. It can be used for free.





Many tourists, leaving the room, worry about the safety of their valuables and money. To ensure security, the apartments are equipped with mechanical safes, which are not charged. On the bedside table is a telephone. In the bathroom, guests will find a hairdryer, towels and a set of cosmetics for the shower. Internet is free throughout the complex, but it works especially well near the reception.

Hotel infrastructure

Choosing Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * (Bulgaria) as a place to stay, tourists will appreciate the wide variety of services offered. We list the main infrastructure facilities that all guests can use at any time:

  • A conference room where conferences, seminars and business meetings are regularly held . It is not equipped with computer equipment, but here is a magnetic board and markers.
  • Private parking. A car can be left on it only after a preliminary reservation. The fee for one day of its use is 6 euros.
  • 24 hour reception. For tourists who do not like to wait long, there is an accelerated check-in or check-out service. It is provided for a fee.
  • Car rental. Available to people over 21 years old. It is worth knowing that tourists pay for gasoline on their own.
  • Organization of transfer from the airport and back. Paid at checkout.
  • Luggage storage. You can leave your suitcases in it so that they do not take up space in the room.
  • Currency exchange.
  • Laundry.
  • Three modern elevators that will quickly deliver guests to their floors.




Entertainment Options

Not a little attention is paid to entertainment. The main pride of the hotel is a large outdoor pool filled with fresh water. Next to it is a sun deck, where tourists can take sun loungers and umbrellas for free. Beach towels, on the contrary, are issued only after paying the deposit. Wellness services are offered to its guests by the spa center operating on the territory of Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 *. Guests can enjoy a tonic or relaxing massage, or visit the jacuzzi, sauna or solarium. There is also a beauty salon and hairdresser.





Tourists who prefer outdoor activities, often visit the local gym, equipped with modern equipment. Also at the reception you can rent a bike and ride it in Sunny Beach. A water sports center is located on a public beach. Mass games of water polo or volleyball in the sand are also held there. You can relax and take a breath in the common room, which is located on the ground floor of the complex. It has soft sofas, a TV and air conditioning.

Catering

The catering services at the Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * are also given great attention. When booking a tour, future guests can choose the all-inclusive system or pay for breakfast only. The first option is suitable for tourists who spend a lot of time on site. The price will include not only full meals, but also snacks and local soft drinks and spirits, as well as cocktails, desserts and ice cream.





The complex has a restaurant that can accommodate about 200 visitors. In clear weather, guests can have lunch or dinner on the outdoor terrace, decorated with vines. You can always enjoy a drink or just a snack in the pool bar.

Positive reviews

Many tourists, before issuing a voucher, prefer to read the reviews of previous hotel guests. What do they think of the rest at the Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * (Bulgaria)? Reviews are most often positive. In them they talk about the following advantages:

  • The hotel is close to the sea. Go to him just a couple of minutes. The beach is very clean and the water is clear. The entrance to the sea is gentle. Depth is suitable for bathing young children.
  • Good soundproofing in the rooms. If you close the windows, then the noise from the street is unlikely to bother you. Tourists from neighboring rooms are also inaudible.
  • Clean pool, which is constantly monitored. Garbage is removed every morning. The water in it does not smell like bleach.
  • Responsive and friendly staff who speaks good Russian. They not only try to quickly solve the problems that have arisen, but can also tell where it is better to go, relaxing here.
  • Good housekeeping. Toiletries are replenished regularly. Maids tidy up well and without paying a tip.




Negative reviews

Unfortunately, not all tourists rated the quality of service provided at Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 *. They believe that the administration should pay attention to the following disadvantages:

  • Monotonous food. Most of the dishes on the menu are fast food, which is not suitable for tourists with stomach diseases.
  • The staff does not monitor the pool, so it is often visited by tourists from other hotels or locals who live nearby. Because of this, it was almost always crowded.
  • Internet only works on the ground floor. At the same time, day and evening, the speed is terribly low.
  • Hot water can be turned off for several days without warning.
  • The hotel does not at all monitor the safety of guests. Tourists describe the frequent cases of theft of money from the room, and the administration did not take any measures to capture the criminal. Neither the pool nor the beach has lifeguards.

Draw conclusions

Grand Hotel Oazis Apartments 4 * (Bulgaria), the description of which can be read above, has many disadvantages. You should definitely study them before buying a tour. Coming here, tourists need to keep track of valuable items and money. If you do not pay attention to shortcomings, then the hotel is well suited for tourists who prefer a relaxing beach holiday.
