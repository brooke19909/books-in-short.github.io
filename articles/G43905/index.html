OneClickMoney: reviews, loan terms

Many people have situations when they need money urgently, literally any minute, and the salary is still not long. An even more difficult situation arises when unforeseen expenses arise in connection with a car breakdown, the need for urgent repairs in an apartment or illness of a family member.

Not everyone is happy to borrow money from friends and acquaintances. Moreover, not everyone has friends who could lend a large sum. Banks do not issue loans if a person has a bad credit history. In these cases, MFIs are very helpful, one of which is OneClickMoney. Reviews about this method of obtaining money are rather mixed. Let's analyze in more detail.

What is an MFI?

MFI is an abbreviation that stands for ‚ÄúMicrofinance Organization‚Äù. As a rule, MFIs specialize in lending to the population for small amounts: the so-called payday loans.

Mostly, such organizations do not require guarantors and certificates of income, and also offer relatively small amounts of debt - up to 50 thousand rubles. You can place an application on the websites of such organizations or directly in the office. Of the documents in any case require a passport or passport data. The remaining information is already specified directly in a specific MFI.

OneClickMoney is one of the leading microfinance organizations in Russia. She has her own site - oneclickmoney.ru. Reviews, detailed information on the loan, interest rates, public offer - all located at this address. Thanks to this, before applying for a loan, you can get to know all the data as much as possible and make a decision.

Most reviews of OneClickMoney are generally positive, but it is worth noting that some MFIs remove negative reviews or ask their partners to do so. Therefore, it is advisable to focus on reviews left on verified independent resources.

To get a loan in this organization, you need to fill out a form and wait for a decision. Mostly it is automatically accepted after processing the data entered by the user. On average, it takes from four to ten minutes of time. After approval of the application, the client receives money to the card or to the account of the electronic payment system within 10-20 minutes. In addition, it is possible to get a loan through the Unistream or Golden Crown translation system.

Sign up for OneClickMoney

Without registration, only a few functions are available:

  • Using a special calculator to roughly estimate the interest rate on the desired loan.
  • Ask a technical support specialist questions online.
  • Read the general information and rules for using the service.

To apply for a loan and enter OneClickMoney you need to register. Registration takes place simultaneously with the process of applying for a loan.

It is necessary to pass verification and sign a public offer agreement. The system sends a message with a code to the borrower‚Äôs phone number, which must be entered in a special field. The introduction of this code indicates that the user agrees to the contract, and also confirms the fact that he provided the correct personal data.

After entering all the necessary data, you are logged into OneClickMoney, in your personal account.

Information requested by the system for obtaining a loan:

  • Passport data (F. I. O., date of birth, address of registration and actual residence).
  • Place of work. If the potential borrower is a student, senior citizen or unemployed, this must be indicated.
  • Amount and term of the loan.
  • Passport number and series, place of issue.
  • Primary mobile number.
  • Other contacts are additionally asked (additional phone numbers, Skype login), but this is not necessary.

After entering the OneClickMoney account, you can verify the correctness of all entered data. It is worth noting that no one requires sending a photocopy or scanned copy of the passport. All that is needed is the correct passport data.

Mobile App Features

The microfinance organization OneClickMoney offers its customers the opportunity to use the mobile application. Many of them have already noted the convenience of this solution.

You can enter OneClickMoney not only with a login, which is an email address and password, but also through authorization on the State Services website. For many, this option is much simpler. There is the same opportunity to enter the mobile application.

The application works on both the Android platform and the Apple platform, so it can be used by different categories of users. In addition, the application is quite lightweight - it takes up only 25 megabytes of device RAM.

An undoubted advantage is that you can enter your OneClickMoney account at any time, provided that you have an Internet connection and either familiarize yourself with information on debts or make a payment.

In addition, you can apply for a loan. Consideration of applications received from a mobile device through the application takes an average of four minutes, while the examination of applications from a computer takes about seven minutes.

Conditions for obtaining a loan

Microcredit can only be obtained by adult citizens of Russia with a passport confirming this fact. The conditions are as follows:

  • The maximum age of the borrower is 80 years.
  • The minimum loan amount is 500 rubles, the maximum is 30 thousand rubles.
  • The loan repayment period is from 6 to 21 days.
  • The rate is on average from 1% to 2.2% per day. In other words, the earlier the debt is paid off, the less interest will have to be paid.

You can receive money on a bank card, on the account of an electronic payment system, through the Unistream or Golden Crown transfer systems.

Reviews on OneClickMoney indicate that the loan application is approved in most cases, and the money comes to the borrower almost instantly.

When you receive a loan again, you no longer need to go through the entire registration procedure again. It is enough to enter your personal account and apply for a loan. The data previously filled in will remain in your personal account, and they can be edited if they change (change of address, last name, passport, and so on).

Risks and Pitfalls

In fact, any options for loans and borrowings carry enormous risks: many people gradually get used to living on credit and do not notice that they are starting to give more than they get.

In addition, obtaining loans in microfinance institutions is easier and faster, but at the same time the interest rate is much higher. For comparison: you can find banks with an interest rate of 10% per year, while for a loan in an MFI for 20 days you will have to pay more than 15 - 20%.

Some unscrupulous citizens find it easier to trick MFIs than a bank. In fact, the debt in any case will have to be paid. Judging by the feedback of OneClickMoney debtors, it is impossible to bypass the payment of debt: the organization has all the passport data of the client that makes it easy to find. Therefore, it is better not to take risks and include common sense. Moreover, if for some reason it is not possible to repay the debt on time, it is better to contact OneClickMoney employees and discuss the possibility and methods of solving the problem.
