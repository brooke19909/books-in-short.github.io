The best hotels in Sortavala

Sortavala is one of the oldest cities in Karelia. The appearance of the city developed under the influence of three countries: Russia, Finland and Sweden. The city is attractive for tourists with the opportunity, without going abroad, to be in the atmosphere of a Finnish town. Numerous hotels in Sortavala provide excellent conditions for a good rest.

Hotel Sofia

The hotel is located in the city center just a five-minute drive from the train station and in close proximity to local attractions: the Regional Museum of Northern Ladoga, Vakkosalmi Park, St. Nicholas Church.





Accommodation at the Sofia Hotel

The number of rooms includes rooms designed to accommodate one (without sharing), two or three people (with the possibility of sharing). All apartments are equipped with a TV, air conditioning and necessary furniture. The bathroom with shower is shared and is located outside the room. Guests have the opportunity to access the Internet through wireless access. The list of services includes the use of office equipment. Hotels in Sortavala provide city guests with everything necessary to make them feel at home.

Memo to the guests of Sofia

Registration at the hotel is carried out around the clock upon presentation by the guest of a passport. Payment for accommodation is taken per day. Recalculation of the cost when staying at the hotel before the expiration of twenty-four hours is not performed. The hotel staff is responsible for ensuring a comfortable and safe stay for guests. At reception, room keys will not be issued to guests without a guest card. There is a safe for valuables, which guests can use for free. Staying by unauthorized persons in the hotel rooms is possible up to twenty-three hours, subject to their mandatory registration. When the guest leaves the hotel, the staff inspects the room for damage.

Hotel Sofia (Sortavala) does not accommodate people who are intoxicated. No smoking is allowed on site.

Meals are organized in the cafe "Youth" and carried out by reservation. Guests have the opportunity to cook their own meals in the shared kitchen. At the reception , a tourist can get all the information about city attractions that interests him. The staff will help him choose a guided tour.
