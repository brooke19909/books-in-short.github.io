Hurghada, Royal Azur - a hotel or an ideal of your vacation? Decide for yourself!

Egypt, Royal Azur (Hurghada) - a place literally drowning in flowers, perfect for exploring the fauna and flora of the Red Sea. You will forget about the worries and deeds, the bustle of city life here. Opened in 1998, the hotel complex is a two-story building, conveniently located on the seashore. The airport is 28 kilometers from the hotel, and Hurghada is 35 kilometers away. Royal Azur is known for its assortment and quality of water sports, rich in the seabed, which offers amazing opportunities for diving. Endless coral reefs, tropical fish, azure waters - all this for you!

Residence



The hotel has 370 rooms:

  • 360 standard (24 sq. M., Maximum 4 people);
  • 4 Junior Suite (total area 48 sq. M.);
  • Family rooms
  • 10 Chalet.

It is important to note that most rooms have stunning views of either the sea or the magical garden. They are all furnished in a contemporary style and furnished with high quality. Cleaning is carried out daily, as well as a change of linen. Each room has a balcony, bath, air conditioning, high-quality minibar, telephone, TV, toilet, terrace, TV (with Russian channel), hairdryer. Pets are allowed. Russian-speaking staff works at the reception and in the kids club.

Hurghada, Royal Azur. Nutrition Overview

The power system operates on an all-inclusive basis. At breakfast, guests will be invited to use the Blue Lagoon buffet .

A continental breakfast is available from 5:00 to 07:00 , and fried eggs can already be ordered from 07:00 to 11:00. Lunch is served in the restaurant "Le Rivage" (12: 30-15: 30), after this time you can get only snacks (until 18:30). You can have dinner at the Blue Lagoon (19: 00-23: 00). In addition, vacationers can visit other restaurants of the hotel:

  • Italian Il Rondo;
  • fish Le Pecheur with a magnificent panoramic view of the sea coast, it is advisable to book a table in advance;
  • Le Rivage - restaurant on the coast;
  • Tropicana Bar, located by the pool, you can buy snacks and drinks here (12: 30-18: 30);
  • Lobby bar - drinks and desserts;
  • Oriental Al Mandra - plunge into the sophisticated atmosphere of the East and Egyptian folklore.

Hurghada, Royal Azur. Service



Hotel guests are free to use the Internet, attend classes in aerobics, water aerobics, go to the gym, play table tennis, darts, rent sports equipment, bicycles, and also attend show programs and a disco. For an additional fee, tourists have the opportunity to visit the sauna, aquacenter, scuba diving courses, rent water skis, diving equipment, learn horse riding, ride a boat with a transparent bottom. The hotel parking has an open area (for 26 places) and an enclosed space (11 places). The conference hall can accommodate 120 people during a banquet, 160 people during seminars, 250 during conferences.

Hotel Royal Azur, Hurghada. Active and children's rest

Each hotel guest can easily find something to their liking. Both families with children and youth companies will have an equally good time here. For children, special types of entertainment are offered: a children's pool, a club, animation, performances and babysitting services. And since the hotel management permits accommodation in rooms with animals, the rest will definitely not be boring! Hurghada is waiting for you! Royal Azur is always a pleasant and memorable vacation!
