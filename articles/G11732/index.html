Why watches are called "boilers": common versions

What could be common between boilers and watches? Oddly enough, the term "boilers" is most often heard in relation to a wrist accessory in the common people. How to explain why the watch is called "boilers"? There are actually several reasons for this.

Tragic version



It is no secret that prisoners of labor camps in the USSR did not have personal items, in particular hours. Therefore, a bell should serve as a warning about the beginning of the lunch break or the end of daily hard labor. Since most camps did not want to perplex themselves with the search for a special device that would emit a clear signal, the old boiler often served as a replacement for it. Sometimes pots were no longer an alternative; they were no longer suitable for their intended purpose.

As you know, hard labor was carried out in quarries, where impacts of tools on the rock created the strongest noise level. Therefore, it was difficult to hear even the most strained cries of the overseer. Attracting the attention of prisoners could only battle with a heavy object in the suspended boilers, which, in fact, began to be associated with the chimes of the convicts.
