Nokia N97 - a smartphone with a sliding keyboard

This article is devoted to an interesting smartphone model from a Finnish manufacturer. Nokia H97 is a smartphone with a qwerty keyboard. Below will be described the characteristics, advantages of this device. The disadvantages will not be mentioned, since there are practically none, because each owner has his own preferences.

About the unusual keyboard

Before talking about the characteristics, it is better to get acquainted with the extraordinary feature of the smartphone. What is the qwerty keyboard mentioned at the very beginning? Look at the top row of letters on the left. It is these Latin letters that follow each other. But why is it called the keyboard? Most likely, so as not to mislead customers when they get acquainted with the functions and characteristics of the product.



As you can see in the attractive photo, the miniature keyboard has almost the same layout as the computer one. There is an exception. It was not possible to place all the keys for the inventor of the Nokia N97 Mini smartphone. Therefore, so that there are no missing characters, they are placed as if inside a key. The main colors are marked in black and the secondary ones in blue. Using the blue diagonal arrow on the right, you can print a hidden letter, sign, or number. To make it easier to move the cursor on the screen, a joystick is provided on the left. Its internal button, in fact, is Enter, which is more suitable for the Internet. For text programs, the same key is provided as for the large keyboard.

Smartphone Features

The screen size is only 3.5 inches. Such a device can easily fit even in a small lady's hand. Despite these dimensions, you can read the text, open maps and watch videos. The screen is resistive.

The operating system in Nokia N97 is not familiar Android, but Symbian OS 9.4. It is much more convenient, you can download applications on the Internet on trusted sites with applications for the corresponding operating system and smartphone model.



There are: one slot for a standard SIM card, GPS, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 2.0, USB. The presence of 3G makes it possible to fully use the Internet. As for multimedia, you can listen to music, watch movies, read books. The built-in and RAM volumes are 32 GB and 128 MB, respectively.

Additional features

The good news is for those who want to buy a Nokia N97 phone. A camera with a resolution of 5 million pixels makes high-quality photos and videos. Of course, you do not need to compare with cameras, the pictures there are more clear and without noise. Nevertheless, the smartphone will help in traveling or just on the road to photograph something interesting.



And another no less amazing news is the ability to use the navigator for both pedestrians and motorists. You can use the built-in application, or download on the Internet.

Where to buy and what prices?

The smartphone went on sale in 2009. Unfortunately, since 2012 it was discontinued. In stores, he began to meet less often. Currently, it is possible to purchase mainly in the radio markets of Moscow and St. Petersburg and in a number of other large cities, both new and used.





Some online stores sell the remaining copies of the Nokia H97. The price ranges from 11 thousand to 20 thousand rubles.
