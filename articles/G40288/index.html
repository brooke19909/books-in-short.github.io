Exciting Occupation - Crocheted Tops





Especially for girls and women who have not lost their slim figure and smooth skin elasticity, as well as necessarily watching all the fashion splashes, here are some ideas for knitting with a hook of summer tops, t-shirts and small sweaters.

Depending on the level of self-confidence, you can choose a more or less open option. In any case, crocheted tops are clothes that are exclusively adapted to all occasions: for parties and holidays, for a working day in the office, for classes at school, college or college. It all depends on the choice of yarn, which can be bright, shiny (with lurex, for example) or even a modest cotton, silk, linen, which, of course, will not hinder the festive option when choosing a style and knitting technique. Crocheted tops will not require excessive labor and a lot of time. Moreover, this activity is so exciting that the result is unexpectedly quick and easy.

Crocheted tops, the patterns of which are attached, can be made as a single fabric, or consist of individual motifs. The most beautiful look is the so-called "Irish" canvas. Composed of various motifs, crochet knitted tops are unique. The patterns for each motive are different: all kinds of flowers, leaves, geometric shapes are assembled according to a crochet pattern, or are sewn neatly with a darn needle. For only those who master the technique, there are the simplest models: canvas and straps, for example. Or crochet tops like the one below.





Absolutely simple beach top

It will take three hundred grams of cotton yarn, hook number three.

Bodice

For the main pattern you need to dial four air. loops that connect the column. First row: knit air. loop after lifting, then six columns without inc. and connect. column. Second row: air. loop, then in every second column without nak. knit two columns without inc., plus connect. column. Third row: air. loop, in each column without nak. knit another column without inc., and in double columns perform double again, plus connect. column. So knit to the twelfth row. Twelfth row: three air. loops after under., two columns with., two air. loops, again two columns with nak. - all this in one column without nak. previous row.

It turns out something like an outlet. They need to knit two rows in a circle, and then two more rows on the other side of the part to make a triangle. The second such triangle fits symmetrically. After that, you can knit straps. First air. loops along the desired length, then tying them with columns without nak. with pico. Reconnect the triangles and tie them with lush columns according to the scheme: in the loop of the previous row - two columns with nak., Air. loop, two columns with nak .; we skip one loop of the previous row, and in the next we do the same. So to the end.

Before

Dial forty-six air. loops and knit according to the attached pattern nineteen rows, then repeat the first ten.

Bottom part

In terms of hips, dial a chain of air. stitches, then knit with scales (five stitches with three stitches in one stitch, after which a single crochet stitch through four stitches of the previous row). From the sixth row to the eleventh, add in each half of the scales - that is, two columns with three crochets.

Assembly

Connect the details, tie them in columns with pico, and tie the bottom loops for the altas ribbon: nine air. loops, column without nak. in the third loop of the previous row, in the next row twelve columns with nak. in each arch, a column without nak. in a column without nak. previous row. Thread the tape, tie it. That's basically it. Crocheted tops are not as scary in performance as they seem.
