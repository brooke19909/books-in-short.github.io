Hotel "Mercure Sochi Center" 4 *: phone, reviews

Mercure Sochi Center is a modern hotel with an exquisite design, in which literally every little thing has been thought out. It offers views of the blue of the sea or the city. It is located in the very center of Sochi and belongs to the hotel brand Mercure Hotels, represented by 725 establishments in 50 countries of the world. This place is perfect for both leisure and business tourism. Here you can spend time fascinating, interesting, rich and useful, forgetting the hustle and bustle of the big city and plunging into the world of pleasant activities.

Hotel location Mercure

The hotel is located in the resort city of Sochi (Russia, Southern Federal District, Krasnodar Territory) along Ordzhonikidze Street in the building at number 11A. The main role is played by the fact that the Mercure Sochi Center hotel is comfortably located in the very center of the resort. Such an arrangement is advantageous, since guests can reach the water park, concert palace, seaport, shopping center, Winter Theater and many other public places in which you can spend unforgettable and profitable time in a matter of minutes.

Adler Airport is 25 km away, and Sochi Train Station is just 2 km away. The seaport is 800 meters from the hotel, and the central promenade is 100 meters away.





Hotel Description

The four-star hotel is a 16-story building, which was built in 2013. The location makes it ideal for an active and relaxing holiday and productive work. Designed in a contemporary style, the hotel has an attractive but calm interior. Each type of room is individual and does not have such elements that would prevent you from emotionally relaxing or putting pressure on your eyes. In general, there is everything that tourists need to relax and work, and there is nothing that could prevent them from being happy on their vacation days.

Supply system

For vacationers, the Bed & Breakfast type of accommodation is provided, that is, only breakfast is included in the price. The restaurant serves a buffet, which invites visitors to enjoy hot and cold dishes, as well as various drinks. It works daily.

Lunch and dinner at the Mercure Sochi Center are served for a fee at the Lace restaurant, located on the 2nd floor of the hotel, and the bar in the lounge area. You can also order meals in your room, apply for half-board or boarding on the set menu. Special meals are provided for children.

The Lace Restaurant is a cuisine inspired by local traditions, and some dishes are prepared in a modern way. In a word, delicious and satisfying food for large and small guests. And the bar offers a variety of drinks and snacks.

Infrastructure and entertainment

The hotel has its own Fit & SPA Lounge, located on the top floor of the building. It has:

  • hammam and sauna - separate for women and men;
  • fitness room with cardiovascular equipment;
  • relaxation therapy and massage.

The hotel has its own business center with a banquet and conference room. Only 9 rooms for negotiations, which are equipped with the necessary equipment, but have a different area and, accordingly, capacity. Each has a lounge, an exclusive coffee break is possible.

Private parking is available on site and must be booked in advance. It is also possible to exchange currency at a special point and purchase tickets. Guests are invited to book an excursion to make a short trip to Sochi. Dry cleaning works, after which the clothes will be like new.

Upon request, the Mercure Sochi Center provides everything you need for washing, iron and ironing board.
