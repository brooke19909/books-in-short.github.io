Sherwood Greenwood Resort 4 * (Kemer, Turkey): description and reviews of tourists about the hotel

By right, Kemer is considered one of the best resorts in the Republic of Turkey. Despite the fact that this region is quite young, it is developing very dynamically and today it is ready to offer tourists all the opportunities for a wonderful holiday on the shores of the warm Mediterranean Sea. The hotel base here is represented very widely: from inexpensive pensions to luxurious five-star complexes. If you plan to spend your vacation in Kemer and expect to stay in a comfortable, but not too expensive hotel, then Sherwood Greenwood Resort Hotel 4 * will be an excellent choice. Today we decided to take a closer look at this four-star hotel and find out what our compatriots think about the rest in it.





Where is this hotel complex located

Sherwood Greenwood Resort 4 * is located in a very cozy and calm place - the village of Goynuk. Kemer city center is just 9 kilometers away. You can get to it either by taxi or by minibus (here it is called dolmush). Antalya Airport is 55 kilometers away. Upon arrival at the air harbor you can reach your hotel within 50-60 minutes. As for the sea, the hotel has its own beach, located just a couple of hundred meters. However, on the way to it you need to cross the road.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of the hotel complex, the arrival of tourists to the rooms they booked is made from 14:00. In case of early arrival at the hotel, employees will do their best to populate you right away. However, in the midst of the tourist season, the Sherwood Greenwood Resort 4 * hotel complex is often 100% full, so you will have to wait until the previous guests leave the room and clean the maids. In this case, you can leave your things in the luggage room and stroll around the hotel, have a bite to eat in the restaurant, take a dip in the pool or go straight to the beach. On the day of departure, the room must be vacated before noon. Also, at discharge, you should make a calculation for the time spent in the hotel and the use of various services. If you have already paid for the stay with the travel agency through which you booked a ticket, then at checkout you will only have to make money for using additional services (if you, of course, resorted to them). In this case, both cash and plastic cards of the Visa or Mastercard systems are accepted for payment. Payment by cards and other systems is possible, however, this issue should be clarified in advance with the administration of Sherwood Greenwood Resort. Also note that if you book a hotel room via the Internet, the hotel may withhold prepayment from you. In this case, at the time of discharge you will only have to pay the rest of the cost of living.





Placement of children

Since this hotel is mainly family-friendly, children of all ages are allowed. At the same time, if you place a baby up to 2 years old on an additionally installed crib or a child under 12 years old on existing sleeping places, then you do not have to pay extra. Please note that in each room of the Sherwood Greenwood Resort 4 * hotel, it is possible to put only one crib. The possibility of installing additional beds for adults is not provided. If you need to place a baby cot in the room, please inform the hotel in advance.

Pet friendly

If you belong to the category of people traveling in the company of their four-legged pets, then this item will be important for you. So, in accordance with the rules of the hotel, no pets are allowed on its territory. Therefore, you will either have to leave your furry friend at home, or look for another pet-friendly hotel to stay.

I would like to note that regardless of the type of rooms, they are cleaned daily, and towels and bed linen are changed three times a week. Also, you always have the opportunity to order a room service.

Nutrition

Hotel "Sherwood Greenwood Resort" offers its guests meals on the system of "ultra all inclusive." It operates from 10 a.m. until midnight and includes Turkish alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as a number of foreign-made drinks. For a fee, guests are provided with freshly squeezed juice, bottled drinks and ice cream.

Five times a day (in addition to breakfast, lunch and dinner, there is also a brunch and nightly snacks) in the hotel's main restaurant, accommodating 400 guests indoors and 160 guests on the outdoor terrace, experienced chefs serve a buffet of dishes and desserts like local as well as international cuisines. Sea beach

The 4-star Sherwood Greenwood Hotel has its own pebbly and sandy beach. It is located 200 meters from the hotel, and on the way to it you will need to cross the road. On the beach there are sun loungers and parasols, the use of which is free for hotel guests. There is also a bar where you can refresh yourself with your favorite drinks and cocktails. On the beach you can rent equipment for water sports.

Entertainment at the hotel

The hotel has a large outdoor pool (950 sq. Meters), an indoor pool (60 sq. Meters) and a small pool for children. There are also two water slides for adults, and two slides suitable for even the smallest guests of the hotel. Near the pools there is a sunbathing terrace with sunbeds and umbrellas, as well as a bar.

All day until late in the evening, Sherwood Greenwood Resort 4 * hotel has animators who do everything possible to diversify leisure travelers. If you are tired of swimming in the sea and the pool and sunbathing, you can play indoor soccer, table or tennis, basketball and billiards, ride a bike, go to the gym or enjoy water sports. In the evenings, a bright and fascinating show takes place in the amphitheater of the hotel complex, after which an incendiary disco begins.
