Review webcam Logitech C270

The Logitech C270 webcam is considered one of the most inexpensive among similar devices from a world-famous manufacturer. In the article below, we will understand how it fits into its price category, whether it needs to be purchased, or is it still worth looking at another model.

Equipment

The camera is on sale in rather big and high-quality packaging. She immediately attracts attention due to the pleasant design. At the bottom, you can see detailed and important information about the device. On the side of the box is the same data, but in more detail, in addition, special information has been added. On the back surface you can see a comparison of this model with other cameras that will allow you to make a choice. At the bottom of the device, the manufacturer printed the computer system requirements.

The package includes a software disc and documentation. The warranty on the device extends to the first two years of use.





Specifications

The manufacturer boldly considers himself one of the best creators of webcams. He claims that through the use of modern technology, the named device manages to achieve a clear picture, bright colors and clear sound even in noise conditions.

Video is recorded in HD. The resolution of the picture is 1280x720 pixels. In addition, the Logitech C270 webcam, the price of which ranges from 1,100 to 2,700 rubles, is capable of taking 3 megapixel photos. She can independently adjust the correction of light.

The device has a fixed focus. You can take photos and videos with just one click. The instrument cable has a length of 1.5 meters.

Requirements

The described device can work with Windows operating systems: XP / Vista / 7/8/10. In addition, it works great with macbooks.

In order to make a video call using this camera, it is necessary that the processor has a frequency of 1 GHz, RAM - 512 MB and 200 MB of free memory on your hard drive. There must be an Internet connection and a USB port version 1.1.

From this we can conclude that the camera is able to work even on the weakest computer. But to get the maximum video quality, you should have a processor with a capacity of 2.4 GHz, 2 GB of RAM. The speed of the Internet should not be lower than 1 Mbit per second, and the screen resolution should be 1280x720 pixels.



Design

When buying a Webcam Logitech C270, many pay attention to the appearance of the device and only then begin to become interested in its characteristics. The device has a glossy plastic front. The rest of the body is made of the same plastic only matte. The device is black.

The camera looks pretty good. Under the grille on the front you can see not only the lens, but also the microphone. On the opposite side there is an LED that lights up with a green tint during a call. Inside the stand, if you take it apart, you will notice an elastic band. It is necessary for mounting to the monitor.





Programs

Often, buyers do not use the proprietary program to make calls from the manufacturer, because there is a well-known Skype, so in the latest versions of the device its offer has already been refused.

The camera can work seamlessly immediately after installation. Additionally, there is no need to download drivers. But many advise to use the native utility, since it significantly improves color and clarity. Moreover, it is equipped with an additional face tracking function. Thanks to her, the camera can monitor a person. It looks great, as it creates the feeling that the device is turning, but in fact, only optics work.

The installation package includes a special utility that allows you to start shooting videos or photos with a single key. It also has several functions and options that improve the process of using the device.





Finally

Many citizens say that they have often bought cheap webcams. However, only Logitech C270 was able to show a truly decent result. Given its quality and price category, it is quite possible to prefer it.

True, if a person is interested in shooting video with a resolution of 1920 √ó 1080 pixels (that is, Full HD) and the highest quality microphone, then you can take the option a little more expensive from the same manufacturer. They will perfectly perform the necessary functions. For the average user, the webcam described in the article is an excellent option.
