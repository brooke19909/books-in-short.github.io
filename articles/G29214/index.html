Vologda city: hotels. Addresses, reviews

Since ancient times, Vologda - the same age as Moscow - was considered the capital of the North. Ancient buildings, wooden architecture and dozens of museums - the city has a unique historical and cultural heritage.



The traditional symbols of Vologda are often called elegant lace and delicious butter. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay with family, work colleagues or friends. Vologda hotels are designed for a variety of travelers - in the city there are budget accommodation options and luxury apartments with spa services.

"Atrium"

For guests of the Atrium Hotel, a trip to the railway station takes only five minutes, and in fifteen minutes they can slowly reach the Vologda Kremlin.

The unusual building stands out in the city landscape. The hotel project was implemented on the initiative of the Club of Business Communication and the Government of the Vologda Oblast.

The Atrium Hotel (Vologda, Herzen Str. 27) is located in the administrative center, therefore it is most often stayed here during business trips.





Business class, luxury and apartments - regardless of room category, they are equipped with a refrigerator, TV and telephone. For fruitful work there is a desk and stationery.

For an additional fee, guests can use the service center with a computer and office equipment, as well as rent a conference room with all the necessary equipment.

The Atrium Restaurant is ideal for business meetings, negotiations or romantic dates. In the mornings, breakfast is served here according to the "buffet" system (cost 250 rubles), and during the day on weekdays there is a special offer called Express Lunch.

Guest opinion

The most modest room in the Atrium hotel, excluding breakfast, will cost 3800 rubles. For Vologda, this is above average, so for their money, guests expect appropriate living conditions and service.

Most of all, guests are disappointed with breakfasts, which hardly fit the definition of "buffet". A small selection of food and drinks, instant coffee - the picture is rather sad for a business hotel.

Minuses:

  • lack of air conditioning;
  • weak Wi-Fi signal;
  • lack of a first aid kit;
  • high prices in the restaurant;
  • poor water pressure.

Pros:

  • location in the city center;
  • spacious rooms;
  • Friendly staff.




Immediately after the opening, the Atrium was considered one of the best in such a beautiful city as Vologda. Competitor hotels worked tirelessly on their own reputation and service, and over time Atrium ceased to stand out.
