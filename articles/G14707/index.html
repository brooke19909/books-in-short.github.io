The best hotels of Chernihiv

Who would have thought that there are more hotels in the Chernihiv region than in Kiev itself. Yes, this is true, and each of them is different and interesting in its own way. But tourists coming there cannot decide which hotel to stay in, because there are so many of them. This article will be a short description of the most visited institutions of Chernihiv.

Chernihiv hotels: prices and conditions

In each hotel in Chernihiv, prices primarily depend on its location. If the hotel is located in the city center, then the amount will naturally be greater, but the farther, the lower the cost. Also, the presence of stars in a hotel plays a big role, if there are three, then you will have to pay up to $ 70 per day. If there are four or five stars, then the total amount can go up to almost $ 200. In summer, the cost of living rises, as there are many tourists.

Choosing inexpensive hotels in Chernihiv with a small number of stars, it is worth remembering that there are low standards of service. And in expensive hotels, guests expect a wonderful view from the windows and excellent service. It should be noted: hotels in the city center are better because all the sights of Chernihiv are at an affordable distance. In any case, the choice is always up to the guests.

"Ukraine" - one of the hotels in Chernihiv

In 1961, in the Chernihiv region, the well-known hotel "Ukraine" was opened. It is located 200 meters from the center and therefore attracts visitors. Near the hotel there are all amenities: shops, cafes, cinema, banks and much more. You can find it at the address: Chernihiv city, Prospekt Mira, 33. The hotel goes to the fourth star.





Hotel services:

  • 91 rooms are available, and there are special apartments for people with disabilities.
  • All rooms have televisions, mini-refrigerators, safes and air conditioners.
  • Hot and cold water comes around the clock.
  • Wi-fi is free.
  • The hotel has a meeting room with modern equipment for 30 seats.
  • Any vacationer of the Chernihiv hotel can have lunch, breakfast or dinner at the restaurant, which is open 24 hours a day. There is a wide selection: Ukrainian, Russian, French cuisine plus a sushi bar. Order can be made in the room.
  • The hotel has the opportunity to purchase some kind of memorable gift.
  • A few meters from the Ukraina Hotel is Detinets, it is called the center of the historic city.

Hotel "Comfort"

Hotel Uyut (Chernihiv) is located in a wonderful quiet area. All unusual decisions of designers were displayed in it. Charming, pleasing to the eye interior creates a picture of home comfort. Mini-hotel "Comfort" is suitable for family pastime. And you can find it at the address: city of Chernihiv, Sergei Efremov street, 1.





All amenities for a good stay are included:

  • The rooms are cozy and bright: a regular room costs 350 hryvnias (900 rubles) per day; Junior Suite - 450 UAH (1100 p.), And Suite - 600 UAH (1400 p.).
  • In the hotel "Comfort" specially for the guests made a mini-kitchen.
  • Highly qualified and friendly hotel staff will always help and advise.
  • The hotel has free internet.
  • For those arriving by car, there is a special parking zone.

Hotel "Profsoyuznaya" (Chernihiv)

The structure of the "Trade Union" is a bit like the hotel "Ukraine". And this is despite the fact that all Chernihiv hotels are completely different. The comfortable hotel is designed for 81 guests, 45 rooms are available. You can find it at the address: Taras Shevchenko street, 105a.





The creators tried to do everything to justify their name:

  • There is a conference room for business meetings and events.
  • The cafe is divided into 3 rooms, where you can enjoy delicious homemade dishes, and there is also a menu for banquets.
  • Library.
  • High-quality free Internet, which can be used anywhere in the hotel.
  • A few steps from the hotel there is a cultural park for recreation and entertainment.
  • Since the "Trade Union" (hotel) is located on the territory of the Desna River, it is possible to go down to the beach.
  • Nearby are many shopping centers.
  • And the cost is almost the same as in the Hotel Uyut: standard room - 300 hryvnia (800 rubles), standard room for two - 540 UAH (1300 rubles), and a suite for two - 600 UAH (1500 rubles).

Park-hotel "Chernihiv"

All Chernihiv hotels are original in their own way. But there is one hotel that visiting tourists highly praise. It is located at: Shevchenko street, 103a.





The park-hotel "Chernigov" created in a simple style is considered the best in the city. There is everything you need for a real holiday:

  • The Desna River is 400 meters away, at any time you can admire it from the windows.
  • A distinctive feature is that all rooms are soundproofed.
  • Each room has a comfortable bed, flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a minibar.
  • The hotel has a gorgeous pool.
  • Wireless and free wifi.
  • Also available is a room service for food or drink.

Arriving in a city like Chernigov, you will not stay without an overnight stay. There are an insignificant number of hotels, so you can choose for every taste.
