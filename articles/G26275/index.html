Rest in the mountains in Sochi: features, an overview of the best hotels, photos and reviews

Holidays in the mountains in Sochi is a great opportunity to enjoy the local unique nature and clean air. And you can come here on vacation throughout the year. If you are attracted to hiking on the mountain peaks, then here you will get a brilliant opportunity to go on such a trip. Joint overcoming of obstacles and difficulties along the way will bring family members or companies closer together, improve the atmosphere in the team, and help forget about old grievances. It is especially important to go to the mountains with a large family. Together with the children, parents will learn how to support each other, together overcome difficulties.

About tourism

Rest in the mountains in Sochi is an extreme sport that will require you to overcome yourself and transport more heaviness at altitude. Tourists face serious physical exertion, due to which some will be able to lose weight.

Be sure that holidays in the mountains in Sochi will appeal to all tourism lovers. Upon reaching the summit, indescribable sensations arise, this is one of the strongest feelings that a person is able to experience. Thus, you will be able to, after covering a distance of only two to three kilometers on foot, become a real conqueror of peaks.

A walking trip along the slopes, which is also called tracking, which is widespread in these places, is designed even for people without experience in overcoming such obstacles.

During a vacation in the mountains in Sochi, travelers will have an amazing opportunity to visit the Zmeykovsky waterfalls in the Krasnaya Polyana area, to explore local landscapes.

If you decide to spend your holidays in the fresh air, then it's time to start looking for the best hotels or recreation centers located in the mountains. About the most interesting and attractive options that may interest really active tourists, we will tell in this article.

Recreation Center "Sochi"

The recreation center "Sochi" on Lysaya Gora is located on the Black Sea coast in the city center. It is about one kilometer from the sea, the recreation center itself is located on the territory of the Svetlana microdistrict.

The territory of the recreation center "Sochi" on Lysaya Gora is literally immersed in greenery, especially there are many subtropical plants. Nearby is a park with extremely rare plants that are collected from all over the world.

Vacationers here will be able to accommodate in separate well-appointed and comfortable cottages or a five-story dormitory building. Most rooms have amazing balconies with park and sea views from their balconies. All rooms are double or triple, there are all amenities, and if necessary, visitors are available deluxe rooms.

How to get there

The exact address of the Sochi recreation center is Sochi, Lysaya Gora, 34. If you fly by plane, you can land at the airport in Adler.

From Adler to Sochi for a vacation in the mountains in the Krasnodar Territory, it is best to take a bus to the Svetlana stop. After this, you will need to transfer to bus number 44 or number 92. Drive to Lysaya Gora Street.

There is an option how to get to this recreation center in the mountains of Sochi by rail. By the North Caucasian railway you have to get to the Sochi station. From here you need to take a bus or minibus at number 92. Next, get to your destination in the same way as from the airport.

"Krasnaya Polyana"

One of the most comfortable recreation centers in the mountains of Sochi is located about fifty kilometers from the city itself. It is located at the foot of the Main Caucasus Range at an altitude of 730 meters above sea level. Here is a unique sea and mountain climate, which turns the entire village of Krasnaya Polyana into one of the best resorts in all of Europe.

From here, regular and simple trips are organized, which you can go to unprepared travelers, even children will take them out. Among the visitors, excursions to the Caucasian State Biosphere Reserve are very popular, they can walk around the lakes, the peaks of the Main Caucasian Range, waterfalls, and alpine meadows.

No wonder Krasnaya Polyana is considered one of the most promising domestic mountain resorts. In winter, it is sunny and a lot of snow, and air temperature rarely drops below ten degrees of frost at night, and during the day it varies from zero to five degrees of heat.

Stable snow cover lasts from late December to mid-April. At the same time, the depth of snow is from two and a half to three meters.

Guest Services

According to reviews of holidays in the mountains of Sochi in Krasnaya Polyana, travelers note that many are attracted by the large list of services that is included in the price of the ticket.

The recreation center itself is located at: Krasnaya Polyana village, 5 Defenders of the Caucasus Street.

The cost of the voucher, which vacationers purchase, includes accommodation in a room of the corresponding category, three meals a day according to the "Custom Menu" system, one hour a day swimming in an indoor pool, which is open all year round. Judging by the reviews, many are attracted by the fact that this is a vacation in the mountains of Sochi and the pool can be visited.

Guests have the opportunity to use the gym one hour a day, visit the library, summer tanning salon, sports and children's playgrounds, and tennis courts. In the evenings, discos and dance evenings are organized here, from June to October - study tours of Krasnaya Polyana by bus.

For a fee, you can play billiards, go to the sauna, and rent ski and tourist equipment.

Hotel-cottage "Vanderbel"

In autumn, holidays in the mountains of Sochi can be arranged for you at the Vanderbel hotel-cottage. In fact, this is a resort with a homely and cozy atmosphere, which is located in the famous village of Krasnaya Polyana.

Just three hundred meters from here is a bus stop and a heliport. The cottage is rented out completely for ten to twelve people. The distance from the ski lifts to the ski slopes is three to six kilometers, depending on which particular ski slope you prefer. This is the best place to spend a vacation in a large company of friends or a friendly family.

It is worth emphasizing that you can only book rooms by telephone, as no online booking system provides such services. However, the number of people who want to take a break from this does not decrease at all.

The rooms have a mini fridge, TV, in the bathrooms - bath, shower and hairdryer. On the street there are places for cooking barbecue, and there is also a landscaped area to have a great time in the fresh air.

The cottage is rented at least for a day. For guests there is a special kitchen-dining room in which they can cook their own meals. It is also possible to order three meals a day. In this case, you will have to pay 1,100 rubles for each guest. The cottage also has karaoke, a home cinema, a hookah, a fireplace room with a cigar room, a sauna.

The hotel has a courtyard with barbecue, parking, artificial pond with a mill and waterfalls, a hammock, a Chinese house, a swing.

Renting this holiday home in the mountains of Sochi will cost from thirty thousand rubles a day at the peak of the season, and in autumn and spring the cost drops to fifteen thousand rubles a day for the whole cottage. The use of the bathhouse and parking are included in this price.

Guest house "Flamingo"

Another option for relaxing in the mountains of Sochi in summer is the inexpensive Flamingo Hotel. It is located in the village of Esto-Sadok. It has comfortable and clean rooms with a fridge and TV.

There are very budget accommodation options: economy and youth. In this case, the bathroom is on the floor, with the rest of the rooms there is a bathroom with a shower in each room, and the suites also have a bathtub.

It offers a home cinema, a cafe-bar with karaoke, a dryer for equipment, rental equipment and skis, ATVs and mountain bikes. The bathhouse has a tea room with a samovar and a fireplace, as well as a pool. There is a guest room and a kitchen equipped with all necessary appliances, in the courtyard there is a covered gazebo.

The cost of an economy class room at the peak of the season will be 1,800 rubles, rooms with all amenities will cost about five thousand rubles in one day. It must be emphasized that the price includes the use of a dryer for equipment, breakfast and a shuttle to the ski slopes.

Hotel Solis Sochi

This is a comfortable five-star hotel, which is located eleven kilometers from Krasnaya Polyana and 73 kilometers from the city of Sochi itself. From here you can go down to the Black Sea coast or climb the mountains.

The hotel is located directly next to the slopes, which are equipped with slopes for snowboarding and skiing, as well as cafes and ski lifts. Horse, walking and helicopter rides, canyoning and rafting are regularly organized for guests. The ski concierge is ready to provide any assistance to the guests. Also on site are a restaurant, spa, grill bar, and a rooftop terrace heated all year round.

Each room has an interactive TV, a safe, a telephone, a touch panel over the bed, a kettle, an iron, and the necessary utensils. In the bathroom there is a shower and a bath with underfloor heating, as well as all kinds of bath accessories.

The cost of each issue per day is about five thousand rubles. This price includes breakfast, which is organized according to the buffet system.

Radisson Rosa Khutor Hotel and others

One of the most famous and comfortable hotels in these places is called "Radisson Rosa Khutor". This is a five-star hotel just two kilometers from the village of Krasnaya Polyana. If you want to relax with complete comfort, then you probably should not find a better place. In 2015, it was officially recognized as the best ski resort in the whole country. The hotel has the highest ratings and excellent reviews among tourists.

It offers rooms from standard to executive suites. There is the possibility of accommodating guests with disabilities.

All rooms have satellite TV, minibar, hairdryer, safe, iron.

It offers guests a sauna, a fitness room with a personal instructor, a hammam, a night club, massage, children's professional animators, a room for storing equipment and ski equipment, bicycle rental is organized.

The hotel has a bar and a restaurant serving international cuisine. The hotel itself is located in close proximity to two ski lifts: Strela and Olympia.

At peak season, room rates reach fifteen thousand rubles a day. The price includes breakfast buffet, as well as the thermal zone in the spa, gym, Wi-Fi and tea delivery to your room.

It is worth emphasizing that many Sochi hotels are very popular among tourists. Much of this depends on their successful location.

Such places include the chalet hotel "Mountain Olympia". It is located on the Khmelevsky lakes in the village of Krasnaya Polyana.

Each room has a bathroom and a TV, while the two-room apartments also have a living room with a fireplace. The hotel has two saunas with steam rooms and relaxation rooms, several banquet rooms, gazebos, a complex of "BBQ", parking and a cafe.

The hotel is three kilometers from the ski slopes and ski lifts. In addition, the hotel has its own ski slope, called "Amateur". Guests can rent snowboards, skis and other sports equipment. The hotel itself has bars, restaurants, a fitness center with a swimming pool, a sauna, a jacuzzi, a terrace where you can take sunbaths, five conference rooms and a business center. There is a club house with a cafe-restaurant serving Russian and European cuisine.

Each room has a home theater, telephone, wired high-speed Internet, air conditioning, satellite TV. The bathrooms have underfloor heating, some have washing machines.
