Beach availability

The hotel has its own sandy beach with a length of 350 meters. Near the coast, the sea is very shallow, and for the convenience of guests, a pier was built, on which it is easy to get to deeper places. Vacationers can choose deeper or shallower areas for swimming. In some places in the water there are many marine life and corals. But there are also sections of the sea where the fauna is practically absent and you can swim for your pleasure. Nearby is a coral reef, which tourists often admire.

Difficult bathing often becomes the main cause of indignation of guests. But for people with young children, this is a real paradise where you can not worry about the safety of their kids. There are shells at the bottom of the sea, so it is better to use special bathing shoes to avoid troubles.



Sunbeds are on the beach and on the pier, which is very convenient for lovers of swimming and sunbathing. There are plenty of free places. Vacationers are provided with mattresses, umbrellas and beach towels. For a bite to eat or quench your thirst, go to the beach bar. Here guests are offered french fries, various sandwiches or pizza.

Infrastructure

Hotel Titanic Palace Spa & AquaPark 5 * offers guests various opportunities to have a wonderful and useful time. The first thing that pleases guests is the pools, one of which is heated. Judging by the reviews of tourists, these reservoirs are kept clean, there are always enough sunbeds. You can have a great time in the water park, where there are slides for adults and children.

On the territory of the hotel and beyond, there are many shops in which tourists are offered a fairly large selection of souvenirs or other goods. There are currency exchange, parking and laundry facilities.

For fans to always keep themselves in shape there is an opportunity to work out in the gym. For an additional fee, you can use the services of the SPA-center, steam room, jacuzzi or sauna. A massage therapist is working in the center. You can put yourself in order in the beauty salon and in the hairdresser, too, for a certain price. If you have health problems, you can contact your doctor for help. Many people who have used the services of a local doctor advise you to study several phrases in Arabic that are necessary to explain frequently occurring problems. The doctor working at the hotel is far from a polyglot and practically does not understand the Russian language.

For business people the opportunity to use the three conference rooms. Two halls have excellent technical equipment.

Animation at the hotel works by the central pool. The views are often quite fascinating.

Internet access is paid. As tourists who visited the hotel advise, it is much more profitable to purchase a special SIM card. You can also use the services of an internet corner.

Check in and rooms

In the reviews of tourists there are practically no complaints about settlement. The reception is open around the clock, so you can get room keys at any time. If delays occur, you will be offered to sit comfortably in a very beautiful and cozy lobby with excellent upholstered furniture.

- Standard Room - 338 beds.

- Family Room - 50 beds.

- Family Room 2 Bedrooms - 90 beds.

- Bungalow Room - 47 beds.

The hotel also has a room where everything is equipped for a comfortable stay for people with disabilities.



The rooms are quite spacious and comfortable. The furniture is new, all appliances are in working condition. The interior is not satisfactory. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, LCD-TV, minibar with free water, safe. The bathroom has a shower, hairdryer and toiletries. Depending on the category of rooms, some bathrooms have a jacuzzi.

Only adults are allowed to settle in the bungalow. Since the rooms are located directly above the pool, it is dangerous for children to stay here.

Rooms are cleaned daily; linen is changed three times a week. The staff is very courteous at the Titanic Palace Spa & AquaPark 5 * hotel (reviews of tourists confirm this) and when complaints arise, quickly eliminates all the shortcomings.

Nutrition

The food system in the hotel Titanic Palace Spa & AquaPark 5 * - Royal All Inclusive. The two main restaurants provide breakfast, lunch and dinner. The menu is quite varied, and guests will not have to starve. Often there are complaints of too spicy food. The restaurants offer diet meals. Young guests are provided with a children's menu.

There is enough space in restaurants and you can always find yourself a free table. The service is good, there are practically no queues.

Entertainment at the hotel Titanic Palace Spa & AquaPark 5 * (Hurghada)

For a pleasant pastime guests are offered a huge selection of various entertainments. Classes are held systematically with an aerobics or aqua aerobics instructor. The hotel has courts where tennis fans can play. On a special platform you can fight volleyball. Guests can also play table tennis, darts or billiards.

The hotel offers to try out many water sports. There is a diving center here, which will help to master scuba diving.

Evenings at the hotel do not have to be bored. Guests here are entertained by various show programs. Disco is open until the morning.

Children's leisure





No less interesting will be able to spend time and young guests of the hotel. Kids will have fun on a special playground or in the games room, attend a kids club or a cinema. If parents want to leave for a while, they can use the services of a professional nanny for a certain cost.

Nearby Activities

Guests will appreciate the variety of excursions that are offered at the Titanic Palace Spa & AquaPark 5 * hotel. Hurghada is a very beautiful city, which often organizes excursions. Its sights can impress even a seasoned tourist.

Glass bottom boat excursions are also available. Since the fauna in the sea is quite diverse, guests can enjoy the sea inhabitants. During such excursions, guests are invited to go fishing.

Impressions of tourists

Often, people who relaxed at the Titanic Palace Spa & AquaPark 5 * hotel, whose photo can be seen in the article, leave positive feedback. Most of all, tourists like the exterior of the hotel. They also speak well of cleanliness, which contains both the entire territory of the hotel and the premises. But cleaning the beach leaves much to be desired. According to some tourists, trash can be seen in the water.



Not very flattering speak about the animation. It works only at the main pool, although the hotel has a beautiful amphitheater. The performances are also not very impressive for the guests. Many complain about the monotony in the repertoire.

The guests are not satisfied with the difficulty in swimming, but this cannot be regarded as a complaint to the hotel. On the other hand, the parents of young children were very pleased with the rest, because they could not worry about their safety.

Many positive reviews are addressed to the hotel staff. All employees are very respectful and friendly to guests. They are always ready to help and resolve issues.

Despite the shortcomings, most tourists recommend the hotel Titanic Palace Spa & AquaPark 5 * as a pretty good place for an exciting vacation.
