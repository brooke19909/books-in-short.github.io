Park-hotel "Goncharov": overview, features, services and reviews

People who come to the hotel to relax and have a good time in the company of relatives or friends always dream of a cozy atmosphere and interesting entertainments. Therefore, hotel staff is guided by these criteria when creating their institution. Some hotels are located in the city center, where there are many historical monuments that are interesting to visit. Others are located in the countryside, and their guests can enjoy relaxing in the lap of nature. A hotel that combines all these advantages is described in the sections of this article.

Park Hotel Goncharov: general information

This hotel is suitable for both business trips and family vacations and festive events. The hotel is located in the Kaluga region, in the village of Polotnyany Zavod, on Trudovaya Street.

It is located in an old manor - the estate of the Goncharovs aristocrats. Therefore, its name corresponds to the surname of these famous historical figures.

Park Hotel "Goncharov" in the Linen Factory - the village of Kaluga Region - is the first hotel created in this village. Due to the beautiful atmosphere and good quality of service, the hotel has earned a good reputation among the guests. The hotel with an elegant interior and comfortable rooms will appeal to even the most demanding client. In addition, the hotel is located next to the coast of the river, in an area rich in history.

About the building

Many hotels are located in areas where there are a large number of attractions, such as hotels on the Polyanka in Moscow. Goncharov Park Hotel is a building that used to house the estate of famous Russian aristocrats. This place is one of the most famous cultural objects both in Russia and abroad. The factory was located on the estate, and its architecture has not changed much since the building was still completely new. This building is a complex of premises designed for both production and everyday life. Many are interested in this historical site and specially come to the Goncharov Park Hotel to see it.

Rooms and service

In the hotel complex "Goncharov" rooms for guests are provided both standard and with high comfort. There are three types of rooms in the hotel:

  1. A room designed for two guests. There is one or two beds, a bathroom.
  2. Room for three persons. There are beds (one or two) and air conditioning.
  3. Double room. There are two beds, a bathroom.




In the Goncharov Park Hotel, room rates vary from two to two and a half thousand rubles per day.

Dining at the hotel provides a cafe of the same name. The design of this institution is original and sophisticated, reminiscent of the atmosphere of the early twentieth century. Many guests were delighted with the atmosphere in which they were able to immerse themselves when they visited the cafe.

The venue is perfect for gatherings with friends, dinner with a loved one, or for celebrations such as a birthday or a wedding.

Event Organization

Many newlyweds choose a banquet hall of the Goncharov Park Hotel for a wedding. Those who celebrated the holiday in this institution can say with confidence that the cafe staff should be entrusted with organizing their holiday.

The employees of the banquet hall will help the bride and groom to choose dishes for the wedding table, as well as an entertainment program. The celebration room is beautifully decorated, flower bouquets are selected for decoration, taking into account the wishes of the newlyweds. The ceremony can be held by employees of the civil registry office. In the park-hotel, a gazebo is specially reserved for this, where the great poet Pushkin made an offer to his beloved, a representative of a noble family.

Entertainment and recreation opportunities

The hotel is located on the coast. It offers boat rental. Floating on the river, guests of the establishment can enjoy beautiful landscapes.

Guests of the park-hotel "Goncharov" are offered the opportunity for active pastime, both in winter and in summer. They can rent bicycles in the warm season, skates, skis or sledges in the cold. The hotel also offers to try relaxing in an unusual place - in a floating bath. This is a great opportunity not only to improve health, but also to enjoy beautiful landscapes, and to hold a solemn event in the company of relatives and friends.

Such a holiday will undoubtedly turn out to be original, bright and unforgettable.

Opinions of the guests about the institution

About the Goncharov Park Hotel reviews are both positive and negative. The guests usually call the advantages of the hotel a beautiful environment, high quality work of the staff, and many opportunities for outdoor activities (boat rental, bike, ski and skate rental). The guests of the restaurant are satisfied with the varied and tasty food offered in the cafe located on the hotel territory. They also appreciated the opportunity to visit interesting historical places. Most of the guests liked the rooms for living, they seemed comfortable and clean. In addition, many appreciated such advantages as comfortable beds, clean towels, regular bed linen, and recently renovated hotel rooms.



However, there are clients who are dissatisfied with the quality of the institution. Some say that people who arrived at the hotel in order to celebrate the wedding, prevented other guests from sleeping and relaxing with loud music. There are also clients who are not satisfied with the lack of items that provide comfort in the rooms (water cooler, tea, coffee, window screens to protect against insects). In addition, some complain that the living quarters are not warm enough and sometimes there are problems with plumbing equipment. People were also unhappy with the malfunctions of the wireless Internet. Visitors were disappointed by the lack of refrigerators in rooms and a grocery store near the hotel complex. In addition, the rental of sports equipment seemed some too expensive service.
