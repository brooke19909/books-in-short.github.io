About cutting and sewing

The pattern of men's shorts is done in two ways.

First way

Modeling shorts on the main pattern of trousers. Here you will need to calculate the length of the future product, make allowances for a free fit and remove the tuck tucks. The first two points are clear, but what about the last? There is nothing complicated about it. Shorts at the waist are usually made with an elastic band and a lace; in most cases, the trouser leg is straight from the hip. Therefore, to align the outer section of the leg, you need to remove the tuck. For this, a perpendicular is drawn from its top to the bottom, along this line the pattern of men's shorts is cut to the top and aligned at the waist. Increases are made at the side seams so that the shorts can be easily worn.



If the style provides for a standard trouser fit and a zipper, then it will be enough to adjust only the length. Such a construction of a pattern of shorts is used by professional cutters.

Second way

He‚Äôs a little clumsy, but if you don‚Äôt have the basic base of trousers of the right size at hand, you just can‚Äôt do without it. In this case, the pattern of men's shorts is done as follows:

  • we take our favorite trousers, we turn inside out, we put one leg in another and put it on paper;
  • alternately circle the back and front halves, unfolding the trousers so as to correctly transfer the lines and bends;
  • we make allowances for a free fit and the desired length of the workpiece.

Depending on the chosen style, shorts can be modeled using a pattern made in one way or another. So, for example, in the side seams it is easy to leave decorative cuts with rounded edges of the front and rear halves. Side inserts made of contrasting fabric will look interesting, and these can be not only directly vertical lines. The pattern of men's shorts can be cut into several parts with smooth lines or sharp corners, however, it should be remembered - the more shaped parts, the harder the assembly of the product.
