TV LG 49UJ630V: reviews, specifications, instructions

In 2017, LG released a whole line of new LG 630v TVs. Their sizes vary from 43 to 65 inches. Almost the entire series is positioned as a budget option, but this does not mean that it does not have unproductive fillings and interesting ‚Äúchips‚Äù. In this article, the LG 49UJ630V TV will be considered, its features, functionality, as well as reviews of its lucky owners.

Description

The appearance of the LG 49UJ630V TV is not unusual. All the material that was used for the production is plastic. No stylistic refinements or decorative elements are observed.





Almost all interfaces and connectors are located on the back of the TV. Therefore, if you install the gadget directly on the wall, then reaching them will be problematic. And you still have to go there, since it is here that the LAN, HDMI, optical output for audio, analog antenna input, and video connection in the form of tulips are located. A bit strange, but this TV has only one USB port, which is also located on the rear panel. Next to it there are two more HDMI ports. The control panel is located at the bottom of the TV.

Image output

The picture quality indicators of the device are not the highest. For example, in a bright room with good lighting, gray tones will be observed instead of black. Viewing angles are also not the largest, so watching from the side of the TV will not always be convenient.

Trumotion function

Test LG 49UJ630V showed that this function as a whole performs its role. And it serves precisely to draw dynamic scenes. Of course, the limitations impose a bit on the response frequency of the pixel, so sometimes small loops can be observed behind moving objects.

Device management

The TV uses the Web OS platform as software. It is based, as was to be expected on the Linux kernel. The main feature is that it can be built into almost any technology. This device presents the system version 3.5. It is also equipped with a built-in DLNA client, which is able to detect compatible devices nearby, with which you can transfer data to a TV.





Setting up and controlling the LG 49UJ630V can be done using a special Magic Remote. True, you have to buy it separately. A gyroscope is built into it, with which you can control the cursor on the TV screen. In this case, you can not even direct the remote at the screen. It has a special scroll wheel, similar to that located on an ordinary computer mouse. Using it is convenient to browse sites on the Internet. Well, and an interesting feature is a voice search. By pressing a special key you need to pronounce a phrase and the TV will start searching by keywords. Some recent models may include the Ivi online movie call button.

More technical features

The TV screen resolution is 3840 by 2160 pixels. The display itself is an LCD type. Screen size as already mentioned 49 inches. The TV features an active type of HDR technology that allows you to correctly handle high-contrast scenes. The total power of the speakers is 20 watts. Supports Ultra Surround technology, creating a spectacular surround sound. On board there is also the so-called Magic Sound Tuning technology. With its help, you can adjust the sound based on the analyzed room. More precisely, the system will do everything by itself.

For the processing of incoming sound, that is, the voice, Clear Voice 3 algorithms are responsible. The codecs that the TV is capable of playing are a fairly extensive list: MP3, AAC and other well-known formats.





You can also control the TV from a smartphone. There is a special application LG TV Plus for this. If you just need to establish a connection with the TV from the phone, then there is the Mobile Connection program. To expand the resources of a personal computer on a TV, WiDi technology is available. And if you need to distribute content from your mobile - Miracast.

The TV has a built-in light sensor, the so-called White Sensor, which can adjust the brightness to the situation. The power consumption of the device is about 0.3 watts in standby mode.





The TV has an interesting Simplink feature. In short, it allows you to control devices that support this type of technology from the TV remote. To do this, connect the device to the HDMI port of the TV. Then, in the settings, select Simplink and put it on. It is possible to synchronize two devices - a TV and an external gadget. In this case, when you turn on, for example, a media player, the TV will turn on.

TV Reviews Analysis

As the review showed, the LG 49UJ630V is a very budget option. That is, gourmets of good quality are better off looking for more expensive models. When playing scenes that are too dynamic, some distortion and loops will follow, reaching for objects. Although reviews of the LG 49UJ630V show that with the right settings, you can achieve a pretty acceptable image.





Playback of numerous formats can be recorded as a plus of this device. Appearance is not burdened with decorative elements, it looks simple, but at the same time stylish.

Another undoubted advantage of the TV is the support for the free operating system Web OS. The network always has various options for firmware and software for it. In general, the Linux-based system itself is fairly stable and reliable.

Judging by the reviews of the LG 49UJ630V, one can distinguish low brightness, incorrectly displayed black color in some cases, and inaccurate pixel structure of the RGB standard among the disadvantages of TVs.

conclusions

Judging by the reviews on the LG 49UJ630V, this TV is more than a decent model for its money. It has all the necessary functionality that flagships can boast of.
