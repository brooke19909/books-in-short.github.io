Viking Nona Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, service, reviews

Holidays in Turkey are very popular for many years. Tourists are attracted to all-inclusive hotels. Thus, rest is not burdened by domestic problems. In Viking Nona Beach hotel 4 * this system operates, and every tourist is surrounded by attention and comfort.

Where is?

The hotel is located in the center of the resort town of Kemer. Distance from the airport is 55 km. You can get here by taxi or book a transfer, which must be agreed in advance at the travel agency.





This complex belongs to the category of Kemer hotels on the first line with an all-inclusive service. In 2006, a complete reconstruction of the hotel and the surrounding area was carried out.

Hotel Features

The appearance of the hotel is not much different from the standard complexes in Turkey. A white 4-storey building with many balconies and having a small, very well-groomed area around.

There is completely no animation. Therefore, very often this complex is chosen by people who do not like noisy events, but who come here for a quiet rest and silence.

The hotel has friendly staff. Mostly they speak English and Turkish, but there are workers who speak Russian well enough.

The complex provides a doctor’s services for a fee, who can receive patients daily from 10.00 to 12.00. A nurse is on duty at the hotel from 9.00 to 17.30.

Viking Nona Beach hotel 4 *: room description

In the hotel all rooms are designed in a classic style. There is a minimum of furniture and the rooms are quite free and comfortable. The hotel offers tourists standard-class rooms. There are 94 of them in the hotel.

They can accommodate from two to 5 people. The rooms have a double bed and 1-2 single beds, depending on the number of guests. Many rooms have a private small balcony. It has a table and comfortable garden chairs.





Windows overlook the surroundings of the city or the sea. Thus, every vacationer can enjoy a beautiful view in the evenings with a glass of wine.

The hotel has one room that is suitable for people with disabilities. It provides all the nuances in the domestic respect, which enable disabled people to feel free and comfortable.

Room amenities

All rooms have modern split systems. There are TVs with cable and satellite channels, you can watch Russian programs. Every room in the hotel offers guests hair dryer, bathtub.

For an extra charge, you can use the minibar and safe. Also, telephones are installed in the rooms for communication with employees at the reception and calls abroad are acceptable for a separate payment.

The beds in the rooms are large enough and equipped with orthopedic mattresses. Cold and hot water is supplied without interruption. Large windows make it possible to enjoy a beautiful view of the sea and the surrounding area.

Viking Nona Beach hotel 4 *: service

At the hotel, the workers are kind to all residents. Any domestic problem is solved very quickly. Maids change bedding every three days.

Rooms are cleaned daily. Delivery of food and other necessary things is done by staff for a fee. The hotel has an experienced nanny. Her services are paid separately.

Dry cleaning and laundry services are available on site. You can also make currency exchange at the hotel. People with disabilities who relax at the hotel are given extra attention. The hotel staff is ready to help them at any time of the day in moving or solving household issues.

Dining at the hotel

The hotel provides full three meals a day. Between the main meals, light snacks are offered. The main menu necessarily includes meat and fish dishes.

A variety of cereals and potato dishes are served. For each meal, large trays with a variety of fruits are required. For dessert, baked goods, cocktails, ice cream.

A special menu is available for young vacationers at Viking Nona Beach hotel 4. Meals here you can choose a separate kid: boiled meat, dairy products, light first courses, quality sausages.

Snacks are served fruits and small sandwiches. Such a variety of dishes is offered to vacationers at every meal. Tourists independently choose their favorite items from the menu and can take them in unlimited quantities.





The dining room is decorated like a good classic-style restaurant. It has large French-style windows with sea views. The furniture here is very comfortable, and the staff is always ready to help with the choice of dishes or with the solution of other issues regarding the menu.

The cost of food is included in the price of the hotel. Thus, vacationers do not have to look for cafes and restaurants for food during the holidays. The hotel has experienced chefs who carefully monitor the quality of the products that are used for cooking.

Pools and beach

Near the hotel there is a promenade. This area is designed for walking tourists. Sidewalks are laid out with special tiles and places to relax in the shade are organized.

Behind the promenade is a pebble beach. It belongs to the territory of Viking Nona Beach hotel 4 Kemer and guests of the hotel have a rest there. There are sun loungers on the beach, which tourists can use for free.





Towels, parasols and mattresses are also provided. The length of the beach is 100 meters, so there is enough space for all hotel guests and there is never any debate about this.

At Viking Nona Beach hotel 4, a beach rescue service is constantly available. Garbage is cleaned daily, so guests can walk around without shoes without fear of damaging their legs.

The hotel has a swimming pool. Special staff takes care of everyone. Several times a day, water is cleaned from small debris using special nets. In the morning, clean the bottom with a suitable vacuum cleaner. Water goes through several levels of purification.

The pool has an area of ​​140 m2 and an unusual shape. This size makes it possible for everyone to swim freely. Around him are comfortable sun loungers and large parasols.

Entertainment

Viking Nona Beach hotel 4 * adheres to its concept and does not use the services of animators. Thus, tourists who like to spend time in relative silence come here to relax.

The hotel is located in the center of the resort town, so there are a lot of places where you can have fun. There are a large number of restaurants, cafes, nightclubs and discos.

Also in the city you can visit the water park and centers with attractions. This resort has many places for walking. Fans of extreme relaxation are given the opportunity to go diving or surfing.

Hotel Territory

There are many places to relax around the hotel. The entire hotel is landscaped. Large spreading palm trees create a shadow. Here you can stroll along the equipped sidewalks and enjoy the aroma of flowers that adorn flowerbeds throughout.

Decorative small bridges give the area elegance and beauty. In the evening, the whole area is lit with original lights. The pool also has interior lighting. In the evening, hotel guests enjoy spending time here with a cup of aromatic coffee or with a glass of wine.

The entire hotel is guarded around the clock, and guests are completely safe.

Original place to stay

The hotel administration has allocated a separate zone for a comfortable pastime and communication for tourists who relax in the hotel. A special corner is allocated in the green zone on the territory, where soft pear chairs are placed.





Here, in the shade of palm trees, guests often gather and communicate with each other. Children love to play games in this area. Adults at this time can relax at the tables in the hot afternoon.

Often tourists here make new interesting acquaintances, and friendship continues after a vacation.

Rest at the hotel in different seasons

Kemer 15 years ago was an unremarkable village for tourists. Then began the rapid growth in popularity on holidays here. At a very fast pace, the small village has become a fairly large and modern resort.

The season for tourists here lasts from April to October. Usually the maximum influx of holidaymakers in this region is observed in July and August. At this time, the water in the sea is warm enough and the temperature in the street is very high.





With the advent of autumn, the velvet season begins. At this time, there is a lot of free space on the beaches, and the water temperature in the sea drops by several degrees compared to the summer months, but this does not stop even small children from swimming.

The weather in Kemer in September is milder and the heat no longer prevents tourists from enjoying their vacation. At this time, it can only be somewhat cool in the evenings, which does not scare away travelers, but allows them to enjoy the sea during the day and even sunbathe without harming their health.

Weather in Kemer in September and October makes it possible to use the rest time very productively. So, for example, in the summer months during the day it is almost impossible to attend excursions because of the heat. In autumn, there is a chance to see the sights of the region at any time of the day.

The hotel is also not empty during the winter months. There are many tourists resting here who make off-season trips at the lowest cost. A large number of spa services in the city makes it possible to enjoy a vacation at any time of the year.

Additional services

The hotel has a lobby bar. He is waiting for customers from 10.00 to 23.00. There is also a small cafe near the pool. Here, the tea time is set from 17.00 to 18.00 and the distribution of snacks from 10.00 to 11.00. Such services are included in the price. The rest of the time, guests can order drinks and light snacks here at an additional cost.

Throughout the hotel guests can use free WI-FI. For the newlyweds, a compliment from the hotel to the room is provided - romantic decor and wine with exotic fruits.

At the hotel there are playgrounds for active games for children. Also in the dining room, parents of babies can use special high chairs. At the request of tourists who come to rest with children under 5 years old, a cot with sides is installed.

Hotel reviews

On various Internet resources you can find many opinions about the rest in this hotel. In 95% of the reviews are positive. The description of rooms and services on various sites and in travel agencies is true.

Tourists are amazed by the beauty and arrangement of the territory of the Viking Nona Beach hotel 4 * . Reviews of the service are also written in a positive way. Guests enjoy spending time at the reception with a cup of tea and read the latest press. Workers are always friendly and welcome guests only with a smile.





Room cleaning is done on time. The beds are covered with high-quality bedding, and it is changed quite often. In the bathroom, a hygiene kit is always regularly updated, consisting of disposable shampoos, conditioners, bath gels and soaps.

The dining room is organized at the highest level. A wide variety of dishes is offered for each meal. The hotel provides additional snacks and desserts throughout the day. Many vacationers write that after a hearty breakfast or lunch there is no desire to take advantage of additional food.

On the beaches, tourists note, a sufficient number of sun loungers and parasols. Near the pool, according to reviews, it is always clean and there is a special anti-slip coating.

The rooms have air conditioning without interruptions, communications are also in good condition. The hotel is quiet in the evening, noise can be heard from neighboring complexes. Vacationers are satisfied with the lack of animation, which is suitable for families who come for relaxation and tranquility.

A separate area in the shade of palm trees is very popular with people aged and children. In comfortable easy chairs, guests like to spend lunch time here, during the period of the highest temperature outdoors.

Guests note that not far from the hotel there are many shops and trade stalls with souvenirs. The hotel also organizes interesting excursions to the sights of the region.
