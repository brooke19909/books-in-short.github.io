The meaning and origin of the surname Kostin

Most of the people on the planet are carriers of proper names. The name is also paired with the name. It determines which family or tribe we are from. A surname is a family, clan, name of a father, an ancestor to which each of us belongs. However, not all people know where their names came from. From this article you can find out the meaning and origin of the name Kostina.

How did most surnames appear

Many surnames are associated with the name of the locality. Does a man live in Small Puzyaki? So, to be him Puzyakin. Lives in Belyavki? So, Belyavsky will walk.

Many surnames came from a kind of activity. Man is engaged in pottery - to be him Goncharov. Forges a horseshoe - will become Kovalev.

Some surnames went on behalf of the ancestors. Mikhail became children Mikhailov, Nikolai - Nikolaev. And this is still without memories of serfdom, when all the serfs of one gentleman, even not closely related to him by blood, called his name. So a huge number of Trubetskoy, Menshikov or Kurochkin - far from noble origin.

Origin on behalf of father

So the name Kostin, according to one version, was formed after the baptism of Russia, when Greek names began to be used when baptizing children.

The legend of two brothers

According to another version, the name Kostin comes from two brothers. According to legend, two brothers lived, Konstantin and David, they reigned in the Argve side and fought with the Arabian prince Murvan Glukhim. They fought with varying success for a long time. Either the Arabs defeated, then the princes beat them mercilessly. And then one day they lost. They were captured where they were brutally tortured for many years, forced to accept the Muslim faith, and then executed.

They suffered for the Christian faith, refusing to accept Islam. Therefore, they were drowned in the year 730.
