Pleasant with useful: health resort "Melikhovsky"





I would also like to dwell on such an amazing place as the village of Melikhovka (Rostov Region). Recreation facilities, motels, boarding houses and hotels located in this place, as a rule, are very popular among vacationers.

For example, on the picturesque bank of the Don River there is the Melikhovsky sanatorium. Around the clock there are accepted for treatment and rest, both adults and parents with children. For accommodation there are double and triple rooms with different price levels and the service provided. There is a common room with a TV, a buffet, a library, a gym with exercise equipment.

The sanatorium treats diseases of the nervous system and respiratory organs. In the hydropathic institution there is an opportunity to enjoy pearl and ozonized baths, a bath with sea salt or needles, underwater massage, various types of showers (Charcot, circular, ascending), as well as ozokerite and mud therapy. A separate massage room offers therapeutic, vacuum and cosmetic massages. In the sanatorium, you can also undergo a course of treatment for osteochondrosis.

In the places of our ancestors. Decent rest on the Don

The MELEKHOV country club is located 135 km from Rostov-on-Don and 10 km from the small town of Kamensk-Shakhtinsky.

On this territory, well-known director Sergei Gerasimov shot some scenes from the movie "Quiet Flows the Don".

Later, in honor of the main character of this novel, Grigory Melekhov, it was decided to name the country club. It was at this amazingly beautiful canyon lake with crystal clear water that the MELEKHOV club was opened in June 2007.





As you know, in ancient times, the coast of the Don was inhabited by Cossacks. The creators of the country club tried to preserve and recreate all the features of the cultural heritage of this colorful people with its distinctive history.

The club can accommodate only a small number of guests - only about 20 people. In general, the hotel complex consists of separate comfortable houses.

On the territory there is entertainment for both children and adults. Attention vacationers are offered spacious pools, a bar with various cocktails and a hookah, a Russian wood-burning bath, a sandy beach with a volleyball court, tables for billiards and table tennis, a conference room, and a guarded parking lot.

The organization of fishing and formal receptions in nature. Club parties are arranged.

There are always catamarans, sun loungers, air mattresses and any sports equipment for rent. The restaurant as a whole specializes in Russian and Cossack cuisines. This is an amazing historical place where you can relax and get a lot of impressions.
