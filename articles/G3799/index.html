Hotel Grand Uysal Apart Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya / Obakoy): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

One of the hottest countries, which attracts millions of tourists to their lands from year to year, is Turkey with its many resorts in the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas. Here you can not only wallow in the warm rays of the sun, sunbathe, but also spend informative leisure time, because thanks to its long history there are many interesting places to visit. The beauties of the surrounding nature fascinate tourists.

The four-star Grand Uysal Apart Hotel offers comfortable accommodation in the wonderful city of Alanya. In addition to beautiful landscapes, this place is famous for its welcoming and cheerful population, which is infinitely happy for new guests.

general description

The hotel complex Grand Uysal Apart Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) was opened in 2001. Over the years of its existence, it has undergone many updates and repairs, the last of which was carried out in 2011. The distance to the airport is 125 km, which promises a two-hour drive after the flight. However, this does not prevent the return of tourists to these parts from year to year. It is impossible to live with pets, as well as smoking in rooms is not permissible.

The park area is incredibly green, spacious and fragrant. Trimmed shrubs, paved alleys, spreading palm trees - that creates a general backdrop of relaxation and affects the excellent mood of travelers. The staff speaks several languages. Payments are made by cards of international banks. For the comfort of guests, the building is equipped with elevators.





Accommodation

The room fund of the Grand Uysal Apart Hotel 4 * (Alanya) has 122 rooms, which are divided into two categories: standard and family. All have a spacious balcony with plastic furniture. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles. Of the decorating elements, there are fresh flowers in vases. In general, as the guests note in their reviews, the interior is simple but cozy thanks to the soft and bright furniture. Housekeeping is provided for daily housekeeping and wet cleaning. There were no complaints about cleanliness. Lingerie changes twice a week. You can order food or newspapers in your room. For those who are afraid to oversleep, it is proposed to use the "wake-up call" service.





Arrangement

The furniture set consists of a standard set, which includes soft beds, a table for TV, chairs, a cupboard and a spacious wardrobe. There is an individual air conditioning, which changes the temperature in the apartment depending on weather conditions outside. A TV with Russian channels allows you to have fun watching your favorite TV show or movie. There is a telephone and a safe, the use of which is paid extra. The presence of a refrigerator allows you to cool drinks and keep food fresh.

The bathroom is decorated comfortably and unusual. Equipped with shower, sink, hairdryer and mirror. Complete with towels and cosmetics.





Satisfying hunger

The All-Inclusive concept, which corresponds to the Alanya Grand Uysal Apart Hotel 4 * (Alanya), implies a five-time meal organized in the form of a buffet. A variety of dishes is presented by meat and fish masterpieces, salads from fresh vegetables, fruits, desserts and other products. Talented chefs put a piece of their soul into the work. There are two eating areas: an indoor lounge and a summer terrace. The total capacity is 450 people. As many guests note, it is very difficult to stay hungry here. All food is fresh and amazingly delicious.

You can quench your thirst in one of four bars: on the beach, in the snack bar, service bar, in the lobby. All local drinks do not require additional charges.





Aquazone

The swimming area is always crowded and popular among travelers, because here you can improve your health by swimming and enjoying the fresh air. The main pool is crossed by a wooden bridge, which is strictly forbidden to dive from. It is filled with fresh water and does not have a heating system. In the district there are sun loungers, umbrellas, showers. Experienced instructors regularly conduct water aerobics classes. They are attended by men and women, adults and children.

For bad weather there is an indoor aquazone with a children's pool.





Children's leisure

Grand Uysal Apart Hotel 4 * often receives positive reviews for organizing children's activities. According to the guests, the pastime of the children here is thought out to the smallest detail. From 4 to 12 years of age, you can visit the kids club, which is equipped with numerous tracks, designers, dolls and cars. Under the guidance of a qualified teacher, the kids will make original crafts, play board games and calmly watch cartoons among their peers.

In the fresh air there is a playground with many slides, ladders, houses, tolokars and other entertainment elements. The evening usually ends with a disco.

You can take advantage of babysitting services and order a crib in your room.

Beach area

Hotel Grand Uysal Apart Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) is located on the first coastline. A distance of only 50 m separates guests from swimming in the waters of the Mediterranean. The beach is sand and gravel, the entrance to the water has a gradual increase in depth. The beach infrastructure is represented by showers, changing rooms and a lifeguard service. A sufficient number of sun loungers will allow every vacationer to soak up the rays of the hot sun. For those who prefer shade and air baths, there are umbrellas and awnings.

You can diversify your leisure time by playing volleyball on a specially equipped field and go to conquer water attractions. The most popular, as noted by travelers, are canoes, scooters, parachuting for a boat, diving and snorkeling.





Sport and beauty

For those who are accustomed to outdoor activities, the doors of the fitness center are open. Its equipment will delight every athlete: steppers, cardiovascular equipment, dumbbells and much more, which will help maintain ideal body shape and health. Here you can leave extra pounds, increase immunity for absolutely everyone. Experienced trainers will help in this, who will create personal load schedules.

A visit to the bathhouse, sauna and temples has a beneficial effect on overall well-being: metabolic processes are normalized, blood circulation improves, and the supply of strength and energy is restored. Massage performed by professionals is an essential component in achieving complete relaxation and enjoyment.

The spa offers wellness and therapeutic treatments using mud and seaweed.

Entertainment

For a change of pastime it is proposed to play table tennis, billiards or darts. In the evening, show programs are organized where a professional team of animators entertains guests in the amphitheater. Talented dancers, artistic actors and unsurpassed artists give a lot of positive emotions and memorable impressions to all viewers. Turkish nights held several times a week are not uncommon.





Additional services

For the convenience of guests, the reception operates around the clock, so that all wishes and preferences are fulfilled with lightning speed. Car rental and bike rental services are available. Parking spaces are provided free of charge. At the reception you can book an entertaining tour of the local memorable sights. Nearby is a currency exchange. Doctor services on request. Dry cleaning and laundry are paid upon delivery. Beauty salon is glad to every visitor. Professional photographers will give fantastic photo shoots to everyone.

Grand Uysal Apart Hotel 4 *: reviews

Travelers do not skimp on positive reviews about the hotel in question. An amazing abundance of culinary masterpieces appeals to every guest. Resorts and helpful staff note that immediately answers all questions of interest. A wide range of entertainment allows each guest to be filled with new emotions and unforgettable impressions.
