How to disable Sberbank overdraft for individuals?

In the article, we will consider how to disable the overdraft in Sberbank.

This is the name of the increase in the limit of funds on debit cards that are currently available for use. You can explain what constitutes an overdraft at Sberbank using this example: on a plastic card, there is a sum of money in the amount of 1,000 rubles, and the cost of the goods that you need to purchase in the store is 1,400 rubles. The client of this bank can pay with his card or withdraw 1400 rubles and buy the desired product if he is able to use the "overdraft" service.

The bottom line is that this client takes a small loan from a financial institution at interest, which he agrees to repay over a period of time.

This service can be connected to a debit card (in certain cases, to a credit card). It can be used by owners of all gold, classic, platinum, youth and premium plastic cards that are connected to MasterCard or Visa payment systems. Many are interested in how to disable the overdraft at Sberbank.

Who can use the service?

At the time of introduction of the overdraft service, it was available exclusively to legal entities. It was introduced in order to ensure a constant flow of financial turnover of companies that, in the absence of money in the account, could currently use borrowed money, after which, when replenishing the balance of the account, the required amount of money was automatically debited.

Under the terms of the bank, when using an overdraft, a banking organization has the right to direct withdrawal of funds from its customer‚Äôs card in order to repay the debt. This means that money is debited automatically, regardless of the desire or unwillingness of the owner of the funds.

Today, overdraft is available not only to legal entities, but also to individuals. Depending on the specific situation, this service is connected to the plastic card automatically, or it can be done by submitting a written application from its holder.

The overdraft limit is determined by the bank individually for each client on the basis of information received about him (income, regularity of cash turnover on the card, etc.).

The sum of money for legal entities is from 100 thousand to 300 thousand rubles, and for individuals - from 1000 to 30 000 rubles. The interest rate is 18% per annum for a ruble account, 16% if the account is foreign currency.

Many customers are interested in whether it is possible to disable the overdraft at Sberbank or whether the service is temporary.

Terms of its provision - 1 year. That is, during this period, the card holder may from time to time use overdraft funds. The debt repayment period is 30 days. Over the course of a month, the money used with the overdraft service must be fully returned to the card. In cases of exceeding the restrictive amount or delay in returning funds, the amount of debt shall be calculated at the rate of 33% for the currency account, and 36% for the ruble account.

The difference between overdraft and credit

The overdraft service is similar in certain respects to lending, but there are significant differences. The first is set for both credit and debit cards, but most often the owners of the latest settlement accounts use it. The main difference between this service and a loan is that overdraft limits are significantly lower than for lending services.

Access to overdraft is much easier than obtaining a loan, and the conditions here are the most loyal. The interest rate for the use of borrowed funds is 18%. Repayment is mandatory within 30 days, otherwise certain sanctions will apply. Repayment of the loan for the service should be in full, and not in parts, as when using credit funds.

In case of violation of the terms of use and repayment of the debt, recalculation is carried out at an increased interest rate: 36%, moreover, it begins to operate from the first day of the use of funds, and not from the day the delay arises.

Overdraft on a salary card

This banking institution willingly offers all holders of these cards the opportunity to use financial resources using the overdraft service, since they are considered trustworthy customers with reliable solvency, since the monthly receipt of funds on the card guarantees timely payment of debts.

In such a case, the bank sets overdraft limits based on the amount of cash receipts. In most cases, Sberbank sets a limit of half the salary of its customer using this card.

Upon receipt of a salary and an advance on the card, the payment is debited to pay off the overdraft debt.

If you do not want to use this service, it is useful to know how to disable the overdraft in Sberbank on a salary card. One option is to call the facility hotline.

Credit card overdraft

The appropriateness of applying an overdraft on credit cards is determined by each client independently, since money at interest is borrowed from the bank, however, the provision of this service is possible. There are various cases and the client may always need money urgently, and the credit limit has been exhausted, and it often happens that there is simply nowhere to get it from. In such a case, an overdraft on a credit card allows the user to get out of a quandary.

Confirmation of solvency

For credit card holders, this service is provided only upon confirmation of their solvency. Positive decisions of the bank in this case are also affected by confirmed additional sources of income, the presence of other debit cards or a valid debit account, which is considered a guarantee of a refund.

Service Design

To apply for an overdraft on a Sberbank card, it is enough to have a debit card (if it concerns individuals) or a current account (when applying for a legal entity). Based on the information about the cash flows on the account or card, as well as other documents provided that confirm the solvency of the client, the banking organization decides on the size of the cash limit.
