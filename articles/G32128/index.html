Umm al-Quwain, UAE: hotels, tours, reviews

Umm al-Quwain is a popular tourist destination for exploring the main attractions of Islamic civilization and wildlife.

It is a relaxing resort with beautiful landscapes, pristine private beaches and the emerald sea.

Umm Al Quwain Overview

The emirate is located just 40 kilometers from Dubai with its ancient settlements and historical sites and contrasts sharply with the exuberant architecture of the capital of the UAE.





It is said that this is the best place in the world for travelers who want to enjoy the sound of the waves, good beaches and high quality service. Sailing in the calm water of the lagoons will leave a good impression on the Northern Emirates.

Where to stay in Umm Al Quwain

Flamingo Beach Resort Hotel is a modern resort located on the beach overlooking the mangrove island, which makes it an ideal place for relaxation in the north of the United Arab Emirates, just 58 km from Dubai International Airport and 45 km from Sharjah Airport.

From here, guests can easily get to water activities such as deep sea fishing, crab hunting, scuba diving and flamingo tours.





The sea, the sun, a wide variety of excursions will leave the best memories for the whole family after a stay at the Umm Al Quwain Hotel (UAE).

The hotel has 55 spacious, well-equipped simple rooms and suites with soothing and relaxing garden or pool views.

Most travelers in Umm Al Quwain reviews have a good rating for their stay at this hotel. Exit the hotel directly to the ocean. Magnificent beaches and a multicultural island are convenient for exploring the picturesque water world. The rooms are spacious, clean and comfortable. It has everything: nightlife, casinos and bars. The beaches are located in the warm and serene Pelican Bay.

A few minutes from the complex there are many restaurants, a grocery store, various entertainments. The city of Umm Al Quwain is a short taxi ride from the hotel and offers shopping opportunities for lovers of diamonds and jewelry.

According to reviews, the hotel is comfortable. Especially visitors noted the studio on the second floor overlooking the beach. She was comfortable and well equipped.

The staff is polite, the guys at the front desk and the concierge tried to please the guests. Minor repairs on demand were completed quickly and efficiently.





The beach and sunsets are magnificent, and the water is sky blue. The gym is open around the clock and seven days a week. To ensure security, the access key to the complex changed every day, which is very good for a quiet sleep.

If you want to rent a car, there are several agencies nearby.

All in all a wonderful stay.

Crab Hunt (6 hours)

Guests have the opportunity to enjoy a unique tour in Umm Al Quwain - hunting for crustaceans. Experienced guides will take tourists to the natural mangrove forests with crabs living in them.





Vacationers will have a unique experience in hunting crustaceans, which will bring a lot of pleasure. With the help of special underwater lights, you can hunt at night, and the culmination of this adventurous tour will be the catch, which will be prepared as a reward in a night cafe on the barbecue. The entire program is designed for the evening at low tide, so that even children can try their skills.

Water skiing

The tour is ideal for water skiing, kayaking, windsurfing and other water sports. Tourists canoe in clean and calm water to abandoned islands and explore the mangrove swamps, which are inhabited by pink flamingos, turtles, crabs and jumping fish.

Desert Safari

Fans of extreme sports can get a drive on a unique desert safari with camel riding, jeep riding in the red sand dunes, ATV racing.

Dubai City Tour (4 hours)

A four-hour city tour is one of the best ways to explore the historical side of the capital, which is often called the pearl of the Middle East. The tour includes a visit to the museum, which is located in Fort Al Fahidi of the XVIII century. It shows works of art and artifacts that reflect the ancient history and traditional way of life of Arabs.





An ideal illustration of the combination of outstanding ancient and modern Islamic architecture is the Jumeirah Mosque - a popular attraction covered by the route.

Flying club

The UAE Aviation Club, located near Dreamland, was created under the patronage of Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Mualla. It is famous for its skydiving championships. It also offers the opportunity to fly in a balloon, parachute, jump with a parachute (both singly and in tandem) . Beginners are offered training throughout the year.

Horse riding, fishing and falconry

Fishing, camel racing and falconry are just a few of the popular leisure activities in the United Arab Emirates. In this region, in addition to the Al-Khura hunting hawk, you can see a shahin or a peregrine falcon.





The camel riding track in Al Labsa offers extreme nerves to tickle nerves. It is located among the large dunes to the left of the road leading to Falaj al Moalla. The dunes in the area, combined with wooded valleys, offer a rather difficult terrain for riders.

Dinner at Burj Al Arab

If you want to make a pleasant surprise for your loved one, then it is worth ordering lunch in one of the many restaurants of the hotel Burj al-Arab.

Since its opening in 1999, Burj Al Arab has become a symbol of Dubai. This is due to its luxury, grace, stylish design and convenient location, as well as to a stunning assortment of dishes in restaurants that offer everything you can imagine, including delicacies from the countries of the Far East, Europe, America, the Mediterranean and a huge selection of seafood.

You can also dine in the corporate-style restaurant or enjoy a romantic dinner in the open-air dining room. Dinner at Burj Al Arab will satisfy the tastes of the most demanding customers.
