Hotel Royal Mirage Beach Resort: photos, description and reviews of tourists

Goa is an incredibly beautiful place with well-groomed beaches, exotic nature and mild climate. It is here, near the coast, that the Royal Mirage Beach Resort is located. Many tourists choose this place for a vacation. So what can a guest expect?

Description of the territory and location

Where exactly to look for the hotel Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 *? Goa, namely the picturesque village of Candolim in the northern part of the island, is where a cozy hotel was built right next to the coast. A tourist is waiting for a small but clean area and cozy buildings designed in a pleasant Portuguese style.





The city center is about 14 km away. The international airport is approximately 47 km away, so you should prepare for the move, although it will not be too long. There is a large train station 22 km away, which is important for people planning to travel more around the country.

Hotel Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * (Goa): what do the rooms look like?

The hotel can be considered small, as it consists of 45 rooms. On the other hand, a small number of holidaymakers provides comfort, peace and silence. The rooms, albeit small, but very comfortable and furnished with comfortable furniture. Each room comes with a balcony or terrace where you can relax, breathe the sea air and enjoy the vibrant beauty of an exotic garden.

At your service there is a necessary set of household appliances. For example, an air conditioning system will create pleasant temperature conditions. There is a refrigerator in which it is convenient to store food. Filling the minibar is paid. There is a satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. By the way, for a small fee you can rent a laptop. The bathroom also has everything you need, including a shower, sink, hair dryer and a set of clean towels.





Restaurants and cafes in the hotel

As is customary at most Indian hotels, Royal Mirage Beach Resort offers complimentary continental breakfasts. A buffet is served every morning in the hotel restaurant. It offers toast, scrambled eggs, granola, fresh fruit, homemade cakes and some other dishes. Lunches and dinners are also possible here, but guests pay them separately, ordering the dishes they like on the menu. There is also a bar with a stylish atmosphere and a pleasant atmosphere where you can have fun.

In any case, you should not worry about food, since there are many restaurants, cafes and other institutions near the Royal Mirrage Beach Resort 3 * hotel. They can be eaten for a small fee.

Hotel Royal Mirage Beach Resort (Goa): beach holiday

Most travelers come to the island in search of an interesting holiday on the beach. Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * hotel is located about 450-500 meters from the picturesque sea shore. The luxurious sandy beach of Candolim can be reached in 10-15 minutes. The sea here is mostly calm, there is a sandbank where children like to splash around. For a small price, you can rent a comfortable sun lounger and umbrella for the whole day. And on the shore there are several cafes that sell not only refreshing drinks, but also snacks - here you can have lunch and even dinner in the rays of the setting sun. For lovers of a more active holiday on the beach, sports clubs are organized where you can rent a boat, ride a boat, swim with dolphins, and fully enjoy the local beauty.





Leisure and additional service

Travelers can rely not only on beach activities. Royal Mirage Beach Resort also has something to do on site. For example, you can sunbathe and relax by the pool, which is regularly cleaned. A fitness club is constantly working, where you can work out in the gym or sign up for one of the group classes. For a small fee, guests can use the spa and steam room. You should definitely try on yourself all the wonders of Indian massage. And there is also a nightclub that will appeal to lovers of uncontrollable fun. And do not forget about sightseeing holidays, because this country has a lot to offer to an inquisitive traveler.

As for amenities, the hotel offers laundry services, currency exchange. Throughout the territory there is access to the Internet. There is no own medical office, but a good doctor will be called upon request.
