What you need to know when planning a vacation in Portugal? Resorts in Portugal





If you are planning a vacation in Portugal, reviews of experts on the sights and features of this country will help in choosing a resort. The westernmost country in Europe, washed by the waters of the Atlantic Ocean, is the country of seafarers and discoverers, Portugal. Its centuries-old history and ancient monuments always attract many tourists. Portugal is quite suitable for both curious people who like to spend time actively, and for beach lovers. Magnificent palaces and ancient castles have been preserved here . In some, you can now live by renting a room or even the entire castle. These are the so-called homesteads - the restored medieval manors that were turned by their owners into hotels. The southern coast of Portugal is famous for its magnificent beaches with fine clean sand. You can combine a historical tour of the country and a beach holiday in Portugal. Reviews of such tours will help you choose the best program. It should be noted that one of the most inexpensive places in Europe is Portugal. This is evidenced by the price lists presented by tour operators for holidays in Portugal. 2013 prices are quite acceptable even for a very small budget.

Resort areas of Portugal



The magnificent resort of the Algarve offers quite comfortable hotels with accommodation at low prices. Algarve is an almost uninterrupted chain of small resort towns with wide beaches, which are never crowded. More expensive and more aristocratic are considered resorts located along the coast of the Atlantic Ocean from south to north of the country. This is the Lisbon Riviera Costa do Sol. There are fewer sandy beaches, but more private estates and villas. In order to understand which of the resort destinations is more suitable for you, if you are planning a vacation in Portugal, the reviews of tourists will not be superfluous. There are very noisy party places where life boils and rages even at night, and there are quiet towns, more suitable for families and families with children. There are fashionable resorts, and there are also very democratic ones.

Ancient cities of Portugal

Sightseeing and historical excursions in a country such as Portugal, holidays, a tour of castles and monasteries is best to start with Lisbon. It is the capital of the country, a city with unique architecture and ancient monuments. The medieval quarters of Lisbon with their narrow cobbled streets, the stunning statue of Christ on the hill, the largest Oceanarium in Europe, many palaces and

museums will not leave indifferent any tourist. Nowhere in the world are there facades of houses lined with azulejos - glazed tiles with bright colors. Nowhere in the world is there a vertical funicular or just an Eiffel elevator that helps residents from the bottom of the city quickly get to the top. There is the famous Bethlehem Tower in Lisbon, standing on the ocean as a symbol of past greatness. The ships of Magellan and Vasco da Gama once departed from here to distant lands. For those who choose an individual excursion vacation in Portugal, reviews of independent tourists about visiting small ancient cities will help not to miss the true pearls of this region. The most famous ancient cities of Portugal are in the north - Porto, Guimaraes, Braga. The city of Porto is recognized as a monument protected by UNESCO. There are Catholic cathedrals famous for their beauty in Braga .
