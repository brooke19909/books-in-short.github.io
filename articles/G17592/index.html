Modeling career

The popular Nigel Barker was born in 1972 in a large family. The boy studied well and planned to enter the medical faculty after school. However, his mother, a former winner of the beauty contest, having estimated the attractiveness of her son, brought him to a television show, a confident victory on which brought him a signed contract with a modeling agency.

Own business

A handsome young man traveled around the world for more than 10 years, participating in shows of famous brands. Having learned the model business from the inside and realizing that young fashion models are stepping on his heels, Nigel opened his own photo studio in Manhattan, which has been collaborating with various publications since 1996. Of course, connections in the fashion world helped him a lot, but every day his growing talent was noticed by everyone.

The photographer is not limited only to studio work, he produces films, writes books, participates in advertising campaigns and arranges exhibitions, but emphasizes that his family will always be in first place.

Three years ago, he released unisex signature fragrances, and the inspiration for them was New York, in which Nigel Barker lives with his family. Laconic form bottles he adorned with his work.

Barker Books

In 2010, Nigel Barker released a book that became a real bestseller, in which he shared with all readers the secrets of beauty. And five years later, the new edition saw the light, illustrated with stunning pictures.

Nigel wrote about 50 influential women in the beauty industry who are commonly called style icons. Successful models of various appearances that have turned the standards of beauty appear on two hundred colorful photographs, which were carefully selected by the author.





Each shot is equipped with interesting stories and fun captions, and, as Nigel Barker assures, this is not just a work introducing the catwalk divas. He created a very significant work for readers, telling about the history of fashion, starting from the 40s of the last century.

Traveling around the world, the photographer often holds master classes, inspiring other people with his work. He recognizes himself as a happy person in work and family life, saying that he has found a long-awaited balance.
