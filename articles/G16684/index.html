Azurro 3 * (Sunny Beach): hotel description, photos and reviews of tourists

Resorts in Bulgaria have long been loved by our compatriots. They are no less popular than Turkish. Therefore, in our article we want to talk about one of the budget hotels - Azurro 3 * (Sunny Beach).

A bit about the hotel ...

Azurro 3 * (Sunny Beach) is located in a very famous Bulgarian resort. The hotel has an incredibly convenient location, just a hundred meters from the Black Sea. The complex is a four-story building located in the southern part of the resort. The nearest airport is in Burgas, 30 kilometers from the hotel.



The hotel complex has a compact area with a swimming pool and is recommended for outdoor enthusiasts and youth. Around it there are many nightclubs, bars, shops and restaurants.

Food in the complex

A little bit about the resort ...

Sunny Beach is currently the most popular resort in Bulgaria. Over 800 hotels of various levels have been built on its territory. Sunny Beach is nothing short of an incredible and amazing combination of a man-made biosystem and the natural conditions created by the sun, sea, needles, and breezes. The tourist area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe resort occupies a seven-kilometer strip on the coast. The resort has a rather favorable location, away from big cities, highways, railways. It is for this reason that every year the famous Blue Flag goes to Sunny Beach.



The resort is incredibly lively, especially in summer. It is great for holidays with children, because the local sea bay is moderately shallow and has a gentle slope. Almost every hotel has a children's pool, there are playgrounds with slides and entertainment for the youngest tourists. Since 2003, a water park has been operating, located ten kilometers from the resort. All complexes are located along the coast, and therefore there are no large distances.

Sunny Beach adores young people for whom all the conditions for entertainment (discos, clubs) are created here. The resort has a lot of retirees from Europe, which indicates the universality of this place.

The resort is best known for its stunning beaches. The width of the coastline is 30-40 meters and is covered by high sand dunes. The beaches are covered with fine sand of golden color. In addition to stunningly beautiful nature, the tourist area has a wonderful climate. In the summer, the temperature is between + 22-30 degrees, and the sea temperature reaches +26 degrees.

Local Attractions

Sunny Beach has no significant historical sights, but Nessebar, located nearby, is considered one of the oldest cities in Europe. The city is on the UNESCO list. It is also called the site of 40 churches. The best preserved of them are dated to the fourth and fifth centuries. Sunny Beach itself is an amazingly fun place, all kinds of contests and folklore holidays are held here.
