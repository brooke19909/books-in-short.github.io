Hotel Prassino Nissi 2 * (Greece, Corfu Island): photos, reviews

Lovers of ancient architecture, history, who want to enjoy a chic beach holiday, are increasingly choosing Greece for their holidays. But getting to know the state is unlikely to be complete without getting to know one of the stunning Greek islands of Corfu. It is the westernmost point of Greece and is washed by the waters of the Ionian Sea. The island is located at the intersection of trade routes of East and West, therefore, the influence of several countries and continents is felt here.

This place is perfect for a respectable and even elite vacation. The island is literally surrounded by greenery. The coast offers stunning views of the local beauty. Everyone will find entertainment for themselves here. And all this thanks to the developed infrastructure of the resort. Gorgeous sandy beaches, warm gentle sea, a rich excursion program attract lovers of beauty. And one more thing: there are not so many vacationers here as on the mainland. Therefore, the island is ideal for those who want to plunge into the atmosphere of silence and tranquility for several days.

Corfu is a resort for discerning and demanding tourists. Hotels here offer excellent service. At the same time, pre-booking allows you to choose inexpensive options for your stay. Hotel Prassino Nissi 2 * is one of the budget complexes, which managed to acquire a good rating at the largest tourist portals. What can tourists expect when booking a room here? We will try to conduct the most objective review.





About hotel

Prassino Nissi Hotel 2 * is a small hotel with a pleasant and friendly atmosphere. It is open to visitors from April to October. Judging by the reviews of the guests, it offers simple but quite comfortable rooms and good service. Hospitality and hospitality is what all visitors to Prassino Nissi 2 * (Corfu) note . Reviews of many vacationers describe those pleasant trifles, the attention that was given to them throughout their stay.

So, no one will leave the guests arriving on the street if they arrive earlier than the indicated time. Some tourists note in their reviews the following: when the flight was delayed at the hotel, they were offered a small compliment when they checked in - sandwiches, pastries and fruits. This is a good compensation for a missed dinner.





Location

Hotel Prassino Nissi 2 * is located just 300 meters from the local village of Moraitika. In addition, it is located 20 km from the center of Corfu. The international airport is just 18 km away. Therefore, travel time will not be long and tiring.

Rooms

The room fund of the hotel Prassino Nissi 2 * (Corfu) is represented by 32 standard rooms. Tourists can choose single, double and even triple apartments. Guests note that each room is equipped with modern and comfortable furniture. Moreover, vacationers can enjoy the sound of the surf, meet the sunrises and watch the sunsets on the balcony facing the coast.

Comfortable microclimate and pleasant coolness in the rooms are provided by powerful modern climatic equipment. But the use of air conditioning for unknown reasons is not included in the room rate. A day's work costs 6 euros. Upon payment, vacationers will receive a special card that will bring the device to work. In the season without air conditioning, it is simply impossible to stay in the room: suffocating heat, stuffiness.

Tourists will also enjoy satellite TV and a minibar. A telephone is provided for communication with hotel staff. Each room has a private bathroom with a hairdryer and all necessary amenities.





Room service

Judging by the reviews of visitors, the rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen and towels - as needed. In general, the hotel is clean and tidy. For an additional fee, hotel staff will bring breakfast to your room and put things in order. When booking a honeymoon apartment, staff will arrange it accordingly.





Hotel Territory

An excellent well-kept garden is what the Prassino Nissi Hotel 2 * (Corfu) can offer to all vacationers. In the evening, hotel guests gather here, share their impressions, socialize and have fun together. For the organization of leisure, a common relaxation room with a TV and the necessary equipment for watching movies is intended. Sometimes it turns into a real cinema for vacationers.

Guests note that for the youngest guests on the territory of the Prassino Nissi Hotel 2 * there is a good playground. Everyone can take a dip in the outdoor pool, take sun baths here. Almost always, the water bowl is empty, because most tourists spend their free time on the beach and visit local beauties on their own or as part of a rich excursion program.

The pool is small, but rather clean: every night a robot vacuum cleaner is launched into it, which perfectly copes with all pollution. From sports activities, the Prassino Nissi 2 * hotel can offer only table tennis.

Nutrition

All those tourists who stay at the Prassino Nissi 2 * hotel can count on a continental breakfast. Morning meals are served in the spacious, home-style restaurant. Here, all vacationers can get acquainted with amazing Greek cuisine. Breakfast is typical for Europe and does not impress with its variety: granola, milk, eggs, sausages, cold cuts, cheese, yogurt, cottage cheese, sometimes fresh fruits and vegetables, drinks to choose from.

For an extra fee, the hotel restaurant offers lunch and dinner. But tourists recommend visiting local cafes, and preferably Greek ones. Here you can have quite inexpensive tasty lunch and dinner. And, of course, this is a great opportunity to get to know better the national cuisine, which has prepared many surprises for vacationers.





Service and additional services

The reception is open around the clock, which allows you to quickly solve all the problems that tourists have. Guest reviews suggest parking is available on site for an additional fee. Quite often, tourists coming to Corfu rent a car in order to get to know the resort as close as possible as part of their vacation, to visit the most beautiful places and attractions. So in this case, secure parking will be a real salvation.

Moreover, everyone can use the safe for storing documents and valuables. He is at the reception and is paid extra. In addition, for an additional fee, you can put your things in order, as well as access to wireless high-speed Internet. The cost of a week of use when connecting to one access point is 10 euros - for Europe it is quite an adequate price.

Of course, many tourists can be confused by the abundance of paid services. But do not forget that this is a 2 star hotel. And in Europe, in principle, it is difficult to find hotels operating on an all-inclusive basis. Rest here is not the most budgetary.





Beach

Just 60 meters from the hotel there is an excellent sand and pebble beach. The sea is crystal clear. Entry into the water is not the most pleasant, small pebbles come across, but literally a few meters later a pleasant sand begins. Of course, there are enough vacationers here in the season. But lovers of a more relaxing and secluded holiday can stroll along the coast. Surely they will find places remote from civilization for themselves.





To summarize

If you are looking for a paradise, want to enjoy the comfort and impeccable service, choose the island of Corfu. Prassino Nissi Hotel 2 * is a small but quite comfortable hotel, remote from the center of spa life. It is quite quiet, calm and clean. The hotel is not without some drawbacks, but offers the best combination of cost of living and quality of services provided. Therefore, we strongly recommend paying special attention to it.
