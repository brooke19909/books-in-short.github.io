You are going to relax, but do not know what kind of sea is in Spain. We will tell you

Travelers who go on vacation to this country often have the question: "What is the sea in Spain?" Let's try to figure it out. First, Spain is located on the Iberian Peninsula, washed in the north by the Bay of Biscay, in the west by the Atlantic Ocean, in the south and east by the Mediterranean Sea. The first answer to this question we already know.



What is the sea in Spain? Crystal clear, not without reason most of the local beaches have a European blue flag for comfort and cleanliness. And they are deservedly considered the best on the continent.

The Spanish authorities have to try hard so that vacationers fill the local hotels and beaches. After all, rest here is quite expensive, but of high quality and worth the money spent. When considering what kind of sea is in Spain, you need to know that it is the climate that depends on it to a greater extent. In the resorts of the Mediterranean, it is subtropical. Thanks to the warm sea, the air temperature here in winter is +12 - 17 degrees Celsius, and in summer +25 - 30. Sometimes in summer the air warms up to +40. Despite such high numbers, the heat is tolerated relatively well, as there is little rainfall and is dry. On the Atlantic coast, the climate is marine with warm summers and humid winters.



For beach lovers, an important issue is the length of the swimming season. What is the sea of ​​Spain in this sense? What can tourists expect? On the warm sea from May and at least until mid-October.

And to this should be added chic hotels, a large number of restaurants, cafes, bars with excellent local cuisine. As well as holidays, festivals, concerts, museums, flamenco, bullfighting, excursions, regular and water sports. If you combine all of the above with wonderful weather, then what else is needed for a wonderful, long-memorable vacation.

Now a little walk through the regions. For family vacations, water sports, Costa Dorada is excellent. This beach is called golden. And not in vain. The sand here is really bright golden color, it is really soft. On all beaches there are yacht clubs, in some places even two.



The beaches of Andalusia, Canary and the islands of Formentara are white sand, sand dunes, warm and shallow sea, boat trips and many quiet secluded corners.

If you are interested in the maximum wildlife, go to the wild coast of the Costa Brava. Here, 130 kilometers stretched rugged coast, mountains, bays. An excellent place for diving. Be sure to visit the marine reserve on Medes.

Near the town of Guardamar de Segur, Costa Blanca, there is a small beach, which is a paradise for nudists. You can bring your favorite dogs there and sunbathe naked.

The resort of Costa del Sol is known for its artificial beaches. The sand here is made from granite, which is mined in the mountains, and then finely crushed. Such beaches are considered healing.



Learning about what kind of country is Spain, what kind of sea is there, you need to pay attention to a unique place - the resort of La Manga. Its location is a 20-kilometer narrow sand spit bounded on one side by the Mediterranean Sea, and on the other, by the Little Sea, called the lagoon of Mar Menor. The water in the lagoon in terms of salt and iodine, in healing properties is similar to the water of the Dead Sea.

In principle, the question "what kind of sea is in Spain" can be answered with a single word: magnificent!
