Zilim - a river in Bashkiria: description, kayaking

Zilim - a river along which visitors annually carry out rafting. It is a right tributary of the river. White flowing through the Southern Urals. A high level of ecology is preserved here. The beauty of the landscape attracts a large number of tourists.

How to get there

Many people come here every year. For urban residents, the cure for fatigue, the monotony of gray everyday life is Zilim (river). Where is it and how to get there?



People stop near Tolbazy, a village located 30 km from Sterlitamak, and make a left. Then continue the journey towards the village of Krasnousolsky. There you can get free mineral water.

Drivers are guided by signs when they go to Lake White. Passing picturesque mountain gorges, for 60 km tourists move along a gravel road. The conditions for travel are normal, using a speed of 100 kilometers per hour.

Description

Achieved by tourists offers a beautiful view. Zilim is a river in Bashkiria, which is surrounded by picturesque mountain landscapes. Cliffs are located off the coast. Enjoy the walk through the caves and near the waterfalls.

The vegetation is rich. Water is clean. Its level is different in some parts of the river. There are rifts of medium and small size. The current has a speed of 3 to 5 kilometers per hour.

Gorgeous looks

Kayaking - an opportunity to admire the magnificence and beauty of steep rocky shores, coniferous forests and gentle mountains, steep cliffs. These landscapes are replaced during the journey by the river channel. Passing the village of Kuyli-Tamak, travelers see how 4 bastions of rocky walls protrude into the river bed.

Next, a 5-kilometer massif opens around, around which Zilim makes a detour. The lintel has a width of 300 m, the circle is almost closed. One of the most important attractions of these places is Mambet - the highest rock in the region of the Near Urals. Over the river it rises 250 meters, there are three steps.

The lower one is sheer, on the others there are ancient terraces of water arteries, pine thickets. Zilim is a river, a visit to which brings an unforgettable experience. Climbing Mambet reveals the beauty of the surrounding expanses. The air here is clean and literally intoxicating. People make bonfires, spend the night in tents, sing songs in the close company of friends, and fish. This is a great example of an active and fun holiday.





Tours to the Zilim River

In order to carry out rafting on the Zilim river, special training is often not required. Children and elderly people are allowed (from 7 to 70 years old). Groups of at least 15 people gather.

During the trip, people forget about mobile communications, the Internet, but at the same time about all the hardships of everyday life. Muscovites get used to an unusual time zone for them (the hourly difference is 2 hours).

For movement on water using catamarans for 4-6 places, rafts, kayaks for one and two persons. The best time to go is summer. Then the air temperature fluctuates between 15-35 degrees during the day and 5-15 at night. You should bring a warm sleeping bag or blanket with you.

Many bathe, as the water warms up to 15-25 degrees. Zilim is a river in Bashkiria, swimming with its waters, people admire not only Mambet, but also another interesting rock - Kuzgank.





Group Travel

People who nevertheless decided not to kill their own car, but to go on vacation in a group tour, gather in Ufa. The bus picks them up from the stations, leaves the city and arrives at Krasnousolsky.

The guide leads tourists around the village, introduces the local church. Then there is a bath in the bath with hydrogen sulfide, then lunch break. In the village of Bakeevo also conduct a tour. They set up camp for the night. On the second day, an influx of the White River becomes available.

On the river Zilim carry out rafting, stop for lunch, relax in the evening. The following days are subject to weather conditions. Over a five-day period, it is possible to overcome 50 kilometers. Separate time is allocated for going to the bathhouse and relaxing. In front of the village of Tolparovo visit the rocks Kuzganak and Mambet. Then the group moves to the cliff of the Tears of Zilim and Kinderlinsky cave on kayaks. They conduct excursions and head to the village of Tash-Asty, go to the bathhouse, have dinner before leaving home.

After the camp is collapsed, people go along the river to the village of Shmendyashevo. They catch a bus and get back to Ufa. The road takes 55 kilometers.





Preparation for the tour

You need to properly prepare for the trip, because the conditions will be unusual for people living in city apartments.

It will not do without light physical exertion. This will shake and cheer up anyone who is used to working and relaxing in front of a computer. Instructors teach the members of the group safety rules, accompany and monitor them at every stage of the trip.

Travel agencies take care of food. It is three times a day. Necessarily present hot dishes. So there is no need to take a lot of bread and canned food.

Be sure to take a medical kit with you. However, if a person has a special disease, it is worth taking medications for yourself separately. Before the tour, it is advisable to get a tick vaccination. These pests are found here.

The wardrobe includes long pants. The fabric is preferably slippery. They are tucked into boots and boots. Jackets and T-shirts before leaving are treated with an insect repellent, sealed in a plastic bag and kept there for several days to soak the fabric. Every day, tourists examine themselves and loved ones for a bite.

Drinking alcohol on a camping trip is undesirable. Leaders are not required to be responsible for drunk people. Most of them do not drink themselves. It is better to make the rest really useful and leave more pleasant memories in your memory, not shading them with the influence of alcohol. If you follow all the rules, the rest will be pleasant and safe.
