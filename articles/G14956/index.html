Plastic modeling lessons. How is polymer clay made strawberries?

Jewelry made of polymer clay confidently conquer the hearts of modern fashionistas. In it you can read about how to make strawberries from polymer clay. This simple element can later become an earring or pendant. Polymer clay molding: preparatory stage

In order to make a strawberry from plastic, you will need the following materials and tools:

  • stucco material of red, green color;
  • clerical knife;
  • wooden or plastic plate;
  • accessories for jewelry;
  • small beads or yellow beads;
  • toothpick;
  • varnish for plastic products.

Master class on the topic "Strawberry made of polymer clay"

  1. Mash the red material (a whole slice) in your hands, so that it becomes soft and elastic.
  2. Swing the plastic into a ‚Äúsausage‚Äù and cut into two identical halves with the help of a clerical knife. Each piece will later become a strawberry earring. If you decide to make a pendant, you can not divide the clay, but blind a large berry from a whole bar.
  3. Form an oval shape from plastic . Then, with your fingers, give it the shape of a berry: make one edge thicker and the other thinner and longer. Polymer clay strawberries (main part) are ready.
  4. Wash your hands clean and dry them. Make sure that there are no particles of red stucco material on the fingers so that they do not transfer to other colored pieces of clay. Further work will continue with green plastic.
  5. Cut a clay block into ten identical pieces. From them, blind small petals. Attach them (5 pieces) to the thick edge of the strawberry, forming a sepal.
  6. Using a toothpick, draw veins on the petals. This will give the product a natural look.
  7. Next, polymer clay strawberries are decorated with beads. Attach them in a checkerboard pattern over the entire red surface of the product, slightly pressing into the plastic. In this way, you have formed seeds on the berry.
  8. The next stage is the fastening of accessories. Depending on what type of jewelry you are making, insert the appropriate pins and fasteners into the product.
  9. Put the product in the oven and bake at the temperature indicated on the package in which the material for modeling was bought. For different types of polymer clay, the drying time and degree regime may vary. Therefore, carefully read the instructions.
  10. After cooling, strawberries made of polymer clay are varnished and dried naturally.
