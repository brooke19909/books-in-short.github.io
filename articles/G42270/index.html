Description of residential complex "Height" in Irkutsk

The new residential complex is located in the October JSC Irkutsk. Its perimeter is limited by such streets as Krasnoyarskaya, Piskunova and Yadrintseva. In this area is the cultural and business center of Irkutsk.

The general plan of "Heights" provides for the construction of houses consisting of several sections. The height of all residential buildings is different - from 7 to 17 floors. The developer of the project is the company of the same name - SC "Height". The houses being built in the framework of this project belong to the economy class.
