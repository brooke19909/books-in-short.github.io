VIK Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * Dominican Republic / Punta Cana: description, service, reviews of tourists

The Dominican Republic is a small state that occupies an impressive part of the island of Haiti. It is located in the Caribbean, so the climate there is hot and humid. And although the Dominican Republic is far removed from Russia, the distance does not bother travelers who want to relax on its warm coast. In the country there are several large resorts, one of the most visited is Punta Cana. Often, tourists prefer to stay here in the modern beach complex of Vik Hotel Arena Blanca 4 *. Photos, descriptions and reviews about this place will be presented in this article.

general information

Punta Cana is considered a place for a respectable vacation, so the cost of living here is quite high. Hotel Vik Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * in the Dominican Republic offers tourists a high quality service. Its doors for tourists were opened in 2000. Since then, it annually receives travelers from all over the world. Only qualified staff work at the hotel, so its staff speaks excellent English. In high season, you can find several Russian-speaking guides in the complex. They will be happy to accompany guests on excursions, and will also help solve problems that arise if you do not know English.

Complex Vik Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * in the Dominican Republic is built right on the beach. Next to it is another hotel with a higher status and cost of living. Hotels have a common territory and a beach. As a rule, such a neighborhood does not bother guests of this hotel. Although it cannot boast of its large territory, there is enough space for all tourists. Its area is about 120,000 square meters. m. On it are located not only buildings and the beach, but also an extensive tropical park. In addition, the hotel has its own outdoor pool and outdoor sun deck where tourists can sunbathe.

Hotel accommodation is offered standard. Registration and check-in of all guests in the rooms starts from 15:00 local time. If you arrived earlier, you can leave your luggage in the luggage room and take a walk around the hotel. When registering, you will need to provide your administrator with your passport and bank card. Departure is from 09:00. It is also worth noting that the hotel does not allow tourists who come with any animals to check in to the rooms.

How to get to the hotel

The flight to the Dominican Republic takes a lot of time, therefore, after landing, tourists dream of getting to the hotel as soon as possible to relax. Punta Cana has its own international airport, which also accepts flights from Moscow. From here, tourists can reach the Hotel Vik Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * in the Dominican Republic in just half an hour. A comfortable bus picks up guests from the airport and quickly takes them to their destination. If you wish, you can get to the hotel yourself by renting a car or calling a taxi.

The hotel itself is in a picturesque location. On the one hand it is surrounded by the sea and the beach of Bavaro, and on the other - a dense tropical garden. There are no major infrastructure facilities near the hotel. If you wish, you can reach a large shopping center in 5 minutes. The complex has everything necessary for recreation, including restaurants and shops, so the lack of infrastructure does not bother guests. They can also always take a bus or taxi to the center of Punta Cana.

Number of rooms

And although the VIK Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * hotel in the Dominican Republic was built in 2000, its number of rooms cannot be called obsolete. The last repair of living rooms was made in 2017. Now the complex offers guests 456 rooms of the following categories:

  • Superior - standard apartments, consisting of one living room, bathroom and balcony. Designed for two adults and two children. You can choose between rooms with garden or sea views. The last option is slightly smaller in area - 30 square meters. m. Rooms with a view of the garden are more spacious - 36 square meters. m
  • Family Suite - spacious apartments designed to accommodate a large company or family. Up to four adults can live here. The room consists of two bedrooms, a bathroom and a balcony. Its area is 43 square meters. m
  • Superior Family Suite is another option for family apartments. It has the best equipment, but a smaller area - 36 square meters. m. Consists of two bedrooms, a bathroom and a balcony overlooking the tropical garden. Suitable for 3-4 people.

Room amenities

Each hotel room is equipped with everything necessary for comfortable accommodation. For the convenience of guests, each of them has not only beds and wardrobes. From furniture, guests are offered armchairs, dining and writing tables, bedside tables, clothes dryers. All rooms are decorated in a modern classic style with a predominance of light colors. In the living rooms of VIK Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * in Punta Cana, guests can find additional equipment. It includes a TV, fridge and minibar. In the Dominican Republic, summer is all year round, so all rooms are equipped not only with air conditioning, but also with ceiling fans. In addition, the rooms have a telephone and Internet access.

The bathroom has a hairdryer and a mirror, towels and individual sets of hygiene items, consisting of shampoo, shower gel and soap. Rooms are cleaned daily.

What to do in the hotel?

Tourists who come to VIK Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * in Punta Cana spend most of the rest on the beach. Therefore, a large selection of other entertainment on site is not provided. For the leisure of tourists every day a team of animators is responsible. They conduct recreational activities, as well as gymnastics and water aerobics classes. Guests can play tennis, volleyball or table football at any time. An equestrian school is equipped on the territory of the complex and huge chess in full growth is set. For a fee, guests can visit the spa, where you can book a massage or wellness course. It features a sauna and a jacuzzi.

Beach vacation

A clean and calm sea is what many tourists fly to the country for, spending many hours on the road. VIK Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * in the Dominican Republic offers for its guests a fully equipped sandy beach with a length of about 400 meters. Guards are on duty daily, who do not let local residents and guests of other people's hotels here. The beach has sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as showers, changing cabins and toilets. In addition, so that tourists are definitely not bored, guests are offered a wide selection of entertainment, from beach volleyball to diving lessons.

If the sea is stormy, you can swim in the outdoor pool, located next to residential buildings. Next to it is a comfortable sun terrace with free sunbeds.

Hotel Territory

If you are one of those tourists who like to spend most of their vacation at the hotel, then the VIK Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * hotel will definitely appeal to you. For guests here are many infrastructure facilities. The main one is the reception desk. She works around the clock. Here is the registration of all guests. You can also exchange your currency or cash it out at any time. For a fee, the administrator will help you purchase tickets for excursions or arrange car rental. The hotel has a small shopping center where tourists buy clothes, souvenirs, food, as well as jewelry and exotic tropical flowers. Guests can use the free gym at any time. A laundry service is available at the front desk, where you can rent clothes, shoes and leather goods. For a fee, businessmen can use the services of the conference room.

restaurants and bars

Conditions for accommodating children
