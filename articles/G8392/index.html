Aiyaree Place Hotel 3 *, Pattaya: reviews, photos, description

Thailand ... This amazing country, in the name of which there is the word "freedom" - "thai", many Russians consider exotic. In this fabulous kingdom you can spend an unforgettable vacation.

Thailand

The abundance of Buddhist temples, coral reefs - expanse for divers, unprecedented plants and crocodile farms, vibrant nightlife and shopping make tours to the resorts of Thailand desirable for all tourists. The holidays here are so varied that everyone, even the most experienced traveler, will be able to find something of their own in ultramodern Bangkok or the fiery club Pattaya, on the exotic islands of Samet and Koh Chang, on the pristine beaches of Samui and the fantastic Krabi. This country attracts Russians not only because of its unusualness, combined with the study of numerous local attractions, or the developed tourist infrastructure, but also by its relatively inexpensive opportunity to relax comfortably in any season of the year.



It really has everything that a tourist wants to get from a tropical resort: these are the magical waters of two oceans - the Pacific and Indian, magnificent hotels with a huge range of both services and prices, as well as a surprisingly friendly population. Those who have traveled to Thailand joke that for Thais, simply raising their voices while speaking is tantamount to inflicting a near-fatal resentment. And if you add to this the centuries-old history of the kingdom of Siam, numerous ancient cities that can be seen in the northern part, a stunning variety of fruits, many of which are unknown to Russians, and, of course, a fabulous Thai massage, you can understand the desire of tourists to visit at least once this corner of the globe.

Pattaya

This small town is located on the coast of the South China Sea. It is reliably protected from tsunamis and hurricanes. Pattaya is often called the Asian Riviera. It is visited annually by a very diverse army of tourists: Europeans, Americans, young people and families with children come here. About four million people fly to Pattaya every year to not only swim and sunbathe, but also to see such interesting objects as the Temple of Truth, a crocodile farm, look at the surroundings from the observation platform of Pratamnak Hill, take a walk through the Nong Nooch tropical garden or zoo with tigers, visit the ‚ÄúElephant Village‚Äù and, of course, on Walking street - Red Light District.

In Pattaya there is literally a royal abundance of cafes and restaurants with national or European, Russian or Arabic, Indian and even Turkish cuisines. Along the resort area there are a lot of coral islands, where everyone can retire to the bosom of exotic nature, enjoying a world untouched by civilization. Fans of shopping will be delighted with the numerous salons, boutiques and noisy markets where you can buy everything from pareos to beautiful necklaces by local craftsmen. Those who prefer a vibrant nightlife will be satisfied with visiting clubs and discos where dance programs and various shows are held.

Picky tourists who prefer a democratic vacation, ideally suit three- and four-star hotels with their excellent combination of price and quality of services. Aiyaree Place Hotel is a 3-star hotel. Its infrastructure is fully consistent with the declared category. There is free parking, in the main building you can exchange currency or use an ATM. At the reception you can arrange a transfer, order a taxi or use the services of a tour desk. Fax and photocopying machines are also installed here. Those who wish can use the deposit safe. Cell rental is free. Also, without an additional amount, you can use the wireless Internet in the lobby. Since Aiyaree Place Hotel (3 *, Pattaya, Jomtien Beach) is located in seven-story buildings, for the convenience of customers, modern elevators run between levels. There is also a concierge service.

The hotel area is fenced off from the street. She is guarded around the clock. In many places, camcorders are installed.

Excursions

Aiyaree Place Hotel (Pattaya, 3 *) has an excellent location. It is located far enough from places where there is noisy entertainment, so it is always quiet and calm, which is very important for young children. Their daytime sleep will not be disturbed by anything.
