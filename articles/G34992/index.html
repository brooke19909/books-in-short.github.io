The recreation center "Rodnichok" (Krasnodar Territory): description, reviews. Recreation centers of the same name

Not yet on vacation? You will need to drive 45 minutes from Krasnodar to be in the city called Goryachiy Klyuch. Recreation centers "Rodnichok" are available in the Samara, Chelyabinsk regions and the Krasnodar Territory. In the article, we will dwell in more detail on the one located in the Valley of Charms. This is the real name of the town in the Krasnodar Territory, where the Psekups River (a tributary of the Kuban), forest and fresh air will make your vacation truly unforgettable.



Recreation center "Rodnichok": description

Various conditions are offered for accommodation, for any income and composition of vacationers. There are six options for staying at the base:

  • For a large company (up to 80 people), a gazebo, barbecue, toilet and fresh water are provided for relaxing during daylight hours. You can hold any outdoor corporate event in nature for a purely nominal fee of 800 rubles.
  • For people who prefer to relax individually, a room in the hotel building is suitable. It can be with or without amenities. A daily stay will cost 1,400 rubles.
  • The remaining four options are separate cottages of different price categories and capacities. Double rooms (from 2700 rubles) are provided for in the Amber building, and for four in the Honey and Jasmine rooms. For extra comfort conditions (satellite TV, kitchen, air conditioning) you will have to pay 5,300 rubles. For a thousand you can stay in triangular wooden houses, designed for two guests.




And the recreation center "Rodnichok" holds banquets. Two rooms can accommodate 160 people at a time. If vacationers have no desire to cook on their own, they also have a tasty and satisfying lunch every day. And what does the base offer for fans of active pastime?

Leisure

Not every camp site can boast of having its own extreme park. But the recreation center "Rodnichok" has its own rope stadium, where not only children but also adults can go through the individual stages of the route. Trampolines are provided for kids, and everyone can participate in a variety of quests. Sports fields are suitable for volleyball and football competitions, the necessary equipment is easy to rent. There is everything you need for playing paintball, cycling trips and even rafting. Kayaks are provided for lovers of rafting on a real mountain river.

Location

You can get to the recreation center on highway No. 4. Landmarks - this is the city of Goryachiy Klyuch and the village of Bezymyannoye. From Krasnodar it is easy to reach by signs. Traveling by car will take about an hour. You can also use public transport. Regular buses, as well as trains and trains run to Goryachiy Klyuch. Next, continue by minibus No. 119. The final destination is the Rodnichok recreation center. Check-in starts at two in the afternoon, so every traveler can get to the appointed time without any problems. For contact you can use the phones published on the official website.

Reviews

All visitors are united by one thing - a positive opinion about the rest in the village. Nameless. The main thing that characterizes the majority opinion is the feeling of comfort. Nature, peace and cleanliness of the territory create conditions for relaxation and peace. What else was remembered recreation center "Rodnichok"? The reviews relate to the service and quality of service, which is called decent and unobtrusive. Among the shortcomings are paid parking and finding private cars in the open sun. Unfortunately, in the hot season there are no awnings. Another negative point, some say the need to book in advance. In season, this should be done in a few months.

What do Chelyabinsk and Tolyatti offer?

For lovers of relaxation on the Middle Volga on the Kopylovo Peninsula (Samara Region), there is also a recreation center "Rodnichok". In the pine forest on Lake Elovoe there is another recreation center of the same name. Chelyabinsk region offers excellent conditions for lovers of mountain lakes in the southern Urals. The lake has created conditions for a beach holiday, as well as active pastime. There is a tennis court, sports grounds for playing games.
