Hotel Blue Green Hotel 3 * Turkey: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Bodrum is one of the most popular regions in Turkey, which every year is visited by a huge number of tourists from around the world. According to many travelers, tickets to hotels here have a relatively low cost, so representatives of almost all walks of life can afford them.

One of the most frequently visited places in Bodrum is Blue Green Hotel 3 * (Turkey). Reviews about this hotel can be found on various thematic sites, as well as on the personal pages of travelers posted on social networks. As a rule, they are positive, with recommendations to visit this place.

general information

The hotel was built in 1996. Throughout the entire period of its existence, regular repairs are carried out in the territory of this resting place and in its buildings during the absence of tourists. Thanks to this, the hotel currently looks modern and beautiful. The appearance of the buildings attracts tourists, often in reviews of Blue Green Hotel 3 * it is said that inside its buildings, fresh repairs and modern furniture were installed. In 2016, another reconstruction was carried out here, during which the technical equipment of the rooms was improved and the climate equipment was updated.

Location

The hotel is located in the small town of Gundoan. This settlement belongs to a large resort region called Bodrum. Gundoang city center is only a couple of kilometers from the hotel. It is not far away. And if you want to get to the central part of Bodrum, tourists have to cover a distance of 25 km. However, many do this in a car rented at the hotel. According to those who were vacationing here, you should definitely visit the center of the region, since there is a huge amount of entertainment focused on different interests.

The nearest airport, where all foreign tourists arrive, is located in Antalya, which is 45 km from here.



Rooms

All apartments that are available in the hotel are located in its 15 buildings. One of them - the highest (7 floors) is the main one. It has 168 rooms, in which everything is arranged in such a way that guests are as comfortable as possible. For convenience, one elevator is equipped here.

All other rooms are located in fourteen buildings, each of which extends up two or three floors. In total, Blue Green Hotel 3 * (Bodrum) presents 479 apartments. All of them are divided into three categories, depending on the conditions provided: Club, Hotel and Family.

In all rooms, regardless of the level of comfort, there is a private bathroom with a standard set of sanitary ware, as well as a bathroom. In addition, tourists are provided with a set of bath accessories, large bathrobes and towels.

The rooms of all categories have air conditioners, with the help of which vacationers can adjust the temperature regime favorable to themselves.

Family

Blue Green Hotel 3 * has a small number of apartments of this category - only 38. Each of the rooms has a small area - 40 square meters. m, which is divided into two separate rooms. These rooms are located in small buildings.

Each room has everything you need for a comfortable sleep and rest. Tourists note that everything here is decorated in a classic style, in a combination of beige and white shades. It is this color scheme that sets guests for an excellent holiday.

Here, the furniture set is a large double bed and two single beds. In addition, each room has a wardrobe, as well as a pair of cabinets near each bed - on them tourists can put small things they need every day. For a comfortable stay of guests, a safe, a minibar and a telephone with the ability to connect to an international communication line can be installed in the room, however, such services will need to be paid daily.

For free, guests are entitled to watch TV in the apartment. It is connected to a cable broadcasting system.



Hotel

Rooms of this type are located in the main seven-story building, they are small in size (25 sq. M.) And consist of only one room, designed for two guests.

There is a double bed made according to European standards. In some apartments it is replaced by two single beds, which also have orthopedic mattresses. Hotel guests can enjoy a noble combination of white and brown colors in the interior, which, according to tourists, is pleasing to the eye and does not cause irritation. Many recommend staying in such apartments to those vacationers who are forced to combine vacation with work. There is a well-equipped area with a desk and chair. In addition, the rooms have a storage space for clothes and a pair of cabinets for small items. All apartments are required to have wall-mounted TVs, the plasma screens of which broadcast satellite channels. Many travelers are pleased that among them there are several Russian speakers.

The rooms have private balconies with magnificent views of the sea, mountains or flowering gardens. From the upper floors it is possible to view the city panorama.

Club

This category of rooms is located in separate small buildings. All of them are quite comfortable, overlooking the sea or beautiful nature.

Tourists note the bright, youthful design of this type of apartment in Blue Green Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Bodrum). Here, the standard white-brown gamma in the interior is harmoniously combined with a contrasting blue color. Some rooms have personal exits to the balcony, which is furnished: there are a couple of chairs and a table. Many travelers say that it is pleasant to have intimate conversations with a cup of hot coffee or tea. By the way, drinks can be prepared using the substation that is installed in the room.

There is a large bed for two guests as a sleeping place; two bedside tables are installed near it. There is a large wardrobe for clothes; a working area is equipped along the wall. Near the balcony, guests can sit on a pair of soft chairs, at a round table.

In their free time, tourists relaxing in Club apartments can watch their favorite shows on TV, showing some Russian-language channels.



Conditions for children

Guests of Blue Green Hotel 3 * (Bodrum) in their reviews often note the fact that the hotel is ideal for families with children. Such comments are fairly easy to explain. The apartments of the hotel and on its territory have all the conditions for a comfortable stay not only for adults, but also for small guests.

First of all, tourists pay attention to the possibility of installing a playpen type bed in the room, designed for ages from 4 to 12 years. Some guests who rested at this hotel with their kids consider it a big minus the lack of the ability to rent a pot or stroller.

On the territory of Blue Green Hotel 3 * there is a wonderful kids club, where, according to many parents, there is a wonderful team of animators who can entertain kids of all ages. By the way, as they note, some of them are fluent in Russian, which is a positive point for guests from Russia.

In the open area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel there is an aquazone, where two special pools are equipped for children, in which everything is arranged in compliance with safety standards. Not far from it there is a large platform where small guests can actively spend their free time.



Nutrition

Tourists often in their comments about the Blue Green Hotel 3 * (Bodrum / Gumbet) write that the food system is organized here in accordance with the concept of "all inclusive". All those who bought a ticket and stay at the hotel have the opportunity to attend the buffet in two restaurants. One of them serves Italian cuisine, and the other - Turkish. According to travelers, the food here has a rather interesting taste and is also very nutritious. It is served in an original way and in rather big portions.

If desired, anyone can visit any of the six bars located on the hotel territory, one of which ("Disco") is paid. The menu offers a wide range of various drinks, many of which are locally produced. It is also possible to order light snacks and dessert.



Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle recommend that while staying at the hotel, they should definitely look into the gym, equipped with a large number of necessary equipment. According to them, professional instructors work here, who, if necessary, will help to complete some exercises and write training programs (for an additional fee).



Tourists have the opportunity to arrange various team games. There are all conditions for this. On the territory of Blue Green Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Bodrum / Gumbet) there is a beautiful golf course, as well as a tennis court.

Near the hotel buildings there is an excellent aquazone, in which there is a huge sun terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas, in the center of which there are four swimming pools with slides.



Beach

Blue Green Hotel 3 * has its own beach site with a platform, which, according to tourists, is perfectly arranged for a comfortable stay. Its total length is 300 meters. Across the strip, it is lined with large sun loungers, which vacationers can use, and for free. Umbrellas and mattresses are also issued here, but towels will have to pay rent.

Many tourists talk about the fact that along the coastline there are many attractions that will appeal to both children and adults.
