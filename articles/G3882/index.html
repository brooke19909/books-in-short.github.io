Mortgage

It is no coincidence that housing lending was chosen as the main activity. Today, not every Russian can save up for his own house or apartment. Meanwhile, a little overpaying, it will be possible to acquire housing after the conclusion of an agreement with the bank. What does the Russian mortgage bank offer ? Reviews show that everyone can buy real estate both in a new building and in the secondary market. After the conclusion of the contract, the client can immediately move into the purchased housing, register his relatives in it. This is a great alternative to savings.
