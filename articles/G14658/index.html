Water Park "Niagara" in Krasnodar - the best place for the whole family

More than a year ago, a beautiful open-air water park opened in the suburbs of Krasnodar. This place was created by all modern technologies, there is a large number of various entertainments for any age category. Incredible foam parties are held at night.

What is located on the territory of the water park





Water Park "Niagara" in Krasnodar - Great place to relax with the whole family. For the youngest visitors there is a special children's aquacomplex. On the territory of Niagara there are a large number of attractions and entertainments that will appeal to both children and adults. Of the slides and attractions here are:

  • slide "pigtail";
  • multislide slide;
  • hill "tabagan";
  • slide "wave";
  • swimming pool;
  • children's aquacomplex.

There is a lot of entertainment here, so you can spend a whole day in the water park and not notice how time has flown by. In addition to standard entertainment, various entertainment programs are often held here, you can do water aerobics. At night, the Niagara Waterpark in Krasnodar turns into a popular place where young people gather. Great foam parties are held here - noisy and funny.

Do not forget about the safety rules: it is forbidden to enter the territory with bracelets, chains, rings, hairpins and other jewelry items.

Infrastructure, work schedule

During the construction of the water park all the details were provided. There are comfortable, and most importantly, clean showers and changing rooms. In case of injury or other unforeseen situation, the first-aid post will help. If visitors are tired of having fun and just want to sit and eat, there is a wonderful restaurant. Vacationers arriving by car can use the parking lot right next to the water park.



Water Park "Niagara" in Krasnodar works seven days a week, from 10:00 to 19:00. At the same time, do not forget that from 14:00 to 15:00 sanitary works are carried out in the territory, and the water park does not work.

After 19:00, cleaning is repeated for two hours. Then the slides and aquacomplex stop their work, but it is possible to spend time in the restaurant and by the pool until 03:00. It was at this time that a large number of young people gathered here.
