How to hem a collar on a military uniform? The main nuances

During the period of exercises or various training events, persons liable for some time are in the fields. According to hygiene standards, they must be provided with specialized clothing for the field. To keep the uniform in the norm, for this you need to hem a clean gateway every day. It is cut out of white cotton fabric. How to hem a collar on a military uniform?

Necessary fittings





To work, you will need:

  • threads
  • needle;
  • the collar.

Binder material

A collar is a part that is sewn with hand stitches to the stand of the collar. It is necessary in order to prevent contamination of the product itself in the neck. This item can be bought at stores where military hardware is sold.





Using a centimeter tape, you can measure the length of the collar, this is necessary in order to know the size of the collar before buying it in the store for your shape. If for some reason you did not buy a ready-made stitched collar, then a self-prepared one will suit him. How to make a collar yourself?

DIY item

To do this, you need to prepare a white cloth called calico. After the purchase, it is advisable to iron it, so that later you will not find any creases on the canvas and it was easier to cut. If you have prepared the material, then after that you can proceed to cutting the gateways: do as much as you need.

For accuracy of the size of the part, you need to measure the length and width of the stand-up collar. When cutting, it is necessary to take into account the length and width of this part, not forgetting that if it is folded in two layers, then the width will increase by one time, as well as additionally add 0.5-0.7 mm on each side to the hem so that it takes a rectangular shape. This sidewalk is ironed on all the sides that were taken into account for bending, only after that we double it and turn it over to wet heat treatment. Such a prepared part can be used to process the gate with manual stitches.

Recommendations

How to hem a collar on a military uniform? Tips will help you get the job done right. A well-ironed new collar is sewn to the collar in the rack area on the tunic, which has undergone wet heat treatment. The length of the thread is from 70 to 100 cm. If the standard universal collar is at your fingertips, then its length can be adjusted when sweeping the collar onto the main product. Before starting the work process, the part is placed on the collar of the unfolded tunic in order to determine the size ratio.





If the white collar when laying out turned out to be longer than the main collar, then you can clearly determine how much it is necessary to fold the cotton fabric so that it turns out evenly and equally along the edges during the period of troweling. If the binder material looks shorter, then you need to determine which gap you need to step back from the edge of the turn-down collar at the same level on both sides. A properly sewn collar looks perfect if there is symmetry.

Work Execution Rules

How to hem a white collar on a military uniform? The thread should be white and worn into the needle in a single form. Basting of a part advances along its perimeter. The thread stitch should be 1-2 mm from the edge of the cotton material. How to hem a collar on a military uniform and arrange it so that it is comfortable to work with it?

To do this, we will place the tunic so that its turn-down collar is close to us, it will be much more convenient to align the straight line of the seam, which is located at the top. We will tuck the hem of the coarse calico fabric and proceed to the first stitch, moving away from the edge of 1-2 mm, not forgetting that the bundle of thread should be under the collar. Then we place the sewing material to the collar as it will lie, and we completely pierce the fabric part and the collar with the needle in the place where the thread comes out, but the needle and thread punctures should be at a very small level from each other, barely noticeable and should not coincide.

The same action occurs when the punctured seam is returned from the outside of the mold so that the thread is not visible. After we fastened the collar, we immediately sew the first inner stitch. Then we begin to flash the upper side. Pressing the binder material with the fingers to the main product, while creating the desired shape. After this action, the collar will be better to lie on the neck, but if you do not press and do this roundness, then after sweeping, creases or folds may occur either on the collar or on the main product. By this sequence, we continue to sweep the line. The stitch should be no more than 2 cm.





When we finished hemming the upper line, then go to the other side of the collar and immediately bend the excess fabric and continue the manual seams. Then you will go to the bottom seam processing. How to hem a collar on a military uniform without difficulty? For this process, you need to be patient and follow the instructions. And after a short period of time you will succeed.
