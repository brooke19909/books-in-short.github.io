Rayong (Thailand): reviews. Rayong's best beaches: reviews

In the past few years, Thailand has become a promised land for many Russians . The country of eternal smiles was christened by experienced tourists. And indeed it is. The locals are so welcoming and hospitable that in this country you feel as if you are visiting a good friend. Most often, for travel choose the untwisted destinations: Bangkok, Phuket, Pattaya. Do you want to visit Rayong? The reviews of those who have been there eloquently hint that perhaps there is no better place to stay, especially with children.

Rayong (Thailand) on the map

If you open the atlas, then this is an amazing place you will notice in the south of the country. It is conveniently located on the coast of the Gulf of Thailand, so you will not have problems with swimming.

In fact, Rayong is a continuous beach with amazing sand, stretching over a hundred kilometers. It is adjacent to the Moon archipelago and islands such as Koh Samet, Koh Man, Koh Cody. Only 220 km separates this province from Bangkok. Pattaya is also at hand. It is advisable to pre-find Rayong (Thailand) on the map in order to plan sightseeing routes during your vacation.

A bit of history

Once upon a time, during the war with the Burmese army, Rayong became famous for his courage. A detachment of patriots, led by commander Paya. So he broke through the screen of enemies, reached a quiet province and created a fleet here. Thais still remember this ancient feat. Maybe that is why they relate to Rayong with special trepidation.

Rayong today

It is probably useless to talk about the beauty of this amazing place. This must be seen in person. But one cannot fail to note the cozy charm that Rayong (Thailand) possesses. Photos of tourists eloquently demonstrate this feature.

The province, like all of Thailand, is made up of contrasts. Majestic mountain landscapes are adjacent to gently sloping valleys where orchards and rubber groves grow. Huge waterfalls, talkative and sparkling, and quiet, cozy bays complement each other. Small but diverse Thailand. Rayong District is administratively divided into 6 parts:

  1. Pluac Deng.
  2. Mueang Rayong.
  3. Ban Kai.
  4. Klang.
  5. Bang Chang
  6. Wang Chan.

In all these areas, the main occupation is agriculture and fishing. Exotic fruits such as durian, mangosteen, rambutan grow here. And if the first of them causes mixed feelings, then the last 2 are very tasty. Almost all tourists like them. Appreciate the Asian delicacy and you. Then there will be something to tell friends at home.

In addition, only here they prepare the national Thai brand - Nam Pla sauce. It is one of the important dishes in the cuisine of this country.

To Rayong for warmth and sunshine

Excellent weather conditions attract tourists to Rayong (Thailand). Traveler reviews indicate that you can relax there all year round. There is no reason not to believe these allegations. After all, there is a tropical climate, and it has 2 seasons: rainy and dry.

You do not know when it is better to go to Rayong (Thailand)? Reviews from tourists will help you choose the perfect time to travel. Visitors to Tai claim that the season lasts from November to April. This is a worthy alternative to slushy in the off-season and snow-covered Moscow in the winter months. You can easily travel to a fairy tale. Just imagine: in the homeland there is a gray gloom or huge snowdrifts, but here is the beach, turquoise waves, the gentle sun, leaving a chocolate tan.



However, if your vacation falls in the summer months, you too can not deny yourself getting to know Rayong. The period from May to October is called the rainy season, but this does not interfere with the rest. Rayong is famous for short rains that last only a few hours. They just refresh the air, knocking down the heat. In addition, there are no strong storms, which are characteristic of other provinces of Thailand, so nothing will interfere with your swimming in the sea. Moreover, it is possible to splash in the azure waves not only on metrics, but also on the islands. Particularly attractive for tourists is Sammet Island.

We plan a route: how to get to the resort?

So, have you chosen Rayong (Thailand) as the final destination of your trip? Reviews tell in sufficient detail how to get there. Unfortunately, there are no direct flights to this area. But do not despair. Thanks to its convenient location, you will easily find yourself in a beautiful, modern province.

  1. You can get from Bangkok by plane, bus or taxi. The flight will take 45 minutes, but you will spend about 2.5 hours on the train.
  2. Buses run from Pattaya to Rayong. The trip will last no more than an hour.
  3. Planes fly from Samui and Phuket. You will spend about 1 hour in the air.

As you can see, there are many ways to get to Rayong.

Features of rest in the Thai province

Vacationers from all over the world are attracted here primarily by the clean and not crowded beaches of Rayong. This place is also famous for its magnificent landscapes, a huge number of national parks and attractions. Amazing and mysterious Rayong (Thailand). Photos of tourists give us the opportunity to virtually travel around a pretty town. But still it is better to comprehend in its living form its beauty and mystery.



Not so long ago, Rayong was an ordinary fishing village. Tourism infrastructure here began to develop in the late 80s of the last century. Cozy bungalows, trendy hotels, colorful restaurants and supermarkets grew like mushrooms.

However, despite the developed tourism business, Rayong retained some pristine charm. You will find here no less entertainment than in the noisy, sparkling neighbor Pattaya, but the aura of the resort town is completely different. That is why it is chosen by those who long for a quiet rest, silence and peace. Rayong is also suitable for traveling with children. Noisy nightlife here is minimized, so chords of music from the nearest bars will not interfere with your sleep.

Motivation for relaxation: why go to Thailand?

You still doubt: is it worth going to Rayong (Thailand)? Reviews of experienced travelers say: you just need to go there to:

  1. Enjoy a picnic in the Casuarine Gardens.
  2. See a copy of the Enlightened trace in the cave temple.
  3. Admire the celebration of the Chinese New Year and touch the tree of Thaksin the Great.
  4. Pay tribute to the work of Sunthon Phu by visiting the play based on his works. Noisy and vivid performances are held on his birthday - June 26.
  5. Visit the fishing village of Ban Phe to appreciate the local seafood and delicious sauce.

Enough weighty and interesting arguments in favor of a trip to Rayong, is not it?

Choosing a place of residence

Where to stay while staying at the resort? Of course, the first thing that comes to mind is hotels. Rayong (Thailand) is famous for its hotel complexes.

Despite the fact that they are mostly 3 or 4 stars, the service in them is at their best. Rayong Beaches (Thailand): the kingdom of sand and the proximity to the sea

Tourists are attracted to this wonderful place by the opportunity to lie on the beach and swim in the gentle waves of the bay. Rayong's beaches are unique and diverse. And most importantly - they are clean and calm. You can easily find a place to stay alone with your thoughts.

Which one to choose, it's up to you. However, tourists are welcome at any beach.

  1. Hatsaitong Beach . The most popular place with a romantic name "Golden sand beach". In the immediate vicinity of it are hotels, restaurants, shops. So in between lazy meditation on the sand you will have something to do.
  2. Hat Laem Charoen . If you want to not only sunbathe, but also taste the delicacies of the seafood, then here you are. The beach is famous for its fish restaurants.
  3. Xuan Song . It is better to get to it from the village of Ban Phe. This is just the place where you can wallow in silence on the white sand. It often has picnics.
  4. Beaches on the islands . They have remained intact. It feels like a person‚Äôs leg has not yet set foot. Nevertheless, many of them can boast of a well-developed infrastructure. You will have to get to the island beaches by boat.

Rayong Attractions

However, here you will find not only a beach holiday. Curious, inquisitive tourists will easily find food for the mind.

Naturalists will not be left behind. After all, a diverse kaleidoscope of historical monuments, national parks and gardens is at your service.

Take a look at Rayong's photo. Thailand is justly proud of its province. Such a place cannot disappoint. You will personally see this by visiting:

  1. Aquarium . It consists of 3 rooms. One of them is represented by exhibitions. In the second, you can get acquainted with marine underwater life. And in the third is the seashell museum.
  2. Wat Pa Pradu . Thais with reverence cross the threshold of this temple. He is famous for the fact that there is an image of Buddha in an unusual pose for local residents. He lies on his left side.
  3. City tomb . She will introduce you to the features of traditional Thai style. The main decoration of the buildings are the columns.

Among the oases with green pleasures are especially popular:

  1. Princess Mahi Chakri Sirindh Herbal Park . Here is a huge variety of herbaceous plants.
  2. Khao Chamao-Khao . The park is famous for its amazing waterfalls, amazing mountains of limestone and pristine, untouched forests.
  3. Garden Supppatralend . Exotic local fruits are grown here. You can even become a participant in the harvest.

Traditional thai holidays

Who knows, suddenly the date of your stay in this friendly Asian country will coincide with festivities. Thais love holidays, arrange them in various cities. In Rayong you can witness events such as:

  • Fruit fair . Its time is considered to be May-June. At this time, a variety of fruits are presented in abundance on trays. Market squares and rafts are decorated with flowers, exhibitions are held, fun reigns everywhere.
  • Costume Carnival . If you want to get on it, come in November. The venue is Wat Wat Nam. Rowing competitions, religious ceremonies and many other entertainments await you at this time.
