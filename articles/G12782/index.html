3 * Hotels: Induruwa Beach Hotel 3 *, Sri Lanka. Overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Hotel 3 * Induruwa Beach Hotel - a small villa on the shores of the Indian Ocean. Here you can feel the unity with nature, enjoy the local flavor, as well as relax from the worldly bustle of soul and body.

General Hotel Information

The 3 * Induruwa Beach Hotel is a great place for an ocean vacation in Sri Lanka, a popular tourist destination. If the waves seem restless to you, then you can swim in the pool. You can also enjoy the azure surf from the windows of the rooms, because most of them overlook the sea.

Much attention is paid at the 3 * Induruwa Beach Hotel to ensure that the rooms are comfortable and not cause the slightest problem. For this purpose, the apartment has a wide range of amenities. Since the problem of insects, as well as some small animals, is urgent for Sri Lanka, dense mosquito nets are on all windows.





Hotel location

The 3 * Induruwa Beach Hotel is located in the popular resort village of Induruwa. Colombo's world famous tourist center is about 70 km from here. Only 100 meters from the hotel there is a beach (in fact, the mansion stands on the sea coast). Walking just 100 meters to the other side, you will find yourself at the epicenter of spa life, in the midst of many restaurants, cafes, shops and markets.

All tourists are recommended to visit the famous nursery of sea turtles, which will have to travel no more than 1 km. The nearest transport hub is the railway station, which is 3 km from the hotel. But Bandaranaike Airport is more than 100 km from the hotel.





Guest rooms

Hotel Induruwa Beach Hotel 3 * (Sri Lanka) is a cozy three-story mansion, which consists of 90 comfortable rooms. Of these, 84 rooms can be attributed to the standard category. They are designed to receive two guests, who can comfortably accommodate in one large or two single beds.

Another 6 rooms are classified as a suite category. These are large, spacious apartments that consist of two rooms. The bedroom is quite compact and comfortable, and its windows offer views of the ocean. The living area is more spacious and equipped with comfortable furniture, which, if necessary, can serve to accommodate additional guests.





Room Amenities

Each comfortable room of the Induruwa Beach Hotel 3 * hotel (Induruva) provides the following amenities:

  • a prerequisite for living in such a hot area is the availability of an individual air conditioner, which will help establish a comfortable climate in the room;
  • the bathroom has a toilet and shower, as well as an individual cosmetic set;
  • each room has a telephone that provides communication with the reception, and upon prior request, the opportunity to use the international line;
  • in your free time you can enjoy watching cable or satellite channels on a modern TV;
  • Each room has a refrigerator for your grocery stocks and drinks;
  • You can listen to music or local news thanks to the radio;
  • Hotel staff at any time are ready to eliminate all problems that may arise with the equipment of your room.

Entertainment program and wellness

Additional Information

The hotel has a number of rules that govern accommodation in this resort complex:

  • registration and check-in of tourists in guest rooms begins in the afternoon, but you need to leave the room strictly before 11 a.m.
  • Children under the age of 5 stay at the hotel for free;
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring any animals to the hotel;
  • you can use a plastic card to pay for the services received;
  • for newlyweds a surprise is provided in the form of a romantic room decoration with fresh flowers and scented candles.
