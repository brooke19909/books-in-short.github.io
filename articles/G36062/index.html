Alexander Nevsky Sword - mystical weapons and Slavic relic

Alexander Nevsky, aka Alexander Yaroslavich, the son of Prince Yaroslav Vsevolodovich and Princess Toropetskaya, Rostislav Mstislavovna, left an unforgettable mark on the history of Russia. As soon as the conversation comes to him, most of us recall the Battle of the Ice. It was then that the troops under the command of the prince drove the Livonian knights. Not everyone remembers that he received his nickname for another feat. Then the legendary sword of Alexander Nevsky was first mentioned. This event dates back to 1240. In a place called Ust-Izhora in a battle under the leadership of the prince, the Swedes were smashed to smithereens.

In 1549 he was counted among the saints because he refused to unite with the Catholic Church, and thus preserved Orthodoxy in Russia. The great prince was also famous for not losing a single battle.

Mystic sword

Russian troops won, despite their minority. Nevsky was a terrific tactician, so thanks to his intelligence and fearlessness, the soldiers defeated the enemy. There is also a mystical episode in this story. According to legend, the enemy was mortally scared by the sword of Alexander Nevsky, which glowed very strangely. Alexander had excellent command of this weapon, with one blow blowing the heads off to three Swedes at once. But as they say, fear has big eyes. The mystical halo of weapons was most likely given by Swedish soldiers to justify their defeat. And the sword of Alexander Nevsky just fell under the rays of the sun.

The fact is that the Russian troops were located facing the heavenly luminary. His beam fell on a sword raised up, and the frightened Swedish army mistook him for something supernatural. In addition, in this battle, the prince broke a gun on the head of Birger, the leader of the enemies. Having gained victory in this battle, Prince Alexander received his sonorous nickname - Nevsky.

The find of the monks

After the legendary battle, the sword of Alexander Nevsky was placed in the house of Pelgusius. Later, this building burned down and all property, including weapons, remained under its ruins. There is also evidence that in the XVIII century, some monks, farmers discovered a sword when plowing the land.

How it was? The incident dates back to 1711. On the site of the Neva battle, following the decree of Peter I, a temple was founded. Not far from him, the monks cultivated the land for crops. Then they found the legendary weapon, or rather, its parts. They were placed in a chest. The priests decided that the sword should be in the temple. When his building was completely rebuilt, they put parts of the weapon under the foundation, so that the blade became a charm of this place. And the most unusual thing is that since then, indeed, not a single natural disaster could destroy the church.

The October Revolution made adjustments to history: all documents that were in the temple were burned. Not so long ago, historians found the manuscript of Andrei Ratnikov, a white officer and a true patriot. He devoted several pages from his diary to the description of the sword of Alexander Nevsky. The White Guard warrior believed that Russia would remain indestructible as long as a mystical blade was stored on its territory.

How much did the average sword weigh?
