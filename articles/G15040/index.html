Requirements for borrowers at the Russian Agricultural Bank

To get a loan, you need to meet certain criteria:

  • the borrower must be at least 23 years old;
  • until the loan is repaid, the age of the borrower must not exceed 65 years;
  • a person taking a loan must be a citizen of the Russian Federation and have a permanent ‚Äúresidence permit‚Äù;
  • You need to document income.

The last paragraph is worth a closer look. Here, not only your salary is taken into account, but also other incomes, which you must also document.

To increase income and, as a result, available credit limit, can be used:

  • part-time work;
  • entrepreneurial activity (only taken into account when lending for up to 3 years);
  • private practice;
  • pension;
  • personal subsidiary plots (LPH);
  • renting out real estate (taken into account if the borrower is the owner);
  • intellectual property;
  • work under a GPC agreement (civil law).

Note also that at the current place you must work at least 6 months. In addition, over the past 5 years, your total work experience must be at least 1 year.

If you receive income from private household plots, you will have to provide an extract from the household book stating that you have been engaged in this activity for at least a year.

If your own income is not enough, you can attract a co-borrower. Theoretically, this could be any person who meets the same requirements as the borrower. The co-borrower also confirms his income documented.

For customers who have already taken a loan at the Agricultural Bank and faithfully fulfilled their obligations, the bank is more loyal. So in the last place it is enough for them to work out just 3 months. And for the last 5 years you need to gain only six months of work experience.

In total, you will stay in the bank for about an hour. During this time, employees will fill out your questionnaire and conduct an initial verification of the documents submitted. And the loan decision will be reported to you within 3 days.
