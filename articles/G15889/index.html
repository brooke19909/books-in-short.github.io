Iron characteristics

Screen properties

The display here uses a 5.5-inch Super AMOLED panel. The main difference between this screen technology from others is the presence of rich and vibrant colors. Especially the " amoleds " manage to display full black . The standard screen resolution is HD (1280 by 720 pixels). The pixel density per inch is also decent. In any case, to consider individual pixels, according to reviews, with the naked eye is almost impossible. The colors are somewhat cold in standard. But with the help of the built-in adjustment tools, the color temperature can be corrected to almost perfect. The reading mode is very interesting. When you turn it on, the screen becomes similar to E-Ink, which is used in popular " readers " . Thanks to this technology, the eyes will not be so tired when using the smartphone for a long time.
