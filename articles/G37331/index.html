Short description

Tsnyanskoe reservoir is one of the most famous reservoirs in Minsk. It is located in the north-eastern part of the city, in the Green Meadow area. This is one of the most famous and interesting reservoirs. By type, it is artificially bulk. As for the size, the reservoir is small, its area is only 870 square meters. m. The length does not exceed one and a half kilometers, the recorded maximum depth is about 7-8 m. However, this in no way discourages tourists. After all, having reached the coast, the first thing you pay attention to is clear water, which seems simply endless.

The Tsnianskoye reservoir in Minsk (see photo here) is filled by the nearby Drozdy reservoir: water is supplied through water pipes. It was built in 1982. Previously, the reservoir was intended for industrial purposes, but now this place is a tourist recreation area. There are five equipped beaches on its coast, where local residents or city guests can have a great time swimming in the clear water and sunbathing in the warm sand.

Tsnianskoe reservoir in Minsk is in high demand among tourists. In summer, it is difficult to find a free place on the beaches. Thanks to the equipped volleyball court, vacationers can actively spend time on the shore of the reservoir. In addition to water, sun and beach, in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe reservoir there is a park. These so-called health paths are a great opportunity for those who want to lose their accumulated internal energy and make their lives healthy, easy and free.



Offer for children

And how can you entertain children by visiting Tsnyanskoe reservoir? Everything is simple. There is an excellent playground near the reservoir, where children can enjoy playing games, active sports and other entertainments. Sometimes near the equipped playgrounds there are various attractions. This is a paid service, but there are a lot of children here, because they can play enough with their peers. In addition, you can also play tennis on the territory of the reservoir, which is also an indispensable part of an active and healthy vacation.

Fishing

The Tsnyanskoe reservoir (photo below) attracts tourists only in the summer. And who can be met in these places year-round? Of course, avid fishing enthusiasts, ready to sit out even for the most severe frost for hours in anticipation of their prey.

The underwater world of the reservoir is very diverse. But every professional fisherman knows that just throwing fishing rods is not enough for a big catch. First of all, you need to choose the right place for fishing. The choice of bait and top dressing is also very important. Only thanks to these nuances is an excellent catch ensured.



Arriving at Tsnyanskoe reservoir, you should not neglect these rules. At different times of the day and season, you can catch completely different fish. In spring, fishermen expect to catch bream or crucian carp. But not earlier than snow and ice will completely leave the reservoir. Important: until this time, fishing in a pond is very dangerous. In summer, fishermen catch zander and common carp. True, these species of fish are difficult to catch, but in general, sometimes they succeed. In autumn, perch bites very well, and in winter ruffs come across. Therefore, Tsnyanskoe reservoir is quite popular among fishermen.

Vegetation

But besides the animal kingdom, these lands are also attracted by their diverse vegetation. The entire coast is surrounded by beautiful nature. Environmentally friendly air, grass-covered shores, rare but beautiful trees add beauty to this place. It also attracts outdoor enthusiasts and just those who like to walk in the park, located near the reservoir.

Tsnyanskoe reservoir: how to get there?

But how to get to this paradise? Getting to the coast of the reservoir is very simple. It is located in the city, in its north-eastern part. You can get there by public transport or by private car. The distance of 7 kilometers is overcome quite quickly, and in 15 minutes (from the center of Minsk), a corner of paradise opens all its beauties to vacationers.



To summarize

Tsnyanskoe reservoir is a great place for tourist activities, outdoor activities, sports and just for a great pastime. The reservoir is especially in demand in the summer, when the hot season begins. Many people even prefer to spend their holidays on the reservoir, since this does not require large cash costs. Why choose an expensive resort when excellent sand and clear water are very close by.
