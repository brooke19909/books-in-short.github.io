Hotel CHC Elysium Boutique Hotel 5 * (Greece, Crete): overview, description, rooms and reviews

CHC Elysium Boutique Hotel 5 * is a relatively new hotel complex. It was built in 2010, but the last renovation was made in 2015. So the hotel (like everything that it has) is modern and new. But this is only one nuance of all those that make the hotel attractive.





Location

CHC Elysium Boutique Hotel 5 * is located in a place called Analipsi Hersonissos, in close proximity to the beach, as well as numerous shops, bars and other entertainment venues. The airport is located nearby - a 20-minute drive from Heraklion, which is the administrative center of Crete. From the hotel to the center of Heraklion - only 2 kilometers away.

Pretty quickly you can get to the sights, and to simply popular places. For example, the aquarium is located 5 kilometers from the hotel, as is the Crete Golf Club. A little further is the Lychnostatis Folk Museum. But all the most interesting is in Heraklion. This is the Palace of Knossos, the Museum of the Battle of Crete, the sea fortress of Kules, the Venetian walls, and much more.

There is a bus stop within a kilometer from the hotel, from where you can reach any place of interest to tourists.

What is in the hotel?

CHC Elysium Boutique Hotel 5 * offers various services. Take, for example, entertainment. Most people come here for a beach holiday. But, while at the hotel, you can do a lot of things. Take a swim in the pool, for example, or play table tennis. There is also a separate wellness and SPA center. They carry out various cosmetic procedures. Guests can also take a steam bath in the sauna, go for a massage or work out in the gym.

In CHC Elysium Boutique Hotel 5 * there is free Wi-Fi, a 24-hour reception, as well as a service for preparing dinners for tourists on their way. The latter is very helpful, especially if the guests gathered somewhere for an excursion for the whole day.

The staff speaks German, English and Greek. Therefore, intending to go here to relax, it is better to take a phrase book with you - it will not be superfluous.





Restaurants

At CHC Elysium Boutique Hotel 5 * (Crete), guests are served on an all-inclusive basis. Breakfast starts at 7:30 in the main restaurant Euphoria. It ends at ten in the morning. But there is still an early continental breakfast that starts at 5:00. This is suitable for early arrivals / departures and for those who like to get up in the morning and go to the beach while it is empty.

On Sundays, the restaurant organizes brunch (brunch, or early lunch), which lasts from 8:00 to 10:00. During it, guests are served fish and champagne.

From noon to 2:30 p.m. lunch lasts. At this time, the restaurant also hosts cooking shows. Guests take drinks themselves. During lunch, they can enjoy homemade wine, draft beer or something cool.

At 19:00 dinner begins, lasting until 21:30. Every evening is themed here. Cretan, Greek, Minoan, Cypriot, Asian, Italian, Spanish dishes are prepared, as well as much more. Every evening in the restaurant you can find a corner of Cretan cuisine, bio and seafood and diet dishes. Freshly caught fish and lobsters are available at an additional cost.





Bars

From 11:00 to 17:30 you can have a meal in the Bliss snack bar. They serve excellent pizza, sandwiches, popcorn, toast, desserts and, of course, ice cream, tea and coffee.

The hotel also has a Divine bar, open from 17:30 to one in the morning. The all-inclusive system serves water, wine, beer, cocktails, local natural coffee (both plain and filtered), tea, cocoa, juices and soft drinks. Whiskey, freshly squeezed fresh juices, champagne and imported alcohol - at an additional cost.

Accommodation options

The CHC Elysium Boutique Hotel 5 * hotel has several categories of rooms. There are 66 different apartments in the hotel. And all - new, modern, comfortable, distinguished by an elegant interior. Which is not surprising, because the boutique hotel should have just such numbers.

Take, for example, a 2-bed apartment. Their area is 20 square meters. m. Guests can choose for themselves a specific option - either a room with one large and double bed, or with two ordinary, standard. The room opens onto a private furnished balcony (or terrace, depending on the floor on which it is located). Inside there is a huge plasma TV with Smart-TV technology, a telephone, two safes (one of which is for a laptop), a dressing room, trempel, refrigerator, electric kettle and hairdryer.

The apartments also have their own plumbing unit with a bath, toilet and luxury cosmetics. It is also worth knowing that guests are provided with towels (both for the beach and for the bathroom) and slippers with bathrobes. By the way, the mattresses on the beds are from the well-known company Coco-Mat. And bedding released by the brand Guy Laroche. By the way, the apartments boast improved sound insulation and the ability to connect heating.
