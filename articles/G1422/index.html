Samsung 8190: specifications and reviews

A small copy of the flagship device of the South Korean semiconductor giant Galaxy S3 is the Samsung 8190 with the S3 mini prefix. But not only in the dimensions of the device in this case are the main differences. The hardware specifications of gadgets, as software components, have undergone certain changes. It is about these differences that we will talk with you in this review. So, first we find out what the claimed device is.

What owners is the gadget oriented to?

Samsung Galaxy 8190, or, as it was called, "S3 mini", went on sale in 2012. It was a small copy of the flagship gadget from a renowned manufacturer with slightly degraded technical parameters. As a result, this smartphone automatically fell into the middle class solutions segment. Its technical specifications and cost are fully consistent with this class of devices. Now, such devices can only be attributed to "smart" entry-level phones, and then with a big stretch.

Like most devices of this class, in the above list there is no protective case, backup film to protect the front panel of the device and a memory card. Mobile phone central processing unit

Samsung 8190 equipped with a central processing unit NovaThor U8420, which consists of 2 computing modules. In turn, each of them can, if necessary, accelerate to the maximum possible frequency of 1 GHz. At the time sales began in 2012, such semiconductor crystal parameters allowed the smartphone to solve any problems.





Now, when the basic models of mobile devices are equipped with a minimum of a quad-core CPU, the capabilities of this chip are only enough for the most simple tasks (watching videos, listening to music, reading books, surfing Internet portals and simple games). Even mid-level toys on this gadget will definitely not start. The same applies to the most demanding three-dimensional games of the latest generation.

Display and its characteristics

Samsung 8190, like most modern models of mobile devices of this eminent manufacturer, boasts a bright and colorful display, which is based on the first-class Super AMOLED matrix. The screen resolution in this situation is equal to modest by today's standards and advanced indicators for 2012 at 800x480 pixels. The density in this case is 233 ppi.





It goes without saying that without special technical means it is almost impossible to distinguish a single pixel on the display surface with the naked eye. Another important plus of this touch screen is the maximum viewing angles. Even so, image distortion does not occur.

Memory

Samsung Galaxy 8190 Mini equipped with 1 GB of RAM. About 640 MB of them are used by system software. The rest of 360 MB is allocated for launching user application software. The capacity of the internal drive could be either 8 GB or 16 GB. About 4 GB of them were occupied by system software. That is, in one case, the owner of the smartphone could count on 4 GB, and in a more advanced modification of this gadget, this value automatically increased to 12 GB. There was also a slot for installing an additional memory card. Its maximum size could reach 32 GB.
