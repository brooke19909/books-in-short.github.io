Important Hotel Information

First Class Hotel 5 * (Turkey) was opened in 2005. It occupies a vast territory of 36,000 square meters. m. It is a six-story building, built in a modern style, well-groomed garden and large swimming pool. In total, the hotel offers 163 comfortable rooms, including apartments for travelers with disabilities. The complex allows children of all ages. The hotel has a Russian-speaking staff, although most staff understand Turkish and English better.





Hotel check-in starts at 2:00 p.m. and lasts until midnight. When checking in, tourists must present a passport with a photograph and a credit card. No deposit will be charged at the hotel. For a fee, you can use the express check-in service. It is not allowed to check into a hotel with animals of any size. After the rest, tourists must leave their apartments before noon. You can pay for accommodation and services here with Visa and MasterCard bank cards.

First Class Hotel 5 *: room description

The resort complex has 163 cozy rooms. All of them belong to the standard category. 13 of them are allocated for disabled tourists . They have wide doorways, handrails in the bathroom and lowered sinks. The standard room is designed for two adults and one child. The apartment consists of a bedroom, a balcony and a bathroom with a shower. The room has a double and single bed. If necessary, a clamshell is available upon request. The furniture also includes wardrobes and a desk. Ceramic tiles are laid on the floor. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãany room is 28 square meters. m

Apartments First Class Hotel 5 * (Turkey) are equipped with all necessary amenities. Each room has an individual air conditioning with remote control, TV with connected cable TV. Among the channels you can find several Russian speakers. Wi-fi is provided free of charge in all rooms , however, due to the network load, its speed is quite low. For a fee, tourists can rent an electronic safe for storing valuables or a minibar with alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as make international calls. In the bathroom, guests will find a hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers and toiletries in the dispensers.





Employees clean up their premises every day, and linen and towels are changed at least three times a week. In addition, 24-hour room service is available for a fee. It includes the delivery of drinks, newspapers and food within half an hour after placing the order.

First Class Hotel 5 *: hotel infrastructure

Developed infrastructure has this hotel. It is designed for unassuming tourists who prefer to spend their holidays on site. The building has several shops where you can buy swimwear, beachwear and shoes, imported alcoholic drinks and tobacco products, food and numerous souvenirs. You can also visit the beauty salon and hairdresser. You can return your dirty things to your local laundry. Wireless internet is available in the lobby.





For guests with their own car, public parking is open. Tourists can also rent a car for a while by filling out an application with the administrator at the reception. He works around the clock and also carries out currency exchange, photocopy of documents, sale of tickets for excursions. He can also call a paid doctor in the room. The complex has its own business center with a conference room, which seats more than 200 delegates. It is equipped with modern technology (projectors, laptops, interactive whiteboard, fax, printer).

Beach and Pool

A quiet holiday is offered to tourists by the First Class Hotel 5 * hotel. The beach is located 20 meters from the main building. It is separated from it by a highway through which an underground passage is drawn. Near the beach there is a small river. The hotel has its own private beach area. It is sandy, but in some places there is sharp pebble. There are stony boulders on the coast, so it is not recommended to go into the sea without slippers. The beach is additionally equipped with a wooden pier. Its length is 20 meters.





The complex has 2 outdoor pools connected by a sun terrace. Tourists can ride on 3 water slides that work every day with a lunch break. Both the beach and pool have free sun loungers and sunshades. Beach towels can be borrowed at the reception, paying a deposit for them. In cold weather, there is an indoor freshwater heated pool.

Sports and Entertainment

First Class Hotel (Turkey, Alanya / Kargicak ) offers many options for outdoor activities. Tourists can play volleyball or table tennis at the beach. Billiard and computer rooms are available for guests who are charged an hourly fee. Every day the hotel has a team of Russian-speaking animators. They conduct group aerobics training for guests, competitions and show programs . In the evening, a disco is organized for adults. Admission is free, but drinks and cocktails are provided for a fee. Near the hotel there is a field for playing futsal and a gym.

For tourists looking for a relaxing holiday, First Class Hotel 5 * (Turkey) has a wellness center. Visitors are offered massage courses, facial and body treatments, as well as a Turkish bath, sauna and jacuzzi.

Conditions for tourists with children

Many tourists with children annually visit the First Class Hotel 5 * hotel, the service of which is described in detail in this article. For small guests discounts on accommodation are provided. For example, children under 12 years old who sleep in their parents' beds can generally stay at the hotel for free. Babies under 2 years old are provided with cots, and their accommodation is also not paid. In the main restaurant, tourists can use special chairs for feeding.

An entertainment program for children is no different from other Turkish hotels. Both pools have shallow bathing sections (35 cm). A mini club with a Russian-speaking animator is open daily , where all children from 3 to 12 years are invited. You can have fun on the playground, open in the fresh air.

Positive feedback from guests

Tourists vacationing in the First Class Hotel 5 *, leave conflicting reviews . They note that the furnishings of the rooms and the level of service here do not correspond to the declared star rating. In their opinion, for a similar price in Antalya you can find a hotel with the best service. However, the complex still has advantages. In their reviews, tourists indicate the following:

  • Proximity to the sea. The balcony offers great views of the coast.
  • Quiet setting in the evenings. If the weather in Alanya is good, then you can not close the balcony at night.
  • A varied diet, which includes a large number of greens, vegetables, fruits, meat and fish. Guests also note that the restaurant very tasty cooked side dishes with spices.
  • Polite hotel administrators who carefully listen to the guest comments and try to solve them quickly.
  • Excellent children's and adult animation, which includes a fire show, oriental dance lessons, organization of discos.




Negative guest reviews

However, most often tourists who visited First Class Hotel 5 * leave negative reviews . In them they list many shortcomings that greatly influenced their impressions of the rest. They do not recommend other people to come here, but they plan to choose another place for rest next time. Among the reasons they list such disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Dirty rooms. On the furniture you can see a thick layer of dust and debris left over from previous guests. The cleaners do not respond to comments from guests.
  • There is an unpleasant smell of damp throughout the hotel.
  • Internet in the room does not always work. Its speed is not enough even to update social networks.
  • On the beach there are not enough sunbeds for all tourists, so you have to occupy them in the early morning.
  • The terrible state of plumbing in the bathroom. The flush button in the toilet does not work, there is no bathtub, taps flow, spraying water on the floor.




To summarize

Unfortunately, the hotel has a controversial reputation. Families with children and youth companies come here. However, many tourists who rested here believe that it does not correspond to the declared category. Therefore, they recommend choosing a different resort complex, because there are plenty of them in Alanya .
