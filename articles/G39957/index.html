Origami bow: outline

The oldest art of creating various figures from paper is called origami. Developing and improving, over time, ancient art combined several different techniques. Currently, with their help you can create all kinds of excellent crafts. It can be both classic flowers and figures of various animals.

By reading this article, you will learn how to make an origami bow. And also make sure on your own the simplicity of making this amazing paper craft and it is pleasant to surprise your loved ones and relatives.

The ability to make a bow-origami is useful to you both for decorating ideas, and for decorating a gift for any holiday. So, let's start the delightful creation with our own hands!

Take a simple sheet of A4 paper. By bending diagonally, cut off the excess paper. The left square sheet fold in half, then unfold.

Then double again in the other direction and straighten again. Then fold it by connecting together two opposite corners, expand and align two opposite corners. Then bend the corner of the resulting rhombus to the center of the square sheet. Expand, you get additional fold lines in the center. Begin to bend along these lines the square that turned out in the center of the sheet, and wrap the folds diverging from its corners.

Then fold the rhombus as in the previous step. You need to fold the sheet back, but already to the center of the sheet, you need to bend deep into a small square. Now fold the resulting edges first on one side, then flip and bend on the other side. Then expand the resulting part again. Turn what happened inside out.

Now you need to cut the bent edges with oblique lines, to the middle to the fold line. Then bend the fourth part of your sheet and bend the edges to a line running in the center. After that, bend the opposite side and bend the opposite edges of the line to the center. Parts that you have already bent to bend these corners, and leave bent in one direction. Bend these edges towards each other. And flip the craft. The edges of the corners need to be bent, applying the corners to the center of the bow. After that, you just have to hide the corners under the center. At the final stage, simply cut the edges of the parts under the bow with an angle. Your origami bow is ready.

DIY paper material

All you need is to prepare paper for the bow and scissors. To do this, you do not have to use special origami paper. It is permissible to use the colorful pages of any glossy magazine. This option will definitely give your design a special touch. Interestingly, the most ordinary newspaper pages can do. A good option for crafts would be ordinary thin color single-sided paper. No matter what material you use, you will definitely get a beautiful origami bow.

Application

You can decorate any gift with a beautiful bow: a bonbonniere for sweets, a cake or a postcard. It can be an interesting hair clip or brooch.

Also, with a bow, you can decorate a mirror or photo frame, flower pot. To decorate the gift wrapping with a craft, you just need to stick it on the box using double-sided tape. A made origami bow will look beautiful, decorating the packaging and exuding the warmth of your heart.

Conclusion

Origami bow - this is just one of the options that can be made of paper. There are a considerable number of different paper variations that you can use for decoration. Today, items made by yourself are of particular importance.
