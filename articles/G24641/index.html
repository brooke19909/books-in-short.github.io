Dubai Marriott Hotel Al Jaddaf 5 *: description of the hotel, rooms, infrastructure, photos and reviews

Dubai is known to have very few hotels with its own beach. Therefore, when choosing a hotel, tourists are guided by the amenities, the comfort of the rooms and the availability of a free transfer to the coast. And how then not to remember about network hotels? After all, they have the same level of service, regardless of where the representative office is located - in Dusseldorf, Kiev or Dubai.

The Marriott Network is well established. It is also popular with wealthy tourists. In cities, its hotels are located in the center, and in resorts - in excellent green areas. In this article we will consider the hotel Dubai Marriott Hotel Al Jaddaf 5 * in the city of Dubai (UAE). In our description of rooms and infrastructure, we took into account the reviews of tourists who have recently visited this hotel.

Location

This Marriott office is located far from the sea, in the Al Jaddaf area, as the name implies. The first guests (the Dubai Marriott Hotel Al Jaddaf 5 * opened in 2014) complained that there was nothing outside - no shops, no cafes. But Dubai is a rapidly developing metropolis. Nearby lay the city‚Äôs largest motorway, Sheikh Zayed Road. A lot of public transport runs along this traffic intersection, so taxis are no longer the only way to travel around the city.

In the immediate vicinity of the Marriott representative office in Dubai, there are such iconic sights as the Burj Khalifa Tower, Sheikh Zabila Residence, Golden Souk Market and Dubai Mall. Tourists claim that the city center is a 10-minute drive away. Dubai Airport from the hotel is only 8 kilometers, therefore, with a collective transfer, they are brought here one of the first. The exact address of Marriott Hotel Al Jaddaf 5 * is Bur Dubai, Oud Metha Road. A five minute drive away is the International Financial Center. Therefore, businessmen of high rank often stay at the hotel.

Territory

Since the Marriott office is located almost in the center of the metropolis, surrounded by urban development, it does not have its own territory. All infrastructure is concentrated in a wide and impressive eight-story building. Parking in the basement, a rooftop pool - this is how you can briefly describe the territory of the hotel. But guests do not blame for the lack of a park and spacious green lawns. A charming panorama of the Bourj Khalifa skyscraper opens from the pool terrace.

In a wide building, 4 elevators work to prevent lines. On different floors there are restaurants, bars, a spa, gym and beauty salon. Tourists mention that the specific needs of people with disabilities are taken into account in the new building: there are ramps and wide doors for strollers. And 11 rooms from the hotel rooms are reserved to accommodate such guests. But in the Dubai Marriott Hotel Al Jaddaf 5 * pets are not allowed, which tourists consider a disadvantage. In addition, a deposit is required upon check-in, which for some reason depends on the length of stay in the hotel.

Number of rooms

The hotel has 351 rooms for guests. The rooms are divided into categories. The cheapest is considered a guest room. They have no balconies, but there is a wide king size bed. We'll talk about filling numbers a bit later. Standards at the hotel are called Dubai Skyline View. They are also without balconies, but are located on the upper floors of the building, so they offer a wonderful view.

Most rooms are deluxe. Their area is 40 square meters, there are small balconies. Guests of the executive rooms, in addition to the additional amenities in the room, enjoy the privileges of club members. They can visit the living room, where breakfast is served for them, and in the evenings they are served snacks, tea and coffee. Executive Suite consists of two rooms. Guests are also owners of a club card. The pride of the hotel is its suites. They bear their own names: ‚ÄúPresidential,‚Äù ‚ÄúSheikh Scored,‚Äù and so on. Beach and Pool

Dubai Marriott Hotel Al Jaddaf 5 * is located far from the sea. Therefore, for his guests, he provides a free shuttle service to two public beaches - Kite and Jumeirah. Bus schedules vary depending on the season. It must be recognized at the reception. Registering for boarding a bus is not required. Services

Dubai Marriott Hotel Al Jaddaf 5 *, in addition to transfer to the beach, provides free trips to the Dubai Mall and to the nearest metro station. The schedule of these buses can also be found at the reception. By the way, one of the employees of the latter speaks Russian. For guests, the hotel provides several more free services.

Wi-Fi is available in the lounge area, and the sauna and jacuzzi are unlimited in the spa center. For those who can not imagine life without sports, the doors of the gym are always open. On one of the floors of the building there is a playroom for children. Parking is also free for guests. The hotel has a beauty salon, laundry, currency exchange.
