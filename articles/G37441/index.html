The charm of the recreation center (Spruce) "Rodnichok"

On Lake Elovoe, which is located in one of the most picturesque places in the South Urals, there is a cozy and beloved by many recreation center "Rodnichok". Birch and pine forests with small ponds, one of which is Lake Elovoe.

Description of recreation center "Rodnichok"

The Rodnichok base is not located here by chance. Firstly, the places here are not only picturesque, but also environmentally friendly. Near the recreation center (Spruce) "Rodnichok" is the Ilmensky reserve, and next to the base there are many more small lakes. Another indisputable plus is the healing and warm water of the lake. Due to the fact that the lake is sheltered from the winds, the water here is kept warm, prolonging the swimming season by almost a month. And this bonus from the nature of vacationers gives the opportunity for a longer rest.





Rodnichok is open all year. There are several types of accommodation for visitors. If you prefer accommodation in the rooms of the recreation center (Spruce) Rodnichok, then you can stay in double, triple and quadruple rooms located in the capital buildings. One of the buildings (2 floors) has 100 seats, its amenities are located on the floors. The second building (4 floors) is designed for 240 beds, here already amenities are located directly in the rooms.

Things to do in Rodnichka

In the summer, you can settle in a boarding house on the shore, which stands separately, which is designed for 100 people, or in cozy small forest houses, each with 2 rooms. In both cases there is central heating, so you are not destined to freeze there.

Meals in a separate dining room are three meals a day. The base also has an excellent beach, from water sports catamarans and small boats are available, in good weather - water slides. Active vacationers are not forgotten either. For them, volleyball, basketball, football, table tennis and a separate tennis court, billiards are provided. Children can use their favorite trampolines and carousels.

For those who love a more relaxing holiday in the arsenal of the recreation center (Spruce) "Rodnichok" there is a sauna, fishing, as well as karaoke, short excursions and forest walks. In the evenings, a bar and a disco are open for all guests of the base. Modern music, great acoustics, parties on certain topics as a wonderful end to the day. For kids there is a games room, sports equipment, summer stage, animators, playgrounds where your child can have fun in his own way.





On the territory of the recreation center spruce "fontanel" there is a shop, an outdoor cafe, a water park, a parking lot, a barbecue area, a kiosk, sports equipment rental, a bathhouse with a small pool, a meeting room, a seminar, a children's room with a teacher.

The recreation center "Rodnichok" (Spruce): prices

- In the first building, a double room without meals will cost you 450 rubles, with meals - 900 rubles. Triple and more - 400 rubles. and 850 rubles. respectively.

- In the second building - double 700 rubles. and 1150 rubles., 3- and 4-seater - 650 rubles. and 1100 rubles., 4-seater (with bath, refrigerator and TV) - 700 rubles. and 1150 rubles.

- Superior double rooms cost 1000 rubles. and 1550 rubles.

- The fee for living in a separate house is 1100 rubles. and 1550 rubles.





Also, a child under 4 years old (without space and food) is free; children from 4 to 12 years old will cost 250 rubles extra meals (meals separately).

Like any recreation center, Rodnichok (Spruce) reviews have their own, very positive, since a wide variety of activities, a well-thought-out place for the base, responsive and cultural staff, and much more create in the whole a unique vacation that gives new strength in vacationers.
