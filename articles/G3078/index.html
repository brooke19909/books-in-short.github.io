Rest, Sverdlovsk region: recreation centers “Crystal”, “Baltym”, “Chusovaya”, “Beloyarka”, “Forget-me-not”

Every corner of Russia, whether it is north, south, west or east, has its own charm. To a large extent, this applies to the mountainous and lowland territories of the Urals. On the rivers flowing here and on the spreading blue lakes you can get a delightful vacation. Sverdlovsk region, recreation centers which meet the highest requirements, hospitably open the doors in summer and winter. Meet some of them.

The nature of the Sverdlovsk region

The Ural Mountains stretch across the entire territory of the country from north to south , dividing it into West and East. Although not high, they create special natural and climatic conditions on the plains adjacent to them and provide excellent relaxation. The Sverdlovsk region, whose recreation facilities provide the opportunity to spend a full vacation or recharge with energy on the weekend, are located on the West Siberian Plain, the middle and northern parts of the slopes of the Urals. These places are famous for endless forests, majestic cliffs, amazingly beautiful rivers, lakes, man-made ponds. In the okrug of the city of Yekaterinburg, these are the Chusovaya, Pyshma, Sysert, Iset and Lakes Baltym, Krasilovo, Shartash, Shuvakish and others. You can rest on them with your family, a cheerful noisy company, or only together.



Climate

The climate in Zauralie is sharply continental, which means long frosty winters with lush snow cover and moderately warm, but not hot summer months. For residents of the region and all comers, Sverdlovsk Region gives a year-round vacation. Recreation facilities in the cold months offer their guests winter fishing, snowboarding, ice skating, skiing, sledding. In summer, vacationers enjoy boating, catamarans, fishing, horseback riding and hiking in the vicinity, swimming in ponds, picking mushrooms and berries. And of course, all year round, young and old alike enjoy beautiful landscapes and get huge health benefits from the healing scents of pine needles.

Baltym

With this name in the Sverdlovsk region there is a wonderful lake and a village that grew on its shores. The reservoir area is 7.5 km 2 .

Located 20 minutes from the capital of the Urals - Yekaterinburg, it is surrounded by a forest of pine, spruce, cedar and birch trees. In the waters of the lake there are bream, pike, crucian carp, perch. On the banks of the Baltym there are beautiful gentle slopes, beaches, piers from which you can fish. The Balti Recreation Center of the Sverdlovsk Region provides a variety of tourists - from modest, with budget accommodation, to luxury, not inferior to the best world resorts. And on the basis of "Forest Fairy Tale" there are several choices at once - Russian huts with a capacity of 6 to 10 people, alpine houses for two and wigwams. Another highlight of the base is winter swimming in ice holes and snowmobiling, and in summer, training in extreme driving sports cars. For lovers of steam, there is a Russian, Finnish and Scandinavian baths, for kids - a children's town, for celebrations - small and large banquet rooms.

Fairytale “Lukomorye”

This recreation center in the Sverdlovsk region in Baltyma gives its guests an awakening in the morning to singing birds in a birch grove, in the afternoon the opportunity to hunt mushrooms and berries, fish, in the evening enjoy their own cooked barbecue or relax in a cozy cafe, and at night to observe constellations and nebulae. Exactly so, because the Lukomorye has a real telescope. The number of rooms in the base is represented by buildings with spacious rooms furnished with high-quality furniture. For the convenience of guests they have televisions, bathrooms and showers. For children, playgrounds are equipped on the territory, for adults, gazebos, barbecue facilities, a sauna, free parking.



The best "Fun"

On the picturesque shores of the hospitable Baltim Lake, there are many places where you can spend a weekend or celebrate a celebration. One of the most comfortable is called "Fun", a recreation center in the Sverdlovsk region. It is located in a pine forest, which greatly increases the benefits of staying here for the whole organism, and especially for the lungs and nervous system. This base can simultaneously accommodate only about 50 people, which contributes to a cozy home environment. For accommodation, 17 diverse rooms are provided, including standard double and single, superior, family two- and three-room apartments, and a room for those who have a honeymoon. Newlyweds “Fun” gives champagne, flowers and fruits. All rooms have televisions, refrigerators, hygiene rooms, comfortable furniture, paintings on the walls. For those who want to live in seclusion, the base has summer houses for two and four people. In addition to relaxation, you can hold corporate parties, weddings, celebrate anniversaries and other events. For this purpose there is a banquet hall.

Who are welcome in Baltim

Provides the most diverse holiday Sverdlovsk region. Recreation facilities that grew on the banks of its rivers and lakes, were built with the expectation of people with any income level and needs. So, the Baltym camp site is designed for the middle class. They accommodate guests here in two buildings (rooms for 2, 3 and 4 people). On the territory there is a dining room, a paid parking lot, a sauna, sports grounds, a hall for conferences and celebrations, gazebos, barbecue facilities, a climbing wall. And on the basis of "Cones" among 25 rooms with all the amenities of class 11 VIP, guests who have fresh fruit as a gift. In "Cones" there is a hookah room, billiard room, sports grounds, a conference room, a chic restaurant (2 rooms), a parking lot, a Finnish sauna, a Russian bathhouse with birch brooms, barbecue facilities, gazebos, a tent, a children's town. Children under 12 rest on this base for free.



The recreation center "Chusovaya", Sverdlovsk region

Near the village of Sloboda there is the Chusovaya River. She became famous for her sights, "participation" in the filming of films and mention in novels, but most of all for her unique beauty. Chusovaya flows among mountain spurs, overgrown with fir, fir, cedar and birch. In its waters roach, ruffs, pikes, chubs, breams swim, muskrats and beavers live along the banks, and in the vicinity you can find foxes, chipmunks, squirrels, hares, even moose, lynxes and bears. This is the unique place where the Chusovaya recreation center is located. Its comfortable residential buildings and cottages are on the rock "Dog Ribs", original and unique. The rooms are provided in categories of standard, deluxe, junior suite, apartments. The base operates in winter and summer. For leisure activities, fishing, horse riding, riverboating, boating, water skiing, excursions, snowmobiling, ice skating, skiing are offered. On the base you can also hold various celebrations.

Rest on the Beloyarsk reservoir

Beloyarskoe is the largest reservoir in the Sverdlovsk region. It is located on the Pyshma River, famous for its always successful fishing and very beautiful banks.

Here bream and scavengers, burbots and zander, perches and tench, carps and ruffes are caught, and once they even caught a piranha that got there by accident. In addition to fishing, they go sailing to the reservoir, windsurf and arrange ice races. Here is a fabulous place located recreation center "Beloyarka" (Sverdlovsk region). It is approximately 50 km from Yekaterinburg. For accommodation are offered year-round rooms in the building, designed for 2 or 3 people. In summer, you can rent detached houses. They have rooms for 8 people and it is possible to establish an extra bed. Showers and a toilet are located on the floor. On the base you can rent a boat, water ski, banana, play tennis, volleyball or football, horse and trekking, go fishing. There is also a sauna and a cafe with a disco.

Unforgettable vacation in the "Forget-me-not"

There is another wonderful place on the shore of the Beloyarsky reservoir surrounded by centuries-old pines and cedars. This is the recreation center "Forget-me-not" (Sverdlovsk region). Here, for an excellent pastime, absolutely everything is provided - cozy detached cottages with amenities designed for a company or family, playgrounds with barbecue facilities, gazebos and children's pools, a Finnish sauna, a Russian bathhouse with brooms, playgrounds for kids, billiards, fishing, tennis, there are even an artesian well with amazing water. Leisure here includes activities such as yoga classes, special treatment programs to relieve stress, correct weight, and relieve spinal problems.



Where to relax in a big company

About 67 km from the city of Yekaterinburg, on the highway, near the junction at Verkh-Neivinsk, Serov, there is a small but very picturesque forest lake Krasilovo. The water in it is clean, there are a lot of fish, trees approach almost the water itself, but there are some excellent piers. It is in this place that the Krasilovo recreation center (Sverdlovsk region) is located. It consists of several cozy houses that are heated in winter. The capacity of each is up to 20 people. The living conditions in the rooms are quite simple (bunk or double beds, stove, dishes, washbasin), but everything was done with love and diligence. In front of each such house are equipped platforms with barbecue facilities and a gazebo. On the territory there is a sauna, sports grounds, parking. “Krasilovo” is ideally suited for relaxation by a large youth company, as discos and open-airs are often arranged here, to which the most popular DJs of the country are invited. Good here and corporate clients. For events at the base there is a spacious pavilion, in which heating works in winter.

Rest in Upper Sysert

Not far from Yekaterinburg (about 45 km) is the village of Verkhnyaya Sysert. The name comes from the river on the banks of which it grew. In this village there is a large pond, formed in the middle of the XIX century. The old dam is still preserved there. It has a fabulously beautiful peninsula overgrown with coniferous and deciduous trees. It divides the pond into two bays - the North, with a length of about 5 km, and the South, respectively 4 km. Around this beautiful place are numerous recreation centers of the Sverdlovsk region. Sysert has a depth of up to 4.5 m. In clear waters, perch, ruff, chebak splash, and bream, pike live, which is very appreciated by fishermen. The highest rating are the base “Salute”, “White sail”, “Niotan”. “Salute” offers comfortable rooms in the building and detached VIP-cottages. Each of them has its own name - "Family", "Fisherman", "Hunter". In summer, two additional buildings work. The “White Sail” has spacious and very cozy houses for relaxing in winter and summer. For leisure activities on the bases are provided barbecue facilities, gazebos, saunas, sports grounds, summer and winter fishing, skiing, snowmobiling, boats, catamarans are organized. In "Salute" there is a gym. In the "White Sail" billiard room. "Niotan" is a base-center for environmental recreation, where all products are only natural, houses are built from eco-raw materials, rooms are made according to special design. There is a stable at the base, so you can ride on horseback. A lot of interesting events are held here, for example, “Gourmet Evening” with culinary master classes.



Recreation facilities: Zarechny, Sverdlovsk region

Zarechny is a city of orange lanterns. It is located on the Pyshma River, about 40 km from Yekaterinburg. Many recreation centers have been built here. One of them is the Wave, small but cozy. On the "Wave" there is only one 4-story building (for 17 people) with a dining room, a kitchen, equipped with everything necessary, comfortable bedrooms, a bathhouse, a sauna, barbecue facilities, a wonderful beach with clean sand, a paintball field. Equipment for underwater and ordinary fishing is provided. On the "Wave" often hold youth festivals and parties with the best DJs. There is a wonderful Zarechny Podvorye base in Zarechny, where the most affordable prices and the best service await guests, the Zeleniy Bor recreation center, the Lesnoy, Polevskoy, Renda and many others boarding houses.

Rest on the "Crystal" base

In the district of Yekaterinburg, many recreation centers are always happy to welcome guests. "Crystal" of the Sverdlovsk region, located just 30 km from the capital of the Urals and 15 km from the city of Pervouralsk, will undoubtedly be remembered by everyone. This base has grown amid a birch grove, away from the bustle of the city. It offers rooms from economy to VIP class, located in the hotel, cottage and dormitories. The cottage is two-story. On the ground floor there is a large hall with a banquet hall, a TV, a refrigerator, a microwave. On the territory of the base there is a dining room, a cafe, a bar, a shop, gazebos with barbecue facilities, sports grounds, a bathhouse with a small pool, a separate pool with 5 paths, a sauna, a relaxation center, a solarium, a shooting gallery, creative workshops, a spa. The base works year-round.
