Heroes of the Battle of Kulikovo: Rodion Oslyabya

Saint Rev. Andrian in the world - Rodion Oslyabya. One of the famous historical characters of Ancient Russia, the hero of the famous battle with the Mamaian army during the Battle of Kulikovo. His name is immortalized not only by the Orthodox Church, but also by modern culture - the motor ship of the Volga River Flotilla is named after Rodion Oslyaby.



Rodion Oslyabya: biography to ...

Rodion was a native of the Bryansk region. Born supposedly in the city of Lubutsk. He came from an ancient boyar clan and had a close relationship with another hero of the battle on the Kulikovo field - Alexander Peresvet. It is believed that they were brothers. The degree of relationship is indicated as blood. But perhaps they were cousins. Prior to being tonsured as a monk, Rodion, like his brother, served in the princely army and participated in battles against Lithuania. However, it is believed that it was the failure in the battles with the Lithuanians that led the brothers to the decision to leave worldly life. They took tonsure and went to the Trinity-Sergius Monastery.

Studying the descriptions of Rodion Oslyaby's appearance, we find out that he was a middle-aged man with a "brown mustache and a lush beard." In addition, some sources mention the presence of his son Jacob, who allegedly died with his father during the Kulikovo battle. Both Peresvet and Oslyabya were not only very strong men, but also experienced warriors, including in the management of the army. Sometimes they are even called generals.

Hero of the Battle of Kulikovo

Together with his brother Alexander Peresvet, Rodion Oslyabya was sent by the Monk Sergius of Radonezh to a righteous battle with the hordes of the Horde Khan Mamai, who usurped power in the western part of the split Golden Horde. An unrecognized ruler who was not a descendant of Genghis Khan, Mamai decided with the help of military victories to strengthen his power among the Horde warriors.



Before leaving for the princely army, Sergius of Radonezh instead of armor put on his monks cloaks with embroidered crosses as a sign of God's protection and protection - the Great Schema. In it, worn on a monastic dress, and they went to battle. Sergius of Radonezh blessed Peresvet and Oslyabyu before the campaign with the miraculous icon of Our Lady of Tikhvin.

After the famous duel between Alexander Peresvet and Chelubey and their deaths, two troops converged on the Kulikovo field in a hot battle. From the first minute, Rodion Oslyabya fought valiantly in the front rows. His contribution to the battle largely predetermined the result.



According to one of the existing versions, Rodion Oslyabya died in a battle with the Horde on Kulikovo field, and according to another - he returned to his monastery and continued his service.

Was there a find?

In the XVIII century, in the Mother of God-Nativity Church of the monastery began to dismantle the bell tower. The idea is expressed that during these works a brick crypt was discovered, the floor of which was covered with nameless tombstones, after removing which the builders found the sarcophagi of Alexander Peresvet and Rodion Oslyaby.

Over time, tombstones were installed over them. They were destroyed twice: in 1794 and 1928. And only in 1989 were created for the third time - now at the initiative of the artist P. D. Korin. A wooden tombstone was installed above the burial place of the heroes of the Kulikovo battle, exactly copying its first cast-iron version. The proposed grave is open to all comers. However, it is still unknown whether the remains of Peresvet and Oslyaby are actually resting here. But next to the graves are set precious lamps, made at the expense of the Naval Department of Imperial Russia.

Rodion Oslyabya is back in service

In 2005, by order of the Commander-in-Chief of the Russian Navy, one of the multi-deck landing ships of the Pacific Fleet, designed to transport and land assault forces with military equipment, was named Oslyabya.

From February to May 2017, the Rodion Oslyabya motor ship underwent scheduled repairs at the docks: all engines were brought into working condition, the pumps and pipelines were repaired, the propeller-driven complex was completely cleaned, the old sections were completely blasted and the new ones were painted, and the hull was repaired. In addition, the ship was modernized: the hydraulic system of hatches and a superstructure with decks were refitted. Technical support was carried out around the clock.
