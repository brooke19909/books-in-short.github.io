A good microphone for the stream: a review of manufacturers and models, specifications, reviews

Now more and more people learn about streams. If a couple of years ago this was alien to many, now there are more streamers and those who know about this work.

Thanks to live broadcasts, everyone can find friends around the world, creatively realize and earn money. But first you need to get a good microphone for the stream.

The importance of the microphone

Not many people know which microphone to stream. Most novice streamers believe that you can opt for the cheapest model. It will be enough and simple headphones with a microphone.

But if you take the matter seriously, you will have to accumulate a certain amount. Of course, it is not necessary to immediately purchase an expensive microphone for 15-20 thousand rubles, if it does not concern musical streams. In this case, you will have to immediately invest in a good device.

By the way, the same applies to webcams. Beginner streamers can use the simplest models or smartphone camera for this. But it‚Äôs worth understanding that this option is definitely not suitable for broadcasts that are carried out in full resolution.

How to choose a microphone

There is a budget microphone for the stream. There are several models that will cost 2-3 thousand rubles. At the same time, the sound quality will be enough for the broadcast.

In the future, when streams will generate revenue, it will be possible to gradually improve equipment. In this case, it will be possible to purchase an external sound card, which will significantly affect the sound quality, and after that pay attention to an expensive microphone.

Before choosing a device, you should understand what broadcasts you will conduct, as well as what amount you have. At the initial stage, it is worth separating conversational, gaming and music streams. For each of them, you may need different budget models.

The cheapest device will cost you 2-3 thousand rubles. Later, you can look at the models, the cost of which exceeds 20 thousand rubles.

Help from other streamers

When choosing a good microphone for a stream, you can turn to other streamers for help. Very often they indicate in the channel descriptions all the equipment that is used for broadcasting.

If you have a favorite streamer, the sound quality on the broadcast also suits you, you can spy on which microphone it uses and purchase the same one.

But there is a catch: many popular streamers have already acquired more expensive equipment. Some models may not be affordable for you. Therefore, it is worth taking a closer look at less popular streamers, but with good air quality.
