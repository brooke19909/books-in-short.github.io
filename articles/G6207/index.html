OTP Bank in St. Petersburg: addresses and schedule

In total, several OTP bank offices are open in the city. In St. Petersburg, a head office is also located in the central region of the Northern capital. The addresses and the work schedule of the bank branches are presented below.

Central

The main office of OTP Bank in St. Petersburg is located at 5 Voronezhskaya Street, Building A. It is important to note that individuals are not served in this branch. Details of the main office:

  1. TIN: 7708001614.
  2. Transmission: 784043001.
  3. BIC: 044030812.
  4. C / C: 30101810600000000812.

Opening hours: from Monday to Thursday, the office is open from 09:00 to 06:00, while the operating time is until 16:00. On Friday, the bank is open from 09:00 to 16:45, while also operational services are not provided after 16:00. You can contact the reception department of OTP Bank in St. Petersburg by the phone number indicated on the official website.

Branches serving individuals

An additional office is located at 5 Krasnoarmeyskaya Street, Building 2, Building 39, Building A. The Technological Institute metro station is located nearby. Individuals can receive the following services at the department:

  • consultation, processing and issuance of loans and credit cards;
  • currency exchange;
  • money transfers (including international);
  • registration and issue of MasterCard debit cards, as well as VISA and many others.

Also in this office corporate clients and small businesses can be served. The number of services for legal entities includes:

  • remote banking services;
  • CSC;
  • salary projects and many other services.

DO Vladimirsky OTP Bank in St. Petersburg is located at the address: Bolshaya Moskovskaya street, 1-3, building A.

The office is open on weekdays from 09:30 to 20:30, on Saturday - from 10:00 to 18:00. Sunday until closed. Opening hours may change during the holidays, you must specify in advance.

Branches serving corporate clients

The address of OTP Bank in St. Petersburg serving legal entities: Bolshoy Prospect P.S., Building 46/1, Building A. The following metro stations are located nearby:

  1. Chkalovskaya.
  2. "Petrograd".

The services provided to clients include:

  • placement of free cash;
  • treasury products;
  • trade finance and documentary operations;
  • customer lending;
  • bank guarantees and many others.

Working hours with corporate clients: on weekdays from 09:30 to 17:00. ATMs installed in the branch are working around the clock.

It is important to note that the details of the branches can be clarified on the official website of the bank, as well as in any of the branches. It is also necessary to specify the opening hours of offices on holidays on the official website of a financial organization.
