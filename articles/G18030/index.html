Hotel "Scandinavia" (Sestroretsk): address, reviews

If you have long wanted to take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city, go to the Scandinavia hotel. Sestroretsk with its clean air and beautiful nature is one of the best options for a rural holiday.





a brief description of

The best traditions of outdoor recreation are guided by the Scandinavia hotel. Sestroretsk is a suburb of St. Petersburg, located near the Gulf of Finland. This complex was built at the beginning of the last century. Then the whole city nobility came here to admire nature and breathe fresh air. The architectural solution, landscape design, interior, although they were updated, but retained the spirit of that time.

Luxury and elegance are some of the few flattering features that the Scandinavia hotel can award. Sestroretsk will be your favorite place to swim in the transparent pool, undergo many spa treatments and enjoy the luxurious interior of the rooms.





Hotel Scandinavia (Sestroretsk): address

Scandinavia Hotel is located in Sestroretsk on Parkovaya Street 16. It is only a 2-minute walk from the beach. The distance to St. Petersburg is about 35 km, and to get to Pulkovo Airport, you have to overcome about 70 km. The hotel is surrounded by coniferous and deciduous forests. If you want to join the cultural life, then after 40 minutes by car you will walk along Palace Square.
