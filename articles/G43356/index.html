Centara Karon Resort 4 *, Phuket: photos, reviews, description

Having a little accumulated money, you can fulfill the desire to have a nice rest and visit the beautiful and mysterious Thailand, which, according to many tourists, today is more attractive than the tourist countries of the world familiar to us. Thailand is that new exotic that conquers and falls in love from the first moment of acquaintance, it is that state that can open in such dissimilar forms that each subsequent visit will be completely different from the previous ones. When all expectations are met ...

A great hotel on the ocean is not the ultimate dream or a luxury complex that can be afford only by the powerful, this is the Centara Karon Resort 4 * (Phuket), which joyfully welcomes its guests, opening in front of them the doors of 335 comfortable rooms, the price of which is not too high even for a middle-income person.

On the one hand of this magical castle stretches the sapphire surface of the ocean, and on the other hand it is buried in exotic tropical plants, which previously could be seen only on the pages of glossy travel magazines and interesting corners of the world.

This is the hotel in which everything is really thought out to the smallest detail, ranging from interior and exterior to interesting entertainments that will be interesting both for families with children and for fun companies looking for vivid impressions.

Hotel location

Centara Karon Resort 4 * (Phuket) is located not only in a very picturesque place, but also in a very comfortable place, because from here it is close at hand to a clean and decent sandy beach (only three hundred meters). It is only 45 km away from the airport, that is, it takes less than an hour to get to any vehicle. If necessary, you can pre-book a transfer service - you will be surely met and delivered directly to the hotel. For the price this service is quite affordable.



Not far from the hotel there are spontaneous markets and shops where you will find a lot of interesting things that reflect the local culture, wonderful souvenirs, the variety of which makes your eyes run wide, and delicious tropical fruits that you definitely need to try, because they are simply excellent here.
