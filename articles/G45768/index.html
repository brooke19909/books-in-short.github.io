Hotel Surf Mar 4 * (Lloret de Mar): description of rooms, service, reviews

Spain is perfect for a budget summer vacation on the Mediterranean coast. Many of our compatriots come here who dream of having a good time in the hot sun. More often than not, our compatriots choose small resort towns, such as Lloret de Mar, for example, to relax. Interestingly, there are a large number of inexpensive hotels with three and four stars. They are most often located in advertising catalogs of travel agencies. Today we will tell you about a nice and comfortable hotel located some distance from the city center - Hotel Surf Mar 4 *. From our article you will learn all the necessary information in order to understand whether it is worth choosing it for your holidays in Spain.





General description of the hotel

The hotel complex Hotel Surf Mar 4 * belongs to calm and cozy places where it is so good to relax with children. Quite often traveling European pensioners stay at this hotel, so it is never noisy here, which allows you to get enough sleep at night and relax during the day.

Hotel Surf Mar 4 * (Spain) is a four-story building in which, for the convenience of guests, there are four elevators. The hotel is one of the oldest in the resort, it was built in 1976. Nine years ago, the hotel complex was completely renovated, which significantly raised its rating among European hotels. Keep in mind that in Russian catalogs it is designated as "four", but in Spain it is listed as "three rubles". Let this discrepancy not bother you, such inconsistencies are quite common in travel agencies. This nuance will not affect the quality of service.

In Hotel Surf Mar 4 * there are two hundred sixteen rooms of two categories. For those who come to Spain for the first time, it may be an unpleasant surprise that the rooms here are very small, but this is quite natural for Europe. The hotel is part of the well-known chain Samba Hotels, which seriously monitors the quality of the services provided.

The location of the hotel complex

Hotel Surf Mar 4 * has a very convenient and favorable location for tourists. This is written in almost every review and comments about the hotel complex, because the location significantly affects the quality of leisure for tourists.

The hotel is located in the small resort town of Lloret de Mar, almost nine hundred meters from its center. This allows vacationers to easily get to entertainment venues if necessary, and relax in their rooms when you just want to relax after an eventful day.

The hotel is thirty kilometers from the Girona airport, but from Barcelona the road to the hotel complex will be a little longer - ninety kilometers. Twenty meters from Hotel Surf Mar 4 * - Fenals Beach. It is characterized by cleanliness and tranquility, there are not too many vacationers, which is very popular with families with small children.





Hotel infrastructure

Against the backdrop of European hotels, which usually do not have their territory and developed infrastructure, Hotel Surf Mar 4 * stands out. It can please not only with its location, but also with an abundance of various objects, including those intended for children.

Of course, the Surf Mar 4 * hotel does not have a large territory, but this does not spoil it at all - all the free space has been turned into a blooming garden, where it is very convenient to relax in the midday heat. Opposite the building of the hotel complex is a fairly large open-air pool. It occupies three hundred and twelve square meters and is completely hidden from prying eyes by trees and flowering shrubs. The pool is surrounded by sun loungers and parasols, which are free for hotel guests.

Vacationers can use the restaurant and bar, working on a convenient schedule. In the restaurant, guests have breakfast, lunch and dinner. But the bar is designed for evening entertainment, although it operates in the daytime. Here those who do not plan to go to the coast and spend time by the pool love to relax.

Since in Europe many tourists often travel by rented car, the hotels try to make fairly spacious parking spaces where cars and motorbikes can be accommodated. Hotel Surf Mar has a parking lot for one hundred and twenty cars and a garage for twenty more cars. This service is free for hotel guests.

On the ground floor of the hotel complex there is a TV room where vacationers spend time in the evening. They have a wide selection of channels and films in different languages.

The reception is open around the clock, so hotel guests can contact the staff at any time for help on various issues. There is also a luggage storage room, and there are various vending machines selling drinks and snacks. They are usually very fond of kids who prefer a high-grade lunch of soda and chips with crackers. There is a safe and a currency exchange at the reception, so vacationers do not have to go out to the city to exchange cash.

The hotel has a small conference room where you can hold a serious event, as well as solve issues with business papers - send a fax or make copies. Please note that this service is not included in the room rate.

The hotel has various sites for outdoor activities, we will talk about them in the next section of the article.





Entertainment at the hotel

Despite the small size of the hotel complex, on its territory there is enough entertainment for children and adults. First of all, fans of active sports are surprised by the presence of a tennis court and a basketball court. They are available free of charge to guests during daylight hours. You can also spend the evening playing billiards or table tennis. For those who prefer evening shows, live music is played several times a week, and various events are organized with the participation of animators. Kids enjoy spending time at slot machines or on the sports ground.

Many tourists were surprised by the presence of a mini-golf course at the hotel and morning aerobics, which is conducted under the guidance of a professional trainer. In general, the hotel is focused on couples, and therefore provides such a wide selection of entertainment.

Children at the hotel

Since the hotel complex accepts families with very young children, it can please vacationers with various objects of infrastructure for children of any age.

Kids have fun in a twenty-four square meter children's pool. It is very conveniently located so that parents can always see their offspring, but at the same time do not interfere with their rest.

For sports children, a beautiful playground is equipped where you can swing on a swing and conquer numerous structures. Keep in mind that they are all safe, so don't worry about your little ones spending time there.

If parents need to leave and leave their child at the hotel, they can call a nanny or bring him to a kids club, where he will spend some fun hours under the supervision of professional animators.

Several times a week for children, separate animation shows and mini discos are held. All this makes staying at the hotel one of the most vivid impressions for children during their vacation.

Hotel Surf Mar 4 *: room description

Most tourists say that the hotel rooms are far from new, but very clean and comfortable. It has everything you need to feel almost at home.

The hotel provides guests with the following room categories:

  • Double These rooms are the most common; there are one hundred and twenty of them in the hotel. They have an area of ‚Äã‚Äãsixteen squares, which can accommodate up to four people.
  • Single There are ninety-six such rooms; their area also does not exceed sixteen square meters.

Each room has a terrace where you can sunbathe on a sunny day. Most rooms have sea views, which makes the hotel very attractive to guests. The rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning, cable TV and a telephone with access to the city.

The bathroom includes a hairdryer and toiletries, which are replenished daily. Tourists can also use a personal safe, but this service is paid extra.

Hotel stay with children

The hotel complex can accommodate up to two extra beds for children. This must be specified at the stage of booking a room.

Toddlers under two years staying in extra beds can stay in the hotel complex for free.





Check In Rules

When checking in, credit cards are accepted, and guests are warned that payment in cash is extremely undesirable. The hour of the day is the time of check-in, it is necessary to leave it before ten in the afternoon.

For those who will fly out of Spain at night, a late check out is provided, when the checkout time is postponed to evening time, and accordingly, a recalculation of the nights spent is made.

Hotel Features

Each hotel complex has some features that guests should be aware of. At Hotel Surf Mar they are represented by the following list:

  • smoking is prohibited throughout;
  • for accommodation in a room with pets you need to pay an extra five euros per day;
  • the hotel complex is suitable for people with disabilities, all conditions are created for them here;
  • on the beach you need to take towels for painting.

Nutrition

Tourists eat in the hotel restaurant, located on the ground floor. Breakfast is served in a buffet-style room with sea views. It is interesting that the hotel complex provides the opportunity to book a room with different food systems:

  • breakfast
  • half board;
  • boarding house;
  • "all inclusive".

Each tourist can choose the option that suits him best.





Hotel Surf Mar 4 *: reviews

If we summarize all the hotel reviews left by vacationers, then we can say with confidence that most of them are positive. Of the advantages, tourists highlight the following points:

  • excellent location with respect to the sea, cafes, restaurants and shops;
  • pleasant and unobtrusive service;
  • cleanliness of the rooms;
  • well-equipped area for children;
  • some staff speak good English.

Minuses are noted a little, but they nevertheless are:

  • not too varied food in the restaurant;
  • lack of a refrigerator in some rooms and inconvenient switches;
  • shabby furniture in Single.

In general, the hotel is recommended by almost all of its guests, it is ideal for family and relaxing holidays.
