Frick Wilhelm: biography, political activity

Frick Wilhelm is one of the most prominent figures of the National Socialist Party of Germany. This man stood at the very beginnings of Nazism. He was personally acquainted with Hitler and in many ways contributed to his ascent.

Until the fall of the Third Reich, he held key government posts. He wrote several "scientific" Nazi works. Involved in crimes against humanity.
