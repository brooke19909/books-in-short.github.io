Serebryanskoe reservoir (Murmansk region) - description, features, photo

In the west of the Kola Peninsula is located Serebryanskoe reservoir. The map below shows its coordinates. This reservoir was created on the territory of the Murmansk region in the 1970s on the Voronya River in parallel with the construction of the hydropower plant of the same name.

When the reservoir was created, 220 hectares of agricultural land were flooded, about 40 buildings were moved. The purpose of the construction of the reservoir is to regulate the movement of water between a number of hydropower plants.

Natural features

Serebryanskoe reservoir is famous for its extraordinary nature. This area is a lot of hills with gentle slopes surrounded by slender birches and clear water. Fishermen can enjoy something for everyone here: they are often found in the reservoir, burbot, trout, trout, perch, whitefish and pike. Some lucky ones had a chance to see grayling! By the way, fishing is practiced here both by boat and from the shore.

Hunters, too, do not remain out of work: everyone can go for prey - white partridges and other game. Parents bring their children here to distract them from city life. This whole area is surrounded by clean air, bright dense forests and various herbs, which during flowering emit a heady aroma.
