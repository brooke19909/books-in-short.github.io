An unforgettable vacation in Thailand

One of the most sought after destinations in tourism is Thailand. Here you can enjoy the sun, white beaches, spa treatments and the best nightly shows of the world all year round. The most popular holiday in Thailand is considered to be on the islands of Phuket and Samui, as well as in Bangkok. This country is often called the "country of a thousand smiles." Thai culture involves a very careful and respectful attitude towards the guest. Such sincere friendliness and courtesy can no longer be found in any corner of the world. One gets the feeling that the most important people for Thais are their guests.

Last minute tours to Thailand are very popular, which allow you to relax in a tropical paradise for modest money. The optimal time for a vacation is considered from December to the end of February. Conventionally, a year is divided into three seasons: hot, rainy season and cool. For residents of northern countries, even a relatively low temperature of + 27s during the cold season will be an excellent addition to the vacation. If a beach holiday in Thailand was chosen , then most tourists choose Phuket or Samai and prefer to check in to small hotels located near the coast. The country is washed by the Indian Ocean and the Gulf of Thailand. Thai beaches are rightfully considered one of the best in the world. There are also SPA centers that can restore strength and beauty to any traveler.

You can spend your holidays in Thailand by exploring numerous attractions. This is a country with a huge centuries-old history and interesting traditions. In its capital, Bangkok, a large number of memorable places that can not be ignored. It can be tasted both in large restaurants and in small family cafes, where the traditions of its preparation are carefully kept. She is very spicy and bright.
