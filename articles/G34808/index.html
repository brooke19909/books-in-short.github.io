Mezmay: attractions, their photos and description. Tours in the Krasnodar Territory

Holidays in the Krasnodar Territory - this is not necessarily living on the coast, in large resort cities like Sochi and Gelendzhik. This region can offer many interesting things to tourists seeking unity with nature and the knowledge of its pristine beauty. One of such places is the Mezmay village, whose sights are not modern water parks and man-made sculptures, but mountain rivers, stormy waterfalls and mountains.

Mezmay is the pearl of the Caucasus!



It is difficult to believe that in the Krasnodar Territory, which has been popular among tourists for more than a decade, there are still reserved corners cut off from civilization. It will be useful to know if you are planning a vacation: Mezmay is not a city of entertainment and nightlife. There are no ultramodern hotels and large shops. However, it is here, among the mountains and forests, that you will see rare plants, centenary trees and enjoy the incredible atmosphere of a natural and calm life that the locals live. Do not think that such a vacation will be boring, there are still entertainments for tourists, and the abundance of natural attractions will delight even the most demanding guest.

The history of the settlement and our days

At the beginning of the 20th century, at the confluence of the Kurdzhips and Mezmay rivers , a settlement was organized for workers engaged in logging. There was also a camp for prisoners engaged in correctional labor. Around the same time, a narrow single-track railway was built. Today, only memories have remained of those times, but, despite this, the distinguishing feature of the settlement is mainly wooden buildings. Stone buildings in the whole village are easy to count on fingers. Local residents are engaged in agriculture - they cultivate cultivated plants and raise animals. Despite such a short and prosaic story, Guamka, Mezmay and all the neighboring points on the map have the glory of special energy zones. Mezmay is surrounded by three gorges: Guam, Upper Kurdzhip and Mezmay, each of them is characterized by incredible beauty and an abundance of natural attractions.

Horse and Hiking



What to do on vacation if you have chosen Mezmay as your place of residence? First of all, enjoy the silence, the abundance of greenery and the incredibly clean and fresh air. Agree, all this is very much lacking for all residents of megacities. Walk along the quiet streets, take photos as a keepsake, chat with the locals, whose legends are told about the hospitality. The best way to get to know the nature of the surrounding area is to go for a walk as part of a tourist group on foot or on horseback. These are the most popular excursions for tourists, which you can learn about without leaving the village of Mezmay. The routes will delight you with their diversity, choose the most interesting types for you - mountain gorges, forests or alpine meadows. Everything is here. If you do not know how to stay in the saddle, rest in the village of Mezmay is the best time to learn. There is no organized riding school, but horses are still one of the main means of transportation. And almost any resident of the village is ready to give riding lessons to tourists.

Mezmay: Attractions of Cosmic Importance



The surroundings of the village are considered an abnormal zone. One of the most interesting places here is the cosmodrome. This is a giant funnel, which gradually increases and continues to draw trees and large fragments of rocks into its center. Not far from this mysterious place there are two large caves. There is also a "house of astronauts" in the Guam Gorge , and near it there is a unique cave, from which a stormy stream flows, which is divided into two cascades. A television repeater is installed at the highest point of the Guam mountain range. It would seem that this is a miracle of engineering, and not a mysterious attraction. However, residents of the village claim that during church holidays and the full moon around this object, an unusual glow can be seen with the naked eye.

Prehistoric world



Want to see how this area looked at the dawn of civilization? Mezmay attractions have a variety of, among them there are very ancient. Go to the southern part of the village, it is here that the relict tisosamshit grove begins. Head upstream of the Kurdzhips. Scientists have confirmed that some trees growing here are more than 2 thousand years old. The paved route passes by a spring with purified silver water. Here you can see a picturesque waterfall, which has the equally beautiful name of Dragon's Mouth. And soon you will see the most interesting thing - the parking lot of ancient people, and if you climb the rocks, you can appreciate the picturesque view of the valley from a small observation deck. In the vicinity of Mezmay there are also dolmens, ancient stone blocks of ritual significance, whose age is about 5 thousand years.

Other attractions and interesting places



No other excursions around the Krasnodar Territory will give you as many impressions as exploring the territory around a village like Mezmay. Nature pleases here with its picturesqueness and diversity. Natural caves can be considered her real miracle, many of them can be accessed as part of organized groups. Underground halls amaze with the beauty of stalactites and stalagmites. In many of them you can see underground lakes and streams. The most famous are the cave N. Isichenko and the cave Monk. If you relax in such a wonderful place as Mezmay, waterfalls are a must-see among other excursions. The settlement is surrounded by mountain rivers and streams, and there are a great many water cascades here. Among them there are stepped waterfalls, and water baths, and other hydrological attractions.

Features of leisure in the village

Absheron region is a real land of miracles, where different eras and cultures are intertwined into a single ball. How to get to rest in Mezmay? In fact, the biggest difficulty is getting to the village. There are no hotels here, but the locals are very hospitable and gladly accept tourists for billets, as well as for a nominal fee organize full-fledged meals in the best traditions of national cuisine.

Mezmay sights are surrounded by a variety of sights, but take into account the specifics of this area when planning a trip. If you are physically weak, cannot live without a high level of comfort, or do not like outdoor activities, consider choosing another option. It is not recommended to choose this place as a vacation destination for older people or families with small children. But lovers of outdoor activities will certainly like it. Speleology, mountain climbing, hiking, hunting, fishing, picking berries, mushrooms and herbs - choose entertainment to your taste. In addition, not far from the village there are two monasteries of Orthodox monasteries, and many come here to personally touch the shrines. This area also attracts creative people, in the vicinity of the village annually held festivals of music and literature.

How to get into the lost world?



Do not be surprised if the travel agencies of your city have never heard of the village of Mezmay. Sights of this region are not always included in standard excursions in the Krasnodar Territory. It is most convenient to drive a private car or arrange a transfer. Today there are two roads. One of them, more comfortable, starts from the village of Nizhny Novgorod, the landmark is a monument to the founding Cossacks. The road is covered with gravel, but despite this, in good weather you can drive here by almost any car.

Another option is the exit from the highway Dakhovskaya - Lago-Naki. However, this road is in worse condition (the length of the emergency section is about 12 kilometers), and it is better to go along it only if you are completely confident in your own car.

You can also come to the village of Mezmay by single-track railway. However, the possibility of such a trip should be clarified upon arrival, since the passenger train sometimes runs along the rails to the "house of astronauts."
