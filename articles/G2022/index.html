Cozy Kotor. Montenegro and its attractions

The city, protected by UNESCO, a nature reserve on the Adriatic Sea, a living monument of architecture and a stunning resort - all this Kotor, Montenegro. This recreation area has everything: a warm blue sea, wide beaches framed by lush green vegetation, mountain ranges that lean over the waters, and a small river. This city will be a great haven for lovers to explore the most amazing corners of our planet, and will also serve as a great resort if you decide to spend a honeymoon or relax in the summer with children. By the way, a vacation here is quite a budget pleasure, therefore, if you are looking for something "such", but at the same time do not count on big waste, then go to Kotor.



Montenegro is a country that played a key role in the formation of European history, therefore, almost every city in it bears the imprint of past centuries. This is manifested in architecture, in local traditions, and even in the very structure of the area. The local culture has been forming since the era of the Early Middle Ages and still continues to improve. Montenegro, the sights of which, as a rule, date from 12-15 centuries, is mainly built up with cathedrals, Catholic churches and monasteries. The city of Kotor, which is located in one of the quiet bays of the Adriatic Sea, is no exception. Wandering through narrow, typically European streets, you will find the Cathedral of St. Tryphon, as well as the Church of St. Luke - the oldest monuments of architecture. They were erected here in the 12th century, and a hundred years later, for their additional protection and savings, a fortress with a clock tower was erected near the Lovcen mountain.

Also on the territory of the city is the fortress of St. Ivan, which was built in the 15th century, and since then it has been defending Kotor. Montenegro has long been under the auspices of the Byzantine Empire, therefore, in its territory, in addition to the Catholic cathedrals, there are also Orthodox. They are not only majestic and beautiful, like their relatives, but also deserve the attention of tourists. In order to learn more about such architectural monuments, contact local residents, and with typical hospitality typical for this country, they will tell you everything in detail.



Optimal beaches for any vacation, equipped with everything necessary, are located throughout the city of Kotor. Montenegro is almost all indented with picturesque bays, so the sea here is always calm. And finding a deserted beach is not a problem. In urban recreation areas, holidays are always arranged. You can find a colorful carnival, which lasts all night, and a beach party in the sand, and a more extraordinary procession in the spirit of local traditions.
