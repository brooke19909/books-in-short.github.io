Location

For the construction of the residential complex "Solar Valley" (Obninsk), the center of the city's sports and educational infrastructure was chosen. The first buyers just focus on the good location of new buildings. The construction is located at the intersection of Marx and Lenin streets, right behind the House of Scientists.





Infrastructure

On the territory of the residential complex "Solar Valley" (Obninsk) there are no objects of its own infrastructure, but the developer laid the construction of an underground parking in the project. Residents of the house emphasize convenience, because in order to get to the car, you do not need to leave the house: a high-speed elevator takes you directly to the parking lot. It maintains a positive temperature, due to which it becomes a modern alternative to a separate garage. However, as the residents of the house note, in order to use the parking, it is necessary to purchase a parking place in the property or to issue a rental right for it.





LCD "Solar Valley" (Obninsk) is located surrounded by all necessary infrastructure. Security

The territory of the residential complex "Solar Valley" is fenced and is under round-the-clock video surveillance. Closed yards are an excellent solution for ensuring the safety of children playing on the playground.
