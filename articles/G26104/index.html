The One Club Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Dalaman) - photo and reviews of tourists

For a long time already many Russians prefer to rest in Turkey. No wonder this country is firmly taking a leading position among the destinations offered by tour operators for spending summer vacations. Sea and sun, low prices and excellent service are offered in most hotels in Turkey.

But, despite the fact that this country is one of the most popular for travel, Sarygerme is still little known to domestic tourists. And completely in vain. This is a tiny settlement in which the one and only central street turns into a place where many tourists gather in the evenings. Despite its modest size, this former fishing village attracts tourists with souvenir stalls and shops, traditional cafes and restaurants.

And although inveterate party-goers, judging by the reviews, it can be boring, for those who crave peace, silence and enjoy the unspoiled beauty of the local nature, there will be a real paradise, since they cannot find a better place to relax in body and soul.



The mountains, completely covered with olive and pine groves, approach very close to the sea coast, stretching along it for many kilometers. They give the air the finest delicate aroma, making it easier for those tourists who suffer from pulmonary diseases.

The One Club Hotel 4 * Marmaris

Syrygerme (Sarigerme) is located twenty kilometers west of Dalaman. There are many hotels, including luxury five-star hotels. Many of them are located somewhat far from the coast, but there are also those that are built on the first line of the sea.

The One Club Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), surrounded by a dense pine forest, is in some privacy. It is this feature that makes it attractive for those tourists who want to spend their vacation away from the noise and constant fuss. The hotel was built in 2008. Its total area is ten thousand square meters. Cozy alleys, benches under the canopy of palm trees, beautiful lawns, nooks and crannies - everything was done with love and care for their guests.

Here silence and peace reign all around, there is only the sun, sea and mountains. The One Club Hotel 4 * was built sixteen kilometers from Dalaman Airport, and this is another plus in its favor. The transfer for young children is very fast and calm, the kids do not have time to get tired.



Infrastructure

The One Club Hotel 4 * (Turkey) is ten cozy two-story buildings, each of which has a comfortable terrace. Population



The reception is open around the clock. Tourists arriving at The One Club Hotel 4 * settle in very quickly, without any delay. Rooms can be booked in advance on the Internet without a fee.

Here, at the registration desk, you can arrange a transfer, order a taxi or rent a vehicle, including a bicycle.

The hotel allows children of any age. When placing a child under the age of twelve in existing bedding, the property does not charge a separate fee. Children under two years stay free of charge in a cot.



In The One Club Hotel 4 * pets are not allowed.

Nutrition



The One Club Hotel 4 * feeds its guests according to the all-inclusive concept. Three meals a day are served in the restaurant. All inclusive is valid from ten in the morning until eleven in the evening and includes not only three meals a day, but also the provision of snacks in the hotel bars throughout the day, as well as drinks, including alcoholic drinks from local producers. Ice cream and freshly squeezed juices are issued for a fee.

The main restaurant can accommodate up to two hundred and fifty people. It has a summer terrace, which is very convenient for parents who can enjoy food and at the same time keep track of children playing in the playground.

There is also one poolside bar on site. The second is on the beach and runs until six in the evening.

Beach



The One Club Hotel 4 * has its own seven-hundred-meter shoreline. Since the beach is almost one kilometer distant from the hotel, a free shuttle service is organized twice a day every day. Shuttlebass takes and brings tourists, although many prefer to go to the coast on their own along a very beautiful, picturesque path.

The beach is sandy, with a gentle entrance to the water. There are also changing cabins, a shower room and toilets. Guests can enjoy cold drinks and snacks at the bar.

Facilities for children

The One Club Hotel 4 * offers high chairs for feeding in the restaurant and a crib in the room upon request of parents to its young guests. For older children there is a small pool, a children's playground. Animation on site is not provided.



Entertainment

The hotel has a small pool with a depth of one and a half meters. Around are sun loungers and umbrellas, there is also a bar.

Those who lead an active and healthy lifestyle, it will be convenient to visit the fitness center, hammam, sauna. Guests can play table tennis and volleyball.

Paid are all activities on the beach. Among them are banana- and parasailing, boat rental, canoeing, surfing and water skiing.

Impressions

The One Club Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), reviews about the rest of which are overwhelmingly positive, has become a favorite place for those who first discovered this resort. Tourists are happy with everything: the rooms, the services provided, and the full and tasty food.

Many are particularly happy with the fact that the hotel has few compatriots. The One Club Hotel 4 * has a convenient location in order to comfortably explore all local attractions, including the turtle coast of Iztuzu.

The staff speaks Russian, it is always friendly and efficient.

Reviews of tourists agree on one thing: both service and accommodation prices are in excellent proportion. Therefore, many express a desire to return here next year to again immerse themselves in a calm and friendly atmosphere. The hotel is recommended by many for those who are tired of noisy megacities and like to relax surrounded by unspoilt nature, but with comfort.
