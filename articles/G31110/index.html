The best hotels of Yerevan: photos and reviews

If you want to appreciate the color of the eastern metropolis, feel free to go to Yerevan. Many tourists and travelers call this city Eastern Paris - it is so remembered by it for its originality and fabulous beauty. In order to make your own opinion, to confirm or refute at least this statement for yourself, at least once visit this eastern city. To start a trip, or rather preparation for it, of course, you need to choose a hotel. In Armenia, hotels (Yerevan) are presented in a sufficient variety, so every tourist will be able to choose the best option in every respect. Let's focus on the most interesting and worthy offers.





Subtleties of choice

If you are coming to Yerevan for the first time, we recommend staying in the city center, since only in this case all cultural and historical sights will be within walking distance. There really are many options, each of them deserves special attention.

If you are interested in Yerevan hotels in the center, we suggest paying attention to the three-star Nairi Hotel. It is located on Armenakyan street, in the Nork-Marash district, close to the city center and all its cultural attractions. Guests are invited to stay in rooms of the categories of "standard", "deluxe" or "junior suite", which makes the hotel affordable for guests with different budgets. This option is quite often chosen by tourists, as well as entrepreneurs and businessmen, because there is everything that is necessary for the most relaxing and comfortable stay. Regardless of which category you choose for your stay, a pleasant atmosphere, comfortable and functional furniture, a TV, air conditioning, a kettle with a set of dishes will be waiting for you in your room. Internet is free for all vacationers. Those city guests who rent a car for a comfortable ride will receive a free parking space on site. For entertainment, guests are invited to use the pool and sauna.





Nairi includes a buffet breakfast in the room rate, like many other hotels. Yerevan is a major tourist city, the economic center of the country, therefore guests are treated with respect and attention. The cost of a standard double room is on average 4000 rubles per day, subject to prior reservation.
