iPhone 5S: screen specifications

The convenience of using a smartphone depends on many indicators. However, the main one can be considered the characteristics of the screen. In the model with the 5S index, it does not differ from its predecessor. Its diagonal remained at the same level - 4 inches. At first glance, the display may seem small, but the high resolution helps the situation. For example, the pixel density per inch is 326 ppi. Note that this is a rather high indicator. Display Type - IPS LCD, Retina. The picture is displayed on a screen of excellent quality. The detail is high, the color is excellent (the tint range is juicy and bright), the brightness is enough even when working under the bright rays of the sun, there are no complaints about the viewing angles. Autonomy

An equally important point in the technical characteristics of the iPhone 5S is the battery life. The smartphone has a 1560 mAh battery. The chemical composition is lithium polymer. Type of construction - fixed. The rechargeable battery provides decent results. For example, in standby mode, the device will last about 250 hours without recharging. If you limit interaction with the gadget at the average load level, you can safely count on 24 hours of work, after which you will need to connect the device through the charger to the mains.
