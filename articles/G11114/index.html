Nokia 6700: specifications and reviews

Phones of the Finnish company were previously the best-selling. There was no doubt in their quality and reliability. In 2009, a new model appeared in the line of this brand - Nokia 6700 Classic. The characteristics of the gadget impressed customers. Properly balanced functionality combined with an attractive design made this phone very popular. Of course, the famous brand also influenced the increased demand. Were the owners disappointed? If you believe the feedback from users, then this device fully met all expectations. Let's take a closer look at the specifications of the Nokia 6700 Classic and highlight the strengths and weaknesses of the model.



Appearance, dimensions, assembly reviews

As mentioned above, the characteristics of the Nokia 6700 are quite impressive. This model can rightfully be called legendary. The phone claims to such a high rank thanks not only to the hardware, but also to the features of the external design. First of all, users highlight the original design in the comments. The manufacturer harmoniously combined the plastic and metal elements of the case. The latter are used on the back surface. The phone is presented in four colors. According to users, the most elegant is gold.

The dimensions of the device are 109.8 √ó 45 √ó 11.2 mm. As for the weight, it seemed a little heavy to some users, since together with the battery its weight reaches 116.5 g.

In order to describe all the characteristics of the Nokia 6700, you must definitely consider the features of the keyboard. The unit itself is somewhat recessed into the housing. Such a decision made it possible to focus on it. The keyboard is made of a solid metal plate. Each horizontal row of the digital block is separated from each other, so erroneous clicks are completely eliminated. Right below the screen are soft keys. Large size navigation button. It is framed by a chrome edge. For convenience, the developers have built into it a light indicator that starts to flash after missed notifications.

Let's consider what reviews sounded to the assembly address. The owners unanimously assure that the case is made soundly and efficiently. There is no crunch and backlash. The panels do not bend, there are no gaps.



Nokia 6700 screen: characteristics and reviews

Considering that this model is equipped with a full-fledged button keyboard, it was quite expected that the developers would install a small screen. It is colored. Has a resolution of 240 √ó 320 px. Type - QVGA. Despite its modest characteristics, the display is capable of displaying more than 16 million colors, so that the picture is reproduced quite clearly and juicy. In this model, the manufacturer used a unique technology that allows you to automatically change the backlight level depending on the surrounding lighting. This not only protects the eyes from fatigue, but also significantly saves battery life.

Three service lines and nine text lines are simultaneously placed on the desktop. The font size for most users is perfectly matched - easy to read. Reviews about the screen owners leave only positive. The only thing that upset a little was the lack of a forced adjustment of the backlight level. Otherwise, users have no comments.



Overview of Key Features

The technical specifications of the Nokia 6700 by modern standards, of course, can no longer be called flagship, but in 2009 this model made a splash. The manufacturer installed a 5-megapixel camera on the device, with which you can make pretty high-quality pictures. The viewfinder opens horizontally. You can change the settings without leaving the shooting mode, as the developers have provided a special panel of shortcuts, which is filled at the request of users.

In the Nokia 6700, the memory characteristics are pretty decent. 170 MB of internal storage can be expanded with a flash card. Hot swapping is not provided, so you will have to remove the battery to install an external drive. In the kit for the phone, the buyer receives a 1 GB card.

A USB cable is used to work with a PC. When connected, the user is prompted to choose one of three modes:

  • PC Suite - provides access to applications and to all functions of the phone.
  • Data Storage - only works with device memory without installing drivers.
  • Printing & Media - used to print photos.

For wireless data transmission, a Bluetooth module is installed. The developers have provided various services (Share, Flickr, Windows Messenger), games and other applications.



Nokia 6700: battery specifications

A significant advantage of this model is the battery life. In the reviews, users claim that a 960 mAh lithium-ion battery provides the phone for up to 3 days under heavy load. In order to charge the battery to 100%, it will take 2 hours.

Manufacturer autonomy specifications:

  • 300 hours of standby time;
  • 22 hours of playing music tracks;
  • 2.5 hours of recording videos or shooting photographs;
  • 3.5 hours of video viewing;
  • up to 5 hours talk time.
