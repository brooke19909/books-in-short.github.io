Sea Galaxy Congress & Spa Hotel: all the most interesting about the famous Sochi hotel

Sea Galaxy Congress & Spa Hotel is one of the most popular hotel complexes in the entire Olympic Sochi. It is no secret to anyone that today in this beautiful city there are many hotels. Especially a lot of them became after the 2014 Winter Games. This hotel also belongs to the new ones, since the year of its construction is 2013. So, what can this 18-story hotel complex please its guests with?





Briefly about the hotel

So, for starters, I would like to talk more about the hotel itself. It is located in the center of Sochi - next to the Frunze park. And only 100 meters separates it from the Black Sea coast. In general, the whole of Sochi is supposedly located along the coastline. So from almost all places you can reach the sea in 10-15 minutes walk. But immediate proximity is just what the guests need. And in this case, 100 meters is a 2-3 minute walk at a calm pace.

Also close to the hotel is the central Kurortny Prospekt and the famous arboretum park. Nearby is the Sochi State Circus and the Summer Theater. And besides, cafes, bars, restaurants and supermarkets were located nearby. Public transport stops - in the same place, within walking distance of the hotel.

Suite and Junior Suite

These are two more popular room categories at Sea Galaxy Congress & Spa Hotel. These apartments are more spacious (living area is 32 and 33 sq. M respectively) and, of course, comfortable. "Junior" apartments will cost 105 thousand rubles for two (14 days) with half board, and for the suite will have to pay 120 thousand rubles.

The rooms are equipped with large double beds (apartments with separate beds can also be provided). Views from the junior suites and suites open only to the sea. Inside, everything is the same as in a standard apartment. But there is more space, plus a spacious balcony with a beautiful view. And Wi-Fi is also available. These rooms at the Sea Galaxy Congress & Spa Hotel are especially popular, so if you want to reserve one of them, you should take care of this in advance. Say, two months before the trip, otherwise there may simply not be any free apartments.

Service and Facilities

Sea Galaxy Hotel Congress & Spa has its own bar and restaurant. Diet menus can be provided upon request, if necessary.

The reception is open around the clock, and the hotel has an excursion menu, a ticket sales point, a baggage and baggage storage room, an ATM, concierge and even babysitting services. By the way, the playground in the hotel is also equipped. Laundry and ironing and shoe shine services are also available.

The hotel also has a banquet hall and a business center. In general, everything is at the level that a 4-star hotel complex should correspond to.





General impression

Sea Galaxy Hotel Congress & Spa gets mostly positive reviews. And no wonder. After all, everything is there for a good and comfortable stay. The rooms are cleaned daily - the guests note this first of all, the food in the restaurant is fresh, tasty and varied, the portions are generous in Russian. The staff is polite and not spoiled by visitors: drinks, newspapers and food are delivered to the apartments, VIP services are provided. The hotel has separate wedding luxury rooms and even amenities for those people who have limited physical abilities.

In general, the attitude of the hotel towards the guests is good, which is why he managed to gain such a good reputation in three years.
