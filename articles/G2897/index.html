Biryusa refrigerators: reviews, models, specifications

A huge number of refrigerators appeared on the market, so it is difficult to choose a specific model for yourself. Due to the large number of import options, domestic appliances have become less popular. The opinion that they are much inferior in quality is fundamentally wrong.

Inexpensive Biryusa refrigerators have been known since Soviet times, and they have proven themselves excellently. They are characterized by low price, excellent functionality and reliability.

Product Description

The company was founded in 1963. The name was borrowed from a tributary of the great Yenisei River. The first production of Biryusa refrigerators at the plant in the city of Krasnoyarsk was a huge success. Copies were collected for a year, but it was worth it. The first issue of more than 300 thousand units was quickly sold out. The plant worked successfully even during the crisis, the range was gradually expanded. In addition to refrigerators, the company began to manufacture medical and commercial equipment. The configuration and design of the devices are constantly changing.

Today, not only ordinary refrigerators are made, but also wine cabinets, coolers for changing the temperature of water, ice makers, chest freezers and some other devices. In sales, the company is among the top three leaders in the territory of the Russian Federation. Goods are also exported to Kazakhstan, Belarus and some other CIS countries.

The advantages of refrigerators

Reviews of Biryusa refrigerators have more positive points than negative ones. The disadvantages of these devices are insignificant. Build quality, functional set, specifications, prices - all this is at the best level. Many buyers note that these refrigerators are able to compete even with imported appliances.

In the reviews, the following advantages are noted: spaciousness, efficiency, high-quality refrigerant, as well as the presence of additional technologies. Let's consider everything separately.

  • Freezers and refrigerators have a large number of shelves, drawers, as well as chambers. Due to this, they are able to accommodate a large number of products. Judging by customer reviews, it is best for families to buy a two-cell refrigerator. And in the office or office, more compact models are suitable.
  • The energy class is A. The refrigerator spends about 270 kW per year, this is confirmed by customers.
  • The refrigerant is installed brand R600a. It is considered the most harmless, since it does not have synthetic components. In addition, it does not destroy the ozone layer, while having a good refrigeration coefficient.
  • Buyers confirm that the refrigerator has two additional technologies: No Frost and Low Frost. The latter is a drip system. Refrigerators with these options should not be thawed, they thaw themselves.

And additional advantages in the reviews are highlighted by electronic control with special buttons and good mounts. Refrigerators can withstand mechanical damage.

Cons of refrigerators

Of course, there are negative comments in reviews of Biryusa refrigerators. Buyers notice not very critical cons, but they can bring some discomfort.

If the door is not closed within 10-20 seconds, an unpleasant sound is triggered. The doors are uncomfortable shelves. Handles made of too fragile plastic. Some buyers report that they are deformed by accident. Drops of water flow down the back of the device.

These shortcomings are insignificant, and given that refrigerators are inexpensive, many buyers turn a blind eye to them.

Biryusa-6

This refrigerator is available in white. The design is small, thanks to this the device can be installed in small rooms. The device is divided into two sections: a refrigerator and a freezer. Both of them are 280 liters, class A energy consumption.

The refrigerator compartment has received a volume of 252 liters. It is divided into several parts using glass shelves. Below there are shelves for storing fruits and vegetables. On the door is an egg container. Defrosting is carried out by a drip system. In reviews of the Biryusa-6 refrigerator, many write that this is an extremely inconvenient feature.

The freezer received a volume of 28 liters. The minimum temperature in it is -12 degrees. You have to defrost manually.

Buyers claim that the refrigerator does not consume much electricity. Consumption is 219 kWh / year. The noise level is also minimal - 42 dB, so none of those who bought this refrigerator complains about unnecessary sounds from the Biryusa-6 refrigerator.

129th model refrigerator

This refrigerator has an attractive price, although it allows you to set the temperature yourself, has an excellent design, a high-quality cooling system and assembly of materials, and also received Antifreeze from the manufacturer. Due to such functionality and low cost (16 thousand rubles), many buyers prefer it. The refrigerator is silver in color, two motors are built into it, the height is 205 cm. The freezer is quite roomy, you can store not only meat and fish, but also products for the winter. You do not need to defrost it, which is why many people choose the Biryusa-129 refrigerator.

R110Ca

This refrigerator is considered compact and versatile. Made in white. Due to the small size of the refrigerator, its weight is only 38 kg. Many buy it for a summer residence.

The total volume of the device is 180 liters, of which 130 are related to the refrigerator. It has three shelves. The reviews on Biryusa R110Ca refrigerators describe that they are glass, but made of reliable and durable material. There are separate shelves for vegetables and fruits. The lighting inside is great. The freezer is able to freeze no more than 4 kg per day.

Defrosting is carried out by a drip system.

Biryusa 10

This device has only one door. Is economical options. Decorated in a classic shade. The refrigerator is easy to maintain, and it also runs quietly. The volume of the chambers is 235 liters. The refrigerator compartment received 207 liters. In reviews of Biryusa-10 refrigerators they write that the freezer is disappointing. Its volume is only 28 liters. Moreover, each of the cameras has to be defrosted manually.

Biryusa-132

This refrigerator was released relatively long ago, but it is in demand due to the volume of the chamber. A total of 330 liters. This characteristic is ideal for the Biryusa refrigerator. The freezer has two compartments. You have to defrost them manually. A special tray is used to assemble melt water. When the air temperature rises in the chamber, the freezing system automatically turns on.

The refrigerator compartment defrosts itself. Many buyers praise the manufacturer for this. Shelves in it are made of thick glass and can withstand 22 kg of weight. In the reviews, customers confirm that they are really hardy and hard to break.

Biryusa-149

This device receives good feedback from consumers. A sound indicator is built into the refrigerator. When the door is open for a long time, it works. The refrigerator compartment received 225 liters. It is divided into certain zones: glass shelves, coasters on the door for drinks and sauces, as well as two drawers for vegetables.

The freezer received a volume of 95 liters. It is divided into four separate drawers. A camera can freeze up to 7 kg of food a day, which is a high rate.
