Sea Wave Hotel, Dzhemete: traveler reviews

If you are looking for a place to relax with the whole family on the Black Sea, we suggest you consider the Sea Wave Hotel (Dzhemete).

About hotel





The Sea Wave Hotel is located in the resort village of Dzhemete, not far from Anapa. This is a new building, consisting of two buildings. The first has five floors with double, triple and quadruple rooms. The corps is designed for 120 people. The second has six floors with double and triple rooms. There are even two-room rooms of the first category with a small personal kitchen. Up to 200 people can live here. The total number of rooms of the Starfish Hotel consists of 102 rooms (38 in the first building and 64 in the second).

The hotel was recently built. The lack of a large territory and pool is compensated by the proximity to the sea with a sandy beach. So, various types of outdoor activities are available: scooter riding, water skiing, water slides and so on. The hotel staff constantly cares about their guests, so there is daily room cleaning, little children are looked after, guests are deliciously fed.

The hotel positions itself as a place to relax with children, so you can come here not only with schoolchildren, but also with babies.

Rooms





All rooms in the hotel are made in a modern style: spacious furnishings, interior in warm pastel colors. Each room is filled with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. There are comfortable beds, a small refrigerator, LCD TV, landline telephone, table and chairs, bedside tables and a spacious wardrobe. It is also possible to order an extra bed for a child.

You can control the room temperature using an air conditioner. Each room has a bathroom with a full bathtub and a balcony. For each guest in the room there is a set of towels and dishes (cups, plates, cutlery).

Services





Hotel "Sea Wave" (Dzhemete) offers its guests the following services, which are already included in the price:

  • safe boxes ;
  • access to wireless internet (WI-FI);
  • a children's room and a playground on site;
  • animator services for children on Wednesdays and Saturdays;
  • Ironing room
  • alarm clock;
  • provision of sewing accessories (needles, threads);
  • first aid kit provision.

Additional services that can be ordered for a fee:

  • the ability to transfer and call a taxi;
  • conducting excursions;
  • the ability to call a nanny;
  • conducting a massage session;
  • visiting the salt room;
  • laundry;
  • use of a minibar.

Separately, it is worth mentioning nutrition. The Sea Wave Hotel (Dzhemete) has a pastry shop and a cafeteria serving delicious home-made cuisine. The hotel also has the opportunity to order breakfast, lunch and dinner separately or in the complex. Meals can be included in the price or paid separately. In the first case, it will be cheaper.

Negative comments from tourists





Despite a bunch of positive reviews and a high level of service, Sea Wave, according to guests, still has work to do. In particular, people note the following points, of which not everything is related to the hotel itself:

  • Bed linen is changed every five days.
  • Small parking.
  • There is no private beach.
  • Small surrounding area.
  • There is no way to pay by credit card.
  • The elevator is only in the second building. In the first one you have to climb the stairs.
  • Fiscal menu in the dining room. I would like the opportunity to choose.

Despite such comments, many come to the Sea Wave Hotel more than once. And this suggests that resting here is very good.

Where is the hotel situated?





Full hotel address: 9 Prestizhny Lane, Anapa, Dzhemete. The Sea Wave is located on the left just between the Black Sea and Pionersky Prospekt, right on the first coastline. And a few steps from the hotel is a park area.

A shuttle bus runs from Anapa, which can easily be reached almost to the hotel itself. The duration of the trip is 15-25 minutes.
