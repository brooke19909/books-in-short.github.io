Smartphone LG G Flex. LG Mobile Phones

Each of us wants to have a powerful and stylish smartphone that does not scratch in your pocket and does not lose appearance. The main event at CES 2014 was the presentation of the American smartphone with the original form of the body LG G Flex. The device instantly attracted a lot of viewers who wished to purchase it. LG mobile phones have always been a sought-after product, but the Flex series has become a sensation. Compared with other devices, it retains external qualities for a long time, overcoming almost any mechanical damage.





Mobile device design

Perhaps this is the most original flagship smartphone of the last decade. The main objective of the invention of a device with such a housing is the convenience of using it as a handset and watching videos.

The thin plastic case of the product looks very stylish. A 6-inch mobile device LG G Flex weighs 177 g. The original form of the smartphone overshadows the lack of external elegance. The back panel is made of self-healing material. If you accidentally scratch the body of the smartphone, it will ‚Äúupdate‚Äù itself, but this applies only to minor scratches.

Despite the external fragility, the product is made with high quality. The classic type of body will appeal to both male and female. Unfortunately, removable panels are not provided, so you have to use a smartphone of a standard color Titan silver. The built-in antenna is invisible, does not bulge and does not cause inconvenience during use.

Display

The screen of the mobile device has an extension of 1280x720 pixels. The mobile device is equipped with an HD OLED screen that supports 16 million colors. Thanks to the body with a bend, the colors become more distinct, and the impression of the IMAX effect is created. The display has the ability to bend. The highlight of the screen is the maximum approximation of the picture to the panoramic. The viewer thinks that events are happening around him. Looking through the LG G Flex reviews, you can see that they mainly concern the display. Users note the relevance of this form. The smartphone holder can use it with gloves, as the display sensor is very sensitive. In addition, the screen is equipped with a gyroscope, accelerometer and multi-touch function.

Contrast and brightness are very high quality. These options are important for viewing data in aggressive sunlight. According to studies, the brightness is 311 Cd / m 2 , which is more than enough.



Using the virtual keyboard is very convenient. It is worth using it in a portrait position, because due to the curved shape the text is more comfortable to type in this way. While the user enters the front camera lens, the screen remains on all the time. This function works more accurately than in Samsung phones.

Firmware LG G Flex

The smartphone runs on Android OS (4.2.2 Jelly Bean). Before you purchase the LG G Flex, reviewing a smartphone on the Internet is quite advisable. User "shell" of the phone LG Optimus UI is no different from previous models. Also, QTheater has been added to the main functions of KnockON and Guest Mode. You need to move your fingers apart like curtains, and you will see new options with which to watch the video will be unforgettable.

The smartphone supports several basic methods of data transfer, for example, IRDA, Wi-Fi, EDGE, Bluetooth. If we talk about mobile Internet, the device is equipped with WAP, built-in modem, GPRS, HTML5 browser and POP3 client. Owners can send SMS and MMS using a mobile device. New from LG is equipped with speakerphone, auto redial and standby mode.

Performance

When you study the LG G Flex, the characteristics of the smartphone are impressive. The model is equipped with a powerful Qualcomm MSM8974 Snapdragon 800 processor, which has a frequency of 2260 MHz. The graphic editor is the Adreno 330 GPU. Similar data can be seen in Sony Xperia models. Flash memory is 32768 MB. Each action passes quickly, without any hangs, even working with applications is extremely enjoyable and no negative. Even during the presentation, the experts did not notice the lags, they were not found in the manufactured products, which pleases buyers. The size of RAM is 2048 MB, which means the ability to store a huge amount of information, among which may be music, movies, videos and much more.





In this model, the Quick Remote function has appeared, thanks to which the phone turns into a remote control. In addition, the owner can see simultaneously two open applications, which was adopted from Samsung. You can notice the potential increase in the number of combined tasks that the operating system called Android, unfortunately, can not yet boast.

The famous company Qualcomm is developing excellent graphics for mobile devices, which in quality can create competition for Nvidia products. Naturally, the installed Adreno 330 easily copes with games for this OS.

Camera

Selfie is currently doing absolutely everything. The next-generation smartphone has a useful feature for such users. While you are taking various poses, a yellow color appears that flashes. This means that the mobile device is conducting face recognition. When the camera finds you, the light stops blinking and changes color to green. This function is not only convenient, but also is a significant advantage over other smartphones. You can always use the flash if you wish. The main camera is 13 megapixels, and the front camera is 2 megapixels. The following image formats are supported: BMP, GIF, PNG, MPEG-4, H.264, JPEG, H.263. The mobile device LG G Flex has a slight drawback - the device does not have an optical image stabilizer, despite the high quality of the picture.

Battery

LG G Flex has a weight that is only a few grams superior to the popular Galaxy S3, but the battery capacity of the first option exceeds in this case the second. Most buyers pay attention to the quality of the battery, since the duration of the device is always important. The lithium-polymer (Li-Pol) battery has a capacity of 3500 mAh, which, without a doubt, ensures long uninterrupted operation of the mobile device. The smartphone battery is not removable.





Navigation

LG G Flex is equipped with a built-in GPS-receiver in the car DVR. Using the global satellite system, you can determine your location. Data is not always 100% accurate. The clarity of coordinates directly depends on the number of satellites falling into the field of view of the receiver. The included DVR with GPS receiver records video with coordinates. You can always watch the footage on the computer by installing the player first. In the video, in addition to the image, you will see a map of the area where the location of the car is indicated.

A-GPS system

Sometimes you have to wait a certain time before the first readings of the receiver. In LG G Flex black, the A-GPS system will reduce the duration of a cold start. To get started, the receiver must determine the exact location of the satellites. Assisted-GPS was designed to accelerate the time of the appearance of the first coordinates from the operator‚Äôs server, which provides these services. In the absence of this system in your smartphone, a cold start lasts from 0.5 to 2 minutes, in LG G Flex this process takes no more than 20 seconds. It is worth considering that at present the service is in most cases provided free of charge.

Entertainment

Many use their phone to play, listen to music, watch videos, and more. Headphone jack in a smartphone 3.5 mm. As in any other modern mobile device, a video player is installed here that supports MP4, DviX, XviD, H.264, which allows you to watch movies in any format.
