Railway

It would seem that the railway - what's so surprising? But Flomskaya is a unique attraction, which is very appreciated by tourists. Over 20 km, incredibly beautiful panoramas open from the windows, and breathing stops from the difference in height.

The train follows from Fl√•m to Myrdal and back, making sharp turns and driving past deep valleys, high cliffs and powerful waterfalls.

It all started at the end of the XIX century, when it was necessary to quickly and conveniently deliver the goods brought by water from Fl√•m to Oslo and Bergen. And even then, only after the Floma commune categorically spoke out for the urgent need for rail links, recalling that they had allocated money for the construction of a railway between Oslo and Bergen, provided that they received a branch in their own direction.

Building a 20.2 km long road was not easy. I had to break through the mountains, dig 20 tunnels and build 1 bridge. Moreover, 18 tunnels are made using shovels and hoes, manually.

The road either rises or lowers - the difference of 863 m leaves no one indifferent, there simply is no other such railway in Europe. The speed of the train does not exceed 40 km / h.

The first flight hit the road in 1939, the road was electrified in 1944.

The road operated until the 90s, when the loss-making of the enterprise led to the fact that NSB - the Norwegian railway - gave control of the Flam-Myrdal section to the local commune. It was supposed to close the road.
