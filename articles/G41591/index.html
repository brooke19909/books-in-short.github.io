Creasing and sewing lambrequin with swag

Now you can proceed to the formation of folds. The first of them is formed from the extreme point of the right shoulder to the left by lowering the so-called roller 10-15 cm down from the bar line. In this case, the distance between them should be the same on both sides.

After the shape of the product is ready, you will need to cut off the excess fabric on top of the plank and, laying the blank on a horizontal surface, bend all the edges, and secure the top with a seam at the points previously marked. Lambrequin with puffs: preparatory phase

Another interesting option for the manufacture of lambrequin is a product with puffs. True, they have one drawback - they cannot be ironed. The process of making lambrequin with puffs

These actions must be done with all brands. It is important that the fasteners are made as reliable as possible, otherwise the appearance of the product can deteriorate very quickly.
