Payment from card to card "Tinkoff" - instructions, tips

Payment by credit cards is not only one of the most popular ways, but also the most convenient. In addition, often for paying for purchases with a card various bonuses and cashbacks are credited. But sometimes there are situations when it is necessary to make a payment from a card to a Tinkoff card. This is what this article will be about.





Payment via Internet Banking

You can transfer from a Tinkoff card to a card without leaving your home. Make it easy and in the workplace. To do this, open a personal account in the online bank and access the Internet from any device. The Tinkoff card has very simple and transparent replenishment conditions. In most cases, no transfer fee will be charged.

To pay from a card to a Tinkoff card, just log in with your username and password to your personal account page. There, select the card you want to recharge, click the appropriate button. You can make a transfer from a third-party bank card. This operation will be performed instantly, extremely rarely the transfer of funds is delayed up to one day. It all depends on the conditions of the Tinkoff card, which are described in detail in the contract.





Mobile app

In addition to Internet banking, payment can also be made using the mobile application. This method will be no less convenient, since the card replenishment procedure can be absolutely anywhere. But provided that the mobile phone has Internet access. You can download this application to your smartphone both on the official website of Tinkoff Bank and through most virtual directories.

The procedure itself is very simple and is performed as follows:

  • go to the page "Translations";
  • Next, select the transfer to the card;
  • we dial sixteen digits, that is, the card number;
  • select "Continue".

If necessary, confirm the operation with the code that will come to the phone in the form of SMS.

Funds will be instantly transferred to the recipient, if he is the owner of the Tinkoff card, other transfers may come with a slight delay.

Payment from a Tinkoff card to another bank card

There are several ways to make such a transfer. Payment is easy to make via the Internet or mobile bank. In addition, you can transfer from a Tinkoff card to a card of another bank at any ATM. But it is worth considering that a commission may be taken for this procedure, in contrast to transfers via the Internet. The payment procedure itself is simple and does not take more than three minutes.

Established limits

Payment from card to Tinkoff card has some limitations. They are different for individuals and legal entities. Limitations are set on the amount of the transfer and on their number per day and month.

Consider the restrictions that are set for individuals:

  • for one operation, the amount of money transfer cannot exceed one hundred thousand rubles;
  • no more than five money transfers can be made from the card account within twenty-four hours;
  • the sum of all transfers within one calendar month should not exceed five hundred thousand rubles;
  • from a VISA card in four days you can transfer to other cards no more than three hundred thousand rubles;
  • during the whole calendar month, more than twenty different transfers of funds are not allowed.

The Bank has set such limits for payment from card to Tinkoff card.

What to do if money is not credited to the card account

Crediting funds usually does not take much time. Money comes to the card within one to five minutes. In the rarest cases, a transfer may be delayed for five days, especially if it has fallen on holidays. In such cases, do not worry.

Information about the status of the transfer and the location of the funds sent can be found by calling the hotline listed on the company's website. A bank specialist will tell all the information in detail.

In cases where the transaction was not completed after five days, you must contact the bank. The client must provide an extract from the account to which the funds were transferred.

The bank will conduct a check, and the money will either be returned to the sender or credited to the card account of the recipient.

The most common reason for the delay in receiving the amount sent to the card is a technical malfunction.
