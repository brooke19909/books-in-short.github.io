Smartwatch History

This device was once patented by Apple. Its engineers struggled for a long time to create a gadget that could pair with a smartphone and display information about a call, a song being played, or something else. There were the boldest ideas. They even planned to design such a device in the form of a ring. But stopped at the clock. And did not fail. The first iWatch combined the functions of a fitness tracker, an additional screen for a smartphone and a watch. Samsung soon pulled herself up. The Korean giant released something similar to the brainchild of the Yabloko, but with its own signature flavor and at a more adequate price.
