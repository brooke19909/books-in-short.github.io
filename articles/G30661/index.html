Pension "Scarlet Sails" in Alushta: description and reviews

On Sudakovskoe highway, eighteen kilometers from the city of Alushta, the Alye Parusa pension, beloved by many tourists, is located. On the south coast it is the most famous, people come here from neighboring cities, often the way to it is kept by holidaymakers.



Location

Pension "Scarlet Sails" in Alushta (Crimea) is located near the picturesque Black Sea coast, in the tract Sotera, at the foot of the majestic amphitheater of the Crimean mountains . Tourists who come here to relax are attracted by the incredibly beautiful nature and clean air, as well as gorgeous mountain landscapes. In the area where the boarding house is located, a moderate favorable climate reigns, which many travelers like.



Rooms

As for the number of rooms of the boarding house, it is quite large. On the territory there are several buildings and cottages, in them you can choose a room of different levels of comfort: economy, standard and comfort. There are also several rooms, consisting of two rooms - they are classified as deluxe.

Economy

Economy rooms offer guests quite a good rest in stone buildings and cottages. Several rooms of this level have one bathroom and a shower room. In the hotel "Scarlet Sails" in Alushta, the cost of an economy class room is from 1035 rubles per person.

For this fee, guests receive in their room a minimal set of furniture, consisting of two or three twin beds, a refrigerator, one fan, and some rooms also have a table and a couple of chairs.

Standard

Rooms of this class cost guests a little more - from 1485 rubles per person per day, but for a higher fee more comfortable living conditions are provided. First of all, each room has a private bathroom and a personal shower. A higher level of comfort is also determined by more comfortable furniture. In addition, guests staying in the standard can use air conditioning, it allows you to maintain optimal climatic conditions in the room, regardless of the weather on the street.

Comfort

The hotel "Scarlet Sails" 3 * in Alushta are particularly popular superior rooms. This is due to the fact that the amount for living in them does not differ much from the one that will need to be paid, for example, for an economy class room, but the conditions are significantly improved.

The rooms have modern double beds, bed linen is changed regularly (every three days). There is also a TV, Internet access, a refrigerator and a private bathroom. Guests staying in these rooms can take advantage of the option of catering on a custom-made menu. Some rooms of this class are two-room.

The cost of renting comfort category apartments is 1755-1935 rubles per person per day.



Two rooms

"Scarlet Sails" offers families who come to this boarding house to settle in two-room apartments, ideal for families with children. They provide the necessary number of beds (up to 5), as well as the possibility of installing a crib.

In apartments of this level, absolutely all conditions for a comfortable stay are created. They contain a complete set of furniture, the necessary electronics, as well as an individual bathroom.

Rest in this room will cost about 2000 rubles per person per day.



Conditions for children

Coming to Alushta (Crimea) reviews about the "Scarlet Sails" leave enthusiastic. They claim that this guesthouse is an ideal place to relax for the whole family. All this is due to the fact that it is here that everything is equipped for the most comfortable stay of representatives of any age category, including for children.

For small guests there is a large playground in which kids can not only have fun playing educational games, but also get to know their peers. There are also several playgrounds that are equipped with all safety considerations in mind. All of them are in the dense shade of trees.

Beach

Pension "Scarlet Sails" in Alushta is located on the very shore of the Black Sea and has a clean pebble beach, on which you can admire the beauty of nature and wonderful views of the mountains. The beach is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable pastime and a pleasant stay. On its territory there are several locker rooms, toilets and showers. All the guests of the boarding house can use the sun loungers and beach umbrellas - they are issued to them without any rent.



Shops nearby

Near the pension there are some important infrastructure objects, the presence of which is of great importance. However, most often guests go to the nearest shops for food. For business

Many vacationers who come to the Alye Parusa boarding house in Alushta are business people who do not have the opportunity to distract from work for even a minute.
