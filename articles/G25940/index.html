Gauss LED lamp - a leader in lighting technology

Today it is difficult to imagine a modern apartment without LED lights. The use of semiconductor lighting devices allows you to emphasize the features of design decisions, display the play of light and shadow, make your home special and unique.



On the shop windows you can find a huge assortment of LED products, sales consultants will offer various options: they will differ in power consumption and radiation power; in size and type of cap; in quality and, accordingly, in price; You can be offered lamps with different colors of light; and, of course, from different manufacturers. In this article we will talk about such a device as a Gauss LED lamp. This trademark belongs to one of the leading world companies in the production of lighting equipment. The Gauss brand can be found on more than 500 different modern lighting fixtures - this is the Gauss LED lamp, and decorative, spotlights and general-purpose lamps, fluorescent lighting fixtures, LED strips, table lamps, flashlights and much more. Under this brand, many accessories and components for lighting devices are produced.

The Gauss LED lamp is ahead of its competitors in terms of quality, price, and after-sales service. Gauss is one of the first companies to introduce innovative technologies in the manufacture of its products, thanks to which it is a leader in the production of energy-efficient lighting. The Gauss LED lamp emits warm, soft light, completely identical to the light of incandescent lamps, but it is much more economical. These lamps have excellent quality, unique design. The highlight of Gauss is the aesthetics of its products. Thanks to a huge variety of design and technical solutions, lighting products of this brand are able to satisfy even the most picky buyer.



Gauss company presents its products in a wide range:

- a new trend - dimmable lamps, allow you to adjust the emitted light stream;

- semiconductor lighting devices of various capacities: Gauss LED lamps 5W, 10W, 30W, etc .;

- Mirror lamps of various capacities allow you to create various light accents in your interior;

- Tablet lighting devices are used for high-quality lighting of commercial and industrial facilities, they are also suitable for lighting residential areas;

- LED lamp-balls, lamp-candles are used in different, even the most unusual models of chandeliers and lamps;

- spotlights are used as decoration elements.

Especially popular among consumers are low-power diode devices. They give a strong uniform soft light, but at the same time they consume a small amount of electricity. An example is Gauss led 4W LED bulbs - the best choice for lighting your home.



To summarize, let us say that all products of the Gauss trademark undergo very strict control. Product quality is checked by independent laboratories in Russia, China, and Europe. The awards received at international competitions and specialized exhibitions also speak a lot.
