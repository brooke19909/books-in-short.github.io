Club "Theater" (Moscow): description, address and reviews

In the capital there are many interesting places where you can have a good time. However, not all institutions can boast that they conduct concerts of groups of various musical directions. Club "Theater" (Moscow) is well known to many music lovers and lovers of a unique atmosphere. In the institution you can hear both your favorite hits and completely new songs from beginner performers. For maximum comfort, a restaurant is open where you can order delicious and popular dishes for every taste.

General information

Club "Theater" (Moscow) is located in an old mansion, which was built in the XVIII century. This detail already makes the place special for many visitors. Previously, balls and various receptions were held here, as well as a real theater. The building has absorbed this atmosphere, so visitors respond most flatteringly to the design of the club. In the hall you can see balconies with stucco, which has been restored from past centuries. The institution stands out among the others by the fact that the building itself has a high-quality sound. Guests can enjoy it during concerts. After all, it was not for nothing that the theater used to be here, so the acoustics here are very good. That is why on the stages of the club you can see a variety of musical groups, as well as enjoy the sound of each note.

The institution has opened two whole halls, each of which looks presentable and spectacular. On the Internet you can often see a photo of the hall of the Teatr club in Moscow, since even a rare connoisseur will like the premises. For VIP-guests there is a separate area, as well as balconies with tables for 300 people, from where you can watch performances. For visitors open four bars. The first room usually houses about a thousand people. In the second room fewer people gather, as it is designed for two hundred people. In the halls regular concerts are regularly held, comparable to real festivals in large complexes. The institution has modern equipment for large-scale performances. On the stage, guests can see not only Russian pop music, but also foreign groups. The club has no restrictions on musical styles, which is why it sounds like rock and metal, as well as hip-hop. The venue is often used for various parties; musicians also organize presentations of new albums here.

Reviews about the club Theater in Moscow are both positive and negative. Many guests like it here. They celebrate the pleasant atmosphere, as well as the original design of the institution. A considerable number of visitors were satisfied with the sound and concerts that they could get to. Regarding the price of drinks, guests respond differently. Some items are overpriced, so club guests do not recommend spending money here. By cons, many attribute the presence of a smoking area directly in the room. The institution has a rather large wardrobe, but still large queues are formed. These points are also indicated by some users in their reviews. Guests also write that club employees do not always behave politely.

The address of the club "Theater" (Moscow)

The institution is located at the following address: Barclay Street, house - 6, building - 2. The club is open from 20:00 to 6:00.

How to get there

Club "Theater" (Moscow) can be found quite simply, as it is conveniently located from various points in the city. You can recognize the building among the rest, as the old mansion undoubtedly stands out. To come here is available both by car and by public transport. Not so far from the club there are several metro stations at once: Bagrationovskaya, Victory Park and Filevsky Park. In addition, you can get off at the Bagrationovsky passage stop. There are buses under the numbers m2 and 116.

Additional features

Many newlyweds decide to spend their important day in a special place. Club "Theater" (Moscow) is perfect for this. The original design and furnishings will delight guests, and young people can boast great photos from the establishment. In addition, here you can spend a birthday or corporate party, which will be remembered for a long time. The club has a restaurant with a good selection of Japanese and European dishes. Guests can enjoy a banquet in one of the rooms. Maximum capacity is 250 people. A spacious wardrobe is open for visitors, where you can leave your outerwear.
