Hotel Wyndham Sanya Bay 5 * (China, Hainan): photos and reviews of tourists

Since Chinese resorts are especially popular among our compatriots, in the article we want to talk about the Wyndham Sanya Bay 5 * hotel.

Resort popularity

This complex is located on Hainan Island - one of the resort areas of China. Most of it is occupied by parks and forests, but the southern coast is reserved for tourists. Hainan is known as a medical resort. Local motels offer a variety of water treatments. The island enjoys no less popularity among divers, since it has long gained fame due to the presence of countless coral reefs in the South China Sea.





Hotel Wyndham Sanya Bay 5 * is located in the city of Sanya, near the airport (international) Sanya Phoenix and the railway station. Getting to it is not difficult. Of course, the hotel can book a transfer. But for those who are going to get to the complex on their own, it does not hurt to know the exact address of Wyndham Sanya Bay 5 *: China, Sanya Bay, 572000, Xincheng Road 93.

Luxurious modern complex located on the coast and has a beautiful view of the blue sea. Wyndham Sanya Bay 5 * - a good hotel for families.

Hotel infrastructure

Wyndham Sanya Bay 5 * (China, Sanya) has a developed infrastructure. On its territory there is everything you need for a good and carefree holiday: five conference rooms, a bar, a pool with slides, dry cleaning, laundry, parking, car rental, exchange office, shops.

This is not entirely convenient. Therefore, many hotel guests prefer to visit the public beach. It is located quite close, the only drawback is that on the way to it you have to pass by the cemetery.

Entertainment

Resting in Wyndham Sanya Bay 5 * (photos are given in the article), you should definitely visit the sights of Hainan Island.

The island is considered one of the best resorts throughout the Middle Kingdom. And once Hainan was a place where they sent to the link of objectionable nobles. Currently, many have heard of him as a well-known health resort. Many residents of Siberia and the Far East have long chosen a resort and treat their ailments here. Do not underestimate the beneficial effects of the warm waters of the South China Sea.





Traditional Chinese beaches are very beautiful: azure water, white sand and wonderful palm trees. But not only the beauty of the coast attracts many tourists here. Hainan has long been chosen by divers from all over the world. And the reason for this is the rich underwater world of the South China Sea. Visibility is twenty meters, and diving is possible throughout the year due to the good mild climate and the complete absence of strong currents. Very rarely echoes of severe storms reach the island, and then divers relax on the shore, and surfers come to replace them in the sea.

Tourists, resting on the island, certainly want to get souvenirs. The hallmark of Hainan is pearls of various colors. Also, silk, tea, rhinestone, wood carvings, porcelain are brought from the island.

Sightseeing Tours

Vacationers should visit the Xinglong Valley of Radon Springs. It is located a hundred kilometers from the town of Sanya. Since the island is of volcanic origin, an amazing number of thermal springs formed on it. All of them have temperatures from 45 to 65 degrees. Such radon sources are very useful for people with diseases of the respiratory system, skin and musculoskeletal system. In the valley, tourists can also visit the garden of amazing tropical plants. Sources are surrounded by coconut groves and tea plantations. The whole valley is filled with chic SPA centers and hotels, medical centers where you can visit wellness treatments.

Monkey Island is a favorite place for vacationers. It is located near the city of Sanya. The reserve is somewhat unique, as it is the only place in the world where a huge number of monkeys live in natural conditions. Tourists can reach the island by cable car or boat.





No less interesting place for vacationers is the "Butterfly Gorge". The reserve is located in Yaluvan bay. On its territory you can not only see a huge number of butterflies, but also admire exotic flowers and plants. The uniqueness of this place is that there is a rare collection of insects from around the world.
