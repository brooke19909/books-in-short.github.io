"Krasiya" - the ski resort with the longest ski run in the country

Guests from different countries of the CIS come to the Carpathian ski resorts every year. To do this, they do not need to apply for a visa. Those who come here for the first time can see for themselves that this area is no worse than other similar places.

Popular ski resorts

Among those who are not coming to the Carpathians for the first time, Bukovel, Slavske and Dragobrat became their favorite places. Not only the local population knows about them, but also residents outside the country. These large ski resorts of the Carpathians have good equipped recreation centers and excellent slopes. The price for which they offer to relax is quite moderate, and the living conditions and services offered are decent.

Resort "Krasiya"



But in addition to the well-known resorts there are other good places that Transcarpathia offers. "Krasia" refers to this number. The main advantage of this ski resort can be called low prices, lack of lines.

Mount Krasia has been known since the Soviet years. Already at that time a ski slope was equipped here . The first ski lift and track in Ukraine were here. Years later, chair lifts appeared on this mountain, which the country had not previously had. Although the resort in question has been operating for many years, it is not widely known about it. Perhaps this is due to the remoteness of the area in which it is located. But this makes Krasiya attractive to all regular customers, because in the midst there are not too many people. The skiing season usually starts in December and ends in April. There are quite snowy winters, but sometimes a thaw occurs. But thanks to special equipment, the track is always covered with snow.

You can stay in one of the hotels or in the private sector. The height of the mountain is 1036 meters.

Equipped Resort



Krasiya is a ski resort with the longest ski run in the country. Its length is 3400 meters. But this is not her only descent. There are several tracks that differ in difficulty levels. The slopes of the mountains are mostly gentle.

Also on the mountain are six lifts. Single-seat begins at the foot of the mountain and is lucky to the very top. A two-seat drive to the middle. Then you can transfer to a four-chair lift or a yoke. Also, the resort has a yoke for children, runs from the foot to a height of 200 m.

There is a plateau in the middle of the mountain. This is where the transplant site is. Here you can relax a bit in the restaurant. There is also a hotel built and there is an observation deck.

Krasiya (ski resort) additionally offers off-piste skiing.



For sports beginners

Experienced athletes come to this resort, but the tracks are also suitable for those who have never been skiing or just want to try snowboarding. There are all conditions for this:

  • wide, cool roads, while they are quite long;
  • in winter, a comfortable temperature, shallow, loose snow remain;
  • low congestion, i.e. few people on the slopes;
  • there is a specialized school, so you can contact the instructor;
  • the cost of equipment rental and accommodation is moderate.

Learning how to freely ski, you can enjoy the measured smooth descent. Krasia (ski resort) also has freeride and back country seats.

Vacation with children

Parents with children can come to these places. There is special equipment of small sizes, simple tracks and a special lift. You can hire an instructor. Also from steep slopes you can ride in a pair with a child on a snow tub (rubber circle). Children from three years old are admitted to it. Skating on such a tablet is equivalent to a sled, so do not worry that this type of entertainment is very dangerous.
