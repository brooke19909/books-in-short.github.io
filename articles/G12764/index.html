Rating of three stars hotels Kemer

This resort is considered one of the most popular among Russians. Pine forests with mountain air and clear sea water make vacation in this Turkish small town in the province of Antalya the most attractive.



Kemer hotels are divided into two varieties. In some of them, those who like a relaxing vacation prefer to stay, in others, on the contrary, are young people who like the proximity of noisy entertainment.

Regardless of the star rating, all hotels provide tourists with a clean beach and fresh air, but most importantly - an oriental friendly service. In Kemer there are not only luxury complexes, but also three-star inexpensive family hotels suitable for families with small children. They can be located on the first line, for example, Asia Hotel or Larissa Park Hotel, or somewhat removed from the beach. The hotel was built in 1997, when Kemer as a resort was just developing. The last restoration in it was carried out two years ago. The total territory on which this city hotel of economy class stands is 1700 square meters.

The hotel was built in the center of Kemer, three hundred meters from the coast. It is positioned as a youth, because its location is perfect for visiting many entertainment venues nearby. The airport is fifty-five kilometers distant.

Sea
