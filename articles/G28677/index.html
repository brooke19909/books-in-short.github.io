Motor ship "Radisson" - a five-star icebreaker

The young foliage of the Neskuchny Garden, the green lawns of Moscow State University, the gold domes of the Novodevichy Convent , fogged glasses with champagne against the background of Christmas tree lights and towers of the Kremlin - every ship "Radisson" is ready to show its passengers. After all, the navigation of these icebreakers, capable of breaking ice 10-20 cm thick, does not stop for any of 365 days a year.





Many saw how these motor ships ply along the Moscow River. Radisson provides the opportunity to take a ride on a regular flight or rent a boat for a private banquet. If the company is small, you can stay in a first-class cabin trimmed with African rosewood and equipped with its own climate control system. For an additional fee, experienced craftsmen will decorate the room with flowers or balloons. And in the evening, special lighting makes the icebreaker unusually beautiful.

The name of the French adventurer Pierre-Esprey Radisson is strongly associated with the luxury and comfort of five stars. The former hotel "Ukraine", the only one of all the hotels in the European chain, has its own flotilla. And any ship "Radisson" corresponds to a given high level. It offers convenient parking, taxi service, an audio guide that can be downloaded to your phone via free Wi-Fi, photo and video shooting, and music programs. We prepared here for the meeting of passengers with disabilities.





A walk on the ship "Radisson" is remembered for Italian cuisine and magnificent views. No wonder the owners of the flotilla lured Chef Lorenzo Strappato from the Bellagio. The menu is not very large, but interesting, with mouth-watering names of dishes. Duck breast in apple and raspberry sauce and lamb loin on a plate of mashed potatoes are unlikely to leave anyone indifferent. Lunch in a restaurant will cost an average of 1000-1300 rubles per person.

It is cool in the heat of the cabin, and in the winter cold it is cozy and warm, the most thermophilic guests are provided with rugs. Panoramic glazing provides excellent visibility even from places that are not near the window. And for those who want to smell the fresh river air, open decks are equipped.





The Radisson motor ship prepared a special surprise for experienced and sophisticated passengers: thematic trips with original gifts, special decorations and interesting entertainments.

Tickets for the Radisson ship cost 900 rubles for a 2.5 hour trip. They can be purchased at the box office at the berths, but it is safer to buy at the terminal or book online. In the latter case, there is no need to print a ticket, just show it on the screen of your mobile device.

The cruise on the ship "Radisson" is an original and romantic gift and an unusual option for a celebration. The abundance of impressions and the availability of price, time and the possibility of buying tickets make it very popular among foreign tourists, young couples and families with children. It will provide an unforgettable vacation that will be remembered for a long time.
