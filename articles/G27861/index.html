How to activate the Promised Payment (Rostelecom)

Without access to the Internet, interactive television, landline and mobile communications, it is already difficult to imagine work or leisure. These services are in most cases provided on a prepaid basis.

Situations are different, and sometimes replenishing the account may not be time or technical feasibility. In this case, the promised payment service will be necessary for Rostelecom subscribers. "Promised payment" for Internet and IP-TV subscribers

Most actively, Rostelecom has recently been promoting Internet and interactive television services in the market. In this case, to resume the service, you need to replenish the account or activate the "Promised payment" option. It is most convenient to connect the option through the "My Account".





It is worth remembering that the activation of the Promised Payment service (Rostelecom) for Internet and TV subscribers is available only with a negative balance. If there are funds on the account, then connecting the option will not work.

Terms of Service

Despite the fact that the "Promised payment" is available to most subscribers of the company, there are some requirements that must be met to connect the service, consider the main ones:

  1. The service contract must be concluded more than 4 months ago. Subscribers who have connected the number, the Internet, etc. from Rostelecom less than 120 days ago, will not be able to use the Promised Payment.
  2. Lack of locks. If a subscriber has used voluntary blocking of access to the Internet or phone number over the past 3 months, the company will refuse to activate the service by notifying him of this.
  3. Negative balance. Internet and TV users can activate the Promised Payment only if there is a minus in the account, for mobile subscribers it is permissible to have a small amount on the balance sheet that does not exceed 20 rubles.
  4. No debts. If the subscribers have a negative balance for a long time, then it will be impossible to activate the Promised Payment service.

How to pay off debt?



After activating the service, subscribers must replenish their account with the amount of the "Promised Payment" within 5 days. If this is not done, then in the future the company may refuse the "Promised payment".

Important! The service is paid. Its cost is 7-10 rubles, which will be automatically deducted from the next payment.
