Alcatel One Touch Smartphone - reviews and overview

Alcatel products occupy a specific niche in the Russian market, as well as in the CIS countries. These are mainly middle-class youth smartphones. But there are exceptions. Each model is unique in its own way, each has its own audience of admirers. And, of course, everywhere there are flaws.

Alcatel: a bit of history



Initially, this company had French roots. It got its name in 1985. Its main task was to compete worthily and even squeeze giants such as Siemens and General Electric on the market . The production of mobile phones has become one of several areas of activity of the French company.

In 2004, the Alcatel brand was acquired by the global electronics company TCL. Today it can be called the largest Chinese manufacturer of machinery with a main office in Huazhou.

Alcatel is currently working with such well-known Russian companies as MegaFon and MediaTek. Therefore, in the lineup of the manufacturer appeared not only individual, but also joint samples of smartphones.

In addition, expanding the lineup, the creators do not forget about the technical characteristics and new systems. Therefore, many buyers choose this particular brand of mobile phones.

Customer reviews and main problems at work

Like any modern phone, the Alcatel One Touch smartphone has its own strengths and weaknesses. And after the purchase, many users find a number of problems and shortcomings. Among them, one can single out the standard ones, which are found in almost all modern smartphone lines, and the individual ones, which are mainly inherent in Alcatel models. The most common include:

  • Software problems or delays. This is a system crash, SD search, problems with the Internet connection, the inability to configure one or another option, application errors, etc. Such shortcomings, according to the owners, can be due to both factory defects and inept use of a smartphone.
  • Problems connecting individual modules or devices. Often this is GPS or Wi-Fi, as well as working with a computer or laptop via USB. Those who purchased Alcatel One Touch leave reviews that these modules do not work quite stably and are constantly turned off.
  • Problems playing music files and applications. What could be caused, among other things, by using the wrong format or damaged tracks. Try to fix this by installing a new player.

Key Competitive Advantages of Alcatel Smartphones





Now in our electronics market there is a huge amount of various equipment. In this case, you can find models for every taste. What makes consumers choose Alcatel One Touch products among many other smartphones?

The main advantages of these models include:

  • Competitive cost. The presented models are distinguished by an optimal ratio of price and quality. Here you can find many quite budget options for smartphones and phones with good filling and design.
  • Time-relevant specifications and features. Despite the fact that the phones are in the middle price segment, they have all the qualities of modern models of large and well-known manufacturers. There is Wi-Fi, support for SD cards of various formats, etc.
  • Ergonomic design. Chinese manufacturers, unlike many competitors, pay great attention to the appearance of Alcatel One Touch smartphones. Customer reviews talk about the functionality and convenience of all the keys and the phone as a whole.

Otherwise, for each individual buyer there are pros and cons. After all, this is a deeply individual matter.

  • Hero These are smartphones with a huge screen of 6 inches. It is on him that the whole bet is made. It is bright, fast and responsive.

  • Star Well-equipped and quite budget smartphone. A version with two SIM cards is also available.

Now let's take a closer look at this Alcatel One Touch. Customer reviews speak of several positive points:

  • Loud speakers and good sound quality.

Also worth noting in this Alcatel One Touch smartphone are specifications. Here 2 Gb of RAM, a good camera and a fast processor.

There are several disadvantages:

Alcatel pop

A good budget multifunctional gadget. You can choose a model with two SIM cards, which is quite convenient. The most popular representatives of the series can be called Alcatel One Touch C5 and C3. Their main difference is in cameras, display size and dimensions.



Models of the Pop series have a bright, cheerful shell. Despite the quite affordable price, they are assembled quite qualitatively. The design of these smartphones is simple and recognizable.

  • High-quality assembly - nothing crashes anywhere, does not stagger and does not creak.
  • Built-in working GPS module, which facilitates the work of travelers.

There are several tangible disadvantages:

  • The cameras do not meet modern requirements. Agree, 3.2 and 5 megapixels weakly compete with 12 megapixels or more.

One touch ultra

Another representative of the Idol family. Ultra is the thinnest smartphone in the Alcatel line. Its width is only 6.5 mm and weighs 115 g. The company introduced it at CES-2013 as a flagship, along with the Idol model.

Smartphone Alcatel One Touch Ultra is endowed with the following advantages:

  • Large (4.65 inches) and bright screen. The picture is colorful thanks to AMOLED technology.
  • Good battery capacity. The 1800 mAh battery lasts almost the whole day.
  • Decent sound quality and volume. However, it is solely on a hard surface.

Despite the fact that the manufacturer positions this gadget as a flagship, its only competitive feature is a slim body. Otherwise, this is a very standard budget smartphone, which has a number of disadvantages:

  • The lack of a standard headphone jack. This is due to the same subtlety.
  • There is no flash card slot. There is only built-in memory, which sooner or later may still not be enough.
  • Pretty slow smartphone. Despite 1 GB of RAM, the device constantly slows down, even when taking pictures.

Alcatel Star: review



Very compact budget smartphone. Externally, almost completely copies the famous iPhone. Introduced by manufacturers in 2013 as a good budget gaming smartphone.

Let's move on to the main characteristics and features. The small size and weight of 119 g are encouraging. The screen surface is perfectly protected by a special Gorilla Glass, and the display itself is made using Amoled's unique technology, which provides it with bright colors and a good viewing angle.

Alcatel One Touch Star runs on two processors of the MediaTek chipset. The RAM is only 512 MB. Implemented the ability to work two SIM cards, there is a slot for a flash drive.

From the pros, you can distinguish:

  • Convenience and ergonomics. Thanks to its compact size, it fits perfectly in the hand, and the buttons are arranged so that they are quite comfortable to control.
  • Good sensitive sensor. Thanks to the technology used, the screen responds to the slightest touch.
  • Bright and rich colors that are visible even in the sun.

Now you can pay attention to the main disadvantages of this gadget:

  • Quite quiet speakers and vibrating alert. It is heard only if the phone is very close.
  • There is not enough battery charge for a long time. With active use - a maximum of a couple of hours.
