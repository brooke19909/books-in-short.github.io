Additional Information

Before traveling to the Apsara Beachfront Resort Villa 4 * hotel, read some additional useful information. Namely:

  • Check-in of newly arrived guests is made from 14:00 to 23:30.
  • On the day of eviction, you need to leave the room in the period from 07:00 to noon.
  • Toddlers under two years old are provided with free cots.
  • Placing a child under the age of 11 years in an extra bed will cost you 50% of the cost of a full bed.
  • Pets are not allowed, regardless of breed and size.
  • Guests can pay with credit cards for services received.
  • A prerequisite for registration and settlement is the presentation of a valid identity card.
