Theo Bungalows 3 * (Greece / Halkidiki) - photos, prices, description and reviews of tourists

One of the most densely populated and popular peninsulas of Halkidiki (Greece) is Kassandra. However, along with the developed infrastructure and the availability of entertainment for every taste, there are secluded and cozy places where people who are tired of the daily noise of big cities will certainly enjoy their vacation. One of these places is the resort village of Kriopigi. If you decide to come here, then as a budget, but comfortable hotel, we can recommend Theo Bungalows 3 *. We will talk further about what tourists expect here, as well as the impressions of our compatriots from this hotel.





Location

Theo Bungalos Hotel is located 300 meters from the center of the resort village of Kriopigi. So in the vicinity of the hotel you will find taverns, restaurants, supermarkets, shops and other infrastructure. In addition, there is a bus stop nearby , from where it is convenient to travel to nearby towns. The hotel is 600 meters from the beach. So you can walk to the coast in just 10 minutes. The beaches here are very clean, sandy. There are almost no stones at the bottom. As for the airport, the nearest of them is 90 kilometers from the hotel. So the journey from the air harbor will take you about two hours.





Hotel description, photo

Theo Bungalows 3 * hotel is a very cozy place, suitable for a great vacation for families with children, as well as for older people and couples in love. However, young people thirsting for noisy entertainment may seem a little boring here. The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 45 comfortable rooms of a standard type. They are located in several small buildings located in a picturesque green area. It offers guests a restaurant, bar, pool, tour desk, car rental and much more. The cost of accommodation at the hotel includes breakfast in the format of a buffet. In addition, guests have the opportunity to have lunch or dinner in the hotel restaurant. Here the chef prepares delicious dishes of both national Greek and traditional European cuisine.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules adopted by Theo Bungalows 3 * (Greece) , guests are checked in after 13:00. On the day of departure, the occupied apartments must be vacated before 11 a.m. However, if your flight is in the evening, and the hotel has available rooms, the administration will extend your number for a few more hours at no extra charge. By the way, you can pay for your stay at Theo Bungalos both in cash and with Visa or Mastercard payment cards.





Cost of living

Prices for accommodation in Theo Bungalows 3 *, according to domestic tourists, are consistent with the level of service and the conditions of accommodation in the hotel. So, a seven-day stay here in the month of August will cost you in the amount of from 455 euros for a double room and from 525 euros for a triple room. Prices shown include breakfast.

Theo Bungalows 3 *: reviews of Russian tourists

Since today so many people when planning their foreign trips pay attention not only to the description of the hotels, but also to the comments of other travelers about them, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the impressions of our compatriots about their vacation at Theo Bungalos Hotel (Kassandra, Greece). Looking ahead a bit, we note that almost all tourists were happy with their choice, since their vacation here was a 100% success. But we will understand in more detail.





Comments of our compatriots about the hotel itself and its number of rooms

Many travelers remember Theo Bungalows 3 * with undisguised delight. So, they really liked the look of this hotel. It is decorated in the traditional Greek style, replete with many ladders, and surrounded by pine groves that fill the air with a rich coniferous aroma. The rooms in the hotel, although small, are very comfortable and decorated in a pleasant style. In addition, they have everything you need for a comfortable stay. Pleased with the guests and the availability of free air conditioning in the rooms (in many other three-star hotels in Greece, the use of air conditioning is paid). Also, travelers were satisfied with the quality of cleaning. So, according to them, the maids in the apartments were cleaned daily, and once every three days they renewed bed linen and towels. The only minor drawback of the rooms of Theo Bungalos, our compatriots considered an insufficient number of outlets. Therefore, they recommend that tourists take a tee with them whenever possible.





Hotel Location Reviews

As for the location of Theo Bungalows 3 * (Kassandra), only those tourists who, when planning a trip, did not study the hotel description well were dissatisfied. After all, he is in a small resort village. Therefore, you can‚Äôt count on a lot of stormy and noisy entertainment here. But this place is great for those people who are pretty tired of the noise and stress of the big city and want to spend an unforgettable vacation in a calm and relaxed atmosphere, enjoying the coniferous aroma and listening to the sound of the surf. However, you should not think that there is absolutely no infrastructure in Kriopigi. So, there are taverns, restaurants, supermarkets, souvenir shops, and even a fur coat. In addition, you can always rent a car and go anywhere in Greece.





Impressions of tourists about food and staff

Our compatriots were generally satisfied with the quality of food at Theo Bungalows 3 * hotel (Kriopigi). So, breakfasts were monotonous enough, but there was always plenty to choose from. For lunch and dinner, every day the chef tried to cook something new. Please note that drinks are chargeable during lunch and dinner. Seasoned travelers still recommend taking breakfast only. In this case, you will have the opportunity to taste the delicious dishes of national cuisine in taverns and restaurants of both Kriopigi and neighboring towns and cities. In addition, the prices in the restaurants are quite comparable with the prices in the hotel.

As for the staff of Theo Bungalos, our compatriots have only positive impressions of it. So, according to tourists, all the staff here are very welcoming, friendly, attentive and always ready to help. I was also pleased with the fact that some of the employees speak Russian. Therefore, if you do not speak English at all, then, if necessary, you can also explain in your native language.





Beach holidays and entertainment at Theo Bungalos: the opinion of tourists from Russia

According to our compatriots, the beaches in Kriopigi are very good. There is clean sand everywhere. When entering the water, almost no stones are found. The beaches here are in municipal ownership, so if you want to soak up the sunbed and use an umbrella, you will have to pay extra (about 5 euros) for this. However, you can always sit comfortably on a towel brought with you in the area where there are no sun loungers. As for remoteness, from Theo Bungalows 3 * to the beach you can walk in 10 minutes. In this case, the road goes from the hill. On the way to the hotel you will have to climb the hill, which may require a little more time and effort. Many tourists were not embarrassed by the need to perform such physical exercises, however, everyone can always get from the beach to the hotel and on a compact train that runs regularly in this direction (travel costs 1 euro).

As for other entertainments, if for some reason you do not want to go down to the sea, you can always spend an hour or two at the outdoor pool in the territory of Theo Bungalos. It features a sun terrace and a bar with a cheerful bartender and a large selection of drinks. Wi-Fi is also available here. Experienced travelers definitely recommend not to spend the whole vacation exclusively at the hotel and on the beach, but to travel around the country. So, in the neighboring towns and settlements you can go by bus (the schedule is available at the reception) or taxi. For longer trips, it is best to rent a car. Moreover, this can be done directly at the hotel. There is also a tour desk.

Conclusion

Summing up, we can safely say that most of our compatriots liked to relax in the Theo Bungalows 3 * hotel (Halkidiki). In their opinion, this hotel offers guests an excellent combination of price and quality of accommodation and service. Therefore, if you want to spend an unforgettable vacation in a calm place with a national touch on the beach, you should definitely go to Theo Bungalos.
