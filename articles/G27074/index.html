Holidays in Cambodia: reviews of tourists

For many decades, Cambodia has not been open to tourists. Therefore, only the most daring adventurers dared to visit the country. Now it is open to guests. Gradually, Cambodia has become a popular holiday destination. A country located in southeast Asia can offer not only a lazy vacation on the beach, but also educational trips through ancient temples and walks through the jungle. According to tourists, Cambodia is an incredibly interesting country, which is worth seeing with your own eyes.

Climate of Cambodia

If you consider yourself an active traveler, then a beach holiday in Egypt or Turkey will definitely not surprise you. Therefore, in search of new sensations and adventures, you can go to Cambodia. On the Indochina Peninsula you will find wild beaches and quite modern resorts. In addition, excursions to amazing places will make your stay memorable.

Thinking about a trip to an exotic country, you must first understand the features of climatic conditions. This will allow you to choose the best period for the trip. According to reviews of Cambodia, the concept of a season in the country does not exist. You can have a good rest on the coast at any time of the year. Whenever you visit a country, the water temperature never drops below +21 degrees. From the beginning of autumn to the beginning of spring, the most favorable period for a beach holiday lasts. At a time when cold weather begins and winter comes, on the Siamese coast the air is already warming up to +28 ... + 30 degrees. But in the rest of the months, the weather will not ruin your vacation. According to reviews, Cambodia simply does not have bad weather. Of course, rains still happen sometimes, but they do not affect the temperature of the water in the sea. In addition, showers here pass very quickly.

The average annual air temperature in the country is +27 degrees. From November to the end of February there is practically no rain. Sometimes in the evenings it can be a bit cool (+17 degrees). But the hottest months are May, April and March. At this time, temperature indicators rise to +40 degrees. Spring is good for relaxing for those tourists who are not afraid of heat.

In general, the country's climate can be described as hot and tropical, which largely depends on the monsoon.

Cambodia Hotels

Currently, there are approximately 1300 hotels in Cambodia. Among them there are complexes of different levels. You can find a variety of options - even from 5-7 dollars (300-400 p.) Per night. Mid-level housing will cost an average of 500-1500 p. Well, the most luxurious five-star hotels offer rooms from 5,000 rubles and above. Hotel service is quite actively developing in the country. The construction of new complexes, guesthouses and restaurants is ongoing. The largest selection of hotels is available in Phnom Penh, Sihanoukville, Siem Reale. According to reviews, Cambodia hotels are so diverse that you can find rooms of any price category. If you do not want to spend extra money or do not have such an opportunity, then you can pick up quite budget options.

Sihanoukville

Based on reviews about Cambodia, we can say that the most popular among our compatriots is the resort of Sihanoukville, Kepe and the local islands. The most famous is Koh Rong. It is not difficult to get to it from Sihanoukville by boats that run on a clear schedule.

The cost of housing on the islands is quite acceptable. Basically, the complexes are presented in the form of bungalows, the rental of which costs - 500-1000 r. per day. You can also go to the island of Koh Rong Layer. All islands have very beautiful beaches and clear sea.

Among the most worthy hotels of Sihanoukville, one can distinguish "Song Sea Private Island." The five-star complex has beautiful comfortable rooms. The hotel is located on two islets that are connected by a pedestrian bridge. The guests of the establishment have the opportunity to settle in one of the villas, surrounded by greenery. The hotel area is equipped with swimming pools and relaxation areas.

Another good hotel, Golden Sand, is located near the beach. The institution has only three stars, but this does not affect the service. Tourists also praise the three-star Hotel Divers Inc Hotel. Of course, it is remote from the beach, but it is equipped with an entertainment system.

Cambodia Attractions

If you are planning a vacation in Cambodia, then it is worth considering in advance what you would like to see in this amazing country. There are enough sights here. All of them are connected with historical and cultural heritage. According to reviews, excursions in Cambodia are very diverse. Colorful and exotic country attracts many tourists for a reason.

One of the main attractions can be called the temple city of Angkor Wat, which was dedicated to the god Vishnu. It is believed that the temple is the largest religious building in the world. The complex is a complex three-level building with 5 towers and numerous passages and stairs. The temple is the soul of the people, because it represents the skill and art of the Khmers. The territory of the complex occupies about 400 square meters. It houses a number of temples. Angkor Thom is one of them. It was erected in the 11th century. Each of the temples is impressive and has its own characteristics.

The Royal Palace is no less interesting. Its golden roofs dazzle in the sun. The palace is still striking in its architecture. By the way, not all of his chambers are open to visitors, because the royal family still occupies part of the complex. But tourists can visit the throne room, the silver pagoda, the Royal Treasury. The palace was built in 1866. The place for construction was land in the old quarters of the city. Interestingly, even in our time, the king of Cambodia is considered the governor of the gods.

Many tourists will be interested in visiting the Angkor National Museum. It was opened in 2007. The museum uses modern technology to tell the story of the Khmer empire. An interesting fact is that the museum complex covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãabout 20 thousand square meters. Its exposition consists of eight thematic galleries.

Silver pagoda

Another attraction of Cambodia is the Silver Pagoda. It is also called the pagoda of the Emerald Buddha. In this amazing building, the floors are lined with real silver bars. And along all the walls are valuable gifts presented to the king from the heads of other eastern and European countries. The central part of the composition is a Buddha statue made of gold and decorated with diamonds. The Silver Pagoda Complex also includes a number of buildings, including the ceremonial pavilion and the tomb of the king.

Tourists reviews

What is a good seaside vacation in Cambodia? According to tourists, a beach holiday on the Cambodian coast will surely appeal to all lovers of exotic countries. The variegated exotic of the tropics is amazing right when you exit the plane. After a gray and dull winter at home, you find yourself in a real riot of colors. Of course, many will say that Cambodia is a poor country. And this is true, but there is another side, more attractive. The local population is very welcoming. Most people are Buddhists. Moreover, the population is very religious. Literally every house has its own Buddha statues.

If this is your first trip to the country, then in all seriousness choose a resort. Especially if you want to relax with your children. Do not take risks and choose wild beaches. Pay attention to proven and more equipped resorts. According to reviews, a holiday in Cambodia is very interesting. When choosing a good hotel you will get decent conditions at an affordable price. Tourists recommend paying attention to the three-star establishments of famous resorts or go to the islands. The choice is wide enough.

In general, according to tourists, a holiday in Cambodia is very good, because the sea is always warm here, and the climate is suitable for year-round swimming. A minor rainfall is not able to spoil the vacation. Experienced travelers recommend seeing the most important shrines and attractions of the country. Believe me, they are worth it.
