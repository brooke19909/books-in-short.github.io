Concert Hall of the Hotel St. Petersburg: Description and Address

Many modern hotel complexes offer their visitors not only the possibility of living with comfort, but also other popular services. It can be not only banquet rooms, but also venues for performances. The concert hall of the hotel "St. Petersburg" is well known to both spectators and artists. Many regularly perform here and take part in various competitions.

general information

The concert hall at the St. Petersburg Hotel is considered to be the largest in the institution. The convenient location of the stage allows all viewers to see well from their seats. For artists there are not only separate entrances, but also several make-up rooms. Various performances regularly take place on the stage. Excellent soundproofing of the premises allows concerts to be held right at the hotel. The adjacent small hall is a transformer, so it is easy to prepare it for a variety of events. The hotel also has other small rooms.

The concert hall seats about 796 people. Beautiful and modern design of the room is liked by many guests. It can be rented for conferences for at least 2 hours. On the stage of the hall is a large display that can broadcast video. Therefore, many shows or seminars are more spectacular.

The concert hall of the hotel "St. Petersburg" in the photo below.

The stage regularly becomes the venue for choreography competitions. In preparation for the performances, participants live in a hotel. The establishment has large and spacious halls and foyers, which are often used for various exhibitions. The location of the hotel is very good. From its windows a beautiful view of the Neva and the cruiser Aurora. Guests can also stroll along the Embankment, exploring the sights on the other side of the river.

Address

The concert hall of the hotel "St. Petersburg" is located on Pirogovskaya embankment, building - 5, building - 2. This is a very picturesque place that can be found quite easily. Visitors can focus on Pirogovskaya embankment and Sampsonievsky bridge.

How to get there

There are several ways to get to the famous hotel. The nearest metro station to the establishment is Ploshchad Lenina. You need to go a little from her. You can go down from the square and go straight along Pirogovskaya embankment to the required place. You can also come to the concert hall by other means of transport. Next to it is a stop called Hotel St. Petersburg. There is a 49 bus or a K-30 minibus. Tourists from other cities can take the rail. Trains arrive directly at Finland Station.
