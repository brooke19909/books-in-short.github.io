Simple ideas on how to design the first page of your personal diary

A personal diary is a collection of all secrets, notes and notes, a pleasant and loyal interlocutor, ready to always listen carefully to his friend.

Do you think that keeping a notebook or notepad with notes about yourself, about your thoughts and about the past day is the last century? You are wrong. A personal diary is not only a pocket psychologist, but also an excellent notebook for the implementation of his work. Such notes are gaining popularity again, turning from a simple notebook into a bright sketchbook with various drawings and clippings.

If you decide to start your own collection of thoughts, then you probably thought about how to arrange the first page of your personal diary. We have some interesting ideas, maybe you will find something for yourself.
