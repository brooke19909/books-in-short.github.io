General impression

Arriving in Bulgaria on Golden Sands, it is worthwhile to understand that this is although a popular, but very poor resort, and therefore you will not find a service similar to, for example, Turkish. Nevertheless, the hotel makes a rather positive impression, given the budgetary rates for accommodation. The cozy territory located in a park zone deserves special attention. Well, of course, one cannot fail to say about the friendly attitude of the staff with whom you will practically have no language barrier.

The nutritional impression is rather mixed. On the one hand, this hotel is one of the few in Bulgaria that operates on an all-inclusive basis. Nevertheless, the quality of the dishes leaves much to be desired. Therefore, experienced travelers recommend staying only at breakfasts included in the price, and you can have lunch and dinner in the numerous restaurants of the resort.

Given the low stardom, as well as the honorable age of the hotel, it is not surprising that it has a lot of flaws in the form of old furniture, centuries-old dust and cobwebs in hard-to-reach places and so on. Nevertheless, having arrived in Bulgaria, you should not spend days in a hotel looking for flaws. Given the convenient location of the resort, you can go on an excursion to neighboring settlements, as well as make budget shopping. If you do not like the beach located next to the hotel, after exploring the resort, you can find many beautiful and sparsely populated places with white sand and clear water.
