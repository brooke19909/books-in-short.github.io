Hotel "Paveletskaya Square", Moscow: address, description of rooms, reviews

Moscow is the largest cultural and business center not only in Russia, but throughout the world. Thousands of people come here every day for different purposes. And, of course, the capital offers them the opportunity to rent comfortable housing. There are many good hotels in Moscow. At the same time, the Paveletskaya Ploshchad hotel is considered one of the most convenient, offering guests of the capital rooms of several categories and convenient service.

Location Features

This hotel is located almost in the very center of Moscow - on the square of the same name. Literally a few steps away is the Paveletsky station. This means, first of all, that guests of the capital who have booked rooms in this hotel can get to it upon arrival very easily. The road to the hotel from the railway station takes no more than a couple of minutes.





From the airport to the hotel "Paveletskaya Square" can be reached by minibus or taxi. Also, many tourists come here simply by metro. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are several stations. This, many guests of the capital consider the main advantage of the hotel. The closest to this hotel is the Paveletskaya station.

Another convenient way to get to the place is to order a transfer. The Paveletskaya Ploshchad hotel provides this service to guests of the capital at an additional cost. The exact address of the hotel: Kozhevnicheskaya 3, building 1, Moscow, Russia.

general description

This hotel is a modern, beautifully decorated building. This hotel was built in Moscow not too long ago. Therefore, its decoration both outside and inside is quite new.

Not very convenient for many guests of the capital consider that respected in the hotel "Paveletskaya Square" is located on the second floor.

Of course, this can also be attributed to the disadvantages of the hotel. However, if desired, guests who decide to stay in Moscow in this particular hotel can order a service such as express check-in for a fee.

Smoking is not allowed in the rooms of the Paveletskaya Square Hotel. Also, pets are not accepted. The Paveletskaya Ploshchad hotel belongs to the three-star group. At the same time, its infrastructure, as many believe, is quite consistent with this class. The owner of the hotel is MosInvestHotel LLC.

Number of rooms

If desired, guests of the capital can rent one of 77 comfortable rooms in the Paveletskaya Square mini-hotel. The hotel offers tourists, business travelers and students rooms of various categories. The most popular among Moscow guests in this hotel are budget rooms of the Standard class. If desired, you can remove a single, Twin or Superior. Equipped with standard rooms of all varieties in the hotel almost the same. The difference lies only in their area. Singles are designed for one guest, Suprior - family apartments. Standart Twin - a room designed to accommodate two people. There are two single beds.





In addition to the standard rooms, the 2-room deluxe rooms rented at this hotel are also very popular with tourists. These apartments are just great for guests who come to Moscow with their whole family.

The rooms rented at the Paveletskaya Ploshchad Hotel, in addition to beds and bedside tables, include:

  • flat screen tv;

  • air conditioning;

  • kettle.

The television in this hotel is satellite. Tourists who have booked hotel rooms need not take second shoes with them to Moscow. This hotel provides slippers for all rooms.

Among the amenities provided at the hotel, it is also possible to note the presence of an individual shower room in each rented room. Here, tourists have the opportunity to use for free not only a set of household chemicals for bathing, but also a hairdryer.

In addition to standard equipment, the rooms of the Paveletskaya Square Hotel also have minibars. Judging by the reviews of the guests, they are equipped quite well. However, hotel residents can use them only for a fee. Many guests order a minibar at this hotel. It costs in a hotel not too expensive.

Paveletskaya Square Hotel: room reviews

Most of the guests of the capital who have ever stayed at this hotel consider the rooms rented in it to be quite comfortable and well equipped. Many tourists praise the decoration of the rooms in the hotel. Repair here was carried out recently and really high quality. The interiors of the rooms handed in at the hotel are decorated in bright colors, which is very popular with many guests.





The windows in the hotel rooms are plastic. Many also praise the Paveletskaya Ploshchad Hotel (Moscow) for this. Almost no noise from the street penetrates the rooms of this hotel. The soundproofing of the walls in the rooms, judging by the reviews of vacationers, is not too good. Noises coming from the corridors of the hotel, in some cases, can disturb residents.

Maids clean the rooms quite often. In this regard, the hotel "Paveletskaya Square" also earned relatively good reviews. The floors are mopped, dusted and maids take out the rubbish once every five days. Bed linen and towels are changed at the same frequency in the hotel.

Infrastructure

Of course, various services are provided for the guests of this hotel, and actually on its territory. Tourists living on the upper floors can take elevators to their rooms. Last work in the hotel without fail. The hotel residents consider the inconvenience only that the elevator entrances are located between floors. That is, the hotel guests still have to climb or go down the stairs.

The hotel also provides parking for the convenience of its residents. You can leave your car here for free. True, the hotel parking area is not too large. Only two cars fit here. But in most cases, guests of this hotel do not rent cars in the capital. Paveletsky station and metro stations are, as already mentioned, in the immediate vicinity of this hotel. Thus, even the available parking area for hotel residents is usually quite enough.

Paveletskaya Square Hotel features free WiFi throughout the property. Reviews about him are positive. According to many guests, the Internet works in the hotel well.





Also for the convenience of guests at the hotel are equipped with:

  • laundry;

  • business center;

  • luggage room.

There is a conference room in the hotel.





Additional services

In addition to the standard set of amenities, the Paveletskaya Ploshchad hotel provides tourists with services such as:

  • flower delivery;

  • assistance in organizing excursions;

  • seminars and conferences;

  • organization of weddings, family celebrations and corporate parties.

The Paveletskaya station of the Moscow Metro, as already mentioned, is located next to this hotel. So hotel guests have the opportunity to quickly and easily get to almost anywhere in the capital. But if necessary, in this hotel you can, among other things, order courier delivery of documents, things, etc.

Dining at the hotel

Rooms of all categories are rented in the Paveletskaya Ploshchad hotel with breakfast. There is a restaurant on site. Food is served buffet style. If desired, guests can order breakfast in the room. Reviews about the food in this hotel on the network are mostly only positive. Most of the former residents of the hotel believe that the food here is very good. The dishes are served tasty and fresh, in a heated form.

In total, the hotel restaurant room accommodates up to 30 people at a time. If desired, it can be removed including for banquets.





Tariffs

Reviews of the prices for rental rooms in this hotel are mixed. Some believe that the hotel is quite cheap. Other Moscow guests believe that the prices here are higher than the services provided. In any case, guests of the capital have the opportunity to choose one of the most convenient rates for themselves at this hotel. Separate price lists at the Paveletskaya Square Hotel are provided for tourists who want to rent a room for a period of less than 4 hours, at night, for several days, etc. For some categories of guests, the hotel provides discounts on accommodation.

Average room price

On average, the cost of renting rooms in this hotel is about 3,500-6,000 thousand rubles per day. When renting comfort or deluxe rooms, the hotel provides a free airport shuttle service. You can book rooms at the Paveletskaya Square hotel through a special form on the official site of the MosInvestOtel company. If you wish, you can also just call the Paveletskaya Square Hotel. Phone of this hotel: +7 (495) 255-15-55.
