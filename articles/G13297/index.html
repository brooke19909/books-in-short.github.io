Orchids Resort Phuket in Karon (Thailand): hotel description, reviews

Karon Beach on Thailand's largest island of Phuket is famous all over the world. There is pure white sand, a wide coastal strip, a gentle slope into the sea, and for those who like to snorkel, they have their own coral reef. The beach is so popular among tourists that many come here daily from Patong (the main resort of the island). And the price of 400 Thai baht per tuk-tuk one way does not scare anyone away. The only drawback of the beach is that all hotels are across the highway. But do not be upset: the safety of tourists is monitored by special hotel traffic controllers. Hotels in Karon Beach cannot be called cheap. It is believed that for such a beach near you need to pay. But there are also hotels suitable for budget tourists who come to Thailand . Territory

Phuket Orchid Resort 4 * (Phuket, Karon) carried out the last reconstruction in 2013. Now he can carry four stars with honor and add the word ‚ÄúSPA‚Äù to his name, since the hotel's range of services includes the use of water wellness treatments. The territory of the hotel complex is large. This is a real tropical garden where bougainvilleas bloom at every step. The design of the territory is sustained in the aesthetics of Ayutthaya - the ancient capital of Thailand. Bridges are thrown across three pools, and one of them is decorated with a large stone head of a Hindu deity. The hotel complex consists of six four-story buildings. The rooms overlook the mountains or the greenery of the garden. Reviews of tourists recommend booking rooms in the fourth or sixth building. As for the number of storeys, opinions were divided. Many people like living on the first level, as these rooms have terraces rather than balconies. But other reviews claim that the upper floors offer amazing views and less noise.





Rooms

Guest rooms at Orchids Resort Phuket are categorized into Superior, Family, and Deluxe. The latest rooms have individual access to the pool from the terrace, which has rattan chairs and a small table. The Family Room is one spacious room with two double beds. Each room has an electric kettle, and in the Deluxe there is also a coffee maker with a set for making drinks yourself. As befits a hotel of this class, the rooms are equipped with manual air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a hairdryer, and daily updated hygiene items. Wi-Fi is available for a fee, just like a safe.





Nutrition

Like most hotels in Thailand and Phuket in particular, Orchids Resort Phuket only provides breakfast for its guests. This does not mean that in order to have lunch and dinner, you need to leave the hotel. It offers guests the restaurant "Le Bayonne" (which is served in the menu or in the format of the "buffet") and the pizzeria "Il Ponte". Near one of the pools there is a bar with drinks and snacks. At lunchtime, they serve plain hot dishes such as french fries and burgers. As for breakfast, the hotel guests were satisfied. In addition to the usual set (scrambled eggs, scrambled eggs, sausages, yoghurts, juice, sweet pastries, fruits), there are always meat and fish dishes and, which surprised many tourists from Europe, soup. There is no shortage of cafes on Karon Beach. Many tourists bought food from the macaques. Reviews recommend this cheap and tasty type of fast food. The dish is prepared with fresh products.





Pools and beach

Phuket Orchid Resort in Karon is central. All infrastructure on the beach is paid. So reviews are advised to stock up on a rug and an umbrella. But, however, finding the shadow on Karon is not difficult. Exotic tamarind trees grow right on the beach. They cast an openwork shadow in which it is good to relax from the burning Thai sun. The sea, like the beach, is very clean. But lovers of wildlife should walk about twenty minutes to the south - there a coral reef approaches close to the shore. Pools at the hotel tourists praise. There are always enough sunbeds and umbrellas. The inhabitants of "Deluxe" did not feel the need to go to the sea at all - the pool splashed near their terrace.

Conditions for children

Orchids Resort Phuket is positioning itself as a family vacation destination. Therefore, much attention is paid to young guests. They have a children's pool with several water slides and a playground. Upon request, a baby cot will be provided in your room. And if parents want to devote time to shopping, a long excursion or go to a restaurant in the evening, you can hire a nanny at the hotel. For breakfast, the hotel serves dishes that are quite suitable for a children's tender stomach. There are always salads, fruits, pastries, yogurts, sausages, fresh milk. One of the satellite channels on TV shows Russian-language cartoons, which the children really like.
