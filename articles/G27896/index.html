Club "Honey" in Rostov-on-Don: photos and reviews

Every year, the popularity and attendance of the Honey Club in Rostov-on-Don is gaining momentum. This is due to the fact that this entertainment institution keeps pace with the times and meets all the requirements of modern youth.

Description

Here the most relevant music plays under the arrangement of famous DJs, delicious cocktails are sold. The rather large dance floor of the "Honey" club in Rostov-on-Don will not leave indifferent. Address of the institution: st. Krasnoarmeyskaya, 157. Opening hours: from 21:00 to 6:00 daily.





In addition to themed parties held on different days of the week, interesting projects are being implemented here (for example, Apricot, Fashion Night, Olympics-80, Insomnia, etc.), as well as events dedicated to the holidays (for example, City Day, Freshman Day, Halloween, etc.).

A pleasant moment for frequent visitors is getting club (for men) and beauty cards (for girls), which give the right to enter the club freely, as well as discounts on the purchase of drinks and snacks in the bar.

Not only young people of student age come here, but also the public of 30-40 and even 50 years old. Often on the second floor of the institution corporate parties are held, birthday parties, graduations are celebrated. At the same time, the club can accommodate about 600 visitors.

The interior of the institution, zoning

The photo of the club "Honey" in Rostov-on-Don shows that it is made in an ultra-modern style, pink and black tones prevail in the interior. Pink is a symbol of glamor, and black represents the kingdom of nightlife. The walls of the institution are originally decorated with a facing brick. In combination with a large number of spotlights and neon bulbs, an unforgettable unique atmosphere of celebration and fun is obtained.





The first floor of the Honey club in Rostov-on-Don includes a rather long bar counter with high comfortable chairs and tables, where you can eat and drink soft or alcoholic drinks. Next comes the dance area - a spacious and roomy dance floor with an abundance of spotlights, light and shine. There is also a large LCD-screen, equipped with a stage, a place for a DJ and musical equipment. A luminous wide staircase leads to the second floor, where there are soft sofas, tables and VIP-zones, private rooms. Here you can sit in a more relaxed atmosphere, order a hookah. Everything that happens on stage is visible from the upper balconies.
