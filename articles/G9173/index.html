Hai Au Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Hai Au Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) will give you a complete picture of the culture of Vietnam, as well as pleasantly surprise you with local traditions of hospitality.





Hotel Overview

Resort Hai Au Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) is a cozy and welcoming establishment, which, despite the lack of a large number of stars, gives its guests a comfortable and unforgettable vacation. And thanks to the work of the tour desk, you can explore all the most interesting sights both in the vicinity of the hotel and in remote areas.

It is especially worth noting the guest rooms of the Hai Au Hotel 3 * hotel (Nha Trang). Of course, you can‚Äôt talk about ultra-modern equipment and new furniture, but you are provided with a sufficient level of comfort and a rather pleasant interior. In addition, it is interesting that the design of the apartments is in accordance with local traditions.

Guests at Hai Au Hotel 3 * Resort (Nha Trang) each morning can enjoy a buffet breakfast. The rest of the time, this institution works on the menu and offers you to try real masterpieces of Asian cuisine, as well as those unique dishes that are typical only for this area.





Resort Location

Hai Au Hotel 3 * Resort (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is located on the outskirts of the city, very close to the public beach. It takes only three minutes to get to the Kua Dai coast. Going a little further, you will find yourself in the old historical part of the city, where until today many sights have survived, indicating important events in the life of the city and the country.





Rooms for hotel guests

It can be said that laconic modesty and pretentiousness of Vietnamese traditions are combined in the interior of the rooms of the Hai Au Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang). Photos taken by tourists demonstrate the neatness of the design of the apartments. Since the hotel is small enough, there are only 32 rooms in it. Guests are offered a choice of the following apartment categories:

  • "Standard" is a small bedroom where guests can stay in single beds or in a large double.
  • "Family" - is a large apartment, which is a huge room, divided into several sleeping areas.
  • "Deluxe" is an improved category of rooms, which differs not only in a large area, but also in an elegant interior.
  • Superior offers you a huge room, which, due to the interior design, is conditionally divided into a bedroom and a small living room area.




What is provided in the rooms

Hotel Hai Au Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) offers its guests to use the following amenities that are provided in each of the rooms:

  • depending on the category of the room, the bathroom of your apartment will be equipped with a shower or bath;
  • You can enjoy watching dozens of satellite channels, among which mainly entertainment and music programs (there is one Russian news resource);
  • a hairdryer is provided in the bathroom, as well as a set of bath and hygiene items;
  • you can adjust the temperature in the room with the help of an individual air conditioner, which works both for heating and cooling;
  • You can use the wireless Internet for free, the signal of which applies to all apartments;
  • Each room has an ironing board and an iron so that you can tidy up clothes that are wrinkled on the go without assistance.




Hotel facilities

The hotel offers the following infrastructure facilities:

  • one of the best restaurants in the city where you can try specific local cuisine, as well as a wide range of international dishes;
  • at the reception there is a safe in which you can leave documents, cash and valuables when leaving the hotel;
  • excess baggage and large suitcases can be taken to the left-luggage office for the entire period of stay at the hotel;
  • if a solemn event or business negotiations are planned during your stay at the hotel, then you will be provided with a special room with all the necessary equipment and with the appropriate design;
  • you have the opportunity to rent a car or bicycle, which you can leave absolutely free in a private parking lot;
  • The laundry will help to cope with stains on your clothes.




Positive reviews

Hai Au Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) managed to fall in love with many tourists and travelers. The reviews are evidence that the living conditions are quite decent, and the service is at a fairly high level. The following comments can confirm this:

  • the sea is relatively close, walk to it for about 10 minutes;
  • quite tasty and substantial menu for breakfast;
  • towels are replaced daily in the bathroom;
  • budget rates for accommodation and related services;
  • in the immediate vicinity of the resort there is a huge hypermarket where, at fairly reasonable prices, you can buy groceries, as well as household chemicals and other necessary things;
  • in rooms with separate beds, the beds are one and a half, which is quite convenient;
  • there are very thin walls between the rooms, so there is always a strong audibility (especially in the bathroom);
  • all plumbing, as well as the electronics in the room are fully operational and work without interruptions.

Negative reviews

Guests note a number of negative moments that accompany holidays in the Hai Au Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang). Reviews of tourists contain the following significant comments and complaints about the hotel:

  • very old furniture, which at any contact with it makes a terrible and loud creak;
  • there is a lot of mold in the bathroom both on the tile and on the plumbing;
  • a very small pool in which it is impossible to fit everyone;
  • very expensive excursions to the tourist office at the hotel (in the Russian cultural center, which is nearby, prices for similar services are 2 times lower);
  • the whole process of cleaning the rooms takes no more than 10 minutes, during which the floors will not be washed and dust will not be wiped out (maximum - they will make the bed);
  • tablecloths in the restaurant in a huge number of spots (it would be better if tables were not covered with anything at all);
  • since the financing of the hotel is partially made by the state, locals have free breakfast here, so you need to come to the restaurant around 6 in the morning, otherwise you just won‚Äôt get food;
  • Despite the fact that towels are regularly changed, stains often occur on them;
  • the pool is very dirty, the water in it is icy, and the pair of sun loungers that stand around it are broken and completely covered with mold;
  • beach towels are not issued (you need to bring along or take those that are provided in the room);
  • in the morning the hotel is very noisy because it stands above a busy highway.

The choice is yours.
