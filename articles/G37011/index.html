Turkey: "Turan Prince World", 5 stars

Every year, crowds of tourists rush to relax on the Mediterranean coast. Their choice is Turkey. "Turan Prince World" is a territory of unforgettable relaxation, a luxurious 5 * hotel located in the heart of Antalya. Literally 15 km from the hotel is the old town of Side, where monuments of ancient culture are well preserved.



This hotel for a quiet and relaxing holiday, the main contingent of it is people of age, as well as couples who decide that Turkey is the best place to relax with children. "Turan Prince" is updated every year, changes in design and improved service delight even the most demanding guests. Many tourists, having once visited this beautiful hotel, come back here again and again.

Hotel (Turkey) "Turan Prince", owns a sand and pebble beach 200 meters long. The beach is equipped with many sun loungers and wide awnings. All this, including towels and mattresses, is provided free of charge.

What offers guests "Turan Prince"

The hotel area is huge and includes the main outdoor and indoor restaurant. There are three a la carte restaurants: fish, Chinese and Mexican, which will surely appeal to spicy food supporters. Before heading to a la carte, you must first reserve a table. Each tourist can visit all a la carte once for free. Also on site there are 10 bars offering light snacks and soft drinks, 3 swimming pools (outdoor, indoor and children), an amphitheater, a cinema, 4 tennis courts, a large conference room for 350 people, an aqua park and an amusement park, which in the evening time is becoming crowded.





Number of rooms

The hotel has 678 apartments of various categories:

  • 62 cozy Annex Rooms of 16 m2, maximum occupancy - 2 people;
  • 332 Club Standart on 24-27-28 m2, with the possibility of settlement up to 4 people;
  • 94 Standart Room of 29 m2, located in the main building, maximum occupancy - 4 people;
  • 74 Club Duplex of 40 m2, two-level, check-in - maximum 4 people;
  • 47 Hotel Duplex, also duplex;
  • Select Villa include 11 Select Standart, 25 Select Duplex, 11 Select Villa with one room and 11 Select Villa with two rooms. Each room at Select Villa has a private pool, a restaurant and relaxation area.

Services of the hotel "Turan Prince"

Telephone, fax, massage and motor sports are the services that the Turan Prince Hotel, Turkey offers for an additional fee. Prices should be clarified upon check-in, in order to avoid further misunderstanding.

Free facilities include a gym, jacuzzi, Turkish bath, sauna, mini-golf, soccer, beach volleyball, surfing, table tennis, amusement park, gymnastics, aerobics and a disco.
