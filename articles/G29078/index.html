Hotel Jaz Aquamarine Resort 5 *, Hurghada, Egypt: review, description, features and reviews

If you plan to spend a vacation in Egypt on the Red Sea and are looking for a comfortable hotel with excellent service, working on an all-inclusive system, then as a suitable option we suggest considering Jaz Aquamarine Resort 5 * (Hurghada).



Location

This hotel complex is located in the southern part of Hurghada, on the first line. The nearest airport is 13 kilometers away. The hotel is 16 kilometers from the city center.

General information, photo

The Jazz Aquamarine Resort Hotel in Hurghada (Egypt) is part of the Jazz Hotels, Resorts & Cruises chain. His housing stock includes 1001 standard, superior, family and deluxe family, executive and suite categories. The apartments are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. So, in all apartments there is comfortable furniture, a balcony or terrace, a bathroom, a hairdryer, a TV, air conditioning, a telephone, a safe, a minibar. Towels are cleaned and changed every day.

Housing stock

Judging by the reviews left by our compatriots, basically everyone was satisfied with the rooms of the Jaz Aquamarine Resort 5 * hotel. So, according to the guests, the apartments here are very spacious, stylishly decorated, with a fresh renovation. All furniture and appliances in the rooms are modern, in good condition. Tourists also liked the spacious furnished balconies and terraces with a table and chairs where you can comfortably accommodate. Travelers also note large comfortable beds on which they managed to get enough sleep. Also, hotel guests were satisfied and soundproofed. Even those guests whose windows overlooked the pool did not complain about the noise when the balcony door was closed.



Cleaning

As for the work of the maids, then, according to some guests, it has its own characteristics. So, according to them, in most cases, housekeeping is regular, but very mediocre. If you want the maids to be more responsible in fulfilling their duties, then leave them a tip. In this case, as tourists say, your room will always shine with cleanliness. As for the change of towels, it is produced every day. Underwear is updated several times a week. Also, as expenditures, maids replenish supplies of bath accessories, drinking bottled water and the contents of the minibar.

the Internet

Jaz Aquamarine Resort 5 * (Hurghada, Egypt), reviews of which we are reviewing, like most other hotels in this country, cannot offer its guests free Wi-Fi in the rooms. According to the guests of the "Jazz Aquamarine Resort" wireless Internet in the hotel can only be used in the lobby near the reception. Territory, location

Many guests of the "Jazz Aquamarine" with pleasure several times went for a walk in Hurghada. You can get to the city center from the hotel by taxi. Such a trip is worth, according to our compatriots, quite inexpensive. For the rest, tourists are not recommended to leave the hotel, however, guests do not particularly complain about this fact, since there are a lot of entertainments on its territory.

So, about twenty pools are equipped here, half of which are equipped with a water heating system, which is very important for winter time. Near each pool there is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers and parasols. Also on the territory of the hotel there are several bars where you can always treat yourself to alcoholic or soft drinks.

Tourists also note the presence of a mass of pretty places where you can take a walk and take beautiful photos as a keepsake. In general, the territory of the hotel, according to our compatriots, is very large, green, well-planned and well-groomed.
