Explanatory note to the balance sheet and its role in reporting

The explanatory note to the balance sheet is an obligatory part of the financial statements. This is regulated by paragraph 5 of the Accounting Regulation No. 4/99 ‚ÄúOrganization's financial statements‚Äù. This document discloses information about the accounting policies of the enterprise, necessary for the full analysis of all indicators of economic activity.



An explanatory note to the balance sheet may be absent only at small enterprises or in socially oriented non-profit organizations, for which audits are not required.

The explanatory note to the balance sheet includes information about the organization: full name of the legal entity, legal form, management structure, actual and legal address, size of the authorized capital, information about the founders of the company and average annual number of employees. All these data are indicated at the very beginning, and followed by an item on accounting policies. This is a very important section, because without it this note is incomplete and does not correspond to the required format. It discloses accounting rules. If they were changed in the reporting period, then all changes that have occurred and the reasons for their occurrence are indicated.





The third part contains all the information about fixed assets, stocks of the enterprise, as well as data on financial investments and loans. If the company has funds denominated in foreign currency, they are also indicated in this section. The fourth paragraph of the explanatory note includes analysis and assessment of the balance sheet structure: liquidity ratio, solvency, profitability, etc. Next is information disclosing all sources of income and expenses of the organization, including barter transactions.





According to the information of the Ministry of Finance of Russia No. -10/2012, an explanatory note to the balance sheet can be drawn up in a free form, since it is not an element of strict accounting statements and is intended for company owners. A sample explanatory note to the balance sheet can be found in the official appendices to the circular letters of the Ministry of Finance. At the same time, do not confuse the note with the official explanation of the balance sheet, which is carried out in a tabular form, determined independently by the organization. The task of the explanations is to give a breakdown of all lines of the financial statements.
