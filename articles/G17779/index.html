Abkhazia, Ldzaa: recreation and attractions

One of the most popular holiday destinations of our compatriots is Abkhazia. Ldzaa is a small resort-type village most visited by tourists. Picturesque places, cozy beaches, low cost of living - all this makes it very popular.

Resort Feature

The village of Ldzaa was located (in Soviet times it bore the name of Lizawa) in the bay of Cape Pitsunda. This picturesque place is surrounded by relict pines. Today, this village is very attractive for tourists. The freshness of the air, beaches of sand, the presence of centuries-old attractions attract guests from many countries. It can be families with children, lovers or a group of friends. Important prices for accommodation, which are famous for Abkhazia, are important in choosing a place for recreation. Ldzaa is ready to give tourists a measured, relaxing holiday. This is due to the fact that the patency of tourists here is several times less than in larger tourist cities.

Pitsunda Reserve is famous for its special kind of pine, striking in its uniqueness. It is on this territory that the village of Ldzaa (Abkhazia) is conveniently located. Pine trees growing throughout the cape have created a unique microclimate. Tourists will remember the sea air for its crystal clearness for a long time. And coniferous forests saturated the air with a healing aroma. The beach is completely surrounded by trees, which creates incredible natural beauty.





Where to stay in Ldzaa (Abkhazia)

The private sector is the best place to stay. Among tourists, Lizawa is better known as the Fish Factory. In the village, this is the name of the small microdistrict. The district received this name in honor of the main street, which bears a similar name. Every tourist knows that relaxing in the Fish Factory is cheap and comfortable.

Ldzaa is an ideal place to relax. This can be explained by the lack of industrial enterprises in the village. The local population lives mainly through the organization of tourist vacations. In order to attract as many vacationers as possible, the prices for accommodation and entertainment here are several times lower than in other resorts located in the neighborhood. I would like to note that for residents of a modern metropolis who want to hide from civilization, Ldzaa (Abkhazia) is ideal.

The private sector is the best place for tourists here. More vacationers decide to stay in one of the houses located on the main streets of Lizawa. It is almost impossible to find a house in which there would be no room for people who came to rest.





Beach in the village

It is worth telling in more detail about the sea, which Abkhazia is famous for. Ldzaa is located in the bay. Due to the fact that there is no open water, the arrival of large waves and cold air flows is not necessary to observe. All this determines the purity, warmth of the sea.

The beach stretched along the coast for four kilometers. On the coast you can find several separately isolated beach sites. All of them are spacious enough, this allows you to avoid the "crush" among vacationers. The surface of the beaches is covered with sand and pebbles. Each tourist can choose his favorite beach. But, as long-term practice shows, vacationers most often prefer to relax in the sand. This is understandable, because it is much more pleasant to walk on it with bare feet.





Entertainment in the village

However, not only in private homes, you can comfortably spend your vacation. Today hotels are located conveniently in the village . They, as a rule, attract guests with a wide variety of additional services provided. Therefore, each visitor will be able to choose a place for housing, which will meet all his requirements.

Tourism business in the territory under consideration is quite developed. A large number of recreational attractions are located on each beach of Ldzaa. It can be both land and water. Numerous cafes, restaurants, bars will allow you to have fun in the evening in a pleasant company. Souvenir shops are located on every corner.

Outbound fishing is very popular. You can go fishing for a river in the sea. For lovers of relaxation, active cycling and horse riding are provided. It is such an abundance of entertainment that Abkhazia is famous for.

Ldsaa Attractions

There are many beautiful places and cultural and historical monuments. Each of them can be visited on their own or as part of a hiking trip to places that are famous for Abkhazia. Pitsunda, Ldzaa and other surrounding villages have a convenient location. From them you can very quickly get to many historical monuments.





  • The most popular cultural site is Ldzaa-nyha. This place is part of the seven holy places of Abkhazia. Ethnic Abkhazians, Muslims, Christians, have been worshiping this place for centuries.
  • Not to visit groves of relict pines is simply impossible. Some trees have been around for about two hundred years.
  • Also, you should definitely visit the relict water area. One of these is Lake Inkit, which for hundreds of years has retained its original appearance and enchanting beauty.
  • Four kilometers from Lzzaa is the Church of St. Andrew the First-Called, which was built in the tenth century.
  • The Great Pitiunt Historical Reserve contains the remains of ancient settlements of the Romans and Greeks.
