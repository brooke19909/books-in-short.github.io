What knitting needles to use for knitting?

If you decide to knit a cardigan by hand, then remember the right choice of knitting needles. According to the opinion of professional knitters, who have been practicing for many years and drawing up schemes describing a knitted female cardigan, their thickness should be 1.5-2 times greater than the thickness of the yarn. As a rule, they recommend using circular aluminum or steel knitting needles No. 3, 4 or 5 to create a stylish thing. Bamboo can also be taken, but you should be prepared for the fact that they can easily bend or break.

How to care for a knitted cardigan?

Now you know how to knit a female cardigan with knitting needles. Schemes and description will help to do this. It remains the case for small - just get to work and complete it. If you already managed to do this, do not forget to put things in order. Immediately after stitching all the parts, it is necessary to wash the cardigan by hand with ordinary soap or a few drops of shampoo, and then rinse in warm water. So that the cardigan does not immediately fall off and fade, it is important to ensure that the temperature of the liquid is the same throughout the procedure.

After washing, the cardigan, no matter what yarn it is made of, cannot be twisted. It must be laid out on a thick sheet or towel, carefully folded into a ‚Äúsausage‚Äù. Then unfold and leave to dry completely, away from the battery. You can not hang on a rope, since the product can be deformed under its own weight. After the cardigan has dried, it is worth ironing. It is better to do this from the wrong side, through wet gauze or a sheet, at an iron temperature of 110 degrees.
