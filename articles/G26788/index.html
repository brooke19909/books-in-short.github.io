Zvenigorod hotels: addresses and reviews

Zvenigorod is a settlement in the Moscow region, which bears the title "City of Military Glory." It is 30 km from the capital on the banks of the Moscow River.

Zvenigorod and its environs are a popular resort and boarding region. Among tourists, he received the name of Russian Switzerland. If you want to relax in nature away from a noisy and dusty metropolis, Zvenigorod hotels are exactly what you need.

Hotel "Iskra"

One of the most popular holiday destinations in the vicinity of Zvenigorod is the Iskra Hotel. It is located in the Odintsovo district, the village of Svatovo, 6. For guests, the following accommodation options are provided:

  • One-room standard - from 3900 rubles per day.
  • One-room improved standard - from 4100 rubles.
  • One-room junior suite - from 5300 rubles.
  • Two-room junior suite - from 6200 rubles.

For a comfortable stay and relaxation, the following services are provided:

  • Restaurant for 200 people. Buffet service is available here, as well as original dishes from the chef.

  • Lobby bar with a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as snacks.

  • A karaoke bar for 40 people where you can spend time in the company of friends.

  • Indoor swimming pool 25 meters long with four lanes.

  • Billiards.

  • Gym and gymnasiums.

  • Sports fields for volleyball, basketball, football and tennis.

  • Ping pong.

  • Spa area with Finnish sauna, phytobarrel, massage, aromatherapy and beauty parlor.

  • Sports equipment rental.

  • Seven playgrounds outdoors.

  • Organization of business, ceremonial and corporate events.

  • Organization of sports camps and competitions.

  • Possibility of rest with pets up to 5 kg.

Reviews

About this hotel in Zvenigorod you can hear the following positive information:

  • excellent work of a massage therapist;
  • beautiful, clean and well-groomed area;
  • Friendly and well-trained staff;
  • spacious rooms;
  • great pool;
  • good animation program for children;
  • Bike rental is available.

And such negative ones:

  • there are no hairdryers in the rooms, they can be taken at the reception (there are only 5 devices for the entire hotel, they must be booked in advance);
  • poor nutrition;
  • The rooms are not equipped with bathrobes and slippers.

Hotel "Heliopark Thalasso"

On the street Lermontov, 1 is the hotel "Thalasso" in Zvenigorod. For guests, the following features are provided:

  • Accommodation in comfortable modern rooms of varying degrees of comfort.
  • Organization of excursions to local attractions and picturesque surroundings.
  • Rental of barbecue pavilions, each of which is designed for 9 people.
  • Paintball and laser tag - a game similar to paintball, which uses rifles with laser beams.
  • Russian and American billiards.
  • Fishing on the shore of the pond. The restaurant staff will kindly prepare your catch.
  • Horseback riding lessons and horseback riding.
  • Rope park, which implies a route at an altitude of 3 to 6 meters above the ground.
  • Creative workshops for children and adults (for groups up to 10 people).
  • Sports equipment rental.
  • Animation program.
  • Spa center with a salt room, massage room, alpha capsule, hairdresser, beauty parlor, solarium and gym.

Hotel Taiga Dachi

For a secluded holiday in the vicinity of Zvenigorod, the Taiga Dachi hotel is ideally suited. The institution is located in the village of the rest house "Svyazist" on Ratikhinsky highway. Guests have the following leisure options:

  • Accommodation in hotel rooms (from 2000 rubles per day), apartments (from 7200 rubles per day) and cottages (from 28000 rubles per day per house).
  • Organization and holding of weddings and other celebrations (field registration, banquet, outdoor festivals and so on).
  • Organization and holding of corporate events.
  • Meals in a cozy restaurant with a fireplace and massive wooden furniture.
  • Indoor pool with cedar saunas.
  • Several types of baths (herbal, cedar, pine, black, linden).
  • Several thematic museums.
  • Culinary and creative workshops.
  • Organization of excursions to attractions and picturesque surroundings.
  • Active pastime.
