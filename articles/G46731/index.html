Naturally, this evaluation criterion applies not only to the plant or country of manufacture, but also to the availability of various additional functions, or as they are now commonly called gadgets. And these gadgets can be a huge number, ranging from simple organizers, which were equipped with the first mobile phones and ending with various online maps and navigators. According to many experts, the best phone in the world is a phone that always rings and never fails its owner. That is, the phone should remain, first of all, the phone, and only then the camera, video camera, memory card, game console, navigator, etc.

In addition to the fact that the phone must be reliable, it must also have a sufficiently powerful battery so that you can always use your pet. The best phone always leaves only the best memories of it. Remember the first mobile phones that you had. Many of you will smile, remembering black and white screens and primitive polyphony, but, nevertheless, at that time there were always leaders among them. Such leaders include the world-famous company Nokia, which has been rightfully considered the world leader in the production of high-quality and reliable phones for several years.
