How to get directions to Navitel? Routing: tips, reviews

The cost of portable devices that can interact with the signals of navigation satellites has decreased so much that even schoolchildren have gadgets with GPS (Global Positioning System) . It is not surprising that their owners want to know how to properly use the capabilities incorporated into the device.

Luxury or necessity

There is still an opinion that a navigator is necessary, first of all, for those people who, by the nature of their work, are forced to drive a car.

Taxi drivers, truckers, private carriers - all of them without satellite navigation are quite difficult to navigate in the streets, roads and cities. Of course, you can expand the paper sheet card and look for the addressee. But if you have access to a modern navigation device and know how to get directions to Navitel, you can solve this problem much faster. Pedestrians also often have to resort to GPS. For example, sometimes only the exact address is known, but not its location on the city map. In this case, knowing how to set a route to Navitel, you can easily pave the most optimal way. This opportunity is indispensable for those people who first visited this village.

Software

So, the Navitel navigator is bought . How to get directions using it? First you need to do the preparation. For the navigation program to work, it must have access to maps of the country or area where the route is planned. If various Internet services of a similar orientation (Google Maps) constantly load the necessary data from the Web, then Navitel, on the contrary, uses the appropriate files from the device‚Äôs flash drive.

The advantage of this approach is obvious - complete freedom from Internet providers while traveling. If you are in another country or in an area where there is no access to the Network, this is a decisive factor in favor of Navitel.

There are several ways to get maps for GPS navigators. The easiest way is to purchase a device where they are already preinstalled. This is a fairly common practice and, admittedly, effective. Having bought such a solution, you can be sure that everything will work without failures and various incompatibilities. You can also purchase the licensed Navitel program and a map of Russia directly on the developer's website for just 800 r.

The next option is to download the appropriate files from the Internet. The source can be both the developer's site and third-party resources. Nm7 files (new version) must be placed in NavitelContent / Maps.

Satellite Tracking Activation

It is not enough to download maps and run the program. The device must be connected to navigation satellites. In most highly specialized navigators, the GPS function is always activated. But communicators require the inclusion of this feature. In Android systems, you need to lower the curtain and click on the satellite image - GPS.

Satellites

After the initialization of the navigation program, a dialog box will be displayed allowing the user to decide whether to use the Internet service about the current state of traffic jams. This is a very useful feature, but it requires a network connection, which is not always possible.

If everything is done correctly, an image of a map with roads and objects will appear on the screen. Also a prerequisite for the operation of the navigator is the "capture" of satellites. In the upper bar of the menu of the program "Navitel" there is an image of a satellite dish. If it is red, it means that the device has no communication with the satellite or the function is disabled. The yellow color indicates that the signal is caught, but for some reason it is impossible to work with it. And finally, the green color of the image is a signal that everything is fine and then you can figure out how to set a route to Navitel. Cloudy skies, the roof of a building or car, poor location - all these are the reasons that satellites and the device will not be able to communicate.

Starting point determination

After the satellite signal is registered, data is matched and a dot appears on the displayed map - the current position of the device. Depending on a number of factors, an error of several tens of meters is possible. From this moment, you can begin to pave the desired path. Since this stage is very important, it is recommended to provide the device with reliable communication with the satellite, at least for the first time leaving the shelter (roof), or to install it near the window itself.
