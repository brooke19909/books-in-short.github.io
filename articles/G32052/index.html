Recreation facilities in the Tver region: "Fish place", "Beach of dreams", "Sterzh". Camping, fishing

Choosing a place to relax or conduct a business meeting in nature, it is most advisable to opt for a recreation center. In the Tver region there are many different recreation facilities. In them, everyone will find entertainment to their taste. Moreover, recreation facilities in the Tver region are not only entertainment, but also comfort and peace. Below will be described in detail the most popular holiday complexes.





"Fish place" - hospitable and comfortable

On the shore of the Rybinsk reservoir, on the border of the Tver and Yaroslavl regions, at the address: Vesyegonsky district, the village of Antye, st. Zhigareva, 27-29, located a small fishing base "Fish place". In a small area there are three cozy houses with rooms designed for one or two people. Also, guests are offered a triple room of deluxe category.

For guests organized two or three meals a day. The cost of living in rooms ranges from 950 to 3,000 rubles per person, depending on the chosen category (standard or luxury). Each room has a kitchen where vacationers can cook their own meals. There is a bathroom. Satellite TV and internet access are available for guests.





Entertainment infrastructure includes sauna, fishing, boat and boat rental. In winter, tourists can ride a snowmobile. Vacationers who want to learn such an exciting activity as fishing can use the services of an instructor.

The recreation center "Dream Coast"

In the Ostashkovsky district, in the village of Ivanovskoye, on the expanses of one hectare, there is another recreation center. Its territory includes four cottages, playgrounds and playgrounds, a bathhouse. The recreation center is open all year. Cozy modern cottages have everything you need for a comfortable stay. Catering for vacationers is organized at the neighboring recreation center "Sterzh". Also, all cottages are equipped with kitchens for self-cooking. In front of each cottage there are barbecue areas with barbecue facilities.





Cottage "Sterzh"

Near the recreation center "Beach of Dreams" there is a similar institution called "Sterzh". The name of the recreation center was given by the lake located in the neighborhood. Lake Sterzh is famous for its abundance of fish. There are pikes, perches, ides and zander in it. For hunters, a licensed hunt for ducks, moose and wild boar can be organized.

Accommodation and entertainment

On a vast territory there are five buildings, cottages and a guest house. The recreation center "Sterzh" is ready to accept at the same time about a hundred vacationers. In one-room and two-room rooms there is everything necessary for relaxation. For a small company, accommodation in the Wigwam Cottage for up to five people is optimal. You can also comfortably stay in the guest house. It offers guests billiards, kitchen. Nearby is a picnic area.

On the territory there are equipped sports and children's playgrounds. Rental of sports equipment, boats and boats is also available.

Meals are organized in a cafe. Upon request, tourists can make an individual order. You can get to the recreation center, having reached Tver and then going towards the city of Ostashkov to the corresponding sign. The recreation center "Sterzh" is always glad to new guests.

Accommodations

The recreation center "Ostrovka" has its own hotel for 12 guests. The building is built of wood, equipped with modern heating systems. The first floor is occupied by two rooms (single and double), a kitchen and two bathrooms. On the cozy veranda with fireplace you can dine in the comfortable dining room. Large companies will be happy with various additional devices (barbecue, cauldrons). All rooms have the necessary furniture. Two rooms have a refrigerator.

Country complex "Zavidovo"

Around the club are picturesque forests and meadows and several beautiful lakes (Boykovo, Sterzh, Sheyno), rich in pike, perch, common carp and carp. These lakes are regularly stocked to maintain resources.

Organization of fishing and hunting

Country Club "Zavidovo" is distinguished by the highest level of service for tourists. The main area of ‚Äã‚Äãwork is the organization and provision of vacationers fishing and hunting services. For fishing in ponds, special pontoons are installed. Boat and boat rental services are available. To popularize this type of recreation, such as fishing, various contests and competitions are regularly held at the recreation center. The most experienced instructors for all comers conduct fishing master classes. Recreation facilities in the Tver region provide their guests with the best conditions for a comfortable pastime.

Holiday Village "Yar"





Some recreation centers in the Tver region are located in amazingly picturesque places. The specified institution is located 40 km from the town of Ostashkov, on the shores of the beautiful lake Seliger. It can simultaneously take about thirty vacationers. Directly on the picturesque lake shore is a well-maintained beach.

Talking about the recreation center "Yar", it is worth noting that picturesque pine forests spread around its territory. Clean air, gorgeous landscapes, an abundance of animals, birds, berries and mushrooms create a unique atmosphere of these places. Vacationers, having escaped from the stuffy city, have a unique opportunity to strengthen their health and gain new strength and experience.

The recreation center operates year-round. To find yourself in this beautiful corner of nature, you need to get to the city of Ostashkov and then follow the road signs to the village of Zaborki.

The recreation center accepts vacationers of all age categories. Separately, it is necessary to talk about the features of the organization of accommodation and meals of tourists. Vacationers are accommodated in a two-story brick cottage, which can accommodate more than two dozen people. Double rooms are connecting rooms equipped with two main and additional (optional) beds. One of the options for a double room is the availability of two beds and a sofa. Some rooms also have a dressing room.

The territory has created all the necessary infrastructure to ensure a comfortable stay. Along the perimeter, the base is surrounded by a fence. There is a guarded parking lot on the territory. Tourists can visit the traditional Russian bathhouse and such a variety as the black bathhouse. On the territory there are numerous gazebos equipped with barbecue facilities. Tourists have the opportunity to smoke freshly caught fish or meat with their own hands. In summer, the list of entertainment for holidaymakers is complemented by boating and speedboats, bicycles. The base is equipped with modern sports equipment (billiards, basketball and volleyball courts). In the evening, you can sing songs under karaoke and dance from the heart to the disco. Catering for vacationers is organized on the principle of full board and is carried out in the dining room, directly on the recreation center "Yar". Seliger is an amazing lake, so many tourists visit these places every year.

A trip to one of them will be an unforgettable event that will remain in the human memory for a long time. The level of leisure organization achieved in the region indicates a revival and a new round of development of the domestic tourism industry.
