The magician's fancy dress is one of the most magical images for the carnival!

Modern holiday goods stores offer their customers a huge assortment of all kinds of carnival accessories and ready-made fancy dresses. Today you can buy an outfit of any fabulous character. But is it possible to claim originality by choosing a factory-made costume for a masquerade? It‚Äôs much more interesting to do it yourself. How to make a magician costume?

The cylinder is the main accessory of the illusionist

What element makes a magician's outfit recognizable? That's right - this is a cylinder hat, necessarily large and tall, the one from which the most famous overlords of illusions get rabbits, scarves and other interesting items.





The easiest way to make such a hat is from very thick paper (such as Whatman paper) or flexible cardboard. Cut a rectangle - this will be the long part of the hat. Fold the cylinder out of it, fix it using glue, tape or a stapler. Cut out the upper part - a circle corresponding to the diameter of the cylinder. Glue on one side of the workpiece. Next, you need to make the headgear fields. To do this, circle the existing cylinder on paper. And then around draw another circle with a large diameter. Cut this element and glue it into place.

The cylinder is ready, but your magician costume will be completely uninteresting if you do not decorate the hat. Paint the headpiece with paint, cover it with cloth or glue it with colored paper. Before decorating the hat, decide on the main color of the outfit. The traditional costume of the magician is kept in dark colors with bright accents. If desired, you can make this outfit more vivid and original.

We master a magician's cape

Many illusionists, to create a more mysterious image, enter the stage in a cloak-mantle. Such a spectacular accessory is easy to make with your own hands. Take a piece of cloth of a suitable size and cut out a rectangle. The mantle can be up to the middle of the thigh or go down below the knees. When cutting material, consider the growth of the model.

If you need to make a magician costume as quickly as possible, choose a material for the cloak whose edges do not crumble without special treatment. A ribbon must be sewn into the top of the rectangle, thanks to which the mantle will be tied around the neck. When time permits and the quality of the finished suit is important, do not be too lazy to process all the edges of the future cloak.





An interesting idea: the mantle for a magician can be taken from any other suitable masquerade dress. If your home dresser has a superhero or stargazer costume , try on his cloak with a top hat. And it is quite possible that you don‚Äôt have to make anything on purpose.

Basic clothes

So, we have two most important elements for the outfit of an illusionist - a hat-cylinder and a mantle. The remaining things to create a fancy dress are sure to be in your wardrobe. Take classic pants and a shirt. It is very good if there is also a vest from a three-piece suit, but you can do without it. Choose basic clothing to match the primary color of the fancy dress. If you made a magician‚Äôs costume with your own hands in bright color, and it‚Äôs difficult to find the same trousers and shirt, they can be plain black. A good option is also the eternal classic: a dark bottom, white top. Do not forget to pick up simple black classic shoes in addition to the costume.

Important accessories and useful props

Not sure what else to add to the costume of a magician? Photos of famous illusionists can help you find interesting ideas. Many famous magicians complemented their stage outfit with bow ties. And this is a great idea for repetition at home. You can make the look more expressive by picking up thin white gloves.





If you are making a New Year's magician costume for a children's party, it is appropriate to decorate it with tinsel and shiny elements. For a serious adult illusionist costume, any accessories that emphasize the classic style are suitable. Think when making a costume - will you show tricks for the holiday? If so, you should carefully rehearse them and pick up all the necessary props for such magic.

Should a magician be able to show tricks?

An interesting addition to the fancy dress of the illusionist will be a deck of playing cards, multi-colored shawls or a magic wand. It will be useful to learn a few simple tricks on the eve of the masquerade. Believe me, it‚Äôs not at all difficult even a small child can show the disappearance of a coin or a playing card.





If you make a magician costume for a school-age boy, you can prepare several more complex numbers and arrange a mini-performance at the holiday. However, if you do not want to show tricks, this is optional. At any costume party or celebration, the most important thing is that the costume is original and fits well.
