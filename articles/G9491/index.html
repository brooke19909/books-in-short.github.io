"Birch Grove" in Ulyanovsk: description and reviews

Many holiday homes receive guests not only in the summer, but also in the cold season. Beautiful landscape and fresh air are in demand at any time of the year. Base "Birch Grove" in Ulyanovsk is waiting for visitors all year round and is ready to offer them excellent options for recreation. Here are all the necessary conditions for living and a good rest.

general information

The recreation center "Birch Grove" (Ulyanovsk) is becoming more and more popular every year. This place is located near the city, so guests come here not only on weekends, but also on weekdays. Here you can spend your whole vacation or a couple of days with family and friends. Unique in its beauty nature, fresh air, a lake, its own beach - this is only part of the advantages of a popular base.

For accommodation, rooms of different price categories are offered. Arbors create additional comfort, where it is so nice to relax in the fresh air. In the rooms for a comfortable stay of guests prepared everything you need:

  • Linens.
  • Plaids.
  • Crockery with cutlery.
  • Microwave ovens.
  • Household appliances (kettle and hairdryer).
  • Towels.
  • Robes.

Hot shower facilities are also provided. If there is a veranda, then it is equipped with a table with benches.

Meals - in a cafe on the territory of the Birch Grove in Ulyanovsk. Here you can taste delicious dishes. Especially guests praise the dishes of Caucasian cuisine: khachapuri, khinkali, vitamin teas with unsurpassed taste and aroma.

How to get there

You can find an institution both with the help of a map, and thanks to the navigator. The exact address of the Birch Grove in Ulyanovsk is: Seldinskoye Shosse, Building 1. You can get to the base from the north. To do this, go to the bypass road, where the Obkomov cottages are located. Next, take a bend where the village "Dachny" is located.

Guest reviews

Every year, a lot of people visit the base, many are happy with the rest, so they without fail share their opinions with others. "Birch Grove" in Ulyanovsk is very popular both among residents of the city, and among those who come here from other parts of Russia. Many famous musicians prefer to stay here, appreciating the service and quality of their stay.

A big plus for many is the fact that the base is located very close to the city. At any time you can pack up and come to relax from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

In their reviews, guests not only recommend the base as a great place, but also want to return there again. The guests did not ignore the delicious menu, excellent service and friendly staff.

But there are those who did not like the rest. Most often this is due to individual moments or poor-quality work of employees.

Otherwise, visitors are satisfied with the rest. Indeed, for each guest, everything necessary is prepared in advance. Sunbathing enthusiasts are pleased that sun loungers are already included in the price. It is possible to settle right on the shore near the reservoir. People also report the availability of free internet, although the quality of communication is not the same everywhere.

Additional features

"Birch Grove" in Ulyanovsk often becomes a place of celebration of anniversaries and weddings. After all, here you can arrange a truly grand celebration, which, undoubtedly, will be remembered not only by the bride and groom, but also by the guests.

For a wedding, they often choose one of the banquet halls or celebrate it directly in the fresh air. Especially popular is the hall, which is located directly on the water.

Often other celebrations are held on the territory: banquets and anniversaries. To celebrate them, you can also pick up several options for every taste and budget.

Vacationers can also rent gazebos with barbecue facilities. It will cost about 7,000 - 10,000 rubles.

Guests can continue their stay in the bathhouse.

For lovers of outdoor activities - rental of sports equipment and various modes of transport. So, you can rent a catamaran, a boat and even a boat.

Especially guests like paintball here, many come to play on the weekend.

Fans of fishing also have a place to roam, as they have at their disposal a whole pond.

Kids are not bored here either - a special playground is intended for children's games.

In winter, the Birch Grove camp site in Ulyanovsk is never empty. People come here to ski and sled. Many visitors seek to arrange a photo shoot in nature against the backdrop of beautiful views.
