However, if an IP decided to work without a current account, a lot of questions will appear about how to settle accounts with partners, receive payments from customers and others. Sometimes the only way to solve these problems is precisely r / s.

How much is needed

In the coming 2019, account opening is carried out in the same way as before. The relevant agreement with the bank is concluded on a voluntary basis. The current account plays an important role in the financial activities of the entrepreneur. Thanks to him, it is possible to separate the funds received in the process of commercial activity from personal money. It is easier to compile and report, as well as exercise control over the fulfillment of obligations under the contract.

An additional guarantee when concluding contracts is provided by the bank details of the IP settlement account. Payments reflected in the bank statement confirm the solvency of the entrepreneur in the event that borrowed money is required. The bank is more likely to issue loans to individuals whose activities are characterized by financial stability.

If payments exceed 100,000 rubles, then cash will not be limited. Is it necessary to open a current account for individual entrepreneurs in this case? Yes. In addition, wholesalers usually prefer cashless transfers when transferring large amounts of money.

In the case of retail, more and more people prefer to pay with a plastic card. In this regard, the need for this bank account becomes even greater.

Also, entrepreneurs sometimes use a deposit account for settlements. However, this is inconvenient, since the use of funds stored in such an account, as a rule, has different restrictions, as well as an additional commission when transferring to third parties.

Responsibility for using a personal account

The Central Bank is obliged to use deposits and accounts of individuals exclusively for accumulative purposes that are not related to commercial activities. The Central Bank of the Russian Federation obliged banks to open settlement accounts for doing business. At the same time, for the fact that this rule is neglected, legal liability for IP does not arise. Therefore, even if the bank security officers find out that the personal account is used for commercial purposes, fines will not be imposed on individual entrepreneurs.

In an effort to fulfill the obligation of the Central Bank, some financial organizations in the purpose of payment require customers to add the phrase that the money transfer is not related to commercial activities. However, such a requirement is illegal.

If the entrepreneur gave himself a negative answer to the question of whether an individual entrepreneur needs a current account, he also does not always remain honest with the financial institution in which he opened a personal account. So, using it for commercial purposes, the IP does not indicate the basis of the payment, which is true.

Bank selection

Having answered the question of whether a current account is required for an individual entrepreneur, positively, the entrepreneur proceeds to the choice of a financial organization. At the same time, it is worth asking about where it is located in the reliability rating. This is especially true in cases where the country has an unstable economic situation and banks often have their licenses revoked.

The second most important factor in accordance with which a bank is selected for individual entrepreneurs is the cost of servicing the account. The size is set by the financial institution, but may increase depending on the payments made by the account holder. Also, a certain amount must be paid for opening a settlement account.

When choosing a financial organization, you must first familiarize yourself with the rates. In addition, it is worth finding out about the timing of the transfer of funds. Reliable banking organizations strive to transfer money during the day. You can also ask about the necessary documents for opening an IP settlement account.

Documents for opening

Different banks require their own package of securities. But the main ones include the following:

  1. Identification.
  2. Registration certificate.
  3. INN
  4. Extract from the USRIP.

Also, the bank will need to fill out an application, a customer questionnaire, as well as a card with the possibility of signing and concluding an agreement. If you have all the necessary documents with you, the procedure does not take much time.

Nuances when opening an account

Documents should be provided in the original. If necessary, the bank will make copies. It should be borne in mind that some of them have a limited period of validity. For example, an extract is relevant for a month. After that, you need to order a new document.

After signing the contract, you need to carefully study all the items and find out the prices. If in any doubt, you can contact a lawyer for advice.

Separate banks for IP are asked to put a print of the seal. However, individual entrepreneurs are not required to use a stamp in their work. Therefore, many of them assure the contract only with a signature.

Advantages and disadvantages of a current account

Having dealt with the question of whether a checking account is required for individual entrepreneurs, it is necessary to compare the advantages and disadvantages of its availability. Economically p / s has both positive and negative properties. The main benefits include the following:

  1. Current account provides excellent safety of money.
  2. Settlements with counterparties become comfortable and transparent.
  3. Tax authorities do not closely monitor money transfers, since this function is under the supervision of the bank itself.
  4. An IP can manage money remotely through an online banking system.
  5. Transfer and return of taxes with the help of r / s is much more convenient and reliable.

At the same time, it is impossible not to say about the shortcomings. You will have to pay for settlement and cash services, and in some cases to visit the bank, for example, to obtain an extract and collection.
