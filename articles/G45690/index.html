Golden Five Paradise, Hurghada - one hundred percent rest!

In Egypt, there are many good "fives", and quite often among them there are hotels with German, French and Italian management. Golden Live Paradise (Hurghada) is controlled by the Egyptians, but neither the quality of the rest, nor the services of the hotel deteriorate at all, and the impressions of the tourists they share after visiting it only increase its rating. The hotel is part of a whole complex of various Golden Five City hotels, palace-style buildings and bungalows. They are picturesquely scattered in a huge tropical garden with gilded sculptures in the Egyptian style.

Egypt, Hurghada, Golden Five Paradise: Accommodation



This is one of seven hotels (both “fours” and “fives”). Five of them are called by the names of precious stones: “Diamond”, “Diamond”, “Emerald”, “Sapphire”, “Topaz”. There is also a "Club". The guests of the complex can visit all buildings, are in all restaurants, participate in all entertainment programs, without exception. The Golden Live Paradise itself (Hurghada) itself consists of three buildings in the form of the Latin letter L. The newest building is called Andalusia. This part of the City stands in the very center of the complex, and from its windows you can see the huge main pool with various slides, and the dancing fountains famous throughout the resort. Here guests can take 466 well-equipped rooms. Among them are 10 suites, including royal and presidential. In the lobby - free Wi-Fi.

Hotel Golden Five Paradise (Hurghada): how to feed here



Restaurant "Red Sea" is the main food point in this hotel. Here you can eat on an all-inclusive basis, and also sign up for one of the à la carte establishments. There are nine of them, and the kitchen here is your choice. There are Russian, and German, and Italian, and Turkish, and Asian, and Mediterranean. And also special restaurants for barbecue and seafood. In four of them you can have dinner once for free, only by appointment. The hotel’s own bars are Day and Night, Piano, and Billiards. I don’t mention the restaurants and eateries of other hotels in the City: there are so many of them that if you relax for less than ten days and walk everywhere once, you still don’t have time to get into everything.

Hotel Golden Five Paradise (Hurghada): how to sunbathe and swim here



The beach is huge, kilometer-long, sandy. It is accessible to all guests of the City, as well as a huge swimming pool with a real water park (five good slides). There are all amenities, not only sun loungers, towels and umbrellas, but also mattresses and wicker barriers from the wind (which is very convenient in winter). There is another pool with warm water and three children's, with heating and slides. On the beach - volleyball, gymnastics, including water. The sand is very small and smooth, the entrance to the water is excellent. Nearby there is a private reef with corals, so you can not spend money on a special excursion. Enough of your own mask.

Golden Five Paradise (Hurghada): reviews

Tourists often leave positive feedback about the hotel: praise staff who try and clean, a well-groomed beach, slides. True, they are disappointed that they only let people enter the restaurants of other hotels at the last hour, but this is probably due to the fact that the hotel is popular and there are a lot of people, and the management is trying not to create queues. But the food is delicious. Great pastries, especially French puffs. A good and varied animation: fakirs, belly dancing, cobra shows and so on. The night bar in this hotel is called the most fun in the entire City. You can smoke a hookah for free.
