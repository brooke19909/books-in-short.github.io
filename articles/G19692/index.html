Hotel Castellastva 4 * (Montenegro, Petrovac): traveler reviews

Traveling is great. For some, trips abroad are a way of life. For others, a way to have a good vacation. But both in one and in the second case, people are looking for a decent hotel, which could become a place for temporary residence. Recently, Montenegro is gaining more and more popularity. Many people come here. And there are a lot of excellent hotels. One of those is Castellastva 4 *. Montenegro should be the next country to travel? Then it is worth staying at this hotel. Why? But about this - in more detail.





general information

Castellastva 4 * (Montenegro) is located in a picturesque town called Petrovac. It is approximately 35-40 kilometers from Tivat Airport. And also - a 15-minute drive from the bustling Budva, founded about 2,500 years ago. This city is the noisiest in the whole country. But Petrovac is one of the most secluded and quiet. There you can relax from the bustle, enjoy the clean beaches, picturesque views and unforgettable sunsets.

There are many interesting places in Budva. To get to this city is simple - there is a bus stop 200 meters from the hotel, from where there are minibuses in different directions. You should definitely visit the citadel and pier of Budva, the church of St. John, as well as Mogren Beach.

By the way, about the sea. The beach from the Hotel Castellastva 4 * (Montenegro) is in close proximity. You can reach it in two minutes. And this is the most important thing, because most tourists come here for a beach holiday.

Entertainment

Hotel Castellastva 4 * (Montenegro, Petrovac) can hardly be called a resort hotel. This is a hotel ideal for a comfortable stay. There is no entertainment show program for adults, but there is a sports field and a pool with a bar, hidden in a cool shade.

In the meantime, adults will relax, children can have fun in the mini club. There are several age groups. An animation program is organized for the kids, so no one will be bored. In the mini-club, children are supervised by educators, so parents can be calm about them.

And Hotel Castellastva 4 * (Montenegro) has a modern gym with treadmills, barbells, cardiovascular equipment and much more. It also houses the Welness Center with a Finnish sauna and steam bath. Many lovers of an active lifestyle like to spend free time there.





Nutrition

This hotel has a wonderful restaurant. The spacious, bright, originally decorated room with air conditioning can comfortably accommodate 450 people. Guests can enjoy a wide range of drinks and dishes. By the way, they can choose either half board or three meals a day. Breakfast here starts at 7:00 and ends at 10:00. Lunch is from 12:00 to 14:00. And dinner begins at 19:00 and continues until 21:00.

Guests who were in Castellastva 4 * (Montenegro), reviews on food leave extremely positive. They assure - the quality of food here is on top. Breakfasts are European, classic - there are sausages, omelets, scrambled eggs, sausages and cheese slices, beans, honey, jams, cereals and crumbly natural cottage cheese. Of the drinks - milk, coffee, various juices and teas. And, of course, they serve prshut here. Montenegrin national dish, which is smoked or cured ham. In this hotel, it is also slightly fried. Especially good with crispy bread, which is baked before breakfast.

They also offer soups, juicy fruits, ice cream, pastries, vegetables, meat and fish of various kinds. The waiters serve quickly - they clean the dirty dishes, as soon as they manage to empty. By the way, reasonable prices in the bar are no different from those offered in other institutions of Petrovac.

Suites

The hotel also has top class apartments. There are only 14 suites. Therefore, in order to manage to book just them, you need to be puzzled by the reservation issue long before the planned trip. And the price is determined individually.

A feature of the suites is in their modern design, expensive decor elements, increased comfort and large space. They also have a safe. In general, luxury is an option for true aesthetes.

Staff

People working at the hotel appreciate their guests - this can be seen immediately. Their attitude towards guests is good-natured and respectful. They try to do everything possible to ensure that tourists are satisfied.

Daily cleaning, towel change - too. Moreover, regardless of whether their guest threw them to the floor or not. Linen is replaced a little less often - every 3 days. The bathrooms have hygiene kits for each guest and are replenished daily. In general, everything is at a decent level.





Important information

Check-in at the hotel starts at noon and ends at midnight. Departure lasts from 8:00 to 11:00. The hotel can accommodate children of any age. Moreover, if the baby is not two years old, then he will live for free. But only on the beds available in the apartments. If you need a cot, you have to pay extra for 7 euros per day. Are you planning a trip with someone older than two years old (whether it be a child of kindergarten age, or another adult)? Then you will need to pay extra 25 euros per day. There is an extra bed in the apartment.

But if a berth is necessary, you must contact the hotel in advance and inform about your wishes. Proof of service will be sent to potential guests.

By the way, when booking you will be required to provide credit card details. The accommodation facility accepts Diners Club, Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Maestro. The card must belong to the guest, since upon check-in he will be required to present it with his passport.





What do the guests say?

As you could already understand, the Castellastva 4 * hotel (Montenegro) is pretty good. The reviews of 2016 allow us to verify this.

Tourists who stay here are satisfied. They assure - the place is wonderful. It is perfect for families with children. Since for kids there is everything. The most important thing is the mini club. Many advise booking apartments with a balcony leading to a staircase leading directly to the pool. It is very comfortable. By the way, the TV broadcasts local, European and Russian channels. Guests pay special attention to the availability of water treatment. Water from taps can boast of drinking quality, which is good news. By the way, the plumbing is all new, good, sparkling with cleanliness.

The beach is excellent - clean and spacious. The same can be said about the pool. The water is moderately chlorinated and very comfortable in temperature. There are enough deck chairs for everyone.

In general, the hotel is good. Without surplus and excess of luxury, but comfortable and cozy. Those wishing to relax their soul and body will definitely like it here.
