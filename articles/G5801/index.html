How to get to Suzdal from Moscow by different means of transport

Suzdal is a beautiful ancient town in the Vladimir region, which invariably attracts tourists. Ancient monasteries, streets of the ancient city attract foreign and local guests. How to get to Suzdal from Moscow? This question interests many travelers.





How to get there

If you went on a trip along the Moscow-Suzdal route by car, you can leave along the Shchelkovo or Gorky highway. The distance is about the same - 220 km.

On Gorky Highway (M7) you should leave Moscow along the Enthusiasts Highway. The road is good almost all the way to Suzdal (four-lane). Hitches can be at the exit of Moscow, in the Balashikha area and in the Lakinsk district. There are a lot of heavy trucks on the road. If you are lucky, the journey will take approximately 3.5 hours. Especially great traffic here on weekends in the summer season. It should also be noted that there are many settlements along the way, respectively, there are speed limits, and there are also many traffic police posts. What to see on the way?

If an independent traveler, studying the question of how to get to Suzdal from Moscow, chose the Shchelkovo highway for traffic, then he will be able to admire the charms of the old Yuryev-Polsky, making a stop for several hours.

Here the city buildings of the XIX century and the beginning of the XX century, many churches, the oldest of which is St. George's Cathedral, have been preserved. The building is dated 1230-1234 years. Three monasteries of the 16th, 17th and 18th centuries are a rich heritage of history in such a small town.





Moving along the Gorky highway, you get to the famous Vladimirka. Here the convicts walked to the transit Vladimir prison.

On the way you will meet the Veil, built on the site of the Anthony Desert. The city named after the main temple - Pokrovsky monastery. In the city you can buy delicious Pokrov gingerbread cookies with nuts and condensed milk.

In Petushki there are two museums that may interest travelers - the local history museum and the Rooster museum.

In Lakinsk you can admire the cathedral of the XVII-XIX centuries. The city is famous for its delicious draft beer. It should be noted that the beer is natural, so its shelf life is only 2-3 days.

Undoubtedly, ancient Vladimir is worthy of attention, therefore, when deciding how to get to Suzdal from Moscow, be sure to include a visit to this city in your plans. Moreover, all roads, one way or another, lead exactly here. Entering the city through the legendary Golden Gate is already an extraordinary event. Bus "Moscow-Suzdal"

Suzdal can be reached by bus from Moscow. The specified transport leaves from the Central city station, which is near the Shchelkovskaya metro station.

There are 4 direct flights per day in the direction Moscow-Suzdal. The bus takes 4 hours 10 minutes.

You can get on the bus "Moscow-Ivanovo", which leaves every hour or "Moscow-Vladimir". Next, you will have to transfer to local scheduled transport and overcome the remaining 35 km of the route on it or use taxi services. The interval between buses is also one hour. A local shuttle bus runs from Vladimir to Suzdal in about 40-45 minutes.

As you can see, Moscow-Suzdal is not the only bus with which you can get to the desired place. The cost of tickets for transportation of the Suzdal direction ranges from 485 to 613 rubles, depending on the type of bus and the flight chosen for the trip.
