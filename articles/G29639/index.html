What are the criteria for choosing a cordless portable vacuum cleaner: an overview of popular models

Cleanliness is the key to health. This phrase is familiar to each of us since childhood. And if earlier, in order to achieve perfect cleanliness, the apartment had to run with a rag all day, now there are new-fashioned devices that will help clean the entire apartment from dust and dirt within half an hour.

For example, cordless vacuum cleaners. They are relatively inexpensive, take up little space and last for several years. Their operating time varies depending on the manufacturer and the battery capacity. With them, cleaning will be pleasant and quick.

But when buying, the question arises: what to choose. In this material we will figure out what characteristics cordless vacuum cleaners have and which of the existing analogues is most suitable for cleaning your home.

Wireless vacuum cleaner PUPPYOO D-531

PUPPYOO D-531 has just appeared on our market. His cost - about 30,000 rubles., However, the manufacturer at first promises a 70% discount - a great opportunity for a bargain. But what kind of vacuum cleaner is this?

Brief characteristics of the D-531:

  • a full charge of the battery - 2.5 hours;
  • dust collector volume - 0.65 l;
  • operating time without recharging - 30 min;
  • battery - 2200 mAh;
  • weight - 1.6 kg;
  • weight with accessories - 3 kg;
  • power - 150 W;
  • the price for September 2016 - 33 718.52 rubles. (for the stock - about 10,000 rubles).

PUPPYOO D-531 comes with three nozzles. With their help, you can clean toys, carpets with any pile, sofas, curtains and remove cobwebs. The design of the nozzles is such that you can easily get to the most inaccessible places. The handle is coated with anti-slip material. A cyclical dust collection system will suck in even the smallest litter. The air in your apartment will be absolutely clean.





Dyson V6 Cordless Vacuum Cleaner

If you choose a cordless vacuum cleaner, you have probably heard of the Dyson V6. This model comes with an electric brush, the pile of which is made of carbon fiber. This solution will allow you to clean any type of coating. Thanks to the special technology of 2 Tier Radial, 15 cyclones capture more fine dust. To clean the garbage container, just click on one button.

Brief characteristics of the Dyson V6:

  • battery full charge time - 3.5 hours;
  • dust collector volume - 0.4 l;
  • operating time without recharging - 20 min;
  • weight - 2.04 kg;
  • power - 100 W;
  • price for September 2016 - 19,990 rubles.

The vacuum cleaner comes with special combination and crevice nozzles. They will allow you to reach even the most inaccessible places. The battery charge provides constant power to the vacuum cleaner thanks to a nickel-cobalt battery.

As you can see, this representative of cordless vacuum cleaners is inferior to the D-531 in terms of power, operating time, and the size of the dust bag, and weighs heavier. The advantage in price, but this point is debatable, if you buy D-531 for stocks.





Wireless vacuum cleaner LG VS8404SCW

Powerful and economical vacuum cleaner LG VS8404SCW provides excellent cleaning and high suction power. The air in your apartment becomes clean and fresh.

Short characteristics of LG VS8404SCW:

  • battery full charge time - 4.5 hours;
  • dust collector volume - 0.65 l;
  • operating time without recharging - 60 min;
  • weight - 2.8 kg
  • weight with accessories - 5.3 kg;
  • power - 90 W;
  • price for September 2016 - 24,280 rubles.




Wireless Bissell MultiReach 1313J

The Bissell MultiReach 1313J handheld vacuum cleaner is equipped with an electric turbo brush. The kit includes three nozzles. The brush is turned off by a button, which allows you to quickly replace the nozzle.

Brief characteristics of the Bissell MultiReach 1313J:

  • battery full charge time - 8 hours;
  • dust collector volume - 0.4 l;
  • operating time without recharging - 15 min;
  • weight - 2.4 kg;
  • power - 25.5 W;
  • price for September 2016 - 12,990 rubles.

The vacuum cleaner is quite powerful and easy to use. It will provide excellent and high-quality cleaning in inaccessible places. Even those who are far from technological progress will quickly master the mechanical control system.





Wireless vacuum cleaner Electrolux ERG105

Wireless Electrolux ERG105 comes complete with turbo and crevice brushes. This will ensure excellent cleaning quality even in places where it is usually difficult to reach.

Brief characteristics of the vacuum cleaner:

  • battery full charge time - 16 hours;
  • dust collector volume - 0.5 l;
  • operating time without recharging - 30 min;
  • weight - 3.04 kg;
  • power - 14.4 W;
  • price for September 2016 - 9999 rubles.




Which vacuum cleaner is worth buying

Note that all of the above vacuum cleaners are modern, comfortable and of high quality. The most powerful is a vacuum cleaner from LG. But charging time, a small dust collector and high cost are not too conducive to purchase. For those who value the correspondence between reasonable price and high quality work, we recommend that you pay attention to D-531 by PUPPYOO . It is lightweight, powerful, charges quickly, and works for a long time. This model is ahead of many others in technical specifications, and the issue of price is not yet acute due to discounts (the cost is comparable even with the most budget option - Electrolux ERG105).
