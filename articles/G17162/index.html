Would you like a quality refrigerator? "Electrolux" to help you!

Those days are gone when the main problem in the fight for hygiene of refrigeration equipment was its defrosting. Many people remember how they had to wield various devices, trying to break off thick pieces of ice from the walls of the freezer. Another problem that the owners of the ZIL, Minsk and other refrigerators periodically encountered was the inability to set an acceptable temperature. Fruits, vegetables and meat were not sufficiently cooled, then covered with a crust of ice.





Quality comes first

Fortunately, now the housewives can breathe a sigh of relief: the modern, taste-preserving device is completely unpretentious in operation. Everyone can choose their own refrigerator in terms of size, price, color and spaciousness. "Electrolux" is a brand known even to small children, producing appliances for domestic use. Products born under this brand are highly respected among connoisseurs of quality and durable products. Refrigerators "Electrolux" (two-chamber, single-chamber, giant wine storages and freezers) take into account the tastes and preferences of all segments of the population, regardless of their gender, age and position. At the same time, the company adheres to the basic principle: price differences of goods consist of the presence or absence of additional parts and accessories, but not of the degree of quality. That is why the washing machine, stove, vacuum cleaner and refrigerator "Electrolux" are always in great demand.





One and only compartment

The very first and smallest model is a single-chamber refrigerator. This household item is ideal for a small space, one person or even a small family. At the same time, despite the size, it is an example of the highest quality. Dimensions and volume vary depending on the model. So, the Electrolux refrigerator is considered the smallest option, the height of which is only 85 cm. The useful volume of such a cabinet is 136 liters. This model is called ERT1506FOW. It also has a small freezer. A larger option will be a 153-liter refrigerator. The main differences from the previous crumbs are the size and the absence of any additional storage.

Refrigerator "Electrolux" ERF4161AOW is one of the largest representatives of the family of single-chamber storages manufactured under this brand. The useful volume of this refrigerator is 381 liters. At the same time, the overall height reaches almost 190 cm.

One plus one

The second category of the product in question is a two-chamber refrigerator. Like the previous miniature (and not quite) models, this resident of the kitchen can be of different sizes and have different characteristics. The freezer can be located both at the bottom of the device, and on top. Each product in this category is equipped with a No frost system. For ease of use, refrigerators are provided with various plastic containers, hanging grids for storing bottles and containers. It is worth noting that some models are equipped with an air filtration function. It allows you to get rid of unpleasant odors that appear inside the device.

Non-European flaws

When purchasing this or that refrigerator, many owners are in a hurry to leave reviews about the Electrolux product . The refrigerator of domestic assembly, regardless of its size and device, has several negative qualities. The first is the inconvenient location of the shelves. The second is the noise made during operation. The third is the fragility of the containers. At the same time, the majority of owners who recognized these shortcomings purchased refrigerators of the Russian assembly. Those who did not dare to save money and became the owner of the Italian or Hungarian version had no problems. Of course, you should decide which is more important: quality and service life or money saved, which will be spent on troubleshooting and breakdowns.
