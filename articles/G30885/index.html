Hotel "AZOT" in Tolyatti: photos, review, service features and reviews

Hotel "AZOT" in the city of Tolyatti is one of the most popular holiday destinations for city guests who come to a developed village for various purposes. Let us consider in more detail what conditions for living are offered in this place, as well as what reviews about this hotel are found on social networks and on various tourist portals.

General information

The AZOT Hotel in Tolyatti has been hosting guests since 2016. This place for rest and overnight is managed by OJSC KuibyshevAzot.

According to the hotel administration and the vacationers themselves, this place is perfect for family pastime and for those who are in Togliatti on a business trip. According to the reception staff, the regular guests of AZOT are musicians and athletes, as well as theater artists who come to the city on tour.

Among all the hotels in Togliatti, the place in question has a rather high rating, put on the basis of reviews of vacationers. So, on some well-known tourist portals, AZOT receives a rating of 8.1 out of 10, and on others - 4.2 out of 5, which is an almost excellent result. In accordance with international standards, he was given the status of "three stars".

Location

The hotel in question is very popular among city guests not only because it offers excellent conditions for living. Another reason is the advantageous location. It is located in the central part of Tolyatti, at the address: Victory Street, 40.

Not far from the AZOT 3 * hotel (Russia / Tolyatti) there is a stopping point through which public transport can deliver anywhere in the city. In their comments, guests of this place recommend taking advantage of this situation and, in their free time from scheduled tasks, go to the city surroundings to explore the sights. Among them, the most interesting for nonresident guests are Spaso-Preobrazhensky themselves and a monument to Tatishchev.

There are several restaurants and bars in the neighborhood. Just 200 meters from here is a McDonald's cafe, as well as a large shopping and entertainment center that can be visited by the whole family.

Speaking of international and intercity hubs for transport, it should be noted that they are all at a distance of about 10 km. That is why the hotel offers a shuttle service. As for the airport, the nearest one is in the city of Kurumoch, which is almost 50 km from here.

Rooms

In the reviews left by the guests about the hotel in question, it is often mentioned about the comfortable conditions that are provided to visitors here. The city guests are offered cozy apartments of various categories and luxury levels: from simple studio rooms to luxury apartments with individual kitchens, which can be used for self-cooking.

In total, the fund of the AZOT Hotel (Togliatti) has 45 rooms in which 92 beds are provided. All of them are divided into several categories: luxury, apartments, and there is also a separate group of rooms for honeymooners.

All apartments have a private bathroom, which has modern high-quality plumbing, as well as free toiletries.

Suite

In the fund of the hotel in question there are several suites that are designed for one or a couple of guests. According to vacationers, there are all the conditions that are needed for a comfortable pastime.

Among the furniture equipment, there are single and double beds made of high quality material and covered with good bedding. Each room of this type has a lounge area, represented by a combination of a sofa and a coffee table. There is a wardrobe for clothes and personal belongings of tourists, as well as a pair of bedside tables. This type of room is equipped with a work area, represented by a large desk and armchair.

Guests can spend their free time watching their favorite TV shows on the plasma screen connected to the satellite broadcasting system. As a technical equipment, the luxury apartments feature a telephone and a minibar.

Apartments

This type of room is a combination of several rooms. As a rule, they are rented by guests of the city who came as a family, a large group of friends or business partners. In rooms of this category, one-time accommodation from 3 to 6 guests is possible.

Apartments in the AZOT Hotel (Russia / Tolyatti) are divided into two types: two-room and three-room. Each of them has an individual kitchen, which is equipped with furniture and appliances necessary for cooking fresh food. In addition, there is a place for eating. In all other respects, the furniture and technical equipment fully coincides with that presented in the deluxe rooms, but based on the declared number of guests.

Honeymoon Rooms

At the AZOT Hotel in Tolyatti, special attention is paid to those couples who are newlyweds and have a desire to note the fact of marriage and the creation of a family in this particular place.

Number of this type is one-room and double. Its furniture equipment is fully consistent with that presented in a double suite. By the time the bride and groom arrives, decor is made here, which must be agreed upon in advance, as the customer can be offered several options for decoration, depending on the budget.

Among the features of honeymoon rooms there is the possibility of ordering breakfast in the apartment at any time of the day.

Nutrition

The guests of the hotel in question pay special attention to what kind of food is provided to the guests. A bar is functioning here on an ongoing basis, the menu of which offers delicious and mouth-watering dishes prepared in the style of European cuisine. Vacationers note that this place is a great place for family pastime, romantic evenings or fun meetings with friends.

The guests of the AZOT Hotel have the right to order food directly to their rooms. They are offered a separate menu, which includes interesting drinks, lunches, as well as custom-made dinners. As for breakfast, it can be prepared and served in English or Irish style. The cost of breakfast is usually included in the rental room - it is 150 rubles.

You can visit this institution at any time from 7 am to 11 pm.

Additional services

At the reception desk, which is located on the ground floor of the hotel building, there is an administrator working round the clock, to whom guests can always turn for additional services. Among them - a taxi call and advice on the location of interesting places, attractions, as well as city tickets. As for calling a taxi, the hotel cooperates with the official service, whose employees provide reporting documents - they are especially valuable for city guests who are on a business trip or on a business trip. If necessary, they can also assist in booking tickets for interesting events and events that will be held in the city.

The hotel provides the opportunity to register foreign tourists with the migration service of the Russian Federation.

If desired, guests have the opportunity to order breakfast, dinner or lunch directly in the room. To do this, just make a call to the front desk and the order will be placed in the near future.

Speaking in their comments about the features of the service in the hotel in question, many guests note in their reviews that the staff here are very helpful and polite. In addition, the AZOT Hotel always takes into account the opinion of the client and all his wishes. The rooms are constantly maintained clean, the cleaning service here is top-notch.

Prices

The reviews about the AZOT Hotel (Togliatti) often say that the cost of living in this place is pleasantly surprising, especially when compared with the conditions provided. The price of renting a room directly depends on how many guests need to be accommodated in it, as well as on the degree of its comfort. So, an apartment type room for three guests will cost about 4900 rubles per day, and if you take into account a studio type, such as a suite for one guest, then its cost is about 2000 rubles.

Considering the above prices in the hotel "AZOT" (Togliatti), it should be understood that they include the cost of breakfast.

If desired, guests of the city can rent a separate bed in large apartments of a general type. In this case, its cost is only 950 rubles per night. It should be noted that this feature is popular among guests - this is due to its cheapness. However, not all visiting tourists consider this option to be comfortable, which is often noted in their reviews left to the hotel.
