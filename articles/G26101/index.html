Holguin (Cuba): description, interesting places, recreation

Cuba is a paradise resort with amazing energy. If you are tired of the dullness and monotony of everyday life, dream of finding yourself on white sand, surrounded by tropical plants, get involved in a festive atmosphere, enjoy the hospitality and color of the resort, go to Cuba. Holguin is perhaps the most picturesque province of this country. Here, tourists and vacationers can enjoy the rich natural and plant world of the resort. If you are interested in tours to Cuba from Moscow, we strongly recommend that you pay attention to this particular province. And within the framework of this material we will carry out its objective review.

Historical reference

Few people know, but it was in this part of Cuba in the distant 1492 that Columbus landed. Incidentally, he was fascinated by the beauty of the area and the picturesque views. It was here that the first traditions and customs of the Cuban people arose. Every resident of the country knows that Holguin is the birthplace of the famous Fidel Castro.





Holguin received the status of the city only in 1525, when it began to grow rapidly. Today, most of the local population is engaged in agriculture and, of course, glad to see tourists and vacationers. Friendliness and hospitality are what all travelers celebrate. If you are planning a trip to Cuba, we strongly recommend paying special attention to the city of Holguin.

Landscape

Many love Cuba for its paradise vacation, the opportunity to soak up on the white sand, get a beautiful even tan and just enjoy the calm and measured relaxation. The city of Holguin in Cuba is not very popular among tourists. But in vain, since this is a real paradise, the most picturesque and beautiful in the resort. The majestic mountains, combined with prolific plains and endless sandy beaches - all this is Holguin. It is very beautiful here, tourists admire nature and amazing, indescribable atmosphere.





Hotels

Holguin (Cuba) is one and a half hours summer from Havana, it is the second most important resort in the country. Hotels here are considered elite, the cost of living in them is the most expensive in the country. Nevertheless, here you will hardly find three-star apartments, and those luxurious hotels that are located right on the coast are devoid of a variety of entertainment. They offer tourists a calm and measured rest. The best hotels are Paradisus Rio de Oro and Sol Rio de Luna y Mares, offering first-class service, luxurious accommodation and all-inclusive meals.

Beaches

Let's see what beaches the resort of Holguin (Cuba) can offer. Its description only confirms the presence of the most picturesque beaches in the entire province. The turquoise, warm and gentle sea is a natural defense for coral reefs, which affects the popularity of the resort.





The beaches with white sand are surrounded by vegetation, creating a contrast of a rich green hue with gold sand and azure sea water of the Caribbean. Plants create a pleasant natural shadow and coolness, turning a beach holiday into a paradise pastime. It is here that the famous Playa Esmeralda beach is located with stunning coral reefs.

Entertainment

Yes, the hotels in Holguin (Cuba) are deprived of entertainment, but this does not mean that there is absolutely nothing to do here. In addition to the picturesque nature, excellent climate, there are enough attractions that really deserve special attention to themselves.

If you value the comfort and luxury of a beach holiday, but gladly dilute it with an examination of cultural and historical places, the resort of Holguin (Cuba) is perfect for you. Reviews from travelers and tourists confirm that the city really has something to see and something to admire.





It is no coincidence that Holguin is called the "city of parks"; there are a huge number of them. Only in the city center there are six of them - all of them are picturesque, well-groomed, for local residents are a real salvation from the sun. A local attraction is the Loma de la Cruz hill, to the top of which there is a staircase with 428 steps - a great option for lovers of heights and breathtaking views.

Be sure to visit the San Jose Church with its amazing bell tower and delightful dome. On Thursdays and Sundays, organ concerts are held in the temple - be sure to visit at least one of them if possible.

Excursion program

If you want to get to know the resort of Holguin (Cuba), its history and traditions as close as possible, pay attention to the variety of routes and programs for tourists. Sightseeing tour of the city with a visit to the most outstanding sights - a great opportunity to explore the city, accompanied by a professional guide. Jeep safari, visiting the aquarium, spearfishing, fishing on the high seas are excellent options for organizing leisure activities. And, of course, Holguin with its stunning beaches and rich underwater world is an excellent diving site.

Issue price

Tours to Cuba from Moscow are quite popular, especially in the cold season, when you so want to change the grayness and mud of the cold capital to warm sand and the gentle sea. If you have ever been interested in this area, you probably know that this is not the most budget option. The lion's share of the cost of the tour falls on the flight, and living in a luxury hotel on an all-inclusive basis will be quite expensive.





On average, the cost of a tour lasting 10-12 days for two adult tourists starts from 100,000 rubles. It is much more profitable to plan your vacation yourself, buy plane tickets of the chosen airline, book a hotel or villa. This option allows you to save about 30% of the cost of the tour.

To summarize

Holguin (Cuba) is a great option for those who want to combine all the charms of a beach holiday with exploring a huge number of attractions of one of the most popular resorts. A fabulous and incredibly positive atmosphere of an endless holiday is what you need after a gray and dull Moscow with its frantic pace of life. Be sure to pay attention to this resort. Holguin will provide you a measured and relaxing holiday.
