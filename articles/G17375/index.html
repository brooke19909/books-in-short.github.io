Turkish Bodrum: reviews, description of the rest and interesting places of the resort

Can't decide what kind of vacation you want? Tranquility and solitude surrounded by trees, clean air and salty sea? Or maybe active, with excursions and exploring the hidden corners of an unknown city? Or is it still fun and fiery, with night bars, discos on the beach and a youth party awake until morning? Well, you do not have to dwell on one thing, if you choose Bodrum as the place of your vacation. The reviews of tourists from different countries confirm that this is a resort for everyone: for children and the elderly, for families and bachelors, for adventure seekers and lazy inhabitants of the beaches. Go?





So different but always beautiful Bodrum

The coast of the Aegean Sea is considered one of the best holiday destinations in Turkey. Everything is here and more! Probably the most atypical, but also attractive for spoiled tourists of Turkey resort - Bodrum. Reviews vacationers here are striking in their diversity. Some people with admiration recall the incendiary parties until the morning, night bars, seething with fun and energy of life, dark night streets lit by millions of bright lights, spectacular shows and much, much so much that young people who dream of discos and entertainment love so much. Others are happy to describe the sweet moments on the picturesque beaches and swimming in the cleanest waters of the Aegean Sea, the high level of service in hotels, the presence of entertainment in the water and on land, which do not let you get bored on vacation, where "all inclusive". Still others admire the atmosphere of the city, numerous sights telling about the history of Turkey and its culture. Everyone here has their own impressions. And all this is Turkey, Bodrum. Reviews only confirm the versatility of the resort, which for some reason is considered a hackneyed place.





Fun for party people

If you came to Bodrum for the nightlife, you made the absolutely right choice. This place is called the city of sleepless nights. Here is one of the coolest establishments in Turkey - Bebek, which is included in the top 50 of the most famous night bars in the world. And it is here that you will find yourself at the largest disco in all of Europe - Halicarnassus. Everyone who is interested in parties and clubs has heard about it. It offers an incredible transparent dance floor, under which the waves of the Aegean Sea have fun with you. And also water and foam parties, costume shows, rivers of booze and a lot of delicious food. You can watch the fun from the side from the many cozy bars, and if you want privacy, the lounge areas with pillows, a hookah and Turkish tea will open their doors for you.





Vacation for beach lovers

For those seeking a peaceful vacation at sea, each hotel has comfortable beaches with warm and clean sand, sun loungers and umbrellas, fresh fruits, cocktails (which will be prepared especially for you), as well as water activities. What can beach Bodrum have to offer you? Reviews of tourists tell about fascinating windsurfing and scuba diving, catamaran rides, breathtaking water skiing and sailing. Try it and you! In the evening, you can arrange a romantic evening in one of the local restaurants with excellent Turkish or Mediterranean cuisine.





Inspiration for creative people

Did you know that the favorite place of Turkey‚Äôs creative elite is Bodrum? Reviews of tourists confirm that there are a lot of artists, musicians, writers who are inspired by the nature and atmosphere of the city. And there really is something to see. Be sure to visit the Halicarnassus mausoleum, built by one Carian queen in memory of her husband. This building is considered the fifth wonder of the world, so take the opportunity to see it. Another picturesque place is the Bodrum Amphitheater, renovated in 2000. It offers a magnificent view of the panorama of the city. But how to get past the gates of Mindos, which once served as the entrance to Halicarnassus and protected it from strangers? As you can see, there is something to see here.





One road for everyone: to Bodrum!

For those who do not like the monotony, want a vivid impression of the holiday, all kinds of tours to Bodrum are offered. You can choose the option that suits you best, depending on who you are traveling with and for how long, what you expect from your vacation, and how you plan to spend time. Have a nice trip!
