The best hotels of Batumi

Batumi is a wonderful city located on the southeast coast of the Black Sea. It is known not only as the capital of Adjara, but is also a popular seaside resort. Every year the city is visited by many guests. When planning to visit the resort, everyone thinks about where to stay for a while. Currently, there is a fairly large selection of hotels in Batumi, among which you can choose the most suitable option. Hotel Intourist

For those travelers who appreciate, first of all, comfort and service, the Intourist Hotel is ideal. It belongs to five-star establishments and is incredibly comfortable for living. The Intourist Hotel is located at the address: Batumi, st. Ninoshvili, 11. The hotel has a long history, it was built in 1939, and in 2006 it was completely renovated. It is located in the very center of the city, just 50 meters from the beach. The complex is located near the promenade, from it you can walk to the most interesting places of Batumi.



The hotel has a fairly large assortment of rooms, among which are the following apartments:

  1. Standard.
  2. Improved number.
  3. Superior Apartment with Pool View.
  4. Deluxe
  5. Deluxe Suite.
  6. Suite with a terrace.

All rooms are equipped with wireless Internet, LCD TV, minibar, air conditioning and other pleasant little things that make your stay in the hotel comfortable. The apartments are decorated in a classic design and decorated in bright colors.

Seaside Boulevard is located next to the hotel, and the distance to the central square is only two hundred meters. Hotel guests have the opportunity to visit the pools and fitness center. The building also has a casino, a spa and wellness center with a sauna, hot tub and massage parlor. The hotel has an Italian restaurant and bar.

Guest reviews about the Intourist Hotel

Not all Batumi hotels have such a favorable location as the Intourist Hotel. The proximity to the sea makes the establishment quite popular. According to guests, the hotel is fully consistent with five stars. The comfortable rooms are equipped with everything you need, including cosmetics. On the roof of the building is a swimming pool with beautiful city views. The entrance to the complex is decorated with a fountain that glows at night.





The hotel guests note the friendly staff and recommend the establishment as the best hotel in Batumi.

"Prestige"

The Prestige Hotel is located at 62 May 26 Street. Bus and train stations are a five-minute drive from the hotel, and the airport takes ten minutes to get to the beach in five minutes. Due to the proximity of the hotel to the coast, it can be attributed to one of the most popular hotels in Batumi by the sea.

The three-star complex has free parking. The rooms are equipped with flat-screen TVs and air conditioning. All apartments have a modern design and are equipped with a seating area. In addition, each room has a balcony with beautiful views of the Caucasus Mountains or city skyline.

The Prestige Hotel has a restaurant, visitors can enjoy the best dishes of Turkish and Georgian cuisine, and the local bar will delight guests with a good assortment of Georgian wines.

Guest reviews about the hotel "Prestige"

Tourists staying at the Prestige Hotel note its convenient location. The hotel is quite young, it is noticeable that the number of rooms has recently been renovated, so the rooms are very clean and comfortable. Maids clean apartments and change towels daily. The price includes breakfast. According to guests, they are quite good.



A very friendly and welcoming staff makes an impression. The only drawback of the hotel is the lack of an elevator, with heavy bags it is quite difficult to climb the fifth floor. The hotel has an employee who helps to lift luggage to the room, but, unfortunately, he alone is not able to serve all guests. In general, a three-star establishment is worthy of attention from tourists, since the hotel can have a great vacation and fully enjoy your vacation.

Euphoria batumi

Fans of luxury establishments will appreciate the Euphoria Batumi. It is located at: Green Street, 1, New Boulevard. The complex opened its doors to visitors only in 2016, so guests are offered accommodation in new modern, incredibly comfortable rooms. The hotel apartments are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Some rooms have balconies or terraces with sea or mountain views. The hotel has free parking for guests. The guests of the establishment can use the outdoor and indoor pools, a conference room for events of different levels, dry cleaning and laundry. The hotel features a beauty salon, a la carte restaurant and bar, gym and spa. In general, the complex has a developed infrastructure corresponding to a five-star establishment.



Hotel Euphoria Batumi has an extensive number of rooms, in which there are apartments of various categories: standards, superior rooms, family suites, executive and presidential rooms.

Traveler Reviews for Staying at Euphoria Batumi

If you are exploring Batumi hotels on the eve of your vacation, then as a possible option for accommodation, you should pay attention to the new Euphoria Batumi hotel. Since its opening, the complex has established itself on the best side. Of course, the hotel has minor flaws and shortcomings, but overall the establishment pleases with its modern design and cleanliness of the rooms. Tourists leave the most positive reviews about the hotel staff and its wonderful spa with outdoor and indoor heated pools.

Hotel Hilton Batumi

Hilton Batumi is located at 40 Rustaveli Street. The modern hotel was built just 200 meters from the sea coast, surrounded by developed tourist infrastructure.

The hotel has an extensive number of rooms with 247 apartments, among which there are standards, suites, as well as executive rooms.

The hotel has several rooms where you can conduct business and leisure events at various levels. The complex also has a spa, fitness center, sauna, pool, restaurant and bar. Due to its convenient location, the complex is suitable not only for business trips, but also for a beach holiday.

Hilton Batumi Travel Reviews

Hilton Batumi is a new hotel, which opened only a few years ago, so it is pleasantly pleased with its beautiful modern interiors and fresh rooms. All apartments of the hotel are quite spacious, some of them are equipped with balconies, which is very convenient. There is a restaurant on the nineteenth floor of the building, where guests can order a variety of dishes not only from Georgian but also from European cuisine.



According to guests, the disadvantage of the hotel is the lack of an outdoor pool, as well as the beach itself. However, it is worth noting that not a single complex in Batumi has a plot on the coast. Tourists have the opportunity to use the municipal beaches of the city.

Guests at the Hilton Batumi are noted by the incredibly welcoming and friendly staff of the establishment. According to the guests, the hotel has a certain symbiosis of foreign service with Georgian national hospitality, which creates a unique atmosphere of the establishment. The hotel restaurant leaves no less pleasant impression. Numerous positive reviews about Hilton Batumi, gives reason to recommend the hotel to guests of the resort.

Hotel "Antika"

Batumi mini-hotels are very popular among resort guests. These institutions include the Antika Hotel. The three-star family-type complex has only twenty rooms at its disposal. It is located in the business center of the city. Despite its rather modest size, the Antika Hotel (Batumi) offers accommodation in standard rooms and spacious luxury apartments. The hotel is equipped with new TVs and air conditioning. It offers free Wi-Fi.

Hotel guests have the opportunity to have breakfast at a local cafe. In addition, the hotel has a restaurant with Georgian cuisine called Kalakuri.

Reviews about the Antika Hotel

Antika Hotel is located in the very center of Batumi, a fifteen minute walk from the coast. The guests of the establishment are very pleased with the number of rooms and the service at the hotel. Especially tourists note the hospitable and welcoming owners of the institution. Antika is one of those places that you want to visit again. The family-run hotel has a pleasant homely atmosphere that gives a feeling of comfort and warmth. A wonderful chef of the restaurant does not cease to please visitors with incredibly delicious national Georgian dishes.



All rooms of the mini-hotel are in good repair. The apartments are kept clean thanks to quality daily cleaning. Hotel "Antika" can be recommended to tourists who are not supporters of pathos, but prefer to relax in a cozy home atmosphere. The hotel is located at: Pushkinskaya street, 108.

Hulus hotel

Continuing the conversation, it is worth recalling the private hotels of Batumi, which are popular among vacationers. One of these places is Hulus. Hotel The beachfront hotel is located in Makhinajuri, just 50 meters from the beach. The advantage of the hotel is not only its proximity to the coast, but also to the train station, which can be reached in just five minutes. The hotel has an outdoor pool.



The hotel rooms are decorated in a classic style and decorated in warm colors. All apartments have their own bathroom and a balcony, which is incredibly convenient for a beach holiday. The hotel has a Georgian restaurant, a cafe with barbecue and a lounge bar.

Near the hotel there is a bus stop, from which you can reach the city center. Some remoteness from the central part of Batumi is more than compensated by the proximity of the coast, which is why tourists who go to the resort for the sake of the sea prefer the hotel.

The guests of the establishment are satisfied with the quality of service and the friendly attitude of the staff. Good rooms on the Black Sea coast and incredibly delicious Georgian dishes are the key to a good rest.
