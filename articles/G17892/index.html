Beach

Beach towels here can be taken without restrictions, and the beach itself is very clean, with fine white sand, which is simply magnificent. The best places for a beach holiday in the world are located in the Maldives - clear water, a bewitching bottom, calm atmosphere and complete relaxation.

There is a pier on the beach and at 5:00 in the morning you can see how predatory mantas are fed. In the afternoon, they swim far, and vacationers can only see the fins. On the border of a small embankment and a cliff, you can swim with masks looking at very beautiful corals, various fish and moray eels.

Diving

For high-class divers and connoisseurs of diving, this place will fit perfectly and leave a lot of memorable sensations. The island is located near the coastal coral atolls, which makes it possible to get to the delightfully beautiful sites simply by swimming. Thousands of divers come exclusively to the Maldives. Having mastered the diving school, which is in the hotel, everyone will be able to sink to the bottom. Auspicious and graceful lagoons have become home to many striking residents. These are intricate corals, graceful mollusks, large jellyfish, various sizes of shrimp, crabs, lobsters. And it is not surprising that most of the aquarium fish were caught directly in this place. Tropical fish alone, there are more than a thousand species. Outlandish starfish of the most extraordinary colors are strewn with the bottom. Among them, especially the jellyfish "Crown of Christ." Interesting inhabitants are sea urchins and cucumbers. The latter pose a threat to vacationers, as they have large, pointed and dangerous needles. Carelessly stepping on it, you can drive the needles into your leg, which is very difficult to pull out. In addition, a dangerous stone fish lives in the water. Having become on it, you can get a powerful burn, which heals for a long time. Diving and swimming with slopes will leave a lot of impressions. In the Maldives, corals are considered public domain. In the daytime, they look ugly, and under the cover of night they bloom with unimaginable multicolor bouquets, night corals present a fantastic picture!
