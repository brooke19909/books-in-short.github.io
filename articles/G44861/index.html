Whala Bavaro 3 * (Dominican Republic / Punta Cana): reviews, description

The coast of the best Dominican resort of Punta Cana, where the Whala Bavaro 3 * hotel is located, is framed by snowdrifts. This is the impression when you look at the island of Haiti from above. The coastline surrounding it is covered with dazzling white sand, contrasting with the bright blue of the welcoming ocean.

The hotel does not impress with its luxurious interiors, but the natural beauties of its territory lead every tourist to an indescribable delight. The coast, shining with the whiteness of pearl sands, is strewn with coconut trees. Comprehensive silence and the desired calm reigned over him.





Punta Cana has provided shelter to the world's leading hotel chains, which include the Whala Bavaro 3 * hotel complex, which has settled on Bavaro Beach. Youth companies, tourists with families and fans of active marine entertainment prefer to relax in a hotel with a democratic atmosphere.

Hotel location

The hotel complex is located on the island of Haiti, 22 kilometers from Punta Cana Airport. It occupies part of the first sea line on the beach of Bavaro with unique snow-white coral sands. From Hotel Whala Bavaro 3 * to the ocean 100 meters. It is adjacent to two golf clubs - Cocotal and The Lakes Barcelo.

Next to it is the Palma Real shopping area. In addition, it is surrounded by many shops (in two of them tourists pay in rubles). An entertainment venue with a disco and a casino is located two kilometers from the hotel. Within walking distance from it, on the coast, a hundred meters from the sea, Dolphin Island Dolphinarium was built.

Description of the infrastructure of the hotel complex

The hotel belongs to a large resort complex, combining in addition to it three more hotels, connected by pools. Three three-story buildings, immersed in the shade of luxurious tropical vegetation, surround a huge pool, framed by a terrace. The hotel underwent a major renovation in 2012, which significantly changed its appearance. Now the well-kept rooms of the hotel look modern and fresh.





Thanks to renovation, 13 additional apartments equipped with new equipment appeared in it. The facade of the building was restored, the pool area was expanded, and sun loungers with umbrellas were placed on the resulting free space (beach accessories are not charged). The restaurants have updated furniture. The rooms have created modern interiors, replaced mirrors and installed new plumbing.

In the lobby of the hotel Whala Bavaro 3 * (Punta Cana) created a bar and spent wireless Internet. There is no internet in the rooms, but in public areas it is paid. In addition, the hotel has a Beach Club, which has a snack bar. The hotel territory was equipped with a parking lot, there was allocated a place for a mini-market and a souvenir shop on it. The quality of service brought to a higher level.

On call guests are provided with a doctor whose services are paid. Guests exchange money at a foreign exchange office and use the laundry service after paying for its services. The hotel has two freshwater pools . They have a section for bathing children. A smoking area has been created for smokers.

Description of apartments

All apartments in the hotel are non-smoking travelers. The comfort of the rooms corresponds to the stardom of the hotel Whala Bavaro 3 * (Punta Cana). They created a cozy atmosphere and provided amenities for a great budget holiday on the famous exotic coast. Children under three years stay in rooms for free. Baby cots are available on request.





The hotel is equipped with 22 connecting rooms. It has standard junior suites and apartments, including a bedroom, a guest room, a bathtub, a jacuzzi and an isolated toilet. There are no apartments for people with disabilities.

It features multilingual staff. The rooms are cleaned daily. Maids timely change the used towels and linen (a clean bed is covered in a day), produce wet cleaning.

The apartments overlook the pool and have spacious balconies. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, fans, paid safes and minibars, TVs. The rooms have satellite TV. Some apartments have a sofa bed. Bathrooms include a hairdryer and hygiene products. They have a shower or bath.

Beach vacation





Tourists relax on their own beach spit, sheltered by natural coral sand. From the buildings to the beach a hundred meters. Tourists use all beach accessories (umbrellas with sun loungers, towels and mattresses) for free. Coral reefs reliably protect the beach line from strong waves rising high upward. There is a bar on the beach serving snacks, fruits and drinks.

Hotel restaurants catering system

Guests are happy to use the All Inclusive catering system in force at the Whala Bavaro 3 * hotel. The price includes a variety of drinks, buffet served for breakfast, lunch and dinner, afternoon snacks. Food in restaurants is prepared with locally produced products.

The range of hotel restaurants, without spoiling a special variety of dishes, delights visitors with their taste and nutritional value. The taste of juices and fruits is very different from those inherent in goods from stores. Fragrant juicy fruits ripened under the rays of the tropical sun, and drinks from them have an amazing taste. Their use gives true pleasure.

Features of restaurants and bars

Restaurant Rompeolas specializes in international cuisine. Once a week it hosts a themed dinner. A restaurant at the Beach Club invites you to barbecue once a week. A La Carte serves Dominican food. Amazing drinks and light snacks are served in three bars - on the beach, in the hotel lobby and in the Orquidea's bar.





Surrounded by the Whala Bavaro 3 * hotel, in Punta Cana, there are many restaurants and cafes, where chefs, expertly skilled in the intricacies of gastronomy, indulge guests with all kinds of dishes from different cuisines of the world. Entertainment

The absence of excessive civilization made this corner desirable for travelers who crave rest in solitude, peace and quiet, in a word, where there are conditions for complete relaxation and relaxation. In addition to the luxurious beach, covered with velvety sand, and the sea, breezing with a light breeze, of course, there are active entertainments in the Republic of Dominican Republic.

3 * Whala Bavaro settled in Punta Cana - in a town with a vibrant nightlife. Dozens of clubs with a hot atmosphere give pleasure to those in whose veins hot blood flows. Tourists outside the hotel play golf, learn horseback riding, and explore the sights of the Dominican Republic.

The hotel has several types of free outdoor activities. Tourists are offered to join the participation in daily animation programs. Guests are engaged in aerobics, snorkeling (diving with a mask and snorkel into the water, getting to know the beauty of the rich underwater kingdom), playing beach volleyball.





They swim on the water surface of the sea in kayaks (inflatable boats) and catch a wave while windsurfing. They have a fitness center and gym. Separate payment is required for water skiing, catamarans, marine motor transport, diving lessons; treatments in the spa, wellness center and massage room.

Extreme vacation

The beach, stretching along the hotel, as if created for extreme relaxation. Tourists who love the thrill, are not only engaged in snorkeling, diving, windsurfing and parasailing. They go to Manati Nature Park to observe the behavior of wildlife in the natural environment.

Many vacationers embark on fascinating jeep safaris, the route of which is laid along sugar cane, coffee and cocoa plantations. Jeeps roar them through the eucalyptus jungle, making stops in typical Dominican villages where residents offer guests to taste fresh tropical fruits that have just been picked from tree branches.





Vacationers get an unforgettable experience of communication and swimming with dolphins in the marine park Dolphin Island. There are always a lot of visitors to the dolphinarium who came to rest in Whala Bavaro 3 *. A photo of dolphins allows you to permanently keep in your memory pleasant moments from swimming with huge good-natured animals.
