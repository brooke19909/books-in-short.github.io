Image for a photo shoot - original options

Each of us has photos. Color and black-and-white, funny and not-so - from year to year the photos are put into albums so that one of the evenings we can remember the past, perhaps relive past emotions once again, share them with friends or relatives. These stories are different, and the photos may not be bad, but for the most part they are similar in the absence of that highlight, only the background and people change.

The strongest impressions and feelings are caused by the pictures taken by the real master of photography. And if the image is also selected successful and original, the result can exceed all expectations. The choice of a certain role is a creative process, there is no definite framework, but a certain style must be sustained, otherwise the image for a photo shoot risks becoming a faceless mask. Retro style



In old photographs, there was always something that beckons, attracts glances and awakens nostalgia for bygone times. Plunging into the space of the old fashion, a person complements his taste, develops a sense of style, and the process of selecting an image, searching for clothes, accessories and actual makeup sometimes captures more than the photo shoot itself.

There are so many variations in retro style that can not be counted on the fingers. This is Marilyn Monroe, and the young ladies of the times described by Russian classics, and gangsters of the beginning of the last century - all that remains is to choose what is to your liking, and the photographer will find the necessary moments, capturing frozen images.

Greek style



If you are attracted to the myths of ancient Greece, then perhaps it will be interesting for you to try on the image of one of the gods living on Olympus. Light bright clothes, laurel wreaths, fragments of columns, stylization of expensive jewelry - these and other elements will allow for several hours to turn the photo studio into a corner of the ancient state. The ancient Greek image for a photo shoot involves light and natural makeup, hair can be laid in lush curls.

Military style



This is a great opportunity to create an original image for a photo shoot in the studio or outdoors. In addition, this is a great theme gift for a holiday for him or her - the military is in perfect harmony with both courage and courage, and with femininity, beauty and, of course, sexuality.

There are many original ideas to create an image for a military-style photo shoot. Determine the era you like, its accessories, military uniforms, possibly even equipment, find a good photographer, and bright unusual frames for your photo album (and, possibly, portfolio) are provided.

Photoshoot with flowers

An option that will not leave any lady indifferent - with flowers. You can create the necessary conditions in the studio: fill the free space with flowers so that there are no catchy voids in the frame, prepare the appropriate lighting. The image for a photo shoot on the street in this version, of course, looks preferable. Pick colors that match your hair, makeup, and skin tone. Makeup can be done both natural and in bright colorful colors, in harmony with the selected colors.
