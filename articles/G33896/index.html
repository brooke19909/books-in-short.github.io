Monoblock air conditioner - convenient solution

Monoblock air conditioner is one of those solutions, during the installation of which the user should not have any difficulties. Such a solution does not need any special fasteners, for the installation of this device there is no need for the construction of a complex air exhaust system. Monoblock air conditioning, you can easily drag from one room to another, it can even be moved from a city apartment to the cottage, if the need arises.



The installation of such a device is associated with only one problem - you will need to bring out only the corrugated hose. Conveniently, it can be displayed in a slightly ajar window. However, in this case, the share of the air conditioner's power will be spent on cooling the street, which is not entirely good for you. It is also worth noting that the duct hose that sticks out of the window does not look aesthetically pleasing. You can solve this problem by ordering a special plastic window with a special design.



To make a hole in a double-glazed window is practically impossible. It is required to order a similar window with a double-glazed window so that it is tightly closed and does not open. Its lower part should be covered with plastic about twenty centimeters. It is appropriate to use any thickness of plastic, a prerequisite is that it can be easily cut. It turns out that in the absence of the ability to make a hole for the air duct in the glass itself, you can make it in plastic. It will look much more aesthetic than just a hose looking out of a window. In addition, the monoblock air conditioner mounted in this way eliminates the need for any specialized knowledge. However, those people who will be engaged in the manufacture of windows will need them, since the latter will not be of a completely standard design.



Monoblock air conditioner is a device in which technical details are located in one housing. Thanks to this, the design becomes noticeably simpler and, accordingly, cheaper. Among such devices can be distinguished window, roof and mobile. Window structures can be mounted on a wall or to a window. This is due to the fact that it is convenient to transport it, move it from room to room, while there is no need to order expensive installation, which becomes only more expensive with every kilometer that separates the summer cottage from the city.

The monoblock floor-mounted air conditioner is an excellent solution, but it is not without drawbacks. Here, the main disadvantage is the high noise level during operation (in comparison with split systems), which is critical for many.
