Movenpick Beach Resort Taba 5 - Amazing 5 Star Hotel

Description: Movenpick Beach Resort Taba 5 is an exotic five-star hotel located between the extraordinary beauty of the Sinai Mountains and the bay called Aqaba. The hotel is only five to ten minutes away by car from the border with Israel, Saudi Arabia and Jordan. Eilat's Israel International Airport is eight kilometers from this hotel.





Rooms: Movenpick Beach Resort Taba 5 has a variety of room types: Standard, Bungalows, Deluxe, Superior, Executive Rooms, Family Rooms, Suites. In total, the hotel has four hundred and twenty-four stylish rooms for living. In the rooms, travelers find all the amenities necessary for a memorable and first-class stay. These are modern soft beds, air conditioning, cable TV with a wide selection of music and other channels, a reliable safe for securities and things, a desk, a telephone through which tourists can call wherever they want, a free minibar with all kinds of soft drinks, and a balcony with the furniture. In the bathroom you will find a toilet, shower, washbasin, hairdryer and hygiene items. At the request of the tourist, cots are available in the hotel room. Thorough cleaning is done daily. Linen is changed three times a week.

Meals: On the territory of Movenpick Beach Resort Taba 5 guests will be pleasantly surprised by a considerable number of restaurants serving delicious and delicious dishes of Egyptian, Chinese, Lebanese, as well as international cuisine. All of them are prepared by highly professional chefs. Tourists can easily order delicious snacks, cocktails, coffee, alcoholic and cold drinks at the Mermaid and Pharaoh's bars located on the beach and by the pool.





Beach: An equipped sandy beach four hundred meters long is located directly in front of the Movenpick Beach Resort Taba 5 and is his personal property. Sun loungers, towels, mattresses and umbrellas are provided free of charge to everyone. On the beach, tourists have the opportunity to simply sunbathe, and learn to snorkel and diving, while gaining a ton of new experiences.

Holiday Information: All Movenpick Beach Resort Taba guests can visit three pools (one of which is indoor), a sauna, a jacuzzi, a fitness center, treatment rooms, and a steam room. At the hotel you can freely play billiards, darts, volleyball, table tennis. Any tourist has the opportunity to get high-speed access to Wi-Fi Internet, use the conference room for business negotiations. At the request of all hotel guests, the following services are provided: doctors, hairdressers, masseurs, babysitters and laundry facilities. Palm Beach Casino is also located on site, where you can have fun.





Reviews: The excellent Movenpick Beach Resort Taba 5 hotel, which is only positive reviews, has an undeniable reputation. He does not leave indifferent neither families with children, nor youth, nor elderly and business people, because his wide range of services is able to satisfy the needs of any population groups. Travelers are delighted with the peaceful atmosphere prevailing in spacious rooms, polite staff, convenient location of the hotel and reasonable prices.
