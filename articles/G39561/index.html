Since the days of the Soviet Union, the Balkan countries were considered the most optimal place for a quality beach holiday. Magnificent natural and environmental conditions, the atmosphere of cordiality and friendliness, southern flavor, excellent beaches and a great many comfortable hotels. Today, after the collapse of the socialist camp and the acquisition of sovereignty by the Balkan countries, the Black Sea, Mediterranean and Adriatic coasts of these countries have acquired a more respectable appearance. Around the perimeter of the resort towns, many modern hotel complexes have risen, for example, Dolce Vita 4 * (Bulgaria / Varna / Golden Sands) and others, which have begun to provide tourists with European-level service, and at affordable prices. This determines the popularity of these resorts among residents of the CIS countries and Europe.

Montenegro, Bebici

All over the world Montenegro is called so romantic and exotic - Montenegro. And not only its name, but also the natural landscapes and picturesque settlements of this small South Slavic country are beautiful and attractive to numerous tourists from various countries. By its nature, Montenegro is a bit like the southern coast of Crimea. The hotel fund is not so developed here, so fans of super-comfortable conditions are unlikely to find a suitable deluxe hotel here. But the resorts of Montenegro will appeal to lovers of a quiet and secluded vacation. The most popular resort area is the Budva Riviera. It is here, in the village of Becici, 4 kilometers from Budva, that the 4 * Dolce Vita hotel is located. Unlike more secluded villages, this resort has a superbly developed infrastructure. The beach, although not sandy, is finely pebble and very clean. All along the coast there are restaurants, bars, cafes, shops and souvenir shops, discos, as well as guest houses, villas and hotels of various categories: from two-star to five-star. In principle, the latter are not so many. For a moderately comfortable stay, most tourists choose four-star hotels, such as Dolce Vita 4 * (Becici).

General description of the hotel

As already mentioned, 4 stars appear on the facade of this hotel next to the name. Hotel Dolce Vita 4 * was opened in 2009. However, today, after 5 years, it continues to remain fresh neat both outside and inside. The hotel is a snow-white seven-story villa with an elevator. Since it has a heating system, the doors of the hotel are open to vacationers all year round. This means that you can relax in the 4 * Dolce Vita outside the beach season. Especially popular are the New Year tours, when dozens of tourists from different countries come here for a lot of positive emotions and unforgettable evenings.



Location

Dolce Vita 4 * (Montenegro) is located on a small hill in the village of Becici. From it to the sea only 150 meters. Next to it are the Splendid and Mediterian hotels, as well as a modern, well-equipped water park. The nearest airport is Tivat (25 km), and the capital, in Podgorica, is 60 kilometers away.

Holidays in Bulgaria: Golden Sands resort

Varna is a large port city on the Black Sea. It is here, more precisely, in its suburbs, the most prestigious of the Bulgarian resorts - Golden Sands. From its name it already becomes clear that the beaches of the coast are distinguished by the fact that they are covered with magnificent, golden sand sparkling in the sun. Over the past decade, infrastructure has largely improved. Even the old hotels underwent reconstruction and began to look new and modern. However, this does not apply to the Riu Dolce Vita 4 * hotel, built in 2011. He is still very young and has a great layout.
