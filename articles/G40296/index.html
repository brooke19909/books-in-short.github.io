Cape Panwa Hotel (Thailand): description, rooms and reviews

Cape Panwa Hotel is an exclusive beachfront hotel complex in Thailand located on Phuket Island. It is designed for people who seek solitude and relaxation. The hotel is a network hotel belonging to the Cape And Cantari line. In Thailand, it is considered a five-star, although according to international classification it is more likely to be positioned as the "four". It is not new, built in the eighties of the last century. But the hotel is constantly undergoing a renewal process, which, by the way, does not interfere with the rest. Maybe that's why the services, living conditions, beach, location and food at this hotel, the majority of tourists, even quite picky and spoiled by various exclusives, evaluates as impeccable. Now we will find out if this is so.



What is Panwa Beach?

This resort region of Phuket is located in the southeast of the island. Previously, there was a fishing village, but now they have built a beautiful promenade, an aquarium and many hotels, cafes and restaurants. But even now there are quite a lot of long-tail boats near the cape, which depart in the morning for a catch. The beaches at the resort are not very wide, five to six meters. But the views from the hills of Panwa are very beautiful - both on the coast of Phuket and on the nearby islands. There are not many people here, and the resort is very popular with privacy lovers. After all, not only party people, but also romantics come to rest in Thailand. Cape Panwa Hotel is an ideal place for travelers like this. And if you want to go to some island, then any fisherman can take you there for a price that you will agree among themselves. You can go here all year round. Location of the Cape Panwa Hotel

As we already mentioned, Cape Panwa Hotel is located on Cape Panwa. This is a fairly gentle hill with a tropical garden. It takes about forty-five minutes to get to Phuket Airport from the hotel by transfer. Patong can be reached in half an hour. To the capital of the island, Phuket Town, about twenty minutes. The location of the hotel can be considered the best in the entire resort of Panwa Beach. Around the hotel there are many massage parlors with different prices. Of course, the hotel is secluded, but many travelers believe that this is its comfort. After all, almost everything you need can be found without going beyond its gates.



Hotel Territory

The hotel complex Cape Panwa Hotel is surrounded by lush flowering vegetation. This is a four-story building with an elevator, from which a cable car goes down to the beach. In addition to the main building, among the hotel premises there are bungalows and villas. The architectural concept of the hotel feels the quality factor and style. Since the hotel is remote from large villages, its infrastructure includes shops - both grocery and souvenir. There is even a shopping center. Here you can buy various little things for the beach. Rooms

The Cape Panwa Hotel is quite large. It consists of two hundred nineteen rooms. The vast majority of them - 166 - belong to the category of superior. There are also fourteen suites, six bungalows and one villa. Since the hotel is located on a hill, most rooms have excellent sea views. When you get up in the morning, then all the beauty of Thailand is revealed to you. Each room has a huge balcony with sun loungers. Each guest room has a flat-screen TV and DVD player, minibar and safety deposit box. There is tea, coffee, sugar. Bathrobes and slippers are provided for the shower. Guests are put a special bag for the beach and a half-liter bottle of drinking water daily. One of the suites is built as a two-bedroom penthouse. Villas have their own pools. They are also divided into two categories - with two or three bedrooms. The rooms are very spacious, the plumbing is working, the bedding is new.
