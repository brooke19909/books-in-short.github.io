Apple has a unique service. It's about iCloud. What is this And what is the appropriate option used for?

ICloud Cloud is a service that allows you to synchronize data with devices of a particular Apple ID. With it, you can transfer information from your phone or tablet to new "apple" devices, as well as view them or restore if necessary.

Apple's cloud service is a common, albeit very convenient, data warehouse. When configuring this option, a person will be able to choose how to save and synchronize information. We will talk about this later.

About registration

How to log in to the iCloud cloud? First you have to register here. But how?

iCloud is a service from Apple. He uses Apple ID for work. There is no and cannot be any separate registration in the cloud service. Therefore, all that is required for the owner of the "apple" device is to get yourself an Apple ID.

Turn on on your mobile device

How to see the cloud on the iPhone? We have already said that a separate account and registration for working with iCloud is not required. Instead, you have to be puzzled by the creation of Apple ID. Suppose that one or another user already has such an account. What's next?

Entrance to "Iklaud" from the "iPhone" is as follows:

  1. Turn on your mobile device and look at the main menu of the device.
  2. Go to the "Settings" section.
  3. Find and click on iCloud.
  4. Tap on the line with an example e-mail.
  5. Specify a password to log in to Apple ID.
  6. In the field "Required" enter data from the "apple" identifier. Namely, the password for authorization in the system.
  7. Click on the sign "Login".
  8. Agree or refuse to attach Safari to iCloud.
  9. Set up geolocation. Usually just tap on the inscription "OK".

That's all. Now you can work with the cloud service from Apple. There is nothing difficult about this.
