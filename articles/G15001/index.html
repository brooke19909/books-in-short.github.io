Do you know how to charge your phone’s battery properly?

The initially correctly charged battery of a mobile phone is able to work for quite a long time.

The correct answer to an important question

The new battery of the phone needs to be properly charged and calibrated by the battery management system. Therefore, the phone you just bought should not be "strained" without having subjected it to the process of charging to the full volume of the battery capacity. It is important to always recharge the battery on time and adhere to a specific “charge” pattern.

So, how to charge the phone battery? We offer you step-by-step instructions:

1. Despite the fact that the indicator can show a level of battery activity that is quite acceptable for use, nevertheless, the capacity of the capacities should be brought to its full extent.

2. After the first charging cycle, in no case do you charge the device until the battery of the phone runs out completely.

3. The new battery must be subjected to 2-3 cycles of full discharge and charge.

4. The next calibration step must be carried out after 3-4 months of operation of the power source of the mobile device.

How to charge the phone’s battery directly?



There are times when the original memory is not at hand or the telephone function responsible for recharging the battery is not functional. In this case, a universal device, known to many as a “frog”, will help. Probably not everyone knows how to charge the phone’s battery with a frog correctly? Therefore, the description of the process below will significantly reduce the risk of “killing" the working battery.

Firstly, the practical solution to the question of how to properly charge the phone’s battery with a frog must comply with safety rules. Pay special attention to the polarity of the battery: it must correspond to the pole. Usually on the device there is a designation of plus and minus contacts.

Secondly, the charging time should not exceed 2 hours, since the “frog” charging process is much more intensive and not as correct as when using the original charger. A rechargeable battery can have varying degrees of wear and tear, so it is advisable to periodically check the condition of the battery and prevent the moment of overheating of the tanks.

This is worth knowing!





Given the importance of the question of how to properly charge the phone’s battery, you should always remember: use a universal charger (“frog”) should only be in special cases when the need for the battery’s working condition is very high and extremely in demand. In normal mode, you should always charge the phone’s power source only when the charge level drops below 20% of the full volume. Do not allow moments of systematic recharge of the battery, this reduces the battery life and often causes the battery controller to fail. Considering the temperature factor of influence on the battery, it should be remembered: in the talk or standby mode, the battery potential drops significantly. It goes without saying that charging the phone from the cold is unacceptable, as the consequences can be unpredictable.

Finally

Well, we hope that the question “How to properly charge the phone’s battery?” For you it became more clear. But still. It should be noted that the simultaneous charging process and intensive use of the phone become a frequent cause of failure of the mobile device’s energy source: games, “heavy-duty” SMS, Internet surfing, talking or taking pictures. Using the original memory, constantly monitor the working status of its connector. Backlash, the moment of loosening or other mechanical defect of the memory slot can be the cause of failure of not only the battery, but also the mobile device as a whole.

Often, poor contact in the outlet leads to intermittent charging pulses to the battery, which leads to premature wear of the cell phone’s battery, in addition, the charger overheats due to poor contact, which can lead to battery failure.

Follow the above rules and recommendations, and your battery will work for a long time and flawlessly.
