Ancient art

It seems that the dolls always accompanied the person. Archaeologists find artifacts that are more than four thousand years old. Not all types of this folk art have survived, but the Japanese amigurumi dolls succeeded. Now, big-eyed knitted men are an indispensable part of kawaii fashion, which began with Japanese cartoons.

These toys are no more than eight centimeters in height. The head is traditionally done more than usual. A classic example is Pok√©mon. All amigurumi are good. Even if it is animals or home decoration. A smile adorns everyone, says eastern wisdom.

To tie such beautiful dolls with your own hands, you will need thick yarn and a thin hook. Crochet in a circle starting with three loops, rolled into a ringlet. A knitted fabric is stuffed with synthetic winterizer and sewn with cotton threads - they are completely invisible against the background of yarn. Now you need to give the toy a name. And even in the case of a sale, it will remain with her - this is a feature of amigurumi.

Tilde

Those who have never made toys, it is better to start by sewing Tilda. It was invented by a Norwegian artist. Tilda has long, thin arms and legs. Its feature is a flat profile - this is how the pattern is conceived. Tilde design can be almost any: girls, boys, bunnies, cubs and kittens.

A special class of dolls is interior. They serve as decoration and most often live with collectors. These are not only knitted or fabric dolls, but also made of polymer clay. They can be similar to figurines, but can be made in human height and sit in a chair. Such decorate shop windows. Pumpkin Head

These cute dolls got their name for a large head on a thin neck. They usually have very large eyes and long hair. It is made both on a wire frame and without it. These dolls are quite large, so they can be put on the windowsill or on the sofa. But there are also little girls - they are sewn with a frame inside.

The face is painted with acrylics. Eyes are made with large eyelashes. They can look away. The author can give the face of the Pumpkinhead a special expression - funny, thoughtful, the doll can smile or tighten her lips and even bite her lip. This gives her character.

For the doll they make accessories - a book, a kitten, a handbag, a backpack. The doll can be dressed in the form of a flight attendant, nurse, skier or tourist - all according to the author‚Äôs plan. Such dolls are a good gift.

Instructions for sewing Waldorf dolls

This doll is traditionally handmade from natural fabrics. A special feature is the knitted skin under which the doll‚Äôs body is pulled in a special way.

Pattern dolls from fabric. Sizes are standard:

  • height - 20 centimeters (of which 8 centimeters per head);
  • body width - 5 centimeters;
  • the width of the handles is 2.3 centimeters;
  • the width of the legs is 2.5 centimeters;
  • foot width - 1.7 centimeters.

Build Order:

  1. For the head, use a skein of yarn (preferably white, so as not to fade). It is pulled at the eye level of the doll. Attach a ball of wool with a diameter of a centimeter to the place of the nose. After that, a knitted pipe is put on the ball and tied so that a neck is obtained.
  2. According to a sewing pattern, the body is stuffed with a synthetic winterizer. Places bend handles and legs stitch. Fingers on the palms prostrate.
  3. Stitch the body and head.
  4. Shoes are sewn on the legs, put on and sew.
  5. Hair is made from yarn, sewn in a circle, braid a pigtail.
  6. Sew a dress and put on a chrysalis.
  7. Two dots are put on the face and a blush is applied.

Professional Tips

Surely you had to meet ugly dolls with crooked faces. Sometimes in a toy store, sometimes in an interior furniture store, and sometimes in a supermarket. Such poor things were done in the stream, without investing a soul. The artist, answering the question of how to sew a beautiful doll, will first of all pay attention to the need for love for his creation. Then it turns out to conduct the seams more accurately, and redoing something is not difficult.

In the manufacture of dolls, it is sometimes difficult to twist small details. To do this, artists use the method of a cocktail tube - insert it into the part, and then turn it out with the help of a thread drawn through the tube.

In textile techniques, it is important to dye the fabric that goes to the body of the pupa. Otherwise, it will become like a geisha. They are painted with an aqueous solution of tea, coffee and glue. Stand for some time and dried in the air or in the oven - it‚Äôs faster.
