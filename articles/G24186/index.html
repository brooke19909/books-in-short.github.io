Checking the balance of the discount card

Ile de Beaute has four methods for checking the total accumulated amount and interest on the card.

The first way to check the balance of the Ile de Beaute card is through the official website: www.iledebeaute.ru/mycard. In the corresponding field enter the numbers indicated on the back of the plastic card. After filling in the field, click the "Check accumulation" button. The site page will show information about the size of the current discount and the total accumulated amount from purchases.

The second option on how to check the balance of the Il de Bote discount card is to do it at the cash desk of the store. The consultant will provide detailed information and additionally notify about ongoing promotions and sales.

The third way is through the mobile application. To do this, download it to your smartphone, fill out a short registration form and activate the barcode number of the plastic discount card. The amount of savings will be indicated in the "Card" section. If there is no card, the application allows you to issue a virtual card, which is used when paying for purchases in the application itself and on the company's website. When placing the first order, the application issues a card with a 10% discount. The discount increases according to the same principle as with a plastic card.

The last way to check the balance of the Ile de Beaute card is to call the phone number. The consultant will tell you about the status and current balance of the card.

Gift program

A gift or gift card is a ready-made option for those who doubt the preferences of the person being gifted. Such a card is a monetary equivalent, and with it you can choose what a person will like.

A client can buy a card of any face value, starting from 500 rubles and ending with 100,000. The card has a limited validity period, indicated on the reverse side. The customer is offered the following gift card options:

  • for the purchase of any product;
  • to purchase products of a certain brand;
  • for day or evening makeup with a face value of 3,000 or 4,500 rubles, respectively;
  • for an eyebrow care service or Benefit brand products with a face value of 1,500 rubles.

When using a card, you can buy goods for a larger or smaller amount. If the limit is exceeded, the difference will need to be paid. If the purchase amount is less than the face value of the card, the balance can be spent on the following purchases.

Gift Card Balance Check

In contrast to the discount one, there are only two ways to check the balance on the Il de Bote gift card.

The first option is to find out the balance from the consultant at the cash desk of the store. The second way to check the balance of the Il de Bote gift card is to make a phone call. The consultant will inform you in detail about the status and current balance of the card.
