Bakhchisaray hotels: review, reviews, photos

In Bakhchisarai, hotels are located mainly in the central part of a small ancient city, not far from the main attraction - the Khan's Palace.

Arriving in this city, one must take into account that hotels are often small, but the modest size pays off with the friendliness of the reception, cordiality and kindness.

general characteristics

In Bakhchisarai, hotels will cost tourists inexpensively, the minimum room rate for 1 person in 2018 is 500 rubles.

Tourists who choose to visit the old town in the foothills are given the opportunity to live in a variety of hotels:

  • there are mini-hotels;
  • recreation centers;
  • hotels and hotels;
  • hostels
  • guest houses.

Pools are a rarity for Bakhchisarai hotels, but, according to tourists, this does not prevent a good rest.

In Bakhchisarai, hotels provide free Internet services, many organize fishing, horseback riding, excursions, photo shoots. There are many picturesque places, historical and cultural monuments in the vicinity of Bakhchisaray, so tourists take away beautiful photos about the trip.

You can choose a suitable hotel on the network resources where photos of rooms are presented, or get acquainted with the website of a particular hotel.

Mini-Hotel "Alie"

Hotel "Aliye" is located in Bakhchisarai on the street. L. Ukrainka, 1.

This is the center of the old city, nearby is the Khan's Palace - the main attraction of the city.

The mini-hotel is decorated in the style of the Crimean Tatar town: whitewashed walls, wooden balconies with a beautiful view of the city. At the same time, the level of service is quite European: there is free parking, in a teahouse, arranged in a shady garden, you can drink tea, wi-fi is available.

The issue of nutrition is solved in the hotel restaurant, which serves Crimean Tatar and European cuisine.

Hotel Isabella

At the entrance to Bakhchisaray on the highway "Tavrida" is the hotel "Isabella" (Bakhchisaray, st. Promyshlennaya, 3 a).

42 comfortable rooms are located in 2 buildings and detached wooden cottages.

The hotel is convenient to stay when you are on vacation or on business. The complex has a conference room and a wellness center with a sauna, billiards, gym, parking, restaurant and cafe.

For those who want to get acquainted with the beauties of Crimea, hotel staff prepared excursion programs, bicycle rental is offered. Tourists leave good reviews about these services.

Hotel "Prival"

Since 1922, the recreation center and the Priv Hotel in Bakhchisarai have been hosting guests. The area is surrounded by a grove of fragrant juniper; A variety of sports fields, an outdoor pool and secluded gazebos make outdoor activities exciting and enjoyable.

The hotel "Prival" is chosen by lovers to relax in comfort. The hotel has a sauna, a restaurant, a shooting range, a games room, as well as a medical center "Paradise Park and K", which offers a variety of wellness treatments.

The hotel is suitable for outdoor enthusiasts:

  • 26 variants of sightseeing tours across Crimea lasting from 2 to 7 days are offered to guests;
  • There is a rental of sports equipment.
