Small update

Nothing cardinal - repaint a thing, for example, or add applique, frills, bows from satin ribbons. On old skirts, patterns from sequins will look interesting, and on jeans you can make holes and scuffs. If you are considering how to alter linen trousers, then try decorating them at the side seams with cotton lace.



New clothes

It can be done in two ways - slightly adjust or alter old things completely. In the first version, midi and mini skirts are obtained from long skirts and dresses.
