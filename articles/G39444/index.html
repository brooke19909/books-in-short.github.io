Grand Yazici Club Turban 5 *, Marmaris: photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

If you, like tens of thousands of residents of Russia and other states of the former Soviet Union, love the magnificent resort region of Turkey - Marmaris, or if you want to discover this paradise for the first time, then the magnificent Grand Yazici Club Turban hotel will be an excellent place to stay . Let's find out today what this hotel complex offers its guests and at what price. We will also find out what impressions the rest at this hotel made on our compatriots.





Where is the hotel complex "Grand Yazitsi Club Turban 5 *"

This magnificent hotel is located on a picturesque seafront, between the center of the tourist region - Marmaris - and the small resort village of Icmeler. Nearby is a pine forest, the aroma of coniferous trees of which in combination with sea air makes the local microclimate very useful for health. The nearest international air harbor, located in Dalaman, is 90 kilometers away. So from the airport to the hotel you have to get about two hours. With regard to remoteness from the sea, the hotel complex is located on the beach and has its own beach, which you can walk slowly in just a couple of minutes.

This magnificent hotel complex for the first time opened its doors for guests in 2006. The last large-scale restoration was carried out in 2011. Today the hotel is a modern complex, which has comfortable and stylishly decorated rooms, all necessary for an excellent pastime infrastructure. The hotel has several swimming pools, great restaurants, bars, a clean beach. An interesting animation program is held here. The hotel complex is perfect for families. However, young people will find something to do here.





Hotel Policies

In accordance with the rules established in the five-star Grand Yazici Club Turban, guests are booked in their rooms after two in the afternoon. If you arrive earlier, then most likely you will be able to settle without the need for waiting. However, at the peak of the season, when the hotel is almost 100% full, you may have to wait until the previous guests have left the room and the maids have prepared it for check-in. In this case, you can leave your things in the luggage storage room and go for lunch, take a walk around the territory, take a swim in the pool or immediately go to the beach. As soon as the room you book is free, the hotel staff will transfer your bags and suitcases there, and you can check in. As for the discharge from the hotel, on the day of departure the keys to the room must be returned by noon. At the same time, payment should also be made for accommodation, as well as for the use of additional services. If you have previously fully paid for a room with a travel company, then you only need to pay for the use of services that are not included in the price. At the same time, you can pay in cash or with plastic cards of the Visa or Mastercard system.

Children and pets

Since, as we have already mentioned, Grand Yazici Club Turban 5 * (Marmaris) is mainly designed for family people, children of any age are allowed here. Moreover, if you place a child under two years old on a crib or on existing bedding for a child under the age of twelve, their stay will be free. Please note that only one cot can be accommodated in this room. Extra beds are not available for older children or adults.

If you belong to the category of people who prefer to travel with their four-legged pets, then we are forced to disappoint you. After all, this hotel does not allow guests with their pets. Therefore, you will either have to leave your pet at home, or choose another hotel from the pet-friendly category.





Number of rooms

Grand Yazici Club Turban 5 * has 434 comfortable rooms. All are stylishly decorated and furnished with quality furniture. They are located in the main building, as well as in 25 two- and three-story bungalows. We offer a detailed look at all the categories of rooms of this hotel complex. Regardless of the type of room, it will be cleaned daily, and towels and bed linen will change every other day. You also have the opportunity to order room service. In addition, all rooms have a safe, which can be used at an additional cost.

Standard numbers of the main body

These rooms are designed to accommodate a maximum of three people. There are: French bed, single bed, bathroom with bath or shower and hairdryer, flat-screen TV with satellite channels (including those broadcasting in Russian), air conditioning, heating system, telephone and minibar. On the floor - carpet. All standard rooms in the main building have their own balconies, most of which offer magnificent sea views.





Family rooms of the main building

This type of room at Grand Yazici Club Turban (Marmaris) is designed to accommodate a maximum of four guests. They have: one French bed, two single beds, two bathrooms (one of them has a shower, and the other has a bathtub), TVs with satellite channels, cooling and heating system, telephone, minibar and balcony. The floors in the rooms are carpeted.

Nutrition

The food in the Grand Yazici Club Turban 5 * is organized according to the all-inclusive system, which has long been appreciated by both Russians and citizens of other CIS countries. In the main restaurant of the hotel (there is also a non-smoking section here), skilled chefs serve a buffet consisting of Turkish and European dishes, various snacks, desserts and fresh fruits three times a day. In addition, guests of the hotel have the opportunity to visit one of the three restaurants working on the "a la carte" system. So, in the fish, Turkish or Italian restaurant you can order your favorite dishes √† la carte. All restaurants have a children's menu, as well as special high chairs for babies. It should be noted that the food in the Grand Yazici Club Turban, hotel reviews only confirm this, it is at a very high level.

In addition, there are seven bars on the territory of the five-star hotel complex. Among them: a lobby bar, a poolside bar, a beach bar, a vitamin bar, as well as a rock bar, a jazz bar and a snack bar. Here you can always order your favorite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as a snack between the main meals and taste delicious ice cream.

Pools

On the large territory of the Grand Yazici Club Turban HV-1 there are four pools: two open, one indoor and one for children. Next to them there is a spacious sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols, the use of which is free for hotel guests. In addition, there are several water slides designed for both adult guests and children.





Sea beach

This hotel complex is located on the first coastline and has its own beach. From the hotel, walk to it literally a hundred meters. The beach is sand and pebble. There are sun loungers, mattresses and parasols, the use of which is free for hotel guests. In addition, the beach has everything you need for water sports.

Entertainment

From morning to late evening, animators work at the Grand Yazici Club Turban Hotel (Marmaris). Their goal is to bring variety and fun to the rest of the guests of the hotel complex. They offer guests to participate in fun contests, play futsal, volleyball or other outdoor games, as well as do aerobics. In the evenings, the hotel hosts vibrant shows and discos. Much attention is paid here and pastime of the youngest tourists. So, in the afternoon there is a children's club for them, and in the evenings a mini-disco is held. There is also a playground on site.

If you are tired of lying on a sun lounger, you can play table or tennis, billiards, darts, rent a bike, visit the gym or go to the beach and enjoy water sports.

For those who want to take care of their own appearance, the five-star Grand Yazici Club Turban has a hairdresser, spa, fitness center, jacuzzi, sauna and Turkish bath (hammam).

Grand Yazici Club Turban: traveler reviews

In general, our compatriots left positive emotions from visiting this hotel. Most of them found the hotel complex quite consistent with the declared five stars and the cost of living.

Since mainly family people come here to relax, many tourists appreciated the location of the hotel. It is located a little distance from the main chain of hotels, in a very quiet place, surrounded by pine trees. As for the hotel rooms, they were all clean, comfortable and well furnished. True, those who lived in distant bungalows were a little embarrassed by the need to go to restaurants, pools and the beach for a rather long time. In addition, some tourists complained about the not very high-quality cleaning of the rooms. However, most of our compatriots did not mention a similar problem in their reviews.

As for the topic of food, which is often very painful for tourists, no special complaints were made regarding the hotel Grand Yazici Club Turban (Marmaris). So, the vast majority of travelers said that the food here is very tasty and quite diverse. Every day, vacationers found something new on the menu. The cooks worked conscientiously, so no one here could remain hungry. Perhaps, as the only minus of such nutrition, our compatriots note the fact that many of them returned to their homeland with a few extra pounds in the waist and hips.

Most tourists in general were satisfied with the work of the hotel complex staff. However, some hotel guests did not like the fact that in restaurants they didn‚Äôt rush to clean the dirty dishes from the tables. Instead, the waiters preferred talking to holidaymakers from Turkey itself.

As for water activities, for which we mainly go on vacation, the five-star Grand Yazici Club Turban hotel has plenty of them. Both adults and children enjoyed roller coaster rides in a small water park on site, as well as practicing water sports on the beach. Hotel pools also left a favorable impression. However, some tourists were unsatisfied with the fact that sun loungers on the sundeck should be taken early in the morning. If you go down to the pool after breakfast, then you will hardly succeed in finding a free deck chair. A similar problem was observed on the beach. However, there was a shortage of sun umbrellas. Also, some tourists noted that the beach is too small for a hotel of this magnitude.

In general, the hotel complex was quite liked by our compatriots. Many of them note that they are ready to return here again and will recommend it to their friends and relatives.
