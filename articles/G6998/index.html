DIY crafts for the City Day: interesting ideas for creativity

In all kindergartens and schools, creative competitions are held from time to time. Everyone can participate in them, just do something beautiful with your own hands. What crafts for the City Day are suitable for a thematic competition? We offer you a selection of the most original and interesting ideas for children's and family creativity!

Fireworks: a simple hobby for the smallest



Very often, children are invited to participate in creative competitions in the younger groups of the kindergarten. It turns out that this is a task for parents (What can a 2-3 year old child do?) We bring to your attention an interesting idea - a festive firework. The simplest option is a drawing. Take black paper / cardboard for the background or paint a dark white sheet. Take out the bright colors and invite the baby to arbitrarily set bright points of all the colors of the rainbow. Tip: Gouache is best for such drawing. You can also use finger paints or any other thick. But the salute drawn in watercolor will not look bright and festive enough. Crafts for City Day in kindergarten are made in various techniques, and drawings, applications, and voluminous miniatures are also usually accepted for competitions. Fireworks can be made by gluing finely cut colored paper onto a dark background.

Coat of arms of the city from improvised materials



An interesting and fairly simple craft - an application depicting the emblem of the city. The easiest way is to make it out of colored paper. Find the finished image with the emblem of the hometown of a suitable size. Print it on the printer or accurately redraw manually. Now you need to carefully cut out all the elements separately and transfer them to colored paper. Next, we cut out each detail and glue everything together, observing the sequence and arrangement of fragments relative to each other. Crafts for City Day with their own hands can be made from plasticine. It all depends on the complexity of the coat of arms itself. If it has simple shapes, try fashioning it randomly. Symbols of hometown - original craft



Every city in our country has something special and unique. Think about what your native land is famous for. Perhaps this is some kind of production, special painting techniques or another type of art. As symbols of the city, one can also consider some special architectural structures: monuments, the Kremlin, or the ancient cathedral. Making crafts for the City Day, you can include similar symbolism in your work. Mini-presentation about the native land

If, however, schoolchildren were asked to make crafts for the City Day contest, you can try to make a small presentation, for example, by making a folding folder, each page of which will tell you about one production / symbol of the region. Invite your child to think through the implementation of such an idea on his own, devote time together to studying the history of the region, the development of industries that are most famous for it, and the appearance of other important symbols.

Poster-guide - an original craft for the City Day (photo and description)



Not sure what to do for the creative contest? Easy to execute and original idea - a poster introducing the main attractions of the region. Buy a Whatman paper and make a list of buildings and objects that you plan to talk about. Option for the lazy: to purchase a set of tourist cards with views of the city and the most popular attractions. You can also take pictures of them yourself, and then print the pictures or draw. Of course, such children's crafts for City Day, like a wall newspaper, are more appropriate for school competitions. Glue pictures on the Whatman paper and sign each attraction. You can add a small description. If the competition is held in elementary school, offer to personally describe all these places and monuments to the child in any form. Let your son or daughter independently tell about what he saw in the museum or the more he is struck by a particular street sculpture.

Models of the main attractions and other interesting ideas



All children make crafts for City Day in kindergarten with great pleasure. Invite a child from improvised materials to make a model of some special structure. It can be a Kremlin or an unusual monument. Use any materials: cardboard, plasticine, twigs and everything else that can be found in your home workshop. How to make such crafts for City Day with your own hands? Be sure to keep in front of your eyes a sketch of your future creation, and even better - photographs of a real building or other attraction. It is easiest to sculpt sculptures from plasticine, but houses and walls are excellently made from cardboard. You can also use junk material - for example, cardboard packaging. Do not try to make the most detailed layout if the child is small. It is only important to keep the proportions if you create several objects, and take time to decorate your creation. Making crafts for the City Day is very exciting and pleasant, because you not only make something with your own hands, but also introduce the child to the history of his native land and its features, which is very important for his education and broadening his horizons.
