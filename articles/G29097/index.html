Cargo insurance

Cargo insurance coverage is another attractive feature of the carrier in question, as reviews say. When registering this service, Pony Express always compensates for damage that was caused to the ship by accident or during transportation by any means of transport of the company or the carriers cooperating with it during transportation both within the country and abroad.

The company is only liable for the goods indicated on the consignment note that are permitted for transportation by current state legislation. If the item was lost that was not recorded in the invoice or one that is officially prohibited for transportation, the damage will not be compensated. Therefore, it is important to carefully read in advance what rules, requirements and standards are established by the legislation today in this regard.

It is important to accurately indicate the declared cost of departure, because insurance compensation will be carried out taking into account it.
