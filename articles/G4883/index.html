Dress pattern with a bat sleeve is again in demand by fashionistas

In the 40s of the twentieth century, a dress with a bat silhouette came into fashion. And it immediately became popular with fashionistas. It could be sewn by every woman who could find a piece of any material, whether it be cotton, staple, lavsan, wool, crepe de chine or silk. A fashionable dress of any length could be sewn on your own simply and quickly. The simplicity of cut and tailoring makes it affordable for any modern lover of fashionable dresses.

A bit of history

Fashion historians suggest that these dresses are a transformation of the Japanese kimono that appeared in Japan in the 5th century AD. In the era of Muromoti, it was considered underwear, then they began to wear it without pants. Then the obi belt appeared, soft and wide.

Sleeves increased over time, especially for single women. Since the 19th century, this costume has become national for the Japanese. Currently, he is worn for major holidays and celebrations, and in everyday life they rarely wear kimonos, because European fashion has pushed the national costume to the background.

Basic pattern

The dress returned to fashion, finalized by designers, in the 80s. And practically did not leave the podium for many years. The "bat" sleeve has been appearing all these years not only in dresses, but also in tunics and blouses. Many actresses and singers are happy to wear clothes of such a cut. These dresses are universal, they suit beauties of different ages and complexions.





A pattern is a design drawn on paper, according to which a dress, blouse, tunic, skirt or trousers are later sewn. You need to take the material for the dress "bat" at least 140 cm wide, fold it in half in half, and then in length, with the wrong side up, to get 4 layers. The back and front are cut out one pattern. The only difference is the neckline.

If the width is less, then you need to take 2 lengths of material, multiplied by 2, by its measure of length. It is necessary to lay the pieces of matter inside out. Then lay the backrest pattern at one end of the material and pin it well with pins so that the entire structure does not move apart. And from the other end put a pattern in front. In this case, the middle of the front and back will grind. Chip along the folds, as well as the back. Now we need to designate the sleeve of the desired length and width.





Dress pattern with a sleeve "bat"

Summer version of the pattern





This is a pattern of a summer blouse. On it you can make a dress pattern with a sleeve "bat" for the summer for both thin girls and for full. It is only necessary to enter your measurement data into the design of the pattern and from the waist extend the skirt of the desired length and style. Otherwise, everything, as in the previous case, patterns for a dress with a bat sleeve.

For the summer version, you can take light, airy material. For example, silk, stretch, light satin, summer knitwear. The length of the dress can be any: for young and slim shorter, for respectable ladies - to the knee, just below the knee or to the ankle. It is advisable for girls and women of short stature to wear high-heeled shoes, for example stilettos, since dresses of this silhouette slightly reduce height.
