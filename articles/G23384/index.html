How to sew epaulettes: basic rules and nuances of work

Despite the fact that all the nuances of sewing epaulets to uniforms should be familiar to every man who has served in the army, many have difficulties with this process. First of all, it is necessary to determine where exactly it is necessary to fix them. So, the question of how to sew epaulettes should arise only if we are talking about tunics or outerwear. But they are not sewn to shirts. They can be attached using buttons or ordinary paper clips in the winding.

Fastening shoulder straps to the shirt



With the approach of heat, all employees of the armed forces, police and other bodies, where it is necessary to wear uniforms, take off their tunic. At this time, they have to remember how to sew epaulets on a shirt. This process is not too complicated and does not take much time, because in fact they are not sewn, but simply fastened with buttons.

On the shoulders of the uniform shirt should be special loops for attaching insignia and several eyelets. Before starting work, take the company (coming with the shoulder strap) and the usual buttons. So you can make a removable structure that can be unfastened at any time. Take the buttons, place the company one above the shoulder strap, the usual one under it and sew them. When attaching the bottom, make a small leg to make it easier to fasten these decals to the shirt. Now it is enough to thread a dense strip of fabric located at the bottom of the shoulder strap into special loops on the shoulders. After that, you can fasten the button in a specially designed buttonhole for it.

Attaching shoulder straps to outerwear

The easiest way to deal with the fastening of uniform insignia to the shirt. But figuring out how to sew epaulettes on a tunic is a bit more complicated. You will have to work hard during this process. It is very important to ensure that during sewing this distinctive mark is correctly located on the shoulder and does not move anywhere, for this it can be seized with several stitches around the edges and in the center or attached with pins.





Having figured out how to sew epaulettes, prepare everything you need: matching threads, scissors, a thimble. Be prepared for the fact that this process is quite painstaking and complex, it is not easy to flash dense fabric. Lay the epaulette on the tunic so that its lower part is on the seam line with which the sleeve is sewn. To make your task easier, insert a needle into the seam holes of this distinguishing mark - you won‚Äôt have to make new holes. Note that the epaulette is sewn through through the line that connects the edging and its main part.





Sew on police epaulets

If you need to learn how to sew on police epaulets, then keep in mind that they are evenly attached to the base of the seam of the sleeve, and the rounded edge should fit the collar of the product. The back of the law enforcement insignia should be 1 cm from the shoulder seam. It is located with a slight shift forward. The top stitches should be very small so as not to catch the eye. In addition, the shoulder strap should be pressed firmly against the tunic and hold tight. To do this, the seam needs to be done around its perimeter.

These are the basic rules on how to sew epaulettes. If you follow them, you will be satisfied with the result of your work.
