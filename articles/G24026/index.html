DIY stone application on cardboard

Often children, walking along the coast of the sea, collect pebbles and make various buildings out of them. They examine objects, understand that they can be of different shapes, structures, colors, even shades. Sometimes a separate element reminds someone of a child, looks like a heart or a fish, a triangle or a cone.

You can offer a child to make a picture of stones on the sand right on the sand. The first time you can send him and help. Find the missing element, submit a simple idea. But the picture made in the sand cannot be taken with you and shown to relatives and friends.

Parents can give the idea to the baby - to assemble a set of different parts home and there to make an application of stones on cardboard. Advise her to first lay out on the shore, and then gently collect all the elements in a bag and carry with them. And at home, you need to keep the picture longer, to conduct the preliminary work correctly.

Preparation for work

First, stones are prepared. If these are marine finds, then they need to be cleaned of sand and washed. If they are found on the river bank, then after washing they must be boiled for 10 minutes in an enameled pan. This will kill the microflora and the stone will be clean and dry. Over time, mold will not hit him, and he will not be covered with green algae. After boiling, all items are laid out on a cotton napkin and dried thoroughly. Only now it will be possible to make an application of stones.

You will also need good glue to work, for example, ‚ÄúMoment‚Äù. You need to think in advance, maybe other additional details will come in handy for the design of the image. It can be branches, sticks. If necessary, supplement the drawing with pencil lines or use gouache paints to apply small details to the stones themselves. Consider several options for simple applications of stones, which are able to make children of preschool and primary school age.

Sunflower

To make such a flower, you need a twig on which it will be located. It can be glued to cardboard, or it can be attached using wire. To do this, puncture holes on both sides of the branch with a sharp awl and tightly twist it from the back. After the branch does not fall, does not move, you can begin to make an application of stones.





You will need one large and flat element, rounded small pebbles for leaflets and petals of the same size and shape. In order for the picture to be bright and colorful, each element needs to be prepared in advance.

Stone painting

You can, of course, just lay out the picture from unpainted elements, many artists do just that, but children love bright colors to make the picture multicolor. To do this, each element must first be painted with gouache. And first one side. Wait until it dries, turn over and cover another. You can dye it twice so that there are no spots.

Next, each stone is covered with acrylic varnish. Then the details will be juicy, shiny and smooth. Yes, and sticking such an element will be much easier, and it will hold on tight.

After all the elements are ready, you need to first lay out all the details on the cardboard, as they do with a simple application of paper, and then glue them. When the glue dries, the picture is ready.

Birds on a branch

For such an application of stones, six small oval or triangular parts will be needed. It will be the bodies of birds. Two thicker branches are also useful, with thin and small ones located on them. Thick ones are attached with wire, and small ones stick out to the sides. So the picture is added bulk.





Bird stones are not placed on the branch itself, but higher. Leave room for paint or marker paws. Elements should be placed with the thickened side down, similar to the structure of the bird. It remains to draw beaks. If desired, the child can add even more small details by drawing wings, eyes, coloring feathers. Such an application of stones for children will be simple. You can do it even with 3-4 year olds.

Girl with balls

To make such a picture you need to pick up stones in the form of balloons. This is not difficult, as they come in different shapes. Both round and oval objects will do. Balloons can be painted, or can be left in kind. Then it remains to correctly place them first without glue and draw the rest of the details. These are the hands, feet and ponytails on the girl‚Äôs head. And for balls - threads, for which they are held by the character of the picture. Further, the stones are smeared with glue and glued to already prepared places. Such an application of stones on cardboard can be inserted into a frame and hung in a children's room. She is very positive and will cheer up the child in the morning.

Owls in winter

Such a plot picture can be done by older children: despite the fact that there are only two flat stones, the image is complex. First you need to prepare the background by painting the details on the cardboard. You can take black cardboard for the background and make the moon also from the finished part. To do this, you need to cut out the yellow circle according to the template and stick it in the center of the future picture. Then they draw snow by sticking the end part of the brush and twigs powdered with snow.





Remains the work on the stones. They are first painted, making a black background and drawing an owl. Then, after drying, varnish. Now you can put them on a branch. The self-made stone application is ready!

After attaching a branch to the wire, begin to shape the crown. To do this, you need a lot of small rounded elements. You can even not pre-lay them out, but immediately stick them on cardboard.





The second layer in the photo is attached round pebbles, similar to apples. If your child wants to make an apple tree, then you can look for such round details. It is also recommended that the fruit be painted bright red or yellow. This will enliven the image.

Ballerina

You can also make an application of stones and shells. There are many of them on the coast. You can use those and other natural materials in one image.





To make a dancing ballerina, you need to pick up a round stone for the head, conical for the body, a large shell that will depict a magnificent bundle of ballerinas. For the image of arms and legs, 4 elongated and thin elements are needed.

Since the girl is dancing in the picture, the hands are arranged in different ways, transmitting movement. Legs are also attached in various positions. One leg is on the floor and the other is raised.

Cats

Such a plot picture has many details. They are all of different sizes. I‚Äôll have to try to find such peculiar pebbles. The bodies of cats are oblong and thicken downward. The heads are rounded, oval. Ears need small and pointed stones at the ends.





Before making an application of stones, all the details can be laid out on a sheet of cardboard and check how it looks, maybe something needs to be added. Then proceed to sticking each individual element in the desired order.

Such art develops in children the ability to fantasize, to take initiative. In addition, working with such natural natural material fills with energy. Children learn to navigate in space, to correctly distribute the image on the sheet.
