What dictionaries are there? What are the dictionaries of the Russian language?

You rarely see a person who, at least once in his life, has not looked into the dictionary. With their help, we not only learn the meaning of certain words, select synonyms or antonyms, but also learn a lot of new things.

Let's talk about what dictionaries are, what is their classification, and recall the main "linguistic reference books" of the Russian language.

Dictionary Science

Lexicography is one of the sections of linguistics that deals with the problems of studying and compiling dictionaries. It is she who is engaged in the classification, puts forward requirements for the design of articles and their content.

The scholars who make up the dictionaries call themselves lexicographers. It is important to note that dictionaries have no authors, only compilers. This is due to the fact that they are compiled with the help of special cards on which the meanings of words and their forms are recorded. At the same time, the compiler can use both the cards he personally collected and the cards collected by a whole staff of linguists.

Further we will consider what dictionaries of the Russian language are, we will find out who compiled them.

Classification of modern dictionaries

All dictionaries are divided into encyclopedic and philological, or linguistic.

Encyclopedic dictionaries provide information about various events. A striking example of such a dictionary is BES - the Big Encyclopedic Dictionary. Dictionaries of terms are also encyclopedic .

What are linguistic dictionaries? This group of dictionaries deals directly with words and their interpretation. They are also divided into bilingual and monolingual.

Bilingual dictionaries contain the words of the Russian language and their equivalent in a foreign language.

Monolingual dictionaries are divided into groups depending on their purpose.

The most used types of dictionaries

What type of dictionaries are there? Among monolingual dictionaries, the following should be distinguished:

  1. Explanatory, in which an interpretation of a given word is given, its meaning is described.
  2. Dictionary of neologisms - contains a list of words that have recently come into use and their interpretation.
  3. Foreign words - captures not only the meaning of the word, but also notes from which language it came to us.
  4. Synonyms - helps to find synonyms. It indicates a list of words that are similar in meaning to the main one, the most used in this synonymous series.
  5. Antonimov - contains antonymic word pairs.
  6. Homonym - describes the main homonymic pairs, while pointing to the meaning of a word, the features of its use in speech.
  7. Phraseological dictionary - contains a list of all known phraseological units and gives them an interpretation.
  8. Grammar dictionaries - indicate the grammatical meaning of a word - to which part of speech it refers, what are its main features.
  9. Spelling - helps to avoid the most common mistakes when writing a particular word.
  10. Orthoepic - fix the correct stress in words.
  11. Word-building - show what basis was used in the formation of the word, what parts it consists of (prefixes, suffixes, roots).
  12. Dictionary of epithets - helps to choose the most successful epithets for a particular noun.
  13. Etymological dictionary - tells the story of the word.

The structure of the dictionary entry

In conclusion, I would like to add a few words about the structure of the dictionary entry.

Any dictionary entry begins with a heading word, which is often spelled out in capital letters and in bold.

We note right away that the words used in dictionaries are always spelled correctly, therefore, if you doubt the spelling of a particular word, it is not necessary to refer to the spelling dictionary. Just open any available at your fingertips.

Most dictionaries also indicate proper stress. Almost all Russian dictionaries will contain this information. What other notes are there?

After the heading word comes information about which part of the speech it belongs to. Then its meaning is described or there is a list of synonyms, antonyms - it all depends on the type of dictionary. The dictionary article concludes with examples of use - quotes from books, magazines. If this word has features in use, this information is also indicated at the end of the article.
