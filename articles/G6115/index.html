The larger the company, the lower the risks?

Looking through stocks, you will notice that they are much more expensive for large enterprises than for young companies. This is due to the fact that the former show stable annual growth, increasing their capital and improving their reputation. Thanks to this, a large company has clear advantages and the ability to influence the country's economy. Investing in stocks of such companies is certainly the best choice. However, it is worthwhile to understand that securities will be more expensive in this case.





This does not mean at all that earnings from the sale of shares of small rapidly growing companies are doomed to failure. Having spent a smaller amount on the purchase, you will receive less income.

At the same time, large companies with all their rapid growth cannot always guarantee success to their shareholders. Stocks will grow only if the company continues to grow, otherwise they will not carry special value. Less risk bears the purchase of shares of small businesses. The main thing is to do the right monitoring and choose a promising company that promises good growth in the future.

The most popular are shares of large Russian companies, for example, Gazprom, Sberbank, Rosneft, Uralkali and others. You can be sure of the stability of these firms for several years to come, therefore, if you choose rapidly growing enterprises, it is better to give preference to just such.
