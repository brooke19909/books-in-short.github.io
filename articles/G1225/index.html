Which smartphone with a powerful battery is best to choose?

As part of this material, a choice will be made as to which smartphone with a powerful battery is the best of the following models: IQ4403 from Fly, X-treme PQ22 from Sigma mobile, Ascend Mate 2 from HUAWEI with 4G support and IdeaPhone P780 from Lenovo. Each of them is equipped with a capacious battery, which makes the battery life significantly longer. But other technical characteristics and the cost of the gadget also need to be considered. It is by the most optimal ratio of all these parameters that the choice will be made.

Fly

The Fly brand in this niche is represented by the IQ4403 model. Its second name is Energie 3. This is a smartphone with a powerful 4000 mAh battery , which is enough for 2-3 days of battery life with not very intense load. The computing center of this device is the 6572 processor from the Chinese company MTK.

It consists of 2 revision cores A7 operating at a frequency of 1.3 GHz. The graphics subsystem of IQ4403 is implemented using the Mali-400 MP. Its performance will be enough to perform the most common tasks on a screen with a diagonal of 4 and a half inches and a resolution of 864 by 480 pixels. He has 512 MB of RAM, and 4 GB of internal memory. There is also an expansion slot for installing a 32 GB MicroSD standard memory card. Estimated cost of Energie 3 is 160 USD. Among the advantages of this model, one can distinguish a capacious battery, a balanced hardware platform and a reasonable price. This smartphone with a powerful battery is not able to cope with demanding tasks (for example, with 3D toys). It has a weak CPU. Therefore, such a solution is best suited for those who do not make high demands on the performance of the smartphone and want to get the maximum battery life in combination with an affordable price.

Sigma mobile

Sigma mobile products are aimed at those who lead an active lifestyle. At the first stage, only mobile phones were presented. Well, now there are smartphones. One of the first among them was the X-treme PQ22. Given the positioning, it was logical to equip this model with a capacious battery, the capacity of which in this case can be 2800 mAh or 4500 mAh (you need to check with the seller before buying). During long trips, it is not always possible to recharge a mobile device. Therefore, the longer the battery life, the better. In the second version, this indicator for PQ22 is still the same 2-3 days at a not very intense load. This is the smartphone with the most powerful battery available today. At the same time, the processor in this case is more productive - 6589 from the same Chinese company MediaTEK. It already consists of 4 revision cores A7, operating at a frequency of 1.2 GHz. The graphics adapter in this case is the SGX 544 developed by PowerVR. This is a great solution that allows you to run most of the modern toys for Android. The screen size of this device is 4 inches with a resolution of 854 by 480 pixels. It has 1 GB of RAM, and 4 GB of integrated memory, which can be expanded with a memory card up to 32 GB in size. A distinctive feature of the PQ22 is the degree of protection of the housing - IP67. It is because of this that he is not afraid of moisture, dust and other harmful factors. This is an indestructible smartphone with a powerful battery, which is perfect for those who lead an active lifestyle. In other cases, it is impractical to buy it, since its cost is 270 USD, higher than that of analogues without a protective case.





Huaewi

HUAWEI did not forget about this niche. In this case, we are talking about the new Ascend Mate 2 - this is a Chinese smartphone with a powerful battery 4050 mAh. Technical characteristics are much better than those of competitors. Qualcomm 2-core processor 8928. Its clock frequency is 1.7 GHz, and it is built on the basis of the Krait 300 architecture. Its resources are enough to solve any problem today. No less productive in this case, and the graphics subsystem. Here it is implemented using Adreno 306. The screen size is 6.1 inches, and its resolution is exactly the same as the previous model. At the same time, it is protected by Gorilla Glass. The amount of RAM installed in it is 2 GB, and the integrated memory is 16 GB. It is also possible to install memory cards. As a result, it can be noted that this smartphone is perfect for those who need uncompromising performance at all costs.





Lenovo

One of the first to focus on this niche was Lenovo. Her model P780 can be called a veteran of this segment. She made her debut in the domestic market in July 2013 (much earlier than all the competitors listed above). Specifications she has similar models PQ22 from Sigma mobile. The only difference is in the diagonal (5 inches versus 4.5), resolution (1280 √ó 720 versus 854 √ó 480) and battery capacity (4000 versus 4500 mAh). The main minus of the P780 is the high cost of 320 USD. Therefore, buying it is not entirely advisable.
