Smartphones "Samsung", all models: photos and specifications

Samsung in 2016 made significant updates to the line of smartphones. At the end of 2015, the company announced the improvement of the A and J lines, and before the New Year released mid-range smartphones. In the spring of 2016, it came to the J7 model. Further in the article Samsung smartphones will be considered: all models, as well as features of their functioning.

Samsung Galaxy J7 SM-J710F

It is a simpler variation of J7. New Samsung smartphones (SM-J710F) cost about $ 270. Characteristics fully confirm the class of the device.

The design has not changed much. Everything is similar to previous versions of the Galaxy J7. The only difference is that the base of the case has become metal. Also, the smartphone is framed by a silver frame. Colors can be different: classic white and black and eye-catching golden. The cover in this model is removable, made of plastic.





As for the dimensions, the size is 15X7, weight - 170 g, thickness - 8 mm. Such parameters have the Galaxy J7 SM-J710F - Samsung smartphones. All models (photos you will find below) have slight differences, but in general they are similar. For example, this model differs from the previous version in mass - 1 gram less.

The front panel of the SM-J710F is made of glass. It has a speaker, proximity and focus sensors, a front camera, and a flash. Below are one regular button and two touch buttons. At the back of the smartphone you can find a camera, flash and speaker.

The processor model SM-J710F is 8 cores and has a frequency of 1.6 GHz. Built-in memory for use is 11 GB. It is also possible to equip with an additional memory reserve - a flash card. The maximum size is 2 TB. However, in practice, such media does not yet exist, that is, you can install any microSD in this model.

Samsung smartphones, all models, are of good quality. In particular, the SM-J710F has a main camera of 13 megapixels. The front is also pretty good - 5 megapixels.

Samsung Galaxy J5 SM-J510FN

Another Samsung smartphone (2016 models) is the Samsung Galaxy J5 SM-J510FN. The gadget was priced at $ 250. The creators focused on the youth audience. The smartphone is made of plastic, and the side faces are completely made of metal. The front panel is glass. On the back of the device are the main camera with flash and speaker. The cover of the smartphone is removed. SM-J510FN supports two SIM cards, which is very convenient. Next to them there is a place for a microSD flash drive.





The size of the smartphone is 15x8 cm, the thickness is 8 mm, the weight of the phone is 160 g. The processor has 4 cores. In principle, Samsung smartphones (all models) have a fairly powerful processor that ensures the normal operation of various applications and programs. Regarding memory, 2 GB in RAM and 11 GB of internal reserve can be noted. The smartphone can support a flash drive up to 128 GB.

Considering Samsung smartphones, a comparison of models can be used to identify the pros and cons. However, the described model does not have significant differences from previous versions. The main differences are only in the frequencies that smartphones support, and in the Android version. The new model runs on Android 6, while its predecessors can only support version 5 of the system.

Samsung Galaxy J1 mini SM-J105H

This is the smallest handheld computer that Samsung has ever produced. It has only 4 inches in diagonal. It is also the cheapest smartphone from Samsung. In size, however, this mini-smartphone is not so small: 121x61 mm, thickness - 11 mm. Although thanks to these parameters it is convenient to hold it in your hand. If it were thinner, a tiny device would slip out of your fingers.





The processor of the device is 4 cores. Galaxy J1 mini can even support 3D applications, but playing on it is still not very convenient, since the display is small. Built-in memory corresponds to the name mini - only 4 GB of free memory for documents and applications. If necessary, you can insert a USB flash drive up to 120 GB. In the RAM, there are even fewer units - only 770 MB.

The smartphone has a front and main camera. But the quality of the pictures is not at a high level. The front camera is 0.3 megapixels. There is no autofocus and any flash even on the main camera.

Comparing Samsung smartphones (all models, a description of their characteristic features), we can say that the Galaxy J1 mini SM-J105H is suitable for those who need the device mainly for work. The device can open various types of documents, supports social networking applications, video calls. The sound quality is good enough for a conversation.

Samsung Galaxy S4 LTE + GT-19506

The model features a large screen and the presence of gesture recognition. However, for the most part, no one uses this function. The approximate price of the device is 17,000 rubles. The advantages of the smartphone is a high-speed LTE connection, which allows you to use the Internet at high speeds and watch videos and movies online in the highest quality.





The device runs on the Android 4.2 platform. The version is not the latest, but the smartphone on it functions quite well. The glass front panel creates a presentable look. Weight and thickness are small, which allows you to carry the device in your pocket.

Samsung Galaxy S4 MINI La Fleur

This smartphone is perfect for girls who love style and sophistication in everything. It costs about 14 thousand rubles. Samsung created this model with all its love for the female half of humanity. Not only the design is made in a special style, but also the interface. The smartphone is supplemented with special wallpapers and themes for women.

The functionality of the device also does not lag behind other versions of the smartphone. There are two slots for SIM cards. It is important to note that they can work alternately. The battery is economical - it takes about 2-3 days to recharge, even with a constant connection to Wi-Fi.

Samsung GALAXY Core GT-18262

This smartphone is a budget option - convenient, functional and inexpensive. It operates on the platform "Android 4.1". The version is very old, but it works no worse than the latest. The smartphone screen is small - only 4 inches. You can insert two SIM cards. The model is similar to the Galaxy Ace, but it is more powerful. The design is neutral, suitable for both men and women. The battery lasts long enough.





The disadvantage is that SIM cards work only alternately, and not simultaneously. But on the other hand, this mode does not overload the device, and the smartphone is functioning properly and stably.

Tips for choosing a smartphone: why do you need a device?

When choosing a smartphone, you need to build on what you want to buy it for: for work, only for communication, for entertainment.



Here are some suggestions:

  • If you play a lot of games or use 3D applications, then you need a smartphone with a powerful processor. Four cores will not be enough.
  • For those who use the Internet a lot, they need a device that supports Wi-Fi, GSM, and preferably LTE.
  • To watch movies, you need a smartphone that supports different formats and resolutions.
  • If you store a lot of media files on your smartphone, then it should have a large built-in memory and space for an additional flash card.

These are the main points that buyers should be guided by. So you can get the most out of the new smartphone and its functions.
