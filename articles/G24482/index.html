DIY slippers pattern: how to sew slippers

Home slippers should be comfortable, light and soft so your feet can relax after a hard day.

Today, various styles of slippers do not cease to please our female eye. We cannot pass by beautiful home-made shoes, which are embroidered with crystal stones, decorated with sequins and rhinestones. Winter options for slippers enchant us even more: boots with ears or warm flip flops with fur. In this article we will tell you how to sew home slippers on a pattern.

Models of home shoes

Today in store windows we can see a huge assortment of home shoes. Modern designers annually update the range of slippers with new looks and interesting colors.





Home slippers are not cheap pleasure, so many women are interested in how to make a pattern of home slippers with their own hands and sew shoes on it. But for this you need to decide on the style of the product.

Domestic shoes can be divided into several types:

  • slippers;
  • slippers with a closed toe;
  • indoor boots;
  • 3D house shoes;
  • ugg slippers;
  • loafers;
  • slippers-socks.

Tips for tailoring slippers

To understand how to make a pattern of slippers, slippers or uggs, slippers with a closed toe or slippers, you need to take a few tips:

  1. In order not to take measurements, you can take an old slipper, put it on paper and outline the sole.
  2. In order to sew the edging you will need an awl.
  3. As a rule, the sole is cut out of leatherette, split leather or leather.
  4. The insole can be cut out of cloth or suede.
  5. To sew a high-quality and durable product, you need to purchase special kapron threads.

Material for sewing home shoes

To pattern home slippers with your own hands, you need to pick up a fabric and stock up on all the necessary sewing accessories. Material for sewing home-made shoes should be high-quality and light, for example:

  • fleece;
  • mahra;
  • jeans;
  • wool;
  • plush;
  • suede leather;
  • felt.




From jeans, felt and suede, you can cut and sew lightweight home slippers or moccasins. Beautiful products are obtained from fleece, terry and plush. From these types of fabric you can sew any model of home shoes.

How to sew slippers: a felt pattern

To create this slippers, you will need to make a complex pattern. You will need the following materials:

  • 0.5 m felt;
  • scissors;
  • gypsy needle;
  • glue from the gun;
  • bright threads of floss;
  • patch in the form of an apple;
  • cardboard;
  • pencil.

DIY home slippers pattern will look like this:





Manufacturing process:

  1. Take the cardboard and put your feet on it, with a pencil outline the left and right feet.
  2. Now cut out the blanks of the soles with scissors and circle them on the fabric.
  3. Cut out these elements.
  4. Next, we need to create the top of the shoe. To do this, we again take the cardboard and draw two half circles. The dimensions of these parts depend on the fullness and length of your foot.
  5. After that, transfer these two parts to felt and cut out.
  6. You should get four patterns.
  7. We must sew the upper part of the slipper with the sole.
  8. This can be done with a gypsy needle and floss thread.
  9. After the two elements are sewn together, we can proceed to the decoration.
  10. We must sew a colorful patch to the top of the product.
  11. Now, according to the same pattern, we sew and decorate the second slipper.
  12. Home felt shoes with bright stripes are ready!

How to sew slippers ugg boots

For sewing such a product, fleece fabric is ideal . Slippers made of fleece will turn out to be warm, soft and will last you more than one year. To create a pattern of slippers, you need 1 m fleece, 0.5 m suede and 0.5 m leather. Good if these materials are matched to match. You also need the following sewing supplies:

  • threads to match the fabric;
  • needle;
  • scissors;
  • glue "Moment";
  • centimeter;
  • ruler;
  • cardboard;
  • pencil;
  • pins.

  2. We pin the patterns with pins to the fleece, circle and cut with a 1 cm allowance.
  3. We cut insoles from suede 23 cm long.
  4. Now we take the skin and cut out the soles about 24 cm in length.
  5. Using pins, we chop off the upper parts of the boot face to face.
  6. Cut the seam allowances, fold the rear sections of the boot and grind them, leaving a hole on the lining, through which you will turn the product.
  7. Sew the top of the boot vertically from the foot to the top.

How to sew home slippers-socks on your own

Building a pattern of home slippers with your own hands - quite laborious and scrupulous work. To simplify the task, you can use beautiful terry socks. Thus, you can create original home shoes in just 10 minutes!

For this you will need:

  • red terry socks;
  • leatherette - 0.5 m;
  • glue "Moment" (or glue from a gun);
  • decoration (bows, beads and stars);
  • scissors;
  • needle;
  • marker;
  • red threads.

Walkthrough on how to make sneakers:

  1. The sole should be made of leatherette. To do this, place your feet on the material and, using a marker, circle the outline of the feet.
  2. Use scissors to cut the soles and glue them to the socks.
  3. After the slippers have dried, you can proceed to decorate.
  4. Lapel on the blank of the product.
  5. Take slippers and on the side (from the outside) decorate it with a striped bow, right on the lapel.
  6. Sew beads a little lower and attach a pair of metal stars.
  7. Thus, you should decorate the second slipper.

According to this pattern, you can sew similar slippers, but come up with other ways to decorate. It all depends on your taste and ideas!

Original slippers: what decorate shoes?

In 2017, chic fur slippers and slippers with fur have become a fashion trend. The pattern of household slippers made of fur is simple: for this, you can take an old fur coat from a fox (nutria or rabbit) and carve four details according to your measurements - two semi-circles and two insoles (the process has already been described).





Then gently sew the upper parts of the shoe with the lower elements using special nylon threads. From leatherette, we cut the sole on old slippers and glue it to the insoles.

If you want to surprise your guests, you can originally decorate slippers. Everything will depend on your imagination here. You should start from the general color scheme of slippers, you do not need to glue a large number of diverse decorations on them. Everything must be done with taste. For example, after you have made a pattern of slippers, decorate the product with bows. Attach jewelry to the sides of the outside of the boots. So, what else can decorate slippers?

Decoration elements for home shoes:

  • tapes;
  • stars;
  • braid with a zigzag;
  • thread floss;
  • buttons
  • fur;
  • stones
  • embroidery;
  • rhinestones.

In order for the jewelry to sit firmly on the product and not fall off after the first wash, they should be glued or sewn tightly with thread.
