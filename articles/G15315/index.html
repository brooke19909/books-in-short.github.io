You can talk about the sights and features of the resort for a very long time, but in the framework of this article it is also necessary to note a few words about one of its hotels. Such is the Adonis Hotel Kemer.

It is located 150 meters from the sea. Near the hotel there is a picturesque harbor, forest, as well as mountains. The hotel consists of two buildings in a classic style. The hotel was built in 1985, and in 1996 was completely renovated. On the territory of this hotel, tourists can visit the open-air restaurant. There is also a bar. It is important to have your own outdoor pool, in which there is a place specially reserved for children. Near it there are changing cabins, comfortable sunbeds, as well as umbrellas. All this is provided for free. Now about nutrition. Adonis Hotel Kemer offers buffet-style Italian and local cuisine. The price of the permit, as a rule, includes only breakfast and dinner, but lunch is provided for a fee. The hotel has a separate territory on the city beach, its length is about four hundred meters. Here sun loungers, umbrellas and other attributes necessary for relaxation are provided for rent. It should be noted that this beach is pebble. In general, Adonis Hotel Kemer 3 (hotel reviews can be found in the global network) is more suitable for young people who prefer a vacation with city infrastructure.
