Collecting in pieces

Step-by-step process as a guarantee of top quality

You can always create a harmonious image from disparate elements of your wardrobe if a crocheted knitted tunic appears in it. A master class on such work will include several stages:

  1. Choosing your favorite motive.
  2. Buying suitable threads for a specific model and tool - a crochet hook.
  3. Knitting out one motive according to the chosen pattern.
  4. Measuring the size of the resulting motive, calculating the required number of motives, in accordance with the size of the thing and its cut, taking into account the sleeves or their absence, the length of the tunic, its width for the desired volume.
  5. Vyvyazyvanie the necessary number of motives.
  6. Assembly of the product.

The finished product should be slightly moistened, let it dry when unfolded. It is possible to iron knitted things from the wrong side, through thin cotton, in no case pressing the iron strongly against the fabric, but only touching it slightly and working with steam. You can use the steamer by hanging the finished tunic on a coat hanger.

Build Secrets

A boho tunic will become a unique thing for a needlewoman of any skill level, if all the motifs are sewn together very carefully. For this, it is necessary to use either the same threads that connected the motifs, or bobbin threads of the same shade.

Joints are sewn together, with small stitches, all knots and ponytails of the thread are made invisible and are located on the wrong side of the product. Moreover, when assembling motives into a single whole, one should also take into account the fact that each of them has a front and wrong side and you need to collect them by turning everything the same way on one side.

  • For example, choose a simple drawing, then another and one more. Let them be three.

  • When the length of the canvas reaches 50-70 centimeters (depending on the height of the needlewoman or customer), the canvas is divided into two parts that are knitted separately, without decreasing to a height of 30-40 centimeters.
  • Then both parts are connected again into one fabric and are knitted again to the same length. The neckline, which turned out in the middle of the panel, is the neck.
