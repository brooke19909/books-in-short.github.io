How to find out a BIK card of Sberbank: all ways

How to find out a BIK card of Sberbank? The information below may be of interest to those who wish to make a wire transfer to another credit institution. This is due to the fact that within Sberbank itself it is possible to make a payment using only the card number indicated on the front side (long line of numbers). For interbank transfers, this is not enough.

How to find out the BIC of a Sberbank card with a plastic carrier on hand? You need to look at the number of the branch that issued the card, then go to the site and try to determine which regional government it belongs to. The fact is that Sberbank OJSC is the largest credit organization in the Russian Federation, therefore it has a bank code for each region. For example, BIC for branches in Moscow and BIK for branches in Tyumen will definitely be different.



They will always tell you

How to find out the BIC of a Sberbank card if you are not very well versed in the structure of this financial institution? In this case, you need to get an agreement to open an account to which the card was issued, and see the details from Sberbank. It contains a correspondent account, BIC, full name of the organization, individual taxpayer number, address, signatures of responsible persons, stamp, etc.

How to know the BIC card of Sberbank for sure? It should be borne in mind that the bank organization has a certain mobility: some branches open, some close, details change. Therefore, if you have not used your card for a long time, then it is better to call Sberbank at the place of receipt of the "plastic" and clarify the necessary data. Or go directly to the department, where the information can be obtained from the administrator of the room or from the operator, either verbally or by printing the details.
