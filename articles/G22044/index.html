Crochet a summer topic

In summer, the most suitable clothes are crocheted topics and T-shirts. They allow air to circulate freely, which undoubtedly leads to cooling of the body, and also perfectly absorb evaporating moisture. There are very modern and original models, schemes for which were developed by famous fashion designers and clothing designers. These things can easily be worn at a party or at an important event. The crochet topic has long earned fame among the stars of Hollywood, and among ordinary citizens of our country.

What is necessary for knitting summer clothes



The crochet topic is made only of environmentally friendly material that is pleasant to the skin. This yarn is 100% natural cotton. Only such things can please you with their comfort and ease in the summer season. Always select the thickness of the threads for the T-shirt model. Fine and light openwork threads are obtained from thin yarn, and denser T-shirts come from thicker yarn. After buying yarn, you can proceed to the selection of the hook. They all differ in numbers, i.e. in thickness, and each of them corresponds to certain threads. Not only the density of the entire canvas, but also the size of the finished product will depend on which hook you choose. Remember that when crocheting, topics, T-shirts you will get a little wider, but after the first wash everything will fall into place.

We select the scheme according to the skill level



To get your crochet summer topics neat and really beautiful, you need to choose the appropriate model. If you have never before been involved in knitting big things, then you need to choose the simplest schemes of topics, not complicated by decorations. If you already have some experience, then you have huge opportunities for creating copyright summer T-shirts. Schemes you can safely invent yourself or combine from several existing models. Just remember that it is necessary to calculate the expected result and size.

Nuances that arise during work



It often happens that when knitting a ball with threads ends and it is necessary to make knots for bonding yarn. In the manufacture of summer T-shirts, this nuance is especially important to work out correctly, because when creating a lace, even the smallest knot will be noticeable. To make your topic crochet neat and without flaws, you need to make hidden nodules that are not visible on the front side and firmly connect the thread. You may also encounter the problem of combining individual motives. Sew them only with a needle or with a hook using a thread of the same yarn as the whole thing was knitted. In this case, the connection of motives must be performed on the wrong side, but there are models with the front connection of individual elements.

What to consider after completing the product

Knitted summer T-shirts, especially a crochet topic, tend to stretch and lose a little their original color. To avoid this, choose only high-quality yarn from well-known manufacturers, without knots and differences in thickness, as well as stably dyed with natural dyes.
