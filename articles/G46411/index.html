How to make a paper cap. Create a festive mood

Bright beautiful caps will complement any children's holiday, because the guys like them so much. Also, this accessory may be appropriate for celebrations with adult guests. An ordinary cap will create a festive mood for the New Year, at a birthday, a bachelorette party and at another joyful celebration. Such a hat can be purchased at the store, but you can not spend money and make it yourself in a matter of seconds. So, how to make a paper cap and create a festive atmosphere?





The easiest and fastest option is a pattern cap

If you do not have the time and desire to bother with the manufacture of the cap, then the most primitive way will suit you. Take a ready-made template with a picture. Customize if necessary. Take thick paper and print on a color printer. Cut and fold according to instructions. Usually it is intuitive and without text. For reliability, you can fix it with glue or a stapler. We put in a rope - and the cap is ready!

How to make a paper cap according to the scheme

The method of folding the cap is the same as in the previous embodiment. The main difference of the scheme is that it is not color, but white. There is scope for imagination. You can arrange the cap in any graphics editor as you like, and then assemble it. Or first print, and then decorate with paints, pencils, felt-tip pens and decorate with decorative elements.

This option can be successfully used at children's parties. Just invite the children to decorate and collect caps themselves. Usually, kids love to draw and craft with their pens, so they will readily agree. So you fill in the holiday time, and the guys will be carried away. Just do not forget to help and show how to make a paper cap.





Step-by-step master class: make a cap from a whole sheet of cardboard or paper

No less simple and quick way. Such a cap is made without any patterns and patterns:

  1. Take plain color cardboard or thick paper.
  2. Roll into a cone to the desired size, cut off the excess corners. As a result, the cap should stand flat on the table.
  3. If your headpiece is too high, trim with scissors. Usually caps are made small so that they look funny on the head.
  4. Mark with a pencil the connection points.
  5. Fasten to marked points with a stapler, glue or paper clips.
  6. How to make a paper cap so that it rests on your head? Fasten a rope, satin ribbons or elastic band on the sides of your headgear with tape or pieces of cardboard.
  7. The cap is ready, now it can be decorated to taste.




We make an origami cap

Origami usually uses square sheets of paper. If you didn‚Äôt find such, do not worry, we will do it ourselves. Take a regular sheet of A4 size, bend one corner so that you get a triangle. Cut out protruding parts with scissors.

We do not expand the triangle; we continue to work with it. Visually divide the sheet into three equal sections. Fold the sides to the center. Bend up the protruding corners. So the flat cap for the birthday is ready. How to make it voluminous? Just open the cap with your hand, as if flattening the corners to the center.

Ideas for decorating a paper cap

The most creative and fascinating process is decorating the cap. Decorate it the way you like. A little advice on what materials to use for this will help you with this.

You can make a festive birthday cap unusual and original by wrapping it in cloth or foil. Decorate from above with any material: sequins, rhinestones, floral butterflies, small toys, brooches, beads, photographs, etc.





If you have good artistic abilities, paint the cap with paints. Draw funny images, make a colorful gradient transition. Caps can be signed with guest names or funny nicknames.

Decorate the top with a star or a foil or thread pampushka. For trimming, use ribbons, tinsel, and ruffled corrugated paper or satin ribbon.
