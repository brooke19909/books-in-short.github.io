Visa to Greece for Russians

In order to enjoy the gorgeous beaches of the country, our compatriots must issue a "Schengen". A tourist visa allows you to stay in Greece for up to thirty days. This time is enough for those who dream of a vacation on the islands or the mainland.

You can apply for a visa at more than twenty visa centers. Every year, the list of cities in which you can draw up all the necessary documents is increasing. Next year, Greece plans to open at least ten more visa application centers in Russia.

To obtain a "Schengen" you must present the following papers:

  • profile;
  • copies and original of a foreign passport;
  • copies of a Russian passport;
  • two photographs of a standard sample;
  • insurance;
  • confirmation of hotel reservation and airline tickets;
  • financial document.

The cost of a visa is set at sixty euros. Your documents are usually considered for about two weeks in the regions and from two to five days in Moscow and St. Petersburg.

Elounda: a resort with incredibly beautiful beach areas

If you decide to go with your child to Crete, then choose this part of it. Elounda is famous throughout Greece for its luxurious hotels and an abundance of entertainment. Here you can forget about everything, immersed in the incredibly diverse life that is provided to tourists by employees of hotel complexes.

The beaches of Elounda are a special topic of conversation. They are considered one of the most beautiful in Greece and, despite their small size, are very popular among vacationers.

Private beach areas are located in the Pigadakia area. The most luxurious hotels were built here, which managed to "take hold" of a part of the beach. Almost every hotel complex has its own marina, where cute boats moored in bright colors approach.

Skisma has a city beach, which is ideal for children. Here you can relax, sunbathing and swimming in a cozy lagoon or playing volleyball and doing water sports. Kids enjoy spending time on the playground, equipped right on the beach.

On the way to Plaka there are numerous wild pebble beaches. They are surrounded by olive groves, which gives them a slightly fantastic look. Sometimes trees go down to the water, creating an incredibly beautiful landscape.

If you like beaches where everything is very well organized, then you should go to Hionas. It is municipal, but equipped with everything necessary for a good holiday. Here you will find not only sun loungers and umbrellas, but also showers, a lifeguard base and picturesque bars with an abundance of delicious snacks and drinks.

It is worth noting that most hotels in Elounda are quite expensive. But we are ready to tell you about a small hotel complex that will be affordable for any average Russian family. If you are looking for the best value for money, then opt for the Aristea Hotel.
