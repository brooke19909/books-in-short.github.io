Courchevel 1850 - the most popular ski resort in the French Alps

Courchevel 1850 is the most popular and most visited ski resort in the French Alps. People from all over the world come here to relax. The resort is famous for its wonderful tracks of different difficulty levels, excellent living conditions and excellent restaurants. We will discuss all this in our article today.

Gourmet Luxury Resort

Courchevel 1850 is one of the six ski resorts of the Three Valleys of the French Alps. It is not only above the rest, but also distinguished by the exquisite luxury of hotels and restaurants, shops and recreation areas. Prices are much higher than in the resorts located below. Courchevel is one of the ten most expensive places in the world. But it is never empty in the winter season: it is a favorite vacation spot not only for athletes, but also for monarchs, politicians, businessmen, film and pop stars.

Russian Courchevel

This most popular ski resort is often called Russian: a flag of Russia flutters over some buildings, large nesting dolls are installed, and restaurants have menus in Russian. Especially a lot of tourists from Russia are here on Christmas and New Year's Eve according to the old style, this period is even called the Russian week.

From Moscow to Courchevel - from 170 to 2200 euros

There are several options to get to this wonderful ski resort in Europe. The cheapest: fly to Chambery (distance - 2560 km, flight time - more than 3 hours, ticket price from Moscow from 110 euros), then take a train to the station Moutiers Salins (travel time - 1 hour 20 minutes, ticket price - 45 euros), then by bus to the lower resort, after spending half an hour on the road and paying 15 euros, go up to Courchevel 1850 by lift.

You can fly to Geneva (distance - 2500 km, flight time - just over 3 hours, ticket price from Moscow from 100 euros), take a bus to Courchevel (distance - 140 km, travel time - 4 hours, ticket price - 80 Euro). You can use the airports of Lyon (distance - 2540 km, flight time - 3.5 hours, ticket price from Moscow from 110 euros) or Grenoble (distance - 2520 km, flight time - 4 hours, ticket price from Moscow from 580 euro) and come to the resort by bus, then transfer to the lift.

Traveling on winding mountain roads by bus or car can be overshadowed by traffic jams, especially on weekends and during snowfalls. The most expensive option: from the air harbors of Geneva or Lyon to get to Courchevel 1850 airport by STOL light plane or helicopter in a few minutes, paying 2000 euros for the flight. It is important to know that this airport is one of the ten most dangerous in the world: it has a very short runway and no instrument landing system.

Weather in Courchevel

Although Courchevel is an alpine resort, the climate here is mild and comfortable even in winter. Already in early November, the first snow falls, which melts in May. The ski season lasts from late February to late April. During the day, the average air temperature in winter is minus 3-6 degrees, at night it can drop to 15. Snowfalls are not uncommon in this region, and snow cover by the end of November is 80 cm, in the future it can reach 3 meters.

In late February, there is already warming and plus temperatures are observed. But the quality of the tracks does not suffer from weather changes in Courchevel: hundreds of snow cannons create ideal conditions for skiing.

Accommodation in Courchevel

Accommodation in Courchevel (France) is very expensive, prices can be one and a half times higher than the cost of similar rooms in resort hotels located below. The choice is very large: hotels, apartments, chalets. But, despite this, you need to book in advance, especially in winter. As for the quality of service, you should not worry about this - everything is at a high level. In every institution of Courchevel they take care of the comfortable stay of their guests. The staff resolves all arising issues as soon as possible. In hotels, chalets, everything just sparkles with cleanliness, a chic interior pleases the eye, and high-quality service is provided. This is due to the huge competition and the "high" name of the resort. Let's consider some really worthy accommodation options.

Trails

The ski slopes of the Courchevel 1850 resort are considered the best in the country: with excellent cover, of varying difficulty and length, in picturesque places: 10 black, 38 red, 44 blue and 27 green. This is a favorite place for skiing aces and confident skiers, because the black slopes of this resort are one of the most difficult in the world. Feature - the abundance of unprepared slopes, allowing you not to repeat the route.

The most difficult tracks:

  • Saulire station (2738 m) in the areas of Col du Pas du Lac, Grand Couloir, Les Avals.
  • Col de la Loze station (2274 m) - two 1.5-km black tracks;
  • difficult sections on the slopes of Emile Allais, Jean Blanc.

Numerous lifts allow you to get to the selected track without waiting in line. Trainers work with beginners and those wishing to improve their skills. It is important to know: Courchevel ski school is considered the best in the world.

Ski pass

As in all ski resorts, Courchevel (France) has a two-pass ski pass system: the Three Valleys (600 thousand km of pistes) and Courchevel (200 thousand km of pistes):

  • 1 day: adults - 60/55, children - 50/44 euros;
  • 6 days: adults - 300/255, children - 240/205 euros;
  • 13 days: adults - 630/550, children - 505/4405 euros.

Families with children and large companies have their own rates. Children under five are free of charge. A special ski area with gentle slopes and a cottage is provided for kids, where they can relax, play, eat under supervision.

Holidays without skiing

The resort has many options for spending time: cross-country skiing tracks, sledging track, skating rink, curling, toboggan track, three snow parks, halfpipe, Forum sports complex, dog sledding, paragliding, helicopter, ballooning, racing snowmobiles. Here, skater performances, competitions of skiers, ice shows, celebrity concerts are held. Art lovers will appreciate the works of masters not only in the art galleries of the resort, but also in the design of streets and highways.

The best restaurants:

  • for lunch: Le Chalet de Pierres, Le Cap Horn, Le Tremplin;

Regardless of whether the restaurant has a star or not, each visitor will be served at a decent level. The best chefs from all over the world work in every establishment. Even the most sophisticated tourist can choose a dish to his taste.

Entertainment

In the evening and at night, the inhabitants of Courchevel move to numerous bars, restaurants, nightclubs, discos, where there are bright parties and colorful shows. The most popular clubs are Les Caves, Le Coyotte and La Grange.

Aprs-ski

In little Courchevel there are hundreds of boutiques, shops with products of famous brands. The assortment of goods is the most diverse: jewelry, perfumes, cosmetics, watches, clothes, shoes, interior items, accessories and, of course, everything you need for winter sports. Almost every hotel has several shops.

Holidays in Courchevel at any time of the year are full of events, experiences, new skills and a desire to visit the highland starry corner again. Everyone who has been here at least once wants to come back here again and enjoy an unforgettable pastime. The Courchevel 1850 ski resort is always recommended to be considered as an option for a vacation, a winter vacation or to visit it for any other reason.
