Sheraton Shenzhou Peninsula Resort 5 * (China, Wanning): photo with description, infrastructure, service, reviews

Many people, trying to decide in which exotic place they spend their vacation, decide to go to China. This is an excellent choice, especially if the goal is set to the picturesque Shenzhou Peninsula, located by the waters of the wonderful Shimei Bay. It was here that the chic 5-star Sheraton Shenzhou Peninsula Resort 5 * hotel is located, which we will discuss now.

Location

The elegant hotel complex is literally at arm's length from the South China Sea. The hotel is in an incredibly picturesque place, and just 7-10 minutes from it you can walk to the famous Dunes Golf Club in these parts.

It is also a 40-minute drive from the scenic area where the Sunlong Hot Springs and the famous Botanical Garden are located.

In terms of infrastructure, the location of the Sheraton Shenzhou Peninsula Resort is a secluded paradise. The nearest supermarket is 1.5 kilometers away, and it takes about half an hour to drive to the market by car. Sanya Phoenix Airport is 120 kilometers from the hotel.

Features of accommodation and cost

The price of a weekly stay for two people in a standard room will be approximately 40-45 thousand rubles. And this is only payment for the reservation. The option is not entirely cheap, and therefore it is better to book a ticket that already includes air travel, transfer, accommodation, meals and medical insurance. It will be a much more profitable solution.

It is important to make a reservation that the hotel has special conditions for guests with children. When placing all kids who are not 12 years old, you do not need to pay extra for them. And for very tiny guests (up to 2 years old) the administration will provide a cradle, and this is absolutely free.

If the guests arrived with older children or just with another guest, you will have to pay an amount based on the calculation of 688 CNY per night.

By the way, pets are also allowed. Only the guest must notify in advance that he will come to rest with a pet. Other additional services, as well as their provision, are also discussed individually.

You should also know that upon check-in you will need to provide not only your passport, but also a credit card. The property accepts Visa, American Express, JCB, Diners Club and Master Card.
