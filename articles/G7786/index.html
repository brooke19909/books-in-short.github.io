Negative reviews

There are some significant shortcomings in such a resort institution as Sveltos Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Larnaca, Oroklini). Based on the reviews, you can highlight the following negative points:

  • youth accustomed to noisy entertainment, this institution may seem boring;
  • low speed wireless internet;
  • inconvenient location (nearby there is not so much infrastructure, but even normal walking sidewalks);
  • paid use of wireless Internet;
  • very limited range of buffets;
  • sliding panoramic windows do not close on a latch, lock or in any other way, and therefore there is constant concern for the safety of personal items;
  • poor sound insulation in the rooms, because of which everything that happens behind the wall, as well as in the corridor, is audible in the room;
  • low-quality instant coffee is served during breakfast;
  • use of the refrigerator and safe in the room is paid;
  • lunch is not held according to the buffet system (that is, guests cannot choose their own meals);
  • tennis court, as well as related equipment is in poor condition;
  • with a certain direction of the wind there is a strong unpleasant odor on the territory, which comes from neighboring farms;
  • Despite the ban on smoking in the territory, many guests ignore it, and the staff does not react to it in any way;
  • Some of the rooms overlook the road, which creates additional noise;
  • there is no clothesline or clothespins on the balcony (you need to bring with you or buy on the spot);
  • due to the poor functioning of the ventilation system, the bathroom is very damp and mold is present.
