Bogmallo Beach Resort 4 *. Holidays in India, Goa: reviews of tourists

This article is about the Bogmallo Beach Resort 4. It is located in the Indian state of Goa. This is a front page hotel. White clean sand, gentle waves of the Arabian Sea - what else could a soul want? Below we will talk about what Goa (India) is famous for, what you need to see and try there. The information in this article is completely taken from the reviews of tourists.



What is Goa?

Those who have already visited India will be surprised a lot when they arrive in this state. Its European nature immediately catches your eye: narrow streets, many Catholic monasteries, Spanish architecture of buildings. There is nothing surprising. Until the middle of the last century, this territory belonged to Portugal. As a place of rest Goa discovered hippies in the seventies. "Children of the flowers" was chosen by the once deserted coast and tiny fishing villages. So the first resorts were formed, dotting a hundred and ten kilometers of the coast of the Arabian Sea. When in Europe it became known that there were forty excellent beaches in Goa, a richer audience poured into India. Hippies and their later followers - downshifters - gradually moved north. And on New Year's Eve, when many Russian tourists appear at the resorts, lovers of trans parties generally move for two weeks to the neighboring states of Maharashtra and Kerala. Thus, over time, Goa began to be divided into two parts - northern and southern. Each of them has its own specifics and color.



North and South Goa (India)

The conditional border runs along the fort of Aguada. The two halves of the state look complete opposites of each other. The resorts of South Goa are located on magnificent beaches, but they are completely devoid of any hint of ancient and colorful India. Expensive hotels are designed for very wealthy tourists from Western Europe and the USA, as well as for wealthy Indians. The prices for living here are high. At the same time, resorts position themselves as places for a quiet, measured, sometimes family vacation. North Goa - democratic, noisy, funny. Hippies, rastamans and ordinary downshifters (those who sacrificed their careers in Europe for peace of mind under the generous sun of India) live here. Since Bogmallo Beach Resort 4 is located four kilometers from the international airport and eight kilometers from the city of Vasco da Gama, it belongs to South Goa. The resort where it is located is called Bogmallo.

When do you need to go on vacation in Goa?

The west coast of Hindustan is famous for its mild, uniform climate. From the east, resorts distance the Western Ghats from the mainland . Therefore, monsoons, bringing in June-July rains in other regions of the country, are not felt much here. This provides year-round holidays in India. Reviews of tourists say that the best time to travel to Goa is from October to March. There is practically no rain at this time, and the air temperature is very comfortable for Europeans (from +22 to +32 ¬∞ C). Then, from April to June, it gets too hot. The high humidity in the summer exacerbates the heat, although this is not felt on the beach. From June to September, the sea can storm, and the temperature may drop to +20 - +25 ¬∞ . If you plan to come to Goa with a child, it is best to choose the period October-March. The nights are warm, the sea is calm and not very hot. The kid will not need acclimatization. Although prices at this time are becoming slightly higher than in other seasons.



South Goa: its advantages and disadvantages

The resorts of this part of the state - Majorda, Mobor, Cavelossim, Palolem, Benaulim, Colva, Dona Paula and, of course, Bogmallo - are famous for their beaches. The sand on them is snow-white, while in North Goa it is dark gray, volcanic. But the best beach in the south of the state is the ten-kilometer strip of Varca and Cavelossim, stretching from the resort of Benaulim. About a dozen luxury hotels have been built on this coastline of the highest category. The dignity of the South is also considered the terrain. The coast here is low, gentle, which allows you to build hotels almost on the beach. And South Goa fully meets these expectations. The coast here is incomparable - this is evidenced by the reviews of even the most fastidious tourists. The sand is small, white, soft.
