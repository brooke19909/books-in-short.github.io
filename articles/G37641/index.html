How to make a choker on the neck with your own hands?

The word "choker" in translation from English means "strangler". But do not be afraid and go to another page. The article is not about crime, but about a very original decoration that looks stylish and fashionable. Consider how to make a choker on the neck with your own hands.





Decoration story

This decoration was the first to be worn by the Indians of North America. They made it from bones, fangs, skin and veins, which emphasized their status as a warrior. In the Victorian era, the choker got to the UK. Queen Victoria, who was a trendsetter at that time, reigned passionately in love with such jewelry. Therefore, tightly adjacent to the neck "stranglers" have become an integral part of the rococo.

Then the choker on the neck was woven of lace, leather, with the addition of ribbons, precious stones and so on. Jewelry returned to the modern world thanks to the hippie generation, when they began to weave from fishing line. And the dark color allowed to get a tattoo imitation on the neck.

Style and Choker

Today, necklaces are especially loved by representatives of informal movements: punks, Goths, tumblr and others. The decoration mainly symbolizes rebellion and protest. So, the famous rebels Britney Spears and Angelina Jolie often wore them.





Of course, not all people will wear such jewelry due to the fact that not every style is suitable for them. But the youth loved to wear them, protesting against the serious life of adults. It is so simple to master weaving that even a child can create a simple decoration.

How to make a choker with your own hands?

Let's try to figure out how to weave a necklace. To do this, you will need very little. The simplest jewelry is made of ordinary thread or fishing line. If there are already unnecessary wires from the headphones, then they can also be used. Whatever is chosen as the material, the scheme of how to make a choker on the neck remains approximately the same.

First, two threads should be fastened or tied to each other. This can be done using a binder on the table. Next, the macrame technique is used, namely the principle of fours. In this case, one thread is taken to the side and pressed under the next one. Then, if at this time the finger is released and the thread is not pulled, a four will be visible.

Next, you need to take the working thread and insert it into the window through the top, simulating a knot, and slightly tighten. The same is done both on one side and on the other side until the thread ends.





If you want to use the wires from the headphones, then only the thinnest of them will do. Otherwise, the decoration will be too coarse. A beautiful and unusual effect can be achieved if the choker on the neck with your own hands is woven from several assembled threads. Get a multi-layer original drawing.

For weaving, it is best to find a dark fishing line or a thin waxed cord. Only with the latter one should be careful, as it is unpleasant to the body. Well, to simulate a tattoo, whatever material is selected, it should be thin, dark and soft.

The pattern is the same. But if you wish, you can familiarize yourself with other macram√© patterns and use different colors. Then a do-it-yourself choker on the neck will become a very interesting and non-standard option, attracting the eye.

Various options: use fantasy

Bead jewelry will also look beautiful. It is best to choose an oblong view or bugle. Naturally, you can choose ordinary beads or having different sizes and shapes. You also need to purchase a fishing line or kapron thread.

Another equally effective option would be netting. The principle is as follows. A round bead is strung on the center of the line. Then on both sides add one long. Then round and long again. After that, a round bead is strung on one side, and a thread is threaded into it, forming a bundle and pulling so that in the end a square is obtained.

So repeat to the required length. You can expand this choker on your neck with your own hands if you walk in the opposite direction, using as one side already woven beads that will connect both rows.

The decoration can be weaved from elastic lace or fabric and decorated with stones or beads, and fasten fasteners on both sides.

Some needlewomen even crochet jewelry. At the same time, it can look no worse than an expensive designer necklace. The main advice for those who are considering how to make a choker on their neck - use your imagination and imagination. Then the result can exceed all expectations.

Conclusion





In the article, we learned how to make a choker on the neck from a fishing line. In addition, other materials may be used. Threads, satin or leather ribbons, a variety of beads and much more can truly emphasize an individual style and at the same time adorn the neck so that this area stands out and attracts the eye.
