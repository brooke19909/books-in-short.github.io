Hotel Sundance Resort Hotel 5 * (Bodrum, Turkey): description and reviews of tourists

Wanting to change the atmosphere of noisy megacities, upon the arrival of the long-awaited annual vacation, many opt for a wonderfully colorful country - Turkey. It is here that not only the azure shores, the purest smooth water and cozy bays are concentrated, but also for lovers of active night life, there are many discos, clubs and parties where they can relax and enjoy their stay in this hot state.

Resort Information

Bodrum is a picturesque town located off the coast of the Aegean and Mediterranean Seas. Rocky terrain, pine forests and clearings with fruit trees cause an ecologically clean atmosphere and contribute to the healing of the body. The resort is great for youth and family spending.

The five-star Sundance Resort is located on the beachfront and welcomes guests of all ages. The courteous and attentive staff gives a good mood to every spa person, turning the days spent here into a holiday.

All rooms vary in total area, maximum capacity and views. The interior is presented with modern design without any frills. The decor of the room is practical and concise. The color scheme is represented mainly by a combination of pastel colors with bright splashes. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles.

Each apartment has access to a balcony or terrace, where you can meet the first rays of the rising sun or witness the appearance of the first star in the sky. French windows allow sparkling rays to penetrate, awakening every guest.

Reviews about Sundance Resort Hotel 5 * are extremely rarely negative. Basically, all tourists are satisfied with the quality of cleaning. They note that the hotel staff makes every effort to ensure that your stay here does not leave a negative impression. Housekeeping is carried out daily, bed linen is changed three times a week. Room Service is available 24 hours a day, but requires an additional charge.





Hotel Room Features

The rooms of the Sundance Resort Hotel 5 * (Bodrum) contain everything you need for a comfortable stay and a carefree pastime. The furniture set includes soft beds, a desk, a spacious wardrobe, chairs and bedside tables. A large mirror visually enlarges the space. An individual air conditioning system maintains the necessary microclimate. Staying in the room will not be boring thanks to the multi-channel TV with Russian channels. An internal telephone allows instant communication with the reception.

The presence of an electric kettle, a set of dishes and a tea set allows you to drink aromatic coffee or a cup of tea on a cozy balcony equipped with plastic chairs and a table. Use of the safe is free.

The minibar is replenished every day with a variety of soft drinks, beer and drinking water. In order to take care and ensure the safety of holidaymakers, the apartments are equipped with fire safety systems. Wi-fi - in the public domain.

The bathroom is equipped with a shower, hairdryer and sink. Decorated with ceramic tiles. Has the required number of towels that meets international standards. White terry bathrobes help to feel at home.





Supply system

The functioning of the hotel complex Sundance Resort Hotel (Bodrum) adheres to the concept of All Inclusive. According to her, catering is carried out in the form of a buffet three times a day, in accordance with a predetermined schedule. The Islands main restaurant, designed for 390 seats, has both an indoor lounge and an open-air terrace. The variety of culinary delights, in the opinion of experienced, is very large and unsurpassedly delicious. The interior is decorated in the same theme with the main building, it blows warmth, comfort and cordiality, which favorably affects the growth of appetite and its speedy satisfaction.

Bars and restaurants A la Carte

Restaurants of Ottoman, Indian and Chinese cuisines, whose work is carried out according to a custom-made menu, are available for visiting in the evening. Preliminary reservation of the table and the execution of the dress code are required: beach suits are not allowed at the entrance.

You can quench your thirst in one of the bars: on the beach, by the pool or in the lobby. Consumption of local drinks is included in the All Inclusive program. Freshly squeezed juices and imported alcohol are additionally paid.

The snack bar allows you to satisfy a slight feeling of hunger with a snack of pastries, Turkish sweets, ice cream or a light snack.





Beach

In order to reach the treasured waters of the Aegean Sea, vacationers need to spend only a few minutes. The beach infrastructure is quite developed and contains everything necessary for a long time to stay here. Multiple sun loungers with soft bedding allow you to experience a sense of relaxation while sunbathing. Umbrellas and shadow canopies are intended for those who hide from the sultry sun.

The essential components are locker rooms, a toilet, showers, where everyone, leaving the beach, can wash off sea salts.

The beach strip is quite narrow, which is why the pontoon was built, which is equipped with additional sunbeds. Many travelers prefer to sunbathe here, because being surrounded on all sides by a deep water immerses you in the feeling of being on a small island.

The beach is sandy, the entrance to the water is gentle, which ensures the safety of the play of young children. However, not everyone wants to bask in the sun. For lovers of outdoor activities provided a volleyball field with the necessary equipment. A variety of water activities allows you to get a lot of new sensations and a dose of adrenaline. Go out into the open sea and sink to the bottom, getting acquainted with the rich world of flora and fauna of this environment, is accessible to both professionals and inexperienced divers.

Organization of children's leisure in the hotel

Sundance Resort Hotel receives positive reviews from grateful tourists who have come to rest with their children. To organize a rich and unforgettable children's vacation, a team of cheerful animators works tirelessly, who daily turn into new fairy-tale characters, give children fun, smiles and joy. Sports, games for the manifestation of ingenuity, cooking classes are held according to a predetermined schedule, which can be found on special stands.

The doors of the kids club are open to guests aged 4 to 12 years. Under the supervision of a qualified teacher, the children will make unusual crafts, draw unique drawings and play various games.

In the fresh air there are playgrounds in shady areas with many slides, swings and carousels. Running through the intricate labyrinths, wasting their strength before an evening sleep, the kids enjoy a wonderful pastime. The evening ends with a disco, where dancing talents and plastic of each baby are manifested.

High chairs and a children's buffet are provided for the youngest children. A cot is available on request. Babysitting services are paid extra.
