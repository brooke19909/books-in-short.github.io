SunConnect Evita Resort 4 * (Greece, Faliraki, Rhodes): general information

The resort opened its doors on August 24, 2015. Iza 2 years of existence received a lot of good reviews. It features 2 outdoor pools, Wi-Fi, barbecue facilities, restaurants and bars, a sun terrace, a children's playground, and free private parking.

Special attention should be paid to leisure activities at SunConnect Evita Resort 4 * (Rhodes, Greece). At the request of guests, staff can help rent bicycles and cars and provide everything you need for playing billiards and tennis.

Beach and water activities

In the immediate vicinity of the SunConnect Evita Resort, you will find the main attractions of the resorts awarded the UNESCO Blue Flag - Faliraki. The beach is as much as 4 km of golden sand strewn along the sea. Rumor has it that even swimming far behind the buoys, you can see every pebble at the bottom.

2 sunbeds and an umbrella will cost about 8 euros, and every 100-150 m there are free showers (open) with fresh water. If you come to sunbathe and enjoy the calm atmosphere, then Faliraki is not the place, because the beach offers the largest number of water activities on the island.





At the height of the season, guests at the SunConnect Evita Resort swim not only on water, but also under water, and they also fly through the sky with parachutes tied to a boat.

Approximate prices for entertainment on the beach of Faliraki:

  • windsurfing - 18 euros for 1 hour + 3 euros for the rental of wetsuits;
  • rent a jet ski - 35 euros for 15 minutes;
  • water skiing - 25 euros per lap;
  • "Crazy sofa" - 12 euros;
  • Fly fish - 20 euros for 10 minutes.

An interesting feature of the beach: part of the territory is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of nudists. They also bathe, rent sun loungers and ride bananas.

What do Rhodes and Anthony Quinn have in common?

On the thematic forums, reviews of the SunConnect Evita Resort have repeatedly emphasized its advantageous location. Just look at the photo and you will understand that the place is worth a visit and how lucky the hotel guests are.





Anthony Quinn Bay was named after the famous Hollywood actor of the last century. However, tourists do not come here because of this fact, especially since few people even know and remember this talented person. Guests of SunConnect Evita Resort 4 * attracts to this place for two reasons: the first is the proximity to the hotel (only 700 m), the second is natural beauty.

Rocks rise on the shore of Anthony Quinn Bay - some of them are occupied by sun loungers, while others customarily jump into the crystal clear water from which one does not want to leave. Firstly, the place is hidden from the winds and the sea manages to warm up well. Secondly, the emerald-transparent surface attracts to the study of underwater secrets.

Like most of the beaches of Rhodes, the bay is perfectly adapted for a comfortable rest of tourists - there are changing cabins, deck chairs with umbrellas, several taverns, an outdoor shower, parking and water activities. True, it is worth noting one drawback: the beach is narrow and small, so the sun loungers are arranged in 2 rows, and so close to each other that during a large influx of vacationers it can be uncomfortable.

Wealthy tourists come here on rented or their own yachts to swim not from the shore of the bay, but to dive directly from the sides.
