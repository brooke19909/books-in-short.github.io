Resort Abkhazia. Sanatoriums and pensions with treatment: rating, description and reviews

Abkhazia is famous not only for its hospitality, but also for its wonderful natural wealth, which allows it to occupy a leading position in the resort business. Abkhazia is also famous for its cultural traditions. The country is buried in numerous historical buildings and cultural monuments. "Cote d'Azur"

The favorable location and modern repair made the resort a leader among the boarding houses of Abkhazia. The resort is located at the foot of the Gagra Range, on the Black Sea. In 2010, a major overhaul of the health resort was carried out. Not far from the pension is the palace of the Prince of Oldenburg and a picturesque park. On the territory there are two three-story buildings, accommodating 77 rooms, which are not inferior in quality to the apartments in a four-star hotel. The price includes a round-the-clock hot and cold water supply, a fresh water pool, a gym, and children's attractions. For an additional fee you can use the Internet, a bar, a cafe, a parking lot, a spa complex, excursion services. Guest reviews about this place are enthusiastic.





Aibga

The health resort is located in the New Gagra area. The distance from Adler is only 26 km. The complex is considered one of the safest, as it is located in a closed guarded area. Vacationers highly appreciate the infrastructure, as just a few steps from the resort there is a market, numerous cafes, bars, shops, ticket offices and even a water park. The distance from the clean landscaped beach is only 150 m. Visitors are provided with a guarded parking lot, massage room, satellite TV, Internet, heated pool, karaoke. "Moscow"

The health resort occupies a vast territory of 6.3 hectares, which is a park area. The resort has its own beach with sand and pebbles.
