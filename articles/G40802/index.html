Ruby Hotel Nha Trang 3 (Vietnam / Nha Trang): description and reviews

Over the past few years, Vietnam has become a very attractive country for travelers. The tourist infrastructure here is constantly evolving, and the number of tourists is increasing annually. Naturally, a person deciding to visit this country expects to spend at least a few days on one of the picturesque beaches.

If we are talking about resort holidays on the coast of Vietnam, then one of the most popular tourist cities, without a doubt, is Nha Trang. There are countless hotels of different categories and sizes. Quite often, travelers choose the Ruby Hotel Nha Trang 3 * as a place of temporary refuge.

When planning a trip and choosing a hotel, an experienced tourist pays attention to many factors. For example, you should find out about the location of the hotel, the distance to the beach (because you have to overcome it daily), as well as about living conditions. What is a hotel and what are the impressions left by travelers?

Location Features





As you know, the location of the hotel is an important point. Where to look for the hotel complex Ruby Nha Trang Hotel 3 *?

Description of infrastructure and hotel territory





The hotel complex Ruby Hotel Nha Trang 3 * consists of one large four-story building - this is a city-style hotel. Of course, the hotel has an elevator system. The first floor is reserved for the relaxation room, reception, but on the last there is a cozy restaurant. Own territory and courtyard here are very small. Nevertheless, they equipped a cozy green terrace for relaxation, as well as parking.

By the way, the hotel is relatively new - it was opened in 2012. Over the past years, they have carried out cosmetic repairs several times, changed equipment and updated. Today the hotel complex is considered one of the most convenient and cozy in the area - tourists are happy to spend time here.

What categories of rooms can I choose from?

The Ruby Hotel Nha Trang 3 * hotel offers its customers a choice of one of 75 small, but stylishly decorated and comfortable rooms. There are rooms of several categories. For example, guests can stay in one of 53 Superior Rooms - here you will find a large sleeping area, as well as a balcony with amazing views of the city and the sea coast. There are also 20 Deluxe Rooms - these are rooms with a high level of comfort. There are two suites. The hotel administrator will advise you on the most suitable room at the time of booking.

Hotel Ruby Hotel Nha Trang 3 *: photo and additional information about the rooms





Of course, after a day full of interesting events and entertainment, I want to return to a clean comfortable room and relax. Hotel guests can count on modern comfort and quality service.

The rooms are decorated in a discreet but pleasant style. Large beds, a spacious wardrobe, even a desk - this makes staying in the room really pleasant. Of course, at your service is a new, modern technology. For example, air conditioning will help to cope with the heat, and you can pass the time watching TV channels (though almost all of them are local). The room has a telephone and a compact safe with a combination lock.

Surely you will enjoy the spacious bathroom with shower, toilet, washbasin and a large mirror. Guests are provided with high-quality hygiene items, a hair dryer, as well as a set of towels (including large beach ones) and soft slippers. The rooms are always clean, as maids come here every day.

Do they provide meals?





Of course, for tourists, the important question is whether it is possible to rely on food when settling in the Ruby Nha Trang Hotel 3 *. Vietnam is a country in which most of the hotels operate according to the BB scheme, offering their guests only breakfast. This resort is no exception - paying for accommodation, guests also receive the right to daily morning meals, which takes place in the form of a buffet from 6 to 10 in the morning. Breakfast is served in the luxurious restaurant located on the top floor. Through panoramic windows you can enjoy views of the surroundings.

By the way, reviews say that the menu here is not bad - guests are served salads, fresh fruits, pastries, toasts, fried eggs, in a word, traditional breakfast dishes. The rest of the day, you can also eat at the local restaurant, but already paying the bill. Visitors are offered traditional Vietnamese cuisine, as well as some European dishes.

Near the hotel, and on the beach itself, there are several cafes and restaurants where, for a small fee, you can have a delicious lunch or dinner, so meals are unlikely to be a problem.

Where is the beach located? Entertainment and facilities





Of course, when arriving in Vietnam, many tourists wish to spend at least a few days on the coast of the sea. What can the hotel offer Ruby Nha Trang Hotel 3 *? Nha Trang, one of the most picturesque beaches, is located just 50 meters from the hotel building. A convenient walking path leads to it. Most travelers consider this location an important advantage of the hotel.

It‚Äôs worth saying right away that this beach is public. Nevertheless, there are not too many tourists here. For a small fee, you can rent a sunbed with a parasol. A chilled drink is also available here. The coast, as well as the entrance to the sea, is clean - it is cleaned daily.

If you want to spend time more actively, you should contact your local water sports center. Here you can rent a catamaran or boat, as well as ride a boat, banana or water skiing. Those who wish can engage in windsurfing and sailing, scuba diving - on the shore you can not only rent the necessary equipment, but also take the help of an experienced instructor.

The list of additional services





As already mentioned, the hotel is small and designed primarily for a comfortable overnight stay and relaxation in the evenings. Nevertheless, you can count on some additional services. The reception is open 24 hours a day - you can register or receive the necessary information at any time.

Throughout the hotel there is wireless Internet access, and the connection speed is decent - this factor is very important for many travelers. Parking is available near the building, which guests can also use for free. By the way, in the city you can easily rent a car or a moped - this is a convenient and not too expensive way to move.

How to spend time at the hotel? Leisure tourists





The hotel has a rooftop terrace with a spacious terrace where guests can relax and unwind. There is a small pool with clean fresh water where you can swim and refresh while enjoying breathtaking views of the beach and the blue expanse of the sea.

There is an excursion center at the hotel, where polite staff will tell you about the most interesting places in the vicinity, help you plan a travel route or choose an interesting tour. By the way, if you wish, you can even visit the nearby islands - reviews indicate that these are incredibly beautiful, heavenly places. And many more guests enjoy spending time fishing or cycling.

Is it possible to relax here with a child?

Of course, rest and recreation on the beach is necessary not only for adult travelers, but also for their children. Is the hotel Ruby Hotel Nha Trang 3 * suitable for family holidays?

In fact, there are no special services or activities for the child at the hotel. Nevertheless, guests can count on a certain level of comfort. For example, a comfortable folding bed is delivered to the room upon request. For breakfast in the restaurant you can also choose something tasty and healthy for the baby (by the way, the waiters offer special high chairs).

Children get a lot of pleasant experiences playing and swimming on a luxurious sandy beach. And in the city and its environs there are a sufficient number of entertainment centers where not only children of different ages can have a good time, but also their parents.
