Lublino: Shopping center "Moscow" - a wholesale and retail center of the South of the capital

general characteristics

Initially, the market in Lyublino shopping center "Moscow" was opened to meet the needs of residents of nearby areas. Here it was possible to buy first and second necessities at an affordable price.



For these purposes, equipped with 175,000 square meters. m more than 5,000 retail locations. The shopping center itself is not only a two-story building, where there are retail outlets, catering places, an ice rink and a cinema, it is also 2 construction markets that are located nearby, and a parking lot for 8,000 places. From 30 to 70 thousand people visit the center daily, not only from the capital, but also from the regions.

The main groups of goods in the market

Most often in Lublin shopping center "Moscow" people come for clothes. Here is one of the largest jeans markets in the region. Moreover, their prices are much lower than even in provincial cities, which allows entrepreneurs to purchase here.

There are also household appliances on the market: from players and mobile phones, to televisions and refrigerators.

On the second floor there are points selling clothing for adults and children, interior textiles and bedding, jewelry, accessories and leather goods.

The structure of the shopping center includes two construction markets: one of them offers goods for repair, and the second sells everything for construction.



The assortment of the market is constantly expanding and updated in accordance with current trends. But along with this, many notice a significant drop in the quality of goods offered at the Moscow Shopping Center. Therefore, it is worth purchasing them with special care.

The cafe and fast food system in the center

In Lublin, the Moscow Shopping Center offers a wide selection of Asian cuisine in a variety of cafes located on the second floor of the center. Since most of the sellers in the complex are representatives of Asia, places to eat delicious food are open to their taste.

Visitors to the complex can taste Chinese, Vietnamese, Tatar, Kyrgyz cuisine. At the same time, the cooks are representatives of those nations whose cuisine they cook.

For lovers of more traditional food, you can find a couple of cafes with European cuisine. But in any case, you need to carefully approach the choice of the place where you are going to have a bite. Despite this, the food here is delicious, and many visitors specially go here for lunch or dinner.
