3 * Hotels: Alor Grande Holiday Resort (India / Goa). Photos and reviews of tourists

The 3 * Alor Grande Holiday Resort provides its guests with an excellent opportunity to relax on sunny Goa for a little money and get acquainted with the color and traditions of India.





Hotel Summary

The 3 * Alor Grande Holiday Resort is just the perfect place for those who have long dreamed of an exotic sea vacation, but are not ready to pay a large sum for staying in a fashionable hotel. The city beach is just a 5-minute walk from the hotel, and if you do not want to go anywhere, a spacious clean pool awaits you in the hotel's courtyard.

The rooms of the 3 * Alor Grande Holiday Resort hotel are characterized by a rather modest interior, however, the apartments will seem quite comfortable to the picky tourist. The emphasis is on wooden furniture with elements of a classic Indian decor. The rooms also have a balcony from which you can admire the well-groomed courtyard or watch the busy traffic of local streets.

Indians know a lot about relaxing and improving procedures, so guests of the 3 * Alor Grande Holiday Resort should definitely take this opportunity to visit the hotel‚Äôs wellness center. A sports hall is responsible for an active lifestyle. Be sure to use the services of a tour desk and go on a trip along the most popular sightseeing routes.





Hotel Location Features

Hotel Alor Grande Holiday Resort 3 * (Goa) is characterized by a rather favorable location. It is just a 5-minute leisurely walk from the famous Candolim Beach. The hotel complex is located in the northern part of Goa and is 21 km from the railway station, and 45 km from the nearest airport. The bus station is 12 km away.

Hotel rooms

Hotel Alor Grande Holiday Resort 3 * (India, Goa) is a large three-story mansion, which houses 70 guest rooms. All of them are similar to each other and differ only in color scheme. All hotel rooms can be classified as Standard. They are designed to accommodate two guests in a large bed or a pair of single beds.

The following amenities are available in each room of the hotel:

  • bathroom with toilet and shower; there is also a hairdryer;
  • there is a small TV that is connected to the hotel satellite dish (shows one Russian news channel);
  • the phone serves as a means of communication with the reception staff, and to make an international call, you must first wait for the connection with the operator;
  • mini-bar can be used both for its intended purpose and as a small refrigerator;
  • a set of all necessary furniture for living.




Hotel infrastructure

The Alor Grande Holiday Resort 3 * hotel has a good tourist infrastructure, which includes a number of objects:

  • spacious terrace for gatherings and contemplation of the surroundings;
  • a small but well-groomed garden, where many exotic flowers, palm trees and other plants characteristic of India grow;
  • a modern and spacious conference and business meeting room;
  • room for registration of marriages and special occasions, decorated in a festive style;
  • an outdoor fresh water pool located in the courtyard of the hotel;
  • a fitness center with several spacious rooms and a decent set of equipment and other equipment;
  • for children there is a large and bright playroom with a large number of various toys;
  • a restaurant where you can try local dishes, as well as less piquant European products;
  • a bar serving alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks (a TV also works here - you can watch sports matches);
  • tour desk where you can order interesting and exciting tours;
  • private parking area;
  • currency exchange office;
  • a left-luggage office where bulky suitcases can be handed over so that they do not take up space in small enough rooms;
  • at the reception there is a paid safe.




Hotel Services

To make the stay of tourists as comfortable as possible, the Alor Grande Holiday Resort provides them with the following services:

  • for 300 rupees per day, you can use wireless Internet throughout the hotel;
  • childcare and childcare services;
  • the ability to order food, dishes or drinks from a restaurant or bar directly to your room;
  • You can rent a car, bike or motorcycle;
  • By prior arrangement, you will be provided with a transfer to the airport or to any other point you need (for example, a tourist attraction or attractions);
  • if you have certain problems (for example, problems in the room) or questions regarding hotel accommodation, you can contact the reception at any time;
  • Hotel staff can assist you in purchasing tickets (by plane, train or bus);
  • You can send correspondence by mail;
  • in the laundry room you can help remove stains from your belongings, as well as iron clothes;
  • if you feel unwell, it is possible to invite a qualified doctor to the hotel;
  • if you wish, you will be delivered daily local fresh press.




Entertainment

A full and vivid vacation full of unforgettable impressions is impossible to imagine without entertainment. That is why the Alor Grande Holiday Resort Candolim 2 * hotel provides guests with a lot of opportunities for an interesting pastime:

  • staff at the tour desk organize walking tours of the local natural and major tourist attractions, which are located near the hotel;
  • literally five minutes walk from the hotel there is a well-kept public beach;
  • You can also swim in plenty in the pool with clean fresh water, which is located in the shade of the palm trees of the hotel courtyard;
  • drink a glass of beer and play billiards in the room located next to the bar;
  • You can do your figure in a small but well-equipped gym;
  • Hotel guests have access to equipment for playing darts and table tennis;
  • You can go on short or long excursions along the popular tourist route in order to explore natural and architectural sights;
  • Every evening, the hotel's restaurant hosts an entertainment program with invited musicians, dancers, singers and artists from several other genres.




Positive reviews

Despite the fact that the Alor Grande Holiday Resort 2 * hotel can be attributed to the budget category, it offers its guests quite comfortable living conditions, as well as a decent level of service. All this is reflected in the many positive reviews from guests who have already managed to visit this hotel:

  • the price includes a tasty and satisfying breakfast (the only drawback is that it is held too early);
  • the rooms have a snow-white bed, which is often changed;
  • good quality of cleaning (every day the floors are washed and polished to shine)
  • friendly and friendly staff;
  • clean well-kept pool;
  • the road from the hotel to the beach is ennobled and planted with greenery;
  • it is possible to use the safe, which is located at the reception;
  • round the clock there is hot water;
  • the hotel is very conveniently located both relative to the beach, and in terms of the distance to the infrastructure facilities of urban and tourist life;
  • excellent quality and taste of the dishes in the restaurant.




Negative reviews

India is a rather specific tourist destination, which is manifested not only in the local flavor and climatic conditions, but also in the peculiarities of the hotel business. In this regard, tourists have a number of comments on the hotel, which are reflected in negative reviews:

  • on the day of departure you need to leave the room before 10 a.m., although in most other hotels the check-out time is at noon;
  • very old furniture and plumbing;
  • holey towels in the bathroom;
  • in the rooms and corridors of the hotel there is a persistent smell of poison for insects;
  • in the evening and at night around the hotel it is too noisy, and due to poor sound insulation everything is heard in the room.
