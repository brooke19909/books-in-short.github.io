Bella Vista 4 * (Montenegro): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

One of the favorite destinations of our tourists, especially in the summer is Montenegro. Hotel Bella Vista 4 * has long been hosting travelers from Russia. Therefore, we were able to collect information of interest to tourists about this hotel, which was left in the form of reviews by people who have already been there. Information about the rooms and meals are accompanied by photographs, from which you can make an opinion in what area this hotel is located.

Despite the fact that this hotel is on the second line, to get to the beach, you just need to cross the footbridge to the road. This is a private hotel, built in 2003. It serves as a real decoration of the Budva Riviera. Nearby there are restaurants, fish taverns, and on the beach vacationers will find a lot of water activities.



Location of the hotel Bella Vista 4 *

Becici (Montenegro) is a popular resort five kilometers from the ancient city of Budva. The hotel is twenty-seven kilometers from Tivat Airport. One and a half kilometers from Becici is another resort of the Budva Riviera - Rafailovici. These places attract tourists with magnificent pebble beaches, clean Adriatic Sea and a high level of service. It will be comfortable for both lovers of a relaxing holiday and fans of an active lifestyle. From Becici, excursions are organized around the country, as well as to Albania and the Italian city of Bari. Tourists are strongly advised to visit Tara Canyon. In size, it is inferior only to Colorado, in the United States. In addition to the natural attractions of the canyon, tourists are delighted with the 365-meter "Dzhurdzhevicha" bridge that connects both coasts over the abysses. Tourists also leave favorable reviews about fishing on the Adriatic. Numerous playgrounds are provided for young children, and slides on the beach.

Rooms, service

The territory of the Bella Vista 4 * hotel (Montenegro) is small. The hotel itself consists of two buildings. In one - the usual hotel rooms, and in the second - apartments (studios). They can consist of either one or two bedrooms. It offers views of the sea, side streets or mountains. All guest rooms have balconies. Tourists leave positive feedback about two categories of rooms at once. Spacious bathrooms, hairdryer, free safe and Wi-Fi, new comfortable furniture, satellite TV, air conditioning, daily replenishment of hygiene products. The studio has a kitchenette with a refrigerator, sink, stove, full set of dishes. They clean the rooms every day, regularly change towels. Despite the modest size of the territory, the hotel can please its guests with various services. For example, there is a fully equipped gym. There is also a sauna with a jacuzzi. Living in the room can use it for free for one hour. Tourists warn that when checking in, you need to ask at the reception code for "Wi-Fi": the Internet also catches in rooms, and each room has its own password.



Nutrition

In the Bella Vista 4 * hotel (Montenegro), reviews are advised to order breakfast. Tourists claim: after such a hearty morning meal, you will not want to eat until the evening. Two types of omelettes, sausages alone, three varieties, and also cheeses, sausages, stewed vegetables, salads, fruits and delicious pastries of their own preparation. For fans of a healthy lifestyle, breakfast includes cereal, cereal, yogurt, boiled eggs, fresh vegetables. The morning meal is held in buffet style, but the evening meal is a la carte. The chef of the restaurant offers to try the delights of Montenegrin, Italian cuisines. Tourists living in the apartments recommend shopping in the supermarket at the Mediterran stop. There it is cheaper than in small shops, a richer choice of products. The hotel also has a bar where you can drink coffee or something stronger. Food can also be brought to your room at an additional cost.



Sea and beach

Hotel Bella Vista 4 * (Montenegro) is on the second lane. And in Becici nothing is located directly on the shore. So you have to cross the road. To avoid accidents and accidents, a special bridge is provided for this. The beach in Becici is among the top ten best in Montenegro. To appreciate this, take a trip to Budva: narrow stone shores, crowds of people, dirty water. But Becici and Rafailovici - as if not five kilometers away, but on another planet: sand, clear sea, not many people. The beach stretches for two kilometers. Admission is free, but an umbrella plus a sunbed - ten euros per day. Although many tourists are not shy and spread their towels in the sand. People who like to spend time actively advise to sunbathe on the beach of the nearby Arena Bar Hotel. There is a bar, there is free wireless Internet, and around - a lot of water activities.
