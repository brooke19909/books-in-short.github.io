Phone "Lenovo A536": reviews, overview of characteristics

An affordable entry-level smartphone with a diagonal of 5 inches is the Lenovo A536. Reviews about the model, the capabilities of the phone and its filling - this is what will be described in detail in this short review material.





Packaging and design

Although the gadget belongs to the segment of budget devices, the configuration of the device does not cause complaints at Lenovo A536. Reviews once again convince of this. The set of documentation includes a warranty card and user manual. In addition to the phone itself, in the kit there are accessories such as a stereo headset (albeit an elementary class, but it is still there), a 1 A charger, a 2000 mAh battery, an interface cord, a protective film for the front panel and a silicone bumper (case) . The only thing missing from this list is the memory card. An external drive will have to be bought separately and, of course, for an additional fee.





Computational Capabilities

Today, the Lenovo A536 phone is equipped with a fairly efficient processor. Reviews on this subject indicate the same. We are talking about MT6582T, which was developed by MediaTEC, the second most popular manufacturer of mobile chips. In peak computing mode, its clock frequency is 1300 MHz. When there is no need to use CPU resources at maximum, it is automatically reduced to 300 MHz. If this is not enough, then the computing modules are disabled, and only one of them can remain in operation. The semiconductor crystal of this CPU itself is manufactured according to the standards of 28 nm process technology. Certainly, at the moment, the computational capabilities of this processor are enough to run all applications, including gaming. The memory subsystem is well organized on this device. The amount of installed RAM is 1 GB, and the capacity of the built-in storage is 8 GB (of which about 2.5 GB is occupied by system software). There is also the possibility of installing an external drive in 32 GB. In general, the CPU and the memory subsystem are not satisfactory.

The graphics subsystem of this device is based on the Mali-400MP2. Of course, it cannot boast of outstanding characteristics, but its capabilities are quite enough for comfortable work in most applications today. Her quality is far from the best, but for communication on Skype this is quite enough.

Autonomy

The strong point is the complete battery of Lenovo A536. Customer reviews indicate this nuance without fail. Its capacity is 2000 mAh. For a 5-inch display, this seems a bit small. But if you take into account its small resolution, then that's okay. Also, the energy efficiency of the processor is not satisfactory.

As a result, the specified battery capacity is enough for 2-3 days of battery life. This in itself is an excellent indicator. If you set the maximum battery saving mode, then one battery charge is enough for 4 days of operation.
