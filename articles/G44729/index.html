Hotel "Vysotsky", Yekaterinburg: description, reviews, rating

Hotel "Vysotsky" (Yekaterinburg) provides its guests the opportunity to stay in luxurious apartments. Guests will have direct access to all objects of business and tourist infrastructure, because the hotel is located directly in the city center.

Short hotel description

Hotel "Vysotsky" (Yekaterinburg) offers its guests comfortable accommodation in luxurious rooms. It is worth noting that they are equipped with the most modern amenities: air conditioning, TV, minibar, free Wi-Fi, as well as high-quality plumbing and comfortable furniture.

Special attention deserves the restaurant, which the hotel can rightfully be proud of. Here you can taste not only Russian and European cuisine, but also popular Japanese dishes. There is also a bar that provides a wide range of drinks and snacks.

The hotel administration pays great attention to providing leisure and entertainment for its guests so that they can properly relax after a busy day of work or sightseeing. You can play billiards or bowling, and relax in the sauna or spa. A luxurious indoor pool is also available.





Hotel "Vysotsky" in Yekaterinburg: contacts and address

This hotel is located on the upper floors of a large business center. This gives guests the opportunity to enjoy the picturesque panoramas of the city, which become especially attractive at night. It is worth noting that this hotel is considered the highest in all of Europe.

Many tourists, as well as business people, prefer the Vysotsky Hotel in Yekaterinburg. The address of this hotel is 51 Malysheva Street (37th floor). It is worth noting that this is the very center of the city, and therefore, regardless of the purpose of your visit, you can quickly get to the desired point.

If you want to book a room or clarify some information about your stay, you just need to call the Vysotsky Hotel in Yekaterinburg. Phone of this hotel: +7 (343) 378-46-46. You can also contact the administration by e-mail: hotel@visotsky-e.ru.





Guest apartments

In total, the Vysotsky Hotel (Yekaterinburg) has 73 apartments. Photo rooms demonstrate incredible comfort and luxury. Also, the highlight of the hotel can be considered that it is located on the 37th floor of a huge business center, and therefore it offers simply stunning views of the big city.

Guests can choose the following accommodation options for themselves:

  • Junior Suite is a large two-room suite. Due to the fact that they are angular, guests have a wide enough viewing angle to fully enjoy the beauties of the city. The bedroom is quite spacious with a large set of furniture. In the living room, in addition to upholstered furniture, there is also a small corner for work. These apartments are designed to accommodate two guests.
  • Deluxe Twin is a chic one-room apartment with a large panoramic window. They are equipped with two single beds. Also, thanks to the decorative partition, next to the window there is a small corner for reading or admiring the scenery, equipped with a soft armchair and a floor lamp.
  • The standard single room is compact in size and very comfortable. There is a bedroom with a small working area, as well as a bathroom with a modern shower. In addition, like in other apartments, there are huge windows that open a beautiful view of the city.
  • "Executive Suite" - huge apartments with an incredibly stylish and sophisticated design. Its windows overlook tourist attractions. Due to the fact that the TV is mounted on a rotating panel, you can watch it from both the bedroom and the living room.
  • Deluxe is a relatively small apartment with a king size bed. In addition, there is the possibility of placing an extra bed. Depending on which of the rooms you are staying in, you will have a beautiful view of one of the busy central streets of the city.
  • Standard Double Room - compact apartment with a large bed, a work area, and a seating area. Several rooms are interconnected, which will provide a comfortable stay for a large family.

Hotel Services Ratings

If we study the reviews of the guests of the complex, we can conclude that the Vysotsky Hotel in Yekaterinburg is quite popular among tourists and businessmen. The rating of its services (on a five-point scale) is as follows:

  • The hotel infrastructure earned a high score of 4.7. It has well-organized leisure guests, as well as a high-level security service.
  • The quality of service is rated at 4.6 points. The work of the staff deserves all praise, but the Internet is intermittent and slow enough.
  • The number of rooms earned a rating of 4.6. The condition of the apartments is generally satisfactory, but some issues are caused by sound insulation.
  • The location of the hotel, of course, deserves the highest rating - 5 points. Nearby there are many entertainment and shopping centers, tourist attractions, as well as business and city infrastructure.

Positive reviews

Many guests visit the Vysotsky Hotel in Yekaterinburg. Reviews about it are accompanied by a lot of positive comments, namely:

  • professionalism of the staff and courteous attitude to hotel guests;
  • the apartments have huge panoramic windows from which an unforgettable view of the city opens (especially a bewitching picture can be seen at night);
  • quick check-in procedure without unnecessary red tape and bureaucratic moments;
  • a huge cosmetic set is provided in the bathroom, which includes shampoos, shower gels, conditioners, lotions, toothpastes and brushes, as well as shaving accessories;
  • perfect cleanliness in the apartment;
  • fast food delivery from the restaurant to the room (literally 10 minutes);
  • a huge assortment of fresh, tasty and healthy dishes is offered to guests as a breakfast (buffet);
  • Room rates include use of the indoor pool, as well as access to the steam sauna;
  • furniture and plumbing are fairly new.




Negative reviews

Despite the high status possessed by the Vysotsky Hotel (Yekaterinburg), reviews about it also contain a number of negative comments:

  • since the rooms are located on the upper floors, there are some problems with the hot water supply - very low pressure;
  • in some rooms designed for two guests, bath amenities and cosmetics are provided for only one;
  • despite the fact that a visit to the spa is included in the price of the room, a preliminary appointment is needed, which the reception staff do not warn guests about;
  • some rooms have insufficiently clean windows, which significantly spoils the impression, because a beautiful panoramic view is the main highlight of the hotel.

Beautiful cityscapes will be opened before you by the Vysotsky Hotel. Yekaterinburg will be at your fingertips. This is a posh hotel that will appeal to both tourists who want to get to know the city and business people who want to relax after work in a luxurious setting.
