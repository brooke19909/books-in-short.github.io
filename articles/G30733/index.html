Hotels in Cheboksary. Hotel "Ibis" Cheboksary (photo)

The city of Cheboksary is an administrative, cultural and industrial center of the Republic of Chuvashia. The list of urban cultural heritage sites includes the Transfiguration Monastery, built in the 17th century, ancient merchant houses, museums. Fans of theatrical art will be interested in visiting the Chuvash and Russian drama theaters, a puppet theater and a young audience. Every year, the city hosts the International Opera Festival named after Mikhailov, the ballet festival, the festival of modern youth culture "Coffee Grinder" and the Cheboksary International Film Festival. Cheboksary hotels are a worthy place to stay for those who decide to visit this city full of cultural events.





A rich cultural life, an abundance of events of the all-Russian and republican level, a large number of industrial enterprises provide a constant influx of tourists and all kinds of business travelers. For them, a developed tourism infrastructure has been created and successfully operates in the city. Arriving in Cheboksary, tourists have the opportunity to stay in modern hotels, which will be discussed in this article. Cheboksary hotels located in the heart of the city deserve special attention.

Hotel Ibis

The hotel is located in the city center, close to the Cheboksary Bay and city attractions. Republican and city authorities are also nearby. In this regard, the location of the hotel is extremely convenient for guests of the city.





Number of rooms

The hotel has one hundred and ten rooms, including for people with disabilities. All are equipped with modern televisions, refrigerators and telephones. There are safes for storing valuables. Separately, mention should be made of orthopedic mattresses on beds in all rooms, which ensures a deep and relaxing sleep for guests. All rooms are decorated in an original style and have excellent soundproofing. For motorists there is parking. Guest services include free internet access and luggage storage. Vacationers also have the opportunity to use dry cleaning and laundry services. According to the reviews of numerous guests of the city, one of the best is the Ibis Hotel (Cheboksary). Food and Services

Dining is organized in the Ibis Kitchen restaurant, located on the ground floor. The menu includes dishes of European and Russian cuisine. There is also a bar for guests around the clock.

The hotel offers great opportunities for organizing corporate events that can be held in three conference rooms (Volga, Vetluga and Sura) located on the second floor. All of them have the necessary sound, audio and video equipment. There is also a business corner for business people.
