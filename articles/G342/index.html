Hotels in Ulan-Ude: overview, addresses, reviews

The city of Ulan-Ude is the capital of Buryatia, as well as a major scientific industrial and cultural center of the country. Here you will meet original culture with interesting monuments of architecture, beautiful nature and lots of business opportunities. Not surprisingly, the city is visited by many tourists. The hospitality and comfortable living conditions will be provided by the hotels of Ulan-Ude.

Hotel "Buryatia"

Looking for accommodation options in the center of Ulan-Ude, pay attention to the Buryatia Hotel. It is located on Kommunisticheskaya street, 47A, which is only a 10-minute walk from the train station. Here are the options provided to guests for a comfortable stay:

  • Comfortable accommodation from 2100 rubles per day;
  • Modern conference venues with a capacity of 10 to 600 people. Rental price - from 500 rubles per hour.
  • Catering, as well as the organization of banquets in the restaurant "Buryatia".
  • Twin capacity - up to 4 people. Rental price - 400 rubles per hour.
  • Gym with a treadmill, exercise bike, elliptical trainer, dumbbells.
  • Free wireless internet.
  • Laundry and ironing services.
  • Massage chair. For hotel guests the service is free.
  • Organization of transfer. To the airport - 700 rubles, to the railway station - 250 rubles.

Reviews

You can hear such positive reviews about this hotel in Ulan-Ude:

  • convenient location in the city center near the main attractions;
  • Responsive and friendly reception staff;
  • affordable rates for accommodation and related services;
  • modern renovation and a pleasant atmosphere in the rooms.

And such negative ones:

  • wireless internet is slow;

  • a shampoo bag cannot be opened without scissors;

Hotel "Baikal Plaza"

Within walking distance from the Philharmonic is the Baikal Plaza Hotel in Ulan-Ude. The four-star establishment is located on Yerbanova street, 12. At your service are such opportunities for living and recreation:

  • Accommodation in comfortable rooms of various categories from standard to presidential. The cost of accommodation is from 3,700 rubles per day.
  • Authentic Tengis restaurant, which specializes in modern Siberian cuisine.
  • Shish kebab bar "Ali Baba", which specializes in dishes of Uzbek cuisine.
  • Cozy lobby bar with a rich selection of drinks and snacks.
  • Three fully equipped conference rooms that can accommodate from 10 to 200 people.
  • Organization of transfer. For stays of more than 7 nights, this option is provided free of charge.

Hotel "Ulan-Ude"

The address Ulan-Ude Hotel is located at 78A Revolution Street of 1905. This is the central part of the city 10 km from the airport. Here are the benefits that this institution will delight its guests:

  • Accommodation in modern comfortable rooms with stylish renovation. The cost of accommodation is from 2400 rubles per day.
  • Photoshoot in a luxurious room with panoramic windows. Cost - 1500 rubles for 2 hours.
  • Restaurant "Caravan Bashi", which specializes in oriental dishes. There is a large dining room for 120 people, as well as secluded cabins for up to 20 people.
  • Free wireless internet access.
  • Private car parking equipped with video surveillance system.
  • Laundry and ironing.
