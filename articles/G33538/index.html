Quiet bay, Crimea: description, rest, how to get

Quiet Bay is one of the unique natural monuments created in Crimea. It got its name due to the fact that the sea here is always calm and calm. The bay itself is surrounded by hills, which ensures not only the tranquility of the sea, but also the amazing beauty of the landscapes. From year to year, this place attracts thousands of tourists.

How to get to the quiet bay?

Those who decide and want to spend their holidays in such a wonderful place as Quiet Bay (Koktebel) will find it very useful to get there. It is located between two small villages: Koktebel and Ordzhonikidze, so if you go by car, then you need to, before reaching the first, turn left and take another three kilometers to take a dirt road. Do not be afraid, the bay has a good campsite, camping tents and a huge number of cars.

The beach here is very large, even more than in the village itself, there is enough space for everyone. Around is very beautiful, picturesque nature, which can not but rejoice your eyes.

The origin of the name of Cape Chameleon

The cape got its name due to the ability to change its color throughout the day. If in the morning it is gray-blue, then in the evening the color already becomes golden ocher, and before sunset it acquires a blue and slightly lilac color. This effect is observed due to the fact that the cape consists of shale, which tend to reflect the sun's rays in different ways. Cape Chameleon is very eroded by storm waves, on old maps it looked almost a peninsula, and now only a narrow strip remains of it. It is possible that soon only memories will remain of him.





Despite the fact that Cape Chameleon is very attractive for tourists, there is absolutely nowhere to stay. Only the most desperate dared to make trails here and admire the beauties of the sea.

Quiet Bay: reviews

If you read the reviews about Quiet Bay, the impression can be very mixed. The fact is that lovers of noisy parties and large-scale parties here do not really like it. However, lovers of a relaxing holiday or those who have come to rest with their family are satisfied and always find a hobby for themselves.





The name speaks for itself: Quiet bay is devoid of any oversized events, but tourists say that there is always where to go to have fun. That is why you can hear conflicting reviews.

How to get from Sudak to Silent Bay

Quiet bay (Crimea) is located not only in close proximity to the village of Koktebel. Route transport is not difficult to get here from Sudak, however you will have to make a transfer in Koktebel. Interestingly, the road from this village to the destination will cost much more than from Sudak to Koktebel. Drivers attribute this to the fact that the road there is very bad. How to get there on foot

In Quiet Bay you can be in just an hour on foot, however, for this you will need to get to Koktebel. After this, you should go along Desantnikov Street to the sea, and then turn left and move along the promenade to the end of the village. Then you should go along the path, which can either dive into a ravine or climb up.

Flora and fauna

Due to the lack of wind, rare species of animals live in the Quiet Bay. Examples of such are the marsh turtle, leopard snake, which are listed in the Red Book and protected at the legislative level. There are rare plants here.





In general, about 27 species of rare animals are recorded here, and about 15 species of plants.
