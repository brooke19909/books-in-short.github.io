Hotel "Astral" (Tikhvin, Leningrad Region): description, service, reviews

Now very often mini-hotels are organized at large shopping complexes. The city of Tikhvin was no exception. Guests of the shopping center can shop and relax in the same building. Hotel "Astral" in Tikhvin belongs to such institutions. It has a variety of rooms that are homely warm and comfortable.

Hotel "Astral" in Tikhvin: address

The hotel is located in a large shopping center. It has a similar name to a hotel. The institution itself is located on the street. Sovetskaya, 18. On the second floor of the water-entertainment complex and equipped with modern rooms.





The hotel has been operating since 2011. Settlement occurs around the clock. At the reception there is always a manager who will help determine the number and conduct a quick registration.

Features

The hotel has 14 rooms for 2 people with the possibility of extra charge for a separate bed. All rooms are individually decorated in accordance with the traditions of certain countries.

There is a spacious parking lot near the shopping center, which is under round-the-clock surveillance. Also, in the lobby of the Astral Hotel in Tikhvin, a security guard is constantly on duty, who monitors video surveillance on and inside the hotel. Thus, guests feel completely safe.

The hotel has free internet, which residents can use at any time. Upon check-in, visitors are informed of a code from the network system.

Hotel service

The price includes an English classic breakfast. Guests of the Astral Hotel (Tikhvin) can visit the aquacenter at any time without a time limit. The price also includes the adoption of procedures in the bath complex.

Housekeeping is daily. Bed linen is changed once every three days. At the reception around the clock there is a manager who will answer all questions and solve problems on household issues.

The water park has staff that monitors the order and safety of visitors.

Entertainment

The building of the shopping center has a modern sports complex. Guests at the Astral Hotel in the Leningrad Region (Tikhvin) can visit it at a 20% discount. It is equipped with a hall with cardio and weight training equipment.

Also, for fans of active games, a tennis court with the necessary equipment is provided. If you wish, you can use the services of a coach. There are also two modern table tennis tables.

After this activity, guests can relax in a horizontal or vertical tanning bed, or in a massage chair. Beauty treatments are also available here.

There is a water park in the shopping center of Tikhvin. There is a large pool with several areas in which different depths are - the smallest for children - 0.8 m and the largest - 1.8 m - for adults. The water park is equipped with hydro and air massage devices. Counterflows and waterfalls work.





The pool has two water slides. Also has two freestanding Jacuzzi. Visitors can use the services of the bath complex:

  • Finnish sauna
  • Russian steam room;
  • Turkish sauna.

The aquacenter has a cafeteria with a varied menu.

Nutrition

The restaurant "Astral" is located near the hotel. It serves breakfast for guests. In addition, you can have a delicious lunch and dinner here. The restaurant offers a varied menu with traditional Russian and European cuisines.





Here you can order a banquet for any number of invited guests. The room is decorated in a classic style, with a predominance of light colors. The institution presents a good wine list with a sufficient assortment of spirits.

The menu includes diet meals. Delicious desserts are also prepared here. When staying in a hotel, you can order dinner with an average cost of 400-500 rubles per person.
