We make an original home decoration - a tree of coffee beans

A handmade coffee tree made of coffee beans will be a wonderful gift for loved ones and, in addition, it will certainly bring special comfort to the interior of any living room.





You can make this original and stylish element of decor from improvised means without spending too much time and effort. Such a tree will surely bring your family happiness and joy. In this article we will share with you the technology of making crafts "tree of happiness" from coffee beans. So, we proceed to a pleasant creative work.

Coffee Bean Tree: Making Crown

To complete this wonderful craft you will need the following materials:

  • coffee beans (packaging - 100 g);
  • newsprint or a plastic children's ball (ball);
  • fine twine;
  • a twig from a tree or a piece of plastic pipe for the trunk;
  • superglue and PVA glue ;
  • a beautiful pot or glass;
  • knife, scissors, threads, brush;
  • various decorative elements (butterflies, bows, braid, etc.) for decoration.

The first step in making this charming craft will be the formation of the crown. To do this, take the newspaper and crush, giving the workpiece roundness.

Tip: it is not necessary to achieve perfectly correct outlines. The fantasy shape of the crown will give the craft a great naturalness and attractiveness.

Now you need to wrap the newspaper blank with food foil in order to maintain a given shape. Using a plastic ball, you will simplify your work and make the process of making craft "coffee beans tree" faster.



After the preparatory work, you can begin to design the crown of the tree with coffee beans. The brand of coffee does not matter. The main thing is that you like the aroma and appearance of the consumable. For gluing grains, you can use superglue. It is applied in a thin layer to the workpiece and immediately the grains are laid out in a chaotic manner.

Tip: decorating the ball with grains is better, starting from the top of the head and moving in a circle down. Thus, gradually we glue the upper half of the ball.

Coffee Bean Tree: Making a Trunk

The second step in our work will be the manufacture of a tree trunk. To do this, take a beautiful dry branch (a piece of a thin plastic pipe) and pierce it with foil from the bottom. For convenience, the ends can be sharpened with a knife. Try the trunk to the tree. Now pull the branch (tube) out of the crown and pour glue into the holes, and then place it back into the crown blank. The trunk of the tree is almost ready. After making it, you can continue pasting the newspaper ball with coffee beans. If you use a plastic tube, you need to decorate it with twine, ribbons or cloth.
