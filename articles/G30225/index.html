Holidays in Crete: Bali will be remembered for a long time

In the north of Crete in Greece, in the Rethymno region, is the famous village of Bali, which in the recent past was an unknown fishing village. Now it is a favorite resort of many.



It is located between Rethymno and Heraklion. The village received its name during the Turkish rule. From Turkish Bali translates as "honey." In these places they really collect a fragrant product.

On the island of Crete, Bali is a very popular village. Tourist boats come to its small port, where you can take a fascinating tour and enjoy the beauties of the island, the village and get acquainted with the surrounding territories. Water sports enthusiasts are invited to go diving, rent canoes and go fishing.

Tourists from all over the world love to visit the island of Crete, Bali in this sense is no exception. The beaches off the coast of the village are both pebble and sand. There are numerous cafes and taverns on the coast. Like many settlements on the island of Crete, Bali is a village with a well-developed infrastructure. Despite this, families with children relax here calmly and serenely.

On the picturesque island of Crete, Bali is a unique resort. There is a beach covered with black sand of volcanic origin. Dark grains of sand so warm in the sun that it is impossible to walk barefoot on them.



Tourists have always been attracted by ancient and mysterious Greece. Crete (Bali and other resorts) every year receives numerous travelers from around the world. Here, not only those who wish to soak up the gentle sun, but also those who want to plunge into the atmosphere of national Greek flavor, can relax.

Numerous small and large bays are characteristic of the island of Crete. Bali, reviews of which are only the most admired, has four of them, in which sandy beaches are located. The largest and most open bay is located near the highway. In windy weather there are strong waves. The second is much smaller and calmer. The third bay is the busiest. It has a small pier, where you can always see the boats of fishermen, and here come the tourist "pirate schooners" - small boats making excursions along the coast.

The calmest and quietest bay is to the right of the main beach. There are never waves here. It is called Karavostasi, or Evita. This is the most secluded place in the village. Here is one hotel and two taverns.

Like the entire island of Crete, Bali has many souvenir shops and two supermarkets. There are a huge number of large and very small taverns, which became famous not only for their magnificent and original cuisine, but also for huge portions.

Lovers of an active lifestyle will be able to visit the Melidoni Cave, the entrance to which is two hundred and thirty meters above sea level. The main hall of its very impressive size is 44x55 meters. The ceiling height ranges from 10 to 25 meters. The stalactites hanging from it are illuminated by multi-colored lights.



The magnificent island of Crete and the village of Bali receive vacationers from all over the world throughout the year. Locals cordially and kindly welcome guests. Tourists note the excellent service and good attitude of the population of the island.
