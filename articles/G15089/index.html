Zlata Prague. Neighborhoods and Attractions

Zlata Prague, whose areas are picturesquely stretched on the banks of the Vltava, falls in love immediately and forever. Its bridges, landscapes and long tangled streets will attract tourists and force them to return to this eternal city again and again.

The city is popular at any time of the year. In winter, you can admire the magnificent Gothic for an infinitely long time, admiring the beauty of ancient cathedrals covered with snow. In addition, you can decently save on a hotel - at this time, housing prices fall almost 3 times. History Center

To enjoy the spirit of this amazing city and appreciate at least a small fraction of its beauties, it is worth spending at least a week in Prague. Convenient housing can be rented in any of the 10 districts of the capital, and transport that works with amazing punctuality will deliver you to the right place quickly and safely.

Especially appreciated by tourists is the old Prague. The 1st and 2nd districts, located in the historical center, are built up with the best and most comfortable hotels. This is the center of the most famous Prague attractions, so the prices for all are very high.



Hotels in Malaya Strana, a beautiful area with ancient pavements and magnificent palaces, can be an excellent choice. This bank of the Vltava River is far from the hustle and bustle, and to get to the Old Town, you just need to cross the bridge.

The new city is suitable for lovers of fun and nightly entertainment. There is a lot of noise and music, and cabarets and discos are open until the morning.

Comfort and tranquility

The Zizkov district in Prague is considered one of the most comfortable for living. Quiet and calm streets do not differ in a large number of attractions, although it is here that the television tower rises - the tallest building in the city. There are many good and affordable hotels. The path to the center takes only a few minutes, and many tourists prefer to stay here.



Hradcany - an area with luxurious residences, gardens and historical monuments, is suitable for lovers of tranquility and expensive comfort.

Native places

The most beloved and popular among Russian tourists is the 6th district. There is everything for a comfortable stay - a lot of greenery, proximity to the center, excellent schools, the best hospital in the city, a prestigious university and many embassy buildings.



This part of the city is Russian Prague. The areas of Nabuszyce and Dejvice are populated by immigrants from Russia in the first wave of emigration, there is an Orthodox church, a private Russian school, it is as prestigious to have housing here as in the 1st and 2nd districts of the city.
