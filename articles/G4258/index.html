Eco friendly place

Ten years ago, the influential Forbes magazine recognized the most beautiful among all European coasts, the popular pebble beach Punta Rata, located in the city of Brela (Croatia). Ecologists note here the most transparent sea water.

The ancient town is surrounded by mountains that separate it from the noisy freeway and serve as natural protection from the wind. To get to Brela from the airport, you will have to go down the winding serpentine, in some places observation platforms are specially designed for tourists, from the height of which a stunning view of the rippling sea opens. The mountain range is unusually loved by climbers who come here to test their strength.

Clean clear air and pebble beaches

Beautiful pebble beaches are not the only feature of the quiet resort, vacationers celebrate the healing air, filled with the scents of centuries-old pine trees, almost approaching the edge of the surf.





The famous Punta Rata beach, stretching for several kilometers, is the real pride of the village of Brela. Croatia has always been famous for the high purity of its coastal territories, and the blue flag of UNESCO, which is displayed in the resorts of the Makarska Riviera, only confirms the guarantee of an appropriate level of security and excellent quality of service.

The large beaches of Berulia and Stomarica are no less well-groomed than the main attraction of the resort, and their excellent ecological condition is also confirmed. Equipped with sunbeds, showers, umbrellas that protect from sunlight, they are the best suited for family vacations. Thanks to the close proximity to the pine forest, even on the hottest day, you can find delicious coolness.

Hidden from sight rest place

An unusual holiday in Brela (Croatia) would be incomplete without visiting the Vruja beach hidden from the eyes. Located away from the main resort, it hides under high cliffs. This secluded beach was chosen by couples in love and all lovers to be alone with nature. Quiet and calm place, located far from civilization, gives peace and special emotions.

Brela (Croatia): reviews

Tourists, tired of passive relaxation on the beach, go to explore the picturesque bays located on the coast, preserving in the photos their amazing beauty at dawn or sunset.

In summer, the village hosts numerous holiday shows that attract a huge number of tourists. Vacationers celebrate a special atmosphere of fun at the festival "Fishing Nights", which offers various entertainment events especially for guests of the resort. Every evening, various evening programs are held for tourists, which can be observed sitting on the verandas of cozy restaurants, and then take an active part in them. Invited and local animators, according to vacationers, organize catchy shows.
