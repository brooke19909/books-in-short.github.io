Where is the hotel located and how to get there

From Havana airport, where most foreign tourists land, you need to travel more than two hours to Varadero. But this city has its own air harbor, which, incidentally, has international status. Varadero Airport is only twenty-five kilometers from the Gran Caribe Club Kawama 4 * hotel. The former Hacienda is now within walking distance of the village where the local people live. Typically, tourists (independent, which does not include a transfer package) get from Havana by bus to Varadero. There, they transfer to two-story cars that run through the resort area. But guests of the Grand Carib Club Kawama do not need to spend cookies on this last mode of transport. In five minutes you will reach the hotel. Since this is the very beginning of the cape (or, if you want, a sand spit), there are a lot of entertainment venues, shops and night clubs around.

Gran Caribe Club Kawama has fully implemented the all-inclusive concept. Beach and Pools

All reviews agree that the coast at the hotel is the best in all of Varadero. Gran Caribe Club Kawama has an elongated territory along the ocean. Therefore, almost all bungalows go to the beach for a minute or two. The sand on the beach is snow-white. He is combed every morning with a tractor with a furrow. The number of sunbeds abound. Umbrellas are covered with palm leaves, which emphasizes the atmosphere of Caribbean relaxation. The only negative is the bar. He is the only one on the whole beach, and if you want to sunbathe away from most people, you will have to walk along the hot sand for drinks. But the hotel has four swimming pools. For children there are dedicated areas. Fresh water in the pools is cleaned in a modern way, and does not carry bleach from it. The terraces around these tanks are also filled with sun loungers, by the way, of better quality, with a net rather than solid transverse structures. This property provides beach towels.



Services

Gran Caribe Club Kawama 4 * is taken for the organization of business meetings and meetings. For this, the hotel has two conference rooms. What are the conditions for children at the hotel

A child between the ages of four and twelve can be enrolled in a mini club. This is a separate wooden structure where there are playrooms and where qualified teachers are constantly engaged with children. High chairs for feeding babies - this is the norm for hotels with four stars. The Gran Caribe Hotel Club Kawama also has a soft-covered outdoor playground. There is no special animation for little guests. But tourists say that the program of evening shows for adults often includes performances of magicians, so that children like it. The contingent of vacationers at the hotel is mainly Canadians and tourists from Western Europe. Animation, respectively, is conducted in Spanish and English. But children easily overcome the language barrier, and parents say that their children enjoyed visiting the mini-club, where they played with peers from other countries.

The hotel Gran Caribe Club Kawama 4 * (Varadero) has a tour desk. But in order to travel to Havana, it is not necessary to turn to his services. Tourists recommend visiting the Roma House and the cigar factory in Varadero. This can also be done without overpaying the guide.
