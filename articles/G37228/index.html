South and North bus stations Ufa

Currently, Ufa bus stations - South and North, serve the passenger flow of the city, sending buses to nearby towns and distant cities of our country. Where do the buses of each go? How to get tickets? How to get to the bus stations in Ufa?

Brief historical background

The first passenger traffic from Ufa began to be carried out in 1924. In the role of buses, then trucks were used, in the bodies of which benches were installed. The first intercity route was the bus Ufa-Sterlitomak. It was carried out in 1927.

In 1937, the first real bus station appeared in the city, which carried out communication with eight cities. He acted before the start of World War II.

Modern bus stations in Ufa send and receive about 300 intercity and approximately 500 suburban bus services.

South Bus Station Ufa

In 1968, the largest bus station in the region, the South, was built. Currently, buses from this station leave in the western, southern, eastern directions. 60 suburban buses and 40 intercity buses arrive and depart from the Southern bus station of Ufa. 5,000 people go here every day. South Bus Station (Ufa) sells tickets on line , and also serves customers through traditional ticket offices. The train station is located in the city center on 13 Richard Sorge Street.

You can get here by any public transport that goes along this street. Trams No. 5, 10, 18, trolleybuses No. 12, 3, 11, buses No. 11 and 101, 107 and 112, 115 and 142.





North Bus Station

In 1992, another modern Ufa bus station, Severny, was commissioned. It serves passengers of the north and north-east direction. The station building has everything you need for the convenience of passengers. The station is located at Kemerovskaya street, 82. Trolleybuses going here: No. 1, 4, 5, 11, buses No. 15, 216, 239, 244 and 293.



Ufa bus stations carry out transportation by transport cards, which can be replenished both via the Internet and at the box office. Transport cards can be of several categories, for example, school, student, perpetual.

Bus stations in Ufa have many different services for passengers. Waiting rooms, mother and child rooms, cash desks, left-luggage offices, cafes, pharmacies, shopping pavilions, help desk.

It should be remembered that the ticket office of the North Station is open from 07:30 to 19:40 with a lunch break of 13: 00-14: 45. Ticket offices of the Southern Bus Station operate around the clock.

Ufa bus stations connects the route of the 11th trolley bus and a fixed-route taxi with the name of the stations on the plate.
