Hotel Throne Seagate Belek Hotel 5 * (Belek, Turkey): description and reviews

Having decided to make a romantic trip or to realize a long-awaited family vacation, many choose Turkey. A country with interesting customs, a colorful population, a lot of resort settlements, a large number of beaches with velvet sands and the azure sea is interesting for travelers from all over the world to visit.

The 5-star Throne Seagate Belek Hotel is located in the small resort of Belek. The once wild territory today has turned into a series of four- and five-star hotels offering first-class service, comfortable accommodation and a variety of entertainment for all generations.

General characteristics of the hotel

The hotel complex was commissioned in 2003 and from the first years of its existence has gained incredible popularity among tourists. Its total area is 7000 square meters. It housed the only main building, equipped park area and pools. The infrastructure is to the liking of every guest. It is placed compactly. There are a lot of greenery, shrubs and trees. Last renovation made in the current year.

The main building of the Throne Seagate Belek Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey, Belek) is represented by a five-story building, most of which have amazing views of the endless distances of the Mediterranean Sea.

The nearest airport is Antalya International Airport, 35 km away, resulting in an influx of travelers with children. The absence of heavy crossings, exhausting queues on the bus makes it easy to get to your destination with a taxi.





Check in hotel

The standard check-in time is at 14:00, you must leave no later than 12:00. Pets are strictly prohibited, as is smoking in all apartments. There are three equipped rooms for people with disabilities. Payment is accepted by payment cards of world banking systems.

The staff of the Throne Seagate Belek Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey, Belek) speaks English, Turkish and Russian, is distinguished by unprecedented friendliness and cordiality.
