Water parks in Tomsk: addresses and description

A trip to the water park with family or friends is a great way to spend a day off, celebrate a birthday or other holiday, especially in the warm season. First of all, water slides, attractions and entire playgrounds will appeal to children, but for adults there are also various additional services, for example, spa treatments. In the city of Tomsk, 3 water parks are currently open for visitors.

What to bring

The first thing you need to pack in a bag before visiting the water park in Tomsk is a swimsuit or swimming trunks. It is recommended to choose models without decorative elements made of metal, beads and rhinestones.

Do not forget about special shoes: slippers or slates, in which you can move around the territory. In some water parks, towels are issued (this can be clarified by phone or on the official website), but sometimes you have to take them with you from home. The minimum number of towels is 2 pcs.

Before you plunge into the pool, and also after that, you need to take a shower, so you should take hygiene products with you: shampoo, shower gel, washcloth and soothing body lotion - chlorinated water can cause irritation, especially for owners of sensitive skin.

"Jasmine"

The recreation center "Jasmine" is located at ul. Ferghanskaya, 65. The nearest public transport stop is Pervomaiskaya Street, which can be reached by buses under numbers 13 and 14.

This Tomsk water park cannot be called the largest in Russia - its area is about 1 thousand square meters. m, and the maximum number of visitors is 60. However, despite its small size, this recreation center is popular among city residents.

Water park of Tomsk "Jasmine" is equipped with four water slides - two for adults and children. The height and length of the largest of them are 13 and 60 m, respectively. Slides end in a 16-meter pool. The adult and children's areas are separated for reasons of convenience and safety of visitors.

In addition, the Tomsk water park "Jasmine" also has a jacuzzi, baths and saunas, working around the clock. After swimming in the pool and warming up in the sauna, visitors can relax and dine in the bar located in the recreation center.

An hour of visit for adults costs 300 rubles, for children from 4 to 7 years - 200 rubles. No prior appointment required.

The family recreation center "Victory" was created in the city with the aim of popularizing sports and a healthy lifestyle. In addition to the water park, it includes many more sections. Depending on the time of year, you can go skating, skiing, rollerblading or cycling; play hockey, soccer, volleyball or basketball; pass the baton in the obstacle course; cook kebabs on fire in a specially designated area.

Open water park of Tomsk "Victory" is available for visiting in the summer. The 5 by 10 meter pool is equipped with an oxygen treatment system. Nearby are 2 inflatable water slides and two terraces with sun loungers, where you can relax after swimming.

The Kingdom of Neptune

The Kingdom of Neptune is a family bath complex in Tomsk with 2 branches in the city: one of them is located at ul. Frunze, 240a (buses 16, 53, 23, 13, stop "Autocentre"), and the other on the street. 79 of the Guards Division, 4/5 (buses 11, 401, 13, 14, Vokzalnaya Street stop).

Both health centers include a 23 m long swimming pool divided into 3 paths, a Finnish and Russian sauna, a Turkish bath (hammam), and an infrared cabin. In addition, visitors can enjoy the services of spa, massage, solarium and gym.

To visit the pool, you must submit a medical certificate confirming the absence of skin diseases. It can be obtained both in the clinic and in the center of the "Kingdom of Neptune", having passed the examination.
