"Turquoise Katun" - recreation center, interesting places

Every year, thousands of tourists from Russia and abroad, when choosing a place to stay, give preference to Altai. A variety of travelers find entertainment there to their liking. This can be fishing, hiking, hiking, sightseeing and much more.

Specialized Zone

A great place for a family holiday is the Turquoise Katun complex. The recreation center in this place is not alone. They are presented in large numbers and have different price categories. A whole range of services is offered for living - from the camping area for camping and comfortable cottages of European level.





For many reasons, the tourist zone "Turquoise Katun" is attractive. The recreation center, where you can sit, as a rule, is within easy reach of all interesting objects. Of course, the most attractive moment in a holiday in Altai is nature itself. Mountain landscapes, a river winding among the hills, forests, clean air - all this leaves a lot of impressions, restores physical health and nervous system.

This system is located in the Altai Territory on the right bank of the Katun, it borders on the Altai Republic. Thanks to the developed infrastructure, it is easy to get there and organize leisure activities.

Ponds

On this territory there is an artificial lake. The water in it is warm, and the beaches are sandy beaches. This is of key importance in the Turquoise Katun complex. The recreation center, inviting vacationers, as a rule, focuses on the opportunity to enjoy a full bath and sunbathing in the usual conditions.



Kamyshlinsky waterfall is another attraction that can be reached on foot from local locations. In the same area is the freshwater lake Aya. There is an interesting, beautiful legend about this hydronym. It is said that a long time ago the moon descended into the valley and grabbed the cannibal Delbegen there to save the human race. Then a dent formed in this place and a lake arose. Today Aya is very popular among both locals and guests of the region.

The Katun River is the largest in the Altai region. The water in it is always cold, because it is formed from the melting of glaciers. From the deep blue color, the name of the local recreation area - "Turquoise Katun" - went. Photos of these places show the steep banks of river valleys, waterfalls, lakes, mountain views. In the upper and middle sections, the river has a fast flow; rapids are often found. In the lower reaches, Katun is poured by a calm ribbon.





sights

Tavdinsky caves are also attractive for travelers . They have been formed for many millennia in rock masses under the influence of running water. There ancient people arranged dwellings and shrines. The karst arch is the most famous link in the whole system. Currently, this object is assigned the status of a natural monument.

So, without spending a lot of time and effort, you can visit this unique place, being within the boundaries of the Turquoise Katun complex. The recreation center where you decide to stay can be located within a few kilometers of the attraction.

Here, for fans of history and simply enlightened people, students and teachers of Altai State University organized the “Crossroads of Worlds” park. There you can see the reconstruction of costumes and life of the local population, the parking lot of ancient people.





Sensible choice

“Bay”, “Helion”, “Manzherok”, “Talda” and many other camp sites offer their guests rooms of various levels of comfort in the recreation zone “Turquoise Katun”. Altai is famous for its beautiful places, but not everyone is ready to spend time in "Spartan" conditions. So, one is enough to put up a tent, others would like to live in summer cottages, and someone does not imagine a good rest without air conditioning, soft feather-beds and a bathroom.
