Hotel "Gostiny Dvor" (Novosibirsk): photo with description, service, reviews

Gostiny Dvor in Novosibirsk is a classic business hotel that allows a business person to combine work and leisure profitably. Thanks to the convenient transport interchange, you will not spend precious time on the road, and the rich infrastructure of the establishment will provide you with opportunities for entertainment and relaxation.

Location

10 minutes from the city center is the hotel "Gostiny Dvor" in Novosibirsk. Address of institution: Red Avenue, 234/1. It is about 18 km from Tolmachevo Airport. The road to the train station will take you about half an hour.

When staying at this hotel, be sure to visit the nearby tourist sites:

  • Zoo - 2 km;
  • Ascension Cathedral - 3 km;
  • State Circus - 4 km;
  • Spartak Stadium - 4 km;
  • central park - 5 km;
  • Opera and Ballet Theater - 5 km;
  • State Philharmonic Society - 5 km;
  • Transfiguration Cathedral - 5 km;
  • Monument of Glory - 10 km.

They can be reached on foot or by public transport.

Sauna

After a busy sightseeing or working day, you can relax in the Gostiny Dvor sauna in Novosibirsk. Guests are provided with such opportunities for recreation:

  • Finnish dry steam sauna
  • Turkish hammam
  • pool;
  • massage chair;

The cost of visiting the sauna from 06:00 to 16:00 is 900 rubles per hour. From 16:00 to 06:00 the cost for 4 people is 1300 rubles per hour.

Conference hall

Since the main audience of the Gostiny Dvor Hotel in Novosibirsk is business people, it is worth noting the presence of a modern comfortable conference hall for 40 guests. Visitors can rent such equipment:

  • flipchart;
  • projector;
  • screen;
  • microphone;
  • acoustic system.

The cost of renting a conference room without equipment is 700 rubles per hour. Provided the group accommodates from 5 rooms, the use of equipment is provided free of charge. Provided the group accommodates from 10 rooms, a conference room rental is not charged (1 day no longer than 7 hours).

A restaurant

Favorite vacation spot for guests of the Gostiny Dvor in Novosibirsk is a restaurant and a karaoke bar. So, the White Hall restaurant is open from 07:00 to midnight daily, and the Malina karaoke bar is open from 18:00 to 06:00 on Fridays and Saturdays.

The banquet halls of Gostiny Dvor in Novosibirsk are ideal for corporate events and celebrations. There is a banquet hall for 100 people, as well as a hall for 25 people. For small parties, a VIP room with a pool table is suitable.
