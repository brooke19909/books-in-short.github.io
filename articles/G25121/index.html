Waterpark "Gorki" (Ryazan): prices, photos, reviews, how to get there

Waterpark "Gorki" (Ryazan) is a family recreation center where everyone can find entertainment to their taste. It was built in the style of a "lost island", and indeed, getting there, for the whole day you forget that there is another world, besides pools and magic slides. Infrastructure

Waterpark "Gorki" (Ryazan) is made on two levels. The first is slides and rides for adults. In addition, there is a wave pool and a solarium so that even in winter you feel like you are on a real beach. There is also a lazy river attraction, 4 types of saunas, a calm pool and a jacuzzi. The second floor is a territory for children, there are many small slides, a shallow pool with rides and a children's cafe with favorite treats.

There are many advantages that daily attract tourists here. This is the most modern indoor water park in Russia, residents of Ryazan can rightfully be proud of it. Here is a whole complex of pools and jacuzzis, baths and saunas, bars and restaurants, as well as a variety of relaxation areas. Children here are not at all bored, because there is a separate floor with fun rides. An absolute plus is a perfect water purification system and a high level of security.

Waterpark of a hill (Ryazan). How to get there?

The builder chose a place for the future entertainment center is not accidental. This is done with a long view, as there is enough space around to expand the business. Waterpark "Gorki" (Ryazan) is located outside the city. On the one hand, this is a plus, more fresh air, less noise. But on the other, it creates additional difficulties. Without a car, you have to get a long time, the bus here, of course, runs, but rarely enough. If you are traveling with children, be sure to arrange in advance with the driver who will pick you up, children play on water slides while they can stand on their feet. After that, the option to go to a stop and wait for the bus is not the best end to such a day.



Adventure World

It all starts at the checkout. Here you can pay for your stay and get an electronic bracelet. They are of different colors and sizes, blue for adults, and red for children. According to the reviews of vacationers, this thing is quite inconvenient, but necessary, without it, a cabin with things will not open. And she constantly strives to slip out of her hand, and the penalty for loss is quite substantial. Here you will also need to change shoes in replaceable shoes. If you do not take this into account, you will be offered to buy cheap shale.

The campaign into the unknown begins. Go through the turnstile to the locker room, change clothes and exit through the second door. Here, with a bracelet, you can open the cabinet and put things together. There is everything you need to then put yourself in order: mirrors, hair dryers and chairs. Then you go to the toilets and showers, where you must wash yourself with soap, and only then exit to the park itself.



Fun rides

The first thing you see is the "lazy river." If this is your first visit to the Gorki water park (Ryazan), this attraction will be a great opportunity to see everything around and swim in parallel. It is the largest here and runs throughout the territory. You can go with the flow, against the current or use a large inflatable raft and calmly explore everything around. Your path will run through fabulous grottoes and mysterious caves, which adds excitement to the journey. After swimming and looking around, you can get out of the river and go to conquer the slides. By the way, there is a bridge over it, so if you do not want to get into the water, you can use it.

Extreme entertainment

This is one of the big slides for adults. Its feature is that you have to roll in complete darkness, and the descent itself takes place on a raft. Of course, this adds a pungent sensation. In addition, it is a very long slide, and during the journey you will constantly fly up on steep turns. It is in great demand among those who come to the water park "Gorki" (Ryazan). Reviews say that it gives the most powerful experience and will forever remain your favorite slide. Additionally, in certain areas of the slide there is a backlight. You fly in complete darkness and suddenly some figures appear and disappear around.

Tranquil entertainment

On the ground floor, besides the slides, there is also a jacuzzi where you can relax in the bubbles. Next is the wave pool, with a gradual increase in depth. Children's area

Very lucky kids of the city of Ryazan. Waterpark "Gorki" ("Okskaya Pearl" - this is the name of the whole complex) has provided everything so that the relaxation of the smallest guests is unforgettable. Of course, they are not allowed on adult slides, the minimum height should be 150 cm, but in their area they can have fun without restrictions. Here all the rides are reduced copies of those that we have already described. In addition, many bright slides for the smallest, they are made in the form of a variety of animals.

On the second floor there is a children's cafe where you can have a tasty meal or arrange a magical celebration, such as a birthday. Funny animators, clowns and mischievous presenters will make sure that the holiday of the day goes well and is remembered for a long time.



Cost of rest

Before planning a trip, be sure to check the timetable. The center operates from 11:00 to 22:00. But keep in mind that there are also sanitary days. The need for this measure is difficult to underestimate, especially in such a public place as a water park in Ryazan (Gorki). The prices here are quite affordable. For an entire day on weekdays, an adult pays 750 rubles, and a child - 450. Weekends, as usual, are more expensive - 1150 for an adult and 850 for a child. But it is not necessary to spend all day here, in addition, there are a number of discounts for group and family visits, for large families.
