Pattern skirts with a smell. Style Features

One of the most comfortable is a wraparound skirt, especially suitable for those who tend to gain weight quickly or lose weight. Sewing such a wardrobe item is easy, just follow the technology and be extremely careful. The pattern of a skirt with a smell, or rather its design, will also not be difficult even for a beginning seamstress.



Style Selection

Since such a simple skirt looks extremely simple, it is important to choose a style depending on where you are going to wear it - for work or for a walk. Will it be on buttons or ties, will it have additional decor or accessories in the form of bows, locks, pockets and other things. The standard pattern of a skirt with a smell allows you to give free rein to your imagination and not be limited to the classic version. You can make an asymmetric thing, and embroidery made with your own hands will look especially impressive.

There are many options for flare - from the sun to a quarter of the sun. In addition, the skirts can also be different in length, the skirt to the knee (plus or minus 5 cm) looks best, but keep in mind that there will be a lot of fabric consumption if you like a long skirt with a smell. The pattern is made depending on the style, so sit down and think it over carefully, draw or find a similar photo in the magazine. This is the basis of everything.



Wrap skirt pattern

To build such a pattern is not difficult, you only need to have two measurements - OT and DI. Waist circumference is measured using a centimeter tape at the waist or lower, if you prefer to wear a skirt that way. The length of the product depends on the model you choose, measure the length from the waist with a centimeter to the level where the skirt will end, the measurement must be done on the side. In further constructions and calculations, we will need only half of the OT, that is, the half-circumference (ST) of the waist. The first thing to do is calculate the radius to draw the waist line (R). To do this, multiply CT by 0.9. The last number is a constant coefficient of ticking, use it to build patterns on any figure. The next parameter is the radius of the bottom of the skirt (R1), it is calculated by adding the length of the product to R. As you can see, the pattern of a skirt with a smell is built quickly and without much difficulty.

If your model involves additional construction of various parts, for example, pockets, ribbons and other things, then take this no less seriously, because these are also parts of the product, and they do not tolerate neglect.

If you are a beginner, then do not greatly complicate the style, you may not be able to cope, then the work will go to dust. The pattern of a summer skirt with a smell can differ only in the radius of the waistline, it will be slightly larger, because in the summer you want to wear more loose clothes.

the cloth

Any fabric is suitable for a wrap skirt, it all depends on where you are going to wear it. A denser fabric is suitable for a business classic skirt, but light chiffon is a material exclusively for a summer product. If you are sewing for the first time, then do not choose expensive fabric, it is better to practice cheap ones, and when you get the necessary experience, you can fork out.
