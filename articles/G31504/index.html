How to tie booties - step by step instructions for beginners

Recommendations on how to knit booties for those who prefer knitting needles.

We need: yarn in two colors (take pink and purple), knitting needles.

To knit booties with knitting needles for beginners is not a difficult task, the main thing is to follow the instructions below.

So let's get started:

We knit about 22 loops on the needles (we get a width of about 9 cm) and we knit 62 rows (about 14-15 cm); knitting shawl.

Next, we close 8 stitches, tie the purple thread and knit:

63 row - we knit facial loops

64 row - we knit the wrong loops

65 row - facial loops

66 - purl loops

Then we tie a pink thread and knit like this:

67 row - facial loops

68 row - front loops

69 row - purl loops

70 row - facial loops

So, alternating 4 rows of pink and purple flowers, we should get 8 purple stripes and 7 pink stripes.

Then we sew the last row and the first row.

We twist the booties on the left side and collect the striped part on the thread on both sides from point 2 to point 2. We've got a sock!

Sew in a straight line. From 2 points to 3 points (3-4 cm). And we collect the rest in the same way (from point 3 to point 3) on a thread and tighten! We got a heel.

We knit the second bootie in the same way as the first, and now you can tell all your friends about how to tie booties with your own hands. With the least expenditure of time and effort.

Now consider the option - how to crochet booties

Proceed:

1. From the yarn we make a ring, then 1 loop for lifting and 7 single crochet into the ring.

2. From each single crochet, we knit 2 single crochet stitches.

3. Next in a circle we knit a sock of our future booties. This means that at the end of each row we make a connecting column, and at each beginning of a new row we make one loop for lifting.

4. The length of the toe of the booties is ¬Ω the length of the legs (about 4 - 4.5 cm)

5. When this part of the booties is tied, we knit a pigtail of air loops, the length of which is equal to the width of our sock.

6. Next, we knit the bootleg with an ordinary straight cloth. We will make this part of the booties a little wider, so that it is convenient for the child‚Äôs legs.

7. The little bootie is ready! We only need to sew and decorate it (bows, ribbons, ribbons) according to your desire.

A small working tip: leave the working thread more authentic, as it is more convenient to stitch the product. Booties are ready!

And now the general helpful tips on how to tie booties:

1. If several sizes are indicated in the instructions, highlight those that are right for you.

2. If you knit your future product from threads of different types, it is desirable that the thread length is the same in the skein itself.

3. Read the care instructions! The first wash can have unpleasant surprises.

4. If you knit for the first time, I advise you not to use tight knitting. Choose something simple and easy, because for a fit model, you will need training.

5. If you knit for a child, and know that you will need to extend the sleeve, I advise you to knit from top to bottom. In the future, it will be convenient for you to dissolve the hinges and tie on the sleeve.

6. When knitting large parts quickly get tired hands. I advise you to use circular knitting needles.

7. A practical and very useful thing is colored tips for knitting needles. The hinges will not slip if you suddenly interrupt your work.

8. An equally useful thing is a row counter. He puts on a knitting needle, and the number changes at the end of each row that you have already knitted. This will save you a lot of time! It is especially useful for knitting complex patterns.

9. Even if you are in a hurry very much, try to finish knitting the row you have started, otherwise the loops that go down in the middle of the row can ruin the product.

10. If the product you are knitting from dark or black yarn, lay a white scarf under your knees. This is necessary so that your eyes do not strain.
