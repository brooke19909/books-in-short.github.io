Georgia, Ureki. Sanatoriums of Georgia. Rest in Ureki

Hospitable Georgia is famous for its warm clear sea, beautiful landscapes, mild climate, healing mud and springs. Ureki is one of the most popular seaside resorts in the country. Not so long ago, the village was covered with swamps that dried up. In their place, they discovered a real treasure - magnetic sands that cure any disease, healing, charging with strength and positive energy. Thousands of tourists come here every year to enjoy the clean air, the hospitality of the locals, the beautiful nature, as well as the delicious dishes and impeccable wines that Georgia offers.



Ureki - resort on the Black Sea coast

The town is located right on the Black Sea coast between Poti and Batumi, not far from Kobuleti. Couples with young children come to Ureki to have a rest, because the sea is shallow here, convenient for bathing babies. In recent years, the city has noticeably improved infrastructure. New hotels have appeared that are famous for their high level of service, good food, and an attentive approach to each guest. Beautification of the beaches was carried out, concerts are often held on the seashore in the summer, many musicians come to Ureki. But the most important wealth of the resort is the gentle sun, warm transparent sea, velvety fine sand with unique healing properties.

Unique properties of magnetic sand

Ureki can be ranked among the developing resorts of Georgia, more and more people come here every year. There are no cultural and historical attractions that would be of genuine interest to travelers. The town is famous for another treasure - magnetic sand, which has no analogues anywhere else in the world. Its healing properties were known in ancient times. Indian yogis, Greeks, Egyptians actively used sand for healing. Today he found application in modern medicine, namely in magnetotherapy.



Over the years, people have been cured of various diseases in Ureki. Magnetic sand on the Black Sea coast was found in the 19th century. The inhabitants of the town magically cured their chronic diseases. Over time, physicians, scientists, researchers became interested in the properties of sand. It was proved that a magnetic field is capable of exerting an anti-inflammatory, analgesic effect on the human body, increasing protective functions, and stimulating tissue regeneration. Perhaps the secret of longevity of many Georgians lies precisely in magnetic sand. Magnetotherapy is used today in cosmetology, gynecology, orthopedics, urology, and diseases of the circulatory and cardiovascular systems. Holidays in Ureki serve as prevention and treatment for many diseases, therefore adults and children from nearby countries come here.



Who should visit the resort?

Magnetic sand can be dangerous for people with blood diseases, malignant tumors, severe bronchial asthma, and tuberculosis patients. Everyone else can and should even come to Ureki. Georgia (reviews from grateful travelers will not allow to doubt its hospitality) will help people suffering from diseases of the musculoskeletal system. These include osteoarthritis, chronic polyarthritis of infectious and rheumatic origin, osteochondropathy, osteoporosis. Magnetic sands help recover more quickly from injuries. In Ureki you can cure neurosis, depression, neuralgia and other diseases of the nervous system. Also here come people suffering from infertility and other chronic urogenital diseases. Unfortunately, many children do not shine with health, kids with birth injuries, rickets, spinal paralysis and cerebral palsy come to the resort.

Sights of Kobuleti

Ureki has no special places, so if you want to see something unusual, you need to go to a neighboring city. In Kobuleti there is Mtirala National Park. Georgian resorts often use their water for medicinal purposes. There are a lot of different types of plants in the Mtirala park, and there are those that are found only in this part of the planet. Attractions Poti

In this resort town you can visit Colchis National Park, located near Lake Paliastomi, right on the Black Sea coast. The reserve protects the unique flora and fauna of the Colchian lowland. It is here that you can look at the sections of the rainforest that appeared here 65 million years ago. Today, not many ancient trees have survived, most of them have been cut down, now half of the park is covered with swamps.

Another attraction of Poti is Lake Paliastomi. Its area is a little more than 18 km 2 , today it is more like an estuary. Scientists believe that the ancient Greek city of Fasis rests at its bottom. Traces of a second-century settlement were discovered here in 1961. Now on the lake arrange sports fishing, watching migratory birds.

Georgian cuisine

National dishes always cause genuine delight among travelers. Georgia has been famous for culinary masterpieces for many years, so when you come here to relax, you must definitely try everything. Kharcho soup, barbecue, lobio - these dishes have long become international, although they are rooted in this hospitable country. Georgians are very fond of meat, so this product is added to almost all dishes. The visiting card of national cuisine can be called chakhokhbili, chikhirmu, khachapuri. Spices are the highlight of Georgia, they are added everywhere, give the dishes a spicy taste.
