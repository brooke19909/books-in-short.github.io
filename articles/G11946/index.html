Hotel Sunrise Hotel Rhodes 4 * (Rhodes, Greece): description, photos, reviews of tourists

Greece is a very sunny and warm country. Of course, there is something to see. A very large number of people dream of visiting Greece. Every year a lot of tourists flock here, each of which pursues a certain purpose. Some dream of learning more about the culture of Greece, to see the sights, to walk the streets. Others, on the contrary, prefer a more relaxed and imposing vacation. Just for such tourists , the Sunrise Hotel Rhodes 4 * hotel is perfect. It is located in a beautiful place in a resort village called Lindos. It often attracts tourists, as this resort is located on the Mediterranean coast. The hotel has four stars, and many tourists are convinced that the value for money of this hotel is fully consistent with the declared level.





Accommodation for tourists

Most vacationers in their reviews describe in sufficient detail the procedure for their placement in the hotel Sunrise Hotel Rhodes 4 * (Greece, Rhodes). After analyzing, we can conclude that, depending on the time of day, there are no problems with accommodation. Basically, everyone is satisfied with the staff and the rooms themselves. Only some tourists say that they got the rooms are not very successful, in remote buildings. But, as a rule, hotel staff never refuses vacationers in a request to relocate them to a particular room.

Sea and beach

One of the undoubted advantages of this hotel, vacationers consider its location. The chic sea with blue water and the sandy beach attracts most tourists. The rental service for sun loungers and parasols is paid. For a set of two sunbeds and umbrellas, the visitor needs to pay eight euros. You can also pay for this service for several days at once. In this case, a person is assigned a nameplate. However, the sand on the beach is quite clean, in principle, rest without a sunbed is also possible. Visitors also note that the beach is literally a stone's throw from the bar and restaurant. Each vacationer can spend his breakfast, lunch or dinner on the Mediterranean coast, enjoying the beautiful view and the stunning sounds of the water.





Nutrition

This is an important aspect for many tourists. When choosing a new vacation spot, most people attach particular importance to the quality of food. As for the hotel Sunrise Hotel Rhodes 4 * (Rhodes, Lindos), then on this issue the opinions of visitors differ. Of course, absolutely everyone likes the all-inclusive system. Most tourists thought the food was ok. That is, everything was enough, the choice is wide enough, the quality of the products is also completely satisfied. But still, there are those vacationers who did not really like the food in this hotel. They note a not very large selection of meat dishes, the almost complete absence of children's food accessories and uniformity. However, in most cases, vacationers are satisfied. Also, many note a fairly large abundance of fresh vegetables and fruits.





Bar

At the bar, all drinks are also included in the price here, even alcoholic. However, all this splendor only lasts until twelve in the morning. After this time, the bar is open for another hour, but for the money. Visitors note that, in comparison with Russian prices, alcohol here is not very expensive. But still, after twelve, only a few people usually remain near the bar.

Cleaning

Here the opinion of vacationers is absolutely identical. All visitors say that the rooms are always clean. Cleaning is carried out daily: dust, floors, bathroom are wiped. In general, everyone was happy with everything. Tourists also note that the cleaning is timely and fast enough, about ten to twenty minutes. That is, there are no such cases that you need to get into the room, and there they wash it.





Additional functions

This is indicated by few tourists, since not everyone has such cases when it is necessary to turn to such services. However, those vacationers who encountered some problems claim that everything went well, the staff quickly orientated themselves. This applies, for example, to medical care. If there are any problems on the technical side of the room, then all problems will be fixed as soon as possible. The hotel also has the opportunity to rent a car. This service, by the way, is also paid.

Staff

Most of the guests completely liked the staff. Namely, they were satisfied with their work, quality of service and just a good attitude. According to tourists, hotel workers are always ready to meet, help, suggest. Since the hotel Sunrise Hotel Rhodes 4 * is supposed to accommodate tourists from Russia, Russian-speaking staff is also provided. But even if the staff does not speak Russian, visitors will be able to chat in English. In general, we can say that no one has ever had problems with understanding.

What else

In addition to the hotel itself, there is a small supermarket on the territory. Small goods are sold there, some of the products. Of course, prices here are much higher than real ones. Visitors rarely visit it, basically everyone prefers to walk twenty minutes to the usual market with normal prices. In addition to the store, on the territory of the hotel itself there are no more additional buildings. However, most tourists consider this a definite plus.





Entertainment

On the territory there is a very decent pool, judging by the reviews of vacationers. Large enough, regularly cleaned. The hotel also has a small option for the smallest. The hotel also has an amphitheater, it is where all evening events are held. In addition to evening concerts, entertainers entertain entertainers throughout the day. They conduct water aerobics, volleyball. Sports games in this hotel are quite enough. This is where the main entertainment ends. Occasionally, the Sunrise Hotel Rhodes 4 * (Greece, Rhodes) also hosts additional entertainment events.

Instead of an afterword

In general, we can conclude that the hotel Sunrise Hotel Rhodes 4 * receives positive reviews. It is a very decent option for a family vacation. Most tourists, despite some shortcomings, still recommend this place for their holidays. The main thing that most people expect from the hotel is a comfortable, beautiful sandy beach. This is the main advantage of the Sunrise Hotel Rhodes. Also, most tourists celebrate the magnificent nature. Of course, this place is more suitable for a large family with children. The hotel is always happy to welcome guests. The staff and administration of the hotel always make every effort to ensure a comfortable stay for guests.
