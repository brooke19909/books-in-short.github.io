Popular camp sites of Crimea

Crimean camp sites are very popular. This is due to the unique nature, beautiful landscapes and clean air. The peninsula constantly receives tourists who want to not only see the sights, but also relax on the sea beaches. On the Crimean coastline are some of the most famous resort towns among Russian-speaking vacationers: Alushta, Yalta, Koktebel, Kerch and others. Among these, there are also health centers, for example, Theodosius. Of course, they have recreation centers, motels, hotels, hotels and other establishments, where you can stay both for a short period and for a long period. The most popular among them are the following: "Sea Breeze", "Surf" and "Stepnaya". About them and some other bases will be discussed in the article.

"Sea breeze"

The tourist base is open all year. The administration takes from 1 thousand rubles per person per day. If we consider the issue of nutrition, then for these purposes a bar works here. In it you can order both one-time and three-time food consumption. It is worth noting that there are various discounts and promotions. For example, for a child under 10 years old, parents will pay only 50% of the cost. If you want to enjoy barbecue or seafood, then you can make an individual order - and the cook will satisfy this request.

As for accommodation, the Sea Breeze is located in Sevastopol. Next to it lies the coastline of the Black Sea, as well as Cossack Bay. The recreation center is 10 km from the city center. At the guests' disposal is a two-story cottage where there are comfortable places for 2 or 4 people.



"Surf"

Chalet "Surf" (Crimea) operates in summer and autumn. For one person a day you will have to pay from 250 rubles. For three meals a day, the administration will take 600 rubles. (breakfast - 149 rubles, lunch - 306 rubles, dinner - 145 rubles).

On the territory of the "Surf" there are more than 20 wonderful institutions where you can go eat for yourself or with your family. Bars, pizzerias, ordinary cafes, pancake houses work. If you want to cook yourself, then you can always buy at the nearest market or in a store.

This recreation center has become popular due to its vast territory (4.5 ha) and a significant number of cottages. No wonder "Surf" was called the "city in the city." There are more than 300 residential buildings. They differ in the number of floors (2, 3 or 4), as well as the rooms that are equipped with them. There is a suite, a room with standard amenities and partial.



"Stepnaya"

The recreation center "Stepnaya" welcomes guests all year round. For a day, the minimum cost per person is 700 rubles.

In the rooms of economy and standard there is no kitchen equipment for self-cooking. In rooms that are somewhat more expensive, appliances are available. You can rent a barbecue and other barbecue grills.

The camp site is located in the village of Olenevka, which is located in the steppe zone of the Black Sea coastline. There is Cape Tarkhankut nearby . You will have to walk about 2 km to the sea coast, but many decide to relax on Lake Liman. The latter is located just a kilometer from Stepnoy.

There are different types of rooms, as well as a house. It is designed to receive guests all year round. A maximum of 7 people can occupy it.



"Artemis"

When describing Crimean camp sites, it is necessary to tell about Artemis. She is one of the most expensive options on this peninsula.

The base works in all seasons except winter. The minimum cost per room per day is 3 thousand rubles.

Breakfast here is based on the principle of a modern buffet system, however, you will have to pay separately for dinner and lunch. On the territory of "Artemis" there are two small, but solid restaurants.

Children under 3 years old stay free of charge, but they are not provided with an extra bed. You will have to pay extra for their food. If one person lives in the room, then he is given a 30% discount.



Golden

Crimean camp sites are in great demand. One of the popular is Golden.

It is worth noting that, due to its price category, it is available, unfortunately, not to everyone. The minimum cost per day for a room is 3010 rubles. It works all year round.

People who miss the USSR or simply love this state often come here. Many Soviet dishes are served in the local dining room, and the decor itself is designed in the appropriate style. The pool has a bar, it is open only in summer. There is a pizzeria.

The complex is located in Alushta. Relict tree species grow on its territory, which allows not only to purify the local air, but also to make it as useful as possible.

Belbek

Crimean camps located on the slopes of the peaks are most often visited by people who need to improve their health. The mountainous areas of the peninsula are particularly popular with tourists.

The lowest room rate in Belbek per day - 2500 rubles. The recreation center operates year-round.

There is the possibility of self-cooking. There is a kitchen in every room. Near the houses there is a gazebo where barbecue is allowed to be fried. A menu is also provided for those who for some reason cannot or do not want to waste time cooking. Tourists have at their disposal a cottage, a chalet, a deluxe room and a log cabin.
