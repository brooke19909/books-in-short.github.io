Tuapse hotels: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Tuapse, one of the most popular resorts in the South of Russia, offers a large selection of guest houses and hotels. Therefore, the issue of housing for the holidays is resolved without problems. There are budget Tuapse hotels, luxury hotels and middle class pensions.





Conditions and assortment

In any guest house of this resort, tourists are greeted kindly and try to help with adaptation, because everyone knows how difficult it is sometimes to navigate an unfamiliar city.

Tuapse hotels offer everything you need for the convenience of guests and a good stay. The technical equipment of hotels is not inferior to foreign guest houses. Visitors can stay in budget mini-hotels or rent luxury apartments. Modern rooms equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay will appeal to every tourist. You can safely come to Tuapse - hotels by the sea will take any traveler.

Budget Guest House

Private Tuapse hotel called “The Seagull” is a guest house located literally five minutes walk from the beach. The hotel is attractive in that it has its own outdoor pool. There is a summer kitchen where guests can prepare their own breakfast or afternoon snack.

All rooms in the guest house are stylishly and harmoniously decorated, each room has a TV. Near the guest house there is a restaurant of Russian cuisine - the chefs prepare delicious national dishes here. This Tuapse hotel has a very favorable location - close to the train station. True, the airport is far, but calm. The hotel, by the way, offers transfer services. It is worth mentioning the prices. A twin room will cost 4200 rubles per week for two! This is extremely cheap, taking into account all the above advantages.

Middle class





Hotel “Caravela” can be safely attributed to this category. It has a favorable location: 10 minutes of leisurely walk separates it from the beach.

The modernly furnished rooms have minibars and flat-screen TVs. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. Toiletries and bath amenities are provided.

The hotel serves a delicious rich breakfast. The restaurant also serves international dishes. On request, a children's menu is provided. Another nearby restaurant specializes in European dishes.

Guests can book beauty treatments at the hotel. Lovers can play billiards. The reception is serviced around the clock.

Five minutes separates the Caravelle Hotel from the Tuapse Central Station, and to one of the entertainment, to the dolphinarium, is only twenty minutes by car. This is an excellent middle class hotel, and you can spend an unforgettable week in a double room for only 25 thousand rubles for two.





All the stars of comfort

In the city of Tuapse, hotels are available for every taste and color. So, for example, lovers of luxurious and expensive vacations can be accommodated in the hotel complex “Aquamarine”. It is located near Tuapse itself.

This place is ideal for spending an unforgettable vacation in it. The hotel has several restaurants where guests can try refreshments of European and Russian cuisine. Every day a varied breakfast buffet is organized. Guests can spend their free time in the cozy bar. In general, you won’t be bored here: you can go for a swim in the spacious pool, take a steam in the sauna, or rent a bicycle. Lovers of outdoor activities have the opportunity to visit the fitness center.

Many people come here with children, and this is the right decision: a playroom has been created especially for small guests. Wi-Fi, air conditioning, TVs, free parking and unusually picturesque surroundings - this and much more can be offered by far not all Tuapse hotels. But the complex “Aquamarine” is able to provide a truly magnificent vacation.

The very center





Many visitors are looking for a hotel that is located near the city center. The ideal option would be “Rus” - Tuapse Hotel, located a few steps from the train station and 15 minutes from the Black Sea coast.

The comfortable rooms are decorated in a classic style. Each is equipped with air conditioning, a plasma TV, a private bathroom and a fridge.

A hundred meters from the hotel are many restaurants and cafes. Only 50 meters away the hotel is from the main street of Karl Marx. Across the road is a bowling club.

For people who want to live in the center of the spa town, this is a great place. And you won’t be bored in the hotel itself: you can visit the sauna, take a book in the library, soak in the jacuzzi, play billiards, go to karaoke, have a barbecue, enjoy a massage in the SPA center, visit the beauty salon - all this and much more at the hotel. Here everyone will find something to their liking. If you are tired of looking at the surroundings, swimming in the sea and sunbathing, you can enjoy the delights of the hotel.





Guest Comments

Many potential tourists read the comments of already experienced guests of Tuapse hotels. Hotel reviews have different: from restrained to positive emotional.

Take, for example, the Tuapse Hotel of the same name. This is a budget option with excellent service and polite staff in the very center of the city. An economy class room here costs 250 rubles per place, and equipped with all necessary amenities - from 650 rubles. Agree, quite democratic for a resort town?

On the aforementioned “Caravel” the guests leave pleasant feedback, claiming that it is a hotel with a high level of service, a systematic management approach. The staff is polite, trained, professional, ranging from maids to bartenders. And of course, I like a well-equipped complex. So if you look, you can find just such a hotel in which I would like to spend my vacation.
