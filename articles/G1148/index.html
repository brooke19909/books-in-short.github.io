Sanatorium "Dunes" (Sestroretsk, St. Petersburg): recreation, prices, beach complex "Rainbow", photos and reviews

At the beginning of the 19th century, the active development of the tourism industry began in Sestroretsk, Leningrad Region. It is a holiday resort that has important socio-economic importance for the region. This is facilitated by a combination of fresh air, a unique natural landscape and proximity to the center of St. Petersburg.

The advantage of this city is the absence of industrial enterprises on its territory and the presence of the Dubki nature reserve park. In addition, Sestroretsk has long been known for its healing mud and mineral springs. This is a real balneological resort on the coast of the Gulf of Finland.

It was here in 1979 that the Dunes sanatorium was founded, which received wide publicity in the Soviet years. Many people from different social backgrounds came here to rest and improve. Recreational institution and now enjoys no less popularity and specializes in rehabilitation, preventive and spa treatment.
