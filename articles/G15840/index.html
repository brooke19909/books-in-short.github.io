How to get a Perfect Money wallet. Features of working with him

Many earn on currency exchange, because they have several electronic wallets. Recently, along with WebMoney and Yandex Money, the Perfect Money wallet (Perfect Money) has become popular . This is the most convenient, affordable and safe system that allows you to save and increase the financial means of users.

Brief information about the Perfect Mani wallet

The Perfect Money wallet allows you to create an account in dollars, euros and gold. On it you can store money, exchange and increase it at interest. The company "Perfect" has a group of professionals: bankers, lawyers, economists, financiers, programmers. Therefore, difficulties with this service does not arise even for beginners.

Create a Perfect Money wallet can citizens of any country. This is especially convenient if you need to make money transfers from one state to another. It is enough to create accounts in the "Perfect" system and make an internal transfer from 0.5% (for premium wallets).

If it is required to transfer electronic money from one service to another, the percentage will be as follows: 0.5% for verified accounts and 1.99% for unverified ones. In addition, when depositing and withdrawing funds, a certain amount is also withdrawn, so on the replenishment page you need to familiarize yourself with the methods of depositing money and a list of exchange partners.

How to verify your account

After filling out the questionnaire, first verify your account. This will allow you to reduce the commission from 1.99% to 0.5%. Of course, if you operate with small amounts, then the withdrawn percentage is not felt, but if you work with big money, then the losses increase.



As for the time, verification can take from a couple of days to several weeks, so do not put off this matter until later. You will be required to take screenshots, a scanner or photos of the first page of your passport, where your real name appears, and receipts for payment (utilities, Internet, phone, taxes, etc.), which will indicate your address and surname with name.

When all the documents are ready, open the Perfect Money wallet.

  • Go to your page and go to the settings section.

How to fund your Perfect Money wallet

In your account on the right side, look at three wallets (dollar, euro and gold), copy the number along with the letter value. Let's say you need a dollar wallet, then copy all the numbers with the letter U.



Next, find the points of exchange currencies and see the rate. To do this, select the currency in the "Give" section and RM (dollars or euros) in the "Receive" section. RM is a symbol of currency "Perfect Money". Once you have decided on the exchange, follow all the steps and get the money. Choose exchangers with not only a profitable exchange rate and instant exchange, but also online support that will help you solve translation problems.

From the exchanger to the Perfect Money wallet, electronic money can be received in the period from 5 minutes to 2 days.
