In the concept of V.I.Vernadsky about the noosphere, a deep and comprehensive analysis of the evolution of the Earth as a result of a single cosmic, biogenic and anthropogenic processes is carried out. Noosphere is   the sphere of interaction between nature and society, where the rational activity of man begins to become the main determinant of development. The human mind, which manifests itself in scientific and technical achievements, is an important factor in the further development of planet Earth. The concept of the noosphere believes that the appearance and development of man is a logical, sequential stage in the evolution of the biosphere.
