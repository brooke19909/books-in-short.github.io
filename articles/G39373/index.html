Montpalau 3 * (Spain / Costa del Maresme) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Pineda de Mar used to be a small, modest fishing village. Today it is a popular resort on the coast of Spain. It is located in the Costa del Maresme between Girona and the capital of the country - magnificent Barcelona. Today, this place attracts tourists with its luxurious multi-kilometer beaches, as well as the availability of hotels and entertainment for every taste and budget. If you decide to spend an unforgettable vacation in Pineda de Mar and at the same time want to save money, then as an option for accommodation we recommend the Hotel Montpalau 3 *. Today we take a closer look at this hotel, and also find out whether tourists from Russia liked it here.





Where is?

This hotel has a very favorable location. So, it is located in the center of Pineda de Mar, in the central square of the city. Therefore, the entire infrastructure of this resort town for guests "Montpalau" will be around the corner. Another advantage of the location of this hotel is its proximity to the sea. So, you can walk to the nearest beach in only 10 minutes. As for the neighboring major cities - Barcelona and Girona - the distance to both is about 50 kilometers. In addition, in just an hour and a half you can reach the border with France, and in a couple of hours - to the border with Andorra. Thus, during a trip to Spain, you can cover at least two neighboring countries.





How to get there

Most often, tourists traveling to Montpalau 3 * (Pineda de Mar), tourists arrive in Spain through the air harbor of Barcelona. Many European air carriers, as well as the Russian Aeroflot, fly here. From Barcelona to Pineda de Mar can be reached by bus or train (run every 20 minutes). If you want to travel in comfort, you can order a transfer at the hotel or take a taxi. However, such a trip will cost you more.

Spain - hotel Montpalau 3 *: photos and description

Montpalau is a comfortable three-star hotel, offering its guests to stay in one of 127 comfortable rooms located in a multi-story building equipped with an elevator. A pleasant bonus for guests will be the presence on the roof of the hotel pool with a sun terrace. Here you can have a good time and view from the heights of the surroundings. The hotel also has a restaurant, a bar, a 24-hour front desk, a games room, car rental and a tour desk. This hotel is perfect for all categories of tourists who want a budget vacation in one of the most popular resort cities in Spain.





Hotel Policies

Check in for 3 * guests staying in Montpalau is carried out after two in the afternoon. On the day of departure, your room must be vacated before 11 a.m. You can pay for accommodation and additional services at the hotel with both a plastic card and cash.

Number of rooms

All 127 rooms of the Montpalau Hotel, although small and furnished with no frills, are very cozy and have everything you need for a comfortable stay. So, they are all equipped with their own bathroom, air conditioning, TV, telephone, safe and balcony. Hairdryer and iron, if necessary, you can take at the reception. Rooms are cleaned every day, and bed linen and towels are updated twice a week.





Nutrition

The Montpalau Hotel offers its guests to choose one of three catering schemes: breakfast, breakfast and dinner only or all inclusive. All meals are served in the hotel restaurant, where a buffet is served . There is also a bar with a wide selection of drinks and snacks.

Beach vacation

Just ten minutes walk from Montpalau 3 * there is an excellent pebble beach. For the rental of sun loungers and parasols here have to pay. However, you can always sit on a towel or rug you brought with you on a stretch of beach not occupied by sun loungers.





Infrastructure

The Montpalau Hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable stay of tourists. So, the reception desk is open around the clock. The hotel also offers car rental and a tour desk. Also, guests have the opportunity to order food in the room, use the laundry and dry cleaning services.

Accommodation cost

As for the price of accommodation in this hotel, it is fully consistent with its category and the services provided. So, a seven-day accommodation in August will cost you two in the amount of 40 thousand rubles.





Montpalau 3 * (Spain): reviews of Russian travelers

In order for you to have a more complete and closer to the real state of affairs impression of the three-star Hotel Montpalau, located in the Spanish city of Pineda de Mar, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the impressions of our compatriots who were lucky enough to stay here recently. Looking ahead a bit, we note that most tourists were quite satisfied with their choice of hotel. So, according to them, Montpalau is a hotel quite worthy of its category, offering its guests the best value for money. But we will understand in more detail.

The greatest advantage of this hotel, many guests considered its location. So, it is located in the heart of the resort town of Pineda de Mar, in the main square. Therefore, the entire tourist infrastructure for Montpalau 3 * residents is within walking distance. So, in the vicinity of the hotel you will find a lot of shops, supermarkets, small shops, restaurants, bars, etc. Montpalau is also located quite close to the beach - you can walk to the coast in a leisurely step in just a few minutes. Due to the fact that the town of Pineda de Mar is small and not as noisy as its closest neighbors, loud sounds will not interfere with your night's rest. The only exception to this rule is the time for celebrating the days of the city, which falls at the end of August. In this case, every evening you can watch a variety of entertainment events: performances by artists, musicians and singers of various genres, costume processions and much more. In addition, according to our compatriots, from Pineda de Mar it is very convenient to travel to other Spanish cities. Thus, the Russian tourists the location of the hotel in question was almost unanimously evaluated for a solid "five".





As for the Montpalau 3 * rooms, reviews about them are mostly positive. So, they seemed to our compatriots small, but quite comfortable and well furnished. Furniture items and appliances, of course, are far from new, but everything is in working condition and suitable for use. If any malfunction occurs, then inform the reception at this and all breakdowns will be quickly fixed. Most of the travelers were satisfied with the cleaning of the rooms. So, maids daily wet rooms, and also change towels and bed linen a couple of times a week. Experienced travelers also warn that towels cannot be taken out of the hotel to the beach. Therefore, you will either have to buy beach towels already in Spain, or grab them from home.

In addition, guests enjoyed the small rooftop pool with a sun terrace equipped with sun loungers. According to them, quite a few people come up here, so you can have a good time plunging into the warm water of the pool, and then relaxing on a sunbed, almost completely alone.

As for food, then, according to most tourists, at the Montpalau 3 * hotel (Spain) its level is quite high. So, judging by the stories of the guests, even though the dishes in the restaurant were regularly repeated, there was always a sufficient choice. In addition, the quality of food here is very good. Therefore, you will not stay hungry here. Experienced travelers recommend choosing only breakfast or half board when booking a room. In this case, you will not be attached to the hotel, but you will be able to dine in the many restaurants of both Pineda de Mar and other cities. Also, guests are reminded of the options available in the hotel in the form of lunch boxes. Therefore, if you plan to go on an excursion early in the morning, then warn the administrator in advance that skip breakfast and you will pack it with you.

The beach closest to the hotel was also mostly liked by travelers. It is very clean and well equipped. So, there is everything you need: sunbeds, umbrellas, equipment for water activities, showers, cafes, bars, etc. True, at first some were confused by small pebbles, but in a couple of days it would seem easy to get used to this discomfort. In addition, you always have the opportunity to go to any other beach. You can do this either by bus or by train, or by taxi.

The staff of Montpalau Hotel 3 * also deserved very flattering reviews from our compatriots. So, tourists really liked the friendly, pleasant, welcoming, attentive and always ready to help staff. Even despite the fact that no one speaks Russian here (some employees know only a few of the most common words), you can always communicate in basic English or Spanish without any problems. Also, experienced travelers recommend that guests contact the administrators even if you have questions that are not directly related to the hotel. Reception staff will always be happy to answer and advise you on interesting sights, inexpensive shops, etc.

Summing up, we can confidently say that the three-star Montpalau Hotel, which is located in Pineda de Mar, offers its guests the best value for money. Almost all tourists who visited here were satisfied with their choice and are ready to recommend Montpalau 3 * to those who want to spend an excellent vacation on the coast of Spain, while staying in a comfortable but inexpensive hotel.
