The list of “Your Start” channels from Rostelecom for small and adult TV viewers

These are Russian news channels, educational, entertaining, sports, introducing the achievements of culture and technology, the latest events in the world, showing series and feature films.

What channels are included in the offer?

The list of Rostelecom channels “Your Start-Up” includes popular international programs. For example, Eurosport, which tells about sports news and events taking place around the world.

The Animal Planet channel shows programs about the life and habits of animals in the wild and nature reserves of the world.

With Viasat Nature, the Discovery Channel, you can make exciting journeys around the planet.

Viewers of English Club TV, France 24, BBC World News will not only be aware of international news, but will also be able to practice learning foreign languages ​​and pronunciation.

Viewers will be able to watch their favorite Russian channels, such as STS, TNT, Ren-TV, Domashny, and central news programs.

Interactive tv

Connecting interactive television using the latest technology expands the list of channels from Rostelecom Your Start, as well as user capabilities.

In the house or apartment it will be possible to watch several TVs at the same time, which is important for families in which grandparents, parents, schoolchildren and kids live together: everyone has their own preferences.

Interactive television allows you to stop broadcasting, watch programs at a convenient time, record the necessary information. Customers get access to the archive, where you can find your favorite movies and music. With interactive television, you will watch any broadcasts in high image quality and stereo sound. Connecting a multi-screen allows you to watch a program started on one TV and end on another.
