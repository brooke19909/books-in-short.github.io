Two ways to transfer money from Utel to Utel

This article will discuss how to transfer money from Utel to Utel. Two methods will be described, an explanatory instruction is attached, and the conditions for its observance are described. In the end, let's talk about the service "Money in debt", which can also help out in a difficult situation.

General information





Utel is a mobile operator represented by Rostelecom. At the moment, the operator has more than 5 million subscribers.
Utel is currently operating in the Urals, but it may soon appear outside its borders.

Many subscribers are wondering how to transfer money from UTEL to UTEL. The operator provides its users with the Mobile Transfer service, which in turn helps subscribers transfer money from one Utela number to another.

For ease and joy of communication, subscribers should remember only three key numbers - 126. They help users instantly transfer money to each other and continue communication. With this combination of numbers, it became even easier to put money on Utel!

Let's consider several ways how to transfer money from Utel to Utel.

1 way: USSD request

By entering a specific command on the screen of your mobile phone. To transfer money to another number of the Utela operator, you just need to dial the key combination: * 126 * subscriber number in 10-digit format * transfer amount #. Then you need to confirm the transfer.

2 way: SMS translation





The second way, how to transfer money from Utel to Utel, will also not take much time and will not force you to run to the nearest terminal. You just need to send an SMS to number 126. In the SMS text, you should indicate the number of the required subscriber in a 10-digit format and indicate the amount needed for the transfer through a space. Also, after sending a message, you need to confirm the transfer.

Money Transfer Confirmations

As mentioned above, after sending or entering a combination, you need to confirm the money transfer. An SMS with the text "To transfer the amount to the subscriber enter * 126 * 2 * confirmation code #" comes to the number of the subscriber who wants to send funds. The combination is entered in the same way as the command from the first method. After confirming the transfer, the money will be transferred instantly to the right subscriber and he will be able to continue communication calmly.

Thus, you have learned how to transfer money from UTEL to UTEL.

Terms of Service

Now let's look at the conditions under which a transfer is made.

  1. The minimum transfer amount at a time is the ruble. The maximum is 1,000 rubles.
  2. The number of transfers per day is not limited. Their amount is limited, so you can transfer 1,000 rubles at a time. You can transfer 5,000 rubles per month. That is, if in one day you make 5 transfers of 1,000 rubles each, you can no longer use this service within a month.
  3. From the account of the subscriber who makes the transfer, 5 rubles is additionally debited as a commission.
  4. This function is easily locked and can also be easily unlocked. Simply type the key combinations * 126 * 0 # and * 126 * 1 #, respectively.
  5. Transfers are made only to Utel numbers.

Borrow money

Utel and borrowed money are also compatible things. For its subscribers, the operator provides the promised payment service, which can be taken by dialing a simple combination of commands, for example, * 122 * 1 #. Next, press the call button - the promised payment on the phone is ordered. You can also order payment in your Utela account.





By a simple number 100, you can also take the promised payment. You can call him by dialing a combination of 100 * 17, or send SMS to a short number 100 with the text "17 * amount of the required payment".

The account is replenished very quickly, the waiting time is several minutes. The subscriber has only 5 days to return the promised payment. It is worth noting that the commission for the service is quite small, for example, for 100 rubles of the promised payment, only 7 rubles of the commission are debited.
