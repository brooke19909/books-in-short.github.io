Active winter activities



In the winter cold season, the Logoisk ski center is pleased to offer its guests remarkably equipped pistes. They are read here 5 pieces. Each is well lit, so you can ride even in the dark. For the convenience of customers, the tracks are equipped with a cable car, so there will be no problems with lifting. Due to the high throughput, there are no queues, and people can comfortably climb the track. In addition to the cable lift, there is an additional rope hoist.





The state of the tracks always pleases the guests of the club. This makes them come from year to year to ride in "Logoisk". The ski center, the photo and description of which is given in this article, pays great attention to the comfort of visitors, therefore it uses exclusively modern equipment for maintenance. Each guest can feel in a snowy paradise. The administration also took care of the newcomers. A special training route has been created here, where even children can get the basics of skiing. Everyone has the opportunity to take the help of an attentive and professional instructor who will explain all the nuances. In addition, you can rent a car and take all the necessary equipment. The ski center "Logoisk" offers guests a lot of entertainment. For example, the attraction "tubing" or "funny cheesecake." The administration is trying to organize a complete leisure and, in addition to skiing, organizes contests, draws and many other entertainment events.

Rope town

The ski center "Logoisk" is not only snow and skiing. In summer, you can have fun visiting the rope town. This is a whole range of tasks, obstacles at a height of 3-16 meters. Here you can test the capabilities of your body, feel the taste of victory over yourself. Thanks to such entertainment, one can overcome the fear of heights and get a huge boost of energy and new emotions.

On the trees, special platforms are reliably fixed, between which crossings with an obstacle course are stretched. These are all kinds of bridges, suspension logs, descents and much, much more. Such an adventure is sure to enjoy.

Gazebos with barbecue

The ski center "Logoisk" provides its guests with the opportunity not only to play sports, but also to relax in comfort. The complex has gazebos of closed and open type. Arbor rental is for a nominal price. In each there is a barbecue, a set of skewers. You can use light and electrical energy.

The enclosed gazebo offers a large capacity of about 60 people. This gazebo is located in a very picturesque corner of the center. The windows offer a wonderful view of the mountain slopes. It also has electricity, a private dance floor and even a patio with barbecue. In addition, the guest can be offered the organization of a holiday in the gazebo, a great entertainment program with a lot of great ideas for every taste.



If the guest wants to stay here, then at his services are offered small houses where you can stay.

Fun in the summer

Those who love outdoor activities should definitely visit Minsk, the Logoisk ski resort. The recreation center offers a wide range of services that you definitely need to use:

- horse riding lessons;
- Hiking and horse riding in the fresh air;
- organization of any events, both business and solemn;
- riding a real horse-drawn carriage.

Such entertainments will bring a lot of unforgettable emotions. Communication with horses is very beneficial for humans. This is especially true for children. The main thing is not to be afraid of the animal, because the horse really feels the mood of a person. Therefore, a bad mood is better to hide, otherwise the walk will not succeed.

Paintball

A special club has been opened on the territory of the complex. Here you can play both professionally and learn the basics. It is such a game that will allow you to get rid of unnecessary emotions and completely relax.

There is also a shooting range on the territory where you can show your shooting abilities.

The scale of the complex allows holding events of any level here, and a professional administration will help organize everything at the highest level. Many visitors speak rather flattering about the Logoisk complex.
