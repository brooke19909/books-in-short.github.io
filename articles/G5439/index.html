Chaba Samui Resort (Samui, Thailand): room description, service, reviews

Koh Samui in the Gulf of Thailand of the Indian Ocean is the standard of quality holidays in Thailand. Different categories of tourists come here - party youth, surfers, divers, families with children, people who crave privacy and want a variety of excursions. And all of them are satisfied with their stay on Koh Samui. But the island, though not to say that large, but not small. And since everyone has their own concept of quality rest, it is necessary to choose the appropriate location for themselves on Samui.

In this article we will tell you where to settle on the island for tourists who want to be in the epicenter of resort life and at the same time sleep in a quiet and relaxing environment. Hotel Chaba Samui Resort 3 * (Thailand) positions itself as a youth and budget. And how is it characterized by reviews of tourists? In this article you will find not only a description of rooms, territory and services, but also real photos of this resort hotel.

When is it worth going to Samui?

Do not unconditionally trust tour operators who say that it rains in Thailand only in the summer, and even then at night. In some parts of the country, this statement is true. But Thailand has a fairly large territory, so that in terms of climate everything was so unambiguous. And on the islands, including Samui, the weather is completely different. For example, the rainy season is still ongoing here in December. Showers stop somewhere around Christmas (12/25), but they can affect the beginning of January.

High season lasts February, March and April. But mid-spring is no longer a good time for a European. It's getting too hot. But in March, the weather in Thailand is almost everywhere clear and sunny, and the sea is calm. However, no one is immune from the violence of the elements. For example, in March 2011, after the earthquake that caused the tsunami in Fukushima, floods began in Thailand due to heavy rains. In summer, Samui is cloudy, and this softens the heat a little. It rains often, but indeed in the evening and at night. But autumn for Samui is the wettest season.

Hotel location

The largest and most popular resort on the island is Chaweng. It is here that all the entertainment of Samui is concentrated, there are the largest shopping centers and famous entertainment venues. In Chaweng, in fact, in the very center of the town, there is a lake. You can‚Äôt swim in it, but the water here is fresh. It is near this lake that the Chaba Samui Resort hotel is located. The hotel address says a lot about its location: Beach Road, 19. And here we are not talking about the lakefront, but the sea promenade.

The hotel is located in a narrow strip leading from the lake to the central beach. All the activities of Chaweng are within walking distance. Nearby is a pier, to which ferries arrive from the mainland. But the minus of this location is the proximity of the airport. But Samui Air Harbor does not accept flights at night. Accordingly, the old began to be called Lake Wing (Lake Wing), as opposed to Ocean Wing. In 2016 and 17 years, overhaul was completed in both buildings. Refurbished not only buildings, but the entire territory. What is the Chaba Samui Resort (Samui) now?

The road, and quite busy, no one can remove. She still divides the hotel into two halves - Lake and Sea. The narrow passage to the beach is very well equipped. Everywhere there is greenery, a beautiful shady alley has been laid, and at the Ocean Wing building there is a pond with goldfish. There is a pool between this building and the beach. There is also a playground and a beautiful gazebo. What are the disadvantages of the territory:

Number of rooms

Therefore, all rooms in the building by the lake are cheaper than those in Ocean Wing. Guestrooms at Chaba Samui Resort (Samui) fall into the following categories:

All rooms at Ocean Wing have balconies (on the upper floors) or terraces with street access from the first. The last two categories of rooms for guests are two-room. There are rooms decorated in hi-tech style, and there are - with a cute Thai decor.

Sea and beach

Chaba Samui Resort 3 * - hotel of the first line. But in Thailand, the beaches belong to the king de jure, and the public de facto. That is, hotels do not have the right to restrict access and passage of people along the coast. Since the Shaba Samui Resort hotel is located in the very center of Chaweng Bay, traders of all sorts of things are constantly scurrying along the beach. But, on the other hand, you can have a cheap lunch without leaving the sunbed. After all, macaroons go to the beach with their braziers.

Sun beds and umbrellas are reserved for hotel guests and are provided to them free of charge. Guests will find beach towels in their room, they are changed once a day along with others. Maids are cleaned while guests have breakfast. The hotel's large freshwater pool is lagoon-shaped. It is located between Ocean Wing and the beach. According to tourists, there are almost no people near the pool, although the water in it is very clean. This tank has a shallow compartment for bathing children.

Hotel Facilities

Service at Chaba Samui Resort is fully consistent with the three-star status of the hotel. There is a free gym, a playground, a small spa with sauna and massage services. The reception is open around the clock. Kayaks and snorkelling equipment can be rented on site. Babysitting services are also available. The hotel has a tour desk, laundry and dry cleaning services. Despite the fact that the buildings of the buildings have only four floors, they have elevators. Parking at the gates of the hotel is free for guests' vehicles.

Chaba Samui Resort: reviews

Opinion about this hotel among tourists has been mixed. All vacationers claim that saving on a room in Shaba Samui Resort is not worth it. Indeed, life next to the foul-smelling lake (and hordes of mosquitoes) is difficult to call relaxation, and no comfort in the room can improve it. The closer to the sea, the more spacious and better are the guest rooms. No noise is heard from the road there, and a wonderful view of the water surface opens from balconies and floor-to-ceiling windows. Basically, free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. But he catches well only at the reception, in the lobby. The rooms are cleaned normally, and tips do not affect the quality of maid service.

The conclusion of tourists is this: the Shaba Samui Resort hotel is suitable for youth recreation and for those who spend days on excursions. Those who want to spend all their time in a hotel, it is better to look for another place to stay, with a larger territory. But Samui will not let you relax. There are so many interesting things around: elephant and monkey farms, waterfalls, Buddhist monasteries, sea fishing. It is worth taking a sightseeing tour of the island, and see the bizarre cliffs of Lamai, the temple of the Big Buddha and much more.
