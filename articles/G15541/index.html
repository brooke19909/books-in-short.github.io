Sberbank cards: types and cost of service (photo)

Bank cards were introduced to the market relatively recently - in the middle of the 20th century. Despite this, they have become a very popular product due to the comfort that they provide to the user. At the same time, the card is a means of payment and a tool that ensures the safety of your funds. Even if the plastic is stolen or lost, you can not worry about your money in the account - just block the card.

Each bank has a set of cards that it can issue to its customers. Especially a wide choice for those who use the services of an institution such as Sberbank. The largest bank in the Russian Federation has a really large selection of instruments. Therefore, in this article we will consider the cards of Sberbank, types and cost of service. In parallel, we will try to explain why it is better to use one or another card.





Credit cards

Let's start with such a popular means of payment as usual credit cards. This is a very convenient form of issuing loans for a short period mainly for the purchase of goods and services. Their work scheme is simple: a person receives it with a certain credit limit (for example, Sberbank credit cards, the types and costs of servicing of which we discuss in this article, can be with a limit of 120 thousand rubles or more). As soon as the borrower wants to make a purchase, he simply pays with a card, due to which he also receives additional bonuses in the form of some kind of discounts.





According to the conditions of Sberbank, the user must pay a certain rate on the card (for example, 25 percent per annum). If payment does not occur within the allotted time, penalties will be charged. It should also be noted that in addition to interest on the use of credit funds, the user must regularly pay a fee for servicing the card.

Types of Sberbank Special Credit Cards

All cards of Sberbank, the types and cost of servicing of which varies depending on the type, are intended for issue to certain groups of customers. For example, Youth cards are designed for a young audience of borrowers. These are Visa and MasterCard (optional) with a service cost of 750 rubles. To receive them, you need to confirm the income. The amount of the credit limit is 200 thousand rubles, the interest rate is 33.9%.





For those who would like to take a loan without delay, there are so-called instant cards. The credit limit for them is lower - only 120 thousand rubles, and the interest rate is 25.9%. To receive it, only a passport is required from the borrower, the service is free.

Classic credit cards

Of course, there are also classic cards in the bank's product line. These are credit cards that do not provide any additional difficulties for the client - everything is extremely simple. The credit limit is 600 thousand rubles, the borrower is obliged to confirm the income, the cost of servicing varies from 0 to 750 rubles per year (it depends on whether you are a regular customer of the bank or not). There are two types of classic Sberbank cards, types and cost of services, the photos of which we provided - Visa Classic and MasterCard Standard.
