"Spring edge" (Hohryaki) - economy-class housing in an ecologically clean area

Not far from the capital of the Udmurt Republic - Izhevsk, from a city with a population of already one million people, there is the Rodnikovy Krai residential complex. Hohryaki - this is the name of the village, which gave a place for its construction.





A bit about the city and the village

Izhevsk is a huge city, one of the twenty largest cities in Russia. This is the largest center of industry, culture, education. It focuses on the defense and engineering industries. This is the "arms capital" of the state.

Hohryaki, for their part, are also the largest village of the Udmurt Republic, the population reaches five thousand people.





Location of the complex

If you look at the map of the Udmurt Republic, it becomes clear that as soon as the border of the Industrial District of Izhevsk ends, the village of Khokhryaki begins. Thus, to the future owners of apartments in the RC "Rodnikovy Krai" (Khokhryaki), the developer has provided all the amenities that are provided by the proximity to the city.

If you dream of living in the fresh air, but in a city apartment - then the "Spring Country" (Khokhryaki) will provide you with such an opportunity. Three-story houses, consisting only of studio apartments, one-bedroom and two-bedroom apartments at low cost - a real find.

They are located on Tsentralnaya Street, where you can get by turning off Votkinskoye Shosse to Tract Street. The distance from the center of Izhevsk is just over ten kilometers.

Apartments in the complex

Balconies, loggias in apartments are not provided. The hallways in their layout are quite long, narrow and not quite comfortable. The apartments are for rent with a fine finish, which includes wallpapering, stretch ceilings, linoleum - in living rooms. Similar finishes are performed in the hallways. Bathrooms are equipped with one and a half meter bathrooms, compact sinks. The walls are painted, the floor is laid out with ceramic tiles. Stretch ceiling.





The apartments have steel entrance doors. Kitchens are repaired on the same principle as living rooms. Thus, the apartments "Rodnikovy Krai" (Khokhryaki) are offered fully ready for occupancy by their future owners.

Infrastructure

Since there is a close proximity ("Rodnikovy Krai", Izhevsk, Khokhryaki), then, accordingly, the entire infrastructure of the city is available for residents of the complex. Khokhryaki is the largest village of the Udmurt Republic. More than five thousand people live in it. All the necessary infrastructure for life is available in the village. About one kilometer, which is about twenty minutes of leisurely walking, is a secondary school. This is for those who moved with their children to the "Spring Country" (Khokhryaki). Reviews of both children and adults indicate that the developer has very successfully chosen a place for the construction of this residential complex.

Those who are sick will be able to help in the Republican Clinical Hospital, located just three kilometers from the complex. The remaining objects of social services that provide a certain comfortable standard of living for citizens are also available both in the village and in the city of Izhevsk.

Surrounded by woodland, immersed in the greenery of trees - this is this residential complex, and the whole village as a whole. A quiet place where you can not hear the noise of the big city. Complete unity with nature. The ability to breathe fresh air. What more could you ask for?

The "Spring Country" (Khokhryaki) provides its residents with the opportunity not to change their usual way of life with moving to a residential complex. And at the same time provide yourself with a more favorable environmental situation. The ability to make your lungs healthier.





The Izh River flows not so far, in which it is good to swim on specially equipped beaches on a hot day. There are many other lakes surrounded by the residential complex that delight the eye with their beauty and coolness.

The houses are equipped with the latest technology. They have all the necessary conditions: sewerage, heating, power supply. In a word, all those conditions that are inherent in other new buildings not only in Izhevsk, but in all countries as a whole.

Izhevsk is a large industrial center, whose factories and production enterprises create not very favorable ecological conditions for the population. Many residents have already appreciated the benefits of living outside the city, within transport accessibility. It is this option that is offered by the developer of the "Spring Country". Excellent transport accessibility, remoteness from the city, the ability to breathe fresh air. And all this at a very budget cost. True, large families are unlikely to be able to settle in the residential complex, since the developer designed only small-sized apartments designed for families without children or with one baby.
