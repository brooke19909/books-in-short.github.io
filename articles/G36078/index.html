Samsung SC6573: reviews. Samsung SC6573 Vacuum Cleaner: Feature Overview

Vacuum cleaners began to be used in the household more than 100 years ago. Over time, more and more advanced models of the device appeared. Designers and inventors at one time came up with how to improve ergonomics and power. To date, the peak of their creation has become a robot vacuum cleaner, which practically does not require human involvement. But the good old hand-made "monsters" will be in demand among the hostesses for a long time, and their numerous reviews confirm this. Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC6573 has become the protagonist of today's article.

Technical and General Specifications

The device has the following technical and general characteristics:

  • Ability to change gears on the handle.
  • Power Samsung sc6573 - 1800W.
  • The presence of a turbo brush.
  • Filter - HEPA 11.
  • There is a pre-motor filter.
  • The generated noise is 80 dB.
  • The maximum amount of dust in the container is 1.5 liters.
  • Steel handle, telescopic.

The dust collection system is a cyclone. Garbage is pressed into a transparent container.





Characteristic

The noise produced by the vacuum cleaner is relatively quiet. The smallest indicator can boast of the Electrolux Ultra Silencer vacuum cleaner - 71 dB (the level of noise produced is comparable to normal human speech).

Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC6573 (1800W power consumption) can easily absorb fine dust, hairs, animal hair and large particles of debris with a power of 380 watts.

Premotor filter is a foam sponge. It needs to be washed several times a month. You can not dry it on heaters and the sun, so it can lose its properties.

Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC6573 does not have a dust bag. Instead, there is a container that is convenient and practical. It is enough to throw out the pressed dust after each cleaning and rinse it.

The steel telescopic tube extends easily to the desired size.





Vacuum cleaner appearance and equipment

The design is made in accordance with fashion trends. Large rubberized wheels provide unimpeded movement in the apartment and do not damage the floor covering. Samsung SC6573 is equipped with an indicator that lights up when the dust bag is full. Additionally, you can find five nozzles in the kit:

  • turbo nozzle;
  • crevice;
  • for floor and carpet;
  • for furniture upholstery;
  • brush.

Case color - red metal. The SC6573 vacuum cleaner has a height of 282 mm and a width of 252 mm. The device weighs about 5 kg. The vacuum cleaner can be stored in both vertical and horizontal positions. A cord 6 meters long is wound into the device case automatically when you press a large button equal to the width of the device. A turbo brush is needed to collect animal hairs and hair.

disadvantages

Not only receives positive reviews from the Samsung SC6573 vacuum cleaner. Let's start with breakdowns, although some malfunctions indicate the use of the device for other purposes, for example, during the cleaning of repair debris.

Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC6573: instructions and precautions

Before starting cleaning, it is recommended that you read the operating instructions and act according to the recommendations in it.

Precautionary measures:

  • Do not use the vacuum cleaner on wet surfaces. The device is not intended for suction of water.
  • Do not pick up cigarette butts, matches, hard and sharp objects with a vacuum cleaner.
  • You can turn off the vacuum cleaner only after pressing the power button, and only then pull the plug out of the socket.
  • Do not leave children under 8 years old unattended alone with the vacuum cleaner turned on.
  • To carry, use only the handle, but not other parts, such as a hose or cord.
  • In the event of a breakdown, contact a service center for servicing household appliances.

For cleaning floors with carpet, use a nozzle without bristles, and for floors, on the contrary, extend the pile of the turbo nozzle. To clean the curtains, set the power to the minimum value.

After finishing work, you need to remove the dust bag. To do this, press the button located on the bowl. It is recommended to immediately put a bag on the tank and pour the contents into it. So less dust gets into the airways.

Despite all the minor flaws and negative reviews, the Samsung SC6573 vacuum cleaner is a worthy household appliance that has the right to be in any house or apartment.
