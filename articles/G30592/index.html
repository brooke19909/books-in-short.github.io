Orenburg, LCD "Victoria": reviews

Orenburg is a multinational city, once the largest shopping center. Geographically, it is divided into several areas offering different living conditions. Of course, areas with developed social infrastructure that are separated from the industrial zone are very popular. In truth, apartments there are not cheap pleasure; they are becoming less accessible for the main category of citizens.

The active construction of new residential buildings, entire neighborhoods with their own infrastructure made it possible to solve the housing problem and provide all the conditions for acquiring your own real estate. If you have chosen Orenburg for your family, the Victoria residential complex will be a good option for you. As part of this material, we will try to highlight all the advantages and disadvantages of the complex. Reviews of the first tenants will help in the objectivity of the review.





about the project

A 17-story four-section building with a foundation of reinforced concrete. Walls with three-layer insulation provide excellent insulation properties to every resident: the apartment is warm, comfort is not disturbed by extraneous sounds. The project provides for the construction of a two-story building, tightly adjacent to the main building. It will be reserved for retail and office space.

Location

Location in the central part of the city makes LCD "Victoria" (Orenburg) quite attractive for living. Proximity to the main transport arteries, protection from smog in the form of a forest zone and access to the necessary infrastructure are embodied in an excellent residential complex.





LCD "Victoria" (Orenburg, Tomilinskaya) is removed from city noise, cars, which allows all residents to enjoy peace and quiet. Many residents of the microdistrict and those who managed to purchase an apartment in the complex, focus on a good location. This is a kind of "city in the city" - a format of modern housing, combining all the charms of urban and suburban life.

Transport accessibility

A well-developed transport network can also be attributed to the advantages of RC Victoria (Orenburg). Reviews of the first tenants confirm that the new building is located near the main transport hub of the city. Nearest bus station "Russia". From it with the help of buses and minibuses you can get to any point in Orenburg. Convenient exits to Shevchenko Street and Victory Avenue are provided for car owners, due to which residents quietly get to jobs without experiencing any inconvenience. The traffic here is not as dense as in other parts of the city, so the travel time is minimal.





Infrastructure

RC "Victoria" (Orenburg) is being built in a lived-in area with developed infrastructure. Near it there is a comprehensive school, kindergartens and nurseries, shops, pharmacies. In addition, there is enough entertainment for every taste: a cinema, a palace of culture, a swimming pool, a huge stadium for outdoor team games, cafes and restaurants. New buildings of the microdistrict are literally buried in verdure, the developer pays a lot of attention to improving the internal territory of the house: modern playgrounds and sports grounds are organized here, where children frolic from morning to late evening. Due to the special rubberized coating, it is possible to provide the necessary safety for restless kids. We were pleased with the wide sidewalks, walking areas and bike paths - now the residents of the complex will not have to leave the microdistrict in order to relax.





Ecology

From the main part of the city LCD "Victoria" (Orenburg) is separated by a forest belt. Parks and squares with the rarest species of trees and shrubs have already become a favorite place for walking and recreation for the first residents of the microdistrict. Greenery isolates the complex from the noise of cars, saturates the air with oxygen. That is why the complex is ideal for the life of all groups of the population, especially families with small children.

Layout of apartments

A choice of buyers were offered one-, two- and three-room apartments ranging in size from 33.5 to 82 square meters. The layout of the apartment complex LCD "Victoria" (Orenburg) will satisfy any, even the most demanding, taste - all buyers have come to this conclusion. There are small but cozy one-room apartments, as well as large "treshki" with a spacious kitchen, an additional bathroom, a wardrobe and isolated rooms.

All apartments are rented with a rough finish: the walls are plastered, the floor is screed, the builders will install plastic double-glazed windows in the rooms and on the balcony, entrance metal doors and heating radiators. Each apartment has high-speed internet, television, intercom, electrical wiring. Of course, I would like to get furniture and move after receiving the keys. At the same time, reviews confirm that all the work was done efficiently, the walls are completely ready for decorative decoration.





But the porches are finished with fine finish: the walls are plastered and painted, high-quality ceramic tiles with anti-slip surface on the floor, equipped with a fire safety system. In each staircase, high-speed silent passenger and freight elevators are installed, ramps provide comfort to low-mobility categories of citizens.

Landscaping of the local area has been carried out: wide sidewalks and driveways are equipped, ample parking is provided for car owners and guests, litter bins and benches for residents are installed.

Purchase terms

The cost of apartments in the residential complex "Victoria" (Orenburg) starts from 1.6 million rubles. Buyers will have access to all financial instruments and various ways of drawing up a contract of sale. You can buy an apartment not only for cash, but as part of state support programs for young families, using maternity capital funds. In addition, the developer offers the opportunity to save thanks to stocks. If you decide to purchase an apartment in the residential complex "Victoria" (Orenburg), the contact manager will definitely come in handy. It is a specialist, starting from your wishes and opportunities, will help you choose the best option for real estate.
