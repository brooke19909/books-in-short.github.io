LCD "Michurinsky", Yekaterinburg: description, developer

Yekaterinburg is a city among the Ural Mountains. Its population is more than 1.5 million people. Housing is necessary for all of them, so developers are constantly building ever newer residential complexes. One of these is the Michurinsky residential complex. Yekaterinburg allocated for its construction the most comfortable area - the Broad River.





A little about the city of Yekaterinburg

More than one and a half thousand kilometers separate Ekaterinburg from the state capital - Moscow. They are separated by the mountain system - the Ural Mountains. Therefore, its inhabitants have to live in a harsh mountain climate. To somehow brighten up the harsh living conditions of urban residents, the developers of Yekaterinburg are trying to provide them with the most comfortable residential apartments. LCD "Michurinsky" just meets these requirements. Being both an urban type building and housing almost out of town.





Location of the complex

The residential quarter is located in a promising place - the Wide River district of the Verkh-Isetsky district of the city of Yekaterinburg. When designing, the developer of the residential complex "Michurinsky" was guided by the European level of housing, so urban comfort and environmental friendliness of suburban life were combined in the quarter.

About 10 kilometers separate the complex from the city center. Residents can get to their place of residence by private car on Repin Street, crossing the Bypass Road. Further, Svetlorechenskaya and Sukhodolskaya streets will lead directly to the quarter. In addition, the complex can be reached by public transport.





The “Capital” program provides for the construction of a road connecting the areas of Academic and residential complex “Michurinsky” (Yekaterinburg). The aforementioned Sukhodolskaya Street is also planned to expand and increase the number of public transport stops up to 44. It will be additionally lit and equipped with sidewalks and bicycle paths.

Description of the complex

The complex has built more than 70 residential buildings of three- and four-story sectional buildings. All houses are built with brackets, which makes their territory closed and safer for walking. In addition, the entrances to all yards are limited by barriers, which creates a complete isolation of the courtyard territory. LCD "Michurinsky" (Yekaterinburg) very successfully combines individual housing and low-rise multi-apartment buildings.





The developer tried to create a living environment for the residents of the complex, which provides them with the most comfortable accommodation. This includes comprehensive landscaping of the complex: completely asphalting driveways and access roads to houses, arranging sidewalks, pedestrian and bicycle paths, walking alleys with landscape design.

Complex infrastructure

The Michurinsky residential quarter inside the complex offers for its young residents equipped playgrounds and sports grounds for recreation and sports. Adhering to the concept of “a yard without cars”, the developer ensured the maximum safety of children's games.

This year, in the immediate vicinity of the complex, a unique educational center will open, combining a kindergarten with 150 seats and a secondary school with 800 seats. Each apartment house is equipped with video intercom. An additional convenience in the form of apartment gas heating can boast part of residential buildings. But the residents of the remaining houses will not experience inconvenience, since the gas boiler house will provide heat.





Owners of their own vehicles are also not left unattended by the developer. They are provided with a multi-level underground parking. As is customary in almost all new buildings, and the Michurinsky residential complex (Yekaterinburg) is no exception, the first floors of the houses will be given over to non-residential premises that will house the necessary social infrastructure: shops, pharmacies, offices.

Ecological situation

The developer chose a very good place for the location of the quarter away from busy streets, city noise, industrial enterprises. The complex is buried in a forest, which makes it possible to completely immerse yourself in nature. The south side of the complex is bordered by forests, the west - with gardens. The territory of the quarter itself is ennobled by the natural Lake Polovinnoe.

Thus, having settled in the residential complex “Michurinsky”, reviews about which are only the most positive, residents will be able to combine urban living conditions and real suburban life in almost their own homes. The remoteness from the city creates such a feeling; I want to complete all things faster, come home to breathe in the freshest air.





Developer

Sooner or later, everyone needs to buy an apartment. Wanting to purchase residential property directly from the developer, a person is faced with the need for his right choice. The main thing here is not to make a mistake by carefully examining all the available information.

LCD "Michurinsky" erects CJSC "LSR. Real Estate-Ural. " This is a large organization that has always maintained a stable leadership in the top three industry leaders for several years. The company may present many thousands of square meters of built residential and commercial real estate on the territory of Yekaterinburg in accordance with 214-FZ. The company is constantly striving for the development of new territories, building up areas of the city. Each year is affected only by an increase in the facilities being in operation at one time, since the demand for new housing does not stop growing.

Apartments and their cost

Options for apartment layouts presented by the developer in his project can be seen on his official website. Apartments are for rent in finished or turnkey. Fine finishing provides for their owners the opportunity to bring their ideas about repair to life with minimal cost.





LCD "Michurinsky" offers prices for apartments and their layouts are very different. You can choose a studio apartment with a loggia and a balcony. Apartments are available from studios to three-room apartments. The footage of the first begins with approximately 24 square meters. Three rooms on average 80 square meters.

The highest price, as, indeed, in all new buildings, in studios and one-room apartments. The average cost per square meter is about 76 thousand rubles. For a three-room apartment, the developer asks for a much lower price - about 45-50 thousand rubles per square meter.

You can purchase apartments for any type of payment. The simplest and fastest is cash. The developer also offers the acquisition of real estate through mortgage loans, installment payments, housing certificates, maternity capital and offsetting of old housing.

The developer even designed two-level apartments with a second light. Such a layout allows you to expand the space, separate the various areas: the working area from the recreation area. Such apartments are most suitable for those residents who have clear ideas and their visions about the layout and repair of the premises. They will allow you to realize even the most daring design decisions, without limiting customers to a small space. As an addition to the layouts - the obligatory presence of a loggia or balcony.

The benefits of living in a complex

Of course, moving to a permanent residence in a given residential quarter, a person will not lose anything. The developer designed it in such a way as to provide its customers with a maximum of comfortable life with city amenities (sewage, water, gas). And at the same time, living in a more favorable ecological environment than other residential complexes located in the city limits can provide. A kind of urban life in the country.

It affects the not-so-high price that the developer set for the property he built. The purchase of an apartment in the complex is affordable for almost anyone in the city.
