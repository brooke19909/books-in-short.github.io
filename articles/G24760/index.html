Zelenodolsk hotels: addresses and description

Search on the map the city of Zelenodolsk should be near the capital of the Republic of Tatarstan, Kazan, on the left bank of the Volga. Arriving in Zelenodolsk, hotels can be chosen for every taste and cost.

This city is called the place of thousands of artists, because the hands of local painters create talented creations. It is not surprising that the city has an art gallery, a museum of folk art and historical heritage.

Zelenodolsk has many historical and architectural monuments; moreover, it is an important transport hub and business center. The city is surrounded by beautiful nature, fishing and walks attract vacationers to the banks of the Volga.

Where to stay in Zelenodolsk?

The cost of rooms in hotels starts from 850 p. In the center there are about a dozen hotels. Each hotel in Zelenodolsk offers comfortable conditions, trying to make the stay of guests comfortable and leave a positive impression.

It should be borne in mind that in the summer the cost of rooms rises. This is due to the fact that many residents of Kazan consider Zelenodolsk as a holiday destination on weekends or holidays.

"Volga" on the Volga

Hotel "Volga" in Zelenodolsk is located on the street. Gagarina, 1, a kilometer from the river bank.

Located in a park zone, the hotel is at the same time a sanatorium, where wellness and medical procedures are provided.

Modern medical equipment, the latest techniques and technologies in the hands of experienced specialists - this is a guarantee not only of relaxation, but also of full recovery.

Guests are accommodated in a residential building for 200 people. This Zelenodolsk hotel has rooms in the following categories:

  • single in the block and improved;
  • double in the block and improved;
  • Suite (1 or 2 rooms);
  • rooms for people with disabilities, including wheelchairs.

In the Volga there is a swimming pool and a medical building, all hotel buildings are connected by a warm transition. A modern gym, barbecue areas, a library, rental of sports equipment, excursions and entertainment programs make your stay in the Volga really interesting.

Inexpensive travel shelter

In Zelenodolsk hotels, as in any city, offer rooms of various comforts and prices.

In the city center, the Soul Hostel (43 Komarova St.) accommodates from 1 to 8 people in rooms, the cost of a place is from 300 r. The kitchen and bathroom are shared, guests receive bed linen and towels.

There are comfort rooms in Soul with a private bathroom, designed for 1-3 people.

All rooms have TVs and wi-fi.

The Finnish sauna in the hostel allows you to relax and unwind.

Inexpensive hotel in Zelenodolsk is suitable for youth companies or sports groups.

Average price category

In the central part of the city, not far from museums and shops, there is the Ternopol Hotel (44 Lenin St.). An interestingly constructed multi-storey building in the park area offers rooms ranging from single rooms with a minimum set of additional options to apartments. In total, the hotel has 13 rooms, the cost of accommodation starts at 1,500 p.

Each room is equipped with shower, fridge, TV, safe.

Many tourists choose Ternopil, because in the morning guests are served breakfast, which is included in the price. In the evening, you can stop by the hotel restaurant, which serves national cuisine.

The hotel has parking, a hairdresser, billiards and table tennis, a kids club.

When listing hotels in Zelenodolsk, one cannot miss the Uyut Hotel (Volzhskaya St., 14).

Each room is equipped with its own kitchen, which allows arrivals to save on food.

Not far from the cultural and business center is the recreation center "The Seagull" (Sportivnaya St., 11). Guests can choose from a rich number of rooms suitable option: standard room (cost from 2000 p.) To the suite. The rooms are located in cute wooden cottages, which have a refrigerator, TV, bathrooms, Wi-fi. An attractive feature of the recreation center is the equipping of rooms with the Garden sleep system.

The hotel has a sauna and offers services that will make your stay comfortable and enjoyable.

Another popular option in Zelenodolsk is apartments. For example, on Shustova 4, in the city center, there is an apartment fully equipped for a comfortable stay with a living room and a bedroom. The cost of placement is 2000-3000 p.

Sport and the Lighthouse

Those who prefer to actively spend weekends and holidays will like the Mayak Hotel in Zelenodolsk. A large sports base is located on the street. Rogacheva, 34b.

Famous athletes like to spend time here, including Olympians, the Mayak is used as a venue for training camps. The hotel is surrounded by a luxurious forest.

For guests 25 rooms of different levels of comfort are provided.

At Mayak you can play field hockey, tennis, volleyball, handball and, of course, football.

5+ accommodation

Hotel Country Club Uspenka (Lesnaya St., 93) provides 8 rooms. Each is equipped with all necessary appliances: refrigerator, TV, air conditioning. There is an iron and hairdryer. The kitchenette allows you to have snacks at a convenient time, because in addition to the stove, it has a kettle, kitchenware and a microwave. The rooms have garden or lake views. Some rooms have a terrace. Non-smoking rooms have special rooms.

Barbecue facilities and an outdoor pool attract guests who want to combine relaxation and work. Having rented a bicycle, many go for a walk in the vicinity, the fishermen find rest on the lake, engaged in fishing.

Staying in Zelenodolsk in hotels of this level will cost from 4,500 to 7,000 p.

Manor on the Volga is an even more expensive and prestigious option. Depending on the category, an overnight stay in a room costs from 14,300 to 28,000 p.

In total there are 5 rooms in the Estate on the Volga (Lesovod 49):

  • double room with separate beds;
