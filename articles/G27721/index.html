Night tours of St. Petersburg: mystical appeal

It is believed that night is the time for excursions. Especially when it comes to St. Petersburg with its unique architecture and rich history of events. Someone will argue: "City attractions are better visible during the day!" Maybe. But at night, “St. Petersburg secrets” look special: romantic, intriguing, fabulous. It is no accident that night tours of St. Petersburg are in demand by tourists.





Mystic Peter

There are many settlements in Russia that are attractive for tourism. The age of some totals five hundred or more years. It is noteworthy: a relatively young Peter in terms of attendance breaks all records. Night excursions in St. Petersburg confirm: its architectural ensembles, squares, monuments, bridges have an almost mystical appeal, which is noticeably enhanced at dusk.

Night Petersburg “erases” everything superfluous, the main thing becomes weighty, visible. It is hard to believe that the architectural creations of famous masters, like the unbending Leningraders themselves, during the Great Patriotic War of 1941-1945. survived the blockade, many examples of architecture were destroyed, some restored.

There are cities that can not boast of style endurance. In their appearance, tiring variegation, which interferes with a single perception, predominates. The dense development of St. Petersburg gravitates towards harmony: it is no coincidence that during the tour the phrase “architectural ensembles” often sounds.

The general style and functional purpose of the building groups create the feeling of the proud reign of unfading classics. At the same time, even different urban planning styles do not get out of the architectural context, but complement it, make night Petersburg more expressive, unique.





Darkness does not exist

Night tours of St. Petersburg ... It is indescribable: to walk along the cobblestone streets, look into the courtyards hidden from prying eyes, watch the game of shadows in the historic city on the Neva. Night, street, lights ... Their light is not at all meaningless and dim - it is life-giving, filling with new content all that seemed so familiar by day.

In the thickened darkness, take a look at the Bronze Horseman. It seems like another minute, and he rushes along Senate Square, turns into an old street, right on a random passerby. In the silence of a sleeping city, it is so difficult to separate fiction from reality. However, "... it’s not difficult to deceive me, I myself ..."

When is it best to go on a tour of St. Petersburg at night? The first of May is a signal to start. From this time until the end of July, white nights reign in the city. From June 10 to July 2, the “night excursion” is just a statement of the time of day. In fact - everywhere light, light. Darkness does not exist!





Reflecting in the Neva

White nights do not include lights, the street is crowded. But a dozen more July days will run through, and twilight will remind you of the eternal motion of the Earth around its axis. A sightseeing tour of St. Petersburg shows: in the darkness, the Northern capital is becoming quieter, more humble, but it is still expressive and mysterious.

The street lighting system of the city, including artistic and architectural lighting, is a complete exclusive. During a tour of St. Petersburg by bus (and pedestrian), almost all tourists note this. Lamps and searchlights favorably emphasize the aesthetics of the appearance of Petrograd.

The facades of the houses form unrealistic paintings: the inspired outlines of the past and the brilliance of modernity are reflected in the Neva waters. Night tours of St. Petersburg allow recalling Pushkin's words from the school curriculum in literature. Adjusted for the age of Peter, they sound like this: three hundred years have passed, and "... hail, full-bodied countries of beauty and wonder, from the darkness of forests, from the swamp blat ascended magnificently, proudly."





Great route

During a tour of St. Petersburg at night, buses and residents of St. Petersburg are invited to visit Palace Square, Smolny, the Cathedral of the Resurrection of Christ, the Savior on Spilled Blood (church). Night travelers are brought to Konyushennaya, Birzhevaya Square (and, of course, to the Senate Square ). The people will visit the Champ de Mars, Nevsky Prospect.

Do not pass at night the Mikhailovsky Castle and the Peter and Paul Fortress. The alternation of bus and pedestrian zones allows tourists to see a lot, and the most interesting to consider in detail. As soon as midnight arrives, the route will turn to the Neva, where the ship is waiting for the group.

The vessel will quietly pass through the channels so that viewers can admire an unusual sight - the city’s views from the water, the construction of bridges.
