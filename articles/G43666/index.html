Wall split system: what is it?

This type of air conditioner is the most common. They are performed in a wide range in terms of power, so you can choose a model for both small-sized premises and large ones. Column split system: what is it?

This type is designed to cool rooms with a large area, while air conditioning serves as an interior decoration. Typically, such devices are used in restaurants, banquet halls and cinemas. They are attached to the floor. Externally, this device resembles a column whose width is 50 centimeters and a height of two meters. A distinctive feature of such a split system is a powerful air supply for a short period of time. This is exactly what is required in the cases described.

Floor and ceiling split system: what is it?

Such an air conditioner is mounted on the ceiling or on the wall below. This allows you to place the device in the room exactly where it is convenient for you, in full accordance with the chosen interior design. The room in this case is ventilated very efficiently.
