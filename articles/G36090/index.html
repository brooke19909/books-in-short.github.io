How many km from Kirov to Kazan? How can I get to?

Tired of everyday worries, the body requires rest, this is no secret. The only question is where to relax and what to do there. If the path to the distant seas is not for you, then a trip to Kazan will be a good solution to such a difficult task, because in this beautiful city there are as many historical places as there are practically nowhere throughout Russia. We will answer the popular question: how many kilometers are from Kirov to Kazan?

The road from Kirov to Kazan

How many kilometers is the distance from Kirov to Kazan? The path to the capital of Tatarstan was laid by several routes, of which the closest passes through the village of Suna, the cities of Urzhum and Malmyzh. If you stick to this path, the road to Kazan will take only 395 km. But in fact, its most frequent users, drivers of intercity buses, do not advise driving along this road. Many of them speak of the terrible quality of the road, especially noting the sections in front of Urzhum.

That is why a more balanced decision will be to choose the path through Yoshkar-Ola, passing also Sovetsk and Yaransk. How many km from Kirov to Kazan on such a route? Do not worry, such a road is only 65 km longer than the first track, that is, in general, you will have to go 460 km to Kazan.

Sovetsk and Yaransk

The first 120 kilometers you will pass without problems, the road to Sovetsk is good. If we talk about the road in the village itself - not everything is so rosy. As in all other cities, asphalt is badly broken. However, when a traveler passes a settlement, the road again becomes excellent.

Problems begin near the next settlement - the city of Yaransk. Pits will accompany you both in the city itself and at the entrance to it. Of course, you do not need to call into the village. You can go around it, adding another 30 km to your path, but drive along a better road.

Passing (or bypassing) Yaransk, you can again see the road better. After you cross the border of the Kirov region, the coverage will become even better.
