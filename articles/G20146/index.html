Settlement and cash services for legal entities. List of banking services

Any organization in the process of work carries out settlements with suppliers, receives payments from customers, issues a salary to employees. Trading outlets credited cash proceeds to the account, and participants in foreign economic activity carried out settlements with foreign partners. That is, settlement and cash services for legal entities is a demanded banking service. The question is how to choose the right credit organization for these purposes.

What to look for?

Most organizations use a standard set of services. And RKO has expanded greatly in recent years. Now this is not only cash settlement services for customers, but a whole range of services, including:

  • account management and transfer of funds;
  • remote service;
  • salary projects;
  • cash services (receipt of cash proceeds).


Choosing a bank for service, you need to consider a number of factors.

RKO cost

Opening of the main and additional foreign currency accounts is sometimes charged. This is a one-time and insignificant payment unlike, for example, from cash customer service. A retail outlet needs to take cash proceeds to the bank every day. For these purposes, collection is ordered. This service is paid. If the client independently brings cash to the department, then the fee for crediting it to the account depends on the number of transactions. The more of them, the lower the cost.

Non-cash payments are also charged depending on the number of transactions. Some banks charge a fixed monthly fee.

Some financial institutions offer package services. It includes a number of operations in limited quantities. To calculate the profitability of this service, existing IPs can. To do this, you need to compare the proposed number of operations in the package with the actual. Why only IP? Because only a private entrepreneur can carry out five operations per month: send taxes to the budget and transfer his salary from a current account to a card account.

Other payments

Enterprises that issue salaries to employees have to withdraw funds from the account at least once a month. A commission is also charged for this operation. The lion's share of the cost is the transfer of funds to plastic cards. However, this cost item can be reduced by filling out a salary project at the bank.





Very popular today, remote service is used by both private entrepreneurs and organizations. The system "bank - client - online" allows you to save time, paper and money on making payments. Most credit organizations charge a fee for installing the program and providing electronic keys, but its maintenance is usually included in the total cost of the service package.

Service

An important role is also played by the quality and speed of service. Before opening a bank account of an LLC or individual entrepreneur, come to the bank branch and talk with the staff to understand if you can cooperate with them. At this stage, you need to evaluate the entire range of services provided, the lack of bureaucracy and the length of the working day.

Settlement and cash servicing of clients is also evaluated by the speed of processing documents. It is clear that payments are carried out in the general order. But sometimes there is a need for quick processing of the operation, for example, if the payment is formed at the end of the working day. In most banks, this problem can be solved with one call to the operators, asking them to quickly complete the transaction. Others have to apply in advance for quick payment processing.

Location of the credit institution

This is not the most important, but still an essential factor. The presence of a network of branches is very important if the entrepreneur is not concentrated in one city. In this case, you can contact any bank branch and quickly withdraw cash from your current account.





This also includes the availability and cost of software. The system "bank - client - online" allows you to make payments anywhere in the world if you have an Internet connection.

Getting a loan

The use of borrowed funds is also best discussed at the initial stage before opening an account. It is always easier to negotiate with your own bank than to look for other offers in the market. In turn, a credit institution requires fewer documents from its customers and quickly considers applications.

The probability that the proposed conditions do not suit the entrepreneur is very high. The easiest thing for this LLC. They are allowed to have several checking accounts. But the IP will have to look for another bank. And amicably parting with a financial institution will not work. For transfer of service to another credit institution, a penalty will be charged.

The situation on the Russian market

CJSC VTB 24

More attractive and high-quality service conditions are provided by the second state bank. VTB 24 considers applications for cash services to legal entities for up to three days, does not carry out any additional checks, and immediately opens a current account for 2 thousand rubles. The first month the customer is served on a start-up package for 1.2 thousand rubles, and then selects the most suitable tariff plan (from 3 thousand rubles). Payments are processed at a price of 25/60 rubles apiece (paper and electronic versions, respectively). For cash withdrawal, the client will have to pay 1% of the amount. For a quick high-quality service a small fee is charged. This is the only drawback of a credit institution.

This credit institution has a developed network of branches and a high service level. Having decided to open an account here, you can use the service of booking details, call the manager directly to the office, if it is not possible to visit the department yourself. At the same time, the credit institution tightly controls all the documents submitted.

To become a client of this credit institution, you need to pay 590 rubles. for opening an account and another 550 rubles. for his conduct. The bank accepts advance payments for three, six months and a year, while the cost of the service is reduced. Service is provided in batch. ZAO RaiffeisenBank
