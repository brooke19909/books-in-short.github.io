Let's talk about how to tie a beautiful ribbon bow

It is often necessary to decorate an item (hair clip, sewn dress or gift) with a cute bow. Therefore, the question arises of how to tie a beautiful ribbon bow so that it harmoniously adorns the product. Let's consider different ways.

A classic bow is the easiest option that does not require a lot of time and materials. To work, you need a satin ribbon and scissors.



1. Measure the desired length of the tape and cut it.

2. Fold it into 4 parts.



3. We put two ends on top of each other, creating a cross.

4. We pass one tip over ourselves through the loop formed at the bottom.



5) We tie a bow.

Next, we will look at how to tie a beautiful ribbon bow in a simple style that will originally decorate a handbag, shoes or dress. In addition to tape and scissors, you will need glue that can glue the fabric.

1. Cut the ribbon to the required size.

2. Fold the ends of the ribbon inside the bow so that they are in its center.

3. Lubricate the ends of the tape with glue.

4. Wrap the junction with satin ribbon and stick it.

The tiered bow will turn out spectacular in a harmonious combination of colors. To work, you need several types of ribbons: 10 cm long and 4 cm wide, 8 and 3 cm, 6 and 2 cm, respectively, a narrow tape, scissors.

1. Cut the corners inside the edges of the ribbons, thereby decorating the bow. The ends of the edges can be carefully treated with fire.

2. Fold the ribbons in a decreasing order on top of each other so that the widest one is at the bottom.

3. We tie the resulting bow with a thin ribbon. You can leave thin ends to fix the bow on the box.



Consider how to tie a beautiful bow from a ribbon of two colors. For work you will need: a satin ribbon 4 cm wide of one color and 2 cm wide of another color, a narrow ribbon, fabric glue, scissors.

1. We put ribbons on top of each other and form a ring, apply the tips of the atlas to the center.

2. We put a narrow ribbon on the middle of the obtained product and tie a knot.

3. Smooth the workpiece and glue the tips.

4. Bandage the workpiece and leave the tips so that you can tie a bow to something.

How to tie a beautiful ribbon bow in the shape of a lush luxurious flower, which is a wonderful gift decoration? It takes a little patience and a satin ribbon of the right length.
