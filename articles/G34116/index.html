Hotel Sentido Mamlouk Palace Resort 5 * (Egypt / Hurghada): photos and reviews of tourists

Hurghada is one of those places where millions of tourists flock annually. Here are concentrated beautiful hotels and resorts, the best of which can be considered the hotel Sentido Mamlouk Palace Resort 5 *. Having been here once, you will no longer want to look for another place to spend your vacation.

Hotel Feature

The hotel complex Sentido Mamlouk Palace Resort 5 * is located on the picturesque Red Sea coast in the resort city of Hurghada. Overcoming 13 km (just 10 minutes by bus) separating this hotel from the airport, you will plunge into the magical world of green gardens, blue pools and bright rooms. This is a truly magnificent palace that looks like an oasis, spread out in the middle of the desert. Its distance from the airport is 20 kilometers, but in order to be on the azure sandy beach, you have to walk only 200 meters.





The main feature of the Sentido Mamlouk Palace Resort 5 * hotel is the traditional oriental style, which is manifested both in the interior decoration and in the traditions of a hospitable reception of guests. The staff of the hotel complex does everything possible and impossible for tourists to remember their visit to Hurghada for a long time and always want to visit this beautiful place at least once more.
