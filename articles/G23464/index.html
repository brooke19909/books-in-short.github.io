What is a cardigan?

Count Cardigan, notorious as the leader of the Battle of Balaclava, left a bright mark in history, introducing into the army uniform a woolen sweater without a collar with buttons that could be worn under a uniform.

Surprisingly, the cardigan was long considered men's clothing until Coco Chanel offered a short model to wear with a pencil skirt.

Spacious and fluttering cardigans without fasteners, which have become an integral part of women's wardrobe, are now in fashion.

Cardigan - the basic thing in a summer wardrobe
