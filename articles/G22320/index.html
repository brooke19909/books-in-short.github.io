Crafts from Toothpicks, or Prickly Joy





In any home there is always a box with toothpicks. However, it happens that the quality of this product is poor. It seems to be inconvenient to use, but throwing it away is a pity anyway. So these toothpicks stand on a shelf in the closet ... And few people realize that they can make great crafts. Yes, you heard right. Crafts from toothpicks can be made even the smallest. And what some craftsmen do is hard to imagine!

Crafts from toothpicks. Materials

For the manufacture of various souvenirs or jewelry, you will need glue and a brush, acrylic paints, several flat plates of disposable tableware. You may also need an apple or plasticine, a hedgehog figurine, a sharp knife. And, of course, the main material is toothpicks. Depending on what crafts you plan to make from toothpicks, cotton wool, polymer clay, various yarn residues, colored paper, a button may come in handy ... Any materials can be used, especially if children will take part in the process. The brighter and more varied the crafts, the more joy you can get from their manufacture.





Crafts from toothpicks. Description

If you are going to do needlework with your children, then start with something simple but interesting. You can do crafts from toothpicks for beginners. Kids like to paint. Therefore, invite them to make a lion or tiger. To do this, paint the plastic plate in suitable colors, draw the eyes, nose and mouth. And then stick a few toothpicks in the shape of a mustache. The craft can be done by imitating the real color scheme of the animal or you can come up with your own fabulous lion. Let it be pink with green eyes or blue with bright yellow stripes. Allow the baby to show imagination. Older kids can be asked to mold a hedgehog figure from polymer clay or ordinary plasticine. And then, using the halves of the toothpicks, make him needles. The resulting hedgehog will be even more like a real one if you plant it in the yard under a Christmas tree.



Crafts from toothpicks. Schemes and manufacturing methods

More complex products will have to be done on their own. There are craftsmen who can make a ship model or an ancient fortress, observing the smallest details. Using various types of toothpicks, they manage not only to create real masterpieces, but also to achieve a game of shades. Having decided to make crafts from toothpicks, you can either start looking for manufacturing patterns, or draw a future model yourself. Try to make a dollhouse for toys from Kinder surprise or a fairy house for a bear from the cartoon about Masha. Starting to make such toys, you will not only train on simpler models, but also understand what glue is better to use, how long it takes for the parts to dry completely.
