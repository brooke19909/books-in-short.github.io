How to make an egg out of paper for Easter?

Have you ever heard about quilling? The sound of this word resembles a sport, but it is not. Quilling is a technique with which you can make crafts from colored paper with vivid drawings and unusual mosaics. It is by this technique that today we will try to make a convex Easter egg that will serve as a decoration for the holiday.

Surely each of you is familiar with the technique of drawing anime, that is, with the art of "manga". And if the latter is aimed at making high-quality and interesting cartoons, then the quilling technique will help to make an origami egg for the bright and beloved Easter holiday. Now we will consider all the important stages of this process.



We will pay attention not only to how to make an egg out of paper, but also to what is the art of quilling, how it is remarkable and what result can be expected from gifts of such an unusual format. If you are interested in this, this article will definitely not leave you indifferent.

Despite the prevailing opinion that paper crafts have long lost popularity, there are many gifts made by yourself and comparing favorably with regular store purchases. It must be remembered that when you make such a souvenir, you try to put your soul into it, give it beauty and grace, and this will be precisely appreciated by the person to whom you are going to hand your creation. Even if a fairly large amount of time is sometimes spent on manufacturing, it is worth it. Materials for our today's crafts will need a little, as well as the time required to create it. The most important thing is to know how to make an egg out of paper. And this is exactly what the quilling technique will help us with.





The first thing we need for crafts is a blank in the form of an egg. You can use an ordinary shell or make a base using the papier-mache technique - the choice is yours. The most important thing is that the workpiece is thoroughly dried, because the glue does not fit well on a damp surface. In addition to the blank, we will need paper in several colors: yellow, white, pink, light green and dark green; awl; PVA glue and a small ruler.

So, how to make an egg out of paper for Easter?

For those who are not familiar with the papier-m√¢ch√© technique, we tell. It is necessary to take the usual shell inside peeled. Pour warm water mixed with PVA glue into a small bowl and drop the newspaper cut into squares there. When it gets wet enough, pull it out and glue it on the eggshell in several layers. The billet should dry overnight, after which it is possible to break and pull out the inner shell with a needle, leaving only the paper base.

The next step in making an egg out of paper is the manufacture of jewelry. To do this, come up with a pattern and cut colored paper into thin strips. You can come up with a drawing consisting of the sun, grass and flowers, because it is very easy to reproduce.



Twist the cut strips with an awl. This must be done for a long time so that the paper does not unfold on the egg. It is worth remembering that the ribbons were twisted in different ways, that is, some are stronger and the other is weaker.

When all the ribbons are ready, put a little glue on their ends and attach them to the egg according to the design. We give some time to dry, and paint with the brush the missing fragments (butterflies, clouds). The quilling Easter egg is ready.
