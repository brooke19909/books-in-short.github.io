Greasy week

It started quite quietly. By Monday ice and snow entertainments were being prepared, participation in which was strictly obligatory if you did not want to spend old age in the mountain and crippled. The daughter-in-law went to her parents, and in the evening the elders discussed who to call and what to treat. The first pancake was given to the poor. All the fun began at Zaigrysh - eat from the belly, make fun, have fun, joke and look closely at the brides ... And the next day the son-in-law went to his mother-in-law - for a glass, and for pancakes too. These relatives were the main characters of all representations. This day is not in vain called Gourmand - after all, on Wednesday they indulged in gluttony. On Thursday, unbridled fun began with fistfights, fun and games. The scarecrow of Shrovetide already saw the light and rode through the streets. On the fifth day of the week mother-in-law with friends paid return visits to the husbands of their daughters - as a mother-in-law, the mother-in-law loves and respects. And on Saturday, hiking and festivities continued. The daughter-in-law invited guests, mainly relatives of her husband. All this was accompanied by the presentation of gifts and delicious dinners. On Forgiveness Sunday, peasants went to church. On this day, it was customary to apologize to everyone to whom a person had inadvertently done harm in order to join the Fast with a clear conscience. The fun gradually subsided, half-eaten pancakes and treats were burned, dishes were rubbed to a shine. And of course, the effigy of Shrovetide was burned as a symbol of death and rebirth.
