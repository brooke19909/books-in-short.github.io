The main attraction of Astana is the Palace of Peace and Accord

The Palace of Peace and Accord, built in Astana, is a unique structure made in the shape of a pyramid. It was built in 2006 in the capital of Kazakhstan. The grand opening of the palace took place on September 1, 2006. Construction was carried out under the guidance of renowned architect Norman Foster, who arrived from Britain. Today this building is the main attraction of the capital.





The eighth wonder of the world

The pyramid, in fact, is a symbol of the unity of various religions and numerous cultures. By right, this magnificent Palace of Peace and Concord, the photo of which is in all the guidebooks of Kazakhstan, is called the eighth wonder of the world.

The first to propose this bold project was Nursultan Nazarbayev, who has been president of the republic for many years. It was originally planned that the constructed structure will be used for meetings of leaders of world religions, as well as for multinational congresses. Today, the Palace of Peace and Accord has become more than just another business center.





Symbol of friendship of peoples and cultural center

The pyramid is built on such a principle that each person can realize three concepts that are fundamental to all world religions. The underground part of the structure is built in dark colors (lower world), the part of the building, which occupies a middle position, is made in white colors (symbol of peace), and the palace is crowned with a glass dome - a symbol of boundless sky.

The grandeur and elegance of the construction are given by the interesting upper part, made in the form of a stained-glass window, on which pigeons are depicted. The number of birds - 130, this symbolizes the number of all nationalities living in the Republic of Kazakhstan.





It is also unique that the Palace of Peace and Accord was built in strict accordance with the golden section rule: the length of one side laid in the foundation is equal to the total height of the structure.

When breathtaking!

A truly bewitching spectacle opens when you visit the hall, which is called the "Cradle", it is located in the highest part of the pyramid. You can climb into this room by elevator, but not a simple one, but a glass one, and, in addition, it moves diagonally. While the elevator rises, you can admire the magnificent landscapes surrounding the palace.

Many people prefer the elevator on foot, because as long as you rise in this way, you can enjoy the "Hanging Gardens of Astana", which are located on the territory of the palace. Plants from all over the world are represented here, and this spectacle is fascinating. The palace of peace and harmony delights with its splendor.





The unique structure, created by the hands of people from steel, glass, aluminum and other modern materials, is simply breathtaking. At night, the stained glass window at the top of the pyramid is illuminated from the inside, which creates the impression of a bright path to unity, the world of all religions of our vast planet.

The center of religion and cultures of the world is considered the Palace of Peace and Concord. Working hours of the institution: Mon - sun. from 10:00 to 18:00. Tours are conducted in three languages: Russian, Kazakh and English. This place is worth a visit to see the eighth wonder of the world with your own eyes. It will not leave you indifferent.
