Health resort "Simeiz" in Crimea: photos, reviews

Off the coast of Crimea there is a quaint village Simeiz, which is reliably sheltered by mountain archipelagos from strong winds. Tourists who visited this picturesque resort, call it a tropical garden for the abundance of evergreen plantings, fragrant shrubs and junipers. The village harmoniously combines high mountains, clean nature and warm sea. What else can a person dream of planning a long-awaited vacation?





A relaxing atmosphere with notes of dimensionality attracts people from different regions and countries. The resort continues to actively develop the business industry: new dispensaries, boarding houses, hotels with a medical profile are being built. The leading position in terms of the number of tourists is occupied by the Simeiz sanatorium. The advantageous location - half an hour from Yalta and Sevastopol - makes it even more popular.

The health resort is simply buried in thickets of tropical greenery and tall trees. An important factor is the proximity to the Black Sea - only one hundred meters. The beach area belongs to the complex, it is equipped with sun loungers, awnings, changing cabins. Terms of accommodation

Sanatorium "Simeiz", the photo of which you see in the article, was founded back in 1961. Since then, the health resort has received universal recognition from many tourists. More than 400 rooms are located in a four-story building. All rooms have balconies with views of the park area. The building has an elevator, furnished lounges.





The choice of rooms is quite large: starting from economy class with general conditions on the floor and ending with two-room junior suites. A cot or sofa is available upon request. Also, a two-story building, the so-called climate pavilion, opened in the summer season, was built to accommodate. It has several rooms in a simple and concise style with a bathroom on the floor. Cleaning in all rooms takes place daily at hours convenient for guests. Change of bed - once a week.

Feature of wellness activities

Sanatorium "Simeiz" pays special attention to medical procedures. The main therapeutic areas of the health resort are the treatment of the respiratory, cardiovascular and nervous systems. The medical base is equipped with a modern laboratory with the latest equipment. Here you can go through more than eighty different manipulations, including passing tests, and get expert help from the best doctors.

The following doctors conduct treatment at the medical institution: cardiologist, psychotherapist, dentist, pulmonologist, otolaryngologist. And also here they practice a homeopath, neurologist, urologist and gynecologist. Sanatorium "Simeiz" focuses on balneotherapy. Hydrotherapy and herbal medicine are widely used. In addition, laser therapy, magnetotherapy, acupuncture and many other effective procedures are performed.

Service and Facilities





Fun, active and interesting you will spend time in the dispensary. The main building has an indoor pool filled with real sea water. Sports people can play tennis or volleyball. For these purposes, spacious areas are equipped.

To correct the figure you are invited by a fitness trainer. Under his leadership, your muscles will regain their former elasticity and beautiful relief. Sanatorium "Simeiz" is equipped with a library, a sauna, a billiard room and a rental point for sports equipment. There is a disco club and a cinema. A tour desk with a wide selection of tours is available. Guests can rent a boat or a boat and take a walk along the Black Sea or go scuba diving.

Sanatorium "Simeiz": reviews of people





The health resort is in great demand among our compatriots who speak with admiration about quality service and effective treatment. The democratic atmosphere of the pension allows you to fully enjoy your stay. At any time of the year they are welcomed here with warmth and hospitality.

Guests noted good and varied food in the spacious dining room. As prescribed by the doctor, diet meals are served. A pebble and sandy beach with clear sea water and a flat entrance made a positive impression. The village of Simeiz is a paradise in the Crimea, where all hardships and problems are forgotten.
