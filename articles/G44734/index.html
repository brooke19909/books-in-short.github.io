Hotel The Beach Heights Resort 4 * (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

Planning a vacation in Phuket in Thailand? Would you like to live in a comfortable modern hotel with new furniture and appliances? Are you used to high-end service? Then be sure to consider the four-star The Beach Heights Resort hotel as an option for accommodation.





Location

According to many tourists who visited this hotel, he can confidently boast of a very good location. So, there is a hotel in the city of Muang on an island called Phuket. Kata Beach is 600 meters away. So guests reach the sea in just five minutes. On foot you can reach Karon Beach. This will take you no more than 20 minutes. Some travelers, they said, walked to Kata Noi Beach. This journey takes about 40 minutes. In the vicinity of the hotel there is a developed infrastructure with a lot of cafes, shops, supermarkets, massage parlors, tour agencies, rental cars, markets, etc. The main entertainment venues popular with tourists are in Patong. The distance to it is 5 kilometers. You can get here by tuk-tuk, taxi or rented scooter. As far as the distance from the airport is concerned, Phuket's airborne harbor is 35 kilometers from the hotel in question.





General information, photo

The Beach Heights Resort 4 * (Phuket) was opened in 2013. This is a modern hotel whose rooms are decorated in four different styles displaying different regions of Thailand. The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 154 comfortable apartments located in two eight-story buildings, equipped with high-speed elevators. The territory is shared with two more hotels of the same chain. It has several swimming pools, a gym, a spa, restaurant, bars, a children's playroom, laundry, parking, and an Internet cafe.

As for the opinion of tourists, the guests regarding the hotel in question leave mostly positive reviews. The Beach Heights Resort 4 *, according to our compatriots who have been here, is quite consistent with both price and category. A confirmation of this is the hotel rating, which is compiled on the basis of guest ratings. So, it is 4.4 points out of five possible. Therefore, we can confidently say that this hotel is an excellent solution for those who want to spend an unforgettable vacation in Phuket. Moreover, the hotel is equally suitable for family travelers, and youth who love noisy entertainment and parties.





Housing stock

As already mentioned, the hotel in question has at its disposal 154 rooms. They are represented by the following categories: deluxe (30 sq. M. Area, view of the pool or garden), deluxe premium (30 sq. M., View of the garden or pool, kitchenette), deluxe pool access (30 sq. M. , direct access to the pool), one-bedroom duplex (81 sq. m, pool view) and two-bedroom duplex (97 sq. m, city view, living room, kitchen).

Regardless of the category, each room is equipped with comfortable furniture, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning, a refrigerator, a safe, a bathroom with a hairdryer. Slippers and bathrobes are also provided.

Apartments are cleaned every day. As necessary, maids replenish supplies of personal hygiene products. Towels and bed linen are updated several times a week.



Nutrition

The cost of living at The Beach Heights Resort (Kata Beach) includes breakfast only. They pass in the hotel restaurant. In general, according to tourists, they are tasty and satisfying. However, many guests expected a little more variety from a four-star hotel. During the day, vacationers can eat in polynomial cafes located in the vicinity of the hotel and on the beach. Experienced travelers are also advised to drop by the local market and buy fresh seafood there. They will be prepared for you here and packaged with you.

Sea

As already mentioned, Kata Beach, the closest to the hotel, is a 5-minute walk. For the rental of sun loungers here you will need to pay. Many tourists were very pleased with the Kata beach . According to them, there is pleasant sand, not very many people, always clean. In addition, the sea in this place is not as turbulent as on most other Phuket beaches. Also, many travelers enjoyed visiting Karon Beach and Patong Beach. They can be reached on foot. If you rent a scooter, do not deny yourself the pleasure of visiting other beaches in Phuket.
