The number of people who want to relax on the Greek islands is not decreasing. The popular tourist destinations are the islands of Crete, Rhodes, Corfu, Ithaca and others. It is located in the southernmost part of mainland Greece on the Peloponnese peninsula and is the capital of the prefecture of the same name. Not far away is the island of Zakynthos: very quiet and calm, ideal for families.

Surely many people first hear the name of this island. Nevertheless, it is a magnificent seaside resort with wonderful beaches, beautiful nature and cozy small hotels. What is interesting in Zakynthos?





This small island in the Ionian Sea is considered the most creative in island Greece. Zakynthos gave the world the greatest Greek poet Dionysios Solomos. Andreas Calvos, a no less talented minister of Euterpa, the muse of lyric poetry, was also born here. In the XVII century, the Zakynthian school of painting was born on the island. As for music, the locals always treated it with special warmth and love. In the evenings, under the starry sky of the island, the melodious sounds of bouzouki and other Greek instruments playing the famous cantatas are heard. This gives Zakynthos a special flavor, an atmosphere of cordiality and carelessness. Fans of excursions will find on the island a lot of interesting routes to attractions that Zakynthos is proud of (a photo of the beautiful island is in our article). Moreover, if you rent a car (the cost of rent per day, depending on the brand of the car, is from 25 euros), then you can go around the whole island and explore every corner of it. Museums and Orthodox shrines will tell about the rich history of the island. Tourists have the opportunity to visit the most beautiful and secluded beaches, get acquainted with rural traditions, taste the local cuisine, the diversity of which is famous for Zakynthos (as Zakynthos is called in the Greek manner). Prices in restaurants and taverns are quite affordable. Dinner for two can cost 25-30 euros, and you will be sure that all products are fresh and environmentally friendly. By the way, the islanders came up with a very interesting promotion for tourists: ordering two identical dishes, you will receive a significant discount. Fans of water entertainment can visit the giant water park. Ticket price is 21 euros for adults and 15 euros for children.

How to get there

Recently, holidays on the Greek island of Zakynthos, which is located in the Ionian Sea, has become popular with Russian tourists. In season, weekly scheduled flights are made from Moscow and St. Petersburg to the island's international airport. The flight time is about 3.5 hours from Moscow, and about 4 hours from the northern capital. In addition, you can fly to the island with a change in Athens, Tripoli, Corfu, etc. So if you bought a tour to one of the wonderful hotels on the island of Zakynthos, for example, Iliessa Beach Hotel 3 *, then you can get there by any means having previously discussed the road with your travel agent.



Iliessa Beach 3 * (Zakynthos / Argasi): location and general description

This wonderful 3-star hotel is located in the Argasi area. Golden fine sandy beaches stretch over 120 km. The hotel has a small but private beach. The entrance to the sea is gentle. Between the beach and the territory of the Iliessa Beach 3 * hotel, a wall with access to the sea is built. The hotel is a cottage complex, which consists of ten two-story buildings. Ilessa Beach was built in 1988. Reconstruction was carried out in 2001-2002. This is a modest but very comfortable hotel, adapted for families. Of course, this is not an all-inclusive hotel, but it is quite decent. A large green area with olive trees, exotic plants and flowers, children's and adult pools, slides and a playground for games is very popular with guests. Entertainment and sports



Iliessa Beach 3 * has few places for entertainment, so this hotel is considered ideal for lovers of a quiet and secluded vacation. Nevertheless, there is entertainment here, for example, a swimming pool (adult and children), around which there are sun loungers and umbrellas. Many tourists like to sunbathe here, among the lush flowers, and not on the beach, because it is very small. The hotel has a TV room, billiards and a children's playground. From time to time, evening outdoor shows with Greek songs and dances are organized for holidaymakers. Guests are invited for hiking and horseback riding around the hotel, boat trips around the island. Despite the fact that the hotel has an excursion bureau, former vacationers do not recommend using its services in their reviews, but advise you to join the group with a Russian-speaking guide - an employee of one of the travel agencies that can be found in Argasia. However, as noted above, the best option for exploring the island of Zakynthos is an individual trip on a rented car.

Excursions

In the capital of the island, in the same city of Zakynthos, tourists will be interested to see the beautiful building of the belfry of St. Dionysius. This is the first thing that catches your eye, if you get to the island by water. On the largest square of the city, Solom, stands a monument to the poet Solomos, and the magnificent building of the Byzantine Museum in architecture. Here is the city library with a rich collection of unique books (about 50 thousand volumes). Another Zakynthos square - St. Mark's - is famous for the majestic Catholic Cathedral, next to which is the Solomos House Museum. And the church of St. Nicholas Mola is notable for the fact that St. Dionysius served as a parish priest here. Some of the interesting attractions include the Keri Sea Caves. In them, the water is so pure and emerald that it is difficult to tear your eyes from it. Other natural monuments are the Big and Small Arches of Maratias. They look like real bridges formed by rocks over the sea. But the peak of perfection is the Cote d'Azur, where you can sail by boat.
