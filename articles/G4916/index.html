Knitted skirt: scheme and description of work

A skirt is one of the most common and popular elements of women's wardrobe. Warm, light, knitted, sewn, openwork, dense - any only emphasizes femininity and elegance.

This season, again at the peak of popularity, knitted skirts. With knitting needles (with diagrams and a description in the kit), even a novice needlewoman can connect this.

Plain skirt

The simplest pencil skirt is knitted with English rubber. To get started, you need to take measurements - waist circumference, hip circumference, product length. The last parameter is the length that the skirt should be. For non-standard figures, this indicator is measured both in front and behind.

A mandatory step is the calculation of the density of knitting and the manufacture of a test sample. To do this, it is necessary to knit a flap 10 by 10 cm, wash it, dry and heat-treat (steam). After the sample has dried, a ruler measures the number of loops, which is included in 1 cm.

For example, in 1 cm 2.5 loops. The waist circumference is 72 cm. In order for the skirt to fit the figure on the waist, you need to dial 72 x 2.5 = 180 loops. This number of loops will fit the product clearly on the waist; the skirt will not be too wide or narrow.

Knitting description

The first 5 cm are knitted with an elastic band 2 x 2 (2 loops - front, 2 - wrong). Wrong row - as loops look.

After 5 cm, they begin to knit with an elastic band 1 x 1. They are knitted evenly, without additions or decreases to the thigh line. Then you need to check how the skirt will look on the hips. If the difference between the waist and hips is very large (more than 20 cm), then several increases are necessary. If additions are not made, then the loops can stretch too much and the product will visually give more volume to the hips. Additions must be made at a uniform distance from each other.

After the additions are made, they knit exactly about 20 cm. The product must be periodically tried on the model (if possible). After knitting 20 cm, make reductions. This is necessary so that in the knee area (if the skirt is of such length) there is no large canvas around the leg and the ‚Äúpike tail‚Äù effect is not created when the canvas is wrapped and puffed from the back of the knee.

Decreases are also made evenly.





Depending on the wishes, the skirt can be knitted with a 2 x 2, 3 x 3 elastic band or even a garter or hosiery knit.

If you want a knitted skirt, the scheme and description help to create the model that fits directly to the type of figure and occasion. You can knit a skirt both on two knitting needles by rotary knitting, and on four, in a circle, the so-called "pipe". Knitting on four needles has a great advantage - there is no seam along which the product is assembled.

Children's skirt

For little girls, a knitted skirt is a great solution both for every day and for festive events. Looks great on the child any skirt knitted. The scheme and description (the children's model differs in some aspects) will help to connect a beautiful thing.





A children's model in most cases, unlike an adult, fits exactly. There are no additions or reductions for fitting the figures, since the child does not yet have such pronounced bends of the body.

Knitting steps

To knit a skirt for a girl, you need to choose a soft comfortable yarn and determine the pattern. For an interesting, but elegant skirt, a variant in which the upper part fits evenly and the lower part is decorated with a pattern is well suited. Such a skirt will be warm, but elegant.

The skirt is knitted from top to bottom with a smooth surface. In order to be able to pass the gum, the first 10 rows are knitted with English gum 1 x 1 with smaller needles. Then they switch to the main knitting needles and knit with the main pattern until the length is sufficient.

The bottom of the product is decorated with an openwork pattern.





Pattern repeat - 15 loops on 27 rows. These parameters should be considered when knitting a pattern. If necessary, in the last row make additions.

Symbols of the scheme:

  • + - the front loop.
  • Oh - the yarn is crocheted. On the wrong side, knit with a wrong loop.
  • Triangle tilted to the right - two front loops are knitted at once for the front walls.
  • Tilted to the left triangle - two loops of the front for the rear walls are knitted at once.
  • Isosceles triangle - the loop is removed by the front, then two are knitted by the front loop, and the resulting loop is knitted through the removed one.

Due to the volume of the picture at the bottom of the skirt, a peculiar shuttlecock is obtained, which looks very interesting.

Skirt year

A more elegant version of the skirt is elongated, with a flare down. This model is called the "Godde." A festive skirt-year, knitted with needles. The scheme and description of the work are simple and easy to perform.





The peculiarity of the skirt is that it is tight to the knee and sits tight, but does not constrain movements. And below the knee, due to a sharp increase in volume, the skirt goes in flounces.

Linking this yourself is very simple. The basis of the top is chosen in a simple form - elastic or smooth. But the lower part can be made by any pattern.





The legend on the diagram is classic. The product is knitted in two ways:

1st method: to the knee, the skirt fits exactly, wedge-like increases are made below the knee. The skirt is knitted with a single piece of fabric.

2nd method: separately connected wedges are sewn to the main part.

3rd method: the skirt is assembled from trapezes, the upper base of which in total gives the girth of the waist, and the lower - the width of the year.

It turns out a warm knitted skirt with knitting needles. The scheme, description for the girl or for an adult woman is the same.

Aran Skirt

A fashionable option is with arana knitted skirt. The pattern and description of the pattern explain how aranes differ from classical braid knitting.





The peculiarity of knitting aranes is that for work, additional knitting needles are not needed. To create a pattern, a sequential knitting technique is used, during which the loop is first knitted, which should be on top, and then the loop, which will not be visible.

On the diagram, the vertical dashes depict the front loops, horizontal - wrong.

Two vertical ones with underlining, continuing over two vertical ones, are loops knitted sequentially: first, the fourth loop from the beginning of the knitting needle is knitted with the front loop, then the third. They are knitted with facial ones, while the loops are not removed from the knitting needle, then the second and first loops from the beginning of the knitting are knitted in the classical way and all the loops are removed from the knitting needle.
