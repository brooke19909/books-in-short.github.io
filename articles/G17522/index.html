Mastryukovsky lakes

From Mastryukovo to Zadelny along the Volga on 132 hectares of land, there is an amazing lake region. The lakes located in the old Volga are constantly fed from fresh springs from springs. The banks are covered with dense vegetation. Numerous water birds and animals settle in clean, warm water, sheltered by dense thickets. The water element is rich in plankton and various mollusks. It serves as a reliable food base for numerous fish.

Coastal meadows are no less rich in vegetation and wildlife. They are cut by forest pegs. Oak, pine, hazel create unique landscapes. Numerous birds reign here. From the north, Mastryukovsky Lakes is protected by Zadelnensky Bor. It is difficult to imagine the most ideal place to host numerous health resorts. On the western part of Mastryukovsky Lakes, near the village of Pribrezhny, a remarkable recreation center of the Samara Region - Strezhen - was equipped.

Recreation center

Just 54 km from Samara, on the border of the valley of lakes and Zadelnensky Bor, five well-equipped three-story buildings were built. Surrounded by mixed forests, Strezhen recreation center is a good place for families. There is access to the lakes, equipped with its own sandy beach on the Chernoyar River, a great place to relax and swim with young children. Three-meals a day, accommodation in cozy 1-4-bed rooms with all amenities are at guests' disposal. All this at relatively low prices.

Families with children (under 10 years old) and AvtoVAZ employees receive a 10% discount. For seniors - 20%. Accommodation with meals for a child under 3 years of age - only 100 rubles per day, up to 5 years - 150 rubles, according to the "Guest Card" - free of charge. There is a children's room. With the participation of animators, a large number of entertainment events are held. There is an outdoor pool.

It offers lovers of increased comfort, provided 1-2-level rooms "Suite". Check-in is possible for any number of days.

Services for vacationers

In addition to the sandy beach, the recreation center "Strezhen" offers a cozy sauna with a swimming pool.

Entertainment events are held daily, a disco is open. Equipped with a sports and gym. Tennis fans can use the outdoor court. In the presence of volleyball, basketball, football sports fields. There is a pool table, a library. For those who can not part with the computer, a special room is equipped. All services to vacationers of the recreation center "Strezhen" are provided for free. There is an opportunity to rent a bicycle, drive through the surrounding places, admire nature. The nearest lakes have all the conditions for good fishing, and the surrounding forest is rich in mushrooms. In winter you can enjoy skiing.

Directions to the vacation spot

For many years, the famous Grushinsky Festival has been held near the Mastryukovsky Lakes. Every year lovers of romance and travel songs come from all over the country and from many foreign countries. For tourists, the most convenient route began from the Zadelnaya station, located a little more than 2.5 kilometers from the Strezhen recreation center.

Samara is connected to the village of Pribrezhny by a regular bus route No. 79, which leaves from Baraboshinaya Polyana. From Tolyatti, from the collective farm market, a shuttle bus 392T runs.

The most convenient way - in your own car or taxi on the highway M-5 (Urals). Congress on the sign "Vintai / Coastal". You need to cross the village along Sovetskaya and Zvezdnaya streets. According to the index - the camp site "Strezhen", we drive up to the desired gate. Only 6.5 kilometers from the highway, and we get to the right place.

Sotskultbyt-AvtoVAZ LLC

Since 1974, the recreation center "Strezhen" is subordinate to the management of social and cultural life of VAZ. Today, the management has been transformed into Sotskultbyt-AvtoVAZ LLC. The enterprise does a lot to improve and improve the camp site in order to make the guests rest comfortable and memorable. In addition to the year-round base Strezhen, the company manages summer bases:

  • The Blue Harbor.
  • Usinskaya.
  • "Piston".

It also includes the Lada boarding house in the Temryuk district of the Krasnodar Territory, the Scarlet Sails sanatorium. There is a children's entertainment center on the territory of the sanatorium. Corporate clients receive substantial discounts, and seminar services are also provided.

The management of the company, with the support of the administration of the AvtoVAZ plant, is doing everything possible to constantly improve the rest.
