Cheap mini-hotels in the center of St. Petersburg: addresses, description, reviews

This chance is given by inexpensive mini-hotels in the center of St. Petersburg. It is worth noting that the city center and areas abound in comfortable housing. Excellent living conditions, private parking, competent staff - these are not all the pluses of institutions of this type. They belong to the three-star category of comfort and justify this by all criteria.

Inexpensive mini-hotels in the center of St. Petersburg, where a huge number of attractions, it is easy to find. But in other areas of such hotels quite a lot. You can stay at the mini-hotel alone or with young children - excellent conditions will also be created for them.

Mini-Hotel Romanov

The Romanov Mini-Hotel (St. Petersburg) with a classic interior, located in the city center, offers accommodation for 1,500 rubles per day with the possibility of renting both the room and the entire hotel for various events.



It has a convenient location and comfortable and quality accommodation. The number of rooms of the institution is 7 rooms of several categories.

All are equipped with 1- and 2-sleeping beds, wardrobes, TVs, clothes hangers, chairs, lockers for storing things, desks, bedside tables.

In addition to the rooms, the institution has a cozy living room - this is a room that is designed for relaxation. It has a TV, fireplace and sofas. In addition, judging by the reviews, there is a fully equipped spacious kitchen-dining room, which has a minibar with 12 seats, as well as 2 bathrooms with a jacuzzi.

Address: st. Civil, 1B.

Mini-hotel "Pajama"

Mini-Hotel Pajama (St. Petersburg) is located on the 2nd floor of the building. A few steps from here is the Vladimirskaya station, a little further away is the Dostoevskaya station, and you can walk to Nevsky Prospekt in just 10 minutes.



The hotel offers 5 comfortable rooms of several classes for placement: Classic, Economy, Lux and Comfort.

Since the institution opened four years ago, household appliances and furniture are new, while the interior is clean. The owners of the hotel are positioning it as a family - in fact, everything here, including the friendliness and friendliness of the staff, has a comfortable, relaxing stay. Judging by the reviews, the absolute silence in the rooms contributes to this, since all the windows look into a closed quiet courtyard.

The mini-hotel is perfect for guests who are aimed at an excursion active program. All the main attractions are located within walking distance, while distant objects can be easily reached by metro.

Address: st. Bolshaya Moskovskaya, d. 4.

Hotel "Lira"

Are you planning a tourist or business visit to the Northern capital? Want to take a trip to St. Petersburg, discover something new and are looking for a good place to relax? Interested in the sights of the legendary city on the Neva? In this case, the modern and comfortable mini-hotel "Lira" (St. Petersburg) was created for you!



This is a business hotel . It is located in the historical part of the city (5 minutes from the Obvodny Canal station). The institution is located near the Moscow station and Ligovsky prospect. The excellent location of the hotel allows you to get to the significant objects of the city as soon as possible, in addition, visit the best shopping centers, restaurants and cafes, as evidenced by numerous reviews. Hotel Lira offers 43 rooms of three categories: Standard, Junior Suite and Comfort. All are equipped with modern amenities. The rooms are suitable for both leisure and business travel. On-site parking is also under video surveillance.

Address: st. Dnipropetrovsk, 57, lit. A.
