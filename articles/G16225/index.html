We sew a skirt ourselves





Every year, delicate chiffon skirts and dresses with a maxi, mini and even asymmetrical hem remain in fashion. A special place among them is occupied by pleated versions ‚Äúon the floor‚Äù. They are not worn in order to hide figure defects, but, on the contrary, emphasize the smooth curves of a graceful female body. To sew a long chiffon skirt, you will need, of course, a fairly large amount of material. However, it will be worth it, because such a simple element of clothing is successfully combined with wide-brimmed hats and belts, and from shoes - with high sandals, shoes and even traditional sandals. The option will look very sexy if you sew a chiffon skirt with high slits in front. To do this, you need to buy the material itself and a wide elastic band for the belt, measure the half-circumference of the waist, hips and, in fact, the length of the product. We lay out the chiffon in one layer and draw two rectangles on the wrong side: the first with the sides equal to the half-circumference of the hips and the length of the skirt, and the second - twice as wide (a whole circumference of the hips).

All free edges should be processed in advance, and then sew them to the finished gum belt. So, in order to sew a chiffon skirt from pre-prepared parts, you will need to notice, and then stitch on a sewing machine, first a narrower rectangle, and then a wide one. Moreover, it is necessary to do this so that the edges of the wide cover a narrow one of about 3-5 cm from each side.
