The best film cameras: an overview of modern models

Today, most photographers use digital cameras, which were invented only about 15 years ago. Many tend to think that film is no longer popular. However, experts in the field of photography know how useful it is and is worth attention.

Film use today

Did the film find its place in the modern world? After all, there was a time when they forgot about it due to the appearance of the first smartphones, and there was a time when the film became popular again.

By the way, many of the methods that are used in modern photography originate from film cameras. The Instagram application, for example, emulates the techniques of lomography, which was created by fans of cheap Soviet LOMO cameras . Cross-processing, which also uses the experience of processing films with the wrong reagents in order to distort colors and increase contrast, is growing in popularity.

Despite the fact that the production of film is now somewhat difficult, the American corporation Kodak continues to produce a large number of them. And the British company Ilford, which produces black and white films, is thriving again, producing films, despite the inevitable bankruptcy that it was prophesied 9 years ago.

Should I buy such equipment?

Almost none of the novice photographers are aware of the importance of using film cameras. They give preference to digital, not even assuming that they can learn a lot by using film.

However, some photographers still realized that this could benefit them. One of such experts says that only after using a film camera he was able to understand the meaning of concepts such as aperture, exposure. He also claims that the time that the master spends on the picture is significantly reduced.

Which camera to buy?

So, you are ready to buy a film camera. Before that, having decided on the type and type of film you need, you do not know which company is suitable. Fortunately, there are many companies that are engaged not only in the production of digital cameras, but also film ones. Of course, the new film cameras are better.

It must be remembered that when choosing a camera, it is important to consider the expansion, range, control of such a model, and so on. For starters, you can try the film from Lomo. They are easy to use, and also have features that make it easy for beginners to quickly figure out the process.

And for those who have been taking photography for quite some time and want to make a choice in favor of more professional cameras, Canon film cameras are more suitable. They are quite simple with regard to use, unlike many old models, because they are made taking into account today's technologies. Such models belong to modern film cameras.

When buying, be sure to check the condition of the shutter shutters and the camera at all shutter speeds. If the camera has a built-in exposure meter, you need to check it at least visually. If the camera does not have a light meter, then this will not cause you big problems - everyone has a smartphone on which you can install the application.

A photographic enlarger and a photo tank, other equipment that you need, can be bought for about 1500-2000 rubles. You can make a purchase on the sites of various Internet sites.

As a result, for 2-3 thousand rubles you can buy a camera, shoot frames, develop it and print your first film. The main thing is that you will get an unforgettable pleasure.
