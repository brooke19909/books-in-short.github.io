DIY bouquet for men: step-by-step instructions

For a holiday, men want to give some kind of original gift, but interesting ideas do not always come to mind. When you are preparing a surprise for a loved one, you basically know that he loves, what he is interested in, what he lacks for complete happiness. But to come up with an inexpensive, but cool surprise for a co-worker or comrade, for example, on February 23 or some kind of professional day, is more difficult.

The omniscient Internet comes to the rescue. Only there you can find an original bouquet for a man. Do it yourself with your own hands. The main thing is to have an idea and buy the necessary elements. The article presents several interesting options for such products with step-by-step instructions for execution.

Hot drinks

For comrades who love funny and noisy parties, you can prepare such an attractive bouquet for the male half. To make it, you need to have a foundation. You can advise you to buy a stand in the flower shop for tying long stems. It consists of three plastic sticks, expanding upwards, with stiffening ribs, suitable for strengthening the details of a bouquet for a man. With your own hands to make a gift on such a basis is not difficult.





You can buy three, or you can have more of the same or different bottles. Bows tightly tie them to the base, adding festive elements. It can be cocktail stars, hearts or other figures. If the company is very close friends, then you can plant salted cucumbers on sticks. Then all this beauty is wrapped from below with beautiful bright paper and a bow is attached. Thus, an original bouquet for a man assembled in an original composition with his own hands will be ready.

For sweet tooth

For such a surprise you need to buy a whole package of different sweets. Choose wafer bars, chocolates, candy packaging , chewing gums. The main condition for creating a beautiful bouquet for a man made with his own hands is that all the details of the future work of art should be elongated and fit well into the general ensemble.





It is recommended to fasten the elements with a simple clerical transparent tape. Just need to decompose them correctly, the package should be in the shape of a cone. To keep the structure, a strip of cardboard or a small wooden stick is inserted in the center. It remains only to pack everything together in a decent wrapper.

Vitamin kit

DIY sausage bouquet for a man

The photo below shows that a surprise can have countless options. Here you can realize any creative ideas. Bouquet of socks

For a man with his own hands, you can make a more necessary gift for February 23. It can be men's underpants or socks. Such jerseys are always needed, as they are often used and torn. You can, of course, just give them a pack in a bag, but it will be much more original to pack in the form of a bouquet.





We will consider further how to make such a bouquet for a man with his own hands step by step.

detailed instructions

To begin with, the right size socks are selected. A set of multi-colored and bright products will look beautiful. Of course, we must take into account the tastes of men, because not everyone wears such colorful socks. Then you need to make or buy sticks. They must be smooth so as not to tear the product.

The next step around the stick is to roll the sock with a roll, starting from the top point, gradually lowering the turns below. The edge bends down a bit like a rose petal. The last turn must be attached with a pin, tightly fixed.

When all the "flowers" are made, you need to connect them together. To do this, you can tie the sticks at the bottom with a rope and pack them in colorful wrapping paper, bandaging them with a bow. So, a bouquet of socks for a man, made with his own hands made as a gift, is ready!

The most delicious packages

For men, many products can be beautifully decorated as gifts. Such a delicious package will appeal to absolutely anyone. It contains all the necessary products for the festive table. These are various sausages, smoked cheeses, portioned suluguni, hot red peppers, onions, spices.





All products are laid out in such a way that large parts of the structure are located behind, and smaller ones are in front. Pieces of cheese are put on a stick, several in a row. For brightness and similarity with this bouquet add a couple of sprigs of rosemary or other available at hand greens. It can be long branches of celery or green onions.

Then the bouquet is connected below. It remains only to make a gift wrap. For this, any contrast paper and ribbon are suitable.

Making gifts to your friends and family is always a pleasure. And when the surprise is also creatively designed, it looks even better. Please your men!
