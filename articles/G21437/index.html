The main criteria for evaluating collection books

The rarest and most expensive books are selected according to the following criteria.

  1. Books of Ivan Fedorov or his students. There are not many editions of the first printer in the world, but because of their sad state, the price of some works does not rise above 300 thousand rubles.
  2. Antique books published in the 18-19th centuries, illustrations for which were written by famous artists.
  3. Not completely destroyed books. Writers are creative people and therefore easily injured. For various reasons, including negative reviews from critics, they bought up almost the entire circulation and destroyed their own works. Books that survived this frenzy are valuable collector's books.
  4. The first books of future luminaries of literature. Drafts of writers are especially valuable. Of course, finding such ones requires a handwriting examination.
  5. Intravital editions of great writers, especially with their autographs.

The most expensive books, as a rule, are the rarest.
