Crocheted ruffles: schemes, photos, description

Multilayer volume ruffles are often used to decorate a variety of types of clothing and textile interior items. They can be seen on blouses, skirts, dresses, cardigans, pillows and bedspreads.

For crochet knitters, this decor is of particular interest. Crochet ruffles, the patterns of which can be easily found in the specialized literature, are very easy to knit, although for a long time.

Varieties of ruffle

Based on the appearance and patterns used in knitting, we can distinguish several main types of ruffles:

  • short solid;
  • simple with smooth edges;
  • fishnet.

Summarizing, we can say that ruffles, frills and shuttlecocks have a common principle of knitting. The difference lies in the number of consecutive elements. Ruffles are a few frills or flounces located nearby. The essence of their manufacture is that the length of the last row of the ruffle sheet should be greater than the length of the first.

The value of materials and tools

The manufacture of products, including ruffles, is not very difficult, it is enough to master the initial crochet. The ruffles (schemes for their implementation can be literally any) are made of soft, relatively thin yarn. The optimal thickness of the thread for knitting warm products is from 400 m / 100 grams to 600 m / 100 grams. When knitting summer cotton products, you can apply yarn even thinner - about 700 m / 100 grams.



The thinner the yarn, the lighter the fabric. For knitting ruffles, the formation of beautiful folds is very important, therefore, it is better to choose the average density of knitting. Experienced craftswomen recommend this technique for beginners. Large hook prevents tightening the canvas.

We crochet short ruffles: schemes, description, application

The photo shows a bag with a decor in the form of ruffles.



Their manufacture can be safely called the simplest. Such short crochet ruffles, the schemes of which will be presented below, are imposed on the finished canvas.

The fillet grid is best suited as the basis. Her knitting consists in alternating columns with a crochet (CCH) and two air loops (VP). Thus, uniform square cells are obtained. If you plan to knit a more openwork fabric, you can increase the number of yarn in the columns and, accordingly, the number of VP between them.

The diagram shows ruffles consisting of two rows. You can focus on one if the thread of sufficient thickness and the quilling looks complete, or add another third and fourth rows. When increasing the height of the ruffle, it should be remembered that each next row should contain at least 1.5 times more loops than the previous one. Optimal - 2 times.





Knitting openwork ruffles

For the manufacture of lace ruffles, a special scheme should be used. An even openwork pattern will not work, since the ruffle sheet should expand. You can find a ready-made pattern or adapt a fragment of a circular knitted napkin. Quite often, napkins are decorated around the edge with a lace border of several rows. This section should be used. The expansion of the canvas is already in the scheme and there is no need to puzzle over the invention of the bicycle.

Most often, crochet ruffles (patterns are used both tight and openwork) are knitted to decorate skirts.

This item is suitable for children and adult products. Of course, the specifics of each model have its own, but there is a common feature: the method of connecting the canvas of the skirt with ruffles.

The cloth of the skirt is knitted with a sirloin net and then the ruffles are crocheted (it is better to take openwork patterns for the skirt) at an equal distance from each other. It should be ensured that the last rows of each ruffle overlap the first rows of the next.

The knitting obtained by this method is voluminous, at the same time light, mobile and airy.
