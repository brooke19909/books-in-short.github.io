Inexpensive rest on Baikal: tours, recreation facilities and the private sector

The majestic Siberian nature has always attracted the attention of travelers. Mountain rivers and springs filled with clean and cool water are a haven for a tired traveler. Even those who like to relax in the resorts of Thailand and the Maldives, with enthusiasm perceive the beauty and diversity of Siberian wildlife landscapes. In this region, one of the most breathtaking places to relax and enjoy the beauties of the world is Lake Baikal. Rest in this place is incomparable pleasure. The rich nature and developed infrastructure, wild, untouched corners and recreation centers famous among tourists are closely intertwined here. Many travelers travel a great distance in order to at least for a moment see the world's largest freshwater lake.





"Savage" to Siberia

Of course, many are primarily interested in inexpensive holidays on Lake Baikal. And this is no coincidence: after all, the worldwide popularity of this place played a role in the cost of boarding service. Rest "savage" is less developed here than on other lakes in the country. The reason for this is the abundance of recreation centers and resorts. As a rule, residents of nearby cities prefer to relax away from civilization and soft beds. However, among travelers who have come a long way to the majestic freshwater lake, there are those who prefer relaxation in tents. This, as you know, is the most inexpensive holiday on Lake Baikal. For this type of pastime, it is better to choose a quiet place where there is no huge concentration of tourists. This can not be attributed to the Baikal surf and Kultushnaya. These "islands" of Siberian nature are very crowded. Unfortunately, there are very few territories left where inexpensive rest in Lake Baikal in the "savage" style is still possible. Almost all the land near the reservoir is leased. Therefore, the second option is a boarding holiday.





Buying a ticket

Today around the lake there is a huge variety of hotels, hotels and resorts. Some establishments offer tours of tourists, including accommodation, meals, excursions and outdoor activities. For example, if you want to buy a ticket, coupled with various entertainment, then you may come up with an option called "I've never seen such a miracle." This tour takes nine days in duration. This includes hotel accommodation, meals, a sightseeing tour of Irkutsk, visiting museums and a tourist voyage. During the latter, you will be able to see the extraordinary beauty of the fortresses of the Pribaikalsky ridge, the island of Olkhon, the Tazheran steppe, Shebetsky, Khul, Sarai and Togai bays and much, much more. Overnight in tents, evenings by bonfires, alluring sky-high stars - a mass of unforgettable impressions awaits connoisseurs of Siberian nature. The estimated cost of such a tour is 19,000 rubles. However, this is far from the most inexpensive vacation on Lake Baikal.





Relax on the island

There are simpler options. So, for example, the recreation center "Solar". This establishment is located on the largest island of the freshwater lake - Olkhon. Here, the services of travelers are offered not only accommodation, but also a transfer, and packages of excursions. As for any other place, seasonality is also a feature that characterizes relaxation on Lake Baikal. Prices for accommodation from July 1 to the end of August in almost every institution rise. So, this season the Solnechnaya base provides travelers with the opportunity to relax in a cozy economy-class room for 1200 rubles per person. If you want to live in a house - the cost increases by two hundred and fifty monetary units. At the same time, three meals a day are included in the price.

Until the first of July and after the twentieth of August, similar apartments will cost the traveler 950 and 1200 rubles. There are private rooms with dry closets. This place is one of the most inexpensive vacation options on the island.





Private sector

Some guest houses and tourist camps allow you to cook in shared kitchens. Then the cost of living per day decreases. If you want to spend your holidays on hikes and excursions, you should not book a set lunch. You can choose: cook on your own, visit cafeterias or order food at your chosen hotel. Any of the possibilities is provided by the guest complex "Rainbow", located in the village of Maksimikha. Despite the opinion that it is quite expensive to relax in this area, here you can find a very acceptable option, which can be a great alternative to overseas resorts. Accommodation in this village can give you the best vacation on Lake Baikal. Private sector or commercial pensions - any of the institutions offers a wide range of services to tourists. Moreover, the range of services varies depending on the preferences of customers and their material capabilities.





Sleeps and Shared Kitchen

So, an institution called "Rainbow" offers travelers a separate house with three entrances, the total capacity of which is twelve people, and a complex for sixteen people. Both that and another room can be removed completely. The house has three rooms, two of which are designed for three people, and the last - for six. The cost of living per person is seven hundred rubles. In the guest complex - nine hundred monetary units per day. This does not include meals. You can cook your own meals on stoves installed in the guest house or order in a package.

There are also sleeping places: on the top floor of the guest complex there are small rooms with single and double beds. The cost per person is 400 rubles.
