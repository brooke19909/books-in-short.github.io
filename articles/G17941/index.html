Where is the best place to relax in Vietnam? Beach and sightseeing tours

Vietnam is a rather mysterious country. Even if you are not coming here for the first time, you can discover something new here. Warm weather all year round creates the best conditions for relaxation. The gentle sun, which simply warms, but does not burn, white sand, islands of bizarre shapes, high mountains, clean, humid air - this is not a complete list of what you can see here. Only one thing remains to be decided: where is it better to relax in Vietnam?



All interesting places are difficult to list, but there are several cities that can help you get acquainted with the culture of the country and have a great time.

Saigon

Saigon, or Ho Chi Minh City, is a modern metropolis in which many skyscrapers are built. But at the same time there are a lot of architectural monuments. For tourists of particular interest is the Reunification Palace with a luxurious adjoining park. Indoors, you can watch various exhibitions and a film about the past of this building. History buffs should visit the War Crimes Museum. It contains items that confirm the terrible actions of Americans against the people of Vietnam. You can find out about different periods of the country's life in the Museum of Vietnamese History. Other attractions worth noting are the House of Culture, Ben Tan Market and Ku Chi Tunnel Network.

Danang

Where is it better to relax in Vietnam to get to know its culture more closely? Of course, in Da Nang! The city has a rich collection of mysterious and ancient boorish culture items. There are also excellent beaches and the largest water park in the country.



Hue

Where can I relax in Vietnam to touch eternity? For many centuries this place was considered the center of political and spiritual life. To this day, many historical monuments have been preserved in Hue. The most impressive are the palaces and tombs of the emperors of the Nguyen dynasty.

Hanoi

Where is the best place to relax in Vietnam? In the capital of Vietnam - Hanoi - you can admire the amazing symbiosis of Western style and oriental culture. The most popular places in the metropolis are: Lake of the Returned Sword, where, according to legend, a huge turtle lives; Temple of Literature built in 1070; Mausoleum of Ho Chi Minh, in which lies the great leader of Vietnam; Tower of Hanoi with its unique architecture. In Hanoi, you can even see a monument to Lenin. By the way, his name, written in Vietnamese style, looks like Le-Nin.

Where is it better to relax in Vietnam for beach lovers?

The most famous resort in the southern part of the country is Nha Trang. Its beaches are dotted with white sand. There are coral reefs, desert bays. Nha Trang is perfect for families with children.

Not far from Ho Chi Minh City is another cozy place - Phan Thiet. It is an ideal resort for couples and surf lovers.

Calm and peaceful pastime can offer the island of Phu Quoc. Sandy beaches, coconut palms, tropical forests await tourists all year round.



It is worth noting that the local population is friendly with tourists. How much does it cost to relax in Vietnam? On average, the cost of a voucher for 11 days is a little more than $ 1,000. This figure is quite approximate, it all depends on the season, hotel and many other factors.
