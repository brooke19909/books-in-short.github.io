The best hotels of Varadero. 2nd place: Barcelo Solymar

Hotel Barcelo Solymar consists of one four-story building and a complex of several two-story cottages. Among the best hotels in Cuba in Varadero is the four-star Gran Caribe Puntarena playa Caleta hotel. Here you will find: Caribbean exotic, water sports, discos, entertainment at any time of the day. The hotel is a complex of two nine-story buildings with 510 rooms. The city beach is within walking distance, hotel guests enjoy free sunbeds and umbrellas.
