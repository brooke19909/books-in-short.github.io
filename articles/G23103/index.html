Rest in Sukhumi: reviews of tourists, photos

Sukhumi is the capital of sunny Abkhazia. It attracts tourists from all over the post-Soviet space. Today we want to tell you about the rest in Sukhumi. Reviews call these places the best on the entire Black Sea coast. Beautiful beaches and beautiful surroundings, a huge number of attractions, all this creates ideal conditions for relaxation and entertainment.

Not only the natural beauty of this region deserves special attention, but also the developed infrastructure. Therefore, especially often young companies come here, sociable young people, however, business guests do not forget these hospitable lands.

How to get there

This is the first question that a tourist planning a vacation in Sukhumi should ask. Reviews say that the city is equally distant from the Russian and Georgian borders, and the distance from both of them is only 100 km. Sukhumi is located on the shores of the bay of the same name in the Black Sea. Part of the city is built up by the private sector, this is mainly Cape Red Lighthouse. And the whole city lies in a beautiful valley surrounded by mountain ranges.

To get here from Russia, you must initially get to Adler. From there, you can already get a commuter train to the Abkhaz capital. There is also a direct train that goes to Abkhazia from Moscow. From a large number of cities in Russia there are intercity buses.

Excursion holidays in Sukhumi (reviews of tourists)

If you like to walk and learn a lot every day, then come to Abkhazia. Here, every corner of the country is a magical miracle of nature, or a creation of man. Waterfall and rocks, rivers and picturesque bays, stunning architectural monuments, wonderful memories will remain for life.

The most interesting is, of course, an excursion vacation in Sukhumi. Reviews of tourists emphasize that staying here, you can see the whole country. The presence of the airport allows using a helicopter to fly around everything, and even to visit the inaccessible mountain villages of Pskhu. Here people have been hunting and fishing for thousands of years, and the mountain honey collected in these parts is considered the best.

The cost of the tour is not the lowest, about 20,000 rubles, but a group of about 20 people is going to. But it's definitely worth it. Flying over picturesque gorges, stunning snowy peaks will definitely not leave anyone indifferent.



Most popular tours

If you are fascinated by the beauty of Switzerland, then you will also like a holiday in Sukhumi. Responses of vacationers with admiration describe a trip to Lake Ritsa. Magnificent and inaccessible, it is surrounded on all sides by high mountain ranges. This is an exciting day trip that runs along the gorge of the Bzyb River. At the same time, you can visit the Bzyb fortress, the watchtower of Hasan Abaa, Getsky waterfall, as well as the blue lake.

Lake Ritsa itself is a real gem of these places. It is striking in its beauty and spaciousness. You can take a walk along its shores, but if you want to see it in its entirety, you better agree to a bus trip. The cost of such an excursion is only 800 rubles. There is a travel option around the lake in Oise. Then you can visit the most inaccessible places, alpine meadows and Lake Mzu. With an open car, it's easier to explore all the surroundings.

Cultural rest

Not everyone wants to spend time in the mountains, on glaciers and admire the charms of the wild. But for everyone there is certainly a vacation in Sukhumi 2016. Reviews colorfully paint city sightseeing tours, in which you can take a walk along the Mahadzhirov Embankment and Glory Park. The Theater Square, decorated with fountains , is very beautiful.

If you want to get acquainted with the cultural heritage, then be sure to visit the Annunciation Cathedral, which is the main attraction of this region. Typically, a tour involves a trip to the Lighthouse and the Great Abkhaz Wall and the ruins of Bagrat Castle. The Abkhaz State Museum will give a lot of information about the history and culture of the region. And tired of the heat, you can stroll through the Botanical Garden.



Sun and sea

That beach entertainment is a priority for most tourists, and if you want to enjoy the sea and the sun forever, then the ideal choice for you is a vacation in Sukhumi 2016. Reviews emphasize the special, uniform temperature. Moderate humidity and a lot of sun, complete absence of harsh winds, only occasionally a gentle breeze blows from the sea. The climate is determined by the location of Sukhumi in the center of the subtropical zone of the Caucasus.

Beach weekend

Where is the best place to plan your vacation in Sukhumi in 2016? Reviews tell us that the whole bay of the Black Sea coast is great for spending time on the beach. However, the most popular are comfortable places specially designated for swimming. This is primarily the Central Beach. Here, breakwaters overlooking the sea are installed, there is a rental of catamarans and a huge number of water attractions.

Agudzersky beach is also crowded, it is equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas, there are changing cabins and toilets, which is a pleasant element of civilization. Here you understand how hospitable Sukhumi can be. Holidays with children (reviews call this the best option for spending summer vacations) on local golden beaches - this is ideal for restoring strength and health for the whole year ahead. If your children are still small, then the best option for you is the sandy Sinop beach, where the entrance to the sea is very gentle.

There are times when you want to be on the beach in solitude and unity with nature. Then go to the pebble, "Doggy" beach, located in the city center, but not too crowded.



Leisure

Today, more and more people are planning their vacation literally in minutes, trying to get the most out of it, and not just lie on the beach. Hundreds of opportunities for self-realization will provide you with a vacation in Sukhumi 2016. Photos, reviews of tourists, as well as their numerous reports with illustrations show how diverse it can be.

Tennis tables and sports fields are places to have fun in the company of friends. If your passion is fishing or hunting, then you will definitely like it here. Trips to mountain rivers are regularly held, and mountain hunting is also organized.

For lovers of extreme relaxation there is a descent along mountain rivers or rafting. The difficulty of the slopes can be different, so everyone will like it. Horseback riding is a favorite sport of locals. You will be happy to learn all the tricks of this art. And then you can go on horseback excursions. There are plenty of amazing routes for beginners and experienced riders.

For diving enthusiasts there is simply a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the city bay. At the bottom lie the ruins of an ancient city. There are also diving spots for beginners with instructors and professionals.



What month to plan a vacation

In fact, every season is beautiful here in its own way, so that whenever you get a vacation, you are guaranteed a wonderful experience. However, most often the choice of tourists is summer and autumn. An ideal vacation in Sukhumi is June. Reviews say that the weather is already warm, but not very hot.

If the first week of this month can be rainy, then dry and sunny days are set. By the end of the month, pleasant warmth will turn into real heat, so if you do not tolerate the heat, it is better not to plan the period from June 20 to August 20.

After June 15, the water warms up to 22 degrees. Thus, it is better to devote the beginning of the month to sightseeing, but from the middle of June it is time to master a beach holiday in Sukhumi. Reviews of vacationers in 2015 fully confirm the information about weather conditions, from this we can conclude about the stability of this climatic zone. So, planning a vacation will be easy and simple.



At sea in the fall

If you decide to go on vacation at this golden time of the year, then nature has prepared you a lot of pleasant surprises. How good is your holiday in Sukhumi in October? Reviews say that at this time you can divide your vacation into two parts. The first week of this autumn month is very warm, the velvet season continues, and you can lie on the beach all day. The second half of the month is less favorable for swimming, but the time comes for sightseeing, sightseeing tours, for which it was too hot in the high season.

At the end of October it becomes cool in the afternoon, the sea is rapidly cooling. However, this month, unlike the moody September, is practically never rainy. This is a time for connoisseurs of half-empty streets and beaches, as well as an abundance of fresh fruit and the lack of strong heat during the day.



Wellness rest

Most often tourists come here who want to just have a good time. However, Sukhumi (Abkhazia) is valued not only for its beaches. Rest, reviews of which are always only positive, can restore health and relieve chronic ailments if you spend your vacation in one of the local sanatoriums.

Prevention and swimming pools and a full course of treatment and prophylactic procedures are becoming increasingly popular among Russians. Favorite services are oil and herbal inhalations, as well as mud and radon baths.

One of the best is considered the sanatorium "Sukhum". It is an ideal place for treatment and relaxation. A health resort is located on the seashore in the central part of the city of Sukhumi. The subtropical park around the sanatorium creates a wonderful impression. The main direction of the sanatorium is the treatment of diseases of the cardiovascular system, musculoskeletal system, digestive organs and nervous system. Every year hundreds of satisfied patients leave here, who have experienced an improvement. Judging by the reviews, here you will find excellent service, comfortable rooms, five meals a day, and qualified staff will do everything possible to improve your condition.



Accommodation in Sukhumi

A huge number of resorts, hotels, hotels and pensions gives you the opportunity to choose. There is a camping option where you can put up a tent or just spend the night in a car. However, not everyone is adapted for such an uncomfortable vacation. Therefore, there is a third option, it is comfortable, homely conditions, at a more affordable price than in a hotel. This is the most popular holiday in Sukhumi. The private sector, reviews of which in most cases is very good, offers dozens of options for a comfortable stay.

Of course, a lot depends on the owner, so choose based on the reviews. Having picked up suitable conditions for yourself, book your seats immediately next summer, then you will be sure that your vacation will not be spoiled for sure. The cost is different, it starts from 150 rubles per day per person. Decent options can be found for about 300-500 rubles per person per day. To relax on the Black Sea coast in a comfortable house, with a homemade breakfast, this is not so much.
Rest here is most profitable from early May to mid-June, or in October. In the middle of summer it is very hot, and prices are kept the highest. It's rainy in September, so plan your vacation carefully.

Average rates for accommodation in boarding houses

Pension "Aitar" is located almost in the center of the city. The cost of living starts from 1200 rubles per person per day. Three meals a day are included in the price.

Atrium Victoria Hotel offers accommodation options for 1800 rubles per day, breakfast is included. The most budget option is the Kyasur boarding house. A family-run hotel, it has low prices, the price of a room starts from 950 per day. Power is not included.

A more budgetary option would be living in the private sector, or just in a tent on the seashore. This type of recreation is also very popular, camping sites are almost never empty.

What to bring to relatives

Surely your loved ones will ask you to buy souvenirs for them. And, as usual, they remember this at the end of their vacation and begin to rush around the city in search of something unusual. In fact, you can just before leaving to buy a basket of fruits and present it at home. But if you go far, you have to come up with a more practical option.

Judging by the reviews of tourists, there will be no problems with this. The whole city is full of tents, souvenir shops and departments. Clay and bamboo souvenirs, wicker vines and boxwood are very popular among tourists. And how do you like beautiful bottles in the form of sabers or daggers. They can be filled with wine, brandy or chacha.

For colleagues at work, you can buy souvenirs from seashells and colored glass, panama, t-shirts and magnets. Usually their cost does not exceed 30 rubles. By the way, in every house there will be honey and spicy adjika, tea and medicinal herbs, suluguni cheese, as well as local spices, which are sold in a huge assortment.
