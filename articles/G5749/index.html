The best smartphones with moisture protection: an overview of popular models, reviews

Contact with water is perhaps the most common reason for the failure of a mobile gadget. Naturally, many users would like to minimize damage or avoid such unpleasant situations at all.

Many popular brands went to meet consumers and began to produce smartphones with moisture protection. Of course, they cost much more than ordinary analogues, but you have to choose the lesser of two evils. But here there are some nuances.

Features of waterproof smartphones

The level, like the protection class, is different. This indicator is usually denoted by the letters IP. For example, IP53 class is a protection against dust and falling splashes. While IP65 is a dustproof gadget that can easily survive a direct jet. The best class to date is IP68. Such a gadget can be safely immersed to a depth of 1 meter for half an hour.

We will consider devices starting from IP65. They meet the real concept of "waterproof". All phones below this level, alas, will survive only splashes. So do not flatter yourself and believe in advertising. For example, a well-known Chinese brand announced the latest Huawei smartphones with IP53 moisture protection. But in fact, this level will protect your gadget from dust and more. A small spray to mobile phones is not a hindrance.

Next, consider the best smartphones with moisture protection. Denote the remarkable characteristics of the devices, as well as the opinions of users regarding each model.

Top smartphones with moisture protection:

  1. Samsung Galaxy S8.
  2. iPhone X.
  3. HTC U11.
  4. Sony Xperia XZ1 Compact.
  5. Google Pixel 2.
