Heaven Beach Resort & Spa. Holidays in Turkey, Side - 5 star hotels

Holidays in Turkey are a great opportunity to visit the warm sea and experience at the same time all the charms of oriental hospitality and high-class European services. The interest in the sea, sun and beaches of this country among our compatriots is growing every year. In summer you can sunbathe and swim here, they are fed well and entertained with dignity. Therefore, people who want to abandon everyday life, seek precisely in Turkey. For this, the concept of "all inclusive" was invented (sometimes with the addition of "ultra").

The most popular coast among tourists is, of course, Antalya. There are so many great places to relax that even your eyes run wide. Kemer and Alanya appeal to lovers of beautiful nature and antiquities. Antalya - for youth and parties. But a resort like Side is good for almost everyone. In this article we will look at one of the new conceptual hotels - Heaven Beach Resort & Spa Side, on the example of which we will show how good the hotels of this particular area are.



Holidays in Side

The best visiting card of this resort is beaches and historical sites. Although Turkey is a country where you can find the monuments of many ancient civilizations, it is in Side that there are probably more per square kilometer than in any other area. The ancient city turned into an open air museum. Here you can see ancient temples, aqueducts, squares, streets, baths. Europeans are very fond of spending their holidays at this resort, so the level of service is very high.

Near the city there is a beautiful waterfall Managvat, where excursions are organized. It is interesting in that the river rushes right into the depths of the sea. What else needs to be done here to make you remember your vacation in Side? Reviews of tourists say that it is worth not only to see all the ancient ruins and natural attractions, but also try the local trout. Haven Beach Resort - A Brief Description

According to tourists, this is a completely new "five", which opened to guests only in the spring of last year. The hotel is located seventy kilometers from the Antalya airport. This is the Kyzylagach (Side) district. If you have the transfer turned on, then you will reach the hotel in about an hour and a half. Complex Heaven Beach Resort & Spa - suburban, resort, beach, located directly on the sea. In addition, this hotel specializes in spa services. A private hammam and fitness center are some of the best in the area. Free wi-fi catches perfectly throughout the territory.

Territory

Heaven Beach Resort & Spa 5 is a whole mini-town. There is a shopping center, a beauty salon, car rental, and an Internet cafe. The five-story building itself is majestic, white, with a stunning gilded staircase. Between the floors are modern, large and comfortable panoramic elevators. A gym is open, and a salt room is available for healing. In general, the territory is not very large, but well-groomed. Wooden paths are laid along the lawns. In addition, guests of "Haven" can visit the neighboring hotel "Lira", walk there in the park, go to discos and shows, sunbathe by the pool.



Big new numbers

The hotel is ready to receive guests about two hundred rooms. When checking into the hotel you will be offered champagne and oriental sweets. The rooms at Heaven Beach Resort & Spa are simply luxurious. All rooms have a minibar. Replenish it every day, without reminders and problems. There is beer, mineral water, cola, sprite.

The location of the rooms is very thoughtful. The rooms are in perfect cleanliness. The beds are large, everywhere mirrors the entire wall. There is a kettle, dishes, board games (checkers), bathrobes, slippers. All appliances and furniture are top notch and the newest. The shower room is comfortable, roomy. Air conditioners work well, softly and silently. TV - mirror panel with built-in plasma (one hundred channels).

There are family rooms with a lockable door to the parent bedroom and beds for children in niches. Views of the sea or mountains. Beach towels bring directly to the rooms.



Food for the "five plus"

Heaven Beach Resort & Spa has an ultra all inclusive system, but three more restaurants serve ala karta. There you can try the best dishes of Turkey, the Mediterranean and Asia. These restaurants have a very beautiful, professional serving. There are six bars in the hotel. The menu is varied, and the food is very tasty. There are excellent gozleme baked near the beach bar. Champagne is served periodically at the entrance to the restaurant. Playing live music.

On the beach you can eat delicious grilled fish. The bars are amazing snacks, drinks, cocktails and fruits, nuts. Ice cream and sweets - like in paradise. The hotel has its own mini-pastry shop. Turkish coffee is amazing. Watermelons and melons are sweet, plums are the size of apples.



Entertainment and animation

Heaven Beach Resort & Spa offers animation all day and evening. A variety of shows, competitions, dances, step aerobics, water gymnastics - you will not be bored. And once every two weeks, a gala party is held. All this happens on the site by the sea.

For children there is a club, a playground, and special animators, and a mini-disco. Many educational toys for kids.

A hammam, sauna, jacuzzi and fitness center are also at guests' disposal. Massages at an additional cost. They are very diverse. You can try a qualified Thai massage, with stones, chitzu, and others. Twenty minutes of spa services are provided for free, and then they give a certificate for a discount. On Thursdays and Saturdays, from midnight to two in the morning, animators take guests to discos in the city.





Sea vacation

The beach at Heaven Beach Resort & Spa has its own beach, with many sun loungers, deck chairs and umbrellas. There are pebbles at the entrance, and sand on the shore. Active holidaymakers can go in for water sports. On the beach there are various offers of catamaran and canoe surfing. You can play volleyball, or you can fly by parachute.

Very nice scourge. When you come to the beach, he always wipes the sunbeds so that there is no dust and sand. It is very clean here. Free sunbeds are always there. The sea is clean and warm, although there are waves. The hotel itself has several pools - deep and originally designed.
