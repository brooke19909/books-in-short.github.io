How to make a bell out of paper with your own hands? Manufacturing options

Often an attribute of a New Year's holiday is a bell. In the form of such an object they make Christmas tree decorations, decorate a spruce wreath that is hung on the door, and the bells decorating the lamp look beautiful. How to make a bell out of paper with your own hands? This question worries many masters. In fact, there are quite a few options.

As a Christmas tree toy, you can cut out the bell shape from cardboard, glue it with colored paper, sprinkle the smeared contour with a golden golden rain and hang it on a branch on a rope. This is the easiest option that a preschooler can do.

If the child is already 4-5 years old, then you can offer him the option of volumetric crafts. Before you make a bell out of paper with your own hands, you need to draw a template. Then circle several contours on colored paper. You can use paper of the same color, but you can make different. Then each bell cut out according to the pattern needs to be bent in half and glued the halves together. Get a voluminous Christmas tree toy. It remains only to pierce the hole on top and tie the rope.

Craft from disposable plates

It is very easy to make New Year's calls from paper plates. It is desirable that their edges were curly, then the finished toy will look more spectacular. Each plate needs to be rolled up with a cone and smooth the edges with a ruler. You can put a metal round bell tied to a beautiful rope in the middle for beauty. Then the bells hung on the door or on the Christmas tree will be able to make beautiful melodic sounds.





You can paint the plates in any color, but preferably golden, yellow or silver. If you cover the product with sparkles, then in the light of a garland the bells will beautifully shimmer.

To prevent the ends of the plates from diverging, you can fasten the edges with a stapler before making a bell of paper with your own hands (see photo above), since the glue may not hold tight edges. If you have an adhesive gun, then you can use it.

Ribbed bells

To make such ribbed bells from cardboard, you will have to draw a template and cut 5-6 separate elements on it, which then need to be glued together.

Here is what the pattern is:

  • We draw an isosceles triangle.
  • From the corners of the base we lower down parallel two lines of any length, depending on the size of the bell, and connect them together. It turns out a rectangle.
  • It remains to draw a trapezoid from below. This will be the bottom bell extension.




So that the parts can be glued, an additional 0.5 cm of paper must be left on each side, on which glue will be applied. Then all the wedges are put together. After complete drying, it remains only to paint the product beautifully and hang it on the Christmas tree.

Bouquet of bells

Now you know how to make a bell from paper with your own hands. A flower of the same shape is also easy to make. It is enough to cut a rectangle from tissue paper and cut one edge into semicircles.

Then you just need to roll the paper. The bottom of the cylinder adheres to a wire wrapped in green paper. After making 3-5 flowers, you can put a bouquet of bells in a vase.
