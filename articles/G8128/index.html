Bosch Classixx 5: description, recommendations, photos

The Bosch Classixx 5 washer is a appliance that is designed for home use only. She copes with textiles and wool, which are often washed by hand. During operation, cold water, household detergents, as well as special conditioners, bleaches, etc. can be used.

In order to protect the washing machine from the bad effects of chemicals, it is necessary to comply with the dosage that contains the instructions.

All Bosch Classixx 5s are allowed to use, except for children under 8 years of age, persons with persistent mental illness, impaired mental abilities and physical functions. For these people, the use of technology is allowed only in the presence of the guardians responsible for their lives.

Pets should be controlled to avoid personal injury due to the operation of the washing machine. All documentation is recommended to be stored in a specially designated place and in no case should be thrown away.



Specifications

The dimensions of the washing machine are 60x85x40 cm. It has not been created as an additional element for countertops or other interior options, therefore it is freestanding. The maximum weight of the loaded laundry should not exceed 5 kg. The washing efficiency is A. This class is one of the highest, which guarantees maximum results even with persistent old stains. The download has a frontal type.

Bosch Classixx 5 (the manual has more detailed specifications) is equipped with 5 temperature options. Among the washing options can be noted a separate erasure of cotton, wool, jeans. Combined mode is allowed. Total options 11. For one wash, the machine uses 45 liters of water.

There is a spin function. The program also has a button that cancels this process. In addition, a leakage protection system is connected.



How to save by washing in Bosch Classixx 5?

It should be remembered that the maximum weight of the loaded laundry is 5 kg. In order to use less water (and, accordingly, pay a small amount of money for it), you need to load for one approach to the full mass. If the laundry has slight impurities, then do not connect the prewash.

Sensory systems

In order to rationally use washing powder, conditioners, etc., it is necessary to choose a dosage in accordance with the amount of water consumption. The washing machine has a program that is able to figure out the required amount of the washing substance itself.

Thanks to the spin start, the unbalance detection system automatically tries to redistribute the laundry. If this is not done, then it is likely that the Bosch Classixx 5 washing machine will fail much faster, and it will have to be repaired more often as a result. This is due to the fact that an imbalance is likely to lead to breakage of the drum. If it is not possible to fix the problem, the spin will be turned on in minimum mode or will not be performed at all.



Repairs

In order to carry out repairs, it is necessary to inform the service center. No need to try to fix the problems yourself, as this can lead to a complete inoperability of the device.

The after-sales service will always help in the event of any breakdown, even if the warranty period has ended. The phone number can be found in the instructions or in additional documents that are provided with the equipment. Employees who work in the center have advanced skills.



Safety precautions

It must be remembered that the Bosch Classixx 5 washing machine is an electric appliance, which means there is the possibility of electric shock.

Touching objects that are energized should be avoided. In order to protect yourself, you need to pull the plug out of the outlet only for its base, and not for the cord extending from it. Do this only with dry hands.

If there are children in the house, it is recommended to monitor them and not leave them unattended. It is forbidden to play with equipment. Moreover, the Bosch Classixx 5 has a large drum, which means it can fit a small pet. Children at a young age do not understand the consequences of such actions.
