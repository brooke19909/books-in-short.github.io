Rest in the suburbs with tents. The best places for recreation and fishing

With the onset of the summer season, every Muscovite thinks about where to spend his vacation. Of course, many dream of going to popular foreign resorts. However, such a vacation involves a large financial outlay. You can go to the nearest sea. But not everyone has such an opportunity. What to advise in such a situation? Rest in the suburbs with tents!



"Wild" rest

Moscow is a very large and noisy city. All its inhabitants are tired of the insane pace of life. In order to gain strength, you need to get closer to nature. To do this, you can go to the suburbs. There are many beautiful ponds that are surrounded by beautiful nature. Fresh air, lack of fuss, silence and calm. This is exactly what you need for a good rest. Of course, there are people who find it difficult to understand how to stay for a long time without the usual comfortable conditions, that is, hot water, electricity, the Internet and other things. However, what can be gained by integrating as much as possible with pristine nature will never replace the benefits of civilization. So, where can you relax with tents in the suburbs? Mozhaisk Reservoir

Tent sites in the suburbs can also be found on the Mozhaisk reservoir. However, it is worth considering that on its coast there are a lot of recreation centers. Zhostovo quarry and lake Senezh

Rest in the suburbs with tents is also possible at the Zhostovo quarry. These places are ideal for lovers of wildlife. You can get here, heading north on the Ostashkovskoye highway. Camping places are almost everywhere. Convenient relief, picturesque surroundings - what else is needed for a good rest?
