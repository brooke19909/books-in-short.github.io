Private hotel "Diva", Sudak: how to get, living conditions, reviews

Crimea is a favorite vacation spot of Soviet citizens. A few decades ago it was quite difficult to find a citizen of the Soviet Union who had never been to Crimea. Modern tourists have become selective, picky, their taste preferences and requirements for resorts have changed. Moreover, foreign resorts became available, where the main stream of Russians rushed. The fashionable European and Asian resorts boast amazing service, high quality services and developed infrastructure. One problem - the cost of tours is quite high. In addition, to visit the near abroad, a passport is required, the registration of which is fraught with a number of difficulties and considerable financial investments.

Now that Crimea has become part of Russia, and vacationers do not need to issue foreign passports, why not consider this region for yourself? Here you can really have a good and inexpensive rest. The largest travel companies, air carriers offer profitable tours, low-cost charter flights to Crimea. All this helps to increase the number of vacationers in this region. The southern coast of Crimea remains the most popular, but so many vacationers gather here in season that literally there is nowhere for an apple to fall. If you, as a tourist, are looking for a more secluded, not crowded and calm place for your vacation, we suggest paying attention to the northern region of Crimea. An excellent option is just Sudak. With children, vacation in this region is safe and even useful.





About the resort

Sudak impresses all tourists and vacationers with the beauty of local landscapes. The majestic mountains compete with the endless valleys buried in verdure and cozy coves. If you love hiking, are a true connoisseur of nature, you will definitely like this place.

Beach lovers come here every year, all thanks to beautiful beaches and crystal clear warm sea water. Tours to Sudak choose families with young children for their vacation - a great chance to strengthen the baby's immunity and enjoy quality leisure. Of course, when planning a trip, you need to decide on a hotel or hotel. Sudak offers options of various comfort and price category - it all depends on your preferences and financial capabilities. We offer to evaluate a small private hotel "Diva" (Sudak). Reviews of real vacationers will help to make an objective assessment of this option.

About hotel

Of course, all potential vacationers are interested in information about the hotel, its location. So, the hotel "Diva" (Sudak) is considered a beach type hotel. Of course, it is not located on the coast, but in order to get to the beach, just cross the road. For hotels and hotels of Sudak this is a rarity. Numerous positive reviews from tourists to a greater extent and provided with an excellent location. The hotel is located near the city center, which means that all vacationers will have access to the infrastructure of the resort, will be able to fully enjoy their holidays here. But the nearest airport is just over 100 km away, but for the sake of amazing beauty and clean mountain air, you can come to terms with this drawback. Moreover, tours to Sudak offered by tour operators most often involve a transfer to the hotel. Tourists can even contact the hotel in advance and book a transfer if they go on a trip without the support of a tour operator.





Location Features

The hotel is located in the southeastern part of Sudak, close to the Alchak Mountain conservation area. The sea is literally at hand, so all tourists have the unique opportunity to meet the sunrises and see off the sunsets on the coast - a real romance. In walking distance from the hotel there are shops, cafes, air and railway ticket offices where you can purchase tickets, as well as rental equipment for sports equipment, travel agencies, where all curious tourists will be able to purchase excursions. Well, of course, all tourists will be able to visit the local water park every day if they choose the Diva hotel (Sudak) - it is literally 100 meters from it.

How to get there

This question most often worries tourists who refuse the services of intermediaries, that is, tour operators. So, airplanes, trains arrive in the center of Crimea - the city of Simferopol. In the direction of Simferopol - Sudak, buses, fixed-route taxis and trains run regularly. You can buy tickets at the station, see the schedule on the official website. If you go by personal transport, again you have to move in the direction of Simferopol - Sudak, the road here is quite busy, but the condition of the roadway is excellent.

Accommodation

Already from the name it is clear that we have before us a small private hotel. So, he offers vacationers one of 55 comfortable rooms. So, all numbers of various levels:

  • Standard - represented by one room with a private bathroom and a balcony;
  • Superior - superior room, represented by one room with a larger area with a private bathroom and a balcony;
  • Family - two-room suite with kitchen, living room, bedroom, bathroom and balcony.

Tourists will have to decide on accommodation in advance, if the view from the room is fundamentally important, and also inform the hotel administration about their desire. If you believe the reviews of tourists, check-in occurs within 10-15 minutes - the hotel administration always goes towards its guests. Even if vacationers arrive before the time set for check-in, they will receive the room keys almost immediately.





Description of rooms

Tourists are probably interested in the level of comfort that the Hotel Diva (Crimea) can offer. The reviews could not but touch on the description of the rooms and their technical equipment. All rooms are facing Sudak Bay, so the window offers stunning views of it. Each room has a private bathroom and a bathroom with working fixtures. Depending on the selected room category, tourists can rely on a shower or bath.

Each room has carpeted floors creating a homely atmosphere, satellite TV, air conditioning and a mini fridge. The furniture is not so much, but quite enough for tourists.





You can see traces of freshly renovated rooms, the interior is stylish and modern. Some of the rooms are a true work of design art, the arrangement of which has been worked out by true professionals. Bright color accents, modern and laconic furniture, luminous flux - all this creates a feeling of lightness, literally weightlessness, so necessary on vacation. Many of those vacationers who got a room with a balcony note that this is a real gift from the administration.

Nutrition

Unfortunately, the Hotel Diva (Sudak) does not have its own restaurant, so meals are not included in the price. You can have breakfast, lunch and dinner at local cafes and restaurants - there are a huge number of them. The friendly staff will tell you the most remarkable and noteworthy places. Moreover, on the beach you can enjoy boiled corn, taste local barbecue, buy soft drinks, fruits and vegetables.

Those vacationers who chose a room with a kitchen can independently prepare breakfast, lunch and dinner. All necessary products can be purchased at the nearest supermarket. Fresh vegetables, fruits, meat are sold at the local market. The room has a small refrigerator, but its internal volume is enough to store the necessary food.





Service

Private hotel "Diva" (Sudak), being completely small in size, can offer tourists and vacationers excellent service. All vacationers will be able to use the guarded parking lot for free. This service will delight all those who come to rest in their own car, now you don‚Äôt have to worry about finding a parking space, the safety of your car and overpay for parking.





Guests can make use of high-speed wireless internet on site. Tourists note that its speed is enough to exchange news with friends and relatives and even download a movie. The hotel administration offers round-the-clock service for vacationers - at any time, day or night, you can contact the reception staff with your question.

The staff is friendly, polite, trying to answer all the questions of tourists. Room is cleaned daily. This is not just surface cleaning: plumbing is cleaned, the floor in the bathroom is cleaned, furniture, towels and bed linen are changed. Room cleaning occurs during the absence of tourists, so nothing will disturb their vacation.

Beach and sea

The nearest pebble beach is only 10 meters - you just need to cross the road. Hotel "Diva" (Sudak) cannot offer its own beach, but the municipal one will surely satisfy every tourist. The coastline is quite wide, so even in season it is quite comfortable here. There is an observation tower on the beach, lifeguards work daily. Here you can rent sports equipment, do water sports. Of course, a sandy beach would be ideal for families with children, but despite this, a huge number of vacationers with small children come to Sudak every year.





Price policy

Each tourist is also interested in the pricing policy that is characteristic of the Sudak resort. Hotel "Diva", the price of accommodation in which primarily depends on the category of the selected room and time of arrival, is considered one of the most budgetary in the region. In season, the cost of a standard double room is 2400-2600 rubles, a superior room can be rented for 3200 rubles, but a two-room suite with a kitchen will cost you 3600 rubles.

To summarize

If you want to spend your vacation on the coast, there is absolutely no time to apply for a passport, you are limited in funds, go to Sudak. This place can hardly be called the most popular among tourists, but the rest here due to the chic unspoiled nature, clean and clear sea will be unforgettable. If you are aiming for a beach holiday, you want to forget about all the hardships of life and really relax your body and soul. Pay attention to the hotel "Diva" hotel 3 * (Sudak). It offers the best balance between the quality of the services provided and the cost of living, and it is located just a few minutes walk from the sea - what else is needed for an excellent beach holiday.
