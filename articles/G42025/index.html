Samara Region. Sanatorium "Samara", "Sergiev Mineral Waters", "Volga Rock" - reviews

Going on vacation with your family today is quite expensive, getting treatment with a good doctor is also not a cheap pleasure. Choose a great alternative - plan your vacation in a sanatorium! Regular visits to the health resort will help keep the body in good shape, which will positively affect health. Less often you will have to go to doctors and drink medicine, strength and good mood will increase. At any time of the year you will find the Samara region. Sanatorium you can choose any of the list below, all of them are proven, with very high-quality service.

Sanatorium ‚Ññ1 - "Samara"

He is often chosen for his close proximity to the city. You will need to get to Samara, and here on a regular bus (route number 6) to reach the final stop. Surprisingly, having arrived here, it is impossible to say that you are a stone's throw from the metropolis. Sanatorium "Samara" (Samara region) is located in a green suburban area.

On the territory there is a five-story building. It is worth booking a ticket in advance so as not to run into the fact that at the last moment there will be no seats. Sanatorium "Samarsky" (Samara region) is focused on treatment, not entertainment, but still there is a children's town and an outdoor pool, so you can relax with all members of your family. The evening can be spent on the banks of the Volga or in the city.

Genitourinary, endocrine, cardiovascular and nervous systems are being treated. In addition, the rehabilitation of children and adolescents is widely practiced.





"Sergiev Mineral Waters" - the oldest sanatorium in Samara

It will take a little more than an hour to get to it - it is 120 km from Samara. This is a health resort which is widely known far beyond the borders of Russia. The local attraction, which formed the basis for the opening of the hospital here, is Sulfur Lake. The smell around it is quite specific, in the early days it can be very disturbing, then you get used to it. The lake itself is located in a hollow overgrown with old trees. You can go to him once on an excursion, but it is often not recommended. The color of the water itself is not too pleasant, it is dirty turquoise, and the head may hurt from the smell.

The treatment itself takes place in a health resort building. It offers luxurious baths of heated mud, in which you want a sweet nap, hydrogen sulfide baths, a huge selection of medical procedures and the services of qualified doctors. A separate topic is nutrition. The menu here is well thought out, even for those who are shown diet food. A varied and tasty meal awaits guests every day. The Sergievsky sanatorium of the Samara region is a real salvation for diseases of the skin or nervous system, muscles and bones. They successfully undergo rehabilitation after operations.





Environmentally friendly zone: sanatorium "Volga rock"

What is plenty here is fresh air. This is another health resort, which is famous for the Samara region. The Volzhsky Skal Sanatorium (one of the most popular in the region) is located on the shore of the reservoir, at a distance of about 200 km from Samara. Such remoteness makes it possible to relax in places untouched by man. The healing microclimate is provided by the remoteness from the city and the proximity of the Zhiguli Sea, as well as the mountains.

Cardiovascular diseases are successfully treated here. It offers vacationers - a sports ground for volleyball, basketball, tennis and other sports. All year you will find the Samara region. In the summer, the sanatorium offers the opportunity to ride a bicycle or a boat, take a walk in picturesque places. In winter, you have the opportunity to go skiing, breathe the ringing frosty air.





Prevention Center "Stavropol"

If you are considering the sanatoriums of Samara and the Samara region and choosing where to relax, pay attention to another picturesque corner. Located in the city of Tolyatti, on the left bank of the Volga, near the Zhiguli Sea, it offers the opportunity to relax and improve health in a short time.

A five-story building is waiting for you on the territory, which is divided into specialized sectors: central, residential and medical. Now you can enjoy the beautifully renovated building and the latest equipment, as recently a complete reconstruction was carried out. The residential building includes rooms for 200 beds, each has a shower, in the "suites" - a bath or a jacuzzi.





No wonder the Samara region is so popular among tourists. Sanatorium "Stavropol" offers a unique treatment system, including mud, water, electric light therapy. Additionally, the doctor can offer a number of pleasant and useful procedures. Patients with respiratory diseases, circulatory system come here.

Sanatorium "Volga"

This small health resort operates in the city of Samara, 500 meters from the Volga. Already the very opportunity to sit on the shore in the evening and look at its unhurried course is healing. But this range of services is not limited. The basis of the spa resource and powerful natural healing factors are mineral water and mud. The supplier is the village of Sernovodsk, the sanatorium (Samara region) "Sergievsky".

The physiotherapy department offers more than 30 types of various treatment procedures. In addition to the main course, which is included in the price of the permit, each vacationer can use the services of a dentist, urologist, gynecologist and other specialists.

On the territory there is a medical and sleeping building for 46 people. This is a classic health resort, children under 14 years old are not accepted, except under the program "Mother and Child" or on the urgent recommendation of the attending physician. An entertainment program available for young children is not available here.

"Molodetsky mound"

This is a magical, unusual resort. Like a rejuvenating apple, it gives strength and fills with energy, restores health and good mood. Undoubtedly, this is the most beautiful place that the Samara region gave us. The sanatorium is located on the territory of the Samarskaya Luka National Park. The possibility of excursions around the park, as well as walks at the foot of the Zhiguli Mountains and along the banks of the majestic Volga, is noteworthy. The profile of the sanatorium is the general healing of the body. Many diseases of the respiratory system, neurological, are successfully treated here.

Here, not only adults rest. A summer camp opens for the summer holidays. A special category of vacationers is often ill children, sent by district pediatricians. They are provided with five meals a day, and counselors organize leisure activities.

Sanatorium "Green Forest"

Who doesn't like the smell of conifers? And the air around them is special, lively. Sanatorium "Russian Forest" (another name - "Green Forest") is conveniently located on the outskirts of the city of Togliatti in a pine forest. Fresh air, the opportunity from the window to watch fluffy squirrels jumping on the branches - all this in the morning charges with energy and a positive attitude.

Here you can get standard wellness treatments, undergo examination and experience the new trends of medicine: aromatherapy, hirudo and laser therapy. Three meals a day and the possibility of individual selection of the menu will delight each guest.





The resort offers a wide selection of medical services. Diagnostics using modern equipment will determine the cause of the disease, and experienced doctors will select the treatment. It is good that, having paid a ticket, you get the opportunity to undergo a multifaceted examination and get a whole range of medical and rehabilitation procedures. In a regular clinic, such services will cost much more, and queues before admission will not add a good mood.

Sanatorium "Red Glinka"

Located in a park area, 35 km from the center of Samara. The territory of the health resort is about 4 km. All proposed types of examination and treatment are included in the price of the permit and do not require additional costs. The local therapist completes the individual course based on the instructions of the attending physician and your wishes. Vacationers note the great convenience of such a system. With a relatively inexpensive ticket, it is possible to undergo a full examination and choose the optimal treatment regimen.





The pride of the resort is its own mineral water. It is used for dining and medicinal purposes, for baths and inhalations. In its composition, it is very rich in mineral salts and trace elements, but at the same time it is low-mineralized. A large set of nutrients and their low concentration make it possible to use water daily, like an ordinary dining room.

Brief conclusions and reviews

If you want to have a good rest, breathe fresh air and strengthen your health, then the sanatoriums of the Samara region work for you. Reviews of vacationers emphasize the high level of medical services, good food and comfortable rooms. The only negative is entertainment, especially for those who come with children. Many note that outside the procedures, vacationers are left to their own devices, and undeveloped infrastructure does not have the opportunity to find an interesting lesson. On the other hand, these institutions are created for treatment, and there is always the opportunity to take a walk in the fresh air, disconnect from the worldly fuss and read books. Take board games with you to make the evenings more fun.
