Holidays in the Kaliningrad region: sea, beaches, hotels, fishing

The Kaliningrad region is separated from the rest of our country by land borders and international sea waters. But the uniqueness of this region is not limited to this. People come here to enjoy the beauties of nature, receive spa treatment, as well as see interesting sights. Today we will talk in detail about the rest in the Kaliningrad region, where you can stay and what to do in your free time.





Climate and weather

This region can please tourists with frost-free winters, when temperatures rarely drop below -6 ¬∞ C, and warm summers. In July, air temperature usually reaches 21 ¬∞ C. What else can please the Kaliningrad region with tourists? The sea warms up to 18 ¬∞ C, and in some places up to 21 ¬∞ C. The climate in the region is close to temperate continental and marine, and there are no less sunny days than in Sochi.

Hotels

Since the development of the region is closely connected with tourism, hotels, hotels, guest houses and tourist centers are open in cities and towns. If you wish, you can stay in the private sector or rent a city apartment.

Hotels in the Kaliningrad Region are marked with stars that indicate the level of comfort and service. However, before booking a place, find reviews on the Internet and read what the guests who have been here write.





Treatment in the Kaliningrad region

One of the main healing factors in this region is the climate. The air is saturated with particles of sea salt, as well as compounds of iodine and bromine. Meteorology also plays an important role in this process. The fact is that under the influence of their fluctuations, a person's metabolism is activated, the cardiovascular system works better and the respiratory system begins to function well. In addition, analyzes show that patients have increased levels of oxygen in the blood.

Treatment and rest in the Kaliningrad region is also attractive because it can be used for therapeutic peat mud treatment. They improve the condition of joints and muscles, help with chronic diseases of the nervous system, and also treat gynecological diseases.





Local mineral waters are the pride of the inhabitants of this region. The Svetlogorsk and Zelenogradskaya brands were awarded bronze, silver and gold medals at various international exhibitions.

Sanatoriums of the Kaliningrad Region

Next, we will talk about where you can get treatment and relax if you decide to come to this wonderful region:

  • The Amber Coast Sanatorium is one of the best health resorts in the region. It is located away from the main streets of Svetlogorsk in a green, ecologically clean area. Gynecological diseases, neurology, diseases of the musculoskeletal system, digestive organs, and cardiovascular system are treated here. It uses aromatherapy, mud therapy, hirudotherapy, massages, diet, and balneotherapy. The sanatorium has a good diagnostic base, including clinical and biochemical laboratories, an ECG room, ultrasound diagnostics and much more. On the territory there are wells with warm mineral water, as well as wells that supply a strong solution for preparing bathtubs.

The beaches of the Kaliningrad region

The western border of this region is a continuous series of beaches. The best of them is considered "Amber" and located on the Kushira Spit. If infrastructure is important to you, then pay attention to the beaches of Zelenogradsk and Svetlogorsk. Those who want to escape away from civilization should go to the Baltic Spit, Primorye and Kulikovo.





The beaches of the Kaliningrad region are very clean and have a large length. They are divided into equipped, belonging to sanatoriums and camp sites, and "wild." In some places it is forbidden to make fires and put up tents, so take care of food and residence in advance.

Fishing

The quarries of the Baltic Sea, numerous rivers and lakes attract fishermen from all over the country and neighboring countries to this region. Fishing in the Kaliningrad region is of interest to many people. According to statistics, over 600 thousand people visit water bodies annually. Most of them come from the CIS countries, and the rest come from Poland, Austria, Germany and many other European countries.





Fishing in the Kaliningrad region can be paid and free. Those who choose the second option usually go to the Neman River, Lake Vishnetets, the Rzhavka and Prokhladnoy rivers. Chalets are charged, which give vacationers rent boats and equipment. You also have to pay if you decide to go fishing in a private pond. The latter include the Razino pond, Lake Karpovoe and some others.

Excursion program

Rest in the Kaliningrad region is impossible to imagine without visiting famous attractions. There are many popular routes, and we list some of them:

  • Sightseeing tour of Kaliningrad - in a few hours you will see the most interesting places of the city, among which there is the Cathedral, ancient bridges and gates, fortifications and much more.
  • Knight's castles - they appeared in those days when the lands of West Prussia were conquered by the Poles and knights of the Teutonic Order. If you love history and adventure attracts you, then do not give up this amazing trip.
  • Curonian Spit - this trip should be a must-have item on your cultural program. Here you will see the unique nature of the Baltic coast, get acquainted with its flora and fauna. You will certainly be interested in a dancing forest, a bird ringing station and the height of Efa.
