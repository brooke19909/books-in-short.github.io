Description

The recreation center "Surf" in the city of Yeysk has a fenced, guarded territory around the clock. For vacationers, there are one-story buildings, two-story cottages and Finnish houses. The main building has a library. Car owners are given a place in a guarded parking lot. For an additional fee, you can rent household, beach and sports equipment. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout. Meals are provided in a private dining room, which is located in a separate building. Lovers of an active lifestyle can work out both in the indoor gym and in outdoor sports grounds. Young tourists spend their time on the playground or in the games room under the supervision of a teacher. Animators also work for them. Private sandy beach is located at a distance of 50 meters from the camp site. It is equipped with umbrellas, awnings, sun loungers, changing cabins, a cafe. The recreation center "Surf" in Yeysk is located at 2/16 Plyazhnaya Street.

Guest Accommodation

Tourists are offered several options for accommodation:

  • double - there are one-room, there is a kitchen and a courtyard;
  • triple - also have one-room, some have a kitchen;
  • four-seater - one-room or with a kitchen;
  • six people are accommodated in a two-story four-room cottage.

Most of the rooms at the recreation center "Surf" in Yeysk are equipped with a TV, refrigerator, air conditioning, telephone. Double, triple and quadruple rooms in the main building are equipped with a refrigerator, a shared bathroom is located on the floor. Breakfast is included in the price. If you wish, you can include three meals a day.

Additional services

In order to make your stay rich and memorable, at the camp site guests are offered various services and entertainment:

  • There are sports fields with both rubber and hard surfaces.
  • Playgrounds with attractions for children are located on the street. Indoors, under the supervision of an experienced teacher, young guests can be in the games room. In the evenings, animators will invite them to entertainment events.
  • Meals are organized in the spacious dining room.
  • Many activities are offered for the adult population. They can attend concerts, discos, and participate in sports.
  • The recreation center "Surf" in Yeysk offers massage services.
  • You can grab a bite to eat at the barbecue and ice cream parlor.
  • Staff at the tour desk organize sightseeing tours for holidaymakers.
