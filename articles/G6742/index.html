The hotel operates during the beach season, that is, from April to the end of October. Despite its small size, it is quite capable of captivating the hearts of tourists. After all, the main thing during the rest is good impressions and a positive mood. And the owners and staff of the hotel do everything to make you feel as if you are in a real Greek family, and even spent a very budget vacation. Crete - hotels, beaches and everything for relaxation

This ancient legendary island as if created in order to travel on it. Well, and rest, of course. Great beaches of golden sand or pebbles, decent and comfortable hotels, as well as excellent public transport and infrastructure are what tourists in Crete expect. You can swim on the island from April to the first half of November - the sea, as a rule, is warmer here than in other places. In the summer it is quite hot, so people who do not tolerate stuffiness are better to go here in June or September. But in any case, the breath of the sea breeze softens any intrigues of sunlight.



Crete hotels - classification

Since tourism has been developing on the island for about fifty years, many hotels have been set up here. There are very chic hotels - villas, palaces, planted with gardens on the shore, complexes in the style of ancient Greece or Gothic castles ... Moreover, they rest in such hotels not only on the sea, there are also five-star mountain shelters. Most hotels are located in the north and east of the island. West and south are calmer places suitable for a quiet family or romantic getaway. The most expensive resorts are Elounda and Agia Pelagia, budget and fun are Heraklion and Chania with their many suburbs. In short, Crete is a large recreation area. Here everyone chooses a place affordable and in mood. But the most popular hotels in Crete are 3 stars. It is cheaper, more convenient and more practical. Good reviews, for example, about Akti Corali in Amoudara, Sea View in Chania, Sergios in Hersonnisos. At the same time, their simplicity and budget does not mean at all that it is worse to relax there.

Two and three star hotels

What about the economical option of living? These are the hotels of Crete - 3 stars, or even 2 - are the most characteristic of the island. When the tourist boom began here - namely, in the seventies of the last century - mainly family hotels of the "trouble and breakfest" type were built. The then minimalist travelers believed that more was not needed. After all, it was believed that people who came here would not sit all day at the hotel, but would eat there, where they would find time for lunch or dinner. And to stay hungry in Greece is very strange. Everywhere you are deliciously fed in huge portions. Therefore, mainly such hotels flooded the coast of the island of Crete. 2 star hotels often offer even better services than treshki because they try to ‚Äúget around" competitors with the quality of services. Amoudara

This resort town is often called the suburb, or even the "beach" of Heraklion. We can say that this is one street with shops, souvenir shops and bars, running parallel to the sea. Hotel Roxani 2 Ammoudara, therefore, is located in the area where they mainly go to relax. Here are the best beaches and the cleanest and azure sea in Crete. There are often waves over the sandy shore, but they are quite safe, it is nice to ride them. In addition, those wishing to see the island will be pleasantly surprised. Amudara is located almost in the center of intersection of the main highways and is approximately equally distant from everything. If you want to go east, to Agios Nikolaos, or west, to Chania, then both will be conveniently carried out from here.



How to get here

To get to Amudara, you first need to get to Crete. Flights to Greece are flying to the island. Even from the mainland get here on a ferry. KTEL buses run from Heraklion Airport to Amoudara. They romp around the island. There is an interesting feature - to get to Amudara, you need to take bus number six, and vice versa - the first route. The stop is right next to the hotel entrance, which is very convenient.

Hotel location and infrastructure

Roxani 2 is not very far from the center of Heraklion resort - five kilometers. The airport is also close enough to the hotel. Before him, only eight kilometers. Opposite the hotel there is a supermarket chain Rania with fairly low prices, as well as public and tourist transport. The sea is right around the bend - unlike neighboring hotels. From here you can leave for Heraklion - see museums and antiquities or just for shopping. There is also a bus station from where you can get to any corner of the island. The buses are comfortable, air-conditioned. The hotel has an elevator, there is a beautiful garden, terraces. On the second floor there is a very spacious veranda with tables where you can sit, relax, eat, read. Nice area and near the pool where they eat - there are many different cute figurines and decorations.



Number of rooms

Roxani 2 is a small hotel. It has sixty two numbers. There are rooms for one, two and three people. They are of a standard type. You can also rent apartments at the hotel. The rooms are quite large. There are family rooms. The furniture in the rooms is good, modern, comfortable. Everywhere there is air conditioning (but it is paid), refrigerator, TV. In the bathroom - good plumbing, shower. Shower gel and soap are replaced regularly. By the way, the water in the bathroom is heated by solar panels, so in the morning it is pretty cool, and in the evening - hot. There is a telephone, a TV with satellite channels. True, there are quite few outlets, so it is better to take a tee with you. Each room has a balcony with beautiful views. There is a table with chairs, ropes for drying towels and linen.

Beach and water treatments

Roxani Hotel 2 has a nice pool with a sun terrace. If the weather is bad or the storm, swimming here is very good. There are sunbeds, both in the sun and in the shade of lemongrass. The pool has a children's section, the water is clean. You can swim there around the clock. But, of course, they come here for the sake of the sea. And it is very close. Only one hundred and fifty meters - and you are on a beautiful sandy beach. A path from the hotel leads to the Malibu Beach Resort Recreation Area. Amoudara is famous for its coast, it is the longest in all of Crete. The sea is very warm, the water is clear, you can see how small fish swim. In addition, from the shore you can walk through the territory of all neighboring hotels on the sea. Sunbeds and umbrellas, as on all municipal beaches, are paid. However, you can significantly reduce the cost of your vacation at sea, while making it comfortable, taking loungers belonging to some cafe, and at the same time take some water for fifty cents. After all, the whole beach is dotted with various bars. Excursions

What can I see for vacationers who come to Roxani 2? Crete is a legendary place, so you should not say that you were only on the beach upon arrival home. Many tourists believe that if you really want to explore the island, it is better to rent a car. But in the case when you do not have rights or you are not very mobile, you can buy a full-day sightseeing tour - there are a lot of options. At the very least, you can see those nearby attractions, such as Agios Nikolaos. Still advised to independently go to Heraklion. Firstly, the local archaeological museum has many interesting artifacts from the Knossos palace, and secondly, you can walk around the old center and the Venetian fortress. Would you like to go to the capital of ancient Crete yourself? No problem, the number one bus runs directly from the hotel and should go to the Astoria Hotel. And from there, No. 2 will take you directly to the Knossos Palace. And if you just want to see the surroundings, then in front of the hotel there is a tourist trip stop. He takes you to the mountains, from where you can take a bird's eye view of Amoudara. In addition, he makes great stops at the convent and the authentic Cretan village.
