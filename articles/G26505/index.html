Where to go in Penza with a child or friends?

Toy railway

The city of Penza for children is great. After all, there are many different entertainments. For example, traveling by rail, created specifically as a cognitive-entertaining attraction, will be a great way to spend leisure time. A steam locomotive carries its passengers as much as 2.5 kilometers. At the same time, transport makes two stops along the route. The attraction is a real simulator designed so that children can learn more about rail travel.

Rope park "Tarzania"

In the open air there is a whole town created from rope attractions. In this place, everyone is invited to ride a bungee, walk along the rope ladder and much more. The park has several runs for passing, designed for different ages of visitors. Tired of outdoor activities for the whole family, you can visit one of the children's cafes in Penza.

"Olive"

This place is very popular among local residents, because a whole children's town is located right in the cafe. In addition, the menu offers the most delicious desserts and all kinds of dishes.

Cafe Donna Mozzarella

Great place, decorated colorful and elegant. The institution is always full of children. The cafe has its own play area.

Night club

Where to go in Penza with friends? Several nightclubs are open in the city at once, each of which has its own distinctive features. For example, you can dance to the fiery music that accompanies the laser show, in the club "Amsterdam" or in the "Gladiator". By the way, the last place includes three floors, on each of which visitors will find separate dance floors with different music directions. In addition, the doors of show bars, clubs, restaurants and art clubs are open to all comers.

Leisure

Where to go in Penza for those who love outdoor activities? The city has a huge number of places where you can relax with a whole company. For lovers of sports entertainment, many rental points are open. People in whole groups take rollers, bicycles or skateboards to relax in nature.
