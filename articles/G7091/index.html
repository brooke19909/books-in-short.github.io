Amaya Beach Resort. Holidays in Thailand (Phuket): photos, prices and reviews of tourists



Thailand is not in vain called the country of smiles, because all the locals are always happy to see guests of the kingdom. The exotic place is also famous for its amazing nature. What could be more beautiful than snow-white beaches with majestic palm trees and rocks that go directly from the Gulf of Thailand or the Andaman Sea? Thailand is translated from the local language as "country of the free", and the capital of the state - Bangkok is called the "city of angels." Newlyweds often come to the kingdom to enjoy each other in a romantic setting, or people who appreciate the exotic nature, ancient temples and culture of the local population. There are a huge number of hotels in Thailand, but special attention should be paid to Amaya Beach Resort. It is chosen by those who like to surround themselves with good-quality things and first-class service. 3 star hotels in Phuket are also popular, but for a more comfortable stay it is recommended to choose this one.

Short description

In order not to regret subsequently the choice made, it is worth reading some information useful to traveling people. The exact location of the hotel for tourists will show a map of Thailand (Phuket). Having studied it, you can easily then get to the hotel after independent walks.

The map shows that Amaya Beach Resort is located in the center of the picturesque city of Patong, suitable for lovers of active and passive relaxation, as well as shopping.

The hotel building is designed in national style, which successfully combines with modern elements of decor.

Phuket Airport is 23 km from Amaya Beach Resort.

The road to the beach is just 2 minutes on foot. If desired, you can reach another coast called Tri-Trang, located 2.9 km from the hotel. Tourists arriving in Thailand (Phuket) use the beaches as their main place of stay.

At the reception, in addition to cash, Visa and Mastercard are accepted.

Standard Amaya Beach Resort Rooms

Apartments play an important role for guests. It is no secret that many tourists want to relax in comfortable conditions, so special attention in Thailand is paid to the arrangement of residential premises. For some guests it is important to have a desk, and someone wants to see a jacuzzi.

In total, the hotel has 85 modern rooms.



The standard room is 30 square meters. m. Guests are accommodated in double or two single beds. Mandatory attributes of such numbers: cable TV (Russian channels are required), telephone with international access, air conditioning, wireless Wi-Fi, refrigerator, safe. Pleasantly surprised by the presence of beach towels, which are replaced daily.

A roomy attribute is a roomy luggage rack, and the spacious bathroom has a walk-in shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. The view from the balcony opens onto the neighboring buildings or the panorama of the city.

Important information

Each tourist before a trip to Amaya Beach Resort SPA 4 * will be notified of the following points:

  1. Check-in of new guests takes place at 14:00, and check-out at 12:00.
  2. In addition to the passport, it is obligatory to check in cash or by credit card before check-in.
  3. Self-registration is only possible from the age of 18.
  4. One child (up to 12 years old) is entitled to free accommodation only if he is accommodated in the room of his parents or guardians in existing beds. In this case, children are not provided with a free breakfast.
  5. All rooms are equipped with video surveillance cameras in order to ensure the safety of guests. For all the years of existence of Amaya Beach Resort SPA, no thefts have been recorded in the apartments.

Nutrition

Balanced food, decorated with edible decorations, daily cheers up guests. Amaya Beach Resort has a BB system, which means free breakfast. Lunch and dinner can be purchased for a fee. Breakfast offers a large number of fresh fruits, juices, national and European dishes. It is important to remember that in Thailand, most often spicy food. Drinking bottled water, which is provided to guests absolutely free of charge, is perfect for quenching thirst in hot weather.

Maya Restaurant is located on site and specializes in international, Spanish and Italian cuisine. Here you can enjoy huge aromatic portions of amazing food at a low cost.

Most tourists meet morning in a coffee shop. Delicious drinks help guests cheer up after sleeping or exploring Patong's nightlife. Coffee is brewing before your eyes. Due to the alluring smell, it is impossible to calmly pass by this establishment. A large assortment of fresh pastries is offered.

Service

Some services are provided free of charge. But there are those for which you must pay.

Free service

The main advantage over other hotels is the conditions for the stay of disabled people - special paths have been built for their movement. When studying information about Amaya Beach Resort, reviews can often be read about this equipment, as not every hotel accepts people with disabilities.

Rooms are cleaned daily, after which guests are delighted with a variety of figures of towels placed on the bed.

An ironing board and iron are available upon request.

Reception is open 24 hours a day. At the reception, guests can make a currency exchange and consult on some issues.





Paid services

Guests often book an excursion on elephants or to the fascinating islands of Pipi with their wildlife. It was there that the film "Beach" was shot with Leonardo DiCaprio in the title role. It is also possible to visit a snake farm, spend the whole day riding elephants, or go to a bamboo factory. Most often, several excursions are already included in the tour program.

Amaya Beach Resort provides a wedding service. For more information, please contact the reception.

Amaya Beach Resort 4 * also has a car rental service with a driver, which will show you the main attractions of the city or just take you on various matters.

Paid ironing services are available. An airport shuttle is included. In order to use it, you should contact the employee of the registration desk.

Hotel Territory

The hotel has not in vain got its name Amaya Beach Resort SPA 4 *. On its territory there is a massage parlor where professional masters will help tourists improve their health. SPA-center is the most popular type of leisure activities for guests of various age categories.

Tourists leave their own or rented cars in the free parking lot, which is the property of the hotel.

Laundry will save vacationers from washing, giving them more free time to explore the sights of the famous Patong.

On the territory there are 2 open-air pools: adult and children's. After a swim, you can relax on the sun loungers under the umbrella.

Bars

Alcohol is not included in the BB system. On the territory of Amaya Beach Resort near the pool there is a bar where polite staff will meet you with a smile and make an original cocktail decorated with an umbrella or a bizarre slice of exotic fruit. All drinks undergo a quality check, so guests can not worry about their health. The bar / lounge is designed for visitors who value relaxation in a calm cozy atmosphere with good music. Here you can hide from the scorching rays of the sun and enjoy a soft drink.

The pub is also an element of the infrastructure of this hotel. It is decorated in oriental style. Here, the bartender invites visitors to try several varieties of fresh local beer with a seafood appetizer.





The shops

Shopping enthusiasts are always delighted with their stay at Amaya Beach Resort. Reviews of tourists indicate the presence of a large number of shops inside the hotel. There, guests buy souvenirs for their near and dear ones, as well as basic necessities, clothes, bags and much more.

From the hotel to the main shopping center of Patong called Jungceylon distance is only 800 meters. You can buy absolutely everything in it. A shopping center is sometimes compared to a small city. There are boutiques, banks, souvenir shops, restaurants, cafes, fast food, a beauty salon, shops of inexpensive clothes, shoes and groceries. You do not have to go shopping here, you can just walk past the singing fountains or the ship, which is the symbol of Jungceylon, enjoy a laser show, and also visit a night club. For tourists who love an active and interesting vacation, Thailand (Phuket) will be the most suitable place.

Natural factor



The people of the Kingdom of Thailand are very environmentally friendly. On the territory of all hotels there are always gardens where guests take walks or doze in the shade. In this state, nature is striking in its beauty and balance.

The work of the people who arranged the Amaya Beach Resort deserves unlimited respect. In the center of the pool are gazebos. Each miniature structure is connected to the main platform through a beautiful bridge. The hotel has a national park. Tropical plants give it a special touch.

Prices

The cost of staying at the hotel varies from 3,000 to 6,000 rubles per night, depending on the type of room selected. The total price of the tour is affected by the flight distance, the number of days spent in Amaya Beach Resort, the availability of a food system and the number of excursions included. Most often, guests choose a ticket for a period of 10 to 15 days. During this time, you can get acquainted with local customs, cuisine, visit temples, and improve your health in the SPA. Those who choose a vacation in Phuket are quite happy with the prices. Often tour operators offer various options at a discount, which is quite profitable at a cost.

Useful Facts



Before the flight, several customs of the local population should be studied. For example, do not get too naked and allow yourself frivolous behavior in public places. It is also not recommended to look closely at the Thais, as excessive curiosity is perceived as a sign of disrespect and poor education.

It is strictly forbidden to speak negatively against the king and point a finger at his portrait. For insulting the ruler, even a prison term is possible.

You can withdraw money from the card at any ATM. They are located on the streets, shops and in shopping centers. Local currency is considered a baht equal to one ruble.

A smile on your face and respect for others will make your vacation memorable.
