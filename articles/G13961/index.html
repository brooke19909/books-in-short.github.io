Ethiopian capital is a city of contrasts

Ethiopia is located in the east of the African continent. This state attracts numerous crowds of tourists with its unique climate. 346 days throughout the year in this region of Africa are absolutely clear days and there is no such heat as in the rest of it. This is because Ethiopia is at a great height. The state is located on the highlands, and is often irrigated by rains. The eastern and northern sides of Ethiopia are the only part of Africa where agriculture with arable land has been preserved, although already with forced irrigation.



The capital of Ethiopia is the city of Addis Ababa, but residents shorten the name of the city to Addis, which means - New. The capital was founded in 1884. The city is not high
culture and cleanliness. Along with modern buildings, you can find slums where the poor residents of Ethiopia's capital huddle. It is difficult to determine where you left off - near the presidential palace or on the outskirts with bazaar points. This is the color of the capital.

However, Addis Ababa is currently the largest city in a state such as Ethiopia. The capital has about 2.2 million inhabitants. Wide avenues with beautiful plantings of tropical trees give a unique cosiness to the city.



Addis Ababa is the capital of Ethiopia, it is a large industrial center of the state. Metalworking, woodworking enterprises, shoe factories, printing plants produce products that enter the huge urban open market of Mercato. There are also agricultural products.

The capital of Ethiopia is rich in its sights and cultural institutions. Menelik II Palace was built in 1894, the House of Africa is a modern building, built in 1963, an octagonal-shaped cathedral of St. George, founded in 1896. The capital of Ethiopia is famous for the UN headquarters for Africa OAU (Organization of African Unity).



An increasing number of tours are organized in the wonderful African country of Ethiopia. The state took under its wing the development of tourism business in the country. Rest - Ethiopia, this is an unforgettable time spent. Tourists visit the largest cities of the country: Addis Ababa, Bahr Dar, Gondar. Here they can get acquainted with the history of Ethiopia from antiquity to the present. Fans of the animal world are attracted by the numerous inhabitants of the African continent. Comfortable, modern hotels provide vacationers with all kinds of services at a level no worse than European standards. It was Ethiopia that became the first African state in which a five-star hotel was built. For travelers suffering from a hot climate, a vacation in this country is the most comfortable compared to other African countries.

Visiting the African state of Ethiopia, you will get acquainted with the extraordinary inhabitants, and the indescribable beauty of nature and the many inhabitants of the African fauna.
