Autumn crafts for kindergarten from pumpkin and not only

In almost every preschool, in the fall, there are some fairs, sales, exhibitions, competitions or just autumn holidays, for which children and their parents are asked to bring autumn crafts to the kindergarten. Such forms of work, of course, are useful in bringing the child and parents together: after all, the baby cannot cope with the manufacture of the crafts himself, and his mother or father, and sometimes grandmother and grandfather, come to his aid. And here are a couple of workshops that will help you easily make autumn crafts for kindergarten.





Crafts from the leaves

Autumn leaves are a fertile material for the manufacture of various crafts. Collecting them brings unprecedented joy to kids, and even adults can return to the world of childhood. During the gathering, you can fix with the child the names of the trees, the colors and even the shape, remember the verses about autumn and just take a walk with good use. To make autumn crafts for the kindergarten from leaves, they need to be properly prepared. We offer a master class on making an autumn wreath of leaves:

1. Wash and dry the leaves of different colors.

2. Smooth with an iron.

3. Make a ring from thin wire.

4. In each leaflet, make a hole and string the leaves on the ring, pressing them tightly against each other.

5. Spread the wreath and decorate it with ribbons, beads, flat figures.

Crafts on the autumn theme of vegetables





Vegetables ... This is where the real scope for imagination opens! In the autumn, so many of them keep up, and they are all so beautiful that you just can not help but use in their work.
Autumn crafts for kindergarten can be very different: this is a farm with piglets and cows from potatoes, boats from zucchini, octopuses from carrots. A pumpkin can become either a mask for the Halloween holiday or a candlestick, which is popular abroad, and can serve as the basis for decorating a flower meadow or a basket filled with fruits, berries and flowers.

Pumpkin crafts workshop

1. Wash and dry the pumpkin, cut the stalk so that the diameter of the slice is 10-15 cm.

2. From the pumpkin, select the seeds and pulp, treat the inside with sandpaper until smooth.

3. Dry the pumpkin at room temperature or in the sun, to speed up the drying process, you can fill the pumpkin with newspapers.

4. Using a stencil, apply a picture to the outside of the vegetable.

5. Cut on a pumpkin is better with special tools for carving, but in their absence a sharp knife will do.

6. Finally, the product can be coated with transparent varnish: this will preserve it for a longer period and give shine.

Huge opportunities open to those who know the art of carling. Using tools, remove the middle of the pumpkin, cut out the eyes-mouth or patterns, if you are not making a mask. You can insert a candle inside and light it. But unfortunately, autumn crafts for a kindergarten made of vegetables are short-lived, they quickly deteriorate, so you need to make them on the eve of a holiday or exhibition.





Crafts made of natural material

It is not in vain that the autumn season is called generous; it truly gives us such an amount of material that can be used in our work that we should only be surprised and thank it from the bottom of our hearts. In the forest and park, you can collect cones of spruce and pine, acorns and chestnuts with your child. They are perfect for decorating crafts or directly for its manufacture. Do not neglect simple branches, you can make a large number of wonderful crafts from them. For example, make an autumn panel:

1. A fancifully curved twig to clear of excess debris.

2. Using PVA glue, attach the branch to a sheet of thick cardboard.

3. Glue colorful autumn leaves onto a twig.

4. Make a frame of natural material: acorns, spruce needles and so on.

Autumn crafts for kindergarten is a flight of imagination of the child and parents, and making them is a wonderful pastime that helps bring the whole family together.
