Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 * (Tossa de Mar, Spain): hotel infrastructure, room description, service, reviews

Tossa de Mar is one of the most popular resorts in Spain, which at the same time is considered a budget holiday destination. The city is located in the north of the country in Catalonia - near Barcelona and the border of France. Tourists come here not only to swim in the warm sea, but also to admire the rocky coast, landscaped parks and ancient medieval sights. If you like to relax in comfort without sparing money, then pay attention to the resort Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 *, which offers a high level of service. Let us consider this complex in more detail in our article.

Hotel location

The favorable location of the hotel is the basis of a good rest. The tourist complex is built on a vast landscaped area away from the central part of the city. Therefore, in the evenings and nights, guests are not bothered by noise from cars and loud music of night clubs. In this case, if desired, they can easily get to Tossa de Mar, because its center is only 300 meters away from here. You can overcome this distance on foot, but if you do not want to take a walk, you can call a taxi or take public transport. Therefore, get ready for the long road and be sure to bring drinking water with you, because the journey can take 2-3 hours by bus. Girona Airport (38 km) is much closer , but tourists rarely choose it because cheap charter flights do not fly here.

But this is where the virtues of the location end. Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 * complex (Spain) is significantly removed from the beach, which is 800 meters from this hotel. At the same time, she does not organize any transfer to the coast, so tourists should get to it daily at their own expense by taxi or walk. But there are also advantages here. For example, on the way to the beach you can see a beautiful panorama of a medieval castle of the 12th century, built right on the coast.

General Hotel Information

The hotel was built in 2003. It is located on the outskirts of the city and consists of one five-story building, from which several wings depart. Next to the building is a large swimming pool and an adjacent sun terrace. In total, the Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 * hotel has 380 rooms, which can simultaneously accommodate up to 1000 guests. You can come here on vacation with young children, but at the same time, pets are strictly prohibited. Registration of guests here runs from 14:00 until midnight. Leave your rooms after the rest is necessary from 07:00 until noon.





The hotel staff is qualified workers with appropriate education and work experience. They speak excellent Spanish, French and English, but they understand Russian much worse. As a rule, a Russian-speaking guide helps tourists from Russia get comfortable on the spot. Usually they are sent by all major domestic travel agencies .

Hotel Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 *: room description

As mentioned above, this hotel has 380 rooms of different levels of comfort. All of them are undergoing annual cosmetic repairs, respectively, the last renovation was carried out in 2016. The hotel apartments have their own private bathroom, and some of the rooms are additionally equipped with an open balcony or terrace. Most of the windows overlook the pool. Choosing a budget option for accommodation, tourists will have to be content with a view of the city street. Daily maids tidy up the rooms. Linen and bath towels are changed less often - 3 times a week, that is, 1 time in 2 days.

Tourists who come to rest in this hotel will be offered apartments of the following categories:

  • Standard - single rooms with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã16 square meters. m. At the same time, 3 adults can be accommodated here.
  • Family - consists of a bedroom, which accommodates 2 double beds and a separate sofa, and a bathroom. Area - 24 square meters. m
  • Superior - rooms additionally equipped with a jacuzzi on the balcony. There is also a separate dressing room. Area - 32 square meters. m
  • Single - a small room of 12 square meters. m, where there is 1 single bed.

The room equipment here is very diverse, which will surely appeal to fastidious guests. Tourists can use both free and paid amenities. We list the main ones:

  • electronic safe - provided for a fee, in addition, guests must pay a deposit for it, which is returned after the end of the holiday;
  • a small fridge - in the superior room there are additional welcome drinks, and the rest is available for a fee;

  • free Wi-Fi in all rooms;

  • a set of towels, bathrobes and slippers, as well as bath accessories in the bathroom;
  • built-in hairdryer and cosmetic mirror - in the same place.

Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 *: hotel infrastructure

The hotel also has a high level of service. On its territory there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay, so it is suitable for tourists who do not like to leave the hotel during their holidays without extra reason. We list the main infrastructure facilities of the hotel:

  • 5 conference rooms of various sizes and equipment - the largest can accommodate up to 375 delegates, and the smallest - only 20 people;
  • therapist's office - you can visit it only at an additional cost;
  • currency exchanger;

  • Internet cafe , where you can not only get quick access to the network, but also take time for computer games;
  • gift shop, beach paraphernalia, soft drinks and snacks;
  • beauty saloon;
  • 24-hour reception
  • car rental;
  • rent of safes and individual luggage storage at the reception;
  • free wifi throughout the hotel.




Catering

Dining at the Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 * hotel is perfect for tourists who prefer a relaxing stay at the hotel. It offers the popular All-Inclusive concept, which includes all full-fledged meals, soft drinks and spirits made in Spain, as well as desserts and light snacks. However, alcohol is only served for lunch and dinner. Alcohol is also available in the hotel bars. It is poured into plastic glasses, with which it is forbidden to walk around the complex. One glass is issued in one hand. The same goes for ice cream, fast food and other light snacks. In addition, alcohol under the age of 18 is prohibited. For tourists who want to eat on their own, there is also a system of half board and full board.

The hotel has many catering establishments. We list the main ones:

  • Costa Brava Restaurant is the main restaurant serving breakfast, lunch and dinner at the buffet. Gala dinners are held once a week . In high season, a children's menu is also available, the dishes of which do not contain gluten and lactose.
  • Bar Capri Restaurant - Open in the evenings and √† la carte. It serves traditional Spanish cuisine.
  • La Pergola Restaurant - Serves Italian cuisine, ice cream and light snacks.
  • Tapas Restaurant - A restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine and seafood. You can visit it on a preliminary reservation.




The hotel also has several bars, one of which holds night discos. They are located in the hotel lobby and by the pool.

Beach holidays and other activities

For a calm and measured beach holiday, the Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 * complex is suitable. The beach here is citywide and it is located at a distance of 800 meters from the hotel. Visiting it is free, but for umbrellas, towels, mattresses and sunbeds are issued for a separate amount. The coast of the resort consists entirely of pebbles, so special shoes are also needed here. There is a lifeguard team on the beach, there is a water entertainment center, a bar and sports grounds.

For tourists who do not like to walk far, the hotel has 2 spacious outdoor pools with a maximum depth of 1.4 meters. Next to them is a sun terrace with free sun loungers and parasols. Day and evening, a lifeguard is on duty at the ponds. In cold or rainy weather, tourists can visit the indoor heated pool.





The following entertainment options are also available on site:

  • spa with sauna, steam room, massage room and jacuzzi;
  • basketball, table tennis and five-a-side football courts;
  • archery or rifle;
  • evening entertainment programs;
  • gym;
  • billiard room.

Positive reviews

As a rule, the Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 * hotel receives positive reviews from its guests . In them, they indicate the following advantages of the complex:

  • always friendly and polite hotel staff;
  • great variety of dinners with lots of meat and fruit on the menu;
  • there is good animation in Russian, which is considered a rarity in the resorts of Spain;
  • spacious, always tidy and well-lit rooms;
  • good location - you can walk to the sea in 10 minutes.




Negative reviews

However, it was not without criticism either. According to guests, the Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 * hotel has the following significant disadvantages:

  • poor soundproofing in the rooms, especially the noise from the street is audible to people living on the ground floor;
  • old repairs in living rooms;
  • poor-quality housekeeping - some visitors found other people's hair on the floor;
  • Internet works well only on the ground floor of the hotel;
  • the smell of sewage in the bathroom .




Draw conclusions

Thus, the Golden Bahia De Tossa & Spa 4 * hotel can be called a great place for a family or a relaxing friendly holiday. According to tourists, the level of service here is fully consistent with the declared price.
