Hotel Atrium Beach 4 * (Bulgaria, Elenite): description, photos, reviews

On the Black Sea coast there is a resort complex Atrium Beach 4 *. Here you can relax surrounded by beautiful nature, entertainment venues and golden beaches.





Hotel Description

The history of the Atrium Beach 4 * Resort Hotel dates back to 2007. This is a complex of 129 luxurious apartments, which are surrounded by a lush blooming garden. The hotel occupies 4600 square meters. The last reconstruction with the renewal of the number of rooms was carried out in 2014.





Location

In one of the most popular tourist places is the Atrium Beach Hotel 4 *. Bulgaria, Elenite, 8259 - the address of this institution. The hotel is about 10 kilometers from the popular resort of Sunny Beach. Burgas International Airport is about 30 km away. As for the beach, it is not more than 100 meters from the hotel.

Hotel Rooms

Atrium Beach 4 * Resort Hotel (Bulgaria, Elenite) provides guests with quite comfortable living conditions. So, guests can stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • The standard rooms can comfortably accommodate two adult guests. At your request, one and a half beds are moved or installed separately. Opposite the bed there is a desk with a TV. A comfortable armchair with a coffee table is located next to the furnished balcony. The room is decorated in neutral pastel colors. You can live in a room with a view of the garden or the sea coast (the second option will cost you a little more).
  • For guests accustomed to luxury and comfort, the Atrium Beach Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria) has prepared luxury apartments. By area, they are almost twice as large as standard rooms. The convenient format of the studio made it possible to combine a bedroom, a living room, and a kitchen area within one living space. The room is always a lot of warmth and light, thanks to the huge panoramic window, which opens before the guests a bewitching seascape.

Hotel Restaurants and Bars

Having been in Bulgaria, you must certainly get acquainted with its culinary traditions. This opportunity will be provided by numerous restaurants and bars operating on the territory of the Atrium Beach Hotel 4 *. So, guests can visit the following institutions:

  • The main restaurant offers guests a good meal. Here, all-inclusive and half-board services are provided. Three times a day, guests can enjoy a huge assortment of dishes served in buffet style.
  • Sozopol is a restaurant serving traditional Bulgarian cuisine. The colorful atmosphere is created not only due to national dishes, but also due to the corresponding interior and folk music.
  • Talyana is an open air restaurant that is open during the summer season. This establishment specializes in barbecue, as well as seafood dishes.
  • The Mediterranean Restaurant will introduce you to all the characteristic features of Mediterranean cuisine. In summer, there is also an outdoor terrace.
  • From the early morning until midnight, guests can indulge in the lobby bar with pastries, various sorts of tea and coffee, as well as a huge assortment of local spirits. Quiet atmosphere and discreet interior contribute to a friendly conversation and business negotiations.
  • Cafe Philipopolis will delight you with fast food. Here you can try pizza, chips, cheese cakes and much more.
  • Galaxy nightclub always has a holiday atmosphere. Here is a wide selection of spirits and many original cocktails.
  • Broadway is an animation hall in which fiery performances take place every evening. While watching the show you can enjoy drinks and snacks.
  • Fregata is a beach bar that serves refreshing drinks and ice creams. If you like to spend all day by the sea, here you can also have a pizza snack.
  • Cozy bar Piano will certainly appeal to lovers of live instrumental music.
  • The Aqua Bar serves drinks and snacks to visitors to the water park.

If you want to have a good time, then you will not be mistaken when choosing Atrium Beach 4 * for your vacation. Elenite resort is famous for its beautiful nature and well-developed infrastructure. Having studied the reviews of tourists, we can highlight the following positive points:

Negative reviews about the hotel Atrium Beach 4 * (Elenite)

Unfortunately, ideal hotels do not exist. Even in the best hotel you can always find flaws and shortcomings. If we talk about the Andalucia Beach Atrium Beach 4 * hotel, then the following negative reviews should be noted:

  • terrible quality of cleaning (we can say that it is practically nonexistent);
  • bed linen is changed very rarely (despite what should happen in a day);
  • only one elevator works, and therefore huge queues always gather near it;
  • from the windows of rooms with sea views you can see only a small stretch of water in the distance;
  • on the beach it is almost impossible to find free sunbeds;
  • in the whole territory and in the halls of the hotel it is very dirty;
  • almost no children's animation; at the beach bar, the food portions are very small (even children are not enough to satisfy their hunger);
  • Despite the fact that many workers know Russian well, they basically do not speak it and pretend that they do not understand;
  • acute shortage of beach towels ;
  • Check-in is done strictly after 15:00 (only in exceptional cases they can go to a meeting and register at two in the afternoon);
  • reception staff treat guests not just indifferently, but also rudely;
  • the procedure for visiting the dining room is quite complicated and lengthy - first you need to take an empty table early, and then stand a huge queue at the distribution;
  • in spite of the fact that it is forbidden to occupy sunbeds in advance, for a small tip the beach caretaker will be allowed to leave his towel on the sun lounger (as a result, the seats are empty and there is nowhere to lie down);
  • the entrance to the sea is a lot of sharp stones;
  • a very narrow coastline, which is occupied by two rows of sun beds standing close to each other;
  • the carpet in the rooms is very ancient and, moreover, it is never cleaned, and therefore you can walk on it only in slippers or slates;
  • in order to achieve the replacement of towels, as well as replenishment of cosmetics, you need to contact the reception several times;
  • due to the huge number of guests, most of whom rest with children, the territory is very noisy;
  • in the restaurants on the tables, old torn tablecloths are laid;
  • in the dining room there are a lot of dishes with chips;
  • some days the restaurant does not serve fruit at all;
  • very loud and unpleasant music plays on the beach;
  • after riding on a roller coaster in the water park there are abrasions on the back;
  • quite often, guests are accommodated in neighboring hotels, citing the fact that there are no empty seats (but in this case there are a lot of questions about the appropriateness of the booking procedure);
  • the bathroom in the room is very cramped and narrow;
  • the balcony is very small, and therefore it is comfortable to be there only for that person;
  • no extra dishes, cosmetics, or towels are provided for the extra guest;
  • public toilets located on the territory are never cleaned at all, and therefore there is always an extremely unpleasant smell near them;
  • in the dining room, salt shakers and pepper shakers are common on three tables;
  • to take a beach towel, you will have to leave a deposit of 10 euros.

General impression

The impression of the Atrium Beach Hotel (Atrium Beach 4 *) is extremely negative. Of the positive aspects, perhaps a lot of greenery in the territory, as well as beautiful lighting in the evening. Of course, with the naked eye it is noticeable that they worked hard on the hotel, making a beautiful design and fresh repairs. But apparently, after the completion of the reconstruction, the hotel is not maintained in good condition (either due to lack of funds, or for some other reasons). As a result, everything around is dirty, sometimes broken.
