Teaching technologies in pedagogy

Teaching technologies in pedagogy are a set of methods, forms, educational tools and teaching methods that are systematically used in the educational process. This is one of the significant ways to influence the learning, education and development of the student. On the other hand, teaching technologies in pedagogy represent a whole science of the methods that a teacher uses to influence students through technical or information tools. Each teacher can be considered the creator of technology, let this creation and will be with borrowings. For a teacher who has learned to handle technology, in his developing state, the main process will be the cognitive process.
