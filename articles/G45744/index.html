How can I control the costs of MTS?

In order to carry out cost control on the room, MTS offers its customers a number of related services and tools. With their help, you can always stay informed about how much money you pay for communication and other additional services. For customers using the Mayak tariff plan, it is also possible to view information about the balance of another MTS subscriber through a USSD request. About how it is possible to control the costs of MTS, what is the price of such services and the conditions for their use will be described in this article.

MTS Internet Cost Control

For users of mobile Internet, regardless of the type of device on which it is connected, data on the remaining traffic is checked in the following ways:

  • through a personal web account (or a similar functionality app for mobile gadgets);
  • through the USSD functionality (in this case, if traffic is used within the framework of the tariff plan, a request of the form * 100 * 1 # will be relevant, for options and Internet packages you should use the combination * 100 * 2 # or * 111 * 217 #.

Get a daily expense summary

Every day, MTS subscribers can receive information on how much money for which services was spent absolutely free of charge. Of course, you should not expect a detailed report: the types of services are grouped and the total cost per day is indicated: for example, SMS messages - 6.00 rubles; 3G Internet - 36.20 rubles. Previously, such a servant was provided under slightly different conditions: it was possible to send a request and find out the cost of five actions. In order to control the expenses of MTS in this way, the combination * 152 * 1 # is used. As mentioned earlier, such requests are not charged and their number is not limited.

MTS roaming expenses control

In order to track expenses outside your region and even your country, you can use the same standard methods: balance check is performed through * 100 #, minutes remaining and other types of services are checked through the previously indicated requests, the user's personal account (as via the web interface, and through a mobile application) is also available when you are in another country (if you have an Internet connection; it is advisable to use wireless networks, since Internet tariffs abroad are quite high).
