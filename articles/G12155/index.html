How to check an account with Sberbank: hotline, Internet, SMS and other ways to check accounts and bonuses

Cash is slowly but surely a thing of the past, becoming part of history. Today, payments in almost all areas of life are made using bank cards. The benefits of such changes are obvious:

  • Unlimited access to your own finances. You can withdraw money from your account using a card at any time of the day and in almost any corner of the world.
  • No need to carry cash when shopping in stores.
  • The ability to pay taxes, fines, utilities and other obligatory payments without leaving home .
  • Transfer of wages and other income to a bank card, thereby eliminating the risk of receiving fake paper bills.
  • Convenience and security when performing settlement operations with large sums of money.
  • Protection from all sorts of scammers. Even if the card is lost or stolen, you can immediately block your account by calling the bank hotline.
  • Cost planning. Removing from the card exactly as much as is necessary for a specific purpose, it is easy to cope with the temptation to spend all the money on a spontaneous purchase.
  • Convenience of making payments while traveling to another country. The presence of a payment card eliminates the need to purchase local currency. It is enough to conduct a cashless conversion operation.
  • The absence of unnecessary problems with customs and the preparation of a declaration when traveling abroad. The maximum allowable amount of cash for export is $ 10,000, and the limit on the card is not limited.
  • A simple and quick way to make a purchase in the online store.
  • The provision of a number of additional services: informing the bank about operations on the account, accruing annual interest on the balance of funds, bonus programs, one-time promotions.
  • The control. At any time, you can get information about the status of your account.

We will consider the last point in more detail using the example of the largest participant in the Russian banking system.

Why is it worth abandoning the passbook?

Savings books - an old banking tool, gradually fading into the past due to the low degree of protection and inconvenient service. Starting from the middle of this year, a partial cancellation of savings books is planned. However, their individual types, for example, for transferring pensions, will be valid for now.

For the older generation, modern innovations logically arouse suspicion and fear. In fairness, it is worth noting that many of them are quite reasonable. For example, the following disadvantages of payment cards are often noted:

  • Technical difficulties in using the terminal. At a considerable age, it is difficult to remember a new algorithm of actions, and even in an automated system. In addition, all banking devices work differently.
  • The need to constantly remember or store the PIN code of the card in written form.

  • Periodic malfunctions in ATMs, for example, card retention. The situation is unpleasant, and if this happened not at the bank branch, but on the street, then it is almost catastrophic.

What to do if the ATM does not give out the card?

There are situations when the ATM captures the card and does not return it back. Why is this happening?

  • The program classified the operation being performed as suspicious (for example, you enter the amount absent on the account several times).
  • The client forgot the PIN code and entered the wrong numbers a certain number of times using the ‚Äúpoke method‚Äù.
  • The client hesitated and did not have time (forgot) to remove the card from the ATM in time.
  • Card is blocked or expired.
  • The card is damaged.
  • There was a communication failure or technical malfunction in the ATM itself. In this case, the corresponding message appears on the monitor. Wait ten minutes. It is likely that the device will recover and your card will be returned.

In all these situations, you should immediately contact the employee of the nearest department, following his instructions further.

General recommendations

Some more useful tips:

  • Do not leave the card unattended. To copy your data from it, a lot of time is not required.
  • Do not tell anyone the PIN code, and most importantly, do not store it with the card.
  • If you have several cards, it is very difficult to remember all the codes without confusing them. Encrypt the combination of numbers in a certain way, for example, add 1 to each digit (your code is 1234, you get 2345). The source code can be written to the card. You know that when entering, one should be subtracted from each digit. And if the card falls into the hands of fraudsters, they will begin to dial the original combination, and the ATM will block it. Encryption options may vary. The main thing is that you yourself remember them.
  • Save the bank hotline number in your telephone directory.
  • Before you withdraw money, make sure that there are no extra cameras and overhead keyboards. Also check that the general information about the bank and its telephone number are posted on the ATM.
  • If you plan a long trip, notify the bank in advance. Since transactions performed in another region may be considered suspicious, which will lead to the blocking of your account.
