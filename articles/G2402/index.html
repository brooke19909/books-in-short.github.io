Hotels in Belokurikha: addresses, reviews, photos

Belokurikha is a great place to relax with the whole family. The resort is located in the Smolensk region, its climate is characterized by mild winters and mild summers. The average summer temperature is 20 degrees, which is incomparable with popular resorts. The predominant calm weather of these parts helps people to temper the body and improve their health.



Hotels of Belokurikha

Popular hotels, the number of which exceeds 30 establishments, are ready to receive guests who want to spend an excellent wellness stay in the south-eastern part of the region. Sanatoria in total accommodate about five thousand people. Belokurikha hotels are especially popular among outdoor enthusiasts. Many agencies are ready to organize trekking along the mountain peaks, which offer stunning views of the nature of Belokurikha.

Due to the rapid pace of development, this place has become one of the most popular ski resorts. High quality of services is confirmed even by experts, awarding Belokurikha with prestigious awards. The variety of housing in these parts is really amazing. What are the best hotels of Belokurikha?

Hotel "Neo"

The most popular tourist destination is the Neo Hotel. It boasts inexpensive prices, high quality service, delicious and varied food. There are four types of rooms to choose from. The budget option is a two-room compact room. Despite the small footage of the room, it can accommodate up to three people in comfort.

A TV with 30 channels will serve as entertainment. Hotel "Neo" has 11 double rooms, which differ from compact alternatives by the presence of a refrigerator and other household appliances. Junior suites have more expensive fittings and a spacious bathtub with a hairdryer. For the most demanding guests, luxury rooms with all the necessary furniture are provided: coffee and dressing tables, a spacious wardrobe and a huge bed, you can order an extra bed if you wish.

Hotel "Neo" has many services that other Belokurikha hotels offer. The cost of the cheapest option starts at 2,000 rubles. The price of a luxury room can be 4400 rubles, and each additional place will cost visitors 800 rubles.



Hotel Bavarenok

The best hotels of Belokurikha are also represented by the Bavarenok hotel - a complex that includes a restaurant, bar and sauna. The hotel can offer visitors 19 first-class rooms with all necessary furniture and appliances. Hotel guests receive a 10 percent discount in the restaurant, whose menu will satisfy the most demanding gourmet. A wide range of dishes complemented by pleasant prices. After a long journey, it's time to have a tasty beer and visit the sauna, which can accommodate four people at a time.

Hotels in Belokurikha have excellent breweries, so you can enjoy your favorite drink in plenty. The cost of one hour in the sauna is 500 rubles. Free use of wireless Internet allows you to stay connected any minute. The cost of three meals a day reaches 600 rubles, and breakfast will cost visitors 150 rubles. The restaurant is open from 10 a.m. to 12 a.m.





Hotel complex "Belovodye"

New Belokurikha hotels can please guests with excellent rooms with first-class renovation and quality food. The hotel complex "Belovodye" is also included in the treasury of the best places for tourists. The hotel is located in the resort area and is perfect for any type of vacation. The hotel organizes spa treatment from two weeks to 21 days. Those wishing to get a wellness stay are invited to go on tours from three days with meals on the buffet system.

The hotel doors are open to all guests. The good-natured staff will make your stay as comfortable as possible. The eight-story hotel building is open to visitors 24 hours a day daily. There are 61 rooms in total. Each client can choose the class that suits him. The cost of the budget option will be 5.5 thousand rubles for a room of 20 square meters with one double or two single beds.

The most expensive hotel room is considered "Grand Suite", which has not only a bedroom, but also a living room and an office. A pleasant trifle will be free accommodation for children up to five years old inclusive. Breakfast, lunch and dinner can be in the restaurant, the price of a meal does not exceed more than 550 rubles. Experienced chefs will delight you with culinary delights.
