Vacuum headphones "Panasonic": types, prices, reviews

Panasonic is a Japanese company specializing in the invention and production of various equipment for the home, garden, production. Corporation products are considered one of the best in manufacturing quality and easy to operate. Headphones "Panasonic", like other products of the Japanese manufacturer, are in no way inferior to the technology of other well-known brands.





Most popular models from Panasonic

The most popular devices for listening to music are wireless Bluetooth headsets, classic in-ear headphones, as well as vacuum headphones. According to statistics, users often prefer products with an average and high price, and products with a lower cost are suspicious. Headphones "Panasonic", having a price higher than average (from 1000 rubles), are distinguished by durability, high sound quality and extensive functionality.

Highly Demand Models:

  • Panasonic Vacuum Headphones: RP-HJE900, Panasonic RP-HJE 125 EA.
  • Semi-closed and closed wired headphones: Panasonic RP-HTX7, Panasonic RP-DJ600, Panasonic RP-HT 161 EK.
  • In-Ear Earphones: Panasonic RP-TCM50E, Panasonic RP-HJE355E (channel type), Panasonic RP-HV094GU-K.
  • Overhead: Panasonic RP-HS46E-K.
  • Premium Bluetooth Wireless On-Ear Headphones.
  • Headphones used with DECT format phones: Panasonic RP-TCA400E-K.

Panasonic Vacuum Headphones - Comfortable and Ergonomic

The RP-HJE900 model is distinguished by a special sound depth, price / quality ratio, durability, and sensitivity. Thanks to the replaceable cable, the headphones have a long service life. Of the minuses, consumers note the high cost of the device: due to the cancellation of product supplies in the United States, the price of headphones today is from 10,000 to 17,000 rubles.





Another minus: the case of the device consists of a zirconium alloy, which makes the headphones heavy. According to reviews, the RP-HJE900 has a cord that is not long enough and inconvenient cable bend stops.

The Panasonic RP-HJE 125 EA wired headphones have a capacity of 200 mW. Compared with devices from other manufacturers, the RP-HJE 125 has several advantages:

  • high power (200 mW);
  • durability;
  • wide color gamut;
  • good sensitivity (97 dB);
  • low cost.

The RP-HJE 125 model (Panasonic headphones are vacuum), the price of which varies between 400-600 rubles, is considered an excellent analogue of more expensive sound devices.



Wired headphones

Panasonic RP-HTX7 is a rugged and compact device. At first glance, it seems that the bowls of the device are small and will not be able to cover the ear of an adult. But this is far from the case. Headphones "Panasonic" tightly fit the auricle, but have not too good sound insulation due to the characteristics of the material used to make the lining. On the head, according to consumers, they hold comfortably, do not fall off, but because of the rigid structure, discomfort appears after a few hours of wear. Sound reproduction at the RP-HTX7 headphone at a height: low and high frequencies are perfectly traced, bass is well felt.





Cons of the device: weak average sounds. Pros: reasonable cost (headphones are in the middle price category), high-quality sound. Price - from 1500 to 2500 rubles.

The RP-DJ600, according to most reviews, is considered one of the best in the line of Panasonic wired headphones. Their advantages:





  • good sound insulation;
  • light weight (205 grams);
  • excellent bass reproduction;
  • reasonable price - from 1200 to 1600 rubles.

Headphones "Panasonic RP-HT 161" - an excellent analogue of studio devices. They have a capacity of 1000 mW, equipped with a convenient long cable, and are durable. The manufacturer claims that the model RP-HT 161 has a large range of reproducible frequencies - from 10 to 27000 hertz. This is confirmed by almost all device users. The average price is from 700 to 1000 rubles, depending on the store selling the goods.





Inserts

The Panasonic RP-TCM50E is a headset that works with smartphones on virtually any platform. This is one of the best headphones from the Japanese manufacturer. Light weight, ease of use, as well as low cost make the RP-TCM50E demanded and popular. The cost of headphones ranges from 400 to 650 rubles.





Panasonic RP-HJE355E - a device that combines the advantages of "earbuds" and vacuum headphones. High wear resistance, excellent sound quality and comfortable to wear - these, as consumers say, are the main advantages of the model. A rich sound (from low to high sounds), a stylish appearance and a snug fit to the auricle - not every more expensive device boasts such characteristics. The price of Panasonic RP-HJE355E varies between 700-1000 rubles.





Headphones "Panasonic RP-HV094GU-K" - a simple and cheap device. According to reviews, they are durable, wear-resistant. The model, despite its low cost, has a high frequency range - from 20 to 20,000 hertz. Of the minuses, it is worth noting the low power (40 mW), as well as discomfort during wear. The average price of a device is from 90 to 150 rubles.





Headphones for phones and PCs

Panasonic RP-HS46E-K is a great option for sports. Lightweight, compact earphones that fit snugly around the auricle. Judging by the reviews, they are fixed tightly on the ears, do not protrude, but the sound still leaves much to be desired. Without additional pressing, bass and high sounds are poorly audible, while medium ones are perfectly reproduced. The price of this device varies between 400-600 rubles.





Premium Bluetooth Wireless On-Ear Headphones - perhaps the best Panasonic wireless headphones

The model first appeared on the market for sound devices in May 2014 and immediately received a positive assessment. Convenient control buttons located on one of the headphones, as well as the rugged design of the device, make its high cost justified.





Benefits:

  • Built-in program for voice dialing, accepting / rejecting a call, repeating a number.
  • Ability to connect via cable.
  • It works at a distance of up to 10 meters.
  • Light weight (198 grams).
  • Long time in listening to music and talking (30 hours).
  • A moisture resistant material was used to manufacture the headphone membrane, preventing moisture from entering the device.
  • Ergonomics
  • High-quality sound transmission from media to headphones.

The cost of the device is from 6000 to 8000 rubles.

Headset used with DECT phones





Headphones for Panasonic RP-TCA400E-K are compatible, among other things, with analog and digital systems, as well as DECT-format telephones. The peculiarity of the device is that during a conversation almost 100% of external noise is completely blocked. The advantages include the following characteristics:

  • simplicity in operation;
  • light weight;
  • the ability to mute the microphone.

The cost of the RP-TCA400E-K is from 900 to 1200 rubles.

Where is better to get Panasonic headphones

The most optimal purchase option is to purchase the goods directly from an official supplier or in a store (both physical and online), specializing in the sale of household appliances. When buying, you should make sure that the device is actually manufactured at the Panasonic factory, since you can often find fake sold under a well-known brand.

Reviews about headphones from a Japanese manufacturer

Headphones "Panasonic", reviews of which are mostly positive, are of high quality performance, reasonable cost, availability and long life. Negative on the part of users arises due to breakdowns of the most vulnerable places: plugs (jacks, mini-jacks), cable bends, wires moving away in the case, often manifested due to the constant, frequent, and sometimes incorrect use of devices.
