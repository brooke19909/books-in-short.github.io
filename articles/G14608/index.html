Ibis Hotel (Nizhny Novgorod) - comfortable rest all year round

One of the most comfortable hotels in Nizhny Novgorod - "Ibis" - is located in the central part of the city. The Volka River flows nearby and the Novgorod Kremlin was built. Within walking distance are a tram stop, Gorky Square, Kulibin Park, a river port, shopping pavilions, business congress centers, cinemas, and major attractions.

The IBIS network belongs to the ibis hotel. Nizhny Novgorod is the largest city where tourists can immerse themselves in the world of ancient monuments and exciting adventures. A variety of entertainments, unusual show programs and interesting excursion routes are prepared for guests, which will not leave you indifferent.

Number of rooms

220 modern rooms are concentrated in a nine-story hotel building. For couples are offered to accommodate large two-room premises of 35 square meters. For the comfort of tourists, the rooms are equipped with necessary furniture and appliances. For individual use, there is a bathroom equipped with a shower cabin, a bathroom and hygiene products.





From the windows, guests can watch amazing city attractions. Scheduled cleaning and linen change. Satellite TV is also available. There is a direct dial telephone for communication with the administration. Installed split system with the function of heat / cold. Private rooms for people with any physical ailments are also offered by the ibis hotel (Nizhny Novgorod).

Nutrition

Delicious breakfasts are served daily in the dining room. Hotel "Ibis" (Nizhny Novgorod) offers a restaurant Sud-Cie. Highly qualified chefs will delight you with wonderful dishes in the spirit of Mediterranean cuisine. Rende Zvous Bar is open 24 hours a day, here you can have a tasty snack and a glass of red wine.





Additional Information

The Ibis Hotel (Nizhny Novgorod) offers comfortable conditions for tourists arriving by their own cars - there are two open parking lots and an indoor parking nearby. Free internet is available throughout and in the rooms. For seminars, conferences and business meetings, six technically equipped rooms are provided.

There is a meeting room with 120 seats with audio and video equipment. Hotel staff can help organize large events. The Ibis Hotel (Nizhny Novgorod) has an exchange office and a left-luggage office. Dry cleaning / laundry service is available.





Visit the religious Annunciation and Pechersk monasteries, the temple of Alexander Nevsky, numerous museums, theaters, elite night clubs - all this is offered to you by Nizhny Novgorod. The Ibis Hotel boasts a convenient central location. The complex is designed for an audience of different ages.

Everyone will find activities to their liking and interests. Low prices combined with European service will delight you. Reviews are proof of this. High-quality service and attentive attitude are the main priorities of the hotel.
