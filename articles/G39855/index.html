Hotel Gelidonya Park Magic Dream Hotel - rest and excursions in the territory of ancient Lycia

A virtual trip illuminating modern pensions will take you to the resort town of Kemer (Turkey), located at a distance of 50 km from Antalya.

Consider the four-star second-line hotel Gelidonya Park Magic Dream Hotel. The exterior of the only four-story building is decorated with a mezzanine. Its characteristic smooth contours and wide panoramic windows resemble a moored steamer with a captain‚Äôs bridge. Renovation and modernization took place here in 2013. The boarding house has 80 standard rooms equipped with a bathroom and a TV. The lobby area has Wi-Fi. A modern conference room is equipped with the necessary office equipment.

The hotel complex is located a 20-minute walk from the city center. To get to the beach from the Magic Dream Hotel, you must cross the road, and then move about 7 minutes past several hotels to the coast. Beach holidays here are quite consistent with 4-5-star category: clear water, pebbles and sand. However, walking barefoot will not work: the southern sun actively heats the surface. Recreation areas are equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas.

A good corner for relaxation is the hotel‚Äôs spacious pool with harmonious smooth outlines of the bowl.

Here is a special area for bathing children. Nearby there is a bar offering juices, cocktails, fruits according to the "all inclusive" system.

The boarding house has a gym. Bath lovers can choose between a sauna and a hammam. Guests use the services of experienced masseurs.

Meals are organized by a hotel restaurant and two bars. However, often only one of them works, which is located by the pool. Admittedly, the menu is extensive. The team of chefs Gelidonya Park Magic Dream Hotel professionally does its job. Guests will find an abundance of meat and vegetable dishes, and a scattering of fruits.



In addition to passive relaxation on the beach, during the day it is advisable to diversify your stay in Kemer with excursions. A unique corner of nature can be seen when going to the Goynuk Canyon for an excursion. By the way, lovers of beautiful landscapes can be offered at the Greenwood Resort Kemer * 4 hotel located in the eponymous canyon . You will see two 14-kilometer ridges of impressive three-hundred-meter-high cliffs hanging over a narrow valley along which a shallow stream flows. Transparent jets of water slide down the mountains from the waterfall, filling the surrounding space with coolness. But do not forget to take comfortable shoes: you have to go along a shallow stream.

Most excursions are historical. This land played a prominent role in the ancient world, called the kingdom of Lycia. The latter is mentioned in the annals as a faithful strategic ally of the fantastic warrior Alexander of Macedon, who conquered 30 kingdoms and founded 30 cities over 33 years of his life. After driving just 15 kilometers from the Gelidonya Park Magic Dream Hotel, you will see the ruined city of Phaselis, where this warrior, referred to in the East as Iskander Zulkarnayn, is buried.

An unforgettable trip will be to the archaeological pearl of Turkey (the deserted city of Xanthos, where King Sarpedon is buried).

His warriors fought shoulder to shoulder, defending Troy. According to the chronicles, this legendary ruler of Lycia, called the son of Zeus and Europe, the supreme god measured the triple duration of life. He died from the sword of Petroclus under Troy.

Relaxation at the Gelidonya Park Magic Dream Hotel attracts with a healing climate, a crystal-clear sea, vivid impressions, courtesy of the servants, positive attitude of the staff: cooks, animators, sports instructors.
