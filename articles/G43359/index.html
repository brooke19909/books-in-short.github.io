Thanks to its wide sandy beaches, sparkling water, a huge marina and magnificent comfortable hotels, it has become very famous and popular. In 2013, on the seashore in the village of Sykhadzhik-Seferihisar, a chic hotel for family and youth holidays appeared under the name Euphoria Aegean Resort Spa 5 *. It is located 6 kilometers from the center of the village, 35 km from Izmir airport, 22 km from the center of Izmir and 80 km from the city of Kusadasi.
