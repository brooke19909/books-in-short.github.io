What it is?

At the moment, in Russia there is one very important rule: all drivers for transport pay the corresponding annual fee. It is called the transport tax. This is the payment of funds by the owner of the car for its registration in the country.

When will the transport tax be canceled in Russia? The question is difficult. Indeed, at the moment, drivers who have row boats (and motor boats) are completely exempted from this. True, the power of the engines of such funds should not exceed 5 horsepower.

Transport for the carriage of passengers owned by a private entrepreneur, as well as sea and river fishing vessels, agricultural vehicles owned by producers, as well as federal vehicles, airplanes and helicopters of medical services - all this is also not subject to taxes. Therefore, the drivers of the listed vehicles may not think when they will cancel the transport tax in Russia. This change will not affect them in any way.
