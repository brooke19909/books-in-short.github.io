How to make a lego base? Putting together the foundation for future games

The base is the main component of most of the further buildings. Its construction will not be difficult if you have large sets of constructor.





  1. First, we determine for what purpose we are building a base - for building a new great city or for warships. The material and details of which the base will be made, as well as its color, depend on this.
  2. So that the base does not fall apart in the hands along the route from the table to the floor, and vice versa, it is better to choose ready-made fundamental slabs or mesh bases for construction. The former are specifically designed for bulky buildings and significantly reduce assembly time. Mesh bases will help if the required color is not enough to cover the fundamental slabs or if you need to create different levels of the structure.
  3. The basis, depending on the chosen building, can be created in relief. To do this, we select the tiles of the desired size and color and connect among themselves in the sequence that is necessary for the selected landscape. Its edges can be uneven, roundish and convex. Turn on the fantasy.
