The model is sold in a rather modest (by design standards) box of white and thick cardboard. Inside the box you will see:

  • rechargeable battery;
  • protective glasses for smartphones NTS (optional);
  • 8 GB memory card;
  • stereo headset with additional pads;
  • detailed manual in Russian;

Basically, nothing special - a kit as a kit. But sometimes poor-quality charges (squelchy and backlash) or safety glasses for NTS smartphones may be missing, so take the time to open the box in the store and check the contents.

If one could compare this smartphone with a car, then the EBO would be a classic SUV. That is, not in the sense that it can be easily taken on any impassability and deserts, but on the impressions of the design.





The smartphone turned out to be massive, heavy and inspiring confidence. No, the gadget does not exceed the usual comfort limits in terms of weight or overall dimensions - it just inferior to third-party competitors in the thinness of the case and, perhaps, in lightness. Nevertheless, the characteristics of the case of HTC EVO 3D do not allow calling it a "brick" or another offensive word.

The appearance of the device has strict notes and, as it happens with the NTS models, this attracts its client. Dark gray and pure black plastic tones act as a key to the hearts of potential consumers, responding with a business and serious echo.

Interfaces

The back of the device is a cover that completely captures the perimeter of the entire device. Just above the central part of the gadget, you can see a paired massive camera unit with two flashes on high-power LEDs.





On the left side is only one micro-USB-connector. On the right side of the smartphone, you can see the camera control button, volume rocker and the shooting mode switch (2D / 3D). The upper part of the gadget is reserved for the headphone and headset jack (3.5 mm) and the power control key, as well as a backup microphone. Below is a recess for removing the cover and the main microphone of the HTC EVO 3D.

The characteristics of the case and the design of the model as a whole leave positive emotions.
