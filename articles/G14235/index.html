Kalofer 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): hotel description, services, reviews

Since the days of the Soviet Union, Bulgaria has been one of the most popular tourist destinations among our compatriots. Spend your vacation in this country today is very nice. Bulgaria pleases everyone with its beautiful nature, amazing places that you can visit on your own or with excursions.

There is also a warm Black Sea, the cozy beaches of which annually with joy welcome many tourists from all over the world. Holidays in it will surely appeal to many who are not looking for pompous and luxurious options.

Holidays in Bulgaria

Washed by the waters of the Black Sea, Bulgaria is one of the favorite places of many Europeans and Russians. People come here to relax with the whole family, enjoy the warm climate and even heal.

Not only summer holidays are popular in this country. Many winter sports enthusiasts spend their winter vacations here, as there are a large number of ski resorts in the local mountains. They are also more attractive in price than many European ski centers, but at the same time here you can get great impressions and a lot of positive emotions.





In the sea resorts of this country throughout the summer there is a beautiful and warm weather. Sunny Beach, for example, pleases its guests with temperatures approaching 30 degrees above zero Celsius, as well as an abundance of clear sunny days. Other Black Sea resorts in Bulgaria also delight guests with beautiful weather and excellent water, in which you can literally swim without a shower.

Resort Sunny Beach

Arriving at the resort of Sunny Beach (Bulgaria), tourists find themselves in a magnificent place, which is suitable for recreation for both youth and couples of all ages, and children with parents.

The length of the sandy beaches of this place is 7 kilometers, and they are located north of the city of Burgas for 30 kilometers. Every year, the beaches here deservedly receive the Blue Flag, because thanks to the distance from highways, large cities and railways, they remain clean and comfortable.

Undoubtedly, the weather and the people here are happy. Sunny Beach is literally created for a relaxing summer vacation, enjoying the warm sea and the hot sun, under which many people like to sunbathe.





For accommodation on the Black Sea coast there are a large number of hotels that are located both near the sea and closer to the city center.

Nice hotel Kalofer

The Bulgarian hotel Kalofer 3 * (Sunny Beach) was built in 2005. A year later, repairs were carried out here, and in 2016 a partial renovation was also organized.

In a small area owned by the hotel, there is one building, conditionally divided into 3 buildings. Two of them have 3 floors, and in the central part of the building - 7 floors. The hotel building is equipped with two elevators, on the ground floor there is a spacious reception where check-in of guests takes place. By the way, it starts at 2 p.m., but departure must be done no later than 12 noon. The hotel has the possibility of late check-out, however, this service is provided for a fee and is possible only if the hotel has available rooms at this time.

Although the adjacent territory at the Kalofer 3 * hotel (Bulgaria) is small, on the other hand there are pretty flowerbeds and a pool with sunbeds around it. Of course, there are few entertainments in the hotel, but thanks to them, guests will be able to diversify their vacation and have a great time free from swimming and sunbathing.





Tourists who have chosen the Kalofer hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) for their vacation should be prepared for the fact that they will have to take a little walk to the sea, since the hotel is located on the third beach line.

Great hotel location

And although proximity to the beach is not the strongest point of this hotel, otherwise its location is very good.

The hotel is located directly in the central part of Sunny Beach: you will only have to walk 300 meters to it. On the one side of Kalofer Hotel 3 * there is a rather large and noisy road, but not many rooms go to it. On the other hand, the windows overlook a quiet and cozy street, as well as the hotel‚Äôs own pool.

And at a distance of only 1.5 kilometers is an ancient city called Nessebar, which is also very interesting for tourists to visit.

How tourists get to Kalofer

The hotel is only 30 kilometers from the airport of the Bulgarian city of Burgas. This distance is quickly covered by public transport or taxi. Only 30-40 minutes of the trip, and guests will be able to check in the purchased rooms.

Guest rooms

The choice of type of accommodation at the Kalofer Hotel (Bulgaria) is limited to only three types of rooms. Guests have at their disposal standard rooms, superior rooms and spacious apartments.





There are 55 standard rooms in the hotel. They have an area of ‚Äã‚Äãapproximately 18 square meters and are designed to accommodate a maximum of two guests. There are two single beds set along a long wall and separated by bedside tables, a wardrobe, a TV, air conditioning, a telephone, a desk with a chair, a mirror, a refrigerator with a minibar, and a bathroom with a shower. Each room also has a balcony with a special table and chairs.

There are 66 superior rooms in the hotel. Their area is larger than the standard, and they are designed to accommodate a maximum of four people. There are two single beds, which, if desired, can be shifted together, and a folding sofa. Otherwise, the equipment of rooms of this type is no different from the standard category.

The most spacious accommodation options available at the Kalofer 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach) are apartments. In total there are four of them in the hotel, four of them can comfortably live in each of them. These rooms are distinguished by the fact that guests have at their disposal two separate rooms. One of them has a bedroom with a double bed, two bedside tables, a dressing table with a chair, a wardrobe for everything you need. Another room is a cozy living room where you can sit on a sofa or in an armchair, use a coffee table, sit at a small dining table in one of several chairs. A balcony in this type of room is also larger than the others, but it also has special outdoor furniture.

Like any other currency of a foreign country, the Bulgarian lev has an unstable rate against the Russian ruble. This rate changes daily, but on average one lion costs 34 rubles. Hotel catering

Each room at Kalofer Hotel includes breakfast. It is served daily in the dining room located on the ground floor of the main building. Guests are offered a variety of dishes according to the buffet system, so no guest will definitely leave here hungry in the morning. It is also possible to order dinners, the cost of which is 15 lions per adult and 7.5 lions per child.





There is also an a la carte restaurant on site, where you can taste delicious and delicious dishes of both local and international cuisine. There is also an outdoor bar where you can order any drink for some money.

What can hotel guests do

As mentioned above, Kalofer is a fairly small hotel with a small surrounding area. However, it has as many as two pools. One is designed for adult swimming, it even has a hydromassage compartment. Around him are plastic sun loungers and several umbrellas, which in the heat can create a shadow from the burning sun. The second is designed specifically for children. It is small in size, shallow, and there is a slide adjoining it, on which you can have fun rolling directly into the water.

On the ground floor of the building, near the reception, there is a billiard table and table football, there is also a ping-pong table, which, if necessary, can be put on and play tennis for your own pleasure.





If guests want to relax, they can use the services of a local small spa. It can use massage services, visit the bath or sauna, sit in the jacuzzi. There is also a modest fitness room with the necessary equipment, so those who keep track of their sports form will also find something to do here.

Song lovers can enjoy the opportunity to sing karaoke with their friends. And with the onset of darkness, anyone can light up at a hotel disco, which will be a great end to the day for young people.

In public areas of the hotel, for example, near the reception, there is wireless Internet. There is a currency exchange kiosk, so it‚Äôs not necessary to come to the hotel with a pack of Bulgarian lions.

Conference Opportunities





Not only a place to relax at sea is Bulgaria. Hotel Kalofer 3 * will be happy to provide everyone who is interested in the opportunity to hold a business meeting, presentation or any other important conference. For this, the hotel has two conference halls of different capacities. One of them is suitable for an audience of 80 people, the other will fit 50 guests.

Both of these rooms are equipped with all the necessary equipment, which may only be needed during a business event. It has the most advanced video and audio systems, microphones and computers.

Good impressions of the Kalofer Hotel

Not only our compatriots, but also guests from other countries come to rest at the Kalofer 3 * hotel. Reviews about this place mostly agree that the hotel is excellent in terms of price and quality.

The breakfasts and the rest of the food in the local restaurants are quite tasty and varied. In the rooms all the furniture is quite new, everything is whole and clean. The staff at the hotel is friendly and welcoming, only barely speaks Russian. Vacationers also like the location of the hotel, because it is convenient to get to the beach, shops, and the city center. For those who do not set themselves the goal of sitting at the hotel for days on end, accommodation here is a great option.

What hotel guests do not like





Of the minuses, tourists note central air conditioning, which cannot be adjusted independently. The hotel also has some problems with housekeeping and pool cleaning, which are not always regular. Our compatriots in their reviews report that it is best to remind about cleaning the room at the reception, then the cleaners will soon come and do their job. There is not enough internet for vacationers, which could be used throughout the hotel, including in rooms, as well as beach towels, which are not provided here.
