Budva (Montenegro)

On the coast of the azure Adriatic coast lies the small but most beautiful city of Montenegro - Budva. If you are planning a trip to this country, be sure to visit this cute picturesque corner.

This is a real resort city where there will be no time to miss, especially young people, because life in this city is seething all day. Budva (Montenegro) is unique and loved by tourists. A taxi ride from the airport to the town will cost about 15 euros, and the journey will take only 15 minutes.

You need to look for such beautiful, snow-white, green beaches! Amazingly harmoniously, nature created an extraordinary blueness of the sea and piercingly bright sky. Budva (Montenegro) - these are elegant, very neat villas, hotels and, of course, the incomparable green mountain landscapes.

The sun warms this town gently and gently, that's where you can have a great time. City beaches are mostly paid, but with cozy tents, gazebos and parasols. There are several of them, but Rafailovici and Becici are the most calm, and the sea here is surprisingly clean. The Slovenian and Mogren beaches are considered to be elite, and therefore the entrance here can be by special passes.

Montenegro (Budva) vacation offers a variety of, including exciting water activities: jet skis, fast catamarans. On land, a popular beach volleyball awaits you. By the way, there is also great entertainment - fishing. One has only to rent a small boat, and the catch is provided.

Every traveler considers it obligatory to visit the city of Budva (Montenegro), because, despite the small size of the town, there are many remarkable places. The population of this city is 4.5 thousand inhabitants. Undoubtedly, they are very happy people, because they live in such a fertile land.

Budva (Montenegro) is an ancient street, diverse in architecture of the building and everywhere the sun. The city looks elegant, the walls of the houses are white, and the roofs are terracotta-colored. All this, together with the blue sea and sky, creates an unusually vivid picturesque picture. This is a very suitable place for family vacations - the country of Montenegro. The city of Budva has prepared a surprise for lovers of antiquities.

Want to get into the 15th century? Then you are in the Old Town, which is surrounded by walls built in the great Renaissance. In this part of the city in the Citadel fortress there is a stage where a theater festival takes place. To get on it means to enjoy the art of actors on the ancient historical stage.

The church of Santa Marina de Punte is no less remarkable. It represents the center of musical culture, since the temple has unique acoustics and an unrealistically beautiful interior. In general, Montenegro, the city of Budva in particular, is famous for its churches.

For example, the church of St. Sava the Illuminated was built at the turn of the 15th century, here pilgrims come from here. Be sure to check out the Holy Trinity Church, which has no equal in beauty.
