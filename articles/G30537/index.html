Good recreation center: Novosibirsk, Dubrovino

One of the most popular tourist camps in Novosibirsk is Dubrovino. This comfortable complex offers residents of the city and the region inexpensive, well-equipped rooms and many amenities on site.

General description of the complex

What attracts vacationers to this relatively small recreation center? Novosibirsk (Dubrovino is quite far from it) is a large and crowded city. And of course there are many convenient bases in its vicinity. Dubrovino is popular with tourists primarily because it offers its guests relatively inexpensive rooms. Vacationers are placed at this base in houses built of natural wood, equipped with a Russian stove.





Each such building is designed to receive from 4 to 12 people. There is also a sleeping complex on the base, designed to accommodate small companies (2-3 people each).

The complex itself is located on the banks of the Ob River in a very picturesque place. Including, therefore, this recreation center is, of course, popular with vacationers. Novosibirsk - Dubrovino - the route, however, is quite long (90 km). It will take at least 1.5 hours to get to the base from the city by car.

Number of rooms

Tourists who have rented rooms in the houses of the base can, among other things, use:

  • double or single beds;

  • washbasins;

  • mini fridges;

  • teapots;

  • infrared heaters.

Next to each house is a heated and lighted wooden toilet. In the bedroom building, a refrigerator, kettle and microwave are shared.

Base infrastructure

Dubrovino (recreation center, Novosibirsk) offers its guests a comfortable stay, of course, not only in the rooms, but also directly on the premises. Guests of the center, among other things, can use here:

  • Russian bath;

  • a sports ground.

There is a well-equipped children's town on the territory of the complex. If desired, guests of the base can also visit the shooting range. Of course, there is no outdoor pool in this center. However, on its territory there is a small pond. Tourists can park their car in free parking.





Entertainment

Of course, not only a comfortable stay in the rooms and on the territory this recreation center offers its guests. Novosibirsk (Dubrovino is located on the banks of the river) is a city in which, like in any other, many fishing enthusiasts live. Tourists, of course, have the opportunity to organize their leisure time at this base, including with a fishing rod. In the center, among other things, there is a fishing instructor. You can rent on the territory of the base and all the equipment necessary for fishing.

In the dormitory building of the center, among other things, there are two banquet rooms. If desired, they can be rented to celebrate a birthday, wedding, corporate party, etc. There is a karaoke room at the base. Also, tourists have the opportunity to visit the billiard room or ride horses.

Leisure for children

The city whose residents most often visit this recreation center is Novosibirsk. "Dubrovino" is located far from it, as already mentioned. However, many tourists, of course, still come here with their children. For younger guests, the base also provides many additional amenities. On the playground equipped in the complex, children can:

  • to ride "cheesecakes" from the hill;

  • jump on inflatable attractions.

Entertainment is, unfortunately, not free. However, they are relatively inexpensive. For the rental of "cheesecakes" at the base, they take about 100 rubles per hour, for attractions - 1,500 rubles per hour.





Food Based

Dubrovino (recreation center, Novosibirsk) received good reviews from vacationers, including the fact that you can order delicious dishes for breakfast, lunch or dinner in the local dining room. The dining room at the base has its own. The food in this place, according to most tourists, is very good. The dining room menu includes mainly Russian dishes. Food is prepared on the basis of an experienced chef from the freshest products.

In addition, in the Dubrovino complex, tourists can rent a gazebo with barbecue or a cauldron. Such a service is based on a relatively inexpensive one - about 350 rubles per hour.
