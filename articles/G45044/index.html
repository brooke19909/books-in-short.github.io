We plan a vacation together. Sights of Georgia

Sights of Georgia ... Admit, because you have often heard the rave reviews of those who have already managed to visit this country? What attracts travelers there? What makes tourists, having arrived once, forever fall in love with this corner of the planet?

Section 1. Sights of Georgia. General description of the object



Georgia is a stunningly beautiful country, the local flavor is literally mesmerizing. It can take a month to see all the sights, and it still turns out that it was not possible to visit most of them. No wonder the period of stay on the territory of the country to Russian citizens was extended to ninety days.

Holidays in Georgia are very diverse. Travelers can choose from climate resorts, sports tourism, sightseeing tours and much more. There is a place for families with children, newlyweds and elderly couples. How many times have you heard: "Ah, Georgia ... Tbilisi ... Sights worth seeing at least once in your life!"

If you love mountains, then go straight to Svaneti. Gourmets and lovers of Georgian wines should like it in Kakheti. And tourists who prefer the sea, palm trees and other exotic, await in Adjara.

Section 2. Sights of Georgia. Choose a tour



A few kilometers from Tbilisi is the Betania Monastery . The church in the shape of a cross was built in honor of the Virgin in the XI century and to this day is active. Here you can see frescoes depicting the royal family, the altar and ancient icons. Tradition has it that this was the favorite place of Queen Tamara.

Not far from the village of Vardzia there is a wonderful cave monastery, built nine centuries ago. It is also called the "Castle of the Roses". Initially, it was planned to build a defensive fortress on this site. And now it is a real multi-story underground city with secret passages and living rooms.

The most popular monument in Georgia is Jvari. The monastery of the Holy Cross is located in the ancient capital Mtskheta. Located on the very edge of a high cliff, it seems to be a continuation of it. The monastery feels the complete harmony of severity and simplicity, tranquility and pacification.

Batumi, located on the Black Sea coast, will meet you with the warm southern sun and fresh sea air. In addition to excellent service, pebble beaches and palm trees, vacationers have the opportunity to enjoy the sound of the surf and the splashing waves.

The highlight of Batumi is a French fountain. In the evenings, tourists and locals admire the real dance of water, accompanied by bright lights and fiery music. This place is as if saturated with romance and vitality.

And just recently, another fountain opened - the Chacha Tower. Its peculiarity is that instead of water, jets of real grape vodka fly out of it. Those interested can not only admire its beauty, but also taste the chacha.

Section 3. Sights of Georgia. Ecological tourism



First of all, they go to Georgia to enjoy the beauties of the local nature, breathe in the clean mountain air, and also relax in the famous resorts with mineral waters.

People come to Borjomi, Gudauri, Utser or Akhtal to support their health. But Adjara, located on the coast of the sea at the foot of the Caucasus Mountains , enjoys greater popularity .
