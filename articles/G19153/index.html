Scarlet Sails Park in Voronezh for family vacations. Address, reviews

The left-bank region of the city of Voronezh, which is located on the banks of a wide reservoir, is very quiet and picturesque. This area is planted with tall pine trees, which give coolness to the townspeople in the hot summer. Here is one of the most beautiful green areas of the region - the Scarlet Sails Park. Our material will tell about the history and features of this square.

Scenic Area

Most of the garden compositions appeared on this territory with the beginning of the reign of Soviet power. It has become a good tradition to plant new squares and solemnly open them.





This happened in Voronezh. Those who visited this city before the collapse of the USSR remember exactly the interesting and beautiful green town. The park began its history on July 8, 1975. The site for the object was chosen very well: the shore of the reservoir was planted with lush and tall pines. These trees today delight the townspeople with a shady coolness in the summer. Another advantage was the Voronezh Sea. On the shore of the reservoir, people swam and sunbathed. But in the early 1990s, sanitary services banned swimming, as the quality of the water did not meet the norm. Since then, fishing here has been exclusively of sporting interest.

New breath

After the collapse of the Soviet Union, the park began to decline. A beautiful and quiet square awaited several years of indifference. In 2009, the object became municipal property.

A year later, the square began to come to life. On its territory held various festivals and competitions for citizens. But the complex needed a general reconstruction and updating. Therefore, in 2011, restoration work began.

And already on September 15, the Scarlet Sails Park was inaugurated. This event was dedicated to the 425th anniversary of the founding of the city of Voronezh. Olivier Dame worked on the composition. This is a French landscape designer and architect, working as an adviser in the Ministry of Culture of his native country. Thanks to the light hand of the master, the park is characterized by Parisian airiness and luxury. Then the professional set about another Voronezh square - Dynamo.

A quiet harbor

Since the park was updated, the Left Bank district of Voronezh has been a favorite place for families. Here, both children and their parents can find entertainments to their liking.

You can have fun at the sports fields. They play football, basketball and volleyball in the park. You can compete for free on tennis tables. In winter, the stadium turns into an ice rink. Children can play in sandboxes, on slides and carousels. Visitors spend interesting time on the dance floor. There is a convenient open-air cinema.

Along the alleys are benches where you can calmly sit and watch nature. In the park, a man rests from the bustle of the city. The fact that in the territory of this small reserve it is forbidden to drink alcohol, smoke, use foul language and be intoxicated is also pleasing to visitors. And guests of the complex note that on weekdays they observe these rules. But this, unfortunately, can not be said about the holiday dates.

Versatile relaxation

The territory of the park is uneven. This 6.2 hectare plot of land is covered with hills and steep slopes. But they are very convenient for travel.





Practicing and mass festivities Scarlet Sails Park. Voronezh celebrates City Day in a special way. Famous musicians are invited to this square, and the holiday ends with a traditional salute.

You can sit calmly and comfortably in the chess house, which is also known as the library. Many visitors come here with their books. There is also internet. But guests of the park note that the signal is very weak. In addition, modern phones do not hold energy for a long time, and there is nowhere to recharge the gadget. If you prefer outdoor activities, you can ride bicycles in the alleys.

For fans of history in the center are stands with information about the different periods of the city's development.

Sights of the complex

Everyone likes the Scarlet Sails Park (Voronezh). The complex is open from 7 a.m. to 11 p.m.
