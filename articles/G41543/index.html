Soyuz Bank: customer reviews, services, services and interest rates

This financial institution was established in 1993, at that time it was established in the form of joint-stock commercial bank Alina Moscow. However, in 1999 the bank acquired one of the largest insurance companies, Ingosstrakh, in the country, in connection with which the institution was renamed the commercial joint-stock bank into Ingosstrakh-Soyuz. In 2003, it included other commercial organizations - Sibregion Bank, Avtovazbank and Halyk Savings Bank. Customer reviews about Soyuz Bank are presented at the end of the article.

Subsequently, the financial institution was given the name Soyuz Bank OJSC. Today, the main owner of the bank is the insurance company Ingosstrakh, which owns 95.86% of the bank's shares.

Sometimes in customer reviews of Soyuz Bank it is noted that this is not the largest financial institution in Russia. Nevertheless, at the moment it has 36 territorial divisions, which are located in different parts of the country, including in some large cultural and industrial cities, such as Tyumen, Novosibirsk, Rostov-on-Don, Moscow, Krasnoyarsk, Nizhny Novgorod , Samara and others.

The main specialization is the provision of retail financial services to private and corporate clients, medium and small businesses.

The customer base of the bank includes all categories of customers. Soyuz Bank offers many products. Priority areas of activity are working with various business sectors, as well as providing services to special customers. For legal entities, there is a wide range of standard financial products, a large number of banking operations. Soyuz Bank offers private clients loans, deposits, money transfers, plastic cards, and Internet banking.

A network of branches of Bank Soyuz has been developed, which is represented by 36 branches in various cities of Russia. Service is carried out today by about 1300 specialists.
