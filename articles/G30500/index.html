Scientific activity

Verbitskaya Lyudmila Alekseevna wrote about 300 educational and scientific manuals in the fields of phonetics, philology and general linguistics. In her writings, much attention is paid to the choice of an effective teaching methodology. Many of them consider the problems of modern pronunciation of words. They formed the basis for the creation of new directions in philology, which are associated with the interference of sounds and pronunciation standards. An important place in her works belongs to the problems of the culture of speech, semantics, vocabulary and stylistics.

The woman never rested on her laurels; therefore, in 1965, Verbitskaya Lyudmila Alekseevna defended her thesis, and in 1977 she defended her doctoral work. In the fall of 2013, he assumes a new position - the president of the Russian Academy of Education. Lyudmila Verbitskaya receives government approval for the position.





Under the President of the Russian Federation, Lyudmila Verbitskaya is a member of a number of Councils: on science, linguistics, and guaranteeing the constitutional rights of citizens. In 1998, she became President of the branch of the Union of English Speakers, which was created under the patronage of Queen Elizabeth.
