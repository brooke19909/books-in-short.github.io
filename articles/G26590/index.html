Flight modes

Phantom 4 has five modes that affect its behavior in the air. The user should choose the one that is best suited in specific conditions.

  • Positioning mode. The Phantom 4 quadrocopter uses satellites and a camera to determine its location and follows the commands of the control panel.
  • TapFly - a mode in which it is enough to touch the screen to show the drone a new flight destination.
  • Active Track. In this mode, the quadrocopter follows the person or the specified object. He is able to do this without GPS, visually determining the position of the target and keeping it within sight.
  • Smart flight - a mode with dual satellite and visual positioning system. In this case, the drone automatically bypasses obstacles.
  • In sport mode, the Phantom 4 can reach speeds of up to 20 m / s (72 km / h) without compromising its satellite and visual positioning.

Smart flight is much more than the ability to maintain position in space. This system includes components, which will be discussed below.





Satellite and visual positioning

The Phantom-4 quadrocopter supports two positioning systems. The device uses satellites to determine its place in space, and also visually focuses on what is in front of it.

The visual positioning system deserves special mention because of its ability to keep the device on the route of movement, even without the help of GPS and GLONASS. In addition, vertical accuracy of 0.1 m and horizontal of 0.3 m is achieved, which allows you to bring the Phantotm 4 quadrocopter exactly to the specified location. In addition, this allows the drone to brake sharply, return to the specified location after a forced deviation, and immediately hang after releasing the joystick. All these features make this system one of the most important safety features of Phantom 4.

Automatic take-off and return are provided by advanced GPS technologies. Thanks to this, the drone is very easy to operate and difficult to lose.

Perhaps the most striking feature of Phantom 4 is its ability to see and avoid obstacles in its path. This function ensures a safe flight, even in case of pilot errors or unexpected obstacles. This is achieved thanks to sensors in the front of the aircraft and firmware. It operates at distances from 0.7 to 15 m. It supports TapFly mode, which allows you to send a drone in any direction with one touch of the screen, without worrying about obstacles in its way. This reduces the number of accidents in the air.

Duration of work

Phantom 4 differs from its competitors in its ability for long flights. Most drones can remain in the air for several minutes, and this model stretches the pleasure of the pilot, according to the manufacturer, up to 28 minutes. According to user reviews, this is not entirely true. When the battery charge drops to 10% of its maximum, the quadrocopter goes into emergency mode and lands as soon as possible. Therefore, the real flight time is reduced to 23 minutes, which still exceeds the capabilities of most other drones. For maximum performance, you should purchase additional batteries worth $ 150 each.
