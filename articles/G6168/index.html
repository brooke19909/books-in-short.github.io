Needlework lessons: crochet toys

Years passed, and the methods of making toys in our lives became more and more professional. Important was the influence of tradition. Gradually, hard materials gave way to soft ones, straw, linen and yarn were used. Difficult

track the exact moment from which we crochet toys. At first, they were the most ordinary people who had a filling of straw. Then, people gradually realized how to learn how to crochet toys. After all, in fact, this is nothing complicated. And now we crochet toys, with a small amount of yarn and directly crochet.

Everything is simple

When we crochet toys, we can plan for ourselves both the image of the finished product and its constituent material. This type of needlework does not require a complex knitting pattern, a combination of single crochet and front smooth is sufficient. If you rack your brains on how to master the technique and create crocheted knitted toys, the master class will help you in the best way possible.

Master Class. The first stage of work: fingers

Consider creating a knitted toy with a specific example. Let it be a lizard. The manufacture of our masterpiece will best start with the fingers, because they are the smallest part. Thus, you can already understand at the beginning whether the thickness of our yarn will make such small details. It will be much more offensive if it becomes clear after the creation of the head that the yarn will not allow this. There is no need to count the loops, you can take an approximate amount. So, let's start by creating three air loops that we close. We tie them with crochets. Create another row through the column. We will simply tie row number 3, we will reduce the fourth. It will turn out something like a tiny ball, which will need to be stuffed with cotton wool or tied around a bead.



We knit paws

We continue to reduce, and we knit out the base of the foot. So we knit five fingers, logically reducing the size to the edge of the paw. We lay out, grab two or three loops from each base of the foot, as if pulling them together. We knit in a circle, reducing the number of loops first and adding when we begin to knit the foot itself. Then the turn of the elbow: we turn what we have in the other direction, create a semicircle and turn it around, thus completing the circle. Having reached the last row of the paws, we act as when creating the elbow, so that the most natural combination with the body is obtained. You need to make four such parts, paws. It is important not to confuse the right and left limbs.

Basis - the body of a lizard

We start the body from the end, that is, from the tail, by adding loops. For the effect of bending, insert a wire into the tail. When tying the body, do not forget to leave room for the head.
