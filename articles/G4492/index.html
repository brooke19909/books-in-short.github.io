Description

The main building of the Grand Park Lara 4 * hotel has a cozy lobby, a shopping center, dry cleaning, a paid medical office, an entertainment center and a small conference room. In all cases of Grand Park Lara Antalya 4 * brand new high-speed elevators work. The hotel has convenient parking, which must be booked in advance. Beach

Grand Park Lara 4 (Antalya, Turkey) is a hotel built on the second line from the Mediterranean Sea. The famous Lara beach in the region is only two hundred meters away. Only three minutes walk, crossing the road and vacationers find themselves on the sandy shore, where everything you need for a comfortable bathing and sunbathing - umbrellas, sun loungers, trestle beds and mattresses - is free of charge for residents of this hotel. A kilometer from Grand Park Lara 4 is its own shore. There, a hotel shuttle departs three times a day from the hotel. The entrance to the water here is comfortable, not requiring special shoes. There is no pier.

Reviews

The hotel was built only a year ago, so the number of guests in it, respectively, is not very large. Many of the vacationers are satisfied with the food offered at the hotel, although there are those who prefer to eat by prior reservation in order to taste more dishes from the local cuisine.
