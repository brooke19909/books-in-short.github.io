How to create an account on the phone: step by step instructions. Login, delete, unlock

All modern smartphones are linked to a specific account. For example, Android gadgets are registered with Google. An account helps both identify the user (and protect his phone from intruders), so synchronize data between several devices (for example, old and new gadgets). In addition, it opens up a wide range of opportunities for the owner of the gadget. How to create an account on the phone, we will deal with you in detail and in the article.

What gives you an account on your smartphone?

Many users find it unnecessary to bother with registering their device in the Google system. But in vain. The Google Account on your phone provides you with the following:

  • Sync all recorded contacts with Google Cloud.
  • Saving the settings of the applications you downloaded.
  • Moving contacts and other data to a new smartphone and other devices based on Android.
  • Saving both history and browser bookmarks.
  • Identification in all the variety of Google services: the social network Google+, YouTube, Google Drive, Play Music, Play Press, Play Movies and so on.
  • Player identification in the "Play Games" - with your account you will be transferred to a new device while maintaining all your achievements.
  • Access to the cloud and storage of various information in it - photos, audio, video.




How to create an account on the phone?

The procedure will be quite simple. So, how to create an account on the phone:

  1. Connect your gadget to the Internet.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Find the "Accounts" item.
  4. Click on "Add Account" or a similar line.
  5. The device can offer several systems besides Google, Samsung, Microsoft, Dropbox and so on. Choose the one you need.
  6. Next, click on "Create ...".
  7. How to set up an account on the phone? First enter your first and last name.
  8. Click on the "Next" arrow.
  9. In the next window, come up with a username and password - a unique combination of numbers 0-9 and letters of the Latin alphabet of various registers (lowercase and uppercase). As for the password, it can also use a number of special characters: No.,%, dash, underscore, exclamation and question marks, dot, comma, and so on.
  10. A password is considered reliable, including at least 12 characters. It must be entered twice at this stage for confirmation.
  11. How to set up an account on the phone? In the next window, you need to specify your mobile phone number. It is he who will help you recover your username and password if you forget them.
  12. A message with a special code will come to the indicated number, the characters from which you need to enter in the window on the next window.
  13. Then you will be presented with the terms of use of the account. Only by accepting them you can use your account in the system.
  14. Then a window will appear with your username and password - click "Next."
  15. If you want to receive news from Google on your e-mail, then check the box.
  16. Click on "Next." Account created!




Creating an account using a PC

We looked at how to create an account on the phone. If this method is not convenient for you, then you can turn to the help of a computer or laptop. This is done as follows:

  1. Open your Google homepage in your browser.
  2. Click on "Login", select "Register".
  3. Here, during the registration process, you will need to specify similar information - login and password (you need to remember this information - you will enter it on your smartphone to bind it to your account), last name, first name, gender, date of birth, your country, mobile phone number, spare email address.
  4. Once the procedure is completed, go to the "Settings" of the gadget, then to "Accounts", and attach it to your account by entering your username and password in the Google system. How to do it - read on.




How to log in to your gadget account?

We continue to find out how to set up an account on the phone. We have sorted out the registration with you - booking a unique address in the Google system or any other. And the entrance to the account is the binding of the smartphone to the previously created account, the username and password of which are known to you.

It is done like this:

  1. Connect your phone to the internet.
  2. Go to "Settings".
  3. Select Accounts.
  4. Click on "Add ..."
  5. Select from the list the system in which you are already registered. For example, Google.
  6. Enter login - email address. Click on "Next."
  7. Then enter the password.
  8. The next step is an agreement with the privacy policy.
  9. Enable / disable backup as you wish.
  10. That's all - you have tied the smartphone to your account.
