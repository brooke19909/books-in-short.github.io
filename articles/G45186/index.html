How to transfer money from Tele2 to Beeline?

Not all subscribers of the Tele2 mobile operator know that money on the balance of a number can be used not only to pay for communication services. At the moment, it is possible to transfer to other numbers of some mobile operators, pay for purchases in online stores and other resources, and even transfer funds to pay for housing and communal services charges. If the latter options are not very common, since it is unlikely that the necessary amount will be present on the account, then, for example, the ability to transfer funds to another number is very popular for buying goods in an online store. How can I transfer money from "Tele2" to "Beeline", what conditions the service implies, to whom it is available - we will consider all these issues in this article.





How can I make a transfer?

In order to share with friends or relatives money that is placed on the balance of your Tele2 number, you can use one of the transfer options:

  • through a payment service of a mobile operator (in order to transfer from Tele2 to Beeline a certain amount of funds using this method, you must have Internet access);
  • sending a text message to the service number of the communication service provider;
  • enter the USSD combination on the number from which you want to transfer finances.

Let us consider in more detail how money can be transferred from Tele2 to Beeline using each of the previously listed methods.





On what conditions are transfers made?

As is the case with any service of a mobile operator, funds can be transferred under certain conditions. Before telling how to transfer money from Beeline to Tele2, I would like to talk about important points:

  • it is impossible to transfer less than 10 rubles;
  • for one transaction the maximum transfer size is allowed - 1 thousand rubles;
  • no more than 10 operations can be performed daily;
  • the size of all transfers within a day should not exceed 5 thousand rubles.




Payment service from Tele2

The specially developed Tele2 portal allows you to pay for some goods and services from your number. In this case, unlike other methods of transfer, here you can find the whole list of possible recipients of funds from your balance. This may be useful, for example, in the future for making a monthly subscription for the Internet. How to transfer a certain amount of money from "Tele2" to "Beeline"? To do this, you should visit the official website of the company and go from it to this payment service. It does not need to register and log in. All that is required from the user in order to transfer money from "Tele2" to "Beeline" is to select the "Mobile Communications" - "Operator Beeline" section, and then:

  • enter the number of the sender (that is, indicate your number in the specified format);
  • indicate the number of the subscriber to whom the transfer is made (also in the specified format);
  • specify the amount of the transfer amount (we talked about possible restrictions earlier);
  • Click the "Pay" button.

Transfer to Beeline number from a mobile device

About whether it is possible to transfer from "Tele2" to "Beeline", we mentioned earlier. Now dwell on this in more detail. There are two options for transferring funds from a mobile phone: via SMS or USSD.

In the first case, you need to create a new text message, add the <bln> combination in the field, set a space, then add the recipient's number, put a space again and indicate the amount that must be debited from your account and sent to the Beeline number. Thus, if we want to transfer to the subscriber with number 89011111111 the amount of 250 rubles, we need to create a message and enter the text <bln 89011111111 250>. This message must be sent to service number 159.





In the second case, when sending the amount of funds from your balance via USSD, you should dial the following sequence of characters on the digital panel of your device: * 159 * 8XXXXXXXXXX * amount #. The recipient number, as in the case of sending a text message, must begin with a figure eight. For example, if we want to send the subscriber with the number 89055555555 the amount of 500 rubles, then you need to dial * 159 * 89055555555 * 500 # and press the call button.

You will be notified of the successful completion of the transfer by message, just as in the case of incorrect transfer of funds to his account.
