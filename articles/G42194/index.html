Overview of the best Kirillov hotels: description, addresses, prices

Kirillov - a city in the Vologda region, which is located near the regional center - only 100 km. On its territory there are a considerable number of attractions that attract the attention of tourists. In particular, this is the Kirillo-Belozersky monastery, the national park "Russian North". In the city and its environs there are a huge number of ancient churches and monasteries to which pilgrims come.

Arriving here, travelers are often puzzled by the choice of place to stay. Recreation centers and hotels of Kirillov are in great demand among visitors. Consider the most popular of them.

"Cornflowers"

This is a real recreation center, in the territory of which there is a small number of cottages. They can accommodate whole families, there are separate conditions for children. It is located on the picturesque shore of the lake.

Executive employees work at this recreation center, who care about the comfort of each vacationer. Attention guests are offered a billiard room, a real Russian bath, as well as a small cafe.

The total number of houses is designed for 65 guests. Banquets and events with up to 100 people are possible on site.

The cost of living in the house of the complex starts from 1150 rubles per person, on weekends it rises to 1250 rubles. This amount already includes breakfast.

The recreation center "Cornflowers" is located at: Khorhorinskaya street, 4.



"Russia"

Hotel "Rus" in Kirillov - a place in which visitors can sit with complete comfort. It is located in the very center of the city, within walking distance from the main infrastructure.

The room stock of this Kirillov hotel has 38 apartments, which are divided into different categories of comfort. The building also has a small cafe with a total capacity of 70 seats. The rooms are equipped with standard furniture sets, consisting of beds, cabinets for storage, as well as tables and chairs. Here in each room there is a shower cabin and a complex of bath accessories. The cost of living is quite small - it is 500 rubles per day.

Hotel "Rus" is located at the address: Kirillov, street Uritsky, 8.



Ecotel

On the shore of Lake Siversky there is a small village where the Kirillova Hotel Ecotel is located. It is a whole complex, which includes small houses built in the Swedish manner. There is also a houseboat and a large main building, which has several rooms. Guests from nearby cities like to relax in this place - fresh air and beautiful nature contribute to the huge flow of tourists.

Each house that is part of this hotel complex is made of wood. Inside, everything is landscaped: there are sleeping places, a refrigerator, electricity, as well as places to store things. The cost of renting a cottage for a day starts at 3900 rubles.

You can find the Ecotel complex on the outskirts of Kirillov, near the village of Shilyakovo.

In total, it includes 9 rooms of different price categories: 6 of them are luxury, and 3 are standard.
