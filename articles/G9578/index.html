Hotel "Kadashevskaya" in Moscow - overview, features and reviews of tourists

If you plan to visit the capital of Russia for business or tourist purposes, pay attention to the Kadashevskaya Hotel. This is one of the best institutions in Moscow, which is popular among both locals and city guests. It provides comfortable living conditions and a host of related services.

general description

Hotel "Kadashevskaya" was opened in Moscow on the embankment of the Moscow River in 2009. The institution is notable for a facade made in the old style. In a three-story building for comfortable accommodation of guests rooms of different categories of comfort are provided.

Location

The convenient location attracts tourists and business people Kadashevskaya Hotel. Hotel "Kadashevskaya" is located on the street of the same name in the house number 26. Dislocation can be characterized as follows:

  • walking distance from the embankment of the Moscow River;
  • a quarter hour walk to Red Square;
  • 5 minutes walk to the Tretyakov Gallery;
  • 500 meters to the Novokuznetskaya metro station.

How to get there

Hotel "Kadashevskaya" (Russia) provides guests with a shuttle service. If you want to get to the hotel yourself, use these administration tips:

  • From the Domodedovo International Airport to the Paveletsky Train Station follows Aeroexpress. Take the subway and follow one stop to the Novokuznetskaya station. After leaving the metro, turn right onto Pyatnitskaya. At the first traffic light, turn left onto Kadashevskaya Street.
  • Aeroexpress follows from Sheremetyevo International Airport to Belorussky Train Station. Take the subway and follow 4 stops to the Novokuznetskaya station. After leaving the metro, turn right onto Pyatnitskaya. At the first traffic light, turn left onto Kadashevskaya Street.
  • At the Leningradsky train station, go down to the metro (Komsomolskaya station). Take the red line to the Okhotny Ryad station and go to the green line. Follow one stop to the Novokuznetskaya station. After leaving the metro, turn right onto Pyatnitskaya street. At the first traffic light turn left onto Kadashevskaya street.

Negative reviews

Some flaws and shortcomings are inherent in the Kadashevskaya Hotel in Moscow. Guest reviews focus on such negative points:

  • entrance doors are massive and slam strongly at closing (it would not hurt to install closers);
  • inconvenient location of plumbing in the bathroom;
  • due to the thin walls between the rooms there is a strong audibility (this problem is especially acute for adjacent rooms);
  • near the hotel there is a busy driving road, the movement on which does not stop even at night (of course, this is accompanied by a loud noise, which is heard even when the windows are closed);
  • an old dirty carpet needs to be replaced or at least dry cleaned (it is very unpleasant to see numerous spots on a bright floor);
  • if you arrive not at the beginning, but towards the middle or at the end of breakfast, you will find the food trays almost empty (there are very few dishes on the distribution, they are not replenished);
  • not all rooms have windows overlooking beautiful city views (from some rooms you can observe only the wall or roof of a neighboring building);
  • when corporate events or holidays are held in the hotel restaurant, noise is heard until late at night throughout the hotel;

  • when booking a room, you cannot predict which side the windows will face;
  • malfunctioning iron (when filling the tank with water, liquid flows through the holes on the sole);
  • poor picture quality, sound on the TV;
  • the interior is "tired" and needs to be updated (in some places scuffs are visible, wallpaper is peeling off in some places);

  • there is no private parking (you cannot also park cars along the road - a tow truck immediately picks them up);
  • uncomfortable design of the shower cabin (water from the shower does not pour on the bather, on the wall);
  • the restaurant closes at 23:00, and to find an alternative restaurant outside the hotel, you need to go at least 20 minutes;
  • the towels in the rooms are already quite washed;
  • uncomfortable entrance to the hotel;
  • hotel staff do not help guests bring their bags (this is especially important, given that the entrance to the hotel involves climbing stairs);
  • the forced hood works non-stop in the bathroom, and its noise is very annoying (especially at night);
  • the washbasin is very small, so water sprays scatter throughout the bathroom;
  • rooms of various categories do not differ much (which cannot be said about the price);
  • poor-quality bath cosmetics, does not correspond to the level of a four-star hotel;
  • constantly occurring hiccups with the issuance of payment documents (the problem is that management is rarely in place, and documents are not valid without printing it);
