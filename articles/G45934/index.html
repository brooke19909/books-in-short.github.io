What is a Visa Electron of Sberbank?

Owners of cards with the Visa Electron payment system issued by Sberbank become holders of the opportunity to pay for purchases of goods by bank transfer in many countries of the world.

In addition, Sberbank's Visa Electron and other cards are participants in promotions held by the bank and its partners, and can also use special offers and receive additional discounts.

How to get a Visa Electron card?





In order to apply for a Visa Electron card from Sberbank, you should contact the employees, present your passport and write a statement. For registration does not require additional documents that confirm the level of income and a permanent place of work.

A Visa Electron debit card can be provided by Sberbank as a main, additional or salary card. The main card is provided to any individual who is a citizen of the Russian Federation no younger than 14 years old and is registered in the bank's service area. In addition, cards are provided to non-residents or those who do not have a residence permit at all. In this case, you need to contact the head of the office in person.

An additional Sberbank card is issued to both residents and non-residents at least 14 years old, but only if the owner of the main card contacts the bank directly. In addition, a person who has reached the age of ten, who is a close relative of the holder of the main card, can own such a card.





As for salary cards, they are provided only if the relevant agreement is concluded between the bank and the employer. You can get wages not only in rubles, but also in other currencies.

The cost of annual maintenance on such cards is 300 rubles. Additional cards cost 150.

When opening a card, it is not necessary to make a down payment, but it should be remembered that upon receipt of it there will already be a negative balance, since annual service may be debited.

What are the benefits of a Sberbank Visa Electron card?

First of all, the advantages of using such a card is that it provides free services. For example, re-release is next or early. In addition, the suspension of the card is also completely free. Overdraft on cards is not provided, therefore, when it occurs, forty percent per annum is charged regardless of the currency used.

A small drawback is that a small fee is charged for providing an extract and a request for a card account, even when performing transactions through an ATM. To find out what the amount of the spending limit on the card is, you should also pay a certain amount.
