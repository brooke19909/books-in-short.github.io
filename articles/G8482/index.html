How to choose an air conditioner in a car?

A person buys a car to be mobile and to be in time always and everywhere. It is difficult to imagine a modern vehicle without air conditioning. Even if, in order to save money, you purchased a car without such an option, sooner or later you will realize that living without it is simply unbearable. By installing air conditioning in the car, you will improve the quality of the trip, especially in urban traffic jams.



Most car owners first argue that winter in our region lasts longer than summer, so you can endure the heat by opening a window. However, often this does not save at all. That is why many decide to buy air conditioning in the car. It is worth noting that it is necessary to carefully weigh all the pros and cons at the stage of buying a car, so that later you do not regret your choice.

Typically, in new foreign cars, the manufacturer provides for the possibility of installing an air conditioner in the engine compartment. However, for example, it is almost impossible to mount such a device on an old domestic car. Specialists give a recommendation for the installation at the same time as replacing the heater. This will prevent breakdowns associated with non-conforming systems.





If you do not know how to choose an air conditioner in a car, then it is worth paying attention to the fact that it can assume different places for installation. For example, a number of models are traditionally focused on installing the device under the hood. However, there are those in which the cooling unit is located on the roof of the car. It is worth paying attention to one more point, which will facilitate your trips in the future. The air conditioner installed in the machine must be able to work simultaneously with the heating system, which will allow you to get rid of such a problem as the constant fogging of car windows. At the moment, there are also such models that are able to change not only the temperature, but also the humidity in the cabin. This feature is relevant enough for too dry or too humid climates.





And how much does air conditioning cost in a car? It depends on the type of system. It is worth noting that the most common at the moment are mechanical systems that are directly driven by the engine through a system of tension belts. It is important to carry out timely prevention. A fully functional electric air conditioner allows you to avoid such a need. On the roof of the car, its block is installed, in which the compressor and condenser are located. Such a device is too large for a passenger car, so it is usually used for minibuses. If you decide to install the air conditioner in the car, then at the stage of its purchase you need to think over where, if necessary, you will service and repair the acquired system. It should be understood that prolonged and uninterrupted operation is possible only with careful care. With a certain periodicity, a number of procedures are required to carry out cleaning of the device and its washing. Some car owners do this on their own, while others resort to specialist help.

It is worth saying that all modern cars are equipped with climate control, which ensures the provision of comfortable conditions inside the vehicle.
