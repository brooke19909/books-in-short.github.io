Pulkovo hotels: description and reviews

St. Petersburg is traditionally one of the most attractive cities in Russia to visit. Every year, thousands of domestic and foreign tourists visit it, dreaming of touching the historical past of the country. In addition, St. Petersburg is not only a tourist center, but also a business center. The constant influx of tourists and people who arrived in the city as part of business trips dictates the need for the development of hotel infrastructure.





From the point of view of convenience, Pulkovo hotels are the best option for guests of the city, that is, those that are located near the airport. This is a great option for those who visit the city for the first time or do not want to spend a lot of time looking for housing. In addition, here you can find a fairly budget option.

Hotel Park Inn





Pulkovo Inn Hotel is a ten-minute drive from the airport. From the hotel by metro, you can get to Nevsky Prospekt within fifteen minutes. The hotel is a multi-storey building, overlooking the Victory Square. From the windows of the hotel you can see a stele dedicated to the defenders of the hero city.

Accommodation

Almost all Pulkovo hotels are characterized by comfortable living conditions. And Park Inn is no exception. The hotel has eight hundred and forty standard, business and luxury rooms. All rooms have original design decoration and modern materials. A mandatory element is the presence in each room of the air conditioning system, modern TVs. There are safes for storing valuables. Hotel guests in the room have the opportunity to access the Internet and can use international communication services.

Services





The list of services available to guests includes access to the fitness room and the modern spa, equipped with saunas, a beautiful pool. For motorists there is a secure parking. A bus runs from the airport to the hotel and back.

For guests there are four restaurants, a lobby bar. Park Inn Restaurant serves buffet service. The hotel also has the Hagakure Japanese restaurant and the Pauliner Bavarian restaurant and brewery. Atrium Restaurant, designed to accommodate three hundred guests at a time, hosts all kinds of special occasions.

The hotel is the best place for business meetings, corporate events. For corporate clients there is twenty-one conference room. The congress hall seats up to six hundred people. For business people, office equipment and personal computers are provided that have access to the Internet. In many respects, Park Inn is the best hotel in Pulkovo (St. Petersburg).

Reviews

According to guest reviews, the advantages of the hotel are comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary for living, varied and high-quality food. Guests note the modern design of the rooms, quality service (room cleaning is done several times a day), a wide range of delicious dishes in restaurants. Guests especially enjoy their own baked goods. Among the shortcomings can be noted the high cost of living.

Thus, summing up the reviews, we can safely recommend the hotel for visiting guests of the city.

Hotel Crowne Plaza





The hotel is located in close proximity to Pulkovo Airport. Despite the four-kilometer distance separating the hotel from the nearest Moskovskaya metro station, this does not cause inconvenience to guests due to the presence of a free bus to the metro station and vice versa, as well as delivering guests to the airport. Choosing Pulkovo hotels, you can count on convenient infrastructure and good conditions.

The hotel's room stock includes about three hundred rooms belonging to the category of standard. In addition, there are forty-three superior rooms and six suites. Accessible rooms are available for guests with special needs.

Food and entertainment





Meals are provided at the SkyLight Restaurant, the Russian Standard Signature Bar and Cafe Plaza Illi Lobby Bar. The restaurant menu includes a large number of various dishes of European and Russian cuisine. The cafe specializes in Italian dishes. Hotel guests can spend their free time in the fitness center and sauna. Their visit is free for hotel guests. A solarium is available at an additional cost.

Reviews

Most hotel guests celebrate the highest quality of service. Among the pleasant bonuses for tourists is the issuance to all guests of sets that include flavored oil and a special flavor for the pillow, which indicates the degree of attention to hotel guests from the administration. Also noted is the variety of dishes in the restaurant and cafe, exceptionally thought-out room design, excellent quality of staff work. There are fears that hotels near Pulkovo Airport are not the best option for a relaxing stay. But this is not so. Guests note that, despite the proximity to the airport, due to the excellent sound insulation, the buzz of aircraft engines in the rooms is not felt.

Hotel "Pulkovo"





This hotel has been operating for a long time and is popular with tourists visiting St. Petersburg. The hotel is located in Aviagorodok near the new international terminal Pulkovo. The hotel at the airport is very convenient for tourists, since the offices of the largest domestic and foreign airlines are located nearby. Also near the hotel there are many office buildings and large shops. From the hotel, you can get to the city center in half an hour using public transport. Fifteen minutes by shuttle bus is the nearest metro station.

Pulkovo hotels are ready to host children's and student organized tourist groups. The number of rooms consists of one hundred and sixty rooms of various categories. All rooms have telephones, refrigerators and Internet access. Three meals a day are organized for guests. Tourists have the opportunity to choose the best food option.

The hotel has a cafe. Guests can visit the sauna. For guests' convenience, a theater and museum ticket reservation service is provided. The advantage of the hotel, distinguishing it from other numerous hotels, according to many guests, is the relatively low cost of living. Also, guests of the city emphasize that the hotel has a peculiar atmosphere that inspires memories of the past. Many speak of this as a virtue. But some still prefer housing with a more modern interior. The only thing that pleases is the affordable price and friendly staff.

Many tour operators and experienced travelers claim that the best option for living is a hotel. Pulkovo Airport (St. Petersburg) can offer its customers affordable accommodation almost at the airport terminal.





Thus, there are many first-class hotels near the airport, among which a tourist or a person who has arrived in St. Petersburg on a business trip can easily find a suitable accommodation option. As part of a short article, it is impossible to talk about all the options located near the airport, but tourists can safely check into those of which the information about which is proposed above. Hotels (in Pulkovo) of St. Petersburg offer convenience and comfort for a small fee.
