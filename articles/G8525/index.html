Hotel AddPlus Hotel & Spa 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

Do you plan to choose the island of Phuket in Thailand as a place for an unforgettable vacation? Looking for a budget hotel with comfortable rooms and close to the beach? In this case, the three-star AddPlus Hotel & Spa (Patong) may be a suitable option for you. We will talk about what this hotel offers to its guests.



Location

The hotel in question has a good, according to many tourists, location. So, it is located within walking distance from Patong Beach in an area with well-developed infrastructure. At your service there are a lot of cafes and bars, massage parlors and shops, supermarkets and markets, tour agencies, car rental points and much more. Bangla Road, an entertainment street known to all tourists, is just 800 meters away. Five minutes walk to the Jungle Zeilon shopping and entertainment complex. From Phuket Airport, AddPlus Hotel & Spa is only 24 kilometers away. So on arrival, the path to the hotel will take you no more than half an hour.



Description

AddPlus Hotel & Spa 3 * is a modern city-style hotel. It was built in 2014. The hotel consists of two nine-story buildings. He has practically no own territory. However, the hotel has a rooftop pool. By the way, it offers a magnificent panoramic view of the surroundings. There is also a small sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers. Therefore, if you do not want to go to the beach, you can have a nice time by the pool without leaving the hotel.

The hotel has 80 rooms. All of them are comfortable and equipped with everything necessary for living. The hotel also has a restaurant, bar, spa, laundry service and business center. Guests always have the opportunity to use the hotel parking for free.



Rooms

As already mentioned, EddPlus Hotel has 80 apartments at its disposal. They are located in two nine-story buildings equipped with high-speed elevators. The rooms here are presented in three categories: standard (22 sq. M, maximum two guests can stay), superior (36 sq. M, designed for two guests) and deluxe (50 sq. M, max. 2 + 1 people can stay) . Regardless of the type, all apartments have comfortable furniture, a TV (there are channels in Russian), air conditioning, a telephone, a minibar (for a fee), an electronic safe (free of charge), a tea set, a bathroom with a shower and a bathtub. Please note that balconies are only available in Superior and Deluxe apartments. Standard rooms are equipped only with windows. On request, a baby cot can be accommodated in the rooms. Also, according to tourists, throughout the hotel, including rooms, guests enjoy free Wi-Fi.

Cleaned in the apartment every day. Sheets and towels are updated several times a week. Also, as expenditures, maids will report shampoo, shower gel, soap and other personal care products.



Nutrition

According to our compatriots who have left AddPlus Hotel & Spa 3 * reviews, the cost of hotel accommodation includes breakfast only. They are held in the hotel restaurant. In general, as experienced tourists say, breakfast here can be called standard for three-star hotels in Thailand. So, in the morning you will be offered to eat fried eggs, sausages, soup, vegetables, fruits, toasts with jam, juice, tea or coffee. Guests can also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant. However, most travelers prefer to eat outside the hotel. Indeed, in Phuket, according to them, there are a lot of cafes and restaurants where you can eat tasty and cheap. In addition, you can always buy fresh fruit or seafood on the market, which you will immediately be prepared for a small surcharge.
