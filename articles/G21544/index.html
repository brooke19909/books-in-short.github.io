Hotel Continental Palace 4 (Greece / Kos): services, photos and reviews

Would you like to go on a trip to the hospitable sunny Greece, where the sun rules the country almost all year round and the aroma of coniferous plants soars in the air? Then, without hesitation, draw up documents. On this amazing continent you will find the azure sea, clean sandy beaches and the stupefying smell of freshly brewed Turkish coffee.

The unique atmosphere of the holiday has a bright and memorable vacation. The favorable Mediterranean climate contributes to this, it provides ideal conditions for an unforgettable vacation. Even in winter, this country is comfortable, and you can devote your vacation to acquaintance with local traditions and unique sights.

Greece has many faces, it combines absolutely all types of tourist orientation - from beach to extreme, including ecological, agricultural and skiing. Numerous resorts of this country are very picturesque and rich in tropical vegetation. For a versatile family and relaxing holiday, the island of Kos is perfect - the Green Park of the Aegean Sea, that's what tourists call the city.





The resort is a harmonious combination of modernity and ancient history, which has preserved many cultures and monuments of different eras, which makes the island even more attractive to guests. It is in this place surrounded by greenery that you can plunge into the atmosphere of natural beauty, temporarily shield yourself from reality and relax emotionally.

In the resort town of Kos, the tourism sector is highly developed, about 200 hotels of different levels and stardom are founded on the territory. Almost every hotel is aimed at travelers with children and offers a wide range of activities for children. Continental Palace 4 is considered one of the largest and most popular complexes. At an affordable cost, the management offers comfortable conditions and multifaceted leisure for any age category.

general information





The time-tested Hotel Continental Palace 4 (photos are presented in the article) is located near the central part of the island, near the coast. The contingent of vacationers consists mainly of Germans with children and older couples. The highlight of the hotel is a well-groomed area with flowering shrubs and fruit-bearing orchard. The refined courtyard attracts with its original landscape design. The hotel erected a temple, admission is free for guests.

Accommodations





The hotel Continental Palace 4 has two residential three-story buildings, decorated in pale white, where the concentrated rooms are equipped in the amount of 210 pieces. The premises are cleaned regularly.

Room Types:

- Double Bunk Bed with an area of ​​17 m2 with air conditioning and a spacious terrace overlooking the azure sea. Guests have at their disposal a private bathroom with a shower and necessary hygiene products. The bedroom has a cable TV, radio and landline telephone.

- Double Standard and Single Standard with the same area and amenities. For a fee, there is a bar with a rich selection of drinks, a compact refrigerator and a safe.

Supply system





Payment only includes a continental buffet breakfast . For lunch and dinner, a luxurious restaurant with an open veranda invites you . In the evenings, skilled chefs prepare delicious grilled dishes. The menu is dominated by dishes of international and national cuisine.

You will be able to try real Greek dishes: pastizio, moussaka, zazizy. The table is not complete without popular snacks - feta cheese and olives. At the tavern of the Continental Palace 4 hotel (Greece) you will be treated to sea delicacies. The bars serve authentic Greek wine and sweets.

Coastline

The Continental Palace 4 (Kos) hotel does not have its own beach, but only 50 meters away is a municipal shallow coast with sandy coating and very clear water. The beach is quite wide, so even at the peak of the influx of holidaymakers there is a lot of free space - there are enough sunbeds, no crush. The only negative - paid sun loungers and water activities. There is a diving center on the shore, catamarans and ATVs are for rent. There is a place to play volleyball.

Internal infrastructure





Guests will not have to be bored, daily at the Continental Palace 4 hotel are held incendiary show programs, and once a week - Greek evenings with karaoke and dancing. For lovers of sports entertainment there are volleyball and basketball courts, a tennis court, mini-golf. A rental point for bicycles and sports equipment has been opened.

For educational tourism , excursions are organized with a guide to neighboring resorts and islands. Those who wish can rent a car and explore local facilities on their own, specifying interesting tours with the administrator in advance. Near the residential complexes there are swimming pools with free amenities and a bar.





It offers paid parking, a laundry department and an exchange office. Cinema fans will appreciate the TV lounge with a wide selection of films. Well, business people and businessmen will be able at any time to hold a presentation of their goods or negotiations at a large table. For these purposes, a conference room is equipped with a high-quality sound system, advertising stands, the Internet, a monitor, a microphone and other important attributes.

For children there is a playground and a safe pool. A babysitting service is provided , respectively, for a fee.

Continental Palace 4 Hotel (Kos): traveler reviews

After analyzing the numerous reviews of vacationers, we can conclude that the complex harmoniously combines a welcoming atmosphere, quality service and versatile leisure activities. The dimensional and leisurely rhythm of life in this corner of paradise allows you to relax, forget yourself in the bliss of pleasure and feel yourself refreshed.

Many positive words were addressed to the courteous and sociable staff, always ready to come to the rescue. It is impossible not to mention cooking. Continental Palace 4's restaurants do their best to meet the needs of guests. The menu is very diverse.
