On the way to the stars

Today, the name of Yuri Gagarin for all is inextricably linked with space. And perhaps it‚Äôs hard to even imagine that someone else could have made this legendary flight. The same applies to the Vostok-1 ship itself, although due to the arms race, the designers had to abandon a number of fundamental decisions. It did not provide for an emergency rescue system at launch, there was no emergency braking system and a soft landing system for the ship.

Special requirements were presented to the first detachment of astronauts. In addition to good health and high moral qualities, it was required that their height be no more than 170 cm and weight up to 75 kg. This is due to the fact that Gagarin's spacecraft simply could not accommodate a heavier and taller passenger. As a result, the leadership, and the general designer himself, for a long time could not choose a candidate. And just before the start Sergey Korolev gave preference to Gagarin. Herman Titov was appointed his understudy .

Life after

From that day on, the whole world became aware of the name of the Gagarin spaceship. And Yuri Alekseevich himself became a real legend during his lifetime. Everyone wanted to meet him and shake hands with the first "heavenly man." As a result, fearing the loss of the first astronaut, the command did not allow Gagarin to take off again for a long time.

But the ship "Vostok-1" was repeatedly modernized and until 1963 was successfully operated. German Titov and Valentina Tereshkova still went into space on it. And then, after the next series of ships was developed, it was exhibited in 1965 at the Exhibition of Economic Achievements. What was the surprise of the foreign media, which until then knew only what the name of the spaceship Gagarin was.
