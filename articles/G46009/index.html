Sunis Elita Beach Resort Hotel Spa (Turkey / Side): photos and reviews of tourists

Turkey has been a leader in the tourism market for many years. The Russians love the resorts of this country for the low cost of living and first-class food. Those who want to relax on the Mediterranean Sea, as a rule, go to Antalya or Alanya. However, between them is a small resort town of Side, which is chosen by tourists tired of crowded beaches. Side Resort in Turkey

Side is an ancient city in which a large number of historical monuments of ancient times have been preserved. But the resort gained popularity with its coast, marked with the Blue Flag, which is given to beaches that meet international standards of cleanliness and service. The sea in Side is shallow, and the entrance is gentle, therefore families with small children often relax here. Most of the beach is divided into private areas owned by hotels and restaurants. And the Sunis Elita Beach Resort Hotel & Spa also has its own stretch of coast, where strangers are not allowed . On the beach, tourists can play active games, go diving or go boating.

Shopping in Side is much worse. There are few shops here, and prices for goods are much higher than in Antalya and other large resorts. But the restaurants here can be found absolutely everywhere. Local cuisine combines Turkish and Mediterranean traditions. Almost everywhere you can try some original dish made from seafood. Many tourists in Side combine a beach holiday with a sightseeing. There are many monuments preserved, whose age exceeds 2000 years.

Location of the complex

The tourist complex Sunis Elita Beach Resort Hotel & Spa, the description of which can be seen in this article, is located on the first beach line. The hotel was built right on the coast, so the sea can be reached in 5 minutes. To the north of the hotel is a Turkish bazaar, consisting of clothing stores, shoes and souvenirs. It is separated from the hotel by road. There is also a pharmacy nearby. The nearest resort village of Kyzylach is 1 km from the complex. It can be reached by bus or taxi.





There are no significant attractions near the hotel. Manavgat Waterfall is 11 km away, the ancient Roman ruins and Side amphitheater are 13 km away, and Alarahan Fortress is 19 km away. Travel time is 1-2 hours. However, guests of the complex can also get on their own by paid taxi. Its cost will be much higher, but the travel time will also be reduced.
