Amata Resort 3 *, Phuket: reviews and photos of tourists

Thailand is a country that many travelers associate with a warm climate, soft beaches and gentle waves. And quite often, tourists choose the famous resort city of Phuket as a temporary refuge. It is here that the beautiful and comfortable Amata Resort Hotel is located. So what level of service and accommodation can guests expect? How far is the beach? How do other travelers respond about it? These questions are asked by many people.

Where is the hotel situated?





The cozy hotel complex Amata Resort 3 * is located in one of the most popular areas of Phuket called Patong Beach. The beach is pretty close, within walking distance. The center can be reached in about 20 minutes - the hotel staff will call you a taxi. The nearest international airport is approximately 40 km away. But travelers, as a rule, are provided with a transfer to the selected hotel, so you should not worry about the distance.

Hotel Amata Resort 3 * (Phuket): description of the territory

This hotel consists of one six-story building. Amata Resort Hotel owns a small private territory, part of which is reserved for a swimming pool with several sections. By the way, another pool is located on the roof - it is much calmer here, and the view of the mountains and the sea opens up breathtaking. The hotel area is clean and well maintained, as they clean it every day. And here there is a sufficient amount of greenery that pleases the eye.

Photo and brief description of the rooms





Regardless of the category you choose, you can count on a truly comfortable stay. All rooms are spacious and bright, some even have access to a private balcony, which offers wonderful views of the surroundings and the hotel courtyard.

Naturally, there is all the necessary furniture, including comfortable beds, wardrobes, bedside tables and even a large desk with a table lamp. Seed rooms, as well as luxury rooms, have a kitchen with all necessary equipment, kitchenware and tableware.

Of household appliances, there is air conditioning, a safe with a combination lock, as well as a telephone and a TV (although it shows only cable channels). A minibar is also available, but is filled at the request of residents.

In the bathroom, you can refresh yourself in the shower at any time of the day or soak in the warm bath. Residents are provided with the required number of clean towels, as well as a set of hygiene products. There is also a stationary hairdryer. The rooms are cleaned daily, so none of the travelers complains about cleanliness. In fact, many tourists choose the hotel Amata Resort 3 *. Phuket is a wonderful place to relax at any time of the year.

Food scheme for hotel guests





Paying for accommodation at the Amata Resort hotel, tourists can choose their own meal plan. For the most part, guests prefer to have breakfast at the hotel, however, you can order a full three meals a day.

The hotel has a comfortable and cozy restaurant Amata, where guests are offered a wide selection of Thai and international dishes. The service here, judging by the reviews, is at a high level, and the atmosphere is very pleasant. Snacks and drinks are also available at the local bar.

If you do not like food at the hotel, then you can easily eat at any of the cafes or restaurants in the city - there are really a lot of them. And in the market you can buy exotic exotic fruits, vegetables and other food products at low prices.

Where is the beach located?





Naturally, many tourists go to Thailand in search of a fun beach holiday. What can the hotel complex Amata Resort 3 * offer? Immediately it is worth saying that the hotel does not have its own beach. The public is located about 600 meters - it can be easily reached in 10-15 minutes on foot.

Reviews of tourists say that the shore of the recreation area is covered with soft sand. There is a convenient entrance to the sea and a shallow area. On the beach for a normal price, you can rent a deck chair with a mattress, as well as a sun umbrella. There are also changing cabins. Also nearby is a cafe and bar where you can buy chilled drinks, try exotic cocktails and even have a delicious breakfast.

Fans of more active pastime will not have to be bored either. Here you can easily rent a catamaran or a boat, go water skiing and banana, try parasailing or take a boat trip around the coast. The water here is clean, and the underwater life is quite diverse, so many tourists prefer to snorkel. This beach is also popular with fans of windsurfing and sailing. In any case, every tourist can find an interesting lesson.

Additional services and entertainment for tourists





In the courtyard there is a large swimming pool, divided into sections of different depths. Guests can swim, sunbathe or just relax on comfortable sunbeds. Another outdoor pool is located on the roof of the building - its advantage is a breathtaking view of the surroundings.

The hotel has its own small shop where you can buy various little things, interesting souvenirs, as well as branded products of the Amata Resort hotel. Phuket is a vibrant city with many attractions. Using the services of a tour desk, you can travel around the area. And next to the hotel there is a large shopping complex, as well as the famous massage parlor, which offers a variety of types of Thai massage.

Hotel complex Amata Resort 3 *: traveler reviews

A huge amount of relevant and useful information can be obtained by asking the opinion of people who have already managed to visit a particular place. So what do they say about the hotel complex Amata Resort? Reviews are mostly positive.





First of all, travelers note a really convenient location, as there is a large grocery market, many shops and cafes, a shopping center, health salons, etc. near the hotel. The hotel area is clean and well-groomed. The rooms stand out with a truly original design. The rooms are spacious, although not all have large balconies. Furniture and appliances are in good condition, cold and hot water are constantly supplied.

Food may seem a little monotonous. On the other hand, near the hotel and in the city center there are a lot of catering establishments of different price categories and a varied menu - every traveler here will find something to his liking. The beach is relatively clean. The advantage is a large number of water rides, the cost of which is quite affordable.

On the territory of the hotel it can be boring, but you should understand that this is a three-star hotel, and the living conditions here fully correspond to the category. All employees, without exception, are polite, friendly and helpful, but very few speak Russian, so you should learn a little English.
