Residential complex "Ilyinsky Park", the village of Ilyinsky: overview, layout and reviews

For many Muscovites, the housing issue is global. Renting an apartment is not cheap. That is why, sooner or later, the question arises of buying your own home. Have you been dreaming about your own apartment for a long time? Can't afford expensive metropolitan new buildings? Pay attention to modern residential complexes, which are actively being built up in the suburbs.

"Why are they exactly?" - you ask. Everything is extremely simple: such new buildings are located outside the MKAD, which immediately affects their cost. At the same time, all residents will have access to a developed infrastructure and transport network, so you do not have to give up a comfortable life in the capital. Modern developers cover a large-scale territory, on which they not only build residential buildings, but also carry out landscaping works. That is why we can safely call the new buildings of the Moscow Region an affordable alternative to Moscow apartments. We strongly recommend that you pay special attention to them.





We bring to your attention LCD "Ilyinsky Park" - a project that has managed to establish itself well in the world of capital real estate. Our task is to give him the most objective assessment, to evaluate the conditions that he offers to modern residents. Reviews of the first buyers and those who have already managed to move into apartments will help to do this.

about the project

Two monolithic brick houses of varying number of storeys belong to elite development projects, although they are located outside the MKAD. The project is distinguished by its excellent location, modern well-thought-out layouts, developed infrastructure, which many residents managed to note.





The project was awarded a diploma of the international real estate market award for a creative approach to creating a living environment. At the moment, the house is rented out and almost completely inhabited, which allows you to give it an objective assessment based on the feedback of the first residents.

About technology

Surely you are interested in knowing what technologies were used to build the Ilyinsky Park residential complex. Responses of residents leave only a good impression. Monolithic technology is the basis of the longevity of any building, especially in the case of an apartment building. The system of ventilated facades insulated with high-quality European-style mineral wool is an opportunity not only to give the exterior of the house the necessary aesthetics, but also to protect it from the aggressive effects of the environment.

All entrances are equipped with ramps for moving people with reduced mobility, as well as high-speed elevators with silent operation. Entrance groups are finished with modern materials that add a certain charm and zest to the building. The apartments are rented without finishing, but all residents emphasize that modern innovative materials were used to conduct basic work. Cold and hot water is supplied to each apartment, heating radiators, metering devices, two-chamber double-glazed windows are installed, equipped with fire alarms.





Location

For the construction of the residential complex "Ilyinsky" (Zhukovsky), Ramensky district of the Moscow region was chosen. Today it is one of the most favorable areas for living near Moscow. The absence of harmful industries, unique protected areas, parks and squares are the conditions that modern residents of the metropolis are increasingly paying attention to.

Residential complex "Ilyinsky Park" (Zhukovsky) is located in the village of the same name in its very center, so the residents of the new complex will have access to all the infrastructure of the village.





Many fear the relative proximity to the airport in Zhukovsky - they do not want to experience inconvenience from the endless noise and roar of the aircraft engine. But, according to the reviews of the first residents, due to modern and high-quality double-glazed windows of European production, it was possible to solve the problem and save the residents from discomfort.

Transport accessibility

For everyone who is considering complexes of the Moscow Region for their residence, the issue of transport accessibility will come first. Indeed, no matter how comfortable and thought out the complex, no matter how spacious and affordable the apartments are, you are unlikely to want to lose comfort, mobility. Moscow is a huge multi-million city, the inhabitants of which spend a huge amount of time on their way to work, cultural events, which is to say about those who live outside the Moscow Ring Road.

The developer made a lot of efforts so that the residents of the complex do not spend all their free time on the road. Currently, the Novoryazanskoye highway is under reconstruction, in the near future there will be new road junctions, ramps, high-speed sections, which will save the residents of the region from endless traffic jams. The lack of a personal car is not a reason to abandon the Ilyinsky Park residential complex. Residents' reviews confirm that within walking distance from new buildings there are enough public transport stops, from where you can get to the Vykhino and Kotelniki metro stations. Do not like to crowd in buses and minibuses? Take the electric trains departing in the direction of Moscow from the Ilyinskaya railway station with enviable regularity.
