How to sew a summer skirt with your own hands

To date, long skirts are very popular among fashionistas. This beautiful thing can be worn both on the beach or in the store, and for a walk in the evening city or for dinner in a cafe. A top or T-shirt will complement the outfit in everyday life, and in combination with a jacket or a light blouse, you can surprise others at a dinner party.

But you do not need to immediately run to the store and buy an expensive product, since it can easily be created independently.

So, we sew a summer chiffon skirt that will make your look light and airy. She with any top will become a unique thing in a summer wardrobe. Next, we describe in more detail how to sew a maxi skirt .

First you need to choose a chiffon - plain or with a print. It all depends on what you are going to wear a skirt with. Next, you need to determine the amount of tissue that you will acquire. You will need about 2.5-3 meters of fabric, you will need to cut 107-110 cm in width, depending on your height. There will not be much chiffon, since it flows quite easily from the waistline to the bottom and will not look bulky on any figure.





How to sew a summer skirt with your own hands? Cut off the required amount of fabric. And the best thing is to make a small incision and then simply tear the fabric with your hands - the line will be quite straight.





After that, it is necessary to cut the fabric for the belt. The width of this fabric cut should be slightly smaller than the width of the cut for the skirt. But the belt needs to be cut out with an uneven rectangle. The upper and lower lines of the belt should be the same length, and the middle line of the belt is shorter, round the edges on the sides. Such a curved shape of the belt will help him to sit more harmoniously on the figure and comfortably hold on his hips or waist.

Two edges along the length of the fabric need to be sewn together (this will be the back seam). Next, you need to pick up with the help of a threaded thread along the top of the skirt the fabric in accordance with the width of the belt.





And now we are very close to the answer to the question of how to sew a summer skirt with our own hands.





The lower edge of the belt and the upper edge of the skirt must be joined and sewn on the sewing machine from the wrong side of the product. For accuracy, you can outline a future seam before sewing. It is also necessary to process the lower edge of the skirt and the upper edge of the belt with a seam.

On the question of how to sew a summer skirt with your own hands, it is important to consider that you need to handle chiffon very carefully when stitching on a typewriter, as this is a very thin fabric and is easy to damage. There is no rush to anything!

We hope you have received the answer to the question of how to sew a summer skirt with your own hands, and easily master this sewing. Depending on your figure, you can make the belt narrower or, conversely, even wider. It can be sewn from a different color chiffon so that the image can easily be supplemented with accessories such as shoes, a handbag, etc.





You may eventually want to experiment and try to sew a long skirt with a ruffle or even a few ruffles. Good luck in tailoring. After all, clothes made by yourself make it possible to always be on top!
