Yakutov Park (Ufa). Description, rides

Yakutov Park invites you to have fun and provocatively time for all children and adults. If you do not know where to go on a weekend or take your kids on vacation, come to this place.

Description

Yakutov Park is located in the very center of the city on Lenin Street, building 65/3, so finding it will not be difficult.





In this place there is the opportunity to have fun on various attractions. You can also sit in the Jolly Harbor restaurant or visit the Jolly Roger entertainment center.

Yakutov Park is planted with various trees and plants, which will help you feel yourself somewhere outside the city, in the forest. You can also rent catamarans and boats. For children, a children's railway and game simulators were built. For adults, there is beach volleyball. Shooters will be able to visit the shooting range. Different prizes are drawn in it. Two shooting ranges were built in the park - pneumatic and crossbow. If you want to capture important moments of your pastime, you can visit the photo studio. There, a professional photographer, in addition to high-quality images, will help you choose a new image.

Park Yakutov. Attractions for children

For the smallest there is a family train ride. Small children rode along the paved route. On the ride, kids can ride with their parents. Already for more adults there are children's electric cars. In them, your child will be able to feel like a real racer. Also for children there is a special ferris wheel. As in any park, there are several types of trampolines for kids. The main rule of visiting them is to have removable shoes with them, or the child can jump in socks. A wedding carousel is an attraction where different animals and cartoon characters ride in a circle. This attraction is very popular with children.





One more roundabout for kids - "Elephant". Big pink elephants ride around in circles. This carousel differs from the rest in that on it the kids can independently adjust the up and down rises with special levers.

The Mini Pirate is a large boat that holds up to twenty people. The boat sways in a circle, creating the illusion of a storm.

Attractions for adults

For adult visitors there is an airdrome - cars on the management. They can be dissected in a designated area. There is also a chain carousel for them. What is this attraction? This is an ordinary carousel, which sways in a circle around the seat, attached by chains.

The swimming pool with boats is available for both children and adults. Those who cannot swim will be forced to wear life vests as a precaution.





The virtual simulator of a Boeing is an imitation of an airplane where everyone can test themselves as the captain of a large airliner. The essence of the attraction is that you go into the cockpit. At the same time, the view that the pilots see during the flight is displayed on the screen. You can control the plane with levers.

The Tower of Fall is not an attraction for the most nervous. In this case, a row of seats is attached to the tower, which rises and then sharply falls down, simulating a fall. Therefore, if you have a phobia of fear of heights, think in advance whether you should ride such a tower.

The Corsair is perhaps the most dangerous attraction in the entire park. It looks like a big mill with blades. Seats are attached to them. They rotate around their axis. The mill gradually begins to rotate, gaining momentum, and at the same time, the seats begin to move. Gradually increasing speed. Only adults and children from fifteen years old can go on the ride.

Water recreation and entertainment complex

And of course, on the territory there is a lake called Soldier. There you can rent catamarans and boats.





There are several cafes on the shore. There you can sit and spend time enjoying the view.

If you get tired of walking and carousels in a day, you can go to the Vesely Roger entertainment complex. Here you will play bowling. It is modernized, with new tracks and a soft sitting area.

The complex also has a large number of game simulators for different age categories. If you get tired, you can easily sit and have a tasty meal.

Park Yakutov (Ufa): prices

There is a card system in the park. If this is your first visit, you will be given a guest card. Money will be transferred to it through the cash desk. With this card you can pay for any attraction. It is enough to conduct on a magnetic reader, which is located near each. After that, funds will be debited from the account in the amount of the cost of one ticket. Then the turnstile will open in front of you, and you can go to the selected attraction. It is important to remember that with cards you can only pay when riding an amusement ride. The entertainment center and restaurant will have to pay separately.





Ticket prices on weekdays and weekends are slightly different (higher on weekends). The minimum price of attractions is 60 rubles. For such an amount you can ride on the "Family Train", "Mini-Pirate", a children's game complex and other children's carousels. You will have to pay the maximum price if you want to ride the simulator "Boeing", "Corsair", "Autodrome" (120 p.).

Conclusion

We hope that the information about this wonderful vacation spot was useful to you. Yakutov Park (Ufa) is waiting for you with the children. Here you can get a lot of colorful and unforgettable emotions.
