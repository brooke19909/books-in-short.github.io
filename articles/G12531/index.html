Five Star Belek Hotels

Rest in the modern sense of the Russians is connected with the sea and the sun. A trip to Turkey is the usual solution for a family or youth vacation. Among the huge number of resorts, a special place is occupied by the Belek region. A small town with two-story buildings located on several streets is an hour's drive from Antalya. Therefore, tourists from the nearest airport immediately fall on the endless beaches that stretch along the coastline of the Mediterranean Sea.

Sand and pebble beaches, clear sea, helpful staff - all this is a holiday in Belek. This area is awarded the ‚Äúblue flag‚Äù, which is a proof of the crystal clear coastal waters. Belek is distinguished from many other resort areas by the highest concentration of five-star hotels with different orientations. There is everything for lovers of a relaxing beach holiday, and for those who prefer active water activities. There are even targeted accommodation facilities for golf fans and professionals, the quality of local fields was noted by European experts.

All Belek hotels are united by impeccable service, all-inclusive meals and highly professional animation teams. Every evening the hotel will delight with a laser show, dance program, performances with elements of circus and gymnastic art. Despite the busy highway, the Belek hotels are separated from it by a dense forest belt, and even each of them has enough greenery, which gives a feeling of privacy. Eucalyptus and cypress plants have a beneficial effect on the body, giving strength. It seems that every breath of air is saturated with nutrients.

In the Belek area, 5 star hotels are mostly designed for families. There are many exciting programs and attractions for children. Water slides, outdoor pools and other activities for families with children can be found at IC Santai, Cristal Hotels.

For youth holidays, Adam & Eve is perfect. The relatively young hotel, built in 2007, is an ideal place for a honeymoon and a secluded family vacation. A romantic setting and a huge selection of room categories are a distinctive feature. Belek hotels stand out in a separate area, providing a chic choice of SPA services. Attaleia Shine Tennis Spa, Cornelia Diamond Golf Resort & SPA offer the perfect combination of beach relaxation and spa treatments. Voyage Belek is considered one of the best complexes in the country, the most complete range of Spa procedures is concentrated here.

Separate accommodation offers the best golf courses in the country. Fans of this game, picturesque landscapes and lush green fields should definitely visit one of the "Golf" hotels: Adora Golf, Altis Golf Hotel, Cornelia Diamond Golf Resort & Spa, Gloria Golf Resort. The latter complex belongs to a large hotel chain. Gloria are Belek hotels, which are presented in different directions. This chain of hotels, stretching along the coast of the district, offers separate complexes for holding family and youth, active water and therapeutic recreation. Choosing any hotel in the Belek district, tourists are faced with the optimal ratio of quality of services and their cost. An interesting and fairly common solution is to spend a vacation in two hotels of different directions.
