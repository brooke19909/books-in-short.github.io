Dolphinarium at VDNH - a place where you can get to know dolphins closer!

VDNH is one of the most interesting places in Moscow. Many tourists from other cities and countries come here, and at the weekend many Muscovites like to walk here. And this is not surprising: even in the capital it is difficult to find another place in the territory of which a similar number of cultural and entertainment facilities would be collected. There is even a dolphinarium at VDNH, it is about this interesting object that we will tell you in more detail today.

The place where dolphins live

In Moscow, at VDNH, you can visit the world's largest mobile dolphinarium. This is a unique design, which is a pneumatic tent, suitable for living marine animals and conducting performances year-round, under any conditions. The dolphinarium at VDNH is striking in its size: in its stands, 550 guests can simultaneously accommodate. Watching the show is comfortable and completely safe, as all fire safety and sanitation standards are respected. Presentations are conducted by experienced trainers, and the programs shown were highly rated at international competitions.





The main artists of the dolphinarium

Most dolphinarium visitors purchase tickets to performances primarily to look at exotic animals close by. The dolphinarium in Moscow has been working at VDNH since the end of 2011, and many regular visitors already know the names of all the main artists. During the show, viewers will learn a lot of interesting facts about marine animals.





Dolphinarium show program

During the performance, the audience will see a variety of tricks - from the simplest (swimming in a circle, "soccer fins" and others) to the most difficult. Especially for the gathered audience, the trainer will ask the dolphin to draw a picture, which can then be acquired during the auction. The real highlight of the performance is the diving of specially trained actresses into an aquarium with real sharks. A variety of surprises await the guests of the dolphinarium, come and you will see everything with your own eyes! The duration of the presentation is 45 minutes, which means that even the smallest viewers will be able to watch the show until the very end.

Cost of attendance and presentation schedule

On weekdays, the dolphinarium at VDNH holds one performance, and on weekends two or three. And every time this is a real full house. Tickets can be purchased right before the start of the show at the box office located in front of the dolphinarium. The cost depends on the selected row. The most expensive ticket costs 1200 rubles, and the cheapest - only 400 rubles. Children under 3 years old can watch the show for free accompanied by their parents (upon presentation of a birth certificate). During the presentation, photo and video shooting on their equipment is allowed. After the show, you can take pictures with artists for an additional fee. Also, during the presentation, any viewer can participate in the auction and purchase a picture painted by a dolphin. How to find a dolphinarium at VDNH?

You can watch dolphin shows on the territory of the All-Russian Exhibition Center. If you go to the dolphinarium by car, it will be most convenient to leave it in the parking lot from the entrance to the exhibition area from Khovanskaya Street. The nearest metro station is VDNH. The dolphinarium has the following address: Moscow, Prospekt Mira, territory of the All-Russian Exhibition Center, building 119. You can orient yourself to Pavilion No. 8: the complex, which hosts performances with the participation of marine animals, is located next door.

Viewers reviews about visiting the dolphinarium

Over the years, this entertainment venue in the capital of the show with the participation of marine animals was visited by thousands of people. Opinions of the audience about the dolphinarium are significantly different. Someone really like this place, but it is not impressive for others. Dissatisfied spectators are difficult to find only among children, because dolphins are popular with everyone, without exception.





The dolphinarium at the VVC is often scolded for the high cost of tickets. But in fact, the complex offers its guests a wide range of prices, and from which tier to watch the performance is the personal choice of each visitor. All animals performing at the dolphinarium look well-groomed and clean. Artists have long been accustomed to the attention of the public and are always ready to pose for photos and delight guests with new tricks. In general, the dolphinarium show program can be confidently called decent and appropriate to the level of the complex. Be sure to visit the dolphin show at VDNH if you are interested in the world of marine animals.
