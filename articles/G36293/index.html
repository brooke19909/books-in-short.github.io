Parental Control (MTS). How to connect, how to use?

I always want to control the child. And while he is still a schoolboy, this is simply necessary. At such times, Parental Control comes to the rescue. MTS offers this service on favorable terms. But what do the subscribers think about it? How to use this opportunity? What does she give her customers? All this will be discussed later. However, keep in mind that often this service package is used on computers. On mobile devices, much less often.



Description

The Parental Control (MTS) service is what allows you to control your child while working on the World Wide Web. Are you worried that your children will read inappropriate information online? Or will they start visiting dangerous pages? Then it's time to connect the "Parental Controls".

With this option, parents will be able to install a filter on a phone or computer with a network from MTS, which will protect the child from unwanted information. A very popular feature, which is mainly used by subscribers who still have schoolchildren. But how to connect and use it?

Cost

Before that, it is worth considering that Parental Control (MTS) is not at all a free package that is provided to everyone. You have to pay for it. Charged from the balance daily in the amount of 1.5 rubles. Not so much if you think about it.

But the direct disconnection and connection of this option is free. And this moment is extremely pleasing to subscribers. You can start using the service at any time, as well as stop working with the option called "Parental Control". MTS offers several options for connecting and disconnecting options. About them a little later. For now, let's learn how to use this opportunity.

In it, set the desired settings and confirm your actions. Nothing complicated. It is customary to pay more attention to the "Parental Control" function on the phone. MTS offers several filtering options in this case.

Shutdown

But how to disable "Parental Control" on the MTS. Sometimes in this service there is no need. And here, too, there are several options. You can use the Internet, and resort to writing a special statement.

In the case of connecting to a computer, it is better to call the operator at 0890 and inform about the desire to terminate the "Parental Control" on the SIM card. Or use the "My Account" on the official website of MTS.



If we are talking about phones, then here you will have to disconnect both from the child in the room and from the parent. To do this, you need to contact the nearest MTS office and write a statement to renounce "Parental Control". This option is offered on the official website of the operator.
