Repeat again. Even the Russian Post cannot help us without a track code. The search for mail is possible only with an identifier.

Sender

So, you sent a package to relatives in the village of Kukuevo. Two months have already passed, but they never received it. What to do? You need to apply for a mailing item. This is a simple matter.

  1. You will need your passport and departure number. In addition, it is very desirable to have a check proving your ownership of the shipped package.
  2. Arriving at the post office, you should ask the operator to check the location of the parcel by track code.
  3. If her location is unknown or has not changed for quite a long time, you can write a statement for the search.
  4. It is written in any form, but with the obligatory indication of the above data. Also, do not forget to leave your phone and home address so that you can be sent an answer.
  5. After the postal officers conduct an internal investigation and search for the postal item, you will be informed of the results.
  6. Remember: you can receive a refund for a lost parcel only if you sent it with a "declared value".




Recipient

If you are a recipient, your actions will be radically different. When delivering mail in Russia or from abroad, there are always control deadlines. For example, when ordering a parcel with EBay or AliExpress and choosing delivery by regular mail, you should remember that the period for such mail is up to two months! And yet, even with such huge deadlines, delays are possible. Remember the main rule:

  • Only the sender can apply for a shipment! It doesn‚Äôt matter that you paid money for delivery and parcel. Until the moment you are handed it, it is the property of the sender. And to receive compensation for the loss can also only be the one who sent it. You can only get compensation from the one with whom you made a deal to buy goods. The post provides exclusively transport services and is in no way associated with any online store.

Based on this, if you think that your package has been lost, you need to do the following:

  1. Contact the sender. Most online stores and services maintain constant communication with their customers. It is beneficial for them to maintain their reputation.
  2. Describe your problem to him. Ask to know the fate of the package.
  3. If the parcel is lost, you can ask the sender to return the money to you, and the sender, in turn, will demand damages from the post office.




the Internet

In addition to everything described above, there is another way that the Russian Post offers us. The search for mail can be carried out independently, without getting up from a computer. If you have a track code, you can do the following.

  1. We go to the Russian Post page.
  2. On the left in the menu we find "Tracking of mailings" and click "Details".
  3. We enter our track code and anti-spam verification code.
  4. We look at the travel history of our parcel.

Remember, the Russian Post website works exclusively with internal mail and EMS items. If you used the services of any other express mail, you can track the package on their personal website or using the general tracking service, of which there are a lot on the Internet.

Order and terms

If you have been taken to extremes and still filed a claim in writing, then remember that the application is considered within 2 months. As in any other government agency, due to a clear hierarchy and paperwork, you will have to be patient. After submitting the application, be sure to save the coupon receipt with the reference number and the post office stamp. When ordering a parcel, be sure to find out the number of the post office that serves you so that you do not have to go to the other end of the city to get the parcel.
