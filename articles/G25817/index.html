Samsung Wireless Home Theaters - Overview

Undoubtedly, any movie fan and an ordinary movie buff in cinemas are impressed by the surround effects. It is real to receive similar acoustic special effects at home thanks to home theater equipment from leading manufacturers. Samsung is a world leader in the production of audio, video, home appliances and electronics.

Samsung Home Cinema

Samsung Corporation has been producing acoustic equipment for home theaters with great success. The company's engineers are developing and implementing the most advanced systems with wireless speakers, the ability to connect mobile gadgets and transmit information via Wi-Fi-connection or Bluetooth. The full Samsung Wireless Home Theater Kit includes:

  • receiver;
  • audio equipment (subwoofer and 2, 5, 7 or 9 speakers);
  • DVD or Blu-ray player;
  • large screen projector or LCD TV.

Difficulty choosing

For high-quality and spectacular sound of a home theater with Samsung wireless speakers, you need to take into account the correct selection of equipment with the optimal power for your room. Cinema 7.1 and 9.1 are suitable for large and spacious rooms with decent noise and sound insulation. But for a small apartment or house, it is better to choose kits 2.1 or 5.1. If you have already purchased video equipment, be sure to check its compatibility with your preferred acoustic equipment.

Choosing a kit for a home theater, you need to determine the type of speakers. They can be:

  • wall mounted;
  • mounted;
  • built into furniture or a niche;
  • on racks.

Wireless speaker models are usually only rear (rear). And, despite its name, there are still wires for them, although their number is minimized.

The set includes a convenient remote control that allows you to control all the soundbar functions from a distance without leaving a comfortable sofa.
