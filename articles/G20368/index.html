Terletsky Forest Park: description and how to get

Terletsky Forest Park in Moscow is one of the most visited places. In addition to beautiful nature, it is also interesting for its history, which is very popular with citizens and tourists. The park has places for relaxation, as well as a cafe and a stage for performances. There is a sandy beach near the ponds, where people like to sunbathe in the summer. But swimming in water is not allowed. However, this does not affect the popularity of this place among visitors.

general information

The pride of the place of rest is the relic oak forest. Therefore, in addition to the main name of the park, you can still hear others. The most popular of them is Terletsky oak forest. Many trees in it are over three hundred years old. Pine trees have been growing on its territory for more than half a century. In addition, Terletsky Forest Park is rich in many species of trees, thanks to which the air brings real health benefits. Among them are oak, alder, linden, maple, as well as many others.

The park itself was founded in 1972. Then he separated from the Izmailovsky Forest Park and became more and more visited every year. Terletsky ponds are well known throughout the country, as they have a fairly long history. They exist in this place since the 18th century. Water open spaces have become not only a favorite place for visitors. Here you can also see a variety of birds fed by guests of the park.

A bit of history

In ancient times, this territory was in the possession of many famous personalities of Russia. Among them are the Sheremetyevs, Golitsyns, Stolypin and Torletsky. At the moment, you can see the buildings that have remained since past centuries. Among them are the Torletsky estate, built in the 19th century, as well as the Spasskaya Church. The merchant A.L. Torletsky traveled a lot and loved to enjoy nature. Therefore, he dreamed of making clean ponds in his estate. Since earlier the area was rather swampy, he did his best to keep the ponds clean. For this, he used, for example, cattail, which is quite similar to reeds. Now Terletsky Forest Park cannot boast the same crystal clear water in the ponds, but the reservoirs are always carefully monitored and cleaned.

How to get there

Most Muscovites know the location of the park, but not everyone can give the exact address. You can find Terletsky Forest Park on a map on the Internet if you enter 41 Metallurgov Street into a search engine.

You can get to the final destination by metro and bus. You need to get to the Novogireevo station, and then change to buses under the numbers 615, 617, 645 or 776. Get off at the stop called Terletsky Park.

If you get to the metro station "Perovo", then you can get to the park by minibus 473. Then you need to get off at the stop "House of Veterans of the Scene". Minibuses number 104, 291 and 202 from the metro station "Enthusiasts Highway" go to the same point.

Visitors reviews

Terletsky Forest Park is very popular among Muscovites and guests of the capital. Its bewitching nature simply catches the eye, allowing you to relax your soul and body. The townspeople like the place so much that it has its own pages on the Internet. On Instagram you can see recent photos of visitors to the park, and you can also join similar groups on other popular social networks. The marvelous nature captivates people so much that many of them write entire posts on their blogs, backing them up with photographs.

A considerable number of guests leave their reviews, so that it is easier for other users to have an idea about this place. They describe beautiful nature, numerous species of trees, as well as unique ponds. It is to them that visitors come to feed the ducks. These residents of the forest park are always happy to welcome guests. Many citizens love to ride bicycles, and also feel unity with nature here.
