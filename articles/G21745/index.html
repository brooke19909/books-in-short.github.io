Kansashi Flowers - Japanese Art

Japan is a mysterious and interesting country with many traditions and a rich history. For example, a simple hair ornament in the form of a hairpin or comb has its origin and is of great importance for Asian culture.



History of occurrence

In the Middle Ages in Japan, women switched from old-fashioned to more modern hairstyles, which were very different from the old ones in that they began to style their hair with intricate shapes. And in order for all this to last as long as possible, we used the well-known hairpins, hairpins and combs. Initially, maidservants began to make kanzashi flowers, traditionally this was considered their occupation, which they could do in their free time. But then this craft flowed into the masses, and fabric items in the form of flowers began to be used to decorate a variety of hair accessories. Moreover, they are so loved by everyone that they have acquired special significance. So, for example, by the kind of hairpin or hairpin on a girl or woman, it was possible to determine almost everything: whether she was married, how many children she had. Kanzashi flowers also indicated the lady‚Äôs social status, because some hairpins were much more expensive than the most sophisticated kimono. They have become an important part of fashion and culture in general.



Modern significance

Now, kanzashi flowers in Japan are the same as kokoshniks in Russia, they are worn only by people associated with the traditions and rites of medieval Asia. Geisha, yuzo, brides put on them during tea ceremonies or use to create ikebana. Although recently, young girls have begun to revive the culture of this craft, which is most likely explained by their desire for elegance and grace. More recently, this technique has come to us, more and more young women are beginning their journey in understanding the Japanese art of creating flowers from fabrics and ribbons.

What are kanzashi flowers

These jewelry are made of almost any fabric, but it is preferable to use satin ribbons, they look more impressive. But where you adapt them depends only on your imagination. This can be an elastic band for hair, a plastic, wooden or fabric rim, and a crocodile clip, or you can create whole bouquets and give them to friends. This will be a great hobby for you or your children. Already now in shops or magazines it has become possible to find master classes that will not only tell you how to do

kanzashi flower, but also explain in detail where to apply it. You can go now and stock up on everything you need, namely ribbons, glue and a cigarette lighter; you can easily find everything else at home. A lighter is needed to solder cuts, because the tape has the peculiarity of strewing, and glue is needed to make more complex flowers.

Color selection

Oddly enough, color is of great importance, it should be chosen in accordance with the time of year and month. In the Middle Ages, geishas wore kimonos and accessories strictly in the sequence of changing seasons. Colors also changed depending on the class. For spring, for example, white and pink colors are characteristic, since fruit trees are just starting to bloom, but autumn is habitually appearing in orange and red tones, all this is also displayed in hair ornaments. It may not be of such importance to us, but traditions should be respected and respected with such ancient art. Satin ribbon kanzashi flower is a simple but elegant decoration that can be worn at any age.
