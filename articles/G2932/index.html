Hotel Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort 3 *: overview, description, features and reviews

Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort is a great place for those who want to combine swimming in the sea, acquaintance with cultural attractions, as well as comfort and low cost of living.





Hotel Summary

Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort offers its guests excellent conditions for relaxation, because, in addition to the fact that the institution is literally a few steps from the beach, there are also two outdoor pools for those who do not like to swim in the open sea. In all public areas of the hotel you will be able to use free wireless internet. Which, you see, is of great importance today.

Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort has at its disposal a large number of rooms, which consists of comfortable guest apartments. They provide a complete set of all necessary furniture for relaxation, as well as many technical capabilities. A pleasant moment is that from the windows of your room you can admire the flowering garden or the sea landscape.

Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort offers its guests many opportunities to fill their leisure time. In this regard, the services of a tour desk are very popular, which will help you choose an interesting and eventful tourist route. There are also many opportunities for sports lovers. And in order to relax after an active day, visit the spa or use the services of professional massage therapists.





Resort Location

Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort 3 * hotel is located on one of the most beautiful beaches, which is called Ham Tuan Nam. Also not far away is the famous Ke-Ga lighthouse, which is considered almost the main attraction of this area. For golf lovers, the good news is that there is a specialized club only 20 minutes from the hotel.





Resort Resort Guest Apartments

Hotel Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort (4, not three stars, according to tourists, the hotel deserves this criterion) is quite large and has 160 rooms in its room stock. The hotel can offer travelers several accommodation options:

- Standard Apartment - This is a small bedroom, designed for two adults. It is also possible to provide additional space for the child. Here you can be offered accommodation on a king-size bed or a pair of single. The balconies of these rooms overlook the garden.

- The deluxe rooms are distinguished by their refined interiors, although they do not exceed the standard in size. The apartments of this class have recently been redecorated, so their appearance is consistent with the declared high level.

- Double room is a two-room suite, which is quite suitable for families. Each of the bedrooms is adjoined by its own bathroom, which creates comfortable conditions for the simultaneous accommodation of four guests, in addition, two more additional folding beds can be provided.

- A suite is a large apartment with a large level of comfort. In addition to the bedroom and living room, there is also a very small kitchenette, as well as an entrance hall.

One of the most popular accommodation options is a bungalow. These small houses are located near the beach, so you can enjoy the picturesque sea landscape at any time of the day, sitting on your own terrace. The interior and spaciousness of the bungalows resemble standard apartments.





Amenities at Guest Apartments

Taking care of the comfort of its guests, the Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort 3 * hotel provides the following list of necessary amenities in the rooms:

  • depending on the class of your apartment, the bathroom will be equipped with a shower or a large bath;
  • a modern TV broadcasts many satellite channels, among which are Russian-speaking;
  • so that you can contact the reception staff at any time of the day, there is a telephone in the room, which, in addition, has access to an international line;
  • individual air conditioning is responsible for a comfortable microclimate in your apartments;
  • the room has a desk, which, if necessary, can also be used as a dining room;

  • you can always make yourself hot drinks, because the room has an electric kettle, a service, as well as coffee and tea bags.




Positive reviews

Tourists from around the world have long had time to fall in love with the Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort (Vietnam). A huge number of positive reviews only confirms the good reputation of this hotel:

  • at night, the hotel and its entire territory looks very beautiful thanks to the spectacular illumination with LED bulbs and garlands;
  • the hotel staff is very polite and friendly, and it is especially worth noting the work of the reception staff, who quickly and without unnecessary delays solve all issues related to check-in;
  • despite the fact that the hotel is located far from the city, and there are no catering facilities nearby, the restaurant prices are not too high;
  • and at the pools and on the beach there are always many free sunbeds, even at a time when the hotel is completely full.

Negative reviews

In addition to the positive comments, tourists also leave a number of negative feedback regarding their stay at Sai Gon Suoi Nhum Resort. Traveler reviews contain the following negative information:

  • it takes almost 7 hours to get to the hotel from Kamran International Airport, which is simply unbearable after a long and tiring flight;
  • the beach is rarely cleaned, and therefore it is constantly bombarded with bottles, plastic dishes and other garbage that tourists and fishermen throw out;
  • breakfast menu, although quite extensive, but the dishes are prepared very poorly and tasteless (even stale products come across);
  • it is very difficult to communicate with the hotel staff, as they do not speak not only Russian, but do not even speak English;
  • very inconvenient location - away from city infrastructure, as well as entertainment and retail outlets.
