Burgas, Bulgaria - city hotels and beach hotels

Burgas is the fourth largest city in Bulgaria, located on the Black Sea coast. Burgas has an international airport, a sea station, a railway station and two bus stations.

Transport in Burgas

Burgas International Airport welcomes and sends passengers from 130 cities around the world. It is second only to Sofia Airport, serving more than 2 million passengers a year. The new passenger terminal is equipped with modern luggage systems, comfortable spaces and shopping areas.

The highway from Burgas to Sofia can be reached in about 3 hours, and the path to Varna will be about 2 hours.

Since 2013, the sea station has been operating in the city, where passenger ships call. In the summer season, you can get to Varna, Nessebar and Sozopol by sea on a high-speed comet.

Acquaintance with the city

Upon arrival to Burgas in Bulgaria, tourists always want to get to know the city as close as possible, see all its attractions, visit restaurants and shops. Public transport in the city is the most modern in Bulgaria, taxis are affordable, and for lovers of bicycles in Burgas there is a municipal rental system.

Holidays in Burgas in Bulgaria mean getting acquainted with city sights, architectural objects, visiting museums, galleries and temples, swimming in the sea and in pools. The beaches in the city are world famous for their dark color due to the impurity of magnetite - a black mineral with a healing effect.

The hotels of Burgas Bulgaria have different stars and locations, but they all offer a wonderful holiday on the Black Sea.

Grand Hotel Primorets 5 *

This hotel combines discreet luxury, a high level of service, a unique location and the services of a modern wellness center. This is one of the best hotels in Bulgaria, awarded many prizes and awards. Primorets is located in the heart of the Burgas Sea Garden, right on the beach 10 minutes from the pedestrian promenade and 15 minutes from the airport.

The hotel offers:

  • Over 100 hotel rooms and suites with sea and city views.
  • Wireless internet access in all rooms, restaurants and bars.
  • Ground and underground parking.
  • The shops.
  • Restaurant "Primorets" with a VIP lounge.
  • Salini Restaurant with summer terrace and conservatory.
  • Lobby bar with summer terrace, lounge bar.
  • Conference center, banquet hall.

Hotels of Burgas 4 stars

The Mirage Hotel is located just a kilometer from the center of Burgas. It offers air-conditioned rooms and free Wi-Fi in public areas. The refined and comfortable restaurant with 100 seats offers local and international cuisine.

Hotel Bulgaria is located in the very center of Burgas and offers rooms with views of the bay or the city center. In two restaurants, guests can enjoy dishes of national Bulgarian cuisine, while bars on different floors of the buildings invite you to enjoy a cocktail or coffee.

Majestic Beach Resort is an elegant and comfortable hotel on the beach, about 30 minutes from the airport. This complex is considered a paradise for both a modern tourist who wants to relax and for a business person negotiating. Each room in the hotel creates a feeling of warmth and comfort and is equipped with central air conditioning, a TV, a safe, a bathroom and a minibar.

Atlantic Resort - the complex is located just 200 meters from the sandy beach in the luxurious Sarafovo district. The hotel offers apartments and studios with stunning sea views. The price includes access to the outdoor pool and the largest spa in Burgas. Free internet and air conditioning are standard amenities at the resort. All studios and apartments have a comfortable living room and a balcony. Accommodation in Atlantic Resort is a vacation in Burgas on the seashore.

The best 3 star hotels in Burgas

Hotel Bulair is located in a historic 19th-century building, just 100 meters from the city center and 300 meters from the nearest beach. The restaurant serves Bulgarian dishes, and breakfast is included in the price. Air-conditioned rooms come with free Wi-Fi, cable TV and a minibar. The junior suite has a cozy seating area. Bathrooms have showers and hairdryers are available upon request.

The Plaza Sarafovo complex is just a 5-minute drive from Burgas Airport. The hotel is open year-round and offers apartments with a bedroom, a living room, a kitchen and a bathroom. Guests can also relax in the lobby bar, sauna and seasonal outdoor pools. The Plaza restaurant offers a wide selection of excellent seafood and a full menu with varied cuisine. Guests can enjoy their meals in the elegant restaurant, or enjoy casual dining by the pool.

Hotel "Aqua" is a modern business hotel offering "special" accommodation. There are large modern rooms, and presidential apartments, as well as budget rooms for business travelers. The hotel is located 1.5 kilometers from the Sea Garden, two kilometers from the city center and 10 kilometers from Burgas Airport.

Hotel Milano is a small boutique hotel with a Mediterranean restaurant and an outdoor garden.

Bulgaria, Burgas and the sea - a great cocktail for a summer vacation!
