Fishing village, Lazarevskoye: an inexpensive vacation for families with children

The coast of the Black Sea, despite the openness of its borders, is attractive to our citizens. One of the attractive places is the Lazarevskoye coast, the Fishing Village. What is he like?

Lazarevskoye village

The settlement is large enough, about 60 thousand people live in it. The coastline stretches for 100 kilometers, from the Shepsi settlement to Dagomys, on the right and left sides of the banks of the Psezuapse river. The width of the beaches averages 50 meters. There are no breakwaters and underwater fortifications. Pebble beach.

The village of Lazarevskoye and the Fisherman's Village are located at the foot of the Caucasus Range. The climate here is subtropical, and this is an opportunity to improve your health and see the unique vegetation. Rains are rare here, and if they are, then short-term.

Railways stretching along the coastline are closed with special shields. Special bridges are equipped for the transition.

The village itself has a developed infrastructure, and in the evenings it will not be boring. There are many restaurants, cafes and nightclubs. During the day on the beach, all the entertainment known to date is presented, from riding a "banana" to parachuting.





Historical reference

For the first time, people began to populate these territories 4,000 years ago.

The official date of foundation of the settlement is 1839, and it was created as a military fort. Military action destroyed the fort several times. Then the Pontic Greeks settled here , and by the beginning of the XX century the village was already developed, the railway was working, summer residences began to appear, a hospital, shops and other social infrastructure facilities were built. The village received the status of a resort only in 1949.

How to get there

You can get to Lazarevskoye and Rybatskiy villages directly by train, which can take you to your final destination. The number of trains in the summer season is increasing.

You can fly to Adler by plane, and then get to the village by train, train or taxi. To the coast from the airport is only 94 kilometers.

If you decide to travel by personal vehicle, first you need to get to Krasnodar, turn to Dzhubga and follow the signs for Sochi.





Fishing. A place for those who love comfort

There will be no problems with housing in Lazarevsky, the Fishing Village. In the village there are many hotels, private houses, apartments, resorts, boarding houses and boathouses. In terms of pricing, they are all very different. The main thing is to correctly prioritize for yourself, which is more important - comfort, proximity to the sea and other parameters.

If peace and comfort are important, then it is best to give preference to rest in the Fishing Village (Lazarevskoye). It is here that the sea is close, there is no noise and nearby nightlife. You can not hear the noise of moving trains on the railway.

The beaches here are equipped with everything you need, there are no cars, and the center is just 15 minutes on foot with a leisurely walk. Conventionally, three beaches are attached to this village:

  • sanatorium "Odyssey";
  • sanatorium "Lazarevskoye";
  • "Fisherman" or "Lagoon".

The cost of living here is average. In the village there are many cafes and dining rooms with the same affordable prices.

The main feature of the village of Rybatsky is that there is a lake called Divnoye, where you can fish and then cook yourself dinner from what was caught.





Housing

Rybatskoye (Lazarevskoye) village is a small settlement with private boathouses of different floors - from 3 to 5. The rooms here are for every taste and pocket. Boathouse can be rented completely for a large family or company, or just a room.

For example, Elling 29 is a four-story building with 3 rooms. It has a shared kitchen. And, most importantly, the beach is just a couple of steps. There is a washing machine, Internet, multicooker and ironing board with iron. The rooms can accommodate from 2 to 4 people, all sanitary conditions in the room are met. In the season of high prices per person, you will have to pay about 1 thousand rubles.

Nearby is the buoy boathouse. This is a 4-storey building with 6 rooms. The beach is only 30 meters, all the amenities in the rooms. For a standard room in the season of high prices, they will ask for 2,300 rubles. For additional conditions, for example, for a balcony and air conditioning, you will have to pay more - about 2600 rubles per room.





What vacationers say

Naturally, the reviews about the Fishing Village (Lazarevskoye), as well as about other places, are very different. Someone does not like the pebble beach, and someone does not like the fact that there are few entertainment venues nearby. Indeed, the village is quiet and more suitable for families with children, there is no fuss and fewer people, when compared with the center of Lazarevsky. The village of Rybatsky is suitable for an economical but comfortable vacation on the Black Sea.
