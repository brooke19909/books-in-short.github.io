Pattern of children's pajamas for a boy and a girl: description, diagram and recommendations

What is the key to good mood and energy for the whole day? Healthy and sound sleep. That is why both children and adults need to relax with maximum comfort, putting on delicate and soft pajamas.

Pattern of children's pajamas, recommendations for choosing fabrics and colors - all this you will find in our article.





The importance of pajamas

Today, this type of clothing is not only a nocturnal wardrobe item, warming up during sleep, but also convenient clothing for the home, in which it is comfortable to move around the apartment in the evening and Sunday morning.

Children grow extremely fast, and as they grow they need to change their wardrobe. This also includes children's pajamas. A pattern that is easy to manufacture, minimal needlework skills and the desire to make something pleasant for your child - that's all it takes to sew a night suit for the baby. Of course, it is desirable to have a sewing machine and an overlock.

How to choose the color of children's pajamas?

There is no shortage of fabrics on store shelves today. However, remember that design pajamas need to be taken seriously. For example, many mothers want to sew son pajamas in the form of a superhero costume.

However, it will be extremely difficult to put the baby to sleep in such pajamas, because children are so easily addicted. And then try to convince that you dressed him with Spider-man so that he went to bed, and did not go to perform feats.

This wardrobe item with vivid pictures will produce approximately the same effect. The kid will want to see his brother, sister, grandmother, aunt, uncle in him, but just going to bed in him is so boring.

It is better to give preference to fabric with soft pictures or even monophonic. This item is especially important for children who suffer from sleep disturbance.

Pajamas style

The pattern of children's pajamas for boys is presented, as a rule, in the form of a free-cut jacket and the same pants. For younger children, you can also sew a jumpsuit. The main rule - it is better to make pajamas a little more: nothing should hamper the movements in a dream.

The pattern of children's pajamas for the girl is represented by a wider assortment. The most classic version is close to the male, that is, wide pants and a wide shirt. An analogue of a more modern one is narrow pants that resemble men's pants with an elastic band on the ankle. The summer version may consist of short shorts and a shoulder strap.





What material to sew pajamas?

It is known that children are more difficult than adults to tolerate both cold and heat, so each baby should have at least two pajamas: a summer version and a winter one.

Summer option . It will be optimal to sew a T-shirt with short sleeves and short shorts. It is better to give preference to fabrics that are at least 80% natural. A material such as cotton is ideal: it will pleasantly cool the body in the heat, hypoallergenic and wear-resistant.

Winter option . Give preference to a set of pants and a shirt or long-sleeved T-shirt. Material - warm dense knitwear or flannel. They are made of cotton. In addition, we recommend paying attention to bamboo: fabrics made from it are distinguished by their silkiness, softness and durability.

Pattern of children's pajamas from flannel

This material is soft, warm and durable. He is in great demand. Flannel is extremely pleasant to the body due to the fact that it has a rare pile. As a rule, this fabric is used for sewing home clothes and diapers.

Flannel: varieties and characteristics

This stuff is very soft. It is not without reason that it is used for sewing children's things. Flannel clothing has many advantages, for example, durability, hypoallergenicity. It looks beautiful, allows the body to breathe, is resistant to stretching and bending, and also tolerates washing.





Flannel Varieties

Often, ready-made clothes are not of high quality. Therefore, many mothers are interested in how to sew baby pajamas from flannel. First of all, you need to determine the type of fabric.

For children's pajamas, you can use printed, bleached, dressing gown or one-colored material. Stuffed flannel will delight the eye with different patterns, bleached is completely devoid of pattern and color, and one-colored is the same in color from the front and the wrong side.

Can I sew pajamas without a pattern?

In order to please your baby with new pajamas, just have a sewing machine, fabric and desire. Knowledge about the pattern of the product is not always required. Indeed, in order to sew something for the child, you can simply make the necessary patterns for any of his existing clothes.

For example, if you need to make pajama pants, then you can take pants from a tracksuit, or from an old pajama set. Next, add a few centimeters from the bottom and top, circle. Everything is ready, it remains only to sew!
