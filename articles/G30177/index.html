PrivatBank: reviews of specialists and ordinary customers

PrivatBank was founded more than twenty years ago in 1992. At the moment, he occupies a leading position in the credit market of Ukraine and is the largest institution in terms of domestic capital. PrivatBank, reviews of which allow you to make a variety of opinions, currently serves more than 30% of the country's population. The authorized capital of a financial organization is more than 5.5 billion UAH., And the net annual profit exceeds 2 billion UAH.



PrivatBank: customer reviews


Today, this financial institution is the most popular among its citizens among Ukrainian citizens. This is partly due to the fact that the majority of state-owned enterprises entered into agreements with the bank and forcibly opened salary cards in it to their employees. This has increased the number of customers by more than three times. With a large flow of people, service usually starts to deteriorate first. This pattern did not bypass PrivatBank either. Reviews about him recently are more negative than positive. Many people cannot understand the work of the banking system and worry about their savings. In addition, a commission is paid for the cards paid by its owner, which is not always liked by workers with low wages.



In most of its brochures, a financial institution advertises the Privat-24 online banking system. It allows you to work with your account without visiting a bank branch. In addition, you can connect various cards to it and carry out various transactions both to the accounts of other organizations, and to PrivatBank itself. Remote work, reviews of which praise its convenience, but scold instability and poor technical support, allows you to make SWIFT transfers, transfer money to a foreign bank account. Transfer of funds is possible only after entering a special password sent via SMS to the registered mobile phone of the account holder.



PrivatBank: credit card reviews

A financial institution issues a huge variety of different plastic cards. These include:

  • credit;
  • salary;
  • registered.

Card activation occurs at an ATM, and work with it through an online banking system or with the help of a manager in a branch of PrivatBank organization. Reviews about the system are very different. Some people like her, others can't even start working with her. However, many note frequent interruptions. SMS may not come, and the site itself is offline for a long time. Due to such problems in the system, it can be very difficult to make a money transfer even between two different accounts within the PrivatBank financial institution. Reviews of situations when this action is necessary to perform, but cannot be done online, recommend the Privat Money system. With it, you can send a transfer to any country with only a passport in hand. In the form you need to indicate the surname and name of the recipient and then pass him a special code. He will be needed to receive money in any department where operations with Privat Money are performed. The organization takes 1% of the transferred amount for its services and charges this commission from the sender.
