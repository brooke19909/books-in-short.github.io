Ski resort Dombay: how to get from Moscow

Over the past decade, the village of Dombay in the Karachay-Cherkess Republic has become one of the most popular Russian ski resorts with developed infrastructure. Snow-white mountain peaks, swift rivers with powerful waterfalls, beautiful alpine lakes, picturesque fir forests and alpine meadows, clean mountain air - this is all Dombay. How to get from Moscow? This question is increasingly being asked in search engines by Internet users. Consider the various options for getting to Dombai, so that when you travel, you can choose the most comfortable option for yourself, and minimize the time loss.





Features of a trip to Dombay from Moscow

When going to Dombay, first of all, it should be noted that the resort has no airport or railway station, so transfers are inevitable. The rest of the journey will have to be overcome by bus or car. From the major cities of the North Caucasus region, where you will arrive by plane or train, you can reach Dombai in several ways.

Regular buses regularly run between the cities of the Stavropol Territory and Karachay-Cherkessia. Most likely, you will have to do another transplant in Cherkessk or Teberda. Avoid the shortcomings of travel by public transport will allow a taxi, which can be ordered in advance, using the capabilities of a special portal.

Many owners of private hotels in Dombay can arrange transfers from the airport or train station by agreement. Renting a car (or initially driving your own car) is the best option for those who plan to visit not only Dombai but also visit other interesting places in the North Caucasus.





How to get to Dombay from Moscow by plane

Aircraft is the fastest, but also the most expensive way to travel. You can fly from the capital to the Mineralnye Vody airport closest to Dombay at any time from Sheremetyevo, Domodedovo and Vnukovo by direct flights of various airlines. About two hours after departure, you will already arrive at the largest of the Caucasian airports. An alternative is to fly from Moscow to Stavropol.

The further way from the airport to Dombay can be done with the Gulliver tourist bus, which runs daily during the ski season from December to March. The departure time of the bus is better to clarify in advance. At other times, you can take a minibus to the Minvod bus station and overcome the rest of the way to the resort by regular bus with a change in Teberda. Connoisseurs of speed and comfort will prefer to continue the route from the airport by taxi, and in less than four hours they will arrive in Dombay - a ski resort!

How to get from Moscow by train

Going to Dombay by rail is a relatively inexpensive and quite acceptable option. From the Kursk, Paveletsky and Kazan railway stations of the capital, trains leave for Mineralnye Vody, Nevinnomyssk and Kislovodsk. You will have to spend 28‚Äì38 hours on the road.

Options for further movement depend on which train station you are arriving at. From Mineralnye Vody station, the ways to get to Dombay are the same as from the airport.

You can always leave Kislovodsk by regular bus to Teberda, and then transfer to a minibus to the village. You can also take the opportunity of sightseeing buses of Kislovodsk travel companies organizing one-day tours to Dombai.

Despite the fact that Nevinnomyssk from the railway stations is the closest to Dombay, it is impossible to directly get from it to the village. You will have to get by bus or train to Cherkessk, and then by minibus to the resort.





How to get from Moscow to Dombai by bus

There is another way to get to Dombay. How to get from Moscow? There is another option - bus. A number of companies organize direct flights from Moscow to Dombay on comfortable and modern superior-comfort buses equipped with everything necessary for a pleasant trip. They are equipped with dry closets, televisions, audio-video systems, boilers. This is the cheapest (also non-stop) option, perfect for those who are not in a hurry.

True, such buses make daily or weekly flights only during the ski season, so those who wish to relax in Dombay at another time of the year will have to look for other ways to travel.

Dombay: how to get from Moscow by car

Traveling to Dombay with your own car is a budget option that many people like. This option attracts with its independence from transport schedules, the lack of transfers and a great opportunity to admire the amazing panoramic views of the Caucasus. You can stop where you want.
