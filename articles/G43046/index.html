The most important issue when planning a trip is the choice of accommodation. One of the best hotels in Mexico is Riu Lupita 5 *. Hotel address and other useful information

Riu Lupita 5 * (Mexico) is located at: Lote Hotelero 1, Manzana 20, Condominios Playacar, Playa del-Karmen, QROO, 77710, Mexico.

The hotel is 50 km away from Cancun International Airport, 2 km from Playa del Carmen and 3.5 km from the resort's main beach, where the hotel has its own plot. Not far from Rio Lupita is also the Port of Playa del Carmen, the Golf Club Playacar, the Quinta Avenida Promenade and the Yucatek Divers Diving Center.





Description of Riu Lupita 5 * in Mexico

It is part of a large chain of Riu hotels and is a complex consisting of a main building, one- and three-story buildings. Each building is equipped with rooms of two categories, the total number of which is 300 rooms.

The complex is surrounded by lush, lush, vibrant tropical vegetation. She, like a fence, protects vacationers from the outside world. The common areas and rooms at Rio Lupita are decorated in a Mexican style, with modern design elements found in some areas.

The hotel has a well-developed infrastructure, cultural and entertainment programs and leisure for children and adults are thought out, sightseeing routes are developed, and therefore Riu Lupita is perfect for youth, romantic and family vacations.

Rooms at Riu Lupita 5 *, Riviera Maya, Mexico

The rooms are spacious, the design is dominated by calm tones, which have a comfortable rest. These are standard double and triple rooms, as well as family rooms, which are suitable for couples in love, groups of friends and families with children. Each room has a balcony or terrace with views of the hotel. The rooms are furnished with modern quality furniture: a bed, bedside tables, a chest of drawers and a table with chairs. In Riu Lupita 5 * (Mexico) there are special rooms adapted for people with disabilities. There are holders to make it convenient to use the bathroom in a wheelchair, and the location of the rooms allows you to easily get inside.





Dining at the hotel

Accommodation in Rio Lupita is provided through the All inclusive system. The price includes basic meals, snacks, alcoholic and non-alcoholic local and imported drinks, replenishment of the minibar and dispenser in the rooms.

Three meals a day are served according to the "buffet" system:

  • breakfast: light meals, whole grains, snacks;
  • lunch: hot and cold dishes at Cozumel Beach Club;
  • dinner: various dishes of local and other cuisines of the world.

On the territory of Riu Lupita 5 * (Riviera Maya, Mexico) there are 3 restaurants and 2 bars. A buffet is available at Adelita with an outdoor terrace. Maria Bonita Restaurant offers guests Mexican cuisine while Cozumel, located on the beach, serves grilled meat. Tequila Bar is located by the pool, while gourmet snacks can be enjoyed at Panchito.

Living conditions and requirements from the administration

Hotel Riu Lupita 5 * (Mexico) offers all-inclusive accommodation. Thus, not only the main three meals a day, snacks and drinks are provided for free, but also many services, such as daily room service, a shuttle service to the beach, a night club, use of the pool, fitness room and sports grounds.

To take advantage of all the benefits provided for the newlyweds, when booking a room, you must make an appropriate note. When visiting the hotel on Christmas (24.12) and New Year's (31.12), a gala dinner is not paid separately, as it is already included in the price.

Check-in is at 15:00, check-out is at 12:00. Upon check-out, you will be required to pay a compulsory health tax of 20 pesos per night.

The cost of a trip can vary significantly depending on the date of travel, length of stay and room category. Hotel reviews

If you combine all the reviews of vacationers, the average hotel rating will be 9 points out of 10. Tourists note a high level of service, as hotel workers are trying hard to meet the requirements of their guests. In restaurants and bars a large amount of food and drink. Three meals a day are very tasty and varied: they serve meat and fish dishes, there are always a lot of vegetables and fruits. Rooms are regularly cleaned and bed linen is changed.

The negative points that occur in the reviews of Riu Lupita 5 * (Mexico) are mainly related to the design and equipment of the rooms. Many vacationers believe that a five-star hotel should have more stylish and furnished rooms. Also among the minuses are the lack of sinks for washing hands in a restaurant and garbage in the sand on the beach.
