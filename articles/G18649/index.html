The best quotes of Napoleon Bonaparte about love, war and the state

The French commander Napoleon Bonaparte was a great man. Naturally, today many people condemn him for trying to conquer the Russian Empire, but the truth is that he has long been paid for this arrogant move. More importantly, this Frenchman had an amazing intellect, able to shed light on many philosophical questions. In support of this, we cite the most striking quotes of Napoleon Bonaparte, preserved to this day.

Reasoning about the war

The French commander was an unsurpassed strategist and tactician. Thanks to this, his army won many victories, and the commander himself was able to ascend the throne of the emperor.





Therefore, it is not surprising that many quotes by Napoleon Bonaparte are devoted to the intricacies of military affairs:

  • If the army of barons is headed by a lion, then it will easily defeat the same army of lions under the head of a ram.
  • Even the most gifted commander, losing his fighting spirit and dignity, turns into complete mediocrity.
  • The truth is that one bad commander is much better than two good ones. After all, where one gives orders, two argue about their importance.

About love

Despite the military character of the commander, he knew how to take good care of the girls. Moreover, some quotes by Napoleon Bonaparte describe the relationship between people so subtly that sometimes it can be confused with a poet or philosopher:

  • A beautiful lady attracts her eyes, and a good lady draws her heart. The first can be a real decoration in the house, and the second - the most valuable treasure.
  • There is only one way to defeat love - by running away as far as possible.
  • Love is great stupidity done together.

Quotes of Napoleon Bonaparte about Russia

The French emperor talked a lot about Russia, and about how he wanted to conquer it. For him, this country has become that bewitching peak that he could not conquer. Subsequently, he uttered a phrase that even today sounds from the lips of many peoples: "Russia deserves to be unchallenged."
