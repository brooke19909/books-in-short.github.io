How to get from Krasnogorsk to Moscow? Several budget ways

Krasnogorsk is one of the most densely populated cities in the Moscow region. Most people who need to travel to the capital use public transport. How can I get to Krasnogorsk from Moscow? By public transport, and each route has positive and negative sides.

Train

This type of public transport is the fastest and most convenient, because unlike a bus service or a taxi, the electric train follows directly, without the risk of being caught in an hour-long traffic jam. You can go from either of the two stations: Riga or Kursky. Travel time takes about 40 minutes with all stops. A big plus is that there is no need to go to end stations. The train departing from the center of Moscow arrives in Krasnogorsk, making only eight stops:

  • Pavshino
  • "Knitted";
  • Tushino
  • "Pokrovskoye-Streshnevo";
  • Leningradskaya
  • "The Red Baltic";
  • "Civil";
  • Dmitrovskaya.

It is worth remembering that between following the trains there is a technical break from 11:00 to 13:00. This fact must be taken into account and plan ahead your route and travel time.

Many electric trains connecting several satellite cities also pass through Krasnogorsk.

Bus

Now about how to get from Krasnogorsk to Moscow by bus or minibus. The first option is one of the most popular. On the territory of Moscow and the region, special lanes have been created for buses along which they follow without delay according to their schedule. But here, too, you need to be careful and take into account city-wide traffic: a time when there is a huge chance of getting into a serious traffic jam. Bus route 542 leaves from Tushino metro station every 20 minutes. Ticket price is 100 rubles. Student and pension certificates, the age of children under five are taken into account. At more frequent intervals follow minibuses No. 120.

To Krasnogorsk by metro

Many residents are considering different options for how to get to Krasnogorsk from Moscow by metro. Unfortunately, there is no direct route directly to the city. If you use the metro, it will be more convenient to get to the Tushinskaya station. There, transfer to the bus that goes to Krasnogorsk. Another metro station is Mitino. From here, too, buses and fixed-route taxis depart to Krasnogorsk at different intervals. The first type of public transport leaves from the metro stations Tushinskaya, Mitino and Skhodnenskaya.

Passenger transport

If you ask car drivers how to get to Krasnogorsk from Moscow, you can hear only one answer - on the MKAD. Krasnogorsk is one of the cities closest to the capital, and this distance is only five kilometers from the ring road. Travel time by car in the absence of traffic jams is about 25 minutes.

If a person got into a traffic jam, then everything is very unpredictable. You can stand for an hour or more. The most convenient routes run along Volokolamskoye and Pyatnitskoye shosse. Getting to Novorizhskiy will be more difficult. Among all directions, it is the last highway that is considered the busiest.

Is it worth it to get to Krasnogorsk by taxi?

The same applies to ordering a car. While for a taxi there is no separate lane. They move on the basis of the same rules as ordinary cars. Therefore, it is necessary to take into account all possible errors in advance and lay extra time on the road. The average price of a taxi varies from 800 to 1,000 rubles one way.

Each person decides how to get to Krasnogorsk from Moscow, based on personal needs and financial capabilities. When planning a trip, you always need to consider the factors of time and unforeseen circumstances that can ruin the plans.
