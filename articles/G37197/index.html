When to come

When can you see such a stunning sight as the singing fountains (Sochi)? Opening hours from May to August daily from 20:00 - 21:00. With the onset of September, time moves to 19:30 - 20:30, and from November 2, the performance can be seen even earlier - from 18:00 - 19:00. This applies only to evening shows.

Scale

The show of singing fountains (Sochi) is compared with the flapping of the wings of a firebird, whose image is firmly entrenched in our tales. The scale of the structure is amazing, because the diameter of the bowl is 75 m. Each of the 264 fountains has an individual illumination.

The guns with which the design is equipped can lift water up to 30 m. There are 252 of them, of which 6 are considered hyper-guns, and the rest are mini-guns. Manages the action of a specialist sitting at a special remote control. He has the responsibility of combining all the functions performed by the structure into a single composition.
