Unrivaled Gegsky waterfalls - a magical place of the earth

The historical, cultural and incredibly colorful region called Abkhazia is one of the most marvelous places on the planet. Its inhabitants experienced many coups, constant changes in the political regime, war, revolution and other negative factors, but despite all this they managed to remain faithful to their traditions and customs. In the same beauty and health is the local nature, which people come from all over the world to see. And among all the assets of this region, it is worth highlighting, perhaps, the Gegsky waterfalls, which are located near the city of Gagra.



Incredible beauty of nature envelops the entire Gagra mountain range. Evergreen trees and shrubs grow there, extraordinary animals live and, of course, the purest rivers flow that flow down the rocky shores and steep stone slopes. Thanks to them, Gegsky waterfalls were formed in the northern part of this mountain range. They are pure clear water, which reflects in its depths the greenery next to the growing trees and shrubs. It is also worth noting that the Gegsky waterfalls are part of the Gega river, from which they got their name and natural purity.

It is often difficult to figure out exactly where the spring comes from, because there are a lot of small grottoes in the local mountains, through which, however, without problems in large quantities and under strong pressure, water makes its way. The Gegsky waterfalls, which are fed by the waters of the underground river, also belong to the category of such ‚Äúinvisibles‚Äù. The second, in turn, rising to the very top of the cliff, make their way out through a narrow gorge and fall to the ground, forming a panorama of incredible beauty.



The Gegsky waterfall itself is 55 meters high, but it is considered one of the calmest in the territory of Abkhazia. Despite all the might and power of the local nature, tranquility and silence reigns here, which can only be broken by the sound of water and the rustle of leaves. Many argue that Gagra is one of the few places where everyone can find peace of mind and rethink their whole lives. Here you can also improve your health, get rid of many ailments and just breathe in fresh, ozone-full air.

It is also worth noting that any excursion to the Gegsky Falls is a kind of wellness lecture, during which you can admire the local nature and feel the adrenaline in the blood from the breathtaking peaks from which the water descends at high speed.

However, before sending, you should evaluate your own endurance - some people, especially from big cities, during such a long walk can become sick from a lot of humid air.

Now you understand that it is not without reason that Gegsky Falls is considered one of the most beautiful places in the world. It is very easy to find this place on the map, and if you have problems traveling to this area, the locals, who have always been distinguished by kindness and cordiality, will always help you find the right path.
