Investment life insurance: types, description, profitability and reviews

Almost everyone thinks one way or another about their future. Now there is a fairly large amount of collateral after a certain amount of time, and one of them is life insurance. The investment income of this approach, which will be considered in the framework of the article, allows not to lose the earned funds, but to receive additional amounts.

general information



What is cumulative investment life insurance? This tool is at the same time a symbiosis of two directions. It combines insurance and investment. Relate this tool to one thing, alas, it is not possible.

What is he like? In fact, this is a hybrid of classic accumulative life insurance, to which an investment component, presented as mutual funds, is added. This approach involves placing part of the portfolio (if the client wishes) in more risky and at the same time profitable financial instruments.

What is interesting about this combined approach?

Among the very first advantages it should be noted the availability of standard insurance coverage. What does this mean in practice? If a person decides to use such a combined tool, then he receives insurance to cover the initial investments and the amounts received as a result of investments. To this aspect, many are quite frivolous. Although, admittedly, accidents occur quite often.

Insurance programs are aimed at combating their consequences. They help maintain a stable financial position even in the most unforeseen circumstances.

The statement of such a problem is quite popular: if a person has lived to the end of the insurance period, and he has no problems, he will receive the amount deposited and the interest that has run over it. That is, money is not lost, and there is protection.

At the same time, a lot of pleasant bonuses are provided, which can vary in different companies. The most popular - in the event of a client losing his ability to work, the insurance company makes contributions for him, so that, ultimately, he receives all the necessary payments.

Profitability



Although the previous advantages can also boast of various savings programs. But in the case we are considering, there is still a small feature. And it consists in the income received. So, insurance savings programs allow you to get about 5-6%, which in our conditions does not even cover inflation. This is due to the conservative allocation of funds. And there is no desire to save it for decades.

And here comes investment life insurance. Rosgosstrakh or another company is not important. The role is played only by the mechanism itself. So, a significant part of the funds goes to investment tools. Their ratio in this case is usually 1: 4. That is, for every monetary unit that goes to the insurance part, four are invested.

Profitability between investments can be written in approximately the same ratio. In this case, you should be careful about those companies that offer incomes that are one and a half times higher than bank deposits and more.

Caution!



Why should you be careful about those to whom the money is transferred? A feature of investments is that they are more profitable than deposits, but at the same time more risky. But first things first.

If the interest is too high (30 or more), then there are two options: either scammers or organizations that invest in very dangerous assets. Yes, there is such that profit of 70, 100 and 300 percent per year is possible. But the likelihood of burnout here is also very high. Therefore, if there is a desire to save your money and not lose it, then you should focus on indicators in the region of 20-30 percent. Then there will be a low probability of losses, besides that, it will be insured, and by the end of the term solid capital will be collected.

To avoid falling into the hands of scammers, you should always ask about where the money is invested, ask for supporting documentation, and, of course, check it through other channels. You should also carefully study the investment life insurance contract, and if in doubt, consult a third-party lawyer (preferably not from across the street from the company).

Features in the Russian Federation



Most interested in the legislative aspect. In the Russian Federation, such quite useful tools as investment life insurance are practically undeveloped. Of course, there are basic prerequisites for work, but, alas, it is still far from a complete and clear design.

In order to circumvent legislation, two contracts are concluded. One regulates the issues of insurance, and the other - the investment program. And then a certain percentage of the funds goes to the necessary direction.

For a novice investor



We can confidently say that we live in a time when financial markets are quite unstable. Therefore, beginning investors face a serious dilemma: what to choose? Investment life insurance is suitable for those who want to invest in their future, and not get a penny from bank deposits, and at the same time do not want to take full risks. Therefore, you can take such a balanced and cautious approach.

Let's talk with an example about investment life insurance. Sberbank The profitability of one of the largest representatives of this type of service in the Russian Federation allows you to increase real, not nominal capital, up to 15 percent per year. Of course, this is in the optimal perspective. It may be such that it gives an increase of only 5%, but this is already an increase.

It is worth noting that the investment life insurance program is still designed for quite significant amounts. So, you need to save at least 3 months of income on the deposit in order for the application to be considered.

Why is there such an inconvenient restriction? The fact is that the market is constantly experiencing periods of growth and decline. And the likelihood of catching luck exists in the medium term. And for this you need to wait. And working with small amounts and deposits in such cases is extremely inconvenient.
