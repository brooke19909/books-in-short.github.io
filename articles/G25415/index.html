Mobile phone Nokia 6500 Slide: features and reviews

The history of the mobile phone, which has become the subject of our today's review, is quite entertaining. More specifically, today we will analyze the Nokia 6500 Slide, reviews of which the reader can find at the end of this article. Well, let's start with the introduction and a short story about the positioning of the device in the international mobile arena.

History of creation





The device was developed in the face of a change in the concept of model lines of the Finnish manufacturer. The company looked to the future with hope, clearly at the same time understanding what tasks it was facing and what it really would need to do in order to at least maintain its old position in the market and to make its way to leading positions as much as possible. Obviously, the peak of dissatisfaction with the current course of the company took place in 2005. Then, I remember, the Finnish manufacturer worked on a very important thing - changing the design and appearance of their devices. This saga ended in some ways sadly: the chief designer (and it was Frank Nuovo) voluntarily (but, of course, without a loud explanation of official reasons) left the post that he had previously held for several years.

Of course, I would not want to dramatize once again, but it was after this event that the next dark period came for the Finnish company. This manifested itself in a sharp and significant drop in sales. I remember that even then the company began to lag behind Motorola and the South Korean Samsung. But as expected, the Finnish manufacturer found the strength to return to the right path through more active advancement in terms of studying the appearance of competing devices. By the way, to resolve this issue, Nokia again used the above-mentioned competitive companies. And it should be said, very successfully.

Thus, the dismissal of Frank Nuovo from the post of chief designer can be regarded in two ways. On the one hand, the company lost its popularity due to it. But who knows, would Nokia manage to break into the international arena of mobile devices as effectively as it did now if Frank would remain in his post? It is possible that he would suggest a different direction and events would develop for the company not so colorful.

Project code name





Thus, the company soon came to the need to launch several separate projects. Among them, a model, codenamed Lynn, entered the development phase. And few people know that this pseudonym once received the subject of our review today - the Nokia 6500 Slide phone. The project was developed and taken over by the manager Christian Rice, and the engineers of the company started the technical aspects of the preparation of the apparatus near the end of summer, in August 2005. It is interesting that an approximate set of device functionality, as well as the first development results, were provided to the public by the company employees very, very soon, literally next month. And when the audience‚Äôs first opinion about the new product became known, the work on preparing the hardware and manufacturing materials entered the most active phase. Well, of course, then dozens of design options were tested at the same time, among which the only one, but really the best, was selected.

As a result, the final opinion on how the new product will look was formed already closer to the New Year, in December of the same 2005. The first variations of the device were made of ordinary plastic. After a couple of months, in February, testing of the device with focus groups was held in several cities (including Moscow). Note that they also presented the design that had the Nokia 6300 model. Interestingly, it gave way to a device under the name 6500 according to surveys. As it turned out, the appearance of this model was more preferable and pleasant.

From the very beginning, the Nokia 6500 Slide, the specifications of which will be indicated in this article, was made in silver. However, participants who tested the device as part of focus groups repeatedly spoke out for the use of a different design, since the ‚Äúsilver‚Äù device allegedly did not fit. Thus, the Finnish manufacturer decided to release the model in black, and after that - a bronze color scheme. But this only happened in March 2006.

At the same time, the hardware, which was developed specifically for the Nokia 6500 Slide (including the battery), formed the basis for the creation of subsequent devices. It has been applied to, for example, a model such as the Nokia 5310. Of course, we will not list all the devices, but the fact is still a fact.

Dimensions





In three-dimensional space, the dimensions of the device are 96.5 millimeters in height, 46.5 in width and 16.4 in thickness. At the same time, the mass of the device reaches the mark of 125 grams. The parameters clearly indicate that the device will fit perfectly in any pocket without causing discomfort when carrying it.

Specifications

  • Memory - 20 Mb.
  • Camera - 3 megapixels (optional - 1.3 megapixels).
  • Support for memory cards up to 4 MB.
  • The battery capacity is 900 mAh.

Color schemes





As mentioned earlier, the device was originally presented in silver. However, today you can buy a phone in black or bronze design. More variations are not provided and not made. But there is one very interesting fact. For a separate color scheme, a special theme is preinstalled on the phone. Thus, it complements the appearance of the device.

Keys





The material of manufacture is the most ordinary plastic. There is a key illumination for working and typing text messages in low light conditions, as well as in the complete absence of light. Actually, in the sun, letters and numbers on the keys are also visible quite well. The buttons are medium in size. Ergonomics are at a good level here, and the keys themselves are easily squeezed, practically without resistance. The responsiveness of the keyboard is beyond praise in general.
