The magnificent Karakan pine forest and relaxation in it

The Karakan forest is a stunningly beautiful pine forest that fascinates any traveler with its mystery and pristine nature. This place is a favorite place for those who like to pick mushrooms, enjoying the untouched virgin nature or for those who prefer to just walk in the fresh air.

It's no secret that a trip to Karakansky Bor is a great alternative to boring watching TV with the whole family. It is here that you can find cozy tourist camps, almost forgotten by God and hidden in the very wilderness of a pine forest.

Recreation facilities in the Karakan forest

Tourists can choose one of the many recreation centers in Bor. There are really a lot of them, but they are almost always busy at least 60-80 percent, and on holidays - all 100. Karakan, Oasis, Green Wedge or Chalet Karakan are just some of the options from the long list. But, undoubtedly, one of the “favorites” of travelers is the Sinemorye complex.

Go to an unforgettable adventure!

“Sinemorye” ... Often the entire number of rooms in the complex is in the “foot” a few months before the desired date of settlement. What exactly attracts tourists here from year to year?

  • Firstly, many people really appreciate their own huge green territory, on which it is difficult to find at least one small tick.




  • Secondly, the extremely good location of the camp site is right on the banks of the fast river.
  • Thirdly, the presence of a wide range of entertainment for every taste - for women, and for men, and for children.
  • Fourth, the warm welcome of the guests, the friendly attitude to the guests of the whole staff and the incomparably delicious food in the dining room.

What will the Karakansky pine forest give during the warm summer season?

The recreation center "Sinemorye" will equally successfully delight customers both in winter and in summer.

  • Horseback riding. Fans of these smartest animals will be happy when they find out that in the complex you can constantly play and communicate with them. You can feed the horses carrots, freshly baked bread or cookies. Also, with a specially trained instructor, you can go horseback riding in the complex or go to Karakansky Bor for 30 minutes or 60. The price of such pleasure is relatively low - in the range of 300-600 rubles.




  • A mini trip to the other side of the river on a local ferry. Going to the other coast of the reservoir, you can take food and have a picnic in a new place, in addition, everywhere there are special small arbors with barbecue facilities, which is very convenient.
  • Sport games. Tennis and small tennis, badminton and volleyball - what could be better than such a fun and healthy pastime with your family? That's right - nothing!
  • Relax on the beach. Sinemorye has its own small sandy beach where you can soak up the sun, play beach volleyball or build real castles. The entrance to the water is very convenient for children - it is gentle and soft.

Winter time

In this cold season, the Karakan pine forest is good, rest in it will be more beautiful than ever. Walking along it, you can see the traces of forest inhabitants - bunnies, squirrels or foxes.

Near the camp site there is an excellent slide with which you can ride on the cheesecakes rented.

Parents can not worry about their beloved children - there are also low elevations for children, and admirers of adrenaline can find a higher slope. Another option for outdoor activities is renting a whole snowmobile and skiing in the most beautiful places of the forest.

Also in honor is a real Russian bathhouse, having steamed enough in which, you can jump into a fluffy and clean snowdrift.

In the evening you can arrange a whole competition in chess, checkers or board games.

Accommodation conditions at the tourist base

Guests are located in one of the heated main buildings, in a standard room or in a cottage. The rooms have a bathroom with a shower, a single or double bed, bedside tables, a TV with several connected channels, a mirror and a desk. Travelers note that the number of rooms is quite pleasant and clean.

For a good meal, you can go to the dining room, which is located at a maximum distance of 800 meters from the farthest building on the very shore of the reservoir.

At the tourist base, you can easily park your car in a secure car park.

The peculiarity of the complex is that its employees make compliments to “special” guests - newlyweds, birthday people and anniversaries.

Our pearl of Siberia

Karakansky pine forest is a most valuable protected place in which important elements of flora and fauna are preserved. For example, special types of moss that can only be found in this pine forest and on the peaks of the Altai Mountains.

Also, the territory of the boron (whose area exceeds 99 thousand hectares) is crossed by many rivers with nowadays rare fish species.

You can get to the forest from Novosibirsk in 2-3 hours, because it is located at a distance of 140 kilometers from the city. That is why the vast majority of local residents rest at the bases. However, we can safely say that both Muscovites and Khabarovsk citizens will certainly visit such a nature reserve, which is so important for Russia, with pleasure.

Many vivid photographs, fresh emotions and impressions will give tourists Karakansky Bor. The recreation center "Sinemorye" will help him with this with his good-natured attitude towards guests and a sea of ​​entertainment!
