High Castle (Lviv): the place where the history of the city began

If you ask any person about the most interesting cities in Ukraine, you will definitely hear the name of this settlement. The historical and cultural capital of the western region of the country, a real gem of Europe! A High Castle (Lviv) is one of the main attractions of the city.

Blowing dust of history

The High Castle (Lviv), a photo of which can be seen in our article, has almost not been preserved. In the eighteenth century, the fortress was destroyed by the troops of Charles XII, and then the locals dismantled it for their needs. In the nineteenth century, the remains of the castle were completely dismantled, leaving only a small fragment of the south wall, and a park was set up on the mountain.

But before the fort was almost impregnable. It consisted of a detinets, a roundabout city and a hedge. In plan, the stone High Castle (Lviv) had the shape of an elongated rectangle with towers in the corners.



In the wake of time

Park "High Castle" Lviv cherishes and stores. The city is proud of him and must include in every tourist guide. But not only lovers of antiquity and fortification architecture should climb the hill. Castle Hill is located in the old part of the Pearl of the Crown of Europe (one of the names of Lviv) and is its highest point. The Emerald Park is considered a monument of landscape gardening art, and from its viewing platforms an unrivaled panorama opens. It was from this place that the history of the city began, which continues to this day.

You see, there, on the mountain ...

Undoubtedly, it is worth to see the High Castle Park (Lviv). Where it is located, the reader already knows. It is interesting that this object, included in the number of nature reserves of Ukraine, occupies two terraces at once.



At the top you can still see the ruins of the fortress, as well as a mound created in honor of the centenary of the Union of Lublin. At the top of the artificial mound is a large observation deck. Romantics should go here on the eve of sunset, as a city buried in the sun is simply charming. As they say, the eye does not take his eyes. There is also a television tower and a man-made cave guarded by two stone lions. These statues used to adorn the old city hall, but now they have been moved here. On the lower terrace there is another observation deck, a restaurant of the same name with a park and a gardener's house.

On the Castle Hill of the city of Lviv there is another object worthy of the attention of lovers of antiquity. This is the Powder Tower, which is considered one of the oldest buildings. Built in the sixteenth century, it is a vivid example of military-defense architecture. As the name implies, ammunition was stored in the tower, and when peace reigned, grain was stored in it. Now, within its walls is housed the Architect's House.

Park location

Do you want to visit the High Castle (Lviv)? How to get there from the city, we will tell further. The hill is not difficult to find, and it is visible from anywhere in the city. In the center there are a large number of signs that will help you not to miss the attraction. And if this still happened, then the locals will always be happy to help you find your way.



There are two ways to get to the mountain. The first of them is located on the side of the street of the same name (High Castle). This is a pretty steep but short staircase. The second way will be longer, but easier. On the street of M. Krivonos, you can see the beginning of a small serpentine, on which you can even get to the hill by car.
