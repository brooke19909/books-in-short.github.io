Additional Information

When planning your stay at Hilton, be sure to check out some of the additional hotel policies. Here are the highlights:

  • Check-in of newly arrived guests starts after 14:00. On the day of eviction, you need to leave the room before noon. For an additional fee, subject to availability, early check-in or late check-out is possible.
  • For late evictions until 18:00, a fee of 50% of the room price will be charged. If you evict after 18:00, a full day will be charged.
  • This room cannot accommodate extra beds.
  • Pets are not allowed. Accommodation with guide dogs requires prior approval by the administration.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout the hotel.
  • We accept plastic cards.

Negative reviews

The description of Hilton in Novosibirsk, given on the official website and other resources, does not provide complete information about the living conditions and the quality of the services provided. Objective information can be obtained from traveler reviews. In their comments, they express the following basic comments:

  • inflated prices for accommodation and related services (in Novosibirsk there are many hotels with lower rates and a decent level of service);
  • despite the fact that the hotel is non-smoking, in the corridors quite often there is a smell of tobacco smoke;
  • the rooms are poorly heated, you have to ask for an additional heater;
  • poor soundproofing in the rooms (it is especially unpleasant in the morning to hear neighbors slamming doors);
  • the rooms are quite cramped;
