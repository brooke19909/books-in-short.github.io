Adler, guest houses with a swimming pool for every taste

One of the most popular places on the Black Sea coast is Adler. Guest houses with a pool in this city are always crowded with tourists. It is especially difficult to find housing in high season. Any guest house (Adler) with a swimming pool becomes like an anthill as soon as the vacation begins. You can’t pull to the last with the order of a cozy place if you want to relax inexpensively and comfortably. Booking a room in advance, you guarantee yourself a relaxing holiday without any problems.

South Coast Pearl

For a start, two words about what kind of city this is - Adler. Guest houses with a pool in any resort town are a success, but this is not only a matter of the coast. After the Olympic Park was built here, the popularity of this place has increased several times. Many people want to see with their own eyes the places where athletes lived and won medals. And in a complex with the neighborhood of Sochi, this is just an explosive mixture for a lover of bright and beautiful relaxation. In the city you can find places to stay of different types, designed for people with different incomes. We will tell you about several options with different rental prices. Thus, you will see that you can choose housing for every taste. In our article, we will look at four different guest houses.





"Acacia"

Let's start with the cheapest option that Adler offers. Guest houses with a pool of this type have a cost from 600 to 1800 rubles per day per room. This place is called "Acacia." A distinctive feature of it is the lack of noisy establishments nearby. This is suitable for those who like a quiet relaxing holiday. And those who like to have fun can easily find everything they need after only fifteen minutes to the center. All interesting places to visit are in the area of ​​half an hour from here. As for the sea, it will also open to your eyes if you walk ten minutes in the direction of the coast.





Here you can rent both a double and a triple room, which is equipped with air conditioning, a refrigerator and a TV. On the territory there is a beautiful pool and kitchen. Ideal for those who prefer to cook their own meals. It has all the necessary kitchen utensils for both cooking and eating. In the rooms, customers can use free internet.

"Lavender"

This option is a bit expensive. “Lavender” refers to the average level of the cost of living of those that Adler offers. Guest houses with a pool cost from 700 to 2500 rubles per day per room. In this institution there are different categories of rooms: luxury and luxury comfort. They can accommodate two, three or four people. For large companies offer rooms with two rooms. The presence on the territory of the kitchen and pool makes the rest even more comfortable.





There are many restaurants, bars, nightclubs nearby. This place is usually chosen by lovers of noisy relaxation, who are tired of the gray everyday life and want to brightly spend their vacation. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, they have a TV, free internet, a refrigerator. To go to the sea for about ten minutes.

"Helena"

“Elena” is another guest house (Adler) with a swimming pool. Customer reviews speak for themselves. Great atmosphere, good staff and comfortable rooms attract more and more tourists every year. The price for living in this cozy corner is from 900 to 2600 rubles per room per night. Despite the fact that the house is further from the sea, guests do not suffer from this, because there is a beautiful pool on the territory. But in the immediate vicinity are such entertainment centers as “Waterpark”, “Oceanarium”, “Dolphinarium”. In general, a real paradise for children.





The rooms for two, three or four guests are equipped with air conditioning, TVs and refrigerators. You can cook yourself in the modern kitchen, or you can go for lunch in a cafe or dining room nearby. Particularly attracted to the yard, planted with exotic plants.

"Santa Barbara"

And the last guest house (Adler) with a pool by the sea that we want to talk about is Santa Barbara. Last on our list, but not in quality. This is an option of relaxation with increased comfort. The cost of living here is from 900 to 1850 per day per person. Being close to the sea, just a 10-minute walk, the house is a two-story luxury building with rooms for 2, 3 and 4 people. All rooms with full amenities, each room has a bathroom.





In the courtyard is a large pool. Workers in the dining room provide three meals a day for those who want to relax during the holidays, and not stand by the stove. Guests can also use the free internet. It is very convenient that the territory has its own store where you can buy basic necessities.

As you can see, everyone can find housing in Adler to their taste. One has only to take a closer look and book in advance what is liked.
