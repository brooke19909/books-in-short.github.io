Golden Sands Beach, Yevpatoriya: reviews and photos

Crimea is a peninsula with a rich history, delightful forests and the gentle sea. Among the sights of this magical land, one can distinguish a lot of unique and beautiful places: both natural ones - various parks, reserves, bays and mountains, and man-made cultural monuments, for example, "Swallow's Nest", the palace of Count M. S. Vorontsov and many others.

Pearl of Crimea - Evpatoria

Among the wealth of choice of resort places on the peninsula, it is worth highlighting the city of Yevpatoria, founded by the ancient Greeks. It is located in the western part of Crimea and at all times attracted tourists with sandy beaches, the territory of which covers about thirty kilometers, a gentle descent to the sea and a mild climate.

Of great importance when choosing a vacation spot with family and friends is the infrastructure, which in the city of Yevpatoriya is presented at the most modern level. There are many theaters and cinemas, restaurants and cafes, dolphinariums and water parks, arboretums and night clubs. In addition, for outdoor enthusiasts, there is everything you need: go-kart stadiums, diving, exciting jeep tours, paragliding and hang-gliding flights, horseback riding, ATV rides, and that's not all.

How to get to Yevpatoriya?

There are currently no direct flights to Yevpatoriya. Therefore, you will have to get with transfers. Of course, the fastest option is an airplane. You need to purchase a ticket to Simferopol. The distance between the cities of Simferopol and Yevpatoria is about 70 kilometers. Therefore, when reaching the airport, you need to transfer by train, taxi or regular bus. Travel time is about 1.5 hours.

You can also get by train or regular bus. To do this, you must also purchase tickets. It is worth noting that there is no direct connection with the peninsula, so you will have to cross the Kerch Strait by ferry. Comfortable buses carry it with passengers, but if you choose a train, you will have to buy a ticket to Krasnodar or Anapa, and then get to the Caucasus port on your own, of course, it is most convenient to transfer to a regular bus to the city of Yevpatoriya.

Some tourists travel by car. It will take a little more than a day, depending on the departure point. It is worth considering that the distance between the cities of Moscow and Yevpatoria is approximately 1,500 km. Crossing the Kerch Strait will also have to be included in additional costs. But at present, the construction of the Russian bridge in the Crimea is actively underway, and in the future all these difficulties can be avoided.

Golden Sands Beach (Evpatoria)

Special attention deserves a beach holiday on the peninsula, which is washed by the Black Sea. Among all the beautiful beaches, the main pearl is the Golden Sands in Yevpatoriya. They are located on Simferopolskaya Street, in the eastern part of the city. This place is a great choice for a family holiday in Yevpatoriya.





Golden Sands Beach, the photo of which is given in the article, is often compared with the resort of the same name in Bulgaria. The only drawback of this resort place is its remoteness from the city limits. You will have to get by public transport, taxi or private car. But the clean, wonderful golden hue soft sand, the gentle sea and the beautiful, white-colored organization of the beach area is worth it.

Golden Sands Beach Infrastructure

The beach area is equipped with developed infrastructure, which includes a massage room with professional masseurs and various water attractions, including a banana ride, cheesecake rides and parachute flights from a boat, where you can enjoy spectacular scenery, you can also ride water scooters, motorcycles and catamarans. In addition, the beach area is equipped with sports grounds such as volleyball and badminton. For your safety, there is a medical center and a rescue post.

Also, the area is equipped with sun loungers, gazebos, shady canopies and bungalows, changing cabins. There are showers and latrines throughout the resort. Between the beach arbors there are neat white paths leading directly to the sea.





It is worth noting that the length of the Golden Sands beach zone (Yevpatoriya) is about three hundred meters. On the beach there is a bar and various outlets for the purchase of everything you need, also within walking distance there is a dining room. Beach workers conduct daily cleaning of the area and carefully monitor the cleanliness. And so that the sand always remains so soft and clean, the administration of the Golden Sands beach uses modern technology to loosen it.





In general, the Golden Sands beach (Yevpatoriya) is ideal for a comfortable family vacation. A gentle descent to the sea allows the water to warm up quickly and throughout the season, which lasts in the city of Yevpatoria from May to October, to keep the temperature on the order of twenty degrees Celsius.

Children's rest on the beach

A special note deserves the presence of well-maintained children's corners on the beach, which are a bright playground with ladders and a water slide with a trampoline. Also on the beach there is a group of professional animators who show tricks and organize contests, apply face painting, play funny miniatures and, of course, give gifts to the most active participants. Therefore, tired parents can temporarily transfer custody of their beloved child and drink mojito in a cafe nearby.

Residential complex "Golden Sands" (Yevpatoriya)

Currently, along the Simferopolskaya street, outside the city limits, the construction of a new Golden Sands microdistrict is underway. It will be located within walking distance from the sea coast on one side and the salt lake Sasyk-Sivash on the other. The building area will occupy about fifteen hectares and stretch for one and a half kilometers along the coast.





It is worth noting that the recreational complex will be located less than a hundred meters from the beach area, which will allow you to enjoy a first-class, comfortable rest all day near the house. What will be the uniqueness of the Golden Sands residential complex? About everything - in order.

Infrastructure of the Golden Sands complex

In Yevpatoriya, the Golden Sands residential complex will embody the wildest comfortable dream of any homeowner. It will be a small and cozy town, equipped with modern infrastructure, which will include:

All related components necessary for preventive procedures will be delivered from the Moinak lakes system and Sasyk-Sivash lake.

Elite apartments of the Golden Sands complex
