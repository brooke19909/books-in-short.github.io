Holiday Village "Mirage": addresses, description, reviews

Rest in the country in the silence of pristine nature. What could be better after hard workdays? Increasingly, residents of large cities are trying to travel for several days outside the boundaries of megacities in order to temporarily escape from the hustle and bustle.

Holiday Village "Mirage" in Minusinsk

This tourist complex is located in the south of the Krasnoyarsk Territory near the village of Znamenka. Minusinsk is 22 km from the Mirage recreation center. It combines comfortable conditions and the enchanting beauty of a pine forest.

The aroma of needles in these places is able to calm the nervous system and increase the protective properties of the body in any visitor to the complex. A tourist base awaits guests throughout the year.

Accommodation for visitors is arranged in 9 wooden houses with heating and necessary amenities:

  • cold and hot water;
  • shower and toilet;
  • refrigerator;
  • TV
  • CB oven;
  • electric kettle.

Each cottage has 2-3 rooms with modern furniture. In these houses there are also small bathhouses that are built in compliance with the necessary technologies.

In the summer, guests receive a mini-hotel, which is designed for 40 people. There is a spacious porch with a large table with benches. In this area, vacationers often gather in the evenings and have fun together.

Food and entertainment

At the recreation center "Mirage" in Znamenka there is a cozy cafe. It serves dishes from the menu of Russian traditional cuisine. The cafe has orders for organizing a banquet for any reason.

On the open veranda you can not only try original dishes, but also enjoy the clean and fresh forest air. Here, staff will surround visitors with care and attention.

The recreation center "Mirage" is located on the banks of the pond, which was created artificially on the river Lugavka. In the reservoir, fish of different species are bred:

  • pike;
  • carp;
  • perch;
  • crucian carp;
  • tench;
  • grayling;
  • burbot.

There are small berths for fishing from the shore. Also at the tourist base you can rent a boat. Visitors bring the necessary equipment with them or rent in the complex.

In the autumn-summer period, tourists make walks in the forest and pick mushrooms and berries. Such a rest not only allows you to relax and rest, but also helps to make homemade preparations for the winter.

Sports grounds are equipped at the Mirage recreation center in Minusinsk. Here you can play football, volleyball or try your hand at tennis on the court.

Holiday Village "Mirage" in Lermontovo

Holidays on the Black Sea has remained popular among residents of the country for more than a dozen years. Tourists are looking for complexes where comfortable conditions are provided at an affordable cost.

The recreation center "Mirage" is located in the village of Lermontovo near a beautiful bay with a sandy beach. The complex is located at the foot of the mountains, so the combination of views of them and the sea attracts more and more tourists every year.

It can accommodate 72 guests at once. The rooms have air conditioning and necessary appliances. Meals are made in a cozy dining room with dishes from Caucasian and European cuisine.

The complex has two swimming pools and several covered pavilions for organizing picnics. You can keep fit in the equipped room. A sauna is available on site, where guests can take a steam bath for an additional fee.

In the evenings there is a night bar where discos are held and you can sing your favorite songs in karaoke.

Vacation Reviews

Tourists who rested in the Mirage on the Black Sea note good living conditions and friendly and very hospitable hosts. The customers of the complex are satisfied with the food here and the cleanliness. Small complaints arise due to the remoteness of the base from the beaches. But many guests say that the staff of the complex often takes them to the sea for free and takes them back at the right time.

Tourists who spent time in a pine forest at the Mirage recreation center in Minusinsk are pleased with the living conditions. They note that the rooms are clean and smells of natural wood.

Delicious dishes are prepared at the cafe, and an interesting entertainment program is organized during the holidays. Small remarks arise about the untidy sports grounds and the rather perceived wear and tear of fishing equipment and boats. But all these nuances definitely overlap the very beautiful landscapes around.
