The recreation center "Kingdom-Kingdom": description and reviews

If you are tired of everyday life and the soul asks for fairy tales, then welcome to the recreation center "Kingdom-Kingdom". This is a club cottage village, which may be interesting for spending weekends or vacation stays. Luxurious nature, rivers, fresh air - all this attracts tourists. The village is located among a pine forest. In order to stay in the lap of nature and breathe life-giving air, many are going to come to the base.





For lovers of tourist and comfortable rest

Usually these are two concepts that are incompatible. But the recreation center "Kingdom-Kingdom" fully meets the needs of any tourist. If you want to walk in the fresh air and make many hours of hiking in the forest - please, plenty of opportunities. After that, you can return to a comfortable hotel where you can take a hot shower and dine in the restaurant.

It turns out that here nature has a very favorable effect on the physical health of a person, add to this the developed infrastructure of the village and get ideal living conditions for the whole family. Here everyone has the opportunity to do what he likes.

Living conditions

At the recreation center "Kingdom-Kingdom" you can rent a different category of housing. For this, there are cottages at the base, each of which includes everything necessary for a carefree pastime. The most important details that provide a comfortable stay are thought out on the territory. A huge number of sites for active and interesting recreation are organized here. The recreation center "Kingdom-Kingdom" makes sure that your vacation goes as well as possible. Therefore, on the territory there are animators who organize various events.

And the best part is the cottage village is located very close to St. Petersburg. Therefore, even if you had one day off, this may be enough to enjoy a vacation in the countryside.

How to spend leisure time

There are definitely no problems with this. The recreation center "Kingdom-Kingdom" (Roshchino village) invites guests at any time of the year. In summer, you can have a great weekend walking in the pine forest. And in order to have more fun, it is recommended to rent table tennis and badminton, bicycles and flying saucers.

All the tracks of the club are asphalted, you can have a great time riding bicycles and a skateboard, roller skating. There are football, basketball and volleyball courts on site.
