How to tie a sweater with your own needles

It is usually difficult for beginner knitters to understand the instructions that are presented in the magazines. This article will present simple knitting patterns for sweaters.

Recommendations: how to knit a sweater with knitting needles

First of all, decide on the model and size. The choice is great enough. If you are just starting to learn knitting, it is better to focus on simple options. Openwork and other more complex models set aside for later, as you gain experience.

How to knit a women's sweater with knitting needles

For work you will need:

- cotton yarn (four hundred grams);



- a needle with a rounded end;

- knitting needles (No. 3).

Back and front knit pattern

We will knit a women's jacket in size 46. We start from the back. Dial 102 loops. Knit four rows with an elastic band. Continue on with facial stitching. Having knitted 41 centimeters, close seven arm loops for armholes. We continue. We collect seventeen centimeters. We make the neck. For the cut, close the twenty middle loops to round it, in each row two more. Getting to the knit front. We collect 110 loops and knit four rows with an elastic band. To tie a sweater with knitting needles, you should be patient and have about seven free evenings left. So, continue to type loops of the front surface. At a height of forty-one centimeters, close seven loops for armholes. We knit the neck. Decrease in rows first one loop, and then six, five, four, three and two. Close the 26 remaining shoulder loops. Tie the braid separately (for decoration). Dial twenty loops, the length should be 35 centimeters. Then sew it to the edge of the neck.

Knit sleeves and assembly

Make side seams. Dial 92 loops and continue to knit with the front stitch. At the end, make four rows with an elastic band and close the loops. Knit the second sleeve in the same way. We begin the assembly of the product. We carry out side seams and sleeves.

Knitting summer sweaters





Many people associate a sweater with something warm and are used to wearing it only in the cold season. But there are models that can be worn in warm weather. Summer sweaters are loosely knitted, which allows the body to breathe. They look quite original. Before you knit a sweater with knitting needles, you need to decide on the model. After all, there are a huge number of them. There are shortened, openwork, elongated, sweaters in a vintage style and with a voluminous pattern or collar. Next, we will introduce you to how you can knit a summer sweater with knitting needles.

We will need:

- acrylic yarn (three hundred grams);

- a needle;

- knitting needles (No. 3).

Back and front knit pattern

We collect 92 loops. Next, with a rubber band, knit thirteen centimeters. As the product is twenty-seven centimeters high, proceed to knit the front surface. Then we close for armholes on both sides five loops once and one more five times.

We also knit seventeen centimeters of front stitch. Before processing the cut, first we close the middle loops (22), and then in each second row - five. The front fits in the same way as the back.

Knit sleeves and assembly

We collect 66 loops and twelve centimeters knit with an elastic band. Further facial smoothness. In the fourth row, dial three times in one loop. Knit two centimeters with an elastic band and close the knots at the end. Now we are assembling a sweater. Make shoulder and side seams with a needle. The knitted summer sweater is ready!

As you can see, knitting a sweater with knitting needles is easy. The main thing is to choose a model and be patient. Good luck
