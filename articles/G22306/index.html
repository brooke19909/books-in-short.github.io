Hotel Location

So, let's find out where the Hyton Leelavadee Resort is located. As we said, it is located in Thailand. This country has long been developing in the tourism sector, so it can offer a huge selection of hotels for every taste and budget. It is worth noting that this is due to access to the sea and a favorable climate. Our hotel is located in Phuket.

This is one of the southernmost provinces of the state. It is located off the west coast of Thailand in the Andaman Sea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Indian Ocean. Phuket is one of the first places in the country by territory. It is connected to the mainland by three bridges. It is worth noting that the island has always been quite developed and famous, before it specialized in the extraction of tin and rubber. In addition, he was often mentioned by French, Dutch and English sailors, as it is located on the main trade route between China and India. There are several more provinces near Phuket, for example, Phang Nga and Krabi.

The climate in Phuket, despite its location in equatorial latitudes, is subequatorial in nature, which requires a clear division into two seasons: dry and wet. The first lasts from November to April. The second is from May to October. The temperature is almost always the same - it ranges from 28 to 37 degrees. It is worth remembering that during the summer months there are strong undercurrents, so it is often forbidden to swim on the beaches. After all, the risk of death is great. At this time, red flags are hung everywhere.

Infrastructure: free entertainment

It's time to get to know Hyton Leelavadee Resort's infrastructure. We start, of course, with free services:

  1. Large outdoor swimming pool.
    It is located in the center of the Hyton Leelavadee Resort 3 * hotel complex. Next to it are comfortable sun loungers for a comfortable stay.

Fun for kids
