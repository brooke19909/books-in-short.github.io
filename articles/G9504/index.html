Turkey, Kusadasi. Reviews, tours and prices

This small resort town, picturesque and green, is called very poetically - "Bird Island". This is far from being the well-known Turkey - Kusadasi. Reviews about these places can not be read everywhere, because the holiday season here is quite small. Just three summer months. There is not even a velvet season, for strong unpleasant winds begin. But this region also has its own charms that tourists like. Starting from the price. They are lower than anywhere else.



Turkey, Kusadasi. Beach Reviews

To get here, you need to take a bus at the Izmir or Bodrum bus station (this is not possible directly from the airports). And you can also get on a ferry from Greece.
