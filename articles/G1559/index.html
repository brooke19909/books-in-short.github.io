Features of the camp "Brigantine" in the Leningrad region

In the summer, it is best to send the child to the camp. After all, in such establishments, leisure time for the holidays will be filled with variety and spent with benefit. It is worth paying attention to the Brigantine camp in the Leningrad region. This children's institution will appreciate the level of services provided and the mass of positive reviews from those who have already sent their sons and daughters to this camp.



What is interesting about the Brigantine camp

An institution for the summer holidays of children attracts the attention of parents for many reasons. The main ones are:

  • Camping "Brigantine" can not but rejoice. After all, it is located in a picturesque place surrounded by bewitching nature and beautiful ponds. DALE is nestled in the green of a mixed forest. Nearby there is Lake Sukhodolskoye. All this indicates that the Brigantine camp is fresh, clean air that envelops every child, charging them with good health and immersing them in an atmosphere of relaxation.
  • Pleasant pricing will attract the attention of moms and dads. After all, vouchers are inexpensive, in addition there is the opportunity to relax in the camp at a discount.
  • With the help of the camp, you can increase the self-esteem of your beloved child. Therefore, it is worth sending all the children to the Brigantine without exception, so that they improve themselves and become stronger morally.
  • A healthy diet also cannot but please caring moms and dads. In the "Brigantine" children are given fresh vegetables and fruits, fresh and varied dishes.
  • Professional teachers, organizers and psychologists will give children an unforgettable vacation, which will be filled with pleasant emotions and a great mood.

All this says that a child should definitely be sent at least once to the Brigantine children's camp.

Nutrition and health care

In the Brigantine camp, children are provided with 5 meals a day. The following meals are included in the diet:

  • Breakfast.
  • Lunch.
  • Dinner.
  • Dinner.

Children are fed healthy and fresh food. The menu also includes fruits, vegetables, natural juices, sweets.

As for the medical care in the Brigantine children's camp, everything here is also at a high level. On the territory there is a round-the-clock medical center, which will provide medical assistance if necessary. There is also a room reserved for an insulator.

Camp infrastructure

The administration of the Brigantine camp, reviews of which are mostly positive and friendly, made sure that the children's leisure was varied and multifaceted. That is why, in addition to daily entertainment, the DOL also has:

  • The pool, which is located indoors. There are three lanes in the pool, its size is 25 meters.

  • A cafe is open around the clock where children can buy sweets and fruits.




  • Circles on various topics are also in the camp infrastructure.

On the territory of the camp "Brigantine" there is everything that can make children's rest rich and varied. Therefore, you will not have to miss this institution for children.

Accommodation of children in the children's camp "Brigantine"

It is clear that parents are interested in the conditions in which their son or daughter will live. In the Brigantine camp, children are assigned to groups, depending on age. In one room of a brick building, 4 or 5 people live. There are also rooms with 9 beds. Amenities such as showers and toilets are located on the floors. In wooden houses, 8 or 9 children live. They also have a toilet on the floor, and the shower is in a separate building near the houses.

Near the buildings there are washstands in which hot and cold water is available around the clock.

Everyone who once visited the Brigantine camp in the Leningrad Region wants to return there again and again. And parents are satisfied with their rested, positively charged and matured girls and boys.

What documents must be collected for arrival at the camp?

Before sending the child to the camp, you need to undergo a medical examination. This process is being carried out in district clinics. To pass the inspection, you need to take with you:

  • Birth certificate or passport of the child.
  • Help form No. 079-U.
  • Tests for ovum, scraping.
  • Medical insurance policy.
  • Help about vaccinations.
  • A ticket to the camp.
  • All these documents should be on hand upon arrival at the camp.
