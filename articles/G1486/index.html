Knee socks: diagram and description for craftswomen of any level

Nowadays, homemade warm accessories are gaining popularity. Slippers, footsteps, socks and even knee-high socks: you can knit all this at home, giving the product value and quality.





Warm friends

As soon as the cold starts, people immediately begin to prepare for the winter, making edible preparations, various household chores, making knitted socks and slippers. They, as it turned out, are indispensable accessories for any wintering, as they do an excellent job keeping the heat of our feet. Therefore, such a part is always at home, even with a spare kit for guests.

Knitted variety

Everyone chooses a product for themselves, relying on personal preferences. So, if slippers are a long-worn and boring part, then knee-high socks look very interesting and unusual, compared to other home attributes. Moreover, they can be made at home with your own hands, using improvised materials.

Of all the lace and motley variety, attention will be paid to jacquard knee-highs with knitting needles, the schemes of which will be discussed in detail below. This style of knitting is very suitable for the winter season. In addition, thanks to this choice, we will be able to decorate our knee-highs with any patterns that can only be depicted in the diagram.

Everything you need

It's time to decide on the materials that we will use in the future. If you are an experienced skilled craftswoman practicing knitting, you should already have all the necessary set. But do not worry if it is absent, as you can use all purchased equipment in all of the following projects.





  • Set of hosiery knitting needles. In our case, only 4 are needed, combining which, we get the perfect girth for the product. The thickness is chosen based on the thickness of the thread.
  • Yarn. It is best to choose a thread with a low wool content so that the product is not too scratchy for the leg. In addition, such a thread is quickly wiped. The perfect combination of synthetic and natural fibers will allow you to achieve high wear and comfortable golf. Do not forget that it is necessary to use several colors to make the knitted pattern noticeable.
  • In order to make knee-high socks with knitting needles, pattern patterns are also needed. So, in order.

Knee socks: diagrams and description for beginners

Let us consider this issue in detail. Now everything is ready to knit golfs with knitting needles, schemes and description of which you can find below. To facilitate the process of perception, we will write down all the important points in stages. Having made knitting affordable even for real beginners in this business. So:





  1. Before you start knitting golf knitting needles, patterns and description should be carefully considered to understand all the features. Our pattern is not difficult, as it is simply a combination of threads of two colors, which alternate according to the pattern. Now you can get to work.
  2. We collect sixty-eight loops on four knitting needles so that each of them has an equal number. Now we knit an elastic band consisting of 2 front and 2 wrong loops alternating between each other. In this way we knit about fifty rows to create a dense base.
  3. Now we knit ten rows with just the front surface, preparing a place for a jacquard pattern. According to the scheme, we knit each loop, carefully following the colors of the thread. A careful attitude will help to avoid confusion in the work and further distortion of the picture.
  4. After the main ornament is ready, you need to knit about ten rows with ordinary threads with the front surface, creating knitting needles with knitting needles. The diagrams and description below indicate that the reduction of loops begins. The next row we knit each of them together with the next. Getting double loops, decreasing in number.
  5. Again we knit a familiar elastic, as at the top of the golf course. In parallel, we remove the loops from each knitting needle, alternating between one decrease on the first needle, then two decreases on the second, and so on, until we do this on four knitting needles. After that, we continue to knit the gum for ten more rows.

No heel - nowhere

And finally. It's time to tie the heel for golf with knitting needles, the schemes and description of which are almost complete. We distribute the loops on the needles, leaving 10 units on the first, and 8 loops on the even ones. On the third spoke, all remaining loops move.





We knit all the loops on the first knitting needle, and finish the last part already with the first loop of the second knitting needle. After collecting ten more loops, it is necessary to knit 3-4 rows of facial loops. You can use the same color or contrast, taken for the pattern. Now we knit to the desired length to make the leg comfortable.

We complete the product by knitting all the loops for another five rows, and then, reducing two units on each of the knitting needles. Then we collect the remaining loops on the thread and tighten in a tight knot, tucking unnecessary fragments inward.

That's it, knee-high knitting needles are ready. Schemes and description can be put aside and enjoy the completed result. The products turned out to be soft, absolutely not scratchy and so hidden to the body that you are unlikely to want to take them off all winter.
