Original DIY fabric brooches

A very original trend of the new season are vintage brooches and handmade accessories. Brooches differ in texture, design and decoration, but, as a rule, they are made in the form of flowers. Making fabric brooches with your own hands is not so difficult, and such a decoration is perfect for any wardrobe. They can turn the most ordinary blouse into a masterpiece. And the creation of such an accessory will require imagination, patience, and a minimum of financial investments. Want to know how to make original fabric brooches with your own hands? This is what will be discussed in this article.





Brooches made of fabric adorn not only clothes, but also hairstyles, handbags, and are used as bracelets and necklaces. They become a great addition to scarves and shawls, they stick on elegant hats. Original silver brooches and jewelry made of fabric have long become unchanged companions of women, able to enhance their status and make their owner even more chic. Any fashionista must have a similar thing in her collection, because she will never go out of fashion. And real craftswomen even make brooches of fabric with their own hands. Do not miss the opportunity to become unique and original.

Each brooch has two important components: a decorative element and a clasp. In the hardware store you can buy good fasteners of various types and sizes. It can be platforms to which decorative parts are glued, and simple pins.





To create an accessory, you can use a variety of materials: ribbons, beads, fabric, beads, lace, plastic. Anything that is enough for your imagination. If you want a brooch made of fabric, silk is ideal for creating petals. Such flowers will look elegant and delicate.

Making a fabric flower

First, select a fabric of pink, red or any other color, cut it into strips, from which we will twist the rose. To make a rose with a diameter of about five centimeters, you need strips of fabric about 7 cm wide and 50 cm long. You can also use ready-made tapes, which are sold in fabric stores. Then we take a strip and fold along the length, ironing the bend.

Now we turn the strip of fabric inside out and bend both edges to the center. In the center of the length, we bend the strip again to get a strip that looks like a slanting inlay. From one end, it is necessary to cut the inner edges and secure the needle from the wrong side in the middle bend. Then on the front side of the bend we sew a bead, which will become the center of the flower. We wrap the bottom edge of the beads or buttons with a cloth and fasten with a thread. Now bend the strip of fabric at an angle of 90 degrees to the outside and again fasten with a thread. And now, in the process of twisting, we bend the fabric at an angle to the center, fixing the flower with a thread. Rose is ready. It remains only to attach it to the hairpin.





With the help of this article, you learned how to make brooches from fabric with your own hands. There are many more ways to create original accessories. Imagine and show your originality!
