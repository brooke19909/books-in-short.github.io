Asus Memo Pad 7 tablet: review, description, specifications. Owner reviews

Asus is a well-known Taiwanese company that manufactures various computer equipment. The company has gained worldwide fame thanks to its high-quality laptops and smartphones. However, the guys from Asus did not stop there and decided to go even further. Relatively recently, a whole line of tablets from this company came out. Could specialists from Asus create something worthy? Should I buy the brand new Asus Memo Pad 7? You can find answers to these and many other questions in this article.

Asus Memo Pad 7

A new line called Memo Pad 7 attracted attention even during the announcement of IFA 2014. Thin and lightweight tablets with amazing, high-quality screens made a splash. Among other things, pleased with the support of the long-suffering LTE, which provides high-speed data transfer between devices. Well, the cherry on the cake was the announcement of the release of a new shell called ZenUI. Such "tasty" announcements made millions of users around the world wait for the release of a new line of tablets. So, a year has passed - and the device appeared on store shelves. But is it as good as we were promised? Could Asus reach the set bar? You will find the answers in this material.





Design

The first thing that catches your eye is the excellent appearance of the device. The Asus Memo Pad 7 is not just a piece of plastic with rounded corners. The painstaking work of designers is noticeable. Elegant lines of the case give the gadget a certain aristocracy. Particularly pleased with the ultra-thin frame of the gadget - the width on the sides is only 9 millimeters. The assembly is also laudable. Everything is fitted with perfect precision. No backlash, creaks and gaps are observed even after a long service life. It is also worth noting that the Asus Memo Pad HD 7 ME173X is sold in various color variations. Therefore, if you are bored with the classic black and white colors, then you can buy a red, blue and even yellow version of the gadget.

Also pleasing are the dimensions of the tablet. The entire line of Memo Pad 7 has excellent balance. Despite the powerful stuffing, the device is quite compact. This allows you to put the gadget directly into a bag or backpack without any problems. Asus Memo Pad 7 weighs less than 300 grams. Thanks to this, the hands do not get tired and do not sag even with prolonged use of the device.

The back panel of the gadget is made of matte plastic. It is pleasant to the touch and, most importantly, does not collect fingerprints. At the top is the camera. Around the lens there is a special influx that protects the camera from scratches and other damage.





Performance

Asus Memo Pad 7 ME572CL is a pretty powerful device. Few can compete with him in terms of performance. Asus specialists made a rather interesting decision. Instead of standard and already boring chips from NVIDIA, Qualcomm, MediaTek, Memo Pad 7 used a modern 64-bit Z3560 processor from Intel, a well-known company. Each of the four cores of this monster has a power of 1.89 GHz. And coupled with the PowerVR G6430 video accelerator (which is exactly what is found in the iPhone 5S), an incredibly productive device is obtained. Asus Memo Pad HD 7 ME173X is capable of processing information in large volumes in a matter of fractions of a second. Thanks to this, the tablet is able to run the most demanding applications, games without the slightest sag. What can I say about Internet surfing, watching movies, listening to music and reading books! These tasks Asus Memo Pad 7 clicks like nuts.

Screen

The Asus Memo Pad 7 ME173X display deserves special attention. The screen has a standard size - 1280 x 800 pixels. Nevertheless, the tablet boasts quality detail. The picture is very clear, without any blur and blur. Pixylation is not at all noticeable. The IPS matrix with a special LED backlight has a positive effect on image quality. Thanks to her, the screen boasts bright, saturated colors. And reading books through Asus Memo Pad 7 is a real pleasure. Perhaps the main omission in terms of display is the lack of anti-reflective coating. Because of this, working with the tablet in the sun is uncomfortable - you have to set the brightness to maximum.





The Asus Memo Pad HD 7 16GB tablet has an excellent sensor. Each touch is instantly processed without any delay. Moreover, the screen is capable of supporting up to 10 fingers at a time.

Sound

The tablet has two speakers: one is located on the upper edge, the other - on the bottom. Thanks to this, the sound is quite clear and loud, which is undoubtedly a plus of the device. Also, while watching movies, TV shows through the tablet creates the so-called stereo effect. It gives extra atmospheric.

Music lovers will also be pleased. The speakers do a good job playing various frequencies. Therefore, at the output we get a crystal clear sound without any interference.

Software

Asus Memo Pad 7 16GB runs on a modern version of Android. Updates come immediately after their official release. The operation of the reproach interface also does not cause. Everything works very smartly, without any slowdowns.





It is also worth noting that on top of the classic Android 4.4 KitKat, a special graphics shell from Asus is installed called ZenUI. It in no way reduces the functionality of native software. The main function of ZenUI is to change the appearance of the interface, making it easier to work with. And she copes with this function pretty well. Asus designers have greatly simplified the interface, it has become intuitive.

The brand new ZenUI looks pretty nice. Icons have become much simpler, the user interface is consistent with all modern trends. Standard applications have also been modified to match the overall style. In general, ZenUI causes only positive emotions among users.





Autonomy

The battery is the scourge of all modern gadgets. Now it is very difficult to find a tablet that would work long enough without connecting to a network. Asus experts understood this very well, so they tried to make the Asus Memo Pad 7 ME176CX as autonomous as possible.

The capacity of the built-in battery is about 4000 mAh. And this is one of the most tenacious tablets on the market today. In normal mode, with measured use, the gadget can last up to two days. And this is a wonderful indicator. Therefore, the device can be safely taken on long trips - the battery will not fail.

In addition, the tablet has a special section. In it, you can quite flexibly adjust the energy consumption. For example, by slightly lowering the backlight, you can delay the trip to the outlet for a couple of hours.





Total

In conclusion, we can say that the Asus Memo Pad 7 16GB tablet is an amazing gadget that has a stylish design, high performance and huge functionality. The device is perfect for both home use and for long trips. Although the price of the device bites, but it is fully justified by the quality of the final product.
