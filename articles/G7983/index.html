What is the coolest water park in the world? Description of the best water parks

What could be better than outdoor activities and a good pastime? The coolest water park in the world is able to provide active and enjoyable leisure. In such an institution, people can get only the most enchanting emotions and a lot of positive impressions. Today in every country there are so many amazing water amusement parks that it is difficult to say with certainty which one is the best. Each of the world famous water parks deserves the title of the coolest in the world. Therefore, let's talk briefly about the best and most successful water parks on the planet.

The largest water park on the planet

If we evaluate the size of water parks, the coolest water park in the world is Tropical Island. It is also the largest institution on Earth of such a plan. This "Tropical Island" is located in Germany, in Berlin, and more precisely 70 km from the city. The temperature in Tropical Island is 25 degrees Celsius year-round, and the water temperature reaches 28 degrees Celsius. The building, which houses the entertainment center, was originally intended for the construction of airships.





The coolest water park in the world Tropical Island is divided into thematic sections. So, there is a "Flower World" with mangroves, tropical thickets, orchids and palm trees. In the "Tropical Village" are the corners of Amazonia, Thailand, Bali, Malaysia and Congo. In addition, the park has two pools, a Jacuzzi, waterfalls, a 25-meter water attraction and many, many other attractions.

Japanese water park

The Seagaia Ocean Dome is another entertainment center that is striking in its huge size. The establishment is located in Japan, on the small island of Kyushu, in the city of Miyazaki. An amusement park opened in 1993 and impressed visitors with its extraordinary scale. He is able to simultaneously accommodate about ten thousand people.





Seagaia Ocean Dome is distinguished from all other water parks in the world by the presence of a unique roof: it opens in sunny weather and closes in rainy. The complex has a large number of various slides, various pools, attractions and sunbeds. There is also an unsurpassed volcano attraction. Every day he "erupts", and people have the opportunity to look at the destructive power of artificial lava.

Park in dubai

In Dubai there is a water park, which is one of the top 10 best places in the world. It is called Wild Wadi Waterpark. The institution is decorated in the style of Arabian tales and stylized under the Arabian architecture. This is the most expensive park in the United Arab Emirates. Attractions opened in 1999, but since then their collection has become much wider.

Park in Kuala Lumpur

If you are traveling in Malaysia, you should definitely visit the Sunway Lagoon water park in Kuala Lumpur. Its area exceeds one square kilometer. The institution is replete with all the attributes that should be in such institutions. There is a jacuzzi, and a pool with artificial waves, and water slides, and a wall with a waterfall, and many, many other attractions. Here the most extreme of them is considered a slide, which you must move out on a special pillow.





Kids here will especially enjoy the wildlife park. Here in various enclosures birds, animals and reptiles are kept. In addition to predators, there are peacocks, rabbits, monkeys and other animals. Some individuals can be fed if you take food from the workers of the complex.

American Water Entertainment Center

In America, there is also an institution that adults and children are happy to visit - this is the Schlitterbahn water park. You can find it in Texas. More than once this entertainment center was awarded the title of the best water park in the world. That is why many travelers tend to get here. Since the institution is located on one of the banks of the Komal River, some of its parts are filled only with cool river water. And it cools so nicely on exhaustingly hot days!





The longest descent in the park lasts 30 minutes, and its path lies just across the Komal riverbed. For children in the entertainment complex provides seven play areas. All of them (depending on the degree of danger) are divided into four categories. After intense slides and other activities, you can relax in the beautiful picnic area.
