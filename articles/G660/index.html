The best expensive Telman Ismailov hotel in Turkey for wealthy clients

In 2009, the truly magnificent Telman Ismailov hotel in Turkey was built under the name Mardan Palace. This event was loudly covered with the involvement of journalists and world-class stars. According to unofficial data, more than a billion dollars were invested in the construction of the complex. The opening was accompanied by a grandiose firework and a colorful ceremony. Monica Bellucci, Sharon Stone, Paris Hilton, Richard Gere and other pop and film celebrities were invited to the event.





The hotel received an unusual name in honor of the father of businessman Telman Ismailov. Today it is the most expensive and fashionable complex, occupying 180 thousand square meters. m. On its vast territory housed seven and eight-story buildings: Anatolian Wing, Dolmabahce Wing, European Wing. Also 15 two-story cottages: Anatolian Wing and Garden Suite. The decoration of the buildings is made of gilding, Italian marble and a million crystal ornaments. The Mardan Palace Hotel in Turkey has a huge swimming pool and its own sandy beach. For the arrangement of the beach site, 9 thousand tons of fine sand were delivered. In addition, there is an aquarium on the territory where thousands of exotic mammals, brought here from different continents, live. Guests will be able to personally witness the life of wild animals, as the hotel has a large zoo.

The vast territory is equipped with numerous "singing" fountains, an artificial reef and a pond, romantic bridges, water attractions, respectable SPA salons and other important attributes for a meditative pastime. Hotel Telman Ismailov in Turkey Mardan Palace is recommended for people with a high level of income, as well as for those who prefer pomp, luxury and first-class service.





Number of rooms

The hotel has 546 designer-designed apartments of various categories and sizes. The most demanding customers can stay in comfortable villas with their own garden, swimming pool, concert piano and a bathroom with remote control. All rooms have laminate or marble flooring.





The spacious bathrooms include a jacuzzi, bathtub or shower. Bathrobes, slippers and bath accessories are provided. Among the amenities: a cozy terrace with access to the courtyard, free internet and a safe. In the presence of air conditioning, plasma TV, telephone and hairdryer. You can use the minibar, but only for a surcharge. Every day, at a time convenient for tourists, the premises are cleaned, bed linen is changed.





Nutrition

Since 2012, the Telman Ismailov Hotel Mardan Palace in Turkey has been operating on an all-inclusive basis. But if you wish, guests can choose a different concept: half board, breakfast or Fullboard Plus. Tables are served in the main restaurant for 612 people. The hotel has 10 restaurants - each institution has its own cuisine (Turkish, Russian, Mediterranean, Japanese, American, Thai, seafood, French).





In addition, another 11 bars are open. You can enjoy real Turkish coffee with aromatic oriental sweets in the tea lobby. Connoisseurs of elite alcohol will find the most suitable wine for themselves.

Beach

Hotel Telman Ismailov Mardan Palace boasts the best beach in the resort. The coast is strewn with snow-white clean sand. All amenities are at your disposal for free. There is a pier, bar and water activities. If you wish, you will be taught to dive with a mask and scuba diving, parachute and windsurf. Adrenaline rush is provided to you.
