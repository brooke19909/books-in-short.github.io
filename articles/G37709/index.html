How to borrow on an NSS? Features of the procedure

If there is not enough money to use the services, many operators lend money. The same service is available in Rostelecom (HCC). The option will be useful in cases where there are no terminals nearby or there is no time to deposit funds. How to borrow on an NSS? To do this, you need to perform a simple procedure.

Promised Internet Payment

When money is not paid on time for the Internet, the provider usually suspends the services. But what if the Internet is urgently needed, but there is no way to replenish the balance? For this, the promised payment is used, so the service is extended for 5 days. During this time, it is possible to replenish the balance and continue to use the Internet.





To borrow on the NSS, you must perform the following steps:

  • visit your personal account: access to it is even in the absence of the Internet;
  • need to find the payment section;
  • you should choose the promised payment, enter the personal account;
  • indicate the amount needed to connect to the Internet and confirm the application.

After that, a network connection occurs. Usually the operation takes 3-5 minutes. You need to return the funds in 5 days. If this is not done, then the service becomes blocked.

Promised payment by phone

Funds may be required not only on the Internet, but also on mobile communications. If there are no funds on the phone, then you can use the convenient service. But how to borrow on the NSS? To do this, several conditions must be met:

  • number must be registered from 4 months;
  • for the last 7 days the number should not be disconnected.




A maximum of individuals can take 100 rubles. The service is provided for 5 days. If the funds are not returned in due time, then the promised payment is blocked for a month. The cost of the service is 7 rubles.

Connection Methods

How to borrow on the NSS more convenient? There are several proven methods for this:

  • request by the USSD-team: you need to dial * 122 * 1 #, then confirm the operation, and then prompts on activation of the service will be provided;
  • call to the company: for this, the number 8-800-300-18-00 is used, by which the operator will tell you about further actions;
  • in your account: on the site, find the service management section where you can connect the necessary service.

How to borrow on the NSS, everyone can choose on their own. In any way, money comes in quickly. The service will help out at any time when it is not possible to replenish the account. The service is always provided to customers.
