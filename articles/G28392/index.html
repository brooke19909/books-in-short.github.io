Kemer attractions will conquer you from the first minute

Turkey is full of interesting places for educational tourism and outdoor activities. Kemer - considered one of the most stunning resorts that is really worth a visit. After all, it was there that all the natural resources of the country were so beautifully combined. The main attractions of Kemer are, of course, the cleanest Mediterranean coast, the Taurus Mountains and indescribable natural landscapes. Once upon a time, it was a small village, which, by and large, did not have access by land. Today it is a town with a population of twenty thousand people. People from different countries try to visit there at least once in their life.





Sea, sun, beaches, mountain air, unforgettable nature - all these are the sights of Kemer. In this town, it is recommended to visit four famous villages: Tekirova, Cirali, Camyuva and Goynuk. Also, do not neglect Adrasan Bay. If you plan a quiet and deserted vacation for the soul - this is an ideal place.

Caves, canyons and bays are mysterious and fascinating sights of Kemer. All tourists are advised to visit the Goynuk Canyon. On the road to it will take an average of forty-five minutes. On the road you pass beautiful, fabulous villages - and time flies unnoticed. In the canyon, large and small waterfalls, streams, gorges open up to the human eye. One gets the feeling that you are in a fairy tale. On the territory of the canyon you can see many paths, bridges and passages. The atmosphere of wildlife and thickets of quiet places have long attracted tourists. Here you can swim in the cold springs, soak up the deserted beach or just wander through the jungle.





Two rocks (Twin Rocks) are the most popular attractions of Kemer. They are located in the village of Camyuva. After her visit, unforgettable memories are provided to vacationers. Palm trees , orange trees, deserted sandy beaches and oleanders simply can not leave tourists indifferent. And the miracle of the world, Twin Rocks, can be seen from anywhere in the village.

To visit Kemer's attractions on your own, you can purchase a booklet with possible itinerary excursions. For example, it is worth visiting at the foot of the Taurus Mountains without a guide and a noisy group. To the top, tourists enjoy a magnificent panorama of small villages, nature - and all this against the backdrop of the Mediterranean Sea. In addition, it is worth leaving for the city center. There in the square is set its symbol - a white chapel. It is full of other monuments, as well as shops. You can visit the market and port. The Yoryuk Park Museum is a little to the right.

The city of Kemer, whose attractions fascinate with its beauty and diversity, has a dolphinarium, small parks for vacationers and comfortable hotels for tourists. As for the dolphinarium, there is the opportunity to swim with the marine inhabitants. Vacationers can always walk along the promenade and breathe fresh air. A little further from it is equipped with a small zoo where animals and fish are as close to people as possible.



Turkey is a country where everyone can find something to their liking. Pack your bags, Kemer is waiting for you!
