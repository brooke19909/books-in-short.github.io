General impression

If you want to immerse yourself in an atmosphere of luxury and comfort, then the Leningradskaya Hotel is ideal for you. Here, modern service standards and the best traditions of Stalinist architecture are organically interwoven. The interior of the rooms is not only beautiful, but also very thought out, which makes the guests stay especially comfortable. Guests also note the good quality of breakfast. Another undoubted advantage of the hotel is its location in the central part of the city near two metro stations. An undoubted plus is the ability to bring your pet to the hotel.

If we talk about the shortcomings of the hotel, it is worth noting its incomplete compliance with the five-star status. This is manifested in the lack of towels and cosmetic sets, in the lines that are collected at the reception, as well as in the restaurant during breakfast. At the same time, prices for accommodation and related services are clearly overpriced. Nevertheless, if you want to feel the atmosphere of a historical building, as well as get to know Moscow and its main attractions closer, then this accommodation option is just perfect for you.
