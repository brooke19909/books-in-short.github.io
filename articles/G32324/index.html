LCD "Tsvetnoy Boulevard", Voronezh: reviews of apartments

Voronezh is a huge, dynamically developing and attractive city wishing to settle under the hot sun from the southern part of Russia. It is located at a distance of about 460 kilometers from the capital of the state in a favorable chernozem zone. The city is promising for real estate developers, one of whose projects is the Tsvetnoy Boulevard residential complex in Voronezh.





A little about Voronezh

The "cradle" of the navy, the birthplace of the airborne troops, a large transport hub - all this can be said about Voronezh. In addition, the city has many industrial enterprises that create the basis of its economic activity. Voronezh is an active producer of food products, which is well known and liked not only by the residents of the city, but also famous far beyond its borders.

The population of the city has exceeded one million people and has been growing steadily for almost ten years. This is reflected not only in the natural population growth, but also in the active relocation to the city of residents of other, especially northern, regions. G. Voronezh (LCD "Tsvetnoy Boulevard") provides such resettled housing.





Location of the complex

The developer very successfully chose a place for the construction of a residential complex - the street of the 45th Infantry Division in the Comintern district of Voronezh. This is not in the city center, but within the city limits, allowing residents to use all its rich infrastructure. Within walking distance from the complex there are squares and parks that give it additional attractiveness.

Transport accessibility

LCD "Tsvetnoy Boulevard" (Voronezh) is located near the major transport arteries of the city - January 9 and Antonov-Ovseenko streets. You can get to the complex from the city center by spending about 10-15 minutes on the road, since this distance is about 8 kilometers. It is also easy to do this by public transport, as the routes of numerous fixed-route taxis and buses run along the indicated streets.





Description of the residential complex

The entire LCD "Tsvetnoy Boulevard" (Voronezh) will occupy a little more than ten hectares. The developer designed multi-storey residential buildings: on the 17th and 25th floors, which are built using monolithic technology. The residential complex is being built in several stages of construction. The deadline for putting the houses into operation is 2020.

The developer gave the complex the name "Tsvetnoy Boulevard" because of the bright design of the building facades, creating a favorable atmosphere in the unified architectural style that they create. In addition, during the construction of the Tsvetnoy Boulevard residential complex (Voronezh), it is planned to equip the boulevard for leisure and walking of its residents.

The residential complex is designed with maximum amenities for the life of a modern family, starting from the house adjoining territory, which will be decorated with lawns and flower beds. Children will be able to play in children's playgrounds and sports fields. In addition, the Tsvetnoy Boulevard residential complex (Voronezh) provides the project with the necessary social infrastructure facilities.

Ecological situation

The residential complex is located at some distance from the city center and the noise and roar of the metropolis does not reach here. Therefore, residents of the complex can enjoy peace and tranquility. In addition, the complex is surrounded on almost all sides by parks: Oasis, May 9, a forest park on the street of the 45th Infantry Division. All this green massif makes it possible to enjoy fresh air and breathe fully.

Internal infrastructure

In order for the residents of the complex to feel comfortable, the developer has provided all the necessary infrastructure in the project. A kindergarten will be erected for the kids. Owners of their own vehicles will be pleased with ground parking and underground parking. The safety of the residents will be ensured by the fully fenced territory of the complex, which will all be ennobled and landscaped.





Apartments in the Tsvetnoy Boulevard residential complex (Voronezh) will be equipped with a metal entrance door, and the walls will be glued with wallpaper and PVC panels (in bathrooms). Stretch ceilings, switches and sockets, flooring (linoleum and tile), interior doors from MDF, plumbing in kitchens and bathrooms, heating appliances will be installed everywhere. At the request of the buyer, the apartment can even be equipped with furniture, the demonstration of which is necessarily done before buying a residential property.

Traditionally, during the construction of a residential complex, all the first floors will be given for placement of non-residential objects of social infrastructure in them, designed to provide residents with a comfortable life. There will be pharmacies, beauty salons, office rooms, shops, entertainment centers.

External infrastructure

The location of the residential complex within the city allows its residents to use all its rich social infrastructure. In walking distance is a comprehensive school. Also nearby there are many shops and supermarkets, bank branches, a stadium, pharmacies and other various infrastructure facilities. Near the complex there are many public transport stops, which allows its residents to easily and quickly get to their homes after a working day.

Layout of apartments

Layouts of apartments in the complex are very diverse. From one-room to three-room. The smallest apartments start at 36 square meters. The largest will exceed 87 square meters. The advantage of the complex is that all apartments are for rent with a fine finish, which will allow their customers to significantly save money on repairs and provide an opportunity to quickly enter the purchased housing and live in it.

Cost of housing

The developer of Tsvetnoy Boulevard offers its customers housing at very reasonable affordable prices. The average cost of apartments is from 38 to 44 thousand per square meter of living space. Thus, the cost of a one-room apartment is approximately one million six hundred thousand rubles. One bedroom apartment is priced at approximately 2.5 million rubles. A three-room apartment will cost its customers a little more than 3.2 million rubles.

Construction Positions

In the summer of 2017, not only 1, but also 2 positions of the Tsvetnoy Boulevard residential complex (Voronezh) are commissioned. Keys are issued to apartment buyers at the end of June this year. These are houses at number 259 d, buildings 11 and 12.





Residents reviews

Responses LCD "Tsvetnoy Boulevard" (Voronezh) has already managed to get, as some residential buildings have already been built, commissioned and settled by residents. As a rule, during the construction of any new building, reviews are divided into positive and negative. So is the case with this residential complex.

Negative reviews consist of dissatisfaction with the tenant of the location of the complex. It is located not in the very center of the city and among non-residential facilities. In addition, the quality of construction materials used by the developer, according to residents, leaves much to be desired.





But at the same time, many who bought the apartments are satisfied with the fact that they received them with a finished finish, which saved a lot of time, energy and money on repair work. Many people like the surroundings of the residential complex with parks, squares and a forest park, which allows you to enjoy clean air, silence at any time, and resembles a measured life in a country house.
