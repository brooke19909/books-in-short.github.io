Hotel Roy Villa Beach Hotel 2 * (Sri Lanka, Beruwala): photos and reviews of tourists

Beruwela is a small resort town on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka. Its name in translation means: "the edge where the sails are lowered." From Colombo, the capital of the state, the resort is separated by a distance of 56 km, from the international airport from here 75 km. The founders of the city are Arabs who traded here in the 7th - 8th centuries. pearls. Many people are surprised by the fact that in today's Beruwel , all the charm and charm of a small fishing village has been preserved, despite the fact that the resort has a developed tourist infrastructure, it has luxury hotels, bars, restaurants and cafes.

To accommodate tourists, hotels have been built here for every taste and budget - from luxury five-star villas and apartments to modest economy-class hotels . Among the well-known options that have received mixed reviews from guests - the hotel Roy Villa Beach Hotel 2 *.

More about the resort

Tourists from all over the world come to Beruwela (Beruwala). Roy Villa Beach Hotel 2 * (Sri Lanka, Beruwela ) is one of the most popular accommodation options. This resort evokes special sympathies among Germans, British, residents of Australia and New Zealand. Its popularity does not decline in the winter. Foreigners are happy to come here to celebrate Christmas on the beach.

Guests who commented on Roy Villa Beach Hotel 2 * (Sri Lanka) or other local hotels are happy to describe Beruwela , noting the presence of very special beaches surrounded by slender coconut trees. According to reviews, the local beaches differ not only in impeccable cleanliness, but also in great length - up to 130 km. In addition, in Beruwel, tourists like to walk around the city, visit colorful markets, watch the life of local fishermen and, of course, do not miss the opportunity to fish themselves. Also , this resort is famous for its excellent diving center. A lot of the brightest, unforgettable impressions are provided to vacationers by immersion in the waters of the Indian Ocean, the study of its fauna and flora.

Roy Villa Beach Hotel 2 * (Sri Lanka, Beruwela): Acquaintance

This hotel is just a few steps from the boundless Indian Ocean. The modern Roy Villa Beach Hotel 2 * hotel (reviews, photos are presented in the article) attracts with its unique architecture, bright, spacious rooms. According to tourists who chose this hotel for leisure, leisure time spent among slender palms and plenty of sunshine, if possible daily enjoy the soft sand and warm waters of the ancient ocean, excellent service and comfort of rooms, like a real fairy tale.

Roy Villa Beach Hotel 2 * is called an excellent offer for a comfortable beach holiday in an atmosphere of peace and quiet, suitable for traveling romantics as well as for families with children, couples or lovers , friendship groups or corporate trips.

Location

This beachfront hotel is located in the southwestern part of Sri Lanka, on one of its most beautiful islands, between the most famous tourist areas of Kalutara and Aluthgama. The hotel is located on the first coastline, 100 meters from the sea. Bandaranayke International Airport (International Airport) is just over 70 km from here.

Tourists who were lucky enough to relax here tell that the hotel is located in the village of Kutukurund , in the resort area near Beruvella. The local railway station is called Payagala . There are no shops or cafes in the village, the authors of the reviews share. If you want to visit shops or eat in a restaurant, you need to take a bus ( tuk-tuk ) to a nearby resort town. Beruwela can be reached in around 10 minutes. To the north of the hotel is Kalutara - a large city, which can also be reached in 10 - 15 minutes. The institution is located 35 km from Ratmalan (airport) and 20 m from Bentota beach. Address: Sri Lanka, 12070, Beruwala, 1st Cross Street Nainawatta, North Payagala.
