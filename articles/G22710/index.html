Park-hotel "Gorodok" (Samara): description of rooms, service, reviews

If you like to relax by the river, be sure to pay attention to the Park-Hotel Gorodok. Samara is a beautiful city on the banks of the Volga. The hotel is located in a forest resort area. Staying here will give you the opportunity to enjoy the peace and fellowship with nature.





Hotel location

Park Hotel Gorodok is located away from the center of Samara (20 minutes by car), and only five minutes walk from the Volga River, surrounded by a shady forest. Hotel address: Russia, Samara, Studeny ravine, 3 line, house 116. The hotel has a category of 3 stars. Kurumoch Airport is a 25-minute drive away . For those who wish to diversify their vacation 9.3 kilometers from Gorodok, Victoria Aquapark is located, and at a distance of 4 kilometers is the Samara Arena stadium. You can admire the beautiful architecture of the old city.





Park-Hotel Gorodok: room description

The hotel offers guests spacious rooms, furnished comfortably and comfortably. Perhaps the settlement of one to three people. Three room categories are available:

  • Double standard with one double bed. Room rate is 2200 rubles per day.
  • Junior Suite with queen-size bed and sofa bed. Room rate is 2800 rubles per day.
  • In a separate building of the hotel there is a suite with a double bed, a sofa and armchairs. The cost for this number is 3300 rubles per day.

Each room has the following amenities:

  • TV with plasma screen;
  • air conditioning;
  • microwave;
  • small fridge;
  • ironing facilities;
  • safe;
  • free WI-FI (its speed may vary depending on the number of guests);
  • carpeted floor;
  • the rooms have a telephone connection;
  • each room has a desk;
  • The bathroom includes free toiletries and toilet paper;
  • In the cold season, the hotel has central heating.

Rooms are cleaned daily. The hotel allows guests with pets. A preliminary request is required to resolve this issue. Pets are free in the rooms.





Placement of children

For visitors to relax in the Park-Hotel Gorodok, children are accommodated on the following conditions:

  • if children under the age of three are accommodated in existing beds, no fee will be charged for their stay;
  • when an extra bed is provided for an older child, a fee of 500 rubles per night will be charged;
  • only one extra bed can be put in one room;
  • Cots are not available at the hotel.




Additional hotel amenities

If you decide to come to the Park-Hotel Gorodok, the service will be provided at a fairly high level.

  • Free private parking is available on site for guests with private cars.
  • There is luggage storage space.
  • The hotel has a safe for storing documents or valuables.
  • The hotel has a kiosk with fresh newspapers and local souvenirs.
  • Room service is available.
  • Room service is available.
  • The hotel has a launderette.
  • For guests in winter, the hotel has a left-luggage office for ski equipment.
  • For the maximum convenience of visitors, the reception is open around the clock.
  • Express check-in and check-out are available.
  • For relaxation and walks guests are provided with a terrace and a large garden.
  • If newlyweds are resting in the Gorodok, then a surprise awaits them in the rooms - a gift from the hotel.

No smoking is allowed on site. There are separate places for smoking. There are separate non-smoking rooms. The hotel has its own security service, video surveillance.

Catering

The price includes a hot breakfast in the restaurant. Upon request, guests can be provided with full meals. Packed lunches are available. Room service is also available. The garden has a barbecue area. If residents of the hotel prefer to eat on their own, then they have a bar and a restaurant. The restaurant is large, has two rooms:

  • red - up to 50 people can be accommodated;
  • white - accommodation for up to 100 people.

If you want to celebrate any event at the hotel (birthday, wedding, etc.), the restaurant has specialists in the preparation and conduct of banquets. They will help you solve problems such as:

  • menu selection;
  • interior decoration;
  • preparation of the holiday program.

If you plan to hold an official reception in the restaurant for any reason (presentation, anniversary, etc.), the restaurant staff can offer you a buffet table. The serving of buffet tables will be fully ready for the start of the event, a bar with a large selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks is also being established. Restaurant Park-Hotel Gorodok has modern conference rooms and offers guests who come to combine leisure with business a range of business services for which you can hold events or negotiations in a rented room. The cost of renting a conference room ranges from 5,000 to 10,000 rubles. Additionally, during negotiations, you can order lunch and dinner, which will cost 650 rubles per person.





Park-Hotel Gorodok: paid services

For the convenience of guests staying at the Gorodok park-hotel, there are additional paid services, namely:

  • installation of an extra bed in the room - 500 rubles;
  • organization of transfer airport - hotel - 850 rubles;
  • one hour of visiting the sauna - 1000 rubles;
  • use of a gazebo with barbecue, charcoal and skewers, 2 hours - 2500 rubles.
  • hotel staff will arrange a transfer to the point you need in Samara and back to the hotel - 300 rubles;
  • playing billiards, one hour - 200 rubles;
  • a game of table tennis - 100 rubles;
  • bicycle rental for two hours - 400 rubles.

If additional services on the hotel territory are used by business travelers, then their cost may be included in the cost of living with the provision of the necessary reporting documents.





Entertainment

In the park-hotel Gorodok (Samara) you will not have to be bored. Guests are offered a fairly large selection of entertainment.

  • For lovers of good weather, there are barbecue facilities and picnic tables located in the large garden on site. There are beautiful gazebos in the garden.
  • In the evenings, lovers can sing karaoke. Entertainment is also provided for guests, which are prepared by experienced staff.
  • For those who want to actively move around, the hotel has tables for playing table tennis, as well as the opportunity to ride bicycles.
  • The hotel offers organized hiking along shady forest paths and invigorating skiing in winter.
  • Also, for the most comfortable stay, the hotel has a spa, sauna and billiards.
  • For children there is a children's playground in the room, as well as a playground on the street.

Beautiful nature around the hotel, silence and fresh air will make your stay at the hotel unforgettable.

Positive feedback from travelers about staying at the hotel

Park Hotel Gorodok (Samara ), of course, leaves a lot of pleasant impressions.

  • Almost all vacationers celebrate very tasty food in a cafe at an affordable price.
  • In the area adjacent to the hotel, there are beautiful gazebos where you can enjoy the silence and listen to the birds singing.
  • A picnic area is also well equipped, where outdoor enthusiasts can cook kebabs. Many come to the hotel for the weekend for this.
  • All hotel guests unanimously celebrate clean and fresh air, beautiful nature in the vicinity of the hotel.
  • The territory occupied by the hotel is large due to the fact that it was rebuilt from a former pioneer camp. The hotel is completely surrounded by a dense high forest.
  • A beautiful scenic road through the forest leads to the Volga.
  • Near the hotel is the famous Bald Mountain, which offers beautiful views of the surroundings. From here you can photograph unique views.
  • Given the proximity of the city, some guests choose the time to go to admire Samara - an ancient city with beautiful architecture.
  • Many visitors are noted by the polite and attentive staff.




Negative Hotel Stay

Unfortunately, a number of vacationers at some moments of rest in the Park-Hotel Gorodok (Samara) had a negative impression.

  • Many hotel guests unanimously complain of poor sound insulation between rooms. When you want to relax in the evening, loud voices can be heard from the next room.
  • Also, those who come to rest in their own car note that it is rather difficult to find a hotel, it is almost impossible without a navigator. Signs are only in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.
  • There were also complaints that a non-smoking room had been pre-ordered, but upon check-in the room was not only for smokers, but was poorly ventilated.
  • It was also noted that in some rooms WI-FI is poorly caught. When contacting the administration, no alternative was proposed.
  • There were precedents when vacationers who had previously booked a room were not confirmed on the spot.
  • The negative review was also about the fact that, having arrived by prior arrangement with the hotel administration, the guests found that the room that had been booked was not yet cleaned after the eviction of the previous guests.

Conclusion

If you still decide to come to the Park-Hotel Gorodok, transport accessibility is ensured by the proximity to the city center. And after twenty minutes of driving you will find clean forest air. It is also convenient to get to Kurumoch airport ( or from the airport). Fatigue after the road will help you relieve the cozy atmosphere in the rooms, delicious food and friendly staff. Having a rest in a quiet calm place, you will surely remember the hotel location - Samara, Studeny ravine - and want to return here again.
