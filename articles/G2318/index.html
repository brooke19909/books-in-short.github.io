What is an iPad - dimensions, memory, processor

Among analogues, iPads firmly occupy one of the leading positions in the market of electronic tablets. All tablets under the general name iPad, the sizes of which can be divided into two types, often have the same filling. By their popularity, these gadgets also go ahead of the rest. Let's look at what an iPad is: dimensions, specifications and user reviews. And the second question that deserves attention: what size iPad should I buy? What is the difference between the two? And what factors are worth considering when deciding between an iPad and an iPad mini?

IPad tablet specifications

In fact, as already mentioned, today there are three types of iPad. Dimensions may vary even within the same type. This is a line of eight-inch tablets, which are commonly called iPad mini. Gadgets belong to the second type, which are simply called: iPad. Their dimensions are ten inches. And finally, the iPad PRO is thirteen inches in size. Each line also has several models, all of which are combined under the general name "iPad". The dimensions inside the line itself, if they differ, are slightly. Most of the differences relate to weight thickness. Yes, and technical specifications can differ dramatically.





Choosing a tablet by its physical size

The first thing a buyer pays attention to when choosing an iPad is its physical size and weight. For example, the iPad mini, the size in cm of which is 200 * 134.7, is very convenient for laying down in your hand.





On the other hand, having decided on the size, you can begin to choose a model. Further determination with a specific model will depend on the technical parameters necessary for the buyer, such as processor speed, RAM, the presence of an RF module, and so on. Also of great importance when choosing is the cost - it can differ significantly at times for different devices.
