Millennium Park Hotel 3 *, Turkey: description and reviews

Today, Turkey is considered one of the most open countries for domestic tourists. There are comfortable conditions for a beach holiday. Tourists from all over the world come here. Hotels offer relaxation in various categories of rooms. Service, accommodation, meals can vary significantly. In order not to be disappointed in the selected tourist complex, you need to consider reviews about it. One of the three-star hotels that offer our compatriots to relax in comfort is Millennium Park. How holidays are organized in this tourist complex will be discussed later.

general characteristics

Millennium Park Hotel 3 * is located 1 km from the center of Alanya. It is located within the city. The center can be reached on foot in 20 minutes. The complex is built on the second line from the sea. The coast is 200 meters away. An underground passage leads to the sea.



This is a picturesque area where it will be comfortable for various tourists to relax. The hotel was built in 2005. Periodically, its restoration. The last time the repair was carried out in 2012. Specialization

The 3-star Millennium Park Hotel is a great place to relax in the beautiful Alanya area. This is a relatively budget holiday. The hotel organizes a wonderful pastime for both adults and children. Rooms

Considering reviews of Millennium Park Hotel 3 *, it should be noted the condition of the rooms. A total of 118 rooms are equipped on site. There are standard and family rooms. They differ in the size of the room, the number of beds. There are special apartments for people with disabilities. The beds in the rooms can be single, double. There is a children's furniture.



In the cold season, heating is turned on. Each room has a balcony. The rooms have television, free wireless Internet, telephone, and air conditioning. There is a free minibar with refreshments. You can store valuables in a safe for a fee. The bathroom has a shower, toilet and wash basin. Towels are also provided. The rooms are regularly cleaned, linen is changed.

Nutrition

Millennium Park Hotel provides various dining programs. Most hotel guests prefer the all-inclusive system. The restaurant has a buffet. They serve local (Mediterranean, Turkish), international cuisine. There is a children's menu.



The restaurant is designed for comfortable accommodation of 250 people. You can eat on the outdoor terrace or in the equipped hall. A large selection of meat, fish dishes, fruits, salads and desserts will allow you to choose a dish to your taste. There is a bar near the pool. Here you can take alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, cocktails. There is also a large selection of juices, ice cream, desserts. There is also a bar on the beach. Here you can also order drinks, light snacks. It makes the rest enjoyable, relaxing.

Beach Vacation Reviews

Reviews of Millennium Park indicate a good organization of a beach holiday. The sandy coast is clean, the water is warm during the season. For a fee, you can rent umbrellas and sunbeds, mattresses. Various water activities are also available. You can go diving, surfing or windsurfing, play beach volleyball. Equipment can be rented. Also, vacationers can rent a water scooter, ride a "banana".





There is a sun terrace by the outdoor pool. There are also sunbeds and umbrellas. Nearby is a bar. There is a children's pool. Adults and kids can enjoy the on-site water park. This makes the pastime diverse and fun. Beach holidays at the hotel are well organized, the wishes of tourists with children are taken into account.

Service Reviews

Considering reviews of Millennium Park Hotel, it should be noted diverse service. The reception is open around the clock. This allows you to quickly populate visitors into rooms. Free parking is available on site. Wireless internet is available on site. All conditions for a comfortable rest for the disabled are created. Currency exchange also works. You can go to the laundry service, wash and iron the necessary things here. The complex operates around the clock security.

For families with children, appropriate conditions are created for an interesting holiday. The rooms are equipped with special furniture for babies (cribs, high chairs). There is a playground, a mini club. Kids can also swim in a special shallow pool. A fun kids waterslides really like kids.

Entertainment Reviews

The Millennium Park Hotel offers various activities for its guests. If you get bored of basking in the sun, you can do one of the popular sports. It can be football, volleyball or table tennis. Sports equipment is issued to everyone. Darts and minigolf competitions are also organized. In the evening and in the afternoon animators work. They run programs for kids and adults. However, most attractions, clubs and discos are located in the city center. Youth companies most often spend their leisure time here.





Various excursions are held. You can hike around the area. This will help to feel the color of Alanya, enjoy the views of the Mediterranean. Everyone will find an activity for themselves. In general, the hotel sets up for a calm, measured rest. This is a quiet tourist complex with well-groomed territory. The hospitable staff is always happy guests. The rooms are cleaned efficiently. Food and beach vacations are well organized.
