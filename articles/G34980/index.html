Probably everyone wants to visit Paris at least once in their life. Eat a fresh crispy croissant and enjoy the aroma of real perfumes. Many simply do not have enough financial resources to realize their dream. Some tourists try to save on travel by hitchhiking or passing vehicles. However, most try to find inexpensive accommodation for an overnight stay in the city. Hostels

The city has a huge number of places to sleep, which differ in comfort, remoteness from the center and, accordingly, cost.

Inexperienced tourists should be warned that cheap is not always far from the center. And Paris itself is such that not all sights are in the center. Those who are not in this city for the first time, certainly know what you can see in the city on the periphery.

Always reserve a place to sleep in advance. Paris does not tolerate spontaneous guests. You can book a hostel in Paris online on the website or by contacting the administrator directly. They will communicate best in English, not all staff understand and speak Russian.

All hostels from the list below were selected according to tourist reviews. It should be borne in mind that over time, everything in the apartment can change for the better (for example, repairs were made), and for the worse.

Vintage Paris Gare du Nord by Hiphophostels

Address: 73 rue de Dunkerque, 9th arrondissement. This hostel received 8.2 points from visitors.

The main advantage of most of the guests of these apartments consider the proximity to Montmartre (700 meters). Nearby are several metro stations, so you can get anywhere in the city very quickly. The Loft Boutique Hostel

The small hostel is open in the 20th arrondissement of Paris. Belleville Underground Station (lines 2 and 11) is 500 meters away. According to numerous guest reviews, the inn received 8.2 points. It is necessary to warn tourists that this area is quite specific. Many different nationalities.

The biggest disadvantages of this hostel, most of the guests include the fact that in the cold season there is very poor heating. Additional blankets or blankets are not issued. The beds in the rooms are uncomfortable (by the way, they creak very much). May not be lucky with the neighbors. In mixed rooms, most men are sometimes found. The lack of an elevator and a modest breakfast also somewhat overshadow the rest. The hostel is quite noisy at night.

A great plus, many consider the proximity to the metro and friendly staff. The rooms are spacious and clean. You can use the kitchen and everything you need. Bath and toilet in the room is a big plus. Nearby are many cafes and restaurants, as well as a beautiful park.

Laundry facilities and clean towels are available for a fee. There is free internet, but with a large number of guests it is very slow.

St Christopher's Inn Paris - Gare du Nord

St Christopher's Paris is located in central Paris (North Train Station 200 meters away). Tourists rated the hostel 8.0 points.

The area itself is somewhat alarming. A lot of homeless people and suspicious men. Some guests have noticed a lot of noise due to the music from the bar. Many say that in the off-season the hostel is very cold and even a warm blanket does not help. Small kitchen and only two dining tables (they are always busy).

In general, the hostel is clean and comfortable. Each bed is equipped with a curtain, its own lamp, socket and shelf. Therefore, personal space is preserved even in a room for 6 people. Downstairs there are 2 bars, a lounge area, and there are many entertainment venues and shops in the area. Towels are included with a delicious breakfast.
