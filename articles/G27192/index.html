At sea to the village of Lermontovo (Tuapse district)

The Black Sea coast has always been and remains an attractive place for a beach holiday. Among the many towns of various sizes located here, stands out the village of Lermontovo (Tuapse district).





On vacation with children

This small village is an ideal place for family holidays, as well as spending holidays with close friends. Many quiet resorts and pensions offer a break from the bustle of the city and enjoy the fresh sea air. And for those who prefer a more active holiday, in the village. Lermontovo has car camping and diving.

In recent years, he began to attract more and more tourists. Such popularity is explained by a wonderful combination of atmosphere of silence and comfort with a developed infrastructure and convenient location.

Weather

Located in a cozy bay, the village of Lermontovo (Tuapse district) is surrounded by mountains on which conifers grow. It is because of this that the air here is very clean and transparent.

The beach season begins in May, when the weather is not very hot, but it already smells in the summer, and the sea beckons into its waters. At this time, there are few vacationers, the beaches are deserted, and you can enjoy a relaxing holiday in the silence of the sea and mountains.





Despite the fact that in summer the air temperature warms up to + 26 ¬∞ C, it is quite comfortable to relax in the village of Lermontovo (Krasnodar Territory) . This is due to the fact that forests approach almost the very sea, therefore, despite the high degree, the heat is almost not felt. But at the same time, the water in the sea warms up to + 25 ¬∞ . Thus, a vacation in Lermontovo can be called ideal due to weather conditions, due to the combination of an unusually warm sea with the coolness of forests and mountains.

Until the middle of October, the beach season continues, and from the end of the month the rains begin, and the resort life freezes until spring comes.

Places for rest

The village of Lermontovo (Tuapse district) offers a wide selection of places to stay. Modern hotels, cozy pensions and holiday homes, as well as beach houses - all this can be found in a quiet village. Also, tourists are offered large entertainment complexes, parks and health centers. At the same time, housing and recreation prices here do not differ from other resort towns.





Beaches of Lermontovo

In the village there are two main beaches - Central and the famous beach "Gold Coast".

The first is distinguished by a large extent, so that it is not crowded even in the midst of the season. There are many shops, cafes, souvenir shops and other useful places for vacationers. Alternatively, you can rent a catamaran or jet ski and take a short boat trip. There is also a rental of children's cars and many attractions. Thus, vacationers of all ages will be able to find entertainment on Central Beach.

It is noteworthy that it is liked by both lovers of the sandy shore and those who prefer pebble beaches. Along the entire length, you can find places for tanning of both types.

Another advantage of this beach is the terrain and the seabed. It is quite gentle, which makes local vacations comfortable for the category of tourists of childhood and old age.

As for the beach "Golden Coast", it is located quite far from the center and does not offer such a variety of entertainment for vacationers. This is where car camping is located, which is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. In addition, prices there are significantly lower than for housing in the private sector. Such a vacation in Lermontovo (Tuapse district) will be remembered for a long time. A map of the area will allow you to navigate in this village and find the right place.

If you wish, you can find very wild beaches, which only local people know about. This will be a good choice for those who do not like crowds.

Entertainment for vacationers in Lermontovo

In this village, despite its modest size, you can find a lot of entertainment. For nature lovers horseback riding in the mountains, tent rental and just walking in the picturesque places of the village are provided. Gorges, waterfalls, mountain valleys and rivers - all these picturesque places will not leave anyone indifferent.





For lovers of more active relaxation, the Black Sea water park operates on the beach, and in the evening discos and night clubs open with entertainment programs and competitions.

For housing, you can choose the private sector or any guesthouse. Lermontovo (Tuapse district) is rich in various establishments for those who want to spend several weeks in this area or even a weekend.

What to see

In addition, vacationers are offered a variety of excursions for sightseeing of the village. These include:

  • Eagle Rock is a complex of entertainment and excursion programs where you can ride horses, play paintball, take mud baths and go mountain climbing and jumping.
  • A trip to the Tenginsky and Teshebsky waterfalls, as well as to the Kiselev rock will leave an unforgettable impression of the majestic beauty of the nature of the Caucasus.

In addition, as part of the excursion program, you can go to the Ashe river valley, which is located in the Lazarevsky district of Sochi, and even cross the Russian border and visit Abkhazia.





Thus, choosing the village of Lermontovo (Tuapse district) for your vacation, you will enjoy not only the clean sea and fresh air, but also the beautiful nature of the Caucasus.
