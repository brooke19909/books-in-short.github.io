Pension "Dzhubga". Description, photos, reviews of tourists

"Dzhubga" is a boarding house located in the Krasnodar Territory in the village of the same name in the Tuapse region. Among all the accommodations in the resort, he occupies a special position. general description

In the boarding house "Dzhubga" buildings are located between Mount Hedgehog and the river of the same name. The staff of the complex with awe protects the local unique environmental conditions and invites guests to taste all the beauties of the Black Sea resorts.

Accommodation

The resort center places its vacationers in five dormitory buildings, which were built in stages, so as not to harm the environment by a global construction project. The whole complex of the boarding house fits perfectly into the general landscape and organizes an attractive shade of the shift of various eras, as well as nature and culture. To the sandy beach from the sleeping quarters to get about 2-3 minutes on foot. The rooms are organized in a varied color palette and styles. The first building of the boarding house "Dzhubga" (Tuapse) is a seven-story building in which you can choose for yourself a room of a different budget category. In double and triple rooms (the bathroom is shared), vacationers are provided with the following bedroom set: 2 single beds, 2 bedside tables, wardrobe and a nice bonus in the form of a balcony. You can relax in the lobby, sitting on comfortable furniture while watching TV. In the rooms of this configuration, but an improved standard category, there is a TV and more comfortable furniture. Two-room rooms have a living room and bedroom with separate loggia exits. The living room is equipped as follows: there is a sofa and an armchair, if necessary, a folding one, a refrigerator and a TV. In the rooms of this level the bathroom is separate. The second building of the boarding house is a building three stories high, from the windows of which you can see sea and mountain landscapes. Nearby is the beach, from which guests are 150 meters away. There are double and single rooms (the bathroom is combined) with first-class furniture, a refrigerator and a TV. In the 5th building, guests are offered triple rooms of economy class. In eight- and seven-story buildings are provided double rooms economy and standard with personal entrances. The buildings are located on the coast.

Leisure

Pension "Dzhubga" offers a classic selection of entertainment characteristic of the resort: the opportunity to make a swim in a mask and fins, play table tennis and volleyball on the beach, and make a boat trip. The guesthouse has a library, a cinema room and its own beauty salon.

Nutrition

The boarding house "Dzhubga" has a two-story dining room, designed for 1000 seats, with several rooms and built-in split systems. In terms of nutrition, guests are offered a wide selection of fruits and vegetables. Guests do not need to worry about cooking, as the boarding house provides three meals a day, included in the room price.

Infrastructure

On the territory of the "Dzhubga" boarding house there is a guarded paid parking, an impressive size sauna with a swimming pool, an indoor saltwater pool (open during the off-season), a tea room, a bar, a solarium, a relaxation and massage room, rental of sports equipment, a sports hall and playgrounds , billiards, library, dance floor, two-story dining room with a total capacity of 1000 seats, a cinema. Various excursions are also organized here.

Services

Vacationers are provided with the services of a dentist, as well as a beauty parlor, manual massage, first aid. The following treatment methods are widely used: massage, inhalation, herbal medicine, rehabilitation and general recovery programs, physiotherapy. The treatment of diseases: the nervous system, metabolism, urological, respiratory system.

How to get, vacationers comments

You can get to the place from the Tuapse railway station by minibus, bus or taxi directly to the village of Dzhubga. Reviews about the boarding house "Dzhubga" are different. But the health resort has a large number of satisfied guests. This is due, first of all, the courtesy of the staff, comfortable rooms, delicious food, private beach. Pension "Dzhubga" is what you need for those who are going to organize a family vacation, as well as a vacation for two.
