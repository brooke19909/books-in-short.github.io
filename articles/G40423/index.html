Hotel Moremar 3 * (Spain / Costa Brava) - photos, prices and reviews

One of the most popular resorts in Spain is the Costa Brava. This region is famous for its wonderful climate, magnificent nature, picturesque coast and an abundance of excellent beaches. The center of the tourist life of the Costa Brava is the city of Lloret de Mar. He really likes young people looking for a fun, vibrant and rich in impressions of relaxation. The local hotel base is represented quite widely. However, the most popular among vacationers from around the world are two- and three-star hotels. Today we propose to consider one of them. It will be about the Moremar 3 * hotel. We will find out what is offered to tourists and at what cost, as well as find out what impressions our compatriots have already lived in this hotel.





Location

Hotel Moremar 3 * has every right to be proud of its location. So, from the center of Lloret de Mar it is only 800 meters away. Therefore, all the entertainment of the largest resort town of the Spanish region of the Costa Brava is within walking distance for hotel guests. In addition, the hotel complex "Moremar" is located in the commercial quarter of the city. Therefore, it is surrounded by a wide variety of shops and souvenir shops, as well as restaurants and bars. As for airfields, the nearest airborne harbor is in the city of Girona. It is only thirty kilometers from Lloret de Mar. Thus, after landing, you can reach the hotel in just 30-40 minutes. The hotel is also accessible from Barcelona Airport. The length of this path is 90 kilometers, and it will take you a little more than two hours to get there. One of the most important factors for all tourists going on vacation to the coastal countries is the proximity of the hotel in which they plan to stay to the seashore. As for the Moremar 3 * hotel (Spain), it does not have its own beach. However, the nearest municipal beach, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable and varied vacation, is only 80 meters from the hotel. Thus, guests of the hotel, leaving their room, in a few minutes can plunge into the clear azure waters of the Mediterranean Sea.





Moremar 3 * (Lloret-de-Mar): photo and description

This hotel was built almost forty years ago, in 1976. Since then, repair work has been carried out here several times. The last large-scale reconstruction was carried out in 2009. Today, this hotel complex consists of three three-story buildings, consisting of 76 comfortable rooms. There are also apartments specially equipped for disabled guests. The hotel has a well-kept clean area with a garden, an outdoor pool, a sun terrace, a restaurant and a bar. In general, we can say that Moremar 3 * (Spain) is ideal for young people who come on vacation in search of a vibrant fun pastime and vivid impressions, but at the same time wish to stay in a budget hotel. However, it will be quite comfortable here for families with children who come here for a beach holiday and sightseeing.

Hotel Policies

Hotel Moremar 3 * (Costa Brava, Spain), like most hotels around the world, has a so-called checkout time. So, the arrival of tourists in the rooms booked by them category is carried out after one in the afternoon. However, the presence of such a rule does not mean at all that if you arrived at the hotel early in the morning, you will be forced to wait 13:00. If the hotel has free and prepared for arrival rooms, you will be settled immediately. If the previous guests have not yet moved out, and the maids have not had time to clean the rooms (most often this happens at the height of the tourist season), then you will be asked to leave things in the luggage room and relax by the pool, eat in a restaurant, go for a walk in the vicinity or straight to the beach. As for the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before 11 a.m. At the same time, it is necessary to hand over the room keys at the reception and pay for the entire period of stay at Moremar 3 *. Here it is possible to pay both in cash and with a plastic card "Visa", "Mastercard", "American Express" and some other popular payment systems.

This item will be very important for you if you are used to going on vacation in the company of your four-legged pet. Unfortunately, the rules of the Spanish Hotel Moremar (Lloret de Mar) prohibit these guests from staying on site. Therefore, you will either have to find another hotel from the Pets Friendly category for accommodation, or leave your pet at home for the duration of the trip.





Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the Moremar 3 * hotel (Spain) has 76 comfortable rooms. They are located in three three-story buildings. The hotel complex is represented by single, double and triple rooms, among which there are rooms for disabled people. In addition, there is the possibility of extra beds. All rooms have their own bathroom with the necessary bath amenities, as well as a TV, telephone, comfortable furniture, and a safe. Some rooms have a balcony or terrace with views of the surrounding landscape. Some rooms also have a kitchenette with everything you need to cook. There is ceramic tile everywhere on the floor. Rooms are cleaned and towels are changed every other day. Bed linen is changed once a week.

Nutrition

In the three-star Hotel Moremar, guests have the opportunity to choose one of two types of food: half board (breakfast and dinner) or full board (three meals a day). Three times a day, in the restaurant of the hotel complex, chefs serve a buffet, which offers a variety of dishes of both Mediterranean and international cuisine. There is also a children's and diet menu.

Entertainment
