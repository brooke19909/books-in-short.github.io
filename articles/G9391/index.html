Wish Card: Compilation Rules and Reviews

Treasure map, visualization map and wish map - all these names belong to one unique method that allows you to fulfill your plan. Does the wish card work? The reviews of those who compiled it speak of the fulfillment of the mystery. And this sometimes applies even to funny trifles.

Drafting Methods

You can make a wish card like this:

  1. Manually. For this purpose, pictures representing clippings from magazines or printed photographs are pasted onto the Whatman paper. Further, such a card is drawn up with felt-tip pens or paints. Pictures are also signed manually.
  2. On the computer. To do this, you need to own a photo editor, which compiles collages from found or your photos.




How it works?

Do not think that creating a wish card is to build some kind of magic thing. The effectiveness of this method is explained even by psychologists. The fact is that when a person clearly understands what he is dreaming of, he has a clear image of his desire. For example, it can be a house where you want to live, a child, applauding fans and much more. If you think about it constantly, the image will go into the subconscious.

After some time, a person, without even realizing it, will take the necessary actions that will lead him to fulfill his dreams. This effect has its name, namely - visualization of desires. You can strengthen it when setting out life goals on paper in the form of text or in the form of images. And if all this is also beautifully designed, and the person begins to consider the fruits of his labors every day, then the desired will come soon. Psychologists call this technique a visualization board. At its core, this is the wish card we are considering.

An explanation of her work can be found in Feng Shui. This is an ancient art that helps to plan the fate of man, says that people's thoughts have a strong connection with the universe. And whoever sends clearly articulated signals is already on the path to success. The universe will certainly help their implementation.

Creation technique

A do-it-yourself wish map is made according to certain rules. First of all, a person should clearly and clearly articulate his concept of what he most wants. After this, it is necessary to imagine that this has already come true, that is, to visualize your desires.





Various methods are suitable for this. Most people are helped by a relaxing and calm atmosphere at the moment when a wish card is made. The rules for composing a collage dictate to start presenting your dreams to a pleasant melody, comfortably sitting in an armchair or on the couch. For example, if a person‚Äôs cherished dream is a large and beautiful house, then it is worth collecting his image in the imagination. It is necessary to mentally imagine the facade of the building, walk through all the rooms, light a fireplace, sit in a comfortable armchair, etc. After that, it is necessary to remember those pleasant sensations that were experienced at that moment. After a clear statement of all desires, they need to be written on a piece of paper. This will be needed in the future in order to create a map of desires.

The rules for compiling a collage warn against the use of a ‚Äúnot‚Äù particle. All cherished dreams should be described in the present tense and as specifically as possible. But do not think that everything that you dream of can be formulated in just ten minutes. In this case, the wish card will most likely upset than please its owner. After all, this collage will not reflect those dreams that are really important. So how should the wish card be planned? The compilation rules require a person to devote at least an hour to formulate his goals, during which one could calmly think about what he really wants. However, this process should not end there. After preliminary consideration, several days should pass to double-check your desires. Are they really the most important in life? Only after that a person can confidently say that he wants to have exactly what he wanted.

Practical steps

How is a wish card created? The rules for compiling such a collage require you to do this work yourself. You cannot involve relatives or friends, acquaintances or even strangers in this process. In addition, the production of such a collage should begin only with a positive mood.





A wish fulfillment card is applied on a piece of paper. In addition, at hand should be:

- old magazines;

- ruler;

- glue;

- paint or colored paper;

- scissors.

How to make a wish card? According to Feng Shui (if you follow the instructions of this practice), you will need to fill in nine sectors that correspond to the main areas of human life. Ancient art correlates each of them with the cardinal points. In addition, each sector must have a specific tone. Thus, the first step in solving the question is "How to make a wish card?" (see photo below) consists in dividing the Whatman paper into 9 equal parts.





Further, each of the obtained sectors is painted over or sealed with a color that corresponds to a certain side of the world.

The importance of sectors

So, your Whatman is divided into nine equal parts. What does each of these mean when compiling a wish map? We consider each of the sectors individually, following the same rule that is followed when reading books, that is, from left to right and from top to bottom. Inside each of these parts you will need to place your photo, combined with the most cherished desires. In other words, these should not be separate texts or pictures. Filling each part should be a collage.

Wealth sector

Let's start with the first top of the wish card. It represents a sector of wealth. This area is painted with light green or light green color. The wealth sector on our treasure map will be responsible for everything that connects a person with money and wealth. Dreams about a summer house or an apartment, a car or a motorcycle, a cottage in the mountains or on the Black Sea coast, etc. are placed here. However, in this case too, the rules dictate their wish card. All dreams must be expressed in the wealth sector in a specific amount. The desire "I want a lot of money" just does not work. This sector can be strengthened. To do this, you will need to place a red pyramid with the LAM mantra in it.

Sector of fame

Love sector

Central sector

What should the correct wish card include? Reviews who work with this unusual visualization are advised to pay attention to the central section of whatman paper. This sector should contain the most cherished and main dream! The rules for creating a treasure map allow you to place several wishes, if it is difficult to choose only one of them. When to embed pictures?

According to the rules of Feng Shui, you need to make a wish card with the growing moon. However, the best time to fill it out will be the first two weeks after the Chinese New Year. He does not recommend Feng Shui to place his desires during periods of lunar or solar eclipse.

Those who do not adhere to the Chinese art of visualization are advised to draw up their wish card at a time when you really want to do it.

Activation

So, you have compiled a map of desires. How to use it? It should be borne in mind that pictures with desires should always be in your sight, but at the same time hidden from prying eyes.
