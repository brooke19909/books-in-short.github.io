DIY tie pattern: elastic model and aristocratic samovyaz butterfly

A tie has long ceased to be the subject of an exclusively male wardrobe. Ladies wear it with pleasure. Sometimes, for a certain image, a girl needs a tie of a specific shape and color, and there is nowhere to buy it. This article presents patterns of accessories of various types: long with an elastic band and a butterfly-samovyaz.

Elastic tie

And is it so difficult - a pattern of a tie with an elastic band? Let's figure it out.

  • We draw the pattern as shown in the photo. Add along the fold line.




  • Cut out the pattern. Then we turn it over and mark the place where the lining will be.




  • At the end of the manipulations, the pattern (tie) should look like this.




  • We lay out pre-ironed fabric. We cut the tie obliquely.




  • Take a piece of non-woven fabric and glue it with an iron. This is necessary so that the accessory retains its shape. We will tuck the corner and stitch.




  • Fold the tie along the fold line and stitch.




  • The workpiece must be turned out and ironed.
  • Measure the width of the top of the tie. If necessary, trim it with scissors.
  • We cut out the knot: we need a strip equal to the double width of the upper part of the accessory, plus a small seam allowance.
  • The part must be strengthened with non-woven.
  • Fold it in half and sew.




  • Turn and iron so that the seam is centered.
  • We smooth it back and cut off the unnecessary.
  • We spread the knot with a trapezoid and sew on an elastic band.








  • We put the tip of the tie into a knot and beautifully straighten it.




As you can see, the pattern of a tie with an elastic band is very simple. In order to create an accessory, you will need elementary skills of a seamstress, fabric and a sewing machine.

Do not forget that such a tie can only be worn in an informal setting. The accessory is considered an alternative to the classic form, which must be tied. A tie with an elastic band is suitable for boys, women and men as part of a stage costume. For celebrations, as well as in the business world, it is customary to wear classic models.

Types of Samovyaz Butterflies

There are several varieties of such accessories:

  • Butterfly is a classic of the genre.
  • Large Butterfly is a chic solemn option. The tie is slightly wider than the classic in width, but completely repeats its shape.
  • Modified Batterfly - A modified form of the first option. The waist of such a butterfly is slightly narrower than that of a classic accessory. As you know, it was worn by Winston Churchill.
  • Batwing is a butterfly shape that differs from Butterfly in the absence of a ‚Äúwaist‚Äù. When untied, it resembles a bat.
  • Batwing - Diamond Point - a mixture of classic shapes. The size is the same as the Batwing model, and the waist is the size of the Butterfly.

Do-it-yourself bow tie: pattern

In this workshop you will learn how to create a classic accessory.

Tools to be prepared for work:

  • pattern;
  • scissors;
  • a piece of chalk;
  • sushi stick / pencil / knitting needle;
  • sewing machine.

Materials:

  • non-woven;
  • the cloth;
  • fasteners.

Let's figure out step by step how to sew a tie with your own hands:

  • The pattern for each person is built individually. Take the necessary measurements and build a pattern according to the scheme. Cut it and pin it to the fabric from the inside.




  • Circle the contour with a seam allowance (7 mm). Cut out the part.
  • We put the workpiece on the fabric and pin it with studs. Together with the allowances, we cut out three more such details. There will be four of them.
  • Four parts are also cut out of non-woven fabric.
  • We glue the non-woven blanks to the fabric using an iron.
  • We sew the parts, folding them face to face, leaving a small hole for turning out. Before turning the workpiece, we cut off all excess fabric and corners. You can also make several cuts at the bend points.
  • Unscrew the bow tie with a long object. Close the hole with a blind seam.
  • Velcro can be sewn or glued to the ends of the butterfly.
