Tulips from satin ribbons: a master class and detailed instructions

You can create a joyful and bright spring mood in the apartment with the help of a picture that shows tulips from satin ribbons. The master class will be presented in the article.

Ribbon painting

Recently, sewing with ribbons on fabric is very popular. The flowers are voluminous, bright and shiny, and the picture is spring and joyful.





We will consider in detail step-by-step instructions on how to make such flowers. You will need satin ribbons. Not too wide are selected, approximately from 1.5 to 2.5 cm. For leaves and stems, green ribbons will be needed wider, and for flowers, select natural shades of natural petals.

Ribbon Sewing

Before you begin, you need to buy a background piece of crepe satin or gabardine and a wooden frame. We pull the fabric from the back of it and draw the contours of the tulips in small pieces. We start the master class of satin ribbons by inserting a ribbon into the needle with a wide slot and stitching one flower with several stitches along the length of the bud.

When all the flowers are done, we begin work on the stems. Having pulled the needle with the tape from the wrong side, they wind it up to the required length. Then, with the needle again, we lower the lower part to the back of the picture.





It remains to consider a master class of tulips from satin ribbons, showing how to create leaves. To do this, take a wider tape and thread it through the fabric from the back of the picture. The first puncture is made at the bottom of the picture, then the tape is twisted to the other side and inserted into the fabric from above, in the place where the leaf will end.

Kanzashi Tulips

Satin ribbons can be used to make tulips using the famous and popular kanzashi technique . A bouquet of such flowers can be attached to a girl‚Äôs hairpin or used as a wedding bouquet.





Let us consider in more detail how the elements of such a flower are made. It is necessary, in addition to tapes, to buy another glue gun. With it, all the basic elements are made.

How to make leaves?

We take a wide ribbon of green color and cut it into several identical rectangles. Further, each part is folded in half on the wrong side, and the edges are connected using an adhesive gun. When the leaf is completely dry, the part is turned on the front side. We will supplement the master class of tulips from satin ribbons with a story about how the leaves are attached to the rest of the structure.





Many leaves and flowers are made at once, the manufacture of which will be described below. Then the whole bouquet is sewn with thread from the bottom.

How to make petals?

To make tulips from ribbons with your own hands, you need to cut out squares from a wide ribbon. Then the part is folded four times, the raw edge is cut in a semicircle. Next, the elements are sewn along the edge of the stitches and tightened so that the petal was picked up. In order to make a tulip, you need to make several such petals.

Then from below the flower is folded together, the petals are distributed on all sides. The lower part is sewn to the stem. Now you need to combine all the flowers in a bouquet. First, tulips gather in the middle of the bunch. Then the leaves are distributed around the edges so that they are supported all together. Below the bouquet is tied with a beautiful bow. It can be sewn so that the decoration does not slide down the slippery satin.





Such a beautiful bouquet can be made for the bride to the wedding, using white ribbons to create tulips. Also, the technique of kanzashi is often used by craftsmen for making hairpins or bows for girls. Such beautiful bouquets of tulips can be sewn to a round basis of felt and make a festive bracelet on your hand or attached to a hat, bandaged with a ribbon. Not to mention the creation of wall panels and framed paintings that can be put on the table.

Creating flowers from satin ribbons is interesting and fast. A large beautiful picture can be made in a couple of hours. So do not hesitate and try to make your masterpiece, especially since you already read the detailed instructions in the article.
