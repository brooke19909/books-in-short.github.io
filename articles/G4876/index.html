Pattern rabbits Piglet: how to sew a soft toy with your own hands

A cute and funny bunny with long ears and a timid face is very popular with girls of any age. Each needlewoman can make such a toy on a Piglet hare pattern, and without leaving her home. A fluffy animal will be an excellent gift for a child, Easter decoration or a small cozy attribute of a house.

Funny bunny

With the help of a bunny pattern, a piglet with your own hands to make a toy is not difficult. The main thing is to determine the size, material, arm yourself with the necessary fabric and get down to business.

The full-size Piglet hare pattern will allow you to sew a large toy, which, of course, will delight the kids. In the process of making a fur bunny, use your imagination and skill. A hare can be either large or small, made of fur and other fabric, with or without clothes.

Easter guest

In some European countries, rabbits are considered a must-have accessory when decorating a home or Easter holiday table. You can bring a charming tradition to every home and put a sweet guest at the Easter table.

For the sewing of an Easter rabbit, the following materials are needed:

  • Piglet hare pattern;
  • pieces of fur;
  • filler;
  • non-woven;
  • needle, thread, scissors;
  • "eyes" or buttons for the eyes;
  • blush
  • pieces of fabric for a rabbit wardrobe.

Work algorithm:

  1. Before starting work, we take a pattern of a Piglet hare and carefully transfer it to a fur fabric. It is necessary to cut parts, leaving allowances for seams.
  2. Turn inside out, sew all the details, fill with filler and flash with a blind seam.
  3. We cut out the ears from the non-woven fabric. We apply them on the same elements of fur with the adhesive side down. We iron long ‚Äúears‚Äù through a damp cloth. No filler needed: they should hang down. To give the ears an interesting shape, part from the side of the nonwoven of one ear we fold in half and sew with stitches.
  4. The muzzle, sewn according to the pattern of the Piglet hare, should be cute and meek. Sew on buttons instead of eyes.
  5. From multi-colored patches sew a blouse for a bunny. It will be smart and festive.

With a bow around the neck

A full-size Piglet hare pattern will allow you to sew a large toy. She will surely please the kids. Among the cute toys created by the Piglet hare pattern, there are several interesting creations:

  • Easter bunny.
  • Simpleton with a bow.

For sewing the second option, the following materials are needed:

  • Piglet hare pattern;
  • bright fabric for the body and inner side of the ears;
  • plain fabric, for example, fleece;
  • beads for the eyes
  • threads, scissors, needle;
  • sewing machine.

Step-by-step instruction:

  1. Transfer the pattern to the wrong side of the fleece: 2 ear parts, 4 paws, 1 muzzle, 1 tail. We will transfer other details to the colored fabric: 2 parts of the body, 2 parts of the ears.
  2. Neatly stitch all parts on a typewriter or sew them by hand. First, we will take up the body of colored fabric. We cut out the parts, sew, fill with filler. Sew parts of the legs, tighten, sew to the body. Sew the details of the ears so that the lower parts are made of colored material. We connect the parts of the head, fill it with a sealant. We fix the ears.
  3. Making the face: sew from the beads of the eye. From a red bead - a nose.
