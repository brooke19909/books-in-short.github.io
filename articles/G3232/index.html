Hotels in Berezniki: names, review, addresses, reservations and reviews

The city of Berezniki is the second largest in the Perm region. Despite this, there are quite a few hotels. Most of the offers for an overnight stay are apartments for rent. But for a person who has arrived in the city on business, or for a family with a child, this option is not always advantageous.

Therefore, you should familiarize yourself with the list of the most popular hotels among tourists in Berezniki of various types: business, economy, mini.

The popular hotel "Berezniki"

In the city center at 3 Sovetskaya Square, the famous Berezniki Hotel is located. It belongs to the economy class, but offers quite comfortable comfortable rooms of various categories:

  • single;
  • double economy class;
  • standard double;
  • Suite
  • Studios
  • Suite
  • apartments (1 bedroom, living room, kitchen).

Room rates - from 1800 to 4500 rubles per day. Advance booking is optional.

Pluses noted in the reviews by former guests of the Berezniki hotel:

  1. Breakfast buffet. The dishes are varied, prepared with fresh products.
  2. The rooms are warm and clean.
  3. Wi-Fi is free, it works not only near the reception, but also in the rooms.
  4. The prices are affordable.
  5. Convenient location, close to the Central Department Store and public transport.

Hotel Aelita

"Aelita" is the only hotel in the city of Berezniki of the Perm Territory, which offers only rooms of the category "apartments". The hotel is located on Pyatiletki street, 116A. Nearby are a shopping center, shops, pharmacies, ATMs, stops.

At the service of vacationers are available one-, two- and three-room apartments, suites. Booking is most conveniently carried out on the hotel website, because in this case there is an additional discount.

The cost of apartments is from 3200 to 3900 rubles / day. Living in a suite will cost 6,000 rubles. Breakfast is included in the price and takes place on the territory of the Gogol-Mogol cafe-grill or in the room.

The main advantages of Aelita, which are noted by hotel guests: comfortable and clean rooms, high-quality textiles, a set of bathroom accessories, a set of dishes in the kitchen area, free wi-fi in the rooms, a full range of hotel services.

Business Hotel Pride Hotel

The hotel offers comfortable rooms in the following categories:

  • single standard;
  • double with a double bed;
  • double standard;
  • superior comfort;
  • Junior Suite
  • Suite

Room rates - from 3900 to 6500 rubles per night. When booking on the hotel website you can get a discount of 5%. For corporate clients and newlyweds special offers.

Breakfast is included in the price. Dinner is available at an extra charge.

Address: Bolshevik passage, 5.

Highlights that guests of the Pride Hotel celebrate:

  • the rooms are bright, spacious, comfortable;
  • beds with orthopedic mattresses and hypoallergenic pillows;
  • all rooms have hairdryer, split system, mini-bar;
  • For breakfast you can order a dish of European or Russian cuisine;
  • any discounts on the cost of living are constantly valid;
  • On the ground floor there is a conference room for 70 people with the necessary office equipment (telephone, fax, printer, projector and so on).

Hotel Edem

"Eden" - a hotel in Berezniki of the Perm Territory with affordable prices. Visitors appreciate the hospitality and comfortable living conditions that are offered here.

The cost of accommodation in standard rooms (single, double with one or two beds) is from 3300 rubles per day. 2460 rubles per day - the price of a room without a bathroom (bathroom and toilet in the block for two rooms). The cost of living in a suite is from 8650 to 12180 rubles.

It offers guests: parking, free Wi-Fi, breakfast included in the price, business lunches during the day and dinner with Italian dishes.

Reviews about this hotel are mostly positive. In most cases, guests especially note comfortable quiet rooms, convenient location, nutritious breakfasts.

The Edem Hotel is located at 149 Lomonosov Street. It is better to book rooms in advance on the website or by phone.

Economy Hotel "Birch"

"Birch" is the most budget option of a hotel in Berezniki. Accommodation in a triple room costs only 300 rubles per day per place. It has three single beds, a wash basin, a set of dishes. A separate sauna and shower room is available on a block of six rooms.

The cost of living in a single room is 950 rubles per day, in a double room - 1150 rubles per day. This apartment has its own bathroom with shower, fridge, cable TV.

Guests note that the Berezka hotel is most suitable for accommodating no more than a day or for large tourist groups (up to 200 people can be accommodated at the same time). Among the advantages of an economy hotel, guests also mention:

  • budget prices;
  • free wifi in rooms;
  • convenient location of the hotel (in the city center);
  • free parking for 25 places;
  • excellent sound insulation.

Hotel address "Birch": Lenin Avenue, 47A.

Mini-hotel "Family"

In addition to standard hotels, there are small guest houses in Berezniki. One of these is the Family Hotel. It offers guests accommodation in one of three one-story houses:

  1. Standard. Single or double occupancy, it is possible to install an extra bed in the form of a folding bed. Area - 20 square meters. m
  2. Improved. Single, double or triple occupancy. Area - 30 square meters. m
  3. Family two-room. Accommodation options: from one to five. Area - 60 square meters. m

The cost of living is from 700 to 3000 rubles per day. At night check in there is a 20% discount. There are also discounts for long stays. This is especially true for business travelers.

Each house has a kitchen area with a microwave, an electric kettle and a set of dishes, an LCD TV with satellite antenna, and a bathroom. The family house has a sauna (surcharge).

Due to the fact that only three houses are available for accommodation, advance reservation is required.

Residents of cottages celebrate such moments:

  • the possibility of arrival at any time;
  • the presence of secure parking;
  • each house has free wi-fi;
  • free grill;
  • clean rooms;
  • comfortable furniture;
  • budget prices;
  • special discounts;
  • romantic atmosphere for the newlyweds (holiday dishes, elegant linens, fireplace with live fire, holiday basket).

There is a mini-hotel "Family" on the street Lomonosov, 165.

In total, eight double rooms are available to vacationers. Each has modern single beds. Showers and toilets are located on the floor. The kitchen is also shared.

The cost of living in a guest house is only 500 rubles per day. This is significantly lower than hotel prices, which is what guests say. At the same time, living conditions are much better than in a hostel. In addition, when booking on the hotel‚Äôs website, a discount of 5% applies.
