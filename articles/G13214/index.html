Camping - Volgograd, camp site "Barvikha"

City life is a daily stress: troubles at work, family troubles, domestic problems, annoying hum of cars and public transport. A person just needs a break from all this fuss, and what could be better than a rest in nature away from civilization on the banks of a river or lake!

Chalet Barvikha

The city of Volgograd is one of the largest regional centers of our country: more than a million inhabitants, developed industry, a huge amount of land transport. The population simply needs places where you can hide from all this city life. Fortunately, there are opportunities for this, because the city is located on the banks of the beautiful Volga River, on both sides of which there are many recreation centers and just good places. Very convenient in this regard is the picturesque Volgograd.

Barvikha is located on the outskirts of the city on the Hungry Island. The center of Volgograd is only thirty kilometers from the recreation center .

The nature in these places is simply incomparable. Not only vacationers, but also fishermen chose the island of Golodny, because the fishing here is magnificent.

Accommodation for vacationers

Volgograd, the camp site "Barvikha" is a resort complex that is ideal for families, holding various celebrations and for leisure activities of a small friendly company.

Wooden log cabins are not rented out according to numbers, but as a whole. All houses are designed for the number of vacationers up to six people, while additional accommodation is possible.





Six log cabins are alike. Each of them has three bedrooms, an equipped kitchen, two bathrooms - on the first and second floor, as well as two toilets. The house has all the necessary household appliances, in each room there is a TV with satellite channels.

There is a family house, which is designed for families with small children. For the convenience of accommodation, among other things, there is a crib-playpen.

There is a house for the newlyweds. He is also a six-seater, but decorated in a romantic style.

The cost of rest in the above houses from Monday to Thursday will cost you 5400 rubles per day, rest on Friday, Saturday and holidays will cost 8900 rubles per day, and rest on Sunday will cost tourists 6900 rubles.

Among the cottages there are two that are different from the rest. House number 9 is characterized in that it has a large covered balcony and terrace, which can accommodate up to 20 people. This house is perfect for celebrating various celebrations. The cost of the house is from 6900 to 12000 rubles per day.

The second house is the Fisherman's House. It is designed to rest from 12 to 18 people. It has four bedrooms, a large living room (80 square meters) and all other amenities. This house is in demand for New Year's holidays and other celebrations with a large number of people.

Tourists reviews

In order to better understand all the pros and cons of this recreation center, we turn to the reviews of vacationers.

An elite vacation in ideal conditions near the city offers the camp site "Barvikha" (Volgograd). Reviews of vacationers say that its territory is very large, well-groomed and simply excellent. Everything is decorated with the help of a landscape designer, stylish and beautiful. Alpine hills, exquisite plants, picturesque flower beds and magnificent night illumination did not leave anyone indifferent.





The beach is also happy, reviews about it are positive. Guests of the recreation center say that the price includes breakfast in the restaurant. Almost everyone is satisfied with their quality. There were no complaints about the restaurant either.

However, among the positive aspects, there are also disadvantages. Firstly, the price - according to many, it is overpriced. Well, in some reviews they spoke negatively about the cleanliness of the houses themselves. Apparently, noisy companies leave a lot of dirt, and the staff does not clean very well, hence the complaints.

In general, the reviews of tourists are quite satisfactory. If you want to relax away from city noise and do not have time for long trips, pay attention to this country place - Barvikha (camp site), Krasnoarmeysky district, Volgograd.
