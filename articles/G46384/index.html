Oba Time Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description of rooms, service, reviews

Most of them note the excellent conditions that are presented at the hotel for travelers: for them there is a huge amount of entertainment, excellent service, delicious food, as well as a beautiful interior in the rooms.

general information

The date of construction of Oba Time Hotel 4 * in all official sources is 2012. Immediately after the opening on its territory, there were some changes for the better, which corrected some flaws in the technical equipment identified with the first flow of tourists. At the moment, the hotel consists of a large six-story building, which houses all the rooms and some infrastructure facilities, built on an area of ‚Äã‚Äã4000 sq.m.

In the open area, the hotel has an excellent aquazone and garden, which undoubtedly pleases tourists with its flowering.



Location

The hotel is located almost in the center of the resort city of Alanya. This village is considered a fairly quiet area, where you can rarely hear loud music at night. That is why the hotels in this town prefer to go on vacation with young children or elderly couples who do not like the noise.

Oba Time Hotel is located at some distance from the sea shore. In order to get to the beach, tourists need to pass a distance of 300 meters.

The airport from here is located at a very great distance - 135 km. That is why, without fail, tourists who purchase a ticket to the hotel receive a transfer service in her package.

Rooms

In total, Oba Time Hotel 4 * has in its fund 90 apartments, which are very beautifully decorated, taking into account all the requirements for the safety of guests and observing the level of comfort. All of them belong to the same category - "Standard".

Each room has a private bathroom, equipped with plumbing and a hairdryer for drying hair. In the cabinets that are installed here, there is a set of bath accessories that the hotel provides for each guest, as well as large bath towels.

If desired, an electronic safe can be installed in any room to preserve valuables. There is a telephone connected to the international communication line. Guests also have the opportunity to request a minibar in the apartment, which has a small selection of soft non-alcoholic drinks. The use of these facilities is paid and is paid daily.



Room Interior

The description of rooms at Oba Time Hotel often says that they are all made in the same style - classic. The decoration is dominated by material such as wood, and dark wood. As for the color scheme of the interior, it is represented by a beautiful combination of white and brown shades.

There is not much furniture. A large French bed, made according to European standards, is installed as a sleeping place. In some rooms it has been replaced with two single beds. There are small cabinets near the beds, and on the other side there is a large wardrobe for personal items. If desired, guests have the opportunity to watch satellite broadcasts on the plasma TV, which is available here.

As for the decor, there are several paintings on the walls of the apartments, and also several wall lamps are installed here that visually make the interior of the room more expensive. In their reviews of the Oba Time Hotel, tourists often note that the windows are especially elegant due to the large windows covered with dense and lightweight textiles. They offer stunning views of the sea, the garden and the picturesque surroundings.

The apartments have an individual air conditioning system, which allows you to maintain the air temperature in the room at the optimum level. The rooms also have private balconies, on which there are a couple of small sun loungers and a table for tea - here you can have a great rest together, admiring the views of the sea, garden and aquazone located on the hotel premises.



Conditions for children

Even the guests themselves, leaving comments, note that the Oba Time Hotel 4 * is not too well designed for living with children, especially with small ones. They determine this by the fact that in this place for rest there are practically no conditions for this category of guests. All that is offered here is small cots, which can be installed at the request of the parents. In the open area for them there is a pool, which is located near the main one. In catering establishments, children can be accommodated in special comfortable chairs.

Nutrition

A large number of reviews about Oba Time Hotel say that the food provided is very tasty, and the dishes are quite unusual. Guests note that here you can visit a large restaurant, where you will certainly be pleased with dishes prepared in the best traditions of Turkish and European cuisines. A buffet is served here every day, serving nutritious and unusual foods.

Guests also note that the hotel has two bars, one of which is located by the pool, and the second - in the main building. Tourists in the comments about the rest often tell that in the bar near the pool the menu places a lot of emphasis on soft drinks and cocktails that can amaze with their variety, and the lobby bar serves a lot of snacks and desserts.



Aquazone

Guests often note that most of the time they spent at Oba Time Hotel was near the pool and on the beach. The pool here is located in an open area and gathers fans of sunbathing and water procedures. There is an excellent water purification system, thanks to which the niche filling is always crystal clear. As for its temperature, guests can enjoy warm water, however, its heating occurs under the influence of sunlight.

Near the pool there is a large sun terrace where tourists can sunbathe on the comfortable large sun loungers that are located under beach umbrellas.



Beach

The coastline is located at some distance from the hotel (300 meters), but some of it is the property of Oba Time Hotel (Turkey). Tourists claim that the beach area is always clean, it is covered with medium-sized pebbles and fine warm sand.

Arriving here, guests can sit on huge sun loungers, under umbrellas that are able to shelter from direct sunlight. If desired, hotel guests can use air mattresses and bath towels, which are issued at a special point on the beach, for a deposit of $ 10.

Sport

Sports life at Oba Time Hotel (Alanya) is in full swing. In particular, this is due to the presence on the territory of the hotel a large gym equipped with exercise machines for working on different muscle groups. In the open area, tourists have the opportunity to play basketball, soccer, volleyball, for which a small playground is organized. Equipment for games can always be taken at a separate point, which is available at the hotel.

Tourists who are well versed in the area, often in their reviews are advised to go to sports complexes that are located in the neighborhood. They have several tennis courts, golf courses, and an equestrian club.

SPA

The hotel has a large spa area, the main element of which is the thermal zone, located on the ground floor of the complex. It consists of a sauna and hammam, where parmeisters can offer their services. As tourists say, visiting the sauna is paid, and you can visit the hammam at any time and on a free basis.

In a separate room located next door, you can enjoy a massage, but all the services provided here are paid.



Entertainment

Oba Time Hotel has practically no noisy entertainment, as the animation team does not work here, as happens in other hotels in Turkey. However, so that guests do not get bored, the administration organizes night discos here three times a week, which gather a large number of guests at the venues. At this time, incendiary hits of foreign pop performed by a local DJ play here.

On the ground floor of the building there is a tour desk where you can book a tour of the sights of Alanya and neighboring settlements. Vacationers often recommend choosing and paying for sightseeing trips or hikes on the spot, since in this case they are much cheaper than if purchased from a tour operator in their native country.

Service

Oba Time Hotel provides a wide range of services for guests, some of which are paid. One of the most popular among them is the laundry and dry cleaning services for washing clothes and linen, and you can also iron your things in a separate order.

On the ground floor of the main complex there is a beauty salon where you can have a haircut, do manicure or visit cosmetic procedures (peeling, applying masks, body wraps, etc.). Next to him is the doctor‚Äôs office, which can be called upon request. If necessary, the doctor himself can visit the tourist in his room. Doctor services at the hotel are paid, but you can use them throughout the day.

Most of the tourists in their reviews note that they purchase all souvenirs for relatives, friends and acquaintances in a store, which is also located in the hotel building. There are many traditional Turkish products that can definitely surprise people.

Car and bike rental are very popular here. Many hotel guests rent such vehicles in order to visit local attractions on their own, as well as explore the surroundings. By the way, if a guest takes a car for short-term use, then for her a free parking space is provided in a guarded parking lot.



Prices

The cost of a trip to Oba Time Hotel can be different - it all depends on where the flight will be from. According to statistics, two residents of Moscow can pay for it about 60,000 rubles in case of planning a weekly stay in the hotel apartments. Tour operators include in this price a standard package of services, which includes not only a round-trip flight with accommodation, but also a transfer from the airport and back. According to tourists, the hotel provides a transfer at a fairly high level - a comfortable car with air conditioning takes the guests, which quickly delivers the guest to the destination.
