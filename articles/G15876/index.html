Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * (Egypt, Hurghada): description of rooms, service, reviews

Egypt is a country of deserts, rich cultural heritage and snow-white beaches. Tourists are welcome here throughout the year, so for travelers from the CIS countries there is no place to stay better and cheaper. Even a middle-class person can stay in a luxury hotel, because they offer accommodation at fairly reasonable prices. However, not all five-star complexes are equally good. For example, Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * hotel is popular among them. But is it worth it to stay in it? We will try to answer this question by considering the location of the hotel, its rooms, as well as the proposed infrastructure and leisure options.

Features of rest in Hurghada

Hurghada in Egypt deservedly bears the title of the busiest and most popular resort in the country. It was here that large respectable hotels first appeared, which are so loved by tourists from many countries. This resort town is located on the Red Sea coast, stretching along the coast for almost 40 km. Hurghada is the main diving center in Egypt. Moreover, we can say that the resort is focused on absolutely all categories of tourists. For young people in the central part there is a huge number of night clubs and bars. Amusement parks have been built for families with children, there are entire quiet neighborhoods and beaches where you can relax in a relaxed atmosphere. Fans of studying the culture of Egypt will surely like the old city. In addition, this tourist center is located near the famous Valley of the Kings with the pyramids and sphinxes.

It is still impossible to get to Hurghada in Egypt directly from Russia, so the flow of compatriot tourists has fallen dramatically in recent years. However, already in February 2018 it is expected that the resort will again accept travelers. Choosing a hotel in Hurghada, you should pay attention to the area. For example, in the old city nightlife is constantly seething, so tourists with children should not stop here. For them, New Hurghada, fully landscaped for a comfortable stay, is suitable. At the same time, hotels here are more often targeted at tourists who prefer to spend time precisely on the territory of the hotel, therefore they have a developed infrastructure and a wide selection of leisure options.

Where exactly is the hotel located?

Thus, Hurghada is a huge resort city, divided into several areas. The hotel we are describing is located in one of the best. Sahl Hasheesh is a world-class modern resort offering a high-quality holiday. Its development is not yet fully completed, but several luxury hotels have already been opened, stretching along the pristine coast of the Red Sea. Residential neighborhoods are located nearby, and real estate is bought exclusively by wealthy people. The hotel was built on the beach, so it has its own vast stretch of sandy beach. To reach it, tourists need to overcome several tens of meters. Within walking distance from the complex is a huge supermarket and pizzeria. 5 km is the eastern market, where you can inexpensively buy souvenirs, jewelry, spices, clothes.

Another advantage of a holiday in Sahl Hasheesh is its proximity to the international airport, which is located in Hurghada. The distance to it is only 18 km, so tourists will spend about one hour on the way after the flight. In this case, all hotel guests are invited to purchase a transfer when buying a ticket. If you arrange it, then tourists will be delivered to the hotel by comfortable bus in the shortest possible time.

General Hotel Information

Description Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * is a must-start with general information. This hotel is considered a fairly large and populated place to stay. In total, it offers tourists 851 rooms, so in the hot season almost 2,000 people can simultaneously live on its territory. However, overcrowding is not felt due to the huge area that is reserved for the complex. A large part of it is occupied by a tropical garden - palm trees grow everywhere, the facades of buildings are decorated with lush vegetation. The hotel itself looks very respectable, resembling an oriental castle with its appearance.

The main building of the complex is a cascade building, the height of which can vary from two to four floors. Next to it is another similar structure. And along the perimeter of the territory there are additionally three two-story villas. The construction of the hotel was completed in 2007, so now renovation of rooms is being carried out in stages. It is worth noting that pets are not allowed in the rooms. For the convenience of tourists from the CIS countries, Russian-speaking employees are always at the reception, but most of the staff speaks English.

Complex Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 *: room types

The number of rooms includes several categories, but they all differ in rich decoration and impressive size. For the convenience of guests, separate apartments for large families or friendly companies are separately allocated. Non-smoking rooms can also be accommodated. All living quarters, of course, are carefully cleaned by the hotel staff three times a week. At the same interval, they change sheets and towels.

Consider in more detail the types of rooms presented in this hotel:

  • Single apartments. They consist of a living room, in which there is a sleeping and living area, a bathtub and a balcony. On the ground floor it is replaced by an open terrace. In addition to an adult, a child can be populated. Area - 36 square meters. This room type offers rooms with sea, garden or pool views.
  • Double rooms. Also consist of a bedroom, a balcony and a bathroom. Choose from apartments with single or double beds. Area - 46 square meters. m. There are rooms with side views of the sea, as well as the garden or pool.
  • Triple rooms are almost the same as double rooms, but are additionally equipped with a single bed. And they have a slightly larger area - 49 square meters. m
  • Junior Suite - a spacious room. The room is divided into a dining, living room and sleeping area. Designed to accommodate two adults and a child. There is an open balcony with a dining set. Area 75 sq. M. m

Deluxe - double apartments, consisting of a bedroom, a bathroom with a jacuzzi and a spacious furnished balcony. Its area is 60 square meters. m

What facilities are there in the rooms?

Complex Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * is a hotel for a respectable vacation, so the rooms here are equipped with a maximum range of amenities. Of course, in addition to quality furniture, tourists can use modern appliances, such as plasma TVs. A huge number of satellite channels are connected to it, among which there are Russian-speaking ones. For a comfortable sleep in hot weather, the rooms are equipped with individual air conditioners, which are equipped with remotes for adjusting the temperature. The apartments also have an electric kettle, coffee maker and a set for making hot drinks. In the bathroom you can find towels not only for the bathroom, but also for the beach and pool. Hygiene products are provided free of charge: shampoo, shower gel, toothbrush, soap.

An electronic safe is provided for storing valuables. All rooms have wireless internet connection, you do not need to pay extra. Some apartments have a private fireplace. A minibar with or without filling is available on request.

Service in Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 *

Developed infrastructure is another advantage of this hotel. A huge number of infrastructure facilities have been built on its territory, whose services help to make the rest as comfortable as possible. We list the main ones:

  • laundry and dry cleaning;
  • 7 conference rooms equipped with the most modern equipment;
  • ATM;
  • several shops with clothes, souvenirs and jewelry;
  • doctor's office;
  • barbershop;
  • free parking;
  • 24-hour reception, where you can exchange currency, order a taxi or buy a bus ticket.

More about hotel catering

Meals at Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * are provided on an all-inclusive basis. Accordingly, the price includes all meals, including additional snacks in the cafes and bars of the hotel. Guests are provided with free local alcoholic drinks, ice cream, fruits and vegetables, desserts. In addition to the main restaurant, there are catering establishments on the territory of the complex serving Japanese, Italian, Turkish, Mexican and even Russian dishes. Their visit is provided for a fee and by appointment. The hotel also has several bars, including on the beach, in the main lobby and by the pool. There is also a separate night bar serving drinks during discos.

Does the hotel have a beach and a pool?

Yes, the hotel has its own stretch of coast. The beach at Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * is sandy. For tourists there are sun loungers and parasols. Towels and mattresses are also provided free of charge. Corals are located near the shore, so you can go diving, but you can only swim in special shoes.

The outdoor pool is filled with fresh water and is additionally equipped with slides. Six of them are intended for adults, and another four - for children. Next to it is traditionally located a sunbathing area. The hotel also has a heated indoor pool, which is open during the winter season.

What else can you entertain yourself in the hotel?

Despite the fact that Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * focuses on beach vacations, the complex has many other leisure options. So, the hotel has:

  • Sauna and Jacuzzi
  • aerobics lessons;

  • massage room;
  • playgrounds for playing bocce, water polo, beach volleyball, basketball;

  • Internet cafe.

Is it possible to come to the hotel with children?

The Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * complex is perfect for families with small children. They are given good discounts on accommodation (for guests under 12 years old). A cot is provided in the room, and the restaurant has a separate children's corner for the convenience of feeding. The hotel has 2 shallow pools for babies with water slides. For a fee, you can visit these attractions.

Positive hotel reviews

The guests about the rest in Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh Beach Resort 5 * in the reviews indicate mostly positive ratings, although they note that the hotel also has drawbacks. As a rule, they believe that the complex corresponds to the stated stardom, citing the following advantages as evidence:

  • very friendly staff, while not only the receptionists are friendly, but also the cleaners, porters, waiters in restaurants;
  • sometimes hotel workers bring free fruit to the room, they also change linen in a timely manner, make figurines of flowers and animals from them;
  • in the rooms and on the territory of the hotel is constantly maintained perfect cleanliness;
  • the hotel's beach has a convenient entrance to the sea, the water in it is crystal clear without jellyfish and traces of motor boats;
  • A wonderful entertainment program for children, which is conducted by very sensitive administrators.

Hotel shortcomings marked by guests

The disadvantages of the complex, according to the guests, are unlikely to greatly spoil the impression of a vacation, but it is still worth knowing about them. They recommend that hotel owners pay attention to the following service deficiencies:

  • low internet speed, especially at rush hour;
  • food is not very diverse, which is usually inherent in five-star hotels;
  • a large number of local residents have a rest in the complex, who do not always behave quietly and decently;
  • a dirty pool, where you can often see floating food or plastic dishes;
  • long lines in the main restaurant of the hotel, at rush hour there are not enough tables for all guests.

Draw conclusions

Should I recommend the Dessole Resorts & Hotels? Most likely, yes, because it is perfect for a respectable vacation with friends or family. On its territory, developed infrastructure, a large selection of entertainment, catering establishments with a varied menu are presented. In addition, tourists in their reviews definitely recommend coming here, believing that the quality of service justifies the high cost of living.
