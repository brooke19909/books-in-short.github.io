Affordable holidays in Spain

If you choose the most welcoming and warm of all European countries, then this, of course, will be Spain - a favorite vacation spot for families with children, honeymooners, history buffs and beach lovers . Its gentle sun and the purest Mediterranean Sea with adjacent sandy beaches has become an excellent alternative to a vacation in Turkey and Egypt. Each time you come here, you can rediscover Spain.

In the spring, the honeymoon season begins, and the country turns for several weeks into the abode of bright and tender feelings of the newly made spouses. They choose holidays in Spain, because here you can spend time at the sea and still visit various architectural monuments and attend purely Spanish events, for example, bullfighting. In Madrid, you must visit the Gothic Cathedral of Sagrada Familia, built by the architect Antonio Gaudi in the 15th century. In this city you can not get past its port, the Gothic quarter, the Del Prado Museum, the Palacio Real and the city square. Although Madrid is filled with historically important objects, and it carries a huge centuries-old history, nevertheless, it is a developed city with its own infrastructure. Since rest in Spain is still European, here in all hotels and public places European standards of service quality are observed.

Connoisseurs of club life will prefer to settle on the island of Ibiza. It is he who is considered the birthplace of all electronic musical culture. What is played in the clubs of Ibiza is considered the highest quality music. This island sets the benchmark for partying around the world. There is a huge number of night clubs, hotels, the main emphasis is on young people and related entertainment. Such a holiday in Spain will appeal to lovers to lie in the sun. The island is located in the Mediterranean Sea and is rich in various types of beaches.

Another important city in this sunny and hospitable country is Barcelona. This is a big metropolis where, in spite of its development, the truly Spanish culture with its architectural monuments is carefully preserved.
