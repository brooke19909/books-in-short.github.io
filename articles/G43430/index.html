The cheapest hotels in Santorini, Greece

In the Aegean Sea there is a beautiful island with the beautiful name Santorini. It enters the Cyclades archipelago (Greece). Locals believe that the island is a lost Atlantis. The crowded cities coexist next to an extinct volcano, amazing black sand lies on the beaches, and legends want amazing sunsets on this island. A real lost paradise! Many tourists deny themselves this pleasure, mistakenly thinking that holidays on the islands can be very expensive. This is not entirely true. In this article we have selected the cheapest hotels in Santorini (Greece). At the same time, you should not imagine sloppy motels on the side of the highway with the word ‚Äúcheap‚Äù. These are pretty comfortable, albeit small, hotels. And most importantly - you do not have to worry about how much it will all cost.

"Stelios Place"

The most fun and lively hotels in Santorini (Greece) are located on Perissa Beach. This beach is the epicenter of the island's nightlife, and it is here that the Stelios Place Hotel is located. This budget option is just right for young people. Prices at the hotel start at 30 euros and above. Every evening on the seashore there are noisy parties with live music and dancing.

Hotel "Apanemo"

Among the inexpensive hotels in Santorini, "Apanemo" is considered the undisputed leader. This cheap hotel offers its guests amazing views of the Caldera volcano, as well as a huge three-level pool. The best hotels of Santorini (Greece) have a view from the window on the volcano. Surprisingly, a fashionable sea view does not suit tourists and is not very popular in this place. A volcano is what distinguishes this island from thousands of others in the Aegean Sea. Off-season hotel prices start at 80 euros, and at the height of the tourist season, from 140 euros.

Hotel "Manos Villas"

If you choose Greece, Santorini island , hotels at the lowest prices will help to save your budget. One of these is the Minos Villas Hotel. This new and small hotel has a large swimming pool, as well as friendly and helpful staff. The hotel has a total of 25 rooms, 12 of which are equipped with kitchenettes. This is ideal for a complete family vacation in a paradise such as Greece, Santorini.

Oceanis Beach Hotel

This small hotel is located near the airport. The hotel boasts a quiet and peaceful environment. Territory "Oceanis Beach", although small, but well-groomed. There are 2 pools, one of which is for children. Under the all-inclusive promotion, three meals a day are expected. Despite the lack of animation, vacation can brighten up excursions around the island or entertainment on the beach.



Sky of Thira

The name of the hotel can be translated as "sky of Tire." Thira is another name for such an amazing place as Greece, the island of Santorini. Economy class hotels here are small in size and have a cozy, one might say, homely atmosphere. Sky of Thira does not have a view of the volcano, but it can offer you comfortable rooms at low prices. Each room has air conditioning and a balcony. There is a small pool with a corresponding bar on site. Another advantage of the hotel is that they are open year-round. And in the offseason, you can request a significant discount.



Conclusion

Thus, the cheapest hotels in Santorini (Greece) are quite reasonable price (from 30 euros per day). Many of them have their own pools and even bars.
