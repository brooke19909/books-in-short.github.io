Gloucester Cathedral - the magic corner of Great Britain

Gloucester Cathedral is an 11th-century English cathedral church , which is one of the masterpieces of Gothic architecture throughout the world. Before the church here in 681, the royal abbey of Northumbria was located. This magnificent cathedral, in a slightly embellished form, can be seen in the famous Harry Potter saga: filming of school days of young magicians and sorceresses took place here.





History and architecture of the temple

Gloucester Cathedral also has an official name - the Cathedral Church of St. Peter and the Holy One Trinity. The temple was founded in the first century AD, but acquired its cathedral status only in the 16th century. The building has quite impressive dimensions: it is 130 meters long, 44 meters wide, and the height of the tower located in the center reaches 79 meters.

The architectural elements of the church harmoniously combine the Norman trend, as well as signs of later styles, including Gothic. The entrance to the temple from the south is made in the Gothic style, and the choirs of the church are Gothic elements superimposed on the Norman style.





After modernization, the Gloucester Cathedral acquired unique features unique to it. At the end of the 19th century, some architectural changes took place in connection with the restoration of the roof and tower of the church. The restoration was carried out under the direction of George Gilbert Scott.





Sights of the Gloucester Cathedral

Gloucester Cathedral (Great Britain) is one of the most ancient temples in the kingdom. Noteworthy sights of the temple are rightfully a monument to Ozric, King of Northumbria, the tomb of King Edward II, buried in the walls of the abbey church, and huge stained-glass windows that amaze with their splendor.





On one of them you can see an image of a golf game dating back to 1350, as well as a carved picture of a medieval ball game. On painted stained-glass windows you can see scenes from the life of commoners and crowned persons, the reception of guests, the coronation of rulers, as well as other events typical of medieval England.





The most beautiful church in England and the Harry Potter movie

The Gloucester Cathedral (Hogwarts in the world famous film about wizards and wizards "Harry Potter") was rented for some time to shoot some scenes of the film, and the rent cost the producers a tidy sum (one day of shooting cost $ 12 thousand). In the temple, moments of the distribution of future wizards by faculty were filmed. Also, scenes of Christmas dinner and Halloween were shot here. Hogwarts School of Magic always appears differently, and young heroes have walked more than once along its corridors (cathedral galleries).





In the credits after the film, the Royal Gloucester School is also indicated, and its pupils even took part in mass scenes. Gloucester Cathedral in England fits perfectly into the magical atmosphere of magic and sorcery. Some parishioners and members of the religious community did not want the film about sorcerers and witches to be shot in the sacred walls, but the clergy came to the conclusion that, on the basis of the film, the good resists evil, and this idea does not go beyond the Anglican religion.





Gloucester Cathedral - the magic corner of England

Thanks to the special atmosphere, during the stay in the walls of the temple an unusual feeling arises that you are in a sacred place where mystery is everywhere and smells like a fairy tale and notes of magic. The amazing Gloucester Cathedral personifies power and unwaveringness and enthuses with its appearance and makes believing parishioners and curious tourists tremble.

Due to its architectural attractiveness, the temple is very popular among tourists. The stunning Gloucester Cathedral in England very organically combines the interior decoration of various eras, from the ancient Norman trend to the present.





Each year, the cathedral has approximately 331 thousand visitors and parishioners. And many fans of the Harry Potter movie come here to personally witness the majestic and mysterious school of magic and witchcraft at Hogwarts.
