Guest house Victoria, Adler: overview, description and reviews

In the resort town of Adler, you can stay not only in an expensive hotel, but also in the private sector. For a good rest, comfortable conditions and everything you need for a comfortable stay are desirable. There are a lot of guest houses for tourists in Adler. It is worth stopping at three of them with very similar names: "Victoria", "Victoria Plus", "Viva Victoria".



Guest house "Victoria" (Adler)

In Adler, many know the resort town. It is in it that the guest house "Victoria" is located (Adler, Chkalova, 17). From this mini-hotel to the sea - a stone's throw (100 m). It is easily accessible from Sochi, only 20 minutes by shuttle bus. You can walk to the sea on foot, it only takes 5 minutes. Why choose the Victoria Guesthouse (Adler)? The infrastructure is very developed here.

Those wishing to enjoy rare exotic marine life are invited to visit the most famous Oceanarium in Russia. Those who love animals can head to the local dolphinarium for a performance or visit the terrarium. Vacationers will be able to get a boost of vigor in the Amphibius water park.

What about the smallest tourists? There is also a huge selection of different entertainments for them. Guest House Victoria (Adler) is located next to children's playgrounds. Children can be taken to the children's entertainment center. Entertainment programs, children's contests and festivals are held regularly for young visitors.

For those who love cultural rest, a cinema and concert hall with 300 seats is open, various exhibitions are held.

Sports enthusiasts will also not have to get bored - there are tennis courts, an outdoor pool, volleyball and basketball, badminton, and a gym nearby. The tour desk offers an interesting trip. Nearby are pharmacies, telegraph, post office, branch of the Savings Bank. There will be no problems with any purchases either: a supermarket and a gift shop are located nearby. It will not be at all problematic to buy a train ticket or call a taxi, the order service is well established.



House description

Guest house "Victoria" (Adler) has 2-, 3-, 4-room rooms. The hotel offers the following rooms: economy, standard, junior suite, two-room suite with private facilities. All rooms are equipped with a bathroom, in the presence of air conditioning, TV, refrigerator. There are no problems with nutrition. You can cook it yourself in the kitchen or visit the dining room with European and Caucasian dishes. Crockery, an electric kettle, a microwave are at the guests' disposal.

The economy room has single beds, bedside tables, chairs, a wardrobe, a mirror. In the junior suite the same plus a coffee table, the bed can be double, exit to the balcony.

The following services are offered from additional services: free home delivery, barbecue in the yard, green yard, summer houses, parking in front of the building, ironing.

Price-list

Prices for accommodation in Victoria are approximately the following:

  • Economy class - 300-400 rubles.
  • Double standard with a balcony - 500-750 rubles.
  • Triple standard with a balcony - 400-700 rubles.
  • Quadruple standard with a balcony - 400-600 rubles.
  • Junior Suite - 2000-2600 rubles.

Pleasant memories are left after visiting the guest house "Victoria" (Adler). Reviews about him are mostly positive. Many tourists come here not the first year.



Guest house "Victoria Plus" (Adler)

In Adler there is another beautiful mini-hotel "Victoria Plus". It has an outdoor pool, a terrace for walking.

Each room is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV. Tourists can use the fully equipped bathroom.

For those who wish to fry the barbecue, everything is provided for barbecue. You can also have lunch at a cafe, which there are several nearby.

To be in the center of all events, vacationers spent Wi-Fi. You can safely park your car in the free parking lot.

Getting to Victoria Plus is very easy. From the international airport in Sochi to this guest house lies the path of only 14 km, from where they can deliver for free, you only need to request.

The hotel rooms can be rented as follows:

  • Suite
  • 3-seater;
  • Double with separate beds.

The approximate price for accommodation in the complex "Victoria Plus" starts from 800 rubles. per day.



One of the best accommodations in Adler

250 meters from the Black Sea is the guest house "Viva Victoria" (Adler). Reviews about it tourists leave only positive. But what else! Here you can swim in the outdoor pool, use free Wi-Fi, relax in the green area.

The rooms in the hotel are very comfortable, some of them have a balcony. Each room is equipped with the necessary furniture, air conditioning, TV, refrigerator. A bar and a small shop where you can purchase the necessary products are open in the Viva Victoria zone.

Everything for a comfortable stay

Getting to the guest house is easy. It is just 3 km from the local train station.

"Viva Victoria" - a five-story building, rooms overlook the street and the courtyard. On the roof of the building there is a platform where you can admire the beauties of the city and just sunbathe. In total, the hotel has 30 rooms for 2-3 people.

What sights are nearby? Bestuzhev Park is a 15-minute walk from the guest house. Very close to the shopping center "Mandarin". Here you can visit outlets, sit in a cozy cafe, swim in the pool, have fun in a nightclub, enjoy the singing fountains and a fantastic laser show.
