Infrastructure

Festival Le Jardin Resort is a single building of five floors and several facilities located on the territory. In the building on the ground floor there is a reception where tourists can leave their bags and suitcases in a storage room, take a safe, call a doctor, taxi, take things to the laundry or dry cleaning, buy sightseeing tours, use the fax, exchange currency, solve all current issues.

In the lobby, near the reception, there is a lobby bar, comfortable armchairs, tables are installed, shops with cosmetics, clothes, souvenirs, beach accessories are working, there is an ATM. For business people combining leisure with work, the hotel offers to use a well-equipped conference room, which, if necessary, can easily be transformed into a banquet room.

The hotel has been operating since 2008, in 2015, innovations began to be carried out here. However, in those rooms where the reconstruction has already taken place, the decor and condition of the plumbing are wonderful. The design of the rooms is quite simple - nothing more. The view from the windows can be on the sea and the territory or on the outbuildings. Many tourists report that for the best room at the reception require a surcharge.

Nutrition

As for the food at Festival Le Jardin Resort 5 *, the reviews are also mixed. It seems to many people that there is no variety of dishes, especially few fruits. The quality of products is also not everyone likes. Another claim to long lines during breakfast, lunch and dinner. They are held on the basis of the "buffet", but some dishes are given in portions.





Festival Le Jardin Resort has a central restaurant with an outdoor terrace to cater for its guests. The breakfast menu includes cereals, milk, coffee, tea, juices (from packages), scrambled eggs or scrambled eggs, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãsausages and cheeses, salads, side dishes, pastries, pancakes. For lunch, meat dishes (chicken, beef), fish, side dishes (potatoes, pasta, rice), fresh and stewed vegetables, fruits in the form of salad, desserts, ice cream are offered. The dinner menu is about the same.

In addition to the central one, Festival Le Jardin Resort has a restaurant with an a la carte system (by appointment). It is called "Mama Mia" and offers Italian cuisine.

The hotel also has several bars - in the lobby, on the beach, by the pools, on site. On the beach, tourists can not only take drinks, but also have a snack. The bar is open around the clock in the lobby, but the all-inclusive concept here, as in the entire hotel, is valid from 10 a.m. to midnight. Drinks are given out only in plastic cups, regardless of whether it is water, beer or a cocktail.

Service for children

Festival Le Jardin Resort for young guests has a children's pool on the hotel premises and on the beach, a playground with slides and swings, a Bambi mini-club with a games room where educators spend interesting events with children. Parents can also order babysitting services. The rooms have the opportunity to request a cot upon request, in the restaurant - use highchairs. The menu in the hotel is made without taking into account the nutrition of kids, but there is always something to choose from. True, for the smallest (up to a year or a little older) special dishes are not provided.

Animation for kids is as interesting as for adults. It includes games, all kinds of activities, and in the evening a mini-disco.





Sea

Literally a minute is required for guests to walk to the beach from the Festival Le Jardin Resort 5 * hotel. Hurghada is famous for its coastline and wonderful sea, but in this case, a beach holiday can upset. The coating here looks like a sand, but in fact it is a small crumb. Stony steps without railings lead into the water. Depth, where there is the opportunity to swim, begins about 700 meters from the coast. Another unpleasant feature of this section of the coast is the ebbs and flows. During the latter, swimming in the sea is possible only after lunch.

The bottom of the coast is covered with uncleared corals, which can be injured, there is also a risk of stepping on a sea urchin, so it is advisable to have special shoes. There is no pier or pontoon at this beach; entry into the sea is only from the coast. Exotic fish and other inhabitants of the underwater kingdom are present, but there are not so many of them as in other areas of the Red Sea.





Leisure outside

Unfortunately, guests of Festival Le Jardin Resort will not be able to take exciting walks. Egypt is an interesting country with many attractions and unusual color, but there are plenty of desert sites here. Just such landscapes so far can be contemplated near the hotel. To diversify your vacation, as well as for shopping you need to go to Hurghada. To do this, you can use buses or taxis, which the hotel kindly provides. The second option is to rent a car. But the hotel guests have a wonderful opportunity to visit the El Gouna area, which can be reached in 5 minutes. This is real Venice in the desert - with blue canals, like toy houses and bridges, funny cars that move around the area, and boats cruising along the canals. Someone may be interested in a brewery, someone in the Kafr district, styled in antiquity, and someone in supermarkets, discos, a huge aquarium or the best golf club in the country.

In addition to this fabulous place, tourists are offered a number of classic excursions - in Hurghada, Cairo, Luxor, Jerusalem, Paradise Island (a yacht with a glass bottom), karting in the desert.
