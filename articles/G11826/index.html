LCD "Arkazhskaya Sloboda" (Veliky Novgorod): description, photos, reviews of apartment owners

High-quality new housing for those who dream of warmth, comfort and coziness is the residential complex "Arkazhskaya Sloboda" under construction. During its construction, modern technologies are applied that make the new building an ideal place of life for a young family.





Location

In a beautiful old city, calm as the water of Russian rivers, solid and knowing its worth, since it keeps the memory of many centuries, a residential complex is being completed a kilometer from its historic center. Its location will allow its residents to work within the city. And after a hard day, they will enjoy the cleanest air in the new buildings of the Arkazhskaya Sloboda residential complex.

There is a convenient transport interchange, with a lot of public transport, an area with excellent infrastructure. That is why the inhabitants of Veliky Novgorod so impatiently (probably because for a long time - since 2011) are waiting for the completion of construction. Town

It was here, in Veliky Novgorod, that the first birch bark letters were found, the first books in Russia were written here, merchants from the most distant countries came here to a huge bargain, taking with them to different corners of the earth incredible stories about the greatness of cities and settlements, about rich stone temples Novgorod, about the strong people of the Russian people. Dolgostroy

This residential complex was planned to be completely commissioned in 2011, but the constant lack of funds not only slowed down, but also stopped the construction several times. So, from 2012 to 2014, work on the site was not carried out. In May 2014, the first phase of the buildings was undertaken by a new developer, Concord LLC. However, he did not succeed right away, the deadlines were postponed many times, until the summer of 2015.

Finally, Sberbank allocated additional funds, after which the first phase was completed at the end of 2016, and the full completion of construction is planned for 2017. This is a difficult story survived "Arkazhskaya Sloboda". Veliky Novgorod is certainly great, but today this city cannot be called rich. That is why the dreams of its inhabitants have been fulfilled for so long.





Start

The implementation of this project began in the distant 2009, after the allocation of forty hectares of land at the site of the former airport. It was then that the Novgorodians began to wait for the delivery of the first ten three-story houses with attics - five hundred forty-four apartments were promised to the residents of the city, plus more than two thousand square meters of infrastructure were commercial premises. The dates were constantly postponed: from 2011 to the spring of 2012, then to the end of this year, then the construction stopped altogether. The facility has been frozen for more than two years.

In this state, apartments in the Arkazh Sloboda could not be sold. Veliky Novgorod was afraid to invest its last funds in this project, and Sberbank had to persuade for a very long time. Therefore, developers even avoided conversations with journalists - there was not a single positive moment with which to reassure the population. And when the construction was resumed, the developers did not reassure people, because there was no one hundred percent guarantee that the dates would not have to be postponed again. Many buyers are so imbued with this honesty that they took back their money.





Road

The main stumbling block, and for quite some time, was the unfinished road connecting the Yuryevskoye Highway with the Arkazhskaya Sloboda residential complex. Veliky Novgorod, where the main road construction department is located, promised developers not only to design it, but also to build it. However, for the private land that was to be pre-purchased, the construction of the road has long been an impossible task, since the price is too high - fifteen million rubles per hectare.

In total, it was necessary only seven and a half million, but in order to spend them, it took a lot of time, in 2013 they were only laid down in the budget. The existing road is too bad, and although it was difficult to drive along it, four almost finished buildings were not to be surrendered.
