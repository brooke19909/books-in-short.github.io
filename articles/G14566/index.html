Bravis tablet: model review, reviews

Tablets have recently gained great popularity. They occupy an intermediate place between laptops and phones. It should be noted that they can be used as a weak PC - for typing or other manipulations. Also, many modern models are able to connect to cell towers, and this allows calls.

It is not surprising that tablets are now very widespread. This wonderful technique has appeared relatively recently on the gadget market. Large companies are constantly competing among themselves, adding new and interesting features. Often, when people need budget options, buyers pay attention to inexpensive and less popular brands. As a rule, they are Chinese firms. They are able to create inexpensive, but surprisingly high-quality analogues of many popular gadgets. One such representative is the Bravis. Without it, it is already difficult to imagine the current assortment.

A special place in the line of devices from different companies is occupied by Bravis tablets. Reviews about them are more positive and neutral than negative. Let's take a closer look at some models of this brand.



Bravis NP 725

The screen of this tablet is 7 inches. The operating system that is installed in it is Android. The model is equipped with graphic acceleration, this allows you to not hang in the most unexpected moments. There are modules 3G, bluetooth, Wi-Fi. The Bravis NP 725 tablet has two cameras: the main one at 2 MP and the front one at 0.3 MP. Sold in black and white. Total weight - 610 grams. Battery capacity 2500 mAh. The manufacturer gives a guarantee for one year. The internal memory is small, there is only 510 MB.

The tablet is perfect for work and study. You can freely view any photos on it, moreover, the device has excellent color rendition.



Bravis np 101

Another good Bravis tablet is the NP 101. The diagonal is 10 inches. Screen resolution allows you to view in good quality video and photos. Color rendering is average. The battery is quite capacious - 5 thousand mAh. This is enough to charge it once every 1.5 days. Sold in black. It weighs a little, only 650 grams. It is not recommended to use it as a camera, since the rear and front cameras do not differ in high rates. It is possible to install a USB flash drive up to 32 GB in the described Bravis tablet.

Reviews about the device are not bad. Of course, it does not differ in high power and is most often used as a temporary gadget. However, the tablet copes with all its functions perfectly, does not slow down and does not fail at the most crucial moments.



Bravis NP 71

Bravis NP 71 is a Bravis tablet that has been in demand for a long time. Seven-inch device, perfect for work purposes. It is also often bought as a phone. The battery is not very capacious - 2100 mAh. The front camera is 0.3 MP.

According to people's reviews, you can understand that this Bravis tablet is great for watching news, making calls, communicating on social networks. Hoping that he will pull the game is not worth it. The option is quite good, but only for those who need it only as a means of obtaining information. You can watch pictures and videos on the tablet; it has good color rendition and screen size.
