Choosing the best headphones for phones

Surely many of us wondered what kind of headphones to choose for our mobile device. To do this, you must have certain knowledge. First you need to decide how much you are willing to spend, because the headphones for phones vary greatly in price.





We do not recommend purchasing cheap devices. They are inexpensive, but any troubles can be expected from them: extraneous noise and annoying hiss, crackles and intermittent shutdowns. When choosing headphones for phones, be sure to try them on. Cheap devices tend to rub your ears or drop out of them unexpectedly. This is very distracting from listening to your favorite music and spoils the whole impression of the process.

We invest or impose?

You also need to decide on what device you need headphones for: for a mobile phone or flash player. Of course, there are no global design differences in them. Just massive headphones will look silly on the background of a small cell phone - in this case it is more appropriate to choose neat earbuds. They are compact, so you can take them with you everywhere. By the way, while listening to music, the low weight of the headphones will not cause you any discomfort.

Moreover, you simply forget about them, completely immersed in the sound of your favorite tunes.

However, none of the in-ear headphones will provide the level of sound produced by overhead devices. They are represented by two types: closed and open. The former stand out for their excellent durability and perfect fit to the ears, which completely eliminates all third-party sounds. This is very important, especially if music is not just a way to spend time or an easy hobby for you. Headphones for open phones differ in less fit to the ears, and therefore, more noise transmission from the outside. This, on the one hand, creates a sound more realistic than with the closed type, but on the other hand it interferes, and sometimes frankly annoys everyone around.

Device connection

Headphones for phones differ in the type of transmission of the audio signal to wired and wireless. The advantage of devices that transmit sound through wires is the excellent sound quality. And the minus is the stiffness of movements in connection with the presence of wires. Wireless headphones for a Samsung phone or another manufacturer allow its owner not to be constrained in movements. However, the sound in them is much worse than that of wired opponents. By the way, they cost a lot more. The signal to the headphones for the Nokia phone and for models of other manufacturers is transmitted using special infrared radiation.



Convenience and comfort

They most often become decisive factors when choosing headphones, because devices that fall out of your ears will not give you any pleasure. If you prefer volume over- ear headphones more, then a number of nuances should be taken into account. This is their weight and size (because of which you have to say goodbye to the hairdo). Therefore, once again weigh the pros and cons.
