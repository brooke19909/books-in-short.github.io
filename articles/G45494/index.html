London clubs: what to give preference to

London is deservedly called the capital of the world in various aspects: fashion, shopping, attractions, nightlife. A lot of people are attracted to the city by evening shows. London nightclubs are represented by a variety of the most diverse institutions. They are able to satisfy every taste, give an adrenaline rush and introduce new people.





Ministry of Sound

The best clubs in London - a wide variety of institutions. One of the most sought after is the Ministry of Sound. It features a modern sound system, an impressive dance floor and renowned DJs working daily. All this attracts a lot of people to the institution.

The club was first opened in the distant 1991 year. Then he became a kind of innovation in the field of night entertainment. Thousands of tourists came here to enjoy house music, have a great time and meet like-minded people.

Quickly enough, the Ministry of Sound became one of the most popular institutions in the capital. This led to the organization of a network of similar clubs around the world. In addition, clothing companies and a recording studio were created.





Storm

London clubs are also represented by an interesting institution called Storm. It is located near Leicester Square, between the metro station of the same name and Piccadilly. In other words, finding a club is easy enough.

Now lovers of night parties gather here. Favorite DJs for visitors are Ricky "Magic" Martin and Lady Spirit. It is interesting that most people drop in a club not only to enjoy music, but also to listen to modern humor. Standup gum nights are held every Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday and Friday. Here you can meet legendary comedians, for example Tim Vine or Lee Mack. Frequent guests of the institution are the heroes from the sitcom Not going out.

Those who want to enjoy the music should know that the most sought after destinations are reggae, funky house, hip hop and R&B.





Heaven

London clubs are distinguished by a democratic attitude towards people of any preferences, as well as the availability of high-quality live music. One of the best establishments, which displays all the advantages of night shows in the capital, is Heaven. The club is constantly full of people. Any public is welcome here.

The club is very popular in many respects thanks to such legendary musicians as Ellie Goulding, Adam Lambert, as well as members of the Drums. Now inside you can often meet many other celebrities, and if you are very lucky, even chat with them.

The atmosphere of the club is rather unusual and is an organization of many different halls. Thanks to this, each person can find an atmosphere to their taste: comfort and privacy or fun and excitement. Those who come to the club to dance should choose one of the many dance floors. The establishment has a friendly atmosphere that attracts many people here.





Ronnie scott's

When choosing where to go in London, you should take a closer look at Ronnie Scott's. It should be understood that this is not a nightclub in the truest sense of the word. It is hardly possible to meet party-goers, although the institution is open until 3 in the morning. Visitors are admirers of good jazz.

Here you can often meet world-class musicians, for example, such as Peter King or Ronnie Scott. Ronnie Scott's club will give its visitors a lively sound of jazz or soul, an excellent mood and many like-minded people.

Fabric

London clubs are often located in the most unusual places. One of these is Fabric. It is housed in a former Victorian butchery . The club itself appeared here relatively recently - at the turn of the twentieth and twenty-first centuries.

The institution was located here thanks to the impressive area. Now the architectural advantages of the building are fully realized. The club has an innovative audio system, separate rooms and many dance floors. Among the latter, vibrating is distinguished. This is achieved through a sound system with 400-hbasovye converters, which are mounted directly in the floor section.





Interesting clubs

Entertainment in London is represented not only by night clubs. It is interesting to look into the Soho area. In the capital, there is an opinion that he "never sleeps." Life here just begins in the evening. It was then that numerous shops and bars, cafes and restaurants, showrooms and various shops opened.

Fans of Irish themes should visit a pub called The Auld Shillelagh. The age of this institution exceeds 130 years. The pub is famous for being the best Irish-themed establishment.

Another interesting club is Proud2. Everything here reminds incendiary Ibiza. A special role in the organization of the premises is played by the innovative audio system, which gives many positive moments of evening relaxation.

There are those institutions in London that harmoniously combine many functions. That is the O2 Arena. It combines at different times of the day or night a concert hall, a basketball court, a hockey rink, cinema halls, nightclubs and restaurants. "Arena O2" is a complete complex that allows you to have a great rest. Families come here very often: children spend time at the cinema or at the rink, while adults enjoy a delicious dinner in the company of friends.

An interesting institution in the capital is the restaurant Paramount. It is located on the 32nd floor of one of London's skyscrapers, which attracts attention. The restaurant is also famous for its gourmet cuisine. Chefs prepare delicious and hearty dishes in the best English traditions. You should definitely try coffee with dessert, enjoying the view from the window.
