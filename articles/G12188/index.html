Where is Xenios Anastasia Resort Spa located?

The institution is located in Greece, the peninsula of Halkidiki, the resort of Kassandra. Nea Skioni is located 3000 m from it, Hanioti is 10000 m, and Kallithea is 25000 m. Distance from Xenios Anastasia Resort Spa 5 * to Thessaloniki Airport is 100 km.

The location for the construction of the hotel was more than successful. Its territory is 30,000 square meters. The building is surrounded by an olive grove, it stands on the very shore of the Aegean Sea. Therefore, it is not surprising that the hotel is popular among tourists.

The hotel Xenios Anastasia Resort Spa 5 * only 115 rooms. They are divided into certain categories:
