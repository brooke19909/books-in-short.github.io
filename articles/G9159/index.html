An interesting idea for a vacation: by car from Voronezh to St. Petersburg

Traveling by car Voronezh - St. Petersburg will make even a short vacation unforgettable.





Car travel is popular due to a number of objective reasons:

  • economy - provided that the machine is fully loaded, fuel costs are much lower than the purchase of air or railway tickets;
  • independence in choosing interesting sights;
  • variability - the route is easy to change or adjust;
  • fascination, because along the way you can meet a lot of interesting things.

Choose a route

An experienced traveler, before setting off on a journey, will study the route and select the best one. Voronezh and St. Petersburg are separated by about 1200 km.

Route options:

  1. Take the M-4 route to Moscow, then go around the capital along the Moscow Ring Road, go through Zelenograd to the M-11 or M-10. It should be borne in mind that many sections of the M-4 and M-11 are paid. This route has a length of about 1200 km.
  2. The M-4 route to Yelets, where you can use the P-119 highway, having traveled around the city, and then return to the M-4. For the city of Bogoroditsky, go to 70K-229 and go to Tula, from where on the M-2 drive to Moscow. Using the Moscow Ring Road, take the M-10 to St. Petersburg. Track - 1470 km.
  3. Take the M-4 to Moscow, then, before reaching Domodedovo, go to the bypass A-107 and go to the intersection with M-10. Then follow the M-10 to St. Petersburg. Track - 1540 km.

Travel time takes from 16 to 18 hours, depending on speed and route.





The route Voronezh - St. Petersburg passes through crowded settlements. It is necessary to carefully observe the high-speed mode, since a lot of automatic video recording cameras are installed on the M-4 and M-10 routes.

Calculate the cost of the trip

A bus ticket for 1 person on the route Voronezh - St. Petersburg costs from 2 thousand rubles. Having set off on your own or rental car, you will need to pay:

  1. Fuel. With an average fuel consumption of 8 l / 100 km, approximately 100 l will be needed all the way. The cost of gasoline will be about 4 thousand rubles. If the engine is more economical, then gasoline will be consumed much less.
  2. The fare on the toll sections of the M-4 highway. The fare depends on the time, it is much more profitable to go from midnight to 7 a.m. On average, the fare will cost from 8:00 to 00:00 in 840 p., From 00:00 to 07:00 about 500 p. Payment with T-pass allows you to save 2 times. On the M-10 highway there is a paid site near the town of Vyshny Volochyok, payment is 200 p. Of course, you can go around paid sections, but this will make the road much longer, and more fuel will be spent.
  3. Food on the road. At lunches in roadside cafes, you can save by taking a thermos with tea or coffee and a snack from your home.

Conclusion: if a family of 4 sets off on a journey, it is much more profitable to cover the distance Voronezh - St. Petersburg by car than to buy the whole family tickets for a bus, plane or train.

Power distribution

Despite the fact that the overall quality of the asphalt pavement connecting Voronezh and St. Petersburg is not bad, the road requires attention and does not forgive mistakes and relaxation. Highways M-10, M-11 and M-4 are distinguished by high traffic of trucks, the road passes through settlements, bridges, there are traffic lights, there are traffic jams.

If there is only one driver behind the wheel, most likely it will be difficult for him to spend 15 hours controlling the movement of the car. You have to make a stop by choosing a roadside motel, hostel, hotel or recreation center, there are many of them both along the route and in the cities through which the route is laid.

Well-maintained and comfortable hotels provide an opportunity to rest and gain strength.

What to see along the road

Going along the Voronezh-St. Petersburg path, at the same time you can see a lot of interesting things, which guides will show at best from the bus window. What you can see on your own on the road:

  • in Tula - gingerbread and samovar museums, the Tula Kremlin in good condition, a weapons museum, also 14 km from the city there is a museum dedicated to Leo Tolstoy - his estate Yasnaya Polyana;
  • in Tarusa - the museums of K. Paustovsky and M. Tsvetaeva;
  • in Torzhok - Borisoglebsky Monastery and the Museum of Wooden Architecture;
  • in Valdai - The Museum of Bells.

The M-10 highway approaches St. Petersburg from the world famous museum cities of Pavlovsk and Pushkino.





How to make the road comfortable

The road Voronezh - St. Petersburg is well suited for road travelers. Many shops and gas stations allow you to purchase everything you need on the go. But it‚Äôs worth using a few tips to make the trip simple and convenient:

  • the route must be built in such a way as to go round the capital of Russia at night, avoiding traffic jams;
  • in the car, a radar detector and a recorder come in handy;
  • it is worth taking an extra charger on the road;
  • well, if the gadget has a navigation program, it will help you drive faster through unfamiliar areas and not be distracted by finding a route.

Good music and a pleasant company will make the road easy, and at the end of the way, vacationers will find the leader of world tourism - brilliant St. Petersburg.
