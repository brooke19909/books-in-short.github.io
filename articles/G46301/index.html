Opening a current account

After registration of a legal entity, it is necessary to apply to the FSS, PFR, IFTS and obtain certificates that the IP is registered. To conclude a settlement account agreement, it is necessary to submit to Sberbank:

- certificate of state registration of legal entities;

- statement of current account;

- a document confirming the authority of the director;

- notarized copies of the contract and charter;

- a document confirming the authority of the chief accountant;

- cards with sample signatures;

- certificates of registration with the PF;

- a license that allows you to carry out activities;

- registration card;

- if it is not the entrepreneur but the third person who will manage the funds, then a power of attorney must be issued for him.





After submitting the application, the bank checks the documents, verifies the identity of the client, signs the contract and enters the data into the Registration Book. After these operations, you can begin to use the account. The legislation stipulates a period of one day to comply with all formalities. In fact, operations occur in real time. If a complete package of documents that meet the requirements is provided, then the registration procedure takes about one hour.
