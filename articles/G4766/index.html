Hotel Reef 3 *, Adler: description, features, rooms and reviews

Hotel Reef 3 * is an ideal option for a sea holiday. The proximity to the beach, as well as the high level of service will make your vacation truly unforgettable and bright.





Resort Feature

Hotel Reef 3 * is a small cozy hotel with a beautiful and well-groomed territory. Here you can swim in the clean outdoor pool, sunbathe in the comfortable sun loungers, and use the wireless Internet anywhere in the resort. It is also worth noting the presence of private parking, where you can leave your vehicles under 24-hour security.

The apartments, which the hotel provides its guests, provide the most necessary and modern amenities that a traveler needs for a good rest. This is a TV, and a refrigerator, and air conditioning, and much more. It is worth noting that you do not have to bring a bathrobe and slippers with you, because they will already be waiting for you in your room.

Great attention is paid to the full nutrition of its guests. Particularly noteworthy is the work of the restaurant. The most popular dishes are freshly caught fish. But the real highlight is the availability of opportunities for organizing a barbecue. You can cook the dishes on an open fire yourself or order from the restaurant chefs.

Resort Location

Reef Hotel 3 * is located on the Black Sea coast in close proximity to the beach, which can be considered an indisputable advantage of this hotel. As for the location relative to transport nodes, it is also quite advantageous. Adler Train Station is approximately 4 kilometers from the hotel. But the nearest airport is in Sochi, and it will take about 6 kilometers to get to it.





One-room guest apartment in Reef Hotel 3 *

The most popular accommodation options in Reef Hotel 3 * (Adler, Hosta) are one-room single apartments. For lovers of independent travel, this is a real find, because you do not have to overpay for an individual stay or move into a room with an outsider. There is everything you need for a comfortable stay - an orthopedic one and a half bed. The bathroom is a combined toilet and shower. There is also a small sofa that can accommodate an extra guest.





One bedroom guest apartment

Many tourists come with companies or families to the spa town of Adler. Hotel "Reef" for such travelers provides two-room rooms that can accommodate four people, as well as additional guests. There are options with one and a half and large double beds. Also a pleasant moment is that each room is equipped with an individual bathroom. It should be noted that there is a private balcony on which comfortable rattan furniture is located, as well as a rope for drying towels and bathing suits.





What facilities are provided in the rooms?

If you want to get a quality vacation on the very shore of the sea, then you should definitely visit the Reef 3 * hotel (Adler). The rooms in this hotel are equipped with everything necessary for you to feel real home comfort. It is worth noting the presence of the following amenities:

  • landline telephone, which is provided for communication with the reception and administration (for an additional fee, it can be connected to the long-distance line);
  • a large modern TV allows you to view several dozen satellite channels, as well as files from disks, flash drives and other external media;
  • due to the fact that the bathroom has a built-in hairdryer, you can dry your hair in a matter of minutes and make a spectacular hairstyle;
  • there is an individual air conditioner that will allow you to adjust the air temperature in the room according to your needs;
  • there is a complete set of bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • cozy rattan chairs and a clothes dryer are provided on the balcony.




Resort Infrastructure

Infrastructure is one of the most important characteristics according to which a hotel is rated. Reef Hotel 3 * (Adler) provides you with the opportunity to use the following facilities:

  • in the courtyard there is a garden with benches where you can hide from the hot summer sun;
  • a large outdoor pool with clean fresh water, around which there are many sun loungers and umbrellas;
  • Private guarded parking, where each guest can leave his own vehicle (the estimated cost of the service per day is 100 rubles);
  • a restaurant serving traditional Russian as well as international dishes;
  • reception desk, where at any time of the day you can find answers to your questions regarding accommodation;
  • a laundry room where you will be provided with cleaning, washing and ironing services;
  • a business center with a wide range of computer and office equipment;
  • specially equipped room for business events;
  • large banquet room with a festive decor and musical equipment;
  • there is a small souvenir shop on site;
  • free internet, the signal of which extends to the entire hotel.




Facilities and service

A huge number of services provides its guests 3 * Reef Hotel (Adler). Traveler reviews especially note the availability of the following services:

  • You can use the services of a tour desk, in which you will find a familiarization tour of local attractions;
  • reception staff can assist you in acquiring tickets for any date convenient for you;
  • for an additional fee, you can speed up the registration process or stay in the room after the checkout time;
  • if you do not want to go down to the restaurant, then one call will be enough for the waiters to bring your order directly to the room;
  • If you are going on an excursion or a long walk, be sure to order a lunch box;
  • if you want to buy any products, then you can order their delivery, which saves you from having to go shopping on your own.

Sports lovers

If you are committed to a healthy and active lifestyle, then the Reef 3 * hotel will delight you with the following features:

  • equipment for table tennis, located in the garden and near the pool;
  • it is possible to rent a bicycle to go for a walk in the local natural and cultural attractions;
  • on the beach there is equipment for playing volleyball;
  • all kinds of water activities both at sea and in the pool;
  • after you have plenty of fun, head to the sauna to relax.

Useful hotel information

Hotel Reef 3 * is characterized by the following features:

  • check-out hours at which check-in and check-out are made are set at 14:00 and noon, respectively;
  • extra beds will cost 500 rubles per night, regardless of whether they are provided for children or for adults;
  • the room can accommodate no more than two additional guests;
  • you cannot bring animals to the territory of the hotel;
  • You will be able to pay for the services provided with a plastic card;
  • Within 5 days after booking the room, you must make an advance payment, otherwise the reserve will be considered invalid.




Positive reviews

When choosing a place to spend your vacation, always rely on the experience of travelers. Quite a lot of information can be found on specialized resources about the hotel Reef Hotel 3 * (Adler). Reviews demonstrate the following advantages of this institution:

  • very polite staff who are trying in every way to please the guests and instantly solve any problems;
  • in the event that the room you have reserved is free, you do not have to wait two hours in the afternoon in order to check in;
  • the rooms are very bright and clean with a beautiful sea view;
  • there is an ironing board and an iron in the corridor, which are shared by several rooms (this is much more convenient than asking them at the reception or at the maid each time);
  • very beautiful, clean and well-groomed area;
  • very convenient location near the public beach and the central promenade;
  • very beautiful design of the hall, as well as a pleasant decoration of the rooms;
  • daily clean cleaning as well as changing towels;
  • within walking distance there are many grocery stores, as well as cafes and entertainment clubs;
  • There is a good transport interchange nearby, which is very important if you like to travel independently to sightseeing places.

Hotel Reef 3 * (Adler) - reviews, description of the shortcomings

In no hotel, perhaps, everything is perfect and without flaws. The same can be said about Reef Hotel 3 *. Traveler reviews speak of the following negative points:

  • an unpleasant moment can be considered that the description of the numbers on the sites does not correspond to reality (or rather, all the positive characteristics concern only the suites);
  • in the bathroom there is no bathrobe or slippers (although their presence is indicated in the advertisement);
  • a prerequisite for service in the restaurant is the order of a comprehensive meal, otherwise you will not be able to try dishes from the menu (but this rule only applies during off-season);
  • not very hearty breakfasts;
  • Wireless Internet does not work very well, and therefore, if you urgently need to resolve some issues via the network, the only way out is to ask the administrator for a stationary computer
  • terrible soundproofing in the rooms (especially for rooms located on the ground floor);
  • in most rooms there is no balcony, and therefore there is simply nowhere to dry swimwear;
  • if your bathroom has run out of hygiene items, then the maid will not bring them until you ask her about it;
  • in some rooms guests noticed cockroaches;
  • upon arrival, you may not be accommodated in the room you booked;
  • the beds have very hard pillows, after sleep on which the neck is terribly sore;
  • despite the fact that a huge package of satellite channels has been announced, in fact it shows no more than 10.

General impression

Hotel Reef 3 * makes a positive impression. Thanks to the small but very clean and well-groomed area, an incredibly cozy atmosphere is created. There are few rooms in the hotel, and therefore almost all guests manage to get to know each other and make friends.

The hotel has an incredibly favorable location. Just a couple of minutes walk is the public beach (and if you do not want to go anywhere, you can swim in the pool). Very close is the central promenade, which can be safely called the epicenter of the resort life of Adler. There is also a bus stop nearby, which gives you the opportunity to independently visit the nearby resort towns, as well as cultural attractions.

Separately, it is worth noting the infrastructure of the hotel. There are all the necessary facilities to feel comfortable and not to leave the hotel once again. The rooms are quite spacious and bright. But with regard to the work of the restaurant, it does not satisfy all guests. So, for example, if you rest in the "off season", then you can not try the dishes from the menu.

Despite the fact that the furniture and repairs are a bit outdated, the atmosphere is quite cozy. This is largely due to the good work of the maids, who carefully maintain the cleanliness of the rooms. Also, one cannot fail to say about the professional work of the reception desk staff, who strive to please and assist the guests in every possible way.
