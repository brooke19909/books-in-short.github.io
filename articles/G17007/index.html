Hotel Mirador 3 * (Rimini, Italy) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation on the Italian coast in the resort town of Rimini and at the same time do not want to empty your wallet very much, then consider the three-star Mirador economy hotel as an accommodation option. Today we will get to know him better and find out what tourists expect here.





Location

Mirador 3 * is located in a quiet area called Rivazzurra of the Italian resort town of Rimini. The distance to the city center is only three kilometers. In the vicinity of the hotel there are lots of shops, restaurants, cafes and pastry shops where you can taste delicious dishes of both local and international cuisine. This hotel is very close to the beach. So, you can walk to the seashore in just three to five minutes with an unhurried step. All beaches in Rimini are in municipal ownership, so the use of sun loungers and parasols is paid. In addition to sun loungers, beaches also have equipment for active entertainment and water sports.

Hotel Mirador (Rimini): photos and description

This three-star hotel belongs to the category of economy class and consists of one four-story building, located not a small private territory with a garden and a playground. There is a restaurant where guests are offered breakfast and dinner. You can eat both indoors and on the outdoor terrace. To the great joy of our compatriots, there is a staff who speak Russian. As for the housing stock, it is represented by 25 standard rooms equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. This hotel is suitable for people who do not plan to spend much time in the room, but who prefer to combine a magnificent beach holiday with active trips to the sights of Italy.





Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the Mirador 3 * hotel (Rimini, Italy) offers everyone to stay in one of 25 rooms located in a four-story building. All of them belong to the standard category and are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. So, each room has its own bathroom, air conditioning, hairdryer, safe, refrigerator, TV and a balcony or terrace. Moreover, the use of air conditioning and a safe is free. All rooms have ceramic flooring. For the youngest guests a cot can be provided. Cleaning in the rooms of Mirador (Rivazzurra) 3 * is carried out on a daily basis. Towels are changed every two days, and bed linen is renewed once a week. Maids also regularly replenish toiletry supplies.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of the Mirador 3 * hotel (Rimini), settling of the staying tourists is carried out in the afternoon. However, if you arrive at the hotel earlier, then, in the presence of free rooms, they will try to populate you immediately. On the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before ten in the morning. Please note that cots are available upon prior request. Also, according to the policy of the hotel, guests with pets are not allowed on its territory. For payment in Mirador 3 * (Rimini) both cash and plastic cards are accepted.

The hotel reception is open around the clock, so you can solve any problem you may have very quickly. Concierge services are also available.

Beach vacation

As already mentioned, Mirador (Rimini) is only a hundred meters away from the sea. The beach here is urban. For the use of sun loungers you will have to pay about 4-5 euros per day. However, you can always sit directly on the sand, spreading a towel or mat brought with you on a site not occupied by sun loungers. The beaches here are sandy and very clean. Entering the water gently, without stones. It is very convenient to relax with small children. The beaches have equipment for water activities and sports. Also on the promenade you will find many different cafes, restaurants and bars where you can eat and enjoy your favorite drink.





Prices

As for the cost of living at the Mirador Hotel (Rimini), according to our compatriots who have been here, it is more than attractive. So, travelers assure that the prices for accommodation in this hotel are more than adequate and quite comparable with the prices of not three-star hotels, but hostels. Some guests say that they managed to stay here, paying only 10 euros per person per night.

Hotel Mirador (Rimini): traveler reviews from Russia

In order to save you time on getting acquainted with the reviews of tourists about their vacation in the Italian hotel Mirador (Rimini), we bring to your attention the generalized comments of our compatriots who happened to stay here. Running a little ahead, we note that, as in the case with most other hotels, there are both positive and negative reviews about this hotel. However, according to most tourists, Mirador is a very good place for a budget holiday. Despite the fact that you will find comfortable rooms equipped with everything you need, the price of accommodation at the hotel is one of the lowest in Rimini. So, if you plan to spend your holidays combining sightseeing trips with a beach holiday, and looking for a hotel mainly for sleep only, then Mirador will be a great option. And it will be comfortable here both for travelers going on vacation with children of different ages, and for older people. But we will understand in more detail.





Impressions of tourists about the hotel, its room stock and location

According to travelers, Mirador 3 * is located on a quiet and calm street, so here you can safely relax away from city noise. The center of Rimini is only two bus stops from the hotel. So you can easily reach it on foot, making a pleasant short walk. In addition, the hotel is very close to the beach - about a hundred meters.

The hotel "Mirador", according to our compatriots, is quite compact. So, you will not find huge halls and spacious apartments here, because this is still not a luxury hotel. But this hotel is very cozy, and there is enough space in it for comfortable accommodation of all guests. The hotel has a restaurant and bar. Wi-Fi is free in the lobby and some of the rooms. The reception desk here, by the way, works around the clock.

As for the rooms of Hotel Mirador 3 *, then, according to tourists, they are very clean and comfortable. It is noticeable that recently a repair was made. Furniture and appliances here, of course, are not new, but everything is in good working order. The rooms have a refrigerator, TV (though without Russian channels), a hairdryer, a safe (free, unlike most other hotels), air conditioning (open from 7 pm to 6 am), a furnished balcony. Some rooms have an electric kettle. If necessary, at the reception you can ask for an iron (free). There are a lot of sockets in the room, so there will be no difficulties with charging phones and electronic gadgets. In a small bathroom, in addition to the basic plumbing, even a bidet fits. Hot water, according to the guests, is always there. A small minus for those who come here from November to March will be the fact that the rooms at this time are quite cool, as they are not heated. However, this feature is characteristic not only for Mirador, but also for many Italian hotels. Therefore, if you are used to spending the night in a warm room (even subject to the availability of blankets), it is better to choose a warm season for travel.

As for cleaning in this hotel, it is carried out very high quality and daily. Towels are renewed every other day, and linen once a week. Maids also regularly replenish toilet paper and soap.





Comments of tourists about food at the Mirador Hotel

Most travelers staying at the Mirador Hotel (Rimini) opted for the half-board option (breakfast and dinner). According to our compatriots, breakfast at the hotel can not be called particularly hearty and varied. However, this fact is the norm for budget hotels in Europe. So, in the morning, guests at the restaurant are offered bakery products, butter, jam, dry cereal, tea, coffee and fruit. As for dinners, most tourists consider them quite worthy. So, the daily menu includes salad, soup, pasta, fish or meat dishes, as well as fruits as a dessert.

In addition, there is a supermarket near the hotel where you can buy almost any product of your choice. There is where to store them, since there are refrigerators in the rooms of Mirador. If you do not plan to eat at the hotel, then you can have lunch and dinner at any of the local restaurants and cafes, of which there are a great many in Rimini. By the way, experienced travelers are sure to recommend to all tourists traveling to Italy not to indulge in pleasure and try the local pizza and pasta. According to our compatriots, these dishes here are almost everywhere perfectly cooked.

So if you plan to go on an early excursion, you can ask for breakfast to be prepared early. Hotel staff never refuse guests such requests.
