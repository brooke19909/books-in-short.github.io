What is the Arabat Spit and Schastlivtsevo?

Between the Crimea and the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov, a long and narrow sand spit stretches for 110 kilometers. It is called the Arabat arrow. The nearest town is Genichesk. Resorts come there too, but the rest in the town is still very different from hanging out on the spit, where on the one hand there is a gentle sea, and on the other, the healing brine of Sivash. One hundred kilometers of sandy beach remained undeveloped until the end of the XIX century. On the advice of the famous breeder L.P. All this, plus low prices, implies a cloudless family vacation. This village is the golden mean between the bustling Genic Hill and Strelkov, remote from any civilization.

Inns

For those who prefer a more comfortable stay, Schastlivtsevo offers a more or less developed hotel base. Mini-hotels, erected by enterprising residents on the site of private huts, grow like mushrooms after rain. They are cheaper than boarding houses, and in terms of services, they are higher than state holiday homes. Of course, the rooms in them are of different price categories. For some apartments they may ask for 500-600 hryvnia per day. But the room will have everything for a comfortable stay: a plasma TV, refrigerator, air conditioning, hot water around the clock. The only drawback of such mini-hotels is that they, unlike pensions, are located away from the sea. Access to the beach - through the recreation center "Seagull-2" or "Azov".

Excursions

The sea and the sun are not the only things that go to the Arabat arrow. Wellness holidays are also practiced here. Schastlivtsevo from the eastern side is washed by the waters of Sivash. This shallow bay is famous for its salts, which are similar in composition to the brine of the Dead Sea. Many sufferers of arthritis and joint diseases annually come to the Arabat Spit to be treated with the healing mud of Sivash. Three kilometers from Schastlivtsev, a hot geyser beats from under the ground. Its water is rich in iodine and bromine and is useful for those who have heart and respiratory problems. You can make independent or organized excursions to the Biryuchy island, on which the lighthouse towers, as well as to the ruins of the Arabat fortress, which was erected by the Turks.
