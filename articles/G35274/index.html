DIY tomato crafts: interesting ideas

Have you decided to do DIY crafts from tomatoes? Looking for original ideas for a children's exhibition or table decoration? In the article you will find options for both. Read, choose your favorite sample. Try again. This exciting activity will be a great entertainment for children.





Autumn crafts from fruits and vegetables

Traditional competitions at the beginning of the school year in a kindergarten or school are the presentation of products made from natural materials or "sights" grown in the garden. Everything is used: potatoes, zucchini, eggplant, squash, apples, cucumbers, tomatoes.





Moreover, they use both one type of workpieces and combine different vegetables and fruits. This option allows you to get the most original and unusual products.

DIY Tomato Crafts for Exhibition

If it is assumed that the work performed should be carried to school or kindergarten, it is better to use uncut vegetables. Violating their integrity is minimal, as in this case there is a risk of not delivering the craft in its original form. The simplest thing that can be done is a variety of faces or figures made of small cherry tomatoes.





It is convenient to connect the elements with the help of toothpicks, matches, sticks. If allowed, it is interesting to combine several vegetables or decor of non-natural origin (beads, threads), use this. Having a little imagination, it’s easy to complete a whole family of little men or, for example, caterpillars with different emotions on their “faces”. With this task, the child will be able to cope on his own. The main thing is to give him an idea.

In general, working with the shape of a ball of various sizes, it is not difficult to create abstract compositions in the form of connected rings, beads, arches. You can string cherry or other tomatoes on sticks, wire. The second option allows you to get rounded extended shapes, such as a snake, figure eight or spiral.

Making animals

If you have grown specimens of an unusual shape or simply diverse, and not just round, use this. Do crafts from tomatoes with your own hands in the form of existing or fantastic animals.





If necessary, cut out some elements. Connections are easy to make using matches or toothpicks, as in the previous version.

If you do not have a specific task to create a voluminous sculptural composition, use the following sample. Here the cow is executed in the form of a relief. It is important to firmly connect the parts together and with the base, if you have to carry the product.





Edible table decorations

Tomato crafts made using the carving technique look very impressive. With your own hands you can make flowers that will surprise your guests.





Such crafts are performed using curly cutting. Professionals use special tools. You can try repeating this simple pattern with a regular, only sharpened knife.

These products are also suitable as crafts for the exhibition, however, only a schoolchild will be able to cope with this task, most likely with the help of parents. But the costs give a very good result. The only drawback is that these decorations begin to deteriorate faster than whole vegetable compositions. However, in this way you can create whole bouquets of tomatoes.

Thus, you can do the crafts of tomatoes with your own hands in different ways, both using whole vegetables of the correct or unusual shape, and using tools for figured cutting slices and slices.
