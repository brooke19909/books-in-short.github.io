Sochi Beach Hotel: description, contacts, rooms and reviews

Resort Sochi Beach Hotel provides its guests the opportunity to relax on the beach in comfortable rooms. In addition, a huge number of services are provided for you here.





Brief description of the resort hotel

Sochi Beach Hotel is a luxury hotel that will give you the opportunity to fully enjoy a marine holiday. Here you have developed a rich resort infrastructure, the highlight of which can be considered a spacious terrace located directly on the roof. You will also have the opportunity to swim in the pool, visit the traditional Turkish hammam and much more.

As for the guest apartments at Sochi Beach Hotel Resort, they are characterized by modern design and a high level of comfort. The presence of huge windows from which a bewitching view of the sea coast opens up can be considered a feature. It is also important to note the full range of modern amenities that will provide you with the most comfortable living conditions.

In order for the rest to be complete, special attention should be paid to nutrition. Sochi Beach Hotel has two luxurious restaurants, one of which is located indoors, and the second - on a spacious outdoor terrace. Here you can taste dishes from different cuisines of the world. Particular attention should be paid to options made from fresh seafood.





Resort Location

It is worth noting that the Sochi Beach Hotel resort is characterized by an extremely favorable location. Sochi is a resort city, and therefore it is important to be at the epicenter of events, and this hotel provides you with such an opportunity. You will live on the very shore of the sea near a huge number of cafes, shops and entertainment centers. As for the transport interchange, the distance to the railway station or bus station is about the same - about 8 kilometers. As for the international airport, it will have to overcome about 40 km.





Hotel Rooms

In the resort hotel Sochi Beach Hotel 3 * you will have the following accommodation options:

  • Standard rooms are characterized by a relatively small area, however, they have a lot of living space. The visual expansion of the room is due to the design in bright colors, as well as a large amount of sunlight. This is a bedroom with a large bed, as well as other necessary furniture and amenities. There is also a small entrance area.
  • The apartments of the "Comfort" category have a rather impressive area, as well as a wide range of amenities. In addition to a large bed, there is also a folding upholstered furniture (it can very well be adapted to accommodate additional guests). The highlight of this room can be considered a huge window over the wall, which not only makes it possible to enjoy the seascape, but also lets a lot of sunlight into the room.
  • Suite is a huge apartment of 40 square meters. There are two rooms here, one of which acts as a living room, and the second as a bedroom. It is worth noting that this room, like the previous category, is equipped with huge windows over the wall with a sea view. In addition, they are tinted (that is, no one outside will see what is happening in your room). At the same time, glass transmits sunlight well.




Facilities at the guest apartment

If you want to enjoy a comfortable stay, then feel free to choose the Sochi Beach Hotel. The 3-star hotel gives you the opportunity to take advantage of the following list of amenities in the apartment:

  • individual air conditioning system that allows you to make a cool oasis from your room, even if there is extreme heat outside the window;
  • a large flat-screen LCD TV connected to a satellite dish broadcasting about a hundred entertainment and news channels;
  • a reliable electronic safe with a combination lock allows you to always be sure of the safety of your valuables;
  • thanks to a landline phone, you can chat with the administrator without leaving your number (it is also possible to connect to an international line for an additional fee);
  • the minibar can be used both for its intended purpose and also serve as a small refrigerator for storing food;
  • the bathroom provides a set of necessary bath and cosmetic accessories.




Resort Infrastructure

Sochi Beach Hotel has the following resort infrastructure:

  • a large terrace for sunbathing and an overview of the surrounding landscapes, located directly on the roof of the hotel;
  • a traditional Turkish hammam, as well as a Russian bathhouse, where you can have a good time, as well as relieve emotional and physical stress;
  • There is a private beach area where you will be spared of garbage and crowds;
  • free wireless internet in public areas of the hotel;
  • Guests have the right to use public parking free of charge (for this you should definitely reserve a place in advance);
  • the bar always awaits a huge selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as light snacks and a wide variety of salads;
  • in the restaurants working at the hotel, you can taste dishes of different nations of the world (you can order food directly in the apartment);
  • the reception is open around the clock, and therefore you can always count on help from the hotel staff;
  • There are specially equipped smoking areas.




Useful information for tourists

The Sochi Beach Hotel has the following policies:

  • check-in of the guests begins strictly at 14:00 (if there are free rooms, then this rule can be changed individually, subject to a fixed payment);
  • on the day of departure, you must return the keys to the reception before noon, otherwise an hourly fee will be charged for excessive stay in the room;
  • extra beds for children up to 12 years old will cost 500 rubles per night, and for adult guests - 1000 rubles;
  • if you come on vacation with babies, then upon prior request, it is possible to provide special cots;
  • guests are strictly forbidden to bring animals to the hotel, otherwise, the administration has the right to refuse accommodation;
  • There are special terminals for paying for services using non-cash payment systems.




Sochi Beach Hotel (Sochi) - reviews, advantages

Choosing a good hotel is not an easy task. So, help to evaluate the quality of service in Sochi Beach Hotel reviews. Based on the comments of tourists, we can talk about the following positive points:

  • helpful and friendly staff;
  • good evening program with concerts, contests and a disco;
  • the presence of two pools - indoor and outdoor (the second is heated in cold weather);
  • next to the hotel building there is a large landscaped area for walking;
  • perfect cleanliness of the rooms;
  • if you are planning a celebration or you have chosen this hotel for your honeymoon, then, by prior arrangement, your room will be decorated accordingly;
  • proximity to the sea and the corresponding view from the window.

General impression

It is worth noting that this hotel can be proud of its location, because not every resort institution can boast that it is located directly on the beach. In addition, you will simply have a breathtaking view from the window. Moreover, the room is very clean and beautiful. If we talk about the shortcomings - this is expensive and tasteless food in a restaurant. In addition, waiters often try to draw money out of customers for breakfast, which is included in the price. It is also worth noting that due to the proximity of the train station, you may be disturbed by the noise of trains. In general, this accommodation option can be considered very attractive for those who want to fall asleep and wake up to the sounds of the sea, and also do not want to spend too much time getting to the beach (in this hotel it is closed, that is, it works only for guests) .
