Pawnshops of Nizhny Novgorod. List of addresses and telephone numbers of organizations

A pawnshop is a microfinance organization that issues loans with a fairly low interest rate on the security of the borrower's property. Pawnshops of Nizhny Novgorod work according to the principles generally accepted in the state. You can contact them in case of financial problems.

What you can take to the pawn shops of Nizhny Novgorod

Previously, in such organizations only products made of precious metals, namely gold and silver, were accepted on bail. So it was in tsarist and Soviet times. Given the dynamic development of civilization, pawnshops significantly expanded the range of property that those who wish to borrow money can bring and hand over.





An exhaustive list of property that pawn shops of Nizhny Novgorod take is as follows:

- jewelry;

- watches (mechanical, electronic);

- household and digital appliances (mobile phones, televisions, tablets, laptops, washing machines, etc.);

- diamonds;

- fabrics and articles of fur;

- items of antique value.

The procedure for processing the transaction and loan conditions

Note that a deal at a pawnshop is simple to execute. A client can be any adult citizen of Russia. Persons under the age of 18 cannot donate things to a pawnshop, since legally they are not yet responsible for their actions. For registration of documents it is necessary to present a passport.

Getting a loan at a pawnshop is easier than at a bank. Why? The client brings a specific thing to the pawnshop, which, in case of non-payment of the loan, is taken away for sale. When applying for a consumer loan at a bank, a client does not always purchase goods, but may also request cash. Therefore, the bank is more attentive to the design of such contracts. The probability of failure is much greater than in a pawnshop.

Nizhny Novgorod pawnshops issue loans for a month, a week, two weeks, or at the request of a client. For each day of using the loan, the client pays interest. Rates can be different and recently there has been a downward trend. In numerical terms, people pay a commission to the pawnshop in the amount of 0.25-0.35% of the value of the goods per day.
