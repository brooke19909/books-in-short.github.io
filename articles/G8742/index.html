Holidays in Cyprus in March

While snowdrifts are slowly melting in Russia, fragrant flowers bloom in Cyprus in March, and the sun gently warms. If you dreamed of visiting an island where the beautiful Aphrodite came ashore from the sea foam, do it in the spring. Beautiful nature, flowering tangerines, lemons, jasmine, almond trees, sweet smells floating around everywhere relieve winter spleen and arouse interest in life.





What is the weather like in Cyprus in March?

Spring arrives on this island in mid-February. In early March, the air warms up to +18 degrees in the afternoon. At night, the temperature drops to 8 degrees, so do not forget to bring warm clothes. Sometimes strong winds and rains are observed. According to statistics, bad weather costs about 5 days during the whole month. The rest of the time it‚Äôs sunny and clear. There are ski slopes in Troodos and you can enjoy the steep descent.

Every day the air temperature rises. At the end of March, Cyprus becomes even friendlier. The average temperature is +22 degrees, sometimes it can rise to +26. At this time, you can already get a beautiful tan. Therefore, the number of tourists is increasing markedly. But swimming in the sea is decided only by the most daring. The water is cool, on average +18 degrees. Vacationers solve this problem by choosing hotels with a heated pool.

In general, March is the best time to visit the island with your children. It is also chosen by elderly people, travelers who have cardiovascular diseases, or simply have poor tolerance of acclimatization. Warm weather provides an opportunity to explore local attractions. In the summer, in extreme heat, doing this will not be so comfortable.





Tour Prices

The main season begins in late May. Therefore, buying tours to Cyprus in March, you can save a lot. Prices are lower on average by 30-40%. You can purchase a ticket for half the price. Some hotels lure tourists with the promise of free spa treatments. In other places, children are not charged for accommodation.

Travel agencies offer a lot of options: a short trip for the weekend or holidays, a vacation with the whole family, travel for couples and fun youth companies. Cyprus attracts pilgrims to the holy places, spa lovers, sightseeing hunters. In March, you can actively relax and stock up on positive impressions until the next vacation.

Excursion program

In the summer, when the temperature rises above +30 degrees, I want two things: to lie in the room under air conditioning or splash around in the sea. For excursions around the island, such weather does not have. Another thing is Cyprus in March! Guest reviews are unanimous: the island at this time of the year is very beautiful, the weather is sunny, you can wander the streets of the city for a long time or choose walking excursions in nature. Those who wish can rent a car or bicycle.





What sights can I see? The most famous are:

  • the medieval castle of Kolossi, located 10 km from Limassol;
  • tombs of kings at the exit of Paphos, most of which date back to the 4th century BC. e .;
  • the ancient city of Amathus in Limassol, whose city walls now go to sea;
  • Archbishop's Palace in the capital of Nicosia, on the territory of which there are several museums with maps, statues, murals, ancient icons, jewelry, books and finds of archaeologists;
  • the ancient settlement of Hirokitia, whose age is 9-10 thousand years;
  • Lefkara village, famous for its lace and silverware;
  • Kyrenia Castle, which houses a shipwreck museum;
  • the beautiful 16th century caravanserai of Buyuk Khan, where Ottoman merchants traded;
  • Kato Archaeological Park in Paphos.

Natural beauty

Architectural buildings, historical finds attract many tourists. But in March, when Cyprus is buried in verdure, and the smell of flowering trees makes you dizzy, you are tempted to go to nature. You can spend time in the fresh air while exploring natural attractions. The most famous of them are:

  • Akamas Nature Reserve, where the Adonis trail and the bath of the goddess Aphrodite are located;
  • the rock of Petra tou Romiou, near which, according to legend, Aphrodite was born;
  • Avakas gorge;




  • Troodos Mountains, in which the 15-meter Millomeris Waterfall is hidden;
  • Cape Cavo Greco, where there is a natural arch, called the "Bridge of Lovers";
  • Cavo Greco National Park, where you can choose walking, cycling or horse riding trails;
  • Park of birds and animals (Paphos), where, in addition to observing its inhabitants, you will see a show of parrots and owls.

Pilgrimage to the holy places

Ancient temples and monasteries, ancient relics, miraculous icons make Orthodox Christians come to Cyprus. In March, the weather is great for pilgrimages. Here you can see:

  • Larnaca Temple, where the relics of St. Lazarus resurrected by Jesus are stored;
  • the monastery of Stavrovuni, in which there is a fragment of the life-giving cross of the Savior;
  • the monasteries of Kykkos and Trooditissa, where you can bow before the icons of the Virgin, painted by the Apostle Luke in the years of her life;




  • the monastery of John Lampadist, where the miraculous relics of this saint are stored;
  • the monastery of St. Neophyte cut down in the rocks;
  • Monastery of St. Nicholas, in the territory of which a large number of cats live.

Interesting events

Holidays in Cyprus in March will be remembered not only for excursions. At this time of the year there are several interesting events, as well as local holidays. Among them are:

  • Green Monday (the first day of Lent). At this time, residents clean the houses, bake pies with vegetables and go on picnics.
  • Bicycle marathon in Limassol. Everyone can take part in it.
  • Rally on local villages on retro cars.
  • Fashion Week in the capital of Cyprus Nicosia.
  • Festivals of documentary (Nicosia) and author's (Limassol) cinema.
  • Carnival in Paphos and Limassol with concerts, dances and a children's parade.
  • Greece Independence Day, which is celebrated on March 25. On this day, military parades, concerts, orchestras play the national anthem.

Thalassotherapy

A trip to Cyprus in March, as well as in any other season, is a wonderful opportunity to improve your health and relax from stress. Many hotels offer thalassotherapy courses (treatment with seafood). This includes mud baths, massages, gymnastics in sea water, body wraps, aromatherapy, a sauna and other procedures depending on the indications. There are different courses: for losing weight, "anti-stress", for women and for men, for young mothers, tonus restoration, "anti-cellulitis", procedures for the treatment of arthrosis, rheumatism, severity and leg pain.





The best places for rest and recreation are:

  • Four Seasons Hotel (Limassol);
  • "Le Miridion Limassol Spa and Resort";
  • Elite hotel Anassa in Polis;
  • Elysium on Paphos;
  • Amathus Beach in Limassol.

Cyprus in March attracts tourists with low prices, the sun, the awakening nature and numerous attractions. It is very pleasant to travel from snowy Russia to where spring already reigns supreme, orchids are blooming, butterflies are fluttering and the sea is splashing.
