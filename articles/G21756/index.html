Deer Park turned its head in China: how to get there?

On a large island in the Chinese province of Hainan, the famous park "Deer Turned Its Head" is located. Its other name - Luhuitou - is given in honor of the peninsula of the same name. The mountains offer gorgeous views of the sea and the city. Here you can get acquainted with unique plants and observe animals, take stunning pictures against the backdrop of nature and just relax and unwind. The place is quite popular with tourists, you can book a tour or explore the surroundings on your own.

Tale of lovers

The unusual name of the park came from a long legend. One landowner wished to get the beautiful deer horns and sent a young guy for them. The young man searched for the beast for a long time and found him in the local area, on the mountain. But the animal was so beautiful that the man did not dare to kill him and returned home with nothing. Then the landowner became furious and ordered to imprison the mother of the young man.

The guy again went to the mountains in search of a deer. He found and saved him from the leopard, but the story repeated itself: the young man could not shoot at the beast. And then he turned around and turned into a beautiful girl. Upon learning of the trouble, she helped the young man and his mother. The cruel landowner was punished. And the guy and the girl fell in love and soon got married. They built their house on the hill, where the park is now located.

How to get to the park "Deer turned his head"?

Finding a point of interest on the island is quite simple: it is located at the highest point in the area. Tourists who know the Chinese language, local residents will be happy to tell you how to get from Sanya to the park "Deer turned his head." There are special signs in English. Those who are not familiar with other languages, it is advisable to build a route in advance and clarify information about transport. Buses and taxis go to the park entrance. For the pleasure of driving directly to the top, you will have to pay a significant amount - at least 30 yuan.

Those who wish to walk on foot can reach the park from Dadonghai Bay in half an hour . The best time to visit is considered in the afternoon, after 16 hours. During this period, the heat subsides, and climbing uphill will not be so difficult. In addition, this attraction is worth seeing in the dark, in the light of bright lights. Meeting animals

At the foot of the mountain there is a small farm where deer are raised. These animals are a symbol of not only the park, but the entire city of Sanya.

Taking pictures of them is not forbidden, and, having received permission from the owners, you can even pet or feed the animals. Such an event will especially entertain children and adolescents. It is worth noting that for the smallest, the entrance to the "Deer turned his head" park is free. There are various discounts on ticket prices for other categories of visitors: students, retirees.
