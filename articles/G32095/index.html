Village Andreevka (Crimea): choose a place to stay

Since the Soviet times, the Crimean peninsula has become a favorite vacation spot for citizens of different countries, including Russians. Basically, many come here in order to improve their health in sanatoriums. Also, lovers of beach and outdoor activities will feel very comfortable here. The favorite budget place was the village. Andreevka (Crimea), which is part of the northern Nakhimovsky district of the city of Sevastopol. This settlement is located on the southwest coast of the Crimean peninsula. Throughout the coastline there are various holiday homes, pensions, campsites and parking lots.

Briefly about the village

The first thing I would like to tell tourists is where the village is located. It is located near Sevastopol, only 30 km. This location is convenient enough for those who come to this place not only from the peninsula, but also from the mainland. Between the village and the city regular transport links are established. And this, in turn, allows vacationers to move freely, both on buses and on boats.

Andreevka (Crimea) has very good reviews, because it is located in an ecologically clean area. In the north, the settlement is located between Cape Lukull and Tyubek, and in the south of the village is Cape Margopulo.

The infrastructure is developed here, which pleases tourists. This is a well-maintained beach with water activities, mini-markets, pharmacies, exchange offices, shops, a post office, a large green park with a variety of Crimean trees and shrubs.

In the evening, holidays in Andreevka (Crimea) are diversified by incendiary beach discos and cafe-bars.

Offers for vacationers

The village of Andreevka (Crimea) will delight tourists with sparing prices for room and board. The private sector has opened its doors to vacationers since the beginning of May, since the sea warms up here quite early. Guest and private houses provide guests with rooms with a private bathroom and shower. Prices for such housing range from 300 rubles. per person up to 700-800 rubles. Here, the hosts will offer home-cooked dinners, which are already included in the total price.



Guest house "Lavandina"

Lavandina Guesthouse is a small guest house located next to Cape Lucullus. The rooms have all the amenities for a cozy stay: shower room, bathroom, TV, kettle, refrigerator, fan. There is a shared kitchen equipped with stoves and tables. The green area with a gazebo and barbecue grill is collected in the evening by the guests of the "Lavender". The cost of living is 350-650 rubles per person.

Holiday Village "The Sun"

Half a kilometer from the sea is an inexpensive private recreation center "Sun." The road to the beach passes through a park with relict trees and ends with a gentle descent very rare for this area. This recreation center is quite popular, as the village. Andreevka (Crimea) annually receives private individuals, children and youth groups, participants in festivals and sports camps. Rooms are available for up to two to five people. At the request of vacationers in private, three meals a day can be organized. The price of accommodation will be no more than 400 rubles per person.
