Original DIY necklace

Not always in jewelry stores is what I would like to give preference to. That size is not the same, then the color, then the style. Therefore, at all times needlework was revered, and was also considered one of the ways of self-expression. Nowadays, having such a variety of materials for creativity, it is sometimes difficult to decide on them. Today's article is devoted to how to make an unusual necklace of beads and beads with your own hands.

Training

To make a necklace you will need:

  • iron chain;
  • round mother-of-pearl buttons of different diameters;
  • beads, wooden beads and tear beads;
  • silver copper jewelry wire (0.5 mm);
  • clamp rings;
  • Lobster snap fastener
  • pliers.

The choice of colors for making a necklace with your own hands can be anything, depending on the taste. For example, bright and colorful beads are perfect for a summer and sunny look. For a restrained neutral appearance, you can choose translucent and pastel shades. And if you add a few dark buttons to them, you get a very stylish option. You can also alternate wire with dark beads with wire with light. As you can see, the flight of fantasy is completely unlimited and is due only to creative abilities.

Assembly of blanks

Having prepared all the necessary materials, you can proceed to the necklace itself. First you need to cut a piece of wire and put a tear bead on it. Then it is necessary to twist the end of the wire over the top of the beads. Next comes a bead made of wood or beads, which is worn on top. Screw the wire again. Thus, after each bead or strung button there should be a fixing unit made of wire. For the mother of pearl button you will need another wire that will go into the upper hole. In total, about 15 such blanks should be obtained.

Fasteners

After all the beads are strung, it's time to start their attachment to the chain. By the way, the chain can be both large and not very large. But you need to consider that the size of the beads depends on the choice of chain. The larger the chain, the more massive they should be. So, retreating one or two divisions, pass through them and fix the wire blanks. Fix sharp ends so that they do not cause inconvenience.

Castle

And finally the clasp. Thread the clamp rings through the ends of the chain. And in one of the rings pass the lock. All the DIY necklace is ready!

Here is such a simple to manufacture, but stylish and unusual decoration can be made literally from improvised materials that are likely to be found at home for every woman.
