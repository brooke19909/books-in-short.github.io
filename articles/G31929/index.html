Description of the Grand Hotel Vidgof in Chelyabinsk

This review presents to your attention a description of the Vidgof Hotel, located in the city of Chelyabinsk. The hotel is considered the best in the city. It has a category of five stars. Meets all international standards and requirements.

About the complex

Grand Hotel Vidgof in Chelyabinsk began to receive guests in the summer of 2012 and is still considered one of the best facilities for accommodation in the city. Here, customers are provided with a huge variety of services, there is a swimming pool, gym, conference rooms, restaurants, spa and much more. Highly qualified service and an individual approach to each client are offered.

All children are welcome at the hotel. Accommodation for children up to two years is free.

Pets are allowed (surcharge).

The hotel meets the requirements of the most spoiled and demanding customers.

Facilities and service

Grand Hotel Chelyabinsk "Vidgof" provides its guests with many services.

Among the main ones:

  • free Internet;
  • Private parking
  • restaurants
  • conference rooms;
  • health Center;
  • pool, sauna;
  • transfer;
  • gym;
  • karaoke;
  • library;
  • dry cleaning;
  • tour agency.

Accommodation

The complex has 139 comfortable rooms of various categories, including luxurious presidential apartments.

The rooms of the Grand Hotel Vidgof (Chelyabinsk) are individually decorated. Among the amenities there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay: modern furniture, flat-screen TV, safe, refrigerator. In the bathroom slippers, bathrobes, toiletries.

The number of rooms is presented in the following categories:

  • Deluxe twin (from 6 thousand 500 rubles);
  • Deluxe twin premium (from 7 thousand);
  • Deluxe queen (from 8 thousand 500 rubles);
  • Deluxe Queen Premium (8900 rubles);
  • Deluxe queen exclusive (9 thousand 400 rubles);
  • Studio room (12 000 rub.);
  • Suite (21 thousand);
  • Suite exclusive (23 thousand);
  • Presidential room (from 39 to 41 thousand rubles).

Restaurants

At the disposal of the Grand Hotel Vidgof (Chelyabinsk) there are three chic restaurants, a lobby, fitness bars and a karaoke room.

  1. The Senator bar in the lobby will delight guests with a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks and light snacks. Here you will find a pleasant classic interior, cozy gazebos, an area for watching sports.
  2. Courage is a restaurant with a terrace. Here, guests can indulge in Japanese, Italian, Russian and European dishes, enjoy seafood, sushi, pizza and spend the evening in an atmosphere of exclusivity and privacy.
  3. Clouds Restaurant serves European, Japanese and Russian dishes.
  4. The Kupol restaurant with a transparent arch in the center of the hall, a festive interior and an abundance of gilded decor contains international dishes on the menu.
  5. After dinner, a karaoke bar invites everyone to relax in a relaxed atmosphere.
  6. In the fitness center there is a fresh bar, which offers sports and healthy nutrition and delicious dishes made from healthy and natural products.

Recreation and entertainment

The Grand Hotel Vidgof in Chelyabinsk has an excellent fitness center. Here, each guest will receive a full range of services from independent sports classes to individual training, massage and VIP-service. On the territory of the center there is an ultramodern swimming pool 25 meters long, a Turkish hammam, a sauna, a SPA-procedures salon and massage rooms.

Congress service

The pride of the Grand Hotel Vidgof (Chelyabinsk) is the unique banquet halls and meeting rooms. The capacity of the halls is from ten to three hundred people. All rooms are equipped in accordance with modern innovative technologies, there is a unique simultaneous translation system, high-speed Internet.

Where is the hotel situated

The exact address of the Grand Hotel "Vidgof": Chelyabinsk, Lenin Avenue, building 26 a.

Near the hotel, just a five-minute drive away, there is a city railway station, less than 30 minutes to the airport.

There is an official site of the complex on the Internet where you can find out all the information about its work and get acquainted with its detailed description. Also on the resource there is an opportunity to book a room you like on a date of interest.

Reviews for the Grand Hotel Vidgof (Chelyabinsk)

This five-star hotel complex is known to many residents and guests of the city. Judging by the guests, the hotel fully justifies its category. Very clean, luxurious, with an incredibly beautiful and rich interior. Inside, everything is decorated with marble.

The rooms are spacious, elegant, very high quality, with a good large bed, modern appliances.

Many positive reviews about the restaurant, karaoke bar, spa.

The hotel staff is attentive, friendly, polite. The population passes instantly.

It should be noted the good location of the hotel, in the heart of the city, next to the shopping center and developed infrastructure.

According to many tour operators in Chelyabinsk, "Vidgof" is the best option for accommodation in the city. Everything here is organized at the highest level.

It is also worth noting that the hotel serves good breakfasts. The menu is varied and tasty.

For customers who come by car, there is a spacious and secure parking.

Guests with children can use the services of a qualified babysitter.

Among the shortcomings, customers highlight the lack of a kettle and tea set in the room and smoking in the lobby bar.

Conclusion

Summing up, we can say that both in the opinion of the tour operators of Chelyabinsk and in the words of the hotel guests, in Vidgof, the guests will find really high-class service and excellent service. The hotel is very cozy, beautiful, comfortable, meets all the requirements of modern tourists.
