Understand monsters





In astrophysics, the Compton effect is not used, but the effect opposite to it. With the help of knowledge of this physical phenomenon, it became possible to study the so-called black holes. The inverse Compton effect is that electrons moving with light speed in the corona of a black hole collide with low-energy photons and change their frequency. So scientists can establish the characteristics of a black hole, approximately quantify the processes occurring in a pair of merging black holes.

Black holes are not the only "patient" whose features an astrophysicist can study using the Compton effect. Cluster of galaxies is also judged by this phenomenon, since particles passing by photon sources change their frequency. Also, the background radiation, the ancient voice of the Universe, is exposed to the described effect. Perhaps you can understand this world better if you are fond of astrophysics?
