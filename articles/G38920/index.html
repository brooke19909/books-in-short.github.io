Crocheted dresses for girls: photos and description

With a hook you can knit not only lace napkins, hats and scarves, but also delightful children's dresses - stylish and unusually elegant. DIY, they will highlight and decorate your child and become a favorite part of his wardrobe. No one will have such charming outfits!

In this article, using two beautiful models as an example, we will tell you how to make a crocheted baby dress for girls with your own hands. We will provide a detailed description of the work process and share understandable schemes. We hope you find them useful!

Model No. 1. Warm autumn dress with pockets - hearts

The autumn season has come, which means that it is time to warm up. Let's learn how to knit a cozy children's dress with funny pockets in the shape of hearts. It is perfect for walking in the cool season and will warm your child.

For work, you will need 2 skeins of gray yarn (with a density of 100 g per 270 m) and 1 skein of purple, as well as a hook number 3.5, scissors and 3 buttons. We recommend using crocheted dresses to use acrylic threads with the addition of wool. Perspective Pecherka yarn, LANAGOLD Alize or Alize Alpaca Royalc are perfect.

The size of the finished product is designed for a one-year-old girl. The dress is made from top to bottom, starting from the neck. The coquette is made in rows that do not first fit into the ring, which allows you to make a fastener on the back. Sleeves and lower part knit in a circle. Stage Five: Pockets

Our beautiful crocheted dress is almost done. It remains to make pockets, sew three buttons and make loops for them. We will knit pockets according to the scheme presented below. For work, we use a purple thread.

Model No. 2. Bright orange dress for a girl

We bring to your attention one more simple scheme of a crocheted dress for a girl of 3 years old. A light, delicate and bright product will become your baby's favorite outfit. For work you will need thin yarn made of orange cotton and hook number 2.

We start knitting with a chain of 100 air loops. In the first row, we first perform 1 double crochet (C1H) in 4 stitches from the hook. Next, use the 1 crochet pattern in each second loop to the end. The row ends with a connecting loop (SP).

In the second row we make 3 air loops (VP). And then we work according to the scheme: 1 1 (in the second column with the crochet of the previous row) - 2 VP. We complete the series using the joint venture.

We start the third row with 3 VP. We use the pattern to the end: 1 C1H (in the next column), 1 C1H (in the next loop of the chain), 1 C1H (in the next loop). We end with a connecting loop.

In the fourth row we do 3 VP. Next, we knit according to the scheme 1 1 in the next loop, a chain of 2 VP, we repeat until the end. We complete 1 joint venture. We knit the fifth row by analogy with the third, the sixth - with the fourth. We continue to knit the yoke of the dress alternating the fifth and sixth rows three times.

In the thirteenth row, we carry out a chain of 5 VP and 1 single crochet in the third loop from the hook. We knit so to the end. The layout of the fourteenth row is as follows: a chain of 5 VP, a single crochet in the arch. We knit until the nineteenth row using the pattern of the fourteenth row. Coquette crocheted dress for summer for the girl is ready.
