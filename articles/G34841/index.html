Hotel Estelar Crystal Ormos 3 * - Greek style of peace and quiet

So the long-awaited spring has come. Cherries will bloom soon and leaves on the trees will bloom, hills and valleys will be covered with emerald greenery, and after them the holiday season will come. And the whirlwinds of thoughts spin in the heads of travelers: where to go to relax, which hotel to choose?

Description of the hotel. Numerous tour operators do not always tell the truth about the hotels that offer customers check-in. So in order not to spoil your vacation, you need to listen not only to the travel company, but also the reviews of compatriots who have already visited this hotel. Or, as a last resort, make your own opinion on the description of the hotel on the Internet.

So, for example, consider the hotel of the island of Crete - Estelar Crystal Ormos 3 * . This establishment is located in the village of Ormos, a couple of kilometers from the famous Cretan resort of Agios Nicholas. Situated in a quiet and comfortable place, under the shade of branched olive trees and palm trees, this hotel includes two buildings built in 1986, which were once separate hotels. The number of rooms in both buildings is 85.

The rooms. The room categories of the Estelar Crystal Ormos 3 * hotel are as follows: standard double, triple and quadruple rooms. In each of the rooms the client will find a refrigerator, shower room, hairdryer. Mandatory is the presence of a balcony or terrace. The hotel administration promises that in each room there will be a TV with many satellite channels, broadcasting also Russian programs.



Nutrition. Clients may choose to use all-inclusive meals, that is, breakfast, lunch and dinner, or they may prefer two meals a day. At the same time, each of the options is a buffet, which offers dishes not only of local Greek cuisine, but also international gastronomic delights. There are 2 restaurants, a lobby bar and a cafe.





Beach. Hotel Estelar Crystal Ormos 3 * cannot brag of its own coastal sandy piece. Travelers who decide to opt for this establishment are invited to visit the nearest public sandy beach, located half a kilometer from the hotel and equipped with everything necessary for relaxing along the sea coast. The Estelar Ormos Crystal Hotel also has an outdoor pool, which comfortably houses sun loungers, umbrellas and a bar.





Additional Information. Paid air conditioning in the room is a feature that unites almost all three-star Greek hotels. Turkey, unlike that will be said, does not charge extra for this service. Also paid will be services for the use of wireless Internet and safe. A positive aspect is the presence of a children's playground, a games room, as well as babysitting services. Like many similar establishments, the Estelar Crystal Ormos 3 * hotel gives pleasant surprises to newlyweds who have arrived on their honeymoon on this magical Greek island.

Reviews Of course, the reviews of tourists who visited this hotel are quite diverse. Numerous travelers, accustomed to the brand new three-star hotels, are a little amazed at the rooms of this establishment. Although for the building, which celebrated its 26th anniversary, the rooms are very decent. Some people do not like paid air conditioners. The average rating for this hotel on a five-point scale is 3.7. At the same time, tourists note the excellent work of the staff, delicious and varied food.

It is worth noting that almost all Greek hotels of this category are similar to each other. Therefore, those travelers who expect breathtaking, luxurious relaxation in average Greek hotels may be disappointed. But inexperienced connoisseurs of natural beauty and silence will rejoice at the right choice.
