The recreation center "Ocean" (Andreevka, Primorsky Territory) is ready to receive tourists all year round

The recreation center "Ocean" (Andreevka, Primorsky Territory) is always glad to welcome tourists and vacationers. It operates all year round.

Base location

The Okean camp site is located in the vicinity of Peter the Great Bay in the southeastern part of the Hasan district of Primorye on the shores of Trinity Bay. The surrounding natural landscape is truly unique and inimitable. The bay is just buried in forests and hills.



The weather in the Primorsky Territory has always been attractive in different seasons.
Near the mouth of the Andreevka River is a village of the same name. This settlement has recently been quite actively developing services related to the tourism business.

From Vladivostok, the village of Andreevka is located 220 kilometers. To Khabarovsk, the distance is 845 kilometers.

In total, 800 people can be accommodated on the territory of the base.

Services offered to tourists

The recreation center "Ocean" (Andreevka, Primorsky Territory) overlooks the water surface, it has a sandy beach, so it is very convenient to relax with children here.

Tourists who prefer outdoor activities will also find something to their taste.

Methods of accommodation and meals

The recreation center "Ocean" (Andreevka, Primorsky Territory) has:

  • a four-story hotel with luxury rooms;
  • two two-story family buildings, in which four-bed rooms are located;
  • the whole tent city with two- and four-seater houses.

There is the possibility of accommodation at the base with your pets, for this you should only pay extra.



The recreation center "Ocean" (Andreevka, Primorsky Territory) has a large dining room where vacationers can eat three times a day. They can cook their own meals, for this you can rent a variety of dishes.

There is also the option of purchasing food at a local store.
On the territory of the base there is a large number of barbecue facilities, on which you can always cook independently purchased seafood.

You can also grab a bite to eat in the many mini-cafes, as well as in beer tents.

Holiday Village "Ocean" (Andreevka, Primorsky Territory): prices

Depending on the month of stay at the camp, the cost of living varies. The lowest rates are from November to April. The highest prices in August.

The cost of living in the winter in a double house is 990 rubles per day, in a quadruple room - 2490 rubles, in a quadruple room of the family building 2590 or 2690 rubles, the VIP room of the hotel costs 4490 rubles, a double room on the north side - 1990 rubles, and on the south - 2490 rub.



In August, the rates are as follows: a double house - 2490 rubles, a four-bed house - 4790 rubles, four-bed rooms of family buildings - 4890 or 5590 rubles, a VIP room of the hotel - 9890 rubles, a double room on the north side - 6590 rubles, on the south - 7090 rubles.

In the absence of a ticket, the daily pass to the base is 100 rubles. A chip system has been introduced to enter the base. A deposit of 150 rubles is required to receive the chip.

How can I get to the camp site?

There are several ways to get to the camp site. It is most convenient to travel by bus from Vladivostok or Ussuriysk. Buses run daily.

You can get to Slavyanka by ferry, and from there drive to the camp site by taxi. In addition to the ferry, which runs more than two hours, you can get to Slavyanka by "Comet", spending about an hour on the journey.



If vacationers get to the base "Ocean" by their own vehicles, they should go to the highway from Vladivostok to Ussuriysk and focus on signs located along the road.
