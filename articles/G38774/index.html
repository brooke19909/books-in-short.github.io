How to withdraw money from webmoney

For various reasons, more and more people find additional, or even basic, income on the Internet. Someone works on the currency or stock exchange, someone is engaged in freelance activity, and someone knows how to correctly predict sporting events.

There are many options. But sooner or later, practically every person who is temporarily or constantly engaged in remote work raises the question of withdrawing earned funds. This article will discuss how to withdraw money from WebMoney, since it is this particular settlement system that has received the greatest distribution in the countries of the former Union.

Immediately, we note that most services are available only to those participants who have a wallet certificate not lower than formal, and all information about its owner is verified and confirmed by the system. Therefore, when creating a wallet, it is advisable to enter reliable information so that afterwards you do not have to rack your brains on how to withdraw money from WebMoney.

Withdrawal methods

  1. To Visa or MasterCard

On the WebMoney.Cards website, each participant can link their old card (Mastercard or Visa) to their wallet or order a new one of five types:



- PaySpark EUR MasterCard (currency - EUR, suitable for ATMs with the MasterCard logo of the ATM network);

- PaySpark USD MasterCard (currency - USD, ATM network);

- PaySpark China UnionPay (currency - USD, suitable for ATMs with the UnionPay logo);

- Payoneer USD MasterCard (for withdrawing dollars from a WMZ wallet, ATMs of the ATM network with the MasterCard logo);

- T24 Pay Vault VISA USD (also for withdrawing dollars, but already at ATMs with the VIZA logo)

The transfer lasts no more than two days, and the commission for all operations is 1%.

2. You can also withdraw money from WebMoney using Western Union. The general commission will be 4.3% (Western Union will take 3.5% + 0.8% - Webmoney). The withdrawal time is about two days.

3. You can also cash out your money using a bank transfer. By the way, compared to others, this is a very cheap and very convenient way to withdraw funds. To do this, open a personal account in any bank that allows you to make Internet transfers. Then you need to attach it to the WM system in a special service window. The transfer will take up to 24 hours, and the commission will be equal to 0.6% of the withdrawal amount. At the same time, the Webmoney system does not charge an additional fee.

5. Using the Exchanger exchange. The withdrawal period will depend on the conditions of the selected transaction, and the commission will be from 0.02% to 0.05%.

6. Through a bank card. First you need to attach the card to your work wallet and pass its verification. It should be noted that you can display on the card only in your name.
