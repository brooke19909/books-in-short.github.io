LCD "Odintsovo Park": address, developer, reviews. New buildings of Odintsovo

Own apartment is the dream of all Muscovites. After a hard and tiring day, you really want to go to your own, not a rented apartment. Yes, the rental price of real estate in Moscow is fabulous. Even if you do not have the funds to purchase an apartment, place your purchase on a mortgage basis. Bondage? But you must admit, it is better to give 30,000 - 40,000 each month for your housing than to enrich the owner of a prestigious property. In addition, no one is insured after several years of living in the selected rented apartment to literally stay on the street. Apartments within the capital are impermissible luxury. That is why the complexes and entire neighborhoods that are actively rebuilding in the suburbs are very popular. The cost of apartments here is an order of magnitude lower, while residents do not lose access to the developed capital infrastructure and, if necessary, can reach any point in a matter of moments. Odintsovo Park is a comfort class residential complex that has already attracted the attention of many. Our task is to give the project the most objective assessment within the framework of this material.





about the project

New buildings in Odintsovo are currently impressive in their diversity. The largest developers in Moscow and the Moscow Region rebuild excellent complexes and residential quarters here that meet all modern requirements. I must admit that the Odintsovo district, namely the direction of the Minsk highway, is currently breaking all records for the sale of real estate.

The project provides for the construction of 10 buildings with variable number of storeys, where apartments with a variety of planning solutions are presented. Customers are offered one, two and three bedroom apartments to choose from. All buildings are built using monolithic technology in the southern part of the city. In the process of individual development, the developer paid great attention to the mutual arrangement of the houses - each apartment will be provided with a maximum of daylight, and from the windows a picturesque view of the beauty of the surroundings will open. For all residents of the metropolis, tired of the hustle and bustle of endless noise, the opportunity to finally feel the solitude and tranquility is priceless. If you have been looking for an apartment in an ecologically clean area for a long time, pay special attention to the Odintsovo Park residential complex. The developer is the Gratis company, which has managed to establish itself well in the Moscow real estate market and to implement several excellent projects.





Location

Surely you are thinking about how attractive the living area of ​​the Odintsovo Park residential complex is. The developer really offered an excellent option: the Odintsovo district is currently being actively built up. The secret of its popularity is extremely simple: it is one of the most developed and environmentally safe areas of the Moscow Region. And the cost of apartments here is much lower than in the capital. In the vicinity of the new buildings there is a sufficient number of sports and fitness complexes, a little north of the premium health resort "Barvikha", where you can not only improve your health, but also have a great time.

Technology

If you are looking for a project that embodies modern technology and innovation, you will definitely like Odintsovo Park LCD. Reviews of the first buyers and all those who managed to visit the construction site focus on excellent planning decisions. Rational placement of load-bearing structures allows each tenant to adapt typical layouts to their personal preferences and wishes. Each entrance is equipped with high-speed silent elevators, including freight.





The use of hinged ventilated facades for facing each building increases the operational capabilities of buildings, and also allows solving energy-saving issues with maximum efficiency.

Transport accessibility

The issue of transport accessibility is relevant for all new buildings located outside the MKAD. Many work in Moscow, attend cultural events, metropolitan parks and squares, so I would like to get to any point of the capital without problems, even in the absence of personal transport.

LCD "Odintsovo Park" (9 microdistrict) is really well located, which is confirmed by all customers. If your family has a car, then you need to drive only 8 km in the direction of the Minsk highway, and you are there. Buses and minibuses depart from the metro stations "Kievskaya" and "Victory Park" with enviable frequency.





Of course, many are interested in knowing how the situation is on the roads. So, car owners focus on complex traffic interchanges, collecting quite large traffic jams. But according to local authorities and the developer himself, in the near future the reconstruction of the Minsk highway, the organization of their own exits, which will help solve the problem.

Infrastructure

LCD "Odintsovo Park" (Odintsovo) offers all residents access to their own infrastructure. On a territory of 12 hectares there is everything necessary for a full and comfortable life - its own multi-level parking, a kindergarten, a comprehensive school, a shopping and entertainment center, grocery and grocery stores, as well as well-equipped areas for walking and recreation.

Currently, most of the buildings have been commissioned and settled, so you can summarize the first results. So, the first residents emphasize how large-scale the project turned out, what a huge territory it occupied. The houses are in relation to each other at an optimal distance, which allows to maintain the unity of each resident and a beautiful view of the beauty of the city. Of course, not all infrastructure facilities were put into operation, but soon the problem will be completely resolved as they are built up. In the meantime, residents of new buildings can take advantage of the city's infrastructure.





Apartments, layouts

Residential complex "Odintsovo Park" offers a choice of one-, two- and three-room apartments, represented by a variety of planning solutions. Among them, everyone will find the best option for themselves: a small cozy one-room apartment for a young family who is just starting to live separately from their parents, a comfortable “kopeck piece” with a spacious kitchen, isolated rooms and a separate bathroom. Well, the lucky owners of a large and friendly family should pay attention to the excellent three-room apartments with thoughtful layouts and an additional bathroom for the comfort of all family members.

Issue price

And, of course, an important indicator for real estate is its value. Buy an apartment in the LCD "Odintsovo Park" at a price of 3.4 million rubles. The developer offers excellent conditions for the acquisition of real estate, the ability to attract maternity capital, military mortgages. And cooperation with the largest banks in the country allows you to arrange a mortgage with maximum benefit and minimum overpayment.





To summarize

In general, the Odintsovo Park residential complex is an excellent project that combines all the advantages of urban and suburban life. If you have been looking for an apartment in the Moscow Region for a long time, but are not ready to overpay and spend a huge amount of time on the road to the capital, this option is perfect for you. Moreover, at present, an apartment in a new residential neighborhood can be purchased at a discount. Do not miss your chance, pay attention to the best new buildings in Odintsovo.
