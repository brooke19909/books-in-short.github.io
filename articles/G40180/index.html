Community socialism, which arose in the middle of the 19th century in Russia, became a clear example of the situation that prevailed at that time. It was A.I. Many years of experience in the community system led to the emergence of a whole range of measures that supported the ruined farms. For the peasant community were characteristic features of the redistribution of land, mutual responsibility, collective decision-making.

On the basis of a philosophical definition of what socialism is, a political ideology was formulated that put forward as a goal a society where there is no exploitation of man, and justice and social equality are affirmed. Having discarded national specifics, this concept has long been defined as a form of social organization, where the main productive means, as well as land, belong to the state, which organizes farming. In addition, it deals with the distribution of labor products in conjunction with the principle: "from each according to his ability, to each according to his work."
