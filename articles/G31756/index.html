Repairing an electric stove is expensive. How to choose a high-quality household appliance

Today, perhaps, no kitchen can be imagined without a powerful and modern electric stove. At the same time, when choosing a device, Russians prefer completely different manufacturers of household appliances. Bosch electric stoves are in great demand. The thing is that this manufacturer is considered one of the most famous giants in the world, producing a variety of equipment, without which it is simply impossible to imagine the life of Russians.

About malfunctions

But no matter how perfect your stove is, it is likely that after a certain period of operation it will require after-sales service, or even a thorough repair. Of course, this often applies to technology that has already served for a single year. And nevertheless, sooner or later you still have to turn to specialists for help in order to eliminate the malfunction. Repairing an electric stove can include two types of actions - this is either correcting the malfunction of the constituent elements that are responsible for heating the frying surfaces, or correcting any malfunctions that arose in the control unit.





In the spirit of the era

Apparently, pursuing the goal of keeping up with technological progress, or maybe just succumbing to the powerful marketing policies of manufacturers, most Russians buy electric stoves on which a touch control panel is installed. Is it good? How justified is such an acquisition? And will the presence of a touch panel affect the amount that will cost you repairing an electric stove?

We advise you to weigh all the pros and cons of such a technical filling before buying. This is especially true for people who are avid cooks and plan to use the stove in maximum load mode. Violation of the correct operation of the touch control unit is perhaps the most common cause of equipment failure. However, such a malfunction can be completely eliminated. True, repairing an electric stove with a touch panel will cost more than in the case of simpler analogues. If a mechanical control system breaks down, be sure to replace the complete set of switches.





How much does an electric stove repair cost?

We bring to your attention the approximate cost of servicing household appliances. It is worth noting that all of the amounts indicated below do not include the purchase of spare parts. They are paid separately. The estimated cost of repair work, the master, as a rule, calls only after you describe to him the malfunction of the electric stove by phone. Also we draw your attention that the cost of repair work for different models of electric stoves can vary significantly. The article provides only approximate amounts.



For minor repairs, which will not include disassembly of equipment, you will have to pay about 600 rubles. It may include the replacement of a blown bulb, an inoperative switch, button, and so on. Work requiring partial disassembly of a household appliance will cost more. Here you will have to lay out an average of 800 to 1200 rubles. In this case, the wizard will replace the surface of the desktop, install a new thermostat, and so on. The cost of a complete disassembly of an electric stove ranges from 1300 to 1600 rubles. Perhaps the malfunction, the elimination of which will require the greatest costs, is a breakdown of the electronic unit. In the event of the expiration of the warranty period, repair will cost the consumer an amount of up to 50% of the cost of the entire household appliance. Diagnostic works on imported electric stoves cost about 600 rubles. As a rule, money is payable in case of refusal to repair or when it is simply unprofitable.

Choosing an electric stove is a responsible matter

In order not to bring the matter to repair at all, extremely carefully and responsibly approach the issue of choosing an electric stove. After you decide which manufacturer to give your preference to, you will have to decide on a whole range of parameters. For example, how many burners do you need? Most often there are either 2 or 6. However, the most optimal option is 4. The burners usually differ from each other in size and shape, as well as in the speed that is necessary for heating, and here, of course, the power of a household appliance is not the last thing .



Electric cookers often have automatic burners. They are able to determine the temperature. This is simply necessary in order not to be distracted by the control of the process of boiling water - a small heating device will first work at maximum, and then, when the water reaches a boil, it will automatically reduce the power level. The choice of electric stoves today is simply huge. When buying a household appliance, proceed from your individual preferences, and, of course, financial capabilities. Only then can you make the right decision.
