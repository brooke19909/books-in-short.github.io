Pension "Akvaloo": reviews and description

On the territory of Greater Sochi, in a small resort village is a boarding house "Akvaloo", offering vacationers comfortable accommodation and a range of health promotion measures. The facility is located on the territory of an ultramodern water park operating all year round.



Aqualoo reviews are recommended as an institution for families, as the hotel complex offers a huge amount of entertainment for kids. The institution has a very developed infrastructure, as well as high-quality service, which is not inferior to the best European hotels. On the territory of the complex there are restaurants and bars where you can get to know the Caucasian cuisine more closely or prefer traditional European dishes. In addition, guests of the boarding house can visit a beauty salon or a hairdresser, massage or beauty parlor, a medical center.

Sanatorium "Akvaloo" offers vacationers a range of procedures to promote health and treat various diseases. The unique subtropical climate of this area is recommended for patients with respiratory diseases and to strengthen the cardiovascular system. The institution employs medical specialists in various industries who can give competent advice and recommendations for the prevention of the development of diseases.





The resort complex "Akvaloo" reviews are called one of the best institutions of a similar orientation in this corner of Sochi. It offers covered parking, where you can leave your transport. On the territory of the institution there is an ATM and a terminal for receiving payments. The boarding house has shops that offer original souvenirs, beach items, clothing and products. It offers guests a tour desk, assistance is provided in the acquisition of railways, cars and airline tickets. You can rent a car, which will greatly facilitate movement around the area.
