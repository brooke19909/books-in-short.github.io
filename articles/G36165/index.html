Rest on Seliger with a tent: where to stay?

Seliger is the name of a complex of lakes that are of glacial origin. This natural zone is located in the Novgorod and Tver regions of Russia. In another way, the territory of Seliger is also called Ostashkovsky, which corresponds to the city of Ostashkov, which stands on the shore of the lake. This place has gained popularity among those who like to relax within the country. Rest on Seliger with a tent is interesting and, most importantly, affordable.

It is not hard to get here, in addition, the infrastructure for tourists is developing more and more every year. Those who prefer a secluded pastime in nature also like to relax on Seliger. There are enough people here who come, as they say, "savage", set up entire camps or settle alone.





Camping on Seliger with tents

If this is your first visit to this region, this may be the most suitable vacation option. On the shore of the lakes there are many campsites where you can find a variety of tourist services. According to visitors' reviews, rest on Seliger with a tent has both pros and cons. Although this issue remains purely individual and depends on the personal preferences of travelers.

If we talk about the benefits of such a vacation, then this is the presence of convenient parking and relatively comfortable conditions in campsites. There is access to electricity, a place for tents, dining rooms and a landscaped area.

Keep in mind that camps live, which, obviously, not alone, but by tourist groups. To some this may seem an advantage, but for some the disadvantage of such a holiday on Seliger.

Among the minuses should be called the need to pay for a tent (from 150 to 400 rubles per day for one), however, such a small fee is rather charged for the convenience of living.

How to find a campsite? Just search for suitable services in local forums.





Seliger: where to stay with a tent

If you do not want to live in a campsite and prefer to pitch your own tents on the lake coast, then choose the following options. In the case when a visitor travels by car, it is best to find a tourist base or hotel, which has a decent parking with security. Leave the car there and look for a secluded place on the shore. Thus, the car will not become a burden, and you can rest easy. But even if necessary, you will always have the opportunity to use it.





Khachin Island

If you are interested in a truly secluded vacation "savage", then most likely you will not be able to travel by car. One of these places is the island of Khachin. On the island you can choose a free place for a tent. But keep in mind that in summer most of the convenient sites will be occupied.

Holidays on the Lakes Savage

Is it possible to rest on Seliger with a tent savage? Of course, you can do without hotels and parking. Just find a suitable site and put your tent. Thus, you will get rid of the need to pay for anything, except for what you really need or want, for example, food and entertainment. But if vacationers choose this option, then it is still recommended to find a site in relative proximity to tourist centers. So you get a convenient entrance and the benefits of using local infrastructure, as well as even more comfortable camping places. Simply put, there is a more refined territory and convenient landmarks for travelers.





How to find a secluded place?

Rest on Seliger with a tent still has one contradictory drawback. The most comfortable places can be occupied by tourists, as well as be owned or serviced by nearby hotels and camp sites. Therefore, you risk not getting the long-awaited secluded vacation.

The way out of this situation may be the following: look for more inaccessible areas on the lakeshore, communicate with local people and spend a certain amount of time searching for them on the Internet.

If you decide that having neighbors is not a problem for you, then this question is removed. But if this presents difficulties for you, then think about its solution.

Seliger is a whole lake complex. Among them are Sig, Peno and Volgo, etc. On those lakes that are not central, there are always fewer vacationers, and sometimes there are practically no people. Therefore, in remote areas of the coast, the likelihood of being alone with nature is much higher.
