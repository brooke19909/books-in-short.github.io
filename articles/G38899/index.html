Golden Evpatoria. Rest with a child



The history of the development of Evpatoria has about 25 centuries. The city is named after the Bosporan king Mithridates Eupator (132-63 BC), and translated from Greek "eupator" means "noble". When the healing properties of mud were discovered in the 19th century, Yevpatoriya slowly gained the fame of a famous and popular place. To date, the Crimean resort city proudly bears the title of children's Ukrainian health resort.

Evpatoria is considered one of the best children's health resorts on the entire Black Sea coast. In this place, nature itself seemed to take care of the little guests. The conditions that nature generously endowed with the surrounding areas allowed the city to be included in the list of favorite places for family vacations. Evpatoria ... A vacation with a child spent on sandy beaches, as well as a shallow sea, combined with a mild climate, healing mud and mineral springs - all this will leave an unforgettable experience.



The advantages of the territorial location of Evpatoria are the southeastern coast of Crimea, which excludes the cold water flow in the June-July month. The swimming season begins in mid-May and lasts until the second half of October. There are no storms and sharp temperature drops. The climate of the city is somewhat reminiscent of the climate of France and northern Italy, and thanks to this, in the city of Yevpatoria, a vacation with a child proceeds without pronounced acclimatization.

In Evpatoria, there are a huge number of recreation centers, boarding houses, resorts. Here is the largest number of health facilities for children. Having a bright wellness orientation, Evpatoria is a worthy healing resort with medical facilities that have no analogues in the world! Here they tried to preserve the best traditions of domestic balneology, and in combination with modern technologies in health resorts help children of all ages heal.

Bright and unforgettable impressions will give Evpatoria! Holidays with children (reviews of many vacationers indicate excellent living conditions) will be held in comfort and convenience. They return to these places again and again, thanks to the hospitality, service and special atmosphere of home comfort, which can create worthy competition to other places for recreation. Soul Evpatoria - a vacation with a child will be warm and not cause much trouble. You have a chance to see this!

Having decided to spend your vacation in the Crimean territory, you thereby ensure a cheerful and emotional rest for the child. Boredom is inappropriate here! The resort is literally saturated with entertainment. The famous dolphinarium, an aquarium, a zoo and a fairytale street filled with characters from children's fairy tales will already deliver many joyful minutes. The coast is literally strewn with children's attractions: cars, carousels, slot machines, etc. Various water activities are also offered, such as riding a banana, a boat, a catamaran, a scooter, a water bike.
