Where are the best beaches of Kabardinka

Many Russians do not know not only which Kabardinka beaches are better, but also where such a settlement is located. And this Black Sea resort is located in our country, not far from Novorossiysk and only fourteen kilometers from Gelendzhik. The smooth coastline of the Tsemess Bay ends at Cape Doob in the south of the city. Kabardinka seemed to rest on the steps of a giant green amphitheater, steps descending to the sea. This town is not very ancient and has existed since 1836, when a Russian military fort was built here.



The natural and climatic conditions of Kabardinka are ideal for both relaxation and treatment. People come here to treat the bronchi and lungs with mountain air. The tourist infrastructure of this resort is very developed, and you can easily find housing to your taste - from the simplest to the most fashionable hotels. By the way, this is the only five-star hotel located in the only Gelendzhiks area so far. But this is not what distinguishes the town from other resorts. The coast, as if specially created for a comfortable stay, is what Kabardinka is rightly proud of. The photo of the beach of this Russian resort is not much different from the advertising pictures from the pages of tourist brochures of the resorts of Italy or Montenegro. The tourist season lasts at this resort for seven months a year. This is all thanks to the mountain range that protects the bay from the icy Novorossiysk pine forest. The beaches of Kabardinka are mostly pebble. The central city is well equipped, but, unfortunately, in the high season it is full of people. Therefore, if you do not like a large society of your own kind, it is better to go further from the entrance. It will be calmer there. But this place is still worth a visit, if only for the sake of the beautiful city promenade with a cafe.


The nudist beaches of Kabardinka are located right behind Cape Doob. We can say that this recreation area, hidden from prying eyes behind juniper bushes, is considered one of the most famous among lovers of naturist recreation throughout the Krasnodar Territory. It is very comfortable there, because you can always find a shadow under fragrant pine trees or behind juniper thickets. To get to the nudist recreation area, you need to overcome the cape, go down to the water and walk along the sea for another quarter hour.



The wild beaches of Kabardinka begin almost immediately after Cape Penai (it is closer to Novorossiysk). There are high shores in this place, but you can always go down the ladders to the water edge outlined by natural pebbles. True, there is no point of rescuers on such a coast, so a few vacationers are left to their own devices. In the resort there is no problem to find secluded areas with medium pebbles or among the rocks. But there are also many zones with very small pebbles, a gentle slope to the sea and a sandy bottom - Kabardinka is famous for them. There is also a sandy beach here, but closer to Gelendzhik. And spacious areas with light gray sand (and not only in the water, but also on the shore) can be found right in the center of the city, surprisingly.
