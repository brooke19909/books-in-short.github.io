Automotive compressors "Aggressor" - power and quality



A compressor is a device that is several times more powerful than a traditional pump and which is an absolutely indispensable thing for any car owner. This device is characterized by high power and performance, as well as compact dimensions and the absence of noise. Currently, a large number of a wide variety of models are presented on the automotive market, however, Aggressor compressors are a model of truly advanced technologies, each detail of which is carefully thought out. And if you need to purchase this kind of equipment for your car, then devices of this brand will be the best option.

Compressor specifications



Considering the technical characteristics possessed by Aggressor compressors, it should first be said that, basically, they are made of an aluminum alloy having very high strength. In addition, it is worth noting their low weight. On average, it is about five kilograms. As for the case material, it has a rather high thermal conductivity, which means that the Aggressor automobile compressor will cool down very quickly after use. In addition, company specialists place particular emphasis on paint coating equipment. It is able to easily withstand any sudden changes in temperature that occur on the surface of the device. Also, absolutely all components are connected to the compressor with special hardened studs that are completely resistant to corrosion. The device receives power from the car battery by connecting with copper clamps. This charging method is currently the most optimal.





Advantages of Aggressor compressors

As for the list of the main advantages possessed by Aggressor compressors, for starters it should be said about their multifunctionality. Using them, you can not only pump the car wheel, but also thanks to the various nozzles that come with the kit - an inflatable boat, a ball or a mattress. Such a device will become a faithful companion during any trips to nature or to the country. It is also worth mentioning that quite often a special hose is supplied with the compressor, the length of which is more than seven meters. Thanks to him, you can pump all four wheels without transferring equipment. In addition, the fact that "Aggressor" automobile compressors, reviews of which are only the most positive, are the best for tires, is confirmed by a high-precision pressure gauge mounted in the body of the device. Due to this, the cost of equipment of this brand quickly pays off due to the fact that the tires with the same pressure on all wheels do not wear out longer and less fuel is consumed.

How to choose a good compressor

When choosing the most optimal compressor, you must pay attention to the inflation speed of the tire. As a rule, the wheel only has to be slightly pumped up, which means that a device with an automatic shutdown function will be the best solution. In addition, it is worth paying special attention to the power of the device and its ultimate pressure. The higher these indicators, the faster the Aggressor compressors will be able to pump the wheel. Also, one should not forget that a properly working device should practically not emit sounds or vibrations. And finally, the last thing you should definitely pay attention to is the warranty period of the service. It must be at least one year old.
