Hotel Le Mirage New Tower Hotel Resort 4 *: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Hotel Le Mirage New Tower Hotel Resort 4 * is a cozy hotel complex, located very close to the Red Sea. The resort fully justifies its high status and gives its guests an unforgettable vacation. Perhaps only here you can see such a beautiful coral reef.

general information

Hotel Le Mirage New Tower Hotel Resort 4 * belongs to a fairly well-known family of resort hotels Alba Club Tower Bay, which is widely advertised in the tourism sector and enjoys a good reputation. It is worth noting that this resort complex received its current name only in 2012, and before that the hotel changed several more brands.

The hotel is relatively small and has only 175 rooms, which can be called a fairly modest indicator. The rich history of the hotel, which dates back to 1998, is in no way associated with disrepair, because more recently (in 2014) all buildings and facilities underwent major repairs.





Where is the hotel

Due to the fact that the Hotel Hotel Le Mirage New Tower Hotel Resort 4 * is located on a certain hill, from the windows of the rooms, as well as from its entire territory, a beautiful view opens. The tourist area Tower Bay, where this hotel complex is located, is located 18 km from the international airport and only 5 km from the so-called Old Town. The same distance separates the hotel from the famous and picturesque Naama Bay. Depending on the building in which you live, you will have to walk from 200 to 300 meters along a convenient staircase to your own beach.

Guest apartments

Le Mirage New Tower 4 * (Ex Tower Bay Resort Spa) has 175 rooms in its room stock (a relatively small number), which can conditionally be divided into several categories:

  • Standard apartments are 42 square meters. m and consist of only one room, in which a double bed and a folding armchair can accommodate three guests (plus extra beds). The rooms, which overlook the pool or the sea, are equipped with a balcony or terrace, the same apartments that are facing the hotel garden do not have such an option.
  • The Junior Suite is much like standard rooms, but it consists of two bedrooms that can accommodate 4 people. It is possible to choose a room with a view of the pool or the sea coast.
  • Executive suites are cozy, detached bungalows that are perfect for family or romantic getaways. It has a bedroom and a living room, as well as its own cozy terrace. This room can accommodate three people, as well as one in an extra bed.




Room features

Comfort for guests is one of the most important rules that guide Le Mirage New Tower 4 * (Ex Tower Bay Resort Spa) in its work. In this regard, great attention was paid to equipping the rooms with all kinds of amenities, the list of which is as follows:

  • own balcony or exit to the terrace (with the exception of those apartments with windows overlooking the garden);
  • TV with a large selection of popular Russian channels;
  • air conditioning tied to a general temperature control system in the hotel;
  • mini bar;
  • individual safe, for the use of which no additional cash is charged;
  • the bathroom also has a hairdryer;
  • international telephone;
  • wireless Internet is available for a fee;
  • Upon your request, children's furniture can be provided, as well as a set that includes an electric kettle and a service.

Infrastructure facilities

Reviews of the hotel Le Mirage New Tower 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) indicate the presence of the following infrastructure facilities on its territory:

  • a large tennis court with a soft coating of the ground (this eliminates serious injuries in the fall);
  • a beauty salon where you can get the services of professional hairdressers, cosmetologists and masters of nail service;
  • a doctor takes a few times a week (an emergency call is also possible);
  • thanks to the modern laundry you can not worry about the cleanliness of your belongings;
  • an internet club as well as a wireless network covering the entire hotel.




Entertainment

The cost of the resort tour to the hotel Le Mirage New Tower 4 * ("Le Mirage New Tower") includes the following opportunities for entertainment and relaxation:

  • use of a tennis court;
  • ping pong game;
  • beach volleyball field;
  • regular and water aerobics in the company with a sports animator;
  • visit to a disco (drinks are paid separately);
  • participation in entertainment and show programs organized by a team of animators.




Some services and recreational activities require an additional payment. These services at the Le Mirage New Tower Hotel in Sharm El Sheikh (Egypt) include the following:

  • visits to the fitness center and gym;
  • spa services, among which the most in demand are the sauna and relaxing massage;

Schedule of restaurants and bars

Much attention is paid to the full and high-quality nutrition of tourists at the Le Mirage New Tower 4 * hotel (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh). Guest reviews highly appreciate the work of restaurants and bars operating in the territory of this hotel complex. Hotel facilities operate according to the following schedule:

  • Va Bene is a traditional Italian restaurant that invites guests to dine on delicious pizza or pasta prepared according to a signature recipe. The institution is open only in the evening and can accommodate up to 50 guests at a time. When planning dinner in this restaurant, it is worth considering that it does not work on Fridays.
  • Aqua Star is a restaurant specializing in seafood dishes. The schedule of his work is similar to the previous restaurant, and the capacity is also 50 people. It should be borne in mind that before visiting the institution you must definitely book a table at the reservation desk, which is open all day. A pleasant surprise is that each of the guests can visit this restaurant absolutely free once a week;
  • The pool bar , which is open from 10 a.m. until midnight, daily generously treats its guests with drinks, a variety of snacks and several varieties of ice cream. In winter, the duration of his work is reduced to half-past six in the evening.
  • Billiard room combined with a bar. The schedule of her work, like most of these institutions from 10 am to midnight. Additional payment is required only for a game of billiards, while drinks and snacks, however, are included in the scope of the all-inclusive system.
  • The beach bar opens at 10 a.m. and is open throughout the day. The assortment of treats is absolutely similar to the bar that functions near the pool.
  • La Perla is the main restaurant of the hotel, which has a large hall that can accommodate up to 250 people. Breakfasts, lunches and dinners begin at 7:00, 13:00 and 19:00, respectively. Three hours are allotted for each meal. Guests should be advised that visits to this establishment are not permitted in bathing suits. Also, men should not come to the restaurant in shorts, and women in too open and overt clothes. The same rule applies to other institutions of this class.




Positive hotel reviews

Many tourists from all over the world have already visited the Le Mirage New Tower Resort 4 * hotel. Reviews help to create an objective picture of ideas about the hotel, composed of independent opinions. So, from the positive moments of relaxation in this hotel, the following can be noted:

  • it is convenient that each room has its own safe with a combination lock;
  • good food, a variety of lunches and dinners, the assortment and quality of pastries deserves special praise;
  • a beautiful well-kept beach with picturesque corals;
  • many palm trees and flowers throughout the hotel;
  • throughout the hotel it is quite quiet and calm, which makes it simply ideal for family trips, as well as for relaxing with young children;
  • almost all members of the staff to one degree or another speak Russian;
  • tourists arriving at the hotel early in the morning, long before check-in, are offered breakfast.




Comments and complaints about the hotel

For a long time, the Le Mirage New Tower 4 * hotel (Sharm el Sheikh) has established itself in the tourism sector. Reviews of tourists contain not only positive comments and gratitude, but also some comments and complaints about this hotel complex:

  • during the cold season, rooms are practically not heated, which is especially felt at night;
  • the number of towels provided per person does not match the stardom of the hotel;
  • for breakfast, the same assortment of products will be added every day;
  • the service does not match the price of the tour;
  • problems with hot water in the rooms;
  • guests are offered little entertainment, and the work of the animators is also not satisfactory;
  • in the dining room are large lines;
  • poor quality of cleaning in the rooms, as well as hotel employees are in no hurry to change the guests bed and update towels;
  • in the main restaurant, dishes are poorly washed, and cases of dilution of drinks with running tap water have been noticed;
  • for unknown reasons, guests are not provided with cosmetic sets for the shower.

We can say that at this life stage of the hotel there is something like a crisis period. The management should seriously work both on the general condition of the number of rooms and on the quality of staff work. Nevertheless, if you do not belong to the category of whimsical tourists and do not pay attention to trifles, then this option is quite suitable for you, especially since the hotel‚Äôs own beach can be considered one of the best on the coast.
