"Galaxy" - water park (Krasnaya Polyana): water activities in a ski resort

Krasnaya Polyana is a famous ski resort located near the Olympic capital of Sochi. Not all tourists know that visiting these parts will be interesting not only for lovers of active winter sports. Modern hotels, entertainment and health centers, as well as its own water park. Krasnaya Polyana will also delight you with picturesque natural landscapes and a pleasant microclimate.

Water extravaganza all year round!



Until recently, the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory and the Caucasus were considered a place for summer holidays. But with the development of infrastructure in the mountainous areas, local sports bases and tourist complexes operating year-round have become very popular. What is especially nice, in the ski resorts you can not only go skiing. Now you do not need to choose between a vacation at sea or in the mountains. After skiing, every tourist can visit the water park. Krasnaya Polyana is a ski resort, several years ago it was here that the galactic social and cultural center was built. Among other entertainments, in its territory there is also a zone of water attractions. The main investor in the project of the social and cultural center was Gazprom. To date, the complex has been equipped and receives guests daily: an ice arena, a cinema, a bowling alley and a billiard club, as well as a variety of shops. "Galaxy" water park (Krasnaya Polyana): slides and pools



Fans of extreme sensations will have to be upset, in the center of water entertainment in Krasnaya Polyana there are only 4 large slides. But there are only twelve different pools, many of them have fountains, geysers and hydromassage systems. There is a water area for the youngest visitors to the complex.

Want to swim in the pool or relax in the jacuzzi while admiring the mountain scenery? And this desire will be fulfilled by the Galaxy water park. Krasnaya Polyana is a very picturesque place, the water entertainment complex boasts an open summer area, which every guest can visit in the warm season. The water in the pool and jacuzzi is heated to a comfortable temperature, which means that you can enjoy the mountain views, getting indescribable pleasure. Also in the water park you can visit a choice of the Finnish sauna or hammam.

Rules for guests



It is forbidden to bring your own drinks and food to the territory of the water entertainment complex. If you wish, you can have a snack in a cafe located on the territory. Children under the age of 3 years must be constantly supervised by their parents. Bathing of the youngest visitors to the center is allowed only in special diapers for swimming. Waterpark "Galaxy" (Krasnaya Polyana) is a place for families. However, not all attractions are suitable for riding children taller than 140 centimeters.

Cost of visiting

The water park is open daily from 10.00 to 23.00. For the convenience of visitors, several tariffs apply. You can choose the option of visiting the water entertainment complex for 2 hours, half a day or all day. Children up to 100 centimeters in height attend the water park for free. Children's tickets are intended for children whose growth is 100-140 centimeters. Teenagers above 140 centimeters visit the center of water entertainment at adult rates. The cost of a full day for children is 800 rubles. And an adult daily subscription can be purchased for 1000 rubles.

"Galaxy" water park (Sochi, Krasnaya Polyana): visitor reviews



Many tourists vacationing in Sochi and neighboring settlements seek to visit the indoor water entertainment center in Krasnaya Polyana. Most guests experience extremely positive emotions from such a holiday. The complex really has a lot of pools and a variety of attractions. Children are not bored here either. However, it is worth noting that it is unlikely that this water park will appeal to those who like to ride a lot and choose the most extreme.

A water entertainment center is located in a ski resort. It is nice to relax after skiing on snow-covered slopes and just swim heartily in the cold season. But fans of extreme rides here can be bored. In general, the Galaxy is a modern, well-equipped and clean water park. Krasnaya Polyana is a resort that most tourists come to during the cold season.
