Hotel Belle Vue Hotel Beldibi 3 *, Turkey: description and reviews

Beldibi resort, formerly belonging to the Kemer zone, and now annexed to Konyalty district, is certainly one of the most popular tourist destinations in Turkey. The first thing that catches your eye here is the magnificent nature. In Beldibi, vacationers can admire small green bays, pine-covered mountains, banana groves and, of course, the turquoise blue sea. The resort infrastructure in Beldibi, hosting thousands of tourists annually, is developed simply superbly. There are, among other things, comfortable hotels here. For example, vacationers can rent rooms in Beldibi Hotel Belle Vue Hotel Beldibi 3 *.

Do tourists find the location of the complex convenient?

This hotel was once built on the side of a mountain, above the only road in Beldibi to Oludeniz. And although this hotel is not located on the first coastline, the views from its windows, from a height, open, of course, simply magnificent. The distance from the Belle Vue Hotel Beldibi 3 * to Kemer is only 15 km. That is, tourists can get to this city by public transport in just 20 minutes. Hotel guests do not even need to go to a stop for this. In order to get on the bus heading to Kemer, you just need to raise your hand. The driver will definitely stop in this case.





The distance from the hotel to the beach is approximately 300 m. That is, in terms of the sea, this complex is located quite conveniently.

Tourists who rent rooms at this hotel usually fly to Turkey at Antalya Airport. The distance from this transport hub to the hotel is approximately 55 km. That is, the future guests of the complex need to go by operator’s transfer for at least an hour.

Tourists who rented rooms at the hotel themselves can get to Kemer from Antalya by bus No. 178 or by taxi. Getting from this city to Beldibi is not difficult.

Hotel Features

The Belle Vue Hotel Beldibi 3 * hotel was built in Kemer, in Beldibi it was almost two decades ago - in 2000. However, since then it has undergone several reconstructions. The last repair in this hotel was done in 2013 (for 2017).

By the standards of Turkey, this hotel is relatively small. The total area of ​​its territory is 2000 m2 and the rooms for rent are only 59. This hotel accepts vacationers in one four-story building with facades decorated in Turkish style. Unfortunately, there is no elevator in this complex.

The courtyard of the hotel is fenced with a low fence of blocks and is almost completely occupied by the pool. The "southern" design of the complex is provided by tropical greenery planted along the hedges and flower beds.





This hotel operates, despite the fact that it belongs only to the triz class in the riding class, according to the all-inclusive system. On the city beach, the Belle Vue Hotel Beldibi 3 * hotel (Kemer, Beldibi) has its own private part. In addition to cash, the hotel accepts cards.
