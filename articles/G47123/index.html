Via Card Reader

Another way to upload music to Android is to use Card Reader. What it is? This is a special device thanks to which your personal computer can see the memory card (Micro SD).

What do I need to do? First, download all the necessary music to your computer, for example, in a separate folder, then insert your memory card (Micro SD) into the Card Reader and connect it to the computer. Ultimately, your computer will see a memory card, and you can transfer to it all the selected music.

What should be taken into account? One of the most important and unchanging rules is to first disconnect Card Reader from the computer and only then pull it out of the system unit. Otherwise, the memory card may be severely damaged or even become unusable.

It is also worth noting that not all computers are equipped with a slot for Card Reader. Therefore, make sure that it is on your PC in advance.
