Hotel Luciano (Kazan): description, reviews, photos

A rather attractive accommodation option can be considered the Hotel Luciano. Kazan is one of the most beautiful and unusual cities in Russia, in which several cultures harmoniously intertwine. Naturally, this place attracts tourists not only from neighboring regions, but also from near and far abroad.

Short hotel description

Associated with luxury and a high level of service is the melodic name of the Hotel Luciano. Kazan will give you even more pleasant and unforgettable experiences. Since this institution specializes in spa treatments, you will have access to several saunas and pools. You can also not interrupt your usual workouts, because the hotel has a modern gym.

The design and equipment of the rooms is of great importance. You can rest assured that the "Luciano" apartments are in the best condition. There is all the necessary equipment and furniture, but a special emphasis needs to be done precisely on the interior. The color scheme is dominated by green shades that create an atmosphere of calm and comfort. Gold decor elements also attract attention.

Guests at this hotel are invited to the chic signature restaurant. Every morning you are kindly invited to breakfast, and during the day you can order delicious dishes from the Italian menu. In the evening, a bar opens where, in addition to drinks and snacks, you can also enjoy live music.





Hotel location

Hotel Luciano (Kazan) is located almost in the city center. Nearby is the metro station, and therefore, having passed one stop, you can immediately be at the famous Kazan Kremlin. Fans of shopping will be pleased with the proximity of a large shopping center. A couple of minutes walk from the hotel building is Bauman Street, on which a huge number of attractions are concentrated.

Guest apartments

Hotel Luciano (Kazan) boasts a chic number of rooms, which is represented by the following categories of apartments:

  • Suite is a large three-room suite, which consists of a bedroom, a living room and a spacious entrance hall. Special attention should be paid to the bathroom, namely its equipment and palace decoration.
  • Standard apartments are quite compact in size, but luxury in design. It can accommodate two people in twin beds.
  • The number of the category "studio" implies combining within a single living space several separate zones that are separated by decorative partitions. Special attention should be paid to the presence of a kitchen corner, thanks to which you can independently prepare delicious and nutritious dishes for yourself and your fellow travelers.
  • Studio Deluxe differs from the previous category in its huge space and a large number of gilded elements in the interior.
  • Double room includes king-size bed. In all other respects, it is similar to the standard one.

It is worth noting that all visitors to the hotel have the right to visit his own spa complex. Nevertheless, you have the opportunity to exclude this service from the cost of living.





What is provided in the apartment

One of the most popular tourist cities in Russia is Kazan. Hotel Luciano offers guests the most comfortable living conditions, which is largely ensured by the well-equipped apartments. So, in the hotel rooms you will be offered the following amenities:

  • reliable safe with electronic combination lock, which will ensure the complete safety of your valuables;
  • minibar with a wide selection of both carbonated and alcoholic drinks;
  • a modern plasma TV connected to a satellite dish;
  • radio, which also serves as an alert to hotel guests in case of emergency or unforeseen situations in the institution;
  • air conditioning, which can both cool and warm the air;
  • internal telephone for communication with the reception, which upon prior request can be connected to a city or international line;
  • free wireless Internet with high speed and excellent connection quality;
  • bath accessories and cosmetics;
  • bedding made of hypoallergenic materials.




Recreation and Sports Opportunities

Hotel Luciano Residence (Kazan) offers its guests the following leisure services:

  • visiting the steam room, sauna;
  • a gym with a huge selection of fitness equipment;
  • a solarium, where you can get a chocolate tan even in the cold season;
  • massage room, where they provide both relaxing and wellness treatments;
  • a playroom for children, as well as an outdoor playground;
  • billiard club;
  • relaxing hydromassage;
  • traditional turkish hammam;
  • indoor pool, access to which is available at any time of the year.

You get a lot of pleasant services when you come to the hotel of the SPA complex in Kazan. Luciano is considered a real highlight of the city.





Hotel Infrastructure

Speaking about the infrastructure of the hotel, it is worth mentioning the following objects:

  • a cozy bar where you can drink your favorite drink to the sounds of live music;
  • a restaurant specializing in traditional Italian dishes;
  • a snack bar where you can have a bite to eat delicious light meals throughout the day;
  • free wireless internet;
  • Private car parking, which hotel residents can use for free;
  • reception staff at any time of the day are ready to come to your aid and provide information of interest to you;
  • bulky luggage can remain in the storage room for the duration of your stay at the hotel;
  • laundry, where you will be provided with services not only for washing and cleaning, but also for ironing your belongings;
  • banquet hall, as well as a specially equipped room for seminars and conferences;
  • Luciano Beauty Studio (Kazan has a huge number of beauty salons, but this is one of the most popular);
  • office services, as well as access to computer equipment.




Positive reviews

Many guests of the city have already visited the Luciano hotel (Kazan). Reviews reflect all its advantages:

  • very convenient location;
  • high quality food in the restaurant;
  • SPA services are provided at a very high level;
  • pleased with the availability of iron in the room;
  • beautiful landscapes from the windows of the rooms;
  • luxurious and at the same time cozy design;
  • good hearty breakfasts.




Negative reviews

The common expression that spots sometimes occur in the sun is quite applicable to this hotel. Of the negative reviews, it is worth noting:

  • it is inconvenient to drive to the hotel by car;
  • since there is no ramp at the entrance, suitcases have to be dragged along the stairs;
  • for unknown reasons, it is forbidden to take photographs in the SPA center;
  • even for a hotel of such a high level prices are sky-high;
  • interruptions in the operation of the ventilation system, due to which there may be stale air with an unpleasant odor in the bathroom and in the rooms.

Hotel "Luciano" is one of the best accommodation options in Kazan. The highlight of this institution is the presence of its own large spa center, where you can get a wide range of relaxing and wellness services. The hotel is shrouded in an atmosphere of luxury that complements the vibrant positive experience of visiting this city.
