The best hotels of Kemer (5 stars, 1 line): names, descriptions and reviews

If you are going on vacation, and your choice has fallen on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey, you can not find a better place to stay than Kemer. Unlike Antalya and Alanya, here the mountains come close to the village. And this means that you will be surrounded by the fragrance of pines, as well as citrus and pomegranate gardens. In the past, a modest fishing village, Kemer has now become a major resort. He acquired satellite towns. These are Kirish, Camyuva, Tekirova, Goynuk, Beldibi. If you want to ensure your relaxation in the highest class, you should choose a hotel of the same category - five stars. Please note that in Kemer hotels can be located on the second or even third line from the beach. And in satellite villages stretched along the sea, often consisting of one street-embankment, all hotels are built on the front page.





City hotels have a smaller territory than those located in the surrounding area. But the proximity to the tourist center has its advantages. If you are looking forward to a fun pastime in Turkey, choose a city, and if you want a quiet and measured rest - villages. In this article, we will provide a list of the best 5 star hotels in Kemer and its environs. The rating is based on the reviews of tourists.

Kemer specifics

This resort can be described in one word: diversity. There are mountains and secluded coves, each of which has its own microclimate. Kemer's beaches are pebbled. But some hotels in the front page made sure to bring soft sand for their guests, or at least built solarium platforms for entering the sea. By the way, hotels in Kemer 5 stars (1 line) are also very diverse. There are both hotel complexes, which are multi-storey buildings, and a scattering of bungalows among the greenery of the park. The latter type includes Robinson Club in Camyuva, Amara Wing Resort, Marco Polo Club and many others. In five-star hotels in Kemer and its environs, you will find everything: all-inclusive meals, incendiary animation, water parks, pools with sea and fresh water, spa complexes, shopping centers, free sightseeing programs.
