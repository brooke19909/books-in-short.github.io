How to make a boat out of paper from paper

Paper boats have always been a favorite toy of many children, especially in spring, when the snow melts and funny streams begin to run. However, a beautiful boat can be a great souvenir and even a gift. So, how to make a boat out of paper?





Options

A paper boat can be made in several ways. Perhaps the easiest way is to get or draw a template yourself, cut it out of cardboard and glue it with ordinary stationery glue, after painting it. The second way is to lay down an elegant boat, guided by the principles of ancient Japanese art, which is gaining more and more popularity around the world. Origami gives you the opportunity to create interesting, diverse models at minimal cost in materials.





A paper boat without a sail: getting started

So, how to make a boat out of paper? First you need to take an ordinary piece of paper. Suitable for both landscape and A4. The sheet is placed on a flat surface and bent into two halves in length. Now you need to bend it one more time, and then bend it all. This is done in order to form lines for the folds. The next step is to bend the upper corners of the sheet to a line in the middle, and then from the face, and from the back side you need to bend the lower edges halfway up. When this is done, it is possible to bend the corners at the bottom of the sheet from both sides inward upward clearly by 90 degrees. And then the remaining lower edges are bent to the end. It turns out an even triangle.





Shutdown

How to make a boat out of paper when all of the above has already been completed? Now the hardest part begins. You need to take the triangle by the center and gently stretch the model, trying not to tear anything anywhere. It turned out to be a square. Now its lower edge is bent up, and then the model again stretches beyond the center. It remains only to bend the bottom edge. Everything - a light boat without a sail is ready, you can start playing games.

Boat with a sail: preparation

But how to make a boat out of paper if you want to supply it with a sail? There is nothing particularly complicated here. Take a square sheet of paper, colored or white. Its corners need to be bent to each other, and then bent so that there are lines of folds that cross the paper. After that, the sheet is bent two more times - vertically and horizontally to make two more lines, and then, for the same purpose, the bends must be done in all corners (that is, all the corners should be folded to the center of the sheet to make a square, and then unbend it) .





The bulk of the work

First, the right and then the left halves of the sheet are vertically stacked in the center. Now the lower one from the upper half is bent in exactly the same way. The next step, which seems difficult for many beginner origami lovers, is to pull the corners on both sides out. That's it - the layout of the future boat can be turned up. The resulting model is carefully bent diagonally. The bottom corner needs to be lifted and fixed. Everything - a nice boat is ready. You can paint the hull in one color, the sail in another, and start playing.

Another boat option

Many people want to learn how to make a boat out of paper. The scheme is quite simple, you only need to practice a couple of times. So, the same sheet of paper in the shape of a square is taken. First, it bends down, then with both halves in the center. Do not unbend anything. Now on each side of the boat you need to bend the corners to the center, then bend the new corners in half to the middle of the boat. The last bend - the corners in the center are bent inward. It remains only to straighten the edges of the boat to get a finished model. Having figured out how various origami figures stack up, you can easily create anything you want. For example, how to make a submarine out of paper? Of course, guided by the same rules of folding an ordinary paper sheet. Children with enthusiasm use the resulting figures in games.
