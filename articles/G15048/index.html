Resort Location

Many tourists choose for their summer (and not only) holiday resort hotel "Majestic" (Crimea). This hotel is located about an hour and a half from the train station and Simferopol International Airport. The hotel building is located on the shores of the azure Black Sea, at the very foot of Ayu-Dag Mountain, which is very popular among tourists. After driving about four kilometers, you can see the famous Artek children's camp.





Hotel Rooms

Majestic Resort Hotel (Crimea) offers its guests accommodation in comfortable apartments of the following categories:

  • The standard offers double occupancy within one small room. There are two single and half beds for you, as well as a full set of other necessary furniture. A soft folding chair acts as an extra bed.
  • Junior Suite is a large room designed for comfortable accommodation of two guests. Here, tourists can choose the option with a double or a pair of small beds. There are all the amenities necessary for a comfortable pastime.

Executive apartments

The following accommodation options are available for luxury lovers:

  • Suite is a spacious two-room suite consisting of a bedroom and a living room. The first is equipped with a double bed, and the second has a folding soft sofa, which can accommodate two additional guests.
  • Executive apartments are two spacious rooms decorated in a luxurious classic style. The bedroom is equipped with a large bed with an orthopedic mattress, and the living room has folding upholstered furniture and a dining table.

It is worth noting the cozy design of the rooms that the Majestic Hotel (Crimea) offers its guests. Photos show the classic design in warm colors.

Hotel "Majestic" (Crimea): reviews, advantages

Many tourists dream of spending holidays on the seashore. For this purpose, it is worth going to the Majestic Hotel (Crimea). Reviews of this resort show the following positive points:

  • incredibly beautiful landscape opens from the windows and from the observation deck;
  • very comfortable and spacious rooms;
  • interesting sightseeing tours;
  • excellent playground for children;
  • good food.




Negative reviews

Not only positive aspects, but also disadvantages is the resort hotel "Majestic" (Crimea). Reviews about this place contain the following negative comments:

  • balcony partitions are very low, and therefore, through negligence, you can simply fall out (especially for children);
  • drinking water in the minibar is not replenished;
  • due to the fact that there are no partitions between balconies, it is very uncomfortable to be with an open door;
  • maids do not have an additional key with which to open a cleaning number;
  • the cost of services is somewhat overstated;
  • The Internet is intermittent;
  • clogged drain in the bathroom;
  • Despite the fact that the hotel has a generator, it is not included at a time when there is no electricity;
  • there are no window panes on the windows, and therefore, to air the room, you have to open the balcony.

General impression

Partenit is one of the most popular tourist destinations, because here you can not only enjoy swimming in the waves of the Black Sea, but also admire the incredibly beautiful nature. At the very foot of Ayu-Dag Mountain is the luxurious Majestic Hotel, which offers guests many services related to both relaxation and wellness. Here you will find comfortable rooms, quality service and good food. It is important that the hotel has its own beach area, which is open exclusively to guests.

As for the shortcomings, it can be noted excessively high prices for accommodation, as well as a number of services. In addition, there are some questions regarding the layout of the rooms. So, the guests are embarrassed by the non-observance of safety measures on the balconies (the handrails are too small), and there are no partitions between them. In general, if you love nature, quality service, as well as silence, this hotel is exactly what you need.
