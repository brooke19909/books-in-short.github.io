Hotel Ramada Liberty 4 * (Monastir, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia's white beaches have attracted the attention of tourists from all over Europe for many years. The hotels of this country offer high quality services, without requiring a huge amount of money for a vacation. Staying in Monastir, travelers often choose the Ramada Liberty Resort 4 * hotel , which combines affordable value and first-class service. What does he offer tourists?

Hotel Description

The hotel was built in a traditional Arabic style and was opened in 1994. It is a huge snow-white building, which is located 300 meters from the sea shore. The territory of Ramada Liberty 4 * (Monastir) is surrounded by a tropical garden, consisting mainly of palm trees.

The building has three floors, which host 235 comfortable rooms. The hotel is focused on a quiet and measured rest. Elderly people and families with children often come here. The property does not allow pets with tourists. Most often, travelers relax here in June, July and March, although the warm weather is held in Tunisia all year round.





You can get to the hotel from the nearest airport, which is approximately 8 km away. If desired, tourists can order a paid transfer in both directions. You can also get here on your own by taxi and your own car. Near the complex there are several golf courses. It is worth remembering that you can check into your room no earlier than 14:00, and you will have to leave it in the early morning. Eviction takes place from 6:00 until noon.

Housing stock and room amenities

A cozy and spacious apartment offers its guests the Ramada Liberty 4 * complex. Their interior is made in a classic style, and the walls are painted in bright, eye-pleasing colors. The hotel has single, double and triple rooms, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is 36 square meters. Each of them is equipped with a small balcony and a private bathroom, which is equipped with a shower, washbasin and toilet.

There is a hairdryer, a set of cosmetics, a large mirror and several towels. On the balcony there is a clothes dryer, a dining set consisting of a plastic dining table and several chairs. All rooms have marble-tiled floors. Twice a week, maids change bedding and towels. Tidying is carried out daily.





In addition to a standard set of furniture, all rooms are equipped with cable TV. Some rooms may have old lamp models. Among the channels available for viewing, there are several Russian-language ones.

Tunisia is a country with a hot and humid climate, so the apartments are equipped with modern, individual air conditioning. Guests can adjust the cooling level by themselves. The room also has a minibar, which holds soft drinks. There is a telephone and an electronic safe for storing valuables and documents.

Complex infrastructure

Justifying its status as a four-star hotel, Ramada Liberty 4 * (Tunisia) offers its guests a well-developed network of infrastructure facilities, which includes a large list of services. Tourists can use private parking. The hotel provides a free parking space. Guests can travel to Tunisia on their own by renting a car at the reception. There is also a tour desk, luggage storage, ATM and currency exchange.





Smoking in the hotel is strictly prohibited. Tobacco products can only be used in designated areas. The complex has several shops selling food, alcohol, cigarettes, beachwear and shoes and souvenirs.

Tourists can also visit the beauty salon and hairdresser. Wi-Fi in public areas is offered at an additional cost. For business meetings and negotiations there is a small conference room with modern computers and projectors. Tourists can relax at any time in the lobby, where soft sofas and coffee tables are located.

Entertainment Organization

A rich entertainment program was prepared for its guests by the Ramada Liberty 4 * hotel. It combines activities involving both active and passive relaxation. Guests can take a refreshing dip in the outdoor swimming pool. Next to it is a spacious sun terrace, equipped with a large number of sun umbrellas and sun loungers. In rainy weather, the heated indoor pool is open.

The private beach area has a water entertainment center. Mass water polo and volleyball games are also organized, and water aerobics classes are held. Throughout the day, tourists are entertained by an experienced team of animators. In the evenings, they conduct thematic show programs telling about the history and culture of Tunisia.





Guests can relax in the spacious spa, which offers massage courses, a sauna and a Turkish bath. The hotel has a modern gym. Sports enthusiasts can sign up for aerobics and archery classes, play basketball, tennis and golf. There is also a billiard room, but it is paid. In the evening, a night club opens, which holds mass parties, including on the beach.

Catering

When making a voucher, tourists can choose one of two food concepts: only breakfast or half board, which additionally includes lunch or dinner. The all-inclusive system is not provided here. The main restaurant of the Ramada Liberty Resort 4 * hotel (Tunisia) serves European and Arabic cuisine. A common buffet is served here in the morning, and in the evening visitors are served exclusively by the menu.

The poolside bar is open daily, serving refreshing drinks, cocktails, snacks and ice creams. Sample original seafood at Al Maeeda Restaurant. There is also a pub on the territory where grilled meat and fish. For baked goods, sandwiches can be enjoyed at the snack bar. It also serves tea, coffee, concentrated juices and mineral water.





Vacation with children

Tourist complex Ramada Liberty 4 * is considered a family hotel, in which couples with children often stop. For his younger guests, he has prepared a large selection of entertainment. Happy children are entertained by experienced animators who hold competitions, discos and sporting events for them. While the kids relax in the mini club, parents can go about their business. Children can swim in a shallow pool with fresh water, which is constantly watched by lifeguards. For outdoor games, there is a playground with slides, a sandbox and a swing.





Guest positive opinions

The opinions of tourists who had previously been in the hotel help to find out the real state of affairs in it. Reviews about the Ramada Liberty 4 * hotel are most often written by good ones, although guests note that recently the quality of service has noticeably decreased. They distinguish the following advantages of the complex:

  • Friendly staff, a very fun animation team. Problems are resolved promptly. In the evening, interesting shows are held.
  • Clean and spacious rooms. Maids are thoroughly cleaned every day. If you leave them a little tip, then on the bed spread the figures from the sheets, leave flowers in the vases.
  • There are no shops near the hotel. But there is a public transport stop. The resort center can be reached by bus in 20 minutes.
  • The beach is tidy. The guards make sure that no strangers enter its territory. Sunbeds enough for everyone.
  • A huge variety of fresh vegetables and fruits in the restaurant. Every day something new appears on the menu.
  • Jellyfish almost never occur in the sea. Water is promptly cleaned of algae and other debris.




Negative guest opinions

As mentioned above, the Ramada Liberty 4 * hotel in recent years began to receive more and more negative reviews. Guests of the complex note that rest here has many shortcomings. Their comments describe the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Tourists are provided with old and washed bedding, which smells of damp.
  • The plumbing in the rooms is in terrible condition. The taps are leaking, and it would be nice to clean the bathtub, toilet bowl and wash basin thoroughly.
  • Meals do not match 4 stars. Yesterday‚Äôs weathered bread is served. A very small selection of dishes is offered. Cooking is very tasteless.
  • The hotel area is in poor condition, there is a lot of garbage everywhere. Weeds grow on the lawn, a lot of dry grass.
  • The staff does not speak Russian, so you should take along a phrase book or dictionary of English or French.

Instead of an afterword

Once the Ramada Liberty 4 * hotel (Monastir) flourished, but now it is in desolation. Tourists who visited it in 2016 note that almost no one is resting here, and the level of service is at a very low level. They advise you to find another place to stay, which will correspond to the declared price.
