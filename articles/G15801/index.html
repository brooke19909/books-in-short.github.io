Cross stitch, "cat" patterns - the beginning of creativity

Are you just learning to cross stitch? Or maybe you already know how? And where did you start your career? After all, it may well be that they were cats. Cross-stitch, the patterns of which with cats and kittens, or even tigers and lions, are quite simple, could well become that simple material with which it is easy to learn how to craft.





Before choosing a drawing, let's decide on the types of patterns that the exhibition can embody with a cross. Schemes "cats" can be of several types.

Types of schemes with cats

  1. Miniatures are small and uncomplicated drawings embroidered with a cross. Usually depicted on them are either painted animals or simple sketches reminiscent of an ornament or pixel art. Such schemes are suitable for beginner needlewomen.
  2. Large-scale images. As a rule, they are realistic and require a lot of time, as well as the application of strength and attention. Such patterns are chosen by those who know how to embroider very well.

A few words about the size of cats and manufacturers of patterns for embroidery

You can even get a tiger at home. And in this case a simple cross-stitch will help. After all, cat schemes involve not only images of pets. So, tigers, leopards and lions are present on schemes from manufacturers Dimensions and KustomKrafts.





Cute domestic cats of all breeds, as well as their cartoon images, are offered by all manufacturers of cross-stitch embroidery. And this is understandable, because cats and kittens cannot be loved.

Thus, we can conclude that the ideal hobby is cross-stitch. Schemes of "cat" in this case, perhaps the best option for both beginners and for real craftswomen.
