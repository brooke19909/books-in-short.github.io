GPS Navigator Prology iMap-7300: review, features and reviews

Portable navigators for most motorists are associated with small devices equipped with compact displays with a small diagonal. However, the dashboards of many cars allow you to take full advantage of large screens, in connection with which many users opt for larger gadgets. One such device is the Prology iMap 7300 GPS Navigator.





Which is better - a smartphone or a navigator?

Despite the fact that smartphones today have become an indispensable part of everyday life, portable navigators have not disappeared from everyday life, remaining popular gadgets of motorists due to convenience and benefits. For a one-time route to the destination, a smartphone with online navigation is also suitable, but a separate device is more suitable for constant and regular use for the purpose of moving around the city or traveling over long distances.

The main advantage of a portable navigator is that all its functions are tailored specifically for navigation. Basically, this affects the reliability of the parts - the antenna and the chip. Navigation in a smartphone or tablet is a secondary function that can cause problems even in expensive models. The first drawback is that a call may be made to the phone at any time, and there is no guarantee that at this moment the driver will not make a difficult maneuver at the road junction or try to get directions. The versatility of the smartphone in this situation can play a bad joke.

If the driver prefers to use online navigation from Google or Yandex, then it depends entirely on the Internet connection, which is not always convenient. Off-line navigation software, in most cases, is paid. For example, Navitel provides a Commonwealth card package for smartphones, which will cost the user at least 1,650 rubles.

And in this situation, it is worth mentioning the cost of the Prology iMap 7300 Black navigator - taking into account all the accessories (car charging, bracket and others), such a gadget will cost much cheaper than similar software and accessories for a smartphone. For this reason, the acquisition of such a device is not only useful, but also beneficial for the car enthusiast.

Navigator functionality

All multimedia and navigation functions necessary for work are already built into the Prology iMap 7300. In addition to the GPS module necessary for navigation, the gadget is equipped with an MP3 player that allows you to watch videos, listen to music or voice messages and view text files.

The 500 MHz MStar microprocessor integrated in the Prology iMap 7300 provides software performance. Despite the small amount of RAM gadget, its capacity is enough to perform all the functions provided. The internal memory of the navigator is 4 GB, the operational memory is only 128 MB. As already mentioned, the RAM is very small, which is very unusual for those users who use modern smartphones and tablets. The navigator of this capacity is quite enough: the gadget responds very quickly to the input commands, it easily plays audio and video, there are no delays when viewing maps. Downloading and updating navigator maps

The software and map package of the Prology iMap 7300 navigator are updated as follows:

  1. Special software is downloaded from the official website of the developer - Navitel Navigator Update Center.
  2. The software is installed on a personal computer.
  3. If necessary, the software is updated to the current version.
  4. The Navitel Navigator Update Center program starts.
  5. The navigator in USB Flash Drive mode is connected to the computer via a USB cable.
  6. Following the prompts of the program, you need to update all the maps and software of the navigator to the latest version.
  7. It is necessary to disconnect the navigator from the computer and restart it to update the software.
