Hotel Dendro Hotel 3 *, Vietnam, Nha Trang: overview, description, features and reviews

Dendro Hotel 3 * is a beautiful and modern hotel, the territory of which is literally immersed in greenery, and very close is a gorgeous sandy beach.





Basic hotel information

Hotel Dendro Hotel 3 * offers its guests to take advantage of many services. So, they will have the opportunity to swim in the indoor pool, while admiring the beautiful sea and cityscapes. Also important is the availability of free wireless Internet, the signal of which is distributed throughout the hotel.

The morning of guests of Dendro Hotel 3 * begins with a nutritious breakfast, which can be obtained not only in the restaurant, but also order delivery directly to your room. Then rent a bike and ride through the scenic surroundings to explore the city and its attractions.

The staff of the Dendro Hotel 3 * hotel cares about the comfort and rich leisure of its guests. You will be offered comfortable rooms, as well as plenty of entertainment. So, you can sing karaoke, book a sightseeing tour, or take a study trip yourself by renting a car.





Hotel Location

Dendro Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) is located in Nha Trang, which is visited annually by many tourists from around the world. It is located just a few steps from the public beach of Tran Phu, which is considered one of the best in this village. The train station is only 2 km from the hotel, and the airport is 30 km.

Guest apartments

Hotel Dendro Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) has 63 guest rooms and can offer you the following accommodation options:

  • Premium suites are spacious and comfortable rooms, each of which has a space of 50 square meters. There is a large bedroom with a huge bed, as well as a living room with a soft sofa, which can serve as a place for an additional guest.
  • Senior Deluxe offers guests a comfortable bedroom with panoramic windows, which offer a unique view of the sea.

Description of amenities in the apartment

Hotel Dendro Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) in order to ensure the comfort of guests offers them to use the following amenities provided in the apartments:

  • the bathroom is equipped with a shower or a large bathtub (depending on the category);
  • each apartment has access to the wireless Internet (password is provided free of charge at the reception during check-in);
  • You can set the temperature in your room that is comfortable for living using an individual air conditioner;
  • To fill your leisure time while in the room, a TV is provided that provides the ability to watch many cable channels;
  • You can store your valuables and money in a safe with an electronic combination lock, the password for which will be known only to you;
  • in the bathroom, in addition to bath accessories and a cosmetic set, a hairdryer is also provided.




Useful hotel information

Like any other hotel, Dendro Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) has certain internal rules, which are subject to both staff and guests. The following main points can be distinguished:

  • Check-in and check-out are carried out after 14:00 and before noon, respectively (early check-in and late check-out are possible at an additional cost, subject to the availability of a sufficient number of free rooms);
  • kids under 6 years old stay free of charge on existing beds, and for older children, such a service will cost $ 3.5 per night;
  • extra bed costs $ 17 per person per night;
  • the hotel does not provide special cots;
  • check-in in rooms with animals is strictly prohibited;
  • You can pay for the services provided with a plastic credit card;
  • One of the features of this hotel is that smoking on its territory is strictly prohibited;
  • if you want to use the transfer services, then this should be indicated at the stage of booking or inform the administration in advance;
  • each guest is required upon check-in to leave a deposit of $ 100, which will be returned if the results of the room check are satisfactory;
  • newlyweds living in the hotel are given a bonus in the form of romantic decoration of the room with flowers;
  • regular guests have the right to use the early check-in and late check-out service.




Positive reviews

Tourists generally remain satisfied with their stay at the Dendro Hotel 3 *. The reviews contain a number of positive comments:

  • the rooms are cleaned every day and very carefully (no dust, no dirt, not the slightest garbage left);
  • the hotel boasts an incredibly good location, because the beach from it is literally a couple of steps away;
  • very good food - at the buffet there is meat and fish, and cheese, as well as many fresh vegetables and fruits, a good selection of products for children;
  • There are many shops and cafes around the hotel, so there are no problems with food.




Negative reviews

After staying at the Dendro Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam), travelers have a number of significant comments:

  • all rooms, except the most expensive, are very cramped and small;
  • for unknown reasons, the attitude of the reception staff towards tourists is extremely unfriendly;
  • in this hotel for a fee it is impossible to upgrade the room category, as is usually the case in other hotels;
  • on the lower floors (up to the 7th) it is very noisy due to the proximity to the carriageway, and there is also no beautiful view from the window - it is blocked by nearby buildings;
  • maids often do not report towels, and during a room check at check-out guests can be charged with theft and collect a fine;
  • a huge number of complaints are caused by the work of the hotel guide, since it does not help in communicating with staff and solving problems, it only imposes costly sightseeing tours (you can buy much cheaper from local companies);
  • loungers on the beach are paid (but the further you move away from the hotel, the cheaper they will cost you);
  • despite the fact that the schedule for issuing beach towels is from 8 to 9 in the morning, you need to come and take turns much earlier, since they are not enough for everyone;
  • it is inconvenient that the rooms do not have a balcony, since bathing suits and towels are simply impossible to dry indoors;
  • the elevator works very poorly - it constantly creaks, travels very slowly and it feels like it is about to get stuck;
  • the rooms are very noisy due to the carriageway, and also because of the loud music that plays in the bar on the ground floor.
