Canary Beach Resort 3 * (Vietnam, Phan Thiet): hotel description, reviews of tourists

This beach hotel - Canary Beach Resort 3 * - has earned good reviews from tourists, despite its low "star rating". We can say that the hotel combines all the advantages that are so appreciated by tourists. It stands right on the beach and at the same time is surrounded by a beautiful shady garden. The trees in it are constantly watered, and the greens are looked after. The hotel has several buildings of two, four and five floors. Some are closer to the sea, while others are closer to the road. There are elevators, and on the roof of a five-story building there is an observation deck. Tourists do not even complain that the hotel is far from the center of the resort village. Indeed, because of this, it is not so noisy here, and the prices are lower. However, all the shops and good restaurants are located at some distance from the hotel.





Location

If we are looking for a Canary Beach Resort 3 * hotel on a map of Vietnam, then we need to explore the south of the country. The complex is located in the province of Binh Thuan, in the Gulf of Mui Ne. This is one of the areas of the resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãPhan Thiet. Getting here for quite some time - the nearest airport to it is in Nha Trang. And the road from there takes about five hours by bus (this is due to speed limits). Therefore, experienced travelers are advised to travel by night bus to Nha Trang, stop there for a couple of days, and then get to the airport in about half an hour. This makes departure a lot easier. From hotels to the promenade of Bo Ke with its entertainment - about three and a half kilometers. Take a taxi about five to ten minutes. You can still get on any local bus. And there are only two of them - the first and ninth numbers. Stop very close to the Canary Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Vietnam).





Phan Thiet, Mui Ne

This is a separate town, a former village that has the same name with the bay. Now it has become part of sixteen quarters of Phan Thiet resort. So now it is one of the most popular resort towns for which tourists come to Vietnam. Sea, sandy beach, sun - all of this is abundant here. In this corner of Vietnam there are a lot of vacationers, especially from Russia. Nevertheless, food and fruit prices are quite affordable. Mui Ne Beach, on which Canary Beach Resort 3 * stands, stretched for as many as fifteen kilometers, and most of it is occupied by hotel complexes. Tourists travel mainly on rented bicycles or scooters, as well as motorcycle taxis, like those that run in Thailand. The beach is nice, sandy, with a shallow entrance, very convenient for children. If there are waves, then those who like surfers. They even learn to work out special tricks with boards. In addition, it is one of the most fishing places in Vietnam. If you want to try seafood - all kinds of and different, and in large quantities - then you are definitely here. Lobsters, tiger prawns and other goodies are more accessible here than anywhere else.



Rooms for guests

The hotel Canary Beach Resort 3 * seventy rooms, where holidaymakers lodge. The rooms are different - mostly standard, but there are improved suites. They are beautiful, stylish, decorated in such a way as to create an impression of traditional Vietnamese culture. At the same time, furniture and sanitary ware are modern, comfortable, in good working condition. The rooms are air-conditioned with balconies. In the bathroom - a decent shower, Panasonic hairdryer. Every day they add gel, soap, shampoo, toothpaste, and also give two small bottles of water to the room. There is a small refrigerator, a kettle, cups with spoons. The TV is flat, with the ability to use a flash drive. Some rooms catch Wi-Fi, and even at a good speed. Settled without any problems and surcharges, quickly. The rooms are clean and tidy. From the windows you can see the pool, garden or sea.





Where is cheaper to eat

If you believe the reviews of our compatriots, the Canary Beach Resort 3 * hotel has very good breakfasts according to the "buffet" system. There are spring rolls, soups, omelets, fried potatoes with onions, noodles, dessert cakes, pancakes, fruits, vegetables. Natural juices. The choice is great, like at lunch or dinner. The restaurant in the hotel is very stylish, wooden, made of coconut trees. Vietnamese cuisine is offered here, and the highlight of the menu is seafood. By the pool - a very cozy bar, music plays. They serve delicious ice cream. Despite the fact that in the area where the hotel is located, the infrastructure is not as developed as in the center, here you can eat tasty and inexpensively. You can have a seafood snack with various interesting and exotic sauces right on the beach. In cafes nearby, they also offer other dishes that you will not try in ordinary life, for example, crocodile meat. The portions are very large, so you should not order much. Lobsters are especially good here.





Services

The hotel Canary Beach Resort 3 * has parking for cars. Here you can rent a car or bike, as well as book a transfer from the airport. There is a safe at the reception, where valuables are stored. There is also a tour desk. There is even an exchange office. Laundry can be washed. Access to the World Wide Web is provided. A safe and convenient area for games is open for small guests. At the reception there is a Russian-speaking staff. The people at the hotel work very nice. The staff are quick, although it is preferable to communicate with them in English.





Beach

Many Russians will answer with one word the question of why they are coming to Vietnam. The sea is the main reason for their journey. And this hotel is the best suited for such a pastime. After all, from the rooms to the coast no more than fifty meters. There is a scourge that keeps order and removes trash. They offer massages on the beach, a lot of water activities, in general, it will not be boring. The coast is considered one of the cleanest in the entire Mui Ne. On the beach, tides are very pronounced . But even if it gets shallow, it's okay. The hotel has a nice clean pool with pleasant blue water and a jacuzzi. It is located in the open air, very large and can accommodate everyone. Sun loungers and umbrellas are on the beach and by the pool. At the sea, loungers can be occupied only by hotel guests - they strictly monitor this and identify strangers by hotel towels for sunbathing. To the right of the hotel there are beautiful natural marine parks with pink dunes. You can also swim there, and the water is unusually clear. Many hotel guests prefer this wild beach. There is no diving in these waters, but surfing and kiting are very good. Therefore, during the peak season in Mui Ne there is no end to the lovers of these water sports.





Excursions and shopping

It is better to buy trips not from hotel guides at the Canary Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Vietnam), but from travel companies on the street. It is both better and cheaper. There are several interesting natural attractions right next to the hotel. One of them is the Fairy Creek, which can be reached on foot directly by water. Even small children go there, especially since there are snack bars and a mini zoo near this beautiful reservoir. You can negotiate with local taxi drivers, and for fifty dollars a day you will be driven to all the local attractions. You will see the Ke Ga lighthouse, and pink dunes, and Chama towers. If you are ready to tear off two or more days from a beach holiday, you can ride in Dalat, Ho Chi Minh and even Cambodia. In the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel you can buy good and cheap fruits. At the same time, you will spend less money than in other Vietnamese resorts, and shopping stalls are very close to the hotel. But for souvenirs it is better to go to Phan Thiet.
