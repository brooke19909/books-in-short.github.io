Silver Lake (Khanty-Mansiysk): recreation opportunities and climatic conditions

Silver Lake, located in the vicinity of Khanty-Mansiysk, attracts here not only local city residents, but also visiting tourists. Here you can not only have a great time in nature, but also have a wonderful fishing trip.

Climatic features

Silver Lake (Khanty-Mansiysk) is located in the Far North, and therefore has characteristic climatic features. In this region, summers are very short, hot and long winters, so much less time is allocated for those who like to relax in nature than in other regions. However, the recreation center "Silver Lake" (Khanty-Mansiysk) operates year-round and is ready to receive guests.

The average annual temperature in this region reaches -0.8 ¬∞ C. In summer, the thermometer mark rarely exceeds 23 ¬∞ C. Frequent winds and air humidity, the average annual value of which reaches 77%, are not conducive to relaxation for an ordinary resident, but for local residents this is the time to sunbathe and relax.

Recreation center

Silver Lake (Khanty-Mansiysk) can offer its visitors not only outdoor recreation and swimming in clear lake water, but also the opportunity to go fishing. True, poaching here is punishable and suppressed, according to the law, but a significant trophy can be caught by fishing rod.





To get to the recreation center "Silver Lake" (Khanty-Mansiysk) is not difficult. Moreover, enterprising carriers offer you transfer services that allow you to get to your destination for a fairly reasonable fee. You can also immediately order a return ticket and get a discount. However, it is unlikely that you will want to immediately leave these wonderful places.

Why should you visit Silver Lake (Khanty-Mansiysk)?

Visiting these places will be useful not only to locals and residents of nearby cities. Tourists and guests will also enjoy sitting on the shore of the lake, swimming in its clean and cool water, as well as catching trophy fish. The recreation center "Silver Lake" (Khanty-Mansiysk) offers its guests comfortable living conditions, service, food. You will experience only comfort and get a pleasant experience from communicating with the bewitching nature of these places.





If you are going to relax with family and children, then for them you can certainly find something to do. In addition to walking in nature, the base provides the opportunity to use the Internet, television and other benefits of civilization. Guided tours of historically significant corners of these places are also offered.
