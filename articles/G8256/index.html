How to contact the MTS operator - brief instructions

MTS is the largest mobile operator in Russia. According to 2013 data, the company services about 70 million subscribers in Russia. Its subsidiaries are licensed to provide GSM services in Armenia, Belarus, Turkmenistan, Ukraine and Uzbekistan.





Sometimes there are situations when the subscriber needs to personally ask the cellular operator his questions. But not everyone knows how to contact the MTS operator. And begins a long listening to the answering machine and wandering around the keys of the phone. This usually happens after dialing 0890, which is designed for subscribers in Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, and Uzbekistan. But communication with robots on the other end of the phone does not please anyone, especially if the question of interest does not correspond to any of the proposed menu items. When looking for a way to contact the MTS operator (Russia), you should know that there are five main sections. The unit is responsible for the state of the account. Number 2 provides information regarding the tariff plan, all kinds of bonuses and promotions. The number 3 will help to deal with all the issues that relate to Internet connections. By pressing the number 4, the subscriber will hear information regarding travel. Number 5 is responsible for all sorts of unplanned situations, such as card recovery, problems with replenishment of the balance. And only after waiting for the announcement of the digit 0 and clicking on it, the subscriber gets the opportunity to contact the operator of the MTS company. But this will take a lot of time and nerves, especially if the current situation of the user is urgent.





There is a way to contact the MTS operator quickly, without waiting for listings of all menu items. There is one little trick. As practice shows, to connect to the operator, and not with the answering machine, you need to make at least two menu transitions. That is, for example, by pressing immediately a deuce and entering the menu of tariff plans, it is enough to press deuce again, after which a connection with the operator appears in the list of all proposed options, for which you need to press the number 0. So, the answer to the question of how to contact with the MTS operator quickly, there will be the next. After connecting to the answering machine, the subscriber who called 0890 should press a combination of numbers 2-2-0, while maintaining an interval of 2-3 seconds after each dialed number. This will make it possible to hear the voice of a real operator and ask him all the questions of interest.





Sometimes it turns out to connect with the operator by listening to all menu items and pressing the number 0. But this does not always work, very often the repetition of menu items starts. Subscribers have the opportunity to contact the operator through a landline telephone or from a telephone of another cellular connection. To do this, dial the easy-to-remember number 8-800-250-0890. Calling this number is free. In order to contact the operator in roaming at MTS, you need to dial the number in the international format - indicating the plus sign and the number 7. The dialing number is next (+) 7-495-766-0166.
