Hotel Numa Konaktepe Hotel 4 *, Turkey, Alanya: photos, reviews

People are serious and responsible in choosing a place to spend their summer holidays or their long-awaited annual vacation. When planning, it is necessary to take into account the preferences of each participant in the trip: after all, someone loves only to sunbathe, someone is interested in getting to know the sights, and for someone, the possibility of active entertainment plays a special role.

Alanya and its advantages

All of the above components are successfully combined in the Turkish resort of Alanya, which from year to year attracts a large number of tourists, being not only youth, but also a great option for a family vacation.

Picturesque natural landscapes, a mass of historical ruins, crystal clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea are waiting for everyone who decides to arrive in this village.

The 4-star Konaktepe Hotel offers a high level of service and a homely atmosphere in the apartments. Friendly staff warmly welcomes each guest, setting up for a carefree and unforgettable pastime.

The walls are decorated with paintings. Cleaning is done daily, towels are changed five times a week. The Wake Up Call service allows you to easily wake up at the scheduled time.





Equipment

Comfortable accommodation conditions include everything you need for a relaxing stay. The presence of an individual air conditioning system allows you to create a favorable microclimate. The presence of a TV with Russian-language channels will brighten up the evenings spent in the walls of the apartments. The phone allows you to communicate with the reception and solve the problems with lightning speed.

The furniture is represented by a standard modest set: soft double beds, a table and chairs, a cupboard and a spacious wardrobe. Minibar and its replenishment are paid extra. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel complex, its use is included in the price.

The bathroom at the Numa Konaktepe Hotel 4 * (Alanya) is decorated with ceramic tiles, equipped with a bath and shower, sink and hairdryer. Sets of cosmetics and towels are located here.

Supply system

According to the All-Inclusive concept, meals are served according to the buffet system three times a day. Culinary masterpieces and amazing dishes of local chefs will delight each guest with their taste delights. The main restaurant has both an indoor hall and an outdoor terrace. The cuisine is predominantly international.

The presence of three bars allows you to quench the feeling of thirst and snack during the day with pastries, ice cream or light snacks. Freshly squeezed juices and elite alcohol are subject to additional payment.





Aquazone

Most of the time, the spa people spend outdoors. On the hot days of summer, each of them is saved by the opportunity to plunge into the cleanest pools, which are equipped with sunbeds with soft mattresses and umbrellas for being in the shade. The main one is located near the main building, intersected by an imposing bridge, diving from which is prohibited. Night illumination turns this zone into a real fairy tale.

In order for swimming not to bore guests, you can diversify swimming by playing water polo or regular water aerobics classes. Qualified instructors involve everyone, regardless of age and gender. Light physical activity has a beneficial effect on well-being, giving an excellent mood. A free-standing mini-water park with two slides will give everyone going down a feeling of drive and an unforgettable extreme.

Nearby is a children's pool, where little kids frolic and are saturated with positive emotions.

At the private beach of the Numa Konaktepe Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya) there is another aquazone, which includes an adult and a children's pool. In addition to the open, there is a similar swimming area for the cold season. It is covered and heated. Glass transparent roof creates the feeling of being in the open.





Beach

The goals of most travelers come down to the desire to swim in the cleanest waters of the Mediterranean. For this, the administration of the Konaktepe Hotel has thought out the beach infrastructure to the smallest detail. It is possible to reach the coast by overcoming a distance of 500 meters by crossing the roadway through an underpass. A free shuttle is available in both directions.

Numerous sun loungers, convenient for sunbathing, are located under awnings, which protect some travelers from direct sun exposure. However, those who wish to find a dark-skinned tan and soak up the rays of the scorching sun are allowed to move the sunbeds along the sandy shore.

The entrance to the water is gentle. This guarantees a safe game for young children and the opportunity for them to enjoy swimming in the cleanest waters, tirelessly jumping and diving into the waves.

The beach infrastructure includes changing cabins and showers, which allow you to wash off the salt after prolonged splashing in the sea. Towels are secured.

sports and health

Sport is the basis of good mood and well-being. That is why for those who prefer active and mobile entertainment, a volleyball court is equipped with everything necessary near the coast, which daily collects a large number of players united in teams to achieve a single result - victory. Here you can ride a banana, ring or water scooters.

Surfing and the opportunity to go to the open sea by boat or motor boat with the aim of diving into the bottomless depths of the Mediterranean Sea will bring unprecedented delight. The amazing underwater world, filled with various inhabitants and unique plants, reefs and corals, will surprise everyone who dares to get acquainted with this. Having passed the preliminary training, this can be done not only by an experienced diver, but also by a beginner.

Classes in the gym, according to the spa, are important and necessary for health. A variety of fitness equipment, including treadmills, steppers and a number of other equipment, will help maintain excellent body shape, find a slender waist, dropping a couple of extra pounds and strengthen the immune system. Experienced instructors will help you make a schedule of individual training, conduct fitness classes and talk about how to take care of your health through physical activity.





Entertainment

Darts, table tennis and billiards will dilute the monotonous days with the opportunity to compete for victory. Inventory is issued free of charge, the game is available to everyone.

The evening ends with a fun performance of the animation team. Talented artists, fiery dancers, acrobats and stunts will easily captivate each guest, giving positive, smiles and fun. After - a night disco, the entrance to which is included in the price.

In order to diversify the time spent in the walls of the Numa Konaktepe Hotel 4 * (Turkey), travelers are given the opportunity to visit the SPA center. Relaxing skin rejuvenation and anti-aging procedures performed to the sound of calm music in an atmosphere of exotic essential oils will give each visitor an unforgettable feeling of happiness and pleasure. Massage performed by professionals in their field will revitalize every part of the body and breathe in a portion of strength and new energy.

Sauna and hammam will allow you to relax your body and soul at the same time, abstract from everyday problems and life's hustle and bustle. Relieved fatigue and tension, improved blood circulation and cleansed skin are the result of such procedures.





Toddler Leisure

All guests of Numa Konaktepe Hotel 4 * in the reviews of 2017 note the excellent organization of leisure for kids. Wanting to realize children's dreams and fantasies, the administration of the hotel complex thought out the details of entertainment to the smallest detail. During the day, a children's club operates, the doors of which were opened to receive guests aged 3 to 12 years. Under the supervision of a professional educator, kids will have plenty of fun with a variety of toys, board games, designers and puzzles. Developing and creative activities, exercises and games for the manifestation of dexterity and ingenuity are to the liking of every baby.

A joyful and mischievous team of animators, daily transforming into new characters, will not only show amazing scenes, but also hold a series of master classes and games with children.

Staying in the fresh air is useful not only for adults, but also for their children. For this, a playground was built, made of safe material and containing swings, slides and labyrinths. Here, the children will spend all their energy in the evening sleep. The day ends with a disco. Sometimes the foamy parties held bring kids to an unprecedented delight.

The restaurant provides high chairs and a children's menu. A cot is available on request.





Additional services

A transfer from the airport and back is possible, but it is paid extra. Reception works without breaks and days off. It is possible to exchange money at the exchange office, without leaving the walls of the hotel. To book an unforgettable trip to local attractions is realized using the tour desk. No parking for cars. Souvenir shops and grocery stores are located on site.

Dry cleaning, laundry and ironing are additionally paid services. You can use the services of professional photographers, pictures with captured moments of relaxation and emotions will be included in a family photo album. The services of hairdressers and a visit to the doctor are paid upon the fact of necessity.

Instead of an afterword

Judging by the numerous positive feedback and the unprecedented popularity of the four-star Konaktepe Hotel, the desire to return here again arises for everyone who has previously been here. As guests note in their positive reviews, Numa Konaktepe Hotel 4 * is really worthy of attention. It is definitely worth considering as an accommodation option during your stay in Alanya.
