Review of Nokia Lumia 930. User reviews

On June 30, 2014, another model of the modern smartphone of the global Nokia brand entered the Russian market. The Lumia 930 was introduced back in the spring at the Build event. The smartphone has a powerful processor with four cores. Many users have already been able to appreciate the Nokia Lumia 930 mobile device . Thanks to the high-quality 20 megapixel camera, you will receive photos and videos of excellent quality.





Filling

The Qualcomm Snapdragon 800 smartphone processor has a clock frequency of 2.2 GHz. The main advantages of the phone were the core type Adreno 330 and installed memory, the volume of which is 32 GB. Unfortunately, the memory card is not supported by this smartphone, but this is not a reason to be upset, since the Lumia series devices have 7 GB in storage called OneDrive.

The phone supports the following types of communication: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LTE, etc. When examining the details of the Nokia Lumia 930, user reviews should also be taken into account. Most often, the responses relate to the OLED display and its five-inch diagonal. The screen resolution is 1920 by 1080 pixels. As in other models of the Lumia series, the display has a technology called ClearBlack and increased sensitivity, which allows you to control the device with gloves on.

The screen of the phone is covered with Gorilla Glass 3. The camera is equipped with PureView technology, a large-scale sensor and autofocus. The flash has two LEDs. Optics manufactured by ZEISS.





Microphones

Do you carefully consider the quality of equipment Nokia Lumia 930? Reviews often mention a technology called Rich Recording. It is designed for high-quality sound recording. The mobile device has 4 microphones, two of them are located on the front side, and two on the back. Using the digital method of processing sounds and thoughtful arrangement of elements of the system, four microphones are able to combine and act as two devices.

During stereo recording, sound coming exclusively from the front is taken into account, and noises that are heard on the sides are completely ignored. In this case, the smartphone user receives a blank recording.

operating system

Nokia Lumia 930 is considered the second mobile device in the domestic market, which runs on Windows Phone 8.1. Especially the phone will be relevant for business users. Microsoft Office Mobile is installed on the device, including Excel, Word, etc. If necessary, install Skype. Owners of the new smartphone at any time can download the desired application for free.





The flagship smartphone Nokia Lumia 930 supports the functions of Mobile Device Management, which affect the settings of VPN, SharePoint and much more.

Corporate Services

This model provides a voice assistant called Cortana. At the moment, the function concerns only the beta version, designed for the American market, but this is a matter of time. The smartphone has a notification center and high-speed access to shortcuts from the drop-down menu.

Interface Nokia Lumia 930 significantly improved. There is a possibility to choose among several rows of icons on the main screen, and the user can independently set the desired background, which is located under the "tiles".

The intelligence of the flagship smartphone is even more developed than previous models. The Word Flow keyboard has swipe gestures.

The developers updated the store with applications, now users can download universal programs, games, navigators and much more. It is worth noting another useful innovation - separate volume control for signals and applications.

Design

The appearance of the smartphone was dealt with by Jonne Harju, who for 10 years has been developing the "cover" of Nokia's mobile devices. It is he who is responsible for the design of all phones that go under the Lumia brand.
Journalists testing the Nokia Lumia 930, owners reviews were studied with special attention. All users noted the perfect balance between easy operation and style.





The smartphone is simple and original. In this mobile device you will not find unnecessary details, the design is made in a concise and minimalistic style. The aluminum frame goes well with the display and the back panel, which has a bright color.

The back of the Nokia Lumia 930 has a pillow-shaped shape, as this model does not have an outstanding camera module. This decision was the starting point for creating support for a wireless type charger . Thus, harmony was achieved between the attractive "cover" and modern technology. The back panel is made of high-quality matte polycarbonate, which is resistant to dirt and fingerprints. For users who prefer the classic style, white and black models are relevant, and for fans to stand out from the crowd - orange or green. The Nokia Lumia 930 phone has a thin body, which is convenient when carrying the device in your trouser pocket or in a tiny handbag. In various stores on the Nokia Lumia 930 black, the price may vary slightly. Most often, the cost slightly exceeds 20,000 rubles.

Owners of Nokia Lumia 930 leave only good reviews, which once again confirms the high quality of this device.
