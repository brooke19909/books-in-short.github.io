Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Kusadasi) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

One of the best resorts in Turkey is Kusadasi. If you plan to spend a summer vacation in this place, then there should not be a problem with choosing a hotel. After all, the hotel base is represented by a mass of options for every taste and size of the wallet. Today we offer a closer look at one of the Kusadasi hotels - Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 *. We will find out what it represents, what services and entertainment it offers to guests, as well as what impressions he left with our compatriots.





Where is the four-star hotel Grand Ozzelik

Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi) is located on the shores of the Aegean Sea, five kilometers from the center of Kusadasi. The nearest airport is Izmir Airport, 73 km from the property. Thus, after arrival, it will take you about an hour and a half to get to the hotel. Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 *: photo, description

The hotel was opened in 1992. The last large-scale reconstruction was carried out in 2005. The hotel complex includes the main nine-story building, all of which have a partial sea view, and an additional four-story building. The hotel itself positions itself as a calm and comfortable place for a great family vacation.





Hotel Policies

Check in at the Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi) is from two in the afternoon. If you arrived earlier than the specified time, but your room is not ready yet, you can leave your suitcases in the luggage storage room and go for lunch or go directly to the beach. In accordance with the rules of the hotel, on the day of departure, the room must be vacated before 12 noon. Payment for accommodation (if you have not previously paid the full price to the travel agency) and additional services are made upon discharge from the hotel. You can pay in cash or with a plastic card.

Due to the fact that the Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * positions itself as a place for family holidays, children of any age are allowed here. If you place a child under 12 years old in a crib or extra bed, then you will not have to pay for his stay. As much as possible in each number it is possible to put only one additional or a children's bed. However, if you require such a service, you must notify the hotel in advance.

As for accommodation in the Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * pets, unfortunately, those who like traveling in the company of their four-legged friends will have to leave their pets at home or look for another hotel to stay in Turkey.





Number of rooms

In the main building of the Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * there are 96 apartments. 90 of them are standard rooms that can accommodate a maximum of three people. Five more rooms are suites designed for a maximum of four guests. There is one room designed for people with disabilities. The annex houses another 20 standard family rooms.

Almost all apartments have excellent sea views. Absolutely all rooms have a balcony, a bathroom with a hairdryer, satellite TV, a telephone, a minibar, a safe and air conditioning (central). All rooms have carpet flooring. For a fee, you can order room service.





Sea beach

Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * - hotel reviews confirm this - it has an excellent private beach. It is located only fifty meters from the hotel. The beach is sandy. Sunset at sea gentle, you do not hurt your legs. On the beach, vacationers are provided with sunbeds and umbrellas for free.

Infrastructure

Hotel Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * has all the necessary infrastructure to make your stay comfortable. So, at your service there is a 24-hour reception. Thus, if you check in at an inopportune time, or if you have a problem that requires an urgent solution, you can always contact the duty administrator.

On a paid basis, hotel guests can use the laundry, dry cleaning, hairdresser, order room service. Here you can also rent a car or book an excursion. In addition, there is a shop on site where you can buy souvenirs as a keepsake, as well as everything you need for your holiday.





The Grand Ozzelik Hotel has ample parking, which guests can use for free. Thus, when you come on vacation in your car or rent a car in Turkey, you will not feel any parking problems.

The hotel administration pays attention to the health of its guests. For these purposes, there is a medical center where a qualified doctor works. Thus, if you (or someone from your family or friends) feel unwell, you can immediately seek medical help from a hotel doctor.





In addition, the hotel has a conference room. It is equipped with all the necessary equipment for presentations and business meetings.

Nutrition

Meals at the four-star Grand Oztselik Hotel are all-inclusive. For breakfast, lunch and dinner, the hotel restaurant serves a buffet consisting of a variety of dishes from both Turkish and international cuisine. In between main meals, you can have a bite to eat in the hotel bars. In total, the hotel has one restaurant and three bars (including a beach one).

Entertainment

The hotel has three pools: two adults (outdoor and indoor) and a children's (outdoor). Umbrellas and sunbeds are located near them, the use of which is free for guests. You can also play table tennis or visit the cinema. For an extra fee, guests can use the sauna, jacuzzi and massage services. In the evenings, the hotel hosts a disco.

Cost of living

Due to the fact that this hotel is a four-star hotel, the cost of living here cannot be called high. So, a seven-day vacation - depending on the season - will cost an average of 400 euros.





Grand Ozcelik Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi): reviews

As a rule, people planning a vacation in a particular country carefully study the comments left by tourists who have already been there, both about local attractions and features, and about hotels. In this regard, we decided to summarize the impressions of Russians and residents of other CIS countries about their vacation at the Grand Oztselik Hotel (Kusadasi).

So, in general, most tourists liked the hotel. Many believe that it is consistent with the declared four stars and is the best institution in terms of price and quality.

Some tourists were very pleased with the proximity of the beach. After all, leaving the hotel building, you need to walk literally several tens of meters in order to be on the very shore of the sea. However, the beach itself seemed to many too small. In this regard, sunbeds here are close to each other, and sometimes they are not enough for everyone. Therefore, many tourists were forced to get up early to take a deck chair. As for water activities, then, according to vacationers, here you can ride a scooter and a banana at an affordable price.

If we talk about food at the hotel, then most tourists were satisfied with it. However, those who rested here for more than a week noted a certain uniformity of dishes. Also, some of our fellow citizens were embarrassed by the need to stand in line for meat dishes that can not be taken at the buffet, as they are portioned by cooks.

A very controversial point was the local animation. So, often many expect from Turkish hotels that both day and evening animators will entertain both children and adults. Here, as such, there is no animation. There is only a disco. Those who like to relax in a relaxed atmosphere, without constant invitations to participate in a variety of fun animators, really liked the hotel. However, tourists, accustomed to having fun in this way, were noticeably disappointed.

As for the rooms of the Grand Ozzelik Hotel, practically no complaints were noticed here. Most of the guests considered the rooms quite comfortable and clean. The service was also at its best: they cleaned the rooms daily, and also very often changed towels and bed linen. In addition, many noted the good location of the hotel on a hill. Thus, an excellent view of the sea was opened even from the windows of rooms located on the lower floors of the hotel.

The hotel staff made extremely pleasant impressions on holidaymakers. They are always friendly and willing to help. Many of them speak Russian or at least understand Russian.
