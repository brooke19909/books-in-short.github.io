The recreation center "Lyudmila", Vityazevo: photos and reviews

Well-groomed territory, attentive staff, a wide variety of rooms and prices attract tourists to the recreation center "Lyudmila". Vityazevo has several beaches on its territory, many cafes and restaurants, a modern water park, and all this is located in the immediate vicinity of Lyudmila. The recreation center "Lyudmila": how to get there?

The village of Vityazevo, where the Lyudmila boarding house is located, is 10 km from Anapa, so first you need to get to this resort city, and then use a taxi, bus, minibus or transfer booked in advance and get to your destination. Although if you choose the air mode of transport, the distance that you will have to cover from the airport to the village of Vityazevo will be only 5 km, while to Anapa - 15 km.

There is a railway station in Anapa, more precisely in the village of Dzhemete. And since the price of train tickets is the most affordable, most vacationers choose this type of movement. Residents of cities located at a distance of 300 km from Vityazevo (recreation center "Lyudmila") can also get to Anapa by bus. However, if the distance is greater, then it is better to refuse this type of transport, since such a road is very tiring, especially in summer. Arriving in Vityazevo, you should find 4 Serebryanaya Street, where Lyudmila is located.

Infrastructure

Upon arrival in Vityazevo recreation center "Lyudmila" makes a positive impression on all its guests. Its territory, whose area is 1.2 hectares, is distinguished by cleanliness, well-groomed, as well as an abundance of trees, bushes and flowers. There are many benches and gazebos for relaxation. All guests can splash around in an outdoor pool, divided into three parts. The first is designed for bathing very young guests, the second for older children, and the third for adults. The hotel territory is guarded and is under video surveillance, so that guests of Lyudmila can feel completely safe.





At their request, vacationers can enjoy a game of table tennis, visit the sports ground, read something interesting in the library, watch a movie in the summer cinema, dance at the disco area, enjoy refreshments in the bar.

Number of rooms

For living, the recreation center "Lyudmila" (Vityazevo) offers rooms in the administrative building, a three-story building and small cottages. The rooms located in the administrative building are equipped with a private bathroom, TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, necessary furniture. There are double and triple rooms. Of the rooms located on the ground floor, there is access to the veranda. Fully equipped rooms are also located in a three-story building and a one-story cottage. In addition, all of these rooms have balconies.

Vacationers who are not ready to spend too much money on their vacation can stay in the economy rooms offered by the Lyudmila recreation center. Vityazevo has many budget hotels on its territory, but it is unlikely that you will be able to find the best option. Economy rooms are located in one-story cottages. The room has comfortable beds, bedside tables, wardrobe and fridge. Washbasins on the veranda, 4 toilets are located at the end of the house and are designed for vacationers from 8 rooms, showers in the boarding house.





Food and beach

The recreation center provides three meals a day, organized in the form of a "buffet", however, if desired, vacationers can refuse it and eat in any other place convenient for them. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the spacious dining room. The daily menu abounds with a variety of delicious dishes.

Own beach, equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas, is a huge advantage that the recreation center "Lyudmila" has. Vityazevo is distinguished by the presence of magnificent sandy beaches, however, vacationers of this pension also have the opportunity to enjoy a relaxing holiday by the sea, while on the city beach there is always bustle and noise.

Reviews about "Lyudmila"

What is the first thing to say? Despite the fact that modern tourists have the opportunity to relax in almost any corner of the world, many still think that the best place to spend a summer vacation is Anapa - Vityazevo. The recreation center "Lyudmila" offers the best conditions for recreation for families with children and single young people, so most tourists return from their summer trip with a pleasant experience. After all, there is everything that is required for a pleasant pastime - luxurious sandy beaches, quality food, many opportunities for entertainment and so on. However, there are people who claim that they will never rest in Vityazevo again. The recreation center "Lyudmila" also receives negative reviews. Although, as a rule, they complain about the food of the boarding house. Some vacationers claim that it is too fat, and it is better to eat in other places. Sometimes people living in economy rooms leave negative feedback about some of the inconvenience caused by the lack of a shower and shared toilets, however, the administration warns about this in advance and you need to think about these nuances before booking a room. If comfort is very important for you, then it is better to opt for more expensive options, which are many at the Lyudmila recreation center.
