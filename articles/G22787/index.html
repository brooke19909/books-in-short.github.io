"Mysfor" (Lazurnoe, Ukraine) - description of a boarding house, services and reviews of vacationers

Pension "Mysfor" (Lazurnoe) is located in the Kherson region of Ukraine. It is located right on the beach, right next to the beach. This recreation center was loved by families with children. There is parking for cars, Internet, and other services needed by modern holidaymakers. You can order food, including a children's menu. Tourists leave good reviews about this guesthouse. People come here to rest not only from Ukraine, but also from neighboring countries - Moldova, Belarus, Russia.



Azure, rest

Misfora is located in the village, which deservedly got its name for the amazing color of sea water. There are many offers for sea holidays. There are boarding houses and recreation centers that have been functioning since Soviet times. But a huge part has been built in recent years. Good competition are mini-hotels and the private sector. The number of resort housing is increasing, because more and more vacationers are coming here.

The village is located a hundred kilometers from Kherson at the base of the protected spit Dzharylgach. The air here is healing, especially for children, cures asthma and other diseases of the respiratory tract. The eastern beaches of the village are interesting in that you can swim here both in the open sea and in the Dzharylgach Bay. It happens that jellyfish come here, and after storms a lot of algae swims in the water. But it pays off with a wonderful climate, warm sea and amazing landscapes. The Dzharylgach spit is separated from the Cote d'Azur by a shallow strait, which at low water levels you can even wade.



Territory and services

Pension "Mysfor" (Lazurnoe) is a few two-three-story buildings and cottages. The area is very stylish, green, with many lawns and flower beds. Everything is very well maintained, harmoniously fits into the aesthetic concept of the base, including the original-shaped pool with a fountain in the middle. At the boarding house there is a guarded parking. You can play billiards and tennis. There is a trampoline for children, a playground, an artificial lake. There is a spa center with a sauna and massage room. A policeman is constantly on duty on the territory. In the evenings, live music is played.

Azure (boarding house "Misfora") is a good starting point for various short and long walks. Therefore, vacationers often book excursions here. Among the most popular are a trip to a healing spring and a geyser, a walk to the Dzharylgach island and a trip to the famous Askania-Nova nature reserve.



Rooms

The recreation center "Mysfor" (Lazurnoe) lodges holidaymakers in rooms with amenities. They are designed for two or three guests, as well as for companies or families of four. All rooms have cozy balconies, a toilet and shower, TVs with satellite channels, refrigerators, electric kettles, safes. The rooms are partially upholstered in wood, good, good furniture, comfortable beds. The rooms are air conditioned.

The rooms for four people are two-room. These are either Suite rooms or cottages with private access to the kindergarten. The rooms are very clean, there is everything you need. Great cleaning, no dust anywhere. Clean and pleasantly smelling bedding. The rooms have good sound insulation, very quiet, no street noise is heard. The sea is visible directly from the windows of most rooms. Tourists write that they are very fond of drinking coffee on the balconies in the mornings, and in the evenings sitting late with a glass of wine, breathing in the breeze.

Can I order accommodation with food?

Vacationers in the village of Lazurnoe (boarding house "Mysfor") can order food at their discretion, including only breakfast, half-board or three meals a day. By the way, a morning meal is included in the price of rooms of any category. Cafe-bar "Neptune" and barbecue work right on the beach. Grilled meat is fried in the evenings. Everywhere catches wi-fi. The cuisine is delicious, cooked at home. The cafe has an open terrace on the terrace and gazebos in the shade.

Prices are very affordable, especially considering that Neptune is located right on the seashore. Cheaper you can eat at the market, in the center of "Azure", in the cafe "Glass". For lovers of exclusive food recommend the institution "Shambhala." They make good sushi and meat in Milan.
