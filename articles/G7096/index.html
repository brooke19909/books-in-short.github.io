Hotel Radisson, Adler: description, photos and reviews

A truly royal holiday will give you the Radisson Hotel. Adler is a great place for lovers of marine entertainment.

Brief description of the hotel

If you are looking for a place for a summer vacation, then pay attention to the Radisson Hotel. Adler attracts attention with beautiful beaches and mountain landscapes. Guests of the resort complex are provided with a huge list of services that meet the most modern requirements. This is high-speed wireless Internet, and a modern gym, and a large outdoor freshwater pool, and a tennis court, as well as a steam sauna, Turkish bath and much more.

It should be noted the number of rooms available to the Radisson Hotel. Adler is fraught with many interesting places, attractions and entertainment, which requires a lot of energy to visit. You can make up for it thanks to a complete rest in comfortable apartments, decorated in calm, warm colors. It is also worth noting the presence of a complete list of all the necessary amenities that a modern tourist needs. And thanks to the presence of your own balcony, you can enjoy the picturesque scenery without leaving your room.

Be sure to visit the restaurants that the Radisson Hotel can rightfully be proud of. Adler is rich in catering establishments, but why experiment with delicious food at your hotel. There are three restaurants here, where you can taste European, Japanese, Mediterranean, as well as oriental dishes. If necessary, you can order a comprehensive meal or include only breakfast in the price. Meals can also be delivered directly to the apartment.





Hotel Location

Many tourists choose the Radisson Hotel (Adler) for their summer vacation. The address of this institution is st. Blue 1 / A. This luxurious establishment is located on the seashore. The distance to Sochi is about 27 km. If you want to visit Krasnaya Polyana, then you have to overcome about 40 km. The distance to the nearest airport is no more than 5 kilometers.

Radisson Hotel (Adler), reviews: advantages

Reviews about this resort and hotel complex contain the following positive information:

  • the price is fully consistent with the quality of the services provided;
  • friendly and smiling staff who in every possible way takes care of a comfortable rest of guests;
  • delicious and hearty breakfasts;
  • convenient location - there are many resort infrastructure facilities nearby;
  • beautiful view from the windows of the rooms;
  • excellent gym;
  • Cleanliness of the rooms and the hotel as a whole at the highest level.

Negative hotel reviews

Guests of the Radisson Hotel note the following negative points:

  • the beach is located far enough from the hotel, but a free shuttle saves the situation;
  • waiters in restaurants are not very polite;
  • rather weak animation;
  • Of the outdoor activities, only a tennis court is available;
  • when checking in, you need to make a deposit for additional services that you may not use;
  • large pebbles on the beach, which is why it is very inconvenient to enter the sea.

General impression

The Radisson Hotel in Adler is an excellent accommodation option for those who are used to relaxing on a grand scale. Particular attention should be paid to the design of the rooms, as well as their thoughtful layout and modern equipment. The hotel has all the conditions for both leisure and business people. You will enjoy a delicious and hearty breakfast buffet. Of the shortcomings, it is worth noting the remoteness from the beach, as well as a limited amount of outdoor entertainment. The work of adult animators is also not satisfactory. In general, it is worth noting that the quality of services provided in this hotel corresponds to their cost.
