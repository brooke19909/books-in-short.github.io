Cheap hotels of Omsk: photos and reviews

The leisure industry is gaining momentum more and more. People turn to hotel services for a variety of reasons. Whether it's just a desire to relax from the hustle and bustle of everyday life or the walls of your home, a business trip to another city or the need to spend the night, hotels always open their doors to their guests. Their existence makes life much easier, because a hotel is not just a place to spend the night, but a whole range of services that are available to each of their visitors.

At the same time, people are looking for a good place with quality service, but they can not always afford to pay a large amount for a room. That's when inexpensive hotels come to the rescue . Omsk, like many other cities, offers accommodation in fairly good places for a small fee. This helps people out and allows them to solve a problem with a combination of quality and price.

Hotel Aero

Aero is one of those places that characterize as inexpensive hotels. Omsk is full of such services, which allows people who are careful about spending their money to choose the right place to stay. Hotel Aero is located opposite the airport and is ready to receive guests at any time.

The number of rooms consists of sixteen rooms of different levels of comfort, namely, "Economy", "Comfort", "Standard" and "Standard plus". Turning to the services of this hotel, everyone can choose a room based on their budget. High-quality repairs, good furniture and room equipment with everything necessary allow guests to get the desired quality for reasonable money. Guest reviews about this place are positive and boil down to the fact that Aero is a decent hotel for a good time.

Hotel Millennium

What to do if you need a place to relax for just a few hours and there is no way to pay for a day in a room? In this situation, cheap hotels in Omsk will help out. Hourly services that can be obtained in such places are much cheaper, which is not reflected in any way on their quality. One such hotel is the Millennium. Within its walls, tourists, athletes, students who can get a place in a colorful, modern room for a small price are gladly welcomed.

The network of these hotels provides access to their services in the city center, near the train station or near the airport. You can stay in a room of different levels of comfort, while receiving high-quality service and good mood. Guests can take advantage of additional services, namely: ordering excursions, transfers to the station or airport, washing and ironing. The qualified staff of the Millennium Hotel will answer all questions, monitor the implementation of orders and ensure the best possible rest.

Hotel "Tourist"

By popularity in the city "Tourist" heads all the cheap hotels. Omsk is a real treasure for a tourist, and you need to approach the choice of a hotel responsibly. The number of rooms of the Tourist Hotel is the largest in the area. It is very easy to find this hotel, because it is located in the very center of the city and every resident knows about it. Being in the center of Omsk's cultural life, the Tourist Hotel provides its guests with a good pastime. Near the main building there are parks, philharmonic societies and theaters.

All 170 rooms are characterized by modern conveniences, homeliness and are divided into different categories: Standard, Business Class and Deluxe Suite. All of them are decorated in a classic style, are particularly spacious and illuminated. Guests have the opportunity to use Wi-Fi and enjoy drinks from the minibar in each room. Prices and level of service are incredibly attractive, and therefore the choice of many falls precisely on the hotel "Tourist".

Hotel Pilot

The list of "Good and affordable hotels in Omsk in the city center" includes Pilot - a cozy, bright place where you can have a great time and take refuge from everyday worries. The number of rooms is small, but each option has been worked out to the smallest detail.

There are four Junior Suite rooms, which include a bed, a wardrobe, a dressing table, an armchair, a bathroom, and a Suite, which includes a kitchen, sofa, TV and bar. On the territory there is a equipped barbecue area, a sauna and a banquet room. Hotel Pilot hospitably welcomes new faces and provides quality service for a very small fee. The hotel does not have Internet access, but this does not interfere with a pleasant pastime.

Hotel Avanta

Inexpensive hotels in Omsk on the left bank of the Irtysh also allow you to enjoy the privacy and good service to your guests. Avanta is located 30 minutes from the airport and railway station and can simultaneously serve eight people.

The main building has two floors, which are single and double rooms. They have a fridge, a bathroom with a bath or shower, a TV and a DVD player. Nearby are a club-restaurant and cafe-bar; Meals can be ordered directly to your room. The minimum cost in the hotel "Avanta" allows you to have a great time and get your desired vacation.
