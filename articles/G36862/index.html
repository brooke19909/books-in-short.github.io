IPad mini: general and technical specifications

Instead of their full-format tablet brothers come compact, stylish laptops in no way inferior in functionality to mini-format laptops. In practice, these are reduced copies. So, a year ago, Apple introduced the iPad mini on the market, the characteristics of which are very close to those of a full-format tablet. They began to release this laptop, intending to compete with devices based on android. An interesting fact is that Apple founder Steve Jobs was against the release of gadgets with a screen diagonal of less than 10 inches. It is this size that allows applications designed for tablets to work most accurately without freezes and crashes. But after the Apple guru passed away, the company still decided to change such a policy.

IPad mini: general characteristics

Outwardly unremarkable device. The case is aluminum. Due to this, the mini iPad, the characteristics of which we are considering, has a rather small weight. On average, it is 310 grams. You must admit that such a tablet is very convenient on the road when every extra item makes your bag heavy. TO

Another plus can be attributed to compact dimensions. Its size is 200x135x8 mm. This allows the palm to grip the device securely. If we talk about external devices that are located throughout the case, then in the first place it is worth mentioning the stereo speakers that are on the lower edge. Between them, manufacturers have identified a place for the first use of the Lightning connector on the iPhone 5. It is characteristic in that it transmits only a digital signal (the previous connectors supported analog transmission). A video / camera eye is located above the display. Below the screen is the Home button. On the right edge is the volume control. There is also an image lock button. We pass to the upper edge. It has a power strip, jacks for earphone plugs, a microphone. In general, the mini iPad, the characteristics of the appearance of which we examined, retains the trend of minimalism. This does not detract from its merits.
