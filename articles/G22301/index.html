Laguna Beach Resort 4 * (Egypt, Marsa Alam): hotel description, reviews

This place is famous for its untouched beauty of nature, clean beaches, mild climate and luxurious hotel complexes. One of them is Laguna Beach Resort 4 *. This place can offer the traveler comfortable rooms, delicious food, genuine hospitality and plenty of entertainment.

Where is the hotel? What does the territory look like?





The new Laguna Beach Resort 4 * hotel is located on the picturesque coast of the Red Sea, at a distance from the bustle of the city. That is why it is ideal for a relaxing holiday in the lap of nature. Marsa Alam city center is approximately 17 km away. But the airport is not too close, about 75 km, so you should prepare for a long trip. On the other hand, the hotel provides tourists with a comfortable transfer. Hurghada is 350 km away.

This complex was opened in 2012. Every year it is improved more and more, and today it is fully consistent with European standards. Here you will find a spacious area with walking alleys, decorated with palm trees, figured bushes, bright flowers. The hotel itself consists of the main building and small two-story bungalows, where the living rooms are located. There are swimming pools, sports fields, in short, everything you need for a busy vacation.

Hotel Laguna Beach Resort 4 * (Marsa Alam): what do the rooms look like?





This hotel complex is considered relatively small, but very comfortable. There are 96 rooms on its territory, including:

  • rooms with two single beds;
  • rooms with one king size bed;
  • family rooms with one double and two single beds for children;
  • family rooms with a large bed for parents and two soft folding sofas.

Whatever number you choose, you can count on homeliness and stylish atmosphere. The balcony offers views of the sea or the surrounding area. As for household appliances, it is new, in good condition - there is a TV, air conditioning, fan, safe, a small refrigerator for storing drinks, a telephone with direct dialing.

The spacious bathroom is always refreshing after a long day. In addition to the shower, there is a bidet, a washbasin, wall mirrors, a hair dryer, toiletries, large clean towels.

What kind of food can you count on?





Like most Egyptian resort complexes, Laguna Beach Resort 4 * offers its guests good nutrition. This is very convenient, because there are practically no other places near the hotel where you can eat.

Three times a day for residents organize receptions in the main restaurant of the hotel. Traveler reviews say that the food here is quite good - the menu includes various slices, snacks, meat and fish dishes, fresh fruits, vegetable salads, in short, everything you need.

During the day, you can grab a bite to eat or have a drink at the beachfront cafe. Guests often relax in the stylish bar, which is in the hotel lobby - they also offer drinks, cocktails and aromatic oriental coffee. In addition, it is worth visiting the picturesque Bedouin tents, where you will be transported to another world. It serves traditional snacks as well as hookah.

Beach and water activities





Tourists come to this city in search of a pleasant beach holiday. Hotel Laguna Beach Resort Marsa Alam 4 * owns its own stretch of coast, near which it was built. The beach here is secluded, covered with pure golden sand. There is a backwater where children like to spend time. Guests are provided with free sun loungers, umbrellas, mattresses and fresh beach towels.

The main attraction of the hotel is the large coral reefs located some distance from the coast (but you can walk by sand). Naturally, one of the favorite activities of tourists is swimming with a mask, as well as diving. The hotel constantly has a club of divers, where with a certificate you can rent equipment. Beginners are required to take a training course (does not last long). Of course, you can do water sports, ride a boat, water ski, boat, etc.

Vacation with a child: are there any conditions?

For many tourists, it is important to have services that provide the child with the most comfortable rest. What can the Laguna Beach Resort 4 * hotel complex offer? Marsa Alam is an excellent place for children's recreation and recreation. There are spacious family rooms with beds for children, and in the restaurant there are always some delicious dishes for the baby.

For the convenience of parents, a mini-club is constantly working, where funny animators and experienced teachers remain with the children. Your child will be able to play with new friends, draw, dance, take part in theatrical performances, competitions, team games, etc. Near the club there is a small shallow pool where children can splash and sunbathe.

Egypt, Marsa Alam, Hotel Laguna Beach 4 *: additional service





As the tourists themselves say, this hotel has everything you need for a comfortable pastime. Here you can exchange currency or pay with a credit card. Laundry and dry cleaning services are constantly available. There is also a small shopping arcade, consisting of several shops - you can buy a lot from beach slippers and batteries to interesting souvenirs and jewelry.

The Laguna Beach Resort Hotel has a small clinic, and in case of health problems, a qualified doctor is called to guests. There is free internet access in the lobby. If this is not enough for you, then for a small fee you can connect to the World Wide Web throughout the hotel, including rooms. Parents will certainly be pleased with the presence of professional nannies, who for a small fee will stay for several hours with their child.

How do tourists relax?

Laguna Beach Resort 4 * is an ideal place for a pleasant leisurely vacation. Naturally, you can have some fun here. For example, guests often relax by the central pool, around which sun loungers are placed. Here you can swim, play water polo, sunbathe, enjoying the sounds of nature or just enjoy chatting with other travelers.





There is also a smaller pool, next to which there is a spacious jacuzzi. You can relax in the fresh air. If you wish, you can play volleyball. There are tables for tennis and billiards.

Hotel Laguna Beach Resort 4 * offers a tour center. Here you can find tours for every taste, from desert safaris to a trip to the Nile, a guided tour of the legendary ancient complex of Luxor.

The hotel has a team of animators. Reviews indicate that the animation here is fun, but unobtrusive. In the daytime, children organize various contests, competitions, conduct sports, and in the evenings, guests are invited to watch bright shows in the spacious amphitheater on the seashore.

Hotel reviews of travelers

A lot of useful information can be obtained by asking for advice from people who managed to visit a particular hotel. What do tourists say about the Laguna Beach Resort 4 * hotel? Reviews are mostly positive. The hotel is small and extremely comfortable - everyone here knows each other in person, they even spend evenings in warm communication with new friends. The rooms are cozy and clean, furnished with new furniture. Some tourists complain that the bathroom does not always have hot water, although guests rarely encounter this problem.





The food is also quite satisfactory. Of course, for people who spend weeks at the hotel, the menu seems a little monotonous over time, but no one will be hungry. Particularly interesting for tourists are colorful desert tents.

The undoubted advantage is the proximity to the beach. It is calm and not crowded, coral reefs are incredibly beautiful - many divers come here, wanting to enjoy the bright colors of the underwater world.

The only disadvantages are remoteness from the city, since there are only other hotels and the desert around. On the other hand, you can have a good time in the desert, riding ATVs, spending the night in tents, etc. According to reviews, the Laguna Beach Resort Marsa Alam 4 * hotel is a place that is intended for lovers of a relaxing beach holiday.
