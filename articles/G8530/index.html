License Issues

Always one of the advantages of the company was the availability of a license from the Alpari broker issued by the Central Bank of the Russian Federation. However, in December 2018, the company had significant problems.

In the last days of the calendar year, it became known that the Alpari broker had its license revoked. At one point, she lost two more major forex companies - TeleTrade and Forex Club. In addition, the certificates of their top managers were canceled.

After that, conversations immediately began that the Alpari broker had expired. The claims that the Central Bank put forward to the company were in violation of the requirements, the requirements for the ratio of the amount of security that was provided to individuals, to the procedure for maintaining an internal account. Also, the revocation of the license from the Alpari broker was associated with the provision of false information on financial statements and violations committed in the field of organizing a risk management system.

It is worth noting that these forex companies have received a large number of complaints.
