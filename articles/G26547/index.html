Megafon (North-West): how to call the operator? How to call the operator "Megaphone"

Megafon, like other operators of the Big Three, has branches throughout Russia. In each region, accordingly, there is a contact center, as the number of subscribers does not allow to quickly serve everyone using one office.

Each subscriber can contact the support service through 0500. In this case, the number is automatically assigned to any region, and the person gets to his "operator".





For example, if a Megafon client (North-West) decides to call the operator while in Novosibirsk at 0500, he will contact his native branch, not Siberian, or it will not be possible to make a call.

Opportunities

You can call the CC for any reason related to communication services. You can dial 0500 even from a number that was blocked for loss or voluntarily for 6 months. It is also possible to contact the call center for free if the money runs out on the number.

All this is provided to the subscriber if he is in the home area, that is, in that region of the Megafon company (North-West branch), the operators of which connected a SIM card.

Write-offs as one of the reasons for the appeal

In the event that the subscriber has problems with the fact that money is debited from the account, the operator simply needs to call. How to call the operator regarding services

By contacting the company by phone, you can disable or activate the services. In addition, the specialist will tell you which option will be more convenient and profitable, or will offer a service that reduces the cost of non-communication, which the subscriber did not even suspect.





The principles of services themselves throughout Russia at Megafon do not differ. There is a difference only in cost and number of incoming minutes, traffic or SMS.

The unification was made several years ago for the convenience of subscribers, KC specialists and communication salons. Previously, the options provided for subscribers of Kemerovo and Vladikavkaz, for example, were so different that often there was a misunderstanding.

How to call an operator in roaming

In guest networks, subscriber support also exists, as it is especially necessary for people who have left the home region. When traveling in Russia, subscribers are advised to dial the long federal number 8-800-550-05-00.





When roaming outside Russia, the following question often arises for a Megafon subscriber (North-West): how to call the operator? For a call you need to use the numbers + 7-928-111-05-00, since 8-800 will not work abroad or can be perceived by the guest network as an outgoing international one.

When dialing + 7-928, a system call is automatically made to the client. Incoming calls from the operator for subscribers in international roaming are free.
