Hotel Coral Beach 5 * (Paphos, Cyprus): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Would you like to spend your vacation in a luxury 5-star hotel in Cyprus, enjoying the high level of service and maximum comfort? Then you should go to Coral Beach 5 * located in Paphos. This is not just a hotel, but a complex with a superbly developed tourist infrastructure, as well as a very remarkable place. However, all this should be told in detail.





Briefly about the hotel

Paphos is a southwestern part of Cyprus. The hotel complex is located not in the city itself, but on the edge of the Akamas Peninsula, which is not only a part of it, but also a UNESCO-protected territory.

To the sea from the hotel - just a stone's throw. The complex offers magnificent views of the waters of the Coral Bay and the golden sandy beaches.

Interesting sights are dispersed at a distance of 5 to 15 kilometers. Among them - a park of birds and animals, the church of Agios Georgios, the falls of the Baths of Adonis, the monastery of St. Neophytos, the tombs of kings. And this is only a small part of the places that are definitely worth a visit!

What about transport infrastructure? The nearest airport is a 15-20 minute drive from the complex. And right in front of the hotel there is a bus stop, from where shuttles to the center of Paphos leave every 15 minutes.

For sports lovers

On the territory of Coral Beach Hotel Resort 5 * located in Paphos, there is a professional tennis center with five courts, each of which is equipped with a soft coating and lighting. Anyone can come here to realize their sports talents. Including beginners, after all, private and group classes are held here, so everyone has the opportunity to master the racket.

A 25-minute drive from the hotel is his own golf club with three huge 18-hole courses. It is here that official tournaments for this game are regularly held.

Another feature of the Coral Beach 5 * hotel in Paphos is the Olympic pool, which meets all the standards of the International Swimming Federation. It reaches a length of 50 meters in length, 25 m in width, and 2 m in depth. An electronic controller maintains a temperature of 25-28 ¬∞ . The pool is easily transformed into a field for water polo, synchronized swimming, and is also divided into paths.

The hotel also has a gym. It has everything - from bicycles and treadmills to barbells, kettlebells and even a trampoline. Even professional athletes can conduct a full-fledged training here.

In addition, the largest diving center in Paphos adjoins the Coral Beach 5 * hotel (Paphos, Cyprus). You should definitely sign up there, as the waters of the Coral Bay are the best for scuba diving on the whole island. This is a unique chance to see underwater amphora, caves, ruins and wreckage of ships, whose age is more than 2000 years.

Hotel Restaurants

They also need to be noted by attention, telling about the Coral Beach Hotel Resort 5 * hotel (Cyprus, Paphos). In total, there are 8 establishments in the complex:

  • Restaurant "Coral". It is located in a beautiful garden, surrounded by trees, near the pool. Designed for 230 people. Open for breakfast (7:00 - 10:00), lunch (13:00 - 15:00) and dinner (19:00 - 21:30). The main specialization is international cuisine. Coral also regularly hosts theme nights where Spanish, Oriental, Mexican and Cypriot delicacies are offered, as well as barbecue dishes.
  • Restaurant "Calypso". Its feature is the terrace, which offers an amazing panoramic view of the sea. Designed for 112 people, open only in the evening (from 19:30 to 21:30). It has a pleasant atmosphere, live music always plays, and serves international and Cypriot cuisine.
  • Restaurant "Romanzzino". Small luxury restaurant for up to 30 guests. Also works only in the evening. Specialization - Italian cuisine. Restrictions apply - guests under 13 years old are not allowed here, as well as people who do not match the evening dress code.
  • Restaurant "La Rotisserie". The conditions are the same as in Romanzzino, only this establishment specializes in French cuisine and is open from 19:30 to 22:00.
  • Tavern "Limani". Located on the beach, next to a private harbor. International cuisine is served, and there is also a rich and impressive assortment of fish, vegetarian and meat menus.
  • Restaurant - The Deck Bar with an open kitchen . Located near the sea.
  • Mediterranean restaurant "Molos". The main specialization is seafood dishes. Located near the harbor, designed for 20 people. Opens at 19:30, finishes work at 22:00. It works only in the summer season.
  • Restaurant "Armonia". A restaurant with a spacious terrace that can accommodate 400 people. As a rule, gala dinners, banquets and ceremonies are held here.

Bars

In addition to restaurants, the hotel has four bars. Here is a summary of them:

Well, the Coral Beach 5 * hotel (Cyprus, Paphos) really has a lot of establishments. Each guest will be able to find one that will appeal to him.
