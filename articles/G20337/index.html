Knitted cat hat - a fashionable and beautiful hat

Animal hats are very popular today. Hats with the image of animal faces look original and interesting. This accessory is suitable for both kids and adults. Caps are made of various materials: fabric, fur, leather and yarn. It is about the headdress of hand knitting that we will discuss in this article. After examining the information presented here, you will find out how a cat-hat is crocheted and knitted . The description of the process of performing these accessories is given in great detail and is designed for both experienced and beginner needlewomen. Take a look at the photo of cat hats, perhaps some of them will serve as inspiration and incentive for creativity. Easy eyelets to all of you!



Fashion accessory - cat hat

Knitting such a headdress with knitting needles is easy. For work, take 100 grams of wool / acrylic yarn (100 g / 300 m), knitting needles No. 3, a needle, a little padding polyester, a dark mouline thread . The product associated with this description will fit a head volume of 50-52 centimeters.

We are training. Preparatory stage

The cat hat will be made with English rubber. Read the description and practice knitting a pattern. 1st row: 1 facial loop, 2nd - wrong. Repeat in this sequence until the end of the row. 2nd row: 1 front knit under the loop of the previous row, 1 wrong side - in the usual way. Continue this sequence of loops until the end of the row. All subsequent rows fit in the same way as the 2nd.



Process has begun

Dial 25 loops and perform them with an English rubber band as described. Starting from the third row and in each subsequent front row, add 1 loop at the beginning. When there are 32 loops on the knitting needles, set aside the work. Now tie another detail in the same way (symmetrical). Next, transfer both parts of the product to one spoke so that the bevels are inside, while additionally dial 37 stitches between them. You should now have 101 loops in your work. Knit a straight cloth of the correct height. Close the loop, fasten the thread.

Stitch along the top and back of the product. Type along the bottom edge of the loop and knit several rows of garter or stocking knit to form a bar. Fold it to the wrong side and hem.

To make the cat hat look plausible, select the ears on it. To do this, put the filler in the upper corners and sew them obliquely. Embroider the muzzle with thread floss.

Preparation of materials for crochet animal hats

According to the description below, it is advisable to perform a headdress with fluffy thread ("grass", mohair). Measure the volume of the head and divide the result by 2. This number indicates the width of one half of the product. Next, take the size of the head from the forehead to the crown - this is the height of the cap. Write data or draw a rectangular pattern. For work, prepare a hook, the number of which corresponds to the thickness of the yarn, and a needle.



How to crochet a cat hat?

Make a chain of air loops long corresponding to the width of the headgear. Run the fabric in straight rows with single crochet posts. Close the knitting when the height of the product is equal to double the height of the pattern. Fold the resulting rectangle in half and form the side seams. Sew the corners obliquely. Tie a spout of black thread in the form of a triangle or an oval, round eyes of white and dark thread. Sew these details on the hat. Embroider the antennae. The work is over.

Similarly, you can perform other models of animals. The principle of implementation itself remains the same, the difference is only in the design of the muzzle.
