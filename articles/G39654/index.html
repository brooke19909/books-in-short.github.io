Holidays in Sharm El Sheikh - it is not only a surprisingly warm sea, but also coral reefs, as well as good service. Continental Garden Reef Resort 5 *

The hotel is located in El Pasha Bay - one of the best areas of the famous resort of Sharm El Sheikh. Just three kilometers from it begins the promenade Naama Bay, which is popular for the promenade, where life is vibrant around the clock and is full of tourists. Fifteen minutes drive to the Old Town. Continental Garden Reef 5 * is located on the first line of the Red Sea. General information, prices

The former name of the hotel is Intercontinental Garden Reef. It was built in 2002. The last overhaul by the owners of Continental Garden Reef 5 * was carried out four years ago. Then the territory and buildings were landscaped, the plumbing was completely replaced, the rooms were furnished with new furniture. Number of rooms

Considered large in terms of number of inhabitants, Continental Garden Reef Sharm 5 * has four hundred twenty-two rooms. Up to three thousand people can live in them at the same time. The rooms are located in two- and three-story white buildings located on four lines. In front of everyone there are balconies overlooking the sea or the ridge of the Sinai Mountains.

In addition to the standard, the Continental Garden Reef Resort 5 * has five youth and four superior rooms, presidential and royal apartments, as well as six adjacent suites, which can accommodate up to six people at a time.





Standard rooms

Continental Garden Reef Resort 5 standard can accommodate a maximum of 2 + 1 people. Their area is twenty square meters. The flooring in the rooms is ceramic tile. The rooms are decorated in oriental style, the windows have bright curtains, there is also a work desk, split air conditioning (individually adjustable), TV, electronic safe, for which you need to pay for the specialist to show how it works. The walls are painted in pastel colors, a large makeup mirror hangs on the wall. In the minibar for new guests there are two bottles of water, any soft drink or juice.

In the combined bathroom there are appliances: bath with shower, washbasin with countertop and toilet. There are also hair dryers and a variety of beauty products.

Four rooms are for disabled guests. They are located on the ground floor and have specially equipped handicapped showers and balconies. In one of the buildings there are rooms for smokers.





Superior rooms

The presidential and royal rooms can easily accommodate four people. They have two bedrooms with interior doors, as well as a study. This room type has french beds. The balconies are furnished with comfortable sun loungers and a coffee table. Flooring - modern tiles and carpets. There is also a refrigerator, minibar, telephone with intercity exit, exercise machine, safe. The apartments have wireless internet connection.

Bathrooms and toilets are separate. There are showers, bidets. For residents provided hairdryer, slippers, bathrobes. All rooms have individual air conditioning.





In addition to the standard range of services, the superior apartments have LCD TV, upholstered furniture, a bookcase, a coffee table, a dressing table.

Room service

Every day, all rooms are carefully cleaned, cosmetic accessories are replaced. On request, you can get extra bedding and a cot in the room. An iron and ironing board are available upon request. There are services "breakfast in the room" and "alarm clock". Even the smallest pets are not allowed by the property.

Continental Garden Reef 5 * also offers to visit one of four bars: in the lobby, on the beach, by the pool. These are ideal places to relax with a drink of your favorite cocktail.
