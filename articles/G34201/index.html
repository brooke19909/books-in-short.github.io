The recreation center "Achigvar Lake" in Adler: description, features and reviews

Fishing is a great way to spend your free time. This is fresh air, unforgettable impressions and the beauty of nature. In the resort cities of Russia, more and more attention is paid to this particular type of outdoor activity. Base "Achigvar Lake" will pass the time, doing freshwater fishing. The complex has a cafe and baths. This is one of the available places for a quality holiday with friends or family.

Location

Perhaps everyone is familiar with the beautiful resort city of Sochi. Achigvar Lake is located here, in one of its areas - Adler. Here, both local and city guests are happy to relax. The complex offers several reservoirs in which a lot of fish are found. These are not just lakes with dense thickets: the places are ennobled by beautiful and cozy buildings, houses, bridges. You can hide from the weather and the summer heat in a bungalow, while not breaking away from the process of fishing.

Services offered

It all starts with the arrival of the lake. Visitors are offered free secure parking, so you can not worry about your personal car. Friendly staff will introduce you to the infrastructure of the complex. Neat stone tile paths lead to the lake. Everywhere there are trees and blooming flower beds.

If the purpose of the trip was fishing, the visitor will be offered to choose a lake, as well as the necessary equipment. For each fisherman or group of vacationers, a bungalow is offered. In the process, you can fry the kebab and cook the ear from the first catch.





Satisfy your hunting instincts will allow shooting from a crossbow. The complex is inhabited by rabbits, pheasants, ducks, turkeys, guinea fowls, goats. These animals may well become prey.

For families, the complex has a sports ground and equipment for it, a swimming pond and a swimming pool. Built Russian baths. On the territory of the lake there are restaurants where you can spend a full corporate party or celebrate a holiday with family and friends. There are rooms for night rest.

Fishing on Achigvar Lake

Relaxation on a large lake is very convenient both for fishing and for a picnic. Its structure is very interesting: an island was built in the middle. From it lead paths, paved with stone, in the direction of the bamboo arbors. Everywhere neat well-groomed lawns, trees. A waterfall was created on the islet, and proud peacocks and black swans walk along the paths.





All necessary equipment will be brought by personnel, including top dressing and bait. Visitors only have to enjoy nature and fishing. Achigvar Lake in Adler is rich in fish species such as carp, carp, trout, grass carp. Pecks actively. On average, it takes 5-7 minutes to catch one fish. But breaks do happen. For example, at lunchtime, biting is less intense.

VIP lake

Achigvar lake of the VIP-zone is small. This is a great option for a quiet secluded holiday. Here, no one bothers to relax and enjoy the calm fishing process. The fish in the small lake are slightly larger: sturgeon, Canadian catfish, common carp, trout, carp, grass carp and tilapia are found.





The staff will offer all the necessary equipment for fishing. In addition, you can use the barbecue for cooking barbecue, as well as bake the caught catch. Bridges were built on the territory of the lake, from the height of which you can also fish. There are gazebos, bungalows, hammocks and houses for a pleasant stay in between fishing.

Russian baths

Achigvar Lake in Adler is famous not only for the beauty of nature and the diversity of the catch, but also for real Russian baths. In the forest silence on the upper tier (10 m above the reservoir) there are two-story huts. They are drowned on birch wood. The visitor is offered brooms from the foliage of eucalyptus, fir, nettle, birch, juniper and oak.





Each bath is equipped with a font with hot water and a tub with cold. In the hut there is an additional pool with cool running water. The location of the baths is very convenient: a beautiful view of the entire recreation area opens from a height. In the hammock you can fully enjoy the fresh air and beautiful views.
