Resort "Usolye" in the Irkutsk region: address, description, treatment and reviews

The resort "Usolye" is a world health resort located in the Irkutsk region. She has a very rich history. Many famous people rested here. The resort receives treatment annually thousands of residents from different regions of the country. Here you can not only undergo treatment and rehabilitation for various diseases, but also visit interesting excursions and enjoy the beauty of the region.

Where is

The resort "Usolye Sibirsky" is located on the coast of Ankora in the Irkutsk region. The exact address of the health resort: st. Lenin, 1-1, in the city of the same name with a sanatorium.

The resort is open throughout the year. You can get a ticket at travel agencies that work with a health resort, book online or by phone.

The resort "Usolye" in the Irkutsk region has a number of features that make it unique:

  1. The unique composition of mineral water is based on the presence of almost all the elements of the periodic table. And also the salinity of the salt composition exceeds the concentration of the Dead Sea.
  2. In 2018, the sanatorium will turn 170 years old, which means that vast experience and a medical base are better here than anywhere else.
  3. A large staff makes it possible to develop a wellness program for each vacationer, taking into account all the features of his health.
  4. Comfortable rooms allow you to completely relax and enjoy the rest.
  5. A huge selection of medical procedures, including unique ones that are not found in other resorts.
  6. Stunning views from the windows of the rooms to the local nature and clean Baikal air.

There is also an extensive entertainment program in the sanatorium, which is pleasant for vacationers of any age. It further diversifies the stay of guests of the health resort.
