Plasticine robot: how to make a toy with your own hands

Homemade toys are the joy of all children. If parents do not know what to make for their child, a robot from plasticine is suitable, this does not require much effort. Older children can do it themselves, but if something doesn‚Äôt work out, you can call for help from parents.

How to make a robot from plasticine?





A plasticine robot will surely please the boy, because these young masters love to create interesting crafts from improvised things and father's tools.

A plasticine robot is made very simple, you just need to follow the instructions, and after a few minutes the child will be content with a new original craft.

In fact, a robot from plasticine with your own hands is done very easily. This activity is very exciting, so the children will gladly take up the creation of a new interesting toy.

Step-by-step instruction





To create a craft, you need to focus on the instructions:

  1. Initially, it is necessary to prepare material for work. Plasticine of any color is suitable for a home-made robot . In addition, a stack is required.
  2. From the bar of plasticine, you need to separate the third part and roll it into a ball. It must be gradually crushed with the thumb and forefinger. This should be done until the moment when it takes the form of a parallelepiped.
  3. To make the head a plasticine robot, you need to take some more material for modeling in the same color as for the body. First you need to form a ball, then a cube. Then it should be pressed down on one side.
  4. Between the two parts you can make a layer of red or orange. It is important that it is in contrast.
  5. The match must be divided in half and stick the parts into the crown instead of the antenna.
  6. Two bright plasticine flashlights of any color can be attached to the ends of matches.
  7. In order to make the hands of the robot, you need to roll plasticine into balls. Between them, you should make a layer of the same color as the one that connects the body with the head.
  8. Instead of brushes, the toy may have claws that also need to be fashioned.
  9. Hands must be attached to the body.
  10. According to the same instruction, you need to collect the legs and attach them to the craft. Matches or wire may become fasteners. Thus, a plasticine robot will have moving arms and legs.
  11. The final stage of the creation of the robot involves the addition of teeth and eyes, which are also molded from plasticine of a certain color. On the chest, you can attach control buttons.

Conclusion





At this, the master class is over. It turned out an interesting robot from plasticine. It is very easy to make it step by step. You can play with such a craft without fear for the fact that it will break and lose its original appearance. If one of the parts falls off, everything can be repaired without problems.

To create such a toy, you can use clay of any color. Little masters can experiment with shades and make several different robots at once. If the house does not have plasticine in different colors - it does not matter. You can make a robot from a block of the same color.
