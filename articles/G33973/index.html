Khimki hostels and other inexpensive places to stay for visitors

Khimki is the third most populated city in the Moscow region after Podolsk and Balashikha. Many years ago it was only a small village. Today Khimki is an administrative-territorial unit that is constantly growing and developing. Perhaps that is why the city is visited daily by a huge number of people. Some come here on a business trip, while others come to work. Both need housing. True, not everyone can afford an expensive hotel. Khimki hostels are always ready to provide city guests with affordable and decent housing.

Dormitory in the Firsanovka microdistrict

The issues of temporary residence are more interested in people who come to the city to earn money. Due to lack of finance, hotels, even the cheapest ones, cannot afford them. Rented apartments are also expensive. In addition, it is necessary to draw up appropriate contracts. And this requires additional time and financial costs. Dormitory in the neighborhood Skhodnya

Very often, employers use hostel services to accommodate their employees. For example, a group of builders travels to an object. The company's management must provide people with accommodation for the duration of their work in accordance with the contract. Khimki hostels often accept such collective applications. For example, in the Skhodnya microdistrict, the Absolute company offers its customers accommodation in a convenient two-story building. The building has only 11 rooms, which can accommodate 168 people. This is relatively small by city standards. In each separate room, depending on the specific application, from 8 to 20 people can simultaneously live. The rooms have bunk beds, a desk, chests of drawers for personal items, a TV and a washbasin. In the kitchen you can cook yourself. To do this, there are electric stoves, microwave ovens, refrigerators and work desks. After a hard day, people in the showers can wash and iron their clothes. The minimum cost of living in such a place per person is 180 rubles per day. For large collective applications, serious discounts are provided.

Hostels Khimki

Recently, hostels have become very popular in the hotel business system. These establishments are intended for temporary residence of clients with a small income. Most often, the owners of construction companies use their services to place work crews. People are offered accommodation in normal, comfortable conditions. And for their employer it costs quite inexpensively. Which is the best hostel in Khimki? Here everything will depend on where this or that institution is located. It should be convenient for people to get to work. Among the most popular hostels of the city are:

  • "Comrade";
  • "Eagle";

Living conditions in them are almost the same. For example, in the Tovarishch hostel located on Melnikov Avenue, there are 30 dormitory rooms for customers.
