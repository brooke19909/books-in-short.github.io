Manor of the Taneyevs: the history of the Taneyev family, location of the museum-estate, reviews and photos

The Taneyev Manor has a rich history. Here the first buildings were completed in 1623. From that time an interesting story began of the Taneyev family and manor. Now in this place there is a resort area, in which residents from different regions of Russia come.

Rod Taneev

The coat of arms of this family speaks for itself. It depicts clouds from which a hand appears with a sword and shield. The Taneyev clan went from Balt, who came to service from Kazan.

There were many governors and centurions in the family. Almost all had merits before the rulers. On this occasion, they were awarded estates. Several lawyers, poets and ministers also left the Taneyev family.

In 1623, Tsar Mikhail gave Tikhon Taneyev the Marinino deserts. Since that time, the construction of the village began here. Then a temple was erected. In 1758, Mikhail Taneyev built a spacious house, which later became the center of the estate.

Members of the Taneyev clans since ancient times were also servants at the palaces of more than one generation of kings. So, Alexander Sergeyevich Taneev (son of Mikhail) became the manager of all financial affairs of Emperor Nicholas I. He was in charge of all financial matters of the tsar and his family.

Then the children of Alexander Sergeyevich continued his work. So, Sergey Alexandrovich worked for a long time in the state chancellery. He had four children who studied at home.

After the advent of Soviet power, mass repression began. They could not be avoided by the Taneyev family. Daughter Anna was imprisoned, parents were also persecuted for a long time, unable to withstand the next repression, died.

The history of the estate

In 1808, construction continued on the territory of the village of Marinino. Andrei Mikhailovich Taneev ordered the demolition of the old wooden church, and a beautiful stone temple was erected instead.

The estate was planted with original linden alleys. Trees grew very close together and created entire arenas on the territory. Inside such bosquets, ponds were dug or covered gazebos were built for recreation.

Balls were often held at the estate. Famous aristocrats from all over the region came here and not only. The Tanenins family were closely friends with the Kutuzovs, Tolstoys, Tchaikovsky, Scriabin, and with some they were even related.

With the advent of Soviet power, the estate was hit hard. Many buildings were demolished, and the stone components were used by the villagers to build their household and utility rooms.

Homestead today

The area located in the Kovrovsky district plunges all visitors into the past. Here the score is lost in time, and it seems that you plunge into the 16th century. The territory of the estate is maximally preserved in its original form.

There are also white arbors with interesting carved details and benches that remind of gatherings and romantic adventures of those times.

Now the Taneyev estate is a museum and leisure complex. It hosts musical evenings for visitors of all ages. In the halls they play the harp, harpsichord, lyre.

activity

The Taneyevs' estate in Marinino regularly hosts theme nights. It often hosts balls based on the 16th century. Guests are provided with costumes typical of those times. Visitors are happy to plunge into the old atmosphere and forget about all their problems during the waltz.

Guests are happy to take strolling walks on the territory of the Taneyevs' estate (Kovrov). Here, many tourists for conversations find new friends and even start a romantic relationship.

On holidays, folk festivals are held here with various competitions and a show program. Folk groups and other artists come here to perform.

In the estate-museum of the Taneyevs there is a spacious hall for celebrations. Here, often large corporations arrange themed corporate parties, and graduates celebrate their evening.

Master classes

In the estate of the Taneyevs (Marinino village), various classes are held with constant regularity for children and adults. For example, professional chefs teach young visitors to cook the simplest dishes of traditional cuisine.

Adult guests can try to implement quite difficult processes for preparing old recipes of the 16-17th century. It is also often visited by masters who teach visitors how to make various crafts with their own hands.

For example, guests here often make guests with teachers of textile dolls, clay crafts. After the master classes, visitors can use the special room for heating food. If large companies gather, they often use the services of cleaning companies.

Shrovetide and Trinity

Here, these holidays are treated in a special way. At the Taneyevs estate (photo in the article), festivities are organized. Hundreds of people come here from different regions of the country.

There are fun contests in the fresh air. Guests are happy to dance together, sing folk songs. On Shrovetide, a doll is burned, which means seeing off the winter.

Experienced chefs come here to prepare fragrant pancakes with different fillings. Young visitors enjoy a separate entertainment program featuring costumed dolls.

The Trinity also hosts great celebrations. Folk groups come with performance programs. Various cafes and restaurants come here to prepare ritual dishes.

Craftswomen teach everyone to weave beautiful wreaths of herbs. Large samovars are installed on the street, in which aromatic herbal tea is brewed. It also attracts masters who conduct workshops on the preparation of birch brooms for steaming in a bathhouse.

Wedding tourism

In the Taneyevs estate, all the conditions for an amazing photo shoot are created. Newlyweds can take a walk along the park alleys. They are happy to take pictures in a gazebo for lovers.

Then all guests can visit the museum halls directly, where they will have a small but interesting excursion. At this time, in a separate room, representatives of the paste company can set up a buffet table.

Many newlyweds arrange a visiting ceremony for painting here. In the estate, guests can be provided with beautiful costumes of the 15-16 centuries. In the warm season, this process is most often carried out in a park area. And in winter, the ceremony of marriage can be performed in spacious halls.

Administrators of the estate will be happy to help with decorating the venue, and can also advise good catering companies.

The Taneyevs estate must be visited in order to feel the life and traditions of our ancestors with aristocratic roots.
