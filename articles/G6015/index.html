Rita Resort & Residence 3 * (Thailand / Pattaya): hotel reviews

If you prefer budget, but comfortable hotels and want to have a great rest in Pattaya (Thailand), then the three-star Rita Resort Residence hotel can be a suitable option for you during your vacation.



Pattaya Rita Resort Residence 3 *: general description, photo, location

This hotel was built in 2011. It has an interesting modern design. The hotel is located in the southern part of Pattaya, in an area called Pratumnak. The resort town center and Walking Street are 1 km away. Jomtien Beach is two kilometers away. The nearest airport is 130 kilometers away. So on the way to the hotel after arrival, it will take you more than two hours.

Rita Resort Residence has at its disposal 60 comfortable rooms of the following categories: superior, deluxe, suite, executive and luxury. Regardless of the type, all apartments have comfortable new furniture, a large flat-screen TV, telephone, hairdryer, minibar, safe, air conditioning, terrace or balcony. Guests also have the opportunity to use wireless Internet access. Some rooms have a separate kitchen with all necessary equipment. So, if you wish, guests can prepare something for themselves from the products purchased in one of the neighboring stores. Tourists staying at Rita Resort Residence can use the room service.

The hotel area is not very large. However, there is a restaurant, a bar, a pool with a jacuzzi, a sun terrace, parking, and laundry. There is no animation in this hotel. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. If necessary, guests can book a transfer to the airport.

The cost of living in Rita Resort includes breakfast only. However, during the day, guests can eat at the hotel's restaurant for an additional fee.

As follows from the reviews of tourists, the vast majority of them were very satisfied with the living conditions at the Rita Resort Residence hotel. Territory

As our compatriots, who have repeatedly visited other countries popular among our tourists, note that the territory of Rita Resort Resident is quite large for a hotel in Thailand. Location

According to hotel guests, Rita Resort Residence 3 * is perfect for those tourists who do not plan to spend all days on the beach, but prefer an active vacation. After all, you will have to walk about 15-20 minutes to the sea from the hotel. Therefore, if you are mainly interested in beach vacations, then it makes sense to find another hotel. However, most travelers were aware of this feature, so no complaints were made on their part. In addition, Rita Resort is located very close to the main entertainment places in Pattaya. So, you can drive to the famous Walking Street in tuk-tuk in just 5 minutes. Also in the vicinity of the hotel there are a lot of cafes, bars, shops, souvenir shops, currency exchange offices, tour agencies, car and motorbike rental offices, etc.

In addition, next to the hotel there is a large beautiful park in which there is a statue of Buddha. Nearby is an observation deck.

The cost of living in this hotel includes breakfast only. In the mornings, according to tourists, the food here is very good. So, breakfasts for the most part consist of familiar foods and dishes. As already noted, the Rita Resort Residence 3 * hotel is located far off the coast. Walk to the nearest beach for about 15-20 minutes.
