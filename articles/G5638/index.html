Anapa beaches: traveler reviews

Anapa is a sunny city on the shores of the warm Black Sea. Here, clean air, picturesque dunes, a variety of resort entertainment. Beaches are the pride of a resort town. Among the entire Northern Black Sea coast, experienced tourists consider the beaches of Anapa to be the best . The reviews of numerous vacationers, warming themselves carefreely under the gentle southern sun, confirm that this is actually so.



In Anapa there are sandy, rocky and pebble beaches. In the vicinity of the city, with the exception of a narrow strip of pebble-rocky, mostly beaches are wide, sandy.

Sandy beaches

The sandy beach stretches almost fifty kilometers, from the seaport to the cape called the Iron Horn. This part of the coast is characterized by granular golden-colored sand with fragments of frayed sea mollusks, sand grains of rocks and feldspar. Some areas due to granite impurities have a reddish tint and even greenish if an epidote is present in them.

Sand dunes, sometimes reaching 15 meters, covered with tamarisk, clover, silver goof, border almost all the beaches of Anapa. Reviews say that parents with children especially like to relax here. Coastal breezes purify the air and even in the hottest weather on the beaches it is not hot. The depths safe for children, the quiet sea, in which you can swim even in a small storm, are encouraging.

Previously, the beach in the city was divided into two: Central and Medical. Today, such a distinction does not exist. This is a single recreation area - the city's central beach. Therefore, everyone who Anapa accepts is resting here. The central beach is the most populous place in the resort. Its width is several hundred meters, and its length is almost a kilometer. Developed infrastructure, a wonderful water park, and other entertainment facilities make the beach the most beloved among both locals and tourists.

Other sandy beaches of Anapa are located in and around the city. The reviews of vacationers especially emphasize the perfectly clean sand of the beach of the village of Blagoveshchenskaya, the golden sands of the beaches of Vityazevo and Dzhemete, which can be called unique. Unlike the Central Beach, they are not so crowded, so everyone who prefers a relaxing holiday loves them. By the way, on the golden sands of Dzhemete beach there is a fairly popular exclusive club-hotel "White Beach". Anapa welcomes guests here, providing them with a European level of service. If you keep your way away from Anapa, you can get to one of the most peaceful and secluded places of relaxation - the sand spit of the Kiziltash estuary.

Rocky and pebble beaches





According to lovers of beach holidays, the best on the Black Sea coast are pebble beaches of Anapa. Reviews speak primarily of Cordon Beach and the very popular Small Bay beach.

The beaches of Greater and Lesser Utrish and Sukko are also clean, with a gentle entrance to the sea and ideal conditions for swimming. These are the most beautiful places, strewn with small pebbles, with picturesque sea landscapes, healing air and clear, clear water. Pebble beaches here are protected from the winds, well-maintained and equipped. True, the sea in these places is deep. Therefore, rest on Utrish is more suitable for adults.
