Bank reliability rating: what has changed?

The ratings, where you can see the most reliable banking institutions, are updated every quarter. There is nothing strange in this, as it is necessary to keep abreast of the latest changes in banks. These ratings can be a turning point in choosing a particular financial institution.

Ratings of banking institutions



If we compare the third quarter with the fourth, then the reliability rating of banks has not changed. Sberbank, Raiffeisen, VTB, Rosbank, Rosselkhozbank, etc. take the leading positions. All of these institutions are able to provide all the necessary range of services. In addition, it should be noted that the management of banks began to pay more attention to those customers who are corporate.

The services provided by banking systems relate, first of all, to the most dynamic sector of the economic sphere, namely small and medium-sized businesses. It should be noted that every year the number of quite interesting offers for such customers is increasing more and more. Recently, medium and small businesses have seen growth trends, and we can safely say that these types of entrepreneurship began to actively develop.

The changes that the banks' reliability rating has undergone in comparison with the third quarter relate to the emergence of new favorable conditions for corporate clients. The country's enterprises that will cooperate with banking institutions will be able to switch to other tariffs with maximum efficiency, especially for their business.



In order to choose a particular bank, you need to look at all the options available and evaluate the benefits of cooperation. Do not forget about the comprehensive service, namely how convenient it is. It is necessary to consider in more detail the reliability rating of Russian banks in 2014.

The largest banking institutions in Russia

Sberbank today is rightfully considered the largest bank in our country and the CIS countries. It appeared in the early 21st century. Today, Sberbank is such a partner whose reliability does not raise any doubts. Branches are available in absolutely all cities of Russia. In addition, this banking institution is available in China, Germany and India. All addresses can be found on the main information portal of the bank. Sberbank works with absolutely all types of customers. For corporate clients , investment, credit, cash management, consulting and financial services are provided.



One of the leading institutions, which for several years does not leave the rating of reliability of banks, is VTB. It was organized back in 1990. You can use the services of this bank in 20 countries of the world. In the Russian market, VTB Group is in second place in the ranking. Work is mainly carried out with corporate clients. The bank can also offer a complete solution for absolutely all entrepreneurs.

The unique structure of Gazprombank

If we talk about the degree of diversification, then Gazprombank is considered an institution with a unique structure. At the heart of this structure is one of the largest banks in our country. Customer service is carried out under the guidance of highly qualified professionals. They know all the nuances of the bank, so they work with maximum efficiency. Corporate clients are offered those services with which you can simplify the implementation of their financial issues. The bank cooperates with both medium and small businesses. In addition, among the clients of this financial institution there are those enterprises that are capable of boasting a multi-billion dollar turnover.

The activities of Alfa Bank





Appearing back in 1990, Alfa-Bank managed to quickly get into the banks' reliability rating. It is a universal institution, as it is able to provide its customers with absolutely all varieties of financial services. But it should be noted that the main activity of the bank is cooperation with corporate clients. Alfa-Bank is able to finance the business. It should also be noted that the bank has a large branch network not only in Russian cities, but also in the CIS countries. On the main portal of the bank you can find the addresses of all branches.

Changes in the Ukrainian rating



At that moment, when Western banks left Ukraine, the entire financial sector changed. All the money of the population was concentrated only in domestic and Russian financial institutions. British, German, Czech, Dutch, South Korean and Swedish banks stopped their activities in the country. All these changes had a rather strong impact on the reliability rating of Ukrainian banks. The year 2014 was marked by the fact that some financial institutions skyrocketed to leading positions. For example, Credit Agricole Bank, which previously held only 16th position, is in fourth place. Raiffeisen Bank Aval took off in second place. Ukreximbank is in the third position. In the first place is the state-owned Oschadbank.

Other banking institutions included in the rating will also provide all the necessary services.

The above banking systems are the main ones. They are most widely distributed in the cities of Russia and in other countries. But there are still a large number of different banks, the reliability of which is also beyond doubt. They are able to provide a list of services to corporate clients and individuals. In addition, they can provide comprehensive services, as well as business support. Some banks specialize more in agricultural development work. Accordingly, they take part in those state programs that are related to this field of activity.

All banks that are represented in the rating are able to provide a full list of services and all the necessary software tools, through the use of which it is possible to simplify cooperation with small and medium enterprises. In addition, bank employees are constantly working on creating new services to attract more customers of different categories.
