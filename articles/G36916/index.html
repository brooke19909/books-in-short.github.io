Luna Sharm Hotel 3 * (Sharm El Sheikh): photos and reviews of tourists, description

Egypt belongs to those exotic countries whose resorts have already become fond of numerous citizens from Russia. Even if you do not spend a lot of money and time on excursions, but simply live in the Luna Sharm Hotel 3 * or some other hotel, relax on the beach and walk along the nearby streets, you can spend a wonderful and unusual vacation.

Tours to Egypt: where is better to go?

Egypt seems to tourists a sunny country, where summer reigns year round, so you can constantly enjoy swimming and snorkeling in the Red Sea. However, when it comes to organizing a trip to this country, almost all travelers, faced with the question of which tours to Egypt are better to choose, are lost.

Indeed, making a decision is not so easy! But you can certainly say that if you choose Sharm El Sheikh, then your vacation will be fun and pleasant. After all, this resort is not in vain one of the most popular among tourists from both Russia and Europe. There are many interesting activities for people with any preferences. This is a tour of local attractions, and relaxing on the magnificent sandy beaches, and hiking in restaurants or cafes, and visiting numerous sports facilities, such as massage rooms, tennis courts, diving clubs, gyms, etc. In addition, the path to the resort will not cause special difficulties, regardless of whether you buy tours to Egypt or not. After all, the airport is located a few kilometers from the center of Sharm El Sheikh.





Trips to Sharm El Sheikh (Egypt) - prices

The least troublesome way to relax in Sharm el-Sheikh is to purchase a trip to Egypt from one of the travel companies, of which there are a huge number of trips to the territory of the Russian Federation. Prices for them can be very diverse. They depend on the level and location of the hotel, the class of airline ticket, the presence or absence of tours around the country, as well as their number, season, and, of course, on the capabilities of the travel agency itself.

You can only call the approximate average cost of a tour to Sharm El Sheikh in order to have the opportunity to figure out how much to save money on trips to Egypt. Prices of May tours lasting 10 nights, with the ticket included (departure from Moscow), accommodation and meals at the 2-3 * hotel range from 29 thousand rubles. up to 32 thousand per person. If the vacation takes place in June, then for a similar ticket you will have to pay 31-33 thousand rubles, August-September - 36-39 thousand rubles, October - about 43 thousand. The cheapest tours in April are 20-23 thousand. rub. If you choose 4 or 5 star hotels for your stay, the cost of the ticket will be as much higher as the cost of living in a hotel.





Luna Sharm Hotel 3 *: general description of the hotel

If you are interested in a comfortable and affordable vacation in Egypt, then Luna Sharm is a great choice for a vacation stay. This hotel is located at a distance of 25 km from the international airport and 8 km from the famous Naama Bay beach, on the third coastline.

Luna Sharm Hotel 3 * consists of two two-story buildings that were erected in 2004 and restored in 2009. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is 17 thousand square meters. m. The territory is decorated with luxurious palm trees, exotic plants, flowers and beautiful lawns with green grass. The general description of the hotel should also include the fact that often Egyptian families stay in it, which speaks in its favor. Both in the rooms and in the courtyard there is access to a Wi-Fi network, however, you need to pay extra for it.

Hotel accommodation

For accommodation Luna Sharm Hotel 3 * offers its guests 128 rooms. Among them are 1 suite, 114 standards and 13 family. 80% of the rooms have wonderful sea views, and 20% of the rooms overlook the garden. Many accommodation units have a balcony or terrace. Standards are designed to accommodate a maximum of two adults and two children. Family rooms can accommodate four adults and two children. The suite is equipped with sleeping places for six adults and two children, which are located in two bedrooms. It is worth noting that there is no door between the rooms in the suite.





Room features and services

Residents of all rooms have access to a private bathroom with shower. There is air conditioning, TV with Russian channel, telephone, necessary furniture, mini-bar. All rooms at Luna Sharm Hotel 3 (Egypt) have tiled floors. The rooms are changed daily linen, towels and cleaning. At any time, guests of the hotel can order food or drinks in the room.

Food and drink

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served as a buffet in the main restaurant, which seats 86 people. The restaurant is indoor and air-conditioned. The menu consists of dishes of cuisines from around the world, so that each guest of Luna Sharm Hotel 3 * will be able to eat their favorite foods. The bar located on the hotel territory offers a wide assortment of drinks - imported and local, alcoholic and non-alcoholic. By the way, local wines are included in the price and are served in the restaurant at every meal. At any time of the day, in the lobby bar or at the poolside bar, in addition to numerous drinks, you can also try cold snacks. The hotel has an oriental cafe called Al Badyah. The beach also has a small bar where you can buy soft drinks, which are very handy on a hot Egyptian day.





Options for spending time on site

Description of the hotel is inconceivable without mentioning the options for spending time on its territory. Vacationers of the hotel have the opportunity at any time of the day to swim in one of the two pools filled with fresh water. However, it is worth noting that they never heat up, so only the most seasoned tourists can swim in them on cool days. There is also the opportunity to simply lie down near the pools, since there are enough sun loungers and umbrellas in sufficient quantities for vacationers.

For lovers of more active pastime, the hotel has tennis tables. There is also billiards, but you must pay extra for its use. Once a week, evening animated shows are organized at the hotel. Upon request, the hotel staff can arrange a sightseeing tour of Sharm el-Sheikh and other cities in Egypt, or a lesson in one of the local diving clubs.

The restaurant has high chairs provided for the convenient location of the child during meals. Upon request, a cot can be provided in any of the rooms. The hotel also has a babysitter, whose services can be used at an additional cost.

It should be borne in mind that no games and other entertainments are organized on the territory of Luna Sharm, so parents have to invent interesting activities for their children on their own. There are other accommodation options at the resort where the staff entertains children all day, however, the prices in Sharm el-Sheikh in such hotels are significantly higher than in the hotel in question.

Sea and beach

As mentioned above, the hotel is not located on the first coastline, which means that the path to the sea is not as short as vacationers sometimes want. It is located at a distance of about 4 km. Nevertheless, Luna Sharm beach has its own, a bus leaves regularly to it, so the residents of the hotel do not need to spend a lot of time on the road. Umbrellas, towels, sun loungers and mattresses are free of charge for tourists.

There is a volleyball court on the beach. You can also play water polo. In addition, there are many paid entertainment on the beach, including banana riding, windsurfing, parasailing, water skiing, catamarans, etc. And when all the activities are boring, every vacationer has the opportunity to enjoy the taste of refreshing drinks offered by the local bar.





Hotel service

Laundry, hairdresser, massage room, and doctor provide their services on site. A bus regularly leaves for the city center, so you can, without spending much time on the road, explore the Old Town, visit the market and watch the locals.

The hotel has an exchange office with the current rate, a mini-bank, a shop and parking. Guests of Luna Sharm Hotel 3 * have the opportunity to use the safe at the reception, because travel often necessitates (for example, when you go to the beach or an excursion) to store valuables in a safe place. By the way, this service is completely free. And if you need to receive any information regarding the possibilities of your trip, the hotel staff will be happy to help you.

Reviews about Luna Sharm Hotel 3 *

For those who decide to spend their summer holidays in Egypt, namely the Luna Sharm Hotel, reviews from people who have already visited there will be extremely useful. All hotel guests agree that it is a great option for lovers of a calm and measured pastime: no parties until the morning, no constant animators, remoteness from any noisy establishments.

Good travelers also report on the rooms of Luna Sharm Hotel. They note beautiful views from the windows, cleanliness, regular change of linen, the presence of everything necessary in the bathroom. The big drawback of the rooms, according to reviews, are the thin walls in the rooms, because of which you can hear everything that happens around. Some vacationers were upset by the lack of a hairdryer in the room, however its hotel staff provided upon request at any time. As for nutrition, everyone claims that it is not bad, as for 3 *. Dishes are often repeated, however, they are always fresh and tasty.

The beach - it is around it that many negative reviews are concentrated. Sharm el Sheikh, although located on the seashore, but many hotels, including Luna Sharm, are located quite far from the coast. Among the shortcomings of the hotel, some tourists also note unfriendly staff. Dissatisfaction with tourists cause the dirty streets of the resort, but this does not apply to the hotel.

Summarizing the reviews of many tourists about the Luna Sharm hotel, we can conclude that it is a great option for people who prefer a relaxing holiday or those who plan to spend most of their time getting to know the sights of Egypt. This hotel is unlikely to appeal to young people, since there is no interesting entertainment program, no discos, or theme parties.





The best places for photos in Sharm El Sheikh

The main purpose of a trip to Egypt for most tourists is a beach holiday on the Red Sea. Accordingly, photos taken on the magnificent sand near the endless waters must be sure to replenish the photo album of each traveler. Many interesting pictures can be taken on site. After all, it houses a magnificent pool. In addition, from the windows of the rooms of Luna Sharm Hotel, as mentioned above, there are magnificent views of the garden and the sea, which also contributes to the production of interesting pictures.

However, the most unusual photographs will be able to bring with them those tourists who will devote at least a little time to acquaintance with local attractions, of which there are many. The most famous objects are the Monastery of St. Catherine, the Sinai National Reserves, the Color Canyon, with a length of about 700 m, Ras Mohammed Park with its five ecosystems, Nabq Park and Ras Abu Galum Park. It should be borne in mind that on vacation in Sharm El Sheikh, as in all of Egypt, you can take pictures of everything. The only exceptions are cases when local residents can get into the picture - then it is better to ask them for permission so as not to be in an unpleasant situation.
