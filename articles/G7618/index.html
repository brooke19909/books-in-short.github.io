History of the name of this institution

The creative team that created this amazing world thought for a long time about how to better name it. As a result, they decided that the cute little boy Yurka would become the symbol of the park. He is dressed in a sweatshirt and jeans, and holds a dinosaur under his arm. His image turned out to be mischievous, crafty and inquisitive, personifying most of the current schoolchildren and teenagers.





As a symbol of this place, the boy is installed near the entrance to the park. Thus, he meets and escorts guests, and his image can also be seen on branded souvenirs of this entertainment complex. Description of the park

Of course, we can say that in mid-December 2015, all residents of the Kirov region received a huge gift, dedicated to the birthday of their district. This date became a grandiose event for the townspeople - the Yurkin Park (Kirov, Poroshino) was opened. He, perhaps, does not look more like any institution in this city. Each of its details is unique and original, so in the end we got a project that has no analogue even in such a huge country as Russia.

This place immediately won the hearts of the Kirovites, because entertainment of the highest world level is located here. The guests of the park have a unique opportunity to spend their active leisure in the fresh air, learn a lot of new and informative, ride extreme and exciting rides, and of course, see with your own eyes real dinosaurs.

These ancient lizards have always aroused the greatest interest among mankind, which is why they served as the main idea for the design of the territory on which Yurkin Park is located (Kirov). The inhabitants

Visitors to this complex, immediately entering its territory, find themselves in a stunning world called "Yurkin Park" (Kirov), where there are many fascinating and interesting things. For example, there are forty-three giants who lived in the distant times of the Jurassic period. All dinosaurs are huge, some of them can reach twenty meters.

They have a realistic look, as they can turn their heads in different directions, move their tails and flap their wings. After all, it is simply unbelievable that you can see a giant mamenchisaurus with a long neck, which can also move. A quetzalcoatl, with a wingspan of 7 m, awaits guests of this place on a tree, and in the middle of a pond on a small island there is a pterodactyl. Not far from it on the lake is a real thunderstorm of inhabitants of prehistoric reservoirs - plesiosaurus.

From all that is seen, visitors are breathtaking, and only excursions to Yurkin Park (Kirov) can give such a feeling. The ticket price is quite reasonable.

In addition, for such a relatively low cost you can still see the "real" eggs of the ancient dinosaurs. A cub appears from one, and in another, visitors take interesting pictures in memory of such an exciting walk.





Infrastructure

In addition to the reviving giant inhabitants, the park also has an interactive children's room with traces of dinosaurs. There is also a souvenir boutique where visitors can purchase many interesting things and gifts.

For those who feel hungry during such an active walk, the Yurkin Burger cafe works in the territory of this place. In addition, in the winter, visitors can enjoy ice skating, as a small and large ice rink is located here specially for this, as well as ride from the slides on the tubing.

You can also hold any celebration in the park. To do this, on its territory there is a special panoramic restaurant located on a pond with a long pier and a walking terrace.

Cost of visiting

Despite such high-class entertainments and attractions, an entrance to the Yurkin Park (Kirov) is available for everyone. The ticket price for both children and adults is 300 rubles. At the same time, the price of the visit includes such complex services as a tour along the alley among ancient dinosaurs, descent from various slides, searching for dinosaur remains in the pavilion, playing in a large town for children, as well as delicious tea from a real samovar. Children under three years are completely free.

An additional bungee is paid, which will cost one hundred rubles, and an extreme attraction - 150 rubles. In addition, on the territory of the complex you can enjoy a delicious barbecue or purchase a set lunch for only 250 rubles.

Visit time

Monday is a day off at the Yurkin Park entertainment complex. Its operating mode is as follows:

  • From Tuesday to Thursday, this place opens for its guests at 10:00 in the morning, and closes at 22:00.

  • On Fridays and Sundays, the park is open from 10:00 to 21:00.

  • On Saturday, visitors can enter the park from 10:00 and stay there until 22:00.

Any local resident can tell you how to get to Yurkin Park (Kirov), or you can see for yourself, knowing its exact location.

Contact details

This place is located near everyone's favorite recreation center "Poroshino". Just a kilometer from it was the Yurkin Park (Kirov). His address looks like this: Talitskaya street, house 50.





It will not be difficult for locals and guests of the city to get to this place, as route bus No. 21 passes by it. This type of transport will be convenient for any Kirov citizen, since its movement covers the whole city. Visitors can also drive their cars to Yurkin Park (Kirov), where a convenient and spacious parking lot is located. For excursion groups who come from far away, a special place for a bus is allocated in the parking lot.

For all questions of interest, you can also pre-call "Yurkin Park". The telephone for information has the following form: + 7 (8332) 468-292. A pleasant administrator will answer this number, who will be able to conduct a competent consultation by telephone.
