Hotel Korona 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): description, photos and reviews of tourists

One of the largest and most famous resorts in Bulgaria on the sea coast is Sunny Beach. For the guests here, beautiful sandy beaches have been created by nature and equipped with man, more than a hundred hotels, about two hundred and fifty restaurants, many cafes and bars, countless entertainment venues and thousands of outlets have been built.

A 10-15 minute leisurely step from the noisy and crowded resort center, in a picturesque and relatively quiet location on the beach, is the Korona 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach), in which guests of all ages will feel comfortable: both active youth and older people, and families with young children.

Location

Tourists, arriving on a beach holiday in Bulgaria, usually land at one of two international airports on the Black Sea coast of the state. Then follow to the hotel by public transport or taxi. The Korona 4 * hotel, which is located one and a half kilometers from the central part of Sunny Beach resort, can be easily reached by flying to any of them.





The nearest international airport is located south of the hotel complex, near the city of Burgas. The road to it will take about twenty to thirty minutes.

To the north of the resort, in the vicinity of Varna, there are other air gates of Bulgaria on the coast. Travel time from this airport to the hotel will be one and a half to two hours. But a trip to the hotel in a comfortable bus or taxi will not seem to tourists long and tiring, as the highway is simple, easy to carry and with very beautiful landscapes outside the window.

Accommodation

The hotel complex Korona 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is a stylish five-story building with two elevators, one of which is panoramic.

The hotel has 208 rooms, each of which has a balcony and a private bathroom.

Each room is equipped with necessary furniture, air conditioning with a personal remote control, a small fridge, satellite TV and direct dial telephone. The bathroom has sanitary facilities, towels for various purposes and a hairdryer.

The room has two single beds, but there is the possibility of additional installation of two more beds.

Cleaning is done daily. Change of hotel towels and linen - several times a week or on request.





Registration of newly arrived guests begins at three o'clock local time, the release of rooms - on the day of departure until eleven o'clock.

Facilities & Services

The Korona Hotel offers its guests many additional services, and only a part of them will require a separate fee.

  • At the 24-hour reception there is an opportunity to hand over things to the left-luggage office , rent an individual safe deposit box or exchange currency.
  • For motorists on-site parking is equipped.
  • The offices of a general practitioner, dentist and massage are functioning, there is a spa center and a beauty workshop.
  • To maintain good physical shape there is a gym, sauna and jacuzzi.




  • The hotel Korona 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) also has an outdoor pool with a children's section. In addition to the area in the pool, a playground is equipped for young guests and the restaurant has special chairs.

Nutrition

Hotel Korona 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) offers its guests to visit the main restaurant, lobby bar, sky bar and pool bar.

Buying a ticket to the hotel "Crown" 4 *, tourists choose the type of food: only breakfast or half board (breakfast and dinner).





In the mornings, a European-style buffet with traditional Bulgarian dishes is served for all guests in the hotel restaurant, designed for up to 320 guests.

Dinner in the hotel restaurant is also held according to the "buffet", with the exception of the beginning and end of the season. At this time, when guests are few, dinner is possible on the menu.

Sea and beach

Hotel Korona is located on the first line of the Black Sea coast, less than a hundred meters from the public beach.

There is nothing to pay for entering the beach. There is a lot of space, and you can comfortably accommodate yourself by laying your own towels and mats on the warm golden sand or, using the services of a paid rental, take a place in a sun lounger under an umbrella.





Sunset at sea is also sandy, gentle, which is especially convenient for vacationers with small children. Algae in the vicinity of the beach are sometimes, but they are quickly removed. The beach near the Korona 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) - tourist reviews confirm this - it is really very clean, for which it was awarded the European Blue Flag award.

For lovers of outdoor activities there is a wide range of entertainment on water rides and many sporting events on land.

Hotel Rating

Choosing a place for future recreation, most often tourists are guided by the ratings and user reviews from popular sites for travelers. This helps them to see the situation in the hotel and in the resort through the eyes of people who have already been here. There are reviews about the hotel "Crown 4 *" (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach).

The Korona 4 * hotel rating is 3.6 out of 5. It is based on 61 reviews published on the travelers portal over the past few years.

Impressions of tourists about the hotel Korona 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach)

Reviews of the Bulgarian hotel "Crown 4 *" are the most diverse. The indisputable advantages of the hotel include its good location, near the sea and an excellent beach with a reasonable number of vacationers. Tourists also note the friendly and welcoming attitude of the hotel staff, who is trying to solve the problems of the guests.

The main question that excites the guests is the condition of the number of rooms of the Korona 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach). Photos demonstrating the coziness and comfort of the hotel, you can see in the article.

In general, the hotel has a good price-to-location ratio and can be recommended to undemanding travelers planning a budget vacation option at sea.
