Hotel for a relaxing holiday Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 * (Spain / Mallorca): photos and reviews of tourists

Many dream to visit Mallorca. This is not at all surprising, because it is a paradise with a wonderful climate and bewitching landscapes. Not so long ago, vacation in Mallorca was unavailable to most domestic tourists because of the too high cost. But today even people with an average income can come here. The island is so friendly to travelers that party-goers, elderly tourists, and families with children can have a great time here. Therefore, tourists tend to spend their weekend here. Before you go on vacation to Mallorca, you need to clarify a few points for yourself. First of all, decide on the hotel. A worthy place to stay can be the complex "Komlejo Calas De Mallorca Resort". This hotel is really special and everyone will like it.

Unusually, the complex is located on a cliff. The windows of the rooms offer incredibly beautiful views, as in the best films. The Mediterranean Sea will delight the eye throughout the rest. Not far from the hotel there are several wonderful beaches with clean sand, as well as a huge garden of 25,000 square meters. meters.

The hotel administration has created all the conditions for a good rest. There are a huge number of entertainment venues. You can try delicious food and just relax by the water. For guests of the complex there are interesting entertainment programs and more.

Categories of apartments





Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 * hotel offers several types of rooms.

  • Standard Room. This is a one-room apartment. There is a double bed, two bedside tables, a wardrobe and lamps. There is also a refrigerator, telephone, Internet access, but all these services are paid. Hot water is supplied in the bathroom, there is a set of mini-cosmetics, but the use of a hairdryer is included in the list of paid services. Another disadvantage is that the room does not have air conditioning. But you can have a cup of tea on the wonderful terrace overlooking the garden or local surroundings. Room is cleaned daily.
  • Standard Room Sea View. This number is almost identical to the previous one. For a fee, you can use the fridge, safe, kettle and hairdryer. The difference is that the view from the terrace overlooks the Mediterranean Sea. And in the morning you can enjoy a picturesque view of the sea right in your room.
  • Family Room. This room also has additional paid options. It is one-room, but the footage is larger. Therefore, you can check in here with your child, as the place allows. The terrace offers a seascape.

When checking in, it is important to be prepared for the fact that pets are not accommodated. Therefore, you need to leave your pet at home, or live in another hotel. A person with disabilities will also have a hard time. There are no specially equipped rooms. The staff, of course, will help everyone they can, but still it will cause inconvenience.

Entertainment





Every evening, the hotel Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 * (Mallorca) provides a special entertainment program. A whole group of animators works. It is important to know that they do not speak Russian, so you will have to communicate in English.

Nutrition

The hotel has many different cafes and bars. Food here is organized according to the All Inclusive system, so it is simply impossible to remain hungry. You can also try various local drinks, they are also included in the price.

Additional services

A medical worker is on duty all the time on the territory, but treatment refers to paid services. There is free parking. The services of a nanny and animators for children are provided, but at the same time they do not speak Russian, so the child must be prepared. On the territory there is a swimming pool, a playground. At the request of parents, cots can be delivered to the room.
Hotel Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 * (Mallorca) is quite cozy and cute. It is located in a beautiful picturesque place. The range of services is not very wide, but the administration is successfully trying to develop its institution.

What do the guests themselves say about the hotel Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 *?

Reviews

The hotel provides all-inclusive services. The complex consists of two buildings. One of them includes 6 floors. Some rooms are located in the basement. The second building is located on a rocky shore. Along the perimeter of the cliff runs a long promenade leading to the beaches. It takes 5 minutes to reach the small beach, and about 15 minutes to the large beach.

About Territory





The complex "Calas de Mallorca" is located in a picturesque area. Everything is buried in greenery and flowers. For the convenience of guests, trestle beds for rest in the shade are scattered around the perimeter. Younger guests can play in a specially equipped children's town with swings, carousels, sandpits and slides. There is a pool with a bar.

In the afternoon, slides are inflated at the pool for the kids. There are no shops on site, but merchants with various goods regularly come to the hotel. Beach paraphernalia in local shops is expensive. There are mass selling points within a 10-minute walk of the hotel. There everything costs 5 euros cheaper. Internet throughout the paid.

About accommodation

The rooms of the six-story building offer scenic views of the sea lagoon, the beach and the city of Porto Cristo. On the other hand you can observe the terrace, pool and the neighboring hotel.

The rooms are spacious. The room is equipped with two large beds pushed together. There are bedside tables, a chair, an armchair, a coffee table, a dressing table with a mirror, and a bench. There is no air conditioning, but there is a fan that does its job perfectly. There is no TV and fridge. In the corridor there is a roomy closet with numerous shelves and hangers. If necessary, you can use warm blankets.

The bathroom is equipped with a toilet, trouble and a bathtub with a curtain. There is a sink with a mirror. A few pieces of decorative soap are provided from the toiletries. Plumbing is working properly, although not new. There is no hair dryer. Water does not drain in the sink due to an improperly installed siphon. Maids quickly fix this problem, but after a few days everything repeats.

The rooms have a small balcony with a table and two chairs. The bedding is in perfect condition. Change it every three days. Three towels are allocated for each client: bath, for the face and for the feet. Change every day.

There is tile on the floor. They clean the room and take out the trash daily.

Service

The staff is restrained in emotions, but the work is done efficiently. Nobody speaks Russian. The food service is top notch. Waiters work with incredible speed.

Each guest is left with a bottle of champagne with sweets and fruits in his room on his birthday.

About nutrition





The hotel "Calas de Mallorca" is designed for European tourists, therefore, the cuisine in the restaurants is appropriate. But national dishes are also present.

Breakfast includes omelet, beans with sauce, several variations of egg dishes, yogurt, cheese, jamon fried and dried, granola, sausages, marmalade, cakes, dried fruits, croissants, several types of ice cream.

For lunch and dinner, you can enjoy meat and fish dishes, which are prepared in front of visitors. Seafood always has mussels, shellfish and shrimp. Garnish - potatoes in various variations, stewed vegetables, pasta. The first is soups. There are always savory snacks in the marinade. All this is complemented by fresh cucumbers, tomatoes, herbs, olives and salads. Of fruits - peaches, watermelon, plums, apples, melon, nectarines and pears.

A selection of local beer and wine is served at lunch and dinner. The bar offers all kinds of cocktails and other alcoholic drinks. Not very good quality coffee from the machine.

Sun loungers, umbrellas are available at an additional cost. There are almost always a lot of people on the beaches. Sometimes jellyfish are beaten to the shore, which they ruthlessly sting. Entertainment

Throughout the day, games are held for children and not only near the pool. In the evening, you can enjoy live music and singing artists. There are no discos and karaoke. All animations are conducted in English, but in general everything is very fun, especially cultural programs for children.

For a fee, you can work out at the hotel's fitness center or visit the spa.

Additional Information

Complejo Calas De Mallorca is a great hotel for families with children. Youth here may be bored. The contingent of the hotel - the British, Germans, French and Italians, few Russians.

The hotel is 60 km from the airport. Porto Cristo is only 9 km away. Nearby there are practically no attractions, so the tourist should count on a hotel holiday, which can also be quite eventful and interesting.

Nearby is a minimarket and several small shops with beach accessories.

A train runs through the village, which carries tourists living away from the sea. The fare costs about 5 euros. You can take the bus to the nearest villages and to Palma de Mallorca. Price - 11 euros one way.

For trips, it is more profitable to rent a car, but you must book it in advance.

General impressions

The Complejo Calas De Mallorca Hotel 3 * hotel takes an honorable place among Majorca hotel complexes. And this is confirmed by numerous reviews of tourists. Elderly people, families with children and those who prefer a relaxing holiday away from noisy entertainment love to stay here. People come here for the beautiful sea and the gentle sun. All this is complemented by excellent food, comfortable rooms and great service.

Guests can even expect small surprises on special dates. The staff, although not distinguished by friendliness, nevertheless, tries to fulfill their duties in the best way.





The hotel has a guide who, if desired, will select you an excursion route for a relatively small cost. All guests are provided with brochures, maps, bus schedules and full island information.

Be sure to go on an excursion to the city of Palma de Mallorca - the historical capital of the island. There is a huge number of interesting sights, architectural structures, the construction of which goes back to the distant past. All this is complemented by wonderful beaches, shops, cozy restaurants. On certain days, you can visit various festivals that the Spaniards love to arrange. For more details, please contact the hotel, which will kindly provide full information about all the cultural events of the island.

Also, the hotel Complejo Calas De Mallorca Resort 3 * provides its guests with the services of a fitness center, spa, numerous restaurants and bars.

The food is very varied and tasty. Particularly impressive is the selection of seafood. Spain is a country of fruit. In the hotel they are presented in a huge assortment. The products are very fresh and well cooked. Some dishes are prepared in the presence of customers.





Among the shortcomings can be noted the lack of entertainment in the evenings, the presence of rooms located in the basement, slippery tiles near the pool, the lack of air conditioning, a refrigerator and a hairdryer in the room.

The main advantages of the hotel Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 * (Spain) are well-groomed territory, good leisure for children, a swimming pool, excellent food, spacious rooms with scenic views, two well-equipped beaches.
