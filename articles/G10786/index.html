Hotel Nagoa Grande Resort & Spa 4 * (North Goa, India): description and photos

If you chose North Goa (India) as your vacation destination and are looking for a not very expensive but comfortable hotel with excellent service, then Nagoa Grande Resort & Spa 4 * could be a suitable option for you. We suggest further to understand what tourists expect here.



Location

The hotel in question is located in North Goa in India. Its location to some tourists seemed not very successful, since the distance to the nearest beaches - Candolim and Baga - is about four kilometers. As for the vicinity of the hotel, there is no special infrastructure here. However, there are a couple of shops where travelers can buy everything they need. Experienced tourists are advised not to be upset about the location of the hotel. Indeed, according to them, even if it was located a two-minute walk from Candolim, the guests would hardly spend every day on this beach, since it seems to many too crowded and not very clean. Therefore, the vast majority of tourists go to other beaches of the coast. You can do this by taxi or by renting a motorbike. The second option will cost much cheaper and allow you to visit all the places of interest to you. By the way, the hotel in question provides its guests with a free shuttle to Candolim at any time convenient for them.

As for the distance from transport hubs, the distance from Nagoa Grande Resort & Spa 4 * (Goa) to Dabolima Airport is 45 kilometers, and to the railway station - 22 kilometers. So after arrival, the path to the hotel will take you no more than an hour.

Rooms

As already mentioned, in total this hotel has 65 apartments. They are represented by the categories of executive, superior and deluxe. They differ mainly only in area. Regardless of the type, each room has comfortable furniture, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning, a telephone, a fridge, a safe, an electric kettle and tea set, a bathroom with a hairdryer, a balcony or terrace. Some tourists advise if possible to settle on the upper floors. In this case, you will not be disturbed by the noise coming from the restaurant and bar. Also, former guests recommend not checking in to combined rooms. The fact is that in this case you will hear almost everything that will happen in the neighboring apartments. Otherwise, there were no complaints from the travelers about the hotel rooms. As a plus, you can add the availability of wireless Internet access. Moreover, the Wi-Fi speed is quite high. Since this is not so common in Goa hotels, many travelers mention this fact in their comments.



Cleaning service

In general, the guests of the Nagoa Grande Resort & Spa 4 * (India, Saligao) had no particular complaints about the work of the hotel maids. So, according to them, the rooms have always been cleaned. True, sometimes maids forgot to bring fresh towels or replenish stocks of soap and shower gel. However, all these problems were quickly resolved after a call to the reception.

The rest of the hotel staff left the guests also mostly pleasant impressions. So, the staff here are always friendly, welcoming and happy to help. Please note that no one speaks Russian here. You will have to communicate in English.



Population

In accordance with the rules of the hotel, accommodation of newly arrived tourists in the rooms they book is carried out after two in the afternoon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier, you will be asked to wait for the checkout time. If you want to get the keys to the apartment quickly, you can ask for an early check-in service. She is paid. On average, according to our compatriots vacationing at the Nagoa Grand Resort & Spa, it will cost you $ 25.

Nutrition

The cost of living in Nagoa Grande Resort & Spa 4 * (North Goa) includes breakfast only. According to our compatriots, they are designed mainly for local tourists. So, many dishes are too sharp for the taste of travelers from Russia and other CIS countries. However, our compatriots claim that there is always the opportunity to have breakfast with more familiar foods. So, you can order an omelet with cheese or bacon, scrambled eggs, toasts with jam. You can also always enjoy fresh vegetables and fruits, juice, coffee and tea.

Guests can also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant. Here you will be offered dishes of both Indian and European cuisine. However, according to our compatriots, the prices here are quite high. Therefore, the vast majority of travelers preferred eating in cafes and restaurants outside the hotel.
