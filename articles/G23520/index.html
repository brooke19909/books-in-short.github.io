"Guseletovo" (recreation center): opportunities and advantages

Guseletovo (recreation center) belongs to the Romanovsky Plus medical and health complex. The basis of it is the healing mud of Lake Mormyshansky, well-known in the country. This is what attracts thousands of tourists and those who want to improve their health to these places. Among other things, rest in these parts is not only pleasant and useful, but also does not hit the cost of our fellow citizens, which has recently been very relevant.

The location of the village

The recreation center "Guseletovo" (Altai Territory) is located in a picturesque village of the same name. These places are remote from industrial plants, highways, there are no high-rise buildings. Tourism in these parts is developing recently, but is constantly moving to new levels.

An excellent vacation in these parts is ensured not only by a favorable climate, but also by the availability of natural healing resources, as well as the opportunities available for organizing sports and leisure activities. Most of all, people are interested in visiting the healing lakes of Mormyshansk and Gorky.





The recreation center "Guseletovo" (Altai Territory) attracts not only tourists, but also scientists. The latter have repeatedly confirmed the healing properties of mud in local waters. They noticed their beneficial effect in diseases of the musculoskeletal system, gynecological diseases, diseases of the nervous and dermatological nature and others. Even representatives from far abroad come here to be treated.

The healing properties of the Gorky reservoir

Recreation facilities in Guseletovo are mainly based on ponds. Gorky reservoir is no exception. Its bottom is covered with velvety silt; there are no stones or algae at all. Its depth is acceptable even for children and is gaining gradually. On the banks of the vegetation can be found reeds and birches with pine trees.





Water in the pond is a real natural antibiotic due to its high alkali content and therapeutic mud. They contribute to the renewal of the skin, relieve pain and fatigue. Depressive states also disappear safely. This is evidenced by the numerous reviews of tourists. The pine forest located nearby also adds pleasant emotions.

Therapeutic mud of Lake Mormyshansky

Not one pond has glorified the resort village of Guseletovo. The recreation center, reviews of which are saturated with positive emotions and enthusiasm, always welcomes guests. It is here that they have the opportunity to plunge into the mud of Lake Marmyshansky. Due to the high salt content, its waters are absolutely unsuitable for the habitat of aquatic inhabitants. Of living things, only red cancer is found here. But a thick layer of salt keeps a storehouse of healing mud underneath.





Over time, salt crystals begin to form a kind of prickly plantations, but they do not cause harm to the skin, because when it comes into contact with it, they immediately dissolve. In the warm season, these places are always full of vacationers. From a distance, people smeared with mud resemble the pillars of trees.

Rest in Guseletovo

Not so long ago, a recreation center began to function in Guseletovo, a photo of which can be found in this article. Here the attention of vacationers presented houses with the necessary furniture. Some are equipped with a private bathroom and shower. All houses have water. The territory is guarded, it has a Russian bath.

Nearby, vacationers can shop in grocery stores with ecologically clean products. Local craftsmen even make mud soap and sell it to tourists.





In Guseletovo, a recreation center is able to accommodate far from all who want to improve their health. Yes, and many want to save on vacation. In this case, they should like camping. Accommodation in this case is provided in tents. It should be noted that this place is very clean and a sufficient number of garbage cans are installed. There is a shower and toilet on the beach, as well as potable water speakers. You can eat at food outlets or shop at the mall.

Things to do in Guseletovo

Guseletovo (recreation center, in particular) provides guests with not only therapeutic mud, but also recreational activities. A holiday called "Bathing Fun" has already become traditional. You can witness the enchanting views and bewitching spectacles. Locals are in a hurry to surprise guests with culinary talents. Also in these parts, a goose museum is presented to the attention of tourists. Its exhibits were not only numerous posters, but also figures, postage stamps, coins depicting this magnificent bird.





"Guseletovo" (recreation center) will appeal to fans of outdoor activities: they can spend time riding a jet ski, a catamaran, a boat, as well as a water slide.
