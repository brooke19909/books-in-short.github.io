Advice

Before you start knitting, you need to familiarize yourself with some useful tips:

1. The yarn should be the same thickness. If the instructions indicate different - then follow it.

2. Pay attention to the thickness of the hook - this is also very important.

3. It is advisable not to use cotton wool as a filler, otherwise over time your toy will lose its original shape. The most optimal filler will be holofiber.

4. Before you start knitting, familiarize yourself with the basic conventions, otherwise you just won‚Äôt be able to understand the patterns. The main notation is presented below.

Cat pillow

So, the first toy that we will consider is a cat (crochet).



In order to knit such a cute striped cat-pillow, you need yarn, a hook, eye buttons .

Torso: 45 VP and knit sc, alternating the colors of the stripes. (6 rows of black, 4 - orange). Repeat 5 times. Then another 14 rows of black sc. Next, we begin to decrease, knitting in each second row the extreme loops together. After 22 rows, close the remaining loops. Tie your back in the same way.

Tail: black thread 3 VP, close in a circle and knit RLS in a circle of 6 rows, knitting in the 1st and 2nd row in 2 stitches from one. Go to the orange thread and knit 6 rows. Repeat striping 3 p. Knit another 14 p. Black thread, close the loop. Stuff the tail.

Feet: orange knit 3 VP with a thread, close it in a circle and knit 6 RLS in a circle of 7 rows, knitting 2 stitches from one in the 1st and 2nd rows. Go to the black thread and tie another 14 p. Close all loops. Tie 4 legs this way. Stuff them.

Ears: Orange thread 15 VP and knit 2 rows of sc. Next, knit RLS, knitting 2 p. Together in the middle of the fabric. There must be 2 details.

Eyes: tie 3 VP with a white thread. Close in a circle and knit 3 rows of sc, knitting two out of one p. Embroider pupils with black thread.

Nose: pink or orange. knit 3 VP with a thread, close from in the backboard and tie 5 rows of sc, knitting two in one and two rows from one. Close all loops. Sew all parts together.

If you do everything correctly, then you will get a wonderful crochet cat!

Crochet Penguin

Crocheted penguin is knitted quite simply. In order to tie such a lovely inhabitant of Antarctica, you will need a little: yarn (black, yellow, white), a hook, eyes.

So, we knit the body. Tie 2 VP then 6 St. BN into the 2nd loop from the hook. The next two rows need to expand the body, knitting 2 sc in 1 loop. In the end, you should get 24 P. The next row is alternating: 2 sc in 1P, sc and in a circle. Next, in each loop we knit one sc. Thus, we knit 10 rows. The next row, 15, we knit like this: 3 sc, 2 knit together. Repeat 7 times. St. BN in the last item. Total - 29 items. To fill. Next row: 2 St. BN, knit 2 RLS together. Repeat this algorithm until the hole is completely closed. Fasten the thread.

We knit the abdomen. We knit 2 VP, 5 PRS in the 2nd P from the hook. We knit the following two rows in this way: 2 St. BN in 1 loop in a circle. Number P should be 20. Next row: RLS, 2 RLS in 1 loop. Round. Total- 30 P, PSBN. Fasten the thread. Sew to the body.
