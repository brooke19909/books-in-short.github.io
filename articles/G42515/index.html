The best winter resorts in Russia: description

Everyone has their own idea of ​​an ideal winter vacation. Someone goes to the tropics to soak up the beach, someone prefers an active winter holiday in the mountains, and someone likes hot springs. In order to have a good winter, it is not necessary to go abroad. Today we will talk about the best winter resorts in Russia!

Sheregesh

On the endless expanses of Siberia in the Tashtagol region at the foot of Mustag Mountain there is a small but very picturesque resort village called Sheregesh. Snow in this region of the Kemerovo region falls in mid-autumn and lies until May! And therefore, it is Sheregesh that tens of thousands of extreme sports fans choose.





The construction of the ski complex was completed in 1981, after the Spartakiad of the peoples of the RSFSR was held here. Of course, then in this place there was only one small lift and a sports school. And there was no such choice of tracks as now, in those days. Athletes were offered only downhill and the "giant". In the late nineties in Sheregesh appeared chairlift and the first cafe!

Today Gesh (that's what tourists call this winter ski resort in Russia) is one of the most dynamically developing resorts in our country! Why vacationers choose it? If you believe the reviews, there are many reasons for this: the air here is clean and healthy, the slopes are steep, the infrastructure is developed, and the snow cover (popularly called chubby) is ideal for steep turns. What else is needed for a perfect holiday?

Big Woodyavr

A special place in the list of winter resorts in Russia takes Big Woodyavr. It is located near Kirovsk. Locals and guests of the resort affectionately call it Big Wood. This ski complex got its name from Lake Big Woodyavr, located nearby. By the way, this lake is the largest in the Khibiny tundra.





It is worth noting that this resort is located at an altitude of 1060 meters, so it is recognized as the highest resort in the North-West of the Russian Federation. It offers modern ski lifts, slopes suitable for both beginners and experienced skiers. Those who prefer the most extreme relaxation, like the magnificent snow park - one of the best in Russia!

This is where families with children should go! Russian winter resort Big Wood offers vacationers the services of instructors who can teach kids how to ski. You can leave your children with experienced tutors - a playroom is equipped at this resort. There are also rental points, left-luggage offices, cafes, ski-service.

Rosa Khutor

One of the most popular winter resorts in southern Russia is Rosa Khutor. It was built for the 2014 Sochi Olympic Games. Today, this resort attracts hundreds of thousands of tourists due to different heights, excellent infrastructure and excellent slopes. And vacationers compare the beauty of landscapes with the Alps! In their reviews, tourists say: from the top of the Rosa Khutor resort, incredible panoramas of the mountains of the North Caucasus open.





This winter resort in Russia for five consecutive years won one of the most prestigious awards - the World Ski Awards, a kind of Oscar for the tourism industry. This award was made possible due to the proximity of the hotel to the ski lifts, well-designed tracks and the highest standards of service.

Dombai

It is difficult to imagine a more interesting holiday in the mountains of Russia in winter than a holiday in the Karachay-Cherkess Republic. Back in the 20th century, Dombai attracted climbers with its snow-capped peaks that seemed impregnable. This place gained particular popularity in the post-war years - the territory began to expand, in this place there was a tourist complex with the first routes with a length of six kilometers. You can translate the name of the resort as "bison". This is due to the fact that in ancient times, these large animals lived here in large herds.





Dombay is not just a popular winter resort in Russia, but also an amazing natural reserve. Superbly developed infrastructure allows you to take here hundreds of thousands of vacationers. In their reviews, tourists note: both dashing riders and beginners feel equally good on these slopes in the winter season. The weather makes the rest comfortable - the average air temperature in winter is around -5 ° C.

The resort offers guests activities such as snowmobiling, speed riding. There is an ice rink here. In addition, Dombay is a climatic resort. A large amount of sunlight, mountain air and mild climatic conditions allow not only a good rest, but also improve health. In Dombay there are many sanatorium-improving complexes.

The main advantage of this winter resort in Russia is its unique nature. Fir forests, mountain rivers, glaciers - all this is here. In addition, in these places you can find hares and squirrels, mountain goats, roe deer and eagles.

Belokurikha

“Siberian Switzerland” - this is how vacationers call the resort Belokurikha. If you are thinking about winter holidays in Russia with children, pay attention to this resort. Previously, he was associated exclusively with spa treatment. However, today guests of Belokurikha enjoy delightful ski slopes.





This resort is located in the Altai Territory. It is distinguished from other places of recreation by a mild climate: in winter, air temperature ranges from -10 to -25 ° C. There are no drops in pressure and temperature, and therefore rest in Belokurikha is a pleasure.

Novice skiers note: professional instructors work with them, a ski school works. Snowboards and skis can be rented. Extremalov will enjoy the extreme park "Russia", which has no analogues in Siberia. There are:

  • snowboard track;
  • tubing track;
  • biker cross track.

The length of this extreme park is 250 meters.

Elbrus region

Thinking about where to go on holiday in Russia in winter? Would you like to snowboard and ski, admire the marvelous scenery and climb the highest mountain cable car in Europe? Then feel free to go to Elbrus!





More than five and a half meters above sea level - this is what Elbrus is. On a sunny day, it may seem that before this Caucasian giant was just a stone's throw away. However, this is just an illusion. In these places, snow lies all year round, so you can ride here at any time of the year! The total length of the tracks is 36 kilometers, and the vertical drop is about 4 thousand meters.

Experienced snowboarders say: this resort is perfect for freeriding - snowboarding. It is here that you can fully reveal all the features of the "board". By the way, since 2000, unique freeride competitions have been held in the Elbrus region! Competitions are held in two disciplines - snowboarding and skiing. Another reason to go to this winter resort in Russia is national cuisine. Shurpa from lamb and hichina with different fillings is in special demand among tourists.

Arkhyz

The youngest resort in the list of winter resorts in Russia is Arkhyz. Its opening took place in December 2015. To date, the resort infrastructure is represented by two chairlifts and one gondola ropeways. The complex has seven ski slopes of varying difficulty. For beginners, there are two “green” tracks, there is “red”, three “blue” and one “black” for professionals.





There are few hotels so far - only three. All in all, the rooms of these hotels can accommodate 418 people.

Guests of the Arkhyz resort are waiting for sports equipment rental offices, left-luggage offices, a gift shop, a first-aid post, restaurants and cafes.

Malkinsky sources

130 kilometers from Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky are Malkinsky sources. We recommend going here to those who choose a vacation in Russia in winter with children older than five years.





The water temperature in these sources is 84 ° C. It flows from six griffins. The guests of the resort say: before there were no so-called puddles, and water flowed directly into the river. But later pools were dug here. Since then, tourists from all over Russia and from abroad have been choosing holidays here. We should talk about the balneological characteristics of the sources. Malka springs can be attributed to siliceous-alkaline, hydrocarbonate-sulfate waters.

There are a number of indications for the use of these waters:

  • various lesions of the nervous system;
  • problems with the musculoskeletal system;
  • skin diseases;
  • chondrosis;
  • gynecological diseases.

This winter thermal resort of Russia has a comfortable recreation center with houses for vacationers.

Yucca

Do you want to relax with your whole family or a large company? Not far from St. Petersburg is an amazing ski resort called Yucca Park. First-class tracks that meet European standards, convenient location - this is what makes this resort so popular.





Near the "Yucca Park" is a healing spring and the ancient Finnish sanctuary. In addition, at the very foot of the mountain there is a forest park, which includes a reserve for Far Eastern deer! And on the opposite side is a crystal clear lake. Winter holidays at this resort are suitable for both children and adults - for kids there is a training slope here, and for professionals - difficult tracks with drifts and turns. Guests of the Yucca Park note the coordinated work of rescuers and instructors, the rest in this place, according to tourists, is not only interesting, but also safe.

Salma

Only 10 kilometers separate this ski resort from the small town of Polyarnye Zori, in the Murmansk region. It is the vacation here that many tourists call the best vacation in Russia in the winter. At this resort you can see the northern lights, the polar night. Salma has been a pilgrimage site for tens of thousands of lovers of northern exoticism and winter sports for many years.





What is the Salma complex? It:

  • ski slopes of different difficulty levels;
  • cheesecake riding;
  • hotel;
  • rental of various sports equipment;
  • Cafe;
  • lounges;
  • parking.

Khakusy

There are thermal springs in Buryatia. They are located in the Severobaikalsky district of the republic, about one kilometer separates them from the coast. The name of the sources can be translated from Buryat as “hot”. According to Evenk mythology, the cape where the Khakus are located is a symbol of the Baikal deity of Dianda. The peculiarity of this place is a clear and clear echo that cannot be heard anywhere on Lake Baikal.





The Buryat hot spring can be safely called the most powerful on Lake Baikal - water comes out at a speed of forty liters per second! The water temperature is +46 degrees Celsius. The Ton Research Institute of Balneology characterizes this source as highly thermal, characterized by low mineralization, slightly alkaline, and siliceous. That is, the water of this source is ideal for the treatment of a variety of diseases, for example, diseases of the nervous system, skin diseases, circulatory problems, musculoskeletal system.

Immersion in a thermal source expands the capillaries, while reducing the load on the heart muscle. Silicon has a positive effect on nerve endings and skin. The source is a great assistant in relieving inflammation. In addition, it calms, anesthetizes, heals damage and accelerates metabolic processes.

Toksovo

What unites beginner skiers, ski jumpers, biathletes and biathletes? Love for the winter resort located in the village of Toksovo. Some tourists call it Kavgolovo, which is fundamentally wrong, because "Kavgolovo" is just the name of the railway station in the village.





This resort is located in a conservation area. It is about twenty kilometers from Peter. Let's talk about the characteristics of Toksovo:

  • there are three slopes and 10 lifts;
  • the length of the longest ski run is 450 meters;
  • the tracks are lit, which allows you to ride in the evening and even at night;
  • there is a toboggan run and ski runs in the resort, ski jumps, quarter pipes;
  • rental and repair of sports equipment;
  • beginner skiers and snowboarders will be met by qualified instructors.

Tourists note: on the territory of the village sports championships and competitions are often held. Therefore, each guest has the opportunity not only to enjoy skiing on good tracks, but also to receive rewards!

Khvalynsk

High mountains, hills and fluffy snow - this is what the winter resort of Khvalynsk is located in the Saratov region. It opened in 2005. Today it can be called a favorite winter holiday destination of our compatriots.





An ideal snowmaking system helps create a system of artificial snowmaking, snow-sealing equipment, snow groomers. You can ride here even at night, because all the tracks are well lit. Modern lifts, experienced instructors, rental of snowmobiles, skis and snowboards, free parking, restaurants and eateries - your vacation here will be simply unforgettable!
