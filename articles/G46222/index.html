Hotel Aquarius 3 * (Montenegro, Budva): hotel description and reviews

Throughout the world, this country is known as Montenegro, and only we Russians call it Montenegro. Once it was part of socialist Yugoslavia, but now it is an independent country in the heart of Europe. Recently, Montenegro is popular as a tourist destination. People who value the beauty and primacy of nature prefer to come here. Here you can rarely see expensive five-star hotels. Everything is very modest and affordable. Russians especially like small aparthotels like Hotel Aquarius 3 * (Montenegro / Budva). Further in the article we will describe this hotel to you, as well as tell you about the features of relaxation in this Mediterranean country.



Travel to Montenegro

Fans of beach, extreme and ecotourism come to this Balkan country. For lovers of superior comfort, it is better not to come here or to choose a hotel belonging to a particular international luxury hotel chain. And Montenegro is chosen by unpretentious tourists, for whom true peace and complete relaxation away from the hustle and bustle come first. Especially like here people who come with the whole family, with small children. As a rule, they book rooms in apart-hotels, for example, in Hotel Aquarius 3 * (Montenegro / Budva), the photo of which you see in this article. Budva

The Budva Riviera is especially attractive for tourists . This is the main tourist center of Montenegro. There are wonderful sandy beaches, amazingly beautiful architecture (the red roofs are just standing), inexpensive, but quite comfortable hotels. The center of the riviera is the city of Budva - one of the venerable resorts on the Adriatic. The city was founded two and a half millennia ago. This is perhaps one of the oldest settlements on the Adriatic coast. Here and there along the riviera the most ancient architectural monuments have been preserved, which attract fans not only of beach tourism, but also of excursion programs.
