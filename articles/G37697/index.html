Villa Biljana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro): hotel description, reviews

If you are looking for a resort with excellent infrastructure and good service for your holiday, while hoping to get not only a beach holiday, but also visit historical and cultural places, pay attention to Montenegro. This is an amazing resort on the Adriatic coast. Montenegro on the map looks like a small state bordering Croatia, Serbia, Bosnia and Herzegovina. Picturesque nature, clean and wide beaches, clear sea water, lots of attractions attract tourists from different parts of the globe. Recently, Russians have significantly increased interest in this area. The most popular and famous resort of Montenegro is Budva, it is here that Russians prefer to spend their holidays. There are many options for living: a hotel room, a separate bungalow, an apartment and even a villa. The choice primarily depends on the preference of vacationers and their financial capabilities. Almost all hotels in Budva can offer good service and excellent living conditions. Villa Biljana 3 * - quite a popular option among holidaymakers with a good rating. But what conditions can he offer vacationers? We offer you a detailed overview of this option.

Location

Montenegro on the world map looks like a very tiny state, but this does not affect the popularity of the resort, the most important thing for a great vacation - the sea, the sun and well-developed infrastructure - is presented in sufficient abundance. So, if you prefer a quiet and relaxing holiday, looking for a place remote from the center, Villa Biljana 3 * will definitely suit you.

Good location of the villa, many call the most important advantage. Within walking distance are all infrastructure facilities, including small shops, souvenir shops, bakeries, cafes and restaurants.





Population

The issue of checking into rooms worries all vacationers. Agree, after a tiring flight, I want to take a shower as soon as possible, put things out and start getting to know the resort. So what can Villa Biljana 3 * offer us? Guest reviews focus on the hospitality of the hosts. The checkout time, as in most other hotels of the resort, starts at 12 o‚Äôclock, however, if there is a room that is free and ready to move in for new guests, tourists will be accommodated before the deadline.

Rooms

The room fund of Villa Biljana 3 * is represented by 12 comfortable standard rooms. Each room is designed for two people. Modern climatic equipment is responsible for maintaining pleasant coolness. Many tourists note the high power of the air conditioner, which instantly cools the air in the room to a predetermined temperature. There is a fridge, satellite TV and a private bathroom. In the morning, vacationers in Budva (Villa Biljana 3 *) will be able to go out on the balcony and enjoy the sound of the surf, the balcony is for many an ideal place for evening leisure. But it is not in all rooms of the villa - it is necessary to clarify its availability at the booking stage so as not to be disappointed upon check-in.





The furnishings are modest, but homely. All vacationers will be able to take advantage of a set of dishes, represented by cups, glasses for soft drinks, cutlery, as well as an electric kettle. Of the furniture in the room there are beds, a wardrobe, a table, chairs - modestly, but enough for a comfortable stay.

Some vacationers complain of interruptions in the supply of hot water in Villa Biljana 3 *. But this problem is quickly solved by contacting the administration. When checking into the room, vacationers will receive bath towels, but, as vacationers assure, they are barely enough to dry themselves. All lovers of large towels, it is better to take them with you. Noise isolation is not the strongest side of the hotel, if you get noisy neighbors, you definitely should not count on peace and quiet in your room. Disturb peace and cars, public transport.





Nutrition

Villa Biljana 3 * offers rooms without meals, but the villa has a good kitchen with a refrigerator, electric stove, small appliances, a set of necessary dishes, cutlery - a solid "four" for catering. You can always have breakfast in the villa, but for lunch and dinner - in one of the local cafes or restaurants. The cuisine here is excellent, the portion is large, and the prices are quite affordable - no one will be left hungry. Lack of food is perceived by many as a disadvantage, but in this case, tourists are not attached to the hotel, can freely move around the city and resort all day, get acquainted with national cuisine.

Beach

The nearest Slavic beach is an average of 10-15 minutes walk. There are a lot of vacationers here in season, so lovers of peace and solitude should take a walk to Mogren Beach. It is located near the Old Town in a picturesque place. In Montenegro, beaches are sand and pebble, but very clean with a gentle entry into the water. All tourists who come to Montenegro for the first time are struck by the crystal clear sea water with a diverse underwater world.





Service

With all the hospitality of the hostess of the villa, tourists complain about the dirt in the rooms, or rather, the lack of cleaning. Many of those who chose Villa Biljana 3 * for themselves, during the week they stayed at the hotel, only a couple of times saw staff in their room. Cleaning involves the removal of garbage and the replacement of towels and bed linen. Unfortunately, only a few vacationers noted the presence of wet cleaning, which is unacceptable in the case of a hotel of this category. If this fact does not bother you, and you can tidy up the room yourself, feel free to book this option.

The reception is open around the clock: for all questions of interest, tourists can always contact the polite and friendly staff.

To summarize

Villa Biljana 3 * (Montenegro, Budva) has certain disadvantages, but many of those tourists who spent their holidays here were quite satisfied. This is a great option for those vacationers who plan to spend at the hotel only night.
