When to go to Sharjah

Trips to this emirate in the summer are getting cheaper, because the zone is deserted and the temperature here soars to unprecedented heights. But there are people who are not afraid of this. In the spring, Sharjah is more comfortable, but you already need to take sunscreen with you and be sure to wear a hat, otherwise there is a danger of severe burns. In addition, at this time of the year hurricanes and sandstorms are frequent, which reach such a force that traffic may stop for a day or two. The tourist season begins in September because the temperature drops. And the weather in Sharjah in December and January can even be somewhat cooler. It happens that in the winter it rains a few times a month. It‚Äôs comfortable to swim in the bay, the water temperature is 24-25 degrees Celsius. But in the evenings the wind can blow, so you need to take a sweater or a light jacket with you. There are big waves on the beach in winter, and lifeguards do not allow anyone to swim. So you have to be prepared for this.

Al Maha Regency Suites 3 * is located near the central market of Sharjah. Many tourist attractions are located not too far from the hotel, and they are conveniently accessible by public transport.
