Sultan of Dreams 5 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Today, Side is considered one of the best resorts in the Republic of Turkey. This is not surprising, because the magnificent local climate and the clean warm Mediterranean Sea are complemented by a host of luxury hotels that can satisfy the needs of even the most sophisticated travelers, and a variety of entertainment for every taste. If the destination of your trip is Side (Turkey), Sultan of Dreams 5 * will be an excellent choice for your stay. We suggest today to get to know this hotel complex more closely, and also to find out what impressions he left with our compatriots who stayed here.





Where is the hotel "Sultan of Dreams 5 *" (Side, Turkey)

The hotel complex Sultan of Dreams 5 * is located on the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea, in the Kyzylot region. The nearest town, Manavgat, is 15 kilometers away, and the center of Side Resort is 18 kilometers away. Antalya Airport is 83 kilometers from the hotel. After arriving in Turkey, it takes about one and a half hours to travel from the air harbor to Sultan of Dreams 5 *. The hotel complex is located on the first line and has its own beach, where you can quickly reach on foot.

Sultan of Dreams 5 *: hotel description

This hotel complex is brand new. So, he accepted the first guests only in 2013. Sultan of Dreams hotel 5 * has at its disposal 350 comfortable rooms that have everything you need for a holiday. Hotel Policies

In accordance with the rules adopted in Sultan of Dreams 5 *, check-in of guests arriving at the hotel begins after two in the afternoon. However, if you arrived at the hotel earlier, and your previous guests had already managed to free your room, and the hotel staff had already prepared, then you will be settled immediately upon arrival. Otherwise, you have the opportunity to leave your luggage in the luggage room and go for a walk around the hotel complex, take a dip in the pool or sea, have lunch. As for the rules of discharge from the hotel, it is carried out until noon on the day of departure. Then it is necessary to pay for your stay, as well as the use of additional services. If you have already pre-paid for a hotel with a travel agency, you will only have to pay extra for services that are not included in the price if you used them. Payment in Sultan of Dreams 5 * accepts both cash and plastic cards of Visa and Mastercard systems . Please note that when booking a hotel via the Internet by credit card, prepayment may be charged to you. Subsequently, at discharge you will only have to pay the difference.

Since the Sultan of Dreams 5 * hotel positions itself as a family institution, children of all ages are allowed here. At the same time, if you place a child under 14 years old in your room in existing beds, you will not have to pay extra for his stay. Also free is the placement of a baby under the age of two years in a crib. But for placing a child over 14 years old or an adult in an extra bed, you will have to pay 70% of the cost of living per person per night. Please note that only one child or extra adult bed can be installed in each room.

As for people going on vacation accompanied by their pets, we are forced to disappoint them. So, in accordance with the rules of the Sultan of Dreams hotel, guests with four-legged pets are not allowed on its territory.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the hotel complex Sultan of Dreams 5 * (Side) has 350 rooms. Among them are single, double and triple, as well as family rooms. They all have a private bathroom, equipped with a bath or shower and hairdryer, individual air conditioning, TV (with channels broadcasting in Russian), telephone, minibar and safe. Tea / coffee making facilities and all necessary toiletries are included. All rooms have balconies with scenic sea or garden views.

The rooms are cleaned daily and bed linen and towels are changed several times a week. You can also order room service at an additional cost.

Sea beach

Like many five-star Side hotels, the Sultan of Dreams is built on the first coastline and has its own beach. The beach at this hotel complex is sand and gravel. Its length is 250 meters. Here, guests can use the sun loungers, mattresses, beach towels and umbrellas for free, as well as refresh themselves with their favorite drinks in the bar. In addition, the yarn created the conditions for water sports.





Nutrition

Meals at this five-star hotel are all-inclusive. Three times a day, skilled chefs serve a buffet of Turkish and international dishes, snacks and delicious desserts in the main restaurant of the hotel complex. There is also a children's menu for the smallest guests. From ten in the morning until midnight in any hotel bar you can taste local drinks for free. As for freshly squeezed juices and any drinks of foreign production, they are paid.

In total there are three main restaurants on the territory of the hotel, as well as two a la carte restaurants where you can order Turkish and Italian dishes from the menu. It should be noted that the food in the Sultan of Dreams 5 * hotel, reviews from tourists only confirm this, it is very tasty and varied. Nobody will stay hungry here.





Pools

The hotel complex has outdoor and indoor pools, as well as a pool for children. There are also water slides and, as part of the animation program, water aerobics classes are held every day. Near the pools there is a sun terrace with free sun loungers and umbrellas. There is also a bar where you can order your favorite alcoholic and refreshing drinks.

Sports and Entertainment

Fans of outdoor activities at the Sultan of Dreams hotel will certainly not be bored. So, here you can play beach volleyball, boccia, tennis and table tennis, football, badminton, volleyball, basketball and billiards. You can also shoot archery and attend a gym or aerobics classes.

Fans of reading can use the local library. Animators entertain guests throughout the day, and lively shows and incendiary discos are held in the evenings. The youngest guests of the hotel did not ignore here. So, for them there is a children's club, there is a playground, and in the evenings a mini-disco is held. In addition, parents can use the babysitting service for a fee.





health and beauty

If you want to relax, the Sultan of Dreams hotel is pleased to offer you the services of a beauty salon and spa center. You can enjoy a massage, or take a steam bath in the sauna or Turkish bath (hammam).

The hotel also has ample parking, the use of which is free for guests. Cost of living

As for the cost of living in the Sultan of Dreams hotel, it can be called quite acceptable for a five-star hotel category. So, a week-long rest here in the summer will cost you in the amount of 40 thousand rubles.





Tourists reviews

Most of our compatriots appreciated the rest in the "Sultan of Dreams 5 *" (Side, Turkey), the description of which is quite consistent with the actual state of things.

So, since the hotel itself is new, the rooms have freshly renovated and modern new furniture and plumbing. The air conditioner is working properly, no damage was observed. The rooms were cleaned every day, and towels and bed linen are regularly changed.

As for food, the majority of guests found it quite acceptable. So, some tourists expressed the wish that the food was varied, but even they agree that the choice of dishes here is scarce.

Pleased travelers and hotel staff. All the staff here are very polite, friendly and quick. However, due to the fact that the main contingent of the guests of the Sultan of Dreams is Turks and Europeans, not all employees understand the Russian language here. However, having basic knowledge of the English language, you can communicate without problems.

The beach of the hotel made a positive impression on most vacationers. There is always a sufficient number of sunbeds and umbrellas for everyone. From time to time, you can see the garbage, however, many believe that it is the fault of the tourists themselves who throw cigarette butts and empty bottles directly onto the ground. The sea is very clean, warm and pleasant.

Also, a lot of enthusiastic comments from our compatriots relate to the entertainment program that the Sultan of Dreams hotel offers its guests. So, in addition to an interesting daytime animation, evening shows are held daily with invited artists. They take place in a beautifully designed amphitheater and leave a truly indelible impression. Their quality can be judged even by the fact that after the start of the performance, it is already not easy to find a free place here.

In general, the vast majority of tourists staying in this hotel complex left an extremely positive impression. Many admit the possibility of coming here again and are ready to recommend the hotel to their friends and relatives.
