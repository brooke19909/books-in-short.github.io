Hotel Radisson Blue Fujairah - description, rooms and reviews

Fujairah is a popular destination for tourists who value vacation in the UAE. The emirate with the capital of the same name differs from other regions of the country. It is the only one with access to the Arabian Sea (Indian Ocean). All other emirates are located on the coast of the Persian Gulf. In addition, Fujairah has an atypical nature for this region. There are majestic mountains, wide beaches and green valleys. Thousands of tourists visit the emirate every year in search of a good holiday on its coast. In our article we want to talk about one of the hotels - Radisson Blue Fujairah, located on its territory.

A bit about the hotel ...

The Radisson Blue Fujairah can be considered a relatively young establishment since it was built in 2007. The hotel is owned by a well-known Kuwaiti company called ACICO. The complex is part of a network of institutions Nikko Hotels Internationals. Previously, the hotel was called JAL Fujairah Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *.



The Radisson Blue Fujairah is located 120 kilometers from Dubai International Airport. It takes just over an hour to get to the hotel. The complex is built on the coast (first line) and covers an area of ​​about 40 thousand square meters. It consists of four-story buildings located along the coast. All of them are connected to each other and form a single complex.

Number of rooms

The rooms of the Radisson Blue Fujairah Hotel are represented by 257 apartments. Among them there are numbers of the following categories:

  1. Superior has an area of ​​41 square meters. It is designed to accommodate two adult guests and one child. The room has a balcony.
  2. Deluxe is a spacious room (45 square meters) with a guest area and a terrace with ocean views. It can comfortably accommodate no more than three guests.
  3. Sakura Suit - spacious apartments with an improved layout (area 56 sq. M.), Designed for three guests. The room has a terrace and a separate bedroom.
  4. Sakura Suit with one bedroom and living room, which are separated by a door. The room has two terraces. The area of ​​the apartment is 71 square meters.
  5. Sakura Suit with two bedrooms and a separate living room. The room can comfortably accommodate 4-5 people.
  6. Sakura Suit with three bedrooms and a living room. Room size is about 113 square meters, it is equipped with two bathrooms.
  7. Sakura Grand Suit with two bedrooms and a living room. The area of ​​the apartment is 199 square meters. They can rest 5-6 guests.

The main advantage of all rooms are beautiful terraces and balconies overlooking the ocean.



Not every hotel can boast of such a convenient layout. In addition, the apartments are equipped with air conditioning, wireless internet, mini fridge, satellite TV, minibar, telephone and safe. The bathroom is equipped with a shower or bathtub, hairdryer and all the necessary toiletries and cleaning supplies.

Catering

Radisson Blu Resort (UAE, Fujairah) offers its guests to choose the most convenient food option for them. Hotel guests have the opportunity to use 4 systems:

  1. Only breakfast. Meals are organized in the form of a buffet, drinks are not included in the price.
  2. Half board. Guests attend breakfast and dinner, drinks are paid separately.
  3. Full Board (Full Board Plus). This system involves breakfast in the form of a buffet, lunch in the Breeze à la carte restaurant, dinner in one of the restaurants of your choice - Marco Polo, Breeze, Al Nokhada. Wine, beer, some alcoholic and all non-alcoholic drinks are included in the price.
  4. All Inclusive provides breakfast in the form of a buffet, dinners and dinners in one of the a la carte restaurants, snacks in the lounge bar, ice cream in the bar for children under 12 years old, drinks (partially alcoholic and all non-alcoholic) are included in the price.

The main restaurant also offers dietary and children's dishes. And four à la carte establishments serve European, Mediterranean and Japanese cuisine.

Hotel infrastructure

Radisson Blue (Emirates, Fujairah) has a good infrastructure. A business center is located on site. Its services can be used by business people. The complex is equipped with 5 swimming pools, equipped with relaxation areas with umbrellas and sun loungers. In addition, there are shops with souvenirs, accessories and clothes on site. Hotel guests can use high-quality free internet.



The reception service is open around the clock. Upon arrival, tourists are offered to make a deposit of $ 500. If you wish, you can refuse. Then all bills will have to be paid immediately in a restaurant or bar (accept cards and cash). If you prefer to make a deposit, then all your expenses are automatically deducted from it.

Three restaurants, two bars, a steam room, a sauna, a fitness center and a jacuzzi are at guests' disposal. Several times a week, the property organizes a free shuttle service to Dubai and Jumeirah. But the shuttle bass must be pre-recorded at the reception.

An on-site spa complex offers massages, aromatherapy, a jacuzzi and other relaxation treatments. All services of the center are paid and are not included in the price of the permit.

Additional paid hotel services

The Radisson Blue Fujairah Hotel (Emirates) offers many additional paid services. There is a diving center and a diving school on site, where you can not only learn the basics of diving, but also go snorkeling and diving.



In addition, tourists have the opportunity to go sailing, parasailing, water skiing, banana, scooter, canoeing and catamaran. The hotel has a hairdresser, laundry and beauty salon.

Rest for children

“Radisson Blue” (UAE, Fujairah) is focused on family vacations, so the administration of the institution takes care of the rest of the youngest guests of the resort. The hotel restaurants offer a special children's menu. For kids organized their own pool with temperature control of the water.



The hotel has several children's clubs that can be visited by children aged 2 to 12 years. If desired, tourists have the opportunity to use the services of a nanny. Baby cots are available upon request.

Beach vacation

The Radisson Blue Fujairah Hotel, as we have already mentioned, is located on the very coast of the ocean. The beach of the hotel is the biggest pride of the establishment. The wide sandy coast of the ocean is the best place to relax with children. The coastal area is very clean and has a wonderful entry into the water. The beach is equipped with sunbeds with soft mattresses and umbrellas. It has a bar.

Radisson Blue Fujairah: reviews of tourists

Speaking about the complex, I want to pay attention to the reviews of tourists who visited it. The Radisson Blue Resort Fujairah has a very favorable location. According to tourists, the beach is the main advantage of the hotel.

The modern building of the complex consists of only four floors. It is built in such a way that all hotel rooms have a wonderful view of the ocean. The highlight is the glass wall that separates the room from the balcony. Without leaving the terrace from the apartment, you can enjoy stunning views of the coast.

According to tourists, the hotel itself and the entire number of rooms are in perfect condition. The work of the staff is at a high level. Very high-quality cleaning is done daily, towels are changed every day, and bed linen every other day. The quality of service is in no way affected by the presence of tips. If you wish, you can leave them, but this is not necessary.



Guests unanimously recommend the institution for those who are planning a trip to the UAE in the near future. The Radisson Blue Fujairah Hotel can be a great place for a relaxing and measured vacation. There are all the necessary conditions for this.

Catering reviews

According to many tourists, the food is organized in the hotel at the proper level. The only complex in the whole region offers to take advantage of All Inclusive. Experienced tourists do not recommend taking breakfast only, as this is completely unprofitable. If you plan to travel actively, then you can pay half board. However, the best option is All Inclusive. The main hotel restaurant offers a good breakfast buffet. The range of dishes is large, so there is always a choice. The rest of the time, guests have the opportunity to eat in a la carte restaurants offering a wide variety of international dishes. A great advantage of the all-inclusive system is the fact that drinks are included. If you prefer half board, you will have to constantly buy water. There are not enough bottles left in the room for a day.



Non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks are also included only in All Inclusive meals. If you prefer a different option, then you will have to pay for tea, coffee and alcohol. Tourists note that the cost of drinks is clearly overpriced. So, for example, tea costs $ 5.

The rest of the food is organized perfectly, the tables always have meat dishes, seafood and fruits. Young guests of the resort have a separate menu, so there is no problem with eating kids.

General impression of the hotel

An ideal option for a family holiday can be the UAE (Fujairah). The Radisson Blue Resort Hotel, located on the coast itself, is perfectly suited for children. The wide sandy coast with a good sunset in the sea is as if created for children. Of course, sometimes there are storms and excitement at sea, but in a quiet time, swimming in it is a real pleasure. The water on the coast is always clean and clear.

Opposite the hotel, there is a coral reef in the ocean where turtles live. Educational excursions are organized on the island that are worth a visit. The reef is even visible from the windows of the hotel.



The wide beach of the hotel is well equipped. It has a large length, due to which there is never a rush of crowds, there is no need to occupy sunbeds. There are much more sun loungers on the coast than vacationers themselves. The beach has all the conditions for a comfortable stay.

The hotel has five pools, among which it is worth highlighting a heated pond for kids. Young tourists are satisfied with visiting the club, in which animators play with them.

Experienced vacationers recommend using a free shuttle service and a trip to Dubai or Fujairah. There you can take a walk, do some shopping and enjoy the local sights. The main attraction of Dubai are the stunning light and music fountains that begin the show program in the evening. If you want to see this beauty, you will have to use a taxi, because the shuttle bass leaves back to the hotel much earlier.

Instead of an afterword

Resort guests recommend the Radisson Blue Resort Hotel as a great place for families. If you are going on vacation in search of entertainment and outdoor activities, then perhaps you should choose another complex. The resort area of ​​Fujairah is a quiet place for a relaxing pastime. There are no big cities and skyscrapers in this region, but all the conditions have been created for tourists who want to relax from the hustle and bustle of megacities.



Emirate is known as the best place for diving. The underwater world of Fujairah is striking in the diversity and beauty of even experienced people in this matter. For this reason, many tourists prefer a relaxing holiday on the beaches of Fujairah.
