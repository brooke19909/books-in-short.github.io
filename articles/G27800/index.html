Campsites Krinitsa: description, reviews

Magnificent forest, high mountains, wonderful valley and stunning views of the sea. That‚Äôs how tourists describe the resort village of Krinitsa, spread out on the Black Sea. Rest here gives incredible emotions, the main thing is to choose the right place. We advise you to pay attention to the campsites of Krinitsa. Reviews and descriptions await you in this article.





"Pear Orchard"

This car camping is located in a resort village very close to the market. Among the advantages of the "Garden" vacationers celebrate a large territory - there is always a place for everyone. In addition, nearby are shops, cafes, dining rooms, and the sea you need to go only five minutes. The water here is clear, as, indeed, the beach and the territory of accommodation.

Speaking about the rest in this Krinitsa campsite, the guests note a low cost, the presence of a fenced area adjacent to the cliff. There is also a specially equipped place for barbecue. It offers vacationers toilets, showers, electricity. The latter service is paid, in addition, you will need your extension cord.

Gelendzhik

Only 60 meters separates the territory of the camping "Gelendzhik" in Krinitsa from the sea. It is located right at the entrance to the resort village. A large camping area is reliably protected from scorching sunlight. Camping is designed for 60 cars. You can check in both in tents (they can be rented directly on the territory of car camping) and in nice two-story wooden houses.





On the territory of "Gelendzhik" there are showers and toilets, and literally thirty meters from the resting place there are cafes and shops. A gentle descent leads to the sea, to the central beach of the village is only 40-150 meters. The only drawback that tourists talk about in their reviews about this campsite is the presence of mosquitoes.

"Proud Frigate"

This cozy Krinitsa campsite is located in the middle of the orchard. The parking area is designed for 7-10 cars. The camping offers breathtaking views of the forested mountains. Near the tent town "Proud Frigate" flows clean and wide Pshada. The Black Sea is also very close - just 300 meters away.

Guests of the town are offered triple tents, which are on the flooring, mattresses and awnings. The bathroom is shared, it is located in the courtyard. Shared camping and kitchen. Among the free services are sun loungers, a table for playing tennis, TV. There are also paid services, for example, showers, water, charging gadgets. There is an opportunity to put up your tent.

Sarmat

This campsite is located in Krinitsa, on Zarechnaya street. It is 150 meters from the sea. The main part of the Sarmatian territory is located in the open space, only a small part of it is protected from the sultry sun by trees.





In just a few minutes from the campsite you can reach an inexpensive market and shops, there is also a small shop right on the territory of the vacation spot. There are also washbasins, showers, toilets, and drinking water. The only thing you have to do on your own is to organize the nutrition process. You can cook in a tent reserved for these purposes, or you can go to village cafes.

By the way, you can even go to Sarmat with small children - there are playgrounds and playgrounds for sports in the territory. And on the beach adjacent to the campsite, there is a children's town.

"Pension Krinitsa"

This campsite is located in Krinitsa, more precisely, on the road from this resort village to another - Bette. It is located before entering the boarding house "Krinitsa". The territory is not very large, designed for only 20-25 cars. Camping is separated by a leisurely ten-minute walk.

Camping Pension Krinitsa can be used for camping. It is worth noting that there are no toilets or showers.
