Hotel "Brigantine" (Gelendzhik): description, rest and reviews

The city of Gelendzhik has long been one of the five most visited resorts in Russia. The tourism industry is highly organized and aimed at a comfortable stay of different social groups. Climatic conditions strongly resemble Crimean, so our compatriots liked this sunny resort area.

The city has built many modern hotel complexes that meet European quality standards. Now it is not necessary to visit abroad at all, all the classic amenities can be found in Russia. One of the best hotels, according to citizens, is the "Brigantine" (Gelendzhik). Its rich and diverse infrastructure will satisfy the needs of any tourist.

Territorial location and features





The hotel was built just six years ago in the very center of the city, not far from the white sand beach. The seven-story building rises majestically in the middle of a mountain archipelago, next to a tropical park and entertainment venues. The Brigantine Hotel (Gelendzhik) harmoniously combines sophisticated classic style in interior design and architecture and modern functionality, which makes it ideal for business, family and beach holidays.

Relaxing in this cozy place, you can freely visit the city's entertainment and tourist sites such as Safari Park, Water Park, Dolphinarium, Museum of Local Lore, Olympus Park, shopping galleries, restaurants and disco clubs. The complex is focused on different social groups of the population from youth to businessmen.

In the residential building and on its territory there is a ban on smoking, so it is safe to relax with small children here. It is not forbidden to bring pets, but only by prior agreement. Naturally, a transfer is provided from the airport and train station in comfortable cars.

Accommodation





The hotel doors are open year round. The complex is able to accommodate about 130 visitors at a time. The building contains 65 rooms of different class, each equipped with necessary amenities.

Complex "Brigantine" (Gelendzhik) regularly receives enthusiastic reviews from guests. Tourists admire the quality of service, cleanliness of rooms and a friendly atmosphere. Particularly pleased with the panoramic windows, the view from which opens directly to the azure Black Sea and Pitsunda pine.

All rooms have loggias with a seating area furnished with tables and chairs. Even in ordinary standard one-room rooms, a split system, an electronic safe, a plasma TV and telephone are installed. There is a necessary set of dishes. Cots are available upon request, and completely free of charge.

For couples with children, there are two-room rooms, cozy studios and superior apartments. The suites are spacious, consisting of spacious rooms (living room and bedroom). Guests can find a complete range of toiletries and hygiene products. Wireless high-speed Internet access is available for a fee. The price includes daily wet cleaning. Beds and towels are changed on a schedule - three times a week. A room service is available.

Diet





Every day, a delicious hot breakfast buffet awaits guests in the restaurant. If desired, you can pay extra for a three-time diet. Works in the hotel "Brigantine" (Gelendzhik) a luxurious restaurant with excellent cuisine. The establishment is constantly surrounded by the magical atmosphere of the holiday, every tourist here is treated as the most expensive and long-awaited guest.

The restaurant is famous for its exquisite menu. An experienced chef will create a real culinary work especially for you, which will be appreciated even by the most fastidious gourmet. Orders for ceremonial and corporate events are accepted. Professional staff will hold any event at the highest level: they will select a photographer, stylist, decorator or animators who will make the holiday creative, vibrant and memorable. There is a separate menu for children.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has a small cozy tavern for up to 50 people. Here in a relaxed atmosphere you can relax, listen to pleasant music and have a heart-to-heart chat.

Coastline





The Brigantine Hotel (Gelendzhik) does not have its own beach, but just a few steps stretches the sandy municipal coast. The coastline is quite well-groomed, equipped with beach areas, entertainment points and cafeterias. For rent mattresses, sun loungers and umbrellas.

Guests can ride the Black Sea on a boat, yacht, catamaran or ATV. There is everything for scuba diving enthusiasts. Children will not be bored either: nearby is a large water park with water slides, inflatable trampolines and shallow pools.

Services

The entire territory is under round-the-clock video surveillance. The hotel "Brigantine" (Gelendzhik) built a guarded parking lot. A library is open for book lovers, where anyone can choose their favorite literary work. A pleasant surprise for the fair sex will be a beauty salon, which has open rooms for manicure, pedicure, massage. There is a travel agency on the ground floor of the complex. Newlyweds are given discounts and organize romantic walks and photo shoots against the backdrop of magnificent landscapes.

Service for business





Since the Brigantine complex (Gelendzhik) is more focused on business tourism, corporate service here is at a decent level. The building has a spacious conference room for 150 people. The room is equipped with a multimedia projector, a large plasma screen, the Internet and the necessary office equipment.

The administration can organize both business and special occasions. At your request, they can invite famous artists, arrange sports, organize an entertainment program and friendly evenings to enhance corporate culture.

Children's leisure

The hotel has a special attitude towards the little guests of the resort. Free accommodation is provided for children under 10 years of age. Toddlers are given high chairs in the restaurant, as well as strollers, cribs and playpens (for a fee). Parents can use the paid services of an experienced nanny. Kids enjoy the swing area and bike rental.

Conclusion



The optimum ratio of price and quality, an individual approach, a calm and friendly atmosphere - all this will give the hotel "Brigantine" (Gelendzhik). Reviews vacationers for the most part in a positive manner.

The hotel attracts by location. Pleasantly surprised by the perfect order throughout. A plus is the availability of parking and 24 hour security.

Couples with kids appreciated the variety of children's entertainment. Particularly pleased with the separate children's menu. Business travelers enthusiastically spoke of the multifaceted service. Many thanks to the tourists expressed to the administration and staff who put their soul into their work. Without them, relaxation would hardly have become so comfortable.
