Hotel Mercury 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): description of rooms, services, reviews

Bulgaria is a place that has recently enjoyed considerable popularity among tourists. Many want to go to this country. But at the same time, they want to pay a small amount for the vacation. And the hotel was good. Well, in this case, the Mercury 4 * hotel complex (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) will be an ideal option.





general information

This hotel has existed for over 35 years. It was built in 1980. But, since the last repair work was carried out in 2010, everything looks beautiful, new and modern. This is one of the reasons why every year thousands of tourists come to Mercury 4 * (Bulgaria).

Sunny Beach is the largest seaside resort in the east of the country. It is located on the Black Sea bay, along the beach, which extends 10 kilometers into the distance.

The nearest airport, located in Burgas, is about half an hour to drive. A bus stop is located 100 meters from the hotel. From there you can drive anywhere. Even to the city of Nessebar, the old part of which is included in the UNESCO World Heritage List.

However, the hotel itself was located in a favorable tourist location. The beach can be reached in a few minutes, as well as the city center. At 200 meters from the hotel is the Mania Dance Club, Royal Beach Mall, and a little further - a water park. In general, everyone will find where to walk.

Service

Mercury 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is a hotel focused on comfortable rest of guests. The staff working here is doing everything possible to ensure that guests are satisfied. By the way, the hotel staff speaks not only Bulgarian, but also German, English and Russian. The latter is particularly pleasing.

Wired internet access is available in public areas (surcharge). There is private parking - the place costs 5 euros per day. A 24-hour front desk welcomes guests in the lobby. There is also a storage room, car rental, currency exchange, a beauty salon with a hairdresser, and a laundry with dry cleaning. There is even a small conference room.

It is forbidden to smoke on the territory of the complex, but separate places specially designed for this are organized.





Entertainment

The vast majority of people come to Mercury 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) to enjoy beach and sea activities. But, however, if you want to stay in a hotel, you will not have to be bored either. There is an outdoor pool with a terrace where tourists sunbathe, as well as entertainment staff.

Lovers of an active lifestyle will love the fitness center. They can also organize an excursion here - you just need to contact the reception. By the way, the hotel also has a small SPA-center, where they provide cosmetic services and make excellent massage.

For the smallest, a separate playground and a mini club are organized. Even a small water slide was installed for them. And in general, as tourists who have been here assure, children's animation here is better than adult shows. In the afternoon, the kids are under the constant supervision of animators who arrange a mini-disco for them in the evening. For adults, karaoke and sometimes dance evenings are organized once a week.





Nutrition

Mercury Sunny Beach 4 * has an excellent restaurant. People who have been to this hotel claim that the food is always fresh and tasty. The assortment is quite wide. For lunch and dinner - an abundance of meat and hearty dishes. At the buffet there is always chicken, fish, delicious meatballs, lamb, pork, goose, duck, beef and even turkey. This is the case when the all-inclusive system is fully justified. To all this - a lot of side dishes. And for dessert - desserts, ice cream, fruits, cakes. Tables are always replenished with fast and efficiently working waiters - even if the meal is already drawing to a close.

It is also worth noting that you can always find food for the child. This aspect is especially interesting for people with children who decide to take them with them on vacation. They even cook soups and broths.

By the way, there is also a great snack bar serving crispy sweet cookies, flavored pizza on thin crust, ice cream and sandwiches. But the most important thing is the variety of fruits. There are grapes, peaches, oranges, pears, apples, watermelons, melons - everything that corresponds to the season.





Apartment Options

Mercury Sunny Beach 4 * in its fund has 125 rooms. Most of them are 26 square meters standards. m. There are two options. One room has a king-size bed. In others - two separate, standard.

The amenities are the same. It has a furnished balcony, satellite TV, powerful air conditioning, radio, seating area, sofa and wardrobe. There is also a bathroom with toilet, bath, shower and hairdryer. Linen and towels are provided. The weekly price of such apartments for two people will be less than 40,000 rubles (with the all-inclusive service). If you plan to settle with a child, you will have to pay about 54 thousand rubles. And for guests with two children, the price will increase to 57 thousand rubles. The smallest can sleep on a comfortable folding sofa. By the way, for three adult guests, the weekly price of accommodation will be approximately 73,000 rubles.

However, the Mercury 4 * hotel does not set fixed prices. Like many other hotels. The cost of living here depends on a lot of factors, and one of the main ones is seasonality.

Booking Nuances

Hotel Mercury 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is quite popular. Largely - thanks to a pleasant combination of low price, all-inclusive services and quality of service. Therefore, if you want to relax here, you need to reserve rooms in advance.

Check-in starts at 14:00 and lasts until midnight. In any case, it should be so. But, as the guests here said, if tourists arrived at the hotel earlier, then they can arrange them without waiting for 14:00. But only if there are rooms free from guests. And the departure lasts until noon. Return flights should be ordered, taking into account this fact.

With children, as mentioned above, you can live here. Many with them go to Sunny Beach. The reviews left by tourists make sure that this is the right decision. But you just need to know that the hotel does not provide cots. Kids will have to sleep on the sofas available in the apartment.

Tourists about the resort

People who have visited a place like Sunny Beach leave very positive reviews. Everything is fine here. First of all, tourists note the excellent transport connection in the form of buses that go to various directions. Even to Elenite and Burgas can be reached by taxi. And you do not need to wait long for it - buses run every 2 minutes.

Another highlight of the resort is the beaches. Clean, with pleasant golden sand, umbrellas and sun loungers. Although they can not be used - just lay a towel and rest like that. By the way, you can swim without fear. On the beach, if anything, lifeguards are on duty. And they really are on duty, and not sitting for a look. Moreover, if someone does not obey the ban on swimming (this happens during periods of strong waves) - they are "caught" by force from the water.

In general, everyone who was here assures us that lovers of fun should go here. Sunny Beach never sleeps. Except for the holiday season. And in the summer and in the spring nightlife is seething here. We especially love Sunny Beach for young people who sleep during the day, and with the onset of sunset they go out to conquer entertainment establishments.

Staff

And now you can return to the topic of the hotel. Or rather, to the staff. In the hotel, as mentioned above, people work, trying to do everything for their client. Rooms are cleaned daily, replacing towels. Linen is laid fresh every three days.

I am glad that there are ashtrays on the balcony, and in the bathroom - shampoos and gels. Maids, by the way, do not take tips if you leave them in the room - even if in the most prominent place, on the bed. If you want to reward them, then you need to hand the money directly into your hands.
