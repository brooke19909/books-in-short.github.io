How to transfer money to Yandex-wallet - detailed description

Virtual money today is very popular. And this is not surprising: modern electronic payment systems are characterized by safety, reliability and convenience. With the help of them, you can make payments via the Internet, including purchasing goods, paying for services, and making many other lump-sum and periodic payments. E-wallets are also actively used by people working on the World Wide Web. All these features are available to users of the Yandex.Money settlement system.



Yandex.Money: instant payments on the Internet

The Yandex.Money payment system is one of the most popular services for instant payments via the Internet. It appeared in 2002 and from the very beginning was focused on Russian users. The only currency in this payment system is the Russian ruble. Here you can activate independent from each other Internet and Yandex-purses. To open the latter there is a special program. Top-up and payment operations are the main ones, they are most often used by account holders in the system. How to transfer money to Yandex-wallet? There are several ways to do this, for example, you can use the terminal or replenish your account with a personal credit card. Those who are registered in other payment systems, sometimes it is easier to transfer money from another wallet. Consider the most popular ways.

Replenishment of Yandex-wallet: self-service terminals

How to transfer money to Yandex-wallet? It is possible to replenish cash through a self-service terminal through a virtual system. Funds usually arrive immediately. This can be done, for example, in the offices of almost all major banks, in the Euroset and Svyaznoy offices, as well as in shopping centers and supermarkets. Any terminal provides information about the percentage of the commission charged for the transfer. It should be taken into account that the amount for payment should be prepared in the required amount, without delivery. To make a transfer, you need to perform the following steps:

  • In the main menu of the terminal, click on "Electronic Money" or "Internet".
  • Select an instant payment system Yandex.Money.
  • Indicate your account number, check it.
  • Deposit the required amount.
  • Complete the translation.

Bank transfer to Yandex-wallet with a bank card

Cashless payments with a personal bank card are now becoming a good alternative to paying in cash. It is more convenient and safer. The same method can be used when replenishing an account in a virtual payment system. How to transfer money from a card to a Yandex-wallet? This method is available directly from the electronic payment system website. It is only necessary to indicate all the requested data from a personal bank card, enter the amount and complete the transfer. Another option is to replenish your wallet at an ATM. To do this, enter the PIN code, click on the "Payments" button in the main menu, then "Electronic Money", select the Yandex.Money icon, indicate the personal account number, the required amount and confirm the operation. Internet banking is another affordable way to transfer funds from a personal bank card to a virtual wallet. You can do this from your home computer via the Internet. It is better to check with the service bank about the details of connecting and using this service.



Replenishment of Yandex-wallet in partner offices

It is possible to replenish the account of the electronic cash payment system in the salons of communication of some mobile operators, as well as in the Euroset and Svyaznoy offices. How to transfer money to a Yandex-wallet in this case, the office staff will prompt.

Other ways to replenish Yandex-wallet

How to transfer money to Yandex-wallet in another way? You can use:

  • Prepaid cards: after entering data from them in the electronic payment system, the account is replenished by the appropriate amount.
  • By postal order.
  • Cash in the wallet of another virtual settlement system.

Other electronic payment systems: WebMoney and Qiwi

In addition to the Yandex.Money system, there are many other electronic payment systems. Among them, the most popular are WebMoney and Qiwi. Moreover, the same person can be the owner of electronic wallets simultaneously in two or more payment systems. Therefore, the method of exchanging funds between them is of interest to many. How to transfer money to Yandex-wallet from the account of other payment systems?



Wallets of various electronic payment systems: Yandex.Money and WebMoney

How to transfer money to "Yandex" wallet from WebMoney? You can attach them to each other, after which the transfer of funds between electronic systems will not be difficult. Before proceeding to the procedure itself, you should make sure that three prerequisites are met. Firstly, the passport data in both systems are the same. Secondly, a certificate no lower than formal was obtained at WebMoney. In order to do this, you need to scan the main page of your passport, as well as the TIN and send them for verification to the Certification Center. Moreover, copies of documents should be in color. The third prerequisite: Yandex-wallet must be identified. There are two ways to do this: by sending a statement certified by a notary public by mail to the address of the payment system and transferring via CONTACT.

Binding of Yandex.Money and WebMoney wallets

If all the requirements are met, you can proceed to the binding procedure. To do this, you need:

  • Log in to your account WebMoney payment system.
  • Open the "Account Binding Service".
  • Select the Yandex.Money icon.
  • Indicate the number of the virtual wallet to be replenished.
  • Save the received code necessary for binding.
  • Log in to your Yandex.Money payment system account.
  • Click on the link "WebMoney Wallet Binding" and enter the code.

It is enough to perform these actions once. How to transfer money from WebMoney to Yandex-wallet after successful completion of the linking procedure? To do this, open Kiper Classic, click on the icon of the Yandex.Money payment system, indicate the required amount and confirm your intention to complete the transfer.



Wallets of various electronic payment systems: Qiwi and Yandex.Money

The ability to transfer funds from the Qiwi payment system to Yandex.Money does not exist directly. If necessary, make a similar operation, you can use a credit card. First, you need to withdraw to it the required amount from one electronic payment system, and then replenish the account in another. How to transfer Yandex.Money to Qiwi wallet? In the same way. Instead of a bank card, it is possible to use the virtual QIWI Visa Virtual, as well as the WebMoney payment system.

How to transfer money to Yandex-wallet from Yandex-wallet?

The Yandex.Money settlement system allows for internal transfers between owners of electronic wallets. This operation often has to be carried out, for example, when paying for goods in online stores. How to transfer money from Yandex-wallet to another Yandex-wallet? First of all, you need to open your account on the Yandex page. To do this, enter the login and password in the upper right corner, after which access to e-mail, disk, and wallet is provided. Then you can proceed directly to the transfer of funds:

  • Open the "Translate" section.
  • Indicate the email address (in the Yandex system) or the personal wallet number of the recipient of funds.
  • Enter the amount that should come to the recipient's wallet (no commission).
  • Write a comment: payer name, purpose of transfer.
  • Enter billing password.
  • Confirm transfer.

Refund of funds that were erroneously credited to someone else's account in the Yandex.Money system

When working with money, you always need to be careful. This will avoid a lot of trouble. The same rule should be followed when replenishing the Yandex-wallet, regardless of the selected transfer method. That is why when making an operation through self-service terminals, ATMs or with the participation of office employees, a repeated check of the account number is required. However, such measures are not always effective. How to transfer money to a Yandex-wallet with a Yandex-wallet in case of an erroneous replenishment of someone else's account? First of all, you should write to the support service of the electronic payment system and request information about the existence of a wallet, the details of which were erroneously indicated during the transaction. It should be borne in mind that in the case of a positive response, a refund is possible only if an agreement is reached between the recipient and the payer of funds. Of course, if there was no fraudulent activity. Otherwise, you can go to court. However, for this you need to know the real data of the owner of the wallet for which the money was mistakenly credited.

It is not surprising that the number of their users, including the owners of Yandex-purses, is growing daily. At the same time, the number of questions related to the rules of the payment system is increasing. Fundamental is information on how to fund your account, as well as how to transfer money to another wallet. Yandex.Money provides many ways to do this. Each wallet owner will be able to choose among them the most acceptable for themselves. It is worth remembering that in most cases a commission is charged for a replenishment or transfer operation. And its size should be clarified in advance.
