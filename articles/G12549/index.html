Bank income cards: rating, types, conditions and reviews

Many people think that modern banks provide only debit or credit cards, but in fact there are those that provide for constant interest accrual, and they are called "profitable". Cards of this type are currently offered by a sufficiently large number of banks, and each one puts forward its own terms of cooperation. It is for this reason that many people begin to think about how to choose the right cards and use them in the future.

What is it?





Profitable cards are accounts on which the bank will constantly accrue interest, depending on how much money is left in the account. It is worth noting that such a card itself is a credit or debit card, but at the same time, interest on the balance is accrued exclusively in the case of a credit account.

Which to choose?

Today, income cards offer a wide variety of interest calculation schemes. Consider them below.

Monthly bonus





The rate in this case will directly depend on the total size of the minimum balance at the end of the month, as well as on what type of card is being considered.

The interest rate, as well as the requirements for the balance, are quite diverse and directly depend on which bank you gave your preference to. Thus, for example, in accordance with the terms of Promsvyazbank, this minimum is 15,000 rubles, for which the client will be given a bonus of 4.5%, while Binbank provides the opportunity to keep up to 10,000 in your account, and while making a fixed rate of 5%. With this example, you can very clearly understand how revenue cards vary depending on the institution in which you prefer to issue them.

Daily bonus





In this case, the minimum balance must be respected daily in order for a person to receive a bonus. In this case, the percentage in the vast majority of cases varies between 8% and 10% per annum.

What is the benefit for the customer?





First of all, bank return cards are a kind of alternative to modern conditions of short-term deposits, while having a very, very nice interest rate. In addition, unlike a standard bank deposit, you always have the opportunity to withdraw money from your own account in order to spend it on your needs.

Clients themselves in their reviews often say that profitable debit cards or credit always have certain conditions that can not always be met in order to have a decent income, but at the same time the funds received in the majority of cases are quite enough to fully cover the cost of maintenance or payment of a mobile bank.

Next, we will consider the most optimal options from banks so that each user can independently compare existing offers. In this case, a rating based on user reviews was used to select revenue cards.
