How to disable MTS-info: weather or news

Today we have to learn how to disable MTS-info. More precisely, how to connect certain services and refuse them. In fact, many users of the MTS network are asking this question. After all, you have to pay for all connected packages and offers. And the information that comes to the phone is sometimes useless. Therefore, now we will need to study the possibilities of "News" and "Weather". How to disable "MTS-info" in this case? What do you need to connect? How much does this or that service cost? All this will be discussed later.

Connection

But first you need to connect yourself to paid subscriptions. Without this, disconnecting them simply will not work. How to realize the idea? MTS-info has its own USSD request for each service package. You need to know them. Or you will have to work with the Mobile Assistant service for a long time, which helps to enable and disable the MTS-info functions.

To subscribe to the "News", you must dial the combination * 111 * 4766 # from your mobile device. After sending the request, you will be charged 6 rubles for the upcoming day. And after that a message will come with interesting news. True, it is not a fact that they will be to your taste.



"Weather" is connected via * 111 * 4761 #. In response, you will receive daily weather reports the next day. But pay attention - information is provided only within the area where the SIM card was registered. The weather forecast does not depend on your current situation.

A distinctive feature of the MTS-info service is that with a one-time connection, the subscription fee will be automatically deducted from the account until you fill out a request to refuse one or another service. It turns out that there are no conditions for disabling functions as unnecessary. Not very convenient, but that's how the system works. You should independently think how to disconnect "MTS-info".

Next, inform the operator that you want to unsubscribe from the "News" and "Weather". The employee will check for connectivity. In the case when the subscription is really issued, the employee will fill out a request for disconnection. The answer is not long in coming - you will receive an alert. For news

How to disable "MTS-info" ("News")? There is a special USSD request for this. As already mentioned, similar features are available on all paid subscriptions. And just this layout is more popular with all users.

In order to once and for all cancel the MTS Info news messages, you will have to form the command * 111 * 1212 * 2 #. If you send this request for processing, then after a while the problem with the subscription will be resolved. In response, you will receive a notification about the successful processing of the operation. Just what you need! So we learned how to disable MTS News-Info. But that is not all.

For the weather

How to disable "MTS-info" ("Weather")? In the same way as in the previous case. That is, with the help of a special team that will be sent from your phone. This process does not require a fee and is extremely fast.

To refuse the Weather service, dial * 111 * 4751 #. And all problems will be solved very quickly. In addition, this subscription can be removed using the SMS request. To do this, write 2 in the text of the message and send it to 4741. If you are in roaming, you will be charged as for regular SMS. If you are within a city or region, then, as already mentioned, the process will be free. In the end, you will receive a message in which the result of the operation will be indicated.

Through the site

Still wondering how to disable the MTS-info service? Then the MTS page may come to the rescue. There is a "Personal Account", which allows you to manage all services connected on the SIM card.



In order to squirm from "News" and "Weather", go through the authorization on the site and click on the inscription "Services". Find relevant subscriptions there. On the right side of the screen, click on "Disable". In the window that appears, enter the request confirmation code, which will be sent to your mobile phone as an SMS message, and then click on "OK." The result is not long in coming.
