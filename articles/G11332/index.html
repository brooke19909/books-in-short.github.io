Tomsk Regional Perinatal Center: address, photos and reviews

The regional perinatal center in Tomsk serves patients from all over the region. Basically, pregnant women come here with various pathologies of the fetus and with health problems in women themselves.

Regional Perinatal Center of Tomsk: address

The medical facility is located on the street. I. Chernykh, 96/1. The registration desk of the center is open on weekdays from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. The department of the clinic accepts patients for consultations from Monday to Friday.





Hospitals work around the clock. In this block of the hospital there is an emergency room, which takes emergency care with patients or you can enter it with self-treatment.

Description

The regional perinatal center of Tomsk opened in 2011. Since then, over 3000 patients have been treated here annually. At its base is the department of assisted reproductive technologies.





Here, 154 people can be observed at the same time in a 24-hour hospital. The center has equipment that is unique to the entire Siberian Federal District.

And also in the medical institution, a special contact group has been created, which makes trips in severe cases of delivery to other maternity hospitals in the region. If necessary, with a special reanimobile, the kids are delivered to the center.

In the hospital, contractual services for women are possible. Details can be found at the reception.

Department of Obstetric Pregnancy Pathology

The department carries out drug treatment with injections or droppers. If necessary, surgery is performed. Many pregnant women spend more than one month here to save the baby.





The hospital has a modern laboratory, which does various research:

  • biochemical;
  • immunological;
  • clinical;
  • bacterial;
  • hormonal and others

The results of the tests come to the doctors in a short time, which makes it possible to establish the correct diagnosis in time and take the necessary measures.

Clan branch

The regional perinatal center of Tomsk (TIN 7017245682) has at its base 10 specialized halls for the birth of babies. They have installed all the necessary equipment for the case of resuscitation care for both mother and baby. There is a constant supply of oxygen.

All patients, if desired, are anesthetized with modern anesthetic drugs. This type of manipulation is used only if the woman has no contraindications to them.





In the regional perinatal center of Tomsk, the presence of one of the relatives at the birth is allowed. In this case, the woman feels support and does not experience unnecessary excitement. A partner can also relieve pain during contractions using massages.

The department has three operating rooms. Two of them are intended for planned and emergency caesarean sections and one for newborns. During surgery, several types of anesthesia are used, depending on the indications:

  • general;
  • epidural
  • dorsal.

If a woman is conscious after a cesarean section, the newborn is immediately applied to her breast. Thus, from the first minutes, the baby is fed by the mother.

If the woman is under the influence of general anesthesia, then the newborn is placed on the chest to the father to feel tactile contact by both of them.

Qualified anesthetists of the department anesthetize patients during childbirth. And also present at cesarean section. A team of specialists with resuscitation travels at the request of other maternity hospitals in the region to assist heavy women in labor.

Pediatric department

Small children and babies born with any pathologies are nursed here. The department has all the necessary equipment for various medical procedures:

  • open resuscitation systems;
  • mobile cots;
  • mattresses for heating;
  • special heaters;
  • fiber optic phototherapy system;
  • electronic balance;
  • pulse oximeters;
  • a device for determining bilirubin;
  • electronic thermometers.




For infusion of drugs, modern infusomats are used. In rooms for newborns mothers are allowed at any time. With the improvement of the baby's condition, he is transferred to one ward with a woman in labor.

Department of Assisted Reproductive Technologies

The center consults infertile couples. They undergo a high-quality and complete diagnosis and, as a result, treatment is prescribed. If medical measures are not successful, then in vitro fertilization is possible.

Professional gynecologists, reproductologists and genetics work in this department. Preparation for IVF occurs with the help of modern hormonal drugs that carry a minimal amount of side effects.

Materials are taken under sterile operating conditions. If necessary, a woman after replanting an embryo is sent for several days to a hospital for further monitoring of pregnancy.

Happy couples who managed to have a baby leave positive feedback about IVF in the Regional Perinatal Center of Tomsk. Many women managed to get pregnant after the first attempt at replanting the embryo; others took several of them. But they all came to the long-awaited result and became mothers.

Department of Radiation and Ultrasound Research

The center is equipped with ultrasound equipment using high-frequency sensors. After the examination, doctors give the results with the seal of a medical institution and pictures.

The center conducts ultrasound examinations by doctors of the highest category. They have extensive experience and can accurately determine any violations during pregnancy or other gynecological pathologies in women.

If necessary, 3D and 4D technologies are used. Future mothers can get a high-quality picture of their unborn baby. These photos will become an integral part of the family album.

Regional Perinatal Center in Tomsk: reviews

Today you can find a lot of comments about the work of this medical institution. Patients are satisfied with the specialists who take in the antenatal clinic. There are negative comments mainly only regarding the work of the registry.

Women note that it is difficult to get through here the first time, and making an appointment with a specialist is not always quick. As well as patients indicate that even with a coupon for a certain time, you often have to wait in line for a long period.





Regarding comfort, only positive reviews were written in the hospital. Patients are satisfied with the situation in the wards and note that the arrangement here is like in a good hotel room.

And also women are satisfied with the food in the medical facility. They note that they feed here deliciously and with a good variety of dishes. Thus, relatives do not have to bring daily food transfers.

Patients from all departments are satisfied with the quality of medical services. They note that the doctors here are professional and friendly. Delivery takes place according to the plan and most often with a favorable outcome, despite the difficult pathologies in women and children.

Sometimes you can find comments that indicate that in the regional perinatal center of Tomsk, vacancies constantly arise among doctors. This indicates that qualified doctors are moving to other clinics.

Moms in many reviews express gratitude to the staff of children's resuscitation. They note that their children were monitored around the clock and doctors always keep their parents informed. They do not get tired several times a day to report on the condition of the newborn to each mother.

Relatives can visit women in labor at a set time. In individual wards, the presence of a father is permitted. Also, patients are satisfied with the possibility of a partner birth. They note that this service must be agreed upon in advance, and the husband must undergo the medical examination indicated in the center.

Also, parents are pleased with the possibility of a solemn discharge from the hospital. Here you can order a photo and video. Kids with mothers are discharged home in a decorated room with congratulations and the issuance of documents.
