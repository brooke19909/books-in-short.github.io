How to take a screen picture on Android: tips for creating screenshots

When choosing a mobile phone, many people prefer the Android operating system. It is comfortable with its simplicity. Even the new owner of the phone will be able to deal with Android. This operating system has a huge number of features. Sometimes you have to think about how to take a picture of the screen on Android. What will be required to translate ideas into reality? Is there any way to photograph what is happening on the display? Answers to all these questions will be given below.

Ways to create screenshots

To begin with, it should be noted that there are several options for solving the problem. Everyone can take screenshots on an Android mobile phone. But you have to take into account the features of a particular smartphone.



I wonder how to take a picture of the screen on Android? Offered:

  • use the system features of the phone;
  • use the gadget control buttons;
  • through additional devices (for Samsung);
  • work with third-party programs.

In the second case, several methods for activating the image capture function from the display are provided. Next, we will talk about all the possible methods for creating screenshots on Android.

System features

First, consider a method that involves working with the gadget settings. If a person is thinking about how to take a picture of the screen on Android (Samsung Galaxy), you can use one very interesting trick.



It is important to note that the proposed option 100% works on Samsung. To create a screenshot, you will need:

  1. Turn on the smartphone. Go to the main menu of the gadget.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Go to the section "Gesture management" - "Action when moving hands."
  4. Click on the line Palm swipe to capture.
  5. Open the page or image you want to capture.
  6. Swipe your hand (e.g. palm) across the display from left to right.

The actions taken will result in the screen being captured. The owner of the phone will see the save image. That's all!

How on Android to take a picture of the screen? The following tips may help:

  2. Press the "Turn on the power" and "Home" buttons. Ideal for creating screenshots on Samsung.

Additional devices

The next tip is relevant for mobile from Samsung. This manufacturer has a special stylus called SPen. It allows you to quickly capture the image on the display.

How exactly to act in this case? It is proposed to act approximately like this:

  1. Open the desired image on the phone.
  2. Get SPen. Press the device activation button.
  3. Double consult the screen of the smartphone.

The resulting image will be saved. You can find it in Pictures / ScreenShots or Pictures / ScreenCapture.
