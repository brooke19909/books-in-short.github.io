London Outlet Bicester Village

Every woman knows about sales, but not everyone is aware of the existence of outlets. In these specialty stores, new items from old collections of brands from around the world are sold at great discounts. And the more famous the outlet, the higher the chance to catch a branded item. This is the outlet at the London Bicester Village.

About Outlet

Located in Oxfordshire, Bicester Village Outlet is a bastion of comfort and tranquility in the shopping world. Surrounded by beautiful landscapes, you can spend the whole day or even a whole weekend. All year round, you can find numerous global brands that have their headquarters in the most famous capitals of the world.

This is not just a store of discounted collections, but a whole town consisting of 160 pavilions selling the best clothes and household goods at a reduced price. Numerous cafes and restaurants offer guests delicious food and relaxation in between shopping.

Here are the stores of such famous brands as Hugo BOSS, Alexander McQueen, ASICS, Calvin Klein, Dior, Diesel, Jimmy Choo, D&G, Fossil, Max Mara and many others.

How to get there

The Bicester Village Outlet can be reached by public transport, taking the train at London's Marybone Station. There is a direct train from here to the new Bisters Village Station. The trip takes 46 minutes. This means that getting to the outlet is not difficult.

Another way to get here is by car using the navigator. For this, the outlet provides a satellite navigation code: OX26 6WD. Or take a taxi.

A Shopping Express bus also leaves from central London. On the way, the bus makes stops at the most popular hotels in the capital. There are two flights during the day, both in one and in the opposite direction.
