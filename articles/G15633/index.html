Where are the Bardeevsky fonts located?

Those who want to relax in a cozy and quiet place and improve their health should discover such a resort place in Slovakia as Bardeevskie Fonts. However, it gained fame for a long time, the first mention of fonts dates back to 1247. Even then, the people who lived in the district used the healing waters of the springs, which they beat with keys from under the ground. Currently, it is one of the famous resorts in Slovakia, famous for its fonts, baths, mud and unique mineral waters.

Historical reference

Bardeev is one of the most beautiful spa towns in Slovakia. Seven centuries ago, it became known for its mineral waters, which have a beneficial effect on the gastrointestinal tract. Healing water from mineral springs was taken even to Berlin, Budapest, Vienna. In 1505, construction began on the first font for patients. In 1777, a house with 12 rooms was built for relaxation. Polish and Hungarian nobles came to be treated "on the water". The news of the wonderful waters was spreading in Europe, and soon the reigning persons began to come to the Bardeevsky font, even for the family of the Habsburg dynasty, the font was a favorite vacation spot.



In 1795, Professor Pavol Kitaybel made water analyzes from two then known sources - Kurortny and Glavny. The professor‚Äôs report informed the medical community that water should be used in the treatment of headaches, female diseases, gout, and stomach diseases. From that moment began the export of water from mineral springs to European countries. The heyday of the resort falls on the first half of the XIX century. By this time, spa treatments used water from 13 sources.

Resort Location

There are not many resort places where the climatic conditions are ideally combined, a convenient location with a beautiful environment and mineral springs with healing water. Such a place, according to tourists, is in the north-east of Slovakia. Bardeevsky fonts - balneotherapy foothill resort. It is located among amazingly virgin nature, in the Lower Beskids, in the valley of the Bardeevsky stream.

This fertile place on the territory of the coniferous-deciduous forest area of ‚Äã‚Äã1000 hectares is located above sea level at an altitude of 325 meters. Indications for treatment at the resort

Due to the uniqueness and healing properties of natural mineral springs, as well as the natural environment that contributes to a high level of treatment, the Bardeevskie Kupeli sanatorium in Slovakia is popular not only among compatriots, but also among foreign tourists. People with gastrointestinal diseases, chronic gastritis, colitis, and pancreatitis not in the acute stage come for treatment. Here, patients with heart diseases find good treatment and those who have had heart surgery or had heart attacks undergo rehabilitation. Sanatorium treatment is indicated for people with genitourinary problems and endocrine diseases such as diabetes, thyrotoxicosis, thyroid surgery.

Healing factors

The waters of mineral springs are saturated with boric acid, sodium and iodine. The mineral waters of the Bardeevsky fonts are healing, but have a different composition. In some, iron predominates, and they are classified as ferruginous, others contain bicarbonates in their composition. By sodium chloride is more pure mineral water resort springs.

Water in all sources is weakly or moderately mineralized. Out of 13 sources, eight use water for drinking and inhalation. The waters of other sources are used for bathtubs.

Due to the location of the resort of Bardeevskie font in the forest zone, patients with various diseases of the respiratory tract and lungs are treated here. Inhaling air saturated with ozone and coniferous aroma, they undergo this therapy while in nature in a forest zone.

As in Czech Karlovy Vary, in Slovakia there is a colonnade in this resort, in which the waters of eight mineral springs are presented, visitors can pick it up for drinking in circles.





Mineral water sources

  • Hercules is one of the effective sources in the resort. It is used in the treatment of gastric diseases and intestines, diabetes and all diseases associated with the respiratory tract. The content of iodine in water helps to eliminate mucus from the respiratory tract. The water in the source is cold and contains CO 2 Fe in a form that allows iron to be absorbed through the intestinal wall directly into the blood. This water has the largest number of minerals.
  • "Main" - the source water has an acidic taste and is used to treat diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, liver, disorders of the hematopoiesis system and blood diseases.
  • "Healing" is a source of high mineralization water, used for diseases of the gastrointestinal tract and excessive acidity. In spa treatment, he takes inhalations for diseases of the nasopharynx, trachea and lungs, including asthma. Patients with high blood pressure drink this water in the Bardeevsky fonts.
  • "Alzbetta" - mineral water with a low sodium content is used and is very effective in treating the liver.
  • "Klara" - this source of mineral water is rich in iron and trace elements of hydrogen sulfide. It is prescribed for inhalation and in the form of baths for patients with health problems in the respiratory tract and with rheumatic problems with bones. Water has a diuretic property.
  • "Napoleon" - the water of this source is saturated with boric acid, sodium and iron. Water is effective in the treatment of diseases of the liver and biliary tract, is mandatory in the appointment of patients with high blood pressure.
  • "Anna" - the water of this source is prescribed for all diseases of the endocrine system, as well as the respiratory tract.
  • "Colonnade" - the mineral water of the source is prescribed for the treatment of all forms of anemia and problems with acidity of the stomach.




Comprehensive spa treatment

Usually the list of prescribed procedures in the sanatorium "Bardeevskie font" depends on the patient's disease. Before appointing procedures, doctor's examinations and laboratory tests are mandatory. In the sanatorium there is a standard approach to the appointment of procedures. This is a drink of mineral water as prescribed by a doctor from a source corresponding to the patient's disease. The specialist determines how much and when to take water. The next part of the wellness treatments are food and balneological, as well as other water treatments.

Procedures

The main procedures, in addition to drinking, include all kinds of baths with mineral water, hydrotherapy, including Charcot's shower and underwater massage; paraffin and mud compresses, as well as lymphatic drainage and oxygen therapy. The doctors of the sanatorium are also prescribed auxiliary procedures. These are all kinds of swimming rehabilitation measures in the Bardeevsky fonts and baths, swimming pools and saunas.
