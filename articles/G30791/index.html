Explay Golf - all the details

Another new development of the famous Russian company - the model of the mid-range smartphone Explay Golf - differs from its predecessors in more powerful characteristics and a successful combination of performance and good design. This device will satisfy the most capricious user, leaving no chance of complaints or indignation. The classic operating system Android 4.2 Jelly Bean, which includes all updates, will make working with a smartphone easier and more convenient. The harmonious interface is clear and easy to use. Like other models released by this company, this smartphone is equipped with the highest level for phones in this category.

Explay Golf: communicator features





The performance of the device meets modern world market standards. This device instantly took its rightful place among the best solutions of this kind. And this did not happen by chance. The high-capacity battery makes it possible to work without recharging for a rather long period for a smartphone. The battery is quickly filled with energy using a standard USB-cord, which is also an essential advantage of this phone. Its high-performance dual-core processor and 512 MB RAM will give you great speed enjoyment. This is enough for the full use of both the basic and additional functions of the device.

Equipment





With very convenient dimensions (69: 136: 11 mm), the Explay Golf has a modest weight of 152 grams, which sometimes means a lot. The device has a built-in memory of 4 GB, which is enough to store installed applications, music or photos. However, if this is not enough, supplementing the memory with a flash card will not be a problem. Such additional functions as GPRS, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and others, which have already become familiar and necessary in the life of a modern person , provide the most comfortable use of Explay Golf. The main 5-megapixel camera is located on the rear panel and is equipped with autofocus and flash. With it, you can create good pictures for memory. In turn, the front optics will help to make a successful selfie. The sensitive, reliable and convenient sensor of the new generation, not inferior in quality to the equipment of more expensive models of similar devices, guarantees comfort in use.

Display





The large enough screen has amazing color reproduction. An extensive gamut of colors and shades in addition to a clear picture gives the image high realism. Such a phone will for a long time become a faithful companion for both a student and an active Internet user, and a professional who needs an apparatus for serious work.

Pleasant trifles

In addition to the main characteristics, the communicator has a number of undoubted advantages in terms of additional equipment. The built-in light sensor will simplify the work with the device in changing conditions, for example, on the road, when sunlight can be replaced by darkness. The internal memory of Explay Golf can be increased to an impressive 32 GB, which will undoubtedly please fans of large collections of music and pictures that are always at hand. The design is classic and rigorous, and this makes the smartphone suitable for people of different walks of life and all ages. Pleasant colors add appearance to the solidity and high cost. For lovers of exotic body panels are easily replaced by more extravagant ones.

The new Explay Golf phone reviews from users who managed to buy and test it, have already brought to the forefront of popular models that combine an affordable price and decent quality. The device has a proximity and light sensor , FM radio, support for a pair of SIM cards, Android 4.2 platform, a 1600 mAh battery, MediaTek processor, 4.5-inch screen, Mali-400 video accelerator.
