Children's health camp "Iskra" in Kabardinka

One of the cleanest and most comfortable resorts on the Black Sea coast is rightfully proclaimed the sunny village of Kabardinka. A good geographical location - on the coast of Tsemess Bay , surrounded by mountain archipelagos - creates favorable conditions for recreation and treatment.





The mild subtropical climate, the warm azure sea, the abundance of relic plants makes it popular among compatriots and foreigners. It should be noted that the resort is more focused on youth and children's recreation. For this reason, many recreation centers for children have been built here, including the Iskra camp. It is about him that we will speak.

Virtual tour

During the summer holidays, every child dreams of a memorable and active vacation at sea, surrounded by peers. Therefore, parents need to think through this point in advance in order to provide their child with an excellent weekend. The ideal place would be Iskra, a 35-year-old health camp located on the first line of the beach.





The vast territory is under specialized protection around the clock. All buildings are buried in picturesque green spaces. Juniper trees give this place a special charm, purifying the air and exuding a delicious aroma throughout the district. The atmosphere of serenity and fun is everywhere, setting the guys on a good rest after a busy school year.

The complex accepts students from 7 to 15 years. All staff has specialized knowledge and vast experience for working with children of different ages. For young guests, a balanced diet, comfortable accommodation, a number of recreational activities, including sightseeing trips, are provided.

Terms of Accommodation





Children's camp "Spark" is represented by two-story buildings. They have rooms for 3-6 people. The base can accommodate up to 360 children at a time. Residential buildings are annually renovated. The rooms have bunk beds, bedside tables for each child and a wardrobe. On the floors there are toilets and sinks for hygiene.

Catering

On the ennobled territory, a dining room with 400 seats was built. The guys provide food five times a day. The menu is carefully designed and tested. Over the entire existence of the base of poisoning was not recorded. Children's camp "Spark" takes care of their wards.





The diet is enriched with a variety of dishes: meat, fish, vegetables. On the tables are always seasonal fruits, dairy products, cereals, natural juices, mineral water. Young guests are treated to fresh pastry, ice cream.

Coastline

Within walking distance from the housing stock - about 100 meters - concentrated pebble-sandy coast belonging to the complex. Children staying at the Iskra camp visit the beach under the supervision of a counselor and medical worker. A rescue team is on duty on the coast, monitoring the safety of the children.





It is important that the beach area is fenced and closed from prying eyes. The shore is regularly cleaned so that it is convenient for young guests to sunbathe and stroll along the beach. The area is landscaped with aerarias, changing cabins and awnings.

Leisure part

People come to the Iskra health camp for one purpose - to spend holidays with health benefits, as well as chat with peers, swim in the Black Sea and find like-minded people. The teachers of the complex try to interest their wards: they organize sports competitions for them, intellectual competitions, informative quizzes. The emphasis is on initiative and independence.

It is important that each child feels necessary, does not hesitate to show his talents, participate in various events. An exciting rich program allows you to rally the team, instill a team spirit. In addition to active classes and outdoor games, films are scrolled at the base, and discos are held on the dance floor.





In addition, a variety of circles are open on the territory: art studio, needlework, karaoke, a hall for creativity. Children can choose a lesson according to their interests and preferences. The main advantage of the recreation center is its own stable. The children gaze with enthusiasm at the animals, look after them and take horseback riding. As you know, such communication favorably affects the psycho-emotional state of a person. There is an extensive library at the disposal of the children. Organized hiking, hiking trails in the city. And this is not the whole list of services provided.

Health Camp "Spark": reviews

With full enthusiasm and rapture, the young guests say about the resort of Kabardinka. The Iskra camp left fond memories of relaxation. Almost all the guys want to come back here again. A well-planned program captivates all children, without exception.

The guys liked the friendly attitude of counselors and teachers. Many learned to work in a team, made new friends. Good words were said about nutrition: struck by the variety of dishes. Spent vacations at the Iskra recreation center is a real journey where you restore strength, get rid of complexes and return full of energy.
