Choose material

Buying yarn in order to knit toys with knitting needles or crochet is now not a problem at all. So much they sell it even in small sealers of goods for needlework, that you can just drown in it. But any yarn is not suitable for knitting toys. From the whole variety of skeins, a certain type of yarn should be chosen. And since there are many manufacturers, and the products are often very similar, we should only talk about the composition and length of threads in a skein of a certain weight.

Rather tight and stiff toys are made from yarn, which contains 55% cotton and 45% polyacryl, and in a 50-gram skein there will be 160 meters of thread. A knitted teddy bear, with knitting needles, for example, will keep its shape well, since the fabric of such threads does not twist.

Another suitable yarn for knitting toys with needles is 100% mercerized cotton. What does mercerized mean? The cotton processed in a special way, the thread of which after that acquired a light shine, became stronger and more resistant to shrinkage, softer and more silky in the product. For example, knitted bears, knitting or crocheting, patterns for which are so popular, from mercerized cotton will be durable, reliable, beautiful.

Textured yarn should be chosen only when the experience of knitting toys is already sufficient so as not to get tangled in loops in thread fluffy.





Tools and accessories for knitting toys

In addition to yarn, knitting toys need knitting needles or a hook, stuffing material, accessories for eyes, noses, mouths, as well as hair, if necessary, and clothing. Knitting needles and a hook for toys should be chosen a little less than with ordinary knitting. This is done so that the canvas of a knitted toy, knitting or crocheting - does not matter, is dense enough to hold its shape. As eyes and noses, you can use beads or buttons, as well as special parts that are either glued or fastened with special clips in place.
