What is a draft in collectible card games?

Draft and Constructed. What is the difference?

The general principle of the draft tournament

In most games, draft tournaments are held in approximately the same pattern. First of all, we note that such a tournament consists of two stages: collecting the deck and the game part itself. In order to start collecting cards in a deck, players are usually placed at the same table in the amount of 6-8 people. Each of them has acquired packs with cards. The number of these packs and their composition depend directly on the game. For example, in the most popular card game Magic: the Gathering, each player needs three packs of cards of 15 pieces each. After seating, players open one pack, select the best card from it, and transfer the rest in a circle to a neighbor. Accordingly, then you have to choose a card from what the neighbor has transmitted, and so on until all the cards are taken apart.



After all the packs are opened and disassembled, the draft in card games does not end. It is necessary to collect decks from the resulting set of cards in order to play the deck in the most efficient way.
