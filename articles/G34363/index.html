How to find out the current account of a Sberbank card? Where can I see the bank account of Sberbank?





To perform some necessary operations using cards, including using a Sberbank credit card, you will need to find out your personal bank account. It may be needed when it becomes necessary to conduct some payment transactions, for example, send money to someone or get money into your own account from another person. At the same time, the person who will transfer the money will not be able to do this without knowing your current account

If you do not know where you can find out about the card account - do not despair! There are several ways to help you get all the information you need and complete the necessary operation. Since the account number is a confidential type of data that can only be accessed by the card holder, it is simply impossible to find it out without any necessary details. Despite this, it is not difficult to find out the settlement code if you have certain data. In addition, this procedure can be performed in completely different ways, depending on the situation and the opportunities that you have at the moment. How to find out the current account of the Sberbank card, we will consider below.

Envelope with a card

A bank card is issued to Sberbank customers in a special envelope on which there is an official company logo. Having printed it, you can find out the pin code. Your bank account of Sberbank is indicated on this envelope. In the event that the envelope is preserved and it is possible to take it, then finding the necessary numbers is not difficult.

The invoice itself will be written on the front side of the envelope, namely in the column located just below the field where the surname and name of the owner are indicated. If you count, then this will be the third column above. Your current account consists of 20 digits.





Hot line

If the envelope has been lost or there is no way to reach it, but you have some other details, you can call the head office of Sberbank by dialing 8 800 555 55 50. Calls to Russia to this number are absolutely free even from a mobile phone, and you can call around the clock. Dial this number and they will tell you how to find out the current account of the Sberbank card.

This happens as follows: you will be greeted by a robot - listen to all the information and act according to the instructions. Soon you will be served by the freed Sberbank operator, who should be informed about what interests you and what problem has arisen. The operator will ask you to provide some information confirming that the card really belongs to you: you will need to give the operator the name of the person who owns the card, its number and the secret word, which is indicated when you fill out the necessary application for the card.

The secret word should be pronounced very clearly, preferably by letter, in order to avoid misunderstandings and errors. If you name everything correctly, the system will certainly confirm your data, and the operator will inform you of the card's current account. If you provided any incorrect information or forgot the secret word, the operator will refuse to provide assistance and will not provide the cherished numbers. In this case, the employee acts absolutely lawfully, since the main rule of any bank is to maintain confidentiality.
