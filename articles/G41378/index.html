In which smartphone model is the camera better?

Considering these models, one can notice the relative similarity in this parameter. The camera for the two models of smartphones is the same - thirteen megapixels with phase autofocus. The smartphone of the fourth version has focus with f / 2.3 indicators, and the new X-smartphone has f / 2.0. It should be noted that the lower this indicator, the better the shooting.



If the camera is the fundamental function of the phone for you, you very often plan to take pictures and require good image quality, then you need to focus on gadgets with indicators from 6 to 8 megapixels and, of course, higher. A special place today is occupied by the front camera. It should have indicators higher than 4 megapixels, just such an option will be quite suitable for making video calls in Skype and good pictures from the front camera. A phone with a good camera should also be equipped with several photo modes, autofocus and a good flash.

Conclusions on the characteristics of the camera

Thus, it is safe to say that with shooting in these devices, everything is fine, especially in daylight.
