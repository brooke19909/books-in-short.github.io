Principle of operation

What is so remarkable about air grills? Reviews of numerous users claim that this device is an excellent assistant in the kitchen. With it, you can cook tasty and wholesome food, saving you time.

This device consists of a bowl made of glass, and a cover into which a fan and a heater are mounted. In its arrangement, the air grill is similar to an oven in which convection is used. The miracle device is controlled using the panel located on the lid. The hostess can easily choose the mode of air flow and cooking temperature.



Inside the bowl is placed any cookware of a heat-resistant type, made of ceramic, metal. Glass containers are also suitable. In this dish, cooking is done thanks to the heating carried out by vortices of hot air. In other words, the air grill works according to the principle available in the Russian stove. Cooking in it is not done over an open fire, but with the help of heated air.

Advantages

What are good air grills? User reviews note that dishes prepared in these devices have a special taste. In addition, unlike food from the microwave, they are very useful.

What are the main differences between these devices? Almost everyone knows the fact that the microwave during its operation emits waves that cannot be called beneficial to human health. Another thing is air grill. In it, cooking occurs naturally. The fan drives hot air in different directions along the glass bowl of the device. This allows products to cook on all sides evenly.

Can air grills save time? User reviews provide an affirmative answer to this question. It is enough to load products into the device and set the timer on the lid. After that, you can go do any business. The device will notify you about the end of the cooking process with a pleasant signal.

What dishes can be cooked in the air grill?

The device is capable of much. It stews and soars, smokes, prepares yoghurts and pastries, and also sterilizes jars. This miracle machine is a multifunctional device. At the same time, she cooks without fat and oil. Products cooked in an air grill are distinguished by a delicate crust. Inside, they remain quite juicy.

What else can air grills do? User reviews talk about their ability to cook fish and meat dishes, as well as poultry, in a short period of time. There is no need to add oil to them. Finished products are soft and juicy.



How to choose such a practical and versatile device? For this, first of all, you will need to decide on the necessary parameters.

Type of control

Manufacturers offer two types of air grills. The first of them are equipped with a mechanical type of control, and the second - electronic.

First type air grills have a pleasant and simple functionality. In them, the parameters of the cooking time and the required temperature are set manually. The fan in such devices operates in the same mode. It is worth saying that models with a mechanical type of control are the cheapest.



Electronic grills are equipped with touch buttons and a display. The hostess needs only to choose the necessary program. It can be defrosting or baking, grilling or heating, roasting or a pan. After that, the machine will independently set the time necessary for cooking the products and the desired temperature.

Heating element

What other parameter is important to evaluate when compared grills? Expert reviews advise you to pay attention to the heating element of the device. In modern models of universal kitchen assistants, they can be of two types. The first one is metal. The second is glass-ceramic.

In most models, a heating element made of metal is used to heat the air. It is safe and reliable, and also very durable. However, this heater has some disadvantages. These include the slow heating of this element. Despite this, experts recommend choosing an aerogrill with a metal heating element. Such a device when used in the kitchen will be as safe and effective as possible. Manufacturers establish a warranty period of 10 years for metal heating elements.

The second type of heating element is glass-ceramic, or halogen. Often it is found in the latest models of air grills. The main advantage of this heater is its quick heating, as well as resistance to oxidation and corrosion. The disadvantage of the halogen element lies in its short service life, as well as in high energy consumption. On such a heater, manufacturers give only a two-year warranty.

Power

What other parameter of the device should have an important influence on the choice of model? Of course, this is power. The minimum value of this parameter can be in the range from 0.8 to 1.7 kW.

Only positive receive all airgripper reviews. Which is better to choose? Experts argue that as a home kitchen assistant, a model whose power is average is perfect.

Flask volume

According to this parameter, all air grills are divided into mini (8-12 l), medium (10-15 l), and also large (15-18 l). In this case, choose a device based on personal needs. A small family will have enough air grill with a small flask.



A multifunction device has its own distinctive feature. On top of the flask, an expansion ring can be installed in it, which is tightly covered with a lid. This element allows you to significantly increase the volume of the container, which is useful for the preparation of products having large sizes (for example, birds).

Cap type

In modern models of air grills, various elements are used. This also applies to covers. They can be of two types - removable or on brackets. Models with covers of the first type are, according to user reviews, more simple and compact. If your kitchen has a small area, then it is better to purchase such air grills.

Devices with a cover mounted on brackets are in a higher price category. In addition, the process of their operation is quite complicated. Such a lid significantly increases the overall dimensions of the device, which will require additional space in the kitchen. It should be remembered that the brackets can quickly fail, as they bear a heavy load during operation of the device. So if you need a reliable and compact aerogrill, experts recommend purchasing a model with a removable cover.

Functional

When choosing a model, it is advisable to take an interest in the convection speed of the device. For different cooking programs, this indicator varies. For heating and baking, the fan speed should be low, for the second and first courses - medium, and for frying and baking - high.

An important feature in the air grill is that it allows you to set a specific temperature. It varies depending on the type of product. The operating temperature of the device ranges from sixty to two hundred and sixty degrees.

The convenience of using the device lies in the self-cleaning function. It is equipped with some modern models. At the end of the process, you need to put in the aerogrill a cup in which there is water with detergent, and turn on self-cleaning. After that, it is enough to wipe the entire interior of the device with a cloth.

User-friendly timer function that allows you to independently set the time for cooking various dishes, as well as delaying the start and heating.
