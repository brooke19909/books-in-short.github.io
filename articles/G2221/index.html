A good vacation spot is the water park in Voronezh

Voronezh is not a very large (although regional) city. It is located on the banks of the river and has stunning promenades and beaches. For residents there are many places to relax and for every taste. But the most popular are, of course, water parks. Water procedures and attractions are very loved by the locals, large and small.

"Chip" - a water park in Voronezh: photos and description

Water parks are located on the left bank of the reservoir. One of them is in the territory of the Dolphin park, which was once the most favorite vacation spot of the city residents.



This is the territory of centuries-old pines, and in the old days there were many entertaining attractions both on water and on land. Due to the frequent change of owners, the park became desolate. And only recently began to revive. In its northern part an entertainment complex has opened, which includes a water park.

Waterpark in Voronezh "Chip" is designed for 500 guests at a time. It has slides for adults and children. In the children's area there is a pirate ship in a 60 cm deep pool. Safe slides, spray guns and fountains are also thought out in it. Adult rides of different heights and steepness. Especially guests of the park distinguish sheer "roller coaster-guns" that cause a terrible surge of adrenaline.

The adult pool is divided into two halves. One offers rocking, and the other offers a relaxing whirlpool.



The children's area from the adult was separated by sunbeds. In the water center there is an inexpensive restaurant. A health certificate is not required upon entry.

Ticket price

How much does it cost to visit this water park in Voronezh? A standard ticket involves three hours of rest. Children's tickets from 420 to 670 rubles, an adult can be purchased no more than 850 rubles.

Center "Parnas"

What else is there a water park in Voronezh? For example, such a place is in the entertainment center "Parnassus", which is located indoors. Therefore, it receives guests year-round.



It has two slides and a rather large swimming pool with a variety of fountains, hydromassage and geysers. The water park has modern water purification and air conditioning systems. The water temperature is maintained comfortable. If you are fed up with water activities, visitors will be offered a steam room and comfortable sofas.

Services

If you plan to come to Voronezh, visit the Parnas water park too. After all, this is the favorite vacation spot of citizens. The organizers have provided for everything here. Water activities are located in the premises of a large entertainment complex, which makes it possible to organize diverse entertainment events. Within walking distance from the water park "Parnas" are a nightclub, billiard room, cafe and bowling alley.

The following services are offered at this aquacenter:

- descents from water slides;

- swimming in the pool;

- relaxation in the sauna;

- water aerobics session;

- hydromassage;

- help of a swimming instructor.

The pool at Parnassus is an unforgettable pleasure. It is organized in such a way that for both children and adults there is a complete set of pleasures, and there is a place to feel well.

Water filtration in the pool is carried out by a special certified station. The temperature is maintained comfortable. And in specially designated "backwaters" hydromassages and a geyser are installed. They relax after a hard working week and charge with positive emotions.

You can complete the relaxation course in one of the saunas. Up to eight people can enjoy a healing pair right away.

The water park invites fans of productive leisure on water slides. There are two of them. The amazing experience of descending from a ten-meter height is not forgotten for a very long time.

In addition to entertainment, the water park presents a course of wellness and training programs. The Parnassus guests love water aerobics classes for children and adults. There are swimming courses for children, and adults are offered therapeutic swimming classes, which are famous for their healing effect throughout Voronezh. Classes are taught by qualified instructors.



It is especially nice to come to the Parnas water park in the winter cold. Warm water, a cozy atmosphere - a piece of hot summer as a gift!

In the summer, the water park in Voronezh is invited to stunning parties with discos, karaoke and dance shows. A charge of positive emotions is provided.

Waterpark (Voronezh): prices

Children can visit the pool for no more than 110 rubles, adults - 220 rubles. By purchasing a subscription, you can do water aerobics for 1,680 rubles (8 lessons). The services of a swimming instructor for children for eight lessons will cost 1,440 rubles, for adults for 10 lessons they will cost 6,480 rubles. A health certificate is not required.

Stocks

Water parks in Voronezh often offer various promotions and discounts. In particular, children under 6 are allowed to attend these facilities for free. If the guest has a birthday, the organizers give the second hour of stay in the water center (upon presentation of the document). Pensioners are offered a ticket with a discount of up to 30%. Children's groups of more than ten people can buy tickets for 90 rubles on weekdays, and only 120 rubles for weekends. Adult companies also have a discount - 200 rubles per person ticket.
