Bravo Garden 4 * (Hammamet, Tunisia): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Hammamet is a very conservative and fairly calm resort, which is famous all over the world for thalassotherapy centers and clean sandy beaches. A quiet holiday in these places successfully combines with a vibrant nightlife - there is a huge variety of restaurants, bars and clubs. Everyone will find something to their liking - it can be a beach holiday, anti-aging treatments or unbridled fun in the nightlife of Hammamet. But for whatever purpose a tourist goes here, he needs to solve the issue related to temporary residence. Cheap hotels of Tunisia are especially popular . However, the low price does not mean that the hotels are of poor quality. Quite the contrary. And one of the most striking examples is the Bravo Garden 4 hotel complex.





Getting to know the hotel

It was built 11 years ago, in 2003, and the last restoration took place in 2012. Bravo Garden 4 is located in the resort of Yasmin, near the Medina and the casino. In addition, a spacious and clean beach is located very close to the hotel, which is important, especially for those tourists who come to enjoy the sea. There are three airports near the resort. They are located in different cities. 39 kilometers to Enfida Airport, 71 to Tunisia and 101 to the one in Monastir. So, we can say that the location at the complex is quite favorable, which cannot but please potential tourists, especially those who come here to enjoy the beaches.





general description

Before you begin to consider in detail all the advantages that the Hotel Bravo Garden 4 has, it is necessary to consider in general terms what this complex is. This is a hotel, on a well-groomed spacious territory of which there are indoor and outdoor pools. By the way, free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.
