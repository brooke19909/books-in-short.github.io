Hotel "Tourist", Kiev: address, phone, photo, reviews

Kiev is considered the seventh largest city in Europe. It is the capital and largest settlement of Ukraine. Kiev is located in the northern part of the country. The population is over 2.5 million people. This metropolis is rich in attractions and green spaces.

For travelers, there are many different places to stay during your stay in the city. The budget choice of many visitors for leisure or work is the "Tourist" hotel (Kiev). The "Left Bank" metro station is one hundred meters from the hotel. You can get to almost anywhere in the metropolis by metro.





How to get there

Hotel "Tourist" (Kiev) has the following address: Raisa Okipnoy street, house 2. You can get there:

  • From Zhulyany airport - by public transport (minibuses No. 368, 805), then - by metro to the "Levoberezhnaya" stop.
  • From Boryspil Airport - by municipal bus No. 16 or 2 to the Leningradskaya stop, then - by minibuses No. 316, 317, 318 to the metro, then - to the Levoberezhnaya station. There are also direct buses to the subway (white-purple with the Sky Bus logo). Konechnaya - Yuzhny railway station, from there - by metro.
  • From the railway complex, including Central, Southern and Suburban railway stations, - by metro to "Left Bank".
  • From the bus stations to the nearby metro station: Centralnaya - 318 meters, Yuzhnaya - 320 meters, Svyatoshino - 80 meters, Podil - 360 meters, Darnitsa - about 500 meters, Polesye - by bus No. 537 (station "Nivki"), "Summer" - on shuttle buses No. 727, 749, 750, 761, 762, 765, 770 to the station m. "Nivki", then - by metro to the station. "Left Bank".

The fastest and most comfortable ways to get into the "Tourist" are a taxi or a rented car. But they are much more expensive than the rest.

You can also take advantage of the hotel’s additional service and order a transfer by calling the reception desk administrator.

Kiev, hotel "Tourist": photo and description of rooms

"Tourist" began to receive guests since 1987. Then it was one of the tallest buildings in the city - 27 floors. The rooms located closer to the roof offer a magical view of the surroundings.

Hotel "Tourist" (Kiev) three-star. It offers guests - 378 rooms of four types:

  • Suite - three-room, total area - 60 m 2 , two toilet rooms (with bath), two double beds, sofa, armchairs, coffee table, side tables, wardrobes;
  • junior suite - two-room, 40 m 2 , two bathrooms, double bed, sofa, armchairs, table, bedside tables, wardrobe;
  • standard (single and double) - 18-20 m 2 , two (one) single or double bed, bedside tables, chairs, table, toilet room (with shower);
  • economy (single and double) - 20 m 2 , double or one (two) single beds, bathroom (with shower), bedside tables, table, chairs.

For an additional fee, you can use the complex's parking lot (93 parking spaces), ironing room, laundry, send a fax, make copies on a photocopier.

Free of charge, each guest can use the refrigerator, TV, wireless Internet, and in the suites and junior suites there is also air conditioning, a hairdryer, an electric kettle, a bathrobe, slippers.





Terms of Accommodation

Estimated hours:

  • Check-in - 14:00;
  • Departure - 12:00.

Required documentation: passport, birth certificate for a child, voucher and confirmation of prepayment (when booking online).

In the hotel you can stay with children of any age. Without extra bed, accommodation for people under twelve years old is free. Extra beds are available in standard and junior suites (payable separately).

Nutrition

Price per day includes a complimentary buffet breakfast . The hotel has four dining rooms. Two of them - banquets - measuring 30 and 36 m 2 can accommodate 25-35 guests. Other premises with an area of ​​400 and 450 m2 are designed for 150-400 people.

On the ground floor of the hotel complex there is a restaurant-bistro "Francisco". The menu includes a wide selection of European dishes. For children a separate set of dishes is provided. There is a TV, a bar, several tables and a counter where you can buy ready-made food.





Places for rest

Hotel "Tourist" (Kiev) has its own gym, beauty and massage parlor, sauna, strip bar, dental office. The building has a pharmacy kiosk, railway and ticket offices, a left-luggage office, a currency exchange kiosk.

By visiting the beauty studio "8888", you can tidy up your appearance after a tiring road. The range of services includes hair care, face (including hardware cosmetology), nails.

Relax salon specialists offer different types of procedures (vacuum, Thai, Tibetan, Alpine, Creole and general health massage).

A sauna with the same name operates in the hotel around the clock. Three comfortable rooms with a pool are furnished with modern furniture. Here you can order any dishes from the restaurants of the complex.

Business center

The hotel has four conference rooms with an area of ​​50, 90, 130, 340 m 2 . For business events, special equipment is offered (projectors, microphones, screens, flipcharts). The premises can accommodate 40 to 296 people. There is the possibility of organizing receptions, lunch (dinner), coffee breaks.

You can choose a hall in the form of a classroom with a round, T-shaped, U-shaped or rectangular table, depending on the purpose of the event. Conferences, exhibitions, negotiations, trainings, presentations are held here.





Attractions nearby

Kiev is one of the most greened cities in the world. It is rich in special places that are interesting for tourists to visit. The metropolis has many museums (27), theaters (25), architectural monuments, cinemas, fountains, two botanical gardens, a large planetarium. Due to its favorable location, the Tourist Hotel (Kiev) can significantly save the time that travelers spend on their way to the sights. Some of them are located in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.

Nearby is the largest Ukrainian International Exhibition Center. Before him 5 minutes on foot.

The subway electric train in 2-3 minutes (just one stop) will deliver those who wish to the huge hydropark. On its territory there is a small museum - "Kiev in miniature."





Hotel guests will not spend much time on the road to the ancient shrine of Orthodox Christians - the Kiev Pechersk Lavra (two or three metro stops from the hotel).

The journey from "Tourist" to the Patriarchal Center of the Ukrainian Greek Catholic Church takes about 10-15 minutes (on foot).

Hotel "Tourist" (Kiev): visitor reviews

Many travelers staying in Kiev "Tourist", note its very convenient location - in close proximity to the metro station. Right at the entrance to the hotel there is a market, a grocery store, a branch of Sberbank.

The presence of a foreign exchange office, taxi call service, souvenir kiosk, ticket office for selling tickets for trains and planes creates comfortable conditions for residents. According to hotel guests, exchange rates here are some of the best in the city.

The decoration of the rooms seemed to most visitors quite good, fully consistent with the cost. A set of necessary furniture, towels, water supply, disposable toiletries (hats and shower gels, shampoos) are present in rooms of all categories.

Almost all the guests speak positively about the variety and quality of the buffet dishes. The food prepared in the bistro on the 1st floor and in the hotel restaurants is also tasty and varied. The only drawback, some considered the presence of tobacco smoke in the evening and at night near the bars.

The staff is called polite, unobtrusive and responsive. Cleaning of the premises is carried out in a timely manner and with high quality.

Tourists who visited the hotel in 2013-2015 notice that the building needs redecorating and replacing equipment (stained carpet, irons with dirty soles, plumbing with a yellow coating).





Hotel "Tourist" (Kiev) gives its guests the opportunity to enjoy beautiful views of the metropolis every day. All guests living on the upper floors confirm this.
