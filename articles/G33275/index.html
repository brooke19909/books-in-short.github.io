Rest in Dagomys: hotels, beaches, sea

Dagomys is a resort microdistrict located on the territory of Greater Sochi. It stretches along the coast of the Black Sea. The visiting card of rest in Dagomys is magnificent natural landscapes and modern comfortable hotels.

General information

In summer, the resort population increases several times. Its guests highly appreciate the mild climate of the village, which is provided by moist and cool air masses coming from the snow-capped peaks of the Caucasus Mountains. A distinctive feature of the microdistrict is compactness. With Central Sochi it is connected by a railway line and wide highways.

Developed infrastructure explains the high popularity of the resort. Holidays in Dagomys are chosen by families with children, newlyweds, couples and noisy companies. The local air is saturated with minerals that have a beneficial effect on the respiratory system of the body. The beaches here are clean and spacious. The order on them is provided by workers of nearby sanatoriums and health resorts.

Entertainment

Beach holidays in Dagomys are possible from mid-May to the end of September. During this period, the water temperature reaches comfortable bathing values. The entrance to the sea is gentle. Depth increases gradually. The resort offers concert halls, restaurants of Asian, European and American cuisine, bars and discos.

Fans of outdoor activities in Dagomys will find a lot of opportunities for playing tennis, volleyball and basketball. Rental equipment is available for diving, bicycles, yachts and boats. Hiking trails begin in the village, leading to waterfalls, boxwood groves, tea plantations, mountains and gorges.

Beaches

The coastal strip of the resort is strewn with small pebbles, which is interspersed with islands of dark sand. The bottom of the sea is flat, without stones and large boulders. There are no breaks. The beach of the resort complex is popular with guests of Dagomys hotels. Entrance to it is paid. At the disposal of vacationers are plastic sunbeds with adjustable backs, colorful umbrellas and awnings.

Showers function right on the beach of the Dagomys recreation center. Equipped with changing areas, there are toilet cubicles. There are bars and eateries. Next to it is another beach. He is a municipal. Entrance to it is free for all guests of Dagomys hotels.

Wild beach is preferred by followers of a secluded pastime. It is closed from city streets by a high railway embankment and a dam, which was built to strengthen the coastal zone. The length of the beach is almost five hundred meters.

The width of the beach varies. At high tide, it barely reaches 5 meters. Low tide doubles it. The main attraction is the vines, which encircle the slope, giving the beach an authentic look. Be careful, this place has long been chosen by nudists who appear here with enviable regularity.

sights

The resort's calling card is the magnificent Royal Park. It was defeated at the end of the 19th century. For several decades, served as the southern estate of Nicholas II. The existing tea factory is the second place that is recommended for visiting. It was founded during the USSR and was an integral part of the state farm. After perestroika, it passed into private hands. Today it is actively developing, offering new products.

On the embankment stands a monument to Captain Hook, who has become a symbol of the city. Vacationers are taken for a walk in the area, which is a country house grouped on the coast. They stand on stilts, and boats run between them. Teenagers enjoy visiting a museum dedicated to rare automobile technology. Finding it is easy. It is located at the busy intersection of Leningradskaya and Batumi Highways.

Transport accessibility

In order to quickly and comfortably get on holiday to the private sector of Dagomys, it is recommended to arrange a transfer with the host party in advance. Those who come on their own use the railway platform. Some long-distance passenger trains and commuter trains make stops on it.

In Dagomys, shuttle buses and buses run to the settlements of Greater Sochi. An updated motorway runs through the resort. Most guest houses and hotels have their own parking spaces for cars vacationers. Usually parking is free, but there are exceptions.
