Business Center "Benoit" - office building of St. Petersburg

The Benois business center project was prepared by the German architecture and construction bureau NPS TCHOBAN VOSS. This is an eight-story building of class A, its area is 28,400 square meters. Built on the site of the previously standing Rossiya plant. Located in the business district "Polyustrovo". The first phase was commissioned in 2007, and the second in 2008. The office center was recognized as the ‚ÄúHouse of the Year" in St. Petersburg in 2009. Managed by the business center of UK "Theorem". The building attracts attention due to the stained-glass windows, which depict the characters of fairy tales on a black background.

Facade of the Benois business center

The facade of the complex is decorated with ornaments, which was created according to the sketches of the Russian painter Alexander Nikolaevich Benois, and is made of panoramic glass. Color images of unusual costumed figures were applied to the glass panels by digital printing . Not far from the complex is the artist's summer house. Figures by A.N. Benois were prepared for the performances of Diaghilev seasons. At night, they are illuminated by internal lighting, which creates a fabulous impression. This facade is facing Piskarevsky Avenue. Panoramic windows maximize access to sunlight in office premises. And the facade, which "looks" into the courtyard, is lined with frosted glass panels. The Benois business center has not only the original architecture, but also transport accessibility, a serious infrastructure, modern technical equipment and comfortable offices.





Infrastructure and basic characteristics of the complex

The infrastructure of the business center is focused on the needs of tenants. There is a large selection of office space from 30 square meters that can be rented. Offices with floor coverings (tiles or carpet), suspended ceilings, ventilation, lighting and so on. The formation of office blocks is possible. On the ground floor of the office center there is a cafe, ATM, coffee machine, restaurant, payment terminal. The building has a conference room where business meetings are held. So, the Benois Business Center is equipped with:

  • security system;
  • round-the-clock access to office premises;
  • advanced air conditioning system;
  • digital telephony;
  • autonomous water supply;
  • district heating;
  • Reception
  • panoramic elevators (one freight and four passenger);
  • unlimited internet;
  • fire alarm:
  • satellite tv;
  • cleaning;
  • parking (paid is designed for 430 places, and free for 112 places).

Benoit Business Center: How to Get There

Transport accessibility and its location are also noteworthy. You will be taken to Benoit by free buses. They begin their journey from the metro station "Lenin Square".





Benoit Business Center: Address

Located in the city of St. Petersburg, Kalininsky district. It is worth noting that the Benois Business Center is very favorably located at the intersection of the four main streets of the district: Revolution Highway, Polyustrovsky and Piskarevsky Avenues, Sverdlovskaya Embankment. Exact address: Sverdlovskaya embankment, house number - 38. Telephone for communication: 8 (812) 333-04-44. Opening hours of the business center on weekdays from 09:30 to 18:00.

The Benois business center is not only an office building, but also a picture building. If you are in the Northern capital, be sure to visit it.
