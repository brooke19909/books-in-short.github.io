Cretan Village 4 * (Greece, Crete): hotel description, photos and reviews of tourists

Cretan Village is a 4-star privately owned hotel located in Greece. Every person who has been here once remembers their vacation for a long time. For many reasons, this place is ideal for relaxation. All of them should be told in as much detail as possible.





Location

To begin with, it is worth noting that in Greece there are two hotels with the name Cretan Village 4 *. So, now we will talk about that complex, which is located in a resort village called Amudara. And he, in turn, is located on Crete, which is the largest Greek island in the Mediterranean. And no wonder why travelers like it so much. After all, tourism infrastructure is really well developed here - better than on many other islands.

Cretan Village 4 * was opened in 1977. One of the key features of this complex is that it is located opposite a sandy beach. The hotel building blends harmoniously with the overall landscape. And the eye is pleased with the lush green vegetation surrounding the complex.

In general, the hotel is in a very good location. 50 meters away from the bus stop, for example. From there, by the way, transport runs directly to the center of Agios Nikolaos. True, the airport is far away - about 70 kilometers. Although this can be considered a plus. In any case, the take-off and landing planes will not interfere with the rest.

People come here who are interested in sightseeing, family, beach or relaxing holiday. Most often, adults, families with children, older couples or groups stay at the hotel. People who are interested in an experience-rich vacation and various modern entertainment make the choice for more populated resorts. And this is right, because from vacation you need to get everything that a tourist wants. Room Types

Now - a few words about what the hotel complex Hotel Cretan Village 4 * offers . They fall into three categories. The first is a one-room apartment. Their maximum capacity is four people. What is in the apartment?

The rooms of Cretan Village 4 * (Crete) have everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay of guests. To begin with, it is worth noting that there are non-smoking apartments with increased soundproofing.

Inside there are mirrors, heating, a dressing table, bedside tables, chairs with a desk, a wardrobe. Windows - double-glazed windows that open both fully and in ventilation mode. Beds and folding sofas are offered as bedroom furniture. Also, the rooms have access to a private balcony or a furnished terrace.

All apartments have a kitchen equipped with all necessary appliances. Depending on the category of rooms there can be either a gas stove or a hob. A refrigerator, a kettle, kitchen utensils - all this is also inside.

In addition to all the listed rooms, they have a plasma TV, alarm clock, air conditioning, radio, sockets (220/240 volts), telephone, iron and ironing board. Some apartments even have a fireplace.

The bathroom is also equipped as it should - a bath or shower, cosmetic mirror, washbasin and toilet. Hygiene products, a hairdryer and towels are also provided.
