Caves near Moscow Syany

Not all Muscovites know that some twelve kilometers from their city there is a large cave system. This fact may seem unexpected, but it has a logical explanation. These caves - Syany - were made by human hands and for a reason. In Syan, limestone rock was mined for the construction of white-stone Moscow.





A bit of history

These moves have the largest length of moves in the Moscow region. And in Russia they occupy the 5th line. The total length of the underground passages is 19 km, and these are only known and verified. There is a possibility that the Syany caves near Moscow may turn out to be longer. At the same time, their position in the ranking of caves will rise.

Large caves in the suburbs began to be created in the XIII century. The fastest increase in underground passages was in the 15th century, when limestone construction was actively developing. Peasants mined it in adits only in the winter. There are two reasons for this:

  1. In other seasons, peasant people worked on the land, growing bread and raising livestock.
  2. In winter, the risk of collapse of passages was reduced. In the past, people did not yet have sufficient experience and means to ensure security and support arches. In winter, the ground froze from severe frosts, and moving areas froze.

Stones were mined in these adits until the 20th century. Work underground stopped completely in 1917. In the thirties of the last century, a number of stones were extracted from the caves in order to strengthen the runway at Domodedovo Airport. From this time, limestone rocks were no longer taken out of Xi'an. During the Great Patriotic War, a hospital for wounded soldiers was set up in a dungeon.





14 years of silence

Already in the 1960s, cavers became interested in these caves. And there were a lot of them appearing here. The caves began to live a second life. A few years later, the government decided to wipe all entrances and exits for security reasons. The dungeons were empty for 14 years, after which the activists opened one of the exits. In 2007, it was restored to its normal form, concrete rings were omitted and a staircase was made. This did not diminish extremity.

Syana Caves are not considered an ennobled tourist destination. Visitors are required to follow the recommendations for behavior in the caves, they regularly conduct excursions, and some even celebrate holidays. For example, to celebrate the arrival of the New Year in the dungeon is not so commonplace.





Underground life

Making fires is strictly forbidden by the established rules of behavior, because other cavers may suffer from the released carbon monoxide. Some visitors are in the cave for more than one day and are forced to cook and boil water on gas and alcohol. These features of being underground give a certain zest to such trips. It may surprise you, but there are people who may not go to the surface for some days at all. You can lie down and sleep in special extensions of the moves. Underground sometimes performances of artists and dances.

Many tourists are attracted by the cave of Syana. How to get there, they know, unfortunately, not all lovers of extreme relaxation.

Dungeon Conditions

The conditions in the cave do not change all year round. Stably from 7 to 10 degrees above zero, and humidity - about 80 percent. If you have warm and comfortable clothes, then you will not experience unpleasant sensations. This is if you do not have claustrophobia. Ceiling heights can range from 0.3 to 2.5 meters. The width of the passage is about 1.5 meters.

All passages studied by cavers are in special maps, which will help tourists not to get lost. All the passages and halls are named in the cave. In addition, traveling through the adits, you can meet professional cavers who are always ready to help or give advice. Each tourist will be enchanted by the caves of Syana. Photos obtained in these places are mysterious and mystical.

How to get to the cave?

Six kilometers away from the Lenin Hills you need to go along the main road towards the Pit. After the bridge, turn left to the village of Novlyanskoye. At the end of this settlement, you need to cross the Pakhra river, there is platinum. Next, keep the path along the river to the left. The entrance to the caves is located in the second ravine on the right, there is a bus stop nearby .





Syana's caves are most visited during the New Year holidays and winter time in general. There are much fewer people here in the summer, because there are also difficulties. Not everyone can dare to plunge into the underground coolness after being exposed to extreme heat.

There are practically no dangers in the caves, especially if you are well prepared. You can go on an excursion with family or friends. You will remember this vacation for a long time. Be sure to visit the caves of Syana. How to get, now you also know.
