What is the weather like in Turkey in June?

Usually the question of weather comes up when the whole family is going to have a good rest and make their vacation unforgettable. No one wants this desired holiday to be spoiled. Therefore, the issue of choice is approached with all seriousness. If the choice falls on the resorts of Turkey in the first month of summer, it is important to check everything carefully. So, what is the weather like in Turkey in June? Should I plan a trip to this resort country this month? What is the best place to go? A detailed review of forecasts for some resort cities will help determine this important decision. To do this, we draw attention to several cities in Turkey, which are located on the coast of the Marmara, Aegean and Mediterranean Seas, washing this resort country.

Famous istanbul

Istanbul is perhaps one of the most famous cities in Turkey among tourists. Located in the Bosphorus at the junction of two seas, the Black and the Marmara, this large city attracts visitors with its diversity. If we talk about the climate, then it is quite mild. Since April comes the long-awaited warmth. The temperature rises above 10 degrees heat. In May and June, a favorable time comes regarding temperature - it rises to 20 degrees. The average maximum temperature in Istanbul in June is +26. The nights are usually warm. Water warms up to 20 degrees, which allows tourists to relax on the beach. But July-August are hotter. Therefore, tourists often choose the period July-October.

Resort Izmir

Another city that is popular with tourists is Izmir. It is located in the Gulf of Izmir, at the place where in ancient times the city of Smyrna was located. The Mediterranean climate prevails here. The best time to relax is summer, because during this period there is practically no rainfall, and the air temperature warms up to 25-30 degrees Celsius. In June, the weather in Turkey, namely in this city, is favorable for swimming. Water reaches 20 degrees, nights are warm, and days are sunny and hot. The maximum average temperature in Izmir reaches 30 degrees, and in July-August rises by 2-3 degrees. Therefore, the location of the city, and its historical heritage, and favorable weather for recreation will allow its visitors to enjoy during the planned vacation.

Beautiful Antalya

Among the famous resorts of the world you can find the name Antalya or Antalya. This resort and port city is very popular among lovers of good rest on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. From May to October, the weather is clear and arid. If we talk about the weather in Turkey in June, then here in Antalya, already this month, the temperature is favorable. For example, the average air temperature is +25. It can freely rise up to 30 degrees. Water is heated in June to +24. However, the hottest months are July and August. Therefore, Antalya in June is a great opportunity to spend an unforgettable vacation by the sea, when the sun still does not burn with its rays, but at the same time it warms up after the cold winter months.

Cities of the Mediterranean

On the shores of the Mediterranean Sea are the most famous and popular resorts. In Turkey, the weather and temperature in June are just right for those who like warmth, but do not want to experience extreme heat. For example, in the small town of Marmaris, located near the Greek island of Rhodes, the temperature in June ranges between 30 degrees. This allows tourists to feel comfortable. The water temperature reaches 22 degrees, giving a slightly refreshing effect to lovers to swim in the sea. Summer heat due to the favorable location of the city is well tolerated.

In another town, Fethiye, located near Marmaris, in June the temperature also reaches 30 degrees, which allows you to have a good rest for those who do not like strong sunny heat. But already since July, the temperature here reaches 35 degrees and above. Sea water temperature +23. Due to the geographical location, this town is warmer than in Marmaris, for example.

Another name deserves the attention of vacationers. This is Alanya. The city is located southeast of Antalya at a distance of approximately 135 kilometers. The weather in June in Turkey, in Alanya, makes it possible to have a great time on vacation. Due to its location, this city is called one of the hottest in this country. The average temperature throughout the year is about 20 degrees Celsius. In June, it reaches 25-30 degrees during the day and 20-22 degrees at night. Alanya is popular among tourists who like constant warmth.

Ankara - the capital of resort Turkey

The capital city of the country deserves the attention of all who love to travel. Although it does not have access to the sea, it is nevertheless frequently visited. In June, the weather in Turkey is wonderful for traveling around the country. In Ankara, in the first summer month, the temperature ranges from 20-26 degrees. Sunny warm weather prevails. By this time, precipitation is significantly reduced. Therefore, the city welcomes its visitors in June with favorable weather.

June is a great month to relax

So, looking at the statistics of weather and temperature in Turkey, we can conclude that June is very suitable for recreation as a month in which you can avoid extreme heat, but at the same time feel the summer warmth.

The weather in Turkey at the beginning of June on the coast of the seas is stabilizing, and the water warms up to a suitable state for swimming. Sea air, low humidity, nightly freshness are excellent conditions for relaxation. The first half of the month is often considered favorable for parents and children to relax, as the sun still does not burn the skin. During this period, it is best to visit the resorts of the Mediterranean Sea.

In June, in Turkey, the weather at sea becomes acceptable for swimming and sunbathing. When the second half of the month comes, the picture begins to change. The air warms up more strongly, the sand begins to glow, the nights no longer cool enough. The hottest months are approaching - July and August. Therefore, everyone who decided to enjoy the good rest for themselves can freely plan their vacations in June.
