Three full-size Tilda doll patterns

Today, many needlewomen sew Tild. These cute dolls adorn both children's rooms and ladies' boudoirs. Do you also want to sew something like that? Then this article is especially for you. It contains 3 full-size Tilda doll patterns. Look for all the details below.

Simple Tilda

Such a toy can be sewn not only by an adult lady, but even a little girl. The full-size Tilda doll pattern is shown above. It needs to be printed on A4 format. If you want to make a big toy, you can double the pattern on the computer. After you have completed this simple task, you should cut out the details from paper. The next action - patterns of Tilda dolls in full size are transferred to the fabric. The body of the toy must be sewn from material in beige or coffee color. If you want to make a natural doll, you can buy a white cotton fabric and tint it with coffee or tea.

All details of the toy are cut out, it remains to sew them. You need to start with the torso. We sew two parts, leaving a hole at the bottom. We turn the workpiece out and stuff it with synthetic winterizer or cotton. It remains to sew the hole left. Now, by analogy, you need to make arms and legs. Stitched parts are sewn to the body. Now you need to make hair from woolen threads or from a loose satin ribbon. To complete the manufacture of the toy you need to embroider two dots-eye.

Tilda angel

This cute craft will require a little more time to make than the previous version of the toy. The full-size Tilda Angel doll pattern is shown above. It should be printed or redrawn. This pattern should not be increased; a cute angel should be small in size. We cut out the paper parts and transfer them to the fabric. As in the previous version, it is worth using a beige or coffee color of the material.

How to sew a Tilda doll with your own hands? Patterns are ready, it remains to collect them. First you need to stitch all the details. Let's start making a toy from the body, then make arms and legs. There is one caveat. If you want the doll to sit, then you should stuff your legs loose, and then make a seam on the knee for the finished parts. Lastly, we will make wings. Now we sew all the details into place. It remains to make a hairstyle and embroider eyes. You can put on such a doll in anything. In the classic version, all Tildas wear dresses, but this, of course, is not the law.

Doll Tatyana Connee

Tildes with big legs conquered the whole world. These cute girls, unlike classic rag toys, look more modern and relevant. A full-size Tilda doll pattern can be found above. These girls are sewn from beige material, but unlike the original dolls, Tatyana Konne's toys consist of several parts. To begin manufacturing Tilda follows from the head. First, we sew the occipital parts, and then attach the front to them. You can immediately turn out and fill the resulting face. Sew the body and attach the head to it. Putting together legs that consist of three parts. You can insert a cardboard into the sole so that the doll stands better. Sew hands and feet to the doll. Now you need to sew Tilde with bead eyes and brown the cheeks with dry pastel.
