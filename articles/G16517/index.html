Vienna hotels. Sights of the capital of Austria

The most romantic and charming city of Europe with its unique beauty is Vienna. The amazing atmosphere prevailing here makes it especially attractive for tourists. Thanks to its sights, which you can spend more than a month viewing, Vienna is rightfully recognized as one of the main cultural capitals of the world. Arriving here on vacation, travelers will receive a large number of pleasant emotions and will be able to join the world of beauty. Today we will tell you about the most famous sights of the city, as well as where visitors from different parts of the world can stay here.

Heart of Austria

Archaeological finds prove that in the first century BC, life begins to emerge here and the first settlements appear. The heyday of Vienna begins in the tenth century. At this time, magnificent architectural buildings appear, many of which have been preserved to this day: St. Stephen's Cathedral, Hofburg and others. Vienna becomes the capital of Austria, its main treasure. This city was very popular among creative people. Here at different times such musical geniuses as Mozart, Schubert, Brahms lived and drew inspiration. Until now, many people travel to Vienna to enjoy the enchanting sound of the world famous opera.

Unforgettable journey

Better place than Vienna for a memorable vacation with many diverse cultural attractions and you can not think of! This enchanting city is suitable for those who want to relax with the whole family, and those who want to go on a romantic trip. Here everyone will find something for the soul.

Of course, any tourist who comes here begins his acquaintance with the city by studying its main attractions. There are a great many of them. Most likely, you will not be able to see everything at a time, only if very quickly, but then the impression will not be so full. Therefore, we recommend that you first visit the main pearl of Vienna, its symbol is Stefansdom (St. Stephen's Cathedral).

After, go on an excursion to the former residence of the Habsburg dynasty. There are many secrets and mysteries associated with this palace, which the guide can tell you.

Prater Park is also a place worthy of attention. Children will be delighted with the rides and the big "cave of fear", and adults will be able to ride a ferris wheel and see every corner of the beautiful city.

And, of course, you cannot come to Vienna and not visit the building of the State Opera. As for shopping, you will be absolutely delighted. Many shopping centers, department stores and small shops offer a rich assortment of quality goods. Next we will tell you about the most popular hotels in the city.

The best hotels of Vienna

When planning your trip, the first question that tourists decide is a comfortable place to stay. Many Vienna hotels in the city center are always at your service. Among them, there are both luxury and budget options. In addition, various hostels and guest houses are located near the center. The options for these Vienna hotels, according to vacationers, will be able to satisfy the need of any client for a quality and comfortable stay. A nice plus is the low cost of the room, which can be rented for a day at a price of 1,500 rubles. In addition, there are spa hotels in the city where you can take cosmetic and therapeutic procedures, as well as visit the famous thermal baths, which will have a beneficial effect on the health of your body.

Where to stay?

The ranking of the best options for Vienna hotels on the "Bucking" will help you make a choice:

  • Hyatt Park is a combination of true luxury and elegance. Next we will talk about it in more detail.
  • Senator is a 4-star option with an amazing kitchen and a huge park within walking distance.
  • "Royal 4 stars" - a unique hotel in Vienna, where you can relax no worse than in the fashionable options. Its highlight is the rooms, in the interior of which are beautiful, antique furniture.
  • Pension New Mark is a good option for couples with children.
  • Ruby Marie Vienna is the only high-tech designer hotel in the city. Here everything is thought out literally to the smallest detail so that every vacationer feels comfortable.

Hyatt Park

Vienna's popular hotel in the city center, located next to the famous business district - the Golden Ring. Each room has a TV, air conditioning, a cozy bathroom. In addition, polite employees work here, ready to solve any of your problems. An additional plus is the presence of a large wellness center on site, where you can do massages and body wraps, as well as other procedures. For sports lovers there is a fitness room equipped with the best fitness equipment and a pool.

Budget options

If you are somewhat limited in cash, but want to stay near the center, then 3-star hotels are a good option. Among them, tourists usually distinguish: "City Rooms" and "Austria Classic". They are located near the center, the staff speaks excellent Russian. All rooms are very clean and comfortable. The hotel has a cafe with good and inexpensive Viennese cuisine.

Vienna Marriott

One of the great options to stay. Each room is equipped with a large plasma TV, coffee machine, minibar and a special shower with hydromassage function. Beautiful furniture, soft carpets, a spa complex and a sauna - everything you need for a good relaxation!

Choosing any of the above hotels in Vienna, you will have a good rest here at any time of the year. Have a good trip and bright emotions!
