Tea roses made of corrugated paper. Workshop for beginners

Flowers made of paper are striking in their beauty and realism. They revive the decoration of any home, delighting the inhabitants with their bright and elegant buds. Such flowers can be an excellent gift that conveys all the love and warmth of the giver. Any girl will be delighted to receive such a luxurious bouquet.

In this article we will tell you how to make beautiful flowers - roses from corrugated paper. A master class on the manufacture of these products is within the power of anyone, even a novice master. The main thing is to prepare the necessary tools and buy a beautiful corrugated paper. And do not forget about a good mood.

Chic corrugated paper tea roses: a master class

To make these luxurious flowers, you will need the following supplies:

  • cream and green corrugated paper;
  • toothpick;
  • PVA glue;
  • Styrofoam;
  • floristic ribbon;
  • wooden stick for barbecue or sushi;
  • scissors.


Making roses from corrugated paper begins with the creation of its petals. First, take cream paper and cut a square out of it (with a side equal to 10 cm). Fold it in half twice, getting a small square. Now we cut from it the shape of a petal, rounded on top and narrowed at the bottom. When you open the paper, you get a part consisting of four petals. In the center we make a small hole. Next, using a toothpick, round each petal. This will make our rose more realistic and vibrant. By the same principle, we cut out a sufficient number of such blanks. How many will be required depends on how magnificent you want to make a rose.

Cream roses from corrugated paper. Workshop on their manufacture

After all the petal blanks are ready, you can begin to create the stem and core of the flower. To do this, take a wooden stick and carefully decorate it with a green floral ribbon. You got a stem for a rose. We take a piece of foam (if desired, it can be replaced with candy in foil) and string it on a wooden stick. Important: if you use candy as a core - it is advisable to take sweetness without liquid filling. That's all, difficulties in the manufacture of the stem and the core of the flower should not arise.

Putting all the pieces together. Roses from corrugated paper - master class

After that, you can proceed to the design of the core with petals. At one workpiece, grease all four petals with PVA glue. Now we take it and string it on the stem. From the first petals we form a dense bud, laying them overlapping each other. Gradually add the following blanks, making the rose lush and voluminous. After the rose bud is overgrown with all the petals, you can proceed to the design of the sepals.

To do this, cut off the strip of cream corrugated paper and divide it into 5 identical segments. We wrap them in the upper part of the stem, directly under the bud, not forgetting about the glue.
