Miramare Resort & Spa 4 * (Greece, Crete): hotel description, services, reviews

Crete in ancient Greek mythology is the most mentioned island. It is he who is considered the birthplace of Zeus and the place where the Minotaur lived in a terrible labyrinth. In the XXI century, Crete is considered not only an island full of attractions, but also one of the best Greek resorts. And this is where the Miramare Resort & Spa is located - a popular 4-star hotel complex.





general information

This hotel was opened in 1982 in Agios Nikolaos. It is easily recognizable since it was built in the traditional Cretan style. It is often renovated and renovated (the latter was 3 years ago), so the Miramare Resort & Spa looks quite modern - both inside and out.

The nearest airport, located in Heraklion, is about 55 minutes away. But in the immediate vicinity is the beach. And within 5-7 minutes you can walk to such interesting sights as the port of Agios Nikolaos, the church of Panagia Kera and the archaeological museum. About the same distance is one of the two freshwater lakes in Crete called Vulizmeni.

Service

The Miramare Resort & Spa has all the services that guests may need. The reception here works around the clock, in addition to it, in the lobby there is a currency exchange, an Internet corner, a left-luggage office and a luggage room, as well as a tour desk.

There is a dry cleaning and laundry service, a mini market on site, a car rental service and free private parking. You can also rent a motorcycle or bike. Also delivered drinks, food and fresh newspapers directly to the guest room. If they plan an excursion for the whole day, they can order packed lunches with them - for this, you need to go to the reception the night before. By the way, the staff here speaks not only Greek and English, but also Finnish and Swedish.





Business services

Miramare Resort & Spa is a hotel that has become famous not only as a place well suited for relaxation, but also as a good complex for holding various events of an official nature.

There are three large, modernly equipped conference rooms with natural light. They are all located in the main building and offer wonderful sea views. Two conference rooms are large and can accommodate 300 people. The second is small, designed for 20 people. But besides them there are still several meeting rooms. By the way, these halls are often rented for banquets, receptions, conferences, seminars, as well as weddings and gala dinners.

Fitness & SPA

When choosing a hotel, many pay attention not only to what rooms they have, but also to the entertainment offered. Because in our time, hotels are not only a place for temporary residence.

Well, in Miramare Resort & Spa 4 * there is something to do. There is a modern gym, fully equipped with new simulators, which will appeal to lovers of an active lifestyle. It has everything from treadmills to rowing machines.

And, of course, the hotel has an excellent SPA center, which can be understood by looking at the name of the hotel. In addition to standard cosmetic services (such as pedicures, manicures, hairstyles, haircuts, hair styling, etc.), Wellness therapy is also actively practiced. Holistic procedures for the body and face are especially popular. Here guests can enjoy in an atmosphere of tranquility, silence and relaxation. In addition, there is a sauna, hammam, several massage rooms and swimming pools.





Leisure

People who have been to Miramare Resort & Spa 4 * are happy to talk about what to do here. Many are advised to devote time to diving and water sports. The hotel provides for this not only equipment, but also experienced certified instructors. Diving, underwater excursions, boat trips, sailing, fishing - this is just a small list of interesting activities that you can try while relaxing in a seaside hotel.

Many more are advised to go climbing. For this, there is also equipment and experienced instructors. In addition, tourists will be interested to enjoy the Mirabello Bay from a height of 300-meter hills. The hotel even has a covered climbing wall.

There is also a floodlit tennis court in the hotel. If you want to learn how to play, then experienced coaches will help.

However, in the Miramare Resort & Spa (Crete), you can also take a more relaxing pastime. For example, visit the game room. There are table tennis, chess, backgammon, cards and billiards.
