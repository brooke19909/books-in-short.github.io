The best hotels of Artem

Arriving on a business trip or on business in the city of Artyom of the Primorsky Territory, you will probably want to spend a comfortable time free from work. Artyom hotels will come to your aid, where you can relax and have lunch. We offer you several accommodation options.

Hotel Cedar House

Looking at the hotels of Artyom, pay attention to the hotel "Cedar House". It has a great location. Vladivostok International Airport ( Knevichi ) is located at a distance of only two kilometers from it. Guests at the Cedar House can make use of the free shuttle service. The hotel has a terrace for relaxation. Vacationers have access to free internet. Free guarded parking is available on site with private vehicles. There is a restaurant and a bar. Each room is equipped with the following amenities:

  • TV (cable TV);
  • refrigerator;
  • telephone;
  • air conditioning;
  • ironing facilities;
  • hair dryer, shower or bath;
  • free toiletries.

To accommodate guests are offered rooms of several categories:

  • Deluxe twin room for 85,500 rubles for nine nights.
  • Deluxe double with one double bed for the price of 86 961 rubles for nine nights.
  • The cost of a deluxe room for nine nights is 118,453 rubles.

All rooms include breakfast. Pets are not allowed.





Hotel Venice

Venice is one of the hotels in Artem, which also has a very convenient location. Knevichi International Airport is located at a distance of three kilometers from it. Free on-site parking and free Wi-Fi are available. There is a restaurant and a bar. For walks near the hotel there is a garden. Each room offers its guests the following amenities:

  • TV;
  • DVD player
  • kettle;
  • refrigerator;
  • bathroom;
  • slippers, bathrobes;
  • Free toiletries.

To accommodate guests, the hotel offers rooms in the following categories:

  • single standard, its cost is 34 845 rubles for nine nights;
  • standard double with two single beds - 41 369 rubles for nine nights;
  • standard double with one double bed - 43 020 rubles for nine nights;
  • the cost of a junior suite is 46,260 rubles for nine nights.

Pets are not allowed.
