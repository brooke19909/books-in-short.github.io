Elegant and comfortable clothes for sphinxes

Surely many believe that mewing pets do not need clothes. In some ways, of course, they are right. Animals with luxurious fur coats do not need outfits to protect them from the cold, and if your cat is an exclusively domestic creature that does not go to the promenade, it is not necessary to purchase or sew a wardrobe for him. Another thing is if the pet breed came from Ancient Egypt. Sphinxes with delicate skin are sensitive to temperature changes and often freeze even at home. Clothing will save from drafts, otherwise bald cats, the most susceptible to colds, will have to be treated for a long time and carefully.

Practicality

Clothing for sphinxes helps the pet not freeze in the cool season, and in summer a light cape will protect sensitive skin to burns. It is not necessary to buy warm outfits in special stores. And taking into account their considerable price, there is an opportunity to also save if you sew clothes to the sphinx with your own hands.





It should be practical. It is important that the robe does not constrain your pet in movements, is comfortable to put on and is easy to take off. Very spacious outfits that will drag along the ground will not work.

Security

We must not forget about security for hairless creation. No rhinestones and ribbons, beads and buttons to prevent the playful pet from swallowing them. If the owner still wants to decorate his pet's outfit with ‚Äúglamor‚Äù, then all the accessories must be securely fastened. Clothing with a minimum of seams that can cause irritation and rubbing is preferred. It is best to choose Velcro fasteners that do no harm, rather than zippers in which delicate skin can get stuck.





No matter how beautiful the clothes for the sphinxes with a wide collar or drape, it is best to abandon it. An active animal can easily catch on the protruding part, which can create a danger to life. The appearance of the outfits, of course, is not important for the pet; rather, it serves as the pride of its owner. But for the costumes in which your cat will flaunt, the main task is to take care of her health, and not cause tenderness among passers-by.

Easy way to make your pet smart

DIY clothing for sphinxes is made very easily. Of course, everything can be bought, but what could be better than doing it yourself, investing your care and love for a small pet? You can find many recommendations on how to knit or sew an outfit for a cat, but there is a simple option without patterns that is easy to craft with your own hands. And such clothes for sphinxes will become home or pleasure, depending on the beauty of the chosen terry socks. They will make a chic vest for the forever freezing cat. Loose elastic and lack of heel are the only requirements for the selected toe. The lower part of it is removed with scissors, and holes are made for the front paws. It is best to overcast all sections so that the fabric does not crawl. If the animal is large, then you can use one cut off sleeve from an old sweater that is dusting in the closet.





And from the second you get a cute hat for a nice walking kit. A part of the sleeve with the cuff is also cut off, the top is twisted and tied with thread, small holes for the ears are made and the ties are sewn so that your cat does not lose such a cute piece of clothing. Cut off parts are overcast. If desired, the hat is decorated with applique.

As you can see, do-it-yourself clothes for sphinxes are made very simply.

How to accustom the sphinx to wearing clothes

So, ready-made clothes for the sphinxes are waiting in the wings. Now the problem is how to train cats to wear warm clothes. Lovely creatures turn into evil furies, tearing out the outfits created for them with such love. They violently resist wearing clothes and get used to it for a long time. This is due to the sensitive skin of cats, which reacts even to soft material. Therefore, it is worthwhile to teach your favorite kitten to wearing things as a kitten. Naturally, the materials for a sweater or vest are selected only natural and hypoallergenic, so that you do not have to contact veterinarians with dermatitis. Synthetic fabrics or wool with lurex inserts are swept away immediately. Sweaters and socks, from which things are made for animals, should also not cause allergies. Clothing for sphinx cats simply must be comfortable, soft and comfortable.



It is not recommended to use rustling fabrics so as not to scare the cat. A dressed little pet first gets used to the outfits of the house, and then it can be taken for a walk, without leaving it unattended for a minute. With every attempt to free oneself, the host distracts attention with a game or delicacies. Gradually, your pet will get used to walking dressed for your joy, and to your health.
