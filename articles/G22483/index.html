Foros and its attractions. Foros park - Crimean pearl

The Foros resort is located in the extreme south of the Crimean peninsula. Rest here leaves a lot of positive emotions and impressions. The local nature attracts and has always attracted poets and writers, artists and musicians, as well as everyone who wants to replenish their vitality.

This article will focus on Foros and its main attractions.

Foros - the southernmost village in Crimea

Foros is the extreme southwestern village of the Yalta City Council, located on the southern coast of Crimea (South Coast). Where its name came from remains a mystery. Rest in Foros always leaves a lot of positive emotions. The village boasts a mild climate with warm winters and a long, humid, but not burning heat, summer.

Holidays in Foros

The most western resort of the southern coast of Crimea is located near the sea. The path to it runs along a winding mountain road. Overcoming it, you finally find yourself in a real magical corner!

This village is to the liking and distinguished guests. In the distant 1916, famous figures of that time, singer Chaliapin and writer Gorky visited Force for the whole summer! And in memory of this meeting, a memorial plaque was erected on the building where they lived. Moreover, later the Russian government presented the writer with a local dacha, where he created his most important novel.





Modern Foros is one of the elite settlements of Crimea. This is one of the favorite vacation spots of the creative intelligentsia of Russia, Ukraine and other countries.

Coming to relax in the village of Foros, you will certainly find for yourself an interesting activity that will appeal to you and will leave the most pleasant experience. For example, you can go for a walk in the picturesque Foros Park, visit the famous Foros Church or the Kuznetsov Palace.

In any case, Foros is a place that cannot but be liked! In addition, it is located between the hero city of Sevastopol on the one hand and a whole string of small resort towns and towns of the South Coast - on the other. At any time, you can go from Foros to one of the fascinating tours of them.

Foros can offer tourists many options. For example, you can stay in a country house, rent a cottage with a small or large courtyard, or book a room in an expensive hotel. After a successful settlement, you can begin to see the sights of Foros. Believe me, there are plenty of them here!

The upper part of Foros Park is a mixed forest with all sorts of natural trails that wiggle steeply along the slopes.

Foros Park: how to get there

How to get to the park? There are several ways to do this. If traveling from Yalta, then by bus or taxi "Yalta - Foros". From Sevastopol - by a bus going along the Sevastopol - Foros route from the bus station itself, or by the Sevastopol - Miskhor bus, Sevastopol - Yalta bus (in this case, you need to get off the highway and go down to the village).





The park is located on the territory of the sanatorium of the same name. To get into it, you must go through the checkpoint. You can do this for free.

In addition to the beauties of the park itself, you can also see the Foros Church, built with the money of the same Count Kuznetsov.

Foros Church

This wonderful majestic temple is an example of traditionally Russian architecture of the century. It is located on the very edge of the Red Cliff.

In 1892 this church was erected. The temple has nine different domes. This feature gives the church solemnity and a certain pomp. Another important feature of this temple is its orientation. The Foros Church is turned towards the sea, and not to the east (as is customary in the construction of all Orthodox structures).





Kuznetsov Palace

Count Kuznetsov chose a truly successful place for his residence! Landscapes of indescribable beauty open from either side of the windows of the building.

The interior of the palace is no less impressive: oak doors, parquet, marble fireplace - all this has survived to this day in its original form. Impeccable style and elegant simplicity are felt throughout.

Looking at the Kuznetsov palace, we can conclude that the architect did his best. The building wonderfully fits into the local landscape. Today in the count's mansion is the main building of the Foros health resort, which bears the same name with the village - "Foros".
