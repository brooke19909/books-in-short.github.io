How to clean the microwave at home. How to clean the microwave from fat inside

A microwave oven can be seen today in almost every home. It has become an indispensable attribute of modern cuisine. Using a microwave, you can quickly defrost food, prepare delicious food, and warm up dishes before serving.

A kitchen helper is usually used very often by all family members. In this regard, sooner or later, dried yellow spots of fat appear on its surface. How to clean the microwave at home and help it become snow-white again?

General recommendations

If your kitchen assistant is dirty, keep in mind that washing it like a kettle or pot is strictly prohibited. In each case, you should apply your tricks and secrets. How to clean the microwave at home, so that after that it does not have to be taken to a repair shop?



If you wash the kitchen assistant outside, then a damp sponge and any detergent are enough. From the inside, the microwave oven is much more and more polluted. In this case, hard to remove greasy or already managed to dry stains have to be cleaned.

In order for the device to shine again with whiteness and not be damaged at the same time, you should know the structure of its inner surface. It is usually multi-layered. The last, outer layer is designed to reflect microwaves and is protective. That is why it should not be damaged.

Detergents

Before cleaning the microwave oven , carefully read the instructions for use. It should have a section pointing to those detergents that can be used to clean the inner layer. First of all, they should not contain abrasive particles. In other words, different microwave washing powders cannot be used. Only use a soft sponge to clean the kitchen assistant. It is easier to remove dried stains with a rough brush, but this significantly increases the likelihood of damage to the protective layer.



Currently, without much difficulty, you can buy a detergent. The selection of this product is quite large. There are special tools designed for washing microwave ovens. Buying preference should be given to sprays, creams and gels. It is this form of cleaning product that allows you to carefully remove dirty stains.



In the department of household chemicals in supermarkets you can buy Mr. Muscle and special napkins for the microwave oven - Magic Power. Cleaning products such as Sanita 500-Anti-Fat and Sanklin are also being sold.

How to clean the microwave inside? Simple enough. To do this, apply a little tool on a contaminated surface and leave it for several minutes. After that, the gel, cream or spray is removed with a soft cloth.

The main disadvantage of this method is that you will need to sort out several options in order to find the most effective tool. In addition, household chemicals cause considerable harm to health. If you are allergic or have a small child in the house, you will have to use another option.

Water

How to clean the microwave inside using a safe and fairly simple way? Absolutely free to remove greasy stains from the protective layer will help ordinary water. However, it is worth remembering that this method is only suitable for mild pollution.

How to clean the microwave at home with water? To do this, pour a small amount into a bowl, and then put the container in the microwave. Turn on the microwave at full power by setting the timer for five to fifteen minutes. After turning off, a little time should pass before the spots get wet better. Only after this contamination is removed with a soft cloth or sponge. This method is very effective in eighty percent of cases.

Lemon acid

How to clean the microwave at home if stains are difficult to remove? In such situations, when using ordinary water, the desired result is not obtained. But do not despair. There is a simple way to clean your assistant without any extra expenses. To do this, you will need to use citric acid. She perfectly cleans the contaminated surface and whitens it.



How to clean the microwave with citric acid? The principle of using this product is the same as in the version with water. Only a teaspoon of citric acid should be added to the prepared container with the liquid. This method is also good because after cleaning the inner surface in the microwave a pleasant fresh smell is preserved. Instead of citric acid, juice of one lemon or several cloves of this citrus can be added to a container of water.

Vinegar

There is another wonderful tool that will answer the question of how to clean the microwave from fat. This is vinegar. By the effectiveness of the application, it is located on a par with citric acid. Therefore, the choice of funds is yours.



This method of cleaning the inside of a kitchen helper is as simple as all of the above. In a container of warm water, add vinegar in the amount of three tablespoons. The prepared mixture is put in the microwave, which is turned on for five minutes. In order for the spots to soften well, they wait a quarter of an hour after turning off the device. After that, the walls are cleaned with a soft sponge or cloth. If the surface is not very dirty, then you can simply wipe it with the obtained vinegar solution.

Soda

This product has abrasive properties. However, it can also be used to clean the inside of a microwave oven. How to quickly clean the microwave with soda? To do this, the product in the amount of two to three tablespoons must be added to the container with warm water. After that, putting the resulting solution in the microwave, start the timer for five minutes. The container must be inside the device for another fifteen minutes after it is turned off. Only then the spots will finally get wet, they can be easily removed with a soft sponge.

Skins of orange

The microwave can be cleaned with your favorite orange citrus. This will require his skins. They are put in a glass mug in which there is water. The capacity is placed in a microwave oven for three to five minutes. After that, it is enough to carefully clean the walls of the inner surface of greasy stains.
