Best 3 star hotels in Spain, Salou

Spanish Salou was not in vain received the status of a tourist center of Catalonia. Everything here has a great rest: a warm sea, endless beaches and the largest entertainment complexes on the mainland. And he is chosen because the city has a developed hotel chain. As it turned out, the "treshki" are the most popular among tourists. Why? Everything is simple. Hotels in Salou (Spain) 3 stars combine a decent infrastructure, good location (proximity to the beaches), as well as comfortable living conditions.

We will analyze the most popular options, but first a couple of tips.

Tourists recommend

If the view from the window is very important to you, choose coastal hotels. Then beautiful sunrises and sunsets will be provided to you. For example, Casablanca Playa is located just near the beach. In principle, almost all hotels of Salou (Spain) 3 stars are located in close proximity to the sea or 5-15 minutes walk from it. But just in case, before booking, be sure to check with representatives on which line of the beach the complex is located.

As for infrastructure, there is almost everything here: from spacious pools to gyms. Hotel Jaime I is perfect for families with children. For younger guests there is a children's room, animators, as well as playgrounds.

Let's talk about the number of hotels of 3 stars in Salou (Spain). As a rule, the interior is simple, but at the same time it has a comfortable rest. The apartments are equipped with necessary furniture, air conditioning, TV, balcony and private bathroom. Some hotel complexes also provide Wi-Fi, but mostly high-speed Internet is available on request, that is, for a fee.

Remember that almost all Spanish beaches are municipal. Therefore, you either have to stock up on your own umbrella and mat, or pay for renting sun loungers. There is another option: book a hotel room on the 1st line. For example, the loungers of the Best Maritim 3 * complex are located just 20 meters from the beach, which is separated by a narrow promenade. The only problem is the lack of umbrellas. As they say, whoever is in the shadow. Therefore, if you do not plan or do not like to wake up early, we recommend that you stock up on your own umbrella.

Best Cap Salou 3 *

The 3 star Best Cap Hotel in Salou (Spain) is located on the Costa Daurada, next to the Port Aventura hotel complex. Guests are offered check-in in standard triple and quadruple rooms. Upon request, the property can replace regular beds with "king" beds. Each room has a safe, satellite TV, a private bathroom and modern appliances.

On the territory of Best Cap Salou 3 * there is a restaurant, which during the evening meal hosts entertainment shows. At your service - a lounge bar with a large assortment of cold appetizers, hot dishes and cocktails. The hotel also has a Donatello pizzeria.

You can have a good time in the games room. The "Best Cap Salou" has several swimming pools: for adults, children and a shared heated pool. Fans to follow the figure will appreciate the gym. At your service there is a spa center, which offers a huge number of pleasant procedures, after which you can go to a specially equipped terrace and sip a cocktail and enjoy your vacation.

Address: Cala de la Font, 1.

Price per night: from 9000 rubles.

4R Playa Park 3 *

The 3-star Play Park Hotel in Salou, Spain, is located on the beautiful Costa Dorada, in the El Tarragones area. The building is literally immersed in the beauty of flowers and lush green trees. For accommodation, the complex offers comfortable rooms of the following categories: with sea views and standard double rooms. All are equipped with a full range of modern amenities and have a private bathroom.

The hotel is open around the clock. There is an exchange office for foreign guests in the building. In the cozy courtyard are 2 large swimming pools for children and adults. A terrace for sunbathing is nearby.

Leisure can be spent in the billiard room or in the hall with game simulators. There is a mini club for younger guests, as well as a small playground.

Address: Vendrell, 1-3.

Price per night: from 6800 rubles.

Casablanca Playa 3 *

The 3 star Casablanca Play hotel in Salou (Spain) is most often chosen for a relaxing family vacation, business receptions, meetings and conferences. The building is located near the beach, shopping area and train station. Guests are offered check-in in standard rooms and superior. The latter are usually overlooking the sea, their own living room and jacuzzi. Maximum capacity is 1-3 guests. Family rooms can accommodate up to 6 people.

Casablanca Playa has only one swimming pool (open), the territory of which is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay, including umbrellas and sun loungers.

Address: Paseo Miramar, 12.

Price per night: from 3400 rubles.

Best San Diego 3 *

This 3 star hotel in Salou (Spain) is perfect for couples with children. For younger guests in the complex operates a games area. You can also go to the kids club for entertainment. And upon prior request, babysitting services are available.

Conveniently located are shops and restaurants. The beach and restaurant are just 200 meters away. The city airport is 9 kilometers away.

Breakfast is buffet style. A similar serving is available for children. Chefs prepare local and international dishes. Alcoholic drinks are sold at the hotel's bar.

Address: Penedes, 23-27

Price per night: from 3600 rubles.
