Turkey. Hotel Sunmaritim Hotel 4 *

Fans of relaxation in Alanya can afford to stay in hotels of any star rating. The choice of hotels in this Turkish resort is very large. Hotel Sunmaritim Hotel 4 * suits tourists in many ways - friendly service, food and a variety of entertainment. More information about the hotel is presented in this article.





Where is

Those who are familiar with Alanya probably know the location of the village of Avsallar. It is there that the hotel Sunmaritim Hotel 4 * is located. The hotel is located near the center of Alanya, about 25 km from the resort. Getting to the place for a short time, the hotel complex is 100 km from the airport, so the road to the hotel will take a little more than an hour. The nearest airport is Antalya.





Hotel Description

The hotel building is not new - the hotel was built in 1991, but relatively recently there have been redecorated. Full reconstruction was completed in 2006. Two main buildings stand on the seashore. Description of rooms

The hotel has more than 120 rooms, to be precise - 123. All rooms can be divided into 3 categories:

- standard (standart room);

- double (single standart room);

- family room;

- suite room;

- Room for people with disabilities.

The hotel is dominated by standard rooms in the amount of 114 pcs. The maximum area is 17 square meters. m. There can stay 2 adult tourists plus one child. As for family rooms, there are only 6. There can be accommodated from 5 to 6 people, including children. There is one room for disabled people with an area of ‚Äã‚Äãonly 10 square meters. m., one suite room (30 sq. m.) and one double room (10 sq. m.).

Almost all rooms have a balcony with sea access. Not every hotel can boast of this. Inside the rooms there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay - individual air conditioning, shower, toilet, hairdryer, telephone, minibar, TV. For vacationers from Russia there are two Russian TV channels. All rooms have modern furniture and carpeted floors. The rooms are cleaned daily, linen is changed every 2-3 days.





Beach

Sunmaritim Hotel 4 * boasts its own sandy beach. Tourists can relax by the pool or by the sea. As for umbrellas, chaise lounges and mattresses, then they can be used free of charge. Towels will have to pay extra. There is a bar on the beach where tourists can enjoy refreshing drinks and cocktails.





How to have fun at Sunmaritim Hotel 4 * (Turkey)

The hotel has a wide entertainment program for its guests. Evening and afternoon animation, a pool with water slides, sports games, etc. There is a gym on the territory, you can play table tennis, work out on fitness machines, and go for a massage. Hotel guests are actively involved in sports and aerobics, play darts, beach volleyball and billiards. In the evening, in the hotel complex you can relax from an active pastime in the sauna, listen to music in the restaurant or dance at a disco.

Children at Sunmaritim Hotel 4 * also have something to do. Parents can send their offspring to a children's club; admission there is limited by the age of the child - from 3 to 12 years. The hotel has a playground, a buffet with a children's menu and special furniture. In the afternoon, animators will play for free with children in the children's pool, and in the evening, kids can go to a mini-disco.





Hotel infrastructure

The hotel has 3 bars, one of which is located on the beach. For businessmen and tourists who come to work, the hotel has a conference room. It is designed for a large number of guests - approximately 100 people. Conferences, presentations and negotiations can be held there. In addition, the banquet hall can be used for celebrations and weddings, which is very convenient.

The hotel administration took care of the comfortable rest of its guests. The hotel has a laundry and dry cleaning. In case of illness or injury, you can always consult a doctor. On the territory there is a medical office.

Paid / free services

As for the payment for services, the hotel Sunmaritim Hotel 4 * (Alanya) is provided absolutely free of charge:

- use of the gym;

- game of table tennis, darts, volleyball on the beach;

- water aerobics classes in the pool;

- animation program on the hotel territory;

- sauna, bocce, aerobics;

- use of the library;

- video games;

- disco on the territory and on the beach.

However, for certain conditions you have to pay, this:

- massage;

- water sports (surfing, riding on catamarans, banana, boats, diving, etc.);

- a trip to the Turkish bath (hamam);

- car rental;

- cinema hall;

- mini golf;

- the shops;

- beauty saloon;

- currency exchange;

- billiards.

In addition to the above, at the Sunmaritim Hotel 4 * (Alanya / Avsallar) you need to pay for using the Internet. For tourists who just need access to the network, the hotel provides paid Wi-Fi throughout. There is also an internet cafe in the lobby bar.

As for food, the hotel offers vacationers an all-inclusive program. Guests can have breakfast, lunch and dinner for free. During the day, tourists are provided with free cold, as well as hot and alcoholic drinks of local production. Visitors can count on a free afternoon snack and snacks at specific times.

Guest reviews

Every third tourist vacationing at the resort leaves his review of the vacation. Sunmaritim Hotel 4 *, reviews of which are mostly positive, is no exception.

The main disadvantage of the hotel, vacationers consider a cramped beach. Despite the fact that it is owned by the hotel, many simply have little space. In addition, many people do not like the rocky coast. According to tourists, there is no entrance to the sea from your beach, large breakwater stones prevent you from entering the water. Many are advised to swim in the pool, as in the sea there is a great risk of injury.

For some, the outdoor pool is considered an undeniable plus, but there are those who do not like, for example, that the pond is in the shade. But these are isolated cases, since the requirements and expectations of vacationers are different. In order for the hotel not to disappoint, you need to pre-view photos before buying a tour. Vacationers in the Sunmaritim Hotel 4 *, the photo of which can be seen in the article, confirm that all the images of the hotel are real.





Many tourists note a modest menu - little meat, small portions. But negative opinions on this subject should not be taken into account, since it is known that the portions in all hotels are standard.





The presence of the owner of the hotel in the building positively affects the opinion of tourists about the rest. Guests are pleased that the hotel owner is not indifferent to the work of the staff. Moreover, in personal communication with the owner of the hotel you can always express your dissatisfaction with one reason or another. It is likely that the problem will be eliminated, and the rest will pass without any excesses.

Many people like the local animation program, employees always amuse guests, play with children, hold sports games and competitions. In most cases, the staff is polite and courteous, attentive to requests.

To relax in the Sunmaritim Hotel 4 * or not - it's up to you, it all depends on personal preferences and requirements for spending your vacation. In general, Sunmaritim can be called a worthy representative of a 4- star hotel , its class is fully consistent with the declared category of hotels in Turkey.
