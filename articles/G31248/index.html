How to get from Klin to Moscow?

Klin is a small city located in the Moscow region. Its population is just over 80 thousand people. The main attractions are colorful temples, the oldest has retained its original appearance since the XVI century. On its territory is a postal yard. You should definitely take a picture with the famous store under the clock.





From this small village, people travel to the capital of Russia for many reasons: to get acquainted with sights, to work, take a walk, etc. Fortunately, there are several ways to get from Klin to Moscow.

Option 1 - by car

The distance from Klin to Moscow is only 98 kilometers. It is best to overcome such a small path in your own vehicle. If you move without stops, the total time to overcome this distance will be approximately 1 hour 36 minutes. Unfortunately, the exact cost of the trip cannot be calculated, as fuel prices are constantly changing. In addition, each car consumes a different amount of fuel.





To get to the appointed place, you must overcome three settlements: Khimki, Zelenograd and Solnechnogorsk. If you wish, you can stop in them to relax or have a snack.

To get from Klin to Moscow is possible only on the international highway E105. The roadway is carefully monitored, so it is difficult to find pits and potholes on it. We should also talk about the fact that many cameras are installed along the perimeter of the road, so it‚Äôs better not to exceed the speed.

Alternative option

Surprisingly, in the XXI century, not everyone can boast of having their own car. There are three alternatives for resolving this situation.





  • Taxi. Allows you to quickly get to the desired place in a comfortable environment. It is not recommended to use it in the morning and evening. During this period, large traffic jams are possible from Klin to Moscow. Accordingly, the cost for the service can be extremely high.
  • Through the Internet, find a group of people moving in the same direction. Fortunately, through social networks, this is quite simple. In addition, there will be a chance to make new friends.
  • On the federal highway, a huge number of people are moving to Moscow, many of them kindly agree to give a ride to a fellow traveler for a small fee. Just go to the track and "vote". But you should be careful, because this is far from the safest way to overcome the required distance.

The total time and distance is the same as when traveling in a personal vehicle.

Option 2 - by bus

Several times a day, buses operate from Klin to Moscow. The first departure takes place at 5:00, and the last - at 21:00. The intermediate interval between them is less than one hour, this allows you to use public transport at any time.

It is worth noting that passengers are transported on branded buses "Mercedes". They are equipped with air conditioning, large walkways and very comfortable seats. All this allows you to make the move very comfortable.

A ticket can be bought at the ticket office of the station a few minutes before the departure of the transport, its approximate cost per passenger aged 7 years will be 120 rubles.

Option 3 - by train





Unfortunately, everyone‚Äôs favorite vehicle, the train, does not move in the direction from Klin to Moscow. There are no direct or passing flights. But the train runs daily. This vehicle is used by most passengers. From the railway station of the first destination, the very first flight is at 4:05. At about 5:35 she arrives at the Yaroslavl station of the legendary capital. Tickets can be ordered in advance or purchased immediately before departure. The composition runs often enough, so there are always enough free places in it. The approximate ticket price is 200 rubles per person.

Airplane

The Moscow region is considered to be the place of residence for businessmen who are not used to wasting their time on empty chores. Always and everywhere, they prefer to travel by plane to save time.





Since the distance between these points is less than 100 kilometers, it is not difficult to guess that there are no direct flights in this direction, and it is unreasonable to use the option with several transfers due to the large amount of time spent, the long distance and the high cost of tickets.

Every day, thousands of people are forced to travel from the small town of Klin to Moscow. For what purpose are they doing this? As it turned out, the reasons are different. Fortunately, there are several types of vehicles that allow you to get from one point to another. The most popular of them is the train. It allows you to overcome this distance quickly enough, regardless of traffic congestion.
