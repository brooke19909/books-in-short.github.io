Side is considered one of the most interesting cities in Turkey. And every year this place is becoming more and more popular among foreign travelers. Where is the hotel situated?





The luxurious Lake River Hotel is located in one of the oldest Turkish cities called Side. This place is famous not only for its mild climate, sandy beaches and clear sea, but also for its unique natural conditions and rich history.

The hotel complex is located in a picturesque corner, almost on the banks of the Manavgat River. And from the windows of the rooms you can enjoy a view of the beautiful Lake Titreengel. Side, with all its attractions and entertainment centers, is just 7 kilometers from the city center of Side. And the small, cozy town of Manavgat is located five kilometers from the hotel. Antalya International Airport is about 75 km away - it can be reached by bus in an hour.

Beach holidays in the city

Of course, travelers primarily arrive on the Mediterranean coast for a beach holiday. And the Lake River Side Hotel offers its customers the opportunity to have a great vacation. The hotel's private beach is located about 1000 meters from the residential buildings. Naturally, you can get there on foot, but for greater convenience, a bus constantly runs from the hotel to the coast.

Here you will find a clean beach covered with small pebbles, so you should take care of the appropriate shoes. Descent into the sea is safe, and the coastal area is perfect for swimming children. On the shore of tourists are comfortable loungers and protective umbrellas. Towels are also available. And in the beach bar you can always enjoy a refreshing drink.

Of course, here you can do more active types of recreation. In particular, travelers have the opportunity to ride catamarans or canoes, take a short walk along the coast on a boat or jet ski. In addition, vacationers can enjoy water skiing or try parasailing.

The hotel also has a diving center, where they offer training and rental of necessary equipment. In this place, sea water is completely transparent, and the underwater world is full of exotic colors, which creates excellent conditions for an unforgettable diving.

Excursion holidays in Side

Of course, beaches, delicious food and fun shows are not all that the Lake River Side Hotel has to offer. After all, Side is one of the oldest cities in the country, and there are plenty of interesting historical sights here.

To begin with, it is worth visiting the Museum of Ancient Art, which contains a huge collection of exhibits from different eras. In addition, you will have the opportunity to explore the remains of the ancient Greek temples of Artemis and Apollo, which look impressive, especially with evening lighting. Near the museum you can see the remains of the Agora - in ancient times there was a market square where slaves were also sold. Also, tourists often stroll along Kolonnaya Street, which is a wide road, decorated on both sides with the remains of ancient monumental columns. It is worth visiting the Antique Theater, which was built in the second century AD.





In fact, there are many more historical and cultural monuments in the city and its environs than it seems at first glance. Here you can see the Sharavsinsky Caravanserai, the Nymphaeum Monumental Fountain, as well as the Port Bathhouse, Arched Gate and the Bishop's Palace.
