Scarlet Vacuum Cleaners: Customer Reviews

Scarlett is a company jointly created by Russian and Chinese manufacturers. Registration of the company took place in the mid-90s in England. This brand is used to sell various household appliances: electric kettles, hair dryers, vacuum cleaners, etc. The main sales market is in Russia. The factories that assemble the devices are located in China and other Asian countries.

This article will provide information on TM Scarlett vacuum cleaners. They attract a large number of buyers due to the low price, which even for the most productive devices does not exceed $ 70-90. The pleasant moment is that the Scarlet vacuum cleaner always participates in various actions. Customer reviews say that during this period you can make a successful purchase by paying only about $ 30.

It is worth noting immediately that the opinions of people about the technology of this brand were divided. However, most of them believe that in all products the quality is fully consistent with the price.





It is not necessary to compare Scarlett with more expensive companies, as it immediately becomes clear that the Russian-Chinese brand is significantly inferior in terms of assembly reliability, productivity and lifetime. But this does not mean that the devices will only work for several months after the acquisition. On the contrary, according to reviews, Scarlet vacuum cleaners have been functioning without failures for at least 3 years. Such an apparatus is ideal for cleaning small rooms (apartments, houses, garages). It is designed for everyday use. The vacuum cleaner copes with dust and dirt perfectly. Most buyers are completely satisfied with their purchase.

Distinctive features

Even despite some shortcomings, which we will talk about a little later, Scarlet vacuum cleaners become especially popular every year. Consumer demand is easy to explain. The reason for the demand are the features of these devices. The most important criterion is price. Even given the short life, the cost is justified.





No less significant is the design. The case is fully consistent with modern style: streamlined shapes, smooth lines, bright colors. Externally, the device is practically no different from the more expensive "brothers". And the last criterion is the technical specifications. They comply with European standards.

Types of Vacuum Cleaners

Scarlet vacuum cleaners come in many forms. In the model line there are devices with and without a bag.

Models that have a dust bag are classic. Air filtration is carried out by multi-stage cleaning. As a rule, they have a HEPA filter installed. It has a number of disadvantages. These include an unpleasant odor with severe pollution, frequent cleaning. There are two types of dust collectors: fabric (reusable) and paper (disposable). Classic models, as a rule, are small in size, equipped with a convenient telescopic tube and easy to operate.





Scarlet cyclone-type vacuum cleaners have a distinctive feature - the absence of a bag. A transparent container acts as a dust collector. Monitoring the level of its fullness is quite simple. Such models are equipped with a "cyclone" and "multicyclone" systems.

Advantages and disadvantages

In order for the buyer not to feel cheated, before purchasing a vacuum cleaner, it is necessary to carefully study all the advantages and disadvantages of this technique.

Benefits:

  • a large number of service centers that service devices for free under warranty;
  • convenience and ease of management;
  • excellent level of maneuverability;
  • decent technical parameters;
  • beautiful appearance;
  • reasonable cost.

Disadvantages:

  • HEPA filter, which, when contaminated, affects the suction power;
  • poor quality materials (quite fragile);
  • loud noise produced by the engine during operation.

Scarlett SC 287

The Scarlet SC 287 vacuum cleaner is equipped with a good suction power of 350 watts. The workflow is controlled by a convenient switch. If necessary, you can choose the power yourself, setting from minimum to maximum. The cost of such a vacuum cleaner varies within $ 45.





Specifications:

  • Power consumption during operation - 1500 watts.
  • The length of the cord is 5 m.
  • Type of cleaning - dry.
  • The volume of the dust container is 1.5 liters.
  • Power supply - 220 watts.
  • Noise level - 70 dB.
  • Filter - HEPA.

Reviews about this model are not only positive. Many do not like noise during operation, a short telescopic tube and an unpleasant odor. The advantages include relatively low weight (7.2 kg), automatic cord reeling, cyclone dust collector, external design, and cleaning quality.

Scarlett SC 284

This model fully complies with modern requirements. It consumes 1500 watts during operation. The vacuum cleaner is designed only for dry cleaning. Able to absorb dust with a maximum power of 320 watts. Dirt is collected in a special flask with a capacity of 2 liters. The device is equipped with a HEPA filter.

Model SC 284 has a small size: 30 x 31 x 44 cm. The weight of three kilograms makes it very mobile. The wheels are plastic. The cord length is 5 m, the cleaning tube is integral. The kit includes a nozzle for cleaning the floor and carpet. Given that the body of the vacuum cleaner is not transparent, the manufacturer has installed a bulb fill indicator.





According to reviews, this model has many advantages. The most important is the special dust collection option. It is stored in a container in a tightly knit condition, so frequent cleaning is not required.

Vacuum cleaner "Scarlet Indigo IS 580"

This vacuum cleaner is a modern machine designed for dry cleaning. It is made in the original design. It is equipped with a multicyclone air purification system. To collect dust a transparent container is installed, the volume of which is two liters. Power indicators: suction - 400 watts, consumed - 1800 watts. The exhaust air is cleaned through the HEPA 10 filter . During operation, it emits a loud noise, the level of which reaches 80 dB.

The Scarlet IS 580 vacuum cleaner, reviews of which are ambiguous, did not impress customers very much. It turned out to be a lot of flaws, for example, a short telescopic tube, a weak level of maneuverability, a small cleaning radius (7.5 m).
