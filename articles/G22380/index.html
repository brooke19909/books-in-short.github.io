Children's camp "Zarnitsa" - reviews and photos

The Black Sea coast is considered to be the center of children's recreation. Camps for adolescents have settled in resort suburbs, accepting both organized groups of schoolchildren and children on individual trips. Children's complexes are a great alternative for spending summer vacations, where comfortable conditions for interpersonal interaction are created.





Increasingly, in such complexes medical services are provided: therapy and prevention of a number of diseases, as well as hardening. When choosing a camp, you should focus on the needs and interests of your child. The criteria in this sense are individual and directly depend on the personal characteristics of the child.

The main thing is that children should be interested in spending time away from their home. The Zarnitsa camp on the Black Sea is suitable for these descriptions. Friendly and responsible teachers and counselors work here, who professionally organize vacations, teach various skills and teamwork. The complex implements an educational function, health, sports and entertainment events are held.

Geographic location

The children's camp "Zarnitsa" over the years of its existence managed to win the hearts of hundreds of thousands of children. It is located a short drive from the water park, dolphinarium and attractions. A huge territory of 7 hectares is planted with coniferous forests, surrounded by mountain hills.





Even on the hottest days it is fresh and the aroma of subtropical greens is felt. A high fence is installed around the perimeter, the territory is under round-the-clock protection. The safety of your children is strictly monitored. The complex can accept up to 400 adolescents from 7 to 15 years old in one shift. A three-star hotel is provided for parents.

Number of rooms





Surrounded by coniferous pines, two three-story buildings with balconies were built. Camp "Zarnitsa" for living offers cozy rooms for 4-6 children. The rooms are decorated in bright simple colors, equipped with beds, bedside tables. The bathroom is shared, located on the floor or in a separate building. All communications are central, hot water is supplied regularly, without interruptions. In the lobby - a TV with a seating area.

Food concept





The summer camp "Zarnitsa" provides its wards with a balanced diet. Table setting is carried out five times a day, in a spacious dining area. Serving is the responsibility of the waiters. The menu is varied, dishes contain proteins, carbohydrates and fats. Every day on the tables there are first courses, meat, eggs, dairy products, bread, cereals and fresh fruits. An afternoon snack mainly consists of gingerbread, juice or kefir.

Leisure and entertainment

According to the approved cultural and educational program, the Zarnitsa children's complex is functioning. The summer camp organizes leisure activities for adolescents as part of a specialized program, a special place is given to staying in the fresh air: swimming in the sea, hardening, warming up. All events are held according to a strictly regulated schedule.





Recreational activities are being carried out , which are aimed at restoring, relaxing and developing physical and emotional forces. Pupils are taught moral and ethical culture. The educational function is provided for: specialized classes, education, development of communication skills.

A lot of time is devoted to healing, strengthening the body, harmonizing mental balance, increasing stamina, improving mental well-being. Along with the listed activities, fascinating shows, games, creative events, including theatrical performances, sports competitions are organized.





For active classes, football fields, a sports area equipped with horizontal bars are provided. There is table tennis. A library with a wide selection of literary works has been opened especially for book lovers. Open gazebos are built for communication and relaxation, which are distributed throughout the complex.

Counselors organize informative excursion routes in Greater Sochi for an additional payment: to the arboretum, dolphinarium, water park, Boxwood Gorge, dolmens, 33 waterfalls, etc. Your children will have fun and interesting summer vacations in the Black Sea camp.

Coastline





Wellness camp "Zarnitsa" is equipped with its own beach, which is intended only for students. The coast is strewn with small pebbles, the entrance to the water is gentle and convenient for primary school age. The coastline is within walking distance of the residential complex - 200 meters. The beach is equipped with sun loungers, sun umbrellas, a summer shower and a toilet. The safety of teenagers is monitored by experienced rescuers, as well as teachers and a nurse.

This is an opportunity to try your hand at various types of creativity, to show your talents, initiative, in addition, meet peers, find new friends of interest. Spending summer vacations in this camp allows the child to feel independent and a little older.
