The best budget TV: rating, review, specifications, advantages and disadvantages

Low-cost TVs do not have high hopes: a sufficiently high-quality image is not expected from them. In the proposed rating - the best budget televisions that differ not only in low price, but also in good functionality.

What are they - inexpensive, but good TVs?

Low cost plays an important role, however, choosing a model based on its cheapness is not the best option that can lead to serious disappointments. Therefore, when compiling this rating of the best budget smart TVs, several criteria were taken into account.

Manufacturer

TVs of the Korean brand LG occupy a leading position in the market in terms of price and quality. Samsung is in the same category, but the products of this brand are somewhat more expensive. Good TV models are offered by Chinese Supra and BBK.

The countries of production of most budget televisions are Belarus, China or Russia. Reputable brands - Panasonic, Sony - produce their equipment in Europe or Malaysia.

Functional characteristics

The main functional requirements are most often limited by the presence or absence of Smart TV. The need for this platform is determined individually. Before purchasing, it is advisable to carefully study the specification in order to know exactly all the functions of the model.

Diagonal and screen resolution

With the first characteristic, everything is clear - the larger the diagonal, the steeper, but this increases the cost. Image quality depends on the second parameter. For small TVs, the HD-Ready format is allowed, while the matrix of models with a medium and large diagonal should comply with Full HD or 4K standards.

Supporters of the platform are of the opinion that a modern TV must be equipped with network functions, opponents, on the contrary, believe that SmartTV is an extra option. Their arguments are quite convincing:

  1. Smart functions are more expensive, which affects the availability of the TV and its cost - the most important criterion.

  3. Similar features are available for Android-consoles, which are much cheaper, but have better features.
