DIY homemade crafts: handmade rugs

The comfort in the house depends on various components - this is the color scheme, furniture, and overall stylistic harmony. It largely depends on the selection of accessories and trinkets. This article will be useful to those who are interested in needlework for the home with their own hands. Mats can be made from almost any material at hand and in various ways. Any room is transformed and becomes more pleasant, you just have to lay a rug on the floor.

Knitting rugs with knitting needles. The foundation

A big plus of needlework is the creation of unique and original products. What you have done with your own hands will not be anywhere else but your home. So, how to knit a rug? It will be round, oval or rectangular - it depends on your desire and the chosen pattern. Although you can do without it.

For example, if you have a lot of different yarn left, you can knit a very beautiful spiral rug from it. For this you will also need a needle and knitting needles. In which case you can buy inexpensive acrylic yarn. The mat will turn out multi-colored, so it will not be difficult to knit it.

You should start with the middle base, it can be quickly crocheted. Separately, you will knit a spiral row by row. Agree, there is nothing complicated. Let your base in diameter be approximately fifteen to twenty centimeters. Color does not matter. If this is your first time picking up knitting needles, be prepared for the rows to be tied. To keep your rug flat and not puffing, it is important to learn how to make shortened rows.

We pick up a spiral

So your foundation is ready. Now let's talk about how to tie a rug with a spiral. You need to dial about fifteen loops with knitting needles and knit with an ordinary scarf, viscous, about a half to two centimeters thick. Next row - knit ten loops. Be careful. Turn your rug and now again make a row of ten loops. Further, the principle will alternate. Again, knit a row of plain, viscous, of the same thickness. Do not skip loops. Now make shortened rows again. The main thing is to remember the algorithm. Loops that do not knit should always remain on one side of your rug.

The proposed method is good because you do not need to think about the color of knitting, let it alternate with you, as you like. The end result will be interesting anyway.

More often, attach the resulting spiral to the finished center, so it will be easier for you to control the knitting so that it does not puff. The further you advance, the more you will have untied ranks. Just turn the loops down gradually.



When you realize that the size of the mat suits you, take a needle and sew a spiral to the base. All! Knitting rugs with knitting needles is painstaking, of course, but not at all difficult.

Crochet

Knitting a rug is not necessary with knitting needles. There are different ways to do this. You can pick up interesting patterns of rugs crocheted.

The simplest option is a square canvas without a pattern with a pattern. You will need yarn in two different colors. Edging and drawing, for example, are blue, the background is white. One loop according to the scheme corresponds to one square. Framing should be knitted at the very least when the mat itself is ready. If you want, you can make it a third color, for example, blue.



So, according to the scheme, each row we have consists of twenty-eight loops. If you have finished the first one and you need to go to the next, lower the twenty-ninth air loop and knit a single crochet through two of the same loops. Etc. Carefully count the loops where the pattern fits. In fact, there is nothing complicated. When proceeding to the edging, knit one single crochet on each loop. And so on around the row. Edging fits more loosely. You should have three single crochet posts in each corner of the frame.

For those who love needlework for the home with their own hands, rugs will seem very interesting objects for creativity. Having mastered the simple crochet technique, feel free to move on to the next stage, to more complex options.

Crocheted Patchwork Rug

Surely everyone has ever seen very beautiful rustic striped rugs. The craftsmanship of their manufacture was perfectly owned by our grandmothers and great-grandmothers. That which used to decorate the floors in the huts, and in the modern interior will look very stylish, at home. In addition, they are easy to wash and are suitable for both the bedroom and the hallway.

To knit a round patchwork rug, you will need: a thick hook and a long piece of cloth about three centimeters wide. Make it more authentic, because the size of the finished product depends on this.



The technology is as follows. Take the very first flap and make a loop. Pull the hook through it and pry the strip that lies on your finger. Well, it's the same with yarn. When you knit a pigtail in about six to seven loops, connect it in a circular through two loops. So the basis is ready. The principle is similar to how a rug knits in a spiral with knitting needles. The second row will need to be knitted, alternating through an air loop, otherwise you will get not a plane, but a tube. Then the step size will only increase. Optionally, you can sew a fringe of flaps to the edge of the mat. And you can even knit fabric rugs with a cloth so that they are square or rectangular. It will be very important for a long corridor.

We decorate the bathroom with a rug

A great option for the bathroom is a rug made of pebbles. Take for the base a smooth mat or just a piece of rubber of the desired shape, as well as glue. It must be versatile and flexible. Be sure to check if the rubber slides on the floor in your bathroom, if so, then you need to choose another material. However, you can use such rugs for the room where you like to relax, because it has excellent massage properties.

Before using the base, it must be thoroughly washed and dried. The more even pebbles you took, the more comfortable the mat will turn out. Lay them on the base in any order you like, observing only one rule - as tightly as possible and without gaps. If you have a sufficient number of stones of two or three colors, you can lay out a pattern.



This option is perhaps one of the most interesting. The process of laying out pebbles is very similar to a mosaic. Each time, getting up after a shower on such a rug, you will warmly recall your vacation at sea. Well, the use of natural materials is always a merit of any interior accessory.

Towels are used

You will need scissors and a trellised plastic rug. Cut the old towels into flaps and simply tie them onto the base as tightly as possible. The fluffier the material, the softer the mat will turn out. Here it is better to do either a monophonic option, or patterned, using towels of two or three colors. It will turn out more beautiful.

DIY massage mat

It can be made from caps from plastic bottles. Surely in every house there is a need for the number of such dummies. Put them in business!

Rug from old clothes

It happens that a lot of old T-shirts have accumulated in the house, which are a pity to throw away. But they have a chance for a new life. Make a cozy and soft bedroom rug out of them. To do this, they need to be cut into flaps. At the store, get a canvas for the basics. Now you just need to thread pieces of t-shirts into the slots and tie it tightly. Of course, denser. In principle, instead of a canvas, you can use an old whole t-shirt, after cutting through the cells in it. Then your mat will be much more convenient to wash.
