LCD "House with a fountain": overview, description, layout and reviews

St. Petersburg is the second city in the country. There is nothing strange and surprising in the fact that the population here is constantly growing. In turn, all residents of the city need a comfortable living space. Unfortunately, those houses and residential areas that were built in the last century are sorely lacking. Modern construction companies that have managed to establish themselves in the market are rebuilding not only new homes, but entire residential neighborhoods with their own infrastructure.

The neighborhoods outside the city are very popular, surrounded on all sides by a forest belt. Fresh air, the opportunity to walk along forest paths, have picnics with friends and relatives, while living in comfortable conditions, having access to all infrastructure facilities - this is what all modern residents of the metropolis dream of. LCD "House with a fountain" - a vivid representative of such projects. It is about him that we will talk in the framework of this material.





about the project

The project of a unique residential development from the Andromeda company - all this is the residential complex "House with a Fountain" (Scheglovo). A large multi-sectional house is being built on a 2.5-hectare plot, the building area exceeds 40,000 square meters, 359 families will be able to acquire spacious apartments.

The final commissioning of the object is scheduled for the 4th quarter of 2017, respectively, very soon the happy owners will be able to get the keys to their new cozy nest.

Location

Surely you are interested in knowing where the House with Fountain LCD is being built. First customer reviews focus on a great location. So, for the construction of the complex was selected Vsevolozhsk district of the Leningrad region, the village of Scheglovo.





The area is free from harmful chemical industries, so it is ideal for families with small children. At the same time, there is everything that is necessary for a comfortable life: shops, restaurants, service industry organizations, and social infrastructure facilities. If you want to appreciate all the charms of country life, but are not ready to lose access to the objects of urban infrastructure, you will definitely like this option.

Technology

Monolithic technology is used for the construction of the residential complex "House with a Fountain" (St. Petersburg). Monolith is a durable durable material, ideal for an apartment building. For external cladding, aerated concrete blocks are used, characterized by excellent heat and sound insulation properties. Finishing of facades is done with thin-layer plaster with subsequent painting.





Those who have already purchased an apartment in the complex and follow all the stages of the work, noted that the ventilation ducts are hidden in the thickness of the walls, which saved the interior from bulky structures. No unsuccessful protrusions, beams - you can use any materials and technologies to equip your family nest.

Architecture

LCD "House at the Fountains" - Peterhof in a modern interpretation. That is how the first buyers described the project. The main decoration of the complex will be a fountain located on the territory of the house. It is the center of architectural composition, it combines playgrounds for walking with children and an active pastime area represented by playgrounds and sports grounds.





Bright, yet concise facades, represented by calm shades, continue the theme of unity with the environment. Wide, almost panoramic windows allow you to fill the interior with sunlight, and the view from the windows is literally mesmerizing.

Transport accessibility

For all residential areas located outside the city, the issue of transport accessibility is one of the main places. Many work in St. Petersburg, take their children to sports clubs and to developing classes, why it is so important that the road is as comfortable and fast as possible.

There are several ways to get to the complex. The first way, the easiest - by car. In this case, the travel time is only 30 minutes. You should move in the direction of the Ryabovsky highway, smoothly passing into the Road of Life. In Vsevolozhsk you should keep to the right, towards the village of Shcheglovo.

No personal car? It doesn‚Äôt matter, because you can get to the complex by public transport: electric trains and buses leave from the Finnish railway station and the Ladozhskaya metro station. Moreover, in the near future, additional public transport routes will be launched in the direction of the new residential neighborhood, which will solve the problem of transport accessibility once and for all.





The residential complex is located in the very center of the village, has its own exit on Shcheglova street, there are also public transport stops with a schedule of its movement. Just a short walk away is the Shcheglovo railway station, from where you can reach the aforementioned Finnish station.

Apartments, layouts

Apartments in the residential complex "House at the Fountain" impress with their variety. A choice of buyers offered 359 apartments. Quartography provides 52 studios, 196 one-room, about 96 two-bedroom apartments and 14 three-room apartments for large and friendly families. Each apartment has a fairly spacious balcony with stunning views of the beauty of the surrounding nature. The developer took into account all the wishes of modern buyers, so he offered both small cozy studios for young families and excellent one-bedroom apartments with a large kitchen and isolated rooms.

Well, three-room apartments with an additional bathroom and a wardrobe will be an excellent option for families with children. In addition, the project provides for the existence of two-level apartments with a private terrace - a modern alternative to a country house. By the way, such options are most in demand due to their practicality and literacy of the distribution of internal space.





Well, the ground floor is reserved for spacious pantries, where all residents can store baby strollers, bicycles, sports equipment without cluttering up the interior of the apartment.

About security

Buying an apartment in the residential complex "House with a Fountain", located outside the city limits, each buyer wants to get not only a spacious apartment, but also to provide all his households with the necessary level of security. The developer also foresaw this: the territory of the complex is under round-the-clock video surveillance, entry to the territory of the complex is strictly by passes, which simply excludes the likelihood of unauthorized persons being present within the borders of the village.

Now parents can not worry about the safety of children playing on the playground - they will always be under the supervision of qualified security officers.

Infrastructure

Do you think that buying an apartment in the residential complex "House with a Fountain", you are depriving yourself of a full-fledged city life? You are deeply mistaken. And proof of this are the numerous reviews of those who managed to invest their money in the construction of the complex. So everything you need for a comfortable life is literally at your fingertips.

A short walk from the new building is a modern shopping center, food and grocery stores, pharmacies, beauty salons, a fitness center, and service sector organizations. Only 200 meters away is a comprehensive school, a kindergarten with excellent, kind and qualified, according to residents, educators, the House of Culture and even a music school where children can learn the basics of music. Entertainment centers and other missing infrastructure are in the necessary variety in Vsevolozhsk, it is not necessary to go to St. Petersburg.

Not far from the complex is the famous Shcheglov estate, dating from the 18th century with a huge park. This is a great place for family walks. Large-scale forests in the built-up area provide excellent ecology.

About pricing

If you are looking for comfortable and affordable housing, the House with Fountain LCD will probably suit you. Prices for apartments in the complex start at 105,000 per square meter. The studio here can be purchased for just 2.8 million rubles. You can buy an apartment here not only in cash, but also on the terms of a mortgage. Cooperation with the largest banks allows the developer to offer favorable conditions, as well as loyalty programs.
