Hydros Club Hotel Kemer 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): description of rooms, service, reviews

Three years ago, the aforementioned hotel underwent rebranding and major repairs. Previously called Suntopia Hydros Club. Such a change is natural. Shopping

Not far from the Hydros Club Hotel Kemer, in the Waikikki store, you can buy sturdy factory shorts and a T-shirt for only $ 4-5.
