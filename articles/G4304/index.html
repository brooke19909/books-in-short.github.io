Anapa: Dolphinarium "Nemo"

The Krasnodar resort of Anapa can surprise tourists with many different attractions. Dolphinarium "Nemo" can be considered one of them. It is the largest marine entertainment complex in the country, including a penguinarium, a zoo, exhibition halls, an aquarium, several shops with souvenirs, as well as cafes and restaurants. Its auditorium can accommodate approximately one thousand visitors per performance.

Description of this institution

Dolphinarium "Nemo" (Anapa) was founded just five years ago and has already managed to win the hearts of local residents and visitors. It is located just a hundred meters from the sandy beaches. In addition to interesting shows with marine inhabitants, here you can take a walk in the oceanarium among sharks and admire the inhabitants of coral reefs. In this place of the complex there are many diverse and rare representatives of the underwater world, the sight of which can be breathtaking.



On the territory of Nemo there is still a cheerful eight Humboldt penguins and amazing birds. The complex also provides dolphin therapy classes. They differ from ordinary swimming with animals in that this rehabilitation is carried out under the supervision of a team of professional psychologists. This type of treatment is recommended for children with developmental delays and other problems and for adults who have a state of depression or stress.

An important feature of this complex is that people with disabilities can safely come to performances in Nemo (Anapa). The dolphinarium is specially equipped for this with ramps, lifts and comfortable seats. This all creates comfort for the audience.

Cost of visiting

A ticket for the show will cost from 700 to 800 rubles, depending on the time and start of the performance. In addition, you can take a photo by coming to a performance at the dolphinarium (Anapa). Prices for such images start at 400 rubles. Swimming with dolphins will cost visitors 3000 rubles. in five minutes.

A single ticket to visit the show, zoo, aquarium and penguinarium will cost 1000 rubles, which is much more profitable than just entering one performance.

Kids up to five years old and pupils of boarding schools, as well as children without parents, can enter the complex completely free of charge.

Contact and travel

The dolphinarium is located at this address: Anapa, Pionersky Prospekt, house 20. It can be reached without any problems by buses No. 114, No. 128 or No. 8.

You can also go there by car. To do this, you will first need to keep the path to the ring to Chembruka, and then turn left and go straight to the complex itself. In this case, the car at the time of presentation can be left in a free parking lot located near the dolphinarium.

There is another option to take a taxi, which from the center of Anapa to "Nemo" will cost about 120 rubles.

For questions about tickets and about the show, you must call the ticket office at this phone number: +7 (861) 333-35-44.
