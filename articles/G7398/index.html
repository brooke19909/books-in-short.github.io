Credit at Vostochny Bank: customer reviews, applying for a loan, necessary data, interest rate and payment terms

Vostochny Bank is one of the largest lenders in Russia. A wide network of branches, favorable credit conditions and understandable requirements attracted millions of borrowers to it. You can get a cash loan at Vostochny Bank without leaving your home: submitting an application online takes no more than 15 minutes.

Why do customers trust a lender?

Before taking a loan at one of the banks, the borrower should carefully study its features, conditions and customer reviews. It is no coincidence that the Vostochny Bank is trusted by depositors.

  • The company participates in the deposit insurance system. This means that depositors can safely hold cash in the accounts of Vostochny. The state guarantees protection of savings in the amount of 1.4 million rubles.
  • It has been operating in the market since 1991.
  • The Bank of Russia recognized Vostochny as one of the most influential units in the country's banking sector.
  • Forbes has compiled a list of the "100 Most Reliable Banks in the Russian Federation", which included Vostochny.
  • The company is expanding its network of branches, including in small cities of Russia. Not all banks in the Russian market adhere to such a policy, which means the lender is interested in expanding the client base.

In addition, Vostochny offers several lending options. Each of the products is a unique loan, which is developed taking into account the needs of one of the population categories.

Cash loan at Vostochny Bank

A classic product in the line of credit - a cash loan. It is offered to customers who plan to gradually pay off debt. Therefore, the minimum loan repayment period is 37 months, the maximum loan is approved for 60 months.

Other parameters:

  1. Interest rate from 11.5% to 24.9% per annum.
  2. Issue only in rubles on the day of treatment.
  3. Decision on the application - within 24 hours.
  4. Amount - from 150 thousand rubles to 3 million rubles.

The interest rate on a cash loan at Vostochny Bank is calculated individually. Its value depends on the reliability of the client, the availability of existing obligations, credit history.

At the same time, the bank has certain requirements for borrowers (the same for all loans):

  • Age from 21 to 76 years. It is taken into account from the moment of issuing a loan to the date of repayment of obligations.
  • Russian citizenship.
  • At least 3 months of experience for persons not older than 21 years, for the rest - at least 1 year.

Documents for obtaining a loan:

  • passport of the Russian Federation;
  • SNILS;
  • certificate of income 2-PIT;
  • additional document: certificate in the form of a bank, driver‚Äôs license, passport, etc.

To obtain a loan, the bank is entitled to request other additional documents.

A copy of the work book is needed if the loan amount exceeds 500 thousand rubles.

A feature of this loan is the speed of receipt and a minimum of documents. To apply for a loan, it is enough to provide only a Russian passport. But if the client wants to get a minimum interest rate, he must provide information on income.

Of the documents required passport of the Russian Federation. But as an additional proof of income, the client can provide the following:

The interest rate is individual for each borrower and depends on the number of documents submitted, the amount of the loan and the reliability of the client.

A new type of loan that allows you to get a large amount at a fixed interest rate. "Big season" is a loan of up to 1 million rubles under two documents. The decision on the application is made quickly - up to 8 hours.

Features of the program "Big Season":

Loan secured by real estate: reviews

In the "East" Bank, you can arrange 2 types of loans with collateral. One of them requires real estate security.

The difference between this type of loan from the rest are large amounts to issue. The borrower can receive up to 30 million rubles in cash. Moreover, the loan term is much longer than most loans - up to 20 years.

Reviews on the loan of Vostochny Bank secured by real estate on the Net are positive. The loan is issued quickly, interest on the loan is transparent, convenient terms for repaying funds.

Main parameters:

  • Amount from 300 thousand rubles to 30 million
  • Mandatory collateral - property. In this case, the pledgor may be another person (not the borrower).
  • Loan term - from 1.1 years to 20 years.
  • Individual interest rate. On average 9.9% per annum.
  • The decision on the application is active within 45 days.
  • Mandatory registration of collateral property insurance (by law).

The requirements for the borrower are the same as for all loans of Vostochny Bank. But as an exception, the age limits can be shifted after a positive decision by the bank's management.

In addition, the requirements for seniority are also reduced: at least 3 months of cumulative seniority at the last place of employment are required.

A loan can be used as a guarantee, but this is not mandatory. The presence of a guarantor significantly increases the chances of approving and providing a favorable interest rate on a loan. The guarantor is subject to the same requirements as the main borrower.

From the documents you need to provide a passport of the Russian Federation and a certificate of ownership of housing. If the borrower and the pledgor are not one person, everyone must provide an identity document.

Special requirements are imposed on the pledged property. It cannot be owned by a minor owner, housing should not be built before 1998 and located in a closed territory. A complete list of restrictions is available on the Vostochny Bank website.

Another type of secured loan is a loan secured by a car. The advantage of the contract is the absence of compulsory insurance of the pledged property and its ownership by the owner during the entire loan repayment period.

Reviews on cash loans at Vostochny Bank with a pledged vehicle are only positive. Customers appreciate the quick decision making, namely within 1 day. They also note that the lack of compulsory insurance saves payers.

Lending terms:

The main requirements are for the vehicle, namely:

The application is considered during the day. When applying, in addition to a passport, the client must provide documents for the car.

Mortgage Refinancing

Like most Russian banks, Vostochny offers refinancing - repayment of a previous loan at the expense of a new loan on favorable terms. The program is available only as part of a mortgage agreement.

There are practically no reviews on refinancing loans from other banks by Vostochny Bank, since the program is a new product. The conditions and requirements for borrowers coincide with the registration of a mortgage in the bank.

The exception is only the special requirements for the contract in another institution. To refinance on a previous loan, the client must:

  • make regular payments for six months or more;
  • Do not have current delays.

An important point is the lack of restructuring of the mortgage agreement. If all the requirements are met, the borrower can count on the loan application to be considered by Vostochny Bank to reduce the interest rate. The lender offers loans from 9.75% per annum in the amount of up to 20 million rubles.

Credit payment: methods, features

For the convenience of customers, the bank provides several ways to pay off debt:

  1. Payment online on the site.
  2. Redemption in the Internet bank.
  3. Payment at the terminals of the "East" bank.
  4. Transfer to an account in another organization.
  5. Payment at the office of the company.

The easiest and fastest way to pay a loan from Vostochny Bank is to pay off online on the site. To complete the operation, you must go to the active window "Online repayment of the loan" Vostochny "card of any bank."

Next, the client must choose a payment method - by contract or card number. In the first case, the loan agreement number and cell phone are entered. In the second - card data for debiting and crediting funds (since most bank loans are issued to a plastic card account).

After data acquisition, the operation must be confirmed. The cards of third-party banks may be charged according to the tariff.

Repayment of a loan in the Internet bank is an opportunity to pay off without leaving your home. To pay, you need to log in and find the ‚ÄúCredits‚Äù tab, then select the desired contract (if there are several), the payment method and amount. After confirmation, the funds will automatically go to the loan account.

When paying through the Internet Bank on a regular basis, the client can activate a automatic debit of funds from the account in a fixed amount.

Paying a loan from Vostochny Bank in terminals is a way to make payments around the clock. If the loan is debited from the bank card, then to repay the loan it is enough to deposit funds into the account.

When paying for a loan from a savings account, you must first deposit funds to the card, and then make a transfer according to the necessary details. Depositing funds in the terminal is free of charge.

Clients can pay a loan at the office of the company with a passport. It is not necessary to take a loan agreement with you, but if the borrower has several active obligations, then it is better to bring documents with you to expedite the search.

Deposits at the Vostochny Bank cash desks for loan repayments are carried out without commission. A third party can be a payer if you have your own passport and loan documents.

You can pay a loan of Vostochny Bank at the cash desks of other banks or financial companies. The transfer will be carried out with a commission at the tariff of the selected organization.
