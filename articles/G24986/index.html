Hotel "Golden Valley" in Novosibirsk: address, room reservation, services and reviews with photos

Among the vast Siberian forests, on the shore of the reservoir that feeds Novosibirsk, half an hour from the center of this unofficial capital of Siberia is the world-famous scientific enclave - Akademgorodok. With the light hand of the initiator of its creation, Academician Lavrentyev, his location was subsequently acclaimed as the Golden Valley. However, the topic of this article is the modernized comfortable hotel of the same name, favorably located within the Akademgorodok.

The Zolotaya Dolina Hotel (Novosibirsk) has been familiar to many scientists visiting the Academgorodok for many years. They note the renovation carried out in it since 2014, the improvement of living conditions. Working in the daytime in the laboratories, conducting research with colleagues, scientists will return to their cozy rooms in the evening, preparing in comfort for the next working day.

Location

Ideal for the people of science, who were sent to Akademgorodok, the Golden Valley Hotel (Novosibirsk) was built. The photo of her only building contains an image of a modernized standard eight-story building, on the ground floor of which there is a restaurant with a lounge. Some get to her from Tolmachevo airport by taxi, others from the city from Karl Marx Square by shuttle bus number 1015, and others from the railway station by bus number 8.

Within walking distance from the hotel are the main research institutes: thermal physics, inorganic chemistry, catalysis, organic chemistry, nuclear physics, computer systems, computational mathematics, computer technology, cytology and genetics, chemical biology and fundamental medicine, cell and molecular biology, geophysics and oil and gas biology, agricultural chemistry and soil science, electrometry and automation, applied and theoretical mechanics.

Near the hotel there is a shopping center. 27 km - State Scientific Library of Russia. The Novosibirsk State Opera and Ballet Theater is 29 km away.

Rooms

The Zolotaya Dolina Hotel (Novosibirsk) is designed to accommodate up to 220 guests. Hotel rooms are divided into three categories:

  • four-seater - 2200 rubles per day;
  • single standard - 2300 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 3400 rubles per day;
  • double junior suite - 4500 rubles per day;
  • double suite - 7200 rubles per day.

As you can see, by the level of comfort, the gradation of service levels is clearly visible: economy (2200 rubles / day), junior suite (4500 rubles / day), luxury (7200 rubles / day). The three-star hotel is ready to adequately serve guests with various incomes.

Based on the reviews of the guests, we will present an additional recommendation to the readers. Guests wishing to enjoy the Siberian landscape from the windows of their rooms are advised to take rooms on the top floor facing the NSU. In the summer heat, it is preferable to settle away from the roof.

Room Service

Hotel "Golden Valley" (Novosibirsk), judging by the reviews of residents, comfortable, cozy. In it, a business person can really relax and tune in the next business day.

Towels, soap and shampoo in the rooms are updated daily. Cleaning is carried out unobtrusively, in the absence of residents, also every day.

In addition to household appliances and furniture, more or less usual for most hotels of this class (TV, fridge, shower), the rooms have a desk and fast and free Wi-Fi. Note that air conditioning is only available in deluxe rooms. There is also a toaster and a coffee machine in the kitchen corner.

An iron and ironing board are available on each floor.

Leisure at the hotel

Obviously, a city non-resort hotel, located on the balance of Akademgorodok, has a limited leisure infrastructure. After all, its guests in their vast majority in the daytime work.

However, those who wish to have fun more thoroughly do not feel constrained here. The hotel, located in the city limits of the capital of Siberia, is located in an area with good transport interchange, which allows you to use the services of numerous clubs, restaurants, and cultural institutions.
