Hotel Natura Park, Dominican Republic: room description, service, reviews. Natura Park Beach Eco Resort & Spa 5 *

Dominican Republic is a distant, but very beautiful country, which is becoming more and more popular with tourists every year. All its resorts are built up with luxurious complexes located on the white sandy beaches. But tourists are attracted not only by them. The Dominican Republic is also a historical monument of the colonial era and diving in an almost transparent sea. If you want to stay in Punta Cana, then pay attention to the Natura Park Beach Eco Resort & Spa 5 * hotel. A detailed description of this complex, as well as reviews and photos you can see in this article.

Where is this hotel located?

The beach complex is ideal for people who love a relaxing holiday and solitude with nature. It is located on a green area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe coast in the resort of Punta Cana. Natura Park Beach Eco Resort & Spa 5 * fully lives up to its name. It is built in an ecologically clean area and is located away from highways. Proximity to the beach is another important advantage. The complex is located on the first line of the sea, so you can walk to the sea in just a few minutes. But there is a drawback. This is the distance from the center of the resort, where most restaurants, bars, cafes and shops are located. The distance to it is about 13 km. But for some, this flaw will seem to be an advantage, because because of the distance from the center, silence and calm reign in the hotel itself.

Conveniently, this resort complex is located quite close to the international airport of Punta Cana, which receives most of the flights from Russia. The distance to it is about 25 km, which means that tourists can get to the hotel in half an hour.

Hotel Description

The construction of this large beach complex was completed in 1997. It is located in a tropical park, so all residential buildings are surrounded by a huge number of trees, shrubs and flowers. In addition, exotic animals and birds live in the hotel, for example, flamingos, which guests are especially fond of watching.

The hotel consists of several residential buildings and an office building. For tourists there are 524 comfortable rooms. It is noteworthy that all the buildings in the hotel are built from environmentally friendly materials - reed, wood, stone and reed. The decor is also tropical style. Every year, all rooms are renovated.

The rules for checking into the Natura Park Hotel in the Dominican Republic are standard. Tourists are not allowed to come here with pets. But the hotel accepts guests with children of all ages, including newborns. Receptionists are administrators who speak not only English but also Russian.

Description of the rooms at the Natura Park Hotel in the Dominican Republic

This hotel is considered a large resort. With full occupancy, more than 1000 guests can simultaneously live on its territory. When buying a ticket, you can choose one of the following categories of rooms:

  • Standard - small one-room apartments. The bedroom has a large bed and sofa. Area - 30 m 2 .
  • Suite - spacious apartments, which consist of a bedroom and a living room connected by an internal door. Suitable for 4 adults.

For a fee, you can purchase club privileges. These include private check-in, free Wi-Fi and advanced room amenities. Also, upon check-in, guests are handed a large fruit basket and a bottle of sparkling wine. These rooms are equipped with new furniture, bathrobes and slippers, and special hygiene items. Unlike conventional apartments, the bedroom has a plasma TV, coffee maker and a free safe. Drinks at the premium beach bar and 20 minutes of massage per week are also included.

Apartment Amenities

In addition to a set of furniture, which is also made from natural materials, the Hotel Natura Park in the Dominican Republic offers additional equipment. These amenities make your stay as comfortable as possible. So, in the rooms you can see:

  • TV - outdated models are installed in rooms without club privileges;
  • iron and ironing board;
  • air conditioning is central, which means that the degree of cooling is controlled by hotel staff;
  • minibar with soft drinks and local beer - replenished daily;
  • telephone and hairdryer in the bathroom;
  • a kettle and dishes for hot drinks, and daily maids bring tea, coffee, sugar and cream.

Every day, the living rooms are cleaned. Bed linen in the hotel is usually changed after 1 day. Clean towels are available upon request. To change them, you can simply put them on the floor in the bathroom. Then the maid during the cleaning will replace them with new ones.

Hotel Territory

As a rule, due to the distance from the center, tourists rarely leave the territory of the complex. Therefore, the service at the Natura Park Hotel in the Dominican Republic is upscale. It has all the necessary infrastructure for living, namely:

  • computer corner, where high-speed Internet access is provided;
  • parking for cars;
  • Currency exchange at the local and ATM;
  • laundry;
  • beauty saloon;
  • conference room with modern technology, designed for 150 delegates;
  • mini market and souvenir shop;
  • barbershop;
  • gym;
  • A spa with massage services, a sauna and a jacuzzi.

There is a 24-hour reception in the lobby. There, tourists can exchange currency or arrange car rental at any time. Wireless internet access is available for a fee. The administrator can call the therapist upon request in the room. The hotel also has a beach wedding service.

Catering

When buying a ticket, you pay not only for accommodation in the room, but also food. The Hotel Natura Park in the Dominican Republic has only one concept - "All inclusive". It includes an unlimited number of meals throughout the day, as well as soft drinks and spirits of local manufacturers. Wine is served at the main restaurant for lunch and dinner. There is a dress code in the evening, therefore guests in beachwear and sportswear are not served. Imported drinks and a special children's menu are provided for a fee.

All meals are served in the main restaurant. Smoking is strictly prohibited on its territory. In fine weather, an outdoor terrace with sea views also opens.

In addition, tourists can visit 2 more restaurants located on the hotel territory: Italian and fish. Tourists can order drinks, cocktails and alcohol in bars. Two of them are located on the beach. There are also bars in the lobby and poolside.

Entertainment at the Natura Park Hotel in the Dominican Republic

Tourists come to this hotel primarily to relax at sea. Therefore, entertainment here is aimed at relaxing pastime in solitude with nature. The private beach of the Natura Park Hotel in the Dominican Republic is completely sandy. There are no stones, pebbles and other impurities in the sand, so you can safely walk on it even barefoot. There are many sun loungers on the beach, which tourists can use for free. There is also a spacious outdoor pool on site.

So that guests do not get bored during the holidays, a team of animators works daily in the complex. In the afternoon, they organize sporting events, dance classes and Spanish. In the evening, they conduct entertainment shows with live music that tell about the history and culture of the Dominican Republic.

The complex has golf, tennis and beach volleyball courts. For a fee, tourists can ride horses, go diving or take a boat ride on the ocean.

Conditions for accommodating children

For children, the Natura Park Hotel in the Dominican Republic offers a standard set of amenities and entertainment that is consistent with the status of a five-star complex. When parents check in infants, a baby cradle is delivered to the room. In all restaurants you can take chairs for feeding at any time. The complex has children's animators and a mini club for young guests from 2 to 12 years old. They can swim in a shallow compartment in the outdoor pool, and play in the playground.

Positive reviews

Resort Hotel "Natura Park" in the Dominican Republic over the years of its work has earned a good reputation among tourists. Guests are often delighted with the rest here and are willing to return here again with pleasure. In their reviews, they indicate the following advantages of living in this place:

  • a huge tropical park, through which peacocks, flamingos and herons freely walk;
  • friendly and helpful staff, always attentive to the requests of tourists;
  • spacious and bright rooms, which are always very carefully and timely cleaned;
  • a clean beach with snow-white and very soft sand, as well as a clean sea without algae and jellyfish;
  • A friendly team of animators who are always ready to entertain tourists, but never imposed.

Negative reviews

But without the minuses, too, could not have done. Some tourists in the reviews of the Natura Park Hotel in the Dominican Republic note that the complex also has drawbacks. However, its guests, as a rule, call them insignificant. In their opinion, the administration of the complex should pay attention to the following shortcomings:

  • in some rooms it smells damp, and some of the equipment does not work, but at the request of tourists they are immediately transferred to normal apartments;
  • very slow paid internet;
  • rude bartenders who ignore the requests of guests and make them completely different cocktails;
  • lack of meat dishes and monotonous food; there are also no fish and very few fresh fruits on the menu;
  • There are not enough sun loungers on the beach for all tourists.

Thus, this hotel can be recommended for a relaxing and quiet holiday at sea. If you like to spend all your time at the hotel while relaxing, enjoying unity with nature, but this complex is perfect for you.
