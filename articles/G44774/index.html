How to choose a hotel or hotel?

In order not to make a mistake in choosing a hotel or hotel in Naro-Fominsk, you must follow a few rules:

  • Before you go to the hotel, you need to clarify by phone the address and location of the hotel in the city.
  • When choosing a hotel, it is best to clarify whether they can book for a specific time and date.
  • If you plan a vacation with children, it is best to find out from the manager about sleeping places for children.
  • When booking, you need to clarify everything that is included in the room rate. It can be food, bedding and hygiene items.
  • If the hotel is located away from the station or airport, it will be more convenient to immediately find out from the manager whether the hotel provides transfer services.
