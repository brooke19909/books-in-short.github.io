Hotel Iraklis Apartments 3 * (Greece, Crete): photos and reviews of tourists

Do not know where to relax, but there is a desire to see bright night stars, soak up the rays of the gentle sun? Then go to Greece, to the island of Crete. Here everyone can touch and see with their own eyes the ancient culture and civilization, as well as spend time with comfort and preserve indescribable sensations in memory.

Greece is a country in which, as we are used to, everything is there. But seriously - this is the place where the heroes of various legends lived, the gods, and also this is the birthplace of the Olympic Games, olives and the fiery dance of sirtaki.

The pearl of Greece, according to local residents and many tourists, is the island of Crete, which is more than five thousand years old.

Resort of Crete

Want to get a multifaceted range of emotions? Crete is the most suitable place for this. Rest here can be very diverse.

Greece is the embodiment of the dream of any, even the most sophisticated tourist. Each trip is a breath of fresh air. There are so many magnificent places on the island that with a new and new journey you can discover the reduced edge of a tropical corner.

The island has a large number of beaches, as well as numerous mountains and deep gorges.

Crete is divided into parts, there are four in total:

  • Heraklion.
  • Lassithi.
  • Chania.
  • Rethymnon.

And each has its own special infrastructure, architecture and even vegetation. Guests are always amazed how so much of everything fascinating and interesting can be collected on one islet.

In Lassithi, it is advised to stop for those who prefer a beach holiday, loves sunbathing in the sun. There are many hotels, from luxury to simple, inexpensive.

Families with children go to Rethymnon. It has a more temperate climate, gentle slopes and a lot of entertainment.

Heraklion is the center of the island. As is characteristic of this part of Crete, it has gathered numerous bars, restaurants, night clubs, bars, a water park, as well as fountains, cultural monuments and the notorious labyrinth of the Minotaur. Not everyone will risk taking a walk on it, recalling its legendary story.

The most densely populated by vegetation part of the island saturated with outlandish nature is Chania. Here you can see the pink sands and take a walk along the road from the serpentine. This sight certainly will not leave anyone indifferent. It is not recommended to come to the area with small children because of the remoteness from the bus stations and the airport.

All this information will be needed by those who decided to go to Greece, to the island of Crete and stay on vacation at the Iraklis Apartments 3 * hotel, because it is located in the heart of a sunny place, and this makes it possible to visit all of the above places and take a lot of memorable pictures.
