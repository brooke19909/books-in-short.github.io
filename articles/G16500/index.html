Oregon State, USA: photos, attractions, list of cities

Here you can see deserts, steppes and mountains, stunning beauty of the lake and sleeping volcanoes. There are several nature reserves, cave complexes and turbulent rivers. Interesting Facts

The second name of the state of Oregon jokingly consider "Beaver". The reason is that the beaver is the official symbol of the region. The animals are satisfied with the climate of the territory, clean air and the presence of many rivers. And people respect the inventive animals. Beaver can even be seen on the Oregon flag. By the way, the flag is also special: it has two sides.

Another state symbol is labradorite stone. There are deposits of a certain variety, capable of the effect of irisation. In bright light, the surface of the stone casts in different shades, which is especially noticeable after polishing operations. Therefore, the mineral was nicknamed the "sun stone."

Division into zones

By geographical and natural features, several areas can be distinguished. The coast of the ocean and the coastal ridge are in the west. It rains more often, but the climate is milder. Closer to the center, in the southwest, the Klamath ridge rises. In these places there are peaks of 2700 m and higher, amazing forests with a wide variety of tree species, alpine meadows. On the contrary, on the north side, the Willamette Valley is located. This is where most people in the US state of Oregon live. Residents are engaged in winemaking. For visitors, even special tours are organized where you can see how grapes are grown and in what ways they get a drink. Local wine is highly regarded not only in the United States, but also in Europe.

Finally, the last zone is called the High Desert. It has such an unusual name due to the fact that it is located on a small plateau, about 1200 m above sea level.

History

The territory was inhabited by people another 15 thousand years ago. It is known that in the XVI century the tribes of the Indians lived here: the Klamaths, the non-Persians, the bannocks and some others. The non-Persian people still exist, but now their representatives can only be found in Idaho. And Klamath families can still be found in Oregon. This people, by agreement with the Europeans, agreed to move to the reservation for the Indians.

From the 18th century, the first expeditions of the British visited the region. They began to study the land, and Astoria became the first city founded. There was a fort founded by the Pacific Fur Company. Since the XIX century, conflicts began between Indians and Europeans, then between the countries of Great Britain and the USA. The state recognized Oregon in 1859.

During World War II, the territory was somewhat affected by the bombing of Japanese soldiers. As a result, fires occurred in the forest, which were quite successfully extinguished.

State Attractions

Arriving in Oregon, travelers seek to see the Hood volcano, which is the highest point of the state. Its peak is at 3426 m. The panorama of the area leaves a lasting impression. The snowy peak rises above the numerous trees and clear waters of the lakes. There is a possibility that the volcano will still erupt: it is included in the list of potentially active ones. The purpose of visiting most visitors are ski resorts. There are many tracks of varying difficulty on the territory. Attracts the volcano and professional climbers.

There are other attractions in Oregon. You should definitely visit the Pacific coast. Perhaps the most amazing place here is the Torah well.

Formation on the ocean looks like a deep funnel. The water entering there is like a small waterfall, lost in the depths between the stones. This point on the map is a favorite place of all photographers.

Those who wish to visit the wild, to arrange camping in the fresh air, can recommend a trip to the Siskaya mountains. They belong to the Klamath ridge zone. Caves

Scientists from the University of Oregon are engaged in research in the field of archeology. In particular, some archaeological sites are explored, such as Fort Rock Cave. It was here that the oldest shoes on the planet were found. The sandals found in the grotto are about 10 thousand years old! They were made from wormwood, as shown by the results of archaeological analyzes. The cave itself is located in Lake County. There is a similar cave complex near the city of Paisley. A whole reserve with a complex system of underground tunnels can be found near the Siskayu forests. Trails are conveniently equipped for tourists. The numerous groups are accompanied by a guide who tells about the history of the object on the road. When visiting, it is worth paying attention to safety rules and a warning to people with chronic diseases (lungs, heart, and others). Children whose growth is below a certain mark are not allowed to enter the caves.
