Use of tinsel

If you are seriously considering how to make sparkles at home, we can advise you to use sharp scissors and Christmas tinsel. Such small spangles can be made in different colors. Crumble the golden trimmings onto one paper, silver trimmings onto the other; if necessary, cut the colored ones.

Sequins from salt

In the end, we will look at how to make spangles of salt crystals ourselves. For this work, you will need a kilogram of coarse salt. It is advisable to choose a salt such that the crystals are brilliant. To color the salt in different colors, you can use either simple watercolor paints or buy food coloring. You can also use natural dyes, taking the juice of beets or carrots, using brilliant green or potassium permanganate.





For each color, pick up a bag or any other container. Dishes can stain along with salt, so it‚Äôs best to buy tight bags.

Dyes are dissolved in water, watercolor is also prepared by adding water. Then a small amount of coloring liquid is poured into the bag and the bag is thoroughly shaken so that all salt crystals are colored. Then the bag is opened so that there is air access and the salt is dry. After complete drying, the salt can be poured into containers convenient for crafts and used to decorate New Year's toys for the holiday.
