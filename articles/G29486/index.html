The most popular ways to deposit money on Beeline with a credit card

The situation when money runs out on a mobile phone is a lot of concern. In this article we offer 3 ways how you can deposit money from a bank card to Beeline or another operator‚Äôs account without commission and without leaving home.

Payment through Sberbank Online

The opportunity is available for holders of Sberbank bank cards with the activated Internet banking service. You can use the method from a stationary computer or laptop, or from a tablet or smartphone with the Sberbank-Online application installed.

In order to put money on Beeline from a card, you must adhere to the following procedure:

  • Log in to the online banking service. PC users enter the identifier and password on the site. Owners of the mobile version launch the application and confirm the entry with a password.
  • On the left side of the screen, find the item "Top-ups and payments". For the mobile version at the bottom of the application is ‚ÄúPayments‚Äù.
  • In the list that opens, select "Mobile Communications".
  • To simplify the procedure, just find the item "Pay for any mobile phones."
  • In the window that appears, enter the subscriber number, amount and select a payment card (if there are several).
  • You can agree with the decision on the payment by clicking on the appropriate button.
  • A short message containing a password for confirmation will be sent to the attached phone number.
  • In the window that opens, enter the received password digits.
  • We are waiting for the incoming message on the replenishment of the balance.

If this method is optimal, you can set up auto payment. Then you can put money on Beeline with a bank card without your participation. The funds will be debited automatically when the specified balance is reached on the mobile phone.

The whole operation takes several minutes if there is no preventive work on the part of Sberbank.

Payment on the mobile operator‚Äôs website

On the official website you can also put money on a Beeline phone. To do this, find the "Payment Methods" section. In the top menu, select "One-time account replenishment". In the list of services for which you intend to make a payment, highlight "Mobile Communications". The field "Phone number" is intended for entering the numbers of the Beeline subscriber number. In the field "Payment amount", the desired amount is entered in the range from 100 to 15,000 rubles.

In the field "Bank card details" the numbers on the front side of the card, the expiration date, the name and surname of the holder and the CVC2 code on the back are indicated. In the protection field against the bot, enter the characters from the image. We check the box next to "I agree with the conditions" and click on the "Deposit funds" button.

As in the case of Sberbank-Online, an SMS message containing a confirmation code will be sent, which is entered on the page that opens.

Payment through the application "My Beeline"

You can put money on Beeline with a bank card in the mobile application. The procedure is practically no different from the previous version, except for the absence of the need to enter a phone number. The utility on the smartphone or tablet is tied to the installed SIM-card, the data is substituted automatically. An internet connection is required to complete the transaction. If the balance of the number does not allow this, you can connect to a Wi-Fi network at home, in the office or to an open access point in a public place.

Payment via ATM - a case when you have to leave the house

The option is suitable for those subscribers who are deprived of the ability to connect to the Internet or are worried about the safety of bank card data.

It is simpler and more convenient to use ATMs, the network of which belongs to the same bank as the one that issued the card that you plan to use for payment. Third-party banks may charge a fee for conducting a transaction and viewing the balance.

In order to deposit money to Beeline from a bank card using an ATM, it is enough:

  • insert a card into an ATM;
  • enter the PIN code on the keyboard;
  • find the payment section;
  • choose the right operator;
  • enter phone number and amount;
  • confirm the correctness of the entered data.

These 4 methods will allow you to avoid additional costs in the form of payment of commissions charged by other services for the opportunity to put money not only on Beeline from a bank card, but also when paying for services of other operators or making utility bills.
