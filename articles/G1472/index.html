How to extend traffic on Beeline: 3 ways. How to find out the rest of the traffic

More and more smartphone owners are connecting options from their service providers offering unlimited mobile Internet. The famous Beeline in Russia is no exception. However, a careful study of the conditions most often reveals that unlimited traffic at good speed is available within certain limits - 1, 5, 10 GB (depending on the type of option selected). After exceeding this threshold, the speed becomes completely "turtle". In this article, we will look at how to find out the rest of high-speed traffic and how to return to its former speed when using its allowable volume.

Checking traffic with a prepaid system

So, how to find out the traffic on Beeline, if you have chosen a prepaid payment system:

  • Option "Balance control" - an SMS message with information about traffic costs for the current month will come to the gadget. To do this, type the following command: * 110 * 45 #.
  • Option "Financial Report" - providing information about the credit limit, the advance payment made by you and the balance of high-speed Internet traffic in the form of an SMS message. You can receive this message by typing the command: * 110 * 321 #.
  • Information in the mobile application "My Beeline", available for download for all known operating systems. On the main screen you will see the balance of funds on the balance sheet, the amount of consumed and remaining Internet traffic and other useful information.
  • Information in your My Beeline account on the operator‚Äôs official website.




Checking traffic with a postpaid system

How to see traffic on Beeline if you have chosen a postpaid payment system with an operator:

  • The easiest way is a one-time request. To do this, type the command: * 102 #.
  • The ‚ÄúScreen Balance‚Äù service (you can find out whether it is available for your smartphone model by typing * 110 * 902 #) is an option that allows you to always be aware of the rest of the traffic and cash balance. Connection - * 110 * 901 #.
  • Using a mobile application, a personal account on the official website.




Now let's move on to more universal methods.

Other ways to find out Internet traffic on Beeline

You can also find out the rest of the traffic on your smartphone in the following way:

  • Dialing combination * 107 # - SMS with information about the remaining traffic at high speed will come to your phone.
  • Making a call to service number 0611. To get the information you need, you need to wait for a specialist to respond.

The rest of the traffic on the modem and tablet

How to find out the traffic on Beeline if you have chosen a USB modem:

  • Run the modem program, select the "Account Management" section, then "My Data" and "My Balance" - there will be all the traffic information of interest.
  • Call from modem to service number 0611.
  • Personal account on the operator‚Äôs website.




If you have a tablet (including iPad), then go to "Settings", "Cellular data", "SIM-programs." In the last paragraph, select "My Beeline." In this tab, you will need the "My balance" section, where, among other things, you will find information about the remaining traffic.

Option "Extend Speed"

The operator provides the maximum data transfer speed in LTE networks - 73 Mbit / s, in 3G - 14.4 Mbit / s, in EDGE - 236 Kb / s. After you use up the traffic provided by your option, the data transfer speed for your smartphone will drop to 64 Kbps. Now let's move on to information suggesting how to increase traffic on Beeline.





  • The most popular option with the operator is "Extend speed by 1 GB." It allows you to return high-speed traffic in the specified volume for 30 calendar days. The average cost is 100 rubles (you can find out the specific price for your region directly from your operator). Beeline warns that this rescue function is only valid in the home region - when entering the roaming zone you will not be able to use this additional high-speed traffic. How to extend traffic on Beeline here? To connect, you can use the command * 115 * 21 # or service number 06747177.
  • The second more voluminous option - "Prolong the speed of 3 GB." This addition will cost about 200 rubles (you can find out the price for your region on the Beeline official website or in a communication salon). The previous maximum speed will also return to you for 30 days. Like the previous one, this option is valid only in the home region. How to extend traffic on Beeline with its help? Dial the command * 115 * 22 # or call 06747178.

Auto Renew Traffic

Many subscribers consider the service ‚ÄúAuto-renewal of speed‚Äù to be the most convenient for themselves. How to extend traffic on Beeline in this way, you will learn further.

The service operates as follows: when the traffic specified for your option is used up, an additional 200 MB package is automatically connected, and 20 rubles will be debited from your account. After you use this volume, a new package of additional high-speed traffic of the same size will be connected - 200 MB, and your balance will again decrease by 20 rubles, and so on until the new billing month.

The conditions for this additional function are different - if your main option (for example, "Highway") is valid throughout Russia, then "Auto-renew speed" will be available throughout the country, and if it is available only in the home region, then additional high-speed traffic will be available only in it. In international roaming, "Auto-renew speed" is not available.





So, how to extend traffic on Beeline using this service:

  • Connection: short command * 115 * 23 # or a call to the operator number 067471778.
  • Disabling: dial * 115 * 230 # or call the service number 0674717780.

That's all we wanted to tell you about the extension of high-speed traffic when using Beeline. You can always find out more detailed information, as well as prices specifically for your region of residence, in the salons of communication or on the official website of this Russian operator.
