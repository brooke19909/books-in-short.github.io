St. Petersburg - Anapa: how can I get there?

The southern travel destination is especially popular in May-September, during a vacation company. At this time, people go to relax on the sea and see the nature of the south of Russia. One of the most popular destinations during this period is St. Petersburg-Anapa.

Distance between cities

These two Russian cities are located quite far from each other, even by Russian standards. 1736 kilometers - the distance in a straight line (with a possible flight) on the route St. Petersburg-Anapa. The distance by car when traveling on the closest path will be 2186 kilometers. You can overcome this distance by car in about 32 hours (excluding parking).





It is worth calculating how much fuel you have to spend for a one-way trip. Suppose passengers are going to drive a car. We assume that the average fuel consumption per 100 kilometers will be 8 liters. We multiply 8 by 21.86 and we get that for one trip along the route St. Petersburg-Anapa, 175 liters of gasoline will have to be consumed.

An average truck spends 30 liters of fuel per 100 kilometers. Multiplying 30 by 21.86, we will know that 656 liters of fuel will be needed to drive a truck from St. Petersburg to Anapa.





Anapa-Saint-Petersburg: distance by car

We will analyze how you can get from St. Petersburg to Anapa by car. Accordingly, the return trip can be overcome along the same route. We need to go on the road towards the village of Ulyanovka, which is 27 kilometers from the Northern capital. In general, 112 kilometers will have to be traveled across the territory of the Leningrad Region. The following villages are located along the highway: Ushaki, Ryabovo, Lyuban, Trubnikov Bor, Babino. Next begins the stretch of highway located in the Novgorod region. The first settlement to be driven in this area is Chudovo. In the Novgorod region, a motorist will have to drive 218 kilometers to the village of Dobyvalovo. Russia is a big and wide country! The Tver region has always been famous for its hospitality, so any driver, having stopped in the village of Ozerny, will be able to eat and relax. In the Tver region, at the 273rd kilometer of the highway towards Anapa, the last settlement in this direction is located - Zavidovo. The route goes further to the Moscow (244 km), Tula (197 km), Lipetsk (114 km), Voronezh (396 km), Rostov (363 km) regions and the Krasnodar Territory (360 km).





Railway connection

A train runs from the northern capital to Anapa daily. He leaves from the main station of St. Petersburg at 22 hours 11 minutes Moscow time. The length of the route of the train is 2382 kilometers, so it will stay on the road for 44 hours 9 minutes. Arrival at the Anapa railway station on schedule is scheduled for 18 hours and 20 minutes (daily, one day after the departure of the train from the Northern capital). Anapa-Saint Petersburg train also runs daily. By the way, the return flight is a little longer in time, because the train leaves at 22 hours 55 minutes from Anapa, and arrives in St. Petersburg in two days at 22 hours 51 minutes.





Route to Anapa

The first stop after the start of the route is Malaya Vishera station (located 162 km from the main station of St. Petersburg). Train stop - 1 minute. Next, the train goes towards the stop Bologoe-Moskovskoe (about 2 hours drive). After this stop, passengers get off and board the train at Spirovo station. The St. Petersburg-Anapa branded train arrives at the regional center of Tver at 04-18 and costs 1 minute. The next parking lot is Kursk Station in Moscow. After stopping in the capital, the train will proceed towards Tula, where it will arrive in 10 hours 38 minutes of the first day of the journey. Further, the train route will pass through the stations Efremov, Yelets, and at 18 hours 3 minutes the train will arrive in Lipetsk. After this stop, the train will travel a little over 4 hours without parking and will arrive in Voronezh at 22:17. The train will stand for 5 minutes, after which it will head to Liski station (drive a little over an hour). After this stop on the way will be Rossosh station (at 01:17), Chertkovo (at 03:10), Millerovo (at 04:22), Likhaya (at 06:08). The train "St. Petersburg-Anapa" will arrive in Rostov , according to the schedule of the Russian Railways, at 9 hours 12 minutes in the morning. The parking lot will last 28 minutes. The next (and last) train stop is Timashevskaya station (parking time from 12:16 to 12:20).





Anapa-Saint Petersburg: return route

On the way back, this train travels along a slightly different route. On the way from St. Petersburg, this train does not travel through Krasnodar, but from Anapa - it travels. The first stop after the start of the route is Krymskaya station (parking 32 minutes). Then the train will stop at the station of the village of Abinskoye. At 03:17, the train will arrive at Art. Krasnodar-1, and at 03:37 - Krasnodar-2. Then at 03:59 there will be a technical stop lasting 27 minutes. Parking at Myshastovka station will last from 04:48 to 05:32. To a stop at the Rostov train station, the train will follow the stations of Bryukhovetskaya, Kanevskaya, Starominskaya. Then again the train will deviate from the route from St. Petersburg, stopping at Novocherkassk, Shakhtnaya, Sulin, Zverevo. After these stations, the Anapa-St. Petersburg flight (Russian Railways route) will follow the same route as the train from St. Petersburg to Anapa. The difference is that some stops are added. The Anapa-Saint Petersburg train arrives at the final stop at 22:51 on the second day of the road.

Conclusion

The Anapa-St. Petersburg route (railway stretched long ago) is one of the most sought-after trains in the country because it connects the northern and southern parts of the country. Of course, the trip lasts about two days, but still the trip by train will cost passengers much cheaper than traveling by plane.

Tourists love to ride this train, because there is a great opportunity to admire the beauty of Russian nature, its contrasts depending on the region.

The St. Petersburg-Anapa train is the most affordable and most convenient way to get from north to south of the country.
