Dragut Point North Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Bodrum, Turgutreis): description, service, reviews

Bodrum, of course, is one of the largest resorts of the Aegean. However, most often it is visited by Europeans and the Turks themselves. Russian tourists can be met here quite rarely. However, some of our compatriots come here to rest here, of course. Of course, many local hotels including Russian tour operators work with many. The choice of hotels for Russian vacationers here is quite large. For example, many domestic tourists stay in Bodrum at the Dragut Point North Hotel 3 * hotel.

What is a hotel like?

The advantages of this hotel travelers include primarily the fact that it is relatively new. The Dragut Point North Hotel 3 * hotel was built in Bodrum in 2014. That is, furniture, household appliances and decoration are not old here. This hotel accommodates tourists in rooms equipped in one multi-storey building. Of course, this complex has its own territory. The courtyard at the Dragut Point Hotel, according to tourists, is very beautiful and neat. Vacationers in the complex can admire the luxurious tropical trees growing here.





Reception at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. Pets, including service dogs, are not allowed in this complex. The estimated time at the hotel is 14.00. Previously, tourists are usually not allowed into rooms here. At the end of the paid period of stay, hotel guests must leave the rooms at 12 noon. Experienced vacationers recommend that tourists who rest in this complex if they fly home in the morning, be sure to stock up on sandwiches on the last day. The lunchbox in this hotel, unfortunately, is offered to the departing residents very poor.

Contingent

Most of the residents in this hotel are Turks. Here you can also meet Poles, Russian tourists and the British. Sometimes the Dutch check in at the hotel.

Where is the hotel located?

This hotel is located not in Bodrum itself, but in its suburbs - not far from the resort village of Turgutreis. The center of this village is only 1.5 km from the Dragut Point North Hotel 3 *. Bodrum City is approximately 20 km from the complex. Hotel guests can get here by bus in about 30 minutes. Domestic travelers who have rented apartments at this hotel, most often to Bodrum Airport, fly to Turkey . This air hub is located about 50 km from the hotel. That is, the guests of this hotel have to arrive at least an hour on arrival in the country. This, of course, is quite a long time.





Most often, vacationers renting rooms here are delivered by tour operators to the Dragut Point complex. Travelers who book rooms at this hotel on their own can book a shuttle service to Bodrum Airport. This service at Dragut Point costs about $ 75 per adult. In relation to the sea, this complex, according to tourists, is very well located. This hotel was built almost on the beach. Guests can reach the beach from its buildings literally in 2 minutes.

Dragut Point North Hotel 3 *: description of the rented apartments

In total, this hotel offers about 80 rooms to Turgutreis guests. Most of the rooms rented in this hotel belong to the standard class. The hotel also has several comfortable suites. They took care of people with disabilities in this complex. There are rooms specially designed for disabled guests. All rooms for rent in Dragut Point have:

  • TV sets
  • Wi-Fi
  • safes.




Other facilities offered at this property include ironing board and iron. In addition to beds, wardrobes and nightstands, Dragut Point North rooms are equipped with desks. The television at this hotel is cable. Russian tourists can watch four domestic channels here.

What do Bodrum guests think of hotel rooms?

According to vacationers, housing in this hotel is for rent quite tolerable. For those tourists who go to Turkey specifically for the beach and the sea, judging by the reviews, the hotel rooms can fit just perfectly. In any case, in terms of price / quality ratio, the apartments for rent in Dragut Point North are superior, according to vacationers, to the rooms of many other Bodrum complexes.

The pluses of the rooms of this hotel travelers include primarily their cleanliness. The maids at Dragut Point North Hotel 3 * (Bodrum), according to vacationers, work diligent and responsible. They perform their duties simply perfectly. Bed linen and towels in the shower at this hotel staff also changes often enough.





The main drawback of the standards handed over at Dragut Point is tourists consider them to be very small. The showers in the rooms of this class in the hotel, judging by the reviews, are quite tiny. Their dimensions are only 70 x 70 cm. Of course, bathing in such rooms is not even too high and fat tourists are not particularly convenient. Suite rooms in Dragut Point, judging by the reviews, unlike the standards, are quite spacious. And the baths here are equipped with more comfortable ones. In addition to the watering can, in the bathrooms in these rooms there is also a jacuzzi.

Travelers also relate to the hotel's pluses that the safe deposit boxes in the rooms here can be used for free. There are no references to cases of theft at Dragut Point on the Web. However, to be safe, putting valuables under the castle, here, as in any other hotel, will, of course, not be amiss. A serious drawback of the rooms rented at Dragut Point is the absence of balconies here. In the humid climate of the Aegean coast, beach items, unfortunately, dry very slowly. Without a balcony, this process can drag on for hours.

Hotel infrastructure

Living in the Dragut Point North Hotel 3 *, according to travelers, is quite convenient, including because all sorts of amenities are offered to guests here directly on the territory. Many domestic holidaymakers are advised to rent cars in Turkey. Having transport, you can get acquainted with this wonderful country and its rich history much closer, faster and in more detail. Many of our compatriots rent cars in Bodrum, in fact. For such guests, Dragut Point also provides free parking. Bodrum guests can rent, in fact, the car itself directly at the hotel. A car rental point is also functioning here.





This hotel, of course, has its own swimming pool. The hotel also has a small paddling pool. Moreover, it is equipped not directly in the adult pool, as in most three-star Bodrum complexes, but separately. Of course, its guests and laundry can use Dragut Point. If necessary, the residents of the complex directly on its territory are provided with medical services. Of course, animations are held in the courtyard of this recreation center.
