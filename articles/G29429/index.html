The beautiful village of Rybachye. Crimea knows how to surprise

One of the most beautiful pearls of the Black Sea coast is rightfully considered the village of Rybachye (Crimea). Every year he hospitably receives a large number of tourists, many of whom come every holiday season. The local population is very welcoming to vacationers: everyone will receive a warm welcome.

Location

The village of Rybachye (Crimea) is located 30 kilometers from Alushta, on the very coast of the south of the peninsula. It is slightly stretched along the coastal line. Almost through the whole village there is a large central street, on which, starting from lunch, you can watch a lot of different merchants of various goods. Everything is sold here, from swimsuits to barbecue.

How to get there

It is very easy to get to the Rybachye village from Simferopol. Every 10-15 minutes from the stop "Resort", which is located at the railway station of the capital of Crimea, shuttle buses leave. They go along the same highway that leads to Alushta, however, without reaching a bit, turn left.

The road to Rybachye captures with its beauty. From the window you can observe very beautiful mountain landscapes that extend to the horizon.

Almost all the time after turning from the highway on the right side, you can see the sparkling sea and the winding coastline.



From Alushta, Yalta and Feodosia to Rybachy can easily be reached by bus and boat. Tickets are sold at ticket offices of train stations in these cities.

Climate and nature

Holidays in Rybachy (Crimea) are also very good because the climate there is simply magnificent. In summer, in this wonderful place there is practically no rain. The average temperature in July is about 26 degrees.

The village is perfectly closed from all winds by mountain ranges. This unique position allows almost always to avoid the occurrence of storms and keeps the sea calm during the entire holiday season.

The village of Rybachye (Crimea) proudly boasts a very beautiful and picturesque nature. It is located in a mountainous area, and tourists can admire the magnificent landscape.

Housing

All tourists who have chosen the village of Rybachye (Crimea) as their vacation destination, the prices pleasantly surprise. Goods and services have a fairly democratic value.

As for places to stay, here the range of offers and pricing are quite wide and varied. Like the whole resort Crimea, Rybachye (private sector and pensions) has economy, luxury and middle-class housing. You can come here without any trips, reservation and find yourself an affordable home in five minutes.

Prices for housing in the private sector start at 100 rubles per person per day. This price includes only accommodation in a residential building designed for several people. Convenience will be on the street, and you will have to eat yourself. The distance to the sea from such housing is about a kilometer. Depending on the conditions, prices can reach several thousand per day. This amount is already requested not per person, but for cottages and villas located on the seashore, fully furnished with good furniture and equipped with expensive appliances and alarms. Often the owners of such houses themselves offer their cleaning services and cooking.

This contrast is very surprising for tourists who first get to the village of Rybachye (Crimea). Resort boarding houses often offer living conditions far not better than in the private sector, but much more expensive. Sometimes the difference is 200 and 300 percent. Prices for accommodation in boarding houses start at around 190 rubles. This includes the cost of one person per night in a multi-bed room without amenities, but with a balcony, washbasin, TV and fridge. Accommodation for one person in a double room with all amenities will cost 500 rubles per day. Meals are not included in this price. In the dining rooms, breakfast, lunch and dinner in the complex costs about 400 rubles a day.

In the center of the village is a boarding house "Wave". It has a fairly well-groomed territory and comfortable rooms of various classes.

Pension "Mountain" is located 400 m from the sea. He does not have his own dining room, but his guests are provided with meals in a neighboring holiday home.

Pension "Rybachye" is a more modern recreation facility. It is housed in a four-story building and has a very beautiful area, planted with various plants.

Beaches



The resort village of Rybachye (Crimea) has one of the largest beaches on the entire southern coast. It reaches a width of about 50 meters. The beach is completely free. It is strewn with small pebbles.

The seabed is gentle enough, which allows the water to heat up very quickly. This is a big plus for those who come to relax with their children.

The sea in Rybachy is very clean and transparent. There are rarely storms.

Attractions and entertainment

The village has a developed resort infrastructure. Here you can find a large number of restaurants, cafes, bars and other entertainment venues. All of them lure tourists with a cozy atmosphere and deliciously prepared dishes of various cuisines.



There are no historical and natural attractions in the village itself. However, a large number of travel agencies offer their services for the export of people outside the resort town and getting to know the surroundings. Their representatives can be found literally every 100 meters.

You can get acquainted with the proposed excursion programs on advertising stands, where photographs and prices are posted in various directions. Agents are happy to tell tourists the details of the proposed tours, a visit to which will help to remember for a long time the rest in Rybachy (Crimea). There are a large number of possible options, discounts and bonuses.





Of the various attractions that are quite close, tourists prefer to visit the Ghost Valley and Demerdzhi, as well as the very beautiful Jur-Jur Waterfall.

The tours to the Massandra factory are very popular. These tours have various options, including wine tasting.

Vacationers with children often visit the Alushta Dolphinarium.



Loving couples prefer to choose a romantic boat trip to night Yalta.

All tourists who visited the village of Rybachye, speak about the time spent there, very warmly. Many often become regular guests of this resort. Sea, air, nature - all this cannot but leave an unforgettable imprint in the soul of tourists. Many are particularly struck by the sincere goodwill of the locals. Residents of the village really want to please vacationers and make every effort to this. One of the indicators is that in Rybachy, they strictly monitor the order and pay great attention to the issue of the safety of tourists and their property.
