Pattaya, Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort: reviews, ratings, photos

If you decide to go on vacation to the country of smiles in Thailand and are looking for a comfortable place with a high level of service, then the Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya 5 * hotel (Pattaya) can be an excellent option for you . We invite you to find out together what awaits guests here.



Location, general information

The hotel in question is located on the seashore. It has its own beach and water entertainment center. Pattaya Central Street is two kilometers away. Bangkok International Airport is 150 kilometers away.

Centara Grand Mirage was opened in 2009. The hotel is decorated in the style of the film "The Lost World". Its area is 67 thousand square meters. The housing stock and a number of infrastructure facilities are located in two 18-story buildings equipped with high-speed elevators.

In total, the hotel has 555 comfortable deluxe rooms, deluxe family, premium deluxe, spa deluxe, club suite, grand suite, royal suite. All of them are spacious and equipped with everything necessary (furniture, fridge, air conditioning, safe, minibar, set of dishes, hairdryer, bathroom, TV, telephone, etc.). All apartments have sea views.

As for meals, when booking accommodation at the Pattaya Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort you can choose the option that includes breakfast only. The hotel has several restaurants offering both European and Asian cuisine. There are also several bars with a wide selection of ready-made alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as cocktails for every taste.

The hotel has at its disposal its own water park with water slides, attractions and a lazy river. Guests can also visit the spa and fitness center. For children and adolescents, clubs and games rooms are available. Also, in the afternoon and evening, animation in Russian is held here. In a word, even if you do not regularly go on excursions, you will not have to miss the hotel.





Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort (Pattaya) 5 *: reviews

As you know, the hotel during the holidays becomes the second home for tourists. Especially when it comes to a large complex that provides guests with a lot of opportunities for leisure activities. After all, the overall impression of the holiday will depend on how much you will be satisfied with the hotel. Therefore, tourists spend a lot of time collecting information about various hotels. After all, no one wants to make a mistake in choosing and subsequently regret the time and money spent.

An invaluable source of information, according to many experienced travelers, are reviews of former guests of a hotel. After all, they talk about the pros and cons of a particular place, taking into account their own experience. We suggest today to learn about the impressions that our compatriots made at the Pattaya Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort 5 * (Pattaya, Thailand). Just note that the vast majority of travelers were very satisfied with the hotel. This can be judged by his rating, which is 4.7 points out of a maximum of five.





Housing stock

As our compatriots note, the rooms in this hotel will appeal to even the most demanding travelers. So, all apartments here have an original stylish design and are spacious and bright. A huge plus tourists considered the fact that the windows of each room overlook the sea. So you have a luxurious look. The rooms have everything you need - quality furniture, modern appliances, a set of dishes, a hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and much more. The apartments also have spacious balconies with chairs and tables where you can have a good time. Another indisputable advantage of the Pattaya Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort is the presence of free Wi-Fi throughout its territory (including the rooms). Moreover, the connection speed is pretty decent. So you can easily communicate with your friends and relatives who stayed at home through instant messengers, or please them with photos from your vacation.





Cleaning service

Perhaps one of the few moments that caused complaints from some guests of this hotel was caused by the work of the maids. So, some of our compatriots claim that, despite the high level of the hotel, room cleaning sometimes leaves much to be desired. So sometimes vacationers had to complain to the reception manager. By the way, in the event that you notice that the maids do not particularly look into your room, then you should pay attention to whether you accidentally pressed the "do not disturb" button. Indeed, in this case, cleaning in your apartment will not be carried out.

As for the rest of the hotel staff, then vacationers have no complaints about it. On the contrary, according to our compatriots, real professionals work here, foreseeing the wishes of the guests and always ready to help them in any situation. Another advantage, according to tourists, is that a large percentage of hotel workers understand Russian.





Food

The price of accommodation in the Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya 5 * (photo of the hotel can be viewed in this article) includes breakfast only. According to the guests, the choice of dishes is very diverse. So in the morning you can both eat your favorite and familiar foods, and try something new.

You can also have lunch, dinner and refreshments during the day at one of the hotel's restaurants and bars. However, according to many tourists, the prices here are quite high. They cook in excellent restaurants, but the cost of many dishes stopped visitors from visiting such places. So many travelers chose to eat either in cafes and mashinas (mobile food stalls) in the neighborhood of the Centara Grand Mirage, or go to the center of Pattaya. By the way, several times a day a free shuttle is organized from the hotel to one of the shopping and entertainment complexes. Here you can eat cheaply.

Sea

According to experienced tourists, Pattaya Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort belongs to a small group of hotels in Pattaya, located right on the seashore and having their own beach. Moreover, the beach here is quite large, fine sand, equipped with sun loungers and parasols. The places here, according to vacationers, are enough for everyone. Entering shallow water. Waves are not uncommon at sea.
