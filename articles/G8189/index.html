Bone Club SVS Hotel 4 *: reviews, photos

Turkish hotels are in great demand among travelers who want a pleasant and fun vacation on the sea coast. It is no secret that one of the most popular tourist centers is the large city of Alanya. Naturally, there are countless hotels and luxury resort complexes for every taste. One of them is Bone Club SVS Hotel 4 *. This place is famous for its good service, proximity to the sea, decent living conditions and relatively low cost.

So where exactly is the complex located? What does the territory and rooms look like? Can I bring a child with me here? How do other tourists respond about this place? Many people ask similar questions when planning a trip to a particular country.

What does the territory look like?

Many tourists are interested in the appearance of the Bone Club Hotel SVS 4 * hotel. Alanya, Mahmutlar is a picturesque place that combines ancient Turkish traditions and the conveniences of the modern world, so you are provided with the opportunity to admire the colorful landscapes and picturesque city corners.

The hotel owns a rather large private territory (the area is about 10 thousand square meters), on which there are three five-story buildings with living rooms. The yard is reserved for swimming pools, sports fields and terraces for relaxation. There is also a picturesque flowering garden with walking paths. Since the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is large, finding a calm, secluded place will not be difficult.

By the way, this place was built in 1994. And already in 2007, a complete reconstruction was carried out on the territory of the Bone Club Hotel SVS 4 * hotel. Reviews indicate that the hotel has all the conditions necessary for comfort.

How many rooms are there in the hotel?

Bone Club Hotel SVS Hotel 4 * is a medium-sized hotel (by the standards of Turkey). On its territory there are 253 rooms. Living rooms are located in all three buildings.

For the most part, tourists are offered check-in in standard rooms, which are designed for 2-3 people. But, besides this, there are several spacious family rooms, which consist of two separate rooms and are designed for 4-5 people.

Hotel Bone Club Hotel SVS 4 *, Alanya: photo and description of rooms





It is quite normal that for many tourists, especially those traveling with a child, the level of comfort of living is an extremely important issue. What can the hotel offer Bone Club Hotel SVS 4 *? The photo shows that, regardless of the category of the room, they are really spacious, bright and clean. Approximately 90% of the rooms have access to a private balcony furnished with garden furniture - here you can relax while enjoying the beauty of nature and observing the hotel grounds. Depending on the body, the floors are either covered with carpets or ceramic tiles. Naturally, the room has all the necessary furniture for a comfortable stay.

There is a set of household appliances that a modern traveler simply needs. There is a split air conditioning, which is convenient, because in the winter it can be cool in the evenings. You can watch popular satellite channels and even some Russians on TV. There is a telephone, as well as a safe, however, for its use you need to pay extra.

The bathroom is attached to the room - depending on the category and case, it has either a bathtub or a shower cabin. All plumbing in good condition. There is also a toilet, washbasin, mirror and a set of towels, which are changed every day during cleaning or at the request of guests. Bed linen is changed twice a week.

What type of food does the hotel offer?





The all-inclusive scheme is exactly what Turkey most often offers to tourists. Hotel Bone Club Hotel SVS 4 * provides its guests with full three meals a day. Three times a day, guests are invited to the main restaurant, which serves buffet style meals. The reviews indicate that the menu is quite diverse - there are fresh fruits, vegetable salads, several types of meat and slices, traditional Turkish sweets and other dishes of local and international cuisine.

Where else can I eat?

There are also several bars on site. Here you can get chilled drinks and exotic cocktails, try real Turkish coffee, have a light bite and treat your child with homemade ice cream. By the way, tourists are provided with free only local, Turkish-made drinks. Imported products are also available in bars, but you have to pay for them.

The hotel even has a vitamin bar, where for a very reasonable fee you can get freshly squeezed juices and invigorating healthy cocktails. Also, guests are invited to the Turkish restaurant "a la carte", where you need to pre-book a table. There are other cafes, restaurants, pizzerias in the area where you can have a bite to eat at affordable prices.

Beach description





What conditions for a beach holiday can the hotel complex Bone Club Hotel SVS 4 * offer? Alanya is a resort area, which is famous for its clean and warm sea. And this hotel owns its own small section of the beach. Its length is 150 meters, but it is intended solely for the rest of guests - outsiders are not allowed here.

The beach is located just 20 meters from the hotel grounds. Tourists only need to cross the road - for this a safe underground passage has been created here.

The entrance to the sea is completely cleared of stones, although guests are still advised to acquire special shoes. The coast itself is covered with sand and pebbles. On the shore there is a sufficient number of sun loungers and deck chairs, which you can use for free. There is also a small bar where you can get drinks from the refrigerator at any time or order something stronger. By the way, a team of rescuers is working on the shore, who monitor the safety of vacationers.

Water activities for travelers

If you consider yourself to be adherents of a more active type of recreation, then you should not be afraid of boredom on the territory of the Bone Club Hotel 4 *. Traveler reviews indicate that here everyone can find a pleasant pastime.

For example, you can always play volleyball with new friends. There are also plenty of boats, canoes and catamarans that can be rented at an affordable price. On the other hand, this stretch of the sea is not always calm, so you should be careful while swimming. By the way, thanks to the warm wind and soft waves, this coast is very popular among surfers and sailing enthusiasts. Also, tourists can ride a "banana", take a short boat trip, get a dose of adrenaline from a trip on a jet ski or parasailing. There are some attractions for children.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?





Of course, many parents are interested in questions about whether it is possible to bring a child with them. Indeed, for living with children, appropriate conditions are necessary. The hotel complex Bone Club SVS Hotel 4 * is intended primarily for family holidays, so your child will definitely find an interesting lesson.

In terms of amenities, workers bring an extra bed to the room. In the restaurant, kids can count on a high stool for feeding. In the restaurant menu, you will surely find at least a few healthy and tasty dishes. And in the evenings, a special children's corner is organized for children. Here, older children can eat in the company of other children who came to rest.

In terms of entertainment, the hotel also will not disappoint. Of course, for children there is a section in the pool - here they can splash around and play in complete safety. Young tourists also love to spend time on the site with a variety of swings, slides and houses. A mini club is also constantly working, where your child can have fun in the company of educators and animators. By the way, since most of the vacationers are Russian-speaking, difficulties with the adaptation of children are unlikely to arise.

Additional services offered by the hotel





Hotel guests can rely on some services. For example, there is a constantly working ATM and a currency exchange office with a good exchange rate. And guests can use the hotel parking - the staff will even help you rent a transport. There is a small library and a good TV lounge, which will help brighten up leisure time.

Internet access is available, although this service is paid. At affordable prices, you can use the local laundry and dry cleaning services. In case of any health problems, a doctor is urgently called to the room. The hotel also has a spacious conference room, which can be used for meetings, conventions, seminars and even various celebrations.

Turkey, Bone Club Hotel SVS 4 *: entertainment and leisure of guests

The hotel can offer its visitors a lot of entertainment. As already mentioned, in the courtyard there is a large outdoor pool with sunbeds and umbrellas around - here you can relax, swim, sunbathe and just chat with new friends. There are also small water slides that work throughout the day.

Guests have the opportunity to visit the sauna, Turkish bath, relax in the jacuzzi or go for several sessions of wellness massage. There is access to the Internet, but it is paid. In the fitness center, you can work out on simulators or sign up for group exercises, including aerobics, water aerobics, yoga, etc. There is also a video game room, but this service is also charged separately.





The hotel has its own group of animators. The guys will entertain you during the day, arrange interesting performances in the evenings, as well as prompt or even take you to one of the city parties.

And do not forget that Turkey is a country with an incredibly interesting history and ancient traditions. In the tour agency you can pick up and buy a tour to various cities, see a lot of interesting sights and, of course, get to know the oriental culture closer.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

Immediately it is worth saying that the Bone Club Hotel SVS 4 * hotel is primarily focused on a relaxing beach holiday. Most often families with children come here, as there are conditions for living with a child. You can also often meet older travelers. The area is calm, so lovers of noisy nightlife can be a little disappointed. On the other hand, the animators will definitely tell young people where to spend time in the city, when noisy parties are organized in the district, etc.

By the way, newlyweds who have visited the Bone Club SVS Hotel 4 * hotel can count on an exclusive room, a welcome surprise from the hotel and, of course, a lot of secluded places for romantic evenings alone with each other.
