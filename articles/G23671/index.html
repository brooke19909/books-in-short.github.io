Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh, 4 * hotel Rehana Sharm Resort: photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel * 4 Rehana Sharm Resort is located on the coast of Nabq Bay. It is known as an active leisure hotel, experienced instructors are involved in strengthening the health of vacationers. Tourists who are tired of shopping in the shops and shops of Naama Bay, as well as those who do not want to sit on the hotel‚Äôs seat while eating goodies, should at least once buy a tour to this hotel. The cost of the tour for accommodation in a hotel complex in a standard room is approximately $ 350-360 per person.



Tourists travel to this hotel seasonally. A feature of Nabq Bay is its relatively cool climate. Therefore, it is worth going here from May to October. At this time, the climate here is more acceptable than in the rest of the resort of Sharm El Sheikh. The cooling wind that blows in the Nabq Bay, of course, is a blessing in the summer Egyptian heat.

Location

From the airport to the hotel is only 7 kilometers, but the order here is such that check-in occurs only at 14-00. Perhaps for organizational reasons. After all, the hotel * 4 Rehana Sharm Resort is huge. At most 660 guest rooms and suites. This is a second line hotel. Like most of the hotel complexes of Nabq Bay, it was built along Peace Road Avenue and is aimed at family-type vacations.

The hotel complex is located in two- and three-story buildings, forming a semicircle around the pool area.

His signature know-how is a tunnel through which you can safely walk around the territory of the hotel complex.

Infrastructure

The hotel has a 24-hour reception, parking for cars, an Internet cafe, a library. A professional photographer takes catchy shots. And for those wishing to acquire diving skills, a school working at the hotel provides paid, but very useful lessons.



The services of the spa center operating at the Rehana Sharm Resort 4 hotel fit in seamlessly. Reviews mention massages, aqua and stone therapy, a steam bath, a sauna and, of course, a hammam. By the way, it is advisable for newcomers to visit a massage and a Turkish bath. This will prepare the skin (cleanse, open pores, etc.) for subsequent tanning. But after such a procedure, when the skin is cleansed, it is advisable to spend one day without a beach holiday. The latter is necessary for its self-healing.

Animators are actively attracting hotel guests to various types of outdoor activities and physical education (to special fitness programs, classes in the gym, aqua aerobics and water polo in the pool, volleyball, Latin American and belly dance lessons). The Rehana Sharm Resort 4 hotel is remembered by such cheerful, vibrant, active most of the guests. Photos, reviews of these tourists mention the names of positive and funny animators they remember. This indicates the effectiveness of the team organizing leisure activities.

Hotel rooms

Tourists, hotel guests, live in rooms of various types: standard, superior, VIP-room, as well as in suites: junior (improved), VIP and family.

The rooms and suites feature a standard package of furniture and equipment for resort hotels. The rooms are bright and airy. The design is dominated by white and pastel yellow colors. They made a decent repair. Euro-windows - from floor to ceiling provide a wonderful seascape and a view of the pool area.

Air conditioning, minibar, TV, hairdryer, kettle in good condition.



In the bathrooms of the rooms of the guests are equipped with showers, in the suites there are both a shower and a bath.

The furniture in the rooms is working, but not new. A small, non-critical degree of wear is acceptable. Housekeeping, as well as linen and towels are changed daily. Territory * 4 Rehana Sharm Resort is in excellent condition. The pool area blends seamlessly with the landscape design.

Hotel guests here, in the reception area, have the opportunity to sign up for a series of excursions. Among the most popular: Luxor, Cairo and the Giza pyramids, boat trips to the islands.
