Hotel "Parus", Khabarovsk: address, description, reviews

Since 1964 the history of the Parus Hotel in Khabarovsk has its origin. The hotel pleases with its own park, wonderful river views and a convenient location relative to city attractions. This is the first hotel in the city that has passed official qualifications and received a "five-star" mark. The design of the hotel is designed and executed by Italian experts.

The staff of the Parus Hotel in Khabarovsk is constantly expanding. Vacancies of the administrator, waiters, masseur, luggage carriers, maids and others regularly appear on recruiting sites. The hotel needs a proactive and motivated staff to make the travelers' living conditions even better. If you are interested in vacancies at the Parus Hotel in Khabarovsk, please contact the administration for help.

Location

In a beautiful historical area is located the hotel "Parus" in Khabarovsk. The address of the institution is Shevchenko Street, 5. It is about half an hour from the airport and 20 minutes from the railway station.

The following interesting objects are located nearby:

  • Komsomolskaya Square (2 minutes walk).
  • Free Theater and Youth Theater (4 minutes walk).
  • Far Eastern Art Museum (5-minute walk).
  • Regional Museum (7 minutes walk).
  • Archaeological Museum (8-minute walk).
  • Transfiguration Cathedral and Drama Theater (a quarter hour walk).

Hotel Advantages

Hotel "Parus" in Khabarovsk is rightfully considered one of the best in the city. Here is the attraction of this place for travelers:

  • This hotel is chosen by politicians, athletes and show business stars when visiting Khabarovsk.
  • An elegant white piano is installed in the hall, where the best musicians of the city play every evening.
  • For breakfast, among other products, red caviar and champagne are served.
  • Originals and reproductions of works by European artists are exhibited in the lobby.
  • The hall and restaurant are decorated with original compositions from fresh flowers.
  • There is a specialized room, equipped according to the needs of the disabled.
  • For family accommodation, guests are offered connecting rooms interconnected by an internal door.

Catering establishments

Delicious food, quality drinks and a pleasant atmosphere - all this is provided by the catering facilities of the Parus Hotel in Khabarovsk:

  • Restaurant "Sail" is a refined establishment, the interior of which is made in a classic style. The restaurant also has a VIP room for banquets or business lunches. The menu offers European and traditional Russian cuisine.
  • Restaurant "Kabinet" is a cozy restaurant whose interior is made in the best English traditions. The decoration used natural wood, there is a fireplace. The menu features European cuisine.
  • The lobby bar is located in the lobby of the hotel. The institution offers a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks and cocktails. Also here you can always have a cup of tea or coffee.

Special offers

A number of advantageous offers are valid at the Parus Hotel in Khabarovsk. By phone you can always clarify the details and relevance. Here's how to make your stay more profitable:

  • Early booking. When booking a room no later than 60 days before arrival, a 25% discount on accommodation is provided.
  • Long stay. For stays of 7 days or more, a 20% discount is provided.
  • Non-refundable rate. With full payment of the reservation amount without the possibility of cancellation, a discount of 30-35% is provided, depending on the category of the room and the planned length of stay.
  • Weekends. When booking a room from Friday to Monday, a 30% discount is provided.

Helpful information

About the work of this hotel it is useful to know the following:

  • In case of late arrival in the reserved room, the guest will be charged for the day of actual downtime.
  • If you are late for more than a day, the reservation is canceled, and the customer is billed for downtime.
  • Children under 12 years stay free in rooms.
  • All children over 12 years old are charged full.
  • Pets are not allowed.
  • Accommodation is at 14:00, and the checkout time is set at noon.
  • Check-out delay up to 6 hours is paid in the amount of 50% of the room price.
  • A delay of departure of more than 6 hours is paid in the amount of the full cost of the room.
  • With full occupancy of the hotel or the availability of a guaranteed reservation for a specific room, the late eviction service is not provided.
  • It is forbidden to take out dishes from the rooms, restaurant and bar.
  • The administration has the right to refuse accommodation to persons violating the rest of other guests, as well as neglecting hotel rules.
