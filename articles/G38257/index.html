Sochi, the hotel "Mandarin". Photos and reviews of tourists

Before you go to the resort, be sure to decide what kind of vacation you like. Some hotels are designed for youth companies, here the music rattles day and night and the lights sparkle. Others are family oriented, quiet and calm. Most often this is precisely why they come to Sochi. The Mandarin Hotel is an ideal place for you and your family. Here you will find friendly staff, comfort and stunningly beautiful nature. Despite life's problems and various circumstances, here you can fully relax and enjoy a wonderful vacation. Sochi will help to realize the most daring fantasies.



Sochi: hotel location

Even if you have visited dozens of hotels on the sunny coast, you are unlikely to find at least one that is just as convenient. Judging by the reviews of tourists, this is one of the best resorts in Sochi. The Mandarin Hotel is truly universal, absolutely everyone likes it here. It is located very close to the coastal strip, only 270 meters from the sea, which will be convenient, even if your family has small children or elderly people. In their reviews, tourists share their impressions and note that they simply did not meet the best guesthouse. On the one hand, it is located in a stunningly quiet place where it is so good to spend your vacation. On the other hand, the options for entertainment here are just the sea.





Not far from here to the resort town, where tourists will find so many temptations that there will simply be no time to miss, but it should be so, because you are in Sochi. Hotel Mandarin is located just one and a half km from the picturesque city center, in a picturesque suburb - the village of Mamaika, on Landysheva street. However, let's take it in order.

Hotel Description

The first thing you see when you come here is a small neat building, conveniently located on a hillside. Due to this arrangement, a wonderful view of the sea beach opens from the windows and from the observation deck. The territory of the house is immersed in the greenery of various plants, from which it blows with coolness and the smell of flowers. So it should be in Sochi. The Mandarin Hotel is an ideal place to relax, the territory allows you to relax with great comfort, there is a guarded parking lot. Cozy rooms will appeal to even the most demanding tourists. About a five-minute walk is a stop with which you can get into the city center to easily reach the city center.



Services

The price includes a meeting of tourists and their delivery to the place, accommodation in a room, Internet access and the use of gazebos with barbecue facilities. Self-catering, you can cook in the shared kitchen or eat in the many cafes and restaurants of the city. It should be noted that the Mandarin Hotel (Sochi) is very popular among tourists. The reviews emphasize the professionalism of the staff, the cozy atmosphere and the ability to plan your day without reference to the hotel meals. At any time, you can order laundry services or any dishes from cafes or restaurants in the city. Delivery will be made directly to the room.

Hotel Mandarin Adler

Another great place for a family holiday, which is located at ul. Enlightenment 158/2. The distance from the guest house to the sea is only 3 minutes. Perhaps that is why he was so fond of vacationers. You will find comfortable beaches with the possibility of renting sunbeds. But entertainment under the southern sun does not end there. Thrill lovers can ride jet skis and parachute. Near the guest house there are many grocery stores and souvenir shops that tourists love to visit.





There is one wonderful tradition that the Mandarin Hotel adheres to (Sochi, Adler). Food is not included in the package of services, tourists are free to choose for themselves, cook breakfast for them in a first-class and comfortable kitchen, or go to one of the cafes. And sometimes vacationers distribute the duty and prepare breakfast for everyone in turn.

Entertainment

Leisure here depends only on the tastes and wishes of tourists. This is the largest oceanarium and dolphinarium in Russia. For children there is an amazing water park. In addition, guests are invited to a huge network of cafes, bars and restaurants, night clubs. There is an amazing amusement park for adults and children. That is not all. If you like sports, then you are offered a soccer field and tennis courts, sports complexes and health centers, swimming pools and gyms. That is, the "Mandarin" hotel (Sochi, Adler) is just a place to sleep, around the clock life is in full swing, however, it is quiet and comfortable on the hotel grounds.



Mandarin Hotel - 3 * Hotel, Sochi

Until now, we talked about guest houses, small and very comfortable. However, there is also a large three-star hotel, which hospitably opens its doors to its guests. A beautiful five-story building is located at ul. Stanislavsky, d.19. The distance to the center of Adler is 600 meters, to the central district of Sochi 30-40 minutes.





The distance to the city beach is approximately 350 meters, so you have a pleasant walk every time you want to swim.

Vacation with children

Since the hotel is designed for family vacations, holidays with children of any age are allowed. The hotel provides all the conditions for kids. However, parents should remember the need to fill out a spa card for each child with the permission of the local pediatrician. Do not forget to bring your birth certificate with you and warn in advance about the need for additional equipment.

What is included in the service package

It features an outdoor pool, a sun terrace and a beautiful sauna. Each room is equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Each room is equipped with a fridge and is decorated in neutral colors. Bathrooms have a shower. In the dining room you can order any dish or set menu. If you plan to eat only when there is time and desire, then choose any of the nearest cafes, the benefit is only 1 minute walk. Judging by the reviews of tourists, if you are looking for an inexpensive option for a family vacation, then just for you "Mandarin" (3 * hotel, Sochi). Many four-star hotels and hotels offer much less quality rest, while here there are beautiful and fully equipped rooms, polite and helpful staff and everything you need for a great vacation.



Excursions

In order to make the rest more interesting, tourists are offered various entertainment programs and trips. Not an exception and the hotel "Mandarin" (Sochi). Hosta offers you its own tour desk, there are routes of any range and content. They will arrange a trip to Abkhazia, to the New Athos caves or Lake Ritsa. Stunning nature will not leave anyone indifferent. Picturesque places with amazing plants, beautiful lakes, clean air.

However, this is far from all. Want to see the monkey nursery? And to visit a trout farm, to catch and cook fish right on the shore? And nearby is an ostrich farm. A trip to Krasnaya Polyana will take quite a bit of time. This is a magnificent ski resort, huge and luxurious. On the way to it, you can choose a number of excursions - a picnic in the mountains, a visit to a mineral spring or apiary with mountain honey, the opportunity to swim in a mountain lake or in a huge jeep to climb high into the mountains and enjoy the scenery.

Do you think now is enough? Not at all. There are also excursions to Vorontsov caves. These are the largest and most beautiful caves in Russia. In addition, you will find a trip to the Agursky Gorge with waterfalls. Zmeykovsky waterfalls and Mount Akhun are two more opportunities to visit places that are infinitely beautiful.

Tourists reviews

Separately, tourists note the possibility of delivery to the hotel, when you just arrived at the resort, and to the station on the way back, this solves a number of additional problems. The service here is on top, each guest can count on what will be surrounded by attention and care.
