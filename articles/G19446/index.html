Istra Reservoir. How to get to it?

Istra reservoir is characterized by beautiful picturesque nature and unusual shape. Its shores are indented with numerous straits and bays, which give it special interest in the eyes of fishermen. Near the reservoir there are many interesting places, recreation centers and resorts.





Residents of nearby settlements and Muscovites like to come to the Istra reservoir. How to get there and what to do on the shores of this reservoir on a hot summer weekend? Outdoor activities are very popular here. This is fishing, and windsurfing, and kayaking. Everyone here will find something to their liking, and the addition to this will be amazing air and clean water. Recreation facilities on the Istra Reservoir is a great opportunity to change the atmosphere.



The Istra Reservoir has existed since 1935. It was destroyed during the Great Patriotic War. After this, the structures and dams were restored. At the moment, the reservoir supplies Moscow with fresh water. Previously, it was also used for passenger shipping. The Istra Reservoir plays an important role in tourism and outdoor activities for residents of Moscow and the region.

The artificial reservoir is located on the Istra River, in whose honor it is named. In addition, many smaller rivers flow into the reservoir. The maximum depth of this artificial reservoir does not exceed 20 meters. On the reservoir, you can have fun with a large company or the whole family.

A large number of water activities attracts active youth to the Istra reservoir. How to get there and settle down on the shore of the reservoir, it does not matter for them, because the most important thing is how to spend time! One of the most popular activities for outdoor enthusiasts is windsurfing. There are many places on the reservoir where you can master this sport from scratch or recall already mastered elements.





There are several options for how to get to the Istra reservoir. How to get there easier and faster, everyone decides for himself. By car from Moscow you need to move along the Volokolamsk highway to Istra. Then, upon reaching the New Jerusalem Monastery, you need to turn right onto Buzharovo. You need to move along this road until you see the Istra Reservoir. A map of the area will help you navigate and find the right turn. On the shore of the pond, you can easily find a parking place.

Even in the absence of a car, Muscovites will have no trouble visiting the Istra Reservoir. How to get to the place of rest by public transport? The easiest way is the train. From the metro stations Dimitrovskaya, Voikovskaya or the city of Krasnogorsk you need to go towards Volokolamsk to the station Istra. Next, take the bus to the Lamishino stop. After this, it is necessary to overcome on foot a small stretch of the path, about 300 meters, before turning onto a dirt road, at the end of which there will be the long-awaited shore of the reservoir.

There is another option on how to get to the Istra reservoir. How to get to fishing places, many are interested. The best way is to take the train and get off at the Sunflower station. From there, take a bus to the Yakimanka stop. Here you can rent a boat and go fishing for the whole day.
