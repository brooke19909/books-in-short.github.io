Hotel Karon Sea Sands Resort 4 * Phuket, Thailand: photos and reviews of tourists

Karon Sea Sands Resort Spa 4 * is a small and cozy hotel for tourists who want to relax away from the bustling nightlife of Thailand. It offers budget and respectable rooms. The proximity to the beach and the gentle entrance to the sea make it a great option for relaxing with young children. We will tell you in more detail about the hotel itself, its rooms, infrastructure facilities and entertainment, as well as the reviews of previous guests in this article.

Hotel location

The hotel is built on the territory of Karon Beach, which is considered one of the most respectable and densely populated holiday destinations in Phuket. Most of the complexes here are five-star, so the entire local infrastructure is tuned for them. The coast is carefully monitored by numerous employees who promptly clean it from garbage. The length of the beach is about 5 km. At the southern end of Karon Beach is a small barrier reef where tourists dive and snorkel. Near the Karon Sea Sands Resort 4 * hotel there are many restaurants, cafes and bars, and the famous Phuket night market is 500 meters away. Of the island's attractions, the closest to the temple are Suwan Khiri Hat (600 meters) and Big Buddha (3 km). The central part of Phuket can be reached by bus, taxi or tuk tuk . The trip takes about half an hour one way.

Phuket International Airport is 45 km from Karon Beach. You can get to the hotel from here in about 1 hour by taxi, however, this is the most expensive transportation option. When buying a tour, it‚Äôs more profitable to arrange a transfer to the hotel. In this case, a tourist bus will pick up the guests, which will take them directly to their destination. Local minibuses are considered the most budget option , but they make many stops, so the trip time will increase significantly.

Options for posting

The hotel has 89 comfortable rooms. They are large in size and stylishly decorated in a classic style. Most apartments are designed for two adults, to which an additional child can be hooked. However, there are also spacious family rooms. Part of the living quarters has its own small balcony with a dining set. It overlooks a tropical garden or pool. Maids clean the rooms every day. They change bed linen and towels about 2 times a week. Some of the rooms are interconnected by interior doors.

Hotel Karon Sea Sands Resort 4 * (Phuket, Karon) has prepared the following accommodation options for guests:

  • Deluxe Room - a double room with a king-size bed. It is possible to install an extra bed for a child. Its area is 38 square meters. m
  • Pool Side Access Room - a room similar to the previous version. Its main difference is the presence of its own separate exit to the common pool.
  • Family Suite - spacious family apartments, which consist of two bedrooms and a living room. There is a small kitchen with a dining table and chairs. Designed to accommodate four adult guests. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãall premises is 140 square meters. m




Room Amenities

In addition to the standard set of furniture, each hotel room is equipped with a number of amenities to make your stay here as comfortable as possible. To maintain a cool temperature, the buildings have a common central air conditioning, which is regulated by the staff of the Karon Sea Sands Resort 4 * hotel (Phuket). Each room has a private bathroom with a shower, sink and toilet. There is also a built-in hairdryer. Superior apartments offer bathrobes and slippers. Wireless internet is available for each room. The first hour of using it every day is free.





A small TV is usually located opposite the bed. Some rooms have not been renovated yet, so you can find old picture tubes in them. High quality satellite TV is connected to them. The self-catering apartments come with an electric kettle, fridge and coffee maker. All rooms have coffee or tea making facilities. You can leave your documents, money and other valuables in a small safe. For a fee, guests can also ask for a minibar with alcohol and soft drinks in their room, as well as a telephone with direct international access.

Hotel infrastructure

Developed tourist infrastructure offers travelers Phuket. Karon Sea Sands Resort also provides high-quality service. The entire hotel is under round-the-clock security, and by the pool there is an experienced team of lifeguards. Reception is open 24 hours a day. Here tourists can exchange currency, leave bags and suitcases in a luggage storage room, call a taxi or buy tickets. There is an ATM in the lobby where you can cash out cash at any time . Wireless internet is available throughout the hotel, but access is available for a fee. If necessary, tourists can call a doctor to their room, but his services are not included in the price.





The complex has a private car park offering 30 parking spaces. Guests can rent a paid meeting room located in the business center. Its capacity is 15 seats. Laundry facilities are available. There is also a small souvenir shop in the lobby.

Beach and Pool

There is no private beach area at Karon Sea Sands Resort Spa. Tourists resting here visit the city coast of Karon Beach, which is located 100 meters from the hotel. To get to the sea, tourists need to cross the road. Entrance to the beach is free, however, sunbeds and towels are subject to a charge. It is worth noting that Karon Beach is famous for its clean and soft sand, which crunches underfoot due to the high content of quartz. On the beach for tourists, water activities are available, for example, diving, volleyball, snorkelling, sailing.





The hotel also has its own outdoor pool with a sun terrace. It has no heating, so it only works in good weather. Sun loungers, mattresses and beach towels are provided free of charge.

Sports and Entertainment

You can have fun at the Karon Sea Sands Resort Spa. For sports lovers there is a spacious gym equipped with new treadmills. Several times a week, group classes in yoga and water aerobics are held. Near the hotel, tourists can play table and classic tennis, go to the billiards room or rent a walking bike.

It is worth noting that the hotel has its own spa. In it, tourists can relax by attending classic and Thai massage courses. The center also has a sauna, steam room and jacuzzi. Outside the hotel there is a golf course, night club and local library, which can be visited for a fee.

Catering

When buying a tour, tourists can choose one of two available catering systems: only breakfast or half board. As a rule, residents of the Karon Sea Sands Resort complex prefer lunch and dinner outside the hotel. Along the coast there are a huge number of restaurants and bars where you can try not only Thai cuisine, but also fish and seafood dishes. Breakfast is served at Tom Yam Kung, the main Thai restaurant. An extensive buffet is available for guests. There is a spacious outdoor terrace located next to the pool.





The √† la carte restaurant, Navrang Mahal Indian Rest, serves Indian dishes. There is a bar by the pool where tourists can enjoy a cocktail without leaving the water. There is a small cafe in the lobby, accommodating up to 15 guests, where you can have a bite to eat freshly baked pastries with hot drinks.

Conditions for relaxing with children

Many tourists come to Karon Sea Sands Resort with small children. Free accommodation is provided for them if they do not occupy an extra bed. Children under 12 years old may be offered a discount. Cots and English babysitting services are available for a fee. The restaurants have high chairs for feeding.

The shared pool has a shallow compartment for children. Other entertainments for little guests are not provided.





Karon Sea Sands Resort 4 *: positive reviews

It is worth saying that many tourists liked the rest at this hotel. They note that his photographs are fully true. In addition, guests were impressed by the local service at an inexpensive cost of living. In their reviews, they write that they would like to come here again. In their opinion, the complex has the following advantages:

  • A clean pool with warm water, in which there are several compartments with different depths (maximum - 1.60 meters). It has enough space for all guests.
  • Great location. The hotel is close to the beach, restaurants and shops. Nearby are five-star complexes that look very beautiful.
  • Spacious and clean rooms. Employees are thoroughly cleaned and without tip. Tourists note that there are no insects in the premises, although they recommend closing windows because of mosquitoes at night.
  • Friendly staff who speaks good English, which is quite rare in Thailand.
  • Good breakfasts that match the stated category.




Karon Sea Sands Resort: negative reviews

However, some tourists noted that for all the above advantages, the hotel also has disadvantages, although for most guests they seemed insignificant. In their reviews, guests indicate the following shortcomings of the local service:

  • Low speed internet. At the same time, it is constantly disconnected and requires re-entering the login and password from the network.
  • The road that separates the Karon Sea Sands Resort from the beach is constantly overloaded with cars, so you have to wait a long time to cross it.
  • Monotonous breakfasts. They serve stale pastries, there is no large selection of fresh vegetables and fruits.
  • On the poolside terrace, not everyone has enough sun loungers. Because of this, many tourists prefer to occupy them in the early morning.
  • Overpriced at restaurants, bars and spa . You can visit similar places near the hotel 2 times cheaper.

To summarize

The small Karon Sea Sands Resort is suitable for tourists who prefer a relaxing beach holiday away from nightclubs and noisy establishments. Low cost of living, a beach with quartz sand and a large number of positive reviews attract many travelers. Most often, adult couples with small children, elderly people and young people who are tired of Thailand's loud nightclubs come here.
