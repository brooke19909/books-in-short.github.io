How to make a vegetable suit with your own hands?

New Year's holidays are the most long-awaited and magical. Together with them comes the time of carnivals and special children's fun, which is simply impossible to imagine without gifts and all kinds of interesting costumes.

Where is it better to take the New Year's outfit: buy some hare ears or is it better to make a vegetable costume with your own hands? The second option will be much cheaper, and the child will be able to participate in the process, thereby making him even more interested in the upcoming celebration. Moreover, this fascinating activity will be a joy not only for the child, but also the mother will be able to receive a huge amount of positive emotions.



It is not difficult to make a vegetable suit with your own hands, you just need to turn on imagination and ingenuity. Perhaps the main problem here will be the choice of a huge number of ideas. This can be a mushroom-amanita, cucumber, pepper, tomato, eggplant, beets, carrots and even potatoes with sprouts.

So what should the costume consist of? The main clothing is a dress for a girl and panties with a blouse for a boy, an interesting themed hat and all kinds of boots can be an accessory. Children's costumes of vegetables can be made by sewing decorative elements and rain on ordinary clothes or sew the base yourself.



For example, a suit of cucumber. Fleece sweaters or fabric from an old green coat are suitable for him. To give the costume an appropriate look, you need to make a blank of green dense fabric in the shape of a cucumber. To keep it in shape, the fabric must be duplicated with thin foam rubber. Incidentally, it is best for them to form suits of vegetables. The width of the workpiece should correspond to the volume of the child. Holes are cut for the feet so that the baby can walk. At the top, you can make slits for the hands and face or fix the suit on the shoulders. In this case, the costume will organically look with a hat in the shape of a cucumber nose or with green twisted tendrils and a leaf.



To make a vegetable costume with your own hands, any thing unnecessary in the house can be used. For example, an old red stretched sweater is a great solution for a suit of tomato or pepper! Sintepon or foam rubber will help to give the desired shape to the tomato. It will be necessary to conjure a little with the bottom of the sweater, bend it and flash it, and then insert the elastic is not at all difficult. And from the sleeves will come out excellent leggings that complement the image. The finishing touch is green leaves on the neck or head of the baby.

By the same principle as a cucumber suit, it is easy to make an image of peas. To do this, an insert with circles or green rag balls is made on the chest. Need a corn suit? Please - the top of the cucumber blank is made of yellow fabric, and sheets of fabric filled with already known material are sewn in a circle.



It is not at all difficult to make a vegetable suit with your own hands, and you can see this by getting down to business. You just need to prepare for the mass of fun moments in the manufacture, fitting and a huge amount of positive at the end of the work. And if you involve a child in the process, then positive emotions, intertwined with sincere childish delight, are guaranteed.
