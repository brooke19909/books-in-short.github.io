What can be made of small balls of multi-colored threads? Knitting a scarf will not work, because the quality and thickness of the yarn for him is completely different. For napkins, the yarn is somewhat thicker, for socks it is too small ... And it is still very a pity to throw out a whole box of residues. After all, the threads are good, but such a quantity even for the decoration of a new sweater is not enough. There is the simplest and most creative solution. Try crocheting toys for kids. What accessories will be needed to crochet toys for children? Of course, yarn. The most different. Not necessarily fat or the same. You can use wool or synthetics, acrylic or roving. Everything, even the smallest balls, can easily be turned into penguins, bunnies, fish ...

Hooks You will need several of them for different thicknesses of threads and the performance of a variety of parts.

Needles for stitching future toys. Having connected several details, it will be necessary to sew them together to make a kitten or elephant. Even if the toy is connected in a circle, you will need to sew a hole through which cotton wool or other filler was placed inside.

Mouline thread for creating faces in dolls, claws in a tiger or mustache in a dog. You can also use commercially available toy eyes.



Crochet toys. Do you need schemes or not?

Usually, having come up with or drawing a future toy, the craftswoman does not think about where to find the scheme for creating the next masterpiece. Therefore, it is safe to say that it is not necessary to spend a lot of time searching for a description of the technology. Draw what you plan to do. And then, having chosen the threads, just start crocheting toys using your imagination, beautiful yarn and skill! But for beginner needlewomen at the first stage, a scheme may be needed.



We crochet toys. Description of work

Having decided to create a toy with your own hands, you should draw it on a piece of paper in full size. For the first work, choose a simpler model to practice. If the image of objects presents certain difficulties, then use a piece of paper in a box. This will significantly save time and facilitate the process. Then, choosing the right thread, begin to knit parts. Legs and torso are best made in the form of cylinders. Do not forget to leave a place to fill the toy.

If crocheting is not a problem for you, then you can do, for example, a penguin. Simple enough in execution, it is very effective due to the combination of two colors of yarn. Tie the body in the form of a cylinder expanding downward. Then tie the head, add a beak of red yarn, as well as small black wings. After filling the penguin, sew all the details. On your head, either embroider eyes using thread floss, or sew ready-made ones bought in a store. Check that knots and threads do not stick out. That's it, the penguin is ready! Crocheting toys is not only easy, but also very exciting!
