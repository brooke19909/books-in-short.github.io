Feng Shui Resort & Spa 3 * (Vietnam / Hamthunamnam): photos, reviews

Resort Feng Shui Resort & Spa 3 * will give its guests unforgettable moments of relaxation, as well as help you get to know the distinctive and unusual culture of Vietnam.





Brief information about the hotel

Resort Feng Shui Resort & Spa 3 * - this is perhaps the best place for those tourists who want to get to know Vietnam as closely as possible and explore all its features. This hotel is located in an incredibly picturesque place, surrounded by many natural beauties. It is thanks to such a location that the windows of the rooms offer a simply bewitching view.

In the Feng Shui Resort & Spa 3 * hotel you will find many opportunities for a good rest and entertainment. A special pride of this resort can be considered its spa, where both traditional procedures and very unusual sessions using original Vietnamese techniques are carried out, thanks to which you will feel several years younger.

Feng Shui Resort & Spa 3 * offers you to stay in comfortable, spacious and bright rooms, in the equipment and interior of which you can trace the traditional Vietnamese style. Also of great interest are restaurants that will introduce you to local cuisine, as well as allow you to taste international dishes and original masterpieces from cooks.





Hotel Location

Resort Feng Shui Resort & Spa 3 * (Vietnam) is located in Phan Thiet. Around the hotel are many natural and architectural attractions. So, about 2 km from this place is the famous Taku Mountain, with which many legends and legends are associated. Hamthuan Bay is just around the corner, and Kega Lighthouse is 7 km away. After 20 km, you will reach the city center and enjoy the Teithim Palace. But to plunge into the Bintiau thermal spring, you have to drive as much as 80 km.

Hotel Rooms

All apartments of the Feng Shui Resort & Spa 3 * hotel are decorated in a traditional Vietnamese manner. Photos taken by tourists demonstrate how skillfully and harmoniously combines laconic simplicity with the luxury of wooden carved elements. Speaking of guest accommodation options, the following room types can be distinguished:

  • Deluxe is a comfortable room, which is a large and spacious bedroom. There is also a family modification of these apartments, which is much larger in area and consists of two similar rooms.
  • The hotel offers accommodation in small, cozy bungalows. However, some of them are among the green spaces of the garden, while others are in close proximity to the ocean. These rooms are designed to accommodate two guests and are just perfect for a secluded romantic getaway.




Room features

Each guest room at Feng Shui Resort and Spa has the following amenities:

  • air conditioning, thanks to which your apartment will always have such a microclimate as you want;
  • modern TV, which broadcasts a huge package of cable channels, among which there are also Russian-speaking;
  • the minibar is filled for a fee, but nevertheless two bottles of drinking water are provided daily for the cost of the tour;
  • the bathroom is equipped with a toilet, a large spa bath, as well as a hairdryer, all the necessary bath accessories and a large set of disposable cosmetics.




Infrastructure facilities

Hotel Feng Shui Resort & Spa 3 * (Phan Thiet) boasts a rich resort infrastructure, which includes the following list of objects:

  • the reception can be considered the heart of this hotel, because it works round the clock so that you can solve your questions and problems at any time;
  • There is a small parking zone near the hotel, where under your guard and video surveillance your personal or rented car will be located;
  • laundry staff will help you quickly remove stains from your items and iron them;
  • in the conference room you can conduct business negotiations and seminars, because there is all the necessary equipment and furniture for this;
  • in the business center you will have the opportunity to use computer equipment and the Internet;
  • throughout the resort, including rooms, guests have access to wireless Internet;
  • in the courtyard there is a clean pool with fresh water, around which there are many sun loungers and sunshades;
  • Be sure to use the services of the spa, after which you will feel much younger;
  • the restaurant is responsible for hearty and wholesome meals, which serves not only traditional Vietnamese, but also international dishes;
  • in the bars you can find a huge amount of drinks, as well as snacks and light salads;
  • not far from the hotel there is a large and well-groomed sandy beach.




Hotel Features

The hotel has a number of rules that govern accommodation in it:

  • it is worth remembering that check-in and check-out at this hotel are carried out respectively after 14:00 and strictly before noon;
  • children under 12 years old, subject to their existing beds, stay at the hotel free of charge, while breakfast is charged separately;
  • if you need an extra bed for an adult or a child, then for this service every day will be charged $ 15;
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring pets to the hotel;
  • In this hotel you can use a credit card to pay for the services provided.

Positive reviews

In view of the budget prices and quite comfortable living conditions, many tourists prefer the Feng Shui Resort & Spa. Reviews about it allow you to make a fairly positive picture, which consists of the following comments:

  • good transport interchange, which allows you to independently travel anywhere in the city and explore the sights;
  • very tasty food in the restaurant, if ordered on the menu (and the prices are relatively low);
  • a very beautiful sea, although uneasy (but this problem can be solved by swimming in the pool);
  • if you go on an early excursion and you don‚Äôt have access to breakfast, then you are provided with a lunch box (you must first inform the reception about such a need);
  • good rooms - quite spacious and very interestingly decorated in accordance with local interior traditions;
  • a large area where there is a lot of greenery and interesting decorative elements (there is where to take a walk and take interesting photos as a keepsake).




Negative reviews

Despite the general positive impression caused by budget prices and a wide range of services, a number of negative reviews, as well as comments and complaints, can be distinguished from the comments of the guests of this hotel.

  • in the hotel rooms there is almost always no hot water, so tourists have to wash themselves with ice;
  • housekeeping leaves much to be desired: there is a lot of dust left, and it also seems that the floors here simply never wash;
  • Despite the fact that the hotel is characterized by the problem of insects and parasites due to the large number of green spaces, no one even thinks about special treatment and disinfection;
  • the hotel staff does not speak Russian, but speaks English superficially;
  • the hotel is very far from the airport - you need to get almost six hours;
  • a very modest breakfast menu (not only are the dishes monotonous, they are also not very tasty).
