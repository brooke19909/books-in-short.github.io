A1000 mobile PC is an excellent Lenovo tablet. Reviews, technical specifications and other specifications.

A1000 is an excellent and inexpensive Lenovo tablet. Reviews, technical specifications and other characteristics given in the framework of this review will allow you to decide on the need to purchase such a device. From the standpoint of technical equipment, it has no analogues in its class. At the same time, he has practically no shortcomings. All this in total makes his purchase more than justified.





CPU

Lenovo A1000 tablet is built on the basis of ARM - processor from the Chinese company MediaTek. In this case, we are talking about MT8317. It is built on the basis of 2 Cortex-A9 revision cores operating at a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz. Its processing power will be enough to solve most problems today. It perfectly copes with both toys and watching videos or web pages.





Graphics

The graphics subsystem also characterizes the Lenovo tablet from the best side. The TN 7-inch screen will delight you with excellent color reproduction. Its resolution is 1024 x 600 pixels. Maximum supported 2 touches. The type of sensor installed is capacitive. To ensure the proper level of performance from a graphics standpoint, the device is equipped with the PowerVR SGX531 accelerator. All of the above allows you to confidently say that this tablet will perfectly cope with any task.

Memory

Another strong point of this device is the memory subsystem. It has 1 GB of RAM standard DDR3. The integrated memory in it is 16 GB. This allows you to actively use such a Lenovo tablet without a memory card . Reviews of most owners of this device confirm this . But if this is not enough, then you can install a micro SD card up to 32 GB in size.

Other parameters

Among communications, there is support for Bluetooth, Wi-Fi and USB. As a bonus, a GPS transmitter is installed. But the GSM or 3G module is missing. But taking into account the price, this is already not so scary. The second serious drawback is the glossy finish, which, again, is offset by the democratic cost of the gadget. Two installed speakers provide impeccable sound quality. The 3500 mAh battery provides up to 8 hours of battery life. This indicator also compares favorably with the competitors of this Lenovo tablet. Reviews of admired owners on the Internet confirm this. In this case, the weight of the device is only 340 grams. It can be held without problems in one hand, and the second can perform the necessary manipulations on the screen. To connect external speakers there is a 3.5 mm audio jack. For video communications, a webcam is located on the front panel. It will allow you to communicate via Skype without any problems.





Summary

A1000 is an excellent budget Lenovo tablet. The reviews, technical specifications and other characteristics given within the framework of this material allow us to say with confidence that this is a great solution for beginners and not very demanding users. Its processing power and the amount of installed memory is enough to solve most problems: starting from toys and ending with browsing websites, it can handle everything without problems. The disadvantage of this device is the lack of a built-in 3G module. But taking into account positioning and cost, this cannot be called a minus.
