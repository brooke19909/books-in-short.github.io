Hotel Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort 5 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): photos and reviews of tourists

The chic hotel of the famous Hilton chain in Sharm enjoys unique success among tourists. Despite the fact that this institution is not directly on the sea, Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort 5 * has earned very high marks from vacationers. Its rating is one of the best among charm hotels. Even those tourists who thought that one could not even dream of a five-star service in Egypt were pleasantly surprised by their stay at this hotel.



El Salam

The area where the Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort 5 * is located, reviews of 2015 were called the most comfortable and safe in Egypt. Residents of hotels located near Naama Bay never get bored. Cafes and restaurants, hookahs and shops, nightclubs and discos, supermarkets and shopping malls - here you can walk endlessly when you get tired of sunbathing, swimming and watching the animation.

The promenade Naama Bay, which is located in the El Salam district, is a modern, never-sleeping street, lit with neon signs and sparkling lights. Of the local restaurants, regulars recommend the Panorama cafe with a breathtaking view and oriental dances, Tam-tam near Gazaly (famous for its inexpensive prices), as well as Dannanier. If you want to go on boats or yachts, then go to the pier Iberotel Lido. Of the clubs, tourists strongly recommend Le Pasha and Hard Rock Cafe. But they say that they are already losing in popularity to the new Taj Mahal. Naama Bay is Sharma's most prestigious resort, and you will appreciate it when you settle in the Hilton.

Territory

Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort 5 * hotel is located in the middle of a magnificent park. Its territory is huge, but at the same time very well-groomed. The hotel buildings are scattered throughout the park, and therefore from distant buildings it takes quite a long walk to both the restaurant and the beach. Everywhere trees and shrubs, lawns and lawns with green grass, beautiful paths. The hotel has parking, an exchange office. Own shops are available on site. For convenience, between the buildings, in the park and the restaurant you can ride on special cars. They also bring luggage to rooms. When checking in, they give out a card so that guests do not get lost, and tell what is located.

Room categories and standard of living

Tourists claim that staying at the Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort 5 * hotel is a pleasure. Almost four hundred rooms are divided into several types. Even the most standard twin class have panoramic windows. From some you can see the Sinai Mountains. There are rooms with balconies or patios. Almost all junior suites are built so that they offer a mountain landscape or a hotel park. They have a living room where there is a folding sofa, a dining room, two bathrooms.

So-called guest rooms are a bit simpler. They are designed like junior suites, but have only one bathroom. King rooms have king size beds and there is a lounge but no dining room. In any room there are all the benefits of civilization: iron, ironing board, cups, sets for coffee and tea, a huge plasma TV. On the balconies and terraces - chairs and a table. The rooms are very spacious and there is where to put things. Solid furniture. Cleaned every day and give a bottle of water. If not enough, you can take extra at any restaurant. The bracelet is not cut off during eviction, and you can use the bar and restaurant before transfer to the airport.

In the Hilton Sharm Dreams Resort 5 * hotel a lot of attention is paid to sports. Here is a good tennis court, volleyball court. You can ride horses and camels. A very good hotel for children: a mini club, a safe and modern playground, a menu in restaurants and cribs in rooms - all this is provided for your child. Great animation for kids works almost all day. Children really like her.

The staff is friendly. Workers are smiling, greeting, and are constantly interested in how your vacation is going. All arising issues are resolved very quickly.
