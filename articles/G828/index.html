King Saron Hotel 4 * (Greece, Peloponnese): description of rooms, services, reviews

King Saron Hotel 4 * is a hotel complex, perfect for spending your holidays in it. And this can be stated with all confidence, since there are many reasons for this. However, all of them should be described in more detail.





Location

King Saron Hotel 4 * is located in a place called Isthmiya. This is a resort village of small size, which is located on a narrow isthmus that connects the mainland of Greece with the Peloponnesian Peninsula. Thanks to this feature, vacationers can swim not only in the Aegean Sea, but also in the Ionian. By the way, according to the legends, it was here that the sanctuary of Poseidon was located. The ruins of the temple are still preserved.

King Saron Hotel 4 * itself is located a minute's walk from a clean sandy beach. It takes about an hour to get to the airport from the hotel. The closest is Eleftherios Venizelos. By the way, the largest in all of Greece. It is located in Athens, and the capital is 80 kilometers from Isthma.

Another 5 minutes drive from the hotel to the thermal resort of Loutraki and about 10 minutes. to Ancient Corinth. In general, to all the sights - how close at hand.

The hotel itself is surrounded by tall, lush green trees and fragrant gardens.

On the territory of King Saron Hotel 4 * there are many different ways to brighten up your leisure time. You can swim in the outdoor pool or sunbathe on the terrace.
