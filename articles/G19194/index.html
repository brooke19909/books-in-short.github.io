The best hotels in Perm: photos and reviews of tourists

Today in Perm city hotels are increasingly being rebuilt. Perm at the moment offers its guests more than 120 hotels. Some of them are located in buildings built during the Soviet period. Others are modern, fashionable and stylish hotels.

A varied and hearty breakfast buffet is a good start to the day. Head to the cozy bar where you can chat with your neighbors and have a good time.

Hotel rooms are a great place to gain strength and relax after a hard day. Perm is also known for its hotel saunas. The Ural Hotel, in addition to the bathhouse, also has a fitness center. Guests can request for a massage. You will be offered a wide range of health and beauty treatments.

Hotel Travel

Considering the mini-hotels of Perm, it is worth highlighting the Travel hotel, which is located in the very center of the city, not far from the embankment of the river. Kama. Nearby is the Meshkov House Museum, Reshetnikov Square and the Academic Theater. The train station is 4.5 km away.



In the mornings, guests are offered a free rich breakfast served in the rooms. Lunch and dinner are available at nearby restaurants and cafes.

Due to the convenient location of this Perm hotel, it is easy to get to various attractions from here, and in the evening there is the opportunity to stroll along the promenade. The hotel has a tour desk where you can book a tour to a place of interest to you.

The friendly and experienced staff will help you arrange transfers to the train station and airport, as well as booking tickets. Free Wi-Fi helps keep you in touch with business partners and friends.

Eva City Hotel

This hotel in Perm has a very favorable location - not far from the center.

The price includes breakfast served in the room when it is convenient for guests. On the territory of this hotel Perm provides access to free internet. People who come here by car can leave their car in the hotel parking lot. A shuttle service is also available. There is a 24-hour reception desk.

Laundry facilities are also available. No smoking at the hotel.

The hotel has only ten comfortable rooms, each of which has a TV, private bathroom, minibar, wardrobe.

Hotel "Prikamye"

Hotel Prikamye is located in the very center of the city, not far from the art gallery. The train station is a seven-minute drive away, and the airport can be reached in half an hour. Walk to the promenade in about ten minutes.



The Prikamye Hotel on the second floor has a cafe where you can have a tasty and satisfying meal and drink aromatic coffee. At your service on the ground floor is the Irish Bar Seamus.

Guests accustomed to staying fit even while traveling are invited to visit the gym. In addition, this hotel in Perm has a hairdresser.

The 24-hour reception staff will advise you on excursions, book a taxi or arrange a shuttle service. Near the hotel there is parking.

For convenience, a safe and luggage storage are provided. Free internet is available on site.

Hotel complex "Pearl"

This modern hotel complex is located in the city center, near the circus, the Perm Fair complex, the Young Spectator Theater, Zvezda and Dynamo stadiums, as well as the Molot Sports Palace. Nearby is the river station, 4 km from the railway station, 18 km from the Bolshoye Savino airport.



The elegant restaurant of this complex offers a huge selection of dishes of European and Russian cuisine.

Go to the wellness center of this hotel in Perm - it has an indoor pool, sauna and gym. Those who wish can also play bowling.

It also features a beauty salon, secure parking and a currency exchange.

Hilton Garden Inn Perm

This four-star hotel in Perm has a very good location: it is a 10-minute drive from the city center and 15 minutes from the airport. Near the hotel you can find a huge amount of entertainment, including the Kinomax multiplex, the Olympia sports complex, Planet Bowling, and the Stolitsa shopping center.

This hotel is an excellent choice for business trips and leisure. Here you can taste dishes of European and Russian cuisine. In summer, you can enjoy your meal while sitting on the terrace. In the relaxing atmosphere of the lounge bar, you can chat with your friends while sipping your favorite drink.

If you need to relax, go to the Turkish bath or go for a Thai massage. People who like outdoor activities can visit the gym.

You can connect to high-speed free Wi-Fi anywhere in the hotel. Everything you might need on a trip can be bought at a convenience store located here. You can park your car in a guarded free car park.

Hotel City Star

City Star is a hotel located in Perm near the banks of the Kama. A stone's throw from the hotel is the Ural Volunteer Square and the State Art Gallery. From the train station, the journey takes 10 minutes by car.



Hotel guests are always welcome in a very cozy restaurant. In this place you can order delicious dishes of European and Russian cuisine from the menu.

To relax after a busy day, book a massage. You can renew your manicure or correct your hair in a beauty salon. To stay in touch with relatives and friends, use the free Wi-Fi. The front desk is open 24 hours a day for your comfort.

For an additional fee, a shuttle service to the station or airport will be arranged for you upon request. For people who travel by car, parking is provided. Use the laundry service if necessary. At the same time, there is a safe for storing various valuables.

Tourists reviews

If we consider the comments of tourists about Perm hotels, it becomes clear that many of them offer guests excellent service and reasonable prices for accommodation. There are negative opinions, including the fact that in some hotels the price level does not correspond to the living conditions. Hotel "Ural" pleases tourists with its advantageous location, and Travel - the opportunity to get acquainted with the main attractions of the city. Eva City Hotel is said to be of excellent service, although smokers complain that smoking is not allowed in the rooms. But this fact pleases guests who do not have this addiction. Prikamye and Pearl are hotels that are said to be suitable for the discerning public, while living in the Hilton Garden will be affordable only for wealthy people. The City Star Hotel has received a lot of positive reviews due to its home-like cozy interior.
