Description

The hotel complex is part of the Gardenia hotel chain. It is surrounded by an oriental rich garden. Some units have views of the garden with its beautiful floral arrangements. Competent landscape design emphasizes the beauty of the territory of the hotel "Amazonia Gardenia" (Hurghada) 4 *.

The photos show how exotic plants harmoniously resonate with decorative elements, the shape of the pool and the hotel building. Laconic aerial architecture of the building, rooms richly decorated with marble, views of the Red Sea provide a unique atmosphere of comfort.

The staff is friendly. This place is recommended for recreation for youth companies and families with children. Hotel "Amazonia Gardenia" (Hurghada) 4 * is famous for its bright beach holidays.

The water park is equipped with three slides. At three swimming pools, guests are provided with umbrellas, sunbeds and towels free of charge. Water in one of the pools is heated in the cold season.





For the use of parking do not require payment. Doctor services are available for a fee. Barber and laundry services are paid. The hotel has shops. It is possible to order a taxi.

Nutrition

Tourists eat in three restaurants. Two of them serve only paid meals. Drinks are offered in three bars. In one, you have to pay money for orders. In free restaurants and bars, guests eat on an all-inclusive basis.

The main restaurant serves international and Egyptian dishes. The cozy Roof grill restaurant serves excellent lunches and dinners. The menu in it is based on grilled dishes.

Beach

Tourists relax on the beach of a neighboring hotel, which is located across the street from the Amazonia Gardenia Hotel (Hurghada) 4 *. The beach is sandy, interspersed with corals. The hotel complex and the sea coast are separated by a distance of 600 meters. A private bus is available to the private beach.





Hotel guests are entitled to use free sun loungers, umbrellas and towels. The depth of coastal waters is small. Shallow water goes far into the sea, which is convenient for children.

The beach is suitable for active diving and surfing. They rent equipment here and teach scuba diving. Classes are led by experienced divers who know the depths and richness of the underwater world of the Red Sea.

Types of apartments

One-room Standard Room adjoins a balcony. They have a bath with shower. Junior Suite apartments consist of a living room, bedroom, bathroom, terrace or balcony. The Executive Suite has a living room, bedroom, kitchenette, one / two bathtubs with shower / jacuzzi and a balcony.

Room features

The apartments in the Amazonia Gardenia Hotel (Hurghada) 4 * are equipped with a safe, mini fridge, hairdryer and air conditioning. They have access to international telephone communications, television and the Internet. The rooms have the opportunity to make tea or coffee. Extra beds in some rooms are free of charge.





Hotel apartments are luxurious. The design of spacious rooms, with all its brevity, has a highlight - bright curtains, colorful bedspreads, paintings and decorative elements. The floors are decorated with ceramic tiles in oriental style.
