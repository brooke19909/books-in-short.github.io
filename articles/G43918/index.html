Cinema center

It is worth mentioning separately about the cinema located on the territory of the shopping center.

The cinema center includes four halls, in total designed for 256 spectators. The largest of them are Red (96 places) and Blue (90 places). These are standard cinema halls equipped with modern equipment and soft chairs. Here are screenings of high-budget novelties of the global film industry.

The most unusual is the Art Room, decorated in black and white colors. Its walls are decorated with shots from films considered to be classics of cinema. In the Art Hall, paintings from famous film festivals, an art house and other controversial films that have not been released for rental are shown.

Visitors also have access to the VIP lounge with more comfortable leather chairs and wooden tables.

The official website of the shopping center contains the current schedule of sessions. In the shopping center Volgograd "Pyramid" prices for tickets to the cinema center range from 100 to 350 rubles. Including the system of buying tickets online.
