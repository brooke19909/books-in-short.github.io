How to make a real magic wand?

The magic wand is a very important item for any young magician who sincerely believes in good and dreams of defeating all his enemies. And, of course, answering the question of how to make a real magic wand, you must immediately say that you will have to make a lot of effort and effort. Do not think that it is very simple.

Material for creating a magic wand





To create a magic wand, first of all, you need a tree, but not the most ordinary one. Take a walk in the park, forest and try to find a special twig for yourself. It is important not to cut it from a living tree, because you will hurt him. Such a wand cannot help you. The tree species does not matter much either. The main thing is that the future wand "called" you, so that you feel its strength. Only in this way you will understand how to make a real magic wand.

Trees unsuitable for its creation simply do not exist. The sensations at meeting with the wand are completely different for everyone, but you can certainly say that the one that is destined for you will never be missed.





It is also impossible to give advice on what to say or do when meeting with your magical assistant. All people, like all magic wands, have their own personality. It is important that conversion comes from the heart. You should thank the tree that gave you the wand and greet your new friend and helper.

Cooking

Don't know how to make a magic wand? Arriving home, immediately put it in salt water for a day. To prevent the stick from coming up, it should be crushed with a stone or other heavy natural object. This will help the branch to get rid of extraneous information. After that, it should be dried in the air so that it is saturated with solar energy.

Making a magic wand

Are you interested in how to make a real magic wand with your own hands? After the branch has dried, carefully examine it. An image of a future magic item will form in your head. It is not at all necessary that the wand has a classical appearance, is long and thin. Your wand may well be uneven, knobby, with bends. The most important thing is that she is yours. To grind a twig, you can use a stationery knife and sandpaper. After finishing polishing, you need to wipe the stick well and cover it with varnish. Another important point in the guide on how to make a real magic wand is to replace the core. If you feel an urgent need, you need to drill a small hole in the center of the stick and insert a feather, hair, stone, plant root or other substance into it.

It is important to note that when creating a magical item , the use of artificial materials should be avoided. Chemical glue, plastic, sparkles and rhinestones can only be used in the handle. The tip and body of the stick should be made only of natural materials.

In order for the wand to be filled with strength, it is necessary to pronounce magical conspiracies at exactly midnight on the full moon. Ask nature for magic power, promise not to do harm, not to violate the natural order of things.





In fact, as you see, creating your own magic wand is not as difficult as it seems. But it is very important that a magic item bring people only good. Therefore, think again before asking for help from magic.
