Turkish knit yarn Alize: quality from time immemorial

If a few years ago, crocheting and knitting was considered a suitable occupation except for retired grandmothers, today hand-made knitted items are becoming more and more popular and more and more appreciated. Each headpiece, clothes or even handmade shoes is an exclusive product that exists in one piece and can emphasize your taste and individual style. What future thing will turn out depends not only on the chosen model and the craftsmanship of the author, but also on the yarn used in its manufacture.

Alize knitting threads - centuries-old traditions and innovative technologies in every skein

Alize yarn is one of the most common brands in the world of needlework. Handicraft masterpieces from it with equal success will be able to both experienced and beginner needlewomen. Alize knitting products combine centuries-old traditions and modern technology in the best way. Even in the most ancient times, Turkish fabrics and yarn were considered the highest quality in the world, and were purchased by merchants for deliveries to the most wealthy persons close to the emperor. The most luxurious robes that no other in the world could compare with were made from Turkish yarn.

Unique composition of Alize knitting yarn

As time goes on, technology develops, but Alize knitting yarns still preserve the traditions of Turkish quality. Due to the unique combination of materials such as cotton, natural wool, acrylic, polyester and bamboo in Alize threads, it does not fade, does not fade, is easily washed and cleaned without losing shape, dries quickly and does not wrinkle. The composition of almost all types of Alize yarn produced by Yunteks includes a component extracted from sheep's wool, lanolin, which makes the thread more plastic and comfortable for knitting.
