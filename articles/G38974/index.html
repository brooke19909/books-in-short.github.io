Convenience first

Hotel "Orbita" is an excellent option for living in St. Petersburg. It is located in the north of the city, right next to the Ploshchad Vuzhstva metro station. Comfortable stay

On the top floor of the building is a cafe "Galaxy". A buffet is waiting for visitors there . Breakfast is included. The food here is the most varied. For lunch and dinner, a menu with Russian and European cuisine is provided. A cozy cafe with a beautiful interior welcomes guests very friendly.
