Where to beg

How to write a story

We found out where to ask for money on the Internet. Usually, users are begging through social networks and in charity groups. These resources allow you to convey information to a larger audience of people.

As already mentioned, in this case, the history of the occurrence of serious expenses plays a huge role. The more detailed it will be painted, the higher the chances of success.

The story needs to be worked out well. You must be convincing and believable to describe the circumstances. It is necessary to consider everything that can play into the hands of the supplicant. Long registration on the site

How to ask people for money on the Internet? The next tip is a long stay on a particular virtual resource. "Fresh" pages and accounts on forums / social networks may hint at fraud and deception.

That is why begging users are better off using their old active pages. If there are several profiles, indicate this in the ad.

People recently registered on the site (if this is not a specialized resource for those in need) and asking for money from other residents of the service cause obvious distrust. And this is quite normal.

Brief instruction

How to ask for money on the Internet? We examined the main points that each user may encounter. But how to act in order not to get confused?

Suppose money is really necessary. Then the preparation of a detailed story about what is happening will take a minimum of time. This is a key point. After all, if there is a narration of a real picture, the user will be able to convince the audience of his need.

To successfully ask for money on the Internet, a person will have to:

  1. Choose the resources on which the post will be published. It is advisable not to neglect groups of charitable foundations. It is recommended that you first come to the relevant organizations in person. If he succeeds in convincing the fund of its difficult situation, it will itself begin to publish requests for help on the Web.
  2. Register on selected sites. It is recommended to do this in advance. And to choose for this social networks in which the user works constantly.
  3. To invent and write a story of what is happening. The more details, the better.
  4. Be sure to indicate the exact amount of donations. If money is needed for running expenses (life after a difficult divorce in the presence of a newborn child, for example), it is better to ask for help not only with money, but also with products / things.
  5. Indicate contact details.
  6. Attach photos of the supplicant and any evidence of need.
  7. Spread the announcement and wait for the result.

As practice shows, if you enlist the support of charitable organizations and aid funds, you can hope for success. Especially if there is a need for an expensive treatment for a child. People donate money to babies more and more.
