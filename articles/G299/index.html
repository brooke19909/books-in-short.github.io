Some tips on how to transfer money from phone to card

Very often in everyday life there are circumstances when cash is urgently needed. Moreover, the situation is complicated by the fact that they are available only on the mobile phone account, and this amount is quite large. A completely natural question arises: "How to transfer money from phone to card?" Is this even possible?

The need for withdrawal of money is very high

The problem of how to transfer money from a phone to a card has to be solved by those who have run out of cash on vacation. It also happens that your employer pays you for telephone calls, and for one reason or another you have never used a corporate mobile phone for the last month.





In this case, you can well cash out this money. Ultimately, do not disappear to them. And believe me, this is not a complete list of life situations when your only wallet is a mobile phone.

Thus, the question of how to transfer money from a phone to a card is very, very relevant. It should be noted that it is possible to cash out your "hard-earned money" - this will not be difficult.

Ways

There are several ways by which the question of how to transfer money from a phone to a card is resolved. Let's consider them in more detail.

Services of mobile operators

Just a few years ago, specialized services were created that carried out money transfers from phone to card. Naturally, a fee was charged for such a service. Later, mobile operators realized that it was possible to make money on it, and organized similar in-system services. The first of these were Megafon and Beeline.

Unistream

So, how do we transfer money from phone to card using the latter method?

We open the official website of Unistream, then we find the address of the nearest banking institution and at the next stage we send an SMS message indicating the amount of payment, the number of the banking unit where we want to receive money, and, of course, indicate your surname, name and patronymic. After a few minutes, you will receive a confirmation that the payment has been made by the operator, and you will also receive a special code via SMS, which will need to be reported to the bank employee in order to be given the money. You just need to take a passport and go to the office of Sberbank or VTB-24 for cash.





A similar service was developed by Megafon. As additional tools for withdrawing money, you can use electronic systems, such as Yandex.Money or WebMoney.

Cancellation of a contract with a mobile operator

If you no longer need the services of this mobile operator, you need to go to the office of MTS or Beeline and terminate the service contract. 10 days after that you will be refunded.
