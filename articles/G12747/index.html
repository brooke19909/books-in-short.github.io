Card payment system and currency transactions

There is still a small nuance that should be taken into account before receiving a card, or rather, before processing an application for its release. For this, contrary to popular belief, there is no need to travel abroad, many purchases are made on the Internet. And here it is very important to choose the right card payment system.

Credit limit

In this sense, the rules for using Sberbank's Credit Momentum credit card are somewhat different. Features of operations

The next point is associated with the features of conducting operations on cards of this type. Protect funds with 3D Secure

This is an attempt by the bank to additionally make sure that it is the cardholder who makes the payment. 3D Secure is not an absolute protection of money on a card during operations without using a card, for example, a one-time code can be intercepted by computer viruses. However, the system allows you to reduce the risk of financial loss to the client, at least in cases where the active card or data indicated on its front and back sides just fell into the hands of a third party. This is a drawback or a virtue - everyone can decide for himself, but it is important to remember that not every site is ready to provide this degree of protection. These features apply to debit cards, too, but in order to fully understand how to properly use the discussed product, it is best to mentally include this information in the rules for using a Sberbank credit card.
