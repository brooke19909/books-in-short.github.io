Casco is popular due to the wide range of costs that insurance covers. At the moment, the situation is this: many people are already thinking about protecting it while planning a car purchase.

Franchise in CASCO

Surely someone had heard about the CASCO franchise. What it is? This is the name of the part of the insurance damage that the owner indemnifies himself if an insured event occurs. As a rule, we are talking about a fixed insurance amount, although many firms express this value as a percentage. The situation is as follows: CASCO provides the client with a significant discount on its services, but any minor repairs (agreed in advance with representatives of the insurance company) will have to be carried out by spending money from your own pocket. There are two types of franchises for cars : conditional and unconditional.





Unconditional franchise

In the insurance policy, this procedure is stipulated approximately like this: the owner of the car assumes an obligation, which consists in the fact that he compensates a certain amount in the event that he receives damage. It is worth considering a simple example: there is a car that is insured under this program. The franchise limit is, say, 5,000 rubles. If an insured event occurs, for example, for 15 thousand, the company will pay the owner of the car only 10 000.

Conditional Franchise

What can be said about this proposal? Under a conditional deductible, the insurance company fully compensates for all the damage caused to the car only when its size is higher than a certain level. If you pay attention to the reviews on CASCO, then in this situation everything looks like this: if the car crashes, but the amount of damage is less than the franchise bar, the owner pays for the repair work. If the damage is greater than the data in the contract, the owner will be paid for the restoration of the car.
