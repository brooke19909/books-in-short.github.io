"Moving Average"

We continue to consider indicators for MT4 - the most accurate. The Moving Average index, without a doubt, belongs to the category of the best Forex market indicators. On its basis, many other well-known devices successfully used by traders have been created.

Bollinger Band

What other indicators are there for MT4 (the most accurate)? The Bollinger band indicator is used when new Forex instruments are created from a pair of moving averages. In it, all MAs are shifted for a certain period in relation to each other. Thanks to this nuance, the so-called envelope was created in which two curves describe the faces, and the third is placed in between in the middle.

The essence of working with this device is easy to understand. Between faces the price is constantly fluctuating. She only occasionally leaves them in order to be in the envelope range again. Transactions can be opened when the price touches one of the faces or is falsely broken.

Binary options

So, you already know what indicators to use for MT4. The most accurate algorithms for binary options - this is what we will now consider.
