Kempinski Hotel (Belek, Turkey): tours, photos, reviews

The Kempinski House Hotel in Turkey (Belek) is one of the most luxurious holiday destinations on the entire Mediterranean coast. It attracts travelers from all over the world with the beauty of its territory, as well as first-class service provided within the framework of the all-inclusive concept.

general information

The hotel was built relatively recently - in 2005. That is why on its territory everything is done quite modernly, and the equipment is decorated with the latest technology. Tourists who relax at the Kempinski Hotel (Turkey / Belek) can enjoy the luxurious gardens planted on its territory, as well as well-developed infrastructure.

Location

The hotel is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes. It is located within the resort of Belek, almost in the very center of the city of Kedrie - literally five kilometers from it. Here nearby there is a huge number of places for entertainment, presented in the form of restaurants, nightclubs, bars, as well as karaoke. In the vicinity there are a considerable number of attractions that guests seek to visit.

All travelers who arrive here begin their journey across Turkish land from Antalya Airport, which is 25 kilometers from here. Due to the impressive distance that separates the point of arrival and destination, payment for the transfer service is necessarily included in each ticket. As tourists note in their reviews of the Kempinski Hotel (Turkey / Belek), it is provided on a modern comfortable passenger car that is equipped with air conditioning - this is especially appreciated on hot days.

Rooms

The hotel is represented by 175 apartments. All of them are divided into two categories: luxury and junior suite. Each room has all the best living conditions, designed only for those tourists who appreciate the state of comfort and do not spare the funds to ensure it.

In all rooms only the newest comfortable furniture is installed here, they are also perfectly equipped from a technical point of view. The rooms without fail have individual air conditioning, a safe, a minibar, a telephone, and a plasma TV with a large diagonal.



Staying here, tourists can use a rather comfortable bathroom, where all the furniture and plumbing are modern. There is a shower, bathtub with hydromassage, as well as a separate bathroom. The room is decorated with lots of mirrors and white marble. In small boxes of their new owners bathrobes and large terry towels are waiting. For each guest at the time of entry a set of bath accessories is issued.

Hotel Room Features

As for the furniture equipment, the necessary number of beds, presented in the form of large high beds, is established in the apartments in the necessary order. All of them are covered with the most expensive bedding, which has hypoallergenic properties; it is replaced twice a week. Near the beds there is a pair of nightstands in which guests have the opportunity to store their small things. For clothes, there are large wardrobe cabinets.



The apartments necessarily have a working area, consisting of a desk and a comfortable armchair. There is also a place to relax - it is represented by a small corner sofa and a table, at which it is very convenient to drink tea, coffee or a cold cocktail.

The rooms also have a small kitchen where guests can cook their own meals. It is fully equipped with all the necessary equipment, there are several countertops, a sink, as well as equipment for making tea or coffee with all the necessary accessories. It also has dishes designed for a small number of people. In the same part there is a dining area, consisting of a table and several chairs.



Conditions for children

Tourists vacationing at the Kempinski Hotel (Turkey / Belek) in their comments left as a result of their vacation often note that this place is perfect for traveling with children. Such reviews are due to the fact that for small guests a number of conditions are provided that contribute to their more comfortable pastime, which will also be safe.

First of all, hotel guests pay attention to the possibility of installing a crib in any type of apartment - it is designed for a child aged 4 to 12 years. It is provided completely free upon request. In restaurants for babies special chairs are provided.

In the event that parents do not have time to look after their child, they can send him to a mini-club, where animators can do it. You can also use the paid babysitting services, which are charged hourly. Many guests in their reviews note that only qualified specialists with experience in the relevant field are looking after children here.

In the open area for younger guests there are also several places to relax. one of them is a swimming pool, the depth of which is 40 cm. Its bottom and sides have a coating that ensures the safety of a bathing child - it prevents slipping. A large playground for active pastime is built in the garden.

Nutrition

Guests at the Kempinski Hotel in Belek can visit several catering establishments, which offer a wide selection of dishes prepared in the best traditions of different peoples of the world. Among them there are three chic restaurants and six bars scattered throughout the building, as well as in the open square.

After visiting the restaurants, you can take part in the buffet, which offers the most exquisite dishes created from seafood, as well as in Italian and Asian styles. The visit is paid - about 15 euros per person per visit. Before that, you need to reserve a table.

The bars offer a menu that contains dozens of cocktails and other drinks, as well as snacks for them. Sweet tooths definitely recommend tasting local desserts, among which there are many oriental and European sweets.



SPA

The Kempinski Hotel (Turkey) is very popular in the spa area, which is divided into several parts: thermal, massage and pool. Guests often respond positively about this place. According to them, many of the procedures performed here are accompanied by an amazing effect. Also, vacationers are pleased that most of the services are completely free.



Here, travelers can visit the luxurious thermal zone, represented by a combination of hammam and steam room. Here, the team of parmeisters can offer a comprehensive hover, as well as massage and a number of procedures that are beneficial for the skin of the whole body. After visiting the steam room, you can go to the pool or jacuzzi - so experienced tourists recommend doing it.

A visit to the massage room at the Kempinski Hotel (Turkey) is paid, however, as noted by travelers, all the procedures offered here are performed using secret unique techniques that will certainly give a positive result.



Aquazone

An outdoor terrace with a pool is also often visited by many tourists, which they talk about in their reviews. Guests write that on a huge platform you can accommodate with great comfort - for this there are several types of sun loungers, as well as beach umbrellas. If desired, vacationers can use the provided towels and mattresses. On this site there is a bar counter where you can drink cold drinks and original cocktails.

Hotel beach

The description of the Kempinski Hotel (Turkey / Belek) states that it is located on the coastline. This means that in order for tourists to get to the beach, they do not need to travel any distances - it is located on the hotel premises.

The beach area has a rather considerable length - 110 meters, it is completely covered with sand. There are several dozens of sun loungers throughout, where vacationers can accommodate on a free basis. They also have umbrellas and a bar where you can buy drinks. Towels and mattresses are also available free of charge.



Cost of rest

The cost of the tour to the Kempinski Hotel (Turkey / Belek) is on average about 120-150 thousand rubles. This price is based on the calculation of the payment of a weekly stay for two in a room with enhanced comfort conditions, as well as a round-trip flight. The standard package includes payment for the transfer of a tourist to a place of rest, as well as payment of medical insurance necessary for crossing the Turkish border. As for the visa, its registration is the task of the tourist himself.

Most travelers who value the comfort of their stay are satisfied with such prices, since they consider them to be quite acceptable. Moreover, having visited the Kempinski Hotel (Turkey / Belek), they recommend that their friends and acquaintances go here. Often they share their impressions on the open spaces of social networks.
