"Blue Dreams Apartments 3", Crete - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Summer is a time of relaxation and travel. And today Greece is one of the most popular tourist countries in Europe. In particular, the island of Crete is a great place for a perfect holiday. This place is associated not only with clean sea waters and secluded beaches, but also with ancient legends about the heroes and inhabitants of Olympus.

Many tourists consider the Blue Dreams Apartments 3 one of the best places to stay. What is this place? Where is the hotel situated? What living conditions can a traveler offer? Many are interested in this information.

Blue Dreams Apartments 3 Hotel: Location





Today, Rethymno is considered to be a fairly popular resort city of Crete. Blue Dreams Apartments 3 is located about 12 kilometers from the city center, on the territory of a small fishing village called Sfakaki.

This village attracts more and more foreign tourists every year, as it is famous for its wild beaches, wonderful climate, breathtaking scenery and amazing color. By the way, the nearest Chania Airport is 70 kilometers from the hotel complex. On the other hand, you can reach the sea coast in a few minutes, which, of course, can be considered an undoubted advantage.

Accommodation conditions: description of rooms





What conditions does the Blue Dream Apartments offer? Greece is a country famous for its hospitality. And the island of Crete, as well as the hotel itself, is no exception. Here, every tourist is welcomed with all possible warmth and hospitality, offering decent living conditions.

Blue Dreams Apartment 3 is more of a small family pension than a large resort complex. On its territory there are only 20 rooms of different capacities. Nevertheless, each of them is furnished with everything necessary and consists of a bedroom and a kitchen area. Here you will find a soft bed, a spacious wardrobe, as well as bedside tables, tables and chairs. Naturally, there is also the necessary household appliances, including a TV, air conditioning, and a telephone.

In the kitchen area you will find a small refrigerator for storing food, as well as a hob where you can warm up or cook dinner. There is also the required number of cutlery and crockery.

Each room has its own balcony with garden furniture, from where you can admire the well-groomed hotel grounds and the picturesque coast. The bathroom has a toilet, bathtub and shower, as well as a mirror, wash basin and the required number of towels. The rooms are cleaned every day, so it is unlikely to complain about cleanliness.

Beach activities for travelers





Of course, many travelers are interested in questions about what conditions for a beach holiday the Blue Dream Apartments 3 offers. Crete is an island famous for its beaches. And very close to the hotel there is a private "wild" beach. This place has both disadvantages and advantages. For example, there is a rocky shore and not very convenient descent into the water. On the other hand, this place is secluded - here you can have a good rest from the noise of the crowd. By the way, some travelers have romantic dinners at sunset.

And a hundred meters from the hotel there is a public sandy beach, which is more suitable for people who crave fun and entertainment. There are sun loungers, umbrellas, changing cabins and showers. There is also a bar where tourists are offered refreshments. In addition, a huge number of shops are located around the perimeter, where you can buy souvenirs and other interesting things.

On the municipal beach there is also the opportunity to engage in outdoor activities. For example, there are sports grounds on the territory where tourists often arrange playful competitions. You can also go boating, catamarans, water skiing, boat, etc. Of course, a scuba diving school is organized for travelers, where you can get some useful lessons and rent the necessary equipment. Sightseeing holidays in Crete





Of course, excursions and trips around the island are just the type of vacation that most residents of the Blue Dreams Apartments 3 prefer. Greece is a country rich in various historical and archaeological sites, a region shrouded in ancient legends that has been known to everyone since childhood.

And a fairly large number of mythical stories are connected specifically with the island of Crete. Just remember the story of the labyrinth of the Minotaur, where the hero Theseus killed the monster, thereby freeing Athens from sacrifice. By the way, you will have the opportunity to see the ancient labyrinth with your own eyes, as well as visit the legendary Knossos Palace.

Of course, the sights of the island do not end there. For example, Zeus cave, located at an altitude of 1050 meters, is very popular among tourists. It is here, according to legend, that the great Thunderer, the lord of Olympus, was born. Here, tourists can go down a giant cave with centuries-old stalactites, at the bottom of which there is a lake.

Not far from the city of Rethymnon there is also the only fresh lake of Kurna on the island . Here, among the hills, you can have a great time enjoying the scenery and the fresh air. And not far from the village of Kera is the monastery of Kera Kardiotissa, built in the 13th century. For centuries, the most valuable icon of the Virgin of the Cardiotissa painted by Saint Lazarus has been kept here. They say she had healing properties. And visiting the Messar Plain, you can explore the ruins of Gortyna - one of the most powerful cities in Crete in the Dorian era.

If you are interested in more modern pleasures, then you can always visit the huge Anapolis water park, where guests will find a variety of attractions, including giant water slides.





In any case, the hotel staff will be happy to tell you which places are worth a visit, help you plan a route and tell you how to get to a particular attraction.
