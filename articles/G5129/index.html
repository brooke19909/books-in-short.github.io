Pension "Baltiets" (Repino, Leningrad Region): address, description of rooms, entertainment, reviews

If you want to spend time on the Gulf of Finland, you can go to one of the hotels on its coast. Among the variety of establishments, one can distinguish the Baltiets boarding house in Repino. The comfortable hotel is located in a picturesque place and has a huge territory. Everything is provided for relaxing guests. This article will consider all the advantages and disadvantages of the institution.

Restaurants in the Gulf of Finland

In the Gulf of Finland you can find pensions for every taste. All of them differ in terms of accommodation, the cost of apartments, food and infrastructure. Among the institutions there are more affordable pensions, expensive hotels, cottage villages and tourist centers. Some of them have been working since Soviet times. Others are new buildings that offer an excellent level of service.

On the shores of the Gulf of Finland you can find an institution for any wallet. Here you can have a good part of your vacation and enjoy the beauty of nature. Or you can just have a nice weekend away from the bustle of the city. Hotels on the coast of the bay are good for relaxing and for celebrating special events. To celebrate a birthday in nature or to celebrate the New Year - all this can be organized in any institution. However, it is worth noting that relaxing on the bay is not only an opportunity to escape from everyday worries and have fun, but also a great option for recovery. Here you can enjoy the beauty of nature, breathe coniferous air, go fishing, undergo treatment procedures. One of these institutions, in which all the options for active and passive recreation are possible, is the Baltiets pension in Repino.

Catering

Pension "Baltiets" in Repino (Leningrad Region) is a great place for recreation and relaxation. For guests of the institution organized meals like a "buffet". The dining room is located on the second floor of the main building. The panoramic windows of the room make it bright and allow you to admire nature during the meal.

The pension has a restaurant Terrace. It will appeal to all those people who value comfort, tranquility and clean air. According to guests, the institution can be called the most comfortable of those that are located outside St. Petersburg. The chef of the establishment offers guests to taste wonderful dishes of European and Italian cuisine. Every day until 12 a.m. the institution is open to visitors.

For those guests who live in comfort apartments, breakfast is served in the Terrace Restaurant.

For many city residents, a trip to Repino (Leningrad Oblast) is an opportunity to relax in the lap of nature. Well, what is nature without cooking delicious meat? The boarding house staff took care of their guests by placing barbecue houses around the property. Small rooms are designed to accommodate 10-12 people. In the houses there is a table, benches and other accessories for the feast.

The guesthouse also has a lobby bar. The institution offers a pleasant time with a cup of coffee or tea, as well as tasting alcoholic drinks or all kinds of cocktails. The bar offers a wide range of cold appetizers, desserts, hot dishes. For the youngest vacationers, a special children's menu has been developed, which serves dishes such as french fries, milkshakes and all kinds of sweets.

On the territory of the pension, it is possible to hold wedding banquets and other celebrations. It features a 288 square meter hall. m, capable of accommodating up to 120 people. The room is equipped for business and holiday events. In addition, there is another room for organizing seminars and conferences for up to 80 people.

The rental of premises and the order of banquets is carried out by filling out a special form on the website of the Baltiets boarding house (you will find the details and organization data there).

Shares of the institution

Reviews for boarding house Baltiets in Repino

Continuing the review of the boarding house, you need to pay attention to the reviews of tourists. Is the tourist complex so good as it is described? It is worth noting that the guest reviews are very controversial, since the institution has significant shortcomings.

According to vacationers, the pension is located in an incredibly picturesque place. This is the main advantage of the complex. If you live on the upper floors, you can appreciate the view of St. Petersburg, the forest and Kronstadt. Amazing clean air and birdsong is what you need after the hustle and bustle of the city. The boarding house is located near the city. A trip by public transport from St. Petersburg takes only forty minutes.

The main drawback of the restaurant, according to guests, is the number of rooms, which needs updating. Some of the apartments have been renovated, but this did not affect the overall picture. On the territory of the boarding house there are three buildings, two of them were built long ago, and the third is a new one. In which of them to book apartments - you decide. Despite the shortcomings, the boarding house has its regular customers who come here not the first year. They note that recently the situation has somewhat worsened and traces of neglect are noticeable. The number of rooms needs updating and repair. Probably, this is not about all the rooms, but only those that are more in demand due to the lower cost.

Old furniture and TVs are not the only problem of the apartments. Absolutely all guests note the terrible quality of cleaning. Dirty balconies, windows and corridors of buildings are commonplace. Unfortunately, the administration does not pay due attention to cleanliness. No less depressing are the facades of buildings that need updating.

Nutrition Reviews

Food is one of the most important points by which the quality of rest is evaluated. According to tourists, the buffet in the boarding house leaves much to be desired. A modest assortment of dishes, poor service and a large crowd in the main restaurant do not have a meal. Tourists note that when booking you can choose a guesthouse, half board and breakfast. Many are limited only to a morning meal. But it is worthwhile to understand that in Repino's establishments food is not cheap.

General impression

Many tourists come to the boarding house for the sake of beautiful nature and fun. The complex has wonderful animators who devote a lot of time to children. Young guests are incredibly pleased with the rest. For kids there is a lot of entertainment in the boarding house. In addition, daily activities carry out all kinds of entertainment events. Children here are a lot of fun.

The pension is no less attractive in terms of entertainment and for adults. Want to take a break from the fast pace of the city? Come to Repino. Pension "Baltiets" (the details of the institution are given at the beginning of the article) is the best place to relax. Despite the shortcomings, people come back here year after year, hoping that soon everything will change for the better.

A huge advantage of the complex is the presence of a swimming pool, which operates throughout the year. His visit allows you to get in shape, relax and get a lot of pleasure. Positive feedback is also left by guests about the spa center of the pension.

In general, the boarding house makes a dual impression. On the one hand, it is very attractive in many ways, including pricing. And on the other hand, there are flaws that are worth eliminating. Nevertheless, the pension is recommended if you want to relax in Repino.
