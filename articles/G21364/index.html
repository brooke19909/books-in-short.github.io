Is distortion a flaw in the picture or an unusual artistic decision?

Distortion is an error or an error in the aggregate of optical elements, during which the coefficient of linear increase varies along the field of view of the lens.

Definition of distortion

Distortion from the Latin language translates as "curvature." When distortion occurs, the similarity between the object and its visual image is violated. Distortion is an error. It can be fixed at the stage of selecting the optical system when choosing lenses or when editing photos in a PC. Distortion is a noticeable phenomenon if there are straight horizontal or vertical lines in the frame. With distortion, straight lines become curved outward or inward. It is pronounced when shooting architectural buildings, trees, pillars and other objects.

Types of Distortion

Distortion occurs in two forms - it is barrel-shaped and pillow-shaped.

The barrel-shaped or convex distortion is distinguished by the fact that the bend of the line is directed outward, while the object becomes convex, and this is very noticeable relative to the edges of the image.





As for the pincushion or concave distortion, it is distinguished by the bending of the line closer to the center of the frame, that is, the lines appear to be concave into the image.

Also, barrel-shaped distortion is called positive, and pillow-shaped distortion is called negative.

Intentional distortion

Photographers do not always consider distortion a lack of photography, some try to do it intentionally with the lens or when processing photos in the editor.

As for the lens, it is very common Fisheye or as it is also called "Fishy". This is a type of so-called ultra-wide-angle lenses, which have a convex front lens at 180 degrees, which is why distortion is obtained in the pictures. The fisheye lens can be of two types: circular and diagonal. A circular lens is most commonly found in meteorology to take photos of the sky. The diagonal lens is the most common among photographers. We even know the name of the first photographer who first used this type. This is Lev Abramovich Borodulin, who is a famous Soviet and Israeli photographer specializing in sports photography.





There are a number of other lenses, but they are all aimed at creating image distortion, such devices can be found in Nikon, and Canon, and other companies involved in the production of cameras.

Conclusion

Despite the fact that distortion is most often perceived in a negative way, in some cases this effect is used intentionally to focus on a specific moment or part of an object. This effect has been used in the photo industry for a long time, but has gained great popularity relatively recently.



Distortion is a way of self-expression of a photographer, an opportunity to show their individuality. It gives the picture a special chic and specificity. Today, an increasing number of photographers around the world are beginning to work closely with this effect and make full use of the opportunities that distortion provides. Of course, not all professionals, and even ordinary people, like photographs taken using this effect, but there is no point in denying the fact that in recent years interest in distortion has greatly increased.
