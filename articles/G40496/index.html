Quilting. What it is? Needlework technique for beginners and not only

Quilting, what is it? This is one of the needlework techniques that allows you to create original textile items from scraps of fabric with your own hands that can be used not only in the interior. Some masters generalize the quilting technique with patchwork and patchwork, but each of these types of needlework, despite the fact that the principles and materials used are almost the same, has its own characteristics.

Combining multiple techniques

In essence, the quilting technique is quilting and stitching small pieces of various materials. Unlike patchwork and patchwork, things made in this technique are voluminous. The relief pattern is achieved due to the multi-layer product, at least three layers of material are used. The most attractive and original look of a product is achieved when quilting combines patchwork technique, embroidery, applique, and even painting.



Creating Quilting Layers

What is it, we have already figured out. Now you can familiarize yourself in more detail with the method of stitching and decorating products made in this technique. For each layer of the product you will need different materials. The most painstaking work, requiring the manifestation of imagination and accuracy, is the creation of the first decorative layer. The more rags of different shapes, textures, colors, the more chances to create a unique, bright and beautiful pattern.

The middle layer is mainly made of synthetic winterizer or batting, it gives the necessary volume to the picture and insulates the thing. This is useful when creating all kinds of blankets. The bottom layer is selected depending on the purpose of the manufactured item. For example, for the same blanket it would be more expedient to use natural fabric; when it comes into contact with the skin, it will not cause any negative feelings. If it is planned to create a picture, then the lower material can be synthetic, in this case the main selection criterion is the strength of the fabric.



Complexity of work

For all its apparent simplicity, the quilting technique is a long and laborious creative process that requires accuracy and accuracy. Quilting for beginners will be a daunting task. In order not to immediately be disappointed in this type of needlework and subsequently, thanks to experience, to create your masterpieces, you need to start with the simplest and smallest things. For the first experiment, kitchen gloves, decorative pillowcases for pillows and even small bags or a baby blanket, rug are suitable . Quilting (photo of examples of work is in the article) is a great way to show your creative thinking and golden hands. Do not be afraid of the first failures, here, as elsewhere, everything comes with experience.

Quilting. What it is? Undoubtedly, this is needlework that allows you to express yourself with the help of things, whether it be sofas, bedspreads, paintings or other products, to decorate and bring originality and comfort to the interior of your home.
