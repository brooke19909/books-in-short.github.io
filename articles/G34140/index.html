Thank you program from Sberbank: where it works, conditions, reviews

Sberbank's ‚ÄúThank You‚Äù program should be understood to mean a program for accruing bonuses and points that occurs as a result of purchases made with a Sberbank card. When making payments, points are credited to the client's bonus account, which can be further managed.

The essence of the program

Such a program is a promotion from the bank in relation to its customers. The main condition for participation is the availability of a bank card. The advantages of the program are that it allows the cardholder to reduce their material costs due to the possibility of applying bonuses.

As a result of purchases, the customer accumulates points, which are then used to receive discounts in a huge list of stores across the country.

How to get involved

A few steps to become a member of the program:

  • Buy a Sberbank card. The exception is the Maestro, Maestro Social cards of the North-West Bank of Sberbank of Russia (with numbers 676195, 639002551).
  • Register in the program, which is possible in two ways: through a self-service terminal (ATM), through Sberbank Online. These issues will be discussed in more detail below.
  • Make purchases, while paying with a card in all stores where there is such an opportunity.

The requirements for program participants are not particularly stringent. They can be both holders of Premium cards, as well as ordinary pension and salary cards. The exception is affiliate cards. Some features for Momentum cards: you can register in the system only after 5 working days from the date of opening.

If there are several cards in the holder, bonuses will be accumulated for each, and then added up.

Using an ATM when connecting to a promotion

The following steps must be taken to activate the promotion.

  1. Find any Sberbank terminal and insert a card.
  2. Enter the pin code.
  3. Find the main menu, the item "Bonus program".
  4. On the ATM screen, you must enter the cell phone number and confirm your consent to connect the program. There are clues everywhere.
  5. Next, an SMS should come to the specified phone number. This SMS will contain a special password. That it will be necessary to inform the operator when contacting the bank's call center on the functioning of the program.

Application of "Sberbank Online" for registration

The sequence of actions here is a little different.

  1. You must enter your personal account from a computer or mobile device.
  2. Select the menu "My bonuses"
  3. Enter your email and phone number.
  4. An SMS notification with a password will be sent to the cell phone.

Bonuses and points: accumulate and spend

To receive bonuses under the program wherever ‚ÄúThank you‚Äù from Sberbank operates, you must pay with a credit card in all possible situations. For each ruble spent, 1.5 bonuses are paid during the first three months of registration in the program and 0.5 bonuses in the following months of participation in the program.

Sometimes there are stocks with significantly higher accrual of bonuses for purchases, you need to follow this. The terms for receiving points are usually up to 5 working days, if the purchase amount is not higher than 15,000 rubles, if this limit is exceeded, the terms can increase up to forty days. If the card has been inactive for more than 12 months, all points and bonuses on it are burned out.

You need to spend bonuses there, where the "Thank you" from Sberbank operates. There are a lot of options. Since the terms of the promotion stipulate that 1 bonus is 1 ruble, it is precisely in the amount of accumulated bonuses that the card holder will receive a discount. Such bonuses received today are accepted as a means of payment in tens of thousands of stores and online stores where "Thank you" from Sberbank in Moscow and in general throughout the country. If the cardholder has points in the bonus account, he can exchange them for a discount, the size of which can be up to 99% of the value of the goods. That is, for example, the client has 1000 bonuses in the account. The product he wants to buy costs 2000 rubles. In such a situation, he pays half of the bonuses in the amount of 1000 rubles, and the other half pays in rubles. Another situation: the client has 1000 bonuses on his account, and the product also costs 1000 rubles. Then he pays 999 rubles with bonuses and 1 ruble pays extra, which is very profitable.

However, in order to spend bonuses, you must perform the following steps:

  • at the cash desk where the ‚ÄúThank you‚Äù program from Sberbank is operating, inform the seller in advance that it is planned to spend bonuses under the program;
  • indicate to the seller the amount of bonuses to be debited;
  • pay the rest with a Sberbank card.

The use of bonuses in online stores is built around the same pattern, but there is a feature:

  • in the basket you need to place goods for purchase;
  • Among the payment methods, it is necessary to highlight the payment with "Thank you" bonuses;
  • further on the sites there are always tips on how to make the payment.

Program changes since 07/01/2018

In the summer, in the program "Thank you" from Sberbank there were changes in the terms of use. The main goal of such changes is to increase the number of bank card holders, both debit and credit. The new system has allocated four privilege levels for cardholders. Accordingly, with an increase in the level of privileges, the share of deductions in favor of bonuses is growing. In addition, points began to accumulate not only when paying with a card, but also when performing some tasks. The peculiarity is the fact that if the client has a goal to move to a higher level, then he should use the card more actively.

The new system is based on the same principle: 1 ruble = 1 bonus = 100 rubles spent from the card. If the amount is not equal to 100 rubles, then rounding goes downward. All Sberbank cardholders can still participate in the program.

When points are not awarded

Here is a list of situations when points for using the program are not awarded:

  • Cash withdrawal from the card.
  • Transfer money from one card to another.
  • Currency Conversion.
  • Payment through the Sberbank Online system.
  • If paid for the services of Internet providers and mobile operators.
  • Transfer to electronic wallets.
  • If you buy securities and precious metals.
  • If goods prohibited by the legislation of the Russian Federation are bought.
  • If this is the sixth purchase per day, for the first five charges go.
  • Tuition payment.
  • Communal payments.
  • Payment of fines and penalties.
  • Payment is made with Maestro, MTS or MTS bonus cards, Maestro social, Aeroflot cards.

System flaws

Judging by the reviews, the system has been working very successfully and effectively towards progress for several years. However, it is not without flaws. The main ones are:

  • the terms of the transfer to rubles are not clear;
  • level 3 and 4, most citizens cannot be reached;
  • the list of partners for the implementation of bonuses is rather narrow;
  • if not used for 90 days, bonuses are burned out;
  • continuous monitoring of level tasks;
  • Bonus accrual scheme is not very clear and foggy.
