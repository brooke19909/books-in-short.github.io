SKU is not Martengale

The authors of shocking revelations convict Alexey of unprofessionalism and deliberate wrecking of his colleagues in the workshop, using information about his system of compensation for losses (SKU), which assumes a doubling of investments with an unrealized forecast. The methodology is really similar to the Martenhale system, developed for gambling and not well-proven in options trading.

Traditional strategies using the system are to purchase an option when identifying a signal to enter a deal on the chart. If the forecast did not materialize, then Martenheil adherents offer to open positions in the chosen direction, constantly doubling bets, and so until the money on the deposit runs out ...

Designed by Alexei Vilniusov SKU has fundamental differences from Martenhale:

  • if the entry turned out to be unprofitable, then transactions no longer open and the search for new signals begins;

  • the probability of losing all money due to one unsuccessful entry is minimized, because each new transaction is the result of an in-depth analysis. And the probability of 8 unsuccessful operations is lower than when using the classic Martenhale system.

The reviews left about Aleksey Vilniusov allow us to conclude about the effectiveness of SKU, and most importantly, the acute need for its use.
