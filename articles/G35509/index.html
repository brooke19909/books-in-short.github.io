Cheap hotels in Essentuki: a list with addresses, reviews

The small resort town of Essentuki in the south of the Stavropol Territory attracts many tourists with its wonderful climate, clean air, and most importantly, the famous mineral waters. Since guests come here to relax for more than one day, they are interested in accommodation in low-cost hotels. In Essentuki you can find several options for comfortable budget hotels.

The price for accommodation is indicated without meals. Upon request, breakfast can be included in the room rate. All rooms are equipped with TV-set and air conditioning. Hotel "Orange" is one of the best options among inexpensive hotels in Essentuki. Hotel address: 63 Kislovodskaya Street. The reception is open 24 hours a day.
