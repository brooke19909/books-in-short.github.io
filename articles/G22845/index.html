How to weave figures from gum: a bee, a strawberry, a kitten

If you are interested in how to weave figures from rubber bands, arm yourself with detailed instructions and try your hand at this fascinating matter - it is quite possible that after several trial works you can create your own Santa Claus or a beautiful doll. We offer you step-by-step descriptions of the weaving of the simplest, but very stylish figures.





Bee

To make a keychain or pendant with a bee yourself (you can also use these hardworking babies for home decor), you will need black, yellow and white elastic bands. Yellow can be replaced with lemon or orange. For those who are interested in how to weave figures from rubber bands without a machine, this instruction will come in handy: you only need a hook to create small toys.

First steps

  • Throw on a hook a black elastic band twisted by a figure eight.
  • Pick up the tool with two yellow or orange rubber bands, remove all the black loops from the hook on them.
  • Pick up the new black ‚ÄúFanny Lum‚Äù, drop all the loops on it.
  • Repeat the previous steps.
  • On the last elastic band, discard all the loops remaining on the hook. You will have two black loops, from which you need to make a ring. To do this, pull the left half of the black gum through the right.

Shutdown





  • Cut the resulting large loop in half with scissors.
  • Insert the hook into the middle black rubber band.
  • Crochet two white ‚ÄúFanny Lum‚Äù and pull them into a black loop to make wings.

The bee is ready.

Strawberry

To learn how to weave figures from gum resembling real vegetables and fruits, just turn to the materials of thematic forums and stock up on a standard set of elastic bands and a crochet. Although some (usually the most complex) figures are made on special machines, to create a pretty strawberry you only need the red and green Fanny Lum and a hook.

First stage

  • Put on a hook a red elastic band in three turns.
  • Pick up two new red ‚ÄúFanny Loom‚Äù with the tool and drop all the loops on them.
  • Throw the loop from your finger onto the hook, then repeat the previous steps twice.

Second phase





  • Crochet two green rubber bands, throw loops on them from the tool, then repeat these steps again. If you are interested in how to weave figures from rubber bands, it should be borne in mind that many operations will have to be repeated several times in order to achieve the desired symmetry of the figure.
  • Crochet three red loops from the beginning of weaving, then two new red rubber bands. Drop the loops from the instrument onto them and repeat all the steps described again.
  • Hook the green ‚ÄúFanny Lum‚Äù hook and drop all the rubber bands from the instrument onto it. Make a loop. Now, if desired, this voluminous strawberry can be used as a pendant, keychain or even decoration for a miniature Christmas tree.

Kitten

A cute cat will be a wonderful gift for true friends. To make it, you will need elastic bands of only two contrasting shades, however, if you wish, you can diversify the color scheme of the product and bring your own innovations to the weaving method. The purple and pink Fanny Lum are taken as an example. In addition, you need a hook, a filler (as for soft toys) and a pair of black beads. Those who are curious how to weave figures from rubber bands on the machine will be especially interested in this pretty little thing; It is made on a classic slingshot.

Where to begin





  • Like most figures, the kitten weaves from the hind legs. Dial the usual six-loop lumigurumi ring, then add new rubber bands and increase the foot to a dozen loops. For this purpose, two ‚ÄúFanny Lum‚Äù are carried out in each loop of the row.
  • Weave six rows in the same way. Each will have twelve purple gum.
  • Repeat the previous steps and make the second kitten foot. Already now you can fill them with padding polyester or any other filler on soft toys.
  • Connect the tabs with the hook. Thus, you get one part with a row of twenty-four stitches. Weave two more rows.
  • In the eleventh row, you need to add four "Fanny Lum." If you already know how to weave figures from rubber bands for weaving, it will not be difficult for you to add one violet elastic band on the sides and one in the first and last loop of the row.
  • The next 13 rows are made unchanged. Each should have 28 loops.

How to finish the action figure

  • Starting from the twenty-fifth row, weaving is completed, so it will be necessary to reduce the number of elastic bands. Remove the ‚ÄúFanny Lum‚Äù from those fragments where they were added earlier, that is, on the sides, front and back. One row can be weaved without changes.
  • It will be clear to those who feel confident enough about the technique for making such toys how to weave figures from rubber bands further. In the new row, four gum should be removed again, and then continue to work unchanged for another two rows.
  • Use the rubber bands to attach the eyes - black beads. Now the whole body can be filled with synthetic winterizer or cotton. The top of the figure is connected by ordinary weaving.
  • To make a nose, take three pink rubber bands, wrap them on a hook and pull the fourth ‚ÄúFanny Loom‚Äù through all the loops. Attach the nose to the toy, "embroider" the mouth with the same pink rubber bands.
  • The front legs are made separately: put on a hook a ring of five purple rubber bands and weave them in the usual way for five rows. Fasten the tabs on the body, making simple loops.
