Astana hotels: description

Beauty Astana welcomes travelers with an incredible urban landscape, which combines the best traditions of Eastern and European culture. This is a modern metropolis where you can spend several days excitingly.



The Baiterek Monument, the Nur Astana Mosque, the Palace of Peace and Concord, the ethnopark, Water-Green Boulevard, the oceanarium, city parks and embankments await guests from all over the world. Astana hotels will pleasantly surprise you with the level of service and comfort.

Mukammal

Inexpensive hotels of Astana on the right bank are in demand among budget tourists. Mukammal opened its doors in 2006. This small, cozy hotel is two kilometers from the train station.

35 spacious European-style rooms are available for booking. Mahogany furniture, cosmetics, satellite TV, Wi-Fi, a minibar and a safe - there is everything you need for a comfortable stay.



For a fee, the Mukammal Hotel (Astana, 53/1 Avenue) provides round-the-clock security, concierge services and a shuttle service. A gift shop and luggage storage are available in the lobby. There is also a small spa.

Near the hotel there is the Sports Palace, the Museum of the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan, the Russian Drama Theater and other attractions.

On a note

No matter how modern and ‚Äúadvanced‚Äù the capital may seem, it should be remembered that Astana is primarily an eastern city with its own traditions. For example, during religious holidays, guests should stop drinking alcohol.

For outings and sightseeing, choose your outfit carefully. Sporty style and bright, defiant clothing are a sign of bad taste.

Urban residents are not punctual, because of this the start of business meetings and even large events is often delayed.

Smoking is allowed in Astana, and there are special places in shopping centers, cafes and restaurants. When checking into a hotel, if necessary, it is worth separately voicing a wish for a non-smoking room.

Local drivers often do not follow the rules of the road, so even when moving in the right place, be careful.
