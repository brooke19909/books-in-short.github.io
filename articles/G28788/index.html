Fun City Makadi Bay - one of the best hotels in Hurghada

Located at a distance of 35 km from one of the main tourist destinations in Egypt, Hurghada, the hotel cannot but attract the close attention of numerous vacationers from the very moment of opening.

Ideal location in the elite village of Makadi Bay, warm sea, corals, a huge beach area. What else do you need for an excellent holiday?
