Olympic Lagoon Resort Paphos 5 * (Cyprus / Paphos): photos and reviews of tourists

If you have chosen the island of Cyprus for your vacation, you should know that here you will find not only beautiful weather, warm sea and beautiful beaches. A large number of architectural monuments of antiquity, Christian monasteries and churches, charming landscapes and hospitable inhabitants of the island will fill any vacation here with unforgettable impressions.

It is good to rent a car and go on a trip to different corners of the island, each time discovering its amazing nature and new places that you could not even guess about.

Tourists coming to Cyprus often stay in special apartments or villas. The hotel business here, of course, is also very developed, and choosing a suitable hotel is easy.

Guests of the island can stay in any part of it, because everywhere there are popular resort places. On the western coast of Cyprus, for example, there is a famous city called Paphos. Olympic Lagoon Resort Paphos 5 * is one of the best hotels of the whole island, which is located in this city. This is truly a great place to relax with the whole family on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea.





Kanika Group of Companies

Created back in 1975 as a contractor, today Kanika is one of the most famous in Cyprus. Now it includes three companies:

- Kanika Developments, engaged in the development of projects and the construction of a variety of office and residential buildings;

- Kanika Property Management, whose task is to manage various real estate;

- Kanika Hotel & Resorts, which owns four upscale hotels in Cyprus and exercises their full management. It is among these stunning hotel complexes that the Pearl of the city of Paphos - Olympic Lagoon Resort Paphos 5 * is located.

How did the Olympic Lagoon Resort Hotel in Paphos

The first Olympic Lagoon Resort hotel appeared in Ayia Napa in 2010. He immediately won the title of the most luxurious all-inclusive hotel in Cyprus . Families and couples who wanted to get maximum relaxation and the best service from the rest began to gather here.





Wanting to repeat the success of this hotel and create a similar place in another part of the island, Kanika Hotel & Resorts decides to create another similar hotel. Olympic Lagoon Resort Paphos 5 * (Cyprus) was designed as a place in which everyone could get a delightful and exclusive vacation. Of course, he completed his task one hundred percent.

For all guests of the island of Cyprus, the luxurious Olympic Lagoon Resort Paphos 5 * hotel (Paphos) opened its doors relatively recently: only in the summer of 2015, in the month of June. However, even in such a short time of its existence, the hotel managed to fall in love with tourists and earn fame as one of the best places on the island.

I must say that the building of the Olympic Lagoon Resort Paphos All Inclusive in Paphos is by no means new. It used to be called Amathus Beach Hotel, and many vacationers remember it under that name.
