Pension "Crystal" (Anapa): reviews and photos

Anapa is one of the most popular domestic resorts. This Black Sea city is famous for its warm, mild climate, beautiful exotic nature, healing mineral waters and clean air. Reviews from tourists about boarding houses in Anapa are just wonderful. Well-developed infrastructure, responsive and polite staff, pleasant atmosphere ... So, the money spent on a ticket will not be wasted. One of the most popular local pensions is Crystal, located 20 km from the city center on ul. Pioneer, on the very shore of the sea.

Number of rooms

Vacationers who have bought vouchers to the Kristall boarding house (Anapa) will find comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary for a pleasant stay. Guests are accommodated either in a five-story building, or in one of two cottages. If desired, you can purchase a ticket to the room:

  • standard main, "luxury" or "apartments",
  • on the ground floor
  • in the attic.


All rooms in the five-story building are designed for two people and are equipped with a refrigerator, TV and air conditioning. Each has a toilet, shower and a balcony. The Standard Economy rooms are also equipped in the administrative building of the boarding house.

The rooms in the cottage are quite comfortable. There are both a TV and a refrigerator. However, the rooms here accommodate 3-4 people, there are no air conditioners, and amenities are designed for the unit. Extra beds can only be bought in a five-story building. In cottages such a service is not provided.

Pension "Crystal" (Anapa): reviews

The opinion of tourists about this resort complex was the best. On forums and other portals of relevant topics about the Kristall boarding house, you can find mostly positive reviews. The rooms are rated as very comfortable. The pluses of the institution include the fact that the replacement of linen is carried out strictly according to the schedule. Trash from the rooms is taken out daily.

Tourists who visited the Kristall boarding house (Anapa) and its territory are praised very much. Beautiful lawns, blue spruces, exotic plants, comfortable benches, fountains, spectacular lighting in the evenings - all this makes the rest here as pleasant and comfortable as possible.



Every evening, entertainment is organized on site. The boarding house even has its own signature dance. Many tourists who rested here once dream of returning here again.

In general, the place to stay is very good - the Crystal Hotel (Anapa). Reviews of tourists about him are more than positive. The only drawback vacationers consider only that the sea in this place in August already begins to bloom. Therefore, for those whose holidays are associated primarily with swimming, experienced tourists advise at the end of the season not to go to Anapa, but, for example, to the Lazarevsky district of Sochi.

Entertainment

Vacationers who have bought a ticket to the boarding house "Crystal" (Anapa), can not only plenty and sunbathe and comfortably swim in the warm sea. The administration of this resort institution provides for them various kinds of entertainment programs. For children and adults, charging games, sports competitions on land and on water, evening entertainment are held. Also, if you wish, you can visit some interesting themed celebration or disco.

Excursions

How to get to the boarding house

What route should be followed by those who have purchased vouchers for the Kristall boarding house? Anapa is famous and big enough. Of course, there is a bus station and a railway station. Most tourists get to Anapa by train. From the railway station of the city to the boarding house you need to go by minibus number 128. You should get off at the Dolphinarium stop. You can get to Anapa by plane. From the airport you will first need to get to the city bus station, and then transfer to the same minibus No. 128.



From Moscow to Anapa, among other things, you can also get by bus. Many tourists prefer this method. This resort institution is located at the address: Krasnodar Territory, Anapa (boarding house "Crystal"), Pioneer Avenue, d. 19A.
