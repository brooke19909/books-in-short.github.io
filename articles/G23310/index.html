Where to go on November holidays? Excursions for the November holidays

Summer flew by unnoticed, and again outside the window was a light drizzle and a piercing cold wind. How do you want to break out of your hometown! Well, at least for a couple of days feel like a free person, not burdened with work and everyday difficulties! Fortunately, this dream is quite feasible, as there are several days off ahead of us.



Where to go on November holidays? The choice is huge, and everyone can choose a trip to their liking. But first things first.

Visa-free countries of Europe

If you have not yet become the happy owner of a Schengen visa, but want to leave your native country for a couple of days, do not be discouraged! There are many countries that you can visit without any problems, purchasing tours for the November holidays:

  • Bosnia and Herzegovina . Russian tourists are increasingly casting their eyes on little-known and mysterious lands. This country attracts more and more travelers, and to some extent even became the novelty of the season. The capital of the country, Sarajevo, will amaze you with its European-Turkish flavor, cobbled streets, Christian churches and Muslim mosques.
  • Macedonia This country is located in the very center of the Balkans. Travelers will like tours for the November holidays, as they pass through ancient places and attractions. In this country, travelers can explore not only the architectural monuments of the Middle Ages, but also Antiquity.
  • Serbia This country is famous for its history and natural beauties. Fans of outdoor activities in a few days can afford to get acquainted with the most popular tourist routes. Here you can see ancient monasteries, visit ancient cities and national natural parks.
  • Cyprus Our compatriots can enter the island according to a simplified scheme. It is enough to apply for permission via the Internet, which is issued directly at the airport upon arrival.
  • Bulgaria This hospitable country issues its visas upon first application. Tourists can not only get acquainted with cultural attractions, but also go shopping. Prices are relatively low, and European goods are of excellent quality.




Asia

There are many options where you can go for the November holidays. Asian countries welcome Russian travelers in November.

  • Turkey Attracts tourists from all over the world with comparative low cost, comfortable hotels and high quality service. During the autumn holidays, you can allow yourself to relax and enjoy national meat dishes and sweets. The weather is great for walking around the city and sightseeing tours. Also, visitors can walk around the famous Turkish bazaars and shops.
  • Israel This is another inexpensive destination, popular with Russians on November holidays. Where should a tourist go for a few days, if not to a country with a rich spiritual and historical heritage? In addition, here you can relax from the stress of the autumn months and visit the legendary Israeli spa centers. Young people will not be bored in this amazing country, as it has at its disposal a lot of nightclubs and discos.
  • China For just $ 30 you can get a 21-day visa to Hainan. Many consider it an alternative to Egypt and enjoy traveling for a few days to a tropical paradise. Travelers are attracted by the clear sea, hot climate and rich cultural traditions. Here you can go diving and surfing, take your child to the monkey park, try exotic dishes and much more.
  • Thailand . Those who managed to miss the summer sun go to this country. Russians make trips here for no less than eight days, and all this time they are charged with the summer positive of a wonderful country.




Africa

The countries of the African continent are ready to meet Russian travelers, but our compatriots so far choose only one direction - Egypt. It was even traditional to come here for the November holidays. He is ready to offer guests sea, sun, fresh fruits and numerous excursion programs.

Countries of North, Central and South America

Unfortunately, overseas beauties are not yet affordable for the majority of the population of our country. But if you have the financial ability and the supply of free time, you are free to decide for yourself where to go for the November holidays. Recently, Russian travelers are increasingly looking at Liberty Island and Jamaica. Also, those who wish can easily visit Argentina, Bolivia, Paraguay and Chile.

  • Krakow . This old Polish city has always enjoyed the attention of travelers. Guests are interested in its thousand-year history, museums, theaters, churches and castles.

Countries of the former USSR

Russians, as in the good old days, continue to visit friends and relatives from the former Soviet republics. Where to go on November holidays?

  • Vilnius You will find a short flight, the absence of a language barrier and "extreme" nutrition. Tourists also love this city for its active cultural life and a large number of events. Excursions for the November holidays around the city and the country will cheer you up, and you will remember the hospitality of local residents for a long time on winter evenings.
  • Tallinn It is famous for its historical monuments, which are more here than in any other European city. Be sure to stroll through its historical center - the Old Town. You will be surprised, but each time, walking through familiar places, you will find something new.




Cities of Russia

Perhaps you do not want to leave your home country? Then the autumn holidays are a great reason to travel along the Golden Ring or through historical and cultural centers.

St. Petersburg

If you have not been to this beautiful city, then take the opportunity and appreciate all the charm of the northern capital. We are sure that you will definitely come back here more than once.

Kazan

Over the past decades, the city has become a real gem in the crown of Russia. Be sure to go to the Kremlin, take a walk along the pedestrian zone, have fun at the water park and try Tatar cuisine.

Yaroslavl

For the few days that you spend in this city, you will be imbued with the old Russian spirit. Where to go on November holidays? What interesting can you see? Of course, ancient Orthodox churches and cathedrals, historical and art museums.
