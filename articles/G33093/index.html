Needlework lessons for young mothers: how to knit socks for newborns with knitting needles

In anticipation of the birth of a baby, many young women begin to master various types of needlework, including knitting. Who, if not a loving mother, can create the cutest and most comfortable little thing for the baby? One of the most necessary items of children's wardrobe are socks. The baby loses heat very quickly, so he will need warm ‚Äúclothes on the legs‚Äù from the very first minutes of his life. A young mother should think about their preparation in advance. About how to knit socks for newborns with knitting needles, we talk in this article. The attention of readers is given a description of the implementation of this wardrobe item "from A to Z". Even a novice knitter, having studied it, can easily perform such a product.



Preparatory stage

In order to independently perform knitting socks for newborns with needles, you will need about 10-15 grams of yarn of medium thickness (100 g / 200 m). Children's things should be pleasant to the body, so for their manufacture, choose peeled wool or wool mixture. If you want colored socks, then choose yarn of any shades, but the thickness and composition of all threads should be the same, otherwise the product will turn out shapeless and ugly. We prepare knitting needles for work in stockings (socks) No. 3 in the amount of 5 pieces.

We knit cuffs

For two knitting needles, folded together, dial 20 loops. Pull one spoke. Do this very carefully so as not to miss the loop. We begin to perform socks for the newborn with knitting needles, namely their upper part - cuffs. All loops are evenly distributed over 4 knitting needles (5 pieces each). Next, knit 5 rows with a 1 x 1 elastic band: alternating 1 front loop and 1 wrong loop. After that, perform from 6th to 12th rows with facial loops.



Heel stage

We master knitting for newborns (socks) with knitting needles. Transfer the stitches from the 1st and 2nd knitting needles to one. Further, only they participate in the work. From the 13th to the 18th rows, perform hosiery knitting (alternating front and back loops). From the next row we round the heel. We do this using the following technology. 19th row: 6 facial loops, 2 together, turn the work over. 20th row: edge (remove), 2 purl loops, 2 together, turn the product. 21st row: edge, 2 front, 2 together, turn the work. Thus, knit out the heel with a decrease on both sides until 4 loops remain on the spoke.

We learn to knit socks for newborns with knitting needles. Shaping the foot

Next, we perform all the rows in a circular facial loops. Knit the 4 remaining loops, then dial and draw 4 loops on the left side edge of the heel. Next, do the stitches left on the 3rd and 4th knitting needles. Now dial 4 loops on the right side of the heel and knit them. As a result, the knitting needles should have 22 loops. Now knit 11 rows in a circle without decrease. Then, at the end and at the beginning of each knitting needle, reduce the number of loops by 1. Decorate the next row without decrease. In the last row, knit all the loops 2 together, fasten and cut the thread. The product is ready. Similarly pair to it.



You have learned how to knit socks for newborns with knitting needles. There is no scheme for this particular embodiment of the product, since it is made out in the simplest pattern - stocking and elastic 1 x 1. Feel free to pick up knitting needles and strings and start needlework. In just an hour or two you will see the result - small, beautiful socks.
