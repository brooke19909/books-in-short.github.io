The only military sanatorium in Russia "Yalta"

The only naval sanatorium in Russia is the Yalta sanatorium, which is located in the center of a beautiful city of the same name. This resort is considered one of the most popular and sought-after holiday destinations. Proximity to the sea, excellent infrastructure and a picturesque park area give this corner a special zest. This military sanatorium "Yalta" is located on the southern coast of Crimea. Thanks to the slopes of the mountains that descend to the sea, the coast is reliably protected from the cold northerly winds.

Wonderful sea climate, hot sun, warm winter and a very long bathing season provide favorable conditions not only for relaxation, but also for treatment. A good microclimate and medical base can strengthen the immune system and get rid of many diseases. Diseases of the organs of the nervous system, respiratory and digestive organs, the musculoskeletal system, and the gastrointestinal tract are specialties of treatment at the Yalta sanatorium. That is why it is necessary to rest at least once a year for the benefit of health, and the military sanatorium "Yalta" is very suitable for this. The medical base of this resort is very extensive, all modern and effective methods of treatment are used here - a mud bath, a hydropathic center, a hydromassage, an electric light therapy room, physiotherapy exercises, a dental and massage room, a gym and mineral water treatment. A resort such as the sanatorium Ukraine Yalta uses climatotherapy and physiotherapy, as well as therapeutic nutrition. The medical staff in the sanatorium is only highly qualified and with extensive experience, which allows you to fully entrust them with their health.

As for entertainment, the military sanatorium "Yalta" invites tourists to spend time in the reading room, play volleyball or visit a tennis court. In the military sanatorium you can enjoy a magnificent sauna and a steam bath in a real bathhouse. For children there is a special playground. For adults - a dance floor, a billiard room and a concert hall, where they organize music concerts, evenings and artists come on tour. Here you can also watch the latest adaptations or your favorite movie. For business people who come to this sanatorium to combine business with pleasure, that is, relax with work, a conference room is equipped with all necessary and modern devices. Tours are conducted both on foot and by car. For sports enthusiasts, the Yalta health resort Kuibyshev offers various simulators and local tourism along the Stangeevskaya, Botkinsky and Solnechnaya trails, as well as Lake Karagol. For tourists who prefer to rest on a personal car, a paid guarded parking lot is available.

Accommodation at the resort military sanatorium "Yalta" is possible in rooms of various levels - double with amenities, double junior suite, room "Suite". The double rooms have two beds, a fridge, a TV and a bathroom with a shower. The junior suite also has air conditioning and a loggia with outdoor furniture. The Suite room, as the most equipped, is equipped with a set of upholstered furniture, cable TV, a separate toilet and bath, a TV, a fridge and a loggia, also with outdoor furniture. Vacationers are offered three meals a day, as well as a custom-made menu and diet food.

Well and most importantly, what beach resorts offer is the beach. Yalta invites tourists to enjoy the beauty of a clean and comfortable beach. The pebble and comfortable beach in the sanatorium is equipped with solariums, tents, sunbeds, changing rooms, a shower and a toilet. And of course, like on any specialized beach, there is a first-aid post here. A bar, rental water bikes, boats and a playground are on site.

In general, the sanatorium "Yalta" is an excellent vacation for the whole family.
