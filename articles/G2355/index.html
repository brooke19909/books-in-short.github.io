Hotel Magnolia, Sochi: description, rooms, photos and reviews

A great option for a summer vacation is the Magnolia Hotel. Sochi is one of the most popular tourist destinations due to its beautiful nature and well-developed infrastructure. As for this hotel, it will complement the picture of positive impressions.





Hotel Magnolia (Sochi): Location

If you want to relax in the very center of Sochi, then this hotel complex is just perfect for you. Just a 10-minute walk from the sea coast, the Magnolia Hotel is located. Sochi, Resort Prospect, 50 - the address of this hotel. The distance to the international airport is about 40 kilometers. You can visit these nearby attractions:

  • City Art Museum;
  • Cathedral of the Archangel Michael;
  • Caucasian Biosphere Reserve.

Resort Infrastructure

Hotel "Magnolia" in Sochi offers its guests to use the following infrastructure facilities:

  • private parking with 24-hour security;
  • at the reception there is a safe in which each of the guests can rent a cell for storing valuables;
  • if your luggage is too bulky, then you can transfer it to the storage room; at the reception you can exchange currency at a favorable rate;
  • on the territory of the hotel there are shops where you can buy souvenirs, products and essentials;
  • in the laundry, carefully and efficiently clean your clothes;
  • hairdressing and beauty services can be used in the beauty salon;
  • the travel agency will select for you interesting and eventful excursions;
  • for those who love sports, a modern fitness center;
  • if cloudy weather prevents you from sunbathing on the beach, you can visit the solarium;
  • For business events, a conference room is provided.




Hotel Policies

Each hotel has its own policies. The hotel complex "Sochi-Magnolia" is governed by the following rules:

  • Check-in is after 13:00, and check-out is before noon;
  • employees have the right to refuse registration to guests who arrived at the hotel while intoxicated;
  • even if you stay in the hotel for less than 24 hours, payment is charged per day;
  • foreign citizens are charged a registration fee of 300 rubles;
  • if you do not check in to the booked room on time, then you will be charged a downtime penalty (in the amount of the cost of a daily stay);
  • Payment for the entire stay is made at registration;
  • if you plan to extend your stay at the hotel, you must notify the administration about this no later than one day in advance;
  • when checking into a hotel with children, their birth certificates must be presented;
  • upon prior request, cots can be provided absolutely free of charge;
  • Before eviction, you should invite the maid on duty for inspection of the room and get the appropriate ticket;
  • staying in the guest room upon presentation of a passport is possible from 8:00 to 23:00 (if the visitor is delayed, then he must pay for the stay);
  • in case of smoke or flooding, hotel employees have the right to enter the room without the knowledge of the guest;
  • If the hotel caused material damage to you due to your fault, you agree to indemnify it according to the price list;
  • Accommodation with pets is possible only by agreement with the hotel;
  • if the guest systematically violates the public order and hotel rules, the administration has the right to unilaterally terminate the accommodation contract.

Negative reviews

Many resort hotels operate in Sochi. "Magnolia 3 *" (Russia, Sochi) is characterized by the following negative aspects:

  • personnel do not respond to Do Not Disturb signs;
  • the carpet in the rooms and in the hallway is very dirty and gives off an unpleasant odor;
  • constant interruptions in the operation of the elevator;
  • a rather steep descent leads to the sea, and if it is easy enough to descend, it is very difficult to ascend back;
  • ventilation in the bathroom does not work well, and therefore there is constant dampness;
  • not very tasty desserts in a cafe;
  • the elevators are very cramped, and therefore it is almost impossible to fit there with a stroller with a child;
  • in the corridors of the hotel ventilation is practically not working, it is incredibly stuffy there;
  • the room does not have a refrigerator for food (only a mini-cooler for drinks);
  • poor quality of cleaning in the room;
  • despite the fact that the absence of business cards at the reception is a trifle, nevertheless this is a flaw;
  • in the morning it is very noisy due to the technique that cleans the street;
  • there are no handrails in the bathroom, and therefore there is a risk of slipping;
  • air conditioners in the rooms are located directly above the bed, and therefore they have to be turned off at night so as not to get sick.

General impression

Enough diverse impressions are caused by tourists located in Sochi, the hotel "Magnolia". Accommodation prices are rising annually. At the same time, nothing new appears in the hotel. On the contrary, the number of rooms is pretty outdated, and the quality of breakfast for several years has noticeably worsened. Nevertheless, the hotel does not lose its popularity. This is due not only to a convenient location, but also to the possibility of free access to the water park and private beach. Thus, this hotel is ideal for active tourists and families with children who want to get a vivid impression of the city and do not pay attention to shortcomings in the service.
