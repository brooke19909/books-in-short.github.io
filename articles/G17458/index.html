North Goa, Calangute. Travel Reviews for Calangute

For several decades, North Goa has been attracting European tourists for its beauty and exoticism. Calangute is one of the small resort towns of this Indian state. Holidays in this area can not be called aristocratic, so high-quality service can not be expected. People who seek solitude and peace will not like it here. But Calangute will become a favorite resort for cheerful, active, restless tourists who want to spend a memorable vacation in a delightfully beautiful place for a moderate fee.

Location of Calangute



One of the most environmentally friendly states of India is Goa. Calangute is located in its center, on the coast of the Arabian Sea. The beach line passing through the town is about 10 km long and stretches from Sinkerim to Bagi. The resort has a very good location, because it is convenient to get to any corner of North Goa from it. It takes less than an hour to get from Dabolim International Airport to Calangute, and it is also 18 km from the capital of the state of Panaji. From this village it is convenient to get to other beaches and places of entertainment located in nearby towns.

Calangute was originally an ordinary fishing village. Rich citizens came here to rest from the unbearable heat, because the fresh sea breeze always blows on the coast. In the 60s of the last century, North Goa was discovered by western tourists. Calangute became a hippy center, for local residents the behavior of foreigners was both frightening and interesting, so people came from nearby villages to look at the visitors. Over time, the village turned into a pretty town with developed infrastructure. Today it is a popular Indian resort for a budget holiday.

Calangute beaches



A wide sandy strip of volcanic muddy color in the shape of a crescent moon is located near the Arabian Sea, Calangute Beach. Goa (India) boasts many convenient and clean coasts, but in this resort town they are the most crowded. Wave lovers will like the sea in Calangute, because it is rarely calm here, but you will not find it too raging. The descent is gentle and very convenient for both adults and children.

Private and not crowded can not be called Calangute Beach. Sightseeing Tours



Unfortunately, Calangute cannot boast of a variety of local attractions. In the town, tourists can visit the church of St. Alex, built four centuries ago, notable for its inlaid altar and interesting architecture. Many travelers enjoy attending Indian music and dance evenings. For art lovers, there is the Kerkar Gallery, which hosts art exhibitions.

Those who want to learn more about Goa, Calangute (tourist reviews confirm excellent service during excursions) will help organize a trip to any corner of India. The successful location of this resort town allows you to go on a trip to the waterfalls, through the jungle, spice plantations, to the abandoned city of Hampi, busy Mumbai.

Kitchen

Goa is famous for its delicious exotic dishes. Calangute will satisfy the needs of tourists with different culinary preferences. For a meal on the beach, go to nearby cafes and bars. Fans of exquisite dishes should go to the center of the town, where restaurants with Italian, European, Tibetan and Goan cuisine are located. The beach cafes offer visitors a rich selection of seafood and fish dishes, and friendly hosts will also entertain with interesting stories.

Reviews of tourists about the beaches



How many people, so many opinions. Someone likes peace, peaceful atmosphere, solitude, someone cannot imagine life without noisy companies, screams of traders, crush and fuss. In Calangute, the beach is sandy, the entrance to the sea is shallow, deck chairs with umbrellas are provided on the coast, there are many bars and cafes, so no one will die of hunger. Experienced tourists warn of crowding, the presence of beggars and annoying touts, it is sometimes very difficult to get rid of them. The peak of the season is December, January, it was then that the largest number of Indians and Russians gathered in Goa. If you arrive in November or February, you can enjoy the relative peace and quiet.

Reviews of tourists about the resort as a whole

Calangute is considered one of the best resort towns in North Goa. It has developed infrastructure, a high level of service, the choice of housing is available in a large price range, you can settle in expensive 5-star hotels, as well as in individual bungalows or economical hotels. At the peak of the season it is not easy to walk around the city, especially in its central part, where there is a crowd of locals and foreigners. Compared with other Indian resorts, Calangute is the most developed and well-equipped of them. Everyone who wants to have fun, relax with body and soul, look at the exotic, should go to the address: India, Goa, Calangute.
