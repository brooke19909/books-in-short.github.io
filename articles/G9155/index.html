Philips Garment Steamer - A One-Stop Home Helper



Thanks to modern household appliances, everyday life is becoming more comfortable, and any household chores are much easier. Every year new, more and more advanced devices appear. One of these innovative miracles can rightly be considered a garment steamer Philips. Comparing this device with an iron is rather incorrect, because even though these technical means have one purpose - ironing things, their working principle is somewhat different.

Functional Features

There are several models of vertical household steamers in the Philips assortment, but they practically do not differ in their functions. So, the principle of operation of such devices is very simple and consists in the following: they heat water to a very high temperature, as a result of which steam forms, which is supplied under pressure to the nozzle with holes. In such a very simple way very laborious tasks are solved, which the iron can not cope with. The Philips Garment Steamer makes it easy to smooth out even the smallest details on any garment, folds and various difficult areas that are very difficult to reach with an iron. At the same time, you don‚Äôt have to worry about the possible damage to even the most delicate tissues, because in the process of using the device direct contact with the tissue does not occur.

Design differences



Despite the similarity of functions, different models may differ in their device and size. There are vertical and manual devices. For example, the Philips gc670 garment steamer has a vertical design. It is somewhat reminiscent of a vacuum cleaner equipped with a special tripod for a hanger and a tube holder with a nozzle. Manual models are compact in size and can have both horizontal and vertical design.

Variety of models

Modern steamers have a large number of functions and are single-mode and multi-mode. Professional devices like the Philips Garment Steamer feature a variety of modes and the ability to adjust the steam supply. As for single-mode models, they work with the same intensity set by the manufacturer. When choosing a device, you need to decide on the functional set that you may need. After all, one may need the maximum number of functions, while for others this will seem overkill.

Additional features

For example, users who have already mastered the garment steamer will leave the most favorable reviews (Philips). And this is not strange, because this universal device can be used not only for ironing clothes. It can easily remove unpleasant odors and stains, as well as completely destroy the harmful bacteria that accumulate in the tissues. Moreover, the Philips garment steamer tidies not only clothes, but also other household items - upholstered furniture, curtains, tablecloths, bedding, bedspreads, etc. Today, these multifunctional devices are widely used in sewing industries, in furniture and clothing stores, in hotels and sanatoriums.
