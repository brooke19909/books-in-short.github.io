Iron "Bosch" - an indispensable home appliance

Iron is a familiar item in everyday life. Each housewife has it at hand and sometimes does not think about how much progress can be made in improving this device. If it is broken, then only the best manufacturer should choose a new device. Undoubtedly, a Bosch iron will attract your attention in the household appliance store, its elegant exterior design and characteristics.





The budget model of iron

If you are guided by the cost of technology, then a budget model will suit you. When choosing electrical appliances, you need to pay attention to its most important part - the sole. It can be made of stainless steel or aluminum. But these materials have a significant minus: during operation of the device scratches appear, they worsen the sliding of the iron. The 2200 Watt device is lightweight and easy to use. Works from a network. The power cord is 1.8 meters. The iron provides a constant steam supply during ironing at a speed of 22 g per minute. Steam boost - eighty g / min. The water tank that the Bosch iron is equipped with is designed for 220 ml. The device can be used for vertical steam. There are anti-scale and self-cleaning systems.





Devices with advanced features

More expensive models have several additional features. For example, the Bosch TDA-5650 has an anti-drop system. It allows you to avoid the leakage of water on the laundry during operation. The ironing power is affected by the steam supply power. The high-quality Bosch iron provides its feed constantly, up to 40 g / min. In an upright position, you can steam things with a steam boost of 120 g / min. With this function, you can process clothes without even removing them from the hanger. It only takes a few seconds. Such a Bosch iron has a larger capacity for water - 300 ml. The handle has a comfortable Soft-touch coating. More expensive models are equipped with a sole with a ceramic coating. It is durable, resistant to scratches and ensures flawless operation. Most home appliance models heat up very quickly, which saves time.





The advanced appliances that Bosch manufactures are irons with an integrated steam generator. The power of this model is 2400 watts. The strength of the steam boost is two hundred g / min, which allows you to cope with the ironing of a large volume of laundry in a short time. Two and a half meters long power cord has a hinged mount, cleaning fluid is included. Another important plus in devices manufactured by a well-known company is the special anti-limescale AntiCalc system. It is a cartridge made of resin. It holds the scale and prevents it from falling into the iron. The self-cleaning system removes scale particles from the steam chamber. During operation, water can even be used directly from the tap. True, they recommend adding a special tool to it. Then there is a chance to extend the life of the device.

Thus, having studied the characteristics of the device and the reviews about it (which are positive), we can conclude that the Bosch iron is a good purchase for any housewife. After all, this well-known household appliance company always cares about its consumers and produces only quality products.
