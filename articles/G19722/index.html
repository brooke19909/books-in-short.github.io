Pattern of women's summer overalls: a do-it-yourself universal product

The overalls, which we are so used to wearing in childhood and considered an element of clothing exclusively at a young age, are incredibly comfortable and suitable for women with almost any figure.



It is not for nothing that the leading couturiers of our time every season replenish their collections with embodied ideas of this particular subject. The summer women's jumpsuit is especially indispensable , the pattern of which does not have to differ in pretentiousness and complexity, because it is simplicity and classic that are valued from year to year. He can replace a business suit and evening dress, suitable for a mountain hike or a romantic meeting, the whole thing is only in style. A correctly selected pattern of women's summer overalls will ensure the most elegant fit of the product exactly in shape, while discreetly hiding its minor flaws and emphasizing the best sides.
For example, owners of beautiful legs should buy a model with short shorts and wear them with blouses that cover the shoulders without a deep neckline and with shoes. Such an image will look unusually sexy and expressive. And if it is somewhat too expensive or unavailable for other reasons, there will be no better option how to sew a women's jumpsuit . Its pattern is not as complicated as it might seem at first glance, because there are many needlework tricks to simplify sewing and personalization. To do this, you just need to choose the right style, material, invent an exceptional decor.



Training

A pattern of summer women's overalls from light knitwear or even a pastel shade of chiffon will help you create a product that will look very romantic. To add a drop of sexuality, we will make it with an open back and short shorts, and to emphasize the tenderness we use a bow-shaped decor and a shallow neckline. The first needlework trick: if you use your shirt and shorts instead of patterns, then an additional pattern of women's summer overalls will not even be needed. To do this, we put things along in two parts, and then on a two-layer piece of fabric we circle their contour with dry soap, not forgetting the allowances. It is advisable to make them loose in order to further obtain cute assemblies on the belt and so that the product does not hamper your movements.



Sewing

Thus, the pattern of women's summer overalls is made in three stages: the manufacture of the top, bottom and their connection with each other. For sewing shorts, we first circle the front part (first), and then the back (second), and sew them in pairs 1 + 2 with side seams, and then with internal ones. To make the top, we need four details: the front two in the form of vests, which are joined together by button fasteners, and the rear ones are of the same shape, only with the neckline cut to the lumbar region and the straps for binding them into an elegant bow. When attaching the top and bottom, it is better to make a double seam, inside which you can insert either an elastic band or a thick cord to tighten at the waist.
