Lloret de Mar resort in Spain

For a comfortable climate and developed infrastructure, this city is often called the second Ibiza, but the cost of rest here is an order of magnitude lower than in other Spanish resorts. Located an hour's drive from the capital of the autonomous region of Catalonia, Lloret de Mar has a centuries-old history, which locals will gladly tell about.

A bit of history

Initially, the territory was inhabited by ancient Iberian tribes, and later Roman soldiers settled here. New residents turned the settlement into a shopping center, establishing links with other cities. The first mention of the village dates back to 966 AD.

The fishing village, which turned into a big city, flourished throughout its existence. In the XVIII century, the first wealthy rebuild palaces, surprising a special luxury.

The economic prosperity of Lloret de Mar (Spain) continued until the 20th century, and many years of stagnation ended in 1950 thanks to the tourism business. The charming city, hosting hundreds of thousands of guests, creates all the conditions for a comfortable stay.

Resort specifics

Judging by the reviews of the guests, they are attracted to the well-maintained and incredibly picturesque beaches that Lloret de Mar is famous for . Lloret de Mar (Spain) has its own specifics, which should be known to future travelers. The colorful city is called the youth capital of the country, and a large number of young men and women among vacationers will be a confirmation of this.





Local authorities know the features of the resort, so the beaches are equipped with paraphernalia for outdoor activities, and the Porto Pi ship always runs along the coast, which all young guests of the city try to get to have a great time at a fun disco on the high seas.

The main attraction of the city

White beaches stretching for nine kilometers have been awarded the international award for high quality standards. The perfect cleanliness of the bottom and shore pleasantly surprises even the most demanding tourists. The beaches of Lloret de Mar are mostly free, but for the rental of equipment you will have to pay a certain amount of banknotes. Everywhere is full of attractions where you can have fun alone or with children, in addition, lovers of extreme water recreation will not be left out of work.

Most popular beach

Even without going outside the city, tourists can get to the most famous sandy beach on the coast, spread over a kilometer and a half in length - Lloret de Mar beach. Vacationers are warned that it is deep enough here, and even experienced athletes are not recommended to swim further than 50 meters.





The popular Lloret beach is extremely crowded, and the inhabitants of the city bypass it, choosing more secluded places for their vacation.

Holidays for every taste

Another favorite beach for tourists is La Caleta. An unusually picturesque corner is suitable for those who dream of a quiet pastime. The purest sand without a single stone, the complete absence of debris and strong waves will be enjoyed by couples with children and lovers.

But lovers surrounded by cliffs, where there are no people at all, choose campers. In the desert corners come not only tourists enjoying stunning sunsets and sunrises, but also Spaniards with large companies.

It is not for nothing that they say that every vacationer will spend time in the resort with benefit, and there will be entertainment for every taste and budget.

Picturesque Gardens

The ancient city, rich in sights, will delight all tourists. Natural and man-made monuments, famous for their diversity, will remain in the memory of visitors forever.





On the outskirts of the city of Lloret de Mar hides an amazing park, considered the national treasure of Catalonia. Performed in the style of the Renaissance "Gardens of Saint Clotilde" are admired by everyone who fell into a natural miracle. A beautifully preserved masterpiece was built with only one purpose - to show the complete merger of man with nature. The landscaped Gardens Santa Clotilde located on a cliff amazes with unforgettable landscapes and harmony prevailing everywhere.

The ticket costs 5 euros, and this is a very small fee to see firsthand the elegant and luxurious botanical garden, in which the amazing beauty of Catalonia is immortalized.
