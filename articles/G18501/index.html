Papillon Ayscha Hotel (Turkey / Belek): photos and reviews of tourists

The popular Turkish resort of Belek, located between Side and Antalya, is famous, first of all, for its many kilometers of convenient beaches, many of which have been awarded the Blue Flag for their cleanliness. Most tourists consider this city a real paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. Despite the fact that this resort is young, there are a lot of good hotels built here. For example, some Russian tour operators sell tickets to Belek with accommodation at the Papillon Ayscha Hotel.

Where is the complex located?

Getting to the place for tourists who have rented rooms at this hotel will not be particularly difficult. At least the road here would not seem too tedious to them. Domestic holidaymakers arrive who decide to stay at the Papillon Ayscha Hotel , usually at Antalya Airport. The distance from it to the hotel is only 34 km. That is, vacationers arriving by plane on a tour operator's transfer bus can arrive in about 40-50 minutes. The distance from the center of Antalya to the hotel is about 40 km.





This complex was not built in the village of Belek, but about 5 km from it. The closest beach is just a few hundred meters away. Tourists living in this complex can get to the sea on foot in about 5-7 minutes.

Hotel Description

In size, the Papillon Ayscha Hotel (Turkey) is superior to many other similar Belek complexes. The total area occupied by this hotel is 60 thousand m 2 . This convenient complex was built relatively recently - in 2001. At the same time, the last renovation in 2017 was carried out in 2014. That is, the interiors and furniture in this hotel are not too old.

The exterior of the hotel and the design of its territory are also praised by many tourists. The very design of the courtyard of the complex is able to create a holiday mood for the guests at sea. They accept guests at this hotel in several two-story villas and in one main building. Behind the main hotel building are all the main elements of its infrastructure. According to tourists, the territory of this hotel is well-groomed and well landscaped. Tenek here will be easy to find for vacationers.





What facilities are provided in the rooms?

Rooms for rent at Papillon Ayscha Hotel include:

  • air conditioners and televisions;
  • telephones and minibars;
  • hairdryers, bathrobes and slippers;
  • safes.

The floors in the hotel rooms are carpeted. This can be especially convenient for tourists coming to a hotel with children.

Most of the rooms in this hotel are standard rooms. The hotel also has family rooms, suites and some other types of apartments. There is one hotel room in the hotel. The villas in the hotel rent luxury rooms and VIP-rooms.





What is the opinion of tourists about the accommodation offered at the hotel?

Accommodation at the Papillon Ayscha Hotel, according to tourists, is quite comfortable. They let guests here often even before checkout time. When checking in, the residents are required to explain the rules of the hotel and provide information on what features its infrastructure has (in Russian).

The rooms for tourists who decide to relax in Belek, the hotel rent, judging by the reviews, spacious and reasonably clean. The beds in the rooms of the hotel, as tourists note, are comfortable. Among the pluses of this hotel, among other things, guests include the fact that the safes here can be used absolutely free. Pretty good in the hotel rooms and catches a Wi-Fi network.

The maids replenish the minibar at the hotel daily. Air conditioning at the hotel is centralized.

Some complaints from tourists are mainly only in terms of cleaning the rooms. Many guests would recommend the owners of this hotel to increase the staff of maids. Hotel employees try to carry out their work carefully. But due to the large number of rooms in the hotel, they are forced to clean very quickly. And this, in turn, of course, greatly affects the quality of work performed.





Hotel infrastructure

The facilities at the Papillon Ayscha Hotel Resort itself are many. No wonder this hotel belongs to the class of four stars. Tourists staying at the hotel have the opportunity to use, among other things:

  • five outdoor pools;
  • several water slides;
  • indoor pool;
  • parking.

The hotel also has a beauty salon, massage room, hammam, sauna. If desired, the guests of the complex can visit the hairdresser here or exchange rubles for local currency. In addition, the complex has as many as 6 stores of various specializations.

Sports and Entertainment

Of course, equipped in this modern four-star hotel and a gym. Hotel guests can also play volleyball, darts, billiards or tennis. The hotel also offers bike rental. Two tennis courts in the hotel are equipped at once. The residents of the hotel have the opportunity to play golf. However, the field is not located in the hotel itself, but about 4 km from it. There is also an equestrian club next to the hotel.





Staff

Judging by the reviews, the staff of this complex behave politely towards their guests. But tourists from Russia in a hotel, unfortunately, do not feel particularly friendly attitude. The staff at the Papillon Ayscha Hotel Resort Spa, as vacationers note, is rather gloomy, they only serve tourists from Germany who give large tips.

The lack of special hospitality, according to some tourists, may also be due to the fact that the staff of this hotel have to work many hours a day. In any case, for example, in 2017, tourists at the hotel were also served by citizens of Kyrgyzstan. They had to work at least 18 hours a day.

Opinion of tourists about the hotel infrastructure

To relax in the hotels of the Papillon Ayscha Hotel (Belek) , according to tourists, is quite comfortable. All elements of infrastructure on the territory of this hotel, as vacationers note, are placed very compactly. Swimming in the pools at the hotel is convenient. True, the water here, as some guests note, is chilly by the end of the season.

Also praised by tourists at the hotel animations. The shows and sports contests at the hotel are actually very interesting. In particular, they try, as tourists say, animators during children's events. Wi-Fi in this hotel catches well even on the beach. This tourists, of course, also refer to the advantages of the hotel.

Another advantage of this hotel, its residents consider the presence in the territory of a small garden. If desired, hotel guests can stroll in the shade of date palms, citrus fruits, cedars, etc.

Papillon Ayscha Hotel Food Reviews

The dining room of this hotel also earned relatively good reviews from guests. In any case, the food at this hotel is satisfying. Tourists never go hungry. The local chefs cook quite tasty. Hotel guests really like, for example, fried fish served in the hotel restaurant. Also in the dining room of the complex, guests can take salads, meat, side dishes, greens, etc.





The all-inclusive Papillon Ayscha Hotel has a restaurant. Between lunch and dinner, guests can grab a bite to eat at the on-site snack bar. There is also a children's area in the dining room. Here, for example, soups or semolina may be served.

Not too good reviews from tourists deserved mainly only the desserts and coffee offered by the machine offered in the restaurant. They are in this hotel, according to its residents, not particularly tasty.

What is a hotel beach?

Upon arrival at the Papillon Ayscha Hotel, guests are issued cards for beach towels. Hotel bracelets are not provided in this complex. Actually the beach at the hotel itself is compact and, judging by the reviews, quite comfortable for relaxing. The coastline opposite the hotel is covered with sand and small pebbles. Sun loungers by the sea near the hotel guests can use for free. Many tourists consider the pluses of the hotel's beach, among other things, the fact that instead of umbrellas there are large tents.





Guests receive towels at this hotel at the pick-up point near the pool, under the water slides. Some tourists find this somewhat uncomfortable. Usually in four-star hotels in Turkey, such an accessory is issued at the entrance to the beach.
