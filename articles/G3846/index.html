Popular resorts in the Krasnodar Territory: Lermontovo

Every year, the village of Lermontovo in the Krasnodar Territory meets thousands of people who want to relax and gain strength on the Black Sea coast. Many hotels, hotels, guest houses, as well as rooms and apartments in the private sector welcome guests of the resort.

Lermontovo - what is it?

In the foothills of the Caucasus in the Tuapse district is Lermontovo - a cozy small village spread along the coast of the Tenginsky Bay. Lermontovo is surrounded by dense forests, along the main road runs the mountain river Shapsuho.





The village of Lermontovo (Krasnodar Territory) was built in the middle of the 20th century. Picturesque nature, the proximity of the sea and excellent beaches brought him the glory of a family resort. In the center of the village there is everything you need: a market and a pharmacy, shops and a post office, a night club and a cafe.

Natural conditions and weather

The village of Lermontovo (Krasnodar Territory) is conveniently located near the sea, which gives off its heat in winter. From the winds the village is covered by the mountains of the Caucasian ridge. Mountain forests fill the clean air with aromas of greenery and pine needles.

The climate in Lermontovo is Mediterranean subtropical, mild, without temperature differences. The air temperature in summer is +25 ... + 35 0 C.

The swimming season begins in May, when the water warms up to +17 0 , in June, sea water becomes much warmer, up to +26 0 .

Beach

The total length of the beach line in Lermontovo is more than 2 km, the width of pebble-sand beaches reaches 50 m. Everywhere there is a convenient gentle entry into the water, safe for children.

If you go in the direction of Sochi, you can settle down on a 10-kilometer strip of beaches covered with golden sands. Towards Dzhubga there are also beaches with sand.

On the clean sands of Lermontovo vacationers are provided with sun loungers, umbrellas, water transport rental (jet skis, catamarans).





On the beach, everyone will find something to their liking:

  • parachute flying;
  • diving;
  • walks on "bananas", boats, motor ships, boats;
  • beach volleyball, etc.

Things to do in Lermontovo

In the center of the village is the water park "Chernomor". Slides of various heights, cozy cafes, a nightly disco and a movie screening - all this makes the vacation unforgettable.

Vacationers in Lermontovo of the Krasnodar Territory are offered not only beach activities. Daily excursions are sent from the village along the following routes:

  • Mill slot - a great place for a picnic in the mountains, offering magnificent views of the sea and mountains;
  • Tengin waterfalls on Mount Plyakho;
  • extreme mountain trips on ATVs and all-terrain vehicles;
  • horse rides.

Remarkable impressions remain after tastings by local producers of wine, cheese and honey.

From Lermontovo you can go on excursions to Sochi, Abrau-Durso, Novorossiysk or Gelendzhik.

Recreation facilities and campsites

Many recreation centers in Lermontovo annually receive guests. On the territory they can rent not only a room in a common building, but also rent cottages with a capacity of 1 to 8 beds.

"Currant", "Little Mermaid", "Camellia", "Granite", "Kolos" and others accept vacationers from May-June to September.

Arrivals in Lermontovo (Krasnodar Territory) often stop at campsites that are located along the highway running parallel to the coastline. Campsites are equipped with water supply, sewage, electricity, the territory is cleaned every day, barbecue facilities are provided. Having paid a place and putting up a tent, tourists receive an inexpensive and high-quality rest, being almost around the clock on the beach.

How to get to Lermontovo, Krasnodar Territory?

Convenient location made Lermontovo popular.

You can get here in different ways, choose for yourself the most economical and simple:

  • from Gelendzhik airport you need to drive about 60 km;
  • from the railway station in Tuapse - 55 km;
  • on the M-4 highway in Dzhubga, turn onto A-147, which passes through the village;
  • shuttle taxis regularly depart from Krasnodar bus stations.
