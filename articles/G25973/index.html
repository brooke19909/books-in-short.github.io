Alexandria hotels: description, reviews. Holidays in Egypt

This article will examine some of the popular hotels of Alexandria - a beautiful city that is the cultural center of Ancient Egypt and a very modern pearl of the Mediterranean Sea. Holidays here will be remembered for the high level of service in hotels and other public places, the clear sea, well-groomed beaches and incredibly beautiful, impressive sights.





A bit about Alexandria

It is located in the northern part of the country and has the most important property of the state - the old and new libraries, which are the heritage of Ancient Egypt. The foundation of Alexandria dates back to 334 BC, when Alexander the Great personally laid the first stone at this place. From here the locality got its name. Today, about 4,000,000 people live in Alexandria, the city, like several thousand years ago, remains an international port, and is also one of the most popular Egyptian resorts.

Weather in Alexandria

The climate is Mediterranean: winters are mild but rainy, and summers are hot and arid. The coldest, and at the same time cheap, the first two months of the year (January and February). At this time, the air temperature averages 17 degrees Celsius. In winter, storms and heavy rains are not uncommon. The hottest time is in July and August. Air temperature can rise above +30.

The weather in Alexandria is reflected in the temperature of the Mediterranean Sea. In winter, it drops to +15, and in summer it is very warm and pleasant for swimming - an average of +26.





Things to do in Alexandria

It is a shame to visit this beautiful ancient city and not to visit its significant places. This is the best option to get acquainted with the people, way of life and the country as a whole. Although Alexandria hotels are rich in entertainment, it is highly recommended to get out of them on a tour! So, the main attractions of the city:

  • The Alexandria Library - consists of the "old" and "new" buildings, rich in Arabic manuscripts from different times.
  • The National Museum is notable for being located in a chic palace. It has collections of unique artifacts, as well as expositions telling about periods in Egyptian history.
  • Abu Mena is an ancient city 45 km from Alexandria.
  • The Royal Palace of Montaz is an example of unique architecture.
  • Kite Bay - a fort built in the 15th century.
  • Picturesque Montazah Park.
  • Tombs of Mustafa Kamel.
  • Temple Serapeum - a structure erected by Ptolemy the Third (246-222 BC).

Alexandria today is a modern city, which has many entertainment venues like casinos, restaurants, nightclubs and shopping centers. Therefore, a walk along it will be boring. Now is the time to find out which Alexandria hotels are the best.





Hilton borg el arab

  • Description It is a complex consisting of one four-story building and 80 chalets. It has five stars. Built in 1997. In general, this is a respectable hotel having a great beach and comfortable rooms. It is characterized by a high level of comfort. Ideal for those who seek peace.
  • The rooms . In Hilton Borg El Arab, in each room there is something that a vacationer may need: a private bathroom, a minibar, appliances, modern furniture, internet, a balcony or terrace.
  • Food . Three types are offered (optional): BB, HB, FB. There are 4 restaurants and 3 bars on site.
  • Entertainment Many activities for adults and children: swimming pools, playgrounds and sports grounds, a mini-club for creativity, furniture for kids, a gym, a disco, beach volleyball, large chess, a sauna, a steam room, and a tennis court.
  • Prices . An average tour for two adults for 7 days will cost 37,000 rubles.
  • Reviews Clean comfortable rooms, proximity to the beach and neat territory - this is what tourists emphasize, giving the hotel a rating of "5".




Windsor Palace Hotel

  • Description We built the hotel at the very beginning of the century before last. It is a small palace that stands directly on the seashore. It features a chic interior: these are reliefs, murals and painted ceilings. The rooms have balconies overlooking the East Harbor.
  • The rooms . A total of 76 rooms of various categories. They differ from rooms in other hotels in that they have high ceilings typical of past centuries, the decoration of which was hand-made. The rooms are equipped with everything you need.
  • Food . At Windsor Palace Hotel, breakfast is complimentary. What is noteworthy: it can be served on the panoramic terrace. The rest of the time, you can eat in the cafe, restaurant, or head to the lounge-bar with snacks and delicious drinks.
  • Entertainment On the territory there is a tour desk that will send the vacationer on any desired route to get acquainted with the city.
  • Prices . The average cost is 5,000 rubles per day.
  • Reviews The hotel is original and not everyone may like it. On the one hand, he seemed to come from the past to take tourists with him, to show how it was a hundred years ago. On the other hand, many tourists write that the hotel is in dire need of repair and does not deserve the amount of accommodation that the administration requires.




Sheraton Montazah Hotel

  • Description The hotel occupies a 15-story building and is a modern hotel with bright, comfortable rooms and stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.
  • The rooms . Panoramic glazing and sliding balcony doors cannot but please the vacationers. The rooms are equipped with bright colorful furniture and a spacious bathroom. Air conditioning works, as well as other necessary equipment. In total, the hotel has 289 rooms of various categories, including for people with disabilities.
  • Food . There is a restaurant and bar on site, in addition, it is possible to order food in the room. Here, guests are offered to taste international dishes and delicious drinks. There is also a cafe with cakes and pastries.
  • Entertainment It is proposed to rent a car to travel around Alexandria. The hotel has developed infrastructure, for example, there are shops and a beauty salon. For guests there is a club, sauna and massage room. The windows of the gym and the outdoor pool area offer sea views. Sheraton Montazah Hotel has its own beach.
  • Prices . An average of 4,000 to 12,000 rubles.
  • Reviews Small rooms and the lack of balconies in almost all "standards" are cons that vacationers write about in reviews. On the other hand, these shortcomings are compensated by the relatively low cost of living and a very rich menu. Tables literally burst with a variety of sweets, snacks and wholesome dishes.




Mercure Alexandria Romance Hotel

  • Description Mercure Romance Alexandria has 4 stars. Located 50 meters from the beach. This is a cozy hotel, decorated in a modern style and equipped with comfortable furniture, in which almost everywhere there are panoramic windows with views of the Mediterranean Sea.
  • The rooms . Only 36 rooms of a high level of comfort. In addition to the beautiful view from the windows, there is a complete set of necessary furniture and appliances, in the bathroom there are hygiene kits and towels.
  • Food . In the mornings, all vacationers are offered to enjoy a rich and nutritious breakfast, which is served in the restaurant. It works all day, so you can also have a meal here at lunch and in the evening. Guests can enjoy Egyptian and international dishes. Another restaurant offers to enjoy French delights. For drinks and a variety of snacks - in the bar.
  • Entertainment There is a games room for children, and adults are invited to play tennis.
  • Prices . An average of 6000-12,000 rubles per day.
  • Reviews Of the minuses, tourists write about small-sized rooms. Otherwise, the hotel is pleasant to stay, with delicious food and amazing sea views.




Helnan Palestine Hotel

  • Description Helnan Palestine Hotel is surrounded by a bright green garden, strolling through which you can admire the bay. The hotel has 5 stars, it is located next to one of the historical places of Egypt - the residence of the royal family of Montaz. Alexandria is 10 km away and the airport is 15 km away.
  • The rooms . Like other hotels, Helnan Palestine Hotel offers its guests rooms fully equipped with all necessary appliances and furniture. The layout is spacious, there are sets for making tea and coffee, satellite TV. Some rooms have a palace.
  • Food . The hotel has 5 restaurants and 4 bars. Guests are offered a choice of a large number of different dishes, drinks and snacks both in closed and open areas.
  • Entertainment Nearby is a sandy beach, not far from which there is a sun terrace. In the evening, a night club opens its doors, you can also play tennis, swim in the pool, taste water sports (non-motorized and motorized) to taste, get diving lessons or try yourself in billiards. There is a playroom for children. For lovely ladies there is a beauty salon, massage room, sauna and jacuzzi.
  • Prices . From 6,000 to 55,000 rubles.
  • Reviews Tourists confirm that the hotel actually deserves its 5 stars. Landscaped and well-groomed park area, in it playgrounds for children and pleasant air. Many vacationers say that the vacation was a success thanks to this hotel and its staff.

Alexandria hotels are mostly upscale establishments in which, even if there are no conditions for entertainment, you will not be bored. The city is beautiful at any time of the year, therefore, in order to save money, you can come here in the winter, and without suffering from the sweltering heat, visit the sights. Alexandria is an Egyptian pearl worth visiting.
