Hotel Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * (Sanya, China): tourist reviews and photos

Resort Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * is located directly on the shores of the South China Sea, in a picturesque bay. Just a hundred meters from the complex is a private beach, designed for guests to visit. You can drive to the city center in less than 10 minutes. About the same amount of time will be required to get to the airport. The location of the hotel is very convenient, in addition, within a radius of 10 kilometers there are lots of interesting sights. This is one of the most important features for tourists, but in addition, the complex boasts many other advantages.





Services

On the territory of Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * there is everything that should be in a hotel of this level. In the lobby there is a 24-hour reception desk, an ATM, a left-luggage office, a currency exchange office, a tour desk and private parking. Guests, by the way, can use the concierge service. It is also worth noting that accelerated and individual check-in / check-out is available here.

In addition to the above, in Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * (Sanya Bay) there is a laundry room, a small business center, a conference room and a point where you can make a photocopy and send / receive a fax.

Also on site there is a minimarket and several shops, car rental and a souvenir stall. In addition, the hotel provides shuttle service, Wi-Fi (and wired Internet in rooms), food, drinks and newspapers, and VIP services.





Leisure

Most tourists who come to Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * prefer to spend time on a private beach. But, being in this hotel, you can spend time in another way. For example, stay on site and sunbathe on the terrace near one of the two deep clean pools. Or relax in the garden, in the shade of the trees.

Sports enthusiasts can head to the volleyball court or gym. People who love to relax in body and soul will enjoy the massage parlor located here at the hotel. And entertainment lovers will like evening show programs and daytime animation. Events are held in Chinese and English.

By the way, for children, the hotel has a separate shallow (1.2 m) pool and an entertainment program.

A restaurant

He is at Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * one. It specializes mainly in Asian cuisine, but there is also an international menu.

Breakfast in the restaurant is varied - everyone will find something that will appeal to him. Fried eggs, soups, cereals, dumplings, fresh pastries and cookies, various jams, fresh vegetables and fruits are offered. And from drinks - sparkling water, juices, teas and coffee. People who were in Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 *, in the reviews note that the menu was supplemented daily with some kind of new dish.

You can also have dinner at the hotel, but guests recommend visiting the local restaurants for a change, with a huge assortment and low prices.

Service

Many people who stayed at Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * are happy to talk about the benefits of this hotel. And one of those is service.

The hotel, like its entire territory, is very clean. So much so that you can walk barefoot along the corridors. The rooms are cleaned daily; the rooms are cleaned carefully and not superficially. Hygiene kits (shampoos, gels, soaps, lotions, etc.) are replenished regularly.

The only negative that guests note is the lack of personnel who speak Russian. But this is not surprising because the Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * hotel is located in an Asian country and is intended mainly for residents of Northern and Central China. But if you need to communicate with the administrator, there is a solution. We need to go to the reception desk and call the interpreter from there (he has his own at the hotel, located remotely), after which he will explain everything to the administrator of the complex. And although the staff does not know the Russian language, but the people there are polite and friendly, ready to do everything possible for the comfort of their client.





Reviews

In general, about the hotel Sanya Jinglilai Resort 3 * (China, Sanya Bay) leave mostly positive reviews. But in them, guests warn potential tourists that they should not go there if they want a quiet and peaceful vacation. The complex is located on a busy street, close to the city center, so it is always crowded there. For this reason, in fact, the rooms are finished with soundproofing material. It is also worth knowing that a road with very active car traffic leads to the hotel's beach. It is difficult to cross, as the cars do not stop. But many get used to it.

A huge plus tourists consider a free shuttle service. And the downside is the lack of beach towels. However, you can take with you on the beach those that are issued in the room. All the same, the maids always bring them in the amount of several pieces, and change them daily. So there is no need to bring a beach towel with you.

In general, if you want to go to Sanya and spend on vacation not a very large amount, then you can stay at this hotel. It is an excellent example of a good combination of low price and decent quality, which once again allows us to verify the reviews of tourists who have already been here.
