Hotel Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * (Morocco, Agadir): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Recently, the Kingdom of Morocco has been very popular among tourists who dream of vacationing in some exotic, but inexpensive place. The state is rather big, but especially many visitors are observed in Agadir. There are plenty of hotels here - there is an option for every taste, color and wallet. Well, I would like to stop attention at the Tulip Inn Oasis 4 *.





general information

The first thing a tourist should know is that the hotel is in close proximity to the sea. Moreover, it has its own private area on the beach, where it is always clean and there are places. Even in high season.

Yes, and the airport from Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * does not go for long - it is 27 kilometers from the hotel.

In general, the hotel has an extremely favorable location. There is a bus stop next to it, so tourists can not spend money on taxis and move around "on a budget." Walking distance to the Berber Museum, Agadir Harbor and the huge market district called Suq al-Had.

Service

In Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * there is everything that a tourist needs for a comfortable stay on vacation. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, and public parking is available near the building.

In the lobby there is an ATM, a currency exchange office with a good rate, as well as a left-luggage office and a 24-hour reception desk. At the request of the guest can be carried out accelerated registration of guests.

Dry cleaning with laundry, as well as an office with fax and photocopying services are also available at the hotel. In addition, on the territory you can find several shops (including a souvenir), as well as a beauty salon with a hairdresser. The staff working at Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * speaks French, Arabic and English, which is also worth knowing. Nobody speaks Russian, so the phrasebook in the bag will not be superfluous.





Leisure activities

Of course, the main attraction for people who came here to relax is to visit the beach with the aim of sunbathing and swimming enough in the sea. In Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * there are not many ways with which you can diversify your pastime. Which, in principle, is not very bad, since it positively affects the price.

But still there is something to keep yourself busy. You can go for a swim in the outdoor swimming pool, play table tennis or darts, or visit the Turkish bath or sauna. There is also a full-fledged fitness center, which will definitely appeal to lovers of a sports lifestyle, and a SPA center, where welness therapy is actively practiced and massage sessions are conducted.

By the way, if adults decide to take their children with them on vacation, then they can not worry about their pastime: a separate playground is organized for them here. But again, almost all visitors spend time on the beach. Only towels should be taken with you. Bathrooms are in rooms, but beach ones are not issued.





Nutrition

Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * (Morocco) has its own restaurant and bar. Guests who are here are advised to pay either HB (half board), or only breakfast. The food here is not bad, but Agadir also has a lot of restaurants and cafes, so every day you can explore some new establishment and try different exotics.

Breakfast at the hotel is continental: guests are offered fresh rolls with jam, cottage cheese, omelettes, fried eggs, carbonated juices, yogurts, vegetables, olives and boiled eggs. Still there are oranges, melons, watermelons, hot milk, tea, coffee, pancakes. In general, you can‚Äôt stay hungry.

For lunch and dinner, a variety of salads, fried pork ribs, chicken, fish, spaghetti, beans, toasted sausages, cheese slices are served. Even soups are cooked here. Vegetables are also served, both fresh and stewed. Overall not bad, but some lacked variety. Therefore, it is advised to pay either breakfast or half board.

Accommodation options

In total, the Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * (Morocco) has a little over 150 rooms. Budget category includes Oasis Apartments. Area - 16 square meters. m, everything is beautifully and attractively decorated inside, with notes of a traditional Moroccan style in the interior.

The apartment has a large double bed, a balcony with a picturesque view of the sea, a plasma TV with satellite channels, a safe, air conditioning, and a plumbing unit. It has a shower, bath, toilet and even a bidet.

On average, a week of living for two people will cost about 20 thousand rubles. (with breakfast). In Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * (Agadir) there is no fixed cost - the price may vary depending on the season and other nuances. With half board, by the way, accommodation will cost more by 10-15 thousand rubles. A single tourist will have to pay about 15 thousand rubles for a stay. (with breakfast).





Suites

Apartments of this class in Hotel Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * are more spacious than the aforementioned Oases. Their living space is 25 square meters. m. Inside, in addition to a large 2 double bed, there is also a seating area and a folding sofa.

Some of the suites are located on the ground floors, so they do not have a balcony, but a terrace. In principle, all the previously listed amenities in these rooms are there, only guests are provided with free toiletries and any view from the window to choose from (sea, garden, pool, mountains, city or attractions). And the floor in the suites, by the way, is tiled with marble.

A weekly stay with breakfast for two people will cost about 40 thousand rubles. With half board will be more expensive by 15 thousand rubles. For reference, it is worth noting that for a stay with meals on the system "breakfast + lunch + dinner" you will need to pay approximately thousand rubles.





Helpful information

At the Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * (Morocco, Agadir), check-in starts at 14:00. And the departure lasts until 12:30. It is worth taking this information into account - it is useful when selecting a flight. Of course, if the apartments paid by the guests are free, and they arrived earlier, you won‚Äôt have to wait. But it's better to play it safe.

The hotel is allowed to stay with children. For the stay of the baby, whose age does not reach 6 years, you do not have to pay extra. He will only have to sleep on the beds available in the apartment. But for an additional 20 euros per day, another one will be installed in the room.

If guests know that they will come with children, then they are obliged to inform the administration in advance about this nuance. Guests will be required to send confirmation of extra beds.

And when you check in at Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * (Agadir) you will need to present the credit card used during booking. The guest needs to remember - you can only use your own card. Because the name indicated on it must coincide with the full name in the identity card that the tourist will also be required to present upon check-in.





Impressions of tourists

And now you can pay attention to the reviews that leave about the rest at Tulip Inn Oasis 4 *. They are mostly positive. It should be noted right away: you should not expect much from the hotel. This is quite a comfortable and well-groomed hotel, not a resort class. Here you can book a room at a low price and live in good conditions. After all, people come to Agadir in order to study another culture and life.

The clear advantage of the hotel is its silence. Many guests are employees of aviation companies. Crews, to be more precise, who are populated here during the trip. However, due to the fact that the hotel is located next to the road, it is still noisy. But you can close the balcony door, and the apartment will immediately become quieter.

Special attention is paid to the level of service. He is worthy here. All you need to bring, just ask. You can not even go down to the reception. Not without reason, because for communication, an internal telephone was installed in each number.

The sea, as already mentioned, is close. You can go down from the slope along an asphalt path. It does not require much effort. Since the descent is comfortable and gentle.

And guests are pleased to note the fact that the promenade here is very busy. There are various cafes, including famous ones, such as McDonald's and KFS.

The beaches are clean, despite the fact that from June to August they are filled with locals and tourists. Fewer people in spring and autumn - it is worth taking this into account if you want to relax in a more secluded setting.





Other nuances

Also, guests leaving reviews about Tulip Inn Oasis 4 * (Morocco) about their stay, note the quality of cleaning. She is here daily and thorough - the maids are working to their conscience. Towels are replaced with the same regularity. And toilet supplies also replenish.

Experienced tourists recommend throwing a few tubes with cream after / after sunburn and an anti-burn ointment into your bag before going on vacation. The sun in these places is simply incredible, especially in high season.

Interestingly, in the lobby of the hotel for tourists are posted memos. It says: if guests paid "all inclusive", then they do not pay for drinks until 23:00. Then they pour it for a surcharge. By the way, staff does not take tips if you leave them on a table or on a bed for maids. This is considered theft in Agadir. Therefore, if you want to thank the staff for a good job, it is worth giving money directly to your hands. Or a note in French can be left.

The hotel is recommended for people who want to get acquainted with Morocco, enjoy a beach holiday and local culture. Couples, friendly companies or solo travelers will love it here. Traveling with children is not recommended, as this resort and hotel is not for them. They will be bored here. But all others can safely go. The hotel is not bad and also with reasonable prices.
