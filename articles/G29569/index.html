What is interesting about the Zopo phone?

The products of manufacturers from China have long been established in the market. More and more people began to prefer phones from the Middle Kingdom. Attention was not deprived of the devices manufactured by Zopo. What will the products of this company be of interest to the buyer?

Design





Each Zopo mobile phone will delight the owner with an elegant appearance. The manufacturer has definitely worked on the type of its products. Naturally, as with most Chinese firms, there is a borrowing of design ideas from more advanced companies. The ZP950 model is very similar in appearance to Samsung devices. However, the efforts of the manufacturer did not go unnoticed. Almost every Zopo product has its own solutions.

Although outwardly the company's devices look stylish, there are also weaknesses. The main drawback was the uniformity of materials. Almost every device released by the company is made of plastic. That is why the strength and reliability of the devices are in doubt.

Hardware





The MTK processor has become a familiar solution for low-cost devices, especially companies from the Middle Kingdom liked this option. Choosing a cheap "filling" significantly reduces the cost of the device, although it significantly affects performance.

Such a choice of hardware did not pass by Zopo. Having sacrificed part of productivity and giving preference to cheap "stuffing", the company significantly reduced the cost of devices. However, the power of the company's devices should not be underestimated. Any Zopo phone will cope with any daily tasks. More advanced models, such as the ZP920 or ZP980, are comparable to the middle class of well-known brands.

Positive reviews

What did the owners of the Zopo phone like? Reviews highlight the big screen. Image quality is also given credit. The display shows its best side in the sun, and the image without pixels further enhances the impression.

The camera also appealed to users. Perhaps the pictures do not reach the market leaders in quality, but the resulting picture is not bad. In a Chinese device, such a camera looks more than unexpected.

Good performance has become another trump card Zopo. Core performance, RAM and a large amount of memory - what else does the user need? The owners even compared Zopo with representatives of the Lenovo S-class of the company.

Be sure to note the cost of devices. Although the company's products are higher in price than some smartphones in the Middle Kingdom, but it is definitely worth the money.

Negative reviews

The main drawback was the design, or rather the case material. Zopo devices are incredibly fragile and get scratched and damaged even from a slight drop.

A secondary, but no less important problem is a weak battery. Many owners complained about the short duration of the work and a strong dependence on the outlet.
