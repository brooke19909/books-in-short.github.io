Best tube amp: specs and reviews

Many music lovers prefer to listen to their favorite tunes using tube amplifiers. What is the specificity of these devices? Based on what criteria can I choose the optimal model for the corresponding device?





What is interesting tube sound amplifier?

An amplifier is one of the key components of the acoustic infrastructure, which is responsible for increasing the power of those signals that come from sound sources, switching the corresponding devices, adjusting the volume level, and also transmitting a signal whose power is amplified to audio equipment designed to play tunes.

In tube amplifiers, radio tubes are used as a key element of circuitry. They serve as reinforcing elements. Typically, tube amplifiers provide less distortion. As many music lovers note, the corresponding devices are characterized by a warmer, softer reproduction of melodies - especially when playing medium as well as high frequencies.

Another major advantage of a tube amplifier is in providing in many cases a richer sound in comparison, for example, with transistor devices. This is possible due to the unique properties of the lamps themselves, which, for example, are adapted to function without auxiliary correction, which is necessary to maintain the operation of, in turn, semiconductor devices.

One-stroke and two-stroke devices

Tube devices are most often classified into 2 main categories - class A and class AB. The former are also referred to as single-cycle. In them, amplifying elements stimulate an increase in the power of both half-waves in the signal - both positive and negative. The second devices are also called push-pull. In them, each subsequent cascade of increasing power involves the use of different elements - one may be responsible for the positive half-wave, while the other can be responsible for the negative. Class AB amplifiers are usually more economical and more efficient, often more powerful. But on this score, discussions sometimes arise among music lovers.

The considered devices in many cases are much more expensive than transistor analogs, despite the fact that their design is quite simple. Many music lovers assemble the appropriate devices on their own - however, you need to try to find the best schemes for tube amplifiers - on 6P3S, for example, or other popular lamps. For music connoisseurs playing using the devices in question, their price is often of secondary importance - if it is decided not to assemble an amplifier, but to buy it. However, the characteristics, of course, play an undeniably significant role in choosing a device. Consider what they can be, as well as examples of popular models of the corresponding type of devices.

According to experts - reviews that reflect their opinions can be found on many thematic portals - one of the main advantages of the device is its equipped with interfaces that allow you to smoothly load the lamp, which helps to increase its service life. Determining the best tube sound amplifier based on the subjective perception of its operation is quite difficult. However, one can come closer to solving a similar problem by comparing one or another device model by its main characteristics, as well as by analyzing the corresponding parameters.

Choosing the best amplifier: model comparison options

What parameters can be considered as key? According to modern experts, the most important characteristics in this case can be:

- power;

- frequency range ;

- level of harmonic distortion;

- signal to noise ratio;

- support for communication standards;

- level of energy consumption.

In turn, these parameters can be compared with the price of the device.





Choose an amplifier: power

As for the first indicator - power, it can be represented in the widest range of values. Optimal for solving most of the problems characterizing the use of a tube amplifier is an indicator of the order of 35 watts. But many music lovers only welcome an increase in this value - for example, up to 50 watts.

At the same time, many modern high-tech devices of the corresponding type work perfectly at a power of about 12 watts. Of course, in many cases they require connection to a high-performance acoustic infrastructure. But the use of effective audio equipment is one of the mandatory attributes of the use, in fact, of the devices in question. Why a tube amplifier is better than more modern modifications of devices is a question that is not particularly relevant for many music lovers, since they have repeatedly been convinced in practice of the objective superiority of the corresponding devices in key parameters. And therefore, they try to conduct testing and practical use of tube amplifiers on pre-prepared equipment that meets the highest requirements.

Frequency

Regarding the frequency response of the amplifier, it is highly desirable that it be in the range from 20 to 20 thousand Hz. Although, it should be noted, quite rarely, the modern manufacturers of devices in question supply amplifiers to the markets that do not meet this criterion. It is difficult to find equipment in the Hi-End segment that would not reach the indicated frequency parameters. One way or another, when buying a tube amplifier, for example, from a little-known brand, it makes sense to check in which range it supports the frequency.

Harmonic distortion

As for harmonic distortion, it is desirable that they do not exceed 0.6%. Actually, the lower this indicator - the better the sound. The best tube amplifier in a given segment is often determined primarily on the basis of the harmonic distortion index. It should be noted right away that the corresponding indicator is not the most significant in terms of ensuring good sound quality. However, this parameter characterizes the response of the acoustic infrastructure to the input signal. It is rather difficult in practice to provide stimulation of the acoustics reaction during measurement as it is when playing real signals. But modern brands of tube amplifier manufacturers are trying to provide the lowest harmonic distortion. Prestigious models of devices are able to provide it at a level not exceeding 0.1%. Of course, their cost may be incomparably higher than competing models that have a higher harmonic distortion, but for the music lover, the price issue in this case may be secondary.





Signal to noise ratio

The next parameter is the signal-to-noise ratio; in modern tube amplifiers, it most often corresponds to an indicator of 90 dB or more. In general, this value can be considered very common when comparing the characteristics of various devices, even if presented in different segments. Therefore, if the task is to choose a good one-cycle tube amplifier or, for example, a two-cycle amplifier, then the considered parameter will not always objectively reflect the competitiveness of a particular device. One way or another, the higher the corresponding indicator - the better. It is desirable that it be at least 70. Some top models of amplifiers provide a signal to noise ratio of more than 100 dB. But their price, as in the case of harmonic distortions, can be impressive.

Other parameters

The remaining parameters - support for one or another communication standard, power consumption, are significant, but secondary. It makes sense to pay attention to them, all other things being equal, in terms of the indicators that we examined above. One way or another, for a modern amplifier, it is typical to consider the presence of support for a sufficient number of stereo pairs - about 4, audio output for recording sound. Regarding energy consumption - its optimal indicator is about 280 watts.





Of course, when considering which tube amplifier is better, many subjective factors play a role. Most often, music lovers evaluate the corresponding devices based on them: design, build quality, sound level, ergonomics.

All the above parameters can be compared with the price of the device, which can be represented in a very wide range of values. But a person for whom the question is not why the tube amplifier is better than the transistor one, since he knows the answer to it, the price, as we noted above, can not always be considered as the most significant criterion when choosing a device for organizing listening to your favorite tunes.
