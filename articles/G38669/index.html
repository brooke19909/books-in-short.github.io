The best resorts in Bulgaria for recreation: photos, descriptions, reviews

Not all resorts in Bulgaria are alike. Each vacation spot in this country has a number of unique properties and is more suitable for a certain type of people. In order to avoid problems with the choice of terrain during the trip, this article contains all the necessary information about the resorts. The material will be useful to every tourist in preparation for the trip.

Calm for the whole family

Among the resorts of Bulgaria for families with children, Albena is the best choice. On the vast territory of the town, which is located on the northern coast of the Black Sea, there are many diverse attractions. Life here is more calm, and young people who crave nightly adventures in this place will most likely not like it. Clubs and bars are present, but there are too few people in them for fun. In order for family vacations to take place in the best way, the owners of the resort had highly developed infrastructure in the area. Catering establishments and beaches with a length of seven kilometers are only the peak of everything. Tourists can stay in 80 hotels of different classes, on the territory of the town there are mineral springs, mud with a healing effect and health facilities. Far from all resorts in Bulgaria you can find modern spa centers, and two were built in Albena. The beaches are clean and there is a Blue Flag, which indicates environmental safety. In between breaks, you can go on an excursion. Near the city there is a rock monastery Aladzha - a religious building in the country. Also, attention should be paid to the Kiliakra fortress.

A universal choice for every person

In Bulgaria, resorts have their own specifics, and it can be used to determine what type of vacation corresponds to this place. An exception is the territory called Sunny Beach. Every tourist who has visited the country at least once knows about this resort. Versatility brought him fame, because family people with children and young people looking for adventure can relax here. The first type of visitors is better to settle in the north, where a more relaxed atmosphere. Young people from Europe usually settle in groups closer to the center. The beaches are incredibly clean, they are constantly looked after. The entrance to the sea is smooth, which will be pleasant for the kids. They will be able to swim offshore with safety under the supervision of their parents. Amusement on the sand strip is an incredible amount. For children, there are water parks and similar attractions, and adults are offered extreme ways to add variety to the rest. These include skydiving, hang gliding, diving and much more.

The infrastructure inside the resort, despite its not so long existence, is developed to a prohibitive level. Sports fields for all games are present in hotels for every taste, restaurants and cafes at every step. At night, young people can feel that Sunny Beach is not in vain one of the best resorts in Bulgaria. Clubs with discos invite everyone to unforgettable dancing, dating and unbridled fun. During the break, people can go on excursions to Old Nessebar or ride a little further to Sozopol.

Rest with treatment at affordable prices

For several reasons, Balchik is among the best resorts in Bulgaria.

  • Firstly, it is adjacent to Albena and is famous for the lowest prices in almost the entire country. Here you can really relax economically and get great pleasure from it. Hotels predominantly have two or three stars, there are also family pensions. From them to the sea to walk no more than six hundred meters.
  • Secondly, here you can not only enjoy sunbathing and enjoy your free time, but also spend it to good use. Balneological procedures and mineral waters will help many people to recover, especially for problems with joints. Such services at such low prices can offer far from all resorts in Bulgaria.
  • Thirdly, in the resort town there are several wonderful attractions. During their viewing, you can brighten up the time in the interval between trips to the sea. These include the famous botanical garden with a huge number of plants. Separately, it is worth highlighting the magnificent summer residence, which was built specifically for the Queen of Romania, Mary of Edinburgh. A large palace complex surrounds the park, and a walk through this area will be admired. The beach is divided into three parts, and the best of them is New. For a change, you can go to the lake, which is known for its balneological center. Here, each client can experience mud baths and other procedures.

Historic Vacation Places

Before traveling, tourists should definitely know which Bulgarian resorts are more suitable for cultural recreation. This is especially true for people who want to appreciate the sights of the country in addition to rest on the sea.

The first such place is Nessebar, which is one of the oldest cities in Europe. The ancient part of the settlement is an incredibly beautiful place to visit. The huge territory is attractive because you can walk here for a long time. Old Nessebar is an open-air museum with a huge number of attractions of different eras.

You can have a good time in the city not only on excursions, but also near the sea. The beaches are clean enough, the entrance to the sea is gentle, although there are no less people here than in the nearest resort Sunny Beach. To remove a place of residence is also not a problem. Many kinds of apartments are offered by locals for tourists.

The second resort town with a long history is Sozopol. It is recommended that creative people go here to be inspired for future work. Every year, the Apolonia Arts Festival is held at this venue. There are a lot of interesting sights in the city, and you can spend time with variety.

The three-kilometer beach holiday zone is divided into three areas and there is enough space for all vacationers. Not far from the city there is a reserve Ropotami, mountain villages and ancient churches. Description of the resorts in Bulgaria of these types should attract people who are interested in history, as well as a pleasant time at sea.

Rest in the winter

Bulgaria's ski resorts are also famous all over the world, although the country is better known as a sunny place to relax in the hot season. The first of the famous places for winter entertainment in the state is Borovets. This place is located at an altitude of 1350 meters above sea level.

The resort is located in the north of the mountain called Rila. There are three ski areas with different difficulty levels. The infrastructure here is maximally developed, because it is the largest of all ski resorts in Bulgaria. There are a lot of bars, restaurants, cafes and shops here. As an unusual entertainment, you can try to take part in the ski carnival. To explore the surroundings, it is recommended to book a sleigh ride.

Not far from the capital of Bulgaria Sofia is another ski resort Pamporovo. Here, tourists mix with the inhabitants of the country, who often spend weekends in this area. There is a recreation area at an altitude of 1650 meters in the Rhodope Mountains. This ensures the presence of seventeen kilometers of slopes for skiers with different levels of difficulty, another thirty-four are allocated for biathlon and cross-country skiing. In Pamporovo is the largest school in which these sports are taught.

The resort also has its own distinctive feature in the form of hot springs. Their presence attracts even more tourists in the winter in this area. For greater recovery, you can visit the neighboring balneological centers and spas. Such a vacation will be remembered for a long time and will give the desired relaxation.

Another winter resort

The ski resort of Bansko in Bulgaria can compete with the two above-mentioned holiday destinations, despite their young age. It was founded not so long ago, but tourists who come here immediately fall in love with this area due to the presence of all the necessary entertainment.

From November to mid-spring, a dense two-meter layer of snow is kept in the region, which provides convenient skiing. In an active holiday, everyone can have a great time here, but not only this resort is famous for. People who are far from winter sports come here to escape from workdays. The reason for this lies in the developed infrastructure of all directions.

For example, here everyone can easily enjoy spa treatments, recover from mineral water and take balneological baths. Baths, massages, pools and other relaxing services are available. Fresh mountain air complements this picture with the ability to swim in the thermal springs.

You can diversify your stay with excursions around the town, where there are different attractions. The picturesque houses and temples of the eighteenth century impress with their views. You should definitely visit the house-museum, where the famous local artist Veljan Ognev once lived. For kids infrastructure is not provided except for pools and children's rooms. Children from six years old can be taken to a ski school for the rest, and from twelve years old they are allowed to put on a snowboard. There are no problems with places of residence. Apartments, guest houses and hotels for every taste are equipped with all amenities.

Treatment of various diseases

In the photo of the resorts of Bulgaria you can find not only a view of the beach and a large number of people, but also mountain landscapes with beautiful wildlife. Such pictures are most often observed in those places that are intended for wellness procedures. In addition to a fun summer holiday and an active winter pastime, Bulgaria is famous for places where people can be cured of various diseases.

One of these resorts is Sandusky, which is located in the picturesque Pirin Mountains. Mountain air, mild climate and mineral waters greatly contribute to the improvement of the respiratory apparatus, especially the lungs. Procedures are prescribed according to a special system. Traditional methods of getting rid of diseases are honored here, but they don‚Äôt forget about the use of new technologies. Around the sanatorium is the national park of the same name, but because the rest will take place in a natural environment. Thanks to the climate, you can come to Sandusky at any time of the year.

The second good choice for recovery will be a visit to the village of Hisarya. The village is located 160 kilometers from the capital and is distinguished by excellent environmental conditions. Archaeological excavations were carried out in the foothills of Syshchinsk, which proved the use of mineral springs from this place back in the fourth century AD. People with digestive problems, obesity, gout come here. To recover from injuries of the musculoskeletal system, it is also useful for people to spend time at the resort. Mineral waters here have an exceptional taste.

Other wellness centers

Bulgarian resorts for recreation and health improvement are located in different places of the country. All of them have their own field of activity.

For example, a sanatorium called Velingrad earned its fame for its unique mineral waters from hot springs. It is recommended to come here to people who suffer from problems with breathing and digestion, metabolic disorders with outgoing diseases. The hydrocarbonate-sulfate composition of water helps in the case of diseases on the gynecological part and nervous disorders. Specialists at the resort provide professional assistance with the restoration of the musculoskeletal system after severe injuries. The center is located in the Rhodope Mountains, where fresh air helps to restore. On its territory there are also balneological health resorts and a rehabilitation building.

Health resorts also include Pomorie, which visitors love for their special climate. In summer it is not too hot, and in winter there is a mild coolness. The village is located twenty kilometers from Burgas and it is known for its sulfide mud. They are mined from the bottom of Lake Nessebar and the healing effect of them is striking in variety.

People come here with nervous stress, gynecological diseases, cerebral palsy, skin problems and more. At the resort you can have a great time, see some sights. Plus, prices here, according to reviews of resorts in Bulgaria, are much lower than in more popular places.

Ways to save when traveling

If you intend to visit the resorts of Bulgaria with children, a couple or on their own, then you should read a few tips that will help save money.

  • First of all, you should prepare for the trip, buy tanning products, a swimsuit, medicines, etc. in your hometown. After arriving for essentials, you will have to pay several times more money.
  • For shopping, it is better to go to Varna, Burgas or even Sofia. Greater competition gives rise to lower prices, plus a richer selection of souvenirs.
  • Even at the planning stage, taking care of the hotel is worth it. In this way, you can save up to 40% of money only on accommodation.
  • If the traveler is going to eat in Bulgarian cafes and restaurants, then you should practically approach the order. Servings are served large, and therefore an adult will need only one, maximum two dishes. It is even better when asking the receptionist for discounts at hotel restaurants. The practice of reducing prices for their customers is very common here.

It often happens that the local currency is not enough for your needs and you need to make an exchange. The hotels can offer to do it right on the spot, but you should not do it. Owners set the course too low and it is better to spend time hiking to the nearest Crown exchange center.
