Multi-touch - what is it? Multi-touch system. Multi touch



Modern electronic devices are equipped with all new features that are designed to facilitate their use. Each characteristic has its own name. For example, you can see the word "multitouch" in the technical description of a tablet or phone. What it is?

The meaning of the term

First of all, it is worth noting that this is the name of the touch screen. Such a monitor responds to light finger presses and does not have a physical keyboard. Translated from English, the term means "multiple touches."

The first experiments on creating touch screens were conducted back in the 1960s. They later found their application at CERN. They equipped the particle accelerator. In the 1970s, a touchscreen screen was introduced at the University of Kentucky. Modern technology, which once seemed fantastic, appeared in New York. It was developed by Jeff Khan. What commands does the touch screen execute?



First of all, we define the features of multitouch: what is it and what is the difference between the usual touch screen and it. The first is able to recognize the coordinates of the point of one touch, and the second - the set. This circumstance allows you to make working with an electronic device more comfortable and productive. Developed many special gestures to control the device. If you have a smartphone, you must remember that with just two fingers that converge or diverge on the monitor of an electronic gadget, you can set the command to reduce or enlarge the image. They also allow you to open folders, files, collapse, move them, rotate, scroll pages. You can enter text using the virtual keyboard. Electronic devices with multi-touch



As a rule, many modern mobile electronic devices support this technology: smartphones, phones, e-books, iPads and even laptops. They are produced by popular companies, for example, companies such as Apple, Dell, Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard and others. There are several options for making touch screens. The most popular resistive technology. It was designed by Sam Hurst. Its biggest plus is the low cost of production. This continued until 2008. Other options for creating such displays: optical, strain gauge, inductive touchscreens. Now create projection-capacitive touch screens. They are used by Apple in their iPhones and iPads.

Display device



What does a multitouch screen consist of? A capacitive monitor is a glass panel that covers a resistive layer. At the corners of the display are four electrodes. Through them passes an alternating voltage. When a finger touches the touch screen, a leakage current occurs. Large multi-touch screens support multi-touch tracking of multiple users at once. Displays created by various technologies may possess it. Infrared frames are being developed and launched into production - screens with different diagonals, using infrared illumination and a camera. Special sensory films, as well as glass, are in demand among electronics users. They cover the displays, their sizes can range from seventeen to fifty inches or more.

Future technology

Developers have yet to create giant-sized displays that are controlled by touch. This will make them available to multiple users at the same time. Perhaps a touch multi-touch with a pen will be connected. This will solve problems that require a more subtle approach. After all, human fingers sometimes act quite rudely. And with the help of a special subject it will be possible to draw on the display. In this case, both hands of a person will be involved. So, with the left you can influence the image scale, resize windows, move them, and write with the right pen. Existing tablets equipped with touch screens do not meet the requirements listed above and are still not adapted to make it convenient to work with the pen and the touch of your fingers at the same time. First of all, their monitors are not large enough, it is inconvenient to work with both hands on them. The combination of the two options for managing files (touch and pen) is not yet available.
