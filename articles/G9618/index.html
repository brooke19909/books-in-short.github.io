A brief overview of the shopping center "Fair" in Astrakhan: shops, entertainment and photos

The Yarmarka shopping center in Astrakhan is the place where most citizens make their purchases. The center is quite popular not only among Astrakhan residents - it is also sought by many city guests who like to buy new fashion items, interesting goods at affordable prices, and also have fun. You can also come here with the whole family structure - there are classes for representatives of any age category.



general information

This shopping center was built in 2011 and opened in November this year. Immediately, the place for trade, shopping and entertainment gained great popularity as a center in which you can find everything that is necessary for the soul and body.

At the moment, the Yarmarka shopping center (Astrakhan) is a huge three-story building, the tiers of which are located on a total of 81,000 square meters. m, among them, almost half are leased to various retail outlets. Among local tenants, one can notice representatives of world brands, including Calzedonia, Reserved, Peppi, CHIC and many others.



Location

The mall is located in a place through which a large number of people move every day. The location of the object in such an area ensures its high popularity. Not far from the building of the Yarmarka shopping center (Astrakhan) is the Alexandro-Mariinskaya Hospital and another large Magnit store.

This place can be very easily reached by public transport, so buses passing from different areas of the city pass through Vokzalnaya Square, where the shopping center is located. Many visitors use taxi services or come in private cars, which can always be parked in a parking lot arranged near the entrance to the complex.

The Yarmarka shopping center (Astrakhan) has a large number of outlets where goods of various categories are offered. If you wish, you can buy the latest brand models from well-known manufacturers, toys, gifts, cosmetics, perfumes, jewelry and fashion accessories.

Conditions for children

"Fair" is perfect for family vacations, which most citizens arrange on weekends. Often parents and their children come to the large entertainment center "Orange", where not only kids but also adults can have fun. On its site there are large roundabouts, labyrinths, slot machines and other entertainments.

In addition to "Orange", in the "Fair" there are other places in which children are very fond of visiting. Among them, virtual reality rides, art studios, areas with cars and trains, as well as small platforms where there are a lot of entertainment and new acquaintances for them, can be noted.



Cinema

Many residents of the city admit that they very often visit the cinema, which is located in the shopping center. The show schedule at Kinomax (Yarmarka Shopping Center, Astrakhan) always contains films that are new to Russian and foreign cinema, which attracts a large number of fans of this art form.

In the cinema halls it is always cozy and comfortable - such sensations are provided thanks to comfortable armchairs in which guests can view interesting paintings while relaxing. As for the technical equipment of the halls, it is at its best. Lighting and sound equipment, as well as screens and projectors, are made using the latest technologies - they are capable of transmitting image and sound in strict accordance with the original - visitors really like it.

When leaving the halls, all guests get into the lobby, where there is a small cafe - you can have a bite to eat or drink soft drinks in it. There is also a board on which the cinema schedule is located (TC "Fair", Astrakhan).
