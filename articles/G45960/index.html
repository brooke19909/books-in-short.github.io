Holiday Village "Blue Light", Zadonsk. Description. Reviews

So that you can relax from the hustle and bustle of the city alone with nature, on the picturesque banks of the Don guests will be greeted by the cozy recreation center "Blue Light". From Zadonsk it is located at a distance of 3 kilometers. Mixed forests around, clean air, fishing. What else is needed for a perfect holiday ?!

Description

At the recreation center "Blue Light" in Zadonsk, on the fertile Lipetsk land, mostly employees of OJSC Voronezhoblgaz rest. But anyone can also not only spend their holidays, but also come for the weekend to go fishing, chat with friends and sleep off to the soft sound of rustling leaves. The forest zone is not only a good place to relax, it is also strengthening immunity for those who are prone to colds in the cold season.

The camp site is open all year. In summer, you can swim in the river and sunbathe on your own health resort beach. The beach area has a length of 150 meters, equipped with awnings, parasols, showers. There is also a boat station, those who wish can use the services of a rental point. For sports enthusiasts there are volleyball and basketball courts, a tennis court. In winter, sledges and snowmobiles are available to holidaymakers. For fans of the game of billiards, a large billiard room is equipped. Children have fun in the children's town.

Accommodation for vacationers

The number of rooms has places to accommodate 280 vacationers at the same time, including:

  • 160 places - in winter buildings;
  • more than 120 places - in wooden summer houses.

In the buildings there are rooms designed for a different number of vacationers:

  • in the administrative building - double junior suite;
  • two-story log houses No. 1 and No. 2 - single, double, and triple rooms;
  • two-story building number 3 - single, double, and triple rooms;
  • four-story building number 4 - single, double, triple, four-, and five-bed rooms, there are also three-bed two-room rooms;
  • summer houses.

A ticket to the recreation center "Blue Light" in Zadonsk includes accommodation and three meals a day. Its cost is:

  • from 400 rubles per day - for employees of JSC Voronezhoblgaz;
  • from 800 rubles per day - a usual ticket.

Each room is equipped with a fridge, modern furniture and a TV. There are bathrooms in the buildings. In summer houses they are not provided.

Additional services for vacationers

In the "Blue Light", a recreation center in Zadonsk, many different services are provided for tourists, making the vacation diverse and memorable.

  • At the camp site there is a cafe and bar, a large banquet hall.
  • For fans of picnics on the territory there are barbecue facilities and barbecue.
  • Tourists have the opportunity to use the services of a rental center for various sports equipment, namely: boats, catamarans, scooters, bicycles, badminton kits, fishing equipment in the warm season and skates, skis, sledges and snowmobiles in winter.
  • If desired, organize fishing for you.
  • You can ride a harnessed horse or horseback.
  • There are sports fields for playing volleyball, basketball, paintball.
  • The hostel "Blue Light" in Zadonsk has its own comfortable beach.
  • In winter, ski trails are laid for skiers, and you can skate on the rink.
  • For owners of their own cars, parking is equipped.
  • The organization of excursions to the Zadonsky St. Tikhon Transfiguration Monastery, in the territory of which there is a holy spring.
  • After an active day, you can visit the sauna.

All additional services are paid according to the price list.
