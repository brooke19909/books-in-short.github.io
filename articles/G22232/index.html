The principle of PAMM accounts

In simple words, the purpose of a PAMM account is to give an investor the opportunity to earn money, while not participating in direct trading on Forex. Account managers receive additional income for the competent management of the finances entrusted to them. If you take into account the feedback on investing in Alpari PAMM accounts and other information sources, the principle of earnings of depositors and traders is based on the following points:

  • The manager registers the PAMM-account, investing in it any possible amount. It will acquire the status of capital, which will be reflected to potential investors.
  • The account manager risks this amount of money in the same way as depositors. This principle ensures that trading will be more deliberate and balanced.
  • Then an offer agreement is created, in which the conditions for cooperation with investors are noted. Also fixed is the percentage of profit intended as a reward for managing Alpari PAMM accounts.
  • According to brokers, at this stage, the investor's task is to study the performance of the manager. The main indicator here is the rating.
  • Having decided on the choice of a PAMM account, the investor invests funds.
  • The manager works directly with transactions (Forex trading operations), using his capital and trusted investments.


Gains and losses that result from bidding are distributed between the trader and depositor automatically, in a ratio proportional to the amounts deposited. It is worth noting that the manager can develop his PAMM account independently by inviting a partner who does not work in the Forex market and is not connected with the management of the existing portfolio. It should attract investors and assist in the development of a PAMM account, and in return will receive a certain percentage of the manager‚Äôs profit.
