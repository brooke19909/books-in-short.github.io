ASUS 10-inch tablets with 4G keyboard: reviews

Just two mobile devices ZenPad10 ZD300CL and TransformerPad TF300TL from ASUS will be discussed in this review article. These are functional and productive ASUS tablets. 10 inches is the display diagonal of these mobile devices, which, if necessary, allows you to turn them into a full-fledged laptop with a touch screen by connecting a keyboard.

These devices are marketed as ASUS tablets. 10 inches, as noted earlier, the screen size of these advanced mobile computers. It is also possible to connect a keyboard to this tablet. Model ZenPad10 ZD300CL and its characteristics

Be that as it may, the more advanced features of this particular mobile device. First of all, this is the Intel ATOM Z3560 central processor. He has 4 computing modules that are capable of overclocking in the highest load mode to 1.83 GHz. The manufacturing process of this semiconductor crystal corresponds to 22nm. It is this feature of this chip that ensures the overall high energy efficiency of the device.

In this case, the PowerVR G6430 acts as a graphic accelerator , which will cope even with the most demanding tasks without special problems. The amount of RAM is 2 GB, and the capacity of the built-in drive is 32 GB. There is also the possibility of increasing its capacity by installing an external flash card, the maximum capacity of which can be 64 GB. The touch screen is based on the IPS matrix with HD resolution. This allows you to get quite good image quality on the screen of the device.

The main camera of the tablet PC has a 5 megapixel sensor, and the front camera has a 2 megapixel sensor. It is not necessary to expect perfect photo and video quality from them, but nevertheless, at a normal level of illumination, they allow you to get pictures of acceptable quality. Compare tablet specifications

These are very similar ASUS tablets: 10 inches they have a diagonal display, at the heart of each of them is a 4-core CPU, the matrix of screens is almost identical. But all the same, the technical specifications and the software component are preferable to the ZenPad10 ZD300CL. This includes more RAM and an internal drive, and a more efficient processor solution (the frequency of this CPU is much higher - 1.83 GHz versus 1.2 GHz), and a more recent version of system software. But TransformerPad TF300TL has much less advantages: better battery life (14 hours versus 9) and an improved main camera sensor (8 megapixels versus 5 megapixels).





The cost of each model

ASUS 10-inch tablets with similar specifications cannot be cheap. More affordable is the TransformerPad TF300TL model. At the moment, it can still be purchased for 15,000 rubles. It has already been discontinued relatively long ago, now only its stocks, which are very limited, are being sold out.

If you plan to get such a mobile universal computer, then you need to hurry. ZenPad10 ZD300CL is much more expensive - from 22,000 rubles today. This is a more recent model of a transformer device, which will be sold for quite some time.

Buying Guidelines

These are very similar ASUS tablets. 10 inches, 4G networks, GPS, GLONASS, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi - this is not a complete list of technical specifications for these devices, which are common. But the processor part and the memory subsystem are much better with the ZenPad10 ZD300CL. In addition, it should also be noted that this is a more recent device. In fact, he has only one minus in comparison with the second device of this review - a higher cost. But it is compensated by improved parameters and therefore it is more preferable to buy ZenPad10 ZD300CL.
