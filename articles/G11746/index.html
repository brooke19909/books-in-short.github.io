How to make a fishing rod with your own hands?

Traditionally fishing is one of the options for outdoor recreation. For some, this is a serious hobby, for others - the opportunity to test yourself. But sometimes situations arise when fishing can be the only means of subsistence. For example, if you had to linger on an island surrounded by water due to bad weather conditions, or you got lost. Let's see how to make a fishing rod with your own hands directly in nature.





Make the rod

It is easy to make a fishing rod from improvised material even in extreme conditions. The simplest fishing rod can be made from a thin tree trunk, for example, hazel, bird cherry or juniper. To do this, the trunk should be cut at the base, clean from knots. It is not necessary to remove the bark, the quality of fishing will not deteriorate from it. The main thing is to check the strength of the rod. Indeed, the loss of fishing line and hook, if your design does not support the weight of the fish, is similar to a lost fisherman. To do this, you can fill the sock with sand, attach it to the end of the rod, seeing how to make the fishing rod stronger.

We fix the fishing line

As fishing line for fishing rod, you can use a thin rope, loose on the thread. For catching large fish, shoe laces are suitable, in extreme cases, thin wires, previously rubbed with grass, so as not to scare away the fish with an unusual smell. In ancient times, for example, they knew how to make a fishing rod from a braided horsehair. The line should be 50 centimeters longer than the rod. You can fix it at the end of the fishing rod by making a slit incision.





We select the sinker

A good sinker can be obtained from the shots of a hunting cartridge. Any heavy item, such as a nut, a bolt, and even an ordinary stone, is also suitable. Knowing how to make a fishing rod, you can use suitable improvised materials: a case from a film strip, a glove, a handkerchief in which sand is poured. In the old days, sinkers were made of clay. It is better to fix it not directly to the fishing line, but at a distance of 20 centimeters from the hook.

We are looking for a float

If you decide how to make a fishing rod, then you will need a little more imagination. Lightweight material, such as a cork from a bottle, polystyrene, as well as a fountain pen, bird feather, tree bark, or bulrush, can be used instead of a float. We set the float so that with the help of a sinker it protrudes from the water by one third of its size. If you plan to catch a pike or perch, a fishing rod float is an optional attribute.

Set the hook

In extreme situations, the hook can be made from a nail or ordinary metal wire. Its end should be slightly flattened by banging with a heavy object. Then make a deep notch with a knife or an ax, bending the wire up so that the fish caught does not slip. A hook can be made from a ring from a bunch of keys, hair clips, invisibility, brooches or safety pins.





So, knowing how to make a fishing rod, you can even in extreme situations not to worry about being hungry. The main thing is to show a little skill and imagination, and a delicious fish for dinner is provided to you.
