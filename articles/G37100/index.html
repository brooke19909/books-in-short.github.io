What do you need?

Daisy made from balls is very simple. You will need balls of only three colors: 5 white and 2 yellow, as well as a green sausage ball. For inflation you can use a pump. To hold balloons together, you need to prepare gum.





Decorative bouquet

Turning ordinary balls into an elegant bouquet is not only interesting, but also very simple. This is an original and at the same time simple way to design the perfect little princess party or gazebo for afternoon tea in the garden. Or maybe you just want to revitalize your best friend's birthday with a little sign of appreciation? An airy bouquet or even just one daisy made of balls can cheer you up and make you smile.

Step 1

We take a sheet of cardboard and cut out two round holes of different diameters in it. Suitable sizes are 15 cm and 10 cm. You should not worry about the beauty of the circles. This is just the template needed to adjust the volume of the balls.



Step 2

To create flower petals, take five white balls. Using a pump and a template with an opening of 15 cm, we inflate all five pieces so that their sizes are almost the same.





The ends of the balloons are tied together with an elastic band.





Step 3

To create a flower center, you need two small yellow balls. We use a 10 cm template and a balloon for inflation. Chamomile from balls does not have to be yellow-white, other colors can also be used.





However, it will be desirable to use one-color petals and a core of a different shade for contrast. When the balls are inflated, their ends should also be tied.





Step 4

We connect the petals and the core so that the flower looks approximately the same on both sides.



The result was a magnificent daisy made of balls. Do it yourself is very simple to make such a creation. It remains to attach the stems to it, and that's it - the original gift is ready! You can decorate the room. There are several such original flowers.
