Ideal Beach Hotel 4 * hotel offers its guests 103 comfortable rooms with various levels of service and sizes. Most of them are equipped with spacious balconies with magnificent views of the sea, mountain hills or exotic outlandish vegetation.

  1. Standard Room. Standard apartments for accommodation from two to three people, equipped with a balcony with a picturesque view of the turquoise sea surface or garden courtyard. Their total area is 27 square meters, it includes a bedroom and a modern bathroom.
