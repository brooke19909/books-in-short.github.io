With the development of autonomous energy sources, it becomes more and more popular and economically feasible to install solar panels in the country and at home. In our time, technologies have emerged that make it possible to receive not only electric current from the sun, but also thermal energy in an amount sufficient to heat a house.

Solar heating involves the use of a special system in which water circulates, converting kinetic energy into heat. The heated liquid then enters the special radiator systems installed in the house, and it gets warmer there.

But let's talk about panels used as a source of electricity. Installing solar panels as the main source of energy can help solve all problems with power outages at the same time.

What are the types?

They can conditionally be divided into two main types. These are silicon batteries and film.

Photosensitive film elements have just begun to produce. Release them in Denmark. The advantage is that by deploying a roll of a similar film on the roof, it can be cut and sized. The battery is not very powerful, but it is cheaper than others, because for its manufacture not the most expensive materials are required.





Solar panels for the home, which are based on silicon wafers, are still considered the most popular and affordable option. When compared with other types of batteries, silicon can be called the most effective. Silicon batteries can be single crystal or polycrystalline.

Monocrystalline

Monocrystalline panels are composed of interconnected cells, for the production of which the purest silicon is used. This material is obtained by the Czochralski method. When a single crystal hardens, it is divided into plates whose thickness does not exceed 300 microns. These elements are covered with a network of copper conductors, with the help of which electric current is removed from them. Such a set of solar panels for summer cottages is the most effective, it is able to provide an efficiency of 22% and higher. But its price is rather big, because the manufacturing process of panels is rather complicated and expensive.

Polycrystalline Solar Panels

When slowly cooled, molten silicon forms the so-called polycrystal. This process involves the use of not so much energy, so it is not surprising that solar panels made from polycrystal are cheaper for a home. The disadvantage of polycrystalline solar panels is their relatively low efficiency (up to 17%). However, there is an advantage (except for the price, of course). It lies in the fact that these batteries are much less demanding on the quality of sunlight. If the effectiveness of the previous panels drops sharply in cloudy weather, then polycrystalline work even at dawn and sunset.

Inverter

It is used not only in cases when the solar battery gives energy. An outdoor wind station or a hydraulic turbine can also produce current that converts this device.

How are kits installed?

Having bought a set of solar panels for gardening, you need to install it correctly.





A panel of photovoltaic cells, whatever type it is, is mounted on the roof. It is advisable that the panel does not fall shadow during the day from trees growing nearby. You may need to prune the branches or remove the plants completely.

Panels to the roof are not screwed tightly, they are located from the coating at a distance of a decimeter. This circulates air under the batteries. In addition, the modules will not so bask on the surface of the tile or other coating.





The remaining devices must be installed in a special room in the house. The room should be protected from children and free access should be provided to those who will be responsible for monitoring the functioning of the system. All household devices that consume low power current can be connected directly to the controller. Other devices must receive energy through the inverter.

Installation of such systems is a simple matter, any adult who is familiar with the principles of conducting electrical circuits can do this.
