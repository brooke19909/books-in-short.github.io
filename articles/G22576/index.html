Grand Faros Hotel 4 Standard Marmaris: hotel description and reviews

The famous Turkish resort and at the same time a large tourist center Marmaris has long become a favorite vacation spot for Russians. It attracts wonderful beaches, many places where you can have a great time, wonderful restaurants, unique sights and, of course, the opportunity to choose the perfect hotel for your stay. We suggest you stop your attention at Grand Faros Hotel 4 * Standard. Marmaris will surely appeal to those who have never been here and will not deceive the expectations of those who are already familiar with it. And the hotel will provide the most comfortable stay here.

Location

Again, the Grand Faros Hotel 4 Standard is located in Marmaris, but not in the city center, but on its outskirts, surrounded by orange orchards and olive groves, behind which the mountains begin. From here, to the center of Marmaris, where the main tourist attractions and dozens of shops are concentrated, is only 3.2 km away. Many tourists prefer to overcome them on foot. Such a walk takes about half an hour. About 100 meters from the hotel is Mustafa Kemal Pasha Street, one of the main streets in the city. Intensive traffic of vehicles, including minibuses and buses, is carried out along it. Therefore, those guests who do not like to walk have the opportunity to cheaply and quickly get to the center of Marmaris by public transport.





Many tourists believe that the location of the Grand Faros Hotel is very convenient, since in the immediate vicinity of it (on the same Mustafa Kemal Pasha Street) there are the largest supermarkets in the city, Kipa and Migros, an elite Waikiki store, and also unique Turkish baths ‚ÄúArmutalan Hamam‚Äù, where they offer dozens of types of skin and face care services. Another important facility that you may need to visit on vacation is the Marmaris Central Hospital. It is about 5 minutes drive from the hotel.

How to get there

Mostly Russians come to Turkey by air. The nearest airport to Marmaris (Dalaman) is 94 km from the city and 97 km from the hotel. To his front door, tourists who bought tickets to travel agencies are delivered on comfortable buses. The road takes an hour. The second airport, where travel agents can provide tickets, is called Milas Bodrum. It is 120 km from the hotel, and the journey time is approximately an hour and forty minutes.

Hotel Description Grand Faros Hotel 4 * Standard in Marmaris

This hotel was launched in 1999. In 2012, the last renovation took place here. It represents one building in five floors, consisting of 3 sections. Between them are elevators with transparent walls. The rooms are located from the second to the fourth floor, and on the first and fifth are cultural and sports facilities and amenities.





The territory of the hotel is small, it is 2050 m 2 . According to tourists, she is very beautiful and well-groomed. Cleanliness and order reign in every corner of it, green lawns, bright flowers, evergreen shrubs are pleasing to the eye. In the courtyard there is a wonderful garden, which makes it pleasant to stroll on a hot day or a quiet summer evening. There are also swimming pools (adult and children), a relaxation area with sun loungers, a playground, an outdoor terrace with a bar with a TV. Free parking is available at the main entrance to the hotel (on the road side).

Infrastructure

Grand Faros Hotel 4 * Standard in Marmaris is focused only on the rest of tourists. Therefore, the conditions for business events are not here. The hotel lobby is spacious, stylish, all shining with cleanliness. Marble floors grated to shine , comfortable leather armchairs and sofas, exclusive lamps, flowers in beautiful flowerpots contribute to an excellent mood and create the image of the hotel. Reception is located in the lobby.





The staff speak Turkish, German and English. Only a senior manager knows a little Russian, who is not always in place. At the reception you can exchange currency, use a photocopy and fax machine, rent a car or bicycle, put your suitcases in a left-luggage office, and personal items - for laundry, call a doctor and taxi, buy a tour. In the lobby there is a bar and a small shop where guests are offered related products and the press. There is a beauty salon on the ground floor and there are games rooms with billiards and tennis tables.

Rooms

A description of their design and equipment can be given approximately the same, since their category is only one - the standard. The only difference is that there are one-room numbers, designed to accommodate two, maximum three guests, and two-room rooms, which can accommodate a family of 3, 4 or 5 people. In the voucher, their category can be designated as a suite.

They have air conditioning, a large flat-screen TV, a paid safe, a refrigerator with a minibar, and a balcony. Views from it only to the mountains or to the hotel grounds. The hygiene room has a sink, a modern shower, toilet, hairdryer. Individual means for taking a shower are not provided, only toilet paper and soap.

Nutrition

Grand Faros Hotel 4 * Standard in Marmaris has a restaurant, a lobby bar and a poolside bar. Basically, tours to this hotel are offered with the Al food system, which means free breakfasts, lunches, snacks, dinners. In the bar, this system works until 23-00. Later, all drinks can be taken only for money. Tourists on the quality of food at the hotel respond favorably, noting a wide variety of dishes on the menu.

For breakfast, there are always several types of cereals, cereals, milk, yoghurts, crepes, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãsausages and cheeses, vegetables, meat dishes, several types of side dishes, sweets, fruits. The lunch menu, as tourists note, is not very wide, since many guests take tours with the HB food system, which means half board. But the dinner menu impresses with sophistication and variety. Vacationers are offered delicious dishes of meat (lamb, beef, pork), poultry, fish, seafood, as well as a variety of side dishes and desserts.

Leisure

Interesting enough and with health benefits you can spend time at the Grand Faros Hotel 4 * Standard in Marmaris. Photos show an outdoor pool on its territory. The depth in it reaches 1.7 meters, and the water is so clean that cats even drink it. Around the pool there is a sunbathing area. There are always sunbeds and umbrellas. Beach towels at the hotel are issued as a deposit. In addition to relaxing by the pool, guests can use the fitness room equipped with the most modern fitness equipment, a sauna and a hammam for free.





On the ground floor of the hotel there is a place for playing billiards, darts, table tennis, backgammon, and in the beauty salon you can not only do a hairstyle or manicure, but also get a quality massage. In the evenings, a group of animators entertain the guests. They organize shows, concerts, interesting programs. But noisy entertainment here is provided only until 23-00. After this time, everything freezes at the hotel. Those who crave to continue the fun need to go to the city center.

Services for children

Grand Faros Hotel 4 * Standard in Marmaris for children does not offer a very diverse service. Please note that cots are available in rooms upon prior request. On the territory there is a separate children's pool and a small playground. According to tourists, swings and slides on it are no longer new. Children's animation, nanny, games room are not provided in the hotel.

Sea

Regarding the coast, the Grand Faros Hotel 4 * Standard in Marmaris is not very conveniently located. Reviews of most tourists report that walking to the sea on foot at least 15-20 minutes. The hotel provides a free shuttle to the beach and back, but it runs only three times a day: early in the morning, before lunch and in the evening.





The hotel has its own beach, but it is necessary to share it with the guests of the Yunuz Hotel. Despite this, there is enough space for everyone under the sun. Tourists note that the sea on the beach is clean, but due to the fact that the bottom is sandy, it is not as transparent as many tourists expect. But due to the location of Marmaris in the bay, there are almost no waves on the beach, and the water warms up faster than in other parts of the coast.

Opinions of tourists

Reviews for the Grand Faros Hotel 4 * Standard in Marmaris are quite favorable. The rating of this hotel is from 4.14 to 4.35 points out of 5, which indicates a good level of service that is offered here.

Tourists note the following advantages of the hotel:

  • spacious rooms;
  • cleanliness in the territory, in the restaurant, and in the rooms;
  • very tasty food, always a lot of fruit;
  • comfortable swimming pool;
  • the presence in the hotel of a functional fitness club;
  • the availability of free internet throughout.




The hotel's shortcomings noted in most reviews:

  • far from the sea;
  • shuttle provision only three times a day;
  • lack of children's animation;
  • not all rooms are renovated;
  • poor soundproofing of rooms;
  • the presence of warm water in the shower only a few hours a day;
  • no Russian-speaking staff.

Bottom line: this hotel is perfect for those who plan to relax quietly, away from the noise of entertainment centers, bars and discos. For young people and families with children, it may not be very comfortable here.
