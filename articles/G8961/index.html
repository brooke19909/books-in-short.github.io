"Elbrus" (ski resort): photos, reviews, location, hotels

Surely everyone knows about the great volcanic hill of the Greater Caucasus - Elbrus. Over the centuries, it has been one of the highest mountains and is considered the hallmark of the Kabardino-Balkarian region. This place attracts with unchartedness, delights with mystery and attracts climbers and skiers of all countries.

Climbing to the highest point of the Earth is considered not only a dangerous, but also a prestigious route. "Elbrus" is a ski resort that has won worldwide fame. The hill is interesting with magnificent ridges, picturesque ice slopes, extreme relaxation and comfortable natural and climatic conditions.

Conquering a volcanic peak requires tremendous stress, physical preparation and technical skill. Upon reaching this goal, euphoria and indescribable pleasure are felt. Daredevils who have successfully climbed are given personal certificates.

Ice Glade Tour





The tourist season lasts all year on Mount Elbrus (ski resort). Where is the long extinct volcano located? It is located on the border of Karachay-Cherkessia and Kabardino-Balkaria, 130 km from the city of Nalchik. You can get to the foot by car. It is not advisable to visit the highlands during the off-season, since the weather is unstable and the risk of avalanches escaping.

The main part of the reliefs is flat, at an altitude of 4000 m the temperature reaches the level of -35. On the northern and western slopes, glaciers are covered with deep cracks, in the south they are more even and gentle. This place is famous for its Azau Valley, where narzan mineral springs beat. It is also popular with extreme freeriders, as is the Elbrus ski resort. "Azau" is a snowy meadow with long tracks, ice rinks and cable cars.

Leisure activities





The possibilities for winter and summer holidays on Mount Elbrus are endless. The ski resort is famous not only for its rugged landscapes, but also for its rich flora and fauna. Professional climbers come here, thirsting for thrills and adrenaline, tourists looking for solitude with nature, active pastime and positive emotions.

There are many interesting routes leading to gorges and popular attractions. Guests will have the opportunity to personally observe the herds of wild horses and a flock of sheep, drink healthy mineral water directly from the source. Year-round guests can enjoy skiing and snowboarding, as well as hang gliding.





There are tracks of varying complexity and maneuverability that will give delight and a dizzying mood. For safety reasons, all cable cars are equipped with video surveillance, speakerphone. The slopes are equipped with special signs, mats and protective nets. Each interchange station will offer tourists to warm themselves in a cozy restaurant and try various dishes, and then again go on a trip. Points for renting the necessary sports equipment are also open.

The choice of places to stay here is quite rich - you just have to consider suitable options. So, in addition to hotels, it will be possible to stay in private houses, renting a room from local residents.

"Elbrus" (ski resort): reviews of tourists

Fresh air, a firework of feelings, an adrenaline rush, crisp snow and gentle sun - this is how guests and locals characterize the resort. Fall in love with him from the first moment. So, it is not for nothing that the extinct volcano "Elbrus" overshadows the foreign resting places. The ski resort caters to everyone regardless of social status and income.

According to travelers, being at the top, plunges into the bliss of pleasure, dissolves in pristine, pure nature and simply enjoys its generous gifts. It is the snow-covered reliefs and the indescribable beauty of the slopes that determine the atmosphere of this unique place. A lot of nice words were said about highways, lifts and quality service. In addition, guests are very pleased with the prices, the availability of modern hotels and the friendliness of the staff.





The route to the highest point of the Caucasus

There are several options for how to get to this resort area:

  • First, arrival by plane to the airport of Nalchik or Mineralnye Vody. Then change to public transport (bus, minibus) or private taxi that will take you to the village of Terskola (200 km.).
  • By train to the stations Nalchik, Prokhladny, Pyatigorsk and Mineralnye Vody. You can use the group transfer, in which case you will be met by the staff of the booked hotel and taken to the foot of the mountain.
