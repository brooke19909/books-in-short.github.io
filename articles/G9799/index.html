Silver Sands Beach Resorts, Colva Hotel: description, overview and reviews of tourists

India is an exotic country that attracts travelers from all over the world with white beaches, unique nature, rich history and culture. One of the popular oceanfront destinations is Goa. It is here that the small but cozy hotel called Silver Sands Beach Resorts is located. Of course, to make a vacation plan, tourists need all the information about this place. What kind of vacation can you expect?





Description of location and infrastructure

Tourists, as a rule, begin their research with the question of exactly where the Silver Sands Beach Resort hotel is located. Colva (Colva) - a picturesque resort village, which is located on the coast of South Goa. This is where you have to spend your vacation. The beach is about 1000 meters away. Dabolim International Airport is approximately 22 kilometers away. It is not worth worrying about the distance, as the hotel offers its guests a shuttle service. Margao Railway Station is 3 km away.

Catering for hotel guests

Silver Sands Beach Resorts offers its guests free breakfast. On the territory of the main restaurant, receptions are organized in the mornings, where you can eat and chat with other guests. According to reviews, the menu here is quite diverse. On the other hand, meat dishes are not often served for breakfast, which is not uncommon in India. At lunchtime and in the evening, you can visit other cafes and restaurants located in the same area. For example, lunch can be ordered on the beach, and spend the evening in a colorful Indian cafe. By the way, in the hotel restaurant you will also be free to eat, however, for an additional fee.

The hotel also has a stylishly equipped bar where you can relax with a strong drink, taste aromatic tea and enjoy the atmosphere of hospitality.





Where to look for the beach, and is there any opportunity to have fun on the beach?

The distance to the picturesque sea coast is about one kilometer. This stretch can be easily overcome on foot - an exciting journey along a convenient path awaits you, during which you will explore the surroundings and simply enjoy the beauty of nature. Alternatively, you can take a bus that leaves from Silver Sands Beach Resorts several times a day.

The beaches here are public, you yourself will choose which section of the coast you should spend the day on. For little money you can rent a comfortable chaise lounge with a soft mattress. Almost all beaches have their own bars where you can eat and buy a chilled drink - the prices here are quite affordable. Fans of more active leisure will also not be disappointed, because they have the opportunity to rent a boat, canoe, catamaran, go skiing and motorbiking, do more serious water sports.

Additional services and entertainment

Despite its modest size, Silver Sands Beach Resorts has something to offer the traveler. For example, you can use the currency exchange service, take the laundry, visit the business center and hold events in the local conference room. In case of health problems, a qualified doctor will be called to you, whose services are not so expensive. You can rent a safe from the administrator - this guarantees the safety of your belongings.

In the courtyard there is a rather large pool. According to reviews, the water here is always clean, like the terrace - everything is cleaned several times a day. Nearby is a shallow swimming pool for children. By the way, if you need to leave the child for a while, you can turn to a professional nanny.





There is also a gym for guests - you can keep fit. There is also a Ayurveda center where you can attend a variety of classes. Some tourists prefer to sign up for a wellness massage course, since really qualified staff work here. You can spend your free time playing a game of table tennis. And do not forget about excursions, because India is a country with an extraordinary culture and ancient history.
