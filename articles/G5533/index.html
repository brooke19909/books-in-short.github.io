How to make gifts from Kinders with your own hands?

Almost all children love sweets. Caring for the teeth of babies, parents and other relatives prefer to buy kinder chocolates, candy bars and chocolate eggs. However, instead of buying just a set of chocolates in a box, many prefer to make original gifts from kinder in the form of volumetric compositions or some objects: for a boy - a typewriter, for a girl - a basket with flowers.

Buy a ready-made kit: the pros and cons

You can order a souvenir from Kinder at a company specializing in gifts and floristry. You bring sweets, say what you want to receive, and in a couple of days take away the finished object. The more sweet elements you can get, the more effective the product will be, but it can take more than one hour to create it, and it will also take enough patience and desire to create a special souvenir.

Tools and materials

To make voluminous gifts from kinders of some complex shape, you will need not only sweets, but also frame elements. So, prepare the following:

  • Chocolate eggs, chocolates, bars.
  • It can be supplemented with Raffaello candies or any other.
  • Thermogun or glue.
  • Cardboard.
  • Wooden sticks (skewers for barbecue).
  • The wire.
  • Pencil.
  • Ruler.
  • Eraser.
  • Decor (flowers made of paper, fabric, satin ribbons, leaves, corrugated paper).

The specific set depends on what exactly, for whom and for which holiday you are going to perform.

To create a bouquet resembling a composition of tulips, work like this:

  1. Buy the right amount of chocolate eggs, the corresponding number of colored napkins and wooden skewers.
  2. If you use corrugated paper instead of napkins, you will need to cut it first. You can, in general, make petals of any flower out of it.
  3. Wrap the chocolate egg in a napkin and secure it below with wire or a floral ribbon on a skewer.
  4. Wrap the skewer with a strip of green paper, fixing it in several places on the glue.
  5. Cut leaves from green paper and attach to stems. It is also easy to perform sepals, if you do not have tulips, but other flowers.
  6. Place the composition in a vase, a basket. It is also easy to lay out the basket from Kinder chocolates on the basis of a frame from a round box or a blank made of a circle and a cardboard strip made independently.
