Hotel "Pervomayskaya" (Moscow): photos, prices, reviews

As the capital of Russia and a large metropolis, Moscow today is a fairly popular city. Every day people come here, someone on business trips, and someone as a tourist to see the sights of the capital. Moscow guests sometimes need to choose a hotel that will meet all the needs of a traveler.





You can find a home for a while both in the city center and on its outskirts. For example, the Pervomaiskaya Hotel is perfect for those who need a comfortable room at an affordable price.

Description and photo

For all those who are looking for coziness and comfort away from the bustle of the center of the capital, this small hotel will be a good choice. Hotel Pervomaiskaya was awarded two stars. It offers its guests comfortable rooms of various categories and room service available throughout the day.

The hotel area is covered by a free wireless Internet area, so guests living here do not need to run around the building in search of a good signal level.

Guests will enjoy the cozy and simple restaurant that the Pervomaiskaya Hotel has at its disposal. Photos of the restaurant and rooms can be seen in this article.





Where is the hotel?

The location will appeal to those tourists who prefer at every opportunity to escape from the bustle of the city and be closer to nature. In walking distance there is a large and green Izmaylovsky park - a great place for walking with the whole family, with friends or alone.

Where is the Pervomaiskaya Hotel located? Address: Moscow, st. Nizhnyaya Pervomayskaya, house 77. This place is located in the Izmailovsky district of the city. How to get there

One of the advantages is that not far from one of the Moscow metro stations is the Pervomaiskaya Hotel. How to get to it from the Pervomaiskaya metro station?





It is better to come to the metro station in the last carriage if the train goes from the center. Then you can walk to the hotel on Pervomaiskaya Street in the direction of 15th Parkova, then turn onto it and walk to the intersection with Lower Pervomaiskaya Street.

You can reach the public transport stop, take the tram number 11 or 34 or the trolleybus 22 and go to the stop called 15th Parkovaya Street. Not far from the stop will be the Pervomaiskaya Hotel (Moscow).

What rooms does the hotel offer

The hotel's rooms will satisfy the needs of any guests. There are both simple standard rooms and luxurious suites that meet European standards. In any room there is a TV and a refrigerator, all the necessary furniture for living, a bathroom, hygiene products.

Among the hotel rooms there are different categories. One-room single room is perfect for those traveling alone. On the comfortable single bed you can relax after a tiring day or business negotiations, and with the help of the telephone you can make the necessary calls.





The business single room will satisfy the needs of guests looking for superior comfort. It features a room with a small entrance hall and a spacious bathroom.

Economy and standard double rooms allow two guests to stay at an affordable price and enjoy all that is needed on a long trip.

Spacious junior suites are designed for two guests with a small child or three adults. The rooms have a comfortable double bed, a spacious bathroom, air conditioning, hairdryer and even the means by which you can put your shoes in order.

Spacious two-room suites will suit the most demanding guests. The Pervomaiskaya Hotel offers a comfortable bedroom with a double bed, an equipped study, a comfortable sofa, air conditioning and a telephone. In this room, three adults or a family with two children can perfectly live.





Cost of living at the Pervomaiskaya Hotel

The prices for rooms in this hotel are different. The cheapest single room in retro style will cost 1990 rubles. per day. A simple single room costs 3290 rubles, and a business class for one person - 3690 rubles.

For a double room in the "retro" style, guests will pay 1990 rubles, for an extra bed in it 995 rubles. Double economy costs 2790 rubles. (1395 rubles for an extra bed), and a double standard will cost 3290 rubles.

Depending on the type of junior suite, its cost is 3990 rubles. and 4950 rubles. Extra bed in this category of rooms will cost guests 590 rubles.





The most expensive hotel room is a suite. The cost of this category per day is 7790 rubles.

All rooms include breakfast. If guests travel with a child under the age of three, he can stay in the room free of charge, but in this case an extra bed is not provided.

For vacationers who plan to stay at the hotel for a long time, a special offer applies . When paying for accommodation within 14 days, a discount of 7% is provided, and if tourists want to stay for 30 days, then a discount of 15%.

Guest reviews

Hotel "Pervomaiskaya" is always glad to new guests and those who have repeatedly rested in it. In their reviews, travelers note the proximity of the hotel to the medical center. Pirogov. This is very convenient for those who came to Moscow for an operation.





Guests do not always respond well to rooms. Some lack a freshener in the bathroom and lamps above the beds. Other tourists note that all rooms are quite old, but clean.

A bit of negative is present in reviews of local breakfasts: some guests say that they are very monotonous, the choice of dishes is small.
