How to transfer from a Sberbank card to a Tinkoff card?

Modern banks offer quite a lot of diverse services. For example, having different bank plastic, you can make money transfers by bank transfer. The main thing is to know how to act in a particular case. It is not difficult to transfer funds from a Sberbank card to a Tinkoff card . Below we will try to find out how to make this kind of transaction.



Translation Methods

You can pay Tinkoff from a Sberbank bank card in various ways. The answer directly depends on the personal preferences of citizens.

Today, transactions can be made:

  • through Sberbank Online;
  • through ATMs;
  • using Sberbank payment terminals;
  • through online services;
  • from "My Account" on the Tinkoff website;
  • through special programs for mobile devices from Tinkoff.

Next will be described in more detail about each reception. Each person can transfer funds from a Sberbank card to a Tinkoff card . Especially if he knows the details of both plastics. Without this data, transactions do not take place.

Tinkoff website

Let's start with the simplest technique that is used by many citizens. You can pay a Tinkoff loan through a Sberbank card or simply transfer a certain amount of funds through the Tinkoff website.



How to cope with the task? The following instructions will help you do this:

  1. Open tinkoff.ru/cardtocard.
  2. In the image on the left, indicate the details of Sberbank plastic.
  3. In the right part of the window register information from the Tinkoff card.
  4. Indicate the amount of payment. Attention: commission is charged for the operation. Its size will be displayed on the screen.
  5. Put a check mark next to "I have read ...".
  6. Click on the "Translate" button.

It is done! So easily and simply you can pay Tinkoff from a Sberbank credit card. This technique does not require any registration. Any citizen, even one who is not a Tinkoff client, can take advantage of it.

Especially for customers

Nevertheless, no one forbids to cooperate with several financial organizations at once. This is a normal occurrence.

From the Sberbank card to the Tinkoff card, funds can be transferred through the Tinkoff Personal Account. It is not necessary to register on the bank's website - you can do without it.

The algorithm of actions under such circumstances will be as follows:

  1. Go to tinkoff.ru/login/ in a browser.
  2. In the "Login" field, specify either the username (for Tinkoff customers) or the phone number (for strangers).
  3. Enter the login confirmation code. It will be sent by SMS.
  4. Open the "Payments and Transfers" section.
  5. Click on "Map".
  6. Indicate the bank details of the plastic of Sberbank.
  7. Select your Tinkoff card from the list that appears.
  8. Press the confirm button.

Sberbank Online to help

For example, Sberbank offers Internet banking to cope with the task. This is a service called Sberbank Online. With its help you can make any money transfers. And transferring funds from a card to the plastic of another bank will not take much time and effort.

To implement the task it is necessary:

  1. Register in the Sberbank Online system. You can get a one-time password to log in to the ATM.
  2. Log in to your profile on the Sberbank Online site. To do this, use the user ID and password. The relevant data is offered after registration in the system or after submitting a request at an ATM / terminal.
  3. Open the "Payments and Transfers" section.
  4. Select the "To another bank card" function.
  5. Prescribe the details of the beneficiary's bank account.
  6. Indicate the amount of transfer.
  7. Confirm the transaction.

Everything is extremely simple and clear. Such an operation involves a commission. You can transfer money from a Sberbank card to a Tinkoff card with only 1.5% commission on the transaction amount.

ATM

Some users prefer to avoid Internet services when working with money. This is normal. Each client of Sberbank is able to transfer money through ATMs. And even with the help of some payment terminals.



It is necessary to act as follows:

  1. Insert Sberbank plastic into the appropriate ATM.
  2. Enter the access code (PIN).
  3. Go to the "Payments in my region" section.
  4. Select "Transfer funds." Usually this item is located in the "Payments" block.
  5. Indicate your Tinkoff bank card details.
  6. Make sure the information entered is correct.
  7. Indicate the amount of transfer.
  8. Confirm action.

You can pick up a check about payment and wait for the transfer of funds. Usually a transaction is processed for several hours, but sometimes the money goes to another bank within 5 days.

Mobile applications

It is proposed to transfer funds from a Sberbank card to a Tinkoff card using a telephone. More precisely, through mobile applications from the mentioned financial institutions. The algorithm of actions in all cases will be approximately the same.

It comes down to the following manipulations:

  1. Open this or that mobile application. For example, Sberbank Online Mobile.
  2. Log in to the system.
  3. Look in the menu "Payments and Transfers" - "To the card of another bank."
  4. Prescribe data plastic "Sber" and "Tinkoff."
  5. Enter the amount to be transferred to the citizen.
  6. Click on the "Submit Request" button.

It is done. A few hours after processing the request, the citizen will receive the indicated amount of money. Similarly, you have to act in the Tinkoff application.

Finally

We found out how to transfer money from a Sberbank card to Tinkoff. Commissions for such transactions are usually charged. It is different in all cases. The average commission is 1.5-2% of the total transfer amount. Not so much, but you should know about such payments.



If the user works with Tinkoff Bank, no commission will be charged. The proposed techniques work in Russia at 100%. The main thing is not to wait for the funds to be transferred in a few minutes. As mentioned earlier, transactions are processed around a few business days.

Which technique is better to use? There are no recommendations in this regard. All action algorithms are equally good. And every client of Sberbank can use them.
