Population

The population of Easter Island does not exceed two thousand people. Among them you can meet red-skinned, white and black people. The main activities are fishing and sheep breeding.

Climate

This piece of land is in the subtropics, and therefore the summer there lasts all year round. Unlike other islands, there are no prolonged rains, but there are magnificent beaches.

Town

The only residential city on Easter Island is Hanga Roa. It is there that tourist life begins and ends. It has an airport, an Internet center, and hotels.

Riddles

This land hides many secrets, almost everywhere there are caves, stone platforms, alleys in the form of gutters that go far into the ocean, signs on stones. But many researchers are tormented and not left alone by the most important mystery - statues. These idols (moai) are made of stone and have a variety of heights, ranging from 3 to 21 meters. Their weight ranges from ten to twenty tons, and this is not the limit, there are colossi of forty and ninety tons. So glory came to Easter Island, the statues made it famous throughout the world. After all, it is completely incomprehensible who and how squeezed them out? Or they were brought by water, but then why are they? Why such a strange appearance, and what does it mean? Their appearance is really "wonderful." Each has a large head with a huge extended chin, long ears and no legs. Some statues have a headdress in the form of a cap of red stone. Raised pointed nose and mockery on thin lips. Maybe the Moai personify the tribe living here? Some giants have a necklace carved from stone, others have a tattoo made by a chisel. One giant has a whole face in small holes. What do these differences mean? But all the statues have one feature - their eyes are fixed on the sky.
