Mikhail Gavrilov: biography and personal life of the actor

Mikhail Gavrilov is known to a large number of viewers for the memorable, vivid roles played by him in the rating series "Youth" and "New Life". The personal relationships and acting career of this attractive young man are of interest to many fans of his talent.

Facts from the biography

The future actor was born on July 7, 1985 in Togliatti. The boy‚Äôs father was a military man, and after a few years the family moved to a new place of residence in the small town of Chusovoy, located in the Perm Territory. It was there that Mikhail Gavrilov spent his childhood and youth.

At school age, the boy did not dream about an acting career. He was interested in the field of sports, active games with friends in the yard, and he watched a movie only on TV.

After finishing nine grades of the school, Mikhail Gavrilov first decided to connect his life with medicine, and then became a student at the Polytechnic University in Perm. After some time, the young man was called up for military service.

The future actor served in the infantry. Mikhail was attracted to military service, he made plans to continue the work of his father and grandfather and become a military man. Having served the required time, Gavrilov remained in the army on a contract basis. The young man served for three years.

After demobilization, Mikhail unexpectedly decided for everyone to become an actor. He left for the capital and easily entered the Shchukin Theater Institute.

First steps in the cinema

Mikhail Gavrilov successfully graduated from this educational institution in 2012. He was trained in the workshop of Valentina Petrovna Nikolayenko, known to a huge number of moviegoers for her role in the comedy "Wedding in the Robin". She revealed great creative potential in Michael, which gave him the demand for the chosen profession in the near future.





The actor began his career in cinematography in 2009. He starred in a cameo role, appeared on the screens in one of the series of the popular film "Code of Honor." This experience helped the novice actor to feel all the subtleties of working in front of the camera.

The directors noticed the obvious talent of the young actor. Michael got a role in the exciting thriller "Someone Is Here", and then played in its continuation.

Career takeoff

In 2014, Mikhail Gavrilov (the filmography of the actor testifies to this) played a role in ten diverse films. The greatest popularity was brought to him by the role of the hockey player Evgeny Tsarev in the series "Youth".

The actor continued his sports theme in 2015, playing the star of the Soviet hockey Vladimir Konstantinov in the autobiographical film "Glory" dedicated to Vyacheslav Fetisov. Gavrilov demonstrated his creative abilities in the comedy film "Life is just beginning", the mystical film "Black River" and several other films. Mikhail Gavrilov (a photo from the film confirms this fact) talentedly played a role in the film "Thresholds", recently released on the screen.





In 2016, Mikhail starred in the TV series "Hotel Eleon", which was loved by the audience. In 2017, he appeared on the screens in the films "Personal Space", "Weeping Willow".

Personal life

The sought-after actor is in a happy marriage and has a son. With their future wife Anna Nosatova, they starred together in the series "Someone Is Here." They played lovers in it, but were in no hurry to actually become them.





Young people began to meet on the set of the second season of this series and, after its completion, entered into a legal marriage. In 2012, the son of Andrei appeared in the family. This momentous event strengthened the family union of the couple in love even more.

Mikhail Gavrilov is not only a good family man, but also a skilled host. This man can cope with any housework, loves to create various culinary masterpieces and wants to open a restaurant.
