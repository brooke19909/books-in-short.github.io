English Channel - the natural defense of England



But many understood that landing was a risky business, and the Sea Lion operation was rather adventurous, because England had a very good navy and centuries of experience in naval battles. Air parity

Operation Sea Lion was carried out in the summer of 1940. Actually, not the landing itself and the capture of the island state, but the bombing of England, which were supposed to clear the way for the ground forces. And then, for obvious reasons, they did not return to this idea.
