Interesting water park in Kaluga

For some reason, it is believed that water entertainment complexes are the property of either southern coastal cities or large megacities that can allocate space and money for the construction of such aquacenters. But, to refute this opinion, in the small town of Kaluga there is a wonderful water park under the unexpected name "Yolki".

Why such an interesting name? The fact is that the water park in Kaluga is part of a larger entertainment complex, which is called "Yolki". This is a restaurant, and a hotel, and a water park - three in one. For a small town located far from the sea beaches, such a complex has become a real find. This is a great way to spend time with the whole family on a day off.

The water park in Kaluga is an open complex. It is located directly in the open air and awaits its guests all the warm season. The first thing that catches your eye at the entrance to the water park - amazing cleanliness and a huge pool with sky blue water. Its size is 25x12 meters. Two water slides with the saying names "kamikaze" and "figure eight" are taken out into it, in which fans of extreme sports and spray will find what they lack in everyday life - their portion of fun and adrenaline.



For the youngest visitors, the water park in Kaluga will also be interesting - they have two children's pools and a small children's slide. And the children will have something to do while their parents ride the slides or in the pool - they have at their disposal a magical children's town, a folding bridge and a system of artificial waterfalls. There is no doubt that the kids will be delighted! And, of course, do not forget to grab a camera and a video camera to take pictures of your kids conquering the water park in Kaluga - photo and video shooting in the park are allowed and welcome.

Adults can also find entertainment to their taste. Not far from the children's pool there is a bathhouse in which dads and mothers, tired after a working week, can warm their bones, relax and have plenty of steam. In addition, for visitors who prefer outdoor activities, the water park in Kaluga provides a separate pool for playing water polo. Also, none of the visitors to this wonderful institution will remain hungry - an express summer cafe is constantly open on its territory , where you can eat and quench your thirst for any, whether large, small, or park client.



Polite staff will follow your things, provide inflatable vests for those who are not very confident in the water, and will monitor your safety and the safety of your children while you indulge in a well-deserved rest. Therefore, if you do not know where to go to relax - choose a water park in Kaluga to visit. Prices will pleasantly surprise you. For daytime sessions (from 12:00 to 15:00 and from 15:00 to 18:00) and for the evening session (from 18:00 to 21:00) the cost of an adult ticket is 360 rubles. For children, this amount is less than half.
