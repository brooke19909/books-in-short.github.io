Southern coast of Crimea, Yalta, boarding house "Malachite": description and reviews

Yalta is a large resort city, where thousands of tourists from different parts of Russia and neighboring countries come annually. It has a wonderful climate, tourists can enjoy the high temperature and plenty of sunny days throughout the resort period. There is also a huge amount of entertainment.

Almost every guest of the city immediately after arrival wants to stop for rest and overnight in some cozy place. Many vacationers follow to the private sector near the sea coast in order to rent a comfortable room for a certain time, the rest go to look for rooms local hotels. However, a large part of tourists prefer to arrange a planned vacation in boarding houses. One of the most popular boarding houses in Yalta is Malachite. It will be discussed further.



Location

Pension "Malachite" (Crimea / Yalta) is located not on the sea coast, but 700 meters from it. However, this fact does not bother his guests at all, because you can get to the nearest clean comfortable beach in just 10 minutes, and in a picturesque area. In the vicinity of the place where it is located, there is a park area where you can stroll on quiet evenings with your soulmate, friends or the whole family.

The place where the Malachite boarding house (Yalta) is located is very quiet, which benefits tourists who do not like noise and night discos, but just want to relax in the middle of beautiful nature, not far from the sea.

His address: Yalta, Shcherbak street, 15.



Rooms

In total there are 112 rooms in the territory of this recreation area that offer guests wonderful living conditions with all amenities. All apartments are located in two large five-story buildings, which were built during the Soviet Union and are still in excellent condition. All apartments are divided into three categories: "luxury", "junior suite" and "standard".

Regardless of the degree of comfort of living, in all apartments of the boarding house there is an individual bathroom and shower. A hairdryer, towels, bathrobes and slippers are also provided.

"Standard"

Standard rooms are offered to all guests who wish to relax together in the Malachite boarding house (Yalta). If necessary, a cot can be installed here, so that in case of a visit with a child, all necessary conditions for their stay will be provided in the room.

The furniture here has a large double bed, on which there are orthopedic mattresses. There are also two bedside tables, one large wardrobe for storing clothes and personal items. An extra bed can be a folding sofa, which is also available here. As for the other amenities, in the rooms of the standard type of the Malachite boarding house (Yalta) there is a large refrigerator, an electric kettle, a small set of dishes, as well as a TV with the possibility of watching satellite channels.



Junior Suite

Junior suites are divided into two rooms, one of which serves as a sleeping area, and the second as a living room. Guests can enjoy wonderful mountain views from the windows.

The bedroom has a large double bed and furniture, necessary for storing a small amount of things that tourists usually take with them on the road. Upholstered furniture is installed in the living area, which comes with an excellent folding sofa, two armchairs, a coffee table and a TV on the wall. As in the rooms of the "standard" category, the "junior suite" has a refrigerator, kettle and dishes, which are necessary for self-cooking.

In addition to standard bathrobes, slippers and towels, beach mats are offered here.

"Suite"

Luxury apartments offer guests superior comfort. Rooms of this type also consist of two spacious rooms, as well as loggias with beautiful views of the sea and the surrounding nature.

Each room has the necessary furniture set. The bedroom has a large double bed wardrobe with side tables. The living room is equipped in the same way as in the "junior suite". There are two large folding sofas on the loggia, on which guests can relax and sunbathe. There is also a table where you can eat in the fresh air.

The guests of the suites can use the telephone and air conditioning, with which you can create optimal temperature conditions in the room, regardless of the weather outside.



Nutrition

For a fee, guests can use the dining room located on the territory of the Malachite boarding house (Yalta). Here they can be offered delicious dishes of Russian and European cuisine, prepared by excellent chefs. In many reviews of the Malachite boarding house in Yalta, it is noted the taste of local dishes.

Tourists can also cook cold food on their own - absolutely all rooms have a small amount of dishes, a kettle, an electric stove and a refrigerator where you can store food.



Entertainment

On the territory of the boarding house "Malachite" in Yalta there are a lot of entertainments and places where you can spend your free time. In particular, sports fans positively assess the presence of a sports hall, where you can continue to work on your figure even during your holidays. There is also a small but well-equipped thermal zone, which includes a Russian bath and a sauna. Guests love to visit these places with entire companies and have fun in them. There is also a solarium.



Near the boarding house is a clean and well-maintained public beach, which can be visited at any time of the day. There are locker rooms, toilets and showers that tourists can use. Everyone can rent sun loungers and umbrellas that protect against direct sunlight.

On the coastline vacationers are also waiting for a lot of entertainment. In particular, here, for a fee, you can ride a hovercraft, rent a boat or a catamaran, or take a parachute into the air, which only extreme lovers will appreciate.

Prices

The cost of living here changes annually. In reviews of the Malachite boarding house (Yalta) in 2017, guests noted that the price for a night in a standard room is about 1000-1150 rubles, for a junior suite 1050-1250, and for a luxury 1250-1450 rubles per person. This, as they themselves note, is very decent prices for all the conditions that are provided in the apartment.

Separately, guests of the Malachite boarding house in Yalta are provided with a whole system of discounts. In particular, for children aged 5 to 11 years, the cost of living in a room is offered at a discount of 50%, and for those whose age is from 12 to 14 years - 30%. The same discount applies to the installation of an extra bed for an adult.

How to get there

It is best to get to the Malachite boarding house in Yalta from Simferopol. It is from here that trolleybuses, buses and minibuses go. In Yalta, a tourist needs to find the central bus station, transfer to bus number 11 on it and go to the Colonnade. From this stop you need to climb up, right along Shcherbak street - here is the recreation center.
