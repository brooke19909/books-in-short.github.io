Alanya, Cleopatra Beach - the best value for money

Cleopatra Beach Hotel in Alanya is located on the famous Cleopatra Beach, on the border of the city. The road to the airport takes about two hours (120 km). These places are rich in historical and architectural attractions that will be interesting to every tourist. The most important is the fortress of Alanya. Just 5 minutes on a local minibus (dolmush) - and you are already in the city center, full of shops, interesting shops and unusual places, which Turkey is famous for.

Alanya, Cleopatra Beach. Accommodation



The hotel was built in 1988 and renovated in 2003. It is possible to place in 116 standard rooms (two rooms without a door), 18 family rooms (hall and bedroom with a door). Each of the rooms is equipped with air conditioning, a bathroom with shower or bath, satellite TV, telephone with long-distance communication, a safe and a minibar are available for an additional fee.

Some rooms have stunning views of the sea, the windows of the rest overlook the hotel courtyard, mountains. Rooms are cleaned daily, and towels and bed linen are changed three times a week. 24-hour room service is available for a fee.
