Water park "Dzhubga"





The water entertainment complex "Dzhubga" is located in the village of the same name in the Tuapse district. Today it is considered one of the largest and most interesting water parks in the entire Krasnodar Territory. The thing is that it is here that the unique attractions built by the Turkish company are located. This is the slide for the breathtaking Ufil Flying Bords and Tsunami flights. And what about the Big Hall - a 112-meter descent? Offers a water park in Tuapse "Dzhubga" and traditional entertainment: four pools, "Lazy River", slides "Multislide", "Pigtail" and others.

Water park "Dzhubga": prices and address

The cost of visiting this entertainment complex ranges from 1000-1200 rubles for adults and 500-700 rubles for children. For children under 5 years of age, a separate ticket is not required. This water park in Tuapse is open daily from 10.00 to 0.00 with a technical break from 19.00 to 20.00. In addition to adult and teenage attractions, there is enough entertainment for the youngest guests, regularly conducted interactive programs with animators. The safety of the guests of the complex is provided by the rescue service, on the territory there are sun loungers for relaxation and a cafe where you can eat. The exact address of the complex: Tuapse district, the village of Dzhubga, Novorossiysk highway, building 10-a.

"Chernomor" - water activities in the village of Lermontovo



The third water park in Tuapse is considered the smallest and simplest. However, he will appreciate the affordable prices and the variety of entertainment on offer. The complex is called "Chernomor", located in the village of Lermontovo. Guests are invited to ride on their favorite slides: "Rafting", "Kamikaze", "Tobogany", swim in the pools. There is a separate plunge pool for the youngest visitors with a water area. In the evenings, foam discos and theme parties are held here.

Tips for those who decide to visit the Black Sea water park in Tuapse





The exact address of the entertainment complex: Tuapse district, Lermontovo village, Lenin street, building 18. The cost of visiting the water park is 550 rubles for adults and 450 rubles for children. A separate ticket for an evening disco usually costs 100 rubles for a child and 200-300 for an adult. On the territory there is everything necessary to spend all day here with comfort. This is a restaurant with cozy tables, changing rooms and showers, left-luggage offices. Rescuers are constantly monitoring compliance with safety rules, they also monitor compliance with age restrictions when visiting attractions. Very close to the complex is the Golden Beach - a comfortable recreation area on the coast, where you can go after visiting the water park.
