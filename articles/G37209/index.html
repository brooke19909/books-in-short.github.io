Vacuum cleaner cyclone filter, advantages and disadvantages

Who in your family is cleaning the apartment? You? Agree that when using high-quality modern technology, this process turns into a pleasant pastime. The cyclone filter integrated in the vacuum cleaner makes cleaning easy. He introduces his owner to the world of modern technology.





British engineer Dyson James invented the cyclone filter for a vacuum cleaner. At one time he was terribly unhappy that the bags in his vacuum cleaner were filling up too quickly, and the device ceased to function normally. He took as a basis a technology in which air, spinning conically in a collector, moves spirally, gradually increasing speed. The resulting centrifugal force throws particles of debris to the walls, which then, with a loss of speed, fall into the garbage container.
