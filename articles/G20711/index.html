Rosgosstrakh consists of a leading open joint stock company and includes several other companies with a narrow specialization, such as IC RGS Life, RGS-Medicine, and PF RGS.

Voluntary insurance

In addition to compulsory motor third-party liability insurance, voluntary CASCO insurance exists. It differs from compulsory motor third-party liability insurance in that it compensates Rosgosstrakh in case of an accident in case of damage to the car during an attack, accident, theft and other situations. For experienced drivers, there is a separate type of policy called ‚ÄúAnti-CASCO CASCO‚Äù. Its cost, in comparison with the usual, is less than half.

As a supplement to CTP, you can buy DSAGO or DGO, in which damage is compensated if it is caused through the fault of the car owner.





CTP: insurance payout

The insurance company pays money in the event of an insured event if:

  • the car of the accident participant who is innocent of what happened was damaged;
  • traffic lights or signs are damaged;
  • injured driver or passengers.

Compensation for damage to life and health can be up to five hundred rubles, and property - up to four hundred. Payment can be made both in cash and through repair work. In the latter case, if the damage to the vehicle is caused more than the company can pay, the culprit should be compensated for the missing amount.

An insurance company no later than 15 days after the incident must provide a passport, certificate, vehicle registration certificate, a certificate of an accident, a notice of an accident and a copy of the protocol.

What to do if the payment amount is underestimated

So, the documents have been submitted, and you expect to receive a payment for the implementation of repair work. But suddenly it turns out that the amount received on the account is clearly underestimated and will not cover all expenses when the vehicle is brought back to normal. What could be your actions?

Someone will take what they offer and stop there. And others will seek a fair payment. If you have chosen the second path for yourself, then you will have to order an independent examination so that you can prove the understated amount in the future, suing Rosgosstrakh. The payment amount is disputed by the following preliminary actions:

  • filing an application at the office where the policy was issued;
  • hotline call;
  • appeal to the leadership.

It happens that such actions are enough to review the payment. However, if the company still refuses to increase the amount, you can file a complaint with the PCA, then contact the legal advice and, after the actions recommended there, file a claim.

Peer review

Almost all consultations will tell you that it is necessary to order an independent examination. This service is popular with car owners. After all, appraisers of insurers, as a rule, are not so independent. For example, they may not notice hidden defects that will be detected only during repair. And then this breakdown will have to be paid from the pocket of the injured party.

Independent examination, ordered independently, must be paid. But, if the results of the examination do not coincide with that carried out by the insurers, and you will challenge it in court, the statement of claim must indicate that the costs of the independent examination by the insurer are reimbursed.

Moreover, if the amount of CASCO payments is disputed, experienced car lawyers include a claim for non-pecuniary damage, which in the case of voluntary insurance is often satisfied by the court, and insurance companies do not mind.

Independent Expertise





So, an independent examination is carried out as follows. A participant in an accident orders a service and pays for it, receiving a payment document (on the basis of which expenses will be charged in court). It is advisable to describe the nuances that seem to him controversial in assessing the insurance company. For example, no latent defects were detected.

If necessary, independent appraisers agree on the place and time of the inspection with third parties. Upon examination, all vehicle damage resulting from an accident is carefully recorded by photographing and a detailed description. Next, accurate calculations of the necessary spare parts and restoration work are carried out. Valid amounts from authorized dealers are taken into account if the car is serviced by him, or average market. The wear factor is also important. Information on prices is taken from large online portals or stores in the region where the accident occurred. An expert opinion is being prepared . Often in such organizations they offer further legal support, that is, the conduct of business in court.
