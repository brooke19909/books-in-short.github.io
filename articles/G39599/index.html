Wireless parking sensors with rear view camera. Characteristics, installation, cost

It has become quite difficult to drive a car in the conditions of modern big cities - the number of cars on the roads is increasing every day, less and less free space. Many car manufacturers install parking sensors, but in most cases, still this device will have to be purchased separately. Let's figure out how to choose the right model.

Wireless devices

The modern market offers many devices. The most popular product is a wireless parking sensors with a rearview camera. It is often acquired by novice drivers who still do not know how to confidently move on the road in the stream.

Wireless devices are good in the complete absence of any wires, and many people like simple installation or repair.

Like appliances with wires, wireless systems are designed to make parking as easy as possible. The system will warn the driver in advance of an obstacle from behind. In some models, this warning is implemented in different ways. On some devices there is a display for this purpose, on cheaper and more affordable models the warning is implemented by means of a simple sound signal. On some models, the signal is sent by voice. So, speaking parking sensors in the purest Russian language instead of a beeping signal will tell the driver how many meters to the obstacle, and will warn when to stop.

Among the advantages that wireless devices have, they highlight the ease of installation, the absence of problems with the pulling of wires, the comfort of use, the small space that the device occupies.

The system works thanks to the control unit. It processes radio signals and can display. The signals from different devices are different, but do not be afraid - they do not interfere with each other.

The display is connected to the on-board network, and you can install it in absolutely anywhere in the car. Such an inexpensive parking sensors is suitable for many brands of cars. These models can also be used on trucks.

Differences between parking sensors

Wireless devices differ from each other in the same way as wired devices. These differences are in the number of sensors, the type of signal, the quality, as well as the accuracy of the sensors.

Choosing a universal parking sensors, you should pay attention to three things. This is the sensitivity of the device to obstacles, protection against radio interference, as well as a comfortable combination of sound and graphic indicators. The last point is especially important - the signals should not irritate the driver.

Also, the devices differ in voltage and power supply, range, volume.

Parktronic with camera



Wireless parking sensors with a rearview camera is considered one of the most convenient thanks to the display. This is the most effective way to alert the driver of obstacles. The camera can be fixed independently in any place from where the widest and most convenient for the driver view will open.

However, you should not rely only on the display and camera. For maximum safety, it is recommended to use all means. With the camera you can see everything in the way, because in inexpensive systems without it, the sensors do not respond to small animals. For example, dogs or cats. It also allows you to view the space without dead zones, and it is more resistant to weather.

Such a wireless parking sensors with rear-view camera has proven itself when working with heavy vehicles. If the sensors do not react correctly somewhere, then the obstacle can be seen on time in the display.

Manufacturers are ready to offer drivers a lot of devices.

It is important to choose a device with high sensitivity, easy installation and easy connection.

How to choose parking sensors?

When choosing a suitable device, it is necessary to carefully examine the characteristics of a particular device. It is best to purchase a model that has a fairly high sensitivity. Often, low-cost parking sensors have poor sensitivity - it is not suitable for a beginner.

A good device has a very high sensitivity. He can fix any obstacles. It can be elements of the urban landscape, animals, people, other cars. Also, all these obstacles can be seen on the display if the device is equipped with a camera. It doesn‚Äôt matter how much parking sensors cost if traffic becomes safe with it.

It is also very important to get acquainted with how and how qualitatively the device is protected from interference caused by power lines, mobile phones, as well as other sources that generate electromagnetic waves. Ideally, if the parking sensors are protected so that it is not affected by any sources of interference.

Wireless parking sensors with a rear-view camera have a default display - on this display, in addition to the picture from the camera, there will also be other information about obstacles. Often, this information is displayed through colors.

For example, at a safe distance from an obstacle, this will be highlighted in green on the display.

When choosing a suitable device, do not choose what is cheaper and manufactured by unknown companies. It often happens that parking sensors for cars, the price of which is too affordable, do not differ in a sufficient level of sensitivity. Inexpensive devices are also unreliable. Buying such devices is simply dangerous.

Also an important nuance is the colors of the design. You should not choose a device that will stand out from the general style and colors of the interior of the car. The same can be said about outdoor sensors. Color is an important component.

Installation of parking sensors with a camera

How well the parking sensors will sense obstacles depends on the correct installation. It is recommended to install and configure this equipment at the service station. However, if you need to do the installation yourself, this is also possible.

First of all, the bumper is removed, and then holes are drilled on it with a complete cutter.

The sensors are installed in the center, and the extreme ones are mounted at a distance of 9 to 12 cm from the corner. The remaining sensors can be installed evenly along the entire line. Next is the drilling phase. With most devices, a special cutter comes with the kit - with its help they create a hole. Then the sensors are inserted.

Connection

This is the hardest part. Even if the sensors are wireless, the camera will still have to be connected.

This wire is passed through the roof in the existing wiring unit and displayed in the cabin. Here you need to work extremely carefully. Next, the wire is connected to the display.
