How to disable the service "My Beeline": instructions

Today we have to figure out how to disable the "My Beeline" service. In addition, it would be nice to understand what kind of option it is and how to enable it. Maybe it will be completely useless. There are many options that allow Beeline subscribers to turn on or off certain options on their own. And it is their study that we have to do. There is nothing complicated about it. The main thing is to follow the instructions.



Description

My Beeline service is an option that allows you to make profitable calls within your home region and in Russia. The first 100 minutes a day, according to this tariff, are free. But then you have to pay. 2 rubles per minute. True, this rule applies only to Beeline numbers. These conditions exist for daily payment of services. The subscription fee in this case will be 5 rubles.

"My Beeline" also has a more interesting option. If you use a monthly subscription, then in this case the first 3000 minutes per month are free, and then you will be charged according to the tariff. In this case, an additional 210 rubles will have to be paid in 30 days. It is such a fee that is set as monthly.

Connect

Deactivation of the service "My Beeline" is just around the corner. But first you need to connect this function. How can I connect?

For example, through a set of special combinations. The first is 3000. We dial, press the call button and wait for an answer. In this case, My Beeline will be connected with daily payment for services. Not the best option you can imagine. If you want to pay a monthly fee, then dial 0674010333 on the phone and click on "Call". In this case, you will be able to connect your "My Beeline" package and pay it monthly.

We go to the store

You can disable the My Beeline service at any company office. Just in case, take your passport with you. And show up at the nearest Beeline store. Combinations

How to disable My Beeline service on your mobile phone? We already connected it using special combinations. Now it is time to use the appropriate numbers to reject the package. In our case, it does not matter what type of payment was selected - daily or monthly. For everything, there is one shutdown function. And it looks like 3014. Dial these numbers on the phone and click on the "Call" button.



Processing of the request will begin. If you did everything correctly, you will receive a message in response. It will contain information about the successful operation. In the event of a malfunction, the system will notify you of the crash and offer to resume the attempt to refuse the My Beeline package a bit later. True, all this happens extremely rarely.

Website

The official page of the mobile operator can also be extremely useful. You can disable the "My Beeline" service through the "My Account" on the company's website. How to implement the idea?

To get started, visit the Beeline website. Pass there authorization in the system. If you previously did not have an account, you will have to register using your mobile phone. The process does not take much time. Caught in your account?

Now you have to click on the "Services" section. Here you will see all the connected options on the SIM card. And affordable, by the way, too. Find "My Beeline" in the list. Opposite this inscription (on the right side of the line) you will see "Disable".



If you click on this button, the request will be processed. On the phone you will receive an SMS with a confirmation code of the operation. How to disable My Beeline service? Enter the verification code in the appropriate line on the screen and click "OK." It is done. Now you have refused the package "My Beeline." If desired, you can connect the option again in the same way. As you can see, there are many options for solving the task before us. And each subscriber can choose how to connect / disconnect certain options on the Beeline SIM card.
