Hotel Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 *, Greece: overview and reviews

Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * is an integral part of a large complex of places for recreation - Dionysos Hotel Apartments. Most of the tour operators advise tourists who wish to relax on the Kassandra peninsula to settle here, because guests, having come here, will certainly be surrounded by attention, care and proper conditions. On top of that, there are very nice prices for accommodation and entertainment.



Location

Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * is located on Halkidiki - a small peninsula that bears another beautiful name - Kassandra. Not far from this place is the center of Chanoti, where there is a huge amount of entertainment that will surely appeal to the younger generation. The nearest international airport is located in Thessaloniki, 90 km from the hotel.

Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * (Chanioti) is located in a place where there is a mild and very warm climate. In the summer, tourists resting here can observe the maximum number of sunny days. Moreover, thanks to the sea air, they do not languish from the heat.

Rooms

Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * (Kassandra / Chanioti) offers guests a room fund consisting of apartments of three categories: standard, deluxe and grand suite. In each of them a fresh repair was made, all the furniture that is in them is modern and comfortable. Guests can enjoy free internet access, as well as a small amount of electronics. In the rooms, regardless of the degree of comfort, individual bathrooms and showers are installed.



Standard

Standard apartments are designed to accommodate from 2 to 3 people. On their area equal to 20 square meters. m, there is a set of furniture necessary for a comfortable stay. Often guests who come here to rest with small children come here.

There are beds with orthopedic mattresses as sleeping places, and there is also a folding sofa. Guests can neatly put their things in a very spacious wardrobe, as well as in cabinets. Rooms of this class have a well-equipped kitchenette where guests can cook for themselves. As for electronics, the standard apartments have a refrigerator, a plasma TV, and a kettle.

Each room has an individual air conditioning, with which you can adjust the temperature in the room, regardless of weather conditions. From here, guests have access to a furnished balcony, which offers magnificent views of the surroundings surrounding the Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * (Chanioti).



Deluxe

Rooms of this type offer guests a comfortable environment, located on 25 square meters. There is also a private furnished balcony with views of the flowering Greek gardens. As in standard apartments, there is a kitchen equipped with furniture and electronics and a TV with satellite channels. Guests have the opportunity to use the safe, telephone and minibar, which is daily filled with fresh cool drinks at an additional cost. The provided sleeping places can accommodate up to three people.

Conditions for children

Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * positions itself as a place ideal for family holidays. That is why a small number of conditions have been created here that contribute to the comfortable living of small guests. For them, there is a small outdoor pool filled with crystal clear water, which was created in compliance with all safety requirements. They can also be entertained at the playground, which is also in the open air and contains on its territory everything necessary for children's outdoor activities. If necessary, an extra cot can be provided in any room upon request.

Pool

In the open area at Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * (Kassandra, Halkidiki) there is a large pool in which fresh water is regularly updated. Guests can have fun and then relax on the sun terrace nearby. For the comfort of guests, sun loungers and beach umbrellas are installed on it. There is also a bar where you can enjoy soft drinks or enjoy a delicious dessert.

Beach

The Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * (Kassandra-Halkidiki / Chanyoti) has access to the public beach, which is literally a five-minute walk from the main building - 150 meters away. It is always clean, from its territory a beautiful view of the surrounding nature. Guests can rent sun loungers, umbrellas, towels and air mattresses at a special location. They can also use free public locker rooms, toilets and showers.

As for entertainment, the entrepreneurial inhabitants of the peninsula organized a set of services and offers on the local beach that vacationers can use for a fee. In particular, you can ride on air cushions, water skiing, rent a catamaran, boat or yacht.

Nutrition

Guests of the Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * have the opportunity to eat both independently and in the cafe that are on its territory, which serves light snacks, desserts, as well as various drinks. There is also an excellent dining room where tourists can be offered European and traditional Greek dishes. As guests themselves note in their reviews of Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 *, real culinary masters work here who can surprise even the most demanding gourmets.



For business

Often guests rest at the hotel, whose field of activity is related to doing business. Such people very often need to organize business meetings, presentations and negotiations right during the holidays. For such events, the hotel has a small conference room, which is furnished with comfortable seating areas. There is also modern sound and lighting equipment that will make the presentation brighter, as well as projectors and a laptop. In addition, guests can use the office supplies available here.

Services

Each guest who is relaxing at the Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki-Kassandra) is guaranteed a number of free services in the form of Internet access (Wi-Fi), currency exchange, advice from the administration at the reception, etc. However, There are several options that can be used for a fee. In particular, guests can take their belongings to dry cleaning and laundry services, use an individual left-luggage office, room service, as well as rent a car or bicycle.

When entering the Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 *, you should also remember that credit cards are not accepted on its territory, so you should get cash in advance.

Prices

The cost of a tourist ticket to the hotel directly depends on which operator it was purchased from and from the airport of which city will be flown by plane. On average, statistics show that, subject to a weekly stay of two in a standard apartment, the price of a vacation will be about 65 thousand rubles. Operators include a round-trip flight fee, airport transfer, and room and board (buffet) in advance.



What is near the hotel

Dionysos Inn Hotel is located almost in the very center of entertainment that does not subside throughout the night. That is why a vacation at this hotel is ideal for people who prefer an active nightlife. Literally 200 meters from the territory of this place for recreation, there is an entertainment quarter where you can find a huge number of cafes, bars, restaurants, night clubs, as well as attractions that will be glad to visit not only adults but also children.

Guest reviews

Almost all tourists after their stay at the hotel (and some even in the process) immediately share their impressions on their pages on social networks, as well as on thematic sites. Often they attach whole photo reports to their reviews.

Hotel guests in their comments note that, despite the category of "three stars", a wide range of comfortable conditions is offered here. Many tourists are delighted with the interior that is available in the apartments, and they are also pleased with the availability of individual cooking facilities in each room - this is a really good solution, which is very rare in recreation centers of this type. Guests who do not want to cook their own meals share their impressions of meals ordered in the local dining room. Most comments say that it is prepared in the best possible way.

As for accommodation prices, the opinions of many travelers differ. Basically, everyone is satisfied with the local rates, but there are also tourists who consider them to be slightly overpriced - for almost 70 thousand rubles you can buy a tour to a five-star hotel in Turkey.

However, in spite of everything, vacationers of the Dionysos Inn Hotel 3 * (Kassandra, Chalkidiki / Chanioti) in most cases say that a vacation spent here is quite memorable by an abundance of colorful moments, and many plan to return here more than once. They also recommend that their relatives, acquaintances and subscribers go here on social networking pages, confirming their recommendations with vivid photographs taken against the sea and blooming gardens.
