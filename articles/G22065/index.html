Necessary materials

To tie a trendy warm hat, you should prepare:

  • knitting needles;
  • yarn;
  • scissors;
  • markers or pins;
  • the needle.


Since the hat is designed for cold, you should choose yarn, which includes wool. The number of knitting needles is selected taking into account the thickness of the yarn.

Loop calculation

We knit a sample of 10 x 10 cm. Mandatory! Never skip this step. Based on these data, you can calculate the required number of loops that need to be put on the knitting needles. Let's say that there are 18 loops in a 10 cm sample. We expect that in 1 cm of the sample there are 3 rows and 1.8 loops.

The estimated length of the cap is 20 cm, which means that you need to knit 20 * 3 = 60 rows. On a head circumference of 48-52 cm, you need to dial 50 * 1.8 = 90 loops.

Ways to Knit Hats

There are 2 ways to knit cats with knitting needles with a description and pattern. The knitting method directly depends on the selected knitting needles: circular or stocking.

If you are going to knit a hat on circular knitting needles, you should dial 90 loops and knit in a circle according to the pattern of 60 rows.

If you have stocking knitting needles, then knit should be in rotary rows. Initially, 92 loops (2 hem) should be typed on the knitting needles.

Decrease

Knitting the main fabric is easy. After knitting 60 rows, you should begin to decrease. If you knit a hat on circular knitting needles, all the loops should be divided into 4 parts. Each spoke will have 22 stitches. Mark the border with a marker.

61 row. We knit the first 2 loops together front for the front wall. The resulting loop is tightened well. We knit the next 17 stitches. We knit the remaining 3 loops in this way: remove the first without knitting, and the second - front for the front wall. Now, through the elongated face we transfer the untethered. We knit the last face.

In the same way, we knit the remaining 3 parts to the end of the row. As a result, we get 4 wedges with decreases at the end and at the beginning of each.

Decreases are performed through a series according to the same principle. As you can see, knitting an adult cat hat with knitting needles with a description and pattern is easy.

When 12 loops remain on the knitting needles, the working thread needs to be cut. We pull it through the loop with a hook and pull it together. We bring the thread to the wrong side and fix it.





Ear tearing

Ears are the most important in this model. From the crown we count 12 loops in different directions. In this place there will be the first loop of the future abalone. We visually determine the location of the future part so that you do not look like a cheburashka or a hare.

Depending on the yarn, we collect pores in a chain of 14-20 loops. From the vertical chain, we crochet 10 loops on 1 knitting needle and 10 loops on the second. Turn the knitting over and repeat the same procedure on the other hand, gaining loops of the second side of the eyelet from the same chain. Thus, on the one hand, we scored 40 loops.

We knit all the loops in a circle, from the second row making reductions in each row from 2 sides. When 2 loops remain on the knitting needles, a working thread is pulled through them and pulled together. They hide it inside the abalone.

In the same way, the second ear is tied out. Make sure that it is located at the same distance from the crown as the first.
