Hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * (India, Goa): description and reviews of tourists

India is a magical country that attracts travelers from all over the world. Many tourists choose Goa as their vacation destination, where you can enjoy the warm climate and the unforgettable beauty of the Arabian Sea. It is here that the small hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * is located. So what are the living conditions here? How close is the beach? What service can you count on? Answers to these questions will be useful to many travelers.

Description of the hotel and location





Hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * is located in the southern part of Goa, namely in the Cavelossim area. Dabolim International Airport is 37 km away, while Margao Airport is about 42 kilometers away. By the way, Margao is the second largest city in Goa. The distance to its center is 22 km.

This place is famous for its truly homely comfort, relaxed atmosphere and sincere hospitality.

Hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 *: photo and description of rooms





The hotel offers its guests check-in in one of the 16 rooms. These are spacious standard rooms with an interesting design, which is a successful mixture of Indian and European styles. Some rooms have a private balcony.

Naturally, there is a necessary set of furniture, including comfortable beds, bedside tables, wardrobes and chairs. In order to create optimal temperature conditions in the room, guests can use air conditioning. And next to the bed there is a small fan. For a small fee, you can rent a safe for storing personal items. The room has a TV on which you can watch some Russian channels.

The apartments have a private bathroom with toilet, shower, washbasin and mirror. Towels are changed every day. Rooms are cleaned daily or at the request of guests.

Power scheme





Paying for accommodation at the Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * hotel, tourists can choose a convenient meal plan for them, starting from breakfast and ending with a full three meals a day. But do not worry, because regardless of the selected package of services, you can always eat at a local restaurant for a very reasonable fee. There is also a cozy bar with a large selection of drinks. Also in the city near the hotel there are many cafes, snack bars and restaurants.

Where is the beach located?

The hotel complex Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * is located at some distance from the sea shore. The nearest Mabor Beach is about 800 meters away. By the way, a direct road leads here, so it will be difficult to get lost.





The sea is perfect for windsurfing. Naturally, you can go water skiing, boat, boat, banana and even go fishing. Umbrellas and sun loungers are located on the shore, but you need to pay for their use separately. Tourists respond well to this beach, as it is always clean and not too crowded.

Additional services and entertainment

Despite the fact that the Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * hotel is very small, they tried to create the necessary conditions for guests to stay. The hotel has a small parking lot, and in the city you can easily rent a car or any other type of transport. A currency exchange point is constantly working. For a small fee, you can use the local laundry facilities. In case of health problems, a good doctor will be called to you.

There is wireless internet connection in the lobby of the hotel. The hotel does not have its own pool. However, guests are free to use the pool of the neighboring Mobor Hotel, which is a minute's walk away.

Of course, you will have the opportunity to explore the city with all its attractions, markets and shopping centers on your own. The hotel will even offer to make a route for travel, and if necessary, call a taxi. You can also use the services of any of the city tour desk.

This hotel is primarily intended for a relaxing budget holiday by the sea, and many tourists like it.

What do tourists say about the hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * (Goa)?





Having got acquainted with the opinions of tourists who have already managed to visit a particular place, you can get a lot of useful information. So what do travelers say about the Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * hotel? Reviews are for the most part positive.

The hotel area is very small, but cozy and green. The rooms are also clean and comfortable. Despite the fact that bedding and towels are not new, they are always clean and ironed. Electricity, cold and hot water are constantly present. The restaurant at the Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * hotel is also popular among guests of other hotels, as it is deliciously prepared here, and prices are quite affordable. The distance to the beach is not that big. All employees are polite and helpful, some even understand Russian.

The hotel is quite small, so its distinguishing feature is hospitality and real home comfort. Of course, here you can find a lot of shortcomings, but you should understand that this is not a luxurious five-star resort complex. And the price of accommodation is very low, so the hotel is suitable for tourists who are counting on an economical vacation, but want to enjoy all the beauties of the sea and the picturesque Goa.
