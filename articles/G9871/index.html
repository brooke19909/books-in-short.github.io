How to make a volcano at home for your baby?

This article provides a simple and affordable step-by-step guide on how to make a volcano at home. In the process of its development, the child asks more and more questions. It is best to give an answer to them, confirmed by a vivid example. And one of them will be given as part of this article.





Ingredients

To solve the question of how to make a volcano from plasticine at home, the following components and materials are needed:

  • Plastic containers (you can take any bottle with a capacity of 0.5 to 2 liters).
  • Plasticine to create a layout (depending on its size, you may even need several packages).
  • Food foil.
  • Two glass cones for mixing the ingredients (in extreme cases, you can use cups).
  • Dishwashing liquid.
  • Baking soda.
  • The paint is red.
  • Acetic acid.
  • Sand.
  • Medical disposable syringe.
  • Scissors.
  • A spoon.

We prepare all this in advance, so that, in the course of the experiment, there will not be any different hitches. Most of the list is every hostess at home. What is missing - buy.

Making a layout

The most difficult step in how to make a volcano at home is creating a layout. Here you need to include the imagination and properly formalize it. As a basis we use a plastic bottle. It should be in the center, and symbolic lava will erupt from it.

Next, plasticine is layered on it so that a slide is obtained. The crater of the volcano is covered with food foil. At the same time, it is necessary to make a hole in it with the help of scissors in order to be able to mix the ingredients of the lava. At the final stage, the surface of the clay is covered with sand. If desired, for the naturalness of the layout, it can be painted with paint.





Mix and get the result

Now we will figure out how to make a volcanic eruption at home. To begin with, mix baking soda and paint in one of the cones. Then add dishwashing liquid to them . Mix the resulting solution in detail until a homogeneous mass is obtained. Pour acetic acid into the second cone. Now the solution from the first vessel using a spoon through a pre-prepared hole is poured into the volcano.

After that, everything is ready for the eruption. It is enough to fill the syringe with acetic acid and then quickly inject it into the nozzle of the volcano. In this case, a reaction will begin inside, as a result of which lava will flow out through the hole made. If desired, the experiment can be done several times. For this, it is enough to then add acetic acid again .

When thinking about how to make a volcano at home, do not forget that lava will flow from its vent. Therefore, it is recommended to arrange it in advance so that nothing goes bad (for example, on a pallet or in the bathroom). Another nuance is that the model from plasticine can be repeatedly used during such experiments. Summary

In the framework of this article, an algorithm was presented on how to make a volcano at home. Most of the materials used are available for every housewife, the rest can be bought. There is nothing complicated about this, so you can have a bright and informative lesson with your baby. If you wish, this experiment can be repeated already in the circle of his friends and use the same layout. The only things you need are ingredients for imitating lava, which should not be a problem.
