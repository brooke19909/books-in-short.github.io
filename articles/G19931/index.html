Original autumn image for a photo shoot





The art of photography in modern society has become so popular that the concept of "photo session" is firmly entrenched in our everyday life. The time of boring identical shots is irrevocably gone. To compose an original thematic, holiday or family album, photo sessions are increasingly being organized. One of the first places in these processes is taken by photographs taken in different seasons. Not everyone can create a unique spring, winter or autumn look for a photo shoot. It is necessary to think over the exposure, background, surroundings and other important trifles.

Important accessories for autumn shots

Traditional items that make up the seasonal photo composition are selected in accordance with the symbols of a particular time of the year. So, pumpkins, apples, wicker baskets, soft blankets, etc. are chosen for autumn-themed photographs. The main colors are yellow, orange, ocher, terracotta. However, many photo albums are full of similar details and shades. If you want to create an exclusive album, we advise you to carefully select ideas for photo shoots. In autumn it can be ikebana from herbs and dried flowers, gray, white or pink shades in combination with classic autumn ones, caskets, vases reminiscent of a rustic style. It is not necessary to conduct photo shoots in nature, you can decorate any room or porch accordingly.

Umbrellas are an integral part of autumn

Autumn image for a photo shoot is often associated with umbrellas. How to choose a suitable umbrella for original pictures? It can be a bright or unusual instance. But in this case, all attention will be focused on him, and not on the topic of the image. Photos in rainy weather with such an accessory will look great.

Portraits with colorful umbrellas and tense smiles today will not surprise anyone; black-and-white photographs with colored elements look much more successful. On a monochrome background, the silhouette of the model and umbrella can be highlighted in saturated color. Wet from rain sidewalks, hanging drops on leaves - all these are inimitable details of a photo shoot.





Family photos

A wonderful memorable photo album will turn out if you organize a photo shoot for the whole family. A walk in the park or a picnic in nature is a great occasion for photographing. Touching pictures are taken when grandparents play in the leaves with their grandchildren, pick mushrooms or make wreaths of autumn leaves and flowers. The classic checkered plaid on the grass with food in wicker baskets will create a cozy family autumn look for a photo shoot. It can be complemented with rattan furniture for picnics. For frolicing children on such walks, it is advisable to pick up no more than two bright accessories in clothes. Let it be rubber boots of unusual colors or a bright hat with gloves. So the child will stand out in the photo, and a small number of accessories will not distract attention from his sweet and funny smile. The most suitable images for the autumn photo shoot with the kids are shots in tall grass, with pumpkins, apples. There is nothing more charming than laughing crumbs among a rich harvest.

Autumn wedding

Wedding celebration in the fall will be an unforgettable moment in life, thanks to successful and unique pictures.

It is very simple to go beyond the usual framework of established stereotypes of wedding photos: it is worth considering a festive autumn look for a photo shoot. It is not necessary to choose bright colors, monuments or ancient buildings.

It is enough to go to the city park, choose a successful exposure and pose - and wonderful exclusive photos are ready. In our understanding, the autumn season is associated with wisdom and maturity; therefore, wedding photographs taken during this period can be designed without trivial pomp and luxury. Simplicity and grace will emphasize the style and impeccable taste of a young couple who decided on the creative ideas of the autumn photo shoot. For example, a bride and groom kissing in the rain or in leaf fall. On yellow leaves you can photograph wedding rings. The white outfit of the bride will look profitable on a golden-crimson foliage background.

Carpet of Autumn Leaves

Despite numerous ideas for photo shoots in the fall, fallen foliage remains one of the significant backgrounds of the pictures. If you decide to be photographed on such a carpet, think over your makeup and outfit. You should not choose their colors in the autumn palette, it is better to choose contrasting shades. Makeup needs to be made more vivid than everyday, it is important to highlight either the eyes or the lips.





The pose is also of great importance. The unnatural position of the body can ruin the whole picture, and charming images for the autumn photo shoot can easily turn into vulgar ones. A carelessly spread light scarf or stole will give the impression of airiness and give the pictures a natural look. You can lay out a sculpture of stones and shower it with leaves. Such installations are a great backdrop for photographs.
