Sverdlovsk district of Irkutsk - microdistricts and their infrastructure

The largest district of Irkutsk is Sverdlovsk. The district was formed in 1944. However, in 1953, the territorial unit was abolished, and in June 1954 they were re-formed. As of the beginning of 2017, 205 thousand people live in the district.

Previously, the Sverdlovsk district of Irkutsk was the village of Glazkovo. The district also included several settlements: the village of Kuzmikha, the village of Glazkovo, Melnikovo and Sinyushina Gora.

The first inhabitants in this area are mentioned by N. Sappharius from 1675. And at the end of the 18th century, Mogilev Kuzma founded a village here. Then Glazkov Efim appeared in the settlement, in all likelihood, the village was named in his honor. Both names are by far the most common in the region.





Today, there are many industrial enterprises, educational and scientific institutions in the territory. The Sverdlovsk district of the city of Irkutsk includes 15 microdistricts.

Akademgorodok

The village is actually located in the center of the left-bank part of the city on the banks of the Angara River. Nearby is the Irkutsk National Research Technical University and the corps of some faculties.

The main street of the microdistrict is the only connecting highway with other parts of the district. A huge number of researchers, candidates of sciences and doctors live here. Graduate students here are about 30%.

There is a museum on the territory of Akademgorodok, the main purpose of which is to popularize science among the masses. In the museum you can observe how the experiments are conducted, and in some even participate.

The microdistrict is famous for research institutes and other research institutions:

  • Institute for the Study of the Earth's Crust;
  • Institute of Geochemistry and Geology;
  • Institute for the Study of Solar-Terrestrial Physics and several others.

Akademgorodok is famous for the Arboretum and a national installation - a solar radio telescope.

Despite the intelligent composition of the local population, it was here that several armed attacks occurred, which ended in the deaths of several people. Anufriev A. and Lytkin N. were convicted of crimes, about which there was a lot of information in the local media.





May Day

One of the youngest neighborhoods in the city. The development project for this part of the Sverdlovsk district of Irkutsk by local architects was carried out from 1970 to 1990. At the same time, development was ongoing, so the buildings have different space-planning solutions. At the same time, it is a compact microdistrict with developed infrastructure and a well-established public transport network.

Sinyushin Mountain

One of the largest microdistricts of the Sverdlovsk region of Irkutsk on the left bank of the Angara River. The unofficial name of the village is Sinyushka.

The M55 and AN6 routes run here, so the roads are always overloaded. The microdistrict is the largest railway cargo and passenger station Kaya, several large industrial enterprises, shopping centers and cinemas.





University

The microdistrict project was developed in 1987, it was planned as part of the May Day settlement, therefore, it has a common architectural concept with it.

Due to geographical features (complex terrain with a natural slope of 28%), the development was carried out in a terrace way, with many props, stairs and fortifications. Therefore, access to cars is difficult for many houses. There are five- and nine-story buildings in the microdistrict.

Anniversary

The development of the microdistrict was carried out from 1960 to 1990. The project involved the union of several villages for the residence of hydroelectric workers. On the territory there is a hospital for WWII veterans. There are nine-story houses erected without a special concept. On the outskirts of the microdistrict, holiday villages and forests.

District Markets

There are several markets in the Sverdlovsk district of Irkutsk:

TSK "Sverdlovsky", Tereshkova street, 24

With a 100-year history, the main focus is livestock and agriculture products. Only here in Irkutsk you can buy live pets (chickens, goats and piglets). There are rows with manufactured goods, books, spare parts.

"Southern", Academic, 27

Opened only in 2000, the main direction of trade is food.

Wholesale, Radio Plant District

Exists since 1980.

Wholesale, Birch Grove, 64

The main direction is food and groceries, Chinese things.

Shopping Center "Ruchey", 32 Ryabikova Blvd

The format is similar to the Central Bazaar, but smaller in size.

Industrial facilities and new buildings

One of the largest enterprises of the city of Irkutsk in the Sverdlovsk region is located on Sukhbaatar Street 3. The power output is 660 thousand kW. The object was built since 1950 for 8 years. The dam of the hydroelectric power station unites the Sverdlovsk and October districts.





In the Sverdlovsk district of Irkutsk, 67 Ryabikova Street, Novo-Irkutsk TPP is located, providing heat to the whole city, it covers the electrical loads of the power system.

The second largest cold storage plant in the Urals and the Far East is located in the Sverdlovsk district of the city of Irkutsk. The company was built in two stages, in 1954 and 1959. In 1961, the third phase of construction was launched. The oil and fat factory has also been operating since 1951.
