Apple iPod Touch 5 review: features, design and content

Apple iPod Touch 5 (specifications will be discussed in the article) - a new fifth-generation laptop computer with its inherent updates and added features. This model is ideal for both young men and women who prefer to listen to music and be able to shoot videos in high quality, as well as for respectable adult people who pay a lot of attention to maintaining a certain status.

Many people want to buy an iPhone, but its pricing is much higher than average, so such models are not available to everyone. An alternative to it is iPod Touch 5. The characteristics that this model is equipped with are practically not inferior to expensive options. A high-performance platform, a 4-inch large screen, a design that is fully consistent with fashion trends - all this can be found in the updated gadget.

So, let's get started with the new Apple iPod Touch 5.





Packaging and basic equipment

We begin our familiarization with the packaging. Take a close look at what the manufacturer offers in the standard kit. The iPod Touch 5 player is packed (you can read the specifications right away in the instructions) in a stylish and compact container that imitates the outline of the iPod, which adds intrigue. The basic equipment includes:

  • iPod
  • operation and setup instructions;
  • Apple branded stickers
  • Lightning connector (USB cable);
  • strap;
  • Ear pods headset (simplified model).

It is worth noting that, with the exception of a plastic capsule from under the player, the entire container consists of environmentally friendly cardboard, which is easily soluble in water.

Color

For the new Touch, more body colors are now available: black, silver, pink, yellow, blue. Red gadgets are also offered, part of the proceeds from which Apple gives to organizations that fight AIDS. With the exception of black devices, the front side of the remaining models is painted white.





Memory size

The company considered it inappropriate to produce models with a reserve of 8 GB, but devices with 32 GB and 64 GB memory are now available. The most popular models are iPod Touch 5 32GB. The characteristics of such a gadget can be considered quite acceptable for use without restrictions. This volume will be enough for numerous videos and favorite tracks.

Dimensions and convenience

Taking the iPod in your hand, you can see its lightness - weight 88 g. The volume keys are located on the left side, the lock button is on the top, the speaker holes, the connector for the Lightning cable, and the standard 3.5 mm input for the headset are visible. On the front side under the screen is the Home button. On the back of the gadget is Loop - a patented device for attaching the strap. You can bring it to the "open" position with the touch of a finger. After fixing the strap on the iPod Touch 5 (the characteristics of testing the fastener were carried out under different conditions), it closes easily. Note that, according to the manufacturer, the fixation is very durable. The strap withstands various power loads, does not break and does not break out over time in the device‚Äôs socket. Thus, having passed it on the wrist, you can safely play basketball, ski or perform other dynamic actions without fear of losing or dropping the device.



Advantages and functionality

Digital camera 5 p. with autofocus feature located on the back of iPod Touch 5. Features allow you to shoot video in Full HD. On the front side above the screen is a front camera with a shooting quality of 720 p. The new model does not have a light sensor, but the screen is enlarged to 4 inches (1136 x 640). The functionality of the screen remained as reliable. The number of icons on the desktop has been increased, the web browser has been updated, black bars have been removed when watching a video. Now it is displayed on the entire display, regardless of format.

We note that the front camera is inherited from the fifth iPhone, the main camera is from the iPhone 3, as the developers considered that these additions have already proved themselves only from the best side, and they should complement the wide range of possibilities in the new iPod.

Iron and platform

The processor for the device went from the iPhone 4S (2 cores, 800 MGz, 512 MBt RAM). It will provide speed and impeccable work. The software platform is iOS 6 (standard), but you can upgrade it yourself today to iOS 9. It is worth noting that all iOS components, with the exception of the phone‚Äôs functions, are available in the new gadget and are fully operational.

Price

From the start of sales to the present day, the cost of gadgets is fixed and is:

  • for Apple iPod touch 5 32GB (basic specifications) - $ 299;
  • for 64 GB of RAM - $ 399.

In rubles, the price may vary depending on the exchange rate.





To summarize

Despite retaining a number of standard features and capabilities, the iPod Touch 5 has become truly more convenient. Fresh design, improved software, increased memory and other features of the device will give more pleasure when using the device.
