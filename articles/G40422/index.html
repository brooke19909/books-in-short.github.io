3Q Tablet: Feature, Settings, Firmware

Once upon a time, 3Q occupied 3% of the Russian tablet market, saw prospects for further development, updating and expanding the range, and its goal was to produce budget devices that match the trends of the modern market. But something went wrong, and the production of digital technology ceased.

Historical reference

A quick take-off and a sharp drop - this is how the history of 3Q, which began in 2006 with the production of external optical drives and hard drives, can be described. And after a few years, their catalog was supplemented by nettops, laptops, all-in-ones, tablets, smartphones and various accessories. 3Q has partnered with companies such as Microsoft, Nvidia, Intel, Qualcomm and MediaTek. And in 2015, it announced its closure.



But the products remained, it can still be purchased. Therefore, we consider a few of the commercially available tablets of this company.

Large display, and even in high resolution. Such a combination in tablet computers from this company is very rare. First, it is worth noting that its cost is less than 10 thousand. But at the same time, the device has a fairly rich configuration, i.e. there are headphones with a microphone, a charger, a cable for receiving and transmitting data, an adapter for connecting peripheral devices, a manual and a microfiber cloth.



You cannot call a tablet light. With a ten-inch display and a centimeter-thick case, its weight is 600 grams. The back cover is made of aluminum. There is also a 5-megapixel camera lens, 2 speakers, a microphone, and the center logo of the Qoo! The display side without physical keys, only touch. Highlight when necessary. A positive impression is made by the assembly of the tablet: it does not creak and does not play.

Given the low cost of the device, the display quality of the Q-pad RC9727F is perplexing. Here, high resolution (2048 √ó 1536) and pixel density (263 ppi), large viewing angles and enough brightness for comfortable work in direct sunlight.

The tablet runs on a four-core Rockchip RK3188 processor, a Mail-400 MP4 GPU, and 2 gigabytes of RAM. The system itself is powerful, but it spends a lot of resources on the display, so you can not dream of launching powerful mobile applications. But the device supports 3G-connection, Bluetooth 3.0 and connects well to Wi-Fi points.

How to flash?

Updating (flashing) the main control program of a mobile device, as a rule, is carried out with the aim of increasing the functionality of the system, getting rid of glitches, brakes and freezes or gaining the ability to modify the system. Here is one of the options for how to flash a 3Q tablet using the Q-pad RC9727F as an example. Only you should know that after such actions all information from the tablet may disappear.

So, first you need to download and run on your computer the program RKBatchTool version 1.7 or higher, which is designed specifically for flashing devices based on the Rockchip system. Next, connect the tablet to the PC, then turn it off while holding the power button. Only it should not turn off, but reboot. And as soon as the screen goes blank, you must simultaneously press the volume and power buttons. When Windows detects the device, you need to immediately install the driver from the folder of the aforementioned program.



Now that the computer has identified the tablet as RK31 Device, and a green square has lit up in the program window, you can proceed with the update. We find the FW Path field and indicate the path to the update.img firmware file in it. Click ‚ÄúUpdate‚Äù and wait for the completion. At the end of the work, the tablet should reboot itself.

Firmware issues

This is not the only way to flush a 3Q tablet , there are many of them. And each of them can bring both positive and negative consequences. For example, along with the update file, you can download virus software or install drivers that will make the tablet work beyond its capabilities, which will lead to its rapid deterioration.

Some, after the firmware, generally do not turn on the 3Q tablet . What to do in this case? To avoid this, you need to install only verified update files, only the correct versions and only according to the instructions. Otherwise, you will have to spend money on repairs or find out how to disassemble the 3Q tablet in order to try to repair it. But this is unlikely to help.
