Hotel Description

Most of our compatriots, planning a vacation in Turkey, go to the Mediterranean Sea. Most often, travel agents offer Russians hotels in Alanya, from where you can easily go on almost any tour of the country. In this resort, hotel complexes are located on the first coastline, have their own territory, operate on an all-inclusive basis and mainly consist of multi-storey buildings.

The Mirador Resort Spa Hotel 4 * also falls under this description. Photos of the hotel complex in advertising catalogs impress with their beauty, which is why many tourists are interested in it. The hotel itself consists of two buildings with six floors. The first is considered the main, the second - additional. The Mirador Resort Spa Hotel was commissioned in 1991, but the last large-scale repairs were carried out about five years ago. Thanks to this, travelers will be greeted by a rather nice hotel with excellent rooms and a beautiful territory. Tourists do not have to complain about the unsightly interior and shabby furniture, almost all the buildings have been completely updated.

Location Mirador Resort Spa Hotel

Guest reviews suggest that it is located very conveniently. The hotel complex is located almost in the center of the village of Konakly, not far from shops and markets. Room features

Each room has individual air conditioning and cable TV. Guests have the opportunity to exit to the balcony or terrace, located in each room. The balconies have comfortable wicker chairs and tables where you can relax after dinner.

Depending on the category of the room, it will have a shower or a full bathroom. Every day, maids will change their toiletries during cleaning, so vacationers will not experience a lack of shampoos and lotions. Each bathroom has a hairdryer.

Each guest can use the minibar absolutely free, but the safe is paid separately, despite the fact that it is in all rooms.





Hotel stay with children

Guests can check into the hotel with children of any age, and the most comfortable conditions will be created for them. Please note that children under two years old can be accommodated in an extra bed absolutely free. Children under eleven years old who are accommodated in parental beds can also stay in the room at no extra charge.

Hotel staff can put one extra bed for a child in the room. Its cost is seventy percent of the price per night per person. At the end of the stay, the hotel complex employees issue an invoice, which must be paid before departure.

Extra beds must be addressed at the time of booking. The hotel must confirm your reservation with the placement of another person.
