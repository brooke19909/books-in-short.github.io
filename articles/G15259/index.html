HTC One mini phone: customer reviews and smartphone owners

HTC's mobile devices have been on the market for several years. If the first models seemed boring and perfect, then the latest news are more and more interested in fans of touch phones. HTC One mini was introduced in 2013 and immediately received positive reviews from critics - the smartphone is very stylish, multi-functional and powerful, so it is in demand among buyers around the world.





Why this particular smartphone?

To date, the HTC range is represented by fifty mobile devices running on Windows Mobile and Android operating systems. All devices are manufactured by the Taiwanese company High Tech Computer Corporation. Manufacturers try to meet the high demands of users, so every year they develop several new products that quickly find their owners.

The company's products are designed for modern people who prefer to use all the functions of a mobile device. If you want to take high-quality photos, listen to music, watch movies and chat on the Internet, the HTC One mini smartphone will be the best choice. Customer reviews confirm the high speed of the phone, so it can proudly be called one of the best-selling new products.

Model Overview

The smartphone is very lightweight, ergonomic and can fulfill any whim of its owner: in a flash it becomes an MP3 player, a camera and a GPRS navigator. A 4 megapixel camera allows you to take full-fledged pictures that with autofocus mode will seem clearer. Specially for shooting in the dark, a flash is built into the smartphone. Diagonal of 4.3 inches is designed for full viewing of videos and images. You will make the right choice by deciding to buy a HTC One mini phone. Reviews of fans of this smartphone confirm its excellent performance and long life.





The 1800 mAh battery can last for 21 hours. Built-in memory (16 gigabytes) allows you to store your favorite audio recordings, photos and books. Thanks to the processor running on two cores, you can simultaneously run multiple applications. The compact size and light weight will allow you to carry the phone in a small handbag or pocket. Color reproduction of 16 million colors is the ability to perceive a full-fledged image without distortion. Shades look as bright and saturated as in reality.

Less is not worse

The mini-version is much cheaper, but not inferior in functionality to its older brother. Manufacturers have maintained the same elegant design and thickness. Despite the large difference in display expansion, the wide viewing angle of both smartphones will allow you to get bright and clear images.





In terms of the number of cores, RAM and internal memory, HTC One is more delighted, but many buyers do not pay attention to these characteristics. Both devices are equipped with a 4 megapixel camera. The presence of additional functions allows you to take professional pictures, so if you are constantly looking for interesting photo effects, choose a smartphone HTC One mini. Feedback from fans of this mobile device confirms that the small screen size does not affect image quality.

How profitable to buy HTC One mini

In the first few months of its release, a 4.7-inch Taiwanese smartphone was unreasonably expensive. Time passed, and his elder brother was replaced by the new flagship phone HTC One mini. The reviews of the owners of the new product have intrigued potential buyers so much that many have lost the desire to acquire HTC from 4.7 inches. The mini version looks more sophisticated, the phone is less heavy and fits in a handbag. Excellent sound quality, an excellent camera, uninterrupted communication and low cost are the criteria by which customers increasingly choose a dual-core HTC One mini.

How much is the second version of the smartphone?

Since mid-summer 2014, the new HTC One mini has appeared on sale, which has a sophisticated design. A year earlier, the first mini-version of the smartphone was released, which did not differ in functions from the model with a large screen. The second novelty is available in two colors - dark gray and light gray. The phone case is almost completely made of aluminum, so the device may seem quite heavy. Traditional rounded corners reflect the overall concept of the HTC line. A small plastic insert running along the perimeter is almost invisible.





The main advantage of the metal case is the absence of fingerprints, but with active use, scratches can appear, which can be avoided by choosing a suitable cover. The control panel of HTC One mini 2 remained as before convenient. Despite the small dimensions compared to the previous version, the updated phone can not be called small: the diagonal of 4.3 inches allows you to fully use all the features. For the new product, customers will have to pay $ 840, but over time the price will become much lower.

HTC One mini: reviews of happy owners

After the release of the largest smartphone of the Taiwanese manufacturer, consumers eagerly began to wait for its smaller version. The novelty turned out to be a successful copy of HTC One, and, given the difference in cost between the two models, the mini version has become in great demand due to its compact size.





High-quality headphones included - a pleasant trifle for fans to listen to music on the go. Built-in 16 gigabytes is enough to store music, movies and photos. The processor quickly performs all the tasks, but for heavy games you should choose a smartphone with more power. The sound quality deserves the highest praise from the manufacturers of HTC One mini - the reviews of the owners confirm that the speaker does not rattle at maximum volume. Fans of the mobile device confidently declare that the mini version of One is the best smartphone that can compete with more expensive flagship models. Thanks to the convenient arrangement of buttons, users can quickly move around the screen and change the interface themselves. With the active use of all the functions of the smartphone, the battery may not be enough for a day, but the energy-saving mode, which is recommended to be activated at night, will save the battery for up to several days.

iPhone 5 and mini-series

In terms of efficiency, smaller models of Galaxy series smartphones cannot be compared with HTC One mini. Testimonials indicate that application performance remains even higher than stated. The technical specifications of the mini versions of the Samsung Galaxy S4 and HTC One differ in favor of the first smartphone. The amount of RAM, the presence of a slot for a flash card, battery capacity and weight are the parameters by which a Taiwanese smartphone loses significantly.





The Forbes publication claims that the HTC One mini phone is unable to compete with the iPhone due to poor performance, however, this factor does not stop buyers, and they continue to give preference to the Taiwanese model. Both smartphones are made of aluminum and look equally elegant, but the price of an iPhone is still high for some users. Apple's smartphone is significantly inferior in size and number of pixels. Choose your color!

With active use, even scratch-resistant glass can soon be damaged, so tidy users will be able to purchase several types of covers that will protect the case and emphasize the individuality of each owner. Today, HTC One mini is available in four colors: classic white and black will be an excellent choice for people who love solidity and presentability, and vibrant red and blue will appeal to active youth.





Most users refuse smartphones with light cases due to fear of scratches. A white Taiwanese phone looks stylish and expensive, and if you classify yourself as stylish business people, you can safely choose HTC One mini. User reviews suggest that the case material is scratch resistant, so even after several months of use, the smartphone will look like on the first day of purchase.
