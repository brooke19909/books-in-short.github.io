Novosibirsk - Omsk: closer to each other by car, bus, plane and train

Novosibirsk and Omsk are two cities of the Siberian Federal District, one of the largest in this territorial isolation. Both are centers of homonymous regions with a population of more than a million people. The threads connecting Omsk and Novosibirsk are trains, planes, buses and personal cars.

Distance between cities

The distance Novosibirsk - Omsk will be calculated differently depending on the mode of transportation. In a straight line it is 609 km.





But such a measurement is not suitable for understanding the real route. Indeed, in such a calculation, only scaling is applied with a ruler applied to the map. Real ways always avoid complexity and hard-to-reach areas.

Thus, the erection of ground tracks along swampy or mountainous areas is completely avoided. The road is laid in the most convenient and cost-effective place. It is impossible to establish unequivocally the travel time. It will be different, as it depends not only on the selected vehicle, but also on the condition of the road.

Highways

Going on the road with your own vehicles, you should count on 650 km. On average, such a journey will take about 10 hours, depending on the car, the condition of the road surface and traffic intensity. Most travelers recommend avoiding weekend or Friday night trips. In addition, there are tips on the time of departure: preference should be given to an early start.

In the absence of a personal car, you can go the most economical way - a regular bus. But it is worth noting that such a way of traveling will take too much time, approximately 12 hours. At the same time, it is worth noting the small number of non-stop routes. A traveler who wants to go on the road in this way should take care of purchasing tickets in advance.

Airlines

From Novosibirsk to Omsk can be reached by air. It is the aircraft that can deliver everyone with the same direct route of 609 km. This is one of the fastest and most comfortable ways to travel.





Moreover, time will be spent less than one and a half hours on a non-stop flight. But in this case, you need to rely on a rather high fare, in comparison with other ways to overcome this route. If you need to save a little, you should use the services of Russian Railways.

Novosibirsk - Omsk: railway route

There is also a Novosibirsk-Omsk railway line with a length of 627 km. In this case, you will have to spend more than 7 hours on the road. Previously, there was a faster way to get there. It was an accelerated train "Lastochka" Novosibirsk - Omsk. Then the whole route took a little more than 5 hours.





By April 2016, such a message became completely unprofitable, and the train not only began to cause losses, but also became technologically outdated. A ticket for an adult cost a little cheaper than a ticket for low cost airlines. Train maintenance costs increased every month. Therefore, over time, the route was abolished, and the "Swallow" was transferred to another direction.

To date, communication between the two cities by rail is carried out by transit trains. Almost daily there is one or two of such trains, following at both points of the route. It is enough for the passenger to choose a convenient time and day.

In addition, there is also the “Day Express”, which carries out the movement exclusively between Novosibirsk and Omsk. It is equipped quite comfortably. There are compartments, reserved seats wagons, NE and wagons for seats. The cost of tickets depends on the comfort of travel and the end point. The train goes through ten or a little more settlements, in each of which there is a stop and there is an opportunity to get off.

Travel time on any of the trains takes from 7 to 9-odd hours. It is anticipated that some changes will occur in the future, and high-speed rail links between the cities will resume again.

What is the time difference?

Despite the fact that both cities belong to the same federal district, there is an hourly time difference between them. Novosibirsk is ahead of Omsk. The difference with Moscow in this case is minus 4 and 3 hours, respectively.





When traveling, it should be borne in mind that for different travel methods, different times are indicated on tickets. In airline tickets - time of the airport, but in the railway travel documents should be guided by binding to Moscow time. Of course, if both points are located within Russia, such as Omsk and Novosibirsk.

Communication Novosibirsk - Omsk in the future

For a long time, Russian Railways plans to launch high-speed Sapsans along this route. It is planned that by 2020 a transport network in this territory will be prepared.





The travel time in this case will be reduced, the train from Omsk to Novosibirsk will take you 4 hours. After all, the maximum speed that such an express train can develop is 250 km / h, while its speed predecessor on this route could produce no more than 160 km / h. Will there be a cost-effective such transportation - the future will tell.
