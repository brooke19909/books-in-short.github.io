Guest houses Loo: addresses, services, reviews

The small resort village of Loo in the Lazarevsky district of Sochi in recent years is experiencing its rebirth. Today it is actively developing and pleasing tourists not only with subtropical greenery, clear sea water, clean air, amazing pebble and sand beaches, an abundance of fruit in the local market, but also with many bars, cafes, discos, excellent living conditions: Loo guest houses are well-deserved popularity having a rest.

The village is quite affordable prices for all the charms that gives us a summer vacation. In this article we will introduce everyone who wants to relax in Loo, guest houses on the seashore, more precisely, some of them.



Climatic conditions

Loo is located in the subtropical zone on the territory of the Caucasian Biosphere Reserve. These places are characterized by clear sunny summers, mild and rainy winters, prolonged spring and very bright and warm autumn.

The average summer temperature on the coast is +25 ° C, and in winter - +7 ° C. The holiday season lasts almost six months a year. In August, the water in the sea warms up to +28 ° C.

Well, now it's time to find out where it is better to stay in Loo. Guest houses by the sea - this is the best option for all categories of vacationers.



"Orchid" (st. Zhigulevskaya, 13A)

The four-story guest house "Orchid" is located on a quiet small street of the village. On its closed territory there is a cozy courtyard with a summer gazebo, a relaxation area, a decorative fountain, a kitchen for self-cooking, barbecue and parking for six cars.

A five-minute walk from Orchid is a pebbly and sandy beach, near the village market, grocery stores, Sberbank branch, Magnit supermarket, pharmacy, train station and public transport stop. In the hotel you can stay in comfortable rooms of the "standard" category, designed for two or three vacationers.



The cost of living is from 1600 rubles per day (hereinafter, prices are indicated for one vacationer ).

Reviews

As for the reviews, vacationers do not regret that they settled in the "Orchid". The rooms are cozy and clean, the owners are friendly and very quickly solve any problems that arise.

"Lera" (st. Zhigulevskaya, 24)

Guest House "Lera" (Loo) is located in the center of the village. It is located fourteen kilometers from the center of Sochi. The number of rooms consists of thirty-nine rooms, of different levels of comfort, which are decorated in a simple classic style. Each of them has a balcony and a loggia, a bathroom with toiletries.

Guests can enjoy delicious European and national cuisine in the restaurant or on the shaded terrace. The hotel’s 24-hour reception desk provides information on all local attractions as well as transport routes.



Services and reviews

Hotel services include:

  • paid parking;
  • transfer from the station and airport;
  • a bath;
  • karaoke;
  • organization of excursions.

Toddlers up to three years old stay for free, provided that they are placed on the same bed with their parents. Price - from 2040 rubles.

Reviews

An excellent hotel for a summer holiday, the rooms have nothing superfluous, only everything you need. The hostess is very friendly. Of the shortcomings, tourists call the ban on pets.

"Amshen" (Dekabristov St., 29)

Guest houses Loo, in addition to a comfortable stay, attract tourists with magnificent, surrounded by green areas. Amshen has a spacious courtyard, many flowers. There is an excellent sports ground, two spacious summer kitchens, gazebos with tables, where it is pleasant to breathe in the fresh sea air in the evening.

Amshen is located almost on the seashore (150 meters). The hotel accepts children from three years old. There are no discounts for children. But individual discounts are provided to regular customers. Their size depends on the time of residence and the number of vacationers.



The number of rooms is represented by premises of various layouts and levels of comfort. There are one-room, two-room rooms with amenities and a separate kitchen. All of them are equipped with televisions, refrigerators, split systems. For a large company or families with children, two-room rooms are suitable. If you do not wish to cook in the shared kitchen, rent a room with a separate kitchen.

Services:

  • parking;
  • laundry room with washing machine;
  • bath / sauna;
  • playground;
  • barbecue;
  • transfer from Loo and Sochi station (free);
  • organization of excursions.

Reviews

Hotel Amshen is suitable for families with children. It is always calm here, the territory is under round-the-clock video surveillance. There is a good playground for kids, and although there are only ten minutes of walking to the sea, many vacationers believe that a pool for children on the territory would not hurt.

Price - from 700 rubles.

Guest house "Irina" (Loo, ul. Luchesarnaya, 48)

Once vacationers did not have such a wide selection of places to stay. Most often, they chose the private sector with tiny rooms and almost no amenities. Today, Loo has changed dramatically. Guest houses, reviews of which are enthusiastic, are worthy of competition to sanatoriums and boarding houses.

The territory on which "Irina" is located is buried in flowers and ornamental plants. Not all guest houses in Loo have their own pool, but this hotel has one, which allows guests to freshen up in the summer heat in the pool with a waterfall and countercurrent. In addition, on the territory of the house there are places for recreation in the gazebos.



Number of rooms

Guest house “Irina” (Loo) offers accommodation in one and two-room rooms “standard” and “comfort”. There are also separate cottages on site, as well as self-catering rooms. A ten minute walk from Irina is the popular AquaLoo water park. In the evening, guests are happy to stroll through the vast parkland.

Guest house "Irina" is suitable for those vacationers who appreciate nature, silence and a small beach. Prices in rooms - from 1,100 to 1,800 rubles per day, in houses - from 1,600 to 3,000 rubles.

Reviews

Travelers who have visited many guest houses in Loo believe that Irina is the best place to relax with children. Of the minuses, we can name only a steep descent to the sea and, accordingly, a slightly difficult route to the mountain from the sea.

"Commander" (Brizovaya St., 3)

Every year more and more tourists come to Loo. Guest houses, the prices of which are significantly different from other resorts in the Krasnodar Territory, offer vacationers excellent service and excellent living conditions.

Komandor is located on a small hill near the AquaLoo water park. Distance to the sea is six hundred meters. The bus stop is two hundred meters away. This guest house provides guided tours of the sights of Abkhazia and Sochi.



The rooms are decorated in bright colors and feature modern, functional furniture. They are equipped with televisions that diversify the leisure of vacationers. The guest house has a small pool where you can cool off in the heat. Next to it is a terrace for relaxation and sunbathing.

Guests can cook their favorite meals in the shared, well-equipped kitchen. In addition, next to the "Commander" there are several cafes, a restaurant and a bar.

Prices - from 700 to 1100 rubles.

Reviews

Guests believe that this is a very pleasant guest house in every way. Service at a decent level, hospitable and helpful hosts. Many plan to return here next year.
