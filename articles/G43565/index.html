Yacht Club "Royal Marine" (Berdsk): address, reviews

For recreation and entertainment, yacht clubs are wonderful places. Free hours can be spent here not only for lovers of yachting and boating. For lovers of sunbathing on the beach or frying barbecue with friends, there is also a very comfortable place. About one of the best yacht clubs - Royal Marine - we will tell in the article.

About city

Search on the map the city of Berdsk stands in the northern part of the country. A small village is located in the Novosibirsk region, on the shore of the Berdsky Gulf. To Novosibirsk - only thirty-two kilometers.

The history of the city begins in 1716, when a prison was built on the bay. The village had a very favorable geographical location, so it quickly became a center of trade in the region. A few decades later, deposits of gold were found on the Berd land. Thanks to this, the settlement began to develop and in February 1944 received the status of a city.

Berdsk is an industrial city. There is an electromechanical plant, a bakery, a printing house, as well as several factories for the production of medicines.

Berdsk's climate is temperate continental. The average January temperature is -19 ¬∞ C, July + 18-19 ¬∞ C. Summer is short, not very hot, with a lot of rainfall.

Residents perceived the organization of the yacht club with enthusiasm, and the club became a popular holiday destination for residents of Berdsk.

Location

The Royal Marine yacht club is located on the banks of the Gulf of Berd, which belongs to the Birdie River. This is the largest of the bays of the Novosibirsk reservoir. It appeared in 1957, when the reservoir was filled with water. Now it is one of the deepest places of the river with a large number of shallows. This place was chosen for the construction of the yacht club due to its deep water. An amazing pine forest grows along the shores.

How to get to Royal Marine

If you decide to go out of town in your own car, then modern gadgets will show you the shortest path to your destination, just enter the correct name for the final route.

If you decide to get on your own, by route transport, you need to remember, or rather write down, the following address: Berdsk, Severny-1 microdistrict, building 1. You need to move along Berdskoye Shosse from the River Station stop to the Severny microdistrict stop . Minibuses No. 321 and No. 332. go in this direction. From the stop to the yacht club - about a thousand meters on foot. The Royal Marine (address see above) operates around the clock.

Yacht Club Hotel

Since the club is a country-style relaxation area, of course, a comfortable hotel functions here. Vacationers are offered several types of rooms: luxury and standard.

Standard rooms are one room with two beds. Each room has a separate entrance. The architecture of the complex is such that from almost every room you can enjoy the sunset without going outside. Each room is equipped with two beds, a plasma TV, a minimum set of dishes and a kettle. Bathrooms have everything you need for a comfortable end to the day. Fans of reading before bedtime can use a table lamp, which controls the brightness of the light.

Suites are two-room rooms that can accommodate up to four people at a time. There is one large double bed, in the next room there is a corner folding sofa, on which two more people can freely fit. The rooms have a set of dishes, a kettle, a plasma TV with a full package of channels hangs on the wall. If you wish, you can go to the terrace and enjoy the magnificent sunset. Each room has a separate exit to the street. The bathroom is equipped with a bathtub and shower.

In addition, the complex has refrigerators where you can store personal food without fear. There is high-speed Internet, which will allow you to be in touch twenty-four hours a day.

The hotel is located away from the highway, in the depths of the forest, where you can enjoy the clean air, breathing in full smells of pine needles.

The advantages of a hotel complex

Not every country hotel of this magnitude boasts a wide list of additional benefits. A yacht club "Royal Marine" can. If you arrived to rest in your own vehicle, you can safely leave it in a secure free parking. There is a helipad with the possibility of renting a light helicopter.

Guests arriving with a large and friendly family can send their children to the care of experienced animators. The hotel has a playground that will entertain children for the duration of their stay and give parents the opportunity to relax and do their own thing.

Athletes will also not be left unattended at Royal Marine (Berdsk). At their service - a court for tennis, as well as a gym.

You can easily regain your strength in a restaurant. Cooks will prepare any dish for guests.

After a busy day, you can relax in the Russian bath, which will be prepared for a certain time. It is only necessary to warn staff in advance that you are going to visit her.

Yacht and boat rental

The main form of entertainment at Royal Marine, of course, is water sports. And here the main characters are yachts and boats, which the suburban complex provides its guests.

Each yacht accommodates from 5 to 12 people, depending on the model. If you have never steered a boat or are simply afraid, the club will provide you with an experienced captain who will not only take you all over the bay and show you all the secret corners. At the request of guests, the captain can land on the beach at any possible place, if weather permits in Berdsk. You can go ashore and take a walk along the clear forest paths or swim on a deserted beach.

The rental price is about five thousand rubles per hour. However, when ordering a yacht for three hours or more, a discount of ten percent is provided. It is possible to get a nice bonus. When ordering a yacht from three hours, you can choose any additional swimming tool - a bun or water skiing - completely free. During the trip you will be accompanied by pleasant relaxing music.

Service and parking

If you are a happy owner of your own watercraft, Royal Marine Yacht Club can provide maintenance and parking services for the yacht or boat. There are 100 parking spaces on the pier, fully equipped for storage and maintenance.

Specialists of the yacht club, at the request of the owners, will make a complete diagnosis of the swimming means, correct defects or make the necessary high-quality repairs. And they can also make full technical training of a swimming product for the winter or summer season.

In addition, the specialists of the suburban complex will help you to carry out full preparation for the sale of your funds. If you need services for transaction support, buying or selling, then you can order services from Royal Marine specialists.

In addition, on the territory of the complex, it is possible to carry out selection and additional installation of navigation equipment, vessel registration and selection of a sonorous, beautiful name. It is possible to order tailoring of covers.

Rental of additional equipment

Fans of active water activities will find many interesting activities on the water. If weather permits in Berdsk, you can rent water skis, buns, catamarans and even wakeboards. Rent per hour will cost about the same amount as in large Russian clubs.

After registration with the tenant of the water equipment, mandatory training is provided by experienced specialists. You are introduced to the basic safety rules, as well as talking about what to do in case of unforeseen situations.

For guests who do not like relaxing on the water, bike rental is provided. At any time, you can use the rental and take a walk into the depths of the forest, enjoy the silence, purity and freshness of the air.

Guest reviews

The reviews for Royal Marin are quite diverse.

The positive reviews of the guests are mainly concentrated around delicious cuisine, beautiful nature and magnificent beaches. Very clean rooms. Guests note the opportunity to pre-select breakfast to their taste. Guests like the convenient location of the guest houses, from the windows of which you can see the yachts standing on the pier, enjoy the sunsets and magnificent natural paintings. Guests note the well-groomed adjacent territory of the complex: everywhere is clean, there are bins. Designated smoking areas. The rooms are also equipped with modern technology.

There are also disadvantages. Many guests claim that sometimes it is very noisy. However, the staff claims that all the noisy events end as expected, at 23.00, and give their own recommendation. If you are going to a country club for a weekend to spend time quietly with your family, keep in mind that noisy events such as a wedding or a birthday banquet can be held in the complex during the same period. Guests also note very high prices for renting swimming equipment, the inconvenience of the adjacent road, which is not at all equipped for arriving in a personal car, as well as the lack of a microwave oven, which would be useful to many guests for warming up food.
