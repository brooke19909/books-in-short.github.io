Tourists who are more attracted by peace and tranquility are suitable for tours to Greece on the island of Kos. Here the nature is buoying, no one and nothing prevents to enjoy the sunsets, and the days flow measured and leisurely. The island of Kos is one of the smallest resorts in the country. How to get there

Kos Island, although small, but there is an international airport. It is called "Hippocrates" and is located near the village of Antimachia, southwest of the Pyli Bay Hotel for about 15 km. The capital of. Kos lies 22 km northwest of Antimachia. The island's airport receives flights only from Athens year-round, and in season planes arrive here from all over the world, including Russia. It is most convenient to get to the hotel in question by tourist transfer. Travel time is about half an hour. If you arrived in Kos on your own, it is best to take a taxi.

Leisure at the hotel

Beach

It is thanks to the crystal clear Aegean Sea that many people like the island of Kos. Greece, whose beaches are mostly perfectly clean, is considered a country with the best sea resorts. Kos also boasts clean and beautiful beach areas. The coating on them exists for every taste - with yellow sand, white, black and pebbles of different sizes. An important feature of the northern coast of the island, just where the village of Marmari is located - there is almost always a wind. Sometimes, especially closer to the fall, it is so impressive that it is difficult not only to swim in the sea, but also to sunbathe. But for surfers this is a paradise.

The beaches of Kos have a second feature regarding water temperature. Here, even at the coast in July or August, it can be somewhat cool (up to +22 ¬∞ C), regardless of the fact that the air temperature at this time reaches +35 ¬∞ C and higher.

Pyli Bay 3 *, according to guests reviews, is located from the nearest beach no more than 500 meters. The path to it lies along the road through the village, past a stream with turtles. This beach is urban, sunbeds and umbrellas are paid, but you can sit on your own towels under the canopy of trees. Entertainment is available a little away from the recreation area. At 4 km there is another nice Tigaki beach. If you rent a car, you can go the beaches of the south coast. The winds there are slightly quieter, and the sea is warmer.
