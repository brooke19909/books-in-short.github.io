Hotel Soviva Resort 4 * (Tunisia, Sousse, Port El Kantaoui): description, services, reviews

Holidays in Tunisia are not only snow-white beaches and the warm sea, but also the ancient monuments of Carthaginian culture, thalassotherapy, golf courses and a large number of shops for shopping. It will be a great alternative to Egypt, the benefit of the country are very close to each other. Choosing a place to stay in the resort town of Port El Kantaoui, pay attention to the Soviva Resort 4 * hotel. Tour and accommodation here will be inexpensive, and at the same time you will receive quality service, a variety of entertainment and comfortable rooms.

Hotel location and nearby attractions

Hotel Soviva Resort 4 * is located 2 kilometers from the resort of Port El Kantaoui, which is part of the famous Sousse. This is the main center of tourist life in Tunisia. Restaurants are located all over the place, offering travelers to try national dishes, exotic seafood and much more. Healing mud wraps are another popular form of entertainment in Port El Kantaoui. Also, lovers of horse riding, golf and diving will like it here. Going to Sousse, which is 7 kilometers from the hotel, you should definitely take time to view ancient sights. Among them are especially distinguished:

  • Medina - the historical center of the city;
  • Great Mosque of Sousse;
  • Khalifa Tower;
  • House-Museum "Dar Essid", built a thousand years ago;
  • early Christian catacombs.

The nearest airport is in the city of Monastir, which is 25 kilometers from the hotel. The road to Soviva Resort 4 * (Tunisia) from here takes on average about an hour. A private beach is located half a kilometer from the hotel. It will take about 5-10 minutes to reach it.

General Hotel Information

Tourist complex Soviva Resort 4 * (Sousse) was built in 1993. Since then, he has been hosting tourists from all over the world all year round. Rest in Tunisia is best in the summer months, when the weather is hot, and the sea is completely warmed up. At the beginning and mid-autumn, the flow of tourists decreases, but the water is still warm enough for swimming and sunbathing. In winter and spring it becomes cool here, but travelers still relax here, swimming in the indoor heated pool and enjoying wellness treatments. Partially, the hotel staff understands the Russian language, although tourists still need to learn English before the trip. The national composition of the hotel is mixed. Mostly Europeans and Tunisians rest here.





On the vast territory of the complex (50,000 sq. M.) There are several buildings: 2 main buildings four and five floors high, as well as 5 additional three-story buildings. For tourists, the Soviva Resort Hotel (Tunisia) offers more than 500 rooms. Some of them are suitable for travelers with reduced mobility. The last complete reconstruction of the building and rooms was completed in 2014. You can relax here with children of any age. Pets are strictly prohibited.

Number of rooms

By tourist standards, Soviva Resort 4 * hotel is considered a large tourist complex, because 522 rooms are located on its territory. The rooms are decorated in a classic style. The color scheme is calm, so the rooms have a good rest after an active day. Most rooms are designed for two people, but there are both family and single apartments. The rooms have a small balcony, and on the ground floor there are terraces instead. The rooms offer views of the beautiful hotel garden or the huge pool located in the center of the complex.





The standard apartments of the Soviva Resort Hotel (Tunisia) have a classic set of furniture. They necessarily have a double or several single beds, and folding chairs, sofas and baby cribs can also be placed. Some rooms have bunk beds. Present here is a desk, chair, large mirror, bedside tables. On the balcony there is a small dining table and two chairs. Tidy in the room every day, and linen is changed 2 times a week.

Room Amenities

For a comfortable stay alone furniture is not enough. Hotel Soviva Resort 4 * (Port el Kantaoui) offers tourists rooms that are equipped with all amenities necessary for a comfortable stay. Hotel guests can use the following:

  • Central air conditioning, with which guests of the hotel will be able to sleep peacefully, without feeling strong heat in the summer months. It works from the beginning of June until mid-September. During the winter months, the hotel includes heating.
  • A small refrigerator where you can store food or drinks.
  • Free empty minibar. For its filling you will have to pay an additional amount.
  • A small and old TV with the ability to view cable TV channels. Some Russian-language channels also work.
  • Paid electronic safe for storing documents, money and other valuables.
  • Direct telephone.
  • Bathroom equipped with shower, washbasin, mirror, hairdryer. Tourists are given towels, which are replaced daily.

Hotel infrastructure

For its guests, the Soviva Resort 4 * hotel (Tunisia) seeks to create the highest quality living conditions at affordable prices. To do this, the hotel has many infrastructure facilities that make the rest more comfortable and enjoyable. Throughout the holidays, guests of the complex can use the following services:

  • The round-the-clock work of the reception and the administrator, who is ready to quickly solve the problems encountered by tourists.
  • Free internet in the hotel lobby as well as in the main pool. In other places, Wi-Fi is only available for a fee.
  • An additional safe at the reception, as well as cameras for storing large bags and suitcases.
  • Free parking on site.
  • Organization of a paid transfer from the airport to the hotel.
  • Laundry, where for an extra amount you can wash clothes and clean shoes.
  • Currency exchange.
  • A comfortable elevator, wheelchair ramps and other facilities for visitors with disabilities.
  • Own business center with one conference room and banquet facilities.
  • Gift shop and pharmacy.

Food system, restaurants and bars

The main type of food at the Soviva Resort 4 * hotel (Sousse) is the all-inclusive system, popular with Russian tourists. A common buffet is served for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The price also includes drinks that are served daily from 10:00 until midnight. There is the possibility of creating a special diet and vegetarian menu. The following catering establishments also operate on the territory of the complex:

  • The main restaurant where all the main meals are held.
  • A la carte restaurant. It serves national cuisine, as well as a special French menu and a separate room for lovers of Japanese cuisine. A visit to this establishment is not included in the price and is charged separately.
  • Lobby bar.
  • Moorish cafe.
  • 2 bars located by the pool.




Organization of children's leisure

The hotel allows children with all ages. For children, a cradle is additionally provided in the room, and the main restaurant has the opportunity to use high chairs for feeding. For a fee, you can also make a special menu for the child.

On the territory of Soviva Resort 4 * (Tunisia) for children there are 2 shallow pools with water slides and heated water. You can also play for free on a modern playground equipped with safe carousels and swings. There is also a mini club for children, where parents can leave their kids for a while.





How much will it cost to stay at the hotel?

Depending on the type of room and time of rest in Soviva Resort 4 * (Tunisia) prices may vary significantly. Nevertheless, the complex is considered a budget hotel. The cheapest rooms will cost from 3000 rubles per night. A week of rest with accommodation in such a room will cost a tourist 12,000 rubles per person. Spacious suites are much more expensive. The maximum cost of such apartments may exceed 20,000 rubles per night. Also, the type of food affects the price of accommodation.

Positive feedback from guests

The description of the hotel will be incomplete without the opinions of tourists about the rest in this place. Travelers vacationing in Soviva Resort 4 *, leave conflicting reviews. Some of them liked the hotel service, while others, on the contrary, were disappointed with the shortcomings. First, we list the advantages of the hotel:

  • Nice view from the window. The balconies of the lower floors overlook the amazing olive grove, in the shade of which you can spend time in the heat.
  • Fast check-in, which is done before the declared time.
  • Quiet environment, at night inaudible music from bars and nightclubs.
  • Towels and linen are changed on time. It can be seen that the products are not new, but they are always clean and smell good.
  • The menu in the main restaurant is not very diverse, but hearty and tasty. Serve several types of side dishes, fish, several types of meat.
  • Very interesting children's animation.




Negative guest reviews

But not only the advantages described by tourists living here. Soviva Resort 4 * (Tunisia) is criticized a lot. The most serious problems that spoil the rest are:

  • They give out little water. One bottle of mineral water relies on one number, regardless of how many people live in it.
  • The menu has a lot of spicy and seasoned food. The diet is not suitable for people suffering from diseases of the gastrointestinal tract. Of the fruits, only watermelon and melon.
  • The rooms are very dirty, they are not cleaned at all on the balconies. Plumbing in the bathroom is old and works poorly.
  • Ants may appear in the rooms, which bite painfully. Or huge cockroaches.
  • Do not give out toiletries even after request and extra tips.
  • The hotel staff dilutes alcohol, and also asks for money for any services, including free ones.

General impression of the hotel

The hotel leaves a double impression. Soviva Resort 4 * description has a good, but in practice, after leisure, tourists leave reviews advising never to choose this hotel. Of course, it is hardly suitable for a high-quality and well-paid vacation. If you have large means, it is better to choose another hotel. But the tourist complex is well suited for a budget holiday. Inexpensive rooms combined with a rich entertainment program, a large swimming pool and a clear sea will give tourists a comfortable stay at an affordable price.
