Peplum dresses: patterns for home tailoring

Women of various shapes always try to demonstrate only their best features and hide minor imperfections under well-chosen clothes. However, more and more women are doing wrong, hiding their options under baggy t-shirts, large sweaters and voluminous trousers, this is an unforgivable mistake.



A woman should always look in the best way, even if she is going to do a spring cleaning at home, because she always looks at her neighbors, family members or friends, and they must be delighted. If a woman has a small complex about excessive thinness and lack of volume, then this can easily eliminate dresses with the Basques. Patterns or the result of shopping with friends at the best boutiques of the city, the main thing is to choose the most suitable option for you, so that the color and texture harmonize with your skin and hair shade, as well as the type of figure. If you can not afford the purchase of a new instance, then the pattern of the dress with the peplum and its independent tailoring will become the very budget option that will allow you to update your wardrobe and please your beloved husband with a wonderful transformation. If you are extremely talented in needlework, you can easily make Basque dresses, photos of which were found in your favorite fashion magazine or that you saw on one of your friends. And if you have a sheath dress without shoulders that you have been wanting to upgrade for a long time, then the option below is for you.

Sewing dresses

So, you will need lace fabric, suitable not exactly according to the color of your dress, but at least in the range, in the amount of 1-1.2 meters. You also need a short zipper, one or two buttons in the form of pearls and, in fact, threads and a sewing machine with an overlock. So, we do for a dress with a Basque pattern: cut two identical rectangles from the material, the smaller side (A) is equal to the volume of the chest + 5 cm for allowances, the larger (B) is from the shoulder to the waist. Then we cut out a strip 12-15 cm wide and a length equal to the waistline + 5 cm. We attach two rectangles to each other and make marks: on side A draw a low neck, and on side B draw openings for the hands.



We sew the shoulders and sides, after preliminary processing the edges, and cut the back in half and insert the zipper, starting from the bottom edge, sew the button and buttonhole on top. A distinctive detail of a peplum dress, whose patterns are very simple, is, of course, neat assembly.
