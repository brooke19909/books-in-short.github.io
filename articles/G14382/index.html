Adriatic coast and Italy sea hotels

The eastern part of Italy is washed by the warm Adriatic Sea, which warms up quickly and well due to its shallow depth. That is why it is so popular with tourists, and hotels in Italy on the sea are never empty.



A very gentle coast, covered with white fine sand that goes under the water, allows you to walk in shallow water, breathing in the healing air and acquiring a golden tan. Thanks to this feature, the Adriatic coast is ideal for families with children, especially since all the necessary conditions are created for children of all ages. The infrastructure of resorts in this region is directly related to natural conditions, therefore not only five-star, but even cheap hotels in Italy offer a lot of opportunities for entertainment and relaxation. It offers tourists water parks and dolphinariums, children's playgrounds and zoos, amusement parks and discos, restaurants, bars, nightclubs and much more.

Adriatic resorts and hotels in Italy

the sea is also very attractive for active tourists, because from here you can easily get to many historical places - for example, visit Venice, Bologna, Parma and other cities rich in attractions. In addition, Rimini is the final destination of many sightseeing tours, so many tourists prefer to complete their trip to Italy on the Adriatic coast. Naturally, at their service are numerous hotels of Italy on the sea.

The beautiful and cozy town of Lido di Jesolo, located on the territory of the Venetian Riviera, can be called a real tourist paradise. New Italy hotels have been built here on the sea, in which there are all conditions for a comfortable rest of tourists. Along the 15-kilometer coast are three lines of hotels. The first line runs in the immediate vicinity of the coast, from the second it is separated by a road. And from hotels located on the third line, the distance to the sea is no more than 300 meters.

This resort can conditionally be divided into several areas that have their own characteristics. Faro, for example, is famous for its open sea, so it is popular among sailors. Fans of crowded parties, discos and shops prefer Centro. Silence, beautiful nature and peace will give Pineta. Gourmets can also enjoy national seafood in Cortellazzo.



From Italy you can take many interesting excursions in the Mediterranean Sea. For example, one of the most famous Mediterranean islands is Sicily. By the way, not only Italy boasts its resorts, but Sicily, whose hotels are located along the coast, attracts tourists no less than the beaches of the Adriatic.

A variety of beaches can be found on the island, for example, pebble-sand and sand shores predominate in the southern part, and mostly rocky in the north. You should also prepare for the fact that the island is almost always windy, but the wind blows warm, so the swimming season here lasts almost until November.

The Sicilian resort of Taormina attracts young tourists with an abundance of entertainment venues. Those who prefer a more respectable vacation, it is better to go to the capital Palermo, the legendary Syracuse, historical Agrigento or Catania. The excursion program on the island is no less saturated than in Italy, because Sicily is famous for its historical monuments and famous places. The main thing is that there is enough time and effort to visit them all.
