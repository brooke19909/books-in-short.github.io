Dzhemete Village: Hotels, motels and hostels

The village of Dzhemete is a paradise for elderly people, tourists who go on vacation with young children, as well as all who can hardly change the climate and the summer heat.



This resort village of the Krasnodar Territory is famous for its healing beaches with quartz golden sand. Small children feel great here and can safely splash around for a very long time in the shallow waters of the Black Sea. The city beach will amaze you with a variety of water attractions.

In Dzhemete, hotels are located mainly on the first coastline. They offer you, your friends and families, European level service and facilities.

Nearby are eateries, cafes, markets, restaurants, travel agencies, shops, transport stops, entertainment and attractions for children and adults. Not far from here - a dolphinarium and a water park.

Hotel "Seahorse"

Considering the mini-hotels in Dzhemete, one should highlight the "Seahorse". The hotel is three hundred meters from the sea. This is a great place to relax with young children. The hotel offers vacationers comfortable rooms for two or four people. There are eight rooms, four floors. The total capacity of the institution is 30 people. Moreover, on each floor there is an iron with an ironing board.



The territory is landscaped and landscaped. Judging by the reviews, here a courtyard with a cozy canopy, equipped with a kitchen and furniture is provided for vacationers. The kitchen has an oven, refrigerator, dishes, microwave. There is also a parking lot, playground, internet.

Cost: from 1800 rubles / person.

Hotel Villa Costa

Hotel Villa Costa is a three-story modern building located on a quiet street of the resort. This hotel Dzhemete, reviews of which can be found only positive, is located on the first coastline, a hundred meters from the beach. Nearby is a dolphinarium, water park and a huge number of cafes.

The accommodation offers cozy, quiet, comfortable, spacious rooms with private facilities. Free for tourists - Internet access. A hundred meters from the hotel there is a guarded paid parking lot.

Hotel Priobye

Hotel Priobye is located on Pionersky Avenue, on the first coastline. This is an excellent corner of the Black Sea coast, famous for its shallow coastal waters and sandy healing beaches. This is a real find for people who are going to relax with young children. It is also a great place for the elderly and anyone who is having a hard time changing climatic zones.



From this hotel Dzhemete, the prices for accommodation in which are very affordable, to the sea - only 5 minutes of a quiet walk. Here you have a great opportunity to relax with comfort in body and soul. The restaurant, judging by the reviews, has a very pleasant atmosphere, allowing you to spend time in peace and quiet. On the territory there is a green cozy courtyard with a mini-waterfall, an alpine hill, a playground for children and flower beds. In addition, guests can take a steam bath in the sauna, swim in the pool, play billiards, have a cocktail or juice in the cafe-bar, enjoy delicious dishes from the chef, and then share their impressions with friends from the rest using the free Internet. This place has everything for a good mood and comfort, which you really want to get in Dzhemete!
