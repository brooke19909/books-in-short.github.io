What is a rear view camera for a car for?

The rear view camera for the car is designed to transmit to the monitor located in the cabin, images of the space behind the car. Having this option helps the driver to park anywhere. If the car is equipped with a full-time monitor, then the camera can be connected to it. If the equipment mentioned is not there, then you can install the screen yourself. A rearview camera for a car connected to reverse gear allows you to view even that which is invisible to ordinary parking sensors. The user can independently adjust the angle and height of the device image. A correctly adjusted rearview camera for a car even allows you to notice stones and low curbs, which will protect the car body and bumper from scratches. This is especially true for cars with low landing.



Who needs a rear view camera for a car?

The installation of such a device is primarily necessary for drivers who quite often park in new unfamiliar places or at night (especially relevant for taxi drivers). The camcorder allows you to carefully consider possible obstacles at the parking spot, thereby protecting your car from damage. The installation of such equipment is an excellent solution for those who have to park the car in the evening in unlit courtyards. A rear-view camera for a car will be very useful for both novice drivers and experienced car owners, as it is able to provide additional driving comfort.

How to install a camcorder?

Installation of the camera is carried out on the shelf of the car trunk or on the luggage compartment lid. The signal from the device is fed to the monitor via a four-wire cable. Two cores must be connected to the reversing lamp, and the rest stretch through the entire cabin to the monitor. Wires can be drawn from the bottom of the body (for this you need to disassemble the plastic sills) or under the skin of the ceiling. In the latter case, remove the rubber seals and lay the cable.



Navigator with rear view camera

One of the most popular video output solutions from a camcorder is a car navigator that supports the video input function. The advantages of this symbiosis are obvious:

- saving money, because the navigator is now in almost any car, which means there is no need to buy an additional monitor;

- saving space in the car: there is no need to clutter up the interior and windshield with additional equipment ;

- convenience and ease of operation: the navigator is the only media center, respectively, the driver does not need to scatter his attention between different devices, which as a result leads to increased driving safety.
