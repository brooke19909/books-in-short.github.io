Hotel Rios Beach Hotel 4 *, Turkey: description and reviews of tourists

One of the most sought after spa hotels in Kemer is the Rios Beach Hotel, which is deservedly endowed with four stars. It has a lot of advantages, the most significant of which are its favorable location, high-quality service and comfortable accommodation.

general characteristics

The hotel complex Rios Beach Hotel 4 *, reviews of which we will consider in the article, opened its doors to accommodate guests in the distant 1993. During its existence, it was repeatedly renovated, the last of which was carried out in 2013. More than twenty years of experience have influenced today's endless stream of travelers who want to stay here. The total area, which exceeds the mark of 5000 square meters, is paved with green lawns, fragrant flower beds and numerous trees. They form a shadowy space and save guests from the hot summer heat.

The main and only building is represented by a snow-white building on four floors, equipped with elevators. The nearest international airport is 38 km away, which does not require additional tiring trips after the flight. Pets are not allowed. Smoking at rest is unacceptable. There are no conditions for guests with disabilities. Suitable for those who want to stay on the very coast of the Mediterranean Sea in an institution, as guests note, with very affordable prices.

It is possible to install an extra bed. Payment is accepted by cards of international payment systems.

Arrangement

Furniture set - standard, includes a double or two single beds, bedside tables, countertops, luggage space, wardrobe and chair. The presence of an individual air conditioner in the room allows you to create the required microclimate suitable for each individual guest. The presence of a multi-channel TV allows you to enjoy your favorite broadcast and news programs, staying updated on the latest events in the world. Use of the telephone is chargeable. Safe - key, available for a fee. When checking in, the minibar is filled with drinking water. Further updating is paid extra. The chambers are equipped with innovative fireproof systems. Wi-Fi is available throughout.

The bathroom has a modest finish. Equipped with shower with hydromassage, sink, mirror, hairdryer. There are snow-white towels in an amount that meets international standards. Personal hygiene products are represented by soap, shampoo and gel.





Nutrition

Like many hotels in Turkey, the Rios Beach Hotel adheres to the concept of All Inclusive. According to its provisions, the food system is carried out in the form of a buffet. The variety of dishes, as guests note, is incredibly large. Talented chefs prepare real masterpieces that are incredibly tasty, satisfying and mouth-watering. The capacity of the main restaurant is designed for 150 people, the summer terrace - for a similar amount.

There are three bars: by the pool, on the beach and Dispence bar. Tea time - from 16:00 to 17:00, the menu is fixed. An institution on the coast offers, in addition to soft drinks and cocktails, a number of snacks and afternoon snacks, which can satisfy the feeling of an emerging hunger.

Only alcohol brought from abroad, natural Turkish coffee and freshly squeezed juices are not included in the All Inclusive program.

For the catering of Rios Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey), the reviews received mostly positive. Tourists praise local chefs. They say that the food here is made from fresh products and amazingly tasty.

Leisure of little guests

Rios Beach Hotel 4 * (Kemer) often receives praise from tourists who come on vacation with their kids. They claim that in addition to a swimming pool for children, a children's club has been organized, which is designed for ages from 4 to 12 years. Here, under the supervision of an experienced educator and animators, the kids are saturated with new emotions, playing board games, drawing pictures, participating in various contests, competitions and competitions. Evenings of travelers are busy watching an animation program, which includes colorful dances, humorous scenes, entertaining quizzes and much more, which, according to guests, is interesting and exciting.





Additional services and reviews
