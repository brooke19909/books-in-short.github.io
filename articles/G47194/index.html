Washing machine with a removable top cover: model overview, description and specifications, reviews, photos

Built-in appliances are gaining more and more popularity. It is understandable, because it is possible to save space and make the room very stylish. Most often, such equipment is purchased for the kitchen, because it is there that various household appliances should be located. One such appliance may be a washing machine with a removable top cover. It is specially designed to fit in the kitchen and save space. But there are several types of such machines. We will analyze their best models.

Types of built-in washing machines

There are several types of built-in washing machines. They are not particularly different from each other, but there are some differences. That is why they were distinguished into separate species. And that's why you need to consider them.

  • Fully integrated machines. These include devices that are fully integrated in a cabinet or cabinet and even have special curtains for securing doors. These cars can be completely hidden in a curbstone.
  • Machines under the countertop. In this case, the front of the washing machine remains visible. Just instead of the top cover, a countertop is installed. This design is the most common.

It is worth noting that many recommend a washing machine with a removable top cover, which is fully integrated into a cabinet or cabinet. These devices are much less noisy and vibrate, since they are completely covered by the doors of the cabinet. But in this review we will consider various models of cars. Among them will be both representatives of the first type, and the second.
