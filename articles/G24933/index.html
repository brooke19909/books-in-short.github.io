Rest in Tomsk. Recreation facilities: living conditions, prices, photos

Holidays in Tomsk have long been gaining popularity . Recreation facilities in this area are open all year round, and there are few expensive ones. It should be noted that they all provide a huge range of different services. This article will describe especially well-known and truly stunning holiday resorts that will be a great option when choosing a vacation home.

Siberian Quadrille

The Siberian Quadrille is a place where you can have a good rest in Tomsk. The recreation centers of this region always work all year round and this one is no exception. Minimum room rate - 1500 rubles.

Organized meals are not provided, so everyone should do the cooking on their own. There is a marina on the shore where small vessels can park. You can rent rowboats and motor boats, as well as fishing equipment.

For visitors is given a two-story house where there is cold and hot water. Fully equipped kitchen, dining room, living room, bedrooms.

"Cedar Zaimka"

Holidays in Tomsk, whose recreation facilities are in demand, can be perfectly spent at the Cedar Zaimka. This complex is open all year.

Meals can be either independent or not. If desired (and, of course, an additional payment), the chefs will prepare delicious dishes from the catch of the guests.

People are populated in cottages where autonomous heating is carried out. Each house has its own kitchen, lounge, dining room, bedrooms, bathroom, shower. There is a variety of household appliances: TV, refrigerator, music center, etc.

Swings, arbors, verandas are installed. You can use the local barbecue. For those arriving by car, parking is provided.

Tom

The recreation center "Tom" in Tomsk works every climatic season. The minimum daily room rate is 2500 rubles. The complex has several cafes. Unfortunately, food is not included in the price.

For guests there are two buildings with more than 5 rooms. They differ in class and, accordingly, in the price category: standards and suites are equipped. The first are single, they have minimal amenities. The suites are all double.

The recreation center is named after the river, which flows very close to the residential sector. The complex is ideal for training camps.





"Crystal"

Recreation facilities in Kireevsk (Tomsk), which have gained immense popularity, are represented by the Crystal complex. It works all year round, and the minimum room rate is 500 rubles.

In the kitchen, which allows you to cook yourself, there is an oven, dishes and a refrigerator. If you want to have a picnic, you can rent skewers and barbecue grills to make barbecue. In the dining room at the same time can be 60 people.

Recreation facilities in Kireevsk (Tomsk) have many opportunities for providing various entertainment services. Among those that are available in Crystal, the following can be noted: a bathhouse, American billiards, tennis, darts, chess, etc. Trampolines are equipped for children, you can rent scooters. Adults rent boats, mattresses, an armchair, skates, skis and other items if they wish. Cottages are designed for 2-4 people.
