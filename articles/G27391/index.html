How is crocheting a washcloth done? For this picture, in addition to the hook, you need a round stick or tube. Old cardboard covers from thermometers are ideally suited. You can use markers or markers, pens or pencils. Only then the loops will be shorter.

Having knitted the first row, in the second we begin to make loops. That is, the working thread every time before knitting a column, we wrap around a round stick or tube, then we put the hook under the column and pull out the loop to knit the column. We do the same with the next column. There are two windings on our device, but we are not paying attention to this yet. Now, according to the drawing of two loops on the hook, we do one. Next, we repeat the knitting algorithm: we wrap the tube with thread, stretch the loop and knit both loops on the hook into one.

When a sufficient number of wound loops are gathered on the accessory, it must be carefully removed and work continued in the same way. The next row is knitted without the formation of loops, it is fastening. That is, we loop each even row of loops.
