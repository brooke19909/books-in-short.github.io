How to knit slippers easier: crochet or knitting?

There are many works of needlewomen in magazines, in which slippers are solid, made of elements, in the form of socks, in the form of boots, sandals, a trace, of an incomprehensible form. How to knit slippers easier: knitting or crocheting for beginners

Types of slippers

Slippers are conditionally divided into several types:

  • the model starts from the sole of the heel;
  • slippers fit like socks right off the heel;
  • shoes are stitched from a single piece of cloth;
  • slippers, knitted from square motifs;
  • the sole is made of fabric that is tied.

Knitting with a continuous cloth is not very convenient and not so beautiful, since such slippers are shapeless. Knitting slippers with knitting needles is easier for those who know how to knit socks. Crocheted products keep their shape, so you can knit pointed slippers or in the form of animals. It is best to combine knitting and crocheting. For example, knit a heel, and dial loops on the fourth knitting needle using a chain of air loops. Or crochet the sole, and then knit loops on the knitting needles and knit the tracks in a circle.

How to learn to knit slippers

The easiest way to tie tracks from the sole and tie it in a circle. For their knitting, take the threads, 4 knitting needles (for convenience you will need about 8 knitting needles), a hook to hook the loops.

  1. Knit the sole along the length and width of your foot. Knitting starts from the heel (9-12 loops) with the front or wrong side of the stitch as standard.
  2. Measure the middle of the toe with a thread of a different color or a pin.
  3. Dial the loops around the knitting needles, distributing them so that it is convenient to work.
  4. It is necessary to knit slippers in a circle with a gradual decrease at the toe in each row. Having reached the middle of the toe, knit together the last and first loops with two adjacent knitting needles.
  5. Decrease is until it satisfies the height of the product. If in this case the opening of the tracks is large, then further crochet with connecting posts to tighten it.
  6. If necessary, you can decorate slippers with a bow, cord, pico or arched trim.
