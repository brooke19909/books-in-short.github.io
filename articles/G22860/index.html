Hotel San Pietro 3 * (Italy, Sicily, Letojani Island): reviews

The small Sicilian resort town of Letojanni is simply very popular among tourists from all over the world. In season, the population there increases tenfold. The status of the city of Letojanni was received recently - in 1952, immediately after many hotels, cafes and restaurants were built here for visiting vacationers.

Hotels in this town, judging by the reviews of tourists, are quite convenient. And to rent a house, if you want to spend a vacation in this town, there will be absolutely no difficulty. For example, quite good rooms, according to tourists , are offered to guests of Letojanni by Hotel San Pietro 3 *.

Where is the complex located?

Judging by the reviews available on the Web, this comfortable hotel is one of the most popular among travelers in the city. This hotel was built in the center of Letojanni, across the road from the sea. There is a bus stop right next to the hotel . From here you can quickly and easily get to the very popular resort of Taormina in Sicily . This city is located just 3 km from the complex. So if you wish, guests of San Pietro 3 * (Sicily) can even reach it just on foot.



Also nearby are the large Durante Square and the Cathedral. In general, most tourists consider the location of the hotel to be very successful.

Not too far from this complex is the airport. It is located only about 50 km from the hotel - in the city of Catania. Of course, such a bus tour around the country would not be too tiring . Relatively close location to the airport, tourists also refer to the advantages of this complex.

General description of the hotel

Judging by the reviews, vacationers who arrived earlier in the reserved rooms are usually not allowed, referring to the fact that they are not ready yet. But tourists can, of course, be completely free to wait for the appointed hour in the lobby . In most cases, the future tenants of the hotel, as they themselves say on the sites of tour operators, spend time waiting on the beach. Fortunately, it is located just a minute walk from the hotel buildings.





They accept guests in this recreation center in small neat buildings of a traditional Sicilian construction. In season, the walls and balconies of the hotel, completely entwined with weaving plants, look, judging by the reviews, actually very impressive.

Unfortunately, the San Pietro 3 * hotel cannot be called too new . It was built quite a long time ago - in the last century. Yes, and the last reconstruction was carried out already in 2001. However, the administration most likely carries out minor cosmetic repairs at the hotel quite often. At least, the paint on the facades of the buildings of this hotel, judging by its appearance, is periodically updated.

What hotel offers tourists accommodation?

If desired, the guests of Letojanni can rent one of 94 comfortable rooms in the San Pietro 3 * hotel. The rooms of the hotel guests are offered the following amenities:

  • telephone;
  • TV;
  • mini bar.

Air conditioning in this hotel is also, of course, available. But they work only in season, that is, from June 15 to September 15. Of course, all rooms of this modern hotel are equipped with bathrooms. A hairdryer is not offered in the showers. But if necessary, residents can take him to the reception. Almost all rooms at this hotel have small balconies. Here, tourists can sit in the evening with a glass of wine or a cup of coffee while admiring the picturesque surroundings of the town of Letojanni or the sea.





San Pietro 3 * in Sicily: guest reviews on rooms

The rooms in this hotel, according to many tourists, are pretty comfortable. Some disadvantage of this hotel, according to vacationers, is that the administration can populate newly arrived vacationers not in the rooms that were booked, but in apartments of lower cost. The staff explain this is usually a lack of hotel rooms.

When such a situation arises, experienced tourists recommend that you submit a claim to the administration of the complex. In this case, the hotel workers will most likely try to fix the situation as quickly as possible. They move the tenants to the rooms they ordered at this hotel after making a claim, usually within a couple of days. At the same time, hotel employees can offer their disgruntled guests more compensation in the form of free use of sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach for several days.

Rooms with windows overlooking the sea are more expensive than usual in this hotel. But many experienced tourists do not recommend overpaying for them. The sun in the windows of such rooms burns at this hotel almost the whole day. In the rooms located on the north side of the hotel, even at lunch, tourists can sit quietly in the shade on the balcony, without any discomfort.

The maids are cleaned in the rooms rented in the hotel, judging by the reviews, quite often. At least the hotel rooms look always clean and tidy. The same goes for bedding at the hotel. Maids often change it. A hairdryer at the reception, judging by the reviews, on request is provided most often without any problems.

Dining at the hotel

The dining room of the San Pietro 3 * hotel in Letojanni served just the rave reviews of the guests. Many tourists are advised to take full board at this hotel. The local chefs are prepared, judging by the reviews, very tasty. At the same time, the choice of dishes according to the "buffet" system is simply huge for the residents in the hotel restaurant. Over the entire period of their stay, tourists, as many vacationers note, can try almost all the Italian cuisine in the dining room of the complex.

Tourists who go on excursions can order an early breakfast at this hotel. The service of this tenants of the hotel, of course, is also considered very convenient.





Beach Hotels

The coastline of the island begins almost directly at the very buildings of the San Pietro 3 * hotel (Sicily). The beach at this hotel has its own. Among other things, on the shore there are many sun loungers and umbrellas. Depending on which operator the tourists bought a ticket for staying in this hotel, vacationers have the opportunity to use these elements of the beach infrastructure for only 3 first days, or the entire duration of their stay.

But in any case, the hotel guests do not have to give too large sums for renting sun loungers on the beach. This service is very cheap here. The only thing - there are enough deck chairs and umbrellas on the shore near the hotel, unfortunately, not even for a fee, for all guests. Moreover, according to reviews, beach workers give preference not to foreign tourists, but to Italians staying at a hotel , in the provision of sun loungers .





For those vacationers who did not have enough sun loungers on the beach, the guard makes pretty comfortable chairs. Sitting on them, according to many tourists, is very convenient. But with comfort beds they, of course, can not be compared.

Covered coastline near the hotel Hotel San Pietro 3 * sand and pebbles. Big boulders are not here. But many vacationers advise to buy special slippers for relaxing on the beach of the hotel. Pebbles and sand on the shore in this place are very hot.





Entrance to the sea opposite the hotel, tourists find it quite convenient. But the hotel beach, unfortunately, has one very serious drawback. The fact is that a lot of burning jellyfish usually swim in the sea near the hotel. Therefore, swimming on the beach of this complex should be careful. Sometimes jellyfish go away for a few days, but then always come back.

Useful advice

In case of a local jellyfish burn, experienced tourists recommend that beginners on this beach approach the guard. He has an ointment that almost instantly removes all the unpleasant sensations.
