Hotel Aiyara Grand Hotel 4 * in Pattaya: description, reviews

Thailand is precisely the place on Earth that is especially popular with young people in love. The warm sea and the sound of the surf, incredibly beautiful sunsets and a pleasant climate - there is everything you need to create a romantic atmosphere. At this amazing resort you can get a lot of new experiences and see new places. Already the first minutes after the landing of the aircraft will present a special, inspired mood.

Thailand has long been a place that many dream of going to. Therefore, all conditions are created there, a huge number of interesting events and entertainments, educational excursions and much more are offered.

To Thailand together

Especially often Thailand is chosen for honeymoon trips and for filming various TV shows about love hearts. This choice is not surprising, because you want to see all the beauty of Thailand with your loved one.

Traveling to this beautiful place together carries not only a romantic character, but also a certain material interest. The thing is that a trip to Thailand for two will cost less than for one. And if there is no second half, then you can safely take your best friend or one of your relatives with you. Any tourist will like the fact that on this trip you can save on accommodation, and spend money on excursions or tasting local food. The main thing is not to go alone, because there should be someone who will share the joy of relaxation, go with you on an excursion and go through some kind of adventure together.

The good news is that there are even more advantageous offers, such as a hot tour. It is such a trip that the budget traveler can afford, and even a family with a small income can afford it. A trip to Thailand for two as part of a last-minute offer can cost a penny compared to tours to other overseas resorts. Of course, this is a successful purchase that will please everyone, but such a purchase has its own characteristics, which consists in the fact that it takes very little time to collect things and complete the rest of the things, usually only one day.

Thailand's Best Resort - Pattaya Hill

Pattaya belongs to those resorts that await tourists all year round. The climate is divided into three seasons, namely:

- rainy season;
- warm time;
- cool time.

It is best to visit this place in cool weather. During this period, the temperature does not exceed +30 degrees. Most tourists travel precisely at this time, because in the hot season it is unbearable stuffy, and in the rainy season - high humidity. In Pattaya - a huge number of beaches. They are in the city and beyond. The water in the sea is always well warmed up, despite the season, so you can swim all year round.

Beach holidays in Pattaya

Each of the beaches has its own zest. Consider some of the most famous:

  • Pattaya Beach. This is one of the most popular beaches, having several advantages over all others. It is located in the very center of the city. In this place it is simply impossible to be bored. Pattaya Beach offers many water activities, such as jet skis, paragliders and other adult rides. There are more restaurants serving local cuisine. There are shops with many souvenirs, clothes and other trifles. There are several entertainment complexes where you can have fun.
  • Jomtien This beach is located outside the city. He is distinguished by his peace. There are no noisy entertainments, but you can have a tasty meal in cozy cafes. A spacious beach area is suitable for a more measured rest, you can come here with children.
  • Wongamat. This beach is located in the northern part of the city. It is significantly different from other beaches, as it has the cleanest water. There are various water rides and places to eat. Already in the late afternoon, everything closes and the beach becomes empty.

Before you go to the resort, you must decide on the place of residence. In Pattaya, there are many different hotels for every taste and budget. Therefore, it is better to make a choice before departure. This article will provide a description and reviews of tourists about the hotel Aiyara Grand Hotel.

Description





The hotel has four stars. It is small in size and therefore very comfortable. The design has an oriental flavor, which adds a highlight to it. From the windows of all rooms - a wonderful view of the sea or beautiful nature. The general style of the Aiyara Grand Hotel (Pattaya) is modern, the rooms are very cozy and comfortable. Here you can feel at home. The atmosphere is conducive to a calm and measured rest. The pride of this hotel is its location, because such a panoramic view can no longer be found. Aiyara Grand Hotel (Pattaya) promotes outdoor activities, and the complex is perfect for business trips. The hotel is located close to the center, nearby there is everything you need. The beach is not long to reach, only 5-7 minutes by car.

The only thing that bothers you is that the hotel complex is not particularly designed for families with children.

Number of rooms





The rooms are incredibly pleasing to the eye. Laconic sophistication reigns here. The apartments have air conditioning, which is necessary in the heat. Panoramic windows with the best view in the city. Also has a TV, minibar, safe. Each guest is offered free slippers and a bathrobe. In the bathroom there is a hairdryer and a necessary set of cosmetics.

On site there is a wonderful cozy restaurant, decorated in bright colors. Thai, Asian and European cuisines are served.

You can enjoy delicious cocktails at the poolside bar. It can even offer special exotic drinks.

There is also a small cafe on site where you can try international dishes.

In your free time there is the opportunity to make a massage that will relax very well, also visit the gym, where a professional trainer will take care of you. For lovers of a healthy lifestyle, the spa can offer a huge range of different health treatments. The hotel has an indoor pool, which can be used at any time of the day or year, as well as an outdoor pool, near which you can soak up the sun.

For children, a small heated water area is provided. Paid babysitting services are also available.

On site there is a tour desk, guests with a car are offered free parking, there is a dry cleaning service, as well as free access to the Internet.

Comments of tourists





The cost of living at the hotel is quite affordable. Therefore, luxurious conditions should not be expected. Nevertheless, Aiyara Grand Hotel 4 * (Pattaya, Thailand) is a worthy option for living in Pattaya.

The hotel benefits from its prime location. The center with local transport can be reached in just ten minutes. You can also easily get to Jomtien. Aiyara Grand Hotel 4 * is located in Pratamnak (Pattaya area). Unlike the center, it does not stink and is not so noisy.

Accommodation

Check in at the hotel in no time. The only thing that can interfere is the language barrier. In Thailand, almost no hotel speaks Russian, but they speak English quite well. Upon check-in, a deposit of $ 100 is required, which is returned upon eviction.

Aiyara Grand Hotel offers spacious, clean rooms with great views. All apartments are freshly renovated with a stylish interior design. For a nominal fee, employees can bring luggage to their rooms.





Since the hotel is new, all the equipment in the rooms and the design itself are also modern. All household appliances are in working condition. The rooms are cleaned regularly. Service is at the highest level.

Beach and Pool

Many tourists prefer to spend their holidays without leaving the hotel. There are two swimming pools, outdoor and indoor. From the first you can admire the beautiful views of the city and the Buddha statue located on the mountain. An indoor pool is on the 3rd floor.





The path to the sea takes only ten minutes on foot. Despite the fact that the hotel provides a free shuttle service to the beach (every day, at 10 a.m.), many tourists prefer to walk to it on their own. After a hearty breakfast, such a walk is what you need!

The beach closest to the hotel is Pattaya Park. It is very clean both on land and in water. The beach is equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas and other amenities. In Thailand, it is very hot, so not everyone likes to fry in the scorching sun. Pools in the hotel in this case are very helpful. Moreover, they are filled with sea water, as well as equipped with sunbeds.

Nutrition Reviews





The food at the hotel is excellent. Many vacationers remember this unique taste for years. Depending on the choice of tour, various food options are offered.

The service in the restaurant is very good. The staff treats customers with special courtesy and helpfulness. But some points can still confuse. Thailand is almost always flooded with the Chinese. Aiyara Grand Hotel also has a lot of them. In the restaurant they create a lot of noise, and it is not very pleasant to eat in such an atmosphere. Moreover, the Chinese are simply not indifferent to our brother, constantly striving to be photographed.

The food was especially delicious for breakfast. Such an abundance of exotic fruits is rarely seen. In the assortment there are always watermelons, melons, papaya and pineapples. They offer a huge number of dishes. Over the entire period of rest, few have managed to try each of them.

You can also enjoy delicious meals outside the hotel. It is better to choose those institutions that visit local. Food is equally tasty everywhere, but in cafes and restaurants designed for tourists, prices are much higher. Within walking distance from the hotel there is a small cozy cafe. It is located on a Russian street, on the way to the sea, behind the market. It serves delicious food for ridiculous money.

Interesting places near the hotel

Near the complex there are shops of world brands, currency exchangers and cafes. By local transport, tuk-tuk can reach the center.

Within walking distance there is a market with an abundance of cheap fruits and souvenirs. In the evening you can buy clothes here.

Everywhere they sell delicious local beer, which costs several times cheaper than in Russia.

Also close to the complex is a Russian street with numerous cafes, restaurants and markets.

Opposite the hotel entrance there is a salon where you can get a great massage.

It is best to change the currency in the cafe "Parrot", which is located on Russian street. There is the most profitable course.

Walking around

Not far from the hotel rises a mountain on which the golden Buddha majestically sits. This place is within walking distance. There is an amazing atmosphere of peace and tranquility. The soul immediately becomes good.

It is also worth a visit to the island of monkeys. These funny animals charge positively.

As a rule, a tour includes a sightseeing tour and a trip to a crocodile farm and a rock garden. For a fee, you should go to the Erawan waterfall or for a walk on the Madagascar sea. It is simply impossible to see all the sights of Pattaya within the framework of one tour.

There are many temple complexes in Thailand. Many tourists say that after their visit, life completely changes. It is filled with meaning, and it becomes easier to cope with numerous life difficulties.

Minuses

Many vacationers of the shortcomings note the huge number of Chinese people living in the hotel. Special buses are designed for them, which bring them in the evening for the night, and in the morning take them on excursions. Buses, however, like the Chinese themselves, create a lot of noise. Tourists strongly recommend that you arrive on time for breakfast, otherwise after your Chinese brother there may be nothing left and you will have to wait until a new meal is prepared. For the Chinese, the Russian tourist is exotic, so they are trying in every possible way to establish a relationship with him, or at least take a picture.

General impressions





Overall, the Aiyara Grand Hotel deserves four stars. It is clean and comfortable. The excellent location of the complex helps to choose it again and again. Within walking distance are all objects of tourist infrastructure (beach, shops, cafes, restaurants, market). The food at the hotel is excellent. The food is hearty, varied and very tasty. There are a lot of interesting places nearby.
