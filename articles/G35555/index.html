Pension "Blue Bay" in the Crimea (Koktebel): description, rooms, reviews

Holidays in Russia are a relevant topic that affects many vacationers who plan to soak up their summer on domestic beaches and resorts. One of the most popular places is Koktebel (Crimea). This place is famous not only for the bright sun and clear sea, but also for cultural and historical sights. In order for the rest to bring a lot of positive emotions, we recommend that you think about the placement option in advance. For example, you can consider the Blue Bay boarding house located on the east coast.

About the resort

Koktebel is a small resort village of urban type located in the eastern part of Crimea on the Black Sea. For tourists, neighboring Feodosia is available, which is only 20 km away. Picturesque natural landscapes are pleasing to the eye. From the east and north, the resort is surrounded by mountains with noticeable rare interspersed vineyards.





As a result of its geographical location, Koktebel (Crimea) has a steppe climate. Summer is very hot with droughts and a lot of bright sun, and winter is mild, but with a high probability of severe frosts. Autumn is long, warm and dry, with a characteristic velvet season. Frequent wind from the sea creates a specific upward flow of air, which is very convenient for gliding, which has been developed in the area since Soviet times.

Nutrition

In order to ensure its guests a comfortable and pleasant stay, the Blue Bay boarding house has everything you need, including your own stationary dining room with a capacity of 510 people. The food system is comprehensive, the menu is composed of dishes of European cuisine, including Russian and Ukrainian with traditional Crimean flavor and features. Vacationers note that daily, fresh, fragrant and tasty pastries of their own production are served at the table. The tables are served by waiters. The operating mode occurs in two shifts with a difference of 30 minutes. Breakfast for the first starts at half past eight in the morning and lasts half an hour, lunch at 13:00, dinner at 19:00.





In addition to the main dining room, all guests can visit paid establishments in the boarding house: Bochka, Blue Bay restaurants, Yolki-Motalki cafe and Dikanka cafe, Kariba bar and Bohemia jazz club, where daily performances are held music groups.

Accommodation prices

In 2016, the Blue Bay boarding house offers a variety of accommodation options that differ in price category. Each vacationer, if desired, will be able to find something suitable. The main thing is not to forget to take care of this in advance. In season, rooms fly apart very quickly. Tourists note that the price largely depends on the month in which you plan your vacation. So, the cheapest vacation will cost in May and September, at which time category 2 rooms will cost 1600 rubles without meals, with breakfast or dinner - 1800 rubles, half board and board - 2000 and 2200 rubles, respectively. In June, prices rise by 300 p., And for July and August by the same amount. The rooms of the first category will cost guests six hundred rubles more than the original, for each accommodation option and with the same price step for months.





The most expensive, of course, are the apartments. Normal accommodation without meals will be from 2800 to 4400 rubles, with breakfast or dinner - from 3000 to 4600 p., Half board - from 3200 to 4800 p. and full board - 3400-5000 rubles.
