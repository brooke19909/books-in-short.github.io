Abbreviations

Having seen the announcement of the sale of the site, most of the population will try to guess what the letters in front of the name "Mozhaisk expanse" mean. By the way, there are a sufficient number of such abbreviations, and it is simply necessary to navigate them. It can be letters LPH, SNT, IZHS, DNT. Those who did not study the issue will immediately decide that IZHS is the coolest of the options, DNT is probably a summer residence, private household plots are something farmer's, and SNT is a horticultural partnership. But no need to guess even after deciphering the abbreviations. Names are only the intended purpose of the construction. The appointment will indicate all the shortcomings, advantages and features of each type of land acquired by the population.

First of all, a category should be indicated for land. If you are interested in your own house and its construction, then only the land of settlements and agricultural purposes will do. In addition, each of the two categories should have notes regarding permitted uses. This is exactly what the above abbreviations are talking about. For example, SNT and DNP can be located on agricultural land, private housing - on the land of settlements, but LPH - anywhere. First, we decrypt:
