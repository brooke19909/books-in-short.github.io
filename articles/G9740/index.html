How to install Cydia and what is it for?

If you are the lucky owner of a device made by Apple, namely Iphone or Ipad, then you must have heard of a procedure like Jailbreak. This procedure makes it possible to significantly expand the capabilities of your device - install the so-called tweaks that provide new features, as well as install free games and applications for which otherwise you would have to pay. In this article, we will explain how to install Cydia, the main application that appears after the JailBreak procedure.

First of all, we consider it our duty to warn readers that the procedure for obtaining full access to the file system of the device is not approved by Apple and deprives the owner of the device of the right to warranty repairs and maintenance. You perform all the procedures described in this article at your own risk.

So how to install Cydia on our i-device? First of all, you will need the device itself with a connection cord and a personal computer. On a personal computer, you need to download and install one of the hacker applications - Absinthe or RedSn0w. After the backup copy is saved and the device is virgin, you can safely launch the downloaded application and press the Jailbreak button - after that the installation of Cydia will begin. The whole process takes about twenty minutes, during which it is best to go to the kitchen to have a coffee at all - the more you look at the black backlit screen of the Iphone or Ipad, the stronger the desire to intervene in the unlocking process, and you absolutely must not do this .

After all the necessary operations are completed, the device will reboot. If everything went well and you were able to install Cydia, then its icon will appear on the first desktop. If there is no icon, try restarting the device - perhaps after that Cydia will appear. If even reboot did not help, most likely some error occurred during installation, and the only way out would be to repeat the entire procedure.

So, we figured out how to install Cydia, but readers are probably wondering what needs to be done next? And then you need to go into the program that appears on the desktop, and then enter the addresses of the main repositories (they can be easily found on the Internet) in the Sources section. As a rule, most users do Jailbreak in order to be able to install hacked copies of paid toys and programs on their i-devices. If you are one of them, then you will need the Installous program, which contains links to the latest versions of most hacked programs. Be prepared for the fact that the download will be made from free file hosting, which means that the download speed will leave much to be desired.
