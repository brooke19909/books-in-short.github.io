What is a running meter?

This concept is used primarily for the sale of rolled materials. Thanks to him, it is possible to significantly simplify the calculations. For example, a person needs to buy 10 square meters of linoleum. In retail outlets, this material is sold in rolls, the width of which is unchanged for each model. So, to get the required 10 sq. m for a roll of 2 m wide, you need to measure only 5 m. Since 2 m * 5 m = 10 sq. m. These 5 are linear meters. In other words, the running meter is equal to the reference value, but taking into account some additional constant value (in this case, the width).

The confusion arises due to the fact that sellers of such furniture products as previously mentioned constant value do not consider the width, but the cost of the material and labor. If the price of a meter is known, and each subsequent one differs only in spatial configuration (while maintaining the same amount of materials), then it is much easier to use the concept of running meter in rough calculations.
