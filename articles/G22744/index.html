Residential complex "New Kholmogorovka" in Kaliningrad: address, reviews

The city of Kaliningrad, a major transport hub, is located in the westernmost part of Russia . There are sea and river ports, an international airport. For several years now, the population of Kaliningrad has been growing steadily. Moreover, there is a constant population growth not only naturally, but also in connection with the choice of numerous citizens of this city as a permanent residence. Kholmogorovka is a village in the Zelenograd urban district, located northwest of Kaliningrad. Zelenogradsk is a resort where you can improve your health with the help of balneotherapy.





A little about Kaliningrad and Kholmogorovka

Kaliningrad is the best and most beautiful city in Russia, very suitable for business development. It is here that many residents of the Far East, Siberia and the northern regions of the country seek to move. The only city cut off from the rest of Russia by other countries. It is surrounded by many lakes, rivers and forests.





The composition of the Zelenograd urban district of the Kaliningrad region includes the village of Kholmogorovka, located northwest of the capital of the region. It was in the specified village that the developer located his project - LCD "New Kholmogorovka". The village itself is small - only about one thousand people. But its location is considered promising for the construction of residential complexes.

Location of the residential complex

It is impossible to imagine the best place for the construction of the residential complex "New Kholmogorovka". Located at some distance from the hustle and bustle of the big city and at the same time located within the transport accessibility of the residential complex - this is what the developer can offer its residents.





The address of the residential complex is Kholmogorovka, Happy Street. Houses with numbers from 1 to 5. Description of the complex

What is the complex "New Kholmogorovka"? The developer of Eurostroy Invest LLC has designed a large-scale project involving the construction of 24 four-story residential buildings in the above village. This is a whole residential quarter in which urban conditions will be combined with life in a country house. The construction of houses with such a low number of floors will allow its residents to feel as if they live in their own country houses or in the country. The number of apartments in the houses is quite small, and they are designed for small families or single residents, since only one-room and two-room apartments are designed. The largest area does not exceed 64 square meters. The remaining apartments in Kaliningrad are also mostly small due to the originality of the housing stock.





Finishing and improvement

The developer decorates the house with modern building materials, allowing residents of the residential complex ‚ÄúNew Kholmogorovka‚Äù to forget about bad weather conditions and enjoy a comfortable life. The roof of the houses is covered with metal tiles. The windows and loggias available in each apartment are glazed with double-chamber metal-plastic double-glazed windows. The floor is insulated with polystyrene, which also has excellent soundproofing properties.

Each apartment is equipped with an automatic gas boiler, allowing you to heat it at any time. To account for the consumed water, gas, electricity, electricity meters are installed. The developer also foresaw the need to supply residents with clean drinking water, for which three deep wells with a preliminary water treatment system are equipped.

External infrastructure

The location of the residential complex near the city allows its residents to use the entire rich urban infrastructure. The construction of small-sized apartments suggests that families with one child (maximum two children) or single citizens will become residents of Novaya Kholmogorovka in Kaliningrad. A kindergarten will be built within the complex, and older children will have to get to the nearest village - Chkalovsk, located at a distance of slightly more than three kilometers from the complex.

As for shops, pharmacies and other objects of social infrastructure, the most basic of them (shops, a post office, a pharmacy) are available in the village of Kholmogorovka. And the rest - in Chkalovsk or Kaliningrad itself.
