What is the sea in Alanya? Sea Holidays in Alanya

One of the most popular holiday destinations in Turkey is Alanya. What sea is located here? This question is asked by most tourists who are planning to spend their holidays here. We will answer right away: this is the Mediterranean Sea. Arriving here, you will find yourself in beautiful places that will leave a lasting impression on anyone.

Alanya - tourist center of Turkey





So, you are going to go on vacation to Alanya. Which sea is here, you already know. What else do you need to know in order to choose this particular resort?

Several factors speak directly in favor of Alanya. One of the key is that it is here that one of the largest tourist centers of this Asian country is located. Moreover, it is interesting that this was not always the case. As early as the beginning of the 20th century, this region was considered exclusively agricultural. Local residents were actively engaged in agriculture and did not think about crowds of tourists.

Basically, they all lived through the cultivation of various citrus fruits and bananas. This region acquired the status of an international tourist center only in the 1950s. A great role in this was played by a good location and healing local air.

Moreover, it was at this time that Alania itself received the status of a city of international heritage. On what sea this resort is located, soon all beach lovers found out .

Today we can say with confidence that all this was done not in vain. Here, not only beautiful sandy beaches, but also a rich history that can amaze the most demanding tourist. Indeed, in these places people lived in ancient times.

Alanya Coast





Those who have never been to this resort may not know much about Alanya. Which sea - Black or Mediterranean, often they ask just such a question. The fact that Turkey itself is washed immediately by four seas may confuse them. It is Mediterranean, Black, Aegean and Marmara.

But at the same time, all the main resorts of the country are concentrated only on two seas - the Aegean and the Mediterranean.

Mediterranean Sea





The name of the sea in Alanya is Mediterranean. Beaches in this region are scattered all over the coast. It, in turn, by its outlines strongly resembles a horseshoe. So many people think that rest in these places brings happiness.

It is especially nice to relax on small and cozy bays, which are a huge number in these places. The water in the Mediterranean is clear and deep blue, resembling a sapphire gem.

Two types of beaches - pebble and sand. At the same time, the owners of some hotels have a private part of the beach. On it they create an artificial sandy beach.

Climate in Alanya





The climate in Alanya has a pleasant and long rest. Holidays at sea here are especially pleasant because the water temperature is most often moderate. This is a positive effect on the state of the whole human body.

It is believed that such a vacation is especially useful for those who suffer from cardiovascular diseases, as well as experiencing problems with the nervous system and lungs. Here they are guaranteed to improve their health. It is also the best vacation for women who undergo rehabilitation after childbirth.

In general, comfortable sandy beaches are best suited for a good family vacation. Therefore, so many parents with children go to Alanya. What is the sea here, their sons and daughters know from the first years of life. Resting in these places with young children is absolutely safe. Unlike Egypt itself, where stingrays and jellyfish can be found in the coastal strip, there are almost never dangerous marine inhabitants near the coast. But a lot of starfish, sea dogs and sea urchins.

The entrance to the sea is convenient, and the beaches are clean, the sand is literally transparent.

Popular holiday destinations





Tourists have several favorite places to stay in Alanya. What sea in these places, they already know, so they come back again and again.

One of the most famous beaches is called Cleopatra. Every year he receives the Blue Flag - an international award for the cleanliness of the coast. Its total length is about two kilometers.

Equally popular are Kekubat, Portacal and Damtalash. These are the most exquisite beaches of this Turkish region.

If you are determined to go here, then remember that Alanya is intended exclusively for a beach holiday. It is far enough from sightseeing routes and memorable places, so going to such trips from the hotel will be too difficult. Especially with children.

In addition, the resort is on one side closed by mountains. Therefore, in the summer it is very hot. In the center of the city itself the effect of a hot pan is created. So if you do not tolerate the heat, then you should go to another place.

Weather in Alanya





The holiday season in Alanya is very long. It begins in mid-April and ends only at the very end of November.

On the coast - a classic Mediterranean climate. In the daytime, the thermometer rises to +30, the water temperature is at least +20 degrees. In this case, the temperature drops significantly at night.

The hottest time is from June to August. In the shade, usually +40 degrees, and the water warms up to + 23-27.

But even in the autumn months it does not get very cold here. In October, the sea in Alanya warms up well too, at least 20 degrees Celsius.

Types of entertainment

In addition to beach holidays, there is something else to entertain in Alanya. A large number of professional animators always work for couples and parents with young children. Separate delight in the smallest is caused by water attractions, so be sure to plan a trip to the water park. There are plenty of them in Turkey.

Extreme sports enthusiasts can go diving or scuba diving. So they will receive not only a lot of positive emotions, but also carefully get acquainted with the diverse underwater world of the Mediterranean Sea.

If you want a thrill, then at your service are flying with a parachute above the surface of the sea and water skiing.

Those who prefer a calm and measured rest, cruises on comfortable yachts are suitable.

It is definitely worth a trip to the dolphinarium. Real sea artists perform here daily - sea lions and seals, as well as dolphins.

One of the most striking entertainments in this resort is unforgettable foam parties on yachts. They will not leave anyone indifferent. As a rule, they begin shortly after sunset and continue until the morning. Everyone takes part in fun contests, having fun and swimming right on the high seas.

Now you can be sure that rest in this Turkish region is suitable for almost any, even the most demanding tourist. He will give a great mood for the coming year, until your next vacation, which you may spend again in Turkey. And you will not regret it ...
