Healthy Food Helper - Mulinex Yogurt Maker

Yogurt is deservedly popular as one of the most useful sour-milk products. Pure bacteria cultures ferment heat-treated milk - and thus yogurt is obtained. The beneficial properties of this product were known many centuries ago. For modern yogurt, sourdough from a Bulgarian stick and thermophilic streptococcus is used.

What is the benefit of yogurt?

  • High nutritional value, because dairy products contain enzymes, minerals, vitamins, valuable proteins and fats.
  • Healing effect on the work of the stomach and intestines: stimulation of digestion, suppression of the pathogenic flora, normalization of acid-base balance.
  • Immunostimulating effect, participation in the body's production of folic acid and B vitamins, lowering cholesterol, neutralizing and eliminating toxins.

In addition, yogurts are widely used as a treatment for dysbiosis and allergies.

In stores you can find a very large selection of this product - with and without additives, fat-free, sugar-free, with a different percentage of fat content. Always only one thing is alarming: natural yogurt can be stored for no more than 7-10 days, and on store packages are terms of three weeks or more. This means that the beneficial properties of yogurt are almost lost due to the presence of preservatives, thickeners and the like chemistry.

Home appliance manufacturers have already made sure yogurt can be easily cooked at home. Yogurt mulinex does a great job with this process. What are the benefits of self-cooking yogurt?

Firstly, homemade yogurt will contain only natural ingredients, without any preservatives, dyes, thickeners and other things. The quality of the finished product is the main plus.

Secondly, in such a yogurt, beneficial bacteria are really alive, which makes the product extremely useful.

Well, and thirdly, homemade yogurt is much cheaper than any store.

Do not forget that sour milk and a natural fermented milk product are two different things. Milk can easily sour due to the large number of pathogenic bacteria. And then it becomes uniquely harmful. Therefore, milk that has turned sour and curdled in the refrigerator should not be called curdled milk and eaten at all. Better to pour and forget about it.

Another thing is natural homemade yogurt, prepared in compliance with all technologies. Milk for such yogurt should be boiled or super-pasteurized. Leaven is best taken dry in a pharmacy or supermarket. Milk is fermented at a certain temperature, so that bacteria can multiply and not die.

So, to prepare healthy yogurt you will need:

  • milk - boiled and cooled;
  • culture of special bacteria;
  • yogurt maker.
