Sunny Turkey: direct flight from Nizhny Novgorod

Holidays in the Mediterranean resorts have always been especially loved by the inhabitants of Nizhny Novgorod. But travelers faced some difficulties: they had to lose either money, or time, or both. In 2014, this problem was solved. Some travel agencies (the Eurotour company, in particular) began to offer their customers direct flights for holidays from Nizhny Novgorod to Turkey. Travel companies are doing everything to ensure that the vacation was unforgettable, and nothing overshadowed it. A ticket can be freely purchased just a couple of days before departure. Those who wish to make a holiday flight from Nizhny Novgorod to Turkey may have a question about which airlines carry passengers. The flight is made by Transaero, Orenburg Airlines, Tatarstan, Ural Airlines, and Nord Wind airliners. These reliable companies will ensure a comfortable and safe journey. You can choose any length of stay in Turkey, from two to fourteen nights. If you plan to stay in the country for more than fourteen nights, then a direct flight will be calculated individually.



Flight schedule

As for the schedule, flights are operated daily, sometimes twice a day. If you want to go to conquer the savage, say, Ankara, then round-trip tickets will cost you from forty to seventy thousand rubles (including transfers).

Firstly, it is a country with a rich history and culture. You will immediately plunge into the unusual atmosphere of the East. Russian-speaking guides will conduct fascinating and informative tours of the ancient cities.

Hotels of any level include a standard set of services.
