Popular hotels in Bobruisk

The first mention of Bobruisk dates from 1387. In this city you can see a number of attractions, for example, a monument to soldiers who did not return from the war, fortifications, you can see the territory of the former fortress. Thanks to the sights, Bobruisk is a city worth visiting. The most important question: where to stay? Naturally, these are Bobruisk hotels. Consider the most popular and best of them.





"Tourist"

By right, the first place is occupied by the Tourist Hotel. Bobruisk provides an excellent opportunity to relax on the banks of the Berezina River, and just a few minutes walk from the center. The view from the window is beautiful and unforgettable. The surrounding nature alone is worth it to stay here.

What does the hotel offer its customers? Tourists can enjoy SPA-procedures, as well as spend evening leisure in the gym or billiard rooms. There is also a swimming pool where you can swim.
