The technique of knitting loin mesh



Surely you saw beautiful products made in the style of imitation lace "fillet". Here empty and filled cells alternate, creating a play of light and shadow. Tablecloths, stoles and tops made in this technique look pretty pretty. In order to crochet blouses for the summer to fulfill a sirloin net, we will consider the basic methods of knitting it.

The basics of sirloin knitting

This technique uses crochet techniques such as an air loop and a stitch with

one or more crochets. To perform an empty cage, alternate them, that is, make a double crochet, then knit air loops (the number of which depends on the density of the loin net), then again connect the crochet. In that case, if according to the scheme it is necessary to fill the cage, then instead of air loops we knit crocheted columns. It is also possible to fill the net even after the product is connected, while the cell is filled with a needle and thread of the same color as the main fabric. The knitting of the mesh is double-sided, that is, after completing one row, we knit and perform the second strip. If crocheting blouses for the summer in a circle (tubular knitting), then each even row will have to be knitted in the opposite direction (on the wrong side of the work), along with this, at the beginning of the row, 3 air loops must be knitted (for lifting).
