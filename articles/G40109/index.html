Sights of Ulyanovsk: monuments, museums and parks

Ulyanovsk is a cultural and historical city built on seven winds. This large administrative center flourishes and grows annually. It is impossible not to admire and not to love - wide and clean avenues bring us back to the past, and chic parks and protected areas are eye-catching. Although there are many unique cities and countries on earth, Ulyanovsk is special.





With special hospitality and friendliness he meets his guests, without concealing anything and displaying his property. The sights of Ulyanovsk are worthy of separate words of praise, of which there are a lot in the city and they are all amazing. There are still low roofs and narrow streets from the old Simbirsk, but today modern Ulyanovsk is a cultural center and a large industrial city.

Being on the embankment, you can watch the bewitching panorama opening before you on the Volga river and other city heritage. If this is your first visit to this amazingly beautiful place, we recommend starting your acquaintance with the central part, where an ancient building with amazing architecture has been preserved. A map of Ulyanovsk will help you find the way to the object of interest. We bring to your attention the most outstanding buildings, monuments, museums and parks for the excursion route.

Lenin Memorial

The research monument and the cultural and historical center of the city. It was erected back in 1970 in honor of the 100th anniversary of Vladimir Ilyich. Every year, more than five hundred thousand people from abroad and Russia come here to personally see this important historically unique object. The complex itself is built in the form of a massive square with a picturesque courtyard, inside of which are concentrated museum exhibits related to the life of Ulyanov (Lenin).





The construction of the Memorial stands on 50 supporting columns, which creates the illusion of lightness and airiness. Outside the complex is faced with snow-white marble, the walls are covered with smalt and marble mosaics. Such a solemn architectural ensemble fascinates and causes a feeling of delight. In the courtyard of the Memorial you will see the Pribylovskaya cottage - this is a two-story brick building, inside which there is a unique exhibition of gifts from foreign tourists.

Near the complex is the third wooden building, once owned by Zharkova. It was restored more than once, and now a museum is opened here, where objects of the family of the Soviet leader are collected. The great monuments of Ulyanovsk are truly unique, they conceal the spirit of patriotism and historical value.

Alexandrovsky park

This oasis of freshness is located in the city center. The park was built with the help of a local construction company and has become the main recreation area for local residents. The park is under video surveillance for the safety of tourists. On its vast territory there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay. In warm time, water slides, pools, artificial beaches, numerous fountains, playgrounds and entertainment facilities open. People skate and skate here until late at night.





In winter, an ice rink opens on the lake. Alexander Park is a fabulous town with its own infrastructure, picturesque nature, sculptures and wonderful alleys. In the evening, stunning panoramas open, the park is lit by numerous electric flashlights. A comfortable hotel for guests of the city with its own solarium, SPA center and conference rooms was erected on its territory. Near the park are concentrated historical monuments of Ulyanovsk and shopping areas.

Karamzinsky Square

It welcomes guests all year round. This is an amazingly beautiful square, designed by the famous architect Lyubimov in 1866. A green zone with neat paved paths, relict bushes and flowering trees that give the place a special charm and color. Some plantations are already several centuries old. In 1995, the square was officially made a natural monument. Ulyanovsk can rightfully be proud of this magnificence.





With the beginning of spring, a lilac blooms in the park, its aroma intoxicates ... In summer, the place turns into a shady alley of shrubs and trees, and in winter it sparkles with dazzling white snow and sparkles with frost crystals. Against the backdrop of this magnificence stands Clio's muse of dark red granite stone. The bust of Karamzin decorates this place.

Central leisure park "40 years of the Komsomol"





The mass of reserved places is famous for the city of Ulyanovsk. The sights of the city are fascinating at first sight. One of such places is the pine-broad-leaved forest on the Kuibyshev reservoir. Attracts tourists with the indescribable beauty of wildlife. An artificially created corner of nature is distinguished by silence, purity and an exciting vacation. In winter, you can ski and sled, and in summer swim in the Volga River and bask in the gentle sun.

In the park there are plants that are listed in the Red Book: comb keleria, Robert geranium, middle grushanka and mesaceous goatskin. In addition, about 380 species of insects and 58 species of birds live on the territory: urobophobia, small flycatcher, scallop-throat, corolla, spotted woodpecker, white-tailed eagle. There are also many birds of prey: a goshawk, a black kite, a chiglok, a quail.

Interesting sights of Ulyanovsk: monuments and buildings

Arriving in this administrative city, do not forget to visit the monument to Karamzin, which was built on the donations of local residents. The height of the monument is 8.40 meters. The second asset is the Obelisk of Glory, was erected in honor of the memory of those killed in the Second World War. The construction fascinates with its beauty and attracts people's eyes. A five-pointed star rises above the Obelisk , and the bottom of the sculpture is crowned with the names of the dead soldiers.

It is interesting to see the Annunciation, Vladimir, Mikhailo-Arkhangelsk and All-Saints churches, the Resurrection Church.

Historical and Memorial Preservation Zone





The museum is directly in the open air, this place is magnified. On 174 hectares of land a whole ensemble of architectural structures and monuments was built. All this splendor is located in the Leninsky district of Ulyanovsk. The main purpose of his creation is to recreate and preserve the appearance of Simbirsk. And this, I must say, worked out. Guests and residents can watch the atmosphere of the XIX century, immerse themselves in the provincial history of the ancient city and take a walk among the old houses.





Yulovsky Pond

The best sights of Ulyanovsk are formed on the basis of numerous customer reviews. A lot of positive exclamations were received by Yulovsky Pond, which was created a hundred years ago and is now a real decoration of the region. During its existence, the conservation area has been enriched with vegetation and wildlife. This place is particularly attractive with white water lilies floating on the mirror surface of water.

A cattail and reeds surround the shore of the pond: vegetation prevents pollution and is a natural biological filter. In these thickets nests and waterfowl hatch their chicks . Flora is rich in various shrubs and trees. In the vicinity of the reserve there are insects (bumblebees, dragonflies, ants, butterflies, woodcutter beetles).
