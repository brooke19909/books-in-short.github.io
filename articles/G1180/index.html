Good Line: reviews. mobile connection

It is no secret that calls abroad are served in roaming mode. It means that the subscriber does not use the services of his operator, but a foreign company (through the intermediary of his provider) to communicate with the number he needs. Due to this, the cost of calls, messages and Internet abroad is very different from the prices relevant on the domestic market.





Special rates

In this regard, mobile providers often hold various promotions and provide a discount on communication with other countries, trying to choose the most affordable tariff for their subscriber. However, this is not always possible to do - often users still cannot afford the costs that are imposed by companies. Even the various options for working in only one direction, for any particular country, cannot be significantly cheaper due to the presence of two mobile operators - yours and foreign.

Alternative

If a user cannot afford to spend so much on mobile communications while abroad, he has several solutions to this situation. The first is simply to stop using mobile phones. Now there are a lot of Internet services that allow you to communicate with each other. Most of them are free. But communication through the network is not always convenient, and sometimes it becomes completely impossible.

The second way is to buy a special SIM card of another operator, whose tariffs are more affordable. Often we are talking about some international providers that release their own special SIM cards for travel. Just about one of these we will talk in this article. So if you decide to go somewhere, it will be useful to you.





Special operator

This is about GoodLine. The reviews left about her on the Internet indicate that this is the supplier of the so-called tourist SIM cards. In fact, this is what we mentioned earlier - the opportunity to purchase a dedicated card for communication between countries. Its peculiarity lies in the fact that the final price of services is an order of magnitude lower compared to our largest telecom operators. Even if we compare the prices of various special offers of our ‚Äúordinary‚Äù operators and the cost of calls provided by the GoodLine SIM card, the difference will be huge. Services

In fact, the GoodLine (Russia) business model resembles how other mobile operators work. Here, in particular, they serve customer numbers and support the work of your SIM cards. Here they develop their tariff plans and for a certain cost allocate service packages (minutes, messages, traffic).

In addition to serving private customers, GoodLine (reviews confirm this) also helps companies interested in mobile services. For example, it can be some corporate data packages that apply to several interconnected mobile numbers.

The company also provides other services. For example, there is an online store that sells goods that you may find useful in the future on a trip. They also offer room service, for example, topping up an account.

Reviews
