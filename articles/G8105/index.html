A stylish entry-level gadget is the LG Leon. Reviews about this device, its hardware and software specifications - this is the subject of this article. Well, based on its strengths and weaknesses, recommendations will be given regarding its acquisition.





Who is this gadget for?

The price, hardware and software component of this phone clearly indicate that it refers to entry-level solutions or, as they are also called, budget class. He does not have any highlight, for example, in the form of a curved display. But there is a complete set of the most popular functions to date. The design of this device is typical for a series of mobile phones of this manufacturer for this year. The most interesting color variant of the case is the LG Leon H324 Titan. Reviews highlight him as the most striking and unusual.

Appearance

Most of the front panel of this gadget is a display with a diagonal of 4.5 inches. Above it is a speaker, touch elements and a front camera for video calls. A plastic strip with the manufacturer's logo is displayed under the display. But the control panel is moved to the bottom of the screen and consists of 4 buttons: in addition to the three standard ones, there is also an additional one that provides quick switching between active SIM cards. On the top of the smartphone is an audio port, and on the bottom is MicroUSB. The volume control and lock buttons are displayed on the back cover. There is also a peephole of the main camera and LED backlight for it. As noted earlier, there are three color options for this device: white, titanium and gold. Users highlight the smartphone LG Leon H324 Titan. Reviews indicate its unusual color. Well, the "Gold" option is very similar to last year's "apple" gadget in color.

CPU

Reliable and energy-efficient processor installed in LG Leon. Reviews highlight its low level of performance. There is nothing more to expect from the CPU, introduced two years ago. All this is MT6582 of MediaTek. It includes 4 computing modules of the A7 architecture. When solving the most complex problems, the frequency of each of them can increase to 1.3 GHz. This is quite enough to solve most problems: reading books, listening to music and radio, playing videos, navigating, and so on. Even with the simplest games, he will cope without problems. But the latest generation of 3D games with the most serious requirements for the hardware stuffing of a smartphone on this device will not start: there will not be enough computing power of the CPU.
