Currently, two people manage the company - these are Alexander Nikolaevich Andrianov and Andrey Alexandrovich Birzhin. One is the director of Glorax Development, and the second is the actual owner of the company.

In his student years, he began to engage in business, implementing various projects mainly in the suburbs. In 2001, he founded the company "Geostroydesign", which he led for ten years. She worked in the field of reconstruction and construction of office buildings, residential and commercial real estate, industrial facilities, sports complexes and other structures.

In 2009, he took the position of managing partner of the investment and construction company Tekta Group. Over the two years of his leadership, the company has increased its investment portfolio by about six times.

In 2013, Birzhin began to actively expand his sphere of interests by selling his stake in the Tekta Group, the foundation of the Glorax Capital private equity fund. Now he was directly involved in investing in infrastructure and development projects, as well as in IT startups and industry. In 2014 alone, the total investment amounted to about one billion dollars.
