Car loan Alfa-Bank: conditions and features, interest rate and customer reviews

Alfa-Bank is the largest commercial bank in our country. It was created twenty-seven years ago in Moscow. The main owners of the credit institution are Mikhail Fridman, Alexey Kuzmichev, German Khan and Peter Aven. The latter is also the president of the bank. Currently, Alfa-Bank is a universal credit institution that provides a full range of banking services. It serves more than fifty-five thousand corporate clients and over four million individuals. The credit institution accounts for more than one percent of all deposits of citizens of our country. The Bank has a huge branch network covering most regions of the country. Today, three hundred and forty of its offices are open in our country and abroad.

Alfa-Bank holds top positions in the country's banking sector, which is confirmed by its ratings. In 2009, the financial magazine Euromoney named Alfa-Bank the best bank in our country. The Bank is a Russian credit institution providing capital management services to private and corporate clients. In the ranking of banks in the capital, this bank is in sixth place.

Alfa-Bank provides a wide range of services to individuals, which includes deposits, loans (mortgages, car loans, consumer loans), currency exchange, money transfers and much more. A commercial bank is actively involved in charity work.

What is a car loan?

According to statistics, today every fourth citizen of our country has a car. Seventy-five percent of the population has not yet acquired their own mobile means, which means that the relevance of banking products that facilitate the purchase of cars does not lose its strength. You can buy a car using a car loan. Car loan - this is the money that a client takes from a credit institution in order to purchase a car. A feature of the loan is the registration of a pledge in the form of a car.

The key characteristics of a loan secured by a car is its value, expressed as a percentage, and the repayment period. When contacting the bank, the client needs to carefully study the fees for the services of the credit institution, the fines in case of late payments. A car purchased with a loan can be used, but it cannot be sold, rented, exchanged or donated. The bank stores the title deed of the vehicle.

Car loan today is very easy to arrange. This can be done at the car dealership where the car is purchased by contacting employees representing the interests of commercial banks, or at a lending institution. The disadvantage of obtaining a loan at the bank is the limited offers. A car dealership, as a rule, works with only a few commercial banks, and the cost of a loan with them may be higher than with banks not represented in car dealerships. It is much more profitable to issue a car loan by comparing all the offers presented on the automotive market. After all, a person makes such a large purchase as a car once every three or four years.

Prepayment

Alfa-Auto Bank Card issued by a credit institution after a loan is issued allows you to repay a loan ahead of schedule in any amount. The amount of early cancellation must be at least twenty eight thousand rubles or one thousand dollars. If the client has an amount equal to the remaining debt to the bank on the loan, then he can close the debt completely. Penalty for early repayment of a loan is not charged.

The client simply replenishes his account, and repayment is made automatically at the time of writing off the annuity payment. To make an early repayment, it is necessary to fill out a notification form for this process. The notice must be sent to the credit institution no later than three business days before the date of the next charge-off.

If the client made an early repayment of the loan, then the information service of the credit institution will be informed of the amount of the adjusted loan payment and provide an updated schedule of future interest payments.

Using the early car loan repayment tool in Alfa-Bank, the client adjusts and controls his payments, saving on interest on the loan.

Forfeit for delay

If the borrower, for good reason, was unable to make an annuity payment in a timely manner or made an amount smaller than stipulated by the loan agreement, then he must pay a penalty for late repayment of the loan in the amount of one percent per day on the outstanding balance. The amount of the missing payment must be paid off any next day. The penalty will be charged on the amount of the loan outstanding balance (calculated based on the number of days of delayed payment).
