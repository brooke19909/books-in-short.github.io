Staraya Russa: sights that will return to the past

It is located on the southern shore of the picturesque Lake Ilmen, just a hundred kilometers from Veliky Novgorod. It is generally accepted that the colonizers of Kievan Rus founded the first settlement here in the tenth century. But the first mention of him in the annals appeared only in 1167.
