Nakhimovskoe Lake - a reservoir in the Leningrad Region

In the Leningrad region in the Vyborg district, near the village of Pervomaiskoye, there is a beautiful Nakhimovskoye lake. The initial name of the reservoir is Sula-J√§rvi (translated from Finnish sulla - cormorant, j'rvi - lake). Most likely, it was baptized so because of the cormorants living here. Later, the lake was renamed Nakhimovskoye, according to the name of the school, located on the western shore of the lake. The educational institution, in turn, got its name in honor of military admiral Nakhimov.



Fauna

Nakhimovskoe lake is famous for its beauty, but not only. It is also connected by a river with Lake Gladyshevsky, in which rare fish species live. Therefore, Nakhimovskoe Lake is also a great place for professional fishing. The main inhabitants of the reservoir are bream, perch, ruff, pike, roach, thicket, pike perch.

Lake Nakhimovskoye (Leningrad Region) is located near the fur farm, which is a habitat for minks, foxes and arctic foxes. On its shore you can also meet waterfowl. Thanks to such a diversity of fauna, excellent opportunities for hunting lovers are opened here, who will be able to usefully spend time and receive new trophies.

Flora

These places attract beautiful flora, consisting mainly of coniferous forests. Thanks to the rich flora, the air in this area always remains clean and fresh. You can enjoy the nature of this wonderful land both in summer and in winter. In warm times, forests abound with a variety of mushrooms and berries, and in winter open spaces for those who prefer winter sports. In the cold season, the area is equipped with excellent ski slopes and paths. In summer, you can have fun on a basketball, volleyball or tennis court.



Climate

Nakhimovskoe lake is famous for its coasts. The main feature of the reservoir, which attracts many travelers, is its well-equipped sandy beaches. In summer, the water temperature of the lake reaches 22 degrees, which makes it a favorable place for swimming. The course of the lake is smooth, the banks are gentle, and deep-sea places are found only at a far distance from the shore.



Chalets

Those who wish to visit these beautiful places will be happy to host as their guests camp sites, cottage-type villages, boarding houses and children's camps. Nakhimovskoe Lake has long been a "home" for many institutions.

For a comfortable stay, you can stop the choice on the basis of "Nakhimovskaya". It is conveniently located, located just two kilometers from the highway St. Petersburg - Vyborg. The camp site is open throughout the year, with the exception of April and November. It seats 268 people (184 places - in buildings, 84 places - in summer houses). You can check into double, triple or quadruple rooms. If you are relaxing with a large family or company, you can reserve a brick cottage for up to eight people. It is equipped with a kitchen, several rooms, a shower and a toilet. You can also cook in the dining room on the camp site.

An ideal place to stay in this area is any recreation center. Nakhimovskoe Lake is a unique place where every person, both an adult and a child, can truly relax and spend time with benefit. The recreation centers, cottages and pensions located here are equipped with everything necessary to organize the most interesting leisure and spend unforgettable time in nature, in a beautiful ecologically clean area. We invite you to visit this wonderful land, enjoy the beautiful scenery, and recharge your batteries with energy and vigor of Nakhimov Lake.
