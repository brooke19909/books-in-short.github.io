Where to rest in Udmurtia? What is the best base in Izhevsk?

The unique climatic features, the abundance of ponds and huge forest plantations create all the necessary conditions that are needed in order to adequately spend a vacation. The base of Izhevsk is the best option for a vacation stay.

In the summer season in Udmurtia, the air temperature sometimes reaches +40 ¬∞ C, and the average is considered to be +18 ¬∞ C. Winter in this region pampers with a steady snow cover, which is ideal for lovers of snowboarding, sledding, skiing. In January, a maximum temperature drop occurs. Snow cover lies about 160 days. Its height exceeds 0.5 m.

The forests of this region deserve special attention. Here, both mixed and coniferous (fir, spruce) are found. Tourists are invited to have a great time: hiking and walking (hiking, cycling, horseback riding) through the forest, picking berries and mushrooms, hunting, fishing, beach vacations. There are numerous recreation centers offering a wide range of services. Let's look at the most popular options.

"Rainbow"

Club Hotel "Rainbow" has gained popularity, primarily due to its convenient location. The city can be reached by car in half an hour. Visitors will be able to fully enjoy the clean air, which is filled with aromas of deciduous and coniferous trees, picturesque surroundings.





Insulated cottages are designed for living on two floors, which are installed on the territory of this recreation center (Izhevsk). "Rainbow" provides tourists with a total of 52 rooms. Most of them are equipped according to the standard category, and only four are luxury. Given that the base operates year-round, all rooms are heated using an autonomous system. Ground floor rooms feature underfloor heating. The standard has all the amenities: a bathroom with shower, TV, telephone for intercom, comfortable furniture. Deluxe rooms are designed for two people. Room sizes - 13 square meters. m. Prices start at 1300 rubles.

The administration of this complex has taken care that tourists are not bored at any time of the year. Here you can engage in active games: volleyball, cycling or rollerblading, tennis and so on. In the winter season, skates, skis, snowmobiles become the most popular.

The club-hotel has an excellent restaurant, which seats 230 people. It has three halls (large, medium, small), a bar and a dance floor. The menu is as diverse as possible, prices are reasonable.

The recreation center "Ryabinushka" (Izhevsk)

An inexpensive accommodation option is the Ryabinushka base. It is located near Dulesovo on the banks of the beautiful Kama River. You can come here as a large friendly company, as well as a family with children. For everyone there is the most suitable entertainment. Guests are accommodated in large and small rooms, which are designed for 2-8 people. In the summer season, you can rent panel houses. They are quite inexpensive to rest. The base of Izhevsk is focused on people of different incomes. And this is its huge advantage. The minimum costs that will have to be spent on housing will be about 350 rubles.

The infrastructure in Ryabinushka is well developed. There is a tent, gazebos with barbecue facilities, a sauna, a billiard room, a games area, a sports ground. Upon request, guests can organize fishing on the river.





Elk Island

At a distance of 6 km from Izhevsk, an amazing recreation center called Elk Island was built on the pond of the same name. On its territory grows a dense pine forest. For leisure, a wide range of services is offered, in which each guest will be able to choose the most suitable option for himself. Large companies and just families come here, since it is impossible to find such beauty and clean air in the district.

Rooms recreation center "Elk Island" (Izhevsk) are located in the equipped cottages. There are only two offers for resettlement. From 1000 rub. You can rent a double room, from 700 rubles. - a bedroom. In the latter there is only a bed and a small bedside table.

For entertainment, a karaoke bar, fishing, sauna, billiards are provided. Those who can not live a single day without sports are offered an equipped sports ground.





"Wave"

Where in Udmurtia can I fully spend my vacation? The base of Izhevsk with the beautiful name "Wave" is waiting for guests all year round. It has a convenient location: in the green zone of the city near the Izhevsk Pond. Summer and winter cottages are provided for living here. The presence of banquet rooms allows for special occasions.

In winter, tourists are offered to rent rooms in a brick building. All rooms in it are heated. Facilities on the floor. Capacity from 2 to 5 people. There are also suites and block-type rooms (a bathroom for two rooms). In the summer season, houses without amenities are designed for stays of 2 to 5 people. Minimum prices start at around 870 rubles.

"Friendship"

In the vicinity of Izhevsk there is an inexpensive complex "Friendship". Prices for accommodation start from 350 rubles. Guests can rent rooms with or without amenities. All of them have a capacity of 3 to 5 people. Also on the territory there is a ten-seater general's house, cottages with amenities and VIP-apartments, where the most complete rest will be.





The base of Izhevsk offers its services in organizing celebrations. Guests can use the sauna, games area, sports ground, entertainment park.
