How to take a screenshot on Xiaomi: instructions

In each of the smartphones running the Android operating system, it is possible to take a screenshot of the contents of the desktop. This function is very convenient for those who want to show the screen to another person, explain any actions or just save the necessary image in the gallery. Let's figure out how to take a screenshot on Xiaomi with built-in methods and third-party programs.

Differences of options

Xiaomi smartphone developers have added as many as 6 methods that will allow you to capture the contents of the display. Each of the options is convenient in its own way, so it is advisable to remember them all and use as necessary. To do this, use the instructions provided. With their help, you can easily take a screenshot on Xiaomi Redmi or any other company‚Äôs smartphone running MIUI 7 or 8 version shell.





First method

The first option to create a screenshot can be attributed to most devices on this operating system. The creators decided to leave this function from the classic Android shell. For a screenshot, you must simultaneously hold down the volume down and phone lock keys for several seconds. After that, a message appears on the screen stating that the image was successfully saved. You can find it in the gallery in the corresponding ‚ÄúScreenshots‚Äù folder.

Second method

This option has already been added by the MIUI shell developers themselves. To create a picture, you must simultaneously press the volume down button and the leftmost touch key under the display. The rest of the procedure is identical to the previous one - an alert also appears on the screen, and the picture is saved to internal memory. By the way, in the smartphone settings, you can set the image parameters to take a screenshot on Xiaomi Redmi 4 as high quality as possible.

No buttons

This time we do not need the physical keys of the phone. To take a picture, you need to use the notification curtain. You can access it by swiping down from the top of the screen. In the table with all the buttons, find the Screenshot item and click on it. The phone will take a snapshot of the application or page in the browser in which you opened this curtain.

Widgets

To take a screenshot on Xiaomi using this method, you need to use the widget panel. To do this, go to the desktop and hold down the left touch button until the bottom menu appears. Next, open the "Widgets", find the Screenshot and drag the icon to the desktop. Now when you click on it you will create a screenshot.





Fifth method

This and the last screenshot creation options were added by developers only in MIUI 8. You can check your shell version in the phone settings. In the extreme version of the shell, the Quick Ball assistant appeared, which can be enabled in the settings. Now, when you click on an invisible button on the screen, you will call up a menu with the ability to record video, take a picture, etc. Choose a circle with your finger and release. So you can take a screenshot on Xiaomi in new firmware versions.

Three fingers

This option involves the use of three fingers of the user. You just have to hold one hand with three fingers from the top edge of the display down, after which the image from the desktop instantly goes to the gallery, as evidenced by the animation in the upper right corner of the display.





Third party programs

If you are not satisfied with the standard functionality, you can always use third-party software to take a screenshot on Xiaomi. However, most of these applications require root access. Also, almost all of them have a graphical interface on the screen, which interferes with comfortable work with the smartphone. You can find a list of these programs in the Play Market store upon request.
