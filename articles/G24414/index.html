Karelia in winter: attractions, recreation, nature and reviews

Rest in Karelia in winter is popular not only among Russians, but also among foreign tourists. Here you can see unique architectural monuments, enjoy the virgin nature, engage in active sports, as well as spend time comfortably with your family.



Winter hunting

Rest in Karelia in the winter, many guests prefer to spend actively and even extremely. So, hunting has long been a famous tourist "brand" of this region. Everyone can check the strength of their nerves and go to the local forests for a bird or game. At the same time, tourists should be aware that a license is required for hunting martens, beavers, bears, black grouse, capercaillie and wild boars. Obtaining the necessary permission in the regional divisions of the State Committee of the Republic of Kazakhstan in public places or organizations for which their own hunting grounds are assigned is not so simple, since the number of licenses is limited. But hunting for hazel grouse, white hare, squirrel, duck and a number of other animals is possible without special permission.





If you are not a professional in this type of activity, then use the services of a specialist who will accompany you and save you from many difficulties and inconveniences. If you wish, you can purchase a special tour, in which you will be provided with comfortable accommodation in a hunting lodge, meals, as well as the services of a professional huntsman guide.

Fishing in Karelia

Karelia in winter is a great vacation spot for ice fishing enthusiasts. In the central and northern regions of the region there are many rivers and rapids where you can catch grayling, salmon and whitefish. There are many lakes in the north of Karelia, where Piaozero is especially popular. Landmarks of the region

At any time of the year, tourists strive to visit the most famous places of Karelia. Therefore, in the winter season, you can also admire the beautiful lakes, forests and other picturesque places of this region. The most famous natural attractions of Karelia are, of course, Ladoga and Onega lakes, the beauty of which is difficult to convey even to professional photographers. The next places that almost all tourists visit are Solovki, Valaam and Kizhi. Here you can see architectural monuments, museums, cathedrals and chapels with a long history. The list of local attractions will be incomplete without mentioning the old Russian northern cities. We are talking about Olonets, Petrozavodsk, Kemi and many other settlements.





The average cost of a walking tour of Petrozavodsk is 200 rubles, and a bus tour is 350 rubles.

Tours in Karelia

If you want to spend your winter vacation excitingly, feel free to go to Karelia. Here you can draw up a travel program, the impressions of which will last a lifetime. Someone prefers bus excursions, someone moves in their own car. However, experts say that to see all the beauty of this region is impossible in the usual bustle. Stop and try to escape from everyday activities, admire the nature and beauty of the northern cities. In such a journey, you will just need a guide who loves his homeland and knows his story perfectly.





For those who want to see all the sights in a short time, guides make up a special program. It provides for movement in different modes of transport and different ways of spending leisure time. Such a two-day tour will cost about 12,000 rubles per person. There is also a very special group of excursions - extreme tours, where instead of the usual "all inclusive" you get an SUV, tent, fishing rod, folding knife and bowler.

Rest in the winter in Karelia with children

If you decide to spend the winter holidays with your family, then it is in Karelia that you will get a lot of positive impressions. If you are going to Karelia in winter with your children, you can be sure that you will find a wonderful hotel, excursions that are interesting for all ages, as well as fascinating winter fun. It will be interesting for kids to ride dog and reindeer sledges, go skiing or build a real snow fortress. Many hotels and recreation centers of Karelia offer families to celebrate New Year and Christmas at their place. They are developing a special program that will appeal to all members of your family. Here you will be waited by Santa Claus and the Snow Maiden, funny contests and attractions, as well as a lot of gifts and pleasant surprises.

Karelia in winter: reviews of tourists

Those who visited this wonderful region in the winter are usually full of new and vivid impressions. Someone with enthusiasm talks about outdoor activities, someone is delighted with the beauty of the northern nature, and someone received a lot of pleasure from the rich excursion program. However, almost all guests of Karelia agree on one thing - they dream of returning here more than once.
