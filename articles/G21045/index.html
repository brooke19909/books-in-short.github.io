How to make an iron man costume?

The costume of the iron man Tony Stark, the character of many animated series, cartoons and three wonderful films, is gradually becoming extremely popular among cosplayers and among children. What child does not dream of such a wonderful outfit that not only makes him almost invulnerable, but also allows him to fly? Of course, it is impossible to assemble a real costume for obvious reasons, but children's imagination surprisingly well compensates for all the shortcomings if the iron man's costume becomes his own. On how to assemble it yourself, read on!

We assemble in parts



In order to make your child an iron man costume that is as similar as possible to a real one, it is enough to show a little imagination and put a little work and free time into it. It is best to use aluminum for these purposes. It is lightweight, durable, bends well, and you do not need many tools in order to process it. To give the costume a more realistic appearance, it is recommended to paint parts of it with spray cans of yellow and red paint. Slots for the eyes can be closed inside with glasses from dark glasses. In addition, you will need any yellow turtleneck with a neck and long sleeves.

  1. The helmet of the costume is made of two elements: the occipital part and the visor. Using these photos, try to observe the proportions of the child‚Äôs head as accurately as possible, while eliminating the stitching parts of the metal. A good knitting helmet for you will be the scheme of a knight's helmet, and in order to protect the child, sharp corners and edges should be covered with a thin layer of silicone or sealant.
  2. Armbands and greaves. Armbands are best made from one solid piece of metal by bending it with a cylinder. The same should be done with the greaves.
  3. Torso. To make a torso, it is completely optional to use a huge piece of metal. It is enough to make the front plate, and everything else will be compensated by a turtleneck with additional elements that the iron man has. The costume can be drawn with a marker around the back plate and the press. Now he is almost ready, and there is very little left!
  4. Gloves and shoes. The costume of the iron man is not only a torso and a head, but also complete tightness. Paint with spray paint all its components, and then do the same with old gloves and sneakers (or sneakers). This will be the final touch in manufacturing. Iron Man's costume is ready!




In general, there are now quite a few professional copycats trying to copy this superhero. Iron man suits are made by them from a wide variety of materials. They can be of any shape and color. There are a lot of cosplay photos of Tony Stark and his wonderful costume! Do not be lazy and give your child a dream, because who of us in childhood did not dream to be a superhero and perform great feats?
