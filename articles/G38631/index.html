Samsung 42-inch TVs - specifications and models

Samsung TVs with a diagonal of 40 inches or more are designed for installation in spacious rooms. Most often, restaurants, supermarkets or large companies buy such devices in order to diversify their visitors' leisure time. Although, sometimes a similar device can be seen at home.

When installing TVs with a large screen diagonal, perhaps the main requirement is the availability of a sufficient distance to the viewer. Not everyone can afford such a purchase. However, the happy owners of a Samsung 42-inch TV are generally delighted with picture and sound quality.

Specifications

Knowing about the specific parameters of a Samsung TV (42 inches), you can be completely confident in the supported widescreen image with aspect ratio for 4K or Full HD. In just a couple of years, these new virtual reality models have become available to a wide range of consumers for home use. Due to the increased matrix size, the viewer will completely plunge into the abyss of the action taking place on the screen, and with a twofold increase in the number of pixels you can see even more. In addition, the 42-inch Samsung TV offers a range of features available for other smart devices of our time.

Among the mentioned characteristics and capabilities, innovative and unique are the ability to access the Internet, an image cleaning system and a recording function for on-air programs. A wired connection is not required - a Wi-Fi connection is supported.

Key Features

Samsung 42-inch plasma TVs are created using modern innovative technologies that help improve image quality:

  • the presence of gesture control and 3D-mode.
  • 4K resolution and Full HD;
  • detail of the picture;
  • increased image clarity;
  • set of smart features;
  • Internet connection;
  • popular connectors in the case.

Samsung Led TV 42 inches

Using the latest technologies to expand the color range in the Samsung UE-42F5300 model has significantly improved the quality of the resulting image and even the transfer of the smallest details. You will surely pay attention to the widest color palette that is displayed on the screen thanks to the Wide Color Enhancer technology.

Built-in powerful dual-core processor Dual Core provides support for multitasking mode and fast image data processing. Thanks to this, you can work with one application and simultaneously download another. In addition, you can quickly browse the web without interrupting the game or switch to another channel with the Recent History function. And with the Allshare function, you can connect the TV to various compatible devices and upload photos, videos or music to your screen from your smartphone, laptop, tablet or camera or camcorder.

The Smart Hub service creates an absolutely fantastic environment for your entertainment. There are so many games, videos, applications and music that you will never get bored.

The 42-inch Samsung TV design successfully combines a narrow frame and a bright, gorgeous panel that will decorate any interior.

Samsung MU6100 TV

Thanks to a fourfold increase in UHD resolution, you can see the image on the screen of this TV model in great detail. Due to the natural brightness and color reproduction, any picture looks better.

Dimming technology splits the screen to optimize color and sharpness into several blocks, which provides excellent contrast.

Sleek glossy case looks good in any room.

A universal remote control will help to control all external devices connected to the TV. And the voice control function allows you to not switch channels manually.

With the Smart View function, by connecting any mobile device to the TV, you can view content on a large screen.

Summary

In a diverse line of other dimensional models, the Samsung 42-inch TV is an advanced modern multimedia device that has a number of different functionalities. The manufacturer has done a lot of work to improve the quality of the image at all stages of the display. This and noise filtering, and signal cleaning system, and improved detail.

As a result, we have a full-fledged smart device that implements most of the standard wishes of consumers and demonstrates the excellent quality of content.
