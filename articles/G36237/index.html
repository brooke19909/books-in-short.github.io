China, about. Hainan: City Link 3 * Hotel. Description of the hotel and reviews of tourists

Hainan Island is an extremely popular resort among Russian tourists. Many of our compatriots choose this place to spend their holidays. The infrastructure on Hainan Island is well developed, and it is very convenient to relax here. Guests of this Chinese resort have the opportunity to visit restaurants, cafes, bars and various kinds of entertainment venues. Well, of course, there are many good hotels on the island. It accepts tourists from Russia, including the rather comfortable Hotel City Link Hotel 3 *. The prices for rooms here are not high, but the hotel offers its guests many amenities.

Location Features

This three-star hotel is located in the city of Sanya, in the very center of the island. The nature in this part of China, according to most tourists, is truly magnificent. Vacationers who decide to choose a hotel in the Celestial Empire City Link, are waiting for the warm gentle sea, the sun, coconut groves and magnificent comfortable beaches. Sanya International Airport is a 20-minute drive away. Most tourists prefer to get to the hotel by taxi. The City Link Hotel provides a shuttle service for a fee.





China, about. Hainan, City Link 3 *: general description

This hotel is a modern beautiful new building. Hotel City Link was built in 1994. Several years ago, rooms were renovated and the facade was restored. This hotel deserved good reviews primarily because it is located very close to the coastline. The beach is just a couple of minutes walk from it. To get to the seashore, guests do not even need to cross the road or railway.

According to most visitors to Hainan Island, City Link Hotel is fully compliant with hotel business standards. Good rest at the sea in China, choosing this hotel, can be both business people and ordinary tourists.

Sanya City Link Hotel 1 * (Sanya) or 3 *?

Some tourist-related sites provide inaccurate information about this hotel, marking it as a one-star hotel. Of course, this hotel is far from the comfort of the rooms and the quality of service to 5 * fashionable establishments. However, you can still relax here very well.

So the name "Sanya City Link Hotel 1 * (Sanya, Hainan, China)" is not entirely correct. The complex of services to its guests this hotel still offers a minimum of three stars.

Room Reviews

In total, City Link Hotel offers its guests 150 comfortable rooms. Each room has a standard set of amenities for a three-star hotel. All rooms are equipped with modern furniture and equipped with the necessary minimum household appliances. In rented rooms, tourists have the opportunity to watch satellite TV and use a direct dial telephone.

In each room, among other things, there are:

  • mini bar;

  • air conditioning;

  • refrigerator;

  • safe;

  • Kettle and coffee maker.

Showers are equipped with all the hotel rooms, without exception. In addition to toiletries, the bathroom has a hairdryer.

The relatively low cost of rooms is what distinguishes the Sanya City Link Hotel 3 * hotel. The prices here are really loyal. Of course, Russian tourists usually come here on trips to China from the operator. Such a tour for two adults for a week costs about 90-95 thousand rubles.

Tourists note, for example, the fact that the floors in this hotel are washed only in conspicuous places, and dust is rarely cleaned at all.

Some rooms at City Link Hotel have pretty decent street views. In the windows of others, only a heavily littered yard is visible. Mount Five Fingers

This attraction is visited by many guests of Sanya City Link 3 * (China, Hainan). On the mountain "Five Fingers" know all the tourists who have ever rested in the resorts of the island. This high hill got its name for its unusual shape. It represents a large mountain with a single base and five sharp peaks. The height of the Five Fingers hill is 1867 meters above sea level. Tourists are allowed to climb only its two peaks. The path to the mountain leads between picturesque rice plantations.





Monkey Island

This attraction is 90 km from Sanya, on the Nanwan Peninsula. You can get to it by the longest cable car in the country, stretched over the bay. In total, about 2500 rare macaques live on this island.

Thermal springs

Almost all guests of Fr. Hainan. In total, in the vicinity of Sanya there are two types of thermal springs - sodium-potassium and radon. The water temperature in different springs can range from 40-70 C. Most sources are well equipped and beautifully decorated. Therefore, bathing in them is very comfortable and pleasant.
