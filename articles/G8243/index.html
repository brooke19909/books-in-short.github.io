Samsung external battery: how to choose a universal device

Owners of powerful smartphones often face the fact that charging on the device ends at the most inopportune moment. Most often, this problem occurs with owners of Samsung products. This is not surprising, because modern gadgets simply have huge functionality, which makes them not just communication tools, but full-fledged multimedia centers. To solve the problem of a dead phone once and for all, just buy an external battery for Samsung or a gadget from any other manufacturer. However, it is recommended that you check with the seller for several important details before purchasing.





What to look for when choosing

Buying the first Samsung external battery you get is not worth it, as any electronics must be selected based on your needs. Among other things, it is important to pay attention to the following characteristics of the device:

Also on sale are Samsung external batteries with additional features. For example, you can purchase a device with special indicators, a digital monitor, etc. There are batteries that work via Wi-Fi.

External Battery Life

External batteries for smartphones are usually characterized by a limited number of full "charges." Depending on the specific manufacturer and model of the device, the battery can be used from 350 to 1000 times.

Should I buy a Chinese power bank

Choosing an external battery for Samsung, some consumers come across for sale devices called Power Bank. The cost of these universal devices is several times lower than standard batteries, however, most often such products are made of their exhausted batteries, which will not last long.





In addition, cheap Chinese "power banks" have a lot of other disadvantages:

  • The buyer always gets a "pig in a poke." How long the device will last is unknown.
  • If you are in the sun for long periods, the power bank may catch fire.
  • The volume of even the best device may not be enough to charge the phone at least half.

In addition, there were many cases where Power Bank exploded right in the pocket of a user's pants.





Therefore, it is better not to risk it and purchase an external battery for Samsung from a trusted manufacturer. Consider the most reliable and high-quality devices, according to customers.
