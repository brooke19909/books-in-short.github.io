How to put a song on an iPhone on a call. Instruction

If you have just become the proud owner of a modern iPhone gadget, then you probably began to worry about how to put a song on an iPhone on a call. And if everything is really so, then let's learn how to do it together. As you already know, in a regular phone everything is done in an elementary and simple way: you select the ringtone you like and after the necessary settings is set as a ringtone. With the iPhone, everything is completely different. Indeed, in order for the incoming call to make itself felt by your favorite composition, you need to work a little.



How to put a song on an iPhone on a call

So, in order for your super-fashionable gadget to sound cool melody, you will need the iPhone itself, usb cable and iTunes program installed on your computer / laptop. You can, of course, use such a utility in the iPhone itself, so as not to resort to connecting to a PC. But this option is not the best, since the iTunes program installed in it requires low financial costs. So, let's first look at how to download a song to an iPhone, and then how to set it as a call.

Course of action

  1. Download iTunes from the official Apple developer site, and then install it on your computer.
  2. Connect iPhone to PC.
  3. Launch iTunes. Now drag all the songs that interest you into the window of this program.
  4. Go to the section labeled "iPhone."
  5. Click on the "Music" tab and check off all the songs that you want to add to your gadget.
  6. Click the "sync" button and wait for the process to complete.
  7. Disconnect iPhone from PC.

How to download ringtones, you now know. Now it remains to learn how to put a song on an iPhone on a call.

Procedure

  1. Use the usb cable to connect your iPhone to your computer.
  2. Launch the iTunes application and move the melody that you want to put on the call into it.
  3. Right-click on the selected song and click on the "Details" line.
  4. In the window that appears, go to the "Options" tab, find the line with the word "End", check the box and mark the end time of the track being played. Save the result by pressing the "OK" key.
  5. Again, right-click on the song and select "Create AAC Version ." If everything is done correctly, then you will see that the second track appeared in the list. Its name will be exactly the same as the main song. The only thing that will be different is the playing time.

Create a ringtone for iPhone

So, in the previous steps, we created the track, that is, we selected a certain passage from the song. It remains only to make this song become a ringtone for the call. To do this, follow a few more steps:

  1. Right-click on the created track and in the drop-down list
    menu, select "Show in Finder." A folder with files opens, in which you will need to select a record with the extension .m4a.
  2. Drag it to your desktop and remove it from iTunes.
  3. Rename, i.e. instead of .m4a format, write .m4r.
  4. Click on the renamed file with the mouse and, holding the button, drag it into iTunes in the "Sounds" tab.
  5. Connect your iPhone to your computer.
  6. Go to the "Sounds" tab, check the box "Synchronize sounds" and select the created ringtone. Click the Apply button.
  7. Disconnect iPhone from computer

That's all, it remains only to enter the sound settings on the phone, and you will see that the ringtone you created appears on the list, which can easily be put on a call. As you can see, this is not so difficult. Now, having a computer, a usb cable and iPhone on hand, you will not have any difficulties with how to put a song on an iPhone on a call.
