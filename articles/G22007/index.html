Pension "Crystal" (Gelendzhik): description, services

The resort-hotel "Crystal" (Gelendzhik) is located near the recreation center "Solar". The space adjacent to the sanatorium is completely planted with trees and shrubs, and the area covers an average of about 5 hectares. The boarding house is buried in thickets of pines brought from the Crimea and Pitsunda. A bay of the city of Gelendzhik was built very close. The construction of the first hotel from this chain dates back to 2008, however, despite its relatively advanced age, the hotel can be safely classified as modern and meets all European standards of the tourism business.

general information





As for the mode of operation, guests are greeted and served by the full board system, which includes three meals a day, free sightseeing trips and medical procedures. Cozy pension "Crystal" (Gelendzhik) is ready to host up to 200 people, and the so-called "velvet" season, as in any city on the sea coast, lasts from May to October.

Great location

What is the undoubted advantage of the hotel complex is its step-by-step access to all the famous places of leisure and infrastructure of the city, one of which can be called the chic Golden Bay water park with many water attractions, adult and children's pools with the functions of heating and deep water purification. This water park is one of the best in Europe.

At a distance of 30 meters from the main residential building of the hotel is a beach area, sprinkled with sand and pebbles and equipped with sun loungers, mattresses, sunbeds, a lifeguard assistance station and many other entertainments. There, without leaving the beach, you can rent water transport and organize a sightseeing tour of the city of Gelendzhik. Among other things, there are also playgrounds for active children's games and cafes with an abundance of alcoholic and non-alcoholic soft drinks.

Wellness





With regard to the medical focus, the boarding house "Crystal" (Gelendzhik) offers its customers to improve the condition of the respiratory, cardiovascular, musculoskeletal and other affected systems. Especially for the implementation of medical manipulations, the institution cooperates with a recreation center called "Solar". In addition to this, the boarding house can open the doors to those who suffer from manifestations of diseases of the digestive system and gastrointestinal tract.

Residence





Crystal (3 * boarding house, Gelendzhik) offers its guests accommodation in several buildings. Features of accommodation in each of them will be described below.

Building number 3 is divided into 7 floors. Each room has a balcony or a loggia, a TV, a fridge, a bathroom with a set of towels and all the necessary hygiene items. The rooms and lobby of the hotel have access to a worldwide network, as well as a plasma TV panel. In addition, the room has a split system and an electric kettle.

Building number 1 has the name "White House" and includes 7 two-room rooms with a European layout:

  • 2 double rooms are located on the left edge of the hotel;
  • 1 room for 4 people with two bedrooms on the right;
  • 4 double rooms, which you must enter from the main entrance.

The central living room has such fittings as a fireplace, upholstered furniture, a bar counter and a plasma TV. In the remaining rooms there is an ironing board, TV, miniature bar and a bathroom with a bath or shower. In the remaining rooms of the cottage the furniture is laid out, and the windows overlook the coast and a private beach. The hotel has its own sauna with an indoor pool, above which there are also living rooms for guests. In the building of the boarding house called Kostroma, the terrace for sunbathing is shared, and all rooms are equipped with wide beds and air conditioning, as well as everything necessary for receiving guests and taking water procedures.

Leisure





"Crystal" (boarding house 3 *, Gelendzhik) is located almost at the embankment, on the territory of which there is a cafe-bar, a playground for children's entertainment and a cozy, moreover, not at all expensive restaurant with European dishes, as well as exotic sea dishes dishes. Movie lovers can visit the cozy cinema, equipped with the most modern sound and projection equipment.

Additional services





For those who come with children, the hotel offers a children's playroom, an outdoor area, as well as babysitting services. Book lovers can satisfy the desire to read by taking a literary work of interest in the existing library. Beautiful women will certainly appreciate the presence in the boarding house of a hairdresser and an office for carrying out manipulations to care for the body and face. If desired, outdoor enthusiasts can play beach volleyball at a special venue.

The full range of services offers its guests a boarding house "Crystal" (Gelendzhik). Reviews of his regular customers indicate that people are treated in a special way here. The quality of service is on top. But the biggest plus is the advantageous location of the hotel.
