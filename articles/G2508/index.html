Hilton Hotel (Kazan): description with photos, infrastructure, reviews

If you are planning a trip to Kazan, then the question will always arise about choosing a hotel for your stay. The modern city offers a wide selection of various establishments. But if you are demanding of conditions, then you should pay attention to the Hilton Hotel in Kazan.

Where is the facility located?

From the very first glance, the Hilton Hotel in Kazan impresses with its luxurious design inside and out. The interiors are fascinating with interesting design solutions.

The hotel is located in the very center of the city within walking distance from the Kazan Kremlin, which is considered one of the UNESCO heritage sites, one of the most unique pearls of the world level. Not far from the hotel complex is the opera house, pedestrian street Petersburg and Bauman, the Kremlin embankment, where you will find numerous cafes and restaurants.

Reviews about the number of rooms

The hotel is able to meet the needs of the most demanding guests. Since the institution opened not so long ago, its apartments and interiors invariably delight guests. The windows of the rooms have a good view of the city. All are well equipped with the latest technology. And yet, despite the status of the hotel, tourists note the presence of certain shortcomings. During large arrivals maids do not always cope with cleaning. Although in general the quality of their work is at their best.

The hotel has air conditioning and heating, thanks to which the optimum temperature is maintained. But even here sometimes malfunctions occur. In the cold season, you have to wait until the apartment heats up. A serious drawback of the institution is the walled-up windows in the rooms. The lack of natural ventilation does not allow the access of clean air.

The hotel provides daily cleaning and replacement of towels, as well as replenishment of drinks and detergents. If you want to have a more presentable view from the window, then book rooms facing the street, not the courtyard. This nuance must be specified in advance, otherwise upon arrival it is not always possible to replace the apartment. The rest of the rooms are worthy of all praise.

General impression of the hotel

According to travelers, the hotel has attentive and nice staff. Settlement and registration is very fast.
