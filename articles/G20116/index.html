Cat pattern. DIY cats: patterns

Need a cat pattern? See samples, read recommendations. The article presents both very simple and complex options. Choose in accordance with the experience and level of your skill, as well as the amount of time that you are willing to spend on creating an original home accessory.





What are soft cats made of fabric

Patterns of soft cats can be very different. There are complex and simple options.

In terms of shape, objects are:

  • flat in the form of a rug;
  • semi-volume;
  • voluminous.

For functional purposes, fabric cats are made as:

  • Stuffed Toys;
  • pillows;
  • souvenirs and decor;
  • large interior objects.

From the point of view of manufacturing technology, one can give the following classification:

  • simplified or monoobjective, when two parts are cut from the fabric, which are sewn along the line of a complex contour;
  • combined or complex, which separately sew paws, head, tail, and then all this is assembled into a single whole.

In appearance (design) , the following options are made:

  • natural, similar to natural;
  • stylized, simplified;
  • cartoon characters.

Materials used

The following describes how cats sew with their own hands. The patterns shown in the illustrations are suitable for any kind of fabric. The choice is determined by what you want to create and what effect you want to receive. You can sew a toy from the shreds that you have available or buy fabric specially. In the second case, proceed from decorativeness, surface texture, quality and cost of fabric.



If you want to create a natural image of a kitten, it is better to use fur or fleecy fabric. For decorative stylized products or funny cartoon characters, any bright shreds are suitable: from chintz and calico to felt and fleece.

How to sew a cat: pattern and work with fabric

If you decide to do needlework and make a soft accessory, you need to do the following:

  1. Any work begins with the choice of an idea. You need to decide what exactly from the list above you want to do.
  2. The cat pattern is taken ready or drawn on paper with your own hands. You can print your favorite layout in the desired scale. Do not pay attention that the picture is not of very good quality. To circle the contours, it is enough. If it is not possible to print at the moment, take the glass, place it neatly on the monitor, circle parts of the pattern. And the third option is to draw a pattern on a sheet according to the pattern yourself.
  3. Cut parts from paper.
  4. Overlay them and fasten them with pins on the fabric.
  5. Circle the outline.
  6. Cut parts from material.
  7. Connect the parts in accordance with the manufacturing scheme.
  8. Stuff the toy with filler.
  9. Sew the hole through which the previous action was performed.

All is ready.

The simplest options





In this way, it is easy to make any thing - from a small souvenir to a pillow.

Print the pattern to the desired scale, cut it out of paper, circle it on a double-folded fabric to get two parts, sew on the wrong side, leaving a hole (preferably below), turn it on the front side, fill it with cotton or synthetic winterizer, carefully sew a hole with a needle.

The cat pattern in the simplest version is a schematic representation of an animal.


Outlines are usually simplified. In the figure above, the cat is made in the shape of a heart. This option is suitable as a soft fabric valentine of any size. A small heart is sewn on top in the form of an applique element.

If you are making a large object, there can be any choice. If the sample is reduced, try to take one where all forms are streamlined and there are no thin narrow details (strips of paws and tail). On a small scale, it will be very difficult to twist them after sewing the seams. For example, a tail 1 cm wide and 8 cm long can be problematic. This should be taken into account when building patterns yourself.
