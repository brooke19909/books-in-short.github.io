General definition

How to calculate profitability? To do this, you need to understand the essence of this indicator. Each enterprise works for profit. It should increase in dynamics. For profit, the company spends certain resources. This can be raw materials, labor, finance, energy, etc. Their number should not exceed the amount of profit. Otherwise, the company operates at a loss.

The less the company spends resources for profit, the better. These are absolute indicators, when compared, you can get data on the performance of the company. For this, the general profitability formula is used . It allows you to determine the relative indicator. How to calculate profitability as a percentage? The resulting coefficient must be multiplied by 100. This results in a percentage.

To calculate the overall profitability, you need to determine the amount of profit of the company, as well as the costs that arose when this financial result was achieved. Information for the calculations is taken from the financial statements of the enterprise. Profitability ratios may vary. They reflect the effectiveness of one or another aspect of the company.

These indicators are considered both in total for the reporting period and by month. It is very important to study their changes in dynamics. Profitability is compared with similar indications of the plan or competing organizations in the industry.

What does the indicator reflect?

If you want to calculate profitability as a percentage, you need to understand what reflects the result. This indicator reflects the amount of resources spent on each ruble profit. Profitability reflects whether the company's funds have been used effectively. If not, profits will not be high enough to cover costs.

Profitability should be studied in dynamics. This allows us to draw conclusions about the real situation at the enterprise. For this, the calculation is carried out for several previous periods. Next, the analyst tracks trends. Profitability must constantly increase. Its decrease indicates an increase in costs or loss of profit.

If adverse factors are identified, measures are being developed that will help eliminate negative trends. Otherwise, in the future, the enterprise may face a lot of difficulties. Getting net profit will be increasingly difficult. Therefore, the profitability of different areas of the company must be considered in the analysis.

Considering how to calculate the profitability of the enterprise, it is worth noting that a number of factors affect this indicator. It depends on the characteristics of production and the external competitive environment. The result obtained is considered not only in dynamics, but also compared with various similar indicators. So, in the course of the analysis, the actual and planned profitability are compared. Also, this indicator must be calculated for competing enterprises. Be sure to consider the ratings of the company, its products over the past few years.

Varieties

How to calculate profitability? Examples of formulas will be discussed later. With their help, you can determine the effectiveness of the use of almost any resource, sources of acquisition or costs. There are several basic formulas for determining profitability. They allow you to determine the effectiveness of use:

  1. Property (assets) - RI. Reflects the efficiency of the use of available resources that have been sent to production.
  2. Equity - RSK. Shows the return on capital that was contributed by the founders when creating the organization.
  3. Productive assets - RPF. Reflects the efficiency of use of inventories, fixed assets of the enterprise.
  4. Cost - RZ. It characterizes the degree of return on current production costs .
  5. Sales - RP. Product Profitability.

Also calculate the cost of one ruble of production and asset turnover. Each of these indicators reflects a specific aspect of the organization.

To perform the correct calculation, you need to know how much of the profit the company receives from the use of certain resources. You need to compare the relevant indicators.

Return on equity

In the analysis, other profitability formulas are also used. How to calculate how effectively the capital of the organization is used? For this, it is necessary to determine the total amount of financial resources that were allocated to the enterprise in the period under review. It is presented as a result of form No. 1.

To perform the calculation of the indicator of capital use, you will need to determine the amount of net profit of the organization after paying all deductions and taxes. This information is presented in form No. 2 on line 2400. The calculation will be as follows:

RK = PE / K x 100, where PE is net profit, K is the average annual cost of the company‚Äôs capital.

If in dynamics this indicator decreases, it is necessary to consider the profitability of own and borrowed capital. The liability structure may be unbalanced. To do this, it is enough to determine the profitability of borrowed capital:

RZK = PE / (DP + KP) x 100, where DP - long-term liabilities (liabilities), KP - short-term (current) liabilities.

If the borrowed capital is not enough, the company should attract paid resources to carry out its activities. If, on the contrary, there are a lot of them, it is necessary to reduce the number of borrowed financial sources. Otherwise, the performance will decrease. This will lead to a decrease in the amount of net profit in future periods.

In accordance with the purpose of the analysis can be considered the profitability of an individual product or total output. The costs that are involved in the calculation is the cost. It can be industrial or complete. In the first case, the cost does not include commerce costs.
