How to make a simple children's crafts out of natural material:

  6. After which we leave the craft to dry for several hours.

Chestnut Frog

Another interesting product is very simple. And the set of necessary materials is minimal:

Making a frog similar to the one shown in the photo presented after the instructions is very simple. It is only necessary:

  5. Then chop off a small piece of red or pink plasticine.

  7. Having prepared the necessary details, you can start assembling the crafts.
  8. To do this, attach the legs, eyes and mouth to the chestnut.
  9. And the charming frog is ready!

It is very simple and fun to make such a children's craft from natural material. Especially for the kids.

Painting "Autumn Time"

  • black ink for drawing;
  • art brush;
  • PVA glue;
  • scissors;
  • cocktail straw;
  • several dried leaves collected from any trees.

How to make autumn crafts from natural materials:

  1. First, we need to take the paper and put it in front of us in an upright position.
  2. Then open a jar of mascara and ‚Äúarm‚Äù with a brush.

  6. Now we turn the sheet so that it lies in front of us in a horizontal position.
