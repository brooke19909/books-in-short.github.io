Canon Ixus 145 Digital Camera Review and Reviews

Canon Ixus 145 is a digital device for creating colorful photographs and shooting short films. The small and compact camera has gained popularity among those who like to travel, sometimes at banquets, and visits interesting places. In addition, the technique can be entrusted to a primary school student.





The Canon Ixus 145 digital camera is designed for shooting in the daytime and in well-lit rooms. It is possible to photograph text, images from paper.

Camera specifications

This model is appreciated for its small dimensions and weight:

  • width - 95 mm;
  • height - 54 mm;
  • thickness - 22 mm;
  • weight with battery - 127 g.

As you can see, these sizes allow you to place the camera not only in a small bag, but also in the pocket of your trousers.

The CCD type matrix of Ixus 145 Canon has a number of pixels of 17 million. Of these, 16 million are working, which practically does not affect the quality of images. The presence of the crop factor (5.62) allows you to take photos and shoot videos from a long distance. You can adjust the ISO sensitivity to configure manually (range 100-1600) or set the automatic mode.

Thanks to the built-in lens with aspherical lenses and 8x optical zoom, you can zoom in on the image. For example, an amateur photographer wants to capture a dove flying at the level of the fifth floor of a residential building. But the goal is not to shoot a bird silhouette, but a detailed image. This camera allows you to do this without distortion. Aperture with a range of F3.2-F6.9 allows for high-quality photo and video shooting in good light. Unfortunately, in dim lighting, noise may appear in the image.





The presence of a digital image stabilizer is very important when photographing without a tripod. The Canon Ixus 145 has a built-in flash that can cover up to 3 meters. In the settings, you can also set the mode to suppress the effect of ‚Äúred eyes‚Äù.

The 2.7-inch LCD screen is a viewfinder and also allows you to view the result of shooting a photo or video.

The camera uses SD, SDXC, and SDHC memory cards of various sizes. It is possible to redirect images (JPEG format) and video (MOV format) to a PC or laptop via a USB cable.

As for the exposure, it is necessary to separately note the exposure parameters, which are 15-1 / 2000 s. This means that the Canon Digital Ixus 145 automatically selects the shooting speed. Unfortunately, changing this option on your own will not work.

Contrast focusing allows you to make clear images. The minimum distance that a photographer can be in relation to the subject is 10 cm. There is face focusing and autofocus highlighting.

The battery of the camera allows you to take about 300 photos, after which you will need to recharge it. The battery capacity is 680 mAh.

Video shooting is carried out with a maximum resolution of 1280x720. The amount of memory for one clip or the total number of small films should not exceed 4 GB. It is possible to use the zoom if necessary.





The kit includes:

  • wrist strap
  • Charger,
  • battery,
  • speaker cable
  • instruction.

Before buying, you should check whether the camera is operable, whether everything is included.

Model Prices

The average price for the Ixus 145 Canon is 4500 rubles. Unfortunately, in 2017, the production of this model ceased, and therefore it may not be on sale in many stores. If you wish, you can find a similar model from Canon or purchase from owners who want to sell it.

Real reviews of people

About Canon Ixus 145 the reviews are mostly positive. Many users note its advantages:

  • easy;
  • compact;
  • convenient built-in menu;
  • clear photos;
  • quickly responds to pressing the shutter button;
  • Beautiful design.

But there are some comments:

  • the case is glossy;
  • cheap plastic;
  • Noises in the photo created in low light and at dusk.

There is another significant drawback - the low battery capacity. Therefore, if you plan to take long shots without the possibility of recharging the battery, it is better to stock up on an extra battery or take another camera with you.

To whom it is intended

Many parents who bought a Canon Ixus 145 for a child were satisfied. Children will easily navigate the settings in the menu. The camera itself is great for capturing events taking place in children's camps, on a hiking trip.

Sometimes Canon Ixus 145 reviews are negative in terms of unjustified expectations. That is, the person who used the SLR camera wanted to see the same quality of images in a simple model.

Recommendations for use

To make the photos perfect, you need to strive for good lighting: bright light in the room, cloudless weather during daylight hours. Do not photograph an object or people so that the light hits directly on the lens.





The longevity of the camera depends on the respect for it. It is recommended to store it in a case or cover in order to preserve its presentation. It is advisable to periodically clean with a special tool the lens, LCD.

The camera will appeal to those who want to learn how to take pictures in order to preserve vivid memories of events.
