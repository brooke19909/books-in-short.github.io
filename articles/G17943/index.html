Leelawadee Boutique Hotel Phuket: overview, description and reviews of tourists

In this short article, we will take a closer look at the popular Leelawadee Boutique Hotel (Phuket), reviews about it, interior design, and much more. Ready? Then we begin!

General Resort Information

The Leelawadee Boutique Hotel 3 * that interests us is located on one of the largest and perhaps the most famous islands of Thailand - Phuket.



Phuket is the largest of the islands in the southwestern part of the kingdom of Thailand, washed by the Andaman Sea, which is part of the Indian Ocean.

Phuket is very popular among vacationers around the world. All categories of tourists will find a vacation for every taste and budget. For affluent VIP-clients, respectable hotel complexes and five-star hotels of world chains are open here. For families with children and couples who prefer a quiet, inexpensive, but comfortable vacation, Phuket has magnificent cozy 3-4 * hotels with a fairly developed infrastructure.

Young people arriving on the island for outdoor activities and sleepless nights will also not be disappointed, since for her there is a great variety of various entertainments.

An ideal holiday destination depends on the particular region of the island. Regions of Phuket are usually divided along the beaches. In total, there are 9 main and many small beaches and coves on the island, which are famous for amazing snow-white sands and washed by the azure-turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean.

Kathu City and Patong Beach

Leelawadee Boutique Hotel is located near the famous city of Kathu, in the immediate vicinity of perhaps the most famous beach. Patong Beach is a kind of island Pattaya.

It has its own Walking Steet (Walking Street), only it is called differently - Bangla Night Street (Bangla Street). It is here that noisy parties are held every night until dawn, suitable for young adventurers. In Patong, a huge variety of strip clubs, brothels, as well as completely harmless massage rooms and spas.



The resort town of Kathu, near which the Leelawadee Boutique Hotel 3 * is located, is located on a beautiful hilly emerald green area. Among the main attractions of this city, it is worth noting amazing waterfalls that are born from tiny mountain streams in the depths of the island during the rainy seasons, and approaching the coast, these quiet kids increase and gain strength, gradually turning into beautiful stormy streams. Also, going down from the hotel to Patong beach, you should not ignore the unique structure in the old part of Kathu - the Chinese temple.

About hotel

Leelawadee Boutique Hotel (Phuket) is perfect for a different type of vacation and for different categories of tourists. Family and romantic couples striving for a calm and measured rest, noisy young people not sitting still will feel comfortable and comfortable here, who put the visit of various excursions at the forefront of the trip, flew to Phuket to travel around it and visit it whenever possible and the nearest islands.

Hotel location



From the international airport about. Phuket to the boutique hotel Leelawadee Boutique Hotel 3 *, which is located in the south of the island, take about 30-35 minutes by transfer. The city center is within walking distance from the hotel. The hotel is located on a hill and the rooms offer breathtaking views of the nature of the island.

Hotel Territory

On the territory of the Leelawadee Boutique Hotel (Phuket) 3 * there is everything you need for a comfortable stay. The hotel's main restaurant serves Thai and European dishes, and there is also a cafe-bar where guests can get a large selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks and light snacks for an additional fee.

Rooms

The Leelawadee Boutique Hotel Phuket offers 37 beautifully spacious rooms. The hotel consists of one four-story building. The building is built on a hill, and, according to tourists, this causes some inconvenience, since the building is located near the cliff, which means a certain lack of light in the rooms.



The hotel, in spite of the fact that there are few rooms, has three types of rooms: Deluxe Garden View, Deluxe Pool View and Studio Pool View. All are decorated in national Thai style and equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. All rooms have balconies with a modern set of furniture. In each room - individual air conditioning, tea and coffee set, minibar (replenishment - for an additional fee), TV, free safe in the room. A bathroom with all the necessary toiletries is also another advantage.

It is worth noting one important point in this hotel: there are no smoking rooms!

In the case of the Leelawadee Boutique Hotel, remoteness from the beach is one of its advantages. Tourists resting in this oasis of tranquility and solitude can freely enjoy the beauties of nature surrounding them, without worrying that their peace will be disturbed.

Sea beach

The Leelawadee Boutique Hotel 3 * (Phuket) does not have its own beach, but the relative proximity to the beaches of Karon Beach and Patong Beach make it attractive for most tourists choosing this region.



Patong Beach is very popular, it is known to many tourists planning their holidays on the shores of Phuket, but really it does not deserve the attention and worship with which it belongs. Just a few years ago, it really was one of the best and most chic beaches on the whole island of Phuket. But, taking several thousand vacationers a day in the tourist season, Patong is losing ground every year.

Tourists vacationing here do not think about cleanliness and about whether after their vacation the uniqueness of one of the most famous beaches of this region will be preserved. This place is becoming more and more polluted every year, which does not add to its exoticism and popularity. Every year more and more articles appear on the Internet with a call to avoid visiting this beach. Now, every tourist who goes on vacation to the island of Thailand, in no self-respecting travel agency, will be advised to relax and sunbathe on Patong Beach.

All our compatriots are recommended to visit the beaches closest to Patong, as there are both amazingly clean sand and a much cleaner sea. And perhaps such recommendations will serve as a salutary action for the disappearing uniqueness of the four-kilometer magnificence of the island of Phuket.

Reviews

Many visitors to this hotel complex respond positively to the quality of the services provided, the sociability of the staff and, of course, deliciously prepared dishes. Some argue that cleaning is carried out irregularly, sometimes staff can wake guests.

Basically, they solve all problems quickly enough. The administration is always ready to help its guests!

A small summary

Tourists planning a vacation on the island of Phuket, and in particular at the Leelawadee Boutique Hotel, reviews are examined especially carefully. Why? Because the hotel is in relative proximity to Patong Beach, which is losing its reputation.



Nevertheless, the Leelawadee Boutique Hotel 3 * (Phuket) has been open for more than a year and confidently holds the position of an excellent institution for all categories of tourists.
