The most reliable phone: what is it?

What is the most reliable phone? This is a very important issue, which is now of interest to many. Previously, this almost did not bother anyone, since simple devices made in the form of a monoblock worked quite stably, rarely giving failures. At the moment, the improvement of mobile phones keeps up with the technological progress, so more and more new devices are appearing. The quality of the "filling" is increasing, the functionality of the devices is expanding, so the question of which is the most reliable phone arises more often, because with the expansion of capabilities, the quality suffers and the service life decreases. Naturally, developers are trying to fit in one package and reliability, and functionality, but it turns out this is not for everyone.



It can take a very long time to figure out how much one model or another of a particular manufacturer is more reliable than others. Some people understand this as just uninterrupted operation, while others also value resistance to mechanical stress. However, these concepts are not always compatible in one device. Recently, the Russian manufacturer Incrudo made a statement that it had created the most reliable phone, released in a limited number - 10 pieces. The device is called Incrudo Phantom. The user is offered a case with a thickness of 3 mm, which is made of a titanium alloy, and the front and back sides are framed by high-strength composite materials coated with ceramics. The weight of the device is very impressive - 230 grams, despite the fact that the latest innovations usually have a mass in the region of 100 grams. The thickness of the device is also considerable, so not everyone will like it.

The rating of the most reliable phones can show that in the first place, according to users, are models of the SonyEricsson line. Most of them are able to work even after swimming, and their resistance to shock is beyond doubt. Another advantage is the response speed, which is difficult to compare even with Nokia devices.



Samsung is also trying to keep up, releasing devices with a high degree of reliability. Active work on the creation of dual SIM phones and smartphones brings excellent results, making this brand more and more popular among users. With each new model, the firmware and technical equipment of the devices become better, therefore their degree of reliability in operation also increases. The Samsung company rises higher and higher in relation to its competitors. It is this brand that released the most reliable phone of 2013 - Samsung Galaxy S4 Active.
