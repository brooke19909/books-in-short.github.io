Visa Gold Credit Card: Reviews, Terms and Features

The development of consumer lending in Russia today has reached a certain level: credit cards (with a lower or a larger credit limit corresponding to customer income) are issued to most consumers of banking products. For example, participating in a salary project, that is, getting salary on the card of this bank, you can get a credit card on a personal offer. The same goes for deposit clients. Sometimes a credit card can be literally imposed (bank employees also have a plan that needs to be implemented). In today's article, we will consider this type of banking service as a Visa Gold credit card. Reviews on this product abound.



About product

For many years he has been a leader among various banking lending programs. Gold version credit card can be serviced using two payment systems: VISA and MasterCard. This card has a sufficiently large credit limit and a long period of preferential refund. In this article we will talk about the features and conditions of use of this banking product, as well as the benefits of the bank.

Terms of registration

Registration of a gold credit card of Sberbank of Russia takes place exclusively on special offers of the bank for regular customers. Such a card has a number of significant advantages and differences from standard loan programs. According to reviews, the conditions of a Sberbank Visa Gold credit card are understandable.

As a rule, they look like this: the maximum limit is up to 600,000 rubles, the interest rate is 25.9%, the grace period in which money back without interest is possible is 50 days, the card is valid for three years, then it happens automatic replacement. A plastic card is issued free of charge and without annual maintenance fee, is protected by an electronic chip. Additional cards for this program are not issued, which is noted by some customers as a minus in the work of the bank.

Visa Gold credit card reviews are considered below.

Customer requirements

It is not enough to be among the regular customers of Sberbank to issue VISA Gold. To receive this personal offer, you must meet the following conditions:

  • age should be from 21 years to 65 years;
  • a citizen must be registered in the region where the banking product is received;
  • total work experience should be more than one year, and in last place - at least six months.

A passport and a certificate of employment of 2-NDFL are provided to the bank, pensioners provide an extract on the amount of pension accrual. This is confirmed by the Visa Gold card reviews.



Decor

Since this card is issued on a personal offer of the bank, its receipt is carried out according to a simplified scheme. As a rule, the bank reports that the card is pre-approved, two months before receipt. This is done in one of two ways: by notification in your account or via SMS. A credit card is issued at the office of Sberbank, this procedure does not take very long.

VISA Gold card replenishment methods

To replenish a credit card, for the convenience of customers, Sberbank has developed several ways:

  1. Through ATMs and branches of Sberbank, you can deposit funds without a commission.
  2. Cashless transfer is possible with the help of Internet banking and personal account.
  3. Through ATMs and payment systems of other organizations, including from an electronic wallet, of course, with payment of interest stipulated by the conditions.

Third-party banks provide for the withdrawal of a commission (up to 5% of the transfer amount) when replenishing the card through their terminals, ATMs or branches, the term for crediting money can be up to three banking days.

Features of the pre-approved credit card of Sberbank VISA Gold





The reviews confirm that the following factors can be mentioned among the features of using a gold card:

  1. Round-the-clock service, which is carried out through the head office of Sberbank.
  2. In the event that you lost your card while being abroad the Russian Federation, an urgent cash withdrawal is possible.
  3. Sberbank subsidiaries located outside the Russian Federation issue cash on the same terms as in Russia.
  4. It is possible to link an account to an electronic wallet.

The availability of additional opportunities to use VISA Gold provides significant advantages over other loan products. The main advantage of any credit card, including a VISA Gold card, according to reviews, is the lack of the need to enter into direct relations with the bank or its representative office in case money is urgently needed. That is, the procedure is greatly simplified: instead of going to the bank, thinking and worrying about issuing a loan, you simply go to the nearest ATM and withdraw money or pay at the store with a card, limited to a credit limit. A credit card can be kept, so to speak, on a "rainy day" without using it. In this case, the bank will not take interest. From this point of view, such a banking product is very convenient to use, but it is necessary to remember some nuances. The preferential limit is 50 days by credit card of Sberbank of Russia Visa Gold. Reviews are presented at the end of the article.

What does it mean? This means that you can use credit funds absolutely free of charge, but you need to pay off the debt in a period not exceeding 50 days. In this case, you use cash, but the bank does not charge interest. It should be remembered only that this provision applies only to those expenses that you carried out by bank transfer. When withdrawing cash from an ATM, other rules apply. In this case, a commission fee of 3% of the withdrawn amount or at least 390 rubles is charged for withdrawing funds. This must be taken into account. In addition, when using a gold card abroad, there is a danger of a later receipt of information on the balance of funds in the account.



As a result, the correction of the balance occurs later than is necessary for timely payment. This is fraught with the accrual of interest, which can turn the minimum amount into a rather large debt. In addition to all this, when using a gold credit card, you should remember about the so-called secondary dangers. These pitfalls are determined by the characteristics of human psychology. As a rule, a person paying with a credit card does not lose cash, they remain in his pocket. There is no feeling that you spent more than you planned, since in fact there is a calculation of virtual money. As a result, you spend a lot more than you planned, so you should think about it before using this banking product and treat it like cash.

Real reviews of owners about Visa Gold from Sberbank can be found in large numbers.

Excess grace period

If it was not possible to repay the loan amount during the grace period, then, unfortunately, this means that the bank will accrue interest. And they will be quite tangible amounts. They must be repaid on time in accordance with the schedule drawn up by the bank, otherwise penalties will come into force, which can increase the amount of payment by an order of magnitude. Care must be taken to ensure that after depositing funds, no debts remain in the account. This can be done at a bank branch, on the website or at an ATM.



Closing a golden card

If you have an unused Sberbank gold credit card, you should be aware of the terms of its service, as they do not change. The bank does not cease to accrue interest if there is a debt on payments. The card must be correctly closed in order to avoid further problems, disputes with the creditor and make life easier, saving money and nerves. In order for the closure to occur correctly, you must first pay off all your debt obligations on this card (the exact amount of debt can be found by calling the phone number on the card or in your personal account on the bank's website). Then you need to submit an application for closing a credit card to the department that issued it. After the application is submitted, the plastic card remains with the manager, who is obliged to destroy it in the presence of the client. Further, the client has the right to receive a certificate of absence of debt to Sberbank. This document may help in case of a dispute.

Security

It should be recalled that the credit funds on the card can be used by scammers, therefore, in case of loss of a Visa Gold card, you should urgently contact the bank in order to block it. If, nevertheless, the theft of money from the account has occurred, then this is also an occasion for an early appeal to the nearest bank branch. Institutional staff will instruct you on how to proceed. What are the Visa Gold card reviews?



Reviews

Owning a gold credit card has its positive and negative sides. Let's try to figure it out with the help of customer reviews. Impressions based on personal experience of borrowers are always of much greater value than advertising slogans. Most borrowers, among the main advantages, note a long grace period (50 days), during which interest-free repayment of the loan is possible. In addition, there is no annual card maintenance fee, which is also good news. Among the negative reviews, there are often complaints not about the banking product itself, but about customer service: delays in queues, incompetence, and sometimes rudeness of employees. Another significant drawback is customers consider high interest on cash withdrawals. But many consumers are quite satisfied with the service and the loan product itself.

But this is only a small part of the reviews about the Sberbank Gold Bank credit card.



Conclusion

Analyzing all of the above, we can say that this is a good banking tool for unforeseen situations. However, the disadvantages of this product must also be taken into account, otherwise indiscretion can threaten a real debt hole, from which it will be very difficult to get out. If you handle a gold credit card with care, then trouble can be minimized. It is also worthwhile to objectively assess your financial capabilities and real incomes when issuing a card.
