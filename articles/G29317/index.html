Hotel Lesse Hotel 4 * (Halkidiki, Greece): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Greece is one of the most popular tourist countries in Europe. On its coast is a huge number of diverse resorts for every taste and color. Tourists appreciate it for its low prices, quality service, ancient culture and pristine sea beaches. If you want to visit this country, then you should pay attention to the Lesse Hotel 4 * hotel, located on the Halkidiki Peninsula in the Aegean Sea.

General Hotel Information

The hotel opened to guests in the summer of 2003. It is located on more than 12 acres of forest area. Lesse Hotel 4 * is a wonderful secluded place that is suitable for both youth and family vacations. On the territory of the building there are several three-story buildings decorated with stone. In total, they offer 79 rooms of different capacities. The hotel will welcome tourists with children of all ages, but pets are not allowed in the hotel. The staff speaks Russian, which is especially convenient, because not all tourists know Greek or English.





Check in to the Lesse Hotel 4 * (Halkidiki-Kassandra) is from 14:00, and you must leave the complex no later than noon. Complete reconstruction of all buildings was carried out here for the last time in 2009. The hotel has both rooms for one person and entire family apartments. The tourist season begins here from the end of May, when the temperature rises above 25 degrees. It lasts until the very end of September, and sometimes warm weather persists here until mid-October.

To get to the Lesse Hotel 4 * (Greece, Kassandra, Chalkidiki) is possible from the nearest Macedonia airport in Thessaloniki, which is 95 kilometers from Chanoti. A bus stop is just 50 meters away. Lesse Hotel 4 * is hardly big. In total there are 79 rooms in which 150-200 people can live at the same time. Each room is well renovated, and its interior is made in a modern style. The color scheme of the rooms is moderate. The walls and floor are painted in light pastel colors, and decor elements stand out in black and brown shades. The average area of ‚Äã‚Äãone room is 30-35 square meters. m. Family apartments can cover up to 60 square meters. m. Each room has its own small balcony or spacious terrace. The walls of the room are decorated with exquisite paintings that depict still lifes and landscapes. All rooms are decorated with fresh flowers in vases or pots. Entertainment for guests

Resort Hotel Lesse Hotel 4 * (Halkidiki) is ready to offer tourists a range of diverse entertainment. Lovers of active leisure, and couples who want to quietly relax at sea, will not remain indifferent. For its guests, the hotel has prepared the following types of entertainment:

  • Animation shows, evening entertainment.

  • Paid mini-golf course as well as tennis tables.

  • 150 meters from the beach there is a sand and pebble beach. For an additional fee you can buy devices for water sports, as well as parasols and sun loungers.
  • Once a week, Greek evenings are held on site - a free show program where tourists are introduced to the history and culture of the country.




Organization of children's leisure

Arriving at the hotel with children, adults want a variety of entertainment and amenities to be available to them. The hotel also took care of its little guests. For them, they have their own children's pool, which is in close proximity to adults. There is also a playground on the territory of the complex, where kids can ride on a safe swing or play with toys. But children's animation in the hotel is not provided.

The complex allows children of all ages. Free cribs or an extra bed can be delivered to the room upon request. Toddlers under 2 years stay free. Children under 6 years old can also live without payment if they do not occupy an extra bed. If parents need to go away somewhere, they can hire a paid nanny to look after the baby.
