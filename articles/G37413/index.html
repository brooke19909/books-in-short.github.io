Choose an operator

Before answering the question of which satellite television to choose in the suburbs, consider the main leaders of this market. Broadcasting in this area is carried out by four significant operators: Tricolor, NTV Plus, Continent and Telecard.





If you look at all the offers from the point of view of an ordinary user, then, in principle, they are not much different from each other. Before deciding which satellite television to choose in the suburbs, we list several factors that somehow influence the choice of operator.

  • how many reception points are you going to connect (number of TVs);
  • nearby satellite towers;
  • the place of installation of the "plate" and the laying of the cable.
