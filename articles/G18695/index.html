Katkova Shchel, guest houses: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Katkova Shchel is a small resort village located on the Black Sea coast in the Lazarevsky district. It is part of Greater Sochi. The village received its name in honor of the landowner Katkov, who once lived in these places.

Katkova Slit has retained the charm of the countryside, in which time moves slowly. There is no fuss and noise of large resort centers. The splash of the sea, silence, the intoxicating aroma of flowers and the fresh mountain wind are the main advantages of this picturesque place.



For tourists, the resort is of interest only in the spring-summer period, since winter in these places is damp and windy, completely not conducive to walking and contemplating the natural beauties. In summer, the climate, softened by mountain ranges, becomes dry and warm, the sea is rapidly heating up. By early July, the water temperature reaches +25 ¬∞ C.

Relaxation

Katkova Slit is ideal for families with children. The air here is clean and fresh, the soil is fertile, generously giving exotic fruits and berries. The resort industry of this village is represented mainly by the private sector: mini-hotels, private hotels and hotels.

Guest houses in Katkova Slit surprise tourists with their original design and unusual design. Every house is unique here. Prices for accommodation in them are quite affordable, so every year more and more vacationers spend their holidays here.

The beach in Katkovaya Slit is not crowded, it is covered with small pebbles. For lovers of beach holidays, according to tourists, this is a real paradise. There are several shops, cafes and bars in the village. In a matter of minutes, by train or bus, you can reach Lazarevsky. In Sochi, the road takes a little longer. Traveling to mountain caves or dolmens, horseback riding, tasting local honey and wine, getting to know the national cuisine - all this makes a great impression on the guests of the village.



In this article we will tell you what accommodation options Katkova Shchel offers travelers. The guest houses of the village are most popular among tourists, although you can stay in mini-hotels or rent a room in the private sector.
