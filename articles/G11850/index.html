Short review of the LG Spirit H422 model





The smartphone has a curved body and is made in three colors to choose from: Titan Black (titanium black), Titan Gold (titanium gold) and White (white). The manufacturer claims that the gadget, thanks to its unusual shape, is convenient to hold in your hand. The screen diagonal is 4.7 inches, the screen itself is made using In-Cell Touch technology, which allows it to respond to clicks even in the event of cracks. The smartphone has a quad-core processor and runs on the Android Lollipop 5.0.1 operating system. The camera on the device is 8 megapixel, there are 2 slots for SIM cards. The front camera has a flash function.
