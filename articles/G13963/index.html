Principle of operation

The Bosch Tassimo coffee machine can prepare about thirty different drinks.

However, only ten of them are sold in the CIS. Fans of hot drinks can make both ordinary espresso and latte macchiato, cappuccino, hot chocolate , etc. The future drink is loaded into special capsules, or T-disks. It printed a service barcode that is read by a coffee machine. Here is information on how much water will be needed, to what temperature should it be heated, and how much time to cook. After cooking, the user can manually dilute the excessively strong drink.

Capsule coffee machine "Bosch" can use milk. It is concentrated, but liquid, loaded into special capsules. A container of milk is installed in the coffee maker after preparing the base drink.

Positive sides

The Bosch coffee machine is preparing a delicious, high-quality, aromatic drink. Perhaps it is slightly inferior in taste to that prepared in the classic "large" apparatus. However, for those who do not get to fanaticism, this option is most acceptable. For many buyers, price may be a decisive factor. Know that the cost of this device, depending on the model, is two to four times lower than the "classics".



Among the pluses, ease of operation should be mentioned - for control it is enough to press one button on the case and periodically pour filtered water into a special tank. Caring for such a coffee machine is simple - a T-disk is attached to it, which includes the process of self-cleaning. Those who wish can wash some of the parts in the dishwasher.



An important plus is the speed with which a fragrant drink is prepared. This machine will make ordinary coffee in a minute, and you don‚Äôt need to stand and wait at the same time - you can do your daily business. This advantage is especially important in the morning when a person is going to work. Office workers will also appreciate the quick preparation - no need to wait long for coffee to be brewed for a large team.

Negative sides

The Bosch coffee machine also has cons. The main one is the rather high price of T-disks. However, if you drink coffee infrequently, the budget is not particularly affected.

To summarize

Based on the foregoing, it can be said that the Bosch coffee machine is an excellent solution for lovers of a fragrant drink who do not want to spend a lot of time preparing it.
