Step 1. Debt settlement





Some customers of Svyaznoy Bank open a credit card just like that - "what if it comes in handy." But she was not useful, and as a result she lay idle. The client is calm, he thinks: if he did not use it, then there should be no debt. However, not all so simple.

  1. Debt may appear due to the annual fee for its servicing, which is charged even if the card was not activated.
  2. When applying for a credit card, you could have connected any paid service, for example, informing via SMS or life insurance. SMS notification fee is charged monthly. As for the insurance premium, it is written off at the beginning, immediately a year in advance.

So, it turns out that even if the card was never used, a debt on it could appear. Therefore, before contacting Svyaznoy Bank (closing a credit card), you must first find out the debt on it. To do this, you can take a statement on the status of loan debt from the bank office. In addition, debt information can be found through the Internet Bank or ATM.

If there is a credit card debt, then you need to pay it off. How can I close the card of Svyaznoy Bank if the balance of funds on it, on the contrary, is positive? In this case, it is necessary to withdraw the extra money, since to close the balance must be zero.

Step 2. Application to close the card





We look further at how to close the card of Svyaznoy Bank. The procedure includes the next step - writing an application to close the account. In addition, you must also write an application to disable additional services, if they have been connected. Do not forget to attach to them the card itself, which the bank employees must destroy in your presence. Do not expect it to expire on the day of treatment. Most banks take about 45 days to complete this procedure, including Svyaznoy Bank. How to close the card in other ways - bank employees can tell.
