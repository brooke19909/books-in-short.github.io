Smartline Napa Tsokkos Hotel 3 * (Cyprus): overview, rooms and reviews

And of course, there are many convenient hotels on the island. Many Russian operators, for example, work with the Smartline Napa Tsokkos Hotel 3 * hotel.

General hotel description

This comfortable hotel is included in the fairly famous tourists who prefer to relax in Cyprus, the Tsokkos Hotel chain. The hotel was built recently - in 2011. In 2014, reconstruction was carried out here. Therefore, the furniture, plumbing and air conditioning in the hotel are relatively new.

The hotel is hosted by Smartline Napa Tsokkos 3 * hotel (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) in a spectacular multi-story building with snow-white facades and large balconies. There are too many greens on its territory. However, the courtyard of the hotel looks quite attractive and neat. The blue water of the swimming pools located on the grounds in front of the buildings is simply in perfect harmony with the white facades. Actually, the courtyard itself is covered with light pink tiles, which is very convenient to walk on.

Mostly Russian tourists stay at this hotel. First of all, strangely enough, this is due to the fact that the administration considers them the most quiet and problem-free. However, in this hotel, the reception staff speak only one shift. Other administrators will have to talk in English. Those who do not know the language can use the translator on the phone.





Hotel Rooms

For holidaymakers who have chosen a city called Ayia Napa for their holidays, Smartline Tsokkos Napa Hotel 3 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) offers standard rooms designed for 1-4 people. In all rooms rented at the hotel, guests can use:

  • air conditioners;

  • TVs.

For a fee, residents can also access the Internet, use a telephone or a safe. A staff member can open a minibar upon request. Of course, there are refrigerators with kettles in the rooms of this hotel. The property also provides a complimentary coffee set. Showers in this three-star hotel in each room are individual. As already mentioned, there are rooms and balconies rented out at the hotel. Here, tourists can dry beach items or just sit and enjoy the surroundings.

The floors in the hotel rooms are tiled. Carpets are not provided in the hotel rooms. Each room has, of course, a large mirror and a chest of drawers. The hotel rooms are decorated in bright colors. This, according to many guests, not only relaxes, but also creates a sense of celebration.





Smartline Napa Tsokkos Hotel 3 *: room reviews

Since several years have passed since the last reconstruction of the hotel, its rooms look, of course, not as spectacular as in the photographs of tour operators. However, according to many tourists, they still look relatively good - not luxurious, but neat. And living in hotel rooms, according to most of its guests, is quite convenient.

The beds in the rooms are large and unshared. Air conditioners in the rooms work constantly and break very rarely. For using the remote control from the split system, guests at this hotel can take a deposit of 10 euros. If everything is okay when leaving with the air conditioner, this money will be returned to the tenant. The same system is provided for the use of outlets.

Among the advantages of the hotel, many of its guests, among other things, include the presence of slippers. Walking barefoot on the tile, of course, is not too nice. Some flaw in the rooms is considered not particularly high-quality wet cleaning. Hotel staff uses mops of an uncomfortable design when washing floors. Therefore, maids during cleaning, unfortunately, drag hair from one room to another. Many tourists do not pay much attention to this. However, some hotel guests, very demanding regarding cleanliness, consider the presence of other people's hair in the room unacceptable.

Near the hotel there is also an amusement park. Some rooms have a direct view of it. However, the soundproofing in the hotel, according to many guests, is not bad. Therefore, the noise from the amusement park does not penetrate into the rooms of the complex.

A hairdryer and iron are not provided in this hotel. However, guests at any time can rent them at the reception for a deposit of $ 10.

Smartline Napa Tsokkos Hotel: infrastructure reviews

Hotel Food Reviews
