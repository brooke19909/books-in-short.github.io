Detectors

Appearance

The model received a concise design with strict notes. The black plastic case has an inclined surface with a front display. There are no designer refinements like exotic angular shapes that can be seen in competing lines of other brands.





Due to its universal and strict appearance, the Neoline X-COP 5500 radar detector can be installed both in the modest equipment of the domestic automobile industry and in venerable foreign-class cars, it looks quite right here and there.

Despite the fact that the front surface of the glass goes at an angle to the horizontal, the main display is strictly perpendicular. In similar competing gadgets, the screen is simply brought to the end, and here we see an interesting design solution.

Reviews on the Neoline X-COP 5500 in this regard are mixed. Of course, this appearance gives the device individuality and beauty, but this point has its drawbacks. The device is in operation

Before starting work, the manufacturer highly recommends installing the latest update for the Neoline X-COP 5500 from the official site, and only after that start working. Otherwise, you risk missing another DPS radar.

Field tests showed that the Neoline X-COP 5500 GPS copes well with the direction finding of the Strelka-M and Strelka-ST police radars, and at a distance of up to 1 km, which is very impressive. At one time, these DPS devices made a lot of noise among motorists, when no detector could cope with their identification.





In addition, the Neoline X-COP 5500 GPS is capable of detecting such an advanced vehicle control system as Atodoria, which can easily recognize not only the registration number of the vehicle, but also quite accurately determine the average vehicle speed at a considerable distance - from 500 m to 10 km (depending on the landscape).

In addition to the notorious Strelka, the device can warn of such serious complexes as Cordon and Chris, which focus on the speed and passage of vehicles along lanes for public transport in the city stream. The DPS mobile / manual radars like Berkut, Radis, Binara, Iskra and Vizira will not be a problem. The detector from Neoline detects these devices for almost a kilometer, which largely depends on the landscape.

The gadget recognizes some cameras with the help of the built-in module, and some ones are detected by GPS protocols. This allows you to virtually eliminate the sudden hit of a car under police radars.

Owners generally speak quite well about the capabilities of the X-COP 5500 series. Users are quite satisfied with the direction-finding capabilities of the detector, as well as a pleasant and versatile appearance.
