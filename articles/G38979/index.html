The best entertainment in Tula: excursions, restaurants, places for walking and much more

Tula is an old Russian city located just 180 kilometers from the capital of Russia. This village is famous for its abundance of attractions and interesting places. What are the favorite things for locals in Tula and where should tourists definitely go to this city?





The main attractions of the city

It makes sense to start your acquaintance with Tula with a sightseeing tour or an independent walk through the historic city center. The main symbol of the Tula region is the local Kremlin. This is an architectural monument of the 16th century, on the territory of which today you can visit museums and exhibitions. A ten minute walk from the Kremlin is the Museum of Local Lore of the city.

The center of Tula is an interesting place for a promenade. Here, ancient monuments of architecture coexist with modern shopping centers. Cultural rest

Residents and visitors to the city will be interested in visiting local museums and exhibition halls. Tula from time immemorial was considered the arms capital of Russia. Today, a museum dedicated to this craft is one of the most interesting in the city. The exposition is located in an unusual building made in the shape of a giant helmet. During the tour, visitors will find a lot of interactive exhibits.





In Tula, you can visit museums dedicated to samovar and gingerbread, the regional art museum. Parks and other places for walking

If you like to relax in nature, you will definitely enjoy the central amusement park in Tula. Its full name is TsPKiO im. P.P. Belousova. In the territory of the park today there are many modern attractions, beautiful decorative sculptures, a zoo, a rental point for sports equipment. In addition to the central park, in Tula there are several more green recreation areas. Today, the most comfortable among them are Komsomolsky and Proletarsky parks.





The real pride of the city is the new embankment. This local attraction appeared only a couple of years ago. Today, the embankment is a favorite promenade for many Tula residents. Celebrations and festivities are regularly held here. If you are looking for entertainment in Tula for the weekend, go for a walk in one of the city parks or squares. Almost every weekend in these places concerts, master classes, sporting events and other interesting events are held.

Exotarium offers informative excursions and interactive programs. At the entrance to the animal exhibition you can see another city attraction - the monument "mother-in-law". Play areas and activities for children in Tula are available in every modern entertainment center. The favorite place of small Tula is the city circus. Groups regularly known throughout the country come here on tour. There is also a puppet theater and youth theater in Tula.
