How to get to Kabardinka by train or plane?

Every year, the flow of tourists who go on vacation not to distant overseas resorts, but to the Black Sea coast, is increasing. The Caucasian direction is becoming especially popular, because here the Russians are always waiting for a warm welcome, a gentle sea and an abundance of juicy fruits. Among the many resorts located on the Black Sea experienced tourists distinguish Kabardinka. This place, located in Tsemess Bay, is considered the most suitable for families with children of any age. Today we will tell you how to get to Kabardinka by train and plane from different cities of Russia.





Paradise on the coast

Our compatriots love to relax in Gelendzhik, this resort is well known to tourists since Soviet times. However, only fifteen kilometers from the city is the village of Kabardinka, which is simply a unique place to relax due to its geographical location and climate.

The fact is that the resort is located on the shore of a quiet Tsemess bay, and on the other hand is reliably protected from winds and bad weather by the Markotkh mountain range. Therefore, there is low humidity almost all year round, during the summer season the number of sunny days exceeds the figures for other Black Sea resorts, the water in the bay warms up quickly, and the swimming season lasts from May to mid-autumn.

In Soviet times, Kabardinka was focused on children's recreation, because the local mild climate is ideal for healing kids from all over the country. Today, there are several camps, recreation centers, boarding houses and even modern five-star hotels. In this regard, the number of tourists has increased, and their composition has expanded significantly. The level of popularity of Kabardinka has grown markedly.

Amazing nature in many respects provides this resort with constant interest from vacationers. Here, in a fairly small area, you can see coniferous forests, vineyards and orchards. And the magnificent sandy beaches are at least fifty meters wide and stretch for five hundred meters. Rest in this place is filled with some extraordinary pacification, and the leisurely rhythm of a quiet resort town will appeal to the residents of megalopolises, always rushing somewhere.

After all that has been said, you will probably want to know how to get to Kabardinka. It is easiest to do this by train, but you can also use air services in this direction. We will tell you about all the ways.





Route Moscow - Kabardinka: how to get there by train

Unfortunately, the resort village does not have its own train station, so the easiest way to buy a ticket is to Novorossiysk or Anapa. Here, every local resident will tell you how to get to Kabardinka. By train, a one-way journey takes about an average of one day. However, let's talk about everything in order.

You can leave for Novorossiysk from Moscow from two stations - Kazan and Kursky. Usually a trip takes a little more than a day, and trains leave every day. If you want to spend as little time as possible on the road, get tickets for the Premium 030C train, which leaves from Kazan Station. In this case, you will find yourself in Novorossiysk after 22 hours, but the fare for one way is 6800 rubles. Whereas the place in the compartment of an ordinary train does not exceed 4200 rubles.

If you plan to catch a train to Anapa, then head to Kursky or Kievsky station. Travel time does not exceed 23 hours, and the fare varies from 5 thousand rubles. (coupe). A ticket to the reserved seat is much cheaper - 3200 rubles. The composition to Anapa leaves every day with a small time interval.

Travel trip from St. Petersburg

Many travelers are interested in how to get to Kabardinka by train from St. Petersburg. As it turned out, this is a very popular destination among residents of the northern capital. The fastest way is from St. Petersburg to get to Novorossiysk, and from there by bus or taxi go to Kabardinka.

Trains in this direction leave from the Moscow station, on the way you will spend more than a day. The cost of such a one-way trip slightly exceeds 9 thousand rubles. If you buy tickets not before departure, you will be able to save a little on the trip.



Penza: journey by rail

If you need to find out how to get from Penza to Kabardinka by train, the information in this section will be useful to you. Residents of the city have the opportunity to go on vacation through Novorossiysk, Krymsk and Tuapse. All trains leave daily from Penza Station.

It is worth noting that in each of these areas the ticket price is approximately the same, one way it is in the range of three to four thousand rubles. Travel time is also not too different - approximately one day and seven hours.

Lipetsk - Kabardinka: how to get there by train

Residents of Lipetsk planning a vacation in Kabardinka can purchase tickets to Novorossiysk. In this direction there are trains 227 and 481, departure is from the station Lipetsk.

The average travel time is one day, a ticket in a compartment costs a little more than 4 thousand rubles. A trip to the reserved seat will cost our compatriots 2700 rubles.





Bryansk: trip to the resort

The question "how to get to Kabardinka by train from Bryansk" worries many residents of the city. After all, the Black Sea coast is the most popular destination for central Russia.

From Bryansk, trains leave for Krasnodar and Tuapse, from both points you can take a bus to Kabardinka. Travel time is much longer than a day, usually travelers have to spend one day and seven hours on the train. The fare ranges from 4 thousand rubles. The cheapest way to go to Krasnodar, in this case you have to spend 3300 rubles. However, keep in mind that from this city to Kabardinka to get longer than from Tuapse.

From Voronezh to Kabardinka

Voronezhtsev, like many other Russians, is interested in how to get to Kabardinka by train from Voronezh. The route in this direction will not differ much from the previously described.

A train departs from Voronezh-1 station to Novorossiysk, it runs daily, so townspeople usually do not have problems with tickets. The fastest part is the Chernomorets special squad, you will be at your destination after 21 hours. However, the cost of tickets for this train is quite high: reserved seats - about 4 thousand rubles, coupe - 5700 rubles, luxury - a little more than 9 thousand rubles.

The usual train goes to Novorossiysk about a day and the average price of a compartment is 3 thousand rubles. The train departs from Voronezh station.





Train on the route Nizhny Novgorod - Kabardinka: description and fare

Usually, those travelers who think in advance how to get to Kabardinka by train from Nizhny Novgorod, seriously consider only two options. They are also the most convenient - through Krymsk and Novorossiysk.

In both cases, trains depart from Nizhny Novgorod station, and you can leave every day during the season. The train to Krymsk is on the way one day and 17 hours, the cost of travel exceeds 6 thousand rubles. The train runs to Novorossiysk one day 19 hours, a ticket to the reserved seat costs about 4 thousand rubles. Coupe costs vacationers 6800 rubles.

Of course, we are not able, within the framework of one article, to tell you how to get to Kabardinka by train from all Russian cities. However, we tried to cover the maximum possible number in our review.





By plane to Kabardinka: travel features

Naturally, air travel is the fastest way to get from one point to another. But do not forget that it applies to the most expensive. In addition, there are not always flights from every city in Russia on the desired route. Therefore, we consider air travel to Kabardinka on the example of Moscow.

Since the resort itself does not have its own airport terminal, Muscovites have the opportunity to choose any airport located in Gelendzhik, Anapa and Krasnodar. From any point in the future you can get to Kabardinka as soon as possible.

If you choose Gelendzhik, then get ready to pay about 9 thousand rubles for a one-way trip. From here to the village there are several bus routes:

  • 32;
  • 31;
  • 103.

The interval of movement is from one and a half to three hours.

If you decide to get to Kabardinka via Anapa, then keep in mind that the cost of air travel will be a little more than 7 thousand rubles. Next, you will need to purchase tickets for one of the intercity bus routes passing through several resort cities:

  • Temryuk - Tuapse;
  • Temryuk - Gelendzhik;
  • Anapa - Sochi.

In general, the road on any of the buses will take you about two hours.

You can fly from Moscow to Krasnodar by paying 6 thousand rubles. Every day, a bus leaves for the Kabardinka bus from the Krasnodar bus station, which will be on the road for about four hours.





It is up to you to decide how best to get to the resort on the Black Sea coast. Some people prefer a measured train journey, but others like a short flight and a further bus trip. Whatever you choose, Kabardinka will definitely make a lasting impression on you, and you will spend your vacation here more than once.
