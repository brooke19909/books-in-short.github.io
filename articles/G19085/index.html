Cyprus, Liquid Hotel Apts 3 *: tourists photos and reviews

Do you want to plunge into a wonderful world that has a history spanned by legends and myths? Then come to the island of Cyprus. Located in the eastern part of the Mediterranean Sea, among the azure waters washing the shores of Turkey and Syria, it is the most picturesque corner of the Mediterranean. Hotels here are for every taste, it is inexpensive to the most luxurious. Today we will talk about a great economy class vacation - Liquid Hotel Apts 3 *.





Description

One of the convenient hotels for families is the Liquid Hotel Apts 3 *. Most recently opened after restoration and overhaul, it is a true example of style. You will find a nice snow-white building with neat balconies and bright lighting. The ground floor is occupied by a cozy restaurant, which serves a buffet. The rest of the time, you can order dishes on the menu. The size of the hotel is quite small, there are practically no noisy companies, so no one will ruin your holiday with your party. Guests are accommodated on the second and third floors.

Location

Liquid Hotel Apts 3 * is located in Ayia Napa, 600 meters from the beach of Grecian Bay. Especially here will appeal to those who like to combine a beach holiday with city entertainment. Just you were lying on a golden beach, and suddenly you wanted a noisy party and fun shopping with friends? Just a few minutes - and you are in the center of the resort town, where an abundance of fashion boutiques, restaurants, youth clubs will not let you get bored. You will probably be interested to know that this town used to be a small fishing village surrounded by impenetrable wild thicket. Today, there are thousands of tourists annually, whose reviews speak of excellent service for fairly modest money.

Distance to airport





Liquid Hotel Apts 3 * attracts tourists with its convenient communication. It is located 42 km from the city of Larnaca, which has a major international airport. From it to the hotel is only 58 km. Visitors are met by specialized transport, which will deliver directly to the hotel. For tourists arriving from Greece by private car, there is a convenient parking lot.

Hotel

Liquid Hotel Apts 3 (Ayia Napa) is a real island of tranquility, located near many restaurants, clubs and pubs. On its territory there are simultaneously no more than 100 guests. In the morning, a gentle sun awakens the tourists, looking into the hotel windows. The smell of coffee brewing for breakfast gives vigor and arouses appetite. The pool in the hotel courtyard beckons with the clear blue of its waters, and the sun loungers near it offer the opportunity to sunbathe. The three-story building with a spectacular open ground floor, on which the restaurant tables are located, successfully fits into the surrounding nature.

Rooms

The apartments at Liquid Hotel Apts 3 Ayia Napa are decorated in red, blue and white. All 49 bright, spacious rooms await their guests. In each comfortable, wide beds, soft sofas, equipped kitchen. A kitchen corner and tea set will bring harmony to your evenings. The interior is well thought out, all the elements, including bedspreads, are in perfect harmony with each other. Made in warm colors, it gives comfort and tranquility. The room has a TV and air conditioning, a small minus that it turns on for a fee. Pets are not allowed. Credit cards accepted.





In the evening, you can drink tea on the balcony, enjoying the beautiful view and the refreshing breeze. A unique vacation option is offered by the Liquid Hotel Apts 3 (Ayia Napa): great views, excellent service, and most importantly, very inexpensive.

Staff

The cozy and pleasant interior pleases the eye, and the staff creates all conditions for a comfortable stay. Professional service, friendly and responsive administration are the hallmarks of Liquid Hotel Apts 3. Cyprus is famous for its excellent attitude to vacationers. Room cleaning is done daily during the absence of tourists. Bed linen and towels are changing. Beach towels are not provided, you can take them on vacation with you. About a day later, stocks of liquid soap and toilet paper replenish.





Territory

The local guide goes out to meet the guests arriving at the gates of Liquid Hotel Apts 3. You can start taking photos here. A small private car park is ready to take your car. There are few cars, so there is always room. On the territory there is a restaurant that you can visit if you do not want to go to the city. Near the pool there is a snack bar where you can have a bite to eat, almost without leaving a chaise lounge.

When approaching the hotel you will find a cozy terrace, on which it is so good to spend warm summer evenings. A 24-hour front desk is always at your service. Here they will answer all your questions, bring an iron, connect a telephone, air conditioning to the number.

Wi-Fi is available throughout the Liquid Hotel Apts 3 (Cyprus). Reviews of tourists note that in some places the signal is weak, but in general this does not interfere with communication with the outside world. There is a luggage storage room in which you can leave all personal items that will interfere or are rarely needed. For especially valuable things there is a safe. Laundry located on site will help solve all hygiene issues.

Beach vacation

The easiest way is to swim in the pool and sunbathe right in the hotel courtyard. Fans of bronze tans do not leave the water at all, periodically replenishing the supply of snacks and drinks in a nearby bar. But who will deny himself the pleasure of swimming in the Mediterranean Sea? Only here all types of water sports are presented to the services of vacationers. The beach is within walking distance, just 600 meters from the hotel, along a well-maintained path. All tourists note one minor minus: sunbeds and umbrellas are provided here only at an additional cost. No desire to pay - stock up on sunblock and enjoy the golden sand.

The sea itself, affectionate and clean, will give a lot of pleasant experiences. Sometimes vacationers note that it was inconvenient for them to go into the sea, there were a lot of stones at the bottom. Please note that beaches are located on the entire coast of the resort town. The bottom is not the same everywhere, but on the territory adjacent to the hotel, the entrance to the sea is surprisingly smooth and comfortable.





Nutrition

Several different options are in store for you Liquid Hotel Apts 3 (Ayia Napa). Reviews of tourists are rather contradictory, therefore it is better to rely on the opinion of good friends, and even better - try to go yourself. Choosing the HH option, you get full breakfasts, and the rest of the time you should eat on the menu system in any restaurant in Cyprus. It is breakfast that is accused of monotony, only a few options are offered, and they are repeated daily. If you do not like sausages and bacon, then you can safely refuse.

The HB option is much more interesting, the price difference between the two systems is minimal, but here you get breakfast and dinner as a buffet. Tourists note that the evening meal is much better. Guests are offered several salads, three or four main dishes, a mass of side dishes and snacks, vegetables, fruits, pastries and desserts. If you stick to the system of brunch - early dinner, you can completely skip lunch.





The restaurant is kept in perfect cleanliness. The waiters are ready to prompt, help with the choice, carry away and replace the dish you do not like. The cook personally monitors the replenishment of dishes, the hungry will not manage to leave even in the last minutes of dinner, and basically everything is very tasty.

If you like variety, you can choose the type without nutrition. In this case, you can drink morning coffee in the room, having previously bought something in the city, and have lunch and dinner every time in different institutions. So you can get a lot of new experiences, get to know a lot of people and Cretan cuisine in all its glory. Tourists note that for the money it turns out about the same as if you take a ticket with food at the hotel.

Entertainment

The whole city around Liquid Hotel Apts 3 - Ayia Napa (Cyprus) is the real capital of fun. Vacationers can use the swimming pool located on site for free. But if you want to swim, do not limit yourself to small ones; wonderful water parks are waiting for you. The Water World is considered the largest and most beautiful in Ayia Napa. Luxurious slides with downhill slopes and pools with artificial waves will appeal to both children and adults. The second largest is Aphrodite Park. It is full of activities for kids, so it is well suited for families.

Ayia Napa Luna Park is another place worth leaving the hotel‚Äôs quiet abode. The abundance of light, music, spinning roundabouts and happy faces will give a good mood, and a ride on a ferris wheel, you can see the whole city from a bird's eye view.

Want to get to the most fun and beautiful beach of the city? Then get up from your usual hotel lounge chair and welcome to Nissi. Here, mainly young people relax, there are numerous recreational activities and incendiary concerts. Fashionable DJs have stunning discos here. In addition to standard beach activities (banana, water skiing, parachute), here you can take a jump from a 50-meter bungee.





Another famous beach is Makronisos, couples with children have a rest here, and quieter informative and entertaining events take place. If you want to be alone, then welcome to Grecian Bay. There are few visitors, but you can listen to the sound of the surf and the cries of gulls.

The hotel can offer you a tour of the donkey farm. Lovely animals with celebrity names will be happy to ride you on their strong backs. Especially this adventure will appeal to children.

Vacation with children

The best option is Liquid Hotel Apts 3. Traveler reviews say that Crete is generally perfect for traveling with children, and the silence, comfort and cleanliness of this hotel make it indispensable. Returning from intense excursions to the aqua and amusement park, you will not worry that a fun disco will not let the baby relax. The hotel does not have fun playgrounds, but there is a small pool with warm water on site. The restaurant has specially designated tables equipped with high chairs for feeding. If your child is difficult to feed, then in the supermarkets of Cyprus you can find his favorite goodies: cottage cheese, cookies, yoghurts and much more.





The climate of Cyprus is like no other suitable for kids. Summer here begins earlier, and ends later than in other resorts of the Mediterranean. The healing climate helps not only relax, but also improve health. Cheerful days in the center of Ayia Napa and quiet evenings at the hotel for a delicious dinner on the terrace will be your child's dream for a long time.

The city's attractions

The guide, who oversees the Liquid Hotel Apts 3 * hotel, will definitely tell you that in addition to traveling to the beach, you can also visit many interesting excursions. Be sure to get to look at the old Venetian monastery. It is here that the club quarter begins, in which open-air concerts are held. You can look at breathtaking dances or even take part in them. At this time, it is better to send children with an accompanying person to the amusement park. If a romantic has awakened in you, then experienced tourists are advised to visit Cape Greco. Here you can not only enjoy the sea and beautiful sunsets, but also castles and caves.

For those in whose spirit the explorer is snoozing, there is a journey to the ghost town of Famagusta. It used to be a fabulously rich and beautiful city, but today only ruins surrounded by a Venetian wall remain from its old part. The new part is gradually growing and developing. There are many beautiful legends associated with this city that are so good to listen to on board the yacht. Here you can see the tower in which, according to legend, the famous Shakespearean Othello lived and worked.

To summarize

Liquid Hotel Apts 3 (Ayia-Napa) is an amazingly quiet and peaceful hotel where, thanks to the polite and helpful staff, you will have an amazing vacation. There are negative reviews of tourists who say that there is no luggage delivery to the room, the refrigerator is noisy, there are no beach towels, but in general the price-quality ratio is maintained, and the hotel deserves a solid four with a plus. If you do not pretend to be a five-star service, a steam room and a private pool - then here you will find the best vacation in Cyprus.
