Shopping center "South-West" in Voronezh: shops and address

Shopping centers have long made the life of a modern person much easier. Thanks to them, it is immediately possible to make many important and necessary purchases in one place. A considerable number of visitors come to the South-West shopping center in Voronezh to take a walk. After all, there is a very pleasant atmosphere and excellent conditions for relaxation. Every day the center is visited by many people, among whom there are regular and new guests. The complex has small dimensions, but at the same time successfully accommodated many tenants.

general information

Shopping center "South-West" in Voronezh opened in the fall of 2007. Gradually, popular retail outlets began to appear in it, due to which the number of customers began to grow. The building has 4 floors, on each of which any guest can find what he was looking for. The complex very quickly became popular also because it was located on the territory historically predisposed to trade. Nearby is the market and many modes of transport, so there are always buyers. In addition, the area is very densely populated. It contains two large residential complexes called "Northern" and "Greenhouse-Shady." Residents from these areas in most cases are regular customers in the center.

Outlets in the complex are designed for the most diverse financial opportunities of consumers. Not only a city dweller with an average salary, but also a consumer with a small income can make important purchases here. The Yugo-Zapad shopping center in Voronezh was created as competently as possible, so each buyer can carefully distribute his time without losing any extra minutes.

On one territory, all the necessary shops are collected that will allow you to make all purchases, being under one roof. A wide assortment of clothes and shoes for both sexes, goods for children, cosmetic products, jewelry, sports equipment and much more is widely represented at retail outlets. In stores, profitable promotions and discounts are constantly held. The center also has a furniture store where you can see great options. Fans of good literature will be able to look into the "Letter", and presents can be purchased at the Monarch or in the department with bags and gifts on the ground floor.

How to get to the mall

Almost every citizen already knows about a popular shopping center. It is located in a rather favorable place, so there are always a lot of buyers. The exact address of the South-West shopping center in Voronezh: Patriotov Avenue, building 3A.

There are several ways to get to the complex. In addition to your personal car, you can get there by public transport. Trolleybuses (numbers 37 and 50), as well as buses (76 and 80) are excellent for this. The stop is called "Shop Gardener". Not so far from the center is the South-West bus station, where you can come from different parts of the city.

How does the shopping center "South-West"

You can visit the Southwest shopping center in Voronezh every day, seven days a week. It starts work from 9.00 in the morning and closes at 21.00. There is no lunch break in the center, so every visitor will be able to come shopping at a convenient time for him.
