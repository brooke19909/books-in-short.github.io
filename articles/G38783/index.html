Which bank is profitable to invest money in: list, overview of services and interest rates, description of conditions, reviews

Which bank is profitable to invest money in? This reasonable question is asked by any Russian as soon as he has additional income. After all, it is well known that money should not lie just like that. They are obliged to make a profit for their owners, to work for them. If in the Soviet Union, in fact, there was only one bank, so then no options existed, now so many players have entered the market that it is not so easy to decide which organization to entrust your money with. In addition, it is no secret that in recent years there has been a certain crisis in the banking sector, the victims of which were even very large players. For example, the bank "Discovery". In this article, we will try to understand all the existing nuances, advising where it is best to invest our savings.

Reliability

If you decide which bank is profitable to invest in, then you should know what criteria you should pay attention to first. Remember that attractive and high deposit rates can be deceiving. Often, they are trying to hide behind their unstable position in the market, and in some cases, outright scammers do this. However, you should be prepared that you will not be able to fully protect yourself from any unforeseen troubles. The only thing that is determined in which bank is it profitable to invest is, give preference to reputable and prestigious financial institutions. Even if they have lower interest rates than in commercial banks, you can be calm about the safety of money.

There are several options for investing savings. You can draw up depersonalized metal accounts, invest in investment companies, mutual funds or purchase a savings certificate. However, the most common and reliable is still the discovery of the usual contribution. This option is trusted by most Russians when it is determined which bank is profitable to invest in.

How will we choose?

To make a profitable deposit, decide in which bank to invest at interest, several important parameters must be taken into account. We have already said about reliability. It is worth recognizing that this parameter remains one of the key. Even if the interest rate is very high, but the bank itself is very doubtful, you should not contact it. Choosing a financial institution with an established reputation, customers receive guarantees that they can return their money with interest. Currently, the largest banks in the Russian Federation are Sberbank, VTB, Alfa Bank, and Rosselkhozbank. In reviews of which bank to invest in, most current and potential customers are advised to choose from this list. It is these financial institutions that have the largest client base, as well as open offices in all major cities of the country.

The next parameter to which you should pay close attention when deciding which bank to invest money at interest is the size of the rate itself. It is worth recognizing that in large banks, as a rule, you will not find really attractive and high rates. They charge at the expense of reliability. Less well-known, who have nothing more to offer, attract customers as much as possible profit. So do completely new banks that have not yet had time to declare themselves. So, when you are offered a high interest rate, this does not mean that you are definitely deceived, but it is worth fearing for your savings.

Carefully study all the parameters of the contribution. The size of the bet will also be affected by its duration, special conditions present in the contract.

Another parameter can be called controllability. It is very important in determining which bank to invest in. There are deposits without the possibility of replenishment or partial withdrawal of the amount until the expiration of the contract. In this case, you can get the full amount with interest only at the very end. Of course, it is more convenient when there is the opportunity to replenish the account and withdraw money from it, if necessary, in order to more or less freely dispose of their funds. But in this case, the rate is likely to be slightly lower. There are also options with capitalization of the deposit, which also affects the decision on where to invest, which bank to choose.

Finally, it is worthwhile to find out from an employee of a financial organization whether your savings will be insured, whether the bank is cooperating with the Deposit Insurance Agency. At present, in Russia, a deposit is automatically insured if it has an amount of not more than 1.4 million rubles.

This is another important point to consider when deciding which bank to invest in. You need to understand that this will include both the amount you originally contributed and the interest accrued on it. Of course, a deposit will be insured only if the financial institution cooperates with the Deposit Insurance Agency. Then you can not worry about the fate of your money, even if the bank “bursts” or the Central Bank withdraws its license.

The best way to decide which bank is best to invest in is to make a list of financial institutions that you trust the most, and then compare what conditions they offer. As a rule, the sites describe in detail all the details, program conditions, interest rates and tariffs for each deposit. If necessary, you can get additional advice in any department that is within walking distance from you.

Central Bank Rating

When deciding which bank is best to invest in, we recommend that you refer to the reliability rating for opening a deposit drawn up by the Central Bank of the Russian Federation.

Please note that if you invest in a state financial institution, you will receive a relatively small rate. This is explained by the fact that such companies have huge client bases in which there are many millions of people, therefore they do not need to additionally attract investors, promising them high profitability. People will come to them, focusing on status, name and fame.

Also, the decisive factors for many when deciding which bank it is more reliable to invest in are the presence of their branches in small towns, their prevalence in the country, and transparency of the deposit conditions.

If you take the most popular banks, then in Sberbank, the most common in the Russian Federation, you will be able to offer the maximum rate of only about seven percent per annum. At VTB and Rosselkhozbank, you can count on about the same profit. About interest on deposits in each of these financial organizations, as well as about the conditions we will discuss in this article.

Answering the question of which banks to invest in, many experts advise to opt for state financial institutions, since most of them belong to the state. This means that if any problems arise, the company will still be provided with the necessary support. So the likelihood that they will go bankrupt is close to zero. In 2018, Sberbank of Russia was the leader in the rating of bank reliability by the number of assets. VTB is in second place, and Gazprombank in third. The top ten also included Rosselkhozbank, Moscow Credit Bank, Unicreditbank, Alfa Bank, Raiffeisenbank, Otkritie, and the All-Russian Regional Development Bank.

It is on this list that everyone is advised to focus on when you decide which bank it is safer to invest in.

High interest

Of course, when a person invests, first of all, he expects to earn as much as possible. Therefore, no one will reproach you if you carefully examine all kinds of interest rates that offer you.

It is important to remember that, in addition to the rate itself, you need to carefully study all other terms of the contract. After all, if you focus only on advertising, then you can be misled. In the videos they talk about the maximum rates, but you should always remember that there are also minimum rates, and the final value depends on various factors. When determining which banks are the best to invest in, it is important which program you choose, for how long, whether you can withdraw money from your account or replenish it, how often you earn income (monthly or only at the very end of the term). Please note whether you belong to the category of privileged customers (as a rule, those who have a credit card in this bank or who receive a pension through it). All this will affect the final deposit rate, which bank to invest in.

Carefully analyzing the existing market, we can highlight several of the most attractive offers. This should help you decide which bank is best to invest your money in. For example, in Sovcombank there is a record interest deposit. If you have a Halva card, the rate can reach 8.65%. True, there is no possibility of early termination, provided that the interest received on the deposit is maintained.

It is important that throughout the term you have the right to deposit additional amounts not exceeding the original amount. Interest is paid upon expiration of the entire term directly to the Halva card.

Traditionally, profitable deposits are offered to be opened at Zenit Bank. The Leadership Strategy deposit offers a yield of up to 9.5 percent per annum with a minimum amount of one hundred thousand rubles. It is noteworthy that this deposit can only be opened in conjunction with the issuance of an investment life insurance policy for a client at a credit institution.

We emphasize that all these deposits are accepted only in domestic currency. This is the most profitable bank to invest in rubles.

Reminder for contributor

Before giving your money to one or another financial organization, it is important to remember several important things. When deciding which bank you can invest your money in, it should be understood that small firms, which, moreover, are often represented in only one region, often attract depositors with higher deposit rates and other favorable conditions. At the same time, they lack sufficient operational experience in this market, and also lack stability and reliability to ensure guaranteed preservation of your money.

Therefore, when determining which bank to invest in, experts recommend, first of all, to pay attention to those organizations that have significant experience and a high reliability rating.

In addition, do not forget that it is possible to receive a profit, albeit small, with the help of debit cards, with the ability to save available funds. For example, you pay with this card wherever possible, pay for services, purchase some goods, and for this you are regularly charged certain percentages. In this case, the larger the amount in your account, the more substantial commission you can expect. Periodically, banks hold promotions in order to get more customers, for this they can, for example, offer increased interest on their debit cards.

Further, to help you figure out where to invest money, which bank has more interest, consider the offers that the largest banks on the domestic financial market offer today.
