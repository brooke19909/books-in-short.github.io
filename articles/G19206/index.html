City of Gagra: hotels, inns, private hotels and hotels by the sea with an overview of prices and reviews of tourists

The city of Gagra is one of the most beautiful in Abkhazia. Hundreds of tourists come here every year to enjoy the local attractions and soak up the gentle sun. Gagra is located near the famous resort town of Adler. It stretches along the coast for several kilometers. Everyone is happy here: youth, families with children, elderly people. Hotels in Gagra can be found at every turn. You will have no problems with housing. All vacationers are waiting for hospitality and hospitality. Local residents are able to surround everyone with care.

Hotel-club "Amran"

The city of Gagra, whose hotels are always open for new guests, attracts with a unique joyful mood, comfort, sun and warmth. You will receive all this by staying at the Amran Hotel. This cozy place is located near the sea. The beach is just 100 meters away. The nearest airport and train station are about a 30-minute drive away. There are 35 rooms in the hotel. All of them are decorated in a modern style using the latest materials and technologies. Each room has air conditioning, minibar, refrigerator, TV. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and toiletries. It features a large outdoor pool, free parking. A playground is provided for children. Near the hotel there are several cafes and restaurants. Room prices range from 2500 to 5000 rubles. Guests speak great about the hotel. Especially noted by well-trained and polite staff, clean rooms. Everyone really likes the backlit pool, where you can swim at any time of the day.

Guest house "Zvanba"





Hotels in Gagra are very popular with tourists. However, many people feel more comfortable in places where fewer people. If you are among them, then guest houses are just for you. Zvanba is located in the very center of the city. A three minute walk from it there is a pebble beach. In their reviews, tourists write that the guest house looks very comfortable both outside and inside. A small but very beautiful patio invites you to sit in a pretty arbor. Private parking is available for guests' convenience. There are eight rooms in the guest house. They are decorated in light colors. Each of them has a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator. The balcony offers magnificent views of the sea and mountains. The bathroom includes toiletries, towels and a hairdryer. Cafes and restaurants are located in neighboring buildings. Room rates from 2000 to 4000 rubles.

Mini-Hotel "Mill"





Mini-hotels in Gagra are of particular interest to tourists. A striking example is the small hotel "Mill". Her unusual appearance causes a lot of positive emotions. A beautiful mill-like building with wrought-iron fences and a staircase looks impressive. Despite the small number of rooms, there are only two, the number of people wishing to spend their holidays here is growing annually. The original design of the rooms is not inferior to the exterior decoration. They have everything you need for a comfortable stay: air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, bathroom. The balcony offers garden views. Complimentary wireless Internet is provided. The hotel provides bicycle rental, shuttle service, grocery delivery, luggage storage, ticket sales. The garden has a barbecue area. A hotel room will cost from 3000 rubles. According to guests, staying here is worth any money. An unusual interior that you will not see anywhere else, all kinds of services and helpful staff - all this awaits tourists at the Mill Hotel. It is conveniently located. Nearby there is a public transport stop and there are always a lot of taxis.

Hotel Apsilaa





Looking through the numerous hotels of Gagra, be sure to pay attention to this. It is located next to a pebble beach on the Black Sea. There are only 26 rooms. They are all decorated in a contemporary style. There is nothing superfluous in the interior. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer and toiletries. The rooms have TV, air conditioning, free wireless Internet. There is a bar and a 24-hour front desk. Room prices are relatively small - from 2000 rubles. The hotel has a cafe. The menu includes local dishes and drinks. There is a cafe across the road from the hotel. The nearest airport is 40 kilometers away. Nearby is a local attraction - the fortress of Abaath and the castle of the Prince of Oldenburg. Vacationers respond positively about the hotel team, the quality of room service. The rooms are warm, bright, clean and quiet. Nothing prevents a good night's sleep and relaxation. Upon request, food from the restaurant and any drinks are delivered to the room.

Hotel Aibga

If you are looking for Gagra hotels by the sea, then Aibga will do just fine. The beach is only five minutes walk. The hotel has 30 comfortable rooms. They are tastefully decorated. The interior is dominated by warm colors. It offers guests a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, bathroom, 24-hour reception. You can have fun playing table tennis, singing in the karaoke bar or swimming in the pool. Breakfast and lunch are served in the hotel's dining room. The airport in Sochi is 26 kilometers away. The cost of rooms depends on their category. It can be from 2000 to 5000 rubles. Guests staying here are satisfied. They say that the hotel staff skillfully create excellent living conditions. There is some fun here. If there is no desire to go somewhere in the evening, then this is quite enough. Nearby is a supermarket that is open around the clock.

Hotel "Hellas"

The city of Gagra, whose hotels are located mainly near the sea, also receives tourists at the Hotel Hellas. Here you will always be welcome. The polite staff will select the most suitable room according to your wishes. Hotel Rooms 21. Each has air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, hairdryer, bathroom, toiletries. Reception is open 24 hours a day. The hotel bar serves refreshments and cocktails to everyone. Additional services include laundry and ticket reservations. The railway station is a little over four kilometers, and the airport is 34 km. Accommodation will cost you 2500 - 5000 rubles. If you do not want to live in the very center, but you need to often go to meetings, then this place is ideal. According to the guests, the hotel is very conveniently located. It is somewhat removed from the center, but getting to most of the objects is easy. In the evening and at night it is quiet, nothing distracts from sleep and rest.

Hotel Alex Beach





The city of Gagra, whose hotels are mostly miniature, has quite large pearls in its collection. Among them is Alex Beach. It is located 30 meters from the Black Sea beach. It offers 78 superb rooms. All of them are made in the same style, but differ in color. Hotels in Gagra, prices for which are slightly higher than the rest, are located close to the coast. The cost of rooms in Alex Beach ranges from 2000 to 6000 rubles. It offers a TV, air conditioning, toiletries, bathrobes, minibar. The hotel has a beauty salon, bowling club, sauna, night club, indoor and outdoor pools, spa. Private hotels in Gagra organize full-fledged meals for their guests. The hotel's restaurant serves Italian and Abkhaz cuisine. In addition, you can have a snack and a variety of drinks in the cafe located on the hotel premises. You can reach the train station in 10 minutes. The hotel can arrange transfers from and to the airport in Sochi, which is 35 kilometers away. Tourists who stay here are always happy with their choice. They note good food, beautiful and spacious rooms, friendly staff.

Villa "Monte Gagra"





This beautiful place in the lap of nature will make you forget about all pressing problems and deeds. Amazingly beautiful landscapes of the forest, a small river near the house, clean air, some distance from civilization - all that is needed for a good vacation. There are only six rooms in the villa. They are equipped with everything you need. You absolutely will not need anything. Friendly hosts will be able to satisfy all your whims. The large garden has gazebos and a barbecue area. You can rent a bike. Parking is free for guests. The road to the beach will take some time, about 1.5 kilometers to it. This distance can be overcome by city bus or minibus. Public transport is very close. Room prices are very attractive. For one night you will pay from 1000 to 5000 rubles. Despite the fact that the beach is far enough, guests like to be here. Not a lot of cars. It is very quiet day and night. Around the house is beautiful nature, clean air. The rooms are often cleaned, linen and towels are changed. The staff is very attentive to the guests.

Guest house Mamzyshkha

The resort town of Gagra, the hotels in which will satisfy all tastes, also presents the guest house Mamzyshkha. The building is beautiful and elegant. White, air railing, stairs and balconies create a unique image. The house has nine rooms. Each is equipped with air conditioning, minibar, TV. In the courtyard there is a fairly large pool. Room rates from 3000 to 6000 rubles. Many tourists like the fact that the hotel allows pets. The rooms are often cleaned well. There is always fresh drinks in the minibar.

Guest house RusAmra

The cozy RusAmra guest house is located on the Black Sea coast. This place is worth your attention. Nice courtyard, parking and a small house. Here you will be welcomed by the hostess and will help you to conveniently settle down. There are only ten rooms. They have everything you need. Accommodation will cost from 2000 to 4000 rubles. Free Wi-Fi access is available. Guests note the convenience and ease of communication with the staff. You feel at home here. The rooms are cozy and bright. On the ground floor there is a kitchen, guests can use it at any time of the day. In the courtyard there are all barbecue facilities. Just a 10-minute walk there are several cafes and restaurants. It is possible to rent a bike. The nearest airport is Adler. Before him 38 kilometers.
