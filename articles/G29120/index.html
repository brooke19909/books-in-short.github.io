Home tax. Calculation of property tax for individuals

Today we will have to understand what a home tax is and how it is calculated. This issue is extremely important for all individuals. In particular, for those who own a house or its share. After all, the citizens of the studied question relates first of all. It should be remembered that taxes are paid by almost all citizens. Debt leads to serious problems. To avoid them, you will have to thoroughly understand the tax rules that take place in Russia. In addition, continuous reforms do not allow the population to calmly realize what awaits them. So what should you look for? How to calculate home tax? And is there any such kind of payment in Russian law?

House and property

There is no separate receipt for this tax in Russia. In the same way as the concept of "home tax". The thing is that there is a common name for such payments. And it is familiar to many taxpayers. This is a property tax.



What kind of payment is this? This is a monetary penalty levied on property owners. As a rule, in this case only housing is considered. Namely:

  • private houses;
  • shares in houses;
  • apartments;
  • rooms.

But transport and land are not considered such property. You will have to pay for them separately. But what will citizens have to understand regarding property tax? What features should you learn about?

Who pays

Who can receive property tax receipts ? This question, as a rule, at some point causes some difficulties. Why?

The thing is that the tax on a private house or any other property is levied on all taxpayers. That is, if a citizen is 18 years old, he is fully capable, he owns a house, or part of it, or at least a room, then you will have to pay money. About calculations a little later.

Problems arise for citizens who have reached retirement age. After all, these are eternal beneficiaries. Do they have to pay such a penalty? Or does this category of citizens have their own benefits? If so, then payers are only adult able-bodied persons. And organizations (including individual entrepreneurs).

About beneficiaries

In reality, retirees are beneficiaries. And they have special rights in tax obligations. Home tax is no exception. What can every senior citizen count on?



He has the right to full exemption from property tax. Moreover, regardless of their state of health or social status. In Russia, all people who have reached retirement age are completely exempt from paying this penalty. And no matter where the person lives. The main thing is that he lives on the territory of the Russian Federation.

It follows from this that, as has already been noted, only able-bodied adult citizens are payers of the studied payment. Children and pensioners do not pay this tax in any way. This fact should be considered by every citizen.

Calculation changes

Nevertheless, many are interested in what kind of house tax is imposed in a particular case. Indeed, in 2015, new changes came into force in relation to the payment being studied. They brought a lot of problems to the owners. In particular, due to calculations.

Previously, based on the provisions of the legislation of the Russian Federation, the tax was calculated taking into account the inventory value of real estate. As a rule, she was not too tall. Therefore, payments did not come in particularly large.



But now everything has changed. At the moment, the calculation of property tax for individuals will take into account the cadastral value of housing. This figure is quite high. So, as some news said, you can expect a significant increase in the amounts due. Sometimes such payments can exceed those that came earlier, 5-6 times. The exact amount is very difficult to calculate. After all, the cadastral value of the home will be considered the tax base.

Deductions

What's next? You should not be afraid of upcoming payments. After all, in one case or another, all citizens are given a kind of deduction. It allows you to get a "discount" on tax. True, its size is not so easy to calculate. Why? It all depends on the cadastral value. This is what is considered the most important component of property tax.

The deduction will also depend on the type of property. It is important to understand that the tax on a private house will be one, on an apartment - another, on a room - third. And the deductions, respectively, for the listed categories of housing are different.

What is every citizen entitled to? To reduce the tax base. Or rather, deducting a certain amount of the cadastral value of housing. Accordingly, the penalties being studied will be less. The deduction is due to all citizens without exception.

The following restrictions are currently set:

  • room - 10 square meters;
  • apartment - 20 square meters m .;
  • house - 50 square meters. m

What does it mean? When assessing housing at the indicated sizes, the cost of the inventory will be reduced. That is, if 1 square. m. of the house is estimated at 10,000 rubles, then the deduction from the total amount will be 50 * 10,000 = 50,000. Benefit Restrictions

But if a citizen has a big house, then he may not receive a deduction. Indeed, in modern calculation rules there are certain restrictions. Not all cases require a standard deduction of square meters.

A huge role is played by the value of real estate. If it exceeds 300,000,000 rubles, then no deductions are required. It is these rules that are currently in force in Russia. Fortunately, even a fairly large house is not priced at a similar amount. The bulk of the population is entitled to the deductions indicated above.

It should also be remembered that the privilege is provided only for 1 property of each type. What does it mean? Suppose a person owns 2 apartments and a house. What's next? He can receive a deduction for 1 of his chosen apartments and separately for the house. You will have to inform your tax authorities in advance of your choice. Accordingly, the tax on a residential building in such a situation will in any case be reduced. But with the apartments, as already noted, will have to be determined.

If silent

A little about those who own a lot of residential real estate. You should pay attention to the fact that, as already mentioned, they will have to decide which apartment to consider with all the benefits. Or which house to offer a discount. And what will happen if this is not done?

In this case, the deduction is retained. But it will be applied to that property (in each of the categories), which has a higher cadastral value. In fact, the rule applies: where the tax is higher, there is a deduction. This should be remembered. If you want to apply a benefit to another property, you can use this right. But only by submitting a standard application to the tax office.



About cadastral value

A huge, one might say, the main, role at the moment, plays the cadastral value of real estate. Property tax levied on a person will directly depend on it.

As a rule, this amount is determined by the state. And the relevant information is entered into the property registry. The cost of home tax can be calculated independently, knowing how much the cadastral price of real estate is. Where to find out about her?

There are several options. The first way is to contact the tax authorities. True, it is unlikely that everyone will talk about cadastral value. Rather, they will immediately report the amount that will have to be paid in the future.

The second approach to solving the problem is to contact the Rosreestr. It is possible on the Internet, it is possible personally. And order a cadastral real estate passport. In this document, the cadastral value of the apartment or house will be prescribed without fail.

The last way is to contact the registration chamber. There you can also order a cadastral passport for a particular property. Issue it within 5 business days. Therefore, it will be possible to calculate without any problems how much the home tax will be in 2016.
