Relaxed holiday in Dzhemete: reviews of tourists

The resort village of Dzhemete is located 5 km from Anapa in the center of Pioneer Avenue. Active people, couples with children and young people will enjoy a vacation in Dzhemete. Reviews of tourists indicate that this village is an excellent option for a versatile pastime. This is a true paradise for Russian and foreign vacationers.





More than half of hotel complexes are concentrated near the coast. If you take a room with a panorama of the Black Sea, then you will see clean beaches, unforgettable sunrises, sunsets and, of course, the gentle azure sea. Indifferent people do not leave rest in Dzhemete. The village receives positive reviews due to the huge number of resorts, guest houses, hotels of different classes.

Sandy beaches in the resort village are considered the most well-groomed throughout the Krasnodar Territory, because they contain unique gold particles. That is why the sand sparkles so brightly in the sun and quickly warms up. Absolutely all beaches are wide, comfortable, with a gentle slope into the water, which is safe for children.

There is a fairly developed tourism industry for every taste and budget. A wide selection of beach services will amaze the most demanding tourist. You can rent a catamaran, go to the open sea to admire the scenery and swim in the crystal clear water, or rent a jet ski and ride the waves with the breeze.
