The best hotels of Kaliningrad: description, photos and reviews

Kaliningrad attracts travelers with its interesting architecture, many ancient sights, beautiful nature. After admiring the wonderful views of the city, in the evening you will want to relax in a cozy atmosphere. Comfortable leisure facilities are provided by the hotels of Kaliningrad.

Hotel Kaiserhof

The location of the Kaiserhof hotel is the cultural and historical complex Rybnaya Derevnya in the center of Kaliningrad. Under the windows of the hotel flows the river Pregolya. Within walking distance are historical and cultural attractions, as a walk you can walk to the Konigsberg Cathedral, the Wooden Bridge, the Cathedral of the Exaltation of the Cross. The hotel is surrounded by beautiful parks and boulevards.

In the restaurant, travelers can not only taste delicious dishes, but also look at the stained-glass windows. The rooms at the hotel are bright and elegantly furnished. Some have a balcony with views of the cathedral or the river. Tourists have the opportunity to use the services of a spa, pool with bar, sun terrace. On the territory of the Kaiserhof hotel in Kaliningrad there is an ATM, currency exchange, free parking. Pets are allowed by prior arrangement. Rooms are available in the following categories:

  • double with city or river view;
  • Deluxe River View
  • Suite with river view;
  • Executive Suite.

The price of the room, if desired, can only include accommodation, you can rent a room with breakfast or half board. There is free wireless internet access.

Hotel Ibis

From the Ibis Hotel in Kaliningrad to the Konigsberg Cathedral is a 10-minute walk, just 300 meters to the Baltic Expo Exhibition Center. Environmentally friendly materials are used to design the rooms. The rooms are well soundproofed. The restaurant menu features Mediterranean dishes. Parking services are paid. Pets are allowed by prior arrangement. Smoking is not allowed throughout the hotel. Guests are accommodated in these rooms:

  • standard double with two beds - 2790 rubles per day;
  • standard double with one bed - 2790 rubles per day.

Breakfast is not included. Upon request, you can pay for the breakfast served in the buffet. There is access to free wireless internet.

Hotel "Europe"

Monuments to Peter the Great and Friedrich Schiller are located within walking distance from the Europa Hotel in Kaliningrad. Just 800 meters you need to go to the Ausfal Gate and the Astronomical Bastion. You can also walk to the Kant Monument, the Dugout Museum and the Kaliningrad Zoo. An ATM machine is installed on site, and free parking is reserved for people with disabilities. You can make the necessary purchases in the convenience store, which is also located on the hotel premises. For accommodation tourists are offered the following room options:

  • single superior - 3300 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 4500 rubles in one day;
  • family studio - 5500 rubles per day;
  • Studio for people with disabilities - 5500 rubles;
  • apartments - 6000 rubles per day.

All rooms with city view. Breakfast is not included. There is free WIFI.

Hotel Agora

From the Agora Hotel in Kaliningrad you can walk to the Royal Gate, the Lithuanian Wall and the Grolman Bastion. The hotel has a cafe; breakfast can be delivered to your room if you wish. Parking is free. The hotel is small, the number of rooms is 22 rooms. All have good soundproofing. There are several types of rooms for guests:

  • standard double with one bed - 1581 rubles per day;
  • double with two beds - 1581 rubles per day;
  • double comfort - 1860 rubles;
  • triple economy - 1395 rubles per day;
  • family junior suite - 3150 rubles;
  • Family Studio - 2232 rubles per day.

Breakfast is not included. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

Hotels of the city of Kaliningrad. Hotel "Golden Bay"

A two-minute walk from the hotel is the Kaliningrad Philharmonic. Also, a few minutes is enough to stroll to the Church of the Holy Family, the House of Arts, the Friedland Bastion, the Cathedral. Nearby is a cozy cafe and a supermarket. In the evenings, guests can enjoy fresh air by the Pregolya River. The hotel has a bar and restaurant, as well as an equipped kitchen. The rooms are decorated in a classic style. Parking is free. Tourists are offered the following accommodation options:

  • single room - 1710 rubles per day;
  • standard double room - 2025 rubles per day;
  • comfort double room - 2115 rubles per day;
  • Studio - 2340 rubles per day;
  • double deluxe room - 2430 rubles per day.

Breakfast is not included in the price. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

Hotel "Berlin"

Looking at hotels in Kaliningrad for accommodation, pay attention to the small cozy hotel "Berlin". Kaliningrad Central Station is just 450 meters from the hotel. Of the nearby attractions, tourists can see the Brandenburg Gate, visit the House of Arts. The Friedland Gate is a 15-minute walk away . The restaurant offers Russian and European cuisines, in the morning breakfast is served according to the "buffet" system, additional charge. You can include it in the room price. Parking is free. The hotel has the following accommodation options:

  • single standard room;
  • standard double room;
  • Junior Suite

Guests have access to free WI-FI.

Hotel "Villa Glamor"

Among other hotels in Kaliningrad, Villa Glamor stands out for its elegant architecture. Guests have access to a sauna, indoor pool and spa. Right in front of the hotel is a picturesque lake, some of which have a view. The hotel's restaurant serves local and international cuisine. Free parking is available. Each room has a minibar. Of the attractions near Villa Glamor are the Amber Museum and Rosgarten Gate. Vacationers can also stroll to Bismarck Square and visit the Botanical Garden. Accommodation is offered in rooms of several categories:

  • double economy - 2562 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 3300 rubles per day;
  • Suite - 5300 rubles per day;
  • Studio - 6300 rubles per day.

The price includes breakfast. Free Wi-Fi access is available.
