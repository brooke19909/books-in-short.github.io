Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel (Turkey, Alanya): hotel description and photos

Founded once by the ancient Greeks, now the Turkish city of Alanya every season attracts thousands of tourists who want to sunbathe under the gentle southern sun and swim in the warm sea. The resort infrastructure here, of course, is developed simply superbly. Comfortable rooms for guests of the country are offered by thousands of hotels of different stars. And with many of them enter into agreements including tour operators from Russia. For example, domestic vacationers who decide to relax in Turkey can be offered trips with accommodation at the Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel.

Location

Reviews about this four-star complex can be found both good and not very good. But in any case, the majority of tourists consider the location of the hotel to be quite successful. The Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel was once built directly in Alanya itself. That is, it is, in fact, precisely an urban complex. And, of course, all the infrastructure of the old large resort of Alanya is available to its guests.





The distance to the sea from the buildings of this hotel is only 100 meters. Its guests need to go to the beach no more than 3 minutes.

Russian tourists who rent rooms at this hotel usually fly to Turkey to the airport of Antalya. This air hub is located far from the hotel, of course, far enough. Tourists should get to the hotel upon arrival approximately 2-2.5 hours. But buses to their customers by domestic operators, judging by the reviews, in most cases provide quite comfortable. So the trip from the airport to the hotel for vacationers is usually not tedious.

Who mostly lives in the hotel

It is believed that this hotel is best suited for lovers of a quiet beach holiday. Most often, tourists with children or senior citizens come here to rest. But young people at the Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel stay a lot. This hotel is suitable for people with disabilities. Here they are equipped with special rooms.

A lot of Turks also live in this hotel. Some domestic tourists note the fact that they, unfortunately, can often behave noisily and even arrogantly.

Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel: traveler room reviews

There are mixed reviews about rooms rented at the hotel on the Web. And this is not surprising. The hotel is equipped with both very small rooms and quite spacious. On the ground floor, some rooms have direct views of the water slides. And accordingly, tourists relaxing by the pool can see everything that happens inside such a room. Therefore, residents of such apartments have to keep the curtains permanently closed. And they in this hotel, unfortunately, are quite dark and dense.

But there are very good spacious and bright rooms in the hotel. Those tourists who are lucky to settle in them respond very well about them. However, those vacationers who got not too good apartments can fix the situation quite easily. With a certain amount of perseverance or for a fee, hotel administrators can relocate newly arrived guests to more comfortable rooms.

With regard to cleaning, the opinions of tourists about this hotel also differ. In some rooms Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel (Alanya) maids come almost every day. For some reason, they can simply be forgotten. Towels in this hotel, unfortunately, are rarely changed. Maids often do not even pay attention to tourists specially thrown to the floor.

Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel staff reviews

The employees of this hotel are praised in most cases. They treat their guests politely. But sometimes the staff of this hotel, unfortunately, can show some arrogance towards their guests, including the Russians.

What vacationers think about food in the complex

Accommodation at the Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel who wants to spend their holidays in Turkey, as it belongs to the four-star group, is offered on an all-inclusive basis. The menu in the restaurant of the complex, as noted by many vacationers, unfortunately, is not too diverse. But at the same time, the dishes here are quite substantial and tasty. There is enough food offered by the "buffet" system in the dining room of the hotel to all vacationers living in it. Judging by the reviews, the hotel guests are not hungry either at breakfast, lunch or dinner.





The hotel bar offers its residents a fairly wide selection of drinks. This establishment is generally praised by hotel guests. However, some vacationers note the fact that the bartenders at the hotel do not prepare cocktails for tourists very willingly and offer them without much cordiality.

What are the beach hotel reviews?

Vacationers are going to Turkey, including Russian, of course, first of all, in order to spend as much time as possible on the beach under the gentle southern sun, to sunbathe and get better, swimming in the sea. Therefore, an important criterion for evaluating a particular hotel is, of course, the convenience of its beach. In this regard, only good reviews can be read about the Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel on the Internet. The beach at this hotel is really good.

Actually, the coastline itself, covered with soft sand, is not particularly clean here. But there are no mud deposits on the hotel beach. Some tourists recommend that the administration simply put on the beach more ballot boxes. Then it will become much cleaner. But be that as it may, the sea itself near the hotel, according to many vacationers, is simply magnificent. Entrance to it on the beach is very convenient. The depth in this place does not begin too far from the coast. No silt or mud in the water on the hotel beach does not swim.





Where is better to shop in the city

There are many shops of different specializations near the Kleopatra Royal Palm Hotel (Alanya) in Alanya . But experienced vacationers advise newcomers to go shopping in the city center nevertheless. Buses 1, 101 go there . The trip to the city center from the hotel is only about 3 lira (1 lira = 15.94 rubles). Many tourists in Alanya advise vacationers to visit Ataturka Boulevard. There are really very good shops where you can buy good things. To get to this street, you need to get off the bus at the GRAND PAZARI stop and go a little backward.
