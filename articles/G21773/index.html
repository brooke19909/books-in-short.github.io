We perform a warm crochet bactus: schemes and description

We will knit the product according to the following fairly simple scheme.

The size of the scarf will be as follows: 166 x 37 cm. We will begin to crochet the bactus with a magic ring. Next, we will make three air loops (VP) necessary for lifting. Then we make two columns with one double crochet (CH). Turn over knitting. In the second row, we perform three lifting loops and 1 CH in the same base loop. Then we make two more double crochets. In the third row (overturning knitting), we knit three VP and 4 CH, and the last two columns with a crochet are made in one loop. We start the fourth row with 3 air loops. We knit 1 CH in the same base loop. And then perform another 4 CH. We will start the fifth row with 3 VP and we will knit further according to the following pattern: "1 VP - 1 CH", alternating these elements. The next three rows (from the sixth to the ninth) will be executed, like the first four, using columns with one crochet and making the necessary increases in each row. We will make the tenth row openwork, using the alternation scheme of air loops and double crochets. When your product reaches the desired width, begin to make gradual reductions, thus forming a "triangle".
