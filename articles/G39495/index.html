Train "Rostov-Anapa": how to get from one city to another?

The Rostov-Anapa train is a route between major cities in southern Russia. Through Rostov in the direction of Anapa passes a huge number of flights of trains.

History of the Rostov station

Railway "Anapa - Rostov" is considered one of the main directions used by passengers in the North Caucasus. But it all started not from this path! The first railway line, which was held before Rostov, began in Taganrog. The construction of this path ended in 1869. Of course, for the sake of one train a day, no one began to build the station building, but simply set up a platform like modern train stops in villages. In the 1870s, the construction of the railway in the North Caucasus continued actively. A few more routes from different directions were brought to the Rostov station, so there was a need to erect a three-story building for passengers. Anapa Station

Railway "Anapa - Rostov" is the beginning of a large railway line. Anapa station is a dead end, therefore, transit trains do not pass through this transport hub. The branch to this city was built relatively late - in 1978. This year, the railway station was opened. The building was renovated in 2005 using energy efficiency technologies. Most trains to this station are seasonal. In winter, very few depart from Anapa, and much more in the summer. The main passenger flow at this station occurs from June to September, that is, there is a link to the sea holiday season.





The station accepts not only passenger, but also freight trains. It is fairly easy for trains to approach this station, as 10 access roads have been built. At the station itself, 2 platforms for passenger trains are equipped.

Rostov - Anapa: distance by train and stop stations

The distance between cities by rail differs from the length of the path along the highway, and is 410 kilometers. Some trains travel along a longer track (493 kilometers). By Russian standards, such a distance between major centers is considered small. Of course, there are smaller distances between the large cities of federal subjects, but there are also more complex options.

The Rostov-Anapa train makes 5 stops along the standard route. The first parking lot takes place at Starominskaya station, which is 106 kilometers from Rostov. The second is located 51 kilometers after the first. This is a station Kanevskaya. The stopping point Bryukhovetskaya is located 44 km from the second parking lot. In just 20 km there will be a Timashevskaya station. Then there will be 83 km without stops until the train reaches Protoka station (the last stop in front of the terminal station). From Protoka to Anapa, the composition will still have to overcome the 118 km run. The Rostov-Anapa train quickly delivers passengers from one city to another.

The duration of the journey is longer than that of the flight described above, because this train runs along a different route. Travel range 493 km. The train will pass through Kushchevka, Vyselki, Korenovsk, Dinskaya, Krasnodar and Abinskaya station. At 01:04 (the dates do not coincide with the Minsk train), the Smolensk-Anapa flight departs towards Anapa (arrival at the terminal station is scheduled for noon). The train from Yekaterinburg, which leaves from the Rostov station at 01:59, will spend 8 hours 1 minute on the way. This train will follow the shortest path with virtually no stops. How to leave Anapa?

The Anapa-Rostov-on-Don train is a flight to a fairly popular destination. Every day, several trains (mainly transit) that connect these cities run along the route. The time it takes for trains on different routes to travel from one city to another is significantly different. The minimum travel time is 6 hours 59 minutes, the maximum is 11 hours 38 minutes.

There are no early morning flights from Anapa to Rostov. The first train (Anapa - Moscow) leaves at 9 hours 10 minutes. In Rostov, the composition will be at 16:51. The second Moscow train for the day starts from the Anapa railway station at 13 hours and 40 minutes. Passengers will be able to leave in the capital of the Don region in 7 hours 41 minutes. The third flight to Moscow leaves at 14:20. The composition will follow these stations:

  • Bryukhovetskaya;

The train will reach Rostov at one in the morning.

Then the train "Anapa - Rostov-on-Don" (meaning not the name of the flight, but the starting station and the end of the journey for some passengers) leaves at intervals of a maximum of 1.5 hours.





Conclusion

Passengers will be able to get by train from Rostov to Anapa, as well as back, at any time. Trains in these directions run at intervals of 2 to 3 hours maximum.
