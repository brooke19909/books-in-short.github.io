Reviews of holders of the Freebie card in Russia often indicate that problems may arise during plastic processing. In particular, due to credit limits. What is it about?

The fact is that the amount of lending directly depends on the size of the citizen's salary. The higher it is, the more chances there are for a large limit of funds for freebie plastic.
