Private hotel "Villa Reef", Adler: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Adler, along with Sochi and Anapa, is one of the most popular resorts among domestic tourists. This southern city is famous for its huge number of attractions, warm sea, picturesque nature and clean beaches. Of course, the tourist infrastructure in this resort is also well developed. Inexpensive comfortable housing, for example, many hotels offer here. Refers to their number and "Villa Reef."

Location Features

This hotel is located in the city of Adler, on the first coastline, in Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay. You can get to the beach from its territory in just 2 minutes. In the immediate vicinity of this hotel is one of the most popular tourist attractions - the Southern Cultures Park. Also pretty close to the Villa Reef Hotel (Adler) is the Imereti Sea Port. Just a short drive from the hotel is the Sochi Olympic Stadium.





From the airport of Adler for the hotel can be reached by public transport or taxi. But it is best to book a transfer in advance at the hotel. This service (from the railway station and Adler Airport) is included in the price.

General description of the hotel

The Hotel Villa Reef (Adler) is a modern five-story building with painted facades and large balconies. A comfortable wide staircase of several steps leads to the hotel lobby, and its territory is covered with neat tiles. For the convenience of guests, benches are installed in the courtyard of the hotel. Actually, the hotel territory itself is surrounded by a fence.

Most of the rooms at this hotel have magnificent sea views. From other rooms, residents can enjoy the river or the mountains.

Of course, booking rooms in the Villa Reef Hotel (Adler), as in any other on the Black Sea, is in advance. You can arrange rental of rooms in this hotel by calling the administrator or by filling out a special form on one of the travel agency sites.





Hotel reviews

Tourists have a very good opinion of this hotel. Among the advantages, its guests primarily note the well-groomed territory and the proximity of many significant objects of the city.

The main disadvantage of this hotel among tourists is considered to be too close to the airport. When landing, aircraft sometimes fly literally over the roof of the 2 * Villa Reef Hotel (Adler). Which, of course, does not contribute to a relaxing holiday for guests.

Number of rooms

Everything in this hotel is for rent 24 rooms. If desired, guests of Adler can remove:

  • standard with a double bed;

  • standard with two single beds;

  • two-room suite.





All rooms at Villa Reef Hotel (Adler), in addition to beds, wardrobes, mirrors and nightstands, include:

  • TV sets

  • air conditioners;

  • refrigerators.

The description of the hotel on travel agency sites indicates that the televisions in its rooms are equipped with large modern flat-screen TVs. However, many vacationers in reviews write that this is not entirely true. Some rooms are equipped with conventional models. But at the same time, in some cases, guests of the Villa Reef Hotel mention the presence of even two televisions in the room.

Wi-Fi at this hotel is free. You can use it directly in the rooms. Many tourists, of course, attribute its presence to the advantages of the hotel. But, unfortunately, judging by the reviews of the guests, the Internet in the hotel is too slow and not very stable.

Showers at this hotel are individual in each room. If desired, guests can, among other things, use a hairdryer here.

Villa Reef, 2 * Hotel (Adler): room reviews

The opinion about the apartments rented at this hotel was very good among its guests. First of all, vacationers praise the rooms of the Villa Reef hotel for their large area. Many also note that rooms are cleaned regularly. The equipment in the rooms, according to most guests, is not too new, but quite working. About the same applies to furniture. She is not particularly modern in the rooms, but not shabby.

Another unconditional advantage of the hotel, many consider the presence of large balconies. Here you can not only dry clothes, but also admire the surroundings from here. And the views from the balconies of this hotel are really beautiful.





Tourists do not report any flaws in the rooms of this hotel in specialized forums. The only comments are made about plumbing. She sometimes breaks in this hotel.

Hotel Villa Reef (Adler): service reviews

The rooms of this hotel, therefore, appeal to most Adler guests. But about the service at this hotel, unfortunately, there are not too good reviews. Many guests consider the Villa Reef staff not too polite. They may simply not respond to requests from guests to fix any problems. There are times when hotel employees behave rudely towards guests.

Hotel infrastructure

The hotel "Villa Reef" (Adler) belongs to the two-star group. Therefore, it does not provide too many additional free services to its guests. On the hotel territory its guests can use:

  • free parking;

  • outdoor pool;

  • pool table;

  • the terrace.





In the evening, hotel guests have the opportunity to walk in the garden. In addition to billiards, the hotel has a large dining area and a TV.

Accommodations

To book rooms in this hotel, as well as other Black Sea, and especially in high season, is in advance. Hotel rooms may only be paid in cash. There are no special rules for staying at Villa Reef Hotel. The only thing is that guests are not allowed to smoke in the rooms. No pets are allowed in this hotel.

Children in Villa Reef, 2 * Hotel (Adler), reviews of family vacations in which are mostly positive, are accepted from any age. If necessary, their parents can rent a crib at the hotel.

Hotel beach

In relation to the sea, most tourists consider Villa Reef to be located very conveniently. There is a public beach within walking distance of this hotel. The water in the sea here, unfortunately, is usually quite dirty. And the people in this place often rest a lot. Therefore, in most cases, hotel guests prefer to go swimming at another beach - Olympic. Water in the sea in this part of the coastline is much cleaner. And the infrastructure in the city is developed, of course, much better.

The guests of the Villa Reef Hotel also have to reach the Olympic beach not too long - about 10 minutes. In order to get on it, you just need to cross the bridge over the river.





Dining at the hotel

Unfortunately, the 2 * hotel (Sochi, Adler) does not offer the boarding house or even breakfasts at Villa Reef. Therefore, its residents have to eat in the city. Most hotel guests are advised to have breakfast, lunch, and dinner in the nearby Athens dining room. The menu in this catering establishment is quite diverse, and the dishes themselves are delicious. The cost in the dining room of Athens is not too expensive.

For breakfast, guests at Villa Reef can also buy groceries at local supermarkets. The hotel rooms, among other things, include a set of dishes. Also, guests have the opportunity to order breakfast in a small cafe located on the territory of the hotel. True, the dishes here are very expensive and this place is not particularly popular with guests.

Room rates

The availability of room rental in this hotel is considered by many guests to be its undoubted advantages. As in any other Black Sea hotel, the cost of rooms at Villa Reef directly depends on the season. In winter, the rooms cost about 1200 per night per person. In summer, their price reaches 2000. For a hotel with such a set of services, this, of course, is not so much.

City infrastructure near the hotel

In addition to the Southern Cultures Park and various Olympic venues, in the immediate vicinity of the Villa Reef Hotel there are the Amphibius Water Park and the central promenade of the city. Of the significant objects located near the hotel, one can also distinguish the Mandarin television and radio complex. Hotel guests usually buy products at the Magnit chain store located a couple of steps away from it or in the central Adler market. In the latter case, food is the cheapest. However, the market is located, unfortunately, quite far from the hotel. Therefore, in most cases, hotel guests prefer to visit the Magnit store.
