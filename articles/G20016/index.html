Hurghada is a city in Egypt, which is one of the main tourist centers of the country. This resort is located on the west coast of the Red Sea. The hot weather, which is kept here constantly, as well as the almost complete absence of precipitation, allow numerous tourists to come to Hurghada almost all year round (except for winter).

A resort for every taste

Locals who work in hotels are always happy for vacationers who have arrived in Egypt. Hurghada is a first-class resort, here you can choose a hotel for every taste and budget.

The city stretched for tens of kilometers along the coast. Hot and dry climate, as well as rich infrastructure make Hurghada one of the most popular tourist centers of the Red Sea. A unique feature of the resort is coral reefs stretching along the entire coast. This makes Hurghada one of the world's diving centers.

How to get there

Russian tourists can fly here by charter flights from any major city. Usually the price of tickets is included in the price of tours to Egypt. Hurghada is far from the airports. If you want to make the trip yourself, you should fly to Cairo with a regular flight, and then by plane or bus to get to Hurghada. A tourist visa can be obtained at the airport upon arrival.

The resort itself should be reached by taxi. It is advisable to agree on the price in advance, after the trip is over it is better not to enter into disputes with the driver. In Cairo, you can rent a car and get to Hurghada on your own. But be careful. Egyptians are not as honest as Europeans. The car may be faulty, and you will notice this far from immediately. You are entitled to insurance, feel free to demand it.

Locals

According to many tourists, the best resort for anyone who prefers a holiday in Egypt is Hurghada. Guest reviews are filled with memories of beautiful vacation days. Locals are one of the main disadvantages of the resort. Almost all vacationers complain about them. Egypt is a poor country, and tourists here are the main source of income for many people. Therefore, local residents always try to tear off an extra dollar from a gapeous guest. Among them there are wonderful, decent, hospitable people. But be on the alert. If you ask the price of a product from a market trader, then it may be overpriced for you. He will sell it locally cheaper. This also applies to drivers and guides. In hotels, the rules are strict, managers carefully monitor the honesty of employees.

How to avoid unpleasant surprises

The Mirage Waterpark Hotel (Hurghada) offers its guests a variety of animations. During the day, you can play soccer, volleyball, basketball or darts. In the evening, the animators play a fun theatrical performance.

To avoid unpleasant surprises outside the hotel, always ask how much local people pay for a similar service. If a conflict does occur, keep in mind that there is a tourist police in Egypt guarding your interests. Appeal to the authorities is better used as a threat. Doing this is undesirable if you do not know a large number of nuances. You can complain about theft, overpricing, the imposition of goods and services. However, all this must be confirmed - a paid check or other evidence.

Choice of transport

Accustomed to cycling in Egypt, it is better to forget about it. Iron horses can be rented in almost every hotel. But on the roads of this country to travel is very dangerous. Local drivers drive without any rules. And if you add to this the poor condition of the roads, it becomes clear why you should prefer hiking or taxis. Inveterate cyclists who love rest in Hurghada, hotels are chosen based on these facts.





Excursions

The city has three districts: the central (El Dahar), the old (Sakkala) and the new, which is also called the hotel. All the resort hotels are concentrated in it. In Hurghada itself there are almost no attractions. However, in any hotel you will find an abundance of excursions. Most tourists are transported to large cities - Cairo and Luxor. And magnificent pyramids must be seen by every guest of Egypt. Very often representatives of the tour operator offer boat trips on a yacht. Traveling to the picturesque islands, you can go scuba diving and admire the coral.

Pools and beach

Tourists who prefer to relax in five-star hotels often choose the Mirage Waterpark (Hurghada). The hotel got its name thanks to the waterslides located on the territory. Entertainment will find children and adults. The hotel pool is amazing. It consists of several zones. There is a children's pool with a small slide for kids. A large area for swimming lovers, several beautiful figured pools. And a separate area with water slides. Sun loungers are covered with canopies, because the Egyptian sun is very hot.

At a distance of only a few tens of meters from the pool, the beach begins. It is also equipped with comfortable sunbeds with awnings. The Mirage Waterpark Hotel (Hurghada) has a sandy beach, which is rarely seen in Egypt. The entrance to the sea is flat and gentle. The shallow zone extends far - several tens of meters.





In Hurghada, sandy beaches are quite common. Fans of underwater landscapes will be disappointed. Pontoon beaches, allowing plenty of fish and corals to admire, are rare here. Therefore, a vacation with children in Hurghada will be much more pleasant than in Sharm El Sheikh. Pontoon beaches dominate the Sinai Peninsula. The coastline is overgrown with sharp corals, so tourists enter the sea from a special floating bridge. Having descended into the water, vacationers immediately find themselves at a depth of several meters. Therefore, rest with kids in hotels with pontoon beaches is difficult.

Floor plans and equipment rooms

Mirage Waterpark (Hurghada) has a large landscaped area. The building is tall and beautiful. Tourists can choose their accommodation from several possible layouts. All rooms are quite spacious, their area is about fifty square meters. You can order a standard room with a view of the sea or the hotel grounds. At the request of the client, the manager provides a room with a private pool or panoramic window. Each room has a balcony or terrace.





All air-conditioned rooms have a fridge and TV. There is also a hairdryer, electric kettle and telephone. Each room has a free safe, but Wi-Fi can only be obtained for extra money. But free internet is equipped with a lobby bar.

  • imported drinks;

  • the shops;
  • diving school.



Here you can play tennis and billiards, visit the gym. There is also a laundry room and a beauty salon. The lobby bar serves fresh juices. For romantics, an order for flowers or a limousine is provided.

Holiday for baby

The Mirage Waterpark Hotel (Hurghada) is designed for families with children. There is a cot for babies, and the restaurant has highchairs. On the territory there are shallow pools, including those with water slides. The restaurant offers a special menu. For older children there is a mini club, disco, slot machines.





A restaurant

What alluring words - Egypt, Hurghada! Mirage Waterpark is a great option for tourists who like to eat deliciously. The food in the main restaurant of the hotel is very good and varied. A lot of vegetables - fresh, stewed and grilled. Several types of fruit are served every day. A good choice of first courses, among which there is always a cold soup. Hot here is beyond praise. The menu includes not only several types of meat and fish, but also seafood. Grilled tiger prawns are a delicacy that will cost a lot of money in any cafe in the world. And in the hotel restaurant you will be served it for free, because the all-inclusive system works here. There are also several types of pastries, hot and cold drinks, as well as locally produced alcohol.

Arabic style food

Egypt is famous all over the world for its national cuisine. As a cold appetizer, you will be offered cottage cheese with herbs, dishes from pickled eggplant, and thick vegetable soup. Chopped meat - kebab and dolma will be served on hot. Rice with a variety of toppings will serve as a side dish: vegetables, nuts or figs, as well as flat cakes, which are used instead of bread. Egyptian desserts are very original. Most of them are made on the basis of milk and is a variant of souffl√© or pudding. Nuts, dried fruits and ice cream are added to it. All Egyptian national dishes have complex and practically unpronounceable names.





If for some reason you have to dine outside the hotel, you must carefully follow the rules of hygiene. Do not eat vegetables and fruits that lie in the open sun, are damaged, or have a strange color. Do not eat a lot of meat and fish, they can be cooked without proper heat treatment, which in the heat conditions causes at least intestinal upset.

Shopping in Egypt

On the territory of Mirage Aqua park SPA 5 stars there are many shops. Shopping is an indispensable entertainment for vacationers in Egypt. A noisy oriental bazaar is a must for every tourist. Approaching the market, you will see locals who will offer to take to a good and cheap store. Do not believe them. You will be taken to an expensive outlet, the owner of which pays such guides a percentage for each buyer.

If you are attracted to Hurghada, the 5-star Mirage Waterpark is a hotel that will appeal to every tourist. Here you can book excursions to all the attractions of Egypt.

Learn to bargain in the market

In the eastern bazaar it is better to walk on your own. Do not rush to purchase the product you like. Look around first, find out the prices that local pay. In the eastern bazaar, it is customary to bargain. If you master all the subtleties of this process, the Egyptians will cease to consider you a stranger. Then it will be possible to buy a good thing cheaply. Do not buy anything near historical sites; prices are much higher there.

The hotel complex Mirage Aqua park SPA 5 stars includes a SPA-salon. Here you can relax in the sauna, hammam or steam bath, as well as swim in the jacuzzi and use the services of a massage therapist.





Ignorance of the Arabic language, including numbers, is an excellent reason to raise the cost of goods to the ceiling. Ask one of the hotel staff to write an approximate price tag for you. Knowing what the numbers look like in Egypt, you can start bargaining at the bazaar. You can reduce the price not only in the market, but also in Arab stores. In addition to those that are arranged according to the European model.

What can I buy in Egypt? Antique statuettes and jewelry, as well as natural aromatic oils. Often, tourists buy a hookah with elegant coinage or locally produced ceramics. A wonderful souvenir is natural papyrus.
