Hotel Pantheon Hotel 3 * (Crete, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Amazingly beautiful place in which Pantheon Hotel 3 * is located, - Greece, about. Crete, Rethymno. It is perfect for lovers of a cozy family vacation.





Rethymno

The western part of Crete, in which Rethymno is located, is considered the most picturesque place in Greece. No wonder it is called paradise on the island. The city is located between Chania and Heraklion, about 80 km. from Greece. It combines cultural and natural attractions.

In Rethymno itself is quite crowded. The population of the city is approximately 30,000 people, so it is difficult to call it a quiet and peaceful place for silence and relaxation. There are many bars, restaurants, shops and entertainment. The nightlife is also quite rapid. Therefore, lovers of a secluded and relaxing holiday are better off staying in the surrounding villages.

Near one of these villages is Pantheon Hotel 3 *. It is located on a hill in the shade of a magnificent garden. The hotel offers stunning views of the sea and the Venetian castle.

Weather in Rethymnon

Throughout Greece, the Mediterranean climate prevails. It guarantees dry, hot summers and warm winters, so Rethymnon is considered a very comfortable place to spend a vacation. It rains only in winter. In summer, the air warms up to 32 degrees, and in winter the temperature rarely drops below 12 degrees.

Hotel Description

Pantheon Hotel 3 * (Crete) is located in the western part of the city, near the village of Atsipopoulo. Two kilometers from the hotel there is Koube Beach, and nearby are shops, bars, cafes and a supermarket. Distance Pantheon Hotel 3 * - Rethymnon (to the center) is about three kilometers.





The peculiarity of the hotel is that it is located on a unique hill with a stunning panorama of the sea and the surrounding area. Along the perimeter of the whole territory beautiful fragrant plants grow, thanks to the shade of which even in extreme heat there is a coolness.

The hotel has its own outdoor pool with a comfortable sitting area. There is a snack bar by the pool where guests can enjoy refreshing drinks, cocktails or snacks. For children there is a separate pool.

Guest apartments

Pantheon Hotel 3 * has 55 rooms. The following accommodation options are available for guests:

  • standard single rooms;
  • standard double rooms with a double bed or two single beds. It is possible to order an extra single bed or put a crib in the room;
  • family rooms.

Each room has a balcony with beautiful views of the sea and the surrounding scenic landscapes.





Room Amenities

For a comfortable stay in the room provides the following amenities:

  • a guest room with a shower or bath, a guest can use a cosmetic set, a hairdryer for drying hair and an individual towel;
  • the apartments have air conditioning;
  • The rooms have cable TV and radio;
  • a refrigerator is provided for food storage;
  • Each room has a safe and telephone.

Hotel infrastructure

In the Pantheon Hotel 3 * (Crete, Rethymnon), the following infrastructure facilities are provided for vacationers:

  • round-the-clock reception, where friendly and friendly staff work;
  • in the lobby of the hotel there are comfortable sofas for relaxing and a TV;
  • a parking place is provided for free;
  • in the hotel library you can take a book or magazine;
  • bed linen is changed every three days, room cleaning is carried out daily;
  • Awakening service, room service;
  • Those who wish can rent a car;
  • vacationers can laundry in the laundry;
  • fax sending or receiving service;
  • trips to Rethymnon and the beach are carried out by a mini-bus;
  • Hotel guests can take advantage of 24-hour medical and babysitting services.
  • organization of excursions.




For hotel guests there is a cafe-bar, a restaurant (with an open veranda) and a snack bar by the pool.

Entertainment

The hotel has a well-kept clean pool, around which sun loungers are placed. The hotel has a special room where you can play table and electronic games, there is a mini-golf and table tennis court on site, and a children's playground for children.

sights

Rethymno looks like an open-air museum. Here you can see medieval architectural monuments made in the Venetian style of the church, Catholic churches and Ottoman mosques.

The main attraction of Rethymnon is the Venetian fortress of Fortezza, which offers a bewitching view from the Pantheon Hotel 3 *. The fortress attracts tourists not only as a historical monument, but also as an excellent observation deck, from where you can see the panorama of the city.

Preveli is the Orthodox monastery of Crete, the road to which leads through the Kurtaliot Gorge, a very beautiful and bewitching place. Also, tourists are recommended to visit:

  • port Venetian lighthouse;
  • the Venetian village of Chromonastiri;
  • Archaeological Museum;
  • Monasteries of Arkadi and Agia Irini;
  • The medieval village of Amnatos.

Nature lovers should take excursions outside the city. The surroundings of Rethymnon is a completely different world with beautiful vegetation, unique landscapes and winding paths.

Beach

The nearest beach from Pantheon Hotel can be reached on foot (20-25 minutes) or by shuttle bus. The distance is 2 km. This is a small picturesque beach for a quiet family holiday with children. The sea here is clean and calm. A more lively city beach is a 20-minute walk away. The beaches in Rethymnon are sandy, clean, with developed infrastructure.





Hotel reviews

Reviews about Pantheon Hotel 3 * (Crete, Rethymno) are both positive and negative.

Positive information:

  • helpful and friendly staff;
  • delicious breakfasts and dinners, the menu is varied, in 10 days it was repeated only 1 time;
  • free parking is available near the hotel;
  • the pool is not crowded, there are free places for rest, clean it every day;
  • the hotel rooms are quite comfortable;
  • nearby the hotel there is a tavern, cafes and restaurants, as well as three supermarkets and shops;
  • quiet, comfortable and beautiful place.

Negative Information:

  • It is far from Rethymnon, including the sea, you need to get on the bus, after making an appointment;
  • staff speaks English only;
  • there are practically no entertainments on the territory.




General impression

Pantheon Hotel 3 * is a good option for a budget holiday with a family. The hotel is located in a quiet and picturesque place. Holidays here are more suitable for tourists who prefer excursions and sightseeing. The main disadvantage of the hotel is its great distance from the sea, which you need to get to by transport. The rest of the hotel made a positive impression.
