Review of popular multicookers "Vitek"

Creating a slow cooker was a breakthrough. Thanks to this appliance, you can easily cook food without using pots, stoves, pans. One of the large companies that develop such devices is Vitek. It produces not only household appliances, but also various kitchen appliances. Consider the most popular devices. Reviews about the Vitek multicookers described in the article are positive.

VT-4277 BK

This device has interesting characteristics, for example, multi-cook, auto-heating, delayed start and so on.

The multicooker case is made of plastic. It is heat resistant. Basic shades: black and bright steel. A special handle is provided for transportation. In order to open the multicooker cover, press the key. The kit includes a bowl, ladle, spatula, recipe book, instructions for use, measuring cup, glasses for yogurt and a lid. Judging by the reviews, this configuration is pretty good.

Thanks to the cookbook of recipes for the Vitek multicooker, you can cook steamed dishes, fry foods, stew them.
