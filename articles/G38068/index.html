Floral ornament for painting. Ornaments for spot painting

Admiring the skillfully painted thing, one often wonders how the author thought out the composition. It seems that every element - a leaf, a twig, a pattern - everything is in a verified place. And all because the ornament for painting was perfectly designed. If so, then even in a simple work, the absolute completeness of the storyline can be traced.



Definition

From Latin ornamentum is translated as a pattern, which is based on the repetition and / or alternation of its main and minor elements. It was used from ancient times to decorate utensils, weapons, furniture and other things. The ornament for painting can be used for large areas of architectural structures and miniatures in decorative art.

Defining the style of the composition, several varieties of the pattern can be distinguished. Depending on the nature of the surface, it is applied with a tape, like a border, in the center, filling the entire surface or part of it. Based on the motif, an ornament can consist of geometric shapes, abstract forms (dots, straight lines, broken lines), elements that replicate plants, animals, people. Also often fragments of architecture, weapons, heraldic signs are present.



Floral ornament for painting

The variety of natural forms is more pleasing to the eye than the monotony of geometric patterns. Masters often select an ornament for painting, observing nature. Even in a simple blade of grass you can find a motive and translate it into a stylized form.

It is believed that almost every plant is ornamental. Its device involves the presence of repeating elements. Leaves, mustaches, flowers - they are arranged symmetrically in a certain order. Bends have naturally smooth radii, and they can be arbitrarily completed in imaginary circles.

A simple pattern can have one flower, and leaves will diverge from it to the sides. Complication can be associated with the inclusion in the circle of elements. With distance from the center, the sizes of the constituent parts also decrease. If the ornament is located in a strip, then all the following successive repeating elements must have a constant size and organically connect at the final stage.

Gorodets

This craft originated in the middle of the 19th century. The name of the technique received from the village of the same name. What is special about Gorodets painting? Ornaments, in addition to plant motifs, are represented by vivid genre scenes. Often in combination with floral patterns, horses and roosters are present. Feature of the technique: free stroke and white or black graphic stroke along the contour. Spinning wheels, shutters, furniture, doors and kitchen utensils were often decorated with murals.

There are several stages of execution. First, the main color spot is placed. Then gradually undermining takes place. Vegetable elements are added: rosettes, buds, berries, grooves. The more complex the motive, the more steps it takes to model the pattern.

In the Gorodetsky ornament, the type of pattern ‚Äúflower strip‚Äù is well preserved. In different versions, it divided the product into tiers. At the edges, painting is done in narrower stripes. In this design, the product is three-tiered.



Ornaments for spot painting

The peculiarity of this type of creativity in the method of applying paints. It uses spot painting on different surfaces, but looks especially good on glass. In this case, stencils with contours of the pattern located behind the decorated transparent surface are used for work. On a tree or other material, the sketch is pre-applied with a pencil or helium pen in the tone of the future color.

It is painted with dots. The motive of the picture may be different. You can choose a template or come up with an original composition. Points are placed at different intervals. You can make them different in size, combine in different combinations. Of particular importance is the symmetry. The technique is closer in execution to a geometric ornament, since it is important to maintain proportions. Work requires endurance and accuracy, but after proper execution, even a simple painting will look spectacular and stylish.
