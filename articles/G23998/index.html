Hotel Atali Village 3 * (Crete, Rethymno, Bali): description and photos

Just recently, the resort village of Bali on the island of Crete was an inconspicuous fishing village, which did not attract travel enthusiasts at all. But today Bali is a rather popular and modern resort that has cordially opened its doors to tourists from all over the world. It is here that you can enjoy a relaxing holiday, picturesque nature, the beauty of the sea and just a romantic atmosphere. The village of Bali is located literally in the middle between Heraklion and Rethymnon, a little away from National Road, on the banks of several beautiful and cozy bays. The village got its name back in the days of Turkish rule and translated from Turkish Bali means "honey". Indeed, a quiet and in its own way "home" resort town receives a lot of tourists.



Location

Atali Village Hotel 3 * (Bali, Crete, Greece) is located on the largest Greek island - Crete, in the village of Bali, about 40 km from the capital of the island of Heraklion (airport) and 26 km from Rethymnon. Holidays here are suitable for those who value clean and comfortable beaches, the azure sea, hiking in picturesque places. A bus stop is located 500 meters from Atali Village, which allows you to independently visit the cities of Crete. The distance of the hotel from the airport is up to 30 km, that is, 10-15 minutes by taxi (or bus). Atali Village 3 * ( Crete, Rethymno, Bali) is located on the 2nd line, that is, the distance to the sea is from 100 to 200 meters.

About hotel

Hotel Atali Village 3 * (Crete, Rethymno, Bali) was founded back in 1994 and has a central building and bungalows in its location. Today, there are 124 rooms, the design of which is made in a traditional style. In the central three-story building, built in a traditional Mediterranean style, you can book a room:

  • Standard, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.
  • Double Room - a room with increased comfort.
  • Doubles Superior - a deluxe room.




Residential and common areas of Atali Village 3 * (Crete, Rethymnon, Bali) are decorated in bright colors. The modesty of the interiors is compensated by cleanliness, beautiful views from the windows - to the sea or to the low mountains covered with bushes. The hotel positions itself as a budget vacation option, but despite this, the service is at the highest level, the cleanliness of the rooms and territory allows you to relax with young children, and the chic views and fascinating nature captivate the hearts of literally every visiting guest.

Infrastructure

The hotel infrastructure includes the following facilities and services:

  • parking lot;
  • garden;
  • bar;
  • a restaurant;
  • lobby;
  • retail outlets with souvenirs;
  • hairdresser services;
  • currency exchange point;
  • dry cleaning / laundry;
  • free wifi
  • postal services, photocopy and fax;
  • playground
  • small kids club;
  • TV room.

By the order of vacationers, excursions can be organized, and you can also order a taxi and a phone call in the morning. Hotel facilities include car rental. You can use it if you have an international driving license. The territory of the complex is quite compact, but well equipped and landscaped. Here you can easily find a shady corner to read a book or go to the sports ground.

Rooms

Rooms Atali Village Hotel 3 * ("Atali Village Hotel") are designed to accommodate 2-4 guests. They have a double or two single beds and, if necessary, you can order an extra bed. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, in which there are: shower, toilet, washbasin, hairdryer. The rooms are equipped with balconies or access to the terrace. A small fridge, TV or hairdryer is available upon request. For an additional fee, the guest will find a safe, air conditioning or heater. Accommodation at the hotel implies an all-inclusive scheme. Rooms are cleaned daily; bed linen is changed twice a week. Room service is available around the clock, and you can order an additional service by phone from your room. The flooring of the room is ceramic tile.





Beach, pool, outdoor activities

Vacationers can access the free public beach, located 200 meters from the hotel, with a shallow bottom. Bali is famous for its beautiful and golden sandy beaches. If tourists do not want to relax on a common beach, in this case the hotel offers to be located near its own freshwater pool with the possibility of "relaxing under an umbrella." For the smallest there is a children's pool with a shallow depth. Sun loungers and umbrellas on the public beach are paid, by the pool - free.

On the territory of Atali Village 3 * (Greece, Bali, Crete, Rethymno) there is a tennis court, a ping-pong and darts court, as well as billiard tables. Sports enthusiasts are offered numerous opportunities, including:

  • parasailing - parachuting for a boat;
  • windsurfing,
  • diving, diving lessons,
  • sailing;
  • driving on motor or non-motorized boats;
  • aerobics;
  • volleyball.




All water sports are paid, and the courts and courts in the complex are provided for free use for all kinds of games. If you wish, you can sign up for a massage session or go for an aerobics training. Weekly barbecue evenings take place. Every evening, guests will find an entertainment program with the participation of professional animators, singers and even actors.

Nutrition

Dining at Atali Village Hotel is a buffet that offers all kinds of goodies to guests three times a day. Here guests can find a fairly simple, but high-quality prepared food: a variety of snacks, fruits, hot, pastries and soft drinks made in Greece. Guests are offered both Greek and international dishes. The dining room is spacious and equipped with modern equipment. The design is made without elaborate decor elements, but at the same time is stylish and restrained. In addition to the main meals included in the price, you can try food and drinks in one of the bars in the central building or by the pool. If desired, hotel guests can order breakfast in the room.





Vacation with children

Children under 14 years are accommodated in Atali Village 3 * (Crete, Rethymnon, Bali) for free. Guests with children can use the babysitting service, which is available at an additional cost. On request, a cot is provided for free in the room, and a high chair for the baby in the dining room. The hotel does not have a lift, but you can leave the stroller at the front desk. In Atali Village 3 * (Crete, Bali, Greece) you can seek additional help in case of accidents to a certified doctor. Located away from the hustle and bustle of the nightlife, Atali Village Hotel will create the most favorable conditions for a holiday even with the youngest children.





Additional Information

It is recommended to take into account that the hotel does not have specially created conditions for recreation for disabled people. Pets are not allowed because the hotel prohibits pets from staying in rooms. The family-type resort hotel has collected extremely positive reviews during its work. About the Atali Village Hotel 3 * (Crete, Rethymno) you can still talk for a long time, but it is best to see everything yourself! Thanks to a trip to Greece, namely to the resort village of Bali, you can get acquainted with Greek culture, try national dishes and drinks, enjoy the chic nature, which combines both mountain ranges and golden beaches, the azure sea and lush vegetation.
