"Children's Town" (SC "Schelkovo"): mode of operation, features.

With the help of games and entertainment, children not only relax, but also develop. At any age, children love rides and computer simulations. "Children's Town" (SC "Schelkovo") offers a modern recreation area for children and their parents.

Location and mode of operation

There are many similar centers in Moscow . Shopping center "Schelkovo" is located on Shchelkovo highway, 100. Directly children's attractions are located on the second floor of the building.

The game area is open daily:

  • Monday: from 12:30 to 20:50;
  • Tuesday-Friday: from 11:00 to 20:50;
  • days off: from 11:00 to 20:50.

Near the shopping center there is a large parking lot for visitors' personal vehicles. Halls for celebrations work on the same schedule.

Entertainment in the center

While parents go and buy various goods in the hypermarket, the child can visit the "Children's Town". SC "Schelkovo" gives adults the opportunity to buy any product here. At this time, the teacher is watching the children.





Kids under 3 years old cannot stay in the play area without parents. The playground provides young visitors with a huge range of entertainment:

  • labyrinth;
  • dry pool with balls ;
  • rocking rides;
  • soft large cubes;
  • mesh trampoline;
  • slides.

3D-simulators and air hockey are prepared for older children. Adults and children enjoy playing air guns. They shoot with foam balls that do no harm.

"Children's Town" (SC "Schelkovo") is decorated in a bright style. The playground is saturated with all the colors of the rainbow. On the floor is a quality coating. Even if the baby falls during active games, he will not injure himself.

Cafes and restaurants

Adults and children can eat and relax in the cafe, which is located in the same area. Here you can order your favorite dishes for children:

  • cheesecakes;
  • pancakes with sweet fillings;
  • potatoes in different variations;
  • fritters;
  • fruit salads;
  • ice cream;
  • smoothies, juices and soft drinks.

The cafe is located behind a glass wall through which the game area is clearly visible. Therefore, parents can keep an eye on the children during their entertainment. A floor above is the Pizza eshka restaurant. It also features an entertainment area.





The restaurant has a good assortment of both children's and adult dishes. For parents, an extended drinks list is offered. The design of the institution is made in a family format. Large cartoon characters are painted on part of the walls.

"Children's Town" (SC "Schelkovo"): reviews

On weekends and holidays, the entertainment area is visited by a large number of people. On weekdays, the number of visitors is slightly less. Therefore, mothers of babies up to 3 years old come here more often from Monday to Friday.

Parents are positive about the possibility of ordering animators. Thus, the holidays are fun and relaxed. Graduation parties and mass children's events are celebrated here.
