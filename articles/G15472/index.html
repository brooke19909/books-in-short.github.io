Internet banking is the provision of banking services through the Internet

Internet banking is a system that can allow you to manage various financial transactions in any place accessible to the client, at any convenient time and on any computer tool that can access the Internet.

General view

Internet banking is a system in which a client of a bank must register, after which he can transfer money from one account to another, as well as make payments for selected purchases and provided services, and carry out other banking operations while at home.

In order to carry out operations through the studied system, it is enough to have a personal computer or laptop with Internet access and any browser installed on the computer.

List of Services

Internet banking is a system that makes it possible to carry out the following types of services for a client:

  • account statements;
  • obtaining information on banking products;
  • formation of applications for opening accounts, deposits;
  • internal transfers;
  • transfers to other banks;
  • payment for services;
  • repayment of loans.

Positive aspects of the system

Consider the main positive aspects in the use of Internet banking:

  • reduced need for round-the-clock work of the bank for customer service;
  • reduced administrative work;
  • growth in the number of transactions via the Internet;
  • the opportunity to access your bank accounts always;
  • lack of queues;
  • saving money and time on the way to the bank;
  • security.

System flaws

The main disadvantages of Internet banking are those negative trends that are an obstacle to its development:

  • poor development among the retail sector;
  • the difficulty of predicting the payback period;
  • lack of qualified personnel to maintain functioning;
  • low public awareness of these services;
  • psychological difficulties;
  • lack of development of issues related to electronic digital signature.

The user of the system has the opportunity to see their accounts in a special individual account, view the history of accounts, pay for cellular communications, utilities, television, online purchases, configure some automatic payments.

Trends in Russia

In recent years, there has been an increase in the number of Internet payments in Russia. Payments in Internet banking are operations carried out within the system on the accounts of an individual without visiting the bank office. So, at the beginning of this year, the penetration rate of the system in Russia is defined at 16.7%. It is at the initial stage, since, for example, in Canada this indicator is 60%.

Analyzing the use of Internet banking for individuals in our country, it should be said that the volume of payments and transactions within the system is constantly increasing annually, which indicates its advantages.

Thus, the share of payments made by individuals through banking in the number of transactions increased by 18% by 2017 and amounted to 26% compared to 8% in 2011.

In our country, in the use of Internet banking services, the share of payment for mobile operators is leading and amounts to 48%. This is followed by payment for Internet services and utilities, payment of details, making online purchases.

The Future of Internet Banking

There are two directions for the development of Internet banking services in the near future:

  • improving existing systems, focus on attracting new customers;
  • focus on an advanced user and, as a result, a qualitative improvement in the work of Internet banking due to the elaboration and refinement of services, adding new ones.

Conclusion

In modern conditions of high market dynamism and competition for their customers, banks are trying to come up with more and more new ways to attract additional financial flows. To this end, various means of improving customer service are used. One of which is the introduction of Internet banking services. The experience of recent years in a number of banks has shown that the introduction of this system allows not only to improve the quality of service for existing bank customers, but also to increase the number of new ones, which, in turn, ensures the growth of bank profit and profitability. Thus, Internet banking is a service whose growth prospects in the coming years are very bright and significant.
