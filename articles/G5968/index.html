It is not in vain that the Samara Territory is famous throughout Russia not only for the fact that in difficult times the Soviet government found refuge there, but also for its magnificent natural monuments. On its territory there is a unique reserve, which carefully protects its wards. Connoisseurs and admirers of their native land strive for a never-ending stream to Samara land. Recently, fortunately, it has become popular to travel to one's homeland. There are many positive aspects to this. Firstly, there is no change in the climatic zone, which is more easily tolerated by the body. Secondly, in the native land there are so many interesting places that have long been famous. And we must value and love our country. Thirdly, "domestic" trips do not require the collection of various documents and papers.

Pearl Lada



One of the most magnificent places in Zhiguli in its unusual location and rich nature is Strelna Gora. From the site there is a beautiful view of the Volga, the nature around, the Trans-Volga distant. The view of wildlife awakens thoughts about the distant past of this land.

Then the site narrows, forming a narrow ridge, which is popularly called the Devil's Bridge, leading to the top of the mountain. Here a small grotto is formed of stone blocks. According to legend, he served as a refuge for "free people" whose chieftain was Stenka Razin, and the Volga River is visible for forty miles from here. This is what Mount Strelna is remarkable for. Zhigulevsky Reserve is proud of its sights and strictly protects them from uninvited guests.

On the area map



The richest flora and fauna of this region is represented by 158 species of birds and more than 800 species of plants, which attracts many tourists who want to visit a place like Strelna Gora. The Samara region is located in the southeast of the East European Plain and forms a large bend - Samara Luka along the course of the great Russian Volga River. The nature of this region has created a landmark that is known outside the region. In the people it is called Mount Strelna. This is the territory of the Zhigulevsk State Nature Reserve, which was organized in 1966 and bears the name of I. I. Sprygin, its first leader. Mount Strelna is the highest peak of the Zhiguli, which reaches 375 m above sea level. From its main massif, the ridge of the mountain first goes down, and then, quite unexpectedly, slightly up and forms a small area.

How to get to your destination



Visits to these places are strictly regulated. There are only two routes: on foot and by car. A visit to a reserved place costs 500 rubles per person. The car route is asphalted and runs along the mountain serpentine, which makes the trip special.

By the way, it is known for certain that the road was built by German prisoners, who were in the Samara region during the Great Patriotic War. So here you can ride on the "overseas" highway. Some note that even in this case, German pedantry and accuracy manifested itself: bonded workers performed their work exceptionally well. This is what Mount Strelnaya boasts of. How to get to these protected places, we describe below.

Car route



A tourist route called "Strelnaya Mountain" is unusual in that it is the only mountain. The pedestrian part has two viewing platforms with information banners. The age of visitors to the reserve is not limited, and the tour lasts 3.5 hours. To get to your destination, you must turn off the M5 highway onto the Zhigulevsk-Shiryaevo road and drive 35 km to the village of Zolnoye to the Strelnaya Gora sign. Then drive 400 m, also on asphalt, to the Zolnensky forestry checkpoint. It is necessary to register at the duty checkpoint and purchase an entrance ticket there. Then you need to go up to the car park for cars, it is about 12 km. The pedestrian part of the route is 700 m.

Vivid impressions are left by the tourists Strelnaya Mountain, Zhigulevsky Reserve, the beauty of the Samara Territory. Remember, the administration strictly monitors the order and protects the peace of its inhabitants. On the territory of the reserved place Strelna Gora photo, video is prohibited, because the flash can interfere with the "owners".

Walking route



You can get differently to the reserved place Mount Strelnaya. Tolyatti is located 12 km from the city of Zhigulevsk. From the last and you need to start the journey. From the city bus station regularly runs bus number 9, which carries passengers to Shiryaevo. You need to get out at the Zolny stop. And further - to the checkpoint of the forestry. Follow the instructions above.

This route is independent, which is very interesting, because so you can see more color and special life of the province.

On the Volga Mother

Residents and guests of the city of Samara have a unique opportunity to get to the reserved place not only conveniently, but also beautifully. From May to October, the passenger ship "Om", in the colloquial name "Omik", regularly runs from the pier of the capital of the province. For a small fee, you can enjoy the beauties of the Samara Territory, the great Volga River, the Zhiguli Mountains for several hours. You need to leave on the pier of the village of Zolnoye, then you can already reach the reserved place along the well-known route.

Rules of conduct for visitors to the reserve



There are rules of conduct in the reserve, which must be strictly followed by each visitor. They are as follows:

  1. For excursion routes through the reserve, a special permit or tourist permit from the administration is required.
  2. It is forbidden to go off the trail during the excursion, make bonfires, make camps, leave rubbish, or make loud noises.
  3. The time spent in the protected area is not more than 4 hours.
  4. Photographing sights is possible only on the trail of the excursion route.

It is necessary to observe sanitary rules, since the territory of the reserve belongs to the natural foci of tick-borne encephalitis, hemorrhagic fever with renal syndrome, and borreliosis. If you plan to visit the unique place Mount Strelnaya in a large group, you must first contact his administration for a pass.

Unforgettable journey

Having visited the Zhigulevsky Reserve and touched its nature, you will get an emotional discharge, get acquainted with unique plant communities, and see a panorama of the Zhigulevsky Mountains. You will certainly feel the greatness of the Volga River with its many green islands and enjoy the view of the wild nature. The dam of the Zhigulevskaya hydroelectric power station and settlements on the banks are clearly visible here. Everything you see will leave a vivid impression and will charge you with positive emotions for a long time.
