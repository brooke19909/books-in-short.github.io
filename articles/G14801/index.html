Alabino, Moscow Region. How to get to the landfill

The village of Alabino is located several tens of kilometers northeast of the Naro-Fominsk district of the Moscow region on the right bank of the Desna River. It is part of the urban village of Selyatino. The population does not exceed a thousand people. The name Alabino has been fixed since the beginning of the eighteenth century by the name of the temple.

World famous polygon

The territory of the Alabino military training ground in the Moscow region is part of the Patriot military culture and entertainment park and is comparable to the size of Red Square itself. Vast terrain along with deciduous and coniferous forests, as well as a huge number of trenches and barricades gives all this a special military flavor, which is alien to civilians.

Alabino is testing the latest technology and military exercises. However, the main event is considered to be the world tank biathlon.

Tank competitions. Events for guests

In recent years, in the village of Alabino, Moscow Region, tank biathlon has become a tradition. The strongest tank crews from different countries come here every year. Various events are arranged for the guests of the competitions, playgrounds and playgrounds are equipped, and attractions like shooting galleries are set up. In addition to all this, viewers are invited to take part in sports competitions and relay races. Exhibitions of military equipment are often held on the main avenue. On both sides of the sidewalk are fighting vehicles that participated in combat battles of all time.





Events for competitors

The fight for victory lasts for three days. The competition involves the countries of the CIS, Latin America and Asia. Mainly demonstrate Russian armored fighting vehicles, except for China. This country represents its own Type 96 tank with increased engine power.

From year to year, observers of tank biathlon are the Minister of Defense of the Russian Federation General Shoigu, his deputy, the commanders of the troops of the military districts, and representatives of the defense industry. According to officials of the ministry, these competitions are necessary to raise patriotism, develop combat training, determine the most worthy, and strengthen international relations among all the armed forces of the participating countries.





Tank platoons take part on the first day, they are the winners of biathlon qualifying rounds, each in their own military district. In total, three races with obstacles, with a penalty time when making a miss, are assumed.

The main objective of the first frontier is firing with an anti-tank guided missile, the second frontier is with an anti-aircraft machine gun, and the third frontier is in motion with three artillery shells. A penalty half kilometer is given to the crew if he missed.

The next day, a group stage is held between the first and second subgroup. The third day is final, the winners are determined in a tough battle at the same stages as on other days. The winners of the competition receive a brand new Niva car as a prize. Prizewinners are awarded the medal "For the Strengthening of Military Communities." It is worth adding that the T-72B tanks are often the main combat vehicles in biathlon. The maximum range, for example a missile, is about 4000 meters.





How to get to Alabino

Alabino is located in the Moscow region. How to get there? Everything is very simple! Since the Alabino station is located within 47 kilometers of the Kiev highway, you can safely take the train from the Kiev station. A couple of hours on the road, and you're at your destination. Due to the numerous traffic jams on the roads of Moscow and the Moscow Region, a train ride will significantly save time and money. In the evening, take the train number 6163, and in the morning you will be taken by train number 6472.

Those who are used to using public transport should take a minibus at number 569 from the Yugo-Zapadnaya metro station to the village of Alabino, Moscow Region. If the goal is to visit the tank biathlon, then in Alabino a transfer from the Ministry of Defense building to the training ground is usually prepared.

Where to relax

Are you planning to relax in the Naro-Fominsk district, namely in the village of Alabino, Moscow Region? Hotel Costa Rica K&T is an acceptable option. A huge territory with barbecue, new cozy guest houses with a spacious terrace. Bicycle rental is provided for all visitors so that you can take a ride and appreciate all the surroundings of Alabino. Address: Kirova street, house 37. 24-hour reservation.





After the rest, you can visit the Petrovskoye-Knyazhishchevo estate. This attraction is located near the station in the village of Alabino. It is known that this estate once belonged to the famous industrialists Demidov, and from the mid-nineteenth century to the princes Meshchersky. Four outbuildings, a dilapidated main house and a church have remained intact to our times. During the years of the Russian Empire, the estate often passed into the hands of different owners. During the Soviet period it was practically destroyed. Since the beginning of the nineties, a descendant of the Meshchersky dynasty has been living permanently in one of the four wings. When visiting this attraction, it is possible to plunge into those time periods that the estate has experienced.

Finally

In the conditions of city bustle, a trip to Alabino (Moscow region) for tank biathlon or an unhurried walk through the unique historical places of this area will be a good escape. A charge of positive emotions is provided. Have a nice rest!
