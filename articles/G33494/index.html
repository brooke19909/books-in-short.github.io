Novosibirsk-Barnaul: bus and train connections

The direction "Novosibirsk-Barnaul" is one of the most popular in the Siberian region of Russia. You can get from one city to another either by bus or by train. The choice of transport for the trip depends on which trip a person likes more: to constantly sit or lie down, while relaxing his back. The variety and number of bus and train flights allows each passenger to choose the best option for themselves.

Bus "Novosibirsk-Barnaul"

Novosibirsk land and Altai are neighboring regions, so the bus service between them is excellent. Every day, several buses of different capacities leave for both regions. The travel time of vehicles along the route ranges from 4 hours 30 minutes to 4 hours 50 minutes. Of course, under difficult weather conditions, like on any other route, buses can be delayed.





The route and the schedule of buses between settlements

The distance between cities in a straight line is 195 kilometers. Of course, this figure is relevant only for possible flights. 232 km are required on the highway between Novosibirsk and the capital of Altai Krai .

- Bezimenovo;

Trains on the Novosibirsk-Barnaul line

Several trains run from one city to another, including passing ones. For example, daily at 8 hours 05 minutes the fast train "Nizhnevartovsk-Barnaul" leaves for the Altai capital . The first stop after the largest city in Siberia is Berdsk. The train costs 2 minutes and resumes movement at 08-50. After 17 minutes, the flight arrives in the city of Iskitim (parking 2 minutes), and at 09-36 - in Linevo. In this village, the stop lasts 4 minutes. The longest stop at this stage of the journey is Cherepanovo. Here the composition of "Nizhnevartovsk-Barnaul" costs 27 minutes (from 10-07 to 10-34). To the Ust-Talmenskaya station from Cherepanovo it is necessary to go 1 hour 40 minutes. Then the train will follow another stop Altai and at 13 hours 31 minutes will arrive at the railway station of the city of Barnaul.





The next train from the Siberian city to the Altai capital leaves at 16 hours 3 minutes. The Novosibirsk-Barnaul train passes through the same stations as the long-distance train from Nizhnevartovsk, which we spoke about above. Only the first stop differs after leaving Novosibirsk (we are talking about the Sower station, located a 25-minute drive from the starting point). This flight arrives at the final stop at 21-16.

At 17-10, a train departs from Novosibirsk in the direction of Central Asia (Almaty, Tashkent). The duration of the trip to Barnaul by this train will be a little more than 6 hours. Passengers will be able to get to the following stations:

The only important difference between this flight and the above is the hourly parking in Cherepanovo (from 21-57 to 22-57). Passengers will arrive in Barnaul at about 2 a.m.

The last flight in this direction from the Novosibirsk-Glavny station leaves at 21 hours 10 minutes. It is about the Krasnoyarsk-Biysk route. What stops does this train go through? And all the same! According to the schedule, arrival to Barnaul usually takes place at 2 hours 48 minutes.

How to get from Barnaul to Novosibirsk?

Reaching back is also easy. A huge number of buses, including passing ones, will deliver passengers from one city to another even at night. By rail from the capital of Altai to the capital of Siberia, you can get tickets for flights departing from the station of Barnaul at 01-05, 01-25, 02-29, 02-44, 06-10 and 19-38.
