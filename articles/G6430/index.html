How many kilometers from Voronezh to Moscow? What could be easier than a trip to the capital!

Every Voronezh who loves traveling has at least once thought about going on an excursion to the capital of our country. It seems that the center of the state is very far, but modern transport infrastructure reduces this distance. Let's see this together.

How many kilometers from Voronezh to Moscow

The answer to this question depends on what kind of transport to get and what road to go. The average distance between Voronezh and Moscow is 515 kilometers. If you pass all the paid sites and go around them on rural roads, then this distance will increase by almost 100 kilometers. Therefore, the savings on the payment of control points will result in an extra waste of time and gasoline.

In fact, it is not so important how many kilometers from Voronezh to Moscow. It is more important to choose a method of delivery to the capital.

Firstly, it is worth considering the possibility of traveling in your car.

Secondly, you can take the train. Now the capital can be reached by rail in just 8 hours at a price of 600 to 1200 rubles. It is cheaper than by car and often faster, given how many kilometers are from Voronezh to Moscow.

Thirdly, you can fly by plane. There are pleasant promotions, and sometimes a plane ticket can be bought for 1-2 thousand rubles. By the way, an airplane flies less than an hour, which greatly saves time.

Way home

If the journey has come to an end, you will have to figure out how to get to Voronezh from Moscow.

The options are actually the same as when choosing a transport to the capital. Trains to Voronezh come with enviable regularity from the Kazan station, more rare trains run from the Kursk station.

Among other things, you can use the service of car traveling companions, which is even more convenient since you do not need to sit behind the wheel for 6-8 hours. In addition, it is quite cheap. The average cost of such a trip ranges from 800 to 1000 rubles.
