How to beat the bookmaker? Tips and strategies

Many people believe that it is impossible to beat the bookmaker. After all, if everyone can earn money on bets, then why haven't bookmakers gone broke and are still working? And why about 90% of the players are in the red?

In fact, everything is simple. You can earn money on bets in the bookmaker, but one desire is not enough for this. To begin with, the player will have to acquire a significant amount of knowledge, as well as show discipline, diligence and resourcefulness. And then there will be profit. The problem is that most players do not understand the simple truth: before you do something, you need to learn how to do it, and only then act. Components of success

Professional betting is based on three pillars: full accounting, competent analysis and operational management. Failure to comply with at least one of them will greatly reduce your chances of a stable win on bets. If you already have experience in this area, then you probably know the most common scheme of the game: today I won 200 rubles, but tomorrow I lost 300 ... Often, players who do not know how to beat the bookmaker simply throw sports bets without even understanding in a situation. The reason is simple: they violate one, and most often all three of the fundamental rules of betting. You need to understand that compliance with each of the factors is of great importance, since they are all interconnected.

The operational management factor maintains state and controls the distribution of your funds. The success of the bets depends on how competent the analysis is. Well, full accounting is a summing up criterion that sums up the work of the first two and the whole game as a whole. Are there any ways to beat the bookmaker? Of course yes. Just don't break the three rules above.

Start-up capital

Get start-up capital. It is a specific amount of money intended solely for betting at the bookmaker. Consider your capital as a long-term investment on which dividends will be accrued in the form of winnings. The game bank can only be used for its intended purpose. A squandering of start-up capital is clearly not the best way to beat a bookmaker. Betting strategies can be found below. And yet, be mentally prepared for risk.





Choosing a financial strategy

Every successful player who knows how to beat the bookmaker has a certain strategy. But there are general criteria that should be followed.

1. The strategy should not be excessive aggression. That is, do not increase the bid if you lose. It is best to make its size fixed or set a certain percentage of the bank.

2. An analysis element must be present in the strategy, when the percentage of the likely win is determined when calculating the size of the bet.

3. Do not use catch-up and other unsupported strategies. They are designed for beginners and lazy people who do not have the desire to engage in analysis.

Follow these principles when choosing your own financial strategy. If desired, you can use several at once. Only in this case do not forget to create a separate starting capital for each strategy.





Success formula

What does the success formula look like, thanks to which you will learn how to beat the bookmaker? It is quite simple: Analytics + Statistics + Intuition = Forecast.

Event analysis is the most important factor. It directly affects the accuracy of the forecast. When analyzing a game event, you need to consider all possible factors that can affect the outcome.

Statistics are used not only by players, but also by bookmakers when setting odds for events. Analysis of statistics will help to better assess the actual balance of power of players and teams. No need to go deep into it. The most valuable statistics are the last 3-4 games.

All professional bettors have great intuition. They can quickly skim through the line and immediately predict the outcome of the event. Listen to the inner voice. But just listen, not blindly obey.





Keep records

When investing your money, you always need to plan the amount of investment and calculate the amount of profit. The field of sports betting is no exception. Always keep track of bets. Firstly, this will allow an objective assessment of the current situation. Secondly, you can learn not your own mistakes. Do not be like a crowd of players who constantly make common mistakes and still do not know how to beat the bookmaker. Betting strategies were given above.

Improve yourself

Increase your knowledge daily. And, of course, improve your game strategy. There is never much knowledge! Develop intuition, analytics and other qualities that are characteristic of a successful bettor. The strength of any professional lies in his knowledge, which can be effectively applied in practice. Become a specialist in the field of bets, and then you will not need to think about how to beat the bookmaker. Good luck
