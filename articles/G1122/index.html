Nevsky House Hotel, St. Petersburg: overview, description and reviews of tourists

St. Petersburg is a city that attracts the attention of a huge number of tourists. This is where thousands of guests come from different parts of Russia every year to see the local attractions. As a rule, all of them are interested in short-term rental housing, the ideal option of which is a hotel room.

"Nevsky House" 3 * - a mini-hotel in St. Petersburg, which is one of the cheapest in the whole city, it has been operating since 2005. What do tourists say about him? What living conditions are provided here and what services can they offer?

Location

The Nevsky House Hotel (Russia, St. Petersburg) is located in the very central part of the city, at a place where there is excellent transport interchange - it is from here that you can easily get to anywhere in the city. The building in which the hotel is equipped stands in the middle of Nevsky Prospekt, in a place of large crowds and excellent traffic.

Boutique Hotel Nevsky House in St. Petersburg is perfectly located in an ordinary residential building No. 134, on its two upper floors. Guests who check into it must go upstairs on foot, since there is no elevator in the building construction. The general interior of the room is decorated in a classic style, in the best traditions of St. Petersburg. Its corridors elegantly combine peach shades with elements of dark wood.



Number of rooms

There are few rooms in the hotel (20 pieces), and all of them are divided into several categories: economy, standard, family, comfort and junior suite. Each of them has been renovated at a European level and modern furniture has been installed.

Tourists who prefer to stay at this hotel have the opportunity to use individual showers, which are in each apartment, as well as bathrooms. Each bathroom has its own hair dryer designed for drying hair. Each apartment has a small electronic safe, which guests can use for a fixed daily fee.

Economy

There are 11 apartments in the economy category at the Nevsky Dom Hotel (St. Petersburg) . Of these, 3 are designed for one guest, seven - for two, and another one can accommodate four people.

The furniture has modern beds with orthopedic mattresses, designed for the above number of persons. In addition, guests are provided with cabinets for storing small items, wardrobe cabinets and one plasma TV per room.

Among other things, there is a small coffee table, as well as a fan. The bathroom has a standard set of shower accessories, as well as slippers and a bathrobe. During the hot season, guests can request a fan in their room, as there is no air conditioning system.



Standard

In the "Nevsky House" (hotel, St. Petersburg) there are two standard rooms, which are designed for two people. Here, two small beds are installed as sleeping places, you can put the necessary accessories into one small curbstone, and guests can leave their clothes on the hanger. In their reviews of the Nevsky Dom Hotel (St. Petersburg), tourists often point out that Standard apartments are ideal for families with small children, as this is where a small table for feeding babies and a chair are installed .

This room has free Wi-Fi access.



Family

Guests of family apartments have the opportunity to stay here with a company of three people. There is only one room of this type in the Nevsky House Hotel (St. Petersburg) and it is divided into two zones - a bedroom and a living room.

In the sleeping area there is one large double bed, covered with expensive linen. Here, a huge mirror hangs on the gray walls, and the furniture has a wardrobe. In the living area there is a large folding sofa, which, if necessary, can serve as an extra bed, as well as two armchairs. There is a small glass coffee table and two soft leather chairs.



Comfort

In the reviews of the Nevsky Dom Hotel (St. Petersburg), tourists often indicate that one of the best apartments is the comfort category, of which there are only three. Each of them is designed to accommodate two people, but if necessary, an extra bed can be installed here (for a fee - 600 rubles per day).

For guests there is a large double bed with a hard mattress, a small coffee table, two chairs and a fireplace in the corner of the sleeping area. Vacationers can watch their favorite TV shows on a plasma TV connected to satellite channels. It also has a large wall mirror and a small dressing table.



Junior Suite

The Nevsky Dom Hotel (Russia, St. Petersburg) has three junior suites, which guests also love. They can live together, as evidenced by the presence of a double berth. Of furniture, there are also two cabinets (one is larger, the other is smaller), as well as a desk for work and a chair. The decor in the room has a large fireplace made of dark wood, above which a plasma TV hangs. In addition, a mirror hangs on the wall.



Additional terms

The hotel has several places to visit. First of all, it is a small lobby bar where vacationers have the opportunity to drink a cup of coffee or tea or just work on a laptop. The price of the hotel includes meals in this bar from 10 a.m. to noon.

Fans of "hotter relaxation" will certainly take the opportunity to go to a local bathhouse and take a steam bath with birch or oak brooms.

For a fee, guests can use laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as ironing. All motorists who arrive at the hotel in their car have the right to leave it in a free secure parking lot located near the building of the house.

Prices

In their reviews of the Nevsky House 3 * in St. Petersburg, tourists often mention that there are very reasonable prices for accommodation. So, the cheapest economy class room for one person will cost 2,000 rubles per day, while the most expensive apartments will cost 3,500 rubles from October to April, and 5,000 rubles in the remaining months.

Children under 8 years old in hotel rooms can stay for free provided that they are documented of their age and there is no requirement to provide a separate bed.

Nearest city attractions

In their reviews of the Nevsky Dom Hotel, tourists often point out its favorable location as a positive point. Indeed, in the vicinity of the hotel there are several main city objects that any guest of the cultural capital of Russia should look at.

First of all, this, of course, is the embankment of the Neva River, which is famous for its beautiful views and picturesqueness. From here you can watch the famous drawbridges.

Within walking distance (about 35 minutes walk) from the hotel are such cultural and historical sites as St. Isaac's Cathedral, the Russian Museum and the Hermitage. They can also be reached by public transport.

Guest reviews

All guests of the Nevsky House Hotel in St. Petersburg often share their impressions received during the stay. Comments about the rest can always be found on pages on social networks, on thematic sites, as well as on its official page (in the "Reviews" section).

Often, guests respond positively to the general atmosphere that prevails in hotel rooms. They talk about the fact that in any apartment, regardless of the degree of comfort, cleanliness and order always reign. The room service employee regularly replaces bed linen and towels, as well as does room cleaning.

The hotel has polite staff. Local administrators can always help in solving simple issues, as well as suggest the location of important urban infrastructure. If necessary, a taxi call can be made from the hotel.



The cost of living also pleases tourists, as it is very low, given the central location of the hotel. On the plus side, the presence of breakfast in the local cafe is noted. According to them, in the morning the guests are fed very tasty and nutritious, so that the energy received is enough for the whole day.

Participation in projects

In 2017, the hotel was chosen as an object for alteration in the television project "On the Knives". Hotel "Nevsky House" (St. Petersburg) in the first issue of the program was visited by Dmitry Makarov, who launched a project to improve the Russian hotel business. The issue of the show showed its entire number of rooms, its facade, and also highlighted some aspects of servicing guests.

Address

The hotel is located in the very center of St. Petersburg, at 134 Nevsky Prospekt (on the two upper floors of the house). Not far from here is a major public transport stop, as well as the Ploshchad Vosstaniya metro station.

At a relatively short distance (about an hour by public transport) is the Pulkovo International Airport - the largest transportation point. The Moscow Railway Station is also nearby.
