Villa Salus 4 * (Budva, Montenegro): hotel description and reviews of tourists

Budva is considered one of the most beautiful resort in Montenegro. Its amazing nature, a wide selection of entertainments and numerous hotels and guest houses make rest in a picturesque place on the Adriatic coast truly unforgettable.

Of course, in such an ancient city as Budva, there are places that you definitely need to see. This is the Old Town of the fifteenth century, the castle of Kastel and its towers, numerous churches, the Citadel, etc.



Holidays in Budva

Fans of outdoor activities really like tours to Montenegro and especially to Budva. There are many tennis courts, great jogging tracks and comfortable hiking trails, diving clubs offering diving in the most interesting points of the Adriatic, beaches, both sandy and pebble.

The nightlife of the resort is no less exciting and colorful. There are many night clubs and discos, bars and even casinos.

Budva hotels have one feature: almost all of them are located very well relative to entertainment centers and beaches. The most prestigious and expensive of them are built on the first line. Some - the most chic - have their own swimming area on the shore.

Budva Hotels



This resort has a huge selection of places to stay: it can be rooms that can be rented for ten euros per day, and luxury apartments in hotels. Hotel prices are seasonally dependent. In July and August - the most "expensive" months - the cost of rooms soars two or three times. And it can start from ten thousand rubles per person per night.

In Budva there are about a hundred guest houses, hotels and apartments. Nevertheless, despite some high cost in comparison with Turkey or Egypt, today for many Montenegro has become a favorite vacation spot.

"Villa Salus"

That is the name of the hotel, built in the very center of Budva, in its quiet sleeping area. Nearby - two hundred and four hundred meters from it - there are two of the best municipal beaches of the resort. The hotel building is a four-story building immersed in the greenery of a well-kept garden. In 2007, it was overhauled.



The hotel is located directly next to the historic center of the resort. Within walking distance there is a supermarket, a market, a promenade on the promenade with many restaurants and cafes, shops and discos. The remoteness of the hotel from the nearest airport is only twenty-five kilometers.

Infrastructure

Having a category of four stars, the hotel Villa Salus (Budva) offers its guests the opportunity to connect to the Internet throughout its territory, including in rooms, laundry and ironing services. Its reception is open around the clock, therefore, arriving tourists, regardless of the exact time established by the rules of the hotel, quickly populate, of course, if there are free rooms.



The hotel has secure parking. When booking rooms, you need to discuss in advance its reservation, which is free of charge. There are no conditions for disabled guests at this hotel. Check-in with small pets is not permitted.

rules

Villa Salus (Cat A category) has the following rules: check in at twelve and check out at two in the afternoon. Upon delivery, you need to finally pay for all additional services. This can be done both in cash and by bank transfer. Cards of many banking systems are accepted, but in order to avoid turmoil, it is better to discuss this issue with the administration in advance.

Housing stock

Villa Salus is considered a small hotel. It has a total of twenty seven rooms in the following categories:

  • Four single, of which two - with a balcony overlooking the garden or mountains: their area is fourteen square meters. Maximum occupancy is one adult and one child under six years of age.
  • Seven standard double rooms with a balcony, which can accommodate 2 + 1 people.
  • Four triple rooms with a balcony (25 sq. Meters) are designed for three adults and one child. There are only three Studio 02 rooms. Their area is twenty eight and a half square meters. They can comfortably accommodate three tourists.
  • Studio 03 is a nine triple apartment with a kitchen area and a balcony. Six of them have the opportunity for an additional resident - a folding chair.


All rooms have combined bathrooms with shower, hairdryer and all necessary accessories. The rooms, renovated in modern style, have a manually controlled air conditioning, a safe in which you can fit a laptop, refrigerator, TV. The studio category apartments have a kitchenette with everything you need, including a set of dishes. Balconies - furnished. Sitting on them, you can drink tea and enjoy the amazing surrounding panoramas.

Beach and sea

The nearest bathing area is a five-minute walk away. However, many prefer to walk across the promenade to the famous Mogren beach. Sun beds, umbrellas, deck chairs, including soft ones, can be obtained here for a fee.

Judging by the reviews, a walk to the seashore is not at all tiring, so many people enjoy walking on foot, going to shops and supermarkets along the way.

At night, almost all the beaches of the resort are open. Lovers can swim freely under the supervision of lifeguards. On each of the beaches there are bars where live music is played. In the water, fish are often found.

Excursions

Villa Salus 4 (Budva) is located in such a way that tourists can study the city on their own. For further excursion tours you need to register at the registration desk. Many people prefer to go to canyons or to Skadar Lake. In the evening, these excursions end with dinner at the fisherman‚Äôs hut.

Tourists, judging by their reviews, are simply amazed at the purity of the turquoise river waters, the majesty of the mountains. Guided tours organized by Villa Salus are conducted by Russian-speaking local guides.

Many people like a tour to Albania, where on the way, in the border town of Shkotor, you can buy excellent cognac and silver products at very low prices.



Reviews for Villa Salus 4

Many of those who stayed at this hotel left their reviews about staying in it, which help a lot when choosing not only a resort, but also a specific hotel.

Villa Salus (Montenegro) is rated quite well. The Russians consider the location of the hotel to be very successful - close to the center, shopping facilities and transport stops. At the same time, despite the fact that Budva is a very noisy resort, silence and tranquility always reign in the hotel. It seems that when entering the gate on the territory of Villa Salus, a person enters a different world.

Owners and staff unnecessarily never tire of their constant presence. Nevertheless, they can always be contacted and asked for something. The hotel rooms are very comfortable, there have never been problems, especially with water, because Villa Salus has its own well, so the pressure is so strong that you can take a hydromassage with a shower.



A separate emphasis in many reviews is made on the snow-whiteness of bed linen, which is not often characteristic of hotels.

There are several conflicting opinions regarding food: some believe that the type of food offered at Villa Salus is not very convenient, others, especially those who have small children, are very pleased with the kitchen area.

In any case, the sea, the sun and the wonderful beach erase all the shortcomings of the hotel, which, if there is one, are not striking.
