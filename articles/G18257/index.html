How to install?

All radio tape recorders are mounted on the same principle. The installation of the 2DIN radio is the same as the installation of a 1-din device. First of all, remove the old radio. To do this, it is better to use a Phillips screwdriver. With it, you can remove the console, which is attached with four bolts. 2DIN car radios are easily removable from the jack. To do this, you can slightly pry it with a flat screwdriver. Two latches are located on the sides, which are easily opened. After that, the car radio should be easily dismantled. After removing the device, access to the wires will appear. Each plug is quite difficult to push out. Therefore, it is better to tilt the radio toward you and carefully disconnect the plug. The new 2DIN car radio on Android should be installed in the reverse order.
