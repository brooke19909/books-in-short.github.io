Boutique hotel "Yuryevskoe Compound", Veliky Novgorod: description and reviews

Fans of comfort and quality service should pay attention to such an accommodation option as the Yuryev Compound (Veliky Novgorod). Here you can enjoy the silence and traditional Russian flavor.





Short description

Dreaming of a break from the bustle of the city, go to the boutique hotel "Yuryevskoe Compound". Veliky Novgorod can please you not only with its beautiful architecture, but also with its untouched nature and clean air. This institution, stylized as a Russian village, will give you pacification and introduce you to the traditions and life of your ancestors.

The boutique hotel, in addition to a favorable location, also boasts a developed infrastructure and a well-thought-out concept. Thanks to this, the Yuryev Compound has become an ideal venue for corporate and special events. Also here you can relax with the whole family.





Hotel Yuryevskoye Compound (Veliky Novgorod): addresses

This institution is located in a cozy forest zone on the outskirts of the city. Just 11 minutes from the clean river beach is the boutique hotel Yuryevskoye Podvor. Veliky Novgorod, Yuryevskoye Shosse 6A - the address where you will find this institution. It is convenient to get here both on your own car and on public transport. Within walking distance is a museum of wooden architecture, as well as an existing monastery.

Pets are allowed

The Yurievskoye Podvorye hotel treats its guests with care and attention. You can visit Veliky Novgorod with your pets. Pets are allowed in accordance with the following rules:

  • upon check-in, the guest together with the hotel representative must inspect the room for any malfunctions (in order to avoid unpleasant situations during eviction);
  • outside the room, the animal must be on a leash;
  • it is forbidden to visit public catering establishments with pets;
  • for large dogs, a muzzle is required;
  • upon check-in, the guest must present a veterinary passport of the animal and a certificate of vaccination;
  • a tray can be provided in the room, as well as bowls for food and water;
  • if the animal is in the room, cleaning is carried out only in the presence of the guest;
  • cleaning duties for the pet assigned to the guest;
  • if the animal has caused damage to the hotel, the owner agrees to indemnify it.

Positive reviews

A lot of information is contained in the network about the hotel "Yuryevskoye Podvorye" (Veliky Novgorod) - addresses and description, as well as many reviews. As for the positive comments about this institution, it is worth paying attention to the following points:

  • there is a museum across the road from the hotel, which guests can visit for free;
  • good breakfasts with a large selection of delicious dishes;
  • friendly, attentive and qualified reception staff; beautiful hotel design that imitates the Russian flavor of the northern regions;
  • quiet and peaceful atmosphere both in the hotel and in its surroundings;
  • the dishes in the restaurant are home-cooked delicious, and the portions are large enough;
  • the rooms are decorated with a lot of wood, which creates a pleasant atmosphere and aroma;
  • comfortable beds with orthopedic mattresses;
  • warm blankets in the rooms;
  • upon request, an electric kettle or boiling water from a sconce can be brought to the room;
  • the bathroom has a heated towel rail;
  • a beautiful green area where it is pleasant to walk, and you can also make a beautiful photo shoot;
  • waiters work fast enough (an order has to be expected no longer than a quarter of an hour);
  • in the restaurant you can listen to live instrumental and vocal performance;
  • even if the hotel has a noisy wedding, you will not hear the sounds of music and fun in your room;
  • on weekends you can enjoy interesting concerts and entertaining shows;
  • near the hotel there is a public transport stop, from where buses to the city center regularly run;
  • there are many flowers on the territory;
  • good equipment rooms;
  • there are mosquito nets on the windows;
  • The territory of the hotel, including all premises, is subjected to insect treatment.




Negative reviews

Despite the mass of positive comments, the Yuryevskoye Podvorye hotel (Veliky Novgorod) is not without its drawbacks. Reviews give the right to talk about the following disadvantages of this institution:

  • when weddings and other events are held at the hotel, guests cannot get into the restaurant and lobby bar, and 25% of the order amount is additionally charged for delivering food to the room;
  • cramped rooms, cluttered with furniture;
  • too small sauna;
  • due to the small windows in the rooms, the room cannot be properly ventilated;
  • inflated prices in the restaurant;
  • the floors in the bathroom are not heated;
  • the bathroom does not have a stand for toothbrushes, and putting them on a shelf is extremely unhygienic in the absence of a special case;
  • Guests are invited to sleep on uncomfortable feather pillows, which can also cause allergies;
  • expensive drinks in the minibar;
  • the bar opens much later than the restaurant, and therefore you cannot order drinks from the coffee machine during breakfast;
  • beautiful facades of buildings corrupted by ventilation pipes;
  • in the wardrobe there are only two clothes hangers, which is not enough for 2 adult guests;
  • in the shower, as well as on the seams of the tiles a lot of mold has formed;
  • there is no shelf for cosmetics in the shower;
  • photos of the hotel on the official website are strongly embellished and do not correspond to reality;
  • no cooler with drinking water;
  • numerous decorative elements are covered with a layer of dust;
  • lack of soundproofing in the rooms;
  • the drain in the bathroom does not work well;
  • gray towels, washed;
  • ventilation system is too noisy;
  • during breakfast, you have to wait a long time until the dishes that are over at the distribution are replenished;
  • bedding is old;
  • breakfasts are repeated day by day;
  • carpeted spots.

Sometimes you want to escape from the bustle of the city. Hotel "Yuryevskoe Compound" (Veliky Novgorod) will help you not only relax in silence and surrounded by nature, but also introduce you to traditional Russian life.
