Mimosa Beach 3 * (Cyprus / Protaras) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Protaras today is one of the most popular resorts in Cyprus. Although just a few decades ago there was absolutely no infrastructure. Now in this place there are many hotels of various categories, as well as entertainment for every taste. Most tourists who come to Protaras, rely on a magnificent beach vacation surrounded by beautiful nature. If you decide to spend your vacation here and are looking for an economical option for accommodation, then consider the Mimosa Beach 3 * hotel as an opportunity. We suggest that you take a closer look at this hotel, as well as find out what impressions tourists from Russia have left here.





Where is

Hotel "Mimosa Beach" is located on the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea (literally twenty meters from the beach), not far from the resort town of Protaras. The distance from the hotel to the center of this town is about three kilometers. Therefore, you can walk to all objects of its tourist infrastructure in half an hour on foot or in a few minutes by bus or taxi. Also not far from Mimosa Beach 3 * (Protaras) is the famous tourist city of Ayia Napa, which fans of discos and night clubs love to visit. In the vicinity of it is located and the airport. So after arrival, your journey to the hotel will take only about a quarter of an hour.

Number of rooms

The housing stock of Mimosa Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus) is represented by 60 comfortable rooms. Some of them are designed for family accommodation and are equipped with a kitchenette. In addition, almost all windows offer sea views. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom, individual air conditioning, TV, telephone and a balcony or terrace. Rooms are cleaned on a daily basis, and bed linen and towels are changed three times a week.





Nutrition

When booking accommodation in Mimosa Beach 3 * you can choose the right meal plan for you : all inclusive, half board or breakfast only. A buffet spread of European dishes is served in the hotel restaurant. There is also a poolside bar. Here you will be offered a wide selection of different drinks and snacks.

Beach vacation

Hotel Mimosa Beach 3 * (Cyprus) does not have its own beach, but is only a couple of tens of meters from the coastline. Local beaches are municipal, so you need to pay for the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas.





Entertainment and infrastructure

For the convenience of guests Mimosa Beach 3 * hotel reception is open around the clock. Therefore, you have the opportunity to resolve all problems and questions at any time of the day or night. Also at the reception you can exchange currency, leave things for storage in a safe (for a fee), order a taxi, etc.

In terms of entertainment, the hotel has an open-air pool with a sun terrace. You can also play tennis or volleyball. In addition, several times a week the hotel hosts Cypriot parties. Also, tourists have the opportunity to travel to the sights of the island, renting a car or ordering an excursion.





Cost of living

It should be noted that the price for accommodation in Mimosa Beach is quite reasonable and corresponds to the three-star category of the hotel. So, a day of living in the midst of the tourist season will cost you from 55 euros, and in the lower season - from 46 euros. This price includes all-inclusive meals.
