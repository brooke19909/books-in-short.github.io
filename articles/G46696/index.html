Do-it-yourself snowball made of cotton: a method without glue

This technology is simple, but you can make different types of jewelry using it. In this case, you will need to sew a ball of any material, and decorate the surface with just cotton or with additional decor. Materials needed are:

  • old tights;
  • thread and needle;
  • cotton wool;
  • bandage;
  • white or silver tinsel or other decor.

DIY snowball made of cotton in this way is done as follows:

  1. Cut off a part of a suitable size from tights and twist it in the form of a ball, sew, where necessary, with a needle. You can use tights as a shell, and fill the spherical "bag" with scraps of scraps or sintepon. The color of the base is unimportant, as the surface will close.
  2. Tear off a little cotton and distribute on the resulting ball so that the "insides" do not shine through, especially if they are of a different shade. Wrap a layer of cotton wool with a bandage and sew with thread so that it does not turn around.
  3. To give the decorative properties of the surface, wrap the resulting workpiece with white or silver tinsel or grass-type yarn.

There is nothing complicated. The only negative is the use of a needle with a thread, that is, a small child himself will not be able to make a product using this technology.

Cotton wool and glue

If you want to learn how to make snowballs from cotton with your own hands with a baby, then use one of the following methods. In the first case, you will need such materials:

  • cotton wool;
  • PVA;
  • hair spray (preferably with sparkles).

Manufacturing technology consists of three steps:

  1. Take a piece of cotton and drip PVA on it.
  2. Turn the ball so that the glue is inside.
  3. Coat the surface with hairspray.

So quickly you can make snowballs from cotton wool.





We make snowballs with our own hands in another way. Here you will need to weld a paste. You need the following:

  • A glass of cold water.
  • 2 teaspoons of starch.
  • Heat-resistant container (saucepan).
  • Tool for stirring (spoon, stick).
  • Glue brush.
  • Vata.
  • Sequins or other decor.

The technology of work is as follows:

  1. Pour water into the prepared container and gradually pour in the starch while mixing.
  2. Put the composition on a slow fire until there are signs of boiling (bubbles), also stirring constantly thoroughly. If lumps nevertheless form, stir everything with a whisk or use a fork.
  3. Roll the balls of cotton while the glue cools.
  4. Apply evenly with a brush to the surface of the ball the prepared composition and sprinkle with sparkles.
  5. Put the items to dry to the battery or use a hairdryer. It is advisable to turn the balls over during the drying process.

The second technology with a paste is suitable even for young children, since safe materials are used, and the composition is applied cooled down. The paste, of course, will be made by adults.
