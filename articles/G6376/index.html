Pattern of an attic toy. DIY attic toys

If you like to make gifts with your own hands, then the article will be interesting to you. Have you tried sewing textile dolls? They are very popular among hand-made goods. A finished pattern of an attic toy that you can print or redraw from the screen will help you make a cute souvenir that will improve your mood.



Why such a name

All characters made in this style are very similar to things that have been lying in the attic for a long time, because the fabric has grown old and browned, as well as saturated with the aroma of herbs that are usually dried under the roof. Attic toys are also called coffee, as artificial aging is performed using a coffee solution. Vanilla and cinnamon are added to it. The attic toy pattern is always extremely simple. The whole charm of the souvenir is in its delicate brown shade and unforgettable aroma. To make such a thing is very simple.



Manufacturing sequence

These gifts are made using the following technology:

  1. You will need a pattern of an attic toy that you can take ready-made or draw yourself. Details are transferred to a fabric (usually calico, linen or other natural) and cut out.
  2. Finished items are stained with a solution of coffee with vanilla and cinnamon.
  3. After drying, the parts are sewn together on the inside, and then they turn out through a small hole left.
  4. The product is filled with padding polyester or other material, the hole is sutured.
  5. Toys decorate, draw or embroider facial features on them, decorate with bows, lace, humorous phrases.


Character forms are usually very simple. The product is sewn from one, a maximum of two or three parts, for example, tail or ears are sewn to the body. So any beginner needlewoman can make this gift.

DIY loft toys: patterns

If you decide to do this type of needlework, you have two options:

  1. Choose your favorite patterns and sew your souvenir on them.
  2. Take a piece of paper and come up with your own characters.

Choose what suits you best. For those who can draw and have imagination, the second way is optimal. If you have no idea what you can come up with new and interesting, then use any ready-made template.

It is enough to print it on a scale that suits you or simply redraw it from the monitor screen, cut out a paper blank and use it to cut out the form from the fabric.

"Positive"

Many needlewomen display photographs depicting cool, very emotional attic toys. Patterns for them are taken absolutely any, on the principle of "The simpler, the better."

Positive and funny they are obtained through facial expressions and original humorous inscriptions directly on the character. Often animals are given an unusual (unnatural) shape. Cats look like balls or rectangles or, on the contrary, have too elongated proportions with elongated necks. However, the configuration is not the point. You can see the whole "collection", sewn one pattern. At the same time, each character reflects his emotions (he is surprised, laughs, squints) and has a text composed with a sense of humor.
