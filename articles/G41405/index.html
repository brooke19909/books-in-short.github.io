Obolonskaya embankment in Kiev is a fabulously beautiful corner created by human hands

Kiev, not inferior in the number of unique sights to many European capitals, has long been the main tourist center of Ukraine. Interesting places of the cradle of Slavic cities make acquaintance with him an exciting activity. Each street has its own secrets, and a simple walk through the country's business center will bring a lot of pleasant emotions.

The history of the area

Recently appeared Obolonskaya embankment is one of the most interesting places in Kiev, which must be visited by tourists. This is not just a place for walking, but a whole infrastructure with a two-level pedestrian zone.

This area was not once considered habitable. The desert territory was often flooded during the flood of the river, and it was no coincidence that the wetland was called Obolon. About 40 years ago, it was decided to build an expensive neighborhood here, which Kiev could be proud of. The Obolonskaya embankment, stretching for four kilometers, is built up with beautiful buildings that have a unique style.



Private cottages that run along the street are an elite complex. It may seem that the buildings are similar in appearance, but each building is unique. The architectural ensemble resembles the erected scenery for a beautiful fairy tale on the embankment, which is beautiful at any time of the year.

Now it is a very prestigious area in which few can afford to buy an apartment because of its high cost. The Obolon district has turned into a status residential complex, at the foot of which is its main treasure - Obolonskaya embankment, which is a pedestrian.

Bronze statues

Here you can see about 50 sculptural works that have become a real highlight of a well-groomed corner. Even near the Minskaya metro station, next to which Obolonskaya embankment is located, everyone is greeted by a sleepy little sun covered in a warm blanket.

City guests and locals admire the bronze statues of the heroes of well-known fairy tales. Labyrinth in the energy zone

Obolonskaya embankment, whose photo will surprise with various sights, is considered not just a place for a promenade. Here is an energy zone that allows you to know your inner desires. No wonder it laid a unique labyrinth, called the "Journey into yourself." Everyone who passes it will receive answers to exciting questions.



Unlike traditional Japanese "Rock Garden"

The "Rock Garden", which recently appeared at the very beginning of the embankment, does not at all look like traditional Japanese. It is not intended for meditation and is not performed according to all the rules of Zen Buddhism. But here you can take a walk, sit with friends on benches and take a break from all the problems. The scattered huge boulders, on which there are signs where they were brought from, look strange, because they are surrounded on all sides by high-rise buildings.



This unusual idea appealed to the people of Kiev, who believed that the beautiful Obolonskaya embankment, photos of which convey its majesty, deserves the best.

Paths covered with paving slabs pass through the garden, and tiger and eagle sculptures lurk on them, as well as the statue of the little mermaid. An unusual fountain with three girls hiding from the water under an umbrella is very popular in the summer, when whole groups of young people gather near it. And in the evening the design is beautifully highlighted.

Mermaid helping lovers

The charming figure of a cute little mermaid holding a sea shell will help to find a life partner. To do this, touch the sculpture and sincerely ask her to give a soul mate. Happy newlyweds will definitely come here to thank their fairy, who gave them love.

A place that charges positive

According to local residents, Obolonskaya embankment is a positively charged place, and it is no accident that the sun here almost always pleases with its rays. On weekends, even on Khreshchatyk is not as crowded as here. This is the best place for romantic walks and sports. Picnics are arranged on the beach, many bathe in the Dnieper, older people fish. In the evenings there are various entertainment shows and concerts gathering grateful spectators.

A cozy corner, which is always looked after by human hands, delights with an amazing view of the Obolon Bay. The reigning calm on the mirror surface of the Dnieper can be admired from early morning until late evening. Four years ago, a water stadium appeared, where various competitions are organized and classes are held with children.

What else to see?

  • Sandstone made of twelve sculptures that symbolize the signs of the zodiac.
  • Avenue of muses, which consists of eight statues made of cement and marble chips.
  • Sculpture "Traveler Ball", which is a globe sitting on a suitcase with glasses.




Obolonskaya embankment: how to get there?

To get to the corner, unique in any weather, you can take minibuses from Obolon, Heroes of the Dnieper and Minskaya metro stations. True, local residents recommend this path to walk in 10-15 minutes.

According to the reviews of guests of Kiev, here is a special energy. The embankment harmoniously connecting civilization and nature awaits tourists who will again and again be fascinated by amazing views.
