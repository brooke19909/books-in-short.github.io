Hotel Trakia Plaza 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria is a small country in the Balkans with surprisingly hospitable and friendly locals. It is famous for its ski and beach resorts.

One of the largest and most popular sea resorts in Bulgaria is Sunny Beach. Its many hotels, villas, apartments, places for recreation and entertainment, restaurants and bars, discos and nightclubs, water attractions and amusement parks every year gain more and more new and loyal fans. Among the hotels located in the central part of the resort, the nine-story building, original in terms of architecture, in the form of a white and blue ship stands out. This is a modern hotel Trakia Plaza 4 * (Sunny Beach).





Hotel info

Trakia Plaza 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) opened its doors to guests in 2003. Since then, it has not ceased to function year-round.

The hotel has a fairly large green area, on which a variety of plants bloom almost all year round. There are two outdoor pools: a children's 0.5 m deep with water rides and a large adult 0.8-2.5 m deep with pretty bridges. The relaxation area around the pools is equipped with free sun loungers and parasols.

Trakia Plaza 4 * (Sunny Beach) operates on an all-inclusive basis in bars and a main restaurant with an open terrace called Chuchura. In addition, hotel guests can take advantage of additional services:

  • free wireless internet in public areas;
  • Internet club, but for an additional fee;
  • safe deposit box rental at the reception;
  • car rental
  • paid parking;
  • barbershop;
  • beauty saloon;
  • currency exchange;
  • score;
  • medical office.




For younger guests, in addition to a swimming pool with slides, there is a children's playground, a mini club, and an evening entertainment program.

Location

Hotel Trakia Plaza 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) is 30 kilometers from the international airport of Burgas. His exact address is: Republic of Bulgaria, 8240, Sunny Beach. The distance from the hotel to the geographical center of the resort is only half a kilometer, to the historical center of Nessebar - 5 kilometers, to the sea and the municipal beach - 200 meters. Nearby are the hotel-casino "Chrysanthemum" 4 * and the hotel "Trakia" 3 *. All listed institutions are located on the second coastline.

Number of rooms

Hotel Trakia Plaza 4 * (Sunny Beach) offers its guests convenient and comfortable accommodation in 167 standard double rooms with a view of the park or the sea and 2 one-bedroom apartments. All rooms are equipped with amenities for short breaks and long vacations:

  • two beds;
  • extra bed for children under 12 years old;
  • satellite television;
  • air conditioning system;
  • mini bar;
  • telephone;
  • bathroom with shower;
  • restroom;
  • hair dryer.




Cleaning of premises is carried out daily. Towels are changed three times a week, bed linen twice a week.

Pets are not allowed in the hotel rooms.

restaurants and bars

All-inclusive meals (this is a buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner with a wide selection of dishes and drinks) are provided in the neighboring Bulgarian restaurant Chuchura. This institution was opened in 1963 and is considered the oldest restaurant in the resort. During the period of its work, it was twice renovated - in 1990 and 2008, and today guests can dine in the indoor room decorated in the best Bulgarian traditions, or in the open air in the well-preserved green garden.

Chuchura Restaurant also offers a la carte service. A wide variety of national hot dishes and snacks, a selected range of drinks, lively sound in the evening, prompt and proper service are the main advantages of the restaurant.





Between meals at the restaurant, guests can enjoy a meal and quench their thirst at the lobby bar, snack bar and pool bar.

Beach

Sunny weather from May to September, a warm sea, golden sand and a good mood offers tourists Sunny Beach. Trakia Plaza 4 * is located just a few minutes walk from the beach. Here vacationers are offered a convenient, gentle entrance to the sea, professional lifeguards and a medical center. The beach is urban, entrance is free for everyone, sunbeds and umbrellas are available at an additional cost.

There are plenty of opportunities for sports training: you can play a game or two of beach volleyball, surf in suitable weather, go water-skiing, a “banana” with the wind, or parachute after the boat.

Advantages of relaxation at the Trakia Plaza 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach)

Hotels in the resort of Sunny Beach is really a lot. In terms of service and comfort, most of them strive to meet European standards. Therefore, when planning a trip to the sea, determining a hotel is sometimes very difficult.





What attracts or, on the contrary, repels vacationers Trakia Plaza 4 * (Sunny Beach)? Reviews, positive and negative comments regarding the hotel service of tourists who have already been here can be easily found on the information Internet portals dedicated to the hotel business. What did our compatriots like?

  • Beautiful, well-kept hotel grounds and an impressive architectural design of the facade of the building.
  • Pool with slides for kids.
  • A cozy room with a good repair, comfortable furniture and good appliances.
  • Delicious food in the restaurant and excellent work of the bartenders.
  • Polite service staff.

Disadvantages of the hotel, according to some vacationers

  • The territory of the Trakia Plaza 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach) is really rather big, but in the high season due to the huge number of vacationers it does not seem so big.
  • There are often not enough sun loungers by the pool, so you have to occupy them early in the morning.
  • There are a lot of people in the pools and on the slides.
  • Room cleaning is not always performed efficiently, bed linen and towels are changed only after an application for a reception, and there is also a missing hair dryer.
  • Very weak animation for children and adults.
  • Catching a stable Wi-Fi signal is difficult even in public areas.
  • The lack of free sun loungers and umbrellas on the city beach, the fee for using beach equipment is palpable for the family budget.




In general, vacationers agree that at an adequate (inexpensive) cost, the Trakia Plaza 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach) can be safely recommended for youth and couples with and without children. Although, perhaps, the all-inclusive format is superfluous for Sunny Beach, because so many different delicious dishes and wonderful drinks await tourists in neighboring taverns and restaurants. All this will certainly want to try, but it will be difficult to do when buying an all-inclusive tour with excellent food in the Chuchura restaurant.
