The Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus, Protaras): description, rating and photo

The quality of rest depends on two main parameters - a tourist destination and a resort hotel. When the favorable climate is combined with quality service, it is safe to say that the vacation was a success. Many travelers planning a vacation in Cyprus prefer The Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 *.

Location

On the seashore, surrounded by lush gardens, the Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * in Cyprus is located. Protaras, Pinias 64 - the address of this institution. It is approximately 40 km from Larnaca International Airport. Resting here, you can visit such nearby tourist sites:

  • Trinity Beach - 2 km;
  • aquarium - 2 km;
  • Church of St. Elijah - 2 km;
  • Sunrise Beach - 3 km;
  • Ayia Napa Monastery - 6 km;
  • Thalass Museum - 6 km;
  • Konnos Beach - 7 km;
  • Cavo Greco Forest Park - 7 km.

Number of rooms

Cozy and comfortable rooms provides its guests for accommodation The Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 *. Namely:

  • Standard - a compact room with large or twin beds. The balcony offers views of the mainland or the sea.

  • Superior - a spacious bright room. There is a large bed, a dressing table, as well as a seating area with a pair of soft chairs. The furnished balcony offers beautiful views of the mainland or the sea.

  • Superior with Jacuzzi - Room with sea view. It differs from the previous category with a highlight in the form of a spacious balcony equipped with a jacuzzi.

  • Grand Suite - a spacious two-room suite. The bedroom has a large bed. The living room has a soft sofa and a dining table. Both rooms have a balcony with panoramic sea views.

  • Family Room - Designed specifically to accommodate guests with small children. Its highlight is the division into adult and children's zones. On the first 3/4 of the room is allocated, it is equipped with a large bed. There is a bunk bed behind the decorative partition. There is a balcony.

Room Amenities

Each room at The Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * Resort has everything you need to make travelers feel as comfortable as at home. The list of amenities is as follows:

  • wireless Internet;
  • safe;
  • tea and coffee set;
  • fan;
  • combined bathroom;
  • hair dryer;
  • bath and cosmetic accessories.

Restaurants

At The Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * in Cyprus, close attention is paid to wholesome and high-quality food. Traditional local and international dishes can be enjoyed at such establishments:

  • Atrium is an elegantly decorated restaurant where you can have breakfast and lunch both in buffet style and on the ordered menu. There are both traditional local and European dishes. The institution also has an open terrace, from which fascinating sea views open. The restaurant has recently been renovated, its area has been increased.

  • Tavern is a cozy restaurant decorated in a traditional Mediterranean style. The institution specializes in traditional Cypriot dishes, but there is also a European menu. The outdoor terrace offers views of the picturesque surroundings.

  • Mama Leone is a traditional Italian restaurant that will delight you with your favorite dishes and quality wines. In the evenings, you can listen to live music. The windows and the terrace offer sea views.

Bars

Buying a tour at The Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 *, you can be sure that you will not have a shortage of high-quality alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, delicious snacks and vibrant entertainment. This function is assigned to such hotel bars:

  • "Hesperides" - a cozy bar that has a stylish concise atmosphere. There is a calm atmosphere conducive to a solitary conversation. It has an outdoor terrace, where in the evenings you can listen to live music and take part in entertainment. The institution has recently been renovated.
  • Poseidon - Pool Bar. In between water procedures, you can drink soft or hot drinks, fruit juices, and enjoy ice cream.
  • "Tu Antamoma" - a beach bar with a wide range of drinks and snacks. You can also smoke hookah here. The outdoor area, located in close proximity to the sea, is equipped with 35 tables and is surrounded by green spaces. Suitable for romantic dinners and events.
  • Por El Mar is another beach bar located right on the edge of the sea. Decorated in a fishing style.

Leisure

A lot of opportunities for organizing leisure activities will be provided to you at The Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 *. Description of entertainment is as follows:

  • A spa that includes ample opportunities for relaxation and wellness. In addition to therapeutic areas, there is a gym and an indoor pool with a jacuzzi.
  • Outdoor pool with separate children's area. Around there are numerous sun loungers with umbrellas. The pool is surrounded by a tropical garden.
  • Artificial tennis court.
  • Beach - protected from waves and cold streams by an artificial bay. The water depth is small, which is important for vacationers with children, as well as those who can not swim. The beach is landscaped, the water is crystal clear.
  • Kids Club - all kinds of entertainment, sporting events, educational and creative activities for the youngest guests are held.
  • Animation for adults and children.
