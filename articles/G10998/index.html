History

If you want to visit the oldest park in the Urals and Siberia, then be sure to come to Yekaterinburg. Mayakovsky Park has its history since the end of the XIX century. Even then, on this place was a grove where festivities were held. The foundation of the recreation park was laid in 1930, and three years later it was inaugurated.

Then it was a suburban area, and walking paths, ponds were laid out on the territory of the park, a dance floor and three children's attractions were built. In 1936, it was supplemented by a cinema and a summer circus, increasing attendance to the maximum possible. With the outbreak of war, the park zone was transferred to a training ground for a motorized rifle reconnaissance battalion, and a new heyday for it began in 1950.





Global reconstruction has changed the look of the park. The main avenue shifted, arches were erected at the main entrance, the construction of large attractions began. Already in 1957, the park was among the best, this title remains with him until now. Every year, about 2.5 million people visit it, including locals and visitors who came to see Yekaterinburg. Mayakovsky Park regularly organizes concerts of pop stars, city holidays.
