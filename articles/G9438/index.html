Definition

What are a new type of health insurance policies? Where to get them is another question entirely. First you need to understand what kind of document in principle we are talking about.

The indicated paper is a kind of certificate that allows you to get medical care. The new sample is represented by a plastic card. It resembles bank plastic. It serves as an analogue of a compulsory medical insurance policy, helps to receive free medical care.

Documents

And what exactly is required in order to receive the specified document? To date, the MHI policy of the old and new model is issued according to similar principles. A citizen must bring with him a certain package of documents.

Among them are:

  • application for issuance of a policy;
  • old compulsory medical insurance policy (if any);
  • identification;
  • birth certificate (if it is a child);
  • certificate of registration (optional);
  • SNILS of the applicant.




If we are talking about a foreign citizen, then he will have to provide additionally:

  • migration card;
  • other evidence of legal stay in the country;
  • visa (if any).

Accordingly, these documents must be submitted either to the insurance company or to the MFC. The application must indicate that it is the new model policy that is required. After some time (usually about 30 days), a citizen will be able to present an identity card and get a new policy. A similar principle is relevant for all cities of the Russian Federation.
