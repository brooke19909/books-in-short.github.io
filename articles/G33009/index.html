From what you please

After a short digression into history, it's time to discuss the necessary materials that we need.

The first place in importance is, of course, fabric. Her choice should be approached responsibly, since the beauty and lightness of the product depend only on her. The main criteria for choosing a fabric should be: non-fading, ductility, airiness, in addition, not bad if it is easy to wear and does not wrinkle during wear.





You will also have to think about color, relying on the rest of the wardrobe details with which you plan to wear a scarf. If you need a light image, it is better to opt for pastel colors with a high content of white. And vice versa, choose juicy, tropical shades if you need a bright French scarf. How to sew a French scarf with your own hands: step by step instructions

To stitch a good product without difficulty and error, you need to tailor it correctly. Do not forget to check all measurements with a ruler, and if you are completely new, then use special drawing paper, which will greatly simplify your work.





  • Put the paper on a smooth surface and translate the pattern, and then check it with a ruler or measuring tape. It is very important that the side fold is even, so use a small plate or lid of the appropriate size.
  • Prepare the fabric in advance: it must be ironed carefully to remove the smallest folds and bumps.
  • Fold the fabric in half, gently spreading it, lay on it a pattern and pin around the entire perimeter with pins.
  • Circle the blank with soap or a special marker on the fabric, after which you can safely cut the blank and start sewing.

  • Fold the finished patterns with the sides facing inward, pin off along the entire perimeter with pins and slightly sweep the turned product. After that, you can already remove the pins and put them aside.
