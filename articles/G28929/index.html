Save time and money: tablet with phone function

Currently, hardly anyone can be surprised by the presence of a touch phone or tablet from a leading manufacturer. And the point is not that people have become rich and can afford expensive toys, but that companies that produce various gadgets and devices are lowering their prices in the struggle for the client. Now, in order to attract customers to the store, concerns that supply various mobile phones to the market either come up with attractive PR moves or equip their offspring with amazing features.





Step in the fight for the buyer

Take at least a tablet. He is an amazing design invention. With it, you can read books, accommodating a gigabyte of literature in a thin plastic ‚Äútablet‚Äù. The tablet gives us a great mood, you just have to turn on your favorite music. This device is an excellent tool for communicating on the Internet.

At this stage of the "mobile arms race", many companies took a desperate step in order to attract a client and released a tablet with a phone function. What is the risk? The thing is that, by combining two popular gadgets, firms lose huge profits. If before for complete happiness a person needed both a tablet and a phone, now only the first invention is enough. At the same time, the buyer saves money.





Mosaic of various gadgets

A tablet with a phone function can be used as a home device: be both a library, a cinema, an address book, a photo album and a landline phone. It is noteworthy that the presence of these functions in the device significantly saves not only money, but also time. Sometimes it takes a tremendous amount of time to find an e-book, graphic photo frame, or handset from a home telephone base. Now everything is combined in one device.

A tablet with a phone function can also be used as a personal device. The negative point is the need to speak on a rather bulky device. However, this is no longer a problem. It is enough to purchase a bluetooth headset suitable for the tablet, and all issues of inconvenient use will be removed.





Brand devices and their unknown colleagues

Of course, a giant like Apple will never agree to release a tablet with a phone feature. This was repeatedly stated by representatives of company management. The concern, whose emblem is a slightly bitten apple, does not want to lose the niche of the mobile market and put all its eggs in one basket. It is noteworthy that another giant, Samsung, is not particularly worried about this issue. Management cares only about the quality of products that are marketed. And this is no accident. After all, it is the Samsung tablet with the phone function that is the sales leader among the goods in this category. Having announced the publication of its new brainchild, this South Korean corporation has become a pioneer among global brands. The manufacturers of computers, netbooks and laptops followed after him. After some time, Asus released its tablet with a mobile phone function. By that time, in addition to the Samsung Galaxy Tab1, there were many good (and not so) devices without a world name on the market: Ainol, HSD and others. If we consider the options of Chinese manufacturers, then quite good tablets are released by Huawei.
