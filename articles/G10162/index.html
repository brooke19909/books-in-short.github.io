Park Beach Hotel 3 *, Limassol, Republic of Cyprus. Tourists reviews

The Cyprus Park Beach Hotel 3 * hotel (Limassol) is an excellent budget "treshka" with the first-class European service, which is located near the sea, in a forest-park zone. Tourists who come here are pleasantly surprised by the opportunity to combine high standards of relaxation with beautiful nature, clear sea and some elements of oriental luxury. It would seem that you should not expect from this coastal hotel the exclusivity of a five-star resort like the MC Beach Park Resort Hotel in Turkey, but some tourists compare these two hotels, and it happens that they conclude in favor of a Cypriot hotel. After all, the service of the Cypriot treshka is not inferior to the Quartet and even the Quintuple of neighboring countries. And in this price category it is one of the best hotels in Limassol. But you'd better draw your own conclusions.





Limassol

This resort is considered one of the most fun on the island. Yes, and it is located very well. If you have a car, you will immediately understand all the benefits of the location of Limassol. To any sights of the island from it is almost equal distance. Closer to the city center, young people like to relax - there are night clubs and discos even here in winter. And on the outskirts - romantic couples and families with children. For young tourists, Limassol is a real paradise. There are many attractions, an amusement park, as well as a water entertainment palace. There is even a zoo, the only one on the island. There are many tourists from Russia in Limassol, one might say more than in other areas of Cyprus. Get here from the airports of Larnaca or Paphos. This can be done by taxi, minibus or ferry. The beaches at the resort are excellent - sandy or with small pebbles.



Hotel Location

Park Beach Hotel 3 * (Limassol) is located almost in the forest, where pines and eucalyptus trees grow. And this is the only thing that separates it from the magnificent sandy beach. Such features make the hotel very attractive both for relaxation and for health improvement. The resort center is only three kilometers away. It is about forty minutes on foot. If it is too hot and you do not want to go on your own, then buses and minibuses stop right next to the hotel. Drive to the center for about ten minutes. But it cannot be said that the hotel is completely divorced from civilization. One has only to go beyond the territory, and you find yourself in the bustle of boutiques, shops, coastal taverns. And the nearest Papas supermarket is ten minutes away. Thus, the hotel is ideally located: on the one hand - city bustle, on the other - the measured calm of the park area. From Larnaca Airport, get here for about forty minutes, no more.

Where do tourists live?

In the Park Beach Hotel 3 hotel * (Limassol) only one hundred ten numbers. They fall into various categories. There are rooms for two, three guests, for families, as well as connecting rooms. The price of accommodation often depends on the view of the sea, and he, as tourists say, is simply gorgeous! The rooms are stylish, comfortable, with wooden floors, air-conditioned. There is satellite TV, a bathroom. Plumbing in excellent condition. Nice furniture. Wardrobe, mirror. Refrigerators can be rented for an extra charge. Almost all rooms have either balconies or private access to the street. There is a rope for drying things. Linen and towels are changed regularly. Valuable items and money can be left in the room even without any safe. No one will touch.

Entertainment and service

In the evenings, the restaurant has songs and dances. The hotel has a parking lot for tourists with their own or rented cars. For those who like to keep fit, there is a gym, as well as a spa center. There is a playground, you can invite a nanny. You can play billiards. Since entertainment lovers mostly go to "hang out" in Limassol, the rest at the hotel is very calm, the guests behave decently. The staff knows Russian, so there are no communication problems. Sometimes for holidaymakers organize evenings of national dances and music. Wi-Fi catches near the lobby, but to have access to the Network in the rooms you have to pay. The employees are smiling, showing kindness, always ready to help.



What and how to feed

Park Beach Hotel has several restaurants. The main one is indoors, but has a terrace. The rest is a la carte with local fish specialties. There are also bars in the lobby and poolside. In the last evening, music is played. Very good, high-quality half-board meals. You can take breakfast and dinner, or change the evening meal for lunch. There are different salads, an excellent selection of hot. Fish dishes and stewed vegetables are especially good. There are several types of meat - lamb, beef, pork. It is tender, cooked with spices. In general, the menu is very diverse. A lot of fruits - melons, watermelons, grapes, apples, nectarines. For a hotel of this price category, the food is wonderful. Since drinks have to be paid for dinner, tourists mostly buy local wine at a restaurant. Moreover, in Cyprus it is very famous. In addition, many shops and cafes are located immediately at the gates of the hotel.

How to sunbathe and swim

At sea they go to the municipal beach (as elsewhere in Cyprus). Only for umbrellas, sun loungers and deck chairs you will have to pay separately. The sand here is volcanic, gray in color, very useful, with a high content of silicon. The bottom is very good, entry into the water is safe. To get there - two minutes in the shade of trees. But if you do not want additional costs, the Park Beach Hotel has a large, spacious, beautiful outdoor pool, where all these beach accessories are provided in sufficient quantities. And completely free. In addition, you can sunbathe on the territory not only near the pool, but also on the lawn. And it extends to the beach promenade. So you can sunbathe in the hotel, and go swimming at sea. There are few people, there is enough space for everyone.
