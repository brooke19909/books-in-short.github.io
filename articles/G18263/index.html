Maldives All Inclusive Hotels: Description and Travel Reviews

All-inclusive Maldives hotels provide their visitors with a fantastic level of relaxation and a great setting for a relaxing holiday. Currently, potential visitors to this magical corner of the globe are forced to overpay for the selection of a place and its reservation. But a possible alternative to this approach is the global web, which allows you to buy a tour to this archipelago at minimal cost. It is about his best hotel complexes that will be discussed in this material.

About the Maldives. What kind of place is it?

Before we look at the best all-inclusive hotels in the Maldives, we will determine what kind of place it is. This archipelago is located in the Indian Ocean and includes many islands. The climate in this place of the globe is very warm, and nature pleases with diversity. All this allows you to create the most convenient conditions for relaxation.

Where can I get a tour?

There are two different ways to buy a tour package to the Maldives All Inclusive hotels. One of them is to take the help of a specialized agency in which his representative, according to your recommendations, selects a tour and offers a price for it. In addition to air travel in two directions, the price of rest will also include accommodation, meals, airport transfers and insurance. Also in this figure will be included and the premium of the travel agency for the services rendered and paperwork. But you can try to save money and take advantage of the relevant information resources on the global web. In this case, you do not need to overpay for insurance and a travel agency premium.

All Inclusive or Ultra All Inclusive power system?

Also, before choosing a vacation spot, you need to consider another important point. It concerns the power system. In some hotel complexes, the All Inclusive concept operates. In this case, the menu offered to the vacationer is abbreviated and includes only dishes, local drinks and a limited amount of alcohol. Some hotels in addition to this minimize the power supply system. For example, a visitor to the complex is free to eat in the main restaurant, but in the bar he can no longer do this, and will be forced to pay an additional fee. As a rule, this concept is mainly offered by 4-star All Inclusive hotels in the Maldives for the reason that they seek to minimize the cost of leisure. Other hotels have a different catering concept called Ultra All Inclusive. In this case, there are no restrictions on the menu, as well as on the place of consumption of the products. This concept is mainly used in expensive five-star premium hotels.

Hotel complex Paradise Island Resort & Spa 5 *

This is one of the best holiday destinations among all-inclusive Maldives hotels. Its description indicates that it is located on a separate island. Moreover, all of its infrastructure is strictly within its borders. All rooms in this complex are located in clean and well-kept villas or bungalows. The view from the windows in all cases overlooks the Indian Ocean, and this creates a very romantic atmosphere in them.

The hotel complex includes one outdoor pool, where you can swim year-round, and four specialized restaurants.In this hotel you can immerse yourself in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean and watch the life of its inhabitants, ride a surfboard, fry a barbecue and just have a great time time.

Although officially in this hotel there is a food system called All Inclusive, but in fact it needs to be called Ultra All Inclusive. The visitor can eat in any of his restaurants and taste Italian, Japanese cuisine. That is, there is certainly something to choose from. In addition to this, there are Bageecha, Dhaavani and Lagoon restaurants. The first of them is considered the main one and offers vegetarian or halal dishes. And the other two also offer their visitors a pre-formed menu. The rather large size of the island allows visitors to the complex both to retire and find a noisy company and have a great time in it. According to the site of booking.com, the rating of this complex is 8.6. Moreover, it is formed on the basis of reviews. That is, it is one of the best hotels in this part of the Indian Ocean.

Royal Island Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *

This hotel complex, as discussed above, is located on a separate island. In this case, this is Baa Atoll, which is part of the Maldives archipelago. Visitors to this hotel can only be accommodated in individual villas. In all cases, the view from the windows overlooks the Indian Ocean. There is also a separate sitting area in each room.

The list of entertainment at this hotel includes diving, surfing, water skiing and parachuting. But the number of restaurants has been reduced to one. But he has an advanced menu that matches even the highest demands.

Separately, it should be noted the coastal strip of this hotel. The sand on the beach is snow-white, and the water is clean and clear. At the same time, dense emerald vegetation comes close to it. Due to the improved infrastructure, the rating of this complex according to the booking.com resource is higher than that of the previous one, and is already 9.0 points.

All rooms of the complex are located in houses that are located fairly close to each other.

The advantages of Bandos Maldives 4 * include the power system. Kurumba Maldives complex 5 *

On a separate island of North Male, this hotel is located, like any other similar Maldives All Inclusive hotels. Photos of its territory are really pleasing to the eye. There is a coral reef of its own, which can be observed during a dive.

Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu 5 *

This holiday complex is located on the island of Tulado.
