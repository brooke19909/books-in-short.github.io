Terms and conditions for the purchase of an insurance policy

Clients of the Renaissance insurance company can purchase OSAGO online or at the office. For any of the options, it is necessary to provide documents and information about the vehicle.

If the policyholder previously purchased a policy in the Renaissance, he can extend the contract. Conditions for regular customers are more profitable, since the insurer offers a discount for those who have already insured the vehicle in their company.

Those who, in addition to compulsory insurance, purchase a voluntary CASCO policy, receive a discount of 15%. Car owners can issue a CASCO via the Internet by selecting the necessary options.

Documents for the purchase of a policy

The purchase of the policy, according to the Federal Law "On Compulsory Third Party Liability Insurance of Vehicle Owners" dated 04.25.2002 N 40-, is compulsory for everyone who is driving. Insurance companies have requirements for OSAGO buyers regarding the documents.

Owners of a vehicle can buy or extend an insurance policy in Renaissance Insurance upon presentation of the following documents.

  1. Passports Since the policy can be issued for several drivers at the same time, everyone should have it.
  2. Driving license. It is presented by all road users who are inscribed in the policy.
  3. Diagnostic card. It is required for cars that are 3 years or more. If the diagnostic card is expired, the insurer takes an individual decision to extend the CTP insurance policy.
  4. Certificate of car registration. PTS or STS, as well as other types, depending on the make of the vehicle.
  5. The previous CTP policy (relevant when renewing the contract).
  6. Statements.

A client of the Renaissance Insurance company can issue a statement at the office or online.

Not all customers trust electronic policies for fear of possible problems when checking a document. Purchasing e-OSAGO saves time: no need to wait in line at Renaissance Insurance offices. The site works around the clock, so the owner of the vehicle can do paperwork at any time.

Tariffs for the purchase of OSAGO and the electronic document are the same. According to reviews of compulsory motor third-party liability insurance in Renaissance Insurance, the insurer adheres to the rates established by law and does not overestimate the price of a document.

MTPL clearance at a discount: how to get it?

Renaissance Insurance customers can apply for a policy with a discount of up to 50%. The service is not an offer. To receive it, it is necessary to fulfill the conditions of the insurer.

Who can apply for a CTP policy 2 times cheaper? Special conditions apply to drivers who do not violate traffic rules. When calculating the discount, the correction factor KBM is used - bonus malus. It is established at the legislative level.

Bonus Malus allows you to buy OSAGO annually at 5% cheaper if the driver was not responsible for the accident last year. The CBM discount is cumulative. It "burns out" if the owner of the vehicle has not renewed CTP for more than 1 year.
