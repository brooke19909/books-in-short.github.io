Double-chamber refrigerator Liebherr CES 4023: specifications and reviews

A few words about the manufacturer

Liebherr is a German brand that produces premium class refrigerators and freezers.

It is worth noting that the company appeared in the postwar period and was founded by Hans Liebherr in 1949. To date, the company is still managed by descendants, but the headquarters moved to Switzerland.

It is surprising that the manufacturer was initially engaged in the production of equipment for construction, and only five years after active work did the company present its first refrigerator to the public.

Since the goods began to enjoy considerable demand, it was decided to develop a line of high-quality refrigerators. Today, Liebherr is a huge holding company, which has about 130 companies involved in:

  1. Development and production of construction equipment (hoists, cranes).
  2. Equipment and necessary components in the aviation sector.
  3. The release of refrigerators and freezers.

Liebherr CES 4023: model history

And although the main activity of the company was initially the design and production of construction equipment and only after the company launched the production of refrigeration units, the first devices were much different from those that are currently on store shelves. The first models had a small capacity, and also needed constant defrosting.

Desiring to improve the quality and functionality of their appliances, Liebherr tried various freezing options. For example, each section was cooled separately by a vertical evaporator. The system has received quite a lot of application and has acquired a separate name FrostSaFe. In the early 70s, the first electronically controlled refrigerator appeared, which was a major breakthrough in this area.

Some models were equipped with special sound signals that were triggered as a result of an increase in temperature in the freezer or the danger of defrosting. In addition, it really saved oblivious owners who often did not close the doors tightly. However, today all refrigerators of this manufacturer are produced in accordance with the latest technologies, so they have gained wide popularity in the consumer market.

Defrost system

In the early 90s, no one would have believed that in the future there would be such refrigerators that would not have to be thawed every month to get rid of the ice "coat". The two-chamber Liebherr CES 4023 refrigerator also has a modern defrosting system. User reviews indicate its convenience. The drip defrosting system of the refrigerator prevents the appearance of an ice "coat" on the walls and creates a natural microclimate that helps to keep food fresh for much longer. The freezer is thawed manually, but in it, unlike old refrigerators, ice forms much more slowly. Forced defrosting is required once every 6-12 months, depending on the intensity of operation of the device.



The advantages of a drip defrost system

In the refrigerator Liebherr CES 4023 (24 001) this is exactly the defrosting system. Its main advantage is the long-term preservation of products in excellent quality.

Today, No Frost systems, which are referred to as "automatic defrosting", are quite popular. However, in refrigerators with such systems, the products are very weathered. So that vegetables or fruits do not dry, you have to store them in unopened bags.

In refrigerators with a drip defrosting system, food is cooled in the shortest possible time, and when the door is opened, the temperature does not have time to rise much, so it does not spoil the quality of the food already stored there.

Another plus is emergency operation. In the event of a power outage, the products will remain chilled for another 25 hours.

Also, in comparison with the automatic defrosting system, the drip consumes significantly less energy and works much quieter, even during the activation of the ‚Äúsuperfrost‚Äù function.

Even in the absence of an ice coat, in order to prolong the life of the refrigerator, it is recommended to use the manual defrosting mechanism at least once a year and rinse it as a preventive measure.
