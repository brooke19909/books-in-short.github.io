DIY Christmas crafts - who is faster

For the New Year's tournament on the manufacture of crafts and toys, of course, the baby should be prepared in advance. To win in such a competition, he must have developed imagination and abstract thinking, as well as motor skills. Learning to do DIY Christmas crafts from the material that is offered is not always easy, especially for a child. But if he knows in advance what can be made of these or those components, then he will certainly get an original and beautiful souvenir that will deserve praise and admiration from adults.



As a rule, New Year’s crafts for kindergarten are made, and hung on a common Christmas tree or in the assembly hall. This is another exciting process for children - to place on the Christmas trees what they themselves have done. The result is a great New Year's ensemble, which brings a lot of joy to both children and their parents.

In order to make DIY Christmas crafts, children are given materials such as colored paper and cardboard, buttons, cotton wool, paints and pencils, sparkles, as well as scissors and glue. Using them, in fact, you can make a whole host of non-standard Christmas-tree decorations. For example, teach your baby to make cotton balls in advance. To do this, a piece of cotton wool must be dipped in a small amount of glue, and when it dries, pierce it with a thread. Also, such a ball can be decorated with glitter.



Cotton wool can be glued on cardboard in the form of a Christmas tree, a snowman or snowflakes. Such DIY Christmas crafts will be a great gift for the educator or for parents. It is only important that the baby knows how to properly arrange such a card, sign it and give it to whoever he wants. Next to such an application, additional drawings and figures cut out of colored paper will be very appropriate.

Using the origami technique, a child can easily be taught how to make paper balls and flashlights. A chain garland is also included in the category of such jewelry. Most often, the kids in kindergarten make it together, and then it turns out to be long, and even the largest Christmas tree can be decorated with such an ornament.

By the way, in the form of a garland, you can offer your child to make snowflakes, and carved winter mittens, and other figures. Such Christmas crafts with their own hands will look especially gentle if glued cotton or beads to them.

You can also bring New Year's crafts to kindergarten before the celebration. If a toy made by the child’s hands is on a common Christmas tree, this will bring special joy and delight to the baby. Do not forget that you can make Christmas toys at home from the most unusual materials for this business, such as disposable spoons and glasses, matchboxes, matches themselves and jars from various mixtures.

And the most simple Christmas crafts do it yourself for children, these are cones decorated with cotton and colored paper. Let the child figure out how exactly he wants to stick all these elements on the bump, and then help him fix the thread to it. Such a toy can be taken to kindergarten and hanged at home on a Christmas tree.
