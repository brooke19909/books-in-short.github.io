Greece Kos - tour to the blooming garden of the Aegean

In the Aegean Sea there is a small island of Kos, which was once called the "Garden of the Aegean Sea". This is one of the Dodecanese islands belonging to Greece. The lush green forests that covered the whole territory served this name. Olive groves, pine forests, palm trees adorn the coast of the Aegean Sea. Today, Kos is a modern attractive seaside resort in Greece. Its position is also interesting because, being the property of Greece, the island is located very close to the coast of Turkey. Therefore, tourists from this country also visit Kos on sightseeing trips.

Tours "Greece Kos" are very popular among Europeans. The climate of the island of Kos is beautiful sunny weather almost all year round, hot summers with low humidity, mild winters. The beach season here begins in April and lasts until October.

Tour "Greece Kos" is a great opportunity to relax in one of the most environmentally friendly corners of the planet. The beaches of the island are surprising in their diversity. Some are covered with volcanic sand (moreover, the sand is found in three colors - white, gold and black), while others are small-pebble. There are long beaches, but there are very small ones located in beautiful bays. It should be noted that most of the beaches on the island are well equipped.

Tour "Greece Kos" allows vacationers to spend an unforgettable vacation, having accumulated vivid impressions and thrills.

Several modern resorts on the island of Kos are especially in demand by tourists.

Kefalos resort is famous for its picturesqueness, cool waters of the sea, as well as a great opportunity to engage in water sports. Here you can rent everything you need for an active holiday on the water.

In the towns of Kos, Psalidi there are diving centers, a surf station.

Kardamena resort is appreciated by lovers of a fun life. During the day you can enjoy walking along the promenade and having a bite to eat in colorful fish taverns and cozy restaurants, buy thyme honey and olive oil, popular with thyristists. At night, discos and bars will not let you get bored.

Those wishing to restore their health can be advised by the Psalidi resort, where sulfuric thermal springs are located. In addition, many hotels have wellness centers in their arsenal (SPA-procedures, tallotherapy, massages, pressure therapy, caviar therapy). A vacation spent in such a place will allow you to relax, relieve depression, improve well-being and appearance.

The excursion program on the island of Kos will allow you to touch the ancient Greek times. Here you can look at the ruins of the temple of Asclepius and the ancient hospital, the ruins of the ancient city of Pili, leave a photo for memory with the Platan of Hippocrates - the famous ancient Greek healer. On the island of Kos there are many legends associated in the name of this philosopher, and the Hippocratic oath is sold in different languages. Every year, tourists can become participants in the festival dedicated to Hippocrates.
