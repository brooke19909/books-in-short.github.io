Rixos The Land of Legends 5 * (Turkey, Belek): description, service, reviews

Rixos system hotels are recognized as luxury hotels around the world. They confirm their reputation from year to year. A vivid proof of this is Rixos The Land of Legends 5 * in Turkey in Belek. This is the world where a fairy tale comes to life. The concept of the park, located 500 meters from the hotel, was created in a creative tandem with Franco Dragone, a world-famous director. His grandiose show for Cirque du Soleil amazes with its entertainment. In Belek, he created a magical country with incredible heroes and characters that surround tourists. Vacationers are transported into a beautiful world of fantasies. Even adults can plunge here into a joyful carefree childhood. This place is the embodiment of luxury and pleasures, magic and science fiction. The sun and the Mediterranean Sea, Mount Toros and pine groves - all this perfectly complement the landscapes of this fairy tale. Here, water canals await tourists, which combine the park into one mini-city, an embankment arranged in the style of “unreal reality”, an area with thematic buildings, towers, fountains, many colorful, thematically decorated pools.





Description

This is a luxury hotel where everything is done for children and adults. The hall is decorated with balls, chandeliers, vases, flowers, furnished with exclusive furniture. At the entrance, guests are treated to Turkish national sweets. Every detail is carefully thought out in the interior, so the wonderful world, where children and adults fall, will always remain in their hearts.

Four hundred rooms occupy seven floors of the hotel building. Its scale is so impressive that the territory of Rixos The Land of Legends 5 * is equipped with "Venetian channels" through which you can navigate on gondolas. Beautiful figured bridges are thrown through these channels, which are convenient for organizing photo sessions. These buildings are especially attractive in the evening, when they turn on the backlight.

Rixos The Land of Legends is not just a hotel, it is a recreation and entertainment park that opened in 2016. Today it is the largest and most grandiose in all of Turkey.

The hotel is captivating and the grandeur of scale, and carefully thought out every detail, and the abundance of water arteries in the territory, and an incredible amount of flowers, and many figures of fairy-tale characters. The “Kingdom”, created on its territory, is inhabited by wonderful characters, where the role and image of each was specially invented for the amusement park Rixos The Land of Legends. A variety of water slides and attractions, an amazing water and animal kingdom will amaze even experienced adventurers. Amidst all this splendor, nooks and crannies that are sweet to the heart are harmoniously inscribed, where each guest can relax from the festive fun reigning around and be in silence.





Location

This wonderful hotel is located in the village of Kadria (Belek). The distance to it from the center of Belek is only 2 km. Travel time from the nearest airport to Rixos The Land of Legends is approximately thirty minutes. In this place of the Turkish coast, unusually beautiful nature and pristine sandy beaches. Almost all of them have been awarded the Blue Flag. Rixos The Land of Legends (Turkey) is adjacent to the park and is located on the second line (about 1700 meters from the coast). Some remoteness from the sea does not upset the tourists, because as a compensation they have a lot of spectacles and entertainment. And they are delivered to the beach by free transfers provided by the hotel.

Rooms

The hotel has 400 rooms, the design of which is not repeated. They are all different, all exclusive. Each room has a balcony or a spacious terrace, Wi-Fi, Playstation consoles and backlit TVs with a double-sided screen. The rooms are decorated in the style of a fairy-tale country with themed furniture and drawings on the walls. The owners of the Rixos The Land of Legends have taken into account the interests and safety of children. The furniture in the rooms is set without sharp corners, and the materials used in design and everyday life are environmentally friendly and hypoallergenic.

Room categories:

1. Deluxe 38 m². Equipment: air conditioning, safe, TV, new refrigerator, many lamps, very comfortable colorful furniture. The hygiene room provides a set of hygiene products, including not only standard gel, shampoo, soap, but also toothpaste, toothbrushes, hair and body conditioner, bath foam, ear sticks, a razor, foam and shaving gel.

2. Deluxe Connected. The room is two-room. One bedroom has a double bed, the other two separate ones for children or friends. All rooms of this category are equipped with interior doors, woolen carpets, a projector and a screen.

3. Junior with an area of ​​79 m². There are also two bedrooms. The room is ideal for families with children.

4. Family with an area of ​​83 m². Family Suite has two bedrooms and a balcony. The architectural and design idea is aimed at accommodating families with children.

5. Grand with an area of ​​108 m². The room is two-room, made in an exclusive design.

6. Terrace with an area of ​​130 m². It is equipped with a terrace, which provides a unique opportunity to admire the park of the Rixos The Land of Legends 5 * hotel.

7. Kingdom with an area of ​​283 m². This number is unique. There are two bedrooms, a living room, the bathroom has a jacuzzi. This room offers personalized service.

Service for children

The great thing about Rixos The Land of Legends is that it pays attention to the needs of the smallest children. For babies from 6 months to 3 years, all conditions are created for a safe and comfortable pastime. Nannies will take care of the babies as long as their parents wish.





Rixos The Land of Legends is the dream of all children. Here they fall into another dimension, where there is a children's pool, water park, discos, 6 cinemas, show programs. The main attraction and place of entertainment is the park, located in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.





Rixos The Land of Legends Amusement Park

Hotel guests have access to all attractions, but at an additional cost. Entrance to the park for an adult costs 40 y. e., and for a child - 30 at. e. But this park is worth the money! The most incredible thing here is not the number of water slides and attractions, but the concept of the entire “Land of Legends”. The magnificent Water Park will surprise you with 70 of the latest slides. There is a huge pool with artificial waves where everyone can feel the adrenaline rush. What else is there in the park:

- rides that are breathtaking;

- Aquarium with friendly animals;

- a zoo with trained tigers;

- artificial rivers (mountain and calm);

- viewing platforms;

- An amphitheater where world-class stars perform.

Each evening, a grand closing ceremony is held. This grand procession is a boat parade along the water channel along the avenue. The performance is striking in its grandeur.

The following services are provided in the park:

1. Meeting with dolphins.

Children have a great opportunity to swim with dolphins. Duration of communication - 30 minutes. All the time in the pool is an experienced trainer trainer. Children are admitted from ten years. A prerequisite - you must be able to swim.

2. Immersion.

Feeding exotic fish underwater is a good opportunity to see the underwater world from the inside. Professional divers will be with the guest immersed in the water for thirty minutes. The depth of the pool is 4 meters. It also provides a unique opportunity to take pictures in the water kingdom surrounded by marine inhabitants. These photos and experience will remain for life.

3. Climb to the tower.

Like the scale of the entire park, the tower impresses with its grandeur. Its height is one hundred and eleven meters. There are several viewing platforms on it so that everyone can enjoy a bird's eye view of the fabulous beauty of the park. In the evening, the tower is a place where you need to admire the bright lights and sunset.

4. Shopping.

The hotel has a street with a lot of boutiques and shops. Everyone will find something for themselves. All brand stores are located along the canal. You can also have a cup of delicious Turkish coffee during the promenade.

5. Cinema.

To entertain guests, the hotel has IMAX, 5D cinemas. The latest special effects (moving seats, splashes of water, wind) will provide a full viewing experience. There is an area designed for desktop and video games.





Aquapark

In Europe, this water park is considered the most interesting and ambitious. Slides here are of various shapes and configurations. Among them there are open and closed, terrible (speed develops up to 90 km / h) and not very, for the brave and more cautious visitors.

"Typhoon Coaster" is the park's most popular attraction. These are real roller coasters where free fall turns into bends with water flows.

The smallest in the water park will delight bright playgrounds. There is a large water play area, which resembles a magical castle on an enchanted island, where an abandoned sailboat flaunts on the shore.

Children's Aqua-disco like all children. Here they are waiting for splashes of water, clockwork light music, singing and dancing fountains, dancing fairy-tale characters.

Reviews

Trip Advisor certificate - there is evidence that the plan was successful. After all, it is issued based on the reviews of people about a particular place, whether it is a hotel or a restaurant. About Rixos Land of Legend, traveler reviews are mostly enthusiastic. In their opinion, this is the most ambitious hotel and park in Turkey, where you want to return again and again. It is important to note that tours at Rixos Land of Legends are available year round. However, this magical place has its drawbacks. In the reviews, tourists note the following disadvantages of the hotel:

- the power supply system is only "HB", which involves additional costs;

- too bright colors in the design of the rooms, which is not quite suitable for guests without children;

- far to the beach;

- Paid entrance to the amusement park.
