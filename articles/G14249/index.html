Bauman Residence Hotel, Phuket, Thailand: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Bauman Residence Hotel is a hotel complex with comfortable rooms decorated in a modern style and having a good interior. In general, Thailand is considered a resort with a high level of hospitality, so it is not surprising why many people fly here. So, why choose this hotel complex for your vacation?





Briefly about the hotel

Bauman Residence Hotel is a 10-minute walk from the famous Patong Beach. And Phuket International Airport is only 45 minutes away. This location is considered quite convenient and advantageous. And not close, and not far - it is just possible to get to the airport quickly, but landing and taking off planes will not interfere with vacationers with their noise.

And a five-minute drive from the hotel is the large Jungceylon Shopping Center. It is interesting that the part of the city in which this hotel complex is located is the most beloved among the guests - they themselves assure so. And the beach in the immediate vicinity, and all other interesting places that I would like to visit, being here on vacation.

Offered rooms

So, guests are offered a choice of six types of rooms. The most popular is considered a double - with one bed and with access to the pool (3-4 guests are possible). The second most frequent reservation is a penthouse suite for five. Next comes the superior triple room. There are two options - with 1 wide bed or with two separate. Double deluxe rooms are also available. Also to choose from - with one wide bed or 2 twin.

More guests can stay in a family apartment for four. And finally, the last option is a two-bedroom suite (designed for five guests).





Services offered

At Bauman Residence Hotel, guests can take advantage of a variety of facilities and services. So, in the fresh air is an outdoor swimming pool, which will appeal to lovers to calmly swim in the cool water and soak up the shade, and not burn on the beach in the sun.

The Bauman Residence Hotel also has a restaurant and bar, where tourists can enjoy fresh and original international dishes and a variety of drinks. Free private parking is available. And if a person rented a car, then he may not worry - you do not need to pre-book a place, everything is done on the spot.

Bauman Residence Hotel offers high-quality service. So, for example, can be organized the delivery of drinks and food directly to the room or a shuttle service (only for a fee). The registration desk is open around the clock, because there will be no problems with an early check-in or a late check-out - it‚Äôs only worthwhile to make reservation with the administration in advance.

Guests who come to rest with children can be offered a personal nanny or babysitting services. Dry cleaning and laundry services are also available on site. Wishing even provide ironing service.





Important booking information

Before you begin to pay or choose your own room, you should familiarize yourself with the information regarding the reservation. So, check-in at the Bauman Residence Hotel is registered at the same time as in the rest of the hotel complexes. From 14:00 until late in the evening. And departure - before noon. But, as mentioned above, if you previously tell the administration that the guests will have to leave later or check in earlier, there will be no problems, the request will be fulfilled.

The hotel can be accommodated with children of any age. Interestingly, if the child is not four years old, then he is allowed to stay in the reserved apartments for free. And if the baby is not even two years old, then they can provide him with a separate crib. If the child is older than the specified age, you will need to pay an additional 50% of the cost of the reservation. An extra bed is also available upon request, so you should be puzzled by this issue as soon as possible.
