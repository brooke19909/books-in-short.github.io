Rest of the child in the camp "Horizon"

Summer holidays are a great time for children and their parents. Therefore, mothers and fathers should first think about how to make the rest of your beloved child diverse, fun and useful.





Camp "Horizon" in the Poltava region offers an unforgettable vacation for every child. Of course, parents should study the features and conditions of a son or daughter in a summer institution.

Why is it worth sending a child to the Horizon camp

There are many significant reasons why you should send your child to rest. Health Camp "Horizon" offers children:

  • A large territory, which is located in a picturesque point of Ukraine, near the city of Kremenchug. The camp area is more than 7 hectares. Nearby is the river Psel, where a separate beach is equipped for the camp.
  • On the territory there are sports grounds for soccer, volleyball, and basketball. Children who love sports will definitely find something to do.
  • Near each building in which children live, there are gazebos, tables for playing table tennis, playgrounds with swings, slides and other entertaining structures.
  • Vacationers are accommodated in rooms in which 4, 5 or 6 children live. The rooms have all the amenities, a modern fresh renovation. Also on the floors there is an area with sofas where you can watch TV or just relax from active activities.
  • Every day the children will spend their leisure time, immersing themselves in recreational activities prepared for them. Various contests, competitions, relay races, discos, holidays will not let boys and girls get bored for a single minute.
  • There is a tent camp on the territory of the Horizon camp. Here, children can spend time during the sultry hours, feeling like real tourists.
  • The bonfire zone will bring many pleasant memories of rainbow conversations and songs.




These are just some of the nice offers that the Horizon camp will give to kids of different ages.

Camp safety

The administration of the camp "Horizon" in the Poltava region is committed to ensure the safety of every vacationer. The concept of security includes many nuances:

  • 24 hour security of the camp.
  • Medical service.
  • The continued presence of a police officer in the territory increases the level of security of the territory.
  • Counselors are constantly in the know on which clubs and sporting events each child from the squad is.
  • In addition to the fact that there is security on the territory, each of the buildings of the Horizon camp is also assigned a security officer who monitors the order and safety of the things that children leave in the rooms around the clock.
  • Also, for the safety and peace of mind of parents, the administrator has a mobile phone with different operators. Therefore, in case of urgent need, mothers and fathers can personally speak with their child.




The camp administration also asks parents to explain to their children before arrival that they should listen to counselors and other representatives of the administration of the Gorizont children's health camp.

What a child needs to have with him

During the trip, children will need mint sweets or tablets, as well as water. From clothes, the child needs to give with him:

  • sportswear;
  • summer clothes;
  • jacket for the evening;
  • elegant clothes for various events, holidays, discos;
  • comfortable clothes for excursions;
  • swimwear or swimming trunks;
  • Underwear;
  • cap, panama hat;
  • shoes should be athletic, smart, casual and for the soul;
  • you also need to give a toothbrush, paste, washcloth, soap, shampoo, bath towel, toilet paper, sunblock;
  • You can give your child children's magazines, a book;
  • if there is, then you can give carnival costumes to participate in concert programs.




It is worth writing the child a list of things that he had with him so that he does not forget anything important at the end of the shift.

Camp infrastructure

Children's camp "Horizon" has the following buildings in its infrastructure:

  • Residential buildings, of which there are five pieces. Near each building there are gazebos, which host squad events and competitions of the Horizon camp.
  • Administration building, where the director, accountant, shower rooms, and laundry are located. Also in this building is a first-aid post, an insulator.
  • Even on the territory of the Horizon camp in the Poltava region there is a cultural and entertainment building in which concerts, competitions are held, there is a cinema hall in which popular cartoons and children's films are shown. The psychologist is also in this building. Also in the cultural and entertainment complex is a dining room. In front of the building there are discos, recreational activities in the open.
  • Sports grounds.
  • Bonfire zone.
  • Playground.

All this suggests that every child will find a hobby for themselves that will help to spend their leisure time pleasantly and brightly. All factors indicate that young vacationers have a great time at the Horizon Children's Health Camp.
