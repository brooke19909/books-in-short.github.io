An original, tasty, beautiful gift for any occasion - a tree made of sweets

Candy as a gift ... Nice, but so banal and ordinary! Another thing is a tree made of sweets. It is beautiful, bright and original. Such a present can be presented to both a child and an adult. And most importantly, each of you, dear readers, can make such a tasty and effective gift with your own hands. The technology of its implementation, we present to you in our master class. Following the step-by-step instructions, you can create a tree of sweets on your own. Photos of different versions of this sweet product are also offered to your attention in the article.



Making a sweet tree ball. Preparatory stage

In order to make a tree of sweets, you will need such materials:

  • sweets of round or triangular shape (in wrappers);
  • flower pot (metal, ceramic, plastic);
  • foam ball;
  • plastic tube or wooden stick;
  • satin and kapron ribbons;
  • brown or corrugated paper;
  • foil;
  • sisal fiber;
  • gypsum;
  • decorative elements (beads, glass beads, shells);
  • Double-sided tape;
  • glue gun.

We make a tree of sweets with our own hands: MK (master class)

  1. Wrap a tube or stick with tape, stick its ends on top and bottom. This is the trunk of the future sweet tree.
  2. In the foam ball, make a hole in diameter coinciding with the circumference of the stick. Pour glue into it and insert the barrel. Hold for some time so that the parts stick together.
  3. Wrap the ball with foil, securing it with double-sided tape.
  4. Dilute the gypsum and fill it with a flower pot. Insert the stick barrel in the middle. Hold for several minutes until the plaster sets. Leave the workpiece for an hour and a half for solidification.
  5. Apply a drop of glue to each candy and attach it to a foam base. You need to arrange sweets as close to each other as possible.
  6. Decorate the gaps between the candies with flowers made of corrugated paper, sisal fiber.
  7. Wrap the pot with wrapping paper, attaching it with tape. Tie a satin or nylon ribbon bow on top.
  8. Plaster to decorate to your liking. It can be shells, bugles, beads, decorative moss or sand.


The candy tree is done! Such a gift can be supplemented with a small souvenir in the form of a soft toy, figurines, pendants, tying them directly to the pot or attaching them under the barrel.

Sweet Tree Options

A variety of products can be made from sweets. For example, for the New Year the tree may be in the form of a Christmas tree. The basis of such sweet beautiful Christmas trees is a foam or cardboard cone.

Delicious palm trees are also made from sweets. In this case, not a ball is taken as the basis, but a rectangular block and, in addition to sweets, long leaves from corrugated paper are used for decoration.
