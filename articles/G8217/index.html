Verano Phoenix Family Resort 4 *, Side, Turkey





Holidays in the Turkish city of Side is very popular for several years. This is facilitated by beautiful local nature, well-groomed beaches, bright sun and a huge amount of entertainment for every taste. It is also pleasant that Side hotels offer their services at reasonable prices, while their level is either close to ideal or is such. It is worth noting that there are several dozen first-class hotels in the city, each of which deserves special attention. Today I will introduce you to the verano phoenix family resort, which, on the one hand, is famous for its excellent service, and on the other hand, many tourists have not the most pleasant memories associated with visiting it. But first things first.

Verano phoenix family 4 is located on the seafront, 3 km from Side and 75 km from Antalya. Some time ago the hotel had a completely different name: "Club Verano". The hotel was founded in 1988, and the first restoration took place in 2006, thanks to which the hotel has slightly changed its external and internal appearance. On the territory of 30,000 m2 there are 120 standard rooms, 28 single rooms, 142 rooms for a family vacation, as well as various services designed to make your vacation pleasant and varied. Speaking generally about the rooms, they are quite cozy and comfortable. Equipped with bath, shower, TV, air conditioning, minibar, safe, telephone, and some have Internet access. What pleases is the presence of a balcony and terrace, which offers a beautiful view of the hotel and the beach.
