Egypt, hotel Club Reef Hotel 4: photos and reviews of tourists

Egypt attracts tourists from all over the world with the richness of its culture, the splendor of beaches, and the hospitality of its inhabitants. For a traveler, one of the key aspects of a comfortable pastime is choosing a hotel. The options in this case can be a huge amount. Many tourists choose, heading to Egypt, the Club Reef 4 * hotel. What are its features? How do travelers characterize the quality of rest in it?

General Hotel Information

The hotel category Club Reef Hotel is 4 stars. It is located near the resort of Sodf. Another common geographical landmark is Aqaba Bay. The main components of the hotel complex infrastructure: open-type pools, disco clubs, as well as restaurants. Among the notable types of entertainment are an amphitheater and animation (there are daytime and evening programs).



Another geographic landmark of the hotel is Naama Bay. If you determine the location of the hotel complex on a larger scale, it can be noted that it is located in the southern part of the Sinai Peninsula. Actually, Sharm El Sheikh is also nearby. From the center of this famous tourist center to the Club Reef Hotel 4 * hotel - several kilometers. Sharm El Sheikh Airport is a few minutes drive away.

Hotel Club Reef Resort 4 * was built in 1999. The hotel complex is located directly on the Red Sea. Hotel Club Reef 4 * owns a sandy-coral beach. At the disposal of guests are umbrellas, comfortable sun loungers, mattresses, as well as towels. The beach is located at a distance of about 100 meters from the hotel.

The hotel complex is designed for a variety of categories of guests - youth, families. Here, both lovers of solo travel and tourists traveling to resorts with friends will rest in comfort.

All for health

Hotel Club Reef 4 * is a great place for lovers of outdoor activities. Guests can spend time playing sports - tennis, volleyball, play billiards, darts, and train on simulators. There is also the opportunity to play futsal. The hotel has 3 outdoor pools. There is a spa, massage rooms, a jacuzzi. There is also an infrastructure for diving. There is an indoor pool. Hotel guests can spend time in the beautiful garden as well as on the terrace.

Services

What is the range of basic services presented in the hotel complex Club Reef Hotel 4 *? The reception is open around the clock, there is a luggage storage room. If necessary, you can order a transfer from the airport. Free parking is available for hotel guests. Advance booking is not required. You can also order a taxi, rent a limousine. Tourists can rent a conference hall or a hall for a banquet. You can use the services of a business center, print or copy the text. Among the services needed and in demand by many guests are laundry, hairdresser, dry cleaning. If necessary, you can contact the services of a doctor. The hotel provides wheelchairs for people with disabilities. On the territory of the complex you can exchange currency, as well as use an ATM. As for payment for services, only VISA and MasterCard cards are accepted . The range of services available to hotel guests is generally typical of modern Egyptian 4-star resorts. There are all necessary services for tourists.

Rooms

The hotel offers rooms with balconies with magnificent views of the Red Sea. The rooms have TV, air conditioning. In total, the Club Reef Hotel has 4 * 135 apartments.



Each room has a private bathroom, telephone, refrigerator. A hairdryer and towels are at guests' disposal. Rooms are cleaned and replaced daily. A minibar is available at an additional cost.

Nutrition

Meals at the hotel are organized according to the All Inclusive system. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are guests in the main restaurant of the complex. The range offered to guests includes a wide range of dishes and drinks. The main restaurant serves a buffet in the evening , which serves traditional Egyptian cuisine. The hotel complex has a pizzeria. In addition to buffet meals, you can visit the bar, as well as the snack bar, which sells snacks.

Breakfast in the main restaurant of the hotel is organized from 7 to 10 hours, lunch - from 13 to 15, dinner - from 19:30 to 21:30. You can also order an additional dinner at the reception, it will be served at 20:00. The main bar serves various snacks from 11:30 to 12:30 and from 16:00 to 17:00. You can also order an additional seafood dinner, it is recommended that you reserve a table in advance.
