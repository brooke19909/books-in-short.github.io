Montenegro, Kamelia 2 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The proximity of location, affordable prices, luxurious beaches, numerous natural and man-made attractions - this is what attracts the citizens of Russia to Montenegro. Many tourists after the first trip to this country tend to get into it again. However, it must be remembered that all the positive emotions from excursions to local attractions and relaxing on luxurious beaches can be spoiled by a bad hotel. For this reason, his choice must be approached with particular care. Hotel Kamelia 2 *, located in Tivat, is very popular among Montenegrin guests.

Tivat - location and climate

Tivat is a town in Montenegro, located next to the Bay of Kotor. Picturesque resort gives the beautiful Adriatic coast park, created at one time by sailors who brought here exotic plants from all over the world. The Tivat city park is also famous, on the territory of which mimosa, palm trees and many other plants grow. 5 km from the city there is an airport with links to many cities of different countries, so guests of the Kamelia 2 * hotel do not have to spend a lot of time on the way to the place of rest.





Tivat is famous for a large number of sunny days - about 200 a year. Once this resort was the resting place of the aristocracy, and today on its territory there are many museums and galleries dedicated to those times. There is also a large port in the city, from where comfortable liners depart, on which everyone who lives in various Tivat hotels, including Kamelia 2 *, can take a boat trip. Montenegro has on its territory many interesting cities that can be visited just during such a mini-cruise.

Number of rooms

The room fund of Kamelia 2 * hotel is represented by 156 double and triple rooms and 6 apartments. At the same time, up to 280 people can live in them. All standard rooms are equipped with comfortable beds in the amount corresponding to the number of guests. There is a fridge, stools, a table, a telephone, a separate bathroom, and access to a balcony, from where magnificent views are opened in front of the hotel guests. Some rooms are air-conditioned.

The apartments are presented in the form of two-room rooms. There is a lounge with a sofa and a separate kitchen equipped with the necessary utensils and electrical appliances. This type of room has a bathroom with a shower, washbasin, toilet, and all the necessary hygiene products and towels. All hotel rooms are regularly cleaned.





Nutrition

Meals at the hotel - half board: breakfast and dinner are served in the form of a buffet, the hotel does not provide lunch. However, on the territory of Kamelia there is a restaurant with a capacity of 500 seats, where vacationers can order any dish presented on the menu, regardless of the time of day. In addition, there are many cafes near the hotel where you can also eat delicious food for a small fee. Vacationers also like to sit in the bar on the terrace, enjoying refreshments, which are always very handy on hot days.





Beach

Hotel Kamelia 2 * has its own beach, which is located at a distance of 25 meters. The bottom of the sea is flat and smooth. For an additional fee, vacationers can use sun umbrellas and sunbeds. It is worth noting that the sea here is always warm and clean during the summer season, so even the smallest vacationers can enjoy swimming in the water.
