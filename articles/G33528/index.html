How to knit a panda amigurumi?

The fashion trend of handmade now came from Japan. Amigurumi charming panda cubs are popular with everyone. By tradition, such toys are crocheted, small details are sewn with sewing thread. Each bear receives a name. Knitting begins with the so-called amigurumi ring, from which six loops are pulled. Then they knit twelve loops, knitting two out of one. All amigurumi have one drawing - single crochet. Another subtlety is a very tight knitting, which is achieved through the use of a small hook.

All toys are assembled from three forms: a ball, cone and figure, in which on the one hand is a cone, and on the other is a spherical rounding. For knitting such figures, there are patterns. An absolutely even flat circle is obtained by adding after the second row with twelve loops of six loops in each row. The cone requires the addition of the same amount, but through a row. The cylindrical sections are knitted without adding or decreasing loops.

Cute panda amigurumi

For a knitted animal you will need white and black yarn, finished eyes, a 1.5 hook and a syntepuh.

Start knitting with black paws (pens). By the principle of the cone, knitting reaches 12 loops. Then it is created on the principle of a cylinder up to 18 rows. After doing a reduction of the cone. Knit two legs.

Next, knit black legs. Start with the standard six loops, reach 20 and knit a cylinder in three rows. After they make 3 loops on one side (this is the bend of the foot) and knit one row. Then add 3 loops in this place one row higher and continue to the 18th row. After doing a decrease. Such parts need two pieces.

Black ears are knitted from a chain of three air loops. Next, in the central loop, make an addition in each row to the fifth and close them. There will be two such details.

The head is knitted with white yarn in the form of a ball. The muzzle - the white hemisphere - is sewn separately. "Glasses" are made of air loops with black yarn. They are sewn at the stage of knitting the cylinder. After the ‚Äúglasses‚Äù are sewn, the eyes are inserted.

The torso is knitted with a drop, changing the thread from black to white at chest level. Stuff details as you knit.

How to collect a panda?

All parts are sewn with black or white thread. First, prepare the head. Sew on the muzzle and ears. The location of the ears is preliminarily noted so that they do not move to the side. The nose and antennae, eyebrows, tongue are embroidered.

Sew the head to the torso. At this moment, it is important to ensure that the stuffing does not fall into the seam and check whether the toy is weakly stuffed. There is still time to fix it.

The paws can be made movable by sewing on buttons, as in the photo. An amigurumi panda of such a model can sit, stand, raise one leg.
