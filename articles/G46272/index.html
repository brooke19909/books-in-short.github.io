Most tourists in the summer are in this airborne harbor. There is another airport located in the capital of Montenegro - Podgorica. The distance to it from Budva is about 40 km.



Villa Danica: description and photo

This mini-hotel consists of several apartments located in two adjacent two-story buildings. All rooms have the necessary furniture, a private bathroom, a TV with Russian-language channels. Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Each apartment also has a small fully equipped kitchen. Please note that beach towels are not provided here. Therefore, you can take them with you from home or buy directly on the spot in one of the many shops.



Infrastructure and entertainment

Since Villa Danica is a very small institution, it does not provide any entertainment for its guests. However, it was not without reason that Budva was called the queen of tourism, because here every traveler will find something to do. So, in this city there are many nightclubs and discos, a variety of restaurants and cafes. In the Old Town there is a fortress with a museum where tourists like to roam. In addition, in numerous tour agencies you can book a trip to any part of the country. As for the beaches of Budva, here you will also find a lot of water activities for every taste.



Villa "Danica" (Montenegro, Budva): reviews of tourists

Since for most people planning their vacation, it is important not only to get information from a tour operator, but also to find out the opinions of other travelers who have already stayed in a particular place, we decided to save your time a little and familiarize you with the generalized comments of our compatriots about their vacation in the considered mini-hotel.

So, first of all, the vast majority of guests say that Villa Danica is a very cozy, well located place with an optimal price-quality ratio. Especially demanding guests to come here, according to tourists, is not worth it. After all, living here is quite inexpensive, so you should not hope for luxury apartments and high-class service.

In general, the rooms, although not large, are pretty comfortable. There is everything you need, even a small kitchen with utensils. Experienced travelers recommend staying in apartments on the second floor, if possible, as they have separate balconies. Some guests were embarrassed by the presence in the bathroom not of a full-fledged shower or bath, but of a tray with a curtain. However, this feature is characteristic of many economy-class hotels both in Montenegro and in other European countries.

Now we find out what the guests think about the location of Villa Danica (Cat. C). Budva is a small town, so by and large the location of the hotel does not really matter here. As for the villa in question, its location is considered by many to be very advantageous. So, tourists say that it took them no more than 10 minutes to get to the beach, and you could walk to the city center in a quarter of an hour with a quick step.
