Features of working with the device

Redmond meat grinders are equipped with a reinforced gearbox, which significantly increases productivity: in one minute, the unit can process up to 1.5 kg of products. The electric motor of the Redmond meat grinder RMG-1203 is reliably protected by a plastic safety lock against an overload. Its rated power is 500 watts, maximum - 3000 watts. Also included are 5 adapters. Smooth operation of the device is ensured by saber-shaped cutting edges. This feature increases the length of cutting products, which, in turn, increases processing.

Buy a meat grinder Redmond RMG-1203 is still due to its functionality. In addition to processing meat, it can be used to grind other solid products: vegetables, fruits, cheese, nuts. In addition, it can cut food into slices and act as a grater.
