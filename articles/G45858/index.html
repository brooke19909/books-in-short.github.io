Yubileiny Park (Yaroslavl): a great place to relax

One of the most favorite vacation spots for residents of the city of Yaroslavl is the Jubilee Park, which is located in the Leninsky district of the city. This is not only a place for contemplative walks in nature along quiet alleys. The park often hosts various events, sports competitions, various meetings of interest. The official and informal cultural life of Yaroslavl is concentrated in this territory. This is one of the oldest places of culture and recreation in the city, very popular with representatives of all ages.





Park history

Yubileiny Park (Yaroslavl) is one of the oldest in the city. Located in the Leninsky district. The park was founded in honor of the 950th, anniversary, birthday of Yaroslavl. Hence the name.

It was one of the favorite meeting places of the townspeople. A magnificent fountain was broken in the park. On the shady wide alleys, the Yaroslavl people walked with pleasure at any time of the year or day, loved to relax on the veranda. Children sought to this park to ride on the carousel.

In the 1990s, stagnation occurred, the park was forgotten and badly launched. Events and concerts were still held periodically, but there was no life here. Everything radically changed with its reconstruction in 2017. Now, locals with pleasure come on a day off to the Jubilee Park in Yaroslavl. The zoo, updated carousels, playgrounds, an alley of bright colorful umbrellas, a reconstructed fountain that has not been functioning for more than 20 years - this is what makes this place happy!

Contact zoo

The jubilee park has been delighting visitors with recent pleasant innovations. For example, in 2017, a contact zoo (Yaroslavl) was opened here . Yubileiny Park provides an opportunity to watch animals at arm's length and even stroke them, and all this is completely free. For money, only food is purchased, as well as the opportunity to take pictures with the inhabitants of the zoo. You can see such animals and birds as raccoon, sheep, peacock, turkey, hens, ducks, geese, etc. All animals are friendly and even allow themselves to be stroked. The main thing is to follow the rules of visiting. Do not give animals food that is not intended for them, as well as offend them. A contact zoo is open on all days of the week, except Mondays, from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. It is worth paying attention that from 12:30 to 13:30 there is a technical break.

Dinosaur park

In 2017, a small but popular dinosaur park for children was also opened in Yubileiny. An entrance ticket here costs 150 rubles for adults on weekdays, and 200 on weekends. Children, respectively, 100 and 150 rubles. There is a dinosaur park from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m., without days off and breaks. What is this territory? The park has large dinosaur figures, very realistic. They move, growl, sometimes quite frighteningly. The organizers warn that visiting this place with very young children is not worth it. They may be scared. It is recommended to go here with kids from at least five years old. For older children, the quest will also be interesting - a search for dinosaur bones. This can be very interesting for older preschoolers and even schoolchildren.

Where is the Yubileiny park in Yaroslavl

Yubileiny Park (Yaroslavl) is located in the Leninsky district almost in the center of the city. The main entrance goes to Chkalova street, to its beginning. From the side of the service entrance, which is at the DC of motor builders, we get to Lenin Avenue. This is a beautiful city street with typical Soviet impressive buildings.





Along the park, Yaroslavl train can be reached by buses 9, 11, 30, and trolleybus 3. The necessary stop is Yubileynaya Square.

From the Krasnoperekopsky district, you can get on buses and minibuses 3, 85, 95. If you go from the Solovievskaya hospital stop, trams 1 and 7 go to Yubileiny.
