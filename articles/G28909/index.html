LCD Liverpool (Samara) - business-class housing offered by the developer in the city center

Samara is a huge city with a population of more than one million inhabitants. It is conveniently located on the Volga River, being the largest economic, transport and scientific-educational center. Many new buildings are being built in this city. Among them is the Liverpool Residential Complex. Samara is growing and developing.





Location of the complex

The Oktyabrsky district, where the facility is being built, is one of the most prestigious in the metropolis. LCD Liverpool (Samara) is located right in the city center, on Nikolay Panov Street. Nearby is a large transport artery - the Moscow highway. All city transport is available to residents of the complex.

Since Samara is a city with a population of over a million inhabitants, it has a subway, near which Liverpool residential complex (Samara) is located. The developer deliberately chose a plot of land located in the immediate vicinity of the Rossiyskaya and Moskovskaya metro stations. The distance to them is less than a kilometer, that is, you can even get there on foot, and it will take no more than fifteen to twenty minutes.





Description of the residential complex "Liverpool"

LCD "Liverpool" (Samara) was conceived and designed by the developer as a residential complex of business class. In total, four multi-sectional residential buildings of different floors will be erected on the territory. The construction is made of bricks, which will allow residents to significantly save heat in the cold season and not freeze in the off-season.

Elite real estate in Samara, including new buildings, has a developed infrastructure. All the first floors of the complex will be non-residential. It will house offices, beauty salons, pharmacies and many other organizations that provide various services to the population.





One of the main advantages of the LCD is not only its location near the main transport artery of the city, but also that the view from the window will be beautiful: on the one hand - a landscaped courtyard area, on the other - the streets adjacent to the complex, surrounded by greenery.

Affordable Infrastructure

Thanks to such a good choice by the developer of the land plot, the Liverpool residential complex (Samara) is located near important objects: schools, lyceums, a gymnasium, kindergartens, a hypermarket and convenience stores. There is no shortage of polyclinics, or pools, or sports complexes. All the institutions without which the modern resident of the metropolis does not imagine its existence are accessible to future residents of the complex.

The Oktyabrsky district, where the Liverpool residential complex (Samara) is located, is located in the immediate vicinity of the Volga, where everything is buried in verdure. This residential complex was no exception. A hundred meters from it there is a botanical garden where it is good to stroll in the evening before going to bed or come to rest with the whole family. On the other side of the complex, a new recreation area called Peace Park has appeared, ready to hospitably welcome lovers of leisurely walks and fresh air into their arms.





Thus, despite the location in the center of the metropolis, Liverpool residential complex (Samara) can offer its residents a favorable ecological environment created by these parks and green spaces near the houses
