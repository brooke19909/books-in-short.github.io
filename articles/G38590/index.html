Meizu Pro 7: reviews, descriptions and specifications

Every year the number of new products among smartphones is growing. Choosing a birthday present for six months is not an option. In a matter of days, everything can change and a new flagship will appear on the market, which will conquer the audience. In the same way, it happened with Meizu Pro 7. The model has not yet had time to collect reviews, but you can make a general impression.

Market

If you were asked to describe the top-end Android phone of 2017, most likely you would not forget to mention the latest version Qualcomm Snapdragon, a high-resolution display, a dual camera and a metal case.

Last year we see the same type of models that appear in almost every price segment. Even budget models skillfully copied the flagship design, hid metal textures behind plastic and placed a double camera module in the cases.



Therefore, during the presentation of the Chinese smartphone, you do not expect something supernova, because everything that could have already been invented. About phone

Meizu has accustomed customers to annually launch at least one flagship per market. Sometimes there are several modifications, sometimes several top solutions. 2017 was no exception. On July 26, two smartphones were presented - Pro 7 / Pro 7 Plus.

Apparently, the Chinese manufacturer continues to dream of being able to stand in line with the now popular iPhone and Samsung. Hence the name of the models. Unlike the concept that market leaders have chosen, Meizu launches models that differ not only in display sizes.

Meizu Pro 7 Plus 64Gb reviews collected even more than its younger modification. This is due to the increased display, resolution. The inside of the smartphone has also changed. In the older model, an improved processor and graphics controller, more RAM. All this led to an increase in value by 15 thousand rubles on average.

The screen has an oleophobic coating, the fingers glide well, and the prints are easily erased. There are no greasy spots and dust on it. The multitouch responds quickly, without giving out phantom clicks.

"Filling"

Well, we must not forget about the most important thing. Despite its new feature, the phone remains a good device. This is evidenced by its technical component, and its cost. The younger version costs up to 35 thousand rubles, the price tag of the older one can reach 50 thousand rubles with a 128 GB drive.

Meizu Pro 7 64Gb received good reviews thanks to the Mediatek Helio P25 processor. The chip works on 8 cores with a frequency of 2600 MHz. Graphics provided by Mali-T880 MP2. RAM in the younger version of 4 GB, internal - up to 64 GB.

Interestingly, there is no space for a memory card in the SIM card tray. Therefore, you will have to be content with the 64 GB version. But in the older model, in addition to this amount of memory, there is a version with 128 GB.



Meizu Pro 7 Plus is powered by the Mediatek Helio X30. This is a ten-core processor with a frequency of 2.5 GHz. Responsible for the graphics is IMG PowerVR. RAM in this case is 6 GB.

If you can not decide which option to choose, the battery capacity will probably help you. In the younger model, it is 3000 mAh, in the older - 3500 mAh. Although taking into account the difference in screen sizes, then, most likely, the operating time will be the same.
