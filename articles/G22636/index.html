Interesting and elegant crafts from cones on New Year's Eve

Conventional pine, spruce, cedar cones are an excellent natural material used for the manufacture of various crafts. From them you can create all kinds of figures of animals and birds, Christmas decorations, decor items for the interior.

The master is only required to find in the forest a sufficient number of cones of various shapes, stock up on necessary supplies and start creating. Pleasant work will charge you with a good mood and positive. Children can also be attracted to the creation of original souvenirs made of natural materials ; they will surely enjoy such an interesting pastime. In this article we would like to share with you a master class on the topic "Crafts from cones". We hope you find our tips helpful!

Crafts from a cone: an elegant "Christmas tree" on New Year's Eve



In anticipation of the New Year holidays, we offer you to decorate your home with a beautiful and original Christmas tree made of cones. For its manufacture you will need the following materials:

  • bumps;
  • thick paper or cardboard;
  • spray paint gold or silver tint;
  • glue gun or Moment glue;
  • acorns.

Also, to decorate crafts from cones, you may need various decorative elements - bows, ribbons, tinsel, sparkles and so on. First, prepare the pine cones - paint them with a golden or silver spray paint and let them dry. Next, we will make a rather narrow cone of cardboard or thick paper, fastening it with glue. Now gently lubricate the bottoms of the cones "Moment" and attach them to the paper blank. Place the cones on the cone tightly to avoid the formation of ugly gaps. We leave our Christmas tree to dry, and for now, let's deal with acorns - they also need to be painted. Then glue them on the cone, covering the cracks. That's all, our New Year‚Äôs crafts made of natural materials are almost ready. It remains only to add a little gloss to it, decorating it with sparkles, tinsel, ribbons and other holiday paraphernalia. As you can see, making crafts from a bump is quite simple. Such a Christmas tree is sure to decorate your home and delight the household. You can even put gifts and sweets under it!

With our own hands we will make crafts from a cone: a gift basket

Another interesting, and most importantly useful, craft from natural materials is a basket. You can put souvenirs and sweets in it and present to your colleagues, friends and relatives. In order to make this product, you will need the following materials and tools:



  • fir cones;
  • varnish;
  • glue gun;
  • thin wire;
  • paint in a spray can of golden hue.

How to make a craft from cones - a basket? To begin with, dry the main material well, and then cover it with spray paint and varnish. After drying, gently apply glue to the scales and fasten the cones together so that a circle forms. This will be the bottom of the basket. Now cut several cones in half (across) and begin to attach them to the workpiece, inside with scales. Fasten the cones well by applying glue between the flakes. As a result, you should get a basket. Now you can proceed to the manufacture of pens. Take the wire and carefully pass it through the base of the cones. Decorate the handle as well as the bottom, and leave the craft to dry. Everything, the product is ready! Here we have got two original and elegant crafts from cones. The master class presented in this article is not complicated, and any master, even a beginner, can perform it. The main thing is to be careful, patient and create with pleasure!
