Where to go in Ulan-Ude with friends and a child

Do not know where you can go to Ulan-Ude? In our article you will find good ideas. This city has many historical monuments, museums, as well as various entertainment venues. In addition, there are parks where you can take a walk with your family and child, as well as with friends or loved ones.

Museums

Where to go to Ulan-Ude for those who want to learn something new? To the museum. There are a lot of them in the city. For example, visit the city‚Äôs history museum. It is located at: st. Lenin, 26. It was created in 1990. The museum has six permanent exhibits ("Through the Looking Glass", "Urban were", "Nostalgia" and others).





Where to go to Ulan-Ude yet? To the art museum. This is an interesting place in the city that is worth a visit. Located at: Kuibyshev street, 29. The museum opened in 1946, and to this day it works. Various exhibitions are held in it, but there are also permanent exhibitions. The museum presents samples of decorative art, silver jewelry with the inclusion of malachite, coral, and lapis lazuli.





Entertainment center for children Bary club

Where to go to Ulan-Ude with a child? In the Bary club. This is an entertainment center where kids have fun. Bary club also organizes festive events. All the kids in the center are greeted by cartoon characters. There is a cafe on the territory where not only children can relax, but also their parents. The center is located at: Klyuchevskaya street, 76a.

EuroLand

Another entertainment center where the child will have fun is EuroLand. Animators in costumes of their favorite cartoon characters also work here. The address of this institution: 13 Baltakhinova street.

Trampoline Center "In the Clouds" - an interesting place for children and their parents

This is a very interesting place, which is suitable for recreation not only for children but also for adults. In this center, everything is equipped with trampolines, there is also the so-called foam pit. This is a real highlight of the center. The whole pit is filled with foam cubes, the width of which is 15 cm. In the center, not only ordinary people can relax, but also professional athletes.





After all, there is a lot of space for training. Here you can do the most dangerous acrobatic elements and at the same time not be afraid to damage something. The center is located at: Sakhyanovoi street, 9/2.

Night clubs

Where to go to Ulan-Ude with a fun company? To the night club. There are several good places in the city where you can have fun. For example, the club "Once Upon a Time". There you can relax, meet new people. Where else can I go with my friends? At a nightclub Sterling or GoodZone.

Where can I eat? Good cafes and restaurants

Where to go to Ulan-Ude for those who want to eat?

In the cafe "Remix". Here you can eat at an affordable price. The restaurant serves European, Japanese and Russian dishes. There is a cafe at the address: st. North, d. 92/1.

Another cozy restaurant is located at 6 Klyuchevskaya Street. The institution is called Fusion. This restaurant is decorated in a loft style. The atmosphere here is incredibly comfortable, the staff is helpful. The cuisine is very tasty, the menu has salads, side dishes and meat dishes. Fine cocktails are also served here.

On Klyuchevskaya Street, at 41 there is a pizzeria called Dorio`s pizza.

It serves delicious pizza made from fresh ingredients. The atmosphere in the establishment is pleasant. It is also possible to order pizza at home or in the office.

"Aquarium" - fast food cafe. The institution is located at: st. Klyuchevskaya 74 (3rd floor). The hall in this cafe is very cozy, spacious and bright. Prices are affordable. Tourists will be comfortable here for a bite to eat.

Little conclusion

Now you know the answer to the question of where to go to Ulan-Ude. We examined good places that you can visit with children, with friends, or with family. We wish you a pleasant stay in the city!
