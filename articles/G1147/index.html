Pentax lenses: review of models and reviews about them

All cameras, from SLR to small ones, work with optics. She, in turn, is replaceable. Therefore, every photographer must understand how to choose the right lens for the job. Moreover, not all of them are universal and will suit absolutely any cameras. As a rule, you need to pay attention to the type of mount (mount). For example, lenses from the famous Kenon will not fit Nikon equipment. But on the market, you can easily find manufacturers who produce the same model of devices with different mounts. An example of this should be called Pentax. In this article, we will consider the most successful lenses and summarize the Pentax lens reviews made by professionals .



DA 10-17mm

The first will be described lens, which has a special enlightenment of the lenses. The model is equipped with a high-quality optical scheme, received a variable distance for focus. The lens is wide and its frame is made of metal. If desired, the sharpness can always be adjusted independently.

The zoom lens has a viewing angle of 180 ¬∞, which is a unique feature. Due to the fact that the depth of field is quite large, new opportunities for making discoveries appear for the photographer.

The SMC Pentax DA 10-17mm lens has received a special curvature for images, which classifies it as a fish-eye. Due to this, you can create pictures with unusual effects. Using this technology will allow you to change beyond recognition the aura of familiar objects.

A special coating is created on the lens, which is created from fluorides. For this, a unique spraying process is used. Thanks to this approach, the lens is completely protected from moisture and dirt. It is also easy to clean.

The lens itself is small and light. Optics is also created using technology that allows it to work with digital photographs.



DA 560mm

The lens is of good quality. It will allow the photographer to make clear and detailed images of even the most distant objects. The design is completely protected from dust and moisture, like many other Pentax lenses. Now it does not depend on weather conditions, it can be used both in rain and in severe frost. The lens has a special filter holder, which received a removable shape. Due to this, it is allowed to rotate the structure until the desired level of shade is reached.

The white color on the lens is designed to adjust the heating of the coating, which allows you to work at high temperature conditions.



SMC FA 645 33-55mm

This zoom lens is ultra wide angle. Its focal length reaches 3.3-5.5 cm, which can be considered a high rate. Thanks to the wide angle, it is allowed to use the lens as a device that focuses on the size and depth of the image. This will be useful when photographing landscapes. The special lens technology allows you to visually enlarge all the closest details and reduce those that are far away. The optics are small in size, like other Pentax lenses, and the images obtained with its help do not have chromatism. Thanks to the blend, the appearance of light and glare (in the event that the main light source is located on the side) is almost impossible. Users mark this as a big plus.



DFA 50mm

For this lens and the series in which it was created, a covered space is characteristic, equal to the field of SLR cameras by 3.5 cm. This allows the use of optics not only for digital devices, but also for other types of cameras. This is what distinguishes this Pentax lens. 50 mm is the focusing length.

Built-in quick switching system, which can also be called a focus function. It enables the photographer to switch to the automatic (or manual) shooting mode with just a slight twist of the corresponding ring. In order to facilitate the process, a focus lock was built in.

Due to the versatility of the mount, which allows you to work with both digital and SLR cameras, this lens has become widespread among professionals and beginners. Reviews about him in most cases are positive.

SMC FA 645 120mm

We continue to talk about Pentax lenses. FA 645 seems ideal for those who like to photograph in the style of catalogs, magazines. It's about macro photography. The lens has autofocus. The resulting image is clear and vibrant. This effect is achieved due to the fact that the optics received a high level of resolution and low aberrations. This combination is the most successful. Lenses have a good spraying, covered with a special solution that avoids the slightest deformation. They provide comfort when shooting. Moreover, as a result, the photos have no glare, and the color rendition remains at the highest level.

A lens hood is supplied with the lens kit, which protects the lens from light. Many other Pentax lenses have the same detail. Optics is also equipped with a special mode that allows you to quickly switch from manual focus correction to automatic. This allows the photographer not to be distracted by the search for the necessary settings, but to perform this manipulation immediately in the process of work. Moreover, reviews say that such a function is useful if a person takes pictures of an active object.



DA 18-270mm

The lens received a 15x zoom. Focusing with this optics is smooth and quiet. Also the item is compact and light. Professionals call the lens universal and most convenient. This is due to the fact that it is used in various fields for many types of filming, without changing the optics to another.

Due to the fact that the diaphragm is rounded, the pictures are soft, sharp, clear. In this case, the background is beautifully distant and slightly blurry, and near point light sources there is a minimal amount of blurriness. Judging by the reviews, not all Pentax lenses can offer such image quality.

Quiet and smooth autofocus is provided by a special motor, which was installed by the manufacturer. Users say that the system is reliable and works without any complaints.

Pentax DA 35mm

Although the lens is considered regular, it shows excellent photos as a result. It can be used for shooting in various places and angles. It is quite suitable for creating photos in everyday life.
