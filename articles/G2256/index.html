Waterpark is a special invention of mankind, pleasing both adults and children. This is an entertainment complex that has many water attractions - all kinds of slides, pools, fountains, spray and watering devices and much more.

As such, the water park was first built in the early 40s of the last century in the United States and has since become widespread around the world. Almost every country offers its residents and guests fun in this kind of entertainment centers, but they are especially popular in resorts. There is even an organization that regulates their activities and safety - the World Association of Water Parks.

Modern water parks occupy large areas and offer guests a wide range of entertainment - you can slide down the slides, splash, swim in the pool, visit the dolphinarium and an aquarium (they are quite common in water parks) or simply ride along a slowly flowing river on a mattress or inflatable circle.

Troy - water park in Turkey

This is one of the most famous and interesting parks. The place where the Troy water park is located is Belek, Turkey.

Wealthy tourists from all over the world come here. It is located in the fashionable Rixos Premium Belek, which is open to all visitors to the amusement park. Troy, a water park in Turkey, was built relatively recently - in 2005. Its area is relatively small - about 12 thousand square meters.

Design and Design

A distinctive feature that sets Troy apart from all other Turkish water parks is its design. The park is decorated in antique style, all slides and attractions are stylized as Trojans. This explains its name. Throughout the park there are scattered and laid out armor, shields, swords, buildings and structures that look like old Roman-Trojan fortifications. Photography is not forbidden - you can use the whole surroundings. Children love to be photographed with swords and in armor, a life-size chariot layout made with full respect for all the features of the original enjoys special attention.

Trojan horse

In the center of the water park is a model of a Trojan horse made in full size - its height is 25 meters. The owners claim that this design was used to create the film of the same name "Troy". Legend has it that during the Trojan War, the Greeks undertook a cunning maneuver - they brought a huge wooden horse to the inhabitants of the city, supposedly as an offering to the gods. In fact, the best warriors were hiding inside the building, who, after dark, got out and opened the gate. And Troy fell ...



The water park in Turkey uses the above-mentioned design not only to decorate and maintain the appropriate atmosphere - the horse is a ladder through which climb 5 water slides, 3 of which are open and 2 are indoor. Their length exceeds 100 meters.

Rides

Troy offers its visitors a wide variety of entertainment. For lovers of extreme sensations slides "Kamikaze" and "Phantom" are provided - they are designed in such a way that they have a fairly sharp slope - at an angle of 40 degrees. The speed when descending from such a mountain reaches 80 km / h.



Such entertainment will be remembered for a long time. By the way, there are practically no queues for this attraction. For children, special playgrounds are provided in the form of a ship with pools, and small slides create a good mood for the kids. All other designs can be divided into 2 types - open and closed. In the closed dark inside, the descent through the open is accompanied by spray. A total of 15 water slides for adults and 10 for children.


Another highlight of the park can be called the famous attraction "Master Bumbaster" - it is not provided for going down, but going up. Water hits a special gutter from the bottom into the bottom of the rubber ring, and it rises up to a height of 13 meters. All over the world there are only 2 such structures - one in the Troy water park, the second in the Emirates.

All slides are quite meandering and long. Descent to them takes place on special water circles, without them it is forbidden to ride. At the top of each attraction there is a special employee who strictly monitors compliance with this rule.

Security

One of the modern requirements for the entertainment industry is compliance with safety rules for vacationers. Troy, a water park in Turkey, is constantly working on this issue. All attractions are divided into adults, where children and adolescents are not allowed, teenagers (adults will not let them go there), and children.

Everywhere pay attention to the weight category. The staff of the park is following the order. On the territory there is always a doctor and rescuers. Admission is by tokens, they are issued only to adult visitors - you must ensure that the child is not lost. Water in different pools can be different - sea and fresh. Its quality and cleanliness is constantly monitored by a special control service.

Opening hours and cost

The price of visiting this attraction may vary depending on the place of purchase of the pass tickets - if you buy them from tour operators, the price will include delivery from the hotel to the destination. Entrance to the territory is paid. An adult ticket costs approximately $ 45. Tickets for the water park can be purchased at any resort hotel. The park is open from 10.00 to 19.00 local time. Payment for all additional services takes place using special magnetic cards, on which you need to deposit a certain amount in advance at the entrance. Up to 3 thousand tourists visit the Troy every day, so queuing for the most popular attractions and entertainment is a common thing.

Infrastructure

Water park "Troy" (Belek) offers guests not only slides and pools, but also other entertainment and events. There is a dolphinarium on the territory where you can watch shows in which dolphins and seals are involved. For a fee, you can buy as a souvenir a picture painted by a dolphin during the performance.

You can climb the observation tower - its height is slightly higher than 13 meters, from there an excellent view of the whole park and attractions opens. Several catering points operate - cafes and restaurants (lunch is included in the ticket price, but you can take only one drink and one dish, you have to pay for everything else). At the entrance, they check things and bags - it is forbidden to bring alcoholic drinks and lemonade to the park, you can only bring a bottle of water. In the evenings, various shows, discos and shows are held, which last until the morning. Throughout the day, visitors are offered various entertainment events, and for children - a separate entertainment program. There is a gift shop where you can buy all sorts of little things for memory or order a mug, a T-shirt with your own image.
