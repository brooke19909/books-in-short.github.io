Guest House "Dunes Dzhemete", Anapa: description, services, reviews

The most important and important thing that Anapa visitors can first of all offer is rest. Prices for all services depend on the chosen institution, so most prefer to stay not in the city itself, but on its outskirts, which is much cheaper and more convenient in terms of location near the sea.

Those who like to relax in Anapa or near this city have already heard about Dzhemete. The history of the village is not so long: it was created as a military fortification in 1828. Over the past almost two hundred years, the settlement has not really grown - about 1000 people live here. But in the season, the local population increases 4-5 times, or even more. The reason for this is the convenient location of the village, because it is located almost on the seashore: from the most remote street you can reach the beach in just 8-10 minutes.

But the best thing, of course, is to live right by the sea in order to be able to enjoy sea sunsets in the evenings, and in the mornings, having overcome only a few steps to the water's edge, immediately plunge into the waves giving coolness. That is why, if not all, then very many visitors settle in those hotels or private houses that are built right on the beach. One of these places is the Dzhemete Dunes Guest House. The hotel is housed in a modern seven-story building equipped with an elevator. There are noisy establishments nearby, which guarantees a relaxing night's rest.

About hotel

Since the city where the hotel is located is Anapa, which is in great demand among many, rest, prices, services and other aspects of the establishment are of interest to everyone who is going to come here. Therefore, all this and much more will be discussed later.

Those who will live on the fourth floor and above have the opportunity to enjoy the beautiful view of the sea. But if you climb even higher - right on the roof, where there is an open terrace of a cozy restaurant, you can enjoy the taste of amazing dishes and a beautiful panorama of the coast under the breath of a sea breeze. In such an environment it is convenient to hold a business or romantic meeting, sit with friends or family.

On the well-groomed territory there is a gazebo and benches for relaxation. If you want to stretch out after a sleep or before lunch, you can play table tennis in a specially equipped place. Nearby are a supermarket, food market, dolphinarium.

Families with children are not forgotten either: in the courtyard of the hotel there is a children's playground with swings and slides, and there is a swimming pool with a children's section. By the way, the pool is equipped with heating, so you can swim in it even on those days when it is cool outside. In addition, animation is organized three times a month for children, where professional presenters with competent speech conduct provocative games. Moreover, everything is organized in such an interesting and exciting way that usually not only children, but also adults are delighted with the event.

Hotel infrastructure and service



Hotel "Guest House" Dunes Dzhemete "" provides for its guests a range of services aimed at improving the standard of living and relaxation. At any time, each of the guests can use the laundry room with a dryer, a swimming pool, iron clothes, rent a hairdryer, a kettle, a bicycle, other sports equipment (for darts, badminton, table tennis), visit the library, which is open in the hotel building. In addition, at the front desk, which works round the clock, you can order a taxi, arrange rental of a conference room for up to 25 people, and arrange a festive event at the banquet hall.

The institution has its own parking, equipped with video surveillance. For one car you need to pay 75 rubles per day.

An important detail: smoking in the hotel in the corridors, rooms, on balconies is not allowed. For this, special places have been arranged, so the administration calls on all residents to comply with all that is usually prescribed by the rules of hotel accommodation, including with regard to smoking.

Additional cost information



Everyone who chooses the Dunes Dzhemete guest house can stay with children of any age. For children under three years of age, a place is provided absolutely free of charge, but for their meals it will be necessary to pay extra. If it is necessary to provide the child with a separate crib, the institution will arrange its delivery, but you must inform about this even during the booking of the hotel. Cost per day - 200 rubles., Free bonus to a crib - use of a high chair.

The hotel also offers discounts for children. So, if one of the main places is intended for a child under 13 years old, his parents will pay daily only 80% of the total cost of the place. If it is additional, the price for accommodation will be lower by 40%. If children are over fourteen years old, then only 60% of the amount that is paid for the principal in a specific room will need to be paid for an extra bed.

If the client decided to live in a double room alone, he must pay 20% of the cost of the second place. For reservations, a prepayment of the total amount of 10% is charged.

Transfer from the airport / bus station to the guest house "Dunes Dzhemete" - paid. The cost of one car, designed for four:

  1. 350 rubles - Anapa railway station.
  2. 500 rubles - Anapa airport or bus station.
  3. 1000 rubles - Art. Tunnel

If other trips are foreseen, their cost depends on the mileage. All movements must be agreed in advance.
