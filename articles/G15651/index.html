Turkey, Marmaris: climate, attractions, leisure

For tourists from Russia, Turkey is a favorite vacation spot. Marmaris is one of the best resort cities in this country. It is located on the shore of the bay, where the Aegean Sea merges with the Mediterranean. Let's take a closer look at this resort.



Turkey (Marmaris): climate and weather

The resort town is located at the foot of the picturesque green mountains, which allow you to hold on to air humidity of about 35%. It is filled with the smells of pines and the sea, and the heat is much easier to carry, it seems not so exhausting. Due to this, the climate is quite mild, and the air remains fresh. Even mountain air is saturated with vapors of essential oil, which is very good for health. In the summer months, the weather here is rarely rainy. The air temperature reaches a maximum of 35 degrees heat, and the water temperature is 22 degrees. Not only the hot summer attracts tourists to Marmaris, but also the warm winter.



Turkey (Marmaris): Attractions

It is a charming resort. A large number of historical monuments are located in it and beyond. In the center of Marmaris itself lies the huge fortress of Marmara Kalesi. It was built since the time of the Ottoman Empire. In the halls there are souvenir shops - this is a favorite place for all tourists. From the fortress you will have an amazing view of the whole city and its environs. Not far from Marmaris is the amazing Pamukkale. This is a natural monument, which was created without human intervention. To the north of Marmaris is Cleopatra Island. This is a paradise, an unusually beautiful place. The island is wrapped in azure waters and golden sand. There is an ideal atmosphere of calm and tranquility. Excavations of the ancient city ‚Äã‚Äãof Ephesus are ongoing near Marmaris . Turkey (Marmaris): where to relax



This is a great place for tourists who love water activities and sports: diving, surfing, banana riding and much more. You can also go on boat trips on yachts along the azure bays. Landscapes of tremendous beauty will open before your eyes. In addition to water trips, you can enjoy ground-based entertainment, such as safaris, rafting. Marmaris is a modern and youth resort with many nightclubs, restaurants, boutiques, bars, supermarkets, bazaars, souvenir shops. The Turkish bath deserves special attention. The complex of its services includes peeling, sauna, foam and oil massages. Indulge yourself, do not miss this opportunity.

Marmaris (Turkey): prices



Trips to Turkey are very popular in travel agencies in the summer and even in winter. You can purchase tours with any food system. It all depends on the capabilities and preferences. Tourists who live in hotels without meals can visit bars, restaurants, of which there are a huge number. For example, the cost of a cold dish is from five lira, hot - from ten lira, a bottle of water will cost two lira. It is worth noting that the prices for trips in the winter are three times cheaper compared to the summer.

Pick up a ticket to this wonderful resort, which is located at the junction of two seas. Holidays in Turkey (Marmaris) - for those who want to spend their holidays in an atmosphere of fun and surrounded by beautiful nature. This is a resort for active and young people.
