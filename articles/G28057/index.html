Holiday Village "Galdym": description, address and reviews

The recreation center "Galdym" is located in a picturesque corner of the Tambov region on the banks of the Tsna River. It is located in the Galdym forestry, 56 km from the city of Tambov. This is one of the most beautiful places in the region with flood meadows, vast forest territory, streams running along the lowland, and small lakes. Here you can spend a weekend or vacation, relax away from the bustle of the city in private with nature.

Description of the recreation center

Near the hostel flows the river Tsna. There is a well-maintained sandy beach with sun loungers, umbrellas and showers. A rescue station is operating. For lovers of outdoor activities on the river bank there is a football field and a volleyball court. In summer, water rides work, vacationers can ride catamarans, boats or kayaks under the guidance of an instructor.





On the territory of the recreation center "Galdym" are located:

  • houses for living;
  • gazebos with barbecue facilities;
  • Restaurant "Tatyana's Day";
  • conference hall;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • ski base;
  • gym;
  • medical office.

The base can accommodate 86 people. It is better to come here to relax in a large company, as the houses are designed for several people.





Number of rooms

Vacationers are accommodated in houses. All rooms for vacationers have the necessary furniture, TV, refrigerator, bathroom, wireless Internet, telephone, which can be used to contact the staff. The recreation center "Galdym" in Tambov has a diverse number of rooms for winter and summer holidays:

  1. There is a summer townhouse for two people. This is a good place for a family vacation.
  2. Summer house for 5-6 people.
  3. Winter house for 3-4 people.
  4. Summer house "luxury" for 7 people. In addition to all the listed amenities, there is a veranda and a balcony. The price includes breakfast.
  5. Winter house "luxury" for 3-4 people.
  6. Winter house category VIP. There is an additional bathroom, air conditioning and a sauna with shower (use for an additional fee). The house can accommodate 6 people.




Cost of rest

The cost of rest depends on the category of the house and the number of residents. The price of a ticket is from 2600 rubles for two people in a town house, up to 10 000 rubles for 6 people in a winter house of VIP category. Three meals a day are paid separately.

The recreation center "Galdym" belongs to Tambov State University named after G. R. Derzhavin. You can find out the cost of a permit for employees and students of this educational institution on the website of the holiday home.





Entertainment

On the basis of all the conditions for outdoor activities. Lovers of water activities on the beach can rent boats, boats and kayaks. Right on the water in the summer, attractions are arranged that will interest both children and adults. You can go fishing on the river Tsne, on the base all the necessary gear are hired. Lovers of outdoor recreation can organize a picnic in one of the gazebos with barbecue facilities.





Near the base there is a rope park where children and adults can engage in extreme sports with the help of experienced instructors. In addition, water and hiking trails with obstacles are organized.

Fans of a healthy lifestyle can do fitness in the gym or warm up in the sauna. And those who prefer intellectual sports have the opportunity to rent checkers or chess.

On the summer terrace, you can hold a solemn event in the fresh air. The base also has a winter veranda with an oven, where you can relax after a ski trip.

Employees of various companies often come to the recreation center in Tambov for corporate events. Experienced presenters organize interesting and fun games and contests. After the solemn part, a fascinating excursion to the Mammoth deserts is conducted with a visit to the temple, the Holy Lake and the source.

Base Address

The address of the recreation center "Galdym": Tambov region, Tambov district, the village of Gorelsky leskhoz, Galdym forestry, quarter 7.

It is difficult to get from Tambov to the recreation center by public transport. It is most convenient to go by car. The road will take about 1 hour 15 minutes. We need to leave Tambov on the highway leading to Morshansk. Then, before reaching the border of the Sosnovsky district, make a right turn to the village of Troitskaya Dubrava. Next, follow the signs to the base "Galdym".

Many vacationers come here for a few days to celebrate family holidays and birthdays, there is everything you need to conduct a celebration in nature. A lot of people visit this camp site regularly and constantly spend weekends and summer holidays here.
