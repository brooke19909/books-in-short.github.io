Tunis hotels: overview, description, rating

The most famous resort of Tunisia is Hammamet. Here are luxury four- and five-star hotels with modern relaxation and spa centers. Sousse is a large city, famous for its noisy nightlife and a huge number of entertainment clubs, restaurants and casinos. Djerba island is suitable for a relaxing holiday. On the island are the best hotels of Tunisia for children.

There are not many five-star hotels in the country that offer good service and have their own beaches. But then the choice of 4 star Tunis hotels is very wide, they are not much different from the "fives", and the cost of living in them is much lower.

A popular destination is rest in boutique hotels with a homely atmosphere and delicious food. These small hotels do not have a huge territory, thalassotherapy or huge pools. But it is in such hotels in Tunisia that you can understand the authentic atmosphere and immerse yourself in Tunisian traditions.

Services in health centers are always paid, and the all-inclusive system is slightly different from Turkey or Egypt. Often this concept includes food, the use of sunbeds and umbrellas, animation and children's clubs.

A brief overview of Tunisia hotels will allow you to understand the services provided, the location and choice of a resort city or village.

"Jazz Tour Kalef 5 *"

The five-star Jazz Tour Kalef Hotel is located near Sousse, Tunisia's oldest city and popular tourist center. It is famous for its amazing beaches and turquoise sea. The city has many attractions: from the Grand Mosque to mosaic museums and catacombs. The hotel features a 4,700 square meter spa and thalassotherapy center. For vacationers, there are two large outdoor pools and one indoor. There are six water slides in the large pool. Several restaurants and bars offer European and Tunisian dishes.

"Djerba Holiday Beach 4 *"

Located on the seafront in Midoun. Houmt Souk is 11 kilometers from the hotel, and Fort Bordj El Kebir is 10 kilometers away.

The hotel has its own equipped beach, you can have dinner or lunch in one of the three restaurants on site.

Rooms feature balconies with sea, courtyard and garden views.

Hotel services:

  • Reception work round-the-clock.
  • Free internet access.
  • Daily cleaning.
  • Child care services.

The hotel offers excursions to the crocodile farm, the art museum, the ceramic workshop, the Berber village and the location of the famous movie Star Wars.

"El Kantaoui Center 4 *"

The hotel offers an exquisite blend of modern Tunisian style, comfort and coziness. The staff speaks different languages, and a sumptuous breakfast is served each morning.

El Kantaoui Center is built in a great location, just a few steps from the beach and close to nearby attractions, including Porta El Kantaoui, golf courses, Phrygia Park. Monastir International Airport is 23 kilometers from the hotel.

The resort offers a wide range of services and amenities:

  • concierge services;
  • 24-hour reception
  • tour agency;
  • free Internet;
  • babysitting services;
  • laundry;
  • cleaning;
  • use of the pool, restaurants, spa and fitness center.

The cost of a room per day is from 1600 rubles.

"Vinci Helios Beach 4 *"

The main feature of the hotel is that it is located within walking distance of the sea. Hotel Vinci is located on the west side of the island of Djerba, in the south of Tunisia. The hotel has a large outdoor pool, a large terrace and a beach bar.

Each is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, a balcony or terrace. Free internet is available in public areas.

Guests at Vinci Hotel can enjoy Mediterranean cuisine at the hotel's restaurant. Guests can relax with a drink in the bar, which also offers light snacks.

"Rio Imperial Marhab 5 *"

The hotel is located just 7 kilometers from Sousse. It offers an outdoor pool and spa. The hotel has its own beach and water sports facilities. Tourists can enjoy drinks at the bar. The hotel offers free private parking.

Reception is open 24 hours a day. You can play table tennis or golf at the property, and car hire is also available. The nearest airport is Enfida Hammamet, just 14 kilometers from the hotel.

In the ratings of hotels in Tunisia, "Imperial" is firmly in the top ten.

A hotel room is quite expensive - from 12,000 rubles per day.

Odyssey Resort Thalasso

The hotel is located in a unique location near the sea. The deluxe and standard rooms have balconies overlooking the sea beach, garden or pool.

There are several restaurants on the territory:

  • Tunisian restaurant (open seasonally).
  • Poolside pizzeria (during the day).
  • Italian restaurant (seasonal).
  • Beach fish restaurant (seasonal).

The spa offers thalassotherapy treatments. Guests can enjoy a seawater pool, massages or old Tunisian treatments.

During the day, guests have access to a freshwater pool, an indoor pool, a hot thermal pool, a mini club, two tennis courts, volleyball and minigolf courts.

The cost of living starts at 10,000 rubles.

"Movenpick Hotel Tunisia"

The elegant boutique hotel is a unique place for guests who value excellence. This hotel is located in an exclusive area and overlooks the beautiful bays of the Mediterranean Sea and the Sidi Bou Said Hills.

The hotel is easily accessible by car from the airport. La Mars and Carthage are within walking distance. Two Tunisian restaurants with sea views and a Moroccan restaurant invite you to taste delicious dishes. Guests can relax at the spa and work out at the fitness club.

Hotel Features:

  • Private sandy beach.
  • Rooms with sea view.
  • Three conference rooms.
  • Indoor and outdoor pool.
  • Three restaurants and a lobby lounge.
  • Terraces overlooking Gammarth Bay.

The hotel has 119 elegantly furnished rooms. Of these, 102 are executive rooms, two presidential suites, five embassy suites, two junior suites and six deluxe rooms. The hotel has two rooms for disabled guests and twenty seven for non-smokers.

All rooms and suites have a private terrace, air conditioning, telephone, minibar, tea and coffee making facilities, international satellite channels and internet access.

Tours in boutique hotels in Tunisia are becoming increasingly popular, although accommodation in them is not cheap - from 30,000 rubles.

"La Villa Blue 4 *"

From the "Villa" offers a magnificent view of the turquoise sea and the islands of Zembra and Zembretta.

Numbers:

  • Junior Deluxe with a balcony overlooking the Sidi Bou Said marina.
  • Junior Suite - Luxuriously furnished apartment with modern seating areas. Each room has a private balcony or terrace with views of the pool and the Mediterranean Sea.
  • Suite - These rooms have direct access to the terrace.
  • Standard - a room near the pool, equipped with modern furniture and a bathroom with ceramic tiles.

The restaurant serves gourmet dishes, while the lounge serves a varied selection of hot and cold drinks, as well as homemade cakes.

A hotel room costs from 10,000 rubles.

"La Kasba 5 *"

La Kasba Hotel is located in the center of Tunisia in Kairouan. The hotel has comfortable rooms and suites with an elegant decor, equipped with all amenities, including air conditioning and satellite TV. Internet access is available for a fee.

There are two restaurants on site serving Tunisian and European cuisine.

Hotel services:

  • Outdoor terrace.
  • Pool.
  • Turkish sauna.
  • Food and drink buffet.
  • Concierge service.
  • Dry cleaning.
  • Non smoking rooms.
