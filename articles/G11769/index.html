Bulgaria. Pomorie. Reviews and Description

Every year, Bulgaria is becoming more and more attractive for tourists. Pomorie, traveler reviews which characterize this resort as a quite worthy place of rest, has long been known for its unique healing mud. It is located on the shore of a rocky peninsula, 18 km from Burgas.

Mud therapy

For those who want to not only relax, but also improve their health, Bulgaria may be an ideal option. Pomorie, reviews of which characterize this resort as a magnificent health resort, is surrounded by two salt lakes, between which there are numerous medical sanatoriums. Healing mud contains a huge amount of useful substances that have a beneficial effect on the human body. It is used in the treatment of diseases of the nervous system, respiratory tract, locomotive apparatus, gynecological and skin diseases. That is why for many tourists, Bulgaria is the best place for rest and recreation. Pomorie, reviews of which indicate the amazing healing natural capabilities of this region, is famous all over the world for therapeutic SPA procedures. sights

Tourists who choose Pomorie (Bulgaria) for their vacation, reviews of which are left by travelers, will be interested to visit the salt museum, the monastery of St. George and the Church of the Transfiguration. The resort often hosts poetry evenings, concerts and theater performances. Especially popular with visiting tourists is the architectural reserve called "Old Roads", which is a residential building of the 19th century. You can also go windsurfing, fishing or parasailing. Daily excursions are organized for tourists to the Pomeranian wine cellar, where you can taste a variety of wines.
