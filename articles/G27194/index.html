Attica Hotel Vityazevo: overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

Do you want to enjoy the luxury of Greece without leaving Russia? Then be sure to visit the Attica Hotel (Vityazevo). This stunning leisure complex combines many components of this relaxation. Here you will find hospitable and friendly staff, wonderful new rooms, comfortable lounge areas, architecture, stylized in Greek and incomparable atmosphere. This interesting hotel, settled in a resort village near Anapa, has a rather high rating among tourists who come to relax on the Black Sea coast.



Village Vityazevo

Attica Hotel is located in the resort town, which is located eleven kilometers from the famous Anapa. It is also called "Little Greece." Once there was an ancient colony. After all, almost all the inhabitants of the village have Greek roots. Therefore, in the architecture and design of many modern, especially private hotels, you can notice the features connecting the new comfort with ancient culture. The mountains in this area are far from the village, and it is surrounded not by forests, but by vineyards. This further enhances the impression of a Mediterranean country. All entertainment is located on the waterfront or main boulevard. And if you want a rich nightlife, then Anapa is suitable for you. Attica Hotel (Vityazevo) is a good starting point for a trip to the main resort. There you will find a rich excursion program and trips to local natural attractions. The village also boasts its own Greek-style water park, dolphinarium and the Byzantium entertainment palace.



Where is the hotel located

Attica Hotel (Vityazevo) is located in the very center of the village. This is the crossroads of Znoi and Mira streets. At the same time, only five minutes to go to the beach from it. This area is quiet, despite the fact that it is central, and quite calm. Near the hotel you will find a bus stop and minibuses that can go to the center of Anapa. Nearby is the famous Paralia Boulevard, often called the promenade, which stretches towards the sea and ends on the Central Beach.

Territory and infrastructure

Attica Hotel (Vityazevo) consists of several four-story buildings, built in a style reminiscent of ancient Greece. Everywhere you are surrounded by snow-white columns, copies of famous ancient sculptures. It is as if they are telling you that it is worth forgetting about household chores and thinking only about the pleasant and the beautiful. The entrance to the hotel is shaped like an antique portico. The territory is protected, so you can be calm for yourself and your belongings. The fence is high, and each guest has their own key to the common gate.



Rooms

This leisure complex offers guests several types of rooms. The most budget of them are on the attic floor. It can accommodate three guests. These rooms have excellent new furniture with an elegant and at the same time minimal design, a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, a refrigerator and satellite TV (powered by a card). The rooms are air-conditioned according to the split system principle. And if you want to drink something hot, an electric kettle is at your service. The furnished balconies in these rooms are simply huge. The room catches wifi. Quadruple room is good for large families or companies. There is a large entrance hall with a wardrobe. In addition to the electric kettle, the set also includes a set of dishes. Approximately the same content in double and triple rooms. And for discerning guests, the hotel has prepared a surprise. This is a spacious two-room suite. There is a living room (relaxation area) with a sofa and an armchair. From the balcony of the room you can see the sea. This is how Attica Hotel (Vityazevo) places tourists. Accommodation prices start from one thousand four hundred rubles for a double room per day and up to 2800 for a suite. But this is the cost of settlement without food in the low season. Children under two years of age live for free, and from 2 to 10 - for 400 rubles per day.



Services

The hotel administration is ready to meet you at the station or at Anapa airport and deliver it directly to your room. At the same time, they do not take any money for this service. Transfer is carried out by hotel minibus. It is interesting that the railway station and Anapa airport are closer to Vityazevo than to the main resort. There is an iron and ironing board on each floor of the hotel. If you want to wash things, for some money you will be allowed to use the machine. The rooms are cleaned daily and linen is changed twice a week. And how many interesting entertainments are there for little guests! A whole children's town, where there are slides, swings, and a special house for games. The coating there is soft, and even if the child falls, it will not hurt. The rooms are crib. In the dining room, you can use the high chair to make it convenient to feed the child. In rainy times or in the heat, kids can have fun in the games room, where there is everything you need. And for walking with babies, you can rent a stroller. Near the pool there are even children's beds.



Nutrition

The hotel has a large dining room, which can accommodate about a hundred people at a time. The menu here is custom-made, and you can ask to cook not only dishes of local or European cuisine. If you follow a diet, are a vegetarian or do not perceive any products, just tell the staff about it, and all your wishes will be taken into account. If you wish, you can eat in the hotel's dining room once or twice or order a full meal. All products are fresh, undergo special quality control. The portions are large, the dishes are all made with a soul and professionally. Delicious sweets, especially cakes. Children really like it. And on the territory there is a bar located on a beautifully designed observation deck. There you can order pizza or Ossetian pies, drink coffee, enjoy a cocktail or savor a dessert while looking at the endless distance of the sea. There is also a nice pool bar.



Water recreation

The hotel itself has two whole pools. They are heated and also decorated in the same style as the entire complex. An artificial waterfall, a jacuzzi, a safe compartment for bathing babies - this is not all that awaits you if you do not want to go to the sea, but decide to sunbathe on the territory of the Hotel Attica (Vityazevo) complex. But, as befits a Greek resort, the hotel advises its guests thalassotherapy. After all, the ancient sages and healers spoke about the treatment by the sea, especially upset nerves. To get to the coveted beach, you need to walk along the street with trade tents, where you can buy something if necessary. The beach is sandy, with a gentle, child-friendly entrance. Indeed, this is not the open sea, but the Vityazevsky estuary. The water is very warm, and you can swim in it already in May. Across the wide, fifty-five-meter beach - dunes of white sand. True, in the season there can be a lot of algae. And in the estuary there are healing muds that heal skin and eye diseases, indigestion and musculoskeletal system, and many others.

Reviews for the Attica Hotel (Vityazevo)

Tourists believe that this hotel is comfortable, at home, luxurious, as abroad, and at the same time, you can fully feel the local flavor. They are delighted with the refined design of the hotel. The staff and all employees are responsible, polite. Huge chic balconies, protected by a special mesh, so that there is no danger to children. The hotel infrastructure is very practical and thoughtful. Unlike large boarding houses, where everything is standardized, vacationers here fell into family comfort. Tourists write that they felt here as close friends. The hotel is remembered for a long time and leaves a desire to come here again. Therefore, if you decide to rest in Vityazevo, the Attica Hotel is a very good choice.
