Where to do CTP: a review of insurance companies, conditions for registration

MTPL is a policy acquired by a driver in order to protect against material losses associated with compensation for damage that is caused to property and health of third parties. In simple words, thanks to this type of insurance, the culprit of an accident will not pay for car repairs and treatment of people injured in an accident.

Currently, the question of where to make OSAGO is almost a leader. This happens because all motorists want reliability from their insurers, transparency of their conditions and simplicity in the process of processing all documents.

The process and conditions for obtaining insurance in 2018

The basic order in the process of applying for a policy consists of several points. If you stick to them, then you can save a lot on the policy. Initially, a person should decide where to make OSAGO insurance. Then all the documents are collected in one folder, you can go to the office for delivery.

As part of the CTP, you need to know that insurers are also interested in selling additional services to customers. Therefore, it is always necessary to carefully check what is given for signature. After carrying out all the above manipulations on the hands of the car owner should have the following papers:

  • Personal documents.
  • The presence of the original policy, on which the customer and insurer are signed, along with the seal of the insurance company and a special state sign, of the established form.
  • An attachment of a set of OSAGO rules that you must read.
  • Two copies for the possibility of notification of an accident. In the event that there are not enough of them for the insurance period, you can contact the company where the CTP was issued and ask for more.
  • The presence of a memo.
  • Receipt of a receipt of payment, which is issued to the insurer.

Electronic policy

Many people wonder where it is better to make an electronic insurance policy.

Does a motorcycle need insurance?

According to domestic law, insurance is subject to absolutely any type of transport that is capable of moving at a speed of 20 kilometers per hour or more. The law also applies to ATVs and mopeds. Motorcycle insurance is a must. It is worth remembering that the absence of a motor third party liability insurance policy will cost eight hundred rubles. And leaving the road without insurance threatens a fine of five hundred rubles. Registration of this policy is necessary for motorcycle drivers in order to be able to protect their motor civil liability.

In other words, reimbursement based on insurance is only possible in cases where the motorcycle driver acts as a provocateur of the accident. Against this background, payments are not made to restore the bike itself, but directly to cover damage to the injured party. Given the fact that motorcycles are considered the most dangerous participants in the movement of all road vehicles, the acquisition of OSAGO allows drivers to protect themselves from additional financial risks in the event of an accident. Motorcycle owners may insure liability against the following risks:

  • From damage resulting from an accident.
  • Against theft and theft.
  • As an opportunity for insurance against natural disasters.
  • From accidents.
  • Protection against unlawful actions of third parties.

What else should you know about CTP insurance?

Before buying a policy, a person should be aware that not only the official institutions of insurance companies are engaged in the sale of such policies, but also brokers with fraudsters. The last two have positive and negative sides. Brokers give the client the opportunity to choose which company the person wants to purchase the policy from, but in this situation, they act as intermediaries, which means that a person will have to give a little more of their money to purchase insurance.

It is definitely difficult to answer the question of where it will be cheaper to purchase OSAGO insurance for your car with round-the-clock delivery, since there are a lot of similar companies and they replace each other daily, letting new companies in their place. In any case, it will cost the client more than the usual purchase of car insurance in a standard organization.

Fraud

Now for the scammers. Among the so-called brokers, they are also found, which means that you can get caught without much difficulty. They are invited customers to buy insurance with a ready-made seal, at retail or in bulk. And everything would be fine, but such forms may turn out to be false, although they can be true, however, the information after the conclusion of the agreement will not go to a single PCA database, and it follows that in case of an accident, no payments will be made to the person.
