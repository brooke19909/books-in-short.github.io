Excursions in Sanya: the most popular routes

Hainan is an amazingly beautiful island in the South China Sea. Tens of thousands of tourists visit Hainan all year round. Beautiful nature, mild and warm climate, magnificent architecture - this is what attracts lovers of relaxation here. One of the most popular places for travelers on the island is the city of Sanya. Excursions in these places are quite diverse and will satisfy even the most demanding taste.

Description

Sanya is the main tourist center of Hainan Island. The infrastructure of the city is developed at the highest level. The ecological situation is in perfect condition, and special bodies are following this. An interesting fact, but during the reign of various imperial dynasties of the Celestial Empire, officials who fell out of favor were exiled here.

Everyone will like Sanya, because a wide range of resort services is presented here. Sandy beaches attract absolutely all vacationers. Diving enthusiasts will surely enjoy the rich world of waters that wash the island. Those who came not only to relax, but also to learn as much as possible about these amazing places, will be offered a variety of excursions in Sanya.

Binlang Village

It is best to start exploring the island from the ethnic village of Binlang. One of Sanya's proposed excursions is a trip to the center of the island, to the place where the ethnic roots of the islanders are preserved. Travel time will be a little more than half an hour, however, upon arrival, you will be convinced that it was not in vain spent. A detailed review will be devoted to the village with the Miao and Li peoples, their life and customs.

For tourists, a special show program is organized, consisting of songs, musical works and ethnic dances. There is also a large assortment of souvenirs, products for various household purposes and a variety of food products made by local craftsmen and culinary specialists. If desired, all this can be purchased for a relatively small price.

Tropical Paradise - Yalongwan

On the slopes of the hills near Yalongwan Bay, Tropical Paradise Natural Park is located. Its total area is 15 km¬≤, it should be noted that this is the first national park of the island. A variety of tropical plants and trees grow on its territory, and the world of fauna is also widely represented. The park has created special tourist paths that harmoniously combine with the surrounding landscape.

In the park there is a suspended Dragon Bridge, which the locals nicknamed the Bridge of Lovers. Its length is about 170 m, and it stretches between two mountain peaks. One of the attractions is a dragon statue made of bronze in a traditional Chinese art style.

It is worth noting that several beautiful pagodas and a chic hotel were erected here, which offers its guests not only comfortable rooms, several restaurants and cafes, but also unusual scenic views.

Nanshan

One of the excursions in Sanya will offer tourists to go to Nanshan. This is a huge center of Buddhism, the construction of which was completed in 1998. It was built in honor of the 2000th anniversary of Buddhism in China. The complex has several buildings, but the most important thing is located on the top of the hill. You can get there only by a very steep staircase. At the same time, the ascent to the main temple is arranged in such a way that you can reach it only bypassing other buildings.

One of the main attractions of the cultural complex is a statue of a goddess named Guanin (Mercy). Its height is an impressive 108 m, and it is made in snow-white color. During national holidays, tens of thousands of Buddhists come to the cultural temple complex to worship their deities.

A trip to Nanshan is one of the best excursions in Sanya, as here you can not only plunge into the world of unusual Chinese temple architecture, the history of Buddhism in China, but also enjoy the picturesque views of the complex.

Thermal springs

Of the large number of excursions in Sanya offered by agencies, one is especially popular - these are thermal springs. In addition to being a comfortable vacation, this is a whole unique system for healing a number of diseases. In the sanatoriums created near the springs, with the help of thermal waters and various procedures, they treat diseases of the respiratory tract, gastrointestinal tract, musculoskeletal system and skin.

In Sanya, there are a lot of institutions with thermal springs, which will help you choose the right one for the prices. It should be noted that they are quite democratic here.

Mineral water baths with various concentrations of nutrients and nutrients, as well as water treatments with the addition of wine and coffee, are very popular among lovers of healthy relaxation. A novelty for visitors will be taking a bath with the collection of Chinese herbs and rice vodka. Such procedures favorably affect the skin, blood circulation, and also have a tonic and tonic effect.

In addition to water and aroma procedures, they offer a variety of massage techniques, from relaxing to therapeutic.

Prices for excursions

The cost of excursions in Sanya can not be called low, but it pays off with a wide palette of impressions received during the trip. On average, the price of an excursion to a natural tropical park or reserve will cost $ 50, and, for example, a trip to the Nanshan Temple Cultural Complex is already $ 75 ($ 1 is about 66 rubles).

Prices for various types of excursions range from 35 to 160 dollars. The highest prices are for mini-cruises with dinner in the dark. Also, excursions to neighboring small islands for diving or snorkeling from $ 60 to $ 90 will not be cheap. Snorkeling is the observation of underwater flora and fauna using a snorkel without a scuba diving and deep diving. This type of vacation has recently gained a large number of fans in these places.

Which excursions to visit in Sanya (China), everyone will choose for himself, taking into account their own preferences. However, it should be noted that this amazing place, you can find relaxation and entertainment for every taste and budget.
