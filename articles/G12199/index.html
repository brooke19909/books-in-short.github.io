Who will get a loan?

Consumer loans in Samara can be obtained by any citizens who have reached the age of majority and have a constant source of income. This source may be wages (in this case, the borrower must work at the current place for at least three calendar months) or a pension.

With all this, it is desirable to have a positive credit history, and at the time of obtaining a loan there should be no open arrears on loans from other banks. If the borrower falls under all these conditions, then he can easily get a cash loan in Samara.
