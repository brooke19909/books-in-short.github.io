Amigurumi dog: diagram and description. How to tie an amigurumi dog

Amigurumi is the art of crocheting toys. This kind of creativity was born in Japan. Initially, these were small-sized toys: amigurumi dogs, cats, cubs, bunnies, and so on. Later, the craftswomen began to knit products of medium and large sizes. Now this art is spread all over the world. If you wish, you can find thousands of different descriptions and patterns of amigurumi dogs, dolls, and even clothing for them and interior items. Knitters also like to create toy food: cakes, ice creams, fruits and vegetables, french fries and more.

Knitting toys can also make good money. Many craftswomen knit products to order. Knitted toys look very unusual and original, so many get them. Anyone, even a beginner knitter, can try his hand in this matter.

Almost everyone would like to have their own dog, if not real, then at least a toy. We offer some schemes and descriptions of amigurumi dogs.

Abbreviations

For convenience, we will use the following abbreviations:

  • stbn - single crochet;
  • pc - increase;
  • uk - decrease;
  • vzp - an air loop.

Dalmatian: Description

After the release of the world-famous film "101 Dalmatians" many of these beautiful animals fell in love. Let's try to connect such an amigurumi dog in the master class presented below.

To do this, you need yarn in white and black. Great for knitting Dalmatian "Pecherka": "Children's novelty" is a rather thin yarn - for it you will need a hook measuring 1.5-1.7 millimeters.

Scissors and a needle with a large eye are also needed in order to connect all the details after knitting the amigurumi dog. For the eyes you will need beads and floss threads to sew them on.

If you use the eyes on the mount, then the thread floss will not be needed.

Do not forget also about holofiber - this is one of the best fillers for toys: it does not cause allergies and does not deteriorate when washing.

And now more about how to tie an amigurumi dog.

  • (4, yk) x 6 (30);

Torso

Crocheting the body of a dog with an amigurumi crochet is finished. Leave the thread for sewing on the head.

We pass to the front legs. First, use the black thread:

  • 6 short bars;
  • 6 arr. (12);
  • (stbn, pc) x 6 (18);
  • 18 short bars;
  • 7 stbn; 3 double crochet in one loop; 10 stbn (21);
  • 7 stbn; decrease from 3 columns; 10 stbn (18);
  • 18 short bars;
  • 4 stbn, yk, 4, yk, 4, yk (15);
  • 3 stbn, yk, 3, yk, 3, yk (12).

Now the white thread:

  • 23 rows in a row: 12.

We start to knit the tail:

  • 6 base columns in a "magic" ring;
  • 3 rows in a row: 6;
  • stbn, pc, 1, pc, 1, pc (9);
  • 12 rows in a row: 9.

And only spots remain. It is very easy to knit them. We make an amigurumi ring, knit six single crochet into it. Next, based on the desired spot size, we knit the increase. For a small speck, you can stop on two rows (the second of which includes six increases). For the big spot, we continue to make increases: the third row - (stbn, pb) six times; the fourth - (two stbn, pb) six times and so on.

Well, knitting an amigurumi dog according to the scheme is finished. Now we pass to assembly. Details need to be sewn to each other carefully. Before doing this, be sure to tightly fill the parts with holofiber.

Keychain in the form of a dog: description

We tied a bigger dog, a smaller dog, and now we will tie the smallest one, which easily fits in the palm of your hand and even in your pocket.

For knitting you will need yarn in beige and brown.

Head

We use beige thread for knitting:

  • 6 pillar without nak. in the ring of amigurumi;
  • 6 pc (12);
  • (stbzn, pc) six times (18);
  • (2 stbzn, pc) as many times (24);
  • (3, pc) x 6 (30);
  • (4, pc) six repetitions (36);
  • 7 rows in a row: 36;
  • (4 stbzn, yk) six times (30);
  • (3, yk) as many repetitions (24);
  • (2, yk) as many (18);
  • (1, yk) as many (12).

The face is also knitted from beige yarn:

  • 6 pillar without nak. in amiguru ring;
  • 3 stbzn in one loop, 2, 3 st in one loop, 2 (10);
  • 3 pc, 2 stbzn, 3 pc, 2 (16);
  • 2 rows in a row: 16.

Ears - for them we will choose brown threads:

  • 6 pillar without nak. in the ring of amigurumi;
  • 6 pc (12);
  • 8 rows in a row: 12 single crochet;
  • 4 stbzn, yk, 4, yk (10).

Torso

Used beige thread:

  • 6 pillar without nak. in a circle;
  • 6 pc (12);
  • (stbzn, pc) six times (18);
  • 7 rows in a row: 18;
  • (stbzn, yk) x 6 (12).

We also knit back legs from beige yarn:

  • 6 base columns in an amigurumi ring;
  • 2 stbzn, pc, 2, pc (8);
  • 4 rows in a row: 8.

The front legs are the same color as the rear:

  • 7 pillar without nak. in a ringlet;
  • 5 rows in a row: 7.

Tail:

  • 6 short columns in a "magic" ring;
  • 2 stbzn, pc, 2, pc (8);
  • 8 base posts.

Knitting came to an end. Now you need to connect all the details, carefully stitching them together. Sew the muzzle to the head and embroider the nose on it with brown yarn. The eyes are located on two sides of the muzzle. Ears should not be sewn directly on the top of the head, otherwise they will seem too short, it is better to place them a little lower, on the sides. Front and hind legs are equally attached to the body.

That's all, the product is ready! A pendant in the shape of a dog will perfectly decorate your keychain or handbag. We wish you inspiration and pleasant knitting!
