How to make a swan from a ball in 5 minutes?

As you know, one of the favorite types of toys for children of all ages is balloons. Therefore, it is precisely on children's matinees that the invited heroes show the children tricks with them, making balls of funny animals and birds. After such a holiday, many children want to repeat such an exciting lesson, so they ask their parents to lay down some kind of animal. Today we will tell you how to make a swan from a ball no worse than a professional actor of a children's holiday would do.

Materials for creating a swan figure

In order to make any figure out of balls, you will need a few long and thin balls, called "sausages." Depending on the wishes and the chosen hero, determine what color they should be. Also, a black marker and some decorations may be needed if you plan to turn a balloon into a full-fledged toy or an element of interior decoration.

Balloon twisting masters advise to avoid over-stretching the rubber by inflating it. Since when twisting, a strongly inflated ball will simply burst. To make a swan out of a ball, we recommend using white.

Later on, you will draw eyes and feathers on it. On the colored ball, the parts depicted will be lost and will not look so impressive.

How to make a figure?

After you have prepared some white balls and a marker, you can get down to business.

Step one: inflate the ball so that it can curl freely in either direction. Also leave the tip of the ball loose (about 3-4 cm).
