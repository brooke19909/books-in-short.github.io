What happens if the loan is not paid at all? Causes, consequences

Card and benefits

The first and most accurate, as well as safe, method is the use of special credit cards with so-called soft loans. In such a situation, a certain delay will be given for debt repayment. On average, it is 35-55 days. If the citizen does not close the debt, interest will begin to accrue.

In principle, this technique can be called "took a loan and do not pay." After all, until some point, until your debt becomes huge, no consequences will have to wait. Just then pay what you have to and that's it. Or there will come some not quite pleasant consequences. About them - a little later.

Appeal

More recently, Russians have come up with a way to not pay a loan. True, like any other method, it has its limits. If you have a small debt, and the bank has not yet begun to bother you, you can simply contact its branch with an appropriate application for a delay.



At the same time, any money will still have to be paid to the bank every month. And some citizens are not happy with this. They need to figure out what to do so that they do not have to pay the bank a loan and interest on it at all. Such techniques are available. But they, firstly, do not give a 100% guarantee, and secondly, they are not always legal.

Limitation period

Some debtors say: "I do not pay a loan and I live in peace." And unaware citizens are interested in how this result was achieved. The thing is that there is one very interesting trick. Most likely, it will be possible to bring it to life without any problems. But you have to try.

Loan debtors often use the so-called limitation period. With regard to loans and debts, it lasts 3 years. What does it mean? During this period of time, there should not be any contact between you and the borrowing bank. That is, you need to literally evaporate for 36 months from the moment the first delay occurs.

Not on myself

There are different ways to not pay a loan. Another approach to solving the problem is more suitable for people who are ready to set up their loved ones. Bankruptcy

In Russia, the newest, but already very popular trick is the method of tax evasion through ... bankruptcy. Individuals have the full right, according to new laws, to declare themselves bankrupt. At the same time, no one will collect debts from them. So, you can use this technique to free yourself from obligations.

In principle, recognition of bankruptcy by an individual is an analogue of recognition of its insolvency. Practice shows that many people use this feature. Moreover, with success. You can, for example, declare yourself bankrupt. If you are not forgiven for debt, just hide in some way from banks for 3 years. And wait until the limitation period for the loan passes. This idea only seems to be impenetrable. If you try, you can really succeed.
