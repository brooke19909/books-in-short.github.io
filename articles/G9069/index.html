Huawei U8860 Honor Android Smartphone Review: Specifications and Reviews

Equipment

Before looking at the device, take a look at the kit that comes with it. Let's face it, the Chinese have always cared about their customers in terms of the availability of accessories. Navigation

Under the display of the Huawei U8860 Honor there are physical control buttons. There are four of them (whereas, by tradition, there are three on all Android smartphones). Unusual is the search button. In addition to these, there are other gadget controls, in particular the power button of the device (on the upper edge of the device), as well as the swing to change the sound volume of the model.





In all other respects, the device is not much different from other similar smartphones. At the bottom there is a connector for the charger, above the screen you can see the eye of the proximity sensor and the front camera; On the rear panel are the main camera and flash. Screen

The model Huawei Honor U8860 (the characteristics confirm this information) 4-inch display that runs on TFT-LCD technology. This means that the phone is able to display a lot of colors (about 16 million), be moderately bright and colorful, but it loses saturation when the screen is tilted. So, if the viewing angle changes, you can see the full clarification of the picture. In this regard, of course, the device loses to competitors with an LCD display.

The screen resolution is 480 by 854 pixels, due to which the graininess is almost invisible here. The model supports up to 2 simultaneous taps.

For additional protection of the display, the Huawei U8860 Honor Black model is equipped with a special layer of glass that sticks on top of the screen. It resists a significant part of household scratches, chips and other damage that may occur during everyday use of the phone. However, you should not rely on him. As the reviews show, even the protective glass can be damaged. The best way to protect the model remains the film on the screen.





Autonomy

In 2011, when the model was introduced, a question arose for manufacturers of Android phones regarding the duration of their devices. Users even then noticed the low autonomy of many devices on this operating system.

Saying anything specific about the Huawei Honor U8860 is difficult. This model does not have official technical specifications regarding the duration of the work. At the level of private reviews, it was found that in continuous conversation mode the device can work for about 7 hours, while listening to music - 25, and video - about 8 hours.

The indicators are quite decent. With them, the device could work in normal mode for two days. In principle, even active users of the Huawei Honor U8860, Android fans, should be enough.

Communication

Traditionally, the device supports several communication formats. As a rule, they are typical. And the Huawei Honor 3 U8860 (the smartphone we describe) is no exception.
