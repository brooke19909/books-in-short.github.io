Royal Panacea 4 * (Turkey / Bodrum): photos, prices, description and reviews of tourists

If you plan to spend your summer vacation in Gumbet (Turkey), then as an optimal accommodation option, many experienced travelers recommend choosing 4 * hotels. Royal Panacea Hotel, in turn, is an excellent representative of this category. With what awaits guests here, and we will understand in more detail.

Where is

The four-star Royal Panasea Hotel is located in the very center of the resort town of Gumbet, 600 meters from the beach. So all local entertainment for guests will be within walking distance. The distance to the major tourist center of the Republic of Turkey - Bodrum - is only 3 kilometers. So you can go there at least every day. In the vicinity of the hotel there are a lot of travel agencies, rental cars, shops, cafes, supermarkets, etc. The nearest airport to Gumbet is in Milas, 45 kilometers away. Also, some tourists arrive here through the air harbors of Bodrum (60 kilometers) and Dalaman (185 kilometers).





Bodrum Royal Panacea 4 *: photo and description

This hotel used to be called Guler Resort. In the summer of 2012, its owner changed, and then the name. The housing stock of the hotel includes 137 comfortable rooms. All are in small, cozy bungalows. Royal Panacea 4 * has apartments of the following categories: standard (18-20 sq. Meters) and family (35-40 sq. Meters, two rooms). All of them are equipped with their own bathroom, air conditioning, TV, minibar, safe. There is access to wireless Internet. Some rooms have balconies.

Dining at the hotel is carried out on an all-inclusive basis. On the territory of Royal Panacea 4 * there is a restaurant, a bar, a snack bar, a swimming pool, a children's playground, a sun terrace, a hammam, a sauna and a massage room.

Price policy

As for the cost of living in this hotel, then, according to most tourists, it is more than acceptable. So, our compatriots tours here cost an average of 15 thousand rubles per person for a seven-day accommodation (the price includes a flight).

Impressions of tourists about the location of the hotel, its territory and housing stock

According to our compatriots vacationing in Royal Panacea 4 *, this hotel has a very good location. It is located in the very center of Gumbet. So all the entertainments of this resort town can be reached in just five minutes. The hotel itself is located on a hill. Therefore, in order to get into your room, you will have to climb dozens of steps. This can be a problem for older people and family tourists who come on vacation with young children. The very territory of the hotel is landscaped and well-groomed.

Hotel Royal Panacea 4 * pleased its guests with excellent rooms in cozy bungalows. Almost all apartments have beautiful views of the bay. Comments on the nutrition and staff of the Royal Panasea

The choice of dishes in Royal Panacea 4 * (Bodrum, Turkey), according to the guests, of course, is not as great as in five-star hotels. However, it is impossible to stay hungry here. The menu for lunch and dinner always has soups, meat dishes, various snacks, side dishes, pastries and fruits. Some guests also mention delicious spinach pizza in their reviews . As for breakfast, then there was something to eat: sausages, cheeses, pastries, juices, jams, etc.

According to traveler reviews, the hotel staff is pleasant, polite, smiling. The service here is very fast. Many guests are happy to note the fact that the waiters change the tablecloth after each visitor in the restaurant.





Impression of a beach holiday and entertainment

Royal Panacea 4 * does not have its own beach. However, in Gumbet, almost all the beaches are municipal, for the summer they are rented by local cafes and bars. The distance from the hotel to the coast is about half a kilometer, so, according to our compatriots, you can get here in a few minutes by an unhurried step. If you want to stay on sun loungers, then you just need to buy a drink or a snack in the cafe, which "looks out" for your favorite beach area.

There are no special entertainments in Royal Panasea. Animation is not performed here. Only once a week passes the Turkish night. You can enjoy your time by the pool on the sun terrace. By the way, according to the guests, there are a lot of sun loungers here, so there is no lack of them even at the height of the season. A hammam, sauna and massage treatments are also on site. All these services are not included in the price and are available at an additional cost.

In addition, many guests spend time going on excursions to various attractions, of which there are many.
