Hotel "Meliot", Chelyabinsk: address, description, reviews and photos

Hotel "Meliot" in Chelyabinsk is one of the places to relax in which you can have a great time while in the city. As practice shows, for temporary residence it is often chosen by couples in love, tourists and even businessmen. Reviews about the hotel often say that there are conditions even for families with children, which allows you to spend time with your family in the place in question.

General information

Hotel "Meliot" (Chelyabinsk) is a place for temporary residence, the main feature of which is that it has a large spa center, attracting the attention of both its guests and ordinary residents of the city.

The hotel has an excellent infrastructure, which is designed for the most demanding guests who have big needs. Guests of the place in question often leave a lot of positive comments about it, which speak of excellent conditions for relaxation, as well as first-class service at the hotel. Based on the comments left by them, Meliot has a rather high rating - 9.3 points out of 10 available (according to the version of the world-famous Booking portal).

The hotel currently has 4 * status - it was assigned to it in May 2017.

Location

Attractions nearby

Many tourists, who are ardent fans of exploring the most interesting places in new cities, often note in their comments that the Meliot Hotel is within walking distance of many attractions. Not far from it is the famous ice rental "Tractor", several theaters, museums, as well as the City Garden named after A.S. Pushkin.

Many recommendations left for everyone to visit the hotel with children say that there is a large zoo near Meliot, after visiting which the child will have only pleasant impressions.

Rooms

Hotel "Meliot" in Chelyabinsk (pictured in the article) is a place for temporary residence, which is presented in the form of a large 14-story building with modern finishes. Its number of rooms consists of 98 apartments, divided into several categories of comfort. It can accommodate up to 200 people at a time, each of which will be provided with appropriate comfort conditions.

All apartments, which are presented in the hotel, are divided into categories providing for accommodation in standard, improved conditions and in deluxe rooms. Regardless of the type of apartment, they all have well-equipped bathrooms in their structure, which include plumbing, modern furniture, as well as a full set of bath accessories, the number of which is designed for the estimated number of guests.

Reviews of tourists about the Meliot Hotel (Chelyabinsk) often state that, staying in the rooms of this place for a short stay, you can enjoy the beautiful interior made in the classical style, as well as high-quality service. Moreover, according to them, any type of apartment has the necessary furniture and technical equipment that can make living the most convenient.

Standard rooms

The standard class rooms presented in the fund of the Meliot Hotel (Chelyabinsk) are in the greatest demand. This is due to the fact that this class of apartments has all the conditions that are necessary for a comfortable stay, but they are provided for a relatively small fee - from 2,600 rubles per day (for a double room).

Hotel Meliot offers standard-type apartments with both single and double beds, and there is also a room with two separate beds. In addition to the berth, there is a working area, as well as furniture designed to store guests' clothes.

Reviews of standard-type apartments often say that they offer stunning views of the birch grove located near the hotel.

All standard apartments are located on the second floor of the hotel, access to which is available only to residents - thanks to this, the most comfortable accommodation for guests is ensured.

Superior rooms

Spa Hotel "Meliot" (Chelyabinsk) offers a large selection of apartments, which provide improved living conditions for guests. In the reviews of the guests they are described as quite bright and spacious areas (36 m 2 ) with a beautiful view of the urban surroundings.

The apartments of this type have a large double bed (in some it is replaced by two separate single beds), a working area, a sitting area, represented by a sofa and a coffee table, a bedside table, and a wardrobe. In their free time, guests of the Superior Room can watch their favorite shows on the plasma TV in front of the bed.

All superior apartments have a private balcony with excellent city views.

The cost of apartments of the improved type is 4000 rubles per day - most vacationers find it quite acceptable.

Luxury rooms

Apartments of the increased degree of luxury comfort at the Meliot Hotel (Chelyabinsk) are presented in various variations: spa suite, spa senior, spa panorama. Moreover, the hotel has large-sized rooms such as apartments, two-room suites and superior rooms. A feature of all the listed rooms is that they are all located on the upper floors of the hotel - this allows guests to enjoy panoramic views of the city surroundings.

The main feature of the rooms assigned to the categories with the prefix "spa" is that in their living room there is a large font with a jacuzzi. The second room room is equipped with a comfortable bedroom, designed for two guests - a king-size bed equipped with a heating system for the mattress is mandatory here, as well as a place designed to store clothes. Also in the bedroom there is a large plasma TV.

Guests of the rooms of the category providing extra comfort can use Wi-Fi access.

All executive rooms have an expensive design made using natural mahogany finishes, as well as high-class textiles. In addition, high-quality furniture is installed here, which allows guests to stay comfortably. In the rooms of the superior class apartments, as well as in the two-room suite, as well as in the spa, there is a large living room, but instead of a bathtub with a jacuzzi, it has a furniture set designed for relaxing and receiving guests.

The daily cost of living in spa-class apartments is from 6,700 to 8,600 rubles per day, and in those that belong to representative groups, from 4,800 to 5,700 rubles.

Nutrition

The hotel "Meliot" (Chelyabinsk) has a huge restaurant, which is loved by not only the guests of the place in question, but also the residents of the city - "King Gustav". This institution captivates its visitors not only with the amazing taste of the served dishes, but also with the interior design.

In addition to the restaurant "King Gustav", in the building of the hotel in question there are other catering establishments, which are also loved by the residents of the city. These include: Meliot Brewery, Audrey Small Coffee House, and an outdoor terrace where you can drink coffee and tea.

For all those who want to organize a big celebration, many citizens recommend contacting Meliot - the hotel has a large banquet hall "Master", as well as a huge banquet hall, equipped with everything that is necessary to organize a bright and unforgettable celebration.

Spa

Hotel "Meliot" (Chelyabinsk) offers the opportunity not only to relax, but also to visit the spa, which is located on its territory. It should be noted that guests staying in apartments of any type, presented in the hotel room stock, can visit the spa area for free and enjoy all the procedures.

The Hotel Meliot (Chelyabinsk) has individual spa areas, as well as a shared one. As for the general, it consists of four saunas, as well as hot tubs, a rain shower and a fresh bar, which offers an excellent selection of natural juice, smoothies, and other healthy drinks. Speaking about individual zones, it should be noted that each of them is presented within a separate platform, designed as a relaxation room, designed for 12 people. All members of the company resting here have the right to visit saunas and other facilities that are part of the general spa area.

Gym

The reviews about the Meliot Hotel (Chelyabinsk) often say that there is a stunning gym in its building, which guests can use for free, and for everyone else, classes are held for a fee.

Modern German-made simulators are installed in the hall, which are distinguished by their manufacturability and the correct construction of biomechanics. Moreover, professional instructors are always working in the hall, who are always ready to help in their operation, as well as in conducting comprehensive classes.

Hotel Service

The reviews about the Hotel Meliot (Chelyabinsk), left by guests, say that in this place each guest is provided with high-quality service.

First of all, it should be noted that the hotel provides reliable visa support to all foreign tourists. For those arriving by car, a free parking space is provided in a large guarded parking lot.

The Meliot Hotel has a laundry service on a regular basis, as well as a tour desk, where you can buy a tour of local attractions and interesting places at any time.

The hotel building has a large children's room, the services of which need those tourists who stopped at the hotel with the whole family. An interesting show program for a child can be organized in it, or babysitting services can be ordered.
