Home Credit Bank: customer reviews of loans, interest rate, repayment and installment plan

To make a long-awaited purchase, it is not at all necessary to save several years for it, denying everything to yourself. Nowadays, buying things you like is much easier thanks to loans. Customers of Home Credit Bank in their reviews point out favorable conditions for granting loans, often call this financial institution the most reliable and give arguments in favor of working with it.

A few words about the company

The first office of Home Credit Bank was opened in 2002 in Nizhny Novgorod. The company quickly enough entrenched in the financial market of the country, a couple of years later joining the twenty largest banking organizations in the country. According to customer reviews, Home Credit Bank provided loans to buyers of chain stores such as Eldorado, Technosila, M. Video.

Three years later, the financial company managed to enter the market of car loans and housing mortgages, but the 2008 crisis prevented development in this direction. All large-scale lending programs had to be closed. Since then, the bank has been focusing on:

  • Retail banking products.
  • Deposits
  • Salary projects.
  • The development of Internet and SMS banking.

The Russians consider this financial organization one of the most loyal to potential borrowers, as they write in their reviews. The license to Home Credit Bank, which occupies the top ranks of ratings by the volume of individuals' deposits, was issued by the Central Bank of the Russian Federation in March 2012.

Conditions for employees

Opinions about the company are often shared by consultants and company managers. In general, people are happy with their work. Credit experts in reviews of Home Credit Bank report that professional growth is possible here. Commercial banking organization has been repeatedly recognized as one of the model employers. According to the management, such a high place in the list is the result of continuous scrupulous work within the company, customer focus and the desire to create comfortable working conditions for employees.

A clear confirmation that the bank takes care of its subordinates is the opening of a kindergarten in Obninsk. This representative office is the largest. The average age of the staff is 26 years. Among the young employees there are many mothers with young children from one and a half years. Since the issue of placing children in kindergartens in Obninsk is acute, the management decided to create all the conditions so that mothers have the opportunity to work.

Each client has its own reasons for contacting this organization. However, it is impossible to deny the fact that obtaining a loan at Home Credit Bank (according to customer reviews) is often much easier than at any other company. The Bank is known for the lack of strict requirements in relation to individuals applying for a loan. Here they quickly consider applications from new borrowers and offer unusually favorable conditions to regular customers. In addition, you can receive the required amount in cash at Home Credit Bank on the day of treatment. Many citizens mention this in reviews.

For most customers, an important role is played by the simple procedure for obtaining a loan, the absence of the need to seek guarantors and provide property as collateral. This is especially convenient when money needs to be received in a short time.

Other customers are attracted by Home Credit Bank by the opportunity to choose their own method of debt repayment. The company annually launches new programs, offers interesting and advantageous offers.

Borrower Requirements

Unlike other banking companies, Home Credit Bank lends money without attracting guarantors and pledging property. Convenient to borrowers and a concise list of documents that must be presented to a bank employee:

  • Passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation.
  • Certificate of insurance personal account (SNILS).

In the absence of SNILS, you can provide another document (of your choice): passport, driver's or pension certificate. Citizens from 22 to 64 years old can apply for a loan at Home Credit Bank, and 64 is the age limit that a borrower will reach at the time of debt repayment.

A prerequisite for the issuance of a loan is a permanent job. The client must be officially employed for the past three months or more. According to customer reviews, Home Credit Bank does not issue a cash loan to citizens who do not have a permanent residence permit or temporary registration in the region of circulation.

To make a final decision on a loan, bank employees check the credit history of the client, which is contained in the NBCH. Depending on the status of a citizen, the amount of the loan and the interest rate are determined. In the case of a damaged credit history (for example, non-repayment, delay in loans at other companies), it is unlikely that you will be able to get a consumer loan from Home Credit Bank. In reviews, some citizens also mention this.

POS-loan: how to apply at Home Credit Bank

This is the most popular type of consumer loans that are allocated to customers to pay for goods and services. In partner stores, representatives of the bank are present who are involved in the issue of obtaining a commodity loan.

The partners of Home Credit Bank are hundreds of retail chains, each of which has a separate tariff plan. Here are just a few branded stores that collaborate with this financial institution:

  • Ascona.
  • The Messenger.
  • "El Dorado".
  • "M Video".
  • Adamas.
  • "Elena Furs."
  • "The Snow Queen".

Home Credit Bank offers customers to issue not only a standard loan, which implies the calculation of interest on the use of credit funds, but also the so-called interest-free installments.

You can apply for a POS loan when purchasing any product in the store. As for installments, the assortment covered by the right to interest-free purchase is always limited to the distribution network. Thus, the store determines in advance which goods can be purchased in installments and which are not. This service can be of two types. Let's consider each of them.

The first option is actually the same POS loan with a fixed interest rate in the agreement. The monthly payments include interest on the loan. To provide the buyer with the cherished 0% per annum, the store itself makes a discount on goods that it sells in installments. Consequently, the bank levels the discount due to accrued interest. It seems that the client pays only the cost of his purchase, but in fact, goods with a discount on credit are made on credit.

If the store does not want to make a discount, then how will the bank lend to the buyer? There is only one option left - the design of a regular POS loan with interest.

The second option involves the provision of installments directly by the trade organization. In this case, the bank is not involved, the contract is concluded between the seller and the buyer, additional fees and interest are not charged.

How to get a loan

Making a consumer loan does not take much time and does not require a huge package of documents. The bank reviews applications, usually within one business day.

First, you need to fill out a simple form on the website of Home Credit Bank. In St. Petersburg (according to customer reviews) the decision is made in a couple of hours. In order to increase the likelihood of approving a loan application, in addition to standard data (surname, first name, middle name, date of birth, series and passport number), you should indicate your mobile phone number, email address, confirm your consent to the processing of data and sending a request to the National Bureau of Credit Histories .

After waiting for a positive decision, you can begin to select the optimal loan conditions. When a suitable program is selected, the client will be assigned a day of admission at the nearest bank branch to arrange a loan and issue funds.

It is important to indicate reliable information when filling out the application and ensure that the information entered fully coincides with the passport. Having discovered a discrepancy, a bank specialist has every right to refuse to grant a loan.

Payment Methods

There are many ways to make monthly payments to pay off a loan. Home Credit Bank applies an annuity debt payment scheme. The entire amount is repaid in equal installments, but at the same time, the client first pays mainly interest on the use of credit funds, and then the main debt.

Unlike other banking organizations, Home Credit Bank provides for the possibility of changing the date of making monthly payments. This is specified in the contract. This opportunity is most often used by those who are forced to pay off several loans at once.

You can deposit funds to pay off debt:

  • In your account on the official website.
  • Directly at the cash desk of any branch of Home Credit Bank. In Moscow (according to customer reviews) this method of payment is not popular, despite the complete lack of commission when paying.
  • Using ATMs Cash-in by specifying the details of the financial institution and the contract number.
  • Through electronic payment systems.
  • By postal order.

In addition, you can deposit funds for a loan at the terminal or cash desk of any other Russian bank, but in this case you will have to pay a fee for using its services.

Is it possible to pay the bank ahead of schedule

Repaying a loan ahead of time specified in the contract is the right of every citizen, enshrined in law. Not a single bank, including Home Credit, has the right to refuse the debtor to pay the balance ahead of schedule, but at the same time, a loan agreement may provide for various nuances that also need to be taken into account.

The client has the right to partially or in full to fulfill his obligations ahead of schedule to a credit institution. There are a lot of reviews about early repayment of a loan at Home Credit Bank. In none of them do users write about difficulties that have arisen.

The differences between partial or final payment of debt are not so significant, but a little attention should be paid to them. Regardless of whether the borrower covers all of the debt with his installment, or only part of it, he is obliged to notify the lender of the upcoming payment no later than 30 days in advance. We are not talking about monthly payments. For early repayment, you must write a statement in the form of a bank and make the necessary amount. When the date of write-off comes, the funds will be transferred to repay the principal.

If the borrower does not have enough money to fully cover the debt ahead of schedule, he has the right to write an application for payment of the debt indicating the amount in excess of the size of the monthly loan payment. After the funds are credited to the personal account, the bank employees will be forced to recalculate the amount of debt and, in agreement with the borrower, reduce the amount of the fixed payment or shorten the loan term.

Installment card: main advantages and conditions

Today, this banking product is very popular. If you believe the reviews, an installment card from Home Credit Bank is much more convenient and profitable than a regular credit card, because it allows you to solve financial issues in an instant and without overpayments.

The annual interest rate on the installment card is zero, but only if the main condition is observed - to pay the debt on time. The scheme of applying for an installment card from Home Credit Bank (according to customer reviews) is practically no different from the procedure for obtaining a loan. An application for this product can be sent online or contact one of the bank branches.

The size of the financial limit is set individually for each client and is in the range of 10-300 thousand rubles. The size of the loan depends on the income of the citizen, his place of residence, age, availability of real estate and other conditions. The period of validity of interest-free preferences is not limited until the client violates the installment conditions. In the reviews of Home Credit Bank, people note that even with a one-time delay or replenishment of the card, the interest in the amount of 29.8% per annum is not automatically accrued.

In their reviews, customers note that the main drawback of an installment card is the inability to withdraw cash. It will not be possible to withdraw money from an ATM from it, but you can purchase goods through it in any stores equipped with a terminal for electronic payments. In addition, electronic wallets cannot be replenished with an installment card. This banking product is not involved in bonus accumulation programs and cashback services.

However, there are many reviews of installments from Home Credit Bank, in which users write that they were satisfied with this service and advise it to be issued by others. The advantages of such a card include:

  • Simple and understandable conditions.
  • Minimum time and documents for registration.
  • Long-term grace period.
  • No need to pay for card service.

If you follow the rules of use and pay off the debt on the card in a timely manner, this will positively affect your credit history and increase the borrower's chances of obtaining a larger loan, as the need arises.

Types of deposits, interest rates

Bank deposits are a simple and effective way to increase your free finances. Only individuals can make a profit on deposits at Home Credit Bank. To put money at a profitable interest can not only citizens of the Russian Federation, but also foreigners.

According to customer reviews, deposits at Home Credit Bank generate income on the same principle as in other financial institutions. A person deposits a certain amount into his depositary account and does not use it for the period approved by the contract. Having provided money for temporary use of the bank, the client can count on receiving a stable profit from financial transactions.

Home Credit Bank has advantageous offers on deposit processing. The rate for each client is calculated individually and depends on the amount of the deposit and its validity period. The company offers a wide selection of deposit programs that allow individuals to receive a certain income. Interest is paid monthly or at the end of the deposit term, with or without capitalization. The conditions depend on the specific program.

Everyone who applies to Home Credit Bank has the opportunity to choose a deposit that best suits their personal interests. An application for opening a deposit can be submitted on the official website. Today the company offers the following types of deposit programs:
