How to knit a bolero for a girl with knitting needles: job description, patterns, models

Bolero is not only beautiful, but also a very convenient thing. She must settle in the wardrobe of every fashionista. This garment is a short blouse. It is usually worn over blouses or dresses. She gives the beautiful person charm, romance, sophistication, tenderness. There are a huge number of models and options. Therefore, each young lady will be able to find one that suits her. In the material presented below, we will talk about how to knit a bolero for a girl. Although, if desired, you can do a similar thing for an adult.

Choose yarn

Before you start knitting, you need to carefully consider your product. For example, for beginners it is best to make a simple option. Buy knitting needles

To choose a good tool, craftsmen advise to be guided by the yarn prepared for the performance of the bolero. For knitting patterned products, knitting needles equal to the thickness of the thread are needed. If you want to decorate the idea with the now fashionable effect of elongated loops, you should choose those that are one and a half or more times larger in diameter. The material from which the spokes are made can be selected based on your own taste. But if a beginner takes on the matter, it is wiser to purchase metal knitting needles. A bolero for a girl, connected with such tools, will turn out to be better and more beautiful. In addition, the work can be done much faster, because the metal provides a good glide of the thread.

Measuring Model

To perform the next step, you need to prepare a centimeter tape, a schematic image of the intended thing and a pencil. Then we call the girl for whom we will knit a bolero. She should be wearing a tight blouse or turtleneck. After all, a bolero for a girl made with knitting needles will be worn on top of something, and not on a naked body. Having completed the preparatory phase, we proceed to take measurements. We need the following parameters:

  • estimated length of the product;
  • sleeves length;
  • distance from the lower edge to the armpit;
  • chest girth.

Model No. 1: we knit an elegant bolero with a front smooth surface

To implement the idea presented in the photo, no diagrams are required. According to the description, a bolero for a girl with knitting needles is very simple:

  1. We draw on the needles the number of loops equal to the circumference of the chest.
  2. We knit, moving back and forth.
  3. To separate the edging, we knit several rows, alternating one front and back loop in each row.
  4. Then we knit the main part of the product with front stitch, and edging - trim in the same way.
  5. We reach the level of the armhole and finish the back and two front shelves separately.
  6. But when until the end of the bolero there are as many rows as left on the lower edging, we knit them with a series of front and back loops.
  7. Then close the loop.
  8. And we connect the bolero along the shoulder seams.
  9. We collect new loops along the armhole line and knit the sleeves of the required length, the edge of which is also trimmed with the edging.

Model No. 2: openwork blouse

Knitting a lace bolero for a girl is better as follows:

  1. Subtract the distance from the lower edge to the armpit from the estimated length of the product. The total number is multiplied by 2.
  2. Then, according to the technology described earlier, we calculate the number of loops for the set.
  3. We knit the product across an even cloth, that is, starting from one sleeve and ending with another.
  4. Its length is equal to the sum of such parameters: two sleeve lengths + 1/2 chest circumference.
  5. Having reached the desired size, close the loops and sew the fabric, forming the sleeves.
