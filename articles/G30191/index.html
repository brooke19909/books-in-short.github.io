Moscow is the largest city in Russia





For many centuries, Moscow, the capital of our country, has been the historical and cultural center of Russia. It is in this city that the most beautiful architectural monuments are known all over the world: the Novodevichy Convent and the Kremlin, included in the UNESCO list, the Cathedral of Christ the Savior, Red Square, Poklonnaya Gora, all kinds of mansions, palaces, and manors. There really is something to see here. Moscow is the largest city by population.

Moscow theaters

All over the world, fame is booming about the capital's theaters - for example, the Maly and the Bolshoi. But not only in them you can spend time culturally. There are also theaters of Stanislavsky and Vakhtangov, do not forget about them.

Moscow museums

The Tretyakov Gallery and Pushkin Museum has many wonderful paintings and sculptures. They cause genuine admiration. Muscovites should be proud that such treasures are kept in the museums of their city.

Great city ...

Needless to say, our capital, which has a long history, has a large number of places for cultural pastime? Tourists coming to Moscow for the first time are delighted with the magnificence that appears before their eyes. And the population of the largest city, the number of which is 12 111 194 people, apparently already accustomed to the surrounding beauty.

Do you know much about the capital? There are a number of not very well-known, but interesting facts, let's look at them.
