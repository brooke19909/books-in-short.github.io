How to transfer money from MTS to Megafon: instructions and recommendations

USSD commands

How to transfer money from "MTS" to "Megaphone"? The first option is to use special USSD commands. They are in great demand among customers.

The transaction manual is as follows:

  1. Dial * 115 # on the phone.
  2. Click on 1.
  3. Click on number 3.
  4. Indicate the recipient number of funds.
  5. Enter the amount of the transfer.
  6. Click on "Submit".
  7. Double click on 1.
  8. Click the "Submit" button again.

Almost instantly, the recipient will receive an SMS to confirm the payment. You need to type any text (except 0) in the window that appears.



SMS commands

But this is only one of the possible scenarios. When thinking about how to transfer money from MTS to Megafon, the user should take into account SMS requests. They are not so often used, but you need to know about them.

The step-by-step algorithm for transferring money from MTS to the Megafon operator is represented by the following steps:

  1. Open a new SMS on the phone.
  2. Write in the text # money transfer , where "money" is the amount that you want to write off in favor of a friend.
  3. In the "Recipient" section, indicate to whom the money will be transferred.
  4. Send a message to the subscriber.
  5. Follow the steps to confirm the payment. Instructions will be sent via SMS from 6996.

After some time, the payer will receive a message stating that the money was successfully transferred to the specified phone number. This is how easy and simple it is to transfer the necessary amount from MTS to Megafon.

"Personal account" to help

But that is not all. MTS subscribers can perform the operation in a slightly different way. Namely, to use the "Personal Account" for any transactions with SIM cards.

How to act? Everything is not so difficult. Enough:

  1. Go to the MTS website.
  2. Log in to "My Account" using the previously received username and password.
  3. Go to pay.mts.ru/webportal/payments.
  4. Select "Megaphone" in the list of telecom operators.
  5. Enter the number of the recipient of the funds, as well as the value of the transaction.
  6. Put a mark near point "C" of the personal account of MTS ".
  7. Click on "Next".
  8. Confirm the operation.

This technique is called Easy Payment. When transferring money, a commission will be charged. At the moment, it is 5% + 10 rubles.



Limitations and Terms

How to transfer money from MTS to Megafon, we have understood. But this is not enough to successfully complete the transaction. You must remember the conditions that are put forward by the telecom operator.

Among them are the following points:

  • on the mobile phone after the transaction should remain at least 10 rubles;
  • the subscriber does not use the Super MTS and Super Zero tariffs;
  • the one who transfers the money did not impose bans on such transactions;
  • the average transaction fee is 10%;
  • through "Easy payment" it is impossible to conduct more than 5 operations per day;
  • the maximum payment per transaction is 15,000 rubles.

These are all conditions. There are not many of them, and there is nothing wrong with them.

Menu

How to transfer money from "MTS" to "Megaphone"? What does that require? On modern smartphones, at the beginning of work with MTS SIM cards, they install software called MTS service. With its help, it is allowed to carry out any operations with a number - replenishment of an account, money transfers, tariff change and receiving information about the current telephone service.

In general, the algorithm of actions will look something like this:

  1. Open MTS Service.
  2. Select "Translations".
  3. Click on "Another operator".
  4. Indicate the phone number for crediting money.
  5. Enter the value of the transaction.
  6. Confirm the sending of the request.

This is how you can easily transfer finances to the phone number of any other operator. The menu of "MTS-service" is extremely simple and clear. It is easy to use even for beginners.
