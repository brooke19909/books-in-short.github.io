Hotel location

Cornelia Deluxe Resort is located in the popular resort town of Belek, 45 kilometers from Antalya. The distance from the international airport is 35 km. The hotel is located on the beach, so you can enjoy the warm transparent water, sea air and the sound of the surf around the clock.

Having been to Belek, be sure to visit the nearby attractions. The closest to this hotel are the following:

  • Dolphinarium "Troy" with a water park (1 km);
  • large golf course (2 km);
  • Garden of Tolerance (7 km);
  • Legends theme park (8 km);
  • Aspendos Amphitheater (13 km);

Guest rooms

Comfortable living conditions - this is one of the main conditions for a quality holiday. The apartments of the Cornelia Deluxe Resort 5 * hotel boast an original modern interior, as well as a cozy atmosphere. You can choose the following accommodation options:

  • Family rooms with access to the garden have their own terrace with sun loungers, from which you can admire exotic plants. The number of beds allows you to accommodate 4 guests. The installation of additional space is also acceptable.
  • The family suite differs from the previous category in its luxurious design and more spacious area. You can enjoy panoramic sea views from your balcony or terrace.
  • The Blue Suite is designed to accommodate three adult guests. Between the areas of the bedroom and the living room there is a nightstand with a plasma TV. Thanks to the rotating design, you can view programs both lying on the bed and sitting on the couch. A huge terrace with sun loungers provides a sea view.
  • Suite "Harmony" is an example of comfort and delicate design taste. The dining, living and bedroom areas are decorated in gray tones. Upholstered furniture made of red leather can be considered a bright accent.
  • Diamond Suite impresses with its size and luxury. The highlight of the room is the pool located directly in the apartment.

Hotel Restaurants

Tasty and high-quality food is a necessary condition for a good rest, which is observed in the Cornelia Deluxe Resort 5 * hotel. Belek is a resort with many restaurants, but this hotel has some of the best. You can visit the following places:

  • Cornelius is the hotel's main buffet restaurant. Three international meals are served here three times a day. It is not allowed to visit the institution in open and beachwear.
  • In summer, the Daphne Snack Restaurant is located by the pool. Here, in the afternoons and nights, guests can enjoy a junk food.
  • Florian is a luxurious establishment specializing in Italian cuisine. It serves delicious pizza and the best pasta. The unique atmosphere, created thanks to the thematic interior and musical accompaniment, makes this restaurant an ideal place for romantic dates.
  • Hedera is a Mediterranean restaurant located on the outdoor terrace. Enjoying delicious dishes, you can admire the blue water of the pool, as well as inhale the aroma of pine trees growing nearby.
  • Tai Pen allows you to immerse yourself in the enchanting atmosphere of the Far East. Characteristic music, traditional interiors and original dishes - all this awaits you in this restaurant.
  • Sofa is a French restaurant that will immerse you in the atmosphere of Parisian romance. In addition to a wide range of dishes, there is also a wine list.
  • Resting in Turkey, you just can not pass by her unusual cuisine. Get to know the local culinary traditions with the Nazar Restaurant. Themed interiors and live music complement the overall experience.
