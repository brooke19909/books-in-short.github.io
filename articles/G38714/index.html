How to crochet a simple scarf? Schemes will help novice needlewomen easily and simply do the job

Not sure how to crochet a scarf? Perhaps you only comprehend the initial basics of this type of creativity? Follow the tips and instructions - and you will succeed!





Recommendations for beginners

Do you think that knitting such rather large products is a complex process? Do not be afraid immediately, you can try on a small sample, and then proceed to more serious tasks. For this reason, this model looks like a triangular shawl. As a material, you can use any type of yarn, starting from thin cotton threads, and ending with more voluminous wool.

Below is a detailed description of how to crochet a shawl. The schemes presented in this article clearly show the entire technology of manufacturing the product. Even novice needlewomen can easily cope with the task, if carefully guided by a detailed text description and graphic drawings of patterns. The following abbreviations are used:

  • VZDP - air loop;
  • SBNK - single crochet;
  • SSON - a column with one crochet;
  • SSDN - a column in two crochets;
  • AIVP (7) - an arch of seven air loops;
  • ** - repeating rapport.

So let's get started. Let's start with the main ornament.





The implementation of the main canvas of the openwork scarf

A triangular shawl shawl is crocheted very simply and quickly. Schemes may be different. The proposed ornament is simple. It contains a combination of just a few knitting techniques. Work begins at the bottom and gradually expands into a triangle.

  • 1 row - dial the initial chain of 5 VZDP. In the base of the first loop, tie 5 SSDN.
  • 2 row - 2 VZDP for lifting, 6 SBNK in the loops of the previous row.
  • 3 row - 4 VZDP for lifting. In the first loop of the last row of loops, knit 6 SSDN, in the last loop also perform 6 SSDN.
  • 4, 5 rows - 2 WZDP for lifting, all loops are made by SBNK.
  • 6 row - 4 VZDP for lifting. In the first loop of the last row of loops, knit 6 SSDN, in the middle of the row (approximately above the gap between the fans of the 2-crochet columns) make 6 SSDN, in the last loop also perform 6 SSDN.
  • 7, 8 rows - 2 VZDP for lifting, all loops are made by SBNK.

Next 6, 7 and 8 rows are repeated until the desired canvas size is obtained.





Crochet openwork scarf: border patterns

The edge of the canvas can be decorated in different ways. The simplest option is the edging by arches from air loops. But you can crochet a more elegant shawl. Patterns of lace tapes are presented in a simple pattern. Knit the ornament on the two sides of the scarf according to the instructions:

  • 1 row - rise from 3 loops, 4 VZDP, skip 2 loops on the base, * to the base loop, (7), back off on the basis of 5 loops *, to the base loop.
  • 2 row - rise from 1 loop to all loops of the base of the SBNK.
  • 3 row - rise from 3 loops, 3 SSSN to AIVP at the beginning of a row, * 1 SSSN to AIVP (7) of the previous row, repeat 3 times - AIVP (7), 1 SSSN to AIVP (7) of the previous row, 7 SSSN to AIVP (7) previous row *.
  • 4th row - rise from 1 loop to all loops of the base of the SBNK.
  • Row 5 - rise from 4 loops, repeat 3 times - 1 SSSN, 1 VZDP,, * 1 SSSN in AIVP (7) of the previous row, repeat 2 times - AIVP (7), 1 SSSN in AIVP (7) of the previous row, repeat 7 times - 1 VZDP, 1 SSDN *.
  • 6 row - rise from 1 loop to all loops of the base of the SBNK.
  • 7 row - rise from 4 loops, 7 SSDN, * 1 SSON to AIVP (7) of the previous row, AIVP (7), 1 SSON to AIVP (7) of the previous row, 14 SSDN *.
  • 8 row - rise from 1 loop to all loops of the base of the SBNK.
  • 9 row - rise from 4 loops, repeat 3 times - skip 1 loop on the base, in the next knit simultaneously 2 SSDN, 1 VZDP, * 1 SBNK in AIVP (7), repeat 7 times - 1 VZDP, skip 1 loop on the base, next knit at the same time 2 SSSN *.
  • 10, 11 row - in all loops of the base of SBNK.

Agree that the description of how to crochet a scarf for beginners is offered in a very accessible form. In the case of accurate execution of instructions, you can easily understand the basics of this type of applied creativity.
