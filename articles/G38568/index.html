Exotic Taiwan. Which country is this?

There are amazingly warm and beautiful places with magnificent pearl beaches and comfortable conditions for tourists to relax. But first, note that this is a land of amazing contrasts. Here, the magnificent snow-white sea coasts are perfectly adjacent to the bustling modern megalopolises. A huge number of sugarcane plantations grow near numerous temples and cutting-edge resorts.

Administrative separation

Taiwan is a democratic republic in the form of state power. What kind of country? Being one of the many provinces of China, according to the PRC government, Taiwan is in fact a separate state, which is practically not recognized by either the UN or a single country in the world. It is believed that its recognition means a breakdown in relations with mainland China, which no state can afford.

The president is the head of state, the prime minister is the head of government.

Language and religion

Chinese is the official language of Taiwan. Of the many dialects, the northern, otherwise Beijing, is chosen as the national language.

A very colorful state of Taiwan. What country is this in terms of religion? The most common religion is Buddhism. It is professed by approximately 5 million people. Taoism in popularity is the second religion. He is also considered as a philosophical doctrine.





Christianity came here from outside. Approximately 430 thousand Taiwanese residents consider themselves Protestants, and 300 thousand - Catholics. There are Muslims - about 50 thousand.
