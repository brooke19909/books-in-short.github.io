Hot spring, Shadrinsk - healing water available to everyone at any time of the year

The fact that residents of the Kurgan region have their own source of mineral water with a unique composition has long been known. But not so long ago there was an opportunity to even swim in a hot spring in Shadrinsk, created on the basis of well No. 315.

Unique water

About 30 wells with mineral water were drilled at the Shadrinskoye field, some of them are being operated. And these sources are located over 60 square kilometers. But the most interesting thing is that the composition of the water in each source is unique and inimitable. And this means that each water has its own individual composition, a set of micro and macro elements, and taste. And all the water in the mineral wells of the Kurgan region is similar in composition to the waters of Borjomi and Essentuki.

Shadrinskaya No. 315 and how it all began

Initially, the Shadrinsk hot spring was used exclusively for the extraction of drinking water. It belongs to the canteen and has the XII group. The depth of the well is 285 meters, and the temperature is +40 degrees. It is displayed on the surface of the earth by self-effusion. Mineralization from 8.7 to 9.6 g / l.

However, it turned out that well No. 315 gives water abundantly, and it was decided to approach the issue comprehensively - to organize here water treatment procedures for everyone. The first visitors could not say that everything was comfortable - there was only a pipe with beating water and a barrel in which they bathed.

Comfort and wellness

Over time, with the support of local authorities, a swimming pool appeared near the hot spring in Shadrinsk, where you can swim all year round. The bowl is in the open air, because the temperature of the water in it is always stable, at a level of plus 38-39 degrees.

The healing effect is achieved not only due to the presence of minerals of natural origin, but also due to the presence of metasilicon and carbon dioxide in the water, which allow useful minerals to penetrate the human body.

Today, the hot springs of Shadrinsk (Verkhnyaya Polevaya) are a landscaped area, a hotel, baths, rest rooms and warm dressing rooms, a cafeteria.

Infrastructure and additional services

Shadrinsk Hot Spring is an open-air pool bowl. The pool is 12 meters long and the depth is shallow, only 1.1 meters, in some places it reaches 1.5 meters. For lovers of night swimming night illumination is provided. Do not be afraid of salt deposits at the bottom of the pool, they have a therapeutic effect, especially in the presence of pathologies of the musculoskeletal system.

In addition, the wellness center allows you to enjoy air massage and hydromassage. On the territory there is a sauna, a Russian bath and warm changing rooms, where even there are showers. For a cup of tea or coffee with friends, a cozy cafe and a banquet hall are at guests' disposal. And if you decide to stay for a few days, you can settle in comfortable lounges (capacity - 40 beds). And in the period between bathing you can ride ATVs in the mountains, there is a well-laid track.

How to get there

Hot springs of Shadrinsk are located in the village of Verkhnyaya Poleva, 8.5 kilometers from the city of Shadrinsk (approximately 18 minutes by car). Therefore, those who wish to come for recovery should focus on this city. For example, regular flights to Shadrinsk depart from Yekaterinburg (from the North Station). True, it will take a long time to go, from 5 to 7 hours. From the same Yekaterinburg runs a high-speed train.

If you drive your own vehicles, then you should move along the Tyumen highway and turn around Kamensk-Uralsky. Further along the highway R-354 through the village of Kamenskoe you will reach Shadrinsk. The coordinates of the center of the village of Verkhnyaya Polevaya - 56 ¬∞ 04 'N and 63 ¬∞ 34 'East
