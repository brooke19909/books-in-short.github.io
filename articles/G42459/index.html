DIY amulet for money and luck

Representatives of magic assure that money can be attracted with the help of various accessories and jewelry. Making a do-it-yourself amulet of luck and money to attract wealth is very simple. But you need to follow some rules so that it really helps its owner to achieve the main goal.

Such different amulets

Representatives of different magical directions have their own recipes on how to make an amulet for good luck and money with your own hands. Some magicians believe that spoken pouches or irredeemable bills located in the wallet can not only preserve, but also increase wealth. Others are sure that jewelry and other accessories will bring wealth. How to make an amulet for money, and do I need to contact a magician for help? Of course, there is no problem to buy a mascot. It is enough to order a charm from the sorcerer. But you have to spend money - this service is quite expensive. In addition, the amulet for money charged by the sorcerer may be weaker than what the future owner will fill with energy, if, of course, he takes the ritual seriously and with faith in his heart.

But will he help his new master? It is best to make an amulet for good luck and money yourself, and during manufacture charge it with your energy and program it to increase wealth.



The subtleties of making amulets for money

More often, amulets are made of bracelets, rings, necklaces, medallions, watches. More precisely, jewelry is an accessory in which people who believe in the power of such talismans hide their talismans. The main thing is that his master should not part with the charmed charm. You can also talk about wealth and luck gems. The most powerful stones are mages carnelian, amethyst, turquoise, onyx.

It is necessary to conduct the ceremony when a young month rises in the night sky, or at the time of the full moon. Some slander reads exactly at midnight. When choosing a rite, one must take into account the birth date of the owner of the amulet, age and zodiac sign. Even time of birth can affect the power of hex.

To say the amulet for money, you must first attract the attention of the fire element. It is fire that pumps its strength into the chosen object. It is important to carry out the ceremony, strictly follow all the advice of magicians and instructions for conducting rituals. Otherwise, the amulet may be weak and even harm its owner.



A simple amulet around the neck

The simplest amulet can be made of clay, wood or ordinary cardboard. Two circles of the same size are cut out of the material (you can give preference to any of the above). Next, you need to cover them with gold paint and glue them by passing a rope or a strong thread between them. Before saying the amulet made for money, you should apply the appropriate drawings on both sides - runes that attract wealth. During the pronunciation of magic words, you need to tie several knots on a thread or cord, as if sealing up the words spoken. Instead of a cardboard amulet, you can hang letters or hieroglyphs on a rope and speak them.

Ritual with a gem and a coin for making an amulet

Some gems without any rituals and slander attract their master money and wealth. Others require additional recharge. And for this, during the ceremony, the most ordinary coin is used.

A simple ritual is performed at the full moon and at midnight. The coin should be lowered into a container of water so that moonlight, falling into the water, is reflected in it. A jewel jewelery is placed next to the container. Seeing the moon's reflection in the water, you need to ask the celestial body to fill the coin and stone with its energy, as well as reward with the force of gravity. Next, leave a coin and a stone under moonlight until sunrise. After shifting the talismans into a velvet pouch sewn personally with the initials of the amulet owner embroidered on it with yellow thread. This money amulet should always be carried with you and not shown to anyone.



Strong Wax Amulet

It is important that before proceeding with the manufacture of any amulet, put your thoughts in order. During the rite and the creative process, one cannot doubt the power of magic, otherwise it will not work.

To make a wax amulet, you first need to choose a suitable candle. Let it be small and not very expensive, bizarre or the simplest. You need to purchase the one that you immediately liked. The ritual is recommended in the first days of the growing month. The younger the month, the stronger the mascot will be.

The ritual is held at 12 a.m. The selected candle must be placed in a glass and set on fire. In this case, no special conspiracy words need to be pronounced, it is enough just to convey the desire of the candle flame, to connect with it at the energy level. While the flames are playing, you should whisper your desires. Tell the candle about the fears too. According to magicians, candle wax will absorb dreams and desires, will remember anxieties and undesirable plots.

When the candle burns out, you should wait until the wax cools and hardens, taking a bizarre shape with curls and patterns. They will be stored and spoken mysterious information of the owner of the amulet.

The figurine that will remain after the rite must be carried constantly with you and do not let anyone touch it. It is better if the eyes of others will not see the amulet. Usually, the said wax is placed in a medallion or healed in a bag made of velvet or brocade golden fabric.



Amulet from the four elements

To make an amulet for good luck and money with your own hands, charged with the energy of the four elements, you will need a gold or purple ribbon. With it, using natural threads, you need to embroider the symbols of the sun and moon, wind and water in such a way that as a result you get a whole and woven picture. Each character cannot be sewn separately. Therefore, before starting to create a talisman, it is worth considering how everything will look in the end. The embroidered ribbon should be illuminated by the sun and the full moon, blown by the wind and moistened with heavy rain. When all the elements share their energy with the amulet, you can use the talisman.

Amulet Power and Belief in Magic

Without faith in magic, an amulet for money is powerless. For it to truly become a powerful amulet, you need to constantly nourish it with your energy. Thoughts materialize. To become the owner of a powerful talisman, you should not only learn the rules of the rituals, but also perform all the steps correctly. Charging the amulet with energy is a difficult and tedious process. It is necessary to concentrate and connect mentally with the objects of magic ritual. It is equally important to make it with your own hands.

Finally, so that the amulet does not lose its strength, you can not show it even to close people. This is a very personal item. And in no case can it be donated and given for temporary use even to very close people.
