Hotel Rethymno Mare Hotel 5 * (Crete, Rethymno, Greece): description, services, reviews

Many people dream of an unforgettable vacation in Greece. And preferably in some good hotel checked by other tourists. Well, one of these is the Rethymno Mare Hotel 5 *. And here it is worth telling in full detail.





Location

Rethymno Mare Hotel 5 * is located in the western part of the beautiful island of Crete. To be more precise, in the city of Rethymnon. This is a completely unique place. It is distinguished by its unique architecture, narrow streets, carved window frames, the Venetian port and preserved Greek identity. About 10-12 kilometers from it is the village of Scaletta. And it was here, a little less than 30 years ago, that a hotel was built, which is now so popular. The last restoration, by the way, was in 2014, so everything inside is modern and new.

The hotel has its own private beach, which is literally a few steps from the building. True, the airport is far away - in Heraklion. It is a little less than 80 kilometers from here.

Tourists can find a bus stop near the building. A regular taxi runs from there to Rethymnon and other nearby towns. And it is worth taking this into account, since all sights must be reached by transport. This is the Venetian port, the center of Byzantine art, archaeological and historical-ethnographic museums, a city park and many other interesting places.

What is in the hotel?

Rethymno Mare Hotel 5 * offers a variety of services, ranging from free private parking and high-speed Internet to a 24-hour reception and currency exchange.

It also offers services in the delivery of drinks and food, as well as the sale of tickets, caregivers on call, laundry assistance, transfer and rental cars, motorbikes and bicycles. The hotel has several shops, so tourists do not even need to leave the hotel to purchase anything.





For business

Rethymno Mare Hotel is a 5-star hotel complex, so that it has conditions not only for relaxation and accommodation. There is still everything you need for conferences and other events of a similar nature.

There are two modern, fully air-conditioned conference rooms. One can fit 120 people. This room is called Ariadna. In the second, known as Pasifae, there is enough space for 180 people. Both rooms have everything you need - equipment for playing video and audio material, a tribune, tables, microphones, projectors, a flip chart, a large screen and high-speed Internet. If you need something else, you just need to inform the administration about it. Hotel management will do everything possible for tenants and try to get the necessary equipment.

By the way, almost everyone can agree with the hotel staff. After all, the people working here speak five different languages. And to be more precise, English, German, Greek, French and Russian.

Restaurants

The food at Rethymno Mare is excellent. Which is not surprising, because the level is 5-star. Guests booking a room can choose BB (breakfast), HB (half board), or ALL (all inclusive).

The main restaurant is called Hermes. It works from 7:30 to 21:30. There is an early, regular and late breakfast, lunch, dinner, regular themed evenings and a lot of varied dishes. If guests leave somewhere for the whole day, then they can order a lunch box instead of breakfast. Only for this you need to go to the administrator the day before. They also serve a lot of different drinks - water, juices, refreshing and exotic cocktails, wine (white, red, pink), rtsinu (resinous traditional Greek wine, which can be tasted only in its homeland), local beer, etc.

There is also a Dionyssos tavern and a-la Carte restaurant on site. These establishments serve Cretan and Greek dishes. But there is also an institution Jasmine, which will appeal to lovers of exotic. After all, they serve Chinese cuisine.

By the way, the practice of ordering breakfast in your room is also practiced here. If you want to have a meal in your room, then you just need to call the reception and say about your wishes. To do this, the apartment has a telephone.

The most economical option

Many people would like to spend their holidays in Rethymno Mare Hotel 5 *. The description of the hotel really makes you want to come here. But most are still interested in more budget options. Does this 5-star hotel have it? Yes, and this is a 2-bed apartment of 32 square meters. m

Price policy

Well, now about the cost. Prices for apartments in this hotel are unfixed. And sometimes they can differ so that it will be difficult to believe. For example, if you reserve the previously mentioned apartments somewhere six months before check-in, you will need to pay only 42 tons. per week of relaxation (with half board). But if you come to your senses a week or two before the planned arrival, the price will increase to 100 tons. per day. And this is really true - you can check it yourself by making a test reservation. Therefore, it is very important to carefully plan your vacation.

Better to take advantage of early booking. It will be in all plans a justified act. The first plus is favorable prices. Second - you can choose your favorite number, and not reserve the remaining free. Helpful information

Check-in at the hotel starts at 14:00, and ends at 23:00. Check out lasts until noon. But, as the guests here said, this framework is conditional. If tourists arrived earlier, and their rooms are ready to move in, they will place guests right away. Arrived later than 23:00? It's okay too.

The hotel can live with children. If the baby is not two years old, a cradle will be provided for him, and absolutely free of charge. You can also bring pets to the hotel, but this is on a preliminary request. In general, if there are any nuances, then you need to mention them even when drawing up a reservation request. And you must necessarily provide information about a personal credit card. The hotel accepts four different options - American Express, Visa, Master Card and Diners Club.
