The best hotels of Crete: reviews

On the island of Crete, the best hotels are worth knowing for those travelers who are going to have a great time on vacation. The place of residence plays a big role, because you want to feel complete comfort during a trip to this area. This article contains a description of hotels of different price categories that are suitable for families, couples or are universal.

Its location in the center of the northern coast attracts a huge number of tourists. Most people primarily note the proximity to the beach, convenient accommodation and pleasant service. Great service and great view

In search of the best hotels in Crete for a comfortable stay on the island, you cannot go past the building of Porto Veneziano Hotel. It is located in the very center of the old city and overlooks the harbor, as well as the ruins of the Venetian arsenals. The hotel is three-star, and therefore the price is acceptable. The rooms are compact, with all the necessary amenities, the only negative is that for a long time there is no desire to linger in the rooms. For a double room will have to pay in the region of eight thousand rubles for one night.

Breakfast is included in this price, and the food is delicious, as noted in the reviews of people. The hotel is located in a convenient location for walking around the city. Sightseeing in this part of the island can be a long time. Free internet, restaurant, buffet in the morning - all this is present here. It was about this place that the most positive reviews were left among tourists who visited the area.

Award winning hotel

The best 5 star hotels in Crete provide customers with an unforgettable vacation, but few can boast such qualities as Capsis Elite Resort, Out of the Blue. In this resort, everyone will feel relaxed, will not need any services. Service was back in 2008 awarded the highest praise for the attitude towards people. The rooms are equipped to the highest standards with a minibar, safe, air conditioning and other technological devices. The complex includes five different hotels, all of them are individual, and the client can choose their own reservation. The location near the sights of antiquity and 25 kilometers from Heraklion airport attracts tourists from all over the world.

On the vast territory you can do any kind of sport, and at night there are restaurants, clubs and other institutions for lovers of active life. It has its own bowling alley, zoo, water park, diving, services and more. Each client who rested here was satisfied with the service provided.

Seven buildings for the joy of man

Among the best hotels in Crete, it is also worth noting the four-star Malia Beach, in which travelers from Eastern Europe rested and shared enthusiastic reviews. The resort is located near the settlement of Malia and offers customers a huge range of services. A clean sandy beach attracts to itself during the day, and in the evening, vacationers can have fun in bars, restaurants, clubs and other similar establishments. In reviews of vacations in this place, people noted the courtesy of the staff in relation to their customers. Many liked the food, which is included in the price of the room.

There are seven buildings available to accommodate travelers, which were built long ago, but were thoroughly repaired in 2003. Any tourist will be pleased to have fun here, bartenders create an atmosphere of incredible fun when visiting drinking establishments in the resort. The staff works promptly, check-in takes several minutes, because customers are appreciated here.

Cozy atmosphere in an unusual building

For many people, among the best hotels in Crete (5 stars), the Domus Renier Boutique Hotel is strikingly different. It is located in the city of Chania, where there are other places for settlement, but this building has created fame for its originality. An old building of the sixteenth century was remade under the hotel, updated according to the latest technological standards. Each visitor here notes the magnificence of the service and comfort that you feel when you visit. The rooms have an original style, and therefore the settlement will be accompanied by new emotions. There is no language barrier, because Russian-speaking employees are present here. One window view of the sights of the old city with the Venetian port is breathtaking. The service has earned fame for its friendly staff, room facilities and delicious food. The last aspect was praised by many people who have visited this place.

Great choice for family vacations

Among the best hotels in Crete for families, it is worth highlighting the Grecotel Club Marine Palace.
