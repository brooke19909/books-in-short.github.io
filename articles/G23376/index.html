"Vodnik" - camp site (Saratov): description and reviews

"Vodnik" is a camp site (Saratov), ​​which is characterized by favorable territorial location, offers its guests a large number of services and entertainment. Thanks to this, the rest will be unforgettable. “Vodnik” is a hostel (Saratov), ​​where you can spend time with your family. There is also the opportunity to organize various corporate and special events.

Accommodation at the recreation center "Vodnik"





The hostel (Saratov) can simultaneously accommodate up to forty vacationers.

Three options are available for placement:

  • Double rooms at the hotel. This is one room of a standard level of comfort. The room provides everything for a quality vacation: heating and air conditioning, a necessary set of furniture with a double bed, satellite TV, access to wireless Internet (Wi-Fi), a refrigerator, as well as a bathroom with shower.
  • Five-seater winter home. The accommodation is equipped with heating and air conditioning. The house has two bedrooms, a living room and a veranda, as well as a toilet with shower. In addition to modern furniture, there is a TV with a connected satellite antenna, wireless Internet and a refrigerator.
  • Ten-seater winter home. In addition to the rooms, as in a five-person house, there is also a kitchenette, a sauna and a swimming pool. A microwave is also included.

If necessary, you can install an extra bed in any of the types of housing available at the Vodnik camp site (Saratov). Accommodation prices depend on the season and the number of people. The minimum cost is 2,500 rubles.

Service





For vacationers, services such as car parking, several cozy gazebos, playgrounds with barbecue facilities and picnic areas, a volleyball court, equipped sandy beach, pier and parking for small boats, a bathhouse and a pool are available. This is included in the price on the territory of Vodnik.

The hostel (Saratov) also has a bar, a conference room equipped with modern technologies, a dining room with a fireplace and karaoke. All this is located in the hotel building.

For an additional fee the camp site staff offers its vacationers to rent pleasure boats, snowmobiles, sports and fishing equipment, organize summer and winter fishing, as well as banquets, corporate parties, weddings, conferences and other events.

Nutrition





Since the camp site is located in a residential village, here you can easily purchase various food products, including home-made ones. For self-cooking has the availability of minimal kitchen equipment and barbecue. But if you do not want to do culinary business, then you can order three meals a day at a cafe, which is located on the territory of the base. The dining room is made in a hunting style. It has a large TV and a fireplace for cozy gatherings in cold autumn or winter.

The hotel building also has a bar with a wide range of various alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.

Guest reviews





Many people have already visited Vodnik (Saratov). The hostel reviews for the whole time received the most diverse. Here is an example of the positive comments that vacationers note:

  • I really like that the camp site is located near Saratov.
  • The place of rest can be reached not only by private car, but also by public transport.
  • The surrounding atmosphere is very conducive to relaxation: the forest, the banks of the Volga, numerous green spaces on the territory itself.
  • The houses have been modernized, there is all the necessary furniture for a comfortable stay.
  • The beach is clean, as is the water in the river.
  • Many wooden arbors, next to each there is an individual brazier.
  • On the beach there are special places for washing feet from adhering sand.
  • There are designated smoking areas throughout the recreation center . Therefore, no one will smoke near you.

The unpleasant moments of the guests include the following:

  • The price is not very budget.
  • Pergolas are small, designed for a maximum of eight people.
  • Entrance to the beach, as well as rental of gazebos, are not paid for guests.
  • It’s uncomfortable to swim: the bottom is uneven, the depth begins almost next to the shore. You won’t swim with a child.

If we conclude from all the reviews, it turns out that Vodnik is a great place to relax with companies in order to fry kebabs. If you came for one day to sunbathe on the beach and swim in the pond, then most likely you will not get the proper pleasure.

Location





Full address where the Vodnik camp site is located: Saratov, Zavodskoy District, Vodnik Village. A car ride from the city takes about 40 minutes. The camp site is located just twenty kilometers away.

By public transport you can get from Vasilkovskaya street by bus number 39. The road from this stop takes an average of twenty minutes.

The Vodnik camp site is open all year round.
