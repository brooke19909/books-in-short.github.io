Where is the city of Kotlas located? Cities of the Arkhangelsk region

So where is the city of Kotlas? Few people know that in the distant past, namely in the 9th century, in the place of the modern city there was a dense and impenetrable forest. Only on the right Bank of the Northern Dvina, somewhat south of the place where Vychegoda flows into it, was a small settlement. Today Kotlas is a city of the Arkhangelsk region, an administrative center with a population of more than 60,000 people. For many, the city may seem quite small and uninteresting, but within the framework of this material we will not only tell you where the city of Kotlas is located, but also introduce you to its amazing history.





Historical reference

For the first time as a settlement, the current Kotlas (Arkhangelsk region) was mentioned in 1379. Then on this earth lived the Komi tribes. It was through this settlement in the XIV century that trade routes from Siberia, Arkhangelsk and the central regions of the country passed. But even the excellent location of the city did not contribute to its development and prosperity, Kotlas for a long time remained a tiny and almost invisible town.

A turning point in the history of the city was steam shipping, which contributed to its development. And with the construction of the Kotlas railway in the region, it completely turned into a developed transport hub.





What is the glorious city of Kotlas today? Its economy, as during its heyday, is closely connected with the railway industry. She brings the main income to the city budget.

Population

The size and composition of the population is one of the important characteristics of any settlement. So, the population of the city of Kotlas, according to the latest census, is 61 902 people. Mostly Russians live in the city.

What to see

Many sincerely believe that the city of Kotlas (Arkhangelsk region), despite its rich history, cannot boast of iconic places that every guest must visit. But we intend to refute this information.

The Kotlas Local History Museum is perhaps the main attraction of the city, all the secrets of its formation and development are kept here. If you want to get to know the city better, to reveal its secrets, feel free to go here. Outwardly an unremarkable building, not distinguished by the complexity of the design, but it is here that history is stored.





Private moto museum is another interesting place. The exposition includes motorcycles, mopeds of various times and eras, as well as photographs from major exhibitions and motocross competitions. Surely enthusiastic guests of the city will be interesting to visit this attraction.

How to get there

We figured out that Kotlas is a city of the Arkhangelsk region. But it is not enough to answer the question of where the city of Kotlas is located, it is important to understand how to get to it.

Despite the fact that the city is quite small, the transport connection with it is excellent. Just a few kilometers east of the city, planes from Moscow, Arkhangelsk, St. Petersburg, Murmansk and other cities of the country regularly land. Thus, you do not have to take plane tickets to Arkhangelsk, and then overcome a long journey by car or bus - you can immediately purchase a ticket for a direct flight to Kotlas.





Over an impressive period of time, the glorious city of Kotlas was an important transport hub; it has retained its status to this day. Due to the developed railway connection, you can easily get to Arkhangelsk, Moscow, several times a week through the railway station passes the train St. Petersburg - Vorkuta.

If you decide to get to Arkhangelsk, you can do this not only by train, but also by bus, departing from the bus station of the city twice a day. Buses leave from Kotlas to Veliky Ustyug 8 times a week, to Vologda - three times a week. The route Arkhangelsk - Kotlas is also covered by rail and buses. These extreme sportsmen and tourists who like to discover everything new and unknown can take advantage of water transport. Given the sufficient depth of the river from Arkhangelsk to Kotlas can be reached by boat, leaving twice a week. Get ready for a long road: when driving down the river, the road will take 49 hours, in the opposite direction - 64 hours. But such a mini-journey will allow you to enjoy the beauty of the landscapes and the freshness of the northern air.

Where to stay

Going to any city, it is important to resolve the issue of housing in advance. There are very few hotels in Kotlas, but if you reserve a room of the required category, you will save yourself from numerous difficulties.

The Sovetskaya Hotel, the largest hotel in the city, offers guests more than 200 rooms of various price categories. It is located in the city center, so it will be a great option for tourists and business travelers. The cost of daily living starts from 1300 rubles, rooms are not equipped with the most modern furniture and appliances, but in order to relax and restore strength, this is a good option.





If you are a connoisseur of first-class service and want to stay in a beautiful hotel room with all the necessary amenities, pay attention to the Sapphire Hotel. 49 comfortable rooms, furnished with modern stylish furniture and appliances - this is the best that guests of the city can find. It is here that businessmen and business travelers quite often stop, and this is largely due to the presence of a conference room for business negotiations and meetings, as well as a chic restaurant on the ground floor.

Climate

The climate of Kotlas is close to moderate cold. Rainfall is significant, even in the driest season. The average annual air temperature barely reaches 2 degrees Celsius, it is quite windy and humid. That is why, if you are planning to visit this glorious city as part of a business trip, be sure to bring warm clothes and a scarf with you, otherwise the probability of catching a cold will be quite high.
