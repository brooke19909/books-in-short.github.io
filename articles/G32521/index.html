Yalta: reviews, climate and hotels

In the very south of Crimea is the city of Yalta, unique in every sense. And its history began as a resort in 1838, when the famous climatologists Botkin and Dmitriev recommended it for the rest of the royal family. And, as a result, after the imperial family went to Yalta and the noble family. For them, palaces and estates with large park areas were built on request.



All this now constitutes the cultural heritage of Big Yalta. But all this would not have happened if not for the unique climate of the city of Yalta. Reviews about the always good weather and made it one of the best resorts in the world. The mild Mediterranean climate contributes to long summers and warm winters. There are 15% more sunny days than in French Nice. But at the same time, humidity is not so high in Yalta, and summer heat is much easier.

All, without exception, doctors recommend visiting Yalta for patients with pulmonary diseases, including tuberculosis, and those suffering from nervous disorders. Starting in the 19th century, these recommendations quickly made the once-fishing village of Yalta a fashionable resort. Reviews already visited her attracted more nobility to relax.

In the vicinity of Yalta, palaces appeared that make up the world cultural heritage today. These are the Livadia, Vorontsov, Massandra and Yusupov palaces. Their walls are remembered by many prominent historical characters. It was to receive foreign delegations during the Great Patriotic War that Yalta was chosen. Reviews, the most positive ones, were left in both Memorials by Churchill and Roosevelt.





And this is not an accident. Even today, Yalta is not without its aristocratic raid. The winding streets, the historic promenade and a mixture of a variety of architectural styles attract tourists from all over the world. More than a million residents rest in Yalta annually. All this contributes to the development of infrastructure and the emergence of new hotels and restoration of old ones.

The Oreanda Hotel and the Yalta Intourist Hotel are considered the most prestigious for vacation in Yalta . Oreanda is not only the oldest hotel in Yalta, but also the only four-star hotel in the city. In addition, it is also located in the very center of the embankment. But comfort has a downside. It is also one of the most expensive hotels in the city. The other hotel mentioned above is located on the outskirts, but it is near the Nikitsky Botanical Garden and has its own dolphinarium and Olympic pool. The price / reviews ratio in Yalta often does not correspond to the formula: the more you pay, the better the service. You can find a less prestigious hotel with an acceptable cost and with good service.



But for most vacationers, living conditions are often not at all important, since they come primarily for impressions from nature and cultural monuments. For many, for several days, the address of residence is simply Crimea, Yalta. The reviews of such tourists contain more information about trips, not about hotels. Warm nights allow you to live even just on the seashore in a tent.

Regardless of the chosen route and tourist mobility, Yalta is so rich in monuments that even without going beyond the embankment, you can find a new place to visit every day. Perhaps that is why the city you want to return to is Yalta! Reviews of most tourists about him are excellent, and the impressions are indelible.
