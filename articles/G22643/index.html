Sodium tetraborate slime: step by step instructions

Let's talk about the toy beloved by all children - about the slime! A toy with such an unattractive name is a sticky, wetish, jelly-like mass of bright color and odorless. A slime does not mold like plasticine, although it has a certain plasticity, but it adheres very well to surfaces, sliding slowly from them. This ability and a pleasant to the touch texture made this seemingly unremarkable mass one of the most famous inventions in the gaming industry.

History of Handgam

The ancestor of the substance known in the countries of the former USSR under the name "lizun" was an acid-green toy slime, which was released by Slime.

The name "slime" stuck to the slime after the television movie "Ghostbusters" was released. The ghost in whose honor this jelly-shaped toy was named had a similar shape to it.

Making smart clay from sodium tetraborate
