How to choose a USB endoscope?

In production and in everyday life, you can come across equipment whose structure and details cannot be seen with the naked eye. Such a complex design is possessed by sewer pipes, communications covered with drywall, internal engine systems and much more. To check their condition, level of wear, breakdowns or blockages, special equipment is used - a flexible USB endoscope. Such a device allows you to consider the smallest structural details even in complete darkness, which greatly simplifies diagnostic work.

USB endoscope design

The head unit, or processor, is the main part of this device. The illuminated USB endoscope camera module is connected to the processor via a cable. The backlight LEDs illuminate the work area, so you can carefully consider the object under study.





When is such equipment required?

A backlit endoscope model is necessary in cases where it is planned to carry out diagnostic work in places with limited visibility and poor lighting. The simplest models are most suitable for electricians and plumbers, who during work can not do without a detailed study of all communications.

A backlit USB endoscope with advanced functionality is required when the operations to be performed must be accompanied not only by video from the camera, but also by audio comments. Despite the fact that the device is highly specialized, it is acquired not only by specialists, but also by home craftsmen for domestic use.

The principle of operation of the device

To work with a USB endoscope, it must be connected using a cable to a personal computer, tablet or smartphone via the appropriate port. Included with the device comes special software that is installed on a PC. The program for the USB endoscope allows you to view the image coming from the camera on a computer monitor or smartphone.

The backlight brightness of a device equipped with LEDs can be adjusted during operation. Thanks to the bundled software, the image can not only be viewed, but also recorded in AVI format. Parts and faults can be photographed if necessary in high quality.

Some models of USB endoscopes for Android are equipped with special angular attachments for the camera, which makes their operation more convenient.

USB endoscopes began to enjoy maximum popularity after the advent of liquid crystal displays. The first models of such devices had an unbearable price and a complex device, but over time they were improved in technical terms and became more accessible. Often, endoscopes are used in the automotive and vehicle service industries for diagnostic work. The device allows you to inspect all systems and machine parts, in time to identify breakdowns and malfunctions.

As already mentioned, an endoscope is also used in the automotive industry to inspect engines and other closed parts. Using a USB endoscope camera, internal systems are inspected and defects in metal casting, signs of wear, and other breakdowns are detected.

Diagnostics of various metal products allows to identify defects in metal casting. For this purpose, both rigid and flexible models of endoscopes are used.





How to choose an endoscope?

This type of equipment is used by specialists of various professions, from electricians to car service mechanics and plumbers. Such a wide area of ‚Äã‚Äãuse of the device is explained by the fact that its camera allows you to look into hard-to-reach places and carry out their full diagnostics without dismantling and disassembling all the equipment. The most effective operation of the device is possible if, when choosing it, the user pays attention to such criteria as the maximum possible flexibility of the case and image quality.

For the introduction of the endoscope in hard-to-reach places, its body must have a high level of plasticity. For example, in pipes with a diameter of not more than three inches, it is impossible to use a device with a cable thickness of 10 millimeters and a module length of 65 millimeters. For this reason, endoscopes are considered the best option for widespread use. Their length does not exceed 50 millimeters, and the diameter of the wire is in the range of 8-9 millimeters. However, there are models with smaller sizes and more flexible. They are used when it is necessary to gain access to the most impassable places and it is impossible to use wider analogs whose cables do not pass through the studied areas.

When choosing a device, not only the above parameters are taken into account, but also other criteria - for example, moisture resistance, backlight brightness. The degree of protection of the shell for most devices according to the international classification system is IP67 - this level is sufficient for effective protection against moisture and dust.





Benefits

Bright LEDs are built into the camera of the device, thanks to which it is possible to consider the smallest details in complete darkness. The advantages of such equipment, in addition to lighting, include the following features:

  • the ability to manually adjust the focus;
  • 2x magnification of the broadcast image;
  • Snapshot mode
  • Ergonomic and comfortable body.

General tools
