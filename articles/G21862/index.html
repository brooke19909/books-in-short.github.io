Mutual Investment Funds of Alfa-Bank: Profitability, Dynamics Analysis, Reviews

Alfa-Bank mutual funds are becoming increasingly popular among the population of the Russian Federation. The main reason is to increase the yield of deposits.

What is a mutual fund?

The term Mutual Fund is the abbreviation of a mutual investment fund, a complex of property, which is based on the trust management of a specialized company. The purpose of the fund is to increase the value of property in the future.





The property of the fund consists of shares of founders. To create mutual funds, they must transfer the rights to dispose of their property to the management company.

Specialized company

Specialized is considered to be a company that manages a mutual investment fund. Management should satisfy the interests of investors, and not go beyond the law.

The investor's share is determined by the number of mutual funds. A share in itself is a security certifying ownership of the property. The share also determines the owner‚Äôs share in the fund.

The liquidity, risk and profitability of Alfa-Bank mutual funds together with the investment structure distinguish them from each other. Classic mutual funds are bonds and stocks. We must remember the main rule: with a large number of shares in the mutual fund portfolio, the profitability of Alfa-Bank funds will fall, but the risk of losing money will be very small. This rule also works in the opposite direction: the higher the share of shares in the structure of the unit investment fund, the higher its profitability, which means that the risk of the portfolio increases.

As for the industry unit investment funds, they relate to a specific sector of the economy. For example, to trade, resources, infrastructure and so on. Thus, if an investor invests his money in one of the funds, income will be received only if the corresponding sector of the economy grows.
