The resort village of Betta, boarding house "South": description, photo, reviews

Gelendzhik and its environs have long won the fame of a world resort. Holidays in this area have a number of undeniable advantages due to the growth of Pizunda pine trees, clean air and the presence of mountains. Sea air is filled with beneficial phytoncides, which have a beneficial effect on the respiratory system. In Gelendzhik, many cozy villages are concentrated, which are replete with sanatorium complexes, mini-hotels and private hotels.





One of them is the resort of Betta with a population of no more than 800 people. The village is located in a quiet picturesque harbor, where there is no city bustle and noisy entertainment. People come here for solitude and spiritual harmony. Located in the resort Betta boarding house "South", receiving guests from May to September.

Coastline

The pebble coast is located about 150 meters from the complex. The beach is clean, well-maintained, located in the village of Betta. Pension Yuzhny does not have its own coastline, but this does not make it worse. The municipal beach is small, not crowded, with an even descent into the sea, which is very convenient for children and the elderly.

On the embankment are concentrated rental shops for the rental of water equipment and equipment: catamarans, deck chairs, mattresses, awnings, scooters, ATVs. It offers guests a cozy cafe, inflatable slides and other water attractions for young guests. Particular attention should be paid to scuba diving.





The local beach has long been chosen by divers. The flora and fauna of the underwater world is truly unique - with reefs, unusual landscapes and winding gorges. Many species of fish and luminous algae live here. Underwater adventure will bring a lot of pleasant emotions and give a range of vivid sensations.
