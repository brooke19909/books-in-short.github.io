Famous ski resorts in Spain

Everyone knows that Spain is a sunny country of excellent beaches. Although its ski resorts are no less famous. A worthy place among all countries that have at their disposal everything that is required for skiing belongs to it rightfully. At the same time, the ski resorts of Spain on the map of winter holidays in Europe are still unknown to many skiers. Therefore, it is worth considering in more detail some of them.



The famous ski resort of Sierra Nevada, Spain

This is the southernmost ski resort in Europe, which is located in the province of Andalusia. The city of Pradollano is the center of Sierra Nevada. Hotels, numerous bars, apartments, restaurants, rentals, sports schools, discos - such is its infrastructure. In safe condition, the paths are supported by 278 snow cannons. 7 km of black and 24 km of red tracks are intended for professionals. Acropark roads are designed for parallel slalom, freestyle and snowboarding. On the slope of El Rio there are 2 famous tracks designed for night skiing. The length of all routes is 63 km. There are nineteen lifts. Ski stations, dry soft snow, incredibly bright sun, beautifully planned ski roads - all this is the highlight of the resort.

Spain ski resorts: Baqueira Beret

Baqueira-Beret is the best ski resort located in the eastern part of Aran, surrounded by the highest mountains in the Pyrenees. He is famous for the fact that the president of Spain rides in this place , in addition, members of the royal family. From Barcelona you can get here in six hours by bus. Sporting opportunities of this resort: 24 ski lifts, 77 km of roads of three difficulty levels, wide tracks designed for group skating, a snowboard park, 7 km of tracks for skiers, dog sledding, paragliding, hiking trails, snowshoeing, a sports center, freestyle , pool, bowling, ice rink. Well-developed infrastructure with apartments, hotels, cafes, restaurants, training and children centers, experienced instructors, rentals will make your vacation fascinating and safe.



Spain Ski Resorts: Espolite Escui

Located near Lake San Maurico, near Aigrestortes (National Park) , this resort is located. There are 30 km of tracks (9 blue, 4 green, 6 black, 11 red); 7 lifts, 89 guns. Incredible charm is given by the slopes of the Pyrenees.

Spain ski resorts: Port del Compte

The resort is located in the Solsones Mountains in the Eastern Pyrenees. Its tracks go through the forest, therefore, they are reliably protected from the piercing wind. This place has all possible types of ski roads, toboggan run, 15 ski lifts, a snow park, a snowboard, a helicopter station, a mountain bike, a hotel, parking, a ski school, a restaurant. The resort has its own traditions: in this place there are competitions of skiing local clubs, the Solsona carnival with festivities in fancy dress, as well as slopes with brightly flaming torches.



In general, all resorts in Spain are distinguished by excellent communications, infrastructure and services. It is not surprising that they received about 6 million visitors last season.
