Rest in Berdyansk. Recreation facilities and services

It seems that people have always lived on the territory of this modern city since time immemorial, therefore Berdyansk is famous for centuries-old history. The town is small, you can get around it in a few hours, but there is something to see. It is mainly known for its coast. Many residents of Ukraine and neighboring countries choose holidays in Berdyansk. Recreation facilities are here for every taste and budget, so you can choose the most suitable option without any problems.

The benefits of a holiday in Berdyansk

The main advantage of the city is a harmonious combination of pleasant natural conditions for recreation and developed tourist infrastructure. This is what attracts many vacationers here. There is also one more important criterion - price. Often, it is she who is the decisive factor in choosing a resort area. It is worth noting that rest in Berdyansk (recreation centers offer rooms from 30 UAH (80-100 rubles) per place per day) are much cheaper than, for example, on the Crimean peninsula. Of course, the list of services offered will depend on the pricing policy, but this is already a matter of personal preference. However, one does not need to think that a cheap vacation must be uncomfortable. Although the prices here are an order of magnitude lower, the staff of sanatoriums, houses and recreation centers, hotels in Berdyansk makes every effort to provide its guests with the most interesting, convenient and varied vacation. Favorable natural conditions, of course, facilitate this task, because the time spent by the sea always brings untold pleasure. But each institution for recreation, in addition, tries to provide unique services and a higher level of service compared to competitors. That is why we offer you a description of several bases and hotels in Berdyansk, so that you can choose the most suitable option for yourself in advance.



The recreation center "Oreanda"

The Oreanda Holiday Village in Berdyansk is a seaside resort that includes two luxury hotels, a small motel and a boarding house. It is located on the world famous Berdyansk Spit, which goes into the depths of the sea for more than 10 km. The complex is ideal for families, especially with children. They can get a lot of pleasure from swimming in the sea, while undergoing a wellness course of treatment. The most beneficial effect is on the musculoskeletal system, for example, treatment of inflammation of the joints, and on the upper respiratory tract. Also, importantly, the local atmosphere perfectly affects the nerves. The warm pleasant sun and sea breeze have a calming effect on any person. For medicinal purposes, use local mineral water. It has a huge range of healing properties that positively affect the human body and significantly improve relaxation in Berdyansk. Recreation centers located in this city, as a rule, have a private beach. Oreanda is no exception. It is located only 60 m from residential buildings. The beach is equipped with sunbeds, umbrellas. Vacationers are invited to ride a water slide, you can also rent boats, catamarans, etc.

The recreation center "Admiral"

The recreation center in Berdyansk "Admiral" was opened for tourists relatively recently - in 2010, the decoration of the premises was done only with environmentally friendly materials, which is an indisputable plus. The area of ​​the base covers an area of ​​20 thousand square meters. m. The houses are built in such a way that vacationers, looking out the windows, can enjoy the sea. The rooms have both cold and hot water. The whole territory is under round-the-clock protection. There is parking, an equipped playground, banquets, discos are held, bars and restaurants work, of course, there is a beach.



Recreation center Shakhtar

The recreation center Shakhtar in Berdyansk is located on the middle spit. On the territory there is a residential complex of 4 floors, working all year round, a dining room, a sauna and seasonal houses. The beach is very close, so vacationers can enjoy the sounds of the sea even in their rooms. Parking is free. There is a bus stop near the base. The playground is equipped with various attractions. Bars and a billiard room are available for adults.



SPA Club "Diodon"

One of the interesting complexes is the club "Diodon". You can guess right here that you can have a chic rest here as soon as you enter the territory. Between two chic three-story buildings made of chopped wooden beams at the level of the second floor there are three pools at once. In the middle is the usual one, it is the largest. And on the side there is a swimming pool with hydromassage and a children's paddling pool equipped. There are services typical for SPA salons: a solarium, a massage room, an aroma bar, a thalassotherapy room and body wraps, a cosmetology room, etc.

Hotel Arthurs Berdyansk Resort

For those who prefer a holiday in Berdyansk, recreation centers and hotels are always open. No less comfortable is Arthurs Berdyansk Resort. In addition to the standard package of services, this hotel also offers a variety of animation programs, which will be quite interesting for the kids. Arthurs is distinguished by its special policy regarding children: up to 5 years, their accommodation and meals are free. For them, the hotel has a swimming pool, playground, children's room. It is also worth noting that five meals a day are designed specifically for this age category.



Hotel Sun Resort

Sun Resort Hotel, located on the first line of the spit of Berdyansk and surrounded by estuaries with mud springs, has a European level of comfort. There are also happy vacationers with children, for them - a kids club, playground, pool. Dining is provided by two restaurants, one of which works on the basis of the "buffet".
