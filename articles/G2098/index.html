"Renaissance Monarch": hotel description, address, reviews

Decorated in a French style, the hotel in the center of Moscow immediately like visitors. It has many features of the "Renaissance Monarch". Address, additional services, advantages and disadvantages - this is information that can be found later in the article.

Solemn Events

This luxurious establishment was opened in 2010 and immediately became popular among the business elite and tourists. The most important advantage of this place is its location. The room is located in the financial and cultural district of the capital. Both entrepreneurs and couples stay here .



The hotel attracts successful and business people with conference rooms. Each room is equipped with appliances: new audio and video equipment and unlimited access to the Internet. The halls have high-quality air conditioning. It should be noted that there are 12 separate audiences for negotiations. They are joined by a spacious lobby. In total, about 1,500 people can immediately fit here.

Focuses on various categories of guests of the "Renaissance Monarch". Georgievsky, Catherine‚Äôs, Andreevsky Hall are large halls where residents and guests of the capital celebrate weddings, anniversaries and corporate events. Each room is beautifully and unobtrusively decorated. The scenery helps create a sophisticated and festive atmosphere.

Employees of the institution at the request of customers can install the necessary equipment. In addition, the hotel organizes banquets, buffets and tea and coffee breaks.

Impressions of regular customers

It is comfortable in the hotel not only to domestic guests, but also to foreign ones. Foreign television works for them. Bring daily newspapers. Therefore, guests are always aware of important events.

The hotel is quite young, but has already successfully established itself in the metropolitan market. However, many people who have already spent time in this institution claim that every year the service is getting worse. Workers are often inhospitable and tired. It is not uncommon to have to call twice before bringing what is missing to the room.

Another negative point is the distant location from other institutions. If you do not like the kitchen that the complex offers, you will have to look for another restaurant for a long time. There are no upscale cafes nearby. But most visitors are delighted with the dishes and menus.

Nearby is a shopping center, which is connected to the hotel by a passage. There you can buy everything you need.

Getting to the place with your car is not easy, there are traffic jams around. Saves the proximity of the metro to the Renaissance Monarch Hotel. Leningradsky Prospekt 31a - the address of the complex.





Tasty and sophisticated: all in one

The pride of the institution is the kitchen. The hotel offers guests a meal in the Mosaic restaurant. It serves delicious European and domestic dishes. You can enjoy Asian delicacies at the Premier evening restaurant. By ordering a special offer, the client will receive quality and original dishes. You can try the exclusive food that professionals will cook before your eyes. It should be noted that you can ask for dinner with a meat, fish or vegetarian menu. Famous cook Bejamin Joubrele prepares French exotic.

Most of all, visitors like a varied food. The guests say that nobody will be hungry. The food is hearty and delicious. On the table is always a variety of dishes. However, guests note that prices are high, but also correspond to the status of the Renaissance Monarch hotel.





Cafes and restaurants

The Executive Lounge is located on the last two floors. Private and business meetings are held here. The room is decorated in light, bright colors. However, the highlight of this room is the panorama. Wide windows offer amazing views of the center of the capital.

In warmer months, visitors love to sit on the outdoor terraces. This part of the hotel is open until 24 hours. Therefore, guests come here to enjoy the views of Moscow at night.

In addition, the hotel has a ground and underground parking "Renaissance Monarch". However, visitors complain that parking prices are unreasonably high. It is much cheaper to park the car on a nearby street, where the quality of the parking does not differ.

Premier receives visitors in the evening. You can enjoy the delicacies of the East by going to the Sushi Bar. And in the summer, a cafe opens under shady trees. All this is placed on 16 floors.





Additional services

At first glance, the "Renaissance Moscow Monarch Center" is striking in its scope. The construction is very stylish and decorated according to all modern trends. The hall is spacious, there is no crowd and crush. While waiting for your time to move in, you can go to a restaurant or go shopping. Pleasant to expect on comfortable, wide sofas at the entrance. There are no queues. All workers are always on the spot. But for many guests the staff seemed rather cold. However, the restraint of the workers did not affect the general impression.

You can migrate through the floors with five quick elevators. Guests say that the lift is not spacious enough. Sometimes the elevator has to wait a few minutes.

At home, at home

Offers additional services "Renaissance Monarch". The hotel has a gym, fitness center and spa. It is all located on the minus ground floor. There are cabinets that close. Extras include a bathrobe and towel. The sauna is divided, men and women separately. The pool is small. However, on weekdays, when there are few visitors, swimming is free. You can also use the jacuzzi, solarium and massage services. Sun loungers are installed on the roof. Most of all, visitors have positive impressions of additional services.



Guests are advised to take rooms on the upper floors. From there, a better view and less noise. In general, the Renaissance Monarch offers its customers 366 rooms of various categories: from the economy standard to the elegant suite. Among the rooms there are those that are adapted for permanent residence in comfortable, almost home conditions. After all, there are kitchens and living rooms. Customers also receive a subscription to the fitness center, training room, pool and sauna. They are located directly in the room.

Key Issues

Each room is equipped according to international requirements. There is a TV, a reliable safe, landline phones. There is a minibar. But if you do not leave a deposit at the entrance, it is closed right in front of the visitor‚Äôs nose, customers complain. An iron and ironing board are also included. Like any high-quality establishment, the Renaissance Moscow Monarch Center hotel made sure that the rooms had a comfortable, well-lit workplace. Phones are equipped with a speakerphone and an answering machine. Therefore, guests share, you can do business without leaving the hotel.

Among the rooms there is a five-room presidential suite.

The walls are well soundproofed. Neighbors and noise from the corridor are not heard, as guests say. But the windows miss the sounds. The construction work and the signals of the machines interfere with sleep in the morning. However, most residents of large cities are accustomed to such "music."

The Renaissance Monarch establishment offers large and spacious rooms. The rooms have expensive air conditioning, the air is clean and fresh.





The view of the guests

Very spacious bathtubs. There is a hairdryer and a full set of hygiene products. But visitors are confused by the manufacturer - China. Often customers complain that there are not enough bathrobes, towels or slippers. However, if you inform the staff about this trouble, then these things will immediately bring. There are no toothbrushes and caps for a shower, guests complain. Dressing gowns are standard size, so full people are not comfortable wearing them.

A pleasant surprise for guests is the fact that in addition to a minibar in the rooms, electric kettles and coffee with tea. There is also an ice bucket. The beds are very wide and soft.

It has a very convenient location "Renaissance Monarch". Metro is nearby. You can exit at the Dynamo or Jogging stations. The hotel is only 5-10 minutes walk. Near the main attractions of the capital. Therefore, it is immediately good to go on excursions from here.

Comfortable rest

You can come with children. The pool is shallow for them. And staff, upon request, can deliver two small cots to your room. However, parents recommend that you first read the menu. After all, not all guys like foreign delicacies. Therefore, someone may remain hungry.

In general, it meets all the standards of the Renaissance Monarch. The hotel is very stylish and beautiful. However, along with the pros, there are minor disadvantages. For example, often the equipment in the room does not work. Therefore, to use a kettle or iron, you have to contact the administration.

Another of the significant disadvantages is electronic keys. Eviction must take place before 12:00 p.m. If you forget something in the room, you will have to call the administrator, because you cannot open the door with your card.





The key to success - staff

The Renaissance Moscow Monarch Center tries to satisfy its guests as much as possible. Various promotions and discounts are held by the institution for special customers. For example, newlyweds are satisfied with romance. By their arrival, the number is festively issued. However, visitors complain that the hotel does not have its own decorations. Therefore, you have to bring your decorative materials. But a young couple is usually given a bottle of champagne and chocolate.

The staff, according to the guests, is very polite and professional in their duties. Almost everyone who rested in this institution, positively evaluate the work of employees. Both the administrator and the security guard will easily and thoroughly answer all questions.

Guests note that the Renaissance Moscow Monarch Center hotel is suitable for both business negotiations and family vacations.
