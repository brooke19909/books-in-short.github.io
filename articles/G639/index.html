"Aviator"

This camp site is located in the suburbs of Samara, in the village of Tsarevshchina, on the banks of the Volga. There are several accommodation options. You can reserve rooms, for example, at the landing stage. They have a toilet, shower, fridge and TV. Vacationers can stay in a 2, 4 or 6-seater cabin landing stage.

There is also a cottage at the base, which can accommodate up to nine people. On the ground floor there is a living room with a corner sofa, an armchair, a fireplace. Here, below, is a toilet, shower; as well as a kitchen with sink, table, dishes, refrigerator.

On the second floor there is also a toilet, there is also a bathroom. Each of the two bedrooms has 1.5 double beds. The guest house has 2 rooms. Each consists of two rooms.

Those who want to save money can stay in one of 15 houses without amenities designed for three or five vacationers.





Three meals a day costs 400 rubles per day per person.

Accommodation

The houses in the Rook are built of logs, so during the hot season there is pleasant coolness inside. It is easy to breathe in this room and it is pleasant to be.

It offers vacationers - 2, 4, 3-bed rooms with private facilities, located in two- and three-story cottages.

Rates at Rook

If you choose a vacation in Samara, recreation centers will offer different prices for lodging in houses with different amenities. The room rate also depends on the season of residence.

So, if you want to rent the cottage presented above, then in April you will pay 5000 rubles per day for it. If you decide to stay with your family or company for a period of more than two days, then this same house will cost only 3500 for 24 hours of stay.

You can save even more if you come here on weekdays. Then 4 people will pay only 3,000 rubles per day. From Friday to Saturday inclusive, you will have to give already 4,500. Prices for May holidays increase. These days, renting such a house will cost you 6 thousand rubles in 24 hours.





If you are looking for the most affordable accommodation option, then on weekdays you can book a double room with private facilities on the floor in the hotel of the recreation center "Ladya". A day for renting a double room costs 1300 rubles.

Volzhanka





Anyone can come here as well. Volzhanka is a recreation center. Samara is 20 km from this camp site, so you will quickly get here.

Volzhanka can accommodate 333 people at the same time, placing them in a motel, 2-story building, summer house or 2-story cottage. The latter can accommodate 6 or 8 people. On the ground floor there is an entrance hall with furniture, a dining room equipped with a table, a kitchen set, chairs, a stove, a refrigerator and other things and equipment necessary for a comfortable stay.

This recreation center is located on the banks of the picturesque lake Krivoe. You will be able to enjoy it hourly from the windows of your room if you stay at the motel.

If you prefer privacy and increased comfort, book your place in new heated houses. Then you will have an unforgettable vacation on the Volga (Samara). Prices at this base are affordable.
