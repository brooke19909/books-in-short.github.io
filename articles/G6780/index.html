Creating Model Snaps

What is needed from the equipment:

  • the simplest camera (but not a mobile phone);
  • tripod.

You will need an assistant, although theoretically you can cope with the task yourself. Below is a step-by-step instruction for shooting snaps. Familiarize yourself with it and familiarize the assistant so that you do not have to redo the work.

Making a large portrait

Place a stool at a distance of 3-4 meters from the window. Behind your back, hang a neutral background. It can be a white sheet, only without folds, a large piece of whatman paper or fabric. Sit on a stool facing the window.

Near the window put a tripod with a camera screwed to it , the camera should be at eye level with the model sitting on a stool. An assistant is located behind the tripod.

It is important to choose the moment to shoot. It should not be a bright sunny day, when the light from the window hits the face sharply and leaves hard shadows on it. It is better that the day was cloudy, cloudy. If any lighting fixtures are lit in the room, they must all be turned off.

Check that the flash is turned off on the camera. This is a very important point! Choose a shutter release mode with a delay of 3 seconds. This is done so that the camera can take a fixed position after the shutter button is pressed, then the frames will be guaranteed to be clear. The digital zoom on the camera cannot be used, only optical (that is, you need to twist the lens to enlarge the picture, and not press the button).

Take three pictures: full face, turn in ¬æ, profile. Please note that the eyes should not be placed in the middle, but in the upper third of the frame. Look at the picture.





Correct this with the optical zoom of the camera before taking pictures. When shooting a half-turn, you need to follow the rotation of the head so that the tip of the nose does not "go" beyond the border of the cheek. In a large portrait, it is necessary that the shoulders be free of clothing.





There are not one, but several dozen photographs to be taken in each angle to choose the most successful one.
