Humidifier Boneco 7135: description, instructions, owner reviews

Equipment for regulating microclimate parameters is less and less associated with overall installations that are connected to utilities. The leading climate system companies offer compact and at the same time efficient instruments with convenient control tools. Such devices include the Boneco 7135 humidifier, designed for domestic use. Despite its modest size and low level of energy consumption, the device effectively performs its function, focusing on user-defined settings.





Humidifier principle of operation

The model is based on the principle of ultrasonic exposure. In the case of the device is a membrane, the vibrations of which break the water into small droplets. Further along the working chamber, a special Boneco 7135 fan drives air through the flow of which a foggy cloud is formed. Thanks to special filters with ion-exchange resin, the Boneco 7135 humidifier also provides a comprehensive cleaning of the aquatic environment from microbes and bacteria.

Instructions for use

It is possible to start work only after preparing the device for use. The device should be installed in a place remote from the heating equipment. In this case, it is not recommended to direct the nozzle of the device to specific objects in order to intensively moisturize them. Immediately before switching on, the tank should be filled with water whose temperature does not exceed 40 degrees. The functions are controlled through the button panel, which is equipped with the Boneco 7135 humidifier. The manual notes that in normal fogging modes the device only works until the humidity coefficient set by the user is reached. To maintain a constant generation mode, you must set the automatic format of operation by means of the corresponding button. In addition, the operator has the option of heating the fog in the range, which is also set through user settings.

Features of the Boneco 7135 model

Despite the simplicity of the functional filling, the device received many interesting technological additions. For example, a digital display with programmable controls provides high accuracy when setting the humidity level. Along with this, the tank indicator allows you to monitor the current volume of water and, if necessary, fill the tank. Provided in the model Boneco 7135 and many structural additions. Thanks to the rotary evaporator, the user can adjust the direction of the steam. But, again, the instruction does not recommend placing the device close to other objects, especially plants. Positive feedback about the model

Climatic equipment is traditionally criticized by users for high rates of energy consumption and noise operation. However, in this case, the device is spared from such shortcomings. Quiet operation, for example, was made possible by the use of a special design with rubberized legs. As for low energy consumption, it is due to its compact size and optimized principle of operation of the device. But there are other advantages that the Boneco 7135 humidifier has. Reviews, in particular, indicate the absence of a specific white precipitate on furniture that remains after the work of other representatives of the segment. Such raids are typical for humidifiers that work with water without prior preparation. In turn, the design from Boneco provides a separate stage of water purification, eliminating the generated steam from foreign impurities.

Negative reviews

With all the advantages of the design, there are also unfortunate miscalculations in its execution. Users note inconvenience in the process of performing water filling. To complete this operation, it is necessary to remove the casing from the pallet, turn it over, then turn off the filter and fill the tank. The procedure is complicated by the fact that the components do not fit together perfectly and you have to adapt to the specific installation of the Boneco 7135. Reviews with criticism apply to the heating function. Some users say that it is not needed at all, while others testify that in reality it does not justify itself.





Conclusion

In the market of climate systems, there is a division of products into two classes. One is characterized by multifunctionality, and the other involves a clear segmentation of devices for their intended purpose. The Boneco 7135 household air humidifier is more likely to fall into the second category, as it is focused on a specific task in regulating the microclimate. And this device copes with the function of increasing humidity with dignity, although the very concept of specialized devices provides all the grounds for full and effective operation. This is due precisely to the fact that the device gets rid of the burden in the form of excess functionality. However, in the case of modification 7135, there were some auxiliary options in the form of heating and water purification. And if the function of increasing the temperature of the air environment as a whole without enthusiasm was met by the consumer, then the filtration provided quite tangible advantages.
