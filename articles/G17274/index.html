Kirman Hotels Belazur Resort & SPA 5 * (Belek, Turkey)

For a whole year we look forward to and dream of a vacation. We plan ahead where we will go next time. One of the most popular countries for a beach holiday is Turkey. Young, elderly couples and families with children come here.

The Turkish Riviera includes such resort areas as Antalya, Belek, Kemer, Side and Alanya. But it is Belek that is rightfully considered the most luxurious resort on the Mediterranean coast. This resort and one of its hotels will be discussed later.

The most elite resort region of the Mediterranean coast

Belek is conveniently located in the province of Antalya, where cedar and eucalyptus trees grow, and the sea air is filled with oxygen. The resort is also widely known for its sand and pebble beaches and golf courses. Wealthy tourists come here for a relaxing holiday, among them famous foreigners and Russian celebrities.





Five-star hotels stretched for tens of kilometers on the waterfront. All hotel complexes are gorgeous: there is a large territory, several types of rooms, many bars, restaurants, infrastructure for rich leisure and a high level of service. Choosing the best hotel is not an easy task; the map of Belek hotels will help you with this.

Resort Features

Belek is a relatively young resort, active development and development began after 1984. All hotels regularly carry out cosmetic repairs, change designs and furniture. There are also very young hotels, opened between 2008 and 2014. Each of them has its own unique style and unique concept. Such features can be a large water park, the presence of fountains throughout, mirrored walls, a hotel zoo, restaurants with cuisines of different nations and other memorable factors.

In addition to Belek, the resort area includes the village of Kadriye, the town of Serik, located far from the sea, and the village of Bogazkent. There are a number of important advantages, because of which the flow of tourists to Belek does not decrease over the years, but only grows. Among them:

  • the tireless road from Antalya;
  • the map of Belek hotels is annually expanded and supplemented;
  • shallow sand and pebble beaches suitable for families with children;
  • Hotels offer a huge range of services and entertainment;
  • high standards of comfort and level of service will please even the most demanding tourists;
  • suitable for a quiet secluded holiday and family vacation;
  • here you can spend a romantic honeymoon.

How to get there

On the outskirts of the resort, a few kilometers from the town of Serik, a new hotel was opened in 2013. This is Kirman Hotels Belazur Resort & SPA 5 * (Belek, Bogazkent, Serik). From the airport of Antalya, the road to the hotel takes about 40-50 minutes.





You can get here by taxi or using the transfer service that tour operators offer. After you receive your luggage, you should go outside and find a sign with the name of your host company. You may have to stop by other hotels along the way, but the accompanying guide will tell you important and interesting information along the way, and you will get to know the surroundings.

Number of rooms

A room of any kind and size can be found in every hotel in a resort such as Belek. Photos of hotels are fascinating, showing their sophistication and elegance. Due to the fact that Kirman Hotels Belazur Resort & Spa 5 * was opened only in 2013, all the furniture and interior design is new and modern. The hotel has 480 rooms of various types.

  • Standard rooms - 401 rooms. This type of room is designed to accommodate a maximum of 3 adults and 1 child, their area is 25 square meters. They are equipped with one wide bed and an additional single bed. Of these, 5 rooms are designed for people with disabilities.




  • Family rooms - 79 rooms. They have two bedrooms, separated by a door. The total area is 36 square meters. m. Possible accommodation in these rooms - 3 adults and 2 children.




Each room has a balcony, central air conditioning, satellite TV with Russian channels, paid safe, minibar. The bathrooms include a hairdryer and bath amenities. Towels are cleaned and replaced daily. Bed linen is changed 3 times a week.

Food in restaurants and bars

For all guests, the Ultra All Inclusive food system operates, which includes daily food and drinks of local production, as well as some types of imported drinks in restaurants and bars. The main restaurant serves breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner. There is a bar in the disco, beach, pool and lobby. They work from 10 am to 2 am.





In addition to the main restaurant, a la carte establishments are available for vacationers, in which only dinners are held. Here are Asian, Ottoman and international cuisines. To get here, you must first sign up. However, not all of them are free.

A special children's menu has been prepared for children; restaurants have highchairs. And during the day, at certain times, ice cream is distributed free of charge.

Entertainment

If you plan to make a trip with the whole family, then you need to go to Belek. Hotels with children include in their concept as one of the priority areas. On the territory there are several areas for games with slides and swings, as well as a shallow pool where even the smallest can safely splash around.





The list of hotel services includes babysitting, stroller rental, as well as a developing mini-club for kids where you can leave your child. In addition, in the evening, animators and actors conduct a children's show program and a disco. Animators prepare dance numbers, paint faces on children, and then dance. Your kids will enjoy such a holiday, and vivid photos will remind you of a fun pastime.

Adults will not be bored either. In the morning, programs for all comers begin: charging, outdoor games, water aerobics, dancing, darts, evening concerts and discos. You can entertain yourself with games along with the animator, or you can play sports games, such as bowling or tennis, as well as pump up in the gym.

Vacation with children

For family holidays Belek is the best suited. 5-star hotels hire animators and nannies. If you wish, you can send the child for several hours to the playroom or pay for an individual nanny. Many families come with grandmothers or aunts who help to cope with the child during the holidays.

Animators daily hold contests, outdoor games and dances. On the territory, all playgrounds, pools and slides are safe.





In addition, in many hotels you can rent a stroller for the entire period of rest, or even for a few days or hours. The availability of this service is not always guaranteed, because on the day of arrival the stroller can be rented by other vacationers.

The restaurant serves a variety of dishes, including a special diet menu. Among such a variety, even for the most demanding parents, there is something to feed their child.

Tour Price

Experienced travelers are aware of the high cost of trips to Belek. Hotels prices are set depending not only on the level of their service, but also on the time of year and the availability of demand. The minimum cost of living for three days in the described hotel for one adult (in a standard double room) is approximately 21 thousand rubles. At the height of the season, this amount can increase twice or more.

The price of the ticket depends on the flight. For example, lovers of elite relaxation often choose a business class. Vacationers book an individual transfer to the hotel, which has a price several times higher than group transportation. In addition, the price of the tour depends on the selected dates of the trip. At the beginning and end of the season, staying in the same hotel may cost less than at the peak of the season. And when there is a special activity of visiting tourists, in July and August, prices always soar. On holidays and weekends, hotels are filled not only by tourists from Russia, but also by local residents, such days are in high demand, so the price is set different.

In order to save money, it is possible to use the early booking service. To do this, you need to determine in advance the dates of vacation and the place of residence. Booking a ticket for several months, you can get a discount of up to 40%. Last minute, last-minute tours offer good options, but in this case you need to pack in a few days or even hours.

Conclusion: when is it better to go to rest

Arriving in Turkey is affected not only by your vacation schedule, but also by your health indicators. Some people are contraindicated in the sweltering heat, which occurs in July and August. This weather is not suitable for older people or small children. During this period, it is quite difficult to go on excursions. It is better for them to choose the velvet season, when the sun's rays are not so bright, but they still warm and caress.

From early spring to the end of October, hospitable Turkey awaits you on its beaches. The prices in hotels here are different, which makes vacation possible for a person with almost any budget, so almost every our compatriot can go to this mysterious country at least once. Huge hotels, mini-hotels or boutique hotels - no matter where you spend your vacation - it is important that he gives you good emotions.
