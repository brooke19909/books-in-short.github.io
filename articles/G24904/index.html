Hotel "Volkhov" (Veliky Novgorod): description, reviews, address

In any city there is a special hotel, which can be considered the face of the village. So, if we talk about Veliky Novgorod, this is the Volkhov Hotel, where tourists and city guests who have arrived on a business trip stop.

Short hotel description

The Volkhov Hotel is simply an ideal place for those who have arrived in the city for business purposes, as well as for tourists who want to relax in comfort after a busy day of sightseeing. This is facilitated by comfortable rooms with all necessary amenities. One of the main advantages is the availability of free wireless Internet. It also has a TV, air conditioning and a luxurious bathroom.

The Volkhov Hotel (Veliky Novgorod) has a developed infrastructure that allows guests to get all the necessary services without leaving its territory. It has everything from a sauna to a hairdresser. The heart of the hotel is its reception located in the lobby. It is to its employees that guests can turn with any question or request, as well as complain about service deficiencies.

If you want to try the best dishes of Russian cuisine in the original interpretations of professional chefs, the Volkhov Hotel (Veliky Novgorod) will provide you with such an opportunity. The restaurant also offers a set lunch or lunch box in case you are planning a trip. Room service is also available.





Hotel Location

In the central part of the city is the Volkhov Hotel (Veliky Novgorod). The address of the establishment is Predtechenskaya Street, 24. The Kremlin and Sofiyskaya Embankment, which can be considered one of the main attractions , are located very close. From here, about an hour drive to the train station and about 3 - to the airport.

Standard rooms

The Volkhov Hotel (Veliky Novgorod) provides its guests with the following accommodation options:

  • The standard single room is 13 square meters. m. There is an orthopedic one and a half bed, as well as other furniture for a comfortable stay. The soft carpet will not let you freeze, even if you move around the room without slippers.
  • Double "standard" is designed to accommodate guests on a pair of one and a half beds. Given that they are almost identical in area to the previous category, it is worth noting that the living space here will be an order of magnitude smaller.




Superior rooms

The following categories of apartments are distinguished by increased comfort:

  • Superior room is a fairly spacious and bright room with a huge double bed. There is also a folding soft sofa. Business people will appreciate the presence of a comfortable working area.
  • Family apartments are planned in the form of a studio of 30 square meters. m. There is a double bed and a large sofa bed, and therefore it can accommodate 4 people. The room also has a small dining area.




Executive apartments

For especially demanding guests, the Volkhov Hotel (Novgorod the Great) can offer the following accommodation options:

  • Junior Suite is a huge two-room apartment. The bedroom has a large bed, as well as a large wardrobe. If we talk about the living room, it is quite compact and consists of a soft corner and a coffee table.
  • The suite has a huge area, as well as an incredibly elegant design. There is beautiful new furniture, as well as luxurious decor. The bathroom includes a hydromassage.
  • Duplex - these are chic rooms that occupy two floors at once. The first level is occupied by a spacious living room with dining room, and on the second - a bedroom with panoramic windows and an attached bathroom.

Room Amenities

Hotel "Volkhov" in Veliky Novgorod offers guests accommodation in comfortable rooms that are equipped with the following list of amenities:

  • individual air conditioning to create a comfortable microclimate in the apartment;
  • Modern flat-screen TV with many satellite channels;
  • an electric kettle that allows you to boil water for hot drinks at any time;
  • landline telephone connected to the international line;
  • bathroom with bath amenities, hairdryer and cosmetics;
  • an iron and ironing board so you can always tidy your things.




Hotel facilities

The Volkhov Hotel will provide you with the opportunity to take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • the main emphasis in the restaurant is on traditional Russian dishes, which will be especially interesting for foreign tourists;
  • a private guarded parking lot is provided for guests;
  • the bar always has a lot of high-quality alcoholic drinks, as well as the opportunity to sing karaoke;
  • You can take a steam bath in the sauna to relieve stress after a busy and busy day;
  • an ATM has been installed in the lobby to withdraw cash from plastic cards;
  • fans of billiards will be happy to have a special game room;
  • You can put your hair and nails in order using the services of a hairdresser;
  • for special occasions a banquet hall is provided;
  • your soiled or crumpled things will be put in order in the laundry;
  • the elevator will help you comfortably get from the upper floors;
  • in the library you can find interesting books to your taste;
  • the conference room has all the amenities for business meetings;
  • suitcases can be taken to the left-luggage office.




Service at the reception

The Volkhov Hotel is attentive to its guests, and therefore, by contacting the reception, they can use the following services:

  • You can pre-order a taxi call service;
  • there is a terminal for paying for services by bank transfer;
  • Reception staff around the clock ready to answer your questions, as well as respond to requests and complaints;
  • You have the opportunity to order the delivery of fresh print media;
  • by contacting the reception staff, you can book a city tour;
  • foreign citizens can get assistance in obtaining and renewing a visa;
  • You have the opportunity to order the service "alarm", so as not to oversleep an important event;
  • it is possible to make a photocopy of documents or print them from electronic media.

Additional services

Hotel "Volkhov" in Novgorod provides guests with the following list of additional services:

  • catering (organization of catering for events);
  • delivery of dishes from the restaurant to the room;
  • Transfer to the airport, train station or tourist facilities.

Additional Information

Tourists should read the following additional hotel information:

  • one day prior to arrival, it is recommended to inform your estimated time of arrival so that your room is prepared in advance;
  • for foreign guests, a mandatory condition for registration is the availability of a valid migration card, as well as a visa;
  • checking in with children, their birth certificates must be provided;
  • Foreign citizens pay the registration fee.




Positive reviews

A huge number of tourists are attracted by the Volkhov Hotel. reviews about it contain a huge number of positive comments:

  • decent breakfast buffet;
  • very convenient location of the hotel;
  • new plumbing and tile in the bathroom;
  • advantageous offers for a comprehensive meal in a restaurant;
  • Friendly and very helpful staff;
  • the rooms are maintained just perfect cleanliness (the same applies to all other hotel rooms);
  • the hotel building has a rather interesting museum, the exhibits of which are things forgotten by guests;
  • in most cases, guests are settled much earlier than the checkout time;
  • beautiful decoration in the restaurant, as well as live music in the evenings;
  • good soundproofing in the apartment.




Negative reviews

In addition to its advantages, the Volkhov Hotel (Veliky Novgorod) also has drawbacks. Reviews reflect the following negative points:

  • the quality of food in the restaurant does not correspond to high prices;
  • no desserts served for breakfast;
  • prices for excursions at the hotel are much higher than at any agency;
  • paid parking;
  • standard rooms have very old and shabby furniture;
  • very unreliable front door, and therefore scared to leave valuables in the apartment;
  • in the bathroom the lock quite often sticks, and therefore you need to go swimming with the phone in order to be able to call the staff for help;
  • clogged sewer pipes, due to which water flows very slowly in the sink;
  • the numbers are cramped;
  • in many apartments there is no closet, but there is only a hanger;
  • bad coffee for breakfast;
  • There are no shelves for your cosmetics in the shower.

General impression

The main advantage of the Volkhov Hotel is its convenient location in the central historical part of the city. This opens up many opportunities for both tourists and business people. The first will be pleased that many attractions are within walking distance. The latter will appreciate the huge number of offices and business centers. There is also a pretty good traffic intersection. Nevertheless, you will have to overpay for such a convenient location (on the outskirts of the city, similar apartments will cost several times cheaper).

Speaking of rooms, it is worth noting that they are a bit cramped (with the exception of single rooms). Even in large apartments, there is very little living space due to a pile of furniture. By the way, speaking about the latter, it is worth noting that in standard rooms it is not in the best condition. In general, the accommodation conditions here are quite comfortable. It is especially worth noting the perfect cleanliness of the rooms, which is ensured by a thorough, conscientious daily cleaning.

Buffet breakfast is included. The dishes are quite satisfying and varied, but sweet tooth will be disappointed. There are also quite advantageous offers regarding integrated nutrition. But if you want to place an order on the menu, you will be very disappointed with the discrepancy in price and quality. Therefore, for a romantic dinner, it is better to find another place.
