Sanatoriums of the Kemerovo region. Description, services

On the beautiful expanses of the northern part of Russia, in the Kemerovo region, there are a large number of sanatoriums, boarding houses, children's medical institutions for those who want to relax and improve their health. The most popular resorts in the Kemerovo region will be presented in this article.

"Kedrovy Bor" - a cozy health resort in the Kemerovo region





The Kedrovy Bor preventive clinic is located in a picturesque place near the city of Kemerovo and is considered one of the best health resorts in the Kemerovo region. The special microclimate and originality give the wellness center a convenient location away from settlements. The territory of the sanatorium, drowning in a forested area, silence and fragrant aromas of cedar forest, a beach on the lake is a great place for treatment and relaxation.

Services

Sanatorium "Kedrovy Bor" (Kemerovo region) offers its guests many medical and wellness treatments. This includes mud treatment, and water treatment procedures, a sauna, physical procedures, and cosmetologist services. For lovers of outdoor activities in the sanatorium there are gyms with fitness equipment, swimming pools where you can work out and enjoy swimming both by yourself and under the guidance of an experienced trainer. Even in inclement weather, vacationers of the sanatorium can always find things to do: visit the library, use the services of a hairdresser or cosmetologist, enjoy a cup of aromatic coffee in a cafe or bar, which are located on the territory of the sanatorium.
