Xanthe Resort Hotel 5 * (Side, Turkey): reviews, description and photo

Xanthe Resort Hotel belongs to the renowned Emir Hotels family of hotels. This can be considered a kind of guarantee of good rest and quality service.

a brief description of

Xanthe Resort Hotel is a modern resort complex, which received its first guests in 2004. It positions itself as an institution for family vacations, where it will be fun and comfortable for everyone - from the smallest travelers and active youth to the elderly. The hotel occupies 23,000 square meters. m

It is worth noting that in its activities Xanthe Resort Hotel is guided by the principle of environmental protection. In addition, the staff works to ensure that the quality of service at the hotel is constantly improving. Not surprisingly, the hotel has won many international awards, including the Red Star Award (2009), Top Hotel Award (2012), Quality Selection (2014). Also in 2014, the institution received the title of the best sports hotel in Turkey.

Xanthe Resort Hotel - room description

Comfortable living conditions - this is one of the most important components of quality relaxation. Xanthe Resort Hotel 5 * can offer you the following accommodation options:

  • Standard rooms are a unique combination of functional simplicity and cozy luxury. You can sit on a double or separate orthopedic beds, and an additional guest can lie on a sofa bed. There is also a cosmetic table in the room. Access to the balcony is through a sliding glass door. The bathroom has a modern shower. Four standard rooms are equipped according to the needs of people with disabilities.
  • Large groups or travelers with children can stay in a spacious family room. The main bedroom is quite spacious. In its size and equipment, it completely repeats the standard number. The second bedroom is much smaller. It has two small beds, a bedside table and a TV. The room has a large bathroom with a shower.
  • Suite is a spacious three-room suite, which is also ideal for families. These apartments differ from the previous category by the presence of a living room, a small dining area and two bathrooms. From the balcony you can enjoy the azure sea or the blue pool.

Hotel Restaurants

Delicious and wholesome food provides its guests with Xanthe Resort Hotel. A restaurant is not just a place to eat hearty. Here you can also have a romantic date, business negotiations or just organize friendly gatherings. Be sure to visit the following places:

  • The main restaurant serves a buffet with the best dishes of Turkish and international cuisine three times a day. You can eat deliciously in a luxuriously decorated bright room or in the fresh air.
  • Restaurant Lagos introduces you to all the subtleties of Mediterranean cuisine. The elegant interior of the hall is decorated in white with blue accents that resemble the sea breeze. The institution is open in the evening (from 19:00 to 21:00).
  • The cozy Saffron restaurant will immerse you in the fantastic world of spices. This establishment specializes in Anatolian cuisine. A pleasant impression and a special atmosphere are created by oriental patterns in the interior.
  • If you are attracted to the barbecue smoke and the aromas of fried meat, head to the outdoor grill restaurant. All furniture here and other interior items are made of natural wood.
  • You can enjoy the taste of fresh seafood in a cozy fish restaurant. This establishment, located on the Cote d'Azur, is an ideal place for a romantic date.
  • If you prefer sweets, then your favorite establishment will be a cozy pastry shop, which is open from 10:00 to 18:00. Here you can taste traditional Turkish sweets, as well as light French desserts and hearty cream cakes.
  • From noon until 16:00, a pancake house is open in the garden. This is a great place to satisfy your hunger in the afternoon. It serves not only pancakes with different fillings, but also delicious gozleme cakes - one of the most popular dishes in Turkey.

Helpful information

One of the most popular destinations for travelers is Turkey. Xanthe Resort Hotel 5 * is an excellent accommodation option, where your vacation will be held at the highest level. Before you travel, read the following helpful information:

  • on the day of departure, guests must leave their rooms before noon, and from 14:00 the arrival of newly arrived guests begins;
  • You can use the services of early registration and late check-out only if there are a sufficient number of free apartments (for an additional fee);
  • children under 6 years old stay in rooms for free, provided that they do not need a cot, and children under 2 years old are provided with cots;
  • placing an additional guest in a room will cost 70% of the total cost of living;
  • this institution does not provide for the possibility of rest with pets;
  • You can pay with a plastic card for the services received.
