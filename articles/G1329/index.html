Quarry swimming

Finally

For those traveling to the Kurlevsky quarries for the first time, it is important to understand that Donzo is a lake located in a nature reserve and does not have an asphalt highway leading to it. An unpleasant surprise for "refined" tourists may be an uncomfortable access to the reservoir. A dirt road leading to quarries is a difficult test for a car, and every year it only gets worse. In addition, driving close to the water will not be possible, because about twenty meters from the shore there is a moat that cannot be overcome and located near the water with a car. In addition, do not forget about the status of the reserve assigned to the Donzo tract. It imposes certain obligations and responsibilities on vacationers: fishing is officially prohibited here, and it is also impossible to cut the forest.
