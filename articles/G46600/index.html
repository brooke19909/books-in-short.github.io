What can be made of long balls: instructions for beginners

How the balloon for modeling is inflated

It is difficult to inflate such long thin balloons, which some call sausages, sausages or modeling balls, especially if the products are new. What can be done from long balls for beginners we will consider later, but now we will learn how to inflate them correctly.

The easiest way to act is a special pump that is designed for balloons. If you don‚Äôt have one, then use a bicycle, just put on the nipple cap. Before inflating, be sure to rub it in your hands, pull the ends in different directions, so that the rubber is slightly warmed up to avoid tearing with a sharp jolt of air.

The ball hole must be pulled onto the pump and inflated object with light movements. Be sure to leave 10 or 15 cm deflated at the end so that when the figure is twisted, excess air can move into empty places without problems.

If you decide to inflate such an elastic ball with your mouth, then be patient. Do not forget to warm the rubber in your hands by friction and stretching before work. Act in parts, covering with your hand every 5 cm of the ball. When air fills this space, push it deeper with your fingers and inflate again.

At the end, let the air out a little from the tight ring and tie a knot to the ‚Äúinflatable‚Äù edge. To do this, wrap the rubber in a loop around the finger and insert the edge inward.

Dog twisting pattern

If you are thinking about what can be made of long balls, then we can offer the easiest and most popular craft - a dog. A step-by-step scheme of twisting such a figure shows the order of its execution.

After inflation, be sure to leave an empty space at the end. Next, you need to perform the first three turns around, squeezing the ball at the bend points with your fingers. Usually 2-3 turns is enough.

In the figure under No. 3 you can already recognize the face of the beast. The following three details will depict the front legs. The remaining movements are necessary for the formation of the hind legs and tail.

What can be made of long balls? Different dogs. If you leave a large length for the body, then you get a dachshund. If you leave small sections for the ears, you get a curly poodle head. The tail can be made long and short or the air can be shifted to the end, and a little empty space is left in front of it. You get a trimmed poodle tail.

Giraffe

Knowing the principle of twisting a rubber product, what can be made of long balloons yet? We can advise you to create a giraffe figure. Act similarly to making a dog, only to create a head, twist small segments, and for the neck - the long part of the ball.

When the figure has turned out, her child can independently paint with markers, making spots brown or black.

Circus elephant

The following step-by-step diagram shows how an elephant is twisted from a gray ball. After the figure is made, the master must attach it to a small inflated ball of white color.

This round object will depict a large circus ball on which an elephant stands. A child can paint it at his discretion. In our sample, a star was painted on it.

Crown of two balls

What can be made of long balloons for a birthday? On his birthday, you can create an original crown from two different color balloons. In this outfit, the meeting of the guests will be more fun. A detailed diagram is available in the photo below. You need to act in order of increasing numbers.

Each twist is performed 2 times. The crown consists of three pointed corners, between which tiny balls are made. The central triangle is distinguished by a larger size. The rim around the head is first checked with the size of the child, and after the final fitting, the last bundle is tied with thread or twisting.

Indian headdress

On the birthday of the boy, you can organize a game of Indians by appointing a birthday leader. He is supposed to make a magnificent headdress of large feathers. The photo clearly shows that their role is played by multi-colored bright balls.

The bezel is made of neutral white color. The remnants of the balls hang freely below the head. The balls are screwed into the rim at an equal distance from each other both in front and on the occipital side of the headgear.

Flower

What can be made of long balloons as a present for a birthday person or for decorating a room for celebrating an anniversary? Flowers made from sausage balls will not fade and will stand for quite some time. This craft can be presented to each girl before going home. The child will be very satisfied.

How to make a flower from "sausages" is shown in detail in the photo above. It will take two balls, one of which must be green to create a stem and leaves.
