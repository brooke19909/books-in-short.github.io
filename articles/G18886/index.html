Knitting summer tops: patterns, patterns, description

Summer tops with knitting needles for the fair sex this season have become very popular. Many women and girls wear them. Age is not a hindrance. And this is by no means a strange choice: summer tops for women, knitting connected, always practical and comfortable. But the main thing is that you can make them yourself if you wish.

Cotton is the ideal yarn choice because it is good for clothing in both warm and cold seasons. This suggests that the top can be worn not only in the summer, but also worn under a jacket in the fall, creating a slightly romantic outfit from the strict look. Moreover, such tops, worn under a jacket, can be worn not only at work. They are suitable for going to the theater, in a cafe, and will come in handy for receiving guests. Let's try to figure out how they fit.

How to put together?

Probably, it has already become clear that summer tops can be knitted with needles, with some effort. In order to try on a new thing, it is necessary to combine all the details. First you need to make shoulder seams. The back of the product should be treated with purple thread in three circular rows of connecting posts. In this case, from the second circular row, you should enter the prepared hook exclusively for the back backs of the loops of the previous row. The edges of the armhole and the neck of the product are tied with a purple thread - one circular row will be enough - using single crochet and connecting posts.
