Interchangeable Optics Cameras: Overview, Features and Specifications

Quality cameras have always been in price. And even despite the advanced cameras of smartphones, phones can never replace a classic camera. No matter what marketers say. They are generally not worth listening to, since their task is to increase sales at any cost. Nothing can replace interchangeable lens cameras. Therefore, this type of device will never die out. At the moment, mirrorless models of cameras dominate the market. It is understandable. These devices do not require professional skills to work with them. However, classic SLRs have not gone away. SLR Cameras

These sophisticated devices are designed for professional photographers. However, some lovers can use them. Unless, of course, they know what exposure, exposure, focal length and other terrible terms are. The principle of operation of DSLRs is based on the complex design of the lenses that are in the camera itself. They transmit the image already to a digital sensor. This is how the classic SLR works. Its feature is lenses. They allow you to achieve maximum quality. However, in order to cope with such a technique, you need to take special courses. Well, or take a couple of hundred thousand photos without any lessons. Then mastery will come. These cameras traditionally top the rating of interchangeable lens cameras. Even despite the growing popularity of mirrorless models. Knowledgeable people will never choose the latter, if there is an opportunity to buy a high-quality DSLR. And in this they are right. But enough about these cool cameras. Let's move on to simplified models.

Mirrorless cameras

These devices were invented relatively recently. They combine the ease of operation of simple digital soap dishes and the ability to use a top-end lens from some professional Nikon. Of course, in this case, the technology is used much more advanced than in conventional soap dishes, but there is one principle: these cameras do not have lenses. The image from the lens here immediately hits the digital matrix. These cameras have a number of advantages over DSLRs: they are much smaller in size, much easier to use and great for traveling. However, professionals are not yet striving to switch to mirrorless. Since the quality will not be the same anyway. However, these are also cameras with interchangeable lenses. And therefore it will be necessary to consider them in this review. However, enough lyrics. Let's move on to the review.

No. 1. Nikon D750 Body

Opens our SLR rating (which was expected). Nikon interchangeable lens camera. This model is very actively used by both professionals and amateurs. And despite the fact that the camera comes exclusively. Without a lens. The manufacturers had the expectation that beginners would definitely not buy this device, and professionals and advanced amateurs have long had their own set of favorite lenses. I must say that this approach allows you to filter out those who cannot afford this device. Nikon D750 Body is the most affordable professional-grade full-frame SLR. It has a high-quality CMOS sensor with a resolution of 24 megapixels, a bright viewfinder, a rotary screen, a Wi-Fi transmitter and the ability to record directly to two memory cards. And the camera can shoot high-quality Full HD video. A real find for both professional and amateur. This is clearly one of the best interchangeable lens cameras. This unit has an advanced (and well-established) CMOS matrix with a resolution of 18 megapixels. But despite this, the camera is able to produce excellent detailed and clear pictures. And with a top-end lens, she will not have a price at all. Many professionals choose products from Canon. True, those are a class higher and much more difficult in terms of management. And this model is just suitable for a novice photographer. The main thing is to learn how to manage it (which is not difficult at all) and acquire a couple of good lenses for all occasions. Then you can try yourself in real photography, and eventually go to a higher level. But we were distracted. We continue the review.

Top mirrorless, which even professionals in the field of photography do not shy from using. No. 5. Fujifilm X-H1

This mirrorless camera can rightfully be called the flagship. And her price hints at it. This is one of the best interchangeable lens cameras from Fujifilm. But these are trifles. In fact, the camera is much cooler. It has the option of optical stabilization, burst mode, the ability to record video in 4K with progressive scan and a speed of 60 frames per second. And she can write Slo-Mo at a speed of 120 frames per second. The crown of everything is lightning fast autofocus. There are also a huge number of manual settings. This camera is the best of interchangeable lens cameras. However, the price of this miracle of engineering is appropriate. It is understandable. Such an advanced camera cannot cost a penny. But the main advantage of the device is high-quality images with excellent depth of field and excellent color reproduction. By the way, the D3300 is the lightest camera in its segment.
