Architectural features of the complex

LCD Falcon Fort consists of only one building, similar to a castle made in the Gothic style. The house is divided into 5 sections with variable number of storeys. Three towers with a number of floors from 2 to 17-story, one - 19-story. The buildings are interconnected by two inset houses 13 floors high.

Below ground level - under the complex and part of the local area - there is a two-level underground parking for 152 cars.

Terms of Acquisition of Real Estate in Sokolinoy Fort

You can buy an apartment in the Sokoliniy Fort residential complex under a preliminary contract for the sale of real estate. You can take housing by installments subject to a down payment of 50-60% of the total cost or get a mortgage from one of the four partner banks.
