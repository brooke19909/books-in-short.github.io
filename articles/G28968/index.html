Krestovaya Pad (Listvyanka): address, contacts, description of rooms, infrastructure, photos and reviews

Listvyanka is a small settlement in the Irkutsk region. The urban-type settlement got its name thanks to the larch trees that grow in the area. Listvyanka is located on Lake Baikal, so there are often many tourists here. You can settle in the village in the hotel complex "Cross Pad" (Listvyanka). The article will discuss what rooms and services are available at this hotel. How to spend your vacation in this place and what to do. What excursions and types of recreation are offered in the hotel complex.

Hotel complex "Cross Pad" (Listvyanka): address

This hotel complex is located right on the shores of the beautiful Lake Baikal. Mailing address: Irkutsk region, Listvyanka village, Gornaya street, 14a. Infrastructure

There are 2 restaurants in the complex all year round - Baikal Fairy Tale and Krestovaya Pad. They treat guests with a variety of cuisines, as well as exclusive dishes from the chef. There is another popular place where tourists like to eat - a grill bar. From here comes the wonderful aroma of fried meat. Therefore, walking along the lake, I want to look here.

Hotel "Cross Pad" (Listvyanka) provides the opportunity to relax and improve in the bath and sauna on the territory of the complex. Almost all guests like the Finnish sauna. After the "hot" procedures, everyone can swim in the pool. The complex also has a massage room. It is worth signing up for this procedure in advance, because there are a lot of people who want to.

The hotel also has a spacious conference room with all the necessary equipment. That is why various companies often hold negotiations and meetings here, after which guests can relax in comfortable rooms and eat in the restaurant.

As an entertainment, you can rent bicycles for the whole family and friends. You can also visit an organized tour of the nearby attractions.

Most guests are pleased that free Wi-Fi is available throughout the complex. During registration (at the entrance) you will be given all the necessary data to access it.

In the rooms, pets are allowed only by prior arrangement with the administration. Children under 5 years old stay in apartments for free, but without a separate bed.

Leisure

In the winter season, guests can have fun riding a dog sled with a professional instructor. There is a rental of ATVs and snowmobiles in Listvyanka. Also here you can rent skis, a snowboard and a tube.

For those who prefer a more relaxing holiday - horseback riding and billiards. Diving, fishing and a boat trip will not leave indifferent any tourist. Hotel "Cross Pad" (Listvyanka) provides these services at any time of the year.

If you want to see the unusual and unique natural phenomena of Lake Baikal, then you should visit a trip to the Baikal Limnological Museum. Do not miss the opportunity to visit the Taltsy architectural and ethnographic museum and the wooden St. Nicholas Church.

Rooms

All buildings are equipped with three types of equipment: standard, studio and suite. In any apartment you can deliver an extra bed if necessary.

Standard

For guests in the standard room, the following furniture and appliances can be used: a bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer, an electric kettle, a TV, a double bed and two nightstands, a wardrobe and a desk.

Studio

Studio rooms, in addition to the above, have a refrigerator and bathrobes for guests. They also have original lighting of ceiling and wall type.

Suite

The deluxe room has created more comfortable and close to everyday life conditions. Here, in addition to a double bed or 2 single beds with two bedside tables and a wardrobe, there is an entrance hall, a sofa and two armchairs. A bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, an electric kettle, a refrigerator and a plasma panel are also present. In addition, the room has a safe for storing valuables and a telephone.
