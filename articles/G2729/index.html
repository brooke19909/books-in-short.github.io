Hotel Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino & Aquagames 5 * in Punta Cana (Dominican Republic): reviews

In this article, we will take a comprehensive look at the Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino Aquagames. As we can understand from the name, this five-star resort has a casino and a water park. Often, Sirensis in Punta Cana is called a spa hotel. This is also true. But what services does he provide yet? Where is the hotel located, what is its beach and other leisure facilities? We will talk about this below.



Where is the hotel located

Going to the Dominican Republic, you do not count on a budget vacation. Yes, and Punta Canu can not be called a cheap resort. But in this case, I want to get a full range of various services for my money. And it is at the Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino. This hotel complex is located fifty kilometers from Punta Cana Airport, literally on the most magnificent beach in the vicinity, called Uvero Alto Macau. The nearest town Iguei is located forty-five kilometers from the hotel. The hotel complex Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino consists of two parts. These are the Sirenis Cocotal Beach Resort and the Sirenis Tropical Suites Casino and Spa. The vast territory is a garden in which there are two- and three-story buildings. From the sea, a four-meter sandy beach adjoins the hotel complex. The hotel arose in 2002. But since then he has undergone several repairs. The last reconstruction took place in 2010. The complex belongs to the Sirenis family.



Rooms

Beautiful bungalows at the Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino hotel complex fill the rooms of the standard, deluxe and suite categories. All rooms adjoin balconies-loggias with a visor made of palm branches. In addition to air conditioning, a fan rotates under the high ceiling with blades. In the standard room in the bathroom there is a shower cabin, in the suites there is a jacuzzi bath. What pleased the tourists was that the electricity in the room did not turn off when the hosts left the room. So you can leave the air conditioning and come from the beach or lunch to a cool room. The technical equipment of the rooms fully corresponds to the five-star status of the hotel. In addition to a safe, a desk, a hairdryer, cosmetic accessories and other things, there are a refrigerator and a coffee maker among the devices. This is very convenient, taking into account that the hotel only serves breakfast. As you know, plug sockets in the Dominican Republic require an adapter for European electrical appliances. An adapter is already in the room. A trifle, but nice. They also give an electric fumigant against mosquitoes, which, by the way, are many.

Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino includes breakfast. Regarding these morning meals, tourists leave only positive reviews. They are served in the two main restaurants of the complex according to the buffet system. There were always a lot of fruits, vegetables, sweets, as well as hot dishes of fish and meat. It was not at all like breakfasts in other hotels, where besides omelet and scrambled eggs with sausages, they do not give anything. Many tourists booked half board, full board and all inclusive. Beach and Pools

When you relax in the Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino 5 *, it may seem like you are in the realm of flowing turquoise water. Four adult pools have varying depths and bizarre shapes. They have hydromassages. Two indoor pools. Cute bridges are thrown over these artificial lagoons. For children there are two more pools. Uvero Alto Macau Beach is the epitome of the Bounty commercial without Photoshop. By the way, all types of non-motorized water recreation are free for hotel guests. If you go to the beach along the path past the pool system, then along the way there will be a towel dispenser. They can be changed at least a hundred times a day. Sun beds and umbrellas on the beach a lot. An additional shadow is given by a palm grove right on the shore. The sand on the beach is not white, but cream. Sometimes the sea brings very beautiful shells to it.



Water park and casino

Now it's time to find out why the hotel is called Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino Aquagames. Few tourists are lucky to relax next to the seven most complicated water slides. And, by the way, not everyone dares to go down them. For children, too, there are slides, not at all scary. Around these water attractions, like on the beach and near the pools, there are sun loungers, umbrellas. There is also a restaurant. The water park has its own territory, not related to the hotel. It is located to the right of the reception. However, guests of the Sirenis Punta Cana Resort Casino 5 * hotel have free admission. An alley full of shops leads to the water park. And if you go along the path to the left of the reception, then you will get to the casino and the amphitheater. Animation at the hotel is open day and night. And not only for adults. A child from four to twelve years old can be sent to a mini club, where he will be entertained by animators for children. Many tourists praise the evening discos and shows. Animation guys are doing their best.
