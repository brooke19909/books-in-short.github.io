Where is Barcelona located in which country?

The city is located in the north-east of Spain, on the Mediterranean coast. Geographically, Barcelona is located in Catalonia, is the capital of this autonomous region and the province of the same name. This is the largest commercial and industrial center of the country, the most important port of the Mediterranean Sea. Also, the city is not the last in tourist routes in Europe. Barcelona is just 120 km from the border with France.





Historical reference

The exact date of the formation of Barcelona is impossible to determine, but, according to scientists, it was founded more than 2000 years ago. First, a settlement called Barkenon appeared on the site of the modern city. Then it was only a coastal plain surrounded by a ridge. The geographical position of Barcelona was such that it connected Central Europe with the rest of the Iberian Peninsula, and therefore was of great strategic importance.

As for the reasons for the appearance of the settlement and, in fact, the founders, there is no definite answer. Two versions are considered, each of which has the right to exist. According to Carthaginian legend, Barcelona was founded in 230 BC by Amilcar Barka, who created a settlement near Mount Montjuic, naming it, as was already written above, Barkenon.

A more romantic Roman legend, according to which the area was named Barca Nona, which translates as "ninth boat." The story dates back to Jason's search for his Golden Fleece. The expedition consisted of nine ships that were scattered across the sea due to a severe storm. When they managed to get together again, it turned out that eight boats remained. Then Jason asked Hercules to find the ninth ship. An ancient Greek hero discovered a crew at the foot of Montjuic. People who found shelter there after a shipwreck loved this area so much that they decided to stay here, symbolically calling the settlement Barca Nona.

Barcelona climate and weather conditions throughout the year

The weather in the capital of Catalonia is perfect for a comfortable stay at any time of the year. In summer there is no depressing heat and it is dry, autumn and spring relatively warm, but humid. In winter it is cool, western cold winds prevail with little precipitation. But even at this time, the temperature almost never drops below 0 degrees.

Spring lasts from March to May, at which time the temperature gradually rises, the number of light hours per day increases. From June to September - summer.
