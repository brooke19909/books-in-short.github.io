How to decorate a do-it-yourself phone case: recommendations and interesting ideas

Today in stores you can find a wide variety of phone cases. However, people in the 21st century strive for individuality in everything, especially since such a small accessory can tell a lot about its owner. That is why many people want to know how to decorate a phone case with their own hands.





What are

Before you learn how to decorate a phone case with your own hands, you should get acquainted with their varieties. So, today everyone can purchase leather, silicone, knitted and fabric cases for mobile phones. Each of them has its advantages and disadvantages.

For example, leather cases are durable due to their quality and look great even without any jewelry, but they are quite expensive and also not very reliable in terms of protecting the gadget from mechanical damage.

Plastic variants are the worst that can withstand impacts, which often fall apart when they fall, but they are popular due to their originality.

Silicone cases, or, as they are also called, bumpers, are the most common model, with virtually no flaws. The reason is the properties of silicone itself, which has such qualities as elasticity, strength and reliability.

There are also knitted and fabric cases that women and girls like, but they are unlikely to protect the smartphone in the fall.
