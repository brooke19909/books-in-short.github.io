Mesut Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

One of the resorts in Turkey, which has not ceased to attract tourists for many decades in a row, is the cozy town of Alanya, which successfully combines solitude with nature, noisy night clubs, crystal clear waters and a number of attractions interesting to visit. Every traveler will find something to his liking.

An important factor for the influx of tourists is the subtropical climate, which allows you to take sea baths from April to November. Comfortable accommodation, a hospitable welcome and high-quality service are combined within the walls of the four-star hotel complex Masut Hotel.





general characteristics

The building of the Mesut Hotel 4 *, the description of which will be presented to your attention below, was erected in 1976. It was then that the first guests crossed the threshold of this institution for a long-awaited vacation. The last renovation was carried out in 2013: the facade and decoration were completely updated, the rooms were reconstructed. Despite the fact that the nearest international airport is located 125 km away, the number of visitors is constantly growing. Settlement with animals is unacceptable. There are one apartment for the disabled. Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout. The staff speaks four languages: English, German, Russian and Turkish. This eliminates the complexity of the language barrier. The standard check-in time is 14:00. Payments are accepted by cards of international banking systems. If necessary, an extra bed can be added to the room.

Accommodation

The number of rooms is represented by standard category apartments in the amount of 131 units, each area is 20 square meters. Maximum capacity - three people. The interior has a luxurious design in a classic style. Snow-white walls and multi-level ceilings, combined with turquoise curtains and bedspreads, give the apartment an expensive look. The walls are decorated with bright paintings, vases filled with flowers. The floors are covered with carpet or laminate. There is access to a balcony equipped with chairs and a table. From the French windows to the floor, views of the unsurpassed picturesque landscapes of the nearby mountains and the endless sea open.

Tidying up is a daily routine that tidy maids perform. Bed linen is changed three times a week. Newspapers and food delivery service is provided around the clock, but requires an additional charge.





Degree of comfort

In order for the residence to be carefree and comfortable, the administration made a lot of efforts, equipping the rooms with everything necessary. The furniture set is made of high quality wood. It includes double or single beds, a wardrobe with mirrored doors, a desk with a pouffe, bedside tables.

The presence of an individual air conditioning system allows you to maintain the required microclimate temperature. A liquid crystal television will let you keep abreast of world events, and Russian-language channels will delight your favorite TV show. The phone is free to contact the reception. In the case of international calls, payment for use must be made in advance. Wi-Fi is free and available everywhere.

To ensure peace of mind, a safe is provided that will keep important documents and valuables in complete safety. Comfortable living is facilitated by the presence of a refrigerator and an electric kettle, which makes it possible, upon awakening, to meet the bright rays of the sun with a cup of aromatic coffee or healthy tea.





Bathroom

The bathroom at the Mesut Hotel 4 * (Turkey) is decorated with light-colored ceramic tiles. Equipped with a large mirror, built-in marble sink, hairdryer, bath or shower. There are personal hygiene products: shampoo, shower gel and soap. The number of snow-white terry towels complies with international standards.

Supply system

The Mesut Hotel 4 * organizes meals according to the All-Inclusive concept. In accordance with it, five meals a day are carried out according to the buffet system according to the previously established schedule: everyone wants to independently put their favorite dishes in the required amount on a plate, and then goes for consumption at any free table. The variety of culinary masterpieces, as experienced, is great. Professional chefs put a piece of their soul into every masterpiece.

The main restaurant has a strict indoor hall with many columns, sconces and spotlights, an open summer terrace with views of the endless scenic expanses. Table setting - at the highest level, the furniture is upholstered and comfortable, which contributes to a pleasant meal in a friendly atmosphere.





Features of the All Inclusive System

The concept is valid from 10:00 to 23:00. It includes the consumption of soft drinks and local alcohol. Import drinks, wines, freshly squeezed juice and Turkish coffee require an additional payment. In addition to the main food, in the daytime a feeling of slight hunger can be quenched in a snack bar by consuming a number of delicious snacks.

The hotel complex operates a system of bars: by the pool, on the beach, in the restaurant.

Mesut Hotel 4 *: beach

Only the carriageway separates the hotel from the promenade. Having overcome the distance of 100 meters, using the underpass, tourists find themselves on a public sandy and pebble beach. The entrance to the waters of the Mediterranean is gentle, which provides a safe swim for babies.

The use of sun loungers with umbrellas requires payment. There is no beach towel service. However, despite this, the beach infrastructure is well developed. There are showers, changing cabins, a lifeguard service.





Leisure by the sea

Not all vacationers love passive relaxation in the form of sunbathing under the sultry rays of the sun. For those who are athletic, active and full of energy, a volleyball court is offered on which friendly teams of strangers gather daily to achieve a common goal - victory. New acquaintances and easy communication are guaranteed to everyone.

A large number of water sports are available in the coastal area. The most popular are riding a banana and water scooters, you can race in a circle behind a boat or parachute behind a motor boat.

Among extreme activities, windsurfing and diving are in demand. The first allows you to merge together with the deep sea and subdue surging waves. Immersion in the deep spaces in the open sea leaves an unforgettable experience and gives delightful emotions to anyone who dares to do it. And it is available to absolutely everyone: both an experienced diver and a beginner.





Aquazone

One of the healthy activities is swimming. It not only strengthens the body, improves immunity, but also saves from a number of ailments. To implement this important process, the hotel has built a single outdoor pool with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã140 square meters. It is unheated, has different levels of depth.

Fun for kids

Mesut Hotel 4 * receives grateful and somewhat enthusiastic reviews from tourists who come to rest with their children. Children are invited to visit the kids club, which is happy for every guest aged 4 to 12 years. Various mischievous games, sports, contests and quizzes will allow young guests to usefully spend time. In addition, a number of board games, drawing or painting are available. Having plenty of frolics, kids under the supervision of adults go to swim in the pool.

A cot in the room of the Mesut 4 * hotel is available upon request. The restaurants have high chairs.





Sport, beauty and leisure

The main building is open for visitors to the spa, which is ready to receive all visitors, regardless of gender and age. The wellness center with a number of procedures will easily restore strength, give vitality and good mood. Peaceful atmosphere with light sounds of music relaxes the body and gives positive emotions. As a result, the skin is rejuvenated, the functioning of the body is established, and the volume of the body is reduced.

For those who are accustomed to regular physical activity, the doors of the gym are open, a visit to which is included in the price. Treadmills, steppers and other equipment will help you to get your desired body shape. Qualified instructors will draw up a schedule of gentle training, which will positively affect the general condition and physical development of vacationers.

The on-site activities include table tennis and darts. The evening ends with a show program from a team of funny animators, which consists of two people.





Additional services

Due to the varied list of famous attractions and natural beauties, many who come here use the car rental service, which can be ordered at the reception. For guests arriving by car, free secure parking spaces are provided. There is a currency exchange, souvenir shops and grocery stores on site. Laundry and ironing are additionally charged.

Airport transfer service is available around the clock. Experienced drivers will quickly and safely deliver guests to their destination.

Reviews visited

The hotel complex Mesut Hotel 4 * reviews are diverse, among which the positive prevail. The hotel is positioning itself as youth-friendly. The possibility of unhindered access to the Internet appeals to guests. They are indescribably pleased with the abundance of the dining table. Friendly and smiling staff energizes with positive energy for the whole day.

In general, we can conclude that the Mesut Hotel 4 * service offers upscale. All vacationers here were satisfied and recommend the complex to friends and acquaintances.
