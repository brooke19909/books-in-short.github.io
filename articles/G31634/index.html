Guest house on Shevchenko (Anapa): photos, description and reviews

Who at least once in his life did not dream of visiting the golden beaches of sunny Anapa ?. An amazing resort that captivates at a glance, it is ready to offer you dozens of options for cozy accommodation and a comfortable stay. Analyzing the reviews of tourists, we noticed that a huge number of small boarding houses and hotels are located on Shevchenko street. This is a real tourism center where everyone can find a cozy room to their liking. But do not delay, not a single guest house on Shevchenko (Anapa) is empty at the height of the season. Therefore, even before the onset of warm spring days, it is worth booking a place in the hotel.



Mini-hotel "Ilios"

And we will begin to look for the best guest house on Shevchenko. Anapa offers you a vacation so versatile that you will definitely not have to sit in the room. However, despite this, it is important that you are surrounded by coziness and comfort. Ilios is a small hotel that invites you to a family vacation. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, refrigerator and TV, a bathroom with shower. It is located in a cozy corner of Anapa, at the address: Shevchenko, 154.

Guest Accommodation

In total, guests are offered 28 rooms, including three luxury categories. There is a small parking for 8 cars, as well as its own dining room, which provides delicious food. The cost of renting a double room is from 1000 to 1800 rubles, depending on the season. Triple will cost you from 1200 to 2500. And if you like special comfort, then the guest house on Shevchenko (Anapa) is ready to offer you a two-room suite. Price per room per day - from 1800 to 3500 rubles.

Experienced tourists respond very warmly to the work of the administration and all the staff of this small but very cozy hotel. Perfect cleanliness, friendly attitude and delicious food - this is what distinguishes this guest house on Shevchenko. Anapa is a place exclusively sunny and positive, which means that tourists should feel themselves accordingly.



Mini-Hotel "Alexander"

Another great option for both couples and travelers traveling alone. Everyone, without exception, is waiting for Anapa. Guest houses on Shevchenko are cozy and comfortable hotels with a particularly warm atmosphere. Just a 10-minute walk separates this cozy hotel from the beach and promenade, cafes and restaurants, as well as a large amusement park.

For families with children, this complex will be very convenient in that the entire territory is fenced and equipped with a modern security system. Children can safely play in the yard, and parents do their own thing. There are several buildings on the territory, each of which is equipped with its own kitchen. And just 10 minutes away is an equipped sandy beach with lots of entertainment. Judging by customer reviews - this is one of the best mini-hotels of Anapa. Guest house (Shevchenko, 160) is also very affordable. Double occupancy will cost about 350 rubles. This price includes accommodation, use of the kitchen and ironing board.



Guest House "On Shevchenko" (Anapa)

His official address is Shevchenko, 174A. The best option for a family vacation. Moreover, your smaller ones will also not be left without housing for the duration of your vacation. Pets are allowed here, of course, subject to the rules of public living. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout and all rooms are air-conditioned and have a TV. There is also a kitchen where you can cook for your family.

The cost of living starts from 699 rubles. Tourists note the pleasant and even exquisite interior of the rooms, as well as the common areas (hall and kitchen). Well-chosen colors, interesting decor items, harmoniously harmonizing with the decor of lamps and vases. All this creates a homely and even romantic atmosphere that is conducive to relaxation.



Mini-hotel "Sadko"

It is also located on Shevchenko street, house 79. Booking - only by advance payment. A shuttle service can be provided from the airport or train station. The hotel is relatively small, but very comfortable. There is parking for travelers who arrive in their car.

All tourists note that the staff at the hotel is very helpful and polite. The rooms are spacious, there are wonderful balconies from which you can watch the sunsets. The rooms are clean, each guest is given two towels. Given the fact that tourists always bring them with them, this is more than enough. Each room has a fridge and TV, hairdryer and kettle, as well as a set of dishes. Below is a shared kitchen where you can cook your own food. There is a whole set of household appliances and utensils. And nearby there is a dining room and several cafes.

Of the minuses, guests note the lack of an elevator. When checking in, you have to climb to the third or fourth floor with suitcases. In addition, the sea is far enough, about 20 minutes on foot. In extreme heat it can be hard.

Mini-hotel "Bora" (Shevchenko, 93)

It is located on the greenest street, in the center of Anapa. The central sandy beach is no more than 10 minutes walk. In total there are 6 rooms of different sizes to accommodate two, three or five guests. Each of them has a split system, TV and all necessary furniture. Room rates - from 350 to 700 rubles, depending on the season.

Meals are not offered to guests, but there is a private kitchen on site where you can prepare meals for your family. Literally five minutes walk there are several dining rooms and cafes. Wi-Fi is available on site, and there is convenient parking for motorists. Meeting and seeing off tourists by appointment. Many tourists for a long time remember the hospitality of the owners of the hotel, as well as a stunning green courtyard. Here for guests' relaxation there is a spacious gazebo entwined with flowers, where you can taste the best wines or drink green tea, as well as admire flowering flower beds and beautiful butterflies, enjoy the birdsong and the gentle sun.
