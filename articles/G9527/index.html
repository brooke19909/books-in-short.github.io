The biography of Elizabeth Taylor, the true legend of Hollywood, deserves attention. The actress won the hearts of millions of fans with her talent and was recognized as an icon of style and beauty. Taylor won the Academy Award three times, one of them - a special nomination, and two for Best Actress. For creative achievements, the actress was awarded the Order of the British Empire, II degree. Elizabeth became the first world star to receive a million dollar fee for filming a movie.

The biography of Elizabeth Taylor is rich and interesting. Until the last days of her life, she was in the center of attention, despite the fact that in recent decades she almost never acted in film. The attention of the public was attracted not only by the work - icons of the cinema, but also by the vicissitudes of her personal life. The actress has more than 60 films, 8 marriages and 7 spouses. Actor Richard Burton managed to lead Elizabeth twice down the aisle and divorce twice.

The actress was born into the family of Sarah W. Wombrodt and Francis L. Taylor on February 27, 1932. Her parents were American actors who worked at that time in London.
