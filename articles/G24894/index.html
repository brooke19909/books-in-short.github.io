Exploring the moon

So why stopped flying to the moon, what was discovered on its surface? In order to study obscure phenomena, scientific societies were created. What could they find out?

The American flight to the moon, as well as the studies of many scientists, made it possible to reveal inexplicable phenomena on the surface of the satellite of our planet . Both Americans and Russians knew about such anomalies. However, many astronauts have stated that very interesting and strange things are happening on the surface of the moon. Among them, not only bright flashes were noted, but also luminous points periodically entering the orbit of the moon. Their sizes were simply huge. They circled the satellite of the Earth, and then landed. But that is not all.

In 1968, the last century, a document was created, called the "Chronological catalog of reports of lunar events." During the exploration of the moon, about 579 anomalies were revealed. This list includes: disappearing craters, extraordinary geometric shapes of regular shape, large domes that change color, color bars moving at a speed of about 6 kilometers per hour, luminous objects, and so on.

First broadcasts

When NASA launched the next Apollo, the purpose of which was to land on the moon, many hams follow what is happening, listening to the radio broadcast of negotiations between the crew and Houston. It was at this time that the first signs appeared that the pilots of the aircraft are hiding something and not negotiating. Some hams from distant Australia and from Switzerland were able to catch astronaut talks at other frequencies. On another wave, the pilots talked about not entirely clear phenomena.

Only 10 years later, Maurice Chatlin, the creator of the communications apparatus for the "lunar program", admitted that he was present during the negotiations between Houston and astronauts. He confirmed that Armstrong mentioned some objects that accompanied the aircraft.





Those who flew to the moon spoke about obscure anomalies. First of all, some blocks located very close to the landing site were mentioned. Some of the objects emitted a glow. Many astronauts said that they saw something, but they could not describe it in more detail because of the non-disclosure subscription.

Already after the "lunar program" was closed, NASA experts admitted that more than 20 people, members of various crews, witnessed the presence of UFOs on the lunar surface. In addition, it was confirmed from the Earth Observatory that Apollo 12 was accompanied by some objects of unearthly origin.
