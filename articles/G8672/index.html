Hotel Magic Life Waterworld 5 * - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Belek has been one of the most popular resort regions of Turkey among our compatriots for several years. This situation is explained quite simply. After all, there is a mass of comfortable hotels for every taste, accommodation in which, along with a healthy climate and a gentle sea, will make your vacation unforgettable. Undoubtedly, one of the best Belek hotel complexes is the five-star Club Magic Life Waterworld Imperial. Today we decided to get to know this hotel more closely, finding out what services it provides to its guests. We also offer to learn more about Belek itself.





Where is the hotel complex

Hotel Magic Life Waterworld 5 * is located directly on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, in the resort region of the Republic of Turkey - Belek. The city center is only two kilometers away. Also seven kilometers from the hotel complex is the resort village of Kadriye. The nearest air harbor located in Antalya is only 35 kilometers away. Therefore, after arriving in Turkey, you can reach the hotel in just 30-40 minutes. This is a big plus, especially if you are traveling with a small child.

Hotel Magic Life Waterworld 5 * is located on the first line. It has its own sandy, clean and equipped with everything necessary for a great vacation beach.

Belek - Magic Life Waterworld: Hotel Concept

This five-star hotel complex positions itself as an ideal place for an interesting and diverse family and youth pastime. Magic Life Waterworld can rightfully be proud of magnificent animation, a wide selection of entertainment for vacationers of all ages, as well as delicious dishes of national and international cuisines, which are prepared by the best chefs in the hotel restaurants.





Hotel Description

For the first time, Club Magic Life Waterworld Imperial 5 * opened its doors to guests in 2001. Three years later, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here. Today, the building is in very good condition, as it is constantly undergoing cosmetic repairs.

Magic Life Waterworld Imperial is a very beautiful hotel built in traditional Turkish style. The hotel complex has a large (22 thousand square meters) green and well-groomed territory and developed infrastructure. The hotel includes a main six-story building and twenty bungalows surrounded by a green garden with amazing flowers, shady alleys, picturesque paths, gazebos, as well as water attractions. The hotel territory smoothly turns into a sandy beach, which has a podium pier.

Number of rooms

The five-star hotel in Belek Magic Life Waterworld Imperial has at its disposal 772 comfortable rooms. The room fund of the hotel complex is represented by standard single, double and triple rooms, suites (consisting of two rooms) and Superior apartments. There are also seven specially equipped rooms for people with disabilities.

Each room has a balcony or terrace with a magnificent view of the azure waters of the sea or the picturesque surrounding landscape. Each room in Magic Life Waterworld has everything you need for a comfortable stay for guests. So, here there is a bathroom with a hairdryer, air conditioning, TV, telephone, minibar and safe. Cleaning is done every day, and bed linen is changed three times a week. Also daily, if necessary, towels are changed. If necessary, you can order room service.





Nutrition

Meals at Club Magic Life Waterworld are all-inclusive. Three times a day, in the main restaurant of the hotel, talented chefs serve a buffet that serves a variety of dishes, snacks, fruits and desserts. Afternoon tea is served here, as well as late night soup. In addition, the main restaurant has a children's corner equipped with a microwave, blender and bottle warmers.

In addition, guests of Magic Life Waterworld (Turkey) can visit the √† la carte restaurants, where they will be offered dishes of Turkish, Italian, Brazilian and Mediterranean cuisines.

In all six bars of the hotel complex at any time you can order your favorite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as light snacks and sweets. Pamper yourself with real Turkish coffee with mouth-watering muffins and pastries in the Sissy coffee shop. If you want to taste freshly squeezed juice, then go to the Vitamin Bar or Tropic Bar. Fans of Japanese food will not be deprived here either. If you are one of them, then be sure to visit the local sushi bar.

Pools

Hotel Magic Life Waterworld Imperial 5 * on its territory has a total of thirteen pools, two of which are indoor. Three tanks are designed for the smallest guests. There is also a water park with several water slides. Near the pools you will find sunbeds with sun loungers and parasols. There are also bars where you can enjoy snacks, desserts or drinks (both non-alcoholic and alcoholic).





Sea beach

Magic Life Waterworld 5 * is located on the first coastline and offers its guests a rest on their own sandy beach with a length of seven hundred meters. Here, hotel guests can use sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas and beach towels for free. There is a bar on the beach where you can enjoy snacks and various non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks.

Entertainment

Magic Life Waterworld offers a wide variety of entertainment for every taste. So, outdoor enthusiasts can play table or tennis, beach volleyball, basketball, futsal, golf, billiards or darts, visit the gym, enjoy water skiing, canoeing, catamaran, banana, windsurfing and sailing .

If you want to relax, then visit the spa, where you will be offered a variety of beauty services and massages. The hotel also has a sauna and Turkish bath (hammam).

Naturally, the hotel has animators from morning until late in the evening. Their task is to entertain hotel guests of various ages. Vibrant shows are held in the evenings.

Fun for kids

Since most of the guests of Magic Life Waterworld are family people with children of different ages, the hotel has taken care of a variety of activities for the youngest travelers. So, on the territory of the hotel complex there are three children's pools, one of which is indoor. There are also water slides. In addition, the hotel has entertainment clubs for children of three age groups. Here for several hours, your child will be occupied with various funs designed for his age. In the evenings, the hotel hosts a mini disco for the youngest guests.





Infrastructure

The Magic Life Waterward hotel complex has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable and varied stay. So, the reception is open 24 hours a day. Therefore, if you check into the hotel at an inopportune hour, or if you have an urgent question, you can always contact the on-duty administrator, who will do everything possible to help you.

For guests who want to come on vacation with their car or plan to rent a car already in Turkey, the hotel has a spacious parking lot. Therefore, you will not have parking problems here.

Guests can always use the laundry and dry cleaning services, book a room service, rent a car, buy interesting sightseeing tours, exchange currency, buy everything you need in several shops and souvenir shops, visit a hairdresser and a beauty salon. There is also a medical office on the territory of the hotel complex, where you can contact if you feel unwell. Here, a qualified doctor will provide you with the necessary medical care.

For business

The Magic Life Waterward Hotel did not ignore business people. So, on its territory there is a conference room equipped with everything necessary. It is great for holding any seminars, conferences, symposia and other events.

Since the Magic Life Waterward Imperial is a five-star hotel, the cost of living here is quite high. So, on average, a three-day stay for two will cost you 39 thousand rubles.





Hotel reviews

A lot of people planning a vacation both in Turkey and in other countries, when choosing a hotel, try to study, if possible, all available reviews about other tourists staying in it. Therefore, today we decided to summarize the comments of travelers who spent their holidays at the Magic Life Waterward Imperial hotel complex in Belek.

Most tourists agree that this hotel is very good for outdoor enthusiasts. Almost every traveler noted the excellent sports and entertainment infrastructure of the hotel complex. After all, there are several tennis courts, five-a-side football fields, a basketball court, etc. In one word, this hotel literally provokes vacationers to go in for sports, which is a big plus.

Guests also spoke positively about entertainment. So, everyone with pleasure (both adults and children) rode the slides in the local water park. Many also liked the possibility of free rides on a "banana" on the beach.

Many controversial comments related to nutrition at Magic Life Waterworld. Someone considered the menu varied enough, and the dishes were tasty, and someone was sure that the food here was not quite up to the proper level (5 stars).

As for the rooms themselves, some of the guests were confused by the humidity in the rooms. Although most tourists did not show such complaints. Some travelers were a little unhappy with the fact that at night they turned on the central air conditioning in ventilation mode in order to save electricity. In this regard, at night in the rooms sometimes it happened stuffy. As for cleaning the rooms and changing linen and towels, there is no problem with this at the hotel.

Another point that a number of tourists have complained about is that the Magic Life Waterward hotel is mainly aimed at travelers from Germany, who make up about 80% of all guests here. In this regard, the hotel staff practically does not speak Russian. However, if you speak English at least at the school curriculum level, you will not have any communication problems.

In general, most tourists were satisfied with their stay at the Magic Life Waterward and would recommend this hotel to their friends and acquaintances.





Belek: resort description

Belek is a young, but very dynamically developing resort, located on the Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea. The first tourists to this region, famous today for its cedar, eucalyptus and pine groves, pristine sandy beaches and the warm sea, arrived in the eighties of the last century. Today, Belek is the most respectable, and therefore the most expensive resort in the entire Republic of Turkey.

Due to the fact that the settlements here began to appear relatively recently, in Belek you will not find any historical sights. However, due to its favorable geographical location, from here you can make fascinating trips to the most interesting monuments of the entire province of Antalya.

So, for example, if you want to touch history, then after spending only fifteen minutes on the road, you will find yourself in the ancient city of Aspendos. Particularly noteworthy here is the well-preserved amphitheater. In the summer, various concerts are even held here, visiting of which will leave an indelible impression. Also, about twenty minutes from Belek, there are the ruins of two more ancient cities - Sillon and Perge.

Nature lovers should definitely visit the Koprulu Canyon National Park. Here you will enjoy picturesque views. In addition, a bridge erected by the ancient Romans was thrown over it. On the river, passing along the bottom of the canyon, you can go rafting.

In addition, Belek is one of the world famous golf centers. So, here is the National Golf Club, international competitions are held. Beginners can also try their hand at this aristocratic game in numerous venues.

The magnificent beaches of Belek stretch for twenty kilometers. All of them are sandy, clean and even. The entrance to the water is flat everywhere, without stones. All beaches have the necessary infrastructure to make your stay enjoyable and comfortable.

As for restaurants and cafes, there are not so many of them in Belek. Indeed, in this region there are mainly four- and five-star hotels offering all-inclusive meals to their guests. Thus, tourists prefer to have lunch and dinner in the restaurants of their hotel complexes. A similar situation has developed with the nightlife of the city. There are several discos and nightclubs, but in terms of their scope they, of course, cannot be compared with Antalya or Istanbul. This is explained by the fact that each luxury Belek hotel offers its guests an extensive entertainment program, including discos. Thus, tourists do not need to look for additional entertainment outside their hotel.

It should be noted that the rest in Belek is mostly designed for wealthy people who are used to staying in luxurious comfortable five-star hotels. In this regard, you are unlikely to find cheerful companies of noisy youth here. This resort meets the highest international quality standards and is perfect for a respectable family holiday by the sea.
