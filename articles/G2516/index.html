Malaysia Langkawi: attractions, beaches and the best hotels on the island

Near the border with Thailand, in Malaysia, washed by the Andaman Sea, the Langkawi archipelago is located. Of the many islands of the magnificent archipelago, only Pulau-Dayyang-Bunting, Pulau-Tuba, Pulau-Singa, and the largest of them, Langkawi, are inhabited.

Langkawi Island (Malaysia) is a real paradise for travelers who prefer a comfortable and relaxing holiday. It is best to come here from November to July. From July to October, the rainy season begins in these parts.

Island Description

Today, for many tourists, Malaysia has become very attractive. Langkawi, reviews of which are always enthusiastic, amazes guests with its beauty. The archipelago consists of 104 islands located in the Andaman Sea. Most of them are uninhabited.





The largest of them, the island of Langkawi (Malaysia), the photo of which you can see in our article, has a well-developed infrastructure. Its name can be translated as "the island of the red eagle." This is not at all accidental - rare birds actually live here. These are eagles having red-brick wings.

Langkawi Island (Malaysia) is located on the border with Thailand. Its area is 478 square meters. km The population is 65 thousand people. The capital of the island is Kuaha.

Natural conditions

This is a warm and sunny country - Malaysia. Langkawi is no exception. On average, the air temperature is about 25 ¬∞ C (annual). The island of Langkawi (Malaysia), whose photo today is often placed in the brochures of travel agencies, is rich in flora and fauna.

The local population, in addition to the tourism business, is engaged in animal husbandry and crop production. A variety of fruits and vegetables, crops, and livestock are bred here. The most convenient way to travel around the island is by renting a car in one of the many locations.

What attracts tourists to the island?

Malaysia (Langkawi, in particular) is famous for its magnificent beaches, comfortable hotels, perfectly clear emerald water, rich underwater world, mysterious caves covered with many legends. One of them tells the story of the beautiful princess Mahsuri, who was unfairly accused by adversaries of adultery and sentenced to death. We will tell you more about it below.

Another legend says that on one of the nearest islands is a lake of a pregnant girl. According to legend, the sterile woman bathed in him will certainly soon become a mother. And today, thousands of women suffering from this ailment come to the "magic" lake.

The island began to flourish after Mahathira Mohamed took over as prime minister. Langkawi Island became a free trade zone (1987) and gradually began to turn into a center of international tourism.

Relaxation

Every year, the island of Langkawi (Malaysia) becomes more and more popular with tourists from all over the world. Holidays here can be very diverse - from beach to sightseeing. Here you can swim with a mask and snorkel, go down to the depths with scuba diving, ride a jet ski on the waves, fly a parachute behind a boat.

Wildlife lovers will be interested in a jungle trip or exploring the wonderful world of coral islands and caves. Local SPA centers are also popular, which return the harmony of body and soul.

Fans of sailing will not be bored either. They are well aware of Malaysia. Langkawi is famous for its luxurious Royal Yacht Club, which regularly holds sailing regattas.

Beaches

The main beaches of the island are in its northern and western parts. The more democratic of them are located south of the airport - these are Cenang and Tangan. On the border of these beaches is the Underwater World Aquarium, as well as a duty-free shop.

Very popular hotels have been built on Cenang Beach: Holiday Villa, Frangipane, Meritus Pelangi. Langkawi (Malaysia) has beaches for every taste. For example, if you want to spend time in a deserted place, you can recommend the secluded and calm KOK beach. It is located northwest of the airport. There are several luxury hotels here.





Many tourists believe that the best beach of the island is located in Danai Bay. It belongs to the best hotels Andaman and Datai. Another beautiful beach located in the northeast of the island is Tanuung. At low tide, tourists can see here how the spit opens, connecting Langkawi with a neighboring island.

Hotels

Many beautiful hotels are famous for Malaysia. Langkawi Island offers hotels for every taste. Let us introduce you some of them.

Tanjung langkawi

This luxury hotel is one of the most romantic on the island. Newlyweds and families with young children like to stay here. In total it offers 136 rooms.





The hotel is located in the north of the island, has its own beach, which is famous for its white sand, clear sea and magnificent sunset. It offers guests a bar, 4 restaurants, two swimming pools (open), one of which is filled with sea water, a children's club.

The hotel rooms have panoramic views and are decorated in an oriental style. It uses soft and delicate Thai silk, and Indian fabrics, as well as amazing tropical flowers.

The anjungan

This is a small resort located on the most popular Pangkor beach. It is a 2-3-story building. The rooms have a balcony (or veranda), as well as access to the courtyard or pool. The hotel is suitable for families with children, in its rooms can accommodate up to five people.

There is a small pool, a five-minute walk to the public beach, and fifteen minutes to a secluded beach. Hotels in Langkawi (Malaysia), according to tourists, are distinguished by well-groomed territory, a harmonious combination of price and quality.





Numerous cafes and bars are located nearby. If you sail in a boat for only five minutes, you will find yourself on a picturesque island where you can go snorkeling. Internet is paid, at the reception there is a safe.

Pangkor laut resort

This is an excellent complex, which consists of several villas on stilts. It is located on the island of Pangkor Laut (private). The hotel is distinguished by the highest level of services offered and the highest level of service, able to impress the most fastidious guests.

The luxurious beach belonging to the complex is washed by the waters of the Bay of Bengal. Here you can fully enjoy the privacy. The rooms are spacious and elegantly decorated, with beautiful views of the sea and the jungle, which are inhabited by lizards, monkeys, tropical birds.





Malaysia Langkawi Attractions

Tourists who have visited this wonderful island, note that, in addition to a magnificent beach holiday, they spent a lot of time studying local memorial places.

So that guests of the island could fully appreciate its beauty, a heavenly bridge was built at an altitude of seven hundred meters between the Ganung Mountains. Langkawi (Malaysia) can be proud of this building. He rushes to heaven, and from here a tremendous view opens.

This magnificent building was built in 2004, and for visits it became available in 2005. Today it is recognized as the highest (single-bearing) bridge on our planet. Amazingly, it really rests on a single support made of heavy-duty metal. Its weight is distributed on eight cables that are attached to the support. It hangs over a precipice and in windy weather its swaying is especially felt.





This amazing place is Langkawi Island (Malaysia). The bridge at first glance does not seem too reliable, however, its safety was in the first place in the design. Experts say that it is completely safe. It carefully thought out the evacuation system (for emergency).

In addition, at the highest level there are durable handrails made of a special kind of steel, thick wire mesh and wood flooring. The bridge is 125 meters long and 1.8 meters wide. At its edges are platforms of a triangular shape. They play the role of viewing platforms where you can stand and enjoy the magnificent views of the Andaman Sea, the island of Langkawi and neighboring islands. Thanks to its curved shape, you can appreciate the landscapes from different angles.

Cableway

Today, Malaysia has become very popular. Lankawi is an island that is famous for its cable car. It originates in the village of Oriental Village. A modern cabin lifts tourists to the top of Mat Chinchang Mountain. From this point the whole island is in full view.

In clear weather, you can see the Thai coast in the north, and the silhouette of Indonesia in the west. A cable car is included in all hiking trails. It is located at an altitude of 708 meters. The air temperature at the top is about five degrees lower than at its foot.





Oceanarium

"The Underwater World of Langkawi" is the largest oceanarium in Asia, which is famous for the fact that it has more than 5 thousand marine inhabitants living in one hundred tanks. The uniqueness of the structure is that it is a 15-meter tunnel, which is filled with 800 thousand liters of sea water. Here you can enjoy the diversity of the underwater world.

Predatory sharks swim very close to visitors. They are separated from marine life by 38 mm high-strength flat glass. It is considered very rare in the world.

Mausoleum of Mahsuri

This building is located very close to the capital of the island - the city of Kuah (12 km). A sad but slightly embellished tradition is associated with him. Legend has it that the sandy white coast of Langkawi Island is nothing more than the bright blood of an innocently ruined extraordinary beauty - Princess Makhsuri, who was accused of treason to her husband and killed.

In vain she tried to explain that her enemies had spoken about her - the judges were inexorable. The executioner, piercing the beauty with a dagger, like all the inhabitants of the island, saw that the blood of a young woman was white. This indicated its purity and, therefore, confirmed that an unjustified murder had been committed. Dying, the princess said that in the future the island will face various troubles - black buffaloes will produce white cows, the sand will turn black (this is the sand that covers Pasir Hitam beach), and the island of Langwari will completely disappear.

At the beginning of the nineteenth century, Thais repeatedly tried to wipe off the island of Langkawi from the face of the Earth, and the man who blamed the unfortunate princess died unexpectedly. On the site of the murder of an innocent victim, the Makhsuri Mausoleum was built from white marble, like her soul,. Today, tourists from all over the world come here to pay tribute to innocence and cleanliness.





The tomb of Mahsuri is now a place of worship, when entering the mausoleum, you need to remove your shoes. In the garden you will be shown an ancient well, which, according to legend, the princess dug up herself. Even in the driest months, it does not dry out. Local residents claim that if you wash your face with water, happiness will never leave you.

Telaga Tujuh

So called seven magnificent lakes, which are located one above the other on the gentle slope of the mountain. Icy water flows from lake to lake. An ancient legend says that beautiful fairies came down from the top of the mountain to wash their luxurious hair and wash themselves with crystal clear water. And nearby is Durian Perangin Waterfall. Nature divided it into seven streams. The height of this giant is ninety meters. At its foot, below, there is a so-called bath - a natural depression, which was chosen by tourists for swimming. You can approach it along a narrow path cut through the jungle.



Along the way, you will likely meet dozens of curious monkeys. If you are not careful, they will try to steal something from you. Children will be happy to slide into the water along the slopes of the lower "wells."

Eagle Square

This is one of the most frequently visited attractions in Langkawi. On a high pedestal is a sculpture of an eagle, which is preparing for take-off. Its height is 12 meters. The square where it is installed offers amazing views of the bay and the city of Kuah.

There are also small fountains, spacious terraces and delicate bridges - this is a great place for memorable photos. The figure of the reddish-brown eagle is a symbol of the island of Langkawi. The name of the island comes from two words: eagle (helang) and reddish brown (kawi).





Bird park

Talking about the interesting places of this marvelous island, one cannot but say about the Bird Park. It contains more than two and a half thousand representatives of the most diverse birds - owls and eagles, canaries, rhinoceros birds, flamingos, toucans. Some inhabitants of the park can be found only in Asia.

In addition to birds, other animals feel quite comfortable here, for example, an amazing animal, similar at the same time to a cat and a bear. He is called Binturong. In addition, this park has the opportunity to take a fascinating walk along the 15-meter aviary with an artificially created waterfall. This is a great place to relax with children.

Crocodile Land Adventure

Many interesting places are famous for Malaysia. Langkawi, reviews of which are simply enthusiastic, invites its guests to visit this amazing attraction. It was created for tourists at one of the world's largest crocodile farms. It contains more than 1000 predators. If you have already been to a crocodile farm, for example, in Thailand, then you will not be very surprised. If you did not have to visit such menageries, then you are sure that the impressions received here will last you a long time.

Twice a day, visitors trainers show a fascinating crocodile show. Here you can buy crocodile leather and stuffed animals of these reptiles. And as a gift you will be given a real crocodile tooth.
