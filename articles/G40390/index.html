Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 * (Corfu, Greece) - photos, prices and reviews

Greece has a large number of various resort places. One of these is Corfu. There is stunning nature, which is protected by the authorities, because it gives the island charm and makes it attractive to tourists. In addition, Corfu is one of the most popular resort islands in the Mediterranean. There are a lot of various beaches: both sandy and pebble, both private and public. Therefore, if you decide to go to Corfu, then the necessary step is the most suitable hotel. Among the three-star hotels that this island has, Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 * can be distinguished. It is located in close proximity to the beach, which makes it a very interesting option for organizing a wonderful holiday by the sea.



How to get to the hotel

This hotel is located on the Greek island, which is called Corfu. Fortunately for numerous tourists, planes fly to this island. Moreover, from many large cities of the Russian Federation, flights can be non-stop. For example, from Moscow to Corfu can be reached in about three hours, and from St. Petersburg in four. At the same time, the cost of a flight from the capital of Russia will be about three hundred dollars per person. Upon arrival at the island's airport, you can reach the hotel by bus or taxi. Taxi is a more comfortable option, and when you consider that the distance from the airport to the hotel is only 5 km, then it is not too expensive in relation to a bus trip. You can also use the paid service called transfer, which is provided by the Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 * (Corfu island, Perama).



Services that are provided in Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 *

The range of services in this three-star hotel is very large. Here they can offer you a taxi from the airport to the hotel, and the possibility of renting a car so that you can travel around the cities of the island, without wasting time waiting for the bus. Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel. Moreover, if in many other hotels it is available only in public places, then here it can be used anywhere in the hotel. If you need to exchange currency, then this can always be done within the walls of Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 * (Corfu). But at the same time you save time. If you need a safe for storing valuables or important documents, you can rent it. However, be prepared for the fact that for this service you will need to pay an additional amount of money. If any of your items become dirty, you can always use the dry cleaning or laundry services that are available at the Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 * (Perama).



Hotel rooms and their equipment

In total, the hotel has 80 rooms. All of them are standard and differ only in the number of beds. If you are a couple, then you can provide an excellent room with a double bed and sea view. For three friends can offer a room with three twin beds. The room has a TV with cable channels, and satellite TV at an additional cost. Also in the hotel room there is the possibility of using free wireless Internet. This will allow you to contact your family without spending any money directly from the hotel room.

In addition to a bed and a TV, the room has nightstands, a safe and a minibar, which can be filled with drinks at an additional cost. There is also a bathroom and toilet. As in any other self-respecting hotel, in the bathroom you can find a clean bath towel and a hairdryer.

Hotel room rate

What is very expensive at this hotel is the cost of living. In order to settle in a room designed for two people, you need to pay about $ 460. At the same time, a room for three people costs about $ 560.

Sports and Entertainment

Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 * (Greece) - a hotel whose management and staff take care of its guests. Therefore, here you can find several places where settled people can relax and have fun. Especially for its guests, the hotel management provided for a massage parlor. There is also a closed beach area where you can relax in peace and quiet, as well as just play beach volleyball with your friends.

The hotel has a large outdoor pool with clean water. Hotel workers monitor the water in this pool, they change it almost every day. Fans of extreme sports can go to the shores of the Mediterranean Sea and go water-skiing or "banana". There is also the possibility of paragliding. If you want to go diving, then this can also be done. At the same time they will give you scuba gear, and you can completely safely dive to the bottom of the Mediterranean Sea.

Food and drink at the hotel

On the territory of the Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 * hotel there is a restaurant. You can have breakfast, lunch and dinner in it. Moreover, breakfast and dinner are already included in the price. Greek cuisine is served. The food in the restaurant is very tasty, and given the cost of living in a hotel, it is more profitable to have breakfast and dinner in the restaurant. Lunch is not free, so you can go to the city and try to find a place for yourself with a good assortment of dishes and reasonable prices. In order to taste alcoholic beverages, you can head to the hotel bar. A large amount of all kinds of alcohol is offered here: vodka, cognac, wine, and even tequila. At your request, a minibar can be filled in the hotel room. However, this is done for a fee.

What you can see on the island of Corfu

The main attraction of the island is Spianada Square in Kerkyra. They say that she is the most beautiful in all of Greece. You can also go to the church of St. Spyridon and look at the sarcophagus, which stores the relics of the founder of the city of Kerkyra. In addition to these attractions in Corfu, there are many more interesting places. For example, you can go to the water park and ride a water slide. If you wish, you can always visit a golf club or tennis court to have fun and work on your physical fitness.



Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 *: tourist reviews

Guests of this hotel speak about him only positively, calling the only minus the high cost of living. Otherwise, everyone is very happy that they had a chance to relax in this hotel. Tourists note the good service, as well as the work of responsive staff. Many people liked the dishes prepared by the chef of the restaurant. The proximity of the hotel to the beach is also called an advantage relative to other hotels in Corfu. It is important that this beach is private, and only guests of Oasis Corfu Hotel 3 * are allowed to relax on it. Another advantage of the hotel can be called its proximity to the island's airport.

In conclusion, it should be noted the very good quality of all hotel services. If you decide and choose the Oasis Corfu Hotel, you can be sure that the hotel will justify every dollar invested in your vacation. Have a nice vacation on the sunny Mediterranean coast!
