Best Australia Hotels: Description

Australia is an amazing country, occupying the whole mainland of the same name. It has a lot of mysterious and alluring, which is exactly what is attractive for travelers who come here to relax. In order to spend an unforgettable vacation, which will be remembered for a long time and give pleasant emotions, think in advance about your place of residence. Australia hotels are open for you around the clock from simple options to luxury hotels. This article will help you in choosing a suitable option that will provide you comfort and coziness.

Unusual facts

The history of this country begins in 1788, when the American traveler John Cook discovered a new mainland and gave it the name "New South Wales". The first inhabitants of Australia were English prisoners, specially exiled here by the British government.

Among the interesting facts related to this country, one can recall:

  • The main symbols of the country are kangaroos and ostriches. They indicate a forward movement, only in this direction should Australia develop.
  • In this country, the largest number of poisonous spiders and snakes. Therefore, you need to be extremely careful and attentive when walking in little-known places. After all, the coastal taipan lives here - the most dangerous snake in the world. Its poison is able to instantly destroy a person.

Australian specialties are well-done kangaroo meat. This product is sold in any supermarket at an affordable price. You can cook it yourself according to local recipes or try this dish in a cafe or restaurant.
