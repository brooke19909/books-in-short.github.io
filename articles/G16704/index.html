Hotel Melia Bali 5 * (Indonesia, Bali): description, reviews

The popularity of the island of Bali and Indonesia among Russian tourists has grown recently. They are attracted by the pristine natural beauty of this place. Endless sandy beaches, rainforests, ancient temples and the clear sea - all this has become the hallmark of the island. Tourists who are looking for a place for a luxurious vacation with first-class service will appreciate the Melia Bali 5 * hotel, located on the island of Bali.

Features of rest in Indonesia

Indonesia is especially popular with sophisticated tourists looking for new experiences and honeymooners who want to spend their honeymoon in a paradise. Normal travelers are repelled by the long road and the high cost of trips. Flight time with transfers will be approximately 19 hours. But you can come here all year long. After the end of the beach season in Europe, many tourists are looking for a new place to relax. The ideal option would be Indonesia, because the weather in Bali in October is about 30 degrees. However, it is just as hot in winter and spring.



Tourists come here for a beach holiday. But also there are many other entertainments. For example, wellness programs that help improve blood circulation and also reduce body fat and cellulite. On about. Bali offers diving and surfing opportunities. It is also worth advising excursion programs that allow tourists to get acquainted with the sights of the country. There is also the opportunity to go rafting (descent along mountain rivers), and you can also ride elephants. Time in Indonesia does not differ much from Moscow. The difference is 4 hours.

Hotel location

The hotel is located in the south of the island. It is part of the elite resort of Nusa Dua, which specializes in a calm and quiet beach holiday. There are no nightclubs and noisy bars, but there are many restaurants, cafes and shopping centers. The private beach of the Melia Bali 5 * hotel is 100 meters from the main building. To reach it, tourists will have to cross the garden that surrounds the complex. 400 meters from the hotel is the largest shopping center of the resort, where travelers can buy clothes, jewelry, alcohol, souvenirs and much more. The Pacific Museum is 600 meters away. Its exhibits are the cultural monuments of. Bali, as well as other Pacific countries.

You can get to the hotel from the nearest Ngurah Rai International Airport. It is approximately 17 km from the complex. The average travel time will be about half an hour. For tourists to Nusa Dua shared transfers are organized. You can also order a taxi or rent a car.

Basic hotel information

For tourists, the hotel opened its doors in 1985. The main building of the hotel, as well as several private villas are located in the shadow of a dense forested area. For future guests prepared about 500 comfortable rooms, made in the Balinese style. Among them are family apartments, non-smoking rooms. All hotel buildings are wheelchair accessible. Part of the staff speaks Russian. Most often, tourists from Asia rest here. You can also meet a large number of Europeans and Russians.





Thanks to a calm environment and greenery, Melia Bali 5 * complex is well suited for families with small children. Children of all ages are allowed, and guests under 11 years old are free of charge when using existing bedding. Check-in starts at 15:00 local time and ends around midnight. Upon check-in, travelers must provide the administrator with an identification document and a credit card. The hotel accepts Visa, MasterCard and American Express cards. Tourists must leave the complex between 6:00 and 12:00.

Food system and local cuisine

In general, Indonesian cuisine is not very different from Asian cuisine. The main dishes here are fried rice, noodles, baked vegetables, meat and fish. In Bali, baked milk pigs are especially popular , served in restaurants right from the spit. Meat and seafood are also fried over an open fire, then watered with peanut sauce and served on a special stick. Very popular lately in Indonesia enjoys herring. This small fruit tastes like a mixture of pineapple and strawberries. Also, coming here, you should definitely try the duck baked in banana leaves.

For its guests the hotel offers several food concepts to choose from: breakfast only, half board, full board and all inclusive. The cost of the latter option, as a rule, includes the following set of services:

  • welcome drink during check-in, as well as a cold towel;
  • daily breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main restaurant of the complex;
  • soft drinks: concentrated juices and mineral water;
  • Indonesian-made alcohol: beer, wine, gin, liquors, rum;
  • An evening performance describing the cultural heritage of Bali takes place in the restaurant, which also serves a buffet;
  • Free snacks at the poolside bar.

Hotel Melia Bali 5 * seeks to provide its guests with a wide range of services that will make your stay here as comfortable as possible. Throughout the holidays, all tourists can use the following services:

Relax in a hotel with children

The hotel, located in a quiet and cozy part of the island, is ideal for families with children. For small guests there are many amenities. A cradle is delivered to all families with babies. The restaurant has high chairs for easy feeding. Tourists can also hire a qualified nanny for their children.

The children's pool has its own shallow section, where they can swim under the supervision of their parents. There is a children's playground for outdoor games. There is also a lounge in the hotel. Parents can entertain their child with entertaining and informative games, drawing, reading books. There is a mini club for children from 4 to 12 years old.





Positive feedback from tourists

The opinions of tourists are an important factor that should be considered when choosing a place to stay. Melia Bali 5 * reviews have both positive and negative. Guests believe that the advantages of the hotel are:

  • Smooth entry into the sea. It is suitable for both adults and children. But you must definitely take a tide map at the reception to know when you can visit the beach.
  • Huge and beautiful territory. The garden is a great place for evening walks.
  • Non-conflict staff. It will always help to solve problems, at will relocate to another room.
  • The guests resting here behave decently. There is no always drunk youth, no one makes noise in the evenings.
  • There are no fences between the hotels, so tourists can freely walk around the territory of other complexes.
  • The hotel has no ants, cockroaches, mosquitoes, flies and other insects.
  • Varied breakfasts. The chef tries to cook something new every day.
  • Good free entertainment. Animators make sure that no one is bored, but also are not imposed.




Negative reviews of tourists

But the Melia Bali 5 * hotel is famous not only for its merits. According to tourists, the complex has serious flaws that can ruin your vacation. They are:

  • Huge prices in restaurants and spa. In the shopping center near the hotel, massages can be ordered several times cheaper than in the hotel.
  • There is only one Russian-speaking employee in the complex, and he is not always present at the reception.
  • Outdated rooms. Beds and floors creak from any movement, as well as air conditioning.
  • The mini club works only for children who are fluent in English.
  • There is always hot water in the pool, so there is no place to cool in hot weather, since the sea is also very warm.
  • High humidity throughout the hotel. Clothing does not dry for a very long time.

Conclusion

After reviewing the description, we can say that this hotel is well suited for a measured vacation. The disadvantages of the complex are minor and do not affect the ratio of quality and price. It will be a great place to relax in the fall and winter, because the weather in Bali in October is very good, and the temperature rarely drops below 30 degrees. It can be recommended to families with children, business and elderly people.
