Arend Arendovich: reviews. Real estate agency

An initiative exclusively demanded by the population and useful for the infrastructure of large cities is the opening in 2008 of the first Moscow agency Arend Arendovich. Reviews of his work were at first most sympathetic, but now there is no certainty in the assessments. The creators positioned their activities as a revolution in rental housing, guaranteed transparency and civilization of their presence in the market. It must be said bluntly that not everything turned out, Arend Arendovich failed. Reviews are especially loud about this in the regions.





Position and reality

The goal set and the mission voiced by the leadership of the agency are very convincing, it remains only to follow this goal and fulfill the mission in good faith. Nevertheless, the improvement and popularization of the Internet service and the implemented model of relations with the client and the state turned out to be insufficiently effective. On the contrary, there is no longer a social network where numerous groups would not be created, exposing the dishonesty of the Arend Arendovich organization.

Reviews flow in an ongoing stream, and few positive assessments of activity are issued clearly not from the heart, but under duress. This is evidenced by the insincerity of the syllable, and the "duty" phrases. The task of daily assistance to people in solving housing rental issues posed by the agency is not fulfilled - there is neither quality, nor speed, nor benefit. But there are numerous frauds of clients by the Arendovich agency, reviews literally shout about it.

Claimed benefits

The company's management claims three key points that provide an absolute advantage in the real estate market. This is a triple saving of client money, which is guaranteed by a real estate agency, together with contractual obligations and a guarantee of settlement. This is the exemption of the client from any kind of hassle, since it provides quick access to one and a half thousand of the freshest options for choosing housing in any city where the agency branches work. And this is a time saver, because the client settles in rented housing literally within fifteen minutes (this is a record), a maximum of a week later.

In all cities where this real estate agency is present, the formation of the most voluminous quantitative and relevant quality databases is promised. To do this, we use our own software and special technologies that make it possible to instantly notify the tenant about the appearance of new facilities that meet the requirements, control the quality of the information provided and analyze each service in detail.





Promises to Customers

Renting a house is a troublesome, long business, taking away a lot of nerves, time and effort, which is why people are willing to entrust the search for options to various agencies that have a high-quality database of owners. As a result, clients from the entire array of offers can choose what is consistent with all the parameters of their requests. The cost of services is fixed and is not tied to the cost of rental housing. If the selected option for some reason did not suit the client, then there is a guarantee for sixty days. Search for objects in other cities where the Arend Arendovich company is represented. All transactions should have legal support. Professional support and free consultations were also promised.





How it all began

In November 2008, the creation of a full-cycle agency with the specialization of long-term leases was completed, all the well-known beautiful names of the companies were listed, and one was chosen - Arendovich. But the Internet service was fully launched only in May 2013, and the population should have received the full provision of information services. Then all the administrative departments were formed, values, mission, tasks and goals were developed. Management and marketing were supposed to meet European quality standards. It all started so well!

In June 2013, sales offices were sequentially opened in Saratov (where a lot of negative reviews come from today), in Volgograd (where the absolutely terrible quality of customer service and outright financial fraud was noted), in Ufa (where social networks get a huge amount of resentment about the company) as well as Perm, Voronezh, Chelyabinsk, Omsk (and there is no city from which angry reviews would not come). All this means that beautiful company names do not help if it is not possible for the company to remain honest and follow the ideals it has drawn.





Priorities

Many reviews indicate that the quality of customer service is not important for the work of the company, and in the priorities only significant relief of visitors' wallets. A good name probably helped build the image of the agency. After a few years, this turned out to be a ready-made and successful business with perfectly debugged processes that bring monthly profits. Now the company is the largest on a federal scale, a prominent partner in the sales market, not afraid to quickly expand its business area.

Leasing housing is now not the only direction, sales departments are considered the main departments, and reviews are sent to them much less frequently (and the percentage of negative ones is very small). But innovation in the sale of franchises has a very wide resonance on the Internet. It was thanks to this method that numerous branches appeared in the cities of Russia and the CIS countries. The company has developed to its full potential: agents are trained in their own training center, the company has recruiting, an analytical department, advertising, public relations and much more. But it is precisely on a rental basis that the Arend Arendovich company receives overwhelmingly negative reviews.
