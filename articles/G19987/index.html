Easter Crafts - Four Ideas for Kids and Adults

Everyone is preparing for the Bright Holiday as anxiously as for the New Year or birthday. And therefore crafts for Easter, created in a close family circle, will best convey the atmosphere of the holiday spirit that reigns in the house.

Holiday cards, Easter eggs and sweets are what will make this wonderful day unforgettable.

Decorate eggs

The tradition of giving loved ones eggs for Easter goes back to ancient times, to Mary Magdalene and Emperor Tiberius. It is white and red eggs that are a symbol of the fact that Christ has truly risen. But today you can step back a little from tradition.





Glittering Eggs

In order to make such crafts for Easter, you will need:

  • three hard -boiled eggs ;
  • sparkles of three shades;
  • double sided tape;
  • hair dryer, paper and scissors.

Free-form flowers are cut from double-sided tape . Then they are carefully attached to the egg, while the front side of the flowers remains a covered protective film. Each kind of sparkles is neatly distributed on paper. Next, the protective film is removed from the pasted figures , and each egg is rolled in one of the types of sparkles. As soon as the latter completely covers the pattern of scotch tape, the eggs are gently blown with a hairdryer at the lowest speed.

Mr. and Mrs. Egg

Sweet frame

Crafts for Easter will help solve the problem of how to present sweets for the holiday, namely: make a paper basket. She will also need colored cardboard, scissors, a figured hole punch, scissors, glue and a pencil.





An arc is cut out of cardboard according to the principle described above. Only this time the lower arc will be equal to the circumference of the cake pan. From a different color of cardboard, a strip is cut out, which will become the handle of the basket, and an Easter motif is squeezed out by a curly hole punch. The blank for the basket is glued, a handle is fixed on it and the prepared patterns are launched around the circumference. Inside the cake is placed.

A bright holiday will be more fun if the whole family makes such funny crafts for Easter.
