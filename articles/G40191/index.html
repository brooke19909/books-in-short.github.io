Xiaomi Mi 5X phone: features and reviews

Xiaomi is well known to our domestic mobile device users. Her gadgets are well-deserved. The success of the Chinese brand is explained by the fact that the company sells its products at almost cost and does not try to make money on the label. Unlike the same Samsung. And the brightest representative of mid-range smartphones is Xiaomi Mi 5X. Its characteristics are such that it can easily leave behind all the other middle peasants from other manufacturers. Apparently, that's why users like Xiaomi products so much. In the meantime, a few words about the company itself.

A little bit about the company

This manufacturer began his life journey in 2010. It was then that the Chinese smartphone industry enthusiasts gathered, founded the company and began developing their own firmware. They started out of the box. The first smartphone came out a year later - in 2011. It began to be so popular that it was dismantled in moments.

They started talking about the company at home. After some time, Xiaomi released another device that also won the hearts of Chinese users. Many of them called this manufacturer the number one company. Over time, Xiaomi management decided that it was time to enter the European and Russian markets. Which was done. And now, the company has pleased its fans with Xiaomi Mi 5X. Characteristics and reviews about this device allow us to call it the first "camera phone" of the company.





The most interesting thing is that among all Chinese manufacturers, only Xiaomi did not stain itself with scandals related to industrial espionage, copying and other unseemly things. And this cannot but rejoice. We can say that Xiaomi is a completely original manufacturer with its own unique style.

Their devices, although slightly reminiscent of the products of well-known brands, have a unique zest. Therefore, this company cannot be blamed for thoughtless copying. Xiaomi is known as a manufacturer of high-quality, inexpensive and powerful smartphones. A striking representative is the smartphone Xiaomi Mi 5X. We will consider its characteristics later, but for now let's turn to the appearance of the new product.

Appearance and design

In front, the device resembles all devices of the middle price segment. Most of the front panel is occupied by a large screen. Under it are conveniently located touch navigation buttons. Above the display there is a mesh of conversational dynamics, a front camera, light and proximity sensors, as well as an infrared port.

But the back panel immediately recalls the appearance of the seventh iPhone. This similarity is inspired by the fact that the dual camera is located in the same way as in the device from the "Yabloko". But the similarity ends there. Under the camera (only closer to the middle) is a fingerprint scanner. There is nothing more on the rear panel. In addition to the logo.

The volume control buttons are located on the side faces of the gadget. There is also a hybrid SIM card slot. It is worth noting that the front panel is protected by an additional tempered Corning Gorilla Glass with a 2.5D effect. This makes the device more respectable. This is the look of Xiaomi Mi 5X. Detailed characteristics of the hardware platform we will analyze a little later. In the meantime, consider the display.





Screen specifications

So, which display is installed in the Xiaomi Mi 5X. The technical characteristics of the screen suggest that it is very worthy. Instead of the standard IPS matrix, advanced LTPS technology is used here. It allows you to get the same colors as in Super AMOLED displays that are used in smartphones from Samsung. True, there is some departure in cold tones. But this is not a big deal, since you can even out the thermal tone with the settings.

The screen has a resolution of 1920 by 1080 pixels, which means Full HD. The density is decent. With the naked eye, it is impossible to see the pixels. The display is anti - reflective . An oleophobic coating is also present. There is a lot of maximum brightness. Even with bust. In any case, in direct sunlight, the information will remain readable. We can say that this is the best screen in the middle price segment. Xiaomi, as always, did its best. And now it's time to consider the hardware platform.





Hardware platform specifications

Now let's move on to the performance of the Xiaomi Mi 5X hardware platform. The hardware features are such that the gadget can launch and pull quite demanding games such as World of Tanks Blitz and Asphalt 8 without any problems. This is facilitated by Qualcomm's eight-core 64-bit processor with a clock frequency of 2 GHz. A significant role is played by 4 GB of RAM. For graphics responsible advanced coprocessor from Adreno.

The shell works quickly, smoothly and clearly. High-definition video plays seamlessly. The built-in drive can be either 32 or 64 GB. This range of options is standard for mid-range smartphones.

For selfie and communication on Skype, it is quite suitable.





Software platform

What about the Xiaomi Mi 5X operating system? The characteristics of the software platform indicate that the gadget uses a fresh android with a proprietary MIUI shell. The sixth android is already very good, but after the corresponding refinement of the Xiaomi engineers, it became even better.

The appearance resembles both the Samsung and Apple gadgets at the same time. But everything works quickly and clearly. And the elements are arranged in a way that is more convenient for a particular user. In addition, the European version of the smartphone is devoid of any additional trash, which is usually impossible to remove. If you are the lucky owner of the Chinese version, then you have only one way out - to change the firmware to European.

There is another advantage of this device. Xiaomi regularly updates the OS even on relatively old gadgets. Any user, therefore, always has the latest firmware version.

Product Owner Reviews

So, we pass to the most interesting section about Xiaomi Mi 5X. We have already made out the specifications and photos. The time has come for feedback. What do users say about this gadget? The comments of the owners are mostly supportive. Many are pleasantly surprised by the quality of materials and the reliability of the device.

Also, most users note the incredible performance for a gadget of this level. But most of all the laudatory odes went to the smartphone camera. She really deserves praise. The autonomy of the device has also received many positive reviews. In general, almost all owners are happy with the purchase.

But most of all, users liked the price of the gadget. At first, many did not believe that such a device could be sold for a penny. However, there are those who categorically did not like the gadget. All these little things haunt these users. For example, many of them complain that the novelty is not equipped with a metal case. Also, many did not like the sound of the speaker. But all this is empty nitpicking. The main thing is that the device works quickly and clearly. And all these little things like body material do not deserve any attention.

Finally

So, we reviewed the smartphone Xiaomi Mi 5X. Its characteristics make it possible to attribute this device to the best representatives of the middle price segment. He is able to leave most of the devices in this category far behind. But the price is frankly budget. In general, everything is in the corporate style of Xiaomi. The smartphone turned out to be elegant, productive, reliable and cheap. And these are just the qualities that are valued by potential buyers most of all.
