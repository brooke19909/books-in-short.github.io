Phuong Nhung Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam): description, photos and reviews of tourists

If you plan to relax in Nha Trang (Vietnam), but do not want to splurge on an expensive hotel, then Phuong Nhung Hotel 2 * can be a good option for accommodation. We suggest to find out together what is offered to guests here.



Location

This hotel is located almost in the center of Nha Trang, 600 meters from the municipal beach. This area has a well-developed infrastructure. So, in the vicinity of the hotel you will find a lot of shops, retail shops, spa salons, cafes and restaurants, supermarkets, travel agencies, transport rental companies, etc. Also near the hotel there is a market where you can buy vegetables, fruits, and also the freshest seafood. The distance from the airport to the hotel is 45 kilometers.



Description, photo

Phuong Nhung Hotel 2 * was opened in 2009. This is a typical city hotel, consisting of a five-story building. He has no own territory. The hotel has 40 rooms of standard, superior and deluxe categories. In all apartments you will find comfortable high-quality furniture, a TV, a refrigerator with a minibar, a hairdryer, air conditioning, a tea set, a bathroom. The building is equipped with an elevator. Guests can store valuables in the safe at the reception. The hotel has at its disposal parking and a conference room.





Phuong Nhung Hotel 2 *: tourist reviews

As you know, a hotel becomes a second home for travelers on a vacation abroad. And even if you consider yourself an unassuming person, you probably want to be able to return every day to a cleanly cleaned and comfortable room that has everything you need. That is why it is important not to make a mistake in choosing a hotel. Invaluable help in this, according to many tourists, is provided by studying the reviews of those people who have already stayed in the place you are interested in. After all, they share their impressions based on their own experience. We invite you today to find out what opinion has developed among our compatriots who lived in Phuong Nhung Hotel 2 * during their vacation in Vietnam. Just note that almost all reviews are positive. This can be confirmed by the hotel rating based on guest ratings. So, out of the five maximum possible points, tourists awarded him 4.6, which can be called a very good result.

Number of rooms

In general, tourists were satisfied with the apartments of this hotel. However, many guests warn that in some of the rooms there are no windows facing the street, but only windows overlooking the corridor. If this is critical for you, then when booking the apartments, check if they have a full window. However, for many tourists this was not a problem, since they spent a minimum of time in the room. The rest of the guests claim that the apartments have everything you need for your vacation - a spacious wardrobe, a TV with one Russian-language channel, comfortable beds, a minibar, a bathroom, a hairdryer and air conditioning. The guests liked that the cost of drinks from the minibar is almost the same as in stores. Despite the fact that this is just a two-star hotel, guests are offered a full range of personal hygiene products: toilet paper, liquid soap, shower gel, shampoo, toothpaste, toothbrushes, cotton buds, etc.





Service, staff

The employees of Phuong Nhung Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang) had no complaints from vacationers. So, according to tourists, the staff here are very friendly and friendly. The hotel staff is always happy to help guests. A big plus is also our compatriots called the fact that the reception staff speak Russian.

As for the cleaning, tourists were generally satisfied with its quality. However, they note that if you do not plan to get up early in the morning, then you should hang a sign on the door asking you not to disturb. Otherwise, around eight in the morning, a maid will drop in your apartment. Rooms are cleaned and towels are changed daily. Bed linen is changed every other day.

Beach vacation

According to tourists, the path to the nearest beach from the Phuong Nhung Hotel 2 * hotel (Vietnam) takes about 10-15 minutes. In general, such a walk was even a joy for travelers. After all, the road to the sea runs through busy streets, where you can always buy something, as well as visit the massage parlor or tour desk. The beach is municipal. For the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas you will need to pay extra. But you can always sit completely free by simply laying a mat or towels directly on the sand. Experienced tourists warn those who plan to spend a vacation in Nha Trang that there is a very active sun. Therefore, it is recommended to use cosmetic products with a high degree of protection from the very first minutes, since it can be burned here very quickly. As for beach towels, they can be taken free of charge at the hotel reception. However, according to tourists, they are small, the same as towels for the bathroom. If necessary, you can always buy them in the nearest market or in a store. The prices here are reasonable.
