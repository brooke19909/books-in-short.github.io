What to see: excursions, attractions

Excursion trips will not only help diversify your vacation, but will also give you a lot of vivid impressions. What attractions can tourists who choose the Club Hotel Tropicana 3 * hotel for relaxation? Tunisia is located on the territory of the ancient Carthaginian civilization, famous for victorious military campaigns. You can touch the ancient history of this legendary state by visiting the ruins and catacombs of Carthage, located in the vicinity of the resort town of Sousse (Sousse).

But to visit this city, located just 20 km from Skanes, is not only for the ruins of Carthage. Sousse is called a real "gem" for tourists. Here you can find relaxation for every taste. For lovers of fun around the clock an incredible number of clubs, cafes, restaurants, coffee houses, shops, discos. The city has a huge water park and the famous zoological garden. In August, Sousse turns into a huge "stage" for the annual "Aussu" - a summer festival, which includes a dance and music festival and a large carnival procession.

In addition, there are many historical sites in the city of Sousse. It is believed that the port city, founded by the Phoenician sailors, is older than Carthage itself. You can verify this by visiting Medina, the historical part of any Tunisian city. In the surroundings of the Sousse of the city of Sousse there is a historical museum in the building of an ancient castle, a military fortress made of stone, ancient mosques and early Christian catacombs, high military and religious towers, a museum of archeology and the Dar Essid house museum. And this is not a complete list of all the cultural and historical objects of this resort city,

A two-day excursion in the Sahara desert is very popular among tourists. During the trip, tourists will see the caves - the Berbers' dwellings, a delightful oasis, a crocodile farm, the ruins of the ancient Roman amphitheater and the natural scenery of the filming of the legendary Star Wars movie saga.

But a sightseeing tour of the nearby city of Monastir can be quite interesting entertainment. Many of the tourists who visited this historic village claim that Monastir is not very interesting, and the impression of antiquities spoils the plain appearance of modern buildings and a large amount of garbage in the streets. However, a guided tour can be quite informative. In the city you can see the historical part of the city, Medina, the first buildings of which arose in the Middle Ages. There is also an ancient Muslim cemetery dating back to the 12th century, the magnificent Mausoleum of Habib Bourguiba (the first president of this country) and the colossal building of the Bourguiba Mosque, the ancient Great Mosque and the memory of the time of Turkish rule - the Brick Gate. In addition, in the vicinity of the Medina there is an old military fortress Ribat with a viewing platform.

The city has several parks, a beautiful promenade for leisurely walks, the Museum of traditional national costumes and the Museum of Islam and other interesting cultural sites. Shopping enthusiasts can find shops and souvenir shops and local crafts in the city, and visit the bustling and colorful Arab market.
