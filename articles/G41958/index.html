Electric hob 2 burners electric: description, power, photo

When starting a repair in the kitchen, the owners of the house must definitely think about purchasing new household appliances. She should not only harmoniously fit into the overall interior, but also become a real assistant to the hostess. Today, gas stoves and electric stoves have been replaced by compact cooking surfaces. They make it easy to maintain cleanliness in the cooking area.

One of the most popular types of built-in appliances is an electric hob with 2 burners. What characteristics to pay attention to when choosing, expert advice will help you figure it out.

Features of the technique

A 2-burner electric hob (photo below) is a new word in the organization of the kitchen space. She is distinguished by elegance and style. Due to the huge number of options, you can fit this household appliances in almost any interior.



Today, the buyer is provided with a wide selection of products. Electric varieties are very popular. They are easy to install and manage. Any combination of burners can be created from the surface modules . Also the difference lies in the materials, shape and size of the cooking surfaces.

It is not necessary to place burners above the oven. You can mount the hob in any convenient place in the kitchen. Installation height may also vary. The presented household appliances provide high hygiene in the kitchen space. Cleaning is much easier.

Key questions when choosing

A 2-burner electric hob requires a serious approach when choosing. You need to know which parameters to pay attention to first.

First you need to correctly calculate the size of the burners. They should look harmonious in the kitchen space. The shape may also vary. It can be very different. The choice depends on the taste preferences of the owners and the features of the interior.



Also pay attention to the material of which the surface is made, power. The type of surface may differ according to the heating principle. Having decided on these issues, you need to pay attention to the manufacturer of the equipment. It is necessary to purchase products from trusted brands.

Criteria

An electric hob for 2 burners is suitable for both one and a small family of up to 3 people. Their size can vary significantly. You should also consider how often you have to cook at home. Some bachelors do not need large, powerful burners. They need a small hob.



The presented household appliances can be installed in almost any convenient place in the kitchen. If it is mounted above the oven, their control panels can be combined. This will save space. A separate hob and oven require separate adjustment. This is a more expensive installation option.

If the owners choose between a stove and a hob, it should be said that the second option is more expensive. However, this technique will decorate the room with its stylish appearance. It will complement the design of the kitchen, will become functional equipment.

Material

Various hobs for 2 burners can be made of various materials . Glass ceramics, enamel and stainless steel are the most common options.



The most inexpensive option is an enameled surface. The hobs in this case protrude above the surface. This is a durable form of technology. Enamel color can be any. However, the enamel is difficult to clean from plaque, contamination. Hot plates heat up for a long time.

Today, stainless steel surfaces are on sale. This is a more modern type of household appliance. The surface requires special care. Glass ceramics belongs to the category of the most popular varieties. Hot plates are hidden under the surface. This gives the panel a stylish, sleek look. Caring for the coating will be very simple.

Glass Ceramics

The popularity of glass ceramics today remains consistently high. This material is used in combination with several varieties of electric hobs for 2 burners. Induction, rapid, halogen and Hi-Life varieties are today the most popular.



Rapid burners are made in the form of a spiral. Heating occurs within 10 s. Halogen devices are characterized by greater efficiency. In addition to the spiral, a lamp is present in their design. It radiates not only light, but also heat. In this case, the entire surface is heated. Burners of the Hi-Life type have a tape heating element.

Inductive hobs are economical and efficient. Heat is generated exclusively at the bottom of the dishes. However, only pots and pans that have a magnetized base can be used for these purposes.

The advantages of induction cookers

The 2-burner built-in electric hob is a stylish element of a modern kitchen. However, the most popular in this category are induction hobs. They have a lot of advantages.



The included surface remains cold until special cookware is installed on it. It has a magnetized bottom. The burner is intended exclusively for such kitchen utensils. The walls of the pan remain cold.

These are the safest and most economical burners. The food heats up very quickly. In this case, the cost of electricity will be significantly less. If you accidentally come into contact with the included surface, you can not be afraid to get a burn. Caring for such surfaces is easy. Therefore, most buyers today prefer this type of burners.

Disadvantages of induction cookers

An electric hob for 2 induction-type burners also has some disadvantages. You need to know about them before buying. First of all, it will be necessary to revise all the kitchen utensils, which previously could be put on the stove. Enameled and cast iron pots, pans are suitable for the induction surface . Copper, glass or aluminum cookware is not suitable.

The radiation that these burners emit is not yet fully understood. The effect of such plates on the human body is also unknown. There are reviews that workers of enterprises producing domestic appliances are paid extra for harmfulness, and short shifts are established.



The cost of induction cookers is high. This is one of the latest developments in the field. Therefore, the purchase of an induction cooker will require more family budget funds than buying a surface made of stainless steel or enamel.

Shape and size

Cooking surfaces consist of modules. This allows you to arrange them in the kitchen in different ways. The surface configuration may vary depending on the design chosen. Rectangular hobs are on sale. Modules can be put in the form of rhombuses that touch the edges.

The size of the modules has standard dimensions. This will allow them to be embedded directly into kitchen furniture. This greatly facilitates the work of designing the space in the room. Modules can be hexagonal or rectangular.

The dimensions of the electric hob for 2 burners may vary depending on the power. The width of the smallest panels is 25 cm. Powerful varieties of equipment reach dimensions of 90 cm. Depth is 60 cm.

Power

The power of the electric hob for 2 burners can vary from 2 to 6 kW. The consumption indicator is affected not only by the number of burners, but also by their size. Most often, models with heating elements of different diameters are on sale. They differ in performance.

Standard models include one powerful burner up to 3 kW. It can be supplemented with a small heating element. Such a burner can consume 1-1.5 kW. This indicator depends on the diameter. The smaller the burner, the lower the level of energy consumption.

There are other varieties of burners. The smallest circuits can consume 0.4-1 kW. They are used in the process of making porridge, coffee. Quickly heated burners have the highest power. They are designed for large dishes. When choosing a cooking surface performance, it is necessary to take into account the features of its operation.

Wiring requirements

Deciding to purchase an electric hob for 2 burners, it is necessary to take into account the requirements of the presented equipment for wiring in the house. It must be new. To connect the hob, you need to draw a separate line from the cover.

To choose the correct wire cross-section, it is necessary to calculate the total power of household appliances. If the central power supply line in the house is old, it is not recommended to install a device with a rated power of more than 3.5 kW.

The wire for connection must have a copper core. It is recommended to give preference to wires with a special system of protection against overheating and fire. Wire cross section must be at least 4 mm¬≤. The network must provide grounding. Connecting the hob via a power outlet is not recommended. No other electrical devices may be connected to the line.

Manufacturer

Today, hobs are produced by many well-known manufacturers. Conventionally, they can be divided into three categories in accordance with the cost of technology. In the low price category, one can distinguish such brands as Ariston, Ardo. If you want to purchase a simple white electric 2-burner hob, pay attention to these manufacturers. Their products will be of high quality, without additional features.

In the mid-price segment, manufacturers Electrolux, Bosch, Zanussi, Whirlpool, etc. stand out. Here you can choose both a simple and reliable hob, as well as multi-functional household appliances.

Among the elite brands that produce high-class kitchen equipment, AEG, Miele, Gaggenau stand out. These are the most expensive and high-quality devices. They include many advanced features. Such devices will be durable and comfortable to use.

Additional functions

A 2-burner electric hob may have a list of additional functions. They significantly increase comfort in the process of using the stove.

Some functions provide for the self-switching off of a burner when liquid enters it. When boiling water from the pan, the equipment stops heating. This feature greatly improves equipment safety. Some models have a timer. You can leave the dishes on the stove without worrying that you can forget about it.

If the house has small children, you can configure the lock function. The kid will not be able to independently establish the operation mode of the stove.

Most models have a protective cover. During operation of the hob, it protects the wall from accidental contamination. When the equipment is turned off, the cover will protect the equipment from accidental mechanical shock. The closed surface can be used as a table. There are models with metal and glass covers. The choice depends on the type of interior decoration.

Having considered the features of electric hobs for 2 burners, everyone will be able to choose the right option for themselves. A variety of shapes, sizes, heating principles will allow you to purchase the best equipment for your kitchen.
