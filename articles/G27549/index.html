ClubHotel Riu Palm Azur 4 * (Tunisia / Djerba): hotel description, services, reviews

Our article will be of interest to those who are planning a vacation in Tunisia and are choosing the right hotel. For active tourists, the island of Djerba will certainly be interesting, it is where ClubHotel Riu Palm Azur is located.

A bit about the hotel ...

ClubHotel Riu Palm Azur was renovated in 2011. Since then, it belongs to the Riu network. The hotel is located in the southern part of the island of Djerba in the middle of a beautiful oasis, 23 kilometers from Houmt Souk (the island's capital), 30 kilometers from Djerba Airport and 6 kilometers from Midoun. The complex offers vacationers good spacious rooms and a high level of service. The hotel is focused on families with children. If you are going on vacation to Tunisia and consider exclusively hotels with a children's room, then you should pay attention to Riu Palm Azur.

Number of rooms

ClubHotel Riu Palm Azur has 326 rooms:



  1. Standard rooms are double rooms, in which there is the possibility of placing two additional beds. Among the apartments of this category there are also connect-rooms.
  2. Family rooms - rooms equipped with two bedrooms.
  3. Prestige suites - apartments with a separate bedroom and beautiful sea views.

All rooms are equipped with a terrace or balcony, hairdryer, bathtub and shower, mini fridge, central heating or air conditioning, satellite TV.

Dining at the hotel

ClubHotel Riu Palm Azur operates on the popular all-inclusive system. It has an Asian restaurant, Wassabi, and a traditional Tunisian establishment, Le Jasmin. In addition, the hotel has three bars: a lobby bar, a lounge bar and a poolside bar.

Food is served in the Royal's main restaurant with an outdoor terrace. For breakfast, lunch and dinner, a large assortment of buffet dishes is served. According to the all-inclusive concept, local drinks are served to tourists free of charge. The menu includes vegetarian options. In the summertime, for lunch and dinner, guests are offered food prepared directly at the restaurant.

A little bit about the resort ...

Djerba is the southernmost resort of Tunisia, where the temperature is always a couple of degrees higher than on the continent. It is famous not only for its sandy beaches, but also worthy of thalassotherapy centers.



Useful procedures can be alternated with walks on the medina of the capital city of Houmt Souk. The island is worth seeing the fort of Bordj el Kebir and the pottery workshops of the town of Gellala.

Djerba boasts the most beautiful flamingo birds, which can be seen here in natural habitats.

The island is incredibly beautiful and romantic, a large number of legends were composed about it. It was first inhabited by the Phoenicians in the seventh century BC. A good location, rich nature, and non-decreasing reserves of fresh water - all this attracted representatives of various nations who were eager to win a small piece of paradise.

Djerba is an exquisite resort. Thanks to the mild climate, vacationers visit the island almost all year round. Palm, olive and fig groves, fruit plantations, lotus placers make Djerba a stunningly beautiful place. The island has long intertwined the ancient traditions of different nations.

Tours and activities

In the capital of the island of Houmt Suk, there is an old city located behind the fortress wall with numerous mosques. Walking along the medina along the white Arab houses, you can walk to three mosques: Jamaa-Ettruk, Ekh-Sheikh, Jamma-al-Gorba.

On the island is the famous crocodile farm.

The soul of the island is the old Jewish district of Riyardh, seven kilometers south of the capital, Djerba. Here, tourists can admire the oldest synagogue in North Africa, El Riba. In its walls are kept the manuscripts of the Torah.



Vacationers seeking to learn the culture of the local population can visit the enographic museum of Guelala. It presents a variety of displays of household items and utensils of Tunisians, which talk about wedding customs and family life. In addition, this place is considered the city of potters, where you can cheaply buy beautiful dishes, vases, figurines. I must say that all the villages of the island are engaged in some crafts, so tourists can buy not only souvenirs at affordable prices, but also useful items. Djerba, for example, is famous for blankets, carpets, silver jewelry and wickerwork.

Have a good time at Djerba Golf. In addition, the island has the largest African casino and diving center. Relaxing on Djerba, you can take riding lessons, see the island from a bird's eye view, visit fish taverns, which prepare the best dishes of fresh fish and other seafood.

Nature has endowed this region with beautiful sandy beaches with large palm trees and a turquoise sea. The best beaches are in the north-east of Djerba.
