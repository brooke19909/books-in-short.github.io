Best bowling in Irkutsk

If you want to organize a bright and active holiday, then bowling is the perfect place for this. This gambling and addictive game is popular around the world among people of different ages. If you live in Irkutsk or are visiting this city, then you should definitely visit one of the bowling clubs.

Sports and entertainment complex "7 Mile"

When choosing a bowling alley in Irkutsk, you should definitely visit the one located in this sports and entertainment complex, as it meets all the requirements and the highest level.

The bowling center is equipped with all the necessary professional equipment. Thanks to the rational use of space, game and recreation areas are conveniently and comfortably located. Polite and qualified employees will provide all their visitors with complete information about all the services provided.

Among other things, the school has a bowling center for both children and adults. It regularly hosts promotions and various events.

You can visit this bowling at the address: Irkutsk, st. Shiryamova, 19 B.

"Star"

Especially for all residents and guests of the city, the bowling "Star" was opened in Irkutsk. It is equipped with the latest and greatest equipment. It offers 8 special tracks, 4 of which are located in a particularly comfortable area and have an amazing effect. There is nothing like this in any bowling center in Irkutsk.

Thanks to the comfortable room, there is enough room for bowling for everyone, both amateurs and professionals. Experienced instructors will not disregard a single person. They will explain the rules of the game on a professional level, tell and show how to throw the ball correctly, pick it up in size and weight.

Bowling "Star" is located at: st. December Events, 102/1.

Cosmodrome

Bowling center "Cosmodrome" will not leave indifferent any visitor. Here for visitors all the necessary conditions are created. The institution has 12 branded tracks, which are additionally equipped with special children's sides and equipped with comfortable and lightweight balls.

The club is considered to be one of the best venues both for celebrations and for daily bowling. The facility also has a special relaxation area. Where you can relax and order food and drinks. For the youngest visitors a special children's menu.

Bowling is located in Irkutsk on the street. Soviet, 109/1.
