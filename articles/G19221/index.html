How to choose an industrial vacuum cleaner

Anyone who encounters repair work knows how much dirt and dust is generated. Sources of dust generation are wood sawing, building mixtures, metal cutting, and much more. All this is formed only in the apartment, where the volume of work is quite small. And if you imagine how much dust is generated in the production, it immediately becomes clear why an industrial vacuum cleaner is needed. It will allow you to clean the room from dirt and dust, which is not only pollution, but also acts as a dangerous production factor that can cause the disease. Therefore, the industrial vacuum cleaner "Karcher" is necessary not only to ensure cleanliness, but also as a tool for caring for the health of staff.



This device differs from a domestic analogue in its ability to continuously work for a long time and adaptability to rather difficult operating conditions. It can not be attributed to the category of household appliances, since the scope of its use is more industrial. An industrial vacuum cleaner is selected according to such a basic parameter as power. The higher this indicator, the better and better the device will cope with the tasks assigned to it. In some models, an increase in power is achieved by installing two engines. As a standard one works, and if necessary it is possible to connect the second to work. But an increase in power leads to an increase in the weight of the device, so it is important here not to overdo it. The mass of the unit is one of the main characteristics, since an industrial vacuum cleaner needs to be moved along a construction site, which sometimes has several floors. And here when choosing it is worth considering this nuance.


When choosing, it is necessary to pay attention to such parameters as tank volume, support for wet cleaning, the ability to connect to a dust suction tool directly during operation. The presence or absence of such options affects the cost of the device. The presence of nozzles of different profiles in the kit is an important point. They allow cleaning in hard to reach places.



An industrial vacuum cleaner, the price of which is from 20 thousand rubles or more, differs from a household one in that it does not fail when working in difficult conditions. High-quality industrial equipment is able to cope with its tasks. From the effective collection of construction waste, cleaning of various difficult places, timely disposal of waste from various construction processes, high-quality cleaning of various objects, not only the speed of construction, but also business efficiency, people's safety, and environmental friendliness depend. That is why there is no need to save on production tools, which gives comfort and coziness, and also makes a profit.

If you decide to choose an industrial vacuum cleaner, then you must understand that this is an important and crucial moment, which should be approached with an understanding of the matter, as well as the tasks that will face it.
