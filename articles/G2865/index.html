Holiday Village "Polushkino", Ryazan Oblast: description and reviews

Ryazan region is famous for its natural landscapes. Here you can still find protected forests, untouched by human hand meadows and lakes with clear water. The Meshchera Lowland is a natural park whose beauty was praised by poets and writers several centuries ago. Description and photo of the recreation center



The modern tourist center "Polushkino" is located in close proximity to the village of the same name. The recreation center is surrounded by coniferous forests and fragrant meadows. The Pra River flows nearby, there are several natural lakes. "Polushkino" is a recreation center located at a distance from major highways. Here you can fully enjoy the fresh air, silence and admire the diversity of the plant world. At the same time, it is not necessary to refuse the benefits of civilization and domestic comfort. Vacationers are offered accommodation in modern rooms or individual cottages.

Accommodation and prices



The recreation center "Polushkino" welcomes guests year-round. Vacationers are offered various accommodation options: these are individual rooms in shared buildings or detached cottages. The cost of apartments is from 3000 rubles / day. Each room has all the necessary furniture for a comfortable stay, a private bathroom, and a kitchen area for cooking. A separate cottage is an ideal option for a large company (up to 18 people). In fact, this is a full-fledged house in which several families can live comfortably at the same time. Isolated bedrooms, shared living room and kitchen. The VIP cottage has a gym and table tennis table. A nice bonus for vacationers is a personal area for relaxing on the street, a gazebo for cooking barbecue and a warm veranda. The cost of renting a cottage is from 14,000 rubles / day (capacity - up to 18 people).

Organization of leisure and additional services

What to do while relaxing at Polushkino? The recreation center has well-equipped barbecue areas, playgrounds, a bathhouse and a billiard room. There is also a restaurant with a banquet room, where you can have a delicious dinner or celebrate the celebration. There is a rental of sports equipment and accessories for outdoor activities. Here you can always rent bicycles, balls, badminton sets, everything you need for fishing, skiing, skating and tubing. Just 150 meters from the apartment houses is the beach of the Pra River. In summer, it is ideal for swimming, and you can fish all year round. Another entertainment option is walking in the picturesque surroundings. The recreation center "Polushkino" is an ideal place for fans of ecotourism. In summer, you can pick berries, mushrooms and herbs.
