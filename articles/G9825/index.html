Smartphone processor

The basis of the Lenovo A369i is a very modest chip by today's standards. This is MT6572 with the index "W". It has only 2 computing modules based on the energy-efficient A7 architecture, but it cannot boast a high level of performance. The processor itself is manufactured according to the standards of the 28-nm process. The clock frequency of each of the modules can reach 1.3 GHz at maximum load. This CPU can easily cope with tasks such as browsing Internet resources, reading books, listening to radio and music, simple entry-level games. Even movies can be watched on it, but not in 1920 x 1080 resolution (although this may work in some cases). What certainly will not be launched on this device is the most demanding 3D toys. This chip is not designed for them - for these purposes you need to buy a more expensive device.

Device camera

There is only the main camera in the Lenovo A369i. Photos and videos obtained with its help are of very modest quality. This is not surprising: it is based on a sensor of only 2 megapixels. This is clearly not enough to get high-quality photos and videos. Also, the smartphone does not have a backlight and autofocus system. That is, this device cannot take pictures when there is a lack of lighting, and when you try to capture the text, it will turn out to be blurry in most cases. A video camera can only shoot in 720 p.
