The best Vietnam all-inclusive hotels: photos and reviews

Vietnam - once the state of the "third world", and today one of the most popular Asian resorts. If you want to be alone with yourself, feel the peace and merge with the virgin nature, go now to a travel agency and book a tour to Vietnam. If you compare it with Thailand, Vietnam boasts endless sandy beaches, stunning nature, exotic, cleanliness and silence. There are not many vacationers, and therefore the sea is clean and fresh. Demand, as you know, creates supply, which is why the tourism industry in Vietnam is growing rapidly. Here, a lot of first-class hotels have appeared, working on the all inclusive system . As part of this material, we will evaluate the best all-inclusive hotels in Vietnam. The basis for the review will be reviews of real vacationers.





Features

Tourists, accustomed to relaxing in the luxurious resorts of Turkey and Egypt, begin to look for the same service and comfort in other countries of the world. Many believe that the resorts of Vietnam are far from civilization, they are unlikely to satisfy the demanding and demanding taste of tourists. In fact, over the past few years, several excellent all-inclusive villas and hotels have appeared on the South Sea coast. All of them are located in the resorts of Nha Trang and Phan Thiet, but most likely, in the near future, something similar will appear in other very popular resort cities of Vietnam.

Dessole Sea Lion Nha Trang Resort

If you are interested in the best all-inclusive Vietnam hotels, pay attention to this option. Judging by the reviews of tourists, today it is the most luxurious hotel, endowed with a number of advantages. First of all, it is only a 5-10 minute drive from the airport, which is so important in the case of such a long and tiring flight. The hotel occupies an impressive territory, located near the Bai Dai beach, just 20 meters from the warm and gentle South Sea.





The hotel has 311 rooms, 16 of which are freestanding bungalows buried in verdure of tropical palm trees. By the way, if you want to fully experience the exotic of the Asian resort, give preference to a bungalow. As for entertainment: here they are presented in sufficient variety. For leisure, a team of professional animators is responsible, in whose company it will never be boring. The food is varied, of high quality, pleased tourists and a decent selection of alcohol, available around the clock. The hotel combines the advantages of a peaceful, remote from the hustle and bustle of relaxation, at the same time it will be an excellent option for those who prefer nightlife, bars, discos and outdoor activities.

Issue price

Of course, it is difficult to compare Vietnam all-inclusive hotels with hotels of the same category, say, in Turkey or Egypt. It is important to understand that the cost of rest in such hotels will fly you into a tidy amount. On average, prices for vacations in all-inclusive Vietnam hotels range from 150,000 to 300,000 rubles. for double occupancy. This price usually includes a round-trip flight, hotel accommodation (11-12 days), all-inclusive meals, including local drinks and insurance. For Russians, visiting Vietnam does not require a preliminary visa, so many advanced tourists wait for a "collapse" in prices when it becomes possible to purchase a last-minute tour with a discount of up to 30-40% of the initial cost.

To summarize

All-inclusive Vietnam hotels are a great opportunity to spend an unforgettable vacation in an Asian country without worrying about additional expenses. If the main purpose of your visit to the country is to get acquainted with its color, history, you expect to spend most of the time on excursions, assume active movement along the beaches and regions of the country, paying extra for round-the-clock meals, drinks and chic hotel grounds is the height of recklessness.
