Hotel at the airport of Kazan. Where is she and what is she?

Hotel in Kazan near the airport - "Flight"

The first of the places that I want to describe is the Polet Hotel . It is located 270 meters from Terminal No. 1. Having settled in it in case of an unexpected delay in your flight, you will have the opportunity to relax right in your room. In this hotel you can choose one of the offered levels of room comfort based on your needs and, of course, the amount in your wallet.

The hotel rooms have beds (double or single), TV, bath or shower, comfortable tables and chairs, as well as comfortable sofas. The hotel has eight rooms of the first category (designed for nine beds), as well as thirty rooms of the second category (that is, forty-nine beds). Total in the hotel there are fifty-eight places for visitors. The sum for the numbers of the first and second categories does not exceed 3800 rubles per day.

Aviator is another Kazan hotel near the airport

The next hotel is less than one kilometer from the airport. Here you will be provided with Wi-Fi in the lobby and in the room, parking. In addition, there is a restaurant, bar and gym. By the way, pets are allowed in this hotel. And if you have a little four-legged friend with you, then this place will be the best option for a temporary stop with your pet.

The rooms have a shower, Internet access, cable TV and, of course, TV, air conditioning, desk and electric kettle. Central heating is also provided. Ordering one number per day will be about 2215 rubles for you.

It should be noted that this Kazan hotel near the airport has not only a convenient location for people who have come here from far away, but also has many good reviews from its guests. All clients noted the cleanliness of the rooms, their compliance with the basic requirements, as well as the friendliness and friendliness of the staff and the excellent service.

Hotel "Dune"

This small hotel Kazan from the airport is almost four kilometers away. It is the farthest of all presented here, but nonetheless is not inferior in quality to the others.

This is a hotel with twelve comfortable rooms, each of which has a TV, air conditioning, Internet and Wi-Fi, mini fridge, telephone, separate toilet and bathroom, and you can stay here with your pets. The cost of one room per day here is about 2500 rubles.
