What to choose - Nikon or Canon?



The world of photography today is filled with many different photographic equipment from a variety of manufacturers. But the real giants that have been fighting each other for many years are the companies "Canon" and "Nikon". When buying the first SLR camera, you will surely seek the advice of your friend who has it - Nikon or Canon. Most likely, he will advise you the one that he has. When choosing between these brands, you should pay attention to their characteristics and price ratio. It is important to remember one simple thing that not a company, but a separate model acts as a determining factor. For example, when choosing among entry-level and advanced-level cameras, competition is conditional. In the lines of both companies, relative to the two above segments, there are no models absolutely identical in their capabilities. All of them are presented to us as if in steps, that is, at first a model of one company is produced, and then another, thereby gradually raising the interested person to the upper price segment. You can spend a lot of time for nothing, just studying and comparing the ergonomics of these cameras, as well as looking at mountains of all kinds of graphs and analytical reports. The logical question arises: "Why are you buying a camera?" Obviously not for photographing rulers or comparative stands. Based on this, it should be remembered that the main distinguishing feature of these two brands is the large "mushroom" at the "Canon", which makes it very convenient for people with a large palm. On the contrary, for those with a small palm, Nikon is perfect. The company "Canon" for convenience combined the basic settings with the control buttons in the menu. Nikon - cameras that have a beautiful graphical menu, and not digital, like a competitor.
