What is a

Location

Samara region is one of the most beautiful territories of Russia. It has a continental temperate climate with hot summers and cold winters. The area is covered with rocky mountains, turning into forests and swamps. The Volga and Samara rivers, unique mineral rivulets carry their waters throughout the territory. On the shore of the Zhiguli Sea is located "Hope", the resort of Togliatti. It is located in the heart of the green zone of the reserve. If you go by car, then you will have to drive only 100 km from Samara on the highway. You will have to go part of the path through the forest, it will be an unforgettable walk.

Guests from neighboring areas or remote cities can take advantage of air transportation services. Landing at the Samara International Airport will almost complete your journey, there will be 40 km to the place, which can be reached by taxi.

The local microclimate, due to the proximity of the sea and the Zhiguli mountains, intoxicating air of crystal purity will begin to heal you immediately. The mood will improve, sleep will normalize, the metabolism will come in order. And the most interesting landscape and historical monuments make its location unique.





Sanatorium Profile

This is a wellness, general therapeutic institution. If there are serious diseases of the skin or individual organs, doctors are likely to recommend another resort. But given that in the Samara region there are about a hundred of them, there is still a high probability of treatment in these parts.

To relax, restore strength, prevent the disease at its very beginning or consolidate the effect of medical treatment, tourists come to the sanatorium "Nadezhda" in Togliatti. Experienced medical staff and the latest equipment will allow for accurate diagnosis and prescribe effective treatment. The main areas are examination of the genitourinary and endocrine systems, the next (according to the frequency of visits) are the respiratory and digestive organs, then the musculoskeletal system, the cardiovascular and nervous systems.

On the territory of the sanatorium there are narrow-profile specialists who can advise you at any time.
