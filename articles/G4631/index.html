Hotel Margadina 3 (Cyprus): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Ayia Napa has been considered among the Russians one of the most popular Cypriot resorts for many years. Not so long ago, on the site of this developed town was a small fishing village. Now Ayia Napa, conveniently located in a secluded bay near Cape Greco, has become one of the first-class resorts in the region, which at the same time managed to not lose its provincial charm.

From mythology

According to legend, once upon a time in place of modern Ayia Napa an impenetrable dense forest grew, and therefore the whole territory was considered uninhabited. However, a stray hunter in a cave often found an image of the Mother of God on the wall. Since then, this place is called Ayia Napa, which from ancient Greek translates as "sacred forest." This small fishing village became a flourishing resort not so long ago, however, despite its comparative youth, it is rapidly developing and is gaining more and more popularity.

Holidays in Ayia Napa

This direction is chosen mainly by young people. The reason is that the resort is quite lively and noisy for fans of solitude and a relaxing holiday. During tours to Ayia Napa, tourists live a busy life, not even calming down deep at night. Here you can dance all night long at discos or in numerous clubs, show your singing talent in karaoke, enjoy excellent cuisine, drink the legendary Cypriot wine and just enjoy spending time in tiny, but very cozy taverns.



In Ayia Napa, cultural or entertainment holidays are constantly held, for example, Green Monday, the Day of the Apparition of the Holy Week, the carnival before Maslenitsa, the festival in commemoration of the global flood, Easter holidays, etc.

To the tourist note

The summer season at the resort is rich in a wide variety of holidays, so those who like mass events come here. However, if tourists cannot combine their holidays with any of the holidays, you should not get upset: Ayia Napa is generous with other entertainments. For example, in the Marine Park you can visit dolphin shows here every day. Children under twelve years old can come to them for free. Among the places worthy of close attention, one can distinguish the Venetian monastery, built in the sixteenth century in honor of the Virgin Mary. This holy monastery, which since 1978 serves as the Ecumenical Conference Center, is considered the hallmark of the resort. Hotels

The resort annually receives thousands of tourists from around the world. A significant proportion of travelers in this army are Russians. Ayia Napa pleases visitors not only with a comfortable climate, but also with picturesque bays and sandy beaches, vibrant nightlife and interesting sights. Conquers tourists and the hospitality of hotels in the resort. All conditions have been created in them so that residents feel at home here.

Ayia Napa hotels, like many in Cyprus, are built right on the coast or across the road. Those apartments that are offered in four- or five-star hotels have a very developed infrastructure and are fully consistent with their category. For example, living in an Alion Beach Hotel 5 * per person per night will cost an average of about three thousand three hundred rubles with the concept of a continental breakfast. In general, almost all Ayia Napa hotels offer good service and a high level of comfort, so even two- or three-star accommodation options will not disappoint travelers. One of these hotels is Margadina 3 (Margadina 3 *).

Description

The hotel is located one hundred and fifty meters from the municipal white sandy beach. It is surrounded by many bars, taverns and restaurants. Just ten kilometers away is one of the most beautiful places in all of Cyprus - Cape Greco.

Larnaca International Airport is only forty minutes away. This factor is very relevant for those who come to the Margadina 3 * hotel with children. As a rule, little travelers do not tolerate transfers, so the shorter the transfer, the more convenient it is not only for the kids, but also for their parents.

The hotel area, although relatively small, but fully equipped. Everywhere there is a lot of greenery, palm trees and citrus trees are planted, between which you can stroll along the snow-white paths in the evenings, breathing in the aroma of flowers and the sea.

The last overhaul in Margadina 3 * (Cyprus) was carried out in 2012. The rooms are stylishly decorated, all furniture and plumbing are completely replaced.

As in any other hotel, Margadina 3 * has certain canons for residents. Time for check-in is set at two in the afternoon, check-out is at noon. Beach

Tourists call the sea in Ayia Napa nothing less than incomparable. In May, it is still slightly cool in the morning, but the water heats up during the day. The beach is municipal, like everywhere in Crete. It is large and long with a snow-white sandy coating. You can walk to the coast from the hotel in five minutes. Sun beds and umbrellas are issued for a fee - five euros per set.



Many Russians living in Margadina 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) liked Nissi Beach more, where a city bus leaves every half an hour from the hotel. Travel time is twenty minutes, the fare is 1 euro one way. Nissi is considered one of the best beaches in all of Europe. And although there is always a lot of people on it, it is so big that those who bathe each other do not interfere at all.
