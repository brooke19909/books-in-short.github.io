Natural features

Ulyanovsk region is located in the western part of the country, in the very center of the Volga region. It is located at the site of several rivers. Among them are the Volga, Cheremshan, Sura and Sviyaga. They formed a closed pool, which is located in the Caspian Sea. The Kuibyshev reservoir is also located in these territories. The advantages of this area include a large number of water resources concentrated here. Local motels used this feature in the formation of wellness techniques. Each visitor can count on an individual approach and care. The cost of services will pleasantly surprise any guest.
