Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Alanya): reviews and photos

Turkey, as before, remains one of the most popular countries for recreation. For this reason, in our article we want to talk about the Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * hotel located on the Mediterranean coast.

Hotel location

The hotel was founded in 2004, the last restoration took place in 2006. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe complex is fourteen and a half thousand square meters. Previously, the hotel was called Aska Club N Resort & Spa. Later the name was changed, and now it is known as Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 *. The complex consists of a number of buildings. The main building is five-story, in addition, there are eight more five-story and three-story buildings. More than half of all rooms have beautiful views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * is located on the coast. From the complex to the international airport in Antalya, only ninety-five kilometers, and thirty kilometers to the town of Alanya.



The hotel is located at: Alara Riva Turizm Merkezi, Okurcalar Beldesi, Alanya.

Number of rooms

Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * has 323 rooms. The hotel is capable of hosting over 650 guests.

All apartments are divided into categories. The complex has 299 standards and ten family rooms. Staying in a hotel with animals is strictly prohibited. For physically challenged people, there are three specialized rooms.

Apartments are cleaned daily and linen is changed a couple of times a week.

  1. Standart has an area of ‚Äã‚Äãup to twenty-four square meters. This room can accommodate up to three people. The apartment has a bedroom, a balcony and a bathroom. There are 299 such rooms in the hotel.
  2. Famile room is an apartment ranging in size from thirty-six to forty square meters. These rooms can accommodate up to four guests due to the presence of two bedrooms.

All apartments are equipped with a hairdryer, safe (for a fee), telephone, TV, minibar (replenished several times a week). The rooms are connected to a single air conditioning system. For our compatriots, a pleasant bonus is the fact that you can watch five Russian channels on TV. All rooms have extra beds. Room service and reception are open around the clock.



All the furniture in the rooms is in good condition. As for cleaning, sometimes there are comments among vacationers regarding its quality. For example, maids may forget to dust. But overall the hotel is pretty clean. Bed linen changes regularly, although it is clearly noticeable that the sets should be replaced long ago (sometimes there are holes and spots).

According to vacationers, the best rooms are located in the building, which is the closest to the sea.

Hotel infrastructure

Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya, Okurcalar) has a small but very green area. The infrastructure of the complex is well developed so that vacationers do not get bored during the holidays.

The hotel has two outdoor pools, in addition, there is another indoor heated pool (one hundred seventeen meters long). There are also water slides. Guests of the complex can visit the tennis court, amphitheater, shopping center, spa (for a fee), a hairdresser and a fitness room. For business people there are several conference rooms with all equipment. If necessary, you can use the services of a doctor (for a fee).



If you wish, you can rent a car for travel on site.

Sport

Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * (photos are given in the article) offers its guests a huge selection of a variety of water activities: skiing, banana, catamarans, parasailing. Women can please themselves on vacation with a visit to the Turkish bath, jacuzzi, sauna, massage, peeling and hairdresser.

Like any hotel, Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * (Alanya) tries to organize the leisure of its guests. In the evenings, a team of adult animators actively works here, who comes up with everyday show programs. For lovers of night club life there is a disco.

Relaxing in the complex, you can play billiards, volleyball, tennis, aerobics.

Nutrition

Dining at Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya) is organized as an all-inclusive. For dinner, breakfast and lunch, a buffet awaits you. There are several restaurants on the territory, to visit them you must pre-register at the reception. They serve great food. There is a bar on the beach where you can refresh yourself for free. As for drinks, here tourists are served wines, beer, juices, imported and local coffee. But for freshly squeezed juices have to pay, although the price is much more affordable than in domestic resorts.

Beach and sea

Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * (Okurcalar) has its own beach (sand and pebble), on which there are free sun loungers with umbrellas. On the shore there is enough space for absolutely everyone. The beach is spacious and wide, so there is no need to take your place early. But towels are issued on bail, but for free. Sometimes they line up.

Places near the pools are not as many as on the shore, so vacationers try to take sun loungers by 8.30.



What attracts Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 * to itself? Guest reviews of the complex are always full of enthusiasm for the sea. The proximity of the hotel to the coast is an indisputable advantage. For those who go on vacation for the sake of the sea, you should not look for a better place than the Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 *. The reviews of tourists confirm this.

The sea is warm, the coast has a gentle slope, in the morning the water is clear, and after lunch, algae occasionally appears, but they do not interfere at all. Walking a bit along the coast, you can walk to the cape and take beautiful photographs there. Those who truly love the sea will appreciate this place.

Children's animation

The administration of Sealife Buket Resort Beach Hotel 5 * takes care of its youngest guests. For them there is a special pool with a depth of 0.3 meters. The hotel has children's animators. There is a mini club for young tourists under twelve years old, and for juniors - a junior club. Daily for children discos are organized from 9 to 9.30. In addition, the complex has a children's playground.

Upon request, a cot is provided free of charge. If desired, you can order babysitting services.

According to hotel guests, children's animation has one drawback. A lot of songs sound in German. And this is due to the fact that a lot of Germans always rest in the complex. Animators always try to captivate children with interesting games, yet the hotel is designed for couples with kids.

Entertainment

For those tourists who find there is little sea and water entertainment, there is the opportunity to do shopping. You don‚Äôt have to go far. To do this, go to Alanya to the bazaar. There are a lot of souvenirs and locally produced goods. Only their prices can be very high, you need to be able to bargain well with merchants, but this is a tedious task and not everyone likes. If this is your first time in Turkey, you can devote some time to excursions. For example, ride a quad bike inland, and look at life away from the sea. Tourists who visited sightseeing tours are very pleased. By the way, they are advised to take them not from the street barkers, but from local travel agencies, where they are cheaper.

Sealife Buket Beach Hotel 5 *: visitor reviews

The hotel is a good holiday destination for those who love the sea. The coast here is gentle, comfortable for relaxation. But, according to tourists, the sea is too deep, which is not very convenient for kids. In such a situation, a good solution to the problem may be bathing the child in the children's pool. The beach of the hotel is quite spacious and clean, it is cleaned daily. In addition, there is a bar on the shore where you can always eat. There are no problems with sun loungers and umbrellas, there are a lot of them on the shore.

Decent adult animators who will not let you get bored and will take a rest in the morning will be praised. They will offer girls to learn belly dancing and or do aerobics. Attending such classes is a very fun and rewarding affair. Even if you do not participate in such events, but simply contemplate them from the side, then this is also exciting. Many workshops take place directly by the pool or on the beach. Therefore, it is difficult to resist, so as not to participate in them. Rest in any case turns out to be active, fun and intense.

As for children's animation, then, according to tourists, it is insufficient. Those parents who plan to leave a child for a long time in the kids' club will not like it much. The main time when animators organize games falls at a time when children are at sea. But evening discos for kids are a great entertainment for young vacationers. Not all parents after this event reach adult programs. Still, outdoor activities take a lot of energy.

For those tourists who want to see the local exotic, there are lots of excursions that will appeal not only to adults, but also to children. As a rule, vacationers are completely delighted with such trips, which make it possible to get to know the country, local customs and customs. Hotel workers are happy to tell guests where they can go and how to do it better. Their advice is definitely worth using. You will not regret the time and money spent on travel.



The food at the hotel is quite good. Especially pleasing to our compatriots is the all-inclusive system. It is very comfortable. Throughout the day, you can have refreshments and drinks at any bar. Ice cream is offered to guests in sufficient quantities, which pleases the children. Chefs prepare delicious desserts that leave no one indifferent. As for fruits, they are served quite a lot. The menu contains a variety of dishes. There is also porridge, which is very convenient, since they are usually loved by children. We can safely say that the power system is well established here.

The hotel area is not large, but very well maintained. There are a lot of greenery and flowering plants around, it is always very nice to walk along the paths and admire their beauty. Children can safely let go play in the complex.

Some vacationers advise you to pay extra money at the reception at the settlement, then you will be accommodated much faster, and they will give the room better (10-20 dollars). Other tourists do without such surcharges and are quite happy with the rooms. So, judge for yourself.

The hotel rooms are clean, not very spacious. But, on the other hand, no one is sitting in them, because everyone is going to swim in the sea and sunbathe. Some vacationers have some comments regarding the five-star status of the complex, since there are not many attributes inherent in this category. In their opinion, the hotel is more in line with the four-star level (there are no cosmetics in the bathroom, plastic furniture on the terraces, etc.). It is difficult to judge the validity of such judgments. To form an objective opinion, you must visit this beautiful place yourself.

Of course, the hotel has some drawbacks, but it must be borne in mind that all this is offset by fairly affordable prices for holidays in comparison with other similar complexes. Therefore, you can not pay attention to some points.
