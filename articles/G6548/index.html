Hotel Orion Hotel Rhodes 3 * (Greece / Rhodes): description and reviews

To fully enjoy your vacation in sunny Greece, pay attention to the resort complex Orion Hotel Rhodes 3 *. Here you will be surrounded by care and attention, which will allow you to forget about all problems and deeds.





Orion Hotel Rhodes Description

Greece is one of the most popular resort destinations that has appealed to millions of travelers from around the world. If we talk about a hotel such as Orion Hotel Rhodes 3 *, then it is worth noting that it was erected in 1991. The last reconstruction and repair was carried out in 2003.

The services of the Orion Hotel Rhodes 3 * hotel guests are all that is necessary for a comfortable stay and a good rest. There is a swimming pool, restaurants, bars, and wireless Internet, and much more. It is also worth noting that the hotel features 109 comfortable rooms located in three modern buildings.





Hotel location

Only 4 minutes walk from the beach is Orion Hotel Rhodes 3 *. Greece, Faliraki, Kallithea Avenue, 851 is the address of this resort. This is a lively resort village, which has long been loved by tourists due to the huge number of shops, restaurants and entertainment venues. The nearest attractions that you should definitely visit are:

  • the largest water park in Rhodes (2.5 km);
  • luxurious thermal springs in the bay of Kallithea (5 km);
  • the ancient Acropolis of Ialysos (8 km);
  • Rhodes Palace (14 km);
  • Faliraki city beach (1 km);
  • Anthony Quinn Bay (4 km).

Guest apartments

Orion Hotel Rhodes 3 * offers its guests comfortable conditions for living. Guests can choose one of two accommodation options, namely:

  • Standard double apartments give guests the opportunity to stay in two single beds with orthopedic mattresses. An extra bed is available for an extra guest or child. You can also enjoy the beautiful surrounding views with your own private balcony.
  • Superior apartments are spacious and have more modern facilities. There is a small seating area, presented upholstered furniture, as well as a tea table. Like the previous category, superior rooms have a balcony. It is also possible to install an additional clamshell.

One can not say a few words about the design solution of the rooms of the Orion Hotel Rhodes resort. The photos of the guest apartments demonstrate a concise and homely interior. Light shades prevail here, which allows you to visually expand the space.





Guest apartment features

It is worth noting the special care with which Orion (hotel, Rhodes) treats its guests. Each guest room has the following amenities:

  • a flat-screen TV connected to a satellite dish broadcasts many channels, among which there are also several Russian-speaking ones;
  • thanks to the presence of a refrigerator, you can always keep a small food supply in your room to eat at any time convenient for you;
  • For communication with the reception and restaurant, a landline telephone is provided, which can be connected to an international line for an additional fee;
  • in summer, the room has air conditioning (it is worth noting that the system is centralized, which means that the device turns on according to a certain schedule);
  • each room has a kitchenette, thanks to which you can cook for yourself and your loved ones, but it is not suitable for preparing hot dishes;
  • the combined bathroom has a shower;
  • if you want to sleep longer in the morning, close the windows with blackout curtains and the sun will not disturb your sleep;
  • the bathroom has a built-in electric hairdryer;
  • thanks to the balcony, you can enjoy the beautiful surrounding landscapes while sitting in a comfortable wicker chair.

Cleanliness is one of the many virtues of the Orion Hotel. The hotel (Rhodes) has strict rules in this regard. Maids carefully monitor the cleanliness of the guest apartments. Cleaning is done daily (except Sunday), and bed linen and towels are changed twice a week.





Resort facilities

Modern hotels try to provide the most developed infrastructure to their guests so that they can find everything they need without leaving the territory. Orion Hotel 3 * does not lag behind modern trends. Here, guests can take advantage of the following list of infrastructure facilities:

  • in summer, an outdoor swimming pool;
  • the entire hotel is surrounded by a garden with green grass, tropical palm trees and other exotic plants;
  • for those who like to spend time reading an interesting book, there is a library with a large selection of literature (including in Russian);
  • if you are tired for a day, then you can relieve physical stress due to hydromassage;
  • there is the possibility of playing billiards;
  • the restaurant is responsible for the full nutrition of guests;
  • in the bar you will always have access to a wide selection of drinks, snacks and other snacks;
  • Wi-Fi is available in public areas and is free of charge.
  • there is a parking zone on the territory, the use of which is included in the price of the ticket;
  • Luggage storage will allow you to leave your suitcases before check-in or after eviction;
  • if you want to see all the sights of the resort with a guide, contact the tour desk;
  • the hotel has a laundry service that launches not only bed linen and towels, but also guests' personal belongings;
  • reception is open around the clock;
  • for the comfort of guests an elevator is provided;
  • at the reception you can rent a safe deposit box (weekly use will cost you 10 euros);
  • stationary computers and cable network can be used in the Internet corner located in the lobby area;
  • on the beach you can rent equipment for water sports;
  • around the pool there are many sun loungers, as well as umbrellas to protect from the scorching Greek sun;
  • a spacious sun terrace;
  • there are terminals for paying for services using bank plastic cards;
  • there is a currency exchange office on the ground floor of the hotel;
  • You can visit the grocery store without leaving the hotel (basic necessities are also sold here).




Services for guests of the hotel Orion Hotel 3 *

The soul of any resort hotel is its staff, which is designed to provide guests with quality service and reverent care. If we talk about Orion Hotel (Rhodes), it is worth noting that guests can expect to receive the following list of services:

  • delivery of food, as well as alcoholic and refreshing drinks from the restaurant directly to your apartment;
  • booking and selling tickets for aviation or water transport;
  • if you need to iron things that managed to wrinkle in suitcases, then at the reception you can take an iron, after leaving a small deposit;
  • if you are interested in water sports, you can get a consultation from a professional instructor;
  • at the reception you can use the fax, as well as make a photocopy or scan your documents;
  • In the mornings, a rich and delicious breakfast buffet is served for hotel guests.
  • if necessary, cots and other accessories are provided in the rooms;
  • if you feel unwell, you can get a doctor's consultation and first aid;
  • in case you need background information about the city, its infrastructure facilities and attractions, contact the front desk staff;
  • for an additional fee on the beach you can use sun loungers and sunshades.




Useful information for guests

Any hotel has its own internal rules, and Orion is no exception. Rhodes boasts a huge number of resort hotels, and in each of them the order of residence may vary slightly. As for this institution, it will be useful for guests to know the following:

  • registration and check-in of newly arrived guests begins strictly after two in the afternoon, but after paying the amount indicated in the price list, you can use the early check-in service (this is possible only if there are free rooms);
  • on the day of departure, you must return the keys to the room before noon (but if you do not want to expect your late flight at the airport, use the late check out option, which implies an hourly fee);
  • children under the age of two years stay in rooms for free, and if necessary, they can be provided with a cot;
  • hotel policy does not allow more than one extra bed;
  • this resort hotel does not provide the possibility of staying with pets, and therefore, for violation of this rule, the administration has the right to refuse the guest to provide services;
  • if you plan to arrive at the hotel without booking a room, notify the administration in advance of your arrival;
  • upon registration and check-in, each newly arrived guest is required to pay a fixed amount of the deposit, which will be refunded upon eviction if you do not cause material damage to the hotel (this amount can be paid in cash or by credit card);
  • the guest can be checked into the hotel only if a valid photo ID is provided;
  • if a child under 7 years old does not need to provide an extra bed, then for his stay in the room there is no charge.

General impression

The first thing you should pay attention to is the convenient location of the Orion Hotel Rhodes 3 * resort. Faliraki is a small village, but this does not mean that travelers will have to be bored here. There are many cafes, restaurants, night clubs and other entertainment venues. In addition, in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a bus stop, so you can go for a walk in neighboring cities. Perhaps the only drawback of the location of this hotel can be considered that on the way to the beach you need to cross the road with busy traffic. Unfortunately, in Greece it is not customary to let pedestrians pass, and therefore it is necessary to wait a long time for the window between passing cars.

The territory of the Orion Hotel Rhodes 3 *, although small, but there is a lot of space for relaxation and walks. The garden, pool and other areas are very carefully looked after. As for the building itself, it is also pretty enough, although the architecture is old-fashioned. The interior of the lobby area is very pleasant and pleasing to the eye. As for restaurants and bars, they need not only repair, but also the replacement of equipment, especially air conditioners, which absolutely can not cope with their functions. The hotel is quite quiet, with the exception of the site near the pool. If you like silence, check the location of your room in advance.

The rooms of the Orion 3 * hotel (Rhodes), although modest, but quite comfortable. There is a fairly fresh repair, although there are some drawbacks. In general, going on vacation to this hotel, you should pre-configure yourself that in the budget "troika" you will not find five-star luxury. thus, no disappointments will befall you. It has everything you need to fully relax and restore strength between trips to the sea and trips to exciting excursions.
