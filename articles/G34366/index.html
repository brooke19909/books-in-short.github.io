Philips Xenium W8500: specs, reviews, unlock

Philips is not as active in the mobile device market as the giants Samsung, Lenovo, or, say, LG. Despite this, she manages to produce high-quality smartphones that are in demand by a large number of people.

We will talk about one of these devices in this article. Meet the Philips Xenium W8500 in front of you. Read about what this gadget is and what features it has, read in this article.

general characteristics

Perhaps we should start with the fact that we have a real smartphone with a degree of protection of IP67. This means that the device is able to withstand the ingress of dust, moisture and at the same time maintain its performance. Without any extra thought, the device can be used for active sports, take with you on hunting, fishing, camping and so on. The second feature all the more contributes to this - we are talking about the long life of the device.





As the manufacturer claims in the technical parameters for his model, the device is able to last at least 3-4 days on a single charge. Users note 2-3 days, which is already a good enough indicator for a device that runs on the Android operating system. And this is due to the balanced battery consumption of a 2400 mAh battery.

The capabilities of a mobile phone provided by its processor, camera, and other modules will be revealed later in the article. However, even without this, we can safely say that the device is positioned as a smartphone suitable for use in extreme conditions - this is what the manufacturer obviously relies on. In addition, the device has an attractive stylish design, which in normal conditions will appeal to any user.

Appearance

From what the telephone looks like, we will begin our more detailed description. So, Philips Xenium W8500 is presented in the form of a typical ‚Äúbrick‚Äù. In his image, you will not find elements common among other secure phones like large rubber caps of bright color and incredibly thick glass. No, from the side the device looks very neat, although there is not much elegance in it. Along the perimeter of the case there is a frame, the thickness of which reaches 8.5 millimeters. It looks as if it has straight edges, but if you take the smartphone in your hands, you will notice a smooth transition to the back of the device.





As for the materials, it should be noted that aluminum, which the manufacturers of such devices like so much, did not find a place in the Philips Xenium W8500 - the case consists of durable plastic with a textured texture on the back cover. Due to this, picking up the phone and working with it is very pleasant. This plastic has such a structure, thanks to which scratches are almost invisible on it. On the case you can see only three physical navigation buttons - one (screen lock) is located on the upper edge of the device, the other two (volume keys) find their place on the right side. The headphone and charger connectors are covered with rubber plugs, which in color harmoniously fit into the overall concept of the phone.

Smartphone display

As stated by the manufacturers, the screen that is installed on the Philips Xenium W8500 phone has a diagonal of 4.3 inches. To protect it from bumps and scratches, the display was covered with Gorilla Glass. A similar coating is used on many models, and there is debate about how well it protects the smartphone screen from scratches. It is believed that glass only reduces the likelihood of damage, but does not provide complete protection.

The resolution of the picture on the Philips Xenium W8500 leaves much to be desired - we are talking about a size of 960 by 540 pixels. In general, the screen operates on the basis of TFT technology, so the smartphone can only transmit 16 million colors. But the image density may please - it is 256 pixels per inch.

Device battery

As already mentioned above, manufacturers position the phone as a tough, unpretentious (in terms of energy consumption) device. Contributes to this battery, whose capacity is 2400 mAh. As we noted above, practice shows: the device can withstand 2-3 days of active use before charging. Camera

The Xenium W8500 has two cameras located on the front and back of the case. Multimedia

Thanks to the operating system and the hardware on which the smartphone is built, the device is able to play almost all the most common multimedia formats. In particular, it can be movies in HD-quality, and work with all audio files, and even play radio stations. In order to listen to the radio, by the way, you will have to connect a headset to the phone - it will just use the role of an antenna to receive the signal.

Communication

Smartphone Philips Xenium W8500 has all the necessary communication capabilities that are inherent in other models of devices from other manufacturers. This is primarily work in GSM networks of different networks, as well as the option of 3G access to mobile Internet. You can also note the presence of a Wi-Fi module: with its help, the device has the ability to work with high-speed wireless Internet connection. In addition to the specifications of the Philips Xenium W8500, the phone also has a Bluetooth module for transmitting and receiving files.





Finally, the function of determining the location of the device on the ground, as well as the orientation in space, is performed by the A-GPS module. This feature is especially useful if you use the model for trips to an unfamiliar area or traveling where there is a chance of getting lost.
