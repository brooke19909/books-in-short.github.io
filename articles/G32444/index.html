Pension "Grenada" (Lazarevskoye): photos and reviews of tourists

The most popular holiday destination for Russian tourists is, of course, the city of Sochi. Lazarevskoye is the central village of its largest microdistrict. This settlement was built in a beautiful ecologically clean place of the Black Sea zone. Pension "Grenada", located in the heart of Lazarevsky, is very popular with tourists due to the high level of service, room comfort and well-developed infrastructure. This complex is a great place for both family and business leisure.

The territory of the boarding house

As a resort village, the recognition of the village. Lazarevskoye received in the middle of the twentieth century. Soviet tourists appreciated the beauty of these places and the warm climate. In 1976, a large comfortable boarding house Grenada was built on the territory of the village. Not so long ago (in 2009) it was completely reconstructed. Number of rooms

Holiday Hotel "Grenada" (Lazarevskoye) offers comfortable modern rooms with a TV, furniture, refrigerator and a bathroom with shower. Most rooms have balconies. The boarding house is located at an altitude of 65 m above sea level, and therefore from the balconies you can see just a magnificent view. You can enjoy the sea sunset and the picturesque mountains surrounding Lazarevskoye, covered with exotic plants. All types of rooms (standard, economy, superior and luxury) are equipped with air conditioning.





The rooms are cleaned daily. Bed linen is changed once every five days. It is possible to order an extra bed for both children and adults.

Nutrition

The Grenada boarding house has its own comfortable dining room, working on the "custom menu" system. Dishes are prepared by highly qualified chefs and are very diverse. Three meals a day and is included in the price. Also on site there is a summer cafe where you can have a bite to eat, and a bar for lovers of soft drinks.





Boarding house infrastructure

The beach can be reached both on foot and by bus. If desired, vacationers can take a dip in the fresh water pool available on the territory of the complex. The price of the permit also includes visiting:

  • libraries with constantly updated fund;
  • discos arranged in the evenings on the territory of the complex;
  • thematic concert evenings with live music and professional actors;
  • a sports complex with volleyball and basketball courts;
  • gym equipped with the latest technology.

Many tourists are advised to come to Grenada in Lazarevskoye with children. The complex has a small shallow pool for younger guests. There is also a playground and animation. The car can be left in a guarded parking lot. Rental space is also included in the price.





For an extra fee, you can visit the beachfront sauna, enjoy a massage or play tennis on the court. There is also a billiard room in the Grenada boarding house, where you can also have a good time.

Since the complex is located in the busiest place of the village, there are many restaurants and shops nearby. There are retail outlets in the boarding house itself. Here you can buy clothes, beach accessories, products. For outdoor enthusiasts, sports equipment rental is available.

Treatment

In the immediate vicinity of the "Grenada" is the sanatorium "Quiet Don", where tourists are offered treatment of a variety of diseases. There is an opportunity to undergo wellness procedures on the territory of the complex itself. Not so long ago, a treatment department was opened here on the profile of diseases of the cardiovascular system, musculoskeletal system, and respiratory organs. Also in the boarding house operates an emergency medical center.

Business holiday

Like all resorts in Sochi, Grenada provides ample opportunities for businessmen. It will be pleasant to combine business activity here with a good rest. On the territory of the complex there is a conference hall with 200 seats, equipped with modern electronics and appliances. Participants in seminars and conferences are provided with such services as a buffet table, coffee break, various kinds of entertainment programs.

Beach

The coastline of Lazarevskoye is covered with small pebbles and has a width of about 40 meters. The boarding house "Grenada" has its own beach, which he can be proud of rightfully. There is everything for a comfortable pastime. Vacationers are provided with sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as rugs and sun loungers. If desired, you can ride a jet ski or pedalo. Many vacationers book a boat trip on a boat. On the beach there is a massage room.





Only vacationers who have bought a ticket, as well as residents of Sochi after presenting a passport with a residence permit, are entitled to visit the coastal zone of the Grenada sanatorium. The entrance to the sea on the beach of the complex is comfortable and gentle. Large stones and boulders are absent.

Entertainment

In addition, the Grenada boarding house (Lazarevskoye) offers its guests visits to various cultural events. Every evening, the complex hosts discos, concerts, shows, contests for children and adults. If desired, you can buy a tour. In the vicinity of Lazarevsky there are a lot of natural and historical attractions.

Pension "Grenada" (Lazarevskoye): ticket prices

We offer to find out how much it costs to stay at the Grenada boarding house (per person per day for 2015):

  • in economy class rooms - 1500 p. (up to 2000 in high season);
  • in superior rooms - 2000 p. (up to 3000 in July and August);
  • in standard rooms - 1700 p. (up to 2700 p.);
  • in luxury rooms - 3000 r. (up to 4000 p.).

Extra bed for adults will cost 70% of the room rate, for children under 14 years of age - 50%. Subject to availability, rooms can be booked for individual guests. In this case, you will need to pay 1.5 of the cost of the room.

Upon arrival, you will have to present your passport, voucher and medical insurance policy. Parents with children under 14 years of age must have a birth certificate in their hands , as well as a certificate of vaccinations. If the child is traveling without parents, you also need a power of attorney for the escort. Children in the boarding house "Grenada" are accepted from four years.

Sights of the village

The village of Lazarevskoye itself will be very interesting for vacationers of the Grenada complex. Rest (the boarding houses of this resort center are mostly well-maintained) you can organize a very eventful one. In the village you can visit one of the two water parks - Starfish and Nautilus. These are modern complexes with well-thought-out infrastructure. You can have a good time here both with family and friends.

  • Crab Gorge.




There are other interesting sights in Lazarevsky. Excursions are sold directly to the guesthouse. Experienced guides conduct them, and tourists who have already visited the Grenada complex find them very informative and interesting.

How to get to the boarding house

The complex is located at: Sochi (Lazarevsky district), st. Partizanskaya, d. 59. Getting to the village by train is very simple. Lazarevskoye is located on the Tuapse-Sochi railway line, all trains stopping in the direction of Adler stop here. You need to take a ticket to the Lazarevskoye station. From Adler airport, the easiest way is to first get to Sochi, and then get to the village itself by bus or train. From the station to the complex runs minibus number 76.
