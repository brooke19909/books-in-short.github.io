Method number 1 - iclaud.com

There are four ways to transfer contacts from iPhone to Android:

  1. Each user of Apple technology must have an Apple ID account , it is required not only for successfully downloading applications from the AppStore store, but also for synchronizing a PC, phone or tablet with iCloud cloud storage.
  2. The main thing to remember here is that when you start exporting, you must definitely configure the synchronization of contact information. To do this in the iCloud settings, the slider next to the "Contacts" item is turned on.
  3. To do this, you need to log in from a full browser from a PC.
  4. Using your Apple ID account, open the iCloud cloud storage site and log in. Select "Contacts" and wait for the complete download of the contact list. Using the key combinations CTRL + A (for Windows) or CMD + A (for MAC), all contacts in the list are selected.
  5. In the lower left corner, click on the gear icon and select "Export vCard".
  6. A computer browser automatically downloads a file with the vcf extension. It is in it that all contacts are stored. This file must be sent to the new device and export started.

This method of action, such as transferring contacts from iPhone to Android, is ideal for both new smartphones and ‚Äúlast century‚Äù cell phones.

Method number 2 - My Contacts Backup

The AppStore store has many applications for every taste that make it possible to export contacts from the Apple gadget. We offer to stop your attention on the free utility My Contacts Backup.

To perform the transfer of contacts you need to take a few simple steps:

  1. The first thing, as you already understood, is to download and install the My Contacts Backup application and allow it access to the contacts of the iPhone smartphone.
  2. Next, click on the green Backup button and wait for the process to complete.
  3. Then you need to click on the E-mail button and send a vcf file to your mail.
  4. Download the vcf file from mail and run it, while the export of contacts will happen automatically.

Method number 3 - iCloud + Dropbox

If the previously analyzed method No. 1 required the presence of a stationary computer, then in the third method we will consider the possibility of how to transfer phones from iPhone to Android using the standard iOS browser - Safari.

  1. On an iPhone smartphone in Safari, you need to go to iCloud.com
  2. You should click the "Share" button and find the item "Full version of the site".
  3. After the full version of the page is loaded, you need to log in by entering your details from your Apple ID account.
  4. After successful authorization, you will be taken to the iCloud application menu.
  5. Open the Contacts menu.
  6. In the lower left corner you need to click on the gear and double-tap "Select all". As soon as all the contact information turns blue, you need to re-click on the gear and select "Export vCard" already.
  7. After the vcf-file is fully loaded, a new tab will open in the Safari browser, in it you must select the "Open in ..." item and save it in any cloud service convenient for you: DropBox, Google Drive, OneDrive and others.
  8. And the last thing to do is download the file from the cloud storage and export the contacts to the memory of the new gadget.

Method number 4 - CardDAV

Another way to transfer contact information from iPhone to Android is to use the special CardDAV application and the server, of course, iCloud.

  1. From Google Play to an Android gadget, you need to install an application called CardDAV.
  2. In the full list of accounts, select the CardDAV application and click on its launch.
  3. As a server, you need to use the p02-contacts.icloud.com format entry. In the "Name" and "User Password" fields, account information with an Apple ID is entered. Click the "Next" command.
  4. We synchronize contact information from the server to the phone, and then click "Finish."
  5. During synchronization, contacts are automatically transferred to the memory of our new smartphone.

The idea above the proposed method, how to transfer numbers from iPhone to Android, is to export your contacts directly from the Apple server.
