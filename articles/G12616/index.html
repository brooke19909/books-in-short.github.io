How to earn a student. Practical tips



Traditionally, the world has developed the opinion that students are the most active social stratum of the population, however, perhaps the poorest. The image of a hungry student from a dormitory is so firmly entrenched in the brain that nothing can eradicate it. However, as well as numerous jokes on this subject.

However, the students themselves are not laughing: the state of developing countries is not able to at least somehow significantly provide the country with a decent scholarship. The size of this scholarship is usually even below the subsistence level. Despite the fact that the costs of young people studying at universities are considerable, even if you do not thank teachers for exams, term papers, and scientific work. Based on the foregoing, most often the heads of young people are not occupied by science at all, but by the vital question of how to earn a student.

The answer is very mixed. Since 5 years at the university are reserved for mastering their specialty, if a student gets a full-time job (in the absence of a diploma, by the way), there will be no time for full-time study at all. Which subsequently cannot but affect his future career and material well-being. A vicious circle comes out. But financial difficulties force young people to go to all sorts of tricks.

Below are some of the most realistic ways to make money for a student. Whether or not to follow them is up to you.

How to earn a student a promoter?



This involves an advertising type of activity. You may have to hand out flyers on the street, or you will advertise promotional items in some supermarket. Such work has an hourly type of payment and a flexible schedule, which is important for the student. The level of wages varies greatly (from 2.5 cu / hour) depending on the company and the city in which you work. Work experience, as a rule, is not required, as well as special skills, but it is possible that first you will have to go through an interview and a short training course.

How to earn a student a courier?

Another fairly convenient form of income for young people. Your responsibilities will be to deliver mail to various organizations. The road is paid by your employer. Special skills, like education, are not required, but you are responsible for the safety of transportable documentation. Payment is from $ 20 for a full day of work.

How to make a student a copywriter?

A fairly popular type of earnings in the last few years. However, it requires certain skills: you must be fluent in language and be a versatile person who can write a 3-sheet essay about the color of lipstick for blondes and about repairing a car engine. The convenience of such work lies in the same flexible schedule, as well as in the ability to work without leaving your home - remotely. Payment for a beginner copywriter is from 0.5 cu per thousand characters.

How to earn a student a seasonal job?



In many universities there are training programs for counselors for children's summer camps with subsequent employment. In addition, there is seasonal work in the resorts of our country and beyond: animators, dancers, waitresses, etc. The main advantage is that you work in the summer during your holidays and do not break away from school, and you can also combine work with your own vacation . Your salary will be from 2.5 cu in an hour.

How does a student earn with his mind?



The most promising type of earnings for a student is to use their professional skills acquired at a university. You are improving, raising your professional level, and starting a future promising career. For example, if you are studying as a programmer, today you can work (freelance), offering your services for creating sites, for example. Students of language specialties can work as translators. The level of earnings varies widely depending on specific circumstances.

Thus, any young person in his student years can choose his own way of making money, skillfully combining it with his studies. It all depends on your imagination.
