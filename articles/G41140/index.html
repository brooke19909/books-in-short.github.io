One of the well-known resort places of Crimea is Sudak. The city is famous primarily because it has an old fortress. And in the immediate vicinity of it, the Soldaya Grand Hotel & Resort recently appeared, a vacation in which many tourists will appreciate.

What to see in Sudak

The first thing the city of Sudak (Crimea) is associated with is, of course, the Genoese fortress. This magnificent monument, which has come down to us since the Middle Ages, still stands today on a massive mountain. It is possible to approach it only by the sloping descent in the north, so the choice of location for the construction of such a protective structure is understandable and justified.

Today, the fortress is a very valuable architectural monument, which all visitors to the city seek to see closer. You can go inside the fortress, take a walk along its walls and look inside some of the towers. There are also organized various thematic events during which tourists can feel like residents of the Middle Ages, change into appropriate outfits, shoot archery, ride horses and, of course, take pictures for a long memory.

Another interesting place in Sudak is the temple, built in the Byzantine style approximately in the XII-XV centuries. It is located at the foot of a mountain called Polwani-Oba closer to the village of Cozy. Once there were murals depicting Jesus Christ and the Twelve Apostles. Alas, today we are not destined to look at them, but the name of the place is directly connected with them: after all, the construction is called the Temple of the Twelve Apostles. And next to it is a tower called Portovaya, or Astagwera.





Resting in Sudak, it is very convenient to visit the small village of New World, which is famous for its beautiful bays, juniper groves, the Golitsyn Trail and a champagne wine factory. Italian holidays in Crimea

Due to the fact that the Genoese fortress is located in Sudak, the spirit of the Middle Ages is partly felt here. Perhaps the name of the Soldaya Grand Hotel & Resort may seem strange to someone. "Soldaya" is an Italian word. This is exactly what the Italians called the city in ancient times. Where is the hotel situated

As noted above, the Soldaya Grand Hotel & Resort is very close to the Genoese fortress. Moreover, it was built directly at the foot of the mountain, on which this medieval defensive structure towers. Therefore, finding this hotel in Sudak is quite easy.

Of course, it is much easier to find the necessary object in the city, if you know its exact address. Soldaya Grand Hotel & Resort 4 * is located on Morskaya Street, house 23. You need to walk only 300 meters to the equipped beach, which will not take guests a lot of time. You will have to go longer to the Sudak water park, about 2 kilometers.

But in order to be near the Soldaya Grand Hotel & Resort itself, you must first get to the territory of the peninsula itself. This can be done with the help of an airplane landing at the airport of the city of Simferopol, or with the help of a ferry through the Kerch Strait by bus or personal transport.





The journey from the airport to Sudak will take several hours, but guests will be able to fully appreciate the beauty of the nature of Crimea. First, you will need to get to the bus station by regular bus. There, guests should take a minibus or bus at number 2 and go to Sudak to the Morskaya Street stop. There you already have to walk a bit to be near the hotel itself.
