"Nemo" (dolphinarium, Dzhubga): photos and description



A modern dolphinarium with a large water arena was opened in Dzhubga in 2014. The total number of seats for spectators is 800. If you believe the reviews of visitors to the complex, there is enough space between the rows, and the chairs are comfortable. Designers of the entertainment complex paid attention to every detail. What is happening in the pool is visible even from the last rows. The interior of the dolphinarium looks fresh and stylish, so you can take colorful photographs here as a keepsake. The main stars of the entertainment complex are four Black Sea dolphins, and seals and lions also participate in the performances.

Show program



Dolphinarium in Dzhubga boasts an interesting program that will not leave anyone indifferent. During the presentation, viewers learn that each dolphin has its own talents. One sings best, another draws, the third is the kindest, and the fourth is distinguished by courage and curiosity. Agree, these amazing animals are really very much like people!

The program includes presenting ball games, riding instructors, jumping and drawing. Dolphins will show funny tricks, as well as numbers that you definitely never saw before. According to dolphinarium instructors, one of the most difficult elements of the program is choral singing. The thing is that in the natural environment dolphins rarely sing and usually do it alone. To teach the inhabitants of the seas to sing in unison and at a certain moment is a very difficult task. The Dolphinarium in Dzhubga offers not only to watch a show of trained animals, but also to attend an educational program. The entertainment complex regularly hosts a thematic event called "Meet the Dolphins." This is a unique program consisting of a lecture on the inhabitants of the seas and communication with these amazing animals.

What will delight the dolphinarium, besides the show with marine inhabitants?

Dolphinarium in Dzhubga is a modern entertainment center where you can have a great time at least half a day with the whole family. There are no age restrictions. In addition to the dolphinarium, visit the oceanarium, penguinarium and photo zones. Also in the complex there is a cafe where you can have a meal and relax with a cup of coffee. Do not forget to buy souvenirs with dolphinarium symbols, which are sold here in the souvenir shop.

Opening hours and prices



The indoor Nemo Dolphinarium awaits guests year-round. During the active tourist season, performances are held daily. Dolphinarium in Dzhubga showtimes offer a very convenient for visitors. Dolphin shows can be watched daily at 10.30, 15.00 and 20.00. How much does the ticket cost? Single price for visitors of all age categories: 500 rubles. Children under three years old can watch the performance for free accompanied by their parents (without providing a separate place). The highlight of each show is an auction, during which you can purchase a picture painted by a dolphin. The cost of this work of art usually starts from one ruble. Also, for an additional fee, after the performance, you can take pictures and even swim with a dolphin. You can take photos and shoot videos for free during the presentation.

Visitors reviews



Dolphinarium in Dzhubga delights visitors with a bright and interesting program. During the performance, guests will see the classic numbers of dolphins and fur seals, as well as unique tricks set by local trainers. Animals look healthy and well-groomed, during the show you can see how they are attached to their mentors.

"Nemo" (dolphinarium, Dzhubga) has positive reviews and due to its comfort. The seats in the auditorium are very comfortable, and you can fully enjoy the performance from anywhere. Most viewers find ticket prices acceptable. For such a rich show is not a pity to pay more. Dolphinarium "Nemo" is indoor, which means that you can visit it in any weather. If it rains outside, you can spend several hours in the entertainment complex. Watch dolphin shows, local exhibitions and you will get a lot of impressions, regardless of the weather outside. Finding a dolphinarium in Dzhubga is not difficult. It is located at the entrance to the village from the "Nadezhda" microdistrict. This is a large and bright modern building with its own parking.
