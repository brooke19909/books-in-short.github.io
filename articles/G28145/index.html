Multimillionaire Ilya Medkov: biography, condition, version of the murder

How did Ilya Medkov become a millionaire? Why was he killed? We will answer these and other questions in the article. Ilya Alekseevich Medkov was a Russian entrepreneur, manager. He was the head of Pragma Bank, the owner of the DIAM oil company. This man is one of the first multimillionaires in Russia.

Biography

Ilya Medkov was born in the family of a doctor in Moscow in 1967, February 27. In the Chemical-Technological Institute. Mendeleev (Moscow) he entered in 1983. For the first three years, the new student was engaged in samizdat affairs, and in 1984 he transferred to the journalism department of Moscow State University, from where he was expelled in 1988. In the same year, he began working as a freelancer at Kommersant Publishing House.

Further, Ilya Medkov becomes a partner of the Moscow banker Angelevich Arkady. Together with him, in 1990, in February, he co-founded the Pragma cooperative, which was engaged in the import of personal computers from the United States and Japan. In 1991, Ilya turned into one of the millionaires of the USSR and entered the club of the young rich of Russia.





After the August events of 1991, in October of that year, Ilya Medkov created Pragma Bank in order to legalize the intermediary functions of selling goods from state enterprises. At the time of the start of privatization, in 1992, Medkov‚Äôs structures audited about 9% of all supplies of the Russian raw materials industry (including its own factories in Kazakhstan and Lermontov in the Caucasus), outstanding trading houses, mass media institutions, and expensive brokerage platforms on American and Russian exchanges.

In January 1993, the general privatization of oil enterprises in the public sector began. It was on this wave that Ilya Alekseevich Medkov created the DIAM oil company. In the same year, he took up the financing of large news agencies in Russia, social movements and political parties.

He sponsored several galleries in Moscow, acted as the leading sponsor of the exhibition of Russian art in Frankfurt. In 1993, in the summer, Ilya Alekseevich Medkov became a partner of the French authorities, who organized an Interethnic art show in the Paris Museum. According to false information, the state of the young businessman was about 600 million US dollars.

Murder

So, you already know a little such a person as Ilya Medkov, a biography. His parents probably did not think that he would be a millionaire. In 1993, on September 17, at about 01:15, he left the building of the Medkov bank and headed for the car that was waiting for him. Suddenly, he stood still and turned to go back, but suddenly fell. People thought that he had a heart attack.

As soon as they approached their boss and saw a pool of blood spreading beneath him, the bank employees and security guards realized what had happened. They called an ambulance and police. Doctors, examining the victim, concluded that he had virtually no chance of survival: the bullet hit the financier in the stomach and perforated the liver.

Ilya Medkov (banker) died in the Botkin hospital in the intensive care unit. He was buried at the Vagankovsky cemetery.
