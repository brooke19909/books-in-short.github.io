How to make a rope from a plastic bottle with your own hands

A rope from a plastic bottle can help out in an unforeseen situation, on a picnic or on a camping trip. It will become an indispensable assistant to the gardener: the cord is often used to garter vegetables and trees, creates a support for climbing plants. You can make such a tape using a special device or a stationery knife. A bottle cutting machine, if one exists, will help make strips of plastic quickly and with greater convenience than with a blade. To get started, from the usual capacity you need to cut off the bottom and top, leaving only a plastic cylinder.



Use cases for bottle rope

People who often use rope from a plastic bottle know how versatile this material is. It can be used in everyday life and to create various crafts. The rope from a plastic bottle is perfect for fastening reinforcement during construction work and compares favorably with metal wire due to the absence of an oxidation reaction. Another advantage of this material is its low cost, in contrast to special clamps for binding iron rods.





The use of plastic tape in needlework

Another use case for the product is the braiding of tool handles. This allows you to isolate the surface of the handles and prevent slipping. This method works better than using electrical tape. A cord from a plastic bottle allows you to carry out repairs and make furniture for the garden. Transparent rope firmly holds together the details of the products and does not spoil their appearance. Another type of bottle crafts is baskets and handbags. Cords are used to create original and durable containers, containers and various decorative items for interior decoration.





How to make a rope making machine

To facilitate the process of manufacturing a rope from a plastic bottle, you need to make a special machine for cutting thin strips. There are several ways to create such a product. In the first embodiment, for the manufacture of the machine you need two necks from ordinary bottles, the edges of which should be sharpened, and a regular CD. The necks are attached to the disk with glue, and the machine itself is fixed on the table with the usual wide tape. And you can also use iron blanks and fasten them on bolts to a wooden surface - this option will last longer. Before starting work, the bottom of the bottle is cut with a sharp knife, then you need to separate the small tail from the bottom, place it between the sharpened parts of the necks and turn the bottle in the direction of the cut. The fishing line will start to separate from the tank. For convenience, it is better to turn the resulting strip into a skein.





Machine from the corner

For the next option, additional materials will be needed. They can be purchased at a hardware store or searched in your pantry. The base of the machine is a dural corner up to 30 cm long, a blade from a construction or office knife. For their fastening, a thin metal rod, screws and nuts should be prepared. Of the tools will be used a drill, pliers and a hacksaw for metal. At the first stage, several holes are drilled in the corner according to the size of the screws used, and the knife blade is screwed on one side. Above it, you need to make slots through which the plastic tape will pass.





Cut the saws with sandpaper so that there is no burr. The width of the tape depends on the size of the holes, so you can cut holes of various diameters to make cords for different types of work. From the side of the knife, emphasis should be made of a curved metal bar so that it does not bend during operation. In another hole, a handle from a thin rod is attached. It is not very convenient to hold such a tool in your hands for a long time, because experienced craftsmen use tesos to fasten it, which increases the speed of work.
