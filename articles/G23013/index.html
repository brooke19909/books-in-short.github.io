Model selection

It would be better if before you sew a half-skirt on an elastic band, we decide on the model and make the necessary calculations. First, choose the length. What will be the skirt? To the knees, a little higher or maxi? When choosing a model, you need to consider the features of your figure. For example, a short wide skirt can visually increase fat hips, and a maxi length will not suit girls of short stature. Another important feature is the season. Summer and winter fabrics are very different from each other. For summer, you should not take synthetics, it is better to stay on cotton, linen, calico, silk, then the skirt will not be unbearably hot. But for cold time, fabrics with an admixture of artificial thread, for example, wool mixture, are quite suitable. Such fabrics are even more comfortable to wear and care natural, because the admixture of acrylic makes them resistant to shrinkage and stretching. Yes, and the moth does not like the taste of synthetics, but she likes natural wool.

Fabrics and accessories

Let's start with a trip to the needlework store. Having decided on the color and type of fabric, choose 2-3 favorites. It remains to connect the imagination and imagine how the finished skirt will look. The fabric that will cause the most pleasant associations, and buy. And from the hardware we may need only a tape-edging for the bottom - of course, if it is conceived. A zipper for this type of skirt is not needed. Do not forget about the gum itself - its length is equal to the length of the waist. It is best to take a flat, large width. And we will take care of the sewing threads in the tone of the fabric: if there is no suitable spool at home, we will definitely buy it.

Building patterns

If you decide to sew a half-sun skirt with your own hands, but are engaged in cutting for the first time, you will have to tinker a bit. Prepare a measuring tape, crayon or remnant, paper. And then everything in order:





  1. We take measurements: FROM (waist volume), OB (hip volume), CI (product length).
  2. In the upper corner of the sheet of paper, put the point X.
  3. We draw 2 perpendicular straight lines emerging from this point.
  4. On a horizontal line we measure the distance equal to a quarter of the exhaust gas + 4 cm.
  5. We also make a mark on the vertical line.
  6. We build a bisector that leaves the corner, put the same point on it.
  7. With the help of the curve we build a curve connecting the three upper points - this is the waistline.
  8. From this curve, measure the length equal to the desired CI, put points on the corresponding lines.
  9. Connect the lower points.
  10. It remains to cut a strip for the belt




How to sew a half-skirt on an elastic band?

When cutting out, do not forget about the seam allowances. On all lines, except for the lower edge, one centimeter is enough, and from the bottom you need to leave 2-2.5. We add the details, make a basting. After that, it is advisable to carefully try on the skirt to see if it sat well. If everything is in order, you can start stitching on a typewriter. The seam is only 2, so the process does not take much time. It remains to overcast the edges of the slices and retract the rubber band.
